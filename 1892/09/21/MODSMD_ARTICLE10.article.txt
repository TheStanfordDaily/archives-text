# Physical Examinations.
## 
### 
Physical examinations of the young
men will be conducted at Encina Gym
nasium office by Dr. Wood as rapidly as
possible. Those who wish to be ex
amined immediately may leave their
names with Mr, Black. Other appoint
ments will be made soon. All who
exercise in the Gymnasium will be ex
amined, and all others may be examined
by making appointments.
