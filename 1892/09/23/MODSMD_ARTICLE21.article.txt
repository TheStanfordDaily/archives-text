# A
						New Club House.
## 
### 
Plans are being completed fora new
club house which is to be built for the
boys from the Sandwich Islands, It is
to be situated at College Terrace close
to the residence of Dr. Jordan. At
present only four of the Islanders are
interested in the scheme. They are
John Wilson, Frank Andrade, L.
Scott and (). Sorenson. The house
will accommodate, when completed,
twelve people. Its 'estimated cost is
twenty-five hundred dollars and is be
ing built by the Carnall, Hopkins Co.
