Where are our track enthusiasts?
The next week ought not to pass with
out some kind of an impromptu field
day, and the track —if we may be par
doned for calling the cracked, baked,
cinder sprinkled oval such— must be
put in "some condition. The Palo
Alto requests that Mr. Ilice take the
matter in hand, and that every one in
the University stand ready to assist
him, Track athletics will this year
be a matter of University instead
individual pride, for we can no longer
refuse to meet our rivals on the cinder
path as well as on the diamond, the
court.and the field.
