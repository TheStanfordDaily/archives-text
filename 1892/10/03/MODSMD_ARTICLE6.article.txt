# Faculty vs. Sophomores.
## 
### 
On Saturday the Faculty defeated
the '95 men by a score of 9 to 8
White and Metcalf did the battery
work for the professors, and Professors
Richardson and Sampson for the sopho
mores. The game was a mixture of
errors and brilliant playing. The field
ing o( Harelson and Calhoun was up
to its usual excellence. The grand
stand play of the day was Sheehan's
catch of llarelson's muff of a high fly
hall. For the professors, Swain, An.
gell, and Stearns did great work both
in the field and with the stick
