# Associated Students.

There will be a meeting of the Asso
ciated Students Monday, Oct. 10th, at
1 :30 p.m. The business of the meet
ing will be to nominate officers, receive
report of committee on student guild,
and attend to some football and base
ball affairs. Tt is absolutely necessary
that there be a quorum of 110 present,
for according to the constitution nomi
nations will be illegal if deferred to a
later date. Carl S. Smith,
