# Notice to Professors.
## 
### 
A box has been placed in front of
the Registrar's office where class no
tices or any items of interest intended
for publication may bo deposited. It
is the wish of the Daily Palo Alto
editors that professors and instructors
in the University make use of these col
umns to publish notices of any kind
pertaining to their work or to the work
of the University in general.
