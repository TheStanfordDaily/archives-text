# Young Republicans.
## 
### 
There is to be a Republican Rally in
San Francisco, Saturday evening, Octo
ber Ktli, under the auspices of the
young men of the party. There are to
be six speeches, all by young men.
The Stanford Republican Club will be
represented by Mr. A. J. Brown, who
will be one of the speakers. Stanford
should be well represented at the meet
ing, as many of the boys live iu San
Francisco, and those residing about the
campus can run down and back on the
theatre train. The "Cardinal" should
maintain her place in the lead. A. J.
Brown has been elected a member of
the executive committee of the L. 8.
Jr. U. Republican Club.
