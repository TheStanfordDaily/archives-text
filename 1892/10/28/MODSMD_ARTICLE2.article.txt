# Physical-Chemical Society.
## 
### 
The society met last evening in the
physics lecture room. Papers were
presented on the following subjects:
Determination of Molecular Weights
from Freezing Points of Solutions,
by Prof. Richardson; Evaporation
of Liquids in Different Atmospheres,
by F. T. Shepherd; Falling Bodies
and the Resistance of tho At, by
C. VV. Green.
