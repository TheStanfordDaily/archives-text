# Northern Students.
## 
###

daring the holidays will please give
their names to H. M. Boutelle, room
109, Eucina. Mr. Knowlton, dis
trict passeuger agent of the S. P.
Co., has requested Mr. Boutelle to
attend to gelling up a party, and has
