# Sunday Evening Service.
## 
### 
The Christian Association meet
ing in the Chapel, Sunday evening,
December 11 th, at seven o'clock will
be conducted by Dr. T. I). Wood.
It is expected that Mr. T. L. M'Olung,
Yale's famous foothill captain, will
speak. Special music will bo pro
vided and the meeting promises to
bo one of exceptional interest. All
are invited to he present.
