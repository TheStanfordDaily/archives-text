# Engineering Society.

### 
At a meeting of the- Engineering
Society last Saturday evening a very
interesting talk was given by Mr.
Soulo, upon the timber of the Pacific
coast, extending from Alaska to tho
southern part, of our own state.
Tho election of officers for the com
ing semesterresulted as follows: S.K.
Kenowor, president; R. M. Drake,
vice-president; A. W. Cuddeback,
secretary; J. W. White, treasurer.
