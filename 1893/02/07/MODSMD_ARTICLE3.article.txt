# Mandolin Club.
## 
### 
The mandolin orchestra re-organ
ized last night with the following
members: H. Harris, flute and leader,
McGuire and Porter, guitars, Cap
ron and Code mandolins. They
intend, with the co-operation of the
quartette, to give a concert every
now and then. On Feb. 22d they
appear at the Methodist church in
San Jose, where they and the quar
tette will render several numbers.
