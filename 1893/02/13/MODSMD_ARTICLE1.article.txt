# University Extension.
## 
### 
Prof. Earl Barnes will deliver a
course of lectures to the teachers of
Alameda on the following subjects:
February 10th. "Children's Spon
taneous Drawing" —as a basis of in
telligent drawing.
February 17th. "Defective Spell
ers" —to determine the reasons why
some children spell naturally, while
others have great difficulty.
February 24th. "Children's Line's
of interest" —to determine along
what lines a child's Interest most
naturally lead* out into the external
world.
March 3d. "Development of the
Number Sense"—with an experi
mental study on lines of error in
arithmetical calculation.
