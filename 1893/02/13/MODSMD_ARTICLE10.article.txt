# Berkeley Glee Club.
## 
### 
On the 24th inst. the Glee Club
of the University of California will
give a concert at Odd Fellows' Hall
in Fan Francisco. Seventeen stu
dents compose this organization,
which a San Francisco critic has de
clared to be a clever lot of singers.
The young men will sing their var
ious college rhymes and ditties,attired
in picturesque caps and gowns. It
is expected that the concert will be
well attended bv Stanford students.
