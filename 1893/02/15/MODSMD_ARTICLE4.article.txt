# New French Class
## 
### 
Prof. Brun will start a class in
French about March Ist, the pur
pose of which will be to read plays
of the sixteenth and seventeenth cent
uries, and some now on the boards
in Paris. This class is supplement
ary to the work in French I , but is
not compulsory. The class will
meet every Thursday evening, either
in Prof. Brun's office or Prof. Grif
fin's recitation icom.
