# Promenade Concert.
## 
### 
A promenade concert will be given
in the quadrangle Saturday evening,
March 4th. The music for the oc
casion will be furnished by the Stan
ford Band, and the Horn Quartette
will probably give a few ■selections.
The band boys are doing some hard
practice, and surely deserve a large
turnout at their first appearance be
fore the public.
