# A 						Proposed Cruise.
## 
### 
Should the weather prove favor
able to-moi row a party of students
intend making an extended cruise of
the bay, to be gone three days.
Two yachts have been secured and
have been amply provisioned.
Messrs. Stuchell, Gardner, Bankin,
Code, and Capron and several of
their friends from Redwood will
compose the party. It is their in
tention to leave Bedwood to-morrow
at 1 p.m., and sail up the bay, stop
ping at San Francisco, Oakland, and
Alameda.
