Many students in the hall are not
considerate enough for those who
are sick. Those rooming near the
sick rooms are urgently requested to
be more thoughtful of the interests
of their fellows. Quiet is absolutely
necessary, if the the recovery of the
sick is expected. Let every one do
what he can to help in this matter,
and the favor will be greatly appre
ciated both by the tick men and
their friends.
