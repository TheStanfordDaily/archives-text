# The 						Joint Debate.
## 
### 
The first joint debate between
Alpha and Congress will take place
Tuesday evening l in the chapel, the
exercises beginning at 7:30 sharp.
The question to be discussed is,
"Should the Hawaiian Islands be
Annexed to the United States?"
The affirmative speakers are
Messrs. Gruwell, '93, Warren, '94,
and Chamberlin, Sp. The negative
speakers are Messrs. Trutnbo, '94,
Bannister, '93, and Rhodes, '96.
The judges Selected are I'rofessors
Howard, Swain, and Maix. Presi
ident Jordan will preside. The
Male Quartet'e will furnish music.
It is hoped that there may be good
attendance.
