# "Echoes of Home Songs."
## 
### 
Only a fair-sized audience greeted
H. Clay TVysbani and Percy H. Jack
son last Friday evening. The mus
ically illustrated lecture given was
quite enjoyable, and the quaint mel
odies very interesting. The flute
solos of Mr. Jackson were especially
good.
