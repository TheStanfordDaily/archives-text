# Hare and Hounds.
## 
### 
There will be a run at hare and
hounds to-morrow morning, Satur
day, at 10:30 starting from the front
of Enciua Hull.
Salisbury and Fry will act as
hares. The field day has been post
poned and it is desired that as many
as possible take part in this run.
The roads are now in good condition
and unless prevented by rain a
number of houudw should enter the
chase. Stuart, Mmgr.
