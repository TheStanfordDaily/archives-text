# The
						U. C.-Oakland Game
## 
### 
The University of California base
bull team, which will play against
the Oakland professional team at
the Piedmont grounds to-morrow
afternoon at 3 o'clock, will be made
up as follows: White (law college),
pitcher; Pierce, catcher: Robinson,
(medical college), first base; Brown
ing, second base; Goslinslcy, third
base; Bernheitn, shortstop; Morse,
left field; Galindo, center field;
Johnston, right field; Fine and
Mathews, substitutes. Mathews of
dental college will pitch Saturday's
game against the Oaklands at the
Haight street grounds.
