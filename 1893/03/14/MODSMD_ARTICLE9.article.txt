# Brown's Psychological Laboratory.
## 
### 
At Brown University a new de
parture has been made in establish
ing a psychological laboratory.
This laboratory is especially well
equipped for experiments in sight,
sound, touch, temperature, move
ment, and other sensations. It con
tains a kymograph and accompany
ing instruments for recording move
ments of the pulse, breathing, and
muscular pressure. There are also
instruments for showing the distrib
ution of blood and for measuring
the length of mental actions
