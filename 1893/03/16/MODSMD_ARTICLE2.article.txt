# Entertainment.
## 
### 
To-morrow evening a dramatic
reading of "The Rivals" will be
given by the Misses Jessie and Susie
Calhoun of San Francisco. The
music will be furnished by the young
ladies of Roble, for the benefit of
whose reading room the entertain
ment has been arranged. The Misses
Calhoun are pronounced true artists
by all who have heard them. A
large attendance is desired. The ad
mission will be twentv-five cents.
