# Joint Meeting of the Societies.
## 
### 
Alpha, Congress, and Euphronia
met in joint session this afternoon
for the purpose ot making arrange
ments concerning the joint debate
with Berkeley. Mr. Edward Hughes,
Sp., was appointed to meet the rep
resentative from Berkeley, and make
all necessary preliminaries as to
place of holding debate, number of
speakers, time given 'to each, and
the choosing of judges.
It was decided that each society
be represented in the debate by one
speaker, and that the speakers be
chosen at the next meeting subject
to the call of the chair.
