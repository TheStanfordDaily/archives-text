# Notices of Meetings.
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of all
members of the Boat Club held in
the chapel to-morrow afternoon at
1:30 o'clock. As the club president
and commodore will be elected, a
large attendance is desired
The regular meeting of the Math
ematical and Astronomical Club will
be held to-morrow (Thursday) even
ing in room 11. Papers will be
read by Prof. J. A. Miller and F. H.
Foster. All are invited.
The Seniors will meet to-morrow
at 1:30 p.m., room 10. An election
of speakers for class day will take
place.
