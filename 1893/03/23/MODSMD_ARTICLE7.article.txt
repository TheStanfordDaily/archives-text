# Stanford-U.C. Debate.
## 
### 
The two great universities of the
Pacific Slope will through three rep
resentatives from each institution
engage in a debate on April 22d,
probably at Odd Fellows' Hall in
San Francisco. The subject decided
upon is, "Shall the United States
Annex Hawaii?" The U. C. has
taken the affirmative audwill be rep
resented by C. H. Smith, C. A.
Reynolds, and L. N. Solomon.
A. H. Barnhisel, Ward Bannister,
and R L. Gruwell, all Stanford sen
iors, will be Palo Alto's debaters.
