# Reception to Juniors and Seniors.
## 
### 
W. S. Faris, '03, W. B. Moulton,
'94, C. A. Fife, '94, and F.J. Batch
elder, '94, gave a reception to the
classes of '93 and '94 at Lauro Hall
last evening. Besides the juniors
and seniors there were a great many
ladies present, irrespective of classes,
and also several professors. About
one hundred in all were invited,
and nearly eighty attended. Ihe
evening was very pleasantly spent.
Light refreshments were served.
