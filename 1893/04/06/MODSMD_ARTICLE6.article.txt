# Leaders for Chapel Exercises.
## 
### 
The following persons have been
appointed to take charge of the
chapel exercises for the week begin
ning Monday, April 10th: Monday,
President Jordan; Tuesday, Prof.
Todd; Wednesday, I'rof. Branner;
Thursday, I'rof. Wood; Friday,
I Prof. Cox
