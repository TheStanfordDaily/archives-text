# '95 Meeting.
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of the
Class of '95 at 1:30 p.m. next Wed
nesday afternoon, April 19 th, in the
chapel. The subject of a Junior
Annual will be discussed, and if
possible editois aud business man
agers will be elected at once. A
class baseball manager will be
elected, and a member of the board
of directors of the Daily Palo Alto
will be chosen. The matter of a
class entertainment will be brought
up. Last semester's and last year's
class historians will please have
their histories ready to hand to the
class secretary to be read, and the
treasurer will please render a finan
cial report. A. B. Rice, Pres.
