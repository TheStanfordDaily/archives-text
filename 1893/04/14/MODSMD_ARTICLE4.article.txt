# Cooney Will Not Play.
## 
### 
Last night Manager Grove re
ceived a telegram from Manager
Wolfe of Berkeley, stating that no
attempt would bo made to play
pitcher Cooney again, so we need
not anticipate any further trouble in
this direction. The team will stay
at the Metropole Hotel while in
Oakland. Following will be the
players in to-morrow's game: Wal
ton, catcher; Campbell, pitcher;
White, larst; Davey, second; Kay or
Pauley, third; Lewis, short; Ilarrel
son, left; Sheehan, center; Rea,
light; Caprou, substitute.
