# Board of Directors
## 
### 
There will be au important meet
ing of the Board of Athletic Directors
Wednesday evening in Encina Hall
at 7 o'clock. The athletic treasurer
will present hia report, along with
those of the managers of the differ
ent departments.
W. 0. Smith, Pres.
