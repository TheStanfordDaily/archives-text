# The
						Alpha.
## 
### 
The last regular meeting ot Alpha
for the present semester was held
last Saturday evening, and was well
attended. The president being ab
sent, Vice-PresidentMagee presided.
Owing to the large amount of urgent
business to be transacted, the lit
erary program was dispensed with.
The following officers were elected
to serve during the ensuing college
year: president, E. Delos Magee;
vice president, A. C. Trumbo: secre
tary, 0. J. Newman; treasurer, Sam
uel Piatt.
The Alpha is in a most prosperous
condition, its members are earnest
and enthusiastic in their work and
will, no doubt, carry the society for
ward with renewed zeal ou their
return next September.
