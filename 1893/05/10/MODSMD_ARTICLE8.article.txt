# Old English Ballads.
## 
### 
A benefit entertainment will be
given to-morrow evening, in the
chapel, by Mrs. Smith and Prof.
Lathrop. The program will consist
of singing old ballads. Ad
mission, twenty-five cents. Tickets
may be procured at room 11 Roble
Hall, room 85 Encina Hall, or at the
Co-operative store.
