# Miss Aus der Ohe.
## 
### 
Prof. Goibel received a telegram
from Miss Aus der Ohe this morn
ing stating that she would come
next Wednesday evening. Now that
she is comiug every one should make
arrangements to attend. Tickets
will be fifty cents to students, and
one dollar to faculty and outsiders.
They will be on sale soon.
