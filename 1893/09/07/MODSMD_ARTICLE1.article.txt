# Student Body.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the student
body will be held to-morrow afternoon
at two o'clock sharp. This will be one
of the most important meetings of the
year, and it is highly necessary that
every oue should be present who pos
sibly can. Besides much other im
portant business to be transacted, live
vacancies on the Hoard of Athletic
Directors must be filled immediately, in
order that football work may not be
hindered It is the duty of all to be
present in the chapel to morrow after
noon.
