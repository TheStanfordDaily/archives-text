# Tennis.
## 
### 
Considerable business was transacted
at the meeting of the Tennis Association
yesterday. Critchlow, '04, Bumiller,
'07, and Hazzard, '07, were appointed
to draft a constitution. After the
adoption of the Wright <fc Ditson ball
Thompson was appointed to purchase
six dozen. Chase was instructed to see
what arrangements could be made to
secure the use of two of the tennis
courts.
