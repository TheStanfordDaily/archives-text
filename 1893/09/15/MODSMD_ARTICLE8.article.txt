# NEW COACH AT BERKELEY AND OAKLAND.
## William Norton the Lucky Man. 
### 
William Norton of Yale has been
chosen to coach the Reliance football
club of Oakland, and the Berkeley
eleven. He played end on the varsity
eleven in the lighter games last season,
and was substitute for hall - and full
backs in the great Harvard-I'rincetou
games. He was highly recommended
by both McClung and Ileffelfinger At
present he is coaching the eleven at
Dartmouth.
