# An Organization Assured at Last.
## 
### 
The registrar's office presented a
lively appearance today and Mr. John
son was very busy handing out registra
tion cards. Over seventy-five students
have registered for military science,
thus securing the organization of a
military company.
To Messrs. Buxton, Andrade and
J. C. White belongs much of the
credit of securing the organization.
After interviewing Lieut. Oyster last
Saturday evening, they returned to the
hall and solicited names for the com
pany Over one hundred were secured
and most will register before tomor
row.
