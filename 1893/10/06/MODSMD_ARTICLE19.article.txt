# Untitled Article
## 
### 
With characteristic enterprise the
Cornell Daily Sun issued football and
crew supplements containing picture*
of the leading Cornell football men and
articles of interest to football players
and oarsmen generally. Manager Johan
son states that sixty men will be kept
in constant training this year and that
four elevens will play every afternoon
This looks like business for Cornell.
