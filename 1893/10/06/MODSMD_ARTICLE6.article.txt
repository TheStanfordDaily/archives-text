# At the Training Table.
## 
### 
The following men took their first
meal at the training table this morning:
Football men: Leadbetter, Code, Ken
nedy, Whitehouse, Wilson, Franken
heimer, Andrade, McKay, P. Downing,
C. Downing, Thomas, Lewis, Field,
J. IS. Rice, Walton, Flarrelson, A. IS.
Spalding, Burnett, Manager Maynard
and Bliss; track men, Fry and Bern
hard.
The men are now put on a diet
regulated by Bliss, and are all highly
pleased. Manager Maynard stales
that other men will be placed at the
table as soon as they begin to attend
the practice games regularly.
