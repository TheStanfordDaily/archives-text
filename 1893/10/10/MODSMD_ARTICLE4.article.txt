# Roble Athletics.
## 
### 
The following officers have been
elected by the Women's Association:
Miss Mabel Ilolsclaw, president; Miss
Stella Rose, vice-president; Miss M. F.
Dole, secretary; Miss B M. Freeman,
treasurer; Misses Ilolsclaw, Ay res,
Shelley, Hartley, Little, Polhemus,
Cushman, directors. The constitution
will be left in the gymnasium, where all
those wishing to join the association
may sign it.
