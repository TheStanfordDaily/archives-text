# Beta Theta Pi Makes the Ninth Here.
## The 						Geological Club. 
### 
At the meeting of the club last Fri
day evening, 11. S. Stark gave an in
structive talk on the metallurgy of gold
and silver. The manner of sampling
the ores and charging the furnace, the
fluxes used, and all the details needful
to observe in obtaining the refined
metals from their ores were explained.
Drawings of a furnace, samples of
ores, and products of the furnace at dif
ferent. stages and conditions were on
exhibition.
