# Mrs. Stanford Back.
## 
### 
.Mis Stanford has returned from her
eastern trip, arriving in San Francisco
Sunday morning, ller train was de
layed nearly a day on the road. Mrs.
Stanford visited the campus this after
noon, and will return to the city this
evening, ller stay in Washington was
made pleasant by much kind treatment
and attention from the Senators. Mrs.
Stanford says that she has now left
Washington for good.
