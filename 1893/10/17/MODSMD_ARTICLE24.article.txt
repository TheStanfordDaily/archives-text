# Musical Notes.
## 
### 
The piano in Enoina reception room
needs tuning very badly.
The Glee Club is arranging an attrac
tive program for its appcarance at
Stockton next Saturday.
The Mandolin Club has settled down
to regular systematic practicc. It will
soon surpass all its former efforts, owing
to increase of numbers and the infusion
of new energy into all its members.
The Military Band is making notice
able improvement. It is expected that
the band will soon be in form to accom
pany the squads while drilling. Leadjr
Kaufman deserves great praise for the
activity and interest, he has shown in
the organization and improvement of
the band.
