# A
						Lecture on Friday Evening.
## 
### 
Mrs. Mary Clement Leavitt, the
"around-fhe-world" missionary of the
Women's Christian Temperance Union,
will lecture in the chapel on Friday
evening of this week, commencing at
7 pin. The subject will be her work
while abroad All are cordially in
vited to be present. A collection will
be taken for the benefit of the \V. C.
T. U. work.
