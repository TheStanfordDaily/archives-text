# Special Rates to San Francisco Tomorrow
## 
### 
A telegram from Mr. 11. 11 Judah to
Dr. Jordan this afternoon announces
that the S P. Co. will send out one of
its ferry boats to accommodate peo
ple wishing to see the launching of the
"Oregon." Round trip tickets from
Palo Alto, including steamer ride,
$1 75. The steamer will leave Market
street, slip at (Ia m. I'roi. Smith de
sires all who have received invitations,
and who intend to go upon the steamer
to turn over their invitations to those
desiring to go to the shipyard.
