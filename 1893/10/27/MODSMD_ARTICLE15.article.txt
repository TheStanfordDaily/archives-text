# College Notes.
## 
### 
Prof. Hernadotte Perrin, formerly
Adalbert College, has been made pro
fessor of Greek at Yale.
Miss Edith J. Claypole of Akron.
Ohio, was the only woman to receive
the degree of master of science from
Cornell University, she having passed
her examinations "with tlie highest
distinction."
