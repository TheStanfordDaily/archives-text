# Y. M. C. A. State Convention.
## 
### 
The annual state convention of the
Y. M. C. A. will be held at Stockton
from Nov. 8 to 12 inclusive.
On Saturday, Nov. 11, there will be
a scries of athletic events in conncction
with the gathering. The man averag
ing best in a pentathlon made up of a
100 yard dash, throwing 12-lb. hammer,
running high jump, pole vault, and
mile run, will receive a gold medal. As
many as can should attend this conven
tion and take part in the athletic events.
Reduced rates will he secured for all
delegates.
