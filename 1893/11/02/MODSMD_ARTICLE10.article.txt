# Reception Tomorrow Evening.
## 
### 
The reception tomorrow evening in
the gymnasium will begin at 7:80, at
which time the music from San Fran
cisco will arrive. There will bo no
dancing. Every member of the Class
of '96 is expected to be present and do
his best to entertain the Class of '97.
