# Quads.
## 
### 
C. Iv. Jenness read a report on the
Charities of San Francisco today before
the Economic Seminary.
There will be an entertainment at
the Palo Alto Presbyterian church Fri
day evening. Refreshments will be
served. Admission will be ten cents.
The Berkeley military company was
asked to take part in the Midwinter
Fair parade. They replied that they
would if the Stanford military company
would also.
