# Shower Baths for Encina Gymnasium.
## 
### 
Shower baths for the men's gymnas
ium are at last assured. Architect
Hodges has consented to furnish pipe
to the interior of the building. Dr.
Wood has jjiven five dollars to start the
subscription and each member of the
gym. classes has subscribed twenty-five
cents. The workmen will begin to
morrow. About $45 has been col
lected, and at least three baths will be
fitted up, and probably several more.
