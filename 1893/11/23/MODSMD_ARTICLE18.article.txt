# A 						Timely Warning.
## 
### 
The crowd which * assembled in the
lobhy of the fourth floor in Encina last
night to celebrate the adoption of the!
long looked for freshman yell leads us
to call attention to the fact that the J
baunister railing between the stair
landings on that floor is very insecure,
and it is fortunate that it was not
broken down by the crowd pushing
against it last night. If such a thing
had occured a horrible accident would
have resulted, fora score of men would
have been precipitated to the lirst floor,
and few, if any, would have escaped
without serious injury.
Such an accident is not at all im
probable, especially when the lobby is
crowded, and we earnestly request the
proper authorities to see if something
cannot be done to strengthen the railing.
