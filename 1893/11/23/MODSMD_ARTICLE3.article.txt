# Football Notes.
## 
### 
Oberlin has not been defeated this
season.
The faculty of Rutgers have ordered
the football team to cancel all of its
engagements, as only two members have
obtained satisfactory grade in their
classes.
For the Yale-Harvard game, which
takes place at Springfield, Mass , Nov.
25, the seating capacity will be in
creased from 10,000 to 25,000, By
mutual agreement, Harvard will play
its university eleven, while Yale will
play according to its undergraduate
rule.
