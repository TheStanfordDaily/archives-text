# Untitled Article

Genebal dissatisfaction has been
expressed because the football manage
ment has made no provision to take our
Band to the football game next Thurs
day. It was caused chiefly by the know
ledge of the fact that the U. C. band
will be at the game and do what they
can to urge their team on to victory
Our Band of forty pieces, arrayed in
their uew uniforms, would so far
eclipse the Berkeley baud of only six
teen pieces that it seems as though the
student body could well afford to allow
an expenditure of forty or fifty dollars
of athletic money in order to show
people the contrast.
Besides this it would help stir up
enthusiasm which is such a necessary
adjunct to a football game. In a battle
it is said that men fight with a vim
that can be incited in no other way
than by martial music. Why not mix
a little of this with football and watch
the effect?
