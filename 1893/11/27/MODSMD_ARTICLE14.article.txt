# '96 Meeting.
## 
### 
A special meeting of the Class of '96
will be held in the chapel at 1:30 p m.
tomorrow (Tuesday). The question of
a class annual will he introduced. A
report of class finances will be made by
the treasurer. As these topics are of
special interest, a full attendance is re
quested. George F.Vanderveer,
Pres. '96.
