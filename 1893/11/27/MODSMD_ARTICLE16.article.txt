At the meeting of the Geological
Club, last Friday evening, Bert Hoover
discussed the general character of the
geological formations of western Oregon,
and F. M. Anderson spoke upon the
lava beds of eastern Oregon.

Francisco Tool Company and is oue of
the directors of the Bay City Wheel
men. In reference to the big suit re
cently settled, he said the club had
been compelled to take action in the
matter, and that personally he very
much regretted the whole affair.
