# Board of Directors.
## 
### 
The Hoard of Athletic Directors met
in Encina Hall last evening. The pro
position of the Maudolin Club to take a
trip north under the managemen of the
Athletic Association was laid ou the
table. Thos. 11. Pomeroy, '05, was
unanimously elected Athletic Treasurer,
and will immediately enter upon hi 3
duties for the ensuing year.
