# A Quintette.
## 
### 
A University quintette was organ
ized this week, and it is composed of
Carl Thomas, '95, Ist violin; Durand,
p.p., flute; Dully, sp., cello; Hirris,
'96, pianist, and L. W. Chase, '05, 2d
violin. Very little work will be at
tempted this semester, but it is hoped
that much can be accomplished next
semester. Durand has a genuine
lioehm flute and Dully has an Amati
cello. The members are musicians of
recognized ability and are bound to
make the organization one of the most
popular in the University. They will
play only high class music.
