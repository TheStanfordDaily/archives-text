# Athletics at the Midwinter Fair.
## 
### 
A number of football games will be
played at the Midwinter Fair. Associ
ation games will be played until ar
rangements are made for the teams of
the other forms, Gaelic and Rugby.
The first game of association football
will be played on next Sunday, and the
other series will commence a week later.
The fodtball grounds are said to be in
good condition.
Some feature of athletic entertain
ment will be in progress during the
whole day. At first the games will be
played on Sunday, but later Saturday
matches will be arranged. Work has
been commenced on the grand stand,
which will be 300 by 100 feet aud will
overlook the athletic grounds.
