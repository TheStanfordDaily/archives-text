# Meeting of "Sequoia" Directors.
## 
### 
The Sequoia Board of Directors will
meet in room 18, quad., Thursday. Jan.
11, at 3 p.m. The purpose of the meet
ing is to consider the resignation of
Mr. Chauiberliu as editor-in-chief
By order of Prof. Woodruff,
per G. H. Brown.
