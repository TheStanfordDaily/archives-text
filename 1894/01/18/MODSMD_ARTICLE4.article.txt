# The
						Stanford Glee Club.
## 
### 
A committee of three has been ap
pointed to draw up a new constitution
for the Glee Club, and officers for the
present semester will soon be elected
It is the intention of the club to
make a tour during the Easter vacation,
and dates have been secured in most of
the towns it will visit. The club
will give concerts in San Francisco,
Oakland, Santa Rosa, Dixon, Sacra
mento, Woodland, Stockton, and poss
ibly San Jose.
