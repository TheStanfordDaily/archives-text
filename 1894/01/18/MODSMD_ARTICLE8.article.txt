# A 						Compliment for Lieutenant Oyster.
## 
### 
Stanford University, Jan. 18, 1804
In view of the resignation by Lieut
enant Joseph Shillington Oyster of the
chair of Military Science and Tactics,
the Faculty of the University desire to
express their sense of the efficiency
with which Mr. Oyster has performed
the duties of his professorship and their
regrets at the loss of so esteemed a col
eague, and at the same time to tender
to him and Mrs. Oyster their best
wishes. Melville B. Anderson,
Frederic A. C. Perrine,
llobert E. Allardice,
Committee.
