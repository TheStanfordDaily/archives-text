Students should be careful of their
eyes. So far as study is concerned, the
last twenty minutes before the electric
lights are turned on in the evening
might better be spent in total darkness.
By having the dynamos started a little
earlier, much time, and also much abuse
of the eyesight, might be spared to
the student who works at this hour.
