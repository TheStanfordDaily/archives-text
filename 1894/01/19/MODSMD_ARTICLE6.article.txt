# The
						Twenty-fifth Anniversary of the University of Nebraska.
## 
### 
Professor G. E Howard, the head of
our-history department, has been in
vited by the authorities of the Univer
sity of Nebraska to deliver the oration
at the celebration of its twenty-fifth an
niversary, Feb 15th. The celebration
will occupy two days (Feb. 15th, and
16th), aud all the prominent men of
the state, besides many from abroad
will be present.
Prof. Howard is an alumnus of the
University of Nebraska, aud was also a
member of its faculty for thirteen years.
He will leave here on February 9th and
will return about the end of the month.
