# English 1b.
## 
### 
A class in Composition, designed for
those who wish to pass the examination
in English \b at the close of this se
mester, is being formed to meet in room
50 (physical lecture room), on Mouday,
Wednesday, and Friday at 10:30. All
wishing to join this class should see me
next Wednesday at hour and room
mentioned. E. D. Siiurtkr.
Jan. 22, 1894
