# Alpha.
## 
### 
Alpha's special Friday evening meet
ing was held as usual in room 71. The
speaker of the evening being absent,
the program was opened by the regular
debate. The question was, "Resolved,
That a Roman Catholic should dissolve
his allegiance to the Pope on becoming
a citizen of the United States." Brown
and Magec took the affirmative, and
Cotton and Durrcll the negative. By
a small majority vote the negative de
baters were awarded the victory. In
the business meeting the impromptu
speeches were made by O'Neil and
Hazzard.
