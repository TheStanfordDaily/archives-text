# At Other Colleges.
## 
### 
Henry Irving has accepted an in
vitation to address the Harvard Union.
Mr. L. F. Post, of the New York
Standard, is lecturing at the U. of M.
on the single tax theory.
The Harvard and Yale chess clubs are
playing two correspondence games,
each move being replied to within forty
eight hours after its receipt.
The board of directors of the Univer
sity of Wisconsin athletic association
will send out letters to all students,
alumni, members of the faculty, and
business men of Madison asking them
to contribute twenty live cents each to
help the association out ot its financial
straits.
