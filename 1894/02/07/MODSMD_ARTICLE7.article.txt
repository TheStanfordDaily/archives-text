# Quads.
## 
### 
All material for the "The Stanford
Quad" is due by the 20th of February.
Ah order has been issued prohibit
ing the holding of meetings of any
kind in the Encina Hall reception
room. The Athletic board meets in
room 96, third floor.
Miss Addams, who will lecture
at the University tomorrow, arrived
in San Francisco yesterday. Miss
Addams delivered her first lecture in
California yesterday afternoon at Berke
ley.
The dancing class recently organ
ized by De Wolfe and Tully is proving
quite a success. About twenty-five
students have already joined the class,
which meets every Friday evening in
Encina gymnasium.
