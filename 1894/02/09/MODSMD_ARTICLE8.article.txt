# Civil Engineers' Club.
## 
### 
The Civil Engineers* Club met lust
in Encina Hull. After a some
what prolonged business meeting the
following program was presented:
Types of American Bridges, Geo.
1). Stratton and 1). A. Porter; Pile
Driving and Strength of Driven Piles,
E. G. Hamilton; The Butte City Pipe
Liue, N. B. Knox. There was a full
attendance, the interest taken being
shown by the lively discussion following
each talk.
