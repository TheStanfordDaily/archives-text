# Quads.
## 
### 
The Class of' 94 will meet in room
94 Thursday at 1:20 p.m. The com
mittee on farces will report. An
amendment will be proposed at this
meeting to change section 2 article 111.
of the constitution in order that one
fifth instead of one third of the enrolled
members shall constitute a quorum.
The following persons gave reports
in Economics 1 today: In the 8:30
division, S. W. Charles, on the mining
controversy in the Sacramento valley;
in the 9:30 division, Lake, '97, on the
banking system, and T. H. Williams,
'96, on the silver question; in the 2:00
division, Arnold, '96, on the repeal of
the Sherman act.
