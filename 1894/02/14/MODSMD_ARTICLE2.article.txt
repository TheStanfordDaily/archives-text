# New Professor in Economics
## 
### 
Mrs. Mary Roberts Smith has been
appointed assistant professor of econ
omics. Mrs. Smith is a graduate of
Cornell, taking the degree of Ph B. in
1880 and M. S. in 1882. She taught
history in the Washington, 1) 0., high
school 1882-84, and in 1884 established
a private school for young ladies in
Cleveland. From 1886 to 1889 she
