# The
						Stanford University Chess Club.
## 
### 
The Chess Club met in the chapel
yesterday with an attendance of about
twenty. A constitution was submitted
by the committee appointed last week,
and was adopted with one or two amend
ments. It is proposed to hold tourna
ments within the club, and to make
arrangements for contests with outside
colleges and other organizations.
The following officers were elected
for the remainder of the semester:
president, C. Magee, '95; vice-president,
Sox, '04; and secretary and treasurer,
Morton, '05. These, with Vanderveer,
'Ofi, and Johnson, '06, form the ex
ecutive committee. The meeting ad
journed until next Tuesday at 5 p.m.
