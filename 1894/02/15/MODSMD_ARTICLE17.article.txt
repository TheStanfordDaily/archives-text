# Palo Alto Notes.
## 
### 
There will be a concert given iu
Nortree Hall, Palo Alto, next Friday
evening for the benefit of the local
Y M.C.A. Among those giving the en
tertainment will be Mrs. A. VV. Smith,
Mrs. E. M. Pease, Miss Lucy Fietcher,
Miss Ellen Lowell, and Prof. Camp
bell. The admission charge will be
twenty-five cents.
The population of the Mayfield town
ship, in which is located Palo Alto and
the University, has justified the appoint
ment of another justice of the peace.
