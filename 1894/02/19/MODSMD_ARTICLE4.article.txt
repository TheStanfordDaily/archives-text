Prof. Nathan Abbott, A. 8., LL.B.
Prof. Abbott graduated at Yale in
1870, and in 1880 received the degree
of bachelor of law at Boston University.
From 1880 to 1890 he practiced law
in Boston, establishing an enviable
reputation as a specialist on wills and
sales. In 1891-92 he held a professor
ship in the University of Michigan.
Since then he has been professor of
law in Northwestern University.
