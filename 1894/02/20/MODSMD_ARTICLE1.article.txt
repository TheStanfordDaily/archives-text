# The
						Prize Poem.
## 
### 
The judges on the prize poem eon
test of "The Stanford Quad" have
awarded the prize to William J. Neidig,
'96, fora poem entitled "Adrift." It
contains about seventy-five lines of
iambic tetrameter. Professors Iludeon,
Lathrop, and Woodruff were the judges.
The poem will be illustrated by Prof.
Bolton Coit Brown.
