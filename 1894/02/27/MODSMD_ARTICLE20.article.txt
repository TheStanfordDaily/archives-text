# Communication.
## 
### 
Editor Daily Palo Alto:
It is desired that the young women
should have opportunity for rowing on
Lagunita. It is therefore suggested
that the young men refrain from bath
ing there unless in suitable costume or
else by special arrangement as todays
David S. Jordan, President.
