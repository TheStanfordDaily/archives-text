A large crowd witnessed some good
tennis at the Enema courts yesterday
afternoon. The contests in singles
between Bumiller and Magee, Watsou
and Badlej, and Derrick and Turner,
were especially interesting. In doubles
most of the interest ceutred in the con
test of Bumiller and Cochran against
Morton and Cotton. The former won,
6-2, 6-4. The event of the day, how
ever, was the contest in singles between
Duraud and Packard. Packard won
after a hard struggle, 6-4, 4-6, 8-6.
