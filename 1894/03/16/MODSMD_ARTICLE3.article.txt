# Yesterday's Baseball Game at San Mateo.
## 
### 
The Varsity drove up to San Mateo
yesterday afternoon and played a
spirited though rather one sided practice
game with the nine at St. Matthew's
Hall. The Stanford players hit the
hall hard and stole bases with impunity.
The opposing team made but two hits
off Weldon's delivery. The team was
royally entertained, returning home
after dining at the school.
