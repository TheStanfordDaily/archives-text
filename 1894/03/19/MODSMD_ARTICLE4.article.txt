# Second Inter-Society Debate.
## 
### 
A very lurge and appreciative audi
ence greeted the second of the series of
inter society debates in the chapel last
Saturday evening, and by Tuesday
evening most of the students will have
become acquainted with the merits of
those who will represent us in the great
debate in San Francisco. The quest'on
for the evening was, "Hesolved, That
the United States should annex Canada
provided she can do so peaceably."
A. M. Cathcart, '9(3, and Dougherty,
'95, supported the affirmative against
G. R. Hill, '97. and B. F. Bledsoe,
'90, for the negative. Prof. Fairclough
pri sided.
