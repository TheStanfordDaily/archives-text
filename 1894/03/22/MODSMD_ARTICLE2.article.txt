# Glee Club in San Francisco.
## 
### 
The Glee Club went to San Francisco
this afternoon, where they will present :!
concert in the Metropolitan Teuiole
under the auspices of the ladies of Dr.
McKenzie's church. During the three
years of Stanford's existence this is the
first appearance of any of her musical
organizations in the city. It has been
well advertised, and from all indications
it will meet with success. After sing
ing at the Fair tomorrow the club will
start upon its Faster tour.
