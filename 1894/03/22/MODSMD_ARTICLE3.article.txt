# Prof. Abbott Has Gone.
## 
### 
With this morning's lecture before
the Law class Nathan Abbott, head of
the law department, finished his course
of lectures here. He left for San Fran
cisco, and will start for the east tomor
row.
No more popular or well liked man

has ever been connected with the fac
ulty. On all sides a general expression
of regret at his departure has been heard
from those who had occasion to hear his
lectures either before the Law class or to
the engineers.
