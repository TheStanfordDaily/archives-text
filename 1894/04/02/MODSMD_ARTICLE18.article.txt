# News of the Day.
## 
### 
Pres. Andrews of Brown University,
who delivered two addresses last week
before the Political and Economic Con
gress of the Midwinter Fair, left Sat
urday for the East.
A serious riot which caused the death
of over twenty people and wounded
twice as many more, was occasioned in
Darlington, S. 0 , Saturday, in an at
tempt to enforce one of the provisions
of the liquor dispensary law of that
state.
Dispatches from Auckland, New
Zealand, announce a serious uprising
in Samoa. The American chief justice
compelled two imprisoned native chiefs
to work on the roads like ordinary con
victs, which so angered the natives that
they rose in rebellion.
