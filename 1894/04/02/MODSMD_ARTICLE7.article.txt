# Stanford Defeated.
## 
### 
Less than a hundred persons wit
nessed the intercollegiate tenuis tourna
ment between the U. C. and Stanford
in East Oakland, Saturday afternoon,
and but ten Stanford students includ
ing the players were present. In the
first-place singles Sanborn, U. C '94,
defeated Bumiller, '97, 6-3, 6-0, 6-1;
and Ellis, '97, lost to Gage, U. C. '97,
the second-place singles with the score
6-3, 6-2, 6-2. In the doubles San
born and liyxbee played against Durand,
gr., and Watson, '96, and defeated
them with the score 6-0, 6-1, 6-1.
All the Stanford players cxcept Ellis
played in far from their usual form, the
games being generally very poorly con
tested.
