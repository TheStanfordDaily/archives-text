# Memorial Day Exercises.
## 
### 
The committee of the faculty having
in charge the program for the Memorial
Day exercises have completed the pro-
gram for that day. It will consist of
music by several University organiza
tions, and papers to he read by stu
dents.
The faculty committee has selected
Miss Stadtmuller, '95, H. H. Brown,
'96, and Benj. F. Bledsoe, '96, to read
papers upon that day. The music will
be furnished by the Glee Club, the
String Quartet, and probably by the
Roble Glee Club.
