# News of the Day.
## 
### 
The new constitution of Hawaii
is almost ready to be submitted.
Senator Hill lias been denounced
by prominent Democrats all over
the coast for his late speech.
David Dudley Field, brother of
Cyrus \V. and Stephen J. Field and
the most eminent law reformer of
the century, is dead.
Fifteen hundred miles of the
Great Northern Railroad are tied
up by a strike. Every employe
on the line is concerned.
