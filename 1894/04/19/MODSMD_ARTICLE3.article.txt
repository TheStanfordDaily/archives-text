# Preliminary Field Day.
## 
### 
A preliminary field day will be held
Saturday for the purpose of selecting
representatives for the corning inter
collegiate field day. The following
events will be contented upon the oval
at 11a. m.: Shot-put, hammer throw,
and running broad jump. At. 2p. m.
at the stock farm the remaining events,
consisting of a 100 yard dash, 220-yard
dash, one mile, half mile, quarter mile,
the one mile walk and the two-mile
bicycle race, will be contested En
tries for these events should be handed
to Captain Bernhard as soon as possible.
