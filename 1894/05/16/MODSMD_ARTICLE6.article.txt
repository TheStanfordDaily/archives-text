# The 						Manager of the "Sequoia" Tenders His Resignation.
## 
### 
Prof. E. 11. Woodruff:
In consideration of other work for
next year, 1 do not think 1 can devote
the time to the management of the
Sequoia that it, requires to make it a
success. Therefore, I send in my
resignation as business mauager. With
thanks to the Board of Directors, I am,
Yours respectfully, O. V. Eaton.

The Board of Control of the Sequoia
will meet Friday, May 18, at 1 :30 p.m
in room 10 to take action upon the
above resignation, and to elect a busi
ness manager lor the year 1894-95.
E 11. Woodruff.
