# Untitled Article
## 
### 
It is the purpose of this issue to re
late the occurences of Commencement
week, and if rather more notice has
been given to social matters than would
seem consistent with that devoted to
other subjects it has been with the hope
that more interest may be created in a
season of the year which, with us, is too
generally left to those alone whom a
personal interest compels to linger at
the University.
