After many unsuccessful attempts
by the business manager to get a rail
road pass from the authorities in charge
of such things, it was very gratifying
to receive by mail a complimentary one
from Mrs. Stanford herself. It is also
pleasing to be assured by such courte
sies as these that Mrs. Stanford has a
deep interest not only in the students
themselves, but also in their enterprises
and hobbies.
