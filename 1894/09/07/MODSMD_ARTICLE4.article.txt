# The 						Freshmen Meet.
## 
### 
The <Mass of 98 held its first meet
ing in the chapel this afternoon at two
o'clock. The sophomores made some
slight disturbance which did not inter
fere with the business to any extent.
J. P Bernard, '90, called the meeting
to order The nominees for temporary
president of the class were John Irwin
and E. E. Hobiusou. Mr. Kobinson
was elected by a standing vote Steu
art W. Cotton was elected tempor
ary secretary without opposition. Two
committees were resolved upon, one on
constitution and the other ou class yell.
