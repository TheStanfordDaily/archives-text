# Mandolin Club Notice.
## 
### 
All students who play the guitar or
the mandolin and wishing to try for
positions in the Mandolin Club will
please report with their instruments at
room 187, Kneina IJ all. Mandolin
players will be on hand at 1:30 p.m.
guitar players at 5 p tn., Friday
Sept. 14. It is hoped that all who
make any pretensions whatever of
playing either of the instruments will
make it a point to be present.
W B. Wells, Leader.
