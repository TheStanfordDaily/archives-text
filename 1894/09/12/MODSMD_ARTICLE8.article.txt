# News of the Day.
## 
### 
The Grand Army of the Republic is
holding its encampment at Pittsburg.
The returns from the recent election
in Maine give the Republicans a plural
ity of at least 38,000.
Pio Pico, the last Mexican governor of
California, died at Los Angeles yesterday
at the age of ninety-four.
An important treaty has been negoti
ated between Japan and Corea. The
treaty consists of three articles, and
defines the relations of the two countries
towards each other and towards China.
The treaty will terminate as soon as
peace is declared between China and
Japan.
