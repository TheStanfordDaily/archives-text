# The 						Tennis Tournament.
## 
### 
The prizes in the tennis tournament
offered bj Clabrough, Goleher & Co. of
Sun Francisco are: First in singles,
'•clears Special" tennis racket; first in
doubles, two pairs handsome Oxford
russett tennis shoes.
The winners in ibis tournament will
hold the championship for the fall term
and will represent the University in all
outside matches. Up to this afternoon
there had been twenty-six entries in
singles and nix in doublet). The tour
nament opens this coming Saturday.
