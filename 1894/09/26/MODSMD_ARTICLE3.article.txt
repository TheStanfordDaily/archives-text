# '98 Meeting.
## 
### 
The meeting held by the Class of'9B
in the chapel this afternoon was enthusi
astic, though not very largely attended.
The only business transacted was the
election of officers for the present se
mester. The following persons were
chosen: President, Keesling; vice
president, Sweitzer; secretary, Miss
McCray; treasurer, Adams; historian,
Miss Mcintosh; sergeant-at-arms, Dole;
baseball Durham.
