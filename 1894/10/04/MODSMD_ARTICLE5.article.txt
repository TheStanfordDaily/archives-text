# Civil Engineering Club.
## 
### 
At 9 p.m. the above club held its
regular fortnightly meeting with Pres.
Dart in the chair. All members were
present.
A talk was given by G. D Stratton,
'95, on " The Purification of Water
Supply in American Cities." This was
followed by a description of "An Ac
curate Rase Line Measurement" by D.
A. Porter, '95. This paper, which was
accompanied by carefully prepared draw
ings, described in detail the method
pursued by the United States Coast and
Geodetic Survey.
