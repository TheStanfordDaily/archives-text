# Friday's Practice.
## 
### 
The practice was spirited and full of
pretty plays. It was most noticeable
for its punting. Soper, '97, distin
guished himself by a number of long
kicks. When this man has acquired a
better knowledge of the game, much
more will be heard of him behind the
line. On the second eleven, Allen and
Webster made two long runs. The
second eleven begin* to show the bene
fit of its practice. The men of the
Varsity are by far the best inteferers.
The members of other teams would do
well to watch their interference, and
imitate it.
