# Alpha.
## 
### 
Alpha was called to order at 7:45
last Saturday evening with Pres. Piatt
in the chair. The principal feature of
the literary program was the excellent
address delivered by Dr. Boss. His
remarks were replete with good sug
gestions and words of encouragement
fur the society, and proved a rare treat
to those fortunate enough to hear them.
