# Last Night's Farce.
## 
### 
The farce, " The Mouse-Trap," by
VV. I>. Howells, was presented last night
in Nortrcc Hall, Palo Alto, under the
auspices of the. Woman's Guild of All-
Saints Mission. 8. E. Simmons took
the part of Mr. Campbell, and Miss
Maud Jones did admirably as Amy
Roberts. At the end of the farce,
the floor was cleared and dancing
indulged in until a late hour. The re
freshments were a feature of the even
ing's eutertainment Many students
were present; the only drawback to the
evening's pleasure was the over-crowded
state of the floor.
