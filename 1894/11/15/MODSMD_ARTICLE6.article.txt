# A 						Lecture on the War in the East.
## 
### 
Next Tuesday evening's lecture will
he given by Mr. Ely 'J'. Sheppard of
San Francisco, on the timely subject,
" The War in Corea." Mr. Shep
pard is exceedingly well qualified to
speak with authority on Oriental affairs.
For over ten years he served on the
IJ. 8. Diplomatic Corps in China and
Japan. He has acted as an adviser in
international law to the Japanese gov
ernment, is personally acquainted with
Li hung Chang, and his name is well
known in diplomatic circles in the
Orient. His lecture should possess un
usual interest.
