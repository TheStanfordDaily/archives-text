# Quads.
## 
### 
W. H. McCord, '96, has returned
to the University.
Gill and Pringle acted as umpire and
referee in yesterday's game.
A young lady cyclist created quite a
sensation by appearing in bloomers in
the quadrangle this morning.
The good work of Jack Sherrard,
Olympic's old captain, at right eud
yesterday, attracted much notice.
The large metal - working lathe
which has been lying idle for some
time in the blacksmith shop, is
being set up in the wood-working de
partment.
