# A 						Sermon by Berkeley's Coach.
## 
### 
The subject of Rev. C. O. Gill's ser
mon in the chapel next Sunday will be
" Foreign Missions from a Y. M. C. A.
Standpoint." The topic is especially
timely as the Y. M. C. A. conference
for the colleges of northern California
will be held here Saturday and Sunday,
and many visiting Y. M. C. A. workers
will be present.
