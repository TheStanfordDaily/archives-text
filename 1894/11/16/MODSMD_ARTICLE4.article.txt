# A 						Benefit to Miss Dawson.
## 
### 
The authors of the Pacific slope
will give a benefit in San Fran
cisco, Nov. 20, to Emma Frances
Dawson, a talented but little known
writer of this coast. The program
will comprise readings by Joaquin
Miller, Miss Rierce, Miss Colbrith, and
our own Professor Anderson. To
arouse interest, Professor Anderson will
give a reading from Miss Dawson's
works in Ruble parlors Sunday even
ing at 8:15 o'clock. All interested are
invited.
