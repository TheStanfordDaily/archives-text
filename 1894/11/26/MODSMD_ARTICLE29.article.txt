# News of the Day.
## 
### 
Jean Victor During, the French his
torian, died yesterday.
San Francisco's great Horse Show
will open Wednesday morning.
Dartmouth will play the Chicago
Athletic Association on Thanksgiving
Day.
London advices from Che Foo report
the fall of Port Arthur, China's strong
hold, after eighteen hours fighting.
Football scores Saturday: Yale 12,
Harvard 4; Michigan 12, Cornell 4;
Brown 20, Dartmouth 9; Lehigh 11,
Lafayette 8.
Italy is preparing to send a fleet
against Brazil on account of the latter
country refusing to arbitrate a pend
ing question.
