# Special Rates to Students.
## 
### 
The management of Hotel Pleasanton,
cor. Jones ami Sutter Sts., San Fran
cisco, lias been induced to give special
rates to Stanford students, beginning
with Thanksgiving Day. The rates for
board and room per day will be from
two dollars tip.
