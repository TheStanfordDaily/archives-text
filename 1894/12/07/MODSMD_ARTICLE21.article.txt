# College Notes.
## 
### 
It is reported that Harvard's observ
atory in l'eru has been badly damaged
by natives.
At Rensselaer the annual sophomore
freshman eane rush was advertised.
About three hundred people witnessed
the rush, and the gate receipts amounted
to $20.
A literary society has been formed at
Harvard which will be known as the
Serapion. Weekly meetings will be
held for the discussion of English au
thors of the 10th century. The mem
bership is limited to twelve.
