# Student Body Meeting.
## 
### 
At, the meeting of the Student Body
held in the chapel this afternoon, it was
voted to place the election of the trus
tees of the Students' Guild in the hands
of the Kxecutivc Committee. The
Committee will meet on Tuesday for
tiiat purpose. Other than this there
was no business transacted.
