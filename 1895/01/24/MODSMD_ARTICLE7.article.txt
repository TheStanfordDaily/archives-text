# Debate for the Carnot Medal.
## 
### 
It has been decided that the Inter
collegiate debate for the Carnot medal
will take place on April sth. Arrange
ments for the preliminary debates for
places in the final will begin soon.
One of the conditions upon which the
medal was given was that the subject
of the debate should be some live French
topic; therefore the contestants will dis
cuss "Was Casimir-l'erier justified in
resigning?"
