Notice to Students —Rooms Without
Board can be obtained in Encina Hall
at the following rates :
One person in double room $15 00
With room-mate 7 50
All rules which apply to boarders in
the Hall shall apply to those parties
who may take or occupy rooms without
board. George Adderson.
