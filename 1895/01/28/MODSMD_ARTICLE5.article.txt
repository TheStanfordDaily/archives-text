# Philolexian.
## 
### 
Philolexian was called to order at
7:30 Saturday, and following program
rendered: Debate, "Resolved, That cap
ital punishment should be abolished."
Affirmative, Walker, Iveesling; nega
tive, Switzer, Steinhardt. Impromptu
debate, "Should Alsace and Lorraine
be given back to France?"; Trwin and
Haas. Five-minute talk, ''Missions of
Lower California" ; Adams.
The debate was close and spirited.
After the literary program a short busi
ness meeting was held.
