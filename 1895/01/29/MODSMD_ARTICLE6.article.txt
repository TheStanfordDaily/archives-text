# Students' Guild Trustees.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Executive Com
mittee held this afternoon the following
were elected trustees of the Students'
Guild, to hold office till May: Donald,
'95, Delos Magee, '95, Arnold, '95,
Rice, '95, Miss Harriet Cory, '95, Miss
Lizzie Chapman, '95, and Dr. Wood.
