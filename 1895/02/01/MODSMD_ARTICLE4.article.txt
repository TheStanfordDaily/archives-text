# Dr. Jordan Home Again.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan and party have returned
from Mazatlan, Mexico, where they
spent the past month and a half, search
ins; for fossils, vertebrae, etc , and all
things of interest to the zoological
world. The party brought back many
specimens of ancient and modern zo
ological life, and report having had a
most delightful and instructive time.
