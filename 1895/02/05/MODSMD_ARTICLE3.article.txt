# A 						Treat For the Economics Seminary.
## 
### 
Dr. J. M. Stallard of Meulo Park
will present before the Economics iSemi
nary, Thursday, a paper on "Conditions
affecting the wage-earning power,"
which he read at one time before the
London Society of Arts.
In view of his experience on the
Manchester Hoard of Guardians, Dr.
Stallard will be interrogated regarding
the present tendencies in city govern,
ment.
The seminary meets Thursday at 3
o'clock in room 20. Visitors cordially
invited to attend.
