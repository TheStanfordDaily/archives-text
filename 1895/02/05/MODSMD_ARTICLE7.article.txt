# College Notes.
## 
### 
In the past twelve years Yale has
scored 5,624 in football to her oppon
ents' 119.
Yale and Princeton will give a joint
exhibition in gymnastics in the Yale
gymnasium during the second week of
February.
Columbia College has challenged
Cornell and Pennsylvania to row a tri
angular race in university eights at
New London, this year.
The Administrative Roard of Har
vard College announces that any stu
dent handing in written work not his
own will be separated from the college.
A "whisker club" has been formed
in the law school of the University of
Michigan. Each member binds him
self to increase his dignified appearance
by allowing his whiskers to grow un
touched by the razor. Twenty seniors
constitute the membership.
