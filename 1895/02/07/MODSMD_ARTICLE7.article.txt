# Entertainment in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
The ladies of All Saints' Guild of
Palo Alto are to give a mueicalc and
social in Notrce Ilall, Palo Alto, Thurs
day evening, February 14. Admission
will be fifty cents. Refreshments free.
Some of the best talent of the Univer
sity will participate in the exercises.
