Tickets for Dr. Iluweis' lecture will
be on sale at the President's office,

On account of the absencc of Dr.
Jordan, Mrs. Jordan , will not he "at
home" on Saturday evening of this
week.
The senior class in Hydraulics this
morning inaugurated a new custom,
that of appearing in the class-room in
dress suits. The event which called
forth all this formal array was the dual
debut of two of their number into swell
dom and the engineering world. The
recipient of the honor gave a report on
the existing water system of the Uni
versity and also suggested several
schemes for improvement.
