# Entertainment at Palo Alto.
## 
### 
The ladies of All Saints Guild, as
sisted by the young ladies of
will give a St. Valentine's entertain
ment at Nortree Hall, Palo Alto, next
Thursday evening for the benefit of the
new Episcopal church. A choice pro
gram will be rendered by the very best
musical talent in the University and
Palo Alto. The evening's pleasures will
conclude with a dance. Omnibuses
will be at the quadrangle to take all who
desire to attend. The program will be
announced later.
