# Meeting of '95 Council.
## 
### 
President Langford called the Coun
cil of the Senior claps to order at 1:45
this afternoon. Mr. liuxton, business
manager of the '05 Annual, submit
ted his report, which showed the Class
of '95 to be still indebted to the pub
lishers nearly $100. Messrs. Kimball,
Stratton, and Durrell were appointed
as a committee to devise ways and
means for removing the deficit.
