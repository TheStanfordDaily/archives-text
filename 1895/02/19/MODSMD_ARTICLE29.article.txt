# The 						Coif Club.
## 
### 
The Coif Club held its second regu
lar meeting Friday evening. The case
tried was one involving the question as
to whether title vests immediately upon
a judgment, or not until the satisfaction
of the judgment. The plaintiff was
represented by Messrs. Brandt and
Mills, and the defendant's side was
presented by Messrs. Jarman and Mal
colm; Messrs. Iluff, Clayton, and Wil
son acting as judges. After the court
was adjourned the Club was briefly ad
dressed by Proi. Abbott and Mr. Frost.
A short business meeting was held, after
which the Club adjourned.
