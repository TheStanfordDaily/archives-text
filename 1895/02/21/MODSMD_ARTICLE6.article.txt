# Sophomore Cotillion.
## 
### 
The Sophomore Cotillion tomorrow
evening will begin at 8 o'clock sharp,
and all who are planning to attend
should make their arrangements ac
cordingly. It has been decided to
have twelve dances and no extras in
part one. The cotillion will occupy
part two, and will last until 1 o'clock.
Carriages may be ordered for that hour.
