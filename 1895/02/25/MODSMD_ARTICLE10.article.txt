Why have our embryo lawyers given
up the mock trials in Encina? The
practice gained in cases of this kind
is more valuable in many ways than
that afforded by the law societies; it
Sets nearer to court work and in
volves the handling of actual wit
nesses Moreover, it is the best and
Mirest way of enforcing the rulos of
the hall.
