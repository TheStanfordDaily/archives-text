# Program of the Olympic Games.
## 
### 
The Gre<k committee, presided over
by the Prince llegal, has just sent out
the official program of the Olympic

games to be played in Athens, April 5
to 12, 18%. The athletic sports will
consist of foot-races at distances of 100,
400, 800, 1,200 metres, long jumping,
his:h jumping, and putting the shot.
$10,000 has been raised by the com
mittee for cleaning the rubbish from
the ancient race course, where these
contests will be held. There will be a
cross-country run of 48 kilometres over
the course followed by the legendary
soldiers of Marathon, from the battle
held to Athens. The gymnastic exer
cises will be the usual feats of the pres
ent time, such as trapeze performances,
work on the parallel bars, etc. Wrest
ling will hold an important place on the
program, but boxing has been ruled out
by the committee. There will also be
shooting, swimming and boat, bicycle,
and horse racing. Cricket, lawn tenuis,
and football matches will also be played.
Amateurs only will be allowed to com
pete in all these events. In addition
there will be a series of fencing contests
open to professionals.—l'rincetunvm.
