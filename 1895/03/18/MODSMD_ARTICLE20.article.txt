# Philological Association.
## 
### 
The next regular meeting of the
University Philological Association will
he held Thursday afternoon, March 21,
at 2 o'clock, in room 31. Papers will
he read by Prof. Pease on "The Greet
ing in the Correspondence of Cicero,"
and by Prof. Flugel on "Skcats' Edi
tion of Chaucer."
