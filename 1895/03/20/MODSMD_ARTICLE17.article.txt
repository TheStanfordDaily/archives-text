# News of the World.
## 
### 
Li Hung Chang, accompanied by
.Johu W. Foster, (China's peace com
missioner, arrived at Shimonoseki,
Japan, yesterday.
The eleventh semi-annual exhibition
of the California Stato Floral Society
will open within a week at Sin Fran
cisco. Prof. E. E. Smith is chairman
of the committee of arrangements.
The missing cruiser lleyna llegenta,
the finest in the Spanish navy, has
been fouud in shoal water off a port
near the Straits of Gibraltar. It is
supposed she went down with all on
board.
Secretary Gresham has requested
Presideut Dole of Hawaii to recall
Minister Thurston on the ground that
Thurston has violated diplomatic eti
quette by giving out certain news to
the papers before reporting it to the
State department.
