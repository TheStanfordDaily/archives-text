# "'96 Junior Hop March."
## 
###

posed by Kaufman, '9G, and played
with fruch success at a recent entertain
ment, is on sale at the Co-Op. Com
petent musicians who have heard the
march have expressed themselves as
surprised that the University has such
excellent musical talent. Judging from
the sales made during the past few
days, the march is destined to become
quite popular.
