# '95 Handbook
## 
### 
The Students' Handbook, which is
issued annually by the University
Christian Association, will appear in
about two weeks. The committee in
charge, Miss Stadtmuller, '95, P. 11.
White, '95, and Laughlin, '90, have
re-written the eutire book and will en
large it by the addition of more pages.
The press-work is to be doue by the
Times' office in I'alo Alto, and two
thousand copies will be issued, which
will be left at the Registrar's office for
distribution.
