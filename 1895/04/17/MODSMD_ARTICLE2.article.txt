# Memorial Day.
## 
### 
The Memorial Day exercises are in
preparation. So far as has been de
termined upon, the program will include
a sketch of the four years of the Uni
versity's life by It, L. Donald; a
short history of the campus itself by
Roy Ballard; and the reading of an ap
propriate poem by some young lady not
yet selected. The exercises will be
Lield on Monday evening, the loth of
May, and will be of interest to all.
