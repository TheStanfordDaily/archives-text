# "Daily Palo Alto" Election.
## 
### 
The Board of Control of the Daily
Palo Alto met at 2 p.m. today in
room 20, quad, for the election of
editor-in-chief and business manager
for the ensuing semester. J. 11. Titn
tnons was elected by acclamation editor
in-chief. Stuart Cotton and George
B. Wilson were nominated for business
manager. The members of the Board
asked for time to consider these nomi
nations, and an adjournment was ac
cordingly taken for one week.
