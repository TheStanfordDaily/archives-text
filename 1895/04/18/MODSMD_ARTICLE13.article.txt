There seems to be a gtneml mis
understanding in regard to the own
ership of the Curnot medal. The
notion is prevalent that the winner
of the debate has the medal in his
charge for one year, whereas such is
not the case. The medal remains
the permanent property of the win
ner. A new medal is made each
year, a sum of money having been
Mot aside for the purpose.
