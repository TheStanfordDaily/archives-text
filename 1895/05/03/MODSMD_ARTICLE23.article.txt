# The
						Debaters Honored.
## 
### 
Messrs, Magee, Bledsoe, and Palmer
have been invited to a luncheon to be
given at the University Club tomorrow
noon by the judges of the Intercollegi
ate Debate in honor of the debaters.
Besides the six debaters and the hosts
—Judge Meyrick, and Messrs. Olney
nnd Page—Professor Gayley of U. C.,
Professor Abbott, aud Judge Morrow
will be preseut.
