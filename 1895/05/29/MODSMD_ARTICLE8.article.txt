# Additions to Theta Nu Epsilon.
## 
### 
On Friday morning the following '97
men appeared with Theta Nu Epsilon
pins: C B Diehl, H C Turner, C A
Thomas, Zeta Psi; F W Lake, W C
Trice, Pi J Green, Phi Delta Theta; J M
Ross, Delta Tau Delta; M S Porter.

In addition to the above the following
persons now absent from the University
are honorary members: R C Kirk, S R
Hofer, Phi Kappa Psi; AW Bumiller,
W A Greenwood, Phi Gamma Delta. I
The active members number about
fifteen men in the class of '98.
