# Alpha Notice.
## 
### 
The regular literary exercises
of Alpha Society will he held
Saturday evening, Sept. 14. The
program will he as follows: Speech,
Outeult; debate on question: "Re
solved, That national and state
legislators should vote according
to personal or representative opin
ion." Lawshe and Oskison will
take the affirmative side, O'Neil
and Wright the negative. Debat
ers, by mutual agreement, may
change places, but no change in
question may be made without
consulting the attorney.
(i. L. Williams, Attorney.
