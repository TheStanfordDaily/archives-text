# A
						Stanford Day.
## 
### 
Next Saturday is Stanford day
at the Mechanics' Fair in San
Francisco. In the evening Scheel's
Band will give a special program,
which will be comprised mainly of
Stanford music. "Hail, Stanford,
Hail" will be played for an opening
piece. Also some of the late
marches, the "Sophomore" and the
"Freshman" composed by A. G.
Kaufman, '90. Mr. Kaufman will
direct his own compositions. A
new composition called " Roble
Hall Schottische," composed by
Miss Alice E. Wheeler,' 97, will also
be played. A large number of
students should be present.
