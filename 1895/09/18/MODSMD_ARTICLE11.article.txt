###

the Knights Templar at Boston,
plans were discussed fora national
Masonic university. While it is
understood that no definite con
clusion was reached at that time,
yet the matter was regarded favor
ably. A tract of land in Ohio is
spoken of as the site, and fifty mil
lions of dollars as the endowment
of the new institution.
The Masons propose to conduct
the university on a strict financial
ha,sis, hut no son or daughter of a
master mason will he rejected for
