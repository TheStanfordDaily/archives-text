The manager of the '97 Annual
is compelled to hurry the comple
tion of lists necessary for procuring
a production of "As You Like It."
The organization cannot be induced
to appear unless six hundred names
can be procured, and that very
speedily. Endeavors will be made
to have the lists reach every stu
dent and faculty member, but it
anyone fails to receive a visit from
the circulators it is to be hoped he
will find time to notify the circula
tor of 11is wish to sign, thus helping
along a student enterprise.
