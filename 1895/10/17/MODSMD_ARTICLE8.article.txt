# Untitled Article
## 
### 
A settlement will probably be
made tonight of the much-vexed
question of an intercollegiate Fresh
man match. It is earnestly hoped
that a match can be arranged, for
it aids in creating a healthy college
spirit and enthusiasm, though for
some reasons it might be advisable
for Stanford to play no Freshman
game this year; but if a game is to
take place it should lie decided
upon at once, and the date fixed
early enough so there will be no in
terference with Varsity practice.
