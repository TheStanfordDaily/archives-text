# Nestorian.
## 
### 
The following is the program of
Nestorian Literary Society for
Nov. 2 :
Extemporaneous speech; oration,
Wardall,'97; extemporaneous de
hale, two members; regular debate:
"Resolved, That the New York Sun
day closing law should he repealed";
speakers on affirmative, Nutting,
'96, and Smith, '97; negative, Mc-
Donnell, '96, Howard, '98. Visitors
are invited.
F. S. Howard, Sec.
