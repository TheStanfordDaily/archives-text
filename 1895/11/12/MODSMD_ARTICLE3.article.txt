# Zoological Club.
## 
### 
A very interesting meeting of the
Zoological Club was held at Dr.
Gilbert's home last evening. AAV.
(ireely gave an account of his trip
in the Sierra Nevada Mountains.
He went with Mr. Price's party
collecting mammals. Dr. Gilbert's
talk on the distinction of salt
water fishes was especially interest
ing. He outlined the distinction of
those on this Coast with those
found at Panama where he and a
party of students expect to do
some work this winter.
