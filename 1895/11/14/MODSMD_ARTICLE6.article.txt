# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Executive Com
mittee was called this afternoon to
take action in regard to the Uni
versity Band. Wilbur, '96, moved
that one dollar and admission to
the Thanksgiving game be allowed
each bona fide member of the Band;
carried.
O'Neil, '97, moved that a student
body meeting be called in the near
future to elect two freshman mem
bers to the board of control of the
Daily Palo Alto. The motion
was carried, and accordingly a
meeting will be called for that pur
pose within a few days. Adjourned.
