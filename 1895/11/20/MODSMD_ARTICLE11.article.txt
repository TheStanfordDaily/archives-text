# Thanksgiving Services
## 
### 
Next Sunday morning a special
Thanksgiving service will be held
in the chapel. Dr. Little, Miss
Little, Prof. Mary Roberts Smith,
and ['. F. Abbott, '98, will sing.
Congregation singing of the old
hymns, led by a large choir, will be
a special feature of the services.
The Orchestra will play two selec
tions. Dr. Jordan will give a short
address on "Thanksgiving."
