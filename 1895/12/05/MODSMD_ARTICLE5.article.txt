# News of the World.
## 
### 
Hall Caine has returned to Eng
land.
England refuses to submit the
Venezuelan question to arbitration.
Spain has added four new gun
boats to her fleet in Cuban waters.
The overdue British ship Lord
Spencer has been given up for lost.
President Cleveland has been
chosen to settle the dispute be
tween Italy and Brazil.
The Sultan predicts a general
uprising of Mohammedans if the
Powers persist in sending gunboats
through the Dardanelles.
