# Botanical Club Organized
## 
### 
A meeting of the Botanical Club
was held at lloble last Wednes
day evening. A constitution was
adopted and the following officers
elected: President, F. H.Lamb;
secretary, Miss Ford; executive
committee, Misses Stokes and Mc-
Fadden and Messrs. Doane and
Knoche. A short talk upon nat
ural history societies was given by
Prof. Dudley, and Prof. Campbell
told of the German Botanical
Society. Mr. F. J. Jack read an in
teresting paper upon "The pollina
tion of Rihes speciosvm." An ex
cursion was planned for this month.
