# Elementary Entomology.
## 
### 
In the absence of Prof. .J. If.
Comstock, the lecture course in
Elementary Entomology offered by
him for next semester will be given
by Prof. Kellogg. Two lectures a
week will be given, the scope of the
course including the principles of
Zoological classification, the classi
fication of insects, their life his
tories and habits, and the elements
of economic entomology with spec
ial reference to the injurious in
sects of this Coast.
