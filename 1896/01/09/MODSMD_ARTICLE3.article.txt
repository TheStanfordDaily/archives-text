# New Society Formed.
## 
### 
At •> p.m. this afternoon a meet
ing was held by a few enthusiastic
students in order to organize a
"Chemistry Society." Lyon, '97,
was elected temporary chairman,
and the interests of the society were
discussed. A committee on pro
gram consisting of Ryder, '96,
and Morse, '97, was chosen to
draw up a constitution.
