# Zoological Club Meeting.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Zoological
Club last Monday evening inter
esting letters from Dr. Gilbert and
McGregor, '97, members of the Pan
ama party, were read. They re
port a remarkably smooth and
pleasant voyage. At Mazatlan
they took their lirst zoological spec
imen, a curious Mexican coon.
Alvin Seale, '96, also gave an in
teresting account of his recent ex
periences at Baird, a U. S. fish
hatchery station. The next meet
ing will be held this evening at the
house of Dr. Gilbert.
