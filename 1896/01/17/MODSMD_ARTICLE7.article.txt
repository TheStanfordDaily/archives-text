# Freshman Class Election.
## 
### 
The Class of '99 held a meeting
ing and elected the following offi
cers: President, A. C. Russ; vice
president, C. A. Schwartz; second
vice president, Miss A. Elliott; re
cording secretary, Miss Lieb; cor
responding secretary, Miss Ruby
Green; Treasurer, J. 11. Crippen;
sergeant-at-arms, C. A. Pistoles!;
sentinel, A. S. Meyer; baseball man
ager, R. W. Logan; track manager,
John Brunton. Members of the
class council are A. C. Whittalcer,
.Miss M. Martin, Miss M. S. Ogden,
B. E. Page, L. R. Freeman, and
A. M. Strong.
