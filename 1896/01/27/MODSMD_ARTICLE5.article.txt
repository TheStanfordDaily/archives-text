# Ladies' Club.
## 
### 
At 2:15, January 25, a meeting
of the Faculty ladies was held in
the physics lecture room, with
thirty ladies present and Mrs. Gil
bert in the chair. It was voted to
form a ladies' club for social pur
poses, to include as members all
the ladies connected with the faculty
and others, it was decided to hold
meetings on the second Monday of
each month, from three to five
o'clock, and a notice of such meet
ing with place of holding will be
given at the proper time through
the Daily Palo Alto. Committees
were elected, and the tirst meeting
appointed at Mrs. Jordan's for the
second Monday in February.
