# News of the World.
## 
### 
The St. Paul, which ran ashore
near Long Branch, New Jersey, is
still fast.
Germany has notified Venezuela
that the railroad claims of German
capitalists must.be paid.
Theodore Runyon, United States
ambassador to Germany, died this
morning of heart failure.
The San 'Jose local was over
turned in a cut near Baden. The
engineer was killed and the fire
man injured.
General Gomez has received re
inforcements and will endeavor to
capture Havana before the arrival
of General Weyler, commander of
the Spanish forces.
The bill introduced into the
Kentucky Legislature providing for
the repeal of the Southern Pacific's
charter is favored by many mem
bers of the legislature.
