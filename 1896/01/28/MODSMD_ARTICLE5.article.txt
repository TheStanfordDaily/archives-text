# The 						Green Bag.
## 
### 
The Green Bag held its regular
meeting in room 81 quad last even
ing. The following case was argued
before .J. M. Ross, chief justice:
A case in adverse possession. A,
by mistake, occupied more land
than is described in his deed. The
entire tract passes by mesne con
veyances during 21 years to defend
ant, the description in each deed
being the same. Plaintiff brings
action in ejectment to recover the
entire land, and defendant pleads
adverse possession. Decker and
Price appeared for plaintiff, and
Brown and Eaton for defendant.
