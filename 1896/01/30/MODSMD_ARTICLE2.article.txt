# Chicago Ladies' Quartette.
## 
### 
'The Chicago Ladies' Quartette
will appear in the chapel Thursday,
February 27. This engagement
will be under the auspices of the
Associated Students. There will
be six people in the troupe, includ
ing an impersonator. Old students
will be glad of a chance to hear
again these sweet singers, who were
with us two years ago.
