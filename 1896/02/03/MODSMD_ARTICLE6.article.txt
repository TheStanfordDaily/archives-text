# A 						Treat for Music Lovers.
## 
### 
On the evening of Feb. 7 there
will he given, in the chapel, a piano
forte recital by Miss ll.Shelley,
B. M. The program selected will
be very elaborate, and from all re
ports there is a pleasure indeed in
store for those who admire the work
of an accomplished musician. The
fad that Miss Shelley is well known
to a large number of Stanford stu
dents will tend to popularize her ap
pearance on Friday evening.
