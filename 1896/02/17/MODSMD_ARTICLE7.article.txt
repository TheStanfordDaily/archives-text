# Basket-Ball Game.
## 
### 
The Stanford Basket-ball Team
will play the team of Castilleja
next Friday at 3:80 p.m. on the
Hoble field. The positions will be
filled by the following players:
Morley, 11. home; Corbert, L. home;
Ryan, C. home; Merritt, R. Center;
McCray (capt.), center; Tucker, L.
center; Kirper, R. guard; Frank, L.
guard; Walter, C. guard.
The Stanford girls expect a vic
tory, although they have not had
as much practice as the opposing
team. If they win next Friday,
Capt. McCray says that a challenge
will be issued to Berkeley. The
admission will be twenty-five cents.
