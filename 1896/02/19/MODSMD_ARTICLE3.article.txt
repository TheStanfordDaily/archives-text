# Results of Senior Farce Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
The Senior Farce Committee met
yesterday afternoon and after con
siderable discussion decided to of
fer a prize of twenty-five dollars for
the best farce written by any Stan
ford student provided such farce be
thought capable of production by
the committee. All farces must be
handed in to Geo. P. Baldwin, the
chairman of the committee, before
April 1.
The plan is to give the farce dur
ing Commencement week; and it is
desired that it deal largely with
college life and contain much local
interest.
The question of the merits of the
farces handed in will be referred to
a faculty committee. For further
particulars inquire of the chair
man or any other member of the
committee.
