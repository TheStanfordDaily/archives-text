# Civil Engineers Meet.
## 
### 
The Society of Civil Engineers
met in room 23 Encinn last eve
ning. After a business meeting in
which M. T. Pratt and Chas. Pope
were elected manager and captain
of the baseball team, the following
program was rendered: "TheSacra
mento bridge," George Gibson; "The
Temescal tin mines," W. L. Brown.
