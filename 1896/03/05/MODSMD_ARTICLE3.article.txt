# Rose Carnival Committee Meets.
## 
### 
The student body committee on
the San .Jose Rose Carnival met
this afternoon to receive the report
of the sub-committee on entertain
ment and transportation and to
make arrangements for Stanford
day. A special train has been ar
ranged for, and there will probably
be a special car for the seventy-five
men who will participate in the
celebration on the track and stage.
There will be fourteen track
events, with three prizes for each.
The evening program will be of a
musical nature; four Stanford clubs
will participate, the Banjo Club,
the Ladies' Mandolin Club, the
Men's Mandolin Club. Besides
these there will be special attrac
t ions on the program.
A dance will follow the enter
tainment at the theater.
