# Curtis Law Club.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Curtis Law
Club last night the decision in the
case of Jones vs. The Chicago Fire
Insurance Co. was read by Wills,
chief-justice. The court was unan
imous in its decision in favor of
appellant'.
The case of People vs. Roe was
then argued before Wills, chief-jus
tice, with Oskison, Morgan, Stowe,
and Biglow associated. Rothrock
and Stansbery acted as attorneys
for the defendant, while Crow and
DeWolfe acted for the state.
