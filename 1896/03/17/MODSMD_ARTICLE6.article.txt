# Notices.
## 
### 
The Constitutional Committee of
the Inter-Society Debating League
will meet in room 33 quad, Wed
nesday afternoon at 1:30 p.m.
G. L. Williams, Chairman.
A meeting of the Class of '99 will
be held in the physics lecture room
Wednesday, March 18, at 1:30 p.m.
A full attendance is desired, as im
portant business will be transacted.
A. C. Rubs, Pres.
The Pacific Grove excursion of
Stanford students and faculty mem
bers will leave Saturday morning
on the 9:36 train. The special
rate of three dollars is open to all
Stanford people, and a considerable
number have already signified
their intention of taking advantage
of the low fare.
