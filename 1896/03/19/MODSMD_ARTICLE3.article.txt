# Intercollegiate Track Agreement.
## 
### 
Dorn anil Merwin of Berkeley
and Toombs and Brown of Stan
ford yesterday signed an agreement
regarding this year's field-day. The
meet is to he held in San Francisco,
not here as formerly announced.
The two colleges are to share alike
in both profits and expenses, but
Stanford is to he allowed two dol
lars and Berkeley thirty cents as
traveling expenses for each man en
tered. The events are to he the
same as those in the 1. A. A. A.,
with the exception of the bicycle
races. There will be but one wheel
event, the two-mile.
