# Americans Win in Greece.
## 
### 
The Olympic games opened at
Athens yesterday in the restored
Stadium. The Americans were
successful in every event in which
they entered. The contests were
only preliminary and do not de
lude the final awards of victory.
F. A. Lane of the Princeton team
won the 100-meter dash in 12 2-5
seconds. Thomas P.Curtis, B. A. A.,
won the second heat in 12 4-5.
In the third heat Thomas E. Burke
of Boston Athletic Association won
in 11 4-5. No Americans entered
the 800-meter run. Captain Rob
ert Garrett of Princeton won the
discus throwing. 11. B. .Jamison of
the Princeton team won the 400-
meter dash.
