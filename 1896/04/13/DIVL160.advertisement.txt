Summer School
FOK BOYS
Mount Tamalpias Mi.itaiy Academy
Summer Session at Ulue Lakes,
Lake County, Cul.
Eight weeks—Juno 10 to August 4, 1HDB.
First—For the entertainment and care of boys.
Second—For the instruction of those who wish
t'< make tip bauk work, or to prepare for fall
