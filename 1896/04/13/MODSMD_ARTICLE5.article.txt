# An Interesting Lecture.
## 
### 
On Wednesday evening at 8
o'clock there will he given in the
chapel a lecture by a well-known
traveler and speaker, Mr. I'. E.
Wolff. This gentleman is a natur
alist of no mean ability and a
member of the Ethnological Society
of Leipsic. He will speak on the
"Fiji," especially on their cannibal
istic features and traditional cus
toms. Inasmuch as Mr. Wolff is
a careful observer and possesses the
power of vividly describing what he
lias seen, the lecture should he
highly interesting. A nominal ad
mission fee of twenty-five cents will
he charged.
