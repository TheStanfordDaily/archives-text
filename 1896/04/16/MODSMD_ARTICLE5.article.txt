# Curtis Law Club.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Curtis Law
Club last night the case of Sher
wood v. Walker was argued by Will
and Morgan for appellant, with
Bigelow and Oskison for appellee.
The case was heard by Stowe, chief
justice, with Stansbery, Crowe, De-
Wolf, and Roth rock associates.
The decision in the case of Men
denhall v. Howell was then read,
and decision given in favor of re
snondent.
