# Meeting- of Woman's Club.
## 
### 
The Stanford Woman's Club will
hold its last meeting for ihe college
year at the house of Mrs. Stillman
on the second Monday in May
from 2:30 to 5 o'clock.
The entertainment for the after
noon will be in the shape of pic
tures, and ladies having interesting
pictures of any kind which they
are willing to lend are requested to
send them to Mrs. Stillman's on
next Saturday.
As important matters of busi
ness are also to be considered a full
and prompt attendance of the club
is desirable.
