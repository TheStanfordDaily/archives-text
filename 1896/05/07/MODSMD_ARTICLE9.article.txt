# News of the World.
## 
### 
The House of Representatives
will adjourn May 18.
Aubrey Beardsley, the celebrated
artist, is believed to be dying of
pneumonia.
General Gomez has ordered a
wholesale destruction of towns in
Cuba. He is supposed to be pre
paring an attack on Havana.
The battle-ship Oregon made
over twelve knots an hour on her
preliminary trial trip yesterday, go
ing at half speed. This insures
about sixteen knots an hour at full
speed.
The Republican State Convention
yesterday selected as delegates-at
large to the Republican national
convention John 1). Spreckels,
Ulysses S. Grant, Jr., George A.
Knight, Lionel A. Sheldon, Charles
F. Crocker, and Irving M. Scott.
John C. Lynch of San Bernardino
was nominated lieutenant-governor.
The convention declared in favor
of the free coinage of silver at the
ratio of 16 to 1, woman's suffrage,
and against the funding bill now
before Congress. The delegates to
the national convention were in
structed to vote for William Mc-
Kinley of Ohio as Republican nom
inee for the presidency.
