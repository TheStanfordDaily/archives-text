The track team has at last made
arrangements for a trip East, and
the financial part seems to promise
success. \Ve have every confidence
in our track men and rejoice with
them at the prospect of so soon
proving their worth in a great inter
collegiate meet. A victory at such
a time would indeed be a triumph
of which to boast. Let us all lend
our endeavors towards helping the
team and accord thanks to the two
gentlemen whose liberal offer has
made the chance of an eastern trip
practicable.
