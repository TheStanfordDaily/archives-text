# Notices.
## 
### 
Young Indies who play the man
dolin or guitar and who wish to
join the Ladies' Mandolin Club,
please meet in the Koble parlors
nexi Monday at 2 p.m.
Miss C. Chovvkll, See.
There will he an important meet
ing of the St. Andrew's Brother
hood in room 18, quad, Tuesday,
Sept. 8, at 1:80 p.m. All members
are requested to be present.
The first regular meeting of the
Klectrical Engineering Club willbe
held in room 18 quad Wednesday,
Sept. 1), at 7 p.m. The following
were elected to membership at the
last regular meeting: Benson,
Brandt, Bean, Robinson, Boyd,
I'omeroy, Kaufman, Hogle, Work
man, Sampson, and Lee. All mem
bers are requested to be present.
