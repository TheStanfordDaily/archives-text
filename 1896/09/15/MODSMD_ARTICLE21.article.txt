# Zoological Club.
## 
### 
The first bi-weekly meeting of the
Zoological Club was held at Dr.
<iilhert's home in Palo Alto Mon
day, Sept. 14. With Mr. Snyder,

last year's president, in the chair,
officers for the ensuing year were
elected as follows: President, G. B.
Culver; vice-president, W. H. Os
good; secretary, A. W. Greeley. A
committee on program, to consist of
three members, was appointed, con
sisting of Messrs. Culver and Os
good, with the third member yet to
be chosen.
An excursion was decided on for
next Saturday, whose extent and
direction are not yet fully known.
