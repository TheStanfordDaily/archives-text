# Executive Committee of Debating Societies.
## 
### 
The newly elected members of
the Executive Committee of the
Inter-Society Debating League will
meet in room 38 Friday after
noon at 1:30 o'clock. The business
for consideration will he to make
arrangements for the first joint
meeting of the literary societies.
