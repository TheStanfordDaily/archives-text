Long articles regarding the phe
nomenal athletes of Berkeley and
their fall training have appeared
daily in the San Francisco papers
for some time past. It can not he
ascertained as to whether the
accounts of the strong athletic team
at the State University are accu
rate or not, hut it is certain that a
great effort is being made in train
ing to develop the new material
and bring out what is latent in the
old.
What is being done on the track
or in baseball at Stanford? Al
most nothing. It is of course our
intention to clinch the tie of last
year with a victory this year, but
it can not be done unless we get to
work early, as we did last fall.

when it vvjis realized that hard
work must be done if we hoped to

want to have a "great eastern"
team at Stanford? If we do let us
train our freshmen now for the
work next spring. Let us begin
now to put our old reliables at
work in the Held and to teach the
new men.
