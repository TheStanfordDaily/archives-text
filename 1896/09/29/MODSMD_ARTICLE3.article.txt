# Josh Wants Contributions.
## 
### 
To facilitate the work of gather
ing contributions, the management
of Josh have placed a box on the
east side of the postofiice.
wants accounts of any humorous
classroom occurrences, suggestions
for cartoons, or any distinctively
college verse. The work so far has
been carried on entirely by two or
three persons, but there have been
very few contributions from the
students at large. Variety is
essential to a paper of this char
acter, and it can be attained only
by general co-operation among the
students. Any suggestions what
soever will be thankfully received.
With the next number there will
be another change of cover. Owing
to the protest to a bi-collegiate
bear, Swinnerton's clever cover has
been abandoned, and two gorgeous
co-eds, in all the bright-lined finery
of the poster girl, will figure on the
new cover, designed by Qui nan.
The next number will have several
special features, including the first
number of Josh's portrait gallery.
