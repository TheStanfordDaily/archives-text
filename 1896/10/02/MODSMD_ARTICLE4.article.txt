# Off for San Jose.
## 
### 
Arrangements are fully completed
for the Stanford side of the festiv
ities at the San .Jose Fair tomorrow
afternoon and evening. The foot
ball team will leave on a morning
train for San .lose, in time for the
afternoon exhibition game of foot
hall. The members of the CJlee,
Mandolin, and Banjo Clubs will
leave on the 4:49 train, and upon
their arrival in the city will be met
by a band, and later driven around
the city in carriages. Stanford
headquarters will be at the St.
James Hotel. Most of the students
will not return to the University
until Sunday afternoon.
