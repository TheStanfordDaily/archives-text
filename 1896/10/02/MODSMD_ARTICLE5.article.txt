# Musical Services.
## 
### 
The Christian Association has
planned to vary its usual program
by having musical services, per
haps twice a month. Some of the
best musicians in the University
have promised their assistance,
forming an orchestra which will
lead the music. Next Sunday is to
have the first of these musical pro
grams, and if the extra effort is ap
preciated the plan will he contin
ued from time to time.
