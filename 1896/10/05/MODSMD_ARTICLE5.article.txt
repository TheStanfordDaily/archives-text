# Dr. Jordan Returns.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan returned from Alaska
last Saturday, arriving in the city
at ten o'clock. He came down to
the University in the afternoon at
three o'clock. The President is in
his usual good health and spirits
and seems glad to get hack. Sec
retary Clark has not returned. Dr.
Jordan left for the city this after
noon and will not he hack until to
morrow.
