# College Notes.
## 
### 
The University of Minnesota has
a new armory.
The Vale academic freshman
class numbers 348 this year.
Columbia has established a de
partment of Dutch languages.
Hull, the ex-Pennsylvania center,
is coaching the lowa College team.
The freshman class at the Uni
versity of Wisconsin numbers about
300 students.
Cornell will no longer make
Latin and Creek requisite for the
degree of A. B.
A course in public speaking has
been instituted at the University
of Pennsylvania.
All of last year's Princeton team
will return to college except Lea,
Rhodes, and Riggs.
Yale feels very keenly Ihe loss of
Cross at center. Her team is very
week at that point.
The freshman class of the Uni
versity of Pennsylvania numbers
about 170, a decrease of 40.
Amherst has conferred the degree
of LL I), upon Rev. C. 11. Park
hurst of New York, a graduate of
18ft6, for his strong leadership in
civic reform.
