# Y. M. C. A. Meeting,
## 
### 
Mr. .1. L. Speares, state secretary
of the Y. M. C. A., addressed the
members of the University associa
tion last evening. Mr. Speares has
lately been making a tour of the
soutnern part of the state, and
spoke of the growth of the young
men's movement and of the interest
the college men especially are tak
ing in the work.
The meeting was quite largely
attended. Notice was given of the
State Convention of the Y. M. C. A.
to be held in San Francisco Oct.
15-18. Representatives will be
sent by the Stanford association.
