# Bryan Rally at Palo Alto.
## 
### 
A number of students attended
the Bryan rally at Palo Alto last
night. Hon. .J. A. Filcher, of the
San Francisco Board of Trade, was
the principal speaker of the even
ing. Morgan, '98, also spoke on
the silver question, and Schwartz,
'91), presided at the meeting. Mu
sic was furnished by the Palo Alto
band.
