# Training Table.
## 
### 
After numerous delays, the train
ing table was started tonight in
Encina. Seventeen men were given
seats, and this number will be in
creased to about twenty-five as the#
season advances. Training rules
were presented this evening, and
will be signed by all who expect to
try for the team. The following
are at the table at present:
Williams, Fickert, Carle, Harring
ton, Cotton, Madden, Fisher, Dole,
Mcintosh, Soper, Seawright, B.
Thomas, C. Thomas, Rice, .Jeffs,
Murphy, Straight.
