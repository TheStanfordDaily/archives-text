# Death of a Stanford Student.
## 
### 
Mr. E. H. Shultz, '00, who has
been ill at the San .Jose Sanitarium,
died yesterday at 5:45 p.m. The
funeral services will take place in
the First Presbyterian Church of
San Jose tomorrow at 9:15 a.m. As
many members of his class as pos
sible are requested to attend.
