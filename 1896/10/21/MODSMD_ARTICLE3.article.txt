# For the Thanksgiving Program.
## 
### 
The publishers of the souvenir
program for tlie Thanksgivinggame
offer a prize of ten dollars open
to all Stanford undergraduates, for
the words fora Stanford song. A
similar offer is made to students of
the University of California.
Contributions should be handed to
Treasurer Morrison.
