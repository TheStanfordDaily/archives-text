# Resolutions of Respect.
## 
### 
Whereas. Divine Providence has
removed our classmate, Elbert 11.
Shultz, from the scene of his tem
poral studies, and as it is but a just
recognition of bis unsellish life and
of bis virtues as a classmate and
student that we pay a tribute to
the memory of the deceased; there
fore be il
Resolved, That our profound sym
pathy be extended to the bereaved
family.
Resolved, that a copy of these re
solutions be published in the Daily
Palo Alto and a copy be trans
mitted to the home of the departed.
N. 1). Miller.
L. I. Gregory,
L. H. Kinney,
Committee on Resolutions.
