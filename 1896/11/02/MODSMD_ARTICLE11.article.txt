A humor has been afloat the
past few clays to the effect that a
team of the old Stanford football
players would line up against the
Varsity a part of the week with
Mr. Cross at center. The sugges
tion is a capital one and ought to
be carried out. There is nothing
which encourages a player so much
as to come into contact with an old
player and receive the points of
the game from one who knows.
There are a great many of our
alumni football players in the city
and on the Coast who could be en
listed. Stanford has to thank her
football alumni for the good work
in coaching last year and in some
degree this year. Such loyalty on
the part of these men can not be
too highly commended. It would
be well to follow out the suggestion
of the alumni team and invite their
attention to it.
