# Palo Alto's New Weekly.
## 
### 
A new publication has made its
Appearance in I'alto Alto. It is
(tailed the I'alo Alto Lire Oak, and
makes its appearance weekly. It
is published in neat eight-page
form. The paper is devoted to the
interests of I'alo Alto and the
University, which is represented in
each issue by a column or two of
correspondence notes. The publi
cation is bright, newsy, and ably
edited, and will undoubtedly meet
with a favorable reception.
