# The
						Varsity Sweater at U.C.
## 
### 
The Athletic Committee has
passed resolutions governing the
" 4 big C." It is to be conferred on
those who play football in a regular
intercollegiate game, besides five
others recommended by the captain;
those who play in a regular inter
collegiate baseball game; those who
win a point in an intercollegiate
field-day, or who hold a university
or a Coast record; those who,are
participants in an intercollegiate
tennis contest or boat-race. The
football captain may also recom
