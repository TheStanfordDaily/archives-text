# The
						Coming Roble Reception.
## 
### 
The coming reception to young
men tit be held at Roble Nov. 2Stli
wilt be an innovation. The plan
this time is to combine the dance
and the regular reception in one
instead of giving each separately
as has hitherto been the custom. |
One of the best orchestras from San
Francisco has been spoken for to
furnish the music for the evening
which it is intended shall be an
exceptional feature.
