# Economics Seminary.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Economics
Seminary this afternoon reviews
were given by Dettriek, gr., and
Nourse, '97. The former on
"Woman from the standpoint of a
naturalist,''called forth a lively dis
cussion between Professors Powers,
Ross, and M. R. Smith, which was
one of the features of the session.
Assignments for the next meeting
are Hell, ".17, Webster, gr., and W.
W. Price, '97.
