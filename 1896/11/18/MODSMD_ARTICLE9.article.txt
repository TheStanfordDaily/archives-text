Reports go to prove that the
cleanest, purest, and most gentle
manly article of football seen on
the gridiron this season is that of
the Carlisle Indians. Poor Lo
seems to he avenging the wrongs of
his race, and at the same time
tacitly rebuking his white brother
in a matter of manners and civiliza
tion.
