# Football Souvenirs on Sale.
## 
### 
One hundred of the football
souvenirs remain unsold and have
been placed in the hands of B. P.
Stanhope. Those wishing copies,
can obtain them from him. The
souvenir contains cuts of the teams,
captains, coaches, and managers,
and is very valuable as a souvenir
of the game. The price remains
twenty-live cents.
