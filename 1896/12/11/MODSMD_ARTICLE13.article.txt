# Credit for Moot Court Work.
## 
### 
Those desiring credit in Moot
Court work must have the cluh
secretary file with me 011 or before
Saturday, Dec. I'.), 1896, a report
showing: 1. Name of cluh and
names of cluh members; 2. Dates
of meetings, with cases argued,
names of counsel and judges for
each; and 8. Uriel's of plaintiff and
defendant, and decisions of the
Court.
Individual members will also file
with me a personal statement
therein giving me (approximately)

to Moot Court work.

such records are tiled and approved.
