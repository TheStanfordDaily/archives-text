# Report of the Freshman Glee Committee.
## 
### 
Messrs. Borden ( chairman ),
White, and Lanagan, and Misses
Holmes, Haskell, and Caldwell, of
the Freshman (J lee Committee, met
this afternoon in room 33 quad to
prepare a report on the Glee. After
a careful account they found they
were $24.75 behind instead of $50,
as before supposed. A full report
will lie handed to the class later.
After some more unimportant busi
ness the committee adjourned.
