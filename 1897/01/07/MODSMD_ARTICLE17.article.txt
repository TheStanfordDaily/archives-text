rooms. Apply to Mrs. Chas. Ed.
ilodges, Alvarado row, campus.
For seven dollars a month, choice
comfortable heated room, for lady
only, in new house at Palo Alto,
without board, which can be ob
tained near by. Address P. O. box
204, Palo Alto.
Visit the Sutler street Hamman
Baths, 415 Sutter street, San Fran
cisco, between Stockton and Powell
sts. $1.00 a bath. Open all night.
If you want something to keep
permanently which will be both
useful and emblematical of college
life, get a watch with the Stanford
pennant on the back. Hammer
smith & Field have them for sale.
Two bay-window rooms at Mrs.
Swain's, with board, $18 a month.
Furnace heat.
Foit Rknt. —Four-room cottage,
near the white gate, in College
Terrace.

sofa pillows, 15 cents up; revolving
office chairs and waste-baskets at
Furniture Store, Palo Alto.
Student body assessments will be
received by the postmaster at the
UDiversity postoffice.
Play billiards at Mosher's place,
UDiversity ave.

photographers in Palo Alto, make
fine photographs, and their prices
are low. These two considerations
should induce you to patronize
them.
