Positions on the editorial staff of
the Daily Paily Palo Alto are
open to all who desire to make a
try at journalistic work. The staff
as now constituted was made up
during the vacation when many
students were away from the Uni
versity and we had no means of
calling for candidates. Additions
will be made as fast as candidates
make themselves known to us and
show us what they can do. The
work is not hard, while the training
received is an invaluable compen
sation for the time that is spent
each week. The only requirement
is a willingness to do conscientious
work. Slight mistakes made at the
start may be easily corrected, and
no one need fear that the paper
will not protect the members of its
staff nor hold itself responsible for
their work so long as it is honestly
and faithfully done.
