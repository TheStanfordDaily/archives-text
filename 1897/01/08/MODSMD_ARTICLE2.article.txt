# Gates on the Track.
## 
### 
During the coming season low
gates will lie used on each curve of
the cinder track. These will he
open fora certain time each day.
When closed it will he impossible
fora wheelman to ride over one
half the distance around without
dismounting Others exercising,
especially after dark, should he
careful not to he injured by them.
Notices of location of these gates
will be suitably placed.
