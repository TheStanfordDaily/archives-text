# In Other Colleges.
## 
### 
Governor Pingree of Michigan
has offered a prize of twenty-five
dollars to the student in the law
department, at Ann Arbor who
shall point out the greatest number
of inoperative and obsolete laws on
the statute books of the state.
The Princeton Whist Club com
posed of professors at Princeton
University, which has been organ
ized but a short time, has made
application for admission into the
American Whist League and will
undoubtedly be represented at the
next congress.
