Two years ago Ninety-seven es
tablished the precedent of holding
the Cotillion on Washington's
Birthday. While the sophomores
of last year failed to accept that
date, Nine-nine could do no better
than to choose that date for their
Cotillion. The cessation of Uni
versity work at that time and the
fact that February 22 is midway
between the Freshman Glee and
the Junior Prom, make that date a
very desirable one for the Cotillion.
