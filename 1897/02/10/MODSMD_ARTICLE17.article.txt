All shareholders in the Co-op.
should investigate thoroughly the
plans that are proposed for the re
habilitation of the association.

urging the change do not wish to
be unjust or unfair to any who are
interested in the present system;
it. is part of their plan that the new
system should be satisfactory to a
larger number of students than the
present system is; and before voting
or acting in the matter it would be
well for students by discussion and
by inquiry to ascertain the com
plete details of the plan proposed,
as well as the counterbalance of
loss and benefit which might ensue
from the change.
