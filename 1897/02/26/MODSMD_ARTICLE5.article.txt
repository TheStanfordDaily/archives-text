# Frank Lincoln Will Return.
## 
### 
Mr. Frank Lincoln, who gave an
entertainment here about two
months ago, will return Friday
evening of next week and re-appear
in the chapel under student body
management. He made many
friends upon his last appearance
here, and since that time many
students have expressed a desire to
hear him again. He is now on bis
way around the world and this will
probably be his last appearance on
the Coast.
