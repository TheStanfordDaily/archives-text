# Palo Alto Delivery.
## 
### 
Hereafter the Daily Palo Alto
will be sent to Hall's drug store,
Palo Alto, on the bus leaving the
University at 7 p.m. Subscribers
who wish to avail themselves of
this method of delivery will please
send their names to some member
of the staff, as papers will be sent
only to those who so order them.
