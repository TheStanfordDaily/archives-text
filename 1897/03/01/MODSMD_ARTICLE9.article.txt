# Announcements.
## 
### 
At Home. — Pi Beta Phi will be
at home Wednesday afternoon.
Tuesday Evening Lecture. -
The lecture Tuesday evening will
he given by Prof. M. B Anderson,
lie will read some selections from
Browning.
Sword and Sandals. —The mem
bers of Sword and Sandals will
meet at 2 p.m. Wednesday, March
3, at Thors' gallery, Palo Alto, to
be photographed.
Executive Committee.—The Ex
ecutive Committee of the student
body will meet at Thors' gallery in
Palo Alto Wednesday at 2:80 p.m.
Horace Pomeroy.
L. A. H. S. Alumni. —Those who
have attended the Los Angeles
High School are requested to meet
tomorrow in room 10 at 1:45 p.m.,
to consider the formation of an al
umni association.
The University-Palo Alto
Walk—The committee appointed
to receive subscriptions for the road
between the University and Palo
Alto requests that subscriptions be
paid as soon as possible.
T. M. Williams, Pres.
Athletic Committee. — There
will be a meeting of the Athletic
Committee tomorrow at 1:30 p.m.
in room 13 quad to consider the
amendments and changes in the in
tercollegiate athletic agreement.
Every member should be present.
H. Pomeroy, Chairman.
Life Study.—Men and women
students are wanted to pose fora
class in drawing the head, on Mon
days, Wednesdays, and Fridays, at
10:30-11:30, 11:30-12:30, 2-3,3-4,
and 4-5. Students who wish to
pose for single hours or more are
requested to call in room 140 En
gineering building, at the times
above indicated. A. B. Clark.
