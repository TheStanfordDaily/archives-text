# BASEBALL GAME TOMORROW.
## Stanford to Be Matched Against Santa Clara on Our Own Diamond. 
### 
The Varsity baseball team will
play a match game on the campus
tomorrow afternoon with Santa
Clara. This will be the second
game of the season with this team
and the first match game to be
played on the campus.
An admission of twenty-five cents
will be charged to defray the ex
penses of the visiting nine.
The following is the batting
order of the Stanford team: Beck
ett p, Jeffs c, Young lb, Strohn 2b,
Lougheed ss, Sharp 3b, Wight rf,
Clemans cf, Thompson If, Free
man cf sub, McLaine p sub.
