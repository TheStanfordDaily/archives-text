# Concert at San Mateo.
## 
### 
The Glee and Mandolin Clubs
g.ive a concert in San Mateo last
evening. The clubs left at five
o'clock in the afternoon, and re
turned at four o'clock this morn
ing. Despite the rainy weather
the concert was fairly well attended,
and about ten dollars was cleared
above expenses.
