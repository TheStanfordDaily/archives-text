# GAMES ON TRACK AND FIELD.
## Freshmen to Contest Tomorrow With the Team From Oakland High School. 
### 
The field-day between tbe Fresh
man Class and Oakland High
School teams will be held on our
track Saturday afternoon.
Games will be called promptly at
two o'clock.
Our freshmen are somewhat crip
pled by the lameness of Burnette,
who will perhaps not be able to
compete in the half and tbe mile.
First place will count five points,
second three, and third one. The
manager of our team, Clem Wilson,
will also act as captain. Follow
ing are the entries:
100 yards: 1900 — liirtch, Boyd,
Harter; 0. H. S. —E. Footer.
440 yards: 1900—Wilson, Hig
gles, McDowell, Nourse; O. 11. S.
A. Cadogan.
120-yd. hurdle (10 hurdles, 3 ft.
6 in ): 1900 —Morgan, Wilson, Ed
wards; O. 11. S. —C. Harris, Mor
rell.
220-yd. dash: 1900 —Birtch, Wil
son, Harter; O. 11. 8. —A. Cadogan,
E. Steele.
Mile run: 1900- Burnette, Dag
gett, Oilman, Thomson; O. 11. S.
—F. Smith, L. H. Westdahl, H.
At water.
880-yd. run: 1900 —Burnette,Gil-
man, Daggett, Thompson; O. H. S.
—Weber, C. Harris, E. Steele.
Shot-put: 1900 —Burnette, Ker
rigan, Beckett, Morgan; O. H. S. —
A. Flaw.
Pole-vault: 1900—Kerrigan, Bal
lantyne, Boyd; O. H. S. —R. Nee
bolson, Wood rum.
12-lb. hammer: Burnette, Holly,
Woodruff, Blanch;ud Morgan; O.
H. S. —Plaw, Woodrum.
Running high-jump: 1900 —Ker-
rigan, Boyd, Morgan; O. H. S. —E.
Forster, F. Cooley, J. McCargar.
Running broad-jump: 1900 —
Diggles, Harter, Nourse; 0. U.S. —
E. Foster, E. Hussey, F. Cooley.
C. R. Roberts is entered for the
mile walk, and I. J. Egan for the
one mile bicycle race. Following
are the officers who will officiate to
morrow :
Referee —T. L. Barnes, U. C.
Judges at finish —C. H. Woolsey,
Hoitt's School; J. B. Osborn, San
Mateo; E. Hoi brook.
Field judges —P. 11. Johnson, San
Mateo; G. H. Hazzard; W. S. Har
rington.
Inspectors —W. T. Reid, Belmont;
.1. S. Emery, Santa Clara; John
Brunton.
Starter —John M. Gregory, Jr.
Timers —Dr. T. I). Wood, A. E.
Davis, H. Pomeroy, C. S. Pope.
Measurers —C. S. Dole, N. A.
Carle, L. R. Freeman.
Clerks of course —G. B. Culver,
C. M. Bradley, J. G. Yocum.
Announcer—J. E. Sharp.
Scorer —C. M. Fickert.
