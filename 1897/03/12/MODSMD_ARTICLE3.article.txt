# Berkeley Debaters.
## 
### 
The first preliminary debate for
the selection of the University of
California representatives in the
Intercollegiate Debate was held
Wednesday evening. Those chosen
for the final on March 17 are:
Clark,'9B; Weil,'97; Cohen, '99;
Treadwell, '99; and Gardner, '97.
Besides these a number of others
have been allowed to compete in
the final without appearing in the
preliminaries, among them being
Son, '97; Dannebaum, '98; Over
street, '98; A. C. Marks; and 11. T.
Miller.
