# Lecture by Haskett Smith.
## 
### 
The students and general public
are promised a rich intellectual
treat for Monday, March 29, when
the Rev. Haskett Smith, M. A., of
Cambridge, England, is to give a
lecture on "The mysteries of An
cient Egypt." Mr. Smith is a dis
tinguished explorer, orientalist,
writer, and lecturer who has lately
arrived on this coast from Australia.
His lecture will be profusely illus
trated with stereopticon views.
