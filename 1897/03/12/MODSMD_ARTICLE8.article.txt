# New Plans Proposed.
## 
### 
The student body committee,
consisting of 11. I). Sheldon, J. T.
Burcham, J. 11. Timmons, R. J.
O'Neil, and F. 8. Howard, which
was appointed to formulate plans
for the reorganization of the Co-op.,
met today at 1:30 p.m. Several
plans were presented and discussed.
The committee adjourned without
formal action on any of the plans
submitted.
