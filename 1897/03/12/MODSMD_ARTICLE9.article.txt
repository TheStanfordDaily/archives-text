# Press Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The Stanford University Press
Club met in room 11 quad yester
day afternoon and elected the fol
lowing officers for the coming year:

President, W. H. Irwin, '98; vice
president, Chas. E. Schwartz, '99;
secretary-treasurer, O. C. Leiter,'99.
It was decided to have regular
monthly meetings of the club here
after. These meetings will be de
voted to the discussion of journal
istic topics. It is also probable
that the club will hold a banquet
at the end of the year, at which a
number of the alumni members will
be present.
