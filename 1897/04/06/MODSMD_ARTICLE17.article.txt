# Choral Society.
## 
### 
The Choral Society, in connec
tion with the Apollo Club of San
Francisco, is planning two concerts
to be given before the close of the
semester. One of these will be on
the campus, and the other in the
city. The fee for membership in
the society has been made one dol
lar for the remainder of the year,
the full semester's fee being two
dollars and fifty cents. Mr. Pas
more will lie in the chapel Thurs
day afternoon to try voices.
