# The 						Chapel Preacher.
## 
### 
The Rev. L. H. Hal lock, who
preaches in the chapel on Sunday,
is the resident pastor of Mills Col
lege and professor of Ethics and
Christian Evidences. He is a grad
uate of Hartford Theological Semi
nary, and has held pastorates at
West Winsted, Conn., Portland,
Maine, Waterville, Maine, and Ta
coma, Wash. Mr. Hal lock has
been especially interested in Chris
tian Endeavor work, succeeding
the Rev. Francis E. Clark, presi
dent of the national organization,
in pastorial work at Portland,
Maine.
