# Christian Endeavor Convention.
## 
### 
Tomorrow the sixteenth biennial
convention of the Santa Clara
County Christian Endeavor
Union will be held at Nortree Hall
and at the Presbyterian Church,
Palo Alto. All visiting delegates
will be entertained by a drive about
the University before the program
begins The meetings will be held
both afternoon and evening, and
talented speakers have been en
gaged for the occasion. A special
train for San Jose will leave Palo
Alto at 11:05 p.m.
