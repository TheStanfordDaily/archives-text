# Rally Tomorrow Night.
## 
### 
It has been decided to hold a
rally in the chapel tomorrow
night to prepare for the contests
with Berkeley Saturday. This will
he in the nature of a joint meeting
of those interested in debating and
athletics, and prominent representa
tives of both interests will be called
upon to voice their sentiments upon
our prospects the eve of going
forth to battle. An attempt will also
he made to have members of the fac
ulty present to lend dignity to the
meeting and to give their view of
the situation.
