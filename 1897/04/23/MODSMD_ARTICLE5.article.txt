Tomorrow's Field-Day Will Settle
the Intercollegiate Championship.
Stanford and Berkeley will meet
tomorrow in tlieir annual field-day
contest. So far as the outcome can
be predicted neither side will have
a walk-over. Berkeley is said to
have conceded to Stanford eight
first places, the other six heing
claimed by Berkeley men. They
expect that Stanford will take the
100, the 220, the 440, the half mile,
the broad jump, polevault, hammer
throw, and high jump. What
Berkeley expects to take are the
mile run, the mile walk, both
hurdles, the bicycle race, and the
shot-put. They expect to make up
enough in second and third places
to win the contest.
