# Announcements.

### 
Associated Students. —There will
be a meeting of the student body
Wednesday, April 28, at '2 p.m. in
the chapel, for the nomination of
officers. Horace Pomekoy, I'res.
Junior Farce. —Tickets for the
Junior Farce will be on hale tomor
row afternoon at the postoflice.
Prices, seventy-five, fifty, and
twenty-five cents, according to the
location.
Mrs. Annie Besant, the noted
theosophist, will lecture in the
chapel May 17 for the benefit of the
Senior Class. Her subject will he
''Man's invisible bodies; a glimpse
of the unseen." Admission will he
fifty cents. All seats reserved.
