# Coronado Summer School.
## 
### 
A printed announcement of the
courses for the Coronado Beach
Summer School has been received.
Among the instructors who will be
in attendance at the s-eision are the
following from the University:
Prof. Edward 11. Griggs, Ethics
and Literature; Dr. O. P. Jenkins,
Biology and Physiology; Carl E.
Durrell, '95, Mathematics and
Chemistry; D. Hutton Webster,' 96,
Sociology; Emma E. Myer, Ger
man.
The school will open July 6, con
tinuing four weeks and closing Fri
day, July 80. In a number of the
courses students may obtain credit
at the University.
