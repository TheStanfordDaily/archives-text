# Woman's Congress.
## 
### 
At the Woman's Congress yester
day in San Francisco papers were
read by Mayor Phelan, Miss Eva
D. Carlin, and Prof. Elmer E.
Brown of Berkeley. At the 2
o'clock session today Miss Estelle
M. Darrah read a paper on "The
child's feeling for the beautiful,"
and Miss Annie G. Lyle, '95, a pa
per on "The commonplace woman."
Dr. Ross lectures this evening on
"Education for enjoyment or cul
ture."
