# Announcements.
## 
### 
Tuesday Lecture. —The lecture
tomorrow evening will be given by
Mrs. Helen Gardner on "Heredity."
Choral Society.—There will be
two rehearsals this week, one Wed
nesday and one Thursday at the
usual time.
Declination. —J respectfully de
cline the nomination as a member
of the Executive Committee from,
the Class of 1900. Carl Hayden.
Alpha.—The last meeting of
Alpha this semester will be the
business meeting and election of
officers next Saturday evening. All
members are urged to be present
and to pay the fifty cent assessment
voted at the last meeting.
H. P. Pearson.
