# Tennis Tournament.
## 
### 
In the matches against Berkeley
last Saturday morning Stanford
and U. C. each won a game.
Freeman beat Mien, 8-6, 3-6,6-0,
4-6, 6-4; and Magee, U. C., beat
Pitcher, 8-6, 6-4, 7-5. The doubles
between Magee and Mien, and Free
man and Pitcher will be held on
the campus on Wednesday. Seats
will be provided at the courts.
So far U. C. has won three games
and Stanford has won two. The
University which first wins five
games will get the intercollegiate
championship for this year.
