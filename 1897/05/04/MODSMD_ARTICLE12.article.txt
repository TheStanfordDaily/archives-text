# Announcements.
## 
### 
Sunday Sermon. —Rev. H. M.
'Penny, pastor of the First Congre
gational Church of San Jose, will
preach the chapel sermon next
Sunday.
Glee and Mandolin Clubs. —
There will be a meeting of the com
bined Glee and Mandolin Clubs to
morrow at 7 p.m. sharp for the
election of officers.
Euphkonia Society.—All active
as well as honorary members are
urged to attend the meeting next
Saturday night, the last meeting of
the society for the present semester.
Track Team. —The photograph
of the track team will be taken
Wednesday at 1:15 p.m. at Thors'
in Palo Alto. Election of track
captain will take place immediately
after. * C. S. Dole.
Varsity Baseball Men. — The
Varsity baseball team will be pho
tographed at Thors' on Wednesday
at 1:45 p.m. Those in the group
will be Jeffs, Freeman, Lougheed,
Clemans, Ruddell, Beckett, Wight,
lvlauber, Strohn, and Thompson.
Euphronia.—The program for
Saturday, May 9, is: Speeches (or
declamations) by S. W. Charles,
'97, Joss, '98, S. G. Bailie, '99; the
rest of the time will be devoted to
speeches (fifteen minutes each) for
the good of the society (speakers
and subjects to be determined by
the chair).
