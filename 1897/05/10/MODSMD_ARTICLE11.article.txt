# Annual Memorial Services.
## 
### 
Next Friday, May 14, will be the
twenty-ninth anniversary of the
birth of Leland Stanford, Jr., and
the tenth anniversary of the laying
of the corner-stone of the University
that stands in his name and as a
tribute to his memory. The day
will be observed by a suspension of
regular university work. Exercises
;jre in preparation for the annual
memorial services, to be held in the
chapel at 8 p.m. After a short ad
dress by President Jordan, a musi
cal program will be rendered. The
program will be printed in full to
morrow.
