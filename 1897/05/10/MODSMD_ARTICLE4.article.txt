# Euphronia.
## 
### 
The last meeting of Euphronia
for this year was held Saturday
night. The most of the members
of the year were present at the final
meeting. A short program was
rendered consisting of a speech by
Joss, '98, and declamations by
Charles, '98, and Bailie, '99. After
this the society adjourned to the
Choral Society concert and after
that the customary annual
"spread" was participated in by
the members at Rice's. A number
of speeches were made by various
members and then the society dis
banded until next fall.
