# Football Meeting.
## 
### 
Manager Switzer met thefootna.il
men in the chapel this afternoon
and discussed plans for next year's
work. The names of those present
were taken, and the clothes needed
for their work next year. An at
tempt will he made to enable play
ers to begin work next year fully
equipped with clothes. Two foot
hall fields will lie maintained if the
consent of the Executive Commit
tee can he obtained.
