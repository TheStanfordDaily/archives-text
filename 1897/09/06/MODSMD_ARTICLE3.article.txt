# Death of Wm. C. Lund.
## 
### 
Wm. C. Lund, the busman, who
had been missing since Friday, was
found today in a room in the Ahl-
Irtirn Hotel in San Francisco. Ibe
gas was turned on in the room and
lie was also shot. Business troubles
are supposed to have led to his self
destruction. Mr. Lund was one of
Palo Alto's most prominent business
men and was one of the most active
members of the town council. His
funeral will be held at the Presby
terian Church Wednesday at 2 p.m.
Dr. Thoburn will probably preach
the sermon.
