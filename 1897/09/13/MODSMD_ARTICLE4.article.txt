—Havens, '01, who holds the
archery championship of the Pacific
Coast, is trying to form an archery
club. All who are interested will
kindly confer with Mr. Havens.
— Miss Hose Johnson, ex-'99, as
sociate editor of the Daily I'alo
Alto last semester, and a member
of the Young Ladies' Mandolin
Cluh, has accepted a position as in
structor of music in the Kanieha
meha Schools at Honolulu. She
sailed from San Francisco the 19th
of August.
