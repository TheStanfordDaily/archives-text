# Stanford Wins at Tennis.
## 
### 
The following is the result of the
Southern California Tennis Tourna
ment. held at Santa Monica, July
26-31:
Picher, '98, defeated by Cravens
(ex-Yalechampion), 6-4, 8-6, o-6.
Freeman, '99, defeated Cravens,
thus winning for third time the
championship in singles.
Picher, '98, and Freeman, '99,
won for fourth time championship
doubles 6-4, 6-1, 6-0.
