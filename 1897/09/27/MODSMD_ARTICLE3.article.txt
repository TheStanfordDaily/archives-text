# Freshman Meeting.
## 
### 
The Class of 1901 met in the
chapel this afternoon to complete
the election of officers and to adopt
a class yell. The following officers
were elected: Baseball manager,
Potter; class council, Tabor, Dolph,
Sobey, Asmussen, Bryant, and
Alden. As no quorum was present
towards the close of the meeting 110
official yell was adopted.
