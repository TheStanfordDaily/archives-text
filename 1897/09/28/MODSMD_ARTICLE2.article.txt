# Tennis Club Meeting.
## 
### 
The meeting of the old members
of the Tennis Club was called lo
order this afternoon by President
McNeil. The organization for ihe
coming year was discussed and all
were heartily in favor of continuing
the club. All that is necessary for
its welfare is a sufficient number of
enthusiastic members. The club
decided that another meeting
should be held on Tuesday of next
week for the purpose of effecting a
final organization. All students
who take an active interest in the
game and who desire to join the
club, are requested to send their
names immediately to Mr. McNeil
in order that plans may be defin
itely settled.
