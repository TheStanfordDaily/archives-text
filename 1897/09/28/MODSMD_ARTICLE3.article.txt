# Zoology Club Meeting.
## 
### 
The program at the Zoology
Club's meeting last evening con
sisted of three talks by members.
\V. W. Price spoke on recent inves
tigations of the mammals of San
Francisco peninsula; Alvin Seale
on the flying of marine birds at
night; W. K. Fisher on mammal
collecting in Oregon. Officers were
nominated as follows: President,
A. W. Greeley, '98; vice-president,
Miss Anna Dixon, '98; secretary
treasurer, Edward Heller, '00.
