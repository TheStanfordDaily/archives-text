# New Chess Club Officers.
## 
### 
The Chess Club met last week
and elected officers for the semester
as follows: President, Erie Veuve,
'99; vice-president, B. I. Bloch, '99;
secretary-treasurer, E. W. McCor
mack, '98; executive committee,
Carlos Serpas, '99, B. L. Bloch, '99,
and J. R. Stowe, '00.
An invitation from Professor
Richardson was read inviting the

clul> to hold its weekly meetings at
his residence. The invitation was
accepted and it was decided to hold
the meetings on Monday evenings.
Several new names were proposed
for membership.
