# An Article on Stanford University
## 
### 
In the October number of Frank
Leslie'* Popular Monthly there is an
article on Stanford University by
Dr. 0. L. Elliott. This article is
the twelfth of a series on \merican
colleges and universities. Dr.
Elliott gives a short sketch of Sen
ator Stanford's life, and of the inci
dents that caused the founding of
the University. He traces its
growth from the beginning and in
terprets the purport and the su
perior advantages of the elective
system, making comparisons with
Eastern universities.
This number of Frank Leslie'* is
a valuable souvenir for students and
their friends. It can be purchased
at the Bookstore for twenty-five
cents a copy.
