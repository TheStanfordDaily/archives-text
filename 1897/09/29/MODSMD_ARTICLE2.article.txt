# Football Men Meet.
## 
### 
Coach Brooke addressed thirty
of the most promising candidates
for the Varsity hist night from
seven until half past eight, lie
outlined his plan of training, which
was not to he too strict or severe,
but which necessitated regular
habits both in eating and sleeping
Mr. Brooke also spoke of the ten
dency of some of the men to lay off
on account of trifling bruises and
said that from now on he wanted
the men tp have good reasons for
laying off. The principal part of
the evening was spent in discuss
ing defensive play.
