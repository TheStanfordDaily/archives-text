# Y.M.C.A. vs. Freshmen
## 
### 
An interesting game of football
will be played on the campus to
morrow at 3 p.m. between the
Freshmen and the Y. M. C. A.
team of San Francisco. The
V. M. C. A. team is light and quick
and will put up a good exhibition
of football. The Freshman team
will he strengthened by the addi
tion of several new men, and the
places of the disabled players will
he taken by experienced players.
The line-up of the Freshmen has
not been definitely determined.
