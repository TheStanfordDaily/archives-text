# Junior Class Meeting.
## 
### 
The CliiFs of '99 met in the
chupel this afternoon with Presi
dent MacKinnon in the chair. The
committee which was appointed at
the last meeting to investigate the
matter of a junior hat reported
upon the merits of the sombrero
plug, and after some discussion
the sombrero was adopted. The
question as to the kind of sombrero
was then considered and was re
ferred to the former committee,
consisting of A. M. Strong, R. E.
Filcher, and Miss Tucker.
Mr. Greenebaum, as manager of
the Annual, then gave some esti
mates upon the probable cost of the
Annual. A motion to levy an as
sessment of two dollars and fifty
cents was carried and the meeting
adjourned.
