# Announcements.
## 
### 
Band Meeting.—There will be a
business meeting of the Band to
morrow at 1:30 p.m. in room 50
quad. All members are requested
to be present.
Orchestra. —There will be a bus
iness meeting of the Orchestra in
room 10 quad Tuesday evening.
All who intend to play should be
present. F. A. Gorbusier,
Lea der.
Women's Athletic Association.
—There will be a meeting of the
Women's Athletic Association in
Roble gymnasium on Wednesday,
October 6 at 5 p.m. to nominate
officers for the coming year.
■Jessie K. Jordan.
Freshman Team. —There will be
a meeting of the Freshman football
team tomorrow in Encina gymnas
ium at 4 p.m. to elect a captain.
The freshmen who have played in
any of the three games the team
has played are entitled to vote.
Chris M. Bradley, Manager.
