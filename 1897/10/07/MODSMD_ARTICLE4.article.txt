# Tennis Club Meets.
## 
### 
The Tennis Club met in room 10
quad this afternoon and decided
upon a plan of procedure for the
coming year. President McNeil
appointed a commmittee of three,
consisting of Klauber, Spencer, and

Schneider (chairman), to look up
new members and to report at the
next meeting, which is to he held
on Wednesday of next week. The
club then proceeded to nominate
officers for the coming semester
The following nominations were
made: President, Pitcher, Hill,
Spencer; vice-president, Schneider;
secretary-treasurer, Oskison, Greg
ery; executive committee, Daggett,
McNeil, Page, Klauber.
