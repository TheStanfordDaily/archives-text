# Announcements.

### 
'99 Annual. —There will he a
meeting of the '99 Annual board
tomorrow (Thursday) at 5 p.m., in
room 11 quad.
C. M. Bradley, Editor.
No Student Body Election. —
The election of baseball manager,
which was announced for tomor
row, has been postponed.
A. B. Morgan, Pres.
Orchestra Meeting.—There will
be a meeting of the Orchestra in
the chapel tomorrow at 6:45 p.m.
fora rehearsal.
F. A. Cor busier, Leader.
At Home. —The young ladies of
Ruble Hull will give a reception to
the young ladies of the University,
Friday evening, October 15. All
are cordially invited to attend.
Executive Committee. —There
will be a meeting of the Executive
Committee of the Associated Stu
dents Thursday, Oct. 14, at 2 p.m.
in room 11 quad. A. B. Morgan,
President.
'99 Class Assessment. — F. 11.
Greenebaum and Duncan MacKin
non are hereby authorized to col
lect and receipt for the '99 Class
Assessment. It is desirable that
the assessment be paid as soon as
possible. S. P. Elias,
Treasurer '99 Class.
