# The
						Zoology Club Meeting.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Zoology
Club Monday evening A. W.
Greeley, '98, gave an interesting
talk on the Alaskan Fur-Seal Is
lands and the breeding habits of
the seals. Officers for the year
were elected as follows: President,
A. W. Greeley, '98; vice-president,
MissS. M. Dickson, '98; secretary
treasurer, Edmund Heller, '00.
