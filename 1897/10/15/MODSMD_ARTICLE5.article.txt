# The
						Student Body Meeting.
## 
### 
The meeting of the student body
this afternoon was not very well at
tended, hardly one hundred stu
dents being present. After the
reading of the minutes a call fora
count for quorum was made by
Horace Pomeroy, '97. The chair
appointed S. G. Bailie, '99, and
J. T. Burcham, '97, tellers. On
count, ninety-five persons were
found present. Nominations were
announced in order. F. S. Fisher,
'98, nominated Roy N. Strohn, '98;
and 1). C. 0. Bigelow, '98, placed
Frank V. Keesling, '98, in nomina
tion. The meeting then adjourned.
