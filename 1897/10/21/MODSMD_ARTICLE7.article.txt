# Santa Clara Game Declared Off.
## 
### 
The game between the Freshmen
and Santa Clara College has been
declared off on account of the
Freshmen who go to the city Satur
day to play against Reliance. In
stead of this game the Freshmen
will play a game of regular halves
against the second eleven.
