# Mrs. Stanford Back From Europe.
## 
### 
Mrs. Stanford arrived in New
York City from Europe last Mon
day. She will probably remain
there about two weeks attending to
railroad business and then proceed
at once to California. Her private
car has already been sent East for
her.
