# Special Trains for the Kirmess.
## 
### 
Attention is called to the special
time schedule of the Southern Pa
cific Company to accommodate
patrons of the Kirmess. Trains
will leave San Francisco at 7:15
p.m. and San Jose at 7:30 p.m.
Returning they will leave Palo
Alto sit 11 p.m. The excursion
tickets will be good on the 5 p.m.,
5:30 p.m., and 6:30 p.m. trains from
the city.
