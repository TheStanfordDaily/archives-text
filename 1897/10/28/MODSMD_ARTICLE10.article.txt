# Announcements.
## 
### 
Senior Class.—There will be a
meeting of the Class of '5)<S in room
50 quad, tomorrow at 1:30 p.m.
W. W. Kemp, President.
Sunday Sermon. —The sermon
Sunday will he given by Rev. S.
M. .Jefferson, D. I)., dean of the
Berkeley Bible Seminary.
Debating League.—The execu
tive committee of the Inter-Society
Debating League will meet in room
13 Friday at 1:30 p.m.
Duncan Mackinnon, Vice Pres.
Freshman Class. —The Class of
1901 will meet in the chapel to
morrow at 1:15 p.m. for the pur
pose of adopting class colors.
H. Havens, Pres.
Tuesday Evening Lecture. —
The next regular weekly lecture
will be by Prof. A. W. Smith on
"Hawaii" and will be illustrated
with lantern slides.
Books Received.—The following
new books are at the Bookstore:
Newth, Inorganic Chemistry; Gar
diner, A Students' History of
England; Rossetti, Poetical Works;
Mussey. Mathematical Tables;
Wines. The Liquor Problem ; Dar
win, The Origin of Species; Farrar,
The Life of Christ; Wilson,
Drunkenness.
