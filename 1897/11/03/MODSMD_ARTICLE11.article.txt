# College Notes.
## 
### 
—One hundred and sixty women
have entered the woman's medical
school which was opened in St.
Petersburg Sept. 1.
—Some time ago several Prince
ton professors signed a petition for
a liquor license for Princeton
Since then they have been con
demned hy various Presbyteries
and synods, and it looks as if the
matter will end in an open rupture
between the Presbyterian Church
and the university.
