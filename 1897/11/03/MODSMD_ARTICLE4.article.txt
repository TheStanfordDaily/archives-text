# Excursion Rates.
## 
### 
Attention is called to the fact
that the Southern Pacific Co. will
sell round-trip tickets to San Fran
cisco for $1.25 which are good from
Friday noon until Monday noon.
Students who purchase their tick
ets on Friday must get a permit at
the Registrar's office.
