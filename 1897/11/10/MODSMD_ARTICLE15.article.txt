# Quads.
## 
### 
—The Christian Association will
go on a picnic next Saturday. The
party will leave the chapel at 8:80
for the summit by the goat's trail.
All will go on foot.
— Major Ben Butterworth, father
of Frank Butterworth, Berkeley's
football coach last year, is reported
to be dying of pneu nonia at Cleve
land, 0.
