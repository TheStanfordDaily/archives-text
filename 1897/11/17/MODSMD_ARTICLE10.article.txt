Notice lias been given of a meet
ing for a grand rally to be lie Id in
the chapel tomorrow night in honor
of the football men, who leave the
next morning for Congress Springs
to complete training. As this is
the last time before the game that
we will see the men who are to
fight for their University on
Thanksgiving day, it is the duty of
every Stanford man to turn out
and show the men that we are
thoroughly with them and appreci
ative of the hard work they have
done in preparation for the great
contest. Stanford spirit has gone
far to win more than one contest,
and an inspiration for the men
may be just what is needed. If
you have University spirit and
honor your teams, come prepared
to express it. Make this rally the
best we have ever had. To be
that, it must be enthusiastic in
deed. We expect every Stanford
man to do his duty.
