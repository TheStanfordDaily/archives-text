# Sale of Seats.
## 
### 
The sale of seats both for the
football game and for the Thanks
giving night show takes place in
the postolfice tomorrow at 1:30 p.m.
Football seats in the yelling sec
tion are $1.50; general admission,
$1.00; grandstand seats, $2.00; box
seats, $2.50.
Seats for the Thanksgiving night
show at the Bush Street Theatre are
seventy-five cents and one dollar.
General admission is fifty cents.
