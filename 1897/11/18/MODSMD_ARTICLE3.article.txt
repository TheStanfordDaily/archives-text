# Exhibition of Pictures.
## 
### 
An exhibition of pictures will be
given at Roble Hall on Thursday,
Friday, and Saturday, November
18, 19, and 20 from three to five
o'clock, by Miss Hadden. These
pictures are from the portfolios of
Mr. W. C. Vickery of San Fran
cisco. ' All lovers of pictures are
invited.
