# Excursion Rates.
## 
### 
The excursion tickets for the
Thanksgiving game will be on sale
at the pos to Alice tomorrow and
Wednesday at 2 p.m. The $1
tickets are good only on the 8:80
train going and on all trains up to
Friday noon returning. The $1.30
tickets are good on all trains leav
ing on Thursday and returning up
to Monday inclusive.
