# Final Rally Tomorrow Night.
## 
### 
The final football rally this
year will be held in Encina gym
nasium at half-past seven tomorrow
night. This rally will be held
primarily for the purpose of organ
izing the yelling and to practice
the Stanford songs. The Univer
sity authorities have given their
permission for the students to use
the gymnasium with the under
standing that if any property is
destroyed it will not be so easy to
obtain permission for its use
for rallies in the future.
White the authorities admit that
the property of the University is
for the free use of the students now
here, they will insist that no spirit
of recklessness shall make it im
possible for other students yet to
come to use the same property.
