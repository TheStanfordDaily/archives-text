# The 						Eternal City.
## 
### 
The fifth lecture of Dr. Kriehn's
public course in Art History, with
stereopticon illustrations, will he
held in the chapel on Thursday,
December 2, at 7:30 p.m. It will
treat of the ruins of ancient Rome;
medieval Rome of the popes; the
palaces and churches of the Renais
sance; and the Vatican collections
of art.
