# Announcements.
## 
### 
Euphronia.—There will be no
meeting of Euphronia Saturday
night. Duncan MacKinnon,
President.
New Books. —The following new
books are at the Bookstore: Capes,
The Early Empire, The Age of the
Antonines; Henley, A Book of
Verses; Oliphant, The Ways of
Life; Heyse, Deutsche Grammatik.
Women's Edition.—All commit
tees on the special edition of the
Daily Palo Alto will meet
promptly tomorrow at 1:30 p.m. in
the chapel. Chairmen will come
prepared to report.
Marion Adams, Editor-in-Chief.
Student Body Meeting.—There
will be a meeting of the student
body tomorrow at 2 p.m. in the
chapel, to nominate candidates for
football manager and editor-in
chief of the Daily Palo Alto.
A. B. Morgan, President.
Football Men.—There will be a
meeting of the football players
Monday evening at 7:30 in the
lobby of Encina Hall to elect a cap
tain. All who played in any
scheduled game are entitled to
vote. S. W. Cotton, Captain.
University Women. —The board
of editors of the last issue of the
Daily Palo Alto requests that all
the women of the student body
meet tomorrow at 1:30 p.m. in the
chapel. Progress will be reported
by the various committees and gen
eral discussion solicited.
