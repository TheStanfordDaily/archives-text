# College Notes.
## 
### 
—A lacrosse team has been
formed at Columbia.
—A Catholic Club has been
formed at Haverford.
—England has no college papers,
while America claims about 200.
—The Harvard-Princeton debate
will be held in Cambridge May 6.
—The University of Chicago has
a vegetarian club of forty-two
members.
—The Universities of Kansas
and Missouri have arranged for a
joint debate.
—The freshman class at Brown
has voted sweaters to the class
football team.
—The Yale-Harvard debate will
take place next Friday evening.
The question to be debated is: "Re
solved, Hawaii should be annexed
to the United States." Harvard
has the affirmative.
