# Christmas Excursion South.
## 
### 
Arrangements are nearing com
pletion for the annual excursion to
the Southern part of the State dur
ing the Christinas holidays. It is
purposed this year to have special
cars for the use of Stanford stu
dents and these will go straight
through to Los Angeles. The ex
cursion will include all points of
Southern California and the rates
will be the same as last year.
Tickets will he on sale at the
Palo Alto station December 21, 22,
and 23, and will be good on all
trains leaving within three days
after date of sale find returning on
or before January 22. Berths may
be reserved by applying to J. E.
McDowell at the Registrar's office,
who will also give further informa
tion.
