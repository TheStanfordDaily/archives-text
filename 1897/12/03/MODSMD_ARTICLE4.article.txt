# Chapel Services.
## 
### 
The services at the chapel the
next few Sunday mornings will
have more than ordinary interest.
Mr. Hyde is planning to have
music an attractive feature from
now until after the holidays. Next
Sunday morning Mrs. Mary Rob
erts Smith will sing "He Was
Despised" from the "Messiah."
The Sunday before Christmas a
full musical program will be ar
ranged for.
