# The
						Women's Edition.
## 
### 
The following young Indies form
the editorial board of the special
edition of the Daily Palo Alto
which is to appear next Friday:
Editor-in-chief, Miss Marion
Adams; managing editor, Miss H.
M. Macgillivray; news editor, Miss
Grace C. Oilman; business man
ager, Miss Frances Tucker; assist
ant business manager, Miss Helen
Swett; associate editors, Misses
Londa L. Stebbins, Grace Dinkel
spiel, Helen Williams, M. L. Cald
well, K. A. Hooper, Bonnie Burk
halter, Mary E. Walter, L. B.
Everett, Katherine A. Chandler,
and L. Patterson.
