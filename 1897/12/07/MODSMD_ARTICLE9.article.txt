# Professor Griggs Entertained.
## 
### 
A reception and dinner was given
by Miss Bertha Monroe Rickoff in
San Francisco on Friday evening
last in honor of Professor Griggs.
The house was tastefully decorated
with cardinal carnations, and about
a hundred notable people, both in
and without the faculty, were pres
ent. Professor Griggs read a paper
on "The scientific study of the
higher human life," which was dis
cussed by those present.
