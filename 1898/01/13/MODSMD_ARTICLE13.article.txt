# Death of Charles Edgar Butler.
## 
### 
Charles Edgar Butler died in
I'alo Alio, Monday, December 20.
Mr. Butler was a graduate of Har
vard from the class of '90, and sit
one time took up post-graduate
work at Stanford, but was com
pelled to leave on account of the
ill health which finally resulted in
his death from consumption. His
parents reside in Palo Alto, The
funeral services were conducted by
Dr. Thoburn.
