# Judges for the Preliminaries.
## 
### 
The executive committee of the
Inter-Society Debating League met
this afternoon at 1:80 p.m. and se
lected judges for the preliminary
debates to lie held on January 20,
21, and 22. The judges chosen are
Professors Ross, Lathrop, and how.
The arrangements for the de
bates are in charge of a sub-com
mittee composed of Hayden, '00,
Yost, '99, and Miss Stebbins
(chairman).
