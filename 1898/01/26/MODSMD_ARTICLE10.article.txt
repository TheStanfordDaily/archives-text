# Day of Player to be Observed.
## 
### 
The"Day o Prayer" for Ameri
can colleges will be observed at
Stanford by a special service in the
chapel tomorrow evening at 7:80
when the topic, "The need of
greater Christian activity in col
lege," will be discussed. In addi
tion to the prayer service a quar
tet composed of Messrs. Gunn,
Dawson, Clark, and Wilkinson
will take part in the musical part
of the program. Professor John
ston will have charge of the services
and President Jordan, Dr. Wood,
Miss Macgillivray, and Professors
Miller and Show will each give a
short address upon the different
phases of Christian activity in col
lege life.
