# Plans for Inter-Society Meetings.
## 
### 
The executive committee of the
Inter-Society Debating League met
this afternoon and discussed plans
for holding inter-society meetings
this semester. Upon motions by
A. B. Morgan and Miss Stebbins
two were appointed to
devise plans for the meetings: the
first consisting of J. T. Burcham,
'97, Miss Andrews, and F. .). Mar
vin, '01; and the second of Miss
Stebbins, Carl Hayden, '00, and O.
C. Leiter, '99. These committees
were ordered to report a week from
Thursday.
