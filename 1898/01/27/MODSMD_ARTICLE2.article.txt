# Senior Election.
## 
### 
The Senior Class meets tomorrow
afternoon at 2 p.m. in the chapel
for the election of officers. The
following nominees have no op
ponents: Vice-president, John M.
Oskison; secretary, H. I. Shoe
maker; treasurer, F. S. Howard;
baseball manager, A. S. Jeffs; track
manager, C. M. Fickert.
The candidates for the other of
fices are:
For president —Charles M. Man
non, S. W. Charles, L. B. Spencer.
For historian —Miss Hallie Hyde,
Miss B. Burckhalter.
For sergeant-at-arms —W. W.
Kemp, C. JVI. Fickert.
