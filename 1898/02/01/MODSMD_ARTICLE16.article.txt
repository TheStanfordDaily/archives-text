Announcements and contribu
tions of any nature intended for
publication in the Daily Palo
Alto may be placed in the box,
provided for the purpose, at the
corner of the University uostofiice.
It is a long established custom of
this paper that no contributions,
unless of an extraordinary news
value, will be printed if received
after 4 p.m. It is also a rule.of
this paper that such contributions
shall be signed by the author, in
order that their authenticity may
be ascertained, if these rules are
complied with, announcements,
notices, and news items will be
gladly printed.
