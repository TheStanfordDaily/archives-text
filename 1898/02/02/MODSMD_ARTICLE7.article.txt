# For '98's Benefit.
## 
### 
The following are a-nong those
who have consented to take part in
the '98 Annual benefit in thechapel
next Saturday evening: The Glee
and Mandolin Clubs, W. H. Irwin,
'98, F. B. Riley, '00, J. 8. Briscoe,
'00, Miss Grace Dinkelspiel, '00.
Mrs. E. D. Starbuck will render
the Hungarian Rhapsody, No. 2,
from Liszt.
One hundred of the best seats in
the chapel have been reserved and
will be on sale at thirty-five cents
in the postoffice from 2to 5 p.m.
Friday.
