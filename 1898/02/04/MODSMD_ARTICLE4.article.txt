# Klauber and Lanagan.
## 
### 
A few of the baseball men of the
Senior Class met in room 10 this
afternoon for the election of a base
ball captain this year. Hugo
Klauber was nominated by (). B.
Wight and being the only nominee
was declared elected.
J. F. Lanagan was this after
noon unanimously elected captain
of the 1900 baseball team. The
following men who played on last
year's class team voted: C. B.
Strohn, Murphy, Beckett, Chand
ler, Cochran.
