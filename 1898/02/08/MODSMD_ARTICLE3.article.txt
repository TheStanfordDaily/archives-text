# Library Association.
## 
### 
The Library Association of Cen
tral California will meet on Wash
ington's Birthday, February 22, at
Stanford. The meeting has been
postponed from its regular date,
February 11, for the reason that it
was desired to hold an anniversary
meeting of the association, and
also that a visit might be paid to
the University. The meeting will
be held in one of the lecture-rooms,
and will commence at 2 p.m.
The following program has been
prepared for the occasion:
Remarks by the president, Mr.
George T. Clark.
"New York State Library and
Library School," Dr. E. Dana Du
raiul.
"The relation of libraries to
righteousness," Dr. Edward A.
Ross.
