# Quads.
## 
### 
—Alpha Literary Society met
this afternoon and decided upon
its representation in the Annual.
—The intercollegiate debating
committees will meet Saturday
morning at the Palace Hotel to
conclude arrangements for the In
tercollegiate Debate.
— Word was received this after
noon from Thomas of Berkeley that
arrangements have been made to
accommodate one hundred Stanford
students in a separate section at
the Carnot Debate.
—The committee of judges in the
Junior Annual competition for
prize story and poem, consisting of
Professors Murray, Lathrop, and
Newcomer, will announce its de
cision tomorrow.
