# Stanford vs. Santa Clara.
## 
### 
The Varsity nine left this after
noon by bus for Santa Clara, where
they will play a practice game with
the college team. Santa Clara has
always pressed the Varsity close
for championship honors, and the
result of the game is awaited with
interest. The Varsity was com
posed as follows for the game:
Swindells, c; Beckett, p; Young, lb;
Harris, 2b; Lougheed, captain, ss;
Strohn, 3b; Klauber, If; Wight, cf;
Murphy, rf. Hill will be substitute
pitcher.
