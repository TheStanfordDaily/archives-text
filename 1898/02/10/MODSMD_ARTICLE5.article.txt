# Announcements.
## 
### 
At Home. — Kappa Kappa
Gamma will be at home the second
Saturday evening of each month.
'98 Baseball Men. —All candi
dates for the '98 baseball team are
requested to be on the baseball
field Friday at 3 p.m.
H. Klauber, Captain.
New Books. —The following new
books have been received at the
University Bookstore: Bryce, The
American Commonwealth; Huxley,
The Crayfish; Kulpe, Outlines of
Psychology.
Faculty Women's Club. —There
will be a meeting of the Faculty
Women's Club on Monday, Febru
ary 14, at 3 p.m., with Mrs. Griffin
on the campus. No program
will be given.
Outing Club. —The Stanford
Outing Club will hold its regular
meeting Monday evening in room
31 quad at 7:30. Mr. W. \V. Price
will address the club on the habits
of the birds and mammals of this
vicinity. Members of the club are
re<|nested to bring their friends.
