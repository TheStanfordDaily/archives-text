# Freshmen Meet.
## 
### 
TheClnssof 1901 met in the chapel
this afternoon and instructed the
class council to purchase twelve
sweaters for the men who played
in the Freshman football game
with Berkeley. Miller and Ed
munds were nominated for first
vice-president and Rodolph, Wads
worth, Wright, and Jackson for
second vice-president. Erb, Eng
lish, and Craig were appointed yell
leaders for Thursday's baseball
game with the Sophomores.
