# Peary Lecture Postponed.
## 
### 
News was received here today
that Lieutenant Peary could not
keep his lecture engagement here
Friday evening, and that in con
sequence it would have to be post
poned until Friday, February '25.
Mr. Peary's advance agent had
booked him for Los Angeles with
out notifying Friedlander, Gottlob
<V r Co., who had made the Stanford
engagement for him. Seats will be
on sale again next Monday.
