# Outing Club Meeting.
## 
### 
The Outing Club of the Univer
sity held its regular monthly meet
ing Monday evening and a very in
teresting program was rendered. W.
W. Price and Milnor Roberts gave
very interesting talks on the habits
of birds and mammals of this
vicinity, and exhibited specimens
of almost every bird and animal to
be found in the neighboring region.
The club now lias fifty active
members and considerable interest
is being aroused in the outing ex
cursions of the club. The next one
will be taken on March 5 to Kings
Mountain, and the expedition will
be in charge of the botanists of the
club.
