# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of the
Associated Students met Friday
evening.
The report of Mr. Adams as a
committee on Women's Athletics
was accepted. Upon motion the
matter of an appropriation for Wo
men's Athletics was taken from the
table, and after consideration, $65
was appropriated for track and
basket-ball purposes.
A motion to take from the table
the matter of appropriating $2 to
each member of the Glee and Man
dolin Clubs from the proceeds of
the concert to be given in San Fran
cisco the evening before the Field
day, was lost. The clubs had re

quested that such an appropriation
he made to re-imburse the members
for the extra registration fee paid to
the University for late registration.
Mr. Bailie's report as a member
of the Intercollegiate Debating
Committee was accepted.
