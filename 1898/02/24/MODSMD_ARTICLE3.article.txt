# Senior Benefit.
## 
### 
President Jordan has consented
to deliver a lecture for the benefit
of the '98 Annual on "The Passion
Play at Ober-Atnmergau," and the
date has been fixed for next Wed
nesday, March 2, in the University
chapel. The lecture will be illus
trated by stereopticon views.
Tickets will be on sale at the Uni
versity Bookstore, Monday, Tues
day, and Wednesday. Reserved
seats are thirty-five cents; general
admission twenty-five cents.
