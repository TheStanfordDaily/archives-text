# Stanford Day at the Mining Fair.
## 
### 
Next Saturday will We Stanford
Day at the State Mining Fair in
San Francisco. The admission fee
for students will he reduced to fif
teen cents, and an agent from the
Mining Fair will be on the campus
tomorrow to place the tickets on
sale. The railroad company will
give the regular $1.25 rate. Noth
ing has heen decided definitely as
to the program, except that Presi
dent Jordan will give an address.
