# Announcements.
## 
### 
Ladies' Chokal. —Meeting of the
Ladies' Choral, 7 o'clock Wednes
day evening in Roble parlor. More
members wanted.
Track Men. —1 hereby appoint
Paul E. Walker captain of the
track team during my absence.
John Brunton.
Track Men. —All entries for the
handicap games, which will take
place on March 12, must be handed
to me before Friday noon.
T. T. C. Gregory, Manager.
