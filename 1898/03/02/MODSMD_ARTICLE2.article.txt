# Encina Club Committees.
## 
### 
The board of trustees of the En
cina Hall Club met last evening
and effected a permanent organiza
tion by electing C. M. Fickert pres
ident, O. C. Leiter vice-president,
J. M. Switzer secretary, and Alfred
Haslacher treasurer.
At the meeting this afternoon
the following committees were ap
pointed:
Trophies —C. M. Fickert, '98, N.
C lea vela nd, '98, 0. M. Bradley, '99,
R. L Wilbur, '96, and Horace
Pomeroy, '97.
Entertainment-- Ralph Arnold,
'99, Charles Mannon, '98, Harry
Weil, '01, W. H. Irwin, '98, and
C. D. Ray, '00.
Decoration —J. M. Switzer. '98,
John Daggett, '00, R. C. Victor, '01,
Garth Parker, '99, and William
Erb, '01.
Literature —William Erb, '01,
Dane Coolidge,'9B, J. M.Oskison,
'98, and R. A. Leiter, '99.
Furnishings—A. L. Haslacher,
'00, Forrest Fisher, '98, W. A.
Prichard, '98, 11. H. Taylor, '00,
and M. C. Leventritt, '99.
Constitution and Rules—o. C.
Leiter, '99, Professor C. A. Duniwav,
J. M. Switzer, '98, J. C. Swenson,
'98, S. P. Elias, '99, and W. A.
Prichard, '98.
Finance —Alfred Haslacher, '00,
James Bentley, '01, Ralph Filcher,
'99, J. R. Hamilton, '01, 1). F.
Meikeljohn, '00. Chandler, '99, and
N. D. Miller, '00.
Games —H. E. Lougheed, '00.
Hugo Klauber, '98, Erie Veuve,
'99, G. T. Forsyth, '99, G. Porter
Roberts, '00, and A. M. Strong, '99.
The board of trustees will con
fer with President Jordan tomorrow
regarding the status and powers of
the board.
