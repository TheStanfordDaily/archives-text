# Berkeley Debaters.
## 
### 
Twenty-four debaters are striving
to represent the University of Cali
fornia in the Intercollegiate De
bate. The preliminaries and finals
will be held within a few weeks.
The list of entries includes
Euphrat, Rainey, Overstreet, Leslie,
Teaby, Dannebaum, Kilkenny,
Clark, Creed, Mannon, Daniels,
Thomas, Fryer, Quayle, Martin,
Young, Ford, Pierce, Gardiner,
Marks, Payne, Golden, Kimbal.
