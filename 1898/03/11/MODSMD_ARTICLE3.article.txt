# Sophomore Meeting.
## 
### 
The Class of 1900 met in room
45 quad this afternoon. Wallace
A. Irwin was nominated for editor
in-chief of the 1900 Annual,
Ernest Wilson and Alfred Ilas
lacher for business manager. After
adjournment the name of O. P.
Palstine was proposed for business
manager. The election will be
held next Friday.
