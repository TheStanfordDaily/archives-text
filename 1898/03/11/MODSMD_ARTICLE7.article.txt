# Yesterday's Lecture.
## 
### 
Hadji Ephraim Benquiat of Con
stantinople lectured yesterday af
ternoon in the chapel on the his
tory, value, and manufacture of
Persian and Oriental rugs and
hangings. The lecture was given
in costume, and was illustrated by
about one hundred rugs, whose ag
gregate value was $10,000, and
many of which were several hun
dred years old. The lecturer, who
has been a rug connoisseur for over
twenty-five years, was awarded the
five highest prizes at the World's
Fair of 1898, and has exhibited his
collection in most of the large
cities of both the old and the new
worlds. The number exhibited at
the University was about one-fifth
of his collection.
