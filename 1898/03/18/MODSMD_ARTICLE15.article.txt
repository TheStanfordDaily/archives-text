—The German Conversation
Club met at Miss Schallenberger's
yesterday afternoon.
Mrs. Fish is at Pacific LJrove
for her health. Professor Fish
spent this week there.

his examination in Economies 1
until after the Easter vacation.

Miss Elizabeth Miller will give a
solo; the choir will sing "tiloria,"
from Mozart's Twelfth Mass.
