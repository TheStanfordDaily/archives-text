# Announcements.
## 
### 
Junior Hop.—There will be a
meeting of the Junior Hop Com
mittee Wednesday in room 10
quad at 1:30 p.m.
R. M. Smith, Chairman.
Women's Athletic Association.
—There will be a meeting of the
Women's Athletic Association in
room 80 quad Thursday at 4:30
p.m. Lou Henry, Pres.
Senior Women. —Mrs. Mary Rob
erts Smith desires to meet all the
fourth year women (graduating
either in January or May) of the
University in the chapel Wednes
day at 1:30 p.m.
Pacific Grove Excursion. —
There will be the usual excursions
to Pacific Grove and Santa Cruz
during the vacation. Tickets will
be good going on Friday, Saturday,
and Sunday, returning up to April
17. Several parties have already
been formed, and the prospects are
favorable for a large excursion.
