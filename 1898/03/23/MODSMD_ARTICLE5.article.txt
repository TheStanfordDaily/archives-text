# For Next Year's "Quad."
## 
### 
President Burnett of the Sopho
more Class has appointed the com
mittee of three, which, with
the editor and business man
ager, will choose the board of
editors for next year's Junior
Quad. The committee is as
follows: Alfred Haslacher, J. F.
Lanagan, N. D. Miller, Editor
W. A. Irwin, and Manager 0. P.
Palstine. The committee will meet
the editor-in-chief tomorrow in
room 126 Encina at 2:30 p.m. to
appoint the board, so that a policy
may be outlined at once.
