# Lectures by Mrs. Smith.
## 
### 
Mrs. Mary Roberts Smith has
prepared the following lectures,
which will be given during the re
mainder of the semester:
March 26 —before the Social Set
tlement of San Francisco, on "The
English ballad." The lecture will
be accompanied by illustrative
songs.
April 16 —before the Child-Study
Association of Santa Cruz, on "He
redity as a social burden."
April 30 —before the Women's
Club of Santa Rosa; subject, yet to
be chosen.
May 3 —before the Women's Con
gress of San Francisco; subject,
"Clothes and character."
