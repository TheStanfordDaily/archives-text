# Her Wish.
## 
### 
Wee Hostess—Mamma, shall I invito
Lucy Littnay ro my party?
Mamma—Certainly. Hhe is the minis
tor's daughter.
"Do minister's daughters get invited
everywhere?"
"Always."
"They has lots of fun, I s'pose. I wish
my papa was a minister 'stead of an isir
uble sinner."—London Tit-liits.
