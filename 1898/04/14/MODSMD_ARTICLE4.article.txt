# Hand-Ball Game.
## 
### 
Some fifty young ladies witnessed
a very enjoyable game of hand-ball
played this afternoon in the Roble
gymnasium by Chester Murphy
and Georgia Caswell, '01, against
Tom Story and Parnie Hamilton,
'98. Dr. Wood umpired the game.
Mr Murphy and Miss Caswell won
with the following score: 21-5,21-8,
21-2.
