# Change in the Time-Table.
## 
###

effect on the Coast Division on Sun
day. A few slight changes are
made in the time of trains, the
principal ones being in the arrival
of the south-hound flyer, whiuh is
fifteen minutes later, at 8:45, and
of the north-hound 4:00 train, now
4:14. The flyer does not stop at
Menlo Park. The Sunday excur
sion train south passes Palo Alto
at 8:38 a.m. and returns nt 7:24
p.m.
