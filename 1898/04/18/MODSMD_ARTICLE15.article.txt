# Choral Concert.
## 
### 
The "Melba Choral" of the
Woman's Club of Palo Alto will
give a concert at Nortree Hall on
Friday evening, April 22. The
Choral will be assisted by Miss
Grace Barstow, violinist, and Miss
Florence Parke, pianist. The or
chestra will consist of nine pieces
from the Stanford Symphony Club.
Miss Barstow is well known at
Stanford as a musician of great
ability. She studied for some years
under Joachim, and has won great
praise for her mastery of the
violin. The Choral, under the
able leadership of Mrs. Genevieve
H. Wright, has enrolled thirty-five
ladies, and the concert promises to
be one of the best ever given in
alo Alto.
