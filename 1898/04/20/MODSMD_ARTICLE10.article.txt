# Vesper Service Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The weekly vesper service will be
held in the chapel tomorrow after
noon at 5 p.m. and will he led by
Professor Johnston on the subject,

''Whom say ye that i am?" The
musical part of the service will be
made especially attractive, the fol
lowing taking part, in addition to
the University Choir led by Mr.
Hyde: Dr. Little, Miss Jagger, Mrs.
Thoburn, Mr. Atkinson, and the
male quartette. The vesper ser
vice has burn substituted for the
daily chap< 1 exercises, and the in
novation has proved very popular.
