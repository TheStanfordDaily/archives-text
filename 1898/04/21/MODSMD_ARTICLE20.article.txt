# Junior Farce.
## 
### 
The Junior Farce by \\\ A. Ir
win, '00. entitled "'The Bone of Con
tention,' 1 will t:ike place in Encina
gymnasium next Thursday, April
29. A large cast under the direc
tion of stage manager W. H. Irwin
is busily rehearsing every night.
Leading students will take part.
The farce is full of life and go, and
there is not a dull line in it. Tick
ets will be on sale next Monday
in the University postoffice.
