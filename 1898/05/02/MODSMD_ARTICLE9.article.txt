# Declinations.
## 
### 
Declination. — I hereby beg to
decline the nomination of baseball
manager. Alfred Haslacheu.
To the Student Body.— Feeling
that 1 shall not have the necessary
time to devote to the track team as
its manager, I must decline the
nomination with thanks.
R. K. Culver.
Declination. —I most respect
fully decline my nomination for
business manager of the Daily
Palo Alto in favor of B. P. Stan
hope, as I have enlisted and taken
the oath for volunteer service in the
U. S. A. G. E. Leavitt.
