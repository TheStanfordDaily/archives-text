# An Open Letter to Mr. Culver.
## 
### 
Stanford University, Cal.,
May 3, 1898.
My dear Mr. Culver: 1 have read
wiih regret yoifr declination of the
candidacy for the managership of
the track team.
The coming year promisee to he
a very critical time for track and
field athletics at Stanford, and it is
especially necessary that the man
ager should he some one in whom
all feel confidence. lam only add
ing an additional voice to the very
general demand that you waive
your personal inclination and re
consider your declination.
I am very sincerely yours,
F. Angkll.
