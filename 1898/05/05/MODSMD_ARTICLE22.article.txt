# Stanford Military Company.
## 
### 
The Stanford military company
which was recently organized with
Chandler, '99, as captain, is drill
ing every evening on the oval with
lifty-eight men in the ranks. The
purpose of the company is to gain
experience in military drill and to
prepare fora possible emergency.
The men drilling receive gymnas
ium credit. More men are needed
to till up the company, and stu
dents desiring to join are urged to
do so.
