# Faculty-Senior Baseball Match.
## 
### 
The annual Faculty-Senior base
ball game will be played on Satur
day, May 14. The game is to be
played one week earlier than usual
owing to the departure of Dr. An
gell and several others of the Fac
ulty nine. A baseball match will
be arranged for Commencement
Week.
