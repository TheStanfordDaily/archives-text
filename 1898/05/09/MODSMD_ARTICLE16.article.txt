Summer Board. — Mrs.' W. F.
Dingley, Kingsley avenue, between
Bryant and Waverley, will furnish
regular and table board during the
summer.
For Rent. — A pretty Santa
Cruz home, furnished completely
for housekeeping. Will rent for
the entire summer or by the month.
Terms reasonable to a careful
tenant. Ten rooms, electric lights,
good piano. Location midway be
tween the postotlice and the beach,
just off Pacific avenue on Laurel
street. Address
Mrs. S. H. Spalsbury,
Palo Alto.
