# Stratford's Souvenir.
## 
### 
A unique souvenir of the Uni
versity has been issued by J. C.
Stratford containing a complete
collection of campus views. In
addition there are portraits of the
founders and faculty. Each page
is printed from a negative. The
entire collection is sold at $3.50
and $4.50. At Wilson's store.
