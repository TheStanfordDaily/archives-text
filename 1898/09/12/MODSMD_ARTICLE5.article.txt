# Ladies' Mandolin Club.
## 
### 
The Ladies' Mandolin Club was
called to order Thursday at 4:30 by
President Dyer, there being present,
Mandolins, Misses Earl, Richards,
McDougald, and Olcovieh; gui
tars, Misses Dyer, Swett, and
Mitchell. With but little prelimi
nary discussion it was decided to
make an attempt to maintain an
active club this year. Regular re
hearsals will begin next Tuesday
at 4:80 p.m. in room 38 quad. It is
urged by those interested in the
club that the example of those new
students who responded to the call
for players be followed by any oth
ers who love the cardinal and can
play.
