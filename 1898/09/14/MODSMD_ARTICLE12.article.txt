# Quads.
## 
### 
— Miss Gertrude Thompson of
Philadelphia, accompanied by her
aunt, Mrs. Thompson of Palo Alto,
visited the University today.
—The sprinkling carts and a
harrow were kept busy all morning
on the football field, which now be
gins to assume its old appearance.
—Mrs. Margaret (1. Hood, ex-'99,
is visiting at the University. She
expects to leave soon for Tempe,
Arizona, where she will resume her
duties as principal of the training
department of the Tempe Normal.
