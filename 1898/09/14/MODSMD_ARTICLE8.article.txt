# Zoological Club Meeting.
## 
### 
The Zoology Club met Monday
night at the resilience of Dr. Gilbert.
Dr. Gilbert gave a talk on the his
tory of the geological hike Lahontan
and the fish fauna of its existing rem
nants. He reviewed the facts upon
which the geological history is
based, and then those based upon
biological evidence, principally the
distribution of Hshes in the Lahon
tan basin, and showed how import
ant are the latter class of facts
when compared with the former in
working out a complete geological
history. Officers were elected for
the present year: President, LI. E.
Snodgrass, '99; vice-president, J. F.
Abbott, '99; treasurer, H.M.Spauld
ing, '01.
