# Sophomore Meeting.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Class of '01 was
held in room 50 today, W. M. Erb
presiding. The following officers
were nominated: President, Otto
Hahn; first vice-president, C. B.
Gillespi; second vice-president, Miss
Sobey; secretary, J. F. English;
treasurer, R. C. Victor; class coun
cil, Messrs. Cantwell, Lemmon,Erb,
Cummings,Carl, and Bradley; foot
ball manager, P. K. Oilman; base
ball manager, C. J. Swindells; track
captain, F. 11. Foster; sergeant-at
arms, Harry Weil; class historian
Miss Winifred Morgan.
