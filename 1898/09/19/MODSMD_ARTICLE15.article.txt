# Athletic Committee Meets.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Athletic Coni'
mittee was held in room 11 quad
this afternoon. Bentley, '01, was
elected secretary of the committee
for the year. The appointment by
Manager Prichard of Alfred Has
lacher, '00, to be assistant football
manager, was confirmed. Dole, '99,
Smith, '99, and Erb, '01, were elect
ed to represent Stanford at the an
nual meeting of the A. A. U. which
is to be hold in Oakland tonight.
