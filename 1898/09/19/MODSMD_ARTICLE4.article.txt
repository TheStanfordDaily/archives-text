# Books for Oral Discussion.
## 
### 
To meet the needs of this year's
class in Oral Discussion and to
more thoroughly equip the general
library, the following hooks have
been ordered upon the suggestion of
Instructors Lathrop and Duniway:
Goodrich, Select British Eloquence;
Snyder, Great Speeches of Great
Lawyers; Donovan, Modern Jury
Trials; Whitman, American Elo
quence; also Orators of the French
Revolution, Household Book of
Irish Eloquence, and Wagner's
Modern Political Orations.
