# Inter-Society Debating League.
## 
### 
The different literary societies
last Saturday night elected the fol
lowing representatives to the ex
ecutive committee of the Inter-
Society Debating League:
Euphronia — James Ferguson,
'99, A. H. Suzzallo, '00, and Duncan
MacKinnon, '99.
Pliilolexia —R. E. Filcher, '99,
Carl Hayden, '00, and S. P. Elias,
'99.
Alpha—L. B. Wickersham, '00,
A. C. Whitaker, '99, and R. A.
Leiter, '99.
Nestoria —Chandler, '99, M. C.
Decarli, '00, and A. L. Dixon, '00.
Saturday Might Club — Miss
Katharine A. Chandler, '00, Miss
Helen Swett, '00, and Miss L. B.
Everett, '00.
