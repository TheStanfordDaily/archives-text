# News from Prof. and Mrs. Hudson.
## 
### 
Professor W. 11. Hudson, in a
letter to Professor E. D. Durand,
from London, writes that he and
Mrs. Hudson are enjoying the best
of health. He is doing work in
investigation in the British Mu
seum and some writing for a Brit
ish magazine. Professor and Mrs.
E. A. Ross had left London for
Paris a few days before the letter
was written.
