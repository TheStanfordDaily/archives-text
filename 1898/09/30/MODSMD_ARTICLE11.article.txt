# Junior Class Meeting.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Junior Class
this afternoon it was decided to
postpone the appearance of the
class plugs from next Monday eve
ning in order to allow time for dec
oration and to permit the ladies of
the class to take part in the initia
tory exercises.
There will be a ceremony Thurs
day evening on the campus and
the plugs will be worn on the qutid
Friday. The following committee
has been appointed with full power
to arrange for the ceremonies:
11. H. Taylor, A. Haslacher, Jas.
Lanagan, Mrs. D. P. Austin, and
W. A. Irwin, chairman.
