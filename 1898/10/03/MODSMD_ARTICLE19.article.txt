# Rice For Freshman Captain.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the freshman
football candidates held Saturday
evening Arthur H. Rice was elected
captain of the team. Rice entered
the University from Mt. Tamalpais
Military Academy where he was
captain of his school's eleven.
While acting in 'hat capacity he
gained valuable gridiron experi
ence. Previous to entering Mt.
Tamalpais he participated in foot
ball at his home in Honolulu. He
weighs about 155 pounds. He is
a dashing man with the ball, very
fast on kicks and a sure, hard
tackier.
