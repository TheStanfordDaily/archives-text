# W.A.A. Election.
## 
### 
The following is the result of the
election of officers held by the Wo
men's Association on Monday and
Tuesday, October 8 and 4:
President, Miss Lillian Ray;
vice-president, Miss May me Mer
ritt, '98; secretary, Miss Frances
Tucker,' 99; treasurer, Miss Helen
Schelley, '00; directors, Miss Marie
Markham, '99, Miss Clara Martin,
'01, Miss Helen Ayer, '01, Miss
Alice M. Cole, '02, Miss Mary Mar
tin, '02 (special), Miss Maud
March (faculty).
