# Ladies' Glee Club.
## 
### 
The Ladies' Glee Club met yes
terday afternoon to take steps
toward permanent organization.
Inasmuch as twenty young ladies
present could speak for as many
more who wish to join, no hesita
tion was felt in going ahead with
arrangements. No time has vet
been set for regular rehearsals, but
try-outs have been arranged for
Friday and Monday afternoons.
Oct. 14 and 17. at 4:30 p. m..in
the chapel, ft is earnestly Imped
by the leader, Mrs. Wright, and by
those who have already given in
their names, that all young ladies
who can will come to the try-outs.
Alto voices especially are wanted.
