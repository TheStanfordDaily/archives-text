# Expedition to the Galapagos Islands.
## 
### 
R. E. Snodgrass '99, and Kd
mond Heller '00, students in the
Zoology department, have been
chosen to make a collecting expedi
tion to the Galapagos islands. Mr.
Timothy Hopkins is backing the
expedition, but all the collections
made by Snodgrass and Heller will
be donated to the University.
They will make an extensive
trip, leaving San Francisco about
November first to be gone about
ten months. The Galapagos islantlj
are about six hundred miles west
of Ecuador and are on the equator.
They are noted for their fine speci
mens of plants.
