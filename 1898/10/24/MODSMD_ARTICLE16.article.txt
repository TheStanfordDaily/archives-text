cital next Friday evening in the

ford Washington Memorial Asso

Tuesday Evening Lecture.
George K French of the San Fran
cisco Call, will deliver the regular
weekly lecture in the chapel Tues
day evening, his subject being
"Kumarsi, Ashanti, and the Gold
Coast Colony." The lecture will be
illustrated by lantern slides.
Republican Club. —There will
be an important meeting of the
Stanford University Republican
Club on Wednesday evening in
room 50 quad at 7:30 p. in. Ar
rangements for the intercollegiate
rally to be held in San Francisco
next Saturday evening will be talk
ed over and invitations to the rally
and badges entitling the wearer to
a seat in student section will be dis
tributed. It is imperative that this
be the most important meeting of
the club during the campaign.
S. G. Bailie. President.
