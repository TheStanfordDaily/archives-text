# Berkeley Notes.
## 
### 
The Board of Regents of the
State University yesterday accepted
the resignation of President Kel
logg. On Charter day, March 23,
Mr. Kellogg will turn over the
presidency and become professor
emeritus of Latin.
The architects who won in the
first trial of the Hearst architect
ural competition at Antwerp are
expected to arrive at Berkeley some
time after Nov. 15.
