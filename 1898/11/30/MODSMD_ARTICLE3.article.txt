# Institute Work of Prof. Cubberley.
## 
### 
Professor Cuhberley lectured last
Friday at Dunismuir and preached
a sermon Sunday at Vreka. He
lias been in attendance at the in
stitute at Red Muff the first three
days of this week, and went from
there to Redding today to deliver a
lecture at the institute of Siskiyou
county. He will return to the
University tomorrow and meet his
classes Friday morning. Friday
night he will lecture in Santa Rosa
before the teachers of Sonoma
county upon the "Underlying mean
ings of education."
