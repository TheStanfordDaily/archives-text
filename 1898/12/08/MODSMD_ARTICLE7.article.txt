# Excursion Rates.
## 
### 
A special round-trip rate of $3.1)0
will be given from Palo Alto to
Pacific Grove for the Christmas
vacation. Tickets will be good
going on December 21, 22, and 23,
and returning up to January 15,
1899.
Special round-trip rates will be
given to the following points on the
Coast Division of the Southern
Pacific for the vacation:
Paso Robles $ 7 J5O
San Luis Obispo 9 20
Guadalupe 10 80
Surf 12 60
Tickets will be on sale December
19, 20, 21, and 22, good fur return
up to January 22.
