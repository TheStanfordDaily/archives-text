# Death of Dr. Jordan's Mother.
## 
### 
Mrs. liulda Hawley Jordan,
mother of Dr. Jordan, died at the
home of Dr. Jordan's sister, Mrs.
Edward J. Edwards, in Minne

apolis, on the 26th of lust month, at
the age of 80.
Mrs. Jordan's husband died in
1888, since which time she had been
living with her daughter. Mrs.
Jordan was horn at Whitehall,
New Jersey, in 1812, her parents
being of Puritan extraction.
Though her death was not expected
so soon, it was known that Mrs.
Jordan could withstand no serious
illness on account of her advanced
age.
