# Musical Clubs Meet.
## 
### 
The combined Glee and Mando
lin Clubs held their first meeting of
this year last evening. It was de
cided that an effort should be made
to hold the clubs together and sev
eral new and important committees
were appointed. The clubs ex
pressed themselves heartily in favor
of the plan of giving a concert in
the near future for the benefit of
the training-house fund.
