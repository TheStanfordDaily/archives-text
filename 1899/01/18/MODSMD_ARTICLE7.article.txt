# Sword and Sandals.

###

Sandals held a meeting last night,
and elected new members. The
following men now constitute the
club: Riley, Briscoe, Sladen, For
sythe, Erb, W. A. Irwin, Culver,
Drake, Rogers, Ross, Strohn, Lana
gan. Riley is president and Bris
