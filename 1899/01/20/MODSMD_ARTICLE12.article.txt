Why should not those who travel
across Continent enjoy the luxuries
of a club-room, at the same time
viewing the beauties and wonders
of our glorious West? Can it be
done? Yes: The Great Northern
Railway, operating the Library-
Observation Car, Diner, First-class
and superior Tourist cars on all
trains, offering choice of four routes,
San Francisco to Spokane en route
to the East, provides the opportu
nity. Those desiring an Ocean
Voyage disembark at Portland or
Seattle. All rail passengers are
ticketed via Shasta and Columbia
River routes or via Shasta and
Seattle. For pamphlets and infor
mation apply to E. S. Blair, Gen
eral Agent, 11 Montgomery street,
San Francisco.
