# Christian Associations Meet.
## 
### 
Sunday afternoon a joint meet
ing of the Y. M. C. A. and the
Y. W. C. A. was held for the pur
pose of discussing ways and means
of improving their work and espe
cially to awaken an interest in the
evening meetings. After a general
discussion it was decided that the
Sunday evenings should be made
more attractive, and that a special
effort should he- directed towards
bringing the work of the Associa
tions before the student body.
