# Memorial Services.
## 
### 
In place of the regular Sunday
morning sermon, services will be
held in memory of Professor Tho
burn. Addresses will be given by
President Jordan, Dr. Elliott, Pro
fessor Whittier, and perhaps also
by Mr. Hutchinson of Palo Alto.
There will be a reading by Profes
sor Murray, and special music, in
cluding a duet for piano and violin
by Mr. Dully and Mrs. Starbuck,
will be rendered.
