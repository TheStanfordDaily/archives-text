# Announcements.
## 
### 
1900 Baseball. —Members of the
1900 baseball team will meet Tues
day at 1:15, room 11 quad.
Alfred Haslacher, Mgr.
Sophomore Meeting.—There will
be a meeting of the Sophomore
Class in the chapel on Wednesday
at 1:15 p.m. to arrange for the
cotillion. P. L. Wilson, Pres.
Graduate Club.— A meeting of
the executive committee of the
Graduate Club will be held in room
31 quad, at 4:30 p.m. Wednesday,
February 1. David A. Curry.
Pres.
Single Tax Lecture.—Dr. J. H.
Stallard of Menlo, a well known
advocate of the Single Tax, will
speak on that subject before the
Class in Finance, Room 31, at 8:15
Tuesday morning. Students in
terested are invited to attend.
Tuesday Evening Lecture. —Dr.
Jordan is unable to lecture in the
chapel tomorrow evening as an
nounced, and instead Professor
Murray will occupy the hour with
a lecture on u Ancient and Modern
Tragedy." The lecture will begin
at 7:15.
