# INTERCLASS DEBATE.
## The Joint Committee from the Two Classes Meets and Arranges A Schedule.
### 
The joint committee from the
Sophomore and Freshman classes
met this afternoon and arranged
the following schedule with refer
ence to the interclass debate,
which will take place in the chapel
on Friday evening April 14. Com
mittee meets March 10 to receive
the names of the teams and deter
mine which side shall choose the
question; question to be submitted
March 17; sides chosen by March
U0; debate, April 14. This will al
low just four weeks, including the
mid-semester recess, from the time
the question is submitted in which
to work up the debate. Each class
will adopt its own method of se
lecting a team.
