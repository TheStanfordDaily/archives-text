# Quads.
## 
### 
— Dr. J. K. McLean, president of
the Theological Seminary of Oak
land, will conduct the services in
the chapel next Sunday. Dr. Mc-
Lean will be one of the judges on
the Carnot debate and will remain
on the campus over Sunday.
— Professor W. G. Johnson, who
in 1892 was an assistant in the
Entomology department here, has
gradually advanced in his work
and has done great service to the
public by his popular papers on
entomological topics. He has
published papers on "The Flour
Moth in New York State," " In
sects in Stored Grain," " The Flour
Moth in France," "Silk Fabric
Made by Insect Source," and other
interesting and instructive articles.
