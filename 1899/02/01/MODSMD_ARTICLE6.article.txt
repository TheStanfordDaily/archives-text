# Outing Club Excursion.
## 
### 
Weather permitting, the Outing
Club proposes to take a walking

excursion next Saturday. Starting
from the quad at 8 a.m. the walk
ers of the party will take the fol
lowing route to King's Mountain
House: Searsville; up Alembique
creek to Bear Gulch road and the
Lion's Ciate; thence along the ridge
trail to King's,which will be reached
by noon.
Others desiring to take the trip
by team, meeting the walkers at
King's, are requested to hand their
names not later than Friday noon
to J. E. Wilson, secretary.
