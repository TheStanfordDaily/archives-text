# Alumni Notes.
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. Henry G. Wood
ward write from Berlin that they
areenjoying themselves thoroughly.
Mr. Woodward, Physiology '95, is
continuing his medical studies at
the Berlin clinics. Mrs. Wood
ward, whom Stanford students re
member as Marie Schaaf, History
'98, is studying drawing and revel
ing in the art treasures of European
galleries.
On the evening of January 3 a
number of Stanford students were
entertained in Berlin by Professor
and Mrs. Powers, among them be
ing Mr. and Mrs. Woodward and
Miss Louise Gerichs.
