# Gym. Credit for Out-Door Work.
## 
### 
The following rules have been
posted in Encina gymnasium re
garding the conditions under
which men doing track, field, and
baseball work may receive gym.
credit for work so done.
GYMNASIUM RULES.
Outdoor work may be substi
tuted for indoor work under the
following conditions:
Credit. — When outdoor work is
substituted two consecutive se
mesters' registration in Hygiene 1 is
required.
Credit for the time substituted
will be given at the end of the sec
ond semester taken.
Attendance. —Regular work must
be carried out live days in the
week, at 4:30 p.m. The present
program for inside work will oper
ate until further notice.
A strict record of each day's work
will be made.
An extra cut will be granted,
otherwise the regular rules for cuts
and absences will be in force. Ex
cuses must come from Dr. Wood.
In days of bad weathermen will
report for work inside, reporting in
oflice before and after the hour.
Work must at all times be in
harmony with advice from the
team management and the de
partment.
