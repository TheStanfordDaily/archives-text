# Y.M.C.A. Officers
## 
### 
The University Young Men's
Christian Association elected the
following officers at its annual bus
iness meeting yesterday afternoon:
President. 11. L. Langnecker, '01;
vice-president, \V. A. Morris,'ol;
secretary, lloy O. Hadley, '01; and
treasurer, J. I). Crunnney, '01.
These officers, who assume their
duties at the present time, are
chosen to continue through next
year. The Association has been
doing much quiet, work since the
arrival of Mr. I'rall last September,
and though still small in numbers
and having many obstacles yet to
overcome, has laid the foundation
for a steady and permanent growth.
