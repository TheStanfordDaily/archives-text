# To Meet the East.
## 
### 
Word conies from New York
that 11. P. Taylor of San Fran
cisco, who went East with the in
tention of arranging some sort of
a football match between a Cali
fornia team and some of the " Big
Four" of the East, is meeting with
success. Harvard is the only
team which has not promised co
operation, and Mr. Taylor hopes to
make arrangements with them
soon.
