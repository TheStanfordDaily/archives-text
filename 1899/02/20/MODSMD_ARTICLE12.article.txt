# Picnic at King's Mountain.
## 
### 
The Christian Associations and
the Outing Club will give a joint
picnic at King's Mountain on Wed
nesday. Members of the Outing
Club who intend to work on the
trail will walk with Professor Dud
ley. Those desiring to ride will
meet at the northwest corner of the
quad at 8:15 sharp. All expecting
to go must leave their names at
the Registrar's office before '2 p.m.
tomorrow (Tuesday). Each one
taking the trip will carry his own
lunch. The public is invited.
