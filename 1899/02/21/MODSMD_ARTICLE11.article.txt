# Announcements.
## 
### 
Kappa Alpha Theta. —At home,
Kappa Alpha Theta fraternity,
Saturday evening.
At Home. —Dr. and Mrs. Jurdan
will be at home to the students on
Thursday evening, February 23.
Junior Class Meeting.— I here
will be a meeting of the Junior
Class Friday afternoon at 1:30 in
room 34. A large attendance is
desired, as business of importance
is on hand.
Mandolin Club. —There will be
a regular practice of the Mandolin
Club Thursday evening at 7:15 in
room 33 quad. All members must
be present. Bring all music.
Ralph Arnold, Leader.
Philological Association. — The
next regular meeting will be held
on Thursday, February 23, at 2:3U
o'clock, in room 31. Principal
paper, "The Tests of the Andria
of Terence," by Professor Fair
clough. Minor communication, " A
new Ms. of Fulgentine," by Profes
sor Pease. The principal paper for
March is promised by l'rofessor
Murray. O. M. Johnston, Sec.
