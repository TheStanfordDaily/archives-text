# WE LOSE AGAIN.
## Fireman's Fund Team Victorious a Third Time, But Varsity Improving. 
### 
The Varsity met another defeat
at the hands of the Fireman's Fund
team un the campus yesterday by
the score of 7 to 3. The game
was more interesting than any
played heretofore this season. The
Varsity showed some improvement
over previous performances. Slierer,
who pitched the first six innings
allowed eleven hits to be made,
lie pitched a good game, neverthe
less, after the first inning, having
plenty of speed. Carson pitched
the last three innings, during which
two hits were made. Krug, for the
visitors, pitched a magnificent game.
For the Fireman's Fund, Web
ber led in the baiting, getting four
hits out of live times at the but.
Schineer and Follansbee played
good fielding games.
For Stanford, Strolln played an
excellent game. Wright led in ihe
batting. The score:
fihkman's fund.
