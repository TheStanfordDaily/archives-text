# Berkeley Notes.
## 
### 
—The Assembly Committee on
Public Buildings and Lands decided
yesterday to report favorably a bill
lor $93,U00 for various improve
ments to the State University
buildings and property.
—It is reported that Eddie Dick
son, the freshman sprinter at U. C.,
has left college. Since Drum left
the university, Dickson has been
the hope of U. C. in the sprints,
lie has a record of 10:1-5 in the
hundred and was counted on at
Berkeley to win two races at least.
