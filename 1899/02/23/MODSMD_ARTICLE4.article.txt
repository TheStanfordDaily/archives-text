# Sophomore Cotillion.
## 
### 
The annual cotillion was given
by the Class of in Enema
gym. on the evening of February
21. The decorations were all that
could be desired, and the music,
provided by Yank's orchestra of
live pieces, was especially well re
ceived. There were only about forty
couples present, perhaps the small
est number that has attended any
class dance of recent years. The
two figures of the cotillion were
placed in the middle of the pro
gram of sixteen dances and several
extras. The whole affair was very
successful from a social standpoint.
It is probable, however, that the
finances ran some seventy dollars
behind.
