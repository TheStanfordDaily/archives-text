# Announcements.

### 
» Mandolin Club. —The Mandolin
Club will meet on Tuesday at 7:15
p.m., and on Thursday at 4:30
p.m., in room 83 quad.
Erskine Moot Court. —The reg
ular meeting of the Erskine Moot
Court will be held tomorrow after
noon in room 32 Encina at 1:30
p.m.
Tuesday Evening Lecture. —
Rev. Edward B. Payne, formerly of
the Unitarian church, Oakland,
will lecture tomorrow night on
"Walt Whitman."
1900 Baseball. — Ml candidates
for the 1900 baseball team are re
quested to appear on the diamond
for practice.
Chester 0. Murphy, Captain.
Woman's Club. —The Woman's
Club will meet Wednesday, March
1, at Fraternal Hall, Palo Alto.
Professor Fetter will address the
meeting on the subject ''Character
and Wages."
Sophomore Meeting.—There will
be a meeting of the Class of 1901
tomorrow at 4:30 in the chapel.
A large attendance is requested as
important business will be trans
acted. P. H. Wilson, Pres.
Ladies' Mandolin Club. —There
will be a meeting of the Ladies'
Mandolin Club for rehearsal on
Tuesday at 4:30 p.m. in room 31
quad.
Louise McDougai.d, Leader.
Graduate Club. —There will be
a business meeting of the Graduate
Club in room 31 on Wednesday,
March 1, at 4:30 p.m. Election of
vice-president and voting upon
new members. David A. Curry,
President.
Golf Tournament. —A tourna
ment, open to both lady and gen
tleman players, will lie held on the
golf course Saturday next, March
4, at 9 a.m. Entries must be sent
in before Friday, March 3. Ad
dress Secretary Golf Club, Box 14,
University.
