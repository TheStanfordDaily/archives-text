# VARSITY IMPROVING.
## We Lose the Game with Santa Clara, hut the Nine Shows Great Improvement. 
### 
The Varsity was defeated in the
last inning on the campus Satur
day by Santa Clara, the score
being sto 4. The game was the
most interesting of the season, and
was thoroughly enjoyed by the
small but enthusiastic crowd or*
the bleachers. The Varsity has
improved greatly in stick work,
batting Kelly out of the box. The
field work also showed improve
ment.
Until the ninth inning it ap
peared that Stanford would be vic
torious, but in this inning Santa
Clara rapped out three hits, and
aided by a base on balls and an
error managed to send three men
across the plate.
Carson pitched very effectively.
During the last inning he gave out
somewhat.
