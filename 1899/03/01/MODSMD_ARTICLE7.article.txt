# The
						Annual Golden Gate Tour.
## 
### 
A solid vestibule train w ill arrive
at Palo Alto next Saturday morn
ing from the East. 'The train is the
one that was on exhibition at the
World's Fair and it will bring a
company of Eastern tourists who
are traveling around the country
under the auspices of the Pennsyl
vania Railroad Company. The
trip is known as The Annual
Golden Gate Tour. The tourists
will spend the morning on the
campus and then depart for San
Francisco where rather an exten
sive stay will be had.
