# Sophomore-Freshman Debate.
## 
### 
The joint Sophomore-Freshman
Debating Committee met this after
noon in room 10 at 1:80. As the
challenged parties, the Sophomores
claimed the option of selecting
question or side. This was con
ceded, and they decided to allow the
Freshmen choice of question.
Next Thursday at 1:15 the '02
debaters must submit a question to
the committee; on the following
Monday at the same time the '01
train must report choice of side.
The debate will take place on
April 14.
