# Quads.
## 
### 
—Alfred 11. Dole, '01, has a Man
ila letter in the Battle Axe, the or
gan of the Riverside High School.
—The Guard and Tackle, the
publication of the Stockton High
School, printed a frontpage picture
of Dr. Jordan in its last issue.
—"Methuselah," a poem by Prof.
W. H. Hudson, which was printed
in the Sequoia last year, has just
been published with credit in Cur
rent Literature.
— The forty-third anniversary of
Sigma Alpha Epsilon fraternity
was celebrated in San Francisco
last Saturday evening with a ban
quet at the Palace Hotel. The
members of the Stanford chapter
were present.
