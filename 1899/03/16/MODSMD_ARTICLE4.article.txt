# Vacation Excursion Rates.
## 
### 
The following relative to the
notice of the $3.00 round trip rate
to Pacific Grove and Santa Cruz
published in last night's paper was
received today:
"In the event of parties desiring
to return from Pacific Grove via
Santa Cruz and the narrow gauge
line, the agent at Pacific Grove
will be instructed to take up the
original ticket and furnish another
reading via Santa Cruz on pay
ment of sixty-five cents, that being
the local fare from Paiaro to Santa
Cruz."
