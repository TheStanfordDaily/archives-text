# Athletic Committee.
## 
### 
There was no quorum at the
meeting of the Athletic Committee,
which was called for this afternoon,
and another meeting has heen
called for Monday afternoon at
1:30. At that time the application
of the University of Indiana for

membership in the Western Ama
teur Athletic Association, in which
Stanford has a vote, will be acted
upon ; the question of a Varsity
cap will be up for consideration ;
the report of the committee to revise
the Stanford-U. C. agreement will
be received, and several matters of
minor importance will come before
the committee.
