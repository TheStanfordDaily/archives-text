# Fireman's Fund Wins.

The Fireman's Fund team won
the last game of the series Satur
day, March 25, by a score of 5 to
3. The game was not exciting and
our team suffered in not having
Young and Wright in the infield.
Sherer, after the first two innings,
settled down to steady pitching
and kept the hits scattered.
Lougheed and Strohn both ac
cepted many chances and made
some good stops, although the
former stacked up three errors to
his credit.

played in good form for the insur
