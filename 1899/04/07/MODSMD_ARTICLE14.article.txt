Entomology 1. —There will be a

books are to be handed in.
Kappa Gamma At Home. —Kap-
pa Kappa Gamma will be at home
Saturday evening, April 8.
Saturday Night Club. — The
hour of meeting tomorrow will be
0:45 instead of 7:30 p.m. as usual.
Zoology Club. —The Zoology
Club will meet on Monday, April
10, at 7:30 p.m. at Professor C. 10.
Greene's, 5 Salvatierra st. J. O.
Snyder will talk on "A Collecting
Trip to Mexico."
SophomOre Class Meeting. —
There will be a meeting of the Class
of 11)01 in the chapel Tuesday at
1:30 for the purpose of electing of
ficers for the class annual.
Phil Wilson, Pres.
At Home. —The Christian Asso
ciations invite their friends to an
at home tomorrow evening from 8
to 10 o'clock at the home of Dr.
and Mrs. Elliott, 24 Alvarado Row.
Mesdames Elliott, Branner, and
Cubberley will act as patronesses.
Maps.— At the request of the
Outing Club the Bookstore has
procured a number ot maps of the
surrounding counties. The maps
are the work of the U. S. Geologi
cal Survey, and consequently the
nominal charge of live cents apiece
is made to cover the cost of pub
lication. They include the follow
ing districts: Palo Alto, San Jose,
San M aieo, Mt. Hamilton, Mt.
Diablo, San Francisco, Mt. Tatnal
pais.
