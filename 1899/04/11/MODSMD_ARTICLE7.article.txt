# JORDAN ON MEXICO.
## First of the Lectures for the Training House Fund in Chapel Tomorrow Evening. 
### 
Tomorrow evening Dr. Jordan
will give the first of his lectures on
Mexico for the benefit of the Train
ing-House fund. In the first lec
ture the external features of the
country will be considered, such as
scenery, architecture, etc.
Dr. Jordan has not written his
lecture, but will give an informal
talk, illustrated throughout with
lantern slides, on what he saw in
his. recent trip. As the lecture is to
be given in the interest of the
Training-House fund, a large at
tendance should be had.
Tickets are now on sale at the
Registrar's office. The admission
will lie thirty-five cents, which en
titles the purchaser to a reserved
seat.
