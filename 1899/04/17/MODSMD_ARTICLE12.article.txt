# Announcements.
## 
### 
'99 Meeting.—The Senior Class
will meet Tuesday in the chapel
at 1:15. Important business.
Ralph Arnold, Pres.
Senior Class.—There will be an
important meeting of the Senior
Class in the chapel Tuesday at 1:15.
Ralph Arnold, Pres.
Annual Election.—The Class of
1901 will meet Tuesday afternoon
in the chapel at 4:30 to elect ofii
cers for next year's Annual.
Phil Wilson, Pres.
Tuesday Evening Lecture.—
The regular Tuesday evening lec
ture will be given by Dr. Jordan on
"The People of Mexico." It will
be illustrated by lantern slides.
No admission is to be charged.
Senior Reception.—The follow
ing committee will have charge of
the arrangements for the Senior
Reception to be given on Friday
evening of Commencement Week
in Encina Hall: R. E. Filcher, F.
S. Fisher, M. C. Leventritt, P. C.
Hatch, H. W. Grunsky, Garth
Parker, and J. F. Ortschild (chair
man). Ralph Arnold, Pres.
Song Competition.—The Senior
Class Plate Committee asks for
contributions of original class songs
from the members of the '99 Class;
the winning piece to be printed in
pamphlet form and sung Class Day
at the dedication of the class plate.
It is hoped that several will com
pete. Those desiring to do so
should hand their names to Miss
Una Fowler or Mr. C. S. Chandler
by Wednesday noon. All songs to
be in by May 10.
