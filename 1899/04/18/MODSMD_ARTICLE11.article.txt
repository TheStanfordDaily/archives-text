# San Jose Alumni Banquet.
## 
### 
The Stanford Alumni Associa
tion iif San Jose will give a ban
quet at the Hotel St .Jameson Fri
day evening, April 1, at 8 o'clock,
for the purpose of promoting Stan
ford spirit and good fellowship.
It is hoped that many now con
nected with Stanford, either as stu
dents or faculty, can arrange to be
present to contribute some of their
fresh enthusiasm to those who have
no longer the privilege of close con
tact with the University.
The tickets are one dollar. Any
who are planning to come will
kindly notify Miss Harriet Cory,
485 South Second street, San Jose.
