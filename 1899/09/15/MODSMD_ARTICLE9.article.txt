# Hamilton Moot Court.
## 
### 
Hamilton Moot Court was organ
ized under the supervision of In
structor Reynolds yesterday after
noon, with the following members:
G. T. Cochran, '00 ; VV. E. Dicks
son, '00 ; W. L. Lewis, '00 ; R. B.
Bid well '00 ; Louis Ferrari, '01 ; C.
G. Morris, '01 ; and F. A. Stevens,
'02. The club organized by electing
Louis Ferrari, '01, president and
G. T. Cochran, '00, secretary.
The club will hold night meet
ings during the semester, each mem
ber appearing once in the capacity
of Chief Justice, attorney for plain
tiff and attorney for defendant.
