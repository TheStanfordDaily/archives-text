# Berkeley Notes.
## 
### 
An enthusiastic crowd of stu
dents turned out to welcome Cochran
on his arrival at Oakland Thursday
morning. The ''Owl" was three
hours late, and this time of waiting
was occupied by speeches and songs.
Steady work was begun on the grid
iron tliut afternoon.
