# Intersociety Debating League.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of the
Intersociety Debating League held
its first meeting for the year in
room 10 quad, at 1:15 this after
noon under call of Chairman Hay
den. It was decided to close the
entries for the semi-finals of the
Carnot preliminaries at 1:30 p.m.
Thursday, Nov. sth.
The Committee decided not to
interfere with the class committee
in their arrangements for the
Sophomore-Freshman debute, but
to stand ready to give any aid or
advice that might be asked.
The try-outs for the Sophomore-
Freshman debating teams will
probably take place on Friday,
Oct. 20th, and the semi-finals of
the Carnot preliminaries during
the week ending Saturday, Nov. 8,
1899.
