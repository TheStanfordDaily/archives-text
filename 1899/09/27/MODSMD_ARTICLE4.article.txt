# Presbyterian Church Reception.
## 
### 
All students, old and new, who
are interested in the Presbyterian
church or who are making that
church their church home, are
cordially invited to a reception to
be given by the Palo Alto church
for their pleasure Friday evening
of this week at Fraternity Hall
from 8 to 10. The ladies of the
church have arranged a program
attractive to the students and as
there are over two hundred Pres
byterians in the student body a
large attendance is expected.
