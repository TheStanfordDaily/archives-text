# Zoology Club.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Stanford
Zoology Club for the year was held
last night at the residence of Prof.
Gilbert.
R. E. Snodgrass, '00, gave an in
teresting talk on the geological
features of the Galapagos Islands,
taking up the islands separately.
Afterwards a short business meet
ing was held and the following of
ficers were elected for the ensuing
year: President, \V. K. Fisher,'ol;
vice-president, Miss Ora Boring,
'00; secretary, \V. F. Allen, '00.
