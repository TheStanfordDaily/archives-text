# Roble Reception.
## 
### 
Tomorrow night the women stu
dents at Roble give their annual
reception to the women of the Uni
versity. This affair takes the form
of a fancy dress party, and is al
ways one of the pleasantest events
of the college year. Cards have
not been sent out, but a cordial
invitation to be present is extended
to all the women students.
