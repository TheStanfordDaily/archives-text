# Freshman Debaters.
## 
### 
The freshmen interested in de
bating have divided themselves
into squads as a preliminary try
out for the Sophomore-Freshman
debate. Each side chooses its own
subject. The present organization
is as follows:
First squad: aff., A. Fruhling,
W. 11. Kingery; neg., M. John,
C. B. Wooster.
Second squad: aff., Roy Brooke,
Geo. Leistner; neg., F. R. Hart, J.
Morris.
Third squad: aff., W. Billings,
W. J. Gardner; neg., C. Chase, L.
E. Woostell.
Fourth squad: aff., A. DeWitt,
F. B. Wagner; neg., Lloyd Steven
son, F. E. Nangle.
Fifth squad; aff., J. M. Beach,
W. F. Harris; neg., H. M. Slemons,
W. Maloy.
