# Berkeley Notes

Professor Soule is preparing a
circular letter to the rifle teams of
eastern universities and colleges,
requesting them to take part accord
ing to custom in the annual inter
collegiate target hooting competi
tion. Califo i i.t u the contest
last year by a good margin, and
has held the college championship
during many years past. Her rifle
team has never been beaten by that
of any other college. Professor
Soule believes that a new team, su
perior even to the excellent one of
last year, can easily be found.
