The list of entries for the Car
not preliminaries last year was
much longer than the one this fall.
Of the thirty entries last fall one
half were from '00, the rest coining
from '01 and '99. Of the eigh
teen entries this year ten are from
'01 and one each from among the
sophomores and the freshmen.
The falling off in the number of
contestants can be accounted for
by the fact that the contest last
year was more open, the whole
team being in doubt, and that rep
resentatives this year have been
chosen from but three of the four
societies, while last year six socie
ties were represented. But,while last
year the sophomores furnished nine
men, this year's sophomore class
lias given but one It is not often
that men enter the debates in their
freshman year, but it certainly is
strange that a sophomore class
under our present debating system
has no more men entered for the
Carnot.
