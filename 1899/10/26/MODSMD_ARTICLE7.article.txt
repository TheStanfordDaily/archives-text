# Announcements.
## 
### 
Hank Coats. —All members of
the University Band are requested
to cull at once at room 130 Kncina
and get Hand coats and caps.
Soldiers' Committee.—The fol
lowing committee is appointed to
entertain the visiting soldiers Fri
day: Fred L. Berry, C. A. Cant
well, C. M. Wardall, E. W. Em
mett, J. F. Wilson (chairman).
J. H. CoVERLEY.
1903 and Others.—All mem
bers of the Class of 1903, and
others who expect to attend next
Saturday's game, are urged to be
<in the bleachers each evening this
week in order to practice yells for
the Freshman game.
Song Practice.—There will bean
enthusiastic song practice at Kn
cina Clubroom Friday evening
at 7:30. It is requested that each
one bring his song book. This
will take the place of the usual
rally for the Freshman team.
J. H. COVERLEY.
Song Books. — Football song
books are on sale at the Bookstore,
the Cardinal Cat, and Hall's Drug
Store, Palo Alto. Fraternities,
dormitories, and boarding houses
are urged to practice the songs
constantly so that the students
may become thoroughly familiar
with the words.
