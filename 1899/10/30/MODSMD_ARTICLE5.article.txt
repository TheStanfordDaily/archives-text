# Plug Ugly Postponed.
## 
### 
Owing to the rain the Plug Ugly
celebration has heen postponed
until Wednesday evening at the
same hour and place as was sched
uled for tonight. The extra time
should add considerably to the skill
with which the ceremony is carried
through, and should therefore not
he amiss.
