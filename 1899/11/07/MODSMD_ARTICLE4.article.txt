# Announcements.
## 
### 
At Home. — Prof.and Mrs. E. A.
lloss and Miss Simons will be at
home to the students and to their
friends next Thursday evening.
New Books. —The following books
have been received at the Book
store: "Selections from Landor,"
Newcomer; Latin Grammar, Gilder
sleeve.
Geology 1. —The class in Geol
ogy 1 will meet at 7:30 p.m. Wed
nesday in the chemical lecture
room. There will he no morning
lecture.
Football Song Practice.—There
will he football song practice every
Monday, Wednesday, and Friday
night in Encina Clubrooms. All
men of the University are invited.
Y. \V. C. A. —Mrs. Green will
lead the regular weekly meeting of
the Y. W. C. A. Wednesday at 7
p.m. at Roble Hall. Subject, "How
Christ Makes Use of Common
Lives."
President and Mrs. Jordan will
hold their November reception to
tlie Faculty and their friends on
Wednesday afternoon from 2:30 to
4. The graduate students of the
University are also invited. Pres
ident and Mrs. Gilmau will be the
guesst of the occasion.
Associated Engineers. — There
will be a meeting of the Associated
Engineers Thursday at 1:15 p.m.
for the purpose of electing officers.
All senior and junior members of
the following departments are re
quested to attend: Geology, Chem
istry, Civil, Electrical and Mechan
ical Engineering.
A. 11. Stevens, Chairman.
