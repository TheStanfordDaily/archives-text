# Freshman Class Meeting
## 
### 
The Freshman Class met in the
chapel this afternoon. The most
important business transacted was
the awarding of sweaters to John
and Narramore, who were barred
from playing in the Freshman
game on account of an error in the
handing in their names. The pres
ident appointed the following com
mittee to attend to the buying of
the sweaters: 11. M. Slemons,
chairman, J. A. Fay, F. 10. Nangle.
An assessment of fifty cents was
levied to pay for the sweaters.
