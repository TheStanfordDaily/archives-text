# Scientists Return.
## 
### 
Cloudesley Ilutter,'9(j, and \V. S.
Atkinson, '99, assistants to the
U. S. Fish Commission, returned
to the University a few days ago
from an expedition to the Sierra
Nevada mountains. They started
on August 20th, and their purpose
was to study the sources and the
streams emptying into the Sacra
mento and the San Joaquin val
leys, from the east. A large num
ber of specimens were secured,
which will be examined and classi
fied at the University.
