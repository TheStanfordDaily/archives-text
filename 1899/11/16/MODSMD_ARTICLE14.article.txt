# Lecture on Irrigation.
## 
### 
Professor Elwood Mead of the
United States Agricultural depart
ment will in all probability lecture
in the chapel next Monday morn
ing at 11:15. Mr. Mead is one of
the leading authorities on irriga
tion and developed the famous ir
rigation system of Wyoming. He
has been lecturing at the irrigation
conference held in San Francisco
last week. In his lecture next
Monday he will deal with the legal,
engineering, and economic prob
lems that confront the irrigation
ists in the arid regions.
