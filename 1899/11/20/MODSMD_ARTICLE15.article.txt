# College Notes.
## 
### 
—A basket-hall team is to be
organized among the men students
at Cornell.
—Harvard is just receiving a gift
of $150,000 for the erection and
equipment of a building suitable
for the use of a University club.
—A new boat-house is to be
erected on Charles river for the use
of the Harvard crews. The house
will cost $80,000, which sum was
subscribed by Harvard graduates
in New York.
