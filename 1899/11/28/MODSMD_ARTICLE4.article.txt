# Executive Committee Meets.
## 
### 
The student body Executive
Committee held a meeting last
evening, with all the members pres
ent except Rice and English. The
principal business on hand was the
clearing up of the odds and ends
preparatory to the Thanksgiving
game. The band was given per
mission to draw on the treasurer
for the expenses of forty-five men.
The souvenir silver football which
was presented to Stanford's win
ning eleven several years ago has
been lost sight of, and Theodore
Hoover was appointed to look it up.
A committee on publications was
appointed, and preliminary ar
rangements were made for the mid
year elections, which are to be held
the second Wednesday after Thanks
giving, for the selection of a football
manager and editor-in-chief of The
Daily Pai.o Alto.
