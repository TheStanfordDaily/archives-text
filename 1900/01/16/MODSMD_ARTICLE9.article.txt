# Glee Club Concert.
## 
### 
The only concert by the Stanford
Glee and Mandolin Clubs to be
given on the campus this year will
occur in the assembly hall Friday
evening at 8 o'clock. Tickets will
he on sale at the University Hook
store and Mall's drugstore, Palo
Alto, tomorrow, Wednesday. Prices
are: Reserved seats, 60 cents ; gen
eral admission, 35 cents.
