# Sale of Concert Tickets.
## 
### 
The tickets on sale for the Glee
and Mandolin Club concert in the
assembly hall Friday evening are
selling rapidly at the Bookstore
and at Hall's drugstore in Palo
Alto. Seats will be on sale at both
places until Friday at 5 p.m.
There are plenty of good seats left,
but the advance sale has been ex
cellent. As this is the only cam
pus concert the clubs will give this
year the assembly hall should be
well filled, and conflicting social
engagements should be postponed.
