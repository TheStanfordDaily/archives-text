# Freshman Class Meeting.
## 
### 
The Freshman Class meeting this
afternoon was called to order by
Vice-President Nourse. As there
was no other business, the class pro
ceeded at once to the nomination of
officers for the ensuing semester,
the following names being proposed:
President, W. W. Copp; vice-presi
dent, W. C. Maloy; secretary, W. E.
Billings; treasurer, C. E. McFad
den; track manager, Paul Parker;
baseball manager, T. \V. Brother
ton.
