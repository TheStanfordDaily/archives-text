# Berkeley Carnot Team.
## 
### 
Willsie M. Martin, \V. B. Greeley,
and Fred E. Borton, with Owen E.
Nolte as substitute, were last night
selected for the Carnot Intercol
legiate debate. Nine men debated,
including seven chosen in the semi
linals, and Borton and Martin of
last year's team. The question was:
"Resolved, That any increase in
the legislative power of the French
President would be fraught with
danger to the republic." The debate
will take place February 9th at
Berkeley.
