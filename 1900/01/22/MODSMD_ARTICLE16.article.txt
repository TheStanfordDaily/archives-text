# Palo Alto High Victorious.
## 
### 
The Palo Alto High School bas
ket-ball team defeated the San Jose
Normals Saturday afternoon by a
score of 12 to 0. The game was
played in two twenty-minute halves,
six points being scored by the Palo
Alto team in each half. The San
Jose girls were at a disadvantage
in that they have never played be
fore spectators. The best work for
the Normals was done by Miss Bal
back and Miss Horn, and for the
High School by Miss Ray.
