# Volunteer's Meeting.
## 
### 
For the purpose of completing
an organization among the volun
teers of the Spanish-American war,
who now are or may in the future
become students at the University,
a meeting will he held in room 25
Encina, tomorrow (Tuesday) even
ing at 7:30. United States volun
teers of any regiment, whether or
not in actual service, are urged to
attend.
