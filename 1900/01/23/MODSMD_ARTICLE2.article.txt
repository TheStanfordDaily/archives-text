# Executive Committee Meets.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of the
student body met last night in reg
ular session. Those present were:
President Coverley, Vice-President
Davis, Wickersham, Hoover, and
Bradley. Little business was
brought up for consideration. It
was provided that the place on the
committee left vacant by A. H.
Rice, '02, should be filled by the
person who stood next highest but
one in the competition for that po
sition last semester, Football Man
ager Gildersleeve having received
the next highest. A motion was
also carried to change the time of
meeting to 4:30 Monday afternoon.
