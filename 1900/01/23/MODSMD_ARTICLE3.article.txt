# Class Elections.
## 
### 
The Junior Class election takes
place next Thursday. The polls
will be situated in front of the
chapel site, and will be open from 12
m. to 2 p.m. The following com
mittee have charge of the election :
Gilman, Ferrari, Foster, Sobey,
Hoover.
The Senior Class election will be
held in room 25 on Friday. The
polls will be open from 12 m. to 2
p.m. The officers in charge of the
election are: Edwards, Riddell,
Dutton, Page, and Nourse.
