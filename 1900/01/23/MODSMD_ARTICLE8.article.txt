# Boarding- Clubs on the Campus.
## 
### 
Of the 172 men living in Encina
hall, about 100 are now boarding
on the Row or belong to clubs in the
Camp. Of the seven clubs, two
have 10 men each, two 14, one
10, and another 18, making 79 men
in all. The University Inn is pro
viding for the remaining 72 men in
Encina, besides other students,
making an average of 90 boarders.
This is a considerable decrease over
last semester.
