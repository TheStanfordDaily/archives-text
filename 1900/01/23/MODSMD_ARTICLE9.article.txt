# Berkeley Notes.
## 
### 
—A series of inter-class baseball
games is being played on the cam
pus.
—The faculty of the university
are to do more university exten
sion this winter than ever before.
A large number of new courses are
to be opened, for which regular
university credit will be given
when done by registered students
of the university. Courses will be
given in geology, philosophy, and
American history, also a series of
three lectures on "Confucianism,"
"Taoism," and "Buddhism."
The lectures will be given at the
Hopkins Institute of Art, Young

Men's Christian Association, San
Francisco Normal School, and at
Alameda. The College of Com
merce has arranged for a series of
lectures to he delivered in San
Francisco.
