# Quads.
## 
### 
—Dr. and Mrs. Fetter went up
to King's Mountain this afternoon
to remain over Sunday.
—J. F. English,'ol, has obtained
a week's leave of absence from the
University. He left for his home
in San Francisco this afternoon.
—W. E. Sherwood, '03, who is at
the Lane hospital in San Fran
cisco with an attack of appendici
tis, is much worse and will be oper
ated on tomorrow.
—Mrs. J. N. Comstock and Mrs.
G. C. Price were the guests of honor
at a reception given this afternoon
by the Kappa Alpha Theta frater
nity. About seventy faculty ladies
were present. A delightful musical
program was given by musicians
from San Francisco.
