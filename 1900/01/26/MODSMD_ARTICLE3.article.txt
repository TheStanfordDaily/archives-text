# Carnot Subject Announced.
## 
### 
The subject for the Carnot debate
was telegraphed to the two univer
sities at 9 o'clock this morning. It
is: "Resolved, That the ministers
should lie responsible to the Presi
dent alone."
The judges will be chosen soon
by the faculty committees on de
bating, that from Stanford being
composed of Professors Duniway,
Boss, and Fetter, chairman.
The debate will be held on the
evening of February 9th at Berke
ley, probably in Hearst Hall. As
signment of places will not be
made until the day before.
