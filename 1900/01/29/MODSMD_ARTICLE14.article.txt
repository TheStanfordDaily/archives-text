# "Quad" Benefit.
## 
### 
The management of the 1901
Quad announce that after much
trouble and negotiation they have
at last secured the engagement of
Ferris Hartman, the well-known
comedian of the Tivoli, San Fran
cisco, who has lately returned to the
Tivoli after a long absence. Hart
man will give a dramatic enter
tainment in the chapel Thursday
afternoon, February Bth. He will
be assisted by Tom Greene, the
tenor of the Tivoli opera house.
