# College Baseball Notes.
## 
### 
—Princeton and Harvard have
agreed to play only one baseball
game this spring, instead of three,
as in former years.
—The Santa Clara College base
ball team has secured the services
of William Hulen as coach. Hulen
was captain and shortstop of the
Kansas City Stars.
—Charles Nichols, pitcher of the
Boston National League team, will
coach the Yale baseball team.
Nichols has already coached Am
herst and Harvard teams.
— Princeton has secured the serv
ices of William Clark, catcher of
the Boston League team, to coach
their baseball team. He will be
assisted by Bradley, captain of the
'96 team.
