# Benefit for 1901 Annual.
## 
### 
On Thursday of next week, Fer
ris Hartman and Tom Greene of the
Tivoli opera house will appear in
the chapel for the benefit of the
1901 Annual. Ferris Hartman is
one of the most popular comedians
who have ever appeared before a
San Francisco audience. His part
of the entertainment will be a
series of funny stories and sketches
and comic songs. Tom Greene is a
well-known tenor, and will sing
some of those selections which have
been popular at the Tivoli lately.
The entertainment promises to be a
good one, and no one should miss it #
