# Harvard Scholarships.
## 
### 
The growth of scholarships at
Harvard has extended over a per
iod of more than two hundred and
fifty years. The first one was found
ed in 1643 by Lady Ann Mowlson,
of London. From 1648 to 1800
the college received about forty be
quests for the aid of poor and de
serving scholars, with an income of
from $50 to $300 each. From 1800
to 1850 thirteen more gifts for
scholarships were given, the re
sources estimated at about $1400.
In 1890-1900 the bequests have
come from all sources and repre
sent about $12,000.
