# Junior Class Meeting.
## 
### 
The Class of 1901 met at 1:30
this afternoon in room 50 quad.
President Symonds called the
meeting to order. On account of
the death of B. C. Baird, who was
a member of the class, a commit
tee consisting of Richard Lockey,
F. H. Foster, and F. 1). Hamilton
was appointed to draw up suitable
resolutions. It was decided to hold
the annual Junior Day on the last
Friday before the Easter vacation,
and to offer a prize of fifteen dol
lars for the most meritorious farce
portraying Stanford life, the
farce to be given on Junior Day.
