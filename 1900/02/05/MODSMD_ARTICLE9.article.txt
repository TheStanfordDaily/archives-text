# Probable Baseball Schedule.
## 
### 
Manager Haslacher met Berke
ley's baseball manager in the city
Saturday, and arranged a schedule
for the intercollegiate baseball
matches, which will probably be
as follows : The first game will be
played on the Berkeley campus, on
the 7th of April ; the second on the
Stanford grounds, on the 14th, and
if a deciding game is needed it will
be played out on the 28th, prob
ably on neutral ground.
