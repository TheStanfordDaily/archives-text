# Meeting of U.C. Graduate Club.
## 
### 
Definite plans have been an
nounced in regard to the meeting
of the University of California
Graduate Club, at Berkeley next
Friday afternoon. The members
of the faculty, the members of the
Graduate Club, and graduate stu
dents of Stanford University are
invited to attend a meeting at
Stiles' Hall at 4 o'clock, to listen to
a paper by Professor Leuschner on
"The University Conference for
the Consideration of Higher De
grees."
Those intending to be present
will take the three o'clock boat
from San Francisco, and a com
mittee from the club will meet
them at Berkeley station. Those
who expect to accept the invitation
will please leave their names at the
Bookstore tomorrow.
