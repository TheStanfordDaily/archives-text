# Yale's Gymnastic Contest.
## 
### 
On March 16th the second gym
nastic contest open to members of
the university will be held at the
gymnasium. The events in the
competition will be the flying rings,
side-horse, tumbling, and club
swinging. The man receiving the
highest number of points in this
contest together with the points
scored in the first contest held be
fore Christmas, will be given a "Y."
