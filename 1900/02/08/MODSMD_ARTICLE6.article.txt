# LECTURES FOR FRUIT GROWERS.
## Schedule Announced for the Week's Work Which Will be Given by Stanford Men. 
### 
The Entomology department has
arranged the schedule, of proceed
ing for the special course of lectures
to be given from February 19th to
23d inclusive. A detailed an
nouncement will be ready for dis
tribution in a few days. It will be
as follows:
Monday, Feb. 19.
11:15 —The University and the
Fruit-Grower. President David
Starr Jordan.
1:30 —The Growth of Markets. Pro
fessor F. A. Fetter.
2:30 —Insect Life. Professor J. H.
Comstock. After the lecture there
will be an exhibition of speci
mens illustrating the structure
and metamorphoses of insects.
Tuesday, Feb. 20.
10:15—The Selection of New Forms
of Fruit. Presidene Jordan.
11:15 —Fighting Insect Pests. Mr.
E. M. Ehrhorn, Entomologist for
Santa Clara County, Mountain
View.
1:30 —The County Bank and Its
Relations to the Horticulturist.
Mr. H. W. Torchiana, Los Banos.
2:3o—The Balance of Nature. Pro
fessor J. H. Comstock. After the
lecture there will be an exhibi
tion of predaceous insects and
parasites.
Wednesday, Feb. 21.
10:15 — The Soils of California.
Professor J. C. Branner.
11:15 —The Relation of Nature
Study to Agriculture. Mrs. A. B.
Comstock.
I:3o—The Old Farmer and the
New. Professor E. A. Ross.
2:30 —Bees, and Fruit - Growing.
Professor O. P. Jenkins.
Thursday, Feb. 22.
10:15 —The Preparation and Mar
keting of Cured Fruits. Mr. S.F.
Leib, San Jose.
11:15 —The Nicaragua Canal. Mr.
Edward Berwick, Monterey.
1:30 —The Laws of Transportation.
Professor E. A. Ross.
2:30 —Scale Insects. Professor J. H.
Comstock. After the lecture
there will be an exhibition of
microscopic preparations of scale
insects.
Friday, Feb. 23.
10:15—The Next Step in Forestry.
Professor W. R.
11:15 —The Principles of Spraying.
Professor C. W. Woodworth, Un
iversity of California.
1:30 —Co-operation. Professor F. A.
Fetter.
2:30 —Bees and Bee-Culture. Pro
fessor O. P. Jenkins. This lec
ture will be illustrated by speci
mens of hives, other bee-keeper's
supplies, and living bees.
