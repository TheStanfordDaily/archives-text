# SATURDAY'S GAME.
## Fireman's Fund Team Wins the Second of the Series of Practice Games. 
### 
The Varsity was defeated by the
Firemen Saturday, by a score of
8-6. The game was not as well
put up as was expected, neither
team playing up to the form of
their first game. The Varsity was
handicapped by the absence of
Lanagan in the box. Moore and
Hampson, both of the Freshman
class, were tried in the box, nei
ther scoring a strikeout. The scor
ing of both teams was done inside
the first five innings, in the third
the Firemen got six men across the
home plate, owing to three succes
sive errors on Stanford's part. In
the fourth the Varsity pulled down
two runs on errors. In the fifth
Hampson was substituted for Moore
in the box for the Varsity, and
Moskimon replaced Krug for the
Firemen. The last innings were
better played, both teams taking a
brace.
