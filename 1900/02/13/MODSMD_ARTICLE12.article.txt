For some reason it has become
history that the Sophomore Cotil
lion is the most efficient debt-mak
ing undertaking into which Stan
ford University classes can enter.
If the growing deficiencies of re
cent years in this regard are much
longer kept up, mutterings of dis
content that are now heard will
break out into active measures.
Sophomore classes are as able to
successfully manage their finances
as are any of the others. It is high
time they were finding out this
ability, and appreciating the ne
cessity of beginning their upper
class existence free from debt.
Something of a surplus to start
with, in fact, would have saved
many Junior classes from much of
their trouble over class enterprises.
IUO2 has the experience of their
immediate predecessor to profit by
with much benefit to themselves.
