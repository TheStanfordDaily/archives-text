# Y.M.C.A. Picnic.
## 
### 
The Y. M. C. A. have planned a
day's outing at Kings Mountain
for next Thursday, Washington's
birthday. The party will go up in
buses, leaving the north end of the
quad at 8:15. Every one will
bring his lunch. Those wishing
to go will leave their names before
next Tuesday evening with one of
the following: Miss Miss
Stubbs, E. B. Dawson, James
Hyde, W. H. Hensey, or at the
Registrar's office.
