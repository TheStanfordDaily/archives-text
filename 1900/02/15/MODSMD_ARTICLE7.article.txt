# Stanford Wins.
## 
### 
The Varsiiy defeated the Santa
Clara team, this afternoon, on the
latter's grounds, by a score of 8 to
6. While the defeated team played
an almost errorless game, making
but two errors, the Stanford team
made nine. The winning team
scored thirteen hits, as against
seven for Santa Clara. Lanagan
was in the best of form, striking
out twelve men, as against thirteen
for Kelly. Havens made the star
play of the day in a home run
which brought in four men. Santa
Clara put up a strong game.
