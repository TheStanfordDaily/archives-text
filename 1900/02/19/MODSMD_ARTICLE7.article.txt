# Exchange Concert Dates.
## 
### 
O. C. Leiter, student body treas
urer, and R. W. Tully, manager of
California's musical clubs, met Sat
urday in San Francisco to arrange
dates for the combination concerts
which the Stanford and Berkeley
Glee Clubs are to give at both uni
versities. It was decided to give
the concert at Stanford either the
evening before or the evening of the
field-day, provided the field-day is
held here April 21. The concert at
Berkeley will take place April 7,
the night of the first intercollegiate
baseball game.
