# To Study at Monterey.
## 
### 
Walter E. Garrey, a Fellow in
Physiology at the University of
Chicago, was the guest of Dr. C.W.
Greene Thursday. He left Friday
for the Hopkins Laboratory, where
lie is to carry on physiological ex
periments on the effect of salt solu
tions on the development of the
unfertilized eggs of marine inver
tebrates. Mr. Garrey will remain
at Monterey until April Ist, when
he returns to Chicago. He is in
structor in physiology at Woods'
Holl laboratory, where Dr. Greene
is to be this summer.
