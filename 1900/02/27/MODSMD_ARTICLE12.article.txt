# Quads.
## 
### 
—Miss Gertrude E. Mackintosh,
'98, who has been spending some
time at the Mackintosh ranch near
here, visited Alpha Phi yesterday.
—The Girls' Mandolin Club
played at the social meeting of the
Episcopalian Club held last Friday
evening in the Diggles home in
Palo Alto.
—Mrs. Robert Burdette, presi
dent of the State Federation of
Women's Clubs, will speak before
the Women's Club of Palo Alto
next Friday afternoon.
—In yesterday's account of the
Press Club banquet, the name of J.
Burt Gildersleeve, '02, was inad
vertently omitted from the list of
newly-elected members.
