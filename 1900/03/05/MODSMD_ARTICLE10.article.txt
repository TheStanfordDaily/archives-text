# Dr. Jordan in the East.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan spoke yesterday in
All-Souls' church, Chicago, on the
subject, "The Blood of a Nation."
He has encountered very bad
weather since leaving California
and writes that he "never appreci
ated before what very poor weather
the absence of weather is." Presi
dent Jordan lectures tomorrow at
Ann Arbor, Mich., under the aus
pices of the University Lecture As
sociation there.
