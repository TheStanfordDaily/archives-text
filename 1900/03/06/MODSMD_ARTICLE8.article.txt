# Graduate Student Injured.
## 
### 
James McNeill, a graduate of In
diana University, who has been
taking special work here in Ento
mology, was severely injured last
Saturday near Los Gatos. In com
pany with Professor Kellogg and
Professor and Mrs. Comstock he was
taking a bicycle trip through Santa
Clara county. On going down a
steep hill he lost control of his
wheel and was thrown to the ground.
He was taken to a hotel, where it
was found that he had broken his
shoulder, besides being badly
bruised and shaken up. He will be
brought back to the University to
morrow.
