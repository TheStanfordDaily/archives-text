# Junior Farce Orchestra.
## 
### 
An orchestra consisting of ten in
struments has been formed for the
Junior Farce. The players are: Miss
Morgan, leader; Scovilleand Waite,
first violins; Dole, Knecht,and Haw
ley, second violins; Foster, viola;
Ayerand Waite,cornets; Olshausen,
flute; and Schultz, piano. A num
ber of rehearsals have been held at
the Kappa Kappa Gamma house.
A good cello player is needed.
