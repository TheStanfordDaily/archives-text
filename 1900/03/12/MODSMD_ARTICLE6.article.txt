# Dr. Jenkins' Trip Abroad.
## 
### 
Prof. 0. P. Jenkins, head of the
Department of Physiology, has
been granted a leave of absence for
six months, and will spend the
time abroad. He leaves here in the
latter part of May, and will return
about February Ist. Dr. Jenkins
will devote his vacation to the study
of the physiological laboratories of
the great European universities,
taking especial note of their plan,
apparatus, and general mode of
work. He will give his principal
study to English and German uni
versities, and will be in Berlin the
greater part of the winter.
