# New Physiology Instructor.
## 
### 
The announcement was made to
day of the appointment of Ray Ly
man Wilbur as assistant professor
of Physiology. Wilbur graduated
from here in '96, and received his
A. M. a year later. While an un
dergraduate he was assistant in the
Physiology department, and was an
instructor during the time he was
carrying on postgraduate work.
Since leaving here he had been at
the Cooper iMedical College, where
he graduated last May. During
his senior year there he was in
charge of the physiology laboratory.
Wilbur will commence his work
here at the beginning of next se
mester.
