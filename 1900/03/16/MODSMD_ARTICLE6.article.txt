# Third Game With Santa Clara.
## 
### 
Tomorrow the Varsity will meet
Santa Clara for the third time this
season. The game will be called in
San Jose at 2:30 p.m. This will be
the deciding game of the series, each
nine having a game to its credit.
A large crowd is expected, owing to
the celebration to be held in San
Jose tomorrow. The Varsity will
line up as follows : Lougheed, c. ;
Lanagan, p.; Havens, lb.; Cowden,
2b.; Edwards, s s.; Strohn, 3b.;
Hampson, 1. 112.; Murphy, c. 112.; Sy
monds, r. 112.
