# Firemen Defeat Barkeley
## 
### 
The Firemen's Fund team de
feated Berkeley Saturday afternoon
on the Berkeley campus by a score
of 4to 1. The game was a poor
one, the batting and fielding being
far below the average. Nourse
pitched for Berkeley and Krug and
Moskimon for the insurance men.
