# Dr. Jordan's Lectures.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan lectures before the
I'atria Law Club of New York to
morrow night. From there lie goes
to Harvard and Yale and from
thence to the University of West
Virginia, where he will deliver the
convocation address.
