# 1901 "Quad" Benefit.
## 
### 
The management of the 1901 An
nual announce a dramatic and lit
erary entertainment of a high order
in the chapel for next Friday even
ing. They have secured Mrs. Ruie
I). Meeke,the well known dramatic
reader, who will give an evening of
monologue and dialect impersona
tion.
Mrs. Meeke is undoubtedly an
artist of a high order and has been
meeting with great success on this
Coast, where she is now touring.
Mrs. Meeke has been before the
public for many years and has met
with great favor. In the dialect
and humorous selections she is un
surpassed and they have won for
her the warmest praise from all who
have heard her.
