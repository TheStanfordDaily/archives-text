# Announcements.
## 
### 
Glee Club. —Regular rehearsal
will he held Wednesday evening at
7:30 in the chapel.
German Majors.— All German
majors are requested to meet in
room 33 quad, Thursday at 4:30.
Y. W. C. A.—Y. W. C. A. will
meet at Roble Hall, Wednesday,
March 21st, at 7 p.m. Mrs. M. E.
Congdon will he the leader.
1903 Meeting.—There will he a
meeting of the Freshman Class in
room 62 quad Thursday at 1:15
p.m. W. W. Copp, I resident.
Tenors Wanted. —Two more first
tenors are wanted for the Varsity
Glee Club. Candidates will please
report at the chapel at 7 o'clock
Wednesday evening.
Junior Farce Rehearsal. —The
cast of the Junior Farce will re
port promptly at 7:30 tomorrow
evening at Roble Hall for rehears
al Wm Ekb, Stage Mgr.
Inter-Society Debating Com
mittee. —There will be a meeting
of the inter-society committee on
Thursday at 1:15 p.m. in room 11
quad.
Helen Swett, Chairman.
Chemistry 2. —All those who
were conditioned in Chemistry 2,
or missed any of the examinations,
will have an opportunity to make
up the work at an examination in
room 62 at 3 p.m. Thursday, March
29th.
Football Men. —Every football
man in the University who has not
given me his name, or whose name
has not been published already,
please inform me as soon as pos
sible in order that all measurements
for football outfits may be taken on
Thursday, March 22d.
J. Burt Gildersleeve, Mgr.
