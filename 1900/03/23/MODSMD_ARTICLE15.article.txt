# An Old Student Returns.
## 
### 
K. S. Howard, '99, a member of
the '9(> Carnot team, is visiting old
friends on the campus.. Howard
graduated last year and accepted a
position with the Cochite Gold
Mining Company, with headquar
ters at Bland, New Mexico. He
entered the company's employ in a
minor capacity, but within a year
had risen to the position of man
ager of the commercial department.
While at the University Howard
made a specialty of transportation,
studying railroading in particular.
He intends, within the near future,
to accept one of several offers in
that line.
