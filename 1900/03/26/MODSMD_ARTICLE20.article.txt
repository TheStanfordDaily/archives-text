# News from Other Colleges.
## 
### 
—A gift of $50,000 has been
made to Oberlin College for a new
men's gymnasium.
—The University of Wisconsin
proposes to establish a school of
chemistry, to rank with the engi
neering department.
—A New England Intercollegi
ate Lawn Tennis Association was
recently organized in Boston. The
membership at present includes
Amherst, Brown, Dartmouth,
Wesleyan, and other eastern col
leges.
—Yale, Harvard, and Princeton
are carrying on spring practice in
football. Harvard and Yale ex
pect to line lip for some scrimmage
work, while Princeton will limit
the practice of her candidates to
punting, catching, and sprinting.
