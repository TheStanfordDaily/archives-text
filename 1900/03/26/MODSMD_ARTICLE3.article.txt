# Baseball on Junior Day.
## 
### 
Arrangements were completed
this afternoon to bring the Santa
Clara baseball team here for a game
on Junior Day. This will be the
last regular game that our men
will plav before they meet Berkeley
next week, and there will undoubt
edly be a big crowd out.
