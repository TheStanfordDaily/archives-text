# In Honor of Dr. Jordan.
## 
### 
President Swain, formerly head
of the department of Mathematics
here, and now president of Indiana
University, has announced that
the trustees of the university have
decided to name the university ath
letic field in honor of Dr. Jordan.
Dr. Jordan, while president of
Indiana, took a deep interest in
athletics, and it was to show in
some way the appreciation of the
university that the athletic field is
to be known as Jordan Field.
