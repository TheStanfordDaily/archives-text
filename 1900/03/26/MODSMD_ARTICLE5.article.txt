# Talk on Hawaii.
## 
### 
A number of the women students
were pleasantly entertained last
Saturday afternoon at the home of
Mrs. It. L. Greet), on Salva
tierra row. Dr. Wood gave an
instructive talk on the Hawaiian
Islands, telling something of their
history and of the social manners
and customs in vogue there.
During the afternoon Miss Milnora
Roberts and Mil nor Roberts sang
several Hawaiian nirs, accompany
ing themselves on the native in
struments. A number of curios
which different people have
brought from the islands were ex
hibited.
Mrs. Green was assisted in re
ceiving by Mrs. Gilbert, Mrs.
Price, Miss March, and Miss Ray.
