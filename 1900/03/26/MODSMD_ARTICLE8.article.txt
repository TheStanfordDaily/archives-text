# Tuesday Evening Lecture.
## 
### 
The regular Tuesday evening lec
ture this week will he given by
George W. Dickie, general manager
of the Union Iron Works of San
Francisco. The subject of the lec
ture is, "The Industrial Develop
ment of Japan." Mr. Dickie is one
of the ablest engineers of the Coast,
and is also a man of broad interests
and culture. His lecture is the out
growth of experiences and observa
tions during a visit to Japan, and
it cannot fail to he of great interest.
