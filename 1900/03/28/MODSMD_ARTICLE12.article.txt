# Junior Farce Tickets.
## 
### 
The advance sale of seats for the
Junior Farce tomorrow night has
been very large. Seats are going
rapidly but there are still a few
very good ones left. They will be
on sale tomorrow at the postoffice
and tomorrow night at the door.
General admission tickets entitling
the holder to a seat downstairs be
low the balcony will be sold at the
door for twenty-five cents. The
doors will be opened tomorrow
night at 7:30.
