# Announcements.
## 
### 
Glee Club. —The Varsity Glee
Club will meet fur rehearsal Wed
nesday at 7:30 p.m. in the chapel.
New music. F. B. Riley,Leader.
1901 " Quad." —Subscriptions for
the 1901 Quad will be taken at the
University Bookstore, Cardinal Cat,
and Registrar's office. The price is
$1.50.
Patronesses of Students' Guild.
—There will be a meeting of the
patronesses of the Students' Guild
in the woman's sitting room on
Wednesday, April 11, at 11:30 a.m.
Notice to Track Men. — For the
remainder of the season all sprint
ers and distance men will report
for work on the track at 5 o'clock
each evening.
W. H. Murphy, Coach.
Library Notice. —Owing to the
fact that a number of books
have been taken away from the
reference room, contrary to regu
lations, and many readers deprived
of their use, the librarians request
students not to bring any private
books to the reading room. Such
books may be deposited in the con
ference room, to the right of the
main entrance. H. C. Nash,
A. V. Babine.
