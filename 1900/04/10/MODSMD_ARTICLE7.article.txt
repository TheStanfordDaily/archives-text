# "Billy" McLeod to Leave.
## 
### 
u Billy" McLeod has accepted an
offer from the Oregon State Agri
cultural College at Corvallie, Or.,
to train their track team for the re
mainder of this season, and will
leave here soon after the intercol
legiate field-day. He will have
complete charge of the training of
the men until the close of the col
lege in June.
" Billy " has been at Stanford for
three years, and has established an
enviable reputation as a trainer.
He is as yet undecided whether he
will return to Stanford next season,
as he has been offered good induce
ments from the Oregon college to
act as trainer during the football
and track seasons.
