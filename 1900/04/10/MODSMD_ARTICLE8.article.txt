# Y.M.C.A. Conference.
## 
### 
Miss Taylor, general secretary of
the National V. M. C. A., spoke
this afternoon before the Christian
societies of the University, and out
lined the plan of work which is to
be done at the summer conference
of the V. M. C. A. to be held in
Capitola from the 18th to the 28th
of May. Tomorrow morning Miss
Taylor will be glad to meet anyone
interested in Christian work, and
will be in room 5 Roble from 8:30
to 10 in the morning and from 4 to
6 in the afternoon.
