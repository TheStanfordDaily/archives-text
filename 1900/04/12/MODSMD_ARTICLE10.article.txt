# College Christian Work.
## 
### 
Miss Harriet Taylor, general sec
retary of the National Y. VV. C. A.,
conducted the weekly service of the
local association at Roble last night.
An unusually large attendance from
the hall and other parts of the
campus was noticed. Miss Taylor
spoke of the association work in
this and foreign countries, with
especial reference to endeavors put
forth in college communities. She
aroused an interest in the confer
ence to he held at Capitola this
coming summer, and suggested
plans for next year's work at Stan
ford. While here Miss Taylor made
her home with Dr. and Mrs. Bran
ner. She left Stanford this morn
iner.
