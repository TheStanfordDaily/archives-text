# Wisconsin Summer School.
## 
### 
In the summer school at Wis
consin, to be held from July 2 to
August 11, a number of courses in
Economics and Sociology are to be
offered. Professor Franklin H.
Giddings of Columbia University
will have charge of the work in
sociology, Professor Jesse Macy
of lowa College of political
science, and Professors Moses Coit
Tyler and H. Morse Stephens of
Cornell, of history.
