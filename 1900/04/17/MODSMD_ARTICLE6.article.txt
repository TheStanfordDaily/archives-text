# Instructor Storey at Ann Arbor.
## 
### 
11. C. Storey has heard from his
son, Instructor T. A. Storey of the

Hygiene department, from Ann
Arbor, Mich., where he has settled
down for the summer's work, after
being detained, together with Cool
idge, '98, at El Paso for several
days by floods. He intends to
study with Professor Lombard,
head of the physiology department
at Ann Arbor, along the line of the
thesis which he wrote here for his
A. M. degree on "Muscular Con
traction." He will return to the
University in time to resume his
work next semester.
