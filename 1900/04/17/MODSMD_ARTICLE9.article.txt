# Announcements.
## 
### 
Y. W. C. A. —The weekly prayer
meeting of the Y. W. C. A. will be
held at Roble Wednesday, April
18th, at 7 p.m. Mrs. M. S. Cum
mins will lead.
Glee Club. —The regular weekly
rehearsal of the Varsity Glee will
be held in the chapel Wednesday
evening at 7:30. New music.
F. B. Riley.
Reception Committee. — There
will be a a joint meeting of the
reception committees in room 11
tomorrow at 1:15.
Clara Martin,
W. M. Erb.
Glee Club Concert.—Reserved
seats for the Berkeley Glee and
Mandolin Clubs' concert in the as
sembly hall Saturday evening,
April 21st, will be on sale tomorrow
at the postoffice on the campus at
1 p.m.
Student Body Meeting.—There
will be a meeting of the Associated
Students Thursday, April 19th, in
chapel at 1:30 p.m. for the purpose
of nominating officers for the en
suing year. The annual election
will occur April 25th.
J. H. Coverley, Pres.
