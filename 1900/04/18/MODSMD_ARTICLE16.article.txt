# Announcements.
## 
### 
1903 Canes. —Owing to the short
notice intending purchasers have
had in which to order their class
canes, the subscription list will be
open until noon Thursday, April
19th, at the Cardinal Cat. A de
posit of fity cents is required with
each order.
Student Body Meeting.—There
will he a meeting of the Associated
Students Thursday, April 19th, in
chapel at 1:30 p.m. for the purpose
of nominating officers for the en
suing year. The annual election
will occur April 25th.
J. H. Coverley, Pres.
