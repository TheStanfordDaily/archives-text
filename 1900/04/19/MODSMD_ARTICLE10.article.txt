# 1903 Canes.
## 
### 
The orders for 1903 canes closed
this afternoon. So far as reported
fifty-seven have been ordered, with
some members of the committee yet
to hear from. When all are in the
number will probably reach sixty
five.
The canes are to be ready for de
livery by May 20th, and after a
formal dedication will he carried
by the members of the class during
Senior Week.
