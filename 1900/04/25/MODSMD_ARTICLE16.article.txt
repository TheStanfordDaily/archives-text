# Scholarships Granted.
## 
### 
W. A. Cannon, '99, has been given
a fellowship in Botany at Columbia
University, New York city, for next
year, and will go there to work for
a doctor's degree. Cannon regis
ters from Washington, Mich., and
graduated from here Christmas,'9B.
Since then he lias been assistant in
the department of Botany. The
fellowship is worth $650, and is one
of the very few allowed by the de
partment of Botany at Columbia.
A scholarship in Economics was
awarded to Albert C. Whitaker,'99,
who held a similar one this year at
the same university.
