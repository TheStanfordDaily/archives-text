# Baseball at San Jose.
## 
### 
A telegram was received from
Manager Belcher of Berkeley today
finally closing arrangements for
holding the third and decisive in
tercollegiate game at San Jose. A
large number of students here have
already signified their intention of
going down. Bus. fares to Palo
Alto may be saved by taking the
excursion train which will leave
from the campus at I:ls sharp, and
from the Palo Alto depot at 1:30.
The round trip rate of 65 cents has
been offered. Returning it will get
to the campus before 7:00, giving
opportunity to attend the faculty
farce in the assembly hall that
evening.
