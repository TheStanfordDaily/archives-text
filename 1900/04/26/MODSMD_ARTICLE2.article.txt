# Excursion to San Jose.
## 
### 
To accommodate those going to
San Jose Saturday, a special train
will leave this campus at 1:15 and
Palo Alto station at 1:30. Round
trip tickets, good only on the ex
cursion train, will be on sale to
morrow at the Registrar's office.
The excursion train returning will
leave San Jose at 6 o'clock.
