# Christian Association Directory.

The Christian Associations are
working on the handbooks for next
season. They will he made on the
plan of this year's little red leather
covered hook on Stanford, and will
hold a high place among the direc
tories issued by the associations at
the various colleges throughout Ihe
country. One will he sent to each
prospective student who sends in
his entrance credit card. Any stu
dent who can assist the manage
ment, by securing desirable " ads "
is asked to speak to the officers of
either the Y. W. C. A. or V. M. (J. A.
