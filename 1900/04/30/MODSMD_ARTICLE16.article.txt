# Delta Upsilon Dance.
## 
### 
The Delta Upsilon gave one of the
prettiest dancing parties of the year
at their lodge Friday night. The
hails and reception-rooms were
gayly decorated with flowers and
greens, and excellent music was
furnished by a San Francisco or
chestra. In addition to University
people, there were present Miss Bur
rell, Miss May Burrell, Miss Lou
Burrell of San Jose, Miss Storey of
Denver, and Miss Cooksey of Menlo
Park.
