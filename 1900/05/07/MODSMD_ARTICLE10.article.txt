# Rates to Students.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Company
has announced a special rate of
one and a third fare to all points
south of Fresno, north of Willows
or Gridley, east of Colfax, and south
of Paso Robles. Tickets will be
good going from May 18th to June
Ist inclusive, and for return from
August 20th to September 15th.
For certificates apply to J. E. Mc-
Dowell, Southern Pacific represen
tative, at the Registrar's office.
