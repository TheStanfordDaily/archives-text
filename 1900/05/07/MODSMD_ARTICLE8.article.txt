# Announcements.
## 
### 
"Sequoia" Meeting.—Those who |
wish to write for the Sequoia next
year will meet in room 62 Wed
nesday at 3:30 or in room 10 at
12:15 Thursday. L. B. Everett.
Glee Club. —The Glee Club will
meet for rehearsal in the chapel
Wednesday evening at 7 o'clock.
Every member is expected to be
present. Concert at West Side,
Saturday evening.
Y. M. C. A.—The regular Y. M.
C. A. service will be held in room
23, Tuesday, at 4:30 p.m. Profes
sor Walter Miller will lead; subject,
"The Crowded Life." The men of
the University are cordially invited.
Senior Assessment. —It is necess
ary to have funds for the prepara
tion for Senior Week, and unless the
assessments are paid, the committee
will be delayed in its work. Assess
ments may be paid to Miss Clara
Martin, Miss Grace Diggles, Miss
C. Wirt, L B. Wickersham, J. H.
Coverley, F. B. Braden, or J. T.
Nourse.
Zoology Camp Fire. —The Zool
ology Club will give its annual
"Camp fire" Thursday evening,
May 10th (weather permitting).
The Club will assemble after lab
oratory hours in the hills, where
the evening will be spent about a
big camp fire. Members intending
to go will please leave names with
J. O. Snyder or W. K. Fisher.
Supper will be served at the camp
fire, and a definite place announced
later.
