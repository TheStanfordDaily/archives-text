# Senior Ball Program.
## 
### 
Arrangements are completed for
the Senior Ball, which takes place
in Encina Club room Monday
evening of Commencement Week.
Yanke's orchestra of seven pieces
will furnish the music, and every
thing indicates that the dance will
be one of the pleasantest affairs of
the year.

dances :
