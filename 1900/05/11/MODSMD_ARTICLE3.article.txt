# Commencement Edition.
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto will issue
a, special edition of sixteen pages
mi the morning of Commencement
Day. It will contain cuts and a
full account of the proceedings of
the week. Copies will be ten cents.
Advance orders will be taken at the
University Press, at the Bookstore,
and at the Palace Pharmacy in
Palo Alto
