# Encina Club.
## 
### 
The directors of Encina Club met
Thursday evening at 7:30 o'clock.
John T. Nourse, '00,was elected grad
uate member in place of Everly M.
Davis, who has resigned. The fol
lowing committees were elected for
the ensuing year: Games and
tables, Raitt, '03, and Boren, '03;
periodicals, Parker, '03; social,
Gaddis, '01.
The following officers have been
elected: President, G. L. Sobey,
'01; vice-president, F. E. A. Gaddis,
'01; secretary, F. H. Boren, '02;
treasurer, J. M. Burkett, '02.
