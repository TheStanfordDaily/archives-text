# Swindells III.
## 
### 
Charles J. Swindells, '01, who
played on the Varsity baseball team
for two seasons, is very ill with ty
phoid fever at the German Hospital
in San Francisco. Last season he
signed as catcher for the Anaconda
team of the Montana league. Lur
ing the season he was considered
one of the stars of the league, stand
ing second and third respectively
on the catching and batting lists.
He was taken ill shortly after his
arrival in San Francisco several
weeks ago and for some time his
condition was critical. However,
at present his condition is much
improved, and he is recovering rap
idly.
