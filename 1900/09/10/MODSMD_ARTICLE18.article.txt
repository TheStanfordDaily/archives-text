Shorthand. —The class in Short
hand will meet in room 13 quad on
Tuesdays and Thursdays at 1:30.
1901 Meeting.—The Senior Class
will meet Wednesday, September
12th, at 1:30 p.m. in room 50 quad.
N. G. Symonds, Pres.
Democratic Club. —All Demo
crats are requested to meet in room
50 quad next Wednesday evening
at 7:30 for the purpose of perfecting
a club organization.
Tuesday Evening Lecture. —
The regular weekly lecture will be
given by Dr. Flugel on ''University
Ideals" in the chapel Tuesday,
September 11th, at 7:30 p.m.
Elementary GREEK.-Elementary
Greek will meet on Monday, Wed
nesday, and Friday at 8:15 in
room " 133, and on Tuesday and
Thursday at 10:15 in room 144.
Freshman Class Meeting.—The
Freshman Class will meet in the
chapel Thursday at 1:30 p.m. for
the purpose of organizing.
F. W. Bancroft, '02 President.
Football. —The office hours of
the football manager will be from
four to five every afternoon. Oflice
will be in new Training House.
J. Burt Gildersleeve, Mgr.
Republican Club. —All Repub
licans of the University are re
quested to meet in room 50 quad
Wednesday at 4:30 p.m. for the
purpose of organizing a Republi
can club.
University Band. —There will
be a meeting in the chapel Wed
nesday, September 12th, at 8 p.m.
for the purpose of organizing the
University band. All students
interested are requested to be pres
ent.
Y. M. C. A. —The Young Men's
Christian Association will hold its
first devotional meeting Tuesday
afternoon in room 23 quad at 4:30.
Professor Miller will preside and
Lloyd E. Harter will give an ad
dress.
Sanskrit. —Students intending
to take the course in Sanskrit, an
nounced for Tuesday and Thurs
day at 8:15, will please meet in
room 31 at 12:15 on Wednesday
to arrange for an hour convenient
for all. Walter Miller.
Student EMPLOYMENT.-The head
quarters of the Student Labor
Bureau will be found in Economics
oflice, room 10 quad. Oflice hour*
Monday, Wednesday and Friday,
9:15-9:45; Tuesday and Thursday,
11:15-11:45. L. E. Harter.
Physical Measurement. — All
students beginning gymnasium
work (Hygiene 1 or 12) must un
dergo a physical examination.
Arrangements for such examina
tions may be made at the oflice in
Encina gymnasium any day this
week between 9 and 12 a.m. or 2
and 5 p.m.
