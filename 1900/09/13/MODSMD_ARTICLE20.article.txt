# Encina Stag.
## 
### 
The directors of Encina Hall de
cided last evening to give a smoker
on Friday evening, September 21st,
to which a general invitation to be
present is extended to all the men
of the University. A program con
sisting of boxing, wrestling, music,
and dancing has been arranged
for the evening. After the enter
tainment every one will assem
ble on Encina steps to sing college
songs.
