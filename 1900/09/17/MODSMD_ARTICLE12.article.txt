# Death of Mrs. Cushman.
## 
### 
Mrs. Cushmart, the mother of
Miss Florence Gushman. '96, died
in Los Angeles on Tuesday last.
Miss Cushman paid a short visit to
Stanford ten days ago, and reached
home just after her mother's death.
Mrs. Cushman, who spent several
years at Stanford, had many warm
friends among the faculty, who
will regret the news of her death
