# Announcements.
## 
### 
Baseball Tjcam.—-All candidates
for the baseball team will appear
on the diamond at 3 p.m. Tuesday,
September 18th, in uniform.
Girls' Glee.—There will be a
business meeting of the Girls' Glee
Club in the chapel Tuesday after
noon at 4:30.
Junior Class Meeting.—There
will be a meeting of the '02Class in
room 50 at 1:30 Tuesday for the
nomination of officers for the ensu
ing semester. F. W. Bancroft.
Homeric Antiquities. —This
course will be given in the Classical
Seminary room on Tuesdays at 2:30
instead of Mondays, as announced.
First lecture, Tuesday, Sept. 18th.
Latin 26. —The class in Latin 26,
History of Latin Literature, in
tended for seniors and graduates,
will meet on Tuesday next at 11:15
according to schedule.
H. R. Fairclough.
Golf Club. —The annual meet
ing of the Golf Club will be held
Tuesday, September 18th, at 4:30
p.m. in room 62 quad. All mem
bers and others who are interested
in golf are requested to be present.
Y. M. C. A. —The regular mid
week devotional meeting will be
held in room 23 quad at 4:30 Tues
day afternoon. F. L. Hess, chair
man of Bible Work Committee,will
speak on "The Profitableness of
Bible Study."
