# University Entries to the Tanforan Field-Day.
## 
### 
The number of University men
who will take part in the field-day
to be given by the Olympic Club
at the Tanforan fair next Saturday
is much larger than was antici
pated. The field-day promises,
in every way, to be the most suc
cessful one ever held on this Coast.

made by men of the two Coast
