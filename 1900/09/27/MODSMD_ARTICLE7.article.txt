# Mandolin Club Meets.
## 
### 
The Girls' Mandolin Club met
Tuesday afternoon for the first time
this semester, and discussed plans
for the work which will he carried
on this year. About twenty girls
were present. It was decided to
hold tlie try-out at the end of the
first month's work. All who wish
to try for the club are requested to
be present at the meeting next
Tuesday afternoon at 4:30 o'clock
in room 33.
The club's officers for this year
are Miss Genevieve Evans. '01,
president; Miss Kathryne Gari
baldi, 'o'2, treasurer and secretary;
Miss Louise McDougald, '02. leader.
