The Varsity lines up tomorrow
for its first contest this season.
Everything points to a good game
although their can be little doubt
as to the outcome. It is only a
question as to the size of the score.
As the games this year are played
on our own campus there is no
reason why everybody should not
be out to see the contest. It will
be the first opportunity this season
of sizing up the team and noting
tts progress. Come prepared to
jolly up and enthuse the team to
do its best.
