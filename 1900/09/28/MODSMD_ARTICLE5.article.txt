# Senior Sombreros.
## 
### 
The sombreros ordered by the
Senior Class arrived this afternoon
and may be obtained at the Book
store on payment of price, $2. No
hat will be sold except to those
who ordered, and only the size or
dered. Hats not called for by
Wednesday night will be returned
to the city.
