# Chairmen of the County Clubs.
## 
###

ing last Thursday chairmen were
appointed for the local county or
ganizations. Those selected so far
are as follows: Alameda, E. S.
Page; Amador, W. E. Sherwood;
Contra Costa, W. E. Billings;
Fresno, M. F. McCormick; Hum
boldt, M. C. Decarli; Kern, E. T.
Maples; Los Angeles, C. M. War
dall; Mendocino, M. A. Thomas;
Monterey, S. Hamilton; Napa, W.
C. Beagles; Nevada, C. 11. Prisk;
Orange, .1. T. Nourse; Riverside,
C. E. Waite; Santa Cruz, W. J.
Stack; San Bernardino, C. C.
Thorns; Sacramento, R. L. Brooke;
San Diego, C. DeW. Scott; San
Francisco, Frank English; San
Luis Obispo, W. G. A. Schulte;
San Mateo, H. E. Holmquist;
Santa Clara, J. G. De Forest; So
noma, A. E. Cooley; Siskiyou, I).
M. Horn; Tulare, B. P. Oak ford;
Ventura, C. A. Whitmore.
There are several counties which
have not yet selected chairmen.
Students from those counties who
are interested in the amendment
are requested to report at once at
room 5 on the quad.
