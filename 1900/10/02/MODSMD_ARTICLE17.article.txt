# Y. W. C. A. Meeting.
## 
### 
At the first general business
meeting of the Y. W. C. A. held
at the home of Dr. O. L. Elliott last
Friday evening it was decided to
henceforth have the mid-weekly de
votional meeting of the association
in some room on the quad. An
nouncement of time and place will
he made at the next Sunday even
ing gathering in the chapel. Forty
new members, many of these com
ing from among the older students,
were voted into the society. Fol
lowing the business transaction
came a "spread" and social chat in
terspersed with college songs.
