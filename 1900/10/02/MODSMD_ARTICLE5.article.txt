# Joint Republican Rally.
## 
### 
The Stanford University Repub
lican Club will unite with the Palo
Alto Club in a joint rally next
Friday evening in Palo Alto. The
speakers will he Hon. Duncan Mc-
Kinlay of Santa Rosa, a Republi
can presidential elector four years
ago, and A. J. Dannebaum of Berk
eley, who represented the Uni
versity of California in the Carnot
and Intercollegiate debates of 1898.
The rally will be held in Nortree
Hall. Professor San ford, president
of the Palo Alto Club, will preside.
