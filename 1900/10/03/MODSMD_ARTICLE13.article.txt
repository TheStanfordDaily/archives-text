# Plug Ugly Meeting.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Plug Ugly
Committee last night nothing in
the way of a ceremonial "was decid
ed on definitely. The committee is
considering two propositions —one
a play to be written by F. B. Wag
ner, '03, and the other an Oriental
show to be given by Chris M. Brad
ley, '01, and W. M. Erb, '01.
It has been definitely decided to
hold the Plug Ugly on the football
field but the date is not fixed as
yet. A small admission fee will
be charged to cover the expense.
