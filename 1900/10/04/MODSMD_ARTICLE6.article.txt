# Republican Rally.
## 
### 
At the Republican rally to be
held in Nortree Hall tomorrow
evening the speakers will be Hon.
Duncan McKinlay of Santa Rosa
and A. J. Dannebaum of Berkeley.
The members of the University
Republican Club will meet in En
cina at 7:15 and march to Palo
Alto in a body. Music will be
furnished by the University band.
