# Freshman Debating- Society.
## 
### 
The Freshman Debating Society
met in room 31 Wednesday night.
After the reading of the minutes
by the secretary, C. B. Wintler,
chairman of the committee on
constitution, read the first draft of
the by-laws, which were finally
adopted after considerable discus
sion. The regular program was
then carried out. Those taking
part in the debate were: O. C.
Spencer and I). V. Noland, affirma
tive, and E. S. Harper and R. H.
Kingery, negative. The judges
awarded the decision to the nega
rive. The society will meet regu
larly on Wednesday evenings at
7:30 in room 31 quad.
