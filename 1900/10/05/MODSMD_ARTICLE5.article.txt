# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of the
student body held its regular meet
ing in room 11 Wednesday evening
with President Marrack in the chair
and Morris, '01, Cantwell, '01,
Sobey, '01, Wheeler, '02, Raitt, '03,
and Traeger, '03, present. The
amendment regarding the disfran
chising of students who do not pay
the annual assessment was recom
mended. W. R. Eraser on behalf
of the University band asked a do
nation for the purpose of purchas
ing uniforms, and $125 was granted.
The remainder necessary will be
collected by subscriptions. An ap
propriation of $10 was made the
University orchestra for the pur
chase of music.
