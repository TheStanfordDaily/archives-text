# Monteray County Committee.
## 
### 
The Monterey county committee
of the amendment club met in
room 1 quad Friday evening
and made a very satisfactory start
in planning out its work of the
coming campaign. The names of
those in Monterey county who are
in a position to take an active in
terest in the amendment have been
selected and arrangements have
been made by the members of the
committee to carry on individual
work.
