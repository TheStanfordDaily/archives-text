# Encina "At Home."
## 
### 
Invitations are out for an "at
home''" to be given at Encina Hall
next Saturday evening. The fol
lowing ladies, have consented to act
as patronesses: Mrs. Pease, Mrs.
Cubberley, Mrs. Newsom, Miss
Ray, Miss Short, and Miss Smith.
Sander's orchestra will furnish
music for dancing, which will begin
promptly at half-past seven o'clock.
