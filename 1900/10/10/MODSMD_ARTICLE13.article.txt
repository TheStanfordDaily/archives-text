# Faculty Ladies' Club.
## 
### 
The Faculty Ladies' Club will
meet at Mrs. Hranner's on Monday,

October 15th, at three o'clock. Dr.
Campbell will show his water-color
sketches made in Egypt and Japan,
and in connection will give an ex
planatory talk.
The ladies are requested to hand
to the secretary, Mrs. Ross, the
names of those whom they wish in
vited to the meeting, and they are
further requested to he ready to pay
their yearly dues of 75 cents to the
treasurer, Mrs. Newsom.
