# Faculty Ladies' Club.
## 
### 
The Faculty Ladies' Club will
meet at Mrs. Bran tier's on Monday,
October 15th, at three o'clock. Dr.
Campbell will show his water-color
sketches made in Egypt and
Japan, and in connection will give
an explanatory talk.
The ladies are requested to hand
to the secretary, Mrs. Iloss, the
names of those whom they wish
invited to the meeting, and they
are further requested to be ready
to pay their yearly dues of 75 cents
to the treasurer, Mrs. Newsom.
