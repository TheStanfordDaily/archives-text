# Dr. Jordan to Classify Fishes.
## 
### 
The entire government collection
of fishes taken in Japanese waters
has lately been received by the
Department of Zoology. These
fishes, among which are many rare
and odd specimens, are to beclassi
fied under the direction of Dr. Jor
dan, whose collecting trip in Japan
this summer has added much to
the knowledge of Pacific ichthyolo
gy. The greater part of the work
will be completed before Christmas.
