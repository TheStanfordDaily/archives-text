# Stanford Alumni in New York.
## 
### 
R. C. Victor, '00, who entered
the Columbia Law School this fall,
writes of the following Stanford
alumni who are now in New York:
" Chauncey " Thomas, '99, who
attended the Columbian Law School
at Washington, 1). C., last year,
has entered Columbia Law School
as a regular first-year student. P.
B. Anspacher, '00, has also entered
Columbia Law School after a six
months' tour in Europe.
T. G. Russell, '95, who has now
graduated at Columbia College of
Physicians and Surgeons, is house
surgeon at St. Luke's hospital, New
York. Harry B. Reynolds, '96,
Jack Reynolds' brother, is his as
sistant.
