# Football Song-book.
## 
### 
The new football song-books are
very nearly completed and will be
put on sale in a few days at the
Bookstore on the campus and in
Palo Alto. Besides the prize song
and the new songs accepted by the
committee the song-book will con
tain two new songs by Charles K.
Field, '95, and "One, Two, Three,
Four," by L. Mac F. Bowman, '01.
The new song-book will furnish
the best chance to judge between
the merits of Bowman's prize song
and the one written by H. 1). Wal
ter, '01.
The song-book will be sold for
fifteen cents a copy and only 500
will be printed.
