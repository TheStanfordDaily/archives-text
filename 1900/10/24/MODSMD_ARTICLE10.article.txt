# Rally in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
On Friday night, October 26th,
the Republican Club of Palo Alto
will bold a grand rally, which will
very likely be the last one of the
campaign to be held in that town.
The Rough Riders' Battalion, a
uniformed Republican marching
club of Ban Jose, will be present
in a body and will give a parade
preceding the rally. The speak
ers of the evening will be John E.
Richards, a prominent attorney of
Sari Jose, and Captain 11. L. \Yells
of Portland, Oregon. Captain
Wells has just returned from Ma
nila and will speak on the "Philip
pine Question."
