# Christian Association Outing.
## 
### 
The Christian Associations an
nounce a trip to King's Mountain
for next Tuesday, provided the
weather is favorable. Buses will
leave the quad promptly at nine
o'clock that morning, and names
of all wishing to go should he
handed in by Monday evening.
Basket lunches will be carried,
while those who wish to take dinner
at King's Mountain house might do
well to notify Mrs. King at River
side of their intentions in advance.
The return trip will be by moon
light. Names may be left at the
Registrar's office, with Miss Martha
Scott, or Miss Mary Skillings at
Roble or with Herbert Adams or
Lloyd Harter at Encina.
