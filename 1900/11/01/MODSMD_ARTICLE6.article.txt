# Freshman Debating Society.
## 
### 
The time for meeting of the
Freshman Debating Society has
been changed to Friday night and
the next regular meeting takes
place on the evening of November
2d, in room 31 at 7:30 p.m. The
subject of a symposium and the
speakers will be announced at the
time. The subject of the regular
debate will be: "Resolved, That
the integrity of China should be
maintained." Affirmative, A. C.
Joy and I). V. Noland; negative,
A. J. Klamt and A. L. Dahlgren.
