# Trip to King's Mountain.
## 
### 
The trip to King's Mountain un
der the auspices of the Christian
Associations, that was planned for
yesterday, has been postponed un
til Saturday, November 11th, on
account of the work to be done on
the amendment. Buses will leave
the quadrangle at 9 o'clock Satur
day morning. All are invited to
go, either taking their lunches or
procuring lunch at the Mountain
House. Those that go will leave
their names with Miss Shillings,
Ruble, or L. E. Harter, Encina.
