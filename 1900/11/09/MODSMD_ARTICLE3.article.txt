# Amendment Rally.
## 
### 
There will be a rally in the En
cina club-room next Monday even
ing to celebrate the passing of the
Stanford amendment. After the
rally in the club-room there will be
a procession to a big bonfire on the
old football field. Dr. Jordan will
probably speak, as will other mem
bers of the faculty and alumni.
The Glee Club and the Band will
furnish music for the evening.
