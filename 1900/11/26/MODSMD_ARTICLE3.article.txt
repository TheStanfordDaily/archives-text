# Thanksgiving Train.
## 
### 
Arrangements have been com
pleted with the Southern Pacific
Company for a special train to the
city Thursday. The train will leave
the University at 8:30 a.m. and
returning will leave the Third and
Townsend street depot at 11:45
Thursday evening. The round
trip rate is $1, good going only on
the special and returning at 5:00,
5:30, 6:30, or 11:45 of the same
day. Tickets are also to be sold at
$1.30, good going on any train
Thursday morning and returning
up to and including Monday. They
will be on sale at the Registrar's
office Wednesday afternoon.
