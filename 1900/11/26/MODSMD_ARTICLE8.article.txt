# Encina Club "At Home."
## 
### 
The <l at home" given by Encina
Club to the women of the Univer
sity last Saturday night was, like
all of the club's entertainments, a
great success. The affair was in
the nature of an informal dance,
about one hundred couples being
present. Music was furnished by
an orchestra from San Jose. After
10:80 candles were lighted and the
dance continued until 12 o'clock.
