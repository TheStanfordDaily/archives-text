# Announcements.
## 
### 
Hooters' Caps.—The last lot of
caps have been received at the
Bookstore and are ready for distri
bution.
Second Eleven. —All men on
the second eleven are requested to
turn in their suits at the training
house at once.
Freshman Glee. —Tickets for
the Freshman (Jlee, December 7th,
1900, are now on sale at the Book
store and at Hall's Drugstore in
Palo Alto.
Y. M. C. A. —Professor H. D.
Stearns will lead the meeting of
the Young Men's Christian Associ
ation in room '28 tomorrow after
noon at 4:30 o'clock.
Freshman Football Teams. —
All members of the 1904 football
team will report at the training
house at 1:30 p.m. tomorrow, in
order to have their photograph
taken. L. P. Bansbach,
Captain.
