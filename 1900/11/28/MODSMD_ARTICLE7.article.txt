# Freshman Glee.
## 
### 
Arrangements for the Freshman
(jlee, which will take place Friday
evening, December 7th, in Encina
club-room, are progressing favora

success, as a large attendance is
assured.

decided to serve only light refresh

of twenty dances and four extras.
Vanke's orchestra will furnish the
music.
