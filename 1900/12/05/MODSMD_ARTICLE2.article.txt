# Football Captain.
## 
### 
Captain Uurnett has issued a
call for a meeting of the members
of the Varsity squad for the pur
pose of electing a captain for the
season of 1901. The meeting will
he held in Encina parlor at 8:30
p.m. tomorrow (Thursday). All
those who have played in any
scheduled game against Reliance,
Normal, Nevada, Oregon, or Cali
fornia are entitled to a vote. The
number of members who have
made the Varsity squad is twenty
four.
