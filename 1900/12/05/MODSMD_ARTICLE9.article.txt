Girls' Mandolin Club. —There

Faculty Ladies' Club. —The
next meeting of the Faculty Ladies'

books ordered by departments of

Latin, and Physics have been
placed on the New Hooks' shelf for

faculty during the current week.
English 1. —An examination
will be held in English 1 (entrance)
on Friday, December 7th, at 2:30
p.m. in room (32 quad, for matricu
lated students. Except by special
permission, such students will not
be admitted to the regular entrance
examination in January.

Professor John Fryer of the U. C.
department of Oriental languages
and literature will lecture tomor
row evening. His subject is "The
Chinese Problem." The lecture

slides as announced in the last
