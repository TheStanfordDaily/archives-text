# Tuesday Evening's Concert.
## 
### 
A concert will be given next
Tuesday evening by Prof. H. B.
Pasmore in place of the regular
lecture. He will be assisted by
the Misses Buelah George and
Queletta Geery, and Mrs. Edith
Scott Basford. Prof. Pasmore's
daughters, the Misses Mary, Su
san, and Dorothy Pasmore, will
render selections on the piano,
violin, and cello. Theconcert will
be given at the Assembly Hall.
