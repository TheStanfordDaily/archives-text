# Freshman Glee.
## 
### 
The dance last Friday night
given in Encina club room was the
first class event of the year and one
of the most successful Freshman
dances given in years. Over one
hundred couples were present, so
that the class is assured of a good
surplus.
The large club room was taste
fully decorated with palms and red
berries, and presented a very pleas
ing appearance. Music was fur
nished by Yanke's orchestra and
dancing was continued until 2 a.m.
