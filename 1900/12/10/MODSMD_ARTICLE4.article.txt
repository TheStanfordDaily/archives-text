# Kalendar Kirmess.
## 
### 
The Woman's Club of Palo Alto
will give a Kalendar Kirmess in
Fraternity Hall on Friday evening,
December 14th, and Saturday
afternoon, December 15th. There
will be for sale Christmas gifts of
all kinds, Stanford souvenirs, and
housekeeper's helps. A silhouette
artist will be on hand to take pic
tures, a gipsy fortune-teller, and
various other attractions. The
proceeds will be devoted to the
book fund of the public library.
