# Excursion Rates.
## 
### 
Special rates will be made by
the Southern Pacific Company to
all southern points for the vaca
tion. Tickets will be on sale De
cember 19th to 22d inclusive, good
for return until January loth. For
Pullman reservations apply to J.
E. McDowell.
The Southern Pacific Company
have announced a special round
trip rate of $3 to Pacific Grove
and Santa Cruz for the vacation.
Tickets will be on sale at Palo
Alto December 21st to 20th in
clusive and good for return until
January 15th.
