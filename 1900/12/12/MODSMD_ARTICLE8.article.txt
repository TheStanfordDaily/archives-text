# Freshman Debating- Society.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the society last
Friday, officers were elected as fol
lows: President, Wood; vice-presi
dent, Miss Barnhouse; secretary,
R. Anderson; treasurer, A. T. Par
sons; sergeant-at-arms, A. Thomas.
At the next meeting there will he a
regular debate, as follows: "Re
solved, That the Porto Rico tariff
hill is contrary to the principle
enunciated in the Declaration
of Independence." Affirmative,
Chandler, Miss Beebe, Harper;
negative, Richardson, Anderson,
Miss Barnhouse.
