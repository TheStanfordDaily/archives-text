# The
						Next "Chaparral."
## 
### 
The initial number of the
Chaparral for this semester will
appear Wednesday, January 16th.
Besides its usual contents in the
way of verses, pictures, and jokes,
there will he special and timely
contributions on a recent article
in the Sequoia. They are in the
nature of comments and answers,
and their nature may he judged
from the title: An editorial on the
"New Dispensation," "What Alma
Mater Should do to Ween Her
Babies," "An Infant's Confession,"
and "Mr. Tooley on a Recent Out
burst." The issue will be sold at
the usual price, 15 cents.
