# Registration at Berkeley.
## 
### 
The total number of registra
tions in the University of Califor
nia will lie between 2,200 and 2,300
for this semester. This is an increase
of about eight per cent over the
number registered at the beginning
of the corresponding term last year.
Berkeley ranks second among the
universities of America in the num
ber of its graduate and undergrad
uate students.
