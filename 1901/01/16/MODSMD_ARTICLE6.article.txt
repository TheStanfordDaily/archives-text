# Vesper Service.
## 
### 
Professor Griffin will lead the
vesper service tomorrow ( Thurs
day) at 4:80 o'clock in the chapel.
A part of the musical service will
be a solo by Mrs. Pease, and two
selections, "Saviour, Source of
Every Blessing," and "Evening,"
by a quartet composed of H. Gay,
E. Gilman, \V. K. Whitaker, and
P. Gilman.
