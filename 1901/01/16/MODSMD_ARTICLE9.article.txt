# PROFESSOR SPENCER LEAVES.
## He Sends in His Resignation and It Is Accepted by the University. 
### 
Professor David Ellsworth Spen
cer, associate professor in History,
sent in his resignation this after
noon and it was immediately ac
cepted. Professor Spencer received
his B. L. degree from the Univer
sity of Wisconsin in 1887 and his
A. M. degree from Harvard in
1891. He came here in 1898 from
the University of Michigan, where
he was acting assistant professor of
history.
