New Books. —The following has
been received at the Bookstore:

Brookey.
Philolexian. —There will he a
business meeting of Philolexian to
morrow evening at 7:30 in room 13
quad. Visitors are welcome.
Girl's Glee Club. —There will
be a meeting of the Girl's Glee
Club next Tuesday afternoon at
4:30 in the chapel.
Jessie A. Moore, President.
Orchestra Meeting. — There
will be a meeting of those who wish
to play in the Orchestra on Mon
day evening in the chapel at 7:30.
Arrangements will be made for the
semester.
Euphronia Debating Society.—
There will be a business meeting of
Euphronia Debating Society to
morrow evening for the purpose of
electing officers for the coming
Hemes ter.
Filing Study Cards Postponed.
—On account of a necessary read
justment of courses the filing of
study cards may be delayed until
Wednesday, January 23d.
0. L. Elliott, Registrar.

There will be an important meet
ing of the Athletic Committee Sat
urday evening at 7:30 in room il
quad. Cecil M. Marrack, .
President.
Football Notice. —All men hav
ing football goods in their posses
sion belonging to the student body
will please deliver them at once to

Acting Manager.
Freshman Reception.—The reg
ular reception of the Y. M. C. A.
and Y. VV. C. A. to Freshmen will
be given in Encina club room Fri
day evening, January 25th. A
short and enjoyable program will
be arranged.

Hall.
