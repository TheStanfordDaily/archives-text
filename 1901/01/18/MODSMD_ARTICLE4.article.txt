# Sunday Sermon.
## 
### 
Mr. Geo. W. Leavitt, the Inter
national College Secretary for
Foreign Missions, will preach next
Sunday's sermon. His subject
will be "The World's Missions."
In the evening he will speak on
the Student Volunteer Movement.
Mr. Leavitt is a graduate of Beloit.
He was Y. M. C. A. General Secre
tary at Perdu, and has taken post
graduate work at Harvard.
