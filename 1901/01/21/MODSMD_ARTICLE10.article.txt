# Death of H. W. Rose.

###

Christian Association last year and

for 1901, died on the 11th of Janu
ary after a short illness. Mr. Rose
is a close friend of many Stanford
students. He came here in May of
last year and held a few meetings
urging students to attend the Stu
dent Conference that was to he held
that summer at Pacific lirove.
Those who attended that conference
