# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of the
student body met in room 11 quad
last evening. The manager of the
"James Wobberts, Freshman"
company was allowed the use of the
scenery for Thursday night, the
usual price of $35 to be charged
him. A. J. Copp, Jr., '02, was
elected business manager of the
Daily Palo Alto for this semester.
A. 11. Suzzalo, 'i) c J, Frank Adams,
'01, and M. F. McCormick, '02,
were chosen to represent Stanford
on the Intercollegiate Debating
Committee. The resignation of
Harry Edwards as baseball man
ager was accepted, and Eugene
Warren, '01, wns elected to suc
ceed him. H. W. Edwards,
'01, was chosen to fill the
vacancy on the Athletic Com
mittee caused by the resignation of
J. F. Lanagan, '00. The resigna
tion of P. K. Gilman, '01, as track
manager was accepted, and his'
brotlfer, C. E. Gilman, '01, unani
mously elected to succeed him. It
was decided to allow the Debating
Committee to use student body
funds to the extent of thirty dol
lars. .
