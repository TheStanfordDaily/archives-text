# The
						Last Word.
## 
### 
The Chaparral will appear in the
morning with a communication
from Dr. Elliott setting forth his
position concerning his original ar
ticle in the Sequoia in the light of
the recent criticism that the Chap
arral made on that article. The
editor has been content to let the
Registrar have the last word and
has made no comment of any sort
on Dr. Elliott's answer.
