# " Quad " Contest Reopened.
## 
### 
The committee having in charge
the awarding of the prizes offered
by the editors of the Junior "Quad"
has decided to reopen the contests.
Prizes will he given, as announced
previously, for the best story, the
best, poem, and the best humorous
verses. It is especially desired
that stories dealing with college
life be submitted. The committee
reserves the right to refuse to award
any prize if the material submitted
is not worthy of it, and also the
right to use any or all manuscripts.
The contest will close Friday, Feb
ruary loth.
