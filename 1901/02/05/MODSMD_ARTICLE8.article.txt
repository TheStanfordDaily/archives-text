# Dr. Jordan Lectures in Berkeley.
## 
### 
President Jordan lectured yes
terday before the Town and Gown
Club of Berkeley, speaking briefly
on the subject <>112 education. lie
said:
"The universities of today are
ready to take hold of any question
and make it go. They are not
working today entirely for 'culture'
and have lost much of the vanity
which before characterized them.
We are in this country lifting the
standard higher than in any other.
Colleges strive now to produce the
best individual and to train him
adequately for that work to which
be is best suited."
