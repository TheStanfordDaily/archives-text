The following regulations apply
to all candidates for graduation in
May, 1901:
1. The final list of candidates
will he posted April 10, 1901, im
mediately after the midsemester
recess. After this date no name
can he added to the list, and no
person, not in this list, will he con
sidered as a candidate in May.
2. After the final semester re
ports have been handed in, Friday,
May 24th, no candidate will he al
lowed before Commencement day
to remove any "conditions" or
make up any "incomplete" work.

The following regulations apply
to all candidates for graduation in
May, 1901:
1. The final list of candidates
will he posted April 10, 1901, im
mediately after the midsemester
recess. After this date no name
can he added to the list, and no
person, not in this list, will he con
sidered as a candidate in May.
2. After the final semester re
ports have been handed in, Friday,
May 24th, no candidate will he al
lowed before Commencement day
to remove any "conditions" or
make up any "incomplete" work.
