# Announcements.
## 
### 
Orchestra. —The University Or
chestra will meet tomorrow night
at 7:30 in the chapel.
Vesper Service. — The Vesper
Service on Thursday at 4:30 will be
led by Dr. Elliott. Mr. Jensen will
sing two songs by Beethoven:
"Hope" and "Penitence."
"Sword and Sandals." —Mem-
bers of "Sword and Sandals" will
meet at Franklin's photo gallery
Thursday at 3 p.m.
F. H. Drake.
Girls' Mandolin Club. —The
Girls' Mandolin Club will meet
Thursday afternoon at 4:30 in
room 30. Genevieve Evans,
President.
Baseball. — All baseball men
will report to me at the training
house on rainy days at the usual
time. The purpose of the meeting
is to review the points of the game.
W. H. Murphy, Coach.
New Books. —The following have
been received at the Bookstore:
Victor Hugo's Hernani; Mittel
hochdeutsches Taschen worterbuch,
Matthias Lexar; Deutsches Leben,
Dr. Julius Diffenbacher; Murray's
Plane Trigonometry.
