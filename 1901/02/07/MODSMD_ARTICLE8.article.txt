# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee met in
room 11 quad last night, President
Marrack presiding. It was decid
ed to pay hack dues to the amount
of $10 to the Pacific Athletic Asso
ciation of the Amateur Athletic
Union, and C. A. Cantwell, '01, was
elected Stanford representative on
its hoard of directors. Owing to
the proximity of the Carnot debate,
the meeting was poorly attended.
