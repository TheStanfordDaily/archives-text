# Special Notices.
## 
### 
For Sale —A Bet of Torts, good
as new. See room 117 Encina.
Call at Peck's Studio and see
the new style," Miniature," at $1
per dozen.
Wanted—A few select mandolin
pupils. Address, Box 124, Stan
ford University.
Wanted —A competent stenogra
pher and typewriter. Address
P. O. Box A, Palo Alto.
There may be others just as
good, but at The Spa you may get
some of the best ice cream, ice
cream soda, and home-made candy
of all kinds.
P. L. Seamans, the Palo Alto
jeweler, has just received a large
stock of the finest Christmas goods
in sterling silver, jewelry, watches,
and noveltiesof all kinds.
Bouquets for the Sophomore Co
tilion will be furnished by F. Peri
cano & Co., the San Francisco
florists. Orders can be left in writ
ing at this office or at room 71,
Encina Hall.
J. T. Nourse has given up the
lied Star Laundry agency and
A. Strout has taken it up and will
represent the company in Encina
and on the campus. In Palo Alto
the Red Star Laundry Co. will be
represented by A. L. Strout instead
of by E. A. Strout, as in the past.
