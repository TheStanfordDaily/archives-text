# Gymnasium Club.
## 
### 
About a dozen members were
present at the meeting of the Gym
nasium Club last night. Boxing
was the main feature of the pro
gram, and several close and scien
tific contests were developed.
Wrestling and fencing bouts formed
centres of secondary interest.
