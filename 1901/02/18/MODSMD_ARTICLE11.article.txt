# Statue for Arboretum.
## 
### 
A statue of peculiar beauty, com
memorative of Henry Lathrop, Mrs.
Stanford's brother, who died about
two years ago, has been placed in
the arboretum, between the Mauso
leum and the faculty God's A ere.
The piece consists of a female fig
ure of heroic size, representing the
Angel of Death, kneeling with
bowed bead over a sculptured ped
estal. The material is snow-white
marble, and the workmanship
Italian.
