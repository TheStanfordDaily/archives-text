# Mandolin Club Meeting.
## 
### 
On account of pressure of work
W. M. Parkin, '01, haw resigned
his position as manager of the
Mandolin Club. The club will
meet tomorrow evening to elect his
successor.
There will also be a try-out for
the mandolin and guitar men for
the concert to be given in Palo
Alto Saturday evening.
