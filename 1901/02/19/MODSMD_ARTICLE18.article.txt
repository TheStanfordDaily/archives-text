# News of Former Students.
## 
### 
—C. DeQ. Whittle, ex-'OO, is an
art student in Munich.
—Oliver P. Morton, '95, is prac
ticing law at Phoenix, Arizona.
—Walter Clark, '99, is vice
principal and instructor of Eng
lish and history in the high school
of Petaluma.
—Will H. Crow, '98, passed a
very creditable examination before
the Supreme Court of New York,
for admission to the bar of that
State, last month. He was one of
fifty successful applicants out of
one hundred and fifty-two who
took the examination. He will
graduate from Harvard next June
and expects to locate in New York
City, where he has already received
a flattering offer.
