# Excursion From the City.
## 
### 
For the benefit of the alumni
and others who may wish to attend
the ball game Friday afternoon, an
excursion will be run from San
Francisco on that day, under the
direction of the Fireman's Fund
team. The train will leave the city
in the forenoon, and will be
switched on to the track at Encina
Hall. The return trip will he
made the evening of the same day.
This excursion will be of particu
lar interest to those who intend to
be present at the Sophomore Co
tillion. It. will now be possible for
them to take in both events with
out any inconvenience. The fare
for the round trip will probably be
one dollar.
