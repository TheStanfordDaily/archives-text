# Basket-Bali Games.
## 
### 
The gymnasium authorities are
contemplating the feasibilty of in
augurating contests in basket-ball
among the different classes repre
sented by the gymnasium students.
Basket-ball has never attracted

much interest as a sport at Stan
ford, and these class games wil
bring it into prominence and ere
ate new interest. During good
weather basket-ball is made a part
of the general apparatus work in
the gymnasium, so that all stu
dents have had an opportunity
to learn the game. There are sev
eral good players in the different
classes, and these contests should
prove very interesting.
