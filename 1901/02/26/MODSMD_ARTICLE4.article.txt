# Hand-ball Tournament.
## 
### 
Forty-two men have signified
their intention to compete in the
hand-ball tournament of singles
which will be played next Satur
day. The drawings for position
will be made today and tomorrow,
and they will be published Wednes
day. It is believed that the singles
can be played off in two Saturdays,
and the finals will probably be
played on Tuesday, two weeks
from today.
