# Concert Next Tuesday Evening.
## 
### 
Mr. Samuel I. Savannah, violin
ist, and Alfred Fickenscher, pian
ist, will give u concert in the
Assembly Hall next Tuesday
evening. This concert is to he
given in place of the regular Tues
day evening lecture. These men
are very well known at the Uni
versity, having appeared here sev
eral times in the past.
