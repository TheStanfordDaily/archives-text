# Sophomores Chose Affirmative.
## 
### 
The Sophomore and Freshman
debating teams met today. By the
flip ' 112 a coin the Sophomores won
the sides, it having been previously
decided to debate the Intercolleg
iate question, "Resolved, That the
extinction of the Boer Republics is
for the permanent interests of civil
ization." The Sophomores chose
the affirmative.
