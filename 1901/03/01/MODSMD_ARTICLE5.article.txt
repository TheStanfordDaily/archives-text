# Founder's Day Exercises.
## 
### 
Regular University work will be
suspended on Saturday, March 9th,
Founder's Day. The exercises in
commemoration of the occasion
will be held in the Assembly Hall
on Friday evening, March Bth, in
order that all may have an oppor
tunity to hear Mr. Lummis.
Music will be furnished by the
Choral Society.
