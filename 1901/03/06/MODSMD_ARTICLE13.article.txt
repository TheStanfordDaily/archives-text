# English Club Meets.
## 
### 
The third meeting of the new
society of students interested in
literature was held last night at
the home of Mr. Seward in Palo
Alto. The attendance was gratify
ingly large, the business meet
ing which preceded the regular
program, several amendments were
offered to the constitution, and the
name of the organization was for
mally declared to be "The Stan
ford English Club."
The more enjoyable portion of
the evening was devoted by Mr.
Seward to an informal account
from a student's standpoint of the
features that distinguish Oxford
from American universities.
