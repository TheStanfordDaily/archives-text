# Founder's Day Program.
## 
### 
Tomorrow night in the Assembly
Hall will occur the annual Foun
der's Day exercises. Dr. Jordan
will preside and the address will be
delivered by Charles F. Lummis,
editor of the Land of Sunshine, on
the subject, "Right and Wrong
Among Students." The Choral
Society will render two selections.
Exercises commence at 8 o'clock.
