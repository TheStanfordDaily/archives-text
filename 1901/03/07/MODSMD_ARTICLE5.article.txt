# Freshmen Play Belmont School.
## 
### 
The Freshman baseball team went
to Belmont this afternoon to play
Belmont School. It is the inten
tion of the manager of the team to
have several games with outside
preparatory schools, though most
of the other games will be played
on the campus. The batting order
this afternoon at Belmont is as
follows: Bowers, c; Tritch, p; Tay
lor, lb; Misner, 2b; Ballagh, 3b;
Yerrington, ss; Luce, 1.f.; Lamb,
c.f.; Wilson, r.f.; McNaught and
Stevenson, substitutes.
