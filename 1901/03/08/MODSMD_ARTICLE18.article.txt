# Announcements.
## 
### 
Christian Associations. —L. L.
Loofbourow, '02, will lead the Sun
day evening meeting of the Chris
tian Associations.
English Seminary.—The Semi
nary in Literature will not convene
Saturday, March 9th.
M. B. Anderson.
University Sermon. — Professor
Charles M. Bakewell of the Uni
versity of California will deliver
the Sunday sermon on March 10th.
Thesuhjectwill he "Immortalities."
Faculty Laoies' Club. — The
Faculty Ladies' Club will meet at
the house of Mrs. C. 1). Marx, in
I'alo Alto, next Monday afternoon,
March 1 lth, at three o'clock. Dr.
Murray will speak of his visit to
Greece.
Glee and Mandolin Clubs.—All
members of the Glee and Mandolin
Clubs will meet at the Delta Up
silon house Sunday, at 10 a.m.
March 10th. The Easter trip will
be settled at this meeting. All
members must be present.
W. M. Parkin.
