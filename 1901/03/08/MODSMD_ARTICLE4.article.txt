# Caps and Gowns.
## 
### 
The girls of the Senior Class ap
peared on the quad today for the
first time this semester in caps and
gowns. Heretofore the Senior
Co-eds have been wearing the som
brero. They have decided to wear
caps and gowns one day in every
week hereafter until Senior Week
when they will be expected to wear
them every day. Monday has
been chosen as the appropriate day
of the week for caps and gowns
until then.
