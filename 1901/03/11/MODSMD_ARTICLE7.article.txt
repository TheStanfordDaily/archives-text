# No Easter Trip for Musical Clubs.
## 
### 
The combined musical clubs met
at the Delta Upsilon house yester
day in conjunction with Treasurer
John T. Nourse to consider the
question of an Easter trip. A
trip to Northern California had
been practically decided on, but
owing to the inability of several of
the prominent members to go,
particularly the specialists, it was
deemed advisable to give the mat
ter up. The student body treas
urer, however, was empowered to
make dates for three concerts.
These will be given at San Jose,
Palo Alto, and in the Assembly
Halli,in the near future.
