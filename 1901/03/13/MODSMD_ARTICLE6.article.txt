# Announcements.
## 
### 
New Book. —Tompkins' Philos
ophy of Teaching has been received
at the Bookstore.
Sunday Sermon. —Dr. A. T.
Murray will deliver the address on
Sunday, March 17th. His subject
is not yet announced.
Vesper Service. —The vesper
service on Thursday will be led by
Mr. Bartlett. Mrs. Brown will
play two violin solos. All are
cordially invited to attend.
Freshman Track Men.— Mem
bers of Freshman Track team will
meet on the track at so'clock Thurs
day, to elect a captain.
A. S. Henley, Manager.
Student Body Meeting.—There
will lie a meeting of the student
body in the chapel Friday at 1:15
to consider the proposed amend
ments to the constitution.
Cecil M M arrack, Pres.
