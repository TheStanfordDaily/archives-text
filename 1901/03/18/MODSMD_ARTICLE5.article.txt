# Noted Visitor.
## 
### 
Professor George J. Brush, one
of the most noted mineralogists
of the United States, was a visitor
at the University Friday. Mr.
Brush was for forty years pro
fessor of mineralogy at Yale
University, and for twenty-six
years director of the Sheffield
Scientific School. He is now Di
rector Emeritus of the same insti
tution. He has written several
very important works on mineral
ogy and chemistry. He is visit
ing his daughter, Mrs. Parsons, of
San Mateo.
