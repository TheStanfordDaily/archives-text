# News from Dr. Branner.
## 
### 
The Cornell Sun of March 15th
has the following to say of Dr*.
Branner :
"Vice-president Branner of Le
land Stanford Junior University is
expected in Ithaca this week. He
is making an extensive Eastern
trip to secure some new professors
for his university. Several Cor
nell men will probably be recom
mended for positions. Professor
Branner is a graduate of Cornell of
the class of 1882. He is a well
known geologist. While a student
at Cornell he accompanied Profes
sor Harrt to Brazil to make a geo
logical survey of that country."
