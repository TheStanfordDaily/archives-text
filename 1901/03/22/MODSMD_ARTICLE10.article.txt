# Prof. Rendtorff Going to Germany.
## 
### 
Dr. Karl G. Remitorff, assistant
professor of German, has obtained
a leave of absence for a year and
will spend his Sabbatical vacation
in Germany. Professor Kendtorff
intends to consume most of the
period in completing several books
upon which he has been working
for Bome time. He will be one se
mester at Kiel, his old university,
and one semester at Berlin, return
ing to resume his work here in the
fall of 1902.
