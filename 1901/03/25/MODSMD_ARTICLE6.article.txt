# Announcements.
## 
### 
Economics Seminary.—The Sem
inary will not meet again till April
9th. Mary Roberts Smith.
Geology Baseball Men.—All
Geology baseball players will meet
for practice tomorrow afternoon at
3 p.m. F. W. Rush, Manager.
Y. VV. C. A. Meeting.— Mary
Roberts Smith will address the
Y. W. C. A. Tuesday at 4:30 p.m. in
room 21 on "Preparation for Phil
anthropic Work."
Junior Arrangement Commit
tee. —There will be a meeting of
the Junior Arrangement Commit
tee tomorrow, Tuesday, at 1 p.m.
in room 10 quad. F. A. Lieb.
Y. M. C. A. Meeting.—The Tues
day afternoon meeting of the
Y. M. C. A. will be held in room
33 quad. Leader, A. C. Hull; sub
ject, "Missionary Work." Letters
from men at the front in China
will be read.
President Hadley's Address. —
President Arthur T. Had ley of Yale
University will visit Stanford to
morrow and will give a lecture at
1:45 in the Assembly Hall. Mem
bers of the faculty and their wives
are invited to meet. President Hud
ley in the Hall at the close of the
lecture.
Junior Farce. — Rehearsals for
Junior Farce will be held daily
from now until the performance,
at 3 o'clock in the Assembly Hall.
It is imperative that every member
of the cast be present. Persons
not taking part in the farce will
not be admitted.
