# News of Former Students.
## 
### 
—Bert Lougheed, ex'-00, is coach
ing the baseball team of the Wash
ington State Agricultural College
at Pullman.
—John D. Crummey, ex-'Ol, was
married today at Salem, Oregon,
to Miss Vivan Gellatt of that
place. Crummey took a promi
nent part in Y. M. C. A. affairs
while at the University. The
couple will make their home in
Sacramento, where Mr. Crummey
is in business.
