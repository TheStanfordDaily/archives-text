# Announcements.
## 
### 
Vesper Service. — Professor Mil
ler will lead the vesper service on
Thursday, March 28th. Music
will be furnished by Mr. Baker of
San Jose.
Marrack's Farce.—The cast for
Marrack's farce, "Saved by a
Knocker," will meet tomorrow af
ternoon in room 21 quad for a pre
liminary reading of the play.
Wm. Erb, Manager.
Band Meeting.—All members of
the band, and others who can play
band instruments, will meet in the
chapel Thursday at 1:15 sharp, to
consider playing at the San Jose
carnival. F. R. Schanok.
