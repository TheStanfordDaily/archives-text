# Stanford Student Appointed.
## 
### 
B. A. Olshausen, '01, has been
appointed field assistant in the
soils division of the United States
Department of Agriculture. He
will leave the University for the
State of Washington next Friday
in order to commence work on the
15th inst. in the vicinity of North
Yakima as per his instructions.
His duties will be collecting soils
in the fields and afterwards ana
lyzing them in the laboratory.
Mr. Olshausen is a Chemistry ma
jor. He registers from Los Ange
les, and this is his senior year in
the University.
