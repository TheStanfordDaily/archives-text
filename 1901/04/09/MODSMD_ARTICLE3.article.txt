# Teachers for Philippines.
## 
### 
Three Stanford men have been
offered positions by the Govern
ment as teachers in the Philippines.
They are: A. L. Walker, Edu
cation, '01; John A. Longley, His
tory, '95; and John W. Hancock,
A. M., Education, '92. Walker has
refused a $1500 position as teacher
and principal; Longley has been
offered a principalship and Han
cock a division superintendency.
Neither Longley nor Hancock have
yet signified their intentions of tak
ing up with the proposals.
