# Dr. Ross in Philadelphia.
## 
### 
Professor JO. A. Ross delivered
the principal address at the fifth
annual meeting of the American
Academy of Political and Social
Science held in Philadelphia Fri
day night. His subject was, " The
Causes of Race Superiority," the
general topic being "America's
Race Problems."
The mooted question as to the
probable action of the regents of
the University of Nebraska regard
ing Dr. Ross's standing in that in
stitution was settled recently,
when the board decided that he be
permanently retained as professor
of sociology.
