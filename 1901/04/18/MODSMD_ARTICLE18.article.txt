# Debaters at Kings Mountain.
## 
### 
This morning the Intercollegiate
debaters, Messrs. Morris, Marrack,
and Morrow, went to Kings Moun
tain, where they will rest until to
morrow afternoon, returning in
time for the rally and farce in the
evening. They completed the pre
liminary work in their debates last
week and have devoted the past
few days to practice. Monday they
held a practice with the members
of the Freshman team.
