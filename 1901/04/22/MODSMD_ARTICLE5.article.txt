# The 						California Quartet.
## 
### 
The California Quartet will give
a concert in Nortree Hall on Wed
nesday evening, April '24th, for the
benefit of the Palo Alto Fire De
partment. The program they will
present contains many excellent
numbers, including quartets, solos,
readings, and monologues. The
quartet will be assisted by local
talent. Admission will be fifty
cents, this admitting also to the
dance which will follow. The pro
ceeds of the entertainment will be
devoted to meeting the necessary
expenses of the department.
