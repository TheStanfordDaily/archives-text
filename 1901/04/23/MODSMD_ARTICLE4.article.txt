# Stanford Man Honored
## 
### 
F. H. Williams, '97, more com
monly known as "Heff" Williams,
has received second honors al the
Postgraduate Hospital of New
York, one of the foremost medical
institutions in America. While at
Stanford Williams took an active
part in football, being according to
experts the best center the Cardinal
has ever had. While at Columbia
he was prominent in athletics and
was a, member of the exclusive
Omega Club.
