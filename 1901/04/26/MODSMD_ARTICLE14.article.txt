# University Chapel.
## 
### 
The sermon on Sunday will be
delivered by the Rev. E. L. Parsons,
rector of the Episcopal Church,
San Mated. Mr. Parsons is also in
structor of Philosophy, giving two
courses and coming down on Tues
days and Thursdays. Mr. Parsons
is a graduate of Yale, and has
preached a number of times in our
chapel.
