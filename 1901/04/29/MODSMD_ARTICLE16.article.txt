# University Mails.
## 
### 
The mails of the University post
office arrive and depart as follows:
MAILS DEPART.
7:45 a.m. —South, daily.
9:15 a.m. —North, week days.
3:30 p.m. —North and south, week
days.
7:00 p.m. —North, Sundays.
MAILS ARRIVE.
8:45 a.m. —Daily.
4:20 p.m. — "
10:05 a.m. —Week days.
POSTOFFICE HOURS.
Week days, from 8 a.m. to 6:30
p.m.; Sundays, 9 a.m. to 7:00 p.m.
The general delivery window is
open on Sundays from 9 a.m. to
10 a.m.
