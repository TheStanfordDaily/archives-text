# Announcements.
## 
### 
Senior Reception Committee.—
Important meeting Wednesday in
room 21 at 1 p.m.
F. W. Bennett, Chairman.
Senior Plate Committee.—There
will be an important meeting of
the Senior Plate Committee Wed
nesday at 4:30 p.m. in room 11
quad.
Class Canes. —All orders for
1904 class canes must he left at
room 22 Encina, with the deposit
of one dollar, before Wednesday,
May Ist.
Economics Seminary.—The Eco
nomics Seminary will meet at 2:30,
immediately after Professor Mil
ler's lecture on Trusts, on Tuesday
afternoon. M. R. Smith.
Music Club. —There will be a
meeting of the Music Club at the
house of Mrs. W. A. Kimball, 959
Waverly street, Palo Alto, on Tues
day evening, April 30th, at 7:30.
English Club. — The English
Club will meet at the Kappa Alpha
Theta house, Tuesday evening,
April 30th. Instructors Seward
and Bartlett will speak on the poet
Woodbury.
Y. M. C. A. Meeting.—H. A.
Moral), '04, will lead the meeting
of the Y. M. C. A. Tuesday after
noon at 4:30 in room 33 quad.
Subject, "A message from the new
president."
Program Committee.—The Sen
ior Program Committee will meet
Wednesday at 1:15 p.m. in room
11 quad to transact some import
ant business. N. G. Symonds.
Band Meeting.—The band will
meet in room 33 Tuesday evening
at 7:30. All members must be
present for rehearsal in the future
if the band is to be put in proper
shape. 1). M. Horn.
Athletic Committee. — There
will be an important meeting of the
Athletic Committee on Wednesday
evening at 7:45 immediately after
the Executive Committee meeting,
room 11 quad. Every member
should be present.
