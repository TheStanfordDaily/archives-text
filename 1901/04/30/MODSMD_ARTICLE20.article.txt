# Death of Woodin, ex-'02.
## 
### 
News has been received at the
University of the death of Irving
Oakley Woodin, ex-'O2, which oc
curred at Los Angeles on April 9th.
Since leaving the University about
two years ago he has been engaged
in the mining business with A. M.
Strong, '99 Woodin was twenty
four years old at the time of his
death.
