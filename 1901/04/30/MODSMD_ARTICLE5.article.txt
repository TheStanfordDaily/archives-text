# Orchestra to Give Concert.
## 
### 
At a recent meeting of the
University Orchestra, C. M.
Stowe, '04, was elected secretary
treasurer and F. Foster, '01, and
H. F. Scoville, '04, were elected
members of the executive commit
tee. Arrangements were made for
a concert to take place on Thurs
day evening, May 9th. Mrs.
Schneider of San Jose will be the
soloist.
