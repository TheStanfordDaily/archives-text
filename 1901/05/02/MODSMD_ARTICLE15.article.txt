# Sale of 1902 "Quad."
## 
### 
Next Wednesday the Junior An
nual is to be placed on sale on the
quadrangle. No subscriptions will
be received previous to that time,
so that all who wish copies must
procure them that morning. The
printing of the book is now com
pleted, and the binding will be fin
ished by Monday, so that the whole
edition will be ready by Wednes
day morning.
