# Announcements.
## 
### 
Band Meeting.--The University
Hand will meet Tuesday at 7:30
p.m. in room 83.
Senior Farce Rehearsal. —
There will he a rehearsal Tuesday
at 3:30 p.m. in the Assembly Hall.
Chorus and principals must all be
present.
Mandolin Club. —There will be
a meeting of the Mandolin Club to
morrow at 7:30 p.m. in room 31 for
the purpose of electing officers for
next year.
Economics 12.—A two-hour ex
amination in Economics 12, Gov
ernment, will be held in room 2
Tuesday morning at 8:15.
Hurt Estes Howard.
Y. M. C. A.—General Secretary
L. E. Harter will lead the Tuesday
afternoon meeting of the Y. M. C. A.
May 7, 1901, in room 33 quad at
4:30 p.m. Subject, "Future Work."
Senior Baseball Team. — All
candidates for the Senior baseball
team will meet for practice on the
diamond tomorrow afternoon at
four o'clock.
J. A. Bentley, Captain.
Baseball Election.—There will
be a meeting of all baseball men
who have played in scheduled
games this season in room 10 quad
Wednesday at 2 p.m. The pur
pose is the election of a captain for
the coming year.
Harry J. Edwards, Captain.
English 1 Entrance Examina
tion. —There will be an examina
tion in English 1 on Friday, May
10th, at 2:30 p.m. in room 62 quad,
for matriculated students only.
Such students will not, except by
special permission, lie admitted to
the regular examination on Satur
day, May 25th. E. B. Pearson.
