# Piano Recital.
## 
### 
Mr. Arthur Fickensher, the San
Francisco pianist, so well known
here, will give a piano recital next
Friday evening at 8 o'clock in Nor
tree Hall, Palo Atto. He will be
assisted by Mr. Samuel Savannah,
violinst, and Miss Maud Fay, mez
zo-soprano, who will sing "Am
Abend," the words of which are
written by Professor Julius Goebel.
There will be an informal dance at
the close of the program.
