# Suit Against Student Body.
## 
### 
Justice Van Buren of the May
field Court yesterday rendered de
cision against Wardall and award
ed judgment and costs to the stu
dent body in the suit brought
against the Associated Students by
C. M. Wardall, former track mana
ger, for alleged work done on the
track during the Christmas vaca
tion. The suit was brought for $59.
E. S. Page, '00, was the attorney
for the student body and J. M.
Stowe,'oo, and Louis Ferrari, '01,
represented Wardall.
