# Announcements.
## 
### 
Euphronia. — Business meeting
Saturday, 7:30 p.m.
Sunday Sermon. —Prof. B. E.
Howard will deliver the sermon in
the chapel on Sunday, May 19th.
Subject, "A Sane Attitude."
Mandolin Club.—Practice will
be held in the Assembly Hall at
11 a.m. Saturday, May 18th. Elec
tion of officers will take place on
Tuesday at 7:30 p.m. in room 31.
Student Body Meeting.—There
will be a meeting of the student
body in the chapel next Tuesday
afternoon at 4:30.
Cecil M. Marrack, President.
Senior Ball Committee.—There
will be a meeting of the Senior
Ball Committee Monday, May 20th,
at 1 p.m. in room 10 quad.
P. K. Gilman, Chairman.
Senior Class Roll. —Ail persons
who received their degrees in Jan
uary, as well as those who gradu
ate this month, are requested to
sign the class roll in the Regis
trar's office on or before Wednes
day next.
