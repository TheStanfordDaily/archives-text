# Freshman Reception.
## 
### 
Tomorrow evening at Encina
Hall the Associations will give their
annual reception to new students
at 8 o'clock. Dr. Jordan will speak
a few words of welcome. Professor
Murray will also speak. Mr. J. K.
Bonnell will give some wise advice
to the freshmen, Miss Lora Belle
Tevis of Denver will render a cor
net solo, and the Glee Club will
sing. Every effort is being made to
make this a pleasant occasion when
the new students will have an op
portunity to meet the old students.
Every one is cordially invited.
