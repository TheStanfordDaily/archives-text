—Some excitement was created
in front of tlie post office and at the
southeast corner of the quad this
morning by a runaway team
hitched to Tehhs & Son's surrey
dashing up Lasuen street from in
front of the Theta house. No one
was in the rig at the time, and
the horses ran head-on into
a stone pillar at the corner of
the arcade. The horses suffered a
few bruises, the neck yoke and one
wheel were broken, and a water
melon was smashed.
