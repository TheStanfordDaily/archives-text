# Telegram of Sympathy.
## 
### 
The following telegram was sent
to Mrs. McKinley, Saturday:
Stanford University, Sept. 7.
Mrs. Wm. McKinley, Washing
ton, D. C.: —The Faculty of Stan
ford University extend sincerest
sympathy and most earnest hopes
for the President's recovery.
(Signed) David S. Jordan.
