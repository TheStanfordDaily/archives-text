# INN WILL BE RENTED.
## Suitable Concessions Have Been Made to Applicants. 
### 
Announcement was made today
that the Stanford Inn will be
rented and opened for business
very soon. Nothing more definite
is known than that suitable con
cessions have been made to appli
cants.
Dr. Jordan and Mr. Adderson
have been interviewed, and it is
understood that the Inn will be
opened as soon as the business
ollice in San Francisco has selected
one from among the many who
have conditionally applied for the
management.
