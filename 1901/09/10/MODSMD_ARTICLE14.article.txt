# McCoy Speaks Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Mr. Henry J. McCoy, General
Secretary of the San Francisco
Young Men's Christian Association,
will give a short address to the
men of the University in the chapel
at 4:30 p.m. Wednesday. This is
the first midweek devotional meet
ing of the Y. M. C. A., which de
sires that as many of the new men
as possible hegin early in the year
to invest forty-five minutes a week
in this valuable exercise.
