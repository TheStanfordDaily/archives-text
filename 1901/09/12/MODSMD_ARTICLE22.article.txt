# Large Zoology Class.
## 
### 
The entering class in Zoology is
so large this year that it has had
to be divided into two sections.
Division 1 will meet for laboratory
work at the scheduled hour, and
Division 2 will meet on Monday
and Tuesday afternoons. Professor
Heath has charge of the work.
