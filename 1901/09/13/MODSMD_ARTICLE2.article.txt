# MEMORIAL SERVICE.
## Addresses and Music in Honor of Our Late President Monday Evening. 
### 
Memorial services in honor of
our late President McKinley will
take place in the Assembly Hall
Monday evening. Dr. Jordan, Dr.
Halsey, and others will speak.
Rev. Gardner of Palo Alto is ar
ranging for a chorus to take part in
the program.
