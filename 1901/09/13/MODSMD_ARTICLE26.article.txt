# Marriage of C.C. Perkins.
## 
### 
C. (J. Perkins, a former Stanford
man, member of the Zeta Psi fra
ternity, was married this summer
at his home in Hueneme, Cal., to
Miss Lucy Kelley of Clearland, 0.,
and they are now residing in San
Francisco. At the wedding Chas.
K. Field, '95, officiated as best
man, and Miss Maryland Main,
'96, of Santa Barbara, bridesmaid.
At the wedding breakfast prior to
the ceremony the engagement of
Mr. Chester Thomas, ex-'9B, and
Miss Main was announced.
