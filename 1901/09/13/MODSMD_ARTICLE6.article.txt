# Sophomores Surprised.
## 
### 
Some Freshmen of 1905 sprung
a surprise the other evening on the
Sophomores. A few bold spirits
among the Freshmen secured a lot
of paint and a brush, and pro
ceeded to decorate the fence of the
athletic grounds with large figures
of their class. When the Sopho
mores arrived on the scene not a
Freshman wast) be found, but the
evidence of their work was still
