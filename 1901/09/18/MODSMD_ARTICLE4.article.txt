# Senior Class Meeting.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Senior
Class, held this afternoon, the sec
retary was instructed to cast
ballots in favor of the nominees
for the class offices.
President Wigie was called on
for a speech, and said: "I hope
that this year will, in all respects,
be a successful one. I thank you
for the compliment."
The committee on sombreros was
called on to report, and a girls'
sombrero committee was appointed
as follows: Miss Toll, chairman;
Miss Gill, Miss Fay.
