# Dr. Dean of Columbia.
## 
### 
Dr. Bashford Dean of Columbia
University is visiting Stanford on,
his way home from a year's travel
in Japan and the Philippine Is
lands. Dr. Dean will lecture in
the chapel on Thursday evening at
7:30 p.m.. His subject will be,
"A Naturalist in Japan and the
Philippines." From here he will
go to Pacific Grove to complete his
collection before returning to New
York. This morning Dr. Dean
spoke before the class in Involution.
