# Girls' Glee Club Try-Out.
## 
###

Club held last evening sixteen
were present, and the following
members were selected: Ist so
prano, Miss Lessey, Miss Lannon,
Miss Atherton, Miss Bird; 2d so
prano, Miss ltichie; Ist alto, Miss
Bruekman, Miss Stroud, Miss
Sehanck, Miss Bartruff; 2d alto.
