# Arboretum Improvements
## 
### 
During the last two weeks exten
sive changes have been made in
the grounds surrounding the statue
of the"The Angel of Grief." The
old fence has been removed, a new
iron one taking its place, and the
enclosure sodded. A walk and
drive have been constructed lead
ing to the statue. Fringing the
edge of the walk, a line of shrub
bery and a number of flowers have
been planted The alterations
were designed by Mr. Hodges, the
horticultural effects by Mr. Lynch
of the Menlo Park nursery.
