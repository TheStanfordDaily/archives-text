# Alumni Notes.
## 
### 
Herbert, C. Thompson, '9(>, who
was in the Philippines as a private
in the Second Oregon Infantry, is
assistant librarian of the Mechan
ics' Institute Library in San Fran
cisco, of which Frederick Teggart,
'94, one time assistant librarian of
the University Library, is the chief.
Frank T. Wilkinson, '95, known
in college as " U. G." because he
came from Berkeley, was married
in Oakland, September 29th, his
bride being Miss Florence Mc-
Donald of East Oakland, a grad
uate of the University of California
with the class of '99. Wilkinson
was in the Philippines with the
Volunteer Engineer Corps, and
since his return has been connected
with the maintenance of way de
partment of the Southern Pacific.
