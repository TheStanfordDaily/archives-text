# 1904 Election.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Class of
1904 in the chapel this afternoon,
the following officers were elected
for the ensuing semester: Presi
dent, M. A. Thomas; vice-presi
dent, Miss M. 0. Foster; secretary,
Miss M. Parkhurst; treasurer, 11.
M. Lewis; athletic manager, R. Mc-
Fadden. Mr. Blodget read the
treasurer's report and it was ap
proved.
