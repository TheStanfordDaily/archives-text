# Nourse Moved to Palo Alto.
## 
### 
Bayard Nourse was moved today
from the training-house to the
sanitarium in Palo Alto. He was
carried on a cot by eight men.
Though the jar of the moving made
him a little seasick, he soon recov
ered when placed in bed at the
sanitarium; and was resting as
elisilv as ever.
