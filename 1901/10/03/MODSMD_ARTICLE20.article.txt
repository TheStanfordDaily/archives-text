# Married in San Francsico.
## 
### 
Miss Frances Bruce, sister of Miss
C. B. Bruce, '03, was married this
afternoon to Lieutenant Joseph
McMullen. The ceremony took
place in St. Luke's Episcopal
Church, San Francisco, and a num
ber of Stanford people attended.
Miss V. (J. Piercy and Miss (J. E.
Bruce were bridesmaids, A. 1).
Geissler, '02, best man, and T. (J.
Hosmer, '04, was usher.
