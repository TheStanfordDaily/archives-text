# Glaciers of Alaska.
## 
### 
Dr. C. Hart Merriam, chief of
the Bureau of Animal Industries
at Washington, D. C., gave a talk
this afternoon on the glaciers of
Alaska. A large collection of pic
tures taken on the Harriman ex
pedition were shown and explained
hy Dr. Merriam. The slides were
from pictures of glaciers, forests,
birds, and various interesting Alas
kan scenes. Owing to the poor
light in the chapel the meeting ad
journed to the Chemistry lecture
room.
