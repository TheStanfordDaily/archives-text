# Tie Game.
## 
### 
The Palo Alto team, which Stan
ford's Freshmen defeated Septem
ber 27th by a score of 6 to 0,
yesterday played a tie game at
Berkeley with the U. C. freshmen,
each side making six points. Kim
ball of the visiting team made a
twenty-yard run to touchdown,
Stewart bucked twenty yards
through the freshman center, and
Jordan made two runs of forty-five
and thirty-five yards respectively;
while it is said that "Debs"
(Buelna) put up a star game.
