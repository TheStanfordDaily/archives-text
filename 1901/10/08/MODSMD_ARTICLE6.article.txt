# College Notes.
## 
### 
—At the University of Michigan
students have instituted the wear
ing of a fireman's shirt, with large
pearl buttons, which is supposed to
take the place of the omnipresent
sweater.
—The University of Nevada
plays the following games during
the season: October 19th, Reliance
at Reno; October 30th, University
of California at Berkeley; Novem
ber 2d, Stanford at I'alo Alto;
November 4th, University of Ore
gon in San Francisco; November
28th, University'of Utah in Salt
Lake City.
