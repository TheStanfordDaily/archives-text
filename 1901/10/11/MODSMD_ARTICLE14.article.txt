# Gymnasium Notes.
## 
### 
A wrestling tournament is being
started, and all students who
have paid their regular fee of $1
are eligible.
No pictures are to be taken of the
students this year, except, perhaps,
in special cases of good physique,
or when subject stands the expense.
The University plumber have
taken measurements and there
will probably be five new show
ers. As yet connections have not
been made with the new water
supply, and there is no hot water.
