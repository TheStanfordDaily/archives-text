# Inter-society Debating League.
## 
### 
President Rice of the Inter
society Debating League announced
today that he would call a meeting
of the organization next Monday
to discuss the subject of schedule
for preliminary try-outs of debates
to be held this year. The hour of
meeting will be posted on the quad
Monday. This is important and
should command the attendance of
every member of debating societies
in the University.
