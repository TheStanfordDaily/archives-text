# Glee Club Concert.
## 
### 
The University Glee Club will
give its first concert next Friday
night, October 18th, in the Assem
bly Hall. In the afternoon of the
same day the Varsity will play the
Alumni on the campus. On the
following day, October 19th, the
Freshmen will meet Berkeley. All
three teams will be present at the
concert, and the whole student
body should be there to give the
Freshmen a proper send-off.
