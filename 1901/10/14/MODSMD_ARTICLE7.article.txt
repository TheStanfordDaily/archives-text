# Freshman Class Meeting.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Freshman Class
was called this afternoon in the
chapel at 1:15, and the following
nominations were made: President,
Van Sickle; vice-president, C. L.
Lyman; secretary, William Tay
lor; treasurer, R. C. Nissen.
A motion was made that the
class should establish a constitu
tion. On this motion being lost
one was passed that the organiza
tion should lie by precedent. A
committee of five men was ap
pointed to draft some yells.
