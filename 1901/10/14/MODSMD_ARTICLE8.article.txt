# Tickets For Sale.
## 
### 
Thursday morning tickets to the
Freshman game Saturday in San
Francisco and to the Glee Club
concert Friday night in the As
sembly Hall will be placed on sale
on the quad. After Thursday
tickets will be on sale at the Uni
versity Drug Store and at the
Palace Pharmacy in Palo Alto.
Prices—football, seventy-five cents;
concert, twenty-five and thirty-five
cents.
