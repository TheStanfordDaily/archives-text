# EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE.
## Football Rally Thursday Evening, at Which the Band Will Play. 
### 
At a special meeting this after
noon the Executive Committee of
the Associated Students made a
call for a football rally Thursday
evening. The band will be out,
bonfires will be lighted, and the en
tire student body is expected to he
present. The crowd is to congre
gate first at Encina.
The committee also voted $50
to the hand for expenses, part of
which will be used in taking some
extra men to the Freshman game
Saturday. Plans for an im
promptu welcome for Mrs. Stan
ford, when she arrives in a few
days, were also discussed. This
may take the form of a public
greeting at the station, if it can be
learned defini tt4y beforehand when
she will come.
