# Prize Christmas Story.
## 
### 
The San Francisco Post offers a
prize of $50 for the best Christmas
story of Stanford or Berkeley col
lege life or of both. The story
must be written by a regularly ac
credited student of Stanford or
Berkeley, man or woman; it must
not contain more than 3,000 words
and must be delivered to the office
of the Post on or before December
1, 1901. offers an excellent
chance for our Stanford writers to
demonstrate their abilities and if
possible to defeat Berkeley.
