# U.C. Notes.
## 
### 
—At a meeting of the junior
class at Berkeley last week plans
were made for a scholarship fund
of $2000, which will be used as a
loan fund. Junior day has been
selected, and will be on Nov. 28th.
—The women students of the
University of California are pre
paring to publish an annual of
their own, devoted entirely to the
feminine side of college life. It
will be issued February 22d, which
has been adopted by the co-eds as
" Woman's Day."
