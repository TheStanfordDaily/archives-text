# Quads.
## 
### 
—Mrs. French has been visiting
her daughter at Roble.
—Miss Carrie Sappington, '05, is
recovering from a severe attack of
illness.
—The Rev. R. M. Church, from
Santa Paula, is visiting R. R.
Knight, '03.
— Bayard Nourse, who was in
jured on the football field some
weeks ago, has gone to San Francisco
for a tew days' rest before resinning
his University work.
