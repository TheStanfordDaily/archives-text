# ROYAL ITALIAN BAND.
## Program Which Will Be Given at the Concert Wednesday Evening. 
### 
The Royal Italian Hand will
render the following program in
the Assembly Hall on the evening
of Wednesday, October 23d, begin
ning at 8 o'clock:
PART I.
March, "Royal Purple", . . Creator#
Overture, "Tannhiiuser".. . Wagner
Harp Solo Selected
Miss Heintzen.
Adagio Cantibile, "Sonata Pate
tica" Beethoven
(For reed instruments only.)
"laicia," Sextet Donizetti
Signori Palma, Aiala, Marino,
Curti, Liberatore and Basile.
PART 11.
Selection from "Walkiire"... Wagner
Prelude, Rule of the Valky
ries, Love Song, Tragic Fire.
Soprano Solo Selected
Mine. Barili.
Intermezzo, "Salome" Loraine
"Carmen," Grand Selection. .Bizet
Prelude, Habanera, Toreador,
Hoys' Chorus. Bohemian Song,
Introduction and Finale, Act
IV.
Signori Palma, Morino, Curti and
Ferullo.
