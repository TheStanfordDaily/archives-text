# "OFFICIAL" AT LAST.
## Plug Ugly Posters Reveal Some of the Mysteries to be Shown Next Friday. 
### 
At last, something "official" has
appeared. Big, yellow posters,
with heavy hlack letters hearing
the headline "Junior Plug Ugly,"
were everywhere in evidence this
morning. They revealed such
choice descriptive phrases and ad
monition as "Snatched from the
Jaws of Hell," "69 Minutes in the
Infernal Regions," "Although the
bus drivers have combined it is no
reason why you should not go over
to the Ovai next Friday evening,"
"See the incarnation of Cerberus,"
"Now go away back and sit down
and pay one dime to see the laying
of the Junior tile," "Come, for we
want your money," "Come and
cultivate your presence of mind,
your personal esteem and dignity
by seeing the canonization of the
plug," "Come and be convulsed
and relieved of ten cents and get a
souvenir picture of Wertheimer in
his golfies."
