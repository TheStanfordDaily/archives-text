# Announcements.
## 
### 
At Home. —Mr. and Mrs. Sned
den are at home to students on
Saturday evening.
Kappa Alpha Theta At Home
Postponed.—Kappa Alpha Theta
will he at home Saturday evening,
November 2d, instead of Saturday,
October 26th.
Glee Club Meeting.— There
will he a Glee Club rehearsal Fri
day, October 25th, at 4:30 p.m. in
the chapel. C. E. Oilman,
Leader.
Bookstore. —The following books
have been received at the Book
store: Lamb's Old China and
other Essays of Elia; Dippond's
Scientific German Reader; and
Stevenson's New Arabian Nights.
