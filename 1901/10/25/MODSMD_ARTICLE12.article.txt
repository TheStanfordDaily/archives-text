Tomorrow afternoon the last
game with Reliance this season
will he called on the oval promptly
at 4 o'clock. The hour is late
enough not to break into the after
noon, and all students who are
cramming for the mid-semester
exes cannot find more profitable
recreation for themselves, to say
nothing of their duly to the team
and to the University, than by at
tending the game.
