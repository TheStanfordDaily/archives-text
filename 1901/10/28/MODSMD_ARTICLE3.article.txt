# "Al" Lean as Rubber.
## 
### 
l 'Al" Lean arrived yesterday and
will have charge of rubbing the
men of the football squad for the
remainder of the season. Lean
rubbed the '96 team and has had
charge of the California team for
the last four years. He holds the
Pacific Coast all-round professional
championship in track athletics and
is a professional wrestler.
