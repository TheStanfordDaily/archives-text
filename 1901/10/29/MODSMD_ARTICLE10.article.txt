# Alumni Dinner.
## 
### 
A dinner will be given by the
alumni of Stanford University at
Harvard, Columbia, and Johns
Hopkins, the night of the intercol
legiate game between Stanford and
California, at the Arena in 'New
York City. Many old Varsity
players will attend, among them
being Guy A. Cochran, captain of
the '95 team; Jackson E. Reynolds,
'96, half-back for two years; Ches
ter G. Murphy, '00; Hilly Erb,
'01; Nat Carl,'9B, guard and one
of the famous giant center trio of
the Stanford teams of '90 and '97.
Nearly forty alumni will be present
to hear the results of the various
games, that are t> be bulletined
during the dinner.
