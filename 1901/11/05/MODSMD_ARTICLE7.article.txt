# Sophomore Hats.
## 
### 
At a meeting this afternoon of
the Sophomore Hat Committee it
yvms decided to adopt a white duck
hat with a narrow hrim and red
hat band. The members of the
committee,consisting of F. A. Ford,
H. M. Lewis and Miss Reynolds,
will take the names of all those
wishing hats. The price of the hat
will probably be about fifty cents.
