# Line-up For California.
## 
### 
The line-up of the California
Varsity was definitely determined
at the final practice yesterday, as
follows: More, quarter; Duden,
full; Hudson, Dibblee, and Starr,
ends; Womble and Mini, halfs;
Hansen and Albertson, tackles;
Stow and Overall, guards ; Uen
dotti, center.
When on the defensive, Womble
and Hudson will probably ex
change places in the line-up.
