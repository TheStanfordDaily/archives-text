# Week of Prayer for Young Men.
## 
### 
In recognition of the fact that
Christian people all over the world
are uniting this week in prayer for
young men and special services are
being held in all countries for this
purpose, the Christian Association
will hold services in thechapel, with
subjects as follows: Monday,
"Christ as King," John 18:37;
Tuesday, ''Christ our King,"
Judges 8:22; Wednesday, J "The
Royal Anointing," Psalms 92:10;
Thursday, "The King's Highway,"
John 14:15; Friday, "The King's
Service," II Samuel 15:15. These
services will take the place of the
regular mid-week services.
