# Bulletin by Dr. Jenkins.
## 
### 
The United .States Commission
of Fish and Fisheries has issued
the third bulletin of the series on
Hawaiian Fishes. It is entitled
"Descriptions of Fifteen New
Species of Fishes from the Ha
waiian Islands," and is written by
Oliver P. Jenkins, head of the
Physiology department. One
species, Sphyraena Snodgrasxi, is
named after 11. E. Snodgrass, '00,
instructor in Zoology.
