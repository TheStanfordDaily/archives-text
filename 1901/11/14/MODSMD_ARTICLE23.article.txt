# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Executive
Committee last night it was voted
to allow Bayard Nourse $90.00 for
doctor's bill during the sickness
that resulted from his injury on
the football field. Treasurer Ed
wards was authorized to make a
contract with C. Swindells, '02, for
the coaching of the Varsity base
ball team. It was voted that the
student body furnish the Freshman
team with, sweaters. Treasurer Ed
wards was instructed to confer with
L. M. Weir, an instructor in vocal
music in I'alo Alto, regarding an
opera to be put on sometime in
February. The Band's bill of ex
penses was accepted.
