# Roble Dance.
## 
### 
The dunce in the liohle gymnas
ium last Saturday night was the
most successful ever given by the
young ladies of liable.
The place was beautifully deco
rated in Christmas berries and
palms, lied candles added to the
effect. Yank's orchestra was down
from the city and gave excellent
music. Punch was served on the
porch.
The patronesses who assisted
Miss Ray were Mrs. Wilbur, Mrs.
Angell, Mrs. Branner, and Miss
Perry.
