# Frank Rodolph on the Stage.
## 
### 
Frank E. Rodolph, ex-'O2, who
made a hit on the Floredora six
tette as presented by the Doctors'
Daughters, will go East with the
intention of going on the vaude
ville stage. While in college he
made the '98 Varsity football eleven
as full back, and has played with
the Reliance Club team.
