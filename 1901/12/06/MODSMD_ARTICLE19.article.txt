# Fishes of Japan.
## 
### 
"A Review of the Gymnodent
Fishes of Japan," by President Jor
dan and Zoology Instructor John
O. Snyder, has just been issued by
the Smithsonian Institute from the
proceedings of the United States
National Museum, volume XXIV.
This paper is based on collections
made by the writers in Japan in
1900, under the auspices of the
Hopkins Laboratory, and of the
United States National Museum.
The drawings are contributed by
Lydia M. Hart and Mr. A. H.
Baldwin. Several of the descrip
tions are of lishes hitherto unknown
to science.
