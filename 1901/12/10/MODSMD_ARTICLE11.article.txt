# The
						New Gymnasium at Princeton.
## 
### 
There is to be a new gymnasium
at Princton which will cost in the
region of $200,00. The following
fron the Vale News may he of inter
est to Stanford students:
On the ground floor there will he
a large trophy room, decorated in
English oak, and inscribed, each
on a separate panel, will he the
name of every man who has repre
sented Princeton in athletics. The
main floor will open off from the
trophy room, and will be equipped
with all the latest apparatus. In
the basement there will be 2,000
lockers, fifteen shower baths, and
outside a large tank surrounded by
a gallery for spectators.
