# Student Transportation.
## 
### 
C. F. Battelle, who lias been tak
ing the names of all those students
and professors who expect to go
North or South during the holidays
in order to place them in whatever
steamer they wish to take, will he
at the Daily I'alo Alto office to
morrow morning from 9:30 to 11 to
complete his list of those students
expecting to leave, but who have
not yet secured berths. No de
posits are necessary. It is merely
an accommodation to students that
reservations are made at this time.
