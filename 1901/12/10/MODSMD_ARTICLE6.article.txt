# Excursion to Mexico.
## 
### 
A specially conducted Christmas
excursion to the City of Mexico
will leave San Francisco Wednes
day, December 18th, and Los An
geles the following day, via the
Southern Pacific and Mexican
Central Railroad. The excursion
will reach the City of Mexico on
December 23d at 9 a.m. The rate
will be $80.00 from San Francisco,
$79.70 from FresUo, and $70.00
from Los Angeles.
