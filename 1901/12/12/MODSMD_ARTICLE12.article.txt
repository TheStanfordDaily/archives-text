# Greek Play.
## 
### 
The Antigone of Sophocles is to he
presented at the University in April
next. The music will he under the
direction of Mr. Scott-Brook, Uni
versity organist. Students who de
sire to take part in the chorus are
requested to hand in their names
before the end of this semester to
Prof. H. R. Fairclough. Credit not
exceeding three hours for work done
to the satisfaction of the director
and Greek department will he given.
A. T. Murray.
