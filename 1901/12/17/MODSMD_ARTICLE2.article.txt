# Athletic Committee Meets.
## 
### 
The Athletic Committee met this
afternoon, with all members pres
ent except Scott, '02, and Cantwell,
'01. The only business of immedi
ate importance transacted was the
adoption of a regulation providing
for the election of an athletic team
captain during the semester in
which they contest with California.
The rule makes it necessary that
the football election for next year
be held between now and Friday,
when the semester closes. Captain
Fisher has announced t hat the elec
tion will take place tomorrow at
Franklin's in Palo Alto, where the
team picture is to be taken.
