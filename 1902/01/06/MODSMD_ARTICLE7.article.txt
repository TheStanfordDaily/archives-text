# Announcements.
## 
### 
Encina Gymnasium. — Registra
tion for work in the Encina Gym
nasium must take place on Wed
nesday, January Bth, from 10-12
a.m. and from 3-5 p.m.
No more Organ Receptions.—
Mr. Brook very much regrets to
announce that, owing to the church
being closed by the contractors to
all visitors, there will be no more
organ receptions for the present.
