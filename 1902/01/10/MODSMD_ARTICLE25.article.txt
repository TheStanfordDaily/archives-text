# W.E.A. Committee.
## 
### 
In printing the circulars of the
A. 0. A. and W. E. A. last night
offering foreign fellowship to
women graduates, omission was
made of the W. E. A. Committee
which is composed of Mrs. M. P.
Hallowell, chairman, West Med
ford, Mass.; Mrs. Louis Agassiz,
Cambridge; Mrs. George H. Pal
mer, Cambridge; Miss Agnes Ir
win, Radcliffe College; Miss S. A.
Brown, 06 Marlborough street, Bos
ton; Miss Mary Coes, Radcliffe
College; Miss S. C. Hart, Wellesley
College. Applications for the
W. E. A. fellowship may be mailed
to any of the above.
