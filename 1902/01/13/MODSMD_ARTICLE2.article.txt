# RETURN CONCERT.
## Musical Clubs to Appear in Assembly Hall Friday Evening. Popular Prices. 
### 
After a successful trip, during
which they received much praise
and enthusiastic support, the Glee
and Mandolin Clubs will give their
annual return concert in the As
sembly Hall on Friday, January
17th. In view of the fact that the
Clubs have, for the past three
weeks, been giving concerts of a
high standard throughout the
South, they will, without doubt,
give an excellent performance.
The program is to be announced
later. Tickets will be on sale on
the quad Thursday, January 16th,
and later at the Bookstore on the
campus, and Palace Pharmacy in
Palo Alto. Reserved seats will be
50 cents, general admission 35 cents
