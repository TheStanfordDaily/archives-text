# Shakspere Recital.
## 
### 
Mr. Lee Emerson Bassett, of the
English department, is to give a
recital in the chapel on Tuesday
evening, January 21st, at 7:30,
from The Merchant of Venice.
The scenes chosen are those which
carry out the plot, and are as fol
lows: Act I, scenes 1 and 3; act 111,
scene 2; act IV, scene 1. The re
cital will he about one hour in
length.
