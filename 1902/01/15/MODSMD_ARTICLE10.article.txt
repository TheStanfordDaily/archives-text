# Preliminary Tennis Try-outs.
## 
### 
One week from next Saturday
the first of a series of preliminary
tennis try-outs will be held on the
Encina courts. These are intended
to give the tennis players practice
throughout the season, just sis the
earlier baseball and track meets
are provided for men in those
branches of athletics. The new
try-outs promise to arouse more in
terest in the competition this
spring.
