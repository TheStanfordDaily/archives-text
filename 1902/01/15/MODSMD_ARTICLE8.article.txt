# Snappy Work in Diamond.
## 
### 
Baseball practice was fast yes
terday, twenty-three men appearing
on the field and working with a
satisfying vim. After the regular
practice the Varsity played a game
of several innings with the Second
team,with McFarland and Studley
pitching. The following men were
out: McFarland, McGilvray, Low
enthal, Cowden, Brown, Crawford,
Tritch, Copp, Knight, Grider,
Studley, Blake, Young, Ballagh,
Parker, Luce, Lockwood, McKen
non, Butterfield, Scales, P. Tarpey,
Christy and Doherty.
