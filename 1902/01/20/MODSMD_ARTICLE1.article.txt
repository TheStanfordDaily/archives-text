# ECONOMICS APPOINTMENT.
## Dr. J.S. McLean, Now at University of Arkansas, Will Be Associate Professor. 
### 
Professor J. S. McLean, lately
head of the department of eco
nomics and sociology in the Uni
versity of Arkansas, has been ap
pointed an associate professor of
Economics.
Professor McLean was a student
for several years at the University
of Toronto, taking the degrees of
M. A. and LL. B. at that institution.
After leaving Toronto he devoted
part of his time to graduate work
at Chicago University, where be
was granted his Doctor's degree.
He has held his chair at, Arkansas
for the past four years, and last
summer was appointed by the Ca
nadian government on its Railway
Commission and spent consider
able time in gathering statistics for
the governmental reports.
