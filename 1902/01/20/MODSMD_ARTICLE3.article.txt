# Sophomore Election Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The election for officers of the
Sophomore class will be held to
morrow on the quad near the bul
letin-board, from 11:15 a.m. to 2:00
p tn. The only contests are: For
president, Carl Kennedy, W. D.
Patterson, John Taylor, and Wal
ter Crossman; for treasurer, Isaac
Russell, M. U. Farrar.
President Thomas has appointed
the following officers to have charge
of the election: Hans Barkan, E. A.
Luce, and F. S. Hozman.
