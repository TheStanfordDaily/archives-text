# Shakspere Recital Tomorrow
## 
### 
The program for the recital to be
given by Mr. Bassett in the chapel
at 7:HO tomorrow evening, as previ
ously announced, is as follows:
"The Merchant of Venice," act 1,
scenes 1 and 3; act 111, scene 2;
act IV, scene 1. The recital, which
is to take the place of the regular
Tuesday evening lecture, will last
about an hour.
