# Building Contracts Let.
## 
### 
Three contracts were signed to
day providing for the woodwork,
masonry, and paving of the new
Engineering laboratory. The stone
work will he done by Mr. McGilvray,
who has erected the other Univer
sity structures; the woodwork by
E. A. Hettinger of Palo Alto, who
has a similar contract in the Me
morial Church; and the successful
bid for paving is that of George
Goodman <>112 San Francisco. Con
tracts for the structure which will
contain the office of the Engineer
ing department, to stand at the
southeast corner of the outer quad
rangle, will he let next week.
