# Marriage of Quayle, 'OO.
## 
### 
Charles Quayle, '00, and Miss
Coral Sanor of Palo Alto were mar
ried at twelve o'clock today, at the
home of the bride on High street.
Quayle was a Law major and a
prominent debater, having made
finals twice. He is at present
practicing law in Oakland, where
he intends making his home.
