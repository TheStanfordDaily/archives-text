# Endowment Deed Recorded.
## 
### 
The deed by which M re. Stan
ford transferred her remaining
property to the Board of Trustees
of the University December 9th,
was filed for record yesterday at
the City Hall, San Francisco. It
formally conveys four pieces of
property, one being the so-called
Grid ley farm in Bntte county; a
second the Vina ranch, situated in
Butte and Tehama; a third the
Palo Alto farm, partly in San Ma
teo and partly in Santa Clara, and
finally, the Stanford mansion on
the corner of California and Powell
streets, San Francisco.
