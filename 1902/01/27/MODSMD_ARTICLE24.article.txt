# Michigan May Come Again.
## 
### 
If the objections of the Michigan
faculty to the idea of a western trip
can he overcome, it if? probable that
San Francisco will see a Michigan-
Stanford or Michigan-California
game next Thanksgiving. A dis
patch from Chicago represents
Yost as strongly in favor of the
trip. He said that it could be
made by missing only four
days' university work, and he had
no fear for the condition of his men
on a long trip after his experience
of last season.
