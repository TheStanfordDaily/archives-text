# Spanish Club Meeting.
## 
### 
The Spanish Club Tertulia held
a second meeting on Monday even
ing at the Delta Upsilon house.
Visitors as well as regular members
were present, to a total of twenty
five. A committee was appointed
to investigate the question of pre
senting the comedy "Zaragiieta."
The club inspected a collection of
photographs from Mexico, and en
joyed an informal musical program
by Miss Kraft, Miss Roberts, Miss
Hartnell, and Mr. Johnson.
