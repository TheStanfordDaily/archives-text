# Inn Changes Meal Hours.
## 
### 
Last evening at dinner the mem
bers of the Inn Club, with the ex
ception of four members, voted in
favor of Ibe change in the meal
hours which follows: Breakfast,
7:30-8:45 a.m.; lunch, 12:15-1:80
p.m.; dinner, 5:45-7:00 p.m., the
last the same as before. These
changes are made to accommodate
students and waiters having 8:15
or 12:15 recitations, and will go
into effect on Saturday, February
Ist. Yesterday at lunch the Inn
served 140 people,an unusually large
number. Several changes are soon
to be made in the kitchen arrange
ments with a view to perfecting the
service.
