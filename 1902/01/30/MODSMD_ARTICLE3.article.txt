# Tomorrow Night's Reception.
## 
### 
The following committees have
heen appointed to take charge of
the reception to be given by the
V. M. C. A. and the V. W. C. A. in
Roble Hall tomorrow night: Re
ception Committee —Miss F. Abbott,
Miss J. N. Bell, Miss A. S. Mills.
Entertainment Committee — Miss
H. Traphagen, Miss G. Holt, chair
man. Refreshment Committee —
Miss M. Guidery, Miss J. 11. Phelps,
Miss E. N. Reeves, Miss H. M. Salis
bury. The reception will be given
to all students in the University,
and there is every prospect of its
being a very enjoyable affair.
