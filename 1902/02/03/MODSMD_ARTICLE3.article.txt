# Sophomore Cotillion Committee.
## 
### 
President Taylor, of the Class of
1904, has appointed the following
persons to compose the Sophomore
Cotillion committee: I). C. Wiley,
chairman; Miss Parkhurst, Miss
Gossett, O. A. Wilson, G. W.
Bernhardt, M. L. Van Norden,
and H. A. Sprague. A meet
ing of the committee will he
held soon, and members will he no
tified later as to the time and place.
The dance this year will be given
on the night of February 21st.
