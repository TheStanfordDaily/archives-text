# Senior Prom. Committees.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Senior Prom.
Committee yesterday Chairman
Copp appointed the following sub
committees : Booths—Miss Gill,
Miss M. Taylor, Miss R. Taylor,
and Miss McDougald. Music —
R. V. Reppy and K. E. YVigle. Re
freshments — A. E. Cooley. Deco
rations — Percy McDowell and C. D.
Scott. Programs—H. C..Lucas and
B. P. Oakford. These committees
will report next Monday at 1:15 in
room 10. The suggestion has been
made to conduct the Prom, this
year as a sort of Mardi Gras or
mask carnival. This will be con
sidered at the next meeting.
