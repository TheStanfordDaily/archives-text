# The
						Inn for January.
## 
### 
Last evening the directors of the
University Inn wentover the books,
which showed the total receipts
from January 6th, when the Inn
opened, until the end of the month
to be $1,700, with a surplus of $59
It was stated that with twenty
more regular members the price of
board could be reduced to $3.75 a
week. The directors report affairs
running smoothly and are fully
satisfied with the progress thus far.
