# Chess Tournament.
## 
### 
The tournament for membership
in the University Chess Club will
close on February 17th. Nineteen
men have entered the contest and
twelve will be chosen from the
number. From the club, six men
will probably represent the Uni
versity against California and other
teams. As soon as the member
ship is determined a captain and
manager will be elected and the
picture for the Quad will be taken.
