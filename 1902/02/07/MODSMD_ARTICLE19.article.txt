# Shakspere Recital Program.
## 
### 
The program for the Shakspere
recital by Mr. Samuel Arthur King
on Monday evening in the chapel
is as follows:
Act I, Scene 2—A room of state.
Act I, Scene 4 —The platform of
Elsi no re.
Act 11, Scene 2 —The "Now 1 am
alone" soliloquy.
Act 111, Scene I—The1 —The play scene.
Act 111, Scene 3 —The closet
scene.
Act V, Scene I—The1 —The graveyard
scene.

Curry of I'alo Alto wants to sell
you, professors and students, furni
ture, carpels and stoves from any
house in San Francisco at their rate
to you. Curry pays the freight and
delivers the goods free.
Students' Stables.—Carriages at
all trains. General livery. Iled 76.
Full-dress Suits to rent, $2.50.
Misfit Clothing Parlors, (upstairs)
23 Stockton street, S. K.
F. L. Crandall has the largest
stock of Bicycles, Sporting Goods,
Photo Supplies, Guns and Ammu
nition in this vicinity.
Orr & Peterson, dealers in Gas
and Electrical Chandeliers and fix
tures, incandescent lamps, globes
and shades. Electrical supplies.
Merchant Tailor. — Cleaning,
dyeing, repairing. Ladies' garments
repaired. Louis Bjcnoit.
