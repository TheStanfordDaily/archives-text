# News of the World.
## 
### 
There were three burglaries in
Sun Jose yesterday.
Elusive De Wett has given the
British the slip again.
Eleven lives were lost in a hotel
fire yesterday morning at St. Louis.
Secretary Root has held that
army officers may occupy civil
positions.
Another successful test of the
Marconi wireless telegraphy has
heen made.
The United States "is," not
"are." So the House committee on
revision of its laws has decided.
A great tire swept through I'ater
son, New Jersey, yesterday and left
in its desolate wake the embers and
ashes of property valued at $10,-
000,000.
Maurice Grau, director of the
Metropolitan Opera Company, has
announced a gorgeous production
of grand opera in honor of Prince
Henry's visit.
The body of Nora Fuller, the girl
who disappeared mysteriously in
San Francisco on January 11th,
was found dead in a Sutter street
house on Saturday. The murder,
in its hoilible details, is very sim
ilar to the Durant case of unsav
ory memory.
