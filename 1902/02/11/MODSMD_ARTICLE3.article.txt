# Carnot Preparations.
## 
### 
The Carnot debaters will leave
the University on the 3:06 train
Friday afternoon and will put up
at the California hotel in San
Francisco. They, will leave the
city on the 6:30 boat, which is the
last boat for the rooters to go on.
The debate will be held in Hearst
Hall, on the Berkeley campus, and
will commence at 8 p.m. The
judges and other officials have not
as yet been appointed, but will be
announced later. Arrangements
for the debate on the part of Stan
ford are in the hands of Frank
English, '01, last year's Carnot de
bater.
