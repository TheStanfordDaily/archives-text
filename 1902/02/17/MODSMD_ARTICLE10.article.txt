# Tennis Tournament Postponed.
## 
### 
The tennis tournament which
was not held on Saturday owing to
the weather, will take place on
next Saturday morning with the
same conditions concerning entries
and rules of play. The fee of fifty
cents may he paid any time before
the tournament begins. In the
afternoon the tie for second place
between Harry Hoyle, '03, and C. B.
Hopper, '05, left over from the
tournament two weeks ago, was
played off and won by Hopper.
