# Stanford Submits Question.

###

Committee of Stanford and the
University of California met in the
California hotel at San Francisco
last Saturday. Stanford lost the
toss, and must submit the question
for the Intercollegiate debate two
weeks from today. Unless the
lield meet is held at Tanforan the
contest will take place on the night
