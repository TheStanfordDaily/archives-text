# Big Audience Assured.
## 
### 
Tickets for the Pirates of Pen
zance were on sale yesterday and
today on the quad and at the Pal
ace Pharmacy in Palo Alto, and
ojter two hundred dollars has so
far been taken in. A large attend
ance is assured, and the manage
ment is well pleased. The scenery
for the opera is now being putin
place, and the final dress rehearsals
will be held tomorrow and Thurs
day evenings. The advance sale
of seats will continue at the Uni
versity Bookstore and at the Palace
Pharmacy in Palo Alto.
