# Vacation for Dr. McFarland.
## 
### 
Dr. F. M. McFarlaiul, associate
professor of Histology, will sail for
Europe in June, returning to the
University in the fall of 1903.
While abroad Dr. McFarlaiul will
visit the zoology stations of Italy,
Germany, and England. The win
ter will he spent mainly at the zo
ological station in Naples, Italy,
and the following summer at.the
University of St. Andrew's, Scot
land. Dr. McFarlaiul was in Eu
rope from 1894 to '96, and intends
to pursue many of the researches
begun at that time. This is his
sabbatical vacation.
