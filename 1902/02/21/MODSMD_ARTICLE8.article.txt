# Gymnasium Notes.
## 
### 
—Brummit, '05, sprained his
ankle while playing basket-ball
yesterday.
—Out-of-door men are reporting
for work in the gymnasium during
the wet weather.
—A new file has been put up in
the office for the periodicals of the
Gymnasium Library.
—A new book has been received
by the Gymnasium Library on
wrestling, boxing, and fencing by
W. H. Pollock, F. C. Grove, IS. B.
Michel 1, and Warm house.
