# Discuss Bees and Beetles.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Zool
ogy Club was well attended last
evening at the home of Dr. Gilbert
in I J alo Alto. Mrs. Ruby Green
Bell gave the principal talk of the
evening. Her subject whs the
"Quantitative Study of Variation,"
and was based upon a series of ex
periments and observations she has
been making on various insects of
this section, particularly bees and
beetles. The lecture was illustrated
with charts and drawings. Follow
ing the lecture a general discussion
was held on the same subject by
Dr. Gilbert, Professor Kellogg, and
other members of the club.
