# No Meet with Pomona.
## 
### 
Captain McCaughern has been
corresponding with both I'omona
College and Occidental College of
Southern California in regard to a
meet to lie held in Los Angeles
with one of the Stanford class
teams. Arrangements with Pomo
na had been partly concluded,
when, upon being beaten last week
by Occidental, they concluded to
end negotiations. At present Occi
dental is anxious to arrange for a
meet. McCaughern will send the
winners of the class meet on this
trip, in case Occidental will agree.
Occidental has the strongest team
she has had in years, and will put
up a pretty tight with any team
that may go south.
