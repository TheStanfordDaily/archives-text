# The
						College World.
## 
### 
—Yale, Harvard, and New York
University will hold a joint gym
nastic exhibition.
—The Yale Daily Ncivs, it is said,
has a subscription list of 2,500. The
price of subscription is four dollars
a year.
—The revolver team of the Rifle
and Pistol Club, at Harvard, re
cently held a match by telegraph
with the Smith & Wesson team.
—There will be a four-mile inter
collegiate relay race at Philadelphia
this month. Probably Columbia,
Princeton, Yale, Pennsylvania, and
Cornell will compete.
—Chicago will hold no track
meet with Notre Dame because of
alleged unsportsmanlike treatment
last year at the hands of the Catho
lics. —Minnesota Daily.
