# Clubs Go to Belmont.
## 
### 
The Glee and Mandolin Clubs
will give a concert at Belmont on
Saturday evening, March Bth. A
party will be given in their honor
by Headmaster Reid. The clubs
will leave here at 4:30 p.m. by
buses, reaching Belmont for a six
o'clock dinner. The concert will
then begin. It will be followed by
a dance and a drive back to the
University.
