# Will Give Reception.
## 
### 
The Young People's Christian
Union of Palo Alto will tender a
reception to John Willis Haer, Gen
eral Secretary of the United Society
of Christian Endeavor,in Fraternal
Hall, Saturday, March Ist, at 3
o'clock. Mr. Haer is making a tour
of the Pacific Coast. He will give
a short talk, after which there will
he an informal reception. The
Union issues a cordial invitation to
every one to attend.
