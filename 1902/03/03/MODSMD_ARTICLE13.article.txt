# Seats for "The Pirates."
## 
### 
Seats for the performance of the
Pirates of Penzance, which is to he
given again in the Assembly Hall
on Friday, will be placed on sale
tomorrow morning, and a hig sale
is expected. The opera made a
very favorable impression at its
previous performance, and is ex
pected to draw a crowded house
when given again. Two rehearsals
will he held this week, and some
new stage "business" will be intro
duced.
