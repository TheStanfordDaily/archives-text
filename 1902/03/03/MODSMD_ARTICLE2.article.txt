# Dr. Jordan Due Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will reach San Fran
cisco this evening and is due at the
University tomorrow morning.
On bis trip East he lectured at St.
Louis, and attended the meeting of
the Society of American Univer
sities at Chicago and the inaugural
exercises of the new president of
Johns Hopkins University, Dr. Ira
Ilemsen. At the latter place the
honorary degree of Doctor of Laws
was conferred upon Benjamin Ide
Wheeler, president of the Univer
sity of California, and upon Presi
dent Jordan.
