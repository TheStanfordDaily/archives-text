# WILL MEET UKIAH.
## Sophomores vs. the High School on 22d. Hawley Elected Junior Track Captain. 
### 
Arrangements are concluded for
a meet between the Ukiah high
school and the Sophomores on
March 22d, to be held probably
on the morning of that date. The
Sophomore team is a good all
round aggregation, being well rep
resented in everything except the
distances.
At a meeting of the candidates
for Junior track team held on the
oval Friday afternoon L. C. Haw
ley was elected captain. Hawley
is entered in the half-mile. He
went with the '03 Freshman team
to Reno, and has represented Stan
ford in the Intercollegiate in the
quarter for two years.
