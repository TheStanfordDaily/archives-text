# Hamilton, '04 Track Captain.
## 
### 
This afternoon the members of
the Sophomore track team met at
the training-house and elected H.
T. Hamilton. B. C. Bubb was put
in nomination, but declined. Ham
ilton is a short distance sprinter.
Last year he made points for the
'04 team, which won the class
championship, in the 100- and 220-
yard dashes. He won third place
in the 100 and 220 at the Inter
collegiate meet last spring.
