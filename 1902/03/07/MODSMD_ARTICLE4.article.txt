# On the Roble Court.
## 
### 
Thirty-two girls have entered the
handball tournament. The class
doubles now being played will be
followed by a contest for the gym
championship. Singles will be
played after the spring vacation.
The preliminary games in tennis
have been delayed on account of
the weather.
The basket ball grounds are to
be putin order [right away. The
girls have been playing good
bail for preliminary work.
