# Will Be Entertained at Belmont.
## 
### 
The University Glee and Mando
lin Clubs have been invited to a
dinner and dance at Belmont
School tomorrow night, and will
give a concert there after dinner.
Three buses will leave the campus
at 4:30 p.m., arriving in Belmont in
time for dinner at six, and return
ing to the University about eleven
o'clock.
