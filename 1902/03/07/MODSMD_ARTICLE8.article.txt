# Interclass Debate April 18th.
## 
### 
The date for the Freshman-Soph
omore debate has been set for Fri
day evening, April 18th. The
Sophomores won the toss and the
Freshmen chose the following ques
tion: "Hesolved, That the United
States should resist, by force if need
be, any further colonization of
South America by Europe." The
Sophomores have until Monday
noon to choose their side, when the
amount of time allotted to each
speaker will be decided.
