# English Club Meets Tuesday.
## 
### 
The English Club will meet at
the Delta Upsilon house next Tues
day evening, March 11th, instead
of Wednesday, the regular night.
S. T. Clover,of the Los Angeles Ex
press, will give a talk on "Graduates
of the Press," reading selections
from Earnest McGaffey and from
Dr. Drummond's Canadian Habit
ant poetry.
