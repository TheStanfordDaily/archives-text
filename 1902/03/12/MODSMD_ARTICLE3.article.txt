# Consideration of Contracts.
## 
### 
Two contracts for construction of
portions of the Mechanical Engi
neering building of Stanford Uni
versity were filed Saturday in the
office of the county recorder. One
contract is with Wm. Thompson
for the construction of the carpen
ter work of the building, the con
sideration being $27,000. The other
was with the McGilvray. Stone
Company for the construction of
the concrete, brick, and stone work
of the building for the sum of
$74,854. — Palo Alto Times.
