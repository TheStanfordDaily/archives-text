# Musical Clubs' Trip.
## 
### 
The Glee and Mandolin Clubs
left this afternoon on the 3:48 train
for a short concert trip. They will
play this evening at Santa Cruz
and Saturday night at the Hotel
Del Monte in Monterey. Waldemar
Young, '04, accompanies the clubs
as monologist, and C. L. Kirebaugh,
'04, as soloist. Maker and Bush
will give their usual "stunts." The
program will be practically the
same as that given on the Christ
mas trip, with the exception of a
solo by Firebaugh and a monologue
by Young. The men who went
on the trip were:
Glee Club —First tenors, H. S.
Gay, '03, L. C. Hawley, '03, B. E.
Nourse, '03, F. A. Leib, '02; second
tenors, A. Perrin, '03, B. 11. Cocks,
'04, G. H. Clark,'o4, H. F.Scoville,
'04, G. W. Dryer, '02; first bass, H.
H. Atkinson, '03, J. E. Cline, '02,
E. C. Epplev, '05, C. H. Baker, '03;
second bass, H. E. Bush, '05, W. F.
Whitaker, '03, R. N. Park, '04.
Mandolin Club.—First mando
lins, H. L. Morrison,'o3, A. J.Copp
Jr., '02, J. E. Knapp, '03, N. C.
Powers, '02, R. J. Keith, '03; sec
ond mandolins, N. Collyer, '04, H.
C. Hubbard, '03, R. N. Park, '04,
H. A. Weihe, '04; guitars, W. G.
Morrison, '03, E. I. Thayer, '08, T. E.
Palmer, '03, H. H. Atkinson, '03,
11. E. Bush, '05, H. S. Gay, '03;
soloist, C. L. Firebaugh; monolog
ist, Waldemar Young.
