# Ukiah Reception Committee.
## 
### 
The following men have been
appointed to serve as a committee
in charge of arrangements for the
"stag" to be given to Ukiah high
school by Encina Club on next
Saturday evening: Hauverman,
'03, chairman; Copp, '03, Nourse,
'03, Cone, '02, Li Hard, '99, and
Nangle, '03. The program of the
evening's entertainment will be
published later in the week. The
committee extends an invitation to
all the men of the University to be
present.
