# Gymnasium Club Exhibition.
## 
### 
The annual formal gymnasium
exhibition will take place in the
gymnasium on Friday evening of
this week. The Gymnasium Club
has been working steadily since
the informal exhibition of last
semester, and on the whole a very
excellent program is promised. The
entertainment is free to the public,
and the men and women of the UDi
versity are especially invited.
