# Chess Club Round-Robin.
## 
### 
The Stanford Chess Team has
challenged California to a meet,
and though the final arrangements
have not been made it is very prob
able that the meet will take place
after the vacation. At Berkeley
the try-outs are in progress and
will probably he finished about
April sth. The players here are
holding a round-robin according to
classes. By this the champions of
the different classes and of the Uni
versity will be chosen. A team of
six will also be selected which will
probably meet Berkeley. Prelim
inary tournaments will probably
he ended the week after vacation.
The club is also considering the
matter of challenging other chess
teams in the State.
