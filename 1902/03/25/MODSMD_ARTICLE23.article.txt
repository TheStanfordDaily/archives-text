# Constitution Being Printed.
## 
### 
The constitution and by-laws of
the Associated Students, together
with their ten amendments, and
also all the athletic and debating
agreements with California, are be
ing printed in pamphlet form. The
constitution has not been reprinted
since 1899. About one hundred
copies will be prepared.
