# Michigan Daily Gives Ball.
## 
### 
About a year ago the U. of M.
Daily board conceived anil carried
out the idea of giving a ball to the
leaders of college life. The affair
was very successful and attracted it
great deal of attention among other
colleges. The Daily News contin
ues the custom this year. Already
the invitations to prominent men,
to the number of seventy-eight,
have been issued.
