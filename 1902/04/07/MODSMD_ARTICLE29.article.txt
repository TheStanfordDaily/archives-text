# U.C. Notes.
## 
### 
A society vaudeville and minstrel
show will be held in Harmon gym
nasium April 18th for the benefit
of the Eastern trip of the Univer
sity of California track team.
Hunt and Baker will constitute
the doubles tennis team for the Uni
versity of California, while Brown
and MacGavin will probably be
chosen for the singles. There is an
abundance of good material at the
university this year.
