# Antigone Rehearsals.
## 
### 
Rehearsals for Antigone will
he held at the Assembly Hall,
afternoons at 4:30, and evenings at
7:00, as follows:
Orchestra. —April 10th, even
ing; 12th, evening; 14th, evening;
15th,evening;16th,evening.
Acting Chorus. — April 9 th,
afternoon and evening; 10th, after
noon; 11th, afternoon; 12th, 9 a.m.;
14th, afternoon and evening; loth,
afternoon and evening; 16th, after
noon and evening.
Supplementary Chorus. —April
12th, evening; 14th, evening; 15th,
evening; 16th, evening.
Principals. April 9th, after
noon; 11th, evening; 14th, after
noon; 15th, afternoon; 16th, even
ing. Other rehearsals of principals
to he arranged for privately. It is
imperative that rehearsals he at
tended regularly and promptly.
