# Antigone Rehearsals.
## 
### 
Rehearsals for Antigone will
he held at the Assembly Hall,
afternoons at 4:30, and evenings at
7:30, as follows:
Orchestra. —April 10th, even
ing; 12th, evening; 14th, evening;
15th, evening; 16th,evening.
Acting Chorus. — April 10th,
afternoon; 11th, afternoon; 12th,9
a.m.; 14th, afternoon and evening;
15th, afternoon and evening; 16th,
afternoon and evening.
Supplementary Chohus. —April
12th, evening; 14th, evening; 15th,
evening; 16th, evening.
Principals.— April 11th, even
ing; 14th, afternoon; 15th, after
noon; 16th, evening. Other re
hearsals of principals to be arranged
for privately. It is imperative that
rehearsals be attended regularly
and promptly.
