# Summer School Announcement.
## 
### 
A pamphlet has been issued for
the summer session of the Hopkins
Seaside Laboratory. The session
will begin on June 9th, for six
weeks' instruction. Courses will
be given in Botany, Zoology, Phys
iology, and Morphology. The in
structors will be: 0. I'. Jenkins,
Physiology; G. C. Price, Zoology;
10. G. Starks, Zoology; A. A. Law
son, Botany; J. Grinnell.and M. H.
Spaulding. The Southern Pacific
offers special rates for students to
Pacific Grove.
