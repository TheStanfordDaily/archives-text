# TO REPRESENT STANFORD.
## Dr. Branner Leaves to Attend the Installation of Columbia's New President. 
### 
Vice-President Branner will leave
tomorrow for New York to attend,
as delegate from Stanford, the in
auguration of Nicholas Murray
Butler as president of Columbia
on the 19th of April. Professor
Cubberley and the new Economics
appointee, Mr. Whitaker, '99, who
are now at Columbia, will also rep
resent Stanford. Dr. Branner will
probably conic home by way of
New Orleans.
