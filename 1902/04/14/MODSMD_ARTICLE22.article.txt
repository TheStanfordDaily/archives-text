# On "Schley and Santiago."
## 
### 
The book by George Edward
Graham on "Schley and Santiago,"
giving an account of the cruise of
the Flying Squadron, the blockade
and the final destruction of Cer
vera's fleet, has just been issued. It
contains a personal narrative of the
battle by Admiral Schley, and is
illustrated with photographs taken
during the fight. It is being pre
sented to the public by B.' Ruhl.
