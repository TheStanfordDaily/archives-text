# Girls' Musical Clubs.
## 
### 
The Girls' Glee and Mandolin
Clubs left this afternoon for Los
Gatos. They will take dinner at
the hotel, returning by moonlight
after the con cert.
The program consists of four
numbers by the Glee Club, three by
the Mandolin Club, a spanish song
by Miss Chita Kraft, a violin solo
by Miss Bruckman, whistling 6010
by Miss Bean, a banjo solo by Miss
May Kimball, and reading by Mrs.
Irwin.
