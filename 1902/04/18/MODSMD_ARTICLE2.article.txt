# The 						Sermon Sunday.
## 
### 
Prof. George M. Stratton, Associ
ate Professor of Psychology in the
University of California, will speak
in the chapel Sunday morning.
His subject will be "An Evolution
ist's Defense of Sin," with special
references to Fiske's view of moral
evil. Professor Stratton is a grad
uate of the University of California,
'88, A. M., Yale, '90, and Ph. J).,
Leipzig, '96. He is regarded as
one of the strongest of the younger
men in philosophy, and his work
has done much to give distinction
to this department at Berkeley.
