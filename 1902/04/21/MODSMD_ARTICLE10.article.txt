# News of the World.
## 
### 
The big strike in Belgium is
ended.
London is threatened with a
meat famine.
The Chinese insurgents are gain
ing strength.
Governor Odell of New York is
on his way to visit the Pacific
Coast.
Frank Stockton, the popular
story writer, died in Washington,
D. C., yesterday.
Russia has agreed to withdraw
all troops except a railway guard
from Manchuria.
Street-car traffic is tied up on all
but two lines in San Francisco.
The strikers number 2500 men.
Fifty thousand people witnessed
the Sheffield United and South
hampton clubs play a draw foot
ball game for the English cup at
London last Saturday.
