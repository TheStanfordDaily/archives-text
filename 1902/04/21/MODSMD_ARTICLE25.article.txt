# English Club Meeting.
## 
### 
Mr. Clover, editor of the Los An
geles Express, will address the Eng
lish Club at the Kappa Alpha
Theta house next Wednesday
evening. His main address will be
on "The Graduates of the Press,"
and in addition he will read some
of Dr. Drummond's French dialect
poetry, and a poem or two of Ernest
McLaughlin's. Larrey Bowman,
'01, will conduct the meeting, in
view of his presence here and his
necessitated withdrawal from the
presidency at about this season last
year.
