It was a veritable California in
vasion Saturday. They brought
their band, their rooters, and their
track team. They took possession
of the bleachers and the field. They
entertained us with their music,
and noise, and exhibition. They
won the day overwhelmingly, but
not without some interesting con
tests. They seemed to have a good
time, and Stanford enjoyed it with
them. We all look forward to more
such meets —with the score re
versed. Meantime California may
well feel proud of its magnificent
team, which will make a strong
showing for the Pacific Coast on
the Eastern trip.
