# News of the World.
## 
### 
The home of the King's Daugh
ters in Oakland has been destroyed
by fire.
The House naval committee
strongly urges the building of more
battleships.
Sol Smith Russell, the famous
comedian, has succumbed to a
lingering illness.
The Chinese Exclusion measure
has been passed, and the life of the
act is made indefinite.
A tornado in the vicinity of Mor
gan, Texas, killed seven people and
demolished much property.
The Metropolitan National hank
of Chicago recently distributed
$75,000 among its employees.
The street-car men of Chicago
have succeeded, after several weeks
of campaigning, in forming a labor
union.
The United Copper Company
with an authorized stock of $80,-
000,000, was organized in New
Jersey yesterday.
