# Faculty Defeated Zetes.
## 
### 
Yesterday afternoon the Faculty
defeated the Zeta Psis at baseball
15 to 7 in a seven-inning game.
The teams were made up as follows:
Faculty. —Allin, 3b; Angell, 3b;
Searles, lb; Newsom, c; Kellogg, p;
Howard, 2b; Murray, If; Marx, cf;
Foster, rf; Starbuck, ss.
Zetes. — Baker, 3b; Luce, [>; Hush,
se; Stillman, c; Bronte, 2b; Leib, If;
Atkinson, cf; Park, rf; Mainwar
ing, lb.
