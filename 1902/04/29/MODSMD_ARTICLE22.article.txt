# Quads.
## 
### 
— Prof. B. C. Brown of the Art
department left for the East last
Saturday.
—A. McNee, ex-'O2, who has been
in Arizona for his health, was on
the campus yesterday.
— President Harriman of the
Southern Pacific, with a party
among whom were his son and
daughter, and W. Crocker, visited
the quad Saturday afternoon. Mr.
Harriman is on a tour of inspec
tion of all the lines under him.
