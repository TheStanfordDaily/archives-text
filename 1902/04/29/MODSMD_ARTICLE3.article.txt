# Spooner, '99, Honored.
## 
### 
D. Brainerd Spooner, '99, has
recently accepted a fellowship from
Harvard University, by means of
which he will he enabled to spend
another year in India studying
Sanskrit with the Buddhist priests,
and two years at Harvard studying
for his Ph. D. degree. Spooner was
a major in the Classical Philology
department here, and one of the
most brilliant language students
who ever graduated from Stanford.
After graduation he taught English
two years in Japan, and during the
past year he has made an exhaus
tive study of Sanskrit among the
Buddhist priests in Benares, India.
