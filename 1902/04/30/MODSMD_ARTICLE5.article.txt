# ADOPTED CONSTITUTION.
## The
						Women's League Elected Officers This Afternoon for Ensuing Year. 
### 
The Women's League adopted a
constitution and elected the follow
ing officers for the year, at a meet
ing held in the chapel this after
noon :
President, Miss Elizabeth Peck
ham; vice-president, Miss Cara
Stillman; secretary, Miss Atherton;
treasurer, Mrs. Branner. Mem
bership committee, Mrs. Jor
dan, Miss Wilbur, Miss Anderson,
and Mrs. Gilbert. Refreshment
committee, Miss Hardison, Mrs.
Matzke, and Miss Parsons.
