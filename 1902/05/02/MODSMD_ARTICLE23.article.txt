# Encina 11, Fraternities 5.
## 
### 
A baseball team from Encina de
feated one from the fraternities
Wednesday, 11 to 2. The players
were:
Encina —Tritcli, p; Knight, c;
Brown, ss; Lowenthal, lb; Trow
bridge, 2b; Copp, 3b; Young, If;
Mori, rf.
Fraternities —Ballard, p; Still
man, c; Luce, ss; Gamble, lb; Hop
per, 2b; Kehrlein, 3b; Leib, If; Car
ter, cf; Dole, rf.
