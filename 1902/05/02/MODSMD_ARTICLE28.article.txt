# Gas for Neighboring Towns.
## 
### 
Jno. A. Britton, president of the
Oakland Gas Light and Heat
Company, has consented to act as
consulting gas engineer for the new
United Gas and Electric Company,
and is now preparing specifications
for the laying of a pipe line from
San Jose north to San Mateo City
for the purpose of supplying the
various towns along its route, in
cluding Palo Alto, with gas for
fuel.
