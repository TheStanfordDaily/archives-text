# Baker Elected by Glee Club.
## 
### 
At a meeting held this afternoon,
the Glee Club elected the follow
ing officers for next year: C. H.
Baker, '04, leader; L. C. Hawley,
'03, business manager; H. F. Sco
ville, '03, and F. P. Whitaker, '04,
executive committee. Baker is a
member of the Psi and Sigma
Sigma fraternities, has sung on the
Glee Club two years, and has acted
as leader since the resignation of
F. A. Corbusier, '04, last January.
