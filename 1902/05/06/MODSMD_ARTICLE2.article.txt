# Senior Ball Committees.
## 
### 
The Senior Ball Committee met
this afternoon and appointed the
following sub-committees: Decora
tions, C. E. Waite, I). V. Cowden,
and Miss Wheeler; refreshments,
F. Leib and Miss Martin. There
will be thirty regular dances but no
extras at the ball on May 26th.
Another meeting of the committee
will be held soon.
