# Dr. Angell Goes to Europe.
## 
### 
Dr. Frank Angell of the Psychol
ogy department has finished his
work for this semester, and, accom
panied by Mrs. Angell, left Saturday
for Wurzburg, Germany. He will
spend the summer studying in
several of the German laboratories,
and will work with a number of
the leading psychologists of Europe,
some of whom were fellow students
of his at Leipsic. Dr. Angell will
return to Stanford early in the
fall.
