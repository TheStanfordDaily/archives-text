# News of the World.
## 
### 
Ninety lives were lost yesterday
in a Texas tornado.
The mine owners have cut off the
credit supply of goods to the coal
strikers.
A gas trust with one hundred
millions behind it is being formed
in Chicago.
The United States coat of arms
has come down from the Havana
custom house.
At the age of sixteen Alfonso
XIII has assumed the cares of
Spain's ruler.
The Cuban cabinet has been se
lected and meets with the approval
of the better classes.
The Halt Lake Telegram advo
cates the election of a woman as
United States Senator to succeed
Sentor Ilawline.
Mount Pelee has again broken
into activity and threatens further
destruction on the unfortunate
island of Martinique.
