# Farce Tickets Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tickets for the Senior Farce, "A
Rank Outsider," which is to be
staged in the Assembly Hall next
Thursday evening, will go on sale
on the quad tomorrow morning at
10 o'clock. The sale will be con
tinued Wednesday and Thursday,
on the two latter days at the Palace
Pharmacy in Palo Alto, as well as
on the quad. Prices are one dollar,
seventy-five, fifty, and thirty-five
cents.
