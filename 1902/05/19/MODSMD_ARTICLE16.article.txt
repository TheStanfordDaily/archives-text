# Faculty Played Encina Club.
## 
### 
Encina Club vanquished the
Faculty baseball nine last Friday
afternoon with the score of 11 to 3.
The features of the contest were the
pitching of Professor Howard and
of Tritch, the fielding of Professor
Starbuclc and F. A. Brown, '02,
and the bundling of errors at crit

ical times by the faculty players.
Six errors and one hit were respon
sible for the Encina club's five runs
in the fourth inning, while four
errors allowed two more tallies in
the fifth, and four errors and a hit
gave two again in the seventh.
