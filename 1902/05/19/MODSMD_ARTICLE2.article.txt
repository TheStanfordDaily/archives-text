# Senior Farce Posters.
## 
### 
The art posters for the Senior
Farce which were drawn by Roger
J. Sterrett, '02, have been received
and put on sale at the Bookstore.
The poster is a remarkably artistic
two-color production, representing
a black-robed executioner with an
immense axe. The title of the
farce, "The Rank Outsider" is
closely worked in across the bottom
of the drawing.
The Senior farce josh posters will
arrive Tuesday night and be placed
on sale Wednesday, Some revela
tions of a startling nature are looked
for.
