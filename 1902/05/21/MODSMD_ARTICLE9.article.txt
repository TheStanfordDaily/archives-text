# The
						Commencement Number.
## 
### 
Each of the regular subscribers
to the Daily Palo Alto will re
ceive a copy of the Commencement
number this year. Orders for extra
copies, accompanied by the pur
chase price of ten cents and mailing
address, should be sent to the office
this week. A large edition will be
printed, and will he put on sale
immediately at the close of the
graduation exercises next Wednes
day. It is especially important
for persons leaving before Com
mencement to have their orders in
now so as to make sure of getting
a copy.
