# Phi Delta Phi Banquet.
## 
### 
The Miller chapter of the legal
fraternity of Phi Delta Phi held its
farewell banquet at the Delta Upsi
lon house last night. Professor
Clarke B. Whittier, an honorary
member of Miller chapter, was the
guest of honor. Toasts were re
sponded to by Professor Whittier,
Professor Lewers, F. W. Doan, E.
S. Page, H. Fry and others.
