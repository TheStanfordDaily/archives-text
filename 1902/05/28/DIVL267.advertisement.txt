During Commencement Week the
large number of visitors who will
visit Stanford will want to carry
home with them some mementos of
the University. At Simians' Book
store, 272 University Ave., they will
find a large variety of photographic
views and souvenirs sufficient to meet
every desire. Printed matter about
the University and surroundings, as
well as pictures. All about the
Chapel, the Windows, the Memorial
Arch, the Frieze, and all other points
of interest. Graduating Seniors are
requested to hand in their names for
the Stanford Alumnus. Price One
Dollar a year. H. W. Simkins, Mgr.
