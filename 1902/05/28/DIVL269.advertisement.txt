WE THANK YOU
for you liberal patronage during the past year
and invite you to visit our NEW store when you
return. We shall be elaborately fitted out with
new fixtures and new goods and can guarantee
you better prices and better goods than else
where. Do not stock up before returning but
wait to see what the latest college styles will be.
