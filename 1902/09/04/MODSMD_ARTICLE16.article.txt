# Reception Next Week.
## 
### 
The annual Y. M. C. A. and
Y. VV. C. A. reception will be given
this year on Friday evening, Sep
tember 12th. The details of the ar
rangements have not been settled
yet, but Secretary Harter will con
fer with President Jordan as to the
use of rooms, etc. The "stag" re
ception, which was to have been
given early in the year, will be
postponed for the present.
