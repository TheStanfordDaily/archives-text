# Football Meeting Monday.
## 
### 
Football suits will be dealt out
on Saturday. On Monday after
noon the men are expected to ap
pear on the oval. Captain Lee will
meet all candidates for the teams
Monday evening in the Encina
Club-rooms, at which time the
captain will take names and the
positions for which they will try.
The occasion will be made one of
general jollification. Every man
of the University is expected to at
tend.
