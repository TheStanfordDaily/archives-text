# Stanford Graduate Honored.
## 
### 
Miss Harriet Brown, '02, was in
June appointed general secretary
of the Y. W. C. A. for the states of
California and Nevada. During
her college course Miss Brown was
a leader in the work of the associa
tions, being president once, and
general secretary last year. After
attending the Y. \V. C. A. Training
School for Secretaries at Milwau
kee, Wis., during the summer,
Miss Brown returns this month for
work among the associations at
Pomona, University of Southern
California, and the normal schools
of Southern California. Next
spring she will return north, and
in May will have charge of the an
nual conference at Capitola.
