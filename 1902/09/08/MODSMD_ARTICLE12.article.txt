# Dr. McFarland's Marriage.
## 
### 
Dr. Frank McFarland, professor
of Histology, was married during
the summer to Miss Olive Knowles
of Cambridge, Ohio. The cere
mony took place at the home of
the bride on August 27th.
Dr. and Mrs. McFarland will
spend a year abroad visiting the
seaside laboratories and will stay
for some time in Naples for ex
tended study.
Another Stanford wedding re
corded during vacation was that of
Will R. White, Civil Engineering
00, to Eva May Weir, daughter of
ex-Secretary of State Weir of
Washington. The marriage took
place in Olympia. While an un
dergraduate White went to the
Philippine service with other Stan
ford men in Company K.
