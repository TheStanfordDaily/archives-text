# The
						Samoan Expedition.
## 
### 
The expedition of the United
States Fish Commission to Samoa
during the summer under the
charge of President David Starr
Jordan resulted in the collection of
about six hundred varieties of fish.
Dr. Jordan was assisted by Prof. V.
L. Kellogg, of the department of
Entomology, who made a study of
the bird and insect life of the
islands. Professor Allardice, of
the department of Mathematics,
accompanied the party as a volun
teer. Dr. Jordan's report will be
published by the Commission and
the collection added to the National
Museum at Washington and to the
Zoological museum at the Univer
sity.
