# Mosaics for Museum.
## 
### 
The eight mosaics that are being
put on the front of the Museum are
almost finished. The Italian artists

will put the finishing touches on
the four figures to the right of the
main entrance this week. These
pictures represent Cyprus, Sculp
ture, Archaeology, and Progress and
Civilization. The panels just above
the doors in the main entrance are
to he carved with figures, the sub
jects of which have not yet been
chosen.
