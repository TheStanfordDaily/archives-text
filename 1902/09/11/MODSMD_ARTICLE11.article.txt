# The
						Flagstaff Arrives.
## 
### 
The flagstaff, in two sections—
one, one hundred and four feet; the
other, one hundred and twelve and
a fourth feet —each section occupy
ing three flat-cars, arrived at the
Palo Alto freight station yesterday
and was hauled to the end of the
avenue today. The lower staff is
two feet thick at the base; the sec
ond, one foot. The lower staff will
rest ten feet in the foundation and
overlap the other thirteen feet. This
will give a total length of one hun
dred and eighty-seven feet. The
staff will he painted immediately,
and putin place within the next
two weeks.
