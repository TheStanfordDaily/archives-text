# Registration at Berkeley.
## 
### 
Official figures of registration at
the University of California have
been given out from the recorder's
office. The number of undergradu
ates is 2314, compared with 2076
for last September. There are 119
students registered from without
the State, while last year the num
ber was 85. The entering class
numbers 672, which is 157 more
than last year.
