# Debaters Elect Officers.
## 
### 
Nestoria and Philolexian Liter
ary Societies met for the first time
this semester Saturday night and
named the following officers:
Philolexian. — President, C. K.
Studley, '03; vice-president, C. B.
Wintler, '04; secretary, E. E. Mil
ler, '04; treasurer, M. A. Thomas,
'04; sergeant-at-arms, W. W.Copp,
'03; representatives in the Inter
society Debating League, L. E.
Harter, '01, P. Martin, '03, R. B.
Knight, '03.
Nestoria.—President, R. W. Ev
erett, '03; vice-president, C. H.
Hatton, '03; secretary-treasurer,
Paul Swing, '05; sergeant-at-arms,
J. H. Page, '03; representatives in
the Intersociety Debating League,
E. W. Rice, '02, E. G. Riste, '03,
A. G. Thompon, '03.
