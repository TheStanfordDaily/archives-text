# Special Rate to San Jose.
## 
### 
A special rate of 70 cents for the
round trip to San Jose will be made
by the Southern Pacific Company,
Tuesday, September 16th, to accom
modate those who wish to see Buffa
lo Bill's Wild West Show. Tick
ets will be good returning on the
special train .leaving the Narrow
Guage depot at 11:30 p.m., and on
all regular trains the 17th.
