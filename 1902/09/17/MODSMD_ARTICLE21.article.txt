# MRS. CHLOE LESLIE STARKS.
## 
### 
Mrs. Chloe Leslie Starks, ex-'97,
and instructor in Drawing, studied
at Corcoran Btudio in Washington.
For the last six years she has been
doing scientific drawings for Dr.
Jordan, the Smithsonian Institute,
and the British and National Mu
seums. Mrs. Starks illustrated
Dr. Jordan's ''Matka and Kotik."
