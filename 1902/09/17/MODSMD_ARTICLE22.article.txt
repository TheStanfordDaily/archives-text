# MISS STELLA ROSE.
## 
### 
Miss Stella Rose returns as
instructor in charge of Itoble gym
nasium, which was under the charge
of Miss March last year. Miss Rose
is not a stranger at Stanford, hav
ing been a member of the class of
'99. Until 1901 she was iu charge
of the physical training at the
Marlborough School in Los Angeles.
