# Roble Gymnasium Notes.
## 
### 
—Two hundred and thirty-five
are entered for work.
—One hundred and fifty-five
girls have registered for basket-hall,
which will begin next week.
—The freslunan class contains
better material for basket-ball work
than have any of its predecessors.
—No cuts will be allowed this
year in Roble gymnasium, the in
structors having dropped the sys
tem which gave each girl four cuts
a semester. Illness is the only ex
cuse for non-appearance at class.
