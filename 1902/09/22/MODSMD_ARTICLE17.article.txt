# Resolutions.
## 
### 
The following resolutions have
been adopted by t lie Executive
Committee of the Associated Stu
dents:
Whereas, The just and all wise
creator has seen fit to remove from
our midst our highly respected Pro
fessor George Mann Richardson,
Librarian Herbert Charles Nash,
friend lliley C. Storey, and fellow
students Alfred Bailey Sill, Charles
Dudley Montgomery, and Theodore
Goodman Hosmer, and
Whereas, The sorrow which falls
on their respective families is in no
small measure visited upon us;
therefore, be it
Resolved, That we wish to express
to them the great esteem in which
we held these beloved professors,
friends, and fellow students for their
sterling qualities and noble char
acters; and, be it
Resolved, That we extend to them
our heartfelt sympathy in this, their
hour of bereavement, and com
mend them to Him who alone can
give aid and comfort; and, be it
further
Resolved, That a copy of these
resolutions be spread on the min
utes of the student body, and pub
lished in the Daily Palo Alto.
B. E. Nourse,
H. L. Hamilton,
C. K. Studley, Chm.
Committee.
