# Untitled Article
## 
### 
Numerous inquiries have been
occasioned by the rather indefinite
nature of the announcement of the
Plug Ugly competition. Several
prospective authors have expressed
themselves as unwilling to enter a
contest where work and glory con
stitute the chief rewards, and have
called attention to the fact that
the Plug Ugly committee neglected
to state whether or not there is to
be a prize offered, or what the prize
is to he. If the committee intends
to offer a prize, or regards such a
thing as a stimulus to effort, the
announcement should be made at
once if the manuscripts are to be
ready by October Ist.
