# Reception to Freshmen.
## 
### 
TheYoung People's Union of Palo
Alto gave their annual reception to
the freshman class on Friday eve
ning at the Baptist Church. Selec
tions were given by a quartette,
Miss Werry, and Miss Williams,
and by the Union Mandolin Club.
The address was given by Rev.
Mr. Jenness.
Each of the Palo Alto churches
will give a reception to the new
members of their denominations
within the next two weeks.
