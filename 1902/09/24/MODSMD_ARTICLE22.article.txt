# Brown Adopts Three-Year Provision.
## 
### 
The one hundred and thirty
ninth year of Brown University
was ushered in on September
17th. According to the an
nual address of President
Faunce, Brown follows Harvard
and Pennsylvania in allowing
students to take twenty hours of
work a week and to graduate in
three years. Provision is also
made that those who are able to
graduate early in their fourth year
may be able to gain the master's
degree by the end of that year.
