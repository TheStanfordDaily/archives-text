# Foil and Mask Election.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the "Foil and
Mask" last evening an election
took place with the following re
sults: President, C. T. Craig, '03;
secretary-treasurer, D. M. Reynolds,
'05; leader, E. V. Kehrlein, '05.
The club decided to hold its meet
ings every Wednesday at 8 p.m. in
Encina gymnasium. Several new
men were tried out but have not
yet been elected to the club. The
annual exhibition will probably
occur next spring, but there will be
no exhibition this fall.
