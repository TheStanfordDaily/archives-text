# Friday Night's Rally.
## 
### 
The second football rally of the
season, held Friday night in En
cina Hall, proved to be a record
breaker in the way of enthusiasm.
"Dad" Moulton and Captain Lee
were the only speakers of the even
ing. Both expressed confidence in
the teams, and appreciation of the
way in which training rules are
being kept. W. Wirt, '06. F. R.
Wheeler, '04, and R. 1). Fleming,
'05, contributed stunts, and the
Band added to the entertainment
by playing several selections.
