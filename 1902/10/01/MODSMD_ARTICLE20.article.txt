# Announcements.
## 
### 
Festival Choir. —The Festival
Choir meets every Friday in the
chapel at 4:30.
President at Home. — Piesident
and Mrs. Jordan will he at home to
the faculty and their friends on
Saturday evening, October 4th.
Freshman Meeting.—There will
he a meeting of the Freshman class,
Thursday, 1:15, in the chapel.
Omar C. Spencer, Pres. '04,
Girls' Glee. — The Girls' Glee
Club will meet at Roble Hall on
Thursday afternoon at 4:30 sharp.
All members are requested to be
present.
Geological Society. — Meeting
Friday evening, October 3d. room
105 Encina. Mr. Hoover will pre
sent a paper, "The Sack of the
Boulder Vein." President.
Women's Employment Bureau. —
Permanent office hours, 11:15,
Monday, Wednesday and Friday,
in the women's sitting-room (un
der Library building.)
Kathryn Romer Kip.
Plug Ugly Meeting.—Meeting
of the Plug Ugly board in room 23,
3:30 Thursday, for the purpose of
reading submitted MSS. All
authors of MSS. are expected to be
present. Chairman.
Y. M. C. A. Dues. —For the con
venience of persons owing member
ship dues or subscriptions to the
Y. M. C. A., J. S. Burcham, O. C.
Spencer, R. B. Hummel, and H. F.
Coolidge will carry receipt-books.
Orchestra Rehearsal. — The
Orchestra meets for practice on
Thursday night at 7:30 in the
chapel. Two oratorios of Rossini
and Meyerbeer will be rehearsed,
and a full attendance is requested.
Song and Yell Competition.—
Those entering songs or yells in the
football prize competition should
hand copy to the following com
mittee on or before October Bth:
J. C. Taylor, Cleveland Baker,
Clarence Bubb, chairman.
General Ciioir. —The members
of the General Choir are requested
to meet in the organ gallery of Me
morial Church on Thursday at 4:30
to decide on the most suitable hour
for future meetings.
Arthur L. Scott Brook.

been received at the University

C. Smith; Cases on Conflict of
Laws, Beale; Latin Grammar, Ben
nett; Flora of Western Middle Cal

Grammatik, Paul.
