# Quads.
## 
### 
—E. C. Rittenhouse is ill with a '
touch of malarial fever.
—Mrs. Stanford left San Fran
cisco this evening for New York.
—The latest in the line of clubs
is a German Conversation Club,
the members of which are residents
of Encina.
—The property of the late Prof.
George Mann Richardson has been
appraised. It consists of life in
surance policies of the value of
$5,000, and a house on the campus
worth $3,000. The inventory and
appraisement were filed in the
superior court by Charles D. Marx,
L. R. Lenox, and S. W. Young.
