# Eastern Football.
## 
### 
—The spirit of the students of
Phillips Exeter Academy is shown
by the fact that $1,600 was sub
scribed for the maintenance of foot
ball this semester, at a recent stu
dent meeting.
—The Yale football team will
play twelve big games this year
with the following institutions:
Trinity, Tufts, Amherst, Wesley an,
Brown, Bowdoin, Pennsylvania
State College, Syracuse, West Point,
Bucknell, Princeton, and Harvard.
—At the government Indian
school at Carlisle, Pennsylvania,
an Esquimaux by the name of Ne
kifer Schouchuck plays center on
the eleven. He weighs only 165
pounds but is strong and fast and
is said to play like Lone Wolf, the
great center of former years.
