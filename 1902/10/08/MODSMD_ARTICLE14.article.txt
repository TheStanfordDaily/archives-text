# Schedule Is Changed.
## 
### 
Several changes in the football
schedule have been made within
the last few days. The tie between
the Senior and Junior teams will
be played on Monday afternoon in
stead of on Friday, as announced
last night. The date of the decid
ing class game will also be set for
ward. A match game between
Hoitt's School and the Freshmen
will take place on Wednesday of
next week, the last contest before
the Berkeley game. The game
scheduled with the Alumni for
Thursday of next week will occur
on the Friday following.
