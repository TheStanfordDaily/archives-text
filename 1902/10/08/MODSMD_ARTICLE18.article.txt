# Avery-Wood.
## 
### 
Miss Clara Avery, '98, was mar
ried in Palo Alto at 1 o'clock today
to W. J. Wood, of Chicago. The
ceremony was performed by Rev.
E. D. McCreary, of Santa Cruz.
Miss Avery was an English
major, and after graduation taught
in the Santa Cruz high school. For
the last two years she has taught in
the Palo Alto high school. The
bridal party left for Chicago late
this afternoon.
