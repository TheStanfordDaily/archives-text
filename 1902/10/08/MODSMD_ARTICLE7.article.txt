# Y.W.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
Miss Harriet Brown led the
Y. \V. C. A. missionary meeting
held in the chapel yesterday after
noon. She read an interesting
paper written hy Mrs. Howard
Taylor, a missionary in China,
which gave account of the ready
acceptance Christianity finds among
native women.
