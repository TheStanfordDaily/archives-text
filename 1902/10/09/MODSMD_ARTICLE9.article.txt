# Saturday Night Club Organization.
## 
### 
The Saturday Night Club met at
1:15 yesterday and elected the
following officers: President, Miss
Cary, '05; vice-president, Miss Ev
erett, '03; secretary-treasurer, Mrs.
Paesmore, '03. Miss Grace Holt,
'04, was received into membership.
There will be a regular meeting of
the club on Saturday evening at
7:30.
