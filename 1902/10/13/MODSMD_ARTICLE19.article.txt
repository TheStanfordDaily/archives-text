# To Consider "Quad" Deficit
## 
###

1:15 to hear the financial report
from the 1903 annual. It is under
stood that this will reveal a deficit
of considerable size, for which no
assets exist hut the resources of
class organization. Just what ac
tion may be taken cannot be fore
told, as the matter has not yet been
presented, and many did not even
know that the Quad had failed to
pay expenses. There will prob
ably be a big attendance at the
meeting.
