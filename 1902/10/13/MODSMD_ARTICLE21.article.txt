# Quads.
## 
### 
—Miss L. Pearl Green, classifier
in the Library, has returned to her
work, after several days of illness.
— Over one thousand persons at
tended the recital in the Church
yesterday, attracted by the organ
program and Miss Mary Ferrell's
singing.
—The rally held in Encina last
Friday evening did not come up in
numbers and enthusiasm to its
predecessors. No speeches were
made. The Band was out and led
the singing.
—At a meeting held in Ruble
Saturday evening the Junior girls
decided on a red leather band,
burnt with the numerals '04, to
wear on the white felt hat recently
chosen as the class emblem.
