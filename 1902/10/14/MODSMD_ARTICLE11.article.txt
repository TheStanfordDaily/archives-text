# Announcements.

Sophomore Minstrel Committee
meets at Training-house Tuesday,
1:15. H. J. Edwards.
Foil and Mask.—The Foil and
Mask will not meet Wednesday 011
account of presentation of the
Spanish play.
Minstrel Committee.—There will
he a rehearsal of the Minstrel Show
Thursday evening at 7:30 at the
Phi Delta Theta house.
Evolution Quiz. —Written quiz
in Zoology 15, Bionomics, Wed
nesday, Oct 15th.
E. B. Copeland.
"Quad" Assessments. —I shall he
in room 10 on the quad from 1:00
till 1:30 Wednesdays and Thurs
days to collect Quad assessments.
A. S. Henley, Mgr.
Prize Yell.—On account of the
few yells submitted, the competi
tion will be left open until Wedneg'
day noon, October 15th. The prize
is $2.50. B. C. Bubb, Chairman.
Women's Employment Bureau. —
Permanent office hours, 11:15,
Monday, Wednesday and Friday,
in the women's sitting-room (un
der Library building.)
Kathryn Romer Kip.
