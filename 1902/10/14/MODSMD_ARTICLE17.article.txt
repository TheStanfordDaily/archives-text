# Exhibition at Studio.
## 
### 
The exhibition of Prof. George
Noyes' paintings will open to the
public next Friday, October 17th,
and continue for one week. The
pictures will be shown in the room
of the Studios nearest the Book
store each day from 10 to 12 a.m.,
and from 1:30 to 4:30 p.m. All
visitors are welcome, and no lover
of beautiful pictures should miss
thischance to view Professor Noyes'
work.
