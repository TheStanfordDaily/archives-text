# Freshmen Adopt Yell.
## 
### 
The Freshman class met this
afternoon for the choosing of a
class yell. Two yells were sub
mitted to the committee consisting
of I. J. Bounds, P. P. Bliss, B.
Matthews, J. L. Maloy, and R. E.
Naftzger. The following slogan
was chosen by the committee and
adopted temporarily by the class:
Ricketty, Racketty!
Re, Ro, Rix!
Rah! Rah! Stanford
Naughty-Six!
P. B. Smith, J. H. Alvord, and
C. B. Osborne were appointed as
a committee on permanent organi
zation.
