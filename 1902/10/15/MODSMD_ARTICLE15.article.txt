# Minstrel Plans Progressing.
## 
### 
Work on the Sophomore Minstrel
Show has been rushed until now
the program is about complete.
The show will be managed by
George Tuslc, of Fischer's Theater
in San Francisco, assisted by
Harold Tracy, '04. Both are com
petent men. Task has been in the
business for twenty years, and
Tracy, who is from Lehigh, has
managed the minstrel shows for
that university for a number of
years. A parade of the actors.will
take place before the show that
evening, and headed by the Band
it will march about the campus.
The prices of admission will be
25, 35 and 50 cents.
