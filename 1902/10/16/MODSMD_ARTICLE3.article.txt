# FOOTBALL RALLY!
## 
### 
The Rally and Send-off to the Freshman team will
take place tomorrow night. It will begin at 7:15 p.m.
in- Encina club-room with song and yell practice, fol
lowed by a bonfire rough-house on the baseball field.
Every man in college is supposed to be there and
make a noise. Every Freshman, who is not a mem
ber of the team, is expected to help in the building of
the bonfire. Report to E. C. Beach, in charge.
