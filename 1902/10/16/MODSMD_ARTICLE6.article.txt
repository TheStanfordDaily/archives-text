# Tuesday Evening Recital.
## 
### 
Fannie Dam-Hilton, whose pu
pils give the free recital in the
Assembly Hall next Tuesday
evening, Oct. 21st, has traveled
abroad for three or four years and
studied under some of the best
European masters, among whom
was Sebastino Breda, the talented
composer and teacher, of Milan,
Italy. The San Francisco Dra
matic Kevicw says of her: "Fannie
Dam-Hilton is without doubt one
of the most artistic singers and ac
complished teachers of California."
There are prospects that the
Girls' Glee Club will make arrange
ments with her to act as instructor.
