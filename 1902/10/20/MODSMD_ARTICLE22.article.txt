# Freshman Rally.
## 
### 
A large crowd turned out to the
football rally which was held Fri
day evening in honor of the Fresh
man team. The first part of the
rally took place in Encina club
room with much yelling and sing
ing. The men then went to Roble,
danced the serpentine to the band
music there, and returned to the
baseball field, where a huge bonfire
bad al ready been built. Here the
members of the team spoke of their
prospects in the game, and Dad
Moulton and Captain Lee also gave
encouraging talks. Good spirit
and much enthusiasm was shown
at the rally.
