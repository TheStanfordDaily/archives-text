# Rally at Mayfield.
## 
### 
The Republicans of Mayfield are
to hold a rally tomorrow night,
beginning at 8 o'clock. Hon. Jos.
C. Campbell of San Francisco will
address the meeting. Bonfires,
fireworks, and the University Hand
will help enliven the occasion. A
free bus will leave Encina Hall at
7 p.m., and return after the meet
ing.
