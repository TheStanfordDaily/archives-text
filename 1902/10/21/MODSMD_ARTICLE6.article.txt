# Residence of Chaplain.
## 
### 
The construction of a colonial
cottage for Dr. David Charles Gard
ner, recently appointed assistant to
Rev. Ileber Newton and chaplain
of the Memorial Church, will begin
soon on the vacant lot on Alvarado
row between Dr. Stillman's resi
dence and the training-house.
Contracts were let today, and work
will be rushed. The new addition
to the row is to be of very attract
ive design and of a style new to the
campus. It will be two stories
high and will contain seven rooms.
There will be a spacious porch on
the east side. The dimensions are
51 by 43 feet.
