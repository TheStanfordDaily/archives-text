# School for Campus Children.
## 
### 
A primary school is to be built
on Salvatierra street opposite
Robert Loser's house. The build
ing is to be a frame structure, pat
terned somewhat after the mission
style, and is to be sixty feet long
by forty feet wide. This school is
to be built by Mrs. Stanford for
the sole use of the children of
campus residents. Contracts are
let, and excavations will be started
soon.
