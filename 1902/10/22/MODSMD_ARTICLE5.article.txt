# Dr. Jordan Presents Exhibits.
## 
### 
A number of articles have re
cently been turned over to the Mu
seum by Dr. Jordan, and will be
placed on exhibition in a few days.
Among these are clubs, spears, and
other implements of war from
I'ago Pago, Tutuila, Samoa. The
other Samoan articles are a cane
of the King of Samoa, Mata'afa,
presented to Dr. Jordan by that

monarch; a piece of kava-root used
in making the national drink,
kava; a stone axe formerly used in
digging out canoes; a fly-brush;
and a kava cup, made from a
cocoanut shell. Dr. Jordan has
also presented an interesting auto
graph letter from President Diaz of
Mexico.
