# New Fraternity House.
## 
### 
Ground was broken yesterday for
the foundations of the. new house
to be erected by the Kappa Sigma
fraternity on Lasuen street adjoin
ing the Kappa Kappa Gamma
house. This addition to the row is
to be a frame structure of colonial
style and will be three stories high,
with accommodations for twenty
men. The lower floor embraces a
large reception-hall, study, library,
dining-room, and kitchen. The
two upper floors will contain the
sleeping-rooms. It is the intention
to have it ready for occupancy by
the first of next semester.
