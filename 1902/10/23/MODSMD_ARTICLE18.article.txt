# Inauguration at Northwestern.
## 
### 
The installation of Edmund J.
James as president of Northwestern
University began yesterday. Some
of the scheduled speakers at the
inauguration are President North
rop of Minnesota, President Bash
ford of Ohio Wesleyan, President
Thwing of Western Reserve, Dean
Briggs of Harvard, President Ray
mond of Wesleyan, President An
gell of Michigan, President Remsen
of Johns Hopkins, and President
Harper of Chicago.
