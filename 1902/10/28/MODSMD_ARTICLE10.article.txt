# Will Wear Red Hats to the Game.
## 
### 
The rooting hats this year will
be in two grades, one of red felt
and lined, the other a cheaper
grade of the same material. The
shape of the hats will closely re
semble those of last year, with the
exception that the crowns will be
higher. The hats are also to be of
better material than last year's
headgear.
Men desiring hats are requested
to givo their size to the committee,
consisting of B. E. Nonrse, H. Ham
ilton, and E. C. Beach, before 6
p.m. Friday.
