# Roble Seniors Entertain.
## 
### 
The Senior girls of Roble Hall
entertained the Sophomores at a
full-dress ball Saturday evening.
A rooters' section of Juniors and
Freshmen sat upon the stairs out
side the parlors and added to the
fun by their comments upon the
occasion. This is the first event
that has brought class spirit among
the girls into play.
