# Dr. Flugel Returns from Europe.
## 
### 
Dr. Ewald Fliigel, head of the
English Philology department, who
left Stanford on May Ist for Eu
rope, reached home yesterday after
noon. Dr. Fliigel sailed from New
York on May 15th. His summer
was spent in England and Ger
many, partly in resting, partly in
working at the British Museum
and the Bodleian Library, Oxford.
While at Oxford he finished his
"History of English Philology."
In Germany he did some work in
the Goethe Archives at Weimar.
On Oct. Ist he left Hamburg for
home. Dr. Fliigel reports a very
pleasant and profitable vacation.
Several publishing houses have ar
ranged with him for his writings.
