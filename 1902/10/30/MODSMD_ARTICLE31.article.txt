# Spalding-Polhemus.
## 
### 
In San Francisco last evening
occurred the wedding of Miss Mary
Polhemus and Dr. Alfred Baker
Spalding, '96, assistant football
coach last year. Dr. Spalding is
now engaged in the practice of
medicine in San Francisco. While
in college he was a member of
Sigma Alpha Epsilon fraternity,
and end on the '95 and '96 Var
sities.
Miss Polhemus is a sister of. John
H. Polhemus, ex-'9B. Dr, Harry
B. Reynolds, '96, was best man,and
Louis S. Beedy, '97, and Emerson
Warfield, '03, ribbon bearers.
