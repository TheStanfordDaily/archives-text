Sunday Sermon. — l)r. Jordan
will preach in the chapel at 11 a.m.
Choir Rehearsal. — Festival
Choir will rehearse in the chapel
Friday, 4:00.
Evolution Quiz. —The usual
optional oral quiz in Evolution
Friday. E. B. Copeland.
New Books. —The following have
heen received at the University
Bookstore: The Rivals, and School
for Scandal, Sheridan.
Minstrel Show Rally.—Bring
your song-books with you to the
Minstrel Show tomorrow night.
Rally during the intermission.
Quad Photos. —Those Juniors
who have had pictures taken please
select as soon as possible the proofs
they want printed.
A. S. Henley, Manager.
Y. \V. C. A. At Home.—The
young ladies of the Y. W. C. A.
Club will be at home to their
friends on the afternoon and even
ing of Saturday, Nov. Ist, instead
of Nov. Bth.
Rooters' llats may be obtained
at the Haberdasher's on and after
Friday, Oct. 31st. The committee
requests all students to obtain hats
before the rally of Thursday, No
vember 6th.
University Fees. —All labora
tory and syllabus fees are due
November Ist. If not paid by that
time an additional fee of $2 will be
charged. O. L. Elliott,
Registrar.
Caps and Gowns. —Orders for
caps and gowns for Senior girls
may be left either at the Bookstore
or at the Haberdasher's, Palo Alto.
Those ordering from the latter may
meet a tailoress Monday from 4:30
to 5:30 p.m., room 10 quad.
Festival Choir. —All members
are requested to attend the prac
tice in the chapel at 4:30 Friday.
This will be the last occasion on
which new members will be ad
mitted. All subsequent rehearsals
will be held in the choir gallery at
Memorial Church. New members
will please report in person to
Organist Scott Brook.
University Assembly. — The
third hour of Wednesday morning
has not been fixed as the regular
hour for the University Assembly.
The first meeting will be held at
10:15 on November sth. The sub

sequent meetings before the Christ
mas recess will be arranged so as
not to conflict with the same
classes.
Fue s 11 ma n Meeting.—Freshmen
will meet, chapel, 1:15, Monday,
Nov. Bd, for the purpose of voting
sweaters to the Freshman team
and arranging for the Freshman-
Sophomore debate.
Ray Howard, Pres.
