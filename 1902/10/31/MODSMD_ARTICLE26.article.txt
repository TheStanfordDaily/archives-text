# Guild Executive Committee.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Student
Guild on Wednesday evening, the
following persons were chosen to
serve as an executive committee:
Dr. Snow, H. F. Coolidge, '03, J. S
Burcham, '02, Miss K. R. Kip, '03,
Professor Sanford, and Miss O. S.
Tarbell, '04. The committee will
hold a meeting shortly and elect
officers for the year.
The membership of the Guild is
increasing rapidly. The financial
condition is good, at present there
being over $650 in the treasury.
