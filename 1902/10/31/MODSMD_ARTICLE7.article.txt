# Ushers at the Game.
## 
### 
The men who are to usher at the
Intercollegiate contest next Satur
day have been chosen by the man
agement. As in other years, the
remuneration will be railway fare
to and from the city
to the game. Those who will seat
the Stanford sections are as follows;
Thomas Coen, F. F. Gundrum,
James Knapp, Charles Peach, H.B.
Hawkins, F. G. Smith, E. C.Beach,
G. M. Beach, L. C. Hawley, O. Gib
bons, F. G. McElroy, K. Richard
son, J. S. Burcham, James Mori,
0. C. Spencer, F. S. Holman, T. E.
Stephenson, C. W. Thomas, F. A.
Brown, A. S. Henley, B. P. Oak
ford, B. R. Cocks, W. W. Swing,
H. M. Elmore, M. L. Walker, J. H.
Page, A. B. Crane, G. A. Waring,
W. E. Billings, R. J. Sterrett.
