The success of the Sophomores
in their entertainment of Friday
evening is particularly gratifying
in view of the seriousness of the
tennis court dilemma from which
it goes a long way toward extricat
ing us. With such a sum received
by the efforts of a single class, the
ultimate securing of the whole
amount is assured. But there are
still many dollars lacking, and the
manner in which the student body
responds to a further call will be
an admirable test of its loyalty.
In the meantime, congratulations
to the men whose volunteer efforts
made the show a success. Launched
as an experiment, the scheme has
already proven its right to be
added to the calendar of annual
events.
