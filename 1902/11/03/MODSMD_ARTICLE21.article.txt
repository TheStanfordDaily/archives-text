# Funeral of Schwartz, '99.
## 
### 
The funeral services of Charles
Ernest Schwartz, '99, who died last
Thursday morning, were held in
the First Unitarian Church at Ala
meda Saturday afternoon at 2:30
o'clock. They were conducted by
Rev. Benjamin Fay Mills. The
pall-bearers were Charles C.
Hughes, superintendent of the Ala
meda schools, A. H. Suzzallo, Stan
ford, '99, George I). Kierluff, prin
cipal of the Mastick school, J. W.
Young, principal of the Haight
school, and representatives of the
Woodmen of the World. Inter
ment will take place at Elkville,
Illinois, Schwartz's home.
