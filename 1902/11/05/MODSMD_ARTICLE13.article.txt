# Minstrel Show for Public Schools.
## 
### 
Mrs. Hilton, principal of the
public schools of Palo Alto, has
made arrangements with H. R.
Tracy, '04, director of the Sopho
more Minstrels, to give a minstrel
show. The show will, in the essen
tial points, be the same as the Soph
omore show given last Friday night.
The proceeds will go to the benefit
of the schools of Palo Alto. The
date of the production is the 22d of
November.
