# REFEREE CHOSEN.
## Bert Oliver, Last Year's Official, Will Serve Again This Season in the Same Capacity. 
### 
Bert Oliver, the Olympic Club
iriftu who acted as referee in last
year's intercollegiate game in a
manner completely satisfactory to
both Stanford and California, has
been chosen to serve in the same
capacity this year. Mr. Oliver
has an enviable reputation both as
to athletic ability and in his thor
ough cooperation with anything
that is productive of honest sport.
He was captain and tackle on the
Reliance'Club two years ago, and
has been prominently identified
with Coast football for the past
decade.
