# Meeting of University Men.
## 
### 
The meeting of the Associated
Universities of America will be
held this year in New York on the
30th and 31st of December. This
organization is the outcome of a
movement organized three years
ago by the University of California
for the purpose of bringing together
at stated intervals representatives
of the several universities of the
country.
The program for the meeting will
be one of exceptional interest.
Papers will be presented by Cor
nell, Pennsylvania, and Harvard.
In addition to this there will be a
report on the membership of the
organization by Yale. The social
side of the meeting will include a
luncheon to delegates, given by
Columbia University, December
29th, and a dinner to be given at
Sherry's on Tuesday by Columbia
University alumni council.
