# "S" on Goat Island.
## 
### 
Under cover of darkess Friday
night an enormous red "S" was
placed on the precipitous western
bluff of Goat Island in San Fran
cisco Bay, where it is in full view
of passengers on the Oakland
ferry. The feat required consider
able courage. The island is gov
ernment property and the shore
line is patrolled at all times by
armed guards. In addition to this,
the precipice is above deep water,
and presents a particularly diffi
cult task to the climber.
