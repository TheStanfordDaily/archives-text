# Reminiscences of Stevenson.
## 
### 
The Faculty Woman's Club met
yesterday afternoon in Palo Alto
at the home of Mrs. Guido Marx.
Prof. V. L. Kellogg talked on the
subject "Robert Louis Stevenson in
California and Samoa." Professor
Kellogg has visited many of the
places in the State where Steven
son lived and worked. While on
the Samoan expedition last sum
mer he with Dr. Jordan and others
made a pilgrimage to Mt. Vala,
the writer's South Sea home. Rem
iniscences of these journeys and
trips made up the subject of the
address.
