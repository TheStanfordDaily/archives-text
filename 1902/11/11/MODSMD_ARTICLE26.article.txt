# Postponed Hallowe'en Set for Friday.
## 
### 
The party prepared by the joint
Christian Associations for Hal
lowe'en evening and postponed on
account of conflicting dates, will he
given on Friday evening next. The
preparations originally made have
been somewhat elaborated. Among
the features will be charades, ping
pong, music readings, gypsy for
tune telling, and chestnut-roasting.
The social will be given at the
home of Miss Needles and the ad
joining home of Dr. Little, corner
Kingsley and Waverly streets, Palo
Alto, and a general invitation is
extended to all University men
and women.
