# Dr. Branner Addresses Geologists.
## 
### 
The Geology Society held an open
meeting last evening at which
Doctor Branner spoke to all the
seniors of the department, upon
"The Essential Points Relative to
Map Making and a Geological Sur
vey in General." The lecture was
illustrated with pictures and model
maps. The speaker closed by ad
vising the students as to their use
of reference-books and hand-books
after graduation.
