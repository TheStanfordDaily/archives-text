# Y. W. C. A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The meeting of the Y. W. C. A
yesterday was lead by Miss L. M.
Toothaker, '04, who gave a short
sketch of mission work in India,
Japan, and China. Miss Pearl
Bartlett, '04, sang "Just for Today."
The Y. W. C. A. has a new mis
sionary library. Those who wish
to join the missionary reading circle
should at once hand their names to
Miss Helen Sailsbury.
