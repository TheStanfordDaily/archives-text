# New York's University Club.
## 
### 
The University Club in New
York is said to be the most mag
nificent club in the world. Its
membership, resident 500 and non
resident 1,500, is made up of col
lege men —wholly graduates—of
every college in the country. The
land upon which the club-house
stands cost $800,000, and the
buildings over $1,200,000. Item
ploys 200 servants at an expense of
$50,000 a year, and conducts a
restaurant the receipts of which
amount to $70,000 annually. In
the club-house are a library of
10,000 volumes, swimming tanks,
and every other convenience.
