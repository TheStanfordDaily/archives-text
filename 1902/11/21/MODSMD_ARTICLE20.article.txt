# "Finland" Presented to Library.
## 
### 
Mr. Anders Knoes, of Sevenoaks,
England, a graduate of the Uni
versity of presented to
the Library a file of Finland, an
English journal devoted to a dis
cussion of the political and social
condition of Finland, a subject in
which Mr. Knoes is especially in
terested. There are also articles of
Finnish literature with transla
tions from the Kalevala, the na
tional epic; the Kanteletar, the
collection of folk-songs; and from
the works of Runeberg, Topelius,
and other Finnish writers.
