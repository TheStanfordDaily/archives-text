# Special Thanksgiving Rates.
## 
### 
A special rate of one and one
third fare for round trip has been
offered by the Southern Pacific
Railroad to students for the
Thanksgiving recess. The mini
mum rate will be $5. Certificates
may be obtained from the Regis
trar.
Tickets to San Francisco at $1.25
for round trip will be issued the
Wednesday before Thanksgiving.
These tickets will be good until the
following Monday.
