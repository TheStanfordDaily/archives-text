# Many Singers at Choir Rehearsal.
## 
### 
The rehearsal of the Festival
Choir last Friday afternoon was at
tended by about a hundred singers,
of whom the Glee Clubs formed a
good part. Mr. J. J. Morris, of
Palo Alto, led the singing for the
first time this semester and spoke
to the students upon the different
hymns to be sung at the dedication.
The choruses practiced were "The
Marvelous Work," "Achieved is the
Glorious Work," "The Glory of the
Lord," and"The Hallelujah
Chorus."
