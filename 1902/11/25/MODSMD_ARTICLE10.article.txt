# Change in Library Hours.
## 
### 
Until further notice the Library
will open at 8 a.m. on Saturday
and close at 3:30 p.m. Books may
be taken out after 2:30 on that
day. During the Thanksgiving
recess the Library hours are to be
as follows: Wednesday, November
26th, 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.; Thursday,
closed; Friday, 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Saturday, 8 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
