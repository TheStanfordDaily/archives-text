# Washington Students Organize.
## 
### 
Twenty-five University students
from various parts of the State of
Washington met this afternoon and
effected a permanent organization.
The purpose of the society will be
to arrange plans for association be
tween Washington students, that
they may keep in touch with one
another.
An effort will be made to arouse
interest in the towns which the
Stanford musical clubs will visit
on their Christmas tour.
The oflicers elected were: Roy O.
Hadley, '03, president; P. C. Har
per, '03, secretary.
