It is significant that of the ten
fatalities reported to have resulted
from football this season, none of
the institutions concerned were
colleges of standing. The summary
tends to confirm the oft-repeated
assertion that it is the physically
unfit who are subject to injury in
the rough-and-tumble game. An
examination of the list by those
who decry college football would
show them that though the game
be severe, when men are prepared
for the contest the outcome is not
attended with serious injury.
