# Wisconsin Coming to Coast.
## 
### 
Clifford G. Roe, manager of the
Wisconsin football team, is in Los
Angeles consulting with President
Charles M. Coleman of the Carni
val Association of Pasadena. He
wishes if possible to arrange for a
California-Wisconsin game on New
Year's Day at Pasadena, and has
already made an offer to Manager
Decoto.
According to present plans the
Wisconsin eleven will arrive in San
Francisco in time for a game with
Utah or the Perris Indians on
Christmas Day.
