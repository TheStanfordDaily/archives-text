# Work of Drawing Students.
## 
### 
During the afternoons of this
week there will be on exhibition in
the east end of the Studio building
some of the work done this semester
by students of the Drawing depart
ment. This work consists of draw
ings of the head done in charcoal
from living models and still life in
oil colors. The department an
nounces that everyone interested is
cordially invited to attend. The
hours are 1:30 to 4:30 p.m. The
exhibition closes at 4:30 p.m. on
Friday.
