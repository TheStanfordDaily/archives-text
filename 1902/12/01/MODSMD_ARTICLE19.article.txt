# Entertainment for English Club.
## 
### 
The members of the English
Club are to be favored with a mu
sical treat next Wednesday night.
Professor Mary Roberts Smith will
sing a series of old English ballads,
accompanied on the piano by Mrs.
Perry Roberts. Mrs. Smith will
give a brief talk on the different
ballads, and follow the talk with a
vocal interpretation. The meeting
is to be held at the Zeta Psi house,
beginning at the usual time, quar
ter of eight.
At seven o'clock there is to be a
rehearsal of the partial cast for the
"Knight of the Burning Pestle" in
the English Seminary room, Li
brary.
