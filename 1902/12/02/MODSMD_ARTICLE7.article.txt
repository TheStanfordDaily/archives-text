# Student Body Managers Will Meet.
## 
### 
Tomorrow Treasurer Edwards
will meet Manager Ezra Decoto of
California for a final settlement of
the finances of the intercollegiate
game. The advisability of bring
ing out a football team from the
East for a vacation game will be
discussed. It is not probable that
either university will accept any
of the many propositions made by
managers of Eastern elevens.
