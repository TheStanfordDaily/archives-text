# STUDENT BODY NOMINATION.
## T.E. Stephenson is Only Candidate for Editor-in-Chief of the "Daily Palo Alto." 
### 
At the student body meeting
this afternoon T. E. Stephenson,'o3,
was nominated for editor-in-chief
of the Daily Palo Alto by Thoreau
Cronyn, '03. No other, nomina
tions were made. According to the
constitution of the Associated
Students, the formal election will
be held on the quad next Wednes
day.
No other business coming before
the meeting, it was adjourned im
mediately after the nomination.
