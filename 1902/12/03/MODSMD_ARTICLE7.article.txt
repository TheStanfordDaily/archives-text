# Quads.
## 
### 
—Mrs. Margaret Collier Graham
and Miss J. E. Collier of Pasadena
are visiting on the campus.
—Dr. David T. Day of the
United States Geological Survey
visited the campus this afternoon.
—The Y. W. C. A. meeting yes
terday was led by Miss Theresa
Wilbur, '03, who described one of
the girls' mission schools in Persia,
and read several letters from one of
the teachers there.
—Organist Scott Brook spent
the day tuning the Memorial
Church organ. One of the attrac
tive features for next Sunday's re
cital will be the singing of Miss
Agatha Cummings of San Jose.
