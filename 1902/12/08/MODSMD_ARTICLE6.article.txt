# Utah Students Organize
## 
### 
On Friday evening the Stanford
students registering from Utah met
at the home of Miss Grace Bruck
man, '04, in Palo Alto and organ
ized a Utah Club. This organiza
tion has the double purpose of fur
thering the interests of Stanford in
Utah and aiding the students who
enter from that State. The follow
ing officers were chosen: President,
Miss Grace Bruckman, '04; vice
president, B. A. Perkins, '06; secre
tary, Miss F. R. Jackson, '06.
