With each successive gathering
of students and faculty at the Uni
versity Assemblies the value of the
institution becomes more evident.
Successful as were the first and
the second, it may safely be said
that the meeting held this morn
ing eclipsed both of the earlier
ones. We have grasped more fully
the spirit of the occasions. Today
as heretofore we were especially
fortunate in the personnel of the
speakers. Notable men and not
able addresses are apparently to
become a tradition of the Assembly.
