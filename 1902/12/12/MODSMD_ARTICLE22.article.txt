# Assembly in January.
## 
### 
At the last Assembly Dr. Jordan
announced as speaker at the first
Assembly next semester Booker T.
Washington, president of Tuskegee
Industrial School, Tuskegee, Ala
bama. The date was inadvert
ently given as January 18th, in
stead of January Bth. The famous
colored speaker will come to Cali
fornia early in January, and his
first appearance at Stanford will be
on the Bth.
