# Y. W. C. A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The subject discussed at the
Y. W. C. A. meeting yesterday was
"A Wholesome Spiritual Life."
Miss Chloe Anderson and Mrs.
Walker sang. Miss Spencer gave
a short sketch of the "Consumer's
League," whose object, is to dimin
ish sweat-shop labor, and secure hy
gienic conditions for factory labor
ers, especially women and children.
In response to an invitation from
the Palo Alto branch of the League,
the Y. W. C. A. voted to join as a
society. Chaplain Gardner spoke
briefly on the topic of the day.
