# MUSICAL CLUBS OFF.
## Northern Concert Tour Begins Tonight with Appearance in Sacramento. 
### 
Twenty-nine men composing the
Glee and Mandolin Clubs left to
day on the noon train for Sacra
mento, where the first concert of
the Northern trip will be given.
The musicians will be accompanied
by Manager Edwards and Advance
Agent F. W. Morrison. The Clubs
are to be absent about twenty-four
days, returning to the campus
January 14th.
