# Changes at Encina Gym.
## 
### 
It is the intention of the Encina
gymnasium management to in
crease the efficiency of the physi
cal training work by raising the
requirements for admission to the
two-hour section next semester.
None but men well equipped for
teaching will be allowed in the
Tuesday and Thursday classes.
The scope of gymnasium work will
also be increased by the addition
of instruction in boxing and
wrestling.
