# Saturday Night Club Election.
## 
### 
The Saturday Night Club at its
last meeting elected the following
officers for the second semester:
President, Miss Elizabeth Everett,
'03; vice-president, Mrs. Maud
Passmore, '04; secretary-treasurer,
Miss Beulah R. Thomas, '05; assist
ant secretary-treasurer, Miss Grace
Holt, '04. At the next meeting of
the club, January 10th, the "Rela
tion of the Government to the
Indians" will be studied. The
members of the club will not
devote much time to debating until
the last part of the semester, but
will continue the usual papers and
book reviews as at present.
