Miss Florence Heywood, A. 8,, Stanford
The Department of Music oi the Thoburn School.
We call the attention of students who desire
to continue their study of music while at the
University to the excellent, department of
music of the Thoburn School at Palo Alto.
This new department of the promising young
institution is directed by Mr. Arthur Ficken
schor, who teaches piano and harmony, the in
struction in vooal music being in the hands of
Mrs. FicUenscher. Mr. FicUenscher does not
need an introduction to the faculty and students
of Stanford. He is recognized as one of the
foremost pianists and teachers of music on the
Pacific Coast. As a composer, he ranks among
the first in this country. In her recent visit to
San Francisco, Frau Schumann lieincU, the
famous contralto, asked it as a speoial favor
that Mr. FicUenscher dedicate several of his
songs to her.
To bo instructed by an artist like Mr. Fioken
scher is a privilege of which many will gladly
avail themselves.
