# Mathematicians Gather.
## 
### 
At the annual meeting of the
San Francisco section of the Ameri
can Mathematical Society, held
December 20th at Berkeley, the
following men represented the
Stanford department: Professors
R. E. Allardice, H. F. Blichfeldt,
L. M. Hoskins, G. A. Miller, and
Instructor W. A. Manning.
