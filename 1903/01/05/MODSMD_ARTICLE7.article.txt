# Stanford Professors Honored.
## 
### 
At the closing session of the
Philological Association of the
Pacific Coast, assembled at the
Hopkins Art Institute in San Fran
cisco, December 21st, Stanford was
well represented in the following
list of officers chosen for the next
year: President, Prof. A.T. Murray;
vice-presidents, Prof. Julius Goebel
and W. A. Merrill; secretary and
treasurer, Prof. J. E. Matzke; exec
utive committee, Prof. 11. R. Fair
clough, E. B. Clappe, A. T. Lange,
J. E. Church, Jr.
