# Coolidge Acting President.
## 
### 
Herbert F. Coolidge, '03, was last
evening appointed by the cabinet
of the Y. M. C. A. to assume the
duties of president of the Associa
tion in the place of Augustine
Jones, '04, who is in the hospital
with appendicitis. The appoint
ment of Coolidge is only tempor
ary. A permanent president will
be elected at the next meeting of
the Association.
