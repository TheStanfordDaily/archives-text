# Executive Committee Meeting.
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of the
Associated Students met last night.
A committee, consisting of Miss
Stella Rose, instructor in Roble
gymnasium, and Miss Cecelia Ath
erton, '03, met them in behalf of
the Women's Athletic Association,
asking the student body to fix
grounds for the game of hockey
which the Association wishes to
make a part of women's athletics.
No action was taken.
