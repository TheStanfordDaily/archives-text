# Addresses by Washington.
## 
### 
Mr. Booker T. Washington ad
dressed an audience of eleven thou
sand people at Mechanics Pavilion,
San Francisco, last night. This
afternoon he visited Mills College,
and tonight he is scheduled for an,
address at the Pacific Seminary,
Berkeley. Tomorrow evening he
will be at Sacramento. Sunday
evening he will deliver two ad
dresses, one at the First Congrega
tional Church of San Francisco and
one at Germania Hall, Oakland.
Monday evening there will he a
visit to Stockton, and Tuesday to
the University of California. He
will be entertained by the Unitar
ian Club of San Francisco Tuesday
evening and will leave for Tuskegee
by way of Chicago Wednesday.
