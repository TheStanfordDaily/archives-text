# Quads.
## 
### 
—The registration in Encina
gymnasium to date is 296.
—Miss Elizabeth Everett, '03, is
teaching school near Mountain
View.
—The name of the Press, a Palo
Alto weekly paper, has been
changed to the Palo Altan. S. W.
Charles will remain in charge.
—Mr. F. P. Stone, president of
the Bancroft-Whitney Co. of San
Francisco, has presented the Uni
versity Library with a very fine
set of Harper's Monthly Magazine.
—Last evening several candi
dates for the leader's course in the
gymnasium took an examination.
Fifteen, about half of those who
tried, were admitted to the course.
