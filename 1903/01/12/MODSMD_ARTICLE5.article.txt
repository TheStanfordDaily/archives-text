# Informal Reception.
## 
### 
Next, Saturday evening an in
formal public reception in honor of
Dr. and Mrs. Newton and Chaplain
and Mrs. Gardner will be given
at Roble under the direction of the
University Christian Associations.
There will be no prepared program
beyond, possibly, a few musical
selections. The affair will no
doubt be attended by a majority of
the University community, includ
ing faculty and students. It is de
signed to introduce the new clergy
men in a personal way to as many
members of the student body and
faculty as possible.
