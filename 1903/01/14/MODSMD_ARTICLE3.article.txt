# "Billy" McLeod Will Return.
## 
### 
"Billy" McLeod, the genial
Scotchman who rubbed the sore
muscles of the track and baseball
men and the bruises of the football
men for four seasons, will be on
the campus by February 1. While
on his northern trip, Treasurer lid
wards completed arrangements
with McLeod engaging him for the
spring season. McLeod has been
in business in Portland, Oregon,
since lie left Stanford at the end of
the track season of 11)01. He has
been connected with the Mult
nomah Athletic Club and had in
charge the crew which they sent to
Astoria last summer.
