# RECEPTION POSTPONED.
## The 						Affair Planned in Honor of the University Clergymen Will Not Occur Tomorrow Night. 
### 
The reception at Roble planned
to be given by the Christian Asso
ciations in honor of Dr. and Mrs.
Newton and Chaplain and Mrs.
Gardner tomorrow evening has
been indefinitely postponed owing
to the illness of both Dr. Newton
and Chaplain Gardner.
