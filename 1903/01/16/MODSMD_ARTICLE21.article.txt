# Communication.
## 
### 
Editor Daily Palo Alto:
I have at different times beert
asked to include special numbers
in the Sunday recital program. I
am always willing to play suitable
selections, though often it may not
be convenient to play more than
one or two selections in an after
noon's recital. I should always
like to know the name of the per
son making the request.
Arthur Scott Brook.
