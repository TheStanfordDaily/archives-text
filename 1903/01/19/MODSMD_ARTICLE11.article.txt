# Palo Alto Horses to Be Sold.
## 
### 
Forty-nine two-year-olds from
the Stanford Stock Farm will be
sold at auction by Fred H. Chase
& Co. at San Francisco Thursday
evening, January 29th. The
horses will be shipped to San
Francisco next Monday. Those
remaining on the Farm will be dis
posed of as soon as they can be put
in proper shape.
