# Changes in Dedication Program.
## 
### 
The program for the dedicatory
exercises of Memorial Church has
been changed in the following in
stances since last night's issue of
the Daily Palo Alto: Rev. R. C.
Foute of Grace Church, San Fran
cisco, will read the second lesson in
place of Dr. C. R. Brown. The
concluding prayer of the morning
services will be spoken by Rev.
M. E. Hill of First Baptist Church,
Oakland, in place of Rev. Foute.
