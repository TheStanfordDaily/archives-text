# Encina "At Home."
## 
### 
The second "At Home" of the year
of the Encina men will be given in
the club-room Saturday evening,
February 7th. A committee consist
ing of C. Naramore, '03, L. G.
Moore, '03, and E. C. Beach, '05,
has been appointed by C. K. Stud
ley, '03, president of the Club, to
make arrangements. .
