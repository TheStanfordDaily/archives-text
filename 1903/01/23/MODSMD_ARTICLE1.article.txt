# CLASS PRESIDENT ELECTED.
## 1903 Chooses L.G. Moore to Fill the Position of Honor for the Semester. 
### 
By a vote of eighty-three to
seventy-four L. (J. Moore defeated
W. G. Morrison this afternoon and
was elected president of the Class of
IUO3. The election wus very close
and the deciding votes were not
counted until the very lust.
L. G. Moore, the successful candi
date, registers from Kinsman, Ohio,
and is a Physiology major. He
has always taken great interest in
athletics, playing on his class base
ball team for three years and on
his class football team in his soph
omore year. Moore has been a
member of the University Band for
three years.
