# Rev. Charles R. Brown Honored.
## 
### 
The members of the First Con
gregational Church of Oakland
have increased the salary of their
pastor, Rev. Charles R. Brown,
from $5000 to $6000 a year. This
action was taken at a meeting in
Oakland last night, when the con
gregation gave expression to their
appreciation of their pastor's serv
ices. Dr. Brown is a lecturer in
Stanford University and this
semester is giving a course in "So
cial Ethics."
