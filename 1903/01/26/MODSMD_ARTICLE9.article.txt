# Evening with Maximilian.
## 
### 
At the next meeting of theSpanish
Club, to be held at Roble next
Wednesday evening, Instructor
Cook of the English department
will read those portions of nis new
book, "Roderick Taliaferro," in
which Maximilian, the emperor of
Mexico, figures as a character. This
will be the first of two meetings
devoted to the subject of Maximil
ian. At the second Prof. C. A.
Duniway of the History depart
ment will read an interesting
paper.
