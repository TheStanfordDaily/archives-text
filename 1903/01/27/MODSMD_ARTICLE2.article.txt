# UNIVERSITY ASSEMBLY.
## Tomorrow at the Fourth Hour, Students and Faculty Will Listen to William Greer Harrison and J.F. Lanagan, '00. 
### 
The second assembly of students and faculty this semester will be
held tomorrow at the fourth hour. The meeting will he devoted to
the consideration of athletic questions, the speakers being authorities
on the subjects which they will discuss. William Greer Harrison will
speak on "Pure Athletics"; James F. Lanagan, '00, on "The Athletic
Relations of the University and Secondary Schools."
The Glee Club will sing "Fill Your Steins.''
