# Large Track Squad at Berkeley.
## 
### 
The largest track squad in the
history of the University of Cali
fornia signed with Captain Cado
gan Friday for the spring season.
There are one hundred and ten
men now in training, and of these
fifty-three are freshmen. The old
men who have returned are Cheek
and Cooley for the jumps and
hurdles, Redewell for the mile, and
Tibbetts for the two-mile run.
Work will begin on the track as
soon as the weather permits.
