# PASSES ASSEMBLY.
## Bill for Determining Legal Status of University Trusts and Amendments Passes One Branch. 
### 
The following telegram, signed
by Geo. E. Crothers, '95, was re
ceived this afternoon at the Daily
Palo Alto oflice:
"Our bill passed Assembly unan
imously today, Assemblymen
Black, Wright, Johnson, Busman,
and Brown having given valuable
assistance."
The bill referred to is that which
provides that the Trustees of the
University be empowered to com
mence proceedings to determine
the legal status of the various
trusts and amendments of the Uni
versity under the provisions of the
educational biils of 1885 and 1887.
