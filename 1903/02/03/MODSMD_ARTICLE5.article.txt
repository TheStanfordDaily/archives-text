# Goes to Printer Saturday.
## 
### 
The first copy for the Quad will
go to the printers Saturday. It
will include the copy for the frater
nities, the debating, and organiza
tions departments. The secretaries
of Euphronia,Nestoria, Philolexian,
and Saturday Night Club have not
yet mailed their lists of members
to the board, while several fraterni
ties are still delinquent. The art
work for these departments is be
ing rushed, as it must be in the
hands of the engravers within a
week.
