# Y.W.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Y. W.
C. A. was held yesterday after
noon in the west transept of Me
morial Church, where the Christian
Association hereafter will meet.
Staff Captain Fynn of the Salva
tion Army gave a brief history of
the organization, dwelling with
special emphasis on its social as
pect. He spoke of institutions
where prisoners are befriended till
they find employment, orphanages,
rescue homes, and refuges for sol
diers who have just returned from
the Philippines. Ensign Simpson
spoke briefly on the same subject.
