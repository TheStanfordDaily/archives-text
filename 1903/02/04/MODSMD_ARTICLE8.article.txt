# Juniors Offer Prize for Farce.
## 
### 
The Junior Farce Committee met
at Ilohle last evening and voted to
offer a cash prize of twenty-five
dollars for a farce to be presented
in Assembly Hall on March 26th.
It was decided that all manuscripts
must he submitted by February
28th in order that a decision may
be reached by the committee before
March 2d. It is planned to devote
more than the usual amount of
time this year to rehearsals. The
farce will be under the supervision
of a professional stage director.
