# California Notes.
## 
### 
—The women tennis players at
Berkeley have gone into active
training. Among the most prom
ising candidates for the team are
Helen Weeks, who is an excellent
player, Itoee Moskowitz, and Ethel
Ilatcliff, the present college cham
pion.
— From Chicago comes the report
that Northwestern University and
the University of California will

meet on the gridiron in that city
next Thanksgiving. Manager De
coto of California, however, has not
yet received any offer from the
Northwestern people.
—Dick Williams, the former
California pitcher, has accepted an
offer of $300 per month to pitch for
the Columbus team next season.
He refused an offer of $400 per
month from Philadelphia, prefer
ring to work yet another year in
a minor league.
