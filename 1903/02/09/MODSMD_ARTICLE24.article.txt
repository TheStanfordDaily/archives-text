# Contract for the Oil Tanks.
## 
### 
The Goodman Stone Company,
of San Francisco, has received the
contract for the construction of
four large concrete oil tanks to be
erected on the campus. Two of the
tanks, excavations for which are
already partly completed, are to go
in the rear of the Science Building
of the Outer Quadrangle, while
the others are to be - built back of
the boiler house. These tanks are
to be used for storing crude oil.
Each will have a capacity for fif
teen hundred gallons, and in di
mensions will be sixteen feet deep
and fourteen feet in diameter.
