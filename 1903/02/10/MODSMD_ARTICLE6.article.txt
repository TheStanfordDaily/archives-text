# Arrival of Machinery.
## 
### 
Among the machinery recently
received by the Mechanical En
gineering department are : A 100
horsepower surface condenser; a
(50 horsepower jet condenser; a 100
horsepower and a 60 horsepower
feedwater heater, both of the Gou
bert pattern; and two pumps from
the Snow Steam Pump Co. for feed
ing the boilers. The iron work for
the platform and stairs around the
condenser-pit are now being erected
by the students of the department,
the intention being to give the stu
dents all the practical experience
possible.
