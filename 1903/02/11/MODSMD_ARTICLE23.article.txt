# Gives a Reception.
## 
### 
Miss Catherine Harker and Miss
Florence Heywood, '02, entertained
last evening nearly two hundred
of their friends of the University
faculty and of Palo Alto. They
were assisted in receiving by Mrs.
David Starr Jordan and Mrs. Mary
Roberts Smith. Other ladies who
entertained the guests were Miss
Edith Jordan, '97, Mrs C. A. Dun
iway, Mrs. Mathews, Mrs. Ruby
Green Bell, Mrs. Wilbur Thoburn,
Mies Alice Dickinson, '05, Miss
Elizabeth Hughes, and Miss Ther
esa Wilbur, '03.
