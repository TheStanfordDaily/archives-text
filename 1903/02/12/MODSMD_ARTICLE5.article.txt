# Senior Week Affairs.
## 
### 
The finances of Senior Week
will have to be very closely
guarded. The Finance Committee
has asked each of the other com
mittees to make an estimate of
their needs, after which the amount
of the assessment on members of
the class will be known.
Order-books have been issued the
chairmen of the committees,and no
bill will lie honored unless accom
panied by one of these orders signed
by the chairman of the respective
committee, the chairman of the
finance committee,and the treasurer
of the class.
The Farce Committee yesterday
decided to offer a prize of $50 for a
farce, it has called for the manu
script March 15th, earlier than
usual in order that the farce may
be Btaged properly.
