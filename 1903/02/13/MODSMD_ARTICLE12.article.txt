# Announcements.
## 
### 
Senior Track Men —Meet on
oval Monday at 4:30 for election
of captain.
Kappa Kappa Gamma —Will
not have their usual "at home"
Saturday eve.
English Play Cast —Will meet
at Donaghho's studio Saturday at
10:15 for photographs in costume.
"Intercollegian" Contributors.
—All articles and drawings for the
Intercollegian must be in before
February 20th.
Girls' Mandolin Club —Will
meet at Donaghho's studio Satur
day, February 14th, at 1:30 sharp.
Bring instruments.
Senior Statistics. —All Seniors
who have not returned Quad in
formation cards sent out some time
ago must do so at once.
Caps and Gowns. —The caps and
gowns ordered about January 23d
have been received and are ready
for delivery at the Bookstore.
Sknior Farce. —The Senior Farce
committee offers $50.00 for the best
farce. All manuscript must be in
the hands of the committee not
later than March 15th.
"Intercollegian" Contest. —The
contest for a prize poem for the
Intercollegian ends next Wednes
day, February 18th. No poem re
ceived after that date will be con
sidered.
At Home. —The young ladies of
the Y. M. C. A. Club will be at
home to receive their friends from
the hours of 3 to 6 in the afternoon
and 8 to 10 in the evening Satur
day, February 14th, 481 Lytton
avenue.
Prize for Song.—The Girls' Glee
Club offers a prize of ten dollars
for the best college song of a gen
eral nature. The competition will
close on Friday, February 20th.
The prize will not be awarded if no
song of sufficient merit is submitted.
Senior Class Bills. —No bills
will be honored by the Senior class
unless accompanied by an order
signed by the chairman of the re
spective committee, the chairman
of the finance committee, and the
treasurer of the class.
W. W. Copp,
Chairman Finance Committee.
Guild Dues. —Attention is called
to the fact that all membership
fees to the Students' Guild must be
paid by Monday, February sth.
After that date no further dues
will be received. The fees may be
paid to Miss K.lt.Kip,Miss O. Tar
bell, Hans Hark an, Frank Hess,
J. S. Burcham, or at room 95, or
the women's waitin-groom under
the Library from 1 to 1:30 and 2 to
2:30. J. S. Burcham, Pres.
