# Change in Founders' Day.
## 
### 
The customary exercises hereto
fore held on the evening of Found
ers' Day, March 9, will be changed
this year to the morning, and in
stead of occurring in the Univer
sity chapel will take place in
Memorial Church. The date falis
on Monday, but a memorial pro
gram is to be carried out on Sun
day morning. Dr. Newton will
preach a memorial sermon.
