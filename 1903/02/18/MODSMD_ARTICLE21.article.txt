# Scottish Band Matinee.
## 
### 
The afternoon of March 3d or
4th under the auspices of the Y. M.
C. A., the Kilties, Canada's famous
Highlanders' hand, will give a

matinee concert and exhibition in
Assembly Hall. The company,
consisting of about thirty men, will
play at the Alhambra Theater next
week. The Stanford matinee will
be given as the company goes
through to San Jose.
The Kilties has a vocal chorus of
twenty voice?. The choir and band
excel in their own peculiar national
music. Bagpiping and dancing
will enhance the program of Scot
tish airs and " stunts."
