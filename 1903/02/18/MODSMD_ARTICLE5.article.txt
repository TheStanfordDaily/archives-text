# New Inn Steward.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Inn Club
directors held last evening the res
ignation of T. W. Strowbridge as
steward of the Inn was accepted,
and H. A. Hunter was chosen to be
his successor. The new steward
has had extensive experience in his
line of work. He has been con
nected with the hotels and restau
rants in Paris, in English cities,
and in New York and other Ameri
can cities.
