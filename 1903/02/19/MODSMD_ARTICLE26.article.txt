# The 						College World.
## 
### 
—Fifty-two candidates reported
for lacrosse at Columbia.
—The University of Pennsyl
vania has commenced work on a
new gymnasium.
—Nearly one hundred candidates
for baseball have reported for
practice at Michigan.
—The name of Michigan's paper
has been changed from the Michigan
Daily News to the U. of. M. Daily.
— Michigan has nineteen thou
sand living graduates. Thebiegest
percentage of these received their
degrees in law.
— Five thousand dollars was
raised by voluntary subscription at
the University of lowa recently to
wipe out a debt on the Athletic As
sociation.
