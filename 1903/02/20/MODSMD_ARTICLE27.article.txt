# VARSITY MEETS DEFEAT.
## Santa Clara College Nine Yesterday Wins Spirited Contest in Last Inning. 
### 
With the game well in handout
to the sixth inning in the contest
yesterday at Santa Clara with the
Catholic college nine the Stanford
Varsity was defeated in a good
struggle by the score of 6 to 5.
Sales was in the box for the tirst
eight innings, and struck out seven
men. Parker went in the last in
ning and, after striking out the
first two men, gave two bases on
balls. Farry followed with a clean
single over second and scored the
winning run for the home team.
The contest was a good exhibi
tion of ball, although there was
little chance for excitement.
