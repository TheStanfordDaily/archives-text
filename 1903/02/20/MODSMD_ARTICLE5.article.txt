# CONTEST FOR CUP.
## Tournament on the Roble Courts for First Honors in Tennis. 
### 
Tomorrow morning, beginning at
nine o'clock, on the Roble courts
preliminaries will be held for the
Mary Roberts Smith cup. A large
number of entries have been made,
and close competition and hard
games are expected. Miss Mary
Hodge, '03, has had her name en
graved upon the cup the last two
years. Among others who will this
year try for the honor are Misses
Mary Foster, '04, Dorothy Abbott,
'06, Ella lbs, '03, Mary Hayward,
'05, and Minnie Mirsky, '04.
