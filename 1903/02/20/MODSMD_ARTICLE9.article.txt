# Reception to Harter.
## 
### 
Monday evening a farewell re
ception will be given by the Y. M.
C. A. in rooms 64 and 65 Encinn,
in honor of Lloyd E. Harter, '01,
who recently resigned from the
general secretaryship of the Y. M.
C. A. to take up organization work
for the national committee of the
Epworth League. He will leave
the University the latter part of
next week.
