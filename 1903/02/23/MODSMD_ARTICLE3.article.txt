# Girls' Tennis Preliminaries.
## 
### 
Miss Mabel Ray, '05, was the
winner in the first of the prelimi
naries played for the Mary Roberts
Smith cup on the Roble tennis
courts on Saturday. The remain
ing preliminaries and the finals
will be played next Saturday.
The results of the contests were
as follows: Miss Abbott, '06, de
feated Miss Esther Crandall, '03,
6-2, 6-3; Miss Ray defeated Miss
M. E. Hendrick, '03, 6-0, 6-2; Miss
J. M. Hay ward, '06, defeated Miss
Minnie Mirsky, '04, 6-1, 6-2; Miss
Mary Foster, '04, defeated Mrs.
Katherine Loeser, '04, 6-2, 6-1.
Miss Ray had the better of Miss
Abbott in the semi-finals, and Miss
Foster disposed of Miss Mirsky.
Miss Ray's match with Miss Foster
resulted in Miss Ray's favor, 6-2,
7-5.
