# Stanford Wins Toss.
## 
### 
This afternoon E. W. Rice, '02,
for Stanford, and C. F. Stern for
the University of California, tossed
up to see which side should submit
a question for the Intercollegiate
debate. Rice won. He will sub
mit the question March 7th at 5
o'clock by wire. California will
have the choice of sides.
