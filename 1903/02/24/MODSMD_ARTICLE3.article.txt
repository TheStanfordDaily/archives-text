# ASSEMBLY AT THE THIRD HOUR.
## Dr. Jordan and Principal Reid of Belmont School Will Speak to the Students and Faculty of the University Tomorrow. 
### 
At the third hour tomorrow the
first Assembly for a month will be
held. The visiting speaker is to be
William T. Reid, principal of the
Belmont School, at Belmont. Dr.
Jordan will speak briefly.
Mr. Reid graduated from Har
vard, and for some time was presi-

dent of the University of California.
In 1885 he established the Belmont
School. He has spoken on the
campus several times, last ap
pearance at the University was in
the fall, when he gave an address
in the chapel on "College Tradi
tions."
