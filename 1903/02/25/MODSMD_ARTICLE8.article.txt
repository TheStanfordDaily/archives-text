# Band Arranges for Dance.
## 
### 
The University Band has de
cided to hold its dance an the eve
ning of March 6th, the arrange
ment committee, consisting of I.
Anderson, '04, K.Tuttle, '06, 11. U.
Fitting, '04, G. H. Beebe, '04, and
G. L. Moore, '03 have decided that
the tickets, the number of which
has been limited to one hundred,
will be put on sale the first of next
week and will be $1.50 each. Mu
sic will be furnished by the Band.
