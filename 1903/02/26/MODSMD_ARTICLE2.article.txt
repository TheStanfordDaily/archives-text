# Debate in Spanish.
## 
### 
Miss Milnora Roberts entertained
the Spanish Club at its regular
meeting last night. The program
was informal and consisted of
music and of an impromptu debate
on the question, "Resolve, Que mas
vale saber quehaber." The affirm
ative was supported by Miss Ella
lbs. 'OG, Carlos Cortes, '05, and H.
Skinner, 'Oti; while Miss Irene A.
Wright, '04, Instructor Manning,
and V. R. (iarfias, '06, spoke on the
negative side, and won the debate.
