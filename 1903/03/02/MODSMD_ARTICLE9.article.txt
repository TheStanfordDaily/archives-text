# Reception to Clergy and Their Wives.
## 
### 
The halls and parlors of Roble
were hung with nets, smilax, and
evergreens and decorated with doz
ens of palms and plants for Friday
night's reception by the Christian
Associations to the pastor and
chaplain of Memorial Church and
their wives. Several hundred
students met Dr. and Mrs. Newton
and Chaplain and Mrs. Gardner.
Informal music and refreshments
made the evening pans quickly.
The Girls' Glee Club sang "Hail,
Stanford, Hail" and "Alma Mater."
