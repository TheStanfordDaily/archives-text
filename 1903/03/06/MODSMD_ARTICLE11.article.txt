# Announcements.
## 
### 
Gikls' Glee Club —Will not
meet Monday afternoon.
At Home. —Kappa Alpha Theta
at home has been postponed to Sat
urday, March 14th.
Orchestra Rehearsal.—A spec
ial rehearsal of the orchestra will
take place Monday at 7:30.
Senior Farce. — Competition
closes March 15th. Manuscripts
must be in the hands of the com
mittee on time.
Musical Clubs. —Members of the
musical clubs will meet at the
University Club in San Francisco
at 6:30 Friday evening.
Syllabus Fees. — All syllabus
and laboratory fees are due March
10, and if not paid by then an addi
tional charge of $2 will be made.
Faculty at Home. — President
Jordan and Mrs. Jordan will be at
home to members of the faculty and
friends Saturday evening, March
7th.
At Home. —For the rest of the
semester, and beginning this month,
Alpha Phi will be at home
on the third Saturday instead of
first Saturday in the month.
"Quad" Illustration. — All
kinds of photographs of the Uni
versity and josh photos suitable for
use in the Quad may be sent either
to Isaac ilussell or B. R. Cocks.
Labor Bureau.— Any one hav
ing typewriting which they wish to
have done may leave the work with
L. G. Russell, who may be seen

Wednesdays, Fridays and Satur
days from 10 to 11:15 in room 60.
Work will be done promptly and
satisfactorily.
Women's Employment Bureau. —
Ladies having sewing or mending
to be done will please send word,
(by mail) to K. R. Kip,
Women's Employment Bureau.
St. Thomas' Day.—A solemn
high mass will be celebrated at St.
Thomas' Church, Palo Alto, Sun
day. Father O'Neal of the Order
of Preachers will deliver the ser
mon. All are cordially invited.
Faculty Women's Club—Will
meet at the home of Mrs. W. A.
Kimball, Lincoln avenue, Palo
Alto, Monday, March 9th, at 3.
Prof. A. B. Clark will give a talk
on "The Ideals of Pictorial Art."
History 15 Special Examina
tion. —Those whose absences from
the recent examinations, February
11th and 18th, were excused, will
have an opportunity to make up
the same Saturday, March 7th, at
9 o'clock, in the History seminary
room. Max Farrand.
