Today the track men commenced
the observance of rules much more
stringent than have been required
of them u [) to the present time.
In the keeping of these rules the
friends of the candidates may do
much to help them. A man should
never be urged to keep late hours;
he should not be offered candy or
pastry; he should not be tempted
to eat between meals.
