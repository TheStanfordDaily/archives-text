# Literary Societies.
## 
### 
Euphronia.—Set speech, J. Cole
man, '05. Regular debate: '"Re
solved, That international bi
metalism would be beneficial";
affirmative, A. M. Dibble, '05, W.
F. Dunn, '04; negative, H. A.
Moran, '05, W. H. Thomson, '05.
Nestoria. — Regular debate: ''Re
solved, That the United Stairs
should allow European govern
ments to seize the land of the
debtor nations to the amount of
their claims"; affirmative, T. S.
Gray, '00, E. G. Riste, '04; nega
tive, C. W. Hatton, '03, \V'. E. Hil
lings, '03.
Philolexian. — Regular debate:
"Resolved, Thai the United States
should maintain the integrity of
China by arms if necessary";
affirmative, G. Thomas, '04, O. S.
Lousley, '05; negative, C. K. Stud
ley, '03, C. F. Dittmar, '03.
Saturday Night Club. — Regular
debate: "Resolved, That Senators
should he elected by direct vote of
the people"; affirmative, Miss A.
Miner, '04, Miss E. I. Reynolds,
'04; negative, Miss B. Thomas, '04,
Miss M. Cutter, '04.
