# Junior Day "Chaparral."
## 
### 
A special colored supplement to
go with the Junior Day Chaparral
is being prepared by 11. W. Borough,
ex-'Ol. It will consist of an or
iginal design symbolizing the fea
tures of the week. In addition to
the supplement the editors are at
tempting to secure a collection of
light verse and prose that will
make the issue the best of the col
lege year. Contributions will be
received until March 20th.
