# "Chaparral" Election.
## 
### 
Tomorrow night the first aunual
election of new members to the staff
of the Chaparral, under the provi
sions of the recently adopted con
stitution, will be held in room 10
Encina. Selections to the staff
will be purely on a basis of work
done for the paper during the pres
ent school year. There are four
vacancies to fill and about fifteen
men win se contributions make
them logical candidates. At this
same election, officers of the board
will be chosen for next year, and
Senior members will retire to the
associate, or advisory board, com
posed of all members who have
ever been elected to the active
board.
This election offers the first prac
tical test of the working of a self
perpetuating board on a Stanford
publication.
