# Mormon Choir on Coast.
## 
### 
The Mormon Choir which sang
in Assembly Hall last spring will
be on the campus Saturday with
365 members. The choir left Utah
Monday, and was in Sacramento
today. It goes to Monterey tomor
row. From Monterey it goes to
San Jose, and Saturday will stop at
Stanford, the members taking
breakfast and lunch at the Inn.
An impromptu organ recital will
be given by Organist Scott Brook.
No arrangements have as yet been
made for a concert by the choir.
