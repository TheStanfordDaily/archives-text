# "Ulysses" Will Be Read.
## 
### 
Prof. S. H. Clark, liead of the
department of public speaking in
the University of Chicago, will read
Stephen Phillips' "Ulysses" in the
chapel at 1:30 Friday, March 20th.
Professor Clark is the author of
several hooks on the subject of lit
erary interpretation, and is known
in the East through his lectures
and readings. As an interpreter of
the spirit of literature he is, un
doubtedly, one of the foremost
readers in the country. A number
of Stanford men and women who
have heard him speak in high
terms of his work.
The recital will be under the
auspices of the English club. No
admission will be charged.
