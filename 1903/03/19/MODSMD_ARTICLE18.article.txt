# "Chaparral" Election.
## 
### 
The Chaparral board met last
night and elected the following
men to the staff: J. Burt Gilder
sleeve, '03, C. S. Thompson, '04,
Delmar Reynolds, '05. Officers for
the next year were chosen, as fol
lows: Editor, Isaac Russell, '04;
managing editor, B. C. Bubb, '04;
business manager, L. H. Roseberry,
'03; assistant business manager,
O. A. Wilson, '04.
