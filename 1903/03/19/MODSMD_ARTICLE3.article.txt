# ORCHESTRA'S PROGRAM.
## The 						Overtures, Choruses, Solos and Selections to be Given in Assembly Hall Tomorrow Night. 
### 
The program for the University
Orchestra concert which will be
given in Assembly Hall tomorrow
night, commencing at 8:15, con
tains numbers which will be listen
ed to with delight by lovers of good
music. Organist Scott Brook has
succeeded in getting together the
men who sang in the "Antigone"
chorus last year. They will sing
the "O Eros'' and "Bacchus"
choruses.
The program is in two parts, as
follows:
PART I
Overture, "Antigone" ...
Male chorus "Bacchus" (Antigone)
Male chorus, "O Eros" (Antigone)
... Mendelssohn
Overture, "Tancredi" ... Rossini
Soprano, "Il Baccio" ... L'Arditi
Miss Dorothy Goodsell
Overture, "Bohemian Girl" ... Balfe
Bass, "The Brigand" ... Spence
Mr. Harry H. Barnhart
Selection,"The Feast of Lanterns"
... Bennett
PART II
Overture, "Ernani" ... Verdi
Soprano, (a) "Summer" . Chaminade
(b) "Violets" ... Wright
Miss Dorothy Goodsell
Selection in Gavotte sty le,"Ricor
danza" ... Haig
Bass, (a) "A Fond Kiss" . Scott Gatty
(b) "Bonny Banks of Lock
Lomond" ... Old Scotch
Mr. Harry H. Barnhart
Overture, "Abu Hassan" . Von Weber
Accompanist
Mr. Arthur Scott Brook
