# Addition to Schedule.
## 
### 
Manager Edwards announces
two additions to the baseball sched
ule as it stands at present. To
morrow the Varsity will play the
Portland professionals on the
campus diamond, and on Junior
Day another game will be played
between the Varsity and Ireland's
Independents. The game sched
uled for March 12th between Port
land and the Varsity was post
poned on account of the rain. The
game with the Independents is a
recent addition to the schedule.
