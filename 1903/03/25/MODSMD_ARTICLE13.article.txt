# Election of Editor.
## 
### 
Tomorrow morning the second
election to decide the editorship of
the 1905 Quad will be held tit the
usual place from 10:15 to 12:45.
The candidates are I). M. Reynolds
and O. DuF. Kehrlein. The
election hoard will consist of F. M.
Wilbur, YV. VV. Gamble, W. H.
Evans, George Stevens, and H. C.
Grigsby, chairman. Voters will be
restricted to those who have paid,
the fifty-cent class assessment.
