# University Guides Appointed.
## 
### 
George Thomas, '04, and A. T.
Griffin, '06, have been appointed as
University guides, being on duty
alternately between the hours of 9
and 12 in the morning and land 5
in the afternoon. For the present
no substitutes will be appointed, as
was at first intended. The guides
are under the direction of Secretary
Clark and are paid by the Univer
sity. The system is in the nature
of an experiment as yet, and will
be changed to suit circumstances.
