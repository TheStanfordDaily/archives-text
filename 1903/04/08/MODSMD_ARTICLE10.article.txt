# WOMEN'S TENNIS.
## Match with California to Take Place Saturday on New Courts. 
### 
Final arrangements have been
made with California for the tennis
match to he held at Stanford Sat
urday, April 11th, at 9o'clock. The
contest will probably come on the
new Encina courts, as it is expect
ed that these will be finished be
fore Saturday. The outcome of the
tournament will be determined by
the total scores of the three matches,
instead of the best two out of three
games. The three California con
testants are Misses Ilatcliff, Wick
son, and Weeks. The Stanford
representatives are Misses Mary
Hodge, '08, Mable Kay, '05, and
Mary Hendrick, '03.
