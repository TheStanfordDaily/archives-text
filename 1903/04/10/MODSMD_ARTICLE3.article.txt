# Baseball by Musicians.
## 
### 
The Stanford and University of
California Glee and Mandolin
Clubs will play baseball on the
Varsity diamond at 11 o'clock to
morrow morning.
The Stanford line-up will be:
T. E. Palmer, '03, pitcher; George
Scoville, '03, catcher; J. E. Knapp,
'04, first base; C. 11. Baker, '04,
second base; H. L. Morrison, '03,
short-stop; H. E. Bush, '05, third
base; Harry Lanagan, '04, Mt
field; B. E. Nourse, '03 center field;
Brookes Sharp, '03, right field.
