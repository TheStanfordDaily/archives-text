# The
						"Pasmores" Concert.
## 
### 
Mary, Susan, and Dorothy Pas
more, who give concerts under the
name of "The Pasmores," will pre
sent one of their many popular
programs in Assembly Hall next
Thursday evening. They will be
assisted by Miss Anne Moore,
mezzo soprano, and Miss Zueletta
Geery, accompanist, who are regu
lar members of the organization,
and by Mrs. Florence Wyman
Gardner, who has a high soprano,
and Mr. A. E. Nowlan, tenor.
The program will be made up of
the finest music, butof such a char
acter as to give pleasure to all who
are fond of music, whether they
are well versed in the art or not.
