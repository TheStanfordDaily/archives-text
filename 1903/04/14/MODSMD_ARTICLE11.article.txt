# Will Not Wear Caps and Gowns.
## 
### 
Tlie senior men this afternoon
voted down a motion which called
for the wearing of caps and gowns
at the baccalaureate sermon, on
class day, and at the commence
ment exercises. Before coming to
a vote the proposers of the measure
asked that it he left to a two-thirds
vote, which was not secured.
