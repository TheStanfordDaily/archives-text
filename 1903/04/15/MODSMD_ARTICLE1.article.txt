# INTERCOLLEGIATE JUDGES.
## Debating Committee Chooses the Men Who Will Decide Debate. 
### 
The Intercollegiate Debating
Committee, of which J. F. English,
'01, is the Stanford representative,
has chosen the following men to
serve as judges in the Stanford-
California Intercollegiate debate:
Mr. William Thomas, a prominent
San Francisco attorney; Mr, Henry
\V. Brandenstein, one of San Fran
cisco's supervisors; and Mr. Peter
F. Dunne, a well known lawyer of
San Francisco. The dehiite will he
held at the Alhambra Theater in
San Francisco next Saturday eve
ning.
