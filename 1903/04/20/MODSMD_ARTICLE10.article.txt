# Graduate Students Meeting.
## 
### 
A very interesting social meeting
of the graduate students was held
Thursday in the parlors of Roble.
Save for a few moments devoted to
the consideration of business mat
ters, the evening was given over to
entertainment and good fellowship.
The following committee was
chosen to take charge of the re
organization of the graduates stu
dents at the beginning of the next
college year: Miss C. M. Lord,
Miss E. M. Jordan, Miss V. Town
send, Mrs. L. K. Mathews, W. A.
Manning, 11. Trader, and J. 11.
Council.
