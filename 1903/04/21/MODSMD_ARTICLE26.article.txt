# Concert in San Jose.
## 
### 
The Glee and Mandolin Clubs
will give two concerts in San Jose
Friday, one in the afternoon in the
San Jose high school at' 2, the other
in the Vendonie Hotel at 8:15.
The evening conceit is to be fol
lowed by a dance, to which all the
students of the University are wel
come. The city superintendent of
public schools has given the clubs
permission to use the high school
building for the afternoon concert,
and all the students of the city
schools and of the State Normal
will he allowed to leave classes in
order to attend.
