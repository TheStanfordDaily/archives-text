# LEAVE FOR SEATTLE.
## Debating Team Goes to Meet University of Washington Friday Night. 
### 
The Stanford debating team that
is to meet the University of Wash
ington in the second annual con
test at Seattle on the evening of
April 241h left the campus this
morning at 9 o'clock. They will
remain in San Francisco until to
night and will arrive in Seattle
Thursday evening. The speakers
who are to represent Stanford are
J. H. Page, '03, A. Sheriffs '05, and
W. C. Maloy, '03.
The men who have been chosen
to render the decision in the debate
are Judge Stiles of Tacoma, .ludge
Hallinger, and Harold M. Preston
of Seattle. All are prominent men
in the Northwest.
