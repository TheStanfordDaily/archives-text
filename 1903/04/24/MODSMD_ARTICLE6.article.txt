# Untitled Article
## 
### 
—Dr. Jordan left this afternoon
for Vallejo, where he will lecture on
"The Demands of the Nineteenth
Century."
—A social of the missionary
classes was held last night at Roble.
M. Shindo, '03, spoke on "Mission
ary Work in Japan." A delight
ful feature of the evening was the
quick and brilliant sketching that
was done by both Mr. Shumada
and Mr. Moriti, Japanese artists.
