# Theta Delta Chi Initiation.
## 
### 
The California chapter of the
Theta Delta Chi held an initiation
Saturday night, and initiated the
twelve men who are to form the
Stanford chapter. After the initia
tion fifty Bat down to dinner in the
Marble Room of the Palace. The
following men were initiated:
11. C. Brown, '04; M. F. Rey
nolds,'o4; W. G. Schulte,'o3; F.
F. Gundrum, '03; W. T. Ogier, 05;
R. Hudson, '06, S. L. Bacon, '03;
W. W. Edwards, '05; W. A. Cross
man, '04; Arch Preston, '04; Lee
Carroll Hawley,'o3; J. Knapp,'o4.
