# California Notes.
## 
### 
— Class day at Berkeley has been
set for May 12th, and the senior
ball for May 13th.
— Miss Eleanor Talcott, '05, has
heen elected captain of the Califor
nia Women's Basket-ball Club, and
Miss Delia Spencer, '06, manager.
— Contests are being arranged
between the University of Califor
nia Boat Club and the University
of Washington rowers. They will
be held at Seattle early in June.
