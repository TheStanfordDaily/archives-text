# Mrs. Stanford May Speak.
## 
### 
Mrs. Stanford will be present
and will address the students at
the laying of the '03 plate in the
quadrangle.
The exercises will occur Mon
day, May 25th, at 11:30 o'clock,
immediately following the class
day program in the chapel. G.
Winans, Senior class orator, will
make the speech dedicating the
plate, and President Jordan will
formally accept it. After Mrs.
Stanford has spoken, the assembly
will sing "Hail, Stanford, Hail."
