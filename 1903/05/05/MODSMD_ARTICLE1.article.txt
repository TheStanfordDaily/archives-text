# SENIOR WEEK PROGRAM.
## Schedule Arranged by Committee Smoker and Reunion Added to Regular Schedule. 
### 
At a meeting of the Senior Week
Committee yesterday afternoon, the
following schedule was arranged for
Commencement Week:
Thursday, May 21. —Senior Farce,
Assembly Hall, 8:15.
Friday, May 22. —Reception to the
Senior Class by Dr. and Mrs. Jor
dan, Xazmin house, 3:30 p.m.
Saturday, May 23. —Faculty-Sen-
ior baseball game, Varsity dia
mond, 10 a.m.
Smoker to Senior men, 8 p.m.
Reunion of Senior women, 8 p.m.
Sunday, May 24. —Baccalaureate
Sermon, Dr. Ileber Newton, Me
morial Church, 11 a.m.
Monday, May 25.—Class Day exer
cises,chapel, 10:30 a.m.
Plate exercises, quad, 11:30 a.m.
Senior Bali, Encina, 7:15 p.m.
Tuesday, May 26. —Alumni Day.
Promenade Concert, 8 p.m.
Wednesday, May 27. —Commence-
ment, address by Dr. Fliigel, As
sembly Hall, 10:30 a.m.
The committee early in the semes
ter decided to omit the alumni
smoker from the Senior Week pro
pram. Yesterday it left off the
Senior reception. There were added
two events which may become an
nual affairs, the smoker for Senior
men, the reunion for Senior women.
The reunion will be held in Ruble.
The place for holding the smoker
has not yet been determined upon,
on account of the sick men in the
Students' Guild hospital. It is
highly probable that the Ball will
be held in Mullen's Hall, Palo
Alto, instead of in the Encina club
room.
