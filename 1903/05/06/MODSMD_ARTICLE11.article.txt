# Quads.
## 
### 
—Work on the Roble hockey
field has been delayed and will not
he resumed until next semester.
— Miss Helen Salisbury, '05, has
been elected captain of the Roble
gymnasium basket-ball team for
next year,and Miss Mary Downing,
'04, manager.
— Among the decorations which
are being made preparatory to the
visit of President Roosevelt on next
Tuesday is a large arch over the
gates at the entrance.
