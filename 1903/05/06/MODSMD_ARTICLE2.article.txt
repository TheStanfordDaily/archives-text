# Glee Club Election.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Glee Club in
the chapel at 4:30 the following
were elected: Arch Perrin, '03,
president; C. L. Firebaugh, '04,
manager; B. Cocks, '05, and IP E.
Bush, '05, on the executive com
mittee. All the elections were
unanimous.
The Glee Club will sing "Hail,
Stanford, Hail," and "Stanford
Red" at the Assembly, May 12th,
at which President Roosevelt will
speak.
