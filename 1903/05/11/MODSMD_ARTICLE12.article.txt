# Announcements.
## 
### 
English 6. — Written report on
"La Belle Dame sans Merci" for
Wednesday, Mr. Cook's section.
Mandolin Club. — Meeting to
elect officers for next year, Tues
day, May 13th, in room 23 at 7:00
Capitola. — All girls desiring to
go to Capitola please send name
and date to Miss Salisbury as soon
as possible.
Boat Club —Will meet Wednes
day evening, May 13th, at 7:30, in
the chapel. Election of officers for
the ensuing year.
Senior Class —Meets at 8:15 in
chapel. The Senior schedule may
be definitely arranged at that time.
L. G. Moore.
Combined Musical Clubs. —Meet-
ing to elect officers for next year,
Tuesday, May 13th, in room 23 at
7:30. E. 1. Thayer, President.
Library Hours. —The Library
will be closed next week as follows:
May 12th, Tuesday, from 9 to
10:30, University Assembly; May
14th, Thursday, Memorial Day;
May 15th, Friday, 11:15 to 12:15,
University Assembly.
Mechanical Engineering 3. —
Those conditioned in "Constructive
Materials" last semester will be
given an examination Friday, May
15th, at 11:15 in room 504. This
will be the last opportunity for the
removal of such conditions.
Law Association. —The follow
ing men composing the Law Asso
ciation Committee on Address are
requested to meet tomorrow in room
10: Rittenhouse, Wintler, Ross,
Heinly, Bobbins,ll. M. Lewis, Mil
ler, 11. C. Jones, Woodruff, Beck
ham, McGilvrny, Dittmar, Nightin
gill, I jowenthal, Harris, Chappel,
Estes, Seibert, Green, Gonsalves,
E. W. Bice, chairman.
