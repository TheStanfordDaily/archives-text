# College Notes.
## 
### 
—The senior class assessment at
Princeton is $10 this year.
-—Swimming has been made a
required course for all freshmen at
Hamilton College.
—At a recent meeting, the Cor
nell trustees voted to duplicate
any amount up to $500,000 given
for dormitories.
—Twelve hundred dollars was
subscribed for the support of the
baseball team at a recent meeting of
the students of Exeter.
—The enrollment of the Scottish
universities is as follows: Edin
burgh, 2.114; Glasgow, 2,037; Aber
deen, 905; St. Andrew's, 264.
— Eight hundred and sixty-seven
students are enrolled in the Uni
versity of Michigan law school,
making it the largest law school in
the country.
