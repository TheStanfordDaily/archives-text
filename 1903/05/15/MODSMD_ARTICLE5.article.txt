# JUNIORS AID GUILD.
## Two Hundred and Fifty Dollars to be Given by Class to Help Typhoid Patients. 
### 
At a meeting of the Class of 1904
in the chapel this afternoon the
motion to give to the Students'
Guild out of the class funds two
hundred and fifty dollars was
unanimously passed. President
Blodget explained the condition of
the treasury, showing that there is
now a balance of over four hun
dred dollars. The treasurer was in
structed to turn over the sum to the
treasurer of the Guild.
