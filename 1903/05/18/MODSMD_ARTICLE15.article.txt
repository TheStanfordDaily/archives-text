# Sigma Sigma Initiation.
## 
### 
The annual initiation of sopho
mores into Sigma Sigma was re
cently held at the Vendome Hotel
in San Jose. The following nine
men were initiated: R. C. Bertheau,
R. S. Carber, B. C. Dey, W. R. Cole,
F. M. Wilbur, M. IL Sprague,
0. K. Grau, G. V. Lawry, L. G.
Dinwoodey. The regular out-of
door running and dance usually
given in connection with the initia
tion was dispensed with out of re
spect for those who have died dur
ing the typhoid epidemic.
