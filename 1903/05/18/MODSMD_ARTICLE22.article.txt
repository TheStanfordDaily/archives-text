# Will Address Law Association.
## 
### 
At the first annual meeting of
the Stanford Law Association, to be
held in Assembly Hall Thursday,
May 21st at 3:00, Mr. Charles S.
Wheeler, of San Francisco, will give
an address on "Origin and Develop
ment of the Legal Profession." The
address will be open to the public.
Mr. Wheeler is a member of the San
Francisco law firm of Bishop,
Wheeler and Hoefller. He is one
of the leading lawyers of the Pacific
Coast. Mr. Wheeler is a graduate
of the University of California, and
is now a regent of that institution.
