# Souvenir of Stanford.
## 
### 
Students going home, visitors re
turning East, and members of the
Faculty who are going abroad,
should not fail to take with them
that charming souvenir,"A Visit to
Stanford University." Handsome
ly printed on deckle-edge paper,
this little book gives a graphic de
scription of University life, the
buildings and the surrounding
country, and contains over fifty
accompanying illustrations.
