# Tourist Excursion Parties to the East Over the Scenic Line.
## 
### 
If you are going East and want
to save money, yet travel with
pleasure and comfort, it will pay
you to investigate our daily person
ally conducted tourist excursions.
These parties are in charge of a
manager, who accompanies the car
through to Chicago and the Atlan
tic Coast and gives his personal
attention to the welfare of each
passenger in his charge. The
schedules are arranged so as to pass
through the world-fanned scenery
on the Denver & Rio Grande Rail
road by daylight. Those desiring
may also have the privilege of a
stop-over in Salt Lake City. Let
us know where you are going and
we will be glad to give you full
information about your trip, the
lowest rates of fare, and send you
free of charge some handsomely
illustrated books of travel.
J. D. Mansfield,
General Agent,
625 Market street, San Francisco.
