# French Play Receipts.
## 
### 
The report of the treasurer of the
Cercle Francais was presented last
night at the final meeting of the
club at the home of Dr. J. E.
Matzke in Palo Alto. The ex
penses of the play "Le Monde on
Pon S'ennuie," May 12th,amounted
to $262.38, and the receipts were
large enough to leave a profit of
$202.40, which will be turned over
to the Students' Guild.
