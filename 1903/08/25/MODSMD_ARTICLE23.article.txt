# To Study in Europe.
## 
### 
E. D. Starbuck, assistant pro
fessor in the Department of Educa
tion, left the University during the
summer on a year's leave of absence.
On his way east Professor Starbuck
delivered a course of lectures at
Western Reserve University, in
Cleveland, Ohio. He sailed for
England July 11th, and delivered a
course of lectures on child study at
a summer school in Biriringham,
England. Shortly after he went
to the continent and expected to
take up his residence for a year in
either Berlin or Paris, where he is
to do experimental study in special
psychological subjects. Professor
Starbuck is to return to the Uni
versity in the fall of 1904.
