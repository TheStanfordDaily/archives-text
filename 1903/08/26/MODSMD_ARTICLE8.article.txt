# Married During Summer.
## 
### 
Instructor Lee Emerson Bassett
of the English department, and
Miss Florence Jackson, '05, were
married on the tenth of last June,
in San Francisco. Miss Jackson
entered the University last year
with advanced standing from the
University of Nebraska.
Mr. and Mrs. Bassett spent the
summer in Southern California,
where Mr. Bassett gave a six
weeks' teachers' course in the sum
mer session for teachers of the
Cumnock school. Mr. and Mrs.
Bassett reside at 421 Anderson
avenue, Palo Alto.
