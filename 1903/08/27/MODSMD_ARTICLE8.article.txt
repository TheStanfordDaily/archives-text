# Registration Continues Large.
## 
### 
At the end of the third day of
registration a total of one thousand
one hundred and two students are
enrolled. Of these six hundred
and seventy-six are men and four
hundred and twenty-six women ;
seven hundred and sixty old stu
dents, and three hundred and forty
two new students. Last year's
total for the corresponding days
was one thousand one hundred
and twenty-eight. Graduate stu
dents have not yet registered under
the new ruling. Forty-five per
mits to register have been issued
in addition, but are not yet turned
in. These will bring the total
number to one thousand one hun
dred and forty-seven.
