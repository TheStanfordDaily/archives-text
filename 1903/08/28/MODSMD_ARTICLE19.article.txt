The football rally of Monday
night will afford the first opportu
nity of the year for a general gath
ering of the men of the University.
At the same time it will put a
strong stamp upon the character
of the coming football season.
There is the greatest necessity that
every man in college aid in mak
ing the rally express a spirit of
confidence and determination that
are prerequisites of a successful
season. The victorious field-meet
of last semester did more than
anything else to strengthen the
confidence of Stanford men in the
ultimate success of the Cardinal.
The spirit which won the field-day
for Stanford is the one that is
needed to win her the coming foot
ball game. It is the spirit which
every man must show who attends
the first rally of the year.
