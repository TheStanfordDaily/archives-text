# On City Papers.
## 
### 
Several of last year's students
have taken positions on newspapers
this year. Waldemar Young, ex
'o4, editor of the Sequoia last year,
is doing reportorial work on the
San Francisco Chronicle, and Mabel
H. Brown, '00, is engaged on the
Sunday supplement of the same
paper. R. E. Remind, ex'o3,
former editor of the Chaparral and
editorof the 1903 Quad, is employed
by the Examiner at its Oakland
bureau. T. E. Stephenson, '03,
editor-in-chief of the Daily Palo
Alto last semester, holds a position
as reporter on the San Francisco
Examiner.
