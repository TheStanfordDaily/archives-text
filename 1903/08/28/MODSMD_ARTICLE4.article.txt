# FIRST FOOTBALL RALLY.
## Candidates Will Sign for Practice on Next Monday Evening 
### 
The initial football rally of the
season will take place in Encina
Hall club-room at eight o'clock
Monday night. The occasion will
be marked by speeches from Pro
fessors Lewers and Angell, Treas
urer Cowden, Captain Bansbach
and Coaches Lanagan and Slaker.
Yell-leader Kuhn will have charge
of the rooters.
Suits will be distributed by
Treasurer Cowden on Tuesday
for the first practice at four o'clock
in the afternoon of that day. All
men who have their own suits are
expected to use thern, as the num
ber owned by the student body is
limited.
