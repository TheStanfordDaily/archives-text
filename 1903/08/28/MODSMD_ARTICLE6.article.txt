# Encina President Resigns.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Encina Club
directors last night Walter F.
Dunn, '04, president of the club,
presented his resignation. Dunn
is one of the charter members of
the local chapter of the Kappa
Alpha fraternity and is now resid
ing in Palo Alto. A special elec
tion will be held next week for the
choosing of a new president.
Iloyce R. Long, '00, who was ap
pointed a director at the end of
last semester, also resigned, as he
no longer lives at the Hall. The
vacancy will be filled by appoint
ment of the board.
