# Gymnasium News.
## 
### 
The registration of Encina Gym
nasium is now practically complete.
The 11:15 and the 5 o'clock classes
are both full, with a membership of
130 each. Ten or twelve are on the
waiting list for each of these classes.
The total registration for this se
mester is 340, which is nearly equal
to that of lust year.
A regulation gymnasium uniform
will be recommended for this se
mester and made compulsory atthe
beginning of next semester. It will
consist of grey turner trousers, reg
ulation black gymnasium shirt,
and black tennis shoes.
