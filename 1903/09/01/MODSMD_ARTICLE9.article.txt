# To Decide on Registration Fees.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the
Board of Trustees of the University
held in San Francisco on Satur
day the petitions from students in
regard to the payment of the
registration fee were discussed
It was decided to send a com
mittee from the Board to meet
the petitioners at an appointed
date. Mr. George E. Crothers,
secretary of the Board, was made
chairman of the committee and
the date will be announced later.
All those students who have con
sidered themselves residents of Cal
ifornia will be permitted to state
their cases individually before the
committee, whicli will pass upon
the facts and decide the matter.
