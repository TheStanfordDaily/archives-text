# Women's League Meeting.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Wo-,
men's League this semester was
held Friday afternoon in the Roble
parlors. In a short address, Miss
Gertrude Smith, '04, the president,
explained the purpose of the League
as that of bringing together in a
more intimate relation the various
groups of University women. A
cordial welcome to the new stu
dents was extended by her in the
name of the League.
