# FIRST STUDENT ASSEMBLY.
## Dr. Jordan Will Address Meeting With. His Traditional Talk to Freshmen. 
### 
Because of the holiday the first
student assembly of the year has
been postponed from the regular
hour on Wednesday to Thursday
at 11:15. Dr. Jordan will address
the gathering. The lecture to
Freshmen which he has delivered
for several years will he the subject
of the President's talk. There will
be singing by the Glee Club, and
impromptu yelling and singing by
the students under the direction of
yell leaders Kuhn and Baker.
