# New Book by Irwin.
## 
### 
Wallace Irwin, ex-'OO, is bring
ing out through Paul Elder & Co.
a college book to be called "A Men's
College Record." The volume, be
sides being a journal, record and
note-book, is to contain over a doz
en bits of college verne, light and
serious. There is to be a college
illustration on almost every page
done by Gordon Ross, the well
known pen and ink artist. The
book will be off the press sometime
(his month.
