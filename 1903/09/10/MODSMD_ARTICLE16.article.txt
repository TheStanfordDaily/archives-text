# SUSPENDED FROM UNIVERSITY.
## Distribution Among Departments of Men Who Were Dropped Last Semester. 
###

twenty-nine students were dropped
from the rolls of the University and
thirty-three received cards warning
them that their work of the next
semester must improve in quality.
