# Chosen President of Society.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Geological
Society, held Tuesday, D. M. Fol
som, 'o'2, was elected to fill the
vacancy left by the resignation of
H. R. Johnson, ex-'O3. R. B.
Moran, '05, and A. E. Preston, '04,
were elected to membership, and
Professor Shedd, of the University
of Oregon, became an honorary
member.
