# MEMORIAL CHURCH SERVICES.
## Pulpit at Morning- Service to be Occupied by Berkeley Clergyman. 
### 
Rev. F. L. Hosmer of the Berke
ley Unitarian church will preach
Sunday at the morning service in
Memorial Church. Chaplain Gard
ner will preach in the afternoon.
There will also he the regular organ
recital in connection with the after
noon service.
