# Poster for "Everyman."
## 
### 
A handsome poster, designed by
B. Clarence Bubb, '04, is being print
ed by the Stanley-Tavlor Company,
and will be used in advertising the
moralty play of "Everyman." It
represents "Everyman" kneeling
to receive in his hands the scourge
which a friar is holding out to him.
The background of the picture
shows part of the setting of the
play as presented by Mr. Greet.
The poster is one of the most strik
ing that has appeared at Stanford.
Copies will be on sale at the Book
store by Wednesday of this week.
