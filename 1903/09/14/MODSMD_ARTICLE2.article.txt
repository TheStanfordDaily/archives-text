# Elected to League.
## 
### 
At the regular business meeting
of Euphronia, last Saturday night,
A. M. Dibble, '05, W. L. Blair, '05,
and M. H. Thorpe, '05, were elected
representatives of Euphronia on
the Intersociety Debating League.
O. C. Spencer, '04, president of
Euphronia, is chairman of the
League.
