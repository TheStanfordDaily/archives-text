# Tickets for Plays.
## 
### 
Tickets for "Everyman" and
"Twelfth Night," which are to he
presented the evenings of Septem
ber 21st and 22d respectively, will
be on sale at the Bookstore and
at the Stanford Pharmacy, Palo
Alto, Friday morning, September
18th. The prices for the first per
formance are 75c and $1.00; for the
second, 50c and 75c. The book of
the morality play, "Everyman,"
with introduction by Ben Greet, is
now on sale at the Bookstore for
twenty-five cents.
