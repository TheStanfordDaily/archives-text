# Will Wear Caps and Gowns.
## 
### 
At a meeting today to decide
finally upon the matterof hats, the
Junior girls after much discussion
chose a distinctive class dress in
preference to the customary head
gear. The 1 ( .)05 girls voted to adopt
the conventional black cap and
gown, hitherto confined at Stan
ford to the senior girls alone. The
only distinction between the junior
garb and the senior dress is to be
either a red tassel or the wearing
of the black tassel upon the oppo
site side of the cap.
