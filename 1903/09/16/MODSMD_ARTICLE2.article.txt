# Prize Song Competition.
## 
### 
The committee which has been
appointed by the Executive Com
mittee to have charge of the prize
football contest are H. P. Kuhn,
'04, chairman, and Miss J. Brown,
'04, and M. H. Thorpe, '05. A
prize of ten dollars is offered by
the committee for the best song,
and five dollars for the second
best. The contest closes October
10th. Preference will be given to
something having a quick, catchy,
original tune, suitable for the
bleachers.
