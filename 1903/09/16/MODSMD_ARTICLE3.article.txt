# Freshman Debaters Organize.
## 
### 
At a meeting held last night the
Freshman Debating Society was
organized. The purpose of the
society was explained to the Fresh
men by 0. 0. Spencer, '04, Page,
'03, and Roehr, '04. The election
of officers resulted as follows: J.
\V. Maloy, president; G. A. Stew
art, vice-president; It. R. Brooke,
secretary and treasurer; \Y r . T.
Dudgeon, sergeant-at-arms. The
constitution, which has been in
force for several years past, was
adopted in full. The appointment
of a program committee was left in
the hands of the president.
