# Seat Sale Begins Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Tickets for "Everyman" and
''Twelfth Night," which are to he
presented the evening* of September
21st and 22d respectively, will be
on sale at the Bookstore and at
the Stanford Pharmacy, Palo Alto,
tomorrow morning. The Bookstore
sale begins at 9 a.m. The prices
for the first performance nre 75n
and $1.00; for the second, 50c and
75c.
