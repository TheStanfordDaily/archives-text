# California Notes.
## 
### 
—Eighty-nine track men have
already signed the trainers' list at
Berkeley.
—The roster of new students has
just been published. It places the
number of undergraduates admitted
at 718.
—Eugene I. McCormac has been
appointed to fill the place in the
history department made vacant
by the resignation of Dr. Babcock.
—M. J. Keller of Oakland has
offered a silver cup as a prize to the
organization in the university
winning the most debates this year.
