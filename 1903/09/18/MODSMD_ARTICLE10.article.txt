# The
						Sunday Services.
## 
### 
The morning sermon in Memo
rial Church on Sunday will be
preached hy Chaplain Gardner.
His subject will he "Moral Evil:
Its Cause and Cure." Rev. N.B. W.
Gallwey of Menlo Park will preach
the afternoon sermon. Rev. Gall
wey is a prominent Presbyterian
minister who has spent a great deal
of time in slum work in Chicago.
