Thk Reliance clubmen will be
the first big team to line up against
the Varsity this season. Their
chances of victory are good but the
Stanford eleven is sure to acquit
itself creditably in spite of the
handicap arising from the earliness
of the season. The essence of to
morrow's game will not be found
on the gridiron. On the bleachers
the keynote to the entire season
should be Btruck. Everyoneshould
come out to show that he intends
to be loyal in his support of the
team. The band will be there, the
yell leaders will he there, every
undergraduate in college should
strengthen the Stanford play by
his presence on the grandstand.
