# Trustees Will Meet.
## 
### 
The Board of Trustees of the Uni
versity will meet in San Francisco
Saturday, at which time the special
committee appointed to consider
the matter of refunding registration
fees to petitioning students will re
port and Judge S. F. Leib is to
give his opinion on the individual
cases.
