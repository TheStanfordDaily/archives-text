# 1906 Debating Squads.
## 
### 
The committee in charge of the
Sophomore debating try-out has
divided the men into the following
squads: First squad—Nibley, Al
vord, Cunha, Sales, and Campbell;
second squad — Maloy, Bittner,
Thompson, Smith, and Menker.
The squads will meet tomorrow to
choose their positions. The date
for the try-out has not yet been de
cided upon.
