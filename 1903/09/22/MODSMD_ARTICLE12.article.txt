# Freshman Debaters.
## 
### 
The Freshman debaters are turn
ing out in large numbers. Twenty
five men have entered the try-out
to meet the Sophomores. The
date of the try-out has not
yet been determined upon, nor
have the squads been chosen.

L. Geer, H. G. Jorgenson, I). H.
Clift, VV. R. Taft, \V. T. Dudgeon,
J. M. Close, G. K. Nupp, G. A.
Stewart, F. L. Everson, J. W.
Maloy, J. E. Catis, J. E. Stephens,
F. VV. Fellows, R. M. Blodget, E.
H. Pier, Smith, Jensen, James
Brennan, F. A. Fisher, Boyd, B.
R. Brooke, S. V. Good, L. R. Rose,
John Brennan, John Leonard.
