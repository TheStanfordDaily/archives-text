# MAY PLAY CALIFORNIA AGAIN.
## Girls' Tennis Team Receives Challenge to Contest with State University. 
### 
A challenge for a match with the
young women of the University of
California has been received by the
Girls' Tennis Team, but no action
has been taken on the matter as
yet. The first step toward getting
a team among the girls this year
will be made Saturday, when a
tournament will be held on the
Roble courts at 9:30, for the pur
pose of bringing out the tennis ma
terial among the University women.
All girls interested in tennis are
urged to be present, and the names
of those who wish to play may be
left on the Roble gymnasium bul
letin-board before Wednesday eve
ning.
