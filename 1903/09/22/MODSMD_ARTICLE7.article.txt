# Sophomore Class Meets.
## 
### 
The Sophomore class met today
and listened to the report of the
committee on caps and Sophomore
minstrels. G. G. Altnow, chairman,
gave the report on the matter of
giving a minstrel show, after which
D. 1). Sales and H. P. Hittner, also
members, made separate reports on
the advisability of giving a comedy
or a minstrel show. Nothing defi
nite was decided on. The class
will hold another meeting tomor
row at 1:15 to take definite action
on the matter.
