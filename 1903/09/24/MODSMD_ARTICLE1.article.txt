# FRESHMAN CANDIDATES.
## Names of Those Who Are Eligible to Play in Intercollegiate Game. 
### 
The following list of Freshmen
has been given to Dr. Angell, head
of the faculty athletic committee,
as the names of those candidates
who are eligible to enter the foot
ball game against the University
of California freshmen October
17th. Dr. Angell will go over the
names tonight. According to the
intercollegiate agreement the list
must be sent to California Satur
day. The list may not be com
plete, and the object of publishing
it tonight is to avoid the omission
of any name from the final list sent
to California, as no name can be
added after Saturday. California's
freshman names will be due at
Stanford Saturday.

worth, L. W. Barbur, Jordan, 11.

logg, E. C. Smith, P. T. Burtis, L.
