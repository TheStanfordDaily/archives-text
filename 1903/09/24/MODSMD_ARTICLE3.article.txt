# Elected to Abbott's Inn.
## 
### 
At the last meeting of the Court
of Abbott's Inn the following new
men were elected to membership:
F. Roehr, '04, A. A. Hampson, '04,
H. W. Strong, '05, A.E.Cooley, '03,
and 11. McCaslin, '04. The old
members are C. L. Firebaugh, '04,
S. T. Montgomery, '04, and L. M.
Sterling, law special. Firebaugh
was made executive head of the
court. The next meeting will be
held October 9th.
