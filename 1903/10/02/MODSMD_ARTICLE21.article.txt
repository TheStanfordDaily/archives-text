# The
						Current "Alumnus."
## 
### 
In this week's number of the
Alumnus, which was issued today,
appears the first installment of the
address delivered by Mrs. Stan
ford on "Freedom of Speech." De
tailed accounts are given of the
presentation of "Everyman" and
"Twelfth Night," and the usual
summary of University news of the
week appears. Editorially the
problem of the University Inn is
discussed.
