# SENIOR ELECTION MONDAY.
## Graduating Class Will Go Through Mere Formality of Choosing Officers. 
### 
The Senior class election will be
held on the quad Monday, from
11:15 to 12:30. There will be no
contest, as there is but one nominee
for each office. The candidates are
as follows: For president, A. E.
Preston; for vice-president, E. C.
Rittenhouse; for secretary, Miss
I. A. Wright; for treasurer, H. L.
Hamilton; and for gergeant-at
arms, W. E. McWethy. The elec
tion officials are H. Shields, E. G.
Brua, and G. Cromwell.
