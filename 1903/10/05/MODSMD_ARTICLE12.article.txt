# Lecture Tomorrow Night.
## 
### 
Dr. J. May of Palo Alto will give
an illustrated lecture on "The High
ways and Byways of Jerusalem,"
in the University chapel tomorrow
evening at 7:15. The lecture is
given under Y. M. C. A. auspices,
but hoth men and women are in
vited. Dr. May will speak from
personal observation, as he resided
in Jerusalem for some time.
