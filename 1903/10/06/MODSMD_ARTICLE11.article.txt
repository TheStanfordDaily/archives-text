# Rules of Guild Hospital.
## 
### 
Attention is called to the follow
ing rules of the Guild Hospital:
1. Visitors shall ordinarily be
admitted, at the discretion of the
nurse in charge of the hospital,
only during the following hours:
Week days 11 to 12 a.m. and 3 to
5 p.m.; Sundays 2 to 5 p.m.
2. Not more than two visitors
shall he allowed to see one patient
at the same time.
3. All visitors must sign their
names in the visitors' register before
going to a patient's room.
4. Patients must not go into
other patients' rooms without the
permission of the nurse in charge
of the hospital.
5. No smoking is allowed in the
hopital.
6. Lights in al" patients' roonu
must be out by nine o'clock.
Order of Board.
