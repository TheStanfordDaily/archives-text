# Dr. May Lectures.
## 
### 
The lecture last night in the
chapel by Dr. J. May of Palo Alto,on
"The Highways and Byways of Je
rusalem," was well attended. The
lecturer supplemented his remarks
with profuse views of the city of
Jerusalem. Dr. May resided in
Jerusalem for a long time, and is
thoroughly acquainted with the
city and its surroundings.
llis views of the walls of the city,
of its gates, of the Mount of Olives,
and of different castles were among
the best. Dr. May also showed
some colored views illustrating the
ife of Christ.
