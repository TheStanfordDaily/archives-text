# Former Trustee Dies.
## 
### 
The Hon. N. \V. Spaulding, one
of the original trustees of Stanford,
died yesterday at New Britain,
Connecticut. Mr. Spaulding came
to California in 1851 and engaged
in mining. He was twice elected
to the office of mayor of Oakland.
Mr. Spaulding resigned his position
as trustee last spring to create the
vacancy for Mrs. Stanford.
