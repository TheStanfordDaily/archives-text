# California Freshmen Win.
## 
### 
Santa Clara College met defeat
on the Berkeley campus yesterday
at the hands of the California
freshmen by the score of 5 to 0.
Both teams were practically equal
in strength. Santa Clara failed
ill a trial for place-kick by barely
an inch.
