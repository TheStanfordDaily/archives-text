# DR. LOEB TOMORROW NIGHT.
## Famous Investigator Will Lecture in the Chapel on Scientific Matters. 
### 
Dr. Jacques Loeb, of the Univer
sity of California, will lecture in
the chupel tomorrow evening at
7:80. His subject will be "Artifi
cial Cross-fertilization in Star
fishes and Sea-urchins." Dr. Loeb
is professor of comparative physi
ology in the University of Califor
nia. His researches in fertilization,
artificial parthenogenesis, and the
physico-chemical factors in life
processes have made him one of
the most noted figures in the scien
tific world. He was professor at
the University of Chicago before
coming to California. The lecture
will be under the auspices of the
scientific society of Sigma Xi.
