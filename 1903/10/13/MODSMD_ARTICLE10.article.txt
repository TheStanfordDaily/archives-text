# Interscholastic Meet.
## 
### 
At the interscholastic field meet
on the Berkeley oval under theaus
picesof the Academic League, Sat
urday, Oakland High School won
with 28 points. Lowell High was
second with 25 and Ukiah third
with 22. The only record broken
was by Rose, of Healdsburg, who
broke his own world's interscholas
tic record by putting the twelve
pound shot fifty-two feet, eight
inches. His former record was
fifty-two feet, two inches. In the
hundred, Schwartz, of Lowell,
equalled the academic record of
ten and one-fifth.
