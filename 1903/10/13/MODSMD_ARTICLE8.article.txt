# Officials Not Yet Chosen.
## 
### 
A conference was held yesterday
by the treasurers of California and
Stanford on the question of officials
for the intercollegiate Freshman
game Saturday. The names of J.
T. Nourse, Stanford '00, Everett
Brown, California, and Major Col
lins, of Belmont School, were dis
cussed, and from these an umpire
and a referee will he selected
Thursday.
Tickets to the game will be put
on sale on the quad Thursday
morning. None will be sold at the
Bookstore.
