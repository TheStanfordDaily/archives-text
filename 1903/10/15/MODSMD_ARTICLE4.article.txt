# English Club Meets.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the executive
committee of the English Club to
day Miss Irene A. Wright, '04,
presented her resignation as a
member of the committee. Miss
Wright withdraws from the com
mittee because of stress of other
work. Her successor will be
chosen at the next general meeting
of the club, which will be held at
the Alpha Phi house Wednes
day evening.
Miss Edythe Hale, '04,and W.H.
Thomson, '05, were elected to mem
bership. Mrs. O. L. Elliott was
elected an honorary member of the
club.
