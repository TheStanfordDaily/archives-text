# THE
						SUNDAY SERMON.
## Chaplain Gardner to Preach Sunday Morning on "Gifts of Civilization." 
### 
Chaplain Gardner will preach
the morning sermon in Memorial
Church Sunday. His subject will
be "The Gifts of Civilization." At
the afternoon organ recital Mr.
Samuel Savannah will play on the
violin the following pieces, with or
gan accompaniment: Andante from
Concerto, Mendelssohn, Romance,
Gade-Legende, NVieniawski.
