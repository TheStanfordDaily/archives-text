# INN OPENED TODAY.
## Student Eating-House Serves Meals After Being Closed a Month. 
### 
The Inn was reopened this morn
ing with a good attendance. The
building lias been thoroughly re
modeled, the ranges have been
changed to burn coal instead of oil,
and a device has been arranged
whereby the hot water from the
ranges is piped to racks for the pur
pose of keeping the dishes hot.
One room has been reserved for the
women patrons and another for
transients, while the large dining
room is to be used as before by the
men students. The football train
ing-table will be established in the
large dining-room tomorrow.
