# Quads.
## 
### 
—Mies S. B. Sankey, '05, has
been elected to membership in the
Roble gymnasium club.
—A handsome silver loving-cup
has been presented to W. H. Irwin,
'99, by the editorial staff of the
Chronicle to keep for his son, born
a few days ago.
—At the last meeting of the
Commonwealth Club, held at the
Palace Hotel in San Francisco,
W. R. Dudley, of the Botany de
partment, spoke on the subject,
"The forest reserves of the govern
ment, and their proper utiliza
tion."
