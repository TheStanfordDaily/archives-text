# Basket-ball Work.
## 
### 
The men who have signed up for
basket-ball this semester as part
gymnasium substitution will play
on Tuesdays and Thursdays only
of each week. The other three
days those taking basket-ball will
be entirely excused from the appa
ratus work of the gymnasium. In
this way the work is scattered
throughout the whole week, and at
the same time those who wish to
play basket-ball and do track work
will l)e enabled to participate in
both.
