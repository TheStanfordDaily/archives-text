— Prof. C. A. Duniway of the
History department attended yes
terday the opening session of the
teachers' institute of Napa county,
at Napa.
—B. W. Crandall, '06, has an
enlarged photograph of the Palo
Alto tree in the photographic
salon at the Hopkins Art Gallery
in San Francisco. His picture
was chosen from among one thou
sand others which were submitted,
as one of sixteen to be used in the
salon-book.
