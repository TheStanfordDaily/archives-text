# Sale of Horses.
## 
### 
The next shipment of horses
from the Stock Farm will be made
November 13th. About fifty-two
horses will go to New York, where
a sale is advertised to occur be
tween November 23d and December
Ist. When this shipment is dis
posed of, there will be left at the
farm about sixty head. These will
be sold by the first of the year.
October 15th Monte Carlo, whose
grandsire was Electioneer, won the
MacDowell stake of $5000 at Lex
ington, Kentucky, in Lou
Dillon, who recently made the
record of two minutes flat, is also
from Electioneer stock.
