# Announcements.
## 
### 
Tennis Club—Meets in room 11
quad Wednesday at 7:30.
Freshman Class. — Election of
officers in the chapel from 1 to 2
Wednesday.
University Band. — Regular
practice Thursday night.
M. H. Thorpe, Leader.
"Chaparral" Office Hours. —
In the new office, daily 11:15 to
12:15, Friday, 1:30 to 4:30.
Sophomore Fezes.— The treas
urer of the Sophomore class has a
few fezes at room 67 Encina, which
will be sold at half price.
Sophomore Assessments — May
he paid to any of the following:
L. R. Weimnann, B. S. Allen, Miss
C. E. Finney, or S. T. Jordan.
Football Men. —Those who have
ceased practice will please turn in
their suits promptly, as they are
heeded. D. V. Cowden.
Meeting for Women. —Miss Ber
tha Cond6 will hold a meeting in
the chapel tomorrow night at 7:30.
All women of the University are
invited to attend.
Track Candidates. — All men
who intend to try for the track
team are requested to meet in room
62 quad Thursday at 7:30.
E. W. Moulton.
Labor Bureau. —The following
positions are open: Work for hoard
and room; waiting on table, 1 Jap
anese (campus); general house
work — room for housework, room
and board; posing; representative
for stationery and engraving firm.
Rooters' Hats.—The same hat
as used last year, has been adopted
for this year. The hat may be had
by leaving an order with the Hab
erdasher not later than October
28th. Price, sixty cents. No one
without an official rooter's hat will
be allowed to sit in the rooting
section. O. S. Lousley,
W. R. Hamilton, Com.
