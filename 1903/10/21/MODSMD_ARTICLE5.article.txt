# Track Meeting Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The meeting of track men called
by Trainer Moulton tomorrow
night is for the purpose of arousing
interest in fall training. Moulton
will talk and explain just how
much work he expects the men to
do. Some of last year's team will
speak, and plans for the fall
handicap meet are to bediscussed.
