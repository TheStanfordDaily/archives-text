# SONG PRACTICE TOMORROW.
## Rehearsals Will be Held in Chapel Under Direction of University Band. 
### 
The first song practice will be held
in the University chapel tomorrow
night at 7:30. The Band will he
present to furnish music, and
everyone is urged to he there with
his song-hook. These rallies, which
will he held hereafter on Friday
evenings, present the only.oppor
tunity of rehearsing the songs to
advantage. The song-hooks are
still on sale at the Bookstore, and
may he had Saturday at the game
with Nevada.
