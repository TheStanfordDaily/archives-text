# English Club Meeting.
## 
### 
The English Club held its regu
lar bi-monthly meeting at the
Alpha Phi house last night. Pro
fessor W. It. Dudley of the Botany
department gave an interesting
address on the trees of California,
illustrating his talk with a large
number of fine photographs. Miss
Clarissa Eby, '05, was elected a
member of the executive committee
to succeed Miss Irene Wright, '04,
resigned.
