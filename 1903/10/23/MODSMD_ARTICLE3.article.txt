# TRACK MEN MEET.
## Nearly Sixty Candidates Hand in Their Names for Fall Training. 
### 
About sixty track men answered
the call of Trainer Moulton to at
tend last night's meeting. The fall
handicap meet was discussed, and
Moulton suggested that in view of
the fact that Captain Holman
could not return until the middle
of November, ex-Captain J. C. Mc-
Caughern be appointed to manage
the meet. This met with favor,
and McCaughern was chosen.
After speaking at length on the
necessity of new men coming out
and the importance of fall training,
the trainer asked each man present
to sign up for his. respective event,
and state the time that he could
appear on the track.
Dr. W. F. Snow spoke briefly on
the benefits derived from keeping I
in training throughout the year, I
and several members of last year's
team added a few remarks.
