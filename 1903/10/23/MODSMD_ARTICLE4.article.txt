# Announcements.
## 
### 
Junior Giri.s. — The cardinal
mortar-boards have been received at
the Bookstore.
Employment Bureau. — Office
hour: 11:20 to 12:20, days of in
struction. The Saturday morning
hour will be discontinued.
New Books. —The following have
been received at the Bookstore:
Short tables of Integrals, R. O.
Pierce; Christmas Carols, Dickens;
Livy, Books XXI and XXII,
Greenough and Peck.
Tennis Players.—Meeting Wed
nesday October 28, at 7:80 in room
11, for the purpose of electing a
tennis captain. A meeting of the
Tennis Club is called at the same
time and place for the election of
officers and new members.
Rooters' Hats. —The same hat
as used last year, has been adopted
for this year. The hat may be had
by leaving an order with the Hab
erdasher not later than October 28th.
