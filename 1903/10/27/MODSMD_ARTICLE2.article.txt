# Theater Tickets Next Week.
## 
### 
The price of tickets to Fischer's
Theater on the night of the inter
collegiate game has been set at one
dollar. The sale will probably
commence next week, and a block
of seats will be put on sale at the
Bookstore. A section of seats is to
be reserved on the lower floor for
the rooters, but the remainder will
be open to the general public. The
regular play is to lie given.
