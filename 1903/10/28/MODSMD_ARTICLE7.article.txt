# Freshmen Levy Assessment.
## 
### 
At the Freshman meeting held in
the chapel today an assessment of
fifty cents was levied on the mem
bers of the class. This is primar
ily for the purpose of procuring
sweaters for the Freshman football
team and incidentally for other ex
penses. A committee of three was
appointed to look into the matter
of sweaters and to recommend the
kind to be procured. The commit
tee is compostd of A. J. Chalmers,
E. P. Stott, and J. B. Stepheneon.
A committee composed of J. W.
Maloy, E. R. Jackson, and C. L.
Geer was appointed to draft a con
stitution for the class.
