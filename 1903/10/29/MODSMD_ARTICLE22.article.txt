# Report on Fishes.
## 
### 
I)r. Oliver P. Jenkins, head of
the Physiology department, has
received from the government
printing office in Washington,
J). C., his report on "Collections of
fishes made in the Hawaiian
Islands." It is a work of about a
hundred pages, with four plates
and tifty illustrations made by \V.
8. Atkinson, '99, of the Zoology
department. This report deals
mainly with the new species of
fishes from the Hawaiian Islands
in the collection made by Dr.
Jenkins in 1889 while professor of
biology at DePauw University. The
color, size and habits of all of the
new species are given.
