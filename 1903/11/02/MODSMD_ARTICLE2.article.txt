# Song Practice Saturday.
## 
### 
A song and cheering practice
was held Saturday evening in the
chapel. About a hundred and
fifty rooters were present. The
singing showed considerable im
provement. Another practice is to
be held in the chapel Friday. A
new yell was adopted and will be
used hereafter on the bleachers. It
is as follows:
Stanford! Stanford!
Rah! Rah!
Stanford!
Rail! Rah! Rah! Rah! Rah!
Stanford!
