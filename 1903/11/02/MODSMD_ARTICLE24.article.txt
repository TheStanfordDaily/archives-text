# Tennis Tournaments.
## 
### 
The first series of tournaments
to determine the best ten players
among the members of the Tennis
Club will be held on the new court
Wednesday afternoon. The tour
nament committee will meet to
morrow and decide on the details
of the event. All members of the
tennis squad are eligible to com
pete in the match, and entries will
be open until tomorrow night.
Captain Baker will receive the
entries and the executive committee
of the tennis club will have charge
of the drawing for places.
