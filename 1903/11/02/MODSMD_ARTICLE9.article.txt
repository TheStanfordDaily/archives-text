# Classical Journal Club.
## 
### 
A Classical Journal Club has
been established this year among
the instructors and graduates of the
Greek and the Latin departments.
At a meeting held Saturday eve
ning at the home of Dr. A. T. Mur
ray, Dr. Fairclough gave an ac
count of his visit to the island of
Crete, and of the archaeological
work at present being carried on
there.
