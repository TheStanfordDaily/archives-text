# Copeland Leaves Next Week.
## 
### 
Dr. E. B. Copeland, '95, who has
been instructor in Bionomics for the
past two years, will sail next week
for Manila to take up his work as
chief botanist of the United States
Philippine commission. Mies Mary
Isabel McCracken, Physiology '03,
will have charge of Dr. Copeland's
work in Bionomics.
