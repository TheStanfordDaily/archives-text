# Contents of the "Alumnus."
## 
### 
The current issue of the Alumnus
appeared yesterday. The editorial
comment is based) on the recom
mendation of the joint Faculty
Athletic Committees for holding
the intercollegiate football game
on the campus of each university.
A full report on the last meeting
of the trustees is given. The foot
ball season is reviewed and full
accounts of the Chemavva and the
Reliance games are printed. The
decision of the University not to
make an exhibit at the St. Louis
exposition is the subject of a short
article. The debate with Nevada
is also discussed. There is an
article on tennis and hockey at
Roble gymnasium. A full report
of the last University Assembly is
printed.
