# Will Address Association.
## 
### 
At the nineteenth annual meet
ing of the American Historical
Association, to he held in New Or
leans, December 29-31, Dr. Max
Farrand of the History depart
ment will present a paper on "The
Compromises of the Constitution."
Professor Farrand will leave for
the East upon the conclusion of
his duties for the semester, and
will address the association on the
last day of its session.
