# Officials for the Game.
## 
### 
The remainder of the officials
who will have charge of the inter
collegiate game were decided upon
today. The complete list is as fol
lows: Umpire, E. C. Dudley; referee,
B. Dibble; head linesman, Dr. A.
C. Steckle; 6takemen and timers,
VV. H. Blake, Stanford '04, Everett
Brown, California '00. Dudley
was a half-back on the Yale varsity;
Dibble was captain and half-back
of the 1898 Harvard eleven; Dr.
Steckle is coach of the Nevada
team.
