# Elected to English Club.
## 
### 
Miss Edith R. Mirrielees, '00,
was elected a member of the
English Club at a meeting of the
executive committee yesterday.
Miss Mirrielees is a member of the
Sequoia staff. The next meeting
of the club will be held at the
Kappa Alpha Theta house Wed
nesday evening.
