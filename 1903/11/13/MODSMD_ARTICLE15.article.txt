# Excursion Trains.
## 
### 
The excursion train to San Fran
cisco tomorrow will he run in two
sections. The first is to leave the
campus at 8:30, and the other will
start from Mayfield at 8:40. Both
trains will depart from Palo Alto
at 8:45, and before reaching the
city will stop at Menlo, Redwood,
Belmont, and San Mateo. Rates
have been fixed at $1.00 for the
round trip. These Bpecial tickets
will be taken only on the excursion
trains, but will be good returning
on any train Saturday, Sunday
or Monday.
