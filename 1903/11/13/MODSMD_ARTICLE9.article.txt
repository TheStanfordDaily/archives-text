# Memorial Church Services.
## 
### 
The Sunday morning sermon in
Memorial Church will be preached
by the Rev. R. Wilbur Chapman,
of New York. He is one of the
best known preachers of the Pres
byterian church. Chaplain Gard
ner will speak at the afternoon
organ recital on "Christ, the light
of the world." Mrs. Raymond C.
Brooks will sing both morning and
afternoon.
