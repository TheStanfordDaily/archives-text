# College Notes.
## 
### 
— A lecture course on "Life In
surance" has been added to Yale's
curriculum.
—At Princeton twenty men re
ported for the first trials for the
varsity gun club.
—Thirteen scholarships have
been given to Yale by its glee, man
dolin, and banjo clubs for the use
of needy students.
—A University of Minnesota stu
dent has received an expensive
sweater of maroon and gold for
making the first touchdown against
Michigan.
—Amherst is to have a new ob
servatory where the largest tele
scope in New England is to be
placed. It has a lens eighteen
inches in diameter, and a tube
twenty-four feet long. The lens
was bought in Paris and is said to
be the finest in the world.
