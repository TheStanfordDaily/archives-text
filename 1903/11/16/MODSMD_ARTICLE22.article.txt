# Handicap Field Meet.
## 
### 
Next Saturday morning the an
nual fall handicap field meet will
be held on the oval. With the ex
ception of the 100-yard dash, the
distance in all the running events
will be materially decreased. The
220-yard dash is reduced to 175
yards, the 440 to 300 yards, the
half-mile to 600 yards, the mile to
three-fourths of a mile, and the
two-mile run to one and one-fourth
miles.
Trainer Moulton announces that
all men who intend to try for the
team next spring must enter this
meet. If they are not in condition
to participate, excuses will be
granted.
