# Committee Buys New Piano.
## 
### 
The faculty music committee has
purchased a new piano at a cost of
$1200. It will he brought down
from Han Francisco in time for the
next chamber concert. The fund
to make the purchase was started
three years ago from the proceeds
of the Henschel recital. The new
instrument will not he for general
use, hut will be available for con
certs and other special occasions.
The present piano, which is the
property of the Associated Stu
dents, will continue to he employed
for rallies and assemblies.
