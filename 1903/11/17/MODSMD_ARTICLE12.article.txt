# Characters in "Herod."
## 
### 
The characters in Stephen Phil
lips' "Herod," to he presented by
Mrs. Rogers Friday evening in the
chapel, are: Herod, king of the
Jews; Aristobulus, high priest and
brother of Mariamne; Gadius, chief
councilor; Sohemus, a Gaul; Phe
rorus, brother of Herod; Mariamne,
queen and wife of Herod; Cypros,
mother of Herod; Salome, sister of
Herod.
