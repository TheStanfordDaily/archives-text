# Association Football.
## 
### 
The California Association Foot
ball League is arranging for an ex
hibition game on the Stanford cam
pus between two rival teams of
tbe league, and -a definite date will
be fixed soon. Last year's exhibi
tion game was played by scratch
teams made up from the players of
various clubs, and there was, conse
quently, a bad mixture of uniforms
which made it more or less diffi
cult to follow the rival players. In
addition tbe combination work on
the field was defective.
In the game to be played this
season, each team will wear the
club uniform and the players will
be able to exemplify the art of
combination and the science of the
play, while their efforts to excel
will be stimulated by club pride.
