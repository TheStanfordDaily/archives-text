# Arranging for Debate.
## 
### 
The debating teams chosen by
the Freshman and Sophomore
classes met today to arrange the
particulars of the debate. It was
decided that the contest will be
held the second week of next
semester. The question to be dis
cussed is to be proposed by the
Sophomore team next Monday.
The Freshman team will then have
until Wednesday to choose their
side of the question. The judges
are to be chosen by the lntersociety
Debating League. Each speaker
is to be allowed twelve minutes in
which to speak, and the leader of
the affirmative will be given five
minutes for rebuttal.
