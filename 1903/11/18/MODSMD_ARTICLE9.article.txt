# Gymnasium Notes.
## 
### 
—Hereafter the Saturday make
up class will meet at 5 p.m., in
stead of 11:30 as formerly.
—A Mart spring-board has been
added to the apparatus on the floor
of the gymnasium. A complete
set of new fielders' gloves has been
procured for the baseball men, and
two new sets of boxing-gloves have
been purchased.
—The first of the fire-drills
planned by the Encina gymnasium
management took place this morn
ing. The 10:15 class drew the
hose-cart to the plug, attached, and
unreeled the hose in seven minutes.
The same drill was executed by the
11:15 class in seven and one-half
minutes.
