# Collegiate Alumnae.
## 
### 
The Association of Collegiate
Alumnae will meet in San Fran
cisco at Sorosis Hall, 1620 Califor
nia street, Saturday, November
28th. Luncheon will be served at
12 o'clock, and a business meeting
follows at 2:15. An address will
be given at 2:30 by Herbert Bash
ford on "California Literature of
Today." The social hour is at 3:30,
when tea will be served. All Stan
ford women are welcome.
