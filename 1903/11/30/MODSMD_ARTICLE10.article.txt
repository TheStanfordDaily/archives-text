# Thanksgiving Services.
## 
### 
Thanksgiving Day was observed
in Memorial Church by a union
service of all the local Protentant
churches of Palo Alto at 10:30.
Appropriate music was rendered by
the regular Memorial choir and the
combined church choirs of Palo
Alto. The sermon by Rev. J. S.
Macintosh, I). I)., president of the
Presbyterian Seminary at San An
selmo, was based on the text, "And
the shout of a king is among
them." The clergyman spoke of
the great cause for Americans to be
thankful. He told of the growth
of our nation from its beginning to
its present position as a world
power, emphasizing the fact that
consciousness of power and ils
proper exercise are the things that
make our government great.
