# Japanese Drawing Exhibit.
## 
### 
The exhibit of Japanese draw
ings that has been at the art studio
since Friday will be open to the
public until Wednesday. Many
of the pictures have been sold and
those remaining Wednesday will
be sent to San Jose. No admission
is charged to see the pictures.
Tomorrow afternoon between two
and four o'clock Shimada will
draw with the idea of showing how
Japanese watercolors are made.
Different costumes of his nation
will be shown by the artist.
