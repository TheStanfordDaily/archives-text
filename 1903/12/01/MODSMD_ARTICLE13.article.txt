# Former Student Killed.
## 
### 
E. C. Ewell, ex-'95, was killed
by a fall from a horse in San Ma
teo yesterday. Ewell entered Stan
ford with the pioneer class in 1891,
but left the University in 1894.
He married a daughter of N. K.
Masten, the contractor, and was
engaged in business in San Fran
cisco. Ewell was thirty - three
years old at the time of his death.
