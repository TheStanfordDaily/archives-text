# Dr. Abbott Speaks Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Dr. Abbott's first lecture on
great lawyers will be given in room
2, quad, at 7:30 o'clock tomorrow
evening. Dr. Abbott proposes to
give a series of addresses upon the
men who were responsible for the
organization and codification of
the laws of England and America,
and tomorrow night he will review
the work of Jeremy Bentham, and
in subsequent lectures he will take
up Broome and Samuel liomilly,
the English codifiers, and Edward
Livingston and David Dudley
Field, who stand at the head of the
American organizers of the law.
The addresses are intended primar
ily for members of the Law de
partment, but they will be of gen
eral interest and are open to any
one who cares to attend.
