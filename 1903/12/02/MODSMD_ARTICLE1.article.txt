# MASS MEETING TOMORROW.
## Students Will Give Last Hour to Informal Discussion of Honor System. 
### 
The agitation in regard to the
adoption of the honor system in
examinations that has occupied
the attention of the student body
to a great extent during the last
fortnight will come to a had to
morrow morning. At 11:15 a stu
dent mass meeting will he held in
Assembly Hall when an informal
discussion of the proposed system
will take place. The meeting will
partake somewhat of the nature of
a regular student assembly with
the exception that the faculty
and outsiders will not be
present. All University exercises
will be suspended, and a thorough
examination of the sentimentof the
student body in regard to the plan
will take place. The president of
the student body will have charge
of the meeting.
