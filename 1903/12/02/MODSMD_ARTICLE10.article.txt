# NOMINATIONS FOR EDITOR.
## Two Candidates for First Place on Staff of "Daily Palo Alto." 
### 
At a meeting of the student body
in the chapel at 1:15 o'clock this
afternoon nominations were made
for the position of editor-in-chief of
the Daily Palo Alto for next
semester. A. A. Hampson, '04,
was nominated by O. A. Wilson,
'04, and F. W. Powell, '04, by U.R.
Stevens, '05. Hampson is at pres
ent managing editor of the Daily
Palo Alto, and Powell news editor
of the same paper. The election
will be held on the quad next
Wednesday. This is the first con
test that has taken place in four
years for this position.
