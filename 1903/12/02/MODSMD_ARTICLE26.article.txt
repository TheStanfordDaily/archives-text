# Roble Club Dance.
## 
### 
The Roble Club dance Saturday
night in the Roble gymnasium will
be the first given by the women
students of the dormitory in two
years. About one hundred and
twenty invitations have been is
sued. Dancing will continue from
seven until twelve. A San Jose
orchestra will furnish the music.
The patronesses are Mrs. Charles
Lathrop, Mrs. D. S. Jordan, Mrs.
J. M. Stillman. Mrs. J. C. Branner,
Mrs. J. E. Matzke, Mrs. C. A. Dun
iway, Mrs. IT. R. Fairclough, Mrs.
H. Blynn, and Mins Gardiner.
