# A 						New Song.
## 
### 
A new Stanford song entitled
''Stanford Pipe and Bowl" has
been written by Arch Perrin, '04.
It is being published by Sherman,
Clay and Company of San Fran
cisco, and will be out December
15th. The song consists of two
verses and a chorus. It is dedi
cated to the Glee Club, whose pic
ture will appear on the cover on a
cardinal background. It will be
sung by the Glee Club on its
Northern trip.
