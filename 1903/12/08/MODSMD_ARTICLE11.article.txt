# ASSEMBLY TOMORROW.
## Professor Searles and Captain Young Will Address Last University Gathering. 
### 
Professor Searles of the French
department will be the faculty
speaker at the last Assembly of this
semester, to be held at the
fourth hour tomorrow. The prin
cipal speech will be made by Cap
tain Charles Young of the United
States cavalry, with headquarters
at the Presidio. The subject of
Captain Young's address has not
yet been announced, but he will
probably speak on some phase of
the negro question, in which he is
greatly interested. The University
orchestra will play two numbers,
and there will be the usual singing
and cheering besides.
