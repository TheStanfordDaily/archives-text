# Reduced Railroad Rates.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Company
will give a student's holiday rate
of one and one-third fare for round
trip in California only, and also
in cases where the minimum fare
is at least $5. Further particulars
may be obtained in a few days.
Sleeping car reservations may be
made through J. E. McDowell.
Rates will begin December 15th.
