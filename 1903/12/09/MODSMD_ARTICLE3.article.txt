# Girls' Glee Club Officers.
## 
### 
At a business meeting of the
Girls' Glee Club Monday afternoon,
the following officers were elected
for next year : Miss L. J. Beebe,
'04, president; Miss M. R. Stolz,
'OG, secretary; Miss M. J. Barn
house, '04, treasurer; Miss A. Hol
man, '0(5, business manager. The
concert, planned for this semester
in San .Jose, will be given some
time next semester. It is probable
that the Glee Club will make a
short tour, but nothing definite has
yet been decided.
