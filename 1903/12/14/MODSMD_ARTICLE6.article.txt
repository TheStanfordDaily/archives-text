# Arcade Moot Court Election.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Arcade Moot
Court Friday night the following
officers were elected: W. E. Bil
lings, '03, president; 0. C. Spencer,
'04, secretary and treasurer; R. C.
McComish, '03, sergeant-at-arms.
The executive committee for the
ensuing semester is 0. C. Spencer,
'04, G. D. Boalt, '03, and J. T.
Cooper, '04, This committee will
arrange the calendar of the court
for next semester.
