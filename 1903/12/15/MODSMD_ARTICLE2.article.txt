# Vacation Library Hours.
## 
### 
The Library will observe the
following hours during vacation:
From December 18th until January
sth the doors will be open from
8 a.m. to 4 p.m. Christmas and
New Year's the building will be
closed. The seminary rooms will
be open as usual, and the regular
library privileges will be extended
to the students.
