# Members of Mandolin Club.
## 
### 
The men who will make up the
Mandolin Club this year have been
selected. The organization will be
composed of sixteen men. Five of
these are also on the Glee Club.
The personnel of the Mandolin
Club is as follows:
First Mandolin.—W. H. Lana
gan. '04, leader; N. E. Dole, '04,
N. Collyer, '04, and J. W. Rosen
feld, '06. Second Mandolin. — R.
N. Park, '04, J. M. Beach, '04, A.
W. Hooper, '05, and G. E. Dole,
'07. Guitar.—S. P. Frisselle, '04,
H. A. Weihe, '04, W. D. Carter,'o4,
M. L. Van Norden, '04, F. B.
Goody, '05, H. E. Mush, 05, and
E. R. May, '06. Cello. —M. M.
Stearns, '06.
