# Handball Committee.
## 
### 
Dr. Storey, head of the gymna
sium, has appointed S. P. Frisselle,
'04, W. F. Dunn, '04, and P. E.
Millar, '06, as a committee to ar
range for the championship hand
ball tournament to be held next
semester. Those desiring to enter
this contest must hand their names
to one of the committee immedi
ately after college opens, as it is
intended that the tournament shall
be run off by the latter part of
January. The contest is open only
to those who are taking gymnasium
work. The winner of the tourna
ment will receive a belt, to be given
by Dr. Storey. This trophy is to
be held one year.
