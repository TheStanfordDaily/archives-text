# Harter Married.
## 
### 
On New Year's Day at the First
Congregational Church in Chicago,
Lloyd Harter, '01, was married to
Miss Agnes M. Hansel. While in
college Harter was prominent in
undergraduate life and as secretary
of the Y. M. C. A. made an envi
able name for himself. He was
also a point winner in intercollegi
ate track athletics. The bride and
groom will make their home in
Chicago.
