# Returns from Trip.
## 
### 
Dr. Max Farrand of the History
department returned from the East
today. During his absence lie
visited the universities of Chicago
and Wisconsin and attended the
nineteenth annual meeting of the
American Historical Association,
which was held at New Orleans,
December 29th-31st. At this meet
ing Dr. Farrand read a paper 011
"The Compromises of the Constitu
tion."
