# Engagement Announced.
## 
### 
At a luncheon given to members
of the Kappa Alpha Theta sorority
at the residence of Miss Moore
in Pasadena on New Year's Day,
announcement was made of the en
gagement of Miss Barbara Hitt to
Dr. Alden of the English depart
ment. Mi'ss Hitt was a special stu
dent here in 1901-02 and 1902-03.
While at college she took part in
student affairs, being an active
worker in the English Club and
prominent in the literary field.
Miss Hitt is a member of the
Kappa Alpha Theta sorority.
