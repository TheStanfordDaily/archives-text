# Wedding Announced.
## 
### 
The announcement of the mar
riage of Miss Harriet Mason Mor
ton, '98, to Mr. John Garnett
Holmes, on December 16th, was
made today. Mr. and Mrs.
Holmes are traveling through
Mexico now, and after February
12th will be at home at 11 Salva
tiera street. Mrs. J. M. Morton,
mother of the bride, has issued in
vitations for a reception to be held
at her home upon the return of her
daughter.
