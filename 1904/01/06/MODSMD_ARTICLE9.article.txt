# New Theses Received at Library.
## 
### 
One hundred and eighty-seven
inaugural dissertations from the
University of Erlangen and one
hundred and ten theses from the
University of Jena have been re
ceived at the Library. The theses
are to be found on the shelves of
the reference room.
Those from the University of Er
langen comprise the following sub
jects: Science, law, philosophy, his
tory, literature, music, religion and
chemistry. The theses from Jena
are on law and economics.
