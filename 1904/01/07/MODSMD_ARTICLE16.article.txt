# Resolutions of Sympathy.
## 
### 
Whereas, Providence has seen
fit to claim the life of Miss Cara
Stillman, therefore be it
Resolved, That the Executive
Committee of the Associated Stu
dents extend to Dr. John M. Still
man and his family in their be
reavement the heart-felt sympathy
of the student body; and he it fur
ther
Resolved, That a copy of these
resolutions he spread upon the
minutes of the Associated Students,
and that they be published in the
Daily Palo Alto.
VV. R. Hamilton.
W. H. B. Fowler.
M. A. Thomas,
Chairman Committee.
