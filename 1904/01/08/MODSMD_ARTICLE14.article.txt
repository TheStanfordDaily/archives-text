# Death of Alumnus.
## 
### 
James A. Tucker, '96, died at his
home in Toronto on Dec.'2l, 1903.
At the time of his denth Mr.
Tucker was assistant editor of the
Saturday Night. Before coming to
Stanford he was a student in the
University of Toronto, where lie
was editor of the college paper.
He was rusticated for publishing
an article criticising the Senate
and rather than return to Toronto,
the following year he took his de
gree at Stanford. Mr. Tucker has
been prominent in newspaper work
since leaving college. He leaves a
widow and one child four months
old.
