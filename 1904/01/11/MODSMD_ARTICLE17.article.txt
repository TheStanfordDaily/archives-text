# Funeral of Miss Stillman.
## 
### 
Funeral services for Miss Cara
Stillman, '03, who met death in
the Iroquois theater fire in Chicago,
were held at the residence of her
parents, Dr. and Mrs. John M.
Stillman, on Saturday. Rev. I).
Charles Gardner officiated. After
the ceremonies, which were attended
only by intimate friends of the
family, the remains were taken to
Cypress Lawn Cemetery for crema
tion.
