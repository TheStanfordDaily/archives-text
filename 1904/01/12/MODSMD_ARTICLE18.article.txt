# Crothers a Bank Director.
## 
### 
The first National Bank of Palo
Alto opened its doors for business
yesterday. Among the directors
is George E. Crothers, '95, secretary
of the Board of Trustees of Stan
ford University. The officers are
as follows: President, Clarence E.
Childs; vice-president, Frank E.
Booth; cashier, Alfred W. Hyde;
assistant cashier, Henry P. Ben
nett.
