# "KILTIES" BAND WILL PLAY.
## Famous Canadian Organization Will Appear at Stanford Next March. 
### 
Arrangements have been com
pleted for the appearance of the
well-known Kilties band from
Belleville, Canada, during the
month of March. The hand is
composed of forty muscians and
sixteen vocalists who will sing old
Scottish songs, in the rendition of
which Mr. Andrew Lockhardt, a
Scotch tenor soloist of note, will
assist. The music furnished will
not be wholly of Scottish origin,
but will include every class of com
position. Another feature of the
concert will be dancing by the
Johnstone troupe of Scottish
dancers.
