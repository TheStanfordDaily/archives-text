# Few Entries for Carnot.
## 
### 
The constant withdrawal of can
didates from the entries in the final
try-outs, which will he held Satur
day evening, for the selection of the
1904 Carnot team, has narrowed
the competition down to five men.
The remaining entries are H. M.
Lewis, '04, Frank Roehr, '04, O. H.
Hitter, '04, A. Sheriffs, '05, and A.
H. Dibble. '05. This number is the
smallest that has entered for Car
not honors in the history of the
debate.
