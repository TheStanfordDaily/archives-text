# New Theses at Library.
## 
### 
Seventy-five inaugural disserta
tions from the University of Kon
igsberg, Germany, and eight from
the University of Leyden, Holland,
have been received at the Library.
They have been classified and
placed on the shelves in the refer
ence-room. Forty-five of the dis
sertations from the University of
Konigsberg deal with medical mat
ters, seventeen are on other scien
tific subjects, and the others deal
with miscellaneous topics.
