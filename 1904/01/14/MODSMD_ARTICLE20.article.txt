# Quads.
## 
### 
— Phi Delta Theta defeated
Theta Delta Chi in a five inning
game this afternoon by the score of
27 to 7.
— Wallace Irwin, ex-'OO, is the
author of "The Discoverers," which
is printed in the current issue of
the Saturday Evening Post.
— Miss Irene Wright, '04, had an
illustrated article on the "Salt
Lake Seniors at Stanford " in the
Deseret Netus of January 9 th.
—The marriage of Miss M.
Wooster and Mr. George Casey,
both of Menlo Park, has been
announced for January 31st. Miss
Wooster has been assistant in the
Stanford University Post-office for
three years. Mr. Casey is in busi
ness in San Francisco.
