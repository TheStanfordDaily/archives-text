# In Regard to Scholarships.
## 
### 
George It. Parker, who visited
the United States a year ago as
Commissioner of the Rhodes
Scholarship Trust, has now com
pleted similar visits in Africa and
Australia, organizing everywhere
committees to take charge of the
scholarships established by Cecil
Rhodes. Commissioner Parker is
expected to visit the United States
in April or Ma}' to complete final
arrangements and to pass upon the
preliminary arrangements made
by the special committees. The
trustees of the Rhodes fund are
now giving final consideration to
the plans and subjects to be used
in making the selections. These
plans will be published within a
few weeks.
