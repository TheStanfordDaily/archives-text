# New Piano Arrives.
## 
### 
The Steinway Grand piano, pur
chased by the faculty music com
mittee for use in Assembly Hall,
has arrived, and will be set up
Monday. A lifting device is being
made by Assistant Professor Browne
of the Department of Mechanical
Arts, by means of which the piano
can be lowered into the space be
neath the stage, where it will be
protected from dust and dampness.
The piano will be used for enter
tainments given under the auspices
of the committee. For other enter
tainments it will be available only
upon consent of the committee and
the payment of a certain rental fee
to be fixed by them. The piano
will lie used for the first time at
the Chamber Concert on the even
ing of January 29th.
