# Burglary Attempted.
## 
### 
The assay laboratory in the
Chemistry building was broken
into last night, but no great dam
age was done, Three chemical
balances were taken, but they were
dropped in the field behind the
building and were found there this
morning. The entry was made
through one of the rear windows
by cutting the glass. No clue to
the identity of the thieves has been
found.
