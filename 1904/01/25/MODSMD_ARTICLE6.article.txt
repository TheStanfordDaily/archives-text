# League Will Meet.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Woman's
League for the semester will be
held Friday, January 29th, from
four to six, at the Alpha Phi house.
There will be the usual short mu
sical program. The treasurer will
be present to receive dueH for the
semester, which will be turned over
to the committee furnishing the
Women's Rest-room on the quad.
Every one will be welcome.
