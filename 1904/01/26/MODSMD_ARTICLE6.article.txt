# Thrown from Bicycle.
## 
### 
F. W. Powell, '04, was thrown
from his wheel on the baseball field
last evening. In falling, he struck
his shoulder against the handle
bars and suffered a fractured collar
bone. J)r. Snow set the bone, and
Powell was immediately taken to
the Guild Hospital. Dr. Black is
now attending him. He will be
confined to his room for about a
week, while the bone is knitting
properly.
