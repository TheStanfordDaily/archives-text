# Meeting of Collegiate Alumnae.
## 
### 
The Association of Collegiate
Alumnae will meet Saturday, Jan
uary 30th, in San Francisco. The
meeting will be held at Sorosis
Hall, 1620 California street. Sev
eral reports and papers will be
read, dealing with the relation of
the public schools and public
libraries in California. David S.
Snedden, assistant professor of
Education, will address the asso
ciation on "The Relation of the
Public Library to the Public
School in California, from the
Standpoint of the Schools."
