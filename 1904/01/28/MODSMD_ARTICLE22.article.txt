# Intercollegiate Chess Committee.
## 
### 
The principal business transacted
by the Executive Committee of the
Associated Students at its meeting
last night was the appointment of
a committee whose duty it is to
make all the arrangements for the
intercollegiate contests. The Chess
Club submitted the names of J. H.
Page, '04, A. T. Parsons, '04, and
R. A. Fuller, '05, and these men
were declared elected.
