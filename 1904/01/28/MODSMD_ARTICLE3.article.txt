# Science Association Meets.
## 
### 
The Stanford Science Association
met at the home of Dr. T. A. Storey
last night, and listened to a paper
by Dr. McFarland of the Depart
ment of Physiology. The paper,
which was entitled "The Nuhi
branch Mollusca of Monterey
Bay," embodied the results of sev
eral years research work by Dr.
McFarland at the Hopkins Seaside
Laboratory.
