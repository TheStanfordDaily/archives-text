# Abbot's Inn Calendar.
## 
### 
The calendar of the Court of Ab
bott's Inn has recently been en
tirely changed. The present cal
endar follows:
Feb. 12.— H. W. Strong, '05,
chief justice; I). K. Seibert, '04, M.
H. Thorpe, '05, attorneys.
Feb. 26. —H. E. Holmquist, '04,
chief justice; A. A. Hatnpson, '04,
S. T. Montgomery, '04, attorneys.
Mar. 11. —A. Sherriffs, '05, chief
justice: L. D. Byrne, '05, E. A.
Luce, '05, attorneys.
Mar. 25.1—D. K. Seibert, '04,
chief justice; H. E. Holmquist, '04,
11. VV. Strong, '05, attorneys.
Apr. 8. —L. 1). Byrne, '05, chief
justice; M. il. Thorpe, '05, A. Sher
riffs, '05, attorneys.
Apr. 22. — L. I). Byrne, '05, chief
justice, renders decision.
