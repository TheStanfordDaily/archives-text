# Dr. Jordan Answers Toast.
## 
### 
President Jordan was one of the
guests at a banquet given at the
Bohemian Club in San Francisco
last night in honor of William
Butlfer Yeats, the Irish poet and
playwright, who delivered a lecture
at Stanford on Friday . afternoon.
The banquet was given by James
D. Phelan. There were a number
of informal toasts, Dr. Jordan
making one of the speeches.
