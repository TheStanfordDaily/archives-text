# Junior Class Election.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Junior Class
this afternoon the officers who were
nominated at the recent meeting
were elected for the coming semes
ter. The vote was by acclamation.
Following are the names of the offi
cers: President, W. H. Dole; vice
president, A. L. Trowbridge; secre
tary, Miss H. L. Crandall; treas
urer, T. M. Coen; sergeant-at-arms,
G. R. Stevens.
