# Guild Report for January.
## 
### 
At the beginning of the month
there were five patients in the
Guild Hospital. Since then four
teen have been admitted, and
eleven discharged. At the end of
the month there were eight remain
ing. During the whole month
nineteen patients were in the hos
pital for a total of one hundred
and forty-five days. At the present
time there are seven patients, the
majority of whom will he dis
charged this week.
