# California Notes.
## 
### 
—President Benjamin Ide Whee
ler left yesterday for the East, to
attend the meeting of theAssociation
American of Universities at New
Haven.
—Beginning with next semester
freshmen at the University of Cali
fornia will be compelled to wear a
blue cap with a yellow button on
top.
— Plans are being formed at Cal
ifornia to set aside February 29th
as a Labor Day. According to the
plans every student will do eight
hours of work on the university
grounds, improving the entrance to
the Greek theatre and widening the
road between the chemistry and
botany buildings. The last stu
dent Labor Day was February 29,
1896.
