# Orchestra Trip Posters.
## 
### 
The posters are out announcing
the concerts to be given on the trip
of the Girls' Glee Club and the Uni
versity Orchestra; they will be
placed on sale at the Bookstore on
Monday. The poster is by Miss
Clara Lathrop, '06.
The two organizations were en
tertained last evening at the home
of Mrs. Wilson, in Palo Alto. A
musical program was rendered,
after which a short reception took
place.
