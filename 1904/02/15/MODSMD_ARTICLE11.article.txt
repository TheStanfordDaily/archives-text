# Gymnasium Club Exhibition.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Encina Gym
nasium Club last week it was de
cided to give the annual exhibition
on Friday evening, April 22d.
This will be the Friday following
the intercollegiate field meet. No
definite arrangements have been
made in regard to the character of
the exhibition, but probably the
work will be much the same as
that of previous years.
