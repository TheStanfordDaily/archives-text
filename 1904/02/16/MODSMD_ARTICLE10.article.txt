# Will Receive Radium.
## 
### 
Princeton is to receive a gift of
twelve and a half grains of radio
active barium chloride for experi
mental purposes. The gift will be
the first output of the Uranium
and Metal Company of Buffalo,
and will be sent to Princeton in
recognition of the service of Profes
sor Alexander H. Phillips, said to
be the first American to extract
radium from an American ore.
