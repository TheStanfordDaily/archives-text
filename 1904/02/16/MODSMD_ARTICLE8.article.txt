# Vesper Services Resumed.
## 
### 
The removal of the scaffolding
and the departure of the force of
workmen from Memorial Church
permits the Thursday afternoon
Vesper services to be resumed. The
first of these will be held on Feb
ruary 25th, at 4:30. A large num
ber of Bibles, psalm-books, and
hymn-books have been unpacked
for use in the church, and one of
each will be placed in every pew
for each person. At the present
time about 1800 books will be
distributed.
The next special preacher will be
Rabbi Voorsanger of the Temple
Emmanuel, San Francisco. He will
deliver a sermon on February 28th.
