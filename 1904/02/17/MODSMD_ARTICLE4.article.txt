# Deciding Match in Tournament.
## 
### 
The final match for the handball
belt will be held Thursday after
noon at 3. The contestants will he
G. Cromwell, '04, and M. C. Sher
wood, '05. This will decide the
championship for the year in
handball among students registered
in the gymnasium. W. F. Dunn,
'04, who has held the belt continu
ously for the three years it has
been offered, is not eligible for the
contest this year, as he is not regis
tered for gymnasium work. Forty
two men have played in the tourna
ment this season. The match will
be an event of interest, and a gen
eral invitation is extended to all
who may wish to see the game
tomorrow to he present at the gym
nasium.
