# Promenade Concert.
## 
### 
The band-stand, for which the
Executive Committee a ppropriated
-$75 at its meeting Wednesday
night, is now being built and will
lie ready for use at the Promenade
Concert next Friday evening. The
stand will be made in sections, so
that it may he set up and taken
down without difficulty. The
Band is practicing diligently on
the new program, the numbers of
which will he published next week.
In case of rainy weather the Prom
enade may be postponed for a
month, or until there is another
full moon.
