# President Jordan Due.
## 
### 
President Jordan is expected to
return to the University tonight or
tomorrow. He will attend the
meeting of the Board of Trustees in
San Francisco tomorrow morning.
Dr. Jordan went East three weeks
ago in order to attend the annual
meeting of the Association of Amer
ican Universities at New Haven,
Conn., and also to speak at the
dedication of Palmer Hall, the new
science building of Colorado Col
lege at Colorado Springs.
