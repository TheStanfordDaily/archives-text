# Stanford Alumni Dinner.
## 
### 
Stanford graduates and former
Stanford students residing in San
Francisco, of whom there are about
two hundred, have arranged for a
club dinner, to take place at Jules'
restaurant on the evening of
March 4th. The dinner will he in
the nature of a big reunion and
will be an informal affair. Charles
K. Field, '95, is chairman of the
dinner committee. The dinner
will begin at 7 o'clock. Plates
will be $1.50 each.
