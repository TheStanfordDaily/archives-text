# Concert by Musical Clubs.
## 
### 
The second return concert of the
Combined Musical Clubs will be
given in Palo Alto next Saturday
night. It will take place in Mul
len's Hall at 8. Tickets will be
placed on sale in Palo Alto within
a few days. The prices will be 50
and 75 cents. This concert is given
for the benefit of people from Palo
Alto and surrounding towns who
were unable to attend the concert
given in Assembly Hall last month.
The program will be practically
the same as that given in Assem
bly Hall, although a few altera
tions and improvements will be
made. The troupe will include
all the man of the (ilee and Mando
lin Clubs, who have been prac
ticing regularly for the coming
concert.
