# Dinner of Stanford Club.
## 
### 
The annual dinner of the Stan
ford Club, which will be held at
Jules restaurant in San Francisco
tomorrow night, promises to be a
very interesting and successful af
fair. The attendance will be main
ly of graduate students living in
San Francisco or thereabouts and
of a number of prominent under
graduates. There will also be a
number of faculty men present.
Charles K. Field, '95, who has the
matter in charge, states that the
attendance will be large. There
will be a number of general talks
and a discussion of the project of
securing permanent quarters for
the club. The dinner, which will
be informal, will begin at 7.
