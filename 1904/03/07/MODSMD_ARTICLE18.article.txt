The Historic Way.
Many see Indian pueblos from
the windows of the "California
Limited," but few realize that they
are centuries old. Through Mex
ico, Colorado, and Kansas, where
now speed the Santa Fe trains,
once roamed Kit Carson the Scout,
with many another border hero. If
interested in going the historic way
to the World's Fair, ask about the
Santa Fe at 641 Market street, San
Francisco.
Moral: The Santa Fe is the
Historic Way.
