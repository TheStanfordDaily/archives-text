# Lecture on World's Fair.
## 
### 
The subject on which Lieutenant
Godfrey L. Garden will speak in
the chapel Thursday evening is
"The Engine Display at the
World's Fair." The lecture will
he illustrated with lantern-slides.
No admission will he charged, and
all students are invited to attend.
The lecture will he of especial in
terest, as Lieutenant Garden has
just completed an exhaustive re
search in Europe in the interests of
the department of arsenal tools, of
which lie is superintendent.
