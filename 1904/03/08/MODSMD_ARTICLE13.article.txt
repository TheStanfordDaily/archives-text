# Quads.
## 
### 
— During the month of January
fifteen patients were cared for at
the Guild hospital.
—A complete file of the San
Francisco Bulletin dating hack to
1860 has been received at the
Library.
— About forty books that will be
used by the debaters in preparation
for the Intercollegiate debate have
been placed in the reference-room
in the University Library.
—The plav recently presented at
California by the dramatic society
at that university will be given at
Stanford under the auspices of
Sword and Sandals, probably on
March 19th.
