# The 						Kilties Concert.
## 
### 
Several hundred persons attended
the Kilties Hand concert in Assem
bly Hall Friday evening. The
band seemed to have improved
considerably since it whs last here,
and its reception was warm. En
cores were given for every piece of
music. There was especial enthu
siasm over the songs, the choir
coming in for a large amount of
applause. There are fifteen voices
in the choir, some of them very
fine. The dancing was also a spe
cial feature.
