# St. Louis Picture Exhibited.
## 
### 
The panoramic view of Stanford
University, which is to he sent to
the St. Louis Exposition, is now on
exhibition in San Jose at the corner
of Second and San Fernando
streets. The picture is fifteen feet
in length. It was taken by the
Board of Trade of Santa Clara
County and is to he a part of the
educational exhibit of the county
at the exposition.
