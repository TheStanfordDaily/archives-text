# Tickets for Prom on Sale.
## 
### 
Tickets for the Junior Prom were
placed on sale this morning at the
post-office. The number of tickets
purchased was good when it is
taken into consideration that the
dance will not take place for nearly
two weeks. The sale of tickets will
be continued at the post-office and
it is expected that there will be an
increase in the number of purchas
ers tomorrow.
