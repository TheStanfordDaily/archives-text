# Statistics of "Pass" Conditions.
## 
### 
Manager Cowden has received a
letter from R. B. Steele of Vander
bilt University in regard to the
manner and extent of giving com
plimentary entrance to campus
games at Stanford. The Athletic
Committee of Vanderbilt Univer
sity is making a study of the com
plimentary admission privileges
extended at games throughout the
colleges of the United States to the
following persons: members of
the different varsity teams; mem
bers of second teams; managers of
opposing teams; newspaper men.
There is included in the inquiry
a question in regard to who issues
the passes.
