# Stanford Man Dies.
## 
### 
Arthur W. Greeley, '98, died yes
terday morning in St. Louis after
an operation for appendicitis.
Greeley was graduated in 1898 and
took the master of arts degree in
1899. He was instructor in Biology
at the San Diego State Normal
School for some time after his
graduation.
Later he went to the University
of Chicago and became a pupil of
Dr. Jacques Loeb, the famous bio
logist. He took his doctor's degree
at Chicago. At the time of his
death he was assistant professor of
physiology in Washington Univer
sity, St. Louis. He was a brilliant
student and published a number of
articles. He was a member of the
Delta Upsilon fraternity.
