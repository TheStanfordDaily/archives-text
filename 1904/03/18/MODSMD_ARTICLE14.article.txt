# Money for Engineering Students.
## 
### 
On Wednesday, March 9th, Sen
ator Matthew S. Quay introduced a
bill into the United States Senate
appropriating $50,000 to be divid
ed among Franklin Institute of
Philadelphia, and Purdue Univer
sity of Lafayette, Indiana, Phila
delphia Commercial Museums, and
the engineering department of the
University of Pennsylvania, for the
purpose of determining the quality
of the so-called hammer-blow, cen
trifugal lift and tangenial throw of
the counter-balance in locomotive
driving-wheels.
