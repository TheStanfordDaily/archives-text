# Calendar Will Be Issued.
## 
### 
The intercollegiate athletic cal
endar, which is published once
every four years by Yale, Harvard,
Princeton, Columbia, Cornell and
Pennsylvania, is now in press and
will soon appear.
The contents will embody cuts of
the principal buildings of all the
colleges represented. There will
also be pictures of the athletic
teams, baseball, football, track and
crew. The calendar will contain
tabulated results of all athletic
events for the past few years.
