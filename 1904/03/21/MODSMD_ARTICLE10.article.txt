# Collegiate Alumnae to Meet.
## 
### 
The California branch of the
Association of Collegiate Alumnae,
will hold its regular meeting Satur
day, March 2 6th, in San Francisco.
Professor Max Farrand will deliver
a lecture on "The New Interpreta
tion of American History, and its
Importance to California." The
Association will entertain as guests
of honor, Mrs. M. I). Trear of
Wellesley; Mrs. L. B. Powers and
Miss E. Keith, hoth organizers of
the California Historic Landmarks
League.
