# Exhibition of Paintings.
## 
### 
There will he an exhibition of
paintings by Mr. George L. Noyes
from two until five o'clock on
Wednesday and Thursday after
noon at Professor H. A. Rolfe's res
idence, Lincoln avenue and
Itamona street. There will be
about fifty pictures on exhibition,
and some very good landscape
paintings will lie among the num
ber. There are some New England
seacoast views and also some land
scapes representing scenes in the
vicinity of Pacific Grove. A few
paintings of local scenes will also
be displayed at the exhibition.
