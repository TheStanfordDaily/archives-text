# Celebrates Seventieth Anniversary.
## 
### 
At a mass meeting of the Har
vard students yesterday, President
Eliot was presented with an
engrossed address signed hy nearly
10,000 Harvard men. The present
was in honor of the seventieth
anniversary of President Eliot's
birth.
