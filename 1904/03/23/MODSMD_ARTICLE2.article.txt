# College Notes.
## 
### 
—The training-table for the
Yale crew has started with ten
men. More will be taken later in
the season.
—Seventy covers were laid at the
sixth annual banquet of the daily
Princeton inn board recently given
in the Princeton Inn. Among those
who spoke were W. K. Van Iteypen
of the Yale daily News; W. It.
Bowie of the Harvard Crimson;
and President Woodrow Wilson of
Princeton University.
