# Elected Junior Track Captain.
## 
### 
W. E. Crawford was elected cap
tain of the Junior class track team
at a meeting of the '05 men on the
oval this afternoon. Crawford took
part in the intercollegiate meet
last year, when he won the hammer
throw. He will throw the hammer
for Stanford again in the next
meet. Crawford is a student in
the Chemistry department and
registers from Cupertino.
