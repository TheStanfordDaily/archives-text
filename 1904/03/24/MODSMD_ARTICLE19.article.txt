# Interclass Meet at California.
## 
### 
The interclass field meet held at
Berkeley yesterday was notable on
account of the performance of
freshman Sperry, who put the 16-
pound shot 43 feet 3 inches. No
other performances were notable
except Abadie's time in the 100
yard dash, which he won easily, on
a poor track, in 0:10 2-5. The
seniors won the meet with 42
points, the juniors 23, the sopho
mores 19, and the freshmen 33.
The highest individual point
winner was Snedigar, who made
13 points, scoring second in the
100 yard dash, first in the 220 yard
dash, and first in the running
broad jump.
