# California Notes.
## 
### 
—In the intercollegiate tennis
match California will be represent
ed in the singles by McGavin and
Baker, and in the doubles by
McGavin and Variel.
—The preliminary examinations
for the Rhodes Scholarship will be
held at Berkeley April 13th. These
examinations will be the first of a
series to determine California's
representative, and will only de
cide who is to be chosen in so far
as those who fail to pass them will
be ineligible to compete further.
Other examinations will come later
and from the successful candidates
in these President Wheeler will
select the man whom he considers
the best qualified.
