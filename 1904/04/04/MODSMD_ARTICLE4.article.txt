# ASSEMBLY NEXT WEDNESDAY.
## Dr. Lyman Abbott of New York Will Speak to Student Body. 
### 
There will be a University As
sembly held next Wednesday at
the fourth hour. The principal
speaker will be Dr. Lyman Abbott.
Dr. Abbott is the best known cler
gyman in the United States, and is
noted as an author and editor.
His articles on the Bible have a
wide circulation. As an editor Dr.
Abbott first came into prominence
when in charge of the Literary
Record of Harper's Magazine.
Later he was associated with
Henry Ward Beecher in editing
the Christian Union. He is at
present an editor of the Outlook.
