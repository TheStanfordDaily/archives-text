# Combined Clubs at San Mateo.
## 
### 
The combined Musical Clubs
gave a conceit in San Mateo last
night under the auspices of the
Women's Auxiliary Civic League.
They were enthusiastically received
by a large audience. Bart of the
proceeds will go towards charitable
purposes.
The dates of the exchange con
certs with the University of Cali
fornia clubs have been definitely
set. The California organization
will give a combined concert with
the Stanford clubs in Assembly
Hall on the evening of April 16th,
the day of the intercollegiate field
meet. The Stanford clubs will
appear at Berkeley on April 30th.
