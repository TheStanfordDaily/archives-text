# Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Stanford
Club of San Francisco last
Wednesday night the following
officers were elected for the ensuing
year: J. F. English, '01, president;
Paul M. Downing, '95, vice-presi
dent; E. R. Zion, '94, secretary
treasurer. Forty men were present,
'95 having the largest delegation.
