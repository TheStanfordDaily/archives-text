# California Notes.
## 
### 
—The plans for the new athletic
field were submitted to the execu
tive committee yesterday. According
to these plans the diamond,football
field, and track will be placed in
close proximity.
—The sophomore class has de
cided to substitute a "cane spree"
for the traditional Charter Hill
"rush," which has been abolished
as a result of the recent interven
tion on the part of the faculty.
—Twenty-eight men of the Cali
fornia Glee Club will make a trip
to the St. Louis Exposition during
the summer. The tour will in
clude stops at the principal towns
of southern California and at the
cities en route to St. Louis.
