# Second Promenade Concert.
## 
### 
The University, Band in conjunc
tion with the Glee Club and the
Mandolin Cluh, will give an open
air concert on the quad on the
evening of April 15th. The pro
gram will consist of eight numbers
by the Band and two selections by
each of the musical clubs. The
concert is intended to serve as a
rally before the intercollegiate field
meet. The concert will begin at
2:30 and last about two hours.
