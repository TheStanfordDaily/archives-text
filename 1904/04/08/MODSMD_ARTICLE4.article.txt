# Finals for Women's Tennis Cup.

###

semifinals and finals for the
women's tennis cup will he played
on the Roble courts. A number of
the preliminary matches have al
ready been played, Miss Abbott,
'06, Miss Foster, '04, and Miss
Paine, '00, being the only contes
tants who have not played.
The preliminary scores resulted
as follows: Miss Pitman, '05, won
from Miss Gilbert, '06. by default.
Miss Owen, '07, defeated Mrs. Mil
ler, Michigan '95, 6-4, 3-6, 6-4.
Miss Hayward, '06, won from Miss
