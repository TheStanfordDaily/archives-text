# Dr. Jordan Receives Appointment.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan has been chosen vice
president of the International
Peace Congress to meet in Boston
October 3d. The other vice-presi
dents are Andrew White, Senator
Edmunds, Edward Everett Hale,
Albert K. Smiley, and Edwin
Ginn. The president is the Hon
orable Robert Treat Paine.
