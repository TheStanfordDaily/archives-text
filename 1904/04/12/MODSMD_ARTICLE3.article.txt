# '06 "Quad" Election Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The election for editor and busi
ness manager of the '06 Quad will
be held tomorrow. The candidates
for the editorship are E. R. May
and Miss C. M. Lathrop, and for
the business managership A. B.
Cheadle and H. I. Cruzan. The
election will be on the quad, and
the polls will 'be open from 11:15
until '2:30. The officials will be
E. A. Cunha, C. M. Hadley, J. E.
Campbell, 1). 1). Sales, and C. S.
Osgood. Every voter will be re
quired to pay the assessment of 25
cents levied at the class meeting a
week ago.
