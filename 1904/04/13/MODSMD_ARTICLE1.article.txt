# RALLY TOMORROW NIGHT.
## Track Team Will Be Given an Ovation in Assembly Hall at 7:30. 
### 
A rally will be held in Assembly
Hall tomorrow evening in honor of
the track team. The doors will be
opened at 7:80 sharp. There will
be speeches, singing, and yelling.
The University Band and the Glee
Club will take part. Among the
speakers there will be Dr. Jordan,
Assistant Professor Lewers of the
Law department, Associate Profes
sor Adams of the History depart
ment, Instructor Whittaker of the
Economics department, and Train
er Moulton of the track team.
