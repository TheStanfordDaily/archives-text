# Philological Association Will Meet.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Uni
versity Philological Association
will he held in room 31 at 2:30 to
morrow. Instructor B. O. Foster
of the Latin department will read
a paper entitled "Word-accent in
Latin verse." Dr. Goebel will
speak on "The Etymology of
Mephistopheles."
