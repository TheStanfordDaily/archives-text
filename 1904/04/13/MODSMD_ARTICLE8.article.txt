# Senior Class Meets.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Senior class
this afternoon the final report of
A. S. Henley, Quad manager last
year, was heard. The report, show
ing a deficit of $67.30, was turned
over to the auditing committee.
It was also decided to appoint a
committee of three to report on the
advisability of electing a perma
nent class secretary. President
Brua has not yet announced the
names of those on the committee.
