# Dr. Harris Will Lecture.
## 
### 
I)r. W. C. Harris, for a number
of years U. S. Consul and mission
ary in Japan, will lecture in the
chapel tomorrow afternoon at 1:30.
The subject of bis lecture is " The
Japan-Russian War and Some
Problems of the Far East." Dr.
Harris lias the reputation of being
intimately acquainted with the
problems in the East which gave
rise to the present war, and his
lecture can not fail to be of the
deepest interest at the present time.
