# Chess Pins Awarded.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Executive
Committee last night it was decided
to award chess pins to all the mem
bers of the intercollegiate chess
team who participated in the
match last Friday night. Those
who will receive pins are: R. A.
Fuller, '05, J. H. Page, '03, F. E.
Ulrich, 'OG, H. W. Strong, '06, A.B.
Rhuart, '04, M. M. Stearns, '06,
and A. T. Parsons, '04. Of these
Rhuart and Stearns win the em
blem for the first time.
