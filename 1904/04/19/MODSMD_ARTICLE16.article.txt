# Article Published by Lawson.
## 
### 
Instructor A. A. Lawson of the
Botany department has recently
published si pamphlet entitled
"The Gametophyte, Archegonia,
Fertilization, and Embryo of Se
quoia Sempervirens." The pam
phlet contains about thirty pages,
it gives a detailed account of the
life history of the Coast redwood.
There are also seven pages of draw
ings by Mr. Lawson, showing the
gametophytes of Sequoia.
