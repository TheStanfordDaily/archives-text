# Reading for Law Students.
## 
### 
In place of the regular lecture to
the Law students of the University
which the dean of the department
has been delivering at intervals
during the past term, Rev. D.
Charles Gardner will give a
reading at 7:30 tomorrow eve
ning in room 2 of the Law
building. He will read from "Pick
wick Papers" the famous case of
"Bardell vs. Pickwick," a suit for
breach of promise, immortalized by
Dickens. Although these lectures
are intended primarily for Law
students, any one interested is in
vited to attend.
