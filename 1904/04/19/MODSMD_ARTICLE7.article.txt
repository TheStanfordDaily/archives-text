# Y.W.C.A. Will Give Reception.
## 
### 
Invitations have been issued by
the Capitola Conference enthusiasts
of the Y. W. C. A. for a luncheon
and reception to be given in the
Roble gymnasium Saturday after
noon, April 23d. The conference
committee, under the direction of
Miss Helen Salisbury, aims at in
teresting more of the women in the
work of the Y. W. C. A., and par
ticularly in the annual Capitola
Conference.
