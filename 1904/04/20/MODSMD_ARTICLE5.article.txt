# Gymnasium Exhibition.
## 
### 
The regular exhibition of the
Stanford Gymnasium Club will be
given in Encina gymnasium next
Friday evening at 8. Besides the
usual apparatus work, there will be
pyramid building and tumbling.
The members of the club are also
endeavoring to secure Japanese
wrestlers or fencers for the evening.
Trainer Moulton will give an exhi
bition of his trained dogH.
