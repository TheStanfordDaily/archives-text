# Athletic Committee Meets.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Athletic
Committee last night the following
were accepted as Stanford records:
H. L. Hamilton 0:10.1 in the
100-yard dash; W. H. Lanagan
0:26 in the low hurdles; VV. F.
Dunn 0:22.3 in the 220-yard dash;
O. E. Hyde 45 feet 64 inches in the
ehot-put; H. W. Bell and N. E.
Dole 5 feet 11 inches in the high
jump; N. E. Dole 11 feet inches
in tlie pole-vault; R. E. West and
H. W. Bell 22 feet inches in the
broad jump.
The following track men were
given Varsity sweaters: F. S. Hol
man, '04, W. F. Dunn, '04, \V. H.
Lanagan, '04, N. E. Dole, '04, J. M.
Beach, '04, O. E. Hyde, '04, H. L.
Hamilton, '04, A. S. Henley, '04,
H. W. Bell, '05, W. E. Crawford,
'05, R. F. West, '06, and J. C. Mac-
Farland, '07.
Varsity sweaters were also grant
ed to the following baseball men:
R. B. Ball, '04, R B. Knight, '04,
W. E. Tritch, '04. A.L.Trowbridge,
'05. D. D. Sales, '06, W. Wirt, '06,

Swain, '07.
