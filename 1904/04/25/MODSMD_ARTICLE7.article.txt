# Give Successful Luncheon.
## 
### 
The luncheon given Saturday by
the women interested in the Capi
tola conference to the women of the
University was a marked success.
As a result of the enthusiatic gath
ering. about seventy-five girls have
expressed an intention of attending
the annual conference, which will
convene at Capitola from May 14th
to May 25th. This will be the
largest number of delegates which
has ever attended from Stanford.
Any desired information will he
given by Miss F. G. Hammond,'o6,
the secretary, or Miss B. 11. French,
