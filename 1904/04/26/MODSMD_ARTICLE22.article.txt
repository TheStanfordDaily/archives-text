# BASEBALL CAPTAIN ELECTED.
## A.L. Trowbridge, Shortstop on This Year's Varsity, Chosen to Lead the Squad of 1905. 
### 
At a banquet given to the Var
sity baseball team in San Fran
cisco on the night of the intercol
legiate game, A. L. Trowbridge, '05,
was unanimously elected captain
of the 1905 team. Trowbridge has
played on the Varsity two years,
his work at the position of short
stop throughout the present season
having been of a very high order.
He is a Civil Engineering major
and registers from Portland, Ore.
He is a member of the Stanford
Gymnasiu u Club and Encina
Club.
