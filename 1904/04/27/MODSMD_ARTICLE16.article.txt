# Meeting of "Quad " Committee.
## 
### 
The 1905 Quad Committee met
last night and made the final ar
rangements for the sale of the
Quad. The book will he placed on
sale next Tuesday. There is a lim
ited edition of about the same
number as last year, and conse
quently one person will not be al
lowed to purchase more than five
copies. The Quad will contain
about three hundred and fifty
pages and will he sold for $2.50.
