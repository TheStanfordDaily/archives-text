# Assembly Next Wednesday.
## 
### 
There will he a University As
sembly held next Wednesday at
the fourth hour. The principal
speaker will he John McNaught of
the San Francisco Call. The sub
ject of his discussion will include
some of the phases of newspaper
work as a profession. Mr. Mc-
Naught is the managing editor of
the Call, and is considered one of
the best after-dinner speakers in
San Francisco.
