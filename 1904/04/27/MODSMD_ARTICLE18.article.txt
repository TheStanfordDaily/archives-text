# Sigma Xi Annual Address.
## 
### 
The annual address before the
Stanford chapter of the scientific
society of Sigma Xi will be given
in the University chapel by Dr.
W. W. Campbell, Director of the
Lick Observatory, Thursday eve
ning, May sth, at 8. The subject
of the address is, "The Motion of
the Solar System through Space."
It will he illustrated by lantern
slides.
