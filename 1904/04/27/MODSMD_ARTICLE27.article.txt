of her one hundred and fiftieth an
niversary.

new buildings to cost $2,000,000

and Cornell universities for the
proposed stale agricultural college
of New York has been won by Cor
nell.

athletic committee of the Univer
sity of Pennsylvania all special
students will be ruled out of ath
letics. This will reduce the num
ber of available men at least
