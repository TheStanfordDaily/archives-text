election hits placed the stamp of its
approval upon certain candidates
who will he at the head of the
affairs of the Associated Students
during the ensuing year. Two
contests for important offices were
hard fought, hut they have been
decided and the officers chosen.

as individual students may have
been, there is but one thing to do
now —support the successful candi
dates. It is in the power of the
student body to help or to handi
cap its officers,and it is to be hoped
that next year's officers will not be
forced to perform their duties
under disadvantageous circum
stances.
