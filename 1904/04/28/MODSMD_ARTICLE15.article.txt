# Lecture by Mr. Sayer.
## 
### 
The lecture on "Electric Drills"
given - by E. Y. Saver, '98, this
morning was attended by a large
number of engineering men. Mr.
Saver spoke about the different
kinds of electric drills used in min
ing work and also about those used
in subway construction. He dis
cussed the merits of each and gave
interesting accounts from his own
experience. Next Tuesday at 11:15
Mr. Sayer will give another lecture
on the "Application of Electricity
to Mining."
