# "Quad" Committee Appointed.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Sophomore
class this afternoon the president
appointed a committee to have
charge of the financial affairs of
tho 1906 Quad. These persons will
form the auditing committee, and
will report on the finances of the
Quad to the class. The following
persons were selected to form the
committee: Miss R. Kimble, Miss
E. R. Mirrielees, W. Wirt, J. F. Ga
lloon, and L. \Y\ Cutler.
