# Quadrangle Club Election.
## 
### 
At its annual spring election
held the first Monday in May the
following men were elected to the
Quadrangle Club:
Harry Wesley Bell, Healdshurg,
California;
Benjamin Clifford Dey, Santa
Cruz, California;
Arthur McQueen Dibble, Portland,
Oregon;
Wilfred Henry Dole, Riverside,
California;
William Henry Lanagan, Denver,
Colorado;
Alexander Sherriffs, San Jose, Cali
fornia;
Merle Harrold Thorpe, Ellsworth,
Kansas;
Alfred Lockwood Trowbridge, Port
land, Oregon.
