# Ornithological Club to Meet.

### 
The regular monthly meeting of
the Cooper Ornithological Club of
California will lie held at the resi
dence of Professor 0. I'. Jenkins, 3
Lasuen street, tomorrow evening.
The speakers for the evening are:
Assistant-Professor J. O. Snyder,
whose subject is "Notes on Junco
hyemalis Thurberi"; C. S. Thomp
son, '04, who will speak on "A Hit
of 1C kr 1 y Ornithological Litera
ture"; and Nelson Carpenter, '07,
who will speak on "Bird Life on
