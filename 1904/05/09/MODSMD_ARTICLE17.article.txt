# Princeton-Yale Meet.
## 
### 
Saturday at New Haven Yale de
feated Princeton in the annual
field-day by the score of 77 to 27.
McLanahan, Yale '05, is credited
with having vaulted 12 feet in ex
hibition, but in competition the
best he did was 11 feet 84 inches.
DeWitt of Princeton threw the 16-
pound hammer 164 feet 6| inches,
and Glass of Yale put the shot 46
feet 34 inches, beating the record
made in the dual meet by Meek of
Yale, who holds the intercollegiate
record.
