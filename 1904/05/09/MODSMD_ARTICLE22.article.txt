# Announcements.
## 
### 
Sword and Sandals. — Impor
tant meeting at 1:15 in room 11,
Tuesday. Election.
Senior Opkra Tickets —Will be
on sale until Thursday afternoon
at the University Bookstore. Prices
$1, 75c, and 50c.
Seniors. —All Seniors who wish
to rent caps and gowns for
Commencement should leave their
orders at the Bookstore at once.
Seniors. — Assessments will he
$4. I will be at room 10, daily
from 1 to 1:30, to receive payment.
H. L. Hamilton, Treasurer.
Senior Ball Tickets—Will be
on sale at the Bookstore until Sat
urday. Saturday and Monday
they will be sold at the post-office.
Senior Opera Rehearsals.—
Assembly Hall, Tuesday and Wed
nesday nights at 7, for principals;
7:30, for principals and chorus.
Training-Table Dues. —All pay
ments due from track and football
men must be made by Wednesday,
May 11th. D. V. Cowden,
Treasurer.
Junior Class—Will meet in the
chapel at 1:15 Tuesday to discuss
the advisability of appropriating
money toward the fund for sending
the track team East. W. H. Dole.
Y. W. C. A. —The final meeting
of the Y. W. C. A. will be held to
morrow at 4:30 in the chapel.
Miss Helen Salisbury, '05, presi
dent of the association, will be the
leader. All University women are
urged to be present.
Applied Mathematics.— Atten
tion is called to the fact that
students deficient in any course in
Applied Mathematics can not be
admitted to courses of the follow
ing year in Applied Mathematics
nor to courses 2 and 3 in Engineer
ing. Examinations for the remov
al of conditions and incompletes
will be given on the first registra
tion day at the opening of the fall
semester.
