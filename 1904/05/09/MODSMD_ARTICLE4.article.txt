# Will Repair Tennis Courts.
## 
### 
Immediately after the close of
college the repairing of the asphalt
court in front of Encina Hall will
he commenced. This court was
completed early in the spring, hut
the heavy rains caused it to settle
in places. The paint used in put
ting the freshman numerals on the
court also made it very uneven.
1). E. Brown of San Francisco, the
contractor who built the court,
expects to have the work com
pleted before college opens in the
fall.
