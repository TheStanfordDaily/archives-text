# OPERA CAST SELECTED.
## Complete Personnel of Senior's Play "Patience" Decided Upon. 
### 
The complete cast of the Senior
opera, '' Patience," has been com
pleted. The principal parts will
be taken by Miss E. J. Brown, '04,
C. H. Baker, '04, and E. R. May,
'06. Miss Brown will take the role
of " Patience," a dairymaid. Bak
er will act the part of "Reginald
Bunthorne," and May will be
"Archibald Grosvenor," an idyllic
poet. The complete list of the cast
is as follows:
Patience Miss E. J. Brown
Reginald Bunthorne.. C. H. Baker

Tickets for the opera were for
sale today on the quad and in Palo
Alto. The majority of the seats on
the ground floor are already dis
posed of, the remainder are practi
cally the same as at the large sale
of tickets at the Junior Farce. The
sum of $225 was taken in on the
quad this morning, while the sale
at Palo Alto was large. After to
day the remaining seats will be on
sale at the Bookstore. A number
of good seats still remain on the
main floor and many in the gal
lery.
