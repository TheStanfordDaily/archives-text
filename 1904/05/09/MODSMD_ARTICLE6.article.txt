# Receives Fellowship at Chicago.
## 
### 
W. I). Smith, graduate student
and assistant in Blow-pipe Analy
sis, has received a fellowship fit the
University of Chicago. He will
take this up next semester instead
of returning to his work at Stan
ford. Smith's line of work at
Chicago will begeology and inverte
brate paleontology. After com
pleting a year's work on his
fellowship, lie will take up an
assistantship in the same subject
at the University of Wisconsin.
During the comparatively short
time that Smith lias been at the
University he has done work that

recognition that it has received.
