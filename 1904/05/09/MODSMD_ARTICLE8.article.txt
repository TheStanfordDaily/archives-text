# Debating League Officers.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the lntersociety
Debating League held Saturday
night the following officers were
elected: President, A. Sherriffs, '05;
vice-president, W. L. Blair, '05;
secretary, P. D. Swing, '05. A com
mittee consisting of
M. H. Thorpe, '05, and P. D. Swing,
'05, to organize and manage the
Freshman Debating Society.
Sherriffs, the new president of
the Debating League, has been a
prominent figure in debating circles
for the past two years. He was a
member of both his freshman and
sophomore debating teams. Last
year he was a member of the team
which contested with the Univer
sity of Washington. He has been
a member this year of both the
Carnot and Intercollegiate teams.
He is a member of the Stanford de
bating society of San Jose. He is
a member of the Kappa Sigma fra
ternity.
