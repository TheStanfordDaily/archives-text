# Annual Reception to Freshmen.
## 
### 
On Friday evening, September 2d,
the Y. M. C. A. and the Y. W. C. A.
will tender their annual reception
to the Freshmen. The reception
will be held in the Encina gymna
sium from Bto 10. It is expected
that Dr. Jordan will give a brief
address to the incoming class. All
members of the student body are
most cordially invited to be present.
