# LARGE REGISTRATION.
## Figures Taken This Evening Show Material Increase Over Last Year. 
### 
The registration at five this eve
ning had reached a total of 1307.
This makes the largest registration
in the history of the University at
this time in the semester. Of those
registered up to date eight hundred
and forty-seven are old students,
and four hundred and sixty are
freshmen. As compared with last
year the increase is one hundred
and seventy-six in the total num
ber, and ninety-nine in the num
ber of new students. Year before
last, which was the largest up to
the present, was one thousand two
hundred and fifty, of which four
hundred and thirty-one were new
students.
