# Will Not Return.
## 
### 
A. B. Cheadle, '06, who was last
semester elected to manage this
year's Quad, will not return to col
lege. News to this effect was re
ceived to-day. A class election will
he held Wednesday to nominate a
successor. Cheadle, while at Stan
ford, was prominent in many stu
dent activities. He played tackle
on his Freshman football team and
was a member of last year's Var
sity rowing crew. He was an as
sistant in the Library, and belonged
to the Sigma Sigma fraternity.
