# "Alumnus" Again Changes Form.
## 
### 
The first number of the Stanford
Alumnus is soon to appear under a
new editorship and management.
Archie B. Rice, '95, will edit the
paper in its new form, 0. A. Wilson,
'04, having resigned from the editor
ship. Charles F. Wright,'96, will
be the business manager. Rice is a
member of the local staff of the San
Francisco Chronicle, and is compe
tently fitted for the position on the
Alumnus. Wright is employed as
bookkeeper in the University busi
ness office.
The Alumnus will be published
as a monthly magazine, and will
be much larger and more attrac
tive than ever before.
