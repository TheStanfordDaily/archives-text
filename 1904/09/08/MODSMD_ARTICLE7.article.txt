# Women's League Meets.
## 
### 
The executive committee of the
Women's League met Tuesday
and formulated plans for the first
meeting of the league, which will
occur Friday, September 16th, from
4to 6. The place of meeting has
not yet been decided.
A committee was appointed by
the president to secure a care-taker
for the 44 Rest-room," which is
being re-furnished through the aid
of the organization.
Miss Florence Foy, '06, was
elected secretary to succeed Miss
H. L. Darby, '05, who is unable to
resume her college work.
The report of the treasurer
showed the league to be in a pros
perous condition. There is a bal
ance on hand of $70.42.
