A CLOSE OBSERVER
Will know or soon learn that it in use
less to wink and blink, move your paper
off, or move it up,—you can't get clear,
easy vision if your glasses are wrong.
It's folly to continue their use, for by
eo doing you subject your eyes to strain
that may work a permanent injury. Al
low us to look into the matter. It will
be a satisfaction for you to know if your
glasses are right, and a comfort to get
them right if they are wrong.
We'll be honest with you—we can't
afford to be otherwise.
