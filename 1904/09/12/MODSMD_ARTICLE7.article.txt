# Returns from Eastern Trip.
## 
### 
Assistant Professor J. R. Slon
aker of the Physiology department
has returned from his Eastern trip.
He left here about June 15th, and
after spending several days at the
St. Louis Exposition went to the
summer school of the South at
Knoxville, Tenn. Here Dr. Slon
aker gave courses in nature study
and biology.
After leaving Knoxville Profes
sor Slonaker spent a day at the
University of Cincinnati. From
there he proceeded to Boston, where
he made a short visit and then
started West. On the return
Professor Slonaker stopped a few
days to do some county institute
work in Indiana, where he formerly
resided.
