# Elected to the Press Club.
## 
### 
The Press Club held its first
meeting of the semester last night.
The only business transacted was
the election of E. R. May, '06,
editor of the 1906 Quad, to mem
bership. Ever since he entered
college May has been prominent in
dramatic and literary work. He
has taken part in practically all
the plays produced during the past
two years, with notable success.
Last year May was secretary of the
English Club. He is a member of
Sword and Sandals and of the
Delta Tan Delta and Sigma Sigma
fraternities.
