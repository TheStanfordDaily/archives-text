# Sophomores Elect Officers.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Sophomore
class this afternoon officers were
elected for the ensuing semester, as
follows: President, F. L. Everson;
vice-president, E. P. Stott; secre
tary, Miss M. E. Turner; treasurer,
C. R. Crider; sergeant-at-arms, G.
J. Presley.
A committeeof six was appointed
by President Everson to consider
the matter of choosing a distinctive
class emblem. This committee will
make a report at the next meeting
of the class: A. B. Packard, Miss
S. M. Hopper, J. C. McFarland,
Miss C. E. Morris, Isaac Dillon,
and L. M. Edwards, chairman.
