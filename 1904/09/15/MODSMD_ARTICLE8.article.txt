# Zoologists Will Have Camp-fire.
## 
### 
On Friday evening, September
16th, Associate Professor H. Heath
and Assistants \V. K. Fisher and
M. H. Spaulding are planning to
have a picnic and camp-fire at the
black basalt columns on the Por
tola road. All those whose major
subject is Zoology are invited to
attend and are expected to provide
themselves with luncheon and
tin cups. After dinner coffee will
be served around the camp-fire.
All those who desire to go will
kindly hand in their names to Dr.
Heath.
