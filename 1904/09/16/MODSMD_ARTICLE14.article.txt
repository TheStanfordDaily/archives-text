# Junior Women Meet.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Junior women
was held in the chapel at 1:15 to
day to discuss the question of
adopting a distinctive emblem to
be worn thin year. After a brief
discussion the question was referred
to a committee consisting of Miss
Florence Grace and Miss Mabel
O'Brien, who are to report at a
meeting to be held Friday, Septem
ber 23d.
