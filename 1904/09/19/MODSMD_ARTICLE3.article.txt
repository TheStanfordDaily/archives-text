# Stanford Men at Convention.
## 
### 
The Republican County Conven
tion met in San Jose Saturday
night and nominated a ticket for
the coming election. About twenty
Stanford men attended and took an
important part in the business of
the convention. O. P. Morton, '95,
J. E. McDowell, '00, and Prof.
R. L. Green represented Stanford
precinct, and R. C. McComish,'o3,
was the delegate from Mayfield pre
cinct. Professor Green acted as a
member of the committee on resolu
tions and platform, and McComish,
besides being on the committee on
permanent organization and order
of business, was assistant secretary
of the convention.
