# Progress in Buildings.
## 
### 
The extensive stonework around
the University is making favorable
progress. At the main entrance to
the campus, the great arch in
course of construction is rapidly
nearing completion. Another week
will see the last stone in place and
only a short time will then he re
quired for finishing the iron work
and decorations.
Work on the new Gymnasium is
being rushed and the contractors

expect to have the exterior work
completed by February, 1905.
Stone for the Library building
is already on the ground. As soon
as the foundations are finished, the
stone-masons now employed on
the Gymnasium will he ready to
commence their work. According
to the contract between Mrs. Stan
ford and the McGilvray Stone Com
pany this work must be completed
by July, 1905.
