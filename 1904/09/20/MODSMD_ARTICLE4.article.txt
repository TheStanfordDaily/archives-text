# Prize for Best Yell.
## 
### 
Besides the prizes to be given
for the best songs, the Executive
Committee has also voted a prize of
$5 to be given for the best yell.
The yells must be handed in to
the committee, composed of A. L.
Trowbridge, '05, M. H. Thorpe, '05,
and A. W. Hooper, '05, before Octo
ber 12th.
