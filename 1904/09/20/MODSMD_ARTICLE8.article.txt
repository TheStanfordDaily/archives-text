# Publications by Stanford Professors.
## 
### 
Dr. J. C. Brainier, head of the
Geology department, has recently
published a memoir entitled "The
Stone Reefs of Brazil, Their Geo
logical and Geographical Relations,
with a Chapter on the Coral Reefs."
This work appeared in the Bulletin
of the Museum of Comparative
Zoology at Harvard University, vol.
44, geological series, vol. 7.
Dr. A. A. Lawson, of the Botany
department, has a paper on " The
Gametophytes, Fertilization and
Embryo of Cryptomeria Japonica,"
in the last number of the Annals of
Botany, Oxford.
