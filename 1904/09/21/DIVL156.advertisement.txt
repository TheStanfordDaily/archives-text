ASTIGMATISM
May sometimes be detected by looking
at tlie dial of a clock.
Figures or lines running in one direc
tion may be clearly seen while those
running in an opposite direction are
blurred or indistinct.
This test should be made with one
eye at a time, covering the eye not in
use.
This defect can be corrected only by
the use of properly litted glasses. Un
corrected it is the cause of much trouble.
We correct all defects of vision that
can be corrected with glasses.
