# Library Contract Let.
## 
### 
Mrs. Stanford has let the con
tract for the carpenter work on the
Library building to Wm. Thomp
son. The contract price named is
$59,600, and the time limit for the
completion of the work January 1,
1906. Contractor Thompson has
done other important work for the
University. The contracts for the
marble work, tiling, interior deco
ration, etc., are in course of prepa
ration and will be advertised in a
short time.
