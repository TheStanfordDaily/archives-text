# FRESHMAN ELIGIBLES.
## Lists from Both Universities Now in Hands of Athletic Committees. 
### 
The lists of eligible players for
the Freshman teams of California
and Stanford are now in the hands
of the faculty athletic committees
of the two universities.
The only question of eligibility
that can now be raised against the
candidates is the one in regard to
their amateur standing. The inter
collegiate agreement on that ques
tion is as follows: "The only
objections to men whose names are
on this list that will be considered
are those in regard to amateur
standing. In case such objection
is made, the manager must make
his objection in writing within one
week after receiving the names,
stating when and where amateur
standing has been forfeited, and
give this to the chairman of his
own faculty committee, to be by
him transmitted to the other com
mittee for investigation and final
settlement."
