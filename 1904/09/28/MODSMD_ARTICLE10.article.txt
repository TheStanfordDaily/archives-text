# Junior Women Meet.
## 
### 
The meeting of the .Junior women,
which was adjourned from last Fri
day, was held this afternoon in the
chapel. The only question under
discussion was that relating to the
Junior hat. A number of samples
received from the city were shown
and discussed. The final choice
was not made, but. was deferred
until the next meeting, which will
be held Friday, September 80th, at
1:15, in the chapel.
