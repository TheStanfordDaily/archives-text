# Famous Italian Will Lecture.
## 
### 
Hon. Atilie Brunialti of Rome,
a distinguished Italian statesman,
member of the Italian parliament,
and one of the honorary vice-presi
dents at St. Louis of the Congress
of Arts and Sciences, will speak in
the chapel Monday evening, Octo
ber 3d, at 8. The lecture will be in
English and will be illustrated
with lantern-slides.
Signor Brunialti is one of the
best known of the public men of
Italy and is regarded as one of the
most famous orators among Italians
of the present time. He presents
his lecture to the students of Stan
ford with his compliments, and all
are invited to be present.
