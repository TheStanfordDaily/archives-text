# Announcements.
## 
### 
Sophomore Class —Will meet
Wednesday at 1:15 in room '231.
Mandolin Club —Will practice
Wednesday at 7:30 in room 50.
W. H. Lanagan.
University Orchestra — Will
meet Wednesday night in the
chapel immediately after the rally.
Homeric Mythology.—Owing to
Signor Bornialti's address the class
will not meet Tuesday.
H. W. Rolfe.
Philological Association —Will
meet Thursday, October 6th, at
2:30 in room 31.
E. K. Putnam, Sec.
Tennis Club. —Players in the
interclass tournament must pay
Tennis Club dues upon entering.
J. F. Cassell, '07,.Treats.
Sophomore Hats. — Orders for
hats must lie in by Wednesday at
noon. A. B. Packard, J. C. Mc-
Farland, F. L. Everson, and L. M.
Edwards will take orders.
Plug Ugly.—Prize of $15, with
$10 added by individual subscrib
ers, is offered for best product. All
manuscripts must be submitted to
the committee not later than Fri
day, October 7th, at 8.
Tennis. — Junior drawings will
be held Wednesday afternoon.
Those who wish to enter the Junior
tournament, and who have not yet
joined the Tennis Club, mut-t hand
in their names before Wednesday
afternoon.
Prize Football Song.—The com
petition for the prize football song
will close October 12th, songs to be
handed in to A. L. Trowbridge, '05,
M. H. Thorpe. '05, or A. W. Hooper,
'05. The first prize will he $10
and the second prize $5. A prize
of $5 is offered for the best yell.
Laboratory Fees. —All labor
atory feeH are now due and pay
able at the Business Office of
the University. If not paid on
or before October 7th, an additional
charge of two dollars will he made.
If not paid on or before November
7th, registration will be cancelled.
In filling out the blanks for the
payment of fees, the student .must,
in every case, give the number of
his registration card.
