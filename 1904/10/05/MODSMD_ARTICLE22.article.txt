$10 added by individual subscrib
ers, is offered for best product. All
manuscripts must be submitted to
the committee not later than Fri
day, October 7th, at 8.

must be in before Thursday noon.
All matches in the Junior class
tournament up to the semifinals
must be finished by Thursday even
ing. All entries for class doubles
must be in before Saturday.
Prize Football Song.—The com
petition for the prize football song
will close October 12th, songs to be
handed in to A. L. Trowbridge, '05,
M. H. Thorpe, '05, or A. W. Hooper,
'05. The first prize will be $10
and the second prize $5. A prize
of $5 is offered for the best yell.

campus at 7:30 and Palo Alto at
7:45. Tickets may be obtained at
the Registrar's office between 3 and
5, Friday, October 7th.
Laboratory Fees. —All labor
atory fees are now due and pay
able at the Business Office of
the University. If not paid on
or before October 7th, an additional
charge of two dollars will be made.
If not paid on or before November
7th, registration will be cancelled.
In filling out the blanks for the
payment of fees, the student must,
in every case, give the number of
his registration card.

English (5 and 8) and Psychology
(5) must be paid by Friday, Octo
ber 7th. After that time an ad
ditional fee of 75 cents will be
charged in Economics, the fee in
English will be doubled, and in
Psychology an additional fee of
25 cents will he charged. Office
hours, room 207, Thursday. 8:15 to
9:15; 12:15 to 12:30; 1:30 to 2:30.
Friday, 8:15 to 12:30; 1:30 to 3.
