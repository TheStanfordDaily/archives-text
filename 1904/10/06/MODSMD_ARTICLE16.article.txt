# SOPHOMORE TENNIS MATCH.
## J.F. Cassell Will Represent Second-Year Men in Interclass Tournament. 
### 
In the final round of the Sopho
more class singles J. F. Cassell
beat C. L. Lewis, 6-3, 6-2. This
places Cassell in the interclass
singles tournament, which will be
played on Saturday if the Senior
try-outs are finished by that time.
It does not seem likely that these
try-outs will be completed by Sat
urday; however, the interclass tour
nament will be held as soon as the
Juniors and Seniors select their
representatives. In the last match
of the Sophomore semifinals C. L.
Lewis beat W. H. Swayne, 6-1,
6-2.
The Junior class tournament has
been started, and will he played off
as rapidly as possible. The results
in the Junior try-out to date are as
follows: Preliminary round, Webb
beat Patterson, 6-0, 6-0; A. Ames
beat C. B. Osborne 6-0, 6-2; first
round Guyer beat Brew, 6-0, 6-1.
