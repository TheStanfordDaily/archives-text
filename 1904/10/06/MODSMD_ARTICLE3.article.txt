# Sophomore Hats Ordered.
## 
### 
Orders have been taken for about
a hundred and fifteen Sophomore
hate. Four men have been taking
orders, and nearly every Sophomore
was approached for his order. The
order was sent yesterday to San
Francisco, and it is expected that
the hats will be received within a
week. As soon as the hats arrive
they will be worn by all Sopho
mores.
