# Rates to Freshman Game.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Railway
will give the usual special rates to
the Freshman game. The round
trip will be $1.10 to Berkeley and
return, good on any train Saturday
morning. For those wishing to go
up on Friday afternoon a rate of
$1.40 will be given. No certificate
from the Registrar's office will be
required. Both rates are good for
return until Monday night.
