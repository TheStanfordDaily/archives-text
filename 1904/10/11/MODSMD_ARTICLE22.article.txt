# Ralph Arnold Honorably Mentioned.
## 
### 
In his address on "Paleontology"
before the Congress of Arts and
Sciences at St. Louis, Dr. Arthur
Smith Woodward, of the British
Museum, had a reference to the
paper on the fossil shells of Cali
fornia by Dr. Ralph Arnold. He
spoke of this paper as "Dr. Arnold's
most remarkable and masterly
memoir on the mollusks of the Pa
cific Coast rocks."
