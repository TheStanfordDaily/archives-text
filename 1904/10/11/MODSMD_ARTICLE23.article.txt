# Contributes Physiological Papers.
## 
### 
A. J. Carlson, formerly assistant
in the Physiological department,has
published two papers in the Sep
tember number of the American
Journal of Physiology. The first of
these, on "The Nervous Origin of
the Heart-beat in Limulus, and the
Nervous Nature of Co-ordination or
Conduction in the Heart," is of a
very important and fundamental
nature. The title of the second is
"The Rhythm Produced in the
Resting Heart of Mollusca by the
Stimulation of the Cardio-Acceler
ator Nerves." Carlson received his
degree of Doctor of Philosophy at
Stanford last May.
