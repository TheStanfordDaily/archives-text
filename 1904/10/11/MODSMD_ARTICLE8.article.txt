# Play Committee Meets.
## 
### 
The Sophomore play committee
met this afternoon to consider the
matter of the Sophomore class pre
senting a play. After some dis
cussion in regard to the advisabil
ity of attempting to present an
orginal farce or play, the commit
tee decided that it would he far
better to reproduce some standard
play, and the report of the commit
tee will advise such a plan.
