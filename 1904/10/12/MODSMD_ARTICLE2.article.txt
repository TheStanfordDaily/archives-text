# OFFICIALS SELECTED.
## Freshman Game Will be Played on the New Field at California. 
### 
Treasurer Barrett met Manager
Decoto of California in San Fran
cisco to-day for the purpose of
selecting officials for the intercol
legiate Freshman game, which will
take place on the new gridiron at
Berkeley Saturday. The men se
lected by the two student body
managers are well-known alumni
from both Stanford and California.
The names of the officials are as
follows: C. M. Fickert, '88, referee;
Percy Hall, U. C., umpire; J. T.
Nourse, '00, linesman; D. E. Brown,
'97, and William Powell, U. C.,
timekeepers.
