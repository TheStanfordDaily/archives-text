# Freshmen Select Class Yell.
## 
### 
The committee composed of J. O.
Miller, J. R. Hickey and F. H.
Abbot, which was appointed by the
Freshman class to select a yell,
has decided on the following:
Kah Hoo, Rah Hoo,
L. S. J. U.,
Naught-Eight, Naught-Eight,
Stanford.
This yell, which was selected from
among twelve others, was submitted
by L. M. Klauber of San Diego.
