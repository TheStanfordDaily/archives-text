# Sophomore Class Meets.
## 
### 
At the Sophomore class meeting
held to-day the report of the com
mittee appointed to consider the
advisability of presenting a play
was received. The class decided to
present some standard play about
the third week of next semester.
It was also decided to leave the
matter of selecting a play, and all
other details, in the hands of the
following committee: J. F. Cassell,
W. G. Bateman, Miss L. Norvell,
Miss M. A. Moore, and D. H. Clift,
chairman. This committee will
begin to make arrangements for
the production at once.
