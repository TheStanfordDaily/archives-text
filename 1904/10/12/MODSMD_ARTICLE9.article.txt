# Senior Tennis Tournament Begun.
## 
### 
Play was resumed to-duy in the
class tennis tournaments. Owing
to the recent rainy weather no
matches have heen played for the
pant few days, and the whole tour
nament has heen delayed. The
Senior class tournament was begun
to-day and will he played off as
rapidly as possible. In the first
match A. W. Hooper defeated H.
Boyle 1-6, 6-2, 6-2. The second
match between M. C. Sherwood and
J. Mori resulted in a victory for
Mori by a score of 6-0, 6-3. The
Junior class tournament is now
being played, and the final match
will probably take place to-morrow.
