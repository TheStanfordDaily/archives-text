# "Chaparral" Staff Meets
## 
### 
The Chaparral staff met last
night for the purpose of passing an
amendment to the constitution
which governs the actions of the
members of that paper's editorial
board. The amendment adopted
last night provides for a staff elec
tion during the fall semester at call
of two-thirds of the members of the
staff. The amendment further pro
vides that any one elected at the
fall meeting shall not be allowed a
vote until after the regular election
in March. In pursuance of the
adopted amendment J. Nibley, '06,
was elected a member of the Chap
arral staff at the meeting last night.
