# College Notes.
## 
### 
—Members of the glee club of
Northwestern University will re
ceive a college credit this year for
their university singing.
—The Cross Country Club of
Michigan began active practice re
cently. Two squads have been or
ganized, one for beginners and one
for the varsity men. From now
on daily runs will be taken, vary
ing from two to five miles in length.
