# Plugs Have Arrived.
## 
### 
The plugs which were ordered
from the East by the Junior class
arrived last Saturday. They were
not due until October 21st, but the
prompt action of the committee in
charge has prevented any delay.
They are on sale at the Haber
dasher at one dollar each.
