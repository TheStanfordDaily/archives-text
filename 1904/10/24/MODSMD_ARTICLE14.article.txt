# Rare Books on Exhibition.
## 
### 
Sunday afternoon at the Delta
Upsilon house H. Robert Hyde, for
merly of Santa Barbara, who is
now connected with the United Arts
and Crafts of San Francisco, had
on exhibition a number of his illus
trated books. These books, which
are in Old English style, are illumi
nated in gold and colors, and illus
trate different masterpieces of po
etry. The books are beautifully
bound in leather with brass finish
ings, some of which are jew
eled. The painting, which took
many years to do, is on vel
lum. The prices of the books
range from $10 to $300, according
to the amount of work on them.
