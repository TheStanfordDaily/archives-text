# Finances of Freshman Game.
## 
### 
The final account of the gate
receipts from the Freshman game
of October 15th shows the attend
ance to have been 4,020, and Stan
ford's part of the receipts to be
$2,010. This crowd was the largest
that has ever'witnessed anintercol
legiate Freshman game, exceeding
last year's attendance by more than
700. The result is extremely grat
ifying to the athletic managers,
who have every reason to foresee a
most successful season resulting
from campus games.
