Pis for Presbyopia,
Sometimes called old sight,
The first sign of its coming
Is dim vision at night.
Advancing years bring a change to all
eyes. The crystalline lens becomes
harder and thus the eye loses a part of
its accommodation or power to change
its focus to suit the working distance.
In those cases which are myopic (near
sighted) it may be that glasses will
never he required for reading or close
work, as the eyes may naturally focus at
the reading distance.
All eyes should be fitted with glasses
as soon as there is any difficulty in read
ing or working at the usual distance.
Almost any convex glasses may make
you see better, but only right glasses
will protect and preserve your sight.
"All eyes come to glasses sooner or
later; when that time comes to you,
that's the time for you to come to us."
