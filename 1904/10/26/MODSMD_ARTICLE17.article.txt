# Wallace Irwin's New Volume.
## 
### 
"Nautical Lays of a Landsman"
is the title of the latest book of
verse put out by Wallace Irwin, ex
'oo, just issued from the press of
Dodd, Mead & Company of New
York. The volume contains over a
score of pieces, filled with the catch
iest kind of humor. There are also
a number of amusing illustrations
by Peter Newell.
