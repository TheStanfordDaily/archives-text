With less than two weeks before
the intercollegiate game and with a
much greater efficiency yet to be
reached in the rooting and singing,
it becomes important that every
man in the University be out at
every opportunity offered from now
until November 12th. This state
ment finds a particular application
in the rally and song practice
which will be held to-morrow night
at a place to be announced on the
quad to-morrow. The California
rooters have gone into secret prac
tice, according to reports from
across the bay, and that means
that we have much yet to do if we
do not want to be overwhelmingly
outdone. To-morrow night's rally
will see some interesting innova
tions that no one can afford to miss
if he wants to be thoroughly
acquainted with what will take
place in the Stanford rooting sec
tion at the big game.
