# GLEE CLUB TRY-OUT.
## Second Preliminary Selection for Places.—Twenty-four Men in Competition. 
### 
The second try-out for position
on the Glee Club was held last
night in the chapel. There will be
two more try-outs before the men
are finally selected to make the
trip. The following were successful
in last night's contest: First tenors-
A. T. Merrill, '05, C. L. Severy, '08,
E. J. Swift, '08, R. M. Blodgett,'o7,
J. J. Ilill,'08; second tenors —L.J.
Hoenig, '08, S. B. Thompson, '07,
A. W. Hooper, '05, A. B. Packard,
'07, W. G. Macdonald, '07; first
bass — H. H. Hurlbut, '06, A. H.
Schaaf, '07, R. S. Kellogg, '07, W.
H. Lanagan, '05, F. R. Lanagan,
'08, H. S. Ross, '08, G. D. Lyman,
'05, L. T. Ryone, '07; second bass—
R. H. Lachmund, '06, H. Park, '06,
H. W. Lawrence, '08, F. P. Whita
ker, '05, B. M. VVotkyns, '06, and
B. Cosgrove, '08. This number will
be reduced to sixteen in the final
try-out just before the trip.
