# Adv. 5 Page 2

Once a year Tiik Bookstore offers broken lines of fine stationery
at greatly reduced rates.
The annual sale is now on, and there are several fine lines of
stationery selling at twenty-live cents for a quire of paper and a
bunch of envelopes. These goods sell regularly in Ban Francisco
at fifty cents for the same quantity.
