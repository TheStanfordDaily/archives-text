# Officials for Intercollegiate Game.
## 
### 
Treasurer Barrett has completed,
with Manager Decoto of California,
the list of officials who will make
the decisions in the annual inter
collegiate football game on Novem
ber 12th. They are as follows:
Umpire, R. Smith, head coach at
the University of Oregon; referee,
Ben Dibblee; head lineman, Percy
Hall, U. C.; stakemen, J.T. Nourse,
Stanford '00, and R. Roos, U. C.;
timers, D. E. Brown, Stanford '97,
and I. J. Muma, U. C.
