# Republican Rally at San Jose.
## 
### 
Arrangements are being made to
run jspecial trains from Stanford
to the Republican rally to be held
in San Jose Friday night. Free
railroad transportation for over
400 students has been secured by
R. C. McComish, 'OH, president of
the Stanford Republican Club.
The rally will be held in the Gar
den Theatre, the principal speaker
to be Hon. Geo. C. Perkins. The
train will leave for San Jose at
about 6:30, and return after the
meeting.
