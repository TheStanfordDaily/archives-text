# Cooper Club in Oakland.
## 
### 
The regular monthly meeting of
the Cooper Ornithological Ciub
will be held in Oakland to-morrow,
November sth, at the home of Miss
Helen Swett, '00. at the comer of
Twentieth and Broadway. The
principal paper will be read by
Alvin Seale, entitled "Birds of the
South Sea." Prof. P. M. Silloway
of the University of Montana will
read a paper on "Notes of 1904
from Flathead Lake."
