# Philological Association.
## 
### 
The University Philological As
sociation met vesterday afternoon
in room 31. The principal paper
of this meeting, which was one of
the best of the year, was read by
Professor Julius Goebel of the Ger

man department. Professor Goebel
took for his subject the "Neo-pla
tonic Influences in Faust." The
next meeting will be held in De
cember, when Professor Johnston
will read the principal paper.
