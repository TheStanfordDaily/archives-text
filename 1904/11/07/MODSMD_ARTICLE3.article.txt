# Song Practice To-morrow Night.
## 
### 
A song and yell practice will be
held to-morrow night in Assembly
Hall, when several new short songs
will be introduced. The rally will
be like that of last Wednesday
night, in that it is called for the stu
dents who are to be in the rooting
section at the intercollegiate game.
The meeting will be held from 7:15
to 8:15, and all the songs and yells
to be used this year will be prac
ticed.
