# Football Game To-morrow Morning.
## 
### 
A football game between teams
representing the Palo Alto Acad
emy and Santa Clara College will
be played on the Stanford oval to
morrow. Both teams are known to
be good ones, having played a num
ber of games this season. The
Santa Clara team defeated the
Stanford Freshman team very early
in the season by a score of 10 to 0.
The opposing elevens played a
game in San Jose some time ago in
which Santa Clara College won
with a score of 6-0. The academy
team is being coached by R. J.
McFadden, '05, and should give a
good account of itself in the game
to-morrow. The game is scheduled
to commence at 10:30. admission to
be twenty-five cents.
