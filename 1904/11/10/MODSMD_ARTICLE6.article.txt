# Stanford Smoker in San Francisco.
## 
### 
The Stanford Club of San Fran
cisco will give a smoker to-morrow
night, which every Stanford man
who can be present is invited to at
tend. The smoker will be in Wil
liams'Cafe,corner Seventh and Mar
ket sts., under the Odd Fellows
building. The undergraduates
who are in San Francisco to-mor
row should make an effort to get
around and meet the alumni.
Everybody is requested to bring
song-books.
