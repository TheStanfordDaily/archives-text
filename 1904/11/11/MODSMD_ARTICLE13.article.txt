# Program of the Day.
## 
### 
The team and thirteen substitutes
together with the coaches and
trainers will leave for San Fran
cisco on the 9:43 train to-morrow
morning. They will go direct to
the Stanford headquarters at the
St. Francis Hotel, and will leave
for Berkeley before the main body
of rooters.
The rooters will leave for the city
on either of the 8:30 trains. One
leaves Palo Alto and the other from
the campus. The latter train will
go direct to San Francisco without
making any stops. The rooters
will congregate in a body at 10 on
the corner of Market and Ellis
