Once a year Tiik Bookstork offers broken lines of line stationery
at greatly reduced rates.
The annual sale is now on, and there are several fine lines of
stationery selling at twenty-five cents for a quire of paper and a
bunch of envelopes. These sell regularly in San Francisco
at fifty cents for the same quantity.
