# Burbank Will Lecture.
## 
### 
Mr. Luther P. Burbank, the
great California botanist, will de
liver two lectures at the University
to-morrow. His first lecture will
take place in the office of the Zool
ogy building at 4 o'clock, and will
he given to instructors and ad
vanced students in the scientific
departments of the University.
The second lecture will he given in
the council room in the President's
office before the Science Associ
ation. The subject at both of the
lectures will he "Heredity and
Plant Breeding."
