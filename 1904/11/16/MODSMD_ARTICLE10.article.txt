# Mr. Riddel Speaks to Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
Mr. G. White of the University
of California, who was to speak at
the regular Y. M. C. A. meeting
held yesterday, was unavoidably
detained, and Mr. Riddel, another
California student, spoke in his
stead. His subject was"The
World's Student Christian Feder
ation and its Work."
