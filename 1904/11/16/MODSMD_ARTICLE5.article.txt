# Freshman Football Picture.
## 
### 
To-morrow at 1 o'clock the mem
bers of the Freshman football team
will he photographed at Franklin's.
The men are to appear in their
suits and sweaters, which have al
ready been awarded. There are
still a few sweaters which have not
been called for, hut which may he
procured by the players on appli
cation to Treasurer Barrett.
