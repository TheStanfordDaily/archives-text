# Meeting of Geology Club.
## 
### 
The regular bi-weekly meeting of
the Geology Club was held at 158
Encina Hall last night. G.
Schrader, '06, read a paper on the
''Ballarat District," which is situ
ated near Randsburg. He ex
plained the geological formations
of the district, and discussed the
gold mines of this region in detail,
dwelling upon the nature of the ore
and the mills in use.
C. B. Osborne, '06, read a paper
on the "Peanut Hill" district of
Alaska, pointing out the peculiar
geological formations found there.
