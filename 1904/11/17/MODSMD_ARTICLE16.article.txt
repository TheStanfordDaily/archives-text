# Y.W.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The last of the services held du
ring the week of prayer will take
place at 4:30 to-morrow in the
chapel. Throughout the week
daily services have been well at
tended, a marked interest having
been shown in the meetings.
Miss Helen Salisbury, '05, the
president of the Y. W. C. A., will
speak to-morrow on ''Hindrances
to Prayer."
All University women interested
are cordially invited to be present.
