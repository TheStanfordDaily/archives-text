# Report of Song-Book Sale.
## 
### 
The report of the song-book com
mittee on the sale and expenses of
the song-books this year shows that
some thirteen hundred books were
sold, and the total receipts, less
commissions, were $123.80. The
expenses of printing, engraving,
etc., amounted to $62.85, leaving a
balance of $60.65 to be turned over
to the student body treasurer.
Compared with last year the num
ber of books shows a falling off.
Last Beason two thousand were print
ed and nearly all were sold. Only
fourteen hundred and fifty were
printed this year, and there are a
hundred still on hand.
