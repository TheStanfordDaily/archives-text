# Gift to the Library.
## 
### 
Edward M. Ehrhorn, ex-'O5, has
presented a number of books to the
University library. Ehrhorn is at
present a Deputy Commissioner of
Horticulture, and he was formerly
Entomologist of SantaClaracounty.
The following is the list of books
presented: Sheller's German-Latin
Dictionary, Sheller's Latin-German
Dictionary, Passow's Dictionary of
German Language, in two volumes;
Ure's Dictionary of Chemistry,
Parke's Chemical Catechism, and
Stieller's Hand Atlas of the World.
