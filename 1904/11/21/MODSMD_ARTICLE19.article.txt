# Plans a Social Meeting.
## 
### 
The Botanical Society, which
has been recently organized, has
made plans for a social meeting, to
take place Monday evening, No
vember 28th. The meeting will be
held in the library room of the Bot
any building, and all students in
Botany are urged to be present, as
an excellent opportunity for mem
bers of the department to become
acquainted will be afforded. The
chief feature of the program will be
the exhibition of a number of pic
tures taken by Dr. P. H. Olsson-
Seffer in Australia and other dis
tnnt countries, of rare and beautiful
flora. After the regular program
is completed, refreshments will be
served.
