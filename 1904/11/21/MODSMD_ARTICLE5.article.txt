# Meeting of Abbott's Inn Court.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the court
of Abbott's Inn will take place to
morrow night. The question to be
discussed will be a case in con
tracts. A. H. Hose, '05, will act as
attorney for the plaintiff, and M.
H. Thorpe, '05, for the defendant.
E. A. Luce, '05, will act as
judge.
