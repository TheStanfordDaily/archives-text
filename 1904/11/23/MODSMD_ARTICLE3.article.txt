# Will Debate for Stanford Trophy.
## 
### 
The debating team from the San
Jose high school defeated Alameda
high Saturday evening at Ala
meda. This puts San Jose in the
finals in the High School Debating
League. Alameda was second to
Commercial Evening in the series
last year, but now loses her right to
debate for the trophy. San Jose
and Commercial Evening will de
bate at Stanford early next semes
ter to determine which school shall
hold the trophy during the ensuing
year.
