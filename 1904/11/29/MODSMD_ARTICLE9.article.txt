# Musician to Lecture.
## 
### 
Mr. Arthur Farwell, organizer of
the Wa Wan Press of Newton
Center, Mass., who has been en
gaged for some time under the
auspices of the Archaelogical In
stitute of America in a study of
Indian folklore music, will lecture
in the University chapel on Thurs
day evening, December Ist, at 8
o'clock. Mr. Farwell's subject will
be ''Toward American Music," in
cluding a presentation of composi
tions developed from Indian themes.
The lecture will be free and all are
cordially invited to be present.
