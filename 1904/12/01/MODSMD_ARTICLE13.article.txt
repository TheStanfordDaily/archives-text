# Reward to Second Eleven Player.
## 
### 
The executive committee of the
University of California granted a
varsity football "C" to Larry
O'Toole of Hastings Law College.
O'Toole is the first man in the his
tory of California football to re
ceive this honor without playing in
an intercollegiate contest. He has,
however, played on the second
eleven for seven years and has al
ways been a consistent worker on
the squad.
