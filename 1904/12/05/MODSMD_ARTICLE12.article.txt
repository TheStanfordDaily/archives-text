# Y.W.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the
Y.W. C. A. will be held to-morrow
at 4:30 in the chapel. Instead of
the subject and leader announced
on the card, the meeting will as
sume the nature of a "Capitola
Conference Rally." This will be
the first of a number of rallies to
be held between now and the an
nual conference in April.
