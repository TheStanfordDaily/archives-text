# Student Body Election To-morrow.
## 
### 
The semi-annual election held by
the Associated Students for the
purpose of electing the editor-in
chief of the Daily Palo Alto for
the spring semester will be held on
the quadrangle to-morrow from
11:15 to 2:30. W. H. B. Fowler,
'06, is the only candidate for the
position.
