# Church Social in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
The ladies of All Saints' Guild
will hold a social and Christmas
sale from 3 to 6 Saturday, Decem
ber 10th. The sale will he held at
the home of Mrs. Fowler, '221
Kingsley avenue, Palo Alto. The
proceeds of the bazaar will be
added to the organ fund of the
church.
