# Michigan Club Dinner.
## 
### 
The University of Michigan Club
of San Francisco will give its an
nual dinner on Monday, December
12th, at 6:30 p.m., at the Califor
nia Hotel. Mr. W. A. Beasley will
preside, and there will be addresses
by several alumni. All University
of Michigan men will be welcomed.
Those who have not yet received
invitations are requested to send
their names and addresses im
mediately to Edward Mills Adams,
126 Stockton street, room 5, San
Francisco.
