# Y.M.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
A regular meeting of the Young
Men's Christian Association will be
held to morrow afternoon at 4:30 in
room 23 quad. Rev. Mr. Hayes
of the Presbyterian Church of Palo
Alto will be the principal speaker.
Mr. Hayes is a son of the president
of Washington and Jefferson Uni
versity, and has always shown in
terest in the local Y. M. C. A. work.
All men of the University are cor
dially invited to attend the meet
ing.
