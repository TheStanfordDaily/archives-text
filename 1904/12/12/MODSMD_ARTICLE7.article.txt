# Y.W.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The regular weekly meeting of
the Young Women's Christian As
sociation will be held in the chapel
at 4:30 to-morrow. The service, as

announced, will be a mission study
rally, with Miss Harriet Stillson,
'06, as leader. Miss Beatrice
French, '05, will address the meet
ing. At this time the classes and
leaders for the mission study next
semester will he announced. All
women of the University are in
vited to be present.
