ination for removal of conditions of

Junior Class. — The '06 Quad
assessment in now due. I will be
in room 14 on Tuesdays and Thurs
days from 11:15 to 12:15, and on
Mondays. Wednesdays, and Fri
days from 1:30 to 2:30, for the pur
pose of collecting. Assessments
may also be paid at room 65 En
cina, J. L. Mai oy.
Entrance Composition.—An ex
amination in Entrance 1 (English
Composition) will be held on Sat
urday, December 17th, at 9, in
room 200. This examination is for
registered students only, who are
not members of the tutoring classes.
The examination to be given in
January will be for new students,
and for only such registered stu
dents as have secured special per
mission in advance.
Employment Bureau. — All per
sons having housecleaning, garden
ing or other work, for which they
wish special help during the holi
days, are asked to file their request
for student labor as soon as possi
ble. Men will be available for al
most any kind of work offered, and
only students who have been tried
and found satisfactory will be sent
out. Drop a postal to the Y. M.C. A.
Employment Bureau, campus.
Descriptive Geometry. — Final
examination Saturday, December
17th, 8:30 to 11:30. Class will be
divided into four sections: A to C,
in room 370; D to K, in room 62;
L to Q, in room 50; and R to Z, in
room 460. Each member of the
class will hand to his instructor
before December 17th one examina
tion blank. Special examination
for removal of conditions of May,
1904, will be held in room 370 at
the same time.
