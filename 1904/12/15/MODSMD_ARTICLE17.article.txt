# First Meeting- of Cast Held.
## 
### 
The cast of "Every Man in His
Humour," as temporarily organ
ized held its first meeting yesterday
afternoon. Every part in the cast
Ims been provisionally assigned,and
rehearsals will commence at once.
The first rehearsal of the play is to
take place Saturday night at the
Delta Upsilon house. No more re
hearsals will be held this semester,
but as the play will be gone over
from beginning to end Saturday
night, the actors will have a knowl
edge of the action of the play
which should assist them ma
terially in learning their parts dur
ing the vacation. Next semester
two or three rehearsals will be held
every week.
