# Pass Bar Examinations.
## 
### 
Seven men of the Stanford Law
department took the bar examina
tions before the state examining
board in San Francisco the first of
this week, and all of them passed
successfully. These men, who are
now privileged to enter into the
practice of law in California, are A.
A. Caldwell, '05, H. C. Ross, '03, S.
W. Cotton, '98, \V. G. Heinly, '05,
E. Klette, sp., L. H. Roseberry, '03,
V. A. McGeorge, '00.
