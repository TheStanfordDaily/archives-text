# Article by W.K. Fisher.
## 
### 
\V. K. Fisher, '01, assistant in
the Zoology department, has re
cently published a monograph on
the "Birds of Laysan Island"
which has drawn much attention
in scientific circles. Laysan Island
is an uninhabited island of the
Hawaiian group in which Mr.
Fisher was able to get very close to
his subjects. Some rare bird pho
tographs, taken by him, aroused
much interest when presented be
fore a recent meeting of the Bio
logical Society of Washington, D. C.
Fisher's work has been further
honored by an extensive review in
the current number of the National
Geographical Magazine.
