# Tickets to Paderewski Recital.
## 
### 
Paderewski, the great pianist,
will give a recital in the Victory
Theatre, San Jose, Thursday after
noon, December 29th, and in order
to make it more convenient for
faculty members and students who
wish to attend, Dr. B. C. Blodgett,
organist of Memorial Church, has
secured a number of choice seats
that may be had on application to
him. The seats may be obtained
from Dr. Blodgett by applying to
him in person or through the mail.
His address is box 246, campus.
