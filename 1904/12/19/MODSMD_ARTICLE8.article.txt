# Freshmen Announce Question.
## 
### 
The Freshman debating team
has selected the question which
will be discussed in the debate
with the Sophomores next semester.
The question follows: "Resolved,
That a federal statute should be
passed providing for federal charter
for all companies doing or intend
ing to do business in more than
one state, and which shall apply
for incorporation under such char
ter." The Sophomore team will
select the side its members wish to
defend by next Thursday.
