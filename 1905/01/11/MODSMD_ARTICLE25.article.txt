# Y.W.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
Added interest is attached to the
regular meeting of the Young
Women's Christian Association for
this week by the presence of Miss
Ruth Rouse, who will conduct
meetings each afternoon this week.
Miss Rouse is the traveling secre
tary of the World's College Chris
tian Associations, and is a speaker
of marked ability and interest. She
has traveled throughout the world

in the college association work,
and will speak on the organizations
in other lands. The topics for the
services during the week will be
announced to-morrow. The meet
ings will he held in the chapel at
4:30, and all University women are
urged to be present. Miss Rouse
will be on the campus until Mon
day.
