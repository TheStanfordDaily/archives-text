# Professor Peirce Returns.
## 
### 
Associate Professor G. J. Peirce
of the Botany department hus re
turned from an eight months' visit
to Europe. Professor Peirce re
ceived a leave of absence last May,
and has since been studying in
several of the noted laboratories of
plant physiology in Europe. He
spent two months in Pfeffer's
laboratory of plant physiology at
Leipzig, and also at the Marine
Biological Laboratory at Naples,
which is noted as being one of
the most perfectly equipped labora
tories in the world. In August he
attended the annual meeting of the
British Association for the Advance
ment of Science. The result of
Professor Peirce's work in Europe
will be published in the near future.
