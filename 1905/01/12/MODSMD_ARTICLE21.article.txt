# Stanford Wedding.
## 
### 
Miss Mabel L. Harris, ex-'O5, of
Merced, and Joseph Kahn, ex-'O2,
of Oakdale, were married at Mer
ced Sunday afternoon, January Bth.
The wedding was a Stanford affair,
all the decorations being of the
Stanford cardinal, and the brides
maids being Miss Mabel O'Brien,
'06, and Miss Louise Norvell, '07.
The bride while in college was a
member of the Girls' Mandolin
Club. Mr. and Mrs. Kahn will re
side in Oakdale, where the former
is engaged in business.
