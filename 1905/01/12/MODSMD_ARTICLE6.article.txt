# Fairclough Wins Golf Trophy.
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough, of the
Latin department, was the winner
of two golf tournaments held at the
Menlo Park Golf and Country Club
links on the day following Christ
mas, and on the second of January.
For winning the New Year's'tour
nament Professor Fairclough re
ceived a handsome silver trophy
offered by the club.
