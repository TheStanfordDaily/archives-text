# Cyclery Moved.
## 
### 
The Encina Gym Cyclery has
been moved during the vacation to
new quarters next to the Bookstore.
The quarters are convenient, and
although they are crowded at pres
ent, they will he enlarged. A skil
ful machinist will be in charge,
and high-class work will be guaran
teed. The shop is run by H. J.
Colberg, who will have supervision
of all work. lie will keep a full
stock of Sporting Goods, Base
balls, Tennis Balls, etc. Remem
ber, next to the Bookstore.
