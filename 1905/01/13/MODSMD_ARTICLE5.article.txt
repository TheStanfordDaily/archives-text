# Rhodes Scholarship Examinations.
## 
### 
Dr. A. T. Murray has received
from the trustees of the Rhodes be
quest the following schedule for the
examinations which are to be held
here next week for the selection of
a Stanford scholar:
January 17 —9 a.m. to 11 a.m.,
Translation from Latin into Eng
lish; 1 p.m. to 3 p.m., Latin Prose;
4 p.m. to 6 p.m., Arithmetic.
January 18 —9 a.m. to 11 a.m.,
Translation from Greek into Eng
lish; 1 p.m. to 3 p.m., Latin and
Greek Grammar; 4 p.m. to 6 p.m.,
Algebra or Geometry.
