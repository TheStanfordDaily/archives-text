# Scholarship Tests To-morrow.
## 
### 
The first of the examinations for
the selection of a Rhodes scholar
from Stanford will be held to-mor
row in room 25, quad. The sched
ule for the day is as follows: 9 a.m.
to 11 a.m., translation from Latin
into English; 1 p.m. to 3 p.m., Latin
Prose; 4 p.m. to 6 p.m., Arithmetic.
Examinations in the remaining
subjects will be given Wednesday.
It is understood that only three
men will try for the scholarship.
