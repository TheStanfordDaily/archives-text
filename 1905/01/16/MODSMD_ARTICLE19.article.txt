# Sophomore Play Posters.
## 
### 
The posters for the Sophomore
comedy, "The Rivals," have arrived
and will appear on the quad to
morrow morning. The poster is
the work of W. G. Beymer, '07, and
represents the figures of Sir An
thony and Mrs. Malaprop in the
minuet scene. The poster will be
on sale at the Bookstore for fifteen
cents.
