# Civil Service Examination.
## 
### 
The United States Civil Service
Commission announces that a
clerk-carrier examination will be
held at Palo Alto on January 28th
for the purpose of securing eligibles
from which to make certification to
fill a vacancy in the position of
substitute carrier in the postoffice
service in that city, and other simi
lar vacancies as they may occur in
that service. Persons desiring to
compete should at once apply
either to the local secretary, Board
of Civil Service Examiners, Post
office, Palo Alto, or to the district
secretary, Board of Civil Service
Examiners, Postoffice, San Fran
cisco, for application form 1371,
which should be properly executed
and filed with the district secretary
at San Francisco not later than
4 p.m. of January 25th.
