# Chimes Will Ring on Sunday.
## 
### 
At the wish of Mrs. Stanford, the
chapel chimes will be rung for fif
teen minutes each Sunday morn
ings, starting at 10:45, announcing
the morning service. It has pre
viously been tbe custom to have
the chimes rung in this manner
only on Christmas day, but from
now on they will be heard each
Sunday. Another arrangement
has been made by which the chimes
will no longer be rung while ser
vices are being held in the church.
