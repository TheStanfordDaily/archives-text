# First Y.M.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
The first regular Tuesday after
noon meeting of the Young Men's
Christian Association for the semes
ter will be held to-morrow after
noon at 4:30 in room 23, quad.
Reports will be read from the State
Conference of College Associations,
recently held at Pacific Grove.
Several of the delegates to the re
cent conference will speak on differ
ent phases of the work. A special
effort will be made this semester to
make these Tuesday meetings of
general interest, and all who are
interested in the work of the asso
ciation are invited to attend.
