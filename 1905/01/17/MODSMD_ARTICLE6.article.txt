# Rhodes Scholarship Candidates.
## 
### 
Three students took the first ex
aminations for the Rhodes scholar
ship which were held to-day.
Those who tried the tests were H.
A. Moran, '05, M. M. Stearns, '06,
and P. C. Millar, '06. The second
and final day's examinations will
be held to-morrow: 9 a.m. to 11
a.m., translation from Greek into
English; 1 p.m. to 3 p.m., Latin
and Greek grammar; 4 p.m. to 6
p.m., algebra and geometry.
