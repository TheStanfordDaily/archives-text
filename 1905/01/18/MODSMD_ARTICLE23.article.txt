# Dramatic Reader to Speak.
## 
### 
Mrs. Bertha Kunz Baker, a well
known dramatic reader, is to give
a recital at 8 o'clock next Saturday
evening at the residence of Presi
dent Jordan. An admission charge
of seventy-five cents will be made
to cover expenses, and the number
of tickets will be limited to one
hundred. The subject of the re
cital will be "Parsifal."
