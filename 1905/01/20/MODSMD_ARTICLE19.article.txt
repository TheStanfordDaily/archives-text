# Brackett Scholarship Established.
## 
### 
The register of the medical de
partment of Howard University, of
Washington, D. C., announces a
scholarship established by Dr. John
E. Brackett in memory of his son,
Foster Ely Brackett, '03, who died
at Stanford in May, 1903, during
the typhoid epidemic. Dr. Brack
ett holds the position of professor
of medicine in Howard University.
The deceased son was his only
child. His death occurred in the
month that he was to have gradu
ated from Stanford.
