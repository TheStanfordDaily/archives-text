# First "Chaparral" Will Appear.
## 
### 
The first number of the Chapar
ral will appear next Monday. It
will be a fourteen-page edition, and
several new features are introduced.
M. Oppenheim, '05, begins a three
number illustrated serial, which is
one of the best innovations. There
is also a full-page drawing by E.
nG. Teale, and some art work by D.
H. Ferry, '08, who is a new con
tributor.
