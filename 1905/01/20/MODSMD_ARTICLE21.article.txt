# Prof. Howard Returns to America.
## 
### 
Professor Burt Estes Howard,
formerly of the Stanford Econom
ics department, has returned to

America after an extended visit in
Europe. While in Germany lie at
tended Heidelberg University,where
he received a doctor's degree. He
spent much time in Berlin gather
ing material for a book entitled
"German Constitutional History,"
which will soon be ready for publi
cation. During his absence he has
written another book, "American
State Rights." Professor Howard
is at present at Cambridge, Mass.,
where he will remain indefinitely.
