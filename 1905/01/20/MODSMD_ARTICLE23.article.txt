# Botanical Society Meets.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Botanical So
ciety held yesterday in the library
of the Botany building the follow
ing papers were read by members
of the club: "Soil Inoculation,"
by Mrs. 0. A. Humphrey; ''Dwarf
Trees," by W. 0. Stuntz, '07; and
an article on "Date Culture" by E.
W. Rust, '08. Professor G. J.
Peirce of the Botany department
was elected to honorary member
ship. The club also decided to hold
a social meeting in the library of
the Botany building Thursday at
7:30. At this meeting Professor
Peirce will deliver a talk on the
subject of his recent trip to Europe.
