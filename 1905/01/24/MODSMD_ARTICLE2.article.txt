# MANY TRACK MEN OUT.
## Over One Hundred Candidates Are Already Under Training' Rules. 
### 
In spite of the unfavorable
weather, the track season lias
opened with a large number of can
didates reporting for work under
the direction of Trainer Moulton
and Captain Bell. Between show
ers part of the track can always be
used, and the runways for the
jumps are so well drained that
they can be used practically all the
time. Trials are being run ever}'
day, and although no official time
has been given out, Moulton re
ports that several of the new men
are showing excellent form. The
early date set for the first field-day
of the season caused most of the
men to begin strict training before
the rules went into effect, and with

a few clear days for hard work they
will he in excellent shape for Sat
urday's meet.
