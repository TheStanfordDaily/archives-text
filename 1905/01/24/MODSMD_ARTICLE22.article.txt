# Euphronia Meets To-morrow.
## 
### 
To permit the members to attend
the Sophomore play Friday eve
ning, the Euphronia Literary So
ciety will hold its regular meeting
to-morrow night. The following
program will be given: Impromptu
debate by four members. Regular
debate, "Resolved, That the Inter
state Commerce Commission should
be given power to revise railroad
rates and regulations": affirmative,
J. E. Campbell, 'OG, C. R. Crider,
'07; negative, B. B. Henston, N.
P. Bryan, '07.
