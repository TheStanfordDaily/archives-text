# Botanical Society Meeting.
## 
### 
The Botanical Society will hold
a social meeting at 7:45 in the Bo
tanical library, room 461, on
Thursday evening, January 26th.
Dr. G. J. Peirce will speak on the
subject of his recent trip to Europe.
Members of the society and friends
are invited. In case of heavy rain
the meeting will be postponed.
