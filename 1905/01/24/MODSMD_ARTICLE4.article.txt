# Freshmen Meet To-morrow.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Freshman
class to-morrow at 1:15 in the
chapel, besides the regular nomina
tion of officers, the question of
granting class sweaters to those
men who represented the Fresh
man class in the interclass tourna
ment last semester will be taken
up and discussed. The question of
granting debating-pins to the suc
cessful Freshman debaters will
also be considered.
