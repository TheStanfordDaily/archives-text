# Russian Exile to Lecture.
## 
### 
Lieutenant-Colonel A. M. Loch
witzky, formerly an officer in the
Russian army, will lecture in the
University chapel on Thursday
evening, January 26th, at 7:30
o'clock, on his experiences as a po
litical prisoner on Saghalien island,
Russia's great prison off the Si
berian coast. Colonel Lochwitzky
is the son of a former Russian Min
ister of War, who was removed to
make way for General Kuropatkin.
The lecture will be free.
