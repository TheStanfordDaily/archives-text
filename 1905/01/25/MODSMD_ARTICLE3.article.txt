# Papers by a Stanford Man.
## 
### 
At the annual meeting of the
American Association for the Ad
vancement of Science, held last
month in Philadelphia, during the
convocation week of the scientific
societies, two important papers by
Dr. P. H. Olsson-Seffer of the Stan
ford Botany department were read.
The titles of these papers were "The
Principles of Phytogeographical
Nomenclature," and"The Stratifi
cation of a Plant Community."
