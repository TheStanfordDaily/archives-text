# Colored Fraternity to Be Organized.
## 
### 
Fifteen colored students of the
University of Michigan are plan
ning to organize a Greek-letter
fraternity in a short time at Ann
Arbor. Negotiations are being
made for the purchase of a house.
In the event of this project being
put through, Ann Arbor will have
the only Greek-letter fraternity in
the world composed of colored stu
dents.
