# Freshman Election To-morrow.
## 
### 
The Freshman class will hold an
election of officers to-morrow at
1:15 in the chapel. The following
have been nominated: President,
A. R. Mac Swain of Santa Clara;
vice-president, G. C. Coe of Port
land, Or.; secretary, F. A. Ayer of
Saranac Lake, N. Y., and Miss
Ethel Beard of Modeeto; treasurer,
F. W. Dorr of San Francisco; ser
geant-at-arms, J. R. Pemberton of
San Francisco.
