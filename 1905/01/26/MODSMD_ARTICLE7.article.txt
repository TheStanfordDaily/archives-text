# Orchestra Meeting.
## 
### 
The Orchestra,
under the leader
ship of M. H.
Thorpe, '05, met
last night in the
chapel to rehearse
for the production

of the "Rivals." A short business
meeting was held in which C. W.
Wiser, '02, tendered his resignation
as manager and A. M. Irwin, '07,
was elected to fill the vacancy.
