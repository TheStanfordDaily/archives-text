# Mathematicians Will Meet.
## 
### 
A regular meeting of the San
Francisco Section of the American
Mathematical Society will be held
at Stanford University on Satur
day, February 25th. There will
be a morning and afternoon session
at which addresses will be given by
faculty members of Stanford and
California. Titles and abstracts of
papers to be presented must be
given the program committee not
later than February 11. Arrange
ments for the meeting are in charge
of Profesor G. A. Miller.
