# INTERCOLLEGIATE TRY-OUTS.
## Squads Choose the Questions and Sides for the Preliminary Debates. 
### 
Those who intend trying out for
the Intercollegiate debating team
are busy preparing for the prelimi
nary contests which will be held on
the evenings of February 16th and
17th. The various squads have
picked questions and chosen sides
as follows:

Pier, '07, G. D. Gibson, '06.
