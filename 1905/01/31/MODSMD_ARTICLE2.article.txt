# MAKE THE RUBBING-TABLE.
## Best of the Athletes Now on the Track Selected for the Privilege. 
### 
A list of the track men who will
have the privilege of the rubbing
table has been announced by
Trainer Moulton. The men chosen
were those who made the best show
ing in the meet last Saturday, to
gether with a few others who were
unable to compete in this contest.
The list of the men selected is as
follows:
'05— H. W. Bell, J. G. Dehy, R.
A. Thompson, \V. H. Thomson, F.
G. Smith, G. A. Waring, W. E.
Crawford, R. C. Bertheau, F. H.
Fowler, F. P. Whitaker, R. S. Car
ter, H. A. Moran, I. J. Bounds, L.
G. Burr, F. B. Goudv, N. E. Dole,
A. B. Swinerton, C. E. Hyde, F. S.
Holman.
'06— R. F. West, A. C. Freeman,
L. R. Weinmann, J. E. Campbell.
'07 —L. M. Edwards, E. J. Nash,
P. A. Wood, J. C. Macfarland,
R. M. Blodget, G. E. Dole, G.
Knupp, L. W. Barbur, C. F. El
well, H. Fitch, A. J. Chalmers, J.
C. Powers.
'08 —R. A. Gartzmann, J. R.
Pemberton, J. P. Dunn, F. H.
