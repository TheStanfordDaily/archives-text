# Girls' Basket-ball To-morrow.
## 
### 
There will be a basket-ball game
on the Roble court at 4 p.m. Satur
day, February 4th. An admission
charge of fifteen cents will be made.
Every member of the squad is ex
pected to be on the ground by 3:45.
The game will be with the girls of
the San Jose Normal School.
