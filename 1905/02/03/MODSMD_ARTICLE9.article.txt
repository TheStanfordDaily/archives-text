# Professor Cubberley at Columbia.
## 
### 
Professor 15. P. Cubberley of the
Education department is now at
Columbia University, where he is
studying for the degree of Doctor of
Philosophy. Professor Cubberley
was granted leave of absence from
Stanford University in May, 1904,
and since that time has spent sev
eral months in traveling about
Italy, Austria, France, and Ger
many. His visits to the various
European cities have been chiefly
in the interests of education, as he
received an authorization from the
Governor of California to visit the
schools of Europe as a California
commissioner. Professor Cubber
ley will resume his work at Stan
ford in the coming August.
