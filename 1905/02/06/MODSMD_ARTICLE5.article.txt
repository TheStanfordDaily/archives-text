# Miss Bentley to Speak.
## 
### 
At the regular weekly meeting of
the Young Women's Christian As
sociation, which will be held in the
chapel at 4:30 to-morrow, Miss
Mary Bentley will be the leader.
Miss Bentley is well known to the
association workers here, since she
comes as the general secretary of
the Young Women's Christian As
sociation at Berkeley, in which
position she has served since her
graduation from the State Univer
sity in 1903. Miss Bentley has as
her subject, "The Marks of Christ,"
and her talk promises to be most
interesting and helpful.
