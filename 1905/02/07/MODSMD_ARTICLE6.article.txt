# English Club to Meet.
## 
### 
The regular program for the
meetine of the English Club, which
will take place to-morrow evening
at 8 o'clock at the Delta Upsilon
house, will consist of the exhibition
of a valuable collection of Japanese
prints by Miss L. J. Martin, in
structor in the department of Psy
chology. Miss Martin will also
talk briefly on the subject of
Japanese art. After the regular
program R. H. Lachmund, '06,
will sing.
