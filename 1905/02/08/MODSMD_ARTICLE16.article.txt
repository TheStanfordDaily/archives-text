terested in athletics to learn that
Trainer Moulton will accompany
the track team when it makes its
trip to Pomona in March. Pomona
is willing to pay for the transporta
tion of only fifteen men, and
Trainer Moulton suggested that he
might stay at home in order that

be taken. The memory of the dis
astrous mistake made last year in

without a trainer in charge caused

a consequence the trainer will ac

step upon the track of the southern
college.
