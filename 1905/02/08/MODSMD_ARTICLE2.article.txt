# Will Have Street Entrance.
## 
### 
The basement of the English
building is to have direct connection
with the street. The necessary
opening and steps are in process of
construction on the east side of the
building. The new entrance will
allow a more ready transfer of
freight to and from the basement.
This basement is now being used
as a storeroom for classroom
furniture and the like.
