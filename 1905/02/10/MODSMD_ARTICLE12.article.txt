# Announcements.

University Band. —Meet Satur
day afternoon at 2 in Encina club
room to play at baseball game.
Girls' Mandolin Club — Will
meet at Franklin's Sunday morn
ing at 10 o'clock for Quad photo.
Kappa Kappa Gamma—Will be
at home Saturday evening, Febru
ary 18th, instead of February 11th.
Women's Faculty Club. — Meet
ing will be held at the residence of
Mrs. W. R. Eckart, 1125 Ramona
Btreet, Palo Alto, on Monday, Feb
ruary 13th, at 3 o'clock.
Physics 6. — Examination in
Light for students conditioned in
Physics 6 will be held on Saturday,
February 18th, at 10:15, in room
414. Fernando Sanford.
Laboratory Fees. —All labora
tory fees are now due and payable
at the Business Office of the Uni
versity. Payment should be made
before 2 p.m. February 11th.
Psychology.—Syllabus fees in
Course 6 (40 cents) and 9 (25
cents) must be paid at room 207
by February 17th. After that date
the fees will be doubled.
Economics. — Syllabus fees in
Courses 1 ($1.25) and 3, 4, s a, 6,
11 ($1.50 each) must be paid by
February 17th at room 207. After
that date the fees will be doubled.
English. — Syllabus fees in
Courses 6 and 8 (25 cents each)
and in Chaucer and Anglo-Saxon
(50 cents each) must be paid by
February 16th in room 207. After
that date the fee will be doubled.
Greek Testament Study.—Any
persons who are willing to join an
informal class for the reverent study
of the Greek Testament on Sunday
afternoons are asked to meet at the
vestry of the Memorial Church on
Sunday next at 3 p.m.

large lecture room, Chemistry build

day, February 17th, 1:30; Chemis
try a, Friday, February 24th, 1:30.
