# Seniors Will Have Smoker.
## 
### 
The men of the Class of '05 are
planning to have a class smoker
within a short time in order to
bring the members of the class to
gether. A number of men have
expressed their desire to forward
such a plan and are now working
up the proposition. The first
smoker is to be held at Woodside.
The day is to be spent in an infor
mal manner, and will end with a
stag dance. If the proposition
proves successful, class smokers
will be held frequently until the
graduation of the class.
