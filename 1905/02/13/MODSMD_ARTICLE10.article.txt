# Y.M.C.A. Meeting.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the
Young Men's Christian Association
to be held to-morrow afternoon at
4:15 in room 23, quad, H. E. Dorr
of the University of the Pacific will
be the principal speaker. Mr. Dorr
will speak on "Christian Enthusi
asm." Special music will be ren
dered by the Y. M. C. A. quartet.
