# Organ Recital Postponed.
## 
### 
Signor Polacco's organ recital,
which was to have taken place in
Memorial Church at noon to-day,
has been postponed until to-mor
row, Thursday, at 11 o'clock. No
definite program has been an
nounced for the recital, but the
numbers will consist largely of
selections from the great operas.
