# College Notes.
## 
### 
—Michigan track athletes who
break records in varsity or confer
ence meets will hereafter recive
medals. The trophies will be given
by the Michigan Alumni Associa
tion of Pittsburg.
—A new rule regarding the eligi
bility of freshmen for the varsity
football teams has gone into effect
in the universities comprising the
"Big Nine." Freshmen who have
not been in college one semester
are barred from playing on the var
sity teams.
