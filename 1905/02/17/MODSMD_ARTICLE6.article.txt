# Tennis Tournament Postponed.
## 
### 
The women's tennis tournament
which was to have been held to
morrow morning on the Roble
courts has been postponed until

one week from to-morrow, Febru
ary 25th. The muddy condition
of the courts makes play for the
next few days impossible.
