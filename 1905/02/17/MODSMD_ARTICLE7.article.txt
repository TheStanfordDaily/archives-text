# SENIOR WEEK COMMITTEES.
## List of Those Who Will Conduct Annual Commencement Festivities. 
### 
The Senior committees that will
have charge of the various festivi
ties during commencement week
were announced to-day by Presi
dent A. L. Trowbridge. The com
plete list of the appointments fol
lows:
Senior week committee —P. W.
Avery, A. M. Dibble, A. W. Hooper,
O. E. Hyde, 0. S. Lousley, E. Y.
Pomeroy, A. Sherriffs, A. L. Trow
bridge, chairman.
Finance committee —H. H. Chan
dler, W. E. Crawford, 0. S. Lous
ley, chairman.
Program committee — Miss A.
Reinhart, Miss H. L. Darby, R. C.
Bertheau, W. R. Cole, 0. E. Hyde,
chairman.
Farce committee —Miss M. H.
Baum, Miss C. M. Burnap, Miss E.
Voris, F. P. Whitaker, L. D. Byrne,
A. W. Hooper, chairman.
Senior ball committee —Miss H.
A. Traphagen, Miss J. A. Comingß,
V. E. Stork, W. H. Dole, G. R.
Stevens. G. D. Lyman, A. Sherriffs,
chairman.
Promenade committee —Miss M.
C. Ray, Miss G. Knapp, Miss I. C.
Dille, Miss H. L. Crandall, Mies E.
M. Reeves, E. B. Favre, T. M. Coen,
A. H. Lett, C. B. Eaton, H. W. Bell,
P. K. Funke, G. E. Gamble, H.
Shields, E. Y. Pomeroy, chairman.
Plate committee —Miss H. M.
Salisbury, Miss R. G. Tarbell, R.
G. Barnett, B. C. Dey, A. M. Dibble,
chairman.
Encina reception committee —Miss
B. F. French, Miss K. Mclntyre, W.
D. Forbes, P. \V. Avery, chairman.
Miss A. M. Richards will be class
historian, P. D. Swing class orator,
and D. M. Reynolds will write the
class will.
