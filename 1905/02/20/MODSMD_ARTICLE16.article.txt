# Death of Dr. Ira G. Hoitt.
## 
### 
Dr. Ira G. Hoitt, founder of
Hoitt's School for Boys, formerly
State Superintendent of Public In
struction, died at his home in
Menlo Park yesterday in the
seventy-second year of his age.
Dr. Hoitt has been known through
out California as an earnest and
active worker in the cause of edu
cation since his arrival here forty
years ago. He has held many
positions of honor and trust in ed
ucational ranks. Funeral services
will he held on Tuesday, February
21st, at his residence at 10:30 a.m.,
and at the First Unitarian Church,
San Francisco, at 2:30 in the after
noon.
