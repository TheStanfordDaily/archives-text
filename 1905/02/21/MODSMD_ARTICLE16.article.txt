# Announcements.
## 
### 
Senior Women. — Meeting in
chapel 1:15 on Thursday.
Library — Will be closed on
Wednesday, February 22d.
Latin 31. —In future the class in
Roman Art and Monuments will
meet in room 231, History build
ing.
Geological Society—Will meet
at the home of It. S. Lewis on Col
lege Terrace Wednesday evening,
February 21st, at 8 o'clock.
Freshman Dues. —I will be in
room 14, quad, from 1:30 to 2:30
on Thursday, February 23d, to re
ceive Freshman class assessments.
F. W. Dorr, Treas.
Junior Farce.—General try-outs
for both women's and men's chorus
for"The Princess Enone" will take
place to-morrow at 2 p.m. in the
chapel. P. C. Edwards.
Economics 1. —An examination
for making up conditions of last
semester will be given in room 226
on Saturday, February 25th, at 11
o'clock. This will be the only ex
amination given for that purpose.
Tennis. —Entries for the first
tournament of the singles tennis
try-outs are now closed. Entries for
the second tournament to be played
Saturday, February 25th, must be
in on or before Thursday, Febru
ary 23d. Tennis Club dues must
be paid when entry is made.
G. A. Hodge, Captain.
Handball Tournament. — All
entries for both singles and class
doubles must be handed in at the
gymnasium office on or before
March Ist. Those entering the
tournament must be registered for
gymnasium work, and are requested
to give the time at which they can
play their games.
