# Sword and Sandals Meets
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Sword and
Sandals last night the following
men were elected to membership:
P. C. Edwards, '06, J. C. Ray, '07,
and R. S. Kellogg, '07. Edwards
has taken part in almost all of the
class performances that have taken
place since his matriculation at

Stanford, acted in the English
Club's production of "The Knight
of the Burning Pestle," and will
have the part of Cob in the present
English Club performance of" Every
Man in His Humour." Ray and
Kellogg both did excellent work in
the recent Sophomore production
of "The Rivals."
E. R. May, '06, was elected presi
dent of the society, and H. Barkan,
'05, secretary-treasurer.
