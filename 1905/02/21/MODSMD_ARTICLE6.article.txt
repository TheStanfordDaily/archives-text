# TENNIS TRY-OUT TO-MORROW.
## Singles Tournament Will Start Series of Contests for Intercollegiate Team. 
### 
The tirst singles tournament in
the series of try-outs that will be
held during the next two weeks to
pick the men to represent Stanford
in this year's intercollegiate tennis
match will be held to-morrow on
the Encina courts. The second
tournament is to be held next Sat
urday, and then the winners of the
two events will join in a round
robin tournament with G. A.
Hodge, '06, to select the two singles
representatives.
Drawings will be held on the
courts promptly at 8:30 to-morrow
morning. Only those will be per
mitted to play who entered their
names with the tournament com
mittee before last Monday. The
following men have entered: A. R.
Mac Swain, '08, P. B. Delano, '08,
J. W. Lilienthal, '08, H. F. Clark,
'08, A. \V. Hooper, 'G5, J. F. Cas
sell, '07, J. L. Gamble, '05, C. G.
Guyer, '06, C. L. Lewis, '07, G. E.
Dole, '07, T. B. Brown, '07, L. R.
Gay, '08, J. E. Cushing, '08, M. C.
Burr, '07, M. M. Stearns, '06, L.
Newland, '08, A. B. Swinerton,
'05, and R. Topp, '07.
All entries for the second tourna
ment must be made on or before
i Thursday, February 23d.
