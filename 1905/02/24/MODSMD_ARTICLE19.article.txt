# To Visit the Selby Smelter.
## 
### 
A section of the class in Metal
lurgy lift, under the direction of
D. A. Lyon, instructor in Geology,
will take a trip to the Selby smelter
to-morrow. The smelter is located
thirty-three miles northeast of San
Francisco, near Port Costa. This
is the second of three trips which
will be taken by the different sec
tions of the class to see a lead
smelter in operation.
