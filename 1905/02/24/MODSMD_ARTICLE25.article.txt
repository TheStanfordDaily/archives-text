# Miller, Freshman Captain.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Freshman
track athletes this afternoon J. 0.
Miller, the wonderful miler from
the Bakersfield High School, was
unanimously elected Freshman
track captain. Miller has run in
both the half and the mile since
entering college. His best per
formance in the mile was 4:39 4-5,
which he did in the Pacific Ath
letic Association try-outs last
spring.
