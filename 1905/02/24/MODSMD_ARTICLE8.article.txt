# "Chaparral" Will Appear Monday.
## 
### 
The annual woman's edition of
the Chaparral will be put on sale
Monday morning. Its appearance,
which was announced for this
week, was unavoidably delayed.
The edition will be a little larger
than usual and there will be a
half-tone supplement.
