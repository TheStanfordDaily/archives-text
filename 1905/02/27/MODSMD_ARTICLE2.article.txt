# California Submits Question.
## 
### 
The University of California on
Saturday submitted the following
question to the University Debat
ing Club of Stanford for discussion
in the Intercollegiate debate: "Re
solved, That the Interstate Com
merce Commission shall be given
power to fix and enforce railroad
rates. Note A —lt is understood
that the commission may be so en
larged and equipped as to enable it
to meet new conditions. Note B —
The question of constitutionality
to be disregarded."
Stanford will have until next
Saturday to name the side it may
wish to defend in the contest, which
is held on April loth.
