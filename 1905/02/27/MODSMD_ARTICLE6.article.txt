# SINGLES TRY-OUTS FINISHED.
## G.A. Hodge, J.F. Cassell, and A.R. MacSwain Will Play in Round-Robin Tournament. 
### 
The final match of the second
preliminary singles tournament,
which was begun Saturday, was
played on the Encina court this
afternoon, J. F. Cassell, '07, defeat
ing G. E. Dole, '07, by the score of
6-4, 6-2. Both of these men
reached the finals by the hardest
kind of playing Saturday, defeat
ing their opponents by close scores.
The result of the tournament
places Casßell in the round-robin
