# Collegiate Alumnae Reunion.
## 
### 
About two hundred and fifty
members of the California branch
of the Association of Collegiate
Alumnae were present at the re
union held here Saturday. Miss
Alice Hays, '86, and Miss Mary J.
Lockey, '01, represented the Stan
ford alumnae most prominently,
and were assisted in their efforts by
the Senior women.
During the morning the guests
were driven about the University,
and at 1 o'clock luncheon was
served at Roble. In the afternoon
the hall of the Zoology building
was the scene of a reception, which
lasted from 2 until 5 o'clock.
After an interesting address of
welcome by Dr. Jordan and a re
sponse by Mrs. Adolph Graupner,
U. C. '03, president of the associa
tion, the time was spent informally.
Light refreshments were served by
the Senior women during the after
noon.
