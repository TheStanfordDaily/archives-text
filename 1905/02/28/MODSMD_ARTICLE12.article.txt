# Women's League Candy Sale.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the advisory
board of the Woman's League held
this afternoon plans were made for
a candy sale which will he held at
the Inn on Friday, March 17th,
from 3 to 6. The candy sale held
last year was such a decided success
that the board decided to repeat the
attempt, and to allow this after
noon to take the place of the regu
lar March meeting of the League.
Already committees are at work
canvassing the faculty and campus
women, as well as the Palo Alto
population, for donations of candy
and dates. The sale.will he held
on the Inn porch, and the proceeds
will be used in adding to the furn
ishings of the rest-room.
