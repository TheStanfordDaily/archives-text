# Reid to Coach Harvard.
## 
### 
According to a dispatch from
Cambridge, Mass., William T. Reid
Jr., of Belmont, has accepted the
position of head coach of the Har
vard football team. The contract
with Ileid provides for one, two or
three years of service at hie option.
The salary appropriated by the
associated students is reported to
be $3500 a year, but it is generally
understood that Reid will receive
more than twice as much for his
services. Reid was one of Har
vard's greatest athletes, playing

full-back on the football team and
catcher on the baseball nine. Since
his graduation he has been em
ployed as sub-master at the Bel
mont School.
