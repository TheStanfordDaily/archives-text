# Training House Election Saturday.

### 
The election of new directors of
the Training House Corporation
has been postponed until Saturday
afternoon. At that time the cor

the quad for the transaction of
business in connection with the
new athletic field. Beside the elec

new by-laws and amendments will
be passed which will in all proba
bility greatly change the policy of
the corporation.
