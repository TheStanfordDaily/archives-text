# Sophomore Resolutions.
## 
### 
The Sophomore class at a meet
ing this afternoon decided to adopt
a set of resolutions expressing the
sorrow of the class at the death of
Mrs. Stanford. Class President
Macfarland appointed a committee
to draw up these resolutions, and it
was voted to have a copy printed
in the Daily Palo Alto. The
committee is composed of Miss I. L.
Johnston, J. F. Cassell, and J. M.
Ward.
