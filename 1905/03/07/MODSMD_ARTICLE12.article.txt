# Elected to Quadrangle Club.
## 
### 
The following men have been
elected to membership in the Quad
rangle Club: A. W. Hooper, '05,
president of the Tennis Club and
leader of the Glee Club; H. G.
Butterfield, '05, president of the
Boat Club; R. G. Barnett, '05.
Carnot debater; E. Y. Pomeroy,
'05, treasurer of the Students']
Guild; W. H. B. Fowler, '06, editor!
of the Daily Palo Alto.
