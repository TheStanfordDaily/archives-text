# Junior Opera Rehearsal.
## 
### 
The committee in charge of the
Junior opera has decided to give the
production in Assembly Hall on
Thursday evening, April 20th. Re
hearsals are being held in the Eng
lish building and will continue
until the funeral of Mrs. Stanford.
After the funeral active work will
begin under the direction of \V. B.
Bartlett of San Francisco. Bart
lett has signed a contract with the
Junior class to rehearse the cast
and direct the production.
All the material for the costumes
of the members of the cast has been
purchased and is being made up
under the direction of D. M. Rey
nolds, '05.
