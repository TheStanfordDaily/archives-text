# Santa Clara Defeats Varsity.
## 
### 
Santa Clara won the third base
ball game of the series yesterday
by the one-sided score of 7 to 0.1
The chief points of the game were
the hitting of Santa Clara and the |
poor batting of the Varsity. Sales
was batted hard by the Santa Clara
players, nine safe hits being ob
tained from his delivery. Two of
these were home runs and one a two
bagger. Stanford hit safely only
twice, and both these hits were of
the scratch order.
The Varsity will play Santa
Clara at Santa Clara again to-mor
row afternoon.
