# Bishop Nichols Will Preach.
## 
### 
At the regular service in the
Memorial Church next Sunday the
pulpit will be occupied by Bishop
William F.Nichols. Bishop Nichols
is at the head of the Episcopal
diocese of Northern California and
is one of the most prominent
churchmen in the West. He is also
at the head of the San Mateo Di
vinity School. The subject of his
address will be announced later.
The sermon will be of a memorial
nature.
