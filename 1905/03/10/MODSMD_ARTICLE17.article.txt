# Library Meeting Postponed.
## 
### 
The annual Santa Clara Valley
session of the State Library Asso
ciation, which was announced to be
held at Stanford on March 18th,
has been postponed until April
22d. This action was taken at a
meeting of the Stanford Library
Club Tuesday. Professor C. A.
iDuniway of the History depart
ment read a paper on the Congress
ional Library before the meeting.
