# POMONA MEET CANCELED.
## Bay Counties High Schools Contest May Be Held on the Last Day of March. 
### 
The track meet between Stanford
and Pomona College, scheduled for
March 2oth, has been canceled.
The team had planned to leave for
the south March 23d, but at this
time the funeral ceremonies of Mrs.
Stanford will be taking place.
The Freshman track meet with
the bay counties high schools,
which was scheduled for March
18th, has been indefinitely post
poned. There is a possibility, how
ever, that the interclass meeting
may be postponed until April Bth
and the meet with the bay coun
ties schools held on March 31st.
