# Postoffice to Be Closed.
## 
###

master-General, the Stanford Uni
versity postoffice will be closed to
morrow during the hours of the
funeral of Mrs. Stanford. The
mails will be distributed and dis
patched as usual, but there will be
110 general delivery.
