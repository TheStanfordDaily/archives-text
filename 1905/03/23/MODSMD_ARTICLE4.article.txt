# To Run Special Train.
## 
### 
In order to accommodate the
large crowds which will come from
San Francisco and San Jose to at
tend the funeral of Mrs. Stanford,
the Southern Pacific will run two
special trains. The San Francisco
special will leave the Third and
Townsend street depot at 11 a.m.
and will return from Palo Alto at
the conclusion of the funeral cere
monies.
No carriages will be allowed
on the route over which the fun
eral cortege is to pass until the
ceremonies are over.
