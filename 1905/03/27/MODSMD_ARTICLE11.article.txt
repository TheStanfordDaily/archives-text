# Junior Opera Rehearsals.
## 
### 
The members of the cast of the
Junior class opera, "The Princess
Enone," are once more hard at
work preparing their parte. Re
hearsals will he held regularly this
week. The first rehearsal will be
held to-night. Rehearsals will also
be held Wednesday and Friday
nights, and probably, Saturday
afternoon, under the direction of
R. \V. Bartlett of San Francisco.
The costumes have been completed.
Photographs of different scenes
have been taken.
The orchestration of the music
has been completed and the orches
tra has begun practicing on the
new score in preparation for the
production on the evening of Anril
20th.
