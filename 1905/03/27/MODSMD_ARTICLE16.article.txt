# Governor Pardee's Tribute.
## 
### 
The following telegram from
Governor Pardee was received last
Thursday evening by Vice-Presi
dent Branner:
"I regret my inability to attend
the funeral and testify by my pres
ence to the profound respect which
I, like all Californians,feel for Mrs.
Leland Stanford, who was a great
public benefactress and one of the
noblest of American women. Ad
jutant-General Lauck has been or
dered to attend the funeral, with
other members of my staff.
"Geo. C. Pardee."
