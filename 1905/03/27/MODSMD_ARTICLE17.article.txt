# Special Railroad Rates.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Company
is offering a special rate of $3 for
the round trip to Pacific Grove and
Santa Cruz for the Easter vacation,
which will begin Friday evening
at 6 o'clock. Parties buying Pacific
Grove tickets can arrange to re
turn by way of Santa Cruz by pay
ing 65 cents additional at Pacific
Grove.
Nothing has yet been done in
the way of granting reduced rates
to other points. An effort is being
made to secure reduced rates to
Los Angeles.
