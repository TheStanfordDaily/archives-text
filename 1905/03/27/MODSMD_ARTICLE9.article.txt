# Hyde to Captain Senior Team.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Senior class
track team held this afternoon on
the oval, C. E. Hyde was elected as
captain of the team for the coming
interclass field-day. Hyde has
been on the Varsity track team for
the last three years, his event be
ing the shot-put. This year he is
putting the shot and also throw
ing the hammer. He played cen
ter on the Varsity football team
lasl season.
