# Freshman Class Meeting.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Freshman
class to-morrow afternoon in the
chapel the question of a class em
blem for next year will be discussed.
Heretofore the classes have been
slow in securing their sophomore
emblems, and it is the purpose of
this meeting to appoint a commit
tee to select an emblem, in the
form of a hat, cap, or the like,
which will be on hand for the
members when they return next
August. Several other questions of
interest to the class will be dis
cussed.
