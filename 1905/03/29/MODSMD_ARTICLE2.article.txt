# Team to Meet Pomona College.
## 
### 
The team which will make the
trip to Pomona was announced by
Trainer Moulton and Captain Bell
after the interclass meet this after
noon. The fourteen men chosen

are H. W. Bell, '05, W. E. Craw
ford, '05, L. G. Burr, '05, I. J.
Hounds, '05, R. S. Carter, '05, L. G.
Russell, '06, M. J. Weller, '07, L.
M. Edwards, '07, P. A. Wood, '07,
J. C. Macfarland, '07, J. 0. Miller,
'08, H. L. Horton, '08, F. R. Lana
gan, '08, and \V. C. Shelton, '08.
They will be in charge of Trainer
Moulton.
