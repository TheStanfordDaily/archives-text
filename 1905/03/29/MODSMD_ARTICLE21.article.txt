# Sorrow of Bohemian Club.
## 
### 
The following letter was received
by Dr. Jordan from the Bohemian
Club of San Francisco:
Bohemian Club, San Francisco.
Dear Sir: The Board of Directors
of the Bohemian Club exprestes for
their organization the most pro
found sorrow over the untimely
loss of Mrs. Jane Lathrop Stan
ford. Her splendid services and
munificent benefactions given in
the cause of higher education have
endeared her to the people of Cali
fornia, and they deplore her tragic
death and mourn her lobs.
The Bohemian Club, interested
so deeply in the intellectual de
velopment of California and enjoy
ing the companionship of members
of your faculty and alumni, hastens
to speak its sympathy and testify
to the magnitude of the common
bereavement. I am,
Very respectfully yours,
James D. Phelan,
President.
