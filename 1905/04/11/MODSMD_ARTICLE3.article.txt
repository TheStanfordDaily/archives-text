# PHI BETA KAPPA ELECTION.
## Honorary Scholarship Fraternity Announces New Members Chosen Last Night. 
### 
One hundred and sixty-nine stu
dents and graduates were elected to
membership into the honorary
scholarship society of the Phi Beta
Kappa at a meeting of the frater
nity last night. President David
Starr Jordan was elected to honor
ary membership in the society. A
provision in the constitution of the
Stanford chapter of the society pro
vided for the election of not more
than one-tenth of the members of
all classes already graduated. Last
fall elections were made for mem
bers of 1892-95, and last night from
1896 up to the present time. The
