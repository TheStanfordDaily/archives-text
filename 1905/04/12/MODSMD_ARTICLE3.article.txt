# Field-day Ticket Sale.
## 
### 
Tickets for the intercollegiate
track meet next Saturday will lie
placed on sale at the box-office on
the quad to-morrow at 10. The
tickets for the section reserved for
the university students will be
seventy-tive cents, while tickets for
the general public sections are
priced at one dollar. Tickets are
reserved by sections, but do not call
for particular seats.
