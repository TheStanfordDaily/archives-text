# Certification of Teachers.
## 
### 
The next annual meeting of the
Joint Board of Normal School
Trustees will take place at the San
Jose Normal School next Friday
and Saturday. The principal sub
jects discussed will relate to the co
operation of the universities and
normal schools in the certification
of high school teachers. Repre
sentatives of the normal schools
and universities will present papers.
The meeting will he open to those
specially interested in the training
of teachers.
