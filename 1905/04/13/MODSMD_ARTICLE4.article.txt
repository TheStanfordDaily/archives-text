# Special Train Saturday.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific will run a
special train of ten coaches Satur
day to accommodate the Stanford
people who wish to attend the in
tercollegiate field-day at Berkeley.
A coach will be reserved on this
train for the track team and the
University Band. The train will
leave Palo Alto at 9:45 Saturday
morning, and no stops will be made
between Palo Alto and San Fran
cisco.
Round-trip tickets to Berkeley
will be sold for $1.10, good until
Monday morning.
