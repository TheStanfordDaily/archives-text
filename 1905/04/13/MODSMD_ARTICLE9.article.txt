# Waseda Team Arrives in Ten Days.
## 
### 
The Japanese baseball team from
Waseda College is due to arrive in
San Francisco from Japan on
April 21st. They will come di
rectly to Stanford and will practice
on the local diamond. The first of
two games to be played between
this team and the Varsity will
probably take place here 011 April
29th, and the date of the other
contest, which will be played in
San Francisco, will be decided
upon the arrival of the team.
