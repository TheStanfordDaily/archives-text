###

Young Women's Christian Associa

chapel the officers for the ensuing
year were chosen, the election re
sulting as follows: President, Miss
Mary Graham, '00; vice-president,
Miss Pauline Gartzmann,'o7; treas
urer, Miss Mary Betz, '06; cor
responding secretary, Miss Helen
Sprague, '06; recording secretary,
