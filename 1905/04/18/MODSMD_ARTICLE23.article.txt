# Hawaiian Club Organized.
## 
### 
A number of former residents of
Hawaii in Palo Alto and the uni
versity met at the home of Mrs. A.
D. Larnach in Palo Alto Friday
evening and organized an Hawaiian
club under the name of the "Hui
Hawaii." The club expects to meet
once a month for social purposes.
The next meeting will be held on
May sth, when a number of ad
ditional members will be elected.
