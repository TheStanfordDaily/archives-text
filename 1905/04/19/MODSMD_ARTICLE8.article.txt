# Announcements.
## 
### 
Student Body — Will meet in
chapel Thursday at 1:15.
A. M. Dibble, Pres.
Senior Pins.— Orders will be
taken at the Bookstore.
F. B. Hathaway.
Philological Association —Will
meet Thursday, April 20th, at 2:30
in room 31. B. O. Foster, Sec.
Biblical History 1. —For those
who are to finish this course, an
examination will he held Thursday
at 1:30 in the chapel.
Senior Week Committee —Will
meet in room 10 at 1:15 Thursday.
A. L. Trowbridge,
Chairman.
Athletic Subscriptions. — Col
lectors who have subscriptions in
their hands are requested to turn
them in at once.
R. W. Barrett.
Track Men. —All track men who
competed in the intercollegiate field
day will meet at Franklin's, Palo
Alto, Friday, at 2 p.m. for photo
graph and election of captain.
H. W. Bell, Captain.
University Band —All members
are requested to return their suits
and instruments to J. E. Stewart,
room 118, Encina Hall, or to L.
W. Cutler, 602 Cowper street, Palo
Alto, as soon as possible.
L. W. Cutler, Mgr.
