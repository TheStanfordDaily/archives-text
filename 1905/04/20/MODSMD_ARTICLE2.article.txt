# English Play Ticket Sale To-morrow.
## 
### 
The sale of tickets for the
English Club's production of
" Every Man in His Humour" on
April 27th will begin at 9 o'clock
to-morrow morning at the campus
Bookstore, at the Stanford Phar
macy in Palo Alto, at Paul Elder's
bookstore in San Francisco, and at
Curtaz's music store in San Jose.
Seats for Saturday's performance
will be put on sale at a later
date. The price for seats at both
performances will be seventy-five
and fifty cents, and no person will
be allowed to purchase more than
ten tickets.
