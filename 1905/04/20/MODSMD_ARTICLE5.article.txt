# GUILD DIRECTORS ELECTED.
## Executive Committee Also Grants Permission for Crews to Make Northern Trip. 
### 
At the regular meeting of the
Executive Committee last night the
annual election of directors of the
Students' Guild was held. The fol
lowing were elected to the hoard:
R. VV. Barrett, '04, H. E. Savage,
'05, J. N. Stanford, '06, Miss Emma
Hayward, '06, and G. J. Presley,'o7.
The committee authorized the
Varsity and Freshman crews to take
the trip north to row the Univer
sity of Washington, allowing them
their transportation except that
portion which Washington will
pay. This trip will he made im
mediately after college closes in
May.
