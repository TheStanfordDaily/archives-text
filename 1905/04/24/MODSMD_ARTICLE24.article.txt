# GENERAL ARRANGEMENTS.
## 
### 
There Will Be Two Performances : Thursday, April 27, at 8 p.m.;
Saturday, April 29, at 10.30 a m.
Reserved seats are on sale at the Bookstore, Stanford Uni
versity; Stanford Pharmacy, Palo Alto; Paul Elder's bookstore,
San Francisco, and Curtaz's music store, San Jose. Prices, 50
and 75 cents.
Trains leaving San Francisco at 9 a.m. and San Jose at 9:20
a.m. reach Palo Alto in time for the Saturday performance. One
fare for the round trip from San Francisco, San Jose, or Santa
Clara. Reduced rates from other points.
The bus fare from the Palo Alto station to the Assembly
Hall is 10 cents.
PROGRAM FOR SATURDAY.
Play begins 10:30 a.m.
Luncheon, at the Inn or Roble Hall 1:30 p.m.
Stanford-Wnseda baseball game 2:30 p.m.
Organ recital, Memorial Church 4 p.m.
