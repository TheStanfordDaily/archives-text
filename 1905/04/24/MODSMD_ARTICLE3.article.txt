# Meeting of Graduate Students' Club.
## 
### 
The members of the Graduate
Students' Club will meet at the
home of Dr. and Mrs. Stillman, 2
Alvarado row, next Tuesday eve
ning, April 25th, at 8 o'clock. Other
post-graduate students, not mem
bers of the club, who desire to be
come members, are invited also. Dr.
Murray and Dr. Angell, members
of the faculty committee on gradu
ate students, will speak briefly on
"College Work and University
Work" and "Original Work versus
Research Work."
Members are requested to come
in time for a short business meeting
to begin promptly at 8 o'clock, at
which there will be an election of
officers for next year. The later
part of the evening will be spent
informally, with refreshments,when
President Jordan's "Compliments
to the Club" will be distributed.
