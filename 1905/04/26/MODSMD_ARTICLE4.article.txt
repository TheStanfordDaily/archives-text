# E.O. James Quietly Married.
## 
### 
E. O. James, '02, who takes one
of the leading roles in ''Every Man
in his Humour," was quietly mar
ried in Palo Alto to-day to Miss
Flora Mitchell of Pacific Grove.
The ceremony was performed at
noon at the Methodist church in
Palo Alto, Rev. H. E. Milnes offici
ating. E. K. Putnam, J. S. Bur
cham and W. 0. Clarke were wit
nesses.
Miss Mitchell has been organist
of the Methodist church at Pacific
Grove, and is prominent in church
circles. Mr. James is principal of
the Pacific Grove High School. The
couple are at present staying on the
campus.
