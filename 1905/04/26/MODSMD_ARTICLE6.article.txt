# Graduate Club Elect Officers.
## 
### 
The Graduate Students' Club
met at the home of Professor J. M.
Stillman last night and elected of
ficers as follows: President, M. A.
Thomas, '04; vice-president, Miss
Anna P. Cooper, Colorado College,
'96; secretary-treasurer, Miss Cora
H. Gibson, '04. P. J. Treat, Co
lumbia '03, and H. B. Humphrey,
Minnesota '99, were elected as ad
ditional officers of the executive
committee.
The club decided by vote to join
the national organization of gradu
ate student clubs. Professor F.
Angell delivered an address on
"The Place of a Graduate Club in
the University," in which he com
pared the German with the Ameri
can graduate student.
