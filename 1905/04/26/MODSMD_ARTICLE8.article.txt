# Professor Abe to Lecture.
## 
### 
Professor Iso Abe, head of the de
partment of sociology and ethics
at Waseda University, will lecture
in the University chapel Friday
evening at 7:30. Mr. Abe will de
scribe the recent development of
intercollegiate athletics in Japan,
and will also tell of his purpose in
bringing the Waseda team to
America. Abe is a graduate of Ox
ford and is noted as the foremost
exponent of the doctrines of social
ism in Japan. The lecture will be
free.
