# FOUR MEN TO MAKE TRIP.
## Debating Team, with E.A. Cunha as Alternate, Leaves for Reno To-morrow. 
### 
The debating team composed of
W. L. Blair, '06, C. S. Osgood, '06,
A. M. Dibble, '05, and E. A. Cun
ha, '06, alternate, will leave for
Reno to-morrow evening to engage
the University of California in de
bate Saturday evening. Stanford
will uphold the affirmative of the
following question:
"Resolved, That laws relating to
marriage and divorce should be
uniform throughout the United
States, and that for that purpose
Congress should, by constitutional
amendment, be given power to
pass such laws."
