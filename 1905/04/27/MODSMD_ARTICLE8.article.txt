# Alumnae Reception.
## 
### 
On Saturday afternoon from 3 to
6 o'clock the Senior women of the
University of California and Stan
ford will be the guests of the Associ
ation of Collegiate Alumnae at the
annual reception to be given in
Century Hall, San Francisco.
Mrs. Adolph Uraupner, president
of the association, will be assisted
in receiving the guests by promi
nent members residing in the city
and by a joint committee of rep
resentative seniors from the two
universities.
