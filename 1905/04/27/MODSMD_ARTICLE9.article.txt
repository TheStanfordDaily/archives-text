# Foreman Soule Is Ill.
## 
### 
Eugene Soule, foreman of the
Manual Training department, is
confined to his home in Palo Alto
with a severe bronchial cold. Mr.
Soule was taken sick last Friday
and it will be some days before he
can resume his duties. During his
absence the work is in charge of
Instructor E. J. Stanley of the
Pattern Making department.
