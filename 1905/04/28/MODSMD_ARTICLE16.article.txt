# Basket-ball To-morrow.
## 
### 
To-morrow at 1:30 the California
and Stanford basket-ball teams
will meet on the Roble court for the
second game in the intercollegiate
series. The admission to the game is
twenty-five cents, and tickets may
be secured from any member of the
team.
