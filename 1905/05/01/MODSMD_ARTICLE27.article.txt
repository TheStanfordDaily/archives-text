# "Princess Enone" Selections.
## 
### 
A limited number of "Stanford
Memories," one of the most popu
lar selections from the recent
Junior opera,"The Princess Enone,"
have been placed on sale at the Uni
versity Bookstore and at the Stan
ford Pharmacy in Palo Alto. The
price is twenty-five cents per copy.
The music is published under the
auspices of the Junior class, which
is guaranteed against loss and will
receive one-half of any net, receipts.
