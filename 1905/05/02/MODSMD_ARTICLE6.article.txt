# High School Field-Day Saturday.
## 
### 
The annual field-day of the Pa
cific Academic League will be held
on the campus oval next Saturday
afternoon. The league contains the
following high schools: San Jose,
Santa Clara, Los Gatos, Palo Alto,
Mountain View and Campbell. All
the schools of the league will prob
ably be represented, but the strong
est teams will come from Palo Alto
and San Jose. Pomeroy and Toma
sini, both of whom made a good
showing in the meet held between
the Stanford Freshmen and the
Santa Clara- county high schools
this spring, will compete for Palo
Alto High School, the former in
the sprints and the latter in the
weights.
