# Honor Memory of Dead Student.
## 
### 
"The members of the Law As
sociation of Stanford University
wish to express the deepest sorrow
for the untimely death of one of
our fellow students and companion,
Robert Fountain.
"We recognize in him an unsel
fish friend and a law student of
pronounced ability. So through
this committee we wish to extend
our heartfelt sympathy to the
friends and relatives of him whose
memory we shall always cherish.
"Ralph McComish, '03.
"Oscar Gibbons, '05.
"Alexander Sherriffs, '05."
