# Concert Tickets on Sale.
## 
### 
The tickets for the Mandolin and
Glee Club's concert which will be
given in Assembly Hall Friday
night were put on sale this morn
ing at the University Bookstore
and at the Stanford Pharmacy in
Palo Alto. All tickets will sell for
fifty cents and there will be no re
served seats.
On Saturday evening the clubs
will give a concert at the flower
festival to be held in Menlo Park.
