# "QUAD" SALE POSTPONED.
## Serious Make-up Errors Discovered by the Publishers at the Last Moment. 
### 
The sale of the 1906 "Quad" has
been postponed until further an
nouncement. Certain typographical
errors have been discovered by the
printers which it is necessary to cor
rect. Editor May was called to the
city this evening for that purpose.
