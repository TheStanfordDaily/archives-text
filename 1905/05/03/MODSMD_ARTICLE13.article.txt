# PHI BETA KAPPA REUNION.
## Members of the Society in California Will Come Together in Big Celebration. 
### 
Plane are being made by the
Stanford chapter of Phi Beta Kappa
for a reunion and celebration of
the society's members in this State,
to be held some time during com
mencement week. Arrangements
have been made to hold public ex
ercises in the afternoon of the day
set for the reunion, and for a ban
quet, open only to members of Phi
Beta Kappa, in the Inn on the
evening of the same day. Pro
fessor Moore of the University of
California will deliver the address
of the day, and a poem will be read.
More definite announcements re
garding the reunion will be made
later.
