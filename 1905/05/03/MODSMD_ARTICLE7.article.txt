# TO MANAGE THE "SEQUOIA."
## G.J. Presley, '07, Elected by a Good Majority in the Second Contest for Position. 
### 
George J. Presley, '07, was to
day elected business manager of
the Sequoia for the next year by a
majority of 82 votes over W. L.
Blair, '06. A total of 408 votes
was cast. The final vote was
Blair 163, Presley 245.
Presley played first base on this
year's Varsity. He is a member of
the Gymnasium Club, the Encina
Club, and the Nestoria Literary
Society. He is also a member of
the Executive Committee of the
Associated Students. He is a Law
major, registering from San Fran
cisco.
