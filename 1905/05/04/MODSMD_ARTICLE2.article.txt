# Seniors Issue Invitations.
## 
### 
The invitations for Senior week
issued hv the class of 1905 have
heen printed and are ready for dis
tribution. Each member of the
Senior class will be given six
invitations upon the payment of
the assessment of two dollars levied
a few days ago. Seniors desiring
more than these six invitations may
buy them at the Bookstore at a
cost of five cents each.
