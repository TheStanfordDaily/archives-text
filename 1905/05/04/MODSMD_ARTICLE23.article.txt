# Candy Sale To-morrow.
## 
### 
Arrangements have been per
fected for the candy sale to be given
under the auspices of the Women's
League to-morrow afternoon. The
Inn porch will be used for this
occasion, and from 3 to 5 o'clock
the league members will receive
members of the college community
and serve them with candies, dates,
and salted nuts. The proceeds of
the sale will be used for completing
the furnishing of the women's rest
room on the quad. With the pro
ceeds of a similar sale held last
spring the room was renovated and
partially refurnished, and the ex
ecutive committee of the league is
desirous of completing the work,
and of providing a comfortable
resting place for the women of the
college.
