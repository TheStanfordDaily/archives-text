# Roble Gymnasium Club Exhibition.
## 
### 
To-morrow evening at 8 o'clock

the Roble Gymnasium Club will
give its first exhibition for this
year. The performance, which is
invitational, will take place in the
Iloble gymnasium before an audi
ence composed exclusively of wo
men. The work done by the club
in practice has been along lines
similar to those of the Stanford
Gymasium Club, and an interest
ing program has been promised.
Beside the regular drill work, there
are to be exhibitions of club-swing
ing, and pyramid work. The mem
bership of the club is limited to
twenty, and the work this term has
been directed by C. L. Duncan, '04.
Mrs. G. N. Brown is the president
of the club.
