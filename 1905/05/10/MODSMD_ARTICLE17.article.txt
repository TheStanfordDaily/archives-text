# Teachers' Association to Meet.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Drawing and
Manual Training Teachers' Associ
ation will be held in the Stanford
Art studio on Saturday morning,
May 13th, at 10:30. Among the
speakers are Miss King of the San
Jose Normal School, E. R. Snyder
of San Jose, and F. 11. Cauch of
Oakland. Several members of the
Stanford faculty will give addresses

explaining work in free-hand and
mechanical drawing and in manual
training and shop work. Professor
A. B. Clark of the Stanford Art
department is president of the as
sociation.
