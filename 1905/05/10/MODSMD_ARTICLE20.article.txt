# Final Fraternity Ball Game.
## 
### 
To-morrow afternoon at 4, Zeta
Psi will contest with Phi Delta
Theta in the final baseball game
for the fraternity championship
cup. The game will be played on
the Varsity diamond. Both teams
have played good baseball through
out the tournament, defeating all
the teams who opposed them. The
batteries to-morrow will be: for
Zeta Psi, Rook and Stott; for Phi
Delta Theta, Chalmers and King.
