# Basket-ball Captain Chosen.
## 
### 
The women's baeket-ball team
met yesterday to choose a new cap
tain and manager for the coming
year. To the position of captain
Miss Hazel Huiskamp, '06, was

unanimously elected. Miss Huis
kamp has played as guard through
out the season and it was due
largely to her effective work that
the team won in the second game
with California. She is a member
of the Delta Gamma sorority.
Miss Florence Grace, '06, was
elected manager to succeed Miss
Jane Spalding, '06, the president
elect of the Women's Athletic As
sociation. Miss Grace has played
on the basket-ball team for two
years, and has also been connected
with the Women's League during
the past year, serving as treasurer
of that organization.
