# Senior Farce Poster Appears.
## 
### 
Half a hundred large posters
were scattered around the campus
last night announcing the ap
proaching production of the Senior
farce, "Kidnaped." The posters
are attractively gotten up and are
clever in subject matter. Personali
ties are freely dealt with, many of
the faculty members coming in for
their share of attention. A limited
number of the posters have been
placed on sale at the Bookstore at
a price of fifteen cents per poster.
