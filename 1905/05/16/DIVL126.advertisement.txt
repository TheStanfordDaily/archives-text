VACATION 1905
IS NOW READY FOR DISTRIBUTION
"Vacation" is issued annually by the
CALIFORNIA NORTHWESTERN RY.
THE PICTURESQUE ROUTE OF CALIFORNIA
and is the standurd publication on the
Pacific Coast for information regarding
MINERAL SPRING RESORTS,
COUNTRY HOMES AND FARMS
WHERE SUMMER BOARDERS ARE
TAKEN, AND SELECT CAMPING
SPOTS.
This year's edition "Vacation 1905" con
tains 200 pages-, beautifully illustrated, and
is complete in its detailed information as to
location, acoouimodations, attractions, etc.,
with terms from $7.00 per week up.
To be had at Ticket Offices, 650 Market
Street (Chronicle Building),and Tiburon
Perry, foot of Market Street; General
Office, Mutual Life Building, corner of
Sansome and California Streets, San
Francisco.
Applications by mail will receive im
mediate response
JUS. L. FRAZICR, Gen. Manager R. X. RYAN, Gen. Pass. Agent
THE DINING
CAR SERVICE
(From the California Limited
Log Book)
It is peculiar that all of us should
compliment the Harvey System, and
yet we do. It's a habit, and we
can't help it. lam really ashamed
to say anything further, but I can't
abstain. The Dining Car Service
on the California Limited is too
good to be true.
K. T. W.
