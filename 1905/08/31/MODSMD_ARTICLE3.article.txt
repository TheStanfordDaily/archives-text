# Annual Address to Sigma Xi.
## 
### 
The annual address before the
Stanford chapter of Sigma Xi will
be delivered by Professor Wil
liston of the University of Chicago
about September 20th. His subject
will be ''Ancient Sea Reptiles."
Professor Williston is one of the
best known paleontologists in the
United States, and was president
of the general society of Sigma Xi
at the time the two California
chapters were establiahed.
