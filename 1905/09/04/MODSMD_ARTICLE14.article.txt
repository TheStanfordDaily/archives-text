# "Chappie" to Appear Monday.
## 
### 
Owing to a delay in receiving
cuts from the city the first issue of
the Chaparral under its new man
agement will not appear until next
Monday. "Chappie" will devoteall
his attention in his first editorial
to welcoming the incoming class.
