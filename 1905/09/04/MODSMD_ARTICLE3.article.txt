# Y.W.C.A. Meets To-morrow.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Y. W.
C. A. will be held Tuesday at4:Boin
the chapel, with Miss Mary Gra
ham as leader, and Miss Theresa
Wilbur as speaker. Subject, Christ's
Call to Young Women. There will
be a solo by Miss Georgina Lyman.
As this is to be the welcome meet
ing it is especially desired that all
University women be present.
