# Encina Directors Hold Meeting.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Encina Club
directors 1). M. Letnmon, '08, was
elected to fill a vacancy on the
board caused by the absence of C.
G. Lewis, '08. The date for the
regular Encina Club "at home"
has been set for November 18th,
one week after the intercollegiate
game.
Plans were discussed for holding
a smoker sometime near the close

of this semester. Such an affair
was arranged for last semester but
was cancelled owing to the death
of Robert Fountain, '05.
