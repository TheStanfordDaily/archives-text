# Arcade Moot Court Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The Arcade Moot Court held its
first meeting of the semester this
morning, and A. D. Larnach,'oB,
was elected to fill a vacancy caused
by the absence of A. Sherriffs, ex-'O5.
Officers were elected for the coming
year as follows: President, E. C.
Rittenhouse; secretary-treasurer, D.
K. Siebert. Regular work for the
semester will begin at once.
