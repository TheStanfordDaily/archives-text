# CLASS TENNIS SATURDAY.
## Club Meets To-morrow Night to Make Arrangements for Tournament. 
### 
To-morrow evening the first
meeting of the Tennis Club will be
held in room 11, and officers for
the coming year will be elected and
other important business trans
acted. The chief business will be to
make arrangements for the class
tournament which will be started
next Saturday. All players in
tending to take part in this tour
nament or in any way interested
in tennis should attend the meet
ing and leave their names with the
secretary. Class sweaters will
probably be awarded to the win
ners in the coming class tourna
ment.
