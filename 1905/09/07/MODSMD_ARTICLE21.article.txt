# Library Club Elects Officers.
## Fencing Club Will Begin Practice. 
### 
The first meeting of the Library
Club was held Wednesday after
noon. The members listened to
the reading of the reports of Mr.
Dodge, Miss Thompson and Miss
Hughes, the Stanford representa
tives at the Portland convention of
the American Library Association.
An election of officers was held,
Melville Dodge being elected chair
man and P. E. Millar, '06, secre
tary and treasurer.
The work of the club this season
will consist mainly of instruction
to the Library assistants.
