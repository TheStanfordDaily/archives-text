At a meeting of the Foil and
Mask Club held last night plans
were made for regular practice dur
ing the present college year. For
this purpose it will meet in Encina
gymnasium every Thursday eve
ning. Interest in fencing in the
University has been at a low ebb
for several years. The Foil and
Mask Club was reorganized last
spring for the purpose of arousing
interest in the work. The club has

received new fencing jackets and
foils and intends to meet regularly
in the future.
