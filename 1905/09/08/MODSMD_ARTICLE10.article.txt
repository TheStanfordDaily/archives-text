# Professor Huberich Has Arrived.
## 
### 
Professor Huberich, one of the
new men in the Law department
this year, arrived in Palo Alto yes
terday, and in all probability will
meet his clashes on Monday. Huber
ich was assistant professor of law
at the University of Texas last
year, lie graduated from there in
1897 and received his D. C. L. at
Yale in 1899.
