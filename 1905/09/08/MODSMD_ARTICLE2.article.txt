# Reception in Gymnasium.
## 
### 
The reception which the Y. M.
C. A. and the Y. \V. C. A. tender
each year to the Freshman men
and women of the University will
be held to-morrow in Encina gym
nasium at half-past seven. There
will he an informal program includ
ing a short talk by Dr. 0. L. Elli
ott, and several musical selections.
Refreshments will be served.
