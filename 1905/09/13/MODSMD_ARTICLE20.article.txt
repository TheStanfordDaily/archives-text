# To Give Prize for Best "Stunt."
## 
### 
The University of California has
offered a prize in the shape of a
watch-fob to the student inventing
the best ''stunt" to be used at the
big game in November. Prizes
have also been offered for the best
song and best yell handed in. The
prize for the winner of the song
contest is another watch-fob, while
the writer of thesuccessful yell will
receive a season ticket to the foot
ball games.
