# ATHLETIC SUPPLIES ARRIVE.
## Thousand Dollars Worth of Goods for Football and Track Teams. 
### 
Manager Barrett is in receipt of
a large shipment of athletic sup
plies, representing an investment
of a thousand dollars. These goods
were ordered some time ago when
the necessity for more football suits
was made apparent by the large
number of men turning out.
The shipment consists of football
jerseys, headgears, stockings, nose
guards and other supplies for foot
ball. There are also a large num
ber of running shoes for the men
who wish to engage in fall training
upon the track.
