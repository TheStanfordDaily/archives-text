# TRY-OUTS COMPLETED
## MEN SELECTED TO PLAY IN INTERCLASS TOURNAMENT. Cassell Wins Junior Try-out.—Contest for University Championship Begins Next Saturday. 
### 
By defeating M. C. Burr in the
finals of the Junior tennis try-outs
yesterday afternoon, J F. Cassell
won for the second time the right
to represent the Class of 1907 in
the annual interclass tennis tourna
ment. Cassell won the champion
ship of his class last year and was
also a mehiber of the '05 Varsity
tennis team, playing with G. A.
Hodge, '06, in the intercollegiate
doubles match last April.
The finishing of the Junior
tournament completes the try-outs
for the interclass singles. The first
round of this tournament will he
played next Saturday, the contest
starting with the match between
the classes of '08 and '09, repre
sented by L. R. Gav and J. N.
Alexander respectively. This will
be followed immediately by the
contest between the Junior and
Senior classes, represented by J.
F. Cassell and A. A. Mathewson
respectively.
The final match for the Univer
sity championship will be played
either Saturday or Monday after
noon, the definite time to be an
nounced later.
The summary for the Junior try
out follows:
Preliminary round —M. C. Burr
defeated C. L. Lewis, 6-1, 7-5. S.
M. Salisbury defeated A. Rosen
feld, 6-4, 6-1. VV. H. Swayne de
feated. W. W. Ward by default.
J. F. Cassell defeated G. E. Dole,
1-6, 6-3, 7-5.
First round—Burr defeated Sal
isbury, 6-4, 6-1. Cassell defeated
Swayne, 6-3, 6-1.
Final round —Cassell defeated
Burr, 6-3, 5-7, 6-1.
