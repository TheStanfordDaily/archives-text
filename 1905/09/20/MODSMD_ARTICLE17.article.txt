# Euphronia Debate To-morrow.
## 
### 
For Thursday evening, Septem
ber 21st, the following program
has been announced by the Eu
phronia Debating Society:
Debate — "Resolved, That the
Japanese immigration should be
prohibited by a law similar to that
against the Chinese immigration."
A. A. Murphy, '08, and L. New
land, '08, will speak for the affirm
ative; G. Knupp, '07, and J. F.
Reilly, '08, for the negative.
