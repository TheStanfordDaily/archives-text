# Stunt Man Not Selected.
## 
### 
A try-out for stunt man on the
Glee Club was held last night at
the Zeta Psi house, but no one was
selected to fill the vacancy. There
were four candidates in the com
petition, and most of them were
new men. As the stunt man will
have a very important place on the
program of the clubs, the commit
tee wants to get the best talent in
the University, and for this reason
another try-out will bo held soon.
