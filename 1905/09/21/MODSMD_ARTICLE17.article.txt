# Senior Election To-morrow.
## 
### 
The election of a treasurer for
the Senior class, which bus twice
resulted in a tie, will be held on
the outer quadrangle tomorrow
from 11:15 to 2. The candidates
are E. C. Smith and W. Y. Wentz.
In the first election each man
received twenty-seven votes. Yes
terday the total vote was larger,
the candidates polling forty votes
apiece. The same committee will
have charge of the ballot-box as at
the last election.
