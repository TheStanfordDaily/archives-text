# Freshman Debaters Organize.
## 
### 
The men of the entering class
who are interested in debating met
last night and organized for the
present semester. D. D. Sales, '06,
president of the Debating League,
took charge of the meeting, and
conducted the election of officers.
J. Politzer was elected president, E.
E. Young, vice-president, and F.
R. Palmer, secretary.
