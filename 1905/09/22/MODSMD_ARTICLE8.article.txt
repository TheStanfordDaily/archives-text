# Palo Alto vs. Lowell Saturday.
## 
### 
Saturday morning at 10 o'clock
the football teams of the Palo Alto
and Lowell High Schools will meet
on the campus. This will be the
first game of the season for the
Palo Alto team. Lowell has played
one game, losing to Belmont by the
score of 16 to 0, last Saturday.
Next week the Palo Alto team will
play the Freshmen.
