# Y.W.C.A. Initiation Service.
## 
### 
The weekly meeting of the Y. W.
C. A. will he held in the chapel

at 4:30 to-morrow The regular
order of business will give place to
the initiation service, at which all
new members will be welcomed in
to the association. Miss Gertrude
Smith, '04, general secretary of the
work here, and Miss Mary Gorham,
'06, president of the association,
will speak on the subject,"Why am
I an association member?"
