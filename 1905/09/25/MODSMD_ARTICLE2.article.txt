# INTERCLASS TENNIS
## J.F. CASSELL, '07, WINS SINGLES CHAMPIONSHIP. Interesting' Matches in Annual Tournament Which Was Played Saturday on Encina Courts. 
### 
J. F. Cassell, '07, won the tennis
championship of Stanford Univer
sity last Saturday by defeating L.
R. Gay, '08, in the final round of
the interclass tournament.
The first round of the interclass
contest was begun by the match
between L. R. Gay and J. N. Alex
ander, '09. Although the play was
close, Gay won in straight sets, his
opponent being unable to master
his strong net game. The second
match of the first round between
J. F. Cassell, '07, and A. A.Mathew
son,'o6, was won by Cassell, bring
ing Cassell and Gay together for
the final round.
The final match was not sched
uled to be played until this after
noon, but both players wished to
finish the contest at once, so the
play for the University champion
ship was started immediately after
the completion of the first round.
The final match was the closest
of the day. Cassell won through
his ability to break through Gay's
net game, his clever passing win
ning him many points.
First round —L. R. Gay, '08, de
feated J. N. Alexander, '09, 6-3,
6-4, 6-4. J. F. Cassell, '07, de
feated A. A. Mathewson, '06, 6-4,
6-3, 6-1.
Final round —J. F. Cassell, '07,
defeated L. R. Gay, '08, 7-5, 6-1,
7-5.
