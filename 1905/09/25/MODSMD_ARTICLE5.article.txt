# Miss Balcomb Wins Honor.
## 
### 
Miss Mary F. Balcomb returned
yesterday from Portland, Oregon,
where she participated in the Paci
fic Coast oratorical contest held un
der the auspices of the College Pro
hibition League Association. Miss
Balcomb won second place in the
contest. Mr. Haeringer, the repre
sentative from Southern California,
took first place, and will represent
the Pacific Coast in the next na
tional contest.
