# English Club Meets Wednesday.
## 
### 
The second meeting of the Eng
lish Club will beheld next Wednes
day evening at 8 o'clock in the
Bachelors' Clubhouse. The chief
business which will come before
the meeting is the election of a
president to succeed A.R. Fletcher,
ex-'O6, who failed to return to col
lege this semester. Professor V. L.
Kellogg of the Department of En
tomology will address the meeting.
