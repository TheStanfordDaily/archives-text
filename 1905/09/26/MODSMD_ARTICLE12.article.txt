# French Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The Circle Francais met last
night at the home of Professor 0.
M. Johnston for the purpose of
electing officers for the ensuing se
mester. Lovell Langstroth,'oB, was
elected president, and W. F. Mor
in, '06, secretary.
The club is open to any student
of the University, provided the ap
plicant possesses a fair degree of
fluency in speaking the French
language. Meetings will be held
every two weeks at the homes of
the members and will include a
social and literary program.
