# ASSEMBLY NEXT WEDNESDAY.
## Dr. Jordan Will Address Students on His Recent Trip to Europe. 
### 
The second regular Assembly of
the year will be held in Assembly
Hall Wednesday, October 4th, at
11:15. The meeting will be ad
dressed by President David Starr
Jordan, who will speak to the stu
dents on subjects of general interest,
for the first time since his return
to the University. Dr. Jordan will
also speak concerning his recent
trip to Europe.
The second speaker for the As
sembly has not been selected, but
efforts are being made to secure an
alumnus from San Francisco.
