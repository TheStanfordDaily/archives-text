# Report on the Typhoid Epidemic.
## 
### 
The report entitled "The Typhoid
Fever Epidemic at Palo Alto,"
made by Professor J. C. L. Fish to
the Palo Alto Board of Health, has
recently been published. This
pamphlet was printed in accord
ance with an act passed by the
city trustees of Palo Alto authoriz
ing its publication as a public
document.
The report includes an account
of sanitary conditions before and
after the epidemic, an analysis of
reported cases by Dr. C. M. Mosher
and Dr. W. F. Snow, and a state
ment of the general facts concern
ing the causes of the infection.
