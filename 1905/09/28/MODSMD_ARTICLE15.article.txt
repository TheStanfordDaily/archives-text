# Song and Yell Contest To-morrow.
## 
### 
The song and yell competition
will close to-morrow night at 6
o'clock, and all songs and yells
must be handed in before that time
to one of the following committee:
C. A. Beardsley, '06, J. C. Macfar
land, '07, and K. L. Fenton, '08.
On Saturday morning at 9 o'clock
the judges appointed will meet in
the chapel and decide upon the
songs and yells submitted. All
competitors will be present at this
meeting.
