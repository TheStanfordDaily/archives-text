# Euphronia Debate To-morrow.
## 
### 
For Friday evening, September
29th, the Euphronia Debating
Society has announced the follow
ing program:
Debate: "Resolved, That it would
be to the best interest of the United
States to form a reciprocity treaty
with Canada." L. M. Edwards,'o7,
and S. Houston, '08, will speak for
the affirmative; P. B. Smith, '06,
and J. H. Brennan,' 07, for the
negative -*
