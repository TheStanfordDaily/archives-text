# Freshmen Out for Baseball Practice.
## 
### 
Yesterday afternoon a squad of
Freshman baseball men were out
on the Varsity diamond. The
time was occupied in light practice,
no game being played. These
Freshmen are planning to organize
a team to play the faculty, and
with this object in view they are
trving to get Freshman baHeball
material to come out and practice
on the diamond.
