wishing to go into fall training re
port at Encina gymnasiun at 4:45
Wednesday. E. V. Henley, '06.

at 7:45 Thursday evening, October
sth, in room 461. Dr. Jordan will
address the society.

Juniok Class. —The '07 Quad as
sessment is now due. Manager
Brennan will he in his office, room
14, on Tuesdays and Thursdays

meet in the chapel at 7:30 Wed
nesday. It is necessary that all
members desiring to try out should
be present.
