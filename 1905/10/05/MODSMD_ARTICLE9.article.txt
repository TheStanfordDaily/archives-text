# California Plays Last Game.
## 
### 
Yesterday afternoon the Cali
fornia freshmen and the Berkeley
High football teams played a 0 to 0
game on the California field. The
Freshmen failed to show the form
of their earlier games. This was
the last contest for the freshmen
before the intercollegiate match,
and hereafter the practice will be
secret.
