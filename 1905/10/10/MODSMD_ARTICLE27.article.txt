# Roosevelt Discusses Athletics.
## 
### 
On Monday the athletic advisers
of the most important universities
of the East lunched with President
Roosevelt for the purpose of dis
cussing college athletics. All of
the branches were touched upon in
the discussion, but football received
special attention. The President
expressed the desire that brutality
he eliminated from football as much
as possible, and hoped that the
rules of the game would be amend
ed with this object in view. The
guests of the President were: Dr. N.
H. Nichols and W. T. Reed, Jr. of
Harvard, Arthur T. Hillebrand
and John B. Fine of Princeton, and
Walter Camp and Mr. Owsley of
Yale.
