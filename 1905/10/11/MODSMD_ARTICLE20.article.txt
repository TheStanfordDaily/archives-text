# Manager Resigns.
## 
### 
James Rennie, manager of the
Stanford ranch at Vina, has re
signed. The property, which is
part of the University endowment,
consists of 100,000 acres in Te
hama and Butte counties, and is
assessed at about $1,000,000. The
rumor that the property is to he
subdivided and sold is unconfirmed.
