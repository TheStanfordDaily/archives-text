# Song-Book on Sale To-morrow.
## 
### 
The Stanford song-book pub
lished annually by the student
body will be placed on sale on the
outer quadrangle opposite the Jinn
to-morrow morning at 8:15. The
hook will contain the four songs
which were awarded honorable
mention in the contest this year.
Beside these songs a great number
from last year's book will be re
printed. The verses of "After the
Game'' have been revised for this
year's contest. The Bong-book will
also contain the new spelling yell
and the best of the older yells. The
books will cost 10 cents apiece or
three for 25 cents.
