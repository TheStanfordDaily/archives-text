# Lachmund and Edwards To Go East.
## 
### 
11. H. Lachmund, '06, and P. C.
Edwards, '08, will leave next Sat
urday for Clinton, N. Y., to attend
the Delta Upsilon convention
which will be held at Hamilton
University. During their absence
of more than two weeks IT. R. Rob
erts of Palo Alto will have charge
of the Glee Club, and Miss E. E.
Mirrielees,'o6, will superintend the
publication of the Sequoia. The
staff of that magazine will have
particular duties to perform in
connection with the editing until
the return of the editor.
