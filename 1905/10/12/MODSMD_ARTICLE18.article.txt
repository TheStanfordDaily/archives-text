# Literary Society's Program.
## 
### 
The Euphronia Literary Society
will give the following program to
morrow night: Set speech, E. A.
Cunha, '06. Regular debate, "Re
solved, That the United States
should continue her present policy
of opposing the combination of
railroads": affirmative, N. P. Bry
an, '07, C. R. Crider, '07; negative,
C. M. Hadley, '06, F. L. Everson,
'07. Nestoria will postpone the
program announced for Saturday
evening one week, because of the
Freshman intercollegiate game on
that day.
