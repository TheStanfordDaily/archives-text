# Stott Will Not Play for a Month.
## 
### 
E. P. Stott, '07, who wrenched
his ankle while trying to heel a
punt in football practic# last week,
is improving slowly. The liagments
were torn from the ankle and a
blood-vessel broken, making an in
jury which will take a month to
heal. He will be able to walk to
morrow with the aid of a cane, but
will not be able to appear upon the
field till after the first of the month.
