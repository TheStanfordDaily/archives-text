# In the City.
## 
### 
The Freshman team, accompa
nied by Coach Lanagan and Assist
ant-Coach Banshach, will leave
Palo Alto on the 9:45 train to-mor
row morning. They will have
headquarters at the Palace Hotel,
San Francisco. At 11:30 lunch
will be eaten, after which the men
will don their uniforms. Manager
Barrett will take the team to the
University of California, leaving
San Francisco on the one o'clock
Southern Pacific boat.
