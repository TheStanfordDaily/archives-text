# Prize Offered for Program Design.
## 
### 
The directors of Kncina Club
met Friday night to discuss plans
for the annual "at home" which
will he given a week after the inter-
Collegiate football game. A prize
of $s'is offered for the best design
for a dance program, competition
being limited to Encina men. A
committee will soon be appointed
by President \V. E. Crawford, '06,
to have charge of the dance.
