# Basket-ball Men Sign Training Rules.
## 
### 
The members of the basket-ball
squad signed training rules last
Friday. The rules observed will
be the same its those for the font
ball men.'- The playing of the
squad, although fast, has shown
that their condition was poor, and
training rules have been signed to
remedy this defect.
A new system, of practice has
been inaugurated in order to give
the men more experience in play
ing together. Two teams will be
selected and these will play together
as first teams until the
final selection is made.
