# Date of Football Ticket Sale.
## 
### 
The date upon which the tickets
for the intercollegiate game will go
on sale has been.set on Monday, Oc
tober 80th. At the same time the
railroad tickets, giving a round
trip rate of $1.00 from San Francis
co, can he secured. Further than
this no definite arrangements for
the sale have been made, hut it will
he held at the two universities and
in San Francisco. Rooters' tickets
will cost $1.50, and seats under the
covered section of the bleachers
will sell for $2.50. The remaining
peats will cost $2.00 each, while
standing room may be had for
$1.00.
