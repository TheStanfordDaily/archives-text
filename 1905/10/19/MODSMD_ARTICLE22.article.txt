# Spalding Is Out of Danger.
## 
### 
The condition of W. 1). Spald
ing, '08, who has been confined in
the Guild Hospital with a critical
case of appendicitis, is greatly im
proved. The operation was en
tirely successful and Spalding has
been pronounced out of danger by
the doctors in charge of the case.
F. R. Lanagan, '08, is also recover
ing from an attack of appendicitis.
No operation was necessary in his
case. There are two Palo Alto
appendicitis patients in the Guild
Hospital at the present time, but
they are not in a serious enough
condition to require an operation.
