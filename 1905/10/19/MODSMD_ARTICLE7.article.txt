# Bail-Room Not for Dances Alone.
## 
### 
Although the general impression
has been that the ball room in the
new gymnasium will be used ex
clusively for dancing, Instructor
H. W.Chappel stated definitely to
day that the room will also he
used as a drill-room for the gym
nasts. It is probable that the floor
will be covered with canvas while
the gymnastic, work is in progress
in order not to injure it.
