# Roble Club to Present Play.
## 
### 
The Roble Club will present the
romantic play, "Land of Heart's
Desire," sometime early in Novem
ber. A committee is at work se
lecting the cast of six characters,
two of which have been chosen.
The play will he given either in
Roble gymnasium or in Assembly
Hall, the exact place to be decided
upon and announced in the next
few days.
