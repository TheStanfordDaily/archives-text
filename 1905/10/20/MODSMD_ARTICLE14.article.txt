# Announcements.
## 
### 
Ckrcle Franoais —Will meet at
the S. A. E. house, 6 Salvatierra
street, Monday evening, October
23d, at 8.
Chess Players—Who have not
entered the Chess Club tourna
ment should hand their names to
H. W. Strong, '06.
Debating.—Candidates for Car
not debating team must hand their
names to W. L. Blair, '06, D. D.
Sales, '06, or E. A. Cunha, '06, be
fore Tuesday noon.
Football Men. — All football
men who are no longer on the
squad are requested to return their

euits to Manager Barrett, as the re
maining squad is in need of sup
plies.
Junior Class. —The '07 Quad as
sessment is now due. Manager
Brennan will he in his office, room
14, on Tuesdays and Thursdays
from 1:15 to 2:30 for the purpose
of collecting.
Basket-Ball Men. — All men
who wish to try for the banket-ball
team should come out for practice
at once. Practice is held Monday,
Wednesday and Friday afternoons
at 4 o'clock, and Saturday morn
ings at 10, at the Encina gymnas
ium. R. H. Bookmeyer, Capt.
