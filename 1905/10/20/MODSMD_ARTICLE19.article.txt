# Sophomores to Announce Play.
## 
### 
The committee appointed by the
Sophomore class to select a play
for presentation next semester will
announce their selection Monday.
The committee have been assisted
by Professor Bassett in the selec
tion of a play. It is probable that
the play will be from the works of
the French writer, MoliGre.
