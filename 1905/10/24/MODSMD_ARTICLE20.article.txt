# STATUS OF GUILD HOSPITAL.
## Legal Controversy of Summer to Be Discussed by State Association. 
### 
Dr. W. F. Snow, professor in the
Department of Hygiene, will ad
dress the California Public Health
Association on Saturday next in
San Francisco. His subject will
be, "Sanitation at Stanford." Dur
ing the meeting the legal contro
versy, which arose this summer
over the status of the Students'
Guild Hospital, will be thoroughly
discussed. The troubles of the

hospital have attracted widespread
attention all over the state, as
there are many other hospitals
which would be affected by a de
cision against it.
