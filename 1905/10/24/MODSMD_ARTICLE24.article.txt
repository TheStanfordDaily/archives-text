# Field Being Prepared.
## 
### 
This week the ground for the
new football field is being putin
shape for the coming intercollegi
ate game. The lumber has been
removed and the soil is being thor
oughly wet down, preparatory to
harrowing it next week. The
bleachers are being painted a light
green on the outside, while the
painted section on the inside of the
field will be in red.
