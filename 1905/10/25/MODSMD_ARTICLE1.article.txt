# DEBATING CANDIDATES
## TWENTY – TWO CONTESTANTS FOR CARNOT TEAM. Two Try-outs Necessary to Pick Stanford Squad which Will Be Chosen in December. 
### 
Twenty-two debaters have sig
nified their intention of trying out
for the Carnot team. Of these four
are veterans, but the others have
not yet served on an intercollegiate
team.
This is an unusually large num
ber of candidates for a Carnot
team and makes necessary two try
outs to select the representatives
for the University. All the new
men must enter a preliminary
try-out which will be held early in
December. At this try-out the five
best speakers will be chosen to com
pete with the old debaters —D. D.
Sales,'o6. W. L. Blair, '06, C. S.
Osgood, '06, and E. A. Cunha,'o6 —
for the honor of contesting with
California for the medal.
The squads as drawn for the pre
liminary try-out follow:
First squad —G. D. Ferrell, '09,
J. F. Brennan, '07, \V. A. Camp
bell, '07, C. D. Gibson, '06, W. F.
Herron, '08, J. L. Maloy, '06.
Second squad —J. E. Campbell,
'06, R. S. Lacey, '07, F. L. Everson,
'07, F. A. Fisher, '07, .T. W. Maloy,
'07, W. C. Shelton, '08, N. P. Bryan,
'07, W. H.Tucker, '08.
These two squads may follow
their own inclination as to choos
ing questions and sides for this
preliminary debate.
