# Will Have Pictures Taken.
## 
### 
The members of the Combined
Musical Clubs will have their
photographs taken to-morrow after
noon at Franklin's studio. A bus
leaving the quad at one o'clock has
been provided to take the members
of the club to the studio. The fol
lowing men will report for the Glee
