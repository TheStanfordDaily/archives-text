# FEMALE CHORUS PICKED.
## Nine Girls Chosen to Take Part in "The Beauty Shop." 
### 
The following girls have been
asked to take a part in the chorus
for"The Beauty Shop": Miss Edna
Brown, '08, Miss Clara Vickers,
'08, Miss M. S. Gray, '08, Miss
Clarice Moise, '07, Miss G. Al
brecht, '08, Miss L. G. Sturgis, '08,
Miss K. M. Rider, '09, Miss Shirley
Hopper, '07. Miss Brown will
also sing the solo parts.
