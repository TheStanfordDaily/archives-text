# Meeting of Geological Society.
## 
### 
The Geological Society held h
meeting last night and Professor C.
D.Marx gave a very interesting talk
on '"The Relation of Geology to the
Foundation of Dams." B. Bryan,
'06, was elected to membership in
the society, and Professor Rogers
and Norman Dole, '04, were placed
upon the honorary membership
role. At a meeting of the society
earlier in the season R. Crandall,
'06, and C. B. Nims, '06, were elect
ed to the society.
