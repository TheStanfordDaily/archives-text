# Freshman Tennis Sweaters Ordered.
## 
### 
The committee on the purchase
of Freshman tennis sweaters has
had the men who are to wear them
measured and the order sent in
through M. A. Thomas, who is act
ing for Manager Barrett during the
latter's absence. All the men or
dered sweater-vests. The sweaters
are to be white with the numbers
"15)09," four inches high in red.
They will arrive about November 2d.
The Freshman representatives
were: Doubles, A. J. Gowan and
M. 0. Adams; singles, J. N. Alex
ander.
