# Song Practice To-morrow Night.
## 
### 
A song and yell practice will he
held to-morrow night in the chapel,
commencing at 7:15. The yell
leaders desire that everyone attend
the rally, as it is of the utmost im
portance that the new songs and
yells he learned thoroughly before
the intercollegiate game. There
will he no opportunity to practice
on the bleachers this week on ac
count of secret practice, so that the
practice rallies give the only chance
for organized rooting.
