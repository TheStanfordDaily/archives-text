# Prize Awarded for Program Design.
## 
### 
The committee in charge of the
program for the Encina "at home"
have awarded the prize to E. E.
Horowitz, '06. There were over
twenty designs handed in atul the
committee had some difficulty in
finally deciding upon the winner.
Horowitz is known by some clever
art work which he has done for
recent numbers of the Chaparral.
