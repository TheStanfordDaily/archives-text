# Close of Bonnheim Competition.
## 
### 
Essays for the Bonnheim prizes,
typewritten on thesis paper of
regulation size, must be handed
in to the Registrar hy Monday, No
vember Gth. The subject this year
is "The Ethics of Monopolistic Con
trol of Industry." No names should
be signed, but a fictitious name
should accompany the essay, and
the fictitious with the right name
of the author should he left in a
sealed envelope with the Registrar.
The announcement of the best five
manuscripts will he made by No
vember 20th. The prizes, of twenty
dollars each, are given by Mr. Al
bert Bonnheim.
