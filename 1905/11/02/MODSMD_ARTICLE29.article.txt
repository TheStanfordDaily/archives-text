# Stanford Entomologist on Campus.

### 
Delos Van Dine, ex-'Ol, ento
mologist of the United States ex
periment station at Honolulu, is
visiting at the University for a few
days. Under a special act and ap
propriation of the Hawaiian legis
lature, he is conducting a fight

Islands. Recently, with the assist
