# Plug Ugly Tickets on Sale To-morrow
## 
### 
Tickets for the Junior Plug Ugly
to-<morrow evening will be placed
on sale to-morrow morning front 9
to 12 in the regular ticket dispens

ary, under the Memorial Arch.
One price of twenty-five cents will
be charged, and no seats will be re
served.
The management has decided to
place the tickets on sale to morrow
morning in order to avoid needless
confusion at the box office to-mor
row.
