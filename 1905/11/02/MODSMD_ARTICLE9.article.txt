# U.C. Rooters May Come via San Jose.
## 
### 
If 500 names can be obtained the
Southern Pacific Company has of
ferred to provide a special train
from Oakland to Palo Alto via San
Jose. It is claimed that the trip
by way of San Jose can be made as
quickly as by crossing the bay and
coining down the Coast line.
The executive committee of the
associated students have sent to
ask President Wheeler to discon
tinue classes on the morning of the
big game in order to allow all who
wish to see the contest.
