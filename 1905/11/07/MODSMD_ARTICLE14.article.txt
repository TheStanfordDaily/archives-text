# DR. JORDAN LEAVES.
## Went East This Afternoon by Way of Ogden and Salt Lake 
### 
President Jordan left for the
East this afternoon to attend the
meeting of the trustees of the Car
negie fund in New York.
He will travel by way of the
Southern Pacific and will stop over
at Ogden and Salt Lake City,
where addresses will he made.
In Dr. Jordan's absence Professor
Kellogg will take charge of his
classes in Bionomics.
