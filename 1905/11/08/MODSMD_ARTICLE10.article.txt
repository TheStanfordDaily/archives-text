# SEATS SELL SLOWLY.
## Many Tickets to "Beauty Shop" Remain to Be Disposed of Before Saturday. 
### 
There are still some 800 tickets
to the football show on hand, which
can be purchased either at the Uni
versity Drug Store in Palo Alto, or
at the campus Bookstore.
The first day's sale fell far short
of expectations, but 491 seats being
disposed of. The rest of the tickets
will be kept on sale at the places
mentioned until (he day of the
game. Any left on the eleventh
will be on sale in the evening at
Assembly Hall.
