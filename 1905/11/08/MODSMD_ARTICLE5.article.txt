# Museum to Have Special Hours.
## 
### 
Special arrangements have been
made by the curator of the Univer
sity Museum to facilitate handling
the great numbers of visitors who
will visit the building during the
day of the big football game. The
doors will be open an hour earlier
than usual, thus making the hours
for the day from 9 o'clock in the
morning until 5 o'clock in the after
noon. A number of student guides
have been employed who will be
stationed in the different exhibition
rooms to give instructions and in
formation to the visitors.
