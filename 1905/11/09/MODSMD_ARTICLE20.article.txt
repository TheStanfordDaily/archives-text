# Arrange Guard for Campus.
## 
### 
The men of the Freshman claps
met to-day to provide for guarding
the campus to-morrow night before
the big game. It was proposed to
divide the class into two shifts,
from 7 till 1 o'clock, and from 1
till 6 o'clock Saturday morning.
For this purpose the men counted
numbers as they sat, 115 being
present. President Morgan ap
pointed the men from 1 to 50 in
clusive as the first shift and the re
mainder as the second. A commit
tee of E. Navlor, chairman, F. B.
Mayers and C. H. Schmuesen was
appointed to have full charge of
arrangements.
