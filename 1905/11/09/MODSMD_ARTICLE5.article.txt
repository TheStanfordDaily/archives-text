# TALK ON MAGAZINE WORK.
## Assistant Editor of the "American Illustrated Magazine" to Address English Club. 
### 
The next meeting of the English
Club will be held at the Kappa
Alpha Theta house Monday eve
ning at 8. William Morrow of
New York, assistant editor of the
American Illustrated Magazine, will
talk on magazine work. Morrow
is a Harvard graduate with the
class of 1900. While in college he
was editor of the Harvard Monthly.
After graduation he was connected
with McClure's Magazine, and later
was appointed assistant editor of
the American, formerly Frank Les
lie's Monthly.
