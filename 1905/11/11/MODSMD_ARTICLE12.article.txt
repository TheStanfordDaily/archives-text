# DIRECTIONS TO GAME
## EXPLANATION OF MAP SHOWING PRINCIPAL POINTS OF INTEREST. How to Reach the Athletic Field. General Information of Interest to Visitors. 
### 
THE students of Stanford Univer
sity are to-day confronted with
the difficult problem of entertaining

many thousands of visitors. Some
of these are familiar with the Univer
sity and its surroundings, but the
majority, no doubt, are to-day en
joying their first view of the Univer
sity campus, and it is primarily for
their convenience that this article is
intended, and secondarily to aid all
in going to and from the game.
