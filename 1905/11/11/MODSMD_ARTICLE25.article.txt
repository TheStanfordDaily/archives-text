# TRAINER CHRISTIE'S RECORD.
## University of California Athletes Are Conditioned by an Experienced Man. 
### 
Walter Christie has trained the
University of California athletes for
the past five years and has given en
tire satisfaction. Christie made his
reputation in the East, first at Colum
bia where he did good work with
track athletes, and later when he
went to Princeton. There he re
mained for four years and was suc
cessful both in training the football
and baseball squads, as well as the
track men. Christie came to Cali
fornia from Annapolis, where he
trained the football team.
