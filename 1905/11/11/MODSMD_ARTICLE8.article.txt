# HEAD-COACH LANAGAN.
## Graduate of Stanford Who Has Coached the Varsity for Last Three Seasons. 
### 
Head-Coach Lanagan graduated
from Stanford in 1900. As an un
dergraduate he was best known as a
baseball player, for he pitched sev
eral years, winning his "S " in his
sophomore year. Lanagan played
full-back on his Freshman team,
which defeated California 5-0. He
has coached Stanford since 1903.
