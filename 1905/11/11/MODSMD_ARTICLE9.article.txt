# ASSISTANT-COACH BANSBACH.
## Prominent and Successful Foot-ball Player Who Has Assisted Head-Coach Lanagan. 
### 
Assistant - Coach Bansbach, '05,
has been closely connected with Stan
ford's football interests for the past
five years. He captained the win
ning 1904 Freshman team, and played
on the Varsity in his freshman, jun
ior, senior and succeeding year teams.
He was captain of the Varsity in his
senior year.
