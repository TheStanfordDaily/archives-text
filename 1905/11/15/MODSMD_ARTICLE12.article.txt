# Auditing Committee Appointed.
## 
### 
The Boat Club held a short meet
ing recently for the purpose of
appointing an auditing committee
to look over the financial accounts
of the committee who had charge
of the farce, "All Tangled Up."
Owing to the fact that E. V. Hen
ley, '06, the president of the club,
was a member of the farce commit
tee, 11. A. Carter, '08, acted as tem
porary chairman of the meeting
last night. He appointed the fol
lowing committee to audit the ac
counts of the farce: J. D. Phelps,
'07, chairman, A. Rosenfeld, '07,
and E. G. Schaupp, '08.
