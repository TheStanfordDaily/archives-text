# DATE OF ROBLE DANCE.
## Committees Appointed by President of Club to Take Charge of the Affair. 
### 
At the last meeting of the Roble
Club the members decided to hold
their semester dance in the Roble
gymnasium, Saturday evening, De
cember 9th. Miss Shirley Hyatt,
'08, president of the Roble Club,
has appointed the following heads
for the various committees in
charge of the affair:
Invitation, Miss Helen Sprague,
'06; decoration, Miss Josephine
Herron, 'OG, and Miss Edith Jar
man,'o7; reception, Miss Cecelia
Atherton, '04; patroness, Miss Alice
Nagel, '07.
