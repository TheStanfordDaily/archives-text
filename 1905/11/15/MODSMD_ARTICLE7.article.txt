# Measured for Sweaters.
## 
### 
The thirteen men who played in
the game Saturday against Cali
fornia were measured for Varsity
sweaters to-day. The men who
were on the side-lines were also
measured for second team sweaters.
Most of the players ordered the
new light sweater with the Varsity
"S" interwoven. The sweaters will
will arrive early next week. The
Athletic Committee will meet
Thursday night for the purpose of
formally voting the sweaters.
