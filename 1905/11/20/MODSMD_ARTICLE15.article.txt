# English Club to Give Pantomime.
## 
### 
The English Club will hold its
annual evening of stunts next Wed
nesday at the Delta Gamma house.
Selections from Guy Wetmore Car
ryPs "Grimm Tales Made Gay"
will he acted in pantomime by the
members of the club. Those who
will make up the cast are Miss S.
W. Carpenter, '06, Miss A. S.
Hayes, '06, Miss N. K. Dunn, '06,
E. R. May, '06, P. C. Edwards, '06,
and L. W. Cutler, '06.
