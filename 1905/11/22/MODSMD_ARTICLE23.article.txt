# Valuable Prizes for Essays.
## 
### 
A California man who desires
his name withheld has offered
prizes for the two best essays on
"Moral Training in the Public
Schools." The first prize is $500
cash, and the second prize $300.
The essays are to be between six
thousand and twelve thousand

words in length and are to be
handed in to the committee, com
posed of Rev. Charles R. Brown of
Oakland, chairman, President
David Starr Jordan, and Professor
F. B. Dresser of the University of
California, by June 1, 1906. The
two prize essays will be published
and distributed throughout the
state.
