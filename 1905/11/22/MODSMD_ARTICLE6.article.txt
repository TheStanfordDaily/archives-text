# WILL ELECT CAPTAIN
## FOOTBALL MEN TO CHOOSE LEADER SATURDAY NIGHT. Captain Chalmers to Give a Dinner to Team, at Which His Successor Will Be Elected. 
### 
Next season's football captain
will he elected at a meeting of the
members of this year's team to be
held in the Phi Delta Theta house
next Saturday night. Captain
Chalmers will give a dinner to the
thirteen men who played against
California, and in the course of the
evening his successor will be chosen.
Coach Lanagan is still in the
East and will not return in time to
he present at the banquet, but
Assistant-Coach Bansbach will
probably be there. After Lana
gan's return the team will hold a
big banquet in San Francisco as a
fitting conclusion to one of the
most successful years in the foot
ball history of Stanford.
At the opening of this season
there were only three veterans of
the '04 Varßity in college: A. J.
Chalmers, '07, R. A. Thompson,
'05, and E. P. Stott, '07. Chalmers
was chosen as captain of this year's
team, and Thompson is no longer
eligible as this was his fourth year
on the Varsity. This leaves E. P.
Stott, '07, as the only man now in
college who has won his Varsity
"S" for more than one season, with
the exception of Captain Chalmers.
Whoever wins the honor will un
doubtedly be a member of the class
of 1907, so that that class will have
the special distinction of number
ing among its members two Stan
ford Varsity captains.
The men who will elect the cap
tain are A. J. Chalmers, '07, R. A.
Thompson, '05, E. P. Stott, '07, G.
E. Dole, '07, T. Vandervoort, '08,
W. Koerner, '08, J. N. Stanford,
'06, W. E. Lyons, '06, H. S. Hor
ton, '08, D. P. Crawford, '09, P. T.
Frizzell, '06, A. Molfino, '07, and
D. Slusher, '07.
