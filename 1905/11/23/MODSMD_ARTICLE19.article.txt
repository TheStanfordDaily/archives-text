# Posters for Glee Club Issued.
## 
### 
Advertising matter costing $278
has been printed by order of Man
ager Barrett and will be sent to the
towns in which the Musical Clubs
will appear on the holiday trip.
The advertising includes large pos
ters for use on the bill-boards,
smaller card-board posters and
hand-bills. The large bills are
printed in red and black. Tho
card-board posters resemble those
issued in past years, with photo
graphs of the Glee and Mandolin
Clubs. Smaller cuts of the stunt
men, E. R. May, '06, and R. S.
Kellogg, '07, and of L. Langstroth,
*08, also appear on the poster. The
lettering is in red.
