# SIGMA NU VS. KAPPA SIGMA.
## Fraternities Will Play Football on New Athletic Field Tomorrow Afternoon. 
### 
Sigma Nu and Kappa Sigma
will play a game of football on the
new athletic field to-morrow after
noon at 1 o'clock. Two halves of
ten minutes each will be played.
The game was scheduled several
weeks ago, and the teams have been
practicing formations since that
time.
