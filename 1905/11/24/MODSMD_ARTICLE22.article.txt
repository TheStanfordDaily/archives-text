# J. S. Burcham, '02, Drowned.
## 
### 
Word has been received from
Yes Bay, Alaska, that J. S. Burch
am, '02, was drowned on November
12th. He was in the employ of
the United States Bureau of Fish
eries, and was with the U. S. S.
Albatross as an expert on salmon
and other fishes. Burcham gradu
ated in January, 1902, and took
his master's degree in May, 1903.
He is a brother of J. T. Burcham,
'97, who was formerly an instructor
in the Law department, and also of
W. D. Burcham, '09. He was a
member of Sigma Xi.
