# Death of Pioneer Alumnus.
## 
### 
George Draper Stratton, '95, died
Monday night in Oakland. Strat
ton graduated from the Civil En
gineering department, and since
then has been in the employ of the
Southern Pacific Company. While
in college he was prominent in
musical circles, and also belonged
to the Civil Engineering Club. He
was a member of the Delta Tau
Delta fraternitv.
