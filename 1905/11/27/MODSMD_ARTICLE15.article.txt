# "Blue and Gold" Offers Prizes.
## 
### 
The management of the 1907
Blue and Gold has offered prizes for
the best cartoon, poem, story and
photograph. A free trip to Yo
semite has been offered for the best
cartoon, $20 worth of books for the
best poem, the same prize for the
best story, and $10 worth of photo
graph supplies for the best picture.
The josh department is also to be
featured,and much attention is now
being given to that work.
