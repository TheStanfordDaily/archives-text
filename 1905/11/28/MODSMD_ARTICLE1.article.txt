# EDITOR IS NOMINATED
## B.S. ALLEN, '06, MANAGING EDITOR, IS NAMED. Election Will Be Held on the Quad a Week from To-morrow, December 6th. 
### 
At a meeting of the Associated
Students this afternoon, B. S. Allen,
'06, waß nominated for editor-in
chief of the Daily Palo Alto next
semester by L. R. Weinmann, '06.
Allen is at present managing edi
tor of the Daily Palo Alto. He
has been on the staff since his
freshman year.
The one nomination for editor
was the only business that took
place at the meeting this afternoon,
no other student body office coming
up for nominations till next spring.
The election will take place a week
from to-morrow on the quad.
