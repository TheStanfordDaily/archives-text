# Offer Special Thanksgiving Rates.
## 
### 
Word was received late this af
ternoon that the Southern Pacific
Company has offered a rate of one
fare and one-third round trip for
students making the Thanksgiving
trip home. To secure this rate,
certificates from the Registrar's
office must be presented at the de
pot in Palo Alto. The tickets are
good returning until December 4th.
