# MAY RETURN TO-NIGHT.
## Head Football Coach Expected to Arrive in San Francisco This Evening from East. 
### 
Head-Coach Lanagan, '00, is ex
pected to return from the East to
night. During hie absence of about
three weeks lie has witnessed three
important athletic events: the
Yale - Princeton, Harvard - Yale,
and Michigan - Chicago football
games. While in Cambridge, Mass.,
Lanagan was the guest of W. T.
Reid, Jr., Harvard's head coach.
Last Friday he left Chicago for
Denver, where he expected to re
main for a day. He will probably
reach San Francisco this evening
or to-morrow morning.
