# Announcements.
## 
### 
Library Club —Meets at 4:30
Tuesday in Economics seminary
room.
New Books. —American Insects,
by Kellogg, has been received at
the University Eookstore.
Combined Musical Clubs. —
Business meeting in room 62 on
Tuesday, December sth, at 7:30.
H. Park, Pres.
Unitarian Church—Of Palo
Alto will give a fair at Fraternity
Hall, Saturday, December 9th. The
sale is to begin at 3, and a supper
will be served at 6 o'clock.
"Quad" Assessment — May be
paid to Manager J. F. Brennan in
room 14 on Tuesdays and Thurs
days from 1:15 to 2:30, or to C. R.
Crider in room 69 Encina. Assess
ment must be paid not later than
December 15th.
Applied Mathematics —Exam-
inations for the removal of condi
tions or incompletes in any course
in Applied Mathematics will be
held on Friday, December Bth, at 2
in room 40. Students desiring to
take such examinations should no
tify instructors in advance.
Philological Association—Will
meet December 7th, at 2:30, in
room 31. Papers: Mr. E. K. Put
nam, "Aftermath Notes on the
Unique Havelock Manuscript";
Professor O. M. Johnston, "The
Story of the Blue Bird and the Lay
of Yonec." C. Searles, Sec.
