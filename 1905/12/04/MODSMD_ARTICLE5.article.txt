# Historians Hold Annual Session.
## 
### 
The second annual meeting of
the Pacific Coast Branch of the
American Historical Association
was held in the lecture hall of the
Mechanics' Institute, San Fran
cisco, on Friday and Saturday,
December Ist and 2d. Among
those who read papers were P. J.
Treat and Professors E. D. Adams
and C. A. Duniway of the Stan
ford History department.
Of the officers elected, Horace
Davis of San Francisco, a member
of the Board of Trustees of the
University, was chosen president;
Professor Max Farrand was elected
secretary-treasurer, and Professor
C. A. Duniway was elected a mem
ber of the executive committee.
