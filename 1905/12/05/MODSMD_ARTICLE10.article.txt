# FIRST GAME FRIDAY EVENING.
## Basket-Ball Men Will Meet California College of Photography Team. 
### 
The basket-ball men have com
pleted arrangements for games with
the California College of Photog
raphy and the Stockton Young
Men's Christian Association. The
game with the College of Photog
raphy will take place in the En
cina gymnasium next Friday eve
ning at eight o'clock. A week from
Friday a squad of six men will
be taken to Stockton to play the
Young Men's Christian Association
there.
The Wheaton College of Illinois
has written to Manager Edwards
asking him for a game between the
dates of January Ist and 10th. As
they wish a large cash guarantee
and entertainment for ten men it
will be impossible to arrange games
with them.
