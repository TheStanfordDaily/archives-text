# Interurban Buys Bight of Way.
## 
### 
A right of way through the town
of Mayfield haß been sold by the
Co-operative Land and Trust Com
pany to the Los Gatos Interurban
Electric Railway. The right of
way, commencing at the Southern
Pacific Company's depot, runs
southward parallel with the rail
road tracks for about half a mile.
It then turns westward and runs
for half a mile, crossing the county
road and turning south again. It
is understood that the entire right
of way from Mayfield to Los Gatos
is secured and that work on the
road will be commenced as soon as
possible.
