# Second Recital of Mrs. Noyes.
## 
### 
Friday evening, December Bth,
Mrs. Noyes will hold her second
musical recital. She will be as
sisted by Virgil Langstroth, '08,
celloist. The following program
will be rendered:
Arabesque Debussy
Consolation Arensky
Tend re Aven Schiitt
Bouree Fantasque Chabrier
Piano Son ate Grieg
Tickets are now on sale at the
Bookstore and at the University
Pharmacy.
