# Place for Football Banquet Changed.
## 
### 
Owing to the closing of Larkin's
restaurant in Palo Alto, the annual
football banquet which was to have
been held there next Friday night
will in all probability take place
in San Jose. Manager Barrett is
making arrangements with the
Hotel Vendome, and unless unfore

seen difficulties arise, the banquet
will be held there.
