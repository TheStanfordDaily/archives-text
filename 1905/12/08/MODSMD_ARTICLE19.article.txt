# Announcements.
## 
### 
Quad Assessments—Must be paid
by December 15th.
J. F. Brennan, '07.
Civil Engineering Society —
Will meet Monday evening, De
cember 11th, at o'clock in room -37
Encina. G. S. Strout, Pres.
Y. M. C. A. Labor Bureau. —
Wanted Japanese student for posi
tion in Palo Alto, light cooking
and housework for board, room,
and $5 per month.
Unitarian Church—Of Palo
Alto will give a fair at Fraternity
Hall, Saturday, December 9th. The
sale is to begin at 3, and a supper
will be served at 6 o'clock.
Faculty Women's Club. — The
next regular meeting will be held
at the home of Mrs. David Starr
Jordan, December 11th, at 3
o'clock. All members are urged to
be present.
Quad Assessments—Must be paid
by December 15th.
