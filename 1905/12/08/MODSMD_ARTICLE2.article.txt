# CIVIL ENGINEERS WILL MEET.
## First Meeting of Newly Formed Society Will Be Held Monday Evening. 
### 
The first regular meeting of the
Associated Civil Engineers will he
held Monday evening in room 37,
Encina Hall. All students who
sign the roll of membership before
the meeting will be considered
charter members.
Professors C. D. Marx and C. B.
Wing will deliver informal ad
dresses.
Juniors and Seniors are eligible
to the society, but Sophomore Civil
Engineering majors will be ad
mitted to associate membership.
