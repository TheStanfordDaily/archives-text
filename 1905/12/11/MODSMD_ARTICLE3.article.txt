# CARNOT PRELIMINARY
## FIVE NEW DEBATERS CHOSEN FOR FINAL TRY-OUT. Brennan, Gibson, Herron, Campbell, and Maloy Will Compete with Veterans for Places. 
### 
J. F. Brennan, '07, I. D. Gibson,
'06, W. F. Herron, '08, W. A.
Campbell, '07, and J. W. Maloy,
'07, were chosen Friday night to
enter the try-out for the Carnot de
bating team next semester. Besides
these above mentioned men D. D.
Sales, C. S. Osgood, '06, W. L.
Blair, '06, and E. A. Cunha, '06,
are eligible to try for the team, and
from these four veterans and the
five new men will be chosen the
three men and the alternate who
will uphold Stanford's interests in
the contest for the medal next se
mester.
There were two full squads in
the preliminary try-out the other
night, and Reverend D. C. Gardner,
Professor A. M. Cathcart and Pro
fessor A. G. Newcomer, who acted
as judges, made their selection
from a dozen candidates.
