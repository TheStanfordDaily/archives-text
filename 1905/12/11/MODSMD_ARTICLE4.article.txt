# RUSSIAN EXILE TO LECTURE.
## Lieutenant Colonel A.M. Lochwitzky Will Tell of Graft in Russia. 
### 
Lieutenant Colonel A. M. Loch
witzky, a Russian political exile
now living in San Francisco, will
lecture in the chapel on the eve
ning of December 13th at 8 o'clock.
His subject will be "The Dead Past
of Russia: Graft and Injustice the
Causes of the Present Confusion."
Colonel Lochwitzky will speak from
his personal experiences. It will
be recalled that Colonel Lochwit
zky lectured before the University
audience last winter on his exper
iences as a political prisoner in the
Russian penal colony on the Is
land of Saghalien. All members of
the University are invited to be
present.
