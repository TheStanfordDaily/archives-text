# Memorial Organ Recital.
## 
### 
To-morrow afternoon the organ
recital program will be rendered in
memoriam to Mr. Henry Holmes,
one of the worlds noted violinists,
who died on December 9th. Mr.
Holmes played frequently in the
Church with Dr. Blodgett last year
and was scheduled to play here at
times this year.
The following program will be
given:
Marche Solennelle (Ervica Sym
phony) Beethoven
Adagio from B flat, Trio, op. 97.
Beethoven
Canzonetta from Sonata in B flat
Mendelssohn
Song: Eu bis die Ruh .... Schubert
