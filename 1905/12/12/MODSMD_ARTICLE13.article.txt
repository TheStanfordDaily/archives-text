# U.C. May Change Method of Election.
## 
### 
The matter of electing the gradu
ate athletic manager at the Uni
versity of California has aroused
no little agitation at that insititu
tion. A movement is on foot to
have the manager apointed by the
athletic committee. It is claimed
that this form will dispense with
the politics now involved in the
election to the office by the student
body. A final decision will be
reached this evening at a meeting
of the associated students.
