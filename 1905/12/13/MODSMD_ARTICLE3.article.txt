# Zoology Club Will Meet Saturday.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Zoology Club
has been called for Saturday eve
ning, December 16th, at the resi
dence of Dr. C. H. Gilbert at 433
Melville avenue, Palo Alto. R. W.
Doane, '96, an assistant in the De
partment of Entomology, will talk
on "The Work of the Key Port,
Washington, Experiment Station."
Mr. Doane was connected with the
entomological work of the North
west after his graduation, being
professor of entomology at the
Washington Agricultural College,
and later carrying on experimental
work around Puget Sound. The
meeting is called for 8 p.m.
