# Will Banquet in San Jose.
## 
### 
The football banquet for the
thirteen men who played in the
intercollegiate game this year will
be held Saturday night at the Ven
dome Hotel in San Jose. The team
will leave the Stanford Inn in a
bus at 6:15, and take the 6:36 train
for San Jose. The banquet will be
held at 7:30.
The men on the team will take
their football suits and Varsity
sweaters to Franklin's studio Sat
urday evening, and have the team
picture taken on Sunday morning
after returning from San Jose on
the 11:13 train.
Manager Barrett, Trainer Moul
ton, and Coaches Lanagan and
Bansbach will also be at the ban
quet.
