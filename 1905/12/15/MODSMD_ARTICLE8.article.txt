# Los Angeles Stanford Club Elects.
## 
### 
At the recent annual meeting of
the Stanford Club of Los Angeles
the following officers were elected:
President, S. B. Osborne, '96; secre
tary-treasurer, A. J. Copp, Jr., '02.
The officers retiring from the re
spective offices are A. J. Copp, Jr.,
'02, and G. \V. Dryer, '02.
