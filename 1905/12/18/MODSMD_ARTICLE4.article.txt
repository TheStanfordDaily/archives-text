# Santa Fe Will Honor Tickets.
## 
### 
Arrangements have been con
cluded by the Southern Pacific
with the Santa Fe whereby students
living in San Diego will get the
one and a third rate offered during
the holidays all the way from Palo
Alto to their home town. The
Santa Fe between Los Angeles and
San Diego will honor the ticket
sold at Palo Alto over the South
ern Pacific and will give the one
third rate on the return trip.
