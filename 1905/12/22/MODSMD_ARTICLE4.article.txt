# CHESS CLUB IS CHOSEN.
## Final Try-out Is Held and Fifteen Men Are Selected. 
### 
The final try-out for the Chess
Club has been held, and besides the
Varsity players of last year, the
following men have qualified for
membership in the club : C. E.
Spaulding, '06, J. F. Cassell, '07,
T. T. Bennett, '08, C. I. Chandler,
'08, T. R. W. F. Figg-Hoblyn, '08,
E. Billman, '09, E. H. Richardson,
'09, S. B. Show, '09, and F. R.
Tower, '09.
Of last year's Varsity chess team
the following men have re-entered
the club this semester- R.- A.
Fuller, '06, L. Newland, '08, G. E.
Dole, '07, M. M. Stearns, '06, F. E.
LJlrich, '06, and H. W. Strong, '06.
