# EDITORS AT CALIFORNIA.
## New Men Elected to Fill Positions for Next Semester. 
### 
At the University of California
new editors and managers have
been elected for two of the univer
sity publications, the Californian
and Occident. Sam Hellman, '06,
forthepast six months managing
editor of the Californian, was elect
ed to the editorship. Hellman is a
graduate of the Lowell High School,
and was editor-in-chief of the school
paper in his senior year. He has
been closely connected with literary
and journalistic work during his
college career. J. Rhea, '07, was
made manager of the paper. The
board of directors of the Occident
Magazine elected E. Blackman,'o6,
to the editorship.
