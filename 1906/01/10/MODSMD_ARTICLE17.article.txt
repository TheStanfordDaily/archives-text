# No Change in Bionomics Course.
## 
### 
In the new edition of the Sched
ule, Bionomics 6 is entered an a two
hour lecture course for which one
hour's credit will be given. Dr.
Jordan and Professor Kellogg have
decided that this arrangement will
not give them sufficient time to
cover the work in the course. It
will, therefore, he continued as a
three hour lecture course for which
two hours of University credit will
be given. The class will be held
in the chapel at 10:15, on Monday,
Wednesday, and Friday.
