# OREGON TOURISTS COMING.
## Business Men's Excursion of Northern State Will Visit the University. 
### 
On next Wednesday morning,
January 17th, an excursion party
composed of the business men of
Oregon will visit the campus on
their tour of Southern California.
There will be at least one hundred
people in the party, the excursion
being under the auspices of the
Oregon Development League.
The party will be carried in a
special train, and in this section of
the state will make no other stops
except at San Francisco and San
Jose. The schedule allows the
tourists two hours and a half on
the campus, from 10 to 12:30.
