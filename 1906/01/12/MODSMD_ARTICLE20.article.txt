# Meeting of the Prohibition League.
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of the
Prohibition League to-morrow eve
ning, Saturday, January 13th, at
8 o'clock. The program will be as
follows: "Saloon Conditions in Eu
rope," by Miss M. F. Balcomb, '06;
"The South Carolina Dispensary
System," by B. E. Long, '06: "Sa
loon Conditions in Los Angeles,"
by W. O. Stuntz, '07; "How the
Gothenburg System Should Be
Modified to Fit Conditions in Los
Angeles," by H. W. Stager, '02.
The installation of officers will also
take place.
