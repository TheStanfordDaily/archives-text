# Bookstore Announces Schedule.
## 
### 
The following schedule of de
posits on books ordered on or after
January 9, 1900, has been an
nounced by the University Book
store: On books costing one dollar
or less, twenty-five cents; one to
three dollars, fifty cents; three to
five dollars, one dollar; five to
seven dollars, one dollar and a
half; seven to ten dollars, two dol
lars.
