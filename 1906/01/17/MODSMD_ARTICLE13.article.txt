# Assistants in University Library.
## 
### 
Some recent changes have been
made in the corps of assistants in
the University Library. G. G.
Altnow, '06, and F. R. Lanagan,
'08, have retired. The new assist
ants who have taken their places
are G. E. Dole, '07, and E. E.
Young, '09. Loan-Clerk Goodwin
now has charge of the desk work.
