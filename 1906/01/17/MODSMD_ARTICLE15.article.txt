# To Measure Baseball Men for Suits.
## 
### 
Manager Barrett desires all the
members of last year's Varsity nine
who are new in college to report at
his office to-morrow afternoon at 1
to be measured for baseball suits.
The men are: D. D. Sales, '06, E.
P. Stott, '07, G. J. Presley, '07, J.
H. Bell, '08, K. L. Fenton, '08, A.
J. Chalmers, '06, and E. G. Dud
ley, '06.
