# Meeting of Euphronia.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Euphronia De
bating Society will be held Friday
evening, January 19th. The pro
gram which has been announced
contains two set speeches, two reg
ular debates, and an impromptu
debate. The first set speech will
be given by D. D. Sales, '06, and
the second by E. H. Cunha, '06.
In the first debate, "Resolved, That
the Franco-English entente should
be eolidified into a formal defen
sive alliance," W. L. Blair, '06, and
E. A. Cunha, '06, will take the af
firmative, and D. D. Sales, '06, and
J. F. Brennan, '07, the negative.
The subject of the regular debate is
"Resolved, That the army canteen
should be restored"; Guy Knupp,
'07, and C. M. Hadley, '06, will
take the affirmative, and C. R. Cri
der, '07, and P. B. Smith, '06, will
uphold the negative.
