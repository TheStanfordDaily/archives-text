# Oregon May Shorten Football Trips.
## 
### 
President P. L. Campbell of the
University of Oregon has expressed
the opinion that the athletic coun
cil will make a ruling permitting
not more than three days' absence
from the university at one time for
any athletic team. There is a pos
sibility that the football team will
make alternate trips to California
and Seattle every other year, play
ing a game with one of the Cali
fornia universities one year and
the next with the University of
Washington.
