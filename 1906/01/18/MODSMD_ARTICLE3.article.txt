# Sophomore Class Meeting Monday.
## 
### 
The Sophomore class will meet
Monday afternoon at 1:15 for the
purpose of nominating officers for
the current semester. The election
will be held in the customary man
ner one week later on the quad.
Reports of officers will also be
heard, and the play committee will
report what progress has been made
on the class comedy, which will be
produced on February 9th. Plans
for a thorough advertisement of
the show will be considered and an
effort made to arouse general inter
est in it.
