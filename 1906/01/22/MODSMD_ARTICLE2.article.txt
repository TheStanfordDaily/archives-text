# Encina Dance to Be Held.
## 
### 
At a meeting of Encina Club in
the club-room Friday night, Feb
ruary 23d was set as the date for
the Encina "at home." This will
probably be the last dance which
men in training will be allowed to
attend. It has usually been the
custom to hold this dance early
so as to allow the athletes to at

tend, and the same plan will be
followed this semester. The com
mittees in charge will be appointed
within a few days.
