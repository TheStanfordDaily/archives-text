# CANDIDATES SIGN UP
## FIFTY-FIVE OARSMEN SIGN TRAINING RULES. Twenty-nine Freshmen to Try for Class Crew.—Work on the Lake Begins Thursday. 
### 
Up to the present time fifty-five
men have signed up for the Varsity
and Freshman crews. More can
didates are continually appearing
for work, however, and it will be
impossible to obtain the final list
until the end of the week. Of the
present candidates, twenty-nine are
Freshmen.
Coach "Dan" Murphy, with the
help of the boating men, has put
the two barges in good shape for
active work, and the oarsmen will
begin work with the skulls next
Thursday.
