# Will Give Course in Acts of Apostles.
## 
### 
Dean Hodges, president of the
Theological Seminary at Cam
bridge, Mass., delivered his first
lecture in the course, the Acts of
the Apostles, to-day at 1:30 in
the chapel. This is a special course
consisting of two lectures each week
with one hour credit. It will run
fourteen weeks, ending the middle
of April.
