# Will Be Guest at Church Smoker.
## 
### 
Dean Hodges, special preacher in
Memorial Church for several
months, and Reverend D. M. Brook
man, rector of All Saints' Church,
Palo Alto, will be the guests of
honor at a smoker to be given by
the Churchmen's Club at the resi
dence of J. M. Price, 543 Bryant
street, Palo Alto, on next Friday
evening.
