Kappa Alpha Theta. — At home,
Saturday evening, January 27th.
Sophomore Election —Will be
held on the quad Monday, Jan
uary 29th, 11:15 to 2.
Withdrawal. —I hereby resign
aB president of the Freshman class.
C. E. Naylor.
Resignation.— I hereby resign
the vice-presidency of the Fresh
man class. :Ci Neymann.
Graduate Club —Will meet at
Dr. Jordan's residence next Friday
night, January 26th, at 8 o'clock.
Y. M. C. A. Labor Bureau. —
Three positions in Palo Alto avail
abl, one for board and two for cash.
Y. M. C. A. —Japanese student
with a little experience available
for general housework position.
Y. M. C. A. —An experienced
Japanese desires to obtain work for
two or three hours eve'ry evening.
Intercollegiate Debate. —The
entry list for the intercollegiate de
bate try-out will be closed to-mor
row at noon/ v *
Prohibition League—President
Jordan will address the meeting in
the chapel, Saturday evening, Jan
uary 27th, at 8.
Woman's League. — Executive
committee will meet at 1:15 Friday
in room 13.
Carolyn Edwards, '06.
Withdrawal. — I hereby with
draw my name as candidate for
treasurer of the Sophomore class.
D. H. Ferry.
Tennis. — All women interested
in tennis will meet in the chapel
Friday, January 26th, at 1:10.
E. Hayvvards, '06, Mgr.
Syllabus in "Teaching of
Christ." —A few additional copies
have been made up, and may be
obtained at the University Press
office.
Visitors in Philosophy—Mem
bers of the faculty excepted, will
please enter the lecture room at the
rear door and occupy seats in the
last five rows only.
French Play—lf the special
train is to be run from San
Jose after the play, notice to that
effect will appear in Friday's issue
of the Daily Palo Alto.
J. E. Matzke.
Program Design.— A prize of
$5 has been offered for the best
program design for the Encina
"At Home" to be given on Febru
ary 23d. All designs must be sub
mitted on or before February 7th,
to M. Oppenheim, room 179 En
cina.
"Quad" Assessment. — Manager
J. F. Brennan will receive Quad
assessments in room 14, Tuesdays
and Thursdays from 1:30 to 2:30.
C. 11. Crider will also receive as
sessments in room 69 Encina Hall.
This last opportunity for Juniors
to pay their assessment will be
open three weeks.
