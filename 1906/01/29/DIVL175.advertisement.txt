SPRING'S
We announce for fall and winter the greatest line of
fashionable suits and overcoats yet shown. Sole
agents H. S. & M. clothing.
COLONIST RATES
TO CALIFORNIA
Will be in cffect every day, Feb. 15 to April 7, and
Sept. 15 to Oct. 3 It 1906, from all points in the Ea.st
and South.
Following are a few :
Chicago $33.00 Kansas City and Leavenworth $25.00
St. Louis 80.00 St. Joseph aud Atchison 25 (H)
Galveston 26.45 Council Bluffs and Omaha 25 00
Dallas, Houston and Ft. Worth 25.00
Nearly all points in Kansas, Oklahoma and Indian Ty. 25.00
Colonist rates are also in effect from all points east of
Chicago and south of the Ohio river.
Santa Fe Agents in California will take deposits of monej to cover price of tickets,
from which these rates are in effect.
California illustrated literature mailed free to any
address or may be had from Santa Fe representatives.
TOURIST SLEEPING CAR LINES
Colonist tickets are honored in tourist sleeping cars
which run via the Santa Fe to Los Angeles and San Francisco.
H. R. STERNE, Gen. Aqt., 27 S. First St.
San Jose
