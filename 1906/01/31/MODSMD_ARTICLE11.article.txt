# Will Play for Benefit of Band.
## 
### 
The Sword and Sandals has de
cided to give a plav for the benefit
of the University Band, and, at a
meeting of the Band held last
Monday night, it was agreed to ac
cept the proposition. The play
will probably be given the night
after the intercollegiate Varsity
field meet, which is scheduled for
Saturday, April 21st. The follow
ing committee has been selected to
choose a suitable play: D. I). Sales,
'08, H. Harkan, '06, R. S. Kellogg,
'07, J. M. Ward, '07, and J. E.
Stewart, '07.
