# CLASS WILL MEET FRIDAY.
## Sophomores Will Make Nominations for 1908 "Quad."—Contest for Treasurer to Be Decided. 
### 
President E. N. Smith has issued
a call for a meeting of the Sopho
more class to he held in room 200
next Friday afternoon at 1:10.
Two matters of importance will
be presented to the clasß. A date
will be set for another election for
treasurer, VV. O. Crawford and C.
L. Nordhoff having tied for this
position at the last election. Nom
inations for editor and manager of
the 1908 Quad will also be called
for, and a date set for holding this
election.
