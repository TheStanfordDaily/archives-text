# Special Services in Church Sunday.
## 
### 
In addition to the morning ser
vice in Memorial Church next Sun
day, at which Dean Hodges will
preach, there will be a musical at
traction in the evening, which will
take the place of the usual after
noon vesper service, which will be
omitted. Sunday evening Han
del's sacred oratorio,"The Messiah,"
will be rendered by the Memorial
Church choir and the following so
loists: Miss Sarah Brown, soprano;
Mrs. Mary Roberts Smith, con
tralto; Dr. G. B. Little, tenor; and
C. D. Moser, bass. Mrs. Smith is
the author of the music of "Hail,
Stanford, Hail." The oratorio will
be shortened and the service will
commence at 7:30.
