# President Modifies Request.
## 
### 
The request of the President in
regard to the holding of elections
on the quad has been modified.
Below the notice requesting "that
these elections be held in the fu
ture at the main arch or at the en
trance to the inner quadrangle,"
is posted the following:
"The above request is modified
to this extent: Elections may be
held only at places in which they
will not disturb the University
classes. David S. Joiidan,
President."
