# Rugby Football Game to Be Arranged.
## 
### 
J. A. McDonald of Vancouver,
13. C., representing the British Co
lumbia Rugby Football Union, will
arrive in San Francisco to-night,
where he will await the arrival of
the victorious New Zealand team,
which is due from New York to
morrow. He will try to arrange a
match game to be played here or at
the University of California.
