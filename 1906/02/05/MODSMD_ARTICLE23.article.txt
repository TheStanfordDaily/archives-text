# RUSSELL RESIGNS AS PROCTOR.
## Sixth Man to Withdraw from List of Monitors. 
### 
L. G. Russell, '00, has tendered
his resignation as a proctor in En
cina Hall. Russell was one of the
men on the list published Thursday.
His resignation leaves eight proctors
still on the active list in the Hall.
Russell is the sixth man to with
draw from the list of monitors ap
pointed by President Jordan.
