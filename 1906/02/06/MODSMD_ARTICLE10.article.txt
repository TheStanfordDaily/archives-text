# Glee and Orchestra Select Numbers.
## 
### 
The University Orchestra, which
now numbers about twenty pieces,
will open the concert to be given
next Friday night by (Stanford
musicians with the overture to
Bennett's "From Dawn to Twi
light," under the leadership of W.
A. Clark, '07, and later in the eve
ning will play "Sonnen-strahlen
und Schatten," a tone-poem by
Keiser. The Glee Club will sing
the following selections: "Pipe
Dreams" from the "Pretenders,"
by 11. C. Veit, and "Sweet and
Low."
