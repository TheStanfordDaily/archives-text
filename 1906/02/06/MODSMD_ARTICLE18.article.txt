# Miller Unable to Meet Classes.
## 
### 
Because of the sudden death of
his mother, Associate Professor G.
A. Miller, of the Mathematics de
partment, will he unable to meet
his classes for two weeks. He
leaves for his home in Kansas this
evening and probably will not re
turn until February 20th. Ar
rangements will he made to meet
all his classes except Mathematics
20, the astronomy course.
