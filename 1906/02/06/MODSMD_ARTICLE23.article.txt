# Books of Freshman Glass Audited.
## 
### 
The auditing committee of the
Freshman class, appointed at the
last meeting, met yesterday and ex
perted the books of G. B. Bush,
treasurer during last semester. The
books were found to be in perfect
order and correct in every detail.
During last semester the total re
ceipts of assessment collections
were $57.50, of which $32.90 was
spent in buying class sweaters, de
bating pins and other necessities.
The balance on hand is $24.60.
During the current semester an ef
fort will be made to collect the re
maining unpaid assessments. The
committee consisted of C. E. Samp
son, J. M. Politzer and H. S.
Goold.
