# Sophomore Show Posters Out.
## 
### 
The art poster advertising the
Sophomore comedy, which will he
given February 16th, was circu
lated this evening.
The design of the poster is a well
executed feminiue bust, reproduced
in colors, from the pen of E. E.
Horowitz, '06. The lettering is
done in gold.
The posters are on sale at the
campus Bookstore.
