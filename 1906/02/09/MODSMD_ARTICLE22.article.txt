# Tickets for Comedy on Sale Monday.
## 
### 
The tickets for the Sophomore
comedy, "She Stoops to Conquer,"
which will he given in the Assem
bly Hall the night of February
16th, will go on sale Monday morn
ing at 9:30 on the quad and at the
Stanford Pharmacy, Palo Alto.
The farce committee has ordered
special scenery and elaborate cos
tumes for the cast of ''She Stoops
to Conquer." For a week the play
ers have been practicing daily.
