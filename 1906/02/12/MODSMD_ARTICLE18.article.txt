# Advance Sale Heavy.
## 
### 
Seats for the Sophomore comedy
were placed on sale this morning
on the quad and in Palo Alto and
the sale up to noon surpassed that
of all previous years. At 4 o'clock
three hundred dollars had been
taken in on the quad, while almost
an equal amount was received in
Palo Alto. For the rest of the
week seats will be obtainable only
at the campus Bookstore and at the
Stanford Pharmacy in Palo Alto.
Daily rehearsals will be held this
week, and Wednesday and Thurs
day the cast, will rehearse in cos
tume in Assembly Hall, when the
scenery specially constructed for
the show will be installed.
