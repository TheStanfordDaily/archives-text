# Sidney Peixotto Will Speak.
## 
### 
Sidney Peixotto, head worker of
the Columbia Park Boys'Club, will
address the students and others in
terested in settlement work on
Wednesday afternoon in room 68
at 3:30.
Peixotto is one of the most suc
cessful settlement workers in the
West, and he has devoted his time
to work among boys, especially in
South Park, San Francisco.
