# PENSACOLA GAME CALLED OFF
## Varsity Will Play Practice Game with Team Picked from Squad. 
### 
The Pensacola team wired Man
ager Barrett to-day that they would
be unable to meet the Varsity to
morrow afternoon. Barrett tried
to fill the vacancy on the schedule
with the Elks or one of the Slate
League teams, but none of them
were able to come. In case it is
impossible to arrange a game with
an outside team, two nines picked
from the Varsity squad will play a
practice contest. The game will
be called at 2:80.
