# DR. SUZZALLO WILL GO EAST.
## Member of Education Department to Attend Association Meetings. 
### 
Professor A. H. Suzzallo, of the
department of Education, will leave
on Wednesday for Louisville, Ken
tucky. Dr. Suzzallo is the chair
man of a committee of one of the
three big educational associations
which meet in Louisville next week,
and he will he called upon to
report on several topics of impor
tance. En route to Kentucky Suz
zallo will deliver three lectures in
Indiana, at Marion, Richmond and
Indianapolis. The entire trip will
take two weeks.
During Suzzallo's absence Pro
fessor E. P. Cubberley will have
charge of hie classes.
