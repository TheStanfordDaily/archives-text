# Seniors to Play Belmont To-morrow.
## 
### 
The 1906 baseball team will play
Belmont to-morrow afternoon on
the Belmont diamond. The bus
for the team will leave the quad op
posite the Inn at 2.
The managers of the four class
teams will meet in Manager Bar
rett's office Wednesday afternoon
at 1:15, to arrange for the date of
the interclass series.
