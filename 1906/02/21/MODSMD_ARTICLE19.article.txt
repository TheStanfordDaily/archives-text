# San Francisco Man Will Speak.
## 
### 
H. J. McCoy, of San Francisco,
will address the University Young
Men's Christian Association next
Monday night at 7:30 on "Associ
ation Work the World Around."
McCoy is general secretary of the
San Francisco association, and his
talk will he the first of a semi
monthly series by professional.men
which has been arranged for. Mc-
Coy's lecture will be illustrated by
stereopticon slides.
