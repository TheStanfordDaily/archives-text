# Trustees to Determine Grant of Links.
## 
### 
C. G. Lathrop, treasurer of the
University, to-day announced that
he will present the question of as
signing links to the Stanford Golf
Association at the meeting of the
University trustees which will be
held in San Francisco on Friday.
Ground near the Cooksey place
has already been selected, pending
the sanction of the trustees. If they
grant the tract of land the work of
constructing the golf course will be
immediately commenced.
