# Activities on Washington's Birthday.
## 
### 
To-morrow being a holiday there
will he numerous athletic attrac
tions throughout the day. The
tennis tournament will begin at
8 in the morning and will last
all day. In the afternoon, at 2:30,
the Alameda State League team
will cross bats with the Varsity on
the diamond, and at 3 o'clock the
track meet will be commenced. Be
sides these contests, the usual
Thursday afternoon vesper service
will comprise patriotic music. The
library will be closed all dav.
