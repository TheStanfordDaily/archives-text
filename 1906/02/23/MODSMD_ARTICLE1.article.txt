# VARSITY LOSES GAME
## ALAMEDA STATE LEAGUE WINS BY SCORE OF 2-1. Ten Innings on Yesterday's Slippery Field Admit of Few Sensational Plays. 
### 
In a ten-inning game, compara
tively fast for the wet and slippery
condition of the diamond, the Var
sity lost yesterday afternoon to the
Alameda State League team by the
score of 2to 1. In the fifth, Sam
pson made the fourth consecutive
single in that inning, scoring Pres
ley. The Varsity's chances for
further scoring were cut in the next
inning, when Schroeder made a
right-hand stop of Stott's drive to
second. Alameda.tied the score in
the ninth and won in the tenth.
Alameda's two runs were made on
errors due to the muddy field.
