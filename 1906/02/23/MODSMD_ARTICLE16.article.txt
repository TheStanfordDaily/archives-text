# Announcements.
## 
### 
German Club —Will meet at the
residence of Charles Reinig.
Kappa Alpha Theta —Will be at
home Saturday, February 24th.
Mathematical Society — Will
meet at 19 Salvatierra street Sat
arday at 11 a.m. and 2 p.m.
1907 "Quad."—All art work in
the different departments must be
in by Wednesday, March 7th.
Senior Class —Will meet at 1:10
in the chapel Monday, February
26th. D. D. Sales, Pres.
Senior Week Committee—Will
meet at 1:10 Saturday, February
24th, at the Sigma Alpha Epsilon
house. D. D. Sales, Chairman.
"Quad" Story Contest.— Quad
short-story and poem competition
closes on March Ist. Manuscripts
may be handed to D. H. Clift, '07.
Junior Farce Cast. — Try-out
for chorus and solo parts will be
held in Assembly Hall at 1:30
Monday, February 26th. Bring
music.
Girls' Glee Club. —Important
special meeting Wednesday night
at 7 o'clock at the residence of Miss
Bruckmann, 400 Waverly street,
Palo Alto.
Junior Prom. Program. — A
prize of $5 is offered for the best
design submitted before March
15th. A. J. Chalmers,
Chairman.
1907 "Quad" Staff. — Meeting
at Delta Kappa Epsilon house
Wednesday evening at 7:30. Every
member of staff must have prepared
report of department up to date.
K. A. Bickel.
Tennis Squad.—lf weather per
mits, first try-out will start at 1
o'clock to-morrow afternoon on
Encina courts. If rain continues
tournament will be held Monday
afternoon at the same time.
P. B. Delano, Sec.
Senior Farce. —A prize of $25 is
offered for the best farce submitted
to the committee by April 9th.
Manuscript must be unsigned and
accompanied by a sealed envelope
containing author's name. There
must also be a synopsis of the piece
and a full description of the char
acters. A straight farce is prefer
red. The committee reserves the
right to reject any and all manu
scripts submitted. Mail all manu
scripts to L. W. Cutler, Palo Alto.
