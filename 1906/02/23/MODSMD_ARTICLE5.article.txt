# Musical Clubs Go to Belmont.
## 
### 
The Combined Glee and Mando
lin Clubs returned from Belmont
School late yesterday evening after
a successful concert at that place.
The party left the campus yes
terday afternoon in tallyhos and
buses. Supper was served them
at Belmont and after the concert a
pleasant informal dance was held.
No admission was charged to the
concert and the whole entertain
ment was in the manner of a pure
social event.
