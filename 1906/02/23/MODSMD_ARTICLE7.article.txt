# Washington's Birthday Parade.
## 
### 
An unusually large and success
ful Washington's Birthday parade
was held on the campus yesterday
afternoon. Immediately after noon
a procession of more than a hun
dred students, befittingly attired,
gathered from the fraternity houses
and, headed by a band, visited the
halls and paraded the principal
streets on the campus. Several
excellent pictures were secured for
the 1907 Quad.
