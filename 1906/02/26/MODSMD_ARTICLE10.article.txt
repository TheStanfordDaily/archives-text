# California Submits Football Question.
## 
### 
According to the new intercol
legiate agreement just entered into,
the University of California sub
mitted the question for the spring
debate Saturday. It reads as fol
lows: "Resolved, That the Ameri
can game of football has done more
harm than good to the ideal for
which universities should exist.''
Stanford has the choice of sides
and may delay action until next
Saturday.
