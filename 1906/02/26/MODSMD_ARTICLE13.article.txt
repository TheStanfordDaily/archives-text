# Address on " Fun and Philosophy."
## 
### 
Miss Marie Shedlock, of London,
will address an open meeting of the
English Club to-morrow evening
in the University chapel. The sub
ject upon which she will speak is
"Fun and Philosophy in Hans
Christian Andersen," which she
will illustrate by selections and
readings from the tales. The lec
ture will be concluded by readings
from "Knight and Barbara," Dr.
Jordan's volume of child stories.
At the close of the program in
the chapel the English Club will
hold an informal reception for Miss
Shedlock at the Alpha Phi House.
Members of the faculty are invited
to meet her.
