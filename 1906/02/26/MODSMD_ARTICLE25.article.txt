# Lecture on Japanese Art Postponed.
## 
### 
The lecture entitled "The Seven
Luck Gods in Japanese Art," which
Henry P. Bowie, president of the
Japan Society of America, was to
have delivered this afternoon, has
been postponed until a week from
to-morrow on account of the in
ability of the class in art to be
present early at the lecture this af
ternoon. In place of the lecture,
President Bowie gave a short talk
on the Seven Luck Gods and the
Five Holiday Seasons, and pictures
by Sekko Shimada were shown.
The lecture will be delivered in the
University chapel at 3:30 on March
6 th.
