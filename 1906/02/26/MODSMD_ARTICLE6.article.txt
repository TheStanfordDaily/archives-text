# Mathematicians Adjourn.
## 
### 
The San Francisco section of the
American Mathematical Society

one-day session at 19 Salvatierra
street. At the next meeting, in
Berkeley in September, the suc
cessors of Professors R. E. Allar
dice and G. A. Miller, respectively
president and secretary of the sec
