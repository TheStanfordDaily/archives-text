# Founds Mining Departments.
## 
### 
Money from the sale of lands in
various states will hereafter go to
endowing departments of mining
in Western colleges, according to a
bill returned to the House of
Representatives by the committee
on mines and mining last Tuesday.
The amount given to each college
will be $10,000 for the first year,
and thereafter will increase $1000
a year until it reaches $20,000 an
nually. California will he among
the sixteen Western states and ter
ritories to profit by the bill if it
becomes a law.
