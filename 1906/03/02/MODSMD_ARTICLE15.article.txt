# Fourth Oratorio Sunday.
## 
### 
Sunday evening, March 4th, the
fourth of the Oratorio Services will
be held in Memorial Church. "A
Hymn of Praise," by Felix M.
Bartholdy, will be rendered by the
choir, assisted by the following solo
ists:
Sopranos: Mrs. Arthur Ficken
scher and Miss Ruth Foster; tenor,
Dr. G. B. Little.
In the morning service the fol
lowing numbers will be rendered
by the choir:
Kyrie Gilbert
Choral, "To God on High Be
Thanks and Praise"
Mendelssohn
