the Fieshmen will meet the track
team from Oakland High School.
This is the first meet of the season
with an outside team, and from all
indications it will he a close con
test. The following officials have
been selected for the meet: H. W.
Bell, '0(i, starter; C. F. Laumeister,
'07, clerk of the course; Dr. Frank
Angell, "Dad" Moulton, and I.

and I). P. Crawford, '09, measur
