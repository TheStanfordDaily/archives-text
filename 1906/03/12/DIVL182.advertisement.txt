COLONIST RATES
TO CALIFORNIA
Will be in cffcct every day, Feb. 15 to April 7, and
Sept. 15 to Oct. 31, 1906, from all points in the Fast
and South.
Following are a few:
Chicago $33.00 Kansas City and Leavenworth $25.00
St Louis 30 00 St. Joseph and Atchison 25.00
Galveston 26.45 Council Bluffs and Omaha 25 00
Dallas, Houston and Ft. Woith 25.00
Nearly ail points in Kansas, Oklahoma and Indian Ty. 25.00
Colonist rates are also in effect from all points east of
Chicago and south of the Ohio river.
Santa Fs Agents in California will lake deposits of mono; to cover price of tickets,
which will be promptly furnished to passengers at any point
from which these rates are in effect.
Culifornta Illustrated literature mailed free to any
address or may be had from Santa Fe representatives.
