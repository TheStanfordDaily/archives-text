# Starter Cary Will Move to the East.
## 
### 
Luther Cary, who has officiated
as starter at a majority of the in
tercollegiate field meets between
Stanford and California and simi
lar contests on the Pacific Coast
for many years, will change his re
sidence from San Francisco to Bos
ton, Mass., March 15th, and conse
quently will not fulfill his accus
tomed duties in subsequent inter
collegiate meets. Cary was invited
to act as starter at the interscho
lastic meet, but will be unable to
serve, as March 24th is the date
set for the contest by the Inter
scholastic Association.
