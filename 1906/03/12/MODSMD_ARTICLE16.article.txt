# Appointed Dean of Summer School.
## 
### 
Dr. E. C. Moore, professor of
education at the University of Cali
fornia, has been appointed dean
of the coming session of the
University of California summer
school. Dr. Moore has tendered
his resignation as a regular pro
fessor of the state university, to
take effect at the close of the pres
ent semester. After the summer
session he will go to Los Angeles
to assume the duties of superinten
dent of schools of that city.
