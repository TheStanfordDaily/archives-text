# Manager Barrett Has No Hopes of Completing Field for Intercollegiate Meet.
## 
### 
There seems to be but little
possibility of completing the new
track in time for the intercollegiate
meet. Manager Barrett says the
work cannot be completed in time,
and even if it were possible to com
plete it, it would not be feasible to
hasten the construction.
Graders have been employed for
two weeks, and it will probably be
several weeks more before the mere
preliminary work of scraping and
leveling the surface is finished.
To secure a good foundation some
time must elapse to permit the
dirt to settle before even the filler
can be added.
The old track is in fairly good
condition now, and it can easily be
putin shape for the interscholastic
meet a week from Saturday.
