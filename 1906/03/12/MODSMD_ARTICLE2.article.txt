# VARSITY WINS EASILY
## TWENTY-SECOND INFANTRY DEFEATED BY SCORE OF 10 TO 1. Theile Strikes Out Nine Men in Five Innings.—Stanford Steals Twelve Bases. 
### 
The Varsity had no trouble in
disposing of the 22d Infantry team
from Angel Island on Saturday af
ternoon. The final score stood 10
to 1. Theile pitched for the first
five innings and sent nine of the
soldiers to the bench on strikes. He
was succeeded by Witmer, who al
so pitched a good game. Stanford
scored one run in each of the first
three innings, three in the fourth
and four in the sixth. The sol
diers' lone tally came in the fourth
on a hit and a wild throw to third
by Theile. The Varsity played a
strong fielding game, the only two
errors being charged to Theile on
poor throws to bases. The soldiers
were weak behind the bat and the
Varsity stole a grand total of twelve
bases without the least trouble.
