# Announcements.

1906 Pins. —The 1906 class pins
have arrived at the University
Bookstore.
Women's Settlement Class. —
Meeting on Wednesday at 3:30 in
room 13 Madrono.
Philosophy I—Will1 —Will not meet un
til Monday, March 19th.
William James.
Press Club —Will meet at usual
place at 6 p.m. Friday, March 16th.
W. H. B. Fowler, Pres.
Encina Gymnasium Club—Will

meet Wednesday evening at
8 o'clock. G. J. Presley.
Chemistry 2 —Examination will
be held on Monday at 11:15, in
stead of Friday.
J. M. Stillman.
Orchestra —Meets at 7:30 Wed
nesday evening in the chapel to
practice music for Junior Farce.
E. G. Sheibley.
At Home. —President and Mrs.
Van Hise, of the University of
Wisconsin, and Professor and Mrs.
Hoskine will be at home to friends
on Saturday evening, March 17th,
at 365 Lincoln avenue.
