# New Clock Ordered.
## 
### 
The castings and small parts for
a chronograph driving clock have
been ordered by the Mechanical
department from Warner and
Swasev, of Cleveland. The appa
ratus is similar to that which the
above company have fitted to their
telescope driving clocks. The appa
ratus will he set up in the mechan
ician shop of the Mechanical de
partment, and will be used in con
nection with advanced engineering
research.
