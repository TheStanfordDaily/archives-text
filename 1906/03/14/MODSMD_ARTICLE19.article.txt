# Dr. Suzzallo Returns.
## 
### 
Professor A. H. Suzzallo of the
Education department returned
yesterday from a three weeks' ab
sence in the East. Dr. Suzzallo
attended the meeting of four sec
tions of the National Educational
Association in Louisville, Ken
tucky. One of the sections Dr.
Suzzallo led, reading a paper on
one of the European schools of
education.
