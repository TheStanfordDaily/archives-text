# Prom. Tickets on Sale To-morrow.
## 
### 
Tickets for the Junior Prom, will
he on pale to-morrow at the post
office from 8:15 to 4:30, at five dol
lars each. The committee in charge
of the Prom, held a meeting to-day
at which the details of the dance
were arranged. Brohaska's orches
tra of San Jose has been engaged
for the occasion. Supper will he
served from the twelfth to the nine
teenth dances, inclusive.
