# BOARD OF GOVERNORS ELECTED
## Men Who Will Hold Long and Short Terms of Office. 
### 
At the last meeting of the board
of governors of the Stanford Uni
versity Intereoholastic Athletic
Association, the governors drew
lots, in accordance with the con
stitution, in order to determine
which of their number were to
serve the long term of two years,
and which the short term of one
year. Those who will serve for
two years are Dr. Frank Angell,
P. J. Treat, J. F. Lanagan, '00, R.
W. Barrett, '04, W. H. B. Fowler,
'06, and C. F. Laumeister,'o7. The
following men will be governors
for one year: J. C. McCaughern, '04,
B. S. Allen, ex-'O6, J. C. MacFar
land, '07, J. O. Miller, '08, H. L.
Horton, '08, and J. R. Hickey, ex
'oB.
A communication from Gren
ville C. Emery, principal of the
Harvard School of Los Angeles,
has been received, stating that a
team of six men will be entered.
A certificate of eligibility accom
panied the names of the men, who
are also entered in the relay.
