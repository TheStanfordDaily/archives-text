# Dr. du Buy Lectures.
## 
### 
Dr. .Jean du Buy, who has lately
become a member of the German
department, at the request of Dr.
Gardner delivered the lecture be
fore the class in "Life and Teach
ings of Christ" this afternoon in
the chapel. Dr. du Buy's subject
was two-fold: the nature of the
kingdom of God, and the spiritual
in religion. He has written two
essays on the subject, one entitled
the "Stages of Religious Develop
ment, a Study in Comparative Re
ligion," and the other a "Har
mony of the Teachings of Jesus."
