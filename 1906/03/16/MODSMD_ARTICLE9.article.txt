# President Van Hise Will Lecture.
## 
###

versity of Wisconsin will deliver an
address on Tuesday afternoon of
next week on Movements of the
Earth. President Van Hise is one
of the most eminent geologists of
the country. The exact time and
place of the address, which will he
given under the auspices of the
Sigma Xi fraternity, will be an
nounced on Monday.
