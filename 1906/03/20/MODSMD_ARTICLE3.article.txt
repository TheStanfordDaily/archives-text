# Women's Tennis.
## 
### 
The women's tennis tournament
is being played off as fast as the
stormy weather and consequently
poor condition of the courts will
permit. The summary of play so
far is as follows:
Miss M. Morris, '08, defeated
Miss E. Hill, 'OS, 6-4, '2-6, 6-2.
Miss M. Dunn, '08, defeated Miss
C. Taverner, '08, 6-4, 12-10. Miss
E. Owen, '08, defeated Miss L. How
ell, '08, 6-1, 6-3.
Miss M. Morris defeated Miss M.
Dunn, 2-6, 9-7, 12-10.
