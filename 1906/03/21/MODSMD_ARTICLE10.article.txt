# Novel Meet To-morrow Afternoon.
## 
### 
To-morrow afternoon at 4 o'clock
there will be a track meet among
the Varsity candidates. It will be
a contest between the Science ma
jors and the men in the Liberal Arts
courses. By this division of the
men Captain Mao Far hind expects
to have an even contest and also to
create some interest in the meet
aside from the individual rivalry.
In spite of the recent rains the
track men have been training al
most every day.
