# To Play at Holmes Recital.
## 
### 
A benefit recital for the widow of
the late Henry Holmes, the noted
violinist of San Francisco, will be
rendered Friday evening, March
23d, at the home of Mrs. Fowler,
221 Kingsley avenue, Palo Alto.
Many well known artists will take
part, among whom are Dr. Blod
gett, organist of Memorial Church.
Carriages will leave the southeast
corner of the quadrangle at 7:30.
A special round-trip rate of thirty
five cents will be charged.
