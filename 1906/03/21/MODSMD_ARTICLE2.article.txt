# Football Sub-Committee Meets.
## 
### 
The sub-committee appointed by
the Football Conference to suggest
changes in the present game held
its second meeting last night at the
University Club in San Francisco.
The committee succeeded in com
ing to an agreement as to what
changes should be suggested in its
report, but its action will not be
made public until the report has
been read in the next meeting of
the conference.
