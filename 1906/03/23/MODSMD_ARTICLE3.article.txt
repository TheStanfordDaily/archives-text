# Undergraduate Is Married.
## 
### 
A. M. Moody, ex-'OB, was re
cently married to Miss Nichols, a
sister-in-law of Major Collins of
Belmont. Moody played full-hack
on the 1908 football team, and left
Stanford at the close of his fresh
man year to enter the medical de
partment of the University of Chi
cago. He is a member of the Chi
Psi fraternity.
