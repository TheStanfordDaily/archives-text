# Dean Hodges Honored by Alumni.
## 
### 
Last Tuesday evening Dean
Hodges was the guest of the Ham
ilton Alumni at the University
Club, San Francisco. Charles P.
Eels, member of the Board of Trus
tees, Dean Hodges and Librarian
M. G. Dodge were among the alum
ni present. Thomas E. Hayden,
a former law student at Stanford,
was elected president for 1906.
