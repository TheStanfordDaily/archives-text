# Junior Farce Posters on Sale.
## 
### 
The posters for the Junior Farce,
which have been displayed for the
past few days, will he placed on
sale to-morrow at the Bookstore.
The drawing on the poster depicts
a Junior girl wearing a plug and a
varsity " S." It was drawn by E.
E. Horowitz, '06. The posters are
well colored and there will be some
on sale without any printing what
ever. The price is twenty-five
cents.
