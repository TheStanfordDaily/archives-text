# Tuesday's Organ Program.
## 
### 
Overture to Egmond.'.. . .Beethoven
Symphony No. 1 C Major.
Adagio 6 allegro con trio.
Andante cantabili.
Menneto.
Finale.
Symphony No. 2 I) Major
Beethoven
Adagio mote e 'Allegro.
Larghetto.
Scherzo.
Finale.
To-day being the anniversary of
Beethoven's birth, the organ pro
gram consisted entirely of move
ments from the seventh and ninth
Symphonies. To-morrow the pro
gram will include the first and sec
ond Symphonies.
