# SENIORS VOTE MONEY
## FIFTY DOLLARS FOR SCHOLARSHIP FUND. Senior Men May Wear Caps and Gowns on Commencement Day. 
### 
At a meeting of the Senior class
to-day it was decided to settle the
question of the advisability of the
men's wearing caps and gowns on
graduation day, by vote of the men
of tbe class. An election has been
called by President Sales for Wed
nesday, at which time tbe men will
have an opportunity of voicing
their opinion on the question.
There was considerable discus
sion whether the men and women
who received their degrees in Jan
uary or whether those wbo receive
them next January shall be allow
ed to place their names on the roll.
This matter will lie decided at the
next meeting of the class after the
Easter vacation.
The chairman of the Senior
Finance Committee gave a lengthy
report on the expenses of senior
week. Among the class expenses
are those in connection with the
class re-union to be given at Mrs.
Stanford's residence. A motion
was passed that an assessment of
two dollars be levied. The treasury
is in good condition, hut a large
assessment was considered advis
able as last year a special tax had
to he levied after the class plate
had been laid.
The permanent alumni organiza
tion idea was discussed, and the
class decided to reunite for the first
time after two years graduation.
The next reunion will he held three,
followed from that time on by a
reunion every five years.
Following out the idea of per
manent organization still further,
the class voted fifty dollars for a
new scholarship fund. The money
will he turned over to the Hoard of
Trustees by the permanent class
secretary upon the death of all
members of the dlass, the interest
on which will be used to put a
chosen student through college.
Hereafter each class will vote $'25 a
year to their scholarship fund.
The Senior class is taking the
initiative in a system used success
fully by Yale and Harvard.
The chairman of the Senior Ball
Committee reported that Treasurer
Lathrop favors allowing the Seniors
the use of the new gymnasium pro
vided a legal technicality can be
avoided. The trustees will meet
Saturday, March 31st, and their
decision will be given the following
Monday.
