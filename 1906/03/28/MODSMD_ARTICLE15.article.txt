# California Chooses Debaters.
## 
### 
In the coming Intercollegiate de
bate California will be represented
by N. A. Eisner, '07, I'. N. Gray,
'06, and M. E. Harrison. '08, with
W. S. Andrews, '06, as alternate.
Eisner won this year's Carnot de
bate, and Harrison was on the Car
not and Intercollegiate teams of
last year. Gray has never debated
against Stanford, but is prominent
in undergraduate affairs, being
president of the Associated Students
and having played on last year's
football team.
