# ADOPT CAP AND GOWN
## SENIOR MEN VOTE FAVORABLY BY 37 TO 29. Graduates Will Wear Conventional Attire During Commencement Week. 
### 
The Senior election on the quad
this afternoon to see whether the
men of the graduating class should
wear caps and gowns resulted in
favor of the conventional attire by
a vote of 37 to 29, 66 votes be
ing cast. This expression of
opinion means that those who re
ceive degrees will wear caps and
gowns during the commencement
exercises on the day of graduation.
