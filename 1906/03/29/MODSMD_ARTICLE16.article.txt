# Bowling Alley to be Skating Rink.
## 
### 
The present bowling alley under
Mullen's Hall in Palo Alto will be
changed into a skating rink, which
will be opened on Friday night,
March 30th, by R. A. Thompson,
'05, Paul C. Green, ex-'O2, and A.
M. Thomson, ex-'O3. The size of
the floor is about 60x80 feet.
Among the improvements which
will be features of the new rink
will be an up-to-date orchestra and
dressing-rooms for patrons.
