# TRAINING-TABLE STARTED.
## Twenty-eight Track Men Are Eating Under Trainer Moulton's Supervision. 
### 
Trainer Moulton has had his
best athletes eating at the training
table for the past week and has
added a few men since the original
list was made out. At present
twenty-eight men are eating under
his supervision. The complete list

next Wednesday afternoon.
