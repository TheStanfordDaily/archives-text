# Spaulding Will Do Scientific Work
## 
### 
M. H. Spaulding, formerly of
the Zoology department of the
University, who left last semester
to join the faculty of the Louisiana
State University, will have charge
of the "Comparative Anatomy of
Vertebrates" in the Gulf Biologic
Station Laboratory, which meets
in July in Louisiana to investigate
the biologic problems of the state.
