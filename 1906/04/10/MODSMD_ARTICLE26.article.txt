# Undergraduate Accepts Position.
## 
### 
J. J. Sheehy, '06, left college yes
terday to accept a position as agent
for the Parkside Realty Company
of San Francisco. The company
owns large tracts of land south of

Golden Gate Park, running to the
beach, and has recently been grant
ed a franchise to construct a rail
road through the district.
Sheehy is the manager-elect of
the Chaparral for next year and
his withdrawal from college will
necessitate the election of another
man to his place. Sheehy is an as
sociate editor of the Chaparral, a
member of the I'ress Club, and a
member of the Beta Theta Pi fra
ternity.
