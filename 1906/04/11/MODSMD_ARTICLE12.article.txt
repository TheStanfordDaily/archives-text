# Track Will Not Be Finished for Meet.
## 
### 
Work is progressing slowly on
the new track. The preliminary
grading is still in progress, and
Treasurer Barrett is making good
his promise that the work will not he
rushed to completion. Only a few
teams are employed, and it will he
some time yet before they scrape
to the desired level. Barrett says
positively the track will not he
built this college year. There is
no need for it now; and, further
more, the student body is not at
present financially able to erect
suitable and adequate bleachers.
