# VARSITY IS SHUT OUT.
## Stanford Plays Errorless Ball in the Field but Cannot Cross Plate. 
### 
McGregor and Theile fought out
a nine-inning pitchers' battle on
the St. Mary's diamond yesterday
afternoon, which finally resulted in
a victory for the Phoenix team by
a score of 2 to 0. McGregor held
the Cardinal down to two scattered
hits. Theile allowed only three,
but two of them, a balk and a base
on balls in the sixth, gave St.
Mary's two runs.
The balk was not allowed by the
umpire, but Lanagan requested
him to change his decision. For
this act the Stanford coach was
cheered by the St. Mary's rooters.
The Varsity played a perfect game
in the field, but could not connect
with McGregor'scurves. The score
follows:
