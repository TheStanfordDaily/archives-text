# Delano Wins in Round Robin.
## 
### 
P. B. Delano, '08, defeated .T.
F. Cassell, '07, in the first match
of the round robin tournament on
the Encina courts yesterday after
noon. The wind was blowing a
gale, and this greatly interfered
with fast and accurate play. De
lano appeared steadier than Cas
sell and won three sets out of four,
as follows: 6-3, 6-4, 5-7, and 6-2.
