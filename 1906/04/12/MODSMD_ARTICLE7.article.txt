# Special "Chaparral" for Track Meet.
## 
### 
The next issue of the Chaparral
will he an enlarged special number
for the intercollegiate track and
field meet and will appear Friday,
April 20th, the day before the meet.
Features will he a supplement—
prose articles, verse, drawings and
an editorial, all devoted to track.
