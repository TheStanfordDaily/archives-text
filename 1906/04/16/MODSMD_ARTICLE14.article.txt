# Orchestra Will Have Constitution.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the
University Orchestra Wednesday
night a committee to form plans
for reorganization was appointed
by Manager R. E. Collom. The
committee will draw up a constitu
tion, embodying the aims of the
organization and establishing elect
ive offices. The intention is to put
the Orchestra on the basis of a reg
ular club, and especially to limit
membership to those who pass
yearly try-outs. The committee,
which expects to report on the con
stitution at next Wednesday's
meeting, consists of E. C. Sheib
ley, '08, H. R. Thomas, '08, R. U.
Fitting, '06, F. W. Turner, '08, and
R. E. Collom, '06.
