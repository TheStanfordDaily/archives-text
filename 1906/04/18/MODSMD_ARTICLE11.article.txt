# Intercollegiate Events Postponed.
## 
### 
Treasurer R. W. Barrett left on
an early train for San Francisco
this morning to arrange for the

postponement indefinitely of the
intercollegiate events scheduled for
this week. Barrett did not say he
fore he left what disposition of the
track meet, debate, tennis and chess
matches will be made, but in all
probability these events will all be
postponed, if not canceled.
