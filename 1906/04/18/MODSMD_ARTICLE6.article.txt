# Provision Made for Hall Students.
## 
### 
The authorities have condemned
Encina Hall and have placed
guards outside to closely watch the
building during the night. Pro
vision has been made for the dormi
tory men who are without place to
sleep. President Jordan's house,
Treasurer Lathrop's home, the
Faculty club house, Escondita
cottage and numerous on and off
the campus. The stock farm barns
and Encina gymnasium have been
thrown open to the residents of the
men's dormitory.
Roble gymnasium will be used
by the girls of Roble Hall who will
leave their dormitory for the night.
