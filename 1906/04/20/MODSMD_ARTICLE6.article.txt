# BANK STATEMENT.
## 
### 
We can inform depositors of the amount to their credit on our
books, but can give no definite information as to when funds will
be available. We cannot certify checks at all. The San Fran
cisco Clearing House has ordered all banks closed indefinitely.
We will open, for business as soon as they permit. Nothing more
definite can be stated now.
BANK OF PALO ALTO,
FIRST NATIONAL BANK.
