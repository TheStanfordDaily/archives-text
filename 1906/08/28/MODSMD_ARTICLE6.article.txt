# Professor Green to Meet Encina Men.
## 
### 
No radical changes in the rules
and regulations regarding hazing
in Encina Hall have as yet been
made by the authorities. The new
regulations posted in the domitory
declare in general terms that haz
ing is prohibited and that (, the
privacy of students' rooms must not
be invaded against the will of the
person concerned."
Regarding the proctor system,
there is no new move. It is the in
tention of Professor It. L. Green,
Chairman of the Student Affairs
Committee, to meet the men living
in Encina and talk over the affairs
