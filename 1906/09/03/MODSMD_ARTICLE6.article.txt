# Elected to English Club.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the English Club
held last Saturday evening the fol
lowing were elected to membership:
Harold Fitch, '07, J. N. Stanford,
'07, G. 0. Spurrier, '07. Miss Laura
Wells, '09, D. H Walker, '09, V. F.
Bellows. '09, Miss A. Ellerbeck, '08
and J. K. Hutchinson, '09.
