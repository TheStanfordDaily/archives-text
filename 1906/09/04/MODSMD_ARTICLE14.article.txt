# Encina Club Elects Officers
## 
### 
The members of the Encina Club
elected the following officers last
evening:
President,—J. N. Stanford, '07;
Directors: from 1907,—M. C. Burr,
J. B. Holly; from 1908— M. R.
Colwell, L. F. Halloran; from 1909
—G. McGregor, E. E. Young.
