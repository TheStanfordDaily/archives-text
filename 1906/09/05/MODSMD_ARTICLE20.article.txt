# Announcements.
## 
### 
Men's Chorus. —All men inter
ested in a men's chorus meet in the
chapel, Thursday evening, at 7
o'clock.
Sophomore meeting.—Class of
1909 will elect officers in room 68
Thursday, Sept. 6th, at 1 o'clock.
E. A. Rogers, Secretary.
Senior Class —Will meet Thurs
day at 1:15 in room 2 on the quad.
J. C. Ray, Vice-pres.
Class of 1906. —The Alumni Re
union Committee desires the pres
ent address and occupation of
every member of the Class of
1906, whether graduate or not.
This information is desired for the
use of the Permanent Class Secre
tary. Please leave this information
with the Registrar, or mail same to
room 35, St. Mungo Building, San
Francisco, Cal.
Harold Ide Cruzan,
Chairman
Y. W. C. A.— Meets Tuesday
afternoon at 4:40 in the Chapel.
H. H. McQuilken of the First Pres
byterian Church of San Jose will
speak.
