# Gymnasium Club Elects
## 
### 
The annual election of the Gym
nasium Club occurred last evening
when J. H. Cutting, '06, and P. E.
Millar, '07, were elected president
and secretary-treasurer respective
ly. Only seven members of last
year's club have registered in col
lege, and new men will be taken in
as soon as available material in
the various gymnasium classes is
discovered.
