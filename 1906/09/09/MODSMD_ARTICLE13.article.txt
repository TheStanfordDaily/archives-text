# Alumni Officers Retain Positions.
## 
### 
Owing to the difficulty of hold
ing an election, the executive com
mittee of the Alumni Association
has decided that the presentofficers
shall hold their positions until the
next alumni reunion. This action
continues in office Dennis Searles,
'95, aB president, Dr. R.L. Wilbur,
'96, as chairman of the executive
committee, J. H. Timmons, '97, as
editor and 0. A. Wilson, '04, as
manager of the Alumnus.
