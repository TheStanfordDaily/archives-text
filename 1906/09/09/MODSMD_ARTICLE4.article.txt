# Annual Freshman Reception.
## 
### 
The reception to the Freshman
class by the combined Christian
Associations will be held in Encina
Gymnasium, Saturday, September
Bth, at eight o'clock. The Gym.
will he decorated with masses of
live oak houghs and strings of col
lege pennants. The university or
chestra will furnish the music for
the evening, and Dr. Jordan will
address the class of 1910. The sys
tem of introduction by signatures,
which was successfully introduced
last year, will be again employed.
Refreshments are to be served be
tween the hours of 9:30 and 10:30.
