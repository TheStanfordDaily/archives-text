# Announcements.
## 
### 
Senior Reception. — President
and Mrs. Jordan will be at home to
members of the class of 1906, to
members of the faculty, their fam
ilies and friends and to alumni,
Saturday afternoon, September
15th, from 4 to 6.
'06 Commencement Programs—
are now at the Bookstore. Each
member of the class is entitled to
five programs without charge.
Programs Committee,
C. A. Beardsley, '06.
Varbity Baseball Team—Will
meet at 1:15 Tuesday in Manager
Sales' office in the gymnasium.
Senior Women.—There will be a
meeting of the women of 1907 in
room 50, at 1:15 Wednesday after
noon.
Senior Election—Will be held
from 10:15 to 12:15 and from 1:30
to 2:30 to-morrow on the quad.
Mandolin Club.—The first try
out for the Mandolin Club will be
held to-morrow night, September
11th, in room 68.
F. R. Lanagan, '08.
Glee Club. — There will be a
meeting of the Glee Club Tuesday
evening at 7:45 in room 63.
A. B. Packard, '07, Manager.
