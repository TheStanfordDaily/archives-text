# Zoology Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The Zoology Club held ita first
meeting of the semester at the Bal
satic Columns last Friday evening.
H. O. Jenkins, '07, was re-elected
president and J. N. Proctor, '07,
was chosen secretary. About fifty
members gathered around a large
camp fire from five o'clock to eleven,
dinner being served at six and re
freshments some hours later. Plans
for an extensive camp meeting,
probably to be held at South Gulch,
were discussed. Dr. Heath gave
the Club an interesting talk, deal
ing with his recent travels through
Japan.
