University Orchestra. — All
members of the orchestra meet for
rehearsal in the chapel, Wednesday
evening at 7:30.
E. G. Sheibley, '08.
. Hammer and Coffin—Will meet
at six o'clock, Wednesday at the
usual place.
1907 Quad Board —Will meet to
morrow afternoon at 4:30 at the Se
quoia office.
'06 Commencement Invitations
—Are now at the Bookstore. Each
mem her of the graduating class is
entitled to six invitations.
W. Y. Wentz, '06.
Senior Reception. — President
and Mrs. Jordan will be at home to
members of the class of 1906, grad
uates taking advanced degrees, the
faculty of the University and their
families, and visiting alumni, on
Saturday afternoon, September
15th, from 4 to 6.
Auditing Committee, '09, —Will
meet at the Delta Kappa Epsilon
house Thursday evening, Septem
ber 13th, at 7:30.
L. R. Minturn,
Chairman.
