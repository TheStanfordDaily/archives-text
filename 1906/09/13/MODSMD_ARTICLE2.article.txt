# RUBBING LIST POSTED
## FOURTEEN MEN TAKEN ON FROM VARSITY SQUAD. All of Last Year's Varsity Who Are Out For New Game Included. 
### 
The first rubbing lint of the sea
son was posted in the training
house last night. Fourteen candi
dates for the varsity team were
placed on the lint., including all the
members of last year's Varsity who
are out for the new game. The
amount of running in Rugby makes
the rubbing list a necessity this
early in the season.
The following is the list: E. P.
Stott, '07, P. T. Frizzelle, '07, J. N.
Stanford,'o7, A. J. Chalmers, '07,
A. A. Molfino,'o7, G. J. Presley,
'07, C. F. Laumeister, '07, K. L.
Fenton, '08, L. R. Gay, 'OS, R. F.
Meyers, '08, J. R. Pemberton, '08
S. Vail. '08, W.Koerner, '08, C. C
Owen, *09.
Last nsght's practice developed
nothing new. Most of the time was
spent in punting and catching and
the men were again instructed in
passing down the line of backs on
the run.
