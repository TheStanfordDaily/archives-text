# Glee Club Try-out Held.
## 
### 
The first Glee Club trial was held
in Encina Hall last evening, about
forty-live new men trying out. Prof.
11. Roland Roberts tested the candi
dates in the presence of the executive
committee, consisting of O. K. Gran,
'06, S. B. Thompson, '0~, and J. J.
Mill, '08. The names of those who
made the club will not be announced
until next week. Another try-out
will be held Friday evening for those
who were anuble to attend last night.
