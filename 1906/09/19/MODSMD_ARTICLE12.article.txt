# Power House Will Soon Run.
## 
### 
The power house will be in run
ning order by the end of the week.
The new fifty-foot iron smokestack
is now being installed, and three
of the five boilers have been entirely
repaired. The other two were so
badly damaged that they are practi
cally useless, but new ones have
been ordered to replace them. The
three repaired boilers, however, can
met the present demand, so power
will be had as soon as the stack is
ready.
