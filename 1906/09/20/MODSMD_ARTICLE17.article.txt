# Zoology Club Will Camp Out.
## 
### 
Starting Saturday morning, Sep
tember J2cl, the Zoology Club will
go on a collecting expedition along
the trail to the Mary Craven's Camp
and through the neighboring country.
The club will pitch camp on Los
Francos Creek, near the trail, on Sat
urday morning and will spend the
remainder of the day in hunting for
zoological specimens.
Provisions for three or four meals
are expected to be supplied person
ally by those making the trip. The
club will furnish coffee and pota
toes. Blankets and provisions will
be collected Saturday morning by 8
o'clock. The club will camp out Sat
urday night and return Sunday.
