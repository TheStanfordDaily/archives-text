# Plug Ugly Committee Is Named.
## 
### 
President C. X. Leach of the Jun
ior class today appointed the com
mittees which will have charge of the
Plug Ugly and the awarding of the
class sweaters to track men who
took points in the interclass meet of
last spring.
The Plug Ugly committee is: R.
H. Baker, chairman; Miss J. E.
Clithero, Miss E. L. Brown, E. J.
Swift and F. W. Turner. The sweat
er committee follows: C. L. Severy,
chairman; C. L. Bradley and J. R.
Pemberton.
