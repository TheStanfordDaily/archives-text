# Prizes Offered for One-act Plays.
## 
### 
For the best and second best one
act plays the Burbank Theater School
of Acting of Los Angeles offers two
prizes of $25 and $15, respectively,
with a guarantee of a public perform
ance to be given at one of the stu
dents' matinees this winter. The
plays will be judged upon the follow
ing points: first, originality of plot;
second, characterization; third, liter
ary value; fourth, situation and
knowledge of stage technique.
