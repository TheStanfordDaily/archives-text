# FRESHMAN ELIGIBLES
## LISTS EXCHANGED BY STANFORD AND CALIFORNIA. Dr. Angell Sends Sixty-one Names Blue and Gold Has Forty-three.

In accordance with the terms of
the intercollegiate athletic agreement
between Stanford University and the
University of California, the chair
men of the Faculty Athletic Commit
tees of the respective institutions
have exchanged lists of the names
of the Freshmen eligible to play in
the annual intercollegiate Freshman
football game. Any protests of
prospective players in the contest
should be made within two weeks
from last Saturday. The names re
ceived by Dr. Angell, chairman of
the Faculty Athletic Committee,
from Col. George C. Edwards, chair
man of the University of California
Faculty Committee on Athletics,
number forty-three. Sixty-one
lU'.mes were sent to California by
Dr. Angell. The list including these,
however, is provisional and subject
to change, as the standing of some of
the candidates is not yet definitely
decided by the Committee on Admis
sion.
