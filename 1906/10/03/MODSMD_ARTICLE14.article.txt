..Freshman Debating League—Will
meet Monday evening, October Bth;
subject, Resolved: That an education
in a small college is more beneficial
to the individual than in a large
collcge. Affirmative: F. Munier,
S. Bryan, J. Wallace. Negative: M.
Malone; B. F. Bickel, C. R. Wright.
Botanical Society—Will meet
Thursday, October 4b, in the Botan
ical Library, room 461, at 4:20.
CI lAS. S. MORRIS, '08,
Secretary.
Track Men—All track men should
report for training on the old track
between it and 12 in the morning and
3 and 4 in the afternoon. T will be
there every day at those hours to
look after the men.
E. W. MOULTON, Trainer.
Faculty Women's Club—Will meet
at the residence of Mrs. Stillman, 2
Alvarado Row, 011 Monday, October
Bth, at 3 o'clock.

Chairman.
