# Schoolmasters Will Hold Banquet.
## 
### 
Professor A. H. Suzzallo, of the
Education department, will be chair
man of the evening at the quarterly
banquet of the Schoolmasters' Club
to be held in San Francisco early in
October. Professor E. P. Cubber
ley will deliver one of the leading ad
dresses of the occasion. A large
number of the Stanford faculty are
members of the club.
