# First Meeting of Roble Gym Club.
## 
###

Gymnasium Club was held Tuesday
evening for the purpose of recruiting J
members, as few remained from last I
semester, no try-out having then been j
held on account of the early closing |
of college. Sixteen reunited for the j
exercises conducted by Dr. Snow, j
The announcement of the successful j
candidates will be given in a few
days, when a business meeting will
be held tor the election of officers.
