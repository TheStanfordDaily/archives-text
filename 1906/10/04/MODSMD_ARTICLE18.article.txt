Faculty Women's Club—Will meet
at the residence of Mrs. Stillman, 2
Alvarado Row, on Monday, October
Bth, at 3 o'clock.
MRS. E. R. STILLMAN,
Chairman.
Plug Ugly.—A prize of twenty-five
dollars is offered for the best manu
script. All manuscripts must be
handed to C. N. Leach, '08, by Friday,
October sth.
Junior Women—Desiring class hats
leave their names before Friday noon
with Miss E. Murdock, room 63,
Roble.
1907 Class.—"Quad" assessments of
$2.00 may be paid at room 14 011 the
quad on Monday, Wednesday and
Friday from 1 130 to 2:30 to S. M.
Salisbury, or to S. S. Smith, room 29,
Encina.
Freshman Yell Committee—Will
receive yells by mail if addressed to
Box 299.
Roble Club—Will be at home Sat
urday evening, October 6th.
Civil .Engineering Society—Will
meet in room 70, Encina, Monday
evening, October Bth, at 8 o'clock.
H. K. SAVAGE, '07, President.
Assistant Yell Leader.—All those
desiring to try-out for assistant yell
leader will hand their names to R. S.
Kellogg, '07, immediately.
Song and Yell Competition—Will
close Saturday night, October 6th.
More songs and yells are wanted.
R. S. KELLOGG, '07.
