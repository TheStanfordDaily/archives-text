# Sophomores Must Pay Class Dues.
## 
### 
The Sophomore class is putting
forth every effort to collect delinquent
dues of last semester to contribute
the $50 to the track fund as promised.
Treasurer F. K. Jackson today ap
pointed nine collectors, representing
every community, and urged upon all
the necessity of forcing all delin
quents to pay tip. The collectors
are: Miss F. Forbes, Miss J. Gil
more, Miss Hazel Binder, C. Wynne,
S. C. Bias, D. W. Burbank, I). H
Walker, Jr., E. A. Smith and J. C.
Boyle.
