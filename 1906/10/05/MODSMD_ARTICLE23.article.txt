# New Tennis Courts are Finished.
## 
### 
Three new tennis courts near Roble
have been finished and the two old
ones have been repaired and oiled.
There are still two more new ones to
he finished, but this will not be done
for some time, as there is 110 im
mediate use for them. Many girl*
have turned out for tennis, and the
Freshman class has .a number of
promising players.
