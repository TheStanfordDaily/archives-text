Song and Yell Contest.—All those
who have handed in songs or yells
will appear before the committee in
the chapel Tuesday afternoon be
tween four and five o'clock.

wishing to try-out for class teams
send names immediately to Box 165,
and report at Roble Gymnasium on
Thursday at 4:30.

meet the members of the Freshman
Class tomorrow at 1:15 in room 68.

days, 1 t : 15 to or at room 44,
Encina.
Rooting Practice.—There will be
song practice on the bleachers at
5:00 Tuesday afternoon. Song books

will be handed out.
R. S. Kellogg, '07.
Euphronia—Will meet Tuesday
evening at 7:30 in room 241. After
an impromptu discussion, a debate
will follow—"Resolved, that the con
trol of municipal politics by that
class of politicians called "bosses"
does not make for the best govern
ment"; affirmative E. E. Young, '08,
W. H. Kerr, '07; negative, C. R.
Crider, '07, W. F. Herron, '08.
Greek 13. —An examination for re
moval of incompletes and conditions
in this course given last semester
will be held in room 21, Friday, Octo
ber 12, at 3:30 p. m.; text book, Gu
lick's Life of the Ancient Greeks.

bers meet for rehearsal in chapel,
