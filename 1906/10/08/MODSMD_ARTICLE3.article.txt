# MURPHY WILLING TO RETURN.
## Coach Will Come If Executive Committee Authorizes Terms. 
### 
Coach Murphy has wired to Grad
uate Manager Sales his intention to
come to Stanford under the new
terms proposed by F. Zimmerman,
'07. Zimmerman wrote to Murphy
a short time ago explaining that
while the Associated Students could
not afford to pay as large a salary as
Murphy asked, the Boat Club had de
termined to raise the surplus, and
asking him to keep his offer open un
til more definite arrangements could
be made.
