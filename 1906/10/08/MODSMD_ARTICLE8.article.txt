# Cap and Gown Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The Senior women's society of
"Cap and Gown' held a meeting Fri
day afternoon at the residenec of
Mrs. C. A. Duniway and elected offi
cers as follows: Miss Ellen Stadt
muller, '07, president; Miss Florette
Hodgdon, '07, vice-president; Miss
Claire Pedlar, '07, secretary-treasurer.
Miss Iva Miller, '07, was chosen to
represent the society in the Presi
dent's Conference of Young Women.
