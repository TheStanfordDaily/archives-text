# 1910 Debating League Meets.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the
Freshman Debating League last even
ing, several new members were voted
into the organization making the to
tal membership twenty-six.
The first debate, on the subject,
Resolved: That an education received
in a small college is more beneficial
to the individual than in a large col
lege ; was won by the affirmative side,
consisting of S. Bryan, F, Munier and
J. Wallace.
