1908 "Quad" Assessment.—May be
paid to W. C. Thiele at room 24, in
ner quadrangle, Tuesdays and Thurs
days, 11:15 to 12:30, or at room 44,
Encina.

Gymnasium Club—Will meet in
Encina Gymnasium tomorrow after
noon at 5:20.
Greek Syntax.— I should like to
meet in room 20 at 1 :30 tomorrow
any undergraduate who may care to
arrange for a series of informal lec
tures on Greek Syntax. These lec
tures will be given weekly at some
hour to be arranged. No credit will
be given for them.
A. T. MURRAY.
Geology Club—Will meet tomor
row night at 8 o'clock at the resi
dence of Professor J. P. Smith, 1251
Bryant street, Palo Alto. All up
perclassmen of the geology depart
ment are invited to attend.
Class in Settlements—And city
work of the Young Women's Chris
tian Association will meet Wednesday
at 4:30 p. m., at Madrono. All wom
en are invited.
Republican Voters.—All student
voters interested in the formation of
a Republican Club will meet in room
2, Law building, at 1:30, Wednesday
afternoon, October 10th.
