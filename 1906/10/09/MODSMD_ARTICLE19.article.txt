# Women's League Being Organized.
## 
### 
The executive committee of the
Women's League, consisting of Mrs.
David Starr Jordan, Mrs. J. C. Bran
ner, Mrs. E. D. Adams, Miss M.
Lockey, 02, Mrs. Caroline Edwards
Hall, '06, Miss B. Henzel, '06, Miss S.
Hyatt, '06, Miss I. Miller, '07, Miss E.
Branner, '07, and Miss E. Owen, '07,
met yesterday afternoon and discuss
ed plans for the reorganization of
the League. The committee will
meet again Thursday for the purpose
of revising the old constitution, which
will he presented for adoption at the
first meeting of the League.
