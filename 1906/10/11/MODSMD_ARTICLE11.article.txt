# Philologists Elect Officers.
## 
### 
At the last meeting of the Stan
ford Philological Association, Pro
fessor A. T. Murray, of the Greek de
partment, was elected president and
Professor J. Elmore, of the Latin
department, secretary.
The association wishes to announce
that all advanced students in the lite

tend the meetings, which are held
