# GOWAN IS CHAMPION
## SOPHOMORE REPRESENTATIVE WINS INTERCLASS TENNIS. Winner Defeats Delano in Final Round of Tournament.—Doubles Contest on October 20th. 
### 
A. J. Gowan, * '09, won the Univer
sity tennis championship yesterday
afternoon, defeating P. B. Delano,
'08, in the final round. The postponed
match between these players was
started 011 the Encina courts at 4
o'clock. Gowan proved to be in the
best of form and forced his opponent
well into the back court, cutting-off
and killing Delano's returns at the net
in good style.
Only during the first games did
Delano succeed in breaking through
the winners strong net work; after
the fourth game Gowan was rarely
passed and very seldom failed to kill
the lobs which were not deeper than
the service line.
Gowan has played consistent tennis
throughout the interclass try-outs
and the tournament proper. He
was a member of last years varsity
singles team and won his numerals
on his Freshman doubles team. Gow
an registers from Los Angeles and is
a member of the Delta Tau Delta
fraternity.
The try-outs for the class doubles
teams arc being steadily run off and
the tournament proper will be held
a week from next Saturday. The
summary of play in the interclass
singles follows:
Sophomore-Freshman.—A. J. Gow
«'in, '09, defeated T. C. Henry, '10, 4-6,
6-4. 6-3, 4-6, 6-3.
Senior-Junior.—P. B. Delano, '08,
defeated S. M. Salisbury, '07, 6-2, 6-4,
6-4.
Junior-Sophomore.—A. J. Gowan,
'to, defeated P. B. Delano, '08, 5-7,
6-4. 7-5, 6-2.
