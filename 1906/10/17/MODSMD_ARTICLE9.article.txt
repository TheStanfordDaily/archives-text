# Officers of Roble Gym. Club Chosen.
## 
### 
At a business meeting of the Roble
Gymnasium Club, held last evening,
the following officers were elected
for the ensuing semester: Miss Pearl
M. Horn, '07, president; Miss Pauline
Gartzmann, '07, vice-president; Miss
Lilian Ramsay, '07, secretary, and
Miss Rosa Bell, '07, treasurer.
