# Japanese Alumni Add To Gerdes Fund.
## 
### 
The Japanese Alumni Association
of Tokio has given nine yen, equal
to $4.50, to the Otto Gerdes fund.
The subscription was made through
K. Otaki, '94, professor in the Im
perial Military Academy, and presi
dent of the Japanese Alumni Associa
tion. Otaki was the first Japanese
Stamford graduate.
