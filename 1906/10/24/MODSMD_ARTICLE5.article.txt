# Women's Tennis Tournament
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Tennis Com
mittee of the Women's Athletic As
sociation this afternoon it was decid
ed to hold a scratch tournament Sat
urday afternoon on the Roble Courts.
Lists for entries will be posted in
Roble Hall, Roble Gymnasium and
the Rest Room. The Women's Ath
letic Association desires that all wom
en interested in tennis, whether desir
ing to join the squad or not, will
turn out for Saturday's tournament,
as this will give the committee their
only basis for handicapping in the
big tournament for the racquet, to be
held the end of November.
