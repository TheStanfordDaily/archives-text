# Nestoria Will Hold Banquet.
## 
### 
The Nestoria Literary Society will
hold a banquet in Fraternity Hall,
Palo Alto, Saturday evening, Oct.
27tli. A. R. Fletcher, '06, will act
as toastmaster. The speakers will be
C. A. Beardsley, '06, N. F. Bradley,
'06, W. A. Campbell, '07, and G. !!.
Bush, '09.
