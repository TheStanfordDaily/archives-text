# Teachers Wanted for Philippines.
## 
### 
The U. S. Civil Service Commission
has announced an examination for
November 30th to till present vacan
cies and such others as may occur
among the teachers in the Philippine
service, with salaries between $r,ooo
and $1,200 per annum. The examina
tion will consist of ten subjects and
two days of seven hours each will
be allowed.
