# Freshmen to Repay Treasurer.
## 
### 
The Freshman class decided today
to reimburse A. H. Myer, the class
treasurer, who was robbed of the class
fund. An assessment of ten cents was
placed on each member of the class.
Money will be received by A. H.
Myer, H. M. Tuttle and B. F. Bickel,
or address Box 295, campus postoffice.
