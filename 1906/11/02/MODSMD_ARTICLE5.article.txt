# Government. Wants Teachers.
## 
### 
The Director of Education in the
Philippine Islands is very desirous of
securing a number of Stanford men
and women as teachers. Science
teachers in the high schools are es
pecially desired. Those appointed
will receive $1200 for the first year,
with an increase later and an oppor
tunity to return by way of Europe at
the end of the session. An examina
tion for candidates will be held in
the Christmas holidays. Ten science
teachers and one hundred other
teachers arc desired.
