# Play to Begin Promptly at Eight.
## 
### 
The last rehearsal of the cast of
"Two Gentlemen of Verona" was
held this afternoon in Assembly Hall,
and all is in readiness for tonight's
production. The management has an
nounced that the curtain will rise
promptly at 8 o'clock and that all
spectators arriving after that time
will not he seated by the ushers until
after the first act.
