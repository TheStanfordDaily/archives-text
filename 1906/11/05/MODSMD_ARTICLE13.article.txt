# Freshmen Defeat Sophomores.
## 
### 
The interclass basketball game
played Friday afternoon between the
Sophomores and Freshmen was won
by the Freshmen by a score of 9 to
2. The game was well played, tbe
Freshmen showing excellent team
work. Miss A. Wright, '10. was sub
stituted for Miss S. Bundy in the
second half. The Sophomore team
was weakened by the absence of Miss
M. Couch, captain, and Miss J.
Johnson, Miss B. Maine, and Miss E.
Atkinson substituted respectively.
Miss Elsie Owen, '07. acted as time
keeper and Miss M. Warde. '08, and
Miss M. Monteith, '08, as umpires.
