# Dr. Jordan to Go East.
## 
### 
As one of the trustees of the Car
negie Fund for the case of aged pro
fessors, President Jordan will go East
and attend the annual meeting of the
directors of the board in New York,
the 21st of this month. Dr. Jordan
will not leave the campus until after,
the intercollegiate game and prob
ably not before the T2th or 15th, The
itinerary of his trip has not been set
tled but it is understood that he will
speak at several of the large cities
en route and will journey by way of
Washington.
