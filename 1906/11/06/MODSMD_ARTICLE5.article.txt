# California Students Work On Field
## 
###

will be in readiness for the varsity
game on Saturday, the student body
has been called upon to render assis
tance and will work in shifts of 125
men. The regular force of laborers
will work at night by electric lights.

000 cubic yards of earth have yet to
be moved.
