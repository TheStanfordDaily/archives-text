# JUNIORS WIN FROM SENIORS
## 1907 Basket-ball Team Defeated with Score of 8-7. 
### 
In the second of the series of the
interclass basket-ball games, played
between the Seniors and Juniors yes
terday afternoon, the Juniors won by
a score of 8-7. Excellent work was
done by both teams. Within a short
time the third and final game of the
series will be played between the
Juniors and Freshmen.
