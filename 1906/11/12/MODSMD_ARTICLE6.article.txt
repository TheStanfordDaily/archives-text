# To Address Social Service Club.
## 
### 
The Social Service Club will hold
a regular meeting for members at the
Phi Gamma Delta house, Tuesday
evening, at 8 o'clock. Fremont Older,
of the San Francisco "Bulletin," will
speak on the political phases of news
paper work and its influence in devel
oping a proper attitude toward the
duties of citizenship.
The next meeting will probably be
an open one, and Mr. Fairfax H.
Wheelan has been invited to make the
address.
