# Special Thanksgiving Rates.
## 
### 
The usual vacation rates for the
Thanksgiving recess have been an
nounced by the Southern Pacific as
going into effect on the 28th and 29th,
good until December 3d. Special ar
rangements will be made whereby
rates to extreme points in California
will begin on November 27th.
