# Enthusiastic Alumni Rally.
## 
### 
The Alumni Association of the Uni
versity held its pre-football rally last
Friday evening, at Campi's Restau
rant, in San Francisco.
Dinner was served, after which
singing, yelling and speechmaking was
indulged in. Dr. Julius B. Franken
heimer, 96, presided, and Charles K.
Field, '95, rendered a number of orig
inal poems. Among the speakers
were Scott Calhoun, '95, Prof. A. H.
Suzzallo, '99, G. E. Crothers, '95, and
E. A. Cunha, '06.
