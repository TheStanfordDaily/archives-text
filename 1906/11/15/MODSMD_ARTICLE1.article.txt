# SWEATERS AWARDED
## EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE VOTES EMBLEMS FOR RUGBY. Gold Footballs Given and Eight-Oared Races Discussed Last Night. 
### 
i he Executive Committee last night
awarded all the football emblems for
this season, including the Varsity
"S," the black German "S" for the
substitutes 011 the side-lines, and the
gold footballs for watch fobs. The
privilege of wearing four white stars
on his sweater was awarded to A. J.
Chalmers, '07, in recognition of his
playing four years 011 the Varsity
football team.
The fifteen members of the Varsity,
awarded "S'' sweaters, are, E. P. Stott,
'07, J. X. Stanford, '07, G. J. Presley,
'07, C. F. Laumeister, '07, A. J. Chal
mers, '07, A. A. Molfmo, '07, L. M.
Edwards, '07, K. E. Fenton, '08, W.
Koerner, '08, J. R. Pemberton, '08, J.
R. Holman, '09, E. R. Minturn, '09,
C. C. Owen, '09, G. E. Cheda, '10, C.
A. Thorpe, '10. The five men to re
ceive the black German "S" follow:
J. C. Macfarland, '07, R. A. Conant,
'08, G. D. Hussey, '09, W. H. Stark,
'09, A. B. Cook, '10.
A. J. Chalmers, '07, played half-back
011 the Varsities of 1903, 1904, and
1905, captaining the last named. This
year as his fourth 011 the Varsity, in
which lie held down the position of
center three-quarters, entitles him to
the emblem of four stars.
The gold football, an ornamental
watch fob, was voted to the members
of the Varsity fifteen and also to
Coach J. F. Lanagan, '00, Assistant
coach L. P. Bansbach, '05, Graduate
Manager D. D. Sales, '06, and Trainer
E. D. Moulton. A slight change in
wording may be made on account of
Rugby but the footballs will probably
be similar in design and engraving to
those of last year, which bore the
year, the inscription "Pacific Coast
Champions," the name and position of
the player, with the score of the game.
On the other side there will be a
raised block "S" in red.
The proposition advanced by rowing
coach "Dan" Murphy, and which has
been taken up by the Stanford Boat
Club, of having cight-oarcd inter
collegiate races, was discussed by the
Executive Committee. No action was
taken.
