# Geology Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Geology Club
held last night officers for next sem
ester were elected as follows: presi
dent, B. Saunders, '06, vice-president,
R. Crandall, '06, secretary, H. Rhyne,
'07. Plans for a trip to Corral Hol
low, near Livermore, on Nov. 29th,
were discussed. F. W. Turner, '08,
read a paper on the Owen's River pro
ject.
