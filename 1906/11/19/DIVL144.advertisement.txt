WILSON'S
HtiUE WEEK.
To introduce our choice assortment of Blue Tinted stationery we will
make a special discount of 20 per cent on all Blue stock for one week only,
beginning Monday Nov. 19, 1906.
This includes: Bluite Linen Lawn, Cerulean University Linen, in pound
packages, Alexis Azure Bond, in pound packages, Crane's Azure Bond,
Hickory and Titan Bond, in Pads. THE BOOKSTORE
