# WOMEN'S LEAGUE MEETS.
## Will Present Petition for Compulsory Fees to Trustees. 
### 
At the meeting of the Women's
League on last Friday, the president
reported on the clubhouse plan. The
committee is drawing up a plan, and
the petition is to be placcd with the
Trustees of the University, concern
ing a compulsory fee on registration,
at the meeting to be held in January.
It was also reported that of the 320
women having thus far voted the vote
had been unanimously in favor of the
plan. Several amendments to the
constitution were adopted. The treas
urer reported a deficit of $6.00, and
that the semester dues of 25 cents
now payable. There was 110 regular
program.
