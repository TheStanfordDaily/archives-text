# ANNOUNCEMENTS.
## 
### 
English ii.—Students in English 11
are requested to consult the English
bulletin-board for a notice regarding
the completion of written work.
S. S. Seward, Jr.
Gymnasium Club—Will hold a reg
ular meeting in Encina gymnasium
at 5:20 011 Wednesday afternoon.
University Orchestra—Will meet in
chapel Wednesday at 7:15.
E. G. Sheibley.
Junior Class—Will meet tomorrow
in room 268 at 1:15.
Football Men.—All old football
suits are to be turned in to Graduate
Manager D. 1). Sales immediately.
New Books.—The following has
been received at the Bookstore ; Cases
011 Code Pleading, Hinton.
Senior Women—Will meet at 1:15
Wednesday, on the Roble steps for
class picture.
Sword and Sandals—Will meet at
the Delta Upsilon house, Tuesday
evening, at 9 o'clock.
F. 11. Bernard, '07, Secretary.
Social Service Club.—Social Prob
lems Section will meet at residence of
Rev. D. Charles Gardner, Thursday
evening, November 22d, at 8 o'clock.
