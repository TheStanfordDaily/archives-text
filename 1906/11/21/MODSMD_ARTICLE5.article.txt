# Social Service Section Meets.
## 
### 
At the last meeting of the educa
tional section of the Social Service
Club, fifteen members were present,
most of them men. There was an in
formal discussion on "The influence
of a group of educated people in the
midst of neglected communities, and
the role of human example, sociability,
congeniality and sympathy as settled
socializing forces." The next meet
ing, the last of the semester, will take
place at the Alpha Phi house on De
cember 6th.
