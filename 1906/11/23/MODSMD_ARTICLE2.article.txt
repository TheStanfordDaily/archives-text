# CARNOT SUBJECT CHOSEN.
## Question Will Concern "The French Government and Socialism". 
### 
The general subject for the Carnot
debate, which will he held next sem
ester, was announced today by the
joint committee, on which Stanford
is represented by Professors C. A.
Duniway and R. M. Alden, and Cal
ifornia by Professors Bacon and
Flaherty. The subject is, "The
French Republic and Socialism.'
From this general field the specific
question will be announced two hours
before the debate.
Because of the small number of
candidates for the Carnot team this
year no preliminaries are to be held,
and the thirteen men who have
handed in their names will be allowed
to enter the finals, which arc to be
held some time early in January.
