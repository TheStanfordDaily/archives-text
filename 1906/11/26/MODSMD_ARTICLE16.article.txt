# Freshmen Defeat Juniors, 10-8.
## 
### 
In the basket-ball game played last
Friday afternoon between the Juniors
and Freshmen, on the Roble Court,
the latter won by a score of 10-8.
This was the last of the series of
interclass games, the squad of 1910
holding the championship. The game
was closely played, most of the points
being made by free throws. Miss
Jane Spalding, '06, acted as referee;
Misses S. Hyatt, '06, and J. Derby, '07,
umpires.
