# Euphronia to Hold Banquet.
## 
### 
Euphronia Literary Society will
hold a banquet tomorrow evening at
the University Restaurant. This will
be the last meeting of the society
this year. G. Knupp, '07, will act as
toastmaster and the speakers will be
D. D. Sales, '06, E. A. Cunha, '06, I. B.
Cross, of the Economics department
and W. F. Herron, '08.
