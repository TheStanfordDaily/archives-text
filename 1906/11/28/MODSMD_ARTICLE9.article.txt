# Bonnheim Result Friday.
## 
### 
The ten manuscripts submitted in
the Bonnheim Dissertation Contest
have been read by two of the judges
and are now in the hands of Professor
T. B. Voblen, of the Economics de
partment, for the final reading. This
will be completed within two days and
the final results of the contest will be
posted on the bulletin board in front
of the President's office, on Friday.
