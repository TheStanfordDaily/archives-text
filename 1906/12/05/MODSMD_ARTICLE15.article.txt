# French Club Meeting Postponed.
## 
### 
The last meeting of the French
Club, which was to be held next
Monday evening, has been postponed
until next semester, owing to the
inability of the committee on pro
gram to procure the services of M.
dnßois, of the University of Califor
nia, as a speaker. The next meeting
will be held at the Kappa Kappa
Gamma house, on Monday, January
14th.
