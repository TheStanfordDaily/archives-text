# Club Called "La Tertulia Espanol."
## 
### 
The Spanish Club, which was or
ganized this semester, held a meeting
last evening and decided that the
club shall be called "La Tertulia Es
panol;" that it shall meet every two
weeks, and that qualification for mem
bership shall be similiar to the quali
fication required by the clubs of the
French and the German departments.
The officers of the first semester were
unanimously reelected for the re
mainder of the year. They are as fol
lows: H. W. Reed, '07, president;
Miss M. Hyatt, '08, vice-president; F.
W. Crawford, '09. secretary-treasurer.
