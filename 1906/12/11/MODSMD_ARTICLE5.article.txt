# English Club Meets Wednesday.
## 
### 
The final meeting of the English
Club will he held at the Alpha Phi
house, Wednesday evening, at 8
o'clock. Dr. G. H. Campbell, of
the Zoology department, will speak
on his recent trip around the
world, and especially on India,
Burma, and Ceylon. The election
of new officers and members of the
executive committee will take
place. The executive committee
will meet at 7:30 o'clock.
