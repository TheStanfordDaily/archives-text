# JORDAN PLANS ANOTHER TRIP.
## Will Speak Before Teachers' Association in Oklahoma. 
### 
Although Dr. Jordan has but
recently returned from an extensive
trip throughout the East, he will
soon leave the University again.
This time he will go to Shawnee,
Oklahoma, where the Teachers
Association will hold its conven
tion on the 26th and 27th of this
month. Dr. Jordan has accepted
an invitation to address this as
semblage, but lie has not yet decided
on his topic. The President will
be absent from the campus about a
week, leaving shortly before Christ
mas and returning before New
Year's.
