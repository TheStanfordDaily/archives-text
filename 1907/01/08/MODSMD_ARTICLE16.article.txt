# "Chaparral" Is Issued Today.
## 
### 
The first issue of "Chaparral" was
placed on sale today. The principal
art features are a sketch by V. F. Bel
lows, '09, entitled "The College Man's
Dream," and a drawing by R. E.
Snodgrass. Editorially this issue
comments on the spirit shown at the
beginning of a new semester, ex
presses gratification that the Trustees
are going to install lights on the
Campus and "knocks" the"Daily Palo
Alto" and final examinations.
