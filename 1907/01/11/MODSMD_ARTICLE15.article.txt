# Palo Alto Women to Meet.
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of all Palo
Alto women students in the parlor of
the Congregational Church, corncr of
Waverly and Hamilton Avenues, Palo
Alto, Saturday afternoon, January 12,
at 3:00 o'clock. Steps will probably
be taken toward the organization of
a Palo Alto women students' club.
Dr. Jordan will address the meeting.
