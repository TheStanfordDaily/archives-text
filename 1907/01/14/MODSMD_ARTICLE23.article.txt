# Registration Shows Decrease.
## 
### 
The total enrollment of students in
the University up to Friday, January
11, was this year only 1321 as against
1476 at the same time last January.
Of these sixty-five are new students,
while the 1476 of last year repre
sented eighty-nine new students. The
figures this year, however, show an
increased percentage of old students
returned to the University.
