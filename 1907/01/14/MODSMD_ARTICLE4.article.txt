# TELEPHONES FOR THE QUAD.
## Sunset Company Planning Installation of New System in Near Future. 
### 
An enlargement of the Palo Alto
branch of the Sunset Telephone &
Telegraph Company both as to offic
ces and service to include the Quad
rangle is a possibility in the near fu
ture. As a part of the expansion
contemplated, the company has under
consideration a telephone system for
the Quad, which will make it possible
for the heads of the departments to
communicate with each other and
with the other subscribers. The di
rectors of the company arc estimat
ing the cost attached to the instal
ment of such a system and when the
figures are ready they will be sub
mitted to the Board of Trustees of
the University for their decision.
