# One Candidate for Scholarship.
## 
### 
C. S. Forncrook, '10, registered in
the Greek department, from Los An
geles, is the only candidate from Stan
ford for the Rhodes Scholarship this
year.
The examinations take place Thurs
day and Friday of this week. The
requirements aside from a qualifying
grade in the examinations include a
"fondness for and success in manly
out-door sports," and general excel
lent qualities of character.
A cable, announcing the successful
candidate, should be received from
London not later than March 1. The
successful candidate is expected to
take up residence at Oxford next Oc
tober.
