# FIRST ASSEMBLY TOMORROW.
## Jordan and Hempl Will Address Student Body Gathering. President May Speak on Football Situation. 
### 
President David Starr Jordan and
Professor George Hempl, head of the
German department, will be the
speakers at the first student body
assembly of the semester, which, is
scheduled for tomorrow morning at
11:15. Professor Hempl will speak

on "Phases of University Life," and
Dr. Jordan will deliver his address to
the entering Freshmen, "Young Men
That Are Wanted." It is also gen
erally understood that Dr. Jordan
will touch upon the present status of
the football situation.
