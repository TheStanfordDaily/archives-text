# Literary Society to Meet.
## 
### 
Euphronia Literary Society will
meet for the first time this semester
on next Tuesday evening, January _>j.
A set speech on the subject, "The
Brownsville Affair," will be given by
K. E. Young, '09. The subject for the
regular debate will be, "Resolved,
That, granting its constitutionality,
Congress should enact a graduated
income tax." A. A. Murphy, '08, and
G. Knupp, '07, will represent the af
firmative side of the question, and
the negative will be defended by R.
H. Borland, '08, and W. C. Shelton,
'08.
