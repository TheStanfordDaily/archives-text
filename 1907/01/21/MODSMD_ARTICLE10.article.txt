# Date Set for Interscholastic.
## 
### 
The date of the Interscholastic
Track Meet has been set for March
23. This will he the first field meet
of any importance on the new track.
The meet promises to be the largest
academic track contest ever held on
the Pacific Coast. Secretary Lau
meister is already in receipt of over
thirty letters of inquiry, and circulars
containing all the necessary informa
tion'will be sent immediately to every
preparatory school in the State.
