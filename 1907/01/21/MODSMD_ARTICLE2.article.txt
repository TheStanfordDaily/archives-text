# Nestoria Adopts Pin Design.
## 
### 
The Nestoria Literary Society has
adopted a design for a pin which is
to be the emblem of the society. At
the meeting Tuesday night orders
were taken for this pin, which is to
be in the shape of the Memorial Arch,
the gateway of the arch being filled
with an Sand the upper part cover
ed with a banner on which is in
scribed the word Nestoria.
