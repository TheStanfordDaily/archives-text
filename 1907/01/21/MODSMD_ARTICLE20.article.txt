# Leach Appoints Committees.
## 
### 
President C. N. Leach, of the Jun
ior class has appointed the following
committee to take charge of the class
election Wednesday: R. H. Borland,
G. C. Coe, F. H. Abbott, K. L. Fen
ton, and F. W. Turner. The books
of Treasurer F. 11. Abbott will be
audited by a committee consisting of
W. C. Theile, E. N. Smith, and C. C.
Johnson.
