# Archaeologists Honor Fairclough.
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough, of the
Latin department, has been elected
secretary for the Pacific Coast, of the
Archaeological Institute of America.
Professor Fairclough was to have at
tended the recent meeting of the In
stitute, which was held in Washing
ton, D. C. He would have lectured
before many of the noted archaeo
logists of America, but was prevented
by illness from taking the trip East,
which would have been an arduous
one in his low state of health.
