Social Service Club. — Professor
buzzallo's section meets at the Alpha
Phi house, Thursday at 7:3° o'clock.
Kappa Alpha Theta. —At home Sat
urday evening, January twenty-sixth.
Tennis CluJ? —Will meet in room 11,
Thursday evening, at 7:15.
Botany Club —Will meet Thursday,
January 24, at 7:15 p. m., in the li
brary of the Botany building.
Daily Palo Alto Staff—Will meet at
Chi Psi house, Friday at 8.
German Club —Will meet Thursday
evening, at Madrono Hall.
New Book.—The following has
been received by the Bookstore: "Ma
chine Design," Smith and Marx.
Foil and Mask—Will meet Friday
afternoon at 5:20, in Encina gymna
sium. Election of officers.
P. E. Millar. Sec.
1908 "Quad" Assessments —Must be
paid at once. Hours: 1:30 to 2:30,
daily, room 14, Mathematics building
or room 44, Encina Hall.
Golf Club—-Will meet to elect vice
president, Thursday, at 1:15, in room
50. All persons wishing to become
members are requested to attend.
E. C. Sterling, '08.
Y. M. C. A. Labor Bureau.—Stu
dents desiring work of any kind, on
or about the campus, should apply at
the Employment Bureau, room 78,
Encina, after 7 p. m.
E. E. Lewis, Director.
Band—Will meet Thursday evening,
January 24, at 7:30, in Engineering
building. "Quad" picture to be con
sidered. Members will bring instru
ments.
D. N. Richards, '07, Leader.
