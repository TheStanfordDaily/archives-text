# No Action By Women's Conference.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the President's
Conference of Young Women, held
last evening at Dr. Jordan's home
a number of matters of vital interest
to college women were discussed, but
nothing definite was done. Two new
members were voted in as represent
atives from the recently formed Palo
Alto Women's Club. Miss M. Cun
ningham, '08, has taken the place of
Miss J. Henry, '09, as the represent
ative of the Kappa Kappa Gamma
Sorority.
