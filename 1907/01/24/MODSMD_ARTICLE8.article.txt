# FOUR WOMEN HONORED
## CAP AND GOWN ELECTS UPPER-CLASS MEMBERS. Misses Derby, Squire, Branner, and Baker Are Elected By Women's Organization. 
###

society, elected four new members at
a meeting held yesterday. The wo
men thus honored arc Misses J.

ner, '07, and M. C. Baker, '08.

men's Athletic Association and a
member of the Delta Gamma soror

Association and was recently elected
secretary of the Senior class. Miss
Squire majors in the Physiology de
partment and registers from Visalia.
Elsie Branner is secretary of the
Social Service Club and a member
of the Kappa Alpha Theta sorority.
She is a History major, registering
from Stanford University.
Mary Caroline Baker also belongs
to tlie Kappa Alpha Theta sorority.
She is prominent in the Y. W. C. A.
and in women's athletics. Miss Ba

partment, and registers from Point
