# Carnot Judges Chosen.
## 
### 
Albert Bonnheim of Sacramento,
Reverend H. H. McQuilken of San
Jose, and Judge Peter Shields of Sac
ramento, are the three men who will
pick the winner of the Carnot Medal
in the annual contest with California,
in Assembly Hall next Friday even
ing.
Bonnheim is the founder of the
Bonnheim prizes awarded annually,
for dissertations and discussions on
ethical subjects, both at Stanford and
California. Dr. McQuilken is pastor
of the First Presbyterian Church in
San Jose. Judge Shields is judge of
the Superior Court of Sacramento
County.
