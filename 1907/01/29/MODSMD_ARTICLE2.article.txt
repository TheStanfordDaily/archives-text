# ARE STILL ON THEIR WAY.
## Cinders for New Track Shipped Saturday Fail to Putin Their Appearance. 
### 
Work on the new track is still be
ing delayed by the non-arrival on tin
campus of cinders from the Pacific
Mail Company. All the track men
are very much interested, and active
work on the part of the athletes is
being somewhat retarded by the fail
ure of the Southern Pacific to ship
the consignment. Manager Sales
went to San Francisco last Saturday
to arrange with the company to ship
the cinders immediately.
Sales said this afternoon: "The
company promised Saturday that the
cinders would be shipped here Mon
day. 1 watched the loading to make
sure they were ready for shipment.
So far they have not arrived, and no
definite time can be given for theii
arrival. They may coma today or to
morrow.
