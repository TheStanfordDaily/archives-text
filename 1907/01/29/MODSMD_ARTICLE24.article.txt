# Big Show in Town Tonight.
## 
### 
Tonight at 8:15, Mullen's Hall, Pair
Alto, will show the original copy
writer's pictures of the Gans-Nelson
fight, which took place at Goldfield,
Nevada, last Labor Day. Views of
Goldtield will also be shown.
This entertainment is enjoyed as
much by tlie ladies as the men.
Admission 35c.
