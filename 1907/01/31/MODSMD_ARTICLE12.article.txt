paid at once. Hours: 1:30 to 2:30,
daily, room 14, Mathematics building
or room 44, Encina Hall.

course 4a, in Gardiner, pages 1-500,

later. E. R. Palmer, Capt.

meet in room 231 at 7:30 next Mon

Ilettman.

invited.
