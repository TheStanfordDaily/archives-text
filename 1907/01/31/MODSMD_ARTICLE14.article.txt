# Reception at Xazmin House.
## 
### 
Through the kindness of Mrs. D.
S. Jordan, Xazmin house will he
thrown open to the members of the
Young Womens' Christian Associa
tion next Saturday afternoon, when
they will entertain at a reception to
all the women of the University.
The Board of Advisers of the Asso
ciation will assist in receiving. The
committee in charge of the affair con
sists of Miss J. McGilvray, '07, chair
man, Miss E. Crumby, '07, Miss I.
Johnston, '07, Miss K. Barnes, '10,
and Miss E. Pearsall, '08.
