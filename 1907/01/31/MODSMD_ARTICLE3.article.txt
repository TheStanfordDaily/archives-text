# Gardner Secures Special Preachers.
## 
### 
Rev. D. C. Gardner hopes to obtain
the services of a special clergyman
on at least one Sunday a month, from
now until the end of the semester.
The following men have already
promised to preach: Rev. J. W. Baer,
president of Occidental College; Rev.
J. M. McLeod, of Southern California;
Rev. F. G. Pcabody, chaplain of Har
vard University and a recent lecturer
at the University of Berlin; and Rev.
A. A. Morrison, of Portland, Oregon.
