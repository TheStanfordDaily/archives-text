# TO PLAN JUNIOR DAY
## MURPHY NAMES THREE 1908 COMMITTEES. Men and Women Who Will Have Charge of Farce, Day, and Prom Appointed. 
### 
President A. A. Murphy has named
the committees who will have charge
of the festivities of Junior day. They
are composed as follows:
Junior Farce.—J. J. Hill, J. F. Reilly,
L. Newland, Miss M. Hyatt, Miss M.
F. Osborne, Miss A. Ellerbeck, Miss
V. A. Steele, E. J. Swift, Chairman.
Junior Day.—J. R. Pemberton, W.
F. Eastham, D. H. Ferry, N. A. John
son, R. E. Hodge, K. L. Fenton, C. C.
Johnson, chairman.
Junior Prom.—C. L. Bradley, C. N.
Leach, G. C. Coe, Miss J. E. Clithero,
Miss M. E. Hutchins, Miss M. MOll
- Miss E. L. Murdock, F. W.
Turner, chairman.
