The Harvard Medical School
Boston, Mass.
With the completion of the new buildinps, which were dedicated Sept< mber 25th, IPOB,
this school now has facilities and fquipment for teaching and research in the various
branches of medicine probably unsurpassed iD this country. Of the five buildings, four
are devoted entirely to laboratory teaching arid research. The nunertus hospitals of
Boston afford abundant opportunities for clinical instiuction in medicine at d surgery.
COURSE FOR TIIE DEGREE OF M. 1).
A four years' course open to bachelors of art, literature, philosophy or science, and to
persons of equivalent standing, loads to the degree of M.D. The studies of the fourth year
are wholly elective; they include labortory subjects, general surgery, and the special clini
cal branches.
The next school year extends from September 26, 1907, to June 29, 1908.
