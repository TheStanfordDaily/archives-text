# Euphronia Meets Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The debate in the Euphronia Lit
erary Society tomorrow night will be
on the subject: "Resolved, That
a Competent Board of Engineers
should be established by the Federal
Government to fix the Responsibility
for Railroad Accidents, and Decide
Upon Methods to be Used in Avoid
ing Such Accidents." Affirmative: E.
E. Young, '09, and P. J. Batkin, '10.
Negative: G. Knupp, '07, and S. Bry
an, '10. Preceding the regular pro
gram there will be an impromptu de
bate.
