# Geological Society Elects Members.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Geological
Society last evening the following
were elected to regular membership
in the society. E. K. Soper, '08, C.
L. Severy, '08, A. C. Luhrs, '08, A.
D. Hughes, '08, F. W. Turner, '08,
C. Randall, 08, J. R. Pemberton, '08,
and C. T. Chandler, '08. E. W. Rice,
'08, Assistant Instructor in Assaying
was elected to associate membership.
