# Franklin at Monetary Congress.
## 
### 
Professor E. C. Franklin, of the
Chemistry department, is at present
in Washington, D. C., on a two weeks'
leave of absence. He is attending a
meeting of the Monetary Commission
there, and during his stay he will
address that body on some phase of
his line of work. Dr. Franklin will
he back at the University by the end
of next week.
