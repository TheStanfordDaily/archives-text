# Three Ball Games This Week.
## 
### 
The Varsity nine will play three
games this week, two on the campus
diamond and one in Oakland. On
Wednesday the Varsity nine will
cross bats with the Elks' team.
Washington's Birthday will take the
Cardinal nine to Oakland to play a
second game with St. Mary's, and
on Saturday Heitmuller's aggregation
will play the Varsity here.
