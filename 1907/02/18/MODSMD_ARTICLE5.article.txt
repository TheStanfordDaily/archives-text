# Euphronia Will Hold Meeting.
## 
### 
The subject for debate before the
Euphronia Literary Society tomor
row night is, "Resolved, That the
Separation of Church and State in
France was for the Best Interest of
the French Republic." Affirmative:
A. A. Murphy, '08, and S. J. Talbot,
'10. Negative: J. E. Stewart, '07, and
F. F. Munier, '10. The debate will be
preceded by four impromptu speeches
by members of the Society.
