The Harvard Medical School
Boston, Mass.
With the completion of the now buildings, which were dedicated September2sth, 1900,
this school now has facilities and equipment for teaching and research in the various
branches of medicine probably unsurpassed in this country Of the tlve buildines, four
are devoted entirely to laboratory teaching and research. The numenus hospitals of
lioston afford abundant opportunities for clinical instiuction in medicine and surgery.
COURSE FOR THF, DEGREE OF M. 1).
A four years' courso open to bachelors of art, literature, philosophy or scienco. and to
persons of equivalent standing leads to the degree of M.D. The studies of the fourth year
are wholly elective; they include labortory subjects, general surgery, and the special clini
cal branches.
The next school year extends from September 20, 1007, to June 29. 1908.
