# Huberich to Give Summer Course.
## 
### 
Professor C. H. Huberich, of the
department of Law, has accepted an
invitation to give a course in law at
the next summer session of the Uni
versity of California. The work to
be given will be either a course in
Constitutional Law or a course in
Conflict of Laws.
