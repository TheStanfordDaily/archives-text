# ANNOUNCEMENTS.

Senior Farce.—A prize of $50 will
be awarded for the best musical or
straight farce, handed in on or before
March 15. The undersigned reserves
the right to reject any or all farces.
All farces submitted to be typewrit
ten and must be complete.
Senior Farce Committee.
Botany Vll.—There will be an ex
amination in Botany VII, covering
the subject up to the present lecture,

next Thursday, February 26, at 1:30,
in room 460. D. H. Campbell.
English 5, Section lII.—A make-up
examination will be held on Wednes
day, February 27, at 2 p. m., in room
210. W. D. Briggs.
Press Club—Will meet Thursday,
usual time and place.
Senior Committees. —Meeting of
Senior Week Committee and chair
men of all other Senior committees
at Zeta Psi house, Wednesday even
ing at 8:30.
Geology Club—Will meet in the
Club room of the Stanford Inn, Wed
nesday evening, February 27, at 8.
Spanish Club—Will meet at the
Delta Gamma house, Thursday night,
at 8.

the Senior women of Y. W. C. A.
Wednesday, at 4:30, in room 44,
Roble.

ciation work will not meet tomorrow.

placed on sale Tuesday, March 5.

"Heredity and Its Laws."

building, 4:30 Tuesday.

be paid by March 1. Payment may
be made in room 14, quad, from 1130

in the chapel.
