# GEORGE WILL SPEAK HERE
## Son of Famous Single Tax Advocate to Address Assembly Early in March. 
### 
While the exact time of the next
University Assembly has as yet not
been determined, Henry George, Jr.,
will be the principal speaker, and a
date during the first week in March,
suitable to his convenience, will be
selected. George is going to stop
over at the University for a short time
on his way to San Francisco, and at
that time it will be definitely decided
upon what date the Assembly is to
occur.
Henry George is probably best
known to the public as a reform jour
nalist and as an author. He is the
originator of the George Junior Re
public, at which institution he is put
ting his political ideas into practice.
He is the son of Henry George, the
famous single tax advocate.
