# FIRST ROUND NEARLY OVER.
## Preliminary Tryouts for the Intercollegiate Team Begun on Saturday. Delano Beats Henry. 
### 
Play in the first tryout for the In
tercollegiate tennis team was begun
Saturday. Eight matches were de
cided, three of them being won by de
fault. The playing was good, consid
ering that it was the first tournament
of the year. The best match of the
day was that between P. B. Delano,
'08, and T. C. Henry, '10. Delano
won, but only after three sets of hard,
close tennis. The tournament will be
continued as soon as the weather per
mits. The summary of Saturday's
play follows:
Preliminary round —Clark beat
Mathewson by default; all others bye.
First round —Henry beat Parton by
default; Delano beat Salisbury, 6-1,
6-4; Mann beat Atwood, 6-1, 6-2; Clark
beat Alexander .by default; Moore
beat Burr, 4-6, 6-4, 6-0; Mackey beat
Brackett, 6-4, 7-9, 7-5; two matches
yet to be played. Second round —
Delano beat Henry, 6-2, 4-6, 6-4; only
one match played.
