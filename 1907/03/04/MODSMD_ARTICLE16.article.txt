# Postpone Action on Track Fence
## 
### 
Definite action in regard to fencing
the new track was postponed by the
Board of Control at a meeting this
afternoon, owing to the fact that all
the bids submitted were too high to
admit of consideration. The matter
of the real cost of the fcnce proposed
will be thoroughly investigated be
fore the contract is let for any high
figure.
