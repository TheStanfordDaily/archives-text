# Tennis Men Lose at Alameda.
## 
### 
Stanford's representatives were de
feated in the Alameda County Cham
pionship Tournament yesterday,
Gowan going down before Hunt, 6-2,
6-4, and Delano losing to Gardner,
6-4, 6-4. Henry and Newland de
faulted their matches. In the doubles
Delano and Gowan found Janes and
McLoughlin too fast and were de
feated in straight sets, 6-3, 6-2.
