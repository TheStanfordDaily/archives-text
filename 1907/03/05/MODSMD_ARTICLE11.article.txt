# Franklin Back from Eastern Trip.
## 
###

Chemistry department, has just re
turned to the campus after a hurried
trip to the East. As a member of
the Annual Assay Commission, lie as
sisted in the test of government coins,

adelphia mint.. He lectured before
| the chemical department of the John

can Chemical Society.
