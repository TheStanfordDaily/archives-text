# Track Fence Contract is Let.
## 
### 
The contract for the wire fence
around the new track has been given
to the Pacific Fence Construction
Company, of San Francisco. The
cost will be six hundred dollars. The
fence will be built of heavy two-inch
mesh wire, with three strands of
barbed wire along the top, and will
be six feet high. Work 011 it will be
begun at once.
