# Founder's Day Services Sunday.
## 
### 
Founder's Day this year comes or
Saturday, March 9, so there will not
be the usual observances. On Sun
day Reverend McLeod of Pasadena,
will preach in the Chapel at 11, and he
will probably make some recognition
of the day. Dr. McLeod is con
sidered one of the foremost preachers
of the United States and is also well
known as a literary man.
