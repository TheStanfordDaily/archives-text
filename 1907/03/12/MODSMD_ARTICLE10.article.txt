# "Bogey" Committee Wants Money.
## 
### 
The "Bogey" Investigation Com
mittee, appointed at yesterday's Stu
dent Body meeting, has decided to ob
tain the money necessary to the fur
therance of its work, by means of a
voluntary subscription fund. All per
sons desiring to contribute to such a
fund, should hand their contribution
to any one of the following men: P.
C. Edwards, '06, T. E. Robinson, '08,
G. L. Ainsworth, '08, L. Newland, '08.
or A. D. Larnach, '08. Money may
also be sent to Post-office box 264,
campus.
