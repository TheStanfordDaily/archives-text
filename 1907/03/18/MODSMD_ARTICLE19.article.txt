# Palo Alto Women's League Meets
## 
### 
The Palo Alto League of Stanford
Women held the last meeting of the
semester Saturday afternoon in Frat
ernity Hall, when Dr. D. H. Campbell
gave a lecture 011 his recent trip to the
Orient. A committee consisting of
Miss M. Williams, '07, Miss N. Keym,
'08, Miss L. Rowe, '08, Miss E. Hill,
'09, and Miss E. Wright, '08, was ap
pointed to form plans for the League
for the ensuing year.
