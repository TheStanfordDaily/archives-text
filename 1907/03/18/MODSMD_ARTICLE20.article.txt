# Y.M.C.A Labor Bureau.
## 
### 
The year 1906-7 has been one of un
precedented activity on the part of
the Y. M. C. A. Labor Bureau. The
report of Manager E. E. Lewis, '07.
shows that over one hundred and
fifty students, through its agency,
have been furnished with work, the
cash equivalent of which to date

This, however, does not include the
wages accruing from brick cleaning,
which alone amounts to $4,000, thus
bringing the grand total up to $12,-
