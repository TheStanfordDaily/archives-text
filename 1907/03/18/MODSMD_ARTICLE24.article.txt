# LISTS ARE EXCHANGED
## BASEBALL ELIGIBLES OF THE TWO UNIVERSITIES. Gore of Stanford is a Transfer and Cannot Take Part in Intercollegiate Series. 
### 
The lists of baseball eligibles were
today exchanged between Stanford
and California. The Blue and Gold
sent thirty-two names to Dr. Angell,
while the Cardinal's list comprised
only twenty-two. It has just been
discovered that J. F. Gore, '10, is in
eligible to compete in the Intercolle
giate series as he came to Stanford
from the University of Minnesota.
