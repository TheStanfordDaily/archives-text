# Law Association Smoker Held.
## 
### 
About one hundred law majors at
tended the Law Association smoker
which was held at the University Inn
last evening. B. P. Oakford, '02,
President of the Association, pre
sided. The speakers were Professor
C. H. Huberich, who spoke on "The
Law Department," Professor W. N.
Hohfeld, on "Moot Courts at Har
vard," Professor L. P. Lewis, in an
informal talk dealing with his leaving
Stanford, and R. M. Blodget, '07, on
"The Reorganization of Stanford
Moot Courts." At a business meet
ing held before the smoker, A. D. Lar
nach, '07, was elected secretary of the
association.
