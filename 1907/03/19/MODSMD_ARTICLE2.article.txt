# Pensacola Plays Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The Varsity will meet the team rep
resenting the United States Training
Ship ''Pensacola" on the Varsity
diamond tomorrow afternoon at 3:30.
This team has played a number of the
semi-professional teams of the mid
winter league and has made a good
showing.
The Varsity line-up will be as fol
lows :
Witmer, pitcher; Stott and Daily,
catchers; Presley, first base; Cadwal
ader, second base, Sampson, short
stop; Fenton. third base; Owen, left
field; Wirt, center field; Scott, right
field.
