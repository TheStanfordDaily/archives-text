# More Entries for Interscholastic.
## 
### 
C. F. Laumeister is in receipt of
two more entries for the Interschol
astic meet, from the Commerical High
School of San Francisco, which en
ters Hoburg in the 880 and mile runs,
and from Pasadena High which en
ters Thompson in the hammer throw.
This entry brings the entire number
up to 205, comprising participants
from thirty-one preparatory schools.
