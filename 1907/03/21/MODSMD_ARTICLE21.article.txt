# Gerdes Fund is Growing Rapidly.
## 
### 
Every effort is being exerted to
make the final payment on the Gerdes
mortgage before the anniversary of
the earthquake. There is still $236
due and subscriptions to cover this
amount will be received at the Regis
trar's office. Subscriptions received
to date are: Faculty $163, Alumni
$556, Students $115.75, Friends $229,
Building and Loan Association $100,
and Native Sons' Lodge $100, mak
ing a Total of $1263.75.
