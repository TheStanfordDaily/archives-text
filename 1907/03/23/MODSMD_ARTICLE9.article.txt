# San Diego Crew Picked.
## 
### 
Dispatches from San Diego state
that Coach Courtney of the San
Diego Boating Club has picked the
four which will meet the Stanford
varsity crew at Naples on the 30th
of March. Winters will row stroke
with Vaughan, Hege and Jackson oc
cupying the other seats in the shell.
The coxswain has not yet been
picked, but either Hackleman or Kelly
will handle the tiller ropes for the
Southerners,
