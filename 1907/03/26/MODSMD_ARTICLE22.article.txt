# New Ball Field Ready Soon.
## 
### 
The new auxiliary baseball field
adjoining Stanford Field on the far
ther side, will he completed by the
close of the spring vacation if bad
weather does not again interfere with
the plans of the Board of Control.
The original plan was to have the
work completed before the vacation
but the heavy rains have made this
impossible. It* good weather con
tinues, however, the work will be
rushed through and the moving back
of the bleachers around the football
Held will be done at the same time.
