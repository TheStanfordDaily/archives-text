# Searles Names Musical Committee.
## 
### 
The formal organization of the
Stanford Musical Club will be com
pleted at a meeting which will be
called soon after the Easter vacation.
A committee composed of E. G.
Sheibley, '08, W. G. Bateman, '07, W.
A. Clark, '07, Miss A. W. Kimball, '04,
Miss R. G. Sterne, '09, and Miss J.
Dillon, '07, has been appointed by
Professor Searles to discuss the plans
and provisions of the society, in or
der to facilitate the adoption of a
constitution, when the meeting is
called to complete the organization
of the club.
