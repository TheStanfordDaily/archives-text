# Commuters Form Association.
## 
### 
The women commuters of the Uni
versity met today and formed them
selves into an organization to be
known as the"The Commuters' Asso
ciation of Stanford Women." Miss J.
Johnson, '10, of San Jose, was elected
president, and Miss E. M. Hansen, '08,
of San Mateo, secretary-treasurer.
Meetings will be held on the second
Mondays of each month.
