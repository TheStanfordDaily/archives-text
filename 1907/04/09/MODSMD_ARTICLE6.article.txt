# Civil Engineers Elect Officers.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Civil Engineering
Society was held at the Theta Delta
Chi house last night. Reverend D.
Charles Gardner and C. E. Keating
addressed the society, both of them
taking "Social Service With Regard
to Civil Engineers," as their subject.
The election of officers for next year
resulted as follows:
President, E. S. Dunbar, '08; Vice-
President, C. A. Strong, '07; Secre
tary, E. D. Cole, '07; Treasurer, H. F.
Clark, '08; Corresponding Secretary,
H. J. Heflfron, '07; Member of Exec
utive Committee, J. H. Riddell, '08.
