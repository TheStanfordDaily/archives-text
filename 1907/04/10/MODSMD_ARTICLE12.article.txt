# DEBATE FOR STANFORD CUP.
## High Schools Will Meet on Rostrum for Trophy Offered by Student Body. 
### 
The debate for the Stanford Cup
between San Jose High School and
the winner in the State League will
probably be held the latter part of the
month.
The semi-final contest between
Humboldt Evening High School of
San Francisco and The California In
stitute for the Deaf and Blind will
be held this week, the winner debat
ing San Jose for the cup.
The trophy for which the two
schools will contest was presented by
the Stanford Student Body, to be
held permanently by the school first
winning it three times. San Jose
has held it for two years and The
Deaf and Blind Institute once.
