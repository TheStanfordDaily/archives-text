# Stanford Men Will Judge Debate.
## 
### 
Professors W. N. Hohfeld and C.
A. Huston, of the Law department
and Professor C. A. Duniway, of the
History department, will judge the
de'bate between the University of
Nevada and San Jose State Normal,
which takes place in San Jose next
Saturday evening. These judges were
chosen as a result of the urgent re
quest of both Nevada and San Jose,
that members of the Stanford faculty
should decide the contest.
