# NEW CHAPTER INSTALLED.
## Pie del Monte Club of University of California Initiated Into Delta Gamma Tonight. 
### 
The Stanford chapter of Delta
Gamma will tonight install Gamma
chapter of that sorority. The new
charter was recently granted to the
Pie del Monte Club, of the University
of California. The ceremonies will be
held in the local chapter house.
N'neteen will be initiated.
The Pie del Monte Club is one of
the oldest organizations of young
women at the University of California.
It has long taken a leading part in the
social life of the University and is
among the best known societies at
Berkeley.
