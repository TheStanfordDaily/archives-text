# Sword and Sandals Elects.
## 
### 
Sword and Sandals elected new
members at a meeting held yesterday
evening at the Delta Upsilon house.
The men elected to the dramatic so
ciety were: Instructor I. B. Cross, as
an honorary member; H. S. Ross, '08,
G. F. Zimmer, '08, H. C. Perry, '08.
G. N. Richardson, '09, M. O. Adams,
'09 and P. R. Johnson, '09. Cross
assisted in coaching "You Never Can
Tell." Perry, Zimmer and Ross all
took prominent parts in "La Serena.'
Richardson, Adams and Johnson
played leading parts in "You Never
Can Tell."
