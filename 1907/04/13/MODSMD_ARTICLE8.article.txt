# OFFICIALS FOR BIG MEET.
## Men Who Will Conduct Intercollegiate Contest on April 20 Are Named Today. 
### 
Lnte this afternoon the track rep
resentatives of Stanford and Califor
nia met and named the officials for
the Intercollegiate meet on April 20.
The entries and heats will be pub
lished in Monday's issue of the
"Daily Palo Alto". 1
The officials follow:
Referees, Col. G. C. Edwards and
Dr. F. Angell; starter, J. C. McCaugh
ern or C. F. Laumeister, O. A. Sne
digar; timers, F. Koch, D. C. Brown
F. Burgess; judges of finish, P. Ger
hardt, J. Elliott, W. R. Hamilton, H.
Cheek; field judges, W. C. Gilmorc,
W. Wilcox, O. E. Hyde, J. R. Ilickey;
measurers, C. Coolev, R. Galloway, G.
J. Presley, W. Koerner; clerk of the
course, W. A. Powell, assistant, G.
Knupp; inspectors, A. M. Thompson,
W. M. Rawles, A. Moody, T. K.
Sweezy; announcer, R. Filcher.
