# Varsity to Play St. Vincent's.
## 
### 
A game between the Varsity and
the St. Vincent team of Los Angeles
has been scheduled for Thursday af
ternoon at 3:30 on the Varsity dia
mond. The St. Vincent team has
been playing good ball in the Soutl
and the game Thursday should be of
the same high class as the Varsity
game last Saturday. As yet no other
game has been scheduled for this
week and Thursday may be the only
opportunity to see the team play, be
fore the second game of the Inter
collegiate series which will be played
next Saturday on the California dia
mond.
