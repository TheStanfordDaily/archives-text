# Glee Club to Sing at Fairmont.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon the Glee Club
w'll go to San Francisco to assist in
the opening of the new Fairmont
Hotel. They are to appear on the
program at nine o'clock in the foyer
and will render two selections,
"Hebe" and "Drink to Me Only With
Thine Eyes." The DeKoven Club
of Berkeley and several of the more
San Francisco musicians arc to
appear on the same program.
