# Senior Class Votes Money.
## 
### 
Acting in conformity with other
classes in the University, the Senior
Class this afternoon appropriated $50
to the Gerdes fund. The class also
voted 1907 sweaters to S. Moser, B.
A. Eby, C. E. Spaulding, G. W. Mc
intosh. for baseball, and T. A. Du
Flon, for track.
