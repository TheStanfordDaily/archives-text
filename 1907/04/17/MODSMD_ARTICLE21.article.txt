# Association of German Teachers.
## 
### 
The next regular meeting of the
California Association of German
Teachers will be held in the German
building on the Quad next Saturday
afternoon at 2 o'clock. Professor
George Hempl, head of the local Ger
man faculty, will read the principal
paper, taking as his subject, "How to
Teach the Pronunciation of a Foreign
Language."
A report of the committee on 1
new constitution and of the standing
committtee on new text books that
have appeared since the last meeting
of the association, are among the im
portant matters that will come up for
consideration.
The literary portion of the meeting
will be open to the University public
