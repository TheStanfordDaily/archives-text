# Senior Women to Give Supper.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the women of the
Senior class, held this afternoon, it
was decided that a "Sunset Supper"
should be held on the evening of May
17th, for all women of the 1907 class.
The supper will be under the auspices
of Cap and Gown. Tt was also sug
gested that the men's organizations
should have charge of the booths on
the Quad the night of the Senior
Prom, this year and alternate years
hereafter, but no definite steps were
taken in the matter.
