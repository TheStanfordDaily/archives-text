# DELANO TENNIS CAPTAIN.
## Chosen to Lead Cardinal in Intercollegiate Tournament at California Next Saturday. 
### 
P. B. Delano, '08, of New Bedford,
Mass., was this afternoon elected cap
tain of the Intercollegiate tennis
team. Delano is a member of Encina
Club and of the Press Club and presi
dent of the University Tennis Club.
He is at present News Editor of the
"Daily Palo Alto," and he registers
in the department of Law. Delano
and Gowan will be the doubles team
which will play California Saturday,
morning. The newly elected captain
was a member of last year's Varsity
team which the earthquake prevented
from competing against the Blue and
Gold.
