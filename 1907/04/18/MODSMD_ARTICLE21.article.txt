# Program for French Club.
## 
### 
The French Club will hold its last
meeting for this year at the home of
Professor O. M. Johnston, 4 Alvarado
Row, Monday evening at 8. Profes
sor Johnston has prepared an inter
esting program for the evening, Miss
D'Hautinolle of Palo Alto, an ac
complished elocutionist, and Miss M.
M.de Luna, '07, will give readings in
French. The following readings will
be given by Miss D'Hautmolle; "La
Scene a Faire," "La Naufrage," "Est
ce toi cliere Elise." All former mem
bers of the club are invited to attend.
