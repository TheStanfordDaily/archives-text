# Y.M.C.A. Makes Nominations.
## 
### 
The annual election of the officers
of the Young Men's Christian Asso
ciation will take place tomorrow in
room 14. The polls will be open
from 12:30 to 5:00. The nomination
committee has reported the following
candidates for the various offices:
For President—P. W. Merrill, '08,
D. C. Boyd, '08; for Vice-president—
G. D. Ferrell, '09, B. S. Chaffee, '08,
E. R. Purdum, '09; for Secretary—G.
W. Wood, '10, S. J. Talbot, 'io, A. G.
Mott, '08; for Treasurer—C. C. San
born, '08.
