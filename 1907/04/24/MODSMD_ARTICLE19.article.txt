# Boating Men Leave on Friday.
## 
### 
The Varsity and Freshman crews
will not leave for Sausalito until Fri
day morning. They will spend the
day in going over the course and
familiarizing themselves with it. The
crews who will represent Stanford will
be named tomorrow by Coach Mur
phy, and the officials will also be made
public at that time. Washington ar
rived yesterday and started in work
at Sausalito today. The tickets for
the observation train will go on sale
tomorrow morning at 9 on the Inner
Quadrangle at the corner where the
college publications are usually sold.
