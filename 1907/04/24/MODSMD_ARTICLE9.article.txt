# Heney Will Speak Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Francis J. Heney, the San Francisco
prosecutor who is conducting the
graft cases, will speak tomorrow mor
ning at 11:15 Assembly. The As
sembly will be under the auspices of
the Social Service Club. Heney will
speak on graft as it exists in the city.
At the conclusion of the Assembly
tomorrow, the oarsmen who row
against Washington and California
next Saturday, will be given a sendoff.
Captain F. Zimmerman, '07, may
speak.
