# Anti-Graft Movement Started.
## 
### 
The only action taken at the meet
ing for the organization of an anti
graft league held in room 2 of the
Law building this afternoon, was the
authorization of a committee of five
men from the Student Body to draw
up a constitution and plan of organ
ization. Professor Boke, who called
the meeting, will announce the per
sonnel of this committee in the near
future.
Francis J. Hency gave a few sug
gestions to the meeting and other
addresses were made by Chaplain D.
C. Gardner and W. Y. Wentz, '06.
