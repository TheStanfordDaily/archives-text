# Indian Musical on Friday.
## 
### 
An Indian musical will be given
under the auspices of the Young Wo
men's Christian Association Friday,
May 3, from 4 to 6 in the parlor of
the Congregational Church, Palo Alto.
Original Indian music and poems will
form the program of the afternoon.
A voluntary offering will be taken, the
proceeds to be used toward the Tokio
Association fund.
