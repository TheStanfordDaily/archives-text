# Dorr Will Lead Mandolin Club.
## 
### 
At a business meeting of the Man
dolin Club, Sunday morning, F. W.
Dorr, '08, was elected leader of the
club for next year. Dorr has been
a member of the club since coming
to Stanford. He registers from San
Francisco in the Law department and
is a member of the Kappa Alpha fra
ternity. E. K. Soper, '08, was elected
manager of the club and G. B. Mc-
Lain, '07, and R. A. Gott, '09, were
elected to the executive board.
