# Women's League Meets Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The Women's League will meet to
morrow at 4:30 at Roble Hall. Pro
fessor Suzzallo will speak, Dr. Camp
bell's water-color sketches of Ceylon
will be on exhibition, and the Girls'
Glee Club will give several selections.
This is to be the most important meet
ing of the year and all women con
nected with the University are invited
to be present.
