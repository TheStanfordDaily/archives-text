# To Give Tokio Association Benefit.
## 
### 
The Indian afternoon to be given
by the Young Women's Christian As
sociation Friday, May 3, from 4 to 6,
in the parlor of the Congregational
Church, Falo Alto, will be devoted to
Indian music and poems. The affair
is to be for the benefit of the Associa
tion of Tokio, Japan, and a voluntary
offering will be taken up.
