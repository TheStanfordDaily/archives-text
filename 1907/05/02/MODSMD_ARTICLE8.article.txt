# PROGRAM IS COMPLETE
## COMMENCEMENT EXERCISES LAST A WEEK. Baccalaureate Sermon by Reverend Gates.—Commencement Address by Weinstock. 
### 
The full program for Commence
ment week has been prepared by the
various committees. The Baccalaur
eate Sermon will be delivered Sunday
morning, May 19, by Rev. George C.
Gates, president of Pomona College,
and the Commencement address will
be delivered by Harris Weinstock of
Sacramento. Exercises will cover the
lieriod from May 16, at which time the
Senior Farce will be staged, until May
22. The program is as follows:
Thursday, May 16.
8:00 p. m.—Senior Farce, in Assembly
Hall, Dion Boucicault's "London
Assurance."
Friday, May 17.
9:30 a. m.—Faculty-Senior Baseball
Game.
4:00 p. m.—Reception to Graduates by
President and Mrs. Jordan, in the
Zoology Building.
6:30 p. m. —Sunset Supper, for Senior
Women.
8:00 p. m.—Senior Smoker.
Saturday, May 18.
11:00 a. m.—Annual Public Meeting
of Stanford Law Association. Pub
lic Address in the Chapel, by
Charles Page, of San Francisco.
12:30 p. m.—Law Association Lunch
eon, at the University Inn.
8:00 p. m.—Senior Reception, in En
cina Hall.
Sunday, May 19.
n:oo a. m.—Baccalaureate Sermon, by
Rev. George C. Gates, President of
Pomona College.
Monday, May 20.
10:30 a. m.—Class Day Exercises, in
the Chapel.
n:oo a. m.—Dedication of '07 Plate.
7:30 p. m.—Senior Ball, in Encina
Club Room.
Tuesday, May 21.
9:30 a. m. to 12 m.—Registration of
Alumni: Classes of '92, '97, '02, and
'05.
9:30 a. m.—Alumni-Senior Baseball
Game.
11:00 a. m. —Annual Meeting of A 1
umni Association.
1 :oo p. m.—Alumni Reunion and
Luncheon, at Roblc Hall.
:oo p. m.—Phi Beta Kappa Open
Meeting, in the Chapel. Address by
Rev. Edward Lamb Parsons. Phi
Beta Kappa Toem, by Professor A.
G. Newcomer.
5:00 p. m.—Phi Beta Kappa Banquet
at Roblc Hall.
5:30 p. m.—Class Banquets and Re
unions: Classes of '92, '97, '02, and
'05.
8 :oo p. m. —Promenade Concert.
Wednesday, May 22.
10:30 a. m.—Commencement, in As
sembly Hall. Address by Harris
Weinstock, of Sacramento.
