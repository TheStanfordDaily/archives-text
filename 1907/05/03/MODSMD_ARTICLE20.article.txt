# Caps and Gowns Must be Ordered.
## 
### 
All orders for caps and gowns must |
be in before Monday noon, as the
Bookstore must send its order to the
Pacific Coast Agency on that date.
This agency supplies all the univer
sities west of the Rocky Mountains
and has but a limited number of
gowns to deliver, so it will be impos
sible to obtain the graduation dress
unless orders are placed at once.
