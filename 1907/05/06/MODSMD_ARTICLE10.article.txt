# Crew Will Elect Captain.
## 
### 
The members of the eight which de
feated the Blue and Gold crew at
Sausalito last Monday morning, will
meet tomorrow afternoon at 1:30 at
the boat-house to elect a captain for
next year. The men who are eligible
to vote arc: F. Zimmerman, '07, R. H.
Conant, '08, L. R. Gay, '08, J. R. Pem
berton, '08, A. N. Cole, '08. A. B.
Shutts, '09, W. H. Starlc, '09, J. C.
Huston, '10, and F. W. Turner, '08.
