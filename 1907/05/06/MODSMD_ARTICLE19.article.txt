# ANNOUNCEMENTS.
## 
### 
Sophomore Class—Will meet Wed
nesday at 1:10, in room 68.
T. R. Cadwalader.
Economics 1. —Examination will be
May 13, at 8:15. All members of the
class whose names begin with A to J
inclusive will take the examination
in room 2, Law building. The re
mainder of the class will be examined
in room 231, History building.
Field Geology.—The work in
Course 4 will begin May 31. A fee
of $15 must be paid through the Busi
ness Office 011 or before May 20th
and the receipt shown to Professor
Rogers before registration in this
course will be complete. All students
desiring to take this work are re
quested to meet Professor Rogers in
room 360, next Wednesday, at 4
o'clock.
Girls' Glee Club. —Last meeting of
the year, Tuesday evening, 7:30, Cor.
Lytton and Emerson.
Bookstore Bank.—The office of the
Mayfield Bank & Trust Company at
the Bookstore will close for the se
mester after Saturday, May 25, 1907.
After that date patrons arc referred
to the main bank in Mayfield. Phone
Main 118.
Varsity Crew.—The members of the
Varsity crew who rowed against Cal
ifornia will meet tomorrow afternoon
at the boat-house, at t 130 o'clock.
F. Zimmerman, '07.
Greek 10. —Professor Rolfe will
meet those desiring to report upon
special topics during his office hours.
Women's League—Will hold elec
tion of officers Wednesday, May 8,
at 1:15, in the Chapel.
