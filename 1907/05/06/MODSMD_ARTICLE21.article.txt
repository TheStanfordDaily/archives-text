# Forrest Fisher is Engaged.
## 
### 
The engagement is announced of
Miss E. Barnhisel, '99, to Forrest. 3.
Fisher, '99: The marriage is to take
place in June. Miss Barnhisel was a
member of the local chapter of the
Kappa Alpha Theta sorority. Fisher
was captain of the Varsity football
team in 1898 and is now a practicing
attorney in Portland, Oregon.
