# PRESS CLUB HAS INITIATION.
## Rixford, Bruning and Bellows are Made Members of Journalist Organization Today. 
### 
H. L. Rixford, '09, H. F. Bruning,
'09, and V. F. Bellows, '09, were today
initiated into the Press Club. All
three men are prominent in Univer
sity journalism.
H. L. Rixford, '09, is an associate
editor of the"Daily Palo Alto," and
a member of the 1909 "Quad" board.
He registers in the English depart
ment from San Francisco and is a
member of the Delta Kappa Epsilon
fraternity.
H. F. Bruning, '09, is the editor of
the 1909 "Quad," and was on the as
sistant staff of the"Daily Palo Alto"
last year. Fie is a member of the
Sigma Alpha Epsilon fraternity and is
a Law major, registering from Oak
land.
V. F. Bellows, '09, is an associate
editor on both the "Chaparral'' and
the "Sequoia;" he is also a member
of the 1909 "Quad" board. He is a
member of the Phi Gamma Delta
fraternity and majors in Drawing from
Cleveland, O.
