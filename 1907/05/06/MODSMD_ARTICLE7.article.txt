# Y.W.C.A. Workers Will be Here.
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Matthew, former
secretary of the Young Women's
Christian Association of the New
England States, will speak tomorrow
at 440 in the Chapel.
On Wednesday. May 8. from 4 to
6. an informal reception will be given
in Roble. for Miss Elizabeth Wilson,
National city secretary of the Asso
ciation, to which all women of the
University are cordially invited.
