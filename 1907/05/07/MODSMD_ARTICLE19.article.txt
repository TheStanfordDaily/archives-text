At its meeting tomorrow night, the
Executive Committee should make
some definite provision regarding a
new contract with Coach Murphy
Murphy has announced that he hardly
feels able to return here unless he is
signed for a full year and his deter

ruination seems only reasonable. The
rowing coach returned here this year
at 110 small personal sacrifice, because
he liked the spirit of the men here
and because he desired to turn out a
winning crew. Most of the Eastern
universities employ their boating
coaches under a year contract. This
system gives the coach an opportunity
to work out his men during the full
semester and to get a good line on
his material before the season opens
in the spring. The retention of Mur
phy under a year contract depends
upon the Student Body treasury and
the Executive Committee should thor
oughly consider the matter from this
view-point before the close of college.
