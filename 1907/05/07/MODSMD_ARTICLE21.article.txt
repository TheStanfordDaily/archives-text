# GEOLOGISTS ON SUMMER TRIP
## Big Party Will Leave on May 31 for Expedition to Tesla Quadrangle. 
### 
The class in Field Geology for the
summer of 1907 will start on its trip
011 May 31. The class will be in
charge of Professor A. F. Rogers, H.
R. Johnson, '03, and W. O. Clark, '05,
and will visit the Tesla Quadrangle, a
region situated east of Livermore,
near Mt. Hamilton. The work will
consist of making a geological map
of the region and collecting speci
mens. The expedition will last seven
weeks. Following are the men who
will make up the party: H. T. Beck :
with, 08, C. 1. Chandler, '08, L. R.
Gay, '08, C. W. Henderson, '08, W.
B. Higley, '08, R. Hornby, '09, A. D.
Hughe*, '08, W. Koerner, '08, J. C.
Jameson, '08, A. C. Luhrs, '08, A. A.
Mathewson, '07, C. W. McDaniel, '08,
R. L. Motz, '08, W. 11. Ochsner, '04,
J. R. Pemberton, '08, B. Prescott, '08,
C. A. Randall, '08, H. S. Ross, '08,
C. L. Severy, '08, F. K. Sopcr, '08.
D. Steel, 'oS.
