# ANNOUNCEMENT.
## 
### 
Express Orders.—Students should
hand in their orders to the "Students'
Transfer Company" at once in order
that the working schedule may be
arranged as soon as possible.
Senior Ball Tickets.—Senior Ball
tickets are on sale at the Bookstore.
Price $5.00.
Bookstore Bank.—The office of the
Mayfield Bank & Trust Company at
the Bookstore will close for the se
mester after Saturday, May 25, 1907.
After that date patrons are referred
to the main bank in Mayfield. Phone
Main 118.
Women's League—Will hold elec
tion of officers Wednesday, May 8,
at 1:15, in the Chapel.
Faculty Women's Club—Meets
Monday, May 13, at the residence of
Mrs. Dodge. Carriages leave resi-
of Mrs. Branner on Campus
and Mrs. Franklin at Palo Alto at
2:30. All those intending to go please
notify one of the above ladies imme
diately.
Emma R. Stillman, Chairman.
Physiology Club—Will meet in
room 118, Encina, Wednesday at 7:30
p. m.
Senior Farce Tickets.—On sale Fri
day morning, May 10, at the Stanford
Pharmacy, in Palo Alto, and the Book
store on the Campus.
Greek 10. —Professor Rolfe will
meet those desiring to report on
special topics Wednesday and Thurs
day from 1 to 4.
