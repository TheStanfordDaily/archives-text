# MEMORIAL DAY TUESDAY.
## Birthday of Leland Stanford Jr., and Twentieth Anniversary of Laying of Cornerstone. 
### 
Next Tuesday will be the thirty
ninth anniversary of the birth of Le
land Stanford, Jr. The arrangement
of fitting ceremonies for the day was
left to the Executive Committee by
the Faculty Committee on Public Ex
ercises. No definite plans have yet
been made, but it is probable that
some informal exercises will be ar
ranged. In his sermon next Sunday
morning Chaplain Gardner will touch
upon the memories and sentiments
aroused by Memorial Day. Tuesdav
is also the twentieth anniversary of
the laying of the cornerstone of the
University.
