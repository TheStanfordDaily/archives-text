# Four Teams in Fraternity Series.
## 
### 
In the Interfraternity scries there
arc four teams left to play for the
championship,—Delta Kappa Epsilon,
Delta Upsilon, Kappa Sigma and
Zeta Psi. The game between the two
former will take place tomorrow af
ternoon at 2:30, and the latter two
will play at 4:30. The winners of
these two games will play a series
of three for the cup, but the exact
date for this series has not yet been
arranged.
