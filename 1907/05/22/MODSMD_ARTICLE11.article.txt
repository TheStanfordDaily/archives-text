# Phi Beta Kappa Meets.
## 
### 
Phi Beta Kappa honor society held
its annual meeting and banquet yes
terday afternoon and evening. The
day's program began at 3:30 with a
private meeting and election of offi
cers in room 41, and this was followed
by the public meeting in the Chapel.
The address was given by Rev. Ed
ward Lambe Parson, of Berkeley, on
'"The Ideal Democracy," and the orig
inal poem was read by Prof. A. G.
Newcomer. After the meeting, a
banquet was given in Roble Hall for
members and invited guests.
