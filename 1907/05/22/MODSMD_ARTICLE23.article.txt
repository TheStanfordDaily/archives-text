# Guild Plans Summer Work.
## 
### 
The final meeting of the Guild Hos
pital board was held Monday even
ing, May 13, and important steps
were taken toward the future work
and plans of the institution. A sum
mer committee was appointed, con
sisting of D. T. Fullaway, '06, and
Professors J. O. Griffin and R. E.
Swain, to decide on an addition to
the hospital, consisting of a nurses'
home and maternity ward, to be in
stalled either in the cottagc next to
the present building or to be built on
a new site. The board also decided
that the hospital should be run for a
period of at least five years longer as
a general hospital taking all cases sent
for treatment.
