# Stanford Marriage Celebrated.
## 
### 
Another Stanford marriage of the
past summer was that of W. G. Bey
mer, ex-07, and Miss J. Hayward, '06.
The ceremony was performed at San
ta Barbara 011 the sixteenth of last
June. Beymer was a member of the
Encina Club and prominent in art
work while in college, and Miss Hay
ward was art editor of the '05 Quad.
