# RECEPTION TO FRESHMEN.
## Interesting Program in the Gymnasium Will be Arranged for Next Saturday Evening. 
### 
Under the auspices of the Young
Men's Christian Association a recep
tion will he given to the Freshmen
next Saturday evening, September 7.
An extensive and interesting program
has been arranged, which will com
mence at eight o'clock.
Xo formal invitations are being sent
out as the reception is open to all
students of the University.
