# Special Economic Investigation.
## 
### 
Under the direction of the Carnegie
Institution of Washington, the Eco
nomics department is investigating
special topics relating to the Econo
mic History of the United States.
Professor A. A. Young is preparing a
report on the History of the Ameri
can Railroads and Instructor I. 13.
Cross is collecting material on the
Labor Movement in California. Other
subjects are being investigated by
economists in the East and it is the
plan of the Carnegie Institution to
compile the reports into a compre
hensive Economic History of the
United States.
