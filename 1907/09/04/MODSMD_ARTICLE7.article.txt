## Rugby Season Will Be Inaugurated.â€” Bull Durham Company to Distribute Tobacco. 
### 
The first football rally of the sea
son will be held in Encina Hall next
Friday evening. No definite program
has been arranged as yet, but there
will be the usual yelling and singing,
with speeches by Coaches Lanagan
and Presley, Trainer Moulton and
Captain Koerner. Every one intend
ing to play Rugby will sign up at the
conclusion of the rally. A special
feature of the evening will be the free
distribution of Bull Durham to all
those present. This custom was in
augurated last year and proved a
great success.
