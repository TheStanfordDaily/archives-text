# LANGSTROTH BREAKS ARM.
## Falls From Second Story Window of Zeta Psi House While Walking In His Sleep. 
### 
L. Langstroth, '08, fell from the
second story window of the Zeta Psi
house while asleep this morning and
suffered a severe break of his right
arm and numerous bruises. He is
at present at the Guild Hospital and
it will be several days before he will
be able to return to the Campus.
