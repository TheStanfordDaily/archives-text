# Freshmen Debaters Organize.
## 
### 
The Freshmen Debating Society
was organized last evening by T. B.
Cross, of the Economics department,
and officers for the present semester
were elected. The society will be
gin active work at once, the first
debate being held next Monday even
ing. Those chosen to hold office are:
Joseph Dixon, president, and Charles
Christian, secretary.
After the new society was organ
ized. I'. J. Batkin, '10, in behalf of
the 19io Debating Society, presented
the newly elected president with a
gavel bearing the seal of the Univer
sity and an inscription directing that
it be handed down to the succeeding
Freshmen Debaters in the name of
the 1910 society. .
