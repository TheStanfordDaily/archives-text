# To Revive Ben Jonson Classic
## 
###

sity of California has decided to give
a production of Ben Johnson's classic
play, "The Hue and Cry After Cupid,"'
in the Greek theatre, some time dur
ing the present semester. This drama
has very rarely been given in recent
1 years, and it is expected will arouse
| much interest among students of lit
erature.
