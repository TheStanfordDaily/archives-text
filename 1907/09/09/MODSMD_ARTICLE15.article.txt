# To Hold Special Glee Rehearsals.
## 
### 
Owing to the proximity of the next
University Assembly, at which the
Glee Club will appear on the program.
Leader Hill has decided to have spec
ial rehearsals this week. The first
one of these will be held at the Kap
pi Sigma house tomorrow evening at
7 30.
The club will meet for the same
purpose at the Phi Delta Theta house
en Wednesday evening at 7:30. All
successful candidates at the tryout this
evening will be expected to take part
in these rehearsals as well as all old
members.
