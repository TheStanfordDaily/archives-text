# Orchestra Fills Vacancies
## 
### 
At the regular business meeting of
the Orchestra, held Friday evening,
H. H. Canterbury, '10, H. Kecsling,
'oq, and H. P. Harrison, '10. were
elected to fill the places on the exec
utive board made vacant by the with
drawal from college of F. K. Wyatt,
*C 9, W. H. Thomas, 'ro, and H. H.
Fitting, '08. About eight new men
will be added to the membership at
the end of the tryouts which close this
week.
