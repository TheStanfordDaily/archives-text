# PREPARATORY SCHOOLS TO PLAY RUGBY.
## 
### 
The majority of the leading pre
paratory schools of Southern Califor
nia. including Redlands, Riverside,
San Bernardino, Santa Ana, Long
Beach. Los Angeles High, and Los
Angeles Polytechnic, will play Rugby
under the English rules this year.
The league also includes some Rugby
Associations. After the scheduled
games of the league have been play
ed. an all-star team is to be chosen.
This team will endeavor to arrange
a game with the winner of the Stan
ford-California Freshmen game.
