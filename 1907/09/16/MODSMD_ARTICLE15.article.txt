# Tillman May Speak Here.
## 
### 
United States Senator B. B. Till
man, of South Carolina, is expected
to visit the Campus Thursday. He
is touring the country for a lectur
ing agency and as yet no invitation
to speak in a University Assembly
has been tendered him. Professor S.
W. Young, chairman of the Faculty
committee on Public Entertainments,
is anxious to have Senator Tillman
address the students and will arrange
to have him speak if possible.
