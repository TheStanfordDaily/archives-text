# Track Meet at California Saturday
## 
### 
The first track meet of the season
will he held at California Saturday
afternoon, when the University
Freshmen will meet Berkeley High
School. The High School team has
been training for a month, while the
Freshmen have only been at work
for a week. However, the Fresh
men have a number of former High
School stars, who are relied on to
make a good showing. The distances
in all events above the 440 have been
somewhat shortened. The Freshmen
have also scheduled a meet with Oak
land High School for a week from
tomorrow.
