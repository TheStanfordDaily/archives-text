# Three Entries for Sophomore Tennis.
## 
### 
Only three entries were handed in
for the Sophomore tennis tryout to he
held on the Encina Courts at 8:30
tomorrow morning. Following are the
men who will compete: L. B. Mac
key, T. C. Henry and H. I. Hadley.
The drawings were held this noon
and the first match will be played be
tween Mackey and Henry. Hadley
will play the winner of this match
for the class championship.
