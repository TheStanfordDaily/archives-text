# Elect Officers and New Members.
## 
### 
J. R. Dillon, '07, was chosen pres
ident and A. F. Meston, '10, secre
tary-treasurer of the Stanford Gym
nasium Club, at the regular semi
annual election. H. V. Poor, '10, J.
Crawford, '11, and K. Dole, '11,
were elected to membership in the
club. Negotiations are being car
ried on with the management of the
Palo Alto Street Fair to have an ex
hibition by the club during the fes
tivities there. It is probable that
this will be given Saturday night.
