# New Class in Philosophy.
## 
### 
Tlie class in religious philosophy,
to be conducted under the auspices of
the Young Men's Christian Associa
tion, will he held from 1:30 to 2:30
i 1 room 228, on Monday. Reverend
Patchell of San Jose, one of the most
prominent ministers on the Coast,
will have full direction of the work.
Any one can register for the work
before next Monday, but after that
date the number of students will be
restricted. The class will meet for
regular work September 30.
