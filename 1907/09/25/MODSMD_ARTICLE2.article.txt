# SUCCESSFUL BAND TRYOUT.
## Stanford Men Will Play at Palo Alto Street Fair Friday and Saturday Afternoons. 
### 
The hand tryout brought forth an
unusual amount of pood material, and
Leader Sheibley and Manager Kees
ling have expressed great satisfaction
over the results. The successful can
didates are: H. S. Ross, 08, A. T.
Case, '09, J. W. Fitting, '10, G. E.
Schide, '09, altos; J. A. Koontz, '08,
C. R. Reyburn, '09, hassos; F. H.
Oliver, '09, A. L. Weiter, '09, H. H.
Canterbury, 'oo, clarinets; A. S. Stev
ens, 08, IT. D. Owen, '11, F. L. Mat
chette, '11, F. K. Stark, '11, W. P.
Green, 'ti, cornets; H. G. Keesling,
'08, G. W. Reyburn, '08, tenors; L. L.
Stanley, 08, G. M. Tyler, 'ti, drums.
Definite arrangements have been
made with the Palo Alto Street Fair
committee and the University musi
cians will play for the afternoon par
ades of the 27th and 28th.
