# New Tackling Dummy Constructed.
## 
### 
A new tackling dummy has been
installed on Stanford Field by the
athletic management. It embraces
the latest devices in this line, and
the men will be at once put to work
on it.
Large stand pipes have also been
putin place, so that all three athlet
ic fields may hereafter be watered as
often as desired.
