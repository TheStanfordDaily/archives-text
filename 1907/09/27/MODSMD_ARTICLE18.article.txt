The idea of games with outside
teams for the fall baseball squad is
a commendable one. A great deal
may be learned from such contests
which could not be picked up either
in the routine of practice or in scrub
play. The baseball men have shown
that they are in earnest in their fall
work and are determined that the
innovation shall be a success. It now
rests with the undergraduates to sup
port the plan by a liberal patronage
of the games.
