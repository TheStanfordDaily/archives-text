# FRESHMAN TENNIS TRYOUT.
## Drawings Completed For 1911 Tournament.-Only Eight Players to Compete For Team. 
### 
Drawings were held today for the
Freshman tennis tryout to be held to
morrow morning on the Encina
courts. There are eight entries and
following is a list of the matches to
be played: Kobbe vs. Lacy, McCon
nell vs. Caddagan, Steinburger vs.
Carat, Collis vs. Baldwin.
