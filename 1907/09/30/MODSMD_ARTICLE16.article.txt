# Stanford Men To Publish Program.
## 
### 
Graduate Manager G. Knupp, '07.
has placed the printing of the annual
varsity football program in the hands
of W. C. Theile, '08. and W. H. Tuck
er, '08. The program will contain
the usual number of pages with cuts
of the squads, captains, coaches, and
yell leaders as in former years. Stan
ley Taylor Co. will do the printing.
