# Senior Women Hold Meeting.
## 
### 
At the meeting of tne Senior wo
men this noon, it was decided to wear
the caps and gowns this semester on
Wednesdays and Fridays and to all
Assemblies. Miss M. Gay appointed
Miss E. Murdoch and Miss S. War
moth to see that the central section
of the balcony be reserved for the
Seniors at all assemblies.
