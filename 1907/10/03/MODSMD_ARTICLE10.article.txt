# ANNOUNCEMENTS.
## 
### 
Law Majors—Are cordially invited
to attend a smoker to be lield in the
Stanford Inn Friday evening, Octo
ber ii, from 8:30 to 11.
Faculty of the Department of Law.
Wanted.—A man to carry D. P. A.
to College Terrace. Sec E. A. Cun
ha, room 19, or call at D. P. A. office.
Football Men.—All football men
who are 110 longer on the squad are
requested to turn in their suits im
mediately. The graduate manager
will be in his office from 1 to 2 on
Mondays and Fridays and on Tues
days. Wednesdays, and Thursdays
from 4 to 5.
Stanford Club of Palo Alto.—There
will he a general meeting "and jolly
up at the new club rooms, on the
third floor of the Simkins Building,
Saturday, October 5, at 7:30. Stan
ford men residing in Palo Alto arc
invited to come.
Women's Athletic Association —
The meeting announced for Friday
has been postponed until Monday at
4:30, at Roble Hall.

Girls' Glee Club.—There will be a
business meeting in the Rest Room
at 1, Friday.
Caps and Gowns—Have arrived and
may be procured at the Campus
Bookstore.
Glee Club, —Will meet for minstrel
show rehearsal at the Phi Kappa Psi
house, Friday afternoon at 4:30.
Glee Club.—Will practice at 7:30
Friday evening at the Phi Kappa Psi
house.
Sophomore Class.—Will meet in
room 68, Monday at 1:10.
B. E. PARSONS, Pres.
Girls' Glee Club.—Will hold an im
portant meeting Friday afternoon at
1, in room 14.
