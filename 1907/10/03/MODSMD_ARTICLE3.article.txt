# Hotel Will Speak Sunday.
## 
### 
Owen Hotel, one of the most prom
inent real estate men of Oakland, will
speak under the auspices of the
Young Men's Christian Association
Sunday afternoon at 4 o'clock in the
University Chapel. Hotel's subject is
not yet announced, but he will speak
merely from a business point of view
He is a forceful and magnetic speak
er, having addressed a number of
gatherings at Pacific Grove.
