# Conference Will Meet Next Week.
## 
### 
President D. S. Jordan lias callcd
the Men's Conference to meet at his
home, Xazmin House, next Tuesday
evening, October 8, at 8 o'clock. The
primary purpose of the first meet
ing will be to organize the confer
ence on lines slightly different from
those along which the body conduct
ed its business last year.
