# Lighting Materials Expected Soon.
## 
### 
The necessary materials which were
ordered for the installation of a light
ing system on University avenue have
left the hands of the manufacturers
in the East and are expected to ar
rive here in the near future. The
specifications call for fifteen arc
lights, ornamental iron poles to sup
port the lamps, and a large amount
of steel cable which is to be used
on the avenue. Construction will
commence immediately on the arriv
al of the material and it is still ex
pected by the Board of Trustees that
the entire lighting system will be
installed by the end of this semester.
