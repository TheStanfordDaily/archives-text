# Women's League Names Collectors
## 
### 
At a meeting of the executive board
of the Women's League this noon,
women were appointed from all the
dormitories, associations and soror
ity houses, to collect the dollar assess
ment. This money was voted, at the
last regular meeting of the League,
for a fund to be used for the new
clubhouse. The collectors are ex
pected to turn in the money by No
vember I.
