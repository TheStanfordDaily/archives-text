# Touch Judges For Freshman Game.
## 
### 
Touch judges for the Freshman In
tercollegiate game have been chosen
by the graduate managers. Coach
Howard of Santa Clara College ,and
Lafferty of the Barbarians will occu
py these positions Saturday. The ar
rangements for the movements of the
Freshman team have been completed.
The men, accompanied by Coaches
Lanagan and Presley, Trainer Moul
ton, and Manager Knupp, together
with the members of the varsity
Band, will leave upon the 9:31 train
Saturday morning for San Francisco.
A special car has been reserved for
them and upon their arrival at San
Francisco they will go direct to Ber
keley, where lunch is to be served at
the Golden Sheaf Cafe.
