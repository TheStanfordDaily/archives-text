# DEBATERS ENTER TRYOUTS.
## Men are Assigned to Squads and Will Begin Their Work At Once. 
### 
Twenty-two Freshmen and twelve
Sophomores have signed up for the
debating tryouts which will be held
011 the evenings of November 6 and
7. The men have been assigned to
their squads, which will meet imme
diately to select the subject for de
bate and determine the order of
speakers. Any who have not yet
signed up for the tryou*s may be
assigned to squads by applying to J.
F. Reilly, '08.
The following Freshmen have al
ready handed in their names:
First squad—J. E. Thompson, F. E.
Hill, F. L. Haworth, J. Dixon, W. C.
Dalzell, and C. A. Christian.
Second Squad.—M. L. Hall, W. S.
Porter, R. J. Miller, F. H. Hilton,
G. Holcomb and A. L. Jacobi.
Third squad.—R. H. Hellman, G.
J. Hatfield, G. M. Wessell, H. S.
Tracy, C. E. Mills and A. C. Talboy.
Fourth squad.—J. R. Richardson, B.
O. Bliven. J. A. Visel and H. G. Fer
guson.
The Sophomores entered are:
First squad.—S. Bryan, D. F. Con
way, W. C. Conrad, C. C. Shoemak
er, E. V. Rosenkranz and W. E. Hett
man.
Second squad.—P. J. Batkin, E. M.
Leaf, J. E. Shelton, S. A. Lindauer,
A. McMillan and A. B. Bianchi.
