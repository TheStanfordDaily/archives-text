# Women's Conference Convenes.
## 
### 
The first, meeting of the Presi
dent's Conference of Young Women
was held last evening at Dr. Jor
dan's residence. It was decided that,
some of the rooming houses on the
Campus were not well adapted for
both men and women students and
Dr. Snow was appointed to look into
the matter. The Women's League
was granted one representative to
the Conference.
