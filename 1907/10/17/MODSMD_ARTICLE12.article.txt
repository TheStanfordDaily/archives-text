# Annual Stag Tramp Friday Evening.
## 
### 
The annual Stag Tramp, which is
given under the auspices of the
Young Men's Christian Association,
will be conducted Friday evening.
The party will leave the Encina Gym
nasium at 7 o'clock and go directly
to the hills back of the University.
One of the features of the tram]) will
be a large bonfire and a lunch.
