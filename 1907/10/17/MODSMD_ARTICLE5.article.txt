# Dr. Gardner To Preach.
## 
### 
Reverend D. Charles Gardner will
be the preacher at the eleven o'clock
service in the Chapel on Sunday
morning. Mrs. Planchard, contralto,
who has sung at the past two ser
vices, will again be present on Sun
day morning. One of the selections
that she will render is, "O rest in j
the Lord."
