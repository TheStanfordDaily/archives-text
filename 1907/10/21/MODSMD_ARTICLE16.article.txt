# Schubert Club Organized By Women.
## 
### 
A number of university women
interested in choral music have or
ganized the "Schubert Club," to
study the works of the composer,
and present a light opera each year.
Professor H. L. Roberts, of Palo
Alto, director of the club, has chosen
the following thirty-two members, af
ter two try-outs:
