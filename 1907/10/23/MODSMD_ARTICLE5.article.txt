# Encina Smoker for Vancouver Men.
## 
### 
The visiting Vancouver Rugby
players will be entertained by Encina
Club at a smoker to be given next
week in Encina Club room. The date

for the affair lias not as yet been
definitely decided upon but it will
probably be Wednesday evening.
There will be an interesting pro
gram of boxing and wrestling match
es, Japanese fencing, stunt, men, and
musical numbers. The following com
mittee has been appointed to work
with the board of directors toward
the success of the entertainment: J.
S. Mitchell, 'OS, A. A. Murphy, '08,
J. F. Reilly, 'OS, D. P. Crawford, '09,
E. P. Tallant, '09.
