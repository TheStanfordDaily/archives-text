# SOPHOMORE PLAY TRYOUT.
## Meeting of All Sophomores Wishing To Try Out For 1910 Play Will Be Held Tomorrow. 
### 
The Sophomores who desire to try
out for the 1910 play, "The Manoeu
vers of Jane," which will be given
the early part of next semester, will
meet in room 231, tomorrow after
noon at 1:30. Coach Cross will give
a short synopsis of the play and dis
cuss the different characters.
The preliminary tryouts will start
as soon as the play can be abridged
and the parts typewritten.
