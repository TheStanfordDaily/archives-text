# Alumni Tickets At Bookstore.
## 
### 
For the convenience of those per
sons desiring to secure alumni tickets
for the Varsity contest, C. F. Wright,
'96, secretary and treasurer of the
Stanford Alumni Association will be
at the University Bookstore on Wed
nesday and Thursday from 10 to 12.
Tickets for seats in section Q, which
is reserved for the alumni, may be
purchased from him during those
hours.
