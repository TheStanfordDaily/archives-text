# FOOTBALL ASSEMBLY NEXT.
## W.T. Reid, Jr. and Dr. T.M. Williams of Palo Alto Will Address Students Thursday morning. 
### 
The next University Assembly will
be held at 10:15 Thursday, Novem
ber 7, and is to be devoted to foot
ball. The speakers are W. T. Reid,
Jr., of Belmont, and Dr. Thomas M.
Williams of Palo Alto. Reid has
been head football coach at Har
vard for several years, where he
has made an excellent record.
Dr. Williams, a graduate of this
institution, was one of the best cen
ters who ever played in California.
He was on the Stanford team for
four years and later gave a great
deal of assistance in coaching the
football teams.
