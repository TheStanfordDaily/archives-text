# Fraternities Meet in Opening Game.
## 
### 
Phi Kappi Psi will meet Kappa
Sigma in the first game of the inter
fraternity baseball series tomorrow
afternoon on the varsity diamond
4 o'clock. Kappa Sigma holds the
cup at. present, and has a strong team
for this season's play. Each nine
has been doing very light work in
preparation for the game, so that the
innings played may be reduced from
the regular number.
