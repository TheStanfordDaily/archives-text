# Football "Smoker" Tomorrow Night.
## 
### 
The Varsity football team will be
f.iven a "smoker" tomorrow nishi
by Skull and Snakes, the upperclass
honor society, at the Chi Psi house.
This will be an informal affair and
besides the coaches, trainer, and
members of the victorious team,
there will be several members of the
Faculty presont. The affair will be
gin at 8 o'clock.
