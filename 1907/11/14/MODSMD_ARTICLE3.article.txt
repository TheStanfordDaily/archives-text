# Kappa Sigma Wins Ball Game.
## 
### 
Kappa Signiii defeated Phi Kappa
I'si by a score of 15 to 5 in the first
game of the interfraternity baseball
series, played yesterday afternoon on
the varsity diamond. The game was
replete with exciting situations. ().
E. Wingate. '10, center fielder for
Phi Kappa I'si, aided in the scor
ing for his team by knocking out
a home run which netted his side two
tallies.
No game will be played tomorrow
afternoon on account of the Senior
Circus parade. The next game will
be played on Saturday morning and
will be between Beta Theta Pi and
Acacia.
