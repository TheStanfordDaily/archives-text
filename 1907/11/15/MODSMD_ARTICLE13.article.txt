# CONTRACT LET FOR FLUME.
## Colusa Stone Company To Do All the Work Except the Repairing of the Dam. 
### 
The contract for building the La
gunita Hume and digging the ditch
which is to replace most of the old
structure has been let by the Com
mission of Engineers to the Colusa
Stone Company. This company will
do all the work except that of re
pairing the dam which will be done
bj the Commission of Engineers.
The Commission started work this
afternoon and the other construction
will be begun as soon as men and
tools can be brought here. The con
tract with the Colusa Stone Com
pany provides for the building of a
new concrete culvert under the road
as well as the construction of the
flume and ditch.
