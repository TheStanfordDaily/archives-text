# CIVIC LEAGUE ORGANIZED.
## Campus Residents Form Society For the Promotion of Interests of the University Community. 
### 
Residents of the Campus last night
held a preliminary meeting for the
purpose of organizing a Civic League
at the University. The organization
is along the lines of the improve
ment clubs which are common in
the smaller cities of the state and is
intended to promote the interests of
the Campus community in the ab
sence of a municipal government.
The membership will embrace those
who are house-holders on the Cam
pus. Dr. W. Li. Wilbur and J. E.
McDowell were elected president and
secretary, respectively, at the meet
ing last, night.
