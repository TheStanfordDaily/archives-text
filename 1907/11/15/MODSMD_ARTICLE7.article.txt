# Noted Explorer Speaks Here Friday.
## 
### 
McDavid (r. Hogarth, Director of
the Cretan Exploration Fund, will
deliver an illustrated lecture on "The
Aegean and Crete," in the Chapel
Friday evening, at 8. Hogarth is a
noted writer and distinguished as an
explorer of Egypt, Cyprus and Asia
Minor. An interesting collection of
views of Greece will he shown. The
address is open to the public.
