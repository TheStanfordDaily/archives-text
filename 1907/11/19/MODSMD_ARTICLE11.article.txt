# Y.M.C.A. Planning Tramp Party.
## 
### 
Preparations are now being made
for a tramp party which will climb
Mount. Hamilton during the Thanks
giving vacation under the auspices
ot the Young Men's Christian Asso
ciation. The party will probably
leave here early Friday morning, re
maining in San Jose Friday night,
and starting the climb Saturday
morning. Both telescopes, the seis
mograph, the chronograph, and the
clocks will be opened for investiga
tion Saturday night.
