# SCHEDULE IS PREPARED
## END – SEMESTER EXAMINATIONS GIVEN DEC. 14-20. Tests Will Be Four Hours or Less In Length.—University Work Suspended During Period. 
### 
The Registrar's office has prepared
schedule for the regular end-sem
ester examinations, and it will be
ready for distribution immediately af
ter the Thanksgiving recess. The
tests begin Saturday, December 14,
and continue through the following
Friday. During this time all lectures,
recitations and laboratory work will
be suspended.
Examination periods are four hours
in length except in the case of a few
one and two-hour courses, but with
in these limits the actual length of
the tests to be given is optional with
the department or instructor in
charge. No preliminary or final ex
aminations will be given during the
week preceding the examination per
od.
