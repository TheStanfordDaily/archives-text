# Press Club Initiates New Members.
## 
### 
The Press Club initiated three new
members into its number this even
ing: F. A. Adams, '10, M. J. Helm
ick, '09, and D. H. Walker, Jr., '09.
Adams was a member of the staff
of the Daily Palo Alto until the be
ginning of this semester. He belongs
to the Encina Club. Helmick has
been prominent in art work for the
past year and is at present 011 the
art staff of the Chaparral. He is
a member of the Sigma Alpha Epsi
1011 fraternity. Walker has been a
member of the Daily Palo Alto staff
for two years and is at present one
of the associate editors of the paper.
He is a member of Encina Club.
