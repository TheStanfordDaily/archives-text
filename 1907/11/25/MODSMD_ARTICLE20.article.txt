# Palo Alto Women Entertain.
## 
### 
The members of the Palo Alto
League of Stanford Women enter
tained the Faculty women at a re
ception given Saturday afternoon in
the Playhouse in Palo Alto. A pro
gram was rendered consisting of the
following selections: vocal solo, Miss
L. C. Tallboy, '11; instrumental solo,
Miss O. Matchette, '08; piano solo,
Miss A. H. Gabel, '08; selection by
the Girls Glee Club. The ladies who
assisted in receiving were: Mrs. F.
Angell, Mrs. J. E. Matzke, Mrs. A. L.
Guerard, and Mrs. C. H. Gilbert.
