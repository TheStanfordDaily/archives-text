# 1910 Society Will Give Banquet.
## 
### 
The 1910 Debating Society last
night voted to have a banquet be
fore tile close of the semester and
M. Malone, H. Nelson and W, E.
Hettraan were appointed a committee
to make arrangements for the enter
tainment. The date of the event
has not been definitely fixed, but
will probably be the evening of De
cember 2.
H. P. Cheda and H. E. Smith were
elected to membership.
