# Will Continue Fall Track Work.
## 
### 
Although the fall track meet was
hold Friday afternoon, Captain Lan
agan wishes it understood that track
work is to continue until the final
examination week and is particular
ly anxious to have the Freshmen
keep up their training. Trainer
"Dad" Moulton will be on the track
every day and he hopes to get a
good line on the 1911 track mater
ial before Christmas as he was un
able to get acquainted with the new
men during the football season.
