# SUNDAY SERMON IN CHAPEL.
## Dr. D. Charles Gardner Preaches On "The Spirit of Thanksgiving." Special Preacher Next Sunday. 
### 
Sunday Dr. Gardner preached on
"The Spirit of Thanksgiving," and
mentioned among the causes foi
Thanksgiving, the blessings tempo
ral which we enjoy as a state and a
nation. He added a plea for recog
nition of blessings spiritual, home
and family, friendship and love, re
ligion and education. He said the
spirit of Thanksgiving is a healthy
spirit, an admirable tonic for spirit
ual depression.
Mrs. Blanchard rendered two solos.
Dr. Gardner announced that the spe
cial preacher for next Sunday will he
the Rev. Dr. W. H. Day, a prominent
Congregationalist of Los Angeles.
