# Y.W.C.A. Meets Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Young
Women's Christian Association will
be held Tuesday afternoon in the
Chapel. Mrs. Theresa llussell, of
the English department, will speak
upon the subject, "A Foolish Ques
tion." The special music will consist
of a vocal solo, "Open Thou Mine
Eyes," by Miss G. M. Davis, '10.
