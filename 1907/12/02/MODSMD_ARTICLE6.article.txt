# DOCTOR JORDAN HONORED.
## Decorated with the Order of the Rising Sun by Emperor of Japan. 
### 
President David Starr Jordan has
been decorated with the Order of the
Rising Sun, by his Imperial Majesty,
Mutsuhito, Emperor of Japan. The
Japanese government is at present
doing all in its power to bring about
a more cordial feeling with the Unit
ed States, and this action is in pur
suance of that, policy.
The Order of the Rising Sun is the
highest of all the honorary orders
in the bestowal of the Mikado. It
is given to those who have perform
ed some signal service for Japan, and
this is the first time that the deco
ration has been given to a foreigner.
