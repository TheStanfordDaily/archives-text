# THIRTY MEN TO TAKE TRIP.
## Personnel of Combined Musical Clubs for Northern Tour Is Announced. 
### 
Thirty men will make up the per
j sonnel of the Combined Musical
Clubs on their trip North. Of this
| number sixteen are from the Glee
and fourteen from the Mandolin
Club. The men who were success
! ful from the Glee Club are: first
tenors—J. J. Hill, 'OS, L. Y. Mattes,
'08, E. J. Swift, '08, E. S. Pridham,
'08; second tenors—C. 13. Bradford,
'09, F. H. Bernard, '08, W. W. Todd,
'09, H. E. Reed, '10, E. E. Chase,
'11; first bassos—R. S. Kellogg, '08,
N. Taylor, '10, R. H. Brown, '10,
E. L. Coffin, '11; second bassos—
A. D. Hughes, '08, M. Vestal, '08,
W. H. McConnell, '11.
The men from the Mandolin Club
who were successful are: first man
dolins —F. R. Lanagan, '08, R. A.
Gott,'o9, L. G. Avery',lo, L. J.Hart,
'10, A. H. Crary, '11; second man
dolins—G. B. McLain, '08, J. R.
Shoemaker, '11, H. P. Knight, '11;
guitars—E. K. Soper, '08; E. S.
Pridham, '08, F. D. Mahone, '08, S.
B. Dole, '09, F. G. Wilson, '10; cello
—H. B. Menardi, '08.
W. F. Turner, '08, will accompany
the club as manager and C. L. Rob
erts will go as advance agent.
