# Dr. Jordan Returns From the East.
## 
### 
President Jordan returned this
morning from a three-weeks trip to
New York, Washington, D. C., and
other eastern points. His chief pur
pose in going East was to attend
the meetings of the Board of Trus
tees of the Carnegie Foundation for
the Advancement of Teaching, and
the Committee on Agricultural Re
search.
Dr. Jordan is suffering from a se
vere cold contracted on his return
journey and is confined to his home.
