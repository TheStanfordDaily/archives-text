# Women's League To Hold Exhibit.
## 
### 
The next meeting of the Women's
League will take the form of a doll
exhibit to be held at Roble Hall dur
ing linal examination week. 105
dolls have been dressed under the
direction of the League, and these
will be packed at the meeting and
sent to the Nurses' Settlement in
San Francisco. The dolls will be
distributed among the children of
the settlement at Christmas time.
