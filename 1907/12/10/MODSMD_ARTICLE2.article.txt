# Mechanical Society Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The Mechanical Engineering Soci
ety held its last meeting for this
semester last night in room 58 En
cina Hall.
The following officers were elect
ed: president, L. F. Halloran, '08;
vice-president, J. M. Goodell, '08;
secretary-treasurer, E. H. Herbert,
'08. E. A. Rogers, '09, and D. H.
Walker, Jr., '09, were elected to
membership. Leslie Ellenwood was
made an honorary member.
L. F. Halloran, '08, gave a short
address in which he told of his ob
servations of engineering schools on
his trip through the East and in
Europe.
