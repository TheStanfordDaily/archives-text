# Arrange Dates For U. S. C. Meets.
## 
### 
Graduate Manager Knupp has fi
nally arranged the dates for the
spring track meets with the Univer
sity of Southern California. The
Cardinal team will meet the South
erners on their oval on March 7, and
the return contest will take place
on Stanford's track on March 21.
The gold footballs which were
granted by the Executive Committee
to the members of the Rugby team,
coaches and manager, came yester
day and are being distributed by
Manager Knupp today.
