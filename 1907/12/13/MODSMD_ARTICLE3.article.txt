# Sunday Sermon By Bishop Moreland.
## 
### 
The last sermon of the semester
will be preached by Bishop More
land. of Sacramento, in the Chapel
on Sunday morning at eleven o'clock.
Dr. Moreland is a. very able speak
er and was well received when he
delivered a Sunday sermon here last
year.
Two solos, "O ye that love the
Lord." from Barnby's "The Lord is
King," and "O Thou that lettest
good tidings," from Handel's "Mes
siah," will be sung by Mrs. Blanch
ard, of San Francisco.
