# ANNOUNCEMENTS.
## 
### 
Sophomore Cast—-Will meet in
room 200, Wednesday at 4:30.
Caniot Tryouts.—All men desir
ing to enter the Carnot tryouts must
hand their names to either J. F.
Reilly, 'OS, R. E. Hodge, '08, T. T.
Bennett, '08, G. B. Bush, '09, L.
Naw land, '08, or C. Coonan, '09, by
Friday noon.
1900 Quad Assessments—Should
be paid at. once at room 14 on the
Inner Quadrangle; hours: 1:30 to
2:30. Tuesday, Wednesday and
Thursday. Assessments may also be
paid in room 127 Encina Hall.
Those desiring to sit for pictures this
semester must pay the full assess
ment.
