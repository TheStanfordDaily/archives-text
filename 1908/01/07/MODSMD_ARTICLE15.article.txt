# Students Reach Home Cheaply.
## 
### 
It. is reliably reported that three
well-known underclassmen of the
University successfully eluded the
vigilance of the Southern Pacific
trainmen just after the close of col
lege and reached their homes in the
South after the expenditure of only a
few cents. The clever trio were 11.
B. Patrick, '10, of Visalia, R. B.
Wheeler, '11, and E. L. Hertel, '11,
both of Pasadena.
