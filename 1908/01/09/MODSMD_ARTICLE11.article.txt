# Six Stanford Men Admitted To Bar.
## 
### 
At the last Bar examinations six
Stanford men were admitted to prac
tice law in California. The follow
ing successfully passed the tests: C.
F. Wright, '01, H. C. Kelly, 'OG, A.
A. Molfino, ex-'O7, G. C. Halsey, 'OS,
li. YV. Cantrell, '10, \V. E. Davies,
'10. Molfino was a prominent var
sity football player in 1905 and 1900
and is at present practicing law in
San Francisco. Halsey is a well
known runner.
