Mini Kaph Mini —Important busi
ness meeting Tuesday at 4:30, in the

English Club. —Open meeting will
be held at the Alpha Phi house on
Monday evening at 8:15. Dr. Strong
will speak on "Robert Browning."
Electrical Engineering Society—
Business meeting Monday evening,
January 13, at 7:30, room 502.
Exemption Examination in Eng
lish Coni|K>sition. —An examination
in English Composition will be held
on Friday, January 10, at 2:30 p. m.
in room 200. All recommended stu
dents who have not taken a univer
sity examination in composition are
expected to take this test. Those
who pass are exempt from composi
tion while in the University, or they
are permitted to register in English
2c. Those who fail are expected to
take English 2a.
Choir —All who desire to become
members of the choir may consult
with Mr. Buehrer, director, or Doc
tor Gardner, Wednesday afternoons,
from 2 to 5, in the organ gallery of
Memorial Church.
1000 Quad Assessments—Should

be paid at once at room 14 on the
Inner Quadrangle; hours: 1:30 to
2:30. Tuesday, Wednesday and
Thursday. Assessments may also be
paid in room 127 Encina Hall.
Those desiring to sit for pictures this
semester must pay the full assess
ment.
