# Professor of Philology Returns.
## 
### 
Professor Ewald Fluegel, of the
English Philology department, has
returned to the University after a
two years' absence abroad. He was
granted a leave of absence in 1905
and has spent the intervening time
doing library work in connection
with a Chaucer Dictionary, which he
is compiling in England and Ger
many under a grant of the Carnegie
Institute of Washington, D. C. With
this semester he again takes up his
lecturing in the University.
