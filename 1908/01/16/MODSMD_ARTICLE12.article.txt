# Whitelaw Reid May Visit Campus.
## 
### 
Hon. Whitelaw Reid, American
embassador to the Court of St.
James, famous diplomat and states
man, who is also a trustee of the
University, is now at his country
home at Millbrae. He will be on
the Coast for about three weeks,
during which time he will probably
visit the Campus. Embassador Reid
has been a member of the Board of
Trustees since the foundation of the
University and has always been
keenly interested in the management
of its affairs.
