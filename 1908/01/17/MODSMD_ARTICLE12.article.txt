# Gives Lecture on Dreyfus Case.
## 
### 
Professor A. L. Guerard delivered
the first of his second series of lec
tures on the problems of modern
France yesterday afternoon. This
lecture dealt with the facts of the
"Dreyfus Case." This case is not
only of great personal and dramatic
interest but also illustrates the po
litical and religious life of France.
Professor Guerard's next lecture will
be given next. Thursday afternoon
and will deal with the moral aspects
of the "Dreyfus Case."
