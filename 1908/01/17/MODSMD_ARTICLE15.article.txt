# Board Will Improve Athletic Fields.
## 
### 
Besides applying to the Executive
Committee for aid in building the
new track bleachers, the Board of
Control last night voted to receive
bids for several contemplated im
provements on the various athletic
fields. It is the intention of the
Board to level up the portion of Stan
ford Field, outside the south gonl
posts and all other sunken places,
and lay out a baseball diamond with
a heavy netting backstop.
The supplementary football field
will be leveled and surfaced with
sawdust as soon as possible. Walks
will also be putin around this field
and concrete benches installed for
spectators.
