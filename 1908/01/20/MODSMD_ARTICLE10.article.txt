Monday, January 20, 190 S.
It has been announced that any
class not having a sufficient number
of players on (he diamond, exclu
sive of those on the varsity squad,
will forfeit its right to compete in
the interclass series. Unless more
of the Senior class come out for
practice at once this class will be
considered as having given up its
right to compete in the interclass
games. As a matter of class pride
and for the sake of making the in
terclass series more interesting, the
Seniors should take immediate ac
tion in this matter.
