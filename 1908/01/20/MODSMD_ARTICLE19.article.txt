# Universities' Association Meets.
## 
### 
The annual meeting of the Asso
ciation of American Universities and
Colleges was held at. Ann Arbor,
Michigan, on January 10. Stanford
was represented by Professor E. D.
Adams, of the History department.
The University of Michigan was
elected president of the Association,
and Harvard secretary. Illinois,
Missouri, and Minnesota were admit
ted to membership.
