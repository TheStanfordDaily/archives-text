# Seattle Minister To Preach Sunday.
## 
### 
Rev. Henry Gowen, D. D., of the
Trinity Parish Rectory, Seattle,
Washington, will deliver the sermon
in the Chapel on Sunday morning.
Dr. Gowen is a. graduate of St.
Augustine's College, Canterbury,
and is at present well known as a
preacher of the gospel in Seattle. He
is the author of several books, "The
Paradise and the Pacific" and"The
Kingdom of Man," being two of
his best works.
The Chapel choir has a special
program arranged for the Sunday
service. "Ye shall dwell in the
Land" will be rendered by J. Stain
er. Miss Richardson will sing "Fa
ther of Heaven," by G. Handel.
