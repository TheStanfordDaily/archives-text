# Women Give Entertainment.
## 
### 
The Women's Athletic Association
entertained the women of the Uni-
versity yesterday afternoon at Ro-
ble Gymnasium. In spite of the
rain the hall was crowded and the
various numbers were enthusiastic
ally received. The following organi-
zations contributed some form of
amusement: Roble Hall, Madrono
Hall, Walden Club, Mrs. Graham's
House, Manteistn Club, Delta (lam
ma, Kappa Kappa Gamma, Gamma
Phi Beta, Kappa Alpha Theta, Pi
Beta Phi and Alpha Phi.
