# Phi Delta Phi Holds Convention.
## 
### 
The first annual convention of the
Seventh Province of the Phi Delta
Phi legal fraternity, including the
chapters at Seattle, Portland, Los An
geles, San Francisco and Stanford,
was held in San Francisco Saturday
afternoon and evening. Representa
tives were present from all the chap
ters except that at Seattle, the lo
cal chapter being represented by sev
eral members. In the evening a ban
quet was held which was attended
by over thirty Phi Delta Phi men and
at which L. R. Weinnman, 'ot>, act
ed as toastmaster.
