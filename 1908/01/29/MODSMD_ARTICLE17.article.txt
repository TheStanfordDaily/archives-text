# To Address Education Club.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the
Education Club, held yesterday,
the following officers were elect
ed for the semester: J. W. Mey
ers, '07, president; C. L. Phelps,
'10, secretary-treasurer.
The next meeting will be address
ed by Mr. Meredith, principal of the
Hoitt School, on the subject, "Why
Do We Educate." The meeting will
he held in the Education Library
Friday evening, January 1, at 7:30.
