# Senior Honor Cards Sent Out.
## 
### 
The 1909 "Quad" board has sent
out 330 post cards to the Seniors
now registered in college for the
purpose of obtaining the honors that
they have taken during their un
dergraduate course. In compiling
this list, the board has selected the
names of all Seniors who are up for
graduation this semester. They have
deduced all those whose names ap
peared in the honor section of the
1908 "Quad." All the Seniors whose
names appeared in the last "Quad"
and whose names are not up for
graduation were also sent cards
These cards have to be mailed to the
"Quad" board on or before February
5.
