# Latest Y. M. C. A. Plans.
## 
### 
Following the regular men's devo
tional exercises yesterday afternoon,
at which Prof. O. M. Johston spoke
on "The Value of Open Mindedness,"
a special joint meeting of the Young
Men's and Young Women's Christian
Associations was held, Prof. A. T.
Murray, of the Department of Greek,
addressing the members on the im
portance of observing the special day
of prayer set apart for college stu
dents.
A reception will be held under the
joint auspices of the two associations
the latter part of the month, prob
ably in Roble Gymnasium. Complete
plans will be laid at a committee
meeting which has been called for
tomorrow afternoon.
