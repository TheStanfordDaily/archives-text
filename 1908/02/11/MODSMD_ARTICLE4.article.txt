# FIRST ELIGIBLE LIST
## NAMES MEN WHO MAY COMPETE AGAINST U . S. C. Eligibles Who Are Not on the List Should Consult with Captain Lanagan. 
### 
The preliminary list of eligibles
for the meet with the University of
Southern California in Los Angeles
on March 7 has been made out by
Captain Lanagau. All the men on
the squad who are eligible to com
pete, but whose names do not appear
on this list should consult with
him so that they may be put on the
filial list.
This contest with the Southern
ers will lie the first outside meet in
which the Cardinal athletes will com
pete this season. As the California
team will later meet the Southern
men, the two meets will furnish a
reliable basis for the comparison of
the performances of our men with
those of the Blue and Gold athletes.
