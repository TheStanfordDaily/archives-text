# Will Excuse Some Track Men.
## 
### 
All 'track men who are taking
gymnasium work and are placed up
on the rubbing list by Trainer Moul
ton will be excused from regular
work in the gymnasium. In lieu of
the apparatus work in the gymna
sium, work upon the itrack three
times a week will be required, ab
sences being considered as regular
cuts and must be made up.
