# California Gets Gift for Research.
## 
### 
Mrs. Annie M. Alexander has giv
en to the University of California
the sum of $7,000 each year for sev
en years, for field and research work
among the birds and reptiles of Cal
ifornia. The university has appropri
ated $7,000 to erect a building in
which the work may be carried on,
and the collected specimens housed.
