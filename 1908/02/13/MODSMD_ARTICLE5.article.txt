# Dates for Payment of Fees.
## 
### 
All university laboratory, syllabus,
and book fees become due and pay
able at the Treasurer's office on Mon
day, February 17. Any student fail
ing to pay the required fee on or
before March 2, will incur an addi
tional charge of two dollars, and if
payment is delayed later than March
16, registration in the University will
be cancelled.
