# Associations Will Entertain Jointly.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the social com
mittee of the Young Women's Chris
tian Association held this afternoon,
arangements were completed for an
entertainment to he given jointly by
the Young Women's and Young
Men's Christian Associations on
March 7 in Ruble Gymnasium. The
entertainment will include at least
two "stunts," one to be given by each
association, and a farce in which
members of both will take part.
