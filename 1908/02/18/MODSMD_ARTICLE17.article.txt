# Discuss Equal Suffrage League.
## 
### 
A number of women students held
:i meeting Friday afternoon at. the
home of Mrs. C. A. Duniway, where
plans for a Stanford equal suffrage
league were discussed. The pro
posed organization met with great
favor among many of the students
and a committee was appointed to
look over the constitution of the
Bryn Mawr branch of the college
equal suffrage league. A report will
be made at the next meeting on Feb
ruary 20, when more definite ad
vances will be made.
