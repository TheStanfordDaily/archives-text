# Women Are Playing Cricket.
## 
### 
Practice in cricket for this semes
ter is being held every Thursday af
ternoon at I o'clock and every Fri
day at 1:45, at Ruble Gymnasium.
The instruction is being given by Ma
jor Murdette and will eventually
evolve a thorough knowledge of the
game. Hatting and bowling are be
ing studied now and no game will be
played until the players are more
proficient in this. Anyone desiring
to enter the class may do so by con
fering with Miss F. Holton, of the
Hygiene department.
