# Marriage of Professor Wing.
## 
### 
Professor Charles B. Wing, of the
Civil Engineering department, and
Mrs. Merriam Colt Browne, of Palo
Alto, were quietly married at noon
today at the residence of Professor
H. W. Rolfe, at Menlo Heights. Rev.
Mr. Gardner officiated at the cere
mony and it was attended by inti
mate friends of the couple.
