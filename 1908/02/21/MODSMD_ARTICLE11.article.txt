# Date for Final Interclass Match.
## 
### 
On the afternoon of Saturday, Feb
ruary 29, when the varsity nine is
in Los Angeles, the 1908 and 1910
class teams will cross bats on the
Campus diamond in the final game
of the present interclass series. In
the contest with the Juniors yester
day the Seniors displayed unexpected
speed, and the deciding game with
the Sophomores should be fast and
close.
