# NAME PROVISIONAL CAST
## COMMITTEE SELECTS CANDIDATES FOR OPERA. First Act of "Butterfly Isle" To Be Rehearsed Tomorrow Afternoon. 
### 
"The Butterfly Isle," which will be
produced by the Junior class in As
sembly Hall, on the evening of March
26, will be given its first trial to
morrow afternoon when the entire
cast of principals and chorus will
rehearse the first act of the opera.
Chairman C. L. Roberts, of the com
mittee in charge, announced this af
ternoon the names of those from
among whom the cast and chorus
will be selected. Fifteen men and
six women have been reserved by
the committee to compete for the
principal parts, fifteen in number. A
chorus of twenty-four women and
twelve men is planned by the op
era committee.
