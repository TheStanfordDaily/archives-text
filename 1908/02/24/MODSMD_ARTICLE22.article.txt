# Plan Capitola Conference.
## 
### 
The intercollegiate committees of
the Young Women's Christian Asso
ciation around the bay held a con
ference on the University Campus
Saturday afternoon. The meeting
was held for the purpose of discuss
ing plans for the annual Capitola
conference. Miss Gertrude Davidson,
a graduate of the University of Cali
fornia and State Secretary of the
Young Women's Christian Associa
tion, gave a short talk before the as
sembled delegates.
