# Faculty Club May Build.
## 
### 
Articles of incorporation of the
Stanford Faculty Club were filed in
Sau Jose last week. The purpose of
the club is to build a new clubhouse.
The directors are Professors G. H.
Marx, of the Civil Engineering de
partment; C. B. Wing, of the Civil
Engineering department; W. A. Man
ning, of the Department of Mathe
matics; J. R. Slonaker, of the Physi
ology department; and A. B. Clark,
of the Drawing department.
