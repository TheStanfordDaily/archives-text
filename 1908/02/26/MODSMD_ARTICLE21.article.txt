# Coe Made President of Guild Board.
## 
### 
At a special meeting of the Guild
Board this afternoon, the resigna
tion of G. J. Presley, '07, from the
board was tendered and accepted.
G. C. Coe, '08, was then elected pres
ident of the board, this office being
made vacant by Presley's withdraw
al. The new president has been a
member of the board since his Ju
nior year and its secretary during
the past two semesters.
