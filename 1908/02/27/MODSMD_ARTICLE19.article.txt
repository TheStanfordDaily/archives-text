# Equal Suffrage League Is Forming.
## 
### 
A number of university women in
terested in equal suffrage met yester
day afternoon at the home of Mrs.
C. A. Duniway, when a constitution
was drafted for an equal suffrage
league to be organized on the Cam
pus. The constitution will be passed
upon at the next meeting and officers
will be elected who will continue
in office during the next semester.
