# Date Set for Basketball Match.
## 
### 
This noon, at a meeting of the ex
ecutive board of the Women's Ath
letic Association, it was reported that
satisfactory arrangements had been
made with the University of Cali
fornia to hold the first intercolleg
iate basketball game in Berkeley,
Saturday, March 7. It was decided to
award the members of the team new
basketball suits.
Arrangements are being made with
Mills' College for a tennis tourna
ment, and the date of April 11 was
set for the match with California,
which will be played on the Stanford
courts.
