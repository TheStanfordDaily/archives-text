# Dr. Gardner Preaches Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
Rev. D. Charles Gardner delivered
the regular Sunday sermon in the
Chapel, taking for his subject,
"Lent."
The preacher explained the pur
pose of Lent and remarked on the
increased observance of it by German
and English Protestants. He express
ed a desire that all devote a great
deal of time during Lent to prayer,
and told of the benefits one would
derive from this expenditure of time.
A solo, "Seek ye the Lord," was
sung by F. W. McNulty, '09, accom
panied by the chorus. M. Vestal, '08,
sang "Fear Ye Not, O Israel," com
posed by Dudley Buck. Both musi
cal numbers were well rendered.
