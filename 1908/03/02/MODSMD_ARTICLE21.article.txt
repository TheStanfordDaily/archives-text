# Mathematicians Met Saturday.
## 
### 
The San Francisco Section of the
American Association of Mathematic
ians met with the Stanford Section
at a luncheon at the Stanford Inn,
Saturday. There are two meetings
of this association a year, one held
in San Francisco in September, the
other held in February. A series of
papers were read by members of
both sections. Prof. L. M. Hoskins,
Prof. H. F. Blickfeldt, and Mr. J.
D. Suter, of Stanford, read papers.
