# Final Interclass Game Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The final game of the interclass
series, which was postponed from last
Saturday on account of the inclem
ent weather, will be called 011 the
Campus diamond tomorrow afternoon
at 3:30. The Seniors and Sopho
mores comprise the contending nines,
both of these teams having triumph
ed over their rivals in the prelimin
ary matches of the series. An ad
mission of twenty-five cents will be
charged.
