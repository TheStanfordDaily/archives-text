# Social Service Club to Meet.
## 
### 
The regular open meeting of the
Social Service Club will be held next
Wednesday evening at the Alpha Phi
house at S o'clock. Mr. Frank Stone
will address the meeting on the work
of the Seaman's Institute of San
Francisco. Mr. St one has been con
nected with this institute for many
years and is in a position to give a
very instructive and interesting talk.
All interested in social service work
are invited to attend.
