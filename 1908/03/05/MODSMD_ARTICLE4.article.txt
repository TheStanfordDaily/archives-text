# TICKETS SELL AT GOOD RATE.
## Junior Prom. Committee Will Soon Be Able To Figure Accurately on Resources for Dance. 
### 
Tickets for the Junior Prom, are
now on sale at the University Hook
store. About three-fourths of the
number which the committee h;is is
sued have already been disposed of
and the remainder are going at a
steady rate. When nearly all the
tickets have been sold, the committee
will be able to make an accurate esti
mate of the amount of money which
will be available to make the Prom,
a success. The present rate of sale
indicates that final decision can be
made in the near future on a number
of important proposed features for
the Junior dance.
