# Jenkin Lloyd Jones Will Preach.
## 
### 
Reverend Jenkin Lloyd .Tones, of
Chicago, will be the special preacher
at. the regular Sunday service in the
Chapel. Mr. Jones is a member of
the University Extension Staff of the
University of Chicago, as lecturer in
English, and is also director of the
Abraham Lincoln Center, a public
education institution of Chicago. It
is said of him that he is one of the
most prominent preachers in the Uni
ted States, and also one of the most
progressive and liberal of men.
