# Starr Goes to the Philippines.
## 
### 
Professor Frederick Starr, of the
University of Chicago, left for the
Far East to spend three months in
search of an unknown Caucasian
race supposed to exist in the Phil
ippines, and may possibly go to in
land China, where the Lolos, another
mysterious white race, exists. He
said he would carry no firearms, but
would take several graphophones
along.
