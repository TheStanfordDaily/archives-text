###

Stanford women this morning on the
Harmon Gymnasium courts at Ber
keley. The final score was 20 to
3. The superior size of the Califor
nia team gave them a decided ad
vantage and they outplayed Stan
ford from start to finlsn.
At the end of the first half the
score stood 11 to 2 in favor of Cal

This is the first of a series of three
games for the intercollegiate cham
