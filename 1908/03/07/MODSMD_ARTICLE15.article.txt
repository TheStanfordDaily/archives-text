# TENNIS TRYOUTS PLAYED.
## G.E. Dole. Gowan, and Mann Are Three Players Left.-Finals on Monday. 
### 
The preliminary tournament for
the singles team was held on the
Encina courts today. In the pre
liminaries the only match was be
tween E. W. Borough, '09, and P.
B. Delano, '08, the latter winning
by a score of 6-1, 6-1.
In the first round the Moore-Dole
match was the first game played. G.
E. Dole, '08, taking the honors by
a score of 6-3, 8-6. A. J. Gowan, '09,
won from A. H. Matthewson, '08,
by default. The Gott-Clarke match
followed which resulted in favor of
R. A. Gott, '09, by the score of 6-2,
6-3.
The Mann-Delano match was the
hardest fought contest, and lasted
the entire afternoon. Delano won
the first set by a score of 6-3,
while Mann took the last two sets
by the score of 6-3, 10-8. The last
set was closely contested throughout.
From the start the games alternat
ed between the two men up to the
eighth game, when Mann took a
brace and won the two following
games, which completed the set. By
Gowan winning in the semi-finals
from Gott by the score of 6-0, 6-8,
6-3, the winner of the tournament
lies between Gowan, Mann and Dole.
The semi-final game between Mann
and Dole was postponed this after
noon. The finals will be played
Monday at 9 o'clock.
