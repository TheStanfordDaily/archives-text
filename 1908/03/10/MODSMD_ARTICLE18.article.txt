# MISS VERNE WILL PLAY.
## Talented Pianist To Appear at Play-house in Palo Alto Next Friday Night. 
### 
Miss Adela Verne, of Southampton,
England, will play Friday evening,
March 13, at a piano recital to be
given under the auspices of the Fort
nightly Musical Club, in the Play
house, in Palo Alto.
Miss Verne has played all over the
United States and on the Continent,
and has recently toured Alaska and
Australia. Everywhere she has been
received with the utmost enthusi
asm. Paderewski has given her the
greatest praise for her technique, her
intelligent conception, and for her
depth of feeling. He has given her
instruction whenever the occasion of
fered.
