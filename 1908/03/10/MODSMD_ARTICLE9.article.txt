# Interesting Sunday Sermon.
## 
### 
Rev. Jenkin Lloyd .Jones, the noted
Chicago divine, preached the Sunday
sermon in the Chapel before a large
audience, taking as his subject "No
blesse Oblige." In referring to the
higher education, he laid great em
phasis upon the duty which the col
lege graduate owes to his country
because of his superior training. The
speaker deplored the selfish and com
mercial spirit that characterizes mod
ern business and political life.
