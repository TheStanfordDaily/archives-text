# TO PLAY CALIFORNIA.
## Women's Basketball Teams Meet in Second Intercollegiate Contest Tomorrow. 
### 
The second basketball game of the
series scheduled with California will
be played tomorrow morning at 11
o'clock at Roble Gymnasium. Should
this result in a victory for Stanford,
the deciding game will be played on
some neutral field within the next
two weeks. The line-up of the Stan
ford team will not be announced
until immediately before the game is
called.
