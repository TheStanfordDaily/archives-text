# Health Board Advises Vaccination.
## 
### 
The following notice was issued
today by the county health officer
and Dr. Snow wishes it. called to the
attention of the Student Body:
"In view of the danger of attend
ing public gatherings at this time, I
would recommend that, all persons
expecting to attend the coming circus
or any public gathering should take
the precaution of being vaccinated.
"WM. SIMPSON, M. D.
"County Health Officer.
"This advice is seconded by the
board of health of Palo Alto."
"GEORGE J. PEIRCE,
"Chairman."
