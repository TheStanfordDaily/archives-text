# ALUMNAE TO MEET HERE.
## One Hundred Women To Visit Campus as Guests of Cap and Gown. 
### 
More than one hundred members
of the Pacific Coast branch of the
Association of Collegiate Alumnae
will be on the Campus Saturday
when they will hold their annual
out-of-town meeting. They come at.
the invitation of Cap and Gown and
will be entertained by them while
here. A luncheon at Roble has been
planned and a reception will be giv
en in the afternoon in the hall of
the Zoology building. An attempt will
be made to have them meet all the
ladies of the Faculty and the Senior
women.
