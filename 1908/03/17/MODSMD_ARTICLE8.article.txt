# University Hand Begins Practice.
## 
### 
The University Band held its first
practice of the present semester at
the Gymnasium last night. It. is the
intention of the band to play at the
Freshman and Varsity track meets
and at the coming baseball games.
The band will also play on the morn
ing of Junior Day during the sports
to be held at the lake. The band
will practice regularly hereafter.
