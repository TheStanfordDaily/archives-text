# Want Juniors tor Aquatic Sports.
## 
### 
W. A. Robertson, '09, and G. L.
Goodell, '09, the Junior Day commit
tee, have been appointed to receive
the names of all Juniors who wish
to enter the aquatic sports which will
be held on Lagunita on Junior Day.
All men who wish to represent 1909
in the interclass water tournament
should hand their names to either
Robertson or Goodell immediately.
