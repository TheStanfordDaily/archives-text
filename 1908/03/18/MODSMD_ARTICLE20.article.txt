# Debating Societies Will Not Meet.
## 
### 
The regular weekly meetings of
the Euphronia and Nestoria Literary
societies will not be held next Tues
day evening owing to the fact that
the preliminary and final tryouts for
the intercollegiate team come on
Monday and Wednesday evening re
spectively. Both of the societies will
resume their meetings the first Tues
day after the Spring vacation.
