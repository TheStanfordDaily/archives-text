# Work Begins on Track Bleachers.
## 
### 
The material for the new track
bleachers is nearly all on the ground,
and is being cut ready for immed
iate use when the actual work of
construction begins, which will be
in the course of the next few days.
The foundation is now complete and
there seems to be nothing that will
interfere with the rapid advance
ment. of the work.
