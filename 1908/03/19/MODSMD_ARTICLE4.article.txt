# Actors Will Appear In Opera.
## 
### 
Chairman A. B. Clark, of the Stu
dent Affairs Committee, announced at
5:30 this afternoon that the commit
tee had decided that, the two prin
cipal actors in the Junior Opera, who
were among the men suspended by
the committee on Tuesday, would be
allowed to take their parts in the
Junior production. This action was
taken after G. N. Richardson, of the
Junior Opera committee, had appear
ed before the committee and
asked for such a concession.
