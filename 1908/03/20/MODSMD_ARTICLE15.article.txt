# Y.W.C.A. Meeting on Wednesday.
## 
### 
The regular weekly meeting of the
Young Women's Christian Associa
tion will he held on next Wednes
day afternoon at 4:40 in (he Uni
versity Chapel. Miss Theresa Wil
bur, Stanford, '03, Miss Caroline
Dow, a graduate of Vassar, and Miss
M. L. Squire, '07, will speak upon
different phases of the spring con
ference at Capitola.
A special musical program has
been arranged.
