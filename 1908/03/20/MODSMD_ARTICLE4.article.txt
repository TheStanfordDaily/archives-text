# Junior Opera Tickets on Sale.
## 
### 
Tickets for tho Junor Opera, the
"Butterfly Isle," which is to be giv
en in Assembly Hall on the even
ing of March 26, will be placed on
siile Monday morning at the Hook
store on the Campus and at Hie
Stanford Pharmacy in Palo Alto.
Owing to the action of the Stu
dent Affairs Committee in allowing
students who had been suspended
to take part in the production, the
presentation of the opera is assured.
No seats have been reserved by the
committee and purchasers will be
served in the order of their arrival.
Tickets will be sold for the usual
ju ices of $1.00, 75 and 50 cents.
