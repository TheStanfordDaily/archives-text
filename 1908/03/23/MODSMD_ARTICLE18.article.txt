# Junior Plans Not Interfered With.
## 
### 
Drs. Snow and Wilbur have de
cided that the case of smallpox ex
isting need not interfere with the
Junior Prom and Opera, as even if
there is infection, no cases can in
cubate and develop in that time.
Prom Programs can be secured
Tuesday and Wednesday night at
room 127, Encina.
