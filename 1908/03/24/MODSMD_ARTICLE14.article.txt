# Professor Hempl To Go East.
## 
### 
Professor George Hempl, of the
Germanic Language department,
will leave tomorrow to attend the
second annual meeting of the Sim
plified Spelling Board, which will be
held at the Waldorf-Astoria, April
7 and 8.
The purpose of the meeting is to
transact the regular business and to
discuss plans and future policy. A
dinner will be given on the evening
of April 7, at which Andrew Carnegie
will preside.
Dr. Jordan is also a member of
this board, but it was impossible
for him to attend the present meet
ing.
