# T6o Address University Women.
## 
### 
Miss Theresa Wilbur, Stanford,
'05, will address the University wo
men Wednesday afternoon at 4:40 in
the Chapel under the auspices of the
Young Women's Christian Associa
tion. Miss Wilbur is national secre
tary of the Young Women's Christ
ian Associations of America, and will
lead the spring conference at Capitola
during the spring vacation. She will
speak upon different phases of the
conference.
