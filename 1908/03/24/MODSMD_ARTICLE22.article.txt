# Work On Bleachers Progressing.
## 
### 
The construction of the new track
bleachers is now progressing rapid
ly and will be pushed to completion
as soon as possible. The supporting
framework is nearly all in place and
the other material is being cut. ready
to fit into place without any delay.
If the fair weather continues the
work should be finished within two
weeks.
