# U.C. PLAYS HERE TOMORROW
## California Baseball Nine Will Meet Santa Clara On Our Diamond. 
### 
According to the present arrange
ments, the California baseball team
will visit the Campus tomorrow af
ternoon and play a game with the
Santa Clara College nine at 3
o'clock. This contest will be held
here for the purpose of giving the
Blue and Gold players an opportun
ity to be accustomed to our diamond,
where the first intercollegiate match
is scheduled to take place on April
4. The game tomorrow afternoon
will give the Stanford community
its first opportunity of watching the
Colifornia players in action.
The admission to this game will
bo twenty-five cents.
