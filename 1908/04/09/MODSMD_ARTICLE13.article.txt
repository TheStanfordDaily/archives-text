# Geology Society Meets.
## 
### 
The Geology Society met last night
at the Phi Kappa Psi house. After
the regular order of business, Mr.
Ralph McFadden, '05, gave a very
interesting talk on the "Cobalt Min
ing District." Mr. McFadden has
had a great deal of practical exper
ience and his talk proved interesting
to the members present.
