# Electrical Engineers Meet.
## 
###

Stanford branch of the American In
stitute of Electrical Engineers, held
last night, Mr. W. F. Lamme, of the
Westinghonse Electric and Manufac
turing Company, gave a very inter
esting lecture, illustrating it by
slides, on "Single Phase Railway."
