# President Jordan Speaks on Japan.
## 
### 
President Jordan spoke at Equality
Hall in San Francisco last night be
fore the Socialist Club on "Life In
Japan." The speaker stated that
the Japanese are not so hardy as the
people of Northern Europe and Amer
ica, and that they do not possess the
mental ability of the Caucasians. He
said that it would be many years
before the Japanese are able to de
velop their natural resources.
