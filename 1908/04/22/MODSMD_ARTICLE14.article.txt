# Final Work on Lighting System.
## 
### 
The actual work of erecting poles
and making final connections for the
system of electric lighting on the
Campus has been begun. All the
material has arrived and the work
is being rushed to completion. Or
namental iron work will surmount
the poles and will support the six
and six-tenth ampere arc lights that
are to be installed. The main ca
bles have been laid throughout the
course where the lights will be
placed.
