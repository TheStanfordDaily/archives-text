# Prof. Show Writes for Magazine.
## 
### 
Professor A. B. Show, of the His
tory department, who is now spend
ing a leave of absence in Nevada, is
contributing a series of articles on
"The Truth About Rawhide" to the
"Death Valley Magazine," a month
ly published at Rhyolite. A recent
issue also contains a poem from the
pen of Dr. Show.
