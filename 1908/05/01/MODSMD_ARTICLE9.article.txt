# Biological Society Meets.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the recently or
ganized Stanford Biological Society,
held yesterday afternoon, the follow
ing officers were elected for the en
suing year: president, C. V. Burke,
'07; secretary, F. \V. Weymouth, '09.
The society draws its members from
students of the Zoology, Botany and
Entomology departments, and has for
Its object the presentation of orig
inal papers on various biological top
ics upon which its members are work
ing.
