# Law Association to Hold Meeting.
## 
### 
The Law Association will hold its
annual open meeting in the Chapel
Monday afternoon, May 18, at 2. The
feature of the meeting will be an
address by Professor J. S. Partridge,
who gives the courses in California
Practice and Procedure. He will
speak on "The Right to Counsel and
the Attorney's Privilege." The meet
ing will be open to all who care to
attend.
