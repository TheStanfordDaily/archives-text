# Alumni Luncheon Planned.
## 
### 
"The twelfth annual luncheon of
the Stanford Alumni Association will
be held at Roble Hall, on Tuesday,
May 19, at. one o'clock p. m. A cor
dial invitation is hereby extended to
the Faculty and to the members of
the Senior class to be present. Plates
will cost $1.25 each. Tickets have
been placed on sale at the Regis
trar's Office. All of those desiring
to be present are requested to pro
cure their tickets promptly, and, if
possible, by the 14th of May, so that
we may know how many plates to
provide for at the luncheon.
"C. F. WRIGHT,
"Secretary Stanford Alumni Associa
tion."
