# Must Sign Class Roll at Once.
## 
### 
The Senior class roll and the roll
to he used by the permanent class
secretary to aid him in completing
the class roll, should be signed at
once as they will soon be taken
down. The permanent class secre
tary roll should be signed by all can
didates for graduation this May,
those who expect to graduate in
September or Christmas, 1908, and
also all those who entered with the
Class of 1908.
At present only 140 members have
signed these rolls. The rolls are on
the desk at the Registrar's Office
and can be signed any time during
the office hours of the Registrar.
