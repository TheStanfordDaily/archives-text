# Electrical Engineers Meet.
## 
### 
At the last meeting of the Stan
ford University branch of the Ameri
can Institute of Electrical Engineers
for this semester, held last night,
Robert D. Brooke, '09, was elected
to membership. The following officers
were elected for next year: chairman,
W. C. North, '09: secretary, A. J.
Gowan, '09; treasurer, S. B. Dole,
'09; librarian, H. H. Buell, '09.
