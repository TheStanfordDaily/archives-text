# C.K. Field, '95, Will Edit "Alumnus."
## 
### 
C. K. Field, '95, the well known
Stanford writer, was elected editor of
the "Stanford Alumnus" at a recent
meeeting of the executive committee
of the Stanford Alumni Association.
The new editor is noted in journalis
tic circles, prominently for the "Stan
ford Stories," written by Will Irwin,
ex-'OO, and himself. He also held the
editorship of the "Overland Monthly"
at one time.
O. A. Wilson, '04, will continue the
managership of the Alumni publi
cation.
The first number of the "Alumnus"
for this year will lie published this
month.
