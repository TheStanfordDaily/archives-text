# Gymnasium Facilities Extended.
## 
### 
During the summer a great many
improvements have boen made at
both Roble and Bncina gymnasiums.
Four regulation outdoor handball
courts have been installed at. the En
cina gymnasium and a new floor has
been laid. Every effort has been made
for appropriate outdoor sports in con
nection with the gymnasium work. The
outdoor drill floors have been scraped
and re-oiled.
The old track has been repaired
and will be used in connection with
the gymnasium courses. The old foot
ball field is now in condition for la
crosse. The Roble gymnasium field
has been improved and two now hand
ball courts have been constructed.
