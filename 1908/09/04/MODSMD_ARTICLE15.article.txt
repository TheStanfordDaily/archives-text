# Will Entertain Geology Students.
## 
### 
Next Wednesday evening, Septem
ber 9, at 8 o'clock, Prof. J. P. Smith,
according to his custom, will receive
the members of the Geological society
and the Geology and Mining Seniors
at his residence, 1335 Cowper Street,
Palo Alto. The students will assem
ble in the back yard, where a camp
er's meal will be cooked and served.
The meeting will be as unique as it
can be made, it being understood that
the guests will wear the regulation
engineering field costume of upper
class men, viz., blue shirts and cor
duroys.
