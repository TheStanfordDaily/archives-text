# Rare Fish Presented to Stanford.
## 
### 
J. P. Latronal lias presented a very
remarkable specimen of the species of
fish called "The King of the Macker~
els" to the University. There are
only three other specimens known,
one of which was taken by Doctor
Jordan in Honolulu in 1901. The fish
that was presented by Latronal was
found at Oceano, Cal., and is consid
ered a very valuable specimen.
