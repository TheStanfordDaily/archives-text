# L.E. Bassett Resumes Work Here
## 
###

department has returned from Eu
rope, where he has been studying for
the past year, and will resume his
work with the department as an assis
tant professor of elocution. Professor
Bassett is a graduate of Stanford with
the class of '01, his university career
