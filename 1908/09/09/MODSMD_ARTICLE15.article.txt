# Political Registration to End Soon.
## 
### 
Voters who have not registered
since January 1, 1908, must re-register
by September 23, in order to be al
lowed to vote at the Presidential elec
tion. A. G. Walker, the deputy clerk,
will register voters at the office of
Senator Marshall Black in Palo Alto.
Changes in precinct registration may
be made up to October 9.
