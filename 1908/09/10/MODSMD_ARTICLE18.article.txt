# Prof. Guerard Announces New Course
## 
### 
Announcement is made by Prof. A.
L. Guerard of the French department
of a new course in advanced syntax
and reading. It is intermediate be
tween French 2 and French 5, and
will be held Tuesdays and Thursdays

announced on the bulletin board.
