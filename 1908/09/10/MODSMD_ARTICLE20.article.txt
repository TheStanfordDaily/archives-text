# Freshman Meeting Postponed.
## 
### 
When the Freshman meeting sched
uled for 1:10 o'clock today was called
to order by the chairman, A. E. Ehr
horn, '10, president of the Junior
Class, it was decided to postpone any
transaction of business till next Mon
day, September 14, because of the very
small representation from the Fresh
man class. Ehrhorn urged that all
Freshmen attend, for the election of
officers will take place. The meeting
will be held in room G8 at 1 o'clock
sharp.
