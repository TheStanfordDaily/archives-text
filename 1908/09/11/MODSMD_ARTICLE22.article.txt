# Shelton Appointed to Fill Vacancy.
## 
### 
Owing to a vacancy on the board of
directors of the 1910 "Quad," left by
C. H. Benson, '10, President A. E. Ehr
horn of the Junior class has appoint
ed J. E. Shelton, '10, to fill the posi
tion.
Benson was forced to leave the Uni
versity on account of ill health.
