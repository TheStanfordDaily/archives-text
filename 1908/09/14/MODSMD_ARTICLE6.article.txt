# LIBRARY MAY NOW BE USED.
## Work Is Rapidly Being' Completed So that Many Desks Are Available in Reading Room. 
### 
Although the Library will not be
completely repaired for several days,
conditions at present are such that
it is possible for a limited number of
students to do their reading there.
The electricians are expected to finish
the wiring today. The desks in the
reading room are in position, and ar3
going to be varnished at once. By do
ing this in sections, which will be
roped off, the students can use a large
number of the desks. The plastering
of the seminar rooms is now com
pleted.
