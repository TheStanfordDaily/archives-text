# French Club to Hold First Meeting.
## 
### 
The French Club will hold its first
meeting for the semester Thursday
evening, September 24, at 8 o'clock.
The gathering will take place at Pro
fessor J. E. Matzke's home, 1211 Bry
ant street, Palo Alto.
The first and last meetings of the
semester will be of a social nature
entirely. These two meetings are
open to all students of French wheth
er registered for a French course or
not. In the regular meetings which
will occur every alternate Thursday,
there will be a reading and discus
sion of modern books. The condition
for membership at these meetings is
Course 4 or an equivalent. There are
no dues.
