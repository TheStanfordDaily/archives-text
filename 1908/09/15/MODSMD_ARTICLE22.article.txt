# Cap and Gown Will Receive Freshmen.
## 
### 
Cap and Gown will give their an
nual Freshman reception next Thurs
day afternoon from 2 to 4 o'clock at
the home of Mrs. H. J. Ryan. All the
Freshman girls will be welcomed by
the hostesses, whether they have been
reached by invitations or not. Mrs.
E. W. Allan, dean of women, and Mrs.
D. S. Jordan will assist in receiving.
