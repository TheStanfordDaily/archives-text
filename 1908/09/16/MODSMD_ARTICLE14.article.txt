# PLANS WITH ARCHITECTS.
## Estimate on Memorial Church to be Submitted to Hoard of Trustees When Completed. 
### 
Plans for the rebuilding of Memorial
Church are in the hands of San Fran
cisco architects, who are preparing
an estimate upon the work. The
business has been with the architects
for six months, so that it is
expected the results are almost
ready to be submitted to'the Board of
Trustees, if the plans are satisfac
tory, work will be started.
The Commission of Engineers will
not have charge of the work as was
the case with the other buildings, but
contracts for the restoration of the
church will be let under the control
of the Business Office.
