# Violinist For Palo Alto.
## 
###

are lovers of music to learn that a
Violinist is now located in Palo Alto;
the Studio of Mr. Winsor being cen
trally located in the Nevada Building.
A permanent orchestra has been al
ready stablished but Mr. Winsor is
paying great attention to tuition, and
combined with the "Sevcik" system
is introducting the main essentials
from the new system based upon psy
cho-physiological principals from Goby
Eberhardt for the rapid development
of Technic.
