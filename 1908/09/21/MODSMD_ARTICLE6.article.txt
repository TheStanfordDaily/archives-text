# Gymnasium Tests Held Saturday.
## 
### 
Try-outs were held in the Encina
gymnasium and on the old football
field Saturday under the supervision
of Instructor Long and Trainer Moul
ton for the gymnasium efficiency
tests. The figures on the results are
now being compiled by Instructor
Long and the outcome of tests will
be ready for publication at a later
date.
