# California Has Interclass Games.
## 
### 
After the example set by Stanford,
the University of California is about
to institute a series of interclass
Rugby games, the first of the series
to be played tomorrow, when the
Freshmen will contest with the Soph
omores. Thursday, September 24,
the juniors and seniors will play
their game, the finals being held up
on the Monday following . At present
predictions as to the outcome ai-3
all in favor of the large freshman
spuad that is upon the field. H. El
liott, of the Barbarian team, has been
coaching the squad, giving the mem
bers suggestions as to the more in
tricate points of the game.
