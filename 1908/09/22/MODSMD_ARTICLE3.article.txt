# Dr. Blodgett Will Play Wednesday.
## 
### 
The next one of Dr. Blodgett's in
teresting organ recitals will he given
Wednesday afternoon at 4:30 o'clock
in All Saints' Church, Palo Alto.

andante and variations for string
quartette, "Death and the Maiden",
Schubert; oriental picture op. 66.
Schumann; Gavotte from Mignon,
