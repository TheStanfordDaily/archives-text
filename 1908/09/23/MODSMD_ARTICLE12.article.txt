# Civil Engineers Elect Members.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Civil Engineering
Society was held last evening at the
home of J. F. Pieper, '09, in Palo
Alto, at which six men were elected
to membership. Those from the
Class of 1908 were: C. IT. Wonderies
IT. L. Horton, .T. O. Miller, H. H. Fit
ting; from the Class of 1909: J. I!.
Swickard and G. R. Wells.
