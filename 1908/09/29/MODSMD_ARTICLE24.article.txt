# STANFORD PROFESSOR HONORED
## Dr. Simon J. McLean, Formerly of the Economies Department, Receives Canadian Appointment. 
### 
Dr. Simon J. McLean, who was for
a number of years at the head of the
Department of Economics here, and
for the last three years Professor of
Railway Economics in the University
of Toronto, has lately been appointed
Railroad Commissioner for the Do
minion of Canada. Professor McLean
is one of the highest authorities in
the world in the matter of the re
lation of railways to economic prob
lems.
