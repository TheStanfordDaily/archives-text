# Women's Club to Hold Festival.
## 
### 
The autumn festival of the Wo
men's Club of Palo Alto will be held
in the Palo Alto skating rink on the
mornings, afternoons, and evenings
of October 1(5 and 17. A fancy-and
needle-work, competition will be held,
particulars of which may be obtained
; roni Miss J. Wright, 1 146 Waverly
street, Palo Alto.
