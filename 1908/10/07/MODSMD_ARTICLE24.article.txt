# Band Is Practicing Hard.
## 
### 
The University Band is putting
forth its best efforts to he in good
shape to play at the intercollegiate
Freshman game. Two rehearsals are
held a week and all the old music
has been run over. The band will
make its initial appearance on the
bleachers next Saturday.
