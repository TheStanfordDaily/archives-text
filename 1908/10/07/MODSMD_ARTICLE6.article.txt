# Senior Women Wear Caps and Gowns.
## 
### 
There was a meeting of the women
of the Senior class this afternoon.
It was decided to wear caps and
gowns every Wednesday and Friday
and to all assemblies. The custom
of the Senior women sitting in a body
at Assemblies will also be kept up
this year. Plans were made for hav
ing a supper: picnic or some other en
tertainment to enable the members of
the class to become hotter acquainted.
A committee will be appointed to
have charge of this and to decide on
the date which will be set as soon as
is possible.
