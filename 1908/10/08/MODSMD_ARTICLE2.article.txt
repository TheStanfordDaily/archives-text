# Seniors Will Consider Circus.
## 
### 
The Senior Class will meet in room
."0 tomorrow at 1:10 o'clock to hear a
report from the committee which was
appointed some time ago to investi
gate the feasibility of holding a circus
this year. At the time of the meeting
it was understood that the affair
could not he held upon the turf.
What decision the committee has
reached is not known, but it is be
lieved some plan will he suggested
for a substitute for the Senior circus
if it should be decided that a circus
is not possible.
