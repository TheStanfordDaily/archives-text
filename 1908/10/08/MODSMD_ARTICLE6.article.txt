# Dr. Jordan Returns Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will return tomorrow
from his journey to Helena, Montana,
where he spoke at the inauguration of
Dr. C. A. Duniway as president of
Montana University. Dr. Jordan will
speak, in San Francisco tomorrow,
October 9, before the Trans-Missis
sippi Conference upon the subject of
"The Protection of Our Fisheries."
