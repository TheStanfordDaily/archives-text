# Doctor Jordan Will Address Men.
## 
### 
President David Starr Jordan will
speak before the Young Men's Chris
tian Association Sunday, October 1.1,
at 7:30 o'clock in the Chapel. His
subject for his address will be "Amer
ican Christianity. " The association
quartette will sing. This will be the
first of the Sunday mass meetings of
the association, which will be held
monthly throughout the year.
