# Mrs. Allan Will Address Women.
## 
### 
At the next regular meeting of the
Y. W. C. A. to be held in the Chapel
at 4:30 o'clock Tuesday afternoon,
Mrs. Allan, dean of the women, will
give a talk. The special music will be
a violin solo. All the women in the
University are invited by the associa
tion to attend.
The association h?.s been very for
tunate in securing the services of Dr.
George Castro, who comes from Berk
eley each week to lead a Bible class
in the study of the Gospel according
to John. The class meets every
Wednesday at 3:30 o'clock. Visitors
are always welcome. Any one desir
ing to enroll in the class may do so
with the general secretary, .Miss Gra
ham. in the Women's Rest Room any
morning between !) and 12 o'clock.
