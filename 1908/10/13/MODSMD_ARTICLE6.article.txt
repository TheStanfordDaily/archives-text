# OFFICIALS ARE NAMED.
## Referee and Touch Judges for Freshman Game Selected From Rugby Union. 
### 
The officials for the intercollegiate
Freshman game will be chosen from
the newly formed Rugby Referee's
Union of San Francisco. Both Man
ager Stewart and Manager Merritt
have decided to allow the referee for
the match and the touch judges to be
selected by the San Francisco experts.
Manager Stewart today stated that
T. .1. Lafferty will be the referee and
that he believed that C. Cameron and
It. Elliott would act as touch judges.
Lafferty refereed the last Barbar
ian game and is favorably considered
by Coaches Lanagan and Presley.
