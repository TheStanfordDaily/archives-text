# Fire Causes Loss to Alpha Phis.
## 
###

The quarters of the Japanese
servants employed by the Alpha
Phi Sorority were gutted by
fire shortly after 2 o'clock this
afternoon. As soon as the fire
was discovered the fact was reported
to the fire house and the apparatus
was hurried to tbe small building in
the rear of the sorority house. The
flames were soon extinguished and
an investigation disclosed the fact
that the fire originated in a half full
gasoline can. It is not known what
started the gasoline The Japanese
reported that they had lost about $10
but they may find their currency in
the ruins.
