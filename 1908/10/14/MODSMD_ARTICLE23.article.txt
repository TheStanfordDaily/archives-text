# Faculty and Freshmen Play Baseball.
## 
### 
The Faculty and Freshman base
ball teams played the second of a
three game series yesterday after
noon. The 11)12 team put up a better
game than last week, but the Faculty
again won by a score of 3 to 1.
The third game will be played to
morrow afternoon at 4:30 o'clock,
