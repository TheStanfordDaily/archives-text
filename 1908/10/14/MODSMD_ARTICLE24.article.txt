# Meeting of A.I.E.E. Held.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the Stan
ford Branch of the American Insti
tute of Electrical Engineers, last
evening, the following men were
elected to membership: F. J. Hett
man, '09, J. O. McDonald, '09, R. F.
Myers, '09, J. I). Taggart, '09, and S.
Motomura, '09.
The speakers of the evening were
H. H. Buell, '09, and C. F. Elwell, '07.
Buell gave a description of the elec
tric plant of the United Gas and
Electric Co. in San Francisco. El
well described the evolution of the
electric furnace as a practical means
of smelting ore.
