# Encina Will Keep "Open House."
## 
### 
Enema Club will keep "open house"
Saturday to all who wish to visit the
big dormitory. President C. 11. Won
dries, '08, has appointed a reception
committee with R. F. Huntsberger,
'08, as chairman, which will welcome
all visitors from 10 o'clock until after
the conclusion of the Freshman in
tercollegiate Rugby game. A rest
room will be provided for ladies.
