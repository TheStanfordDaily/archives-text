# Twenty-three on Varsity Table.
## 
### 
The Varsity Rugby training table
list was announced late this after
noon. Three meals daily will be serv
ed to twenty-three men, besides
Coach Presley, at the University Inn.
The list: Crawford, Koeruer, Terrill,
Higley, McGregor, Cheda, Pemberton,
Miller, Evans, Reynolds, Heinly, Mac
kenzie, Erb, Elliott, Mitchell, Roth,
Ganong, Cook, Scott, Holman, Cad
walader, Jordan, Brown.
Ganong and Scott, who were in
jured in the Olympic-Varsity game of
October 10, were out on the field in
football suits this afternoon for the
first time since that game.
