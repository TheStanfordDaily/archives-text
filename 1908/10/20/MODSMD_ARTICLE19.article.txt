# Arcade Moot Court Meets.
## 
### 
The Arcade Moot Court held its
regular meeting last evening in the
large lecture room of the Law de
partment. Professor J. W. Bingham
presided as Chief Justice. A case in
property was argued, E. L. Howarth,
'10, acting as attorney for the plain
tiff and L. O. Macomber, '10, appear
ing as counsel for the defendent. The
decision was rendered in favor of the
plaintiff. The next meeting of the
court will lie convened next Monday
evening when an important business
meeting will he held directly after
the argument.
