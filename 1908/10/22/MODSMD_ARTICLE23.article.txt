# M.E. Society Holds Meeting.
## 
### 
The Mechanical Engineering So
ciety held its regular bi-monthly

meeting in room J <10, Encina Hall,
yesterday evening. .No business of
great. importance was transacted. J. A.
Young, '09, delivered an interesting
talk on "Rifled Pip.s Lines," giving
a particular description of the oil
lines running from Hakerstield to San
Francisco. The evening was closed
with an enjoyable social hour.
