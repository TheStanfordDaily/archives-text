# English Club to Entertain Cast.
## 
### 
The English Club will informally
entertain members of the cast of
"Arms and tlie Man" at the Sigma
Nu fraternity house next Wednesday
evening, October 28. The social will
be an informal reception to the cast,
at which refreshments will lie served.
Professor 11. D. Gray, who has the
coaching of the Shaw production, will
speak, and the cast may run through
several scenes from the play.
