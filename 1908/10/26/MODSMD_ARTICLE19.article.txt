# Moran Will Address Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
Hugh Moran, ex-05, will speak at
the Y. M. C. A. meeting to be held in
the Chapel tomorrow afternoon at
4:30 o'clock. Moran went to Oxford
as the Rhodes scholar from Stanford;
he received the degree of Bachelor of
Theology last spring and expects to
leave in February for Shanghai, Chi
na, where he will carry on mission
ary work.
