# Arcade Moot Court Holds Meeting.
## 
### 
The Arcade Moot Court held its
regular undergraduate meeting last
evening in the Law building. A case
in Torts was argued, M. R. Kirkwood,
'09, acting as attorney for the plain
tiff and It. B. Stover, '09, appearing
as counsel for the defendant. The
decision was rendered by presiding
Chief Justice P. L. Haworth, '10, in
favor of the plaintiff. At the conclu
sion of I lie argument, a business meet
ing of the court was held when the
old constitution was discussed and re
ferred to the executive committee for
revision.
