# Women Discuss Holding Field Day.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Executive Board
of the Women's Athletic Association
this afternoon plana for a field day
were discussed. The board consider
ed having finals of interclass basket
ball games, as well as several inter
class tennis matches and a cricket
match. The date of the field day will
he decided later in the semester,
when arrangements will be completed.
