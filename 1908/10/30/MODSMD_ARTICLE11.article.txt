# Will Give Organ Recital Sunday.
## 
### 
Dr. R C. Blodgett, formerly Uni
versity Organist, will give a recital
in the Congregational Church of Palo
Alto next Sunday afternoon at 4
o'clock. The following program will
be rendered:
Improvisation upon chorale, "Al
lein Gott im der Hoh sei Ehr"; an
dante from Trio, op. Gfi, Mendelssohn;
duetto from "Stabat Mater", "Quis
est. Homo?" (requested) Rossini;
Crosse Fantasie und Fuge, G moll,
Bach; Largo from Sonata op. 10 No.
.3, Beethoven; duetto, Mendelssohn;
Ave Maria, Bach-Gounod.
