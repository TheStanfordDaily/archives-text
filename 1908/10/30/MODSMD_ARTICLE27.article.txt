# Tennis Tournament Is Progressing.
## 
### 
Several of the preliminary matches
of the men's handicap tennis tourna
ment were played off yesterday and
today on the various campus courts.
It is hoped by the committee in
charge that as many matches as pos
sible will be played tomorrow, wea
ther permitting. First round matches
will be started at 8:30 o'clock Satur
day, unless mutually arranged to be
played any time before Monday, while
second round matches are to be com
pleted by Saturday, November 7.
