# Sunday Excursion to Monterey.
## 
###

run one of their popular excursions
to Santa Cruz and Monterey on Sun
day. The fare will be $2 for the roun 1
trip, the tickets being good on either
the special or regular trains. The
special train will leave Palo Alto at
7:4(5 a.m. and returning, will leave
Santa Cruz ahd MohfereV at 6 p.m.
