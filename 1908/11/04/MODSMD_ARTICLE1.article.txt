# TO COMPLETE MATCHES
## TENNIS TOURNEY PROGRESSING AT STEADY PACE. Handicap Tournament Expected to Be Finished by Next Saturday. 
### 
The matches of the men's handicap
tennis tournament have been pro
gressing rapidly and it is hoped by
the committee in charge that it will
be ended next Saturday. The com

should finish as many matches as
possible this week and all men with
matches to play must be on hand
early Saturday morning.
The standard of play is getting
higher as the tournament progresses.
