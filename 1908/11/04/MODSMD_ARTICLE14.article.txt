# Sophomore Cotillion Tickets on Sale.
## 
### 
Sophomore Cotillion tickets were
on sale this morning at the postoflice
from 11:30 to 12:30 to members of the
class and will again be on sale to
morrow at the same hour. Friday
tickets may be secured by others.
They will also be on sale tomorrow
and Friday in room 83 Encina Hall
from 4:30 to 5:30 o'clock and at the
Sigma Alpha Epsilon house between
the hours of 7:30 and 8:30 o'clock.
Beginning with next Friday, the tick
ets will be on sale to the general pub
lic. The price per ticket, is $2.50 and
the number is limited to 125. Tickets
will be taken up at the door and no
one will be admitted without one.
