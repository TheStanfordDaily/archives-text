# Varsity Receives Commendation.
## 
### 
H. F. Gosse, Manager of the River
side Hotel of Reno, Nevada, has for
warded a communication to Dr. Jor
dan, commending the action of the
Varsity while in that city. In part,
Gosse writes as follows: "I feel it
my duty to you and your institution
to write congratulating you on the
behavior of the young gentlemen who
took part in the football game here
on the twenty-fourth of this month
(October). Their conduct was ex
emplary and it gives me pleasure to
write you this."
