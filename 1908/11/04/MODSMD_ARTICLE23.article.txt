# Arcade Moot Court Holds Meeting.
## 
### 
The Arcade Moot Court held its
regular meeting Monday afternoon in
the new lecture room of the Law de
partment. Professor F. C. Woodward
presided as chief justice. A case in
Sales was argued, J. D. Rutledge, '11,
acting as attorney for the defendant
and W. C. Shelton, '08, appearing as
counsel for the plaintiff. The decis
ion rendered was favorable to the
defendant. All future meetings will
he held as in the past, on Monday ev
enings.
