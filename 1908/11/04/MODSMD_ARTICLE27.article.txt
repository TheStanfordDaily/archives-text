# Last Year's Glee Club Entertained.
## 
### 
The members of last year's Glee
Club were guests of the San Jose
Elks at a banquet last evening. They
contributed several numbers to the
program and received the election re
turns as they sat at the festal
board. About 300 Elks and their
guests were present.
