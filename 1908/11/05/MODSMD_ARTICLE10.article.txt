# Calls Attention to Exposition.
## 
### 
The following communication has
been received by the Daily Palo Alto:
"Stanford University, Cal.
"Hon. F. Wodon, Consul General for
Belgium, asks me to call the attention
of the University community to the
fact that the Government of Belgium
will open at Brussels in April, 1910, a
universal and inter national exhibi
tion, under the High Patronage of His
Majesty the King of Belgium.
"Very truly yours,
"David S. Jordan."
