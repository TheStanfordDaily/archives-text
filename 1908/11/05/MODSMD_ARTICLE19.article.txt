# Prof. Snyder Receives Rare Snake.
## 
### 
Professor Snyder of the Zoology
department has received for this de
partment a horned rattlesnake from
Dr. Fred Baker of San Diego. There
are very few of this variety in cap
tivity, this snake being the first one
to be received here. They are only
to be found in the regions of the
Sal ton Sea, Mojave and Colorado
Deserts.
