# Senior Class Will Meet Monday.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Senior Class has
been called for Monday at 1:10 in
room 50 by President C. E. Sampson.
At this time the proposition of voting
money from the class treasury for
the use of the Yell-Leaders Arrange
ments committee wiM be considered.
The committee is having a difficult
time to raise the necessary money to
meet the expenses of the stunts plan
ned for the big game and relies on
the classes for material help.
