# Science Association to Meet.
## 
### 
The Science Association will hold
its second regular monthly meeting at
the Faculty Clubhouse Wednesday ev
ening, November 11, at 8 o'clock. Dr.
G. J. Pierce will read a paper on the
"Demonstration of a new Respiration
Calorimeter" and Dr. S. D. Townley
will lecture on "The Shifting of the
Axis of the Earth."
