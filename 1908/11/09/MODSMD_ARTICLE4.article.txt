# "Arms and the Man" Nets Profits.
## 
### 
L. S. Walbridge, chairman of the
play committee of the English Club,
made the following announcement of
the receipts from their play, "Arms
and the Man," as follows: Gross,
$(194.25, with net receipts of $339.28.
Walbridge stated today that part of
this profit may go to defray the ex
penses of publishing the English Club
year book, or it may be used to finance
a trip in the southern part of the
state, which the club is planning for
the cast of the Shaw comedy which
would be produced iu Southern Cali
fornia. At present the plans and
possibility of the trip are indefinite.
