# Y.M.C.A. Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
Miss Elise Owen will lead the reg
ular meeting of the Y. W. C. A. to be
held tomorrow afternoon at 4:30
o'clock in the Chapel. A report will
be given of the recent conference in
Berkeley which was led by Miss Carde,
a national secretary of the Young
Women's Christian Association. As
usual, all the women of the Univer
sity will be welcome at the meeting.
