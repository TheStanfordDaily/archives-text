# Association Abandons Barbecue.
## 
### 
Owing to the uncertainty of secur
ing an exact date, ai d on account of
the coming rainy season, the Young
Men's Christian Association have de
cided to give up their barbecue which
was scheduled for the twentieth of
this month or thereabouts. In place
of this a combined social gathering
to be given by the Young Women's
and the Young Men's Christian Asso
ciations was consideied, but this also
worked out unsatisfactorily. The as
sociation is undecided just what to
give in place of the barbecue and will
probably wait until next semester lie
fore doing anything farther in the
social line.
