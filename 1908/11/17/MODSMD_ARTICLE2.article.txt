# WILL ASSEMBLE ON THURSDAY.
## Members of University Will Gather in Morning to Hear Speech by Japanese. 
### 
Reverend D. Ebina of Tokio, will be
the speaker at the assembly which
will be held next Thursday morning.
Ebina is a religious worker in Japan
and is doing a great deal toward con
verting his native people to Christ
ianity. Ebina's work is exclusive of
the missionary work which at the
present time is being so enthusiasti
cally followed up in Japan. Ebina
will speak on "Vocations of Ameri
cans and Japauese." The assembly
will be held at 11:15 o'clock.
