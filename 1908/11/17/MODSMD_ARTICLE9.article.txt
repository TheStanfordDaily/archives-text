# "Billy" Hill Has Accident.
## 
### 
"Billy" Hill, a well known figure
about the campus, formerly chief of
police of Palo Alto and, of late, a
driver for the Business Office, was
this afternoon thrown from the seat,
in his wagon while iurning a sharp
curve near the Stanford Bookstore.
The injured man was removed in an
automobile to the Guild Hospital in
Palo Alto where his case was not di
agnosed as serious. Hill was later
removed to his home near Felt Lake.
