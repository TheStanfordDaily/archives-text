# Freshmen Remove Bonfire Remains.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Freshman Class
wes held this afternoon at 1:10 o'clock
and both old and new business was
finished. The basketball challenge
to the Sophomores was discussed and
men asked to practice for the team
on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fridays.
The men of the class were asked to
come out Thursday afternoon after
2 o'clock to help in clearing away
the remains of the rally bonfire. The
class as a whole was urged to pay the
second-class assessment of twenty
five cents.
