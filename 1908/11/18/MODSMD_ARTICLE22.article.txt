# Will Play Lacrosse Saturday.
## 
### 
Teams from the 11:15 and 5 o'clock
gymnasium classes will play the first
game of lacrosse of the season Sat
urday morning on the old oval. The
men have been practicing regularly
since the opening of the year and now
understand the game fairly well. In
terest in lacrosse has been rapidly
increasing this year and many men
have turned out to learn the game.
