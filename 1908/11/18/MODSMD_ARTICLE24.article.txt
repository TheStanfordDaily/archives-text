# Women Plan To Sit By Classes.
## 
### 
At the request of the President's
conference of Young Women, a vol
untary effort will he made, by the
women of the University, to sit ac
cording to classes >n the Assembly
tomorrow. The Seniors will occupy
their traditional section, the center
of the balcony; the Juniors will sit
behind them and flank them on each
side; the Sophomores will occupy the
adjoining seats on both sides of the
gallery and the Freshmen the seats
nearest the stage. The committee
in charge will make every effort to
avoid confusion.
