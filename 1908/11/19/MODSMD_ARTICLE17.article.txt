# M.E. Society Nominates Officers.
## 
### 
The Mechanical Engineering Socie
ty held a short meeting last evening
at which officers for the next, sem
ester were nominated and a shop
program given. Those nominated are:
for president, P. H. Van Etten, '09,
and C. Breer, 'OSJ; for vice-president,
A. C. Coonradt, '09, and V. H. Her
bert, '09; for secretary-treasurer, R.
L. Daugherty, '09, and H. L. Hess, '09.
C. E. Steinbeck, '09,. gave a short and
interesting talk on "Edgemore Boil
ers," several of which he erected this
summer.
