# Women's Doubles Matches Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The women's interclass tennis
tournament will be held Saturday
morning at 9 o'clock on Roble courts.
For the past two weeks the try-out
matches have been under way and
the winners will take part in the tour
nament tomorrow. No singles games
will be played and the class doubles
teams will not draw for opponents un
til just before play starts.
The teams will be as follows: 1909
—Miss A. Ellerbeck, Miss M. Clay;
1910—Miss S. Bundy, Miss F. Burrell;
1911—Miss M. Knox, Miss F. Haig;
1912—Miss R. Hutchinson, Miss R. Ov
erman.
