# U.C. Freshman Wins Punting Cup.
## 
### 
S. K. Carpenter, who as a forward
on the California Freshman team won
his 1912 class numerals, was awarded
the punting cup yesterday on Califor
nia Field. All the members of the
varsity team tried out in the punting
contest, which was judged according
to distance, speed, height and accur
acy. Ralph Hutler, varsity captain,
won the cup last year.
