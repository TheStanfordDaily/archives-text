# Miss Anderson Speaker For Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Miss Chloe Anderson, secretary of
the City Young Women's Christian
Association in San Jose, will be the
speaker at the regular Y. W. C. A.
meeting, to be held in the chapel at
4:30 on Tuesday afternoon.
Miss Anderson is a Stanford grad
uate of the class of '04. Her speech
will be of interest to university
women and they are urged to be
present.
Special music will be provided.
