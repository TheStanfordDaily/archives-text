# California To Have Tennis Courts.
## 
### 
Grading has been commenced on
the University of California campus
for the construction of six new ten
nis courts west of California Hall.
Tiers of concrete seats will be in
stalled around two of these, which
are being prepared especially for
exhibition courts. All the courts will
have concrete foundations, with as
phalt surface and will be surrounded
with the conventional high wire
fence.
