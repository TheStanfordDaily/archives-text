# Arcade Moot Court Holds Meeting.
## 
### 
The undergraduate section of the
Arcade Moot court held a regular
meeting yesterday afternoon in room
12. A case in criminal law involv
ing an attempt at larceny was argued.
L. B. Craven, '09, appeared as attor
ney for the prosecution and A. E. Roth,
'09, acted as counsel for the defendant.
Presiding Chief Justice J. D. Rut
ledge, '11, rendered a decision favor
able to the prosecuting attorney.
