# TENNIS MEN TO PLAY
## THIELE TROPHY TOURNAMENT STARTS TOMORROW. Weather May Delay Matches For a Few Hours—Over Sixty Will Strive For Cup. 
### 
Play for the Thiele three-time Ten
nis Trophy will commence tomorrow
at 8:30 o'clock, weather permitting,
on Encina and fraternity courts.
Players should be on hand prompt
ly to play or arrange their matches.
Play will be for the best two of three
sets, of six games each; no deuce sets
will be played. All entrance fees
must be paid before matches. New
balls will be provided for all first
round matches and every alternate
round thereafter.
There are 64 entries in the tour
nament, as compared with 96 in the
preliminary. The decrease is due to
the absence of men ior the vacation.
It is hoped that the first round
will be finished by Friday evening.
The tournament would then end Sat
urday with the round-up of the semi
finals and finals. The final match will
be for the best three out of five sets.
In case of rain tomorow matches
may be re-scheduled when necessary,
though play may be resumed an hour
after a shower, for the courts will
be swept when the opportunity of
fers for continued play. The drawing
follows:
