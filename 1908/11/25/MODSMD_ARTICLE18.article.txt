# Growth of Palo Alto.
## 
### 
Palo Alto will one day reach the
foothills. If it grows as has Berkeley
and Princeton the men of 1912 will
see it. What, think you, will be the
choicest part of town.
Lots in University Park are selling
for $275 today. Fifty-four have been
sold, a number of them to professors.
William Cranston, Palo Alto, Phone
Alto 391 K.
