# Dr. Murray To Address Cosmopolitans
## 
###

Cosmopolitan Club Thursday even
ing. December 3, on the general sub
ject of Greek life in the classical per
iod. The meeting will be held in the
old book store which has been fitted
111> as a clubhouse. The date of the
meeting lias been changed from Wed
nesday to Thursday night in order
that Dr. Murray may be present at
a meeting of the Classical Club on
Wednesday night
