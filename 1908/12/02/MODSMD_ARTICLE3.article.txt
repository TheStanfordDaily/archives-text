# Sophomore Girls Win Basketball.
## 
### 
The Sophomore girls defeated the
Freshmen girls in basketball this
afternoon by a score of 12 to 10. The
game was fast and exciting and was
characterized by excellent playing
on the part of the Sophomore for
wards.
Tomorrow at 4 o'clock the prelim
inary npperclass game will be play
ed on Roble court.
