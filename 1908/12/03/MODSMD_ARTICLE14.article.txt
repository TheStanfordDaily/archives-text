# Announcements.
## 
### 
Press Club —Will meet at the Delta
Kappa Epsilon house Tuesday evening
at 9.
A. I. E. E.—Stanford branch will
meet Tuesday evening, December 8,
at the home Professor H. J. Ryan.
Subscriptions—For Daily Palo Alto,
for students residing in Palo Alto, will
be received at Congdon & Crome's
bookstore.
Cotillion Programs—May be secur
ed from R. 13. Wheeler at Beta Tlieta
Pi house by those who have none.
Committee.
American Politics—Professor B. E.
Howard will hold an important meet
ing of his class in Economics 32,
American Politics on next Saturday
afternoon at 3 in room 214.
Y. M. C. A.—Will hold a special
meeting Sunday morning, December
7, in room 55, at 9:4f». H. Harle and
P. W. McNulty, '09, will discuss the
question "Is the Christian Church
(raining or Losing?"
Iff. E. 3 a. —There will be an ex
amination in Elementary Machine De
sign, Friday, December 4th, at 1:30
in room 283. This examination is
intended for all the students who re
ceived marks of "condition" or "in
complete" on the work of last sem
ester.
G. H. Marx.
