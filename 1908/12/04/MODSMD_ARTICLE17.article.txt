# French Club Meets in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
The members of the French Club
were entertained last evening by Pro
fessor and Mrs. R. M. Alden at their
home in Palo Alto The literary
feature of the gathering consisted of
several selected readings given by
Professor Guerard and Miss Stauff.
During the latter portion of the ev
ening the members enjoyed light re
freshments.
