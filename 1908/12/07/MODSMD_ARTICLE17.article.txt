# Y.W.C.A. Has Musical Program.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the Young
Women's Christian Association to he
held in the Chapel at 4:30 o'clock to
morrow afternoon, the following musical
program will he rendered :
Hymn No. 193; chorus, Schubert
Club; piano solo, Miss A. Kimball,'o4;
violin solo, Miss M. Van Zwalenburg,'lo;
scripture and praver; vocal solo, Miss
M. E. Coombe, '11; violin solo, Miss C.
Ryan, '12; vocal solo, Miss E. Owen,'o7;
hymn No. 197. The meeting is open to
all woman of the University.
