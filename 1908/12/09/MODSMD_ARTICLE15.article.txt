# Will Participate in Entertainment.
## 
### 
At the Episcopalian entertainment,
to be held in Jordan's Hall in Palo
Alto, next Friday evening, G. F. Mor
gan, '10, is to give a monologue, and
P. F. Pettigrew, '10, to render a vocal
solo. Besides these, there will be
many other readings and musical se
lections by local talent.
