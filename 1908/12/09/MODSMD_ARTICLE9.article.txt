# Dr. Townley to Speak on Astronomy.
## 
### 
Dr. S. D. Townley, assistant profes
sor in the Applied Mathematics de
partment, who has had charge of the
astronomical observatory at Ukiah,
will give a lecture before the class
in general astronomy Thursday even
ing, December 10, at 7:30 o'clock. The
talk will be upon various matters of
importance in the study of astronom
ical subjects, and will be illustrated
with lantern slides. The lecture will
be held in the Botany building in room
400, and a cordial invitation is ex
tended to the public by Dr. Townley.
