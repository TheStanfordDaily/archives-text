# Association Man to Visit.
## 
### 
E. C. Carter, Harvard, '98, will
speak in the Chapel tomorrow even
ing at 7:15 at a special meeting of
the Young Men's Christian Associa
tion. Carter has been national secre
tary of the Association of India, and
is now associate secretary on the in
ternational committee of North Amer
ica. His subject will be "Young Men's
Christian Associations in All Lands."
Carter will remain on the campus
one day before returning to his home
in the East, which he left to attend
the conference at Pacific Grove, this
vacation.
