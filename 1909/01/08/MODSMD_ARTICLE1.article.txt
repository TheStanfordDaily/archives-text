# DEGREES ARE GRANTED
## HONORS CONFERRED TODAY BY ACADEMIC COUNCIL. Fifty-one Who Completed Work Last Semester Receive Recognition From University.

cil held this afternoon, degrees were
conferred upon Christmas graduates.
Two advanced degrees were granted,
while forty-nine Bachelor of Arts de
grees were allowed.
