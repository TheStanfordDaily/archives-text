# Sophomores to Nominate Tuesday.
## 
### 
President B. W. Lee of the Sopho
more class has called a meeting for
next Tuesday afternoon at 1:10
o'clock in room 50.
There will be unfinished business
to transact and several committees
will make reports. The Cotillion Com
mittee will be heard from, and prob
ably a report from the Play Commit
tee on the progress of their work will
be made.
Nominations for officers will be re
ceived, to be followed by a regular
election on the Quad.
