# Mercer Speaks in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
E. C. Mercer, who has spoken sev
eral times to large college audiences,
gave an address to the men of Palo
Alto in the Presbyterian chapel last
night. Mercer said in beginning that
we can only appreciate things by con
trasts. "Only those who have seen
the lower side of life appreciate their
present environment. He related his
own story, and told that of the great
good that the rescue missions are do
ing to such men as he was.
Mercer will go fiom here to the
other colleges along the Coast and
address the men there.
