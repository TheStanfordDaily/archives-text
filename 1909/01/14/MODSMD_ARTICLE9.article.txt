# Professor Adams Speaks on Labor.
## 
### 
At the first meeting of the Econom
ics Club for this semester, which was
held last night, Professor T. S. Ad
ams of the Economics department
spoke on "Labor Troubles." Professor
Adams expressed the belief that in
junction laws will soon be enacted that
be more favorable to the working man.
After the lecture, a discussion was
held, and the following officers were
chosen for the semester: D. C. Boyd.
'OS, president; C. lb Bradford, '09,
vice-president; H. W. Beard, '09, sec
retary-treasurer.
