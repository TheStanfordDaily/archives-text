# Candidates Will Try Out.
## 
### 
Yell Leader G. N. Richardson has
decided to hold a tryout for the pur
pose of filling the vacant positions of
assistant yell leaders. At a meeting
called by him at the Delta Kappa Ep
silon house last night to discuss the
matter, a number of men signified
their intention of trying out. The try
outs will be held in the near future,
the first on next Wednesday evening
January 20, in the Assembly Hall, and
the final one at the track rally. Two
of the competitors will be selected by
the Executive Committee to fill the va
cant positions.
