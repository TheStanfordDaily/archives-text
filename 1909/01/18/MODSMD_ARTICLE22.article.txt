# Track Captain Horton Returns.
## 
### 
Track Captain H. L. Horton, '08, re
turned to the campus on Saturday and
resumed his university work today.
Horton's return is the signal for be
ginning the active work of the track
season, and he has set the date of the
track rally for Wednesday evening.
The rally will, as usual, be held in the
clubroom of Encina Hall, where the
men will sign up for their respective
events, and hear the plans of Captain
Horton and Trainer Moulton for the
coming season. A short yell practice
will be held and a program of promi
nent speakers is being arranged.
