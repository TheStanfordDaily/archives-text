# Interscholastic Board Meets.
## 
### 
The Interscholastic Hoard of Gov
ernors met at the Zeta Phi house last
evening. The only business transact
ed was the election of A. E. Roth,
'09, to the board to fill the place of
S. H. Bellah, ex-'lO, who has not re
turned.
The question of entering the discus
throw in the next meet was laid over
until next meeting.
