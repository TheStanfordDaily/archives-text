# Y.M.C.A. Plans Entertainments.
## 
### 
The Young Men's Christian Asso
ciation has just completed prelimin
ary plans for several social functions
to he given this semester. The first
will he given in the form of a stay
"jolly-up," and will take place at an
early date. About the middle of the
semester a combined entertainment by
the two Christian associations is
scheduled. The annual barbecue of
the association will be held in some
of the nearby foothills late in the sem
ester.
