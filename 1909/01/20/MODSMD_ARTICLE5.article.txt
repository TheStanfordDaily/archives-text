# Social Service Club to Meet.
## 
### 
Rev. I). Charles Gardner has called
a meeting of the Social Service Club
for next Wednesday, January 27, at 8
p. m. Professor T. S. Adams of the
University of Wisconsin will address
the organization on "Child Labor."
Professor Adams, who is an eminent
worker along social service lines and
the author of several books dealing
with the subject, is giving several
courses in the Economics department.
The place of meeting will be an
nounced later.
