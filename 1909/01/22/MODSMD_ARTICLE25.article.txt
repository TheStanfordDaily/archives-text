# Will Speak Before Geology Society.
## 
### 
The Geology and Mining Society
will hold its first meeting of the sem
ester next Tuesday evening, January
26, at 8 o'clock, at the home of As
sociate Professor J. F. Newsom, 1129
Cowper street, Palo Alto.
Through the efforts of F. W. Tur
ner, '08, president of the society, H.
C. Hoover, '95, the mining expert, has
consented to address the meeting.
