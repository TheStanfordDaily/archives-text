# To Hold "Jubilee" on February 12.
## 
### 
The Young Men's Christian Asso
ciation has completed plans for its
first social function of the semester.
The entertainment will be in the na
ture of a jubilee, and will consist of
stunts, followed by light refreshments.
It will be given on Friday evening,
February 12, in Encina Gymnasium.
A. E. Roth, newly appointed chair
man of the Social Committee, is ar
ranging the program.
