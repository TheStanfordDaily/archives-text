# Durand Entertains M. E. Society.
## 
### 
The Mechanical Engineering Society
held its regular meeting yesterday
evening, at the home ot' Professor W.
F. Durand, head of the Mechanical
Engineering department. The mem
bers were entertained with an inter
esting talk by Professor Durand on
"Aerial Navigation," which was illus
trated with slides. Some of the plates
showed aeroplanes used in the recent
experiments at Fort Meyer, Va.
