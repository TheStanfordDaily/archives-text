# New Faculty Club Organized.
## 
### 
The new Faculty Club, known as
The Forum, organized for the unoffi
cial discussion of questions of general
academic interest, was definitely
launched Friday evening at the Fac
ulty Club-house. The club has no of
ficial relation to the University, ex
cept that membership is limited to
members of the instructive body. It
is the purpose of the Forum to hold
regular monthly meetings during the
academic year.
