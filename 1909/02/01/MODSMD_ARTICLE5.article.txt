# Delta Chi Holds Initiation.
## 
### 
Last Saturday evening, in a secret
initiation, fifteen new members were
added to the legal fraternity of Delta
Chi. Those who were thus honored
are R. H. Borland, gr., R. B. Murphey,
gr., F. L. Haworth, '09, L. Craven, '09,
W. P. Mealey, '09, B. L. Gregg, '09,
S M. Batterson, '10, P. J. Batkin, '10,
C. C. Shoemaker, '10, J. E. Shelton,
'10, S. A. Lindauer, '10, K. S. Macken
zie, '10, C. H. Vose, '10, E. M. Leaf,
'10, and W. S. Porter, '11.
