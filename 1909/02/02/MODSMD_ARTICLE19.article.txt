# Hoover Speaks to Encina Men.
## 
### 
The members ot' Encina Club were
entertained last evening by H. C. Hoo
ver, '95, the mining expert, who gave
an interesting short talk. Hoover
was one of the charter members of
Encina Club, and told of several in
cidents of interest in early club life.
He devoted the main part of his talk,
however, to experiences in China dur
ing (he Boxer uprising, in which he
told of many heroic deeds performed
by Stanford men in the siege of Tien
tsin.
After the talk, Hoover was warm
ly greeted by the club members and
enjoyed a social hour in meeting all
of them.
