# Many Manuscripts Submitted.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Junior Opera
Committee held last night, several
manuscripts were submitted, and sev
eral more will he received before the
end of the week. The manuscripts
that were received were not only from
undergraduates, but also from grad
uates of the University. Ily the first
of next week, the committee will be
in a position to commence the try
outs. Many of last year's cast are
still in the University, and, with the
new material available, little difficulty
is anticipated as far as principals and
chorus are concerned.
