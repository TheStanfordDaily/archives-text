# E.F. Treadwell to Speak.
## 
### 
The fourth lecture to be given in
the series of talks upon "Legal Tac
tics" that are being offered under the
auspices of the Law School, will be
delivered next Thursday afternoon,
February 11, in room 12. The speaker
for this occasion is E. F. Treadwell of
San Francisco, who is now retained
by the Miller & Lux Co. as their at
torney. He is one of the strongest
corporation lawyers that San Francis
co can claim, and has made a high
reputation particularly in the conduct
of litigation in appellate courts.
Treadwell has chosen for the subject
ot his speech next Thursday afternoon,
"Presentation of Cases on Appeal.'
The hour of the lecture will be an
nounced later.
