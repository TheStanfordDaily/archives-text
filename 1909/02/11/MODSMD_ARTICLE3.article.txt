# Guild Board Does Routine Business.
## 
### 
The Guild Board met Tuesday night
and transacted the usual routine busi
ness of the Guild Hospital. Hills were
ordered paid to the amount of $1118.
There was an average of ten patients
during the month of January. The
greatest number of patients on any
one day in the hospital during the
month was twenty-one, and the least
number six. Of the cases treated, for
ty-three were new ones and the re
mainder were patients who received
attention during December. During
January, ten operations were per
formed.
