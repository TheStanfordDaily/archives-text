# Euphronia Elects New Member. Elects New Member.
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society met last
evening and held the regular program
for the meeting, besides conducting
some important business. The sub
ject for the debate was, "Resolved,
That California would be justified in
enacting anti-Japanese legislation,"
the affirmative of the question being
upheld by E. J. Hopkins, '11, and VV.
Tucker, '12, while the opposition was
advanced by B. Bliven, '11, and M. E.
Grimes, '12. In the regular business
meeting of the society, the organiza
tion elected to its membership E. V.
Rosenkranz, '10.
