# Will Hold Rally for Women.
## 
### 
The Women's Athletic Association
lias planned a large basketball rally
to be held at Roble Gymnasium Thurs
day evening at 7:15. Invitations have
been sent to the various women's or
ganizations, and it is expected that a
large and enthusiastic audience will
be present. Speeches will be made
by several of the University women
and stunts have been prepared by the
committee in charge. All the women
of the University are urged to be
present and a jolly time is assured.
Attention is called to the change in
the time of the rally to 7:15.
