# CONTESTANTS ASSIGNED SIDES.
## Candidates for Intercollegiate Debating Teams Given Positions to Uphold in Tryouts. 
### 
At a meeting of the Intersociety De
buting League today, the contestants
for places upon the intercollegiate de
bating teams were divided into two
general squads upon the affirmative
and negative of the question to be de
bated in the preliminary tryouts.
The names of those upon the affirm
ative are: T. T. Bennett, 'OS, G. B.
Bush, '09, A. C. Coonradt, '09, R. M.
Ford, '09, W. E. Hettman, '10, S. Bry
an, '10, L. E. Du Boise, '10, E. .T. Hop
kins, '11, J. A. Visel, '11, P. E. Hill,
'11, G. J. Hatfield, '11, F. M. Russell,
Jr., '12, N. C. Wilson, '12, and J. W.
Henderson, '12.
The negative will be upheld by E.
D Lakin, gr., A. L. Kocher, '09, A.
C Stelle, '09, C. C. Shoemaker, '10,
E. M. Leaf, '10, P. J. Batkin, '10, S. A.
Lindauer, '10, F. H. Hilton, '11, R. J.
Miller, '11, G. Holcomb, '11, H. R. Per
son, '12, L. D. Summerfield, '12, M. E.
Grimes, '12, and E. E. Tincher, '12.
The men have not as yet been as
signed to separate squads, this final
action being contemplated during the
week preceding the preliminary try
outs.
