# Students Will Present Farce.
## 
### 
Arrangements have been completed
by a group of campus students for
the production of a farce entitled
"Who's Who." The play will be staged
it Palo Alto upon the evening of
March 23, and is primarily a benefit
fcr the Episcopal Church of that city.
C. F. Morgan, '10, will supervise and
manage the production, and, although
the cast has not been completed, the
full complement of players is assured.
In addition to the farce, unusual
numbers will be contributed by vari
ous members of the student body.
Among these are P. F. Pettigrew, '09,
H. D. Owen, '11, W. E. Ferguson, '12,
and H. B. Post, '12.
