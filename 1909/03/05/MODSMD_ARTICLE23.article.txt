# Arcade Moot Defeats Abbot's Inn.
## 
### 
The baseball team of the Arcade
Moot Court defeated the Court of Ab
bot's Inn this afternoon on the Var
sity diamond by a score of 20 to 5.
The batteries were: For the Moot
Court; G. N. Richardson, *09, and .J.
1). Rutledge, '11; for the Court of Ab
bot's Inn, W. H. Masters, '09, and W.
P. Mealey, '09. The umpires were
Professor F. C. Woodward and Assist
ant Professor J. W. Bingham, both of
the Law department.
