# PICK FRESHMAN TEAM
## FIFTEEN MEN PICKED TO MEET ST. MARY'S TOMORROW. List of Eligible 1912 Track Men Is Completed and Mailed by Dr. Angell.

The members of the Freshman
truck learn, which will meet St. Mary's
College at Oakland Saturday after
noon, will leave here at 9:81 tomor
row morning under the charge of Man
ager Stewart.
The Varsity tryouts will begin at
9:4!) sharp, so they will be ended in
time for Captain Norton and Trainer
Moulton to catch the 11:34 train to
San Francisco, as they wish to go to
Oakland to oversee the work of the
1912 men.
In order to facilitate this, Captain
Horton desires that all the men who
are to enter the tryouts be at the
training house by 9 :30.
