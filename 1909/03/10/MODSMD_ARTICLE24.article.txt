# Junior Committee Is Appointed.
## 
### 
President K. S. Mackenzie of tho
Junior class has appointed a commit
tee, consisting of P. C. Gier.v, A. H.
Drew and P. J. Bathin, to confer with
the present Senior class about the
Senior Circus material it has in its
possession, with a view of obtaining it
for use in a Senior circus next year.
