# "Ike" Russell Honored in East.
## 
### 
"Ike" Russell, '04, former editor of
the Chaparral and Quad and member
of the Press Club, leaves Salt Lake
City this week for New York, where
he has accepted a position as asso
ciate editor of Collier's. Russell has
been connected with the Deseret
Evening News of Salt Lake since leav
ing Stanford.
