# Guild Hoard Meets at Hospital.
## 
### 
A meeting of the Guild Board was
held last evening at the Guild Hospi
tal. Bills to the amount of $1039.33
were ordered paid. During last month
31 new patients were received at the
hospital. Of these new patients, 29
were students and the remainder out
siders. The number of hospital days
last month numbered 247 1-2. Five
operations were performed.
