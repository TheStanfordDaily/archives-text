# Classical Club to Meet.
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of the
Classical Club Tuesday evening,
March 16, at which Prof. E. H. Clapp,
the head of the Greek department of
the University of California, will give
an illustrated lecture on "New Light
on Greek Painting." The place is the
University Chapel, and the time, 7:30
o'clock. Dr. Clapp has recently re
turned from Greece, where he served
as professor in the American School
in Athens. The public is invited to
attend the lecture. Immediately after
the lecture, the members of the club
will adjourn to the Pi Beta Phi house.
