# Track Meet on Campus Called Off.
## 
### 
The track meet scheduled for Sat
urday between the Freshmen and Uni
versity of the Pacific track teams has
been postponed on account of the in
ability of the latter institution to send
a team here on that date. Instead,
an interclass meet will be held, the
Seniors and Freshmen opposing the
Juniors and Sophomores. All track
men are supposed to enter this meet.
