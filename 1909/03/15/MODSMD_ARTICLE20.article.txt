# S.P. to Make Special Rates South.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Company an
nounces that a return rate of one and
one-third fare will be made to all stu
dents wishing to go South during the
mid-semester vacation, provided more
than fifteen desire to go.
All students intending to go must
leave their names at the Southern Pa
cific office in Palo Alto immediately,
in order that the rate may he made.
