# Classical Club to Hold Meeting.
## 
### 
The Classical Club will hold its reg

Tuesday, evening in the Chapel. Prof.
E. B. Clapp, the head of the Greek
department of the University of Cal
ifornia, will deliver an illustrated lec
ture on "New Light on Greek Paint
ing." Dr. Clapp has recently returned
fi om Greece, where he served as pro
fessor in the American School in Ath
ens. The meeting is open to all who
wish to attend. Immediately after the
lecture, the members of the club will
adjourn to the Pi Beta Phi house, for
a business meeting, after which re
freshments will be served.
