# Y.M.C.A. Room Is Popular.
## 
### 
The room which was opened last
Tuesday by the Young Men's Chris
tian Association in the Engineering
building is proving very popular with
the students. The room is used as a
lounging and general meeting place.
A1 present live daily papers are being
received, and seven current periodi
cals are on file. For further conve
nience, a telephone is at the disposal
of patrons of the room. The associa
tion employment bureau, the informa
tion bureau and the secretary's office
are also located here.
The room is at the disposal of all
men of the University and every one
is welcome.
