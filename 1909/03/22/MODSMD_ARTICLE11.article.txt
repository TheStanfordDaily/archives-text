# Bishop Paddock Preaches in Chapel.
## 
### 
"Our Religion" was the subject of
the sermon at yesterday morning's
service in the University Chapel. Bish
op Robert L. Paddock occupied the
pulpit. The musical program con
sisted in an anthem by the choir and
a baritone solo by P. S. Hunt, '11.
Dr. D. C. Gardner announced that
there would be no service held next
Sunday.
