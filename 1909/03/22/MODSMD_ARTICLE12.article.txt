Festival Rehearsal—Tomorrow in
rc oni 08 at 11:15, 8 and 7:15 o'clock.
M. i:. Society—Will meet Wednes
day evening, 7:45 o'clock, at S. A. E.
Mouse.
Quadrangle Club —Will meet Mon
day night, March 22, at 9 o'clock, at
S. A. E. house.
Hand—Will meet in uniforms in
band room at 2:30 tomorrow for the
White Sox game.
Schubert Club—Will meet for regu
lar practice on Wednesday afternoon
at 4:15 at the Walden Club.
Clee Club.—There will be an im
portant meeting Wednesday evening.
Every member should be present Wed
nesday, room 68 at 7:30.
V. W. C. A. —Will meet in Chapel to
morrow afternoon at 4:30. Professor
Piexotto of the University of Califor
nia will speak, and Mrs. Stewart will
sing.
T. Vf. C. A.—Will hold its regular
meeting Tuesday afternoon in the
Chapel at 4:30. Dr. Jessica B. Peix
otto will speak on "The Fields of Ser
vice for Women."
Boxing and Fencing.—All men desir
ing to enter tournament will consult
Instructor Maloney at Encina Gym
nasium as early as possible. Contest
will be open to all students of the
University.
Junior Day.—Entries for Junior
Day events must be in by Thursday
next. An added feature will be a
quarter-mile swimming race. Classes
should select relay teams.
Junior Day Committee.
Kuglish 10 Shakespeare.—There will
be a conference for the following stu
dents Tuesday at 11:15 in room 202:
Misses McAllister, Lever, Looney,
Newlin, Ozier, Palmer, Porter, Shinn,
Shaw, Smith, Spaulding, Sugars,
Wood: and Messrs. Scofield, Ballard,
Beaufait. Mrs. Allan.
English 10 Shakespeare.—There will
be a conference for the following stu
dents Wednesday at 1:30 in room 202:
Misses Beeger, Brainerd, Bundy. Eh
renberg, Feraud, Flagg, Geohegan
Miller, Morton, Hyatt, Metzner, Otto,
Ware, Wood: and Messrs. Fletcher,
Lilienthal, Reed, Wheeler.
