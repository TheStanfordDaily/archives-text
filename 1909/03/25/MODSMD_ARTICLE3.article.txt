# FRESHMEN ELECT CAPTAIN.
## T.L. Coleman Chosen to Lead 1912 Track Team.—Officials Named for Varsity-U.S.C. Meet. 
### 
T. L. Coleman of Los Angeles was
lust night elected captain of the 1912
track team. Coleman has been doing
consistent work all season in the
sprints and he was unanimously chos
en to the honor as a recognition of
his ability. He will be one of the
mainstays of the Freshman team in
Saturday's meet, and is looked on to
win both of his events.
The Varsity-University of Southern
California meet will be called at 10
o'clock sharp on Saturday morning.
