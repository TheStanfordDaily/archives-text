# Executive Hoard Holds Meeting.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the executive board
of the Women's Athletic Association
this noon, the president, Miss M.
Monteith, 'OS, appointed the entire ex
ecutive board to act as a nominating
committee for the officers for next
year. At the regular meeting of the
hoard two weeks from to-day the
ticket will be adopted and election will
shortly follow.
