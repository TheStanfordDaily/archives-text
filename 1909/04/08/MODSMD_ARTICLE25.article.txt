# Order Interscholastic Medals.
## 
### 
The Interscholastic Association has
placed an order in the hands of Shreve
& Company, the San Francisco jewel
ers, for the manufacture of the med
als which will be given to the point
winners of the meet on May 1.
The medals follow the usual design
with the Stanford sc:;! on the face
and a blank for name and event of
the winner on the reverse.
Fourteen sets of gold, silver and
bronze medals have been ordered, also
a jet, of five bronze medals for the
winning relay team.
