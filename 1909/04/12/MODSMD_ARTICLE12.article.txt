# Good Lacrosse Game Held Saturday.
## 
### 
The lacrosse team from the 11:15
gymnasium section again defeated the
team representing the 5 o'clock class
in a close and hard fought game held
Saturday morning on the old oval.
The former team outclassed their riv
als in team work and thus held the
long end of the 4-3 score when time
was called. J. H. Wiggins, '10, of
ficiated as referee.
This is the first time this semester
the two teams have met, but two or
three games more will be played be
fore college closes.
