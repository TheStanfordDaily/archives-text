# Admiral Evans Will Speak Thursday.
## 
### 
Rear-Admiral Robley D. Evans will
speak in the Assembly Hall Thursday
evening, April 15, under the auspices
of the Faculty Committee on Public
Entertainments. The topic chosen by
the commander is "From Hampton
Roads to San Francisco," and will
cover the entire trip of the Atlantic
fleet around the South American con
tinent.
Tickets have been placed on sale at
the Stanford Hookstore, and the Stan
ford Pharmacy, the prices being $1
and 75 cents, with a reduction of 25
cents to all students.
