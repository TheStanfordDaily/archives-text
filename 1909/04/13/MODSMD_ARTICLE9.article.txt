# CONFERENCE OFFICERS CHOSEN.
## Delegates Hold Initial meeting and Begin Actual Work as Prescribed by Constitution. 
### 
At the initial meeting of the dele
gates to the University Conference,
held last evening, actual organization
was effected and business prescribed
by the constitution of the body trans
acted. Nineteen representatives were
present besides Dr. Jordan and A. E.
Roth, '09, who attended in his official
capacity of President of the Student
Body.
From this number the Conference
elected live members to serve upon
the Student Advisory Committee, re
sulting in the choice of A. E. Roth, '09,
.T. S. Hess, '09, T. R. Cadwalader, '09
.T. E. Shelton, '10, and J. E. Ward, 'gr.,
chairman.
Another committee designated as the
Publicity Committee was selected by
the Conference to enlighten the Stu
dent Body upon the objects and pur
poses of the organization. This'board
is comprised of M. R. Kirkwood, '09,
D. H. Walker, Jr., '09, and A. E. Roth,
'09, chairman.
According to the provisions laid
down by the Constitution, officers were
elected and resulted in the choice of
J. E. Ward, gr., for president, P. H.
Van Etten, '09, as vice-president and
M. R. Kirkwood for the position of
secretary.
