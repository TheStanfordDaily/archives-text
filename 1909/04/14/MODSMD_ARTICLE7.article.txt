# Offers Prize for Ball Programs.
## 
### 
The Senior Hall Committee met last
evening and commenced active work
in preparation for the dance which
will he held during Senior Week.
The committee decided to offer a
prize of five dollars for an original
design for a program 1o be used at
the ball. The committee reserves the
right to refuse any or all designs. Be

fore starting a design, competitors
should see some member of the com
mittee for further details. All designs
must be submitted to the committee
by April 24.
It was decided to limit the number
of dances to eighteen with no extras.
Arrangements linve not been completed
for the supper dance, but it will be
so arranged that it will not, conflict
in any way with the remainder of the
