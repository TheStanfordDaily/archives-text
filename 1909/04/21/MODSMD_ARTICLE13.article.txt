# Interscholastic Entries Received.
## 
### 
The entries for the annual Inter
scholastic field day which is to he held
on the varsity track 011 May 1, are
being received by the Secretary of the
Association.. As yet only a few en
tries have arrived, but a great number
of them are expected within the next
few days, as Saturday, April 24, is the
last day on which entries will be re
ceived. The preparatory schools, the
entire length of the coast, have ex
pressed considerable interest in the
annual meet this year and if this in
terest is continued, the meet will be
the greatest annual contest of second
ary schools in the West.
The Hoard of Governors of the in
rerscholastic will hold a meeting to
morrow night to complete the plans
for the field day.
