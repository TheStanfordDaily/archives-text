# Baker Speaks Before M.E. Society.
## 
### 
The Mechanical Engineering Society
held a special meeting at the Delta
Upsilon House yesterday evening to
hear an address by W. E. Paker, the
Coast representative of the Westing
house Machine Company. Baker who
is an engineer who has made a re
markable progress in his profession
in a comparatively short time, gave
an interesting talk on the subject of
the "Westinghouse-Parsons Steam
Turbine." After the talk, the society
enjoyed a social hour during which
Paker gave much good advice to the
graduating students as to the lies':
ways to achieve future success in en
gineering work.
