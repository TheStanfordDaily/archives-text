# SMOKER TOMORROW EVENING
## Law Students and Faculty to Enjoy Program at Stanford Inn, at 8 O'clock. 
### 
Tomorrow evening at 8 o'clock in
the Stanford Inn, the students regis
tered in the Law School will give a
smoker to the members of the faculty
of that department and to those who
may be registered in the prelegal
division. This will be the second en
tertainment of this nature that the
School has held this year and will be
conducted upon the same lines as
those in the past.
Music will constitute a prominent
part of the program, while speeches
and talks of various character will
also be given during the evening. Re
freshments and smokes will be dis
pensed with to those present. All
pre-legal majors are requested to be
present.
