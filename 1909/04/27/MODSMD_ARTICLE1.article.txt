# PROGRAM IS COMPLETE
## SENIOR WEEK FESTIVITIES WILL REMIX ON MAY 13. Prof. J.P. Hall to Deliver Commencement Address and Bishop Hughes to Preach Baccalaurate. 
### 
The Program Committee of the
Senior Class.has completed the list
of events of the coming Senior Week.
The festivities will be inaugurated by
the presentation of the Senior Farce
on the evening of May 13, and will
not be concluded until May 19, when
Professor J. P. Hall, the Dean of .the
Chicago Law School, will deliver the
commencement address. The Bacca
laurate Sermon will be preached on
Sunday, May 16, by Bishop E. H.
Hughes of the Methodist church. The
full program is as follows:
Thursday, May 13.
8:00 p. m. —Senior Farce.
Friday, May 14.
4:00 p. m.—Reception to graduates by
President and Mrs. Jordan in Zo
ology Building.
8:00 p. m.—Senior Reception in En
cina Hall.
Saturday, May 15.
8:30 a. m.—Registration of Alumni on
the Quad.
9:30 a. m. —Alumni —Senior Baseball
Game.
11:30 a. m.—Annual Meeting of the
Alumni Association.
1:00 p. m.—Alumni Re-union and
Luncheon at Roble Hall.
5:30 p. m.—Class Banquets and Re
unions: Classes of '94, '99, '04
and '07.
8:00 p. m. —Alumni Reception in En
cina Hall.
Sunday, May lfi.
11:00 a. m. —Baccalaurate Sermon by
Bishop E. H. Hughes of the Metho
dist Church.
Monday, May 17.
10:30 a. m.—Class Day Exercises in
the Chapel.
11:00 a. m. —Dedication of '09 Plate.
11:30 a. m.—ln Memoriam: Transfer
ence of Memorial Duty to the Class
of '10, at, the Mausoleum.
7:30 p. m.—Senior Ball, in Encina
Hall.
Tuesday, May 18.
9:30 a. m.—Senior-Faculty Baseball
Game.
12:00 m.—Annual Meeting of the Law
Association.
4:00 p. m. —Phi Beta Kappa Open
Meeting in the Chapel. Address
by Horace Davis. Poem by Clar
ence Urmy.
6:00 p. m.—Phi Beta Kappa Banquet.
7:00 j). m.—Last Senior Singing.
8:00 p. m. —Promenade Concert.
Wednesday, May 1!>. ..
10:30 a. m.—Commencement in As
sembly Hall.
