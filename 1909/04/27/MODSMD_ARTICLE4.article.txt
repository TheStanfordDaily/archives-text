# Roth Appoints Election Hoard.
## 
### 
Student Body President A. E. Roth
has appointed the board to conduct
the balloting at the election to-mor
row. The polls will be open from
11:15 a. m. to 2:30 p. m.and the fol
lowing will have charge at the stated
times:
From 11:15 to 12:15, C. E.
Sampson, '09, will act as inspector;
J. R. Pemberton, '08, and K. S. Mc-
Kenzie, '10, as distributors: H. L. Hor
ton, '08, as poll-clerk and G. R. Wells,
'09, and J. H. Bell, '08, as ballot
clerks.
From 12:15 to 1:30, C. R. Young,
'09, will be inspector; B. L. Gregg,
'09, and A. E. Ehrhorn, '10, distribu
tors; C. Coonan, '09, poll-clerk and
P. C. Geisy, '10, and C. B. Bradford,
'09, ballot-clerks.
From 1:30 to 2:30, J. R. Holman,
'09, will be inspetcor; V. F. Bellows,
'09, and J. M. Ward, '07, distributors;
T. R. Cadwalader, 07, poll-clerk and
J. P. Hemphill, '09, and D. H. Terry,
'08, ballot-clerks.
The ballots will be counted in Room
68.
