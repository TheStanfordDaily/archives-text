# Art Club to Hold Exhibition.
## 
### 
The Stanford Art Club will hold
this week an international exhibition
of etchings. This exhibition which is
the most important one given during
the year by the club will represent the
works of almost every great master
with the exception of Rembrandt.
There will be aqua-tints, dry points,
and soft ground etchings as well as
etchings in pure line. Among the
masters of the needle and the copper
plate shown are Whistler, Corot, Mil
let, Seymore, Hoden, Goff, Legros,
Lalanne, Chahine and Max Lieber
mann.
