# Quads.
## 
### 
P. L. Wilde, '10, who has been very
seriously ill at the Guild Hospital, is
steadily improving. Dr. Wilbur states
that he rested easier last evening than
at any other time during his sickness.
Miss Anita Butler Perrin, '03, and
Earl Hamaker Knepper, '02, were
married to-day at Stockton. Rev. 1).
Charles Gardner read the marriage
service. Mrs. Knepper is a member of
the Kappa Kappa Gamma sorority,
while Mr. Knepper is a member of
Beta Theta. Pi. After their honey
moon the couple will make their home
in Los Angeles.
