# Arizona Botanist to Speak.
## 
### 
On Monday afternoon, May 3, at
4 o'clock, Dr. W. A. Cannon, formerly
assistant in the Botany department,
will speak in the botanical lecture
room on "The Root-Systems of Desert
Plants." Dr. Cannon is now connected
with the Carnegie Desert Botanical
Laboratory in Tuscon, Arizona. All
members of the University are invited
