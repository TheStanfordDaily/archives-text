# Dr. D.T. MacDougal to Speak.
## 
### 
Dr. Daniel Trembly MacDougal will
speak in the Chapel next Tuesday eve
ning, May 4, at 8, under the auspices
of the Sigma Xi Society on "The In
fluence of Aridity on the Evolutionary
Development of Plants." Dr. Mac-
Dougal is the director of botanical
research in the Carnegie Institution,
Washington, D. C. The lecture will
be open to all who desire to attend.
