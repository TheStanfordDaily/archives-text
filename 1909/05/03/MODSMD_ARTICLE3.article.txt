# Last Mooting of the Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
The last meeting of the Y. W. C. A.
for this year will be held in the
Chapel, Tuesday afternoon at 4:30.
A special musical program of five or
six numbers will be rendered, and
Miss A. Shinn, '09, President for this
year, will give a farewell address.
Following this the officers for the
coming year, 1909-1910. will be in
stalled. Every woman of the Univer
sity is invited.
