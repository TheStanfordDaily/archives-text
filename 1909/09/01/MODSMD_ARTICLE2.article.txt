# Freshman Class Will Meet To-morrow.
## 
### 
Student Advisor A. E. Roth has
called a meeting tM" the men of the
Freshman Class for to-morrow after
noon at 1:30, when he will outline
the policy of his office for the coming
year, and endeavor to assist the mem
bers of the class in starting their work
in the most efficient manner. Every
member of the class is expected to
be present in order that he may thor
oughly understand his relations with
the Student Advisor.
Afterwards the class will be or
ganized for the underclass rush on
Friday afternoon, under the super
vision of F. H. Hilton, '11. president
of the Junior class. Plans and details
for the contest will be arranged and
a thorough organization perfected.
