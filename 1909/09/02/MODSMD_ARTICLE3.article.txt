# Sophomores Hold First Meeting.
## 
### 
The Sophomore class held its first
meeting of the year this afternoon at
1:30 in room 214. Definite plans were
made to govern the class in the rope
tying contest to-morrow afternoon.
It was decided that the entire class
should meet on the day of the rush
at. the tennis courts in front of En
cina Hall, at four o'clock, sharp. Va
rious other matters were brought be
fore the meeting, and the class yell
was practiced.
Vice-president Person presided in
the absence of President Grimes.
