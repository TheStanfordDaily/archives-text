# Lecturer to Talk oil Negro Problem.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at 1:30 o'clock,
K. Calhoun will deliver an address
upon "The Negro Problem as Related
to Industrial Education." The speaker
was a pupil and a former assistant of
Booker T. Washington, and at present
occupies the position of director of
the industrial school for negroes at
Eatonville, Florida. Calhoun is very
familiar with the topic of his talk
through his associations with his for
mer instructor. He extends a cordial
invitation to all to attend the lecture,
