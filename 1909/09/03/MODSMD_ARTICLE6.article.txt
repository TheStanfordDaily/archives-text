# R.C. Root, '96 to Speak on Peace.
## 
### 
Next Monday afternoon at 3:30
o'clock, a lecture will be delivered in
the University Chapel upon the topic
"Peace and Arbitration." Robert C.
Root, who is a graduate of Stanford
and a member of the class of 1596,
will be the speaker. He has been act
ing in the capacity of President of the
American Peace Association. A cor
dial invitation to the University public
is extended by the lecturer.
