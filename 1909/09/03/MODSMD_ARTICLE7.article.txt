# Professor Flugel Returns from Europe.
## 
### 
Professor E. Flugel, of the Philology
department, who during the summer
attended the 500 th anniversary of the
founding of the University of Leipzig,
Germany, as the delegate of Stanford
University, has just returned to Amer
ica and is at present in Washington
where he is superintending the print
ing of his Chaucer Dictionary.
