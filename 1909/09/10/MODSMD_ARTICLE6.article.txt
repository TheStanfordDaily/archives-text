# Reception to Freshmen To-morrow.
## 
### 
Tomorrow evening at 7:30 o'clock,
the reception to new students that is
to be given under the auspices of both
the Young Women's Christian Associa
tion and the Young Men's Christian
Association, will be held in Encina
Club Room. The Associations extend
invitations to all who desire to at
tend.
The committee in charge of the eve
ning has made arrangements for an
interesting program, while a general
reception and jolly-up will complete
the entertainment. Special busses
have been secured to accommodate all
those who desire to come from Palo
Alto to-morrow evening.
The members of tin Advisory Board
of the Young Women's Christian Asso
ciation have consented to ae! as pat
ronesses for the evening.
