# Library Receives Valuable Books.
## 
### 
The University Library has just re
ceived through the courtesy of the
Minister of Public Instruction of
France a valuable set of books en
titled, "Documents inedits sur l'his
toire de France." The collection in
cludes more than 100 volumes and
contains much material that will be
of particular value to the Department
of Romanic Languages and the De
partment of History.
