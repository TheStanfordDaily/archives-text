# Classical Club Will Meet Wednesday.
## 
### 
The Classical Club will hold their
first meeting at 7:30, Wednesday, Sept.
22, at a place of meeting to be an
nounced later. Miss Irmagarde Rich
ards, of Mills College, is to be the
speaker. Miss Richards spent last
year in Rome where she attended the
American School of Classical Studies.
All Latin and Greek majors and others
interested are invited to attend
