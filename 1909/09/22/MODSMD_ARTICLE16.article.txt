# N. Frederickson to Address Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
The Y. M. C. A. will hold their reg
ular meeting next Sunday morning at
9:45 in room 264, adjoining the Men's
Room on the Quad. The speaker will
be N. Frederickson who is a mechan
ical engineer in the University. He
will take as his subject, "The Religion
and Politics of Russia." The speaker
has spent twenty years in Russia, and
is thoroughly conversant with the re
ligion and political system of that
country. All who are interested are
cordially invited to be present.
