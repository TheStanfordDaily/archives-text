# Large Squad Starts Training.
## 
### 
Many of the men who signed up for
fall training at (he rally Tuesday
night were seen on the track yester
day and to-day, limbering up their
muscles and becoming reaccustomed
to the use of spikes. All are doing
only light work as yet, and are being
watched by Trainer Moulton who is
at the track every morning and every
afternoon after three o'clock.
