# Date of Sophomore Cotillion Set.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the committee last
night, the date of the Sophomore Co
tillion was set for Friday. Dec. 3.
Although no definite plans have been
decided upon at this date, yet the
committees have been working bard
to make this dance as elaborate a.;
possible and to equal any of the
cotillions given by previous classes.
The dance will be held in the club
room of Enciua Hall, and following
the precedent of former years, flowers
and carriages will not be in order.
Dates for the rehearsals will be an
nounced later.
