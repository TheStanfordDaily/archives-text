# Y.M.C.A. to Meet in Chapel Tuesday.
## 
### 
The Y. M. C. A. will meet in the
Chapel at 4:30 to-morrow, Tuesday
afternoon, for the purpose of discuss
ing important affairs connected with
the Association. All members are
asked to be present.
The speaker at yesterday's meeting
was P. C. Brown, the travelling secre
tary of the Christian Endeavor So
ciety, who delivered a strong talk to
the men.
