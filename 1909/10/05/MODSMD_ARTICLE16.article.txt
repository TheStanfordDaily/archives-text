# Berkeley Notes.
## 
### 
The women students at California
are planning to give a "jinks" in
Hearst Hall. The playlets and stunts
are said to lie the cleverest yet writ
ten by the women students there.
The DeKoven Club, a semi-Univer
sity musical organization at the Uni
versity of California, has reassembled
for the present year and is preparing
for the winter and spring festivities.
