# Sanitation Car in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
The Sanitation Exhibit car sent out
by the State Board of Health was on
the side track at the Palo Alto station
to-day where it was visited by a num
ber of students interested in public
health. Dr. W. F. Snow, formerly
head of the Hygiene department of
the University and now a member of
the State Board of Health planned
and sent out the car last spring. It
is now under the direction of M. B.
Bairos, '09, and is being sent around
the stale in an endeavor to teach
Californians the proper methods of
living.
