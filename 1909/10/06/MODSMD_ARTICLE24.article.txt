# To Present Stereopticon Views.
## 
### 
Stereopticon views of the Island of
Java will be shown by P. .1. Trgat, as
sistant professor, next Friday after
noon at 1:30 o'clock, in room 214.
These views consist of a collection of
pictures that Professor Treat made
during his sojourn in that country
several years ago. Professor Treat
will lecture briefly upon the series of
pictures that he will show.
