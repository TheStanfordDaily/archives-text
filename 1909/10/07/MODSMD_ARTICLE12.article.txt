# Discuss Plans for 1912 Cotillion.
## 
### 
Further plans for the Sophomore
Cotillion were discussed at a meeting
of the Cotillion Committee held last
evening at the Delta Gamma house.
No definite action was taken beyond
deciding that the Cotillion is to he held
in Encina Hall 011 tlu> night of Decem
ber 3, though the committee discussed
various plans for making the function
a financial success.
