# Encina Grants Use of Clubroom.
## 
### 
Permission for the use of Encina
Clubroom on the evening of Decem
ber 3 was granted yesterday by the
Board of Directors of Encina Club to
the Sophomore Cotillion Committee for
the holding of the annual second-year
dance. The matter of raising money
for the purchase of a billiard-table was
also discussed.
