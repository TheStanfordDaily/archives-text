There's Another Big Treat in
Store at Wilson's
Hesse & Williams, tlie groat vaudeville singers
itnd entertiiiners, have Iteen secured for next Fri
day and Saturday and will entertain the people
of I'iiio Alio at our store.
Those artists are among' tiie best on the vaude
ville stage, and sire sure to msike a hig hit. There'll
he ;i crowd and we urge sill who can to come
early Friday evening or Saturday afternoon.
