The Associated Students of Leland
Stanford Junior University wish to ex
press their profound sorrow in the
sad calamity which so suddenly re
moved from our midst Charles Ronald
Wheeler. We realize that by this un
timely death we have lost a comrade,
esteemed and respected.
To the bereaved relatives of our de
parted companion, feeling deeply for
them in their sorrow, we desire to
express, In no spirit of mere formality,
our heartfelt sympathy.
J. E. Shelton, Pres.,
J. M. Wadsworth, Secy.
