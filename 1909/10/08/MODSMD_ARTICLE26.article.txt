# Suffrage League to Present Program.
## 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of the Colle
giate Women's Suffrage League will
have charge of an interesting pro
gram to he given under the auspices
of the Women's League, at Roble Hall,
Saturday, Oct. 9, at 2 o'clock. All
faculty women are especially invited.
The meeting will be over in time for
those who wish to attend the football
game.
