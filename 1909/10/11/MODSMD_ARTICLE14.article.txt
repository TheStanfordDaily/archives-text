# Talks on Conditions of English Women
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Suffrage
Club, a meeting of the Women's
League was held Saturday afternoon.
Short talks were given by Miss E. C.
Jackson, Mrs. C. H. Howard, Dr. Ad
elaide Brown, Mrs. C. C. Hall and
Miss F. W. McLean, all prominent
members of the Collegiate Alumni.
Mrs. Gerberding, President of the
Women's Heney Club of San Fran
cisco also gave a talk on the con
ditions of women in England as she
herself had observed them.
