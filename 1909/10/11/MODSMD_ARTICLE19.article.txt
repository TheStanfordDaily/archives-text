# Sheldon initiated into Phi Delta Phi.
## 
### 
E. R. Sheldon, '10, was this morn
ing initiated into the rank of Phi
Delta Phi, the legal fraternity. The
initiation, according to the custom was
held on the Quad, the neophyte being
dressed to represent Don Caspar de
Portola and being required to deliver
Speeches in front of the Quad and from
the Post-office steps at noon.
Sheldon registers in Law from Palo
Alto and is a member of the Sigma
Nil Fraternity.
