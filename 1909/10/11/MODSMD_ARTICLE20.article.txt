# Sophomore Women's Tennis.
## 
### 
The preliminaries of the Sophomore
Women's Tennis Tournament were
played off last week with the follow
ing results: Miss L. Marcus defeated
E. W. Show 5-7, 6-4, 6-3. D. M. Alder
ton defeated V. Moore by default. E. L.
Franklin defeated H. G. Spaulding 3-6,
6-2, 6-2. F. 15. Rankin defeated L. E.
Ames 7-5, 6-4. V. M. Watkins won
by default for lack of an opponent.
The next, matches will be played off in
the next two weeks.
The Freshman Tournament is to be
played this week, the Junior next week
and the Senior the week after. The
interclass match will follow early next
month.
