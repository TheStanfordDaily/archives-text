# To Make Seven-Cent Round-Trip Rate.
## 
### 
The Peninsular Railway Company
has arranged to sell hooks of tickets
to students which will make a round
trip fare of seven cents lo all points
in Palo Alto. The Mayfield line will
soon be in operation. Ballasting of
the campus road bed will not be

rushed, as the track is heavy enough
to sustain all travel until the rain\
season.
