# Runaway Oil-Car Causes Fright.
## 
### 
Failure of the brakes to hold a
loaded oil-cur on the grade back of the
' Engineering buildings nearly caused
a serious head-on collision late yes
terday afternoon. The tank car had
I attained considerable momentum when
; a crowded electric car rounded the
curve at the Gymnasium. Several stu
dents, realizing the danger, ran down
the track, signalling the motorman to
stop, with the result that tbe electric
car backed away in time. The run
away was finally stopped near Encina
Hall with no damage having resulted
from its wild trip.
