# Shakespeare Recital Before Nestoria.
## 
### 
Sections of Othello as read by G. F.
Morgan, 'JO, and F. H. Hilton, '11, at
the regular meeting of Nestoria Lit
erary Society yesterday evening were
greatly enjoyed by the large number
present. As an encore to the read
in,'?, Hilton ably rendered several pas
sages from Romeo and Juliet.
The regular debate as argued by
C. A. Christin, '11, M. Y. Malone, '10,
affirmative, and H. C. Nelson," '10, and
F. L. Guerena, '11, negative, was:
"Resolved, That Congress should levy
a progressive income tax on all in
comes over $2,000 per year."
S. E. Goode, '11, M. B. Henshaw, '12,
and E. M. Albright, '12, were elected
to membership in the society.
