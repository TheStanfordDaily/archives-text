# Invite Stanford Men to Senior Hall.
## 
### 
Senior President H. E. Reed to-day
received the following message from
President Edwards of the Senior class
at California, in which an invitation
to visit Senior Hall on Saturday is
extended to the Stanford Seniors:
"Mr. H. E. Reed,
"President Senior Class:
"The Senior men of the University
of California invite the Seniors of
Stanford to make use of Senior Hall
as a meeting and resting place hc
fore and after the game on Saturday.
"W. Edwards, '10, President.
