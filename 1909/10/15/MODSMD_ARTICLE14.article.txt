# Announces Time of Portola Regatta.
## 
### 
Word has been received from A. 11.
Hammersmith, the chairman of the
Portola Regatta Committee, giving the
exact time of the race in which the
Stanford four is to participate. The
contest will be rowed at one o'clock
on the afternoon of October 23, and
will be for a distance of a mile and a
half. The course is to be laid between
the lines of the American and the
visiting foreign warships.
