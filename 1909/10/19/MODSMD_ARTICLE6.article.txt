# Economics Professors to Meet.
## 
### 
A plan has been adopted this year
for bringing the faculties of the Eco
nomics department of Stanford and
the University of California into closer
touch. Five meetings, partaking of
the nature of banquets, are to be held,
in order that the professors may be
come better acquainted and that ques
tions of mutual interest may he dis
cussed. The first meeting of the ser
ies is to he held on October 30.
