# WILL DONATE OPERA PROCEEDS.
## Schubert Club to Give Profits of “Chimes of Normandy” to Woman's Club House Fund. 
### 
The Schubert Club, which will pro
duce "The Chimes of Normandy" in
Assembly Hall on October 2D, lias de
cided to donate to the fund for the
Woman's Club House the net proceeds
from that performance. An encour
aging sum should be realized, for al
though the staging, costuming and
coaching of the production will be ex
tremely expensive, a large audience
seems assured.
The costumes will arrive from San
Francisco, Friday, and the pictures of
various scenes will be taken at once
for advertising purposes.
The sale of seats will commence at
the usual places next Monday morn
ing.
