# Student Injured While Fencing.
## 
### 
J. B. Beman, '12, suffered a painful,
although not serious injury, in the
Gymnasium this morning while en
gaged in a friendly fencing contest.
The button from his opponent's foil
dropped off and before its disappear
ance was noticed his opponent's foil
snapped and the broken end passed
through the fleshy part of his arm
back of the biceps muscle.
