# LATE ARRIVALS WILL WAIT.
## 
### 
In order to avoid the disturbance
that has been caused heretofore by
late comers, lite management of "The
Chimes of Normandy" lias decided to
admit no one during the first act. The
doors will he locked when the curtain
goes up and all who come late will
be obliged to wait outside until the
end of the act.
