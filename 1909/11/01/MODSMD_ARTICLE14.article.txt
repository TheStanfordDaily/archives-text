# Pioneer Minister Speaks in Chapel.
## 
### 
The pulpit of the University Chapel
was occupied yesterday by the Rev
erend Doctor lv. MacKenzie, who
preached a strong sermon treating
on the present-day subjection of the
individual to the advantage of the
corporate body. Dr. MacKenzie
preached the first sermon ever de
livered in the University, and for this
reason bis address yesterday was
listened to with special interest.
