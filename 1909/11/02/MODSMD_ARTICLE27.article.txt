# Will Lecture on Public Health.
## 
### 
Dr. Ernest Hoag, Medical Examiner
in the Berkeley Schools, and Lecturer
at the State University, will give two
illustrated lectures in the University
Chapel, Wednesday, November 3, one
at 4:40 to the general public on "Some
of the Common Bodily Defects and
Their Relation to Physical and Mental
Efficiency," and the other at 7:4f> on
"Health Inspection in the Schools."
The latter will be of special interest
to teachers and parents and to the
students who are going out to teach.
A third lecure will be given before
the class in Public Hygiene at 8:15
Thursday morning, room 460.
