# Will Address Classical Club.
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough, of the
Latin department, will be the speaker
of the'evening at the meeting of the
Classical Club at the Alpha Phi house
on Thursday evening. He will speak
upon "A Visit to Crete."
All members of the Latin and Greek
departments and any others who are
interested are invited to lie present,.
