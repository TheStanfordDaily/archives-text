# Fall Baseball Practice Ended Monday.
## 
### 
This is clue in part to the fact that
the varsity field will not be available
for the baseball men for the next two
weeks on account of other needs for
it. The work had already continued
for a month, and this was thought
sufficient time to be spent on the pre
liminary practice. The next appear
ance of the squad will come with the
opening of the regular season in
January.
