# Twenty-three Men on Training Table.
## 
### 
To the nineteen players already on
the varsity training table, four have
been added, J. W. Doron, '12, C. E.
Woodcock, '11, T. Sanborn, '12, and
L. Cass, '13. Cass is tho third Fresh
man to lie placed in the list, S. Mit
chell and E. Kern having been added
some time ago.
