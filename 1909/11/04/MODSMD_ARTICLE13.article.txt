Faculty Women's Club —Will meet

Juniors Attention. —Quad assess
ments are now due. All photographs
must be taken this semester at
Davey's.
Freshmen. —All those wishing to
submit songs or yells mail them to
P. O. Box 1110, Campus. Songs may
have original or adapted music.
Junior Men.—All who can assist in
erecting a stage for Plug Ugly meet
on Baseball diamond every afternoon.
Bring tools. Plug Ugly Committee.
Mandolin Club — Practice Friday,
4:30, room 55, also 7:00 p.m., Satur
day evening, room 55. Important that
all members be present at both meet
ings.
Stanford Tram per?— Will not walk
Saturday, November 6, or Saturday,
November 13, on account of the foot
ball games. The walk for Wednes
day, November 10 at 4:30 will be an
nounced later.
Nestoria—Will meet Tuesday, No
vember 9. Subject for debate, "Re
solved, That a prolonged condition of
international peace is undesirable."
Affirmative, Morgan, Hilton; negative,
Pittenger, Visel.
Hooter's Ilals—Will be on sale at
55 Encina from - to 3 every afternoon
and before the game Saturday at the
bleachers. No one will be allowed in
the rooter's section at the Varsity
game without a hat.

Law.—A special make-up examina
tion in Business Law will be given in

benefit of all persons who failed in
