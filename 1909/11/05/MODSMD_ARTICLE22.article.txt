# Botany Class Will Visit Mud Lakes.
## 
### 
To-morrow morning at 9 o'clock the
class In Botany I will leave the Bot
any building for the fourth trip of the
semester. The members will ride in
a bus to the Mud Lakes and will take
their lunches, but the return of the
party will be in the early afternoon,
so that the members can see the game
with Vancouver. Dr. Pierce will lead
the party.
