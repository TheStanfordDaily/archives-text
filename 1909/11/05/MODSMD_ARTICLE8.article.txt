# To Secure Rugby Suits for Varsity.
## 
### 
Coach Presley and Manager Bur
bank left the campus for San Fran
cisco to-day to secure the rugby suits
which are to be used in the game
against California. Thase will prob
ably, as in former years, consist of
red jerseys and white khaki pants.
The men on the training table were
measured for their suits yesterday by
a representative of a sporting goods
store in the city.
