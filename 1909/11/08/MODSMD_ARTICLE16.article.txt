# Saloon Keepers under Arrest.
## 
### 
District Attorney Bullock of San
Mateo announced this afternoon at
2 o'clock that the eleven saloon
keepers of Menlo Park will be under
arrest before nightfall. Mr. Bullock
spent the fore-part of the afternoon
making out. the warrants for their
arrest. This action is the first taken
to ascertain the validity of the bill
passed at tin' last meeting of the state
legislature to close saloons within a
mile and a half of the University.
