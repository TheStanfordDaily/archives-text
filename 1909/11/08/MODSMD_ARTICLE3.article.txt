## Worswick Added to Training Table. 
### 
S. D. Worswick, '13, has been adder!
to the varsity training table, making
twenty-four on Ihe list, and the fourth
Freshman to be added. Roth, who was
forced to withdraw from the game
with Vancouver last Saturday is but
slightly injured, and will be given a
rest in order to be at his best for
next Saturday. Brown was in a suit
at practice to-day and S. Mitchell will
be out to-morrow.
