# Back up the Yell-Leaders.
## Euphronia Speaks on Plug Ugly. 
### 
Euphronia Literary Society debated
the interesting impromptu question
of, "Resolved, That the Plug Ugly
should be abolished," at its regular
meeting yesterday evening in the
Economics building. The debate was
argued by C. C. Shoemaker, '10, C. H.
Marvin, '13, affirmative; W. H. Pat
terson, '10, K. E. Leib, '13, negative.
P. J. Batkin, '10, and S. Bryan, '10,
as the affirmative speakers on the
question, "Resolved, That three fourths
of a jury should be competent to ren
der a verdict in all criminal cases,"
received the favorable decision of the
judges. T. M. Levy, '13, and F. E.
Hill, '11, upheld the negative.
