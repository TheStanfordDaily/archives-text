# Fraternities Will Burn Red Fire.
## 
### 
As one of the features of the rally
to-morrow night the Yell-Leaders
are planning to have red fire burned
in front of each fraternity house to
morrow nisht just previous to the
rally. This unique innovation is
planned as a special feature to illum
inate the whole Row as the members
of the various fraternities depart
for Assembly Hall. The Yell-Leaders
request each fraternity to procure red
fire and assist in carrying out this
stunt.
