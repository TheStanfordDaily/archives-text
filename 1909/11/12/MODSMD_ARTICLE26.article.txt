# WILL RUN SPECIAL AFTER SHOW
## 
### 
For the accommodation of those
who wish to stay on the campus to
morrow to see the Football Vaude
ville, the Southern Pacific company
will run a special from Palo Alto to
San Francisco at 11:00 p. m. Satur
day night. In case the crowd at that
time is too large for one train, an ex
tra will be made up.
