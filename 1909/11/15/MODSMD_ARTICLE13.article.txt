# Bradley Will Give Interesting Talk.
## 
### 
Ernest Bradley will be the speaker
at the meeting of the Social Service
Club next Wednesday evening at the
Kappa Alpha Theta house. He will talk
on "The Tamalpias Center," an organi
zation located near San Francisco. The
center has been opened during the
last month and is similar to the idea
of the George Junior Republic. It
has had a very promising opening
with Bradley at its head. Bradley
will tell about the objects and work
ings of the Center at the meeting on
Wednesday evening. The general Uni
versity public and any one interested
in the subject is cordially invited to
attend.
