# Cotillion Tickets Go on Sale.
## 
### 
Tickets for the Sophomore Cotillion
went on sale this morning for the
first time, and will continue to be sold
until the number is exhausted. The
price of admission is $2.00 and, as the
demand is great, it is advisable for
intending purchasers to buy their
tickets at once. These will be on sal; 1
on the Quad at the Law steps from
11:15 to 12:15 every morning, or mav
be obtained from J. A. Davis at the
Delta Kappa Epsilon house, or from
CI. 0. Larkin, 111 Encina.
