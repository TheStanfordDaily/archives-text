# Dwiggins Wins Punting Trophy.
## 
### 
.lay Dwiggins, the captain elect of
the California varsity, won the Roos
punting cup on California Field yes
terday afternoon. Jordan pressed the
winner closely, but lost out in the
final punting. The cup is a perpetual
trophy on which are already inscribed
the names of Roy Elliott, Ralph But
ler and S. E. Carpenter, well-known
California punters.
