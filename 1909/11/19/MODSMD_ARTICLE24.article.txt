# Eastern Teams to Play in San Jose.
## 
### 
The Philadelphia Athletics and the
All Nationals will play baseball at

Luna Park in San Jose next Saturday
afternoon at 3. Both teams are com
posed of men who are well known
in baseball circles and their appear
ance in San Jose will probably draw
a crowd. The teams have been mak
ing an extended tour of the coast and
have played in most of the important
western cities.
