# TEAM WILL BE ENTERTAINED.
## Skull and Snakes to Banquet the Rugby Fifteen in Palo Alto Monday Night. 
### 
The Skull and Snakes Society will
entertain the 1900 Rugby team and
several other guests at a banquet next
Monday night, probably at Romona
Hall in Palo Alto. A well-chosen
menu will be served by Caterer Mil
ler, of Palo Alto, beginning at nine
o'clock. Besides the members of the
team and the Society, as hosts, there
will be present Dr. F. Angell, Coaches
Presley and Lanagan, Trainer Moul
ton and Graduate Manager D. W. Bur
bank.
