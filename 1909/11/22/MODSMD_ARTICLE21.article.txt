# Olympics Are State Club Champions.
## 
### 
The Olympics defeated the Barbar
ians by a narrow margin in the an
nual club contest last. Saturday. The
score was 6-3. Briggs, of the winged
"O" men, won his game by two goals
from offside play on the part of the
Barbarians. All the scoring was
made in the second half, both sides
playing equally well in the first period.
Many Stanford and California alum
ni were on the two fifteens. The
victory gave the Olympic Club the
Wolff trophy and the state club cham
pionship.
