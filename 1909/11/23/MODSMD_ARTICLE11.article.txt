# Special Rates for Thanksgiving.
## 
###

that students' rates of a fare and a i
third round trip will be granted to!
Stanford students for the Thanksgiv
ing vacation. These tickets will be
good from Wednesday afternoon until
(be following Monday night and will
be granted only where the ordinary
rates are under $10.
