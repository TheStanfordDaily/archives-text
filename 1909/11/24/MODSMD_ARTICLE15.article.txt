# Special Rates for Thanksgiving.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific has announced
that students' rates of a fare and a
third round trip will be granted to
Stanford students for the Thanksgiv
ing vacation. These tickets will be
Miood from Wednesday afternoon until
the following Monday night and will
he granted only where the ordinary
rates are under $10.
