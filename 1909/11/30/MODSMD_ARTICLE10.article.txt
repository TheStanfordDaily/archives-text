# To Decide Gymnastic Championship.
## 
### 
The University gymnastic champion
ship is to be decided Thursday night
in Encina Gymnasium, at the first
regular "open-meeting" night of the
Gym. Club. The exhibition is to be
an informal one as the men will only
do their regular meeting work In
addition to the contest. Any one in
terested in gymnasium work is invited
to be present.
