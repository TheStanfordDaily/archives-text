# Philological Association to Meet.
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Philo
logical Association will he held on
Thursday, December 2 at 2:30 in
room 31.
Two interesting papers will be read.
The first by Prof. H. D. Gray, of the
English department, on "The Original
Version of Love's Labours Lost."
The second by Prof. A. T. Murray, of
the Latin department, on "A Nole on
Homer."
