# Art Exhibition Next Semester.
## 
### 
Early next semester there will be
an exhibition of illustrations by prom
inent American artists John Scott
Williams, John Boyd, Clara 10. Peck.
H. W. Townsend, and McCormick.
The originals of these illustrations
have appeared in McClure's, Collier's.
Munsey, Harper's and many other of
the leading magazines.
