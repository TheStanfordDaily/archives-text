# W.F. Fowler to Marry Miss Brainier.
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. J. C. Brainier
have made the interesting announce
ment that their daughter, Miss 10.
Branner, '08, will be married during
the present month to W. F. Fowler,
'05. The ceremony will be solemnized
at the All-Saints Church in Palo Alto
on December 20.
