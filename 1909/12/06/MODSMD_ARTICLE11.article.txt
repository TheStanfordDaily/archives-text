# Lecture on Stevenson Postponed.
## 
### 
The lecture that was to lie given by
Mrs. K. I). Osborne, in the University
Chapel, to-morrow evening at 8
o'clock, upon the subject of "Steven
son the Explorer," has been postponed.
The speaker lias consented, however,
to give the lecture some time during
the early part ol' the coining semester.
The date will be announced in the
near future.
