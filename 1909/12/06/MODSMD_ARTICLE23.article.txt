# CAP AND GOWN HOLDS ELECTION.
## Miss H. Green Is Named as President of Senior Women's Society. New Members Elected. 
### 
At a meeting of Cap and Gown last
Friday evening Miss H. Green, '10,
was elected president to take the place
of Miss A. Shinn, '09. Five new mem
bers were also elected to the organi
zation. These include the Misses F.
P. Metzner, '10, F. C. Burrell, '10,
Z. L. Watkins. '10, M. B. Barlow, '10,
and M. L. Ozier, '10.
Miss Metzner has been prominent,
in dramatics and successfully man
aged the "Chimes of Normandy." She
is a member of Pi Beta Phi, the Schu
bert Club and the Executive Board'
of the Y. W. C. A.
Miss Burrell has taken an active
part in women's athletics and was cap
tain of the tennis team both this year
and last. She is president of the
Tennis Club and senior member of the
Athletic Association. She has played
in intercollegiate as well as class
tennis. She is a member of Kappa
Alpha Theta.
Miss Watkins is president of Roble
Club and has managed successfully
the finances of the Y. W. C. A.
Miss Maybelle Barlow is a member
of the cabinet of the Y. W. C. A.
Among many committees of which she
has been a member are the Junior
Prom and Sophomore Cotillion Com
mittees. She has taken part in several
operas and is a member of Kappa
Alpha Theta and the Schubert Club.
Miss Ozier belongs to Kappa Alpha
Theta and has been prominent in
Stanford dramatics for the past three
semesters. She is a member of the
Maskers, Schubert Club and is secre
tary of the Senior Class.
