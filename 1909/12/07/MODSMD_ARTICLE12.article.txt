# College Notes.
## 
### 
Twenty-five men turned out for
basketball at Washington University.
An effort is now being made to have
it reinstated as a University sport.
The University of Pittsburg is to
be supplied with coal dug by under
graduates in the School of Mines. A
thick vein of coal underlies the Uni
versity grounds, to which a shaft will
be sunk from the school yard. The
Pittsburg Coal Company has agreed
to sink the shaft and to make all
preparations for the work.
