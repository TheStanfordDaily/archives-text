# Dr. Jordan Contributes to Magazine.
## 
### 
In the December issue of "The
American College," a new publication
now in its third number, President
Jordan contributes a very interesting
article on "The Fraternity Chapter
House." The new publication seeks
to cover the field of American colleges
and besides special articles by rec
ognized authorities on various phases
of university life, it contains a com
prehensive resume of the athletic, aca
demic and social life of the American
colleges.
