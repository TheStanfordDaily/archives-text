# Track Training During Holidays.
## 
### 
As a number of students will re
main here during the holidays "Dad"
Moulton will he at. the track every
morning from 10 to IL' to assist any
men who wish to work out. This Is
an excellent opportunity for those re
maining here to avail themselves of
Dad's knowledge of the fine points of
the game, as he will he able to give
them his undivided attention.
