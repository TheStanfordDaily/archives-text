# Maskers Elect New Officers.
## 
### 
The final business session of the
semester was held yesterday after
ternoon by the Maskers when the
business of the present semester was
completed and officers for the spring
semester selected. Those chosen
were: president, Miss M. Wilson, '11;
vice-president, Miss E. L. Bucking
ham. '10; secretary-treasurer, Miss
E. Porter, '11.
