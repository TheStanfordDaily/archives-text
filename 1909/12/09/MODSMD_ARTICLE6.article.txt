# Miss Buckingham Will Lead Club.
## 
### 
At a business meeting of the Eng
lish Club held before its regular open
meeting last evening, the following
offices were elected for the coming
semester: President, .Miss E. L. Buck
ingham. '10; Vice-president, B. O.
Bliven. '11; Secretary, iMiss AT. L.
Horton, '11; Treasurer. E. J. Hop
kins, '11; Faculty Member of Board,
Mrs. T. P. Russell.
At the open meeting of the Club,
Professor L. E. Bassett gave two in
teresting readings from Dickens, one
from "Nicholas Nickleby" and one
from "A Christmas Carol."
