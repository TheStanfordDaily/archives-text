# German Club Meets for Last Time.
## 
### 
At its last meeting of the semester
held last night, the German Club de
cided to present some German play
after Christmas and also to study
contemporaneous Teutonic writers.
Officers for the coming semester were
elected as follows: Miss F. Hauswirtn,
'10, president; C. C. Wheaton, '11, vice
president; Miss E. Hoff, secretary
treasurer.
