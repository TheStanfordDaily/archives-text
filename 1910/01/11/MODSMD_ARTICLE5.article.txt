# Regret Dismissal of Pinchot.
## 
### 
With the purpose of expressing
regret at the departure from public
service of former Chief Forester, Gil
ford Pinchot. a mass meeting will be
held at Palo Alto, Thursday January
13. Men familiar with Mr. Pinchot's
work will speak, including Professor
W*. R. Dudley.
The meeting has been called by the
Palo Alto League of Justice, and will
convene at 8 p. m.
