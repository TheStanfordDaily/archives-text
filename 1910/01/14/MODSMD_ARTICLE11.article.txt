# Professors Praise Pinchot.
## 
### 
The Pinchot meeting held last even
ing in Palo Alto under the auspices
of the Citizens League of Justice was
largely attended. Among the speak
ers were Professors C. D. Marx, G. H.
Marx, Frank Angell, Jefferson El
more, S. S. Seward, Jr., it. L. Green,
and W. R. Dudley.
After the speaking a resolution was
drawn up praising Pinchot. A copy of
the resolution was telegraphed to ex-
Chief Forester Pinchot, and a copy
mailed to President. Taft.
