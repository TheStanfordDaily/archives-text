# English Club Executive Board Meets.
## 
### 
The executive board of the English
Club met Thursday and outlined its
policy for the semester. The chief
matter in hand for the uext month
will be the publishing of a second edi
tion of "The First Year at Stanford"
with new illustrations. The number
of open meetings is to be limited this
term and closed meetings, devoted to
the discussion original work by the
members are to be held more fre
quently. E. J. Hopkins, '11, and G. F.
Morgan, '10, were appointed to take
charge of this department of original
work.
