# Will Make Election Unanimous.
## 
### 
President W. M. Wyman of the Jun
ior Class has announced that if no
nominations for any offices in the
Junior Class are submitted to the
Daily Palo Alto for publication by to
morrow afternoon, he will instruct the
secretary of the'class to cast a unani
mous vote in favor of the present nom
inees. This will do away with the
necessity of a Quad election and will
facilitate matters considerably. All
nominations must be submitted to the
Daily Palo Alto before four o'clock
tomorrow afternoon.
