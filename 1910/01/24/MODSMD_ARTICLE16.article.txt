# Henry Is President of Tennis Club.
## 
### 
After the track rally on Friday
night a of the Tennis Club
was held at the Delta Kappa Epsilon
house. An election of officers took
place, resulting in the installation of
T. C. Henry, '10, and J. A. Langfield,
'11, in the offices of president and
vice-president, respectively. The fol
lowing new men were elected to
membership: 10. C. Templeton, '10. W.
A. Boyle, '11, L. E. McDougal, '11, W.
E. Collis, '11, A. C. Talboy, '11, La
Grange, '11, W. F. Geary, '12, M.
Fogel, '12, G. L. Shaul Jr., '13, R. L.
Murray, '13, D. Fyfe, '13. H. L. Loud,
'13. The first singles try-out for the
intercollegiate tennis team will be
held next Saturday, Jan. 2!). the weath
er permitting.
