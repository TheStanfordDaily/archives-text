# Measles Cause Quarantine.
## 
### 
When R. R. Rowatt, '12. of Long
Beach, was attacked with the measles
last Saturday night, the new ruling of
the Guild Hoard was put into effect for
the first time, and 19 Lasuen St., the
home of the patient, was quarantined.
Hut few of the residents were within
when the quarantine was declared,
and. as a result, a dozen or more un
dergraduates are now roaming around
the campus homeless, eagerly hop
ing for Rowatt's quick recovery and.
incidentally, for access to their trunks
