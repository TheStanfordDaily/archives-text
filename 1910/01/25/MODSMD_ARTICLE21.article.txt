# California Notes.
## 
### 
Forty-three men have signed up for
baseball at California.
Efforts are being put forth to effect
a close relationship between the Aca
demic colleges at Berkeley and the
Affiliated Colleges at California.
The mandolin club at California
is making plans for a trip to Chicago
and side trips to colleges of the mid
dle west during the summer vacation.
The library at the University of Cal
ifornia is in receipt of a large con
signment, of books from France, in
cluding miscellaneous works for the
university farm and the department
of viticulture.
