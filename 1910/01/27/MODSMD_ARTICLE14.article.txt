# Shelton Is Euphronia President.
## 
### 
,T. E. Shelton, '10, was yesterday
elected president of Euphronia. He is
a member of Phi Alpha Tan debating
society and won last year's Sarnot
medal from California. E. J. Hopkins,
'11, was chosen vice-president of the
society. Hopkins is also a member of
Phi Alpha Tail and is the alternate on
this year's Carnot team. L. D. Sum
merlield, '12, will hold the joint of
fice of secretary and treasurer.
