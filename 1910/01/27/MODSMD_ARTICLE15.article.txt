# Cosmopolitan Club Hears Lecture.
## 
### 
Eighteen members of the Cosmo
politan Club listened to an address
last night 011 the subject of "The Na
tional Movement in India," delivered
by S. M. Rose, '12, a native of Cal
cutta. The speaker told of the general
dissatisfaction with the English gov
ernment that is prevalent throughout
India and of the coming struggle that
will some day make that country
again independent.
