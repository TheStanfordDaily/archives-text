Palo Alto Freshmen—Will meet Sat
urday at 7:30 at 431 University Ave.
Motility I.—First excursion next Sat
urday, leaving at 9 a.m. and returning
at noon.
Daily Palo Alto Stall'.—"Quad" pic
ture postponed until Sunday, February
G, at 10:30.
Gym. Club—Will meet at Encina
gymnasium Saturday at 1:30 p.m. for
"Quad" picture.
At II ome,—Kappa Alpha Theta will
be at home Saturday evening, January
the twenty-ninth.
Eiiplironia—Will meet at Davey's,
Sunday morning. January 30, at 10:30,
for Quad picture.
Quad Pictures. —All Juniors must
have pictures taken by February 1 at
the latest or they will not be given
places in book.
Tennis Picture.-—All members of the
Tennis Club will be at the Encina
courts at 9:30 Sunday morning, for
tlie 1910 Quad picture.
Stanford Trampers.—The Stanford
trampers will resume their walks as
soon as the weather clears and the
roads are in a better condition.
Cniversity Conference—Will meet
at Franklin's Monday afternoon, at
1:15 for "Quad" picture.
S. A. Lindauer, '10.
Educational Club.—The men in the
Educational department will meet at
Franklin's on the Circle at 11:00 a.m.,
Sunday, Jan. 30, for "Quad" picture.
English Compositions.—Last semes
ter's themes will be held in my office
until February 4. after which time
those not. called for will be destroyed.
S. S. Seward. Jr.
Basketball. —There will be a regular
practice of the basketball squad on
Saturday morning at 10 o'clock on the
Roble court. This practice will take
the place of the one missed last Mon
day afternoon.
N'estoria—Will meet Tuesday even
ing, February 1. Question for debate:
"Resolved, That the mayor of the
French Commune should be given the
final control of local police power."
Affirmative, Holcomb, Morgan, Tin
cher; negative, Hilton, Miller, Malone.
Candidates for Graduation. —All can
didates are requested to file an ap
plication in the Registrar's office be
ore February 10 for graduation in May
1910. Blanks for this purpose may be
secured at the Registrar's office.
Locker Keys.—Students holding
keys to lockers in Encina Gymnasium
and who are not taking work during
the present semester, should return
keys to the office not later than Feb.
Ist. R. R. Long.
