# Max Farrand Champions Rugby.
## 
### 
Professor Max Farrand, formerly
head of the department of History and
now a Professor of American History
at Yale, has been taking an active in
terest in the recent, troubles concern
ing intercollegiate football. Professor
Farrand was at Stanford during the
first two years of Rugby, and he has
contributed several articles to various
magazines in the East, exploiting the
good points of the English game as
opposed to the American.
