# Dr. Stone To Speak Before Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
At the regular weekly meeting of the
Young Women's Christian Associa
tion to he held in (he chapel, Tuesday,
February 1, the Rev. Frank Stone will
speak. Mr. Stone has for years been
connected with the Seamen's Home in
San Francisco and he will speak con
cerning that work. There will also be
special music, as Miss L. Bell, '10, will
sing selections from Stephenson's
songs.
