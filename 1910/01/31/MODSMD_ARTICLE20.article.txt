# Woman's Department Enlarged.
## 
### 
The Women's Organizations are to
occupy quite a space in the 1911
"Quad." The Presidents have been re
quested to write a history of their re
spective organizations for the past
year. There will be pictures of the
various societies, such as The Mas
quers, the Executive Board of the
Woman's League, and others.
