# Set, Final Date For "Quad" Pictures.
## 
### 
February 15 has been set as the
final date for all Organization and
Honor Society pictures for the 1911
"Quad" to be taken. Orders for the
picture from which the engraving is to
be made must be left either with the
photographer or sent to F. H. Hilton,
'11, Organization and Honor Society
editor. The membership roll of the
societies must also be submitted im
mediately.
