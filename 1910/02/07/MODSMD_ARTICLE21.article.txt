# Two Chinese Girls to Entertain.
## 
### 
Two Chinese girls will act as singers
and entertainers at the regular meet
iing of the Y. W. C. A. in the Chapel
tomorrow afternoon. These two girls
are from the Presbyterian Chinese
Mission in San Francisco, having been
brought here by Miss Tsabelle Kersell
of the mission who will speak of her
rescue work there. This will undoubt
edly be the most interesting Y. W. C.
A. meeting of the year.
