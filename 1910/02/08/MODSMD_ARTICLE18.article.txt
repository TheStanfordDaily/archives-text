# "Chaparral" Delayed Till Monday.
## 
### 
The next issue of the "Chaparral '
will not he out until next Monday, in
stead of Thursday of this week. The
delay was not due to the staff but to
the illness of the foreman of the Times
Publishing Co.. causing the unavoid
able postponement.
This issue will be a special one in
honor of St. Valentine's Day and will
contain much unusual material.
