That he may the more completely
perform his duties as editor of the
1912 "Quad," J. W. Howell has forced
upon us his resignation as an assis
tant on the staff of the Daily Palo
Alto. With the departure of Mr. How
ell this paper loses the valued services
of a faithful and interested worker.
We envy the "Quad" its gain at our
expense.
