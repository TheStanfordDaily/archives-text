# Will Teach Fancy Dancing.
## 
### 
Miss L. Corella, of Los Angeles, has
been appointed an assistant in the
Roble gymnasium, and her first, work
will be to organize a fancy dancing
class. There will be a class held for
beginners at the gymnasium twice a
week on Tuesday and Thursday even
ings, beginning Feb. 8. An advanced
class for those who have had some
training will be held on Monday and
Wednesday at 2 o'clock. This sec
ion will take up the Spanish and Mexi
can folks dances in which Miss Corella
has had much experience.
