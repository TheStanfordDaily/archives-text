Daily Palo Alto Staff.—Picture at
Franklin's, Saturday morning at 9:30.
French s.—Condition examination
will be given Friday at 1:30.
German Club—Will meet Thursday
evening at 8 o'clock at Madrono Hall.
Spanish Club—Will meet Thursday
evening at 8 o'clock in English Huild
ing.
Woman's Basket Kail Squad—Will
meet at Roble Court, Thursday at
4:15 for picture.
La Liana.—Will meet Saturday at
the home of Mrs. Flugel, 1153 Cowper
St., at 2:45 sharp.
Geology I.—For students condi
tioned in Geology I an examination
will be held on March 12 at 5:25 to

10:25 p.m. in room 334, Geology
building. J. C. Branner.
Senior Play Committee —Will meet
at the Kappa Kappa Gamma house to
morrow afternoon at 1:15.
Bund—Will meet In room 260 A in
the C. E. buliding at 7:30, Thursday.
All members come. Important.
French Club.—Faculty section will
meet at the home of Prof. R. M. Alden,
427 Embarcadero Road, next Thursday
at 8 o'clock.
English Club—Will hold a business
meeting promptly after Mrs. Os
bourne's lecture, Wednesday evening,
at the Delta Gamma house.
Economics 34, Political Science
Seminar.—ln order to secure credit
for the semester's work thesis topics
must be handed in at once.
Vesper Service—Will be held in the
Vestry of the Memorial Church,
Thursday at 7:30 o'clock. To con
sist of the usual short devotional ser
vice with an address.
Forum.—Regular monthly
will be held at the Faculty Club-house
on February 18 at 8 p.m. Dr. Stillman
will read a paper on "Faculty Legisla
tion and Its Administration."
Junior Prom. —A prize of $5 will be
given to the student submitting a
suitable design for the Junior Prom
dance program. Submit designs to
J. E. Higgins, Kappa Sigma house.
