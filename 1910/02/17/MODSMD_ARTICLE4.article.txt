# Miss Squire Announces Engagement.
## 
### 
Miss M. L. Squire, of Visalia, an
extremely well-known member of the
class of 1907, is engaged to M. M.
Maddox, also of Visalia. Maddox is a
brother of Miss R. E. Maddox, '10, and
Mrs. Carl Ferguson, '09, and a mem
ber of Alpha Delta Phi at California.
Miss Squire is a member of the Wal
den Club and of Cap and Gown.
