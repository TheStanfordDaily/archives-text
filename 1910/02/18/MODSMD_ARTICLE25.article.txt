# Masters Meets Hughes Tomorrow
## 
### 
The final game of the tenth annual
handball tournament will be played
tomorrow morning at 10 o'clock in the
gymnasium. The two men who qualify
for this match are W. H. Masters, '09,
and M. H. Hughes, 'J 3. The winner
will compete with J. H. Wiggins, '10,
for the championship belt during the
coming week.
All men desiring to enter the
doubles tournament should sign up
immediately on the list posted at the
gym.
