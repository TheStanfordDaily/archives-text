The annual Interscholastic meet
will be held on Saturday, April 23, ac
cording to the present plans of the
Board of Governors. Numerous com
munications have already been re
ceived from preparatory schools con
cerning the meet, and from the inter
est evinced it is certain that the com
ing contest will be more thoroughly
representative of all sections of the
Coast than any one hold heretofore.
C. L. Schaupp, '12, has been named
as the successor of C. B. Bradford, '09,
on the Board of Governors. Associat
ed with Schaupp are J. R. Holman, '00.
A. E. Roth, '09, J. R. Hickey, '08, H.
Fitch, '07, W. H. B. Fowler, '05, and
J. F. Lanagan, '00. The officers are
H. L. Horton, '10, president; M. M.
Mitchell, '10, vice-president: R. B.
Wheeler, '11, secretary. Dr. F. Angel!
is the representative of the faclty and
also the treasurer of the association.
