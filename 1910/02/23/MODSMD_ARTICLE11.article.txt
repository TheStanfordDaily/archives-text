# Another Case Of Measles On Campus.
## 
### 
The epidemic of measles has in
vaded another house upon the campus.
Herbert Tharshar attending school in
Palo Alto but residing with Dr. Wil
liams at 2 Lasuen is the latest victim.
Tharshar's sickness is in no way con
nected with the other cases on the
campus, as he is supposed to have
contracted the disease while attend
ing school.
The situation both on the campus
and in Palo A'to is growing much bet
ter. According to the City Health
officer, no cases have been reported in
Palo Alto for some time, while a fur
ther spread of the disease on the
campus is not expected.
