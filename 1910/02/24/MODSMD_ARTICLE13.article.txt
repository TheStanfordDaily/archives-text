# PLAY NEARING COMPLETION.
## Matches In Third Varsity Tennis Tournament Being Rapidly Decided. 
### 
Play in the second singles try-out
tournament, which is being held on
the Encina courts, is rapidly drawing
to a close. Weather permitting, the
final matches will be played Saturday
so that the third tournament can com
menced Monday.

follows:

defeated Oltman, '10, 4-6, 6-3, 6-4;
