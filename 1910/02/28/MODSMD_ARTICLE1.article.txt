# LIST IS INCREASING
## NEW MEMBERSHIPS IN UNION SECURED SINCE FRIDAY. Many First Payments Upon Subscriptions To Be Made At Early Date By Prospective Members. 
### 
Five additional life memberships
have been subscribed in Stanford
Union since the phenomenal subscrip
tion at the last Assembly. This brings
the total number of student member
ships for life up to 283, while there
are some who have signified their de
sire to add their names to the list of
subscribers, but who were unable to
do so at the meeting last Friday morn
ing.
By March 1 $230 will be paid in to
the Union as the total of the first
payments that are to be made within
the next few weeks, while by Septem
ber 20, 1910, first payments alone,
without computing the additional pay
ments that are to be made every
month, will amount to $2420.
