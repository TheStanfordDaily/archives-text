# Mann To Meet Murray In Finals.
## 
### 
H. Mann, '10, and R. T>. Murray, 'IS,
will meet in the finals of the second
Varsity tennis try-outs on the Encina
courts next Tuesday to determine who
shall play in the final tournament
which will decide the personnel of the
team.
The semi-finals resulted as follows:
Mann defeated Jordan, 3-6, 6-4, 6-4;
Murray defeated Talboy, 6-3, 6-3.
