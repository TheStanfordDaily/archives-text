# Dr. Patchall Giving Bible Course.
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Y. M. C.
A., Dr. W. T. Patchall, of San Jose, is
now engaged in giving a course in the
study of the Bible. Forty members
are at present in the enrollment, and
any who wish to join should notify
H. M. Tennant at Encina or Secretary
Weiss at the Y. M. C. A. room on the
quad.
