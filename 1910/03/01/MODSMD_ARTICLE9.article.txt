# Electric Road To San Jose Opened.
## 
### 
The new electric railroad to Los
Altos was opened Saturday when one
hundred and twenty-five people made
an excursion to Stanford and Palo
Alto. The line will be open to regu
lar traffic cm March Preparations
are being made to extend the road to
San Mateo, the authorities announcing
that it will be completed within three
months.
