# Soccer Team Will Play Thistles.
## 
### 
After making an excellent showing
against many or' the soccer players
about the bay, the Stanford team has
scheduled a game for next Saturday
against the Thistles, the champions
of the California football league. The
game will be played at 4 o'clock on
the sawdust field. The local team is
to be composed of an eleven picked
from both the first and second elevens.
The line-up has not as yet been an
nounced.
On the following Saturday the Reds
will meet the Reliance Club in San
Francisco, while the Grays will play
the Bobby Burns team on the campus.
