# Professor Martin May Go To Austria.
## 
### 
The granting of a leave of absence
to Prof. L. J. Martin of the department
of Psychology, makes it possible for
that instructress to attend the meet
ing of the "Kongress fur experiment
elle Psychologie" to be held in Inns
bruck. Austria, on April 19. Prof.
Martin has been invited to make a re
port before the congress. Her leave
will be effective during April only.
