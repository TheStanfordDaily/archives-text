# German Club Presents Farce.
## 
### 
"Er Muss Taub Sein." a farce, was
presented by the German Club yes
terday evening under the direction of
Professor Rendtorff. The principal
characters were portrayed by Miss J.
T. Roll, '10, C. C. Wheaton, '11, E. F.
Hollman, '11, and F. Boezinger. '13.
Miss D. M. Stillman, '12, sang.
