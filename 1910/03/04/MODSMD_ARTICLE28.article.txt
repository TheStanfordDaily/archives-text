# Sophomore Class Play Nets $235.
## 
### 
Chairman C. L. Schaupp of the
Sophomore Play Committee read a
formal report in a meeting of the
class this afternoon. The balance
hsows a profit on the play of $235.10.
The sale of seats amounted to $878.90
and the expenses to $643.80, leaving a
total profit on the play of $235.10.
A vote of thanks was given to the
cast, committee, and R. H. Hamilton
in particular for his untiring efforts
in the last week of rehearsals as un
derstudy to H. A. Lewis.
The matter of banqueting the cast
and committee as was done last year
was also brought up for discussion
but nothing definite was decided.
