# Dr. Gardner Entertains Committee.
## 
### 
The members of the Senior Week
committee were entertained last even
ing at the residence of Dr. D. Charles
Gardner. There occurred an informal
discussion of the program for the
Senior Week and the
names of several well-known divines
were mentioned in connection with
the Baccalaureate sermon.
