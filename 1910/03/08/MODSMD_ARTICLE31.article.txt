# Effect Of Requirements On Schools.
## 
### 
"The Effect of the University En
trance Requirements Upon California
High Schools" will be the subject of
an address by J. H. Francis, principal
of the Los Angeles Polytechnic High
School, before the regular monthly
meeting of the Forum at the Club
house on Friday, March 11. at 8
o'clock.
A special meeting will be called
later in the month to hear President
Pritchett of the Carnegie Foundation.
