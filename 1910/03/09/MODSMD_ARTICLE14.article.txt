# Euphronia Adverse to Suffragettes.
## 
### 
The Intercollegiate question, "Re
solved. That the suffrage should be
extended to women," was debated yes
terday evening before Euphronia Lit
erary Society. P. J. Batkin, ']<), M.
M. Fogel, '12, and E. J. Hopkins, '11,
upheld the suffragette side of the ar
gument and were opposed by C. C.
Shoemaker, '10, K. C. Leib, '13, and N.
C. Wilson. '12, who as the negative
debaters, won their case. W. C. Shel
ton, '08, acted as critic.
