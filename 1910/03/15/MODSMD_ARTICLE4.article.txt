# Intercollegiate Game Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The second of the three-game series
of Intercollegiate Rasketball contests
will be decided on the Roble courts
tomorrow afternoon at 4 o'clock. Cal
ifornia annexed the first game last
Saturday, but the Stanford team is
determined to counterbalance that
defeat by capturing a victory on Wed
nesday. All the women of the Univer
sity are expected to be in attendance.
