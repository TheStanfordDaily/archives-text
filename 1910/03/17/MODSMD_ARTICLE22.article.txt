# Triangular Plot Favored.
## 
### 
The triangular plot of ground be
tween the gymnasium and Alvarado
Row was the location most favored by
the subscribers to the Stanford Union
Fund for the location of the Clubhouse
at their meeting this afternoon. Such
an expression of opinion will be for
warded to the Board of Trustees of the
Union for their consideration.
The following names were added to
those subscribing life memberships: A.
it. Cox, 08. W. C. Dalzell, '11, R. O.
Hollensteiner, '11, .7. O. Oltmans, '10,
E. N, Adams, '11, R. F. Morris, '09.
