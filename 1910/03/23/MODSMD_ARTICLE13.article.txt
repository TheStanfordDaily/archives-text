# Graduates Form Association.
## 
### 
The women graduates of Stanford
residing in Palo Alto yesterday formed
an Alumnae Association and elected
the following officers: president, Alios
M. F. Stevens, '01; vice-president, Mist?
M. Lockey, '02; secretary-treasurer,
Miss L. Culver, '96; directors, Mrs. E.
Rendtorff, '96, Miss M. llyde, '01, Miss
S. M. Cramer, '04, Miss E. Mirrieless,
'06. Mrs. E. Allan spoke about the
proposed women's club-house and the
rally soon to be held to raise the nec
essary funds.
