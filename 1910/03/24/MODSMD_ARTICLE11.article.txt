# Announcements.
## 
### 
Senior Prom Program.—A prize of
$5 is offered for the best design for a

Senior Prom program. Submit de
signs before April 5 to L. H. Cochran
at the Phi Delta Theta house.
Sophomore Women.—Dance will be
held at Roble on April 15.
Senior Men. —Singing at the lake
Sunday afternoon at 4:30.
Geology, Mining, Metallurgy Stu
dents—Will meet in room 320, Friday
at 1:15.
Guild Hills.—All outstanding Guild
Hospital bills must be paid before
April 4. F. K. Pomeroy, Treas.
Art Exhibition—Blashfield's work
will be on exhibition at the Old Studio
every afternoon this week from 2 to 5.
Eiiphronin—Will meet Tuesday
April 5. Question for debate: Re
solved, That vivisection should lie pro
hibited by law. Affirmative, French,
Patterson; negative, A. Hill, T. Levy.
\estoria—Will meet Tuesday, April
5. Question for debate: Resolved.
That Intercollegiate athletics between
Stanford and California should be
abolished. Affirmative, Malone, Gore;
negative, Albright, Person. Dramatic
selection by Hilton and Eliel.
Teachers.—All students planning to
teach next year who have not already
done so should register with the Com
mittee on Recommendation of Teach
ers without delay. Blanks may be ob
tained from the Appointment Secre
tary at the Registrar's office at any
time. All registration should be in be
fore the spring vacation.
