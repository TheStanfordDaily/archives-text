# Will Read "Enoch Arden."
## 
### 
Tennyson's "Enoch Arden" will be
read with Strauss' musical accompani
ment at the residence of Mrs. Joshua
Fowler, 221 Kingsley Avenue, Palo
Alto, on the evening of March 28 at S
o'clock. The music will be played by
Mrs. F. C. Woodward; the text read by
Mr. A. T. Murray.
Tickets will be fifty cents, and may
be obtained at the Stanford Pharmacy
and at the University Bookstore. The
proceeds are to be devoted to the
building fund of All Saints Church.
