# Pres. Pritchett To Address Forum.
## 
### 
This evening at the Faculty Club
house President Pritchett, of the Car
negie Foundation, will address a spec
ial meeting of the Forum and will
use as a subject some phase of that
Foundation for the Advancement of
Teaching. President Pritchett, being
the head of this movement for the
advancement of teaching, will be well
versed in the subject and should give
a very interesting lecture.
