ultimatum Stanford in no way ad
mitted herself wrong in contending
for the six-year eligibility rule. The
Cardinal Student Body merely adopted
that course, which to the majority
seemed more expeditious than the can
cellation of Intercollegiate athletics.

divided upon the issue, it is for the
entire undergraduate body to sanction
it as a unit. A certain speaker stated
that we were defeated in the coming
events as soon as we conceded. Every
man and woman, working together,
can prove him wrong.
