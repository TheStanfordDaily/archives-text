# Important Speakers May Be Hoard.
## 
### 
Many important speakers from the
Capitola Conference will be at the reg
ular meeting of the Young Women's
Christian Association on Tuesday in
the Chapel at 4:30. Among them will
be Miss Caroline McDonald, who has
spent much time in Japan; Miss Ruth
Paxson, of the student volunteer move
ment; Miss Patch, the head of the
American-British Association in Paris;
and Miss Tye Creery, the state secre
tary.
