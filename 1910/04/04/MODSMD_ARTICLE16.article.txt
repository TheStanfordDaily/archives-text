# ASSEMBLY ON THURSDAY.
## Harris Weinstock of Sacramento Will Speak During the 11:15 Period on April 7. 
### 
The April Assembly, according to
Professor 13. E. Howard, will be held
on Thursday during the 11:15 period,
and Harris Weinstock of Sacramento
will be the speaker.
Just what Mr. Weinstock will speak
upon is not known at this writing by
the committee in charge. The speaker
during the last few years has travelled
extensively throughout the United
States, Europe, Asia, and recently cir
cumnavigated the globe. Professor
Howard expects that ihe topic upon
which Weinstock will speak can be
announced tomorrow.
