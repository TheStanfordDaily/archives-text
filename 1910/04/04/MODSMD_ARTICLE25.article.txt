# May Play in Oakland.
## 
### 
Graduate Manager Burbank state 1
this afternoon that on account of some
difficulty at St. Mary's college, the base
ball game which was to have been
played here Wednesday afternoon may
be played in Oakland. Burbank is in
communication with the St. Mary's
authorities and expects to receive defi
nite word from them by tomorrow.
