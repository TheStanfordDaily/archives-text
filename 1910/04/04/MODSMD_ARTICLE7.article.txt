# Dr. Jordan Will Talk on Agassiz.
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will give his postponed
lecture on Lonis Agassiz tomorrow
evening at. 8 o'clock In the Chapel.
This will formally conclude the series
of lectures under the direction of the
Botany department. Dr. Jordan
studied for a time under Agasslz, and
is particularly fitted to talk about him.
