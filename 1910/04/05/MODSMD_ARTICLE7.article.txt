# Announcements.
## 
### 
Schubert Club—Will not meet this
week.
Sophomore Women.—Dance will he
held at Roble on April 15.
M. E. Society—Will meet in room
500, Tuesday evening at 7:30.
Sophomores—Will meet Wednesday
at 1:10 in room 214. Important.
Glee Club—Will meet at Theat Delta
Chi house tomorrow evening at 7:30.
Economics 34—Will meet Monday at
1:30 in Economics Seminary room.
Fancy Dancing.—Class at Roble
Gymnasium will not meet Wednesday
night.
Associate Civil Engineers—Will
meet in Professor Hoskin's Hydraulic
room at 1:10 Wednesday.
Geology and Mining—All students in
this department will meet in room
320 on Wednesday at 1:15.
German Club—Will meet Thursday
evening, April 7, at the home of Pro
fessor Griffin, 14 Alvarado.
La Liana—Will meet Saturday at
the house of Mrs. Newcome, Forest
Court, at 3 o'clock. Important!
Socinl Service Club—Party going to
Seamen's Institute San Francisco, will
take 4:3S train Wednesday afternoon.
Senior Reception Committee ami
Alnninao Reception Committee—Will
meet Wednesday evening at 8 o'clock
at the Kappa Kappa Gamma house.
Tennis.—Women wishing to try for
the second place in the tennis tourna
ment must hand their names to Miss
F. Burrell, '10, before Wednesday.
Women's League—Will moot on
Wednesday, April 0. at 4:30, in the
Chapel to discuss the Women's Cub
house. A full attendance is urgently
requested.
Forestry Club —Will meet Thursday
evening at the corner of University

ave. and Guinda street. Prof. Abrams
will speak on "Distribution of Plants
in Southern California."
