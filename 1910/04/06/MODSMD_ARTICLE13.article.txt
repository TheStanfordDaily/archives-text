# Weather Prevents Smoker.
## 
### 
The committee in charge of the Sen
ior Smoker has announced that as
soon as the weather promises to be
favorable, an open-air smoker will
be held on the banks of San Frans
quito Creek. Preparations have long
been under way, and if the weather
had seemed pleasant the gathering
would have been held this week.
