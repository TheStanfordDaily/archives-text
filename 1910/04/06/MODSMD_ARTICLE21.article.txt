# Eight Men Voted Numerals.
## 
### 
The Class of 1912 this afternoon
voted sweaters to thirteen men. They
are as follows:
Crew—J. W. Howell, H. L. Dietterle,
C. L. Schaupp, R. H. Seward, and J. F.
Partirdge. Track—J. S. Brown, T. S.
Partirdge. Track—J. S. Brown, W. E.
Gatewood and B. Wallace.
