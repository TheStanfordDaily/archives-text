# Sunday's Preacher an Eminent Author.
## 
### 
The preacher at the Chapel service
at 11 o'clock on Sunday is not only an
eminent preacher, but also a distin
guished author. His books on "Friend
ship," "Work," "The Dream of Youth,"
and "Culture and Restraint," are wide
ly read, not only in Scotland, but
throughout the United States.
