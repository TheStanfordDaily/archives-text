# Murray's Case Dismissed.
## 
### 
In the Justice Court of Palo Alto
yesterday afternoon the case of the
City of Palo Alto vs Alfred C. Murray,
'12, was dismissed upon the recom
mendation of District Attorney J. P.
Sex because of insufficient evidence.
This is the case brought to trial upon
the complaint of Health Officer Box
meyer of Palo Alto who swore to a
complaint that Murray, a Stanford
Sophomore, during the measles epi
demic broke quarantine by going to
his home in Woodland when he was
under the surveillance of the health
officers.
The case was tried before Judge S.
YV. Charles of Palo Alto and the de
fendant had as counsel Chas. W.
Thomas, Jr., '06.
