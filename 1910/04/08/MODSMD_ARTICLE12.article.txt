# Winning Crew to go North.
## 
### 
The University of Washington Var
sity crew has challenged the winner of
the Stanford-California Club race to
morrow to a dual regatta at Seattle in
May. Word to this effect was received
today by the Lagunita Boat Club. The
Club had arrangements completed by
which it would row against Washing
ton on May 23, the size of the money
guarantee being the only drawback.
Washington offered $200, while Stan
ford asked for twice that amount,
deeming it necessary. The note re
ceived today offers the winning crew
a $400 guarantee.
