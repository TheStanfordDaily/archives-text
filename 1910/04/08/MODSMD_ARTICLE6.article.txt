# Hails to Forsake Bachelordom.
## 
### 
C. H. Hails, '10, of Santa Barbara,
has become engaged to Miss Mattie
Osborne, a Stanford graduate, also of
Santa Barbara, and the wedding has
been set for the early summer. Hails
left college at Christmas to engage in
business in Los Angeles. Last semes
ter he was editor of the Daily Palo
Alto. Hails was a member of Phi
Delta Theta, Phi Delta Phi, the Quad
rangle Club, the Press Club, and Skull
and Snakes. He was also an associate
on the "Sequoia" staff, a member of
the Executive Committee, the Univer
sity Conference and the Student Ad
visory Committee.
