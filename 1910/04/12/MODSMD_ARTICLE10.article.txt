Press Club—Will meet usual time
and place.
Northwestern Club—Will not meet
Friday.
Junior Class—Will meet Wednesday
afternoon at 1:10, in room 50.
Freshman Class—Will meet Wednes
day at 1:15 in room 68. Important.
Glee Club—Will met at Theta Delta
Chi house at 7:30 Tuesday evening.
Sophomores—Meeting scheduled for
Wednesday night has been postponed.
Schubert Club—Will meet at the
Gamma Phi Beta house Wednesday at
4:30.
Chess.—All members of the Chess
team are asked to meet tomorrow

afternoon, Wednesday, at 4:30 in room
107 Enclna, to elect captain. Those
who cannot be present should send
in their proxy before that time.
Glee Club —Will meet tonight at the
Theta Delta Chi house at 7:30. Im
portant.
Stanford Branch A. I. E. E.—Will
meet Tuesday evening at 7:30 in room
144 Encina.
Skull and Snakes—Will meet Tues
day evening at 9 o'clock at Kappa
Sigma House.
Senior Committees. —Chairmen must
have prospective expense accounts
ready at once.
Senior Play Committee —Will meet
Wednesday at 2 o'clock at the Kappa
Kappa Gamma house.
Spanish Club—Will not meet next
Thursday evening. Notice of next
meeting announced later.
Senior Hall Committee—Will meet at
Delta Gamma house immediately after
track rally Wednesday evening.
Y. W. C. A.—There will be no meet
ing of the Y. W. C. A. this week on ac
count of the President's conference.
Old Studio.—An exhibition of the
works of Professor R. Harshe of the
Art department will be held daily from
2to 5 p.m. Admission free.
Interfraternity Baseball—Will meet
tomorrow evening at Phi Delta Theta
house immediately after track rally to
make schedule.
Fencing and Boxing.—Entries for
the annual fencing and boxing tourna
ments must be handed in before April
16. This competition is open to all
students.
Stanford Field—ls closed for the
season to games of all kinds in order
to prepare the turf for next season.
F. Angell,
Chairman Board of Control.

is justifiable." Affirmative, F. Hill,

Levy.
