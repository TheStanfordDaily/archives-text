# Nimmo Elected to Membership.
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Stanford
Branch of A. I. E. E., L. R. Nimmo, '11,
was elected to membership. Tapers
were also read by C. K. Ransome, '10,
and R. P. Foote, '10. P. Johnson, '09.
was elected manager and E. C. Wood
cock, '11, captain of the Society's
baseball team.
