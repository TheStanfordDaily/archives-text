# ASSEMBLY NEXT WEDNESDAY.
## President Jordan Will Speak at 9:15 on "Taking Polities Out of Politics." 
### 
President Jordan will be the speak
er of the last two assemblies of the
year which take place on April 20, and
May 2, respectively. His topic on tlio
first instance will be "Taking Politics
Out of Politics," while on the latter
date he will expound his views on the
race question by delivering his famous
address, "The Human Harvest."
Dr. Jordan's views in regard to the
present political conditions in this
country should be of great interest to
the students of the University, as his
recent remarks in regard to the In
surgent faction in Congress and
municipal problems have caused much
favorable comment by the press of
the country.
