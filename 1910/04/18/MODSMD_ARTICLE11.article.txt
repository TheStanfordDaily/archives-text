# University to Publish Papers.
## 
### 
On the recommendation of the Com
mittee on Publications, the University
Is now publishing a paper by Dr. John
P. Mitchell of the Chemistry depart
ment, on the "Portable Water of the
San Francisco Peninsula," and another
by Frank W. Weymouth of the Physi
ology department, on the "Crabs of
Monterey Bay." A third paper to be
published shortly is by Professor G.
H. Henipl of the German department
on the Venetic dialect.
