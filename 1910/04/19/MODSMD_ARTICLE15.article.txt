# Announcements.
## 
### 
Daily Palo Alto—Will meet Tuesday
at 9 o'clock.
German Club—Will meet Wednesday
at 5 o'clock at Roble Hall.
Koble Gymnasium.—There will be a
make up class Thursday, at 4:35.
All Guild bills—Must be paid by
April 25. F. K. Pomeroy.
Schubert Club —Will not meet at the
Pi Beta Phi house, Wednesday at 4:30.
Y. Iff. C. A. Bible Study.—Dr. Patch
all's class will meet on April 19 and 26.
La Liana—Will meet Wednesday at
12:15 in room 202 for the election of
officers.
Executive Hoard of Woman's League.
—Will meet in Mrs. Allan's office Wed
nesday, at 1:30.
Quadrangle Club—Will meet Wed
nesday afternoon at 1:10 at the D.K.E.
house. Important.
Freshman Executive Committee—
Will hold important meeting Wednes
day night at 7:30 o'clock.
University Conference.—Those who
ordered pictures of group please get
them at once at 153 Encina.
C. E. Society—Will meet at the Kap
pa Sigma house, Wednesday evening.
Professor C. D. Marx will address the
society.
Womans* Suffrage Club—Will meet
Wednesday, at 4:30 in Roble Hall.
Officers will be elected, and all mem
bers are requested to be present.
Smoker.—The professors and stu
dents of the Geology and Mining and
Metallurgy departments are invited to
a smoker at the Breaker's Club, Wed
nesday evening, April 27, at 7:30.
(Guild*—All outstanding,
hospital bills must be paid on of before
May 1. Those unable to do so should
see the Treasurer at once.
P. K. Pomeroy.
To Whom it may Concern—lt is re
quested that the parties who have been
borrowing Orchestra music from the
Chapel discontinue the practice. If it
is continued, immediate steps will be
taken for its prevention.
John H. Paine, Mgr.
