# Large Attendance at Meet.

here last Saturday afternoon. This
means, of course, that the receipts
were $34,000, part of which will go to
Stanford and part to California. Cer
tain expenses will have to he met so
the amount coming to each univer
sity has not been definitely determined
at the present time.
