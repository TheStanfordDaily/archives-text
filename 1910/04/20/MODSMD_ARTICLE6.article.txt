# Track Picture On Friday.
## 
### 
Captain Scott has arranged with
Franklin to have pictures of the track
team taken on Friday afternoon. The
men are to meet in the studio in Palo
Alto at 1:15, and after being photo
graphed will, it is understood, elect
the man who will lead the team next
year. It is requested that the men on
the team all be prompt.
