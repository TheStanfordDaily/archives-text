# La 						Liana Elects Officers.
## 
### 
At a business meeting of La Liana
Wednesday a new constitution was
adopted and the following officers
elected for next semester: President,
Miss M. M. MacLachlan, '12; vice-presi
dent, Mrs. A. A. Newcomer; secretary,
Miss H. Flugel, '13; treasurer, Miss K
Wiley, '12; advisory board, Miss D.
Marx, '11, Miss D. Alderton, '12, and
Miss M. Everett, '13.
