# Dr. Wullner on Friday.
## 
### 
Seats continue on sale at the Stan
ford Pharmacy and the University
Bookstore for the concert which the
famous Dr. Ludwig Wullner is sched
uled to give in the Assembly Hall on
next. Friday evening. The prices range
from $1.50-down to fifty cents. Dr.
Wullner's. fame needs no advertising
and a large house will doubtless
greet him at Stanford.
