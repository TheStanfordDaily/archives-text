# WILL ELECT OFFICERS
## STUDENT BODY WILL VOTE ON CANDIDATES TOMORROW. Wheeler and Ditz for President.—Opposition for Other Offices.—Polls Open 11:15 to 2:30. 
### 
On the Quad tomorrow, commencing
at 11:15 and continuing until 2:30, the
annual spring election of Student
Body officers will be held. The most
important contest will be that for
president between R. B. Wheeler, '11,
and G. A. Ditz, '11. J .E. Thompson,
'11, and C. A. Christin, '11, will fight it
out for the secretaryship, while but
one candidate is in the field for each
of the remaining positions, save for
the Junior and Sophomore vacancies
on the Executive Committee. Since
the original nominations were made,
.1. E. Elliott, '11, has appeared in the
field as a 1911 aspirant, making three
nominees for the two positions, while
the addition of C. R. Nunan, '12, places
two names before the undergraduates
for the single place among the Sopho
more representatives. The withdraw
al of C. H. Marvin, '13, leaves E. P.
Kern, '13, to run alone.
The ballot in its entirety will be as
follows:
For President—
R. B. Wheeler, '11
G. A. Ditz, '11
