# Morgan Will Speak on Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
"The Purpose of the Y. M. C. A. at
Stanford" will be the subject of the
address of G. F. Morgan, '10, president
of that organization, at the last regu
lar meeting which is to be held in the
chapel at 7:30 Sunday evening.
In this talk, Morgan will discuss
the ideals of the association, the past
policy and the policy to be followed
next year, and will discuss at some
length the financial standing and the
financial policy for next year. An ef
fort is being made to secure some
special musical numbers. All men of
the university who are interested in
the work of the Y. M. C. A. are invited
to attend.
