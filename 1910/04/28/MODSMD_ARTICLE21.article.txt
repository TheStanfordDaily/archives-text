# DR. CLAMPETT WILL PREACH.
## Well-known San Francisco Divine Consents to Deliver Annual Baccalaureate Sermon. 
### 
At the request of the Senior Week
Committee, the Rev. D. Charles Gard
ner has secured the promise of the
Rev. Frederick W. Clampett, 1). D., to
preach the Baccalaureate sermon at
the services on the Sunday of Com
mencement Week. Dr. Clampett for
a number of years has been the rector
of Trinity Episcopal Church, San
Francisco, and is a forceful and elo
quent speaker. Probably a larger per
centage of men attend his church than
is the case with any other parish in
San Francisco. Dr. Clampett is an
Irishman, hailing from Dublin.
