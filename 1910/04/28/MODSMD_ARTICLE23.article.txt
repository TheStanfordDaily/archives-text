# Essay Prize Again Offered.
## 
### 
The prize of $50.00 which was of
fered last year under the auspices of
the Womens' Christian Temperance
Association to Stanford students for
the best essay on total abstinence from
alcoholic liquors, has been renewed
for this year and the subject chosen is
"The Relation of Individual Total Ab
stinence to the Prosperity of the Na
tion." Last year the competition for
this prize was very keen but so far but
few manuscripts have been received.
Not only will the winning essay re
ceive the $50.00 prize but it will also
be eligible for the national prize of
$100.00, which is awarded to the best
manuscript of those which have al
ready won a local reward.
