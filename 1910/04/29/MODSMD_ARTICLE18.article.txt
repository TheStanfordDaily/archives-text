# Well Known Writer Visits Campus.
## 
### 
Mrs. Inez Haines Gilmore, a well
known magazine writer of the east,
who is now traveling on the Coast,
visited friends on the campus yester
day. Mrs. Gilmore contributes prin
cipally to the "Atlantic Monthly,"
"American," and "Success" magazines.
One of her stories, "Phoebe and the
Household Guards," appeared in the
March "American."
