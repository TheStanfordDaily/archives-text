# CHASE WILL LEAD GLEE CLUB.
## Prominent Musician and Thespian Will Pilot Vocalists During the Coming Year. 
### 
E. E. Chase, '11, of San Jose, was
last night chosen leader of the Varsity
Glee Club for next year, succeeding R.
H. Brown, '10. The other newly elect
ed officers of the Club are as follows:
Manager, R. J. Finnie, '13, of Sacra
mento; executive committee, A. E.
Elirhorn, '10, of San Francisco and N.
Taylor, '10, of Los Angeles. Chase is
a member of Zeta Psi and has been
nuir,ager of the club during the past
year.
