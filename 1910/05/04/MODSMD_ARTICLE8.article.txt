Notice.—All subscriptions are due
and payable.
I>. P. A. Freshman Start'—Will meet
Thursday evening at 9:30 o'clock at
the Delta Kappa Epsilon house.
Sophomores—Meet tomorrow at 1:15
in room 214. Important.
Glee Club—Will meet at Zeta Psi
house, Wednesday night, 7:30.
Gym Club—Will meet Thursday, 7:30
in Encina Gym. Ejection of officers.
G. and M. Society—Will meet at
Geology building, Thursday afternoon
at 4:30.
Faculty Women's Club—Will meet on
Monday, May 9, at 3 o'clock at the
clubhouse.
French Club—Will meet Thursday,
May 5, in Romanic Languages building,
at 4:30. Important.
Sophomore Joint Jolly-up—Will be
held in Armory Hall May 7 at 7:30. All
Sophomores expected.
Freslimen Gathering.—All feshmen,
men and women, inept at Faculty Club
house, Friday at 4:30.
German Club—Will meet Thursday
evening, at the house of Miss H. Flu
gel, 1153 Cowper St., Palo Alto.
A.S.C.E.—WiII meet Thursday even
ing at 8 o'clock at Delta Kappa Epsi
lon house. Election of officers.
Senior Clnss.—The Roll is now at
the Registrar's office. All members of

the class should sign as soon as pos
sible.
Band.—Members will please return
uniforms and other band property to
room 2GO, C. E. building at once.
Philosophical Club — Will meet
Thursday at 4 o'clock in Philosophy
building, paper by F. F. Wolff, '11.
Senior Assessments—s2 due. Make
checks payable to Class 1910. Send
same to B. W. Upson; M. Y. Malone or
H. Rittigstein.
Candidates for Masters Degree.—All
candidates for the Master's Degree
who desire to order the cap, hood and
gown for Commencement must do so
at the Bookstore, before Friday, May
6, 1910.
Philologieal Association. —Postponed
April meeting will be held on Thursday
evening, at 8 o'clock, in room 31. The
papers are by Professor Flugel, (Re
port on the Chaucer Lexicon) and
Professor Hem pie. (Some Venetic In
scriptions.)

and payable.
