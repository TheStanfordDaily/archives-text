# Announcements.
## 
### 
German Club.—Business meeting
Tuesday at 5 o'clock. Important.
Cap and Gown —Will meet Tuesday
night, at 7 o'clock, at the Theta house.
Faculty Women's Club—Will meet on
Monday, May 9, at 3 o'clock at the
clubhouse.
Sophomore Joint Jolly-up—Will be
held in Armory Hall May 7 at 7:30. All
Sophomores expected.
La Liana—Will meet Saturday, May
7 at the home of Mrs. J. E. Matzke,
corner Bryant and Kingsley sts.
Executive Board Women's Athletic
Association —Will meet Monday at 4:30
at Madrona. New board will meet with
old board.
Roble Gymnasium.— All girls who
have no more work in the gymnasium
will please take their suits by Wed
nesday, May 11.
