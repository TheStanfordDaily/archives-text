# Y.W.C.A. Elects Officers.
## 
### 
New officers have been selected by
the Young Women's Christian Asso
ciation to direct the affairs of that or
ganization during the coming semester.
The result of the election was as fol
lows: President, Miss A. Steinbeck;
vice-president, Miss E. Wilkins; treas
urer, Miss S. Oppenheimer; secretary,
Miss F. Wendling; Bible chairman,
Miss U. Peraud; Mission chairman
Miss M. Seeley; extension chairman,
Miss R. Hutchinson; intercollegiate,
Miss H. Campbell; social, Miss A. Han
na; membership, Miss E. Wilkins; de
votional, Miss L. Blanchard.
