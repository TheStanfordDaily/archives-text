# Pettigrew is President.
## 
### 
At a recent meeting of Sword and
Sandals, P. F. Pettigrew, '10, was
elected president, R. Woodville, '11,
stage manager, and R. H. Seward, '12,
secretary-treasurer. Pettigrew hasi
played in a large number of dramatic
productions here, notably "The Chimes
of Normandy" and"The Man From
Home." He is a member of Phi Kappa
Psi.
