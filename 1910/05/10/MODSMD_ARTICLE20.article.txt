# Work During Vacation.
## 
### 
I can use 3 or 4 more good men to
demonstrate Aluminum in good loca
tions this summer. Last year 2254
students averaged a daily profit of
$7.42 so don't apply unless you think
that you are worth as much, or more.
Men, who want a guaranteed salary,
on top of past records like the above,
need not apply. I want men, not
weaklings. Any man can make twice
as much in this work as any salary he
ever received. The fact that 22.Y1 men
were engaged during last vacation
shows how aggreeable and profitable
demonstrating Aluminum must be.
Those already engaged please at
tend demonstrations, afternoons after
3:30 and evenings after 7 o'clock,
Tuesday and Wednesday only at Suite
4, Hotel Mariposa, hours 1 to 9 p.m.,
C. A. RICKS, Dist. Mgr.
