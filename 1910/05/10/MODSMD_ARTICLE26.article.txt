# English Club Reprint On Sale.
## 
### 
The English Club's reprint of the
"First Year at Stanford" went on sale
today at the Bookstore and at Hyde's
in Palo Alto. It has several new
plates, rare pictures of the early days
which have never before been seen in
print. The English Club expects a
large sale on account of their excep
tional offer of the "First Year at Stan
ford" and the "English Club Year
Book," both of which they will sell
for $2.
