# Of Interest to the Graduating Class.
## 
### 
The Baccalaureate Sermon will be
given in the Assembly Hall on Sunday,
May 22, at 11 o'clock. Members of the
class meet at he University Chapel at
10:30 o'clock sharp.
Four tickets will be allowed each
member of the graduating class.
These tickets will admit their guests
or friends to the main floor. The ad
mittances will be obtainable at the
Registrar's office on Thursday and Fri
day, May 19 and 20. They will not be
held, however, after 5 o'clock on the
second day.
All members of the graduating class,
who will receive either first or second
degrees, will meet in the Chapel a:
10 o'clock, sharp, on Monday morning
May 23.
