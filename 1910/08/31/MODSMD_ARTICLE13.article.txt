# Organizations Occupy New Homes.
## 
### 
Stanford Chapter of Delta Chi, which
was established at Stanford on May
19, 1905, is occupying their own house
at 10 Salvatierra, as their first chapter
house this year. This house was occu
pied last semester by the Delta Delta
Delta sorority.
A new house was built this summer
on Lausen adjoining the property of
the Pi Beta Phi sorority on the south.
This new structure is in design typi
cally Californian, and will be the
home of the Delta Delta Delta sorority.
