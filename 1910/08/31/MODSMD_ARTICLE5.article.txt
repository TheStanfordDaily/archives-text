# Saloons Still Open.
## 
### 
Whether the saloons of Menlo Park
will be open another year is up to the
Supreme Court of the state. Last May
the final consideration of the case was
placed before this tribunal and so far
no action has been taken by this body,
and indications are that it will be some
time before a decision will be rendered,
