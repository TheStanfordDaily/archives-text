# WILL ENTERTAIN FRESHMEN.
## Y.M.C.A. Animal Reception For First Year Men Will be Held Saturday Night. 
### 
The Annual Freshman Stag Recep
tion given by the Y. M. C. A. will be
held at Encina Gymnasium on Satur
day evening at 7:30 o'clock. The Y. M.
C. A. hopes that all Faculty men will
be present to meet the members of
the Incoming class. Refreshments in
keeping with a Stanford stag gather
ing will be served during the evening.
The evening's program will begin
with informal talks by Student Advisor
A. E. Roth, Student Body President, G.
A. Ditz, '11, and D. W. Weist, general
secretary of the Y. M. C. A.
Instructor J. K. Bonnell, of the Eng
lish department will tell a story, which
will be followed by a song by Paul
Ward, '12. E. M. Albright, '12, will
give one of his interesting Chalk Talks
upon the Freshman Hat. The Chinese
and Japanese students will appear in
some novel stunts. I. D. Paine, '12,
manager of the Stanford Brass Band,
will lead an instrumental quartet.
The well-known trio, composed of In
structor Maloney, E. P. Bly, '13, and J.
H. Wiggins, '10, will be seen in a series
of athletic stunts. The evening's pro
gram will conclude with songs and
yells led by Yell Leader J. E. Thomp
son, '11.
