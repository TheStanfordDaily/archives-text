# Y. W. C. A. Meets Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon in the Chapel
at 4:30, the first meeting of the Y. W.
C. A. for this semester will be held.
Miss Mary Hutchins, local Secretary
of the Association, will speak. A full
attendance is desired, and Freshmen
especially should avail themselves of
this opportunity.
