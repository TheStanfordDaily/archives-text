# Noted Scientist Will Lecture.
## 
### 
Dr. Edmund B. Huey, Psychologist,
at the State School for the Feeble
Minded at Lincoln, Illinois, will give a
lecture on "Recent Developments in
Clinical Psychology," Tuesday after
noon, September 6, at 4:30 in room 50.
Dr. Huey is a graduate of Clark
University, and was for some time
Professor of Education in the Univer
sity of Pittsburg." He has recently
spent some time in Paris in advanced
study along the lines of his work.
