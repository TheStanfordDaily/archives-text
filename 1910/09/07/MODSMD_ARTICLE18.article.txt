# Band Meets Thursday Night.
## 
### 
P. K. Stark, the leader of the Uni
versity Band, has called a meeting of
this organization for 7:30 o'clock on
Thursday evening, in room 260. All
the old members and every new man
who contemplates trying-out for a
place, are requested to be present
promptly on the appointed hour.
Last year the band was a great ben
efit to the rooting section at all the
games. The leader states, that if the
men will turn out for rehearsals this
fall, as well as they did last year, the
playing at the games will be continued.
