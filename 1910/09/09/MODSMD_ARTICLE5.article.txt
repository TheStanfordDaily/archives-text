# RECEPTION TOMORROW NIGHT.
## Affair to Welcome Freshman Class Will be a General University Event. 
### 
Tomorrow evening the Y. M. C. A.
will give its annual reception to the
Freshmen. Inasmuch as this is the
only big reception of the college year,
it has come to be considered as, and is
announced as, a general University
affair. The men and women students
and the faculty, together with their
friends ,are cordially asked to be pres
ent and become acquainted with the
entering students. The affair is to
begin at 8 o'clock.
Speeches are to be made by Dr. J. C.
Branner, acting president of the Uni
versity, and Dr. D. Charles Gardner.
Music will be furnished by Brohaska's
orchestra.
There will be a reception committee
of forty members to see that everyone
is introduced, this committee consist
ing of twenty men and twenty women
students. Those in charge of the re
ception wish it. to be definitely under
stood that this is not a full dress affair.
The patronesses will be the mem
bers of the Board of Directors of the
Y. W. C. A. and the wives of the mem
bers of the Board of Directors of the
Y. M. C. A.
