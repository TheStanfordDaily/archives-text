# To Play Tennis Matches this Week.
## 
### 
The committee in charge of the first
handicap tennis tournament are en
deavoring to run off the first three
rounds of the tournament this week.
In order to do this, all those having
matches in the first round are re
quested to arrange to play them to
morrow if possible.
The drawings for the preliminary
and the first rounds have been posted
at the Encina courts and all entries can
find their opponents from these lists.
The balls for the matches can be se
cured in room 128, Encina. All those
taking balls are requested to return
them after their match.
