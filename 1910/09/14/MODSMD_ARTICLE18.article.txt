# Juniors to Elect Tomorrow.
## 
### 
The Junior election tomorrow prom
ises to be one of the most hotly con
tested class elections ever held on the
Quad. Two candidates appear on the
ballot for each of the offices of presi
dent, vice-president and treasurer and
the race promises to be a close one.
The fifty-cent assessment must be
paid either before or at the time of
voting. The election will be held at
the usual place between the hours of
11:15 and 2:30.
