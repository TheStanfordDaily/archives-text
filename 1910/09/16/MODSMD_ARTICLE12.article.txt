# Prominent Lecturer Speaks Tomorrow
## 
### 
Professor Bacon, a lecturer of the
American School at Jerusalem, will
give an illustrated lecture upon "Pal
estine Before the Hebrew Conquest,"
in the Memorial Chapel tomorrow' af
ternoon at 1:30 o'clock. The lecture
will be given under the auspices of the
Archaelogical Institute of America
and the general public is cordially
invited to be present.
