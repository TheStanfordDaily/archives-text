# C.E. Society Elects Officers.
## 
### 
R. U. St. John, '11, was elected vice
president of the Civil Engineering
Society, at the meeting held last even
ing, and C. H. Beal, '13, was made
corresponding secretary. These two
places were left vacant by the failure
of J. A. Killian, '11, and A. Taylor, '11,
to return to college. In the future the
society will meet in the Y. M. C. A.
room. It is planned to hold two meet
ings a month. At one of these gather
ings, members of the society will
speak, and at the other lectures will
be given by visiting engineers.
