# S.P. Offers Low Fare.
## 
### 
Students may, upon application to
the Registrar, obtain a certificate
which entitles them to a special rate
between Palo Alto and San Francisco.
This ticket, which is good only from
Friday till Monday costs $1.25. A
special rate, open to the general pub
lic, good from Saturday till Monday,
may be obtained for the same price.
