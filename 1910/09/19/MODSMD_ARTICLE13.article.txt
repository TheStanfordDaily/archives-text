# Plan Staging Play Next Semester.
## 
### 
Plans for a play are being discussed
and contemplated by the English
Club. At the last meeting of that or
ganization, the first of next semester
was selected as the most suitable time
to stage a production. Professor Bas
sett, who was chosen as the faculty
member of the executive board, will
coach the play.
At the meeting, the following offi
cers were chosen: President, Miss H.
Horton, '11; vice-president, Miss H.
Campbell, '11; treasurer; L. Robinson,
'12; secretary, Miss S. Porter, '11.
