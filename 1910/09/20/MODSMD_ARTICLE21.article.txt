# Entertains Noted Entomologists.
## 
### 
Professor Kellogg of the Entomol
ogy department entertained today,
two eminent entomologists, Dr. L. O.
Howard, head of the United States
Bureau of Entomology, and Dr. L. P.
de Bussy, chief biologist of the exper
imental station of the government of
Sumatra.
Dr. L. O. Howard is looking over the
situation in California in regard to
some projected improvements in eco
nomic entomology.
Dr. L. P. de Bussy has been sent by
the government of Sumatra on a
search for special parasites of insects
injurious to tobacco.
