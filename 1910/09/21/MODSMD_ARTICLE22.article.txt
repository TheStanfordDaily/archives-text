# Well Known Artist Will Exhibit.
## 
### 
A. F. Matthews, one of the best
mural artists in the West, has prom
ised to exhibit a few of his paintings
some time during the second semester.
Matthews was at one time the head of
the Hopkins Art Institute. He also
designed and decorated the interior
of the Oakland Public Library and
several other public buildidngs in
Oakland and San Francisco. Although
nothing is known of the work he will
exhibit, it is thought that he will show
some of the pictures that he is work
ing on at the present time.
