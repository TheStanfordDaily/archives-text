# Special Train for Dedication.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Company in
order to accommodate the crowds
which will attend the Dumbarton
Bridge celebration at Newark, Satur
day, will run a special train starting
from San Jose at 8:30 and leaving
Palo Alto at 9:00. On the return trip
the train will leave Newark at 5:00.
The fare for the round trip is seventy
five cents, good for Paio Alto or May
field.
