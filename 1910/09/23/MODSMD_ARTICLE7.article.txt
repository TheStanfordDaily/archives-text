# French Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The election of officers of the French
Club on Thursday resulted in the
choice of Miss M. Bowler, '11, as pres
ident, with Miss S. Durnham, '11, sec
retary and P. Ogilvie, '11, treasurer.
Renewed interest was shown in the
affairs of the club, which aims to es
tablish itself on the footing of last
semester. Details for a social meeting
will shortly be announced, to which
all interested in French are invited.
