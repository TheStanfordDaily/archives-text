# Suffragettes to Hear Speakers
## 
### 
The first meeting of the semester of
the Equal Suffrage League will be held
on Wednesday afternoon at 4:30 in
room GB. General plans for the year
will be discussed. Miss Breckenridge's
course, which she gave at the Summer
Session of the University of California
on "The Public Aspect of the House
hold," wil be outlined.
