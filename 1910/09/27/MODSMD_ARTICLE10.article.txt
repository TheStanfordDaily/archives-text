# Law Smoker on October 14.
## 
### 
Students of the Law School will
hold their annual smoker as has been
the custom for several years past, on
the evening preceding the Freshman
intercollegiate Rugby Game. This
was decided upon today at a meeting
of the Law and Pre-legal majors, and
H. McCowan, Jr., '09, who presided,
appointed W. C. Theile, 'OS, W. Slack,
'09, F. L. Guerena, '11, and T. Gore, '11,
as a committee to make all necessary
arrangements. The jollification will
be held' at the University Inn.
