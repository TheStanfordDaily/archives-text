# The 						Peacemaker
## 
### 
When unforseen circumstances pre
vent you from keeping "that engage
ment" let a box of Geo. Haas & Sons'
candies smooth away your difficulties.
Four stores in San Francisco from
which you may send it,: Phelan Build
ing, Fillmore at Ellis, Van Ness at
Sutter and 28 Market St., near Ferry.
