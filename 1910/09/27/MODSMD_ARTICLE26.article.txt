# E.E. Society Elect?.
## 
### 
The Electrical Engineering Society
opens the year with the election of
four new members namely, M. Wenk,
'11, C. P. Christensen, '11, S. B. Shaw,
'12, and A. Endres, '12.
The members of the society heard
short addresses by Professors S. B.
Charters and W. A. Hillebrand at their
meeting held last week.
