# Forestry Engineers Required.
## 
### 
Civil Service examinations are to be
given in various cities of this state on
October 29 to secure engineers in
forestry products. The positions of
fered carry with them fairly lucrative
salaries, ranging from $1600 to $2500
per year, and will be filled by those
passing the examinations with the
highest grade. One feature of the test
will be the writing of a thesis con
taining no less than 1000 words on
some subject pertaining to forestry.
Examinations are to be given in both
San Jose and San Francisco. More
detailed information may be obtained
at the President's office.
