# New Zealand Paper Commends Team.
## 
###

ceived from New Zealand a weekly
paper, entitled "The New Zealand
Illustrated Sporting and Dramatic Re
view," which contains several very
good pictures of the All-America
Rugby Team in action against the
Auckland aggregation. Besides these
there is an article on the game that is
unstinted in its praise of (he playing
done by the American athletes.
