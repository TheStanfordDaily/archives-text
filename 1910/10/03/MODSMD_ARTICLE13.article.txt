# STANFORD GRADUATE DIES SUDDENLY IN SAN FRANCISCO.
## 
### 
Harold Fitch, '07, suddenly passed
away in San Francisco last Friday
afternoon. During his college career,
Fitch was one of the best known
undergraduates and took the leader
ship in many student activities. He
edited the Daily Palo Alto, and was a
member of Skull and Snakes, Quad
rangle Club, Press Club, English Club,
Encina Club and Phi Delta Phi. At
the time of his death, Fitch was en
gaged in the Insurance business, hold
ing a responsible position with the
Board of Fire Underwriters.
