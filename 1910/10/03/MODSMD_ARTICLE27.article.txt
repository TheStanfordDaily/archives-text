# English Club to Present Playlet.
## 
### 
"The State of Ego," an original play
by Miss Helen Cambell, '11, is to be
given at an open meeting of the Eng
lish Club, Thursday, October 6, at the
Delta Gamma house. This play was
one of the three presented at the
matinee for the benefit of the Women's
Clubhouse Fund at the beginning of
the semester. It is to be repeated, by
request, as an example of the original
literary work of the University.
A business meeting of the club will
be held at half past seven, and the
play will be given at eight o'clock.
