# Prize is Offered.
## 
### 
The Registrar's Office received today
a copy of the $1000 offer made by the
executive committee of the Panama-
Pacific International Exposition Com
pany for the most practical colored
seal design submitted to them. The
design must be one that can be used
as a trade mark for the Exposition
Company. It will be used as a seal on
all official documents, will be part of
the poster, on all stationery, on all
literature and other documents that
may be desired to be made official.
The competition will close on Oct
ober 15 and all competitors are to for
ward their designs to the offices of the
above named company.
