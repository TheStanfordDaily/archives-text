# Rev. Campbell to Preach.
## 
### 
By the invitation of the Chaplain,
the Rev. It. H. Campbell, pastor of the
Congregational church, Palo Alto, will
occupy the pulpit at the Chapel service
on Sunday morning. Doctor Campbell
graduated from Pomona College, tak
ing his master's degree at the Univer
sity of California. His theological
course was completed at the Pacific
Theological School at Berkeley, lie
has just been ordained to his new
charge at Palo Alto.
