# Geology Society Initiates.
## 
### 
The Stanford Geological and Mining
Society at their last meeting initiated
six new members. The newly honored
are all prominent Geology and Mining
majors. Those initiated were: K. S.
Jordan, '10, E. Leib, '10, R. J. McCann,
'10, \V. Crook, '10, E. J. Boundey, '10,
and C. A. Waring, '11.
