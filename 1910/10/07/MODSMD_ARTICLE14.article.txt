### 
Special reduced fares to Sentinal
Hotel, Yoseniite Valley and return,
account Indian Fandango. One lowest
first class fare and one-third via route
traveled, for the round trip. Sale dates
October 8-12. Return limit October
31, 1910. No stopovers allowed. For
further particulars inquire of your
