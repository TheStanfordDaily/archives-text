# Motorcyclists Elect Officers.
## 
### 
H. I. Hadley, '10, was elected presi
dent of the newly organized Stanford
Motorcycle Club at the last meeting.
W. T. Smith, '11, being chosen secre
tary-treasurer.
" The club decided to make their first
run tomorrow evening. The start
is to be made from Encina Hall at G
o'clock with San Mateo as' the destina
tion for supper. All who intend to be
come members of the club are request
ed by the newly elected officers to join
this initial run.
