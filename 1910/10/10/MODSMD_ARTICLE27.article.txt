# College Notes.
## 
### 
Several thousand copies of the Col
umbia University catalogue, written in
Chinese ,are being sent to the celestial
empire.
The Trustees of Columbia University
propose spending $3,000,000 in making
their medical school the foremost in
the world.
The senior class at Illinois has in
itiated a movement to abolish final
examinations in the second semester
of the senior year.
There is a movement on foot to hold
the next Conference meet at the Uni
versity of Minnesota instead of at the
usual place, Urbana, 111.
