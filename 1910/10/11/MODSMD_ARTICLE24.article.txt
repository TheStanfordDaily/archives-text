# Stanford Man Runs for Congress.
## 
### 
Thomas E. Hayden, the first Stan
ford man to run for Congress, will
speak in Palo Alto on next Monday
evening. Hayden is a candidate on the
Democratic ticket from the fifth dis
trict, in which Palo Alto is situated.
Hayden was graduated from Hamil
ton university in '91, and in 'Go he re
ceived the degree of Doctor of Laws
from Stanford. While at the Univer
sity he was prominent among the
graduate students. He is a member of
Delta Upsilon fraternity.
