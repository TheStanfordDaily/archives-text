# College Notes.
## 
### 
All forms of underclass hazing have
been abolished at the University of
Wisconsin.
A class fight between the first and
second year men at Ames College re
sulted in breaking up chapel exercises.
At Chicago University the members
of the different classes try to outdo
each other in fantastic costumes when
they attend one of the big athletic car
nivals. The class which makes the
best showing receives a large banner.
