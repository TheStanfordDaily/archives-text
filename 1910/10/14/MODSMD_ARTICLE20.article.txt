# DR. PATCHELL WILL OPEN BIBLE CLASS.
## 
### 
Dr. W. T. Patchell, of San Jose, who
for the past two years has been giving
a course in Bible study here, will
again open his class on next Monday
evening, October 11. The meeting will
be held at 7:15 in the men's room.
The course presented by Dr. Pat
chell is an advanced one, open to '11,
12, and '13 men only, and is given
under (he auspices of the Y. M. C. A.
Those who wish to enroll are advised
to see E. 1). Brown, '11, chairman of
the Bible study committee, or D. W.
Weist at the Y. M. C. A. room on or
before Monday evening.
