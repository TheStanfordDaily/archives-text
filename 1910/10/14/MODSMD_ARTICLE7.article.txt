# Tickets on Sale at Bookstore.
## 
### 
The largest crowd that ever wit
nessed an Intercollegiate Freshman
contest on Stanford Field is expected
tomorrow. In view of this fact, Bur
bank requests all who can, to procure
their seats to the game not later than
tomorrow morning. The tickets will
be on sale from 8 until 10 o'clock to
morrow morning at the Bookstore.
