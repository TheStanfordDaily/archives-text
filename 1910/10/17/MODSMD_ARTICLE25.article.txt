# PROFESSOR ADAMS TO EXPLAIN "BRITISH CARTOONS.
## 
### 
On Wednesday evening at 7:30, Pro
fessor E. D. Adams of the History de
partment will deliver a lecture to the
Art Club in room 231. The subject of
the lecture is "British Cartoons on the
American Civil War." These cartoons
will be shown by lantern slides made
from the London "Punch" for the
years 18G0-65. The lecture will con
sist of an exhibition of the slides with
what explanation is necessary to in
dicate their historical significance.
This is an open meeting and everyone
is welcome.
