# ALARM RAISED FOR SMALL FIRE IN ROBLE SATURDAY.
## 
### 
The alarm of fire which startled the
many hundreds in Assembly Hall dur
ing the first act of the Mikado last Sat
urday evening, was raised owing to a
slight fire in one of the third floor
rooms of Roble Hall. The fire started
from an explosion of a small can of
alcohol, but was extinguished before
the local fire company arrived upon
the scene. Only some light tapestry
and decorative material was destroyed.
