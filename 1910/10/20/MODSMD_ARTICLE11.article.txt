# ATHLETICS DEFEAT CUBS TODAY FOR THIRD TIME.
## 
### 
By a score of 12 to 5, the Philadel
phia Americans defeated the Chicago
Nationals this afternoon in the World's
Championship series. This makes the
third straight victory for the Athletics
and with a victory in one out of the
next four games the championship
will be retained by Philadelphia.
Today's game was the first played in
Chicago, the others being in Philadel
phia. If the Athletics win tomorrow,
the series will be over, but if the Cubs
are victors the teams will play in
Philadelphia again Saturday.
