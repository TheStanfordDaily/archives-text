# GALE SEAMAN TO TALK TO "SPECIAL COMMITTEE.
## 
### 
G. Seaman, International Secretary
of the Young Men's Christian Associa
tion, will speak to the recently ap
pointed "Special Committee" at the
chapel Sunday evening. It is through
the influence of Seaman that E. C.
Mercer, who visited the campus two
years ago, has been induced to come
here again to take up personal work
among the men along a moral and re
ligious line. Mercer will be here from
January 2G to 29 inclusive.
Secretary D. W. Weist announces
that other friends of Mercer, who wish
to become members of the committee,
should report at the Y. M. C. A. room
by noon Saturday, October 22.
