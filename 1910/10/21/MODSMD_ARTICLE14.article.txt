# Stanford Socialists Moot.
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the Inter
collegiate Socialist society held a
meeting Tuesday evening at 260 Homer
Street, Palo Alto. N. L. Greist of San
Jose was the speaker of the evening,
aud addressed the club on the subject
of "The Scientific Attitude." A large
number of members and other students
heard the speaker.
A number of prominent socialuists
from nearby cities have been engaged
to address the club at future meetings.
At a short business meeting held be
fore the open meeting, L. Scofield, '10,
was elected president. Scofield majors
in Systematic Botany from Palo Alto,
and is a member of Encina Club.
