# WILL HOLD EXAMINATIONS FOR RHODES SCHOLARSHIP.
## Tests in Greek, Latin and Muthematics Will be Held to Qualify Man for Choice by Board. 
### 
Examinations for the Rhodes Schol
arship are to he held here tomorrow
and the day following. The following
table has been announced by H. T.
Gerrans, secretary to the Delegacy of
Local Examinations, Oxford, England.
Tuesday, October 25, 9 a.m. to 11
a.m., translation of Latin into English;
1 p.m. to 3 p.m., Latin prose; 4 p.m.
to G p.m., arithmetic.
Tuesday, October 26, 9 a.m. to 11
a.m., translation from Greek into Eng
lish; 1 p.m. to 2 pm., Latin grammar;
2:10 p.m. to 3:10 p.m., Greek gram
mar; 4 p.m., to G p.m., Algebra or
geometry.
These examinations are held
throughout this country at exactly the
same time in order that any possibility
of telegraphing the questions might be
obviated. The scholarship provides
for all the needs of the student who
is successful in the competitive ex
amination to pursue advanced work at
Oxford University.
