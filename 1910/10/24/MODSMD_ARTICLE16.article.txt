# Golf Enthusiasts to Start Club.
## 
### 
Many students are becoming inter
ested In the game of golf and from the
present indications, there is every
possibility that the old golf club will
be revived and a new start made. A
call has been made to all those who
are interested in the sport to meet at
the Phi Kappa Psi house next Wednes
day evening at 7:30 for the purpose of
discussing plans for the organization
of such a club.
