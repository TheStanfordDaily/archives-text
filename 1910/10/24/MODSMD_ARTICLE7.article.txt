A bleacher rally will be held
tomorrow afternoon on Stanford
New songs and now yells
will be tried out for the first time
by the yell leaders. These songs
and yells are the ones which will
be used at the Varsity game. The
rally will start promptly at 4
o'clock.
The change in the manner of
giving yells this year necessitates
the placing of plenty of ginger
in concerted cheers, which will
be given during the Varsity game.
To secure this effect, each under
graduate must know the new yells
Tomorrow afternoon presents the
opportunity to learn them.
