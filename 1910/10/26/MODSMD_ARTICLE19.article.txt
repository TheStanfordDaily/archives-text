# Dr. Patchall Conducts Class.
## 
### 
Dr. Patchall's Bible Class is being
held every Monday evening at 7:15,
the subject under discussion at the
present time being "Growth of our
ideas of God." This class is open only
to upperclassmen, other classes having
been formed for underclassmen. En
rollments may be made at the Y.M.C.A.
room.
