# DR. BLODGETT WILL RENDER REGULAR RECITAL.
## 
### 
Dr. n. C. Blodgett will give his usual
Sunday afternoon pipe organ recital
at the Congregational Church, October
30, at 4 o'clock. The program will be:
Nocturne from the "Midsummer
Night's Dream Mendelssohn
Romerci "Soutenir" Lemare
Transcription of "The Lost Cord"
(by request) Sullivan
Bridal Cong Wolstenholme
Madrigal op. 52 No. 3 Ouilmant
Prelude and Fugue in B Hat —minor —
Bach
