# Southern Players Arrive.
## 
### 
The legal fifteen from the University
of Southern California arrived on the
campus this morning. This afternoon
they held a short practice on the turf
field before the Varsity got into an
tion against the substitutes. The
southerners will hold another practice

tomorrow afternoon to become ac
customed to playing on the sod before
their game with the second team on
Saturday afternoon.
On their way north the Southern
lawyers stopped at San Jose to play
the University of the Pacific, defeating
them yesterday afternoon by a 20-0
score. This score was made against a
team which held the Barbarians to an
8-8 tie. last Saturday.
