# ADVISORY COMMMITTEE TO SUPERVISE THE PLUG-UGLY.
## 
### 
By action of the University Confer
ence yesterday evening, the matter of
controlling the coming Ping-Ugly was
left in the hands of the Student Advis
ory Committee.
That body will convene immediately
and draw up the final rules and regula
tions of the rush and the burlesque,
and will submit them to the Junior
and Senior Classes next week.
As the committee is responsible for
all that will appear in the play and in
the poster, the board is desirous that
the manuscripts be submitted to them
immediately.
