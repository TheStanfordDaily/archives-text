# WALDEN CLUB RECEIVES CHARTER FROM SORORITY.
## 
### 
Last evening a formal announce
ment was made to a few friends gath
ered at the Walden Club, that that or
ganization had received a charter from
a national sorority, and would in fu
ture be seen among the names of the
other sororities associated in Fan
hellenic. Alpha Omicron Pi, is the
body from which the charter conies.
It has been known for some time
that the Walden Club had applied for
a charter and the report was circu
lated as long ago as last semester that
the charter had been granted. It was
not until last evening, however, that
definite formal announcement was
made. Alpha Omicron Pi has a chap
ter at California, and is well known in
the sorority world. At present the
names of those who will comprise the
chapter have not been given out. This
information will be made known at the
time the local chapter is installed.
