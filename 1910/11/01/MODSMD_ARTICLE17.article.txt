# CALIFORNIA MEN INVITE SENIORS TO LUNCHEON
## 
### 
President K. L. Dole of the Senior
class is in receipt of an invitation from
the senior men of the University of
California inviting the Senior men of
Stanford to luncheon on the day of the
Intercollegiate game. The luncheon
will be served at one o'clock in the
Senior Hall on the Berkeley campus.
All Senior men are cordially invited to
attend.
In order that the California men
may know how many to expect, all
who intend availing themselves of this
privilege are requested to notify Pres
ident Dole or Student Body President
Ditz during the next few days.
