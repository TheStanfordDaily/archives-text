siring to tryout for the Berwick Peace
Prize hand in names to Professor R.
M. Alden, room 210, by November 10.

amination in English la (Voice) will
be held Friday, November 4, at 2:30,
in room 202.

luncheon by California Senior men in

ber 12. Notify G. A. Ditz or K. L. Dole
of intention to attend.

on Thursday, November 3, at 8 p.m.
in room 31. Professor Guerard will
read a paper on "Voltarianism" in
French Literature of the Second Em
pire.

girl's handicap tournaments are posted
in the Roble Gymnasium and in the
Rest Room. All matches must be
played off by Wednesday, November 3,
or default.

Clubhouse, Wednesday evening, No
vember 2, at 8 o'clock. Dr. Murray
will deliver an illustrated lecture on
"A Vacation in Greece." All interest
ed are cordially invited.
