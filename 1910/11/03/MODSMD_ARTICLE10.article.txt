# STUDENT IS INDEFINITELY SUSPENDED BY COMMITTEE.
## 
### 
By action of the Student Affairs
Committee in meeting yesterday, a
student was indefinitely suspended
for participating in a beer drinking
party.
The notice reads as follows: "At a
meeting of (he Committee on Student
Affairs, held on this date, a student,
for his part in connection with a beer
drinking party in the hills on the
evening of October 21, was indefinite
ly suspended from the University."
A. B. CLARK,
November 2. Chairman.
