# Freshmen Commence Work.
## 
### 
Saturday morning at 8:30 three
squads of Freshmen in charge of mem
bers of the football rally bonfire com
mittee will begin to gather the mater
ial to be used in the construction of
a mammoth tower sixty feet in height.
At the meeting of 1914, in room 08 this
afternoon, the plans for the bonfire
were discussed and the men were
divided into corps and assigned differ
ent sections of the surrounding coun
try in which to work. The active work
of building the immense structure will
be commenced Tuesday.
