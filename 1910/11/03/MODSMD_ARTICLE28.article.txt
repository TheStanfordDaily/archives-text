# College Notes

year is 4900.
Because of exorbitant prices, the
students at Beloit have boycotted all
laundries.
Roosevelt refused an offer of the
presidency of Minnesota because of
other important work.
The Chinese of the University cf
Pennsylvania recently produced,
"When East and West Meet," a play
written by one of their countrymen.
