t to
| City of Mexico
* Wednesday, December 14th, 1010, 1:30 p.m. a magnificent special train
* consisting of Pullman sleepers, Observation, Library car and dining
car will leave San Francisco for the City of Mexico via the Coast Line;
leaving Los Angeles 0:30 a.m., arriving at destination December 18th
13:30 p.m. in time for Christmas celebration and festivities.
Fare for round trip from San Francisco $80.00; from Los Angeles
$70.00. Sleeping car fare 12.51 one way.
For full particulars and detailed itinery write
E. Shillingsburg
District Passenger Agent
