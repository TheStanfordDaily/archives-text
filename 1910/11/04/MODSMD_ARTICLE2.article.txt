# REV. BROUGHER TO DELIVER SERMON IN CHAPEL SUNDAY.
## 
### 
Rev. J. Whitcomb Brougher, of Los
Angeles, will deliver the sermon at the
regular Chapel service Sunday morn
ing at 11 o'clock. The soloist is to be
Mr. Charles Moser, who will sing the
"Prayer" by Wagner. The Chapel
choir will render "The Lord Most
Holy," by Rossini, assisted by the
University orchestra.
As usual, Holy Communion will be
given in the Vestry of the Memorial
Church at 8 o'clock.
