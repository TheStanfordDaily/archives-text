# Folk Lore Society Will Meet.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Spanish
Folk Lore Society will be held Wed
nesday evening, November 9. The
meeting is called for eight o'clock at
643 Webster street, Palo Alto. This
society is an organization of the Sen
iors and graduate students of the Ro
manic Languages department and any
such students may become members.
The election of officers will take place
at this meeting and the plans for ac
tive work will be discussed. All mem
bers of the University who are inter
ested in the study of Spanish folk
lore, but who cannot become active
participants in the society's affairs
may attend as visitors.
