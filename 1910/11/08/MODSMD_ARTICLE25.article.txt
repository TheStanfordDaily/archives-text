# SAN JOSE STANFORD MEN TO BANQUET BEFORE GAME.
## 
### 
On the night before the intercolle
giate game, Friday, November 11, at
7 o'clock, in San Jose, will be held a
banquet open to all former Stanford
students, as well as those now attend
ing the University, residing in that
city. Among other features of this
interesting reunion will be talks by
James Lanagan and Charles Field of
San Francisco. Those desiring to
have places reserved should communi
cate with J. D. Malloy, Secretary of
the San Jose Club, at 143 East Julian
street, San Jose.
