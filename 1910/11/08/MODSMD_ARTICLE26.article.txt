# FRATERNITIES ARE ASKED TO ASSIST BEFORE RALLY.
## 
### 
According to the present plans of
the yell leaders, the final rally this
year will be preceded by the burning
of red fire upon the row. The yell
leaders have requested the various
fraternities to provide red fire for this
occasion and it is anticipated that
there will he vivid illumination upon
the row immediately before the rally
is started in the Assembly Hall.
