# INFORMAL VACATION DANCE TO BE HELD AT ARMORY.
## 
### 
The Armory, Palo Alto will be the
scene of an informal vacation dance.
The affair will he held on Thanks
giving eve, November 2G and will be
given under the auspices of Company
L, Fifth Regiment N. G. C. The Fifth
Regiment Band of forty pieces will
furnish the music and every effort
will be made to duplicate the en
joyable affair given last year and
which was largely attended by college
peope.
A special car has been engaged to
run to the campus after the dance,
and every convenience will be extended
to the University public.
