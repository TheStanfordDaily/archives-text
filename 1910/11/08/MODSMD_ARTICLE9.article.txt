# CALIFORNIA'S THREE MEN WILL PLAY SATURDAY.
## Jordan, Elliott and Swartz Will Probably Add Strength to Blue and Gold Scrum. 
###

certain that the three California Var
sity players injured in the game with
Nevada last Saturday, Jordan, Elliott,
and Swartz, will lie able to take their
