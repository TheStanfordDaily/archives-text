# Partridge is Resting Bruised Ankle.
## 
### 
C. E. Partridge, the Cardinal rear
rank player, is on crutches for a day
or so in order to give a hrnised ankle
a full opportunity to be rested before
the California game. In scrummage
last Friday he received a small hurt
on the left foot, and at this time is
taking every possible precaution to
keep the ankle in condition.
