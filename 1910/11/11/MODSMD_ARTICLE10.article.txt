# College Notes.
## 
### 
Eighteen men reported at Brown for
baseball practice.
The annual income at Harvard is
over $1,000,000.
The entering class at Princeton
numbers 339, a decrease of 12 from
last year.
A large gray skull cap with an
orange button is the official freshmen
cap at Oberlin.
Princeton undergraduates are ad
mitted to secret practice upon pre
sentation of a pass issued by the ath
letic association.
Columbia, following the example of
the University of Chicago, has estab
lished a student's bank as part of the
college activities. The bank is con
nected with the purser's office and
pays no interest.
