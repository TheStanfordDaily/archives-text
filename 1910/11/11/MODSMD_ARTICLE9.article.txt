# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB ASKS FOR FULL ATTENDANCE.
## 
### 
A number of unusually interesting
features have been prepared for the
regular meeting of the Cosmopolitan
Club, which will be held in the Y. M. C>
A. rooms next Thursday evening, Nov
ember 17. It is expected that a mem
ber representing each one of the var
ious nationalities in the club will
bring and explain some curious article
typical of his own country. President
C. C. Wheaton, '11, states that it is
particularly desirable that the full
membership be present at this meet
ing, and each member is urged to
make especial endeavor to attend.
