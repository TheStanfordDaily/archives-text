# ROTH TO GIVE ILLUSTRATED TALK ON AUSTRALIAN TRIP.
## 
### 
"The Tour of the American Football
Team in New Zealand and Australia,"
will be the topic upon which A. E.
Roth will speak at an assembly to be
held under the auspices of the Asso
ciated Students tomorrow evening.
Besides discussing the work of the
team in the Antipodes, Roth will also
describe the various points of interest
along the route. A large number of
excellent lantern slides have been ob
tained and these will be shown in the
course of the lecture. The meeting
will be held in the Assembly Hall and
will commence promptly at 8 o'clock.
