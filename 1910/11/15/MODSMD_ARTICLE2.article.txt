# DR. BLODGETT TO GIVE RECITAL ON WEDNESDAY.
## 
### 
Dr. Blodgett's midweek organ re
cital will be given in All Saints Church
in Palo Alto on Wednesday afternoon
at 4: P.O. All interested are invited to
attend.

ranged :

Wagner
Adoration (Holy City) Ganl
