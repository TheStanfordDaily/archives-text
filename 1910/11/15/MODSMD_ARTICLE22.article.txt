# EDUCATION MAJORS WILL PICNIC TOMORROW NIGHT.
## 
### 
Beneath the oaks overshadowing the
Roble bridge those who register in the
Department of Education will gather
for an informal picnic tomorrow even
ing. A large bonfire is planned and a
social time for all is planned after the
supper.
The participants will assemble in
room 55 at 5 o'clock sharp and from
there proceed to the picnicing grounds.
