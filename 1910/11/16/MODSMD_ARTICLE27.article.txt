# ECONOMIC PRIZES TO BE SUBMITTED TO J. LAUGHLIN.
## 
### 
All those desiring to compete for the
Hart, Schaffner and Marx ecomomic
essay prizes should send their manu
scripts to Professor T. L. Laughlin, of
the University of Chicago, before June
1, 1911. Two thousand dollars in
prizes has been offered for the best
essays on economic subjects. These
prizes are divided into two divisions,
class A consisting of a first prize of
$1000 and a second prize of $500 is
open to anyone in America. Class B,
with prizes of $300 and $200 is open
to students attending any American
college.
