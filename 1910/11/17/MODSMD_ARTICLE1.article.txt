# TWO GET FOUR STARS
## L. MINTURN, '09, AND G.E. CHEDA, '10, RECEIVE EMBLEMS. Sixteen Men Granted Varsity "S." Eight Others Receive German Letter from Board. 
### 
Two men were granted the privilege
of wearing a four star sweater, and
they and fourteen others were granted
Varsity emblems at the regular meet
ing of the Executive Committee of the
Student Body last evening. The min
utes follow:
President George A. Ditz in chair.
Burbank and Kern absent.
The minutes of the meeting of No
vember 9 were read and approved.
It was moved, seconded, and carried
that the following men, L. Minturn,
'09, G. E. Cheda, '10, K. L. Dole, '11, F.
C. Brown, '11, B. E. Erb, '12, W. Smith,
'12, A. L. Sundell, '12, J. T. Arrell, '12,
S. L. Mitchell, '13, J. H. Thoburn, *13,
A. Frank, '13, E. C. Baumgartner, '13,
E. P. Geissler, '14, P. F. Harrigan, '14,
F. W. Reeves, '14, C. E. Olmstead, '14,
be granted the Varsity emblem, and
that G. E. Cheda, '10, and L. Mintunl,
'09, be awarded four stars. The men
to have the choice of a fob or sweater.
It was moved, seconded and passed
that the following substitutes be
awarded the German S: H. L. Horton,
'08, J. W. Fitting, '10, E. C. Woodcock,
'10, J. F. Partridge, '12, A. Sanborn,
'12, T. Sanborn, '12, G. D. Worswick,
'13, L. Cass, '13.
It was moved, seconded and passed
that the Barbarian and Olympic clubs
be sent letters of thanks for their aid
and courtesy during the football sea
son.
Adjourned.
J. E. THOMPSON, Secretary.
L. Minturn who was granted four
stars last evening has represented
Stanford on the football field in '06,
'07, '09, and '10. Minturn is a member
of Delta Kappa Epsilon and registers
in Electrical Engineering. G. E. Cho
da, the other four star man, played in
'OG, '08, '09, and '10. He is a member
of Encina Club and registers in Geol
ogy from San Luis Obispo.
