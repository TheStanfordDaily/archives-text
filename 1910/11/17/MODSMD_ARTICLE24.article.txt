boxes filled with candies, candy plum
puddings decked with holly and other
appropriate favors for the Thanks
giving feast. At Geo. Haas & Sons'
four candy stores in San Francisco:
Phelan Building, Fillmore "At Ellis,
Van Ness at Sutter and 2S Market
street, near Ferry.
