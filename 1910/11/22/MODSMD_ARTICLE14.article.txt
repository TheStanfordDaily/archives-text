# WILL HOLD SECOND SALE OF COTILLION TICKETS.
## 
### 
The second sale of Sophomore Cot
illion tickets will be held on the Quad
tomorrow morning beginning at 9:15.
The first sale which lasted but a short
time last Wednesday resulted in the
disposal of over half the admittances,
but being of such short duration,
many were unable to get tickets at
that time.
The tickets have not been put on
sale at the Bookstore up to the pres
ent time, but will be obtainable there
after the sale on the Quad tomorrow
morning.
