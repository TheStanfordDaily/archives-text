# Scott Granted World's Record.
## 
### 
The American Athletic Union, the
final authority in all matters pertain
ing to athletics in the United States,
has at last accepted as a worlds record,
the mark set by L. S. Scott, '10, last
May. The record of 12 feet,
inches, was made by Scott on May 27,
at the California Conference Meet on
the Berkeley oval.
