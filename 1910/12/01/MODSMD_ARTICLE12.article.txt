# STUDENT BODY TO PAY SOME EXPENSES OF TRIP.
## 
### 
Little business was transacted by
the Executive Committee at their reg
ular meeting last night. A discussion
of the proposed trip of the Rugby team
to Vancouver was the only matter
touched on.
The minutes follow:
President Ditz in chair.
Brown, Smithrum, Arrell and Kern
absent.
Moved, seconded and carried that
the football men on the northern trip
be allowed all necessary expenses not
covered by the guarantee.
Adjourned.
â– T. E. THOMPSON,
Secretary.
