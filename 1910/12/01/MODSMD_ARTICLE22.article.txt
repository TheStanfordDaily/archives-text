# DECEMBER SEQUOIA TO BE ON SALE TOMORROW.
## 
### 
The December issue of the Sequoia
will appear tomorrow and be placed
on sale on the Quad. The magazine
will contain its usual complement of
stories and articles, notable among
which will be "The Road to Yester
day," by Mrs. T. P. Russell of the Eng
lish department. This will deal with
the series of miracle plays to be pre
sented by the English Club next se
mester. A review of soccer prospects
by R. J. Miller, 11, is another feature
of the magazine which will appeal to
those interested in that sport. Miss
R. A. Sampson, '11, also contributes
