# Chaplain Will Preach Sunday.
## 
### 
At the regular chapel services Sun
day morning the preacher will be Dr.
D. Charles Gardner, the University
Chaplain. In his subject, "Consecra
tion the motive for a true life," the
speaker will present a line of thought
of particular interest to college stu
dents.
Special music will be provided. C.
Moser is to sing "Pro Peccatis" by
Rossini, and the choir wilt present an
anthem, "Lord in Light Arrayed" by
Wagner. In the rendition of the
anthem the choir is to be supplement
ed by an orchestra.
