# Big Four Society Enjoys Dunce.
## 
### 
The monthly gathering of the Big
Four society took the form of an in
formal dance in Roble Hall last Fri
day evening. The members of the
society, who are all former residents
of Minnesota, Illinois, lowa, and Wis
consin, together with a few of their
friends, enjoyed the affair, which will
probably constitute the last gathering
of the club for the present semester.
