According to a custom originated
and observed by the Daily Palo Alto in
years preceding that of 1908 and in
the very infancy of the paper, a wo
man's issue will appear next Wednes
day, December 7. The woman's edition
will be edited by Miss M. M. Hays, '11,
who has diligently served in the in
terests of the Daily Palo Alto for four
years. Complete charge of the paper
will be surrendered to her and the as
sistants she will select to aid her in
publishing this edition.
