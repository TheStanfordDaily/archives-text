# 1914 Jolly-up Friday Evening.
## 
### 
Following the precedent set' by the
other classes 1914 will have a jolly-up
next Friday evening, December 9. The
long deferred get-together of the
Freshman class will he held at the
Palo Alto Armory. White collars and
stiff shirts will not he in order at the
informal. This is the ultimatum of
the jolly-up committee appointed by
President Wells.
Chairman M. Luther announces that
the dance will start promptly at 8:15.
Two special cars have been chartered
to carry the dancers back to the camp
us. These will leave Palo Alto at
twelve o'clock.
