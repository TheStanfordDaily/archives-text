# Women Collectors are Appointed.
## 
### 
Collectors have been appointed in
all of the sorority and boarding houses
011 the campus to secure money from
the women students in ten cent sums
to buy a Christmas tree for the child
ren in the Nurses' Settlement in San
Francisco. Those in charge of the
movement requst that any money not
given to collectors be given to Miss M.
Graham, '12.
