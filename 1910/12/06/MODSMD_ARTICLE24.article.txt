# Y.W.C.A. to Hold Second Sale.
## 
### 
A second candy sale will be held in
the Women's Rest Room and in Mrs.
Allan's office on Wednesday morning
between S: 15 and 12:15. This sale is
also under the auspices of (he Y. W.
C. A., and will swell the fund which is
to send girls to the Capitola Confer
ence next spring. The sale is open
to everybody in the University, and a
sufficient amount of candy has been
secured to furnish all who desire it.
