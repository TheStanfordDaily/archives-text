# U.C. Fencing Class Sends Challenge.
## 
### 
The girls of the fencing class at the
University of California have sent a
cordial invitation to the girls in the
fencing class at Stanford, asking them
to meet them in bout work on Febru
ary 22. It is understood that they have
five girls in their advanced class and
three in the beginning class. There
are about fifteen Stanford girls in
terested in the sport and it is hoped
that they will all accept Berkeley's
challenge for that date. Their invita
tion is in no way connected with in
tercollegiate sports.
