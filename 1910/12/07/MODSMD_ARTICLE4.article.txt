# "FIRST YEAR AT STANFORD" CHRISTMAS EDITION OUT.
## 
### 
Beginning tomorrow, the "First Year
at Stanford" will be found on sale at
the campus Bookstore and Hyde's,
Palo Alto. This book is simply a re
print of the old book by the same
name, which is well known as a series
of real stories about the pioneer strug
gles of the University. Such well
known writers as Dr. Jordan and Ver
non Kellogg are to lie found among
the list of. authors.
The present copy of the book is in
the nature of a special holiday edition,
got out by the English Club for the
benefit of Christmas buyers. It is at
tractively bound in cream and white
with the Stanford seal embossed on the
corner in red and gold.
