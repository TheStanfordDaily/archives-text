# SCHUBERT CLUB HOLDS ELECTION OF OFFICERS.
## 
### 
Miss N. E. Moise, of San Francisco,
was elected president of the Schubert
Club for the ensuing semester at the
mid-vear election, which was held yes
terday. The other officers are as fol
lows: Business manager, Miss M.
Graham, '12: secretary, Miss B. Cole
man, '13; librarian, Miss W. Bassett,
'14.
The regular meetings of the club
will be discontinued for the rest of
this semester. In January, active work
will begin again when the club will as
sist the Oratorio Society in the concert
planned for the latter part of that
month.
