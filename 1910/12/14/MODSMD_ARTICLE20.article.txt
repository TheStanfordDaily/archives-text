# PROMINENT PHILOSOPHER WILL GIVE NEW COURSES.
## 
### 
Two new courses in the Develop
ment of Philosophy will be given by
Professor A. W. Moore, of the Univer
sity of Chicago next semester. The
"Meaning of Modern Philosophy," an
introductory course, and one for ad
vanced students on "Evolution and
Philosophy." will constitute these ad
ditions to the curriculum.
Professor Moore is a teacher of
marked effectiveness and holds a high
place among English and American
leaders of investigation in the field of
Philosophy. He is a frequent and im
portant contributor to the periodical
literature of philosophy, and has just
published a book upon "Pragmatism
and Its Critics."
He has taken a house on the campus
and will arrive a week or more before
the opening of the spring semester.
