# DR. JORDAN WILL DELIVER LECTURE TO MEN STUDENTS.
## First of Addresses Under Auspices of Y. M. C. A. in Encina Club Room Sunday. 
### 
Dr. David Starr Jordan will address
the men of the University in Encina
Clubroom at 7:30 on next Sunday
evening. January 15, under the aus
pices of the Y. M. C. A. The subject, of
his talk will be. "The Religion of a
Sensible American."
The Young Men's Christian Associa
tion in announcing this lecture have
followed the custom adopted in recent
years of having Dr. Jordan speak to
the men of the University twice a year.
The address will be preceded by a
short musical program given by the
University orchestra under the leader
ship of H. D. Owens. '11.
The Y. M. C. A. is exerting every
effort to make this meeting pleasant
and profitable for all. H. J. Smith, '11.
acting president of the association will
preside
