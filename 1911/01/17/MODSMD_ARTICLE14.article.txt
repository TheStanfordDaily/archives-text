# Professor Guerard Offers New Course.
## 
### 
Professor A. L. Guerard of the
French department is offering a course
of weekly lectures in French on the
topics: "Modern Civilization in
France," "Current Events," and "Ques
tions and Discussions." This course
will be open to all advanced students,
the Faculty and visitors. One hour of
credit will be given for outside work
in connection with it.
All who wish to take advantage of
the opportunity offered will kindly
communicate with Professor Guerard,
before the end of this week, and make
arrangements for the recitation hour,
which will be 9:1") any morning or
any afternoon hour.
