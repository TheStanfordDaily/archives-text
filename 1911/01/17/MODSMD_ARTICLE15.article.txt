# Change Made in Education Schedule.
## 
### 
A change in the schedule of the Edu
cation Department has been made
which interchanges the hours of Edu
cation 15 and 16. The former will
henceforth be given from 3:30-5:30 on
Tuesday, while the latter will be given
on Tuesday and Thursday at 8:15.
