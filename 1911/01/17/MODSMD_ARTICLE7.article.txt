# ONLY FORTY-FIVE MEN ANSWER BASEBALL CALL
## OUTSIDE GAME IS SCHEDULED FOR SATURDAY. First Practice Will be Held on Auxiliary Diamond Tomorrow Afternoon at 4:30.

Forty-five Baseball candidates signed
u]> this afternoon in room r»o and will
appear tomorrow at 4:30 on the aux
iliary diamond for the first regular
practice. The squad has three days in
which to prepare for the opening con
test next Saturday with the Olympic
Club, and the men will lie organized as
soon as possible. The old Varsity
squad ot last year being large enough
to make a nine, there should be no
difficulty in ihe line-up for the first
inning. Thereafter the other men will
probablj be given a chance to show
their mettle.
