We trust that it was a failure to
read the announcement column of the
Daily Palo Alto which led to but 45
men signing up for Baseball
Play yesterday. As matters now
Hall stand there are hardly enough
men to make up the interclass
teams. 1911 must bravely struggle to
retain the championship with a nine
of three players. After deducting
Varsity squad men, every 1913 candi
date is sure of a place on his class
team.
A good capable Captain has been
chosen in B. L. Ball, '11, and he is
deserving of better support. The
Student Body is fortunate in the se
lection of the new leader and with
more material to work with he should
captain another victorious Varsity.
