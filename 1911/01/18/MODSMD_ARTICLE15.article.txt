# JUNIOR OPERA TRYOUTS WILL BE HELD EARLY NEXT WEEK.
## 
### 
Plans for the Junior Opera tryouts,
which will be held early next week,
were discussed last evening by the
committee in charge of the production.
The details will be announced in to
morrow's issue of the Daily Palo Alto.
In addition, various other details of
the opera were talked over, but noth
ing definite was decided.
