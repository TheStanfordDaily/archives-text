# CELEBRATED SCIENTIST VISITOR AT UNIVERSITY
## Dr. W.M. Wheeler of Harvard, Noted Entomologist, on Search for Material for New Book. 
### 
The Department of Entomology has
for a guest Doctor W. M. Wheeler, an
entomologist of note, at present con
nected with Harvard. Doctor Wheeler
is considered the foremost authority
on ants in America, and among the
foremost in the world of science. He is
at present on an extended tour in
search of material for a monograph on
"North American Ants." His efforts
have added much to the knowledge of
the psychology of the lower animals.
