# Organ Recital Program.
## 
### 
Dr. B. C. Blodgett today announces
the program for his usual weekly or
gan recital for Sunday, January 22, at.
4 o'clock, in the Congregational
Church, Palo Alto. The following
selections are included:
Prelude op 65, No. 2... .Mendelssohn
Sexteete from "Lucia" Donizetti
Evening Melody Shelley
"Inflannnatus" from the "Stabat-
Mater" Rossini
Symphony No. 2 Beethoven
Adagio E Allegro Con brio
Larghetto
Schetzo (Allogro)
Finale.
