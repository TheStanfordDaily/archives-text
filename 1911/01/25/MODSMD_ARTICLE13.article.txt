# Euphronia Discusses the Referendum.
## 
### 
"Resolved, That the referendum
should be adopted in California," was
the subject of a closely contested de
bate in Euphronia, last night. The de
cision was rendered in favor of C. C.
Close, '14, and C. C. McDonald, '12, who
upheld the affirmative, against the
negative speakers, A. C. Smith and J.
I'. Gregg.
Before the debate, \V. Tucker, Jr.,
'12, gave a short impromptu discussion
on, "The general value of debating in
a college community," and R. F.
Stever gave an interesting talk on.
"Dr. Jordan's address before the Com
monwealth Club in San Francisco."
