# Rehearsals Being Held.
## 
### 
The second rehearsal of the Soph
omore play cast was held this after
noon. Last Tuesday was the date of
the lirst practice when Coach Mathieu
went over this first act. The indivi
dual parts which were received last
night were distributed today, and the
second act was taken up this atter
noon. The coach was very pleased
with the result as all are taking hold
of their parts in a highly satisfactory
manner. Rehearsals will he held every
Tuesday and Thursday afternoon
