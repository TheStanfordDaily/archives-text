# NOTED PORTLAND MINISTER WILL PREACH SUNDAY.
## Rev. W. B. Hinson of White Temple Baptist Church to Fill Pulpit. 
### 
The pulpit of the University Chapel
will be filled by the Rev. W. B. Hinson
of the White Temple Baptist Church of
Portland, Oregon, Sunday morning.
Rev. Hinson is one of the best known
ministers of the northwest and Can
ada, where he speaks to large con
gregations. His Portland church is
one of the largest in the northern city.
