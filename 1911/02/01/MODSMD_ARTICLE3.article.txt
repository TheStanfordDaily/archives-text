# SOCIAL SERVICE CLUB TO REORGANIZE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tomorrow evening at 8 o'clock at
the Delta Gamma house, the Social
Service Club will re-organize and elect
officers for the semester.
At the monthly meetings of the
club, the social, economic, and politi
cal problems of the day will be studied
to better the information of the mem
bers. who will undertake philanthropic
work here and in San Francisco. It
is expected that as soon as the club
gets in good working order, it will be
divided into two parts, one for men,
and the other for women.
From time to time, open meetings
will be held in the University Chapel,,
and an endeavor to have an Assembly
under its auspices will be made.
