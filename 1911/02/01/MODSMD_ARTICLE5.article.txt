# MASKERS TO PRESENT FARCE AT WOMEN'S LEAGUE MEETING.
## 
### 
The Maskers will present a very
charming little two act farce, entitled
"Mrs. Oakley's Telephone," at a meet
ing of Women's League to be held to
morrow afternoon at 4:30 at, Roble
Hall. The cast consists of four
characters; Mrs. Oakley r , who will be
impersonated by Miss E. Porter; Con
stance, her friend, by Miss F. Wendl
ing; Mary, the cook, by Miss M. Wil
soon and Emma, the maid, by Miss G.
Workman. The scene is laid in Mrs.
Oakley's home
