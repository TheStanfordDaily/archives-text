# SOCCER TEAM WILL PLAY IN SAN FRANCISCO TOMORROW.
## 
### 
Rain or shine tomorrow morning at
11:34, the Stanford Soccer team will
leave for San Francisco. In the after
noon they will meet the Pastimes in a
regular league game. The contest will
start promptly at 2 o'clock so that the
men may return to the campus in
time for the debate.
The line-up will be: Outside right.
Pel las; inside right, Maloney; center.
Fyfe; inside left, Pung; outside left,
Higgins; right half, King: left half.
Reynolds; right full, Erb; left full.
McCann; goal, Stuart.
