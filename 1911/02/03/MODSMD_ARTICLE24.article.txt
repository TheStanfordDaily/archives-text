# Rand Elects Officers.
## 
### 
Officers for this year were elected at
a meeting of the band held last night.
B. A. Stevenson, '11, and R. R. Beal,
'12, were chosen leaders as the new
system of leadership calls for two
men. Both have had considerable ex
perience along this line of work. J.
H. Paine, '12, was elected business
manager; P. S. Davis, '14, librarian;
and G. P. Ross, '14, property man.
The new leaders and business man
ager together with the old men who
have held the same offices will make
up the executive committee.
