# Yonker and Miss Wadsworth Engaged.
## 
### 
Mrs. J. 1). Wadsworth of Santa Rosa
lias announced the engagement of her
daughter, Hazel M. Wadsworth, ex-'IO,
to Malcom G. Yonker, likewise ex-'IO
Yonker is at present employed in rail
road engineering work in Hilo. Hawaii.
The marriage will take place in about
a month in Honolulu, and tliey expect
to make their home in Hilo.
