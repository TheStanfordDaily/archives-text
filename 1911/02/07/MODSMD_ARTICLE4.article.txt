# Forestry Lecture Open to Public.

### 
Announcement has been made of a
lecture to be given at the home of
Professor R. W. Doane, 527 Homer
avenue, Palo Alto, tomorrow evening

Forest Ranger," the lecturer being J.
Miller of the Forest Service. This
talk is intended for all students inter

to he present. The matter of reor
ganizing the Forestry Club is also to
