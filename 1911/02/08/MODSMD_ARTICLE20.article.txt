# Professional Couching Discussed.
## 
### 
"Resolved, That the United States
should subsidize a merchant marine,"
was the subject of debate in Euphronia
last night. The decision was given in
favor of the negative, which was up
held by G. N. Wood and G. Miller,
against L. D. Summerfield, '12, and R.
Howard, '13, who argued for the af
firmative.
P. 1). Nowell, '13, defended the af
firmative side of an extemporaneous
debate, "Resolved, That Stanford
should adopt a system of professional
coaches," while C. C. Close, '14, stood
for the negative.
