# R.F. STEVER, '12, ELECTED TO UNIVERSITY CONFERENCE.
## 
### 
R. F. Stever, '12, was elected to the
University Conference today from the
Law School to fill the vacancy caused
by the failure of P. M. Ogilvie, '11. to
return to college this semester. Stev
er was opposed in the election by C.
L. Moore, '12. the vote being 32 to 13.
Stever's home is in Redwood City
and he is a law special here. He is a
member of Euphronia and the Phi
Kappa Psi fraternity.
