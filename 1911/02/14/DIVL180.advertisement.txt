PHOENIX SILK SOX
No student ever had too many silk hose, and now that the Phoenix "has
happened" none need ever he without a sufficient number on account of
price. For the first time in the history of the silk industry we are able to
offer a guaranted silk hose at f.Oc a pair. We are sole agents.
FRAZER & CO.
SAM GUARANTEES
A GOOD TIME, A GOOD SMOKE. GOOD FUN ALL AROUND
PENINSULAR PARLOR CO., Mayfield.
