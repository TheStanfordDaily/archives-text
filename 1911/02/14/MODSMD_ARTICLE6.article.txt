# Cricket Games Started for Women.
## 
### 
This afternoon Miss Cleveland's
Sports Class played its first game of
cricket on the Roble Courts. A great
deal of interest and enthusiasm If
being displayed by the girls over this
sport, as Stanford is the first univer
sity on the coast to innovate this form
of women's athletics. It is hope;l soon
to have complete equipment provided
and regular games arranged.
