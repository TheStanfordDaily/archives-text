Rev. Edwin H. Hughes, one of the
best known bishops of the Methodist
Church, and late president of De Pauw
University, will preach Sunday morn
ing at 11 o'clock in the University
chapel.
Bishop Hughes delivered the Bac
calaureate Sermon in the Assembly
Hall for the Class of 1909 and made a
great impression on all who heard
him. Undoubtedly, these who listened
to his discourse then will not miss
this opportunity to hear him again.
Mr. Charles Moser, the soloist, will
sing.
