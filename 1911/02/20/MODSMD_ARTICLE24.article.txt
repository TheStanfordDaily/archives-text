# PETERS SIGNS UP WITH PHILADELPHIA AMERICANS.
## 
### 
J. D. Peters, '11, who played short
stop for the Seniors in their game
against the Juniors last Thursday,
and also played a star game for the
All-Stars against the Varsity Satur
day, has signed a contract with Con
nie Mack, manager of the Philadelphia
American League baseball team to
play for him.
Previous to his coming here, Peters
was first, baseman for the Santa Clara
College team, and an outfielder for
the San Jose State League nine.
Peters majors in law, and is a mem
ber of the Delta Upsilon fraternity.
He will remain at Stanford for the
remainder of the semester.
