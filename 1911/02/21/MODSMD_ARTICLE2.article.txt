# EIGHT STUDENTS GO ON Y.M.C.A. LECTURE TOUR.
## 
### 
Eight students were sent by the Y.
M. C. A. to Santa Cruz this last week
end, to talk to the high school, Sunday
schools, churches, young people's so
cieties and Y, M. C. A. on matters per
taining to the Christian life. They
were enthusiasticaly received, and
their metings were well attended.
