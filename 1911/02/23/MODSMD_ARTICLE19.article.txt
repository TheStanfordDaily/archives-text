# Krehbiel is Orator of the Day.
## 
### 
Professor E. B. Krehbiel, chairman
of the Faculty Committee on Public
Exercises, was orator yesterday at the
celebration of Old Settler's day at
Campbell. The subject of his address
was, "America's Second Great Gift."
