# Sophomore Seat Sale on Monday.
## 
### 
Seats for the Sophomore play, "The
Senator," will go on sale Monday
morning at the Stanford Bookstore,
and at the Stanford Pharmacy in Palo
Alto. The tickets will sell at the usual
prices of one dollar, seventy-five, and
fifty cents. Lots will be drawn at
eight, o'clock Monday morning. Place 3
in line will be assigned according to
tho numbers drawn.
