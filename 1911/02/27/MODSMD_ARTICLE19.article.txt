# SEAT SALE INSURES BIG HOUSE AT SOPHOMORE PLAY.
## 
### 
On the first day of the Sophomore
Play seat sale, an unusually large
number of seats have been sold. The
amount realized reached $600 by noon
today. The heaviest sale has been at
the University Bookstore, where prac
tically all of thp $1 seats were dis
posed of, and a large block of the 50
and 75 cent tickets. At the Stanford
Pharmacy in Palo Alto there still re
main some $1 seats and a number for
50 and 75 cents. Reservations for
several blocks of seats were received
early this afternoon and it is expected
that the entire house will be sold out
in a day or so.
