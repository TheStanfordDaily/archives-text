# In Memoriam JANE LATHROP STANFORD
## 
### 
Tuesday being the sixth
anniversary of the death of
Mrs. Stanford, there will be a
simple Memorial Service at
the Mausoleum at 4:45 p.m.
The Faculty and students of
the University, and the people
of the community are invited
to attend.
If it should rain the service
will be held in the Chapel.
