# Dr. Daenell to Address Encina Club.
## 
### 
The men of Encina Club will have
the pleasure this evening of listening
to an address by Dr. Daenell. the noted
German historian, who spoke at Tues
day's Assembly. The subject of Dr.
Daenell's address will be, "Student
Life in German Universities." The
time of the address has been set. for
c/clock. All members of the Univer
sity are invited to attend. The com
mittee in charge suggest that all who
wish, bring their smoking utensils.
