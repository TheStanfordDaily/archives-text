# NINETEEN YEARS AGO.
## 
### 
(From the D. I'. A. files of 1892)
At. the Boat Club meeting yesterday
afternoon no business was transacted
as there was not a quorum in atten
dance.
E. E. Painter, lias left on account
of weak eyes.
The training table lias now dis
pensed with its Chinese cook. A Span

ish chef now cooks the meals for the
team.
Condit, the quarter back on the sec
ond eleven had his nose broken in the
game yesterday afternoon.
Murphy, '96, has moved from Encina
to Mayfield.
Born —On Saturday, December 30, to
Prof, and Mrs. Goebel, a son.
Professor Buchanan intends taking
a picture of the Sequoia staff tomor
row.
Carnall, Hopkins and Co. has a
large eighteen foot horn made and
have given it to the University. It is
worked by a bellows and almost
equals a steam whistle in its shriek,
as many who attended the Olympic-
Stanford game can testify.
