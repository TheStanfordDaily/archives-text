# Student Suspended.
## 
### 
The following notice was posted on
the bulletin board today:
"At a meeting of the Committee on
Student Affairs held on this date, a
student for sending to the Daily Palo
Alto an ill-considered communication
containing unwarranted statements
and offensive personalities, and de
clining after ample opportunity and
repeated conferences to make suitable
apology, was suspended from the Uni
versity for the rest of the Semester."
March 1, 1911.
