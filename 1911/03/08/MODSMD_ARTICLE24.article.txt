# Alumnae Association Elects Officers.
## 
### 
At the regular meeting of the Resi
dent Stanford Alumnae last evening at
the home of Mrs. Rendtorf in Palo
Alto, the election of officers resulted as
follows: Mrs. K. G. Rendtorf, '9S,
president; Mrs. E. W. Horine, '02,
vice-president; Miss Beth Thompson,
'10, secretary-treasurer. The Board of
Directors chosen are: Mrs. E. \V.
Allan, '06, Miss M. F. Stevens, '01, Miss
C. S. Stoltenburg, '96, and Mrs. L. T.
Culver, '96.
