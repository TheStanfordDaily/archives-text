# Noted Men at Y.M.C.A. Convention.
## 
### 
The thirtieth annual state conven
tion of the California Y. M. C. A. will
meet, this year in San Francisco,
March 23 to 26. While in session it
will be addressed by many prominent
speakers, among whom are Theodore
lloosevelt, Governor Johnson, P. H.
Burt, head of the Chicago Y. M. C. A.
Training School, and Dr. .T. Whitcomb
Brougher of Los Angeles. The work
of the Y. M. C. A. and its problems
will be discussed and a most interest
ing session is promised. Several Stan
ford Y. M. C. A. workers will attend.
Many Will Go to Pacific Grove.
Twenty men have already signified
their intention to attend the student
conference of the Y. M. C. A. at Pacific
Grove during spring vacation. Sec
retary D. W. Weist is confident that a
larger delegation than ever before will
represent the Stanford Y. M. C. A. at
the coming convention.
