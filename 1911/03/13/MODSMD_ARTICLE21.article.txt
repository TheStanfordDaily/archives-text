# FENCING TOURNAMENT WILL BE FINISHED TOMORROW.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon beginning at
4:45, the annual fencing tournament
will be completed in Encina gym. Half
of the men fenced last Thursday after
noon, nnd these together with the men
who have not competed as yet will
nuct tomorrow.
J. R. Oliver, '11, is at present in the
lead, having defeated all comers, and
unless he shows a reversal of form to
morrow should make a good bid for
first place.
