# PROFESSOR GRIFFIN TO LECTURE ON GERMAN UNIVERSITIES.
## 
### 
"German Universities" will be the
subject of a lecture by Professor J. O.
Griffin, of the German department,
Wednesday evening in the Chapel.
Professor Griffin will take up the var
ious - phases of student life in the uni
versities of the Kaiser's empire, illus
trating his talk with lantern slides.
The lecture is under the auspices of
the Cosmopolitan Club. There will be
no charge for admission.
