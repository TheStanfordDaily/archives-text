# Two Informals Will Enliven Recess.
## 
### 
Lovers of the terpsichorean art
will have ample opportunity to enjoy
themselves during the coming spring
vacation at two dances which will
serve to enliven what might otherwise
be a dull recess. The first of these
affairs will be held on Monday even
ing, March 2~, and the second on the
following Friday. The campus dance
orchestra which has provided such en
joyable music on previous occasions
have been engaged to furnish music
for the two informals.
