# Conference Meet Postponed.
## 
### 
Because of a request, received from
Oregon and Washington Universities,
the Intercollegiate Conference Track
Meet, between the universities of the
Pacific. Coast, which had been set for
April 1") at Berkeley, has been post
poned until a latter date, possibly
May 13. Continued rain in the north
has delayed training so as to necessi
tate the changing of dates.
