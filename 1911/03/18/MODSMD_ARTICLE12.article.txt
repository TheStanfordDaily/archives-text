# UNITARIAN MINISTER TO PREACH IN CHAPEL SUNDAY.
## 
### 
Rev. E. Stanton Hodgin, minister of
the First Unitarian Church in Los
Angeles will deliver the sermon Sun
day morning at 11 o'clock in the Uni
versity Chapel. Rev. Hodgin has been
on the coast but a little more than two
years, having succeeded Professor B.
E. Howard, who is now the Professor
of Political Science in the University.
Miss Maud Hazel Miller, a soloist from
Los Angeles, will sing an anthem,
"The Radiant Morn," by Dudley Buck.
