# Blossoming Beauty of Orchards Begins.
## 
### 
A trip over the Peninsular Electric
will show you the valley in the fresh
glory of springtide. Cars now running
full service, with exception of Con
gress Springs branch, which will open
for regular service on Saturday, March
18th. Congress Springs Park in good
condition for picnics. Round-trip ex
cursion tickets sold on Sundays and
legal holidays at our offices, San Jose,
Campbell, Los Gatos, Saratoga, Los
Altos, Mayfield, Palo Alto.
