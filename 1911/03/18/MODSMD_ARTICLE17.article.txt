# TWO SPEAKERS TO SPEAK TO OPPORTUNITIES CLUB.
## 
### 
The Vocational Opportunities Club
will hear two addresses at a meeting
next Monday evening at 7:30 o'clock
in the Council room. Miss Marie Kid
der, '01, associated charities visitor
and member of the rehabilitation com
mittee of San Francisco, will talk of
her work. The other address will be
by Miss Helen Thoburn, '08, on sec
retarial work in connection with the
National Training school of the Young
Wornens' Christian Association. No
tices of this meeting have been sent
to a number of women on the campus,
and a large attendance is anticipated.
