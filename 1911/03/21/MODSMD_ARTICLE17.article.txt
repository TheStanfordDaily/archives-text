# Y.M.C.A. WILL HOLD ITS ANNUAL ELECTION TOMORROW.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at 1:20 in the
Y. M. C. A. room is the time and place
set for the annual election of the offi
cers and directors of the Association
for the ensuing year. During the past
semester, with H. J. Smith, '11, as
president, S. Bvyan, '10. as vice-presi
dent, T. J. Blethen, '13, as recording

secretary, and E. E. Tincher, '12, as
treasurer, the Y. M. C. A. has enjoyed a
successful year. As nine members of
the board of directors and four men
to fill the executive offices are to be
elected, General Secretary Weist
wishes every man who can to be
present.
