# College Notes.
## 
### 
Yale's Senior Prom brought in $2,-
430 last year.
Amherst leads all American colleges
in percentage of alumni in "Who's
Who."
At Wellesley, anyone mispelling
three words in a theme must join a
spelling class.
The Freshmen at the University of
Pennsylvania show their individuality
by wearing a special class pin.
The University of Michigan will
have an exhibit at the "World's Fair
of Hygiene," which opens May 1 at
Dresden, Germany.
