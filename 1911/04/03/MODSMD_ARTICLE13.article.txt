# Y.M.C.A. HOLDS ELECTION OF OFFICERS AND DIRECTORS.
## 
### 
H. .1. Smith, '11, was re-elected pres
ident of the Y. M. C. A. at its recent an
nual election. F. B. Watkins, '14, will
fill the office of recording secretary, and
E. C. Snath, '12, will be the new treas
urer. Professors E. B. Krehbiel, F. C.
Woodward, R. R. Long, and Messrs.
C. E. Jordan, R. H. Campbell, O. M.
Easterday, and C. E. Childs have been
selected to serve on the Board of Di
rectors for two years. A. E. Roth, '09,
and T. L. Coleman, '12, will be the
student representatives and Drs. R.
L. Green, and O. L. Elliott, will hold
positions with that body as hold-overs
from last year's Board. The president
and treasurer are ex-officio members
of the Board.
