# GOOD MUSICAL PROGRAM FOR SATURDAY EVENING.
## Seats on Sale Thursday for Entertainment in Assembly Hall after Track Meet. 
### 
Tickets for the big musical enter
tainment to be presented in Assembly
Hall Saturday evening by the Com
bined Musical Clubs will go on sale in
the usual manner Thursday morning
at 8 o'clock at the Stanford Bookstore
and the Stanford Pharmacy in Palo
Alto. All seats sell for 50 cents.
An unusually attractive program
which will be announced shortly, is
being prepared by the clubs and no
one can possibly be disappointed.
The Intel-scholastic cup will be pre
sented to the preparatory school ath
letes during the intermission. The
interscholastic entries will be the
guests of the evening.
