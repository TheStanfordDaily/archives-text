# MISS JESSIE D. LAKIN, ex-'13 DIES AT SANTA BARBARA.
## 
### 
Word, was received on the campus
today from Santa Barbara, telling of
the death in that city of Miss Jessie
D. Lakin, ex-'l3. Shortly after Stan
ford closed last May, Miss Lakin suf
fered a nervous collapse, from the ef
fects of which she never completely
recovered. Tho it was known that she
was in ill health, her condition was not
supposed to be critical, and the news
of her death comes as a great shock to
her many friends on the campus. Miss
Lakin is a member of the Delta Delta
Delta sorority.
