# Nestorians Hold Annual Banquet.
## 
### 
Feasting and speechmaking enter
tained the members of Nestoria last
evening at tlieir annual banquet held
at the Theta Delta Chi house. A good
ly number of workers of the society
sat about the board and were enter
tained by an excellent program of
after-dinner talks. F. H. Hilton, '11,
president of the society, appropriately
introduced the speakers as toastmas
ter. Those who responded were, In
structor J. B. Cross, L. Craven, '09, G.
Holcomb, '11, R. J. Miller, '11, E. E.
Tincher, '12, M. H. Henshaw, '12, A.
G. Bradford, '13, and O. H. Castle, '14.
While the talks were all of a high
order, those of Craven and Cross
proved particularly interesting.
