# Will Elect Baseball Captain Friday.
## 
### 
With the assembling of the Varsity
baseball team on Friday afternoon for

a picture, the 1912 captain will be
chosen by the men who played in the
recent series against California.
Twelve players are eligible to cast
their ballot for a diamond leader of
the coming season.
Coach Ganong has set 1:10 o'clock
as the time for the picture, at Frank
lin's.
