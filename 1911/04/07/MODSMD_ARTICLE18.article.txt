# Unitarian Reception.
## 
### 
Kev. Henry W. Foote, secretary of
the Education department of A. U. A ,
is to be the guest of honor at the
monthly reception of the Unitarian
Church at the home of Professor
Fliigel, 1153 Cowper street, Saturday
evening. Dr. Foote is a young man,
formerly of Boston, now of Ann Arbor.
Those who will attend on Saturday
evening are also looking forward to
hearing Mr. Roy Flint, pianist, lately
returned from study in Berlin.
