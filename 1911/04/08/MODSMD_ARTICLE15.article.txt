# DANA BARTLETT AND PHIL SWARTZ TO SPEAK SUNDAY.
## 
### 
Dana Bartlett of Los Angeles and
Phil A. Swartz, Lafayette University,
'10, will be the speakers at the monthly
meeting of the Y. M. C. A. which is to
be held in the Chapel Sunday evening
at 7:30. Bartlett is well known thro
his settlement work in Los Angeles
and his book, "The Better City." Swartz
is secretary of the "Student Volunteer
Movement" and both gave courses of
lectures at the recent Conference at
Pacific Grove. The time will be divid
ed equally between them Sunday night.
It is hoped that the audience will come
prepared to contribute towards the
Chinese relief fund.
