# CONGRATULATIONS!
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto congratulates
the preparatory school athletes on the
success of the Sixth Annual lnter
scholastic Track Meet this afternoon.
Great praise is due the winners,
especially, for entering as individuals
and capturing the honors as such. We
hope that their showing will bring the
Los Angeles school authorities to a
more sympathetic attitude toward 111-
terscholastic athletics.
We likewise extend our heartiest
congratulations to Kelly, the individ
ual winner, for his great work. Praise
is due to all the entries for the general
high standard of today's results.
