# WOMEN WILL GO TO WOODSIDE ON STUNT PARTY TOMORROW.
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Social
Service club, the girls of the Univer
sity will go over to Woodside tomorrow
evening. The occasion is a vaudeville
and stunt show given for purpose of
raising funds for the library in Wood
side.
The bus fare will be 50 cents and
admission to the vaudeville will 25
cents. All girls wishing to go may
consult Miss Marguerite Graham, at
the Delta Gamma house
