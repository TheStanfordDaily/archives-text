# SOPHOMORES WILL SMOKE AND EAT IN THE HILLS.
## 
### 
To smoke and jolly-up will he the
aim of the second year men tomorrow
evening at a picnic to be held 011 the
banks of the stream which pours over
the mossy sides of Frenchmen's dam.
The Sophomore men are to meet at
the Post-office steps tomorrow after
noon at 5:30 and march in a body to
the picnicing grounds. All musicians
are requested to bring along their in
struments and all are asked to bring
along some small change.
