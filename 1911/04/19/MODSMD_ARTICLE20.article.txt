# DELTA KAPPA EPSILON AND PHI DELTA THETA WIN GAMES.
## 
### 
Two more interfraternity baseball
games were played yesterday after
noon. Delta Kappa Epsilon won from
Acacia by a score of 14 to 1 in a very
one-sided contest. Shattuck and Robb
composed the battery for the winners,
while Rankin and Salbach performed
for the losers.
Phi Delta Theta defeated Phi Kappa
Psi by a close score of 10 to 9. Noble
and Childs formed the winning battery,
while Weaver, Kennedy, and Soper
acted in a like capacity for the losers.
