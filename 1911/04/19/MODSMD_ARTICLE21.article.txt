# "THE SERVANT IN THE HOUSE" AT OPEN ENGLISH CLUB MEETING.
## 
### 
A cordial invitation is extended to
the public to attend the open meeting
of the English Club this evening
at the Alpha Phi house. The Reading
Club will give "The Servant in the
House." The parts will be read as
follows: The Lord Bishop, R. D. Scott,
'13; the Vicar, O. R. Lingo, '14; the
Vicar's Wife, Miss G. Workman, '12;
Mary, Miss A. Holmes, '12; Robert, F.
H. Beach, '12; Rogers, G. C. Ingelow,
'13, and Manson, Prof. L. E. Bassett.
