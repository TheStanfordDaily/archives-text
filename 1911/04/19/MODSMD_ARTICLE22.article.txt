# Roble Seniors Will Entertain.
## 
###

Saturday afternoon at 3 o'clock by the
Seniors of Roble Club. An extensive
program is being planned by the hos
tesses, and the last meeting of the
fourth-year women before Senior Week
should prove an enjoyable one.
