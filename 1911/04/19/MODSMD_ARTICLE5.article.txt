# INTERSCHOLASTIC TENNIS DOUBLES WON BY LOWELL.
## 
### 
J. Strachan and W. Johnston of
Lowell High School defeated E. and W.
Fottrell of St. Ignatius College in the
finals of the Interscholastic doubles,
thereby winning the Interscholastic
Championship of California. The tour
nament was finished on Sunday at the
Golden Gate Park Courts in San Fran
cisco. Strachan, who won the singles
hero two weeks ago, played the star
game.
