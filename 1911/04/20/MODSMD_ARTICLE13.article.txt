# Vacation Work.

some literary ability and experience
meeting business men to gather ma
terial for a standard publication.
Write immediately, stating experience.
Information Bureau, 22 4- 6 Foxcroft
Bldg., 68 Post street, San Francisco.
Wanted —College girl with ability
to write, who has had experience in
reportorial work on newspaper or
magazines, for work in Bay cities
during summer vacation. Salary $20
per week. Reply, stating experience.
