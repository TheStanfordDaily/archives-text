# FACULTY WILL RECEIVE ENCINA CLUB TOMORROW.
## 
### 
Faculty men and students will meet
on equal footing tomorrow evening at
the Faculty Clubhouse when the annu
al Faculty reception will be tendered
Encina Club. A baseball game, an
open-air supper and a short program
will be the features of the evenigs en
tertaintent.
