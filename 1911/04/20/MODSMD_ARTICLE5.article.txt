# FRESHMEN AND JUNIORS WIN IN WOMEN'S BASKET BALL.
## 
### 
Twenty to four was the score of the
Freshman-Sophomore women's basket
ball game yesterday afternoon. This
was the second of ihe present Inter
class series, the first game having been
won by the Juniors over the Seniors by
a 17 to 10 score.
Tomorrow the Seniors meet the
Sophomores.
