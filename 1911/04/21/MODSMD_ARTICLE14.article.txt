# Prof. Marx Entertains M. E. Society.
## 
### 
The Stanford Mechanical Engineer
ing Society met recently at the
home of Professor G. H. Marx in Palo
Alto. They listened to a talk by their
host on "The Aim of an Engineering
Education," after which refreshments
were served. Stories by Professor E.
P. Leslie were the chief features of the
remainder of a most enjoyable evening.
