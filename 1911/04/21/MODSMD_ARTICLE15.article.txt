# Sword and Sandals Initiates.
## 
### 
Last evening, Sword and Sandals
held their annual initiation in San
Francisco. Five prominent Sopho
more thespians were initiated into the
honor society. A banquet was held
and after the initation the new and old
members took in"The Follies of 1910."
The men initiated were I.1. Ingrahani,
'13, S. Brown, '13, C. S. Turpin, '13, J.
H. Forbes, '13, and E. F. Kern, '13.
