# Crew Men Will Banquet Tonight.
## 
### 
This evening in San Francisco, the
crews of the University of California
and of Stanford will meet in a joint
banquet, at the Bismarck cafe. The
purpose of the banquet is to promote
boating and general good fellowship
between the oarsmen of the two uni
versities. All of the members of the
victorious Stanford crew will be pres
ent.
