# POSTPONED LECTURE ON "FEET AND FOOTWEAR" TOMORROW
## 
### 
"Feet and Footwear," the lecture to
have been delivered in the Chapel
some weeks ago by Dr. Emmet Rix
ford, Professor of Surgery in the
Stanford Medical College, but post
poned owing to the Junior Week fes
tivities, will be delivered tomorrow
evening at 8 o'clock. The talk will be
illustrated with lantern slides and
should prove interesting. Dr. Rixford
is described as an effective and en
tertaining speaker.
