# WILL PLAY PRESLEY'S STARS FOR KINDERGARTEN FUND.
## 
### 
Next Saturday, the Varsity baseball
team will journey to San Mateo, where
they will meet Presley's All-Stars in a
benefit game for the Free Kindergar
ten. The game will start at 2:30, and
will be played on Corporation Field.
While in San Mateo, the team will be
the guests of the Woman's Club of that
city. .T. H. Lanagan, 'Oft. who is taking
an active interest in the game, has
sent twenty-five tickets down to the
Bookstore where they have been put
on sale. The price of admission will
be twenty five cents.
