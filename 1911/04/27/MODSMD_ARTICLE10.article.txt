# Increased Interurban Service.
## 
### 
Effective Saturday, April 15th, in ad
dition to our regular schedule be
twen San Jose and Palo Alto, extra
cars will run Saturdays and Sundays
as follows:
Leave San Jose 10:00 a.m., 12 m., and
2:00 p.m. Leave Palo Alto 11:00 a.m.,
1:00 and 3:00 p.m.
On Sundays these extra cars from
Palo Alto will make connections at
Merklan Corners for Congress Springs,
in addition to the regular service to
Congress Springs.
Cars leaving San Jose for Congress
Springs at 11:35 a.m., 1:35, 3:35 and
4:35 p.m., and returning leaving Con
gress Springs at 12:47, 2:47, 4:47 and
5:30 p.m., have been made daily cars.
