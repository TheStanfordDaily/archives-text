# SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA WILL PLAY AT SAN JOSE HIGH.
## 
### 
The Russian Symphony Orchestra,
one of the foremost musical organiza
tions in the United States, will give
two concerts in San Jose tomorrow
afternoon and evening. The concerts
which will be held in the Assembly
hall of the high school, are given
under the auspices of that institution.
Special prices are to lie made to stu
dents, and tickets are on sale both at
the campus Bookstore and the Univer
sity Drugstore in Palo Alto.
