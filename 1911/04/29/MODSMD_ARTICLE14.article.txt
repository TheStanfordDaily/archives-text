# Carnival Will be Riot of Joy.
## 
### 
The side-shows and stunts that are
to he featured at. the big University
Carnival, May 18, are to he the hest
ever. The concessions are being snap
ped up rapidly and everything that is
to he staged will he high-grade and
worth going miles to see. From early
evening to midnight, the raucous tones
of the barker will overtop the din of
the merrymakers that will throng
Lasuen street.
The Senior Class is concentrating on
this big event to make it the greatest
joy-riot that was ever pulled off on
the Farm. There will be fifty-seven

varieties of fun-producting novelties,
from the nickie dancing in the frater
nity houses to the egg-throwing stunt.
Nobody can afford to pass up this great
Carnival.
