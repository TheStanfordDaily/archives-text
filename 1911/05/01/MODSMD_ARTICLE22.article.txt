# MISS J. ROWE AND WONDRIES WILL MARRY NEXT FRIDAY.
## 
### 
Miss Jessamine Rowe, '12, wili be
come the bride of Charles Wondries,
'08, next Friday morning at 11:30
o'clock. The wedding will take place
at the home of the bride's parents in
Redlands.
Miss Rowe was a member of Madro
no Club while at Stanford. Wondries
was a member of Sigma Alpha Epsilon
and the '08, Varsity Crew. He was also
president of Encina Club.
