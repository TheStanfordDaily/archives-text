# SENIOR WEEK PROGRAMS ON SALE AT BOOKSTORE.
## 
### 
Senior Week Programs are off the
press and are now on sale at the Stan
ford Bookstore. where they may he
purchased for twenty-five cents apiece.
They are very attractively printed in
gold leaf and brown and are most suit
able souvenirs of the commencement
exercises and festivities.
Invitations may yet be secured at
the Bookstore. All paying their as
sessment. may have five of these free
of charge. Additional ones may be
secured at five cents apiece.
