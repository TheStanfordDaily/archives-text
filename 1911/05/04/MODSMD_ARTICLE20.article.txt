# SENIOR BALL TICKETS MAY BE PURCHASED TO-MORROW.
## 
### 
Senior Ball tickets may be purchased
on the Quad to-morrow morning from
9:15 until 12:15 at the Law steps. This
sale will be the last on the Quad. The
committee earnestly requests that in
tending purchasers secure their tick
ets to-morrow as they must know at
once the number of couples to expect.
Arrangements for supper cannot be
completed until the number of dancers
is ascertained. The price of $(> per
ticket includes transportation.
