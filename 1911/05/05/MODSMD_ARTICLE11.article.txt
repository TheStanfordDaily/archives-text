# FINAL WOMEN'S LEAGUE MEETING HELD YESTERDAY.
## 
### 
The last meeting of the Women's
League for this semester was held at
Roble Hall yesterday afternoon. The
members were fortunate in having a
most delightful speaker present, Mrs.
M. Tnskeep, president of the Califor
nia Branch of the Collegiate Alumnae
Association. Mrs. Tnskeep outlined
the history, the growth and develop
ment of the orgainzation telling of the
great work of Miss Marion Talbot.
The speaker spoke of the college wo
man and her relation to democracy,
and urged that every girl graduating
from Stanford would become a mem
ber of the Collegiate Alumnae.
