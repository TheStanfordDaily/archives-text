# HEBREW DIVINE TO FILL UNIVERSITY PULPIT SUNDAY.
## 
### 
Rabbi Kaplan of the Hush Street
Synagogue in San Francisco will
preach next Sunday morning at 11
o'clock in the University Chapel. "In
heavenly love abiding," is the name of
the anthem to be sung by the choir and
Mr. Jouliu will sing a solo entitled,
"Meditation."
