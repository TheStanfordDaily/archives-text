# OFFICERS ELECTED BY MECHANICAL ENGINEERS.
## 
### 
Officers for the coming semester
were elected by the Mechanical Engi
neering Society at its regular meeting
last evening. C. H. Shattuck, '12, was
chosen as president, A. G. Budge, '12,
vice-president, and C. W. Scholefield,
'11, secretary-treasurer. L. S. King,
'12, C. Smith, '11, and B. C. Orange, '14,
were elected to membership. After the
close of the meeting, the society ad
journed to Wilson's.
