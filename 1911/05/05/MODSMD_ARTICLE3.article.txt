# C.E. Society Elects Officers.
## 
### 
Associated Civil Engineers recently
elected H. D. Owen, '11, president, C.
E. Tabor, '11, vice-president, and P. Y.
Dickinson '12, secretary.
New members of the association,
A. S. Brown, '12, P. Y. Dickinson, '12.
C. G. Walker, '11, and T. Sanborn, '12,
read papers on work on which they
have had experience.
