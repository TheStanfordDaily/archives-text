# WOMEN'S CLUB CORPORATION WILL MEET WEDNESDAY AT 4:30
## To Consider Constitutional Amendment and Elect Officers for Ensuing Year. 
### 
There will be a meeting of the Corpor
ation of the Stanford Women's Club on
Wednesday afternoon at 4:30 in the
Chapel. All those who are members
or who have only taken ont, pledge
cards are requested to attend. The
secretary and treasurer will give re
ports for the full year and the elec
tion of officers for the ensuing year
will be held. The following amend
ment will also be voted on:
Article I. Section 1.
"The corporate powers of this cor
poration shall be vested in a Board of
Ten (10) Directors who shall be mem
bers of the corporation and six (6)
shall constitute a quorum for the
transaction of business.
Section 11.
"There shall be three (3) Student-
Directors who shall be members of (he
corporation. They may be present at
meetings of the board of directors but
shall have no vote."
