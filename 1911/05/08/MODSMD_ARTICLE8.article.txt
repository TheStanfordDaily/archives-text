# WOMAN SUFFRAGE CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS FOR YEAR.
## 
### 
Officers for the coming year were
elected by the Stanford Chapter of the
Collegiate Suffrage Association this
afternoon, as follows: President, Miss
Gertrude Workman, '12; vice-president.
Mrs. .T. C. Branner; secretary-treasur
er, Miss Marjory Seeley; members of
the executive board. Miss Ruth Samp
son, '11, and Miss Gladys Gill, '12.
Mrs. E. W. Allen, Dr. I. McCracken
and Professor 1.. .T. Martin spoke inter
estingly to the society on the present

status of women suffrage in Califor
nia.
