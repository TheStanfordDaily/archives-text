# La
						Liana Elects Officers.
## 
### 
La Liana met this noon to choose
its officers and executive board for
next year. The election resulted as
follows: President, Mary Herron, 12;
vice-president, Mrs. Wing; secretary,
Florence Ober, '13; treasurer, Dorothy
Wood, '14. The executive hoard in
cludes, in addition to these officers,
R. Sydner, '12, Ethel Crosby, '13, Laura
Herron, '14, Marion MacLachlan, '12,
