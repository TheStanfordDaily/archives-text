# PHILOSOPHICAL CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS AT ANNUAL BANQUET.
## 
### 
J. E. Coover, '03, Assistant in Psych
ology, was elected president of the
Student's Philosophical Club last eve
ning, and W. Root, '12, was chosen
secretary.
The election was held at the second
annual banquet of the club at the
Stanford Inn. Retiring President
F. F. Wolff, '11, was the guest of honor.

Professors A. W. Moore and H. W.
Stuart were entertained by the club.
The former spoke in regard to the
work of the Philosophical Club at
the University of Chicago, while Dr.
Stuart addressed the members on
the "Ideal Philosophical Club."
