Trustees and Alumni have recently
cooperated to such an extent that
eating-houses for the Terrace clubs
are within sight. The
The Hating men need them and the
Houses. Alumni are doing their
best to get together a
workable method from the plans sub
mitted to them by the Hoard of Trus
tees. Whosever the blame was for the
delay in getting dining places to do
away with the inconvenience of the
Terrace houses, let all past things be
forgotten and everyone help to make
the eating houses a reality.
