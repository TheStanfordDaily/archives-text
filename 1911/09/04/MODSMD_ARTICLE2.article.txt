# GENEROUS GIFTS HAVE BEEN MADE TO THE UNIVERSITY.
## 
### 
Mrs. S. F. Lick, of Silver Springs,
Florida, presented to the University a
collection of finely polished wood
specimens from various parts of the
United States, also a collection of min
erals and shells.
A gift of $25 was received during the
summer from Mrs. Lucy A. Brown, of
Chicago, to be used for the purchase
of books relative to the history of
Peace, War and Arbitration.
