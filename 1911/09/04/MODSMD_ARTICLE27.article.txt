# Cupid Is Still Working.
## 
### 
The engagement is announced of J.
Young, '09, a member of Encina Club
and of the Civil Engineering Club and
assistant in the History Department,
to Miss S. McAllister, 09, President of
Roble in 1908.
Announcement has been made of
the marriage of Miss C. Larkins, '07,
of Santa Cruz, to Dr. C. Harper, the
ceremony having taken place August
16. The bride is enrolled in Kappa
Kappa Gamma.
The marriage of Miss E. Wallace,
'08, of Visalia, to A. G. Bryant, took
place in Visalia in June. The couple
now reside in Porterville. Mrs.
Bryant is a member of Kappa Kappa
Gamma.
