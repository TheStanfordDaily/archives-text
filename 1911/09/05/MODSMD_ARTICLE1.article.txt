# RECEPTION FOR FRESHMEN SATURDAY EVENING.
## Y.M.C.A. and Y.W.C.A. to Entertain Class of 1915 in Encina. Clubroom. 
### 
The Reception to the entering class
of 1915 and to all new men and women
students is to be given by the Y. M.
C. A. and the Y. W. C. A. in the Encina
Club Room, on next Saturday even
ing, September 9, at eight o'clock.
The University Orchestra will play
during the evening and everyone is
invited to attend. Invitations have
been sent to all the new students and
to the Faculty. The following have
been asked to act as patrons and
patronesses: Vice-President and Mrs.
.1. C. Branner, Dr. and Mrs. D. Charles
Gardner, Mrs. E. W. Allan, Mr. and
Mrs. .J. 0.. Griffin, Mr. and Mrs. R. L.
Green, Mr. and Mrs. O. M. Johnston,
Mr. and Mrs. O. L. Elliot, Mr. and Mrs.
J. E. Goodwin, Mrs. Fowler, Mr and
Mrs. A. M. Cathcart, Mrs. Baldwin,
Mrs. Buffum, Mrs. H. A. Moran, Mr.
and Mrs. Coolidge, Mr. and Mrs. C. H.
Gilbert, Mr. and Mrs. R. H. Campbell,
Mr. and Mrs. A. T. Murray, Mr. and
Mrs. H. H. Millis, Mr. and Mrs. O. M.
Easterday, Mr. and Mrs. R. R. Long,
Mr. and Mrs. C. E. Childs.
