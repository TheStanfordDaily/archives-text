# Freshman Class to Organize.
## 
### 
The first business meeting of the
Freshman Class will be held to
morrow, at 1:10 p.m. K. L. Schaupp,
'12, President of the Student Body,
urges that all freshmen attend. A
president for the class will be chosen
and organization for the year atended
to. President C. H. Heal, of the Juni
or Class, will help to get class func
tions in order.
