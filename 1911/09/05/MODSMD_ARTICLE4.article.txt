# Changes Made In Quad Staff.
## 
### 
Owing to the failure of several mem
bers of the 1913 Quad Board to return
to college Editor H. L. Loud has made
the following additions and changes
in the staff:
.Miss M. L. Gilbert will take the
place on the department of Soro
rities and Faculty vacated by Miss
M. B. Masters,
In the Josh department neither
K. C. Ingram nor T. Cronyn have re
turned. K. R. Levy and W. S. Wilson
have been chosen to take charge of
this important feature. Levy formerly
had the College Year but this depart
ment will now be taken care of by
W. C. Porterfiehl. L. Patterson is to
he the official photographer.
