# Jameson is Freshman President.
## 
### 
At their meeting this afternoon, the
Freshmen, under the supervision of
Student Adviser A. E. Roth. '09, in a
close contest, elected W. H. Jameson,
Jr., president of the Class by the mar-
Kin of two ballots. A. T. Stillar was
elected vice-president against Miss E.
Trent by a margin of but six votes.
Miss Helen Wallace was unanimously
elected secretary, and M. W. Allen
treasurer.
