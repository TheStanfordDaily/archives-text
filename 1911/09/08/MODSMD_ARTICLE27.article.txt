# Largo Enrollment in Physiology.
## 
###

the Physiology Department this year
will exceed two hundred, an increase
over the registration of last year.
Professor J. R. Slonaker has return
ed to the department, after a semes
ter's leave of absence.
The following assistants have been
appointed in the department: J. L.
Oliver, '11, K. L. Schaupp, '12, E. M.
Skolfield, '12, H. J. West, '12, and
R. L. Draper, '13.
