# Annual Reception at Encina Pleases.
## 
### 
Saturday evening the Stanford
branches of the Y. M. C. A. and Y. W.
C. A. gave their annual informal recep
tion to the students in the Club room
of Encina Hall. Between thirty and
forty young men and women, all of
whom wore badges and were well
acquainted with most, of the students,
formed a general introductory and
mixing committee. During the latter
part of the evening, punch was serveti
to all who desired it, and the gathering
terminated at about ten-thirty. The
continuous playing by the University
Orchestra furnished entertainment t
all who attended the event.
