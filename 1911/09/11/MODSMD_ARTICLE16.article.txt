# Glee-Club Tryouts Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Glee-club tryouts will be held to
morrow evening in room 68. All the
men in the University are eligible to
tryout for this organization. The lead
er and executive committee are look
ing for many new men. They are
needed, especially tenors. A long list
of new music has been received from
the eastern publishers, including both
popular and classical pieces. Many
trips for the club are assured for this
year.
