# Men's Meeting Night Changed.
## 
### 
Professor A. T. Murray, of the
Greek Department, will speak on
Wednesday evening, at 7:15, before the
Y. M. C. A. weekly meeting, in the
Men's room, on "The Religion of a
College Man." Special music for the
occasion will be furnished by the
Apollo Male Quartette. The change
of the mid-weekly meeting from Thurs
day to Wednesday night will be per
manent.
