# Senior Jolly-up Set For Saturday.
## 
### 
Jolly-ups are again in fashion.
Nineteen-twelve will hold the first
affair of this nature on Saturday even
ing; at S o'clock, in the Armory.
Preparations to make this a success
from every stand-point are complete.
The evening of Paul .loties and robber
dances to the sway of music from.1. L.
McDonald, 14, and his orchestra pro
mises to please all. The invitations
have been given especially to Hold
overs.. An admission of 50c will be
collected at the door.
