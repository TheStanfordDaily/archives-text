# New Officers For Stanford Art Club.
## 
### 
A well attended meeting of the
Stanford Art Club was held at noon
today, at which the following officers
were chosen for the ensuing term:
T. King, "12, president; Miss A. Wish
ard, '12, vice-president; Miss H. Evans,
'13, secretary; Miss F. Allen. '14,
treasurer. Prof. A. IV Clark, of the
Art Department, spoke to the members
of the club regarding their plans for
the future. No definite business was
carried out. Another meeting will he
held in the near future.
