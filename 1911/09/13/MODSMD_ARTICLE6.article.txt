# Y.W.C.A. Holds Opening Meeting.
## 
### 
The first meeting of Y. W. C. A. of
the year, yesterday afternoon, was well
attended by both new and old students.
The work of the organization was ex
plained by the President, Miss L.
Blanchard. Miss D. Stillman, '12. sang
a pleasing solo. The address of wel
come to the Freshmen was given by
Mrs. E. W. Allan, Dean of Women.
After the meeting the Cabinet and
the General Secretary, Miss Hutchins,
met the new members informally.
