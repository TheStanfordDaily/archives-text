# ENCINA COMMITTEES ARE APPOINTED BY BROWN.
## Social Affairs of Big Dormitory Will Be Managed By Four Groups. 
### 
F. C. Browii, '11, president of Encina
Club, has announced the names of the
men who will act on the various com
mittees in the big dormitory. The
personnel follows:
Invitations—B. C. Woodcock, Ml,
chairman; W. R. Chandler, M3, E. P.
Campbell, M3, R. W. Wilcox, M3, and
F. B. Watkins, M4.
Programs—'M. L. Ward. M2, chair
man; P. Y. Dickinson, M2, G. W.
Hawley, M3, K. Pyle, M3, and J. L.
McDonald, M4.
Social—M. E. Mitchell, Ml, chair
man; J. E. Woolley, M2, H. Endres, M2,
J. ,F. Argabrite, M2, and J. P. Cottrell,
M3.
Decoration —A. H. Frank, M3, chair
man; T. J. Speliman, M3, F. E. Fran
"erchi, M4, G. P. Ross, M4. and V. M.
Smith, M4.
