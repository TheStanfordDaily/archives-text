# Engagement Book is Still in Use.
## 
### 
The "engagement book" at the Reg
istrar's office, so useful last year in
nreventing conflicts in social affairs,
s still available this year. Everyone
planning to give mid-weekly affairs
of any kind, meetings of any sort in
a iy way connected with the Fniver
sitv. are expected to set their dates
down in this hook. It is understood
that this applies to all dances, faculty
and student, gatherings and any events
of a social nature.
