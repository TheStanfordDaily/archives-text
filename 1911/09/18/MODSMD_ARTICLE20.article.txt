# WELL-KNOWN BIBLICAL WRITER SPEAKS.
## "My Love Story" is Given in Place of Usual Sunday Morning Address. 
### 
"I do not intend to preach a ser
mon nor to give an address this morn
ing," said Dr. D. A. Hayes, to the con
gregation assembled in the University
Chapel, last Sunday morning. "Ra
ther," he continued. "I will tell you
a personal story, which may be called
'My Love Story,' with a text from the
twenty-first chapter of John."
