# Pasmores Will Appear.
## 
### 
The Pasniores. a trio of local musi
cians. will appear at the Palo Alto
Playhouse on Thursday evening, Sep
tember 21, at N:ir> p.m. The appear
ance will be the last one in the town,
as they are planning an extended trip
to the Fast in the near future.
