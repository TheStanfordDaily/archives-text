# Faculty Women's Club Organizes.
## 
### 
The Faculty Current Events Club
held its first meeting of the semester
yesterday afternoon at 3:30. Mrs.
J. E. Goodwin was elected to the office
of president of the club and Mrs. Gil
bert to that of secretary. The next
meeting will be held a week from next
Tuesday, at the home of Mrs. Payson
J. Treat.
The club is composed of ladies who
are connected with the faculty and
was organized with the aim to keep
its members in touch with what is hap
pening in the world. At the meet
ings current literature is discussed
and various members give talks on
contemporary politics and like sub
jects.
