# Will Open Skating Rink.
## 
### 
A first class roller skating rink will
be opened up in the old rink building
on Bryant street in Palo Alto, Wednes
day night by J, E. Struges and Gordon
D. Coak. The equipment of the new
amusement place will be complete in
every respect and arrangements will
be made for special parties. Mr. Stur
ges has had considerable experience
in the rink business and absolute
respectability is assured.
