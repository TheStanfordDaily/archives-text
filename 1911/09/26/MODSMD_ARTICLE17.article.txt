# Chapel Ministers.
## 
### 
In looking over the list of ministers
who will conduct the chapel service
this semester, the individual student

may. well see that it will
he to his advantage to hear
one or more of these men.
Stanford church services

are adapted peculiarly to the average
student, in that they are not denomin
ational. Add to this the opportunity
of hearing many of the best speakers
in California and it seems that no
two better pleas could be made for
attendance.
