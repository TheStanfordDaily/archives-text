# Dr. Aked Will Speak in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
Dr. Aked, of San Francisco, will
speak in Palo Alto a the Playhouse
on Monday evening at eight o'clock.
Dr. Aked is one of the foremost
speakers of the day and as he is
particularly interested in the ques
tion of the Equal Suffrage, he will
probably speak on thsi subject.
The Hand will be present at the
Playhouse, and the evening will be
one of active interest to everyone.
As the admission is free a large
attendance is expected.
