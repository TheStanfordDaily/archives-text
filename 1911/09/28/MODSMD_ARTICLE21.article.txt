Specijil Engagement of Popular
Tenor. Mr. Gerald F. Laughlin, so
favorably known in musical circles
of Stanford University and Palo Alto,
will take part in the concert at Con
gress Springs on Sunday afternoon,
singing two duet numbers with Miss
Isabelle Forcade, lyric soprano. Or
chestra under leadership of Mr. Geo.
d'Ablaing. The public are invited to
enjoy this musical treat. On Sun
days cars leave Palo Alto for Con
gress Springs hourly from 9 a.m. to
5 p.m. Round-trip excursion tickets
sold at S. P. Ticket Oftice, Palo Alto,
35 cts. No tickets sold on cars.
