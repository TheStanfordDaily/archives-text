# Australian Boys Visit Campus.
## 
### 
The campus received a visit this
morning from the boys of the Austra
lian League who are touring this
country. The boys saw (he campus
from automobiles and a sight-seeing
bus. There were about sixty of the
young Antipodeans who made the trip
down from San Francisco.
