# Phi Delta Kappa Initiates Three Men.
## 
### 
Phi Delta Kappa, the upperciass
education fraternity, last evening held
Its annual initiation in Fraternity
Hall, Palo Alto. The three men whe
were initiated were F. A. Scofield, '12
O H. Close, '12, and E. R. Knollin,
"12. After the initiation ceremonie?
a banquet was served.
