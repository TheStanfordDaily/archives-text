# Y.W.C.A. to Hold Service in Vestry.
## 
### 
Xext week the Young Women's
Christian Association will hold its reg
ular four-thirty Vesper Service in the
Vestry of the Memorial Church. This
plan was announced at yesterday's
meeting. The association is looking
forward to many being present at the
new place of meeting.
The Social Service committee also
announced that all contributions for
the box for the Indians should be left
at the Delta Gamma house promptly,
since it is hoped to send the box by
the end of October.
The speaker of the afternoon. Rev
erend R. A. Campbell, of Palo Alto,
gave an inspiring talk from the Hook
o. Ruth.
