# Fencing Society to Jolly-up.
## 
### 
Misses R. Boyd, '14. and M. H.
Volkmor, '13, were appointed feed
committee at the meeting of Foil and
Mask, last night, for the fencing so
ciety's jolly-up, to be held at French
man's dam next Wednesday evening.
Besides this committee, S. H. Frank,
'15, C. Ollcott, '15, and B. Barzen, 'ir>,
were appointed on the labor com
mittee. The society decided to have
pins made for the members, the value
of the pins to be $3.50 each.
