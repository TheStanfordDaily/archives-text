# Sermon, Sunday, to be by Chaplain.
## 
### 
Dr. D. Charles Gardner will preach
Sunday morning, at the University
Chapel, on "The Bible —Its Nature and
its Message." Mrs. A. F. Rogers will
sing a solo, "Behold the Master," by
N. .1. Hammond. The choir will ren
der the beautiful anthem, "Praise ye
the Father." K. L. Schaupp, '12, is
to read the scripture lesson.
