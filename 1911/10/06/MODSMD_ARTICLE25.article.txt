# Durand Talks on Aqueduct.
## 
### 
Professor W. F. On rand, of the
Mechanical Engineering Department,
gave an interesting talk. Wednesday
night, to the M. K. Society on the
construction and power of the Los
Angeles Aqueduct. His statistics were
illustrated by charts showing differ
ent section's of the work.
