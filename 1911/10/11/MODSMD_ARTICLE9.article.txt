# Vestry Room Meeting a Success.
## 
### 
'•Jesus Christ, the same yesterday,
today, and forever," was the subject
of Miss Helen Salisbury's inspiring
tiik at yesterday's Vesper service of
the Young Women's Christian Associ
ation.
The experiment of holding the meet
ing in the Vestry Room of the Mem
orial Church was so much appreci
ated that it is intended to hold the
services there regularly.
It was announced that. Miss Evan
geline Booth will not speak at Stan
ford.
