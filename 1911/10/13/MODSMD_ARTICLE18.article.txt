# Philological Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
The first meeting of the year of the
Philological Association was held last
night. Professor H. R. Fairclough, of
the Latin Department, was
President and Professor W. D. Briggs,
of the English Department, secretary.
An interesting paper was given by
Professor A. G. Newcomer on "The
Method of Self-Interpretation as Ap
plied to Shakespeare."
