# Orchestral Rehearsals Encourage.
## 
### 
Next week work will be started to
orchestrate the music for the football
show to be played by the orchestra
on November 11. Rehearsing for the
music which the Orchestra will fur
nish for the English Club Play
"Compromise," was started at the re
hearsal on Wednesday night . In
creased interest seems to be manifest
in the organization as was shown by
the large number who appeared a'
the practice. Leader .T. Paul Miller,
'14, directed the Orchestra for the
first time on Wednesday night.
