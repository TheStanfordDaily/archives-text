# Bishop Nichols to Preach at Chapel.
## 
### 
Bishop Nichols, of San Francisco,
head of the California diocese, will
preach at the University Chapel, Sun
day morning. The special music will
be a solo, "O Divine Redeemer," by
Mrs. V. V. Harrier and an anthem.
"Chorus of the Messenger," by. the
University choir. The service will
commence at 11 o'clock.
