# 1911 Graduate Dies in Columbus.
## 
### 
While on the way to Columbus,
Nebraska, where he was to enter the
practice of the law with his uncle,
the news comes that (J. E. La Grange,
11, of Redlands, was attacked by an
incurable spinal disease. He died on
September 21, a few days after reach
ing Columbus. The disease was of a
very serious nature and his illness
lasted only one week. La Grange was
21 years old, and was buried on
September 2S at Lacon, Illinois.
While at Stanford La Grange was
one of the best students in the Law
Department and was elected to
membership in Phi Beta Kappa last
spring.
