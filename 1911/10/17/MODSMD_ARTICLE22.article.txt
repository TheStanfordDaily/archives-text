# College Notes.
## 
### 
Columbia University will begin crew
training on the Hudson next Monday.
Columbia University has secured the
services of Norman Cox, the crack
Australian swimmer, for coach of the
swimming team.
Ninety candidates turned out for the
initial fall crew work at Pennsylvania.
Fully fifty more are expected in a few
days. Fifty of the ninety were fresh
men.
