# English Club To Elect New Members.
## 
### 
Instructor T. H Cross, of the Econ
omics Department, will give a talk
at the semi-monthly meeting of the
English Club to be held Wednesday,
October IS. at eight o'clock. His sub
ject will be the "Negro Point of View
Upon Lawrence Dunbar." Several
new members will lie elected to the
Club at the business meeting preced
ing the lecture.
