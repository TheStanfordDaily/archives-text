# Announcements.

Press Club—Will meet usual time
and place.
Gym Club—Regular meeting, at
7:30, Thursday night.
Philosophy Club —Will meet Monday
at 4:40, in room 483.
Cosmopolitan Club—Will meet in
room 93, Encina, Monday, 8 p.m.
Senior Women—Meet in room 202
on Friday, at 1:10. Very important.
Junior Men—Plugs have arrived at
the Regent. Secure them as soon as'
possible.
Plug Ugly Committee—Will meet in
room 22, Encina, Friday, 2 p.m. Very
important.
Mandolin Club—Will practice in
Engineering Building, Thursday even
ing at 7:30.
Junior Women — All interested in
Basket Ball meet at athletic field,
Friday, at 4:45. Election of captain.
Stnnfonl Trampers—Will meet at
Roble Gymnasium, Saturday, at 9 a.m.,
to walk to the redwoods. Bring
lunch.
Sophomores—Freshmen. — Tryouts
for places on interclass debating team
will be held Tuesday, October 24, in
Chapel.
French Club—Will meet at the
house of Professor O. M. Johnston,
4 Alvarado row, next Thursday, Octo
ber 19, at 7:30.
Stanford Women—All interested in
playing the mandolin or guitar meet
at 7:30 p.m., Tuesday, October 24, in
room 84. Instruments not needed.
January Graduation Candidates—
Call at Registrar's Office for blanks
to be filled out and filed with the Reg
istrar not later than November 1.
English SO—An examination will be
held for those conditioned in English
30, 2d semester, 1910-11. at 2 p.m.,
Friday, October 27, in room 205.—
W. D. Briggs.
