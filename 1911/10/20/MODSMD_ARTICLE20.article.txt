D.P.A. News Editors—Meet Daily
office, Sunday noon.
Stanford Trampers—Will not tramp
to-morrow, as planned.
D.P.A. Staff Freshmen—Meet at
Daily office, Monday, 1 p.m.
Cosmopolitan Club—Will meet in
room 93, Encina, Monday, 8 p.m.
Junior Men—Plugs have arrived at
the Regent. Secure them as soon as'
possible.
Glee Club—Men going to Belmont
rehearse at Phi Delta Theta house,
Saturday, 11 a.m.
l'lug Ugly—Meeting, Monday after
noon, at 2 p.m. at the Delta Upsilon
house. Very important.
Geology and Mining Men—Attend
Smoker at Alpha Tau Omega house,
Wednesday, October 25.
Madrono Reception—Invitations is
sued for October 27, are re-called be
cause of banquet to President Jordan.
Stanford Trampers—Will meet at
Roble Gymnasium, Saturday, at 9 a.m.,
to walk to the redwoods. Bring
lunch. ,
Sophomores—Freshmen. — Tryouts
for places on interclass debating team
will be held Tuesday, October 24, in
Chapel.
Stanford Women—All interested in
playing the mandtflin or guitar meet
at 7:30 p.m., Tuesday, October 24, in
room 84. Instruments not needed.
January Graduation Candidates—
Call at Registrar's Office for blanks
to be filled out and filed with the Reg
istrar not later than November 1.
Philosophy Club—Meets Monday,
October 23, at 4:40, in room 403,
Psychology department. All interest
ed invited. Topic: "Critics of Prag
matism."
English 30—An examination will be
held for those conditioned in English
30, 2d semester, 1910-11, at '2 p.m.,
Friday, October 27, in room 205.—
W. D. Briggs.
