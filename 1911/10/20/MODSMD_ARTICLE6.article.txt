# Sermon by Dr. Gardner on Sunday.
## 
### 
Chaplain D. Charles Gardner will
preach at the University Chapel, Sun
day morning at'11 o'clock. His sub
ject will be, "Social Morality the
Basis of Judgement."
The special music will consist of a
violin solo by Mr. E. Joullin, an
anthem by the choir, "Choral from St.
Paul," and the Canticle, "Jubilate."
