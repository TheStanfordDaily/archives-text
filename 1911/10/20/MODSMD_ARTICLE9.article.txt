# Botany Class Will Study at Grove.
## 
### 
On November 4 the class in Botany
I will journey to Pacific Grove, where
the afternoon will he devoted to a
study of sea life along the heach.
The class will leave on a morning
train, the members returning at their
own convenience.
All persons interested are invited
to accompany the party, and should
notify Professor S. J. Pierce, of the
Botany Department.
A one-way rate for the round trin
will be obtained if the party numbers
one hundred or more.
