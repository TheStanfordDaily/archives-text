# Philanthropy Will Be Lecture Subject.
## 
### 
The first open Social Service meet
ing of the year will be held next Mon
day evenin. Reverend C. F. Blaisdell
of Redlands, who is to preach here
on Sunday, will make the evening's ad
dress. His subject will be "Practical
Philanthropy."
Plans for regular meetings for those
interested in Social Service will be dis
cussed after the lecture. A large
attendance is expected of all members
of the University, who have an interest
in this important question. The meet
ing will be in the Chapel at eight
o'clock.
