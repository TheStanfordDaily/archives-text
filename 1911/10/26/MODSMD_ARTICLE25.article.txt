# Football Show Rehearsal Held.
## 
### 
A preliminary rehearsal for the
Football Show, the "Follies of Stan
ford," was held yesterday afternoon at.
the Delta Gamma house. According
to Chairman .T. H. Forbes, '13, who
has the show in charge things are pro
gressing satisfactorily and insure a
successful performance.
Miss T. Wyatt, of San Francisco, is
coaching the dancing, which is to be
made an important feature of the
performance.
The original music is catchy and
new, and many good voices are
assured.
Regular rehearsals with the skits
worked into connected form will begin
next week.
