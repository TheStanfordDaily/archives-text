# Australian Boys Visit Campus.
## 
### 
The Australian Boys League visited |
the campus today as the guests of the
Student Body. After spending the
morning viewing the various points
of interest around the University the
boys were taken to the fraternity
houses for dinner.
In departing Lieutenant J. J. Simons,
who is in charge of the Antipodeans'
tour to this country, voiced the appre
ciation of the visit to Stanford. "We
have had unending pleasure," he said,
'while on our visit and everyone has
been so kind and willing to help us
at every point."
