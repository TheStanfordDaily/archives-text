# Roth's Rugby Rouses Rooters.
## 
### 
Two full teams again reported yes
terday afternoon to A. E. Roth '09,
for the second game of the so-called
"Squad A" rugby. The game was hot
ly contested, and furnished interest to
both players and audience, that of the
spectators being slightly tinged with
amusement A number of players
green to the game made their appear
ance, and received their first intro
duction to rugby
These games, which are open to all
Stanford men, are exciting increased
interest, and several players of ability
have expressed their intention of join
ing the squad in the near future
Tuesday and Thursday afternoons and
Saturday mornings have been fixed as
the times of the games.
