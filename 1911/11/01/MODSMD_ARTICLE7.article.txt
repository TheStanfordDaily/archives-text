# ENTHUSIASM RUNS RIOT AT IMPROMPTU RALLY.
## Rugby Spirit Bursts Forth in Spontaneous Serpentine on Campus Last Evening. 
### 
Unable to hold in check the tre
endoua rug'y enthusiasm that ha
been pervading the campus atmos
phere for the last week, the entire
male population turned out to par
ticipate in a spontaneous rally last
night. The affair started in Encina
Hall and within a few minutes the
Hall was vacated and its inhabitants
serpentined the Row. Here the yell
ing crowd was swelled until the root
ers filled all the available space
around the S. A. E. house.
The crowd surrounded the house and
called for the team. After speeches
had been made by the different mem
bers of the squad and others, the
crowd noisily broke up.
