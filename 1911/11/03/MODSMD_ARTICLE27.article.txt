# Christian Science Lecture.
## 
### 
There will be a lecture on Christian
Science at the Armory, on Monday,
November 6, at 8 p.m. The Speaker
will be Bliss Knapp, C. S. B. (Harvard,
'04), of Boston, Mass. Mr. Knapp is
a member of the Board of Lectureship
of The First Church of Christ, Scient
ist, in Boston, Mass., and is prepared
to speak authoritatively on this sub
ject.
