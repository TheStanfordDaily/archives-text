# 1910 Men Will Banquet in San Jose.
## 
### 
In accordance with the custom estab
lished last year, the men of the 1910
class will gather next Friday night,
at 7:30, at the St. James hotel in San
Jose, for their annual dinner before
the Big Game. Permanent Class Sec
retary J. D. Houser desires every man
of the class to spread the news of the
reunion and all who expect to
attend to communicate with him at
once. His postoffice address is Box
1155, Stanford University. The ban
queters are asked to be at the meeting
place as early Friday evening as pos
sible. The dinner will be over in time
to catch the 10:30 train for the cam
pus and San Francisco.
