# Injury to Cass Not Serious.
## 
### 
L. Cass '13, the premier Varsity
half back, who was withdrawn from
Saturday's contest, is not seriously in
jured. Recent advices from the Pen
insula Hospital, to which Cass was re
moved, state that he suffered only a
strained ankle. He will no doubt bp
included in the line-up of November
11.
