# Boost the Varsity.
## Miss Lee Addresses Y.M.C.A. 
### 
Miss E. I). Lee. new general secre
tary of the San Francisco association,
addressed the meeting of the Y. W. C.
A. yesterday on the development of
the city association and the field of
usefulness it offers to college women.
A violin solo by Miss M. Curtner '13,
completed the afternoon service.
The speaker for next week will be
Hishop Nichols, of San Francisco.* lie
is the Episcopal Bishop of this diocese,
and has just returned from a trip
around the world. ITis subject will
be his personal summary of world
missionary work.
