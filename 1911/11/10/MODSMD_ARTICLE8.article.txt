# Askman Will Referee Soccer Match.
## 
### 
James Askman of the California
Football league lias been choosen to
hold the whistle in to-morrow morn
ing's contest between the Stanford and
California soccer elevens. He is one
of the most thorough arbitrators in
the game.
The game will be called at 10:30
on the gymnasium field. No admis
sion will be charged for seats on the
bleachers.
