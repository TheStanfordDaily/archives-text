# Breakers Organize Rugby Team.
## 
### 
In expectation of a post-season rug
by series, the Hreakers have organized
a team.. C. C. Taylor has been elected
captain and A. W. Ambrose, '13, will
officiate as manager. J. O. Miller, '08,
will coach this team.
No definite steps toward an inter
club football series have as yet been
taken, but it is expected that the sub
ject will be brought up at the next
meeting of the Executive Committee
on Wednesday evening.
