# WITH THE COLLEGES
## 
### 
The manager of the Northwestern
barber shop offered a free hair-cut to
every man who plays against Chicago,
provided Northwestern wins.
Princeton is considering the build
ing of a theater.
It is rumored at Princeton that Dr.
John M. Finney of Johns Hopkins
hospital will succeed Dr. Woodrow
Wilson as president of the University.
The University of Illinois has let
contracts for the erection of a Com
merce Building and a Woman's Build
ing.
Columbia University will give even
ing courses in commerce accounts and
finance next fall.
