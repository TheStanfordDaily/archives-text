# WITH THE COLLEGES
## 
### 
Fraternities at Cornell have agreed
not to pay over certain prices for
carriage service during Junior week.
In the East not a single team of
the "big ones" can claim a clean
slate. They have all taken a tumble
or two and there are likely to be
some mix-ups in awarding the title.
A new rule has gone into effect at
Columbia which forbids smoking in
any form. Formerly the smokers
have had free reign and it is expected
that quite an army of proctors, will
be needed to enforce the rule,
Princeton will award a "P" to mem
bers of the cross-country team finish
ing first in the Yale-Princeton meet
or among the first ten in the inter
collegiates.
