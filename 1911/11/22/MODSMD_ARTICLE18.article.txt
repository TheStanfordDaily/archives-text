# WITH THE COLLEGES
## 
### 
The average age of students at the
University of Nebraska is 24 years.
Massachusetts "Tech" has done
away with hazing, after a two years
fight.
The University of Virginia inter
fraternity council has decided to post
pone all rushing until after January 1
of each college year.
There will be no more class officers
at the Yale Sheffield Scientific School
but the Chairman of the Student Coun
cil will preside over all meetings.
In England there are over 16,000
regularly constituted rugby clubs play
ing a schedule of games throughout
the season.
