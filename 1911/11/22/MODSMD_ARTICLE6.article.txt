# COMPANY I. TO PRESENT SNAPPY ENTERTAINMENT
## Stanford Gym, Glee and Mandolin Clubs to Figure in Program Thursday Evening. 
### 
Company L, Fifth Infantry of the
National "Guard of California, will pre
sent an exceptionally fine program,
including vocal, instrumental and mili
tary features on to-morrow night in
the Armory. About one-fourth of the
company's membership is made up of
Stanford men. The Gym Club has
been preparing for some time for
their part of the program. The Glee
and Mandolin Clubs have arranged
several good numbers. It Is a positive
thing that the evening's event will take
place as the Board of Health of Palo
Alto has lifted the ban on all public
gatherings. The proceeds will so to
wards buying gymnasium apparatus
for Company L.
