# ENCINA "AT HOME" WILL BE GIVEN DECEMBER 1.
## 
### 
The Encina "At Home" will be held
the night of Friday, December 1. two
weeks later than the date originally
chosen. The dance was postponed at.
the request of the University author
ities as a precautionary measure
against the spread of the seeming
epidemic of infantile paralysis in Palo
Alto.
