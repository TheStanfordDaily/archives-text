# La
						Follette Supporters to Organize.
## 
### 
Following the example of the sup
porters of Woodrow Wilson, students
who favor La Follette as the next
Republican candidate for President,
will meet to-morrow afternoon at 1:15
in the lecture room of the Law build
ing for organization. At this meeting
delegates will be elected to the State
convention of La Follette clubs to be
held in San Francisco, Saturday, at
the Palace Hotel.
