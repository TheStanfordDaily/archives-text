# Zinsser Addresses Chemistry Society.
## 
### 
Professor H. Zinsser, of the Medical
department, last Tuesday gave an in
formal talk to the members of the
Mini Kaph Mini, the Chemistry honor
society, at a meeting held at. the Alpha
Tau Omega house. The topic of the
speech was along chemical lines and
was interesting as well as Instructive.
The meeting was the regular session
of the society, but no business was
transacted.
