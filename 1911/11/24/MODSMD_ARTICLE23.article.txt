# WITH THE COLLEGES
## 
### 
At the present time there are forty
colleges headed by Yale graduates.
The women at the University of
Kansas went down to the station en
masse to cheer the team as they left
for the Minnesota game.
The faculty at Denver University
are considering the plan of having the
moving picture shows in Denver throw
law cases on the screen.
