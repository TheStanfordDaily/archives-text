# Reconstruction Crew is Increased.
## 
### 
The foreman in charge of the Mem
orial Church reconstruction has a crew
of twelve men at work on the cement
foundation piers and heavy floor gir
ders. The force will be doubled the
first of next week, and by December
1 a gang of thirty-five or forty men,
stone cutters and steel workers, will
be employed on the job. Tt is prob
able that this number will be retained
throughout the winter.
