# Nusbaum Wins chess Tournament.
## 
### 
B. R. Nusbaum won the fall chess
tournament for novices at the last
meeting of the Chess Club, and was
awarded a handsome set of chess men
as a prize. Dr. Emanuel Lasker,
World's Chess Champion, who was to
have given an exhibition here, has
cancelled all his engagements on this
The club hopes to secure Capa
Blanca, the Cuban champion who re
cently created a sensation in Eupore,
for an exhibition game early next
semester. If they do not succeed in
this, Mr. Brown. Pacific Coast Cham
pion, will visit the campus.
