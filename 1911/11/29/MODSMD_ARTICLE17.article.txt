# Stanford Play Rugby at San Jose.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon the football
team which played for San Jose high
school in the fall of 1908 will play
a game with the present high school
team on the Santa Clara field. G. D.
Worswick, '13, who was captain of
the team that season, will play half:
1). 11. Sim, '14, first five; P. W. McMil
lan. '14. center three-quarters; and L.
Keesling, '14, substitute. F. W. Reeves,
'14, will referee the contest.
