# Wiliamson Soccer Trophy Finished.
## 
### 
C. Y. Williamson, of the Barbarian
club, who has offered a handsome
challenge cup for a series of soccer
matches between Stanford and Cali
fornia, has notified H. W. Maloney,
coach of the Cardinal eleven, that the
trophy will be finished by the jeweler
within the next few flays. The terms
governing the awarding of the cup
have been agreed upon. The winner
of the trophy will be decided by a
series of three matches. One match
is to he played each year. The first
contest is scheduled for Washington's
birthday. Permanent possession of
the cup is to be gained by the winner
of the best two out of three matches.
