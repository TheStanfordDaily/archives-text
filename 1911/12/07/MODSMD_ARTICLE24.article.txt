# Hummer and Coffin Elects New Men.
## 
### 
H. H. Beall, '11, E. E. Manning, '13.
and Ft. E. Anderson, '13, were elected
to the Hammer and Coffin Society and
the Chaparral staff at a meeting of
the former held last evening. The
new men have proven themselves to
be fully qualified for the places after
a tryout lasting a year. Beall is a
contributor of fiction, Manning of art,
and Anderson will be assistant to the
business manager.
