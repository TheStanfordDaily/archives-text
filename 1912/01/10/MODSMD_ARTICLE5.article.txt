# Celebrate Silver Wedding.
## 
### 
Registrar and Mrs. O. L. Elliott
celebrated their silver wedding anni
versary on December 28. On this oc
casion they were hosts to almost a
hundred of their friends at their new
home on San Juan avenue. Doctor
and Mrs. David Starr Jordan, Doctor
and Mrs. J. C. Rranner, and Mrs.
Charles Lathrop were among the
foremost to extend congratulations to
the registrar and his wife.
Dr. and Mrs. Elliott were married
twenty-five years ago in Marathon,
New York. They have spent most of
their married life at Stanford, becom
ing campus residents when the Uni
versity was opened in 1891.
