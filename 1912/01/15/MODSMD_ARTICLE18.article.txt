# Women to Meet Next Week.
## 
### 
On account of the general confusion
of the first week of college, the regu
lar monthly meeting of the Women's
League has been postponed until next
Thursday afternoon. This first meet
ing of the semester will be held par
ticularly for the benefit of the new
girls coming into the University and
all are urged to attend. The meet
ing will be social in its nature and a
special program is being prepared,
the details of which will be announced
later.
