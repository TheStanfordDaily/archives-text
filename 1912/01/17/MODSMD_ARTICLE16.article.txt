# EXECUTIVE INSTALLED BY NESTORIAN ORATORS.
## 
### 
M. B. Henshaw, '12, was installed in
the office of president of Nestoria
Literary Society, Tuesday evening, by
E. E. Tincher, '12, president for the
past semester, in the first meeting
of the year. Henshaw outlined the
policy which he would follow in try
ing to build up a larger and more ac
tive club of men. He expressed his
appreciation at being chosen to fill the
office and showed the men the value
of debating here at Stanford.
After Henshaw's talk, interesting
discussions were heard from O. H.
Close, '12, E. E. Tincher, '12, and F.
Uelcher, '13, on topics of current event.
