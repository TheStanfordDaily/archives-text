# WITTER IS AGAIN TO COACH CREWS AT CALIFORNIA.
## 
### 
D. Witter will have charge of the
coaching of the California crews again
this season. He was captain and
stroke of the 190S crew and coach of
the 1909 eight. The announcement
was made yesterday by Captain May
nard. A coaching committee will be
appointed in the next few days to
assist Coach Witter.
