# SECOND YEAR ACTORS BEGIN REHEARSALS.
## 
### 
Rehearsals for the annual produc
tion of the Sophomore class, which
this year will be the sparkling comedy,
"Christopher, Jr.," began this after
noon. The basement of the engineer
ing building has been selected as the
place for the preliminary work of the
nlayers. F. L. Mathieu, of San Fran
cisco, is in charge of the instruction
of the second year actors. Many of the
lines of the respective characters
have already been learned and the
work of drilling the cast in the dram
atic art will be taken up immediately.
