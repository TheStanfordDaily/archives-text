# SUCCESS OF BANQUET TO DOCTOR JORDAN ASSURED.
## 
### 
More than two hundred and fifty
tickets for the Freshman banquet on
next Friday evening have been sold
to date, and the success of the affair
has been practically assured. In or
der that all may be able to purchase
tickets, the time limit has been ex
tended over Thursday, when the sale
will positively be closed as the caterer
must he notified at that time of the
lumber attending.

The three other clashes of the Uni
versity will also be represented at the
banquet. An invitation has been ex
tended to the presidents and secre
taries of the Sophomore, Junior and
Senior classes to attend the function.
The Freshmen will meet Thursday
afternoon to nominate officers for
this semester.
