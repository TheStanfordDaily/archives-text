# Will Start to Lay Floors.
## 
### 
Work was renewed with vigor
today, on the Memorial Church,
when the electric machine just in
stalled for mixing concrete, started to
churn. The work of putting up the
foundation and laying the floor will
lie rushed. Steel ribs have been put
i'.i to strengthen the walls and heavy
teel wire will support the floor.
