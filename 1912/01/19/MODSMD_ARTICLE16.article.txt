# IT WAS WRITTEN NONE SHOULD ABIDE THERE.
## 
### 
There is a magic couch over in En
cina Hall. If you don't believe it, ask
any of the 150 Encina man who tested
it hist night. "For it was written that
none could abide thereon," in the
words of the prophet.
A mock trial of Freshmen was the
lure. Many came, and stayed, but not
on the couch. Scarcely had the un
suspecting victim reclined 011 the dow
ny softness of the couch than he vault
ed, without the assistance of a pole,
the ceiling only limited the ascent.
"Dad" Moulton is said to be thinking
of installing the couch in the training
house to infuse "pep."
A certain editor consented to serve
on the jury. When he took his place,
he went through the evolutions of a
decapitated chicken, until he rolled
off on the floor. Grinning sheepishly,
he stole out in the hall to seek an
understudy in his little poultry ec
centricity speciality.
There are some who try to explain
the witchery of the couch by the
theory of galvanic curents. I hit they
are electrical engineers and not to be
listened to.
