# Soccer Teams Meet Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Stanford's soccer eleven will play
in the next to the last game 011 the
schedule of the California football
league against the Vampires on the
Encina gymnasium field tomorrow
afternoon. H. W. Maloney, who will
act as referee, will call the contest
at 3:45. The following is the line-up
of the Cardinal team: Lyne, goal; Mc-
Cann, Higgins, full; Clawsson, Rey
nolds, King, halves; Shelton, A. Erb,
B. Erb, Pellas, Fyfe, forwards.
