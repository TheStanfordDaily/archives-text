# SEVEN SINGERS ADDED TO RANKS OF GLEE CLUB.
## 
### 
Seven candidates won places on the
Glee Club at the tryouts last night.
Vacancies in the Club were in the
tenor and second-bass divisions, and
the men chosen will qualify in these
positions. Those successful were:
Tenors—H. E. Patter, 'If., W. C.
Lynch, '14, K. Roberts, *15, J. M. Dodd,
'14, and C. Miller, '14.
Basses —A. C. Dimon, '14, and V.
Sheldon, '15.
B. H. Lasky, '12, was also selected
and will take part in the "stunt" fea
tures of the club.
