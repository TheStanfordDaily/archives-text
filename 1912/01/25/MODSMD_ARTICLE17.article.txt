# POWER HOUSE WILL BE FINISHED BY MAY.
## 
### 
It is certain that the new power
house, behind the ruined library, will
be finished by May. This will leave
vacant, the boiler and power houses
and it is here that the Mechanics of
Materials testing laboratory will be
stationed. It is expected that behind
these buildings there will be an ex
perimental hydraulic laboratory, for
which tentative plans are being drawn
up.
The Mechanical Engineering depart
ment may also put up a hydraulic
power plant in this neighborhood.
The plans for the hydraulic testing
laboratory are being made by Instruc
tor Moser under the direction of Pro
fessor Wing. The hydraulic laboratory
will supplement the work in hydraulic
engineering in the same way in which
the laboratory in Mechanics of Materi
als supplements the course of that
name.
