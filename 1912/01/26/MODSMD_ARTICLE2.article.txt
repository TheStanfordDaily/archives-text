# Forestry Experts to Address Club.
## 
### 
E. G. Dudley, '08, and S. B. Show,
'08, will speak before the Forestry
Club next Monday evening on their
work in the government forest reserve.
Dudley is now in the Sierra Reserve,
and is in charge of the conservation
work of the government there. Show
is at present employed in the Shasta
Reserve.
The meeting will he held in the lec
ture room of the Rotany department.
