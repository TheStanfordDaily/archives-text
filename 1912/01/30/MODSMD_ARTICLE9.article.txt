# Chinese Decorate Professor Drake.
## 
### 
Professor N. F. Drake is the recipi
ent of a medal from the Chinese
government for his research work in
Zoology.
The medal is in the form of a double
dragon. There are five different or
ders, the first of which is reserved for
the Imperial officers. The second or
der is reserved for customs' officials,
while the honor bestowed upon Profes
sor Drake is the first grade of the
third order.
