# Boxers Will Meet Next Month.
## 
### 
Hoxing enthusiasts will be given an
opportunity to show their skill during
the coming month, when the annual
contests will be held at the Encina
gymnasium. Every man in the Uni
versity has been assigned to a divi
sion according to his weight. The list
is on the wall of the boxing gallery
of the gymnasium. All men, whether
interested in gymnasium work or not,
are eligible to enter. The contests will
open during the first week of March
and continue until the third.
