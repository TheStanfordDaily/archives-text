# PR. JORDAN CONTRIBUTES TO POPULAR MONTHLY.
## 
### 
The February issue ot' the Popular
Science Monthly contains an abstract
of a speech by President Jordan on,
"The Relations of Japan and the Uni
ted States," given at Clark University
on a recent lecture tour through the
east.
Dr. Jordan gives a short history of
our relations with the Island Empire,
and then seeks to prove, by giving the
present attitude of the Japanese to
word America, their hope of expansion
in the Far East, and, with the generad
anti-war feeling of the people, the im
possibility of war between the two
nations.
