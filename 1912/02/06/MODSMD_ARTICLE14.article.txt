# ENTRIES FOR STOREY BELT CLOSE WEDNESDAY.
## 
### 
Entries for the handball tournament
for the University championship and
the Storey trophy will close tomorrow
afternoon. All who expect to enter
the contest, which will begin on Satur
day, should sign their name to the list
posted for that purpose at Encina
gymnasium before it closes tomorrow
afternoon. Already twenty-one have
signified their intention of competing
for the handball belt. These include
the best players at the Irish game in
college.
