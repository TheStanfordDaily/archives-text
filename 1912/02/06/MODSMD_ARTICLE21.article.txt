# German Club Discusses Rule.
## 
### 
Discusion of a rule to drop from
membership all who are absent from
three meetings in succession occupied
the attention of the German Club at
its recent meeting. It was decided to
enforce this regulation in tne future.
New members elected at this meeting
were Miss C. L. Schaaf, '11, Miss G. A.
Miller, *12, and F. J. Smiler, '13.
