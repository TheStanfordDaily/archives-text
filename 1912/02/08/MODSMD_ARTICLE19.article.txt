# Campus Cooks Caused Consternation.
## 
### 
No longer do the festal Celestials
root the oblong ball nor swat the slip
pery sphere. The Kappa cook looks
long and lingers oft before the peril
ous pass from kitchen porch to domi
cile. The Inn chefs keep a ready exit
open and all possible entrances closed.
The reason? Xing of the Inn weni
down to San Jose. Ah! Naughty Ning!
The sheriff took him in. Oh! My!
It was a gun. Alas, the tongs are out.
