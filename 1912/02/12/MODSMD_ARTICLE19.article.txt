# Sermon by Oakland Divine.
## 
### 
Reverend F. L. Goodspeed of Oak
land, preached at the University Chap
el on Sunday. He talked on what a
man of today must be it' he would he
considered broad and worth while.
Dr. Goodspeed said that such a man
must have intellectual, spiritual and
physical development. If any of these
things were omitted he would not be
an all-round man.
Miss Leona Beal sang "The Earth is
the Lord's.'
