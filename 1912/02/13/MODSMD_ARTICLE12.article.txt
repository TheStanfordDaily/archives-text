# DR. JORDAN WILL ADDRESS WOMEN'S LEAGUE THURSDAY.
## 
### 
The second regular meeting of Wo
men's League will be held in Roble
Hall this coming Thursday afternoon
at 4:30. Dr. Jordan is to be the
speaker. He has chosen as his sub
ject a theme that is of special interest
to every woman in the University,
namely, "After Suffrage." This is
a topic of great present day concern
and all women are urged to attend.
After the address the meeting will be
social in its nature.
