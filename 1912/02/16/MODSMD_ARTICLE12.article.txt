# B'nai B'rith Game For To-morrow.
## 
### 
B'nai B'rith will be played by the
Stanford basket ball team at the San
Francisco Y. M. C. A. court to-morrow
evening. The line-up will be Davis
and Blodget, forwards; Millsap, cen
ter; Kuder and Reynolds, guards.
Wishard will have a chance to sub
stitute as forward while Ling will
substitute for one of the guards. This
game is to substitute for that which
should have been played Wednesday.
