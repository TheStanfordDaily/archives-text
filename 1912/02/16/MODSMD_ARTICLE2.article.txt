# DEBATERS SIGN FOR THE TRI-STATE LEAGUE
## Six Men to Try For Places on Teams to Contest With Northern Schools. 
### 
Six men signed up for the try-ont
of the Tri-State league debates this
afternoon and were arranged in the
following squad: Affirmative—P. D.
Nowell, '13, K. E. Leib, '13, C. C. Close,
'14; Negative—VV. F. Kilcline, '15;
E. C. Smith, '13, \V. P. Butcher, '13.
The try-out will lie held Wednesday,
February 28, and the debate March
-9. The subject will be the recall of
judges. Those wishing to enter the
try-out will be assigned a place on a
squad, upon handing their names to
E. E. Tincher, '12.
