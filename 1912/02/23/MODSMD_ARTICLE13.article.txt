# Soccer.
## 
### 
Soccer has come to stay. The Inter
collegiate game for the Williamson

trophy played yesterday af
ternoon, proves the wisdom
of the Executive Committee

in placing this game on the minor
sport basis.
The Stanford team is to lie con
gratulated on its victory. It was only
by hard, at times brilliant play that
California was defeated, but the Car
dinal players at all times showed their
superiority.
