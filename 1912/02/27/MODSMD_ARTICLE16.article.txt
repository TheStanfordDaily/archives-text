# Will Start Fancy Dancing Class.
## 
### 
It has been rumored that a class in
fancy dancing and folk dances will be
formed at Rncina gymnasium. The
society rag will give place to the more
aesthetic movements of the Russian
ballet and the Irish poetic jigs. We
may expect unknown and unsuspected
George Damorels and Cohans to burst
suddenly into thp public eye.
