# WOMAN'S LEAGUE PLANS POLITICAL DISCUSSIONS.
## 
### 
The Women's Civic League will hold
its March meeting at 8:45 to-morrow
evening, room 60. After a short busi
ness meeting, reports on the following
subjects will be heard: "Tariff Revi
sion and the Presidential Campaign,"
by Miss L. Blanchard, '11; "The Fi
nancial Question and the Aldrich Bill,"
by Miss H. Green, *13. A general dis
cussion will be held upon these topics.
A cordial welcome is extended to all
who are interested.
