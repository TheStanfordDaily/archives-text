# Judges "What Shall We Do Then."
## 
### 
Regent Truxton Beale, of the Uni
versity of California, recently offered
one thousand dollars in two prizes for
the best essays on Tolstoy's "What
Shall We Do Then?" The competition
was open to the students and recent
graduates of the University and was
highly successful in bringing out a
number of thoughtful and interesting
essays. The judges of the contest were
Professor W. G. Mitchell of Califor
nia, Professor Leo Wiener of Harvard
and Professor A. G. Newcomer, of tne
local English department. B. H. Jones
'06, took first honors and S. Cheney
'OS, won second prize.
