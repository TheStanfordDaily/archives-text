# University to Rent Plants.
## 
### 
The university greenhouses have
been put 011 a slightly different basis
by J. E. Huston, who has charge of
university construction. Hereafter,
anyone who wishes to procure palms,
ferns or other plants for decoration
can obtain them from the university
conservation.
