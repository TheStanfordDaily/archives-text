# Dr. Jordan Addresses Debaters.
## 
### 
That college debating societies are
justified by the training they afford
the average man in giving expression
to his ideas, was the conclusion reach
ed by Dr. Jordan in his address be
fore a joint gathering of Euphronians
and Nestorians yesterday evening.
After sketching the history of foren
sic practice in American colleges, he
expressed the view that the sound
logic of good argument should be the
ideal of Stanford debating rather
than oratorical effect. Following the
speech, the societies adjourned for
their regular programs.
