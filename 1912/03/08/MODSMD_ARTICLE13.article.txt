# Announcements.

Quadrangle Club — Will meet at
D. K. E. house, Monday evening at 9.
Freshmen Collectors — Turn iu
money and name list at once to S. A
Oyen.
Examination Postponed—The physi
cal examinations scheduled for to
morrow morning have been postponed
until March 16 at the same time.
Athletes—Candidates for track, crew,
soccer and basketball must take physi
cal examination at Encina gymnasium.
Last dates, Wednesday, 7:15 to 10:30
p.m., and Saturday, 9:30 to 11:30 a.m.
—R. R. Long.
Euplironia—Will meet on Tuesday,
March 12, at 7:30 p.m., in room 231.
Professor Johnson will speak.
Junior Opera Rehearsals—Chorus,
Saturday, 9:30 a.m., in room 68 to
learn songs. Pony Chorus and men
Monday, 3:30 p.m.. at Alpha Phi
house. Principals, Tuesday, 3:30 p.m.,
at Pi Reta Phi house. Reauty Chorus
and men, Thursday, 3:30 p.m., at Pi
Reta Phi house. Principals, Friday,
3:30 p.m., at Alpha Phi house. Satur
day, March 16, 9:30 a.m. Full rehear
sal in Assembly Hall.
Civil Service Examination — I'ost
Office Clerk—An open competitive ex
amination for clerk will be held at the
post-ofTice in this city on March 27.
For application blanks and further
information apply to Local Secretary,
Roard of IT. S. Civil Service Examiners.
Post-Office, Stanford University, Cal..
