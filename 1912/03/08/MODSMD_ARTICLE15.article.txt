# Freshmen Jolly-up To-night.
## 
### 
Immediately following the Robins
lecture in the Assembly Hall to-night,
the first year men will take special
cars for the Armory, where the class
will join in the spring fun-fest for
Freshmen. A unique program has
been prepared by the jolly-up com
mitteee, and the eats are to set a new
mark for excellence and abundance.
