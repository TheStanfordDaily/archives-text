# Senior Runners to be Led by Coleman.
## 
### 
T. L. Coleman, captain of the
track team, has been appointed by
President Seward of the Senior Class
to take charge of the fourth year run
ners in the coming interclass Mara
thon. All 1912 men who intend to
enter the lists should report to Cap
tain Coleman at the training house at
once, as a united effort is to be made
by the class to win the laurels..
