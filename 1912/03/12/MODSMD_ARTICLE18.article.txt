# New School to Open in September.
## 
### 
September 12. 1912, has been set as
the date for the opening of the school
of journalism of Columbia University
which was endowed by the late Joseph
Pulitzer, the famous New York editor.
Talcott Williams, LL. D., editor of the
Philadelphia Press, has been appointed
director of the new department. .T. W.
Cunliffe, D. Litt., head of the Depart
ment of English at the University of
Wisconsin for the past six years, will
lie associate director. The new build
ing for the department will not be
ready for use until the summer of
1913.
