# Euphronia Discusses Panama Canal.
## 
### 
A tree toll canal for American ships
will be the subject of Euphronia's
debate for Tuesday, March 19, at 7:30.
p.m. U. A. Sontheimer, '12, and S. S.
Green, '14, will speak for an open canal
while S. Miller, '14. and W. F. Kilcline,
'In, will oppose this policy.
