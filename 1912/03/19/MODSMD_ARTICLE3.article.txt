# STUDENT CONTROL AWAITS ACTION OF VOTERS ON QUAD TOMORROW
## POLLS AT LAW STEPS OPEN FROM 10:15 UNTIL 2:30. ELECTION OFFICIALS NAMED.---TWO-THIRDS AFFIRMATIVE VOTE NECESSARY. 
### 
When the polls close at 2:30 o'clock
tonioronv afternoon, the question of
Student Control for the men of the
University will be decided, for the
present at least.
If two-thirds of the ballots cast are
affirmative, the Advisory Board of the
University Conference, with the addi
tion of the University Conference, with
the addition of five new members, will
take up the reins of student govern
ment, backed by the male student body
of the University.
On the other hand, if two-thirds of
the men vote negatively, student con
trol will be shelved. Two-thirds of
the ballots negative will signify that
the men of the University do not care
to take upon themselves Student Con
trol at this time.
The polls will be located at the
Law Steps, and will be open during
the regular election hours, 10:15 a.m.,
to 2:30 p.m. Printed ballots have
been provided.
Election Officers Named.
President Schaupp of the Student
Body has urged that every man take
advantage of the election to express
himself on the proposition, whether
for or against Student Control.
