# La 						Liana to Assume Culinary Role.
## 
### 
Next Saturday, between the hours
of 11:30 and 2, Fraternity Hall, which
Is on the corner of University avenue
and High street, will present the ap
pearance of a French cafe with a
thoroughly American cafeteria lunch
eon in progress. La Liana has de
cided to repeat last year's luncheon
and all who were there will remember
the thoroughly good "eats" that were
to be had for very nominal prices.
Resides the luncheon there will be
music and a candy sale. All the re
ceipts will go to the Women's Club
house fund.
