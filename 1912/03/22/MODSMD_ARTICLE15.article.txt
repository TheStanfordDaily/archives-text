# WILL HOLD BEETHOVEN MEMORIAL EXERCISES.
## 
### 
A musical recital in commeration
of the death of Beethoven, will he given
in the Congregational Church, Palo
Alto, next Sunday afternoon at 4:30.
Dr. H. Blodgett will play the third
symphony from Beethoven and also a
newly discovered Jena Symphony by
the same composer. Mr. Jones will
sins two celebrated arias of the famous
German musician.
Professor L. M. Bassett, of the Eng
lish department, will read a poem upon
the death of Beethoven.
