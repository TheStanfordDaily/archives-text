# Summer Work.
## 
### 
One U. C. man's commission the
first month last summer was $750.
A Stanford man made $648. We
want some more men of this kind
this summer. We send an experi
enced man with you for a day to
show you how.
This is 110 snap, but if you have
the brains and energy to hustle and
succeed, let us hear from you. If
you don't mean business, save our
time and yours.
Personal interviews if your ap
plication is accepted. Write today.
W. E. JUDGE,
24 California St., San Francisco
