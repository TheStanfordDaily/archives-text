# Utah Delegation to Visit Stanford.
## 
### 
Governor Spry of Utah is to visit
the campus on Sunday. He will arrive
at ten and stay at the Sigma Chi house.
Probably his visit will terminate at
noon but while here he wishes to meet
all the students from Utah. The mem
bers of the delegation choosing the
Utah building site at the Panama-
Pacific Exposition will accompany him.
It is rumored that Governor Harmon,
presidential candidate, will be one of
the party.
