# STANFORD SENDS RELIEF FUND TO FAMINE DISTRICT.
## 
### 
K. L. Schaupp, '12, treasurer of the
Stanford Chinese Relief Fund, has
sent the San Francisco Red Cross So
ciety a draft for $290, the sum collected
on the campus for the sufferers in
China. The money is to be immedi
ately telegraphed to the Red Cross So
ciety in Shanghai, where it will be
used in the most efficient way for the
relief of the famine-stricken district.
