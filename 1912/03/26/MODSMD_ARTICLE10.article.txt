# Vote For Five.
## 
### 
The upperclassmen of the University
will be called upon tomorrow to choose

five representatives-at-large to
the Conference. All men credit
ed with fifty units or more will
be eligible to vote. The cnly
rule regarding the election laid

down by the Advisory Board relates
to the number of candidates voted
upon. Every ballot must be marked
for five men, otherwise it will not be
counted. This rule will be rigidly en
forced and all ballots not complying
with the requirement will be thrown
out.
