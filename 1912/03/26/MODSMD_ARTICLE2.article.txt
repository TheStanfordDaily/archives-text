# FULL TICKET MUST BE VOTED BY ELECTORS.
## Students Will Elect New Conference Members on Quad Tomorrow From 11:15 to 2:15. 
### 
The ranks of the University Con
ference will be swelled tomorrow by
the addition of five members chosen at
large, regardless of departmental con
nections. The one requirement for a
voter is that he have fifty or more
lionrs attached to his name in the
"Bawl-out."
Every voter is warned to vote for
five of the candidates from the list in
last night's Daily and any variation

from this number will mean the throw
ing out of the ballot.
The balloting will be held at the
usual place on the Quad between the
hours of 11:15 and 2:15.
