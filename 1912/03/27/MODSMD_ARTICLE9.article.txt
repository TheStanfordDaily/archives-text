# Will Play Sailors on Saturday.
## 
### 
Sailors from the Pritish cruiser
"Shearwater" are to be the opponents
of the Stanford soccer eleven on Satur
day morning. The English tars are
excellent players and have a team
which has proven victorious in a large
number of games. The contest is
scheduled for 10:30 o'clock on the turf
field. H. W. Maloney is to referee.
The following players will represent
the Cardinal: Hilton, King, Cass.
Purns, Robinson, Reynolds, Clawson,
Pellas, Steinhart, P. Erb, Fyfe and
Higgins.
