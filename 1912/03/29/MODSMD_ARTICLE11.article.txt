# SOUTHERN TEAM VICTORS OVER STANFORD WOMEN.
## 
### 
The Stanford women tennis players
were defeated by IT. S. C. in two
matches out of the three yesterday.
The doubles went to the Southerners
on a 6-3, 6-4, score. The Cardinal was
represented by Miss 13. Bingham, '14,
and Miss .T. Wand, '14. Their oppo
nents were Miss Kingcade and Miss
Wellborn. Miss Herron won her
matches for Stanford by a 6-2, 6-3
score from Miss Wellborn. The sec
ond singles went to Miss Kingcade of
U. S. C. on a 6-3, 1-6, 6-4, score, Miss
Parker, '12, defending.
