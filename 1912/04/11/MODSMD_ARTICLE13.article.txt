# Reduced Rates on Southern Pacific.
## 
### 
The Southern Pacific Company
through Mr. E. Shillingsburg. District
Freight and Passenger Agent, wishes to
announce a special rate of one and
one-third fare for the round trip to
all points on their line in California
where the fare is over 3">c. Tickets
will be good going May 11-15-20 re
turning August 1 to 31. No stopovers
allowed going but stopover is per
mitted on the return trip.
