# PHILIPPINE WORKER TO SPEAK TO STUDENTS.
## Joseph Robbins Will Visit Campus in Interests of Volunteer Missions For Three Days. 
### 
"The Flag and the Church in the
Philippines," will he the subject of a
talk by J. C. Robbins of the Philippine
Inlands to the students of the Univer
sity in the Chapel. Sunday afternoon,
at "> p.m., under the auspices of the
Y. M.and Y. W. C. A.
Robbins is a graduate of Brown
University and since 1899 has spent
most of his time in the Islands. He
is now in America, travelling among
the colleges in the interests of the
Student Volunteer Movement.
He will be on the campus from
Anril Ito 15, and will spend his
time meeting mission study classes,
Volunteer Hands, Christian Endeavor
committees, Y. M.and Y. W. C. A.
missionary committees. He will also
speak at some of the church services
in Palo Alto.
