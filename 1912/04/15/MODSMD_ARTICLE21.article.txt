# "Big Bill" Traeger Weds in South.
## 
### 
William I. Traeger, ex-'O3, was mar
ried a few days ago in Los Angeles to
Miss Ruth L. McAllister of Topango.
"Hig Bill," as Traeger was known at
Stanford, was a well known football
player. He is at present a practicing
lawyer, deputy clerk of the supreme
court and was a C.ood Government
candidate for police judge at the re
cent Los Angeles election.
