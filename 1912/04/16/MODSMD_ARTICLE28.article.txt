# TO ANNOUNCE OFFICERS AT WEDNESDAY MEETING.
## 
### 
Announcement of the officers and
cabinet of the Y. M. C. A. for the year
1912-13 will be made at the regular
weekly men's meeting in the Y. M.
C. A. rooms. Wednesday evening, at
7:in. In addition to this there will
be a discussion along various lines, as
"Principles and Ideals of the Associa
tion as Related to College Songs;"
"The Side of College Life as the Pre
paratory School Boy Sees It in Many 1
Cases:" "Clean Speech, Clean Life,
and Standards for Christian Effici
ency." ,
Every man in the University has a
cordial invitation to attend.
