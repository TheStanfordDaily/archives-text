# BOXING TOURNMENT TO START THURSDAY.
## 
### 
Right hooks to the jaw, left jabs,
uppercuts and knockouts will be the
order of a friendly mix-up at Encina
gymnasium next week. Beginning
Thursday, April 25, the preliminary
rounds of the annual boxing tourna
ment will be run off to determine the
championships in the special, light,
welter, middle, and heavy weight
classes.
Keen interest has been evinced in
the contests as medals are to be award
ed the winners. There is still time for
signing-up on the lists at the gym.
Instructor Maloney, who will referee
the bouts, has charge of the program.
