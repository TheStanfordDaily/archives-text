# COLLEGE NOTES.
## 
### 
Coach Grant of the track team of
Minnesota will discuss the lessons of
the Bible as a part of the men's
training. The theory of the coach is
that the man who leads a Christian
life is the best athlete.
The medical college of the Univer
sity of Chicago will cease to exist un
less the state legislature grants the
request of the Board of Trustees for
an appropriation of $250,000 for the
building and equipment of a new medi
cal college at Urbana.
