# CLUBHOUSE AND UNION FUNDS PROFIT BY SHOW.
## 
### 
The Track Show receipts amounted
to $768.00. About $270 in profit will
be turned over to the respective funds
of the Women's Clubhouse and the
Stanford Union. The former fund is
to receive two-thirds of this amount,
and the latter one-third, according to
the statement issued by Cap and
Gown, managers of the Track Show.
