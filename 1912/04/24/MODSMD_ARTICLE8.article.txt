# Pellas Will Captain Soccer Team.
## 
### 
S. F. Pellas, '13, today was elected
captain of the soccer team for the
next, season.
Pellas has played on the team since
soccer has been established as an
intercollegiate sport. His home is
in New York City. Tie is a Geology
major and a member of Phi Kappa
Psi.
