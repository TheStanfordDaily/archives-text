# English Club to Picnic Wednesday.
## 
### 
"May Day" will be properly cele
brated by the members of the English
Club, who are to held a picnic supper
Wednesday evening at the grove near
Frenchman's Dam. The affair will
also be the occasion of the initiation of
the new members who were elected
at the last meeting.
The following new members have
been elected to membership: Miss O.
McDermott, '13, Miss J. Don Carlos, '12,
Miss M. Montague, '13, Miss D. Gun
nell, '14, J. C. Thomas, '12, G. Gassa
way, '13, Iv. Green, '13, L. D. Summer
field, '12, and E. C. Behrens, '14.
