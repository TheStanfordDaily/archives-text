# CHAMPIONSHIP MATCHES ARE NOW IN PROGRESS.
## 
### 
Devotees of the foil and mask held
forth at the Encina gymnasium Friday
night when the first matches of the
men's tournament were held. Another
set of matches will he held this even
ing and the finals will come by the end
of the week, when the University
champion will be named.
The events were in the form of
"round robins." E. M. Leaf, *10, G. G.
Howard, '13, R. W. Griffin, '12, and
C. T. Alcutt, *15, all contested in two
events and lost one, while A. M. Bra
zen, '15, was less fortunate and lost
three. This evening a new set of
fencers will compete.
