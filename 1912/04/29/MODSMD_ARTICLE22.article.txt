# Roble Club Names Officers.
## 
### 
Miss C. Crosby, '12, has been chosen
president of Roble Club for next sem
ester. The other officers selected to
lead the Hall activities were Miss G.
Lovering, '14, vice-president; Miss B.
Jameson, '13, treasurer; Miss R.
Hitchcock, '15, secretary; and the fol
lowing new members of the Com
mittee of Five, Miss R. Seely, '13, Miss
M. Driscoll, '13, and Miss M. Merri
man, '15.
The newly elected president registers
in the Zoology department from Santa
Cruz, and is a member of Schubert
Club.
