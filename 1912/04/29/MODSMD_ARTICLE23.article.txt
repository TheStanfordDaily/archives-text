# Clubhouse Directors to Meet,
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Clubhouse
directors have called an important
mass meeting of all the University
women who have paid or subscribed
money to the clubhouse fund, to be
held at 4:30 Wednesday in the Chapel.
The election of officers is scheduled to
take place at this time, and the newly
sleeted class representatives will take
up their duties for the coming year.
