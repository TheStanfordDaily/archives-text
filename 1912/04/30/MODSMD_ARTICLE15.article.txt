# Odd Collections Added to Library.
## 
### 
A number of recently issued works
have been placed on the Library
shelves within the past few days.
Among them is a complete file of the
Denkschriften of the Vienna K. Ali*
demie der Wissenschaften, which in
cludes all the works published prior
to 1906.
The American historical collection
is strengthened by the Archives of
Maryland, and the publications of the
Ohio and Archaeological society.
The Illustrated London News, from
1861 to 1866, is a very interesting ad
dition and shows the popular British

attitude to America during the Civil
War.
