# Ten Freshmen Receive Class Numerals.
## 
### 
Freshmen who took places in the
recent interclass track meet were
awarded their numerals at a meeting
of the first-year class this afternoon.
Ten point winners will receive the jer
feys. They are F. E. McClure, R. R.
Templeton, A. E. Worthy, E. M. Bon
nett, D. Dawson, T. F. Angell, F. E.
Shaw, M. L. McNair, V. L. Sheldon and
B. L. Wines.
The first steps were taken in prep
aration for the Sophomore poster, the
vice-president being empowered to
appoint a committee to make the
arrangements
