# Commencement Edition.
## 
### 
The Commencement Number of the
Daily Palo Alto will be issued on
Commencement Day, May 20. This
twelve page edition will contain a full
account of Senior Week, including the
Baccalaureate Sermon, the Phi Beta
Kappa and Commencement addresses.
Copies can be ordered in advance at
the Daily Palo Alto office. The issue
will be illustrated and will make a
delightful souvenir of 1912's final wee*
of college life.
