# SIGMA XI ADDS LARGE MEMBER TO ITS ROLL.
## List of New Members Includes Stanford Students and San Francisco Doctors. 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of the Society
of Sigma Xi initiated twenty-one can
didates at a recent meeting.
On the basis of original investigation
in some branch of science, the follow
ing have been admitted to the secret
ranks of the foremost scientific so
ciety of the country:
Zoology—C. W. Metz, gr., C. H. Rich
ardson, '12.
Entomology—D. L. Crawford, gr., J.
H. Paine, '12, C. E. Johnson, '12.
Botany—Miss Florence Williams, gr.
Geology and Mining—R. L. Lewis,
'05, S. Taber, '06, E. C. Templeton, '10,
W. L. Walker, '03, G. E. Postma, '12,
H. W. Young, '10.
Medicine—Dr. W. Ophiils and Dr.
E. C. Dickson of San Francisco.
Chemistry—Dr. M. Adams, '95, T. B.
Freas, '96, C. M. Fulkerson, gr., W. N.
Lacy, '11, H. S. Sears, '10, R. C. Pol
lock, '06, Miss E. Jamison, '10.
