# WOMEN'S ECONOMICS CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS.
## 
### 
The Women's Economics Club has
chosen Miss A. Ahlswede, '13, to act
as president for the coming semester.
Miss L. Overaker, '15, was elected
secretary.
The meeting this week was given to
a discussion by Mrs. IT. A. Millis of
articles in current magazines dealing
with Home Economics, and talks on
the Consumers' League by Miss M.
Lothrop, '09, and Miss M. Newcomer,
'10.
A committee will be appointed to
investigate Consumers' League activi
ties in California during the summer.
