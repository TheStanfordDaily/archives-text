# GIFFORD PINCHOT WILL ADDRESS OPEN MEETING.
## 
### 
Gifford Pinchot, ex-commissioner of
forestry under Roosevelt's administra
tion and famous as an advocate of
conservation, may speak to a Stan
ford audience tomorrow afternoon.
The distinguished conservationist, who
is in California in the interests of the

Roosevelt party, has been invited by
the local chapter of Sigma Xi to speak
at its annual meeting open to the
public.
He will speak on "Scientific Aspects
of the Conservation Problem."
Owing to the fact that his train has
been delayed, definite announcement
of the time and place of the meeting
will have to be made by the use of
window-cards.
