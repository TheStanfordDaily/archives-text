# Name Please!
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto is in receipt
of a communication, on the necessity
for a "Reunion of the Stanford

Spirit," which cannot he
published until the true
name of the writer is sent.
A nom de plume was used

by the writer. If he or she wishes the
article to appear in the last edition
tomorrow, the name will have to bo
sent to the office, although it will nol
be revealed.
