# SANTA CLARA AND ST. MARY'S TO MEET HERE.
## 
### 
Santa Clara and St. Mary's colleges
will hold their fourth annual track and
field meet on the Stanford Oval next
Saturday. Competition is always rife
between the two Catholic institutions
and the coming affair promises to be
warmly contested. Both schools are to
send a number of star men, as well as
hundreds of rooters. "Dad" Moulton,
the veteran Stanford trainer, is put
ting the Mission city athletes in con
dition. Santa Clara will have a vet
eran team in ihe field while St. Mary's
places reliance on the men who have
made wonderful showings during the
preliminary season.
