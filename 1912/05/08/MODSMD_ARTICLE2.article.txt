# Five Seniors Appointed Marshals.
## 
### 
Five members. of the graduating
class of 1912 have bene named to act
as Marshals on Commencement Day.
They are F. H. Beach, W. R. Tanner,
T. L. Coleman, L. D. Summerfleld and
H. S. Morgan. These men will have
charge of the seating of the graduates
in the Assembly Hall.
