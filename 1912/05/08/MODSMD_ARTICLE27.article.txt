# Quadrangle Club Awards Medals.
## 
### 
In recognition of the new intercol
legiate records established by Stanford
track men, the Quadrangle Club has
voted special medals to W. D. Fletcher,
'11, G. L. Horine, '13, R. E. Millsap,
'12, R. Wallace, '12, F. J. Card, *14, and
.T. W. Richdale, '14. Horine and
Fletcher have been awarded handsome
gold medals for their records in the
high jump and mile run respectively,
while Millsap, Wallace, Card and Rich
dale will be given bronze medals for
their participation in the relay race.
