# PROFESSOR TREAT VISITS THE ORIENT.
## 
### 
Professor P. J. Treat of the History
department left for Yokohama, Satur
day, for a seven months' tour of the
Orient. This is the second trip Pro
fessor Treat has taken to the Far
East. In 1907, he spent nine months
in the "Countries of Unrest" gathering
material for his lectures. Most of his
attention is devoted to Australian and
East Indian colonial problems to sup
plement his work on colonial govern
ment in the Orient.
During his present tour, some time
will be spent in Corea, Manchuria, and
China, after a two months stay in
Japan. A study of the Philippines will
complete the journey.
