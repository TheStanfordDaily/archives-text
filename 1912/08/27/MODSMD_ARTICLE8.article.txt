# Tryouts.
## 
### 
Tryouts for places on the Daily staff
will commence next Monday. This
call is primarily for the

Freshmen, though candi-

dates from all classes are
eligible for election. Those intending
to tryout are to report at the Daily
office, next Monday afternoon at two
o'clock. No previous experience is
necessary. The tryout period will ex
tend over six weeks or two months,
when the elections will be announced.
These will be based upon ability to find
news, the method of handling and in
terest shown in the work.
