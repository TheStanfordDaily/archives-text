# To Arrange Soccer Schedule.
## 
### 
At the Scottish Rite hall in San
Francisco on Saturday evening the
Annual Meeting of the California Soc
cer Association will he held. At that
time drawings will he made to arrange
the schedule for the coming season.
The representatives from Stanford
will he If. W. Maloney, who lias done
so much to place soccer on a popular
footing at the University, and S. F.
Fellas, *13, captain of the Stanford
Soccer team.
