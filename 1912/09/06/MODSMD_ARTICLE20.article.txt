# FALL BASEBALL PRACTICE TO START NEXT TUESDAY.
## 
### 
Twenty-four men today signed the
roll-call for Fall baseball practice.
Next Tuesday afternoon at 4:30 the
first work-out of the year will start
and be followed by practices 011 Tues
day and Thursday afternoons and Sat
urday morning of each week. The
complete list of names of those who
have signed will be published the first
of next week.
The leader for the 1913 Varsity is
to be chosen Monday afternoon at
a special meeting of last season's Var
sity players.
