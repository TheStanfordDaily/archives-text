# SONGSTERS WILL GATHER TO INAUGURATE SEASON.
## 
### 
The University Glee Club will open
its season of activity Tuesday evening,
when the first meeting of the semes
ter will be called. The club is expect
ed to turn out practically complete,
few men being lost by graduation or
failure to return.
All former members of the organi
zation who wish to continue work are
expected to be present at the initial
meeting, where a short rehearsal and
the election of the semester's officers
will be held.
The tryouts for new men will take
place within the next two weeks.
There is no restriction on the number
which may be enrolled. <
