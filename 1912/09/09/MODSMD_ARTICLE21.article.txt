# INSPIRIGN SERMON GIVEN BY PASADENA PREACHER.
## 
### 
"The big thing about God is that He
is a spirit; the big thing about man is
that be is also a spirit in the image of
his Father, and that lie can commune
with the eternal as a spirit." These
words were the keynote of a sermon
delivered in clever Scotch style by Rev.
Robert Freeman of Pasadena yester
day morning in the Chapel. Miss A.
Westhobi. a new comer in university
musical circles, sang ".lust as I am,"
by Treolar.
