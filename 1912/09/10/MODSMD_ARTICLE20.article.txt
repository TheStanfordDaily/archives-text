# Alumni Chapter is First to be Formed.
## 
### 
Kappa Alpha Theta sorority is to
form a Stanford Alumni chapter, the
first of its kind to be organized here.
Palo Alto will be the home of the new
organization. The formation of this
alumni chapter sets a precedent among
local college fraternal organizations.
