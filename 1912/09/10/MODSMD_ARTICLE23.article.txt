# Big Oratorio Planned.
## 
### 
G. C. Buehrer, University Organist,
intends to present, with the aid of the
various musical societies, an oratorio
of considerable worth during the
coining semester. The Glee Club, the
Schubert Club, the two choirs and the
Orchestra are to combine for the pur
pose. University credit is to be given
for the work.
