# CATHOLIC STUDENTS TO HAVE RECEPTION.
## 
### 
Plans have been perfected for the
annual informal reception tendered to
the Catholic students of Stanford,
which is to take place in Ramona Hall
in Palo Alto, Friday evening. The
general introduction committee will
consist of six men and six women
representing the older students. A
social dance, with Miller, McDonald
and Ross supplying the music will
close the evening. Any Catholic stu
dents not receiving invitations will
please apply for same to Rev. Joseph
Gleason, 74. r > Waverley street, Palo
Alto.
They may also be secured from Miss
R. Rennet, '12, George Lawrence, '12,
and J. E. Mclnerny, '13.
