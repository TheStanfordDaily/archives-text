# Women to Discuss Boating.
## 
### 
Plans for several things of interest
to the women who enjoy boating and
swimming will he discussed next
Wednesday 110011, when the Boating
Manager of the Women's Athletic As
sociation will he chosen. The meeting
will be held in room 200 at one o'clock.
All women who are desirous of taking
part in these activities are urged to
be present.
Among other things to be discussed
is the possible purchase of a new
barge for (he use of the women on the
j lake next spring.
