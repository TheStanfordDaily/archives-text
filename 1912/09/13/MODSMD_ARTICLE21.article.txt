# Pete Gerhardt Retires.
## 
### 
Pete Gerhardt, one of the best
sprinters on the coast and for the past
several years captain of the Olympic
club track team, has run his last
race according to a statement given
out. by himself today. Gerhardt has
been a familiar figure in local and
national meets for many years and
this last summer was one of the
American representatives to the Olym
pic games at Stockholm. His retire
ment will undoubtedly be regretted
by track enthusiasts everywhere.
