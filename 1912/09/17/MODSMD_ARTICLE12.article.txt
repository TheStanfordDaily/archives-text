# MISS D.M. ALDERTON, '12, MARRIES WISCONSIN MAN.
## 
### 
Miss D. M. Alderton, '12, and Her
bert Kellar, of Wisconsin, were quietly
married this morning at the bride's
home in Palo Alto. Only a few rela
tives and intimate friends were pres
ent. Immediately after the ceremony
the two left for the groom's home in
Wisconsin.
Miss Alderton is a member of La
Liana, and Pi Beta Phi. She was en
rolled in the German department and
registered from Brooklyn.
Mr. Kellar will resume his studies
at the University of Wisconsin, where
he will receive bis degree from the
history department in the spring. He
is a member of Phi Gamma Delta.
