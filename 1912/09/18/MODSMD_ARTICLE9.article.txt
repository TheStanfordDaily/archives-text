# Student Conference Meeting.
## 
### 
The Student Conference will meet
for the first time this semester in the
Council Chambers, rooms 112 and 113.
011 next. Thursday evening at 7:30.
The purpose of the meeting is to
appoint a committee that will conduct
the nomination of the five members
elected from the Student Body at
large. They will also conduct the en
suing election and the regular de
partmental elections, at which all
vacancies will be filled.
