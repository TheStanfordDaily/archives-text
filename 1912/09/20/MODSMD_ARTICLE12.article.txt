# October 3 is Date For 1915 Jolly-up.
## 
### 
Saturday, October 5 is the date of
the Sophomore Jolly-up. This was de
cided on at a meeting of the class
this afternoon.
President J. R. Davis was empower
ed to appoint committees that will
take charge of and arrange for the
date of the Sophomore Cotillion. This
will probably be given about two
weeks after the Big Game.
An auditing committee was appoint
ed that will go over the books of the
treasurer. This committee consists
of W. F. Kilcline, F. E. Rehm, and
A. W. Wynne. It was unanimously
decided to levy an assessment of fifty
cents on each member of the class.
