# Twenty to Become Scout Masters.
## 
### 
Twenty men signed up for a course
in training to become Boy Scout mas
ters at a meeting held at the home of
Dr. Jordan last night. Dr. Jordan is
vice-president of the National Council
of the Hoy Scouts of America and he
is to give his personal attention to
the prospective scout masters. The
classes meet at Scout Hall in Palo
Alto and in room 36 Encina on Thurs
day evening at 7 o'clock, and any who
are interested and have not signed up
are welcome to do so at this time.
The University of California has
been giving a similar course of in
struction for the past two years.
