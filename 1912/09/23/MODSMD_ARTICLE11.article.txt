# Geology and Mining Annual Smoker.
## 
### 
The members of the Geology and
Mining department will hold their an
nual smoker on September 25, in the
Sigma Alpha Epsilon house.
Refreshments and smoking material
will be on hand and the entertainment
will be furnished by the members of
the department. Dr. .T. C. Branner
and Professor J. P. Smith will address
the members.
