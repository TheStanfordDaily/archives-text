# FRESHMEN TEAM TO PLAY BELMONT SQUAD TOMORROW.
## 
### 
The inscription on tomorrow's rugby
calendai reads "Freshmen vs. Bel
mont." These two fifteens will put
up the best brand of rugby of which
they are capable Wednesday afternoon
on the Stanford turf, beginning at
4:30.
The nearby school has a hard fight
ing team. This team has an excep
tionally heavy scrum, averaging about
180 pounds. Last Saturday Belmont
defeated the Fremont High fifteen
10-8.
Season tickets or twenty-five cents
will admit to the game.
