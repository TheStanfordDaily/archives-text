# Brazilian Will Arrive Saturday.
## 
### 
Dr. Oliveira Lima, Brazilian ambas
sador to Belgium, who will speak in
an Assembly next Tuesday, will arrive
on the campus Saturday afternoon.
Dr. Lima will be the guest of Dr. and
Mrs. J. C. Branner while on the
campus.
Arrangements are being perfected
by the Portuguese chamber of com
merce in San Francisco to tender a
banquet to Dr. Lima on October 9.
