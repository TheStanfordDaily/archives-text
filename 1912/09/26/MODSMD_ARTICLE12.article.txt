# The 						Dally Recognized.
## 
### 
After twenty years of hesitancy the
Executive Committee decided last

evening to officially re
cognize the work of the
men who issue this pub
lication five days of each

college week. Formerly the work on
the Daily was the only reward given
to the staff. Now it is a Student Body
activity for which student recognition
is given. The reward is deserved.
The men on the Daily now have some
thing to work for.
