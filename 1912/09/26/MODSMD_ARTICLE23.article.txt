# ARBITRATION CONFERENCE OFFERS PRIZE FOR ESSAY.
## 
### 
The men students of Stanford will
lie given an opportunity to compete
for the annual prize of one hundred
dollars offered by the Lake Mohonk
Conference 011 International Peace.
The prize is given for the best essay
on "International Arbitration" written
by a mau student in any American
college.
C. Y. Starkweather, of Colorado
University, was the winner of last
year's prize.
The essays should not exceed five
thousand words and should show an
undertsanding of the nature and his
tory of arbitration apart from and in
connection with the Hague Confer
ence.
All essays must be in the hands of
If. C. Phillips, Mohonk Lake, New
York, before March 15, 1913.
