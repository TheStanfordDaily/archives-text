# Junior Plugs Are Here.
## 
### 
The Regent in Palo Alto has re
ceived a large consignment of plugs
for the Juniors and they are now on
sale at the usual price of $1.10. Mem
bers of the 1914 class are urged to
secure their hats as soon as possible
so as to get them painted in time for
the annual Plug Ugly.
