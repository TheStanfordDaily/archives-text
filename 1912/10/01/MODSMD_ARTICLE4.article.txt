# Conference Holds Elections Tomorrow.
## 
### 
University Conference elections will
take place in the various departments
tomorrow afternoon from 1:10 to 1:30.
Twelve representatives are to be
chosen to fill vacancies on the board.
Lists of those eligible to vote and to
hold office, who have fifty hours of
credit, are posted in the numerous
departments.
Every forty students are represented
on the Conference and in case any of
tho departments have not this re
quired number of registered men
several combine together to fulfill
regulations. Those who will have
charge of the election are requested to
see R. W. Olmsted sometime before
tomorrow noon.
