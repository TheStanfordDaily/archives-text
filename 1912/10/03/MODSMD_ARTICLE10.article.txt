# Chess Players Gain First Victory.
## 
### 
In the first tournament of the sea
son held off the campus, the repre
sentatives of Stanford Chess activities
returned victorious by a score of 3
to 1. A team consisting of R. L.
Vaughn, '12, H. A. Hackett, '13, E. R.
Nusbaum, '13. and W. F. Burbank, '16,
defeated a merchants team in San Jose.
This was the first of a series of fort
nightly chess contests which are to be
played with outside teams this semes
ter.
