# SMITH IS PRESIDENT OF DEBATING LEAGUE.
## 
###

dent of the inter-Society Debating
League, at the first regular meeting
F. L. Foster, '13, was chosen to fill
the position of secretary.
At the meetings of the respective de
bating societies F. B. Belcher, '13, F. L.
Foster, '13, and P. A. Pier, '14, were
appointed to represent Nestoria, and
E. C. Smith, '13, P. D. Nowell, '13, and
G. Wood, '13, were chosen as repre
sentatives of Euphronia in the Inter
society League.
