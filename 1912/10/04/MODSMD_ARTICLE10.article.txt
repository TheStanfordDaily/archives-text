# More Seats For Assembly Hall.
## 
### 
The Peninsula Musical Association
announces that through the courtesy
of Charles G. Lathrop and the Hoard
of Trustees about seventy-five seats
are to be added to the Assembly Hall.
Those on the waiting list whose
checks are received first will have the
earliest opportunity of becoming as
sociate members. Season tickets will
be mailed on October 7, instead of
the sth. as announced.
The first concert is to be by Ricardo
Martin on October 17.
