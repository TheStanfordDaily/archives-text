# FIVE CANDIDATES NAMED FOR STUDENT CONFERENCE.
## 
### 
Five names were submitted at the
nominations today for the represent
atives to the University Conference at
large. R. W. Whitaker,' 13, H. L. Loud,
'13, J. F. Partridge, '12, L. Cass, '13,
and C. T. Keefer, '13, were the men
who will be placed on the ballot for
the election which will be held on next
Wodnesday on the Quad. The polls
will be open from 10:15-2:00. Nom
inations may be made through the
columns of the Daily Palo Alto up to
and including next Tuesday night.
Any student naving fifty or more hours
of credit is eligible to vote.
