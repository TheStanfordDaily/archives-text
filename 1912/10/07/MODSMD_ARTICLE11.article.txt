# Gym Club Dance Will Be October 12.
## 
### 
Two special cars have been char
tered to take the crowd to the annual
Gym Club Dance in the Armory, on
the evening of October 12, and there
will be special cars for the return
trip. McDonald's Orchestra is to fur
nish the dance music. Tickets can
be obtained from any of the members
of the Gym Club, or at the Campus
cigar store, where programs may be
secured upon the presentation of
tickets.
