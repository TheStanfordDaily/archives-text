# THE 						DAILY PALO ALTO TO REPORT WORLD'S SERIES.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at the Polo
Grounds in New York, the World's
Championship Baseball Series will
start. The title goes to the team win
ning the odd game in seven. In keep
ing with the custom of ever giving its
readers the best service on matters
of interest to them, The Daily Palo
Alto has made extensive arrange
ments whereby each day's game will
be reported through these columns.
Thus this news will be transmitted
to the campus readers fourteen hours
ahead of the San Francisco morning
dailies, a matter that is sure to be ap
preciated by all.
