# Red Fire and Parade for Visitors.
## 
### 
Headed by the Stanford Rand, a
parade in honor of the visitors will
start from Encina at S o'clock tomor
row evening and march lip the Row.
At the same hour the Australian and
Stanford football squads will gather
at the Zeta Rsi house. Fraternities
are requested to light red fire as the
parade passes.
