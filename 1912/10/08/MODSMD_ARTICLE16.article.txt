# CHAMPIONSHIP DECIDED IN INTERCLUB SINGLES.
## Murray Defeats Sloss in the Tennis Finals and Wins Weingartner Loving Cup. 
### 
By winning the finals of the Inter
club Singles Tennis Tournament, R.
L. Murray, '13, representing the Kappa
Alpha fraternity, becomes permanent
holder of the Weingartner loving cup.
Murray defeated L. Sloss, '15, of En
cina Club, in three straight sets by
the score of G-3, 6-0, 6-2. Although
an interclub tourney, the victor prac
tically becomes the University cham
pion, because all the players have con
tested from scratch.
The entire results of the Singles
tournament are as follows:
First round —R. L. Murray, '13, Kap
pa Alpha, defeated F. C Hunter, '16,
Phi Gamma Delta, 6-3, 8-6; C. V.
Parke, '09, Encina, defeated J. Hutch
inson, '16, Delta Tau Delta, 5-7, 7-5,
6-0; F. B. Hannawalt, '13, Alpha Tau
Omega, defeated C. E. Clowe, '13, Phi
Gamma Delta, 6-4, 6-0.
Second round—W Hutchinson, '15,
Phi Kappa Psi, defeated — Johnston,
'16, Encina; O. Brunn, '16, Kappa Sig
ma, defeated A. Pierpont, '13, Encina,
6-3, 6-0; K. B. Uhls, '16, Encina, de
feated T. B. Blethen, '13, 8-6, 6-2;
Murray defeated Parke, 8-6, 6-4; G. L.
Shaul, '13, Phi Delta Theta, defeated
E. Wright, '16, Phi Gamma Delta,
6-1, 10.8; F. S. Pratt, '13, Encina, de
feated F. Wilson, —, Delta Tau Delta;
L. Sloss, '15, Encina, defeated H.
Whitted, '16, Phi Gamma Delta, 6-1,
6-4.
Third round —Hutchinson defeated
Brunn, 6-3, 4-6, 6-4; Murray defeated
Uhls, 6-3, 6-3; Sheldon defaulted to
Shaul; Sloss defeated Pratt, 6-2, 6-4.
Semi-finals —Murray defeated Hut
chinson, 6-4, 6-3; Sloss defeated Shaul,
6-2, 8-6.
Finals—Murray defeated Sloss. 6-3,
6-0, 6-2.
