# DR. LIMA WILL DISCUSS FIFTH PHASE OF HIS TOPIC.
## 
### 
Dr. Oliveira Lima will give the fifth
lecture in his series on the history
of Latin America on Wednesday after
noon, at 3 o'clock, in room 231 of the
History building. This lecture will
deal primarily with the intellectual
development of Latin America during
the first half of the 19th century. Dr.
Lima will discuss, among other topics,
the literature of the new countries,
the cult of the past, European in
fluence in Latin America, and tradi
tionalism and modernism.
