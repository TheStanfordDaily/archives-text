# No Conference Election Tomorrow.
## 
### 
Since but five names have been sub
mitted for nomination at large to the
University Conference, the secretary
will cast the ballot and no election
will be held on the Quad tomorrow.
Those who will represent the Student
Body on the Conference and who were
nominated at the meeting last Friday,
are H. L. Loud, '13, R. W. Whitaker,
'13, J. F. Partridge, '12, L. Cass, '13,
and C. T. Keefer, '13 and C. T. Keefer,
'13. All of these men are active in
college affairs and will take office for
two years.
