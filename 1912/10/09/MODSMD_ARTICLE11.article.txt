# DIRECTORIES ARE NOW BEING DISTRIBUTED.
## 
### 
The University directories are be
sold at fifteen cents a copy in the
Business Office today. The total
registration is 1656. But 1243, or 75
per cent of the students are from Cali
fornia, while 44 states are represented.
Eleven foreign countries, represented
by 37 students, also testify to the cos
mopolitan character of the University.
By departments, Law leads with a
registration of 277. History with 177,
Civil Engineering with 158, Economics
with 146, and English with 136, follow
in order. Medicine and Mathematics
show the largest gains, the number
of medical majors having increased
40 per cent.
