# Gym Club Dance Saturday.
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale at the cam
pus cigar store for the Gym Club
dance, which is to be held in the Ar
mory on Saturday evening. This is
an annual function given by the in
door athletes and the proceeds are
used to buy apparatus for the gym
nasium. There will be eighteen
dances and two extras. Music will
he furnished by McDonald's orchestra.
