# Stanford Players Win in State Tennis.
## 
### 
R. L. Murray, '13, was the surprise
in the opening day's play of the State
championship tourney in San Fran
cisco yesterday when he defeated J.
Strachan in straight sets by the score
of 6-2, 7-5. Strachan was the winner
of the Stanford interscholastic Tennis
singles last spring.
Miss Laura Herron, '14, University
women's champion, and Miss Irene
Norman afforded the most interesting
contest in the first round of the wo
men's singles, in two closely matched
sets, Miss Herron winning 6-4, 7-5.
