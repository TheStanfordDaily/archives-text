# Rhodes Scholarship Examinations.
## 
### 
The Rhodes Scholarship examina
tions will be held on Tuesday and
Wednesday, October 15 and 16, in the
Council Room (110).
The following is the schedule:
Tuesday, October 15, 9-11 a.m.—
Translation from Latin into English;
1-3 p.m., Latin Prose: 4-6 p.m., Arith
metic.
Wednesday, October 16, 9-11 a.m.—
Translation from Greek into English:
1-2 p.m., Latin Grammar: 2:10-3:10
p.m., Greek Grammar: 4-6 p.m., Al
gebra or Geometry.
A. T. MURRAY,
Chairman, Stanford Committee.
