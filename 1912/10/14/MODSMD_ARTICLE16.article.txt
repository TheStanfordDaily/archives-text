# Loaders Forge Ahead in Pennant Race.
## 
### 
Team No. 2, Terry's nine, has
strengthened its grasp upon the top
rung of the percentage ladder as a re
sult of the games of Friday and Sat
urday in the Fall league. In the first
game Argabrite's team met and de
feated Workman's aggregation by a
5-0 score. Saturday morning, in one
of the best exhibitions of the series,
Terry's players executed a ninth
inning rally and won from Argabrite's
team by a 5-6 score.
