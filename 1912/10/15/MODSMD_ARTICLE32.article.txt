# Y.M.C.A. WILL HOLD OPEN HOUSE THURSDAY.
## 
### 
Next Thursday evening at 7:15, the
Young Mens' Christian Association
will hold open house in the Men's
Room, as a beginning to the student
activity of the organization for the
year.
Some time has been spent in form
ing the general policy of the Associa
tion this year, and E. C. Smith, '13,
president of the Y. M. C. A., and J. P.
Hagerman, general secretary, will pre
sent the outline of the work at that
time.
