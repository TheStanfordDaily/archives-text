# Manzanita Defeats Mountain View.

on the auxiliary field this afternoon by
a score of 25 to Dickey, Laugenour,
llramschureger and Spencer played
well for Manzanita. Loucks, Martel,
Wright and Rouse were the stars for
Mountain View.
