# Woman's Club Elects Mrs. Hoover.
## 
### 
Mrs. H. C. Hoover, '96, has been
elected to the Board of Directors of
the Women's Clubhouse Corporation,
to lill the vacancy caused by the
resignation of Miss Shirley Hyatt, '07.
Mrs. Hoover has contributed gener
ously to the Clubhouse cause, both in
funds and advisory effort.
