# Women's Press Club Elects.
## 
### 
The Women's Press Club has elected
the following new members: Miss
Marjorie Tisdale, '14, Miss Miriam
Harrier, '14, and Miss Lois McCoy, '14.
The officers elected for the ensuing
year are Miss Lois Raker, '13, presi
dent, and Miss Carol Green, '14, secre
tary-treasurer. The newly elected
members have recently been appointed
to the staff of the Daily Paly Alto.
