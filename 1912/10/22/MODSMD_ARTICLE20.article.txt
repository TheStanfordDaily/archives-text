# Pigskin Echoes.
## 
### 
With Coach Presley on the field in
football togs directing the practice,
Monday afternoon's workout for the
Varsity squad lost the traditional list
lessness which marks the first day of
the week, and the rugby candidates
put in forty minutes of snappy squad
practice. The forwards, under the
direct supervision of the coach, were
drilled in scrum formation and in
heeling the hall out to the half. The
backs are regularly under the direc
tion of Bennie Erb, and practice daily
in passing and cutting-in, dribbling,
falling on the ball, and hooting to
touch.
