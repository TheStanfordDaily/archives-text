# Chaplain Will Discuss Social Service.
## 
### 
Doctor D. Charles Gardner, at the
request of W. H. Bloeser, '14, chair
man of the Y. M. C. A. Social Service
committee, has consented to talk to
all men interested in Social Service,
in the Men's Room, Thursday even
ing at 7:10.
As a member of the original Social
Service Club at Stanford, the Chaplain
knows what needs be done on and in
the vicinity of the campus. His talk
will fall in directly with the general
broad policy of social service which
the Y. M. C. A. is planning to adopt
this Fall.
The meeting will be adjourned to
the Assembly Hall in time to hear
Doctor R. Tj. Wilbur's talk to the Uni
versity men.
