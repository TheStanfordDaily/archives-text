# PLUG UGLY PLANS ARE BEING FAST COMPLETED.
## 
### 
Preparations are well under way for
the 1914 Plug Ugly. The annual affair
will he held a week from Saturday, on
November 2.
The prize for the winning Plug
Ugly play has been awarded, and the
manuscript is now in the hands of the
authors for completion. The general j
committee has been subdivided into
smaller ones, each having in charge
a special part of the whole affair.
