# Wilson Supporters to Rally.
## 
### 
Members of the Palo Alto Woodrow
Wilson Club will rally Saturday even
ing at 8 o'clock in Mullens' Hall.
Speakers at the meeting will be ex-
Ma.vor E. R. Taylor of San Francisco,
and Mrs. Gertrude Atherton, the novel
ist. All students interested in political
issues are invited to attend the meet
ing.
