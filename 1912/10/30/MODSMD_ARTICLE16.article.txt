# Nestorians Postpone Rally.
## 
### 
The pre-election rally that was to
have been held in room 214 last night
by Nestoria was postponed until to
night. The men scheduled to support
Roosevelt are F. H. Hilton, '11, and
P. L. Higgins, '15. Wilson's candidacy
will be supported by E. E. Tinchnr,
'12, and F. B. Belcher, 'IH.
