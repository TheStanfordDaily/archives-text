# DR. JACOBY GIVES FIRST OF SERIES OF LECTURES.
## 
### 
Dr. Guenther .Jacoby of the Univer
sity of Greifswald, gave the iirst of his
series of philosophical lectures in the
Chapel last evening on the subject of
"Current German Thought." The sec
ond lecture is to be delivered this
evening in room 460 on "Bergson,
Pragmatism and Schopenhauer." He
has been lecturing before the various
departments during the week.
The lecturer traced the development
of philosophy from the long prevalent
system of Kant to the two modern
schools of Encken and Rickert.
