# Twenty-One Men Have Perfect Score.
## 
### 
Twenty-one men of the rugby squad
were given a thorough medical exam
ination by Dr. H. W. Chappel, '01, last
evening, and came through with flying
colors. In every case, the player's
condition was marked "excellent" on
the record sheet, and Dr. Chappel ex
pressed the opinion that the squan
were in the best of health and fighting
trim. The examination was an un
usually thorough one, Dr. IT. Zinsser
of the Bacteriology department assist
ing Dr. Chappel.
