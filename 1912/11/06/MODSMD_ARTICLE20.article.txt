# PEARLS WILL BE SUBJECT OF ZOOLOGY CLUB LECTURE.
## 
### 
"The Origin, Development, and Cul
tivation of Salt Water Pearls, will be
the subject of an address by Alvin
Seale, '05, before the Zoology Club
tonight.
Seale, who was a member of the
Zoology Club, is chief of the Depart
ment of Fisheries of the Bureau of
Science in Manila. He has made an
extensive study of pearls and pearl
oysters.
The meeting of the club will be held
at the home of Professor .T. O. Snyder,
of the Zoology department at 1345
Cow per street, Palo Alto.
