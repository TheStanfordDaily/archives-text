# President Jordan is on Peace Tour.
## 
### 
President David Starr Jordan left
Sunday afternoon for a short tour of
the Western states in the interests of
international arbitration.
He will talk today before the
teachers of New Mexico, assembled at
Albuquerque.
Before returning to the campus on
November 13, he will speak at Law
rence. Topeka, and Kansas City, Ivans.
