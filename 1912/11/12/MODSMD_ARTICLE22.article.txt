# Seale Talks About Pearls.
## 
### 
Meeting at the home of Professor
J. O. Snyder of the Zoology depart
ment, members of the Zoology Club
recently heard a talk on the origin and
development of the pearl, given by
Alvin Seale, '05.
Seale has been connected with the
United States Bureau of Fisheries at
Manila for several years, and is now
the head of the Bureau. While in
the Philippine Islands he has made
particular study of the pearl oyster
and pearl.
In his talk before the Zoology Club
he discussed the early and modern
methods of diving for pearl oyster*, by
the natives.
