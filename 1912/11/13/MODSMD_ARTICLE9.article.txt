# CAMP-FIRE GUARDIANS TO HOLD COUNCIL PICNIC.
## 
### 
A picnic supper at Roble Bridge
will be provided for the members of
the campus and Palo Alto groups of
the Camp-Fire Guardians tomorrow
afternoon.
The gathering will take the form of
a Council meeting, and the party will
assemble at Roble Hall immediately
after Professor Krehbiel's talk for the
Women's League.
