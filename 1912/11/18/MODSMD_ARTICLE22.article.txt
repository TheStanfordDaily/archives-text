# SETTLEMENT WORKER TO SPEAK IN CHAPEL.
## 
### 
Mrs. Schweitser of the Nurses Set
tlement in San Francisco, will explain
the purpose and features of her
chosen work to all who are interested
in practical Social Service, tomorrow
afternoon at 4: 30 in the Chapel. The
meeting is under the auspices of the
Social Service committee and will be
led by Miss .VT. Gard, '14. Miss E.
Smoot, '15, will play.
