# COTILLION TICKET SALE CLOSES WEDNESDAY NIGHT.
## 
### 
All who intend to go to the Sopho
more Cotillion should purchase their
tickets immediately. They will he on
sale at the Bookstore until Wednesday
night, after which they will he with
drawn. This action is made necessary
in order that the committee may know
exactly how many to cater for in the
formal affair which will take place
next Saturday evening in the Armory.
The tickets are obtainable tomorrow
and Wednesday at the regular price
of $2.50.
