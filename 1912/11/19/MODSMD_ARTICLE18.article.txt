# Announcements.

1912 Varsity—Pictures will be taken
Sunday at 11 at Franklin's.
Freshmen—Report tomorrow at 2 to
clean up from bonfire.
.Hand(»lin Club—Will meet Wednes
day evening, 7:30, Phi Gamma Delta
house.
Economics Club—Meeting at Delta
Chi house, Wednesday, 8 p.m.
G. and Jl. Society—Will meet Tues
day evening at the Phi Delta Theta
house, at 7:30.
Glee Club —Meet Tuesday, room 6S,
7:30.
Foil and Mask—Meet Wednesday,
7:45, Roble gymnasium. Visitors are
welcome.
Schubert Club —Meet Wednesday at
4:30, Delta Gamma house.
Civic League—Meet in Chapel, Tues
day evening at 7:30. S. I. Miller will
speak on "Pending Citizenship Prob
lems for Women."
Orchestra Practice—Wednesday, in
room 68, 7:30 p.m., sharp.
Forestry Club —Talk by H. Donald
Tiemann on "The Structure of Wood,"
Thursday at 8, room 472. All interest
ed invited.
Campus Mothers' Club—Meet Wed
nesday at the school house at 3:30.
Professor R. R. Long will speak on
"Modern Methods for Promoting Pub
lic Health." All interested invited.
Debaters—Sign up with E. C. Smith,
'13, or F. B. Belcher, '13, for tryouts
for interclass debates. Sophomore
tryout Tuesday, Freshmen Wednesday.
Junior Opera Manuscripts—Must be
submitted to Chairman J. L. McDonald,
not later than December 2. Medals
will be awarded successful writers.
