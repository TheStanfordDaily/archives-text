# AMATEUR ATHLETIC UNION APPROVES HORINE'S RECORD.
## 
### 
G. L. Horine, *13, has been olficially
approved as holder of the world's
record in the high jump. This action
was taken last night at the annual
meeting of the Amateur Athletic Union
in New York. The mark approved is
six feet, six and one-eighth inches and
was made in a dual meet between
Stanford and the University of South
ern California on the local track,
March 29. There has been no claim
for the mark of six feet seven inches
which was made in the Pacific Coast
Olympic tryouts held on the same
track, May 20.
