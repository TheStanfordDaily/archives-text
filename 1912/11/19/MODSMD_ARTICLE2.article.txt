# FORESTRY CLUB TO HEAR TALK BY WISCONSIN MAN.
## 
### 
Speaking on "The Structure of
Wood," H. Donald Tiemann will ad
dress an open meeting of the Forestry
Club on Thursday evening.
The speaker is a forest engineer,
having made a special study of timber
physics. With his station in Madison,
Wisconsin, Tiemann is engaged in
working for the government in the
Forest Products Laboratory.
This address will be held in room
472 on Thursday evening at 8. All
interested in this subject are invited
to be guests of the Forestry Club.
