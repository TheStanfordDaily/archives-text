# ENGLISH CLUB ELECTS SEVEN TO MEMBERSHIP.
## 
### 
The election of seven new members
was held by the English Club last
evening in a meeting at the Delta Gam
ma house. The members-elect are
Misses Agnes Gray, '15, Carol Green,
'14, and Marjorie Tisdale, '14; S. C.
Peck, '11, C. F. Paul, '13, J. L. Duff.
'13, and E. F. Ford, '14. Most of the
new members have been frequent con
tributors to the college magazine.
