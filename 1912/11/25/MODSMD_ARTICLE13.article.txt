# A Duty.
## 
### 
Each Qualified elector should be
present in the Chapel tomorrow after
noon at 1:10 at the semi-annual

Student Body nomination.
The men who are to be
named for office will be

important figures in the student
government next semester. The best
men must fill the vacancies. Choose
your candidate for each place tonight,
and come to the meeting tomorrow and
see that they are nominated. This is
your duty. The places to be filled are
Editor-in-Chief of The Daily Palo Alto,
one representative from each of the
Junior and Sophomore classes to the
Executive Committee. The Junior
candidate also becomes a member of
the Hoard of Trustees of the Stanford
Uion.
