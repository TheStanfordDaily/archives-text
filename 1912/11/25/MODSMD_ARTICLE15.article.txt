# Glee Club Selects Design For Medal.
## 
### 
Members of the University Glee
Club will hereafter wear a distinguish
ing mark in the form of an official
medal. The emblem, to be worn as a
watch-fob, is rectangular in shape and
bears a Gothic "S" flanked by two
singing figures in relief, with the
words "Stanford Glee Club."
In recognition of bis services to the
organization during the season ol
1911-ini2. E. It. Fertig, '10, last year's
leader, has been presented with a gold
fob of the Club design.
