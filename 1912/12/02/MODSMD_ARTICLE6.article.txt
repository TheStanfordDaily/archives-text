# To Lecture on Portraits.
## 
### 
Assistant Professor R. B. Harshe, of
the Graphic Arts department, will give
a lecture, illustrated by the etched, en
graved and photographed portraits, in
the Botanical Library, on the "Methods
of Portrait Reproduction," on Tuesday
evening at S, in the Botanical Lecture
room, room 460. The lecture is open
to all.
