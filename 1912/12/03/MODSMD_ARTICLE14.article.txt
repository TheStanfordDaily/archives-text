# Interclass Tennis Doubles.
## 
### 
Entries for the Interclass Doubles
Tenuis Tournament are to hand theii
names to F. S. Pratt, 13, A. Pierpont,
'13, or R. U Murray, '13, by Friday
night. The tourney will start on the
Encina courts on Saturday morning.
The winners of the matches in their
respective classes will clash for the
doubles supremacy. The victors in the
Junior-Senior match will play the
holders of the underclass champion
ship. The two winning the tournament
will be awarded sweaters.
