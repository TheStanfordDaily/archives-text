# California Note.
## 
### 
Training at the University of Cali
fornia in preparation for the inter
class regatta to be held early next
semester is going on apace. Practice
is held on the water three times a
week, and on Sunday mornings as
six crews out the shells.
The Freshman squad has been re
duced to thirty-eight men, and there is
keen competition for seats in the boat.
Captain Eaton, Marcus Lee and
Charles Denhani will be the nucleus
of the varsity for 1913. No regular
varsity training is toping conducted at
California this semester, although
practically all the varsity candidates
are in training with their respective
class crews.
