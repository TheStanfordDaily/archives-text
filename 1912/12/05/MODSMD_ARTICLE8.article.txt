# SOPHOMORES WILL STAGE PRODUCTION FEBRUARY 28.
## 
### 
February 28 is the date set for the
Sophomore play. This is the first
production of the stage for next
semester which has so far been an
nounced.
Garnet Holmes has been selected
as coach for next semester's annual
underclass production. He has been
very successful as coach at the Uni
versity of California and is well known
for his thorough work in San Fran
cisco theatrical circles.
Coach Holmes will conduct the try
outs tomorrow in room OS, beginning
promptly at 1:30. Those who desire
to tryout may secure parts from W. W.
Thoburn, Phi Delta Theta house, or
from Miss Miriam Bryan, Pi Beta Phi
house.
