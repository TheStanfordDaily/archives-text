# Announcement.
## 
### 
December 9, 1912.
A woman student in the University
has been dropped from a course and
ten units added to the requirements
For graduation because of cheating in
an examination.
(Signed)
ILEEN IUTI.LIS,
Chairman,
Women's Student Council.
Approved:
A. Is. CLARK,
Chairman. Student Affairs Committee.
DAVID STARR JORDAN,
President.
