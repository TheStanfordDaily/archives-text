# Freshman Finances in Good Shape.
## 
### 
According to the report of the
Freshman treasurer in a meeting of
the 1910 class, the finances of the lirst
year students are in excellent shape.
One hundred and sixty-five dollars
have been collected as class dues, and
receipts from the class jolly-up will
swell the fund. The cost of the bon
fire, as reported, was $84.15.
