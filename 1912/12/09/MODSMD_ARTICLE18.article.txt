# SCHUBERT CLUB TO HAVE HOUR OF MUSIC.
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon, tit half-past
four, Schubert Club will make its only
appearance of the semester in a
musical in the Chapel, under the direc
tion of Mr. .Jones. The club will sing
in chorus several songs, including
"The Shepherd's Song" and* "In May
Time." In addition, Miss Dorothy
Stillnian, '13, Miss Emily Coleman, '13,
and Miss Agnes Maloney, '14, will
sing solos. Misses Anna McGovern and
Christabel Elliott, '16, will sing a duet.
