# FORTY-TWO PASS TRYOUTS FOR SECOND-YEAR PLAY.
## 
### 
Forty-two actors and actresses pass
ed the preliminary trials for the
Sophomore Play on Friday afternoon.
Nearly one hundred appeared for the
tryouts.
The second tryouts and the final
cut will take place on Wednesday af
terncon at. 2 o'clock in room 68. Fol
lowing this tlie cast will be picked by
the coach.
A considerable amount of good
material was discovered on Friday.
With the keen competition which was
manifest, those who survived the first
cut are the best, including those who
have previously appeared on the Stan
ford stage and new comers to the
local footlights.
