# Seniors Defeat Freshmen in Tennis.
## 
### 
R. L. Murray and F. S. Pratt, repre
senting the Senior class, defeated the
Freshman doubles team, composed of
If. L. Hahn and .J. H. Hutchinson, and
won the interclub doubles tennis
championship on Friday. The upper
classmen were victorious in the first
two sets but lost the third set, and
won their title in the fourth match.
This is the second year in which Mur
ray and Pratt have won the University
doubles championship.
