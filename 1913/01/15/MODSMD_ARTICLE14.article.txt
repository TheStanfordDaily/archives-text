# Glee Club Plans Trip.
## 
### 
Several trips will lie taken by the
University C 1 lee Club this semester.
Plans were formulated in a meet
ins held last night. Manager Beur
haus will endeavor to arrange with
the Santa Fe Railroad for an extensive
tour through the Middle West. The
olub will probably journey to Mare
Island next week and render a short
program before the Soldier's Guild
til ere.
