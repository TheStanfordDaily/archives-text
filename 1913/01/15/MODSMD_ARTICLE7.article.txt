# Baseballers Practice in the Wet.
## 
### 
Notwithstanding the wet condition
of Ihe grounds the first baseball prac
tice of the year was held yesterday
afternoon. Some sixty-odd candidates
appeared for work under the direc
tions of Coach Peters and Captain
Terry.
A light work-out was gone through
consisting of limbering up the arms,
batting practice, and ending with a
short run around the field.
