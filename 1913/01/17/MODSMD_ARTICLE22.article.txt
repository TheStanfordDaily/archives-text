# Band to Give Sunday Concerts.
## 
### 
Concerts will be given by the Stan
ford Band on alternate Sundays on the
Quad. The first will L'e held January
20. Upon the completion of the new
boathouse at Laguinta. the bandsmen
will play there when concerts are
to be given.
T. P. Martin, '13, and G. P. Ross, '14,
will lead the musicians this semester.
F. It. S. Missner, '13, is to continue
as manager.
