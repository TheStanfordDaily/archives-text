# Professor Harshe Honored.
## 
### 
Professor R. I?. Ilarshe of the Art
department has been chosen president
of the California Society of Etchers.
Professor Harshe is a prominent ex
ponent of dry point work and has re
cently installed an etchers press in the
old studio where he has run off several
of the etchings made by him of sub
jects abroad and on the campus. Pro
fessor Ilarshe is an assistant director
of the art exhibition of the Panama-
Pacific Exposition.
