# Gym Club Give Dance January 31
## 
### 
The Gym Club will give its semi-au
nual dance at the Armory in Palo Alto
on the evening ot' January 31st. The
price of admission is one dollar per
couple, and tickets may be obtained
from any member o)' the club or at
Wilson's cigar store on the Campus.
An innovation will be introduced in
the serving of refreshments at the
Armory. The "eats" will be furnished
by Ozier and will be sold at the usual
prices. McDonald's orchestra will fur
nish the music.
