# Tennis Players to Meet This Evening.
## 
### 
Tennis Captain V. L. Sheldon, 'in.
wishes all men who are interested in
that sport to meet at the Kappa Alpha
bouse this evening at 7:30. The pur
pose of this meeting is to decide on a
system of coaching and selecting the
team that will meet California. For
this reason Sheldon urges that all the
tennis players in the University be
present.
