# INGRAHAM IS NOMINATED PRESIDENT OF SENIORS FOR SPRING SEMESTER
## 
### 
Irving I. Ingraham, '13, was nomi
nated for the presidency of the Senior
Class for the spring semester, at the
1913 class meeting this afternoon. W.
.1. Dodge was named as a candidate
for the vice-presidency. Miss Ruth
Seeley was nominated for secretary,
C. H. Gilbert for treasurer, and W. M.
Argabrite for sergeant-at-arms. The
general elections of the class will be
held Tuesday from 10:15 to 2:30, and
until Monday evening further nomin
tions may be made through tlie col
umns of The Daily Palo Alto.
Ingraham has been prominently
identified with college activities since
coming to Stanford. Last semester he
edited The Daily Palo Alto, and he
has recently been appointed Campus
Chairman of the Stanford Union. Hp
belongs to the Quadrangle Club, Press
Club, Skull and Snakes, and Sword
and Sandals, and is also a member of
the Student Council. Ingraham reg
isters in the History department from
Bristol, and is a mem
ber of the Delta Kappa Epsilon fra
ternity.
