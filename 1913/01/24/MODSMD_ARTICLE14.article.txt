# Date For Carnot Agreed Upon.
## 
### 
The committee in charge of debating
at the University of California has
agreed that the annual Carnot debate
shall be held at Stanford one week be
fore the intercollegiate track meet at
Berkeley. This date was proposed by
the Stanford committee. The general
subject for discussion this year is
"France and Republicanism." The
specific question will be announced to
the contestants two hours before the
commencement of the contest.
