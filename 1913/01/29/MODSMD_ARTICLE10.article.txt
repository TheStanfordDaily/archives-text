# Mechanical Engineers to Hear Doble.
## 
### 
As announced in a meeting of the
Mechanical Engineering Society last
evening, William A. Doble, of the Pel
ton Water Wheel Company, inventor of
the Doble bucket, and engineer in
charge of the water wheel construc
tion of the Los Angeles Aqueduct,
will lecture at Stanford soon. A, M.
Stevens, 'O8. of the Polson Wireless
Telegraph Company will also talk be
fore the society. His subject will be,
"Wireless Telegraphy."
