# Expert Fencer at Roble Gym Tonight.
## 
### 
Under the auspices of Foil and Mask,
Major Reid of San Francisco will give
an exhibition of swordsmanship in
Roble Gymnasium this evening. Major
Reid was formerly fencing master in
the British army, and has the reputa
tion of being a master of his weapon.
For the first part of his program he
will give a series of original feats.
Later he will fence with H. W. Mal
loney.
Foil and Mask invites the public to
attend. The exhibition begins at 8
o'clock.
