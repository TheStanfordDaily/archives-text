# Encina Five Will Play Palo Alto.
## 
### 
As the opening games in the Pacific
Athletic Association basketball tourna
ment will not be played tonight as was
expected, the second five from Encina
Gymnasium will meet the Palo Alto
High quintet on the latter's court this
evening.
The Encina team will line-up as fol
lows:
Wilcox, Heebe, McMahon, forwards;
Price, center; Best, Lammimann,
guards.
