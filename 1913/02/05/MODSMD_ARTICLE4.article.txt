# Professor Folsom Addresses Society.
## 
### 
* /
Professor I). M. Folsom delivered an
interesting address last evening before
the Geology and Mining society. Hp
spoke of the construction of the Rig
Flue Stack at Great Falls, Montana.

Instructor Van Wyck Brooks, of the
English department, is writing a hook
on John Addington Simmons.
Freshmen at the University of Illi
nois have been forbidden their annual
smoker.
