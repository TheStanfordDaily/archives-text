# FRESHMEN TO JOLLY UP ON FEBRUARY 22.
## 
### 
February 22 is the date chosen by
the class of 1916 for its Jolly-up which
will be held at the Armory. There are
several in the class who have dramatic
ability and Chairman Milliken has
asked them to prepare a number of
stunts which will be out of the ordi
nary run of songs and dances.
The treasury of the Freshmen Class
is in a flourishing condition and some
thing special is being promised for
refreshments.
