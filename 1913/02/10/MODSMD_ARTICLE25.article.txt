# Musical Clubs in Benefit Concert.
## 
### 
Thirty-two members of the Glee and
Mandolin clubs will journey to Val
lejo on April eleventh and take part in
a program to be given by the soldiers
under the auspices of the Society for
the Protection of Widows and Orphans.
The boys will remain in Vallejo over
April twelfth and will be shown
through the yards by Chaplain Mc-
Nair.
