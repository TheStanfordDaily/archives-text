# TRYOUTS THIS WEEK FOR JUNIOR OPERA
## 
### 
Tryouts for positions in the cast
and chorus of the 1914 Junior Opera
will be held on Wednesday and Thurs
day afternoons of this week. The
time and place will be announced to
morrow. Fred Carlyle, who As to
coach the production, will be present
at the tryouts and will also confer
with members of the committee to
morrow afternoon. A large number
are expected to compete in the tryouts,
since the opportunity for securing
parts or chorus positions is unusually
good. Owing to the ruling of the Uni
versity Conference in regard to the
"one performance" rule, it. will be
useless for those who have appeared
in any other production this semester
to try out.
