# COLLEGE MEN ASKED TO WITNESS INAUGURATION.
## 
### 
As an escort of honor at the inaugu
ration ceremonies of President-elect
Woodrow Wilson, the former Prince
ton president, a movement has been
started in the east to have represent
atives from all the large universities
in the country at Washington on
March 1. Joseph R. Truesdale, chair
man of the intercollegiate committee
with offices in the Capital, has asked
Stanford to send a delegation to
march in the inaugural parade.
Delegations of twenty men are asked
for from each of the important uni
versities and colleges. Special rail
road rates are to lie obtained at the
time. Whether or not Stanford will
lie represented has not yet been de
cided.
