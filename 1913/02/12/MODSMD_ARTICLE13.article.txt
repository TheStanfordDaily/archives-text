# Freshmen Smoker in Encina.
## 
### 
Oil Friday evening. February 21, the
Freshmen of Encina Club will be the
hosts of the men of the class at an in
formal smoker in the Encina Club
room. The desire to become better
acquainted with each other has
prompted this social assembly of the
first-year men and a suitable program
is now being planned for the evening.
