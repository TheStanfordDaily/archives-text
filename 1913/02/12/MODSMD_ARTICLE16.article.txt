# WOMEN'S FENCING CONTEST ARRANGED WITH CALIFORNIA.
## 
### 
An intercollegiate Women's fencing
meet will be held this year. A team
of eight will be chosen at the meeting
of Foil and Mask at its meeting Thurs
day evening to represent that organi
zation in a tourney with the California
women on February 22. The Stanford
women have been victorious in the last
three annual contests, and this season
under the competent instruction of H.
W. Malone.v have developed some re
markable fencers. California has a
new coach this year who is developing
a strong team for the competition.
