# College Notes.
## 
### 
Dartmouth will send its track team
to a meet in Denver.
The honor system was adopted at
die University of Illinois by a three
fourths vote.
493 students were expelled at the
State Agricultural and Mechanical
College of Texas for hazing and in
subordination.
More than five hundred students at
the University of Washington ban
queted with members of the faculty at
the Commons which corresponds to
the proposed Stanford Union.
Oregon University co-eds who found
themselves shut out of an 8 o'clock
class answered to tlie roll call through
the windows, wrote out their class
papers and handed them in when the
doors opened at the end of the period.
