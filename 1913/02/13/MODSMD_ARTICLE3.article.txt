# Forest Service Open to Students.
## 
### 
Stanford men will he employed in
the Forest Service again this summer.
All students preparing for forestry
who wish to undertake this work
should sign up at once with Professor
L. Abrams of the Botany department.
For several years students of For
estry have been given positions during
the summer vacation. Last year eight
men were assigned positions in the
forests of California as forest assist
ants.
