# Second Opera Tryouts Tomorrow.
## 
###

Junior Opera cast who passed the pre
liminary tryouts Wednesday must be
at the Assembly Hall Saturday at
lOrl'O. The committee will begin the
work of choosing the characters for
the opera at that time.
