# President Talks Informally to Men.
## 
### 
"The College Man in the Home" was
the subject of an informal talk given
the men of the university last evening
by President David Starr Jordan in the
Encina clubroom. Eugenics and hap
piness and many phases of home life
were discussed. Dr. Jordan's last talk
In his series will be given next month
when he will deal with "The College
Man in Social Life."
