# Meeting Called For League Players.
## 
### 
Reorganization of the Peanut League
has been decided upon by Coach Peters
and with that end in view a meeting
has been called for tomorrow after
noon. All players in the league and all
others desiring to get on one of the
three teams are requested to meet in
room 50 at 1:15.
Tuesday team 1 defeated team 3 by a
score of 4 to 1.
The schedule for the remainder of
the week follows: Thursday, 1 vs. 2;
Friday. 2 vs. 3; Saturday morning, 1
vs. 3.
