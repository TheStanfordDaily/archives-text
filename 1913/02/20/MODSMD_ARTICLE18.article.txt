# Fort Barry vs. Stanford Quintet.
## 
### 
The Encina gym basketball quintet
number 2 will meet the fast Fort Barry
team on Saturday evening. Two weeks
ago the local team was defeated by this
same aggregation on their courts by a
36-16 score. The game will be called
at 7 o'clock on the Encina Gym courts.
The Stanford line-up is to be: Price,
Best, guards; Lammiman, center; Bee
be, Wilcox, and McMahon, guards.
