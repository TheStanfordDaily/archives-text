# DRAWING FOR SOPHOMORE COMEDY TICKETS MONDAY.
## Sale For Reserved Seats Changed From Morning to Afternoon at Bookstore and Weingartners. 
### 
Numbers for ticket purchasers for
the Sophomore comedy "On the Quiet'
will he drawn at S o'clock Monday
morning, at the Bookstore. Sale ot
tickets will begin at 1:15,
The sale had to be changed to Mon
day, instead of being held Saturday as

first decided, on account of Washing
ton's birthday. The Bookstore will he
closed all day Saturday.
By holding the sale of tickets over
till afternoon Monday, the manage
ment of the play hopes to escape the
cutting of morning classes that has
always resulted from morning sales.


Miss E. Trent and 3liss I). Cooper in
"On the Quiet."
