# The 						New Boathouse
## 
### 
For a number of years Stanford has
taken great pride in the excellency of

her athletic equipment.
Nor was any praise that
was directed toward the

forty acre park where
football, track and baseball hold sway,
misapplied. But when reference was
made to crew quarters, the less said
the better. Thanks to R. P. Duryea, J.
S. Grepe, Professor C. B. Wing, the
Board of Control and the Board of
Trustees, the ancient rustic shack that
has marred the shores of Lagunita for
so long is no more. Til its stead we
have an up todate boathouse that is
welcomed by oarsmen, swimmers and
the University community in general.
All we need to make our joys complete
is for Jupiter Pluvius to favor us with
his presence so that we can use our
mose recent acquisition.
