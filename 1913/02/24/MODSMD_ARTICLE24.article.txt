# I.B. CROSS TO DELIVER ADDRESSES AT BERKELEY.
## 
### 
As a step in the movement to estab
lish closer relations between the facul
ties of Stanford and the University of
California, Assistant Professor I. B.
Cross has been invited to deliver a
series of public addresses at Califoi -
nia. The invitation which comes from
the department of Political Science
covers a program of five weekly ad
dresses on social and socialist move
ments. The first lecture, entitled
"The Utopians," will be given next
Tuesday. The succeeding topics are
"The Scientific Socialists," "The An
archists," "The Syndicalists." The con
cluding lecture will be in the nature of
a summary.
