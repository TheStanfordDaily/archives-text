# JUNIOR PROM COMMITTEE PLANS FOR ANNUAL DANCE.
## 
### 
In anticipation of the annual Junior
week festivities Chairman C. F. Robb,
chairman of the Prom committee, has
begun active preparations for the us
ual formal. Tickets to the dance are
to lie placed on sale on March 1. As
has been customary in the past the
number has Leen limited. This year
one hundred and twenty-five are to be
sold.
It. has been decided to reserve the
ninth and twelfth dances as the sup
per dances. This year a new plan is to
be tried in regard to these dances. The
committee will not announce this new
"stunt" until the evening of the Prom.
Encina clubroom is to be the scene of
the affair.
