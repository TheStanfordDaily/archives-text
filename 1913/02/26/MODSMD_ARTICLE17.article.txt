# Y.W.C.A. HEARS PLANS FOR EXPOSITION WORK.
## 
### 
Plans for the work of the Y. W. C. A.
during the 1915 Exposition were out
lined by Miss Ella Schooley of San
Francisco at a joint meeting of the
Women's Civic League, the Vocational
Opportunities club and the Y. W. C. A.
yesterday afternoon.
The problem of caring for the thous
ands of young women who will come
to San Francisco lor the Fair has been
turned over by the officials to the Y.
W. C. A.and Miss Schooley has come
from the east to take charge of the
work as executive secretary of the Ex
position committee.
