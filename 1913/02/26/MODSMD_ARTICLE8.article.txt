# Missouri Conference Invites "S" Men.
## 
### 
The Alumni Managing Committee of
the Missouri Valley Conference has
invited Stanford to participate in its
3ixth annual Track and Field meet in
St. Louis, Mo., Saturday, May 31.
The Conference meet is held on
Francis Field, which was used as the
athletic stadium for the Olympic games
in 1904 by the Louisiana Purchase Ex
position. This historic stadium is now
the property of Washington University
and one of the finest of its kind in the
United States.
The Alumni Managing Committee
consists of representatives from Drake
University, Towa, lowa State College,
University of Kansas, University of
Missouri, University of Nebraska, and
Washington University.
