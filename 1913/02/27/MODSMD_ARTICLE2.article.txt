# Masquers to Entertain.
## 
### 
Masquers have decided upon a lively
Margaret, Cameron skit, "The Piper
Pays" as their entertainment for the
Women's League meeting on March
sixth. The club has asked the assist
ance of two students not members of
the society, which is an innovation.
