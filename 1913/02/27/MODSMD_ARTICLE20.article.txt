# Prize Offered for Prom Program.
## 
### 
In anticipation of securing something
out of the ordinary for their dance pro
gram, the Junior Prom Committee
lias offered a prize for designs. Five
dollars will be awarded the successful
competitor and all efforts should be
submitted to C. F. Robb, '14 us, soon
as possible.
