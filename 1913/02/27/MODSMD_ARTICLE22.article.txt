# Schubert Club to Sing.
## 
### 
Schubert Club is planning for a
number of appearances this semester,
in conjunction with the men's Glee
Club it is to furnish the music for the
Founders' Day exercises on March 9.
An entertainment is to be given at
Belmont School and the club is plan
ning a concert on the campus later in
the semester.
Under the direction of Mr. Buehrer.
the two clubs are rehearsing the state
ly music which has been chosen for the
occasion of the Founders' Day exer
cises.
