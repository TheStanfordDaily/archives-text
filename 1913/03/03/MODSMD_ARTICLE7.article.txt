# PROGRAMS FOR BAND DANCE MAY BE HAD AT BOOKSTORE.
## 
### 
..Programs for the band dance that
will be given at the Armory March 7.
can now be obtained at the Bookstore
on presentation of tickets.
New music will feature the dancej
and intermissions are to be allowed
for serving refreshments. Frequent
practice concerts have been held by
the band and three rehearsals are
scheduled for this week.
