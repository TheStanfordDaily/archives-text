# LABOR LEADER TO DISCUSS "DEATH-ROLL OF INDUSTRY"
## 
### 
W. J. French, a member of the State
Industrial Accident Board will deliver
an illustrated lecture on "The Death
roll of Industry" before Professor
Cross's class in Charities on Friday
morning. In the afternoon he will
address the engineers on the same sub
ject and will show for the first time in
public the results obtained by the Ac
cident Board during the past two years.
The address to the engineers will
be at 2:00 Friday in room 550, Metal
lurgy Building and will be open to the
public. French has been an import
ant factor in the San Francisco labor
situation.
