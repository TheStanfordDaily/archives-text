# California Notes.
## 
### 
The total number of A. S. U. C. cards
sold is now 2628.
"The Fortune Hunter" will be given
by the Mask and Bagger society on
March 14.
Managers of the various student pub
lications have formed an "Advertising
Club."
Twenty-five undergraduates were
elected to Phi Iteta Kappa. Twenty sen
iors, or one tenth of the graduating
class and five juniors were chosen.
The Freshmen crew squad at Cali
fornia now numbers nineteen men,
while the varsity candidates have been
reduced to twenty-four. Work in the
shells 011 the Oakland estuary is the
order of the day five times a week,
when six to ten miles are covered.
