# DR. ELOESSER WILL SPEAK BEFORE PHYSIOLOGY CLUB
## 
### 
Dr. Leo Eloesser of the Medical de
partment will give the second of the
series of lectures under the auspices
of the Physiology Club in the Chapei
Tuesday evening March 11. His sub
ject will be "Grafts and Transplanta
tions of the Human Tissues." The
lecture will include a short review of
the history of surgery and transplanta
tions with a survey of the development
of the subject and some of the newer
operations. This lecture will touch
upon Carrol's work at the Rockefeller
Institute of New York, including the
grafting of whole limbs in dogs and
the transplantations of kidneys and
other organs.
