# Mrs. Allan Will Speak.
## 
### 
Mrs. E. W. Allan, dean of women,
will speak Tuesday evening at the
regular meeting of the Women's Civic
League on the subject of "The Injunc
tion of the Abatement Act." This bill
is to come up before the Legislature
soon and is one which will directly
concern the women voters. All of the
women of the University are urged to
attend.
