# Quads.
## 
### 
A. L. Rockwood, '11, is visiting on
the campus this week.
Wheeler O. North, '04 was on the
campus yesterday. He has just re
turned from the Island of Borneo
where he has had charge of the erec
tion of a plant for the manufacture of
rubber for the United Malaysian Rub
ber Company.
T. .T. Hoover, '01, brother of 11. C.
Hoover, was a recent visitor on the
campus. Hoover expects to remain
in this section of the state for about a
week, engaged in business connected
with extensive mining interests which
he represents.
