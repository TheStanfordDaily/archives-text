# Fourth Spring Botany Trip Saturday.
## 
### 
Entrants for the fourth spring trip
of the Botany 1 class will leave the
laboratory Saturday morning March
15, at 8:30, for King's Mountain. A
bus has been engaged for those who do
not desire to make the trip on horse
back or on bicycles. Lunch will be
served at the Mountain House and the
party will return about 5 o'clock.
