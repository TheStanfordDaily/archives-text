# CALIFORNIA MEN TO GIVE SERIES OF TALKS HERE
## 
### 
The Department of Political Science
of the University of California has ac
cepted the invitation of Stanford to de
liver a series of lectures here. This in
vitation is reciprocal to the one ex
tended by California to Stanford, upon
which Professor I. I>. Cross is making
weekly addresses at Berkeley.
The first address at Stanford will be
given on March 20, by Professor Reed
of California. Reed held the position
of Secretary to Governor Johnson, re
signing to return to the University
work. The next address of the series
will lie given by Professor Barrows, the
head of the department and past head
of the Bureau of Education in the Phil
ippines.
