# A.W. HIGGINS. '12 WILL CAPTAIN SOCCER TEAM
## 
### 
A. W. Higgins, '12, was elected by
this year's victorious soccer team to
captain the 1913-14 eleven. The cap
tain-elect has played on the team since
soccer was introduced as an intercol
legiate game. He registers in Electri
cal Engineering from Hockley, Eng
land.
