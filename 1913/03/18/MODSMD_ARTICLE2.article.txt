# LAW STUDENTS WILL HEAR RAILROAD COMMISSIONER
## 
### 
Honorable John M. Eshleman, pres
ident of the state railroad commission,
will address the students of the Law
department Thursday at 3:30, in room
12 Mr. Eshleman will discuss "The
Organization of and Practice before
the Railroad Commission of the State
of California."
The speaker will come to Stanford
at the request of Phi Alpha Delta, the
honorary legal fraternity.
