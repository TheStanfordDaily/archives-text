# DR. JORDAN WILL TOUR EUROPE NEXT SEMESTER
## 
### 
President David Starr Jordan will be
absent from the Univesity next semes
te on a special leave of absence. He
will tour Europe on behalf ol the
World's Peace Society and will deliver
numerous lectures in England in this
cause. Then he will spend some time in
the Balkans, not, as he says in the
capacity of a war correspondent at the
front looking for massacres, but to look
into the conditions which the present
war is causing.
