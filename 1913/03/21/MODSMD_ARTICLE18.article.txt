# BAND TO GIVE CONCERTS SATURDAY AND SUNDAY
## 
### 
The Band will play at the track meet
tomorrow afternoon providing the
weather conditions are favorable.
The regular concert will be held in
the Gncina Club room Sunday at 3
p.m. Chairs will be arranged for the
listeners. The ladies are especially
invited.
The program: —
1. March—Pride of the Yankee Navy
Sousa
2. Overture —"William Tell".. .Roseni
3. Cornet Solo—"Premier Polka —. .
G. P. Ross, '14 Llewellen
4. Suite—"Men of the Western. .
World" Sousa
a. Red Man—b. White Man —c..
Black Man.
5. Waltz—"Jolly Fellows".Vollstedt
6. March —Militare Schubert
7. Intermezzo—After Sunset...Pryor
8. Overture—Poet and Peasant....
Suppe
9. Selection—Songs of the Nations
Dalby
10. March—Federal Sousa
Hail Stanford, Hail.
