# Sacramento Defeated by Second Team.
## 
### 
The second Varsity baseball team
yesterday afternoon defeated the Sac
ramento High School nine by 3 to 0.
The capital city lads put up a Rood
game and, but for one inning, held the
collegians to an even break.
The figures:
R H E
Stanford 3 4 1
Sacramento 0 4 2
Batteries: Anderson, Hunley and
Simpson. Sheehan and Young.
