# Special Edition
## 
### 
A special sporting edition of The
Daily Palo Alto will lie published Sat-

urday afternoon, April 5,
the day of the second base
ball game with California.
This issue will contain the

complete accounts of both Intercol
legiate contests and also the results
of the Wednesday game with Santa
Clara, and the track meet with U. S. C.
which takes place on April 3. Pictures
of both the Stanford and California
nines will be an additional feature.
This paper will be of interest to alumni
and friends of Stanford. All who wish
to have copies mailed to friends must
leave orders at this office before Ap
ril 1. The price including mailing,
will be five cents. This edition will be
delivered to regular subscribers free
of charge.
