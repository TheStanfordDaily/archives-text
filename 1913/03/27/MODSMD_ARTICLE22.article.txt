# STRICT TRAINING RULES GO IN EFFECT MONDAY
## 
### 
Strict training for members of the
varsity track squad are to go into ef
fect on Monday. These regulations
must be rigidly adhered to. A list or
names has been posted in the Track
Training house of the men who must
observe the strict rules. None of these
men are to leave the campus without
permission from "Dad" Moulton or
Captain E. P. Campbell, '13. Eleven
o'clock is the hour fixed as the retir
ing time for the athletes. The follow
ing prohibitions have been announced:
dancing, roughhousing, swimming, pool
or billard playing, motor-cycling, eat
ing between meals, handball or tennis
or any exercise which tends to stiffen
the muscles.
