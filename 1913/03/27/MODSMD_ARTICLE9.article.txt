# MALONEY IS RE-ENGAGED BY EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE
## 
### 
The Executive Committee last even
ing decided to re-engage H. W. Ma
loney as assistant trainer for another
year. Graduate Manager Burbank
was instructed to secure his services
again.
On account of other attractions but
few members of the Committee were
present last evening and but little bus
iness was done. The minutes follow:
Meeting was called to order with
A. W. Ambrose, '13, in the chair.
Roll call. Summerfield, Duryea.
Frank, Clover, Loud, absent.
Minutes of meeting of March 19 read
and approved.
Move, seconded and passed that a
metal cast be made of the Block "S"
meda!.
Karl Schaupp was given permission
to use the Block "S" medal cast.
Moved, seconded and passed that the
Graduate Manager be authorized to
sign up H. W. Maloney for another
year in his present capacity.
Adjourned.
E. P. Campbell, Secretary.
