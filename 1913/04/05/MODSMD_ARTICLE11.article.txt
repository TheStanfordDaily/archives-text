# WOMEN WIN TENNIS FROM SOUTHERN CALIFORNIAS
## Murray and Sheldon Do Not Murray and Shedon Do Not Compete 
### 
Stanford women were returned the
victors in the tourney with the
racket, wielders from the University
of Southern California played on the
Encina tennis courts Wednesday and
Thursday. The Stanford men were
not so fortunate, hut neither Murray
or Sheldon played any games because
of their entry in the track meet with
the University of Southern California,
Thursday afternoon. Both champions
defaulted their matches to the south
erners.
