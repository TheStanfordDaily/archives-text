# PROFESSOR WOODWARD WILL BE ABSENT NEXT SEMESTER
## 
### 
The Hoard of Trustees at their last
meeting granted Professor F. C.
Woodward a sabbatical leave of absence
for next semester. Professor Wood
ward will sail for England from
Montreal on May 30. and will spend
the summer there. After leaving
England he will go to France, where
he will remain for the fall months.
