# CALIFORNIA NAMES TRACK CREW AND TENNIS ELIGIBLES
## Ninety-Seven Have Chance for Track Twenty-Six for Crew, and Twelve for Tennis. 
### 
Professor T. M. Putman, chairman
of the faculty athletic committee of
California, has submitted the names of
the California eligibles for track, crew,
and tennis. The track list numbers
ninety-seven; that of tennis twelve.
The crew list contains twenty-six
eligibles for the varsity boat and
eighteen for the freshman shell.
