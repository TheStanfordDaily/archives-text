# EXPERT WILL SPEAK TO ALL ENGINEERS
## 
### 
H. B. McMaster, of Youngstown,
Ohio, Commissioner of the Associated
Metal Lath Manufacturers, is to ad
dress all engineers in the University
on "Some of the Phases of Fire Proof
Construction" in the Chapel Tuesday
afternoon at two o'clock.
One hundred lantern slides are used
in illustrating the talk, which is gen
eral in character, much attention being
paid to the work now being done by
the National Bureau of Standards at
Washington, along the lines of an in
vestigation of the properties of build
ing materials.
The lecture is a well-known one and
lias been delivered at the principal
Universities and before the leading
Engineering Societies of the country.
All engineers are invited.
