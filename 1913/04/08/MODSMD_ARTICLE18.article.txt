# BASKETBALL TEAM PLAYS WHITTIER HIGH TOMORROW
## 
### 
Wednesday evening the Encina
basketball team will play the Whit
tier high school team on the Encina
courts.
The prep, school lads have defeated
the University of Southern California
and Whittier college teams and claim
the championship of the South.
The Encina team will line-up the
same as in the California games. The
game will be called at 8 o'clock sharp.
Admission will be 25 cents.
