# WOMEN WILL JOLLY-UP ALONE WHILE MEN ENTERTAIN PREPS
## 
### 
Roble gymnasium will be the scene
of a jolly-up for all university women
while the men are playing host to the
Interscholastic athletes Saturday
evening.
The affair is in charge of the con
ference committee of the Young
Women's Christian Association, and
promises many surprises in the nature
of stunts and "eats." An admission
of fifteen cents will be collected for
the benefit of the Conference Fund.
