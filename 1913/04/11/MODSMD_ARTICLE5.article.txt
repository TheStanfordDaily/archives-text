# STAGE IS SET FOR THE CARNOT CONTEST
## Tincher, Smith, and Higgins Represent Stanford This Evening Against Crack California Debaters. 
### 
At 6 o'clock this evening a specific
subject chosen from the general topic
"France and Republicanism" will he
announced to six Carnot debaters rep
resenting Stanford and California. At
eight o'clock, in the twentieth Carnot
contest, these six men will debate this
subject in the Assembly Hall. Presi
dent Jordan is to be presiding officer
of the evening and the Stanford band
will play. A cordial invitation is ex
tended to the public to attend this
leading debating event.
E. B. Tincher, '12, E. C. Smith, '13,
and P, L. Higgins, '15, will represent
Stanford, with S. C. Peck, '12, as their
alternate. L. E. Goodman, '13, J. J.
Miller, '13, and A. W. Drury, '14, com
pose California's trio.
