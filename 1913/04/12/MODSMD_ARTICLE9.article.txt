Tournament has already brought forth
a number of high-class matches. In
the doubles, three teams, Bulls and
Johns of Lowell, Wilder and Rogers

reached the semi-finals in the singles.

be McCormick and Barber of Los
Angeles. This team plays a strong

Rogers of Thatcher appears to have that
best chance of winning the cup that
goes to the winners.

parative ease.
