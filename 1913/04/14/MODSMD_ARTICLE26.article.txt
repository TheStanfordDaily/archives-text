# HOTEL VENDOME CHOSEN FOR THE SENIOR BALL
## McDonald's Orchestra to Provide Music; Comfortable Car Service from Campus to Hotel. 
### 
The Hotel Vendome in San Jose
will be the scene of the Senior ball on
Monday evening, May 19. Accommo
dations are being made for a record
attendance.
McDonald's orchestra has been en
gaged to provide dance music. The
hotel management will cater for the
supper, and special care will be
used in selecting a tasteful menu.
Cars will carry passengers from the
Bookstore direct to the door of the
hotel. To go early and return late
will be the program. The cars will
leave the campus shortly after 7
o'clock.
