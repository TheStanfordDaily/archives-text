# PEACE FLAGS ON DISPLAY.
## 
### 
Two silk Peace Flags will be on ex
hibition in the Library of the Depart
ment of Education, Wednesday, Thurs
day and Friday of this week. One is
the national peace Hag and the other
is the international emblem. The
former is a combination of the Stars
and Stripes and the old flag of truce.
The international flag is a combination
of the emblems of every nation.
