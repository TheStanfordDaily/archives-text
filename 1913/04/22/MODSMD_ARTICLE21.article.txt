# SECOND TALK BY HILL ON CORPORATION METHODS
## 
### 
A second talk ou "Organization of
Corporations" was delivered to law
students yesterday afternoon by (1.
H. Mill, '09. In an informal way Mr.
Hill explained by the use of diagrams
the methods of recording and trans
ferring stock, and exhibited actual
stock certificates and directors' reso
lutions. The conduct of stockholders,
and directors meetings including re
ports was explained. In closing the
speaker touched upon the license tax,
franchise tax, federal incorporation
tax and general property tax.
