# ENGLISH CLUB WILLING TO TAKE OVER SEQUOIA
## 
### 
The proposed amendment to the
constitution of the Associated Students
giving the English Club the control
and management of the "Sequoia,"
was approved by the club yesterday.
The following officers were elected
for the coming semester:
President, .T. L. Duff, '13; vice-presi
dent, E. H. Ford, '14; secretary, C. F.
Paul, '13; treasurer. Miss M. Tis
dale, '15.
J. L. Duff, '13, was nominated editor
of the Sequoia and C. F. Paul, '13,
chosen to run for' manager of the
monthly.
