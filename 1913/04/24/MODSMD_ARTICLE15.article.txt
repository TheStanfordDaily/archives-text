# ENGINEERS ELECT OFFICERS.
## 
### 
A. C. Sandstrom, '14, was elected to
the presidency of the Civil Engineer
ing Society at the last meeting. D.
L. Narver, '14, was chosen vice-presi
dent; J. B. Cox, '14,, secretary, and E.
T. Scott, treasurer. Papers were read
by W. J. Goodwin, '13, R. R. Blase, '14,
VV. H. Doxsee, '12, E. T. Scott, '13, and
J. 15. Cox, '14.
