# Mayfield Marshal Kills Stranger.
## 
### 
City Marshal A. J. Bradshaw of .May
field last night shot and killed an un
known stranger who has made an as
sault on two boys of the town. Brad
shaw tackled the intruder but was
unable to handle him. He called on
City Trustee Antone Anderson and E.
C. Klevesahl, a brewer, for assist
ance. The unknown assailant drew a
knife and severely cut the officer's
assistant's. Both are in the Peninsula
hospital. The body is at Palo Alto
morgue.
