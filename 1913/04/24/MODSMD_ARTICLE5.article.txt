# Science Association Plans Lectures.
## 
### 
The Science Association of Stanford
heard last night an address on "The
Electrical Properties of Small Part
icles in Liquid Media" by Professor
Slonaker of the Physiology Depart
ment. After the lecture Dr. Meyer of the
Anatomy Department was chosen to
be president for the next year. Pro
fessor Slonaker of the Physiology De
partment was elected secretary-treas
urer. A discussion of the advisability
of giving a series of popular science
lectures during the'next semester fol
lowed . The plan was heartily in
dorsed by those present.
