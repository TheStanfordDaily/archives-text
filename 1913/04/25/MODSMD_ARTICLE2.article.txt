# DAD MOULTON RECEIVES GIFT FROM BABE CRAWFORD
## 
### 
Dad Moulton yesterday received a
handsome silverheaded cane from T).
P. Crawford, '1)9, as a token of the
esteem in which Stanford's veteran
trainer is held by the famous Cardinal
athlete. "Babe" Crawford was one of
the greatest athletes who ever wore
the "S." He was a star on the football
team for four years and captained the
winning 10OS Varsity. Crawford also
won points in three Intercollegiate
track meets.
