# SUN FALLS AT NOON.
## 
### 
Vow Sun, driving the laundry wagon
bearing that name, was thrown from
bis wagon today and seriously in
jured. The accident took place short
ly after twelve o'clock on Salvatierra
street. The horse became frightened,
overturned the wagon and pinned the
driver under the front wheel. Sun
was hurried to the Peninsula Hospital
in a passing machine where he is re
ported to lie suffering from a frac
tured frontal hone and a complication
of internal injuries.
