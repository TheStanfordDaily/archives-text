# ADVANCE SEAT SALE FOR SENIOR COMEDY IS BRISK
## 
### 
The drawing of numbers and sale
of tickets for "Seven Sisters" com
menced shortly after one o'clock today
at the Bookstore and at Weingartner's
in Palo Alto. A brisk sale has been
conducted at both offices during the
afternoon, and at an early hour several
hundred dollars had been received.
Tickets will remain on sale both on
the campus and in Palo Alto until the
time of the performance of the Senior
comedy on the evening of May 14. The
most desirable seats will be disposed
of early in the sale, and purchase
should not be delayed.
