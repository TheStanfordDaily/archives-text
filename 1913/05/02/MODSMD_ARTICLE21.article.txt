# Dooling Receives $1,000 Damages.
## 
### 
Presided over by F. W. Charles,
justice of the peace for Palo Alto, the
case of Dooling vs. Warlow was con
ducted by members of the class 011
California Practise. H. E. White, '11,
and G. Holcomb, '11, defended Warlow,
while J. P. Miller, '12, and F. 11. Hil
ton, '11, acted as counsel for the plain
tiff.
By an even three-fourths vote the
jury found for the plaintiff, but re
duced the sought for $5,000 damages to
$1,000 (payable in as many years).
