# CLUBS ELECT OFFICERS
## 
### 
The Mandolin club met last night
and elected C. W. Knight, '14, leader;
G. Wilson, '14, manager; G. H.Mitchell,
'14, G. Hutchinson, '15, and R. Parkin
son, '15, executive committee.
The Women's Educational Club have
elected the following officers: Presi
dent, Miss M. H. Thompson, '14; vice
president, Miss I. R. C'uneo, '14; secre
tary, Miss A. .1. Fortin, '14.
