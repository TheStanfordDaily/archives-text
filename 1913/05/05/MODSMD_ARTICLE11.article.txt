# FRENCH CLUB WILL STAGE PLAY
## 
### 
The French Club of Stanford will
present "L 'Anglais tel qu' on le
Parle'' at the Gamma Phi Reta house
Tuesday evening at S p.m. The cast
is as follows: "Retty." Miss Agnes
Gray, *15; "La Cassiere," Miss Ruth
Lorraine, '16; "G. Hogson," R. 0.
Stidson, sr.; "Jullen,'' E. J. Oberle,
'15; "L'lnterprete," G. Lawrence, '13;
"L'lnspecteur," S. L. Bruce, '16;
"Le Garcon," J. E. Mead, '16.
