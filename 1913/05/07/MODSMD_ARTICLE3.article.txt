# Senior Week Programs New on Sale
## 
### 
Senior Week programs appeared at
the Bookstore yesterday, tastefully
bound in gray leather and embossed in
silver. The contents of the booklet
embrace the Senior committees, Senior
Week program, and the class roll, in
cluding all 1913 men and women. The
reading matter is interspersed with
cuts. Every Senior who has his
receipt for class dues can obtain one,
and the limited remainder will be sold
at fifty cents each.
