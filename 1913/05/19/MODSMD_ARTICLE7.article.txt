# SATURDAY EVENING FOUR CLASSES HOLD REUNIONS
## 1911, 1903, 1898, 1908 Get Together for Renewal of Old Acquaintances. 
###

held reunions. Dinner time was the
hour set in each case and the reunited
classes enjoyed the usual toasts and
after dinner speeches.
