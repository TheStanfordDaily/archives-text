# Graduates are Instructors.
##

viser, and G. J. Hatfield, '11, are among
those named to give courses in the
Evening Law College of the San Fran
cisco Y. M. C. A. Roth is to instruct in
equity, jurisdiction and commercial
law of accountancy. Pleading and
practice are the topics assigned to
Hatfield. 13oth men have the degree
of juris doctor from the law depart
ment.
