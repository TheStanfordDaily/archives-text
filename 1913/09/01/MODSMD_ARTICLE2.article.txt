# Needham Made Y.M.C.A. Secretary.
## 
### 
G. E. Needham, '13, member of tfrS
varsity track squad for two years,
has been made assistant state secre
tary of the Y. M. C. A. of Oregon. His
headquarters are in Portland. Dur
ing the summer Needham took a
course in the Y. M. C. A. training
school at Estes Park, Colorado.
