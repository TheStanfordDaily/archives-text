# Freshmen Handbooks at Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
The Freshmen handbook, commonly
known as the "bible", is now ready
for distribution at the Y. M. C. A.
This year's edition is an improvement
over the former issues and contains
complete information necessary to
acquaint the new students with Stan
ford affairs. R. E. Needham, '16,
edited and managed the book. Stud
ents may obtain copies of the hand
book by calling at the Y. M. C. A.'
headquarters.
