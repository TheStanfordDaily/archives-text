# Harvest Large Crop.
## 
### 
The largest grape crop ever taken
from the Stanford ranch at Vina, Te
hama county, is now being harvested.
It is estimated that 6300 tons of grapes
will be gathered. This is a considera
ble increase over last season. In ad
dition to the grape crop the ranch has
been planted to peaches, prunes, pears,
apricots, almonds, walnuts,
and alfalfa. There are 4200 acres on
the big tract. The nut crop is fair
this year.
