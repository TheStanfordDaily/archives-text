# MATRICULATION SERVICE WILL INTEREST FRESHMEN
## 
### 
Pending the completion of the Me
morial Church, services of public wor
ship will be held as follows: Sun
day, September 7, the Matriculation
Service will take place in the chapel
at 11 o'clock. D. Charles Gardner and
Karl Schaupp, '12, will address the
congregation. Members of the Fresh
man class are especially urged to at
tend the meeting. Beginning Monday,
September 8, daily chapel service will
be held in the vestry of the Memorial
Church, from 8 A.M. to 8:15 A.M..
Mr. L. H. Eaton, the organist, will con
duct an organ recital in the Assembly
Hall every day at 4 P.M.
