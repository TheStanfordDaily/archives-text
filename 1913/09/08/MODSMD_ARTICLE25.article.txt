# Positions Wanted.
## 
### 
E. H. Lockwood, Y. M. C. A. Secre
tary, reports that the demand for em
ployment from students still exceeds
the supply. The Y. M. C. A. employ
ment bureau has already placed forty
eight men in various positions, but
ninety-eight more are desirous of se
curing jobs, and anyone having any
sort of work to be performed is re
quested to inform Lockwood.
