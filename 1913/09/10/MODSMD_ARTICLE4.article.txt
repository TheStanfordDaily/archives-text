# Student Conference to Meet.
## 
### 
For its first session of the year the
University Conference will meet
Thursday evening at 7:45 in the Coun
cil Chambers. Several important
matters which have arisen since th.j
last meeting are to bo brought before
this governing body of men. A large
number of vacancies have arisen in
the ranks of the Conference through
graduation, and arrangements will be
made tomorrow evening for elections
for the filling of these vacancies. The
elections are to be held in the various
departments.
