# Women's Press Club Elects Officers.
## 
### 
Miss Callie Smith, '14, has been
chosen President of the Women's
Press Club. She is a member of the
staff of the Daily Palo Alto and the
1014 Quad staff. The new secretary
treasurer is Aliss Agnes Gray, '15, of
the Sequoia staff. Miss Marjorie
Bailey. '14, was elected to membership.
She is a member of Cap and Gown,
English Club and Art Editor of the
Sequoia and 1014 Quad.
