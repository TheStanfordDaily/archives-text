# Gymnasts to Compete.
## 
### 
Gym club tryouts are to be hel,l
Thursday in Encina gymnasium. The
test is to be conducted as follows.
Three stunts will be set on each piece
of apparatus, including horizontal
bar. parallel bars, horse, buck and
mats. Each stunt is to count from one
to ten points. This makes it possible
to make thirty points on each piece*
or 150 points for the entire test.
Passing grade will be decided by the
committee.
