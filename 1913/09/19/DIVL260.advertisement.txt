CHANGE IN ROUTING OF TRAINS
By the wreck of Train No. 7, at Santa Margarita, indefinite block
ade has been established. Train f-ervice will be as follows:
Trains No. -1 and 22 will be run between San Francisco and
Cuesta, and between San Luis Obispo and Los Angeles, and auto ser
vice will bridge the gap. Trains 23 and 24, from Los Ange/es, will run
the same way. Trains 17 and 18 are annulled.
Trains 78 and 77, the Shore Line Limited. Trains 9 and 10, the New
Orleans Express, and Trains 75 and 70, the Lark, will be detoured
via San .Tose and the San Joaquin Valley. Express will be handled
on the Coast Line trains.
This schedule will prevail until the blockade has been broken.
