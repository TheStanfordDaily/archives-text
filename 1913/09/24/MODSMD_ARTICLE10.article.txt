# SIGN-UP FOR BASEBALL MAKES A NEW RECORD
## "FEANUT" LEAGUE SERIES TO START SOON Four Regular Teams With Substitutes Will Compete During the Fall.

fall practice will start with men
011 the field. At the meeting of base
ball men this afternoon in room 214

name, class, age, and position of each
aspirant. These men and any others
who wish to he 011 the squad will re
