# Phi Delta Kappa Elect Officers.
## 
### 
Stanford chapter of Phi Delta Kap
pa, the national education fraternity,
has chosen the following officers for
the year: .T. W. Williams, *13, presi
dent; J. W. Jones, '12, vice-president;
F. A. Scofield, Ml, secretary; H. W.
Schuyler, treasurer. The local chap
ter was recently instrumental in or
ganizing chapters at California and
Washington.
