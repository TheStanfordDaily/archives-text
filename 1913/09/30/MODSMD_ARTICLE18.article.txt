# Rhodes Scholar to Speak Here.
## 
### 
Hugh Moran, 'o,', will speak to the
student volunteer hand in the vestry
of the Memorial Church Friday after
noon at 4:30. This meeting will be
open to others who care to attend.
Moran was the first Rhodes scholar
from Stanford, lie has also spent
several years in the Orient as a Y.
M. C. A. secretary among government
students.
