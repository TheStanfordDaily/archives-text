# Alumnus Enters Y.M.C.A. Work.
## 
### 
H. E. Dennis, '10, sailed Monday for
Shanghai, China, where he will spend
a year in language study in prepara
tion for work as a Y. M. C. A. secre
tary among the government students
of that country. A party of forty
graduates of colleges in the east and
west sailed on the same boat and will
take up similar work in different
parts of the republic.
