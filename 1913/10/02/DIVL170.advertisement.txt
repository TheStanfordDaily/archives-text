To Chicago in hours. Leave San Francisco 4:00 P.M. Daily.

PACIFIC LIMITED
To Chicago in C 9 hours. Leave San Francisco 10:20 A. M. daily.
Arrive Chicago 9:15 A.M. third day.
Carrying standard and tourist Pullmans. Protected by Automatic
Electric Block Signals.
CHANGE IN ROUTING OF TRAINS
By the wreck of Train No. 7, at Santa Margarita, indefinite block
ade has been established. Train service will be as follows:
Trains No. 21 and 22 will be run between San Francisco and
Cuesta, and between San Luis Obispo and Los Angeles, and auto ser
vice will bridge the gap. Trains 23 and 24, from Los Angeles, will run
the same way. Trains 17 and 18 are annulled.
Trains 7S and 77, the Shore Line Limited, Trains 9 and 10, the New
Orleans Express, and Trains 75 and 7fi, the Lark, will be detoured
via San Jose and the San Joaquin Valley. Express will be handled
on the Coast Line trains.
This schedule will prevail until the blockade has been broken.
