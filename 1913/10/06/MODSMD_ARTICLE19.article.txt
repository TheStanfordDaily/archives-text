# Peanut League Starts Season.
## 
### 
teams One and Three of the Peanut
League have started the series with
1000 in their percentage column. In
Tuesdays games One defeated Two,
and Three won from Four. The games
were for four innings. All the men
were used, the first squad playing for
three innings and were then replaced.
This afternoon the second games of
the series are being played. Tues
day s winners will meet on the varsity
diamond, Two and Four playing on the
auxiliary field.
1.000 Syracuse high school students
were expelled for participating in a
strike for new study hours.
