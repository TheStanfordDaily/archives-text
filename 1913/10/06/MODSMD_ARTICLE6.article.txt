# LOCAL FANS TO GET EARLY "WORLD'S SERIES" RESULTS
## 
### 
Arrangements have been completed
whereby "The Daily Palo Alto" will
give out complete details of the
world's series baseball games, start
ing tomorrow afternoon.
Bulletins will be flashed to the
crowds in front of this oflice detail
ing each inning, and complete results
will be published in each evening's
edition on the day of the games. With
the plans laid, and the arrangements
completed, "The Daily Palo Alto" of
fers a baseball service second to no
other paper on the Peninsula.
