# DAILY PALO ALTO SCOOPS CITY EVENING PAPERS.
## 
### 
News from the World's Series base
ball game as given out by "The
Daily Palo Alto" today gave complete
satisfaction to the great crowds in
front of the office.
The special service of the United
Press gave complete detail of every
play, and served the crowds with the
action in detail inning by inning.
The same service will be offered to
morrow. First wires are received at
this office at about 11:30.
