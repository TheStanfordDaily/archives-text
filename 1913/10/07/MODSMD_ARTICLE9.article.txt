# LAW MEN PLAN ANNUAL SMOKER FOR DEPARTMENT
## 
### 
The annual "smoker" given by this
year's candidates for the J. D. degree
to the other members of the Law de
partment, will be held at the Faculty
clubhouse on the evening of Wednes
day, October 15. All the members of
the Pre-Legal and Law departments
are invited to attend. The usual
"smokes" and "eats" will be supplied
to their guests by the sixth year me.i.
The committee in charge of the affair
is: J. A. Davis, L. D. Summerfleld, S.
C. Young, and E. E. Tincher, chair
man.
