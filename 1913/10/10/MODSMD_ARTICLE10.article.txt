# SENIORS WILL JOLLY UP TONIGHT.
## 
### 
Seniors and Holdovers will Jolly
Up this evening in Encina Hall. The
music will start at a little after eight.
In consideration for those who are
in training, the evening's program
has been arranged accordingly. The
voting contest for the handsomest
man, the presentation or the two
door-prizes, and the major portion of
the musical features will all be staged
at an early hour. The admission price
is 35 cents.
