# Root Will Explain Berwick Contest.
## 
### 
R. C. Root, '93, secretary of the
Peace Societies of California, will be
at the University October 13, 14 and 15
to meet students interested in the
Edward Berwick, Jr., Peace Prize
Contest. He will meet students in
Room 230, Economics building, from
9 to 12 a.m. and from 1 to 3 p.m. on
the above dates.
