# California Notes.
## 
### 
Nine Freshmen have been chosen
to begin practice with the varsity
squad at California. These players
are Finley, Johnson, Montgomery,
Reid, Russell, Sharp, Skinner, Smith
and Weeks.
President Benjamin Ide Wheeler
will sail from Bremen on December
13 for New York. He has been on a
leave of absence because of ill health
and has been touring Europe.
One hundred and fifty men entered
the fall interclass meet which was
staged this afternoon. Medals were
given to the first place men and to
the members of the winning relay
team.
