# College Notes.
## 
### 
Nine crews have been selected for
fall practice at Pennsylvania. In
terclass races are planned.
The turnout of soccer men at Uni
versity of Toronto augurs a champion
ship team this year.
The Freshman-Sophomore class bat
tle at Toronto opened with a volley of
rotten eggs fired into the Frosh ranks
by the Sophs. After the firing, close
fighting ensued.
The annual underclass scrap which
has been held at De Pauw will be sub
stituted by a sack rush this year.
The tennis team of McGill is con
tinuing its splendid play by winning
all games so far.
