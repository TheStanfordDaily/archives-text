# RALPH ROSE SUCCUMBED TO TYPHOID YESTERDAY
## 
### 
Ralph Rose, world's champion shot
putter, succumbed to typhoid yester
day morning in San Francisco, after
a two weeks illness. He is known
in athletic and college circles in near
ly every civilized country of the world.
He figured in the Olympic games in
St. Louis, 1904, in London, 190S, and
in Stockholm, 1912. Many times Rose
has competed on the Stanford oval,
and always been supreme in his par
ticular events. As a student Rose at
tended the University of Michigan,
and in recent years has been engaged
in the practice of law.
