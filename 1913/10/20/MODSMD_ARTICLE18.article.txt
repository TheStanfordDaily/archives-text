# French Club Plans Swiss Evening.
## 
### 
Members of the French club have
arranged a program of Swiss enter
tainment for Tuesday evening when
the regular meeting of the organiza
tion is to be held at the Gamma I'lii
Beta house. E. J. Oberle, 'IT), presi
dent of the club, is to give an illus
trated talk on Switzerland. H. 11.
Uronk, '15, and Miss M. H. Rouiller,
'in, are to entertain with Swiss
and Miss Ruth Lorraine and .T. Heath,
'13, will play on the harp and cello.
The meeting is to be open to both the
members and friends of the club.
