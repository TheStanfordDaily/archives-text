# Homer Lea Estate Divided.
## 
### 
The final distribution of the estate
of General Homer Lea, ex-'OO, who
was prominent in the overthrow of the
Manchu dynasty in China, was order
ed today. The estate is valued at
$6230, and was divided between Lea's
wife and two daughters. General Lea's
death occurred at his home in Santa
Monica November 1, 1912, shortly af
ter his return from the Orient.
