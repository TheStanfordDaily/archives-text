# Eighty-two and Active
## 
### 
Dr. Basil L. Gildersleeve, of Johns
Hopkins University, although 82
years old, is still actively engaged as
editor of the American Journal of
Philology.
He says: "My work is all planned
out for at least fifty years ahead, and
1 will not consider retiring until I am
100." Dr. Gildersleeve will celebrate
his eighty-second birthday Thursday.
