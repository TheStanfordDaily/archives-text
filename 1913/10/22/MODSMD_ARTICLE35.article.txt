# Half-day Drill at California.
## 
### 
The military drill program at Cali
fornia yesterday afternoon consisted
of mimic warfare waged against an
imaginary hostile Red army by the
Cadet Corps. Mounted patrol and
scout detachments in advance, and
trained hospital corps in the rear fig
ured prominently in the drill. This
was the first of the two half-day drills
to be held this semester in the vicinity
of "Thousand Oaks." All classes are
dismissed for the events.
