# Tokio Y.W.C.A. Secretary to Speak
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Matthew, who has
spent the last five years in Tokio, will
be the speaker at the meeting of the
Y. W. C. A. in the Vestry on Thursday
at 4:30. Miss Matthew is general sec
retary of the Association in Tokio,
where she was sent by the Associa
tions of the Pacific Coast. This is her
first visit to America since her ap
pointment. All are urged to go to
hear of her work.
