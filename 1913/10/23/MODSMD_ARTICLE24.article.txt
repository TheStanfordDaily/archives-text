# Freshmen Hear Discussion.
## 
### 
The first of a series of discussions
which are being given for the Fresh
men under the direction of Professor
W. H. Carruth, head of the English
department, was held last evening in
Encina. The subject of the talks is
"The Bible and its relation to other
religious works." The meetings of
all the Freshmen interested are to be
held every Wednesday evening at
7:30.
