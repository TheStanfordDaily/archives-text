# BON FIRE PLANS MADE.
## 
### 
To accomplish a more effective or
ganization to carry on the work of
building up material collected for
the Freshmen fire, the bonfire com
mittee has adopted several new plans
of procedure. Chairman W. L. Mil
ler requests that the Freshmen re
port for duty every afternoon at 2
o'clock, and Saturday mornings at
8 o'clock. Owners of boxes, crates or
barrels who desire them removed, are
asked to notify M. F. McC'omb at the
Delta Chi house.
Plans for effectively guarding the
bonfire have also been made.
