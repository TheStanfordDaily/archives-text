# R.G. OTIS, '13, DIED IN PALO ALTO LAST NIGHT
## 
### 
Richard G. Otis, '13, of Durango,
Colorado, was found dead in lied this
morning in his room at 559 Cowper
street, Palo Alto. It is thought by the
physicians who attended him that
heart failure is the cause of death.
Coroner B. E. Kell has been notified
and an inquest will be held this even
ing. Au autopsy will be held late this
afternoon. The deceased has been
suffering from rheumatism and heart
trouble for some time. Otis was a
student in the Civil Engineering de
partment and has been employed in
various Palo Alto establishments.
