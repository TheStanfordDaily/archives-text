# Repairs Made to Salvatierra Street.
## 
### 
Salvatierra street, is at present be
ing resurfaced. The street will be
cut down. Coarse rock will be ap
plied and then a layer of screenings.
The gutters have been renewed. It
is the purpose of the superintendent
to bring the road up to the highest
standard.
