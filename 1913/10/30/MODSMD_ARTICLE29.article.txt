# Professor Cross Returns.
## 
### 
Professor Ira T3. Cross of the Eco
nomics department and his wife have
returned from an extensive trip
throughout the West, where he stud
ied the work of various industrial
boards and commisions. Among the
universities lie visited were Illinois,
Wisconsin and Kansas. While at the
University of Wisconsin Professor
Cross delivered a course of lectures
before the students of that institu
tion on labor and economic condi
tions in California.
Professor Cross is now on a leave
of absence from his work here, as he
is acting as secretary for the State
Industrial Board.
