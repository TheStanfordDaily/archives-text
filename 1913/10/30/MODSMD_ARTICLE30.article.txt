# BIG JOLLY-UP PLANNED BY ALUMNI OF SAN JOSE
## 
### 
Stanford alurani residing in San
.lose are to jolly-up on November 5.
Stunt men from the campus will help
to enliven the evening. C. F. Paul,
10, and B. M. Melvin, '17, will put
on their usual stunt. Selected chor
uses from the Glee and Mandolin
clubs will furnish amusement during
intermissions. The scene of action
is to be the Press club at San Jose.
Ample refreshments are to be served
during the evening.
