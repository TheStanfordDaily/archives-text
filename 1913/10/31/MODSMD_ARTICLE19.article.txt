# THE 						ZOOLOGY DEPARTMENT RECEIVES GIFT OF FISH
## 
### 
Members of the Zoology department
are much pleased with the recent ac
quisition of a rare collection of Alas
kan fishes. The gift was presented
by J. N. Cobb, formerly a member of
the United States Fish Commission.
The specimens were gathered on a
recent trip made in Alaskan waters,
and contain several very rare and de
sirable varieties.
