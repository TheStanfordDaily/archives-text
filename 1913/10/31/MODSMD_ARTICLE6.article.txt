# REFEREE HILL AND WIFE SPEND DAY ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
The second day on American soil of
W. W. Hill, the Australian rugby ex
pert, and Mrs. Hill, is being spent at
Stanford. Graduate Manager Wilcox
acted as special host to the visitors,
who came to the campus this morning.
The afternoon was spent in visitinv
various friends among the faculty and
students. Mr. and Mrs. Hill returned
to San Francisco late this after
noon.
Hill will referee the All Blacks-Cali
fornia game tomorrow.
