# Song Practice.
## 
### 
Song practice will be held on the
bleachers at the Thursday afternoon
football practice. It is at this time
that the varsity line-up will be first
announced. Printed copies of the
songs to be used in the big game will
be distributed by the yell leaders who
will then conduct a song rally.
