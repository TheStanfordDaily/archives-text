# Band Plays In San Jose.
## 
### 
The Stanford Military Band traved
ed to San Jose Friday afternoon to
assist the San Jose high school stud
ents in their rally Friday night before
the game with Palo Alto high played
here Saturday morning.
A concert was given in the after
noon at the high school to which ad
mission was charged to help defray

school band in providing "pep" for the
