# NEW ZEALAND PLAYERS COME BACK TO STANFORD
## 
### 
Many of the New Zealand ruggers
were on the campus yesterday.
Among them were Captain Alex .Ale-
Donald. Vice-Captain Mitchinson, and
Murray, Taylor, Sellars, Wylie and
Gray.
Harry Atkinson and Ted Roberts, of
the All Blacks, were out in their suits
yesterday afternoon helping at the
practice of the Varsity.
Alex Bruce, the New Zealander who
was laid up in the hospital at Palo
Alto, is visiting the campus after hav
ing recovered from the operations on
his leg.
