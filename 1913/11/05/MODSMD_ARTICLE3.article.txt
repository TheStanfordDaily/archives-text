# "GOGETEM" IS SLOGAN OF VARSITY'S CABLEGRAM.
## 
### 
A cablegram received by the
Varsity rugby team from Rio de Ja
neiro proves how widespread is the
interest taken in the Big Game of No
vember 8. It is addressed to Captain
Jimmy Thoburn and consists of the
one expressive word, "GOGETEM."
The signatures are those of J. R. Pem
berton, *08, and C. L. Decius, '13.
