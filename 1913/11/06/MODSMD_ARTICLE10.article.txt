# FRESHMEN ARRANGE SQUADS FOR GUARDING THE CAMPUS.
## 
### 
Arrangements have been made for
guarding the campus and surrounding
points on Friday, night.
The Freshmen have been divided into
four squads for this purpose. All
Freshmen whose names begin with the
letters from A to F, inclusive, are to
report at the Palo Alto Tree at G:3O
Friday evening, where W. A. Lynn will
be in charge; from G to L, inclusive,
meet at the Football Training House,
where B. Y>. Best will take charge;
from M to R inclusive, report to A. J.
Hutchinson at the steps of the History
building; and from S to Z, inclusive,
report to M. F. McComb in front of
the Alpha Tau Omega house.
