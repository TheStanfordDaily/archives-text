# Standing Room On Sale In City.
## 
### 
Graduate Manager It. W. Wilcox, '13,
announces that standing room to the
Big Game will be on sale at 9 o'clock
Saturday morning in San Francisco.
The tickets may he procured at the
Southern Pacific station, at Third and
Townsend streets.
Standing room will also be placed
on sale at the Stanford football field
at the same time, 9 o'clock, Saturday
morning.
