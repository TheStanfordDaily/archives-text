# Freshmen Will Debate This Evening.
## 
### 
Freshmen debaters will meet this
evening in room 22S with a discussion
of the question "Resolved, That the
policy of the administration toward
the Mexican situation is justifiable"
on the program. The affirmative will
lie upheld by H..A. Frye and H. Burn
ham, and the negative by W. T. Ev
enson and A. F. Anthis.
