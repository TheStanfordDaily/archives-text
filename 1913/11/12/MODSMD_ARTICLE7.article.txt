# STANFORD VARSITY MEN HOLD ANNUAL BANQUET.
## 
### 
Stanford's 1913 Varsity rugby men
held their annual banquet last even
ing at the Heidelberg Inn, in San
Francisco, Besides the seventeen play
ers who opposed California Saturday,
Coaches Floyd Brown and George
Presley, Graduate Manager Wilcox,
Trainer Harry Maloney, and Captain
Jimmy Thoburn were present at the
table. Toasts all around were ten
dered to the two coaches, the trainer,
and captain.
After the banquet the ruggers were
guests at a box party at the Pantages
theater, where they saw the pictures
of the intercollegiate match. They
caught the theater train back to the
campus.
