# Phi Alpha Delta Initiates Six.
## 
### 
The Phi Alpha Delta law fraternity
initiated the following men to its
membership: Harold Brooks Lan
dreth, of Pasadena; Urban A. Son
theimer, of San Jose; Philip S. Brooke,
of Spokane; Arthur Leslie Erb, of Ta
coma; Arthur Edwin Worthy, of San
ta Ana; John Brenner, Jr., of San
Francisco. The initiates were ban
queted at the St. James Hotel in San
Jose last Friday evening.
