# From California.
## 
### 
The California varsity will play the
Barbarians on Saturday.
Various post season contests have
been postponed on account of the
weather conditions.
Rain this week lias interfered with
practice of the varsity for the final
rugby game of the season which will
be played in Los Angeles on Thanks
giving day.
California has entered a team in
the cross country race of the P. A. A.
to be run at the stadium in San Fran
cisco on Saturday.
A raffle has been proposed as a
means of disposing of 79 19ir> pipes
in order to prevent a dealer's injunc
iton being filed against the Junior
class.
