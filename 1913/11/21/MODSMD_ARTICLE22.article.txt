# THANKSGIVING SERVICE WILL BE HELD SUNDAY
## 
### 
The regular Thanksgiving service
will be held on Sunday, in the Mem
orial Church, at 11 o'clock. Chaplain
D. Charles Gardner will preach. The
choir anthem will be "O, Lord, How
Manifold Are Thy Works" (Barnby).
Following the service there will be a
celebration of the Lord's Supper.
