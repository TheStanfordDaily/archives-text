# SKILL AND SNAKES ADMITS TEN MEN TO MEMBERSHIP
## 
### 
Skill 1 and Snakes initiated ten
upper classmen to membership this
morning: F. E. Rehm, '15; E. L.
Peck, '13; L. 1. Tilton, '14; A. M.
Standish, '14; F. W. Reeves, '14; H.!
W. Andrews, '14; W. S. Purns, '14; G. j
A. Jacomini, '15; C. W. Knight, '11:
and R. R. Hlase, '14. The usual stunts'
occupied attention on the Quad be
tween classes, and the climax came at
the postoffice steps, where a burlesque !
on the recent Plug Ugly agitation was
staged.
