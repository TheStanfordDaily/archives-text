# PAINTING BILL IS DEBATED BY FRESHMEN
## 
### 
A lively discussion will take place
this evening at the meeting of the
Freshman Debating Society over the
advisability of paying the $30 bill
levied on the Freshman class for the
repainting of a roof on which some
ono had painted a '17 sign. The form
of the question as it has been put be
fore the debaters is that the bill be
paid. 11. V. Aupperle will argue for
this side and I. W. Talboy will talk
against the payment
