# TURKEY DINNER WILL BE SERVED TO RIVAL RIGGERS
## 
### 
An entertainment and banquet ex
pected to stand out in the minds of
the visiting All-Star Interscholastic
ruggers as an enjoyable feature of
their short stay on the Stanford cam
pus has been planned for tomorrow
evening.
Skull and Snakes honor society will
be hosts at a turkey dinner to be given
at the Theta Delta Chi fraternity
house. All of the visiting ruggers will
be present and the members of the
society will share the feast as hosts.
The best amateur talent on the cam
pus has been asked to provide enter
tainment for the high school men.
