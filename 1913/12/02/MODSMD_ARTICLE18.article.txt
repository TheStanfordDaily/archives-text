# PROFESSOR BINGHAM WILL SPEND LEAVE IN EUROPE
## 
### 
Professor J. W. Bingham, of the
Law Department, will leave Stanford
on December 15th, enroute to Europe,
where he expects to spend the coming
semester. Mrs. Bingham will accom
pany him, and they intend to cover all
points of interest, both in the British
Isles and on the continent. Although
the trip is primarily one for pleasure,
Professor Bingham intends to visit
European courts and study their sys
tems and practices.
