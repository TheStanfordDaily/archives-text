# WOMEN'S CLUB PRESIDENT PLEASED WITH THE WORK
## 
### 
Mrs. Payson J. Treat, President of
the Stanford Women's Club, this af
ternoon expressed herself as follows:
"Work on the Women's Clubhouse
project will start tomorrow morning.
This will be the first step in bringing
to a reality this longed for and needed
acquisition to the life of all Stanford
women. We expect to have the
building completed and in use for the
coming May commencement."
