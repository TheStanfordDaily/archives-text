# PROFESSOR WOODWARD TO RETURN FROM EUROPE IN TIME FOR REGISTRATION
## 
### 
Professor F. C. Woodward, head of
the Law Department, will be back at
Stanford in time for registration next
semester. Word to this effect has
been received from Paris, where Pro
fessor and Mrs. Woodward have spent
the past three months. They expect
to sail for this country on December
10th, and will proceed direct to Mid
illetown, New York, where Christmas
will be spent, with Woodward's par
ents, after which they will come direct
to the campus.
