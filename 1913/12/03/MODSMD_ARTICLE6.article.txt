# Jordan Club to Hear Professor Donne.
## 
### 
"Samoan insects and their relation
to human diseases" will be the sub
ject of a talk by Professor R. W.
Doane of the Entomology department
before the Jordan Club tomorrow
evening at S o'clock. The meeting
will be held in the Botany library.
Professor Doane made a specialized
study of the insects of the Samoan
Islands while there last summer.
