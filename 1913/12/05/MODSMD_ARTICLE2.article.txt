# NEW MEMBERS NAMED FOR INTERSCHOLASTIC BOARD
## 
### 
Five new members have been named
to serve on the board of governors of
the Stanford lnterscholastic Athletic
Association. They are C. V. Park, '09,
J. H. Whitted, 'H>, P. R. Wilson, 'IC>,
C. M. Irwin, '16, and V. L. King, '16.
The other members and officers of the
board who will handle the 1914 Inter
scholastic track meet are E. P. Camp
bell, '13, president; E. C. Behrens, '14,
vice-president; P. C. Hamilton, '15,
secretary; Dr. F. Angell, treasurer
and Faculty member of the board; W.
H. B. Fowler, '00; A. W. Ambrose, '13;
P. B. McKee, *14; M. E. Mitchell, *11,
and R. L. Murray, '13.
