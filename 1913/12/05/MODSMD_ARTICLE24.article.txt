# Gym Club Elects New Officers.
## 
### 
H. 11. Burrows, '15, and N. W. Gib
bel, *15, were elected president and
secretary-treasurer respectively of the
Gym Club at its meeting last evening.
Burrows' election is in the nature
of a promotion, as he has held the of
fice of secretary-treasurer for the past
semester. Gibbel has also been active
in the work of the club.
