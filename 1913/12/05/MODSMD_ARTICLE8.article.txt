# DOCTOR GARDNER TO GIVE SEMESTER'S FINAL SERMON
## 
### 
"Special Service, an interpreta
tion," will he the subject of Reverend
D. Charles Gardner's sermon, at the
last service of the semester, to be held
at the Memorial Church next Sunday
morning at 11 o'clock. The chorus
choir, with J. W. Bennett as soloist,
will sing the anthem "Seek Ye the
Lord."
There will be a special service at
8 a. in., for the purpose of celebrat
ing the Lord's Supper.
The daily chapel services will he
discontinued for rest of the semester.
