# Maud Lowell Wins Women's Singles.
## 
### 
Miss Maud Lowell, '15, defeated Miss
Laura Herron, '14, for the women's
interclass singles tennis champion
ship, last Friday afternoon, the score

being 6-2, 3-G, 6-2. The interclass
doubles team will be selected early
next semester, and the tournament for
the interclass cup will be played in
February.
