# Soccer Players Hold Final Practice.
## 
### 
The last soccer practice before the
southern trip will be indulged in this
afternoon on the soccer field. The
first and second teams will line up
against each other for regular play.
The final selection of the team and
substitutes to make the trip to San
Diego for the game with the Nomads
on Christmas day will be made Wed
nesday evening.
