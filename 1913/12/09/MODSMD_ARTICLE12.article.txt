# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB ELECTS
## 
### 
Officers were elected by the Cosmo
politan Club last evening at the regu
lar meeting held at the residence of
the Reverend Father Joseph M. Glea
son in Palo Alto. The list follows: F.
Flugel, '14, president; R. M. Clark,
'14, vice-president; Y. S. Chuck, '17,
secretary; N. .T. Mittenthal, 'IG, cor
responding secretary; Y. Kusama, '16,
treasurer. "The History of Rooks and
Printing" was the subject of a talk
by Father Gleason that followed the
election.
