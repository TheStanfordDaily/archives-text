# PORTLAND CLUB MEETS.
## 
### 
For the last time this semester, the
members of the Portland Club will
meet this evening at the Zeta Psi
house. Several important matters
will come up for discussion. One of
these will be that of the securing of

a joint rate on the homeward journey.
The decision of plans of holiday enter
tainment will also come up, as will
the announcement of the campaign to
boost the Glee Club, recently inaug
urated.
