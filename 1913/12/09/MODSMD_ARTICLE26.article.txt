# Fencing Jackets Voted Women's Team
## 
### 
The live members of the Stanford
women's fencing team who compete
against Berkeley co-eds next semester
shall be given new fencing jackets.
This action was passed upon at the
last meeting of the Hoard of Directors
of the Women's Athletic Association.
Evelyn Trent, '14, and Mary Gard, '15,
were named as a committee to pur
chase the jackets. This year there are
three veterans back in college who
fenced against California. They are
Evelyn Trent, '14, Mary Gard, '15,
and Francis Odenheimer, '16.
