# SUGGESTIONS WANTED FOR A PLUG UGLY SUBSTITUTE
## 
### 
Suggestions for a revised form of
Senior-Junior fight to replace the his
toric Plug Ugly of past years are
being sought by P. B. Mclvee, '14,
chairman of a special revision com
mittee. The committee will report
progress at a meeting of the Univer
sity Conference next semester. The
object in view is to supply an annual
contest between the two upper classes
that will offer excitement, but pre
clude danger of bodily injury.
Anyone who can suggest a plan
which may aid the work of the com
mittee is asked to hand or mail the
notice to McKee.
