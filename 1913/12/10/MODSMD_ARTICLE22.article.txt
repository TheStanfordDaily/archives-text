# SERVICES HELD IN VESTRY FOR MISS RUBY LYNCH
## 
### 
Dr. Gardner officiated at the private
services held late this afternoon in
the vestry for Miss Ruby Lynch. Only
the girls of Miss Lynch's sorority, Pi
Beta Phi, and intimate friends were
present. Several numbers were sung
by the Schubert and Glee Clubs. Mrs.
L. J. Lynch, mother of the deceased,
arrived on the 'campus in the early
afternoon accompanied by Mrs.
Theresa Russell.
