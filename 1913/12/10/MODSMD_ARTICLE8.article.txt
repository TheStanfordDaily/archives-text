# WOMEN SCORE SUCCESS IN CHRISTMAS BAZAAR
## 
### 
One hundred and fifty dollars were
added to tlie funds of the Young
Women's Christian Association as a
result of the bazaar held at the Delta
Gamma house on Saturday. The ma
jority of this money will he given
to the Tokio committee to assist the
work of the association in Tokio and
to defray the expenses of the secre
tary there, Miss Margaret Matthew,
who visited the campus recently. The
rest of the money will be distributed
among the Women's Loan Fund, the
advertising and the news committee.
