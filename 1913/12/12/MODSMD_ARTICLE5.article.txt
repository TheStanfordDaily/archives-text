# PROFESSOR WILDMAN WILL MEET EASTERN ECONOMISTS
## 
### 
Professor M. S. Wildman, head of
the Department of Economics, will go
east next week to attend a convention
of the American Economics Associa
tion which will meet in Minneapolis,
December 27 to 31. While in the east
Professor Wildman will visit some of
the leading universities there for the
purpose of studying the economics de
partments of those institutions. The
list includes the University of Pennsyl
vania, Cornell. University of Chicago,
NorOliwestern University, University
of Minnesota, and Columbia.
