# MRS. HUGH A. MORAN TO SPEAK AT PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Mrs. Hugh A. Moran will speak 011
"China from the American Woman's
Standpoint," 011 Thursday afternoon
at 3 o'clock at the residence of Mrs.
Bradford, 427 Waverly street, Palo
Alto. Mrs. Moran is the wife of Hugh
A. Moran, '05, who was a Rhodes
scholar at Oxford. Owing to her inti
mate knowledge of the question a
large attendance of those interested
is expected.
