# VOCATIONAL CLUB WILL MEET WEDNESDAY EVENING
## 
### 
The Vocational Opportunities club
will meet on Wednesday evening, Jan
uary 14th, at 7:30, In room 4GO (the
Botany lecture room).
Professor E. W. Smith will speak
on "The Place and Training for Wo
men in Journalism," and Miss Bessie

will talk on "Experiences of a Woman
in Newspaper Work." Miss Beatty
spoke at the University two years ago
on "Happy Land," the camp at Los
Gatos carried on by her and other
social workers.
Anyone interested is invited to at
