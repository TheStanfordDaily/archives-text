# Noted Author in China.
## 
### 
Mrs. Davida French Ure, '08, is now
settled in Shanghai, China, where she
will remain with Mr. Ure until the
end of the year, when they will take
up their residence in London. Mrs.
Ure graduated in History and is well
known as a collaborator with Miss L.
Wells, ex-'O9, and Miss E. S. Stevens,
'OS, in the hook of Stanford short
stories, "Not Included in a Sheepskin."
