# THREE FELLOWSHIPS IN ART NOW OPEN TO MEN
## 
### 
Competition is now open for the
three annual Fellowships in Architec
ture, Sculpture and Painting, given by
the American Academy in Rome, a
school of fine arts. Qualifying work
consists of the execution of works of
art, such as drawings, paintings or
models.
The fellowships are all of the value
of $1,000 a year for three years, and
competition is open to all unmarried
men who are citizens of the United
States.
Details of the regulations and condi
tions of the contest may be secured
from Professor Jefferson Elmore, of
the Latin department.
