# Freshmen Must File Cards.
## 
### 
All new students must file their pink
slips with the Student Adviser by to
morrow noon or incur a fine of two
dollars. The limit of the time was
formally sot for tonight, but has been
postponed until the stated time in
order that all may deposit slips with
out being fined.
Yale won the twenty-second an
nual chess tournament between Har
vard, Yale, Princeton and Columbia.
