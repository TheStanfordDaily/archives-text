# May Form Women's Mandolin Club.
## 
### 
A meeting for the purpose of dis
cussing the organization of a Stan
ford Women's Mandolin club will be
held at the Pi Beta Phi house Thurs
day evening at 7:30 o'clock. All wo
men who can play either the mandolin
or guitar are urged to be present.
