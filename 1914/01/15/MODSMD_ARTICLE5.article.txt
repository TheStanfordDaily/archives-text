# Women's Lake Sports Started.
## 
### 
Twenty members of the Women's
Boat club met recently to open the
season of women's sports on the lake.
Under the management of Miss R.
Seeley, '13, a number of regular crews
will he formed, and later in the semes
ter the club will conduct an interclass
regatta.
A new barge, part of considerable
equipment which has been provided
for the members of the club, is ex
pected to arrive on the campus some
time next week.
