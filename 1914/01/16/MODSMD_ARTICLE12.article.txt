# SEVEN MUSICIANS IN NEW WOMEN'S MANDOLIN CLUB
## 
### 
Seven Stanford women met last
evening at the Pi Beta Phi house and
organized the Women's Mandolin
club. The following are the members
of the new organization: Misses G.
Heltnick, '13, C. Squire, '14, C. Dar
row, '14, H. Batcheller, '14, M. Knox,
'15, D. Stark, '17, and A. Allen, '17. A
committee was appointed to secure a
director for the first practice. Mando
lin, guitar and cello will be the instru
mentation of the club. Other women
unable to attend last evening have ex
pressed their intention of joining the
organization.
