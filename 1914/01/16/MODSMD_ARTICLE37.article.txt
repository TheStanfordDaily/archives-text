# HAMILTON MURRAY, '12, WILL WED SAN FRANCISCO GIRL.
## 
### 
The date has been set for the mar
riage of Hamilton Murray, '12, to Miss
Ila Sonntag, of San Francisco. The
ceremony will take place on February
17 at the home of the bride. The
young couple will make their home in
San Francisco. Murray graduated in
English and was a member of Cardinal
club.
