Women's Conference elections for
the Science and Mathematics depart
ments will be held Wednesday in the
Rest Room. This election is to fill a
vacancy made in those departments by
the resignation of Miss Florence Dun
lop, gr. Polls will be open from 11:15
to 2:00 and the voting is to be from an
eligible list to be printed later.
