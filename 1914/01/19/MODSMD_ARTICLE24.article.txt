# THREE ASSISTANTS FOR GYMNASIUM ARE NAMED
## Thobarn Will Have Charge of a Rugby Section in Connection With Gymnasium Work. 
### 
J. H. Thoburn, '13, L. R. Reynolds,
'15, and P. E. Leland, '16, have been
appointed assistants at the gymna
sium in the places of H. A. Wyckoff,
gr., J. F. Chapman, gr., and G. S. Hole
man, gr., who have gone to San Fran
cisco to continue the study of medi
cine at the Stanford Medical school
there.
Thoburn will have charge of a
spring rugby section for men who are
interested in learning something of
the game and who are not out for
some other branch of athletics.
