# Spanish Club to Meet.
## 
### 
The first regular meeting of the
Spanish club will take place Wednes
day evening at 8 o'clock at the
home of Professor A. M. Espinosa, 543
Middlefield Road.
The program for the evening will
consist of a comedy entitled "El
Flechazo." Miss Gladys Lee, '14, and
Francis Price, '13, will assume the
leading roles.
