# Red Scarf Meets.
## 
### 
The Red Scarf Musical society held
its regular bi-monthly meeting last
night at the home of Marcelle
Rouiller in Palo Alto.
Try-outs for membership will be]
held next Sunday evening at the
Langworthy club, and those playing
the violin or cello are especially urged
to try out. The club is designed to
promote an interest in good music.
