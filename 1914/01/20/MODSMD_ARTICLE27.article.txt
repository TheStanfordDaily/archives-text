# Philosophy Club Retains Officers
## 
### 
At its regular meeting yesterday
afternoon the Student's Philosophy
club reelected W. C. Curry, '14, presi
dent, and Miss G. R. Helmick, '13, sec
retary. Dhangopal Mukerji, '15, of
Calcutta was elected to membership.
F. F. Wolff, gr., read an interesting
paper on "The Relation of Ethics to
Metaphysics." A program committee
consisting of F. F. Wolff, F. E. Brasch,
gr., and Miss Helmick was appointed.
The next meeting of the club will be
held Tuesday, February 3, in room 403.
