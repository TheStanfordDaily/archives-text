# SECOND OPERA TRY-OUTS DATED FOR TOMORROW
## 
### 
The second try-outs for parts in the
Junior Opera will take place tomor
row afternoon and evening under the
direction of Coach Rochester and the
Junior Play Committee.
All women who passed the first try
out last Thursday and any new wo
men desiring to try-out are requested
to report at the Kappa Alpha Theta
house from three to six o'clock to
morrow afternoon. The men who tried
out successfully and those who have
not yet done so are asked to report
at the Pi Beta Phi house tomorrow
evening between seven and ten
o'clock.
