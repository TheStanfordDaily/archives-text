# Tennis and Soccer Men Plan to Form Circle-"S" Society
## 
### 
Wearers of the circle "S" will hold
an important meeting Thursday after
noon in room 41, in order to form a
Circle "S" society, whose purpose will
be to further the interests of soccer
and tennis at Stanford. This new or
ganization will also aim to control the
intercollegiate contests as well as
those of classes and clubs.
A plan to direct the interscliolastic
tennis tournament is also being
formed. It will be the purpose of the
new society to promote the interest of
these two sports among the high
schools of the state.
