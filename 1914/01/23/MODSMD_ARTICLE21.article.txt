Junior Class —Meet Monday 1:10,
room 214. Nomination of officers.
Intorsoholiistie Hoard—Meet Satur
day, 1:10, Dr. Angell's office.

Monday, at Mrs. Branner's.

dollar before voting.
Sophomores—Meet Tuesday, 1:10,
room 214. Nomination of officers.
University Conference —Meet for
picture at Franklin's, Sunday, 10 a.m.
Junior Opera Cast and Chorus—Meet
Saturday morning, Engineering base
ment.

Class. W. P. Darsie.

worthy club. Bring vocal or instru
mental music.
(Jiee Club Tryouts—Friday 7:30
p.m., room 84.
IVu Sigma Nil—Meets Saturday even
ing at 8 o'clock.
