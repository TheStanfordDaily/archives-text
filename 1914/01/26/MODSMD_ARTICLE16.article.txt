# Barbs Defeat Stanford Soccer Team.
## 
### 
On a slippery field the Stanford
soccer team lost to the Barbarians
Saturday at the Presidio by a score
of 2 to 1. Dyke and Elwood scored
for the winners, while Steinhart, Hig
gins, and Butcher played a good game
for Stanford. Captains Higgins kicked
Stanford's only goal.
A large crowd of soldiers witnessed
the contest, and much interest was
shown. Colonel Bell of the Sixteenth
regiment hopes to organize nine soc-!
cer teams among the soldiers stationed I
at the Presidio.
