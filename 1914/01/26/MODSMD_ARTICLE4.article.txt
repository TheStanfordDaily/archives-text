# Y.W.C.A. CONVENTION TO BE TOPIC OF VESTRY TALK
## 
### 
Miss Eileen Everett, '14, who repre
sented the Stanford Y. W. C. A. at the
recent Students' Volunteer Convention
in Kansas City, will give a resume of
the work accomplished there at the Y.
W. C. A. meeting Tuesday. She has
many suggestions to offer in regard to
the application of convention propa
ganda in the local field of work. All
those interested are cordially invited
by the Y. W. C. A. to meet in the vestry
at 4:30, January 27.
