# MANY CHESS PLAYERS ARE OUT FOR VARSITY TEAM
## 
### 
Many chess players have signified
their intention of trying out for the
Varsity team, which will meet Califor
nia for the permanent cup now held by
Stanford on April 17.
The team is to he composed of seven
members and one alternate. Selec
tions will be made from the players
of the try-out tournament scheduled
to begin on February 23. Each candi
date will be required to play one game
with all others entered, and those de
siring to compete must hand their
names to E. R. Nusbaum, '14.
Two games now being played with
New Mexico by postcard correspond
ence have reached their fifteenth
moves, the sides being about even so
far.
