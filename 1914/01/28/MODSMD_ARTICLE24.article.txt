# Oriental Conditions Subject of Study.
## 
### 
A group of Y. M. C. A. men has been
formed for the purpose of studying
"The New Era in Asia," a book writ
ten by Sherwood Eddie, secretary for
Asia in the International Y. M. C. A.
committee, and the result of his study
of conditions in the oriental countries.
This group will meet every Tuesday
noon at the luncheon hour, commenc
ing next week, and is under the man
agement of C. L. Addleman, '15. At
present there are ten men enrolled,
and a limit of twenty has been placed
on the membership.
