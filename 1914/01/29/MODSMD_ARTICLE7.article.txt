# STANFORD SOCCER TEAM WINS FROM PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Stanford soccer team played Palo
Alto high school in a 3 to 1 game
Wednesday afternoon. Steinhart,
Pellas, and Higgins scored for Stan
ford. The line-up follows: Butcher,
goal; Glass, Blodgett, full-backs;
Steinhart, Hughes, Cottrell, half
backs; Pratt, Pellas, Erb, Higgins.
Clawson, forwards.
A game with Palo Alto high Is
scheduled for Saturday. The high
school players are improving rapidly,
and Stanford will again use her first
team against them.
