# Policy of Education is Changed.
## 
### 
The members of the Education Club
have recently changed the policy of
the organization, it now being formed
into a class which meets for the pur
pose of discussing important educa
tional questions of the day. At fort
nightly meetings, two members of the
club will give talks prepared on some
phase of present day problems. One
university credit will be given for
work done in this club, membership
in which is open by invitation, to ad
vanced students in education.
