# Law Seat Sale Reported.
## 
### 
The seat sale for the Masquers'
comedy, "Quality Street," which open
ed this afternoon, is reported as the
most successful financially in the re
cent history of Stanford dramatics. A
long line of ticket buyers awaited the
hour set for the drawing both at the
Bookstore on the campus and at
Weingartner's durg store in Palo Alto,
where seats will remain on sale up to
and including the afternoon of the
