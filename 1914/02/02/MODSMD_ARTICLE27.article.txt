# Special Notices.

Lost—Along car track, lady's closed
face watch with San Jose high school
debating fob. Initials R. B. Notify
Gamma Phi Beta house.
Take your Watch to ISeanians, prac
tical jeweler. Lenses fitted.
Women desiring employment during
the college semester, apply at the
Self Help Bureau in the Women's Rest
Room.
Hook Wanted—Will the person who
borrowed from the geology library
"Martonne's Traite de Geographie
Physique" kindly return it.
—J. C. BRANNER.
