# PROFESSOR WOODWARD URGES WIDER LEARNING FOR LEGAL PROFESSION
## 
### 
In response to an invitation from
the San Francisco Recorder, Professor
F. C .Woodward, head of the Law de
partment, has written a letter to ex
press his opinion of the need for higher
education amongst the members of
the California bar. In this communi
cation. which was printed in a recent
issue of the Recorder, he urges prompt
action on (lie part of the authorities
in charge and the immediate estab
lishment of higher standards. "The
educated bar means a competent bar."
be says,"and the competency of the
bench depends in a large measure
upon the competency of the bar, and
an incompetent bench and bar con
stitute a menace to democratic govern
ment."
