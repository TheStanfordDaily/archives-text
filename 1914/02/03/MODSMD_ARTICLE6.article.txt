# San Francisco Alumni to Organize.
## 
### 
San Francisco alumni are now meet
ing for luncheon at the Press Club,
Sutter and Powell streets, on the first
Tuesday of each month. A Stanford
Club of San Francisco is to be formed.
All Stanford faculty and undergrad
uate men are requested to cooperate.
R. F. Stver, '12, requests that notifia
tion of ony change of address be sent
to him at 410 Montgomery street.
