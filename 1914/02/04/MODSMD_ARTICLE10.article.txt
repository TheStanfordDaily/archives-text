# WOMEN HEAR LECTURE BY PLAYGROUND EXPERT
## 
### 
George E. Dickie, superintendent of
recreation in Oakland, was the speaker
at a joint meeting of the Y. W. C. A.
and Vocational Opportunities Club
held Tuesday afternoon in the chapel.
"Opportunities for Women in Play
ground Work" was the subject of his
talk. He told of the physical and
mental benefits derived from the play
grounds by the city child, and outlined
the course of study necessary for those
wishing to take up playground work.
