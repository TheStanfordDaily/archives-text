# Coover to Address Science Society.
## 
### 
J. E. Coover, of the Department of
Psychology, will be the speaker at the
next regular meeting of the Science
Association, which will he held at the
Stanford Faculty Club Wednesday,
February 4, 1914, at 8 p.m. "Psychi
cal Research at Stanford University"
will be the subject of Mr. Coover's
address.
