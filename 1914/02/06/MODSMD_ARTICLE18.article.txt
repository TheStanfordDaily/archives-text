# Dr. Gardner Preaches Sunday.
## 
### 
"The Divinity of Christ" is the sub
ject chosen by Rev. Charles Gardner
for the 11 o'clock Sunday morning
service in the Memorial Church. The
choir will sing Mendelssohn's anthem
"I waited for the Lord." Solos will be
rendered by Miss Christobel Elliott,
'15, and Miss Candace Caton, Mfi.
