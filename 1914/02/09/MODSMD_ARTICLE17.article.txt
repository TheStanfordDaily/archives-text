# BOTANY DEPARTMENT TO GIVE THREE LECTURES
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Depart
ment of Botany, three addresses on
eminent naturalists, whose portraits
are in the Botanical Library, will be
given on Tuesday evenings in the
Botanical lecture room.
On February 10, Professor Peirce
will lecture on "Asa Gray and His Re
lation to Modern Botany." J. I. W.
McMurphy will speak on "Erwin F.
Smith and the Development of Plant
Pathology," February 24. Professor
Campbell will give the last lecture,
"Sir Joseph Hooker," on March 10.
