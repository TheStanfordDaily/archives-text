# "Herculaneum and Its Treasures."
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough, of the
Department of Latin, will deliver an
illustrated lecture on "Herculaneum
and Its Treasures" in the chapel
Tuesday evening, February 10, un
der the auspices of the Classical Club.
Views of Vesuvius in action will be
shown by means of slides made from
photographs which were taken in
1906 by Professor H. Heath. Other
slides will picture excavations at
Herculaneum, which have been the
delight of archaeologists ever since
the famous eruption of Vesuvius in
79 A. D.
