# Opinions of Law Professors Asked.
## 
### 
Professors P. C. Woodward and
Emlin McClain, of the Law depart
ment, have been invited to take part
in the discussion of the Economic Club
of San Francisco, at a banquet to be
given by the members of the organ
ization at the Commercial Club this
evening. The question to be discussed
is, "Is there any general and vital de
fect in our present administration of
justice?"
