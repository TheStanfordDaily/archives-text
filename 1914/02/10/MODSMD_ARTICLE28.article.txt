# Guild Will Appoint Official.
## 
### 
Tomorrow morning at 7:30 o'clock
in room 10 of the Chemistry building,
will be held the regular February
meeting of the Students' Guild. The
meeting was to have been held last
night, but was postponed. The recom
mendation for appointment of some
one for the directorate to succeed Miss
Florence Dunlop, '13, whose resigna
tion has been accepted, will be made.
