# Mechanical Engineers to Meet.
## 
### 
"Sugar Industry in the Hawaiian
Islands" will he the subject discussed
by .T. A. Gibb, '15, at the meeting of the
Mechanical Engineering society this
evening in room 262, at 7:30 o'clock.
Gibb has spent the last two summers
in the islands and will speak from per
sonal observation. W. F. Williams,
'15, and W. H. Warren, '15, will give
short reports.
