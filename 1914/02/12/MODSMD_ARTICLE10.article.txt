# BUTT-RUMFORD CONCERT PLEASES LARGE CROWD
## 
### 
Mine. Clara Butt and Kennerley
Rumford, singing under the auspices
of the Peninsula Music Association,
delighted a large audience in the As
sembly Hall last evening.
In a varied program, Mme. Butt im
pressed her hearers with the truly re
markable range and quality of her
powerful contralto voice. In addition
to her charming rendition of old bal
lads, for which she has long been
well known, Mme. Butt sang several
more elaborte selections including
arias from two operas.
Kennerley Rumford, who has a bari
tone voice of pleasing quality, gave
satisfactory rendition of several pop
ular numbers.
