# HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS TO STAGE BENEFIT SHOW
## 
### 
Cafe Chantent" is the title of a
show, which will be given at the
Armory tomorrow evening by the
students of Palo Alto high school.
The affair is in the nature of a
cabaret show and promises to be full
of action and humor. An admission
of 25 cents is being charged, the pro
ceeds to go for the track and baseball
teams of the high school.
