# WOMEN'S INTERCOLLEGIATE TENNIS SET FOR APRIL 5
## 
### 
That the women's intercollegiate
tennis tournament will be held April
5 on the Peninsula Club courts was
the decision reached by the directors
of the Women's Athletic Association
Tuesday. This time and place was
agreed upon at a meeting of women of
the two universities.
The committee for crew coaching
reported the engaging of W. F. Hous
ton from the Oakland playground
board to coach on Sunday mornings,
the regular women's coaching com
mittee taking charge of the usual
classes.
A schedule for interclass basketball
games was made out.
