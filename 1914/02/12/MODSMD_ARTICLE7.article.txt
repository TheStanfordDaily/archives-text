# DE HAAS TO LECTURE AT ROTTERDAM UNIVERSITY
## 
### 
Jacob A.de Haas, assistant in the
Department of Economics, has ac
cepted the invitation of the University
of Rotterdam, Holland, to deliver a
series of lectures on "American
Banking" at that institution next
summer.
Assistant de Haas took his A. B. de
gree at Stanford in 1910, and the same
year received a diploma from the
Theological school at Meadville,
Pennsylvania. In 1911 he received
the degree of A. M. at Harvard, and is
now studying for his Doctor's degree
while assisting in Economics.
