# ECONOMICS CLUB MEMBERS ELECTED ON A NEW BASIS
## 
### 
The Economics club decided last
evening at their regular meeting to
elect members on a competitive basis
in the future. In accordance with this
new ruling and owing to the decision
of last semester to admit underclass
men with the proper qualifications,
sophomore candidates will be request
ed to read original papers on some
phase of economics before the club.
Regular meetings have been set for
the second and last Thursday of each
month at the different fraternity
houses. All upperclassmen of the de
partment are cordially invited to at
tend.
Pictures of the club will be taken at
ten o'clock Sunday morning at Frank
lin's studio in Palo Alto.
