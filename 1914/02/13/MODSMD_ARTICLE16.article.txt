# Student Goes to Education Convention
## 
### 
The Stanford, California, and Wash
ington chapters of Phi Delta Kappa
education fraternity will be repre
sented by J. H. Williams, 13, at a
national convention to be held in
Richmond, Virginia, this month. The
meeting of the national council will
open February 25.
Williams is the president of the
local chapter. He left for Virginia
last week and will return to Stanford
in March. While on the trip he will
visit seven or eight of the eastern
chapters of the organization.
