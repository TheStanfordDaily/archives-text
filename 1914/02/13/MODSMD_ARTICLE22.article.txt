# FINAL MATCH IN TENNIS SCHEDULED FOR TOMORROW
## Hutchinson Will Meet Winner of Uhls-Sheldon Contest for Place on Intercollegiate Team. 
### 
The final match in the first trial ten
nis tournament is scheduled for to
morrow, when J. S. Hutchinson, '16,
will meet the winner of the Uhls-
Sheldon match. The victor in the
final game will become one of the
four racquet wielders who will play
against California in April. The tour
nament to choose the second man will
probably be started on Saturday also.
V. L. Sheldon, '15, won the right to
meet K. B. Uhls, '16, in the semi
final round by defeating O. T. Brun,
'16, 6-2, 6-4. Their match will be
played this afternoon, in order that
the winner may appear against
Hutchinson tomorrow.
Hutchinson has been playing in
splendid form throughout the tourna
ment. His victory over Captain L.
Sloss, '15, which gave him his place in
the final round, was the surprise of
the try-out matches. The style of
game that Hutchinson is putting up
insures a fast brand of tennis in to
morrow's contest.
