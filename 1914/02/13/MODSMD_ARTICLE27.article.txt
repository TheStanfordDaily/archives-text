# Exposition Official Will Lecture.
## 
### 
Louis Levy, chief of local publicity
of the Panama-Pacific International
Exposition, will deliver a lecture on
the Panama Canal and the Exposi
tion, Friday evening, March 6, at the
Armory. The lecture will be illus
trated with moving pictures and beau
tifully colored slides, and has been
given a score of times by Mr. Levy.
The workings of the canal, the locks
and the Culebra Cut will be minutely
explained, and in conclusion pictures
of the Exposition grounds and build
ings will be thrown on the screen.
