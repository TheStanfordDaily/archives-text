# Stanford Quintet Will Meet Pacific.
## 
### 
A College Conference league game
will be played tomorrow evening at
Encina Gymnasium between the Stan
ford and the College of the Pacific bas
ketball teams. This will be the first
time that these fives have met this sea
son. Captain A. E. Worthy, '15, an
nounces that there will be seats pro
vided for all spectators. The admis
sion price will be 15 cents. The game
will be called sharply at 8:00 o'clock
at the gym. The Stanford line-up:
Worthy, Davis, guards; Dutton, cen
ter; Farrar, Reynolds, forwards.
