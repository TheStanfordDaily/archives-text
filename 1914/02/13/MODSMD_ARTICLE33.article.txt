# Manwaring to Lecture in East.
## 
### 
Professor Wilfred H. Manwaring, of
the Department of Bacteriology and
Immunity, will lecture at the second
term summer quarter of the University
of Chicago. After a series of intro
ductory lectures on "Functional Path
ology," Professor Manwaring will give
a course of lectures on the "Physiolog
ical responses of the human body to
infectious agents."
