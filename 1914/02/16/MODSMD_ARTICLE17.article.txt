# STANFORD WOMEN DEFEAT SAN JOSE IN BASKETBALL
## 
###

ketball live of San Jose in the south
ern city last Saturday by a score of
23-21, the game being a well matched
and hotly contested one. The tally
stood at 10-10 at the end of the first
half and 21 all at the end of the sec

tossed up and Stanford won the play
