# VALUABLE SCHOLARSHIPS ARE OFFERED STUDENTS
## 
### 
Announcement has been made that
in pursuance of its usual policy, the
Review of Reviews company will this
year offer an unlimited number of
scholarships to college students. These
scholarships are not competitive in
any way, and will not be awarded for
classroom proficiency, but for prac
tical work which may be done in spare
time. They range in value from $2ijo
to $1,000 each. President Woodrow
Wilson and other college presidents
have commended this enterprise and
its purpose, which is primarily to
assist self-supporting students. Full
particulars may be secured by apply
ing to The Review of Reviews Schol
arship Fund, 30 Irving Place, New
York City.
