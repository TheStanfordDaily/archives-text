# Freshman Committees are Appointed.
## 
### 
President Z. I}. West of tlie Fresh
men class gave out today the names of
those who will make up the jolly-up
and vaudeville committees. Those who
will supervise the 1917 jolly-up are
P. W. Patterson, chairman; P. C.
Adams, Misses E. M. Judah, M. R.
Evans, and M. J. Brydolf. The vaude
ville committee will be composed of
D. E. Shepardson, M. E. Tedford, G.
K. Howard, R. L. Owen; Misses M. D.
Stark, F. Wright, and J. L. Smith.
