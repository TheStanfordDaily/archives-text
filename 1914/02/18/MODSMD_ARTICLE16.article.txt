# Alumni to be Married in Palo Alto.
## 
### 
Miss Bertha Knapp, '09, will become
the bride of P. George, '10, tomorrow
morning at 10 o'clock. The service
will be held in the Episcopal church of
Palo Alto. Doctor Gardner will per
form the cermony. Miss Knapp is a
member of the Alpha Omicron Pi
sorority and George belongs to Encina
Club.
