# MANDOLIN FLAYERS WILL TRY OUT FOR SPRING TOUR
## 
### 
Try-outs for a reorganized Mando
lin Club will be held tonight in the
Engineering building at 7:30. The
present members of the club as well
as the new men applying for member
ship, will be obliged to prove their
merits before the committee in charge.
The recently elected manager, H. N.
Walker, '14, expects to take the newly
formed club on a concert tour at some
future date. Programs will probably
be given at Santa Cruz, Monterey and
Watsonville.
