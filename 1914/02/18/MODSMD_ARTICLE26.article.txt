# Krehbiel Returns from Southern Tour.
## 
### 
Professor E. B. Krehbiel has re
turned from the south where he spoke
in the interests of the peace move
ment. In addition to addresses given
according to schedule, at Pasadena,
Long Beach and San Bernardino, Pro
fessor Krehbiel was called to speak
at the high schools of Riverside and
Hollywood. At the invitation of Frank
Miller be spoke in the unique chapel
of the Glenwood Inn.
