# Yoshida Talks to Japanese Students.
## 
### 
E. V. Yoshida, a Japanese student
worker traveling under the auspices
of the International Committee of the
Young Men's Christian Association,
visited the campus yesterday. He is
traveling secretary for Japanese stu
dents in America, and spoke especially
to the Japanese students of the Uni
versity. He also talked to the Mis
sion Study-Group at their noon meet
ing, taking Korea as his subject.
