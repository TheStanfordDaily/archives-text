# UPPERCLASSMEN TO HOLD FIRST JOINT JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
For the first time, the upperclass
men of Stanford will hold a joint
jolly-up. All seniors, juniors and hold
overs will assemble together on Fri
day, February 27, in Encina club
room.
The senior class committee in charge
of the affair promises to liveliest time
ever. Besides the usual dancing the
entire Glee Club will appear in stunts,
songs and skits. And eats enough
for all, and even more will be on hand,
is another assurance of the committee.
Minutes of previous meeting read
and approved.
