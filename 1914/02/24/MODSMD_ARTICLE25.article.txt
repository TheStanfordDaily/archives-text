# Hoover Will Receive Medal March 9.
## 
### 
On March Ninth —Founders' Day,
by a coincidence—H. C. Hoover, '9~>,
and Mrs. Lou Henry Hoover '98, will
be presented with the medal which
they have jointly been awarded by
the Mining and Metallurgical Society
of America "in recognition of their
distinguished contributions to the lit
erature of mining." Members of the
society have received notices of a
dinner in New York on that date at
which time the medal will be formally
presented.
