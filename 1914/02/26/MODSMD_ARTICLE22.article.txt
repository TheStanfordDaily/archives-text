# Engineers To Visit Pelton Works.
## 
### 
Seniors of the Civil Engineering de
partment will be given instructions
Saturday in the Pelton Water Wheel
company's works in San Francisco.
Complete plans for the trip have been
made and all members of the Senior
class desiring to accompany the party
are requested to take the 7:49 train at
| Palo Alto Saturday morning.
