Upper Classmen Jolly-up Tonight.
Don't forget the upperelass jolly-up
at Encina this evening. No effort has
been spared to make it the best. The
committee promises fine entertain
ment, good refreshments, and a big
dance all for the price of fifty cents.
The Glee and Mandolin clubs with
their stunt men will make merry and
all that is necessary now is the at
tendance of upperclassmen.
