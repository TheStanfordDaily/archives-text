# HAHN WINS FROM SLOSS IN FINAL TENNIS MATCH
## 
### 
H. L. Hahn, '16, defeated Captain
L. Sloss, '15, in straight sets, 6-3, 6-2,
6-0, in the final match in the second
tryont tennis tournament yesterday
afternoon. By virtue of his victory
Hahn becomes one of the five men
who will contest for places on the
intercollegiate team after the pre
liminaries have been completed.
Hahn put up the best game that he
has played since his appearance on
the local courts, his overhead work
being especially good. The first two
sets were hotly contested, but in tho
third Sloss seemed to tire, and Hahn
ran the set out without the loss of l
game.
