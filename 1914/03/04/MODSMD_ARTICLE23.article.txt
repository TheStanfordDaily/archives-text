# Sabine Will Lecture at California.
## 
### 
An address on "The Point of View
of Idealism" will be delivered before
the Philosophical Union of the Uni
versity of California Friday evening
by Professor G. H. Sabine of the de
partment of Philosophy at Stanford.
This is the first of a series of three
lectures which Professor Sabine has
been asked to deliver. The other two
are scheduled for March 13 and
March 20.
