# MISS CONDE WILL GIVE SECOND RELIGIOUS TALK
## Large Audience Listens To Views Of Y.W.C.A. Speaker On “An Adequate Faith” 
### 
Miss Bertha Conde gave her first
scheduled talk last night before a
large audience. Her subject was "An
Adequate Faith."
She said in part : "The only real re
ligion which will satisfy the modern
scientific student of today is one
which encourages faith in God
through the teachings and personality
of Jesus Christ."
This evening at eight o'clock in the
chapel, Miss Conde will continue this
idea, and will speak on "Why I Be
lieve in Jesus Christ."
