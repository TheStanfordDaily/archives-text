# FRIDAY IS LAST DAY FOR SALE OF DINNER TICKETS
## 
### 
Founders' Day Dinner tickets will
be on sale at the Bookstore until Fri
day at 5 o'clock. No tickets will be
sold after this time as final arrange
ments to numbers must be made
with the caterers. Ticket-sellers are
requested to make reports at this time,
women to Miss Beatrice Bingham, and
men to A. M. Standish.
