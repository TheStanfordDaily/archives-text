# CONFERENCE VACANCIES TO BE FILLED TUESDAY
## List of Eligibles Will Be Posted in the Various Departments. 
### 
The election of representatives from
the various departments to till the
vacancies in the University Confer
ence will be held Tuesday, March 10,
at 1:15 p.m. All students who have
fifty hours, or more, to their credit
are eligible to vote. The polls for
the various departments follow:
Economics majors, room 214; Law,
room 12; Education, room 50; Phys
iology and Medical, combined, room
260.
The list of eligibles will be posted
in the departments.
