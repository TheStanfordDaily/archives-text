# SECOND TALK OF SERIES GIVEN BY MISS CONDE
## 
### 
Last evening the second talk in the
series being given by Miss Bertha
Conde was heard by a large audience
at the chapel. Miss Conde used as
her subject, "Why I Believe in Jesus
Christ,", and declared that through the
teachings of Christ we may become
as little children, and so find a way
into the Kingdom of God.
This evening at S o'clock Miss
Conde will deliver the last of her
talks in the chapel. The topic an
nounced is "The Secret of Reality."
