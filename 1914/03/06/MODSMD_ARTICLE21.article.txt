# TRYOUT TEAMS TOURNEY TO BE PLAYED SATURDAY
## 
### 
Play in the third tryout tennis
tournament will be continued tomor
row morning. An effort will be made
to complete the tournament over the
week-end. Yesterday afternoon J. H.
Hutchinson, '16, defeated S. Chapman,
'IG, in the first round of the contest.
The scores were G-2, 6-3. In the first
match in the round robin tournament,
in which the winners of the five try
outs will meet to decide which four
are to represent the University
against California, H. L. Halin, 'IG,
proved too strong for Iv. B. Uhls, '16,
defeating him 6-1, 6-0.
