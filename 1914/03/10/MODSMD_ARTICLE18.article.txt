# FLOODBERG APPOINTS 1916 IRISH MARATHON COMMITTEE
## 
### 
R. A. Floodberg, president of the
Sophomore class, has selected a com
mittee to have charge of the second
year candidates for the Irish Mara
thon event. The members of the com
mittee are all actively engaged in
work on the track, and will act in
conjunction with E. C'. Behrens, '14,
the chairman of the Quadrangle Club
committee which governs the race.
The committee consists of J. M. Fitz
hugh, It. G. Fithian, P. R. Wilson, E.
C. Adams, E. X. Wright, and J. P.
Chace, chairman.
