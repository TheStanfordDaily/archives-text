# Popular Song Writer Coming
## 
### 
The rare opportunity of hearing
Mrs. Carrie Jacobs-Bond, the famous
song writer, will be given to Stanford
music lovers next Sunday afternoon,
March 15, at Miss Marker's school.
The recital will take place at 4
o'clock.
Mrs. Bond is the author of numer
ous popular pieces, including "A Per
fect Day,"and "Just a-Wearying for
You." She intends to spend a few
days on the campus in order to per
mit any students who so desire to
meet her. Fifty ce : nts admission will
be charged for the concert next Sun
