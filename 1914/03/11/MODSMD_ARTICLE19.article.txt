# MAIL SCHEDULE ANNOUNCED BY STANFORD POST-OFFICE
## Hours of Receipt and Dispatch Are Made Public for Convenience of Students. 
### 
For the convenience of the Stanford
public the Stanford Postoffice an
nounces the following schedule of
mails received at and dispatched from
the local ofiice.
Incoming
8.00 a.m.—Mails from north and
south.
1:00 p.m.—Mails from north, south
and cast.
3:30 p.m.—Mails from north and
south as far as Pacific Grove.
5:40 p.m.—Mails from Bay Counties.
Palo Alto mail arrives with every
delivery.
Dispatch
S:00 a.m.—To Palo Alto only.
9:00 a.m. —To north and east of
San Francisco.
11:00 a.m.—All directions.
2:30 p.m.—All directions.
5:50 p.m.—All directions.
