# Economic Lecture Postponed
## 
### 
The lecture to students of econ
omics scheduled for this evening, by
Mr. Munsell, general manager of the
San Francisco branch of the Brad
street Company, has been postponed
until next week. The exact time will
be announced later. Sickness in his
family prevented Mr. Munsell from
keeping the engagement.
