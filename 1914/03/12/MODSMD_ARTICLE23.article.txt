# G.A. Clark Returns From Washington
## 
### 
G. A. Clark, academic secretary of
the University, has returned from
Washington, D. C.. where he ap
peared before a committee of the
House of Representatives. Secretary
Clark is an expert on the fur seal
herd of Alaska, and it is in this
capacity that he appeared before the
Committee on Expenditures in the De
partment of Commerce and Labor. In
1596 and 1897 Clark was secretary of
the fur seal commission. Tn the
years 1909, 1912, and 1913 he was ap
pointed special investigator by the de
partment of Commerce and Labor.
