# COLLEGE OF THE PACIFIC TO MEET STANFORD FIVE
## 
### 
Following their second meeting
with the California five, the Stanford
team will play the College of the Pa
cific quintet at Encina Gymnasium to
morrow evening. This is the first
time that these two teams have met
this season. The contest will be a
regular league game of the College
Conference, and will decide definitely
the holder of second place in the final
league standing. California has been
eliminated from the race for the
place. The men who will oppose the
Garden City players are: Worthy,
Blodgett, forwards; Dutton, center;
Farrar, Reynolds, Davis, guards.
The game will commence at 7:15,
in order to be completed in time for
"The Fortune Hunter."
