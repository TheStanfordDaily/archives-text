# RABBI MEYER TO DELIVER SPECIAL SABBATH SERMON
## 
### 
Rabbi Martin A. Mayer of Temple
Emanuel, San Francisco, will deliver
the Divine Service at the Memorial
Church on Sunday, March 15, 1914.
He will take as his subject, "Good
and Goods." As a special musical
feature, "Seek Ye the Lord" will be
sung by a soloist and chorus. J. W.
Bennett, '15, will render a solo.
