# “Justice” Read Before English Club
## 
### 
Stanford English Club held its
recent regular meeting at the home
of Mrs. T. P. Russell, instructor in
English. Professor L. E. Bassett, of
the English department, read John
Galsworthy's "Justice." Revisions in
the constitution were reported, and
will be open for discussion at the next
meeting. J. W. Bennett, '15, reported
on the progress of the English Club
play.
