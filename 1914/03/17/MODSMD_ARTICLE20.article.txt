# BASEBALL ELIGIBLES ARE SUBMITTED BY CALIFORNIA
## Twenty-five Names Appear on Official List of Blue and Gold Ball-players. 
### 
The California authorities have sub
mitted a list of twenty-five names of
members of the baseball squad who
will be eligible for the intercollegiate
series. The list follows:
Abbott, B. R., Adair S., Bingaman,
VV. H., Chapman, E. F., Clark, W. E.,
Dodge, H. C., Dodson, J. H., Eames, L.
V., Earl, G. C., Edwards, L. C., Feeley,
J. C., Forbes, E. F., Ford, F. H., Fur
long, P. W., Gefken, J. H., Gianelli, R.
L., Gimbal, R. E., Glenney, Carrol,
Hayes, K. A., Holmes, A. E., Johnson,
C. 8., Jones, M. F., Lombardi, J. A.,
Montgomery, W. R., Morse, H. A.,
O'Hara, R. F., Rapp, C. P., Rubke, F.
W., Sebastian, C. W., Street, C. E.,
Young, E. J.
Approved,
T. M. PUTNAM.
