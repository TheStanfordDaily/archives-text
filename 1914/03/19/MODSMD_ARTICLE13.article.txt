# 1917 VAUDEVILLE TRYOUTS SCHEDULED FOR TOMORROW
## 
### 
Definite plans for the annual Fresh
! man Vaudeville show are now rapid
ly being brought to a conclusion.
April 17, the Friday evening before
the Intercollegiate track meet, nas
been set as the date for the staging
of the performance.
A skit written by H. S. Marshutz,
'17, was accepted at the last meeting
of the committee. Tryouts for places
on the cast will be held tomorrow
afternoon at the Gamma Phi Beta
sorority between two-thirty and five
o'clock. All Freshmen desiring to
take part in the vaudeville show are
asked to report.
