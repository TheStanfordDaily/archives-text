# Band to Play at Saratoga Festival
## 
### 
The Stanford band will furnish the
music for the "Blossom Festival" at
Saratoga Saturday. In the morning
the Stanford musicians will take part
in the parade. A regular open air
concert is scheduled for the after
noon. The conccrt is to precede an
address by Governor Johnson.
Manager Blanchard lias been noti
fied by the Saratoga authorities that
a special car has been engaged to
leave the campus at 8:30 Saturday
morning to transport the bandsmen to
the scene of the festival. Thirty-six
men will make the trip. The Penin
sular Electric road offers a special
