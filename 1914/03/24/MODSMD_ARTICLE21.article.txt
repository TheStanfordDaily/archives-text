# OFFICERS ARE ELECTED TO LEAD Y.M.C.A. WORK
## 
### 
Officers for the coming year were
elected by the Young Men's Christian
Association at its meeting in the
chapel yesterday afternoon. F. C.
Hamilton, '15, who has worked hard
and accomplished much for the asso
ciation during the past year in his
capacity of vice-president, was re
warded by advancement to the presi
dency.
A. E. Worthy, '15, H. Zimmerman,
'17, and R. E. Needham, '16, were
chosen vice-president, secretary and
treasurer respectively. All are active
members of the Y. M. C. A., and well
fitted for their positions. Chairmen
for the various committees of the as
sociation were also selected.
