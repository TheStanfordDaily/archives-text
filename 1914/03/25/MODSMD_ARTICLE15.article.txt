# Athletic Schedule Is Rearranged.
## 
### 
Manager R. W. Wilcox, '13, an
nounced today that the date of the
Peninsula Sub-league track meet be
tween the high schools has been
changed from Friday, March 27, to
Saturday, March 28. The time of the
meeting is at 1 o'clock, as previously
announced. The track meet with Po
mona on Friday will commence at 2
o'clock instead of 3 o'clock, as was
originally stated.
