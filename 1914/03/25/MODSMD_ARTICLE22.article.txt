# MISS BEATTY WILL TALK ON PRESS EXPERIENCES
## 
### 
The Vocational Opportunities Club
will hold its regular monthly meeting
on Wednesday evening, April Bth, at
7:30, in room 4GO (Botany lecture
room). Miss Bessie Beatty of the
San Francisco Bulletin and founder
of "Happyland" will speak on "Ex
periences of a Woman in Journalism,"
and Professor Everett W. Smith of
the English department on "Prepara
tion for Journalistic Work." All who
are interested are invited to attend.
