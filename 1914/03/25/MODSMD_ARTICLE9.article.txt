# Urges Sophomores to Run Quarters
## 
### 
Try-outs for the Sophomore mara
thon team are being conducted daily
from four to six on the track, but as
yet a sufficient number of 1916 men
have not appeared to furnish material
for the twenty-four man team. The
second year committee urgently re
quests all Sophomore men to try-out.
