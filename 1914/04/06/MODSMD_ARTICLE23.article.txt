# Members Will Address Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
No outside speaker will have charge
of the meeting of the Young Women's
Christian Association tomorrow after
noon in the Vestry. Instead, three of
the young women who are active in
the Association work will talk on dif
ferent subjects. Miss C. Green, '14, F.
Ober, *13, and Miss E. Wheeler, *14,
will be the speakers. The meeting
as usual will be in the Vestry at 4:30.
