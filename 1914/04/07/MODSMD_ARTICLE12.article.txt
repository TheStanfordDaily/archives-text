# SOUTHERN STANFORD CLUB WILL BANQUET DR. BRANNER
## 
### 
On April 11 the Stanford Club of
Los Angeles will give a dinner to Dr.
John Casper Branner, probably in the
new Hotel Clark in the southern city.
Judge Benjamin Bledsoe has been
asked to preside. This will be the
first formal recognition by the newly
reorganized Stanford Club of Dr.
Branner as president of the Univer
sity.
The officers of the club arc: R. V.
Reppy, '02, president; Lois Baker, '13,
vice-president: I). H. Amsden, '13, sec
retary and treasurer.
