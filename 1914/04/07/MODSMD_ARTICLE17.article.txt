# Miss Lois Baker Engaged
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Lois Baker,
'13, to Mr. Charles Hal Cope, both of
Los Angeles, will be announced soon.
Miss Maker was a member of the En
glish department, Women's Press Club,
treasurer of Cap and Gown, secre
tary of Women's Conference, Associ
ate editor of the Daily Palo Alto, 1913
director on the Women's Clubhouse
board, and was on the 1913 Quad
board. The couple will be married
this summer and will make their home
in Los Angeles.
