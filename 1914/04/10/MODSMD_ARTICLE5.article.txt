# RHODES SCHOLAR GOES TO JOIN CHANCELLOR JORDAN
## 
### 
Emil Hollmann, '11, the newly ap
pointed Rhodes scholar from Stan
ford, is now on his way to New York,
from where he will sail on the White
Star steamer Celtic for Naples. On
May 1 Hollmann will join Chancellor
Jordan at the Italian port in the
capacity of private secretaiy. They
will tour the continent together for
the next three months in the inter
est of international peace. Hollmann
will England in time to reg
ister at Oxford on October 7. lie left
the campus late Wednesday afternoon
for San Francisco and yesterday
took the train east from the Oakland
Mole.
