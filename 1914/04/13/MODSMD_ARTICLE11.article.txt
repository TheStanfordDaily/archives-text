While we are speaking of track men, it seems hardly necessary to
remark that an athlete in training and on the eve of a meet is not with
out his nerves. These nerves will not be soothed during this week by
indiscriminate conversation about the coming contest. If you will talk
with your track friend about the weather rather than about his sore
muscle you will accord not only with the expressed wish of Coach
Campbell and Captain McKee but with the dictates of common sense.
