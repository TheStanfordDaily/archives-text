# JUDGE GOSBEY SPEAKS BEFORE LAW STUDENTS
## 
### 
Judge Gosbey, of the Superior Court
of Santa Clara, gave an address yes
terday afternoon before the students
of the Law Department, taking "Tes
tamentary Capacity" as his subject.
"There are three fundamental tests
by which to determine whether a man
has testamentary capacity or not," he
said. "First, does he understand the
nature of the instrument which he is
making; second, does he realize the
nature and extent of his property;
and, third, does he know those per- t
sons who are to enjoy the benefit of '
his bounty."
