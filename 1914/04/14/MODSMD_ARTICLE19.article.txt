# ELECTRIC RAILWAY EXPERT WILL LECTURE THURSDAY
## 
### 
A. V, Thompson, an electrical rail
way specialist of the General Electric
Company, will address the next engi
neering assembly under the direction
of the Stanford Branch of the Ameri
can Institute of Electrical Engineers.
The subject of the lecture will be
"General Comments on Electrical
Railway Work." Mr. Thompson has
been called to the Pacific Coast re
cently for consultation by several
large electric railway companies.
This illustrated lecture will be held
in the Geology lecture room Thursday
afternoon at 3 p.m.
