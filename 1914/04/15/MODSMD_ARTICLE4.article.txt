# TALENTED 'CELLIST WILL ENTERTAIN THIS EVENING
## Frederick Preston Search to Offer Rare Treat for Music Lovers at Assembly Hall. 
### 
A large advance seat sale insures a
good house for the Frederick Preston
j Search violoncello concert in the As
sembly Hall this evening.
A special feature of the program
| will consist of several solos by Mrs.
Sara Bibby Brown, one of Palo Alto's
most gifted sopranos.
The program follows:
1. First Movement of the "Wald
stein" Piano Sonata (C major, Op. 53)
1 (Beethoven).
2. 'Cello Solos: Gavotte (Goldblatt),
Adagio from 'Cello Concerto (Schu
mann), Minuet (Beethoven), Am
Springbrunnen (Davidoff).
3. Sonata in A major (Op. 69) for
'Cello and Piano (Beethoven). I. Al
legro ma non tanto. 11. Allegro mol
to (Scherzo). 111. Adagio cantabile —
| Allegro vivace.
4. Songs: Fruhlingslied (Mendels
sohn), Waldeinsamkeit (Reger), A
Birthday (Woodman).
j 5. 'Cello Solos: Rigaudon (Asliton),
Traumerei am Garda-See (Search),
Aria (Search). Ode to a Carnation,
Tone Poem for 'Cello and Piano
j(Search).
G. Piano Solos: Moment Musical in
F minor (Schubert), Wichtelmann
lein (Korngold), Gavotte pour les
Ileures et Le Zephirs (Rameau-Die
mer).
7. Sonata in G minor (Op. 17) for
'Cello and Piano (Search). T. Allegro
ma non troppo. 11. Scherzo, "The
Frogpond." 111. Allegro moderato.
A large advance seat sale insures a
good house for the Frederick Preston
j Search violoncello concert in the As
sembly Hall this evening.
