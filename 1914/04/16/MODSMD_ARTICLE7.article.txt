# GREETINGS!
## 
### 
Manager \\ ilcox has asked that the coming' gala week-end be fit
tingly inaugurated by an excursion to the I'alo Alto depot tomorrow
afternoon to welcome the Keio team to Stanford. It is most sincerely
to be hoped that an enthusiastic response will meet this request. Stran
gers in a strange land, these journeying athletes will be peculiarly im
pressed and delighted by the characteristic warmth of Stanford hos
pitality. They are the same men who so handsomely entertained our
own ball players last summer; they come to this country at the invita
tion and as the guests of Stanford; and to treat them royally is not
only a pleasure but an obligation. A large and noisy delegation should
leave with the Stanford band 011 the 2:10 car tomorrow to greet our
Oriental friends as they arrive in Palo Alto at 2:27.
