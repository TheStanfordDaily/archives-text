# English Club Elects Ten Members
## 
### 
Stanford English Club last evening
elected ten students, four women and
six men, to membership. The newly
elected members have been prominent
in literary circles, many of them hav
ing had their writings published this
last year. They are Miss Mary L.
Curry, '15, Miss Dare Stark, '17, Miss
Nellita N. Choate, '17, Miss Nellie D.
Knox, '15, W. A. Logan, '14. E. G. Leaf,
'17, R. A. Griffin, '17. H. W. Frantz,
'17, H. A. Burnham, '17. R. M. Finch,
'14, and G. Robinson, '17.
