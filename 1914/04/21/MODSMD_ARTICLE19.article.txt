# SCHUBERT CLUB PROMISES CLEVER MUSICAL PROGRAM
## 
###

usually attractive program under the
direction of L. H. Eaton, in the Fac
ulty Clubhouse Thursday afternoon ut
4:30. The club will sing several se
lections, and individual members will
also contribute to the program.
Refreshments will be served after
the entertainment. Friends of the
club and the ladies of the Faculty
Club are cordially invited.
