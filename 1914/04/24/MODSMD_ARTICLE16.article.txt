# Rev. Ostrum to Preach Sunday
## 
###

sermon at Memorial Church, Sunday,
April 26. The hour of the services is
11 o'clock. "Incline Thine Ear" has
been selected as the anthem. .T. W.
Kaufman is announced as soloist.
