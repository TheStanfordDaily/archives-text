# Rugby Squad Plays Oakland Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Oakland High School rugby
team will meet the spring rugby
squad on the Varsity football field to
morrow afternoon at throe o'clock.
Coach .T. TI. Thoburn, '13, has put the
men through some stiff practice games
recently, which augurs well for the
success of tomorrow's spor'
