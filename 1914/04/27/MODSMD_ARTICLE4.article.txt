# MARY GARD WILL LEAD WOMEN IN BASKETBALL
## 
### 
Miss Mary Gard, '15, was chosen
captain of the women's basketball
team during the coming season by a
ballot of the squad taken after the
final game of the interclass series on
Thursday afternoon. Miss Geneva
Dean, '15, was named as manager.
Both are members of the varsity team.
The interclass championship for
1914 was won by the Senior class, rep
resented by the following lineup: Cen
ters, Misses Holt and Roxburghe;
guards, Misses Rowell and Ellis; for
wards. Misses Herron (captain) and
Bliss.
