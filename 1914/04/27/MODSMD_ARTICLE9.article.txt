# MISS BENTLEY WILL SPEAK AT LAST Y.W.C.A. MEETING
## 
### 
Miss Mary Bentley will be the
speaker at the last meeting of the
Young Women's Christian Association
tomorrow afternoon at 4:30 in the
vestry of the Memorial Church. She
will speak especially for the Senior
women, but all are invited. Miss

Bentley is the student secretary of
the association for the western field.
