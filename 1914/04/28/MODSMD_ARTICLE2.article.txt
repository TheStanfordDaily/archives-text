# DISTRICT ATTORNEYS ARE ENTERTAINED ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
A delegation of about seventy-five
people, composed of the district at
torneys of the state with their friends
and relatives, made a short visit on
the campus this morning, while tour
ing the surrounding country as the
guests of District Attorney Franklin
Swart, of San Mateo County. After
seeing the sights of the University,
they were entertained at the Delta
Chi house, as the guests of President
Branner and Professor F. C. Wood
ward, of the Law Department. From
there they autoed to Princeton-by-the-
Sea, where they were served a lunch
of sea food. This evening they will
be entertained by a dinner dance at
the Peninsula Hotel in San Mateo.
