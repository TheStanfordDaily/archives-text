# SCIENTIFIC SOCIETIES HONOR E.C. FRANKLIN
## 
### 
Professor E. C. Franklin of the
Chemistry department is the recipient
of three great honors, election to the
National Academy of Science, mem
bership in the American Philosoph
ical Society, and an invitation from
the New Zealand Government to visit,
that country next summer in order
to take part in the proceedings of the
British Association for the Advance
ment of Science.
Professor Franklin is the third
member of the faculty to be elected
to the National Academy of Science,
President Branner and Professor T).
H. Camppell having been elected sev
eral years ago.
