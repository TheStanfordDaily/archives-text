# Rooks of Professors Acquired
## 
### 
Dr. Wi S. Cooper, formerly of the
University of Chicago, who has been
making a special study of the vegeta
tion of this region in relation to light,
dryness, and other factors of the en
vironment, has been invited by the
Board of Trustees to give two courses
next year as Lecturer in Plant Ecol
ogy. Dr. Cooper lias been working in
connection with the Carnegie Institu
tion of Washington in the department
of botanical research. Three hours
will be allowed for either course, one
to be given each semester. The one to
he given the fall semester is General
Ecology, based principally on field
study of the vegetation of the Palo
Alto region. Forest Ecology will be
given the second semester.
