# HIGGINS MADE PRESIDENT OF CIRCLE "S" SOCIETY
## 
### 
Circle' 'S" Society this afternoon
elected new officers, admitted five new
members, and amended its constitu
tion to read that one officer should
he elected from each field of sport.
Officers for next year are: A. W. Hig
gins, '13, president; H. L. Hahn, '16,
vice-president; H. J. Ling, '15, secre
tary and treasurer. Basketball men
admitted to the society are: J. R. Da
vis, '15, 112. E. Farrar, '15, L. R. Reyn
olds, '15, H. J. Ling, '15, and A. E.
Worthy, '15.
