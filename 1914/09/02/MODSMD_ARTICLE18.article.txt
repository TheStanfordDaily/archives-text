# Hoover Aids Stranded Yankees.
## 
### 
More than five hundred Americans
in London have been aided by the re
lief committee of the American Society,
of which Trustee IT. C. Hoover is the
chairman. The committee has ar
ranged with the banks for a loan of
$75,000, which will be advanced to
Americans in need of it in sums not
exceeding $200 apiece, in case they
have paper which they cannot nego
tiate or on cable assurance from Amer
ica that they have bank accounts.
Associated with Mr. Hoover in this
work are many influential residents of
London.
