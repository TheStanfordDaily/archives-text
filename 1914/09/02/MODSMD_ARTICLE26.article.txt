# INCREASED NUMBER OF NEW STUDENTS ENROLL
## 
### 
Registration is progressing rapidly,
and figures for the first two days show
a decided increase over the number en
rolled last year at this time. A total
of 139 graduate students registered on
Monday, consisting of 70 men and 43
women, who are old students of Stan
ford, and 15 new men and 11 women.
Last year the total was 108, 6G being
men and 42 women. The number of
new students is larger than ever be
fore, there bein 381 new men and 113
women, making a total of 494, whereas
the total last year was 446, with 334
men and 112 women. The grand total
for the first two days is 633.
