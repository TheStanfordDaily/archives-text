# LAW PROFESSOR RETURNS FROM TRIP TO EUROPE
## 
### 
Professor J. W. Bingham, of the Uw
Department, has returned from Eu
rope, where he has been spending the
sabbatical leave granted him last
semester. He and Mrs. Bingham trav
eled extensively over the continent of
Europe and also on the British Isles.
They started for home two days be
fore the war broke out in Europe, em
barking on the steamship Cincinnati,
one of the Hamburg-American liners
belonging to Germany, and were still
on the high seas when hostilities were
begun between Germany and England.
