# Gibbons Arrives at Home Too Late
## 
### 
United Press Telegraphic Roport, Sept. 3, 1914
.Rome, Sept. I?.—Cardinal Gibbons
and Cardinal O'Connell arrived at noon
today, an hour after the Pope had
been elected. They had left Naples in
•an automobile at five o'clock in the
morning, in an effort to reach the cap
ital.
