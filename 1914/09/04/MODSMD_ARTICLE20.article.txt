# New Pope is Slight of Stature
## 
### 
United Pross Telegraphic Report
Rome, Sept. 4.—The new Pope is a
nobleman with the title of marchess,
but it is said that he has never used
it. He is physically the smallest man
to head the Vatican, the most near
sighted and the most religious.
