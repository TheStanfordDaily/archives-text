# ARMORY PLANS ONE-STEP INITIATION THIS EVENING
## 
### 
An opportunity for campus people to
dance to the accompaniment of music
by Blumenthal's orchestra will be of
fered in the Palo Alto Armory this ev
ening. The first dance of the sea
son will commence at 9 o'clock, in
order to welcome the attendance of
the men after the football rally.
When the date for the dance was set

be held Thursday, instead of Friday.
In accordance with the recent ruling
