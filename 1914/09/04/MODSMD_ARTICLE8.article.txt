# NIXON WILL LEAD YELLS DURING COMING SEASON
## Executive Body Also Chooses Committees and Appoints Officers at First Meeting. 
### 
C. H. Nixon, '15, was officially ap
pointed Yell Leader for the coming
year last evening at the first meeting
of the Executive Committee. Nixon
tried out for Assistant Yell Leader last
fall, and has served in that capacity
since that time. He is a member of
Delta Tau Delta.
The only other business transacted
last night was the appointment of the
officials and committees of the Execu
tive Body. The committee will meet
on Wednesday evening in the future.
Meeting of the Executive Committee,
September 3, 1914.
Absent, Reineman, Siinonson, Wilcox,
Wolford.
Moved and carried that the Executive
Committee meet each Wednesday
night hereafter.
C. H. Nixon, '15, was appointed Yell
Leader for the ensuing year.
H. V. Aupperle, '17, appointed Cus
todian of Trophies.
E. M. Bonnett, '15, appointed Keeper
of Records.
A. E. Worthy, '15; J. E. McDowell
and W. F. Noonan, '16, were appointed
as an auditing committee.
A. E. Worthy, '15, and G. W. Bedeau,
'15, were elected to the Board of
Control of Athletic Fields.
R. W. Wilcox, '13, was appointed to
serve as representative on the Rugby
Union.
Adjourned.
Paul M'Closkey, Secretary.
