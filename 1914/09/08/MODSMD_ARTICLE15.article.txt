# An Error
## 
### 
He charitable toward our mistakes
and inaccuracies, in these our days of
trial, O Reader! And if time be the
question, remember that while it is the
most precious of our possessions, yet
in the present it has no existence. If
we ignore a year in the past in refer
ence to Rugby history, reflect that the
moral is the same, whether it be 1910
or 1909 in illustration.
