# WOMEN TO FILL VACANCIES IN CONFERENCE ON FRIDAY
## List of Eligible Voters for Tomorrow Morning's Election Is Announced.

Woman's Conference will he held to
morrow. The election is made neces

partment.

Room tomorrow from 9:15 until 12:1").
