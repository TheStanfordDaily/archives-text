# MARGERY BAILEY IS NEW CAP AND GOWN PRESIDENT
## 
### 
Cap and Gown held Its first meeting
of the semester yesterday afternoon at
the Langworthy Club. Miss Beatrice
Bingham, '14, who was elected presi
dent last semester, resigned, and Miss
Margery Bailey, *14, was chosen to fill
the vacancy. Miss Felita Smith. '11,
who has returned to the campus for
the coming year to obtain a teacher's
certificate, was elected secretary.
The remainder of the time was spent
in discussing plans for the reception
which Cap and down will give to all
new women students at Mrs. llranner's
home on Monday afternoon.
