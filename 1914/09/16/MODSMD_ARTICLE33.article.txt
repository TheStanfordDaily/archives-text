# Saturday Dance Is Social Attraction.
## 
### 
The second college dance of the fall
semester will be conducted by Blu
menthal's orchestra Saturday evening.
Special cars will run from the cam
pus at 8:15 and 8:30, and arrange
ments have been made to bring the
dancers back to the campus.
Programs may be obtained at the
Bookstore between 2 and 5 o'clock Sat
urday afternoon. An admission price
of fifty cents will be charged.
Professor and Mrs. E. W. Martin will
act as patron and patroness.
