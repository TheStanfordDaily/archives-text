# Yankees Can Gain Trade of Americas.
## 
### 
The European war will act as a
strong stimulant to trade between the
United States and South America, it
is being predicted. It will give this
country a chance to Build up a perma
nent commerce with the Latin-Ameri
can countries, which will continue even
after peace is restored abroad, experts
declare.
Business was practically paralyzed in
South American countries after the
declaration of war, but is gradually ad
justing itself to the new conditions,
Latin-American diplomats in Washing
ton say.
