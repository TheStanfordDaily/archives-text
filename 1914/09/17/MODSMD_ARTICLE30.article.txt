# Servians Abandon Invasion.
## 
### 
Nish, Sept. 17.— It is admitted here
that the Servian Crown Prince was
unable to invade Slavonia, and with
drew when lie learned that the Austri
ans had planned a trap. However, the
Servian and Montenegran invasion of i
Bosnia continues.
