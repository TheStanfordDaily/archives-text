# JUNIORS INVITE SENIORS AND GRADS TO JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
Seniors and bold-overs are invited
to attend the Junior Jolly-up, which
will be held this evening at the Ar
mory in Palo Alto. Chairman C. S.
Milliken, of the Jolly-up Committee,
announced today that several excel
lent stunts have been secured for the
entertainment of the guests. They will
include music by the Freshman string
quintette and a demonstration of all
the latest dances. The refreshments
will form an almost sumptuous repast.
Nothing is being left undone by the
committee to make the affair one of
the most enjoyable in the history of
the school. The Junior orchestra will
furnish the music fo.r the evening.
