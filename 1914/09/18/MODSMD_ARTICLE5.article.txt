# RUGBY RULES COMMITTEES TO MEET MONDAY EVENING
## 
### 
The Rugby Rules Committee, repre
senting Stanford and California, will
meet Monday evening in the Palace
Hotel, San Francisco. Minutes of the
meetings of the last several years will
he considered and revised for use dur
ing the current football season. Stan
ford will he represented by Captain F.
.T. Gard. '14; A. E. Worthy, '14; G. J.
Presley, '07, and Coach Floyd Brown,
Doyle, Dean Schaefer and McKim will
act for California.
