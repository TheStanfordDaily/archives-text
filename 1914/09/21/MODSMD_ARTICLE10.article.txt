# Dr. Wilbur to Discuss Food Poisoning.
## 
### 
Dr. R. L. Wilbur, '96, head of the
Stanford Medical School, in San Fran
cisco, will address the Physiology
Club Tuesday evening, at eight o'clock
on "Food Poisoning." The lecture will
he given in the Physiology lecture
room, and is open to all who are in
terested.
