# EXPOSITION LEAGUE GAME SHOWS STANFORD DEFEAT
## 
### 
Stanford soccerites went down to a
3 to 1 defeat at the hands of the Olym
pic Club players in a fast game staged
Saturday at Recreation Park. Ex-Cap
tain Higgins, Captain Dlodget and Dut
ton starred for the Cardinals, Blodget
tallying Stanford's only score with a
well-placed shot at goal.
This game, together with a Barbar
ian vs. California contest marked the
opening of the season for the Exposi
tion Soccer League, a newly-formed
five-club organization, of which Train
er Harry Maloney is president.
