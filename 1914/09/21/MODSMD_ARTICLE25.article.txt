# Wealthy Advertiser Dies.
## 
### 
United Press Telegraphic Report
San Francisco, Sept. 21.—J. Charles
Green, the wealthy advertising man,
whose operations extend throughout
the state, and who resided at No. 15
Eighteenth avenue, in San Francisco,
died of heart disease today.
