# Vienna Claims Retreats Are Strategical
## 
### 
United Press Telegraphic Report
Vienna (via Rome), Sept. 21. — (Cen
sored)—The following statement was
issued today by the war department:
"Our battle line in Galicia has never
been stronger. The line extending
from Prezemysl to Kracow.a distance of
one hundred miles, has been engaged
by the enemy. For strategic reasons,
the Austrians are retreating slightly
on this line, occupying stronger posi
tions. The evacuating of Jaroslaw,
which occurred Sunday, was deliber
ate, since the city had lost its stra
tegical advantage. The town was
burned before being abandoned.
