# CURATOR PETERSON MADE DELIRIOUS BY ACCIDENT
## 
### 
H. C. Peterson, curator of the Stan
ford Museum, was struck by an auto
mobile on Embarcadero Road, between
the car track and the county road,
while riding to Palo Alto on his bi
cycle this noon. Although Peterson
was not seriously injured, he received
numerous bruises and cuts on the face
and body. He was picked up uncon
scious shortly afte.r the accident by Dr.
R. G. Reynolds, of Palo Alto, and taken
to the Peninsula Hospital. Owing to
the nervous shock of the accident,
Peterson is still unable to remember
anything clearly.
