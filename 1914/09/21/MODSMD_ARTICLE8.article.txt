# Graduates Killed in Auto Accident.
## 
### 
News has reached Stanford of the
death, in an auto accident near Sioux
City, la., of Mr. and Mrs. W. C. McNeil,
both of whom graduated in the class
of '98.
Mrs. McNeil, who was Miss Virginia
Hearne, of San Diego, was a member
of Alpha Phi sorority, and one of the
belles of her day.
McNeil was a member of the Sigma
Rho Eta local fraternity, which later
became the chapter of Delta Kappa Ep
silon.
