# Faculty Entertains Encina Club.
## 
### 
Encina Club was entertained at a
barbecue given by the Faculty yester
day evening at the Faculty Clubhouse
grounds. The program consisted of
several songs and musical numbers, in
addition to speeches by Vice-President
John Maxson Stillman, ,T. P. Smith,
president of the Faculty Club, and E.
C. Behrens, '14, president of the En
cina Club.
