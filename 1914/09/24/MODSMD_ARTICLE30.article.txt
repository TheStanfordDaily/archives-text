# Austrians Still Hold Serajevo.
## 
### 
Cettinje, Sept. 24. —It is announced
that the Montenegrans are bombarding
Serajevo, and that its capture is near.
This report confirms the Austrian
denial of the report circulated yester
day that the Bosnian city had fallen.
