# FRANKLIN TO PHOTO 1916 CLASS MEMBERS FOR QUAD
## 
### 
H. P. Elliott, manager of the 191 '5
Quad, has made arrangements with
photographer .1. C. Franklin of Palo
Alto, to take the individual pictures Jf
members of the junior class, to be used
in this year's annual. As in past years
an assessment of five dollars is levied
on each junior, the receipt of which
entitles holder to have his or her pic
ture taken at Franklin's, without
further charge.
On account of arrangements with
the printers it will be necessary to got
this part of the Quad work done this
semester. All juniors should pay as
sessment to Elliott, and get pictures
taken at an early date.
