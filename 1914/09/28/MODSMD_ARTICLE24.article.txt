# Company "L" Wins Two-Mile Relay
## 
### 
Five Stanford men were members of
the Company "L" two-mile relay race
which won its event at the Army and
Navy Field meet, in San Francisco Sat
urday. The Stanford men were: J.
K. Norton, *10; E. T. Worthy, '14; I.
Talboy, '17; Emil Huttman, ex-'l7, and
M. N. Kirksey, '18. The time of the
race was seven minutes and forty-two
seconds.
