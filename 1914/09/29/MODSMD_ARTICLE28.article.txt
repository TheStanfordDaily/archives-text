# Russia Repulses German Advance.
## 
### 
Petrograd, Sept. 29.—The general
staff declares that the German offen
sive movement into Russian-Poland
has been halted and that Renne
kampf's attempt to penetrate the Rus
sian line has been with heavy
losses.
