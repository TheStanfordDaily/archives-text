# Band to Give Informal on October 2.
## 
### 
The first band informal of the year
will be held on Friday, October second,
at the Palo Alto Armory. An attractive
program of the progressive as well as
the older dances has been selected.
Tickets will be on sale at the Book
store on Wednesday, September 30.
