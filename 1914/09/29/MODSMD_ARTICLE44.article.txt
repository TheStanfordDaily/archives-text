# College Park Transfers Organize.
## 
### 
Transfer students from the College
of the Pacific met last week and or
ganized a social club, in which all
transfers from the College Park insti
tution are eligible for membership.
The following officers were elected:
C. S. Andrus, '15, president; Miss Joyce
Gardiner, '15, vice-president; Miss Ed
na Hannibal, '15, secretary; C. Steph
ens, gr., treasurer.
