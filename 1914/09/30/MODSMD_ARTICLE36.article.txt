# SOPHOMORE JOLLY-UP TO OFFER RARE DIVERSION
## 
### 
The sophomore jolly-up committee is
planning to stage an evening's enter
tainment on Friday, October 9, that
will equal any class informal yet
staged. Unusual stunts, skits, and
numerous dances, with appetizing re
freshments are on the program.
One of the amusing features of the
jolly-up, which promises lively enter
tainment, will be a selection of the
handsomest man on the floor. The la
dies will be given an opportunity of
choosing their partners by the inclu
sion of two-ladies' choice and like
number of robber dances on the pro
gram. Hot doughnuts, cider and ap
ples in plenty will constitute nourish
ment for the one-steppers.
