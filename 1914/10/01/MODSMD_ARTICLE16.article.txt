# British Steamer Strikes Mine.
## 
### 
London, Oct. I.—A Lloyd's dispatch
declares that the Pritish steamer Sel
by, laden with coal and bound for Ant
werp, struck a mine in the North Sea
today, and sank. The crew of twenty
one men we.re rescued.
