# Don't Borrow His Ticket
## 
### 
Somewhere in the statutes covering
illegal practices there is a provision
for the punishment of persons using
another's property as their own. Under
this head should be the use of a card
of admission to athletic games, which
names some one other than the bearer
This short and easy path for the bor
rower to a seat in the bleachers has
been well trod of late, and the num
ber of members of the Associated Stu
dents present by no means has equaled
the total of season tickets punched at
the gate.
As a habit that is mere negligence
needs no more for correction than
critical comment, the practice is one
that will be discontinued from date.
