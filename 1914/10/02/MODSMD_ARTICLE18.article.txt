# SPECIAL PEACE SERVICE IS TO BE HELD SUNDAY
## 
### 
In compliance with President Wil
son's proclamation requesting that
Sunday, October 4, be observed as
Peace Sunday, the morning service at
the Memorial Church will be a peace
service. Reverend D. Charles Gard
iner will preach.
A special music program has been
arranged for the occasion. The stu
dent choir will render the anthem "De
Profundus," by Gounod, and .T. F.
Jones, of Palo Alto, will sing Kipling's
"Recessional."
At the four o'clock vesper service.
Organist L. H. Eaton will play the
"Messe de Mariage," by Dubois.
