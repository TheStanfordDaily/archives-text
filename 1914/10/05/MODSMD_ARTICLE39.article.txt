# New Ore Collections Are Acquired.
## 
### 
Three collections of ores have been
added to the Museum of Geology and
Metallurgy Department, through the
efforts of students in the department.
One of the most valuable is from H.
Park, ex-'O7, who is now manager of
the silver mines of Cobalt, Ontario,
Canada. It contains some unsually
fine specimens of silver o.re.
J. J. Beeson, *15, has just completed
a collection of ores from the mines
around Salt Lake and J. C. Ray, '13, is
working on a collection from Butte,
Montana.
