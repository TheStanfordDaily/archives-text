# Library Receives New Books.
## 
### 
The Library is in receipt of a large
shipment of books this week, the ma
jority of which are for the use of the
Departments of English and Art. The
new addition includes a Biblical His
tory of fourteen volumes, containing
accounts of research work done in
Palestine, Mt. Sinai, and Arabia Pe
traea; R. Bloonifield's latest book,
"Architectural Drawing and Drafts
man," which is a survey of the world's
greatest draughtsman; "Art," trans
lation from the French by Augusta
Rodin, by Paul Gscll, and A. J. Eddy's
work on "Cubista Past Impression
ism," containing innumerable repro
ductions in color of famous paintings."
