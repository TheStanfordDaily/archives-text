# English Chili Plans Open Meeting.
## 
### 
The English Club announces an open
meeting to be held in conjunction with
its regular meeting on October 15th.
Agnes Gray, '15, at the meeting held
recently, has appointed the following
committee to arrange programs for
the semester: Miss Alice Rogers, '15,
chairman; Miss Nellita Choate, '17;
William Logan, '14, and Ed. Ford, '14,
Miss Helen Kreps, '15, was appointed
chairman of the play committee.
