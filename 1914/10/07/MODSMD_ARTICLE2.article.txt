# "THE GIRL IN TOKIO" IS SUBJECT OF Y.W.C.A. TALK.
## 
### 
Tomorrow night will be the climax
of the Y. W. C. A. meetings, concerning
work in foreign fields. .Miss Margaret
Matthew will speak on "The Girl in
Tokio." in the Chapel at eight o'clock.
Her talk will he illustrated by colored
slides of Japan.
Miss Hazel Meeks. of the San Jose
Normal School, will sing and Mrs.
Rranner will preside. The meeting is
open to all Palo Alto people, as well as
Stanford students, and it is hoped that
a large audience will attend.
