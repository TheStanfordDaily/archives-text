# Zeb Terry Visiting on Campus.
## 
### 
Z. A. Terry, '14, captain of the vavsi
ty baseball nine during the 1913 and
1914 seasons, and four-star man, is a
visitor on the campus. For the past
month Terry has been playing in the
utility role for the Los Angeles Coast
League team, and has come north with
the team on its present trip.
As the Los Angeles nine is sched
uled to play a series each with the San
Francisco and Oakland aggregations
during the next two weeks in the Ray
City, Terry will be a daily visitor on
the campus. He will stay at the Delta
Tau Delta house.
