# MALONEY TO DIRECT BOYS REDWOOD CITY GYM CLUB
## 
### 
"The Redwood City Civic Center
Athletic Association" has asked Train
er Maloney to direct a boys' gymna
sium club in that city. Maloney is
assisted in his work by John Wiggins,
'10, who has come back for his post
graduate work in engineering.
The gym club will give an exhibition
of wrestling, boxing, jumping, and oth
er athletics in Redwood City, at the in
vitation of the athletic association.
Miss Mary Ga.rd, Ma, and Miss Doro
thy Metz, M7, will also give an exhibi
tion, performing the "Grand Salute,"
one of the most difficult feats in the
line of fencing.
