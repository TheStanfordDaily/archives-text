# Berlin Claims Advantage.
## 
### 
Berlin, Oct. B.—lt is announced that
the attack on Antwerp is uninterrupt
ed. The inner forts are crumbling and
certain capture of the city is nea.r. The
situation in France is unchanged, ex
cept that the attempts of the French
to regain the lost ground have failed.
