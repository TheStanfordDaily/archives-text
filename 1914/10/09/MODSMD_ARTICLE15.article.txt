# Y.M.C.A. Organizes Palo Alto Club.
## 
### 
A high school club of boys in Palo
Alto is being organized by the Social
Service committee of the Y. M. C. \.
of Stanford. F. Keast, 'If!, of the Delta
Chi house, is in charge of the club,
which will be for the purpose of for
warding Y, M. C. A. principles among
the high school men.
