# WELL-KNOWN MISSIONARY TO SPEAK BEFORE Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Mrs. Dwight Potter, student secre
tary of the Presbyterian Church, will
speak at the Y. W. C. A. meeting to
morrow afternoon on the subject,
"Children in Foreign Lands." Mrs.
Potter has been in Syria as a teacher,
and before occupying her present po
sition was a general secretary of the
Christian association.
All women who are interested in the
formation of an organization for for
eign missionary work are requested
to meet in the Chapel Wednesday af
ternoon at 4:30 o'clock.
