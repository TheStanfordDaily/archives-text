# ENGLISH CLUB PRESENTS ARTHUR ROW IN READING
## 
### 
Arthur Row, of the "Milestones",
Company, now playing at the Columbia
Theater, in San Francisco, will give
a reading of Maeterlinck's famous play,
"Aglavaine and Selysette," at 3:30 on
Tuesday in Chapel. Mr. Row has
been reading for the various clubs in
San Francisco, and in all the large
cities in the cast. The reading will be
siven under the auspices of the
English Club. An admission of twen
t.v-five cents will be collected at the
door.
