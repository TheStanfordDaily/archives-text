# HAVRE BELGIAN CAPITAL
## 
### 
United Press Telegraphic Report
Paris, Oct. 13.—An announcement
from Bordeaux states that quarters
have been provided for Belgian gov
ernment officials at Havre, on the
north coast of France. The Belgium
capital will be establisned there.
