# LAW SMOKER DATED FOR NEXT THURSDAY EVENING
## Talks by Professors, Vaudeville Turns and Attractive Refreshment List Provided for Gathering. 
### 
Three hundred students of the de
partment are wanted at the annual
Law smoker, which will be held at the
Faculty Clubhouse Thursday evening
at 8 o'clock.
Professors F. C. Woodward and J.
M. Cheadle, of the Law Department,
will address the gathering. An in
formal program of vaudeville turns
has been prepared for entertainment.
The purpose of the smoker is to
bring the old and new students of Law
and Pre-Legal standing in touch with
each other. Corncobs, apples, and
cider will be on the refreshment list.
