# Canal Question to Be Debated.
## 
###

should reject. Secretary Bryan's treaty
indemnifying Colombia for canal
rights," is the question which will be
discussed by the Euphronia Literary
and Debating Society this evening.
The meeting will be held at 7:30 in
room 12 of the Law Department, in
stead of in room 225.
