# Stanford Syllabi Sell at California.
## 
### 
"The first hundred of the syllabus
sheets sold in one day. Will you kind
ly send by parcels post fifty sets
more." This message was received
yesterday by Professor M. S. Wildman,
head of the Economics Department, in
regard to Economics 1 syllabus sheets
that were recently put on sale at the
Associated Students' Store, at the
University of California. They will
be used in connection with the course
in elementary economics at the state
institution.
