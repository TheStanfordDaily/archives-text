# DESTINATION OF MR. AND MRS. WILSON IS SECRET
## 
### 
Five hundred guests attended the
wedding of Miss Carol Green. '14, and
Mr. G. O. Wilson. '14, held in the Me
morial Chtireli last evening. A recep
tion was held in the Kappa Alpha
Theta house after the wedding. Mr.
and Mrs. Wilson left by automobile
late last evening, keeping their desti
nation secret.
