To Echo Across tlie May.
The most orderly retreat in the his
tory of college athletics.
Come to think of it, he who bets last
bets best.
For something really absorbing to
read, try what it says on your blot
ter.
Inspiration point—the heights a man
ascends when he asks the girl the
question. Located between Mt. Cour
age and Mt. Determination, just below
the highest pinnacle, Mt. Ambition.
There's a cloud of dust that follows
every fast-moving vehicle, and doesn't
overtake it until the vehicle comes to a
stop. Those who have put off apply
ing for Big Game tickets to the last
minute are akin to the cloud. They
have one mo.re opportunity to catch up
with themselves. The wagon stops to
morrow.
University of Washington women
have abolished stylish dresses, low
nock waists, superfluous hair, rouge,
earrings, and conspicious jewelry.
Let's see. that leaves—? Shucks, might
as well abolish co-education.
