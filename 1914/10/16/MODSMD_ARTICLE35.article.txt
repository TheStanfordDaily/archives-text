# New Book Issued on Reasons for War.
## 
### 
Information about why England,
France, and Germany went to war
may be obtained at the Pookstore.
The "White Papers" of England and
Germany, and the "Orange Paper" of
Russia, and a number of other diplo
matic correspondence documents re
lating to the European war have been
embodied in one paper called "Why
England, Germany, and Russia Went
to War," published by the New York
Times Company.
