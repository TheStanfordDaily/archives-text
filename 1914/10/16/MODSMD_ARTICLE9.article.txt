# J. A. HANNA IS WINNER OF CIVIC LEAGUE PRIZE
## 
### 
.T. A. Hanna, sr., has been .recently
awarded the annual $100 prize offered
by the Intercollegiate Civic League for
the best, paper on the advantage or dis
advantage of Combining outside activi
ties with the regular curriculum.
The keynote of ITanna's paper Is va
riety in work, life, and environment.
Among other things Hanna sug
gested that any man who has re
ceived most of his education in schools
for men only should spend at least one
year in some co-educational institu
tion.
Hanna graduated from Dartmouth
and is doing graduate history work at
Stanford.
