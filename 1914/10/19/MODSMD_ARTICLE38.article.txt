# ORIENTAL TOPIC GROUP TO MEET AGAIN TOMORROW
## 
### 
The group of men who meet weekly
to discuss the problems of the Orient
will get together for the second time
tomorrow noon in the basement of the
Engineering Building. Professor J. B.
Treat, of the History Department, will
deliver a brief talk on the history of
the countries to be studied as a foun
dation fo.r the further work of the se
mester.
The group meets at 12:45 and ad
journs in time for afternoon classes.
Men wishing to bring their own lunch
or to eat a light lunch provided by
the Y. M. C. A. at a nominal charge
will gather at 12:30. Those wishing to
procure their luncheon at the place
of meeting should notify Secretary E.
H. Lockwood tomorrow morning.
