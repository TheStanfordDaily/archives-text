# CAMPUS WOMEN TO SEW FOR EUROPEAN REFUGEES
## 
### 
The women on the campus are in
vited to meet at the home of Mrs. H. C.
Brown, '16 Salvatierra street, to make
clothing for the women and children m
Europe, who have been left destitute
as a result of the war.
Donations of clothing may be sent to
Mrs. Brown's home at any time.
The sewing meetings will assemble
on Wednesday and Friday afternoons.
It is possible to send clothing to
England at the present time, and the
committee would like to accomplish as
much work as possible in the next
few weeks.
