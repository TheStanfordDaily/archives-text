# EUPHRONIA SOCIETY TO DEBATE THIS EVENING
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating and Literary
Society will debate the question, which
was postponed from last week, at its
meeting in room 12 of the Law Depart
ment this evening. President A. F.
Coyle, '15, urges all the members of
the Varsity to be present, as the last
gathering was adjourned on account
of the small attendance.
The meeting of Nestoria Debating
Society last evening was interrupted
by the football rally.
