# ECHO ORGAN TO BE USED AT RECITAL ON MONDAY
## 
### 
The first of the series of daily re
citals on the new echo organ in the
Memorial Church will be held Monday,
from 4:30 to 5 o'clock, by Organist L.
11. Eaton. The program follows:
Chimes.
Silas —March in R.
Guilmant —Elevation in A.
Sodermann —Swedish Wedding March
Saran—Romanza.
