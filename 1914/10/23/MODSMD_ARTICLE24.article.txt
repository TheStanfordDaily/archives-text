# On the Quiet
## 
### 
Com imitsitioii.
My dear OTQ, 1 luiv bin reeding you.r
j littul kolum every daigh in eeger ex
| pecktashun of C'ing sumthing tu laff
at. Up to yesterday 1 hav lived in
disappointment. And that is jut watt
I wunt to tell about. 1 beleeve (firm
ly) that yesterday's On the Quiet was
tu skandluss for our Q-munnity. Altho
publicity for Ram Shed is good for the
kollej it nead nut b raw. Four we
have enuff raw, raw stuff on the
bleechers. I am diskizing my stile,
so my frenz woughnt no it. Pleaz re
fraign from publishing my name. With
best regards from all the girls toyou
and yours, 1 remaign.
HTC. '06.
Pres. Cent) Women's Conference.
