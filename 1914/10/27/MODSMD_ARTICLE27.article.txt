# STANFORD WOMAN DOPES OUTCOME OF BIG GAME
## 
### 
Miss Anita Allen, '17, of The Daily
Palo Alto staff, in answer to the re
quest of the San Francisco Bulletin
that she represent the women of the
student body in the paper's annual wo
men's sporting edition, wrote a column
article regarding the probable outcome
of the Pig Game. The news story was
written in a conservative and convinc
ing manner, and displayed well-in
formed knowledge of the situation in
rugby circles at both California and
Stanford.
