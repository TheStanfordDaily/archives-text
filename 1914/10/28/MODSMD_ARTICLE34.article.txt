# ANDERSON RETURNS FROM LATIN AMERICAN COUNTRIES

### 
M. P. Anderson, '04, a graduate of
the Zoology Department, and son of
Professo.r M. B. Anderson, former head
of the English Department, and now
professor emeritus, is on the campus

zoological laboratories. Anderson has
recently returned from an exploration
trip to South America, where he se
cured mammal specimens for the Chi
cago Field Museum of Natural History,
with W. H. Osgood, '99. Osgood is still
in South America. Anderson was for

ford's exploration in Eastern Asia.
