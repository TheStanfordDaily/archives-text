# FIRST MILITARY BALL OF WEST FOR SAN FRANCISCO
## 
### 
A pageantry of brilliant uniforms,
fashionable gowns, and elaborate
dance numbers of historic interest will
combine to make the first military
ball eve.r held west of Washington a
spectacle of unusual splendor. The
affair, which is to be held for the aid
of the Army Relief Society, will be
given at San Francisco's new Civic Au
ditorium on January 15.
More than 4000 invitations have been
issued for the ball, and it is expected
that over 2000 couples will take part
in the dance, while at least 10.000 will
view the pageant from the great bal
conies of the auditorium.
