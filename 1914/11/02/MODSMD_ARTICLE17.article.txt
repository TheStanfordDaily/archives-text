# Carranza's Successor Chosen.
## 
### 
United Press Telegraphic Report
El Paso, Tex., Nov. 2.—General Eula
lio Gutierrez, governor of the state of
San Luis Potosi, has been elected pro
visional president to succeed Carran
za, according to the latest advices. He
is considered a strong C'arranza sup
porter.
