# Bleacher Practice to Follow Talk.
## 
### 
Following Dr. B.ranner's short talk
to men this evening in Encina Club
room, a practice of the new bleacher
song and Stanford yells will be held
under the leadership of Carey Nixon.
As announced last evening, the Pres
ident will personally tell the men this
evening of the need of relief for the
Belgians, and the methods which will
be employed to raise funds for that
purpose at Stanford. The talk will be
gin at 7:15, and Dr. Branner will con
clude. The song practice will be com
pleted in ample time for those attend
ing to get to the Assembly Hall in time
for the election returns rally.
