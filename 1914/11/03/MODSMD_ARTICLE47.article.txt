# Philologists Will Meet Thursday.
## 
### 
The Philological Association of the
University will meet at the home of
Professor A. T. Murray, of the Greek
Department, on next Thursday even
ing.
The following papers will be read:
"Shakespeare's Sonnets Interpreted in
the Light of His Plays," by Professor
H. D. Gray, of the English Department,
and "Walter Savage Landor as an Ex
emplar of Comparative Literature," by
W. Chislett, gr.
The meeting will be called at 7:45
p. m., at 1019 Bryant street.
