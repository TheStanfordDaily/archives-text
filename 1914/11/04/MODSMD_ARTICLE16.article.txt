# Mrs. Hoover Will Address Faculty Club
## 
### 
Mrs. H. C. Hoover, 'OS, will address
the members of the Faculty Women's
Club at its meeting on Monday. Novem
ber 0. at 3:3 ft. The subject of the
talk will be "Europe Under War Con
ditions," and is one on which Mrs.
Hoover is well posted. The meeting
will be held in the Faculty Club House.
