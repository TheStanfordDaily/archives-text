# ENGINEERS WILL VISIT CALAVERAS EARTHEN DAM
## 
### 
An expedition under charge of the
Civil Engineering Society is being or
ganized for the purpose of visiting the
Calaveras Earthen Dam, now under
construction near Mount Hamilton.
The party, consisting of upper class
men in the Department of Civil Engin
eering, will leave by automobile No
vember 10, at S: 00 a. m. A fare of
$2.50 will be charged.
Part of the Spring Valley Water
Company's plant, which furnishes
water to San Francisco, will also be
visited.
