# STANFORD WOMEN TO DINE WITH CALIFORNIA SENIORS
## 
### 
According to the annual custom, the
Senior women of the University of Cal
ifornia will give a luncheon to the
Senior women of Stanford, on the day
of the Big Game. This entertainment
will take place in the Senior women's
hall.
Miss Marjorie Tisdale, president of
the Women's Conference, .requests that
all the Stanford senior women who in
tend to be at California on November
14. band their names to her, if the>
have not already done so. This must
be done before Wednesday, as it is
necessary to let the California women
know how many to prepare for.
