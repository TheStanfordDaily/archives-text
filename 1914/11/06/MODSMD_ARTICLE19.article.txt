# Student Council Acts.
## 
### 
Stanford University, Cal.,
November, 1914.
The Student Council recommends
that three students be placed on pro
bation fo.r the remainder of the se
mester after November 1; that two
students be suspended for two weeks
after November 1 and placed on strict
probation for the remainder of the se
mester thereafter; and that one stu
dent be forbidden the privilege of the
campus during the present semester.
These punishments are inflicted for
varying degrees of disorderly conduct
and infractions of the rule regarding
the use of liquor on the campus.
OTIS H. CASTLE,
Chairman Student Council.
Approved: Frederic C. Woodward,
University Chairman Committee on
Students affairs.
,T. C. BRANNER,
President.
