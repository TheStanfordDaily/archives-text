# Concert Reception to Aid War Victims
## 
### 
To raise additional funds for the
relief of war victims, Jean Baptiste
Toner, noted Scotch Chopin artist, will
give a pianoforte concert at the Ar
mory, eight o'clock, Saturday evening,
November 7.
Toner received instruction at Lon
don from both the celebrated teach
ers, de Pachmann and Madame Car
reno.
Tickets, costing fifty cents, may
now be purchased at the Stanford
Bookstore.
