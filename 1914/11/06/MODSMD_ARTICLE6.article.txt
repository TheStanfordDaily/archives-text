# NEW SONG BOOK TO BE SOLD BY CAP AND GOWN
## 
### 
Cap and Gown announces that the
new edition of the Stanford Sons
Honk will be on sale at the bleachers
tomorrow afternoon, and at the Big
Game. Besides containing all the old
songs, the new volume includes the
new bleacher favorites by M. A. Hagen,
'15, and by W. G. Paul, '15.
As in former years, the price of the
book will be fifty cents.
