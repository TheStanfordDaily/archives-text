# Entomology Library Receives Book.
## 
### 
"The Manual of Fruit Insects" by
Mark Vernon Slingerland. of the New
York State College of Agriculture, at
Cornell University, has been received
by the Entomology Department, and
will be on the shelf for general Uni
versity use.
Special reference is made to Califor
nia fruit industries, statistics having
been compiled for the apple, peach,
and grape-growing regions.
