# Clinic Worker Will Address Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Mrs. Katherine Barnett, the social
service worker at Lane Hospital, em
ployed by the clinic auxiliaries, will
speak to the members and guests of
the Y. W. C. A. tomorrow in the Vestry
at 4:30 Her talk will be of great in
terest, because Lane clinic is the larg
est charitable organization maintained
by Stanford. Miss Marjorie Tisdale,
'IT), will lead the meeting, and special
music will be furnished.
Miss Marion L. Curtner, '14, is on
the campus for a week's visit.
