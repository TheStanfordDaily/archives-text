# J.C. RAY WILL ADDRESS SAN FRANCISCO ENGINEERS
## 
### 
J. C. Ray, 'ex-'O7, will address the
San Francisco section of the American
Institute of Mining Engineers tonight
in the Engineers' Club room at the Sut
ter Hotel.
The address will be given on "The
Geological and Microscopic Study of
the Disposition of Ore in the Butte
District." Tt will be illustrated by a
large number of lantern slides.
This address was also given before
the National Convention of the Ameri
can Institute of Mining Engineers, at
Salt Lake City in August, where it at
tracted favorable attention on account
of the high grade of economic work
done at Stanford.
