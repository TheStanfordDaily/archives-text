# Alumni Marry In Arizona.
## 
### 
Announcement of the marriage of
Miss Marguerite Graham, '12. to J. L.
Wilson, '11, at Phoenix, Arizona, on
Saturday, October 7, has been received.
Miss Graham was a member of the
Schubert Club, Y. W. C. A. Cabinet,
Women's Conference, and President of
Cap and Gown. She also belonged be
longed to Delta Gamma.
Wilson was the business manager of
The Daily Palo Alto in 1912.
