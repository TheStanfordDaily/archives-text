# ‘15’s Guests Asked to Return Clothes.
## 
### 
Juniors who were guests of the
Senior class at the Beta Theta Pi
house over Friday night, and who bor
rowed wearing apparel to wear home
in the morning, are .requested to re
turn the articles to their owners as
soon as possible.
One suit was lost, and coats and
trousers are missing from others. Half
a dozen sweaters and several pairs of
trousers were appropriated.
While the men in the Beta Theta Pi
house realize that the Juniors needed
the clothes temporarily, they want
them back as soon as possible.
