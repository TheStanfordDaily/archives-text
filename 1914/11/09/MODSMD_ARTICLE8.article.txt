# NIXON ANNOUNCES THREE SONG AND YELL PRACTICES
## 
### 
In preparation for Big Game root
ing, Yell-Leader Nixon has announced
that there will be three sons and
yell practices held during the week.
On Tuesday night all the men will
meet at Encina Hall at 7:30 p. m. On
Wednesday and Thursday afternoons
practice will be held on the football
bleachers from 4:30 until 6 o'clock.
