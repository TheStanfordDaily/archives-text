# ROOTERS WILL HOLD FINAL SONG AND YELL PRACTICE
## 
### 
The last evening Song and Yell prac
tice will be held at Encina Hall tonight
at 7:30, under the direction of Yell
Leader Nixon. The Cardinal rooters
will perfect themselves in cheers and
yells of encouragement for the Varsity
in the Big Game Saturday. Final af
ternoon practice will be held at the
football bleachers tomorrow and
Thursday from 4:30 to 6.
