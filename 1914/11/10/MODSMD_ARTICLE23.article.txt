# Stanford Man Races in Desert Grind.
## 
### 
The name of Maurice Obear, ex-'l2,
former Varsity firstbaseman. appears
in the list of contestants who left El
Paso last Friday morning in the great
motorcycle race across the desert to
Phoenix, Arizona. The annual race
from El Paso to Phoenix is one of the
motorcycle classics of the country, a
purse of eleven hundred dollars going
to the winner. The gruelling contest
lasts about four days. "Jimmy" Obear
is riding a Thor machine.
