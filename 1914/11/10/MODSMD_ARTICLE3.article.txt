# OUTSIDE SQUAD TO PLAT FINAL GAME FOR DINNER
## 
### 
The outside squad of ruggers will
close their activities for the season in
a final game tomorrow afternoon. R.
L. Templeton, '18, and H. P. Hauser,
sp.. will pick two teams of fifteen men
each to contest on the auxiliary turf.
The losers will pay the costs of a din
ner. to be held in the evening at Lar
kin's, in celebration of the end of the
training season.
