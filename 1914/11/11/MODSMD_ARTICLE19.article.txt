# Mrs. Duncan Addresses Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
M.rs. Duncan, of the Maud Booth
Home, spoke to the Y. W. C. A. yes
terday, expressing her appreciation of
the interest of the University women
in her work. She pointed out the need
of individual attention in such an in
stitution as the Maud Booth Home,
and pleaded for more support along
certain lines.
Mrs. Katherine Barnett, who was
scheduled to speak at yesterday's meet
ing was unable to appear.
