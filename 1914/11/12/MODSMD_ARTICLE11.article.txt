# NEW SONG HOOKS WILL BE ON SALE AGAIN TOMORROW
## 
### 
The new sons books, recently com
piled by Cap and Gown, will be sold
on the Quad between the hours of ten
and twelve-thirty tomorrow morning.
These books contain all of the songs
that were printed in last year's publi
cation, with the addition of two new
ones.
This sale will give the returning
alumni an opportunity to purchase
copies of the new songs, which will be
sung on the bleachers on Saturday.
The price is fifty cents.
