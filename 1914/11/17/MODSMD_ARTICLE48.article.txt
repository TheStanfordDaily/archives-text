# VEGETATION OF MT. ROBSON WILL BE LECTURE SUBJECT
## 
### 
"The Vegetation of the Mt. Robson
Region" is the title of the lecture
which Dr. W. S. Cooper will deliver
at the Stanford Faculty Club on Wed
nesday evening, November twenty
eighth. The talk which is being giv
en under the auspices of the Science
Association, will begin promptly at
eight o'clock.
During the past season Dr. Cooper
spent several months among the west
ern peaks of the Rockies, among which
Mt. Robson is conspicuous, not. so
much for its size as for its peculiar
vegetat'on. These unusual features of
the locality will be fully described by
the lecturer.
