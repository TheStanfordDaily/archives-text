# JUDGE MURASKY TO GIVE SOCIAL SERVICE LECTURE
## 
### 
Judge J. F. Murasky, of the Superior
Court, will lecture in the chapel this
evening at eight o'clock on the sub
ject of "Juvenile Dependents." To
night's lecture will be the first of a se
ries dealing with social service work,
which will be held under the auspices
of the Y. M. C. A and Y. W. C. A.
