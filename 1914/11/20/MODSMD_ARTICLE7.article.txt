# S.I. MILLER REPRESENTS UNIVERSITY IN THE EAST
## 
### 
Assistant Professor S. I. Miller, Jr.,
of the Economics Department, and now
studying at Harvard, represented Stan
ford University at the National Con
ference of Mayors on Municipal Prob
lems, held in Philadelphia November
12-11. Each American university was
entitled to one delegate.
Miller will return next fall to give
courses in transportation, in which
subject he is now specializing.
