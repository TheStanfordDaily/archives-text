Have You Ever
Noticed
that in any game where a ball is
used in a competitive way,
that the official ball al
ways bears this trade
mark. whether it be FOOT
HALL. BASKET BALL, INDOOR

BALL or any other athletic game?
There must be a reason for this
universal adoption by the leading
organization connected with
sports, and there is a reason—no
one can make them as good.
The same argument applies to
all things athletic.
