# YALE UNIVERSITY CLAIMS 18,387 LIVING GRADUATES
## 
### 
A newly-published Directory of Yale
Alumni shows 18,287 living graduates.
There are also 6,385 living ex-students.
Of the various occupations, law con
i tinueg to attract the largest number of
graduates, with a total of 3,091; manu
facturing follows with 2,072; then in
order comes engineering, 1,991; educa
tion, 1,930; mercantile business, 1,461;
medicine, 1,352; the ministry, 1,258;
journalism and letters, 455; agricul
ture, 396; science, 255; art, 244; trans
portation, 23G, and government ser
vice, 215.
