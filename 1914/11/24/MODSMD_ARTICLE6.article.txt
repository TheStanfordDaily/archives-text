# Graduate Appointed Police Judge.
## 
### 
Morris Oppenheim, 'ft.', who for the
past five years has been assisting
prosecuting attorney of San Francis
co, has been appointed to fill the po
lice judgeship made vacant by the
election of the former judge, Shortall,
to the superior bench. While at Stan
ford Oppenheim was editor of the
Chaparral. He is the first graduate to
fill a judicial position of this sort, al
though several alumni have served as
superior judges.
