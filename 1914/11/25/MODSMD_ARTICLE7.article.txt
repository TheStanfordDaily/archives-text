# Association Will Picnic.
## 
### 
Friday evening members of the Y. W.
C. A, and Y. M. C. A. will go
on a Thanksgiving hike. All mem
bers of the University are invited.
Each person is asked to bring a bas
ket lunch. The women who intend
to go may leave their names
with Eleanor Walls or Marcie Hunt.
Mr. Lockwood or Fred Murray will
take the names of the men. The pic
nicers will meet at Itoble Gymnasium
at 3:30 o'clock Friday afternoon.
