# Palmer Will Give Last Talk of Series
## 
### 
Rev. A. W. Palmer will deliver the
last of his series of talks tomorrow af
ternoon in the vestry. Rev. Palmer,
who is the pastor of the First Congre
gational church, of Oakland, has de
livered three of these afternoon lec
tures under the auspices of the Y. W.
C. A. and Y. M. C. A. The subject an
nounced for tomorrow is "Religion for
Health, Happiness and Efficiency." All
students are invited to attend the
meeting, which will begin at 4:30.
