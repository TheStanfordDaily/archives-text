# E.W. SMITH ESTABLISHES NEW OFFICE IN ROOM 205.
## 
### 
Assistant Professo.r E. W. Smith, '99,
of the English Department, has estab
lished himself in his new office in the
south part of old room 200. A partition
separates the office from a class room,
which occupies the other portion of
205. The old office of Mr. Smith will
be refitted for other professors of the
English Department. Mr. Smith is
editor of the Alumnus, and general
publicity agent on the campus, and the
immense amount of correspondence
which passes through his hands caused
his removal to a more spacious of
fice.
