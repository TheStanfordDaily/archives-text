# FRESHMAN VARIETY ACTORS WILL REDUCE WOOD BILL
## 
### 
Plans for the Freshman Vaudeville,
scheduled for the early part of next,
semester, will he considered at the
meeting of the first-year students
Thursday in room 214. The class ex
pects to pay the extra expenses of
building two bonfires by means of
this show, which will in all probabili
ty be held in the Assembly Hall this
year, instead of in the Varsity Theatre,
as has been the custom in former
years.
