# On the Quiet
##

l'alo Alto Notes.
Si Washington come into this here
offus yesterday wearing the beaming
est smile we've over seen, lie smole
the bull time he was nere. Si says h"
exspearminted on bis cows by feeding
them nuthing but milk weed, and they
givo 20 galleys more miluk than ever
before.
Statisticks have proved that thir
teen Cl 3) boys and youths have died
from the rough game of football this
here year. Bowl at Joe's. —Adv.
Police Clieef Nobul says none of
them holly berries growing on our
northern city limits is to be plucked
without permisshun from him bissolf.
And lie informs adorous high school
lads and lassies as well as the frisity
college inmates from tbr> neerby insti
tution that there ain't no missultoe
anywhere in this vicinity.
