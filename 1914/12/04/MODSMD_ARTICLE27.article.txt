# La
						Liana Meets Saturday.
## 
### 
La Liana will hold its last meeting
this semester on Saturday, December
fifth, at the home of Mary Curry, 349
Hamilton Avenue. Miss Cavanaugh,
from Detroit, Mich., who is doing ad
vanced work at Stanford, will address
the club. Miss Anita Allen, '17, will
give a reading. Miss Bertha Knox, '16,
and Miss Helen Greer will contribute
vocal solos.
