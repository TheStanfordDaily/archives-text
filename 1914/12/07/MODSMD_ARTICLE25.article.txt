# On the Quiet
## 
### 
Simple Simon Says:
A joke is no laughing matter.
And come to think of it. it's an ill
wind that blows no skirts.
It was a wise peddler who visited
the Chi Yi house last week. He sold
two dozen pairs of 60 power binocu
lars.
Rumor has it that the Mary Posy
Club recently purchased new blinds
and curtains for their back and D. U.
side windows.
Lightning and a woman's fancy nev
er strike twice in the same place.
