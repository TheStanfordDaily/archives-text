# F.H. Hilton to Help Boost Oregon.
## 
### 
F. H. Hilton, '12, has been appointed
third vice-president of the Oregon Fed
eration of State Societies. This socie
ty has been formed by societies repre
sentative of various states, for the
purpose of advertising Oregon
throughout the East, and to induce
eastern travelers to pass through
there on route to the Exposition.
