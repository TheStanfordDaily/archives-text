# Executive Committee Minutes.
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto Prints the min
utes of the Executive Committee meet
ing of December 9, as a matter of rec
ord:
President Worthy in the chair. Si
monson absent.
Moved and carried to appropriate
sufficient funds for the improvement
of the Faculty diamond and for the ex
tension of sprinkling water to the Aux
iliary diamond.
Moved and carried that a committee
be appointed to get statistics on the
scholastic standing of Freshman ath
letes in this and past years.
Moved and carried that the Execu
tive Committee go on record as favor
ing Intercollegiate Freshman football
and Intercollegiate Second Varsity
crew race.
Meeting adjourned.
Paul McCloskey, Secretary.
