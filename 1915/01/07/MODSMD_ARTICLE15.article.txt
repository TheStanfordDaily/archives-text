# TRY-OUTS
## 
### 
Try-outs for positions on the staff
of The Daily Palo Alto will begin 011
Monday. Candidates are requested to
report at this office at 1:30. The try
outs will extend over a period of from
six to eight weeks. There are a few
vacancies on the staff, which will be
filled from the ranks of those trying
out. Selection will be based on the
ability to find news, the ability to
write news and interest in the work.
Ranking of members on the staff is de
termined by tiie merit system. No ex
perience is necessary, but students
who have the ability to write feature
stories are especially urged to try
out.
