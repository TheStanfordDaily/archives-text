# INTERCOLLEGIATE FENCING FOR MEN IS POSSIBLE
## 
### 
Foil and Mask has received a propo
sition from the fencing society at
California providing for a formal in
tercollegiate fencing match for men
on the morning of the women's inter
collegiate bouts.
Trainer Maloney announces a reg
ular class in fencing at Encina gym
nasium, and urges that all men inter
ested, whether registered for gymna
sium work or not, should enter com
petition for places on the team to
meet California.
