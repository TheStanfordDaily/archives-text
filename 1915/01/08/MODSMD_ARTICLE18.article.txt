# Nineteen Years Ago Today
## 
### 
Registrar Elliott announced that up
to date 840 students were registered.
Charles K. Field, '95, writing under
the pen name of "Carolus Ager,' pub
lished one hundred copies of a volume
called "Four Leafed Clover." The
poems a,re reminiscent of the first four
years of Stanford's existence.
A new number of the Hopkins Lab
oratory publications entitled "The
Fishes of Puget Sound," was pub
lished by David Starr Jordan and Ed
win C. Starks.
No word had yet been received from
Dr. Gilbert, who left early in Decem
ber on a collecting expedition to Pana
ma.
