# Eaton Plans to Produce 'The Messiah"
## 
### 
The men and women singers of
Stanford, whether members of the
student body or of the Faculty, are
cordially invited to unite with the or
ganist to form a chorus to give a per
formance of Handel's "Messiah" at the
close of the present semester. The
first rehearsal will take place Wed
nesday evening, January 13. in the
choir gallery of the Memorial Church,
at 7:35 p. m.
LOUIS H. EATON*. Organist.
