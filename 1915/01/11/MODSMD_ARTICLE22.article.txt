# Bloeser Publishes Entomology Paper.
## 
### 
"Annals of the Entomological Socie
ty of America"for December, 1914,
contains an article by W. Hloeser, M 1.
on the "Life History and Anatomy of
Siphona Plus'ae Coq." The article is
the result, of original research work
on the larvae of Phryganldla Califor
nia gathered from oak trees at Stan
ford University, and is accompanied
by ten plates of especial value, in that
they are published for the first time.
