# Prohibition for Washington, D.C.
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 13.—Tt appears
certain that the Senate will add to the
District of Columbia bill the rider pro
posed by Sheppard, of Texas, which
provides for prohibition in Washing
ton, D. C. A vote on this question is
expected in the Senate this afternoon.
