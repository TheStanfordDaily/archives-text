# PROFESSOR BASSSETT TO READ IN ENCINA SUNDAY
## 
### 
Associate Professo.r L. E. Bassett,
of the English Department, will read
Galsworthy's play, "Justice," in Encina
Hall Sunday evening at 7:30. This is
the second Sunday evening talk of the
semester, given under the auspices of
the Y. M. C. A.
This play deals with the treatment of
criminals by the state in a striking
manner. Professor Bassett will ab
breviate portions of the play so that
the entire reading will not consume
more than an hour.
