# LA
						LIANA TO ENTERTAIN PALO ALTO MEN AT DANCE
## 
### 
An entertainment and dance, to
which all Stanford men living in Palo
Alto are cordially invited, is to be giv
en by La Liana at the Armory next
Friday night at eight o'clock.
Entertainment consisting of a scene
from Olcott's "Little Women," and
readings will be given during inter
missions.
