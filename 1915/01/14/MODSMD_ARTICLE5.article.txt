# ENCINA CLUB
## Elects W.B. Blodgett, '15, President for the Coming Semester to Succeed M.A. Oliver, '15, 
### 
W. R. Blodget, '15, was last evening
elected president of Encina Club for
the remainder of the semeste.r. The
election of Blodget fills the vacancy
created by the failure of M. A. Oliver,
'lij, to return to college. Oliver was
elected last December to succeed E.
C. Behrens, '14. Blodget registers in
the Civil Engineering Department
from Huntington Reach. He won his
circle "S" in basketball and soccer,
and captained last season's winning
eleven.
