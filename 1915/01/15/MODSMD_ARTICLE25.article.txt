# Art Club to Exhibit Work at Fair.
## 
### 
Pottery and three mural paintings
will be the offering of the Stanford
Art Club to the World's Fair. The
pottery, which was made and designed
by classes in the Art Department, is
completed, and the three mural paint
ings, under the direction of Henry
Poor, are undergoing the finishing
touches. They will be sent to San
Francisco within a short time.
