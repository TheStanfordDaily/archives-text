# On the Quiet
## 
### 
No hard feelings for Cliff Ireland.
Xo one ever begrudges him a victory.
Tf he didn't win now and then it might
be a case of Erin Go Broke.
Simple Simon Says:
Feelins of joy and sorrow mingle to
gether in notin that it ain't moren
fou.r months before final exes. And it
doesn't take any weather prophet to
discover that around here for a deuce
of a wh'le things are going to be dry
ernell, all efforts of Jupe Pluvius to
the contrary.
The man who takes a hull carload
of hours ain't deservin of any credit
beyond havin the inginuity of knowin
a good excuse when he sees one. The
macksymum of hours and hard work
are common college expressions that
are swallowed without a gulp by the
folks to home who otherwise would be
a-hollering fer more letters.
Many a parson is said to have the
swell head when really there ain't
nuthin the matter with them exceptin
that they are not used to high society
and the altitude makes them dizzy.
