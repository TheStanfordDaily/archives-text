# Fourteen Sign Up for Chess.
## 
### 
Fourteen men have signed up for
the annual chess tournament. Four
of this number, J, D. Arnott, '15, R. H.
Bickel, '17, W. F. Burbank, '10, and R
L. Mott. '17, are veterans of the last
year's team, which held California to
a draw. Several players from the
1918 class have entered.
A tournament will be held at once
for the purpose of chosing the seven
members who will compete in the in
tercollegiate match in April. The
schedule of games has been posted in
the Encina clubroom, and all interest
ed are welcome to enter the tourna
ment games, as well as to attend the
meetings held every Thursday even
ing.
