# Bert Risling Will Wed.
## 
### 
B. A. Risling, '15, who played at
first five-eighths on the 1912 rand-tie
Varsity, will he married to Miss Ma
rie Harvey next Saturday at St. Pet
er's Cathedral, in San Francisco.
They will make their home in Palo
Alto, where Risling is employed by a
local haberdasher.
