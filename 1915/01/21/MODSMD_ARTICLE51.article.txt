# VICE-PRESIDENT HONORED BY CHEMICAL ENGINEERS
## 
### 
Doctor J. M. Stillman, vice-president
of the University, and professor of
Chemistry, was elected to one of the
vice-presidencies of the American In
stitute of Chemical Engineers, which
held its annual meeting in Philadel
phia, December 2-5. He was also se
lected to head the committee on meet
ings, tho next one of which is to be
held this fall in San Francisco.
As the other members of the commit
tee are eastern engineers, the burden
of the preparation for the gathering
will rest on Doctor Stillman.
