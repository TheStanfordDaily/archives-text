# E.B. NELSON, '09, IS PAINTING FOR EXPOSITION
## 
### 
Ernest Bruce Nelson, '09. is spend
ing several weeks in Palo Alto, where
be is engaged in painting a series of
canvases of the University and the
vicinity for the Panama-Pacific Ex
position. His pictures will be hung in
the Palace of Fine Arts.
Nelson has lately returned from a
successful exhibit of his pictures in
Seattle.
