# CIMPLIFIDE SPELURS NTHUSIASTIK & CRY OUT IN JUBLYNT XTC
## 
### 
A metung of the "Cimplufide
Spelurs uv the Wurld" wuz held re
zently at Kolumbya Kollej in Nu York.
Aloa.r than wun hunderd psrfessurs got
togethur to enjoi themselvs and tauk
togethur becoz thay wur intrested mu
tyualy in cimplufide speling.
The meting was deliteful fur the per
fessurs. All prezent laffed hartily and
slapt eche othur on the bak when wun
uv them, m'stur Jeorj I). Napp, usual
ly spelt George 1). Knapp, got up anu
redd an artike] entyteld, yThe Kursez
uv Inglish Pro;:," which wur kompli
kated speling, such as we nou uz. We
shud suhstitut cimplufide, insted.
It wuz desided to wurk for cimplu
fide speling in all pa.rtz uv the land
from Kolumbya tu Stanford, which
wil eventyually bo spelt Stanfurd or
Stanfhurd, we dont (left owt) no
which. It iz hard tu tol just wen the
muvment wil stryk this kollej.
Anuthur thing the perfesurs enjoid
imensli was a pome bi wun uv them,
a take-opli 011 The Oald Oken Buket.
It went like thiss:
"How dere tu mi hart iz the unro
fynd speling,
The unreformd speling left just as it
gru.
When "ea's' and "f's" wuz awl in thare
plasez,
And a "G" and an "h" sownded oph
the wurd 'thru,"
The familyur old speling, ilogical
speling,
Johnsonian speling mi infansy nu."
