# Untitled Article
## 
### 
Lieutenant A. L. Rockwood. '11, of
the Twelfth infantry. U. S. A., was
married to Miss Katherine Mason, of
Sierra Madre, California, recently. The
couple will reside in Nogalez, Arizona,
where Rockwood is stationed. He was
with the Twelfth Infantry when it was
encamped near the University a year
ago.
