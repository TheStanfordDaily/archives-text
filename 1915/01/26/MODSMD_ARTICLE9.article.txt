# WOMEN WANT BASEBALL GAME WITH CALIFORNIA
## 
### 
Miss Florence C. Burrell, '10, in
structor at Roble gymnasium, has sent
a challenge to the University of Cali
fornia, on behalf of the women's base
ball team, for a game, or a series of in
tercollegiate games, to be played this
semester. She has also arranged for
an interelass series of women's base
ball, the finals of which will be played
April 24.
