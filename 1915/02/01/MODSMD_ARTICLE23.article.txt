# NEW CALIFORNIA JOURNAL SPONSORED BY REFORMERS
## 
### 
A new California student paper, to
be called "Student Opinion," will make
its debut March 1. It proponents. It. L.
Johns, Eugene Tay, and J. E. Stiles,
annnounce that the purpose of the
publication will be to develop a "high
er moral standard" on the campus of
the State University, which, they claim,
is impossible under existing condi
tions, owing to the presence of a cer
tain element in control of university
journalism, which does not favor re
form.
