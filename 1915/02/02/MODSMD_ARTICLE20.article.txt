# Student Council Acts.
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Student Council
it was voted that from and after ten
days from date, a student be placed
under sentence of Indefinite suspen
sion from the University for conduct
unbecoming a member of the Univer
sity. unless substantial reparation be
made in the meantime.
