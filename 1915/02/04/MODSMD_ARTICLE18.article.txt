# Twelve Years Ago Today
## 
### 
The eight men who expect to re
ceive the degree of Bachelor of Laws
in May decided last night to appear in
caps and gowns. The costumes have
already been procured and were worn
at the sitting for the Quad picture to
day.
The Junior farce committee met at
Roble last evening and voted to offer
a prize of $25 for a farce to be pre
sented in the Assembly Hall on March
26th.
Forty-nine two-year-olds from the
Stanford Stock Farm were disposed of
at auction in San Francisco on Thurs
day.
