# REAL COURT WORK TO BE INCLUDED IN LAW COURSE
## 
### 
Stanford law students will soon have
the opportunity to practice their case
before real superior court judges and
in real court rooms. Arrangements
have been made with San Jose court
officials to use the San Jose court
rooms.
Judge Goseby, of San Jose, and
Judge Beasly. of Santa Clara, both of
the superior court of this county,
have agreed to listen to the cases of
students in the San Jose court, and to
comment on the form shown by the
embryo lawyers.
Judge Carruthers, of San Francisco
City and County Superior Court, has
agreed to lecture before the law stu
dents in the near future. Judge Car
ruthers is a former Stanford student.
