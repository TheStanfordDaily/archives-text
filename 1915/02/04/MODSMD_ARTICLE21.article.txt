# DOCTOR EWER SPEAKS TO MEN ON "SEX PROBLEMS"
## 
### 
Dr. M. J. Exner was the speaker be
fore an appreciative audience of men
in the Chapel last night on "Dealing
With Sex Life." In the afternoon Dr.
Exner spoke to men of the gymnasium
classes on "Problems of the Sex
Life." His evening talk was largely
taken up with the solution of these
problems.
