# Y.M.C.A. to Attend Conference.
## 
### 
Thirty members of. Y. W. C. A. 1 aft
this morning to attend the conference
to be held at Mills College on Febru
ary 5, 6, and 7. A varied and exten
sive program has been planned by the
women of Mills College. Miss Eileen
Everett, '14, secretary of the local
Y. W. C. A., is to be one of the main
speakers.
