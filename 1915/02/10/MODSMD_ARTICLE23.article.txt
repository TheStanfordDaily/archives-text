# Euphronia Will Discuss Philippines.
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society will
hold its regular meeting Wednesday
evening at 7:30 in room 12. The sub
ject will be: "Resolved, That the Phil
ippines should be granted immediate
independence." The affirmative will
be upheld by V. Hyatt, '16, and R.
Hirao, '16, while E. Henderson. '15,
and H. L. Hows, '17. will argue the
negative. Visitors are welcome.
