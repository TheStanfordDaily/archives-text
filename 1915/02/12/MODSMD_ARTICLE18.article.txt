# EASTERN VISITORS IMPRESSED
## Professor Robertson, of Chicago, Takes Liking to "Farm." 
### 
Professor David Allan Robertson,
of the English department of the Uni
versity of Chicago, and secretary to
the president of that university, has
arrived on the campus as the guest of
Professor J. \V. Bingham, of the Law
department. Professor Robertson is
deeply impressed with Stanford Uni
versity, and expressed his feelings in
glowing terms. The distinctiveness of
the Quadrangle, the campus, and the
customs of the community were espe
cially noted by the visitor.
Many members of the Stanford fac
ility are numbered among the friends
of Professor Robertson. Me will re
main on the Farm for the week-end.
