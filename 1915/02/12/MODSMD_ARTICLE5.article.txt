# Campus Will Dance to Band Tonight.
## 
### 
The Rand dance is on the program
for tonight down on the old Armory
floor. Eight-thirty is the time set for
the rise of the curtain. Sixteen dances
and two extras will be the course of
events, with a lull in the proceedings
between the ninth and tenth. The
patrons and patronesses include:
Professor and Mrs. E. W. Martin,
Professor and Mrs. C. H. Wing, Pro
fessor and Mrs. S. W. Charles, Profes
sor and Mrs. A. C. Whitaker, Dr.
Frank Angell. and Miss Mabel Angell,
Mr. and Mrs. .1. W. Newsome.
