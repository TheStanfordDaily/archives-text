# Competition for the First Tournament Honors. STANFORD JOURNALISTS TO BE GUESTS OF CLUB
## 
### 
The Stanford newspaper man will
come into his own with the opening of
the Panama-Pacific Exposition. The
San Francisco Press Club has invited
all men acting on Campus publications
to enter into and receive the generous
hospitality which will be provided for
them at their headquarters in the
Press building. Visitors will be wel
comed by members of the Exposition
Committee.
