# FIFTY DOLLARS OFFERED FOR TEMPERANCE ESSAY
## 
### 
The annual fifty-dollar prize has
again been offered by the Palo Alto
Women's Christian Temperance Union
for the best temperance essay written
by any Stanford student. In order
that students may utilize vacation
time for the preparation of these es
says, the final date of their acceptance
has been set for July 1. Competitors
are limited to a minimum of 2000,
and a maximum of 3000 words.
A. F. C'oyle, 'l. r >, winner of last
year's contest, not only secured the
local prize of fifty dollars, but was
also victorious in the state competi
tion. and tied for national honors, his
essay netting him a total of one hun
dred and fifty dollars in awards.
