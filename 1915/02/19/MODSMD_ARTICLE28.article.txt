# Carrie Jacobs-Bond Will Entertain.
## 
### 
Carrie Jacobs-Bond, the well-known
writer of "human sympathy" songs, is
to visit Stanford for the second time;
and will appear in concert on the af
ternoon of March 14, at C'astilleja
school. She will leave her country
home at "Grossmont' a week before her
concert here, and will be a guest of the
Gamma Phi Beta sorority for that time.
