# Carruth Addresses Oakland Audience.
## 
### 
Professor W. H. Carruth, head of
the English department, lectured on
"Great Poets and Great Poems" yes
terday evening in the Plymouth Con
gregational church, at Oakland. He
closed his talk with a sketch of the
life of Edward Rowland Sills, with
whom he was personally acquainted,
and the reading of several of his more
noted works.
