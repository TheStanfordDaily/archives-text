# Miss Bentley Will Speak to Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Miss Ma,ry Bentley, the Field Student
Secretary of the Pacific Coast, will
speak at the Y. W. C. A. meeting next
Tuesday at 4:30 in the Reception Room
of the Club House.
Hereafter the regular meetings will
be held in the Club House, instead of
the Vestry, which has been used tem
porarily.
