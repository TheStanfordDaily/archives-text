# English Club and lied Scarf to Meet
## 
### 
The English Club will hold a joint
meeting with Red Scarf Wednesday ev
ening at 8 o'clock, at the Delta Gam
ma house. Members of Red Scarf will
entertain. I). Shepardson, '17, will
play the violin, Miss Mabel Schell
bach, '16, the piano, and Miss Wheeler
will sing.
