# Y. M. C. A. to Elect Officers.
## 
### 
Announcement that the Y. M. C. A.
would elect officers early in March
was made Sunday afternoon, when the
Association met in its room on the
Quad. Any members desirous of mak
ing nominations for the offices uf
president, vice-president, secretary or
treasurer, are requested to place their
nominations in the hands of the presi
dent or general secretary with in the
next few days.
