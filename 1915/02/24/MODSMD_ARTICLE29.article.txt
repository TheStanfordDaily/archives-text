# Y. W. C. A. WILL CONTEST IN EXPOSITION EXHIBITS
## 
### 
The Y. W. C'. A. has decided to take
an active part in the contests and ex
hibits of art and letters, which will be
the center of attraction in the Chris
tian Association building on the Expo
sition grounds.
Miss Helen Thoburn, '05, editorial
secretary of the National Y. W. C. A.
Board, will be one of the judges in
conjunction with Trumbull White, edi
tor of Everybody's Magazine, and Rob
ert S. Yard, editor of the Century Mag
azine, who will finally pass upon the
best manuscripts in the literary con
tests.
