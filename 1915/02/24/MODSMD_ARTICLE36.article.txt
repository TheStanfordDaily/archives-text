# Club House Directors Plan Tea.
## 
### 
The Board of Directors of the Wo
men's Club House is to entertain the
women of the University at a tea to be
held in the Club House Friday after
noon, February 26. The Hoard is com
posed of Mrs. P. J. Treat, president;
Mrs. E. W. Allan, vice-president; Miss
C. S. Stoltenberg, secretary-treasurer;
student representatives, Miss Joyce
Gardiner, '15, Miss Erma Taylor, '16,
and Miss Nellita Choate, '17.
