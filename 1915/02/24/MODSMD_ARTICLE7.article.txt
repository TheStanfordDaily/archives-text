# MRS. STARKS TO EXHIBIT WATER COLORS AND OILS
## 
### 
Mrs. Chloe L. Starks, of the Art de
partment, will hold an exhibit of water
colors and oil paintings in the early
part of March. The exhibit numbers
about one hundred pictures, and in
cludes sketches from England, France,
Holland, and Italy, which were done
last year when Mrs. Starks was
abroad, also paintings of Palo Alto,
Pacific Grove, and Monterey scenes.
The exhibition will be held in the
Old Studio, under the auspices of the
Stanford Art Club.
