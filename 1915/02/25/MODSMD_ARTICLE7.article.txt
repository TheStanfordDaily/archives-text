# Train Kills Toy Quong, the Fat Cook.
## 
### 
That Toy Quong, the 200-pound Chi
naman killed by a fast Southern Pa
cific train near the bridge at the Palo
Alto tree Tuesday afternoon was a
familiar figure on the campus has
been learned by those interested in
the case. The portly chef was known
as "the fat Chinese cook," it is said,
and has been a resident of Stanford
and May field for the past 25 years.
Lately, however, he has been neglect
ing his pots and pans for the lottery,
and it was while on a mission of this
kind that he was overtaken by the
4:45 flyer.
