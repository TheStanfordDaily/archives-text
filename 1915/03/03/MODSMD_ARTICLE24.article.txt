# JUNIOR PROM PROGRAMS INCLUDE FOURTEEN DANCES
## 
### 
The dance programs for the Junior
Prom on March 2C> will contain four
teen dances, as the result of the de
cision of the Prom Committee, at its
meeting last evening. The supper
dance will be off the program. Nine
one-steps, three waltzes, and two fox
trots are to lie the steps danced.
The affair will be held in Encina
Hall. An imported orchestra, com
posed of a piano, drums, banjo, trom
bone, and accordion, promises unusual
ly attractive music.
