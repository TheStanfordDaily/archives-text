# Nestoria to Debate Immigration Bill
## 
### 
Nestoria will debate the question,
"Resolved, that President Wilson was
justified in vetoing the recent immi
gration bill, providing for a literacy
test," this evening at 7:30, in room 228
of the History building. This was the
topic for the meeting of the society
which was postponed on account of the
Fuller sisters' concert. Tuesday even
ing is the regular meeting time of the
society.
H. A. Frye, '17, and M. B. Hanna, 'IG,
will constitute the affirmative. Oppo
sition will be offered by .T. D. Adams,
'15. and M. E. Tedford, '17, negative.
