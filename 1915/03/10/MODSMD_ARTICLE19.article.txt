# Twenty Years Ago Today
## 
### 
Professor M. E. Anderson has just
arranged with Doxey, of San Francis
co, for the publication of a book of
sonnets. The book will contain fifty
or sixty of the best sonnets in the
English language and ten translations
from sonnets in other languages. Be
sides these there will be several orig
inal works never before published.
Professor Anderson also has another
book now in the hands of the publish
er which will appear soon. It is en
titled "Some Representative Poets of
the Nineteenth Century."
