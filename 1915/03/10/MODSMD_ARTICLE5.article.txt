# JUICY FLEET-FOOTED AND QUICK-WITTED PIG WANTED
## 
### 
A tip to the wise is unnecessary. The
tip is this, if you should happen '.o
see a member of Quadrangle Club giv
ing you a comprehensive once over,
don't expand your manly chest, nor
st,retch your hat band. There's no
cause for over-inflation. Far be it
from such. As a matter of fact your
actions should be quite to the contrary,
i.e., should resemble the shrinking
process. When you see Quadrangle
Club a-eyeing you keenly, as it were,
t's your cue to contract into the smal
lest possible space, and sneak home
ward via the back alley route. All of
which means simply this:
Quadrangle Club is minus one vital
accessory of its organization, it's out
one pig, which pig is to play the lead
ing role in the Irish Marathon festivi
ties. Each member of the club has
been invested with the powers of Spe
cial Commissioner to Search the High
and Byways of the Peninsula for one
Juicy, Greasy, and Fleet-Footed. Quick
witted Pig. The hunt has been fast
and furious, but in vain. If there is
any pig answering to the above de
scription in these parts, he is keeping
cove,r. In the meanwhile, remember
Quadrangle Club are scouting about
singing "Pig o' My Heart" with much
gusto.
