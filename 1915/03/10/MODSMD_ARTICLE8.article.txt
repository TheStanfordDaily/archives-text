# MARKET IN CIRCLE WILL FEATURE UNIQUE EXHIBITS
## 
### 
Plans for the Western European
Market, to be held in the Palo Alto
Circle Saturday are progressing with
enthusiasm. Those in charge of booths
report that their respective divisions
will be in readiness for the opening of
the market Saturday morning.
Mrs. David Starr Jordan will have
charge of the pottery booth. Co-oper
ating with her to make this section
one of the most interesting exhibits of
the ma.rket is Mr. Solon, who has
charge of a large pottery works near
Santa Barbara, where some of the most
artistic and beautiful vases manufac
tured in the West are perfected. A
wheel and clay will be used in the
demonstration.
