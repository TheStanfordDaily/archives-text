# Eleven Years Ago Today
## 
### 
After six years of almost, continuous
work the last keystone in the arch of
the arcade connecting the Mining and
Civil Engineering Building was laid
with simple but impressive ceremon
ies. An address was made by Dr.
Jordan and short talks were given by
Charles K. Field, '95, E. O. Rrua.. '04.
president of the Senior class, and I.
Russell, '04. The cornerstone was
laid by E. O. Brua and I. Russell for
the Senior class.
