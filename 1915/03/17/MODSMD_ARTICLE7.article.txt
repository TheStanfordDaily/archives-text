##

ture on "Hymns and Hymn-Writers"
on Thursday evening of this week, at.
7:30 p. m. at the Episcopal church,
Palo Alto. The subject will he treated
from the historical point of view,
many of the familiar modern hymns
being traced to Cireek and Latin orig
inals.
