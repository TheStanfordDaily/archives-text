# ONE HUNDRED FIFTY
## Students Are Included On Membership Roll of Stanford Union.

A survey of the membership list of
The Stanford Union shows nearly one
hundred and fifty students members of
the club. This includes life members,
partially paid life members, and the
students who have paid the $1.50 dues
for this semester. It is impossible to
give a complete list of the alumni and
Faculty members at this time, but
they will swell the membership con
siderably.
The life members and those who
have paid in a part of the fifty dollars
necessary for belonging to The Union
for life are placed on the same basis
in the use of the building as those
who have paid up their dues for this
semester. Those who have paid the
semester fee will be allowed to count
it. toward a life membership fee. if
they determine later to become life
members.

hundred and fifty students members of
the club. This includes life members,
partially paid life members, and the
students who have paid the $1.50 dues
for this semester. It is impossible to
give a complete list of the alumni and
Faculty members at this time, but
they will swell the membership con
siderably.

have paid in a part of the fifty dollars
necessary for belonging to The Union
for life are placed on the same basis
in the use of the building as those
who have paid up their dues for this
semester. Those who have paid the
semester fee will be allowed to count
it. toward a life membership fee. if
they determine later to become life
members.
