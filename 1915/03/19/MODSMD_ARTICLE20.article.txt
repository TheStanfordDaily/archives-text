# Organ Recitals Suspended
## 
### 
Daily afternoon organ recitals in
the Memorial Church have been sus
pended until April 5, to give Organist
Eaton an opportunity to complete
plans for his recitals to be given at
the San Francisco Exposition. Pro
grams and dates for those recitals, of
which there will be five, are to be an
nounced in a few days.
