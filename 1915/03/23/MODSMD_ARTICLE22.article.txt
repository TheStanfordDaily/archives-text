# PROFESSOR GREEN NAMED STATE NORMAL TRUSTEE
## 
### 
The appointment by Governor John
son of Professor R. L. Green, of the
Mathematics department, as one of
the trustees of the State No.rmal
School, at San Jose, was unanimously
confirmed by the upper house of the
state legislature Friday. The others
named were C. F. Crothers, of San
Jose, and W. F. Blake, of Gilroy.
