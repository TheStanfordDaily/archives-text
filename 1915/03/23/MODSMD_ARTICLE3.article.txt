# NAMES OF ELIGIBLE OARSMEN POSTED
## Dr. Angell Sends List of Sixteen Varsity and Thirteen Freshmen to California.

in the crew race with California and
Washington has been submitted to the
California Faculty Committee on Ath

maining on the squad are eligible to
enter the .race.
