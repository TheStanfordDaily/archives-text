It starts tonight when the lid is
blown off at the lake by a unique
display of fireworks and an especially
prepared musical program. The re
mainder of the week will be replete
with numerous events, one following
on top of the other. The Junior
Week program is simply chuck full
and overflowing. For this reason it
should not necessarily be expected to
queen to everything. It takes the
women of the University all vacation
to recuperate usually, if they attend
