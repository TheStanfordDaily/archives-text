# NORTHERN INTERSCHOLASTIC TO MEET HERE TOMORROW
## California Interscholastic Meet. 
### 
The North Coast section of the
California interscholastic Track meet
will he held tomorrow at one o'clock
on the Stanford track. One hundred
athletes are entered in the meet from
fourteen preparatory schools in this
section. The winners of first, second
and third places in this contest be
come eligible to enter the State Cham
pionship meet to he held at Fresno
April 3.
