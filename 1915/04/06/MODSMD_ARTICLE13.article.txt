# Reading at Castilleja.
## 
### 
Miss Katherine Jewell Everts, who
is giving a course in vocal expression
at the University of California, will
read "The War Brides," by Marion
Craig Wentworth, next Sunday after
noon at 3:30 at Castilleja gymnasium.
English students may purchase tick
ets at twenty-five cents at the Stan
ford Bookstore. The proceeds of the
afternoon are for the Belgian Relief
Committee, of which Mrs. Vernon L.
Kellogg is chairman.
