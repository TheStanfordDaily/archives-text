# ORATORIO ORCHESTRA IS PROCURED BY ORGANIST
## 
### 
A thirty piece orchestra, composed
of the leading musicians of San Fran
cisco, has been secured by Organist
Eaton to accompany the choir in the
production of Handel's Messiah, to be
given in the Memorial Church on May
second. Mr. Eaton will lead both the
orchestra and choir and his place at
the organ will be occupied by' Mr.
Wilbur McCall, of the College of the
Pacific.
The instruments consist of five vio
lins, two violas, two cellos, two bas
ses, two flutes, two oboi, two clari
nets, two bassoons, two French horns,
two trumpets, and one pair of tym
pani.
