# Henry P. Cheda Dies.
## 
### 
Hen.ry P. Cheda, '10, died at his
home near San Luis Obispo Wednes
day. Cheda was at Stanford from
190G, when he was president of his
Freshman class, until he received his
degree in Geology and Mining after
an interval out of college, in 1912.
Ho took active part in athletics, play
ing on his class teams. His brother,
(J. E. Cheda, '10, is now in Nicaragua,
engaged in mining enterprises.
