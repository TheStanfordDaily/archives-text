# Leslie Blanchard Talks to Y. W. C. A.
## 
### 
Miss Leslie Hlanchard, '12, gave an
informal talk before the Y. W. C. A.
members yesterday. Miss Hlanchard
was former secretary of the local or
ganization and is now national secre
tary of state universities. Her talk con
sisted o4' experiences which occurred
during her travels since she left the
dent government for the Y. W. C. A.
Miss Esther Liversidge, '17, sang the
solo, ".lust for To-I)ay," being accom
panied by Miss Louise Curtner, 'lfi.
Miss Helen Pratt, '1">, has been elect
ed chairman of the committee on Stu
dent government far the Y. W. C. A.
Conference at Asilomar.
