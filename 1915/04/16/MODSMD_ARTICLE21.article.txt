# Ridgway's Assist Stanford Clinic.
## 
### 
Through the generosity of Ridgway's
Incorporated, the Neurology Clinic of
Stanford Medical School will be the
beneficiary of the entire receipts of
the Ridgway Tea Pavilion at the Pal
ace of Food Products, Avenue C and
Fourth Street, for the days of April
16 and 17. All Stanford visitors at
the Exposition are asked to assist with
their patronage.
