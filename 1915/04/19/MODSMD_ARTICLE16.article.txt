# "Niezsche" Topic of Lecture Tonight.
## 
### 
The Department of Philosophy an
nounces a lecture on "Nietzsche, the
Individualist and Aristocrat," to he
given in the Chapel this evening at
8 o'clock, by Dr. Stanton Coit, Presi
dent of the West London Ethical So
ciety, Bayswater. Dr. Coit was a stu
dent of Columbia and Amherst. Since
1888 he has resided in London, and is
the author of numerous writings on
Religion and Ethics, his most recent
considerable work being "The Soul of.
America."
