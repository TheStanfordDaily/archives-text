###

chemistry honor fraternity, has elect

president, G. J. Ziser, '15; vice-presi
dent. C. M. Kellogg, 'Ifi; secretary, F.
J. Anderson, 'Ifi; treasurer, N. W.
Rakestraw, '16; alumni secretary, N.
P. Moore, '14; counselor, 11. M. Elsey,
'14.
