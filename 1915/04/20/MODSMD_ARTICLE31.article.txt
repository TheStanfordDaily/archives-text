# UNION AND CLUB HOUSE TO BE CARNIVAL SCENE
## 
### 
The Stanford Union and the Club
House will furnish the setting for the
Senior Carnival this year. The
Union will be reserved for vaudeville
shows, while a nickel dance will
reign supreme at the Club House. The
space between the two buildings is re
served for the concessions-. Applica
tions for space should be made im
mediately to 1.. C. Stevens, Box 00,
Campus.
