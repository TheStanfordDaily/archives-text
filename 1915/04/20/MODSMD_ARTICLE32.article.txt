# Euphronia Debates City Manager Plan
## 
### 
A. F. Coyle, '15, affirmative, and A.
W. Clark, '15, negative, will argue the
question, "Resolved, that the City
Manager Plan of Municipal Govern
ment is preferable to the Commission
Plan." before Euphronia Literary So
ciety tomorrow evening. The public
is invited to attend the debate, which
will take place in room 12 of the Law
department.
