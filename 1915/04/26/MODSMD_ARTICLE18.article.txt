# REVEREND DAY DELIVERS MEMORIAL CHURCH SERMON
## 
### 
The Reverend Horace Day, of the
First Congregational Church ot' Los
Angeles, preached in the Memorial
Church yesterday, on the subject of
"Pan-Ame,ricanism," taking as his
text "And I will make of thee a great
nation and I will bless thee and make
thy name great." He discussed the
topics of national greatness and
world love.
"National greatness is of a different
nature from personal greatness. The
Deepest Philosopher exercised His
authority among the Oentiles through
personal greatness.
