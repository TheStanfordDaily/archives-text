# Nestoria To Elect Officers.
## 
### 
Nestorians will close activities for
the present year with their annual
banquet, which will take place at six
o'clock Tuesday evening in Madrono
Hall. At that time officers fo.r next
semester will be elected. J. D. Adams,
president of the literary society, will
serve as toastniaster. An entertain
ing program of speeches and special
numbers has been arranged.
