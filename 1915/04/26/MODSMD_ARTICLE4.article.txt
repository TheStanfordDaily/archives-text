# Women to Mass to Amend Conference Constitution
## 
### 
A mass meeting for all the women
of the University will be held at the
Club House on Tuesday, April 27. The
purpose of the gathering is twofold;
first, to make certain amendments to
the constitution of the Women's Con
ference which have long been neces
sary : and, secondly, to form a wo
men's peace movement.
