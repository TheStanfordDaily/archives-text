# NEW SWIMMING TANK
## Will Be Located to the East of New Gymnasium. 
### 
Mr. Vanderlynn Stow of the Board
of Trustees and the Student body com
mdttee have agreed to the location of
the Swimming tank to the east side of
the new Gymnasium near the road that
runs between Encina and the football
bleachers.
Work will be started on the under
taking soon after the semester closes,
and will undoubtedly be completed for
the fall opening.
Adequate heating apparatus will be
installed to bring the water to a pro
per temperature, while a filtering
plant will percolate the Searsville
water.
