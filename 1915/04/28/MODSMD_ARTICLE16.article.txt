# A 						CLEAR SLATE
## 
### 
It is not an unusual circumstance
for many of the students to owe nu
merous and sundry bills at the close
of the college year. Some of these
bills may be large and some may be
small. To those who have extended
your credit it. is only just, whether
they be friends or merchants, that
you make a clean slate before you
leave the University. It is well
enough to say that you will send your
creditor a check during the summer
when you earn some money, but it is
another thing to remember your re
solve. Plan to pay up all bills when
the monthly check is received on May
1, and start the vacation with a clean
slate. Hack to Poughkeepsie.
