# Dr. Beasley Leaves for Servia.
## 
### 
Dr. Shadworth O. Beasley, Assist
ant in the Medical School, has been
appointed Assistant Surgeon in the
American Red Cross service and left
for Belgrade, Serbia, last Saturday.
Dr. Beasley has been selected by the
Medical School to represent it, follow
ing a request from the American Red
Cross. He is admirably fitted for this
work, having been a Major and Sur
geon in the Philippines. He will be as
signed to one of the American Red
Cross military hospitals in Belgrade.
He will not be connected with the Ty
phus Commission of which Professor
Zinsser is a member.
