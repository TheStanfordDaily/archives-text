# Savannah Ensemble Club Entertains.
## 
### 
The Savannah Ensemble Club, com
posed of thirty local musicians, enter
tained in the Assembly Hall yesterday
evening. The program consisted of
classical and modern selections on
stringed instruments.
This is the second concert the Sa
vannah Club has appeared in this
season.
