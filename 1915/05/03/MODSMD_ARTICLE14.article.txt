# Swimming Meet a Stanford Victory.
## 
### 
Stanford scored another victory in
' aquatics when the San Jose Y. M. C.
A. swimming team was conquered last
Saturday evening. The following men
competed in the meet: A. A. Gold
smith, '16, W. F. Rechorer. '17, A. A.
Burnand, '17, E. E. Davidson, '17, L.
E. Pedley. '18, and \T. D. Flowers, '18.
