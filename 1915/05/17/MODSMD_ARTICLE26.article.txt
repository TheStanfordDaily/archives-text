# Band Leaves for South Today.
## 
### 
Stanford's Band boards the steam
ship President this afternoon in San
Francisco for the tour to Southern
California, during which the organiza
tion will give twelve concerts in as
many different localities. Final ar
rangements for the trip have been
made by Manager W. M. Bradford, '15,
and Professor E. Whitney Martin, di
rector. has decided on the program,
which will consist of ten numbers, in
cluding three Stanford songs. No
stunt men will lie taken with the Band
firs year, owing to the stringency in
funds which the organization has
faced.
