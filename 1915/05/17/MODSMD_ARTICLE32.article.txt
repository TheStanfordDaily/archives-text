# Sequoia Staff Elects.
## 
### 
The following have been elected to
the Sequoia staff: Miss Dorothy Hume,
'17, Miss Dare Stark, '17, R. Donald
son, '17, L. Warren, '17, H. Levy, '16,
and M. Hagen, '15. Levy took first
prize and Dorothy Hume second in the
recent Sequoia short story contest.
Hagen was editor of the Chaparral
this year, and Levy is edito.r for next
year.
