# Staff Tryouts
## 
### 
Freshman tryouts for the staff of
The Daily Palo Alto will commence
Friday afternoon at 2 o'clock. Ap
pointments will be made at the end of
six weeks in competitive work. The
success of candidates will be based
on intelligence, energy, and ability to
secure and write news accurately.
The Daily will welcome men and
women of the entering or other classes
who wish to enroll for the tryouts.
