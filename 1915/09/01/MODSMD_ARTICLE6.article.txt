# STAG RECEPTION SET FOR FRIDAY
## 
### 
Physical Director H. R. Stolz, In
structor W. H. Maloney, Student Ad
visor S. S. Simonson, and Y. M. C. A.
Secretary A. B. Watson, will talk to
the men of the University at the Y. M.

lin, '13. late of the Orpheum circuit,
