# NEW YORK DIVINE TO SPEAK
## Dr. Merrill to Conduct Memorial Church Services. 
### 
Dr. William Pierson Merrill, pas
tor of the 8,l ick Presbyterian church,
on Fifth avenue, New York City, will
occupy the pulpit of the Memorial
church during the Sunday morning
services of the month of September.
In addition to preaching at the 11

of September, Dr. Merrill will deliver
addresses at Encina Hall, The Stan
ford Union, the Women's Club House,

Dr. Merrill is now at Santa Bar
bara. He will arrive on the campus
