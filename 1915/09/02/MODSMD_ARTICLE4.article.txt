# ENROLLMENT IS LARGEST IN HISTORY OF STANFORD
## 
### 
With a total of 1865 students the
Registrar has practically completed
the largest registration ever experi
enced in the history of Stanford. This
was the total at the close of the three
days given for enrollment, but will no
doubt be slightly increased by strag
gling registration during the rest of
the week.
This year's quota of old students is
1244, being an increase of 63 over last
year, when 1181 old students .regis
tered. An increase of 53 has been
made in the number of new students,
there being 621 enrolled thus far.
while last year at this time there
were but 568. The total increase
is 116.
