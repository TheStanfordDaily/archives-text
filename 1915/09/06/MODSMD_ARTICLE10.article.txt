# STANFORD TRACK MEN WILL RUN IN EXPOSITION MEET
## 
### 
Stanford will enter a team in the
track meet to be held at the Exposi
tion Saturday, September 25, under
the branch of the Amateur Athletic
Association. The exact personnel of
the Cardinal squad has not been de
cided. However, it was given out this
morning that the following Stanford
men will begin training for the meet:
Murray, Norton, House, dashes and
hurdles; Chapman, distances; Krohn,
pole vault; Sisson, jumps; Caughey
and Bihlman, weights. This squad
will probably be added to before Sep
tember 18, when final entries must be
made.
