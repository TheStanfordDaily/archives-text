# Freshmen Gather at Stanford Union
## 
### 
The Stanford Union was opened to
the freshmen Friday evening for a
get-together and smoker. R. E. Need
ham, '16, presided and introduced the
various speakers.
H. W. Maloney gave a talk on ath
letics and their value to the under
graduate. S. S. Simonson, '15, spoke
on the value of The Daily Palo Alto
to the freshman. J. B. Watson, the
new head of the Y. M. C. A. concluded
the speaking by emphasizing the fact
that he was the "freshman's friend".
