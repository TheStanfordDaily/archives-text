# Roble Club Holds Meeting
## 
### 
The rules and regulations for the
coming semester were discussed at
length in the first regular meeting of
Roble Club held in Roble Hall last
night. Miss Hazel Fisher was in the
president's chair and a majority of the
sixty new girls were present. Miss
S. Hyatt, '06, an alumnus of the club,
gave a very pleasant talk.
