# Student Body Tickets Sell Well
## 
### 
Graduate Manager B. C. Behrens,
'14, announces that the Student Body
tickets are selling as well as usual al
though the exact number disposed of
cannot be ascertained as yet owing
to the fact that the tickets are on sale
at many different places and have not
been turned in to the office. The fig
ures will be available in a few days,
however, and will be a substantial
indication of the real attitude of Stan
ford toward the break with California.
All students who have not secured
tickets are urged to do so at once.
