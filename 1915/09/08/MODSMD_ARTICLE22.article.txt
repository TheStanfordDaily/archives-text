# Shubert Tryouts Next Tuesday
## 
### 
Tryouts for Schubert Club will be
held Tuesday afternoon, September 14,
from 3 to 4:30 in Professor L. H.
Eaton's office, room 84. All new girls
and freshmen are urged to try for
1-laces. The officers of the club will
act as judges. The members of Schu
bert practice every Tuesday evening,
and a number of engagements are
being arranged for the year by the
manager, Miss Doris Overman.
