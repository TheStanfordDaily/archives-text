# History Professors to Lecture
## 
### 
In connection with the Conference
which is being held this week on
"War, Peace, and International Rela
tions", Assistant Professor P. A. Mar
tin and Assistant Professor E. R.
Vann, of the History department, will
conduct a lecture discussing Pan-
American Relations and the Monroe
Doctrine. The meeting will be held
at 7:30 p. m. Thursday, in room 4GO
of the Zoology department.
