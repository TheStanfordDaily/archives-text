# EIGHTEENERS ELECT NEW OFFICERS FOR SEMESTER
## 
### 
Officers for the first semester were
selected by the class of '18 at 1:15
today. Those elected were: N. R.
Allen, president: Miss Katherine
Ames, vice-president; Miss nob
Shelton, secretary, and E. D. Kneass,
treasruer.
Announcement was made that the
sophomore posters can be purchased
at the bookstore for ten cents.
The date for sophomore play try
outs was set for September 22.
