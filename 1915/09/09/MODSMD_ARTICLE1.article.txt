the Men's Conference will be held to
morrow afternoon In the department
rooms from 1:30 to 2:30, followed by
the student body election on the Quad
the following Thursday, September 16.
Representation in the conference is
based on the number of men registered
in the various departments. Students
who will have over fifty units of work
in the next "bawl out" are eligible to
vote or to be elected. One represen
tative to every forty men is the basis
of determining membership. In order
to make the election valid a quorum,
one-third of the eligible voters, must
vote.
