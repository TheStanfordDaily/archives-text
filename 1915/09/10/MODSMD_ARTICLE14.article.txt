# Sociological Society Elects Officers
## 
### 
The Sociological Society elected the
following officers for the ensuing
semester at the first meeting: Presi
dent, N'ellita Choate, '17; secretary,
M. B. Manna, '18, and treasurer, Ed
ward Goldlnirg, '17. It was decided
to have outside speakers as often as
possible. The speaker for the next
meeting has not yet been decided on.
