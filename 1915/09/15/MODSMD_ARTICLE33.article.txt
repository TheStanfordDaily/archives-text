# Physics Department Welcomes Ulrey
## 
### 
The Physics department welcomes
Professor Dayton Ulrey who has re
cently come to take charge of the
physics graduate assistanceship. Pro
fessor Ulrey took his 15. A. degree at
the University of Indiana in 1911, and
teaching there in 1912 took the Mas
ters degree. During the next three
years he was instructor in physics at
the University of Pittsburg.
