# Black Wins in Peanut League
## 
### 
Only one game was played in the
peanut league yesterday afternoon.
The Blacks defeated the Whites by
a 5 to 4 score in one of the best played
games this season. Heavy hitting fea
tured the five innings of play, Striver,
'19, of the Whites, leading with a two
and three-bagger. Landers, '17, and
Stevens, '17, have been taking charge
of the teams and both state that a
number of likely looking players are
out. Games are being played on Mon
days, Tuesdays' and Thursdays on the
varsity and auxiliary diamonds.
