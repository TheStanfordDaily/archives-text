# Members of Faculty on Program of National Engineering Organs Which Meet Together Tomorrow
## 
### 
The four national engineering soci
eties will assemble in convention to
morrow morning at San Francisco.
These bodies will hold saparate con
ventions for two davs before uniting
in the International Engineering
Congress next week.
The American Institute of Mining
Engineers will meet at the Bellevue
Hotel to discuss all subjects pertain
ing to geology and mining. The Me
chanical Engineers will have as their
headquarters the (Tift Hotel, while
the Electrical and Civil engineering
societies will hold forth at the St
Francis.
A number of Stanford professors
are to deliver addresses and serve on
the various committees, so it is ex
pected that the majority of the faculty
and upperclassmen will attend these
meetings.
