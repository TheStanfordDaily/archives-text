# Pence Congress to Convene October 1
## 
### 
The International Peace Congress
will meet in San Francisco and Berke
ley October 10, 11, 12, and 13. The
purpose of the Peace Congress is to
bring together, as far as possible, rep
resentative leaders from all nations
and from all organizations that favor
world peace, in order to confer on
the most practical plans for putting
into co-operation those forces and
agencies that will lead to the aboli
tion of war.
