# E.H. Lockwood Reaches Honolulu
## 
### 
E. H. Lockwood, formerly secretary
of the Y. M. C. A., has reached Hono
lulu and will in a few days be at his
destination in China. According to a
letter received by Secretary J. B. Wat
son, Lockwood will go first to Shang
hai and from there will be assigned a
port at which to take a year of lan
guage study before taking charge of
the missions of a Chinese city. Many
inquiries have been made by students
concerning Mr. Lockwood and those
who care to write to him can reach
him in care of the Y. M. C. A.
Shanghai.
