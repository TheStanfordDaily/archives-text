# No Entry List for Water Meet
## 
### 
The interclass swimming meet
planned for Saturday of next week
took on a new aspect whan Coach
Brandsten announced today that all
men signed up for the water sport are
expected to enter, it was planned to
open entry lists. The change, how
ever, will enable inexperienced swim
mers to learn the value of real compe
tition, besides giving Brandsten a
bird's-eye view of the raw material
he has to work with.
