# FIFTEEN DEBATERS IN PRELIMINARY TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Fifteen men are competing in the
preliminary intercollegiate debating
tryouts this afternoon in the Chapel.
The six men who show the greatest
ability will compete in the finals to
morrow afternoon against seven de
baters who have had experience on
previous California and tri-state
teams. From this number will be
chosen the varsity and second varsity
trios, which will meet the University
of California and the University of
Southern California, respectively, on
the evening of Friday, November 12.
