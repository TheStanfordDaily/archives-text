# Church Pulpit Filled by Dr. Merrill
## 
### 
Dr. William Pierson Merrill of New
York will fill the pulpit in the Memo
rial church Sunday. Miss Marie De
Forest, '16, and Miss Esther Liver
sidge, '17, will be the soloists. Miss
Liversidge singing "Love Divine."
The anthem will be Nevin's "From
Thy Love as a Father."
The regular ornan recital will be
held at four o'clock. The morning
service will start at eleven o'clock.
The afternoon organ program fol
lows:
Guil mailt—Cantilena.
Schubert—Ave Maria.
H.indel—Largo.
Guilmant —March. Theme by Handel.
