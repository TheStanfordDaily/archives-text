# Fraternity Tennis Begins Friday
## 
### 
Tennis Captain J. S. Hutchinson, '16,
announces the inter-fraternity tennis
tournament for October 1, 2, and 3.
Each fraternity is expected to enter a
singles and a doubles team. A fee of
50 cents will be charged each player,
this being paid before the qualifying
round, Friday. Players are requested
to sign up with either J. S. Hutchin
son, *16, H. L. Halm, '16, Van Dyke
Johns, '18, or Allen Barber, '18, by
Wednesday noon. The committee will
endeavor to have two cups up.
