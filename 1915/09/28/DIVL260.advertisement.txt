Stop Thief
The play that meets all requirements
In addition to the undoubted quality of "Stop Tliief" as
as a source of amusement, the piece offers every other in
ducement to those who are undecided.
"Stop Thief is without the usual tiresome waits and
delays between acts so frequently complained of. The
action takes place in the same room of the Carr residence,
thus doing away with time-eating scenery shifting. Each
act takes up the story exactly where the other left off,
keeping the interest stimulated and leaving no "twenty
years after" gap to the imagination. The story is about
the experiences of only two hours of one afternoon, which
means that the time taken to play "Stop Thief" is exactly
tlie time sequence of the story itself.
