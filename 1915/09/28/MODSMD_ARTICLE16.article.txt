# WAR PREPAREDNESS TOPIC OF ADDRESS
## 
### 
A number of the commissioned and
non-commissioned officers of the Palo
Alto military company will be present
at the Union this evening when J. C.
Ray, '07, will speak on "student's and
businessmen's training camps, and
preparedness as insurance against
war." They will be pleased to give
any information in relation to their
service and will receive applications
of those students who may desire to
receive military training during the
college year.
The Palo Alto company is now con
structing a rifle range near the estate.
Students interested in rifle shooting
are invited to communicate with the
company or call at the armory on Mon
day or Wpdnesday evenings.
