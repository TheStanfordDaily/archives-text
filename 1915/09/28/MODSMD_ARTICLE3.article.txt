# REGENT TOURNAMENT ENDS
## C.E. Wolford Will Hold Cup for One Year 
### 
C. E. Wolford, '18, defeated V. D.
Johns, '18. in a closely contested match
in the finals of the Regent cup tourna
ment yesterday. This cup, according
to the provision of the donor, is to he
held one year by the winner. K. B.
IThls, '16, winner of the cup last year
was not present as defender. After
losing the first set 0-6. Wolford came
hack and won the two following sets,
