# BLEACHER VOTE TOMORROW
## Executive Committee to Elect Assistant Yell Leaders After Wednesday Tryouts 
### 
Candidates for the position of as
sistant yell leader will be given a final
chance to wave their arms at the foot
ball game with St. Ignatius tomorrow
afternoon. The Executive Committee
will, with the assistance of the bleach
er vote, choose the two men who will
assist John H. Goodman, '16, for the
rest of the year. The juniors who are
eligible for the positions are L. W. Mc-
Dermott, W. Gilroy, J. D. McLachlan,
and J. P. Mackel.
