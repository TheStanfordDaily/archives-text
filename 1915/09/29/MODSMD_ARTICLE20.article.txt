# PALO ALTO NOTES
## 
### 
Ol&ine to Ooaeli Team
Grant Olaine, lust year's captain of
the Palo Alto high school rugby fifteen
and now skipper of the Palo Alto Ath
letic Club ruggers, is coaching the
team on which he formerly starred.
Chili! Labor .Meeting'
Miss J. Ksclienbrenner, secretary of
the national child labor committee,
spoke at a special meeting in the in
terests of the child labor movement
which was held at the playhouse last
evening.
T. 0. ('. Spreads to "Preps'*
"T. O. C." chapters have been es
tablished at Manzanita school and at
Palo Alto high school. It has even
spread to San Jose high school, it is
rumored. The "preppers" are enthu
siastically taking up the occulty
shady philosophies of the mysterious
fraternal organization which recently
appeared on the Stanford campus.
