# SOCIETY ADDS SIXTEEN TO ROLLS OF MEMBERSHIP
## 
### 
Sixteen neophites now await initia
tion as a result of the election held
last evening by the Geology and Min
ing Society. Dr. Bailey Willis, head
of the Geology department, received
an associate membership while A. N.
MacKenzie, '17, was elected treasurer.
