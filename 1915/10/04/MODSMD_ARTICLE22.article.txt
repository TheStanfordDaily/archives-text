# Miss Lothrop to Address Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Lothrop, '12, will
speak at the Y. W. C. A. meeting Tues
day at 1:30 in the clnh house. Miss
Lothrop will tell of her experience
with working girls in Boston. Miss
Eleanor Walls, '15, will lead the meet
ing and Helen Kegley, '18, is to sing
Miss Jessie Hunt. '16, will give a
reading.
