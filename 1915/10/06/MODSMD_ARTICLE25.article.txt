# Euphronia to Hold Open Meeting
## 
### 
The Euplironia Debating Society has
chosen for its subject of discussion to
night the following question: "Re
solved, That an embargo should be
placed upon the shipment of munitions
of war from the United States to the
belligerent nations." J. G. Driscoll,
'18, will uphold the affirmative, and
M. L. McClintock, '18, the negative.
The meeting will be an open one and
visitors are invited to attend.
