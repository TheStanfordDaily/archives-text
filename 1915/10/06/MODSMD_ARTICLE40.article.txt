# M.A. Hagen, Turns Playwright
## 
### 
M. A. Hagen, '15, well known on the
campus as the editor of last years'
Chaparral and co-author of the 1915
junior opera, "The College Prince," is
now a member of the Angeles firm
of Hunt and Hagen. engaged in writ
ing one-act vaudeville plays, songs,
etc. The first production of the firm
is entitled "The Woman Hater," and
will soon make its appearance in
vaudeville. Hagen collaborates with
Hunt in the scenario and dialogue,
but furnishes nearly all of the lyrics
i and music.
