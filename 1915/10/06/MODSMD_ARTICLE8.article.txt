# Palo Alto Alumnus Engaged
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Genevieve
Howard to Egerton D. Lakin, 'OS, was
announced this afternoon at a tea
given by Miss Howard at her home in
Palo Alto.
Mr. Lakin is an attorney and po
lice judge in Palo Alto. The wedding
will take place during the winter.
