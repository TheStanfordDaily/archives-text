Announcement of the football bene
fit dance for the evening of October 1">
opens the way to fun all around, and
for a material expression of our in
terest in the welfare of the varsity
ruggers. Money received at the door
is not to be underrated as a means
of financing extra costs of the training
table, and thereby diverting a cus
tomary drain from the student body
treasury.
Hut after all, the greater value will
come from knowledge of people pres
ent at the festival, that they are sub
scribing toward a Stanford cause.
Not that they are expected to spend
the evening in a charitable mood, but
the varsity men are going to be on
the floor, and the round of "small talk"
will be centered on the team, and
other items of community interest.
