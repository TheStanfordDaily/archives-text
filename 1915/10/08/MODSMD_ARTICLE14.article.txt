# Columbia Will Race Yale Eight
## 
### 
Negotiations for a regatta between
Columbia and Yale universities, which
have been pending for several weeks,
have finally been completed and the
event will be held on the afternoon of
November 12, at New Haven. This is

ball game and a big crowd is expected.
