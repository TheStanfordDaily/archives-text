# Scholarship Hits U.C.
## 
### 
The freshman football team of the
University of California will have to
be organized all over as a result of
the scholarship axe following the mid
term examinations. Ten members of
the freshman squad were affected by
the order and it is understood,
although Graduate Manager Stroud
would not allow the names of the men
to he published, that several men from
the first team were dismissed. The
shake-up will be easily noticed when
the freshman line-up is announced for
the week-end game.
