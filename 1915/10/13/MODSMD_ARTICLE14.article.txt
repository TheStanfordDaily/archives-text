# Funds from Friday Eve Dance to Aid Varsity
## 
### 
Every Stanford student will liave an
opportunity to help tho varsity foot
ball I am round into shape by attend
ing the training table dance this Fri
day evening in the gymnasium. The
importance of assisting is seen when
attention is called to the fact that,
although players trade boarding
places and cost with those living in
the house wh re the table is set up,
nevertheless a large deficit will result
because of the unusual expense of run
ning the table.
Much additional pep will bo inject d
into the dance because of the fact that
the players will be allowed to break
training during the fore part of the
evening to participate in the t rpsi
chorean joy. The ticket sale presages
a crowd of dancers. Programs will
be obtainable tomorrow.
The Stanford Pand is well primed
for the occasion with an unlimited
amount of good music. The dances
numb; r eighteen plus two regular ex
tras and the promise of several off the
program.
