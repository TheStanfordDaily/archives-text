# Junior Announces Engagement
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Ruth
Franks ami Mr. Arthur Field Ganong,
'17, was announced yesterday. Miss
Franks, who is the daughter of Mrs.
K. S. Franks of Palo Alto, graduated
from Harker's School last spring.
Ganong registers from Portland, Ore.,
and is a member of Sigma Alpha
Epsilon.
