# MUSIC OFFICERS CHOSEN
## 
### 
Officers for the coming year, to
gether with active and honorary mem
bers, were elected at the last meeting
of the Stanford Music Club. Miss
Mabel Schelback, '16, chosen vice
president, and Mr. Louis Eaton, pro
fessor of music, was chosen as the
faculty advisor for the organization.
Mrs. P. C. Woodward was elected an
honorary member. The active mem
bers elected were Miss Esther Liver
sidge, '17, Miss Edith Harvey, '17, Miss
Dorothy Montgomery, Miss Lucile Mc-
Cracken, L. J. Farrar, '17, and C. L.
McCormick, '19.
