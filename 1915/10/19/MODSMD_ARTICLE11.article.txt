# THREE JOLLY-UPS FRIDAY
## All Four Classes Will Gather for Evening of Fun 
### 
Four classes will begin jolly-ups
Friday night as soon after nine o'clock
as the Masquers, jinks in the Assem
bly Hall can be brought to a close,
seniors and juniors in Encina Club
room, sophomores in the new gymna
sium, and freshmen in the Armory in
Palo Alto.
Though Masquers was unable to be
gin the jinks earlier than eight o'clock
owing to the attendance of Palo Alto
people who cannot get out to the cam
pus earlier, the show will begin at
eight o'clock punctually and will end
between nine and ten o'clock, that suf
ficient time may be allowed for all
class get togethers.
Rough neck attire by both men and
women at the jinks will he in order
that students may go directly to the
jolly-ups. All classes now have com
mittees planning stunts which are
puanned to be the snappiest ever at
tempted.
