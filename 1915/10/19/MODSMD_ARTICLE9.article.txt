# TO PICK SWIMMING TEAM
## Ten-man Squad Will Meet Lowell High School Next Saturday 
### 
Final swimming workouts previous
to the meet with the Lowell high
school will he held tomorrow after
noon. At that time final selection of
the ten men who will contest with the
high school team will he made. Pre
liminary selection of the team was
made last Friday, at which time a suc
cessful hanidcap meet was run off.
Tomorrow afternoon's workout will
include several handicap races, and
will see practically every Stanford
swimmer of importance out for prac
tice.
Judging by the time made in the
races last week fast results are ex
pected in the events tomorrow. Coach
Brandsten is well pleased with the
work of the swimmers to date and ex
pects to develop many first class men.
