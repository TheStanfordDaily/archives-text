# Women Plan Assemblies
## 
### 
Women's Conference began arrange
ments at the meeting last night to
hold two social evenings a semester
at which time all Stanford women will
get together. The first of the gather
ings will be held early in November
in the Clubhouse. Clever stunts and
a discussion of some subject of inter
est to the women as a whole will
form the evening's entertainment. A
committee composed of the Misses
Gertrude Clancy, 'Ifi. Irma Revburn,
'Ifi, and Harbara Alderton, 'Ifi, will ar
range the program.
