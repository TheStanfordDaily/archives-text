# UNDERCLASSMEN WILL JOLLY-UP TOGETHER IN GYMNASIUM
## 
### 
Freshman and sophomores will hold
a joint jolly-up in the gymnasium to
morrow as a result of the freshman
meeting held today. Dancing will he
in order at nine o'clock. The refresh
ments will eonsist of cider, doughnuts
and apples.
The invitation to the freshmen to
unite with the sophomores was given
several days ago. Today's decision re
versed the stand first taken by the
freshmen to hold a separate jolly-up
in the armory.
