# Training Table Enlarged
## 
### 
Coach Floyd Brown announced this
morning the addition of four new
members to the list of varsity foot
ball men now under the care of Train
er Doc Reynolds at the Sigma Alpha
Epsilon training table.
It. R. Templeton, '15, wing, C. S.
Ivong, '19, second-five-eighths, T. E.
Swigart, '17, wing, and E. Smitherum,
'17, front ranker, comprise the new
additions. This raises the total to
eighteen, fourteen candidates having
been selected last Saturday. The four
men enumerated go on the training
table today.
