# STYLES FOR MEN
## Vanity Fair Will Give Tips on Proper Costume for Sophomore Cotillion 
### 
Vanity Fair is to be a real show.
Living models depicting the latest
and correct styles for men and
women will be exhibited. One visit
to this concession will entirely clear
the masculine mind, if there be any
doubt, as to his proper apparel for
the cotillion. Is the Byronic shirt to
be in vogue again; are white socks
still with us? One look at the mod
els will answer these questions
which have overpowered many a
man.
A dansant, rivaling Pavo Real is
to be held in the main room of the
Union, that the "Five Hundred"
may help you spend your nickels.
Queening is in order on Friday
night. Incidentally, so will lie sev
eral free dances.
Are you all in on the "Hall of
Fame"? This will be the place
where all the "big bugs" receive no
tice. Have you seen our football
captain in his new role? Who was
at the Cactus garden last Friday
evening? Cap and Gown promises
"real dope."
