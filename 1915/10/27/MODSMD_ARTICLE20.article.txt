# Dr. Wilbur to be Dinner Guest
## 
### 
Dr. Wilbur will be guest of honor
at an informal stag dinner given by
San Francisco Stanford men Friday
evening. November 8:15, at the
Press Club. Reservations at $1.25 a
plate may lie made to CJ. D. Boalt,
Press Club, before November 4th.
Postcards will not be sent to alumni
or faculty but all. including students,
are invited.
