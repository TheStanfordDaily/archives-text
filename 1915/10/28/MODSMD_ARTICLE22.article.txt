# PALO ALTO NOTES
## 
### 
Arno Dosch, war correspondent for
the New York World, lectured to a
good-sized audience in the Palo Alto
Armory last night on "Louvain to Ar
ras." In graphic style he outlined the
course of the first year of the war and
its results. Many charmingly told an
ecdotes, humorous and pathetic, en
livened his address.
