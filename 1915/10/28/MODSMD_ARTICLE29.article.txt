# Music Club Meets
## 
### 
The Stanford Music Club held its
regular meeting Wednesday evening at
the Women's Clubhouse. The follow
ing program was rendered:
1. "Libestraum"—Liszt
Lucile McCracken.
2. "Kashmiri Song"—from "Garden of
Kanna"
Laura Wheeler.
3. "Quartette —"Woodland Voices"
Esther Liverside, Edith Harvey
L. Fairer, L. McCormick.
4. "The Lark Leaves His Nest"—Parker
Edith Harvey.
5. "Silver Spring"—Mason
Mabel Schellbach.
