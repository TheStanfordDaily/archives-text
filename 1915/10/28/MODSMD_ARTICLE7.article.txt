# Result of Euphronia Debate Condemns LaFollette's Act
## Anspach and Supple Discuss Seaman's Bill 
### 
W. C. Anspach, '17, defeated F. E.
Supple, '17, last, night In a debate be
fore the Euphronia Debating Society,
on the question, "Resolved, That the
La Follette Seaman's Act should be
repealed." Anspach spoke on the af
firmative of the question and proved
to the satisfaction of the audience
that the recently enacted law pertain
ing to shipping will be harmful to
the best interests of the United States.
The entire audience served as judges
of the argument, a popular vote being
taken to decide the winner.
