# LOCAL RACQUETERS COMPETE
## Four Stanford Players Qualify in State Tennis Tourney 
### 
Four Stanford tennis players have
survived the first round of tbe state
championship tennis tournament now
being played in San Francisco. H. L.
Hahn, '16, defeated C. Curley of Oak
land yesterday. The match scores
were 4-6, 8-6, 6-2. H. V. Johns, *18,
beat Allan Barber, '18, in the hardest
fought match of the tournament, 13-11,
6-3. Miss Laura Herron, '14, won from
Miss Mary Marshall of San Francisco,
6-1, 4-6, 6-0. K. M. Hawks, '19, de
feated (}. Clover, '19, in a very close
match, 2-6, 8-6, 10-8. This match was
in the junior class, Hawks now stand
ing a good chance of winning the
event.
