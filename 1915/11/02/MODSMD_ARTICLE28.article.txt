# Cosmopolitan Women Entertain Men
## 
### 
Members of the Women's Cosmopoli
tan Club were hostesses to the Men's
Cosmopolitan Club last evening at the
Women's Clubhouse. A short wel
coming address was given by Miss
Marcelle Rouiller, gr., president of the
women's club, and Dr. Willis spoke of
his travels in Europe and the Far
East, his subject being "Hidden
Friends." Piano music by Miss Rouil
ler and Misses Anne and Henrietta
Rehmke, grs., concluded the program,
after which a social time occupied the
remainder of the evening.
