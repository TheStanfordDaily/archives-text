# Sociology Club to Hear Lecture
## 
### 
The second of a series of six lectures
under the auspices of the Sociology
Club, is to be given Thursday evening
in room 22S of the History department
by George Boswell. His subject will
be "The Menace of the Unemployed."
The four other lecturers will be Mr.
Will J. French, of San Francisco, No
vember 18; Professor C. A. Huston,
December 2; Professor Robert Whit
aker, of Los Altos, January 18; and
Professor E. 1). Adams, January 27.
The first lecture by Dr. Julia Seton of
New York was given October 28.
