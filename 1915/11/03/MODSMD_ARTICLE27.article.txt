# Mrs. Smith Entertains French Club
## 
### 
Professor De Vries read "Deux
Amies" from the Tales of Guy de
Maupassant for the members of the
French Club, which met at the home
Of -Mrs. S. A. Smith, 417 ('ban
ning, last evening. A spelling bee in
French occupied the remainder of the
evening. The next meeting of the club
will be held at the Tri Delta house on
November 16.
