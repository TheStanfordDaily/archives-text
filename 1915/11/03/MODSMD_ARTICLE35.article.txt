# Alumnae to Serve Wednesday Teas.
## 
### 
Every Wednesday the Resident
Alumnae will serve tea in the Women's
Clubhouse after 3:30. The first of
these social gatherings was held last
Friday afternoon, with an attendance
of seventy-five. The hostesses for
Wednesdays's tea will be Mrs. J. C.
Branner, Mrs. W. T. Carruth, Miss
Isabel Lockey, and Mrs. F. Fowler.
Fifteen cents is charged for refresh
ments, tiie money received going to
ward the clearing of the Clubhouse
debt. All former women students,
whether graduates or not. are eligible
to membership in the Alumnre Asso
ciatoin.
