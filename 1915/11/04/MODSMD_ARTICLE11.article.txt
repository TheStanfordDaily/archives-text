# Bleachers to Hear U.C.-U.W. Returns
## 
### 
Complete returns of the Cali
fornia-Washington football game
will be announced by The Daily
Palo Alto from the bleachers at the
Stanford-Olympic rugby contest
next Saturday afternoon. Special
wire service has been secured to
give results of the Berkeley game
to Stanford rooters, who can yell
for two scores at the same time.
