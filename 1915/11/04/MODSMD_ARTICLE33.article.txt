# DEBATERS WILL BANQUET
## U.S.C. Forensic Fraternity Will Honor Cardinal Artists in Southern City 
### 
A banquet will lie given in honor of
the Stanford trio of debaters that
meets the University of Southern Cali
fornia the evening of November 12 in
Los Angeles, by the Southern Cali
fornia chapter of Delta Siijnia Rho at
the IT. S. C. cafeteria Immediately after
the debate. A speech by a Stanford
debater features the after-dinner pro
gram of addresses.
