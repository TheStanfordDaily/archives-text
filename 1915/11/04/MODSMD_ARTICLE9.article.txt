# STUDIO WILL BE SCENE OK ARTIST'S EXHIBITION
## 
### 
Portraits and monotypes by Shirley
Williamson and Celia Seymour will be
on exhibition in the Studio building
November 13 to 20 from 2 to 6 p. m.
Mrs. Williamson, who resides In
Carmel, has featured scenes from Car
tnel, Point Lohos, and Pebble Beach.
She was at one time president of the
Association of New York Women
Painters and Sculptors. She received
her training at Rodin's in Paris. Miss
Seymour studied in New York and was
an instructor in the art department of
Pratt Institute.
