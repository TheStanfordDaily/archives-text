# Foil and Mask Will Oppose U.C. Society
## 
### 
Foil and Mask fencing society will
make a trip to Berkeley tomorrow to
fence with La Rapiere society of Cali
fornia. The ten women and six men
who will make the trip will probably
spend the night in Berkeley as guests
of Berkeley members. The evening
will he passed in informal fencing be
tween the members of the two clubs.
Those making the trip will leave
here on the 6:16 train. They will
travel across the bay on the Key
Route, and are instructed to leave the
Key Route train at Alcatraz and take
the car marked Alcatraz. They will
be met in Berkeley by members of La
Rapiere.
