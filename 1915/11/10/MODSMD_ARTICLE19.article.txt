# NO MORE LIQUOR IN STATE
## Euphronia Will Try to Convert Hearers to Prohibition 
### 
That California should adopt a con
stitutional amendment prohibiting the
manufacture and sale of intoxicating
liquors will be the contention of N.
R. Allen, 18, and R. H. French, '18,
tonight when they will uphold the
affirmative of the question, "Resolved,
That a constitutional amendment pro
viding for the prohibition of the man
ufacture and sale of intoxicating
liquors is desirable in California," be
fore the Euphronia Debating Society.
J. G. Driscoll, 'IS, and L. L. Chapman,
'IS, will speak for the negative.
