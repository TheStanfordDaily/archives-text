# MONEY FORWARDED CREW BY ALUMNI IS REFUNDED
## Executive Committee Votes $300 to Pay Back Poughkeepsie Benefactors 
### 
The Executive Committee at its
meeting last evening voted to refund
the $300.00 advanced to the Stanford
crew at Poughkeepsie by the Alumni
Association.
The minutes of the meeting follow:
President Wilson in the chair.
Walker absent.
MSC that $300.00 advanced to the
crew at Poughkeepsie by Alumni As
sociation be refunded.
MSC that purchase of launch be
postponed until next meeting.
Discussion concerning the bringing
of a Vancouver rugby team to Cali
fornia.
MSC that it be recommended that
no more theater programs be used for
advertising.
A. S. HAYES, Secretary.
