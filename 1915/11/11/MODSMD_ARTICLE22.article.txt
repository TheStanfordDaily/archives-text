# THREE JUDGES NAMED FOR INTERCOLLEGIATE DEBATE
## Judge Angellotti of Supreme Court Will be Official Tomorrow Evening 
### 
Judge F. M. Angellotti of the Su
preme Court has consented to act as
one of the judges for the intercolle
giate debate between Stanford and
California, tomorrow evening in the
auditorium of the Girls' High School,
San Francisco. The other two judges
will be Judges Frank H. Kerrigan and
John E. Richards of the Appellate
Court, San Francisco.
Assistant Professor Stephen I. Mil
ler of the Stanford Economics depart
ment will preside as chairman.
