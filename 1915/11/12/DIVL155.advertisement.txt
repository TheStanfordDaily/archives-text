DAVIS AUTO SERVICE
Official Daily Time Table
In effect October 1, 1915
This linn connects at Palo Alto with Pe
ninsula Rapid Transit rn.'s lino for San
Francisco and way points. Fare: Palo
Alto to San Francisco, 55c. Palo Alto to
San Jose, 40c; round trip, 75c.
♦This bus will not leave l San Jose before
schedule, but will remain until theatre per
formances are concluded.
•♦Saturday and Sunday only.
