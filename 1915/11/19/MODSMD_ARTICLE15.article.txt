# RUGGERS TO CHOOSE NEXT YEAR'S CAPTAIN SUNDAY
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity rugby raptain for
next year will be chosen Sunday morn
ing at 11 o'clock, when the members of
this year's victorious squad gather at
the studio of Franklin, the photogra
pher, to have the group picture taken
for the 1917 Quad. The election will
take place following the picture-tak
ing, according to the custom of past
years.
