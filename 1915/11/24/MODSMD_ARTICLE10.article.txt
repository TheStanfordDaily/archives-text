# Has Dobie Offered His Services to Wisconsin?
## 
### 
Press dispatches from Madison, Wis
consin. today stated that Coach Dobie
of Washington has offered his ser
vices to the University of Wisconsin.
Petitions will be circulated, it was
declared, calling upon the athletic
authorities to accept. According to
Washington dispatches Dobie refused
to admit or deny the report. Tt is be
lieved that his contract with Wash
ington, calling for a salary of $3100
is not legally binding, and under such
conditions would not lie in the way of
Dobie's severing connections with the
northern school.
