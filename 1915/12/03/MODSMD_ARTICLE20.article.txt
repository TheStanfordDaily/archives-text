# TENNIS POSTPINED
## Thiele Tournament Will be Played Later Because of Rain 
### 
On account of unsettled weather
conditions, the annual Thiele handi
cap tennis tournament lias been post
poned until some time during the lat
ter part of next week. Some of the
matches have been played, but the
tournament has not progressed very
fast as vet. This is usually one of the
largest tournaments of the year, it
being a handicap affair, thus giving
every one an equal chance.
