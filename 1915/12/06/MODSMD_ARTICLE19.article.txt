# GRADUATE PUBLISHES
## Book Entitled, "Old Roads From the Heart of New York" 
### 
A review on the recent book by
Sarah L. Conistock, entitled, "Old
Roads from the Heart of New York;
Journeys Today by Ways of Yester
day; Within Thirty Miles Around the
Mattery," appears in"The Nation."
Miss Conistock is a magazine writer
of note. After graduating from Stan
ford, she served as a reporter on the
San Francisco "Call," later going to
New York City.
