# TRI-STATE DEBATE TOPIC ANNOUNCED BY K. E. LEIB
## Stanford, Oregon, and Washington Debaters Will Clash on Navy Size Question 
### 
K. E. Leib, '15, head of the intercol
legiate debating committee, announced
the subject of the tri-state debate with
Oregon and Washington this after
noon. Tryopts to pick the two teams
for this debate will be held early next
semester.
The debate itself will probably be
held March 24, according to Leib. The
question follows: "Resolved. That
the United States should maintain its
navy above third rank in fighting effi
ciency."
