# INSTALLATION OF NEW PRESIDENT IN JANUARY
## Facility Committee Met This Afternoon to Work Out Details of Installation 
### 
The inauguration of Dr. R. L. Wil
bur as President of Stanford will take
place as soon as possible after college
opens next semester, according to
word .received today from Mr. W. H.
Newhall, president of the Board of
Trustees. The tentative date for the
installation is Saturday, January 15.
Chancellor Jordan will be here for
the ceremony, but Dr. Branner will
p.robably be in Panama at that time.
Mr. Newhall met with representa
tives of the faculty in San Francisco
today, but nothing definite was
decided.
