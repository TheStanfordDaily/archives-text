# FRESHMEN ARE VICTORS IN CLASS BASKETBALL
## 
### 
Playing the fastest basketball game
of the series, the freshmen defeated
the juniors at Encina gymnasium and
annexed the championship, by a score
of 22-16 yesterday afternoon.
The game was featured by fast and
aggressive playing on the part of both
teams, but the freshman basket-shoot
ers were more accurate than those of
the junior team and with a brilliant
rally in the second half cinched the
deciding game. The teams lined up
as follows:
Freshman — Estes, Pelouze, for
wards; Righter, center; Green, Sulli
van, guards.
.luniors—Wallace, Roney (Honnett),
forwards; Wheatley, center; Ames,
Lytle (Stewart), guards.
