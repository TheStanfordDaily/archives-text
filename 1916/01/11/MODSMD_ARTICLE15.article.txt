# HUGH C. STEPHENSON, '17, PASSKS AWAY AT PENINSULA HOSPITAL
## 
### 
Hugh C. Stephenson, '17, passed
away at the Peninsula Hospital, Fri
day morning, the cause of his death
being a sudden attack of meningitis
and blood poisoning. Stephenson was
found in an unconscious state Sunday
night in the car barns of the Penin
sula Railway company, where he was
employed as a night watchman, and
was hurried to the Peninsula Hos
pital. He regained consciousness for
a time and recognized his father,
William Stephenson, of Lakeside, who
hurried to his bedside, but from
Thursday night to the time of his
death he was unconscious.
