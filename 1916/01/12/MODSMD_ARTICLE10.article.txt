# LAW MEN IN HAST
## 
### 
As representatives from Stanford,
Professor F. C. Woodward and Assist
ant Professor M. It. Kirkwood attend
ed the annual meeting of the Asso
ciation of American Law Schools,
held during the past vacation at Chi
cago. Professor Woodward read a
paper before the convention, which
was attended by representatives from
some fifty of the leading law schools
in America. After the close of the
convention they attended the yearly
meeting of the Order of the Coif, the
law honor society, of which Stanford
has a chapter.
