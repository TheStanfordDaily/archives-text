# Registration Increases
## Enrollment Totals 125 More Than Last Year 
### 
Eighteen hundred and nineteen
students had registered at the Univer
sity up to 10 o'clock last night, ac
cording to a statement made public
by Assistant Registrar McDowell this
afternoon.
Of this number, there are fifty-three
new men, thirty new women and fifty
old students who have been away
from the campus for one or more sem
esters. Thirty-six of these old stud
ents who have come back to Stanford
this semester are men, and fourteen
are women.
Total registration exceeds the
figures for last year by one hundred
and twenty-five, and those of the year
previous by one hundred and eleven.
