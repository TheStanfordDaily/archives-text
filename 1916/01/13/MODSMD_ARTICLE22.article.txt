# MEMORIAL SERVICES FOR HUGH STEPHENSON SUNDAY
## 
### 
Memorial services for Hugh C. Ste
phenson, '17, will be held next Sunday
evening, at 6:30, in the Congregational
church of Palo Alto. The services
will be under the auspices of the
Young People's Societies of Palo Alto,
of which Stephenson was president.
As he was also president of the
Christian Endeavor of the Congrega
tional Church, Rev. Is. C. Preston, pas
tor of that church, will officiate.
Stephenson's death, which occurred
on the morning of January 7, was the
result of a sudden attack of menin
gitis and blood poisoning.
