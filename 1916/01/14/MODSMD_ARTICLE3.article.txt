# Announce Engagement of Stanford Couple
## W.J. Gross, ex-'16, Former Managing Editor of Daily, Will Marry Miss Dorothy Davy, '16 
### 
Wilmer J. Gross, ex-'l6, former
Managing Editor of The Daily Palo
Alto, has made announcement of his
engagement to Miss Dorothy Davy,
'16, a member of Kappa Kappa Gam
ma sorority. Gross held the man
aging editorship of The Daily Palo
Alto last semester, until he was
forced to leave college, in November,
on account of the illness of his father.
Gross is now in business in San Jose.
He was prominent in Stanford dra
matics, having held important roles in
"The Only Way," "The Pillars of So
ciety," and "Men and Women," the
Sword and Sandals productions of
1912, 1913 and 1914. He was also in
"Quality Street," played by Masquers
in 1914.
Gross was a member of Sword and
Sandals, 1916 Quad staff, and be
longed to Delta Kappa Epsilon fra
ternity.
