# Wood Bees Elect
## 
### 
Members of Wood Hees, recently or
ganized women's dramatic society,
elected Miss Alice Williams, '18, to
membership as club artist, at their
initial meeting, held last evening.
Miss Velona Pilcher, '18, president of
the organization, presided, and it was
decided to keep the same officers for
the ensuing semester. Plans for pro
ductions are under way, but nothing
definite lias been decided as to the
first presentation.
