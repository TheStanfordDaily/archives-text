# PHILOLOGICAL SOCIETY WILL MEET ON THURSDAY
## 
### 
The University Philological Society
will hold its next regular meeting on
Thursday of this week, at 3:30, in
room 31. Professor A. T. Murray of
the Creek department and Professor
J. S. Tatlock of the English depart
ment will speak. Professor Murray
will take as his subject "The Disposal
of Spoil in the Homeric Age," and
Professor Tatlock will speak on
"Hretherhed in Chaucer's Prologue."
