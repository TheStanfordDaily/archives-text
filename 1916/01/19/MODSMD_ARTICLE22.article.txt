# Washington State Coach For U.C.
## 
### 
A dispatch dated from Washington
State College, at Pullman, yesterday,
said that* Football Coach "Lone Star"
Dietz, of the northern school had
been notified of his election as foot
ball coach for University of California
for next year. Dietz declined, and
is under contract with W. S. C., where
he hopes to turn out another "Hrown
beating" eleven.
