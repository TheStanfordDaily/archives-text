# Miss Bentley Speaks
## 
### 
Miss Mary Bentley, Student Field
Secretary for the Pacific Coast, who
js visiting here for a period of three
days, addressed the Y. W. C. A. yes
terday. She exemplified her plea,
which was directed toward self-as
surance, by interesting experiences
she has had during her work in the
foreign mission field and as Coast
Secretary.
Miss Bentley will confer with the
Y. W. C. A. Cabinet and with the
Advisory Board, devoting her time
especially to financial matters, par
ticularly in regard to the salary of
the student secretary.
Miss Edith Harvey, '17, furnished
music for the meeting, singing a se
lection from Gounod.
