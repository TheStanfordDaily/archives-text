# The
						Managing Editor
## 
### 
With this issue we announce tlie
promotion of G. K. Howard, '17, to the
position of managing editor of The
Daily Palo Alto. Over a period of two
and a half years, Howard has done
efficient and thorough work on the
staff, beginning as a reporter and
serving for the past year as a news
editor.
Miss Anita Marcus, '17, retains the
position of woman's editor, which she
held last semester.
