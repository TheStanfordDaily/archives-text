# Class of *39
## 
### 
To Carol Green Wilson, '14, and
George Osborne Wilson, '14, was born,
December 19, a son, George Osborne
Wilson, Jr., '39. Mrs. Wilson, while
in college, was active in various
women's activities, and in the first
semester of her senior year was presi
dent of the Women's Conference. She
is a member of Kappa Alpha Theta
sorority. Mr. Wilson, "Sticky," was
an Encina man, and is now with the
Standard Oil Company.
