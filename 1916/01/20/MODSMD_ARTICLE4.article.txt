# JUNIOR OPERA TRYOUTS TO BE HELD FEBRUARY 5
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Junior Opera will
be held at the Alpha Omicron Pi house
all day Saturday, February 5, begin
ning at 10 o'clock.
J. R. Morgan, '16, manager and co
producer with W. P. Forbes, '15, of
last year's musical success, will coach
the 1917 opera.
