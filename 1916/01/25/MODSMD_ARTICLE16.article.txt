# RAND DANCE FRIDAY NIGHT IN GYMNASIUM
## Stanford Band Enters Twelfth Semester Under Present System. Has Many Members 
### 
Opening the twelfth semester under
the present, system, with the best per
sonnel thus far attained, the Stanford
Rand looks forward to prospects of
nothing hut success. The Band Dance
is scheduled for next Friday night, in
the Gymnasium, and a splendid pro
gram has been arranged.
The equipment of the band is all
of the very best, being second to that
of no other university band in the
country. It is owned by the Associ
ated Students.
