# WOMAN'S "S" SOCIETY TO ENLARGE ELIGIBLE LIST
## 
### 
Women's "S" Society met last Tues
day night and decided to propose to
the Women's Athletic Association that
any woman who has made her class
team for four years in any one sport,
or any woman who has played on a
Varsity team in three outside games
of the same sport in the same se
mester, should he eligible to the "S"
Society. At present, only those who
have won their "S" are eligible, and
the only teams of which women can
he members and win their "S" are
tennis, fencing and basketball.
