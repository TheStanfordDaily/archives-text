# SOPHOMORE JOLLY-UP TO BE HELD ON FEBRUARY 18
## 
### 
Friday evening, February 18, is the
date set for the Sophomore Jolly-Up.
Freshmen women will be invited, and
it will probably lie held in Encind
Gymnasium. H. I'. Mauser was ap
pointed chairman of the Jolly-Up
Committee, with M. G. Kyle, C. G.
Graham, E. VV. Arnold, and L. G.
Lynch to assist him. Any suggest
ions for stunts are requested by the
committee.
