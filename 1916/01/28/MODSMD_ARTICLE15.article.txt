# Band Dance Begins at 8:30
## 
### 
The Hand Dance tonight will start
promptly at 8:30. There will be
special car service tor both Palo Alto
and campus residents. The patron
esses are Professors and Mesdames H.
W. Stuart, S. I. Miller, E. VV. Martin,
and Mrs. Chappie. The sixteen dances
and two extras will he divided as fol
lows: Eighth, twelfth, fifteenth, are
waltzes; third and the two extras are
fox-trots, and the rest are to be one
steps.
