# COLUMBIA PARK BOYS' BAND WILL GIVE CONCENT SUNDAY
## 
### 
Weather permitting, the Columbia
Park Hoys' Senior Hand will give a
concert in the Quadrangle, Sunday
afternoon, from H to 5 o'clock. It'
weather conditions make the open-air
concert impossible, they will appear
in the Memorial Church. This band
has made an enviable reputation,
being one of the features in the mus
ical entertainment at the Exposition.
