# Special Services for Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
For the first time, special recogni
tion services for the new members
taken into the Y. W. C. A. during the
recent membership campaign, will be
held tomorrow at the regular weekly
meeting of the Y. W. C. A. in the
Women's Club House. Old members
are urged to be present; also, Miss
Eileen Everett will speak on the work
of the Jubilee and its significance to
members. A vested choir will sing.
