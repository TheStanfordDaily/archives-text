# LOW TIDE CAUSES CREW TO WORK ON LAGUNITA
## 
### 
Low tides on the Redwood estuary
have brought the crew workouts on
the bay to a sudden halt, and no more
trips will be made by the crew can
didates until Thursday. In the mean
time, the varsity, freshmen, and sec
and varsity men will report with the
remainder of the squad at Lagunita
daily.
Partial training rules for the crew
men went into effect today, but the
men will not be prohibited from danc
ing or required to observe regular
hours for sleep. The permission to
continue dancing for a time will en
able the crew men to attend the "On
to Poughkeepsie" dance to be given
in Encina Gymnasium on February 11.
