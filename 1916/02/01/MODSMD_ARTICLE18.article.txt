# Madame Melba Will Sing in San Jose February 10
## Stunts, Speeches and "Good Old Stanford Time" To Constitute Program 
### 
Madame Melba, well known prima
donna, will sing at the Victory theater
in San Jose on Tuesday evening,,
February 10. Greatest of lyric solo
ists, the opportunity of hearing her
is being looked forward to by those
who appreciate music. Madame Melba
also is one of the leading workers
for the Red Cross Association, giving
her entire profit to this cause when
she sings in England. Sale of seats
will begin at the Victory theater next
Thursday morning at 10 o'clock.
