# ELECT HAZEL HILL
## Freshmen Choose Fair Movie Actress To Fill Office of Vice-President 
### 
Hazel Hill, *19, or so, favorite cam
pus movie actress and subscription
solicitor for "Spare Moments Only
Fifty Cents," was elected freshman
class vice-president at a meeting held
in the chapel today at 1:10. Miss
Hill (sometimes known as Witch
Hazel) fills the vacancy discovered
when the information was unearthed
that Miss Mary Fox, vice-president
elect. is not registered. Miss Hill's
election to office will undoubtedly be
an asset to her in the race for popu
larity in filnidom.
A committee will be appointed in
the near future to make arrangements
for a freshmen jolly-up, not to be
held In conjunction with the sopho
mores, as was the case last semester,
but for the first year students alone.
The report of the committee on the
proposal of giving the winning inter
class basketball team numerals, was
accepted. As basketball has been
changed to a major sport, the inter
class series will be played ovei
again, and should the first year team
win it, the members will be presented
with sweaters and block 19's.
