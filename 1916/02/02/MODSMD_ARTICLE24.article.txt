# To Revise California Constitution
## 
### 
Amending the constitution of the
Associated Students of University of
California overshadowed the reports
of the Intercollegiate Agreement Com
mittee at a meeting held across the
hay on Friday. The latter matter was
quickly disposed of, and the revision
of their student body constitution took
the place of most importance.
