# Ground Hog Brings Fine Track Weather
## 
### 
Track men sans a jubilee, last night,
to celebrate the appearance of the
ground hog. It is seriously reported
by one Reg Caughey, who comes from
up around Ukiah, California, where
the ground hogs are watched for by
all the farmers, that said animal lifted
his head above the grass yesterday,
February 2. This is the traditional
day for that event, from time immem
orial. It means that good weather is
here.
New men have been trying out their
spikes on the track during the last
few days, and under the guidance of
the veterans who are helping Captain
Ric Templeton, it is likely that the
Cardinal will lie represented by one
of tlu: best first year squads that has
donned the colors for some time.
Long distance men have assumed
hardest training to date among the
cinder path heroes. From the looks
of things at. the present time, the dis
tances will lie stronger this year than
last.
