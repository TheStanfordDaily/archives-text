# 1916 Baccalaureate by Harvard Speaker
## Seniors and Dr. Gardner Choose Dr. Francis G. Peabody For Final Sermon 
### 
At tlie request of the Rev. D.
Charles Gardner and W. F. Noonan,
president of the Senior class, Dr.
Francis G. Feabody of Harvard Uni
versity will preach the Baccalaureate
sermon, in the Memorial Church, May
21.
Dr. Peabody is to come to the Uni
versity on the first of May, having
been appointed by the Board of Trus
tees as special preacher and Univer
sity lecturer for the period embracing
the three Sundays, May 7, 14. and 21.
He is the author of several books,
probably the best known of which are
"Short Addresses to Young Men on
Personal Religion," and"The Ap
proach of the Social Question."
