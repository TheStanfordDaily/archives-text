# Juniors Elect
## 
### 
Juniors elected H. V. Aupperle and
J. A. Lander, class vice-president and
treasurer respectively, this afternoon.
President R R. Ileal asked for a pay
ment of all class dues immediately, to
clear the debts of the 1017 class, and
further announced that no sweater or
other honor would he awarded to any
Junior not a member of the class or
ganization, through failure to pay
dues.
