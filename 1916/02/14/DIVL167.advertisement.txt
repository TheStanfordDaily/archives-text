President Wilbur Addresses Men
In the first talk that he has made
to the men of the University since his
election as President, Dr. Wilbur last
night addressed the men students in
Encina Club Room. The meeting was
conducted under the auspices of the
Y. M. C. A., and was the first of a
number of talks that Stanford's new
President will be requested to make
i the men from time to time.
ANNOUNCEMENTS.
