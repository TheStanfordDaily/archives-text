# Inaugurate "Labor Day" at U. C.
## 
### 
With the appointment of February
29th as "Labor Day" at the University
of California, the students have inau
gurated a new idea which they hope
to perpetuate. Once in every four
years, it is planned, they will devote
a day to making needed changes
about the campus.
On this year's "Labor Day"the stud
ents will construct a path, up the
grade to the big "C" on the hill lie
hind the University. L. N. Hamilton,
of the class of 1010, will have charge
of the arrangements.
