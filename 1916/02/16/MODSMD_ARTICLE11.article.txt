# Freshmen Debaters Chosen
## 
### 
G. J, Knight, W. C. Fundenburg, and
W. E. Breneman were chosen last
night to represent the freshman class
in the Freshman-Sophomore debate
which will be held in the near future.
O. C. Fenlason was named as alter
nate. The tryout was held in con
nection with the regular meeting of
the Nestoria debating society. The
judges were Assistant Professor M. R.
Kirkwood, Instructor F. E. Hill, and
E. T. Worthy, '14.
