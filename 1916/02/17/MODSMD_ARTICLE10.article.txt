# Alumni Married
## 
### 
G. (5. Howard. '13, and Miss Mary
Elizabeth Lockwood, '13, wore united
in marriage at the home of the bride's
parents, in Pasadena, last Monday
evening. Howard was a member of
Cardinal Club, while Miss Lockwood
was a member of Pi Reta Phi sorority.
Immediately following the ceremony
Mr. and Mrs. Howard left for Tani
pico, Mexico, where Mr. Howard will
be engaged in geological work.
