# Glee Club Concert to Be February 26
## Popular Prices Will Prevail at Home Appearance of Singers 
### 
Posters for the Home Concert of the
Glee Club, which will be given at the
Assembly Hall on Saturday evening,
February 2G, have been placed about
the campus, as the preliminary ad
vertisement of that event, part of the
proceeds of which will be given to the
crew and track teams. Popular prices
of fifty and seventy-five cents will
prevail.
