# Prize Song Considered
## Executive Committee Adjourns After Short Business Session 
### 
After a short discussion in regard
to offering a prize for a Stanford song,
the Executive Committee adjourned
last night in order to allow members
to attend the basketball game.
The minutes follow:
Regular meeting, February 16, 1916.
Templeton, Hehrens, and McEuen
absent.
Discussion concerning offering a
prize for college song.
Laid on table.
MSC meeting adjourned.
A. S. HAYES, Secretary.
