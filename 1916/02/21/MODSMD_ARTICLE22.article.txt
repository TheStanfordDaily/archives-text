.Monday
Sophomore Jolly-Up—Women's Club
House, 8:15 p.m.
Cosmopolitan Club—.Meeting post
poned until the 28th.
Tuesday
C. E. Society—C. E. juniors and sen
iors will inspect bridges and aqueducts
near Xiles. Take 8:07 a.m. train out
of Palo Alto, returning to San .Tose at
6 p.m. .Men wishing to visit Dum
barton bridge should start at same
time, taking trolley car to Ravens
wood and walking six miles to New
ark. Provide lunch. Bill Fletcher or
Brinkmeyer for further particulars.
C. E. Seniors and Juniors —Trip to
Xiles Canyon will be postponed in case
of rain.
