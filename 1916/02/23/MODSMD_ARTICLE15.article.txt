# Indoor Tennis Title Won by R.L. Murray
## 
### 
R. L. Murray, '13, won the national
singles indoor tennis tournament held
at New York, yesterday, when he de
feated A. H. Man, Jr., former Yale
star, in three straight sets. Man was
unable to successfully compete Mur
ray's deep shots into the corners of
his opponent's court. In fact, Mur
ray's smashing drives were so heavy
in one set that he broke a racquet.
Murray is the first Pacific Coast man
to win the national indoor champion
ship, although Carlton B. Gardner
made the attempt in 1910,
