# Miller Will Speak to Men at Encina
## 
### 
"The Present War and the Doctrine
of Competition" will he the subject
on which Assistant Professor Stephen
Tvan Miller, Jr., of the Economics de
partment, will talk to the men of the
University, Sunday evening, at 7:30,
in Encina Clubroom. Professor Miller
is a forceful thinker and an interest
ing speaker. The subject which he
has chosen, is one which he has spec
ialized upon ever since the beginning
of the present European war.
This is one of a series of Sunday
night talks at Encina, by prominent
men, under the auspices of the Stan
ford Y. M. C. A. All Stanford men are
invited to attend the meeting.
