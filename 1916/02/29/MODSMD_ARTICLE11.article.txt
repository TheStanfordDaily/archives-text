# Faculty Women Guests of Cosmopolitan Club
## 
### 
Between fifteen and twenty faculty
women and students were guests of
the Women's Cosmopolitan Club at
Roble Hall, Monday evening. A mus
ical program composed of folk songs,
ballads and dances of several nations
was followed by a most interesting
lecture on her travels in Jerusalem,
by .Miss Margaret Lathrop of the Eco
nomics department.
