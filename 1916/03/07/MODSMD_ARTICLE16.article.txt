# Chancellor Jordan To Speak in S.F.
## 
### 
Chancellor David Starr Jordan will
again lecture on Peace in an address
before the Pewish Council of Women,
next Thursday, in San Francisco, at
the Native Sons' Hall. This will be
Dr. Jordan's third off-campus peace
talk this semester. Other speakers
in conjunction with Dr. Jordan on the
subject will be Mr. Robert C. Root and
Mrs. C. E. Cumberson of San Fran
cisco.
