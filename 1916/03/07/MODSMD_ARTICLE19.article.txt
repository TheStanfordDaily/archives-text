# California Football Men Work Out
## 
### 
Eighty men, fully equipped for foot
ball, turned out yesterday afternoon
on California field, for the Blue and
Gold's first workout under Andy
Smith, the new coach from Pennsyl
vania and Purdue. Only light work
was indulged in, as kicking and
charging under punts. Smith ex
pressed himself as well pleased with
the enthusiasm shown by the men.
