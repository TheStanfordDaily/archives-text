# Regent Aids Trip Fund
## 
### 
The Recent, clothing store in Palo
Alto has arranged with the crews and
track teams whereby a percentage of
all sales, and money paid on accounts,
will be given outright in cash towards
defraying the expenses of the crew
and track teams east. Crew and
track coupons must be asked for at
the time of the transaction to take
advantage of this offer. They can be
turned over to any crew or track man
before May 20. , This generous offer
has come vdluntarily from the
Regent. It will add materially to the
fupd betyip raised for making the
trips and is an assurance that the
trips will be possible.
