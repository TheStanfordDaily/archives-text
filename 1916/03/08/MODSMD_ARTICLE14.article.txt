# FRESHMEN COMPETITION WITH U.C. SANCTIONED
## Nineteeners Will Meet In All Intercollegiate Sports 
### 
Formal sanction of the Executive
Committee was Riven today to allow
freshmen competition with California
in all sports. The meeting was held
this afternoon instead of tonight, as
usual, in order not to conflict with the
track dance.
The minutes follow:
Special meeting, March 8, 1916.
President Wilson in chair.
Braden, Templeton, Castle, Coleman,
Walker absent.
MSC to sanction freshman competi
tion with California in all sports.
MSC meeting adjourn.
A. S. HAYES, Secretary.
