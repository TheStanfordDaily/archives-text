# GUERENA ESTABLISHES CREW TRAINING TABLE
## Men Will Eat One Meal Each Day In Union Cafe 
### 
Crew Coach Guerena has announced
that beginning tomorrow evening a
training table will lie started in the
Union cafe. The coach will name the
eighteen following tomorrow's work
out. The crew men will eat at the
training table for only the principal
meal each day. On days when the
workouts are held late in the after
noon, the men will gather at the
table in the evening, while on Satur
day and Sunday they will eat at the
Union at noon. Although the other
meals will not be directly supervised,
the men have been given definite
instructions as to their diet.
