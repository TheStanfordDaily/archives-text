# FAIRCLOUGH TALKS ON PLAN OF ROME
## 
### 
Professor H. It. Fairclough of the
Latin department was a speaker last
night before the San Francisco In
stitute of Art. He gave an illustrated
lecture on city-planning in ancient
Rome. For illustrations of his sub
ject lie used a number of views of
the recent Exposition. Because of
the lecture Professor Fairclough was
not present at the alumni dinner last
night.
