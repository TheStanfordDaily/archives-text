# Y.M.C.A. Convention
## 
### 
A number of Stanford men are
planning to attend the thirty-fifth an
nual state covention of the Young
Men's Christian Association, which
will be held in San Jose, March 17
to 19.
James E. Springer, state Y. M. C. A.
secretary, who has just returned from
the war zone, will lie one of the prin
cipal speakers at this convention.
Other noted men who will address
the state gathering will be Dr. James
A. Francis, pastor of the First Bap
tist Church of Los Angeles; Lyman
L. Pierce, general secretary of the Y.
M. C. A., San Francisco; Lieutenant-
Colonel E. W. Halford, retired officer
of the United States army, and C. H.
Robertson, international Y. M. C. A.
secretary, and scientific lecturer on
China, who lectured to the men of
Stanford last Sunday evening at En
cina Hall.
