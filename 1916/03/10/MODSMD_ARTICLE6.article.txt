# QUINTET CLASHES WITH PACIFICS
## 
### 
Winding up the season's schedule
of the California and Nevada Basket
ball League, Stanford and College of
the Pacific basketball teams clash to
morrow night at Encina gym. This is
the second encounter between these
teams this season, the first resulting
in a victory for the Cardinal.
Some changes have been made in
the varsity lineup, Watson going in

in Blodget's place and Captain
Wheatly playing Caughey's guard.
The five as they take the floor have
been in action together before and
can be expected to show the same
team work they displayed in the ser
ies with California.
The game will start promptly at 8
o'clock. Admission is either by sea
son ticket or twenty-five cents.
