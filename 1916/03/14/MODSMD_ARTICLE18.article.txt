# Marine Laboratory to Open May 22.
## 
### 
Professor .T. O. Snyder announces
that the summer session of the Marine
Laboratory at Pacific Grove will begin
one week earlier than previously an
nounced. The change to Monday,
May 22, is made in the interest of the
students who do not wish to lose the
week between commencement and the
usual day of the opening of the lab
oratory.
