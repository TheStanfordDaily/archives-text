# Mrs. Wilbur to Attend Conference Stunt Party
## 
### 
Every Stanford woman Is urged to'
attend the Conference Stunt Party to
morrow at 4:30, in the Women's Club
House. Mrs. R. L. Wilbur will be
present to meet the women of the
University for the first time inform
ally. As an alumna of Stanford,
it is Mrs. Wilbur's desire to become
acquainted with the present problems
and to know every one of the five
hundred individually.
Miss Elizabeth TUickingham will
read; dancing stunts are being plan
ned by Miss Florence Burrell, and
there are to be musical numbers in
troducing local talent.
