# Latest Song-Hits For Band Dance on April 21
## New Date Accepted Will Allow Track Athletes To Participate 
### 
In order that track men will be
able to attend the Hand Dance which
was originally scheduled for April
14, the date for that event has now
been definitely set for Friday, April
21, in Encina gymnasium.
Receipts from the dance will help
to defray expenses for the proposed
trip of the band to the southern part
of the state, and by means of tickets
a thorough canvass of the campus
will be made to insure a large at
tendance at the hop. The very latest
of song hits will be procured and
will be rendered by the entire band
of forty-five pieces. As was the case
at the track dance the floor will be
waxed.
