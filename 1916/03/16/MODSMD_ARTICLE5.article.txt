# Topographic Geology Course Offered at Summer Session
## 
### 
A two months' course in field and
topographic geology is to be offered
this summer by the Geology and Min
ing department, under Professors
Willis, Folsom, and Tolman. The
work will rover part of the Santa
Inez range near Lompoe, north of the
Santa Barbara Channel. The class is
open to a limited number of students,
outside of Geology majors, provided
they have had Geology I, and Civil
Engineering 4a. Any who wish to
join should consult Professor Willis
at an early date.
