# Chancellor Jordan Will Go East Tomorrow
## 
### 
Chancellor David Starr Jordan
leaves tomorrow morning at 8 o'clock
for New York City, where lie will at
tend a meeting of the Simplified
Spelling Hoard, which convenes in
that city the first ot' next month. Dr.
Jordan will speak enroute at several
cities on topics connected with world
peace .
Chancellor Jordan has recently
submitted his contribution on Inter
national Conciliation to the execu
tive committee of the American
Branch of the Central Organization
for a Durable Peace, of which he is
a member. The theme of the contri
bution is "No annexation or transfer
of territory shall be made contrary
to the interests and wishes of the
population concerned."
