# Negro Jubilee Quintet Sing in Union Friday
## 
### 
Placards advertising the appear
ance of the Tuskegee Institute Jub
ilee Quintet. Friday evenins, in the
Union, are on display on the Quad.
The singers from the normal and in
dustrial school founded by Booker T.
Washington, will give a program of
plantation melodies, negro folk-songs,
and dialect readings at Stanford, un
der the auspices of the campus Y. M.
0. A. No admission will be charged,
although a silver offering will be
taken.
The Tuskegee quintet has earned
the reputation of being one of the
best aggregations of negro jubilee
singers now touring America.
