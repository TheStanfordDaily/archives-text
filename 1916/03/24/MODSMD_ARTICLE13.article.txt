# U.C. PROFESSOR SUNDAY EVENING Y.M.C.A. TALKER
## 
### 
Dr. Joel H. Hildebrand, professor
of Chemistry at the University of
California, will address the men of
Stanford University, Sunday evening,
in Encina Hall, at 7:30, under the
auspices of the campus Y. M. C. A.
Dr. Hildebrand was asked to speak
at the University, at the request of
Stanford men who had heard him at
the University of California. He is
described by them as a forceful, con
vincing, interesting lecturer.
A graduate of the University of
Pennsylvania, Dr. Hildebrand rowed
stroke on his varsity crew for three
years. He holds membership in Phi
Delta Theta, Sigma Xi and Phi Beta
Kappa.
