# Exposition Soccer Medals Awarded.
## 
### 
Medals awarded to the Stanford
soccer players for their supremacy in
the Exposition Soccer League, have
been given to the following fourteen
men: Royle, Reynolds, McClung, Erb,
Carroll, Ring, Henderson, McMahon,
Williams, N. Ford, S. Adams, Kester,
Dutton, W. Adams. The Exposition
League has also voted medals to its
president, H. W. Maloney of Stanford,
and its secretary, Edgar Pomeroy of
Olympic Club, in recognition of their
services throughout the season.
