# FORMER DAILY EDITOR SUCCUMBS TO CUPID
## 
### 
The engagement of Henry L. Loud,
'13, to Miss Marguerite Knox, '12, has
just been announced. Loud was form
erly editor of the Daily Palo Alto, and
a big man on the campus. He was a
member of Press Club and Encina.
Miss Knox was a member of Alpha
Omicron Pi. Both live in Los An
geles.
Miss Irene Clark, '14, has announced
her engagement to Alex Ormond. |
Miss Clark is a Chi Omega. Ormond
graduated from Washington in '06,
where he was a Sigma Chi.
