# PREP SCHOOL TRACK MEN IN C.I.F. MEET TOMORROW
## 
### 
Athletes to the number of one hun
dred and twenty-five, representing
high schools from the northern sec
tion of the state, will compete Satur
day afternoon on the Stanford oval in
the second annual track meet of the
north coast section of the California
Interscholastic Federation. The first
event is scheduled to start promptly
at 1 o'clock, so that the meet will be
over by the time the California-Stan
ford freshman baseball tussle com
mences.
Among the northern high schools
which will be represented by teams
next Saturday axe Palo Alto, San
Jose, San Mateo, Oakland Technioal,
Berkeley, Tamalpais, and Santa Rosa.
Many fast races are expected.
