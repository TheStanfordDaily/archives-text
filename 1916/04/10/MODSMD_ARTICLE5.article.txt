# WILL ELECT THREE MEN TO CONFERENCE
## 
### 
The election of two members to the
Men's Conference to represent those
who live off the campus, will take
place Wednesday. All men who live
in Palo Alto or any other off-campus
place, are requested to meet in room
214 at 1:30 o'clock. Wednesday after
noon, when they will nominate and
choose these two representatives.
On Thursday the campus men who
are unidentified with any organized
group will meet at the same time and
place to elect their one member to
the Conference.
