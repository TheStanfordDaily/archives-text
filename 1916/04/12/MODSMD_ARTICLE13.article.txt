# Gym Teams to Contest
## 
### 
Arrangements have been completed
for a gym-team show to be held be
tween Stanford and California on
Thursday, April 20. Events in the
contest will consist of work on the
horizontal bars, parallel bars, side
horse, rings and tumbling. Exhibi
tion boxing matches conducted on the
same plan as former bouts held here
will also be on the program except
that no decisions will be given.
