# STANFORD LIBRARY RECEIVES RARE SET
## 
### 
Stanford University Library is the
recipient of the London Gazette, from
December 27, 1808, to December, 1891,
and from January, 1900, to December,
1909, bound in three hundred and five
volumes. This newspaper is the of
ficial ministerial organ of publicity
of the British government, and is one
of the oldest dailies in the world. It
is also one of the most reliable
and authoritative papers published,
and contains valuable material re
lating to foreign relations, interna
tional law, general law and constitu
tional history, including much re
lating to Canada, Mexico, and the
United States.
