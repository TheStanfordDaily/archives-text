# Stanford vs. U. of W. For Poughkeepsie Race
## 
### 
University of Washington is today
in receipt of an invitation from the
Intercollegiate Rowing Association to
row at Poughkeepsic. Following the
permission granted by the Washing
ton faculty earlier in the week, to
make the eastern trip, it is now ex
pected that Stanford will get another
chance at the Purple and Gold, on the
Hudson, June 17th.
