# The 						Sauce Box
## MINNOWS. 
### 
Prank Tagawa, in the Union Cafe,
diesn't seem to realize it, but one of
his bean-servers discovered that he is
doing a Russian business.
Come to think of it, under the sys
tem that will prevail next semester,
a man will find it impossible to do
himself credit in the gym or on the
athletic fields.

scheduled for the lake, along comes
an inventor with a suggestion that a
woman's jilting contest on the Quad
would arouse no end of interest.

far-sightedness are already specu
lating on the dates of final examina
tions, so they cau write to her at
home and fix up that first night per
formance.

They expect a man to trust them
when he doesn't even trust his own
memory.

Charlie Meyer calls him by his first
name his life-long ambition will be
realized.

age. Instead of a dark brown taste,
however, the effect is rather royal
purple.
