# Will Tell Story of War Prison Camps
## J. E. Sprunger, Back From Nine Months' Service in Europe, to Address Students 
### 
J. E. Sprunger, state secretary of
the California Y. M. C. A., who has
just returned from nine months' ser
vice in European prison camps, will
be the speaker at the Sunday evening
Encina meeting. Always popular as
a speaker. Mr. Sprunger has been in
constant demand since his recent re
turn to this country,, and the Stan
ford association counts itself for
tunate to obtain his presence at such
an early date.
In his address to the men of the
University, Sunday evening, he will
describe his work in Europe, and give
a vivid picture of conditions in the
detentions camps scattered over the
warring nations.
