# J.E. Sprunger Will Address Men at Encina Hall Meeting
## 
### 
J. E. Sprunger, who will address the
men of the University in Encina club
room at 7:30 Sunday evening, has but
recently returned from nearly a year's
service in European war prison camps.
He is state secretary for the Y. M. C.
A., and went to Europe for the double
purpose of studying prison conditions
and of rendering a service to the men

in the detention camps. At Sunday
evening's meeting, which will be con
ducted by the Stanford Y. M. C. A.,
Mr. Sprunger will tell of bis experi
ences and observations while in the
warring countries.


.1. K. Sprunwr, State Y.JI.C.A. Secretary
