# Stanford Wins Six Tennis Matches From Alamedans
## 
### 
Stanford tennis players went into
the lead in the tournament play of
this section of the Northern California
Tennis League, Saturday, when they
won all six of the matches against
the Alameda Club. Twelve matches
were scheduled, the teams to meet
on the Encina courts on Sunday. The
Alameda Club found it impossible to
make the trip on Sunday, so four of
their players came down on Satur
day to try to make up for the re
mainder of the defaults.
Clover, Hawks, Hutchinson and
Wolford won both the doubles, while
these same four racquet experts won
all the singles matches. Captain
Hutchinson announces that the men
who will play against the University
of California next Friday and Satur
day will be announced tomorrow.
