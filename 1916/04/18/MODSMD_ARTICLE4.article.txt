# Professor Franklin Badly Burned in Lab Explosion
## 
### 
Professor E. C. Franklin of the
Chemistry department was seriously
injured early this afternoon, when a
large flash of gasoline exploded in
the organic chemistry laboratory.
Nearly all of the skin on his face and
the lower portions of his limbs was
burned off. and in all probability
grafting will have to be resorted to
in order to supply the lost cuticle.
The accident occurred while Pro
fessor Franklin was engaged in orig
inal research work on the vapor pres
sure of ammonia. Not only will he be
confined to the hospital for some time
as a result of the explosion, but ap
paratus on which he has spent months
of thought and labor has been de
stroyed.
