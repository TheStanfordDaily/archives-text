# VARSITY WOMEN WIN TENNIS TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Women on the California tennis
bleachers were given a great surprise
Saturday when the Stanford women
walked away with the Intercollegiate
Tennis Tournament by taking three
out of the five matches played.
In the first singles. Miss Maud Low
ell, '16, defeated Miss M. Arendt, 6-0,
6-1; in second singles, Miss Ethel Lee,
*19, defeated Miss E. Beall, 4-6, 6-2,
8-6; in the third singles Miss Tucker
defeated Miss Madeline Turner, '16,
6-2, 6-3; in first doubles Miss Lowell
and Miss Lee (S.) defeated Miss
Arendt. and Miss Beall 6-1, 6-3; and
in second doubles Miss Marjorie Hygh
land and Miss Beatrice Goeberding
(C.) defeated Miss Ellen Andrews, '16,
and Miss Edna Hayward, sr., 4-6, 6-4,
8-6.
