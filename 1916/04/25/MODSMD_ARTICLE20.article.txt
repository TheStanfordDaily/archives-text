# FINAL CAMPUS DANCE COMBS OFF SATURDAY
## 
### 
In announcing the program for Sat
urday night's hand dance, Manager
.T. V. Wood, *16, states that the third,
sixth, ninth anil twelfth dances will
he waltzes, while the remainder will
he one-steps. The musical program
shows that the very latest dance hits
have heen secured.
Programs and tickets for the final
hand dance of the year are now on
sale at, the Bookstore. Adequate car
service will he provided both from
the campus and from Palo Alto.
