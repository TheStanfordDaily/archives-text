# La 						Liana Elects Officers
## 
### 
Miss Agnes Boezinger, '17, was
chosen president of La Liana for next
year, at the annual election held this
noon. The other officers of the club
will Jje Miss Dorothy Kelly, '18, vice
president; .Miss Abby Paulsen, '18,
treasurer; Miss Anna Cottell, '18. sec
retary; .Miss Mabel Miller, '17, senior
representative; Lorraine Brown, '18,
junior representative, and Frances
Holnmn, '19, freshmen representative.
