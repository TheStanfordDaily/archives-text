# Rifle Club Meet Saturday.
## 
### 
Members of the Rifle Club will hold
their second shoot of the semester
tomorrow at Alta Mesa range, at
which time they will compete for the
club championship. Those qualifying
in this match will shoot with the
members qualifying last Sunday for
the N. R. A. medal offered to the club.
All wishing to shoot tomorrow should
meet at room 263 at 8 o'clock, each
providing his own lunch.
