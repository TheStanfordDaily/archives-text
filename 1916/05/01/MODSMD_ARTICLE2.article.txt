# Everybody's Quad to Be Out Friday
## 
### 
Everybody's Quad, with the josh
department overflowing with "dope"
and taking up a large portion of the
book, will appear early Friday morn
ing. The secrets of the beauty page
have not as yet been divulged to the
anxious public, but needless to say, it
will contain much of the feminine
charm of the "Farm."
A steel photo engraving of Dr. Wil
bur, to whom the Junior year book is
dedicated, has been sent from the east
expressly for the frontispiece. The
1917 book celebrates the decennial an
niversary of the big treinblor of 1908.
Many of the finest views obtainable of
the Quad before and after the earth
quake are to be used in this sec
tion.
One of the new features will be the
small cuts which will be included in
the margins of the Senior Honor Roll.
Characteristic poses of the members
of the faculty will also be found in
this department.
