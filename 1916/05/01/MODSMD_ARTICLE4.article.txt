# When in Doubt Ask Buford Williams
## 
### 
One may not like publicity, but
when one will do newsy things, one

can hardly expect
to stay out of
print for long.
Buford Williams
—he who queens
houses, attends af
ternoon teas (see
announcement col
umn) and "subs"
at Roble —is again
in the limelight.
Buford queens

early to avoid the rush, so he rises to
walk up the Row at 9:00 instead of
10:00—(the proper time to start pic
nics—j. To be sure, he wonders that
the maidens at 12 Lasuen have not
yet arisen. Hut he isn't daunted —not
Buford! He has risen for a picnic
and a picnic it will be. So he tries
the first sorority on the phone direc
tory, and finds a maid to his liking.
Buford's an authority on queening at
odd times —information headquarters
—room 7, Encina.
