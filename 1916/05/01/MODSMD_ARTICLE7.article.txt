Ray Wakefield of the D. P. A.
Has caused us great perplexity,
Because he hates publicity
And hid with great security,
His picture, from the sheet today,
Because he hates publicity,
Ray Wakefield of the D. P. A.
To Thee we dedicate this sheet
Ray Wakefield of the D. P. A.
We truly think it most discreet
To Thee to dedicate this sheet.

meet,
And for this cut we'll have to pay,
To Thee we dedicate this sheet,
Ray Wakefield of the D. P. A.
