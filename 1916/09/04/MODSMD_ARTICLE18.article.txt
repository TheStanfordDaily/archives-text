# Mrs. Wilbur Entertains.

### 
Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur entertained
the Advisory Hoard and Cabinet of
the Young Women's Christian Asso
ciation this afternoon at her home in
Palo Alto.

may be obtained at the Union, or at
the Registrar's Office.
