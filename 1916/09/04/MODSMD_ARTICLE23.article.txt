# Y.M.C.A. and Y.W.O.A. OPEN YEAR'S SOCIAL ACTIVITIES
## 
### 
Pick out something to steer toward
at the beginning of a college career
was emphasized by Dr. Wilbur in his
talk to the new men of the University
at the Y. M. C. A. stag, Friday eve
ning. Besides being advised the first
year men were "put on the boat" as to
campus organizations.
Members of the Y. M. C. A. and Y.
W. C'. A. joined in a reception to all
new students in the gymnasium Sat
urday evening. Persons entitled to
prizes, but failing to receive them are
requested to call at the Y. M. C. A.
office in the Union.
