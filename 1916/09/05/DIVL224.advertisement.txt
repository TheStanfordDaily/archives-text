A Fable For You
"Always be a perfect lady," read Co-Edna in
her mother's letter. 'Be particular about your
friends, and associate with only the best."
"Mother is right," murmured Co-Edna, remem
bering where her monthly check came from.
Whereupon Co-Edna went into"Sticky" Wilson's
like a perfect lady, struck up a friendship with
Co-Ed Chocolates, and resolved to associate with
them, "The Candy with a College Education."
