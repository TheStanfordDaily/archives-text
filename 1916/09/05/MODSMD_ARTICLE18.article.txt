# Stanford Talent at Carmel.
## 
### 
Stanford talent was prominent at
Carrnel this summer, when the Forest
Theater plays were produced and im
portant parts in them taken by Stan
ford students. Glenn Hughes, '10, was
engaged as producer. Gordon Davis,
'18, played the lead in the big play,
"Yolanda of Cypress," and had an im
portant part in the children's play,
"The Piper," in which play, also, Miss
Alice McDougall, '16, carried away
honors. Arthur Cyril, who coached
the Masquers' plays last semester, also
took part in both productions. Miss
Dorothy Egbert, '10, and Miss Velona
Pilcher, '18, took part in"The Piper."
