The yapping of the gasoline engine
used for concrete mixing in front of
the Quad, sounded infernally like a
motor boat plowing through rough
seas, today. As a result, two women
attending classes in the English build
ing suffered from acute sea-sickness
Men who yap-yap like governor
controlled stationary gas engines are
often found in places of learning. But
they are not wanted, even if they are
good mixers.

as she observed the wooden floor being
constructed on the English corner of
the Quad: "Well, Lucy, it's about
time they gave us a place to dance
between classes!"

woman?" asked the Rev. Gardner at
the recent assembly. Evidently yes!
