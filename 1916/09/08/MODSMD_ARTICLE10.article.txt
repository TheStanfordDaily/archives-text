# Dr. and Mrs. Wilbur "At Home" Tomorrow
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur will be at home to all members
of the faculty tomorrow afternoon
from 3 to 6, at their residence, 1201
Rryant street, Palo Alto,. The af
fair is especially for the benefit of
the new faculty members, as it is de
sired that they become acquainted
with professors and instructors of all
departments. Mrs. Leonard W. Ely
will sing.
All new students will be entertained
by President Wilbur and his wife at
an informal garden party, which will
be given next Friday afternoon, Sep
tember 15, from 4 to 6, at their home
in Palo Alto.
