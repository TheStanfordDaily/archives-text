# Plan Bible Study
## 
### 
As a part of the Y. M. C. A. and Y.
W. C. A. Bible study for the present
year. Professor Stephan Ivan Miller
will give a lecture course on Parables,
every Wednesday evening from 7:15
to 8 o'clock, in the Club House. At
tendance is desired from sophomore,
junior, and senior women.
A discussion course of Christian
questions will be given at the same
hour by Professor Murray.
There will be one freshman class at
Roble Hall, conducted by Miss Pope
and another at Madrona Hall, under
the leadership of Helen Denhart, '16.
These classes will meet on Wednes
day evenings from 7 to 7:45, and will
take up the subject of student stand
ards of action.
