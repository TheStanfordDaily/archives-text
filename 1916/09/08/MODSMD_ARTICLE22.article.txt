# Cosmopolitan Club to Hear Dr. Yamato Ichihashi
## 
### 
Dr. Yamato Ichihashi, instructor in
Japanese history and government, will
address the Stanford Cosmopolitan
Club, at the opening metting of that
organization for the college year, in
the Women's Club House, 7:30, Mon
day evening. A business meeting of
the club will elect new members and
transact other matters of importance.
