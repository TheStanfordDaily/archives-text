##

made to till the vacancies in the Eng
lish department occasioned by the ill
ness of Miss Elizabeth Buckingham.

duct the 8 and 9 o'clock classes; Pro
fessor L. E. Bassett will take the class
at 11 o'clock, and Gordon Davis, '18,
the afternoon classes.
