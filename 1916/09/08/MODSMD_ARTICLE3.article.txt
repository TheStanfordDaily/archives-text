# Racquet Play Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tennis Captain Harbor has an
nounced that the 25 cent fee, which it
is customary for all entrants in the
novice tennis tournaments to pay, will
be due tomorrow at 1:30, when the
tournament is scheduled to start.
Drawings for positions will also be
made at that time. The tournament,
which is the first of a series which
will be held every Saturday, is open
only to novices who are not on the
tennis team. A number of men have
signed up, and some fast play is prom
ised.
