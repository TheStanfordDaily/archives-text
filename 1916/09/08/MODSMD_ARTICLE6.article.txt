# Names Song Judges
## 
### 
Student Body President T. E. Swi
gart, '17, has appointed a committee
to judge songs that are submitted in
competition for the $r> prize offered
by the Associated Students for the
best Stanford song. William Gilroy,
'17, chairman of the committee, will
receive all manuscripts. The other
members are W. 8.-Curtis, '16, and C.
S. Kegley, '18. October 5 is the last
day on which songs will be received.
Although no prize will be given, the
committee would also like to have a
few new yells submitted, from which
to choose for bleacher use.
