# REGISTRATION STILL SHOWS INCREASE
## 
### 
Registration up to Saturday, Sep
tember 9, totaled 1961, according to a
statement issued by Assistant Regis
trar .1. E. McDowell this morning.
Last year there were 1949 students
enrolled at the corresponding time.
This indicates an increase of twelve.
Of this total 613 are new students, as
compared to 643 last year; and 1348
had already matriculated, in contrast
to 1306 in the fall of 1915.
