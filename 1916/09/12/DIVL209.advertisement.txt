'The Student's Friend"
You all smoke —so smoke on us. In the window
of LARKIN'S CIGAR STORE there is a pile of
the best smokes you ever saw —SWEET CAPO
RAL CIGARETTES.
GUESS HOW MANY there are in that pile and
we will present you with 50 SILVER BOYS.
We don't expect you to guess exactly, but be
nearer than the other fellow.
If you are not observing enough to get the first
prize, there are eleven others, the smallest of
which is 400 SWEET CAPORAL CIGARETTES.
Ask Dad, He Knows
