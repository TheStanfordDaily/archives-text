# MISS ABBY WOOD ELECTED CAPTAIN OF WOMEN'S CREW
## 
### 
Women's crew squad have elected
Miss Abby Wood, '18, captain of this
year's crew, and Miss Amelia Ander
son, '19, manager. Miss Wood has
been a member of the 1918 crew for
two years, aiding it to take first place
in the Field Day regatta last year.
Miss Anderson was a member of the
1919 crew last year. The new man
ager will be an ex-oflicio member of
the Board of Control of the Women's
Athletic Association.
