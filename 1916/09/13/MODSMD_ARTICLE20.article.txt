# 1919 Sophomore Play Set for November 3
## 
### 
November 3 will be the date of the
1919 Sophomore play, according to an
announcement made today by the
committee, consisting of D. M. Smith,
chairman, Miss Ruth Taylor, Miss
Christine Weatherby, P. G. Wheal ton,
R. Derby, and M. L. Levy.
F. L. Mathieti, well known for his
work as a dramatic coach at Stan
ford, has been chosen to direct the
play, and tryouts for parts will be
held as soon as arrangements can be
made.
