# FRATERNITIES TO COMPETE FOR CUP
## 
### 
An inter-fraternity basketball league
for the purpose of pro\iding compe
tition between fraternities and to de
velop new material is in the process
of formation. The schedule of games,
which will be started in two weeks,
will be played off during the football
season and act as a preliminary try
out for the regular basketball season.
A handsome trophy will be put up for
competition between the organiza
tions. Every contesting team will
have to put up a deposit which will
be forfeited if they do not appear
for their games.
