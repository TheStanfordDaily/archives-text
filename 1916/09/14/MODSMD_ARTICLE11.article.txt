# Miller Elected Head of Associated Law Students
## 
### 
J. A. Miller, '15, was elected presi
dent of the Associated Law Students
at the meeting of the association, held
yesterday. He won by a slight major
ity over L. C. Stevens. 11. J. Ryan,
'15, was elected secretary.
The meeting was called by Profes
sor Huston for the purpose of elect
ing officers and discussing future
plans. A committee was appointed to
arrange for a law smoker to be held
soon.
