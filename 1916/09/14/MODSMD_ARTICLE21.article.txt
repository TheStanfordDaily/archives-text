# NESTORIA MEETS.
## 
### 
S. S. Schnetzler, '16, and J. J. Tay
lor, '17, wore successful in supporting
the affirmative of the question: "Re
solved, That the Federal Congress
was justified in passing the Adamson
eight hour law for employees on in
terstate railroads," at the Nestoria
meeting, last night. The negative
team consisted of R. H. Overacker,
'17, and H. A. Frye, '17.
