# President Wilbur to Give Reception to New Students
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Wilbur will en
tertain all new students of the Uni
versity and members of the Men's
and Women's Conference, at an in
formal garden party at their home,
1201 Bryant street, Palo Alto, tomor
row afternoon, from 4 to 6 o'clock,
All new students, no matter whether
freshmen, sophomores or upperclass
men, it is hoped, will avail them
selves of the opportunity to meet the
President and Mrs. Wilbur, as both
are anxious to become acquainted
with those who are new on the Stan
ford campus. Those going from the
campus should take the Waverley
street car.
