# Campus Marksmen to Compete Sunday
## 
### 
By paying a special initiation fee of
one dollar, rifle enthusiasts on the
campus will become members of the
Rifle Club and will be eligible for the
competitive practice, shoot to be held
at Alta Mesa next Sunday morning.
Those interested in the sport have
been asked to meet at the Engineer
ing corner of the Quad at 8 o'clock
on that day. Several meets will be
staged in the near future with the
University of California marksmen
and with northern schools.
