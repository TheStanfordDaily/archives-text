# Freshman Fencing Teams Are Matched With U. C.
## 
### 
When freshmen fencing teams of
Stanford and California meet next
semester, the first contest ever held
between the "babes" of these univer
sities will take place. Miller, instruc
tor of fencing for the Olympic Club,
is to have charge of fencing activity
at California this year. Informal
bouts, besides regular matches, will
be held between men's and women's
teams of the two universities through
out the year. W. H. Maloney will in
struct the women in fencing on Tues
days and Thursdays, from 9 to 11.
