# PROFESSOR L.M. TERMAN TO MAKE SAN QUENTIN SURVEY
## 
### 
Professor L. M. Terman of the Edu
cation department, has been asked by
the State Board of Charities and Cor
rections to make a psychological sur
vey of the San Quentin State Prison,
to be completed by next February.
H. E. Knollin, '13, graduate student
in the Education department, will as
sist in carrying out the examination.
Recently Professor Terman and
Knollin made a comparative study of
the intelligence of 100 hoboes, a large
number of normal business men and
the high school students. Information
was gathered at Palo Alto from the
palatial lobby of Hotel de Gink, the
business houses and the high school.
