# DR. WilBUR TO BE GUEST OF CAILFORNIA ALUMNAE
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur, together with President and Mrs.
Henjamin Ide Wheeler of California,
and President Aurelia Reinhardt of
Mills College, are to be the guests of
honor at a meeting of the California
Alumnae Association, at the Century
Club, in San Francisco, next Satur
day.
