# Third Varsity Defeated
## 
### 
Stanford's third varsity rugby fif
teen was conquered yesterday after
noon by an 8-5 score. The Cardinal
men were the first to make a try,
Greve taking the hall over, the try
being converted. Lick-Wilmerding
succeeded in crossing the line in this
half but failed to convert. In the sec
ond half Lick scored a try, which was
converted, making the final score 8-5
in the visitors' favor. "Ric" Temple
ton officiated as referee.
