# Hughes Club Meets Tonight
## 
### 
Intimate pictures or the boyhood
life of Charles E. Hughes, republican
candidate for President of the United
States, will be given by Professor J.
O. Griffin of the German department,
tonight in the Union, at 7:30, when
the Hughes Club of Stanford Univer
sity holds its organization meeting.
Professor Griffin is a close friend
of the former supreme court judge.
Professor R. L. Green of the Mathe
matics department will also address
the members of the club.
The organization is being formed
here by Tal Smith, manager of the
Union, to boost Hughes in Stanford
and vicinity. Membership is open to
all students and others living in
Stanford. Prominent men will be se
cured from time to time to speak at
the meetings.
