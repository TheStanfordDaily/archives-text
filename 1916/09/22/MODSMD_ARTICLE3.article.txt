# White Ballot Elects Two Junior Officers
## 
### 
Marion Kyle and E. R. Martin were
elected president and treasurer, re
spectively, of the 1918 class by a
white ballot, when nominations closed
this afternoon at 2 o'clock.
The only contested office is that of
secretary, for which Miss Margaret
Hinchcliffe and Miss Mary Therkel
son have been nominated. An elec
tion for this office will be held Tues
day on the Quad. No nomination was
made for vice-president.
Kyle is a member of the Beta Theta
Pi fraternity, Martin belongs to En
cina Club, Miss Hinchcliffe to Roble
Club, and Miss Therkelson is a
Kappa Kappa Gamma.
