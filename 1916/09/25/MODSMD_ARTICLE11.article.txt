# TO LECTURE ON AMBULANCE AID
## 
### 
"Our American Boys in the Euro
pean War" is the title of a lecture to
be given in the Assembly Hall to
night at 8 o'clock by Mr. J. Land
field of the Bohemian Club, on the
work being done behind the French
front by the American Ambulance
Association. Motion pictures of (his
service will illustrate the lecture.
These films were taken by Charles J.
Freeborn, who will be present Mon
day evening.
