# ALVIN S. JOHNSON LECTURES TONIGHT
## 
### 
What promises to be one of the best
lectures of the season will be given
tonight in the Little Theater, by Pro
fessor Alvin S. Johnson of the Eco
nomics department, who will speak
on "Labor and Politics." Professor
Johnson was formerly head of the
Economics department at Stalnford,
but went to Cornell three years ago
to teach Political Science. Last year
he was on leave of absente from Cor
nell, and served as one of the editors
of the "New Remiblic."
