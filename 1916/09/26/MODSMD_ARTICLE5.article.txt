# Famous Bridge Builder to Address Engineering Men
## 
### 
According to word received today by
Professor C. B. Wing of the Engineer
ing department, Dr. J. A. L. Waddell,
widely known as a bridge builder and
structural engineer, will address all
engineering students and others who
may be interested, in the Mechanics
and Materials lecture room, at 3
o'clock tomorrow. His lecture will
be on some phase of engineering
work, and will be valuable to en
gineering students, as Dr. Waddell
has had a large amount of practical
experience, having built the great
Southern Pacific lift bridge at Port
land, Oregon, the bridge which con
nects the two parts of Kansas City,
any many other famous bridges.
