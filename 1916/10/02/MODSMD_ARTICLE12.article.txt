# Strict Training in Today
## 
### 
Training rules go into effect today
at f> o'clock. After that time candi
dates for the rugby squads must cease
rough-housing, must seek his couch
before 11 o'clock, give up the sooth
ing nicotine, and all beverages
stronger than malted milk. Rugby
men will be allowed to participate in
the Training Table dance, held next
Friday, until the 10:30 chimes have
been rung.
