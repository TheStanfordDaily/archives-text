# Wood Bees to Give First Play in Little Theatre
## 
### 
The first dramatic production of
the year, and the first to be given
in the Little Theater, will occur next
Thursday night, when the Wood Bees
present "The Flax," an Irish fantasy
written by Miss Marjorie Driscoll, ex
'l3. Miss Ellen Galpin, '17, is pro
ducing the play, and the members of
fhe cast are Miss Alma Steininger, '17,
F\ W. Brown, '19, and Miss Muriel
Hurdick, '19. The performance will
begin at 7:30, and will not last over
half an hour. The public is invited.
