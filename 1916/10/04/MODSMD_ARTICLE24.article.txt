# Women to Meet U. C. Class Tennis Teams
## 
### 
Permission to play intercollegiate in
tor-class tennis with the women of the
University of California, was given the
Women's Athletic Association, last
evening, at the meeting of the Board
of Directors. The teams will he com
posed of live players, which will give
twenty girls an opportunity to enter
the meet.
Permission was also given the base
ball manager to arrange for outside
games with as many as possible of the
following schools: Fremont high
school, San Jose high school, Mills
College, Lowell high school, Palo Alto
high school, and .Miss Marker's school.
Swimming was officially adopted as
a sport. All girls who have paid their
student body dues are thereby eligible
to enter meets and win their block
numerals.
