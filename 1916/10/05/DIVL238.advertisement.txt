NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that the taxes for the year 1916 will be
come due and payable:
First Installment—Taxes on all personal property, a lien on or
secured by land, and one-half of the taxes on all real property will be
due and payable Monday, October 16th, 1916, and delinquent on Mon
day, December 4th, 1916, at six o'clock p.m., when 15 per cent will be
added to all of said first installment remaining unpaid.
Second Installment—The remaining one-half of taxes on all real
property will be due and payable January Ist, 1917, and delinquent
April 30th, 1917, when 5 per cent will be added to all taxes remaining
unpaid.
Taxpayers may, if they desire to do so, pay the whole tax at one
payment.
For the purpose of receiving Taxes, 1 will he in l'alo Alto, at the
Itnnk of Palo Alto, 011 Wednesday, November 8, Thursday, November
0, Friday, November 10, and Saturday, November 11, 1010, to twelve
o'clock noon;
And at my office in the Hall of Justice Building, cor. St. James and
Market streets, San Jose, California, dally, Sundays and Legal Holi
days excepted, from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m„ to and including Monday, De
cember 4th, 1916, and Monday, April 30th, 1917, at 6 o'clock p.m.
The second installment is payable at my office in the Hall of Jus
tice Building, San Jose.
