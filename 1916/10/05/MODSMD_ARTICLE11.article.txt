# Seven Men Elected to Quadrangle Club
## 
### 
Seven men were elected to mem
bership in the Quadrangle Club last
evening, at the regular meeting of
that body, held at the Breakers Club.
The following are the ones chosen
as members of the organization: R.
Maurer, '17, H. V. Aupperie, '17, T. E.
Swigart, '17, W. R. Wheatly, '17, L. R.
Reynolds, '14, W. F. Noonan, '10, and
G. K. Howard, '17.
