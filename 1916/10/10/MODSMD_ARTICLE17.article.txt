# Economists to Smoke in Union Thursday
## 
### 
Professor M. S. Wildman will wel
come new students of the Economics
department next Thursday evening at
a smoker, which is to be given to in
structors and majors of that depart
ment by the Economic Club in the
Union. Professor Whitaker will
speak on the "Dollar Exchange," and
Professor Miller is to discuss general
questions of economic interest. Sev
eral students are scheduled to give
brief talks. Scholarship requirements
arc to be made clear, and any ques
tions concerning the department's new
policy are to be given attention.
Music and "eats" are promised, and
a general good time is guaranteed.
The smoker will begin at 7:30.
