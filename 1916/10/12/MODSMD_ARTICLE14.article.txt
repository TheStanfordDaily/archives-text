# ECONOMICS SMOKER AT UNION TONIGHT
## 
### 
Economic majors will meet their
professors at the Union tonight at
7:30 in the annual smoker given un
der the auspices of the Economics
Club. Professor M. S. Wildman, head
of the department, will welcome the
students. He will be followed by Pro
fessors Whitaker and Miller in short
talks. Professor Alvin S. Johnson
will speak on a topic of current in
terest .
Music will be furnished by local
musicians and eats and smokes will
be in order. A. .T. Hettinger will call
the smoker to order at 7:30.
