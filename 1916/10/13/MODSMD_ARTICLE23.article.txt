# U.C.-Cardinal Women Will Cross Raquets
## 
### 
An inter-class intercollegiate tennis
tournament will be held Friday and
Saturday, November 24 and 25, be
tween Stanford and University of
California women. Each class team
will be composed of five members,
and one single and two doubles
matches will be played to decide the
series.
At present tournaments are being
run off on the Roble courts to decide
upon the players. The winner of first
place will play in the singles and the
other four players on the team will
compete in the doubles against Cali
fornia. The HO girls taking part in
the Roble tournament are causing
close competition for places.
