# MEMBERSHIP LIMITED IN CIRCLE "S" SOCIETY
## 
### 
Limitation of membership in Circle
"S" Society lias been effected by the
members of that organization. Wear
ers of the Circle S of the first class,
given only for soccer and tennis,
will hereafter automatically become
members, while other members will be
chosen from wearers of the Circle S
of the second class, given for minor
sports such as swimming, fencing, and
boxing. Prior to this all wearers of
the Circle S, whether first or second
class, have automatically become
members.
A motion was also passed to recom
mend to the Executive Committee that
the point winners in the cross-country
run against California he awarded a
Circle S of the second class.
