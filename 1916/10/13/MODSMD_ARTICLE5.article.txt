# Sixteen Men Start On Training Table
## 
### 
Sunday at 12 o'clock will see the
start of the varsity football training
table at the Phi Kappa Psi house with
sixteen men named on the list to
start. It is the intention of Coach
Brown and Trainer Reynolds to add
more men in a few days until the
list numbers about 22.
Men named to start on the table
Sunday, are Captain Braden, Carroll,
Kester, Scott, Henry, Winham, Pet
tingill, Johnson, Halsted, Walker,
Wark, Long, Lachmund, Swigart, Tem
pleton and Pelouze. Of this number,
six are veterans of last year and one
of the previous year.
