# Friday Is Last Day for Big Game Applications
## 
### 
Time for the filing of student ap
plications for tickets to the Stanford-
Santa Clara rugby big game has been
extended to Friday by Graduate Man
ager E. C. Behrens. After Friday
alumni will be allowed to apply for
seats. The general public will get
their first chance at big game seats
November 1. Student tickets will not
be issued until after October 30.
