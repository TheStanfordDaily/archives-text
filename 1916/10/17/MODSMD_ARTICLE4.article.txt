# FOUR MEN ADDED TO FOOTBALL TRAINING TABLE---ONE DROPPED
## 
### 
F. C. Sample, '16, R. R. Coleman,
'18, F. K. Murray, '17, and L. R.
Chandler, '18, have been added to the
varsity training table by football
coach Floyd Brown. The men moved
to the training quarters at the Phi
Kappa Psi house last night, and
bring the list up to nineteen, \V. H.
Henry being dropped from the table
on account of the dreaded faculty axe.
Coach Brown has made Henry the
official coach of the freshmen fifteen,
for the remainder of the season. The
frosh have a schedule to complete,
and Henry, not being eligible to the
varsity, will spend his time teaching
the "babes."
Both Santa Clara and Stanford are
now enforcing strict training regula
tions in an effort to fifteens
in perfect shape when the date of the
Big Game comes around. lie Rour
veau has been added to the Mission
Town list of training table men,
bringing their total number up to
nineteen also.
