# Hot Showers Promised in Encina Gym Immediately
## 
### 
Hot showers will be possible in a
few days as workers are now install
ing a new heater. The heater, which
cracked last summer, has remained
out of order since the beginning of
the semester, and glacial showers have
created a great deal of discomfort.
Recent agitation has been effective
in waking authorities to the realiza
tion of the need, and Stanford ath
letes will welcome the change.
