# 1920 Meeting Tomorrow to Plan Big Game Bonfire
## 
### 
A freshmen meeting will be held in
room 211 of the Economics building
tomorrow, to make the first arrange
ments for the "Big Game" bonfire.
President Frank Sloman will appoint
a committee to take charge of the
work and will explain what is ex
pected of each man in building and
guarding the fire. The meeting will
be called to order promptly at 1
o'clock and every freshman is ex
pected to attend.
