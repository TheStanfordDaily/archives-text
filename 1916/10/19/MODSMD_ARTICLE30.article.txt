# Stanford Varsity Soccer Men Play Allies Saturday
## 
### 
Saturday's soccer attraction on the
local field will be staged by the var
sity and the Allies, and will be played
at 10:30, so as not to interfere with
the rugby schedule.
The lineup as announced by H. W.
Maloney, will be slightly shifted from
last Saturday's personnel, and in
cludes:
Williams, goal; W. Adams, right
back; Eyer, left back; McClung, right
haif; Roney, center half; Westen
haven, left half; Hawley or Drury,
outside right; McMahon, inside right;
Farrell, center'forward; S. Adams, in
side left; Weller, outside loft.
The Stanford forwards who will
compose half of the All Star team,
are:
Hawley or Drury, outside right;
Winters, inside right; Quimby, center
forward; Levi, inside left; Kerr or
Say, outside left.
