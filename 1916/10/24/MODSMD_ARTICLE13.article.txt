# Pumpkin Pie and Coffee for "500" Basket Supper
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Women's
Club will hold their first get-together
in the club bouse, Thursday night,
from 6 to 8 o'clock. All members are
urged to bring their supper, and hot
coffee and pumpkin pie will be served
by the Association directors. Enter
tainment will begin promptly at 0
o'clock, so as to allow nil who attend
to keep previously made engagements
for the evening.
