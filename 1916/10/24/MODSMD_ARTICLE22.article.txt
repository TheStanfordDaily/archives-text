# Women Fill Vacancy in Conference by Election
## 
### 
Miss Alice dc Bernardi, sr., was
elected to the Women's Conference,
last Friday. Miss de Bernardi was
chosen as the representative from the
Language department, and will fill the
vacancy left by the departure of Miss
Anita Allen. In accordance with the
regular method of election to Con
ference, only the Language majors
were allowed to vote, and only seniors
were eligible. Miss de Bernardi is a
member of Madrono Club.
