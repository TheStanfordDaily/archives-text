# Women's Pool to Be Closed Nights Unless More Swim
## 
### 
Dr. C. I). Mosher announces that
the women's swimming pool is being
kept open every evening from 8 to
9:30. A record is .being kept of the
number of women swimming at this
time, and unless more are able to
come out than did last week, it will
be impossible to keep the pool open.
