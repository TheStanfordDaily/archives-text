# M.E. and E.E. Societies Combine to Give Smoker
## 
### 
M. E. and E. E. societies join to
morrow evening in giving the big
smoker of the year for all members
of the two departments, at 7:30, in
tho Union. Chairman VV. F. Durand,
head of the department, will introduce
the following speakers: Professor G.
H. Marx, Professor L. M. Hoskins,
Professor Tl. .T. Ryan, and Dr. .T. M.
Stillman.
Good eats and no fees are features
of the evening.
