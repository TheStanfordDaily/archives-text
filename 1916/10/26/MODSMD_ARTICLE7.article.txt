# Second Ice Hockey Workout on Sunday
## 
### 
Stanford ice artists will hold their
second workout with a practice game
at Tait's Winter Garden, Sunday af
ternoon, from 112) to 6. Enough men
are expected to make the journey to
enable the formation of two Cardinal
teams.
Although the rink furnishes equip
ment, the hockey aggregation are
urged to bring their own skates, if
possible, in addition to clothing suit
able for practice.
