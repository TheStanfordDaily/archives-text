# California Coaches to Speak at Encina
## 
### 
Tien Cherrington and Harry Kins
man, Y. M. C. A. secretaries from the
University of California, and athletes
of ability, will speak before men of
the campus in Encina Hall, Sunday
night, at 7 o'clock, under the auspices
of the Young Men's Christian Asso
ciation.
Kingman is an assistant football
coach at California, and a former first
sacker and pitcher for the New York
Americans in the major league.
Cherrington is already known to
Stanford men, having been at Stan
ford a number of times. He is head
of the University 01 California Y. M.
C. A., and also coaches both basket
ball and football teams at the Uni
versity.
