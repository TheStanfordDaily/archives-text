### 
Said Joe to Margaret:
"Let's take dinner somewhere before the
Sophomore Play."
"Lovely, ' she said. "But honestly, there's
no place that / like around here."
"How about Tabasco's?"
"My, no! His prices are outrageous: /
wouldn't go there with anyone / liked very
much."
"How about the Union?"
"Men only, my dear!"
"We forgot—THE INN!"
"Good! Let's go there!"
And they did—that very evening.
And were glad of it.
B. P. LAUSTEN, TAILOR
