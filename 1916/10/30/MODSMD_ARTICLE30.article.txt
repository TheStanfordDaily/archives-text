# UNIVERSITY TO INSTALL ELECTRIC FIRE ALARM
## 
### 
The University is taking steps to
install an electric fire alarm system
on the campus to replace the system
now employed by which fire alarms
are sounded on the power house
whistle on telephone notification.
The University will also purchase an
automobile chemical engine to en
large its fire equipment. The present
chemical engines are pulled to fires

tomobiles.
