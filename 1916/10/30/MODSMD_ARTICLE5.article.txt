# Coach Mathieu Makes Sword and Sandals
## 
### 
Election of Frank Mathieu, of San
Francisco, to honorary membership in
Sword and Sandals society, and his
appointment as producing director,
were announced by Buford Williams,
head of the dramatic organization,
yesterday. Frank Mathieu, formerly
well known on the stage, has acted in
the capacity of coach for amateur
talent productions in and around San
Francisco for several years. He is a
member of the Bohemian Club and
Family Club of that o'ty and the
Lamb's Club of New York.
Mathieu, at Stanord, has been con
nected with a large percentage of re
cent dramatic successes.
