FRESH AND GOOD THINGS
"Fine, fresh days these are," observed Co-Edna
as she strode across the fields, "for fresh and good
things."
Co-Edna knew of other things fresh and good
besides the sunny outdoors.
"Take. Co-Ed Chocolates, take Wilson ices, take
Wilson creams," she continued, ' They're fresh and
good, and will be doubly so when they are made
under the best of conditions in the new Wilson
factory."
