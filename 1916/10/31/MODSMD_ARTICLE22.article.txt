# Brown and Bronson Named as Big Game Timekeepers
## 
### 
Dave Brown, '97, and Roy Bronson
have been selected by the coaches
and managers of Stanford and Santa
Clara to act as timekeepers for the
Big Game on November 11. Brown
has held the clock in all big games
for Stanford for many years, and a big
game would not really be a big one
without him as an official. Roy Bron
son, representing Santa Clara, is a
former graduate manager of that uni
versity.
