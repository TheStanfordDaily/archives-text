# "Joys" of Soccer Men Ended Training Starts Tomorrow
## 
### 
Varsity and freshmen training rules
for the soccer elevens will go into
force tomorrow in preparation for the
contests with California. Coach TT. W.
Maloney announces Ibat there will be
a ban on smoking, drinking, and all
dissipation. The men must be in bed
by 11 o'clock, also.
