# "Drys" Want Men to Watch Votes Counted
## 
### 
Men to watch the count at the polls
at the coming general election are
wanted by the California Campaign
Federation, which through the agency
of Secretary .T. B. Watson of the Y.
M. C. A., is offering free fare to the
city and meals while there to a lim
ited number of men of the University.
Six hundred have already signed up
as watchers from the University of
California.
