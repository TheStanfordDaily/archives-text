# MEN NAMED TO SETTLE FUTURE S. C. RELATIONS
## 
### 
Graduate Manager E. C. Behrens, G.
K. Scovcll, '17, and G. K. Howard, '17,
were appointed by Student Body
President T. E. Swigart, '17, to serve
as a committee to meet with Santa
Clara officials relative to the drawing
up of an agreement for future ath
letic relations, following last night's
meeting of the Executive Committee.
The minutes of the meeting follow:
President Swigart in chair.
Bihlman absent.
M. S. C. That committee to confer
with Santa Clara in regard to future
athletic relations be appointed.
M. S. C. To adjourn.
RTX MAITRER,
Secretary.
