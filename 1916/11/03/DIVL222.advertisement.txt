THE FOXIEST SHOES -
on the Pacific Coast are the Wing
tipped and perforated tans now in
stock at Thoits'. See them 011 your
own feet in the mirror at Thoits'.
They're stout, neat, and the latest
rage in shoes.
3\vo\XS~~
How About Your Shoes?
We Want Your Election Coin:
10 to 8 on Hughes to win
Even money on prohibition
10 to 7 or even money on Santa Clara—
or however you care to wager.
The point is this: put up a
feed as the stake: then enjoy
the steak at Stanford Inn.
