# SANTA CLARANS PAINT BLEACHERS
## 
### 
Santa Clara raiders, taking advant
age of tlie fact that the freshmen
were receiving election returns,
painted the football bleachers and the
stone gates at the entrance to Palm
Avenue with red and white letters,
last night. They were discovered soon
after the damage was done but were
not captured.
Norman Ross, chairman of the lion
lire committee, has asked that every
freshman turn out tomorrow.
