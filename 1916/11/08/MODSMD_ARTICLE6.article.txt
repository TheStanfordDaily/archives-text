# Santa Clarans to Stage Rally in San Jose Tonight
## 
### 
San Jose will be turned over to
Santa Clara students and supporters
tonight for the last big rally at the
Vendome Hotel. The red and white
looters will practice all their songs
and yells and will get up Santa Clara
spit it for the Big Game.
