# Union Plans Dance
## 
### 
The second Union dance of the se
mester will be given in the Union,
November 29, the night before
Thanksgiving. This dance, unlike the
first, will not be free, members' tick
e.s being $1.00 a couple.
in order that the Union may not
be overcrowded the number of tickets
sold will be limited to 00. These will
be sold to the first men signing up
for them. After the list is closed
programs can only be secured by
someone dropping out.
At leant a three-piece orchestra will
furnish music and light refreshments
will be served. The dinner in the
Union Cafe, downstairs, will be dis
pensed with in favor of a lunch served
upstairs.
