# Injured Men Better
## 
### 
The players injured in the Big
Game Saturday are reported to be
doing well. Frank Walker suffered
only a slight concussion of the brain
and it is said that he will be able to
be on the Quad soon. Leslie Wark
was taken to tlie Peninsula Hospital
last night, but it is not as yet known
whether his injured ankle is broken
or not.
The Santa Clara player, Demetrlo
Diaz, will be confined to St. Mary's
Hospital in the city for a few days
