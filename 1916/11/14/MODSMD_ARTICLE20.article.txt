# Wheeler's Secretary Receives U.S. Honor
## 
### 
Clare M. Torrey, secretary to Presi
dent Wheeler of the University of Cal
ifornia, has been appointed special
secretary for Trade Affairs in the
American Embassy to Great Britain.
For the past year Torrey has been
working under the American Commis
sion for Relief in Belgium and had
charge of part of the distribution of
foodstuffs and clothing. He came into
contact with Ambassador Page while
at this work and he received his ap
pointment shortly after.
