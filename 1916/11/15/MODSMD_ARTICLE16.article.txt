# BASKETBALL TO FORE
## 
### 
Basketball seems afforded a rare
opportunity of sailing along into the
front rank as a major sport, from
present indications. With a galaxy
of veterans and an all-star last year's
freshmen quintet to form an initial
nucleus, any pessimistic doubts as 10
the probable strength of the Cardinal
team fade into oblivion. The grad
uate manager is arranging an at
tractive schedule, which includes
every university of importance along
the coast on the roster. Upon the ar
rival of the coach, whose name will
be divulged later, the outlook
has passed far beyond the hope
ful stage, victory smiling towards
Stanford. All that remains to insure
a highly successful season is the ap
proval of a large, enthusiastic body
of basket-shooters as candidates for
honors, aided by loyal and consistent
interest of the student body. Captain
Wheatly and the squad—good luck!
