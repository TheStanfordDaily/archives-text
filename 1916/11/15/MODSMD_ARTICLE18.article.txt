# Miss Spinney Will Read From Euripides Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford students and their friends
will be given an opportunity to hear
Miss Dorothea Spinney in her unusual
presentation of Professor Gilbert Mur
ray's translation of Euripides' "Iphi
genia Among the Taurians," in the
Little Theater, tonight, at 8 o'clock.
No admission will be charged, as
the reading is being given under the
auspices of the Classical Club. Doors
will be closed at 8:05, as those enter
ing late disturb the speaker.
