# Can You Sloganize? Syncopaters Want Name
## 
### 
Are you slogan-minded? Stanford
Union, and more in particular, its
dance committee for the big Union
dance to be staged on the evening
before Thanksgiving, is on the war
path for a catch-phrase, or slogan of
some sort that will be typical of the
superiority of the Union for an eve
ning of syncopation.
The accepted slogan must also dem
onstrate the fact that the Union floor
is known as the best on the campus,
that no other campus organization
can offer equal comforts for between
dance tete-a-tetes, and that the Union
evening will be a Union affair with
a limited attendance.
