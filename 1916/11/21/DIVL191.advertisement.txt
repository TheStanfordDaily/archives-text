The boys are all going to dig
into the ground, and hoe, and
rake, and plant seeds, for the new
Union lawn. How about your
shoes? Are your feet ready? Why
not send those old kicks via the
campus agency to Thoits'—you'll
need them for hiking pretty soon,
anyway. Use the twenty-four hour
repair service.

A DANCE DAINTY
"At the dance," agreed Co-Edna to her part
ner "it's the music, and the decorations, and the
program, that count, to be sure—but—"
"But what?" queried her partner.
"But that box of Co-Ed Chocolates you brought
me was keen. Decorations, and music, and pro
grams may be monotonous be they ever so fine
—but Co-Eds are different. They're a delightful
surprise. I approve of such surprises."
