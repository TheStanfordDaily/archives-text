# Behind His Schedule, Dr. Jordan Postpones Return
## 
### 
Due to return on November 18,
Chancellor Emeritus David Starr Jor
dan now finds himself about ten days
behind his schedule, so that he will
probably not be back at Stanford be
fore November 28. Dr. Jordan left
on September 29, accompanied by H.
V. Aupperle, '17, as private secretary,
on a six-weeks tour across the con
tinent, in which he was to deliver
some 30 lectures in the interest of
peace.
