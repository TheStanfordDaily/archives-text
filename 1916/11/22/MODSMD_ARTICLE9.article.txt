# Fifteen Are Elected to Phi Alpha Delta
## 
### 
Fifteen upperclass law students
have been elected to Phi Alpha Delta,
law honor fraternity, and will be in
itiated this evening.
Following are the neophytes: C. C.
Raker, gr., P. F. Brown, '18, V. D.
Winters, '18, B. F. Davis, '18, T. L.
Dyer, gr., J. K. Maltby, 'Ifi, C. P. Curry,
gr., E. E. Williams, '17, E. R. Mar
tin, '18, R. F. Kahle, '18, L. H. Cassady,
'18, W. T. Evenson, '17, D. Bolinger,
'18, W. K. Phillips, gr., R. S. Ganges
tad, '18.
