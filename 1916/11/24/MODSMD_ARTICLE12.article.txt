# Dr. Wilbur Speaks on Boy Scout Movement
## 
### 
President Wilbur was guest of honor
and principal speaker at a dinner at
the Palace Hotel in San Francisco,
last night, at which steps were taken
to organize a local council for the
direction of Hoy Scout work in the
city. The meeting was attended by a
large number of San Francisco busi
ness men interested in Hoy Scout
work, and there were several other
talks.
