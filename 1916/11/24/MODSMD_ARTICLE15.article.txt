# 1917 Varsity Captain to Be Elected Sunday
## 
### 
Stanford's 1910 varsity meets Sun
day at 10:30, for a Quad picture, at
"Pop" Franklin's studio. At this time,
according to the custom that has been
in practice here for a number of
years, the team will elect a captain
for the 1917 season.
An election and photo was planned
for lust Sunday morning, but owing
to the fact that several of the team
were unable to he there, the taking of
the picture and the balloting were
postponed.
