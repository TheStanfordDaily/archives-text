# Otto Lachmund, Backfield Star, to Captain 1917 Rugby Football Team
## 
### 
0. G. Laclimund, '17, was elected
captain of the 1917 Football Team at
Franklin's yesterday. He played on
the '13, '14, and '16 teams, success
fully holding down the position of
second-five during the intercollegiate
games. He scored Stanford's try in
the Stanford-Santa Clara clash.

The Atistin-Lachmund combination
at iirst and second fives, respectively,
was a great factor in the defeat of
California three years ago, and in the
breaking down of the Santa Clara de
fense last season.
Lachmund is a member of Alpha
Tau Omega fraternity and belongs to
Skull and Snakes.
