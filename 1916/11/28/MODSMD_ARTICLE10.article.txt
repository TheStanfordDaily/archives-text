# Programs Arrive for Union "Dance de Luxe"
## 
### 
Programs for the second Union
Dance, to be given tomorrow evening,
arrived on the campus today and are
being distributed to the holders of
tickets at the Union Cigar Store.
"Dance de Luxe," the title chosen
by the Union dance committee for to
morrow's entertainment, promises to
be more than a name. In addition to
features which contributed to the snc
ccss of the last dance, a novelty will
be, furnished by the C.loe Clnb Quar
tette, which will give a program of
songs during the intermission.
