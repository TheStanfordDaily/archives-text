# President Leaves for San Joaquin County
## 
### 
President Wilbur left the campus
this afternoon at 3 o'clock for Stock
ton, where he is scheduled to make
two addresses before the Stockton
and San Joaquin County Teachers' In
stitute, Wednesday.
In the morning President Wilbur
will speak on "Government," and Avill
talk of "Social Service" at the after
noon session of the organization.
Both addresses will be of a gen
eral and popular nature, and will be
given at Stockton high school. Dr.
Ia M. Terman of the Education de
partment is the other Stanford rep
resentative at the conference.
