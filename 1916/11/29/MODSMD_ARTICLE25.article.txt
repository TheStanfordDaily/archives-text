# Minutes of Executive Committee Meeting
## 
### 
Following are the minutes of the
Executive Committee meeting held
last night:
November 28, 191G.
President Swigart in the chair.
Pelouze absent.
MSC. To award Circle S emblems to
the following men: 13. Williams, 'IG,
R. Farrell, *18, W. K. Eyer, *18, D. E.
Ketchum, '18, G. J. Roney, '19, H. A.
Alderton, *18, D. R. Drury, '20, H. E.
McMahon, gr., W. R. Wheatiy, '18, S.
F. Adams (captain), '17, H. S. Weller,
'17, R. E. Westenhaver, '18, P. R.
Yewell, '17, R. E. McClung, '18, D. A.
Hawley, '17.
MSC. To allow Graduate Manager
Behrens to draw up an agreement for
joining the Pacific Coast Conference.
R. MAURER,
Secretary.
