# Play Champ Court Game on Wednesday
## 
### 
Basketball championship of the
University will be decided Wednes
day afternoon at 4:30 o'clock in tho
gymnasium, when the sophomores
meet the juniors in the final game of
the interclass series.
The contest should prove as close a
one as has been seen on tho floor this
year, as both teams won their pre
vious games by high scores, the sopho
mores running away from the fresh
men and the juniors overwhelming the
seniors.
