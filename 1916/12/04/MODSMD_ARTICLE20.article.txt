Soccer practice for the postponed
game between the varsity eleven and
the All Stars, to be played Saturday,
has been scheduled for Tuesday and
Thursday. The Allies, who are to
meet the Barbarians on Saturday,
also practice on these days. H. W.
Maloney also announces that boxing
and fencing will be resumed, the for
mer Monday, Wednesday and Friday,
4 to 6 p.m., the latter Tuesday and
Thursday, 4 to G p.m.
