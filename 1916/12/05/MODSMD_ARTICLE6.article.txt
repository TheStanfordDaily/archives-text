# Professor Espinosa Will Lecture in Los Angeles
## 
### 
Professor M. A. Espinosa, of the
Department of Romanic Languages,
has been asked to come to Los An
geles to appear before the Teachers'
Institute, which convenes in that city
during the Christmas holidays. He
will make two addresses, one on De
cember 20th, and again on December
22d, his subjects being "Teaching
Spanish in the United States," and
"Spanish Drama."
