# JOHNSTON CHOSEN HEAD OF PHILOLOGICAL BODY
## 
### 
Professor O. M. Johnston, head of
the Romanic Language Department,
was elected president of the Philolog
ical Association of the Pacific Coast
at their annual meeting, which occur
red last Friday and Saturday in San
Francisco. Professor W. A. Cooper
of the German department, was chosen
for secretary of the organization, and
Professor B. O. Foster of the Latin
department was made secretary.
