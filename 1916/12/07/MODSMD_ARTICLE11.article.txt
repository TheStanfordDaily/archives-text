# Stanford Men to Run in Coast Track Meet
## 
### 
Stanford will participate in a Pa
cific Coast track meet for the first
time, when on May 19 a team of ten
men will go to Seattle for the annual
event in the new University of Wash
ington bowl. All other leading Uni
versiites of the Pacific Coast will be
represented at the meet.
Each university will be represented
by teams of the same number of men.
The date for the meet was decided
upon at a meeting in the north, from
which Graduate Manager Behrens re
turned last night.
