# SCIENTISTS' ASSOCIATION WILL MEET AT STANFORD
## 
### 
The Pacific Division of the American
Association for the Advancement of
Science will hold its 1917 meeting at
Stanford, April 4 to 7. With the as
sociation will also convene at that
time a number of societies of the
coast concerned with special fields of
science.
President Emeritus Branner of the
University was recently elected presi
dent of the Pacific Division of the
American Division, and several mem
bers of the Stanford faculty are with
him on the executive committee.
These include Professor E. C. Frank
lin of the Chemistry department, and
Professor V. L. Kellogg of the Ento
mology department at Stanford.
