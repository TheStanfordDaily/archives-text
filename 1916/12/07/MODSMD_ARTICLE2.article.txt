# Stanford Ruggers to Play in Los Angeles
## Michigan and Cardinal Slated for Contests in Canada and Washington 
### 
Prospects of the trip west of the
University of Michigan ruggers ap
pear brighter than ever, but definite
announcement as to whether they will
make the trip or not will be made
within a day or two.
Prospect of Northwest Trip.
While in the north last week, at the
Pacific Coast Conference meeting,
Graduate Manager Behrens, acting for
the Michigan fifteen, completed ar
rangements for two games for the
easterners in Vancouver and one in
Victoria in case the trip materializes.
Behrens also made preparations for a
trip to the north Christmas vacation
a year from now, by the Stanford var
sity. Games will be played in Vic
toria, Vancouver and two other cities
of the northwest, not yet selected, one
of which will probably be Portland.
Varsity Plays In South.
During this coming Christmas va
cation the Stanford varsity will play
in the southern part of the state.
They will play the Michigan stars in
Los Angeles at this time, or in case
that team does not make the trip west
will take on a team of Southern Cali
fornia All Stars.
Prospects of the trip west of the
University of Michigan ruggers ap
pear brighter than ever, but definite
announcement as to whether they will
make the trip or not will be made
within a day or two.
