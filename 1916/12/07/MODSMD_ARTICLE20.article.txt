# English Club Elects Chapman President
## 
### 
Sherwood Chapman, '17, was elected
i president of English Club at the last
1916 meeting of that organization,
held last night. Other officers are:
! Miss Dare Stark, '17, vice-president;
Miss Velona Pilcher, '18, secretary;
M. L. McClintock, '17, treasurer; Miss
Gladys Green, '18, member-at-large,
and Professor J. S. P. Tatlock, faculty
member.
Lady Gregory's "Work House
Ward" was humorously presented by
Professor L. E. Bassett, Instructor G.
F. Evans, and Mrs. G. J. Peirce. An
initiation stunt was given by the five
new members, who presented a highly
diverting satire on the Stanford Book
of Verse and its compilers.
The meeting was held at the home
of Miss Marjory Bailey, Redwood.
