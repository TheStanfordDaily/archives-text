# Name Griffin Major of Officers' Corps
## 
### 
R. A. Griffin, '17, will be created
major of the Stanford Officers' Re
serve Corps, according to announce
ment made today by Major Jens
Bugge. As a result of Griffin's ad
vancement, an extra captaincy will be
vacant, but the man to take his place
will not be announced until the last
drill of the semester.
Announcement was also made by
Major Bugge that several shake-ups
will be made in the battalion. On
Monday, President Wilbur will review
the Stanford Corps at 5 o'clock, in
front of the Faculty Club Mouse.
