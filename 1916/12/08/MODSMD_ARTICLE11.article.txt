# Soccer Season Ends With Saturday Game
## 
### 
But one game remains to be played
before Cardinal soccerites wind up a
season that has been exceptionally
successful. Tomorrow afternoon at 3
o'clock the varsity eleven will line
up against tbe All Star aggregation
composed of former Stanford and Cali
fornia players, on the local turf.
Stanford is at present leading the
league, the winner of which receives
the Hopkins Cup for one year. By win
ning tomorrow's contest the Cardinal
players will hold the cup for the en
suing year.
