# S.S. Schnetzler, '16, President of Nestoria
## 
### 
S. S. Schnetzler, '16, was elected
president of Nestoria debating society
for the coming semester, at the ban
quet of the members of that organiza
tion at the Stanford Inn, Wednesday
evening. Other officers chosen were
H. H. Hoss, '18, vice-president; H.
Shepherd, '19, secretary, and \V. C.
Fundenberg, '19, treasurer.
Nestoria will meet for the last time
this semester next Tuesday evening in
room 12 of the Law department, at
7:30.
