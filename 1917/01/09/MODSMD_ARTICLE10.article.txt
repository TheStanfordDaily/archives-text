# Repairs Completed; Alpha Phis Move In
## 
### 
Repairs on the Alpha Phi sorority
house, which was gutted by fire last
semester, have been completed, and
the house is again ready for occu
pancy. The residence in Palo Alto
which was rented temporarily while
the repairing took place, has been
given up, and the sorority will again
make it's home on the campus.
