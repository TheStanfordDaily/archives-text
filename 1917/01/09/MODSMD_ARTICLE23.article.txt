# Prominent Artist Paints Oil Portrait of Wilbur
## 
### 
President Wilbur's portrait in oil,
which is being painted by Walter I.
Cox, a prominent artist of San Fran
cisco, is now practically completed.
Several sittings have been held dur
ing vacation in Memorial Church,
where the picture is being painted.
Cox is a noted painter in the west,
having recently achieved fame by his
pictures of governors Oddie and
Sparks of Nevada.
