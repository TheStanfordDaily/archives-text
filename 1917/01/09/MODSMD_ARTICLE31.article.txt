# Red Cross Workers to Organize Two Classes
## 
### 
Two courses in Red Cross Work
will be given at the home of Miss
Fairclough, G Cabrillo street, during
the coming semester. A course in
"First Aid," given by Dr. H. R. Stolz,
will cover a period of five weeks, fol
lowed by a series of lectures by a
Red Cross nurse on "Home Care of
the Sick." A fee of three dollars
will be charged for the course, which
is under the supervision of Miss Fair
clough.
