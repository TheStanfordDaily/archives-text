# Newspaper for Women
## 
### 
A new publication, "The Campus,"
lias made its appearance on the quad,
edited by Dorothy Egbert, '16, and
published by women's conference. It
is primarily for the use of the new
women and tells them What's What
and Who's Who. it is to appear only
once a semester, on registration day.
This edition contains a greeting to
the new students by Dorothy Metz, '17,
president of the conference.
