###

Matriculation Test in English, accord
ing to the registrar's announcement
posted this morning. The successful
persons are now not obliged to take
English other than that their depart
ment requires. Others must take the
course in English A. but may be al
lowed to discontinue at the end of a
month if their work is exceptionally
good.
