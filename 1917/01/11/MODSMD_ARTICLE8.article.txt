# Dance to Band Music at Armory, Friday, January 19
## 
### 
Friday, January 19, is the date se
lected for the first band dance of the
semester, which will be held at the
Armory in Palo Alto. The band will
play the latest rag and musical com
edy hits, and the Armory is being
specially fitted up for the affair. Pros
pects for a good musical organization
are unusually bright this year. Ad
mission to the opening dance of the
Semester is one dollar, and tickets will
be put on sale at the bookstore, Mon
day.
