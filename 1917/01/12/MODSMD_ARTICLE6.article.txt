# Hammer and Coffin Takes in Four New Chappies
## 
### 
Hammer and Coffin last night in
itiated into the mysteries and jollities
of its order four stalwart laugh pro
vokers who have consistently adorned
the pages of the Chaparral with copy
in a humorous vein. The initiates who
were admitted at Redwood last night
were D. M. Smith, '19, S. E. Rriggs,
'19, G. W. Hall, '19, and W. M. Rob
erts, '18.
