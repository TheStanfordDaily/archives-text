# Glee Club Tryouts Take Place Tomorrow Evening
## 
### 
Tryouts for vacant positions on the
Glee Club will be held tomorrow eve
ning in room 81, the regular meeting
place of that organization. New tal
ent is needed in all parts, but second
tenors and second basses are especi
ally in demand, and every man with
the potential possibilities of a good
voice is urged to appear. According
to the director, candidates who failed
to make the club last fall should
make another trial.
The club will give several concerts
during this semester, and take one big
trip as a climax of the season.
