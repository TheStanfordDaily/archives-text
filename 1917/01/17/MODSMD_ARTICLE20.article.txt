# Raymond Robins May Lecture at Stanford
## 
### 
Advisability of inviting Raymond
Robins, noted Christian social worker
of the Seventeenth ward in Chicago,
to speak at Stanford during hia tour
of the universities and colleges of the
Pacific coast, was discussed this noon
at a joint luncheon of the cabinets and
advisory boards of the Young Men's
and Young Women's Christian Asso
ciations. If Robins does come he will
probably be at Stanford the first of
March. Robins is prominent in labor
and political circles in Chicago.
Gale Seaman, Y. M. C. A. interna
tional secretary for the Pacific coast,
and Harry O. Hill, Y. M. C. A. college
secretary for California, were the
principal speakers this noon.
