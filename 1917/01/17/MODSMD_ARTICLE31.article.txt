# E.G. MARTIN TO GIVE. SECOND LIQUOR LECTURE
## 
### 
E. G. Martin, professor of Physiol
ogy, will deliver the second of a series
of lectures tomorrow afternoon, in
connection with the new course now
being given in the Education depart
ment, Social Aspects of the Liquor
Problem. Professor Martin will speak
on "Bodily Effects of Alcohol in Gen
eral," while the following week he
| will lecture on the effects of alcohol
on the nervous system.
The course, given at the request of
a number of students, has already
aroused great interest.
