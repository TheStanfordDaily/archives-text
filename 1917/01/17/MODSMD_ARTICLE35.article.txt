# FIRST YEAR MEN MEET; NAME CLASS OFFICERS
## 
### 
L. R. Tilton, who registers in the
Pre-Legal department from Los An
geles, was elected president of the
freshman class at a meeting held to
day. Tilton received 75 votes, against
4ft for J. W. ConPidine. Miss Elizabeth
Husuke is the new vice-president. C.
C. Cowin and .T. T. Murphy are sec
retary and treasurer, respectively.
President Frank Sloman called the
meeting to order and Norman Ross,
chairman of the bonfire committee,
made a report. A motion was passed
to buy Milton Samis a numeral
sweater for the winning of the inter
class cross-country race.
