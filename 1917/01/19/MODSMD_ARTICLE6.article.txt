# Campaign Yields $1900 Pellissier Ambulance Fund
## 
### 
One thousand nine hundred dollars,
two hundred dollars more than was
asked for, has been received by the
men in charge of the Pellissier fund
for an ambulance in the French army.
The original campaign, carried on at
Stanford "and in the city, yielded some
$1700, but since the active work stop
ped $1200 more has come in, making
more than enough for the automobile
and driver.
