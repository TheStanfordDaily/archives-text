# Norman Ross Tears Muscle But Will Go to Honolulu
## 
### 
Norman Ross, ex-'2O, the sensa
tional swimmer, who was intending to
stay out of the University six months
to swim in meets in Honolulu and
the East for the Olympic Club, was
injured in diving off the springboard
into the sand, Saturday, in the gym
nasium.
A ligament was torn, but lie was
: discharged from the Peninsula hos
pital today at noon, on crutches, and
; there is little doubt but that he will
be in condition to make the trip as he
had planned. Ross will leave for
Honolulu to swim in the Mid-Pacific
Swimming carnival in February.
