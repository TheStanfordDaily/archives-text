# Cap and Gown Women to Drop Dignity Tomorrow
## 
### 
If on the quad tomorrow a damsel
accosts you, and speaks to you, and
even smiles at you, don't think that
the maiden has lost her modesty, or
that you are unusually attractive, or
that she wants to queen Junior week,
.lust think a moment and you will re
member that Cap and Gown is giving a
dance in the Women's Club House on
the 27tli of January and the members
of the women's honor organizations
are selling tickets to it.
The dance is to be a cross between a
lively informal and a jolly-up. There
will be some tag dances, but the ma
jority will be straight crawls. For
those not caring to indulge in the
terpsichorean activities, card tables
will be provided down stairs.
