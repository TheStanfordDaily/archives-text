# Expert to Tell How to Benefit Farmer
## 
### 
Colonel Harris Weinstock, state
commissioner of markets, will lecture
in the Little Theater tonight at 7:30,
on "Teaching the Farmer How Better
to Market His Crops."
Colonel Weinstock is widely known
in this state as a merchant and stu
dent of industrial conditions, having
been a member of the state board of
horticulture, labor commissioner, and
a trustee of the State Library of
California. He has been attacked by
newspapers of the state for his ef
forts to help the farmer, they claim
ing that lie has practically brought a
farmers' trust into existence.
Colonel Weinstock spoke at Stan
ford last year under the auspices of
the Economics Club.
