# Barbarians Are Victors in Indoor Soccer Game
## 
### 
In a fast game of indoor soccer,
played last night in the Enoina gym
nasium, the Barbarian team defeated
the local squad by a score of five to
four. As the score was 3 to 3 at the
end of the game it was agreed to play
five more minutes, which being play
ed still left the result in doubt, so
another five minute period was added,
which proved beneficial to the Bar
barians.
