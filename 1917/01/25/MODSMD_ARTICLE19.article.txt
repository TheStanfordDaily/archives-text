# SPANISH CLUB ELECTS L.D. BAILIFF PRESIDENT
## 
### 
Spanish Club last night decided to
keep L .D. Bailiff, '17, as president.
Other officers were elected as fol
lows: Vice-president, Elizabeth Kelly,
'18; treasurer, J. E. Dawdle, '19; sec
retary, J. P. Schamberger, '19. Here
after only Spanish majors and one
person selected by each major are to
be members of the Spanish Club. The
following new members were elected
in this way: Theresita Johnson, '19,
Trene Brooks, '18, J. P. Shamberger,
'19, Jessie Smith, gr., and P. I. Agu
erreuere, '19.
