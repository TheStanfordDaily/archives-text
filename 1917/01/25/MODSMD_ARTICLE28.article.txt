# 1919 to Vote on Smith and Petree for President
## 
### 
Sophomores elect officers tomorrow
on the quad, from 9 to 12. D. M.
Smith and N. H. Petree are the two
men who will contest for the position
of Sophomore president. L. J. Relim,
Ruth Taylor and R. E. Wedekind are
the only candidates for the positions
of vice-president, secretary, and treas
urer, respectively.
