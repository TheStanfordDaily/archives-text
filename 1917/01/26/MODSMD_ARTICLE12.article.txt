# SMITH CHOSEN AS 1919 PRESIDENT
## 
### 
D. M. Smith was elected president
of the Sophomore class in a quad elec
tion today in which but 63 votes out
of about 200 were cast. He received
34 votes to 29 cast for N. 11. Petree.
Other 1919 officers, elected by white
ballot, are L. J. Rehm, vice-president;
Miss Until Taylor, secretary, and R.
E. Wedekind, treasurer.
The newly elected head of the 1919
class is prominent in dramatic circles,
took a leading part in the Sophomore
play of last semester, and was re
cently elected president of Ram's
Head. He is a member of Hammer
and Coffin and Delta Chi.
