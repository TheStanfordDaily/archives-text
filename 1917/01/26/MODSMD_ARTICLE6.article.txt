# Tryouts for "Justice" Scheduled for January 30
## 
### 
Tryouts for "Justice," the play by
.1 olin Galsworthy, selected by English
Club and Masquers, which will be pre
sented in the Assembly Mall on March
I!, will be held in the Women's Club
House, Tuesday, January .10, from
2 to 5 p.m.
The play is unusual in the strength
of the parts and should tempt many
dramatic lights to compete for places
in the cast. It is probable that the
tryouts will be conducted by Mr.
Frank Mathieu of San Francisco.
