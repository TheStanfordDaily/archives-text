# Women's Debating Club.
## 
### 
Open forum on President Wilson's
Peace Speech was the interesting pro
gram followed out in Saturday night's
meeting of the Women's Debating
Club. Several main issues of Presi
dent Wilson's recent speech were de
veloped by various members of the or
ganization in short talks of three
minutes each. Those topics discussed
were:
1. Permanent peace can only be se
cured by organization of major force
of world uniting to guarantee.
2. Peace founded on equality of
rights.
3. Independence and autonomy for
all nations.
4. Freedom of the seas.
5. Limitation of naval and military
armaments.
Ruth Voris, '20, was elected a mem
ber of the society.
