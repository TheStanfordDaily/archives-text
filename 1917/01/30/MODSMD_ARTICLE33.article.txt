# February 17 Is Date Set for Cap and Gown Dansant
## 
### 
February 17 is the date set for a
dansant to be given in the Women's
Club House .by members of Cap and
Gown. Like that given last February,
the dansant will take place in the
afternoon. Tickets arc to go on sale
soon, and proceeds will be used for
clubhouse improvements.
