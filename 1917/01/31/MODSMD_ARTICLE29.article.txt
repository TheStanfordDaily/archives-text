# Dr. Jordan Talks on European Situation
## 
### 
David Starr Jordan gave an infor
mal talk on the European war at the
meeting of the Cosmopolitan Club,
held in the Women's Club House,
Monday night. Dr. Jordan expressed
his views upon the numerous notes
which have passed between Ger
many and the United States, and
talked about his ideas of internation
alism. A general discussion of the
subject was held by the members of
the club. The names of several peo
ple were proposed for membership
and referred to the committee on new
members.
