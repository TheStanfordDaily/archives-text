# Interclass Swimming Is Scheduled for Saturday
## 
### 
An interclass swimming contest is
slated for this Saturday, and Coach
Brandsten requests that all swimmers
sign up immediately with him. The
Piedmont Athletic Club swim comes
the following Saturday, and Stanford
swimmers are going to have hard
work to defeat them.
The Marathon swim is completed to
night, after ten days' swimming, and
the winner will be announced tomor
row. The weather has not been par
ticularly good, but the water is heated
and swimmers are expected out in
strength for the interclass.
