# Ames Names 1917 Finance Committee
## 
### 
Senior President E. R. Ames has
appointed the following men to serve
as Finance Committee for the senior
class: H. D. Weber, chairman; J. C.
Collins, 11. E. Waite, F. O. Rose and
A. Koerner.
Weber is treasurer of Encina Club
and has served as class treasurer.
The committee is already at work
gathering data to make the budget, in
order that assessment can he made at
the first class meeting. The meeting
is announced for next Tuesday at 1
p.in,, in room 214, at which this com
mittee will give a report, and plans
for the senior jollyup will be dis
cussed.
