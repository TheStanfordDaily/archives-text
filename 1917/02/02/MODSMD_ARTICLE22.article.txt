# Eats and Snappy Program for Encina Smoker Tonight
## 
### 
Eats, smokes, and a snappy program
are offered for the entertainment of
the freshmen and new men of Encina
Hall, tonight, at the first smoker of
the semester. The smoker, which
starts promptly at S o'clock, is to be
given to the new hall students by the
Encina Club, and another is planned
later in the semester.
