# Women's Tennis Club Elects Ten Members
## 
### 
Ten new members were elected to
Women's Tennis Club at a meeting
held Monday. Only those were ad
mitted to membership who had won
a place on their class team, and this
standard will be adhered to in the
future.
The following list includes the
names of those just elected: Kath
arine Ommanney, '17, Louise Winder,
'18, Jean Stoddard, '18, Vera Boyd,
'19, Vivian Tompkins, '19, Ruth Gar
land, '20, Minerva Murray, '20, Ruth
Elsinger, '20, Olive Burwell, '20, Grace
Morris, '20.
