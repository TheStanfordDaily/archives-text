# Entries Announced for Swimming Meet

The swimming pool is now filled
with clean, filtered water, and the
interclass swim will be run off tomor
row afternoon at 4:30, as planned.
This contest promises to afford some
competition between the sophomores
and freshmen with the upper classes
hopelessly outclassed.
Women are invited and a big root

by student body card.
