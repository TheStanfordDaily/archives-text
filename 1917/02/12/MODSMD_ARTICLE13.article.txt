# Stanford Volunteers Sail for France at 3 P.M. Today
## 
### 
Stanford's representatives in the
American Ambulance Service in
France left today on the French ship
"Espagnia," according to messages re
ceived by friends of the men. A dis
patch was received this morning
stating that unless some difficulty
should arise at the last moment the
future ambulance drivers would brave
the dangers of the German submarines
and would leave at 3 p.m., today.
