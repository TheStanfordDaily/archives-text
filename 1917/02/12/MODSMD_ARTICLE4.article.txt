# First C.E. Smoker Has Been Postponed
## 
### 
Members of the Civil Engineering
Society postponed the date of their
smoker from Thursday night of this
week, due to the Stanford-California
basketball game. The smoker will
probably be held on Tuesday evening,
February 20, a definite announcement
of which will be made later on.
At the meeting last Thursday, when
plans for the smoker were laid, Pro
fessor liailey Willis described his ex
periences in Argentina, where, in the
capacity of a sort of empire builder
to the southern republic, he was en
gaged in locating a transcontinental
railroad, water supplies and laying out
model cities.
