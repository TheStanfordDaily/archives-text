# Bonnheim Winners to Deliver Discussions
## 
### 
Bonnheim discussions by the four
winners of the theme contest, will be
given tomorrow evening in the Little
Theater at 8 o'clock. The man de
livering the best talk on the Bonnheim
question, which deals with "The Rela
tion of the Government to the In
dians," is the ultimate winner from
the four other leaders.
Winners of theme honors are Rob
ert Binkley, '20, J. C. Holland, '17, E.
Goldberg, '17, and J. E. Kimber, '17.
These men will be the ones taking
part in tonight's discussion.
Judges include Registrar O. L. El
liot. and Professor E. E. Robinson of
the Histoy department.
