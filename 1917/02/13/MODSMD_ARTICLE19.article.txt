# Schwarzenbek Names Jolly-Up Committee
## 
### 
President Schwarzenbek of the
Junior class has appointed the follow
ing committee to co-operate with the
Senior committee in planning the
joint jolly-up: N. R. Allen, chairman;
F. H. Greve, R. P. Johnson, O. D.
Dolan, C. S. Hodapp, Miss Antonia
Hyatt, Miss Marie Rochfort and Miss
Margaret Hinchliff.
