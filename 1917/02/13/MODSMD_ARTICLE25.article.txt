# January "Alumnus" Put in Circulation
## 
### 
January number of the Stanford
Alumnus has been putin circulation
and includes among many other in
teresting articles a letter from the
permanent secretary of the class of
'92, the first class to graduate, saying
that they were going to place a bronze
tablet at the head of the line of class
tablets on the inner quad.
The alumni are urged to return to
the quad 011 Founders' Day, March 9,
for which celebration plans are under
way for the alumni. There is an ar
ticle on Student Control of Athletics,
and one on the University and Junior
Colleges.
