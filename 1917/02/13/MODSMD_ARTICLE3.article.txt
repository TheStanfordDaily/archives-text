# Ambulance Man's Telegram Received
## 
### 
On the evening of his departure for
France the following night letter was
sent to friends on the campus by a
member of the Stanford Unit in the
American Ambulance Service in the
French army.
New York, February 12.
With confidence as our greatest
asset, the Stanford Corps of twen
ty men loaves today on board the
French ship "Espagnia," in com
pany with over eighty other Amer
icans. The delay has been caused
by a coal shortage in New York.
(Signed) CARL RANDAU.
