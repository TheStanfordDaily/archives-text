# Gardemeyer's Cafe Opens to the Public
## 
### 
Equipped in every way possible to
Hive service and satisfaction, Garde
meyer's Cafe is now open on the Circle
in Palo Alto. Mr. Gardemeyer has
been connected with restaurant man
agement in Palo Alto tor many years
and offers a first class lunch counter
and table service to campus patronage
at low rates. — (Adv.).
