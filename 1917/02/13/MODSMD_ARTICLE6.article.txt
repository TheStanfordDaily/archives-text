# W.I. Cattell Will Deliver Vocational Guidance Talk
## 
### 
W. I. Cattell, member of the en
gineering firm of Cattell, Howard &
Ashton, will give the second practical
talk under the auspices of the Voca
tional Guidance committee, at 3:15
p.m., Thursday, February 15, in the
Little Theater. Mr. Cattell is one of
the leading engineers on the Pacific
Coast, and was secretary of the Pan
ama-Paciflc International Engineer
ing Congress, which met in San Fran
cisco in 1915.
