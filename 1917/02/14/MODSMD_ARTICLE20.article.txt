# S.C. Kohs Will Lecture Before Liquor Problem Course
## 
### 
"Alcohol in Relation to Mental De
ficiency" is the subject of a lecture
which will be delivered tomorrow af
ternoon by S. C. Kohs, Fellow in
Bucknell Foundation, before the class
in "Social Aspects of the Liquor
Problem," a new course offered in the
Education department. Mr. Kohs has
had opportunity to gather much inter
esting data on this subject, through
his former position as psychologist in ;
the Chicago House of Corrections.
