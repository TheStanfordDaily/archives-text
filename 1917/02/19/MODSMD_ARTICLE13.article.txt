# Nation Will Settle Its Debts This Week
## 
### 
National Pay-Up Week, a nation
wide movement to release millions of
dollars into the channels of business,
lias been set for the week of Feb
ruary 19 to 24 inclusive, and com
mences today. During this week,
every person in the United States is
expected to pay up as many of his
debts as he can, as the fact is known
that a great deal of business is done
on credit, and that a dollar putin mo
tion will cover many times its value
in payment of debts.
Thus, it is expected, comparatively
few dollars will settle millions of
debts, if all are doins the same thing
at the same time.
