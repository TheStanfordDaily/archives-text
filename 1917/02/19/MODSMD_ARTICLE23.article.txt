With the winning goal thrown less
than five seconds before time was
called, Stanford's basketball team
went down to defeat before the College
of the Pacific, Saturday morning, the
score being U9-31. The game was
marked by quick passing and good
teamwork on both sides. -The follow
ing girls won their "S's" by playing
in this contest: Misses F. Knox, '18,
and I. Bonnett, '19, forwards; V. Scud
der, '18, and L. Hall, '17, touch-cen
ters; H. Westerman, '17, side-center;
N. Schulz, '19, E. Edwards, '18, A.
Marcus, '16, and M. Sprott, '20, guards.
