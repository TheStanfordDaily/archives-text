# Rev. Deems to Talk on Port Conditions
## 
### 
Reverend Charles I'. Deems, super
intendent of the Seamen's Institute oL'
San Francisco, will give an illustrated
lecture this evening at 7:30, in the
Little Theater, taking for his subject
"The Condition and Development of
the Port of San Francisco."
Reverend Deems is particularly well
qualified to speak on the condition and
needs of San Francisco's water front
through his studies in connection with
the needs of the sailor. His conclus
ions have been highly regarded by
business men of San Francisco, whom
lie lias addressed. The lecture will be
illustrated with lantern slides.
