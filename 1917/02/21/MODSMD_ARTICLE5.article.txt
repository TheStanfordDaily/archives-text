# S.I. Miller Talks on Economic War Losses
## 
### 
An analysis of the economic losses
the nations of Europe have suffered
as a result of the present war, and the
possible programs of reconstruction
when peace is declared, will be given
by Assistant Professor S. I. Miller, Jr.,
when he addresses the men of Stan
ford University at the Union this eve
ning at 7:30.
"Economic Reconstruction of Eur
ope" will be Professor Miller's specific
topic. J. D. McLachlan, '17, will furn
ish music before the meeting.
