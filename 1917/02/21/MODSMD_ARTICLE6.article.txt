# Rain-No Game With Santa Clara Tomorrow
## 
### 
With every indication that the
weather will continue to be stormy,
the game between the Stanford varsity
ball tossers and the University of
Santa Clara team, scheduled for to
morrow' afternoon on the Cardinal dia
mond, has been called off by the grad
uate managers of the two institutions.
The game will be played in place
of one of the club contests on the
schedule, as soon as the weather per
mits. When the contest is fiually
staged the Stanford bleachers should
be filled, for the two nines arc evenly
matched.
