# Rain Causes Postponement of 1920 Track and Ball
## 
### 
Due to the recent rains, resulting in
a sloppy track. Graduate Manager
Ileltfens late today called off the dual
meet of the freshmen with San Jose
high school. This will take place
later in the season.
After an investigation of the fresh
men baseball diamond, the outfield
was found to be very soft, resulting
in a postponement of the College of
the Pacific game tomorrow until later
