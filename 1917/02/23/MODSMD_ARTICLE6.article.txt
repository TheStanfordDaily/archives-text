# Belgian Baritone Appears in Concert This Evening
## 
### 
Members of the Peninsula Musical
Association are anticipating a splen
did concert tonight, when Monsieur
Louis Graveure, the noted Belgian
baritone, will make his first appear
ance before the Stanford and Palo
Alto public. Monsieur Graveure is
well known in Europe, and in the
eastern part of this country, but this
is the first time he has made a west
ern trip. His concert in San Fran
cisco was more than successful, ac
cording to all reports, the quality of
his voice being of extraordinary pur
ity and clearness.
