# Wrestlers Start Practice For City Y.M.C.A. Meet
## 
### 
All the weights will be represented
in the wrestling tournament with San
Francisco Y. M. C. A., on March 1.
Stanford mat enthusiasts held a work
out in the nature of a tryout, last
Friday, and will hold a similar work
out Wednesday afternoon in Encina
gymnasium. The workouts are to
give D. C. Wise, '18, a line 011 what
the local wrestlers are capable of
showing, and also to give the novices
experience in legitimate bouts.
The tournament, Thursday evening,
will be held at San Francisco. Coach
Wise will announce the team to repre
sent Stanford, after the workout Wed
nesday afternoon. Anyone is eligible
to enter this tournament.
