# FRANK GREVE TO CAPTAIN QUINTET
## 
### 
Frank H. Greve, '18, was named bas
ketball captain for the 1918 season
at a meeting of the varsity in the
Union, Saturday. Greve lias played
standing guard for the Cardinals all
through the present basketball season.
Greve registers at Stanford from
Hollywood, where he was an all
around athlete. He is a fighting
rugby man, having played a strong
game with the Palo Alto Athletic dub
this year. He is a member of Phi
Delta Theta fraternity.
Greve was not on the varsity last
year, but in the interclass games last
semester he put up a strong game for
the juniors, and has been keeping up
this pace all through the season.
