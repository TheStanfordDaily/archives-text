# Annual Founders' Day Dinner Cards on Sale
## 
### 
Sale of tickets for the annual
Founders' Day Dinner have begun in
earnest this week for the winding-up
event of Saturday, March 10. The
dinner will be given in the Women's
Club House at 6:15, the Ernest Wil
son Company acting as caterers for
the popular event. It will be followed
by dancing. Tickets at $1.00 are on
sale at the registrar's office, Wide
man's, and by representatives of the
living groups on the campus.
