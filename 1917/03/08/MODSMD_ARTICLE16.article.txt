# Skit to Enliven Upper- Class Jolly-Up Tonight
## 
### 
A skit entitled "Just Ice," written
by 1?. C.'Wohlford, '18, and L. D. B.v
num, '18,' will enliven the upperclass
jolly-up in Encina. Hall this evening.
Details of the plot of this skit and of
the personnel of the east which will
present it will not lie divulged until
this evening. C. R. Kegley, '18, and
L. A. Davis, '18, will put on a musical
act. Dancing will begin at 7 and
close promptly at 11 o'clock, as to
morrow is not a holiday.
