## 
###

Jordan lias consented to speak to the
men of the University, Sunday eve
ning, in Encina Hall, at 7:30. This is
one of the series of Sunday evening
talks Riven at the men's dormitory
Sunday evenings under the auspices
of the Stanford Y. ..M. C. A.
