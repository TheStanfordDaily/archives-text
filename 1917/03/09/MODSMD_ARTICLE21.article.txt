# MEMORIAL CHURCH.
## 
### 
Sunday, March 11, 1917.
11:00 a.in.—Divine worship and
sermon.
Preacher: Reverend Russell A.
Thrapp, First Christian Church,
Los Angeles.
Anthem: "O for a Closer Walk
with God" (Foster).
Miss Edith Harvey and chorus.
Solo: "Be Thou Faithful Unto
Death" (Mendelssohn).
Soloist: Mr. Noel Stearn.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital.
