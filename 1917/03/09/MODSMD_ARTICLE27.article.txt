# A. Sbarboro Pleads for Wine Industry
## 
### 
Declaring that the fostering of the
wine industry would be of the great
est advantage to California, Andrea
E. Sbarboro, president of the Italian-
American Bank of San Francisco, yes
terday presented the "Case for
Liquor" before the class in "Social
Aspects of the Liquor Problem." Mr.
Sbarboro, founder of the first Swiss-
Italian colony on the coast, is the first
of a series of lecturers to take his
stand against prohibition.
