# MISSION NINE IS BASEBALL WINNER
## 
### 
After holding their own for fourteen
thrilling innings, the Stanford varsity
baseball team lost the first contest of
a three-game series to the University
of Santa Clara. Saturday. In the last
'ialf of the fourteenth frame, the Mis
ion Town nine squeezed over a tally
on three short singles, ending the
game with the score In their favor,
4 to 3.
Without doubt, the battle waged on
the Santa Clara diamond was the best
played this season, and probably for
many years. The result was always
in doubt, and there was scarcely an
inning minus unusual and brilliant
Plays. Mattei and Mitchell were the
heroes of the Cardinal aggregation,
while Milburn and Liberveau were the
twinkle-twinkle tossers of the oppon
ents.

thrilling innings, the Stanford varsity
baseball team lost the first contest of
a three-game series to the University
of Santa Clara. Saturday. In the last
'ialf of the fourteenth frame, the Mis
ion Town nine squeezed over a tally
on three short singles, ending the
game with the score In their favor,
4 to 3.
Without doubt, the battle waged on
the Santa Clara diamond was the best
played this season, and probably for
many years. The result was always
in doubt, and there was scarcely an
inning minus unusual and brilliant
Plays. Mattei and Mitchell were the
heroes of the Cardinal aggregation,
while Milburn and Liberveau were the
twinkle-twinkle tossers of the oppon
ents.
