# Huston to Talk on Canadian Relations
## 
### 
"Our Nearest Neighbor During and
After the War," will be the topic on
which C. A. Huston, dean of the Law
School, will lecture in the Union, to
morrow evening at 7:30. Dean Hus
ton will show just how the relations
between the United States and Canada
have been strained in the past. He
will analyze the present conditions,
and will explain the importance of
maintaining friendliness with our
"nearest neighbor."
J. D. McLachlan, '17, will provide
music.
