# Untitled Article
## 
### 
Stanford lower class women won a
basketball game, played Saturday
morning with Oakland, by a score of
17 to 15. The game was played on the
Oakland Y. W. C. A. floor. The fol
lowing Stanford girls played: Irene
Bonnett, '19, Olive Burwell, '20, for
wards; Heloise Lysle, '19, Helen
Hopwood, '19, Mary Sprott, '20, cen
ters; Eunice Edwards, '19, Nellie
Schulz, '19, guards.
