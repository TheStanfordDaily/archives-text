# Junior Prom Ticket Sale This Week End
## 
### 
Attendance at the Junior Prom this
year will be limited to 125 couples, ac
cording to an announcement today by
F. F. Walker, '18, chairman of the
Prom committee. Tickets will go on
sale the last of this week at Wide
man's. They will cost five dollars, as
in previous years. Those who wish
to be among the 125, are warned to
purchase their tickets early.
Chairman Walker wishes to correct
the rumor that the Prom will come
on Good Friday. The date will be Fri
day evening, March 30, while Good
Friday falls on April G.
A seven-piece orchestra under the
direction of R. R. Coleman, '18, will
provide music for the big Junior for
mal that culminates Junior Week.
