# Mme Emerick to Repeat Fairyland Play Saturday
## 
### 
Special performance of "What Hap
pened in Fairyland" will be given in
the Assembly Hall, next Saturday, at
2:30 o'clock, owing to requests coming
in that another performance be given
for the children who did not have a
chance to see it when it as put on
before.
In order to give all children a
chance to see the fantasy, Madame
Emerick, in charge of staging tjpr
production, has made a special rate of
five cents in the gallery for children
under twelve years of age. Other
seats will be twenty-five and fifty
cents. Tickets may be secured at the
Stanford Mookstore or Weingartner's
drug store.
