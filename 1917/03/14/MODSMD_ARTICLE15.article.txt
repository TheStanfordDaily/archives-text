# President Ames Names 1917 Picnic Committee
## 
### 
Senior President E. R. Ames has
announced the following people to
liave charge of the Senior Picnic:
T. E. Swigart, chairman; W. F.
Horner, F. L. Baumgartner, A. C. Mat
tel, Miss Helen Ledyard, Miss Eliza

don.
A barge ride will he one of the fea

Launch Company of San Francisco

There will be a meeting of the com
mittee at 11 o'clock at the Delta Gam
