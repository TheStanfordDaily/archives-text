# Indoor Track Meet Tomorrow Evening
## 
### 
Several records are expected to be
shattered in the indoor track meet to
morrow night. The best athletes in
the University have signed up with
N. W. Rakestraw, '16, and E. R. Knol
lin, '13, assuring strong competition.
The events, of which there are eight,
are open to everyone.
During the meet the Gym Club, un
der the direction of Knollin, gymnas
ium instructor, will put on a special
stunt. The meet is open to everyone
without charge.
