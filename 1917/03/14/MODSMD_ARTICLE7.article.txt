# Cardinal Swimmers Meet Piedmont Club Tonight
## 
### 
Varsity "swimmers left the campus
for Oakland, this afternoon, where
they will meet the Piedmont Club
swimming team tonight at 8:15. The
Cardinal men have defeated the Pied
mont Club before, but the meet to
night will no doubt be close. Coach
Brandsten and eleven men made the
trip.
Next Saturday the Olympic Club
team will meet the varsity swimmers
on the campus.
