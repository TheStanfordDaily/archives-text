# To Stage Interclass Meet on Saturday
## 
### 
Warm, sunshiny days are proving a
great boon to track men in their daily
workouts, and presage fast time in
the annual interclass meet, to he run
Saturday at 2 o'clock, on the Stanford
oval. Coach "Ric" Templeton is
working daily with the speedsters of
the four classes and expects that com
petition in all events will he very
close.
All events will be run off without
delay so as to complete the meet by
such time as not to interfere with the
Santa Clara baseball game on the
varsity diamond and the Olympic
Club swimming meet in the local tank.
Graduate Manager Behrens an
nounces that season tickets or twen
ty-five cents will be required to se
cure admittance to the oval.
