# Junior Prom Tickets on Sale at Wideman's Today
## 
### 
Junior Prom tickets were placed
on sale today at Wideman's store in
the Union. Five dollars are required
to secure one of the pasteboards. The
dance will be staged March 30, in En
cina gymnasium, as the final and
crowning event of Junior Week.
Due to the fact that only 125 couples
will be allowed on the floor, accord
ing to an announcement by F. F. j
Walker, chairman of the Prom com
mittee, it is advisable to buy the tick
ets early.
