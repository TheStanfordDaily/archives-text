# Noted Publisher to Lecture Tomorrow
## President of Century Company to Talk on "Publications and Literature" 
### 
William Webster Ellsworth comes to
Stanford tomorrow to speak in the
Little Theater at 7:30, on "Publica
tions and Literature." He is expected
to throw some interesting sidelights
on tliis subject. He has been actively
engaged in publishing work with the
Century Conmpany since 1881. Since
1913 he has served as the executive
head of that corporation.
Besides being a contributor to mag
azines, Mr. Ellsworth has delivered a
number of lectures in American his
tory subjects, one or two of the more
noted being "From Lexington to
Yorktown" and "Arnold and Andre."
