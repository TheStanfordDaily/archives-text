# Over Half of Tickets for Prom Have Been Purchased
## 
### 
All tickets for the 1918 Junior Prom
will be sold by the end of the week,
according to the present demand. A
good many over half of the admission
pasteboards have already been pur
chased and the big demand appears to
be yet to come.
H. S. Crocker & Co. of San Fran
cisco, publishers of the 191S Quad, are
manufacturing something new in the
line of tooled leather programs for
the big formal dance of Junior Week.
