# Hero of Ralph Connor's Novel to Speak Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Shock," the hero of Ralph Con
nor's famous novel, "The Prospector,"
will visit the canipns tomorrow after
noon in real life. Dr. D. M. Oangier,
from whose life Connor moulded the
leading character of his thrilling ro
mance, has been secured to speak be
fore the class in "Social Aspects of
the Liquor Problem." Mr. Oangier,
who is now state secretary of the
Anti-Saloon League of California, will
speak on the subject, "The Ethical
Aspect of the Liquor Problem."
