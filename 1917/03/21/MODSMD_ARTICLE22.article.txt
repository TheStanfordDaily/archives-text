# Spring Rugby Game With Lowell High on Friday
## 
### 
Lowell High rugby fifteen will play
the spring football squad a practice
contest on the varsity field, Friday
afternoon. The game will be called
at 4:15. This is the first appearance
of the spring ruggers this season and
a good game should result, since Cap
tain Lachmund and "Danny Carroll
have been getting a large turnout and
have their men in good condition for
play.
