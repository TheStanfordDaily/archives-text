# No Swimming Meet While Pool Is Being Drained
## 
### 
Swimmers will have a rest for the
remainder of this week while the pool
is being drained and refilled. The
freshmen meet with San Jose, sched
uled for Thursday, has been cancelled
and Swimming Instructor Brandsten
says the tank is closed until Friday.
There is no meet scheduled for this
Saturday on account of the track
meet and California baseball game.
