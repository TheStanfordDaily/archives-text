# Big Series Ticket Sale Is Announced
## 
### 
Graduate Manager Behrens an
nounces that he now has several hun
dred Series Tickets for the baseball
series with the University of Cali
fornia. Five games are to be played
and an admission price of lifty cents
will be charged the fans for each
game. If they buy Series Tickets they
will be able to see all of the games
for one dollar, the price of the paste
boards.
This is a new system and will un
doubtedly prove a success. In the
past, there have been but three games
played in the Big Series and there
was no need for a series ticket. Stu
dents can procure these tickets from
the graduate manager at his office, or
from one of his assistants.
