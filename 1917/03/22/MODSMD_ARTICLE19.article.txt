# Clark Pays High Tribute to H.V. Poor, '10, Painter
## 
### 
Professor Arthur B. Clark, head of
the Stanford Art school, in the Edu
cation department, has recently writ
ten a very comprehensive appreciation
of the work and exhibition, of paint
ings by Henry V. Poor, which is now
taking place in San Francisco, at Hill
Tolerton.
Mr. Poor, who is characterized by
Professor Clark as "California's great-
I est landscape painter," is a Stanford
man of the class of '10. and was later
connected with the Art department of
this University. While in college, Mr.
Poor was active in art lines, contrib
uting freely to local publications.
