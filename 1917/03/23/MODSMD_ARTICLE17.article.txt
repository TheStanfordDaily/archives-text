# Women to Compete in Tennis Saturday
## 
### 
Finals of the women's interclass in
tercollegiate tennis tournament will
be played on the Stanford courts, Sat
urday morning, at 10 o'clock. The
matches were started last semester,
and tomorrow's play will complete
them.
Six matches are scheduled with
teams from all classes. The seniors
will play singles, with Ruth Squires
representing them. Louise Winder
and Mary LeGrange, and Jean Stod
dard and Verona Price play doubles
for the juniors. The sophomore entry
is Ethel Lee in the singles. For the
1920 class Olive Burwell and Grace
.Morris, and Minerva Murray and Ruth
Elsinger will compete in doubles.
