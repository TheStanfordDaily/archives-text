# Campus to Jolly-Up at "Struggle de Dansant"
## 
### 
Although the sophomore "Struggle
de Dansant," to be given tomorrow
night In the Women's Club House, is
to be in the nature of a jolly-up,
queening will be permissable. Any
one on the campus can attend the
dance, the only requirement being
fifty cents.
The dance will start at 8 o'clock.
Kegley's orchestra will furnish the
music, and a number of cut-in dances
as well as straight ones will feature
the program. The committee also an
nounces that there will be food.
