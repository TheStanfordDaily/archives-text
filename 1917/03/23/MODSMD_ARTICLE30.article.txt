# Glee Club Concert in Little Theatre Postponed
## 
### 
Stanford Glee Club will not appear
in a concert in the Little Theater next
Tuesday night as has been previously
scheduled. The concert will be post
poned until May 1, and will be given
in the Little Theater at that time.
In place of the postponed concert
Professor H. R. Fairclough of the
Latin department will lecture on "City
Planning in Ancient Rome." He will
show its application to modern days
and will illustrate his talk with ster
eopticon slides.
