# Freshmen Oppose Normal on Tennis Court Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tennis matches between the Stan
ford freshmen and San Jose Normal
will be held tomorrow morning at 10
o'clock, on the Encina courts.
W. V. Burrill and p. N. Barry have
been chosen as representatives of the
1920 class, having proven their abil
ity in the interclass tournament.
Both doubles and singles matches
will be staged in this first off-campus
competition.
