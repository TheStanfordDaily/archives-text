# CLASS SOCCER TEAMS MEET,
## 
### 
Class soccer teams of six men each
will dash at the annual interclass
soccer matches, Thursday, at 1 o'clock.
The freshmen will meet the sopho
mores .and the juniors will take on
the seniors, the winners to play the
same afternoon.
On account of the condition of the
players, the matches will consist of
two ten-minute halves.
