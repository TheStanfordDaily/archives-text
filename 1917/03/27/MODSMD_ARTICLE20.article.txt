# WOMEN TO TAKE PART IN LAKE SPOUTS JUNIOR DAY
## 
### 
Women will participate in the lake
sports, on Junior Day, according to
decision today. Canoe rowing and
tilting and swimming races will be
the extent of their activities. All
women who wish to compete must re
port. at the Women's swimming pool
tomorrow afternoon at 4:35.
