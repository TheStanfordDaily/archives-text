# Nestoria to Debate California Society
## 
### 
Arrangements have been completed
between Nestoria and the Senate De
bating Society of the University of
California for a simultaneous debate
to be held on April 11. The subject
is "Resolved, That before Congress
shall declare war, the declaration
shall he approved off by a two-thirds
vote of the people." S. S. Schnetzler,
H. li. Iloss and W. Wells are to go to
Berkeley to uphold the affirmative
there, while the California society
sends its affirmative team here to
meet W. C. Chambers, M. H. Hanna
and N. Condee, the Nestoria repre
sentatives.
