# Proposed Amendment Will Be Redrafted
## 
### 
Proposed changes for the contitu
tion of the Associated Students will
be discussed at 11 o'clock Thursday
morning in a student assembly to be
held in Assembly Hall.
The amendments to the constitution
which have been made necessary by
the adoption of the four-quarter sys
tem affect officers of the students' as
sociation, committees, student publi
cations, and class financial account
ings. A committee headed by K. E.
Leib, gr., will meet tomorrow to re
draft that section of the amendments
pertaining to regulation of publica
tions which aroused considerable op
position at the last student body
meeting. The changes to bo discussed
Thursday will be voted on later, ac
cording to T. E. Swigart, '17.
