# Untitled Article
## 
### 
Eleven Stanford men in uniform
will journey to San Francisco tonight
to take part in a monster mass meet
ing to be held in the Civic Auditor
ium for the purpose of supporting the
idea of national compulsory military
service.
The men who have signified their
intention of going are: J. C. Wood,
'17, R. S. Lytic, '17, I). C. Upp, '18, T.
L. Dyer, gr., G. C. Collins, *17, R. A.
Griflin, '17, It. B. Harris, '17, W. F.
Horner, '17, L. R. Skelton, '17, W. P.
Staniford, '16, F. O. Rose, '17.
