# Cap and Gown Will Present Miss Finch
## 
### 
Telling the stories she used to hear
at the knee of her old negro mammy
and singing the songs of the old
South, Miss Lucine Finch will appear
in the Little Theater on April 26.
Miss Finch calls her stories the
"Wonder-Mammy" tales and they pos
sess a charm that wins her rapt at'
tention from old and young alike.
This presentation of Miss Finch has
been arranged by Cap and Gown and
any profits from the performance will
go to the Women's Club House fund.
Tickets will be obtainable from mem
bers of Cap and Gown and at the door
the night of the performance. They
will be fifty cents each.
