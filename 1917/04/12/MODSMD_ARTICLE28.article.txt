# Mrs. Hoover Speaks Here Next Thursday
## 
### 
Mrs. Herbert C. Hoover will speak
at a University assembly to be held
in the Assembly Hall next Thursday
morning at 11 o'clock. Mrs. Hoover,
who was Lou Henry, '98, has recently
returned from the east, where she has
been active in raising funds for the
Belgian Relief, of which her husband
has been the head. Mrs. Hoover
spoke here last year after her return
from Belgium.
