# California Volleys to Racquet Triumph
## Doubles Dropped by Stanford— Captain Barber Victor Over U.C. Star 
### 
California racquet wielders defeated
Stanford's tennis team by a 3 to 2
count Friday afternoon on the Ber
keley courts. The tournament started
off with the loss of the first singles
match by Van Dyke Johns to Graven
of Berkeley. Captain Barber evened
the count, and incidentally showed
his superiority to the field by winning
in straight sets from Rogers, captain
of the Blue and Cold team.
The scores follow:
First singles—Johns lost to Grav
en, 6-2, 7-5.
Second singles—Barber defeated
Rogers 10-8, 6-2.
Third singles—Wolford beat Sim
ard, 6-4, 6-1.
Doubles —Johns and Barber lost to
Rogers and Graven, 6-2, 6-0, 5-7, 6-4.
Doubles —Chapman and Ritter lost
to Clark and Stich, 6-2, 6-4, 6-3.
