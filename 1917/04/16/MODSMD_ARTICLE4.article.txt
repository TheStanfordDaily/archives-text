# Cardinal Fencers Defeat California Foil Clashers
## 
### 
With thirteen wins to California's
eleven Stanford emerged winner in
the fencing clash held at Hearst Hall
annex at Berkeley Friday night. The
Stanford team was composed of L. R.
Skelton, *17 (captain), J. D. Flint, gr.,
R. F. Kahle, '17, J. P. Shamberger,
'19, and R. 0. Bullis, '18. Each man
opposed five men on the other team,
giving a good basis for comparison of
the various worth. J. D. Flint and
R. F. Kahle with four victories each
tied for the individual prize offered
by the Regent.
