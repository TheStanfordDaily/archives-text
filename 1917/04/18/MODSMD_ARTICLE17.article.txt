# Class Captains of Crew Squads Chosen by Women
## 
### 
Women's crew squads for the four
classes elected their captains at a
meeting in the Women's Club House
Tuesday noon. The following girls
were chosen: Freshman, Miss Agnes
Anderson; Sophomore, Miss Emelia
Anderson; .Junior, Miss Anne Duryea;
Senior, Miss Irene Pauly. Class races
will be held on Women's Field Day,
April 28th. as usual, but due to the
fact that there are so many women
out this season, there may be second
team class races also.
