# Senior Basket Team Defeats 1920 Women
## 
### 
Members of the senior women's
basketball team defeated the 1920 six
by a 37 to 5 score yesterday after
noon. This was one of a number of
interclass games which have been
played during the last few weeks in
preparation for the interclass game
which will be played with California
j teams next Saturday morning at ten
' o'clock on the U. C. courts. There
are a number of star players in each
of Stanford's class teams, and the
California women will meet stiff com
petition, as the former expect to take
the majority of the games.
