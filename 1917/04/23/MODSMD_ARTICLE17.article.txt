# Sigma Delta Chi Initiates New Men
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi, the journalistic
fraternity, initiated three men to
membership last Saturday. They
were Harry W. Frantz, 'IS, Burnet
C. Wohlford, '18, and Dale Van Every,
'18. The initiation was held at
Princeton, on the coast near Half
Moon May. The following officers
were elected for the coming semest
er: Gordon Davis, '18, president; Mil
ler L. MeClintock, '18, vice-president;
Burnet Wohlford, secretary, and Dale
Van Every, treasurer.
