# Varsity Tennis Men Defeat Sacramento
## 
### 
Stanford tennis players defeated the
racquet men from the Sacramento
Lawn Tennis Club yesterday after
noon on the local courts. Out of a
total of nine matches played, the Car
dinal team took six. They won four
out of six singles matches, and two
out of three doubles matches.
Stanford was represented by an es
pecially strong team. Hahn, Johns,
Barber, Wolford, Holman and Chap
man competed for the locals against
Chambers, Weber, C. McSwain, Leake
and Lombard for the visiting club.
