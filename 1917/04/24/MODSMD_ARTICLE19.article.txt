Hail the stiff upper lip
Oil the military man—
He's letting ail his whiskers grow
As fast as he cau, so when he
gets up to San Francisco and stands
"trembling before the examining board
he'll look less like sweet 16 and more
like a seasoned vet. A wee trace of
a brush is evidence that its owner
has applied for a commission as sec
ond lieutenant. A full upper lip
equals a first lieutenant. When you
see the man with a full house and a
royal flush on his brow he's trying
to be a captain in the horse marines.
