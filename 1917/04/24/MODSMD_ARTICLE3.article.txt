# Men Take Heed as Fair Gym Artists Must Not Be Seen
## 
### 
Beware, men of Stanford. Keep a
safe distance from Roble Gym to
night, for the Roble Gym artists are
going into action, and there are no
men allowed. They've said so them
selves. It seems that it was erron
eously stated in these columns yester
day morning that the Women's Gym
CI til) was to give a varied program in
the Little Theater Tuesday evening,
and that everyone who is interested
is invited to be present. But this is
all wrong say the fair gymnasts. The
boys will lie interested, of course, but
we don't want them. And it isn't go
ing to be in the Little Theatre either,
but in Roble Gym, and there will be
25 cents admission charged, besides.
