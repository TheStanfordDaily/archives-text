# Stanford Woman to Be Alumnae Leader
## 
### 
Mrs. Lois Kimball Mathews, '03,
was elected president of the National
Association of Collegiate Alumnae at
the convention held in Washington.
This is the largest organization of
college women. Mrs. Mathews is dean
of women at the University of Wis
consin and also associate professor
in the history department. Mrs.
Mathews is a member of Delta
Gamma.
