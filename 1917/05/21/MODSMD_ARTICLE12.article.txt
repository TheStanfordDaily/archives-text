# Durand Receives Honor
## 
### 
Professor W. F. Durand, head of
the mechanical engineering depart
ment. has been elected to membership
in the National Academy of Science.
Professor Durand, who is now in
Washington, D. C., as a member of
the government's board of aeronaut
ics, is the fourth member of the Stan
ford faculty to receive this honor.
The others are Dr. ,T. C. Branner and
Professors D. H. Campbell and E. C.
Franklin.
