# Cap and Gown Elects
## 
### 
Miss Doris Estcourt, '17, was chosen
for next year's president of Cap and
Gown at the last meeting of the year,
which was held at the Women's Club
House. Other officers are: Miss
Helen Westerman, *17, vice-president;
Miss Carol Jameson, *17, secretary,
and Miss Alice Williams, '18, trea
surer.
