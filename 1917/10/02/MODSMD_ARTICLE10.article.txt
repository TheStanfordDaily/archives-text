# Hale Fisher Suddenly Dies While in Training
## 
### 
Hale Fisher, '17, well known Satn
ford man, died at liis home in Palo
Alto, on August. <>, from cerebro-spinal
meningitis, contracted from a cold
while in training at the Presidio.
Fisher had nearly completed his train
ing and his commission for a second
lieutenancy had been granted by the
War Department. He was a member
of Delta Kappa Epsilon fraternity.
Interment was held at Oak Hill
cemetery at San Jose.
