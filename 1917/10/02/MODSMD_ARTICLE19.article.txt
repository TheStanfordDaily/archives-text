# ANNOUNCEMENT
## 
### 
Students of the three lower class
es are invited to report at the Daily
oflice at 1:15 Wednesday afternoon, at
which time tryouts will he started.
Students wishing to register for
choir work should Dr. Eaton this
week from 8 a.m. to 10 a.m., and from
2 p.m. to 4 i).m., in the Memorial
Church.
The annual inter-class rush between
sophomores and freshmen will start
at 4:30 on the afternoon of October 4,
1917.
