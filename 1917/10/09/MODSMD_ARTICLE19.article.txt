# Y.M.C.A. Cabinet Meets Wednesday
## 
### 
Cabinet members of the local Y. M.
C. A. will hold their first conference
of the college year Wednesday, when
it meets in the Y. M. C. A. office of the
Stanford Union. Secretaries Worthy
and Weideinann announce that plans
will be made for the Y. M. C. A. work
at Stanford during the coming year.
The Cabinet will endeavor to increase
the efficiency of the local chapter,
and will assist the national and inter
national organizations in the work
being done for the soldiers at home
and in Europe. All men interested in
Y. M. C. A. work are invited to call
at the office and join the Stanford
chapter.
