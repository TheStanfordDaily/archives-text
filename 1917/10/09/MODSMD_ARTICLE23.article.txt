# Foil and Mask Officers to Be Elected Tomorrow
## 
### 
Foil and Mask will hold the first
meeting of the quarter tomorrow
evening at 7:30, for the purpose of
electing officers for the ensuing year.
It is to he an important meeting
and all members are urged to be pres
ent. The place of gathering has not
yet been decided on but will be posted
on the bulletin hoard on Engineering
corner by tomorrow noon.
