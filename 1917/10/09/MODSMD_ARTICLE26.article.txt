# New Building to Be Dedicated at U.C.
## 
### 
Stanford will be represented at the
dedication of the new Hilgard Hall
at the University of California next
Saturday by representatives from the
trustees and the faculty. President \V.
Mayo Newhall of the Hoard of Trus
tees will be one of the speakers, dis
cussing "Agriculture in California
During Hilgard's Time." From the
faculty Professor D. H. Campbell will
go.
