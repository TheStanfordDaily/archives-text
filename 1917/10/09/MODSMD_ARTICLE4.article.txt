# OPEN DRIVE FOR A.S.S.U.
## 
### 
One hundred per cent membership
in the Associated Students of Stan
ford University is the aim of Graduate
Manager H. P. Mauser, and accord
ingly will tomorrow open a drive for
members through the various fratern
ities and living groups on the cam
pus.
Campaign managers will be ap
pointed in each living group and will
handle the drive. Two pennants have
been offered for the house making the

best showing, one for the men and
the other for the women. Award ot
these will be on a percentage basis,
based 011 the total number of the
house membership. On the other
hand if two houses tie with a 100 per
cent, the house with the largest mem
bership will be the pennant winner.
Standing of the houses will be run
nightly, together with a list of the
new members.
Student body cards may be pro
cured in the Union at any time.
