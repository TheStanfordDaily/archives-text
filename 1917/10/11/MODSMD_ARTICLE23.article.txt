# Red Cross Benefit Musicale Tonight
## 
### 
To aid the Wool fund of the Palo
Alto Chapter of the Red Cross, a re
cital will be given by Miss Amy Hol
man, well known contralto of Oak
land, at the Women's Club House,
Cowper street, Palo Alto, October 11,
at 8:15 p.m.
She will be assisted by Joseph Lamp
kin, the boy violinist, accompanied by
Mrs. Robert Hughes. Both are
musicians of ability.
