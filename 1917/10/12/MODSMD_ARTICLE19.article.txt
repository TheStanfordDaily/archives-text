# Examination for Civil Service Will Be Held
## 
### 
Announcement is made by the United
States Civil Service Commission of
an open competitive examination for
clerk with a knowledge of stenog
raphy or typewriting, on November 2,
1917. Applicants must have reached
their 18th birthday on the date of the
examination.
Candidates will be examined in
spelling, copying, penmanship, letter
writing, arithmetic, stenography and
typewriting. Further information
may be obtained at any liostolfice.
