# French Instruction for Reserve Officer's Corps
## 
### 
Seven sections in Military Spoken
French will start the first of Novem
ber under the direction of Major
Rugge and the Romanic Language de
partment.
These classes are to accommodate
the men of the Reserve Officers' Train
ing Corps, and will be conducted by
Professors Johnston, Espinosa, Gro
jean, De Vries, Smith and Allen. They
will be held every Monday afternoon
from to 6:15, in the Romanic
Language building.
