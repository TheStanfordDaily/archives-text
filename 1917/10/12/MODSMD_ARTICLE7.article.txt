# Y.W.C.A. Welcomes Miss Salisbury, New Secretary
## 
### 
Y. W. C. A. welcomed the new sec
retary, Miss Helen Saliabury, to her
new work at Stanford at the first reg
ular meeting. In the informal talk
she gave Miss Salisbury sounded the
note of service. She spoke of the pur
pose of the Y. W. C. A. and urged all
Stanford women to join. Miss Salis
bury, who is a Stanford graduate and
understands University life, will be a
live factor in the local Y. W. C. A.
organization.
