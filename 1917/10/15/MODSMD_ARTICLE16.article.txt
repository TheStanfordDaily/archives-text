# Music Club Is After Singers
## 
### 
Music Club is to resume its activi
ties beginning with a meeting to be
called the early part of this week.
President G. Shepard, 'IS, Secretary
David Elliot, 'IS, and most of the old
officers are back, together with a
large number of the old members, and
it is expected that the club will be
well under way again, with a full
membership, within the next week.
New talent will be given an opportun
ity to try out, and annocements of
meetings will be made within the next
few days.
