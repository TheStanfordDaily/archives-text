# Peninsula Musicale Will Be Given Soon
## 
### 
The sixth season of the Peninsula
Musical Association will open on No
vember 15 in the Stanford Assembly
Hall, with a concert by the San Fran
cisco Symphony Orchestra. This will
be the third appearance of the or
chestra here under the auspices of the
Peninsula Musical Association, and its
reception has been very enthusiastic
each time.
Season tickets will be ready by Oc
tober 22, and dues will be five dollars,
as before.
