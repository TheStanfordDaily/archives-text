# Stanford Called on for Aero Observers
## Twenty-Five Men to Be Chosen by Assistant Registrar J.E. McDowell 
### 
Stanford University has been asked
to nominate twenty-five candidates to
lie trained and later commissioned in
the regular army as aeronautical ob
servers.
Candidates must be between 21 and
35 years of age, in fine physical con
dition, free from eye trouble. It is
preferred that they shall have com
pleted at least two years of college
training. However, some high school
graduates will be considered if they
can meet the other requirements.
No one will be considered who has
already been called for physical ex
amination under the draft.
Application should he made by to
morrow night at the latest to the As
sistant Registrar, Mr. J. E. McDowell.
