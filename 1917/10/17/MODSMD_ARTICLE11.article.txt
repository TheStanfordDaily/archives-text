# Juniors Ballot for President Thursday
## 
### 
Junior class will ballot for president
tomorrow at a Quad election to be
held from 11 to 1:30. W. P. Winham,
'19, was nominated for president to
day, and will contest the election with
Alfred Johannsen, '19. The other
officers were elected by white ballot.
The following men have been asked
to take charge of the election for to
morrow: F. A. Betts, C. E. Righter,
S. E. Briggs, .1. S. Doron, C. C. Crosby,
and E. S. Winham.
