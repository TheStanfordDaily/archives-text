# Zoology Club Takes First Camping Trip
## 
### 
Zoology Club held its first camping
trip last week-end, hiking over to the
zoology field station on San Francis
quito Creek, about a mile above Sears
ville Lake. At a business meeting
held during the trip, A. W. Warnock,
'18, was elected president, Edith Pat
terson, '19, vice-president, and
Frances Clark, '18, secretary-treas
urer. The Zoology Club is one of the
oldest of its kind on the campus and
extends a membership invitation to
all students interested in biological
science.
