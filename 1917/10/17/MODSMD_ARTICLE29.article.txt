# Skit by Cabinet Members Features Y.W.C.A. Meeting
## 
### 
"Rushing Miss Freshman Greene"
was the title of a skit presented at a
Y. W. C. A. meeting yesterday after
noon by the Cabinet members. It was
in the nature of a rushing stunt, the
purpose being to tell of the work of
the various committees. From the
skit the freshmen could judge the
work done by each ,and at the close
were asked to signify their prefer
ence. Miss Helen Salisbury led the
meeting.
