# Elect Dr. Espinosa to National Society
## 
### 
Dr. Aurelio M. Espinosa has been
unanimously elected a Corresponding
Member of the Hispanic Society of
America at a meeting of the society
held in New York during the past
summer, according to advices received
on the campus. This is an unusuallj
high honor which has been conferred
upon Dr. Espinosa, but few other
scholars having been accorded the
distinction.
