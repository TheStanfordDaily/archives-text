# Freshmen Women Organize for War Emergency Work
## 
### 
Freshmen women met yesterday
noon to organize for War Emergency
Work and to aid the Lane Clinic.
Elections were held for chairmen of
committees instead of the customary
offices. The following were chosen to
serve: Head .chairman, Margaret
Robertson, '21; social, Frances Blew
itt, '21; program, Alice Boulware, '21;
service, Elizabeth Miller, '21, and
Helen Rathburn, '21, tied for office.
Plans were made for a Hallowe'en
party, to be given- November 2.
This organization is under the gen
eral direction of the Y. W. C. A., and
Doris Gibbs is guiding the freshmen
women through their first experience
as a class.
