# Nestorians to Convene Next Tuesday Evening
## 
### 
On account of the Union smoker
being held Wednesday evening, Oc
tober 24th, Nestoria will meet Tues
day night intesad of Wednesday. The
meeting will be held in room 12, Law
department, at 7:30 p.m. Professor
Robinson will address the society on
the very interesting current topic:
"The War Aims of the United States
and the Basis upon which the United
States could conclude an Honorable
Peace." Discussion from the floor will
follow.
