# Cosmopolitan Club May Discontinue Meetings
## 
### 
Cosmopolitan Club will meet to
night in the Women's Club House at
8 o'clock, for the purpose of electing
officers. All those interested are in
vitted to attend, as at this time it will
he decided whether the club will con
tinue through the present quarter. It
is hoped enough interest will he
shown to warrant new plans toward a
successful year.
