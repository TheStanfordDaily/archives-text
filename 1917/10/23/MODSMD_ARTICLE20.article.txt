# First Encina Scheduled For this Saturday Night
## 
### 
Encina men will hold their first reg
ular Encina Dance on Saturday, Oc
tober 27. This was determined last
night at a regular meeting of Encina
Club. There are to be 125 paid ad
missions, seniors, juniors, and sopho
mores being given the preference and
freshmen only being admitted in case
there are not sufficient applications
for admittance from the former.
Dues are payable at the Hall tonight
between 6:30 and 7:30. Bids to men
outside Encina will be out tomorrow.
