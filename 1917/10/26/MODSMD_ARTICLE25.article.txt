# Swimming Pool Open for Women's Use Every Night
## 
### 
Women's swimming pool has been
opened from 8 to 9:30 p.m. on all week
nights, for women of the University,
pend wholly on the number of women
who take advantage of it.
A ticket book is necessary, and may
be obtained at the Business Office for
fifty cents.
