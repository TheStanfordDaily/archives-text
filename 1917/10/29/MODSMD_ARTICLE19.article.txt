# DR. FISHER ON WAY TO EASI
## 
### 
Dr. Walter J. Fisher, assistant pro
fessor of Zoology, lectured last. Friday
at the University of Southern Cali
fornia under the auspices of the De
partment of Biology on "Bird Life on
the Laysan Islands."
Dr. Fisher, who will he absent from
the University until Christmas, is on
his way east, to study the laboratory
equipment of eastern institutions in
the interests of the new marine lab
oratory at Pacific Grove, which is
nearing completion.
