# "American Girl on Trial" to Be Given by Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
"The American Girl on Trial" is to
be given by the Y. W. C. A. at 4:15,
Tuesday afternoon, in the Little The
ater. The play shows how the Amer
ican girl has fallen short of the stand
ard set by European girls in war work.
The cast follows:
American Girl Ellen Calhoun
Spirit of America. .Elizabeth McArthur
Red Cross Girl Dorothy Driscoll
Armenian Girl Frances Willis
Russian Girl Carol Jameson
French Girl Mrs. Walter Bingham
Attorney Mary Therkelson
Attorney Thea Noack
