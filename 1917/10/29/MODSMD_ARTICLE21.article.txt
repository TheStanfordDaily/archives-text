# Euphronia to Argue on U.S. Food Control
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society will
discuss the question, "Resolved, That
the United States Government Should
Take over Complete Control of All Re
sources of the Food Supply, as a Part
of the Policy of Food Conservation."
The meeting will be held on Wednes
day evening.
J. T. Murphy, '20, and G. C. Thomas,
'19, will debate on the affirmative of
the question and E. B. Harper, '20, and
R. Grounds, '20, will speak for the neg
ative.
On November 7, Professor E. R
Krehbiel will talk to the members of
Euphronia on "Standing Behind the
President."
