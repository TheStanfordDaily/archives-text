# Ballot for Union Board of Governors Tomorrow
## 
### 
Election of three men to the Union
Hoard of Governors will be held Tues
day in the Union. There are four can
didates, who were nominated at the
Union smoker last Wednesday night.
Nominations were closed at that time.
The men may be balloted for through
out the day tomorrow, all members of
the Union being eligible to vote.
