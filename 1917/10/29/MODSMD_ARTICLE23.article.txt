At a luncheon held in Hotel Oakland
by the Association of Collegiate Alum
nae, and attended by Miss Harriet
Bradford, Dean of Women, as repre
sentative of the Stanford branch of
the Association, Miss Southwick, spec
ial war-work secretary of the National
Council of the Y. W. C. A., spoke of
the Y. W. C. A. work near and in the
cantonments, and Dr. Wilbur Sawyer
gave a short talk on camp sanitation.
Dr. Sawyer has just returned from a
convention of health officers in Wash
ington, D. C., and he emphasized the
necessity of maintaining high morale
in both the army and civil life of the
country.
