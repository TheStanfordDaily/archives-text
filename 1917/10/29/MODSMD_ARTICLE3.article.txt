# Finals in Class Football to Be Played Tuesday
## 
### 
Finals in interclass Rugby will be
run off tomorrow afternoon on the
Varsity Held at 4:30. No admission
will be required for the contest, which
will be staged between the Seniors
and the Freshmen. Incidentally the
play will mark the last appearance of
the first-year men before their big
game with Santa Clara on Saturday,
November 3.
Fans should see some good football
tomorrow. The Seniors have such
men as Captain Pettingill, Hauser,
Schwarzenbek, Heron, Haines, Tem
pleton, and Weeks, while the Fresh
men will use practically the same com
bination which handed Lowell High
School a 47 to 0 defeat.
