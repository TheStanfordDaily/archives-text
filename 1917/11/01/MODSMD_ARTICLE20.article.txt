# La 						Fete des Poupees! Watch! Look! Listen!
## 
### 
La fete des Poupees, not the Or
pheum varsiety, but something bigger,
better and newer, is coming to Stan
ford. There are going to be all kinds
of dolls, and all nations will be repre
sented. Papa dolls, professor dolls,
mamma dolls and baby dolls; they'll
all be here.
There will be dancing, too; old and
new creeps and crawls, local and for

solo. Every desired variety will be
there. None will lie missed.
