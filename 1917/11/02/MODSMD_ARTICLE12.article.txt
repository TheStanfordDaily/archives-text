# Soccer Team to Mix With Winged-O Men
## 
### 
Varsity soceerites are slated to tus
sle with the speedy Olympic Club ag
gregation as a prelude to tlie fresh
man Rugby contest with Santa Clara
tomorrow afternoon. This game,
which was postponed from last Satur
day, will be the local squad's debut
before Stanford soccer enthusiasts, as
il has been the first, opportunity for
the Cardinal hooters to mix in a real
contest.
The team will be built around
Drury, Yewell, and Captain Roney, the
only veterans on the job this year, but
there are several men, including Sf.v,
Wing, Wedell, Stamp, Brill, and Pills
bury, who have had second varsity or
freshman experience ,and these will
help to make up a strong combination
to run ni) against the club men.
