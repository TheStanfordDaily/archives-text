# Two Men Expelled By Student Council
## 
### 
Dismissal of two students for dis
orderly conduct the night of the Senior
Farce was the result of action taken
by the Men's Student Council at their
recent meeting.
The action follows:
Men's Student Council.
Stanford University,
October 9, 1917.
The Men's Student Council recom
mends that two students be dismissed
from the University for drinking liquor
in the Assembly Hall and for disorder
ly conduct in and about the Assembly
Hall on the night of the 1917 Senior
Farce.
CLIFF R. SWARTS,
President Men's Student Council.
Affirmed:
W. R. ECKART,
Acting Chairman, Faculty Committee
on Student Affairs.
R. L. WILBUR, President.
