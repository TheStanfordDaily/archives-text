# Valnable State Records Received For Library
## 
### 
Among books of interest recently
received at the Library is "Documents
Diplomatiques of the French Ministry
of Foreign Affairs." This publication
is known as the "Yellow Hook," and
is a collection of French diplomatic
documents from I860 to 1914.
"Stenographische berichte iiber de
verhandlunger des Reichstages, 1S75-
1911," is a German state book which
is another recent acquisition of the
Library.
"The Monthly London Review,' a
literary magazine of great interest,
has also been obtained, for the dates
1759 to 1817.
