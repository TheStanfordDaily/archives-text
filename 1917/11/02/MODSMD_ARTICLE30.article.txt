# G. M. Davidson, '17, Is With U. S. Engineers in France
## 
### 
Word lias been received that Gordon
M. Davidson, '17, first lieutenant in
the 117th regiment of U. S. Engineers,
encamped at Camp Mills, Long Island,
left October 18 for field work in
France. .Mr. Davidson was recently
promoted from the rank of second
lieutenant. He is a member of the
Alvarado Club.
