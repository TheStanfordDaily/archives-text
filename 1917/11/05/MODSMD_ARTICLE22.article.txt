# Ballot Tomorrow for Union Board of Directors
## 
### 
Elections for three directors to the
Union Board of Governors will be held
in the Union tomorrow. The ballot
ing will take place from 11 to 12
o'clock, with all members of the
Union, in other words, all men reg
istered in the University, eligible to
vote. Four men were nominated for
the board at the Union smoker a little
over a week ago; three of these are to
be chosen. Those whose names will
appear on the ballot are F. W. Weeks,
'18, N. R. Allen, '18, E. R. Martin, '18,
and J. W. Gaffney, '20.
