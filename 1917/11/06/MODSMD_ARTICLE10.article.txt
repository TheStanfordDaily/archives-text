# Economics Smoker on November 14
## 
### 
Wednesday evening, November 14,
is the definite date set for the Eco
nomics smoker, which was postponed
last week. The program announced
some time ago will be given.
"Red" Mauser, 'IS, will preside at
the smoker, and promises to add a few
events brimful of pep to the events so
far planned.
Underclassmen especially are urged
to come to the Union for the annual
get-together of Economic majors and
faculty.
