# Install New Ventilating System in Little Theater
## 
### 
Work for the purpose of installing
more efficient ventilation for the Little
Theater is now under way. Ventila
tion of the Little Theater has for some
time been unsatisfactory, and though
the present work will be retarded more
or less? by the necessity of avoiding
noise during classes, it is expected
to be finished in the near future.
