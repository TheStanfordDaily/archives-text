# 1921 Promises New Jolly-Up Features
## 
### 
It's going to be something new. Thp
biggest thing of its kind this quarter.
There's to be dandy eats, and music,
too, and lots of tag dances.
That's the program for the Fresh
man jolly-up, which will be held Fri
day evening, November 9, in Encina
Club Room. The usual four-bits will
be charged at the door, and 8 o'clock
is the time sot for the frolic to begin.
There's to be no queening, and the
soft shirt and sport suit will bo the
popular dress for the evening.
