# Six Members Elected to Music Club After Tryouts
## 
### 
At the second tryouts of the Stan
ford Music Club Wednesday night six
new members were elected out of the
ten who tried out. This addition
brings the club membership up to the
maximum limit of twenty members.
The new members are Miss E. Mil
ler. '21, piano; Miss R. Squires, '18,
Miss A. Cross, '18. Miss M. Kerr, '18,
vocalists, and Mr. Vernon Thompson,
'20, violin.
