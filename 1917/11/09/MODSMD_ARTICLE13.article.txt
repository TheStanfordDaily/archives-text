# Juniors and Seniors Jolly-Up Saturday
## 
### 
Sorority houses giving dances to
morrow evening are planning to come
down en masse for half an hour or an
hour to the upperclass jolly-up at the
Women's Club House. This jolly-up
promises to be 'the best of its kind
for several years. A big crowd will
be on hand to dance to Keglej's jazz
music and the advent of the visitors
from the other dances on the campus
that night will swell the numbers
even more.
With the exception of these visitors
all costumes will be of the usual
jolly-up variety. The usual four-bit
piece will admit to the dance floor
and to the bar, where Watsonville
apple cider will be served in large
quantities.
