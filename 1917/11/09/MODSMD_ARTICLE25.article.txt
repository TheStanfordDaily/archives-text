# Spanish Club Meets and Elects Seven Members
## 
### 
Spanish Club met at the Alpha Phi
house Wednesday evening and a typ
ical Spanish program was enjoyed by
the members and guests of the club.
A short business meeting was held at
the close of the evening and the fol
lowing new members were elected to
the club: Kathreen Scudder, '18, Iva
Hunter, '18, Frances Wagner, '21,
Elizabeth Woodbridge, gr., Florence
Holberton, '21, F. A. Chamorro, '21,
and A. E. Hyniman, '20.
