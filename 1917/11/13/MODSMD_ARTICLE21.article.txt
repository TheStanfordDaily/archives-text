# Bawl-Out Now on Sale; Smoke-Ups Due Saturday
## 
### 
Announcement from the Registrar's
office today brought to light the fact
that the Bawl-out, officially known as
the Directory of Officers and Students,
is now on sale at the Business Office.
The usual price of 15 cents is being
asked for the book.

boxes turday at the earliest, and
