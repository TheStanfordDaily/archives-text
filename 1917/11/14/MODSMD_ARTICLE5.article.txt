# Y.M. DRIVE WILL CLOSE THURSDAY
## 
### 
Thursday, at 5 p.m., the Y. M. C. A.
Prison Camp campaign formally closes
and at that time all pledges must be
in. Tomorrow ends a week of arduous
work by both Y. M.and Y. W. com
mittees toward raising the funds Stan
ford has pledged.
Women's pledge cards which have
not been collected should be turned in
at the Women's Club House in care of
Mrs. Ewell, before tomorrow evening.
