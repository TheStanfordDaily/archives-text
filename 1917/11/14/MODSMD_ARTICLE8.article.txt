# Men on Training Table to See "Cinderella Man"
## 
### 
Seat sale for"The Cinderella Man'
has been very satisfactory to date,
both on the campus and in Palo Alto.
The production Friday evening will be
the first amateur showing of "The
Cinderella Man," and owing to with
drawal of the permission of amateur
rights by the author, probably the last
for some time.
Arrangements have been made for
the men on the football training table
to attend the show, a block of seats
being reserved for them.
