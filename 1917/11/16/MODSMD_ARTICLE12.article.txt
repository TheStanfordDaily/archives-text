# Rooters Hats Will Cost Less This Year
## 
### 
Rooters' hats have been secured by
Graduate Manager Hauser and will
be on sale at Wideman's in the next
few days. The hats will be cheaper
than ordinary this year, fifty cents
being necessary to secure them, in
contrast lo sixty-five last season.
Those men who sit in the rooters'
section are urged to secure the cover
ings in order to carry out the nig
(Jame rooters' section idea with the
best results.
