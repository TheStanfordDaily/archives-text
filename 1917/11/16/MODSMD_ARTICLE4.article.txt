# Select Amos Elliot Big Game Referee
## Former Blue and Gold Captain and Rugby Star to Handle Whistle 
### 
Ames Elliott, last year's Big Game j
referee and Rugby coach at Stockton
High School, will handle the whistle
in the Big Game again this year.!
Elliott is one of the best informed !
men on the coast on English football j
and his selection this year for the big \
game assures a fast, clean contest.
Elliott, in addition to being a first-'
class referee, is a coach of no mean
ability. He is a former University of j
California football man, probably one j
of the best ever turned out there.
