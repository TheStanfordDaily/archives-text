# Big Game Ushers Asked to Report for Saturday
## 
### 
Graduate Manager Mauser has an
nounced that Big Game ushers are re
quested to report to his office as soon
as possible. As usual several men
will be selected for this position.
Tickets for the big contest are now
on sale at the student body offices
in the Union, both for rooters and
outsiders. In addition, those paste
boards which were ordered early are
now ready for distribution.
