# Bishop N.S. Thomas to Talk of Cambridge
## 
### 
Stanford people are to have an op
portunity tonight to hear an address
on first-hand impressions of life in a
great English university. The lit. Rev.
Nathaniel S. Thomas, Missionary
Bishop of Wyoming, will have as his
subject at the regular Tuesday even
ing lecture "Sonic Impressions of an
Undergraduate at Cambridge," which
institution he attended in 1891.
Bishop Thomas attended the Univer
sity of Minnesota before going to
England, and received his degrees
from that institution. After a year
spent at. Ihe great British seat of
learning he returned to this country
and became Bishop of Wyoming.
