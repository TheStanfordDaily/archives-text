# Rally to Arouse Football "Jazz" Thursday Night
## 
### 
Plans are practically complete for
the football rally to be held in the As
sembly Hall next Thursday night and
to be followed by the burning of the
freshman bonfire. Speakers will in
clude Coach Jim Wylie and several
prominent alumni. The Assembly Hall,
as usual, will be divided into sections,
and men from the different parts of
the campus will march in together.
Immediately following the rally,
which is slated for eight o'clock, the
fans will march around the Quad and
over to the freshman bonlire, which
will be burned at this time.
A committee consisting of Stanford
yell leaders and several students will
make final plans for the rally tonight
and will announce full details tomor
row.
