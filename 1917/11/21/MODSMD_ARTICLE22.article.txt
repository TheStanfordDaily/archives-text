# Symphony Orchestra Will Meet First Time Monday
## 
### 
Palo Alto Symphony Orchestra will
reorganize for the coming year at a
meeting to be held next Monday night
in l he Palo Alto Playhouse. The or
chestra will bo under the direction of
Caesar Addinando, first oboeist of the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra.
All who play orchestral instruments
are urged to report for tryouts at this
first meeting.
This organization is the only one of
its kind in the west, and is an impor
tant undertaking for a town the size of
Palo Alto. The first concert will be
given next spring.
