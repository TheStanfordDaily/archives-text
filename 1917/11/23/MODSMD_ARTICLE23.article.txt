# Traffic Rules Issued Today
## 
### 
Final instructions liave been issued
| regarding the conduct of automobiles
I tomorrow as follows:
i
After 1 o'clock on that day all nia-
I chines coming into the University will
use Palm Drive. The old automobile
road or service road will be closed to
all traffic except pedestrians.
All machines entering Palm Drive to
the ball game will turn on Pine avo
|nue to the ball grounds. All outgoing
j machines will take the diagonal road
between Pine avenue and the old auto
mobile road. Machines from the cam
| pus going to the game will go down
| Palm Drive or Lasuen street to Pine
avenue and turn at Pine avenut to the
, ball grounds. Pine avenue will be
blocked at the railroad crossing.
| Machines from the campus will be
allowed to go as far as the railroad
j track and will be expected to park in
the field west of the power house.
No machines will be allowed to
[cross the railroad track except with a
special permit from the management
of the game.
All machines from San Jose and
San Francisco that are going direct to
'the ball grounds should enter from the
highway at Embarcadero road and
park in the field near the athletic
: track fields. No machines will be al
lowed on the roads between Encina
Hall and the ball grounds.
Pine avenue by the ball grounds
and the road by the gym will be
blocked. Automobiles on Pine avenue
j coming from the east will not be per
mitted to pass this point.
It is earnestly desired that the gen
jeral public will not leave their ma
chines on Pine avenue, hut will com-
I ply with the request to park in the
' field off of Pine avenue and to the
I west of the power house so that Pine
' avenue will be kept as clear as possi
ble for traffic.
