# Euphronia Will Debate on Question of Shipping
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society will
meet on November 28, to discuss the
question: "Resolved, That the United
States Government should Pass a Law
Subsidizing American Foreign Ship
ping to Insure a Proportional Share
of the World's Trade After the War."
James Petrini, '21, and R. Grounds,
'20, will debate for the affirmative,
while Lyman Wilbur, '21, and N. W.
Hartman, '20, will speak on thp nega
tive.
Nestoria Debating Society will meet
at dinner on Tuesday evening, at the
Stanford Inn.
