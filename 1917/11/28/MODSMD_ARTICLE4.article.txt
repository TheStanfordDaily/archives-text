# ENGINEERING SMOKER ON NEXT WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Civil, Mechanical and Electrical en
gineers will bold a combined smoker
in the Union a week from today.
Prominent engineers from San Fran
cisco will be the speakers for the
occasion.
Art Wilson, 'IS, and H. C. F. Leh,
'17, who are in charge of the arrange
ments, promise that an evening of
stunts will follow the speeches. Box
ing and wrestling will be the principal
features in this line. Eats and
smokes will be furnished.
All Engineering majors are invited
to attend.
