# Three Days' Meet For Women Swimmers
## 
### 
The three-day swimming meet for
Stanford women has been scheduled
to open next Friday afternoon at
4:15 o'clock in an inter-sorority
event, at the Roble pool.
The meet between classes will be
held on Monday the 10th and prom
ises to be a good one.
A challenge from the women of
the Palo Alto High School has been
accepted by the Freshmen women
and this will close the meet taking
place on the following Wednesday.
The program for the meet will be
published later.
