# Library Exhibits New Earthenware Process
## 
### 
An instructive account of the proc
ess of manufacture of vitreous earth
en ware can he seen in the library
this week at the industrial exhibit
counter. The exhibit shows all the
steps in the work from the mining of
the feldspar and other materials used
in the manufacture of the ware to the
boxing and crating of the finished
product for shipment. It is one of
the exhibits sent out by teh Extension
Division of the University of Cali
fornia.
