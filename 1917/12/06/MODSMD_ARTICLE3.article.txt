# Women's Swimming Meet Scheduled for Tomorrow
## 
### 
Over thirty women, representing
eight local houses, have signed up for
tomorrow's swimming meet, to be
held at the Itoble pool at 4:15 o'clock.
The houses entered are: Chi Ome
ga, Gamma Phi Beta, Alpha Omlcron
Pi, Delta Gamma, Kappa Alpha Theta,
Kappa Kappa Gamma, Roble Hall and
Madrono C'luh.
The contest between Palo Alto High
School and the Freshmen scheduled
for next Wednesday afternoon has
been changed to Tuesday at the same
hour.
