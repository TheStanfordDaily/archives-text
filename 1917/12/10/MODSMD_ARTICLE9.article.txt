# Sigma Xi Reception On Tuesday Evening
## 
### 
The society of Sigma Xi will hold
an informal reception in the Faculty
Club House tomorrow night follow
ing the lecture of Dr. W. W. Camp

bell of Lick Observatory in the Little
Theater. This is the first meeting
that Sigma Xi has held this year and
Professor J. P. Smith, president of
the organization, extends a cordial
invitation to all faculty members and
their families to be present.
