# Sergeant Tarbohn to Drill Sharpshooters
## 
### 
Sergeant Henry Tarbohn, U. S. A.,
retired, has arrived from San Jose to
assume charge of the target practice
of the Stanford R. O. T. C., according
to an announcement made by Major
Jens Bugge this afternoon.
Sergeant Tarbohn has had consid
erable experience in target practice
and holds several Medals for sharp
shooting. The instruction that Ser
geant Tarbohn will give next quarter
will enable Stanford to further in
crease the efficiency of the military
department.
The new instructor has been mak
ing his home in San Jose.
