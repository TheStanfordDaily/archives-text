# Untitled Article

Free wool for knitting has arrived
at the Women's Club House. It will
be possible for any Stanford woman
to get 1 his yarn in order to make
sweaters or other knitted articles for
the Red Cross.
The Stanford auxiliary of the Red
Cross will sell Christmas stamps for
the tuberculosis fund at one cetn
a piece. Beginning this week severa
Stanford women will be stationed in
the postoffice at noon and in the after
noon to sell these stamps.
