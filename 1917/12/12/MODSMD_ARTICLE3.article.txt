# STRANGE LOSS OF INTERFRATERNITY TROPHY REPORTED
## 
### 
Lost, strayed, or stolen is the bas
ketball trophy. Last night the Earl
C. Behrens inter-fraternity trophy
disappeared from its place on the
mantelpiece of the Kappa Sigma fra
ternity house. No clue has been
found to the mystery.
An appeal is sent out for the per
son who has the trophy to return it
to its place. If it is not found, an
investigation will be instituted at
once to locate the artitcle. The own
ers hope that such proceedings will
be unnecessary.
