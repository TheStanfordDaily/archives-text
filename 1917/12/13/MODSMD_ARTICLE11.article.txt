# Union Board to Fill Vacancies at Meeting
## 
### 
Union Hoard of Directors will meet
tomorrow at 8 p.m. in the Union for
a short session to fill the vacancies in
tlu Board caused by war depletions.
The positions vacated by I. C. Heron,
'18, and E. R. Martin, '18, will be
Riled by the Board, Heron's place
being automatically taken by H. R.
Hertel, '18, as student body president.
The other is undetermined as yet.
