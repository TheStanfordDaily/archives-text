# HOLIDAY PARTY AT WOMEN'S CLUB HOUSE
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at 3:30 in the
Women's Club House an old-fashioned
Christmas party is to be piven for the
campus children. Each child is to
bring a pair of stockings with his or
her name, age, and sex written on it.
Professor and Mrs. J. Pearce Mitch
ell will be the hosts of the occasion.
Professor Foster will play Santa
Claus, and the Schubert club will sing
the Christmas carols.
