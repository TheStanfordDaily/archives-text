# First Campus Dance Comes Friday Night
## 
###

the official title of the social affair to
lie held Friday night in the Women's
Club House. It promises to be suc
cessful with good music and plenty
of new 1918 jap. The dance is to be
entirely informal and will give every
one a chance to talk over the too
short Christmas vacation.
