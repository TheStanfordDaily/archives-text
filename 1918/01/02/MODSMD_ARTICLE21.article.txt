# Fenlason Elected Head of Euphronia
## 
### 
C. O. Fenlason, '19, was elected
president of Euphronia for the winter
quarter at a banquet held at the Stan
ford Inn the latter part of December.
B. W. Hartman, '20, and J. T. Mur
phy, '20, were elected to the office of
vice-president and secretary, respec
tively. J. G. Driscoll, '18, acted as
toastmaster at the banquet.
The new officers have met to make
plans for the coming quarter and look
forward to a successful period in the
history of the society. The past quar
ter has been an especially active one,
considering the decrease in the num
ber of men registered in college, they
consider.
