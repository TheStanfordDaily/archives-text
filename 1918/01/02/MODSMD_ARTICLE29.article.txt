# PONZER IS KILLED
## ACCIDENTAL GUNSHOT IS VERDICT 
### 
Professor Ernest W. Ponzer of the
Mathematics department shot and
killed himself at his home in Palo
Alto December 21, while attempting
to clean a shotgun. He leaves a wife
and two children. The women's jury
brought in a verdict of accidental
death from self-infiicted gunshot
wounds.
Professor Ponzer was a graduate of
Illinois and had been at Stanford
since 1909. He was a member of Heta
Theta Pi fraternity.
