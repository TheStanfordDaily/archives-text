# HARRIET McCAUSLAND HEADS ENGLISH CLUB
## 
### 
Harriet McCausland, '17, was elect
ed president of English Club at its
last meeting in the first quarter.
Other officers chosen are: Viee-pres
ident, Margaret Riehter, gr.; secretary,
Lisette Last, 'It); treasurer, Chester
Hacker, *18; member-at-large, Doris
Estcourt, '17; editor of Sequoia, Noel
Stearn, '17. New members were also
elected at this meeting. They are
Sidney Moore, gr., Txiraine Franken
keld, '19; Ruth Mantz, '19; Hugh
Boyd, '19, and A. W. Leeds, '20.
