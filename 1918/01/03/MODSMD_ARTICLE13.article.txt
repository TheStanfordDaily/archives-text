# Women's Journalism Club Elects Four
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, the women's
journalistic organization, held election
before vacation and chose the follow
ing women as members:
Margaret Richter, gr.; Katlierine
Ames, *18; Harriet McCausland, gr.;
Ruth Mantz, '18.
This club is the honorary society on
the campus for the women who are
active in journalistic work.
