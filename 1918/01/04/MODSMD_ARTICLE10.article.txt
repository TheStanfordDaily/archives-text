# COL. MAUS TO SPEAK TO MEDICAL SCHOOL
## 
### 
Stanford University Medical School
will hold its first popular lecture of
the winter quarter at Lane Hall, Sac
ramento street near Webster, San
Francisco, this evening at 8 o'clock.
Colonel L. U. Maus of the United
States Regular Army will lecture on
the subject of ''The Control of Vice-
Diseases among the Troops through
Civil and Military Co-operation." The
subject is of interest and the lecturer
has quite a reputation in army and
medical circles.
