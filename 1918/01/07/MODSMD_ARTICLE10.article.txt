# E.E. Leach, '18, Drowned At American Lake Camp
## 
### 
E. E. Leach, ex-'lB, was drowned in
American Lake on Christmas eve,
while on active duty. His body was
shipped to Los Angelrs for burial.
Leach, a member of Company C of the
31Gth Engineers, is one of the lrst
Stanford men to lose his life while
in the service of his country.
