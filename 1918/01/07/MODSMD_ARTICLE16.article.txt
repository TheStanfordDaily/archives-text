# LAST CALL
## 
### 
Tryouts for the staff of The Daily Palo Alto begun today, to continue
for a period of four weeks, at the termination of which the names of the
successful candidates will be published. Any other student, whether a
freshman, sophomore or upperclassman, who wishes to compete, should re
port to the Daily office within the next few afternoons in order to receive
assignments. Positions on the staff are open to women as well as to men.
