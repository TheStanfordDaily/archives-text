# Professor Kato to Speak At Cosmopolitan Club
## 
### 
The Cosmopolitan Club will hold an
open meeting this evening at 8 o'clock
In the Women's Club House which all
interested are invited to attend. Pro
fessor K. Kato of the University of
Chicago will speak 011 "American-Jap
anese Relations from the Standpoint
of Education."
Professor Kato, who is the national
secretary of the Y. M. C. A. for foreign
students and does much of his work
among the colleges of the country, Is
stopping on the campus on his way
back from Japan.
