# TRYOUTS BEGIN TOMORROW FOR '20 QUAD EDITOR
## 
### 
Tryouts for editor of the 1920 Quad
start, tomorrow night, according to an
nouncement made today by S. E.
Briggs, editor of the 1919 Quad and
chairman of the tryout board for the
1920 Quad. Trvoutees should meet
Briggs tomorrow night at Room 10G,
Encina Ilall, promptly at 7 o'clock,
when the work expected from edi
torial tryoutees will be outlined and
assignments made.
All Sophomores are eligible to try
out and a good turn out is hoped for
so that suitable material may be
developed for the issuance of the 1920
year-book.
