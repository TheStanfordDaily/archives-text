Pedro J. Lemos. curator of Stanford
Museum and Art Gallery, will go to
San Francisco, Thursday, in an ef
fort to secure an exhibit of drawings
by Ramaekers, famous Dutch cartoon
ist. Many of these cartoons have ap
peared from time to time in the
Hearst papers, and are known in the
United States as the best examples of
the cartoonist's art relating to the
world war. This new exhibit will
take the place of the present Pennell
exhibit, which will be taken from the
Gallery in a lew days.
