# Benefit Performance For Clinic Children
## 
### 
Stanford Auxiliary to the Lane
Clinics, in its meeting last night, dis
cussed plans for giving a benefit per
formance for the Convalescent Home
in Palo Alto.
Last summer 94 children from the
free clinic in San Francisco were
cared for in the Convalescent Home
for from two to three weeks. Be
sides this permanent homes were
found for some children and others
were kept by Palo Alto people for
several months.
