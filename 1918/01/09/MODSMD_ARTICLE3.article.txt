# Cap and Gown Plan to Give Benefit Pageant
## 
### 
At the Cap and Gown tea at Miss
Bradford's home yesterday, Miss Flor
ence Mason was elected secretary
pro tetn in the absence of Miss Alice
Williams.
Plans for a pageant for the benefit
of the Lane Clinics were made.
it was decided to hold Cap and
Gown meetings once a month, on
Tuesdays. The next meeting will be
at the home of Mrs. H. L. Niebel, in
Palo Alto.
