# Dr. Adams Back From Important Conference
## 
### 
Dr. E. I). Adams, head of the De
partment of History at the Univer
sity, returned last week from his
eastern trip, where he has been at
tending a conference of the Foreign
Service and Foreign Trade Commis
sion at Pittsburg.
Although delayed in starting, owing
to an accident which might have
proved serious to Mrs. Adams' father,
Dr. Adams was able to reach the east
ern city in time for both meetings
of the conference, which took place
011 January 2d and 3d.
The importance of Dr. Adams' at
tendance is realized when it is known
that he is a member of the Committee
of Fifteen which reported at this
meeting.
