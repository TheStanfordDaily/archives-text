# G. LUHRS APPOINTED JOLLY-UP CHAIRMAN
## 
### 
G. H. Luhrs, Jr., was today appointed
chairman of the Senior Jolly-Up Com
mittee hy President C. S. Keglev. The
other members of the committee are
Miss Jean Hall, Miss Mildred Roome,
R. V. Bingham, H. Stewart, and A.
D. Wilkie. Plans for the 1918 af
fair are not yet perfected, but the
date will be announced following the
first meeting of the committee.
