# RED CROSS INVITES NEW WOMEN TO WORK
## 
### 
Freshman women and new women
in the University are invited and
urged to commence Red Cross work
at once. Instructors will be at the
Women's Club House Monday, Tues
day, and Thursday afternoons from 1
p. m. to 5 p. m., who will supervise the
preparations of bandages and surgical
dressings and knitting.
Wool for sweaters has be jn received
and this will be given out at once to
the University women. A large quan
tity of unbleached wool has also been
received. It is to be used for knitting
socks.
