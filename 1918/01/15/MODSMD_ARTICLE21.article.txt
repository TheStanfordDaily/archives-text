# Nestonia Society Will Discuss Russ Question
## 
### 
A lively and interesting discussion
cf the Russian situation is promised
by the Nestoria Debating Society at
their regular meeting tomorrow night
ft 7:30 in Room 12 of the Law de
partment. The subject for dis ussion
is stated as follows: "Resolved, that
the United States should officially
lecognize the de facto Bolsheviki gov
ernment of Russia." Hargrove and
Robbins will uphold the affirmative of
the question against N. Oonde and F.
McElroy. Visitors are welcome.
