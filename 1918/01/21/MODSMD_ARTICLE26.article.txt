# Mandolin Club Wants New Girls at Meeting
## 
### 
The Women's Mandolin Club will
hold its first meeting of the quarter
Wednesday at 7 p.m., at the Alpha Phi
house. New women students who
play mandolins, guitars, ukuleles, or
banjos, are invited to meet with the
members of the club. It will not be
necessary for them to bring their in
struments.
The Women's Mandolin Club is ac
tive in college musical affairs and
last gave the program at one of the
Tuesday evening concerts. Plans for
the coming year will be discussed on
Wednesday evening.
