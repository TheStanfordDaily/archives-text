# Santa Clara R.O.T.C. Officially Installed
## 
### 
Santa Clara College was officially
inaugurated Sunday as a unit of the
Reserve Officers Training Corps, sen
ior division, of the U. S. Army. The
ceremonies, at which many alumni
and friends were present, began with
a review of the cadet batallion by
Colonel S. E. Smiley of the Eighth In
fantry, Camp Fremont. Addresses fol
lowed by Colonel Smiley, Archbishop
Hanna, and Rev. Joseph McQuaide,
chaplain at the Presidio. In the even
ing an address was given by D. H.
Delmas and a program was rendered
by the cadets.
