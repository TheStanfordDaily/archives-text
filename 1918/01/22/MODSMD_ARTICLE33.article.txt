# ALL FRESHMEN TO COME TO SMOKER
## 
### 
Every freshman man in the Uni
versity is invited to be present at the
1921 smoker at the Phi Kappa Psi
bouse, Thursday evening. In previ
ous years this annual event has been
confined chiefly to the new men of
the various fraternities, but this year
every man belonging to the class of
1921 is expected to come. This will
be the tenth annual smoker of this
kind given by the rreslimen of the
Phi Kappa Psi house.
