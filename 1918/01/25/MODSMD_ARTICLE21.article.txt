# FRESHMAN WOMEN TO HELP IN WAR WORK
## 
### 
Freshman women met yesterday
afternoon under the direction of the
Women's Conference, to organize for
war work as a group.
Miss Kirby Ingolsby, '21, was elect
ed president of the freshman girls,
and Miss Edna Peterson, '21, secre
tary.
Miss Harriet Bradford explained all
University rulings to the freshmen
and urged them to work actively in
some line of war relief. Under the
direction of Miss Margaret Robert
son, '21, chairman of the committee
on war work, the organized group
will begin immediately to help some
army unit abroad.
