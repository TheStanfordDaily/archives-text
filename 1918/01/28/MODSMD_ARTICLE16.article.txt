# MANAGING EDITOR
## 
### 
It is with regret that we announce today the resignation of L. O.
Weaver, 'lit, as Managing editor of The Daily Palo Alto. Weaver has en
tered the United States service and has been accepted into the govern
ment ordnance school at Berkeley. To Miss Ruth D. Taylor, '19, falls the
task of managing editor left vacant by Weaver's resignation. By her ef
ficient work and real ability in performing successively the duties of re
porter, women's editor, and news editor on the staff. Miss Taylor has
proven herself entitled to this promotion and well qualified to assume the
increased responsibilities of her new position.
