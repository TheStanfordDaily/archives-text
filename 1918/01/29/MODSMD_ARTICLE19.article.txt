# Euphronia to Discuss Changes in Cabinet
## 
### 
An informal discussion on the
question of giving Cabinet members
a seat in Congress is to he held to
morrow evening by members of Eu
phronia Debating Society. This is the
subject of the annual Euphronia-
Nestoria debate, to take place next
Wednesday, February G., The Eu
phronia team will uphold the affirma
tive of the question against the rest
of the society. * The proposition
stated is, "Resolved, that as a war
measure, Cabinet members should
have a seat in Congress." All mem
bers are urged to be present.
