# 1920 TO JOLLY-UP FRIDAY AT ENCINA
## 
### 
Sophomores will jolly-up Friday
night at Encina Hall, An orchestra
with plenty of "pep" is promised and
there will be a box of Wilson's choc
olates for Ihe lucky-starred winner of
the spot dance. Plenty of refresh
ments will he on hand, punch and
cake being the piece de resistance.
In accordance with the custom,
freshman men are not invited to at
tend. Due to some misunderstanding,
it had been announced previously that
all four classes would he welcome.
This invitation still holds good for all
except freshman men.
