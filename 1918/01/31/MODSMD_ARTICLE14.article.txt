# CONFERENCE PARTY IS POSTPONED ONE WEEK
## 
### 
Women's Conference has postponed
the party which was scheduled for to
night until Thursday, February 7. A
special meeting was held by confer
ence members last night to take into
consideration the work now being
done on the Women's Club House. It
was decided that no interruptions
should be made and for this reason
the party to be held there was post
poned.
The Conference party is a quarterly
event at which freshman and new
women in the University are enter
tained. A short program and dancing
will fill the program at the party next
Thursday evening from 7 to 9 o'clock.
