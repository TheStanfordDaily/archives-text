# WOMEN'S MANDOLIN CLUB HOLDS MEETING
## 
### 
Women's Mandolin Club held its
first meeting of the quarter at the
Women's Club House last night. Miss
Lilly Sherwood has been appointed
director of the club. She is leader of
the Neopolitan Club of San Francisco
as well as of several other musical
organizations, and teaches music at
Miss Harker's School.
The second meeting of the club will
take place next Wednesday evening
from 7 to 8 o'clock at the Women's
Club House. Tryouts will be held be
fore the meeting.
