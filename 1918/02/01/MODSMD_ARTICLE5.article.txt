# JOFFRE TRYOUTS SOON
## 
### 
The Joffre tryouts will take place
on Thursday afternoon of next week
in the Little Theater, at 1 o'clock.
Leib has two veterans as a nucleus
for the team in J. G. Driscoll, '18, and
M. L. McClintock, '18.
