# DR. LANZ TO GIVE RECITAL IN LITTLE THEATER TONIGHT
## 
### 
Tonight at 7:30, in the Little Thea
ter, Stanford music-lovers will have
an opportunity to hear Dr. Henry Lanz
in a piano recital of famous classical
pieces by the master composers and
two compositions by Lanz himself.
Dr. Lanz was born in Russia and
received most of his musical educa
tion there, and finished it by a course
in Heidelberg University. He is at
present living in Palo Alto, and teach
ing several classes in Russian lang
uage and literature here.
No admission will be charged at
the recital tonight, as it is one of the
regular series of Tuesday evening
meetings.
