# English Club Has Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
English Club will hold its regular
meeting tomorrow night at 8, at the
home of Miss Dare Stark, 1020 Ra
mona street, Palo Alto. Professor H.
D. Gray will continue his talk on "The
Drama in New York" which he began
at the last meeting. The members
were so well pleased with his talk at
that time that they asked him to con
tinue it at this meeting.
Professor Gray told his impressions
of plays that he had seen for himself
on the New York stage, and gave an
extremely interesting lecture.
