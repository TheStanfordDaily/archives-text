# ZULOAGO PAINTINGS TO BE EXHIBITED IN CITY
## 
### 
Art lovers on the campus are noti
fied by Professor A. B. Clark, one of
the directors of the San Francisco Art
Club, of the exhibition of paintings by
Ignacio Zuloago, to be held in the
Palace of Fine Arts from February 10
to March 1.
This exhibition is reported to be the
biggest artistic event in the West since
the Panama-Pacific Exposition. Zu
loago is hailed as the twentieth cen
tury representative of the Velasquez
genius and is one of the foremost of
modern Spanish painters.
There will be about four hundred
pictures in the exhibition and they
will occupy two of the largest rooms
in the Fine Arts Palace.
