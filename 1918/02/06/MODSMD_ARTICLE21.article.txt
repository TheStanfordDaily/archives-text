# Alumni Officers To Be Elected Alumni Day
## 
### 
J. E. McDowell, secretary-terasurer
of the Stanford Alumni Association,
announces that the annual election of
officers of the Alumni will he held at
Stanford University on the morning of
Alumni Day, June 15, 1018. The pro
visions of the constitution of the as
sociation which govern the election of
officers can be found in the Stanford
Illustrated Review which is to appear
shortly.
