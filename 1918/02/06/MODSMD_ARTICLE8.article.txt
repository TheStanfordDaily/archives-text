# FROSH TO PLAY CALIFORNIA
## W.I. Ames Named New Director of Daily Palo Alto 
### 
W. I. Ames, 118, was named as busi
ness director of The Daily Palo Alto
at a regular meeting of the Executive
Committee last night. Ames is the
first man to be appointed to the office
since the adoption of the new system
of tryouts under which candidates
from the three upper classes may
try out for a certain period under the
supervision of the graduate manager.
Ames is a member of Encina Club.
Three members were last night ap
pointed to the guild board by the com
mittee. They are W. F. Pritchard, gr.,
W. I. Ames, '18, and J. S. Doron, '19.
The minutes follow:
President Hertel absent.
Schwarzenbek in the chair.
M. S. C., that W. F. Pritchard, W. 1.
Ames, and J. S. Doron be appointed to
the guild board.
M. S. C., that W. 1. Ames be appoint
ed business director of The Daily Palo
Alto.
M. S. C., that the meeting be ad
journed.
