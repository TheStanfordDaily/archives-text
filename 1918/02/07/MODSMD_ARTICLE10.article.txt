# LIBRARY ANNOUNCES HOUR CARD CHANGE
## 
### 
A new hour card is now in use at
the Library. It represents changes
from the old schedule as follows: The
periods beginning at 8 a. m.. 12, 6, and
8 p. m. have been dropped. The even
ing is now divided as follows: 7-S:3O,
8:30-9:50.
Any person who signs at 9:50 for a
book may retain it overnight. All re
served books issued for the night
must be returned by 9 the following
morning. Reserved books not called
for by ten minutes after the hour will
be given to the first inquirer.
