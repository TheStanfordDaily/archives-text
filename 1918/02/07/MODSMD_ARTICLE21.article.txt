# Talk Before Wranglers Given By Miss Kalisher
## 
### 
"President Wilson, the Man" was
the subject of a talk by Miss Helen
Kalischer, 'IS. before Wranglers last
night. "The President has a rare per
sonality, and all the charm of a
Southern gentleman; he is not the
cold, detached machine that we
imagine him to be," declared Miss
Kalischer.
