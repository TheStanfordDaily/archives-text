# Faculty Women's Club to Have Musical Program
## 
### 
An hour of music will be enjoyed
by the Faculty Women's Club at its
first monthly meeting, to be held on
Monday at 3 o'clock, in the Women's
Club House.
Mrs. E. C. Franklin will render as
piano solos selections from Chopin
and modern Russian compositions.
This will be followed by a quartet
composed of Mrs. Austin Rogers, Mrs.
Robert Reynolds, Mrs. Frederick
Wheeler, and Mrs. Post. The program
of their songs follows:
Northern Cradle Songs. .Grace Conant
Summer Wind Macdowell
Flowers and Fancies Mozart
Indian Lullaby Mrs. H. H. Beach
With Lilies Sweet and Daffodils...
Gena Branscombe
