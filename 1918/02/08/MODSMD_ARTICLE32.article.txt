# India Asks for Stanford Men.
## 
###

estimate of business men as well as
civil and military authorities is again
demonstrated by the fact that the Me
chanical Engineering department has
on file letters from the government
and several prominent business con
cerns asking for men who have a
knowledge of mechanical engineer
ing. There has recently been received
a letter from India asking for mechan
ical engineers.
