# Simonson and Proseus Saved from Tuscania
## 
### 
Lieutenant S. S. Simonson, *14, and
R. W. Proseus, ex-'l7. of Stanford, who
wore both on the Tuscania, have been
saved, according to the report re
ceived tliis morning.
Simonson was student adviser and
editor of The Daily Palo Alto while at
Stanford. After leaving college he
became secretary of the State Council
of Defense. He left his home in Mer
ced on January 4 for the Atlantic
coast, where he reported for military
service in the Twentieth Engineers.
Proseus was a member of the 100 th
Air Squadron. He was at Stanford
for the fall semester in 1913-14. His
home is in San Jose.
