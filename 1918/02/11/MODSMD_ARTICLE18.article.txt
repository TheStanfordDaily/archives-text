# Stanford Women to Make Scrapbooks for Soldiers
## 
### 
Stanford scrapbooks will be the
latest means of war relief work un
dertaken by the Women's War Emer
gency Committee. These will be filled
with stories and pictures to amuse
the wounded soldiers in the hospitals.
There will be a picture of the Quad
and the Stanford seal in the books,
which will be sent for immediately.
Women who wish to aid in making
these books should sign up at once
with the committee in charge in order
that the right number of books may
be ordered.
