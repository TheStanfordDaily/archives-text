# SPANISH CLUB GIVES LAS MANANAS DEL SOL
## 
### 
A one-act play, "Las Mananas del
Sol," will be Riven by members of the
Spanish Club at their first meeting of
the quarter, Wednesday evening, at
the Alpha Phi house. Miss Margaret
Bald, 'IS, and L. D. Bailiff, '18, will
take the leads in the play.
P. J. Aguerrevere, '20, will enter
tain with a vocal selection, accompan
ied by L. G. Heynemann, '21, on the
piano.
The meeting will begin at 8 o'clock
and all Spanish majors are urged to
be present and to bring their 50 cent
dues.
