# FACULTY CLUB ENJOYS QUARTET OF SINGERS
## 
### 
Faculty Women's Club was enter
tained with an hour of music at its
meeting yesterday afternoon. The
quartet composed of Mrs. Austin Rog
ers, Mrs. Robert Reynolds, Mrs. Fred
erick Wheeler, and Mrs. Post, gave a
well selected program and their songs
were greatly enjoyed. Mrs. Joseph
Hutchinson accompanied them on the
piano. Mrs. E. C. Franklin, who was
to have played some piano selections
from Chopin and modern Russian
composers, was unable to attend the
meeting on account of illness.
