# WOMEN TO CONSTRUCT SOLDIERS' SCRAPBOOKS
## 
### 
Red covers with the Stanford seal
engraved on them are to feature the
scrapbooks which will be made under
the auspices of the Women's War
! Emergency Committee. Pictures of
the University and other tokens in
dicative of the Stanford spirit will be
placed within the books.
These scrapbooks are to be sent to
France for wounded soldiers in the
hospitals. All women who wish to
fill the books may sign their names
on a card provided for that purpose
