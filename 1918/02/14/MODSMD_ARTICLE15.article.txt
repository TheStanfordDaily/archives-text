# Wranglers Hold Election and Admit New Members
## 
### 
The following officers were elected
at a meeting of Wranglers last night:
President, Miss Henrietta Dickey, '20;
vice president, Miss Frances Willis,
'20; secretary, Miss Ellen Calhoun,
'20.
Three new members were taken into
the club. These were Miss Katherine
Ames, '18, Miss Evelyn Costello, gr.,
and Miss Marion Rogers, '20.
