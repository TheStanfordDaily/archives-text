# OFFICERS AT U.C. WEAR UNIFORMS ALL DAY
## 
### 
The officers of the R. O. T. C. of
the University of California have
unanimously agreed to wear cadet
uniforms from 8 o'clock in the morn
ing to 5 o'clock at night, every day
the university is in session. While
this agreement was made only com
pulsory to officers, all non-commis
sioned officers and privates have been
urged to do the same. It is believed
by the Officers' Club, which passed
this ruling, that it will add to the
military efficiency of the R. O. T. C.
The order for officers goes into effect
immediately.
