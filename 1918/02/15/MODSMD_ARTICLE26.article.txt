# ZIMBALIST CONCERT SALE NEXT THURSDAY
## 
### 
Scat sale for single tickets for the
Zimbalist concert given next Thurs
day in the Assembly Hall will take
place on Monday, February IS, at
Weingartner's drug store in Palo
Alto. Coupons marked "third con
cert" may be exchanged on and after
tomorrow for reserved seats at the
same place.
Music lovers are looking forward
to hearing Efrem Zimbalist as this is
the first appearance of the famous vio
linist at Stanford. On his first con
cert tour of this country in 1911-12
Zimbalist established a record by
traveling from coast to coast. Rarely
has a newcomer, however famous, re
ceived such widespread recognition as
was accorded him at that time. This
is the third concert of this season to
be given under the auspices of the
j Peninsula Musical Association.
