# FRATERNITIES PLAY BALL IN SEMI-FINALS
## 
### 
The remaining game in the semi
finals of the inter-fraternity baseball
series is to be played this Sunday.
The Sigma Chi-Delta Chi-Alpha Tan
Omega team has already worked its
way into the finals and it will meet
the winner of this week-end's game
for the championship. Theta Xi and
Theta Delta Chi will be the teams
to battle Sunday morning for the
right to play in the finals. The game
to decide the champion fraternity
team will be played off as soon as pos
sible.
