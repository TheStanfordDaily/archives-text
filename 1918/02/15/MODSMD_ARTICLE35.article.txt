# Baskets
## 
### 
Parks guarded Salmina to such ad
vantage that he did not get a basket.
The only field goal snared by the Cal
ifornia captain was when Parks was
taken out.
California's team play worked to
nice advantage until the Blue and
Gold men reached the Stanford
guards when the rush toward the Car
dinal basket usually began. The Cal
ifornia forwards could not seem to
fathom the style of guarding put up
by Butt and Parks.
* * * * * * *
Parks' basket in the middle of the
second half was a pretty shot. The
ball hit the basket, bounced up in the
air, rolled around a while and finally
dropped in. The basket seemed to
take the heart out of the Blue and
Gold men.
#****»*
Moulton converted three out of four
foul shots in the last few minutes,
and added three needed points to the
Cardinal score.

in good shape during the game and
