# R.0.T.C. COMPLETES BAYONET INSTRUCTION
## 
### 
Instruction in bayonet combat which
was being given by Captain Short of
Camp Fremont to the commissioned
officers of the R. O. T. C., has now
been completed. Captain Short, how
ever, has offered to. come over when
ever possible and to help the com
panies in their regular drill.
A class in bayonet work for non
commissioned officers will now be held
every Saturday. The members will be
instructed by the commissioned officers
who took the course under Captain
Short and these will be expected in
turn to impart their knowledge to the
privates under them.
