# TWO NEW OFFICERS ELECTED BY GUILD
## 
### 
The Students' Guild, at its meeting
yesterday, elected J. S. Doron, '19, sec
retary, and W. I. Ames, '19, treasurer.
Professor J. P. Mitchell gave a report
on the business of the Isolation Hos
pital, which, due to the large increase
in inmates in the last few weeks, has
needed added equipment.
