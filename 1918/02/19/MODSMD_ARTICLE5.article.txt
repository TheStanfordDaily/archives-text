# Commanders of R.0.T.C. Discuss Modern Warfare
## 
### 
Three company commanders of the
R. O. T. C. addressed the advanced
classes of the corps on various phases
of modern warfare yesterday afternoon
in the Little Theater. The information
given out by the speakers was taken
from confidential pamphlets issued by
the government to the army camps
and R. O. T. C. corps. It is the latest
information on modern warfare and
has been compiled by American of
ficers working in connection with the
leading military authorities of the
Allies.
