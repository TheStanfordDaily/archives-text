# ALFONSO CORNEJO TO LECTURE ON ART MOVEMENTS
## 
### 
Stanford people will tonight, at 7:30,
in the Little Theater, have an oppor
tunity to learn something about the
present-day movements inartistic
fields throughout the world. Alfonso
Cornejo is the speaker, and"The
Modern Movement in Painting and
Sculpture" is the title of his lecture.
It is to be illustrated by numerous
stereopticon slides reproducing works
of modern art, many of which have
never been shown here before.
Mr. Cornejo returned at the out
break of the war from Germany, where
he had been studying the art situa
tion. In consequence he is in close
touch with the developments of paint
ing and sculpture in European centers,
as well as the United States.
