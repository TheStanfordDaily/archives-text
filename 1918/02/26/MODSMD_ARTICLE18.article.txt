# SCHUBERT CONCERT TO BE HELD NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Schubert Club will give its annual
public concert next Tuesday evening,
March 5, at 7:30 o'clock, in the Little
Theater. The club has been working
on the songs since the early part of
last quarter and promises to give a
thoroughly enjoyable program.
There are at present thirty-four
girls in the club.
