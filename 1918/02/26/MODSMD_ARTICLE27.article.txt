# BASE HOSPITAL UNIT 47 TO LEAVE
## 
### 
Word has just been received that
Base Hospital Unit No. 47, stationed
at Camp Fremont, is to leave Satur
day for Fort Oglethorpe, Georgia,
where the unit is to go into intensive
training, preparatory to overseas
service.
There are several vacancies in the
unit, which is mostly composed of
college men, many of them from
Stanford. Enlistments in the unit
will be taken up to the time of de
parture.
