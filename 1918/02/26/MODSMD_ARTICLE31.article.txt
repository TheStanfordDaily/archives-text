# TELLS SERVICE CLUB OF FRONT LINE WORK
## 
### 
Major It. O. Ilullis, 'IS, of the R. O.
T. C. spoke before the Service Club
in the Little Theater yesterday after
noon. His talk was in the nature of a
summing up and putting together of
the talks which have been given dur
ing previous weeks.
Speaking of offensive attacks, he
explained the use of barrages, gas, in
fantry waves, hand grenades, and ma
chine gun fire, showing their proper
use and relation to the attack. He
also dealt with the use of these tac
tics in defensive operations.
