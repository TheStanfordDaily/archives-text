# Guilbert Concert Nets Large Sum for Relief
## 
### 
The total of $776.75 was made by
the Belgian Relief committee at the
concert given by Yvette Guilbert in the
Assembly Hall Monday night.
Mrs. O. L. Elliott, chairman of the
committee in charge of the concert,
said that although the audience was
small the concert had been very suc
cessful, and that a large sum of
money could be devoted to aiding the
people of Northern France who have
been driven from their homes by the
Germans.
