# Professor Durand Now With Forces in France
## 
### 
Word has been received on the
campus that Professor W. F. Durand
has arrived safely in France, after a
voyage of eighteen days. Professor
Durand is at the head of the Aero
nautical Board and has been in gov
ernment service for a considerable
period. While at Stanford he was
connected with the Mechanical Engi
neering department.
