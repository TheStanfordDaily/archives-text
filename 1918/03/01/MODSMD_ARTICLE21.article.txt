# Delta Gamma Gives Show for Soldiers From Camp
## 
### 
Delta Gamma produced the vaude
ville show which was a part of their
Belgian benefit entertainment, March
IG, for the soldiers from Camp Fre
mont last evening in Ramona Hall in
Palo Alto. The performance was
given at the request of the Palo Alto
committee which is providing a series
of entertainments for the men.
