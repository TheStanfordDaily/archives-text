# FRANCIS F. WHEELER OBTAINS APPOINTMENT
## 
### 
Word has been received that Fran
cis F. Wheeler, '15, is one of four out
of a class of eighty-five to obtain an
appointment to the Naval Officers'
Training Station at Pelhani Bay, N. Y.
At the end of a six weeks' course he
will be entitled to ensignship. Before
going into active training he has been
sent on a two-months' cruise to South
America. Wheeler is a graduate of
the Economics department, and was a
member of Encina.
