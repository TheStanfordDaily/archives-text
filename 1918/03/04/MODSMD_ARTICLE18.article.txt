# FOUR WOMEN SELECTED TO DEBATE WITH U.S.C.
## 
### 
Tryouts for the debate with the
women of the University of Southern
California Law School were held in
the Little Theater Thursday after
noon. Miss Margaret Shodd, '20, Miss
Frances Willis, '20, Miss Muriel Ed
wards, '20, and Miss Margaret Mc-
Elin, '19, were selected by the debat
ing coach, Iv. E. Leib, '15, to compose
the team. One of these will be chosen
later as an alternative.
