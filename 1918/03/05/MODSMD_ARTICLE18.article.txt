# Stanford R.0.T.C. Hear Trench Warfare Lecture
## 
### 
Trench warfare was the topic of a
lecture given yesterday by Captain
Stuart of Company C of the Stanford
R. O. T. C. This formed one of a series
of Monday afternoon talks for men of
advanced standing in the R. O. T. C.
The material for the lecture was ob
tained by Captain Stuart from a gov
ernment confidential pamphlet.
Following this there was a discus
sion of conditions and methods of
fighting in trenches.
Captain Volkmar also spoke, his
topic being discipline.
