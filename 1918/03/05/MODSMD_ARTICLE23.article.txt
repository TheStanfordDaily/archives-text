# Alumnae Tea Wednesday
## 
### 
Resident alumnae will give their
third tea of this quarter Wednesday
afternoon in the Women's Club House.
A musical program has been planned
which is expected to be unusually in
teresting, and Hoover cakes with tea
will be served.
The hostesses for this tea are Mrs.
Bailey Willis, Mrs. G. J. Peirce, and
Mrs. E. B. Krehbiel.
