# SCHUBERT TO GIVE CONCERT TONIGHT
## 
### 
Schubert Club will give a concert
tonight in the Little Theater in place
of the regular Tuesday night lecture.
The program promises to be unusu
ally good, as the club has been work
ing on it all quarter. Miss Lucile
Huff, '18, will be the soloist. N. H.
Stearn, gr., C. L. McCormick, '19.
and R. S. Gangestad, '18, will give sev
eral selections.
