# Stanford Hospital Corps Reaches the War Zone
## 
### 
Stanford naval base hospital corps
has reached the war zone, according
to an announcement by Secretary
Daniels of the War Department. The
base was recruited almost entirely
from the ranks of Stanford students
and instructors at the Lane Hospital
and left San Francisco December 11,
1917. It has a capacity for 500 men,
and will take care of the navy per
sonnel ashore and afloat. If necessary
it will be used for the sick and wound
ed of the army and our allies.
