# Ichihashi to Give Talk to English Club Tonight
## 
### 
English Club will meet tonight at
the home of Miss Edith Mirrielees of
the English department, at 1027
Emerson street, in Palo Alto. Mr.
Yamato Ichihashi, instructor in the
History department., will give a talk
on "Japanese Literature." Mr. Ichi
hashi has spoken before the English
Club several times upon subjects re
lating to Japan and its problems, and
his talk tonight is expected to be par"
ticularly interesting. Students taking
the present course in history of the
Far East under Professor P. J. Treat
will be especially interested. A full
attendance of English Club is ex
pected, and the usual excellent English
Club "cats" will be on hand for the
hungry.
