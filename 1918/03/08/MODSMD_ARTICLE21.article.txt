# Dirge to "Betty's Boots"
## 
### 
Oh, Betty, dear, l sadly fear no more
your Booties will appear.
Somehow they missed their goal.
The toes are rent, the counters bent,
somewhere in space they now are
sent,
Because they had no "Sole."
