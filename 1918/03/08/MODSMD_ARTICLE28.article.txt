# Women's Faculty Club To See Japanese Prints
## 
### 
The Faculty Women's Club will
meet next Monday afternoon from 3
to 5 o'clock in the Women's Club
House. Dr. J. M. Stillman, professor
of chemistry, vice president emeritus,
will give an exhibition of some beau
tiful Japanese prints.
