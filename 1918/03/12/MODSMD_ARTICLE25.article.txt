# WOMEN'S BASKETBALL CONTESTS POSTPONED
## 
### 
Women's basketball teams were un
able to hold interelass contests yes
terday on account of the rain. These
contests have been postponed until
tomorrow afternoon, and will be held
at the Roble court. The winners of
the freshman-sopohomore game will
play the winners of the junior-senior
game Thursday. The winners of
Thursday's game will play the faculty
women's team on Friday.
