# New Officers Elected at Meeting of German Club
## 
### 
German Club last night elected the
following members as officers for the
next quarter: Miss Natalie Wright,
'18, president; Miss Victorine Schmitz,
'18, vice-president; Miss Lucile Town
ley, '20, secretary; Lloyd Casebeer,
'18, treasurer. After the election a
program was given. Professor Bruno
Boezinger sang two Swiss songs, and
Miss Katherine Crabtree, '18, gave
humorous German readings.
