# Delta Tau Delta House Has Fire
## 
### 
A fire resulting in the loss
of the roof and considerable
damage from water to the
i rooms on the third floor broke
out in the Delta Tau Delta
house this afternoon at 3
o'clock.
It is believed the conflagra
tion resulted from crossed
electric wires in the attic, and
the damage is estimated at
between $3800 and $4000.
Most of the furniture and
personal property was saved.
The loss is covered by in
