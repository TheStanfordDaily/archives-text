# TENNIS TOURNAMENT TO BE HELD TOMORROW
## 
### 
The last local tenuis tournament
will he played tomorrow at 9 o'clock
on the Encina courts. It will he run
in two divisions, one for freshmen,
and one for varsity contenders. All
may take part in this tournament,
and there are still chances for new
material to make the teams, which to
a large extent will he determined by
the results of tomorrow's meet.
The freshman racket wielders meet
California during the spring vaca
tion, on March 29, on the Berkeley
courts. The varsity meet is sched
uled for April 19. and will he played
hero.
