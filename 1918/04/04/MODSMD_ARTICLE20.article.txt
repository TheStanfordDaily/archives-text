# CAESAR ADDIMANDO TO CONDUCT CONCERT
## 
### 
Caesar Addimando, the Italian
musician and member of the San
Francisco Symphony, will conduct
the Palo Alto Symphony Orchestra in
its annual concert given tonight in
i the Assembly Hall at 7:30 o'clock.
The orchestra numbers over thirty
-1 five pieces, musicians from San Fran
| cisco, Palo Alto. Camp Fremont, and
! Stanford assisting. Faculty members
! who will play tomorrow night are
! Professor and Mrs. J. C. Clark, (Pro
i lessor Clark is president of the local
! symphony society), Professor A. C.
Whitaker, Assistant Professor G. F.
Evans, and Professor C. B. Whittier.
The public is invited to attend.
There will be no admission charged.
