# 1918 Class To Discuss Plans for Senior Week
## 
### 
Senior President C. S. Kegley to
day called a meeting of the 1918 class
for 12:45 tomorrow. Plans for this
year's senior week are to be dis
cussed, as well as arrangements for
class dues for the present quarter at
the meeting, and the officers wish all
fourth-year members to be present, in
order that the sentiment of the class
may be determined on these impor
tant subjects.
