# ELECT TENNIS CAPTAIN
## 
### 
At a meeting today the Cardinal ten
nis squad elected W. C. Bent, '19, to
captain tho Varsity for the California
tournament, to be held on the Berk
eley courts April 12. Captain Bent
stated he would develop a doubles
team this week. Things look some
what unfavorable for Stanford, due to
the fact that no doubles matches have
been played with any consistency all
season. Members of the squad must
show up the few remaining days be
fore the tournament.
