# STANFORD MAN DIES AT TRAINING SCHOOL
## 
### 
Word lias been received here of the
death from pneumonia of L, P. Harts
horn, ex-'ID, on April 3 at the St.
Donover Hospital in Minneapolis,
Minn. He became ill while at the
University of Minnesota Training
School. Hartshorn was a chemistry
major at Stanford and registered from
Hoquiam, Wash. He was a member
of Encina Club and Alpha Chi Sigma,
lie enlisted in the naval militia when
the war was declared and was trans
ferred to the Naval Hospital Corps.
He later went into the Minnesota
Training School.
