# SPRING PLEDGES OF SORORITIES NAMED
## 
### 
Five sororities have announced new
pledges after one week of rushing.
These are: Kappa Kappa Gamma,
Eleanor Anderson; Chi Omega, Ma
rion Lonabaugh; Gamma Phi, Thelma
Carlyle; Delta Delta Delta, Margaret
Wislizenus, and Sigma Kappa, Zelva
Wickersham.
Rushing among the women this
year was comparatively quiet, as only
sixteen new women students entered
college.
