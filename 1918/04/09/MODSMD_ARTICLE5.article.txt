# CHAPMAN IS ELECTED TO TRACK CAPTAINCY
## 
### 
L. L. Chapman, '18, was elected to
act as captain of the track team at a
meeting of the track squad last night.
Chapman succeeds to the position
made vacant by the absence of A. F.
Wilson, '18, who was called into the
aviation service Sunday.
Chapman is the veteran of the squad
and the only Varsity track "S" man
in college. He has run for the Card
inal for three successive years, star
ring as a freshman, and establishing
the intercollegiate two-mile record in
his sophomore year.
Chapman lives in Palo Alto, is mar
ried, and the proud father of three fu
ture long distance runners.
