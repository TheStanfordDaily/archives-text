# Theta Sigma Phi Holds Its Initiation Today
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women's national
journalistic honorary soeiety, will
hold its initiation this afternoon at
4 o'clock in the Women's Club House.
The initiates arc Margaret Richter,
'15, Harriet McCausland, '17, Ruth
Mantz, '10, and Katherine Amos, '18.
