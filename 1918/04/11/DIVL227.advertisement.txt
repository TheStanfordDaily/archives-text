DO YOU KNOW HER?
There is a job here waiting for a
college-trained girl. It is really
more than a job—it's an opportun
ity—a splendid chance for the right
girl to learn and to work in mail
orders, advertising, office routine,
and to be of service in various
ways all over our organization. It's
an interesting job and it offers a
well rounded business training as
well as a living and a future. If
you are or if you know a recent
graduate ambitious for an opening
tell her that
WE HAVE A SPLENDID
POSITION FOR HER
She must have these qualifications:
college training, a few months of
experience out of college, ability to
use a typewriter, much initiative
and pep, common sense and a de
termination to make good for the
love of the game.
WE WANT HER RIGHT
AWAY

store, 151-1(53 Post street, San
Francisco. Come, or send us the
girl today. Applicants, please ask
for Mr. Goldsmith.
