# Daughter of Professor To Study Orthopedics.
## 
### 
Miss Kathrine Fairclough, daugh
ter of Professor H. R. Fairclough, has
arrived in England. She is taking up
special work under the famous sur
geon. Sir William Osier, at Oxford.
Miss Fairclough plans to specialize
in orthopedics with a view to restor
ing the limbs and feet of wounded
soldiers.
