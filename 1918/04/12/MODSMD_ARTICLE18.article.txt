Miss C. Haddock, secretary of the
women's physical education depart
ment, lias given out the following no
tice: Open periods for women's

p.m. beginning about May 1, Miss
Haddock expects to have the tank
open on Saturday morning from 10
to 11:45 a.m. The classes are all
large on account of (he mild weather
and the department hopes that the
girls will take advantage of the open
periods.
