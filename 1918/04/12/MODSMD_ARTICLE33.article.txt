# LILLY CONFIDENT OF WINNING BALL GAME

The rooters will be afforded a rare
treat tomorrow, when the Stanford
team trounces the team representing
the Golden Bear. The California team
is on the defensive, «viiile we are go
ing stronger every mning, and al
though the dope is In favor of the
nine from the north, it is safe to say
thai said dope will be decidedly
spilled.
One thing which makes me so cer
tain of victory tomorrow, is the fact
that the team, besides having more
than usual ability to hit and field, pos
sesses that essential quality fighting
spirit. A team which fights until the
last man is out in the ninth is a hard
team to boat, and sucti a team is the
present varsity.
