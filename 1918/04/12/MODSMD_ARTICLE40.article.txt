E. H. Ford, '14, is now with the At
lantic fleet on the high seas, taking
the course required of candidates for
an ensign's commission. He recently
left from the naval training station at
San Francisco, where he had been
stationed. He was editor of Sequoia
and president of the English Club
while here, and is a member of Kappa
Alpha.
