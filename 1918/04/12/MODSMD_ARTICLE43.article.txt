# Library Officers Give Out Hours for Visiting
## 
### 
Representatives of the California
State Library School announce that
they will hold office hours from 9 to
12 a.m. and from 1 to 5 p.m., on Mon
day, April 15, in the Student Adviser's
office in the Administration building.
All students who wish to attend the
State Library School in Sacramento
in both 1918 and 1919 are cordially
invited to consult the representatives
a( this time. Senior students and
junior students who wish to be teacher
librarians are now being accepted.
The plans for teacher librarian work
are especially interesting for students
according to the representatives.
