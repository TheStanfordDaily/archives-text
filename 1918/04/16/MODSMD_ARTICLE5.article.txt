# A.G. HALM DIES OF PNEUMONIA AT CAMP
## 
### 
Arthur G. Halm, '14, died April 11,
at Camp Lewis, Wash., of pneumonia.
He was attending the third officers'
training camp, to which he had been
recommended by the University.
Malm, during his senior year, was
president of the Associated 'Students,
and was also the baseball team.
He was a member of Delta Upsilon
fraternity. His home was in Phoenix,
Ariz.
