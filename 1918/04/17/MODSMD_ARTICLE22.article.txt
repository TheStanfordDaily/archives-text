Guild Appoints
New Caretaker.
The Students' Guild at its meeting
yesterday appointed a new caretaker
for the isolation hospital, since the
present caretaker, L. L. Chapman, '18,
has been called to take a government
position in a lumber camp of northern
California.
Red Cross Wool
To Be Distributed.
Red Cross wool for sweaters, hel
mets and socks will be distributed at
the Women's Club House on Wednes
day and Thursday between 12:45 and
1 o'clock, or may be obtained from
Ruby Hale at the Kappa Alpha Theta
house.
