# FRED ROGERS MARRIED
## 
### 
Fred Rogers, '19. was married on
April G, 1918, to Miss Beatrice Easter
day of Watsonville. Rogers is now in
the naval reserve. After serving at
the officers' school at San Pedro, he
was commissioned as an ensign. Later
he was sent to Annapolis to complete
his studies and while there was dis
tinguished with the rank of battalion
commander.
Rogers while at Stanford ran the
quarter-mile on the track team and
took an active part in dramatics. He
is a member of Delta Upsilon fratern
ity.
