# TEITSWORTH TO LEAD VARSITY TRACK TEAM
## 
### 
C. S. Teitsworth, '19, was elected
captain of the track team for next
year at a meeting of the squad at
"Pop" Franklin's yesterday afternoon.
Teitsworth won his block letter in the
California-Stanford meet last Satur
day by finishing second in the half
mile. He won the mile for the 1919
babes against California in his fresh
man year. He is also captain of the
cross-country team for next year.
Teitsworth has enlisted in the
naval reserve, but has not been called.
He is a . .chemical engineering major,
from Los Angeles. He is
president of Phi Lambda Upsilon,
chemical honor fraternity, and a mem
ber of Alpha Delta Phi fraternity.
