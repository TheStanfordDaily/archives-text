# ENTRIES FOR P.A.A. MUST BE IN TODAY
## 
### 
Entries for the P. A. A. track meet
to be held at Berkeley next week must
be in the hands of the Graduate Man
ager today, according to an announce
ment given out yesterday. The com
plete list must be compiled before
Saturday at the headquarters of the
organization and this means that
Stanford must have her entries com
plete today.
Several of the best athletes in the
country are entered in this meet, and
some fast time should develop. Any
student at the University is eligible
to enter the meet. The blanks for
registration can be secured at the
Graduate Manager's office.
