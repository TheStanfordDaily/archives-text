# SMITH TO LECTURE
## 
### 
Dr. J. Perrin Smith, professor of
paleontology, will lecture Sunday af
ternoon at 3 o'clock in the auditorium
of Golden Gate Park, San Francisco,
on the "Geology of California."
The lecture will be given under the
auspices of the California Academy of
Sciences, and is one of the popular
scientific lectures given weekly by
noted scientists.
Hr. F. M. McFarland, professor of
histology, was one of the former
speakers from Stanford. His subject
was: "Animal Experimentation and
Medical Progress."
