# G. and M. Smoker Will Be Held in Union Tonight
## 
### 
As previously announced the Geol
ogy and Mining Society smoker will
take place at the Union tonight at
7:15 o'clock. There will be no re
freshments served, but plenty of
smokes, however, will be supplied. Ad
mission will be free. The principal
speakers of the evening will be Dr.
Wilbur and Professor J. M. Stillman.
Upperclass men, and particularly
freshmen, are urged to attend the
smoker.
