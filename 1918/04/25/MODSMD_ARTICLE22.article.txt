# PRESS TO BUY BONDS
## 
### 
Employees of the University Press
are making arrangements to purchase
a $50 Liberty bond to be paid for by
means of a drawing, the amounts to
be paid by each member running up
to a dollar. Each employee of the
plant will contribute to the purchase
of the bond, according to Superintend
ent Garrett. The employees will vote
the bond to one of their number.
