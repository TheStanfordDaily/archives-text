# Red Cross Chapter Wants Old Papers and Magazines
## 
### 
People of this community have been
requested by the Palo Alto chapter
of the Red Cross to take any old
papers and magazines they have to
the railroad station - in Palo Alto to
morrow afternoon, where they will
be stored in freight cars there by
members of the Boy Scouts and
shipped away. They will be sold and
the money used for the benefit of the
Red Cross. The papers should be tied
in bundles.
