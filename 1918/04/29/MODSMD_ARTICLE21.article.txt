Miss Trace to Speak
At Y. W. C. A. Meeting
Miss Gertrude Trace, assistant in
the Psychology department, will speak
before the Y. VV, C. A. and the Red
Cross workers tomorrow afternoon in
the Women's Club House at 4:15
o'clock. Miss Isabelle Young, '19, will
give a vocal selection.
