Miss Sue Foster, '20,
Is Engaged
Miss Sue Foster. '20, announced her
engagement to LeCount Slocum,
ex-'IX, last night at the Pi Beta Phi
house. Slocum is a lieutenant at Fort
Sill, Oklahoma. He is expecting to
return soon to Camp Fremont. Miss
Foster is a member of Pi Beta Phi
and Lieutenant Slocum is a Chi Psi.
