# CHAPMAN TO LEAVE FOR SHIPPING WORKS
## 
### 
L. L. Chapman, '18, will leave to
morrow'for Humboldt county, where
he is to be engaged at the shipping
works. Chapman is well known on
the campus as captain of this year's
victorious track team, and holder of
the Pacific coast three-mile record,
and Stanford-California two-mile rec
ord.
While in the north lie will he sta
tioned probably at either Fort Seward
or Eureka, the two most importtant
shipping points for that work.
Chapman lu.s succeeded in complet
ing his scholastic work necessary for
graduation, doing one quarter's work
during the past month.
