# Fatimas – "500" Will Be Main Features at the Big D.P.A.-Tri Delt"Smoker"
## 
### 
Fatinms, girls—Fatimas, Fatimas
everywhere, but not a drop to drink.
Thus will be the order at the Delta
Delta Delta house when the members
of the Dippy staff, fair and otherwise,
assemble for their semi-occasional
smoker tonight. The window curtains
will be pulled on this exclusive af
fair, as was decided by a unanimous
vote of the feminine members of the
staff, who constitute an uproarious
majority in such cases.
As at all smokers, special stunts
will be pulled, such as blowing smoke
from the eyes* The Dippy staff smok
ers have always been among the most
momentous events of the college year.
They are held whenever enough inno
cent freshman journalists accumulate
to be beguiled into throwing such a
mean party. In former days they
were staged in Redwood, but the pres
ent coterie were not allowed to hold
the affair at Jack's, so they chose the
Tri-Delt house. Right here, it might
be said that tonight's smoker prom
ises to outdo all former ones, in every
way.
