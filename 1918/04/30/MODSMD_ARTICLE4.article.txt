# FRESHMAN JOLLY-UP SLATED FOR MAY 14
## 
### 
A freshman jolly-up is being
planned for Friday, May 17. The
place decided upon for the affair is
the Encina club room.
Wallace Ferris, president of the
freshman class, promises that the
dance will be the best one the first
year class has put on during the
year. Several novel stunts are being
planned and Don McNamee's or
chestra will furnish the '"jazz" for
the occasion.
The following committee has been
named to make the necessary ar
rangements for the jolly-up: How
ard Gould, chairman; Alice Wilson,
Itoy Hill; ex-officio members, Wal
lace Ferris and Ann Hardy.
