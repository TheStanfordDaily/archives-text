Lagunita Driveway to
Be Newly Curbed
Work on the cement curbings or
the Lagunita drive is now in pro
gress, and the contractors estimate
that the driveway will be completely
curbed in three weeks. Tho road
leads from the postoffice to the old
Acacia house, where it joins the
county road. The work is being done
by the Montoya Co.
