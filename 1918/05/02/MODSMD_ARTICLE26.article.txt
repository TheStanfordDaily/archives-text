# Thetas Give Dinner Party for Dr. Pierce and Wife
## 
### 
Dr. Ulysses G. B. Pierce, chaplain
of the United States senate, and his
wife, together with the Rev. and Mrs.
D. C. Gardner, Professor and Mrs. C.
D. Marx and Professor and Mrs.
H. R. Fairclough were entertained
last evening at dinner at the Kappa
Alpha Theta House.
