# BIBLICAL PAGEANT TO BE HELD AT FREMONT
## 
### 
Gurnet Holme and players, of San
Francisco and the University of Cali
fornia, will present a Biblical pageant
portraying the story of Abraham and
Isaac, at Camp Fremont under the
auspices of the Y. M. C. A. next Sun
day afternoon at 3 o'clock.
The pageant links up the story of
self-sacrifice to the present day situa
tion in the call for sons to the war
service for the sake of the cause of
the Allies. It will be given under the
patronage of Major-General Morrison.
