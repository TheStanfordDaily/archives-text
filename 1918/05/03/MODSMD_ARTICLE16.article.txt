##

tion to its contents?
Or have you passed it by?

attempt to compile a history of Stanford men in the service, and the work
will be doubly difficult if students in college now dow not co-operate in secur
ing data for the history. Every one has some "dope" on Stanford men in
service that will aid the eommittee. Remember the box to keep it going.
