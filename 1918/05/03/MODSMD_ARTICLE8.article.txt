# PROFESSOR WEST TO SPEAK MONDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
"Some Aspects of Internationalism"
will be the subject of a lecture by Pro
fessor V. J. West, who will speak on
this topic before the Cosmopolitan
Club Monday evening. The time will
be 8 o'clock, and the place the Wo
man's Club House. The club an
nounces that the meeting will be open
to the general public.
