# ORIGINAL FEATURES FOR 1920 JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
Real "eats" instead of the usual
punch alone, In the way of refresh
ments, are to be served at the 1920
class jolly-up to be held Friday even
ing, May 10. The exact nature of the
refreshments has not yet been di
vulged by the committee.
The orchestra will probably be com
posed of off-campus talent, according
to the latest report. If possible, stunts
and songs will be given by the musi
cians.
The jolly-up is to be hold in En
cina Hall. It will begin at 8:30 o'clock
sharp and the regular admission of
50 cents will be charged. All students
are invited.
