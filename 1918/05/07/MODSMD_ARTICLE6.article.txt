# Sale of Savings Stamps Shows Increase for April
## 
### 
Figures given out by Postmaster G.
E. Meekins on the sale of War Sav-
I
ings Stamps for the past few months
show a decided increase for the
month of April. April's sales totaled
$871.70 in comparison to $556.76 for
December, $593.25 for January,
$1,127.23 for February, and $703.86
for March. Last month's sales were
only exceeded by those of February,
when a house-to-house campaign was
conducted. According to the post
master, these sales are very good con
sidering the number of residents on
the campus.
