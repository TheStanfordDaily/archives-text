# W.E. ANTRIM TO HAVE OPERATION PERFORMED
## 
### 
W. E. Antrim, '19, received a sudden
attack of appendicitis Sunday and will
be operated on this week end. The
operation will be performed by Dr.
H. B. Reynolds at the Peninsula hos
pital. Antrim is well known on the
campus and Is a member of several
honor societies. He is a member of
Theta Delta Chi.
