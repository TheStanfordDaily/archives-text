# QUAD COUPONS TO BE TRADED AT LAW STEPS
## 
### 
Coupons bought at the Bookstore
for the 1919 Quad will be redeemable
on the morning the books are put on
sale in front of the Law steps. This
announcement was made today by tfte
management of the annual.
The josh section for the Quad has
now practically been completed and
will be a feature, which alone will be
worth the price of the book. The
printers are working day and night to
havo the copies ready by the latter
part of this month and are succeeding.
The fraternity and sororoty sections
are practically completed, and the or
ganizations section will go on the
press this week
