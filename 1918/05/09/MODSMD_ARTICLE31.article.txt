# A.T. Murray Appointed As Food Administrator
## 
### 
Professor A. T M.urray of the Greek
department has been appoined food
administrator for the northern unit
of Santa Clara county. Professor
Murray takes the place of P. M. Lans
dale, president of the Bank of Palo
Alto, who has been forced to resign
because of continued ill health. De
spite the difficulties with which he has
had to contend, Mr. Lansdale has or
ganized the work very well, and Pro
fessor Murray's duties will consist
mainly of executive duties.
