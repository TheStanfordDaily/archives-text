# STANFORD'S R.O.T.C. WILL HOLD REVIEW
## 
### 
Word was received yesterday after
noon that, all men who have signed
up for the next reserve officers' train
ing camp will be required to report
for duty at Camp Fremont on May
15th.
In honor of those'of its members
who will enter the camp at this time
tanford's R. O. T. C. will hold a fare
well military review tomorrow after
noon at 4:30 o'clock. The scene of
the manoeuvers will be the Faculty
Club grounds and the faculty, stu
dents, and general public are cordially
invited to be present.
