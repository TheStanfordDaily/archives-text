# Women's Swimming Meet Events Are Announced
## 
### 
Events for the women's interfrater
nity swimming meet on next Wednes
day afternoon have been planned as
follows: 25-yard free style, 50-yard
breast stroke, 50-yard free style, div
ing, 25-yard back stroke, 220-yard free
stroke, and the relay race.
The ten who receive the greatest
number of points on Wednesday will
contest, with the Women's Athletic
Club of San Francisco on Saturday,
May 25, at the Roble pool.
Miss Estelle Nisson, *18, captain,
and Miss Jean Campell, *-8, manager,
are in charge of both meets.
The events Wednesday will start
promptly at 4:15.
