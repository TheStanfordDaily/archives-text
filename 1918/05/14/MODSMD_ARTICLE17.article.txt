# RED CROSS TINFOIL DRIVE BEGINS MAY 18
## 
### 
Saturday, May 18, is the date which
has been selected for a tinfoil drive to
be held in San Francisco under the
auspices of the Red Cross. Palo Alto
and Stanford have been asked to aiil
and the Stanford Red Cross commit
tee has set 100 pounds as the mini
mum amount of tinfoil to be sent
from this community.
All donations must be turned in to
the salvage headquarters, No. 2 Sal
vatierra, not later than Thursday
night, as a special machine will leave
for San Francisco Friday morning.
