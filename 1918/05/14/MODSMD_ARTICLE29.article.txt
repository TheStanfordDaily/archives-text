# SCHUBERT CLUB GIVES UP PLAN FOR RECITAL
## 
### 
Schubert Club lias indefinitely post
poned the presentation of the recital
which it was preparing for tin l benefit
of tlie Women's Club House, in order
♦ o concentrate its efforts on the song
work for the musical festival.
As the club will hold only two more
meetings this quarter, the election of
officers will take place next Monday
afternoon, May 20. The president is
anxious that all members remember
to attend so that a representative vote
may be obtained.
