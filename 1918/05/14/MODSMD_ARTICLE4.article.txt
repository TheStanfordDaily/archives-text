# BEN ALLEN, '07, SERIOUSLY ILL IN BERKELEY HOSPITAL
## 
### 
Ben Allen, '07, head of the publicity
division of the Hoover Food Adminis
tration, is seriously ill in a Berkeley
hospital, according to a news dispatch
from Marysville. This report states
that Allen suffered a severe attack of
inflammatory rheumatism while a
guest at Bella Boka Club near Antlers,
Shasta county. He came west recent
ly in the interest of the food adminis
tration.
Allen is well known as a newspaper
man, and before he joined Hoover in
food conservation work was stationed
at London as correspondent for the
Associated Press.
