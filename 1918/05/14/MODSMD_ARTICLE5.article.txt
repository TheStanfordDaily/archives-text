# KARL E. LEIB WILL ENTER NAVAL RESERVE
## 
### 
Karl E. Leib, instructor in English,
has turned over most of his classes to
M. L. McClintock preparatory to leav
ing for San Pedro where he will be
come a member of the Naval Reserve.
Mr. Leib enlisted several weeks
ago and has been awaiting call for
several days which accounts for his
presence on the campus at the pres
ent time as it was announced he
would leave last week. Mr. Leib is a
public speaker of recognized merit
and has taught'public speaking at the
University for several years.
