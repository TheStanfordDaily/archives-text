# G.L. BEAVER ENGAGED TO NEW YORK WOMAN
## 
### 
The engagement of 'Miss Adelaide
Hazzard of Mount Vernon, New York,
and George L. Beaver, '15, of Palo Alto,
was announced recently by the bride's
parents. Miss Hazzard is the daugh
ter of Dr. and Mrs. Charles Hazzard
of Mount Vernon, N. Y.
Beaver, who has just been commis
sioned a first lieutenant in the 303 d
Engineers, is stationed at Camp Dix.
New Jersey. He was a graduate of
the Electrical Engineering department
and the president of the Electrical
Engineering Society. He served on
Men's Conference and was a mem
ber of Theta Xi.
