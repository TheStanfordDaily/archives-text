# Dr. Stillman To Lecture on Ancient Chemistry
## 
### 
Dr. John Maxson Stillman, former
head of the Chemistry department
and vice-president of the University,
will give a lecture at an open meet
ing to be held jointly by Alpha Chi
Sigma and Phi Damda Upsilon, chem
ical fraternities, next Monday, May
20, at 4:15, in the organic lecture
room of the Chemistry building.
Dr. -Stillman's topic will be "Items
of Chemical Knowledge of the An
cients."
