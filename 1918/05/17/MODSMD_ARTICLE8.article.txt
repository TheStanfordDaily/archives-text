# R.W. DOANE MAKING INSPECTION OF FOOD
## 
### 
Professor R. W. Doane of the En
tomology department, consulting en
tomologist for the Federal Food Ad
ministration for California, is now
making an inspection of the mills and
warehouses in Los Angeles and other
places in Southern California.
These inspections are being made
in order that any attack of insects in
stored food products may be detected
and serious losses avoided.
