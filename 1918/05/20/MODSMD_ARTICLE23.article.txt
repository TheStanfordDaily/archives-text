# Review's Appearance Is Guaranteed Tomorrow
## 
### 
Issuance of the Stanford Illustrated
Review is guaranteed tomorrow.
Originally the Review was to have
come out last week but has been de
layed considerably in the press.
Among the leading articles in the May
issue will be an explanation of the
alleged German motto in the Stan
ford seal, and a war article by Frank
J. Taylor, ex-'lB.
