# Captains Are Chosen for Women Ushers at Festival
## 
### 
Miss Harriet Bradford, dean of wo
men, called a meeting Saturday morn
ing of twenty-five women, as captains
of the ushering at the Song Festival.
Each captain is to have five other wo
men under her as assistants. Two
members of the R. O. T. C. will be as
signed to each section to help the
women in case o ftrouble with the
ushering.
All the captains are to meet on the
