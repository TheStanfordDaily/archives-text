# Ensign Curtis Will Talk On Enlistments in Navy
## 
### 
For the benefit of those students
who are contemplating entering the
navy with a view towards working
for a commission in the United States
Naval Reserve Force, Ensign W. B.
Curtis will meet all interested men
in room 263 of the engineering build
ing on Welnesday evening at 7:30
p: m. He will have information re
garding the various branches of the
naval service for which college men
are particularly qualified and will be
glad to answer any questions at that
time.
