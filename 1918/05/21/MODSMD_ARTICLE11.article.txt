# TWO WOMEN ELECTED TO THETA SIGMA PHI
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, the women's na
tional journalistic fraternity, an
nounces the election of Miss Ruth
Lakin, '18, and Miss Ruth Searcy,
'20, to its ranks. Initiation of the
new members will take place next
week.
Miss Lakin has served as reporter
for two and one half years and as
news editor for the last quarter on
the staff of the Daily Palo Alto. She
is a member of Delta Delta Delta.
Miss Searcy has served as reporter
for one year and as news editor for
the past two quarters on the Daily
Palo Alto. She has also contributed
a number of articles to the Stanford
Illustrated Review. She is a member
of Roble Club.
