# R.O.T.C. MEN MUST TAKE TARGET FIRING
## 
### 
Work on the target range for those
who liavo finished their preliminary
firing with the gallery rifles began
today with a small number of men
under the direction of Captain Park
er. Orders have been issued for all
men to report to the military depart
ment office as soon as possible the
afternoons in which they can take
their range work.
Men who have not completed their
gallery practice may do so in the
mornings. A satisfactory record
must be made before work can be un
dertaken on the target range.
