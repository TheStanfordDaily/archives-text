# SWIGART TELLS OF NAVAL RESERVE MEN
## 
### 
News of several Stanford men in
the Naval Reserve at San Pedro, Cali
fornia, was received on the campus
today in a letter from T. E. Swigart,
'17. At San Pedro personally asso
ciated with Swigart are W. H. Hayes,
'18, D. E. Tell, '19, D. W. Rohrback,
'14, C. M. Dcnham. '14, A. and L. A.
Ogden, '15. These men hear very lit
tle from the "Farm" at present, but
whenever an Illustrated Review or
other campus publication is found it
is eagerly read and re-read.
