# EUPHRONIA TO DEBATE PROHIBITION QUESTION
## 
### 
That a federal constitutional
amendment providing for national
prohibition should be adopted is the
question that G. O. P. Wood, '20, and
P. H. Goddard, '21, supporting the
affirmative will present tonight at
the weekly meeting of Euphronia in
room 100. The negative will be up
held by A. F. Simpson. '21, and R. B.
Montgomery, '20.
In order to accommodate the visit
ors, the Little Theater has been en
gaged for the evening and also for
the remaining meetings to be held
this year.
