# Mrs. Williams' Letter
## 
### 
A letter written by Mrs. William
P. Lucas of San Francisco, who is en
gaged in relief work in Belgium, de
scribing the pitiful condition of Bel
gian children, was read at the joint
meeting of the Red Cross and Y. W.
C. A. yesterday afternoon.
Miss Anna Van Winkle, head of the
information bureau at the new host
ess house at Camp Fremont de
scribed the valuable work that is be
ing done in army camps by the Y.
W. C. A. hostess houses. The host
ess house at Fremont will be open for
the first time Saturday from 2 to 5
p. m.and 8 to 10 p. m.
