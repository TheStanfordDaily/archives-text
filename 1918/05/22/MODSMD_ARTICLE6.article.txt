# TRAINING CAMP FOR MARINES ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Announcement of a summer train
ing camp for enlisted men in the
Marine Corps was made by Captain
Drew of the United States Marine
Corps yesterday afternoon to the
members of the Stanford R. O. T. C.
Men who have reached the age of
20 years and 9 months are eligible.
The candidates for the officers'
camp will go to a recruit camp for
three months and then competitive
examinations will, be held to deter
mine the number to enter the camp
in Virginia. The graduates from the
training school will be awarded com
missions in the marine corps.
