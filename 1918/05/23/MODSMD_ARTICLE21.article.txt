# COUNCIL ACTIONS
## 
### 
Stanford University, Cal..
May 16, 1918.
Women's Council recommends that
a junior student be allowed no en
gagements, either during the day or
in the evening, for the remainder of
the school year; that she report to the
president of her sorority at 8 o'clock
p.m. every evening for the remainder
of the school year, as penalty for
breaking the 10:30 rule three times
during the month of April.
JOSEPHINE WELCH.
President, Council.
Approved:
E. P. LESLEY, Chairman, Student
Affairs Committee.
Approved:
C. D. MARX. President.
