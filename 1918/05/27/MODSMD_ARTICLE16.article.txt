# Interest in Work For Red Cross on Increase
## 
### 
Last week's Red Cross report shows
a greater increase in the time spent
at the Women's Club House. The Stan
ford women are displaying marked
interest in doing their share of this
work, but a certain few houses have
thus far deserved the greatest credit.
Seventy-nine per cent of Delta Delta
Delta members worked eighteen hours
during this last week; Sigma Kappa,
76 per cent, twenty hours; Delta
Gamma, 45 per cent, eleven hours;
Kappa Kappa Gamma, 43 per cent,
twenty-three hours; Chi Omega, 50
per cent, fourteen hours; Gamma Phi
Beta, 62 per cent; Madrona Club, 27
per cent .eleven hours.
