# Adv. 13 Page 6
## 
### 
Owing to the impossibility of main
taining Iceland's wheat supplies with
the present shortage of shipping, the
government of Iceland has made
great preparations for the planting of
potatoes, from which they can obtain
potato flour, thus making the coun
try independent in this respect.
All the wheat flour saved by Cali
fornia will be shipped to Europe in
California-built ships, as fast as the
vessels are put in commission.
