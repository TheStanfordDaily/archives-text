Cercle Francais will present a
comedy, "Le Crime de M. Pompe," at
the last meeting of the club to be held
tomorrow evening in the Women's
Club House.
The French officers at Camp Fre
mont have been invited as guests of
honor at the occasion.
The members of the club taking
part in the play are: Miss Dorothy
Elfing, '21; Miss Elizabeth Brown, '21;
L. C. Hall. '20; Miss Alberta Marx,
'21; Miss Florence Holbertou, '21, and
L. M. Lissner, '19.
