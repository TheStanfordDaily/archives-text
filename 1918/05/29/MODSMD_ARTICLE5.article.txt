# Money Needed to Supply Soldiers With Baseballs
## 
### 
Money is being solicited through
the mail for tho Clark C. Griffith ball
and bat fund, which is being used to
furnish baseball equipment to the men
in active service here and abroad. The
fund has headquarters at Washington,
D. C. The paraphernalia is being
handled by the Y. M. C. A. and stren
uous efforts are being made to equip
every camp of American soldiers with
bats, balls, and glovrs. Donations can
be sent to the Clark C. Criflith Ball
and Bat Fund, 209 Union Savings
Bank Building, Washington, D. C.
