# Asilomar Talk at Y.W.C.A. Meeting
## 
### 
"Asilomar" was the subject of a
talk by Mrs. John Buwalda of Berke
ley before the Y. W. C. A. yesterday
afternoon. Mrs. Buwalda was for
merly president of the Y. W. C. A. at
Berkeley.
Asilomar conference will be held
from August 6 to 16 this year, and
the total expense will be only $2 per
person, which includes room, board,
and registration fee. Miss Mary Tut
hill, '20, is chairman of the Asilomar
conference at Stanford. Any one de
siring further information may get it
from her.
