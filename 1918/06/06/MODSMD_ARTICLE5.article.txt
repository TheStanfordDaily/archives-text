# Secretary of Senior Class Desires Delinquent Dues
## 
### 
Miss Antonia Hyatt .treasurer of
the class of 1918, announces that the
dues of the senior class are coming in
very slowly, and that unless more of
the members immediately comply with
the requirements and furnish the
money for their dues, the class will be
uable to purchase the numeral plate
for the Quadrangle in front of Memo
rial Church.
