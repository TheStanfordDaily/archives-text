# AUGUST REGISTRANTS ARE NOT ELIGIBLE TO THE S.A.T.C.
## 
### 
Men who registered for the selective
draft on August 24 last are not to he
admitted to the S. A. T. C., according
to a telegram received from Washing
ton Monday afternoon. Previously the
assumption had been that such men
would be permitted to enroll here, and
when tlie announcement was made, there
was a hurried exodus for applications
to officers' training camps on the part
of the some dozens of men registered
here who were twenty-one between
June sth and August 24th last.
