# Miss Dorothy Jones to Make Eastern Trip
## 
### 
Mis> Dorothy Jones, '20, a member
of Kappa Alpha Theta sorority, who
attended Stanford last spring, has
moved with her parents to Menlo Park,
where she will remain until her de
parture for Washington. While in col
lege Miss Jones was prominent in V. W.
C. A. work and a member of the Daily
Palo Alto staff.
