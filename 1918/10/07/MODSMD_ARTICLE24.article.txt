# Freshmen Hear Talk By President Wilbur
## 
### 
"Women's Part in the University"
was the theme of a short address made
;In Dr. Wilbur to the women held in
the Little Theater yesterday afternoon.
Miss Ruth Chandler, '19, president of
the Organized Women, introduced the
speaker. Dr. Wilbur urged the women
to realize how important their work is
and how vital their interests that they
earnestly continue their university
studies.
