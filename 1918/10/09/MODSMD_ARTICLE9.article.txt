# Mrs. Wilbur and Miss Bradford to Receive
## 
### 
All women students of the University
have been invited by Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur and Miss Harriet Bradford to
a reception to be given at the Presi
dent's house tomorrow afternoon. Both
old and new women students will be
welcomed between the hours of 4 and
6 by the President's wife and the dean
of women.
