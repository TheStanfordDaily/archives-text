# Stanford Men in Virginia O.T.C.
## 
### 
Marcy Jones, '17, who graduated this
summer from Stanford, is now at an
engineers' officers' training camp at
Camp S. S. Humphreys, Virginia. John
O. Miller, '06, a former Stanford ath
lete ; A. Berni, 'IS; L. Sherwin, '16; J.
Rose, '16, and F. Wasson, '16, are all
instructors at this camp. Two other
Stanford men stationed at this camp
now are Lieut. Cols. II. F. Cameron
and II. Lanagan.
