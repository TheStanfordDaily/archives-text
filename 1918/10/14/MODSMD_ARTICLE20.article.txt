# DR. STILLMAN ACTS AS ASSISTANT TO WILBUR
## 
###

assistant to President Wilbur during
the illness of Professor PI. E. Robin
son. Professor Robinson has just been
appointed as assistant to President Wil
bur in his work as district educational
director of California. Nevada, and
Utah, and it is in this capacity that Dr.
Stillman is acting.
