Goodwin J. Knight
Is Back Again
G. J. Knight, '19, who has been in the
officers' class at San Pedro for four
months, has returned to the University.
Under the S. A. T. C naval men may
take the examinations direct from the
University. Knight is well known on
the campus as manager of last year's
Quad. He is a member of Alpha Delta Phi.
