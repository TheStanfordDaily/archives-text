# STANFORD HANDBOOK WILL BE READY FOR DISTRIBUTION SOON
## 
### 
Within a short time copies of the
books of Stanford songs and yells which
arc now being prepared will be off the
press and circulated among the members
of the student body.
The books will be distributed free of
charge, and it is hoped that every stu
dent in the University will apply for one
of the pamphlets at the office of the stu
dent body president in the Union, if any
omissions are made in the circulation of
the books.
In addition to the most popular songs
and yells, the handbook will contain a
summary of the student organization and
government of Stanford and a page vo
cabulary of local expressions for the
benefit of the new students.
