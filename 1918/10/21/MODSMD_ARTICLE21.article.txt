# STANFORD WOMAN NOW OVERSEAS IN ARMY WORK
## 
### 
Miss Hannah Thompson, '12, who is
a reconstruction aide with the medical
department of the army, has arrived
safely overseas, according to cable dis
patches received on the campus. Miss
Thompson left her home in Pasadena
about three weeks ago for New York.
Miss Thompson expects to be as
signed to a hospital in France, where
her work will lie with convalescent sol
diers, giving them occupational instruc
tion to tit them for new work on their
return home or to hasten their re
covery.
Since graduation from Stanford Miss
Thompson has been art instructor in
several high schools in Southern Cali
fornia and has also spent some time
working in an airplane factory.
