# Mr. Maxwell Anderson Associate Editor of "Youth"
## 
### 
Mr. Maxwell Anderson, former in
structor of English here, is now repre
senting Stanford as associate editor for
the new magazine "Youth," poetry of
today, which is heing published by a
number of Harvard men.
The aim of the corresponding and as
sociate editors of the magazine will be
to secure the best poetry possible, for
publication. The magazine will appear
every two months.
