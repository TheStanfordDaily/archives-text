# Arthur Kimber, '18 Reported Killed
## 
### 
Arthur Clifford Kimber, ex-'lB, has
been reported killed in action in France,
according to recent dispatches received
here. At the beginning of the war Kim
ber crossed the seas and saw service in
the Second Stanford Ambulance Unit
and was the first Stanford man to take
the American Hag across the ocean.
Later Kimber was given a commission
of first lieutenant in tlie aviation section
of the signal corps.
Young Kimber's home was in Palo
Alto, where he is mourned by Mrs. A. C.
Kimber, his mother, and two brothers.
While at Stanford Kimber was an eco
nomics major.
