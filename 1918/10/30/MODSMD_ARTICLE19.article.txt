# Archie Preston,'05 Dies at Fort Riley
## 
### 
Word has been received of the death
of Archibald Edward Preston, '05, at
Fort Riley. Kansas. Deceased was a
captain in the engineers, liquid fire di
vision. and in a letter to Professor J. P.
Smith of the geology department about
ten days ago he stated that he was about
to go to France. It was assumed here
that he had arrived overseas when news
of his death arrived.
Preston had been an oil expert with
several of the country's largest corpora
tions prior to his enlistment. He will
also be remembered by Stanford people
as one of the University's greatest foot
ball players. He played end on the Var
sity.
