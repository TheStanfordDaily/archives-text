# EUGENE F. KERN, '13, PROMOTED TO CAPTAIN
## 
### 
Captain Eugene F. Kern. '13. has been
recently promoted to captaincy and re
turned to the United States as instruc
tor at Camp Meade, Md. He was badly
gassed and shelled at the Marne, but
has recovered sufficiently to be able to
instruct the recruits at Camp Meade.
Captain Kern won unusual distinction
in France. He was awarded the dis

tinguished service medal for bravery at
the Marne and received his promotion
from a first lieutenancy while in active
service in France.
In college Kern was a varsity foot
ball player, holding the center three
quarter? position of the 1913 varsity
He is a member of Beta Theta Pi.
