# FRATERNITY MEN BAND FOR SAFETY
## 
### 
Fraternity men not connected with
the S. A. T. C. have handed together
for mutual protection and have formed
an eating cluh which meets thrice daily,
starting yesterday, at the Kappa Alpha
house. The cluh is being run under the
efficient management of A. D. Wilkie,
'18 ,and includes most of the leading
Greek letter characters not desired hy
the army, and many freshmen whose
immature years dehar them from the
privilege of the mess hall.
