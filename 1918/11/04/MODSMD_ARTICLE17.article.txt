# RED CROSS OPENS FOR FIRST WORK OF YEAR
## 
### 
Red Cross rooms opened this after
noon at 2:30 in the new headquarters of
the Women's War Board. The rooms
will be open every afternoon, including
Saturday, from 2:30 p. m. until 5:30.
The work this afternoon consisted in
making property bags and scrap-books
for the soldiers. The surgical dressing
department will do no work this quar
ter.
The formal opening of the building,

which is being used for this work for
the first time this quarter, will be held
Friday night.
