# S.A.T.C. WILL AID ENGLISH CLUB SHOW
## 
### 
One of the officers of the S. A. T. C.
will meet with the English Club
Wednesday night in the Women's Club
House to give suggestions for the plan
ning of the Stanford history pageant
which is to he given probably in the
early part of February.
One of the features of the pageant in
bringing the history down to the present
will be the camouflage work of the S. A.
T. C. men. The club committee in
charge of the details of the pageant are
to be advised by Professor Rassett.
Old members of the club are asked to
meet at 7:30 Wednesday evening.
Alumni members are especially invited.
