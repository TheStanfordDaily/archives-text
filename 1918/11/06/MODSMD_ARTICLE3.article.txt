# Engineers Called Into the S.A.T.C.
## Men From Sequoia Barracks Will Be Inducted Into the Regular Army 
### 
Orders received today from the
War Department transfer 21 enlisted
men of the Engineering Enlisted Re
serve Corps into active service with
the S. A. T. C. and order them to re
port to the commanding officer of the
unit for duty. Special orders also
call for the transfer into active serv
ice with the S. A. T. C. of 26 enlisted
men of the Medical Enlisted Reserve
Corps who are now attending Stan
ford.

called into actice service with the S.
