# Frank Ordway, '18, Dies Of Pneumonia in East
## 
### 
Another gold star has been added to
Stanford's service flag with the death
of Frank Ordway, '18. His death oc
curred while he was en route from
Camp Lewis to the east with Base Hos
pital Unit 93. He contracted pneumo
nia on the journey and died at the Dee
Memorial Hospital at Ogden, Utah.
Ordway was a law major and regis
tered from Monterey. He was a mem
ber of Encina Club.
