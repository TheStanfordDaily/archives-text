# Funeral of W.D. Wetzel, '22 Was Held Yesterday
## 
### 
The funeral of Wilford D. Wetzel.
'22, who died last Sunday morning of
pneumonia, was held at Memorial
Church yesterday at 2 o'clock. The
ceremony was military in tone, an es
cort of twelve S. A. T. C. men con
ducting the body to and from the
church. After the brief service the
coffin was taken to Alta Mesa ceme
tery near Mayfield. Many of the boy's
relatives and friends attended the
funeral
