# FRESHMEN TO HOLD THEIR JOLLY-UP AT SOME FUTURE DATE
## 
### 
On November 23 the class of 11 will
hold its jolly-up. Where the event will
take place has not yet been decided.
But the freshman executive committee
will hold a meting soon and pick the
best place available.
The payment of the class dues of
25 cents are now in order. D. M.
Hancock, the babes' treasurer, is col
lecting them. The sooner the money is
paid the sooner the committee can make
the final arrangements.
Besides paying the class dues it is
up to the freshmen to learn tho Uni
versity songs and yells.
