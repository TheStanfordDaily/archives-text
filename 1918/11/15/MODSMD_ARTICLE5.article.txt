# Professor Heath To Undergo Operation
## 
### 
, H, Heallt* me zoolog> le
partment has been granted a leave of ab
sence by the school authorities in order
that he may undergo an operation upon
his leg As the result of a bad fall last
summer Dr Heath broke his leg, and
although it was set immediately, it failed
to knit properly ad has given him a great
deal of trouble ever since
Dr. Heath is now in the Lane Hos
pital, San Francisco, under the care of
Dr. Von Geldern It is possible that the
teg will have to be re-broken and re-set
before the injury is remedied
