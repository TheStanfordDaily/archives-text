# Botany Classes Make Saturday Excursion
## 
### 
The entire botany department last
Saturday toured to Moss beach via
King Mountain and Halfmoon Bay for
the purpose of studying the many
forms of plant life on the beach and
rocks nearby. About thirty students
in all took the trip, accompanied by
Professor G. J. Peirce, Professor L. L.
Burlingame and Professor L. Abrams.
