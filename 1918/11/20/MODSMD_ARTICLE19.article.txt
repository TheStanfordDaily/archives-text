# Leigh Allan, '99, Dies Of Influenza at Salt Lake
## 
### 
Leigh Allan, '99, died in Salt Lake
City as a result of pneumonia and in
fluenza the latter part of last week.
He was a Delta ITpsilon and a geology
major. He was the son of Mr. and
Mrs. Hamilton Alan, of Tacoma,
Wash., and was construction engineer
for the Northern Pacific Railroad and
lor the Oregon Short Line. He is sur
vived by a wife, a son and a daughter.
