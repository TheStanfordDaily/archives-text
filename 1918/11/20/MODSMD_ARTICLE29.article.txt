The War Department conducted a
mass singing convention at Reed Col
lege on November'14 and 15. All the
institutions in the northwest, having
units of the S. A. T. C., sent delegates
to Portland. This plan of the govern
ment is in recognition of the value of
singing in sustaining the morale of the
army. At Reed College the singing is
in charge of the chorister. Miss Louise
Huntley.
