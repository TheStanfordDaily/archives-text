# List of Fraternity Pledges Announced
## Fifty-six Men Are Elected by Local Chapters of Greek Organizations 
### 
The names of fifty-six pledges and
recent initiates were announced today
by the fraternities on the campus. A
small percentage of the men in the
appended list had already been in
itiated before Captain Parker, acting
on War Department orders, placed a
ban upon further initiations. Whether
or not this ban on initiations will be
removed following the signing of the
armistice is a question that will be
settled within the next few days, ac
cording to Captain Parker.
