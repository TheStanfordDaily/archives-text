# Problems of Spain Is Topic of Lecture by Dr. Scherill Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Spain Today and Her International
Problems." i- the topic which Dr. Ru
dolph Scherill has chosen for his lec
ture to be given in the Little Theater
at 7:30 tomorrow night, tie will ex
plain the situation of Spain in the war
and is expected to show how some of
her big problems have arisen and what
her attitude will be in the future.
Dr. Scherill is particularly well fitted
to speak on this topic, as he has spent
almost as much time in Spain as in
America, his native land. He has been
professor of Romanic languages at
thee University of California since 1910.
He made a fifteen month tour of Spain,
South America and Argentine, and
studied at the University of Madrid.
He is a member of Phi Beta Kappa
and has just published a book on "Lo-
pez de Vega," the great Spanish trag-
edy writer.
As a brilliant conversationalist Dr.
Scherill is well known and his lecture
promises to be spirited and entertain-
ing as well as full of current history
that will be of value to all students.
