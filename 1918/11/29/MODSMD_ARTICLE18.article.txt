# Basket Men Not to Play This Quarter
## 
### 
On account of the demobilization
of the S. A. T. C. there will be no
basketball team put out this season,
was the announcement that was
made by Lieutenant Ransohoff, coach
ol' the ex-team. Tin* team is to stop
practicing at once.
When the farm is* under civilian
rule once more there maye be a bas
ketball team, but nothing definite can
eb announced, however.
