# Three Members Elected by Theta Sigma Phi
## 
### 
Miss Dorothy Driacoll, '20, Miss
Ellen Calhoun, '20, and Miss Eleanor
Allen, '20. were elected yesterday into
membership of Theta Sigma Phi,
women's journalistic fraternity.
The newly elected members have
been and are prominent in journalism
on the Campus. Miss Driscoll is ed
itor of the 1920 Quad, managing editor
of the Daily Palo Alto, and a junior
member on the staff of the Stanford
Review. She is a member of Kappa
Kappa Gamma sorority.
Miss Calhoun has been active in the
war work campaigns, is a member of
the Daily Palo Alto staff and of Kappa
Alpha Theta house. Miss Allen is also
on the staff of the Daily. She is a
member of Alpha Phi sorority.
