# ELMER WORTHY, '14, IS CITED FOR BRAVERY
## 
### 
Elmer T Worthy, '14, has been cited
for bravery during the fighting in the
Argonne forest, and as his reward to
day wears the gold bars.
Lieutenant Worthy was top sergeant
of his company, which trained at Camp
Lewis. When under fire he charged a
hill and captured a number of mac lime
Ktin nests. He was granted a commis
sion for gallantry in action.
Worthy is the son of J. T. Worthy
of Wintcrsburg, and a brother of A. E.
Worthy, secretary of the Y. M. C. A.
here. He was a lawyer before aroing
