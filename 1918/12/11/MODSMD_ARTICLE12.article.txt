# GEOLOGY AND MINING MAJORS WILL HOLD SMOKER TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Geology and Mining Society will
hold a smoker tonight for all men reg
istered in the department. All fresh
men registered in geology and mining
arc especially urged to attend. The S.
A. T. C. men will be excused in order
to attend and enjoy the free smokes and
entertainment. No admission will be
charged and everybody is urged to be
on time, 7:15.
