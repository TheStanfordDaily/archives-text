# STUDENT CONFERENCE OF Y.M. DURING VACATION
## 
### 
A meeting of students interested in
the Y. M. C. A. student conference,
which will take place at Asilomar, near
Pacific Grove, from December 26 to 31,
was held last Monday evening at the
mess hall. E. L. Dcvendorf, Y. M. C. A.
secretary from the University of Cali
fornia. addressed the students, and Sec
retaries Worthy and Morgan assisted in
explaining the minor details. Fifteen
men have already signed up definitely to
attend the conference, and it is expected
at least fifteen more will go.
