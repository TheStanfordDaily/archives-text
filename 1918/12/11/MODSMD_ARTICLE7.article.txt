# DRAWING FOR ENCINA ROOMS COMES TONIGHT
## 
### 
All men who intend to live in En
cina Hall next quarter are to meet in
Encina tonight at 7 o'clock for room
drawings. The method of securing
rooms will be as follows: First, the
graduates will line up in alphabetical
order and draw for rooms, followed
in order by the seniors, juniors, soph
omores, and freshmen, each class do
ing its drawing in alphabetical order.
The number of floors that will be
opened up depends on the number of
men that draw for rooms, so that It Is
important that all men intending to
live in Encina be there.
