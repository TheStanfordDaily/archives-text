# TIME SCHEDULES ARE ON SALE DECEMBER 20
## 
### 
It ifl planned to issue the time sched
ule for the winter quarter earlier than
usual and so as to be placed on sale at
the Business Office Friday, December
2C. Registration books, containing all
of the various blank forms which have
to do with registration, may be obtained
at the Registrar's Office a few days be
fore registration day, if desired. The
new books are so arranged that no writ
ing need be done by the student in the
Registrar's Office. The book is to be
returned on registration day, when all
of the blanks have been duly filled out.
Giving out books before registration day
is merely for the convenience of stu
dents who may be on the campus at that
time; others will receive them on regis
tration day, as usual.
