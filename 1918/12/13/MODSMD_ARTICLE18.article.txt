# Y.M.C.A. TO AID IN SECURING EMPLOYMENT
## 
### 
The Stanford Y. M. C. A. has issued
the following statement in regard to
employment for next quarter:
"Over fifty Stanford students have
already ipplied at the Y. M. C. A. office
for employment for the winter quarter.
It is expected that at least a hundred
men students will have to earn at least
a part of their expenses in order to re
main in college.
"The disbanding of the S. A. T. C.
of Stanford has greatly intensified the
employment situation. About thirty
men worked all or part of their way
through school during the fall quarter.
The Y. M. C. A. is making every ef
fort possible to find all the available
positions that students can fill. Any
person who has any such position or
knows of any is requested to file same
with the Stanford Y. M. C. A. at the
Union building, telephone Palo Alto
