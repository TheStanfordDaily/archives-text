# WOMEN'S GYMNASIUM PROSPECTS GROWING
## 
### 
Women's Uymnasium classes, due to
yesterday's enrollment, are rapidly fill
ing up, and the prospects are for a'busy
year in all the various activities. Enter
ing freshman women will be required
to enroll in elementary gymnasium as
was the ruling last quarter, but upper
class women are free to choose between
hockey, tenuis, dancing, fencing and
swimming. Schedules for games with
the University of California and Mills.
College will be published as soon as the
different classes are organized for def
inite work,
