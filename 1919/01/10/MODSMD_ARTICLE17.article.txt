# UNION CO-OP STORE WILL OPEN MONDAY
## 
### 
Under new management the Union
co-operativc cigar store announces that
it will open for business Monday, Jan
n It is the intention of the
store tn carry a Tun nur <x
goods and a new and attractive line of
candies.
To fill the many demands for a place
of this kind the management plans to
furnish all the conveniences possible
for the members of the Union, now in
cluding every man registered in col
lege. A telephone is to be installed and
University publications, including the
Quad, will be available.
