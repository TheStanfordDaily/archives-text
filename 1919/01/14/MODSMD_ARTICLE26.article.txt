# DR. E. W. MARTIN IS ORDERED TO GREECE
## 
### 
Word has just been received by Mrs
E. W. Martin, 525 Lincoln avenue, Palo
Alto, that Captain E. W. Martin has
been ordered by the Red Cross to
Greece. Captain Martin was formerly
an associate professor in the Greek de
partment at Stanford University, and
lie had been at Stanford sincc 1902 until
the Untied States entered the war. He
then enlisted and was made a captain,
and went with the Fourth Division of
the A. E. F. as field director of the
American Red Cross.
