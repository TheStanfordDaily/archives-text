# Wierd Costumes Appear At Roble Club Party
## 
### 
The Roble Club "get-together" party
given at Roble Hall last evening was
a marked success. The affair was
lancy dress, and much amusement was
caused by the variety of fantastical
costumes. Every country on the globe
was represented, from the Igoroti
head-hunter to the Spanish senorita.
Dancing and general good time occu
pied the interval between dinner and
study hours.
