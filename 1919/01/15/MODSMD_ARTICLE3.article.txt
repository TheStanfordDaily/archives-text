# UNIVERSITY RED CROSS CHAPTER IS DISCONTINUED
## 
### 
That all Red Cross work oti the Stan
ford campus will lie discontinued, al
though the central committee which is in
charge of Miss Henrietta Dickey, '20.
will not be dissolved, was announced by
the Red Cross officials in Palo Alto.
Although inactive, the auxiliary Will
support all drives that are scheduled for
this quarter. A big Red Cross campaign
will follow the Syrian and Armenian
Drive now in progress.
All those that have taken out yarn are
requested to turn in their work by Fri
day, January 17.
