# RAYMOND C. BROOKS TO SPEAK AT Y.W.C.A.
## Pastor First Congregational Church at Berkeley to Address Students 
### 
Mr. R. C. P>rooks, pastor of the First
Congregational Church of Berkeley, will
speak at a Students' Meeting of the
V. W. C. A. tomorrow evening at 4:15
in the Woman's Club House. "Toward
the Sunrise" is the topic of his lecture,
in which he will review the history of
the Armenian people in an effort to
bring out their chief characteristics.
Mr. Brooks is well acquainted with a
gentleman who has lived among these
Eastern people for fifty years, conse
quently he has a true story of Armenian
sufferings at the hands of the Turks.
Miss Margaret Shedd, '20. will lead
the meeting to which all are cordially
invited. A violin solo by Miss 'Gene
Smith, '22, will close the program.
