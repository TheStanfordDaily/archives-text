# UPPERCLASSMEN TO HOLD JOLLY-UP ON FIRST OF FEBRUARY
## 
### 
The upperlass jolly-up committee
which met at the Delta Gamma House
last night decided that the Junior-Senior
Jolly-up, postponed from last quarter,
will be given February 1. It will be held
at Encina Hall.
There will be a special feature in the
form of a lottery, the winners receiving
checks for use at Wilson's.
"Swede" Larson's orchestra will prob
ably furnish the music.
