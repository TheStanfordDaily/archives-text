# R.C. Collins, '22, Is Typhoid Victim
## 
### 
Raemond C. Collins of Spokane,
Wash., died of typhoid fever at the Iso
lation Hospital last night. Collins was
a freshman here and majored in
mechanical engineering. He came here
from Washington State College in order
to enter the S. A. T. C., and during that
time lived at Encina Hall.
