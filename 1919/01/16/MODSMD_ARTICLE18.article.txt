# M.A. Kline Wants Junior Quad Dues
## 
### 
According to an announcement
'made this morning, Junior Quad dues
and cost of space ordered by different
organizations is now payable also to
M. A. Kline, '21, at the Alpha Delta
Phi house, whose name has been add
ed to the list of tryoutees for business
manager of the 1921 Quad.
The business directors of the Quad
are urging the organziations and
juniors to pay up promptly in order to
get a better estimate on the probable
Quad income.
