# EUPHRONIA HOLDS FIRST DEBATE ON NEW AMENDMENT
## 
### 
The prohibition amendment to the
Constitution was urged at the meeting
of the Euphronia Debating Society last
night. A decision was rendered after
the debate in favor of the affirmative,
which was upheld by L. W. Hodges,
'21, and B. W. Hartman, '20.
All the old members are especially
urged to attend the next meeting, which
will be for the purpose of discussing
business and voting in new members.
