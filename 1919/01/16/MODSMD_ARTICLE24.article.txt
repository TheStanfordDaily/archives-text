# Practice for Spring Oratorio Wednesday
## 
### 
Practice for the oratorio, which will
be Jield at the end of the spring quarter,
will begin next Wednesday night at 7:30.
preceding choir practice, and will be
under the direction of Warren B. Allen,
choir director and organist. Although
the oratorio to be presented has not
been definitely decided upon, it is
thought that "The Creation" will be
sung. One unit of credit will be given
for each semester's work in the oratorio.
