# Peninsula Concert To Begin at 8:15 Tonight
## 
### 
Miss Lucy Gates and the Trio de
Latere will begin the first concert of
the series offered by the Peninsula
Musical Association tonight at 8:15
in the Assembly Hall. The program,
which consists of two numbers by
Miss Gates and three by the Trio de
Lutece, promises an evening of excep
tional entertainment.
It. is hoped that a large number of
students will take this opportunity of
hearing such noted artists. A few sin
gle tickets may yet be obtained at the
University Pharmacy in Palo Alto.
