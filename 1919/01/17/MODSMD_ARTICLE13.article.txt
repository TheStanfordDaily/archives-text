# Lieut. Harold H. Sayer Killed in Air Service
## 
### 
Confirmation of the death of Flight
Lieutenant Harold H. Sayer has just
been received. Lieutenant Sayer was
killed in action on December 14, 1918.
He came from Calgary, Canada, and
while at Stanford was a member of
the Alpha Tau Omega fraternity.
