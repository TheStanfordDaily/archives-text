# Miss Overacker In Civil Service
## 
### 
Miss Louise Overacker, A. B. '15,
A. M. '16, who majored in economics
while at Stanford, has recently been
appointed to the head of the civil
service classification department of
the United States Bureau of Efficiency
at Washington.
Miss Overacker went to Washington
last July to take a second assistant
ship in this department, was soon pro
moted to first assistant, and is now at
the head.
