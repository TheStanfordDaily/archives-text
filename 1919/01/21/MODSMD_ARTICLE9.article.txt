# COACH EVANS MAKES CALL FOR MATERIAL
## 
### 
A call has been sent out by Coach
Evans for battery material for the
varsity baseball team. The coach plan.'
to begin working on these men at oncc
and urges that every man who expects
to go out for pitcher or catcher on the
Varsity see him as soon as possible.
For the purpose of getting in touch
with battery candidates Coach Evans
will he at the gymnasium Wednesday
and Thursday between the hours of nine
and twelve in the morning and one and
three in the afternoon.
