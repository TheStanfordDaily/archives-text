# Lieut. W.M. Wyman, '11, Among Week-end Visitors
## 
### 
Among the recent week-end visitors
was Lieut. William M. Wyman, '11,
lately returned from the Argonne.
Lieut. Wymaji took part in the big
drive, in which he was wounded, and
is now returning to his home at San
Luis Obispo. While at Stanford, Lieut.
Wyman held the Stanford-California
record for the 440, his time being 50
flat. He was a law major and member
of Tlieta Delta Chi.
