# LEADERSHIP CLUB IS FORMED BY Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
A Leadership Club for girls, unaer
the auspices of the Y. W. C. A., has
been organized by Joyce Gardner.
The first meeting will be held in the
Women's Club House this evening.
Every girl is urged to attend. The
meeting promises to be a very inter
esting affair, according to the com
mittee in charge. A few lessons in
first aid will be given, and a program
with good stunts will be rendered.
