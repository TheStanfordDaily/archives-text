# WOMEN'S BASKETBALL PRACTICE TO START TUESDAY AT GYM
## 
### 
Practice for women's inter-class bas
ketball will start next Tuesday after
noon, according to an announcement
made today. All tryoutees for class
teams are asked to report at Roble gym
nasium on Tuesdays and Thursdays
from 4 to 5 o'clock. Practice will be
held on these afternoons for the rest of
the quarter. Miss Nellie Schulz, '19,
captain of this year's Varsity team, has
appointed the captains of the class teams
as follows: Irene Bonnett, '19; Doris
Hyer, '20; Marie Hutton, '21. and Hen
rietta Hoever, '22.
