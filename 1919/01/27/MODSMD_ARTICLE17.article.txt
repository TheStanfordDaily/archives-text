# WAR BOARD DISCUSSES FUTURE WITH CLINIC
## 
### 
Representatives from the Women's
War Board will meet with the Stanford
Auxiliary of Lane Clinic tonight at 8:15
in the Vestry to discuss plans for unit
ing the work of the two organizations
towards enlarging the scope of the work
in the future. The Women's War Board
has already held a preliminary meeting
to formulate their ideas and to deter
mine just what they will be willing to
suggest to the clinic, and the final d
cision, which is expected to be readied
tonight, promises to be an important one
for the future of women's charitable ac
tivities at Stanford.
