# SOPHOMORES TO HOLD FORMAL DANCE FEB. 28
## 
### 
Plans for the Sophomore Cotillion arc
under way, and the committee has
secured February 28 as the date for the
big underclass social. It has not yet
been determined where the dance will be
held. The personnel of the committee is
as follows: chairman, P. W. Kinney;
Harriet Hunt, Dorothy Johnston,
Dorothy Hare, R. I. Hill, 11. C. Craig.
