# BASIC MILITARY NOW GIVEN CREDIT
## 
### 
Three hours credit will be given for
the basic course in military, according
to an announcement today. The course
will consist of three hours a week of
practical work, which will count om
unit, and two hours of theoretical work
which will count two units.
A morning class in military will be
held on Mondays, Wednesdays and Fri
days from 11:15 to 12:15.
