# EIGHTY TRY OUT FOR SOPHOMORE PLAY CAST
## 
### 
Tryouts for the sophomore play
were held in the Assembly Hall yes
terday afternoon and evening, and
judging from the number who were
present there will be no lack of suit
able material for the east. About
eighty tried out, thirty of which were
men.
The tryouts were conducted by
Frank L. Mathieu. who produced last
year's sophomore play, "It Pays to
Advertise.' The cast is to be chosen
from the successful tryoutees, a list
of whom will be announced later.
