# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB WILL HEAR CARRUTH
## 
### 
"The Influence of the Great War
Upon American Literature" will be the
subject of a talk to be given by Profes
sor W. H. Carruth of the English De
partment before the Cosmopolitan Club
Monday night. This is one of the series
of lectures which are being presented
before the club upon current topics.
Included in the talk will be a discus
sion of some of the poetry produced
during the period of the war, and the
nature of this poetry. Professor Carruth
plans also to discuss the effect of the
experiences of war upon the writers of
this period, and to show the greater
earnestness and greater reality which
the war has brought into the literature
produced during the Great War.
