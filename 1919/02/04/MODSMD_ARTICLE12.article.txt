# CHEMISTRY MAJORS WILL HOLD SMOKER TOMORROW NIGHT
## 
### 
Smokes and eats will feature the
gathering of Chemistry Smokers in the
Union tonight at 7:30. The affair,
planned for some time, will he in the
nature of a get-together for upper-class
men and freshmen in the department.
Professor Franklin of the Chemistry
Department will deliver his lecture and
demonstration of liquid air, and J. Gas
ton Levy, recently returned from the
Chemical Welfare Service, will give a
talk on the value of chemical knowledge
at the front, as features for the smoker.
In addition to these two speakers, Pro
fessors Swain and Mitchell will deliver
short addresses to the chemistry majors,
all of whom are urged to attend.
