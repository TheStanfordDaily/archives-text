# MEMORIAL CHURCH
## 
### 
At the organ recital on Wednesday af
ternoon, Mr. Warren D. Allen, the or
ganist, will be assisted by Miss Eliza
beth Pierce, who plays the violin. Miss
Pierce, daughter of Professor Pierce of
the botany department, has already won
recognition as a violinist.
The program is as follows:
Fugue in E flat ("St. Ann's") Bach
Allegretto and Andante Cesar Franck
Reverie Vieuxtemps
Miss Pierce.
Grand Chorus in D Guilmant
