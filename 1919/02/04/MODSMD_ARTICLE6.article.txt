# $1200 COLLECTED FOR ARMENIAN RELIEF
## 
### 
The local committee no Armenian and
Syrian relief wishes to report that as a
result of the recent drive for this cause
on the campus the sum of $1329.93 has
been turned over to the treasurer of the
fund through Mrs. W. B. Allen of Palo
Alto.
The committee wishes to thank the
University public, one and all, for its
generous response to this call, particu
larly the members of the Women's War
Hoard and the faculty women who so
generously contributed of their time and
energy and thus made this donation
possible.
Isabel McCracken,
Chairman Locan Committee.
