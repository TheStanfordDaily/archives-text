# JOLLY-UP FRIDAY PROMISES "JAZZ"
## 
### 
The committee in charge of the
upperclass jolly-up, which will be held
Friday evening at Encina Hall, de
clares that it will be the biggest affair
held on the campus for some time.
No efforts have been spared to make
I the dance an impressive success, and all
j who attend are promised something new
in the line of jolly-lips, which will make
Friday flight's affair unique in the his
j tory of like functions. Svncopation will

be furnished by Monk Antrim's famous
Jazz orchestra, which in itself assures a
good time, while tlie lottery tickets en
titling the lucky holders to a free "jolly
up special"' at Wilson's should prove an
inductement to the hungry hordes who
are fond of games of chance.
Everyone on the campus is cordially
invited—wearers of the freshman dink in
cluded. Admission will be fifty-five cents,
war tax included.
