# CIVIL WAR TO BE THEME OF ADAMS' LECTURE TONIGHT
## 
### 
"London Punch and the American
Civil War" is the subject of a lecture
to be given by Professor E. D. Adams
of the history department in the Little
Theater tonight at 7:30.
The lecture will be illustrated by
slides showing the opinion of the Brit
ish governing class concerning the
American Civil War, and especially of
President Lincoln. The lecture was
originally intended for Professor
Adams' class in American history, but
all interested are invited to attend.
