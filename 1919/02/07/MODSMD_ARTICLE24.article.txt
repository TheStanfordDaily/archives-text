# RAM'S HEAD SEAT DRAWINGS THURSDAY
## 
### 
Seat drawings for the Ram's Head
show will be held next Thursday, Feb
ruary 13, at the bookstore and Robinson
and Crandall's in Palo Alto. There will
be only one performance of Biff! Bang!!
Bullsheveek!!!, the Ram's Head musical
extravaganza, according to the present
plans of the society. This will be held
in the Stanford Assembly Hall Friday
evennig, February 21.
If the consent of the Student Affairs
committee can be gained, the Ram's Head
management intends to arrange for
further productions of the show. Ram's
Head deeply regrets the omission of the
names of A. T. Harzfeld and M. S.
Hunkin from yesterday's announcement
of the cast.
