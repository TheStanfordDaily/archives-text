STANFORD UNIVERSITY MEDICAL SCHOOL
Thirty-seventh Course of Popular Medical Lectures, to be given in
Lane Hall, on the north side of Sacramento street, near Webster, San
Francisco.
General Subject: Lessons of the Great War.
February 21—What the War has taught us in regard to the Physical
Condition of our Young Men, by Dr. Philip Hale Pierson.
March 7—The Children's Year; a Reconstruction Program, by Dr.
Adelaide Brown.
March 21—The Control of Epidemics, by Dr. William C. Hassler, of the
San Francisco Board of Health.
