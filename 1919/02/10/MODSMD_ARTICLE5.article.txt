# Y. W.-Y. M.C.A. Entertain Convalescents at Hospital
## 
### 
A larger committee is to lie appointed
for the entertainment program which the
Y. VV. C. A. and Y. M. C. A. provide for
the soldiers at the llase Hospital every
Sunday afternoon. Machines are also
needed, and those who can spare the time
are asked to loan them to the association
every Sunday between 2 and 4 p. m.
