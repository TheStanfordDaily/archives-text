# SEAT DRAWINGS FOR RAM'S HEAD TOMORROW
## 
### 
Ticket drawings for "Biff! Bang!!
Bullsheveek!!!" will be held tomorrow
just before 1 o'clock at the Bookstore
on the campus and at Robinson's and
Crandall's in Palo Alto. The Ram's
Head musical extravaganza will be given
Friday night, February 21, in the Stan
ford Assembly Hall.
Arrangements are being made for a
second presentation of the show either
on the campus or out-of-town. The
piece has been written by M. A. Hagen,
'15, and is being coached by L. L. Pick
ering, '20.
