# BASEBALL CANDIDATES WILL MEET THURSDAY
## 
### 
Coach Evans has issued a call for
all baseball candidates to meet with
him at the gymnasium at 4 o'clock
Thursday afternoon. With the change
in (he weather baseball practice will
begin the first of the week in earnest.
Men in other physical training
groups will be able to change their
group to baseball at that time, if they
wish, and Coach Evans will outline a
schcdulr for preliminary practice.
