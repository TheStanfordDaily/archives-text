# C.C. COTTRELL, '18, CITED FOR BRAVERY IN AERIAL BATTLE
## 
### 
C. C. Cottrell, '18, returned yesterday
to San Jose from overseas duty, as he
was a member of the aviation corps.
Cottrell was cited for bravery, as he
flew over the German lines through
seven German machines, in order to get
important information. He was success
ful in his hazardous undertaking, and
returned safely to the French lines at
Verdun. Cottrell was a member of En
cina Club, and majored in economics.
