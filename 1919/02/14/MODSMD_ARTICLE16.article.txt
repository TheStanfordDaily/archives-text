# CAMPUS SORORITIES EXCHANGE DINNERS
## 
### 
For the first time in many years, the
sorority houses on the Row held ex
change dinners last night. Alpha Omi
cron Pi and Gamma Phi Beta, Pi Beta
Phi and Delta Delta Delta, Chi Omega
and Sigma Kappa, Kappa Kappa
Gamma and Alpha Phi, and Delta
Gamma and Kappa Alpha Theta ex
changed. Each house had ten guests,
which they entertained with songs and
stunts during and after dinner. Danc
ing was another feature of the evening.
It is planned to have these affairs once
or twice a month. The committee in
charge hopes in this way to better ac
quaint the girls of the different houses
with each other a dto promote univer
sity spirit.
