# Tickets for 1921 Cotillion on Sale
## 
### 
\ ickets for the sophomore cotillion
are now on sale at the Bookstore. As
there are only a limited number of
these, owing to the fact that the dance
will be held at Encina Hall, it is abso
ltely necessary that those who intend
to go purchase the tickets immediately.
No preference will be given to sopho
mores.
The big underclass formal will be
held on Friday evening, February 28,
and every effort is being made to make
the dance a pre-war affair. A three
course dinner will be served during the
evening. The price per couple will be
$2.50.
