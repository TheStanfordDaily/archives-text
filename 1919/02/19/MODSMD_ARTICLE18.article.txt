# Stanford Girls to Have Charge of Girls' Club
## 
### 
Miss Ruth Eastman, '19, and Miss
Vivian Hobson, '19, have taken charge
of the "Girls' Reserve," which is a
club composed of the younger girls of
Palo Alto and run under the auspices
of the Y. W. C. A. They are succeed
ing Miss Joyce Gardner, who carried
on this work, and was also head of
the girls' center in Redwood City. Miss
Gardner resigned in order to go to
San Francisco to take charge of th"
girls' work of the association there.
