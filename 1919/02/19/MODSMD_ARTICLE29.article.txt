# Plans for Mardi Gras Ball
## 
### 
A Mardi Gras ball will be giver.
March 4 at the Defenders' Club under
the auspices of the Ladies' Auxiliary
of St. Thomas Aquinas church. The
Defenders' Club will accommodate 450
couples, and it is planned to stage the
affair on a grander scale than those
held in previous years.
