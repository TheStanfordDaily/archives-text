# International Night Comes February 24
## 
### 
An International Night will he held
in the Little Theater next Monday
night, February 24, under the auspices
of the Cosmopolitan Club. A program
will be given by representatives of nine
different nations. Musical selctions will
comprise the principal part of the pro
gram. and instruments typical of the
different nationalities will be used.
There will be Irish and Scotch songs,
Chinese music, Russian, French and
Japanese musical selections. India will
also be represented, and the majority
of the participants will be in native
costumes.
