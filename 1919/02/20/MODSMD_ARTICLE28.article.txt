# El Camino Club Defeats Alpha Delts in Tennis
## 
### 
The Alpha Delts beat the El Camino
Club players in a tennis tourney yes
terday afternoon on the Encina courts,
the meet following as a result of the
workouts in the inter-fraternity tennis
tourneatnent last week. Due to the
rains, it has been impossible to finish
the remainder of the tournaments.
The results of yesterday's meet was
as follows; ft 4 3-6, 6-1.
