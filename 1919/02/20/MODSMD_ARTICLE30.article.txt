# Miss Inez Wood, '21, Entertains at Fremont
## 
### 
Miss Inez Wood, '21, was a mem
ber of the trio who entertained at the
"Y" buildings of Camp Fremont and
the base hospital Tuesday night. Miss
Wood gave several readings, and the
other members gave vocal and instru
mental selections. Miss Wood is in
the sophomore play and is a transfer
from the College of Pacific.
