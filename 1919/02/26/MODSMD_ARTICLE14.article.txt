# Wet Grounds Prevent Stanford-Santa Clara Baseball Contest
## 
### 
On account of the condition of the
grounds, the Stanford Santa Clara base
hall game scheduled for this afternoon
was called off by Manager Ames. The
next game scheduled is with Santa
Clara on the Mission diamond.
Coach Evans will take active charge
of the squad after the California-Stan
ford basketball game. At present. Cap
tain Hugh Galloway is conducting prac
tices.
