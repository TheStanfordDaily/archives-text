# QUADRANGLE CLUB HOLDS INITIATION
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Quadrangle Club
held last night, the following men were
initiated: E. R. Caughey, '18, former
track captain; L. C. Rogers, '17, former
crew captain; C. E. Righter, '19, basket
ball captain; C. S. Teitsworth, '19, track
captain; H. C. Galloway, '20, baseball
captain; W. I. Ames, student manager.
Prior to the iniation there were only
three members in college. These are
J. K. Lilly. '17, A. E. Worthy, '15, Y.
M. C. A. secretary, and Professor
Frank Angell.
