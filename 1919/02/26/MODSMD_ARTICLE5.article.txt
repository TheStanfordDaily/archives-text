# Girls Are Asked to Use Y.W. Rooms
## 
### 
Mrs. McGee. secretary of the Y. W.
C. A., has issued a cordial invitation
to the women of the campus to use
the rooms of the Y. W. C. A. on the
third floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
The rooms have just been newly and
attractively furnished and are at the
disposal of the girls at any time.
