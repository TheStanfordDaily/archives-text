# Resident Alumni Will Revive Benefit Teas
## 
### 
The custom of having teas at the
Women's Club House on the first
Wednesday of every month will be re
vived next Wednesday, when Mrs.
E. 15. Adams. Msr. R.L. Wilbur and
Mrs. John Stillman will be the hostess
for tea from 3 to 6,
Before the war these teas were regu
larly held by the resident alumni and
the wives of faculty memberss. They
are held for the benefit of the clubhouse,
the money being used to buy new fur
nishings.
Everyone is invited to come Tea is
fifteen cents, and a bridge table one
should notify Mrs. E. R. Adams by
Tuesday.
