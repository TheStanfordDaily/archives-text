# W.M. Prodtor Will Give Vocational Guidance at "Y"
## 
### 
YV. M. Proctor, instructor in educa
tion. will give free vocational guidance
to discharged members of the service
every Thursday and Tuesday evenings
from 6:30 to 9:30 p.m. in the Y. M.
C. A. building at 220 Golden Gate ave
nue, San Francisco.
Whether receiving this aid or not, dis
charged men of the service may receive,
without cost, the free help of the Asso
ciation Employment Bureau in finding
work. The Association also offers three
months' free membership to men dis
charged from the service, and special
inducements in educational classes.
