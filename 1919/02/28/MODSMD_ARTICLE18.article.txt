# Dinner Dance Given for Lieutenant Stanley Schnetzler
## 
### 
Lieutenant Stanley Schnetzler, '17,
was the recipient of a dinner dance
in Rainbow Lane at the Fairmont Ho
tel, given by his mother, last Wednes
day evening, in honor of his return
lrom the war zone on the converted
yacht Venetia. The guest of honor
was the commanding officer of the
Venetia, Captain C. P. Howell. Miss
Katherine Sheldon, '17, Miss Ruth
Squires, '18, and Willard Sheldon, '20,
were among the guests.
