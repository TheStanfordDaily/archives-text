# Parent of Dean of Women Dies Suddenly
## 
### 
Capt. J. O. Bradford, father of Miss
Harriet Bradford, Dean of Women,
died very suddenly last Saturday night
at his home in Sail Francisco.
Capt. Bradford was 77 years of age
and a retired Confederate navy officer.
He had been connected with the Wells-
Fargo company for a number of years.
Miss Bradford did not hear of the
bereavement until Monday, and, being
in Chicago at the time, was unable to
return for the funeral, which was held
Tuesday.
