# SAN FRANCISCO NINE SEEKS VARSITY MEN
## 
### 
Cliff Ireland of San Francisco has an
nounced that the baseball aggregation
known as Ireland's Independents will be
in action again this season. He is especial
ly anxious to get games with the nines
from Stanford. California and Santa
Clara. Ireland announces that he is par
ticularly desirous of getting in touch
with any Stanford varsity players who
have played on the Cardinal team since
1892, the first year lie came down to the
campus. Ireland may he reached at 24
California street, San Francisco.
