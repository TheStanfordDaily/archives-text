# RARE PHOTOGRAPHS TO BE DISPLAYED AT ART EXHIBITION
## 
### 
A number of rare and interesting
photographs will be on display at the
art exhibition which opens Sunday,
March 9, and which will run the entire
month.
Some very fine allegorical 'pictures
used by the Food Administration dur
ing the period of the war to arouse en
thusiasm in saving fund were collected
and contributed to the exhibit by Presi
dent Wilbur.
Mr. Pedro Letnos, together with many
other connoisseurs, has been untiring in
his efforts to make the opening a suc
cess.
