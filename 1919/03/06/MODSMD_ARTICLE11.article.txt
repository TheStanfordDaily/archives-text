# Otto Nagel, '20, Made Captain of Rugby Team
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Rugby team today
at tin- Beta house Otto Nagel, '20, was
elected captain for next year's team.
Nagel is an experienced rugby player,
having played second five on the varsity
this quarter. Nagel also played on the
yearling fifteen in his freshman year.
He is a member of Alpha Tau Omega.
