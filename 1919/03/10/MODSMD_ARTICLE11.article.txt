# Cosmopolitan Club to Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Cosmopolitan Club will meet for the
last time this quarter in the Women's
Club House at 7:30 tonight. The meet
ing is for members only, and after a
short business meeting a general so
cial hour and program will be given.
Miss Gladys Marriott, '21. will render
vocal selections, and other features
have been arranged.
