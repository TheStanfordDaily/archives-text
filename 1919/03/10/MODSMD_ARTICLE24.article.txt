# Lieut. L.A. Skelton, '17, And Wife Here on Visit
##

wife, arrived on the campus yesterday.
Skelton has just received his dis
charge, after serving eight months
overseas. Mrs. Skelton is a Delta
Gamma. The expect to return to Long
Beach after a week's stay.
