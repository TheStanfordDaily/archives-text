# NESTORIA WILL HOLD BANQUET TOMORROW
## 
### 
Nestoria Debating Society will hold
its last meeting of the quarter at a
banquet at the Stanford Inn at 6:15
p.m. Wednesday evening, March 12.
Professor Baasett and Lieutenant
Adams, who recently returned from
France and was with the air service,
will address the members. The usual
election of officers for the coming
quarter will take place and with the
rresence of several members back
from the service a snappy program
and good eats, an enjoyable evening
is assured.
