# BLANKS FOR SIXTY DOLLAR BONUS ARRIVE
## 
### 
Application blanks for the $60 bonus
pay for discharged S. A. T. C. men have
arrived at Captain Parker's office, R. O.
T. C. headquarters.
Captain Parker has announced his in
tention of helping former S. A. T. C.
men in filling out their blanks and take
tip any claims. They are to be sent by
registered mail to Washington. D. C.
