# Stanford Journalism Women Visit Campus
## 
### 
Mrs. Howard Lewis (Olive Yoder,
'1G) visited the campus in the first
part of the week. Mrs. Lewis was
prominent in women's journalism dur
ing her college years. Lewis is a Stan
ford man, of the class of '04, and is
now head of the radio system at Valle
jo.
Miss Anita Marcus, '17, a former
women's editor of the Daily Palo Alto,
was also here.
