Dorothy Jones, '20,
Expected Home
Miss Dorothy Jones, '20, is expected
home Sunday from the University of
Washington. She will pass the spring
vacation with her parents, Colonel and
Mrs. William K. Jones, at Menlo Park.
Miss Jones attended Stanford last
year, transferring from the University
of Washington, and was a popular
member of college and military so
ciety.
