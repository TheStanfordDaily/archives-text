# TRACK MEN WILL RUN MARATHON TOMORROW
## 
### 
All track candidates must report at
the gymnasium on Saturday, March 22,
for the last of the informal meets on
the oval, was the order issued by Cap
tain Teitsworth today. The usual in
formal meet for tomorrow will be called
off on account of examinations. At the
Saturday meet the team that is to run
against the University of Southern Cali
fornia will be picked. The U. S. C. meet
will be held the first Saturday in vaca
tion.
Training rules will be posted on the
bulletin board at the gymnasium from
tonight on. Teits worth requests that all
trackmen familiarize themselves with the
instructions and follow them to the
letter.
