# Alumnae to Give Tea Wednesday Afternoon
## 
### 
The members of the Stanford
Alumnae are planning to hold another
tea for the benefit of the Women's
Club House. The tea will take place
in the clubhouse next Wednesday
afternoon from 3 to 5. Tea will be
served during the afternoon and card
tables may be reserved by notifying
Mrs. A. C. Whitaker.
The hostesses who will preside at
the affair are Mrs. A. M. Cathcart,
Mrs. T. P. Russell, Mrs. Charles, Mrs.
M. R. Kirkwood and Mrs. Elliott Jones.
All campus women are cordially in
vited to attend.
