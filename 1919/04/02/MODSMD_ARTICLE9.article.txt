# QUESTIONNAIRES ON STUDENT CONTROL TO BE DISTRIBUTED
## 
### 
Last night at a regular meeting of
the Students' Council it was moved
and carried that questionnaires »n the
interest of student control be placed
in the hands of every student in the
Universit*-. That this will stimulate
interest in the idea of having student
control, is the hope of the Council.
Following are the minutes of the
meeting:
April 2, 1919.
President Lilly in the chair.
M. S. C.—That Wade Stamp be
elected secretary of the Student
Council.
M. S. C. —That payment of bills of
W. A. A, amounting to $155.10 be
authorized.
~M. S. C. —That questionnaires in
the interest of student control be
placed in the hands of every student
in the University.
