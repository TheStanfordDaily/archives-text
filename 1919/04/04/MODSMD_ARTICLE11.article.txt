# EVANS WILL BEGIN SPRING COURSE IN FOOTBALL MONDAY
## 
### 
Coach "Bob" Evans' class in football
coaching and practice will meet Mon
day afternoon for the first time. All
men who expect to go out for football
next fall should attend this class, ac
cording to the announcement made by
the coach today. The class will meet
at 2:15 p.m.
The same instruction will be given
in this class as will be given the team
next fall. Lectures will be supple
mented by blackboard work and prac
tical instruction. Registration will not
be necessary, but those who register
in the course can get two hours' credit.
Coach Evans announces that all men
who take this course will have a big
advantage next football season.
