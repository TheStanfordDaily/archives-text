# CARNIVAL CHAIRMEN WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Chairmen from the various houses,
Encina, and Roble, for the Athletic Car
nival to be held next Saturday, April 12,
will meet tonight in the Union at seven
thirty. Through error this meeting was
announced in yesterday's issue as be
ing last night.
Plans will be then discussed for the
various house booths. According to
Chairman Roy Ilill, '21, it will be neces
sary for everyone to get behind the Car
nival to make it a success, and a finan
cial help of value to athletics. Besides
the booths, many entertaining lake sports
have been arranged for Saturday even
ing.
