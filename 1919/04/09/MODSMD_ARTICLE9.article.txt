# Parker Visits Campus
## 
### 
George Parker, who participated in
the Olympic games at Stockholm in
1912 and toured Australia a few years
ago as a member of a team from the
Olympic Club in San Francisco, along
with "Reg" Oaughey and "Feg" Mur
ray from Stanford, was a visitor on
the campus yesterday as the guest of
"Dink" Teinpleton.
Parker worked out with the sprint
ers on the track and gave them sev
eral valuable pointers. He has done
9:3 in the 100-yard dash and 21:1 in
the 220.
