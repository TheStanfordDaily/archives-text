# Sophomore Jolly-Up Is Announced for April 25
## 
### 
Sophomores are to hold their jolly-up
on April 25 at the Women's Club House,
it was announced today at the 1921
meeting. A jollyup committee, consist
ing of R. A. Cody, chairman. Elizabeth
Mac Arthur, Bernice Taylor, R. I. Hill,
11. F. Gould and N T . J. Nathanson, was
appointed.
Sophomore caps may he obtained from
A. P. Holt at the Chi Psi house.
