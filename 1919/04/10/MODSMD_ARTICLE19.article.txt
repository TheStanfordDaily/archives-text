# COACH CASS ADDRESSES MEETING OF 1922 MEN
## 
### 
Coach Cass addressed the freshmen
at their men's meeting yesterday, and
explained the coming freshman-soph
niore baseball fight. The report of
the light committee was given and
accepted and final arrangements for
the scrap were made. Details of the
hundred dollar appropriation to the
1 diversity of Washington to bring
down their freshman crew to the re
gatta on May 3d for the purpose of
meeting the California babes and our
own Stanford freshman crew were
made public.
