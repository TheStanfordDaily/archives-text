# INTERCOLLEGIATE WRESTLING MAY 1
## 
### 
The Stanford freshmen wrestlers will
hold an inter-collegiate meet with Cali
fornia on May 9 on the Encina mat.
The meet will be featured by matches
in practically all the weight classes. Ac
cording to Coach Hones, the Bruin
babes will come to the farm "en masse"
to the number of eighteen.
Stanford will not Ik- outdone when it
comes to numbers. The local freshmen
are putting up some very consistent
matches and will furnish good material
for a picked lineup. No intimation as to
the possible lineup has been given out,
hut will he published about a week prior
to the frosh meet.
