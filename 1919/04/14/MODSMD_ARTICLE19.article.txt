# Band to Be Organized By Parker Tomorrow
## 
### 
Captain Parker announces that he will
meet all men interested in organizing a
new band at Stanford, in room 287 to
morrow at 4 : 15.
Providing enough men appear at that
time, an effort will be made to secure
a band master of recognized merit.
Sergeant Miller who led the band dur
ing the fall quarter, has been called
away.
