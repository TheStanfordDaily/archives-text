# PROFESSOR MURRAY TRANSLATES ODYSSEY
## 
### 
Professor A. T. Murray of the
Greek department is working on a
translation of Homer's Odyssey, the
first volume of which will come out
early this summer. The second vol
ume has already gone to the press.
The book is in the Loeb Library,
which is published by a series of
scholars, giving the text of the classi
cal authors with the .English transla
tion.
