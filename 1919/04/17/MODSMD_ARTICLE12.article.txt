# Women's Club Dance Postponed, Tomorrow Being Good Friday
## 
### 
Owin &to opposition of a dnce being
hold on Good Friday, the benefit dancc
that was to he held this Friday in the
Women's Club Honsc has been post
poned. When the date was settled upon
some two months ago, it was not then
realized that it fell upon Good Friday.
Friday.
The clubhouse is in debt $7000. This
debt must be paid. Therefore May 2
has now been settled 011 for the dance.
On that night there will also be a play
staged in the clubhouse, and the dance
will follow the play. However, this is
the only possible date that can be
secured.
