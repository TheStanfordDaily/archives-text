Juniors have existed from time im
memorial. And ever since juniors
have existed there has been a Junior
Prom. Further, each Junior Prom
has been part of a process of evolu
tion in splendor. Each year more
wonderful music, more beautiful dec
orations, more pep. and more good
looking women have featured the
Prom. But in this year's dance the
pinnacle of achievement has been
reached. Even the flowers this time
will have a sweeter smell, and the
little flivver taxies will be newly
shined. The floral decorations will
rival the gardens of Egypt and the big
gym floor will be aflame with the re
sults of the efforts of the junior class.
This is all true if you take 1920's
words for it. Tickets go on sale Mon
day morning at the bookstore.
