# "Feg" Murray Exhibit Shown at Art Gallery
## 
### 
A special feature of the exhibit to
be seen at the art gallery during the
next three weeks will be a collection
of pictures, drawings and cartoons by
F. S. ("Feg") Murray, '17. This col
lection includes practically everything
he has done during his four years at
college, in addition to that done over
seas. The drawings made in France
are especially interesting.
Illustrations for campus publica
tions, cartoons that appeared in
"Chappie," including the cover to the
last, number, and many other of his
best and most well-known drawings,
are on exhibition. They will eventu
ally be put 011 sale.
