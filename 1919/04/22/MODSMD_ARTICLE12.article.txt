# Improperly Inducted S.A.T.C. Men to Get Pay
## 
### 
Orders have been received iiere by
the R. O. T. C. headquarters from the
district military headquarters, to collect
information as to the amounts owed by
the government to former S. A. T. C.
men who were improperly inducted
through no fault of their own.
Data on these S. A. T. C. men will
be gathered at the local R. O. T. C.
headquarters. Students who are en
titled to such settlement, may obtain in
formation there. The government in
tends to settle to the full amount to
which the student is entitled.
