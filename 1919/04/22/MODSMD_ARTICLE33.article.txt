# Hassler and Van Clief Box for Weight Class
## 
### 
Fryouts for places on the Cardinal
boxing line-up will be carried on this
afternoon at the Encina ring, when Van
Clief and Hassler will meet in a de
ciding match for the position of light
heavy on the local team. Some of the
boxers have their places practically
cinched for the big meet with the Cali
fornia boxers, on Friday evening, at
Berkeley, while the remainder of the
crew will be picked by Coach Murphy
during the workouts this week.
