# SOPHOMORES GIVE JOLLY-UP FRIDAY
## 
### 
The biggest, and most congenial
jolly-up of the year is being planned
for next Friday night, according to the
chairman of the sophomore jolly-up
committee. The 1921 committee plans
to make this dance the big success of
the year.
This will be the first time that the
I now Eneina dance floor will be used
for a jolly-up and arrangements are
being made to accommodate a large
crowd. By special arrangement with
the Encina Club the sophomores are
to be allowed the use of all the deco
rations for the Encina informal which
is scheduled for the next night.
The usual number of prizes and
novel stunts are being planned and
the committee announces that there
will be several innovation along this
line.
