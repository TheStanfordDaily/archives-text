# Former Stanford Couple Announce Birth of Son
## 
### 
Announcement has just been made
of the birth of a son, Le Count, to
Mrs. LeCount Slocum, Monday night.
Mrs. Slocum was Sue Foster, ex-'2O,
and is a member of the Pi Beta Phi
sorority. Slocum is a first lieutenant
with the Field Artillery, and is now
stationed in Brest, France, lie is a
Chi Psi.
