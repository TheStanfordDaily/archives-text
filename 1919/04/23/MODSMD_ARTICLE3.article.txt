# ILLUSTRATED REVIEW ON CAMPUS TODAY
## 
### 
"Welcome Home to Stanford" is the
title of an article by Chancellor Mm
eritus David Starr Jordan, appearing
in the Home Coming issue of the
Stanford Illustrated Review, which
was on the campus today. "Stanford
Fraternities," by President. Wilbur,
and"The Stanford War Memorial,"
by Professor William F. Durand, are
contributions to this issue.
