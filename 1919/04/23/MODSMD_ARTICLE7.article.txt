# Snook Receives Croix de Guerre
## 
### 
Walter B. Snook, '20, who was
awarded a Croix de Guerre with a palm
hy the French government 1 , has just re
ceived a notification of his commission
as captain from the War Department.
Snook crossed the water with the
first Stanford Ambulance Unit, and then
enlisted in the French army. In August
1918 he transferred to the American
Army. He was then placed on detached
service with the French forces, and saw
some heavy lighting in the Argonne
Forest. It was while he was in this
scctor that he killed seven Germans,
single handed, for which he was awarded
the highest French military honor.
He recently received his discharge
papers, and has just returned to the
campus. He is a Sigma Nu.
