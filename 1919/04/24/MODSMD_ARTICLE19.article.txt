# Theta Sigma Phi Entertains Authoress
## 
### 
Nelbro Bartley, the author of
"Woman's Woman," the serial recent
ly completed in the Saturday Evening
Post, is the guest of Theta Sigma Phi,
which meets for supper at the Delta
Delta Delta house Sunday evening.
She will tell of her interesting ex
periences writing for the Buffalo Ex
press and New York Sun.
