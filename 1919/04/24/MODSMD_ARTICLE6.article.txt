# Browning Rifle and Machine Gun Received Here
## 
### 
One Browning automatic rifle and
one Browning machine gun have been
received at the It. O. T. C. headquar
ters. Captain Parker announced this
morning. This equipment is in ex
cellent condition, and will be used in
instructing members of the training
corps in the near future.
Captain Parker states that this is
the first of additional apparatus to be
provided for the improvement of the
local unit of the reserve corps. The
authorities are attempting to provide
examples of every device used by in
fantry in modern attack and defense,
such instruments may be obtained,
enough for thorough instruction in
each branch will be available to the
and though only a limited number of
members of the unit.
