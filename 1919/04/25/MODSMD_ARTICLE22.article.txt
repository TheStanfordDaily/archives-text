# Schubert Club to Hold Rehearsals This Week
## 
### 
Rehearsals of the program to be given
before the Stanford Women's Club play,
next Friday night, are to be held this
week. These rehearsals will begin at the
Women's Club House next Monday
evening at 8:15. The second will be
held Tuesday evening at 7:30, and the
third, Thursday evening at 7 :30.
All members are urged to attend these
meetings in order that a good showing
may be made the night of the play.
