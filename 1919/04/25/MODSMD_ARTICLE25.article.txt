# Camera Club to Buy An Enlarging Outfit
## 
### 
Special bids have been received by
the Camera Club for furnishing photo
supplies to its members. These bids
will be discussed at the next meeting
of the club, Monday night, April 28,
at the Women's Club House.
Members have been asked to bring
negatives for discussion which have
made unsatisfactory prints.
Arrangements are being made to
purchase an enlarging camera for the
club if enough members are inter
ested. Any student interested in pho
tography, who desires membership in
the club, is asked to attend the next
meeting.
