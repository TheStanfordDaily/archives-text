# Freshmen Must Pay For Damages on Zete House
## 
### 
In order to disburse the expenses of
the damage caused during the baseball
fight, the freshmen decided in ;i meet
ing this noon to hold a freshman hop
on May 23. The dance will be held in
Encina Hall and the admission will be
one dollar. Mr. Hewston told the class
that the cost of repairing the old Zete
house would be about one hundred and
thirty-eight dollars.
Coach Jones spoke on giving minor
numerals to the freshmen making the
minor teams and asked for the sup
port of the class for them. A discus
sion upon the desirability of returning
to the red sophomore hats next year
was laid aside until next meeting. t
