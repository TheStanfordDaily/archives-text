# Rifle Club Receives New Springfield Guns

### 
Ten excellent gallery rifles (have
been received by the loeal R. O. T.
C. unit, according to an announcement
this morning.
The commandant's office has pro
vided that range practice will begin
tomorrow afternoon, after which there
will be a competent official in charge
from 9 till 12 every morning, and

Captain Parker states that during
this time the range is open to all
members of the Itifle Club as well as
to the students in the military unit.
