# Trap Shooting Club Organized on Campus
## 
### 
Interest on the campus in the use of
firearms has culminated in the organi
zation of a trapshoot club.
The traps, which are located behind
the eating clubs to the east of Encina,
were in use fur the first time last Sun
day. The object of the club is to fur
ther the sport of trapshooting, and those
interested, whether they can handle a
gun or not, should see Arnold Keller
or W. T. Cooper. The fee of the club
is $2.50 a year. Shells and blue rock are
secured at cost.
