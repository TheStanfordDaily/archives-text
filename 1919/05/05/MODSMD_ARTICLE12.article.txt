# Junior Prom Programs Must Be Ordered Now
## 
### 
Programs for the Junior Prom have
been ordered in Los Angeles and are to
be something new and unique for a cam
pus formal. Already 140 have arrived,
while several more tickets have been
sold. In order to secure a program, all
those who have already bought tickets
and have not received them or intend
to go to the Prom, should turn in their
names at the bookstore at once.
The committee expects at least 150
tickets to be sold and further orders for
programs must be made at once.
