# FRESHMAN HOP TO BE AT ENCINA ON MAY 17
## 
### 
Saturday, May 17, is the date an
nounced for the Freshman Hop. It will
begin at Encina Hall, at 8:30. Accord
ing to the committee, it will be the best
informal dance of the year, ranking just
below the Sophomore Cotillion, the
Junior Prom and the Senior Ball, in the
scale of class dances. There will be
sixteen dances, and programs will be
given out at the door. Many novel
features have been planned for the even
ing. A seven-piece jazz orchestra will
furnish the music. Eats will feature the
intermission. Admission will be one
dollar per couple. Everyone on the
campus is invited to attend.
