# FRENCH CLUB MEETS WEDNESDAY AT ROBLE
## 
### 
French Club will meet at 8:30 o'clock
Wednesday, at Roble Hall, instead of
the Womens' Club House. Mr. Dubois
P. de Yries, who has just returned from
I'ranee will talk about his experiences
as interpretor there.
The usual business will be transacted,
and plans will be laid for the French
Club dinner, which will be held this
quarter.
