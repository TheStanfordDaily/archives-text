# NESTORIA TO ARGUE FIUME PROPOSITION
## 
### 
Nestoria assigns to its forensic artists.
J. V. Gomez, '22, and J. J. Delpech, '22,
the task of proving that the peace con
ference should award the Fiume district
to Italy, while W. H. Metzler, '19, and
R. M. May, '22, will defend Wilson's
stand, at Nestoria's meeting tomorrow
night, in Room 12.
The society will also complete plans
for the annual debate with Euphronia
at the business discussion tomorrow.
The inter-club contest is scheduled for
Wednesday, May 14. Strong trios have
been selected by both societies, and a
spirited battle is expected on this date.
