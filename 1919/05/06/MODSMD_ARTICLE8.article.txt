# FOOTBALL PRACTICE STARTS TOMORROW; EIGHTY SIGN UP
## 
### 
About eighty prospective members of
the football team have signed up with
Coach Evans for practice, which will
begin tomorrow. Sixty uniforms have
been issued but these are still many who
have not received any equipment. Re
cause of the great enthusiasm with which
the sport has been taken up a hurry call
for uniforms has been made and more
are expected to arrive soon. All those
going out for the team will be equipped
with football suits.
