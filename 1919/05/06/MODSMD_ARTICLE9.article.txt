# ENGLISH CLUB GUEST WILL GIVE READING
## 
### 
Mrs. Waldo Richards, member of the
National Poetry Society, will give a
reading of modern poetry, Wednesday
evening, at 7:30, in the Little Theater.
Mrs. Richards will be here as the guest
of the English department and of the
English Club, and will present a pro
gram of special interest to all university
people. She has traveled extensively,
and is an expert in her line of work.
The general public is cordially invited
to attend.
