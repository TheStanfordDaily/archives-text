# Y.W.C.A. CHANGES PLACE OF MEETING
## 
### 
The meeting place and the time of
meeting of the Y. W. C. A. has been
changed this week because of the P.
E. O. convention at the Women's
Club House. The meeting will take
place Thursday afternoon at 5 o'clock
in room 200 A in the English building.
Miss Marjorie Bailey of the Eng
lish department will read for the
meeting, which will be a very infor
mal one. All women are invited to
attend.
