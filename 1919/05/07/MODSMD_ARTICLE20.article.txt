# Mrs. Richards to Give Modern Poetry Reading
## 
### 
A varied selection of modern poetry
including many dialect poems, will be
offered in the reading to lie given by
Mrs. Waldo Richards, in the Little
Theater tonight, and a program of un
usual interest is promised. Selections
from poems by Etnelie Josephine Burr,
Charles Hanson Powne, Alfred Hayes,
John Masefield, and Thomas Augustine
Daly, will he given, featuring Italian
Irish and child-negro dialects, as well
as English modern poems. In addition
to her work as interpretative reader,
Mrs. Waldo lias completed and edited
two anthologies of modern poetry,
which have attracted much favorable
comment.
