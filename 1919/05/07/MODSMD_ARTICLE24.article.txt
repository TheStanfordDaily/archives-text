# "Y" Men Will Attend the Saratoga Convention
## 
### 
A deputation of three California and
four Stanford "Y" workers will go to
Saratoga this week-end in connection
with social service work of the Y. M.
C. A. of Stanford.
The deputation plans to take the high
school and grammar school hoys of
Saratoga on hikes and outings, and- to
encourage athletic as well as religious
activities in the schools of Saratoga.
Entertainments and talks will be given
witli the view of interesting the hoys in
the work of the Y. M. C. A.
