# CAP AND GOWN GIVES BENEFIT FOR CLINIC
## 
### 
Lane Clinic kitchen shower will
open at 7:15 sharp tonight at the Pi
Phi house. Pans, knives, potato
mashers and other utensils will be
brought by representatives of the va
rious houses and placed on display,
probably in the dining room.
Miss Vaal Stark, recently placed in
charge of the clinic hospital opened
in the old Acacia house, has promised
that the eight children now under her
charge will be present at the party.
Jazz music and dancing at the high
price of 1 cent a dance will be in
dulged in until 8:15. Cap and Gown
society, in charge of the affair, is anx
ious that a large crowd attend.
