# Kinney Elected Tennis Captain
## 
### 
Paul Kinney, '21, was last night se
lected by the varsity raquet-wielders to
lead the Cardinal in the 1920 tennis
season, at the meeting of the tennis
teams, last night, at the Delta Kappa
Epsilon house.
Kinney, though he lost two straight
sets to Levy of California, plays one
of the best games exhibited on the Car
dinal courts. He is a member of the
Delta Upsilon.
