# SENIOR CLASS DECIDES UPON DATE FOR PICNIC
## 
### 
At a meeting of the senior class to
day, it was decided that the annual
senior class picnic would be held Thurs
day, May 22. It lias not been decided
as yet where the picnic will be held,
but a committee was named that will
look up a suitable place for the affair.
It was announced that the girls of the
class would provide all the sandwiches,
and the hoys will be on hand with the
ice cream, cigars, and cigarettes. Those
on the committee are Miss Ruth Chan
dler, chairman, W. Winham, and Miss
Mary Sargent.
