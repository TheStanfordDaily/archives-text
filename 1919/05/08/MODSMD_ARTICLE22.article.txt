# On'n' Off the Campus
## 
### 
Fight on Water Rights
Will Come Before Judge
Warrants were served on five men
Sunday of the Stanford Acres, a sub
division near Mayfield, on charges of
disturbing the peace, breaking into a
pumping plant, and a technically
worded charge for opening the stop
cock of the water main leading to the
respective holdings. The trial has
been set for May 10.
Interclass Soccer
Series is Schedulued
All classes are to be represented in
an interclass soccer tournament next
Saturday, according to an announce
ment by S. G. Pillsbury, '20, who is
managing the games. Much interest
has been shown in the event and all
teams are expected to be in the best
condition.
