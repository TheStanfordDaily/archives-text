# Championship Meet to Be Saturday
## Intercollegiate Teams Are to Compete for Honors at Neptune Beach 
### 
The close of the swimming season will
be marked by the Pacific Coast Inter
collegiate Championship Swim, that is
to he held Saturday, May 10, at Nep
tune Beach. Stanford, California, Santa
Clara and St. Mary's will compete.
Those who Coach Brandsten expects
to use in the meet are Caughey, Austin.
Mills. Goddard, Newman, Ankele and
Coonse.
There will probably be eight events
featured, among them 50 yards, 100
yards. 220 yards, 50 yards back-stroke,
diving plunge for distance, and relay.
Coonse will star in dashes, Goddard
should take diving and Newman the
plunge.
