# SPORT INSTRUCTION GIVEN BY Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
Stanford and California Y. M. C. A.
sent a deputation to Saratoga last Sat
urday, to promote athletics and religi
ous education among the high school
and grammar school boys of that town.
The"V" men conducted a series of
athletic events, including hikes and
races, and religious talks. California
sent three representatives and Stanford
seven. The Stanford men were: F. S.
Shipp, *20; S. K. Davis, *20; E. Pur
mort, '20; W. Barker, '20; G. Linn, '18;
G. Luhrs, '18, and A. E. Worthy, '15.
