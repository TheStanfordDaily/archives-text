# Camp Fremont Remount Depot Finally Closed
## 
### 
The final closing out of the Remount
Depot was affected the first of this week,
when the buildings were sold as they
stand at private sale, directly to the
property owners, under the auspices of
the Utilities Department. Humors have
been circulated as to the establishing
of a canning factory, hut as yet no
authoritative statements have been
made.
All stock were disposed of when a
shipment of over 500 head were sent to
the Philippines last month, and the
supplies will he shipped to the various
government posts, including surplus
grain, fuel and hay.
