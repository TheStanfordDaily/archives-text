# Editor Elected For Chapparal
## 
### 
At a meeting last night of the staff
of "Chappie," an election was held and
the following were chosen to take
charge of the magazine next year:
Sam Glikbarg, '19, was made editor;
Milt Hagen, '15, manaager, and "Bones"
McDermott, '17, art editor. It was also
announced that according to custom,
the famous hammer is to be on dis
play in the Bookstore for a week.
There will he one more edition of
"Chappie" this quarter and it will be
entitled "Them Were the Happy Days,"
referring to the alumni "Old Home
Week" in Tune.
