# Delta Sigma Rho Initiates Three
## 
### 
Delta Sigma Rho initiated three new
forensic artists into the rites of the
order this afternoon, at Encina, at 3
o'clock. The men. honored were H. G.
Blote, '18, C. O. Fenlason, '19, and W.
M. Crobaugh, '18.
Blote was a member of the 1919
Joffre team, and before the war spoke
in several intercollegiate debates. Fen
lason is a well-known debater on the
present U. S. C. squad. Crobaugh, re
cently returned to Stanford, was promi
nent in debating circles before the war.
