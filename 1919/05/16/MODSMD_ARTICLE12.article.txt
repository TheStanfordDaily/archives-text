# Alumnae Tea Will Be Held Next Wednesday
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Alumni will
give a tea at the Women's Club House
next Wednesday, at 3 o'clock. The host
ess at the affair will he Palo Alto Chap
ter of the Daughters of the American
Revolution.
Tea will be served from 3 to 5 and
bridge tables may be reserved through
Mrs. A. J. Whitaker. Everyone is in
vited to attend.
