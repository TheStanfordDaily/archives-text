# Dr. Jordan to Talk at Little Theater
## "The Future of Ireland" to Be Discussed on Tuesday 
### 
Dr. David Starr Jordan will speak
before a Stanford audience next Tues
day evening, in the Little Theater. This
will be Dr. Jordan's first public address
before a university audience for some
time. t
"The Future of Ireland" is to be the
topic for the lecture. Dr. Jordan has
made a special study of the conditions
in that country and is well versed on the
relations between Ireland and Great
Britain. Dr. Jordan has spent many
years studying international relations,
and is a member of the "League to En
force Peace."
The lecture will take place at 7:30,
Tuesday evening.
