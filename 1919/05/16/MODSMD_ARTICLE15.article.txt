# California Will Send To National Tournament
## 
### 
California will be represented at the
national intercollegiate tennis tourna
ment in the early part of June. A.
B. Gravem and E. L. Levy will be
sent and appropriations to defray
their expenses were made by the ex
ecutive committee last Wednesday.
Most of the eastern colleges will en
ter men in the tournament. Harvard,
Yale, Princeton, Pennsylvania, Cor
nell. Chicago, Illinois, Amherst, Dart
mouth, will all be represented.
