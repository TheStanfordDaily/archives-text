# U.C. to Give Certificates To Men in War Service
## 
### 
War honor certificates are to be
given University of California men
who have been in the military and
naval service of the United States and
now find It. impossible to return to
the university. The certificate will
take the place of diplomas and will
be printed similarly. In the case of
the men who lost their lives in the
service the certificate will be given
thir parents or relatives.
