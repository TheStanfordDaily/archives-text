# STANFORD WOMAN MARRIED SATURDAY
## 
### 
Miss Grace Maddocks, ex-'2O, of Sac
ramento was married to Gustavus Miller,
also of Sacramento, Saturday night, at
the Hotel Oakland, in the presence of
a few friends and relatives. Mr. and
Mrs. Miller arc spending their honey
moon at Inverness, and they will be at
home to their friends in Sacramento at
the Home Apartments, after July Ist.
Mrs. Miller is a member of Delta
Gamma sorority. Mr. Miller attended
Exeter Preparatory School and lias re
cently returned from France where he
has been for the past fourteen months.
