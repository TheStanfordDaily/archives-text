# STUDENT BODY DANCE AT ENCINA SATURDAY
## 
### 
A student body dance will be given
Saturday night, May 24th, at Enema
Hall.
Corey's freshman orchestra has
been secured to furnish music because
of the reputation it has established
playing Tuesdays and Thursdays at
Encina.
Fifty cents per couple will be
charged. The affair is entirely in
formal. and there will be no eats. It
is wholly a student body benefit
event.
