# Breakers Win Game From Beta-S.A.E. Team
## 
### 
By defeating the Beta Theta Pi-
Sigma Alpha Epsilon combination last
night by the score of 16 to 1, the
Breakers won the right to meet the
Alpha Delta Phi team in the next
round of games in the inter-house
baseball series. "Dink" Templeton
occupied the box for the Breakers and
managed to retire the opposing hitters
in one, two, three order. The win
ner of the Breakers-Alpha Delt game
plays the winner of the Delta Upsilon-
Theta Delta Chi contest.
The Sigma Cliis are scheduled to
play the Phi Psis tonight and the
Delta-Upsilon team will play the
Theta Delta Chi nine.
