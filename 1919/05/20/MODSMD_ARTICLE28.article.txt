# Dean McDowell Speaks To Cosmopolitan Club
## 
### 
Dean J. E. McDowell addressed the
Cosmopolitan Club last night in the Wo
men's Club House on "The Part Stan
ford Men Played in the War." At the

business meeting, held directly after the
speech, it was decided to hold the Cos
mopolitan Club picnic next Monday
night, at 6:00 o'clock. The picnic
will be held in the hills. Every one who
wishes may attend by paying twenty
five cents to either H. R. Hosepian or
to Miss Vartania.
