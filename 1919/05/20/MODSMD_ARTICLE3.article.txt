# San Francisco Chemist Will Address Students
## 
### 
"The University Man in Industrial
Chemistry" will be the subject of ii
talk to be Riven by Dr. Thomas B.
Uines in the lecture room of the
Chemistry building, Thursday even
ing at 7:30 o'clock.
Dr. Ilines is the chief chemist of
the Great Electro-Chemical Company
of San Francisco. He is being brought
to speak at Stanford by the Phi
Lambda Upsilon. but the lecture is
open to the general public.
