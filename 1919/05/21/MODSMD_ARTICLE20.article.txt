# Overseas Man Elected Captain U. of C. Team
## 
### 
Ray Rohwer was unanimously elected
captain of the University of California
baseball team for next year. He is now
with the American Army of Occupation
in Germany as a tirst lieutenant in the
91st Division. Rohwer has been on
the California Varsity two years and
was considered one of the best hitters
on the team. He will return to college
next year.
