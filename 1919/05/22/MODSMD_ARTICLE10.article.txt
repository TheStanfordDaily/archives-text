# PAMPHLET COMPILED BY PROF. E. KREHBIEL
## 
### 
Under the direction of Professor E. R.
Krehbiel of the History Department, a
pamphlet has been published upon the
League of Nations. The covenant of
the Legeaue of Nations is given, with
the changes which have been made in
the articles in italics. A short explana
tion is made wherever there is any
difficulty in understanding the text.
Professor Krehbiel has been one of
the most ardent supporters of the
League of Nations proposition, and lie
has devoted much of his time to a
study of the questions concerning the
League, making many speeches in Cali
fornia towns in its support.
