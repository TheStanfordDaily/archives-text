# PLANS COMPLETE FOR JOHNSTON RECEPTION
## 
### 
Plans for the reception which will be
given Sunday afternoon by the V. M.
C. A. and Y. W. C. A. of Stanford in
honor of Dr. Johnston. Ross, are now
complete. The committee in charge,
headed by Miss Frances Willis, '20, and
S. K. Davis, '20, has announced that
the welcome will last from 4:30 to 6:00
Sunday, in the Union. All faculty mem
bers and students as well as members
of the Association arc invited to be
present. Light refreshments will be
served.
Dr. Ross, who will deliver the ser
mons in the Memorial Church for the
next three Sundays and speak Tues
day, evening in the Little Theater, is a
member of the faculty of Union Theo
logical Seminary of New York.
