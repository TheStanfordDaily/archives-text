# Dr. Ross of New York Will Lecture Tuesday
## 
### 
The Reverend G. A. Johnston Ross
has been secured to deliver the regular
Tuesday evening lecture. His subject
has not yet been announced. The Rev
erend Mr. Ross comes from New York
City, being connected with the Union
Theological Seminary in that city.
During his stay on the campus he will
also preach the sermon at the Memorial
Church on Sunday.
