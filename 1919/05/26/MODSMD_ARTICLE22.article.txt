# SENIOR CLASS DUES MUST BE PAID NOW
## 
### 
Senior dues must be paid by the
first of June, is the announcement
made by the treasurer of the Senior
Class.
It is imperative that all those who
have registered as members of the
class of 1919, pay their $2.50 fee im
mediately, as all class money is need
ed at once. A list of those who have
not paid their dues will be published
on June Ist. The money may be paid
to L. M. Lissner, at Encina Hall, or
F. W. Williamson on the Row.
