# Nestoria Society Will Hold Banquet Tonight
## 
### 
Nestoria Debating Society will hold
its annual banquet at Madrona Mall this'
evening. Professor E. D. Adams of
the History Department has been asked
to speak to the club. President Wil
bur also may address the members if
he returns to Stanford in time to speak.
Xestoria's custom is to elect its of
ficers for the next college year at the
Madrona. banquet. Plans have been
made to make the affair tonight of
special interest, and an elaborate dinner
has been provided for.
Special measures will he taken tonight
to insure Xestoria of a successful year
of debating for the 1920-1921 season.
