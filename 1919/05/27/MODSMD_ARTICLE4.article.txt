# FIVE MEMBERS CHOSEN FOR THE PRESS CLUB
## 
### 
Five members have been selected
as eligible to the Stanford Press Club,
and will be initiated into that organi
zation this week. They are: A. S.
Glikbarg, 'Ift, editor-elect of the Chap
arral: G. S. Kocher, '19, associate art
editor of the Chaparral; E. E. Crowe,
'21, and J. E. Thrash. '21, news editors
on the Daily Palo Alto; M. F. Sarais,
'20, former undergraduate editor of
the Illustrated Reveiw and correspond
ent for city papers.
