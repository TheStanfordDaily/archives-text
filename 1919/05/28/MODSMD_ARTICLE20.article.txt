# FRESHMEN MAY ADOPT RED HATS AT MEETING
## 
### 
Action in regard to adopting a new
"red felt hat with a white silk band"
for the sophomore class in the future,
will be discussed at an important
freshmen class meeting on Thursday
noon, at 12:45. Several prominent
freshmen have declared that they are

in favor of the innovation and will
back up the issue.
A few days ago the Student Council
passed a resolution that that body
would advise a red hat for all sopho
more classes in the future. It is of
ficially understood that members of
the 1922 class will back up this action
taken by the Student Council.
At Thursday's meeting of the fresh
man class, important measures, re
garding the work of the class next
fall, will be discussed.
