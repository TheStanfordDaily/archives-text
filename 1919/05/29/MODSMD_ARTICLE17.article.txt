# Students in Rescue Course To Take Test Today
## 
### 
Students taking the Mine Rescue
Course given by the U. S. Depart
ment of Mines this week, are to un
dergo a test today. They will he re
quired to be in a room filled with
formaldehyde gas for two hours,
wearing their oxygen respirators.
While in the room the men will do
various kinds of manual labor. A
preliminary test was made yesterday
when the men wore the respirators
for forty minutes.
