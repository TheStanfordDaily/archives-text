# QUAD ON SALE AT UNION ON MONDAY
## 
### 
1920 yearbooks will be delivered to
those who have purchased coupons
at the Union Accounting Office, 011
Monday. This office is adjacent to
the Graduate Manager's office in the
Union Building.
Managers Brill and Murphy of the
Quad announce that students who
have lost their coupons notify them
in order to secure copies of the book.
No deliveries will be made to such
persons until all available couppons
are first redeemed at the office Mon
day.
