# EUPHRONIA DEBATING CLUB PLANS BANQUET
## 
### 
Euphronia will hold its annual ban
quet at Mrs. Hurd's Cafeteria in Palo
Alto on Thursday evening of this week,
according to announcement made by
President Hodges this morning. As this
is the only banquet of the society this
year, plans hiive been made to make it
a very elaborate affair.
Professor A. M. Cathcart of the Law
School, and Professor Miller of Politi
cal Science department are old members
of the club and have consented to speak
at the banquet Thursday. Dr. Wilbur
is also an old member of the society,
and, if possible, he will be present.
Preparations are completed for en
tertaining thirty-five men.
