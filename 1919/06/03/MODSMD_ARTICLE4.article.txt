# Miss Winifred Senneff Announces Engagement
## 
### 
Miss Winifred Senneff, '19, an
nounced her engagement to Lester T.
Gray, last Sunday. Miss Senneff
graduates from the French depart
ment this June, and is a member of
Delta Delta Delta.
Mr. Gray is a graduate of the Lib
eral Arts and Law Schools of the
University of Southern California and
is at present studying advanced law
in the courts of London. ,
