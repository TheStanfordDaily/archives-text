# Honorary Degree Given H.D. Gray by Colgate
## 
### 
Notice has jusf been received by
Henry David Gray of the English De
partment that the honorary degree of
Doctor of Letters has been awarded
him by Colgate University of New
York.
Dr. Gray graduated from Colgate
University in 1897 with a degree of
Bachelor of Philosophy, and later re
ceived the degrees of Master of Art and
Doctor of Philosophy from Columbia
University.
