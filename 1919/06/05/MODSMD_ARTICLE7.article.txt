# DANCE AT ENCINA AFTER SENIOR FARCE
## 
### 
A. C. Robbing, Chairman of the
Senior Ball Committee, announces
that an informal dance will be held
at Encina Hall, on Thursday even
ing, June 12, right after the Senior
Farce.
This dance will be a fore-runner to
the Senior Ball, which will be held
at the Hotel Vendome in San Jose, on
June lfi. The dance will last until
1 o'clock. Good music will be se
cured and everybody is guaranteed a
good time.
The price of the dance has not
been determined yet.
