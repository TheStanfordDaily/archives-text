# Quadrangle Club Elects Six Men To Membership
## 
### 
Six men were elected to the Quad
rangle Club at last night's meeting
of the Society. They are: W. B.
Wells, '20, Frank Watts, '19, R. F. Pe
louze, '19, C. S. Davis, '20, L. W.
Dinkelspiel, '20, and F. L. King, '19.
Wells is captain-elect of the track
team, and a member of Alpha Delta
Phi.
Watts was 1919 crew captain, and
is prominent in football. He is a
member of Skull and Snakes, and
Theta Xi.
Pelouze was a member of the foot
ball, baseball and track squads. He
is a member of Skull and Snakes,
Phi Delta Phi and Sigma Nu.
Davis is captain-elect of the crew,
and played on last year's varsity
baseball team. He is a member of
Skull and Snakes, Phi Delta Phi, and
Phi Kappa Psi.
Dinkelspiel is editor of the Daily
Palo Alto and prominent in track.
He was also editor of the 1920< Quad.
He is a member of Hammer and
Coffin, Press Club, Sigma Delta Chi,
Phi Delta Phi, and Encina Club.
King has recently returned from
France. He has rowed on several
varsity crews and is a member of
Delta Chi.
