# Students to Get Reports By Leaving Envelopes
## 
###

will be mailed June 24 to all stu
dents who leave stamped, addressed
envelopes at the Registrar's office.
Cards will not be given out over the
desk, except to candidates for de
grees who may receive them. Friday,
June 13th, after 1ft o'clock.
