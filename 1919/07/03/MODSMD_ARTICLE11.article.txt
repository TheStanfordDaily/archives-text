# Davies and Hawkes Reach Tennis Finals
## 
### 
In a hard fought struggle, J. M.
Davies, '22, and William Hawkes, '22,
were eliminated last Sunday from the
Pacific Coast Junior Championship ten
nis matches at Berkeley.
The doubles went well for Hawkes
and Davies nntil they met Allen and
Godshall, to whom they lost in sets
of 4-6, 6-4, 7-5, 5-7 ,6-4. Both teams
played well, and the Stanford men lost
by a close margin.
In the singles both Hawkes and
Davies were eliminated in the first
matches. Davies lost to Allen, 6-4, 2-6,
7-5. Hawkes fell to Yohatoga in sets
of 6-4, 7-5.
Hawkes and Davies were members of
the freshman tennis team and Davies
was the captain of the first-year men.
Both men expect to be present in the
south for the coming matches this
month.
