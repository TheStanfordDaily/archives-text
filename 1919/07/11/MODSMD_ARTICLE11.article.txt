# Summer Colony to Dance Next Friday
## 
### 
The second summer dance will lie
given at the Delta Tau Delta house on
next Friday night, July 18th. Louis Mc-
Dermott. Wheeler Thayer, and Neil Pe
tree are managing this series of dances,
and they promise several surprises at the
next social function. The first dance
was a success, forty-five campus couples
enjoying the excellent music and refresh
ments. Louis McDermott will again
have his well-known jazz orchestra on
hand for the next dance. Every one is
invited, as the dances are given primari
ly for the summer colony to become bet
ter acquainted.
