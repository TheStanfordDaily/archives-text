# Encina Dances Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Kncina Club has sent out fifty in
vitations for a dance in the club rooms
tomorrow night.. The club rooms have
been artistically decorated under the
direction of W. J. Gores. *17. The
dance is expected to be one of tho
most successful social functions of the
summer season.
