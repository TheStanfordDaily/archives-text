# Second Summer Dance Tonight
## 
### 
The second summer dance tonight at
the Delta Tau Delta house will be the
only social event of the campus week
end and will undoubtedly be a delight
ful get-together. Louis McDermott,
Wheeler Thayer, and Neil Petree are
in charge of the arrangements and have
announced that the same orchestra
which provided .the jazz at the first
dance will again furnish the music, aug
mented by Monk Antrim. Refresh
begin at 8:30, the admission charge be
ing 75 cents.
