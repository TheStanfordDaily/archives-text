# REV. DUTTON TO LECTURE
## 
### 
Rev. Caleb S. S. Dutton, pastor of the
First Unitarian Church of .San Fran
cisco, and one of the best-known minis
ters on the Pacific Coast, is to be the
University preacher for Memorial
Church for the coming three Sundays.
Rev. Dutton has come to be recognized
throughout the west as a deep thinker
on religious subjects, and his sermons
delivered from his San Francisco pulpit
have been quoted widely.
Rev. Dutton has been announced as

the speaker for the regular Tuesday
evening lecture in the Little Theatre
next Tuesday. The subject of his ad
dress will be "New Horizons." The
lecture will start at 8 p.m.
Another San Francisco pastor will
come to Stanford following Rev. Dutton.
On August 10, Rev. J. Wilmer Gresham,
dean of Grace Cathedral, San Francisco,
will start a three weeks' occupancy of
Memorial Church pulpit.
