# Col. Wing Speaks Before P.A. Commerce Chamber
## 
### 
Colonel Charles M. Wing, 23d Engi
neers, spoke at a luncheon of the I'alo
Alto Chamber of Commerce in a Palo

Alto cafe Tuesday, on reminiscences of
his experiences overseas. Colonel Wing
told of the exceptional work done by
the Engineers, which were composed for
the most part of Californians.
