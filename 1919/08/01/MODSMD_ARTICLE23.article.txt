# A.T. Worthy, '14, Returns from Overseas and Marries
## 
### 
The marriage of A. T. Worthy, '14,
to Miss Winifred Roberts, took place
on July 15. at the Santa Asa home of
the bride. Worthy has just returned
from overseas, bringing with him a
distinguished service medal for his
part in the Argonne offensive. He
was with Company .M, 362 d Infantry,
91st Division.
The young couple will reside in
Santa Ana, where Worthy will engage
in farming.
