# GRESHAM TO BE NEXT UNIVERSITY PREACHER
## 
### 
Rev. J. Wilmer Gresham of San Fran
cisco comes to Stanford next Sunday as
University preacher for the next Three
Sundays. Rev. Gresham is Dean of
Grace Cathedral, San Francisco, and the
announcement that he has been secured
to fill the pulpit of Memorial Church
means that a man of great religious in
fluence on the Pacific Coast is coming to
Stanford.
