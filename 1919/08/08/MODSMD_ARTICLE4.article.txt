# Business Man To Speak On Ethics
## 
### 
Stanford will have the opportunity
to hear a well-known speaker when
Dr. E. B. Copeland of Chico, Cali
fornia, comes here next Tuesday even
ing, to speak in the Little Theater.
The subject of his address will be
"The State in the Light of Natural
Ethics."
Dr. Copeland is president of the
Chico Chamber of Commerce and he

will speak on his subject from the
standpoint of a successful business
man.
The Public Entertainment Commit
tee feels especially gratified to be able
to obtain Dr. Copeland for one of the
Tuesday evening lectures. He has
made an intensive study of the sub
ject he is to speak 011 and is noted
for his pleasing manner of address.
