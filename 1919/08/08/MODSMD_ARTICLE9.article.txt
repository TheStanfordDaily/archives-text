# EDUCATION MAJORS PICNIC WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Members of the summer session
registered in the Education Depart
ment picniced at Roble bridge Wed
nesday night. This is the last picnic
that will be held at Roble bridge, for
before long the old-time foot bridge
is to give way to a county bridge
which will cross the creek at the point
to accommodate a county road.
