SUNDAY, August 17
11:00 a.m. DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: The
Reverend J. Wilmer Gresham, Dean of Grace Cathedral, San
Francisco.
4:00 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL in the Memorial Church
TUESDAY, August 19
8:00 p.m. RECITAL by Mme. Ina Wright Herbst, Soprano, and
Professor Gottfried Herbst, Violinist (members of the
faculty of the State College of Washington).
Mrs. Charles Moser, Accompanist.
PROGRAM
I. Ah, Perfido (Concert Aria) Beethoven
II. Fantasie Appassionata Vieuxtemps
III. Songs (a) To Rest Wolf
(b) To None Will I My Love Strauss
(c) Serenade Berger
IV. Introduction and Allegro Vivace Herbst
V. Oh, Divine Redeemer Gounod
Soprano, Violin, and Piano.
WEDNESDAY, August 20
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. CHOIR REHEARSAL, in the Memorial Church.
8:00 p.m. LECTURE on "Psychological Tests in the Army," by
Dr. Lewis M. Terman, Professor of Education, Stanford
University, in Room 50.
THURSDAY, August 21
8:00 p.m. ILLUSTRATED LECTURE on "Past and Present Day
Brazil," by Professor P. A. Martin, of the History Depart
ment, in the Little Theatre.
OFFICIAL NOTICES
MEMORIAL CHURCH
University Preachers:
August 17—The Reverend J. Wilmer Gresham, Dean of Grace Cathedral,
San Francisco.
August 24—The Reverend J. Wilmer Gresham.
Half Hour of Music: 4:00 p.m., Sunday; 4:15 p.m., Wednesday.
TUESDAY EVENING MEETINGS
August 19: A Recital by
Mme. Ina Wright Herbst, soprano, and
Prof. Gottfried Herbst, violinist.
August 26—Professor Burges Johnson of Vassar College, a lecture on
"The American Newspaper—Its Present and Future."
