# Public Dance At D. U. House Tonight
## 
### 
An informal dance will be given
at the Delta Upsilon house tonight
by Burdette Joerg. D. F. Maddox and
J. C. Patrick. A jazz orchestra com
posed of Reynolds. Maddox and Thay
er will furnish the music. Refresh
ments will be served.
Numerous dances have been given
with success on the campus this sum
mer, helping the students to become
acquainted. This will probably be
the last dance of the quarter.
Everybody is cordially invited to
this get-together. Dancing will be
gin at 8:30. The admission will be
seventy-five cents.
