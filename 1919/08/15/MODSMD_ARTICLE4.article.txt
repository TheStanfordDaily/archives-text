# TRAINER MALONEY RETURNS FROM WAR
## 
### 
Captain H. Wilfred Maloney, former
Athletic trainer here, arrived in New
York Monday, according to a telegram
received by his wife. It is presumed that
he returned on the transport Imperator,
on which were the officers and men who
won America's prizes at the Interallied
games in Pershing stadium outside of
Paris.
Captain Maloney will be remembered
here not only for his connection as a
trainer at Stanford, f)Ut also as Ath
letic director of Camp Fremont. He
has won recognition abroad as a coach
and trainer of American track and field
teams in Paris. Captain Maloney ex
pects to be here on the 22d of August.
