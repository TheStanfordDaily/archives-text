# BANDITS IMPRISON A STANFORD MAN
## 
### 
Lieutenant Paul H. Davis, one of the
two American aviators captured by Mex
ican bandits and recently ransomed by
Texas cowboys, is a former Stanford
man. He came to Stanford in 1915 with
the class: of 1919 and was here until
1917.
Lieutenant Davis majored in mechani
cal engineering. He registered from
Lindsay, California, which is near
Strathmore, given in the San Francisco
papers as the home of Lieutenant Davis'
father. The aviator now is assisting
an American punitive expedition in a
search for the bandits.
