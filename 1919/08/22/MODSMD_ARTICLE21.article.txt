# On'n Off the Campus
## 
### 
Miss Catherine Fairclough
Returns from England
Miss Catherine Fairclough '12, has
returned home from England where
she has been doing orthopedic work
as a nurse under Sir Robert Jones at
Liverpool. Miss Fairclough is a
daughter of Professor and Mrs. H. R.
Fairclough. Word has just been re
ceived from the professor who is head
of the Red Cross commission at
Montenegro, saying that he is enjoy
ing his work but that conditions in
| Montenegro are in a turmoil.
