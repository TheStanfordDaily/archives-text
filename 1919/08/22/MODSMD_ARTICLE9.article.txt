# "Reg" and "Dink" Back From Paris Games
## 
### 
"Dink" Templeton and "Reg"
Caughey have just returned from
France, where they represented Stan
ford at the Inter-Allied games, held
under the supervision of General John
J. Pershing in Paris, are again on the
campus.
Both men upheld the prestige of the
Cardinal. Templeton won second
place in the high jump, and Caughey
won the shot-put, nosing out his old
rival Liversidge of U. C. "Jud"
House of Stanford did his share by
winning third place in the high
hurdles.
