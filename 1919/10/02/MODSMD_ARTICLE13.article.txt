# Bookstore to Give Rebates On Last Year's Purchases
## 
### 
The Stanford Bookstore announces
tliat in the near future rebates on last
year's purchases will he given to those
who have turned in the profit-sharing:
certificates.
An expert accountant is at present
engaged in determining the amount of
the rebate, and students may expect to
receive dividends as soon as his work
is complete.
