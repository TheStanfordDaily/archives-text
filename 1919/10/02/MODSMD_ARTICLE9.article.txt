# REGISTRATION MARK HAS REACHED 2327
## 
### 
The total registration for the autumn
quarter, including the Lane clinic in San
Francisco, is 2,337, according to figures
issued by the Registrar this morning.
This means that the total before the end
of the period of registration will be at
least 2,500, or 250 above any previous
registration mark.
The number of new men registered is
634. Of this number 124 have advanced
standing, not included in the 450 limit and
the special provision made by the Trus
tees for war-service candidates.
