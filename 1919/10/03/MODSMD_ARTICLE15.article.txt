# HOLD MATRICULATION SERVICES ON SUNDAY
## 
### 
Next Sunday at Memorial Church Ma
triculation Sunday will he observed.
Services will begin promptly at 11 o'clock.
Members of the class of 1923 are re
quested to assemble at the Little Theatre
at 9:45 for the procession to the church.
Dr. Gardner will deliver the sermon, and
Dr. Wilbur will give the official welcome
to the new students.
Matriculation Sunday takes the place
in the life of the new student that Bac
calaureate Sunday holds in the life of
the graduate. It is one of the two times
in the four years of college life that the
chaplain asks people to come to church.
A large attendance of new students is
expected.
