# STANFORD UNIVERSITY
## WEEKLY CALENDAR 
### 
Vol. XI. No. 1

PUBLISHED EVERY FRIDAY

October 5-11, 1919

4
SUNDAY, October 5
11:00 a.m.—MA I RICULATION SERVICE. Addresses by President
Ray Lyman Wilbur and Chaplain D. Charles Gardner.
Soloist: Mr. C. L. McCormick.
(Members of the Class of 1923 are requested to assemble
at the Little Theatre at 10:45 sharp for the procession
to the Church.)
4:00 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
MONDAY, October 6
U :00 a.m.—STUDENT BODY ASSEMBLY, in the Assembly Hall.
TUESDAY, October 7
4:15 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—ADDRESS on "The Shantung Issue," by Professor
Payson J. Treat, of the Department of History; in
the Little Theatre.
WEDNESDAY, October 8
3:00 p.m.—RESIDENT ALUMNAE TEA, at the Women's Club
House. Hostesses: Resident Stanford Alumnae Asso
ciation.
7:30 p.m.—NESTORIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12, Law
Building.
7:30 p.m.—FRESHMEN will meet in the Assembly Hall.
THURSDAY, October 9
4:15 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12,
Law Building.
7:30 p.m.—CHOIR REHEARSAL, in the Memorial Church.

OFFICIAL NOTICES

MEMORIAL CHURCH
University Preachers:
October 5—Matriculation Sunday. Speakers: President Wilbur and
Chaplain Gardner.
Morning Chapel: 7:55 a.m., Monday to Friday, inclusive.
Half Hour of Music: 4:00 p.m., Sunday; 4:15 p.m., Tues
day and Thursday.
TUESDAY EVENING MEETINGS
October 7—Professor Payson J. Treat, an address on "The Shantung
Issue."
October 14—Professor William F. Durand, an address on "The Devas
tated Region in France" (illustrated by French Govern
ment Photographs).

October 21—To be filled.
October 28—Mr. A. F. Whyte, M. P., Editor of The New Europe, an
address on "Labor in Great Britain."
THOMAS WELTON STANFORD ART GALLERY
The Art Gallery is open to the public, without charge, during the
following hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., daily, including Sunday.
THE WEEKLY CALENDAR
Announcements for the Weekly Calendar, legibly written and
signed, should be in the hands of the President's Secretary, President's
Office, not later than 5:00 p.m., Thursday.
President's Secretary.
