# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB WILL MEET MONDAY
## 
### 
The first regular meeting of the Cos
mopolitan Club will he held next Mon
day night. October 6, at the Woman's
Clubhouse at 8 o'clock. All members
arc urged to be present, and an invita
tion has been extended to all those
interested to attend the meeting. An
important business meeting will be held.
It is the purpose of the club to gather
together students of all nationalities and
to foster the spirit of internationalism,
and to give programs with well-known
speakers addressing the club.
