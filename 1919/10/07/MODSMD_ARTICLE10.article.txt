# WORLD'S CHAMPION SWIMMER TO GIVE EXHIBITION HERE
## 
### 
Norman Ross, winner of all
swimming events at Paris re
cently, holder of world's
records in the sprints, and form-,
er Stanford student, will appear
on the campus Monday after
noon, for an exhibition swim in
Encina tank. Ross has consent
ed to swim here at the request
of Ernst Brandsten, Stanford
swimming director, who coached
Ross when he was a freshman
here.
The inter-class swimming con
test will be held on the same
day. Brandsten is anxious for
each class to enter all of its
best water performers in order
to make the meet a success.
Considerable interest was taken
in last year's event.
