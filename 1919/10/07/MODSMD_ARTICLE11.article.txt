# Sword and Sandals to Stage First Fall Show
## 
### 
Sword and Sandals, which was re
organized last quarter, has decided to
produce a play on the campus in the
near future. Full particulars of the com
ing production have not been given out.
At a recent meeting the following
officers were elected: L. W. McDer
mott, '17, president; Gordon Davis, '18,
secretary; and R. G. Whealton, '19,
treasurer. Three new men were taken
in at this meeting: Leland Pickering.
'20, James Boyle, '20, A. A. Curtice, '19,
and Robert Reid, '21.
