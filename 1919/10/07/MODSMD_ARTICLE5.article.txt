# GOLF ENTHUSIASTS WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
As the students of the University
have evinced much enthusiastic in
terest in golf, it has been decided to
organize this sport as a course in
physical training. Credit will be giv
pii for this work as for work in other
departments of physical training.
Temporary arrangements have been
made whereby students uso the Menlo
Park links in conjunction with the
Menlo Park Golf Club until such time
as our own links, existent before the

war. can be putin condition.
All men interested in golf are re
quested to report at Encina Gymnasi
um, tonight at 8 o'clock.
