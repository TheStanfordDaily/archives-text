# WORK FOR CROSS COUNTRY RUNNERS WILL COMMENCE
## 
### 
Practice for cross country men will
be held every Monday, Wednesday
and Friday, under the direction of
Coach Maloney and track captain
"Jess" Wells, '20. Candidates are
asked to report at Encina Gymnasium
where the practice runs will start.
The annual cross country run with
California will be held at Stanford this
year, over a course to be laid out in
the hills near the campus. A. D.
Westwick, '18, a veteran cross country
man will assist in training the squad,
and with the addition of several old
men, a nucleus' for a winning team
is expected to report for practice.
