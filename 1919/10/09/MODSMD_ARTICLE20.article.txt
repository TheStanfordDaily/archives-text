# Miss Angie Cross, '18, Is Married In Merced
## 
### 
Miss Helen Angeline Cross, Stanford
'18, and Julian Young Burton of Salt
Lake, Stanford '19, were married at the
home of the bride's parents, Mr. and
Mrs. C. M. Cross, in Merced Monday.
Mrs. Burton was a Kappa Kappa
Gamma, and the bridegroom was a mem
ber of the Stanford Ambulance Unit
and won the Croix de Guerre.
