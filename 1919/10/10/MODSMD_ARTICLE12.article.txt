# BIG ENCINA HALL PAJAMA PARADE STARTLES THE ROW
## 
### 
Encina broke l'orth last night in the
traditional pajama parade proceeding
through the library arcade, around
Roble Hall, up the row, and back by
way of the various fraternity and
sorority house porches, rendering the
customary vocal accompaniment.
The men then proceeded back to the
Encina steps, where pictures were
taken. The yearly pajama parade is
a tradition of the hall.
