# SCHUBERT CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS
## 
### 
Schubert Club met at 4:15 Thursday
and elected the following officers: Presi
dent, Louise Kelly; secretary, Helen
Haist; treasurer, Isabel Young; libra
rian, Zclda Wickersham. It was de
cided that meetings should be held from
five to six on Wednesday afternoon in
Dr. Allen's office, room 84, Inner Quad.
Dr. Allen will direct the singing for
the first few weeks, and possibly all
quarter. The first regular meeting will
be next Wednesday. Dates for tryouts
will be announced later.
