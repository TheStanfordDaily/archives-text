House. Hostesses: Mrs. H. B. Reynolds, Mrs. G. D.
Clark. Miss Katherine Fay, Mrs. F. H. Fowler, Mrs.
P. J. Treat.
7:30 p.m. NESTORIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12, Law
Building.
THURSDAY, October 16
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12, Law-
Building.
