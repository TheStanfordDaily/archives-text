# Dance Will Provide Funds For Game Stunts
## 
### 
In order to provide money for "Big
Game" stunts on November 22, a dance
will be given Saturday night of this
week at Encina Hall. The Encina floor
is being donated by the members of En
cina Club.
The Olympic Club football team, which
plays the Varsity Saturday afternoon,
will be the guests of honor at the dance.
The tickets will cost $1.10.
