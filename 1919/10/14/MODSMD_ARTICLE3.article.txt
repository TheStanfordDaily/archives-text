# FREMONT POST OF AMERICAN LEGION HOLDS MEETING
## 
### 
An enthusiastic meeting of the Fre
mont Post of the American Legion was
held last night in the Little Theatre,
when the Stanford world-war veterans
heard some inspiring talks on the ideals
of the legion and were later signed up.
This was the first meeting of its kind
on the campus, hut another will he held
soon in order to give all men who served
in the army, navy, or marine corps a
chance to be taken in.
Mr. G. F, Morcll, editor of the Palo
Alto Times and organizer of the Fre
mont Post, closed the meeting with an
impressive talk on the possibilities of the
legion in the future.
