# "Y" Association Will Be Organized by Cabinet
## 
### 
Members of tlie Cabinet of the Stan
ford Y. M. C. A. arc to meet Wednes
day evening at the Union at 8:00 p.m.
The problems of organizing the Associ
ation more effectively and a discussion
of the program for the year will be
taken up. Plans for the discussion
groups campaign will be formulated, and
the project of Bible groups also taken
into consideration. It is necessary that
every member of the Cabinet attend the
meeting in order to have a comprehen
sive knowledge of the activities of the
"Y" for the quarter.
