# ENGLISH CLUB WILL MAKE PLANS TONIGHT
## 
### 
English Club members are to meet
this evening in room 200t-E, in the
English Building, at 8 p.m., where an
election of officers for the coming
year will be held, and plans for this
quarter discussed. A complete report
of the finances of the Pageant which
was held last spring is also to be
rendered. All former members of the
society who have recently returned
from service are urged to be present
and assist in the work of making plans
for 1919-20.
