# FRESHMEN WILL HOLD FIRST CLASS MEETING
## 
### 
According to the old tradition of the
seniors helping to organize the fresh
man class, A. T. George, senior class
president, has called a meeting of all
beginning first-year men and women,
in the Assembly Hall, at 1 o'clock
Friday.
A permanent organization will be
formed and officers will be' elected at
this meeting. All freshmen are urged
to attend in order that the class will
be able to start the year off on the
right foot.
