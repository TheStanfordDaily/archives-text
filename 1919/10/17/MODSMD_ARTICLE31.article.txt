# STANFORD UNIVERSITY
## WEEKLY CALENDAR 
### 
Vol. XI. No. 3.

PUBLISHED EVERY FRIDAY

October 19-25, 1919.

SUNDAY, October 19
11:00 a.m. DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: The
Reverend O. P. Gifford, of Los Angeles. Soloist: Pro
fessor Charles Moser.
4:00 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
MONDAY, October 20
7:30 p.m. Y. M. C. A. FRESHMAN MASS MEETING; in the
Assembly Hall. Address by Dr. David Starr Jordan,
Chancellor Emeritus, on "The History and Founders'
Ideals of Stanford."
TUESDAY, October 21
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. ADDRESS on "The Red Cross in France and Germany,"
by Miss Edith R. Mirrielees, of the Department of
English: in the Little Theatre.
WEDNESDAY, October 22
3:00 p.m. RESIDENT ALUMNAE TEA, in the Women's Club
House. Hostesses: Mrs. Edward Krehbiel, Mrs. L. P.
Collins, Mrs. Howard Eager, Mrs. J. W. Bingham. Mrs.
J. S. P. Tatlock.
7:30 p.m. NESTORIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12, Law
Building.
8:00 p.m. CERCLE FRANQAIS. in the Women's Club House.
Open meeting.
THURSDAY, October 23
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12, Law
Building.
7:30 p.m. CHOIR REHEARSAL, in the Memorial Church.
FRIDAY, October 24
8:00 p.m. RECEPTION to Seniors and Graduate Students, by Presi
dent and Mrs. Wilbur, at the President's House.
