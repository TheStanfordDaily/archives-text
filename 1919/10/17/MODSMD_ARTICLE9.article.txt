# Miss Mirrielees Will Lecture Tuesday
## 
### 
Miss Edith Mirrielees, instructor in
the English Department, who has just
returned home after services over
seas with the Stanford Women's Unit,
has consented to give the next Tues
day evening lecture in the Little The
ater. Iler subject will he the work
of the Red Cross in both France and
Germany.
Although her work was, for the
major part, in the hospitals in France,
especially at Vichy, Miss Mirrielees
was later stationed at Coblenz and
other German cities.
