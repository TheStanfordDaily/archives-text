# King and Queen Will Spend Tomorrow Here
## 
### 
King Albert and the Queen of Bel
gium will be the guests of Stanford and
Palo Alto to-morrow afternoon and eve
ning, when they will come down from
San Francisco to take dinner with Mr.
and Mrs. Herbert Hoover. During the
day the king and queen will be enter
tained by a special committee of San
Francisco women. After spending the
evening here the royal couple will return
to San Francisco, leaving to-morrow for
the beginning of their homeward trip,
which will take them through the Grand
Canyon.
