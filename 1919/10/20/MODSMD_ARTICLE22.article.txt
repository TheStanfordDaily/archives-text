# Albert F. Caswell, '08, Receives Fellowship
## 
### 
Albert F. Caswell, '08, has been
awarded a fellowship in physics by the
National Research Council of the Rocke
feller Foundation, according to an an
nouncement received by Professor
Vernon L. Kellogg.
Caswell received his A. B. in mathe
matics in January, 1908, and his Ph. D.
in physics in May, 1911. He was pro
fessor of mathematics in the University
of Oregon.
