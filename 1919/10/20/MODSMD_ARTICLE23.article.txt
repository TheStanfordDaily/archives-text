# GLEE CLUB REHEARSAL TOMORROW AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Glee Club rehearsals will begin
promptly at 4:30 tomorrow afternoon
in Room 84, according to an announce
ment made today by President George
Luhrs, '18. All members are expected
to be present. Other meetings are
scheduled to take place on Wednes
day evening at 7:15 and Thursday
afternoon at 4:30. Dr. W. D. Allen,
the University Organist and director
of the Glee Club, will have a large
collection of songs on hand Tuesday
from which selections can be made
for the club's quarter work.
