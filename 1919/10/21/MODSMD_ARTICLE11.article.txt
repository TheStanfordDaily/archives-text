# E.R. CAUGHEY, '18, ELECTED TO PILOT VARSITY FOOTBALL
## 
### 
E. R. "Reg" Caughey, '18, former cap
tain of the Varsity track team and win
ner of the shot-put event at Paris re
cently in the inter-allied service meet,
was elected captain of the Varsity foot
ball team last night at a meeting on the
field. Caughey won over a field of sev
eral candidates. In the army he played
fullback with the famous Allentows
(Pa.) "Usaacs" football team.
Caughey is a member of Quadrangle
Club, Skull and Snakes, Phi Delta Phi,
and Encina Club.
