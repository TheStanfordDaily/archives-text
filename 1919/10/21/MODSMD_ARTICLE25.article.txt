# Alumnae Tea On Wednesday Afternoon
## 
### 
Mrs. Edwin Krehbiel, Mrs. Leroy Col
lins, Mrs. Howard Eager, Mrs. J. W.
Bingham, and Mrs. J. S. P. Tatlock will
act as hostesses at the Alumnae tea
which is to be held in the Women's Club
house Wednesday afternoon from 3 until
5. Bridge tables may be reserved by tel
ephoning to Mrs. A. C. Whitaker at Palo
Alto 1081J. The proceeds from these
teas go toward the furnishing and up
keep of the clubhouse, and should they
prove as successful in the future as the
last one, Wednesday will be given regu
larly every week.
