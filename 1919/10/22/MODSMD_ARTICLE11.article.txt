# Graduates of Stanford Are Married in Texas
## 
### 
Word has been received from El
Paso of the marriage on Oct. 16. of
Miss Marion Gilbert, '18, to Alfred
Coleman, Jr.,. Mr. and Mrs. Coleman
will live in Morencl, Arizona Mr.
Coleman graduated from the Engineer
ing department in 1917. He was a
member of Delta Upsilon. Miss Gil
bert belonged to Alpha Omicron Pi.
