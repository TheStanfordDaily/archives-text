# Control Committee to Consider Suggestions
## 
### 
Several suggestions for changes in the
proposed student control constitution
were made to the committee today and
will be considered at a committee meet
ing to be held tomorrow night. It is
expected that points from some of the
suggestions will be embodied in the con
stitution. The amended constitution, if
changes are made in the committee's first
draft, will he republished Friday. F. L.
King, '19 Student Body President, an
nounced today that the quad election for
ratification of the constitution will be
held a week from tomorrow.
