# SEQUOIA CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS AT MEETING
## 
### 
The Sequoia Social Club has organized
and elected the following:
Officers: F. B. Pierson, president; R.
G. Van Gundy, Vice-President; F. A.
Covey, Secretary; J. H. Hollingshead,
Treasurer. The following committees
were chosen: House, E. H. Templin, C.
R. Austin, B. F. Haley, and E. W. But
ler; Financial, W. N. Whiteford and H.
L. Puterbaugh; Social, E. E. Sapping
ton, Bert Silen, S. W. Harrison, H. W.
Powell, and E. L. Harker.
