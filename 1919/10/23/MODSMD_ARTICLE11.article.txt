# WOMEN HIKERS WILL TAKE TRIP
## 
### 
Jasper Ridge is the goal set for
Women's Hiking Club this Saturday.
They are to leave Iloble Hall at 8:15,
and expect to be back on the campus
by noon.
The club is under the supervision
of Miss McCracken of the Entomology
department. A committee of five,
composed of Selma Larson, Mary
Mathews, Margaret Carlsmitli, Eleanor
Hill and Eloise Horton, are to meet
with Miss McCracken this week-end
and plan all the hiking trips for the
quarter.
