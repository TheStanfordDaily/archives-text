# Creglow, 1923 Fullback Will Captain Babes
## 
### 
L. A. Creglow, fullback on the fresh
man football team, was elected captain
of the eleven by the squad at a meet
ing last night. Creglow registered
from Palo Alto and gained his foot
ball experience in an lowa high
school.
He has been one of the mainstays
of the "babe" team in the games
played so far, and Acker is counting
on him as one of his chief ground
gainers.
