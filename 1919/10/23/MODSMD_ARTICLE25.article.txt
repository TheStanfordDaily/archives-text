# SHOOTING BEGINS FOR RIFLE CLUB
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Rifle Club
desiring to shoot will report to Sargeant,
Reard at the R. O. T. C. headquarters
on Monday. On that day shooting will
commence. The range will be open from
1 to 6 on all days excepting Saturday
and Sunday.
The butts are being moved to the
southwest corner of the gymnasium area,
and the carrier targets, which are being
installed, will be ready by the end of the
week.
All men who are not at present mem
bers of the R. O. T. C. but interested
in rifle work can give their names to
Secretary E. D. Barton at Encina Hall.
