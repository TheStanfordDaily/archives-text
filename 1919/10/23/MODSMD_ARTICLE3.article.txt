# STANFORD RUGBY CLUB DEFEATS "PALY" HIGH
## 
### 
The Stanford Rugby Club team met
and defeated the Palo Alto High School
team yesterday afternoon in the first
practice game of the season by a score
of 15 to 9. This afternoon the team will
line up against the San Mateo High ag
gregation. The men who play in today's
game will be the ones who will play in
the Big Game against the Australians
next Saturday afternoon.
