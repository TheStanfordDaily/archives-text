# G.O.P. Leader to Confer With Local Organizers
## 
### 
Raymond Benjamin, regional director
of the Republican National Committee,
will meet with the sponsors of the pro
posed Stanford Republican Club tonight
to consider plans for the organization of
the club. The meeting tonight will be
preliminary to an open meeting to be held
probably within a week, when all stu
dents interested in the Republican cause
will be invited to attend. Benjamin,
whose headquarters are in San Francisco,
will explain the details of organization at
the conference tonight and will assist in
making arrangements for the permanent
organization of the club.
