# AERO TAXI HERE AT STANFORD SOON
## 
### 
Ex-Lieutenant V. H. Brazil, '20, will
be on the campus sometime next week
with his private aeroplane, in which he
will do considerable flying. Passengers
will be taken on air rides at the rate of
?1 a minute. While in the service, Lieu
tenant Brazil was flying instructor at
March Field, Riverside.
