# PRESIDENT WILBUR TO ENTERTAIN SENIORS
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Wilbur will throw
open the President's house to seniors
and holdovers, in an informal reception,
at 8:30 tonight. Mrs. Wilbur has ex
pressed herself as desirous of becoming
better acquainted with the members of
these classes, and the reception will take
the form of a "get together." Dancing
until 11 o'clock will follow the pres
entation of the students to Dr. and Mrs.
Wilbur.
