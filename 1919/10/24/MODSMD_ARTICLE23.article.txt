# HOLDOVERS PLAN TO ORGANIZE ON CAMPUS

### 
Definite plans for the future activities
of Stanford holdovers as an organiza
tion will be arranged by a committee
consisting of five members of this or

The committeemen, who were chosen
at a holdover meeting today, are as fol
lows : Edward Henderson, Chairman;
Walter Gores, Albert Fisher, Bcrtrand
West, and Charles Vandervort.
