# Freshmen Women Tennis Stars Asked to Appear
## 
### 
That special effort be made to enroll
the good tennis players of the fresh
man class was the decision made by the
Women's Tennis Club yesterday. All
women interested in the big tournament
which will be played off in two weeks
are asked to sign up on the schedules
posted in the gymnasium and in the rest
room.
The club is anxious to make this a big
year for tennis and is very desirous of
meeting all the new players of the fresh
man class.
