# Stanford Has Entry In Championship Bouts
## 
### 
J. T. Murphy, '20, welterweight, is the
Stanford boxer entered in the Pa
cific Coast amateur boxing tournament,
to be held at the Olympic Clubhouse in
San Francisco October 29 and 30, at
8:15 p. m.
The Olympic Club is holding these
bouts under the auspices of the Amateur
Boxing Association of the Pacific Coast.
Bouts will be held to determine the coast
champions in all weights from 108
pounds or under to 175 pounds or over.
