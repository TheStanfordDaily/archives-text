# LOCAL G.O.P. MEN ORGANIZED CLUB HERE LAST NIGHT
## 
### 
Temporary organization of the Stan
ford Republican Club was effected at a
meeting of Republican party supporters
last night. Raymond Benjamin, region
al director of the Republican National
Committee, was here from San Fran
cisco and aided the local organizers in
making plans for permanent organiza
tion.
G. P. Wood, '20, one of the sponsors
of the local club, was elected temporary
chairman at the meeting last night. B. S.
Harris, '19, another supporter of the
club, was elected secretary-treasurer.
The club will be permanently orga
nized an open meeting to be held prob
ably next week.
