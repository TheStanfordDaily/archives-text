# EX-AMBULANCE MEN HOLD GET-TOGETHER AT PHI DELT HOUSE
## 
### 
Nineteen Stanford men who went to
France with the American Ambulance
Corps held a reunion last evening at
the Phi Delta Theta house. Dinner was
had, and following that there was a gen
eral get-together meeting of the men.
War experiences were related by all.
At the meeting plans were made for
the permanent organization of the men,
and it was planned to have similar meet
ings in the future. Those present in
cluded many who were transferred to
some other branch of the service upon
their arrival in France.
Among those present were Robert
Dinglcy, '17. and Arthur Cyril, who will
be remembered for his help in the pro
duction of many campus plays.
