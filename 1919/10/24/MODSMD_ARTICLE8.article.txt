# Asilomar Committee Will Serve Waffles
## 
### 
Crisp brown waffles are to be the
feature of the breakfast to be given in
the women's clubhouse by the Y. W. C.
A. tomorrow morning at 9 o'clock. This
breakfast is to be conducted under the
auspices of the Asilomar committee,
and all women students who have at
tended any conferences at Asilomar in
the past are cordially invited to attend.
