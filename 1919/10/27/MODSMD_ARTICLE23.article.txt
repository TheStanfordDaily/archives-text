# Air Service Men Will Organize Tomorrow
## 
### 
Lieutenant Robert E. O'Hanley, a
pilot in the Army Air Service, has
called a meeting of all army, navy,
marine, and civilian fliers. The meet
ing will be held in the Little Theatre
Tuesday evening at 7:30. At the meet
ing steps will he taken toward the or
ganization of an Allied Fliers' Club and
plans will be discussed for the estab
lishment of an airdome on the campus.
All ex-servioc and civilian fliers are
urged to attend this meeting.
