# Stanford Man Receives Release From Russia
## 
### 
Albert F. Coyle, '15, who was im
prisoned by the Bolshevists in the Arch
angel district early in August, has been
released. A telegram from the State
Department made public last Saturday
by the New York Y. M. C. A. Commit
tee confirming news of the release has
relieved anxiety concerning the Stanford
nan. Coyle, when imprisoned, was one
of over a hundred "Y" Secretaries serv
ing with the allied forces on the Arch
angel front.
