# 1923 To Jolly-Up Next Saturday
## 
### 
The first jolly-up of the quarter will
he held in the Encina gym Friday eve
ning, October 31. The class of '23 is
planning the affair, and the committee
in charge is alrealy hard at work in an
effort to make the dance a success.
Lots of pep is promised, and several
novel surprises will be featured during
the evening.
The jolly-up will be the first oppor
tunity that the class of '23 has had to get
informally acquainted, and it is urged
that as many as possible be present.
Dancing will begin at 8:15 p.m. Ad
mission will be 75 cents, and tickets may
lie purchased now at the Bookstore.
