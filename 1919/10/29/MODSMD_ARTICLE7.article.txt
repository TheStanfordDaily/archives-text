# EVERETT W. SMITH IS HONORED BY TEACHERS
## 
### 
Everett W. Smith, '99, Professor of
Journalism, was elected President of
the American Association of Teachers
of Journalism, at its convention, held
at the University of Michigan, Ann
Arbor, from October 1G to 18.
The convention was composed of
teachers of journalism and Michigan
newspapermen. It placed itself on rec
ord as opposing the use of the term
"Journalism Courses." as applied to
the news writing instruction taught
in preparatory schools.
