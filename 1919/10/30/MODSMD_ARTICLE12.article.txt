# First American Football Rally Comes Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford's first American football ral
ly is scheduled for tonight at Encir.a
Hall, where the Cardinal warriors will
be given the first real chance to judge
the kind of spirit with which the men
on the "Farm" are backing the coming
Big Game.
Assistant Leaders R. H. Armstrong,
'21, and W. H. Kinney, '21, will organize
the men of the row parade to Encina at
7:15 this evening. Captain Caughey,
Coach Evans, and other football men
will speak.
Martin stated this morning that the
Rig Game is approximately only three
weeks away, and that the student body
should begin to show real support right
away.
