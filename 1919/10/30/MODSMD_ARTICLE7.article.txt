# "Y" DRIVE FOR MEMBERS IS ON
## 
### 
An intensive, campaign for member
ship in the Y. M. C. A. will be con
ducted next week by the Association as
i step toward increasing its scope on
the campus. The drive will, it is hoped
by the "Y," line up every man who can

membership this year will be on a much
broader basis than before in order to
include everybody. P. Roosman, '21, is
in charge of the campaign, subcommit
tees for which are to be appointed in
.very group on the campus to facilitate
