# POETRY READINGS GIVEN BY ALDEN
## 
### 
Selections from late contemporary
poetry was the subject chosen for the
Thursday reading hour by Professor R.
M. Alden yesterday afternoon.
Professor Alden has for some time de
voted much attention to the reviewing
of recent poetry for The Dial and The
Nation. The poems read yesterday in
cluded Kipling's "France." selected from
the work of Willard Wattels, Clement
Wood, Iris Tree, and other poems rep
resentative of the new types of contem
porary verse.
Professor Alden's reading was the
first of a series of Thursday reading
hours being given according to a new
plan, under the direction of the English
Department.
