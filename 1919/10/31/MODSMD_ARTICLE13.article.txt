# TASTE IN POETRY 15 LECTURE TOPIC
## 
### 
"Can the Taste for Poetry be
Measured?" is the question to be dis
cussed in the next Tuesday evening
lecture by Professor Allan Abbott, of
the Teachers' College, Columbia Uni
versity.
Professor Abbott is making a tour of
the West in an interesting and curious
experiment in literary taste. He dis
torts good poetry and then sees how
many people really feel the difference
between the original and the distorted
version.
