# DR. JORDAN TO TALK OF FUTURE STANFORD
## 
### 
Dr. David Starr Jordan, Chancellor
Emeritus, will address the members of
the Cosmopolitan Club, and the general
public to-night at the Women's Club
House at 8 p.m. Dr. Jordan's topic
will be "The Future Stanford as I
Would Like It." The general public is
invited to attend this meeting, which is
being held under the auspices of the
Cosmopolitan Club. Following the lec
ture there will be a short business meet
ing.
