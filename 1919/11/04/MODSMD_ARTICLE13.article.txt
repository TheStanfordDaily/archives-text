# LA
						LIANA RECEPTION COMES TOMORROW
## 
### 
La Liana has invited all Palo Alto
women students to attend the informal
reception to new women to be held
Wednesday evening, from 7:30 to 9:30
at the home of Shirley Charles, '23,
251 Lincoln Avenue.
The meeting will afford all Palo
Alto women a chance to become bet
ter acquainted, and to start the year
well for the club. Entertainment and
refreshments will be features of the
evening.
Plans for the reception were made
at the first meeting of the club early
in the quarter. Officers were then
elected, and Marguerite Eiskamp, '20,
was made president.
