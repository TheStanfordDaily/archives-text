# NEW RED CROSS DRIVE CAPTAINS APPOINTED
## Final Plans Formulated at Captains' Meeting Last Night 
### 
At the meeting of the Red Cross
Drive Committee held last night in the
Woman's Clubhouse several changes
were announced in the captains for the
drive.
Ray Ground, '21, will have charge
of the work at Encina, and Bradley
Wyatt, '23. will act as captain at Se
quoia.
All the material for the drive was dis
tributed to the captains at the meeting
held last night, and sixty-one workers
were chosen to carry on the work in
cach house and organization on the cam
pus. The committee is concentrating all
efforts to make Thursday, November 6,
when subscriptions will he taken, the big
day in the drive.
