# 25 DELEGATES WILL REPRESENT Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Selection of Stanford's twenty-five
delegates to the Student Volunteer Con
vention which will be held in Des
Moines, lowa, December 31 to January
4, is now being arranged by a joint
Y. M. C. A. and Y. W. C. A. Commit
tee. This committee will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse Monday at 4 p. m.
The Y. M. C. A. members are C. Titus,
'18, D. Lundquist, '22, H. Hosepian, *21,
T. Purmort, '20, S. K. Davis, '20, and
F. L. King, '19. Two faculty members
and five Y. W. C. A. members will also
be present to select the delegates.
