# CHEMISTRY SMOKER TOMORROW NIGHT
## 
### 
Novel stunts and good eats will be
featured at the Chemistry smoker,
Wednesday evening at 7:30 o'clock,
at the Union. This is the first big
smoker of the quarter and all interest
ed in chemistry are urged to attend.
A special invitation is extended to
the freshmen.
Dr. Stillman and Dr. Swain, heads
of the chemistry department, will give
interesting speeches. Admission to
the smoker will be 50 cents.
