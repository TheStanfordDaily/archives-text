# ANNOUNCEMENT
## 
### 
Registrar's Office —Students expect
ing to graduate from the University
this quarter, should hand in their
names to the Registrar's Office, be
fore the 10th of this month, without
fail.
WEDNESDAY
French Club — will not meet tonight as
was announced yesterday.
Women's Conference — Meets at 7:15
in the Clubhouse.
Phi Delta Phi — Meets at Stanford Inn,
at 12 o'clock, noon.
Glee Club — Meets in Room 80, inner
Quad, at 7:15 p.m.
Schubert Club — Meets in Room 84,
inner Quad, at 5 p.m.
Iota Sigma Pi — Meets in the Club
House, at 7:30 p.m.
Sophomore Cotillion Committee — Will
meet at 7:30 in the Pi Beta Phi
house.
Sword and Sandals — Dress rehearsal
for "The First Born" tonight in the
Assembly Hall, at 7 o'clock p.m.
Entire cast and minor characters.
THURSDAY
Law Students' Smoker — In the Union,
at 7:30 p.m.
Hammer and Coffin — in Room 400, at
the Encina, at 6 o'clock pm.
Band Concert and Song Rally — in the
music shell, at 4:15.
Cap and Gown — Faculty and student
members will meet, at Kappa Alpha
Theta house, at 4:30 p.m.
Y. W. C. A. — Meeting in the Club
House, at 5 o'clock p.m.
THURSDAY
Ram's Head — At the Union, at 1:00
o'clock p.m.
