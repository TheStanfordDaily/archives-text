# Freshman Hand Books Are Now Available
## 
### 
Freshman handbooks for this year
are now available for distribution at the
Y. M. C. A. office in the Union. The
"Frosh Bibles" will be given out to all
first-year men, free of charge.
Every year the Y. M. C. A. publishes
these handbooks, which contain all the
important facts and history about Stan
ford, the college songs, yells, and other
information necessary to put the fresh
men "On the Boat."
