# INTERSOCIETY DEBATE WITH C.O.P., DEC. 4
## 
### 
Tryouts for the dual debate to be
held between the Pacific Forum, a
forensic society of the College of Pa
cific, and Euphronia, will be held this
evening at 7:30 in room 12, of the
Law Building.
Tryoutees are expected to discuss
some phase of the proposition: "Re
solved —That railroad strikes should
be prohibited by federal law, consti
tutionality granted." This will be the
subject for the final debates to be
held December 4th.
Four members of Euphronia who
have not participated in any inter
collegiate debate will be chosen from
the contest this evening to represent
the society. •
