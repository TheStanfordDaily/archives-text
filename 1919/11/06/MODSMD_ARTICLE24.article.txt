# Senior Hockey Team Defeats Freshmen, 7-2
## 
### 
In the first game of the interclass
series played yesterday afternoon, the
senior women's hockey team defeated
the freshman team, by a score of 7
to 2. The game was marked by lack
of team work on both sides.
Doris Hyer, captain of the senior
team, made one goal, and Ruth Whit
aker, captain of the freshman team,
made the two scored by her side.
The second game of the series, be
tween the juniors nad sophomores,
was played at 4:30 this afternoon.
