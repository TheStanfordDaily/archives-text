# G. and M. Smoker Will Be Held Wednesday
## 
### 
Eats, smokes, and a rousing good
time is promised to all geology and
mining majors at the Union next

Wednesday evening, at 7:30. This
will be the first G. & M. smoker this
quarter, and the committee in charge
promises that it will be up to all ex
pectations, a good program and lots
of eats having been arranged for.
