# G.O.P. CLUB MEETS FOR ORGANIZATION
## 
### 
Final organization of the Stanford
Republican Club will be effected at an
open meeting to be held in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 Wednesday night.
The meeting first was announced for
Thursday night, but conflicting dates
made a change of the date necessary. All
students who wish to affiliate with the
club will be enrolled at the meeting.
Much interest is being shown in the pro
posed organization, and a large member
ship is anticipated by the club sponsors.
Men and women both are invited to at
tend this meeting.
