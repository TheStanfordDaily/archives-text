# Elton R. Shaw Will Speak On Industry
## 
### 
Elton R. Shaw, head of the Shaw
Canneries of San Jose, will speak at the
Y. W. C. A. meeting in the Women's
Clubhouse tomorrow afternoon at 5. He
will outline the problems that confront
the average employer in such an estab
lishment, and will also give an insight
into the solutions of those problems.
This is the first of a series of three
lectures dealing with labor problems and
their solution that, will be given here.
Next week an employee of one of the
large business houses will give her view
of the situation.
