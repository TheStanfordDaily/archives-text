# Herbert Dunn, '18, Was Married Last Friday
## 
### 
News has reached the campus of the
marriage of Herbert Dunn, '18, to Miss
Mary Taylor of Fresno. The wedding
was solemnized on last Friday.
Miss Taylor is a member of Delta
Delta Delta and has attended the Uni
versity of California for the last two
years. The romance is one which began
in high school. L. R. Chandler, '18, and
W. H. Thayer, '20, both Zeta Psi fra
ternity brothers of the groom, were pres
ent at the wedding. Mr. Dunn will bring
his bride to Stanford, where he will com
plete his course. He served during the
war as a lieutenant in the Army Air
Corps.
