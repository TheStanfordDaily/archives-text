# WORK ON BIG GAME BONFIRE TO START FRIDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Work on the big bonfire will be
gin Friday afternoon and continue un
til Thursday. The freshman class will
be organized for the work at a meet
ing in Room 214 at 1 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon, when the plans of gather
ing the wood and guarding will be
outlined. This meeting will be for
men only and every freshman will be
checked by the sophomore committee
in charge. Sufficient wood for the
building of the fire has already been
located and will be hauled to the
campus by R. O. T. C. trucks.
