# The 						Wrong Door
## 
### 
By Ivan Benson, '18
We ought to have more holidays.
000
A good healthy Wildcat could make
the Golden Bear look like a last year's
meal ticket. Don't crowd, stunt com
mittee. Do we get the fur-lined shav
ing mug?
000
It is rumored that the enthusiastic
strikers who marched Monday noon
were not conspicuous in patriotic
demonstrations Tuesday. No, father,
this is not the STANFORD SCHOOL
FOR BOYS.
000
We started out to write "Build thee
more stately mansions" but thought
better of it.
000
Still, every dog has his tax—or
ought to have it.
000
Prometheus is kicking himself loose.
It'll be a hot race between him and
Jupiter to see who gets here first.
They're both betting heavy on the
Cardinal for the Big Day.
000
MOB SCENE IN P. A. The great
crowds that turned out to see the
policeman in town yesterday were
finally dispersed when it was found
that he was enroute to Redwood City,
but had lost his way.
Well, a little wind on the Quad isn't
such a bad thing.
