# Cosmopolitan Club Will Hold Meeting Monday
## 
### 
New members of the Cosmopolitan
Club will be given an opportunity to
become better acquainted at an informal
social hour which will be held in the
Women's Club House Monday evening.
A program lias been arranged, the
special feature of which will be piano
selections to be played by Professor
Henry Lanz of the Russian department.
Refreshments will be served.
