# WOMEN WILL HOLD HI JINX AT CLUB HOUSE WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Real jazz music for dancing iu prom
ised as the feature of the Women's Jinx
which is to lie held in the Women's
Clubhouse Wednesday night. The party
is being given by the Y. W. C. A. and
special permission has been obtained
from the Women's Council to dance
until nine.
This Jinx is being given for the new
women in particular, but all women of
the campus are invited to attend. Sev
eral other forms of entertainment be
sides the dancing is promised by the
committee in charge. The affair will
start promptly at seven-thirty and will
be held upstairs in the clubhouse. The
price of admission is fifteen cents.
