Orchestra and Singers
Assembly Hall
Monday Night, Nov. 24
The American Syncopated Orchestra and singers will give a con
cert, under the local management of Frank W. Healy, at Assembly
Hall, Monday night, November 24, at 8:15 p.m.
Mr. Geo Edmond Dulf, Orchestra leader, conducted the favorite
Over-Seas Black Devils' Band and was first in introducing to the
French people and Paris audiences the popular "American Jazz"
and "Blues."
Mr. Dulf also conducted the last concert given to General Pershing
prior to his departure from Brest, France, for the United States.
Recent programs by this on hestra at Convention Hall in San Fran
cisco and in Greek Theatre, Berkeley, were heard by thousands of
enthusiastic lovers of music.
