# Student Is Struck and Badly Injured by Auto
## 
### 
M. S. Monfort. a special student, was
struck by an Altomont Creamery Com
pany delivery automobile at University
avenue and Ramona street at noon to
day and badly injured. He was rushed
to the Peninsula Hospital, but the seri
ousness of the injuries could not be
stated. Monfort was crossing the street
when the accident occurred.
