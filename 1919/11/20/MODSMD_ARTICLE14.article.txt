# Fesler Sisters Receive Secretarial Positions
## 
### 
Word has just been received on the
campus of the appointment of Ruth
Fesler, *18, and Rachel Fesler, '18,
to secretarial positions in Washing
ton. Ruth Fesler is with the National
Research Council as secretary to the
division of Educational Relations of
which Dr. Vernon Kellogg is chair
man, while Rachel Fesler is secretary
fo Stewart Chase, the Federal Trade
Commissioner.
