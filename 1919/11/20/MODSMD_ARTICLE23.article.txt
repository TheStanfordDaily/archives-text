# Wienie Bake Scheduled For Friday, Nov. 28
## 
### 
A wienie bake for all of the girls who
will remain on the campus over the
Thanksgiving holidays is being planned
for Friday, November 28, and it will be
given under the auspices of the fresh
man cabinet of the Y. W. C. A.
Girls who wish to attend the wienie
bake will meet at the Woman's Club
house at 3 o'clock on the above date.
The plans provide for a hike up into the
hills.
The tickets for wienie bake will be 15
cents, and may be obtained from Marie
Gunther, '23, or Dorothy Wilson, '23,
or at the Clubhouse.
