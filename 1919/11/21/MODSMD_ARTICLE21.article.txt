# Stanford Standards Lecture Postponed
## 
### 
Professor W. M. Proctor's lecture
to the Freshman Stanford Standards
groups which was to have been given
Monday evening, has been postponed
one week, the Y. M. C. A. announced
today. The talk was the last number
on the Stanford Standards program for
the autumn quarter and was to be on
the subject "How to Choose a Life
Work."
