# ALUMNI AT HARVARD WIRE BEST WISHES FOR THE BIG GAME

Harvard University.
Cambridge, Mass.
1919 Varsilyi
Heartiest wishes for a bang-up, dean
victory in the Big Contest. Rank on all
Stanford men at Harvard pulling harder
than proverbial Hell. Remember if yo»
defeat California at American, the prine
of crepe will soar. Tear into 'em and
lick 'em. Here's luck.
"Bill" Sissonl
"Doc" Howakdl
