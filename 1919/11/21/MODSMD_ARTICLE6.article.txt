# Cardinal Rooters Must Be Seated by 2 O'clock
## 
### 
Members of the rootera' Bection
must be seated before 2 o'clock to
morrow afternoon, in order to see the
special air-plane 'stunt flying .exhi
bition which has been arranged, and
to perform some of the bleacher
stunts prepared by the committee in
connection with the new symbol.
The success of the Stanford rooting
section Saturday depends much on the
early appearance of all students, as
the harmony and co-operation of
Cardinal rooters depends on the ex
planations of the stunt leaders.
