# ENCINA DANCES ON DECEMBER 6
## 
###

coaches and trainers arc to be guests
at the Football Dance to be given by
l Encina Club, December 6. This will be
the last dance of the year for the hall
' men, and it promises to be the most at
tractive.
The dance will be exclusively for the
upper-classmcn. Imitations were issued
at a recent meeting of the club.
