# THANKSGIVING DANCE TO BE GIVEN AT ROBLE
## 
### 
A Thanksgiving dance for the enter
tainment of the members of Roble
Club and their friends who are un
able to return home for the holidays,
will be given Friday evening of this
week at Roble Hall. The dance will
be strictly informal and the decora
tions simple, though in keeping with
the Thanksgiving spirit. This is the
third and last Roble informal of the
quarter.
