# "CHOOSING A LIFE WORK" IS SUBJECT OF FRESHMAN TALK
## 
### 
"How to Choose a Life Work" will
be the subject of Professor W. M.
Proctor's lecture to freshmen members
of the Stanford Standards course this
evening, in the Little Theater, at
7:15. This lecture will conclude the
course for the autumn quarter, and
is to be open to any member of the
student body that can attend. Older
students that have not definitely de
cided upon their vocation are especi
ally urged to be present. The talk
is being given under the auspices of
the local Y. M. C. A.
Professor Proctor it as present giv
ing a course in vocational guidance in
the Education department here. He
is also working in conjunction with
the vocational guidance committee at
Stanford.
