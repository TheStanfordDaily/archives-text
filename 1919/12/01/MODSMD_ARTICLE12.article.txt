# CHRISTMAS PROGRAMS WILL START TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at 4:15, Warren
D. Allen, the University organist, will
give his first program which will in
clude any Christmas music. It is the
idea of Mr. Allen to play the Christ
mas music of different countries dur
ing these next few weeks. The music
that is written with the joy of Christ
mas has been taken from such differ
ent parts of the world, expresses so
decidedly the life of the writer that
the concerts will he hoth instructive,
and enjoyable.
