# GAME WITH U.S.C. BABES IS CALLED OFF
## Satisfactory Financial Arrangements Could Not Be Made 
### 
Unable to make satisfactory financi
al arrangements for tliei freshman
U. S. C.-Stanford football game, the
Cardinal babes did not journey down
to Los Angeles to meet the Southern
California eleven.
Coach Acker kept his men in train
ing until last Wednesday night, when
the announcement came that U. S. C.
would not pay half of the traveling
expenses of the Stanford squad. Be
cause of this fact, the game was called
off by Stanford.
