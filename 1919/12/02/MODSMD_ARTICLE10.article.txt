# FIVE NEW MEN ENTER PRESS CLUB TONIGHT
## 
### 
Five new members will be initiated
into the Press Club tonight. Although
the club was forbidden to hold a public
initiation, certain announcements have
been made on the campus to let the
student body know that an initiation
will be held. Those who will become
members of the Press Club arc: L. O.
Weaver, '19; R. A. Donaldson, '17; W.
YV. Gores, '17; G. W. Hall, '19; and R.
W. Kenny. '21.
