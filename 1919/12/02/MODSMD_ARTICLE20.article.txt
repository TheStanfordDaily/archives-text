# Campaign Is Started For Chinese Club House
## 
### 
A campaign for the establishment
of a Chinese Club House on the Stan
ford Campus, has been started by
C. K. Chan, '20, and H. W. Zee, '21.
The campaign was begun at Vallejo,
California, during the Thanksgiving
vacation, and about $500.00 was se
cured. It will be continued during
the Christmas vacation In San Fran
cisco, Oakland, Sacramento, Los An
geles, and Stockton.
Stanford Alumni among the Chinese
have already lent their co-operation
to the cause contrbiuting several
thousand dollars, and the new appeal

to alumni in China and the larger
cities of California is expected to
bring forth the required amount for
its establishment.
