# Y.W.C.A. To Meet Here Next Thursday
## 
### 
Miss Lily Margaret Sherman, Chief
Secretary of the Y. W. C. A. Chapter
of the University of California will
speak on the relation of the Y. W.
C. A. work to the labor problems of
today, Thursday at 5 o'clock.
This is the third and last of the
series of lectures upon the labor ques
tion to be given on the campus. Miss
Elizabeth Patton will lead the meet
ing.
