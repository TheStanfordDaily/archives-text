TUESDAY
Women's Athletic Association —Meets
at 7 o'clock, in the Women's Club
House.
Phi Kappa Psi—15th annual smoker
at 7:30. All freshmen are invited.
Entertainment, eats, and smokes.
Orchestra—Meeting at 7:30 in Band
Room. Be prepared to start work
on "The Messiah."
Schubert Club—Meets from 5 to 6 p.m.
Room 84, Inner Quad.
Cap and Gown—Meets at 4:30. Mrs.
H. J. Ryan, 453 Webster.
WEDNESDAY
Chess Club—At Eneina, at 7:30 p.m.
All interested are welcome.
Classical Club —Meets in the Women's
Club House at 8 o'clock.
Student Volunteer Band—At the Y. M.
C. A., from 5 to 6 o'clock p. m.
Women's Conference—Clubhouse, at
7:15 p.m.
English Club —Initiation meeting at 8
o'clock, at the home of Dr. Alden,
427 Embarcadero.
