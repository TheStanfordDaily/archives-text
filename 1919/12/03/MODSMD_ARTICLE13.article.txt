# DAILY PALO ALTO TO ELECT EDITOR FRIDAY
## 
### 
A special election for the editor of the
Daily Palo Alto on Friday to fill the
vacancy from January 1 to the begin
ning of the next regular election in
February, was authorized by the Execu
tive Committee at its regular meeting
last night. The present editor, N. H.
Petree, '19, will graduate at the end of
this quarter.
The council also refused the request of
the sophomore class to wear dungarees.
The minutes of the meeting follow:
President King in the chair.
M. S. C. —That the sophomores be al
lowed to give a dance after their play
on December 5.
M. S. C. —That the Executive Com
mittee authorize a special election by the
Daily Palo Alto stalT to till the vacancy
occurring between January 1 and the
regular February election.
M. S. C. —That the request of sopho
mores to officially wear dungarees be re
fused.
