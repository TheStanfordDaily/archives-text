# R. Lindley Murry Donates New Tennis Cup for Winner of University Tournament
## 
### 
The "Stanford" cup donated by R.
Lindley Murray, former tennis captain
and national champion in 1918, has ar
rived from the engraver's at Niagara
Falls, and will be placed in the hands of
Paul Kinney, varsity tennis captain.
The trophy will be put up for the win
ner of the big open tournament next
spring, and will be competed for annu
ally until won three times by a registered
undergraduate.
Mr. Murray decided that interest in
tennis at Stanford needed a boost to
come up to the necessary level to win
from the California team and the pre
sentation of his handsome trophy re
sulted.
