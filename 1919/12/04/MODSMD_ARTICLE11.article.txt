# Junior Quad Pictures Should Be Taken Now
## 
### 
All juniors who wish their pictures to
appear in the 1921 Quad have been re
quested by the management to take
photographs at Davies' in Palo Alto
this week or next. Better service is as
sured at the studio to the students who
report this week. Juniors should take
pictures whether dues have been paid
or not.
