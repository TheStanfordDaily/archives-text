# Daily Palo Alto Will Hold Banquet Dec. 11
## 
### 
The quarterly banquet of the Daily
Palo Alto will be held next Thursday
evening, December 11, at the Delta Tau
Delta house. The banquet will take the
form of an initiation for new staff mem
bers. It has been an old custom of
the Daily Palo Alto that the new mem
bers on the staff give a banquet each
quarter.
