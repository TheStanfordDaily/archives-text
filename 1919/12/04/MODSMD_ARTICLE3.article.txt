# Stanford Student Will Talk at High Schools
## 
### 
Earl Smith, '18, will visit three of
the nearby high schools tomorrow to
address the hoy students at special as
semblies on the subject of clean living.
He will be assisted by Ray Brian of the
College of the Pacific.
They will visit Campbell, Los Gatos,
and Mountain View schools. It is now
planned to visit the remaining nearby
high schools during next week. The trip
is under the auspices of the Stanford
Y. M. C. A.
