# Roth Speaks at Last Encina Club Smoker
## 
### 
A. E. Roth, '09, comptroller of the
University, spoke of former days in
Encina's history and of prominent Stan
ford graduates who were Encina men
in their undergraduate days, in the Encina
Club smoker last night. Approximately
three hundred men were present to wit
ness a varied program of athletic
matches and stunts. A clever wrestling
bout and fast boxing matches were
staged.
Roth urged a policy of co-operation
between the Hall men and the comptrol
ler's office, declaring that he welcomed
any suggestions for the improvement of
the condition of the dormitory.
