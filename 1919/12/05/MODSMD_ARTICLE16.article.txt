# Alice Rowell, ex-'l5, Is Married in New York
## 
### 
Alice Rowoll, ex-'l5, was married
\\ ednesday to Captain Theodore Haley
in Xew York. Miss Rowell is a member
of Gamma Phi Beta. Captain Haley,
who has just returned from overseas
duty, is a graduate of the University of
California. He is a member of Delta
Tau Delta. The young couple will re
main in the East for the present.
