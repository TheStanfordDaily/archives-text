# "Pret" Holt Chosen Football Captain for Next Season
## 
### 
A. P. Holt, '21, was elected captain
of next year's football team, at a
meeting of the varsity Sunday morn
ing after the Quad pictures had been
taken.
Holt played left half this season and
was picked for that position on the
all-Pacific Coast team chosen by the
Pacific Coast Conference Officials. He

was captain of the S. A. T .C. team
and gained his first football knowledge
in Portland, Ore. He is a member of
Chi Psi fraternity.
