# COL. FALLS INSPECTS STANFORD R.0.T.C.
## 
### 
Colonel Moore N. Falls, district mili
tary inspector for the R. O. T. C., is
on the campus today for the purpose of
inspecting the Stanford R. O. T. C.
units.
The infantry unit was inspected this
afternoon at 4:15. Colonel Falls, in
spector for all the R. O. T. C. units in
this district, has San Francisco for his
headquarters.
