# HELLER BELIEVED SAFE IN AFRICA
## 
### 
Friends of Edmund Holler, Stanford
graduate, member of the American ex
pedition in Africa, believe that he is
safe and was not included among those
killed in the wreck in Belgian Congo
reported in the papers yesterday, but was
in another division of the expedition.

Stanford, and was a member of the
