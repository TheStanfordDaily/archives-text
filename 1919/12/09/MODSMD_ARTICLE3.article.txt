# End-Quarter Exams Are Announced by Registrar
## 
### 
The Registrar today announced that
the final examinations will be held
from the 17th to the 19th of this
month. The schedule announced for
the examinations follows:
Wednesday, December 17 —8 o'clock
classes, from 8 to 12; 11 o'clock class
es, from 2 to 6.
Thursday, December 18 —9 o'clock
classes from 8 to 12; 1 o'clock class
es, from 2 to 6.
Friday, December 19 —10 o'clock
classes, from 8 to 12, 2 o'clock class
es, froui 2 to 6.
