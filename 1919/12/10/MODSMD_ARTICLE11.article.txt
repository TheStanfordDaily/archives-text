# WOMEN WILL FILL XMAS STOCKINGS
## 
### 
Christmas stockings for the Lane
Clinic children will be distributed this
evening to each living campus group,
and each woman will be asked to buy
a pair and fill one.
The stockings arc to be filled and
turned into the Women's Club House
not later than Saturday morning. Mrs.
Perec Mitchell is in charge of the an
nual Stanford Clinic Christmas party
which will be held Saturday afternoon
and the stockings must be in then.
