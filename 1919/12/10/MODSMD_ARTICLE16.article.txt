# Spanish Club Will Give "EI Flechazo" Thursday
## 
### 
"El Flechazo," a short Spanish dia
logue, will be given at a meeting of the
Spanish Club in the Women's Club
House Thursday night at 8 o'clock. The
parts in the dialogue will be taken by
Dorothy Elfving, '21, and Rauol
May, '22.
This is the first meeting of the year
of the re-organized Spanish Club, and
all students interested arc urged to at
tend.
