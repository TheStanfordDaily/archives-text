# NESTORIA WILL ELECT OFFICERS AT BANQUET
## 
### 
A banquet will he held this evening
at the Stanford Inn by the members of
Nestoria Debating Society, at which the
society's plans for the next quarter will
be discussed and the question of the
adoption of a new constitution for the
club will be considered. Election of
officers for the ensuing quarter will be
held. Newcomb Condee, '19, president
of the society, will preside.
All active as well as inactive members
of the organization are urged to be
present this evening at 6:30.
