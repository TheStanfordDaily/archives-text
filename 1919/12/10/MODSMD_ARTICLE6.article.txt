# William A. White Will Give Two Lectures Here
## 
### 
William Allen White, editor of the
Emporia Gazette, lectures tomorrow
night at 7:15 in the Assembly Hall
on "A Reporter at the Peace Confer
ence." Mr. White, the author of sev
eral popular novels, is one of the
country's foremost journalists as well.
He was present at the Peace Con
ference so he speaks on first-hand in
formation.
He will also speak in the Assembly
Hall, Friday afternoon, at 4:15, his
subject being. "The Recent Industrial
Conference at Washington, D. C."
