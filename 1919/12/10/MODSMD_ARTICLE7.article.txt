# Miss Ruth Taylor, ex-'19, Announces Engagement
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Ruth Taylor,
ex.-'l 9, to Leonard White, '20, of Cali
fornia, will take place during the Christ
mas holidays in Chicago. At present
Miss Taylor is completing a course of
art in the New York Institute of Art.
While on the campus, Miss Taylor
was prominent in journalistic work, be
ing a former editor of the Daily Palo
Alto and an honorary member of Ham
mer and Coffin. She is a member of
Delta Delta Delta.
