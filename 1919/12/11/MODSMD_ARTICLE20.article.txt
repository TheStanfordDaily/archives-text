# HAMMER AND COFFIN INITIATES FOUR MEN
## 
### 
Hammer and Coffin, honor society
for members of the Chaparral staff,
initiated four new members Tuesday
night. The neophytes are Leon Sloss,
'20, W. G. Hall, '19, W. K. Gilbert,
'20, and T. E. Green, '22.
The customary race on hands and
knees from the engineering corner to
the post office was not conducted be
cause of the ruling against public in
itiations.
