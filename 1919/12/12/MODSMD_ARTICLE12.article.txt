# U. OF MOUNT EAGLE TO MEET STANFORD IN SOCCER GAME
## 
### 
The Stanford Varsity soccer team will
play the University of Mount Eagle
eleven tomorrow afternoon at 3 p. m.
on the Stanford field. The Cardinal
soccerites beat this team 4-0 at Croll
earlier in the season.
The game is on the schedule of the
University and Club Soccer League, and
Coach Maloney will use several players
who were ineligible against California
because of intercollegiate rulings.
The following line-up has been an
nounced : Forwards, Green, Holt, New
land, Strauss, and Gardner: half-backs,
Skene, Quinby, and Robinson: fullbacks,
W. Adams and Mendosa; goal, Hood.
