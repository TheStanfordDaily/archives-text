# ECONOMIC STUDENTS ENROLL DEC. 15, 16, 17
## 
### 
Economic majors planning courses
for the winter quarters may register
on December 15th, IG, and 17th, ac
cording to a statement issued by Dr.
M. S. Wildman, head of the Economics
department. This advance registra
tion arrangement has been planned so
to avoid the usual confusion in regis
tration in the Economics courses.
On Monday the freshmen are ad
vised to rail at Room 223; on Thurs
day, the sophomores; on Wednesday,
the juniors and on Thursday, the seni
ors. The freshmen especially are
urged to confer with Dr. Wildman and
arrange Economic courses before the
holidays.
