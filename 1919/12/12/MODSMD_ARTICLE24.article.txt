# EARL SMITH HEADS STUDENT DELEGATION
## 
### 
Earl Smith, '17, was elected head of
the Stanford degelates to the Student
Volunteer Convention at Des Moines,
December 31 to January 4, at a meet
ing of the Cardinal representatives
yesterday. It was also decided that
the Stanford delegation of twenty
five Y. M. C. A. and Y. W. C. A. work
ers would leave for the east on De
cember 27.
