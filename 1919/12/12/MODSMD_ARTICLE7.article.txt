# Goodman Is Elected Euphronia President
## 
### 
D. L. Goodman, '22. was elected
president of Euphronia at a meeting
of the club held last evening. Goodman
was a representative on the team that
debated against California a few
weeks ago. C. G. Smith, '22, was select
ed vice-president. The office of secre
tary-treasurer is to be filled by G. W.
Burch, '22.
Plans for the annual debate between
Nestoria and Euphronia were made at
this time.
