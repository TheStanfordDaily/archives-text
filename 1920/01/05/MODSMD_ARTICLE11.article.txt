# HOOVER RETURNS TO WORK IN WASHINGTON
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. Herbert Hoover and
son Allen returned to Washington this
morning after spending the holidays at
home. Hoover is at present serving as
the vice chairman of the Industrial Rela
tions Committee appointed by the Presi
dent to investigate labor conditions and
strikes in the United States in order to
obtain some solution to the industrial
question. Hoover is also connected with
several cnarity organizations, whose
headquarters arc at Washington.
