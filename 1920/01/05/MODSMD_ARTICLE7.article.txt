# Chinese Educators Tour On Stanford Campus
## 
### 
A committee composed of twelve
prominent Chinese educators began a
six-months' tour of the United States by
inspecting Stanford's educational system
durnig the vacation. The commission
was appointed by the president of the
Chinese Republic to examine the various
educational methods of America with
the purpose of introducing the best ideas
of tliis country into the Chinese system.
President Wilbur welcomed the Chi
nese. following their tour of the campus
grounds. Professor E. P. Cubberley
and Professor L. M. Terman. of the
Education Department, explained in sev
eral addresses to the committee the edu
cational methods followed at Stanford.
