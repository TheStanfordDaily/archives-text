# Club Will Discuss Treaty Referendum Propositions Tonight
## 
### 
An open discussion of the six propo
sitions which constitute the subject of
the referendum to be held under the
direction of the Daily Palo Alto next
Tuesday, will be held by the members
of Nestoria at their first meeting of the
quarter in room 12 of the Law building
tonight. Plans for the coming quarter
and definite arrangements for the annual
debate between Euphronia and Nestoria
will be made at this time. J. T. Mc-
Menamin, '20, president of the society,
urges that all those interested in the de
flating work be present tonight.
