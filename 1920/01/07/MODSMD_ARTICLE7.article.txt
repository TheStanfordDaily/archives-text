# Prominent Graduates Announce Engagement
## 
### 
The engagement of two of Stanford's
most prominent recent graduates, Fred
erick S. Murray, '16, and Francis K.
Murray, '17, was announced yesterday
in New York City. Frederick Murray
is engaged to Miss Dorothy Hanna, '19,
who is now attending Columbia Univer
sity. Miss Hanna is a Kappa Kappa
Gamma and while at Stanford was a
member of the Women's Council and the
Women's Athletic Association. Miss
Dorothy Maitland Lee of New York is
the fiancee of Francis Murray. Miss
Lee, a former Hryn Mamr student, is
now attending Columbia University.
