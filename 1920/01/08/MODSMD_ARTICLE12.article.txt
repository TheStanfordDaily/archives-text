# TWO MEN NOMINATED FOR STUDENT COUNCIL
## 
### 
H. G. Swendsen, '20, and H. F. Star
key, '19, were nominated at a meeting
of the senior class today, as the two
candidates for the vacancies on the
Men's Student Council.
According to the recently adopted
constitution for student control, the
senior class nominates the candidates for
the election to the council. The elec
tion will probably H held at a Quad
election next week.
