# Professor G.C. Bassett Lectures in Palo Alto
## 
### 
Professor G. C. Basset, of the Stan
ford psychology Department, addressed
the Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce
luncheon meeting in the Palo Alto Hotel
today on the subject of "Hygiene and
Americanization."
Professor Bassett described his work
in the army briefly, and told of the work
of psychologists in determining the in
dividual fitness of persons for tasks and
of adjusting tasks to suit individuals.
