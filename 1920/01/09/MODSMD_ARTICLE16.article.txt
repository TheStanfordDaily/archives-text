# DR. E.W. SCHULTZ IS NEW INSTRUCTOR
## 
### 
Among the new Instructors at Stan
ford for the winter quarter is Dr. Ed
win W. Schultz, of Johns Hopkins
Medical School. He will take up
work here as instructor in Experi
mental Pathology.
During the war. Dr. Schultz was a
major in the American medical ser
vice. He was chief of the pathological
laboratory of a base hospital near
Paris. On the signing of the armistice
he was sent to Poland as Director of
Medical Research in that district. His
work in Poland was directed toward
the prevention of and control of the
typhus fever.
