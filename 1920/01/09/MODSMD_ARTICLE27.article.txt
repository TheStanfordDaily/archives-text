# THETA SIGMA PHI WILL HOLD BANQUET
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, the women's
honorary journalistic fraternity, will
hold a meeting Sunday evening at 6
p.m. at the Kappa Kappa Gamma
house. Professor Everett Smith of
the journalistic department will give
a discussion of journalisstic problems
at Stanford. Professor and Mrs. Smith
will be the guests of honor of the even
ing.
