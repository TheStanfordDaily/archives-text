# TUESDAY IS DATE FOR RAM'S HEAD TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Ram's Head Society has announced
that tryouts will be held at the Kappa
Alpha House next Tuesday afternoon
between 3 and 5 for their annual show.
Tryouts will be open to every man
student in the University, and as many
freshmen as possible arc urged to come
out. A large cast and chorus will be
used, so there will be an opportunity for
all dramatic talent to get a part in the
production.
The Ram's Head show is one of the
big dramatic events of the college year,
and according to present indications the
society is going to have the usual suc
cessful stage setting. It is planned to
import the scenery from New York.
The play is being written by members
of the organization, and will be complet
ed this week.
