# Campus Residents Will Hear Mrs. Robert Dean
## 
### 
Mrs. Robert Dean, chairman of the
San Francisco Housewives' League, will
address residents of the campus who are
organizing to combat the high cost of
living tomorrow afternoon in the Wom
en's Clubhouse, at 2 o'clock.
Although the meeting is primarily for
residents of the campus, all who wish
to come arc cordially invited.
