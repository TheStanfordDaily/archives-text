DETAILS OF THE D. P. A.
STRAW VOTE ON THE
PEACE TREATY
BALLOTS will be passed out
and collected by the instructors
in the 9 and 10 o'clock classes.
A VOTING BOOTH will be
maintained in the Library from
10 to 11, for all those who were
unable to vote in class.
STUDENTS will vote on the
BLUE ballots.
FACULTY MEMBERS will
vote on WHITE ballots.
D. P. A. representatives will
collect the votes from the In
structors during the 11 o'clock
period.
Results of the local balloting
will appear in Tuesday night's
edition. Totals from the entire
Inter-collegiate Treaty Refer
endum will be in by the end of
the week.
