# Water Colors Will Be Shown at Art Gallery
## 
### 
A collection of 105 water-color paint
ings will be exhibited at the Thomas
Welton Stanford Art Gallery* beginning
January 16 and ending February 12. This
is the first group of paintings which will
be exhibited at Stanford and in other
places in the west, by the arrangement
of Pedro J. Lcmos, curator and rep
resentative of the American Federation
of Arts.
An invitational opening of the exhibit
will be held for members of the Stan
ford faculty and members of the press
Thursday evening.
