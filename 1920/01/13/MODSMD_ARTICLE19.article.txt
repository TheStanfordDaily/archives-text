# THREE ARE ELECTED TO THETA SIGMA PHI
## 
### 
Three new members were elected to
Tlieta Sigma Phi, women's honorary
journalistic fraternity, at a meeting
of that organization Sunday evening.
Those elected were Dorothy Hoskins,
Bernice Frankenheimer, '20, and
Kirby Ingoldsby, '21.
Miss Hoskins has ben a contributor
to the Cardinal and has accomplished
much along journalistic lines in other
than campus publications. She is a
member of Sigma Kappa.
Miss Frankenheimer has been a
member of the Daily Palo Alto staff
for the past year and a half. Miss
Ingoldsby was the author of last year's
Junior Opero, and is a member of the
present yar's Quad staff. Both are
members of Roble Club.
