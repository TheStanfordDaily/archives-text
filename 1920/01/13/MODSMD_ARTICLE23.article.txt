# GLEE CLUB WILL HOLD TRYOUT FOR TENORS
## 
### 
There are several openings for first
or second tenors in the men's Glee
Club, according to the announcement
given out by President Luhrs last
night. Those wishing to try out should
report to professor Allen in room 84,
Wednesday afternoon, at 4:15 o'clock.
Arrangements have been completed
for the Glee Club trip throughout the
southern part of the state during the
spring vacation. It is planned to
motor down the coast route and re
turn by way of the valley. Tryouts
of the stunts for the concert will be
held in a few weeks.
