# Resident Alumnae Tea Will be Held Tomorrow
## 
### 
The first of the series of Resident
Alumnae Teas which are being given
for the benefit of the Woman's Club
house will be held in the clubhouse to
morrow afternoon. The outside sup
port of the faculty and friends of the
University is urgently desired, and all
women registered in the University are
also invited to attend. The hostesses
for the tea to-morrow afternoon will be
Mrs. R. L. Wilbur, Mrs. E. C. Cubberly,
and Mrs. E. D. Adams.
