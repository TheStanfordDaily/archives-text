# STANFORD ALUMNA WILL TALK ON RED CROSS EXPERIENCES
## 
### 
Dr. Virginia Murray, '14, will give
an address on war experiences in Red
Cross work tomorrow evening at 8:30 in
the Little Theatre. During the war she
was engaged in American Red Cross
work in France, Poland, and along the
Russo-German front.
Dr. Murray expects to return to Eu
rope in the near future under the
auspices of the Junior Red Cross. She
will carry on investigations from Bel
gium to the Balkans and in addition or
ganize further Red Cross work for chil
dren in Poland.
Dr. Murray was a member of Kappa
Alpha Theta.
