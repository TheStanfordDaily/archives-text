# Dr. Murray Will Relate Her Red Cross War Work
## 
### 
"War Experiences in Red Cross
Work," will be the subject of an ad
dress which will be given by Dr. Vir
ginia Murray, '14, this evening at 7:30
in the Little Theater.
Dr. Murray was active in American
Red Cross in France, Poland, and on
the Russo-German front during the
war. In a very short time she ex
pects to return overseas in the inter
est of the Junior Red Cross. In ad
dition to carrying on investigations
from Belgium to the Balkans, Dr.
Murray will organize Red Cross work
for children in Poland. Thousands of
children will be under her direction
while she is engaged in this new field,
field.
