# One Unit Will Be Given For Orchestra
## 
### 
One unit of credit will be given stu
dents enrolled in the Stanford Orches
tra on the established basis of three
hours of work per week, most of which
will be done at regular practices every
Wednesday night at 7 :30. Students who
play any stringed, wood-wined or brass
instruments not as yet enrolled in the
orchestra may try out tonight at the
first regular practice of the quarter to
be held in the organ loft of the Me
morial Church. Warren D. Allen will
have charge of the work this quarter.
