# Junipero Serra Club Will Give Dance Friday
## 
### 
The second campus dance of the
quarter will take place on Friday night,
January 16, in the Women's Clubhouse,
and will be Riven under the auspices
of the Junipero Serra Club.
Louis Lissner, '20, will have charge
of the music for the evening and will
begin the first dance promptly at 8:00
p.m. An admission of one dollar a
couple will be charged.
