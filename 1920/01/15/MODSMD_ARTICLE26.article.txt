# UPPER-CLASSMEN GIVE JOLLY-UP SATURDAY
## 
### 
Upperclass men and any of the "500"
are eligible to attend the upperclass jolly
up Saturday nitrlit at the Women's Club
house. according to an announcement
of the committee in charge. The affair
is given by the combined upper classes,
and will be an old-time jolly-up with
cords and flannel shirts in style.
The admission will be 55 cents.
