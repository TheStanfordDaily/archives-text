# Minister-Soldier Will Be Heard Here Sunday
## 
### 
A military man and minister in the
person of Rev. Henry R. Sanborn of
Sparks, Nevada, will be the preacher
at the Memorial Church next Sunday.
Rev. Sanborn is a graduate of Wash
ington University with a B. S. in
English. He was adjutant to the chief
of staff of the lGth Brigade, 8t,h Divi
sion, at Camp Fremont. He also serv
ed with the British forces and now is
a colonel of infantry in the Reserve
Corps and is in the ministry, which
he entered after the war.
