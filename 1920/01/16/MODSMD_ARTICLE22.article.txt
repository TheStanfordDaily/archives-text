# Initial Games Played Of Intra-Mural League
## 
### 
Two games of intra-mural basketball
were played yesterday afternoon, be
ginning a schedule which will continue
until the champion squad of forty-eight
teams is determined. The Beta Theta
Pi's took the bigger end of 13 to 3
score in their match with the Kappa
Alphas. El Camino Club, in an even
game with the Beta Theta Pis, lost by
score of 14 to 13. The remaining teams
of the league play their initial games of
the schedule Monday afternoon.
