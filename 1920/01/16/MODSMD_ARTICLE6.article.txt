# SCHUBERT CLUB WILL PRODUCE OPERETTA
## 
### 
The operetta, the "Plying Dutch
man" by Wagner, will be staged by
Schubert Club under the direction of
W. .H. Allen, some time in the near
future. Rehearsals have already be
gun, and Mr. Allen urges that all
members attend regularly the weekly
meetings of the club. If it is suc
cessful on the campus the opera will
be staged in San Jose.
