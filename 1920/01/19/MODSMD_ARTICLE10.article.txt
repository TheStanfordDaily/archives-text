# R.A. COOKSEY DIES IN ISOLATION HOSPITAL
## 
### 
Handle A. Cooksey, *23, unexpected
ly passed away late Saturday evening
in the Isolation Hospital after an ill
ness of ten days. Cooksey was stricken
with spinal meningitis shortly after
registering for the winter quarter, be
ing removed to the hospital a week
ago, Wednesday. Though only at Stan
ford a short time, he showed marked
ability as an athlete, being the captain
!of the freshman wrestling team.
Cooksey majored in Economics and
was a member of Encina Club. His
father is Dr. J. B. Cooksey of Corsi
' e.ana, Texas, where the funeral will
be held the latter part of this week.
