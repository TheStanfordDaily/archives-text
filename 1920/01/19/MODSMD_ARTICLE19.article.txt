# Palo Altan Is Author Of "Mitzi" Comedy Plot
## 
### 
Mrs. Nalbro Hartley, of Palo Alto,
is the author of the novelette on which
the play "Head over Heels," opening
with Mitzi at the Columbia Theater this
week in San Francisco is based. Miss
Hartley's story was entitled "Shadows."
and appeared in Smith's Magazine sev
eral vears ago.
