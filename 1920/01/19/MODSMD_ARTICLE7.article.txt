# Improvements Made On West Side of Encina
## 
### 
Needed improvements have at last
been made on the west side of Encina
Ilall. During the December rains, stu
dents entering the hall through the
basement found difficulty in getting
around the large pools of water.
Workmen yesterday finished two new
walks leading to the basement ent
rance.
