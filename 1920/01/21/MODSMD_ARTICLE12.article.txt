# ENGINEERS WILL HEAR ILLUSTRATED LECTURE
## 
### 
Interesting features incidental to the
largest railroad electrification scheme yet
attempted, will be shown to engineering
students who attend the lecture in the
Little Theater tomorrow afternoon at
4:15.
The meeting has been arranged thru
the co-operation of the mechanical and
electrical engineering societies, who have
secured William Thompson, an expert
in railroad work, to give the lecture.
