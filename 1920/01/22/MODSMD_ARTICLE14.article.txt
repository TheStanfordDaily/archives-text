# THETA SIGMA PHI INITIATES FOUR
## 
### 
Mrs. Nalbro Bartley, prominent
novelist and sliort-story writer, was
initiated as an honorary member into
Theta Sigma Phi, the women's honor
ary journalistic fraternity at 5 o'clock
this afternoon in the Women's Club
House. Other member initiated at to
day's ceremony were Miss Dorothy
Hoskins, '21; Miss Bernice Franken
heimer, '20; and Miss Kirbv Ingolds
by, '21.
A banquet in the honor of the in
itiates will take place at Roble Hall,
