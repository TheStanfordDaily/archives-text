# Professor E.W. Smith Will Write For Palo Alto Times
## 
### 
Professor Everett W. Smith, had of
the Journalism department of Stan
ford University has joined the staff of
the Palo Alto Daily Times as Associate
Editor, according to an announcement
yesterday. Mr. Smith states that he
will write for the Times on local sub
jects. as an individual, and not as an
official of the University. He will,
however, present University affairs
before the whole community so that
they may be intelligently understood.
