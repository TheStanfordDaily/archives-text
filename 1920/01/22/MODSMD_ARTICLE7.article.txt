# Chemistry Fraternity Is Preparing War Memorial
## 
### 
Phi Lambda Upsilon is making a
collection of kodak pictures of all men
in the Chemistry department who were
in the war service. When completed,
the collection will be turned over to
the department as a war memorial
souvenir. Anyone who has pictures
to donate may give them to S. Schein,
gr.; N. W. Rakestraw, gr.; or the de
partment secretary, Miss Houlware.
