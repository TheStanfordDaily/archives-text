# Y.M.C.A. CAMPAIGNS TO BE LAUNCHED SOON
## 
### 
Initial plans were laid for two joint
Y. M. C. A. campaigns to be launched
on February first at a meeting of the
Y. M. C. A. Cabinet and workers, held
at the "Y" office yesterday evening.
One campaign will be launched in the
interest of the local work and another
for the support of the Lockwood Cam
paign which is being carried on in
China. The Lockwood Campaign in
China is a missionary campaign being
carried on in Canton under the secre
taryship of E. H. Lockwood, a former
Stanford Y. M. C. A. worker.
