# Plans For Bible Class Announced Next Sunday
## 
### 
A University Bible Class was organ
ized last Sunday morning in the Chapel
by the election of Ray Grounds '20 as
president of the group, and the appoint
ment of class committees. The class has
been formed for non-sectarian religious
discussion and will be 'ead by Professor
O. M. Johnston. The group will meet
at 10 a. m. every Sunday morning in
the west balcony of the Memorial
Church. All students interested in Bible
study are requested to attend the meet
ing next Sunday when the purposes of
the organization will be fully oUtlined.
