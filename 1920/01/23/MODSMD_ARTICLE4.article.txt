# JORDAN WILL DELIVER ADDRESS ON LEAGUE
## 
### 
Dr. David Starr Jordan will deliver
an address on the League of Nations
before an open meeting of Nestoria
Debating Society, Monday evening, in
the Little Theater.
Dr. Jordan is one of the foremost
authorities in America on the League
and has been heard many times before
by Stanford audiences in his appeals
for its ratification. The results of the
recent college referendum on the mat
ter will also be touched upon by him.
All students and members of the
faculty interested in the vital question
of the League and its future are in
vited to attend the lecture. It will
begin at 7:30 p.m.
