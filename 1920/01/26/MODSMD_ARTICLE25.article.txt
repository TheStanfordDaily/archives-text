# Native of India Will Speak Here on Y.M.C.A. Work
## 
### 
Bepin Chandra Sircar, a native of In
dia and one of the leading Indian exan
gelists in missionary work today, will
address the cabinet and workers of the
Stanford Y. M. C. A. in a lecture to be
held at the Little Theater on Wednesday
at 5 p. m.
Sircar will discuss not only the Y. M.
C. A. work in his native land, but will
also tell of the social and political con
dition of the country.
Due to the popularity of Sircar the
"Y" office has announced that students
and the general public will be permitted
to attend the coming lecture.
