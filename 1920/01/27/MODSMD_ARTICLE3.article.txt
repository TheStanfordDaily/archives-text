# Amateur Footpad Robs New Queener at Roble Bridge
## 
### 
F. L. Mendle '20 was held up and
robbed by a footpad while walking in
company with a co-ed in the vicinity of
Roble Bridge Sunday evening.
After covering him with a flashlight
and revolver the robber demanded Men
die's purse, which he received, and there
upon disappeared in the direction of
Palo Alto.
The highwayman i.<. believed to have
been an amateur, as no stickpin or watch
was requested.
