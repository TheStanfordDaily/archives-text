# ALUMNI DIRECTORY IS NOW BEING COMPILED
## 
### 
An alumni directory of all graduates
and all students who have attended Stan
ford at anj' time is being compiled by
the administration office, and will be
published in the near future. The book
will contain the alphabetical names of
all students formerly associated with
Stanford, and will give their geographi
cal location, present occupation, and ad
dresses as far as the information can
be obtained. President Wilbur has ex
pressed his desire that the book include
as many of the 25,000 ex-students and
graduates as it is possible to locate. Any
assistance or information concerning for
mer Stanford people will be greatly ap
preciated by the alumni secretary, J. E.
McDowell, at the Dean of Men's office.
