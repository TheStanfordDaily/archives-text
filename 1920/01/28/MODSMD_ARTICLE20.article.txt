pictures for the 1921 Quad should be
taken according to schedule arranged
with Photographer Davey. the editor of
the yearbook stated this morning.

"All groups must comply strictly with
this schedule and make arrangements to
sit on date specified. Change of date
may be made by special arrangement
with Mr. Davey.

where you will be sure to remember the
date."
