# ENCINA AND ALPHA SIGMA PHI WIN IN INTRA-MURAL GAMES
## 
### 
Two games played yesterday after
noon of intramural basketball furnished
the closest competition so far witnessed
in the league contest. By a one-point
lead, Alpha Sigma Phi defeated Theta
Delta Chi by a score of 12 to 11. En
cina East Wing Fourth Floor was shut
out by Sigma Nu by a score of 9 to 8.
The Japanese Club offered little opposi
tion to Encina West Wing Fourth Floor,
the latter running their score up to 29
and holding their opponents down to
two field goals.
