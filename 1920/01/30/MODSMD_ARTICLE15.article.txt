# Student Body Dance To Follow Basketball Game
## 
### 
The first student body dance of the
quarter will be held this evening at
Encina Hall. Arrangements have
been made to secure a special jazz
orchestra under the direction of A. W
Whitmer, '21. Members of the Wash
ington State and Stanford varsity
basketball teams will be entertained
as guests of honor at the dance, which
will follow the basketball game in
Encina Gymnasium. An admission
price of seventy-flve cents will be
charged.
