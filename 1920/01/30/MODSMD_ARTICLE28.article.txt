# Ouija Causes Students to Leave Michigan University
## 
### 
A Onija board scare, the statement
that students were leaving the Uni
versity of Michigan Monday, January
12, on account of nervousness caused
by reading unhappy fortunes on the
magic boards, was denied by officials
of that institution after two local
papers had printed the information.
Dr. W. E. Forsythe, head of the Uni
versity Health Service, denied that
any definite statement in this respect
was made to him.
