# Gardiner Award is Given Roble Girl
## Miss Ruth Nethercutt, '19, Will Receive New Scholarship 
### 
The Gertrude Gardiner Scholarship,
established this year, has been awarded
to Miss Ruth Nethercutt, '19, beginning
with the spring quarter this year. Miss
Nethercutt is not in residence at the
University this quarter, but will return
in the spring. She is a graduate student
in bacteriology.
The scholarship was founded by Miss
Gertrude Gardiner, who has for a num
ber of years been matron of Roble Hall,
and pays $200 a year to a non-sorority
student who has been for two years a
resident of the University, and who is
also a resident of Roble Hall.
