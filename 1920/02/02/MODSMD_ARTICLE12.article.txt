# APRIL 24 IS DATE OF CALIFORNIA MEET
## 
### 
The Stanford-California track meet
will take place on April 24 at Berkeley,
instead of May 1, as previously an
nounced ; and the baseball series will be
played on April 17, 24, and 28, the first
and last games to be played at Berkeley.
This change in dates has been made be
cause of the fact that final examinations
will start at the University of California
on May 1.
