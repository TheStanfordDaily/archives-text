# Y.W.C.A. CAMPAIGN FOR $1000 BEGINS ON CAMPUS TODAY
## 
### 
Today, Monday, a campaign to collect
$1,000 from among the women and fac
ulty of the University, will be started
by the local chapter of the Y. W. C. A.
This drive is but a part of the one being
conducted by the national Y. W, C. A.,
to help raise funds necessary to carry
on the work of the institution.
The results of the campaign will be
given out by houses. In Roble the totals
will be given by classes. The campaign
will last for three days.
