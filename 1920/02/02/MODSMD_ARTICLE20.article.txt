# Tully C. Knoles Will Speak Here Tomorrow
## 
### 
Dr. Tully C. Knoles. president of the
College of the Pacific, will give an ad
dress tomorrow evening in the Little
Theater at 7:30. "The Ultimate Test of
Democracy" will be the subject of the
talk by Dr. Knoles.
Dr. Knoles has been president of the
College of the Pacific for a year. Pre
vious to that time he was head of the
History Department of the University of
Southern California, of which he is an
alumnus.
