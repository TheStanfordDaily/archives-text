# "Problems of India" Will Be Subject of Lecture
## 
### 
It has been announced by Miss
Frances Patterson, chairman of the
lecturer committee, that Professor
Payson Treat of Stanford University
will give a talk on "Some Problems of
India" at the Community House in
Palo Alto, Thursday evening, Febru
ary 5, at 8 o'clock. All are cordially
invited to attend.
