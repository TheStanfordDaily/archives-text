# ENGLISH DEPARTMENT WILL GIVE READINGS
## 
### 
Professor W. D. Briggs of the Eng
lish department will read selections
from Sohrab and llustum Wednesday
afternoon, at 4:15, in room 42, Edu
cation Building. This will be the first
of a series of informal readings which
will be conducted under the direction
of the English department. The plan
followed will be similar to that of last
quarter, the readings being given by
members of different departments of
the University, each reader selecting
a subject of interest to him. The in
formal character of the reading hours
will be emphasized and the English
department is anxious for students in
the department as well as all others
interested to attend the readings,
which are open both to the campus
and to the Palo Alto public.
