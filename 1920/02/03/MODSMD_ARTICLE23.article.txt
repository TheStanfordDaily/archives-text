# QUAD "RAZZ CUPS" NOW ON EXHIBITION
## 
### 
Two silver cups, to be given by the
1921 Quad for the best contributions
to its josh section, were placed on ex
hibition at the Bookstore last night.
One cup will be persented to the stu
dent who provides for the yearbook the
best "Razz" on any campus group or
citizen. The fraternity or living organ
ization contributing material of the same
type will be awarded the other cup.
The unknown editor of the Quad's
"Razz" section states that a number of
contributions concerning well-known
groups have already been received.
The contest will probably remain open
for two months.
