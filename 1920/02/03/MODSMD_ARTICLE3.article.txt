# Yale University Will Give Two Fellowships
## 
### 
Word has been received that Yale
University will offer two scholarships
of one thousand dollars each, known
as the Sessel Fellowship, for original
research in Biological Studies at Yale
University. In the award preference
will be given to those who have al
ready received their Doctorate. Ap
plicants should be able to conduct
original research in Physiology, Zool
ogy, and Rotany.
Applications for these Fellowships
should be made to the Dean of the
Graduate School, Yale University, New
Haven, Connecticut, before May 1.
Further details of the scholarship
may be had at the Dean of Men's of
fice.
