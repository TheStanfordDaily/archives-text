# Skull and Snakes Elect Nineteen to Membership
## 
### 
Skull and Snakes honor society has
elected nineteen men to membership in
the society, according to a list announced
today.
The men who have been chosen are:
F. L. Adams, '21; M. D. Flowers, '17;
Paul Campbell, '18; H. F. Campbell, '21;
J. C. Patrick, '21; D. L. Levy, '18; R.
S. Pershing, '21; F. L. King, '19; W.
B. Wells, '20; A. P. Holt, '21; A. F.
Wilcox, '19; H. P. McGilvary, '20; A.
G. Westwick, '19; O. S. Falk. '21; F. L.
Bonney, '18; F. L. Kallam, '21; W. M.
Stamp, '20; B. C. Wohlford. '18; M. E.
Kirksey, '19.
