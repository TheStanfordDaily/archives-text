# IOTA SIGMA PHI INITIATES THREE
## 
### 
Four new members were initiated into
lota Sigma Pi, the women's honorary
chemistry society, at a meeting of the
organization held last evening at the
Kappa Alpha Tlieta house. The initiates
are Adelaide Jameson, '21, Esther Clark,
'22, Gretchen Haufman, '20, and Helen
Douglas, '19.
Before the initiation, the women of
the Chemistry Department entertained
members of the faculty of the depart
ment at Roblc. Hall. An informal hour
followed the initiation service, at which
tlie chemistry faculty and instructors of
the department were present.
