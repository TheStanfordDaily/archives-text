# CITY TEAM WILL PLAY SOCCER MEN
## 
### 
Because of inability on the part of the
National Carbon Company to play its
postponed match with the University
soccerites next Saturday, arrangements
are under way with the secretary of the
soccer league in San Francisco to have
a team chosen from various city organ
izations come here for the Saturday
contest.
Cardinal soccerites will line up for the
game with the team which Coach Quin
by plans to use against California in the
final game.
