### 
Moving picture films for the R. O.
T. C. classes will be shown at the

February 10, at 4:15, Instead of Thurs
day, the 12th, the day originally set

lustrate the work of the "School of
the Company," the use of artillery
guns, and modern methods of equip
ping the army with shoes. Captain

the pictures will be given, invites any
one outside the military classes who
