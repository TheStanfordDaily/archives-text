# MEMBERS OF ENCINA ELECT OFFICERS
## 
### 
Encina Hall unanimously elected Wal
ter I. Ames, '18, president of Encina
Club at a meeting last night.

W. G. Hall. '19, H. F. Campbell, '21,
B. A. Cody, '21, and H. F. Gould, '21,
were elected to the House Committee

posed of E. W. Hadley, '19, J. A. Stur
geon, '19, W. F. Leiser, '21, F. L. Mc-
Elroy, '21, R. C. Connolly, '22, A. A.
Carry, '21, R. A. Papazian. '23, and J.
G. Douglas, '23.
