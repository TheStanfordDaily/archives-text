# Roble Club Will Give First Dance Tomorrow
## 
### 
Roble Club will give its first dance this
quarter tomorrow night. The dance will
take the form of a Valentine party.
The Claremont Hotel orchestra lias been
obtained to furnish the dance music.
Miss Harriet Bradford, Mrs. P. Rock
well, Miss Gertrude Gardner, and Mrs.
A. C. Whitaker will act as patronesses;
Professors A. C. Whitaker and H. J.
Ryan will be patrons of the affair.
A new custom of having a guest from
each sorority house has been inaugurat
ed with this dance. The second dance,
which will be primarily for the under
class women, is scheduled for Febru
ary 28th.
