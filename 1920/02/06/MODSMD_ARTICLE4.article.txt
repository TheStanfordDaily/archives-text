# OLYMPICS MEET STANFORD IN RUGBY HERE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Stanford rugby men meet the Olym
pic "Club fifteen at Ewing Field, to
morrow afternoon at 2:30, in their
last scheduled contest before the Cali
fornia game.
According to the announcement by
the winged "O" manager, the club
men will use the line-up from which
their portion of the squad which goes
to the seventh Olympiad will be
chosen.
A Cardinal victory will keep Stan
ford in the lead of California Rugby
League, making the California game
the deciding factor for the champion
ship. Coaches Wylie and Carroll will
take a squad of twenty men to San
Francisco tomorrow for the game.
