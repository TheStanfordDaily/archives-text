# NORTON TO ADDRESS COSMOPOLITAN CLUB
## 
### 
Henry K. Norton, executive officer
of the state commission on Immigra
tion and Housing, will be the speaker
of the evening at tonight's Comopoli
tan Club meeting, in the Women's
Clubhouse, at 8 o'clock p.m.
The address will be of especial in
terest to Economic majors, in the
opinion of the club officials since Nor
ton is an authority on the subject.
The public is invited. A business
meeting will follow the address.
