# Five Stanford Men Pass State Bar Examination
## 
### 
Five former Stanford students passed
the examinations for admission to the
bar in the examinations held January
19, 20, and 21. Those presented with
the certificates were: James D. Adams,
'15; Allyn H. Baker, '19; Herman H.
Hoss, '18; Dickson F. Maddox, J. D.,
'19, and Bradford M. Melvin, ex-'l5.
