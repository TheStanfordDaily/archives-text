# "SAM" SHORTRIDGE WILL ADDRESS THE REPUBLICAN CLUB
## 
### 
An open meeting of the Republican
Club will be held tomorrow evening in
the Women's Clnbhouse. Samuel Short
ridge, a prominent attorney of San
Francisco, will be the speaker of the
evening. He was at one time a candi
date for the governship of the state and
is well known in Republican circles.
The meeting is open to the public, and
all those interested are urged to attend.
The lecture will begin at 8:00 o'clock.
