# BLASCO IBANEZ VISITS ON STANFORD CAMPUS
## Spanish Novelist is Touring American For New Settings 
### 
Don Vincente Blasco Ibanez, the
widely known Spanish novelist, was
a visitor on the campus yesterday, as
the guest of Professor and Mrs. Espi
nosa, of the Department of Romanic
Languages.
Senor Ibanez expressed himself well
impressed with the architectural beau
ty of the University buildings, which,
he stated, reminded him of Spain.
The author is at present studying
the country with a view to writing
four works based on it. Two of them
will be named "The Republic of
Women," and"The Center of the
World" (New York). The other two
will concern New Mexico and Cali
fornia.
