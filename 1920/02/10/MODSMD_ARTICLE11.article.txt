# Shortridge Will Address Republicans Tonight
## 
### 
Samuel Shortridge will address the
Republican Club at an open meeting
of the society in the Women's Club
House, this evening, at S o'clock.
Mr. Shortridge is prominent among
Republican circles and is a prominent
lawyer of San Francisco. He was at
one time a candidate for the governor
ship of California.
The meeting is open to the public
and all interested in the aims of the
club are urged and invited to attend.
