# REPUBLICAN CAUCUS TO BE HELD THURSDAY
## 
### 
A Republican Club caucus meeting
will be held Thursday evening in the
Little Theater. The meeting is for the
purpose of adopting a model platform
for the Republican party. The club has
been asked by National Chairman Will
R. Hayes to send the results of the
meeting to the convention at Chicago.
An appropriate commemoration of
Lincoln's birthday will be included in the
program. The meeting will begin at
7:45 p. m. All Republican Club mem
bers arc urged to be present.
