# O.A.C. Defeats U. of W. And Washington State
## 
### 
The Oregon Agricultural College de
feated both University of Washington
and Washington State College in bas
ketball last week. The University of
Washington game was a walk-away
for the Aggies, a score of 31 to 10
being piled up. Two games were play
ed with Washington State of which
the first was easily won by Oregon to
the tune of 31 to 11. The second game
was more closely contested, but the
Washington team lost by the score
of 27 to 22.
