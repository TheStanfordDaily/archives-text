# Two Economics Professors Join U.C. Summer Staff
## 
### 
Eliot Jones, associate professor of
economics, and John 13. Canning, as
sistant professor of economics, have
been invited to join the faculty of
the University of California during
the summer session. Professor Can
ning will teach during the six-week
intersessional period beginning May
10, and Professor Jones during the six
week period beginning June 21.
