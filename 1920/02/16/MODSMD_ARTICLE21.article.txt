# TRYOUT FOR RIFLE CONTESTS THIS WEEK
## 
### 
Competition for membership on the
Intercollegiate Rifle Team closes this
week, announced Captain S. M. Parker
today. Due to the delay in the comple
tion of the new gallery adjacent to En
cinag ymnasium. the try-outs will be con
tinued for the next few days in the rifle
gallery under the art building. The
room will be open for candidates from
four to eight o'clock every afternoon.
With the opening of the outdoor gal
lery, which is expected by the end of the
week, the first scheduled shooting in the
National Collegiate Rifle Contest will be
conducted. Captain Parker urges that
all possible candidates sign up at the
gallery the first of this week in order to
be assured of a chance for membership
on the squad.
