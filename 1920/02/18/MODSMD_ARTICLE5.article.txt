# Medical Lecture Will Be Given at Lane Hospital Next Friday
## 
### 
Dr. Alfred Cummings Reed will speak
on 'The Out-Patient Clinic as a Health
Center," Friday evening at 8 o'clock, at
Lane Hall of the Stanford University
Medical School, San Francisco. This is
one of the popular medical lectures
given annually by the medical school,
which are designed for the general pub
lic and are non-technical in character.
Dr. Reed is a San Francisco physician
and a member of the Stanford Univer
sity Medical School faculty, who has
a good deal to do with the out-patient
work of the hospital. Dr. Reed has had
experience as a medical practitioner
over a wide territory, including eastern
cities of the United States and in China.
He is editor of the California State
Journal of Medicine.
