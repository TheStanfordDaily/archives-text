# THIRTY-FIVE STUDENTS SIGN UP FOR POLO
## 
### 
Thirty-five men have signed up for
polo in connection with the field ar
tillery unit of the Stanford 11. (). T. C.,
and are busy learning the rudiments
of the art of riding a polo pony.
A polo field has been laid out on the
land east, of Encina Hall. The mili
tary department expects to develop
several teams, and stage some good
matches in the near future. After the
sport gets under way at Stanford an
attempt will be made to arrange out
side games.
The War Department has encour
aged the development of polo teams in
connection with the field artillery
work, and has supplied Stanford with
ten polo ponies.
