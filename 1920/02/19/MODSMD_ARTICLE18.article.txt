# MANAGING EDITOR
## 
### 
WITH this issue of The Daily Palo Alto we announce the appointment of
" R. E. Wedekind, '19, as managing editor. Wedekind has worked faith
fully on the staff for three quarters. He has shown marked ability as a news
paper man, having had unusual experience for two years on an outside
newspaper. It is entirely upon this consideration and with no reflection on
the other members of^the staff that the appointment is made.
