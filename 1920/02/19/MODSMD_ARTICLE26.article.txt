# Professor H.D. Gray To Lecture in San Jose
## 
###

Department will give a series of lec
tures on "Great Books As an Inspira
tion" before the Unitarian society of
San Jose. The first of these lectures
will be on Ibsen's "Brand," and will
be given on March 3.
