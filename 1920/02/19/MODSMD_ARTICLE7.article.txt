# Stanford Orchestra Men to Play For English Club
## 
### 
The Orchestra for the English Club
play to be given on Friday night has
been chosen from the regular Stanford
Orchestra. Those that will play tomor
row night arc: Violins, R. E. Blackburn,
J. Delpech, R. S. Moore, W. V. West;
cello, G. V. Marx; bass, O. W. Johnson;
flute, E. Lincoln; trumpet, D. R. Hack
ett; trombone, A. Mosher; piano, Mrs.
D. B. Hackctt; clarinet, E. Plaskett;
horns, A. C. Whitaker and D. Whitaker.
