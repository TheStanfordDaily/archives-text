# Influenza Ban Lifted For Stanford Dances
## 
### 
Influenza t>an on campus dances was
lifted this morning by Dr. A. D. Browne,
on the advice of the Stanford Health
Committee in conjunction with the Palo
Alto Board of .Health. The lifting of
the "flu" ban for Palo Alto extends only
to private danccs. The professional
dance halls will remain closed until fur
ther action by the Palo Alto Board of
Health.
The action was taken by the Physical
Director when it was evident that little
danger of the spread of the disease ex
isted. Only a few influenza patients have
entered the hospitals the last week, and
those under the doctor's treatment are
not showing serious symptoms.
