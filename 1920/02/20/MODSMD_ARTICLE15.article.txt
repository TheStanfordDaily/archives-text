# Artillery R.O.T.C. Gets New Equipment
## 
### 
A number of trucks and motorcycles
have been received by the field artillery
unit as additional equipment for their
motorized section. The artillery tractors
are being put to a practical use by drag
ging away sections of the big tree cut
down in the gymnasium yard.
