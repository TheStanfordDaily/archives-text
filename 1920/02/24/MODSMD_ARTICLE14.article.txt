# Professor P.A. Martin To Lecture Tomorrow
## 
### 
Professor Percy A. Martin, of the
History Department, will speak on
"Some Social and Moral Problems of
Latin America,"in a lecture to be given
under the auspices of the Y. W. C. A.
and the Y. M. C. A. Wednesday even
! ing at 7 :3() in the Little Theater.
Professor Martin will discuss ways
in which the future development of Latin
America along political and economic
lines is conditioned on the solution of a
number of moral and social problems.
He will also point out how the United
States can be of assistance in helping
tlie countries of Latin America.
