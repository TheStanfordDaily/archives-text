# MINE RESCUE CAR TO BE EXHIBITED
## 
### 
A car from the Bureau of Mines Sta
tion at Berkeley will visit the campus
Thursday, February 26. Two courses
will be given, one in First-Aid and one
in Mine-Rescue. Those who complete
the courses will be given a certificate
which will entitle them to take charge
of work of this nature in any mining
community. If a sufficient number of
students turn out for the courses, a team
will be selected to compete with a squad
from the University of California. The
contest will take place in the near future.
J. B. Berry, who is in charge of the
car, will meet all students interested
Thursday, February 26, at 4 p. m., .in
Room 500.
