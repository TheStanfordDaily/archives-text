# CAMPUS WOMEN WILL ALTER CONSTITUTION
## 
### 
Campus women will hold a mass meet
ing next Monday afternoon for the pur
pose of making some changes in the con
stitution of the "Organized Women."
Miss Ruth Searcy. '20, president of the
organization, announced that there will
be two speakers from off the campus.
The changes made at this meeting will
be for entrance in the rule bok of the
women's organization. At this time
there are maters of vital importance
to be considered, and it is requested that
there be a large attendance. At present
the exact hour of the meeting can not
be announced, but it will be given in a
later issue of the D. P. A.
