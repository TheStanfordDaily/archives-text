# Nestoria Will Hold Mid-Quarter Banquet
## 
### 
The mid-quarter banquet of Nestoria
Debating Society will he held tomorrow
evening in the Stanford Inn at 6:30
p.m. Professor A. M. Cathcart, of the
Law Department, has been secured by
the club to deliver the address of the
evening. The topic for his speech will
be: "The Acme of This Age."
It is urged that all members of the
society be present tomorrow evening at
the dinner as several important matters
will be settled by the society at this
time.
