# TENNIS MEN TO MEET IN HASTINGS TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
The preliminary and first round of
matches in the Hasting's handicap tennis
tournament were completed yesterday
afternoon. The second round will start
with today's games. The best prelimi
nary matches were played by Ford Tus
sing, '22, who won from L. K. Stod
dard, '23, Tussiug having a handicap of
30 points, score 6-3, 6-3; and Hawley
Mertz, '21, who won from Geo. McGill,
'23, score 6-4, 6-3. The two rounds have
been run off in good succession and
from all predictions the tourney will be
a great success.
Results of the two rounds are: B. J.
Frankheimer won from H. S. Souther
land by default; Jerry Thomas won
from R. C. Durand, score 6-8, 6-2, 6-0;
Halley Mertz won over George McGill,

16-4, 6-3; Joe Bailey defaulted to J.
Neer; Ford Tussing won from L. K.
Stoddard, 6-3, 6-3; C. F. Dimond won
from D. C. Burtrand, 6-1, 6-0; John Sib
ley, won by default over Oliver Wheeler ;
Bradley Morrison won from Ezra Rat
ner by default; William Muir beat E. C.
West, 6-4, 6-3; D. B. Monroe won from
, Geo. Knox by default; Paul Kenney
lost to N. B. Chaney, 11-9,6-1; Rit Hol-
I man won from G. Kai by default; W. F.
Swartz Jost to R. J. Allen by default;
I Cecil Van Wyke won over J. Wernli,
6-2, 6-0; Allen Barker defaulted to Joe
Craig; Ray Leben lost to W. L. Kol
berg, 7-5, 6-1 ; B. A. Cody won from
Atkinson, 6-2. 6-2; Ske Turner won
from L. Fish, 6-4, 6-3; George Hall won
from B. H. Bingham, 6-2, 6-2; W.
Young won from W. Whiteford, 6-1,
6-4; C. Little won from Homer Mitchell,
6-0, 6-3.
