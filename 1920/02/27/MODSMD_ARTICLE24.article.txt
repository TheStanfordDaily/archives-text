# PROFESSOR CATHCART SPEAKS TO NESTORIA
## 
### 
Professor A. M. Cathcart was the
principal speaker of tlie mid-quarter
banquet of Nestoria Debating Society.
The banquet was held last evening at
the Inn.
"The Acme of *This Age," was Mr.
Cathcart's subject. The importance
of the ability of a university graduate
to speak in life was the purpose of
the organization of debating societies
in Stanford, he said. This, at the pres
ent time of confused international re
lations is a great importance to those
who are to be leaders in this country.
