# INFLUENZA SITUATION IMPROVES; MUMPS APPEAR
## 
### 
The influenza situation is rapidly
changing toward the best. There are
a comparatively few cases of "flu" either
among the students or in Palo Alto.
With influenza clearing up fast, mumps
arc setting in. Twelve students are; at
the Isolation Hospital with cases of the
mumps.
