# VICTORIAN NOVEL STUDY LEAVES ITS PUBLISHERS
## 
### 
"Satire in the Victorian Novel," by
Mrs. Frances Theresa Russell, of the
English Department, is a new book
which has just left the publishers. It
is an interpretive study of Victorian fic
tion. emphasizing the tendencies of satire
in this type of literature. How satire
arose and was used, its method and pur
pose, is described by Mrs. Russell with
a view to making the book of value both
to the intimate and to the general stu
dent of literature.
