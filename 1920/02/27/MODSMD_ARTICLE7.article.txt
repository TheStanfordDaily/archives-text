# FORUM WILL HOLD MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
The University Forum will meet this
evening at 8 o'clock in the Faculty
Club House to discuss the proposals
now before the Executive Committee
regarding the maintenance of proper
standards of written expression
throughout the University.
The Forum is an informal organiza
tion of all the members of the faculty,
for the unofficial discussion of uni
versity matters of broad interest.
