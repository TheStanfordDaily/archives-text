# Action Threatened If Basket Not Returned
## 
### 
The Student Council lias been in
formed that on Saturday evening be
tween seven and ten o'clock a basket
containing the remains of a luncheon,
napkins, and silverware, was taken
from the men's dressing room at the
Women's Clubhouse. The Student
Council urgently requests that these
articles be left at the cigar stand in
the Men's Union, in which case no
further notice of the act will he taken,
but if this request is not complied with
a thorough investigation will be in
stigated.
