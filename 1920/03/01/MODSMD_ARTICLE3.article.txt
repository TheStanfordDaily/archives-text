# SCHUBERT CLUB PLANS PROGRAM
## 
### 
The "Spinning Song" from the "Fly
ing Dutchman," along with composi
tions from Purcell, famous Oul Welsh
and Old French songs, besides modern
selections by Arthur Beigh. and Ed
ward Grieg will make up a most en
joyable musical program to be render
ed by the members of Schubert Club,
on the evening of March 16. This
Schubert Club performance will be
held in the Little Theater, and will
take the place of the regular Tues
day night lecture that is weekly held
there. Besides the selections by the
members of the club, there will be a
solo.
