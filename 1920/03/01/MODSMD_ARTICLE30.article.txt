# MID-YEAR DEGREE TOTALS BOOSTED
## 
### 
Degrees conferred in 1920 will greatly
exceed the number conferred in 1919,
according to the following table which
has been given out by the Registrar's
Office:
January, 1920 degrees 20
January, 1919 degrees 60
April, 1919 degrees 37
April, 1920 applications 85
