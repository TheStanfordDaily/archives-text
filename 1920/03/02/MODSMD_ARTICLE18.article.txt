# Stanford Musical Club Admits Two Women
## 
### 
Two students were successful yester
day at the Stanford Music Club try-out
meeting. Those admitted to member
ship were: Miss Anna Ramsey, '22, vo
calist. who has studied with the late
Organist Eaton, and Miss Dorothy
Woodhead. '23. Miss Woodhead is well
known in Los Angeles for her concert
work and while there studied with Rob
ert Martin Staples, a member of the
Los Angeles Symphony Orchestra. She
is a member of* Alpha Phi.
Due to the lack of time yesterday
afternoon, another try out meeting will
he held on Monday, March 8, at the Chi
Omega house.
