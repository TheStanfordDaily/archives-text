# CAP AND GOWN WILL GIVE DANCE FRIDAY
## 
### 
The second of a series of dances to
be given this year by Cap and Gown
will take place Friday evening at the
Women's Clubhouse. Pie raffles for the
"roughs" and candy raffles for the
dancers are the special attractions being
planned for the evening. Whitmer's or
chestra has been secured to furnish the
music. The admission price has been
set at $1.10 per couple, or 85 cents a
person.
Several dances will be given on the
Row this week-end. Delta Chi and
Theta Detla Chi will entertain in
formally Friday evening, while Chi Psi
and Gamma Phi Beta have signed in
the date book for dances Saturday night.
