# COACH EVANS WILL GIVE LECTURES TO BASEBALL SQUADS
## 
### 
During the inclement weather both
varsity and freshman baseball squads
will discontinue work on the diamond,
but will be given lectures in the gym,
according to an announcement by Coach
Evans today. The lectures will begin
tomorrow at 4:30 p. m., and will con
tinue until further notice. Both var
sity and freshman candidates will be
required to attend.
