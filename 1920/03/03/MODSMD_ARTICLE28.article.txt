# EGBERT PIERSON, EX-'15, DIES FROM PNEUMONIA IN FRANCE
## 
### 
Authentic word was just received to
day of the death of Egbert Pierson, a
former Stanford student in the class of
'IS, on September 23, 1918, in France.
According to a letter just received by
Dean McDowell from Pierson's mother,
he died of pneumonia. His name will
he added to the Stanford Honor List.
