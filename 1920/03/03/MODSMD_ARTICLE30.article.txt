# Sequoia Hall Defines "Disorderly Conduct"
## 
### 
Disorderly conduct has been defined
by the House Committee at Sequoia as
follows:
1. Playing of the piano or other mu
sical instruments after 8 p. m., excepting
Saturday and Sunday nights.
2. Willful and negligent destruction of
property belonging either to the hall or
to private individuals.
3. Noisy and boisterous conduct in the
halls or lobby.
4. Profane or indecent language in a
loud and offensive manner in or about
the halls.
5. Any use of the blackboard except
by authority of club officials, except that
telephone calls may be posted thereon.
