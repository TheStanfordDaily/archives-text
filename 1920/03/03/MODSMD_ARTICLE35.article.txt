# FRENCH CLUB WILL GIVE TWO PLAYS NEXT QUARTER
## 
### 
At the meeting of the French Club
at the Chi Omega house last evening,
plans for the plays to be given next
quarter were discussed. It was decided
that two plays should be given, the re
ceipts of which should go to the French
orphans. The cast of the play will be
announced later.
As entertainment, Mr. Savre, of San
Mateo, gave a reading, and Miss Anna
Ramsey sang two vocal selections.
