# WOMEN COMMENCE INTERCLASS FINAL BASKETBALL GAMES
## 
### 
The women's interclass basketball
series begins this afternoon when the
senior team meets the sophomores. This
is the first game in the final series. The
class winning this match will play the
winner of the freshman-junior game
which will be held next week. The class
coming first in the series will be awarded
the cup for interclass basketball by the
Women's Athletic Association.
The varsity game with Mills College
will be held Saturday, March 13. It
has not yet been definitely decided where
the game will be held, but it is probable
that the Stanford team will go up to
Mil!?.
