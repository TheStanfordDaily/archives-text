# SOPHOMORE CLASS ELECTS OFFICERS
## 
### 
John Sibley was elected president of
the sophomore class at the meeting held
this noon.
Class Treasurer Lee Moore reported
that there were no funds in the treas
ury, and recommended some sort of en
tertainment for the purpose of raising
money. He also gave notice of an as
sessment of 50 cents, to be paid immedi
ately.
A committee, consisting of Lee Moore
—and two assistants to be appointed by
him—was appointed to arrange for a
dance in the nature of a Jolly-Up, to be
given the first week in next quarter.
