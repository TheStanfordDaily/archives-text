# NEW SIGNS PUT UP TO WARN MOTORISTS
## 
###

the speed laws about the campus. Comp
troller Roth is looking for the co-opera
tion of the students in preventing acci
dents, but will be prepared to enforce
the rules. The preliminary warning is
sued some time ago was to bring the
matter to the attention of the public be
fore the signs were secured. Four seri
ous accidents before the warning con
trast strongly with none since.
