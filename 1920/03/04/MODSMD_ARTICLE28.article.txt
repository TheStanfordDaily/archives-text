# NOW FOR THE CALIFORNIA SERIES
## 
### 
TOMORROW night the basketball team meets the California five on the
Harmon Gymnasium floor to decide the basketball series with California.
California wilt have a slight advantage playing on their own floor, but the
ardent support of the Stanford student body can make up for any disadvant
age the Cardinal team may have to work under. Stanford has wqn the Con
ference Championship and we cannot fall down at this late hour. We aren't
worried about the team and we have every confidence in our coach so it is
up to every man and woman who possibly can to plan to be at California
tomorrow night. Let's get out the Big Game Spirit and, standing strong be
hind Coach Evans and every man on the team, cheer the Cardinal men on
to another win.
