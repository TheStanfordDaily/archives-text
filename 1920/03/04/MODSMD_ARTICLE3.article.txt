# STANFORD WILL ENTER BULLETIN POLO MEET
## 
### 
Stanford will enter a t,eani in the
First Annual Water Polo tournament
being conducted by the San Francisco
Bulletin from March 1 until March 31,
Coach Ernst Brandsten has announced.
The tourney has been sanctioned by the
Pacific Association of the American
Athletic Union and is being held under
the auspices of the California Water
Polo Association. Some of the matches
will probably be held in the Encina pool,
according to Rrandsten.
