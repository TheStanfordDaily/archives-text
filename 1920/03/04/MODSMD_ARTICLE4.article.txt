# Cap and Gown Dance Promises New Raffles
## 
### 
Raffles of home-made pies for the
"roughs," and candy raffles for the
dancers will be the chief attraction of
the Cap and Gown dance tomorrow
evening in the woman's Clubhouse. This
is the sccond dance which Cap and
Gown has given this quarter, and prom
ises to be of the same enjoyable variety
as those given by the honor society in
the past.
Witmer's orchestra will be on hand to
supply music. Admission is $1.10 a cou
ple ; 85 cents a person.
