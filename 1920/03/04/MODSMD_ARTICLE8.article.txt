# NEW SAN JOSE TRIP OFFERED TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Peninsular Railway Company of
fers an innovation in the special car
leaving the Campus at 7 p. m., Friday,
for San Jose. The car will go wthout
stop or change to the theater localities,
and will return to the Campus at 11
o'clock. Tickets for the trip will be hon
ored on any car, later or earlier.
The great mass of advertising which
flooded the Campus yesterday placed the
matter before the students with bewil
dering suddenness. The experiment will
test the advisability of making this
"Queencr's Special" a weekly affair.
