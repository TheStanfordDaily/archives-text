# Washington Commences Work On Golf Course
## 
### 
Work has commenced on the Uni
versity of Washington gol/ course and
a force of men have already completed
two greens. The course will be of
standard nine-holes, thirty-two hundred
yards in length, fully bunkered and
strapped and the greens will be in
tirst class condition by early summer.
The course is to be laid out on the
ground that was occupied by the Naval
Aviation Fields during the war.
