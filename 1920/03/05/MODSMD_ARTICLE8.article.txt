# ORCHESTRA AND CHOIR WILL GIVE LENTEN MUSIC SERVICE
## 
### 
The Lenten Musical Service will be
given by the University orchestra and
choir. The orchestra will accompany
the choir in the singing of "Gallyia," by
Gounod. The orchestra will give Schu
bert's "Unfinished Sympathy," excerpts
from"The Golden Legend" by Sullivan,
and the closing chorus from the St.
Matthew Passion Music by Bach.
