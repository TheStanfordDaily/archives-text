# All-American Ruggers Defeat British Team
## 
### 
All-American rugby stars staged a
come-back, in the last elimination con
test of the California Rugby Union,
played on the local field last Satur
day, by defeating the hitherto victori
ous All-British fifteen by a score of
10 to 3. Both teams made their scores
in the first half of the game, the last
period being even.
The series between the All-American
and All-British teams closed with the
contests won two to one in favor of
the latter aggregation. Members of
the selection committee will pick the
final Antwerp rugby squad, of thirty
men, from the showing made by candi
dates in the series.
