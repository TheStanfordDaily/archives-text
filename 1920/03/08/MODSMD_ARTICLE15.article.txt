# METHODIST LUNCHEON TO BE HELD FOR WOMEN
## 
### 
Mrs. Clyde Collison, Pacific Branch
student secretary of the Woman's For
eign Missionary Society of the Method
ist Episcopal Church, will address the
Methodist women of the University at
a luncheon to be given for them by a
group of the women of the Palo Alto
Methodist Chuch tomorrow noon, in
the Women's Clubhouse.
The luncheon is an annual affair for
the purpose of making the women
better acquainted, to keep them in
formed concerning foreign mission
ary work and to explain to them the
opportunities in this field. Mrs. Col
lision has been appointed by the Mis
sionary Society to take charge of this
work in schools and colleges on the
Pacific Coast.
