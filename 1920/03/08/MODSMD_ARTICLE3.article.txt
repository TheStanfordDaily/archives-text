# President Wilbur Returns from East
## 
### 
After an absence of over three
weeks, President Wilbur will return to
the campus late this afternoon. Dur
ing his trip east. Dr. Wilbur was pres
ent at the annual meeting of the Con
gress of Medical Education in Chicago,
speaking on "The Care of the Insane."
He addressed meetings of alumni in
Denver and Kansas City, and at the
presentation of the New York Civic
Forum's medal to Herbert Hoover, Dr.
Wilbur was one of the principal speak
ers.
