# PRESS CLUB ELECTS FIVE NEW MEMBERS
## 
###

last Friday night, five new men were
eleeted to membership. The new
members who will be initiated next
Wednesday night are J. A. Quinbv, '18,
F. S. Reeder, '22, D. B. Van Every. '18,
W. G. Hall. '19, and F. L. McElroy, '21.
