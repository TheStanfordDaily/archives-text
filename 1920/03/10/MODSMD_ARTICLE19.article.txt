# ETHEL DICKENSON RUGG ANNOUNCES ENGAGEMENT
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Ethel Dick
inson Rugg, '18, to John Stanwood Tal
cott, of Oakland, was announced at a
dinner Sunday night at the Chi Omega
House. While in college Miss Rugg
took a prominent part in activities. Tal
cott is a special representative of Balfour
Guthrie Company in San Eranciscb.
