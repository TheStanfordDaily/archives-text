# CHOIR REHEARSAL TIMES ARE CHANGED BY ALLEN
## 
### 
Choir and oratorio rehearsals com
bined will be held in the Memorial
Church, at / o'clock tomorrow evening,
instead of the usual hour, 7:30. The
change is on account of the concert in
the Assembly Hall at 8:15. Warren D.
Allen desires all students registered
either for Choir 1 or Choir 2 to be on
time as the work to be done is very im
portant. Mr. Allen has also announced
that on account of the early rehearsal,
there will be no organ recital tomorrow
evening.
