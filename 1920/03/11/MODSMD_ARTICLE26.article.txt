# Stanford Glee Club Singers Before American Legion
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club made its ini
tial public appearance at a meeting
of the American Legion held last even
ing in the Community House in Palo
Alto. Entertainment throughout the
evening was given by the club and by
Mr. Roller of Palo Alto, who delivered
several recitations.
It was announced at the meeting that
the Knights of Columbus will establish
a night school for service and ex-service
men at San Mateo. Fifty scholarships
will be tfiven to service men for the post.
Professors from Stanford and from
Santa Clara Universities will conduct
classes in auto-salesmanship, civil serv
ice, and in several languages.
