# PALO ALTO HOOVER CLUB STARTS PETITION
## 
### 
Petitions for the placing of Hoover
delegate candidates on the Republican
ballot at the presidential preferential
primary election on May 4 will be
circulated by Hoover Home Town Re
publican Club as soon as the delegate
candidates are announced. The pe
tition requires the signatures of 2,500
registered Republican voters, of whom
a number are expected to be supplied
from this locality. Registration clerks
report a majority of voters register
ing as Republicans.
