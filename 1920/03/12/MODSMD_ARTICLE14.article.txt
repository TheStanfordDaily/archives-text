# SONG CONTEST BEGUN FOR CO-ED ATHLETICS
## 
### 
Under the direction of the Women's
Conference, a prize in the form of a
gold pin. decorated with the Stanford
seal, has been offered to the girl writ
iug the best song to be used at inter
collegiate games in women's athletics.
At present, the only songs that are
available for such a purpose are the
traditional Stanford songs, and, ac
cording to the committee, the idea is
to have.a song, full of originality and
individuality, that can be sung at the
games. All those wishing to try for
the prize, are requested to turn their
songs into Miss Esther Gleason. '21.
at the Delta Delta Delta house, be
fore April 10.
