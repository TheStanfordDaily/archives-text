# UNION MANAGER'S POSITION NOW OPEN FOR APPLICATION
## 
### 
The resignation of A. T. George, '20,
as manager of the Stanford Union,
was accepted at a meeting of the
Union Board of Control last night.
George is a candidate for graduation
in' April. ••
Application for the position of man
ager must be submitted to A. E.
Worthy at the Y. M. C. A. office be
fore 4 o'clock. Monday, March 22. The
Union Board will select one of the
applicants to fill the unexpired term.
The board also authorized several
improvements around the Union. A
bid was given for the remodeling of
the porches, and two billiard tables.
The landscape garden to be raised
around the yard, will be started within
the next few days.
