# Stanford Music Club Plans Concert Series
## 
### 
Next Wednesday evening the Stan
ford Music Club will give the first of a
scries of monthly concerts. In the latter
part of next quarter it is probable that
special concerts will be given where only
the compositions of a single'composer
will be rendered. Due to the fact that
the members of the club play a variety
of instruments, there will be an oppor
tunity for splendid programs.
The members who will take part on
Wednesday evening are Misses Ruth
Langer, '22, Ruth Davis, '22, Gladys
Marriott, '22, and Remise Franken
hcimer, '20.
