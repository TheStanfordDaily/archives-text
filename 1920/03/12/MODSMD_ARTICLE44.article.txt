# Sequoia Club Will Give Smoker Thursday
## 
### 
Sequoia Club will give its first smoker
of the year next Thursday night. The
plans as formulated by the social com
mittee last night include boxing bouts,
readings, talks, refreshments, smokes,
and a visit to one of the Palo Alto
"movies." G. S. Lambert, gr., is the
chairman of the committee planning the
smoker.
