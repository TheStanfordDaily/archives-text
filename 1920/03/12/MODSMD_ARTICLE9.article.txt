# LA 						LIANA ELECTS OFFICERS AT MEETING
## 
### 
At a meeting of I-a Liana Club yes
terday it was decided to have an ar
ticle on the club's activities in the
1920 Quad, without a picture.
Stanford women commuters, includ
ing those from San Jose, Redwood
City, San Mateo, and other valley
towns as well as Palo Alto, met and
elected the following officers:
Dorothy Creever, '21, president;
Elizabeth Wallace, '23, vice-president;
Oraee Swittenbank, '21, treasurer, and
Laura Kennedy, '23, secretary.
