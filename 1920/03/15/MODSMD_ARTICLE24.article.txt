# NOTICE
## 
### 
Inasmuch as the High School Princi
pals' Convention is to be held within the
next few weeks, it is very essential that
all those who expect to teach next year
should complete immediately their regis
tration with the Appointment Office.
Those who have already registered
should ascertain at once whether or not
all their recommendations have been re
ceived. Come in today. Don't put it off.
Elizabeth B. Snell.
