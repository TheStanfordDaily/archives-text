# Senior Women's Sextet Will Play the Juniors
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at 5, the senior
women's basketball team will play the
juniors on Roble court. The following
afternoon the seniors will meet the
freshman team. These are the last two
games to be played this quarter in the
women's basketball series. So far the
sophomores have first place in the series,
having won every interclass game in
which they have taken part.
These games have been called late in
the afternoon in order to give all cam
pus women a chance to come out to
support their team
