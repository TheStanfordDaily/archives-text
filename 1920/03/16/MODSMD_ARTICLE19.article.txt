# PROF. GRAY WILL READ PAPER AT CONVENTION
## 
### 
Professor H. D. Gray, of the English
Department, will present a paper on
"Some Indications That the Tempest
Mas Revived," at the meeting of the
Modern Languages Association which is
to be held at Columbus. Ohio, the last
three days of March. Professor Gray
will then go to New York, where he
will remain during the spring quarter.
