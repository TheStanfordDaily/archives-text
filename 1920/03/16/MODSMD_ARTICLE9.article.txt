# RAM'S HEAD ELECTS SIX TO MEMBERSHIP
## 
### 
Six men were elected to the mem
bership of the Ram's Head Club, at last
night's meeting of the dramatic society.
They are: R. W. Sewall. '20; YV. G
Half. "19; G. W. Corby, '22; F. 11. I'.en
nett. '19; C. F. Arnn, '19; and 11. 1.
Mitchell, '21. The initiation will lie held
sometime in the near future.
