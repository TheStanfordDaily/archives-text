# STEAMER TICKETS ARE ON SALE AT STANFORD
## 
### 
Tickets to Los Angeles, San Diego,
and Seattle via the Admiral Steamship
Line, may be purchased in a booth estab
lished in the Administration Building.
Harry Brod, '20, is in charge and has
his office hours there from 12:30 to 1:15
and 4to 5 every afternoon. Rates were
reduced by the steamship company.
Passage from San Francisco to Seattle
was reduced from $58 to $44, and the
San Francisco-San Diego trip now costs
$30 instead of $31.
