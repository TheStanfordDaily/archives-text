# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB PLANS LONG HIKE
## 
### 
A three-day hike to San Gregorio,
is the plan of the Cosmopolitan Club.
The hikers will leave the campus on
Saturday the 27th. All members who
wish to go should register their names
and their guests, with W. F. Hinton,
Sequoia; Hay Ground. Encina, or Miss
Gladys Marriott, Roble. A charge of
$3 will coves the expenses of the trip.
