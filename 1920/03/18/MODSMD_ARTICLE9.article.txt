# Stanford Graduate Publishes New Books
## 
### 
"A Stop at Suzanne's and Lower
Flights" is a book recently published by
Greaver Clover, who was a student at
Stanford in 1916. After completing his
undergraduate work here he attended
Vale, and from there entered the French
Canadian Convoi service and spent six
months in carrying supplies and amuni
tions to the men in the trenches. When
America entered the war he became a
member of the Aviation Corps and met
his death when his plane crashed to
earth in a field back of the lines.
The book consists of his letters and
prose sketches and deals with his life
and experiences during the war.
