# STUDENTS MUST FILE NOTES BY NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
All students who are planning to give
their notes for the tuition next quarter
must have applications in by the end of
next week, March 26. Applications may
be obtained for the men at the Dean of
Men's Office, and for the women at Dean
Harriett Bradford's office in the Admin
istration Building. Students who gave
notes for last quarter's tuition need only
to sign up in these respective offices for
spring quarter. New applications must
be made out in full.
