# Future Stanford Men Receive Cadet Offices
## 
### 
Herbert Hoover, Jr., has been made
a sergeant of the 91st company of the
Palo Alto Union High School cadet
battalion, according to the announce
ment of promotions in the last issue of
The Campanile, the high-school paper.
Eric Jordan, son of Chancellor David
Starr Jordan, is quartermaster sergeant
on the battalion staff.
