# SWIMMERS TO TOUR SOUTH ARE NAMED
## 
### 
The varsity swimmers who will
tour the southern part of the state
during the spring vacation were nam
ed by Coach Ernst Brandsten this
morning. They will be Mills. Drake,
Austin, Overacker, Dickey, Pinkston,
Sparling, Coonse and Winham. Wick
ersham and Goddard will join the men
in the south.
Meets have been scheduled with the
San Diego Rowing Club for March 30
and with the Los Angeles Athletic
Club for March 31. Other contests
with southern teams are being ar
ranged.
