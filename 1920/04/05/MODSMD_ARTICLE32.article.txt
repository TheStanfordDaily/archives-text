# TWO INSTRUCTOR ARE NAMED FOR SUMMER
## 
### 
Two summer quarter additions to
the faculty were appointed by the ,
Board of Trustees at a meeting during
vacation. Dr. Raymond H. Wheeler
of the University of Oregon, will come
here as an acting instructor in psychol- j
ogy for the summer months, and Dr.
J. M. D. Olmstead, of the College of
Medicine of the University of llinois,
will be an acting instructor in Physi
ology. Dr. Olmstead was a Rhodes
scholar at Oxford and received his
degree of B.A. and MA. at that insti
tution. He received the degree of
M.A. and the degree of Ph.D. at Har
vard in 1919.
