# DANCE TO BE GIVEN FOR CREW TONIGHT
## 
### 
A crew dance, which will be the first
campus dance of the quarter, will be
given at Encina Hall this evening at
8:45 p.m. An admission price of one
dollar ner couple will be charged, and
the proceeds from the dance will be for
the benefit of the crew training table
and general expenses. Arrangements
have been made for the securing of a
>pecial five-piece jazz orchestra.
