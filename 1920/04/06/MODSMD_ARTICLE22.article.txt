# Miss Marion Roth, '20, Announces Engagement
## 
### 
At a pretty and cleverly arranged
party, Miss Marion Roth. '20. recently
announced her engagement to Mr. Jo
seph Goldstein. '19. Miss Roth com
pleted her course in the University last
quarter, while Goldstein, who is a med
ical student, is completing his course
as an interne in San Francisco. Miss
Roth is a member of Delta Delta Delta
sorority, and Goldstein is a member of
Encina Club. The couple expect to be
married in the early fall.
