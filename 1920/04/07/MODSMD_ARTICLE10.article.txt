# Y.M.C.A. Cabinet Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Y. M. C. A. cabinet
will meet tonight at the union at 7:30
I), tn. for the purpose of organizing.
Plans for the work to be carried on
during the spring quarter will be dis
cussed, and a nominating committee for
the election of officers for the fiscal year
1920-1921 will be elected.
