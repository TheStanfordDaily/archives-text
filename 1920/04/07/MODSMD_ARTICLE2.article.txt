# QUAD RAZZ CUP IS RETURNED TO EDITOR
## 
### 
All those expecting to enter josh
copy or cartoons In competition for
the Quad "Razz Cups" must submit
it not later than Saturday noon, April
10. The razzes must be handed into
Editor McElroy or mailed to P. O.
Box 1166.
The missing cup (to be awarded the
winner of the "razz" contest, which
was stolen last quarter) was found
during the vacation, W. I. Helbaum,
'18, claiming the ten dollar reward.
He refused to tell the editor where the
cup was discovered.
One will be presented to the person
submitting the best individual razz and
another will be given for the best razz
by one of the living groups.
