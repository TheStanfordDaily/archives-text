# Dr. Jordan Will Speak To Y.W.C.A. Thursday
## 
### 
Women and their place in the Uni
versity will be the topic of the talk
given by Dr. David Starr Jordan at the
Y. W. C. A. meeting Thursday after
noon at 5 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.
This is the first Y. W. C. A. meeting
of the quarter. It will be essentially
Stanford in character, and will be a
general get-together meeting. All cam
pus women are invited to attend.
