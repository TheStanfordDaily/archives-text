# ILLINOIS SWIMMERS WILL PRACTICE HERE
## 
### 
Word has been received from Nor
man Ross that Coach Backrath of the
Illinois Athletic Club is coming to Stan
ford with fourteen of the world's best
swimmers.
These men will arrive here about
April 15 to train for the National Relay
championships. From the advance re
ports the Illinois team is one of the
fastest in the country and some wonder
ful swimming should be shown in the
relay championships at the Olympic
Club on April 27. While here these men
will also take part in the finals of the
National Water Polo championships at
Sutro Baths on Moy 1. The semi-finals
of the polo tournament will be held in
the Stanford tank.
