10:00 a.m. UNIVERSITY BIBLE CLASSES, in the Memorial
Church.

4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen; in the
Memorial Church.
7:30 p. m. ILLUSTRATED LECTURE on "The Ascent of the Mat
terhorn," by Dr. David Starr Jordan; in the Assembly
Hall.
8:30 p. m. PHILOSOPHY CLUB, in the Women's Club House.
Subject: "The Ph.D. Mania." Open to members and
their friends.
WEDNESDAY, April 14
3:00 p.m. RESIDENT ALUMNAE TEA, in the Women's Club
house. Hostesses: Mrs. D. C. Gardner, Mrs. W. D.

Murray, Mrs. T. M. Williams.

Building.

O. M. Johnston, 4 Alvarado Row, Campus. Professor
Johnston will speak on "University Life in France."

meeting.
THURSDAY, April 15
7:00 p. m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen ;in the Me
morial Church.
7:15 p.m. WRANGLERS, in the Women's Clubhouse.
7:30 p. m. EUPTIRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY, in Room 12, Law
Building.
7:30 p. m. CHOIR REHEARSAL, in the Memorial Church.
FRIDAY, April 16
10:00 a.m. UNIVERSITY ASSEMBLY, in the Assembly Hall. Ad
dress on "The European Crisis and What It Means to

SATURDAY, April 17
2:00 p.m. INTERCOLLEGIATE TRACK MEET, Varsity Track,

OFFICIAL NOTICES
MEMORIAL CHURCH
University Preachers:
April 11—The Reverend D. Charles Gardner, Chaplain of Stanford
University.
April 18—The Reverend Merle Smith, Pastor of the First Methodist
Church, Pasadena.
April 25—The Reverend D. Charles Gardner.
Morning Chapel: 7:50 a.m., Monday to Friday, inclusive.
Half Hour of Music: 4:00 p.m., Sunday; 4:15 p.m., Tuesday; 7:00 p.m.,
Thursday.
TUESDAY EVENING MEETINGS
April 13—Dr. David Starr Jordan, an Illustrated Lecture on "The Ascent
of the Matterhorn."
April 20—Mr. Vachel Lindsay, a lecture-reading from his own verse.
April 27—Miss Alice Riggs Hunt, an address on "Personalities of the
European Labor World."
DEAN OF WOMEN
Miss Bradford will be at home informally, from 4 until 6, on Wednes
day afternoon, at her residence, 2 Lasuen Street.
