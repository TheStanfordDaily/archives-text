# OFFICIALS PICKED FOR MAY PRIMARIES
## 
### 
Two thousand six hundred seventy
three voters registered for the May
preferential primary election in Palo
Alto, according to the combined reports
of the three local registration deputies.
The majority registration by parties is
Republican. The polling places and
election clerks for the precincts in Palo
Alto, Stanford University, and Mayfield,
for the election date, May 4, were an
nounced today bv the county clerk.
Those announced for Stanford Univer
sity are as follows:
Polling place, Stanford Women's Club.
Inspector, Sidney D. Townley.
Judges, Emma E. Green, Grace B.
Clark.
Clerks. Laura M. Cross, Margaret C.
Coover, Myra Bergstrom.
