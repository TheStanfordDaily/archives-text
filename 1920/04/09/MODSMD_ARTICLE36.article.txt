# DANCE IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
A dance under the auspices of the
Waverley Club will be given in the
Women's Clubhouse in Palo Alto,
tonight, April 9. The admission
will be seventy-five cents a couple.
All campus people are invited to at
tend.
