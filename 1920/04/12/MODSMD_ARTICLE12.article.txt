# SCHUBERT TRYOUTS TO BE HELD TUESDAY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, musical director, has
requested that all those desiring to try
out for Schubert Club, be present to
morrow, Tuesday, at the regular meeting
of the club. The club holds its weekly
meetings in Room 84, at 5 p. m. After
the meeting, Mr. Allen will try out the
voices of those wishing to become mem
bers.
