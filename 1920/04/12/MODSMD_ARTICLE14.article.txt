# Fourteen Chairs Open In University Band
## 
### 
Professor K. W. Martin, Director of
the Stanford Band, announces that there
are fourteen chairs still to be filled in
the University Band for this quarter.
Tryouts will be held Tuesday, Wednes
day, and Thursday at 4:15 p..m in the
Hand Room, 2.87 Engineering Building.
There are places open for players of
all instruments, including drums, reed,
and brass. One unit of credit is given
for the work.
Those who arc successful in the try
outs will have the opportunity of see
ing the coming field and track meet at
Berkeley as members of the band.
