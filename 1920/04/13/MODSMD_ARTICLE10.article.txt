# Theta Sigma Phi Will Hold Doughnut Sale
## 
### 
Doughnuts fried before the eyes of
prospective buyers, home-made pastries
and other irresistibles will be offered by
Theta Sigma Phi, women's honorary
journalistic fraternity, for campus con
sumption Thursday morning from 8 to
12 between the Women's Clubhouse and
the Postoffice.
The proceeds of the sale will be de
voted to sending a delegate from the lo
cla chapter to the National Theta Sigma
Phi convention at Madison, 111.. April
29, 30, and May 1.
Arrangements are under the supervi
sion of Miss Ellen Ciflhoun, '20. Miss
Kirby Ingolsby, '21, Miss Ruth Elsinger,
'20, Miss Dorothy Hoskins,» '20, Miss
Her nice Frankenheimer, '20, and Miss
Ruth Searcy, '20.
