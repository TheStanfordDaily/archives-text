# "Sticky" Wilson Opens New Store at Fresno
## 
### 
"Stickey" Wilson's candy store has
been opened in Fresno, and is doing a
flourishing business. There were 25,000
visitors in the Fresno store Thursday
afternoon, the day of the opening, ac
cording to the manager, who was in Palo
Alto recently. Among the guests were
many city officials, who attended in a
group, led by the mayor. The Fresno
store is patterned largely after the one
in Palo Alto, and has a force of one
hundred employes.
