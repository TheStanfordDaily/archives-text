# Learned Returns From Trip to Washington
## 
### 
Mr. Henry B. Learned, lecturer in
History, has returned from a trip to
Washington, D. C., where he had been
summoned for a Supreme Court in
vestigation in connection with the ex
isting school system there, since he
is a trustee of the schools in the
District of Columbia. Congress looks
after and controls the schools in
Washington through a board of trus
tees who are noted men of national
reputation.
