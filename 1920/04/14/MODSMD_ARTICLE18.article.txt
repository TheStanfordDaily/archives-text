# Women's Conference Will Nominate Council Tonight
## 
### 
The new women's conference will
meet tonight for the first time in the
Woman's Clubhouse at 7:15. The mem
bers for the coming year were elected at
house meetings last Monday night; each
sorority sends one representative to the
organization, and Roble Hall three.
Nominations for the Women's Council
will be made at the meeting tonight.
The election for the Women's Council
comes on April 18. The day following
the election there will be an assembly of
the women of the University to install
the new officers.
