# Galli-Curci Will Give Concert Here April 29
## 
### 
Galli-Curci, the famous prima donna,
will give a concert the evening of April
29 in the Stanford Assembly Hall, ac
cording to announcement made today.
This is Galli-Curci's first appearance on
the campus. The afternoon preceding
the concert the noted singer will be a
guest of a Stanford committee. Follow
ing the concert Galli-Curci will attend
the ball which formally opens the new
Peninsula Hotel, in San Mateo.
