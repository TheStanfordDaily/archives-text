# ORGAN PROGRAM IS ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, organist, will render
the following program in a recital at the
Memorial Church, Tuesday, April 20,
at 4:15 p. m.
Fugue in E flat (St. Ann's) Bach
Largo, from the "New World Sym
phony" Dvorak
Minuet, No. 2, from the "Suite I'arle
sienne" Bizet
March, from "Tannhauser" Wagner
On Sunday, April 25, Hotlier Wismer,
violinist, will assist Mr. Allen in recital.
