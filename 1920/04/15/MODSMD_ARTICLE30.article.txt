# Cap and Gown Dance Will Be Given Friday
## 
### 
Cap and Gown will give an informal
campus _ dance Friday evening in the
Woman's Clubhouse. This is the third
of a series of dances which the women's
honor society is giving this year. Rey
nold's orchestra has been secured to fur
nish the variety of music which is neces
sary to make the party a success. Ad
mission is $1.10 a couple; 55 cents for
a single admission.
