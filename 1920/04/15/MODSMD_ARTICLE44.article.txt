# W.A.A. SETS RULES FOR ELIGIBILITY
## 
### 
In order to be eligible for any wom
en's athletic team, according to a ruling
passed by W. A. A. and the Faculty Ad
visory Board, the following schedule of
academic work is required of the play
ers :
Freshmen, 0 to 40 hours passed.
Sophomores, 40 to 90 hours passed.
Juniors, 90 to 135 hours passed.
Seniors, 135 to 180 hours passed.
Players must make 10 units each quar
ter during their Freshman year and 12
units each quarter during all the other
years to continue to be eligible.
