# Hoover Club Announces 690 Volunteer Members
## 
### 
The I loover Club has 690 members,
an increase of about seventy-five daily,
the club officials announced today. The
membership, finances, and plans are am
ply provided for, but workers arc need
ed urgently. There arc two kinds of
work to be done: office work, or mail
ing, typing, etc., and field work, or post
ing bills, making canvasses, or organizing
Hoover Clubs elsewhere. A chart will
be posted at the Hoover headquarters
in the Union on which all interested may
sign up for convenient hours.
