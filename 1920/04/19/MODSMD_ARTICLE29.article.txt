# 150 VOICES TO SING IN MEMORIAL CHORUS
## 
### 
Plans for the commemoration serv
ice's on Decoration Day include a chorus
of 150 voices made up of members of
the University Choir, the Morris Club,
and the community at large. The serv
ices, which will be rendered primarily
for the University faculty and student
body, will include Verdi' "Requiem."
Rehearsals will be held every Monday
at 8:45 p.m. in the Community House
after the meetings of the Morris Club.
Anyone who has had singing experience
is invited to try out at these rehearsals.
