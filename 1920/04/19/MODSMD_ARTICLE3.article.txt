# BERKELEY SECRETARY TO ADDRESS "Y" MEN
## 
### 
E. L. Devendorf, general secretary
of the Y. M. C. A. of the University
of California, will speak to Stanford
"Y" workers at a meeting of the Y.
M. C. A. cabinet which will be held
Tuesday night at the Union. At, this
time the electh n of olticers for the
coming year 1920-21 will be held.
The board of directors has accepted
the resignation of A. E. Worthy, '15,
who has been acting in the capacity
of general secretary since the time
of his graduation. Worthy will go
east next fall to complete his theolo
gical studies.
