# SERIES OF EXCHANGE DINNERS TO BE HELD
## 
### 
Five intersorority dinners will be held
this evening. Last year several of these
dinners were given, but those of this
evening are the first of a number of
"get togethers" that the sororities are
planning for this quarter. The following
houses will exchange: Alpha Phi and
Alpha Omicron Pi. Chi Omeea and Kap
pa Alpha Theta, Gamma Phi Beta and
Delta Gamma, Delta Delta Delta and
Sigma Kappa, and Pi Beta Phi and Kap
pa Kappa Gamma.
