# FRESHMEN TO WORK ON CARNIVAL TOMORROW
## 
### 
All freshmen men who have no class
es tomorrow afternoon have ben or
dered to report at Encina Gymnasium
at 1 o'clock, to aid in the construction
work for the Student Body Spring
Carnival. Upperclassmen in the vari
ous living groups are urged to see
that their freshmen report. Sopho
mores will also be on the job to see
that the freshmen are present. The
construction work will be in charge
of the Mechanical Engineers of the
University'. Freshmen are instructed
to take with them to the gymnasium
as many hammers and saws as pos-
Isible.
