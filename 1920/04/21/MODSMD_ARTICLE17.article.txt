# HOOVER CLUB WILL DISTRIBUTE STICKERS
## 
### 
The Stanford Hoover Club announces
that it now has for distribution a large
amount of Hoover literature. This mat
ter includes stickers which the Hoover
Club is distributing for use on letters.
The literature and stickers mav be had
at the club headquarters in the Stanford
Union. The club officials are urging
students to place a Hoover sticker 011
every envelope mailed from the campus
between now and the primary election
which takes place on May 4 and also to
put some of the Hoover literature in
side the letters mailed from the campus.
