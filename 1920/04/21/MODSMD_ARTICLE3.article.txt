# TWELVE MEN ELECTED TO G. AND M. SOCIETY
## 
### 
Twelve men have iust been elected to
membership into the G. and M. Society,
the men's geology and mining honor or
ganization. Plans for the initiation are
now being made. The men taken in in
clude John Potter, '18. M. S. Donaldson,
18, P. Crocker, '18, H. A. Chandler, '19,
11. A. Brown, '19, If. M. Oliver, '19.
R. (i. Rogers, '20, Robert Willis '20,
T. J. Ilamlyn, '19, T. F. Stipp, '21, R.
D. Copley, '21, and Richard Sherman,
'19.
