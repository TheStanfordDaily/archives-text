# FOUR LOCAL JOHNSON MEN ATTEND RALLY
## 
### 
Four delegates of the Johnson Repub
lican Club of the campus attended a
"Johnson for President" rally held last
evening at the high-school auditorium
in San Jose. The representatives of the
club were L. W. Hodges, '21, George
Hall, '19. James Bucklin, '20, and L. H.
Levy, '19.'
The speakers at the San Jose meeting
were Honorable John E. Richards, asso
ciate justice of the court of appeals, and
Frank R. Devlin, one of the organizers
of the Lincoln-Roosevelt league. Sev
eral thousand Johnson supporters were
present at the meeting.
