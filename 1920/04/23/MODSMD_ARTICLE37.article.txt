# Professor Townley and Family to go East Soon
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Sidney Dean
Townley and daughters Isabel and Jane
are now at Carmel, and will go on
soon through the South and Kast for
an extended trip. They plan to visit
their daughter Lucille in Washington,
and will lie at Madison, Wis., in June,
for the reunion of the class of 1890 of
the University of Wisconsin, of which
Professor Townley is a member. The
Townley house on the campus has been
rented to Mr. and Mrs. D. L. Rrync of
San Francisco. Byrne is a Stanford
alumnus.
