# Wild Flower Exhibit Supervised by Abrams
## 
### 
Dr. Lcroy Abrams, associate professor
in the Department of Botany at Stan
ford, is in charge of classifying the
different varieties of wild flowers at the
sixth annufil state wild flower show,
which opened in San Francisco yester
day afternoon. The exhibition which
includes approximately 2,000 varieties
of uncultivated flowers from all parts
of the state, is being held in the Italian
room of the St. Francis Hotel.
