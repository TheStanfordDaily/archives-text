# PINKSTON FOURTH IN A.A.U. TITLE DIVE
## 
### 
C! rence Pinkston, '22, was awarded
fourth place in the national A. A. U
diving championship, held last week in
Los Angeles. Clyde Swenson, former
holder of the national title successfully
maintained his right to continue to hold
it. He won the event with 153.91 points.
Pinkston made 140 points. The men
who came second and third made 142.9
and 142.2 points respectively.
