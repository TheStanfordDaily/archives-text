# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB TO HEAR TALK ON INDIA
## 
### 
Hindu costumes will be exhibited in
the course of a talk on India entitled
"White and Black." which will be given
by V. H. Mchta, '21, before the Cosmo
politan Club this evening at <S o'clock
in the Woman's Clubhouse. Mchta will
describe these Hindu customs which arc
particularly odd and interesting.
Mchta, a Hindu himself, is very
familiar with Indian life. He is a Me
chanical-Chemical Engineering major.
The public is cordially invited to attend
the lecture.
