# Women May Take Hygiene Make-up Examination
## 
### 
Women who did not take the hygiene'
examination last quarter or those who
failed the last examination will he given
an opportunity to make up the delin
quency by taking a test which will be
given at 4:15 in the Little Theater, May
5. The list of reference reading for the
examination is posted on the bulletin
board in Roble gymnasium.
