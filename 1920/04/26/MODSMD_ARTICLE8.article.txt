# THETA SIGMA PHI TO HAVE DOUGHNUT SALE
## 
### 
Doughnuts made-while-you-wait by
the women of Thcta Sigma Piii will
make their second appearance Thursday
morning from 10 to 12 in front of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Ruth Searcy, '20, was chosen by the
women's journalistic fraternity to rep
resent the local chapter at the national
Theta Sigma Phi convention at Madi
son, 111., April 29 and 30, and May 1.
Miss Searcy left for the east Saturday
and the proceeds of Thursday's sale will
be devoted to defraying the expenses of
her trip.
