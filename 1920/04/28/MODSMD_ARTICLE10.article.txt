# Cords Will Hold Sway At Senior Jolly-Up
## 
### 
Cords and soft, shirts will be the
costume for the Senior Jolly-up that is
to be held on next Saturday night at
the Women's Clubhouse. Complete
plans for the program of the evening
were made at, the committee meeting
held last night. Underclass men are
definitely barred from attendance at
the dance, but there are no restrictions
on the underclass women.
Jack Kegley's society jazz orchestra
will be here from IT. C. to furnish the
syncopation. Kegley has a five piece
orchestra that is one of the best on
the California campus. Dancing will
began at 8:15 and will continue until
12 o'clock. Admission will be seventy
five cents.
