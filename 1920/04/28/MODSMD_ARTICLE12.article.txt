# PROF. LEEBRICK WILL LECTURE ON SOCIAL PROGRESS TONIGHT
## 
### 
"The Dynamics of Social Progress" is
the subject of the address which Pro
fessor K. C. Leebrick, of the University
of California will deliver this evening
in Room 214 in the Economics building.
Ihe place of the lecture, which was
scheduled for the Littlij Theater, was
changed to room 214 because a large
crowd is anticipated. Prof. Leebrick is
said to be one of the most popular
speakers on the faculty of the Univer
sity of California.
The lecture this evening is the third of
a series of six to be given under the
auspices of the World's Problems For
um, an organization which is composed
of members of the Y. M. C. A. and Y.
W. C. A. •
