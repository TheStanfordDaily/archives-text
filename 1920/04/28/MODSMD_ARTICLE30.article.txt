# Hoover Club Will Be Represented at New York Meeting
## 
### 
The Stanford Hoover Club has re
ceived a telegram from the National
Hoover Republican Club announcing
a convention of representatives from
all American college clubs, who are
supporting Herbert Hoover for Presi
dent. The convention will be held at
the Harvard Club, New York. 011 May
1. Stanford is asked to either send
a representative or choose a resident
alumnus to take part in the meeting.
The convention will serve as a clear
ing house of ideas, and it is hoped that
a national collegiate campaign for
Hoover will emerge.
The Stanford Club will arrange to
have a prominent alumnus of New
York attend the conference and re
port back the action of the meeting.
