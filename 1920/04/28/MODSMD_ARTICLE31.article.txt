# ROTH WILL ADDRESS BIBLE STUDY MEETING
## 
### 
A large number of campus men will
meet in room 141 Encina, tonight, to
plan a permanent Bible study pro
gram to bo conducted on a voluntary
basis. The meeting will be at 8 o'clock
and approximately one hundred men
from fraternities, the halls, and Palo
Alto have been asked to be present,
according to Ray Ground. Comptroller
A. E. Roth will meet the men at the
hall tonight and will add a good word
for Bible study work on the campus.
