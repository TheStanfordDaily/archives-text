# Homer Spence, Ex-'13, Announces Candidacy
## 
### 
Homer Spence, '13, an attorney and
former first lieutenant of the 80th Field
Artillery, United States Army, lias an
nounced his candidacy for assemblyman
of the thirty-fifth District, which com
prises Alameda and part of East Oak
land. Spence is the law partner of
Judge L. R. Weinmann of Alameda. He
enlisted in (be Field Artillery in May,
1917, and was later transferred to the
Second Officers' Training Camp, where
he received his commission. He was
stationed in France, and was attached
to the French Artillery and was in the
battle of Chateau Thierry. He was also
an instructor at the officers' training
camp at Saumur, France.
