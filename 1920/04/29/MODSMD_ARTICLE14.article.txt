# STANFORD TRADITIONS WILL BE EXPLAINED
## 
### 
Stanford traditions will be the sub
ject of a training course for women
to be given for one hour weekly for
about six weeks beginning next week.
The course will be given by Miss
Berner, for many years Mrs. Stan
ford's private secretary. She has par
ticularly intimate knowledge of Stan
ford life progress from its earliest
days to the present time.
Any women interested in this work
are asked to communicate immediate
ly with Miss Ruth Bacon, at the Y.
W. C. A. club rooms.
