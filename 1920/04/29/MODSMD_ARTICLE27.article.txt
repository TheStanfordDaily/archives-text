# Professor Martin Will Write For War Book
## 
### 
Professor P. A. Martin, of the His
tory Department, has been asked to pre
pare a chapter on "Brazil and the War,"
for a translation of Simond's History
of the War, which is now being prepared
and which is to appear during the year.
The book is expected to have a wide
circulation throughout Latin America,
and will be published by Doubleday,
Page & Co.
