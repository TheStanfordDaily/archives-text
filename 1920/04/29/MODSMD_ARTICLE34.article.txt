# HOOVER CLUB WANTS 250 GUARDS AT POLLS
## 
### 
Two hundred and fifty Stanford stu
dents have been asked by the Hoover
Club to guard the polls at San Francis
co Tuesday afternoon, May 3. Arrange
ments have been made whereby all the
expenses of those desiring to help in the
guarding will be paid. Those wishing to
go may signify their intentions at the
Stanford Hoover Club in the Union
Building.
