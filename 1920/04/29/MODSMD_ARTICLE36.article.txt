# Hunkin-Lee Betrothal Formally Announced
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. John Lee of Palo Alto
announce the engagement of their daugh
ter, Miss Lola Lee. '19, to Milward Staf
ford Hunkin of Cleveland, Ohio. Miss
Lee is a member of Kappa Kappa Gam
ma sorority. Her fiance is now attend
ing Stanford, having transferred from
Cornell University. He is a member of
Sigma Chi fraternity.
