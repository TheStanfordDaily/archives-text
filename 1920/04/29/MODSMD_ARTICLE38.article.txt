# Mayor Rolph Will Give Address Next Monday
## 
### 
Mayor James Rolph of San Francis
co and a number of party speakers will
give addresses on the evening of May
3 at 8 o'clock in the Woman's Club
house. The topics for the evening will
deal with issues of the presidential cam
paign, as well as special features of the
California primary. Mayor James Rolph
is one of the chief supporters of the
Tohnson campaign in California. Both
the campus and the Palo Alto public are
invited.
