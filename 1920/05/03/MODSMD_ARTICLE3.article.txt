# MAYOR ROLPH TO TALK AT JOHNSON MEETING
## 
### 
Mayor James Rolph of San Francisco
will speak on "Vital Issues of the
Campaign," this evening at 8:00
o'clock at the Women's Clubhouse.
Another prominent San Francisco
speaker will be in the party. The pub
lic is invited to attend the lecture.
Tlie address will be given under the
auspices of the Johnson Club.
Mayor Rolph is one of the foremost
supporters of Johnson on the Pacific
Coast and has delivered many speeches
in his behalf, lie will explain the needs
of the presidency for the coming four
years and will give his reasons for sup
porting California's senator in the com
ing election.
Arrangements have been made by the
fohnson Republican Club to bring a num
ber of ex-service men from the base
hospital to the address. If. L. Levy,
the manager of the club, today an
nounced that he expects a number of
people from the peninsula cities to at
teend the meet inc.
