# MUSIC CLUB TRYOUTS TOMORROW AFTERNOON
## 
### 
All student musicians interested in
classical music are invited to try-out
before the members of the Stanford
Music Club, tomorrow afternoon at 4
o'clock, in the Women's Clubhouse.
Plans are now being made to have
this club join a national musical honor
fraternity. Persons who join the club
this quarter will be counted among
the charter members of this chapter.
