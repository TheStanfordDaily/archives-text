# GRADUATE WILL TEACH IN COGSWELL COLLEGE
## 
### 
Miss Elizabeth Lormer, '18, has been
elected to teach bookkeeping and math
ematics in the Cogswell Polytechnic
College of San Francisco, according to
an announcement made this morning at
the office of the University Appointment
Secretary.
Miss Lormer, while a student at Stan
ford University, took a prominent lead
in Women's Conference activities, and
is a member of Phi Beta Kappa. She
received her A. B. in Mathematics and
German in 1917, and her Master of
Arts degree the following year.
Miss Lormer taught commercial sub
jects in the Fair Oaks High School last
vear, and will resume her new duties in
San Francisco next fall.
