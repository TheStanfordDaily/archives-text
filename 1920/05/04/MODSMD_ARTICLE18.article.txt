The following regulations apply to all
candidates for graduation in June, 1920:
1. The final list of candidates will be
posted May 31. After this date no
name can be added to the list, and no
person not on the list will be considered
as a candidate in June.
2. After the final quarter reports have
been handed in Thursday, June 17, no
candidate will be allowed before Com
mencement Day to remove any "condi
tion" or to make up any "incomplete"
work.
