# C.E. Pinkston, '22, Wins P.A.A. Diving Contest
## 
### 
Clarence E. Pinkston, '22, won the P.
A. A. fancy diving contest which was
held in the Sutro Baths of San Fran
cisco last Saturday evening, with a total
score of 143 points. Patterson, of the
Olympic Club, was second, with 112
points.
Drake, varsity 100 yard swimmer, took
second place in the 100-yard junior
event.
