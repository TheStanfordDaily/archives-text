# STANFORD WOMEN IN COMMUNITY PROGRAM
## 
### 
Several Stanford people were on the
program at the Community House Sun
day for the Sunday hour of music.
Mrs. Samuel Swayze Seward, Jr., was
the soloist, and gave several delightful
numbers, lie was accompanied by Mrs.
E. C. Franklin. Anna Franklin, '21,
who is well known to Stanford Memorial
Church goers, assisted in the program.
