# Miss Barker's School To Give Supper Dance
## 
### 
The sophomore class of Miss bark
er's school in Palo Alto will give .1 sup
per dance Friday evening, May 7.
Supper will be served at 5 o'clock
and will be followed by dancing on the
tennis court. One dollar admission will
be charged each couple, exclusive of
75 cents for the supper.
The money thus raised will be used
to pay for the publication of the Echo,
the school paper.
