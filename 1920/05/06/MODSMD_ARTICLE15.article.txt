# "RUMPELSTILTZKIN" IS Y.W.C.A. BENEFIT
## 
### 
Under the leadership of Marjorie
Grant, '22. the Skylark Corps of Girl Re
serves will present the fairy play ,"Rum
pelstiltzkin," on Saturday evening May
15. in the Little Theatre. Tickets are
on sale at the Community House in
Palo Alto, and by members of the corps
for 25 cents.
There are now more than 115 girls en
rolled in Girl Reserve groups under the
leadership of Stanford Y. W. C. A.
members. Two corps have been estab
lished in Redwood City, one in Mavfield,
and five in Palo Alto.
