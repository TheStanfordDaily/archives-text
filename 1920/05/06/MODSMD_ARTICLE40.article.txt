# PROF. CUBBERLEY GOES TO HOSPITAL
## 
### 
Professor E. P. Cubberley, head of
the Education Department, was taken to
the Lane Clinic Hospital iii San Fran
cisco on Wednesday afternoon. He has
been suffering from stomach trouble,
and friends express anxiety about him.
