# Freshman-Sophomore Baseball Fight is Set For Monday Afternoon
## 
### 
Freshmen and sophomore organiza
tions will compete for honors in the
annual baseball fight Monday at 4 p.m.
The baseball fight is the last formal
contest of the year between the under
classes.
Captain Kallam of the varsity nine,
will return from the north in time to
officiate during the early innings of
the game. At his discretion he will
throw a marked ball into the air, and
the class returning it to the varsity
captain at the time and place to be an
nounced later will be pronounced as
winner.
Neither team Is expected to leave
the campus this year.
