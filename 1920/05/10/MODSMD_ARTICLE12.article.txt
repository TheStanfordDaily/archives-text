# Cosmopolitan Club Will Hear Talk by Dr. Jordan
## 
### 
"The Present Mexican Situation"
will be the topic of an address by
Chancellor Jordan at the Women's
Clubhouse tonight, at 8 o'clock. The
meeting will be under the auspices
of the Cosmopolitan Club. A short
business meeting of the club will fol
low.
