# TWO NEW VOCATIONAL SPEAKERS ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Two more names have added to the
list of speakers for the Vocational Con
ference to be held in the Woman's Club
house on May 22.
Anna G. Fraser, '00, vice-principal of
the-Oakland High School, will speak on
the opportunities for women in the
teaching profession. Miss Fraser is
known as one of the best instructors in
history in the state.
Physical education as a profession will
be discussed by Signa Haglethorne, su
perintendent of physical education in the
San Francisco public schools. Miss Ha
glethorne has been active in physical ed
ucation development in the bay cities for
several years.
