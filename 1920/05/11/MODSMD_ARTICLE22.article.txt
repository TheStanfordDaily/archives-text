# BLIND RELIEF DRIVE NETS MORE THAN $100
## 
### 
Somewhat over $100 was taken in bv
the sale of tags at 15 cents each in the
permanent Blind Relief drive carried on
on the campus today. This gives Stan
ford a permanent membership in the
Permanent Blind Relief Association.
The funds collected will be devoted
to the alleviation of conditions among
those permanently blinded in the late
world war.
