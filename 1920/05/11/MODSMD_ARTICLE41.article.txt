# Women's Faculty Club Elects New Officers
## 
### 
New officers of the Women's Faculty
Club, who will go into office in the fall
quarter, were elected at a meeting of
the club held yesterday in the Women's
Clubhouse. Those elected were: Presi
dent, Mrs. George Hempl; vice-presi
dent, Mrs. R. W. Doane; secretary, Mrs.
Theodore Hoover; treasurer, Mrs. W.
D. Briggs; member at large, Mrs. W.
H. Carruth; representative on the Club
house Board. Mrs. C. B. Whittier.
