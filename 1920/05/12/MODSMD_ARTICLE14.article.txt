# El Camino Club Takes Charter in Theta Chi
## 
### 
When the members of the El Camino
Club became members of the Theta Chi
Fraternity last week-end, the number of
national college fraternities on the Stan
ford campus was increased to twenty
two. The new organization is Alpha
Epsilon of Theta Chi, and contains 27
active members. The faculty members,
Dr. M. S. Wildman and J. 13. Liggett,
an instructor in the Engineering Depart
ment, arc alumni members of the fra
ternity.
