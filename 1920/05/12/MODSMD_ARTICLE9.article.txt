# S. A. Howard Goes North For R.0.T.C. Inspection
## 
### 
Captain S. A. Howard, commandant
of the Infantry R. O. T. C. unit, leaves
Friday for a sixteen-day tour of Mon
tana, Washington, Idaho, and Wyoming.
The trip is in the nature of an inspection
tour of the military work and R. O. T.
C. companies in the universities of the
states visited. Before coming to Stan
ford, Captain Howard was the District
Inspector of the northwestern states.
