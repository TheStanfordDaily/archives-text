# JUNIORS WILL GIVE DANCE ON SATURDAY
## 
### 
Representative track and tennis men
from the Northwest universities entered
in the Pacific Coast Conference meet
will be the guests of the Junior class
at a track dance to be held in their honor
Saturday evening. The class is cooper
ating with Skull and Snakes in provid
ing for their entertainment*
L. W. McDermott, '17, will lead the
orchestra which will furnish the music
for the occasion. There will be a num
ber of tag dances in the course of the
evening. It is urged by the committee
in charge that women come in groups.
Admission to the dance will be $1.10 for
"queeners." Single admission will be
75 cents.
