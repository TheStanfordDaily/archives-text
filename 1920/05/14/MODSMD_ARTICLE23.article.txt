The following regulations apply to
all candidates for graduation in June,
1920:
1. The final list of candidates will
be posted May 25th. After this date
no name can be added to the list, and
no person not on the list will be con
sidered as a candidate in June.
2. After the final quarter reports
have been handed in Thursday, June
17th, no candidate will be allowed
before Commencement Day to remove
any "condition" or to make up any
"incomplete" work.
