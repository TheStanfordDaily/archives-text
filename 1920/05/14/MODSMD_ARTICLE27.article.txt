# ADVERTISING CLASS HEARS TALK ON COLOR
## 
### 
Charles W. Duncan, general manager
of Foster & Kleiser, San Francisco
outdoor advertisers, delivered a lec
ture to W. P. Staniford's class in ad
vertising, this week, taking for his
subject "Color in Advertising."
Several charts and practical illustra
tions which had been executed by
such artists as Morris Dell Mue,
Harold von Schmidt and Maynard
Dixon were exhibited by the speaker
and the cardinal facts regarding color
importance explained.
