# Stanford Music Club Holds Special Tryout
## 
### 
For the benefit of those who were not
able to try-out at the last meeting of the
Stanford Music Club, a special try-out
meeting will be held on Tuesday after
noon at 4 o'clock in the Woman's Club
house. 112
Students desiring to try-out are re
quested to have two selections prepared.
All old members arc urged to be pres
ent for a special business meeting will
follow the try-outs.
