# Unitarians Will Hear Miss Edith Mirrielees
## 
### 
At the Unitarian Church of P;ilo Alto
on Sunday morning at 11 o'clock, Miss
E. R. Mirrielees, of the English depart
ment of the University, will make the
address of the morning.
Miss Mirrielees is very well known
and honored as a result of her work in
France during the war.
The public is cordially invited to the
services.
