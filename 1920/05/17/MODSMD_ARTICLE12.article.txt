# SUNDAY SCHOOLS TO HOLD MEETINGS HERE
## 
### 
The annual convention of the Cali
fornia State Sunday School Association,
northern section, will he held here for
three days beginning this afternoon. The
day sessions of the convention will he
conducted in the Palo Alto churches,
and the evening meetings will he held in
Memorial Church. The presiding officer
will be the Honorable Curtis D. Wilbur,
judge of the California Supreme Court
and brother of President Wilbur of
Stanford. The convention includes 400
registered delegates, and it is expected
that the number of visitors will range
between 1.200 and 1,500. They will be
addressed by several prominent men and
women of national reputation.
