# CAMPUS COUPLE WED AT PALO ALTO HOME
## 
### 
Katherine Huntington ,'lO, and David
C. Elliott, 'IS, were married at the
home of the bride in Palo Alto Satur
day evening by Dr. Gardner. After
the ceremony, which was attended by
several campus friends of the couple,
Mr. and Mrs. Elliott left for a honey
moon in the southern part of the state
and Yosemite. They will make their
homo in San Francisco. The bride is
a member of Chi Omega sorority, and
the proom. who is the son of Registrar
and Mrs. O. L. Elliott, belongs to Alpha
Sigma Chi.
