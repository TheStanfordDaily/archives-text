# ANOTHER WIN FOR THE CARDINAL
## 
### 
OTANFORD made another win for the week-end, when P. F. Erickson, '21,
T. E. Bowen, '23, and C. J. Crobaugh, '20, won the debate with the Uni
versity of Southern California by a two to one decision. Although losing the
debate with Occidental, the Cardinal team staged a comeback with the U. S.
C. squad. Much of the credit is due Coach Miller McClintock who has worked
steadily and faithfully with the team. The qustion involved"* mandatory
of Mexico and it was particularly difficult on account of the rapidly changing
conditions in Mexico at the present time. It is with great pleasure that The
Daily Palo Alto takes this opportunity to congratulate the men who de
fended the University so ably.
