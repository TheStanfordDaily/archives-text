# BOOKSTORE MANAGER RESIGNS HIS POSITION
## 
### 
Guy C. Miller, '02, manager of the
Stanford Bookstore since October, 1913,
has resigned his position and will sever
his connection with the store. His res
ignation was accepted by the Board of
Directors Friday night. Before taking
up the management of the Bookstore he
was on the Palo Alto postal force, which
he joined in 1906 as the first mail-carrier
in Palo Alto.
