# DR. BRANNER WILL GIVE NEGRO READINGS
## 
### 
Dr. J. C. Branner, president emeritus
of the University, will entertain with a
number of stories in negro dialect at the
Community House in Palo Alto tonight.
Dr. Branner gave similar readings at a
recent community meeting, which were
so well received that he was asked to
repeat the performance. lie has ar
ranged a new program of "darky stories"
