# University Organist To Give Two Recitals
## 
###

will give an organ recital in the Me
morial Church on Tuesday, May 25, at
4:15, in which lie will repeat the organ
numbers which arc to he played at the
Sunday recital. Tlie Sunday program
will include two numbers by Schubert
Club.

will be as follows :
