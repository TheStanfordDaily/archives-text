# Y.W.C.A. ELECTS ADVISORY BOARD
## 
### 
An election of the Advisory Board of
the Y. W. C. A. was held recently in
which Mrs. Jerome Thomas was made
chairman to succeed Mrs. Marion Rice
Kirkwood. Other new members elected
to the Board are Miss Yolanda Allen of
the Physical Training Department, Mrs.
E. A. Cottrell, Miss Elizabeth Thomp
son, Mrs. N. A. Cecil, and Mrs. J. W.
Mitchell (honorary). C/A members re
tained on the Board are Mrs. R. L.
Green, Mrs. P. C. Rockwell, Mrs. Kirk
wood. Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur (hon
orary), and Miss Harriet Bradford
(honorary).
The Advisory Board of the Y. W. C.
A. is composed of a group of women in
terested in istudeint activities and in
Y. W. C. A. work who meet with the
Y. W. C. A. Student Cabinet for the
purpose of advising and consulting with
them in regard to the various branches
of women's work on the campus.
