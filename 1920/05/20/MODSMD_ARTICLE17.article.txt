# Two Annuals Are Taken From the Quad Office
## 
### 
A 1917 Illio, the annual year book of
the University of Illinois, was stolen
from the Quad office this morning. The
book is the property of an Illinois grad
uate now attending the University who
had loaned it to the Quad editor. The
book is valued highly by the owner as a
keepsake and cannot be replaced. A
1920 Quad was also taken.
Quad Editor F. L. McElroy stated
that if the books are returned immedi
ately no questions will be asked.
