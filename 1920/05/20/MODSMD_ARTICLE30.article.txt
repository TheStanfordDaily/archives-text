# Positions in Automobile Company Offered Men
## 
### 
1,.. I. Thompson, '21, and C. E. How
ell, '23, have secured an agency from
an eastern auto accessory company
and are in a position to offer places to
some men on the campus as agents.
Mn who take up the work can do it in
any territory that they desire. The
work is of such nature that they are
assured of a good return for their
work. Those wishing to investigate
the matter may see Thompson or How
ell in Encina.
