# STANFORD GRADUATE HAS LEADING PART IN THE BIG PAGEANT
## 
### 
George Shaner, '07, will take one
of the leading parts in the Los Gatoa
pageant, "The Californian," in which
several other Stanford graduates will
participate. "The Californian" is a
dramatic historical pageant in two
movements and an interclude, by Wil
bur Hall, depicting the history of the
Golden State in eight stage pictures.
Los Gatos expects to entertain about
ten thousand people and will build
bleachers to accommodate the andi
ence at the natural outdoor amphi
theater where the spectacle will be
given, June 16. An old-fashioned ber
becue will be held in the late after
noon. This is the second annual page
ant presented at Los Gatos.
