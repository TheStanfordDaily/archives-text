# LAST MINUTE NEWS
## 
### United Press United Press United Press United Press 
PITTSBURGH, Pa., May 24.—A
score of persons was injured, one is
missing, when two houses were de
stroyed in a bomb explosion here to
day.

WASHINGTON, D. C., May 24.—The
congressional resolution declaring
peace with German and Austria was
brought to the White House by the
Senate Messenger.

WASHINGTON, D. C., May 24—
Prsident Wilson in a message to Con
gress asked that this country accept
a mandate over Armenia.

WASHINGTON, D. C., May 24.—The
State Department announced the re
ceipt of a dispatch from the American
consul at Chihuahua, saying that
George Miller, a British subject, held
by Francisco Villa, had been released.
No details of how the release was
effected have been received.
