# REQUIEM TO BE PART OF MEMORIAL PROGRAM
## 
### 
Verdi's "Requiem" by the University
choir, assisted by thirty instrumentalists
from the San Francisco Symphony, will
be presented in special dedicative and
memorial services Sunday morning in
tribute to the immortal sixty-nine Stan
ford men who fell on the battlefields of
Europe during the world
Services this year will differ from pre
ceding ones in that music is to play the
feature role. The program will contain
no speeches.
By the courtesy of the Musicians'
Union, eight Stanford students will be
included in the orchestra.
The best available soloists will be en
gaged for the solo parts. The music is
under the direct supervision of Warren
D. Allen.
