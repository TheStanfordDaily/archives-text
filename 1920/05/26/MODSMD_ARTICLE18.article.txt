# GUNS HONOR BRITISH QUEEN'S BIRTHDAY
## 
### United Press 
LONDON, May 26. —Royal salutes
wore fired today at London, Windsor
and naval and military stations in
honor of N the 53d birthday of Queen
Mary. The Queen, who was formerly
Princess May of Teck. married KUig
Goorge in J 893.
