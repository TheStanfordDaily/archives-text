# THREE ARE KILLED AS WOMEN START WORK
## 
### United Press United Press 
BRISTOL, May 28. —Governor Beck
man ordered out militia to quell the
rioting between strikers and police
men.

BRISTOL, K. 1., May 28.—Three men
were shot and doens were injured when
4.500 strikers clashed with guards at the
National India Rubber plant. The light
ing continued all morning. The trouble
started over the arrival of women clerks
and mechanics, who reported for work
sa usual. One woman attacked a girl
clerk and a free-for-all tight followed.
