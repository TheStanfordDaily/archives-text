# TWO ADVERTISERS WILL SPEAK HERE NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
J. H. Eustace, Pacific Coast represent
ative for the Curtis Publishing Co., will
deliver a lecture to the advertising class
Tuesday on "National Advertising."
The second lecture to be given by
John J. Cuddy, will take place next
Wednesday. Mr. Cuddy is identified
with the H. K. McCann Co., a national
advertising agency. He will talk on
"The Advertising Manager."
