# FREMONT POST HAS LARGE WOMEN'S UNIT
## 
### 
Besides having the largest member
ship of any post on the California
coast line between San Francisco and
Los Angeles, Fremont Post, No. 02,
American Legion, also has one of the
largest units of women members.
Thirty women, twenty-six of whom
were army or navy nurses, and Tour
in the yeomanette service during the
war, hold membership in the post.
