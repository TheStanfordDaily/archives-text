# LAST MINUTE NEWS
## 
### United Press 
GRAND JUNCTION. Colo., May 31.
—An engineer and fireman were
drowned today when the Denver and
Rio Grande freiqht train slipped into
the Gunnison River, near Dominquez,
as the river bank gave way under
the heavy train. The high water had
softened the right of way.
MARSHALLTOWN. la., May 31.—
Forty-two persons were injured, four
seriously, when a gun in the hands
of a memorial day firing squad at
Lipcomhe, near here, exploded. The
nun barrel was shattered by the ex
plosion.
