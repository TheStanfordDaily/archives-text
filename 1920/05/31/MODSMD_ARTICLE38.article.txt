# OREGON IS WINNER IN NORTHERN MEET
## 
### 
By scoring 36 points the University
of Oregon won the annual Pacific
Northwest Conference track meet, held
at Pullman, Washington, Saturday
afternoon. Track stars of the colleges
of Washington, Oregon, Idaho, and
Montana participated.
Oregon Agricultural College, with 33
points, was second; the University of
Idaho, with 30 points, was third; the
University of Washington,25, fourth;
Washington State College, 22, fifth;
University of Montana, 13, sixth; and
Whitman College, 5 points, seventh.
