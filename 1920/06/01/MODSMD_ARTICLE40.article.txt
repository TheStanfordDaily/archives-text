# THETA SIGMA PHI TO INITIATE WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
The initiation of members into Theta
Sigma Phi, the women's national hon
orary journalistic fraternity, will take
place Wednesday, at 5 p.m., at the
Women's Clubhouse. A banquet at
Roble Hall will follow the initiation.
Ruth Searcy, '20, who has recently
returned from the national convention
at the University of Wisconsin, will
give a full report of the events that
took place.
The following will be initiated Wed
nesday: Florence Hocking, '22, Elaine
Adrian, '22, Margaret McCall, '21, Mary
Jane Clark, '21, Bertha Vaughan, '22,
and Mildred Thompson, '21.
