# Stanford Glee Club Will Meet Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Glee Club
have been asked to meet at the Delta
Chi House tomorrow night at 7:30
o'clock. The purpose of the meeting is
to elect officers for the coming year.
Plans will be discussed relative to next
year's program. It is understood that
a trip will be taken during next Christ
mas vacation. This question will be
decided upon, and steps taken to insure
successful action on the matter.
