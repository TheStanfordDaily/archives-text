# EUPHRONIANS TO HOLD BANQUET THIS EVENING
## 
### 
Members of Euphronia Debating
Society will hold a banquet this even
ing in honor of twenty-third anniver
sary of the club. The banquet will
be held at 7:00 o'clock at the Ramona
Cafeteria in Palo Alto.
Dr. Wilbur has been secured by the
club to be the speaker of the even
ing. Professor A. M. Cathcart, a
former president of the society, and
Debating Coach Miller L. McClintoek
will also speak.
All active and former members of
the club are urged to be present at the
banquet and to make table reserva
tions through the president of the so
ciety.
