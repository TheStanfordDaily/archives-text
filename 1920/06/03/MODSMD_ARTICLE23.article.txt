# Five Women Initiated By Theta Sigma Phi
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women's honorary
journalistic fraternity, held its spring
Initiation last evening, admitting Mary
Jane Clark, Florence Hocking, Marga
ret McCall, Mildred Thompson, '21's,
and Bertha Vaughn, '22, to member
ship.
After the initiation ceremony, a
banquet was given in honor of the in
itiates at Roble Hall. A business meet
ing followed the banquet, in which
Bernice Frankenheimer, '20, was
elected vice-president; Mildred Thomp
son. '21, archivist, and Florence Hock
ing '22. exhibit collector for the Na
tional Convention.
