# Dr. Browne Leaves For Portland P.C.C. Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. A. D. Browne left today for
Portland where he will preside over
the special session of the Pacific Ath
letic Conference which will be held in
the Imperial Hotel of that city, Friday
and Saturday of Ihis week. The con
ference will consider questions rela
tive to division of receipts at football
games, training tables, and coaching
policy. The Physical Director will be
back on the campus by next Tuesday
morning.
