# CLEAN UP WEEK
## 
### 
pHIEF UNIVERSITY HOUSEKEEPER ROTH is carrying on his spring house
cleaning this week, and since housekeeping lies within a woman's province,
we want to assist him a little in this enterprise if wo can. Weeds along the
side of the Row, by Encina, in back yards of faculty houses, scattered papers
around the campus, old posters put up several weeks ago and unremoved —
all such things must go this week. Comptroller Roth will send wagons around
on Monday to collect all the rubbish, so that will leave this week-end to clean
up in. Pride in the Uuniversity grounds has always been emphasized at
Stanford, and all houses should unite with the authorities In making spring
housecloaning week a success.
