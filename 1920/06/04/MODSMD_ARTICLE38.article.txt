# FOUR CAMPUS DANCES WILL MARK WEEK-END
## 
### 
Four campus dances will be held this
week-end two on each night. Phi Kap
pa Psi will hold a tennis court informal
tonight. Chi Psi will give a one o'clock.
On Saturday night Roble Club will have
its third informal. On the same night
an informal representative dance will be
given by Kappa Alpha Tbeta.
