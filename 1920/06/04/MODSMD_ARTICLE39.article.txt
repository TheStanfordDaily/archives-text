# Rare Bird Skin Collection Presented to University
## 
### 
A collection of 500 bird skins, some of
which are representative of species be
coming extinct in this part of the coun
try, lias been presented the University
by James N. Proctor, '07. The collec
tion has been in the process of making
for a number of years, Proctor starting
it as a boy, and is very complete.
Professor J. O. Snyder, who has been
arranging the collection, says of it,"The
collection contains many specimens
which form a real addition to our study
series. A number of rare specimens are
included."
Some of the rarest specimens will be
placed in the Museum collection, while
the others will be used to increase the
study collection used by students of the
Zoology Department.
