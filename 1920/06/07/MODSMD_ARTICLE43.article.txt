# ALASKA PREPARES FOR POLITICAL CAMPAIGN
## 
### 
JUNEAU, Alaska, May 14—(By
Mail to United Press) —Alaska is pre
paring for another political campaign
the coming fall when a representative
to congress will be elected. This
despite the fact that two previous
elections led to contests, one of which
is still pending before the congression
al committee on elections.
But for all that, Alaska has the ex
citement incident to holding elections.
In addition to the delegate to congress
Alaska this fall will elect an attorney
general, four members of the territori
al senate and sixteen members of the
territorial house of representatives.
