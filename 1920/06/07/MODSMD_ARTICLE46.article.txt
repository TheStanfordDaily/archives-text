# PAGEANT TO BE HELD AT LOS GATOS IN JUNE
## 
### 
On June 18 and 19, a pageant named
/'The Californian" will be given by the
people of Los Gatos in their open-air
theatre, under the direction of Wilbur
Hall the author.
Work was begun on the rehoarsala
and everything is progressing perfect
ly, according to the Los Gatoa Page
ant Association. The out-door stage
,upon which the pageant, will be pre
sented is considered to be one of the
most beautiful of its type in America.
