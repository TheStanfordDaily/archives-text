# NEW ACADEMIC COMMITTEES OF FACULTY NAMED
## Advisory Board, Executive, Graduate Study Committees Appointed by Council 
### 
Academic committees of the faculty
for the coming year were announced
through the President's office today. Of
these committees, the Advisory Board,
Executive Committee, and the Graduate
Study Committee are elected by the
Academic Council through a preferen
tial ballot without nominations. The re
maining committees are elected by the
Executive Committee upon nomination
by the Nominations Committee.
The Administration Committees of the
faculty are appointed by the President,
who will report his appointments for
the coming year to the Board of Trus
tees at its next regular meeting.
