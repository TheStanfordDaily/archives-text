# FOUR KILLED WHEN TRAIN HITS TRUCK
## 
### United Press 
CLEVELAND, Ohio, June 7.—Four
men were killed and twenty injured,
some of them perhaps fatally, when an
Eric passenger train crashed into a truck
load of laborers today at Rarborton,
near here.
