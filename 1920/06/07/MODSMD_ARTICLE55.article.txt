# Euphronia Holds Twenty-Sixth Annual Banquet
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society held its
twenty-sixth banquet Thursday eve
ning. Addresses by President Wilbur,
Professor A. M. Cathcart of the Law
Department, and M. L. McClintock, the
debating coach, together with shorter
speeches by Senators L. W. Hodges, P.
F. Eriekson, and J. M. Cuddy, made up
the program of the evening.
The following officers were elected
for the fall quarter of next year: Pres
ident. G. W. Burch '22; vice-president.
S. C. Anderson '23, and secretary,treas
urer, Joseph Mandl, '20.
