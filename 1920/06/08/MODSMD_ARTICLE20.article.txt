# Laura Durksen, '21, Tells of Engagement
## 
### 
Miss Laura Durksen, '21, announced
her engagement to William Kircher, '20,
at a dinner last evening at Roble Hall.
Miss Durksen is a member of Roble
Club and registers from San Jose. Mr.
Kircher will receive his degree from the
education department this year. He is
a member of Pbi Beta Kappa and En
cina Club, and is from Bellingham.
