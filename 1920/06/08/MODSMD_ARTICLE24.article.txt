# INTERFRATERNITY CONFERENCE ELECTS OFFICERS FOR FALL
## 
### 
Officers for the Interfraternity Con
ference for the next autumn quarter
were chosen at a meeting of that or
ganization last night. C. C. Crosby, '19,
a member of Kappa Sigma, was elected
president, and H. I. Mitchell, '20, a mem
ber of Theta Delta Chi, was elected sec
retary-treasurer.
The meeting last night was the last
meeting of the year. Officers retiring
arc: President, A. A. Curtice, '19, a
member of Delta Upsilon, and Secre
tary-Treasurer, F. B. Bothwell, '20, a
member of Phi Kappa Sigma.
