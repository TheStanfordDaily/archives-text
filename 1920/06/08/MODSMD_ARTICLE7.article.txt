# WAR MEMORIAL DRIVE REPORTS ARE NEEDED
## 
### 
Chairman B. C. Wohlford, of the war
memorial drive committee, requests that
reports from all the living groups be
handed to him immediately. To date
but five of the living groups have re
ported. Of the five, Alpha Delta Phi
has made the largest contribution, with
$220 pledged.
The reports from all the houses should
he in before Wednesday in order that
the total of donations may be made
known by the end of the week.
