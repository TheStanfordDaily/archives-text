# THIRTEEN ARE KILLED IN N.Y. CENTRAL WRECK
## 
### 
By United Press
SCHENECTADY, N. Y„ June 19.—
Thirteen were killed and twenty-seven
were injured when the New York Cen
tral express crashed into the rear of
a passenger train near here today. The
express train was going fifty miles an
hour when it struck the passenger train,
which was stalled at a crossing. The
two rear sleepers telescoped.
