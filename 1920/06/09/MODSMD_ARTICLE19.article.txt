# San Francisco Ad Man Speaks Before Class
## 
### 
Frederick S. Nelson, advertising man
ager of the O'Conner-Moffat Co., of
San Francisco, addressed Mr. Stam
ford's advertising class this morning,
taking for his subject, "The Planning
of the Retail Campaign." Mr. Nelson
discussed the thorough analysis of vari
ous fields and emphasized the need of
accuracy, especially in advertising capy.
He summed his lecture in his last sen
tence, "He brief, have faith, and use
thought."
