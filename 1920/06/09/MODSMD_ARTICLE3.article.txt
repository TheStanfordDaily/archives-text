# Roble Pool to Remain Open Examination Week
## 
### 
Roble Swimming Pool will be kent
open for use from 4:30 to 5:30 during
examination week. All gymnasium
clashes will be ended on Friday, but the
department is anxious to have as many
women as possibly can, come out for at
least an hour of recreation, cither ten
nis or swimming, on the days of ex
aminations.
