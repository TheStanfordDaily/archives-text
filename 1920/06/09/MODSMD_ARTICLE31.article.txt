# AMES WILL PREPARE STANFORD HANDBOOK
## 
### 
A publication combining both the
"Frosh Bible" and a handbook contain
ing information of interest to the stu
dent body will be printed this summer
under the direction of the Student Man
ager. The Executive Committee voted
last night that the Student Manager be
instructed to compile this publication to
be known as the "Stanford Handbook."
for distribution to the students next fall.
In addition to the usual admonitions
to freshmen the new book probably will
contain such items of interest and gen
eral information as a copy of the Student
Body Constitution and lists of officers
who now occupy executive positions on
the various student body and campus or
ganization committees.
