# ARCHIE F. BINNS, '22, WILL EDIT "CARDINAL"
## 
### 
Archie F. Binns, '22, was elected as
next editor of the Cardinal at a meeting
of the English Club held yesterday at
the Woman's Clubhouse. Binns has
been on the stafT of the Cardinal for
some time. Officers for the fall quarter
were also chosen. They are: President,
Dorothy Hoskins, '21 ; vice-president, D.
W. Evans, '21; secretary, Harriet Hunt,
'21 ; treasurer, Elizabeth Spilman, '22.
Following the meeting, the club held
a picnic at Roblc Bridge. Professor
and Mrs. S. S. Seward and Miss Mar
gery Baily were guests.
