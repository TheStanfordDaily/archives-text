# E.W. Plaskett, '22, Will Manage University Band
## 
### 
E. W. Plaskett, '22, was elected
manager of the university band for the
coming year at a meeting of the mem
bers held last evening in the band
room. I. M. Smith, '22, was chosen
director and P. K. Thirst, '19 librarian.
A bass player is needed for the
pummer concert work, it was an
nounced today by the manager of the
band. From 15 to 2ft dollars a week
can be earned by any qualified Stan
ford player during the summer. Those
interested should consult either the
manager or director of the band.
