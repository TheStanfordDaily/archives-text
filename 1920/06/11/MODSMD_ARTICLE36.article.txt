# "Milt" Hagen to Return For Summer Quarter
## 
### 
Milton Hagen, '19, who is at present
in New York with the music publishing
house of Daniels and Wilson, will return
to complete his course during the sum
mer quarter. When in college Hagen
wrote the music of the 1915 Junior
opera, and also of the' Ram's Head
show of last year. He was editor of
Chaparral when bis course was inter
rupted by war service.
