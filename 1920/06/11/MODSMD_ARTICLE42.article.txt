# N.Y.U. ORIGINATES TRAINING SCHOOL
## 
### 
By United Press
"NEW YORK, June 11.—A course of
two years, as a training school for
teachers pt retail selling has been in
corporated in the courses of study of
New York University as the outgrowth
of a conference between the superin
tendent of New York City Schools,
representatives of the university, and
prominent retail merchants in New
York City. The school was organized
to supply the demand for teachers of
retail selling and the course is sup
plemented by practical work.
