# F.C. BAILEY TO STAY IN FRANCE
## 
### 
Forrest C. Bailey, whose work while
on this side was under the Y. M. C. A.
works in France entirely among the
French and under the direction of the
Societi des Foyer del' Union Franco-
Americaine. For six months he was
among six Americans who were em
ployed by the French government to
teach English at Fontainebleau.
