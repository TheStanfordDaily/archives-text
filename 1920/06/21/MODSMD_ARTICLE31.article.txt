# HEAD OF LATIN DEPARTMENT TO RESUME CLASSES
## 
### 
Word has just been received from
Professor H. R. Fairclough, head of
the Latin Department at Stanford, and
now lieutenant-colonel in the American
Red Cross and Commissioner to Mon
tenegro, that he will be back at the
University in time to resume his
teaching at the beginning of the
autumn quarter next October.
In his letter Dr. Pairclough men
tions a visit from Mrs. Vernon Kel
logg at his headquarters at Podgo
ritza. Mrs. Kellogg in a recent letter
mentioned this visit and said that Dr.
Pairclough had gained a very high
reputation throughout Central Europe
for the effectiveness of his administra
tion of the Red cross relief and re
construction work in Montenegro.
