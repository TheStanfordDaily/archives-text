# TWO STANFORD WOMEN RETURN TO UNIVERSITY
## 
### 
Mrs. Helen Green Cross, '14, with
her sister, Miss Hazel Green, '15, are
both on the campus visiting their par
ents, Professor and Mrs. R. L. Green
on Salvatierra. Mrs. Cross has long
been interested in interior decorating.
She lives in San Francisco. Miss
Green is now in the advertising de
partment of Capwell's in Oakland.
They are both members of Kappa
Alpha Theta.
