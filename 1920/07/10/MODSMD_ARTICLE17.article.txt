# ALUMNI OFFICES WILL BE ON SECOND FLOOR
## 
### 
J. R. McDowell. Alumni Secretary,
will be moved to the front office up
stairs in the Administration Building.
His present office downstairs will be
occupied by George 13. Culver, '97. the
new Dean of Men, about the first of
August. Professor J. P. Mitchell of
the Chemistry department will also
use McDowell's office as headquarters
for the new Lower Division plan un
til more permanent arrangements can
be made.
