THE TRIP of the California Rugby Team to Antwerp seems fairly as
sured now. Only a small sum of money remains to be collected. A good
share of this remainder ought to be* raised at the benefit dance in Palo Alto
next week if every one lends their aid in making it the big success that
it is expected to be. Don't let the price of a dance ticket stand in the way
of the boys' going over to Belgium!
