# Recorder of Sunshine Invented by Botanist
## 
### 
An autographic sunshine recorder
which will enable scentists to measure
the amount and quantity of sunshine in
different places on the globe is being
developed by Harold C. Hampton, assist
ant in botany here. One of the chief
uses of this instrument will be in the
measurement of light in regard to food
manufacture; hence it will materially aid
in production.
