# REGISTRATION OF NEW STUDENTS WILL NOT STOP CLASSES
## 
### 
Rules governing the examinations at
the end of quarters and the registration
at the beginning do not hold for the
division in the summer quarter, accord
ing to the announcement made by the
Registrar's Office. The examinations will
be given only during the regular class
periods, except where special dispensa
tion has been granted. The registration
of the students entering for the last six
weeks of the quarter will be carried on
without stopping classes.
