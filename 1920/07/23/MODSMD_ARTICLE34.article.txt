# STANFORD GRADUATE COLLECTS DATA FOR U.S. GOVERNMENT
## 
### 
H. VV. Cutler, who graduated from
the Economics Department of Stanford
in June, has been employed this summer
in gathering valuable data concerning the
living conditions of the laborers in the
oil fields of the Unitedf States under the
direction of the Chief Statistician of the
General Petroleum Co. Cutler has made
a thorough study of the homes of the
oil workers, and the data that he ob
tained is being used by the United States
Mediation Commission, now in session
at San Francisco, to determine the new
wage scale for the twenty thousand oil
workers of the United States.
Cutler is a member of the Theta Xi
fraternity.
