# Lemos Will Supervise "Pictorial" Art Work
## 
### 
Pedro J. Lemos will have charge of
the art work for the Stanford Pictorial,
tlic new student magazine which the
Press Club is sponsoring, lie is now
designing a cover for the first issue,
which will embody a Stanford scene.
Lemos is best known to undergraduates
through his art work for the Stanford
Quad.
