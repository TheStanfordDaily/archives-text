# WORD RECEIVED FROM PROFESSOR ESPINOSA
## 
### 
Professor O. AT. Johnston, head of the
Romanic languages department, recently
received word from Professor Aurelio
M. F.spinosa, who is making a tour of
Europe. He wrote from Rome, from
where he intends to go to Spain.
Professor Espinosa left Stanford at the
end of last quarter to collect Spanish
folk lore for the American Folk Lore
Society. He previously made a similar
collection among the old Spanish fami
lies of California. Tic will return to
Stanford at the beginning of the winter
quarter.
