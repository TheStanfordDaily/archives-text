# OUR LABOR TROUBLES
## 
### 
117ITH this issue of the "Quadrangle" comes the announcement of the resig
nation of its News Editor, Mr. J. E. Thrash. Press of other affairs, in
cluding the management of "The Stanford Pictorial" caused Mr. Thrash to
give up his position with this paper. At the same time that we express our
regrets at the loss of Mr. Thrash, we are congratulating ourselves on secur
ing the services of Mr. L. W. Dinkelspiel, who will act as News Editor for
the rest of the quarter.
