# Santa Clara Maps Out New Football Schedule
## 
### 
The football schedule of the Uni
versity of Santa Clara for 1920' was
announced Wednesday, by Graduate
Manager O'Connor. The football sea
son of the Mission University will open
on September 9th at San Francisco
with the Olympic Club as opponents.
A game has been scheduled with
Stanford for October 23, at Santa
Clara. The rest of the schedule for
the 1920 season is as follows: Sep
tember 25, open date at Santa Clara;
October 2, University of California, at
Berkeley; October 16, University Farm
(Davis), at Santa Clara; November
G, Olympic Club at Santa Clara; No
vember 15, Gonzoga University at
Spokane (tentative); Thanksgiving
Day. University of Nevada, at San
Francisco.
