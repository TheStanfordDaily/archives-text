# STANFORD ATHLETES TO COLLECT PICTURES
## 
### 
The Stanford athletes competing in
the Olympic Games at Antwerp are col
lecting pictures of the contests, which
will appear in the initial number of the
Stanford Pictorial, to be published
October 1.
A subscription campaign for the new
Press Club publication was started on
the campus this week.
Among the editorial features- of the
first issue will be an article on Stanford
journalism by L. W. Dinkelspiel,'21. D.
W. Evans. '21, writes on the rushing
problem and Encina, and S. N. Pierce,
'22, on Stanford at the Democratic Con
