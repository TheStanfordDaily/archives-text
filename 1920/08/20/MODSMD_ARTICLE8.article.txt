# Austro-German Papers Added to Collection
## 
### 
The most recent addition to the
Hoover War Collection is a collection of
German and Austrian pamphlets and
newspapers. This collection was made
early in the war by W. H. Smith a
special agent of the British government.
Professor E. I). Adams, of the History
Department secured the purchase of this
collection, amounting to ten large pack
ing cases, through the recommendation
of Sir George Prothero, the editor of
the Quarterly review and of the peace
studies prepared by British experts and
used during the peace negotiations.
