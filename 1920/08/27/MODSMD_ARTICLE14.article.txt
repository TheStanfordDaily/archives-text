# Professor Fairclough En Route to America
## 
### 
Professor Henry R. Fairclough head
of the Latin Department has sailed from
England en route to America. For the
past two years Professor Fairclough has
been in the service of the American Red
Cross in Europe. He was stationed at
Bcrfie in Switzerland and in Montenegro,
having charge of all the work directed
from those points. He was decorated
with the Order of the Black Eagle by
the King of Serbia for his work in
Montenegro.
Professor Fairclough expects to arrive
in New York about September 15 and
will come directly on to Stanford. He
will resume his duties here in October.
