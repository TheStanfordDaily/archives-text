# GEOLOGISTS RETURN FROM SUMMER TRIP
## 
### 
Forty-five geology majors, who have
been doing summer work in Ventura
county, returned to the campus last Sun
day. They were away nine weeks, leav
ing here at the end of last quarter. The
men were engaged in a number of
courses in field work composed of areal
geology, detail geology, cross sections,
topographical mapping of a square mile
The men are now engaged in writing
reports on the result of their work. The
trip is made every year as one of the
requirements for a degree in the geology
department. The party wis under the
direction of Professor C. F. Tolman.
