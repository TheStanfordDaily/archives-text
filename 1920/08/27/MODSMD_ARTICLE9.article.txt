# All Ex-Service Men May Now Apply for Y.M.C.A. Scholarships
## 
### 
Y. M. C. A. scholarships in this or
any other university are now available
to all ex-service men who are helping
themselyes through college. The Y. M.
C. A. is promoting these scholarships as
the most fitting way to use the. funds
given to it for war work but not ex
pended.
Any ex-service man is eligible for a
scholarship regardless of nature or
branch of military service. The scholar
ships are designed to give a hand to
any ex-service man who is trying to get
an education.
Application blanks for the scholarships
may be obtained from Dr. F. E. Morgan,
Y. M. C. A. secretary, at the Stanford
Union.
