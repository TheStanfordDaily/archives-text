# ROBLE CLUB GIVES FRESHMAN PARTY
## 
### 
Roble Club entertained the freshmen
women at an annual get-together party
held last night at Roble Hall. The party
was a fancy-dress affair, and many new
and original costumes made their appear
ance during the evening.
Stunts by the members of the club
and dancing furnished the entertainment
during the rest of the evening.
