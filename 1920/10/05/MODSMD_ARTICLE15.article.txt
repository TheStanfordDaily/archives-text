# CORNELL WOMEN WILL MEET AT CLUB HOUSE
## 
### 
The next meeting of the Cornell
Women's Club will be held at the
Women's Club house, Saturday after
noon at 1 o'clock. Mrs. David Stan-
Jordan and Mrs. Otis Brun will act as
hostesse sat the affair.
Following a basket luncheon there will
be an early afternoon social, invitation
is extended to all women who have at
any time been students at Cornell.
