# HIT HIS STEP CHILD WITH BOARD, CHARGE
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, Oct. s.—Rccausc
witnesses reported he beat his 5 year old
stepdaughter when she gut her words
mixed up in nursery rhymes, George
Hunt has been arrested here on a charge
of cruelty to children. The officers re
port that on one occasion lie hit the
child with a board, and kicked her after
she fell to the ground.
