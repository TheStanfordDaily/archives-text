# DRAMATIC SOCIETY WILL GIVE DANCE AT WOMEN'S CLUB HOUSE
## 
### 
At a meeting held last night Miss
Melba Berry, '22, was elected President
of Masquers, the women's honorary dra
matic society. Miss Kirby Ingolsby, '21,
was elected Secretary-Treasurer and
Miss Eleanor Anderson. '22, Librarian.
Plans were completed for the dance
to be given at the Women's Club house,
Friday, October K. Admission will be
$1.00 a couple. The dance will begin
promptly at 9. Music will be furnished
by Reynold's orchestra.
Tentative plans were also laid for the
annual dramatic production which will
be given some time during the winter
quarter.
