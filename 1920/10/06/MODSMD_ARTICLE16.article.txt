# UNIVERSITY ORCHESTRA WILL HOLD TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford University
Orchestra will be held in the As
embly Hall tonight at 7:80. Everyone
who can play an instrument is asked
to try out for a place. Plans have
been made to have the orchestra play
at the coming Student Assembly next
Tuesday, making it necessary for the
orchestra members to be chosen as
soon as possible.
