# CORNELL WOMEN WILL MEET HERE SATURDAY
## 
### 
Cornell women from the northern part
of the state will hold their regular
monthly meeting and luncheon at the
Women's Club house next Saturday.
Mrs. H. Otis Rrun and Mrs David
Starr Jordan will act as hostesses. This
will be the first time the Cornell organ
ization has met at Stanford, all pre
vious meetings having been held at the
University of California.
