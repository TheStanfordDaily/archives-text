# SCHUBERT CLUB TO HOLD INITIAL TRY OUTS TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tryouts for Shubcrt Club will be held
Thursday evening after choir practice in
Room 84 and on Tuesday afternoon
from 5 to 6 in the music room at Roble
Hall. Shubcrt Club is the women's
musical society, the members of which
are interested in vocal work.
Each year the members of Shubcrt
Club take part in some big vocal pro
duction. This year the club plans to do
great work and is therefore anxious for
all available talent. All women interest
ed in vocal work are asked to try out.
