# FRESHMEN WOMEN TO MEET Y. W. C. A. AT CLUBHOUSE, TONIGHT
## 
### 
Y. VV. C. A. will entertain the entering
women at an informal get-together party
from 7:15 to 9 o'clock this evening in
the Women's Club house. The enter
tainment is for the purpose of helping
the new women to become acquainted
with the organization and to get them
interested in the various branches of
Y. W. C. A. work. Questionaires will
be issued to be tilled out by the fresh
men, in order that the organization may
know what activities they arc most in
terested in.
Dancing and refreshments will be part
of the evening's program. The part is
an annual affair, and all new women are
asked to attend.
