# Gymnasium Classes to Commence for Women
## 
### 
All classes in Physical Training for
women will begin tomorrow. Women
should be present at their classes in full
gymnastic uniform. Those who are
registered for swimming may appear in
street clothes. The registration number
now totals 304. of which the greater
part is composed of students taking
elective work.
