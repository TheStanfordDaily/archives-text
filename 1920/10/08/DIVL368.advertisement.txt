Girls!!
This is the place to come for
the improvement of your
appearance
You can have a clear skin, dainty white
hands, a lustrous head of hair—all for the
asking.
We maintain a staff of experts in each of
the several lines of Beauty Culture.
An expert Eastern Marcelle Operator is here
to attend to your hair work.
Just scores of special treatments that will
beautify your complexion, are awaiting your
demands. Scalp work, and hair dressing
are our specialties. We have a wonderfully
efficient method for improving the appear
ance of your hands and arms, no matter how
badly sunburned or freckled they are.
And needless to say —"Our rates are quite
reasonable."
