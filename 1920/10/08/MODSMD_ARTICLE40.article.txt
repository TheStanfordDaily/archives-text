# Time For Joint "Y" Reception Changed

### 
A change in the time of the joint
Y. M. C. A.-Y. W. C. A. reception has
been made. It will begin at 8:30 this
evening instead of 8:00 as previously
announced. The place of meeting will
be the Encina Clubroom.
The committee in charge of enter
tainment would not divulge the names
of those who have been secured for
tho program but they assure the new
students that some of the best campus
talent will be present. All new stu
dents, both men and women, are ex
tended a cordial invitation by the
Y. M. C. A. and the Y. W. C. A., to at
tend this get-together party. Women
students are urged to attend either
with or without escorts as reception
is to be in the nature of a jolly-up.
The entertainment will be concluded
in sufficient time for those who wish
to attend the Masquer's Dance to
do so.
