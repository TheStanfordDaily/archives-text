# Palo Alto Republican Club Elects Officers
## 
### 
Officers weer elected and a constitu
tion and by-laws adopted last night at
a meeting of the recently organized Palo
Alto Republican Club held in the Com
munity House.
The officers are as follows: C. P.
Cooley, president; Joseph H. Borden,
first vice-president; Mrs. Norman E.
Scofield, second vice-president; Jas. M.
Hyde, secretary; M. A. Buchan, treas
nrcr. The president will name the com
mittees, which, the by-laws provide,
shall be as follows: Executive, member
ship, finance, election and publicity com
mittees.
