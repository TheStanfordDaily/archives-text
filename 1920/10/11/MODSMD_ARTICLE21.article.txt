# ART WILCOX, '20, TO LEAD FOOTBALL TEAM
## 
### 
C. A. Wilcox, '20, was chosen to lead
the 1920 Varsity football squad at a
meeting of the team Sunday morning.
Wilcox succeeds Pret Holt. '21, wlio was
called home and will be unable to return
this fall.
This is Wilcox's first year on the
Varsity, being ineligible last season. His
early season playing at quarter back
gives promise that he will be one of thf
Varsity's mainstays this season.
