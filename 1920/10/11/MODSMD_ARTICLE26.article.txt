# MEN'S FACULTY CLUB HOLDS FIRST MEETING
## 
### 
The Men's Faculty Club held its
initial meeting of the quarter on Fri
day night, when officers were chosen
and fifteen new members added to the
roll.
Professor E. P. Lesley was elected
president, Professor A. G. Kennedy,
secretary, and Professor J. R. Slon
aker, treasurer. Professor Harold
Heath and Professor E. C. Franklin
were made directors.
Professor Henry R. Fairclough ad
dressed the meeting, expressing his
pleasure at returning to the University
after an absence of two years in the
Balkans.
