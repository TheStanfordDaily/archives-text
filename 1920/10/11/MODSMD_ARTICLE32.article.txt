# MISS LOIS BAKER, '16, MARRIES IN CHINA
## 
### 
To the beating of tomtoms and the
weird strains of strange musical instru
ments, the wedding of Miss Lois Baker,
a member of the class of '16, and George
B. Campbell, an official of the Standard
Oil Company in China, took place on
June 1. at the Episcopal chaple of the
British Consulate at Tsingtau, in the
Shantung province. Campbell is a grad
uate of Yale University.
