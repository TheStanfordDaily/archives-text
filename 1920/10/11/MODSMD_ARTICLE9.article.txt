# Wranglers Hold First Meeting of the Year
## 
### 
The Wranglers held their first
meeting of the year at the Women's
Club House, last Thursday. October
7. After a short business meeting the
new members elected during Spring
Quarter were informally welcomed.
Miss Anne Hardy. '21. president, ap
pointed Miss Elizabeth McArthur, '21,
Miss Dorothy Webster, '21. and Miss
Alice Eddy, '22. a committee to pre
pare a tentative program for the com
ing year.
The Wranglers meet every Thurs
day evening at the Women's Club
House. The next meeting will be an
open discussion of the proposed pro
