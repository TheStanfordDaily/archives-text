# PIANO RECITAL IS GIVEN IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Last Sunday afternoon teh musical set
of Stanford and Palo Alto assembled at
the home of Mrs. John J ago to meet
Mrs. Slsie Cook Hughes, the English
pianist, who has recently come to Palo
Alto. The affair took on the form of a
musicale, piano numbers being given by
Mrs. Hughes and by Richard Halaby.
a Stanford student who is a brilliant
composer and performer, as well as by
the hostess's talented 11 year old son,
Master John Trelawney Jago.
