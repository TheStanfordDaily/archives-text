# WILLIAM G. McADOO WILL GIVE ADDRESS IN P. A. OCTOBER 23
## 
### 
William Gibbs McAdoo will speak
at an open-air meeting in Palo Alto,
October 23, it was announced by the
Stanford Cox-Roosevelt Club this
morning.
McAdoo is on a tour of the West in
support of the Democratic ticket. The
Stanford club is co-operating with the
County Central Committee in making
arrangements for the meeting.
F. E. Supple, '17, president of the
club, announced a meeting for tomor
row night, at 7:30, in room 12 of the
Law Building.
