# WRESTLING MATCHES PLANNED WITH O. C.
## 
### 
Wrestling matches with the Olympic
| Club and the San Jose Y. M. C. A.
: are now being planned. With Bunds
! and Hosepian again back on the mat,
! lively contests are expected.
Bunds made the Varsity the two
last years, and Hosepian won the
freshman contest with California and
is eligible for the varsity this year.
Among the forty men who are tak
ing the sport, the coach sees good
material that, with some practice with
the holds will make a good showing
in the inter-class contest.
