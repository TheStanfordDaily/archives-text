# Shouse Resignation Is Accepted by President
## 
### United Press 
WASHINGTON, I). C., Oct. 14.—The
President accepted the resignation of
Jouett Shouse, as assistant secretary
of the treasury. The President wrote,
"I accede to your request with deep
regret." Shouse said that personal
affairs made his resignation necessary.
