# Nestoria Society Will Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Art Harzfeld, '21, and Floyd McElroy,
'21. are slated as the principal speakers
at Nestoria Society's meeting tonight.
McElroy will discuss the benefits of
debating practice to college men, and
Harzfeld will also develop the same
theme.
Nestoria issues a general invitation to
all students to attend the open meeting
of the literary society at 7:30 p. m. to
night at the Little Theater.
