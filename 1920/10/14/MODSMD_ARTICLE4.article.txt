# D. P. A. to Post Game Results
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto has ar
ranged to post the results of the
Stanford-U. S. C. game at the
Men's Union next Saturday- aft
ernoon. The outcome of the
game will be made known by
special wire, as soon as the
game is over and the score will
be posted on the bulletin at the
Union, between 5 and 6 o'clock.
Wendell Kinney, '21, who will
go south with the team to lead
the yells for the Stanford alumni
section at the conflict, will ar
range for the service.
