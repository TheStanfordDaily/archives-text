# SANTA CLARA LOSES TO COLLEGE OF PACIFIC
## 
### 
The College of the Pacific whipped
Santa Clara freshmen yesterday to the
tune of 9 to 0. This was the first game
of the season and while slow was hard
fought throughout. Captain Telfer, left
halfback for the College of the Pacili.'
was undoubtedly the star of the game.
He accounted for the one goal in the
second quarter and added two more
points by a field goal made in the fourth
quarter from the twenty-five yard line.
