# Laundry Owners Make Stanford Campus Tour
## 
### 
Representatives of the Laundry Own
ers' National Association which is meet
ing in San Francisco this week, toured
the Leland Stanford University campus
yesterday. About 30 sedans containing
delegates of the association were includ
ed in the group.
