# BRUINS START TRACK PRACTICE FOR SEASON
## 
### 
Cinder work in the University of
California is now under way. The
first competitive track meet was held
yesterday and the first leg was won
by the Sigma Phi Sigma fraternity.
The second half will be run off Wed
nesday and Thursday, and the winner
will compete with the Del Rey club
for the championship of the campus.
The distances run were short and the
time was slow.
