# CLUB ORGANIZED TO DISCUSS EDUCATION
## 
### 
A discussion club was organized at a
meeting of the members of the Educa
tion department last night, the purpose
of which is to study all subjects per
taining to problems of education. Ai!
students in the education department are
eligible to membership, and all others in
terested in education are urged to be
come members of the club.
Meetings will be held twice each
month. Members of the education
faculty, and other experienced educatots
throughout the State will be invited to
attend these discussions and contribute
their views on the various topics. In
this manner it is hoped to broaden tht
field.
The officers elected at the meeting last
night were, Walter Bachrodt, president;
J. N. Helnuck, secretary.
