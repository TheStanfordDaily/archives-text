# Investigate Death Of Former Stanford Man
## 
### 
Defectives are today trying to clear up
circumstances connected with the death
in San Francisco of Loeng Ming, said
to be a former Stanford student, whose
body was found on the sidewalk outside
his room early Thursday. Ming was a
Chinese actor and well known in Orien
tal quarters.
The police believe that he fell from a
third-story window while lie was crawl
ing along a ledge that led to the room
of another Chinese who lives in the
same quarter.
An autopsy showed that he died of a
fractured skull. How he came to be
crawling along the ledge is a matter of
mystery, according to the police.
According to the detectives, Ming was
twice married, each time to a white
woman, and had been divorced from
each of them.
