# HANDLEY DEATH TO BE INVESTIGATED
## 
### United Press 
SAN FRANCISCO. Oct. 18.—United
States Attorney Silva has announced
that today he will launch a thorough in
vestigation designed to establish all the
circumstances of the death of Lorcn
Handley, former state prohibition di
rector, who was recently killed in an
automobile accident. There have been
many intimations of alleged foul play
figuring in Handley's case.
