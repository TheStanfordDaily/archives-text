# GOLF SEASON STARTS AT MEETING TUESDAY
## 
### 
Golf enthusiasts will have a chance
to get instruction on their favorite
game and at the same time get their
physical training credit, as prepara
tions have been made for starting
classes in the game at once under the
direction of W. D. Seay. All those
wild wish to take golf for physical
training credit should meet coach Seay
at the Encina Gym Office, at 7:30 p.m.
on Tuesday, October 19. Credit will
be given those who sign up at this
meeting.
