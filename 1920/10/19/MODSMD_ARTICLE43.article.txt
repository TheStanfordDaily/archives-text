# Gym Classes Have Large Enrollment
## 
###

and a schedule of rigorous trailing is
being outlined by Coach Botsford. The
men interested in advanced gym
nastics will be organized into a team
for special work. Indoor baseball,
volley ball and handball teams will
be formed and competition games will
be held.
