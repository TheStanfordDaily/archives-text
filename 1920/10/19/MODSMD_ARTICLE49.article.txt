# POLYNESIA WILL BE SUBJECT OF ADDRESS IN LITTLE THEATRE
## 
### 
Polynesia will be the subject of an
address in the Little Theater this even
ing by Vaughan MacCaughey, who is
connected with the Department of Pub
lic Instruction for Hawaii. He has also
been prominent in public affairs in tins
country.
Since 1908 Mr. MacCaughey has been
active in the exploration and develop
ment of the Hawaiian Islands, particu
larly the islands of Polynesia. His work
has brought him into close touch with
these people, and their customs, religion
and legends will lie described tonight.
The talk will be illustrated by slides of
the coral and volcanic islands of Poly
nesia.
The lecture will start at 7 :30 p. m.
