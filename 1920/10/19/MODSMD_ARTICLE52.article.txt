# Committee Chosen To Handle Song Contest
## 
### 
Wendall Kenny, '21. Professor Mart
in, Warren D. Allen, Thomas Green, '22,
Florence V. Hocking, '22, and Paul R.
Brust, '21, were appointed members of
a song contest committee yesterday by
F. L. McElroy, President of tlie Asso
ciated Students with the authority of
the Executive Committee. A prize of
fifteen dollars is being offered by the
Committee for the best song written by
campus talent. The prize was offered
by the instance of the Sequoia Club.
