# BART GILLESPIE, '18, VARSITY TRACK STAR, LEAVES FOR MEXICO
## 
### 
Hart W. Gillespie, '18. left the campus
today for Tampion, Mexico, to accept a
position as geologist with the Atlantic
Gulf Navigation Company. He will he
connected with the oil industry of that
company.
Gillespie is a member of Encitia Club,
Skull and Snakes, and was a member of
the varsity track squad Inst year win
ning the discus against California. He
was also manager bf the Men's Union,
and secretary of the Student Body.
