# NINE INJURED IN STREET CAR WRECK
## 
### United Press 
SOUX CITY. la.. Oct. 20.—Nine per
sons were seriously hurt here today
when a street car overturned while
rounding a corner. The car was heavi
ly loaded and plunged down an incline
when the breaks failed to work.
