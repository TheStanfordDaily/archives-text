# Air Plane Rides For Subscriptions Offered
## 
### 
Airpane rides have been offered for
one hundred subscriptions to the Oread,
a bi-monthly magazine issued at the Uni
versity of Kansas. Prizes for numbers
of subscriptions varying from 75 down
to 10 have also been offered, but it is
only those who reach the century who
will be taken up over Lawrence, Kansas
by aviators from the Aviation School
there.
