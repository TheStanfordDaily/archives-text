# Tilden-Johnson Tennis Tickets Are On Sale
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale for the
Davis cup matches at San Francisco,
on Saturday, October 30. These are
between William Tilden, 11, and W.
M. Johnson. Tilden the national ten
nis chamion this year is playing a
series of matches in the west for
exhibition. Captain Neer of the Car
dinal squad has the tickets for sale
in the gymnasium and is anxious that
as many of the local tennis men as
can go make it a point to see the meet
in San Francisco on the 30th inst.
