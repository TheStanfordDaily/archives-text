# Invite U. C. Society To Skull and Snakes Barbecue on Nov. 13
## 
### 
Plans for a Skull and Snakes bar
becue for November 13 were laid at
a meeting of the organization at the
Union last night. E. R. Caughey, '18,
was appointed chairman of a com
mittee to extend an invitation to the
Skull and Keys Society of the Uni
versity of California to attend the
barbecue.
Regular meetings of the Skull and
Snakes will hereafter be held on the
first and third Wednesday nights of
each month. Members were reminded
that three unexcused absences will
cancel membership in the organiza
tion.
President F. L. Adams, '21, appoint
ed a membership committee composed
of the following men: F. L. Adams,
'21, chairman; R. L. Templeton, '18,
E. E. Crowe, '21, J. T. Murphy, '20,
C. S. Long, '19, C. E. Righter, '19, W.
M. Stamp, '20, and 11. McGillvray, '20.
