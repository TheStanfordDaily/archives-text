# DR. WILBUR DISCUSSES PRESENT METHODS OF CITY SCHOOL SYSTEM
## 
### 
Declaring that San Francisco's
school organization has been wrong
for many years, Dr. Ray Lyman Wil
bur, in a statement given out by the
Educational Conference, Tuesday, Oct.
19, expressed the hope that out of the
present turmoil there will grow a
sentimen that will eventually lead to
the development of a sound school
system.
Speakers from the San Francisco
Center, the Educational Conference
and the School Defense Association
urged the adoption of the proposed
amendment to the city charter, pro
viding for a trained educator as sup
erintendent of schools and taking the
office out of politics.
