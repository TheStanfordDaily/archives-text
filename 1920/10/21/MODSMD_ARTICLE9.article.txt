# Joe T. Murphy Elected New Manager of Union
## 
### 
Joe T. Murphy, '20, is the new man
ager of the Men's Union, according to
announcement of the Board of Govern
ors of the Union today.
Murphy succeeds P>. W. Gillespie, '18.
who resigned to accept a position as a
geologist with the Atlantic Gulf Naviga
tion Company at Tampico, Mexico.
Murphy is a member of Encina Club,
Skull and Snakes and is boxing in
structor of the university. He entered
on his new duties today.
