# Congregational Church Will Have New Pastor
## 
### 
The Rev. W. P>. Thorp, formerly of
San Diego, will take up his duties as
pastor of the First Congregational
Church of Palo Alto Sunday morning,
October 24th. Mr. Thorp is a graduate
of Amherst and of the Yale Divinity
School, and is recognized as one of the
leaders of his denomination on the Pa
cific Coast. Congregational students are
especially invited to the opening services
on Sunday.
