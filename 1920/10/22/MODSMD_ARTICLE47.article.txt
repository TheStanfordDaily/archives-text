# S.F. COMMERCE HEAD TO TALK NOVEMBER 3
## 
### 
The third lecture of a scries on World's
Problems, forums conducted by the Y.
M. C. A., will be given November 3 by
Robert Newton Lynch, vice-president and
general manager of the San Francisco
Chamber of Commerce. Lynch will talk
on the subject, "Contributions of Busi
ness to Social Advancement." The lec
ture will be given in the Little Theatre
at 7 :30 o'clock in the evening.
