# Davis Cap Team Flans A Tennis Exhibition
## 
### 
The United States Davis cup team
will hold a mammoth tennis exhibi
tion and dantie in the San Francisco
Coliseum, on October 30, at 8 p.m.
The affair will bo the largest of its
kind ever held on the coast.
Some of the performers are William
Tilden, TI, world's champion; William
Johnson, former champion; Norris
Williams and Wallace Washburn,
ranking third and fourth respectively.
Following the exhibition the can
vas will be taken off the floor and a
dance will be held.
