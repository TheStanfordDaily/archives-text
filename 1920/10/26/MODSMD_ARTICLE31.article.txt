# Hunt Man Who Shot Sister in a Quarrel
## 
### United Press 
NAPA, Cal., Oct. 26.—Deputy sher
iffs today were scouring the hills near
here for Lavallc McClain, who was al
ledged to have shot and mortally wound
ed his sistei. Violet Apperson. last night.
The shooting took place in the Apper
son home near here, and was said to
have been the climax of a quarrel over
a dog.
