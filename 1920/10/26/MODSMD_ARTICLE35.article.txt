# Professor F. Anderson Will Be Speaker At English Club Meeting
## 
### 
"Contemporary Writers" will be the
subject of an informal talk to be given
by Professor P. Anderson at a meet
ing of English Club, to be held this
evening, at 8:30 at the Sigma Kappa
house.
A business meeting will follow Pro
fessor Anderson's discussion, during
which the club will outline its drama
tic plans for the year. A committee
composed of Harry Wycoff, '21, D. W.
Evans, '22, and H. D. Darling, '21,
which was recently appointed to work
on the selection of the club's play,
will give its first report.
