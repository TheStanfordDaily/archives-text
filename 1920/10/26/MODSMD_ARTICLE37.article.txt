# HIGH SCHOOL INSTALLS NEW BATIK COURSE
## 
### 
A new course in batik lias been in
stalled in the Polytechnic High School,
First Avenue and Frederick Street,
San Francisco. The work is under
the auspices /jf the University of
California Extension Division. The
first class meets Thursday afternoon,
October 28, at 3:30 o'clock.
Miss E. Shotwell Goeller is to have
charge of the work. Miss Goeller
was a reconstruction worker in the
United States Hospital during the war
and after the armistice a student in
New York for nine months.
Batik work done by students will
be placed on exhibition at the office
of the Extension Division, 140 Kearny
Street. San Francisco.
