# Professor Fairclough To Address Bar Association
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough is to be
one of the speakers tonight at the Bar
Association banquet which will be
held at. the St. Francis Hotel in San
Francisco.
Professor Fairclough's speech will
be on, "American Red Cross Relief
Work in Montenegro." /This is a
topic that Professor Fairclough is an
authority on, since he spent a great
part of the war period in this work.
