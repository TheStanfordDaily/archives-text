# I. W. W. SUSPECTED IN EXPLOSION OF HOME
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., Oct. 26.—That a
member of 1 lie I. W. W. told them that
lie would wreck the home of R. S.
Shong, general claim agent of tlie Chi
cago, Milwaukee and St. Paul railroad,
was the theory adopted by the police to
day after Shong's home was completely
wrecked by an explosion. No one was
hurt in the blast.
