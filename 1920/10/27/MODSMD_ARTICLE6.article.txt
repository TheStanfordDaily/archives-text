# COMMANDER OF LOST BATTALION TO SPEAK FRIDAY IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Talks on campaign issues by Pro
fessor Irving Fisher of Yale, Colonel
Charles Whittelsey, Commander of
the Lost Battalion, and Mrs. Lucia
Ames, Boston author and lecturer,
will feature a program to be held
under the auspices of the Palo Alto
League of Nations Club at the Masonic
Hall in Palo Alto, Friday night, at
8 o'clock.
Stanford students interested in the
campaign problems and issues will
have an opportunity to hear three dis
tinguished speakers at this meeting.
