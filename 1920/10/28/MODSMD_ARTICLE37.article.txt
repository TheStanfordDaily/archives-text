# PIANO RECITAL GIVEN IN LITTLE THEATRE
## 
### 
Warren 1). Allen, University Organ
ist, assisted Miss Francis Martin in a
piano recital last night at the Little
Theater. Miss Martin played the main
solos and Mr. Allen accompanied her
on a second piano, playing the or
chestral parts.
The program was as follows:
To the Sea Edward MacDowell
Caprice Gluck-St. Suens
Carnaval Schumann
Etude in E major Chopin
Jeux d'Eau Ravel
British Sea Chanty . .Percy Orainger
Juba (Dance) R. Nathaniel Dett
Concerto in E flat, major Liszt
