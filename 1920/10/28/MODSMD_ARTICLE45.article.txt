# TRYOUTS SATURDAY FOR HANDBALL TEAM
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Handball
team will be held Saturday morning
from 10 to 12, in Encina Gymnasium.
The men chosen at Saturday's tryout
will be the ones from whom the team
that will compete with California will
be picked.
All men who are interested in the
game are urged to try out. A number
of competent judges have been choesn
to select the men who will compose the
squad. Fteslimen as well as uppcrclass
men are eligible for the team as hand
