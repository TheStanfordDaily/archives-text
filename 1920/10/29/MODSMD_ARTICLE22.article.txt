# Moiseiwitsch Booked To Play at Stanford
## 
### 
Benno Moiseiwitsch, the young
Russian pianist, who scored so decided
a triumph in San Francisco recently,
lias been booked to play in the Stan
ford Assembly Hall on the evening of
November 9, under the auspics of the
Peninsula Musical Association.
Season tickets for the four concerts
are still available. All intending sub
scribers should send their checks at
once to the secretary of the associa
tion, or else apply for the tickets now
at the campus book store.
