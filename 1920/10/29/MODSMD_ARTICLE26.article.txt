# COLUMBIA APPOINTS FRESHMAN ADVISORS
## 
### 
A Freshmen Activity Commission has
been appointed by the Student Board of
Columbia University. The committee
will supervise the affairs of the Fresh
man class, and act in an advisory capac
ity.' it will also have charge of the
Freshman-Sophomore tug-of-vvar to be
held this afternoon.
