# Stanford Music Club Gives Tryouts Monday
## 
### 
The Stanford Music Club held
additional tryouts at its regular monthly
meeting which was to be held this
afternoon at 5 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. This is the only musical
organization on the campus in which
both men and women hold membership.
Both vocalists and instrumentalists are
especially needed. An informal pro
gram by members of the club was
also be given.
