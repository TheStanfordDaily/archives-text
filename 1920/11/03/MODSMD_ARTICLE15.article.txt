Latin Department, will exhibit his
private collction of Greek coins as a
feature of the Classical Club meet
ing this evening in the Women's Club
House, at 8 o'clock. This assemblage
of ancient Greek coins is one of the
most valuable and complete private
collections is existence.
The rest of the evening's entertain
ment will be nn exposition of ancient
narison of these methods with the
Roman election methods and a corn
ones in use in America today. The
meeting will be open to the public.

"Roman and American Political Cam

at Pompeii," Miss Mildred Estues;
"Roman Election Booths and Methods
of Voting." Mr. Francis Hutchens; and
