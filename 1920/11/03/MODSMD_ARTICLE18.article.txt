# Euphronia Contest Is Scheduled For Tonight
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society will
hold its regular weekly meeting this
evening at 7:30 in Room 12 of the
Law Building. The debating season
is now getting Into full swing, and
promising debaters are being develop
ed at each meeting. A contest is
scheduled for tonight, and will help
to give a line on those most likely to
be selected for the debate with the
California society when final selec
tions are made next Wednesday
night.
