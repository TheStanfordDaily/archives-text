# Corda Fratres To Be Hosts to Foreigners
## 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of Corda Fra
tres will hold a reception to all foreign
students Wednesday evening at 7:30 in
the Stanford Woman's Clubhouse. The
reception will be informal. Refresh
ments will be served to the guests.
Members of the local club will dine
together, before the reception at 6 p. m.
at the Stanford Union and may bring
their friends.
