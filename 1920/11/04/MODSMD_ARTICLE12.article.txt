# CALIFORNIA ADOPTS NEW ROOTERS' HATS
## 
### 
California lias adopted a new style
of rooters hats for the bleacher stunts
that will be given at the Big Game.
An order for 5000 now style hats has
been placed with the manufactrers.
The flashy headgear will be a part
of the admission requirement to the
rooting section, and will cost $1. The
sfvln is on the order of the sailor hats
of the navy. This will be the perma
nent style of rooters hats for the Cali
fornia bU aclicr leeches. Yell Leader
"Red" Drew is working out a series
of stunts for the Big Game.
