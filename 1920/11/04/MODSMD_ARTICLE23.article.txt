# Stanford Faculty Men Address Master Masons
## 
### 
Major Collins and Professor Griffin
of the University, and E. G. Lakin,
Master of the Palo Alto Lodge, were
the speakers at the meeting of the
Stanford Masonic Club held last night
in the Women's Club House.
A motion was passed making all
Master Masons of the Palo Alto lodge
and all Master Masons of the faculty
honorary members of the club. It
was further voted that the charter
be held upon until the next regular
meeting.
The following officers for the year
were elected: President, Lionel Jaco
bv; vice-president, W. A. Boekel;
treasurer, W. S. Eddleman; secretary,
Mark Evans.
