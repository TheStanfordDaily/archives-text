# DR. J. C. BRANNER WILL LEAVE FOR ARKANSAS
## 
### 
Dr. John Casper Branner, president
emeritus of the University, and form
fir head of the Department of Geology,
will return today to Arkansas where
he is in charge of a survey of the oil
resources of the state. He has been
visiting on the campus for a short,
time. Dr. Branner was state geologist
of Arkansas and Professor of Geology
at Indiana for several years before
coming to Stanford at its opening in
1891.
Mrs. Branner is visiting her family
in the East.
