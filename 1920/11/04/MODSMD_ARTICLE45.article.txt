# Women's Training Rules For Swimming Announced
## 
### 
Training for the women's interclass
swimming meet will start next week.
Strict adherence to training rules will
be kept this year. The period of
training will last until the meet on No
vember 22.
Slips may be obtained at Roble
gymnasium. These slips must be filled
out and turned in at the end of each
week. The training rules will be
posted on the bulletin board at the
gymnasium.
