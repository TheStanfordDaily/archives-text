# Roble and Row Women Will Exchange Dinners
## 
### 
A series of exchange dinners be
tween the women of Roble Hall and
the various sorority houses will be
gin on November 11, according to an
announcement made at the regular
meeting of Panhellenic Association in
the Women's Clubhouse, yesterday.
The second exchange dinner be
tween the Row and Hall women is
scheduled for November 16. There
will be eight women sent as guests
from each respective living group. On
December 12, there will be exchange
dinners between the houses alone.
The list of houses exchanging will be
announced at the meeting of Pan
hellenic to be held next Monday.
