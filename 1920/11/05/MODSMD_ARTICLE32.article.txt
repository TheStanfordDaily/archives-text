# Mary Matthews, '22, Is Appointed to Council
## 
### 
Women's Council in a meeting this
week appointed Mary Mathews '22 as a
member of the council to replace Liaine
Adrian '22, who has resigned because
of illness. Marjorie Bryant '21, secre
tary of council, has handed in her resig
nation and Helene Searcy '22 has been
chosen to take her place.
