# TELEPHONE OPERATOR KILLED WITH RAZOR
## 
### United Press 
ST. LOUIS, Mo., Nev. 8— Albert
Ellis, alleged murderer ,of eighteen
year-old Edna EHis, his former sweet
heart, is in jail today awaiting the action
of the grand jury.
The body of Edna Ellis, pretty tele
phone operator, was found in a vacant
lot Friday. She had been slashed num
erous times with a razor.
