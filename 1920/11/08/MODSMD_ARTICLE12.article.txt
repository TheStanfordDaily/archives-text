# CALIFORNIA FRESHMEN DEFEAT U. S. C. TEAM
## 
### 
Saturday afternoon at Los Angeles
the Southern California freshmen
went down to defeat to the tune of
14 to 6, at the hands of the University
of California freshmen. The game
was fast throughout, the first half
ending 6 to 0 in favor of the south
erners. In the second half the U. C.
freshmen stiffened and put over the
two winning touchdowns.
