# PARTY WILL VISIT ART EXHIBITION
## 
### 
Professor A. P>. Clark will accompany
a class to the Exhibition of Old Masters,
in San Francisco, tomorrow for the last
time. The party will leave Palo Alto at
8:33, returning some time that evening.
Students accompanying this class are
entitled to a special half-fare admission,
which can be obtained at the registrar's
office.
The party is not restricted to students
entirely, and all others interested in the
exhibition 'are cordially invited.
