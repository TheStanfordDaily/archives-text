# WOMEN TO ENROLL FOR TENNIS TODAY
## 
### 
Beginning today the enrollment
for Women's Tennis can be made for
the Fall Tournament at the Roble
Gymnasium. Everyone is urged to
sign up for this before Friday, No
vember 12.
Dates for the various matches will
be printed each day in the Daily Palo
Alto and all students participating are
requested to watch for these dates.

date, phone Frances Fish, tennis cap
tain.
