# ASSEMBLY WILL NOT BE HELD ARMISTICE DAY
## 
### 
No assembly will be held Armistice
Day because of the inability to obtain
a speaker, it was announced by Pro
fessor L. E. Bassett of the Public Exer
cises Committee today. Efforts were
made to get President David P. Bar
rows, Dean Charles M. Gayley of the
University of California, or Chester
Rowell, former owner of the Fresno Re
publican. but these men were unable to
come.
Armistice Day will not be a univer
sity holiday, it was announced yester
day by the President's Office.
