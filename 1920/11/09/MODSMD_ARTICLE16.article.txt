# 1000 ROOTERS CHEER VICTORIOUS VARSITY
## 
### 
Close to a thousand rooters welcomed
the Stanford Varsity home from its
victorious invasion of the north last
night at the depot.
Yell Leader Kinney was on bawd and
skyrockets for the team, for Coach
Powell and Captain Wilcox were given
as the men stepped off the train.
The Varsity left Seattle after its
game with the University of Washing
ton on Saturday, arriving in San Fran
cisco late yesterday afternoon.
The home-welcoming of the Varsity
developed into one of the best im
promptu rallies of the year, sound
making apparatuses of all kinds and de
scriptions adding to the noisy reception.
