# JOURNALISTIC SOCIETY INITIATES MEMBERS
## 
### 
Elaine Adrian, '22, was initiated into
Theta Sigma Phi, the women's journal
istic honor society, at a special initiation
held in the Women's Clubhouse last
night. Miss Adrian was elected to
membership in the society last spring
quarter, was absent on leave at the
time of the regular initiation. She has
again taken out a leave of absence from
college on account of ill health and left
for her home in the south following
the last- night.
