# EUPHRONIA CHOOSES MEN FOR DEBATE TEAM
## 
### 
E. D. Landels, '22, H. B. Alexander,
'22, H. A. Fendler, *22, and C. J. Cree
gan, '21, were chosen in the tryouts
held by Euphronja Debating Society
last night in Room 12, Law Building,
to represent the society in a contest
with the Congress Debating Society
of the University ot' California. In
the elimination tryouts next week,
these men will be chosen as the final
trio. The question discussed in last
night's meeting was, "Resolved, That
the United States should recognize
Soviet Russia."
