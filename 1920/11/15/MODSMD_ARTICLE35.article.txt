# STUDENT MADE $15,000 A YEAR
## ARTHUR MURRAY TELLS HOW HE WORKED WAY THROUGH COLLEGE 
### 
Realizing that many students would
like to profit by my experience and
earn money while going through col
lege, T have arranged especially for
college students a system which will
show you how to earn from $25 to
$100 a week by teaching dancing dur
ing spare time. To the right per
son, one who is willing to work, a
splendid opportunity for making
money and gaining valuable experi
ence presents itself.
If you can dance fairly well and
want to earn upwards of $25 a week
during spare time, inquire at the
office of the Daily Palo Alto. Write
today to Arthur Murray, 290 Broad
way, New York, N .Y.
