# Judge J. T. Nourse, '00 To Speak at Law Smoker
## 
### 
Judge J. I. Nourse, '00, of San Fran
cisco, will be the principal speaker at
the Law School Smoker Tuesday night
at 7:30 in the Union.
Dean C. A. Huston and Professor W.
B. Owens, of the Law Department, are
also scheduled for speeches during the
evening.
J. T. Reynolds and L. M. Lissner on
the piano and N. J. Nathanson on the
saxophone will keep the men in a good
humor until time for eats.
"The boxing matches are going to he
the goods, too," according to C. C.
Crosby, who has the arrangements in
chargc.
