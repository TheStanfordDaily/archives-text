# Roble Club and Row To Exchange Dinners
## 
### 
T he first of the Roble-Row exchange
dinners will be held tomorrow night.
One-half of the members of Roble Club
have signed up for dinner at the differ
ent houses and one-half of the girls in
the houses will take dinner at Roble.
The object of these dinners is to pro
mote acquaintance between the girls in
the different living units.
