# Pre-Game Dance Will Be Given In Palo Alto
## 
### 
A special pre-game dance is to be
given Friday night at the Woman's
Clubhouse in Palo Alto. Ambrose Whit
mer and his orchestra have'agreed to
play for it and it is expected to be one
of the biggest dances of the year. This
will be a final celebration in honor of
our football men and everyone is urged
to attend. The dance will start promptly
at 9 o'clock and will be informal. Ad
mission is $1.10.
