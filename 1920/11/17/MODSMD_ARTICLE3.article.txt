# CARD-BRUIN SOCCER COMBINE WILL GO TO CANADA IN HOLIDAYS
## 
### 
Soccer teams representing Stanford
and California will play in Vancouver
during the Christmas holidays. The
soccer men will go north with the rugby
representatives from the South, who
will play the annual championship game
with the Canadians. Several games with
local institutions and athletic clubs will
be staged by the soccer team. The
Northerners have guaranteed the team
$1500 to make the trip.
