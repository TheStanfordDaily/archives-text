# METALLURGY STUDENTS TO VISIT STEEL MILL
## 
### 
Students in metallurgy of iron and
steel who signed up to visit the plant
of the Pacific Coast Steel Co., Thursday
are directed hy Professor W. F. Diet
rich to meet at the S. P. depot tomorrow
to take the 1:20 train. The return trip
can he made on the train arriving in
Palo Alto at 4:03. Other members of
the class who intend making the trip
and have not signed up with Professor
Dietrich arc urged to do - so at once.
