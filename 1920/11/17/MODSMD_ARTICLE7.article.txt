# PROPERTY OF FILM STAR TO BE SOLD
## 
### United Press 
NEW YORK. Nov .17—The person
al property of Olive Thomas, noted
film star who died recently in Paris,
will be sold at public auction here
Monday. The articles which are now
on display include automobiles, jewel
ry, furs, and other personal belong
ings,
