# TRAIN ROBBERY HELD GREATEST IN YEARS
## 
### United Press 
OMAHA, Neb., Nov. 17.—That the
loss in the robbery of the Burlington
mail train at Council Bluffs Saturday
is the greatest in the history of crime
in*this country is the statement of an
oflicial investigating the case.
OMAHA, Neb., Nev. 17. —The rob
bers, who, last Saturday, robbed a
storage mail car on Burlington Road
at Council Bluffs, escaped with a
plunder amounting to $3,500,000, fed
eral officers announced here today.
