# FEDERAL STUDENTS WILL MEET FRIDAY
## 
### 
The Associated Federal Students will
hold an important meeting Friday at
12:45 in room 35 to receive final in
formation regarding the big smoker to
which they are invited by the U. C.
house. All details of the entertainment
will be provided by the Blue and Gold
men, so no Stanford Federal student
will need to pay out any money for
room or food Friday night or Saturday.
L. D. Denton, Divisional Vocational
Officer over professional trainers, will
be in room 35 from 10 o'clock till 5
tomorrow to issue pay checks.
