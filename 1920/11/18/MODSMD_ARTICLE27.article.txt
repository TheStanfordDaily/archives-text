# WOMEN ARE ASKED TO SIGN FOR TENNIS
## 
### 
All who intend to compete in the
women's tennis tournament must sign
up on the lists posted on the bulletin
board in Roble Gymnasium, before
Monday afternoon. The tourney will
begin shortly after Thanksgiving. The
contestants will compete for the Stan
ford Cup for Women's Tennis Singles.
The schedule for the matches will
be announced in the early part of
next week.
