# STRIKERS TAKE OVER MEXICAN COAL MINES
## 
### United Press 
EAGLE PASS. Tex, Nov. 18.—
Strikers in the coal regions of the Stale
of Coahuila, Mexico, have taken over
the mines, according to reports received
here today.
