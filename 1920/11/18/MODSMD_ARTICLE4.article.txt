# CORDA FRATRES WILL HEAR DR. D. S. JORDAN
## 
### 
Corda Fratres will meet next Mon
day night at the Clubhouse at 7:30. Dr.
Jordan will lecture on the Cosmopolitan
Movement. The meeting will be open
to the public. Following the meeting
the members will have a business meet
ing.
Dr. Jordan was one of the first inter
ested in the local organization, and his
talk will undoubtedly be of interest
to all.
