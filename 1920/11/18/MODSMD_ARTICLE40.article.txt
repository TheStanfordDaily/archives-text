# C.W. MIDGELY, '23, IS AWARDED SONG PRIZE
## 
### 
C. W. Midgley, '23, was awarded first
prize of $15 for his "Speller Song" at
a meeting of the committee in charge of
the song contest yesterday.
Midgley was also given the second
prize of $10 for his song "Sailing Up
the Bay," and the third prize of $5 went
to M. C. Higby, '22, author of the "Vic
tory Song." The prizes, consisting of
$30 in all, were contributed by the men
of Sequoia and Encina Halls.
