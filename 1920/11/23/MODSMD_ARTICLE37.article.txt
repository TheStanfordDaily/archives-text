# STANFORD FIFTEEN MEETS CALIFORNIA SQUAD DECEMBER 4
## 
### 
Stanford will meet the University
of California in the first of the two
game rugby series, Saturday, Decem
ber 4, on the Stanford turf. The sec
ond game is scheduled for December
11, on California Field.
Coach Maloney is working the Car
dinal fifteen hard every night in order
to develop team work for the Cali
fornia series. With no outstanding in
dividual stars on the squad, Maloney
is depending on team play for success.
Since the close of the American Foot
ball season, the Stanford rugby squad i
has been increased to over fifty can
didates. It is not yet known whether
or not the men coming in from the
American game will be eligible for
competition against the Blue and
Gold fifteen.
