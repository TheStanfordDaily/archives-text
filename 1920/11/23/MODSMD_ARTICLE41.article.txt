# SMOKER PLANNED FOR GEOLOGY DEPARTMENT
## 
### 
The combined Geology and Mining
Departments will put on their first
smoker at the Union. Wednesday even
ing, December 1. This meeting will be
the first big get-together of the depart
ments this year.
The smoker committee has been
planning an excellent entertainment for
the evening, and promises something
new in the way of stunts. Plenty of the
best campus syncopation and a fine col
lection of eats will complete the pro
gram. Nn charge will be made for the
smoker. New majors and lower division
men who expect to go into these depart
ments arc especially invited and urged
to attend.
