# Y. W. C. A. Not To Meet Because of Holidays
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Y. W.
C. A. will not be held this week on
account of the Thanksgiving Holidays.
As the Y. W. did not meet last week
on account of the Big Game it will
be necessary for those in charge of
Y. W. meetings to plan some interest
ing session in order to keep up the
record for this quarter's work.
