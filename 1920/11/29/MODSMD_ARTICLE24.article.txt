# ARMED LEAGUE FORCE READY TO LEAVE FOR POLAND WEDNESDAY
## 
### United Press 
GENEVA. Nov. 29.—The League of
Nations' first armed force will leave for
Vilna Wednesday, it was announced
here today.
British, French and Spanish troops
will comprise the detachment sent to
decide whether Vilna will be assigned to
Poland or Lithuania. —
STOCKHOLM. Nov. 29.—The Swed
ish government has decided to assign
troops to the League of Nations' de
tachment sent to supervise the Polish
elections.
