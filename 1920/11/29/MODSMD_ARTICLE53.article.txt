# Mining Students Will Hold Smoker Wednesday
## 
### 
Geology and Mining Department
smoker, to be held Wednesday evening
at 7:30 in the Union, is to lie a top
notcher, according to R. Willis, chair
man of the Smoker Committee.
Varied entertainment, as well as the
customary speeches and boxing matches,
concluded by eats, is in store for the
members of this department.
