# On'n Off the Campus
## 
### 
Campus is Scene of
Holiday Festivities
The campus was the scene of much
celebration ancf Thanksgiving over the
holiday. Besides hiking and horse
back riding parties, many dances were
given. On Wednesday night, the
Alpha Tau Omega fraternity gave a
formal dinner dance, the Phi Kappa
Sigma gave an informal dinner dance,,
and the Beta Theta Pi, and Delta Chi
fraternities entertained with inform
el dances.
Thursday night, some of the houses
entertained their friends with Thanks
giving turkey dinners. Among these
were the Gamma Phi Beta and Delta
Upsilon houses.
