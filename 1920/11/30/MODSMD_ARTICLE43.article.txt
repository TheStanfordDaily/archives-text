# MASQUERS WILL HOLD TRYOUTS THURSDAY
## 
### 
Open try-outs for Masquer's play to
be produced January 21 in the As
sembly Hall will be held Thursday
December 9, between 3 and 5 in the
afternoon and between 7 and 8 in the
evening. Coach Gordon Davis will
hold the try-outs in the Assembly
Hall. The play is to be a comedy and
will give a wide opportunity for char
acter work, in both men and women
parts.
It will be unusual in that it will be
a period play, and all the originality
of the Masquer's Society will be put
in the costuming and scenic effects.
