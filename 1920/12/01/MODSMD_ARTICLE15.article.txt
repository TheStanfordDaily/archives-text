# Football Men Will Be Photographed Sunday
## 
### 
Captain Art Wilcox of the Varsity
football squad of 1920 requests that all
the lettermen of the squad arrange to
get their suits before Sunday, so that
they may go to Davey's studio in full
regalia Sunday at 10 a. m.
The Quad wants a good picture of
this year's squad and asks that all mem
bers appear on time. .
