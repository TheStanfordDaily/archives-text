# PROFESSOR FOSTER TO ADDRESS CLASSICAL CLUB THIS EVENING
## 
### 
Professor B. O. Foster of the Latin
department will give a lecture on
Lucretius, the great Roman poet,
under the auspices of the Classical
Club, tonight at 8 o'clock at the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
The Classical Club is one of the
oldest organizations on the campus.
Its object is to interest the students
in the different phases of Gjeek and
Roman civilization. Any one inter
ested is invited to attend. After the
lecture refreshments will be served.
