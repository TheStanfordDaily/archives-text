# CAMERA CLUB MEETS, CONSIDERS NEW NAME
## 
### 
Camera Club considered a new name
for its organization at a meeting of the
members held last night. Various names
have been suggested and a committee
has been appointed to investigate and
report those considered most suitable.
Ways and means and membership com
mittees were also appointed.
New officers were elected last night
as follows: President. C. S. Draper;
Vice-President, J. S. Cary; Secretary-
Treasurer, K. S. Ritchie.
