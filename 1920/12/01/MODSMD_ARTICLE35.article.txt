# FROSH SOCCER SQUAD PLAY BEARS SATURDAY
## 
### 
The freshman soccer team will meet
the Berkeley freshmen on the Berkeley
Field next Saturday. This is the second
game of the series, the lirst one having
been played here and resulting in a 3-3
tie. Both teams are evenly matched and
a hotly contested game is to be expected.
The addition of some of the freshmen
football men to the squad will put more
pep into the playing. The freshmen
have been practicing hard and the men
are showing good form. The lineup for
the start of the game will be published
later.
