# ECONOMICS CLUB WILL HEAR GRADE LECTURE
## 
### 
Initiation of new members and an
address by C. C. Collman will be the
main attractions at the meeting of the
Economics Club in the reading room of
the Union at 7:30 tomorrow evening.
Mr. Collman is chief of publicity for
the San Francisco Foreign Trade Club,
and will speak on the trade relations
between the Philippine and the
United States.
This will be the first of a series of
meetings in which prominent speakers
will be secured from the Foreign Trade
Club, according to Washington Con
nolly, president of the Economics Club.
