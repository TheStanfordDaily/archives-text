# THOMAS GREEN RESIGNS FROM EX COMMITTEE
## 'Bob' Shlaudeman Appointed Junior Representative 'Til the Election Next Quarter 
### 
Due to the resignation of Thomas
Green from the Executive Committee,
it was decided at a meeting of the
Committee last night to appoint "Bob"
Sclilaudeman as temporary repre
sentative for the class of 1922. The
first of next quarter a Student Body
election will be held for the purpose
of electing a permanent representa
tive on the committe and to elect of
ficers to fill vacant places in class
offices.
The minutes of the meeting were as
follows:
Vice-President Hill in the chair.
Absent: Ross, Adams,-McElroy.
M. S. C. —That the resignation of
Thomas Green as 1922 representative
on the Executive Committee be ac
cepted.
M. S. C. —That Robert Shlaudeman
be temporarily appointed as 1922 rep
resentative on the Executive Com
mittee.
M. S. C. —That the following dates
be granted: January 29, Beta Theta
Pi; February 4, Delta Delta Delta;
April 5, Beta Theta Pi; April 29, Kap
pa Alpha Theta.
M. S. C. —To adjourn.
J. EDWIN SIMPSON,
