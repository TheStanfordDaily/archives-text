# MASONIC CLUB HOLDS DECEMBER MEETING IN CLUBHOUSE, 7:30
## 
### 
Meeting for the last time this year,
the Stanford Masonic Club will hold
its December session in the Women's
Clubhouse this evening at 7:30. Cur
rent business will not only be considered
but several vital policies will be dis
cussed in this evening's meeting.
John Wicher, Grand Secretary of the
California Masons, is expected to arrive
on the campus today and will deliver
the main address of the evening. As
honorary members, several of the faculty
will be initiated tonight. The club plans
to serve refreshments following the dis
cussion and speeches.
