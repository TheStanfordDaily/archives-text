# Berkeley Pastor Will Speak Here Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Harold E. B. Speight of the
First Unitarian Church, Berkeley, will
deliver the 11 o'clock sermon at the
Memorial Church Sunday. The musical
program will consist of two selections
by Mrs. Esther L. Paul, contralto, and
Mr. Philip H. Richards, tenor. They
will be "Grant Us Thy Peace," by
Gounod, and "Consider and Hear Me,"
by Pflueger.
Dr. Allen will give his regular organ
recital at 4 p. m.
