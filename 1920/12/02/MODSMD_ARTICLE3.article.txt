# FRESHMAN AND JUNIOR HOCKEY TEAMS MEET IN FOURTH GAME TODAY
## 
### 
The junior and freshmen women's
hockey teams will meet for their first
match in the final rounds of the in
tercluss series this afternoon, at
4:30 on the Roble field. So far in
the series the juniors have played and
won one game and the freshmen have
lost the single match in which they
played.
Should the juniors win the match
today the senior and junior teams
will be tied for first place and the
freshmen and sophomores tied for
third place on the list. Up to date the
senior team heads the list, having
won two games, the juniors second
with one match and one victory to
their credit, the frosh third having
lost but one match and the sophomores
last, having lost both matches so far.
