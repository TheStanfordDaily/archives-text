# NESTORIA PLANS TO LAUNCH CAMPAIGN FOR NEW AMENDMENT
## 
### 
The drawing card at a meeting of
Nestoria Debating Society, to be held at
room 12 of the Law Building at 7
o'clock tonight, will be a discussion of a
proposed amendment to the constitution
of the Associated Students. The nature
of the amendment has not been made
public, but it is understood that should
members sanction the move the society
will launch a campaign for its passage.
At the meeting parliamentary law and
procedure will be discusscd and a mock
trial held. Nestoria has accepted the
following question for debate with Santa
Clara here on January 7: "Resolved,
That one of the great reconstructive
measures needed to allay the present
social unrest, is the passage by Congress
of a more stringent and comprehensive
immigration law."
