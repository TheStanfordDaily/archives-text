# Quad Junior Section Will Not Be Closed
## 
###

Junior Section of the Quad has not yei
been set, contrary to announcement
made last evening. Although it is im
portant that the juniors have their pic
tures taken as soon as possible, the sec
tion will not be closed at the end of
the present quarter.
