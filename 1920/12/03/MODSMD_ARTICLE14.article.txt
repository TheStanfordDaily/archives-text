# 135-POUND TEAM TO BE ORGANIZED SOON
## 
### 
Men wlio are too light to make the
varsity basketball team will have an
opportunity to tryout for the 135-pound
team which is to be organized at En
cilia Gymnasium. Those who are in
terested are requested to sign their
names to a list on the bulletin board.
The first meeting of the team will
be held next Tuesday night at the
gymnasium from 8 to 8:30 o'clock.
The team is to play games with bay
city clubs and possibly one with the
135-pound team of the University of
California. Candidates are limited
only in weight and scholarship.
