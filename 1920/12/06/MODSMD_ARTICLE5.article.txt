# OHIO STATE TEAM TO BE STANFORD GUESTS
## 
### 
Ohio State's football team will stop
off at Stanford on their way down to
Pasadena, according to a telegram re
ceived this afternoon by Dr. W. D.
Browne from Director of Athletic Act
ivities, St. John of Ohio State. The
exact date of arrival will be telegraph
ed as soon as it is determined. The
invading team will be the guests of
Stanford at a luncheon to be given
the day they arrive. The stop will
probably only be over one day or part
fo a day, as the team is anxious to
get to the battle grounds as soon as
possible.
