# Encina Reservations To Be Made This Week
## 
### 
Men living in Encina Hall who wisi;
to retain their present rooms for the
winter quarter have been asked to notify
the office of the Dean of Men Thursday
or Friday of this week. It will be neces
sary to make a $5 deposit at the time.
Next week drawings will be held in the
hall for the remaining rooms.
Reservations for rooms in Sequoia
Hall will not be made until the early
part of next week.
