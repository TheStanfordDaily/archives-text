# University Problems Discussed in Meeting of Skull and Snakes
## 
### 
Skull and Snakes Society held a joint
meeting last night with prominent
faculty members and alumni at the Phi
Gamma Delta house for the purpose of
discussing important university matters
that have presented themselves during
the course of the quarter. A committee
was appointed to make recommendations
to the University authorities embodying
the subjects that were discussed. The
committee consists of J. T. Murphy,
'20, W. I. Ames, '18, Dr. T. M. Willioms.
'97, and Leland Cutler, '06, a member of
the Board of Trustees.
