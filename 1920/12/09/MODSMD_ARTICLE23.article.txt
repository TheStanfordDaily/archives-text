# ZETA PSI ANNOUNCES NEW LAMBDA CHAPTER
## 
### 
At a recent installation of the Pin
Gamma Lambda Chapter of the Zeta
Psi Fraternity at the University of
Washington, Dr. Henry Suzzallo. presi
dent of the university, was elected to
membership - and initiated. Also two
other members of the faculty were ini
tiated. The other men chosen were Dr.
Richard F. Scholz, professor of history,
and Dr. Robert Max Garrett, assistant
'professor of English. The installation
ceremonies were held in the New Wash
ington Hotel. Suzzallo was a Stanford
graduate.
