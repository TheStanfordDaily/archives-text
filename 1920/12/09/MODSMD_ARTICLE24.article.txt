# Economics Department Announces New Course
## 
### 
Nathaniel Sanders, instructor in eco
nomics .announces a new course which
will be given next quarter, to be known
as Economics 65. It will be a teachers'
course, open to juniors, seniors, and
graduates who have completed a course
in Gregg shorthand, touch typewriting,
and at least one accounting course. It
will deal with methods of teaching com
mercial subjects, a discussion of the
commercial course of study, and the re
lation of the commercial to the academic
department of the high school.
These units will count towards educa
tion requirements, but will not satisfy
economics requirements. The course will
be given Mondays, Wednesdays and Fri
days at 11 o'clock.
