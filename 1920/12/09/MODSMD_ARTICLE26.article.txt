# 1921 Quads To Be Sent Free To High Schools
## 
### 
All students desiring 1921 Quads sent
to their respective high schools may
have these books sent by communicating
with Floyd L. McElroy, editor of the
1921 year book. McElroy has announced
that a number of these books have been
made available and may be sent free of
charge to schools which may have pros
pective students.
