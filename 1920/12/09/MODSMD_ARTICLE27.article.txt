# Excused Deficiencies Must Go To Whittier
## 
### 
Students who during the present
quarter have been handicapped in
their studies by unavoidable circum
stances such as illness, outside work,
calls home, or the like should report
the facts to the chairman of the Com
mittee on Scholarship. Mr. Whittier
will be in his office, Room 3, in the
Law Building, for the purpose of re
ceiving such statements on Tuesday
afternoon, December 14, 1920.
