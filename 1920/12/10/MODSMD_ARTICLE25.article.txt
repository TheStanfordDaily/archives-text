# SCHOLARSHIPS OPEN FOR APPLICATIONS
## 
### 
There are available for award at
the beginning of the winter quarter
two Marcelle Henrietta Rouiller, '15,
memorial tuition scholarships, of $120
each.
There is also to be awarded the
Gertrude M. Gardiner Roble scholar
ship for the present year, beginning
from December 1, 1920. This amount
is approximately $155.
Applications for these scholarships
may be filed any time before noon of
Friday, January 7, 1921.
Application blanks may be obtain
ed at the Registrar's Office.
