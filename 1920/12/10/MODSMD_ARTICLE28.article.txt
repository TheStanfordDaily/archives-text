# U. of Oregon Women Plan Christmas Ball
## 
### 
The Women's League of the Uni
versity of Oregon is planning for a
Christmas ball to be given December
28, at the Multnomah Hotel, Portland,
Oregon. It has written the Daily Palo
Alto, and wishes to extend through it
a cordial invitation to all Stanford
students to attend the dance. Tickets
can be obtained at the door.
