# BIONOMICS COURSE IS NEXT QUARTER
## 
### 
A course in bionomics will be offered
next quarter at 11 o'clock on Tuesdays
and Thursdays, the course being for
two hours of credit. The course was
originally scheduled for this quarter, but
was not able to be given on account of
conflict.
Students who took the course last
year will remember the interesting lec
tures given by Dr. Jordan. He has again
offered to give some of the lectin es
during the quarter.
Professor R. W. Doane will have
charge of the course and is anxious that
students desirous of taking the subject
sign up before the end of the quarter.
Upperclassmen are eligible for registia
tion in .the course.
