# ENGLISH 19 B WILL BE GIVEN SPRING QUARTER
## 
### 
Course 1915. the study of one-act plays,
scheduled to be given by Gordon Davis
in the spring quarter, has been changed
to the winter quarter. Davis requests
that the people intending to take this
course see him personally in his office,
Room 20715 of the English Department,
at 11 o'clock on Monday, Wednesday,
Thursday, or Friday. The course will
be given on Tuesdays from 1 to 2 and
on Thursdays from 1 to 3.
—Get Behind the Daily—
