# FRESHMEN WILL GIVE DANCE NEXT QUARTER
## 
### 
Plans for a dance to be given by
the freshman class are being complet
ed at present. A committee appointed
by Orion Clark, class president, is
busy with the details for the dance.
The committee is composed of the
following members of the class of '24:
Burton S. Hard, chairman; Y. E.
Creighton and D. D. Gage. The date
set for the dance is January 15, and
the place, the Women's Clubhouse.
Good music is assured for the oc
casion, and an effective decoration
scheme is now being worked out by
the committee in charge.
