# Officers Are Elected By Federal Students
## 
### 
J. B. McNally, '23, was elected pre
sident of the Associate Federal Stu
dents for the winter quarter, at a
meeting in the Union, last night. The
other officers elected are: D. W. Mont
gomery, '22, vice-president; H. J.
Beaver, '21, secretary; E. H. Avres.
'24, treasurer, and A. C. Lane, '23. and
W. L. Bradshaw, *24, executive com
mittee.

Plans were announced for the recep
tion of now trainees next quaiter,
when a considerable increase in the
number of men in training under the
Federal Board is expected.
