# Eighty New Students Will Enter in January
## 
### 
Thirty women and fifty lower divi
sion men will be admitted to the Uni
versity on registration day for the
winter quarter, Monday, January 3,
according to information given out
from the Registrar's office today. At
least half of the thirty women will be
freshmen. The number of men with
advanced standing who will enter has
not yet been announced.

For the first time in the history of
the Univeisity the list of students
matriculating in the winter quarter
will have been filled entirely from
applicants in the third group, and will
have been closed before the end of
the autumn quarter.
