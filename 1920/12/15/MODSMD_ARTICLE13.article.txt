# Students Must Leave Envelopes For Grades
## 
### 
All students who wish to have their
grades mailed to them as soon as the
cards ready during the Christmas
vacation have been asked to leave
stamped, addressed envelopes at the
Registrar's office in the Administra
tion Building, before the close of the
quarter. No information will be given
out from the office except through the
mail until registration day of the
v,'inter quarter.
