# ARTICLES BY ALUMNI APPEAR IN "FREEMAN"
## 
### 
There are articles by Robert L. Duf
fus, '11, and Harry W. Frantz, '17, in
the December issue of "The Freeman."
Duffus, who is now a member of the
editorial staff of the New York
"Globe," contributes a book review to
the periodical. Duffus was formerly
on the staff of several San Francisco
papers, and a member of the English
department here.
Frantz writes of a trip afoot in the
Yellowstone Park.
