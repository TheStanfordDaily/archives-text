# Cosmopolitan Club To Elect Officers
## 
### 
The Cosmopolitan Club will hold a'
special business meeting in the Wom
an's Clubhouse at 7 :30 tonight to com -
plete arrangements for "Chinese Night"
and to elect officers for the new quarter.

by the organization during the coming
year in an attempt to make the foreign
students on the campus feel more at
home. Admission to the affair will be
by invitation only.
All of the members of the chib are
