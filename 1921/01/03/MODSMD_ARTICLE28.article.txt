# Professor E. W. Smith Attends Convention
## 
### 
Professor Everett W. Smith, head
of the journalism department, is due
on the campus this afternoon from a
national convention of journalists
which was held in St. Louis last week.
Primonent newspapermen and heads
of schools of journalism from all parts
of the country attended the convention
and Professor Smith is returning with
many valuabl ideas. He left the
campus Christmas morning.
