# BIDS FOR COMMONS ARE OPENED TODAY
## 
### 
Bids for the new Stanford commons
are being opened today in San Fran
cisco. The new building, which will in
clude a large dining hall on the first
floor and several rooms on the second
floor, will he the first of the proposed
additions to be constructed. It will be
located between the present Women's
Clubhouse and Men's Union, and will
be connected with the latter.
Comptroller "Roth is in the city today
on business connected with letting the
bids. The Board of Trustees will act
on these within a few days, reserving
the right to reject any and all contracts.
