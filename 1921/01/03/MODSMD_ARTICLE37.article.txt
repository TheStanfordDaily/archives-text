# On'n Off the Campus
## 
### 
Mrs. Hoover Will
Explain Relief
Mrs. Herbert Hoover will speak on
the Near East Relief Work at a meet
ing of the Palo Alto Women's Club,
to be held Wednesday in the Club
house in Palo Alto.
Professor Webster
To Give Lecture
Professor David L. Webster, head
of the Physics department, with Pro
fessor E. P. Lewis of the University
of California, are giving a series of
lectures at the auditorium of Golden
Gate Park in San Francisco, on
"Atomic Structure and Radiation."
The first lecture was given yester
day.
