# Knockers Featured In Complaint Number of Chaparral Next Week
## 
### 
Campus grouches will have their in
ning next Monday when the complaint
number of the Chappie appears. Many
and varied slams are taken at the tuition
increase, and the usual barrage is hurled
at the campus women.
Thomas Kleckner, '19, takes a leading
role among campus knockers by his se
ries of slams. Drawings by Janice Dun
ker, '22, Warren Gilbert, '20, and Jas.
Wallace, '17, will be the chief art fea
tures of this issue. "A Foolish Fable"
will be one of the leading articles.
