# PROFESSOR CARRUTH TO TAKE VACATION DURING THIS QUARTER
## 
### 
Dr. William Herbert Carruth, head
of the English Department, is taking
lis vacation this quarter under the
advice of his physicians. He will
spend the winter at Carmel.
Professor Carruth's health gave out
at the end of the last quarter through
overwork and his physicians advised
a complete rest. He therefore deter
mined to take his annual vacation at
this time with the idea of returning
to the University at the beginning of
the spring quarter.
During his absence Dr. Raymond
M. Alden will be acting head of the
English Department. Professor Al
den has not yet returned from the
East where ie has jbeen attending
several meetings and Dr. Henry D.
Gray is temporarily acting as exe
cutive.
