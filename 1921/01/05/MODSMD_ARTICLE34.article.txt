# THREE SCHOLARSHIPS FOR NEXT YEAR OPEN
## 
### 
The Committee on Scholarships asks
that all students wishing to apply for
scholarships make their applications at
once, as the period for receiving peti
tions will close at noon next Friday.
The communication of the committee is
printed below:
"There arc available for award at the
beginning of the winter quarter two
Marcelle Henriette Rouiller, '15, Memo
rial Tuition Scholarships of $120 each.
"There is also to be awarded the
Gertrude M. Gardiner Roble Scholar
ship for the year beginning December 1,
1920. This will amount to approximate
ly $155.
"Applications for these scholarships
may be filed at any time before noon of
Friday, January 7, 1921.
"Application blanks may he obtained
at the Registrar's office.
Oi
