# GLEE CLUB LOOKING FOR NEW STUNT MEN
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club is planning a
number of conccrts for this quarter, be
ginning in the latter part of February.
They will entertain on the campus and
at nearby places. During the spring va
cation the men will go to the Hawaiian
Islands to give a number of conccrts.
For all of these programs, the club
wishes to have a quantity of unusual
numbers and is looking for stunt men.
Any students who have ever done any
work of this sort or who feel that they
can work into it, have been asked to
give their names as soon as possible to
F. 11. Goddard, '20, at the Alpha Sigma
Phi House. It is necessary that they
report early in order that the programs
may be planned.
