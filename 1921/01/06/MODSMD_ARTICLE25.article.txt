will be considered for positions.
(Mrs.) Elizabeth Snell,
Appointment Secretary.
CHANGE IN LAW PROGRAM
The course in Rule Against Perpet
uities has been withdrawn and the
course in Wills is substituted for it.
This course will meet for the present
Monday, Tuesday and Thursday, at 8
o'clock, and Wednesday at 3:05. Some
adjustment of the afternoon hour may
be made after the class has met.
The first meeting of the class will be
Monday. The text is Grav's Cases on
Property, Vol. IV (2 ed.).* The fcourse
will carry four hours credit.
Students wishing to change their reg
istration in order to enter this course
may obtain petitions to change from
Miss McKendry.

CHANGE IN LAW PROGRAM
The course in Rule Against Perpet
uities has been withdrawn and the
course in Wills is substituted for it.
This course will meet for the present
Monday, Tuesday and Thursday, at 8
o'clock, and Wednesday at 3:05. Some
adjustment of the afternoon hour may
be made after the class has met.
The first meeting of the class will be
Monday. The text is Grav's Cases on
Property, Vol. IV (2 ed.).* The fcourse
will carry four hours credit.
Students wishing to change their reg
istration in order to enter this course
may obtain petitions to change from
Miss McKendry.
