# BOXING SCHEDULE WILL BE MADE SOON
## 
### 
Arrangements for this coming quar
ter in Joe Murphy's boxing arena have
not as yet been completed. The re
sults of the last quarter's fast gruell
ing enable many young protegees to
come into prominence.
Last quarter the enrollment in the
manly art of self-defense far exceed
ed that of any preceding year or
quarter in the history of the univer
sity. The class was so large that its
division into sections was the only
remedy for the overcrowded condi
tion. .
