# WOMEN RUSHING ENDS AT 8 P. M. TOMORROW, BIDDING WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Formal rushing for the women will
end at 8 o'clock tomorrow night. The
last night, which is a dinner date from
6 to 8, is considered as preferewce night,
and the freshmen women are expected
to give it to the sorority they prefer.
Each house will send in the names of
the women whom it has bid to the lawyer
by 9 o'clock tomorrow night. On
Wednesday morning the freshmen who
have received bids will be nottified to
appear at the lawyer's office, where they
will state their preference of houses.
The final decisions will be reported to
the various sororities some time on
Wednesday morning.
