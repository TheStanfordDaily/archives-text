# COLLEGE WIT CONTEST CLOSES JANUARY 14
## 
### 
Entries in the College Wit Contest
conducted by Judge must be filed before
Friday, January 14. Prizes arc to be
given for the best, contributions, and all
accepted articles or drawings will be
paid for by Judge. The points of value
for all contributions are to be tabulated,
so that each university will get credit
for the work by its students, The
Chaparral has always rated high among
the magazines of college wit and Stan
ford is expected to make a record in this
contest. Contributions may be mailed
to Campus Box 15 before Friday, Jan
uary 14, or sent directly to Judge.
