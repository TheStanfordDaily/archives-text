# Junipero Serra Club Meeting Is Postponed
## 
### 
The Junipero Serra Club will not
meet Thursday night, as previously an
nounced, on account of the Ncstoria-
Santa Clara debate coming on that date.
The meeting will be held a week later,
at 8 o'clock, in the Women's Clubhouse.
The program will include a discussion
of "Spiritualism," and plans will be
made for the annual club dance to bo
given some time in February.
