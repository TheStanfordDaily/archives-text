# Cercle Francais Will Meet Wednesday Evening
## 
### 
The Cercle Francais will hold their
first, meeting this quarter, Wednesday
evening at 8 o'clock, in the Women's
Clubhouse. After a short business
meeting there will be a discussion of
a play to be presented this quarter.
It is expected that the dramatic pro
duction of this quarter will follow the
precedent of last, and he either a
one art play or a farce sketch.
