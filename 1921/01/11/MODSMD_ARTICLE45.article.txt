# ASSEMBLY HALL WILL HAVE NEW EQUIPMENT
## 
### 
New electrical equipment is to be
purchased for the Assembly Hall, ac
cording to a vote passed at a meeting
of the Dramatic Council yesterday
afternoon. The old wiring system was
a very dangerous one and if any care
lessness was exercised it might have
resulted in a short-circuit or a fire.
The new equipment wiH be a set of
plugging boxes with heavy corded
wire that is more practical and effici
ent, and a great deal safer than the
old equipment, according to business
manager Milton Bulkley, who has been
investigating the matter.
The new poster boxes will soon be
in use, it was also announced at the
meeting. The Dramatic Council is
making these and many other im
provements under the new system of
management.
