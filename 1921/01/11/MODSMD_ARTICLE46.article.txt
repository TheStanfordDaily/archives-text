# INTRAMURAL SOCCER MEN MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Three fraternity soccer teams and one
Sequoia team have organized for the
intramural league. It is planned to
organize one off-campus team and
three from Encina.
Soccer enthusiasts of Encina are hold
ing a meeting in the clubroom tonight
at 6:45 to give everyone interested a
chance to sign up.
# Each team will have an equal number
of varsity soccer men as a nucleus.
This arrangement is intended to secure
a well-balanced league and keen com
petition. Representatives of each team
will meet this week to draw up sched
ules of games.
