TUESDAY
SCHUBERT CLUB —In room 84, 5
o'clock. Election of officers.
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON—Meets at
Breakers' Club, at 7:15.
EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE—Meets in
Administration Blag., at 7:30.
All battery men will report to Duffy
Seay in the gymnasium, to be allotted
time for pre-season baseball practice.
Every man who has ever pitched or
caught any baseball, please report.
Important.
THURSDAY
SKULL AND SNAKES —Meets at the
Union, at 7:30.
EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY
—Meets in Room 12, Law Building,
at 7:30 p.m.
AMERICAN JOURNALISTS' ASSO
CIATION (Stanford Chapter) —In
Room 3, Press Building, at 7:15 p.m.,
Business Meeting.
ZOOLOGY CLUB—Meets at the Sig
ma Kappa house, at 8 p. m. All
interested are urged to attend and
discuss important business of the
.club.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Applications for graduate scholar
ships may be made at the Registrar's
office and must be submitted before
Thursday noon, January 13.
