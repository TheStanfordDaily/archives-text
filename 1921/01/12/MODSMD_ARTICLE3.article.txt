# Officers Elected By Stanford Women's Club
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Club of San
Francisco elected now officers at a
luncheon meeting held Saturday at
the Fairmont Hotel. Mrs. Verne V.
W. Mc.Kinney was made president;
Mrs. A. S. Kalenborn, first-vice presi
dent; Mrs. A. H. Sheehan, secretary;
Miss Lydia Boezinger, secretary. Mrs.
E. B. Warren, Mrs. Gertrude Peck
man, Mrs. E. D. Adams, and Mrs. H.
R. Harvey were elected two-year di
rectors Those elected last year and
still serving are Misses Georgia Cdt
ler, Joyce Gardiner, Eva Poarsall and
Doris Estcourt.
