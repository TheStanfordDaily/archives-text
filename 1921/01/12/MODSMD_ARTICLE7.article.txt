# NEW DORMITORY LOT IS ALMOST CLEARED
## 
### 
Work of clearing the land at the rear
of the clubhouse in preparation for the
new dormitory is proceeding rapidly.
The house-movers rolled off the band
stand yesterday and the benches were
carried off in wagons. Other men are
at work clearing up the tract in prepara
tion for the foundation work which will
commence shortly. It is planned to have
the new building ready for occupation
at the beginning of the next fall quarter.
