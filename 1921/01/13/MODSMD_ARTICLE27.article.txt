# To Hold Meeting Tonight Journalists Association
## 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of the Amer
ican Journalists' Association will meet
tonight at 7:15 in Room 3 of the Press
Building. The meeting will he an open
one and everyone interested in journal
ism is invited to attend.
Mr. Smith, professor in Journalism, is
chairman of the local chapter. He will
report on a meeting he had during the
Christmas vacation in St. Louis with
R. L. Stokes, chairman of the national
body, in which various matters were
discussed of interest to the local chapter.
