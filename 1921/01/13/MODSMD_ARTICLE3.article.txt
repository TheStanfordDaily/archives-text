# Roy Collom, '06, To Head State Gas Inspection
## 
### 
Roy E. Collom, geology and mining,
*06, has been appointed head of the
State Oil and Gas Inspection Service.
Mr. Collom has been with the Federal
Bureau of Mines for the last year and
a half. Previously he was with the
chief deputy state oil and gas inspector.
Collom succeeds R. P. McLaughlin,
geology, '02, who has resigned the office
to go into the oil business. The office
of inspector in the State Gas and Oil
Inspection Service carries a salary of
$6000 per annum.
