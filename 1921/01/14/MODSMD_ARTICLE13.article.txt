# Col. Potter, '01, To Be New Adjutant-General
## 
### 
Col. William Durrning Potter, '01.
formerly stationed at Camp Fremont,
has been recommended to succeed Col.
H. S. Hayward, as Adjutant-general of
the Hawaiian National Guard. rec
ommendation is yet to be confirmed by
President Wilson. Col. Potter is by
profession a consulting engineer. He
is a veteran of the Spanish-American
war, and fought in the Philippine insur
rection and the World war. During the
bubonic plague epidemic of 19(X) in
Honolulu, he organized a sanitation
corps of citizen guards.
