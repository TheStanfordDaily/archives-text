# University of California To Play Boston College
## 
### 
Negotiations preparatory to a football
game between the University of Cali
fornia and the Boston College team to
be played at Boston next fall are under
way, according to the statement of
Frank Reynolds, graduate manager of
athletics at Boston College. The Cali
fornians have been invited to play on
November 5 and California has already
taken the matter into consideration and
the game in all probability will take
place.
