# AUDIENCE PLEASED BY ALLEN'S ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
The weekly Thursday evening Organ
Recital was given last evening by Or
ganist W. D. Allen, in the Memorial
Church before a large and appreciative
audience.

Allen was as follows:
Gothic Suite Lean Boellman
Choral Introduction
Minuet
Pierre a la Vierge
At the Cradle Side .... Hugo Goodman
Pilgrim Chorus (Tannhauser)
R. Wagner
