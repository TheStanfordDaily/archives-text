# Foster, Ex-'21, Married To Northern Girl
## 
### 
Gilbert Foster, ex-'2l, resigned his
position January 8, 1921, as editor of the
University of Washington Daily to be
married to Virginia Rush, '23, Washing
ton. They were married in Lewiston,
Idaho, January 10. Foster was a former
member of the staff of The Daily Palo
Alto during the first quarter of his at
tendance at Stanford, and is a member
of Alpha Delta Phi.
