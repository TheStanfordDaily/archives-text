# Peninsula Legion Posts Form Basketball League
## 
### 
The Peninsula American Legion
Basketball League wtyich was formed
by Harry Maloney some time ago is
is preparing for the coming season.
Mr. Maloney has asked that all mem
bers of Fremont Post No. 52 who are
interested in basketball, to report to
him as soon as possible so that active
practice can begin. A trophy is being
offered for the winner of the league
which consists of the legion posts
from San Mateo, Redwood, Palo Alto,
Mountain View, Santa Clara, Los
Gatos, and San Jose, and Maloney is
very desirous of obtaining the cup for
the local post.
