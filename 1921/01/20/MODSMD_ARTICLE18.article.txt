# Social Workers Will Meet In San Francisco
## 
### 
The California State Conference of
Social Agencies will hold its annual
meeting in San Francis .o the third week
of next month. A special program is
being arranged with speakers of wide
reputation. All types of social work
will be represented, and such subjects
as social work in rural communities, the
standard of living, the training of the
social workers, and health will have a
place on the program.
