# Duffy Seay Declines Recent Coaching Offer
## 
### 
Coach Duffy Seay announced last
evening that he had refused the invita
tion extended to him by Occidental Col
lege to become their varsity coach.
The vacancy of varsity coach was
created by the recent resignation of
Coach Stanton, which will take effcct at
the close of this season. Coach Stanton
in retiring, on account of health, ad
vised as a selection as his successor
Coach Seay, who, as a student at Occi
dental, made a record as an all-round
athlete, and who, since his graduation,
has been coaching various teams in
Southern California, until he came to
Stanford.
