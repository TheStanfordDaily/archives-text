# ELIMINATION BOXING BOUTS START TODAY
## 
### 
The first series of elimination bouts
are slated to take place at 4:30 this
afternoon in Encina Gymnasium. This
tournament is to he held every after
noon of this week and the first part
01 next week. The winners of these
bouts are assured of a position on the
varsity and freshman teams according
to their standing in the University.
The preceding bouts of this kind have
always furnished good material for
Coach Murphy's squad and the bouts
themselves prove interesting to the spec
tators.
