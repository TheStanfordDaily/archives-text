# Delta Gamma Will Give Nickel Dance As Benefit For War Orphans in Belgium
## 
### 
Tomorrow evening Delta Gamma will
give a benefit dance in their house for
the war orphan home which the national
organization of the sorority has estab
lished in Belgium. The affair will be in
the nature of a nickel dance. Partners
have been promised for those who come
unattached and special arrangements will
be made for the "queeners."
Dancing will begin at 8:30 o'clock and
last until midnight. In addition to the
usual entertainment, punch will be served
and there will be concessions of various
kinds to earn money for the needy
Belgian boys and girls.
Whitmer's Orchestra is furnishing the
music for the dance.
