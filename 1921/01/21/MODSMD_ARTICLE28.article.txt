# Officers Are Elected In Military Society
## 
### 
The new military honor society
that recently organized, met Wednes
day night in the Union and adopted
a constitution, and elected permanent
officers as follows: H. R. Stolz, '21, to
be Commandant; James Hicks, '23, to
be Lieutenant Commandant; Carl H.
Bilger, to be Adjutant; and Ritche
son to be Finance Officer.
An Executive Committee was ap
pointed by Commandant Stolz, to con
duct the business of the organization
and to choose new members. They
will also assist Major Collins in pre
paring for the Military Field Day,
which precedes the Military Ball, on
May 21.
The next meeting of the society
will be on Wednesday, evening, in the
Union.
