# Jordan's Autobiography Is Now Being Printed
## 
### 
The first volume of Dr. Jordan's
autobiography is now in the press of
the World Book Company in Yonkers.
New York, a publishing house of which
Caspar Hodgson, '96, is now president.
The second volume of the work can
not be out before 1922, and as yet it has
not been decided whether the first book
will appear separately or be held until
its fellow is completed.
