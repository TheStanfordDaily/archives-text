# Y. M. C. A. Will Have Get-Together Meeting
## 
###

members of the Y. M. C. A. on next
Thursday are being formulated. The
meeting will take the form of a social
gathering similar to the last Y. M. C. A.
smoker. Reports of the Conference held
at Asilomar will be given.
