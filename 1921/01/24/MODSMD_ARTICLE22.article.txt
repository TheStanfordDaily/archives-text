# Local Masonic Club Will Give Dance Feb. 5
## 
### 
The Stanford Masonic Club, which
was organized last quarter, will give an
entertainment and jolly-up in the
Women's Clubhouse on February S. All
sons and daughters of Masons are in
vited.
This Masonic dance will be an annual
r
affair hereafter and in the future will
be given toward the close of the fall
quarter.
The Grand Lodge of California has
helped to finance the dance and there
will be no admission charge. It will also
be a non-queening affair. Card tables
will be there for those who do not care
to dance.
