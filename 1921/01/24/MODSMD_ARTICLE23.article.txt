# MASQUERS WILL MEET THEIR NE WMEMBERS TUESDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Masquers' Dramatic Society will have
a meeting tomorrow afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse at 4:15 for the pur
pose of meeting their new members,
Miss Dorothy Woodhead, '21, aud Miss
Ivo Copeland, '21, who were elected to
membership at the last meeting of that
society. Miss Woodhead played the lead
in "Pierre of the Plains," the play
produced by Sword and Sandals Society
last quarter. Miss Copeland was in the
"Wedding Bells." cast.
There is also some very important
business to be brought up at the meeting
and it is urgent that all the members be
there promptly.
