# WILBUR RECOMMENDS TEACHING OF GERMAN
## 
### 
In a recent communication to Will
C. Wood, State Superintendent of
Public Instruction, President Wilbur
commented upon the desirability of
resuming the teaching of German in
the universities of the state. German
was discontinued at Stanford during
the war, but a knowledge of the langu
age is considered of great help to
students pursuing a scientific course.
It is understood that fcfre Commission
ers of Education will take the matter
up, shortly. ■».
