# Federal Students Change Plans For Meeting
## 
### 
The Associated Federal Students
will meet tomorrow night instead of
tonight as previously announced. The
meeting will take place at 7:15 sharp,
in the reading room of the Union.
Several important matters will be
discussed and D. W. Montgomery, '21,
I acting president of the society, is
anxious to have a big turnout. The
meeting will last about three quarters
of an hour.
