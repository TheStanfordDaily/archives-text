# Daily Palo Alto Will Hold Staff Banquet Tuesday, February 8
## 
### 
The annual Daily Palo Alto banquet
given by the new members of the staff
for the old members will be held at the
Delta Tau Delta house, the fraternity of
the editor, Earl Crowe, Tuesday, Febru
ary 8. Dancyig will be the main feature
of the evening, beginning at 6 and stop
ping at 8 o'clock, when the banquet will
be held. Speeches by the editor and his
assistants will be made welcoming the
new members to the staff.
Plans for this dinner are now under
way and assignments will be made in a
few days for decorations and other mat
ters.
