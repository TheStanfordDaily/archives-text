# NATHANIEL A. EVANS WILL GIVE ADDRESS TO ECONOMICS CLUB
## 
### 
Nathaniel A. Evans of San Francisco
will speak tonight before the Economics
Club at the Stanford Union on "San
Francisco's Empire Trade." Mr. Evans
is an authority upon this subject, for he
has spent many years in the study of
the connections of San Francisco with
the business of the Orient and other
foreign countries and lias gathered an
immense amount of information.
Following Mr. Evans' speech, a short
business meeting will be held. Sugges
tions will he received and a few short
speeches will be given by the various
faculty members of the Economic and
Political Science departments.
