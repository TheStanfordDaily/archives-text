# R. B. RUTHERFORD IS CHOSEN AGGIE COACH
## 
### Pacific Coast Intercollegiate News Service 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, CORVALLIS, Jan. 27.— R. B.
Rutherford was reappointed director of
physical education and athletic coach at
O. A. C. by joint action of 4hc College
Board of Control and President W. J.
Kerr.
