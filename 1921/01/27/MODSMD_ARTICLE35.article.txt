# PROFESSOR WEBSTER WILL GIVE ADDRESS IN SAN FRANCISCO
## 
### 
Professor David L. Webster, head
of the Department of Physics at Stan
ford, will deliver a course of three
popular science lectures under the
auspices of the California Academy
of Sciences in the auditorium of the
Museum in Golden Gate Park, San
Francisco, beginning next Sunday.
These lectures will be given Sunday
afternoons at 3 o'clock, and the pub
lic is invited to attend.
Professor Webster's subject for
next Sunday afternoon, January 30,
"General Properties of X and Gamma
Rays," and the lecture will be il
lustrated with lantern slides. On
Sunday afternoon, February 6, he will
give an illustrated lecture on "X-Ray
Spectra," and on February 13, one on
"The Structure of Atoms."
