# DOCTOR BRANNER CONFINED TO BED
## 
### 
President Emeritus Branner, who, with
Mrs. Branner, has just returned to the
campus from Arkansas, is confined to
his bed with illness.
Dr. Branner has been engaged since
October in geological work in Arkansas.
He came to California on business, ex
pecting to return to Arkansas soon, but
his illness may delay his return some
what.
