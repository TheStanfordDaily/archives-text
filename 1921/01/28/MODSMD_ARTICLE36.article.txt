# INTRAMURAL RUGBY POSTPONED A WEEK
## 
### 
Intra-mural rugby is postponed one
week on account of the wetness of
the field and the unsettled weather.
The teams have pretty well organized
now, and as soon as the weather turns
warmer and the field dries out there
will be some pretty battles on the
turf. Several of the members of the
victorious rugby fifteen that made the
Vancouver trip at Christmas time are
on each of the inter-league teams, and
will help to make skeletons upon
which the organization can be built.
