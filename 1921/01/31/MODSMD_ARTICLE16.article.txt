# MAJOR COLLINS WILL TALK TO LEGION POST
## 
### 
Major L. P. Collins will address Fre
mont Post No. 52 of the American
Legion in the Community House at
their regular bi-monthly meeting, Febru
ary 9. His subject will be "The Function
of Military Training at Stanford and
Its Relation to the American Legion." A
short entertainment program will follow
the talk.
