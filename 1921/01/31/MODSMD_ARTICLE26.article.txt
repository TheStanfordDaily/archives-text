# Alumni To Start New Store In Palo Alto
## 
### 
Articles of incorporation have been
filed by Charles F. Wright, '95, for a
new department store to be located in
Palo Alto. Wright is well known as an
attorney in Palo Alto. The company is
incorporated for $75,000, and proposes
to establish a first-class dry goods
store. It will be located at 332 Univer
sity Avenue.
