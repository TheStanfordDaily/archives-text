# DR. WEBSTER LECTURES AT GOLDEN GATE PARK
## 
### 
Dr. D. L Webster, professor of
physics, gave the first of his series of
three lectures under the Popular Science
Lectures series yesterday afternoon be
fore a large audience in the Museum of
the California Academy of Sciences at
Golden Gate Park in San Francisco.
The subject upon which Dr. Webster
spoke was "General Properties of X and
Gamma Rays." The lecture was illus
trated and many features unknown to
the general public were presented and
spoken of in the course of the lecture.
He will give another lecture at the
Museum next Sunday on "X-Ray
Spectra," and on February 13 his final
lecture will be given on "The Structure
of Atoms." Each of these lectures are
to be illustrated.
