# MARDI GRAS TO BE HELD IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
The Catholic Ladies' Auxiliary of
St. Thomas' church in Palo Alto, will
give a Mardi Gras on April 8. It will,
of course, be a costume affair and
students as well as townspeople are
cordially invited to attend.
It will be held in the De Luxe Hall
in Palo Alto and the price of admis
sion will be fifty cents.
