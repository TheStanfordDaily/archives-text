# PICCOLI ADDRESS THE PHILOLOGICAL SOCIETY
## 
### 
Professor Rafacllo Piccoli, professor
of English at the University of Pisa, in
Italy, and author of number books, ad
dresses the University Philological As
sociation this afternoon on "Literary
Relations of England and Italy During
the Renaissance." Professor Piccoli,
who was exchange professor at Oxford,
has been lecturing in different institu
tions this year, and was at the Univer
sity of California last quarter.
