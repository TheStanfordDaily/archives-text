# Exhibition To Be Given By Gymnasium Teams
## 
### 
The gap between halves in the game
between Stanford and the University of
Washington next Tuesday evening will
be filled by an exhibition which will be
staged by the gymnasium teams repre
senting Stanford and the University of
California. Seven men from each of
these universities will take part in the
exhibition and the program will consist
of various gymnastic stunts that the
teams have been perfecting. Coach Hots
ford of the Stanford Physical Depart
ment has not as yet announced the
names of the men who will take part
in the stunts,
