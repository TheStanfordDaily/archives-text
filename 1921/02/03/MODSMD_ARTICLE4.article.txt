# Nominations For All Student Body Offices Open on Quad Today
## 
### 
Nominations open this afternoon for
all vacancies in student body and class
offices. Names of candidates may be
filed in the nominations box, which
Election Chairman J. E. Petrini will
place on the Quad this afternoon.
All nominations will close Monday at
12 m. Withdrawals may be made at any
time before Tuesday noon through the
election chairman.
Officers to be elected are sophomore
and junior representatives to the Execu
tive Committee, vice-president of the
student body, one member of the Boacd
