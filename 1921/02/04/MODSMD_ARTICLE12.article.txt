# Nestoria Debaters Will Tryout Feb. 10 For C. of P.
## 
### 
Tryouts for teams to represent the
Nestoria Debating Society in a debate
against the College of the Pacific will
be held Thursday evening, February 10,
at 7:20 o'clock, in the Little Theater.
The question chosen by Nestoria and
the College of the Pacific is: "Resolved,
That strikes should be prohibited in
essential industries by federal legisla
tion." There will be two teams chosen
and a large tryout is expected.
