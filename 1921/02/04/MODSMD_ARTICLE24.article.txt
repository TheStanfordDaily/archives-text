# 0. A. C. WILL CONDUCT INTRAMURAL BOUTS
## 
### Pacific Coast Intercollegiate News Service 
More than forty boxing teams will be
entered in the intramural contests to be
conducted by the physical education de
partment of the Oregon Agricultural
College.
Ralph Coleman, director of intro
mural athletics, reported that every fra
ternity, club, and independent organiza
tion on the campus will participate in the
boxing bouts which will start the early
part of this month and continue until
about the middle of March.
All weights from the 115-pound class
to heavyweights will be pitted against
each other. The champions will be de
termined upon a point basis, the organ
idation winning the largest number of
points to be given plaques.
