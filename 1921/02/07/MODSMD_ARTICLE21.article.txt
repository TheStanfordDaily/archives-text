# CUBBERLEY AND TERMAN TO ADDRESS ASSEMBLY OF STATE EDUCATORS
## 
### 
Professor Ellwood P. Cubberley and
Professor Lewis M. Tennan. both of
the School of Education, are to address
the convention of state educators at San
Rafael, which opened today." Measures
for preserving California's educational
pre-eminence will have an important
place in the program of the annual meet
ing.
Proposals for the establishment of
junior colleges to relieve the universi
ties of the elementary work, and ques
tions dealing with part-time education
will be discussed by the educators. In
addition to the Stanford men. State
Superintendent of Schools Will C
Wood, Lieutenant Governor Young and
others will speak.
