# STUDENT ENTERS BID FOR POSTMASTERSHIP AT STANFORD OFFICE
## 
### 
Palo Alto business men are endors
ing a Stanford student, Martin De-
Vries, '21, for postmaster at Stan
ford, and are circulating a petition for
the purpose of gaining his appoint
ment to the position.
DeVries was registered in the Po
litical Science department and lives
at Roberts, Montana. Although badly
crippled from childhood he suceeded
in getting into the Red Cross service
during the war and served with them
for seventeen months. He will re
ceive his A.B. this coming summer.
