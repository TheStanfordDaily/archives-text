# SATURDAY TRACK MEET POSTPONED ONE WEEK
## 
### 
The informal meet among the men
trying out for positions on the track
team which was scheduled for last Sat
f
11 rday was postponed. These meets are
run for the purpose of giving the track
men practice, and as a usual thing
nearby secondary schools are invited to
compete.
