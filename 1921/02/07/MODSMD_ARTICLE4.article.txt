# Y. M. C. A. COMMITTEE INSTRUCTS CHILDREN
## 
### 
About ninety children of Five-Points
in Redwood City are receiving instruc
tion through the efforts of members
of the local Y. M. C. A. The com
mittee in charge of the work, of which
Edward Vail, '21, is the chairman,
conducts a Sunday school class and
boys' clubs during the week. In the
latter the children are instructed in
baseball, basketball, and other forms
of athletics. *
