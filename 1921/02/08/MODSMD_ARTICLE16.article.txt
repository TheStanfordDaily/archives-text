# FRESHMAN DEBATERS RECEIVE CHALLENGE
## 
### 
The class of 1924 has recently re
ceived a Challenge from the freshmen
debaters of the University of California,
challenging them to a joint debate in the
near future.
President Clarke of the frosh class
has made some of the necessary arrange
ments for the contest and appointed
Sam Mendenhall debating manager of
the class.
He states that in all probability the
devatc will be held late this semester or
early in April, and will he a two-team
contest. The question for the tryouts
and debate will he decided soon.
