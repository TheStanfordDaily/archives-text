# Stanford Graduates Will Be Married
## 
### 
The engagement of Louisette Aubert,
'15, and William J. Losh. '16, has just
been announced. Miss Aubert was active
in dramatics while in college and is a
member of Delta Delta Delta. Losh is
well known for his work in campus
journalism and belongs to Theta Delta
Chi. At present he is acting as secre
tary to the Polish ambassador at Wash
ington. The date of the wedding has
been set for March.
