# U. C. Freshmen Warned To Observe Tradition
## 
### 
Strict enforcement of the observance
of the University traditions will be re
quired of all members of the freshman
class at the University of California. A
warning was given out by the president
of the sophomore class, in conjunction
with the announcement of the sopho
more vigilance committee.
Special attention will be given to the
matter of wearing freshman hats, par
ticularly among the January entrants,
who have shown a tendency to lie lax in
their observance of this tradition.
