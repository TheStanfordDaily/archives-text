TUESDAY
| EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE—Meets'at
4:00 instead of 7:30, at usual place.
SPECIAL MEETING—or Wranglers,
at 5:15 p.m., in the Women's Club
house.
WEDNESDAY
ZOOLOGY CLUB—Meets at Dr. Jor
dan's home, at 8 p.m.
INTERFRATERNITY CONFERENCE
—Meets on Library Steps, at 12:45
for Quad picture.
MONTANA STUDENTS—WiII imeet
at the Women's Clubhouse, at 7:30
p.m., to organize club. Men and
women of Montana will please make
every effort to be there.
All trainees under the Federal
Board for Vocational Education in the
Little Theater, Wednesday afternoon
at 5 o'clock sharp, by order of the
District Vocational Officer.
THURSDAY
SKULL AND SNAKES—Meets at 7:30
in the Union.
PHI DELTA PHl—Dinner at Delta
Tau Delta house, at 6:45 p.m.
PI KAPPA TAU —Regular Meeting.
7:00 p.m., Sharp, at Stanford Union.
THETA SIGMA PHl—Meets at the
Library Steps, promptly at 12:45
p.m., for Quad picture.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
The women's 'dress-up' party which
was to be held Tuesday night in the
Women's Clubhouse has been post
poned one week.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
All subscription solicitors of the
Daily Palo Alto are to meet in the
business office of the Daily Palo Alto
on Tuesday afternoon, at 1:30.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Graduate students who are begin
ning advanced work with the expec
tation of securing the degrees of
Master of Arts or Engineer at the end
of the summer quarter, should file pre
liminary statements of candidacy at
the Registrar's Office before February
12th. Students expecting to complete
their undergraduate work in March
should file applications for the degree
of Bachelor of Arts before February
15th at the Registrar's Office.
