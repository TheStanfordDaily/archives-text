# VOCATIONAL OFFICER ORDERS MEETING OF STANFORD TRAINEES
## 
### 
An order for all Federal Vocational
Students to meet in the Little Theater
at 5 o'clock Wednesday afternoon has
been issued by District .Vocational
Officer Nicholas Ricciardi. The D. V.
O. says that every man in training at
Stanford must be there without fail. No
excuses can be accepted. The matter
for consideration came up so suddenly
that there has been no opportunity to
send out personal notices.
