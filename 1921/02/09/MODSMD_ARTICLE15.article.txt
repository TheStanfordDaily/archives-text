# Dr. Jordan to Address Zoology Club Tonight
## 
### 
The Zoology Club will meet at 8
o'clock this evening at the home of Dr.
Jordan. The program includes a talk
on "Early Naturalists" by the "Grand
Old Man of Stanford."
The meeting will be open to members
only, but anyone interested in zoology
who desires to attend may do so after
communicating with any of the club
officers.
