# U. C. WOMEN'S COUNCIL WILL CURB CHEATING
## 
### Pacific Intercollegiate News Service 
That the practice of cheating and
stealing which is prevalent among the
women students at the University of
California may be dealt with severely,
a campaign is to be carried on by the
Woman Student Affairs Committee,
-with the co-operation of the Women's
Council. The Women's Affairs Com
mittee reports a great increase in the
number of cases reported, and states
that. a. severe penalty will be given
to the person who refuses to tell the
facts of the case.
