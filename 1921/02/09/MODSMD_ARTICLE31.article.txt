# H. R. Fairclough To Speak in San Francisco
## 
### 
"Some Antiquities of the Dalmatian
Coast," will be the subject of a lec
ture to be given by Professor H. R.
Fairclough, Thursday evening, at 8:15
p.m., in the Hotel St. Francis, in San
Francisco.
The lecture, illustrated with lantern
slides, will be held under the auspices
of the San Francisco society of the
Archaeological Institute of America.
Right Rev. William Ford Nichols,
president of the society, will preside.
The annual meetting will follow.
