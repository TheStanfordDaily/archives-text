# Seats at U. C. Game Will Be Reserved
## 
### 
"Wash" Connolly, manager of bas
ketball, has arranged for a block of
four hundred tickets to be reserved
for the Stanford students at the first
court game to be played with California
at Berkeley on next Friday night, Feb
ruary 11. *
These tickets will be placed on sale
in the ticket booth in the Administration
Building on Wednesday, Thursday, and
Friday afternoons. The price will be
25 cents for all holders of Student Body
Cards, who must present them at the
time of purchase.
