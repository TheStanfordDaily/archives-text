# JOURNALISTS MEET TO DISCUSS POLICY FOR COMING QUARTER
## 
### 
The Stanford Chapter of the Amer
ican Journalists' Association will hold
an important business meeting Thursday
afternoon at 2 o'clock in the Press
Building for the purpose of discussing
the financial, social, and membership
questions which are before them.
The following are the matters for con
sideration : the method of holding open
meetings, whether in the form of din
ners or otherwise; the desirability of
establishing chapter dues; the matter
of arranging for some recognized mem
bership for those not technically eligible;
and the proposal to have a group pic
ture taken for the Quad.
The executive committee urges a large
attendance in order that something may
lie accomplished soon towards remedy
ing the difficulties.
