# JUNIOR DANCE WILL NOT BE A JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
To correct any misunderstanding in
regard to the status of the Junior Dance
to he held next Saturday night, Chair
man Willet Dorland has issued the fol
lowing statement: The dance will be
informal and a straight dance.
There will be no programs. The com
mittee has practically all the arrange
ments complete for the dancc now. The
music will start at 9 p. m., after the
freshman basketball game. A five or
six-piece orchestra of camps music will
furnish the syncopation for the evening.
