# Stanford Couple To Wed Tomorrow at 5 In Memorial Church
## 
### 
William Albert Boekel, J. D. '20, will
be married tomorrow afternoon at 5
o'clock, in the Memorial Church, to
Miss Herva Dunshce, '19. Boekel at
tended Stanford the last two years, com
ing from the University of Idaho after

Miss Dunshee graduated in 1919 and
