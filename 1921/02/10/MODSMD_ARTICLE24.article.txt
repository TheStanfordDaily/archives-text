# SWIMMERS WILL HOLD INTERCLASS EVENTS THURSDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Varsity 'and freshmen swimmers will
meet tomorrow afternoon in the Encina
tank in the first interclass swimming
carnival of the year. Erny Brandsten
lias his varsity men in good condition
for the mee*t, and lias also been work
ing hard with the freshmen for the
list few days to get them into shape for
the meet tomorrow.
An interclass meet is held as the first
meet in every quarter to bring out any
talent that may have*entered the Uni
versity. Last fall men showed up in the
swimming meet that will make good
varsity material next year.
