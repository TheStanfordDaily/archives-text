# Prof. D. L. Webster Gives Talk in San Francisco
## 
### 
Professor David Locke Webster of
the Department of Physics, gave an
interesting and instructive lecture yes
terday afternoon in San Francisco on
the topic of "The Structure of Atoms."
Professor Webster's talk marked
the conclusion of the course 011
"Atomic Structure and Radiation." It
has been conducted in the auditorium
of the museum of the California
Academy of Sciences in Golden Gate
Park, and has been exceptionally well
attended. The speakers have all been
eminent men and authorities upon the
subject. The lecture yesterday was
illustrated with lantern slides and
open to the general public.
