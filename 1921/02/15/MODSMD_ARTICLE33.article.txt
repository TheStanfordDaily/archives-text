# DRESS-UP PARTY TO BE HELD TONIGHT IN WOMAN'S CLUB HOUSE
## 
### 
All of the University women will meet
! together at the Y. YV. C. A. dress-up
party to be given in the Women's Club-.
! house at 7:30 tonight. Whitmer's or
chestra will furnish music for dancing
and refreshments will be served during
the evening.
The women of Roble Hall are having
a preliminary dress-up affair at their
dinner hour and will attend the Y. W.
party en masse. The entertainment will
only last until 9 o'clock, and all of the
women are urged to forget their studies
and other engagements to join in the
evening's fun.
