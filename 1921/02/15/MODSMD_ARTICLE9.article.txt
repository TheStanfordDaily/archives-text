# INTRAMURAL HANDBALL SERIES BEGINS TODAY
## 
### 
The first contest in the intramural
handball tournament will take place
today, between C. ft'. Putman and J.
Schlesinger of Sigma Nu. and W. D.
Seay and C. S. Botsford of the faculty.
The fraternity men are strong on
teamwork, and expect to give their
more experienced opponents a stiff
battle.
The teams taking part in the tour
nament are to arrange their own time
to play, with their opponents, as
shown on the bulletin board in the
gym. All first-round matches must
be finished by February 18.
