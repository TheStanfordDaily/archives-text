# Federal Students Will Dance After Court Game
## 
### 
Immediately following the Varsity
basketball game with the University
of California on February 26, the As
sociated Federal Students will hold
a closed dance in the Women's Club
House.
Witmer's Orchestra will provide the
music for the affair. Some guests have
been invited from the chapter at the
University of California and from the
District Headquarters in San Fran
cisco.
D. W. Montgomery, '21, vice-presi
dent of the association, says there are
a few more tickets available. They
may he obtained in room 34 from
James B. Greegan, from 1 to 3 o'clock
tomorrow afternoon. Members of the
local organization may invite former
service men, Montgomery says.
