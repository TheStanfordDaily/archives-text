# CONNOLLY ANNOUNCES TWO NEW COMMITTEES
## 
### 
Committees appointed for supervision
of the Junior Prom and Junior Week
were announced today hy R. C. Con
nolly, president of the Junior Class.
J. A. Carey was appointed chairman
of the Junior Prom Committee, to act
in conjunction with Anna Judge, Helene
Searcy, Ford Tussing, Henry Titus, C.
M. Jenks, and Helen Hauge.
Junior Week activities will he under
the supervision of George Drake, work
ing with Hudson Proctor, Mary Jane
Clark, Kathryn Donald, J. M. Davies,
and P. F. Neer.
