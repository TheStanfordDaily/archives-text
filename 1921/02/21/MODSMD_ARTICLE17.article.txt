# Renaissance Club To Hear Dr. Clark Tonight
## 
###

at 8 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse
to hear Dr. Clark of the Art department
talk on Renaissance Art. All the mem
bers are urged to be present, as there
will be discussion and election of new
members after Dr. Clark's lecture.
