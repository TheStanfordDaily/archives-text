# VOCATIONAL MEN CAN GET PAY AT OFFICE
## 
### 
Pay checks for Federal Vocational
trainees are now in thte Federal Board
office, room 35. Men can get their
pay by calling at tho office between
10 o'clock and noon, and between 1
and 4 oclock.
C. J. Creegan will bo in the office
every afternoon this week and will an
swer any questions arising in con
nection with tho Associated Federal
Students' dance, to be given immedi
ately after the Stanford-California ball
game next Saturday night.
