MONDAY
RENAISSANCE CLUB—Meets tonight
in the Women's Clubhouse (tonight) j
at 8 o'clock. Dr. Clark of the Art
department speaks. Election of
new members.
RENAISSANCE CLUB—Room 460
Botany Building, at 8 sharp. Dr.
Clark will lecture.
TUESDAY
Trackmen to meet in the Men's
Union, at 7:15 p.m. Coach Temple
tcn expects every man to be there.
WEDNESDAY
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE—WiII hold
its regular meeting in the Women's
Clubhouse, at 7:15 p.m.
ECONOMICS CLUB—Meets on Lib
rary steps at 1:00 p.m., for Quad
picture.
THURSDAY
EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY—
Will meet in Room 50, of the Edu
cation Building, at 7:30.
NOTICE
Free reading sections for the spring
quarter will be made up next week.
Advance registration will be made on
the regular enrollment cards, and filed
with me next Monday afternoon, Feb
ruary 21, between 2 and 5, in room 209.
Do not enroll for the course unless
you actually intend to take it, as your
registration closes the roll to others.
11. D. GRAY.
