# Handball Squad Loses To San Jose Y. M. C. A.
## 
### 
Stanford's handball squad, represented
by J. B. Sansome and F. M. Koba, was
defeated by the San Jose Y. M. C. A. in
a tournament held there Saturday. Ow
ing to the fact that a tennis ball was
used, while the Stanford men arc ac
customed to playing with a hard ball,
they did not show up at their best. The
Y. M C. A. men arc all players of years
experience and have used the tennis ball.
