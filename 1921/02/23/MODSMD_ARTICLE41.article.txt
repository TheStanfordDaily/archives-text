# Sorority Houses Will Hold Exchange Dinners
## 
### 
The second of the series of sorority
exchange dinners for this quarter will
he held this evening. Each house will
exchange ten members. According
to the arrangements of Panhellenic
the following houses will exchange:
Kappa Kappa Gamma and Alpha The
ta; Delta Gamma and Pi Beta Phi;
Alpha Phi and Gamma Phi Beta;
Delta Delta Delta and Alpha Omicron
Pi; Chi Omega and Sigma Kappa.
