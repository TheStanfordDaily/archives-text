# PAN-HELLENIC ISSUES RUSHING CONTRACT SPRING QUARTER
## Formal Season Will Follow Conference Week Program, New Rules Are Outlined 
### 
Women's rushing for the spring quar
ter will be formal and will be governed
by a contract similar to the one used
for the fall and winter quarter, accord
ing a decision of Pan-Hellenic Associa
tion. *
Monday, March 28. to Saturday. April
2, has been set aside as Conference
Week, and there will be no rushing dur
ing that time. Fraternity women may
ask freshmen for only one date in ad
vance besides a tea date, and both of
these may be asked for before registra
tion day.
Formal rushing begins on Saturday.
April 2, with teas to be given by all of
the sororities from 3 to 6. There shall
be no dinner or luncheon dates on that
day. The remainder of the season is
open for dates for lunchetm from 12 to
1:30 and for dinner from 6 to 8. All
night dates may be had on the second
week-end, April 9 and 10, and shall last
from 6 in the evening until 9 the next
morning. The Sunday dinner date will
he from 1 to 2:30 and there shall be no
dinner date or rushing of any sort on
Saturday night, April 8.
Fraternity women may call on the
freshmen at any time after Saturday
morning, April 2, until Sunday, April
10. No more than one call may be made
by each fraternity to tli* same freshman
during the season. The calls shall in
clude only two women and shall last for
ten%minutes only.
Rushing will end at 8-o'clock on Tues
day evening, April 12. and bids will he
sent to the lawyer at 9 o'clock the same
night. The usual rules excluding men
from all rushing affairs, forbidding the
meeting of trains, calling for or taking
women home, and rushing on Quad,
which includes all communication be
tween fraternity women and freshmen
with the exception of exchanging mo
mentary greeting and answering essen
tial questions, will be enforced.
