# MUSIC FROM HANDEL'S 'MESSIAH' TO BE GIVEN IN MEMORIAL CHURCH
## 
### 
The "Passion" and Easter Music from
Handel's "Messiah" will be given in the
Memorial Church on Sunday evening,
March 6. The choir has been rehears
ing the work for several weeks and to
night the University Orchestra will start
intensive study of the accompaniments,
holding their rehearsal at the usual
hour, 7:30, in the Memorial Church. All
instrumentalists in the community who
would like to play in the "Messiah" per
formance, whether playing regularly in
the orchestra or not, should apply to
Warren D. Allen.
