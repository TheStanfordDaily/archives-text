# Fairclough Will Give Address in the South
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough, head of
the Latin Department and well known
for his Red Cross service in Monte
negro during the war, left today for
the southern part of the state, where
he is to give two addresses during the
week-end. On Sunday afternoon he
is scheduled to speak before the
Archeologieal Society in San Diego on
the subject of "Antiquities 'of the
Dalmatian Coast." In the evening of
the same day Professor Fairclough
will discuss "Problems of Jugo-Slavia"
at a meeting of the Open Forum of
San Diego. He expects to return to
the campus in time for his classes
on Tuesday.
