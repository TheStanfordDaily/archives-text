# STANFORD EDUCATORS RECOGNIZED IN EAST
## 
### 
Stanford's School of Education ranks
high in the opinion of authorities at
Columbia University, according to word
received yesterday from Professor Wm,
M. Proctor of the Education Depart
ment.
C. J. Creegan, who received the com
munication from Proctor, said that the
Stanford professor was given more than
an hour on the program of the Middle
West Vocational Guidance Conference
at Minneapolis, and that his work has
won marked recognition for himself and
for Stanford in the Eastern universities.
Proctor is now at Columbia Univer
sity attending a short special course of
lectures on vocational training. He will
return to Stanford before the end of the
present quarter.
