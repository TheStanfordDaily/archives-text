# MUSIC FROM "MESSIAH" TO BE GIVEN SUNDAY
## 
### 
The Passion and Easter Music from
Handel's "Messiah," will be given in
the Memorial Church on Sunday
evening, March 6, at 8 o'clock) by the
University ehoir-and orchestra, under
the direction of Warren I). Allen. The
Christmas music was from the
"Messiah," and given at this time last
year.
The choir has been rehearsing the
music for several weeks and the or
chestra is now working on the ac
companiments. There will be no
tickets and no seats will be reserved
Sunday evening. The doors of the
church will be opened for the program
at 7:30.
