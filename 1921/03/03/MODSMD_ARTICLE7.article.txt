# Dr. Ralph Arnold, '99, Given Honorary Degree
## 
### 
Word has reached the campus that
Dr. Ralph Arnold, a graduate of the
University with the class of '99, and
formerly a member of the Board of
Trustees of the University, has been
given an honorary degree of Doctor
of Science at the University of Pitts
burg, for "Distinguished service- in the
field of petroleum, geology and tech
nology, especially as regards his work
in the California oil fields and in the
natural resources of the treasury de
partment during the war."
