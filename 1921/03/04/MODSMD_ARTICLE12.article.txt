# Bertha Vaughan, '22, To Head Theta Sigma Phi
## 
### 
Bertha Vaughan, '22. was yesterday
elected President of Theta Sigma Phi
to succeed Kirby Ingoldsby, '21. who has
been holding that office since the begin
ning of the fall quarter. At a business
meeting in the Women's Clubhouse yes
terday afternoon the members decided
on Tuesday, March 8, as the day for the
initiation of new members. Miss
Vaughan took the President's chair as
chairman of the meeting.
