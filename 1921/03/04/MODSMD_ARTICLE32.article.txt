# JOHN JUGO APPEARS IN LITTLE THEATRE
## 
### 
John Trelawny Jago, child pianist,
appeared in recital in a program last
evening in the Little Theater. He is
the eleven-year-old son of Mrs. John
Jago of Palo Alto and has been heard
A number of times informally both in
San Francisco as well as in this com
munity. His program last evening
was his second public appearance.
