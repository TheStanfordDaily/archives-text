# GLEE CLUB WILL GIVE CONCERT AT FAIRMONT
## 
### 
The Glee Club will give its first off
campus concert of the quarter at the
Fairmont Hotel in San Francisco at
8:30 tomorrow evening under the au
spices of the Stanford Alumni Associa
tion. The program, which will last un
til 10 o'clock will be followed by danc
ing until 12. Whitmer's orchestra will
furnish the music.
There will be a charge of $1.10 for the
concert and dance. Tickets are on sale
at Sherman Clay's and the Fairmont in
San Francisco and at the office of the
Alumni Secretary in the Administration
Building.
