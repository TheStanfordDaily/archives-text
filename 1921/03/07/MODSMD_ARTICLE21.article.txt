# JAP PRINTS BEING SOLD FOR BENEFIT
## 
### 
The private collection of Japanese
prints belong to Mrs. David Starr Jor
dan is being sold for the benefit of the
Stanford Home for Convalescent Chil
dren. These prints, which are very val
uable, arc now on exhibition at the Stan
ford Art Gallery, where all intending
purchasers may sec them.
Mrs.**' Jordan gathered the collection
some years ago with the assistance and
advice of Bolton Coit Brown, now a
well-known New York painter, but at
that time head of the Stanford Art De
partment, and an expert on Japanese art.
The prints, with a few exceptions, are
the original impressions from the plates,
and the collection as a whole espe
cially fine.
