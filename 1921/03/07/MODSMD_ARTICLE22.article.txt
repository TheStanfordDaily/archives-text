# HARVARD WINS TRACK MEET FOR THIS YEAR
## 
### 
Harvard University has just been
awarded the forty-fifth annual track and
field championship gained for by
the Intercollegiate Association, which
has charge of this event.
It was decided to hold the meeting
on May 27th and 28th in the Harvard
Staudium. The committee furthermore
decidcd to add the javelin and discus
throws to the amyial games program,
by this ruling will not take effect until
the meeting of 1922. They also agreed
to readmit Colgate University to this
association.
