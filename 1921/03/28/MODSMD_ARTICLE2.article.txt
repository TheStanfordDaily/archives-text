# Euphronnia Announces Officers For Quarter
## 
### 
The Euphronia Debating Society has
just announced the result of its semi
annual election of officers held shortly
before the conclusion of the winter quar
ter. Those chosen arc: President, C.
Victor Smith '21 ; vice-president, Ellis
Hirschfeld '21 ; secretary, Herbert B.
Alexander '22.
Due to the large number of other af
fairs taking place this week, the presi
dent has cancelled the regular weekly
meeting, and the first gathering of the
spring quarter will take place a week
from this Wednesday night at the regu
lar meeting place.
