# cabbages and things
## 
### 
T. 1.. '23 #
Despite bold assertions to the con
trary. ye ed. returneth to cultivate his
crop and drive away the devastating
worm which is wont to infest ye
cabbaege field. Assistant gardeners
will be welcomed with open arms and
ears. Little preliminary practice will
be necessary to become master of the
good old running hook. Get on the
spray cart.
* • •
All those in favor of discontinuing
Cabbages will please rise. Hey! —
Whaddye tiling you arc* —raisin bread?
* » *
"Drick" Morse says our mittmen are
long on swats and short on studies.
Southern California sport writers ad
mit that the Cardinal ring-sharks are
not only proficient in the gentle art
of give and take but are conversant in
Greek, Latin, Econ. and other dead
languages. Which of the two can we
believe ?
* * »
We know a second-string first base
man who's laying bets about a trip to
the. Orient.
• * *
Didn't know we had too many ath
letes till w: took a look at the pro
bation list.
* * *
"Shake well before using," * doesn't
apply to nitroglycerine.
