# CONFERENCE PARTY IS SCHEDULED FOR TOMORROW NIGHT
## 
### 
Associated Women will entertain the
new women at a conference party to
morrow night at 7:30 in the Women's
Club House. The party this quarter will
be a very informal get-together dance
After the dance light refreshments-will
be served.
The purpose of the party is to wel
come the entering women and to become
acquainted with them; for this reason
every women on the campus is asked to
attend.
