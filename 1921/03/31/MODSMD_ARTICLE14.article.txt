# SWIMMING PICTURES WILL BE TAKEN FOR QUAD TOMORROW

### 
Varsity and Freshman swimming
teams will meet tomorrow at 4:15 to
have their pictures taken for the Quad.
At the same time there will be an elec
tion of Varsity captain for the swim
ming team and'for the water polo team.
The captain of the Freshman water polo

With the first of the Freshman meets
