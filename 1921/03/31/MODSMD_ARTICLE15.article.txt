# Warren D. Allen Gives First Organ Recital
## 
### 
Warren 1). Allen, University Organist,
gave his first recital of this quarter this
afternoon in the Memorial Church. His
program was informal and was not an
nounced previous to the recital. The
regular recitals will begin Sunday; Pro
grams for the following week will then
be announced. The recitals this quarter
will be held, as previously, at 4:15 on
Tuesday, Thursday and Sunday after
noons. The recitals last for about half
an hour and carefully picked and pleas
ing numbers arc always given.
