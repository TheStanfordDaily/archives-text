## 
### 
Vlado Vuyacitch, the 9-year-old hoy
who was injured by the Stanford mail
truck Tuesday, is reported to be doing
nicely. The boy was accidentally struck
by the machine in front of the Stanford
Bookstore while riding his bicycle. Tie
was immediately rushed to the Penin
sula Hospital, where he was given first
