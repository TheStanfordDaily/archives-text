# VARSITY NET TEAM BEATS POMONA MEN
## 
### 
Stanford net men defeated the
strong aggregation of racquet wield
era from Pomona College, in three out
of four matches on the local courts
yesterday and this morning. The Po
mona team gave the Cardinals a close
contest in every match, and should
make a good showing against the Blue
and Gold tennis experts this after
noon. Davies and Neer played their
usual steady single game, and in doub
les exhibited a brand of teamwork dis
couraging to the visitors.
