# MONDAY LAST DAY FOR CARNIVAL PLOTS
## 
### 
Monday is absolutely the last day
that representatives of groups for the
Carnival concessions may turn in their
shows to Concession Manager Daniel
Webster Evans. He has announced
that he will be in the office of the
carnival headquarters in the Union
from 1 to 2 on Monday, and will re
ceive the plots of the various conces
sions then.
The Carnival committee will pass
on the songs submitted on Monday
