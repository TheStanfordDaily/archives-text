# EUROPEAN STUDENTS FUND NEARLY FULL
## 
### 
The St. Nicholas Club, which was
organized about last Christmas on the
campus by Mrs. Everett W. Smith, has
contributed five dollars to the Euro
pean Students' fund.
The Stanford treasurer of the fund,
Professor H. R. Fairclough, reports
that up to date the contributions from
this community have amounted to
$3928.77. Nearly all of this money has
been forwarded to the treasurer for
the Armenial Relief administration un
der Herbert Hoover in New York.
if there any who desire to contribute
to this fund, they should do so a: once,
as the accounts will shortly be closed.
