# ORGAN RECITALS ARE REPEATED TUESDAYS
## 
### 
Warren I). Allen, University organ
ist, repeated his Sunday program at his
recital in the Memorial Church this aft
ernoon. Organ recitals will be given
this quarter in the church on Tuesdays,
Thursdays and Sundays, as they have
been heretofore. Allen always repeats
his Sunday programs on Tuesdays for
the benefit of those unable to attend
on Sunday. The program was as fol
lows :
Sonata No. 6, in D Minor
Mendelssohn
Andante Cantabile IVidor
Piece Hcroiquc Cqcsar Franck
