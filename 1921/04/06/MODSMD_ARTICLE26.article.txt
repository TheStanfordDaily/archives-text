# Vegetation of Belgium Is Subject of Lecture
## 
### 
The vegetation of Belgium will form
the subject of a lecture to be given
Friday evening, April 8, in the Botany
Lecture Room (460) by Miss Alice
Scouvert, a Belgian exchange student.
The lecture will be given under the au
spices of the Palo Alto branch of the
California Botanical Society. The pub
lic is invited.
