# H. R. Fairclough to Speak In San Francisco Saturday
## 
### 
Announcement has been made that
Prof. H. Rushton Fairclough, head of
the Latin department, will speak in the
Paul Elder Gallery in San Francisco
next Saturday at 2:30 o'clock before
one of the weekly Saturday afternoon
meetings. Prof. Fairclough will pre
sent an account of the "New Jugo
slav Kingdom, and Some of Its Prob
lems."
