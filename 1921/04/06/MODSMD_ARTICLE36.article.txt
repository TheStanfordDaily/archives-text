# Engineer Head Will Give Lecture on City Planning
## 
### 
Mr. Nelson C. Lewis, former chief
engineer of the Board of Estimate and
Apportionment of the City of New
York, will give a talk, illustrated with
lantern slides, tomorrow afternoon,
at 3 o'clock, in the Little Theater. He
will speak on City Planning. The talk
will he given under the auspices of the
Engineering Council, and all are wel
come to attend.
