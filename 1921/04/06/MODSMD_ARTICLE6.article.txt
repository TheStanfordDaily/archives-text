# Richard Hinkley, '24, Is Elected Tennis Captain
## 
### 
Richard Hinklcy was electcd to lead
the Stanford freshman tennis team
against the California freshmen next
Saturday at a meeting of the squad last
night. Hinklcy ranks as one of the
best junior players on the Coast, and
is holder of the National and Pacific-
Coast Junior doubles championship. In
the inter-scholastic tournament held at
Ojai last spring, he won the California
inter-scholastic singles championship.
Hinkley claims Hollywood as his home
and is registered in the lower division.
He is a member of Delta Kappa Epsilon.
