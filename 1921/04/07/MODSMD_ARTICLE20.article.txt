## 
###

ex-'2l, to Gerald Fitzgeranld of Los
Angeles, was announced at a hous*e
party at the Taylor country house
near Los Angeles, last week end. Miss
Taylor was well-known here as an
artist. Fitzgerald, who is at. present
connected with an engineering firm
in the south, was in the overseas for
ces, and received a captaincy.
Miss Taylor plans to go to Wash
ington for several months, and will
be married there in the Fall.
