# FAIRMONT HOTEL TO ENTERTAIN VETERANS
## 
### 
Disabled veterans at the Palo Alto
Base Hospital will be entertained Sat
urday evening in the Fairmont Hotel
by the San Francisco Chapter of the
Military Order of the World War. The
Fairmont has tendered the use of Rain
bow Lane to the organization for a
military ball, the benefits of which will
be devoted to the aid of the Palo Alto
veterans. <
Following an investigation of the con
ditions at the Palo Alto Hospital by
Captain F. M, MacGraw, commander of
the San Francisco chapter, plans were
made to hold a ball, the funds to be
expended in purchasing clothing and
other articles for men undergoing treat
ment for wounds received in the war.
