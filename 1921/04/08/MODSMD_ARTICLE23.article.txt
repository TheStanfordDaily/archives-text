# MEDICAL FACULTY STARTS MEMORIAL FOR DR. BEASLEY
## 
### 
The Medical Faculty is endeavoring
to raise a fellowship fund of $25,000
in memory of Dr. Sadworth Oldham
Beasley, Assistant Professor of Ob
stetrics and Gynecology who was kill
ed in the late war while carrying in
wounded.
Over $1,000 has already been raised
for this fund. Medical students and
members of the Medical Faculty as
well as other contributors to the Sta
dium Fund, are requested to designate
that their subscription be ultimately
transferred to the Beasley Memorial
Fellowship.
