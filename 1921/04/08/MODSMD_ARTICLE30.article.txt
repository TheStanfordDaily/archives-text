# ALBERT KING TO PLAY IN COMMUNITY HOUSE
## 
### 
Albert King, the young Berkeley pi
anist, who was recently heard in a con
cert in the Little Theater, will give a
program in the Palo Alto Community
House Sunday afternoon at 5 o'clock.
Mr. King also gave a recital in the
Community House last fall.
The Sunday program will be Mr.
King's farewell appearance, as he will
leave for Paris the first of May to con
tinue his piano study under European
masters. He has just finished a tour
of thirty-five concerts in California and
Nevada with Madame Kalova, the Rus
sian violinist.
Miss Cecil Callison, soprano, of San
Francisco, will assist in the concert with
a group of songs
