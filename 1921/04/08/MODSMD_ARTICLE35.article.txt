# Professor Fairclough Will Lecture in City Saturday
## 
### 
Professor 11. R. Fairclough, head of
the Latin Department who recently
returned from service in Montenegro,
where, as Commissioner with the rank
of Lieutenant-Colonel, he was in charge
of the American Red Cross work, will
lecture at the Paul Elder Gallery in
San Francisco, next Saturday after
noon, on "The New Jugo-Slav King
dom, and some of its Problems."
During the eighteen mouths in
which Professor Fairclough was in
charge of all the Red Cross operations
in Montenegro he visited nearly every
province of Jugo-Slavia as well as
Rumania, Albania, and Italy.
