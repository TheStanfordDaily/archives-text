# PROFESSOR ROBINSON TO SPEAK ON POLITICS
## 
### 
Professor E. B. Robinson of the
History department will speak on
"Some Realities in Present Politics"
at a meeting of Wranglers at 7:15
o'clock tonight in the Women's Club
house. Professor Robinson's address
will be based on the result of observa
tions made in east during the past
year. The meeting is in the nature
of a "guest night" for informal dis
cussions will follow the address.
