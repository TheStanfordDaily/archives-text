# Freshman Debaters Are Feature of Hall Smoker
## 
###

was featured by an impromptu debate
by freshman volunteers on the ques
tion: "Resolved, that we are better
than nothing." The negative received
the decision by a unanimous vote.
Professor E. W. Smith, a former En
cina man, talked on his experience in
the hall in the old days. Professor
Smith said that the golden age of the
University was not in the past, but to
day.
