# Rickenbacker Flies To Campus From San Jose
## 
### 
Eddie Rickenbacker, America's pre
mier ace, now president and manager
of the Sheridan Motor Company, made
the trip from San Jose to the campus
polo field, a distance of abnut 17 miles,
in 8 minutes Wednesday. His plane,
which saw service overseas, landed
here about 10:50.
Rickenbacker talked to the business
men of Palo Alto at noon, largely
upon his personal experiences in the
war, and in the morning addressed the
students of the local high school.
