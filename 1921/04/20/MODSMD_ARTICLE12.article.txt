# ENTERTAINMENT FOR BASE HOSPITAL GIVEN
## 
### 
Acting under the auspices of the
social service committee of the Y. W.
C. A. the members of the Sigma Kappa
sorority gave a program at the Base
Hospital last evening. A collection
of songs and dances, and a clever skit
comprised the entertainment. The in
mates at the hospital were enthusi
astic in their praise of the program,
and expressed the desire that many
more such affairs might be contri
buted by campus talent.
