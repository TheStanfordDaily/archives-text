# Martin Discusses Bowl With Representatives of University of California
## 
### 
Graduate Manager Ed. Martin and
several other representatives from
Stanford are meeting with representa
tives from the California Board of
Athletic Control in San Francisco to
day to discuss general finances of the
stadiums of the two universities. The
meeting is primarily to talk over the
different methods of seating, in an
effort to construct the stadiums as
cheaply as possible without sacrificing
the stability of permanence of them.
