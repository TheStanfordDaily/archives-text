# ALUMNI ENTERTAIN COMPTROLLER ROTH
## 
### 
Word has been received from Comp
troller A. E. Roth that he and Mrs.
Roth had a very profitable meeting
recently with the Stanford alumni of
New York.. This evening they will be
entertained by the Stanford alumni
in and around the University of
Michigan and tomorrow by the alumni
of Chicago.
They will be back on the campus on
April 30.
