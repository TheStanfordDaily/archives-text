# OFFICERS ARE ELECTED BY THETA SIGMA PHI
## 
### 
The regular election by The fa Sigma
Phi was held in the Women's Club
house yesterday afternoon, at 5 o'clock.
Bertha Vaughan, '22, was re-elected
president, Mary Jane Clark, '22, was
elected vice-president, Rosemary
Drachman. "23, secretary, and Mollie
Windish '23, treasurer. Janice Dun
ker, '22, was appointed Keeper of
Archives.
Installation of the new officers will
take place on May 5.
