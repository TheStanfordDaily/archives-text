# Three Stanford Men Will Attend Scientific Meeting
## 
### 
Herbert Hoover, '95, a member of
the Board of Trustees of Stanford,
Victor J. West, head of the depart
ment of Political Science of the Uni
versity, and Bruce Bllven, '06, manag
ing editor of the New York Globe,
have been appointed to represent
Stanford at the meeting of the Amer
ican Academy of Political and Social
Sciences, which will be held in Phila
delphia, May 13-14. Professor West
is in the Wast this year on sabbatical
leave of absence from the University.
