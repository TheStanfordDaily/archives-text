# Varied Program Features Allen's Thursday Recital
## 
### 
A widely picked program was given
by Warren D. Allen in the Memorial
Church, this afternoon. Variety has
lately marked Allen's recitals. Selec
tions by Widor and Bonnet, two of
the most prominent of the modern
French school of composers, were play
ed. The program this afternoon was:
Fantasia Kmil Sjogren
Cavatina Raff
Fugue in C major Buxtehude
Angelus de Soir Bonnet
Marche pontificale from the First
Symphony C. M. Widor
