# KENNETH J. SAUNDERS TO GIVE TALK SUNDAY
## 
### 
Dr. Kenneth J. Saunders, Ph. D., will
give a lecture Sunday morning at 10
o'clock in the Little Theater on Britain
and India. Dr. Saunders has lived in
India and will speak from personal ex
periences. He has a statesman's grasp
on the situations there, and at the end of
his lecture he will give an opportunity
for questions. He is speaking under the
auspices of the Y. M. C. A.. As large
an attendance as possible is Tequested.
