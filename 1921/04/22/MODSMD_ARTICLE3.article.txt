# Campus Cobras Clamor For Cardboard Coupons
## 
### 
He who hesitates is lost, or soon will
be. One hundred and seventeen Junior
Prom tickets sold in three days and still
three to go. Only a few of these precious
little pasteboards entitling the bearer to
admittance are left, and at the present
rate of sale many campus queeners are
going to be sadly disappointed. Beg,
borrow or steal the paltry five and a half
dollars and do it fast if you want to
attend the grandest dance of the year in
the Gymnasium, May the Sixth.
These little white cards that the cam
pus cobras are clamoring for will entitle
the holder to obtain the beautiful hand
painted program that will appear at the
bookstore a few days before the big
struggle. Buy your ticket, present it at
the bookstore for programs, and then
attend this dance.
