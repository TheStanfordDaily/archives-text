# HONOR CLUB FORMED BY HISTORY MAJORS
## 
### 
A History Honors Club was organiz
ed last night at a meeting of the Stan
ford history majors, as a result of a
need for more active work in that de
partment. The following officers were
elected for the spring quarter: Louis
Jose de Sousa, (graduate) president;
William Adams, '21, vice-president;
Lucy Wilcox, '21, secretary.
Bids for members for the organiza
tion will be sent out in a few days.
