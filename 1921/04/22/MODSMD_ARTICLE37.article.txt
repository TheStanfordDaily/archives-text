# CERCLE FRANCAIS TO HEAR REGIS MICHAUD
## 
### 
To add to the program for the quarter
which the French Club has planned of
"Song, Dance, and Poetry," Professor
Swartz, president of Cercle Francais,
has been successful in bringing Regis
Michaud of the Romanic Language De
partment of the University of California
to the campus to speak before the club
on the subject, "French Poetry of Yes
terday and Today." - The meeting will
be held on Wednesday, April 27. in the
Women's Club House at 8 p. m. All
those who aer interested in this subject
are invited to attend and hear Professor
Michaud's criticism. He is a popular
French author, his finest work being
"Mystiques et Anglo-Saxonnes."
