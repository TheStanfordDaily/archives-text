# PALO ALTO WILL HAVE MAY DAY CELEBRATION
## 
### 
Final plans are being completed for
the May Day celebration which is to
take place in Palo Alto this week-end,
the first of its kind held wider the
auspices of the town. From 10:30 Sat
urday morning to Sunday evening the
time will "be taken up with various fes
tivities.
, One of the features will be a parade
Saturday morning in which floats have
been entered by the merchants and in
which various military units will march.
A contest is being held for the election
of the most popular girl as May Queen,
and she will preside over the Costume
Ball in the Community House Satur
day evening.
