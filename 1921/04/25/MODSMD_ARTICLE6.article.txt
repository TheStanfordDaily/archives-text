# Alumni Founder Of Washburn School Dies
## 
### 
Arthur H. Washburn, '93, founder of
the Washburn School in San Jose, died
at his ranch near Los Gatos Saturday.
Washburn established his school as a
preparatory institution for college in
1894, following his graduation from
Stanford, and it now ranks with the best
known schools of its type in the state.
The Washburn School is now conducted
by a sister of the founder, Lucy M.
Washburn.
