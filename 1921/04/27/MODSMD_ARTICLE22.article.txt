# Allen Will Give Concert For Alumnae Association
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen will give a recital
Saturday aftornoon for the Associa
tion of Collegiate Alumnao. The re
cital will be given at 2:45 p.m. in the
Memorial Church. His program at
the Saturday recital will be:
Overture —"Kuryanthe"
Carl Maria von Weber

Nocturne, from the "Midsummer
Night's Dronm" Mendelssohn
Scherzo from the Fifth Symphony
Guilmarvt
Paraphrase on Theme by Gottschalk
Theodore Sanl
Toccata in C major Th. Dubois
Allen ropeated his Sunday program
at his recital this afternoon. He will
give his regular recital on Thursday
at 4 o'clock.
