# FENCING TEAMS TO COMPETE TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Stanford and Fremont Post,
American Legion, fencing teams will
go to San Francisco to take part in
a match at the Olympic Club, at 8
o'clock this evening. The Pacific
Coast championship will be decided
between teams representing the
Olympic Club, the University of Cali
fornia, Stanford, the American Legion
and the Army and Navy.
Stanford's team will be composed
of Kahle, Willis and Gardner and the
local Legion Post will be represented
by Wegner, Patterson and Sandston.
