# TEXAS CLUB MEMBERS WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
A meeting of the Texas Club will be
held tonight at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. Every student from
state of Texas is urged to come and en
joy the evening. There will be a short
prografi which will be opened by an
address to the club by a member of the
faculty, which will be followed by a few
musical numbers and dancing. Refresh
ments will also be served and the com
mittee in charge assures a good time to
all those who attend.
