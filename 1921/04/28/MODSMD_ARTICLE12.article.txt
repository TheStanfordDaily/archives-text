# Fraternities To Send Bids Today
## 
### 
The formal rushing season for the
men's fraternities ended at 12 o'clock
last night and bids must be sent today to
Jessec Thrash, Secretary of the Intcr
fratcrnity Conference; the bids to be
accompanied by the address of the per
son bid. Notices will be sent to the
rushees on Friday or Saturday request
ing them to appear at the Men's Union
Sunday morning, where A. B. Cox, '20,
will act as lawyer.
According to the conference rules no
rushing will be permitted from Thurs
day to Sunday at 6 p. m., after which
time informal rushing is in order for the
remainder of the quarter. Men pledged
this quarter cannot be initiated until the
fall quarter.
