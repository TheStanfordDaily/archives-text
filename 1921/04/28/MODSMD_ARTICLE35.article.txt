# INTRAMURAL MEET TO BE STAGED ON OVAL
## 
### 
As a result of a meeting of repre
sentatives of the various campus or
ganizations held last night to discuss
plans for the spring Intramural Track
Meet, it was decided to hold a second
meeting next Wednesday, May 4, at
7:30, in the Men's Union.
The date for the Track Meet has
been set as Thursday, May 19, and the
event will be open to all campus
groups.
