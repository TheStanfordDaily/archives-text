# WIVES OF STUDENTS HOLD MEETING TO FORM ORGANIZATION
## 
### 
Wives of Stanford students organised
at a meeting in the Women's Clubhouse
yesterday afternoon. They chose as
their name "Stanford Dames," modeled
after the "Harvard Dames" and similar
organisations in other universities. The
meeting was sponsored by the Faculty
Women's Club.
Committees were appointed yesterday
and officers will be elected at the next
meeting. The club will gather regularly
on the second and fourth Thursdays of
each month.
A faculty advisory committee was ap
pointed by Mrs. Wilbur, who presided
at yesterday's meeting. Members of the
newly formed organization were the
guests of the faculty womeu at a tea
which followed.
