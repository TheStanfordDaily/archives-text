# Roble Women Elected To Women's Conference

### 
Five women, two seniors and three
juniors, have been elected to repre
sent Roble Hall at Women's Cpnfer
ence. They are: Edna Hawkenson,
'21, prominent in Y. W. C. A. work;
Rosemary Drachman, '21, who wrote
the Junior Opera; Margaret Carlsmith,
'22, who has distinguished herself in
athletics; Grace Pope, '22, a member
of tho Quad staff, and Mary Learnard,
