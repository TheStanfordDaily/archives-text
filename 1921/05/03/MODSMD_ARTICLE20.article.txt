# PROFESSOR TATLOCK WILL DISCUSS LATIN MEDIAEVAL AUTHORS
## 
### 
"Some Aspects of Mediaeval Latin
Literature" will be the subject discussed
by Professor J. S. P. Tatlock of the
Knglish department in a talk before the
Classical Club in the Women's Club
house Wednesday evening at 8 o'clock.
Professor Tatlock has made a special
study of the Middle Ages and is one of
the leading authorities on this period.
The meeting tomorrow night will also
he the first business session of the club
this quarter, and plans will be made for
a banquet and special celebration at the
end of the school year. All those in
terested are invited to attend the lecture
tomorrow night. A social hour and re
freshments will follow in the later part
of the evening.
