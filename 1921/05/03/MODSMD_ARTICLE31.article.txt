# EUPHRONIA TO MEET FOR ORGANIZATION
## 
### 
At its regular meeting Wednesday
night, in Room 12 of the Law Build
ing, Euphronia Debating Society will
consider a number of important
changes in the present organization.
One of the specific questions for dis
cussion will be that of changing the
constitution to make Euphronia pri
marily a law men's organization.
Other matters will be the future regu
lation of membership, the qualifica
tions for active membership, and
whether the number of members shall
be restricted.
The president asks all old members
of the society to be present and help
determine the future of Euphronia*
The meting will start promptly at
7:30 and will be made as short as
possible.
