# OPPORTUNITY OFFERED THOSE WHO CAN WRITE
## 
### 
Students who are desirous of doing
literary work are urged to send their
poems on short stories to Dr. H. T.
Schnitkind, in care of the Stratford
Company, 12 Pearl Street, Boston,
Mass.
The contributions will be received
until May 15. The poems accepted
will appear in Volume V of "The Poets
of Future College Anthology" (1920-
21);' and the stories selected are to
furnish the material for Volume 11,
of "The Best Short Stories."
