# LOCK WOOD WORKERS URGED TO REPORT
## 
### 
Progress on the Lockwood Drive
is being reported slowly and as yet
only $460 in subscriptions have been
turned in. Dr. Morgan hopes to boost
the thermometer over the $500-mark
when all reportß have been returned.
He Bays that the results of the drive
should be telephoned soon, if only ap
proximate, in order that the original
mark of five hundred can be reached
by the red streak in time.
