# SWIMMING AND WATER POLO ON SLATE TODAY
## 
### 
Two aquatic events are on the sport
bill this afternoon, both swimming and
water polo occupling a part of the stage.
The freshman swimming team is bat
tling with the contingent from San Jose
in one of the last meets of the season.
San Jose sends down a "dark horse"
team about which nothing much is
known.
The sophomores and seniors are to
day scheduled to drown each other in
water polo festivities in what is the
second game of the interclass series now
being held. Further games are arranged
for the near future.
