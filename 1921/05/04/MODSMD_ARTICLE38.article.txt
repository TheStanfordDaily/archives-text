# American Legion Meets For Band Organization
## 
### 
Members of the local American
Legion post organized a band at a meet
ing in the Community House at Palo
Alto Friday night. There were twenty
one men present but fifty are expected
by the time the band is fully organized.
The first rehearsal will be held this
evening at 7 :30 at the Community House
and will be under the direction of Mr.
E. G. Bradley, a former army band
master. It is very important that all
men who are interested turn out.
