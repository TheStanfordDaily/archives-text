Names of various knights and ladies
of the Round Table will appear on large
posters around the walls of the gym
nasium at the Junior Prom Friday night.
The committee requests that the places
marked off by these names be used as
dance rendezvous- The following names
will be used: King Arthur, Queen
Guinevere, Sir Galahad, Sir Launcelot,
Elaine, Sir Gawain, Sir Gareth, Lynette,
Sir Merlin, Vivien, Sir Geraint, Enid,
Lady of Shalott, and Fool Dagonet.
