# WRIGHT AND DITSON TOURNAMENT OPENS PLAY FOR CUP TODAY
## 
### 
Drawings for the Wright & Ditson
trophy tournament for non-varsity and
non-freshmen varsity tennis players were
made this morning and play will start
at once, due to the limited time which
remains to stage the tourney. One hun
dred and fifteen tennis players in the
University are registered in the tourna
ment, making it the largest ever to be
played in the history of the sport at
Stanford.

The play is for a large, handsome
hammered silver cup offered by Wright
& Ditson of San Francisco, being one
of the. best trophies ever offered for
competition here. Twelve matches will
be played off this afternoon, daily
schedules will be posted on the Encina
Gymnasium bulletin board. All entered
in the tournament are warned by the
tourney committee to watch for the date
of their match as no games will be post
poned.
