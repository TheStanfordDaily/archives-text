# FAIRCLOUGH, CANNING WILL TEACH AT U. C.
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough of the
Latin department, and Professor J. B.
Canning of the Economics department
will both be on the University of Cali
fornia faculty for the coming stimmer
session. They will begin instruction at
Berkeley about June 20.
i The University of California is in
stituting a divided summer session this
year similar to the system which is in
use at Stanford. Their "inter-session"
will begin next week and will continue
until June 20, when the regular summer
session will commence, lasting until July
30. Credit may be received for attend
ance at either session.
