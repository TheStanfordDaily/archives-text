# SEQUOIA CLUB GIVES DANCE THIS EVENING
## 
### 
Sequoia Club is giving its first dance
of the quarter this evening. It will be
a one-o'clock affair and a great deal of
effort has been put into the
ments and decorations with the idea of
making it one of the most successful
social events in the history of the club.
Reynolds' orchestra will furnish the
music.
