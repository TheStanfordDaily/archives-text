# LISTS FOR WOMEN'S INTERCLASS TENNIS WILL CLOSE FRIDAY
## 
### 
The lists for the women's interclass
tennis tournaments will close Friday
afternoon, according to an announce
ment by Marion Echols, tennis manager.
The lists have been posted in the Roble
gymnasium and there is still room for
additional names.
Owing to the lack of time the tourna
ment last quarter was run off in a some
what rapid manner. The plans for the
interclass tournament will give everyone
a chance. All who are interested should
sign by tomorrow afternoon.
The drawings will be made on Satur
day and the order of matches announced
on Monday;
