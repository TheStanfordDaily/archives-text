# NEXT Y. W. MEETING TO BE OPEN CABINET
## 
### 
In order to give the members of the
association an insight into the work
ings of the executive branch of the
local organization, the next meeting
of the Y. M. C. A. will be in the form
of an open Cabinet meeting to which
all women of the campus are invited
to be present. The regular order of
business will be gone through with,
after which the meeting will be thrown
open to questions and general discus
sion by those present.
