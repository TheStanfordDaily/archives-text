# WAR WORK SKETCHED FOR MEDICAL JOURNAL BY DR. CLEILA MOSHER
## 
### 
In the current number of the Medical
Woman's Journal, the official organ of
the Medical Women's National Associa
tion, appears the first of a series of
sketches of the war work of American
women doctors in France, by Dr. Clelia
Duel Moslier, medical adviser of women
at Stanford* This is the first of four
instalments which will be published.
The sketches are based on observa
tions made by Dr. Mosher during the
time of her own service in France. This
first instalment consists of a number of
brief, sharply outlined pictures of inci
dents in the work of three women en
gaged in American relief work in France
during the war.
