# LEGAL PROFESSION TO BE SUBJECT OF TALK TO EUPHRONIA
## 
### 
Professor C. B. Whittier of the Law
Department will speak at the regular
meeting of Euphronia Debating Society
tomorrow evening in Room 12 of the
Law Building. His subject will be:
"The Elements of a Good Lawyer." I his
meeting is of primary interest to the law
majors of the society, but President
Smith urges everyone interested to be
present. After Professor Whittier's talk
an open discussion will be held in the
form of a political meeting. The main
topics will be prohibition and the free
dom of the press. Members are asked to
construct arguments for either side of
these questions and be prepared to ad
vance any theories which they wish to
defend. The meeting will start at 7:30
sharp.
