# THIRD ROUND WILL BE PLAYED IN HANDBALL SINGLES BY THURSDAY
## 
### 
All matches in the third round of
the Intra-mural Handball singles, are
to be played off by Thursday night,
In order to finish the tournament in
time, it was announced today. The
contests scheduled to take place are:
R. H. Hinckley. Delta Kappa Epsilon,
vs. A. J. Jongeneel, Alpha Kappa
Lambda; W. H. Kinney, Zeta Psi, vs.
C. D. Rutter, Delta Chi; F. M. Koba,
Japanese Club, bye.
One hundred and ten men have
signed up in the handball section for
the spring quarter, making one of the
most successful handball seasons in
the history of the game at Stanford.
