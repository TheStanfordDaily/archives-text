# Women to Compliment Dean Harriet Bradford
## 
### 
To compliment the retiring Dean of
Women, Miss Harriet Bradford, a tea
will be given at the Women's Club
House, Thursday afternoon, from 4
to 6 o'clock. In appreciation of the
services rendered them by Miss Brad
ford, the women of the University are
co-operating in planning this function
in her honor. All women residents of
tho campus are invited to attend.
