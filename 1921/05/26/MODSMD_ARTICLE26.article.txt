# STUDENT ELECTION DATE IS CHANGED BY EX-COMMITTEE
## 
### 
The date for the general student
I body election was advanced one day
by a decision of the Executive Com
mittee last night. The change was
made in accordance with a constitu
tional necessity brought about by the
[ Memorial Day holiday, next Monday.
The minutes of the meeting follow:
McElroy in the chair.
Absent. Cody:
M. S. C.—That the following fresh
men be awarded the privilege of wear
ing the block "24" for tennis: Richard
Hinckley, Theodore Mertz, and Nor
man de Bach.
M. S. C. —That the president be em
powered to advance $5 for a wreath
for Memorial Day service.
M. S. C. —That the date for the gen
eral student body election be changed
to Thursday, June 2, on account of
the Memorial Day holiday.
M. S. C. —That a nominal rate of in
terest be charged on all loans from
the A. S. S. U. to classes and organi
zations.
M. S. C—That the A. S. S. U. pay
their debts before any loans are made.
M. S. C. —That the recommended
changes in the W. A. A. Constitution
be adopted.
M. S. C. —To adjourn.
J. EDWIN SIMPSON, Secretary.
