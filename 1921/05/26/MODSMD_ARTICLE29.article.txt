# AMERICAN LEGION MEN WILL REPORT MONDAY
## 
### 
All members of the American Legion
are requested to report at the postoffice
in Palo Alto next Monday, Memorial
Day, at 10 a. m., coming in uniform, and
wearing their tin hat?, if possible.
All former "gobs" are urged to get in
touch with Jerry Looney, P. A. 1335-J,
at once. The committee in charge wishes
the navy men to come in their white
uniforms. There will be a contest be
tween the army and navy to see which
can turn out the largest and best ap
pearing group of men.
