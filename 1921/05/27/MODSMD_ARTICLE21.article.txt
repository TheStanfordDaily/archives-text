# STUART CLUB WILL HAVE SUMMER HOTEL
## 
### 
To those Stanford students who are
planning to spend the summer studying
in Boston, the Stuart Club there has
announced that it will be open from
June 1 to September 15 as a summer
hotel for women guests.
The club, which is a residence in
the winter season, is located in the
Fens, an attractive part in Boston, and
is adjacent to the Boston Art Museum,
Symphony Hall, and schools and col
leges offering summer courses.
Summer rates will be $18.50 and
$15.00 per week, transient rates being
given if desired. The address is 2 The
Fenway, Boston, Massachusetts.
