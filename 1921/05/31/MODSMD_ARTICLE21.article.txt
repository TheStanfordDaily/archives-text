# BEETHOVEN'S FUNERAL MARCH PLAYED TODAY
## 
### 
Beethoven's impressive Funeral March
on the Death of a Hero was played by
Warren 1). Allen, University organist,
this afternoon at his recital in the
Memorial Church. This number is one
of the most beautiful funeral marches
ever written. The Piece Heroiquc by
the noted French composer, Cesar
Franck, was also given on Allen's pro
gram. The program today was the same
that he gave Sunday afternoon. Today's
program was:
In Memoriam Win. C. McFarlane
Elegy .. Massenet
Funeral March on the Death of a
llero Beethoven
I Elevazione Bossi
Piece Heroique Cesar Franck
