# LAST OF 1922 QUADS ON SALE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tomorrow will be the last day 011
which copies of the 1922 Quad will be
distributed. The Qirtul management
requests that all holders of receipts
(&11 promptly for their hooka, as the
office will be abandoned Thursday
night, and there will be considerable
difficulty in obtaining books after that
date.
