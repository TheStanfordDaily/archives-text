# On'n Off the Campus
## 
### 
Harold Brown, '19,
at Kerto
Harold Brown, '19, left school about
a month ago and la now the assist
ant geologist for the Southern Pacific,
at Kerto. Brown is a member of the
Phi Delta Theta fraternity.
Barton Gillespie, '20,
in Mexico
Barton Gillespie, '20, is working as
a geologist for an oil company in
Tampico, Mexico. According to word
received from him by the members of
his fraternity, he is having and ex
citing time on account of the revolu
tionary disturbances in Mexico. Gil
lespie is a member of Phi Delta Theta.
Mrs. Lester Parmelee
Visits Campus
Mrs. Lester Parmelee spent Wednes
day on the Campus. Mrs. Parmelee
was a member of the class of 1920,
and was formerly Miss Mildred Mer
rill. Since her marriage, in December,
she has been living in San Francisco.
Mrs. Parmelee was a member of Alpha
Omicron Pi.
