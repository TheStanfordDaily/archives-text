# High School to Present Senior Play Saturday
## 
### 
"The Man From Home," a comedy by
Booth Tarkington and Harry Leon Wil
son, will be presented by the Senior
Class of Palo Alto Union High School
next Saturday evening at the High
School Auditorium. The play is being
coached under the direction of Lester
Seib. The leading parts are taken by
Dave Lampson and Evelyn Huntington.
