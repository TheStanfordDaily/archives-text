# LASUEN LIMP TO HAVE VARIED ENTERTAINMENT
## 
### 
Saturday night lias been granted to
j the Organized Women Students as a
closed date on which to hold the annual
Lasuen Limp for the benefit of the
Women's Clubhouse fund. Inasmuch as
there will he no other dances on the
campus that evening, it is anticipated
that the affair should bring out a goodly
number of the trippers of the light fan
tastic.
Roble Club is furnishing the punch
which will be sold at the houses when
dancing is scheduled, and several hot
dog stands will be conducted by other
women's living groups on the Row.
Card tables will be set up at the Delta
Delta Delta house for those who do not
Icarc for dancing.
