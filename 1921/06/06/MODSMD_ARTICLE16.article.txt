# Water Polo Title Will Soon Be Determined
## 
### 
The winner of the intramural water
polo championship will be decided to
morrow afternoon when the winners of
the two games to he played today clash
in the final game of the series.
This afternoon Plii Delta Theta and
Palo Alto will wage a battle to deter
mine which shall play in the final contest,
and El Capitan and the eating clubs'
team will also play to see who the other
fortunate team will be.
The series has been played off in elim
ination style, such as is used in tennis
matches, instead of making a round robin
series out of it.
