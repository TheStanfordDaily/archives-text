# C. H. Bentley Will Speak To Foreign Trade Class
## 
### 
Mr. Charles H. Bentley of the Cali
fornia Packing Corporation will talk be
fore Professor Mears' class in Foreign
Trade next Tuesday morning on the sub
ject, "The Export Trade of the Cali
fornia Canning and Dried Fruit Indus
tries." Mr. Bentley is well known at
Stanford, due to his work with Mr.
Hoover in the United States Food Ad
ministration. as well as to his extensive
experience in direct charge of both do
mestic and foreign sales for one of Cali
fornia's largest and most successful cor
porations.
This lecture will be held on June 7 at
11 o'clock at the Little Theater.
