# PHI ALPHA DELTA INITIATES THREE MEN
## 
### 
Three law majors were initiated by
Holmes Chapter, Phi Alpha Delta, Law
Fraternity, at an initiation held in the
Little Theater Saturday morning. The
men initiated were William Backlund,
Wylie M. Kilpatrick, and Francis C.
Kahle. Backlund is a member of Phi
Beta Kappa, El Cuadro, and Encina
Club. Kilpatrick is editor-elect of the
1923 Quad, a news editor on the Daily
Palo Alto, a member of Sigma Delta Chi,
and Encina Club. Kahle is a member of
El Campo, and Encina Club.
