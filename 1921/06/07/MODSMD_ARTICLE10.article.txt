# Nestoria Debaters Will Hold Banquet at Union
## 
### 
Nestoria Debating Society's annual
end-quarter banquet will be staged this
evening at 6 o'clock in the Union. The
election of officers for next year will
take place. Dan Evans will make the
principal address of the evening, and
other members will give talks.
At this meeting there will be the elec
tion of officers for next year. The out
going officers are: President, Homer
W. Brown, '22; vice-president, William
IT. Mctzler, gr.; secretary, Victor Cappa,
'23; treasurer, Kenneth Gardner, '23.
All members are cordially invited to
attend this function.
