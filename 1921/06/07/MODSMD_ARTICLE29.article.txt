# YOUNGER ALUMNAE WILL BE HOSTESSES AT TEA
## 
### 
A group of younger alumnae will be
the hostesses at the last regular Wednes
day resident alumnae tea of the year at
the Women's Clubhouse tomorrow after
noon. They are Vaal Stark, Mabel
Townley, Lucille Townley, Ruth Squire,
Mrs. Kenneth Hume Hunter, Ruth
Crutcher, and Mrs. J. M. Blackburn.
