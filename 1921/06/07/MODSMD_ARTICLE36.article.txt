# SUMMER COURSES ARE GIVEN AT CASTILLEJA
## 
### 
Summer courses in cooking, sewing,
and millinery will be given by Miss Peet,
home economics teacher at Castilleja
School ,this summer. Miss Peet is a
graduate of the Pratt Institute and of
Teachers College, at Columbia Uni
versity.
Classes from three to six weeks in
length are being formed for adults, for
girls of high school age, and for chil
dren from seven to fourteen years old.
