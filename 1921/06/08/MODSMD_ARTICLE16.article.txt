# OFFICERS ELECTED BY SCHUBERT CLUB
## 
### 
Members of Schubert Club held their
last meeting of the quarter yesterday
afternoon and elected the following of
ficers for next year:
Dorothy Wilson, '23, president; Grace
Rrown, '23, vice-president; Ruth Jame
son, '24, secretary and treasurer; Laurice
Hotaling, '22, librarian.
Business of the club was finished, and
the new officers will be installed next
year.
