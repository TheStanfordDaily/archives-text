# FRESHMEN WILL MEET THURSDAY AT 12:45
## 
### 
The freshman class will hold its final
meeting of the year on Thursday at
12:45 in -Room 214. The semi-annual
election of officers will take place, and
matters of finance will be discussed. It
is especially urged that all freshmen
women be present to take part in this
election, as many of the officers to be
elected are from the five hundred.
It is announced that the sweaters for
freshmen athletes will be on hand by the
end of the quarter.
