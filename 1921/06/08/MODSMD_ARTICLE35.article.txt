# HOWARD BROWN WILL HEAD CLASS OF '23
## 
### 
Howard Brown was elected president
of the junior class for next year at a
meeting of the class of 1923 this nobn.
Brown was elected to fill the vacancy
caused by the resignation of W. L. Rog
ers, who was forced to resign because
of the press of scholastic work.
Brown is a member of the Daily Palo
Alto staff and Phi Delta Theta.
