# Major Collins To Leave Friday for Kentucky
## 
### 
Major Collins of the R. O. T. C. leaves
Friday for Camp Knox, Kentucky, where
more than 2000 R. O. T. C. men will be
gathered for instruction. Over 1000 of
them will he of the field artillery branch.
Stanford will have about 40 men at
this encampmcnt. Major Collins will be
second in command. lie will have
charge of a co-ordinating course. After
the R. O. T. C. course, a brief officers'
course, for the men who have instructed
the R. O. T. C., will be given. Major
Collins will also have charge of that.
