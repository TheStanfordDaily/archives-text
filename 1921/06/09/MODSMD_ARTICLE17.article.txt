# ENCINA CLUB HOLDS ELECTION FOR NEXT YEAR'S EXECUTIVES
## 
### 
C. J. Tauzer, '20, was re-clccted presi
dent of Encina Club by an overwhelming
majority in the election held last night.
This is the first time in the history of
the club that a president has been re
elected. Walter Hales was elected man
ager for the coming year by a unani
mous vote.
A three-cornered tie between Strauss,
Backlund, and Griswold was the result
of the balloting for senior representative
on the House Committee. H. J. Borba
was given the other position. Wylie Kil
patrick was elected junior representative,
and F. S. Ludeke, sophomore repre
sentative.
On the Board of Directors, the fol
lowing men were put into office: Seniors,
Goodman, Fehren; Juniors, Peavey,
Johnson; Sophomores, Coverley, Clark.
