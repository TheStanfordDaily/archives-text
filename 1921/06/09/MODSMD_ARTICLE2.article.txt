# SEQUOIA CLUB HOLDS ELECTION OF OFFICERS
## 
### 
Sequoia Club held an election for next
year's officers last night and Bernard F.
Haley was re-elected president of the
club. Other officers elected are: Vice
president, F. A. Covey, '23; secretary,
R. J. Tennant, '24; treasurer, F. R.
Barnes, '23. House committee—Seniors,
E. D. Landels, '21, A. R. Tyler, '21;
juniors, D. L. Robertson, '23, C. J. \'ogt,
'23; sophomore, O. G. Moe, '24. Social
committee—E. W. Butler, '23 (chair
man), H. S. Laughlin, '24, G. E. McCue,
'23, O. E. Settee, '22, J. E. May, '24. Fi
nance committee —Upperclassmen, C. M.
Senior, '22, E. E. Sappington, '22; under
classman, V. W. Bennett, '24. Athletics
committee — Newton Brinkman, '23
(chairman), H. O. Willard, '22, H. O.
Preston, '23.
