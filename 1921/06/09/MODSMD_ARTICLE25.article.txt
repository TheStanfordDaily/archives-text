# DAVIS AND SEIB WILL ACT IN GREEK THEATRE
## 
### 
Dramatic Coach Gordon Davis and
Les Scib will represent Stanford in the
production of the series of six modern
plays to he given in the Greek Theater
at the University of California during
this summer session. The company will
have as its nucleus the California
coaches, Hume and Pichel, and a few of
the better actors, who will work with
Seib. All of the coaches can act as well
as direct. Coach Davis will have the
entire direction of "Belinda," the last of
the group.
