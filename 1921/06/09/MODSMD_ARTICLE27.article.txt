# CHEM CLUB MEMBERS ELECT NEW OFFICERS
## 
### 
Roble Bridge was the scene of the
Chemistry Club's first annual picnic held
yesterday. Wallace Craig, '21, was elect
ed president, Kenneth Ritchie vice-presi
dent, Francis Smith secretary-treasurer.
A picnic supper jrn.s served.
Speeches and stories were given by
Professor J. F. G. Hicks and A. W. Mc-
Dermott, '18, a graduate of the depart
ment.
