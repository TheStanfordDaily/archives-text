# ENGLISH CLUB MEETS TO ELECT OFFICERS
## 
### 
Members of English Club Tuesday
evening held the final meeting of the
year for the election of officers, and
completion of the year's work. The
first part of the evening was devoted
to an informal picnic supper and pro
gram. San Franciscquito Creek was
chosen as the spot in which to hold
the last get-together of the club.
The following officers were elected
to direct the activities of the English
Club for next year:
Robert Binckley, '21, president.
Mollie Windish, '22, vice-president.
Dorothy Pierce, '22, secretary.
A. J. Peterson, '21, treasurer.
Miss Edith Mirrielees of the Eng
lish department was chosen faculty
advisor.
