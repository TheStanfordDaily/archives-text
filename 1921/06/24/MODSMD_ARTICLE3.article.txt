# Flint Hanner Wins Javelin Throw at National Meet
## 
### 
Flint Hanner again upheld Stan
ford's name by taking first place in
the javelin throw, in the National Col
legiate Athletic Association meet,
which was held at Stagg Field, in Chi
cago, last week. It was necessary for
Hanner to defeat Tuck, the favorite
of Oregon, and he did so by making
a winning throw of slightly over 192
feet.
