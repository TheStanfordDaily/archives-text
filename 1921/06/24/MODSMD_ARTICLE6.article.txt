# President Will Receive New Students at Home Lagunita Knoll Tomorrow
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Wilbur will in
formally entertain the students and
members of the faculty at a reception
to be given at their home on Lagunita
Knoll, tomorrow night, at 8 o'clock.
The reception will follow the plan
of the regular quarterly entertain
ments given at the home of the Presi
dent, to welcome the new members
of the student body and faculty, and
to renew acquaintanceship with the
old. It is expected that all of the stu
dents and faculty will be able to be
present at the reception.
