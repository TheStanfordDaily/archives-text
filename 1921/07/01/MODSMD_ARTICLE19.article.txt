# FOR SOMBREROS ONLY
## 
### 
A freshman this morning ate his breakfast on the senior bench.
Yesterday noon a party of tourists held a picnic with the bench as a
base of activity.
The new senior bench, which was finished at the end of last quarter,
between the library and the art gallery, opposite the law steps. As
the bench is new, it is not yet well established in the minds of all stu
dents. It takes the place of the old senior bench, and is for seniors
onlv, or for men who have previously earned that distinction.
As there are but a limited number of seniors now on the campus,
they will appreciate the aid of freshmen and others in serving notice to
uninformed persons who insist on using the bench that the privilege of
its use is one to which only those who wear the broad-rimmed som
breros are entitled.
