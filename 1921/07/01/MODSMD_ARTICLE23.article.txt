# BENEFIT CIRCUS TO BE GIVEN NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
"The Juvenile Circus and Horse
Show" will be given for the benefit
of the Stanford Home for Convalescent
Children, at the home of Mrs. H. G.
C. Hahn, in Menlo Park, Saturday,
July 9th. The doors will open at 2
o'clock and an admission charge of
one dollar, for adults, and twenty-five
cents for children, will be charged.
The neighborhood will furnish the
talent for the 'amazing and awe-in
spiring aerial acrobatics' and custom
ary acts of a circus.
