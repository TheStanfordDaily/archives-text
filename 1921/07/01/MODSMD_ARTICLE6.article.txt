# Summer Humor Should Be Contributed Now For "Registration" Chappie
## 
### 
Summer humorists can get their
products off their desks and thus save
throwing them out, by wrapping the
brain-child up and dropping it at the
P. 0., addressed to W. F. Lewis, Cam
pus. Cartoons, jokes or joaks, squibs
or duds, articles and other humorous
evidences of thought, are needed for
the fall Registration Issse.
Lewis as the manager of Chaparral,
has issued a call for tryoutees for
managerial positions who are to do
some work during the summer. Men
who are anticipating trying out should
take advantage of this opportunity,
Lewis said, as it will be somewhat
of an advantage in the fall by way
of increased experience.
The manager may be found in the
Associated Students' Office on Tues
day, Wednesday and Thursday, be
tween nine and ten o'clock.
