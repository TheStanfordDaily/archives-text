# STUDENTS TO VISIT IN SAN FRANCISCO'S CHINATOWN JULY 9
## 
### 
Stanford summer students will have
an opportunity to see Chinatown in
San Francisco next Saturday night,
when three Chinese students will con
duct a tour in connection with a
series of trips planned by C. S. Bots
ford, Director of Intramural Sports.
This chance* is regarded as an excep
tional opportunity by many Stanford
people who have often expressed a
desire to see the real Chinatown,
minus the notorious covering of
"bunk" spread by the professional
guides.
The party will leave the Community
Center in Palo Alto, Saturday morn
ing at 8 o'clock, go by bus to San
Francisco, cross to Oakland and in
spect the playgrounds and visit Lake
Merritt.
Those who wish will go in the aft
ernoon to the California Oval and wit

The excursion party will then as

tour of Chinatown. The party will
leave the Ferry Building at 8 o'clock,
and will spend about two hours pry

world.
