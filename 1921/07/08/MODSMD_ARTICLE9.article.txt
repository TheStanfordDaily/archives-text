# MEN RESUME WORK ON BASKETBALL PAVILION
## 
### 
After an idleness of some weeks due
to the strike of the Building Trades
Unions, work on the basket-ball pa
villion has been resumed. The exca
vation and concrete work has been
completed for some time, and the
brick layers are working on the walls.
The work is now nearly a third done.
