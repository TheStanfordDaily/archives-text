# NEW TENNIS COURTS WILL BE READY SOON
## 
### 
More adequate facilities at Encina
gymnasium for the devotees of tennis
is promised with the completion of
the new asphalt courts, each of the
gymnasium, which has-been in process
of construction for some time past.
The surface of the court has been
thoroughly rolled, and the netting for
the backstops will soon be in place.
The courts to the south of the gym
have been over-crowded ever since
the opening of the present session.
