# San Francisco Clubs Welcome Van Gent
## 
### 
Coach Van Gent was yesterday
noon the guest of the Stanford Clubs
of San Francisco, at the Palace Hotel.
Athletic Director W. T. Powell, Grad
uate Manager William Fletcher,
Coach "Dink" Templeton, and Captain
Jack Patrick of the 1921 team were
present from the campus and made
short talks. President F. L. "Husky"
Guerena of the Stanford Club, and
Manager Joe Hickey in charge of the
stadium campaign welcomed the new
coach.
