# Professor and Mrs. Treat Sail For Japan; Return in March
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Payson J. Treat
sailed on Tuesday for the Orient, by
way of Honolulu. They will remain
for a few weeks in Tokio, where Prof.
Treat will lecture in the Imperial
University on diplomatic relations be
tween the United States and Japan.
From here they will go to Kioto, re
maining until November 1. Prof. Treat
will speak on the same subject in the
Kioto University.
After leaving Japan, Professor and
Mrs. Treat will go to China for about
three months, during which time Prof-
Treat will lecture in the Peking Uni
versity and perhaps elsewhere. They
plan to return to California on March
10, 1922.
