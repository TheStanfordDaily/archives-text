# KERN COUNTY ALUMNI ORGANIZE ASSOCIATION
## 
### 
Stanford alumni of Kern County met
during the summer and organized an
alumni association to include the entire
county. Officers were elected as fol
lows: President, Asa Dimon, '14; Sec
retary, Miss Irma Rayburn, '17.
The next meeting will be held in the
Petroleum Club at Taft, November 17,
an dwill take the form of a big game
rally.
