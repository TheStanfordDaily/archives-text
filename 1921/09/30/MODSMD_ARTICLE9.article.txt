# CITY FIRM SUPPLANTS OLD CAMPUS JANITORS
## 
### 
Corps of men from the American
Building Maintenance Company of San
Francisco will be responsible in the
future for the appearance of the cam
pus, as a contract has recently been
let to this concern for all janitor work
about the university.
This system is calculated to save
the business office considerable trouble
which the housing, enrolling and re
employing of the Japanese janitors
formerly gave it. Many of the larger
buildings in San Francisco are clean
ed by this company. The number of
men they will be required to use is
only limited by the work the contract
calls for. White labor is almost ex
clusively employed by the city firm.
