# PRESIDENT WILBUR TO GREET NEW STUDENTS
## 
### 
On next Saturday night, October 8.
President and Mrs. Wilbur will hold
their annual reception for new students
at their home on the campus. The af
fair is given at the beginning of each
college year to receive the new students
and afford them the opportunity of meet
ing both one another and members of
the faculty. Heads of many of the de
partments as well as others of the staff
will be present, and on the receiving line
Cap and Gown women will assist as
hostesses.
There will be an orchestra to supply
the music for those who care to dance
during the evening in the long reception
hall, which is to be cleared for dancing.
