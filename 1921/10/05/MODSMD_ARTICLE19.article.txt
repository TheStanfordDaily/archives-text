# Sellards Appointed Aide On Russian Relief Staff
## 
### 
John Sellards, formerly an instructor
in the French Department at Stanford
University, and a member of the Sigma
Chi fraternity, has been appointed as
aid to Colonel Haskell, director of the
American Relief in Russia, according to
word received here yesterday. After
completing a year's work at the Uni
versity of Brussels, Sellards returned
to America for a brief time. Shortly
after bis arrival here, be received bis
appointment and returned to Europe,
making London bis headquarters.
