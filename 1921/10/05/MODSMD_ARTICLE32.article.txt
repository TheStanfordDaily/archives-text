# University Orchestra to Hold Rehearsal Tonightt
## 
### 
The University Orchestra will hold
its first meeting of the quarter to
night, in form of a regular rehearsal
which will take place in the regular
meeting place of the orchestra in the
basement of the Engineering build
ing. All players who were in the
orchestra last year have been asked
to report and all new students who
have ability in this line are requested
to come out.
Credit for one hour will be given
all those who enroll in the orchestra.
The rehearsal tonight will be held at
7:30 o'clock.
