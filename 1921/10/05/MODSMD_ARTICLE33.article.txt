# Work of Quad Tryoutees To Start Immediately
## 
### 
Managerial tryoutees for the 1924
Quad will get together for the first time
this evening in Room 54, Sequoia Hall.
Manager Phil Landis will explain the
work to be done during the year.
The Editor of the Quad is keeping
the hours from 1 to 3 o'clock every
afternoon open this week in order to
give those wishing to try out for the
editorship every opportunity to see him
at the Quad offices. Administration
Building.
