# Y. W. C. A. WILL HOLD MEETING TOMORROW
## 
### 
New Stanford women will be wel
comed at a meeting of the Y. W. C. A.
in Women's Clubhouse tomorrow after
noon. This is the first meeting of the
association this quarter, and in addition
to the discussion of plans for the coming
year, Miss Margaret Woolsey, the new
Y. W. C. A. secretary for the campus,
succeeding Miss Ruth Bacon, will be
presented.
Work of the Y. W. C. A. will be ex
plained by Edna Wilbur, president of
the local organization. All new women
are urged to be at the meeting. Re
freshments will be served. The meeting
will be held at 5 o'clock.
