# CHINESE DISHWASHER DIES AFTER ILLNESS
## 
### 
Lo Han, a member of the Suey Hing
tong, dishwasher for the last three
days at Sequoia Hall, died this morn
ing from what was explained by Chan
Lo, a fellow worker, as "choking
sickness." The deceased had been
bithered with a choking cough since
the time of arrival, according to the
Chinese informer. This morning, the
malady became much worse and the
sufferer died before help could be sum
moned.
The body was taken to the Hapgood
and Rorrel undertaking parlors in Palo
Alto.
