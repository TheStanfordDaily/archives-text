# W. L. Swartz Attends Dante Celebration To Take Place in Italy
## 
### 
W. L. Schwartz, instructor in the Ro
manic Languages department at Stan
ford, will attend the- celebration of the
six hundredth anniversary of the poet
Dance's death which will be held at Ra
venna, Italy. Schwartz left the univer
sity last June and since that time has
been traveling in Europe, spending most
of his time, however, in Paris. He went
to Rome about a month ago and has
been touring Italy. After the ceremonies
at Ravenna, Schwartz will return to
Paris, where he will be until the middle
of December. He will return to this
country the latter part of the same
month and will resume his teaching in
the winter quarter.
