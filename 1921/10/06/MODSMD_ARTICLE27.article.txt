# Oregon Agricultural College Has Large Roll
## 
### Pacific Intercollegiate News Service 
Corvallis, Ore., Sept. 6. —More than
3,000 students, 3033 to be exact—had
registered at the college up to the
end of the third day, an increase of
approximately 20 per cent over last
year at the same time.e On the basis
of figures for past years, President
W. J. Kerr predicts a registration of
regular students for the entire school
year of approximately 4,200 or 6,000
including short course and summer
session students.
