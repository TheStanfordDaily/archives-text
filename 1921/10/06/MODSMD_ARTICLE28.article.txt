# FREMONT OLDER WILL TALK TO JOURNALISTS
## 
### 
Fremont Older, editor of the San
Francisco Call, will hold an open for
um in room 12, of the Law Building,
on next Thursday evening, October
13. He will talk on the "American
Press and Its Problems." After the
talk Mr. Older will answer any ques
tions that may be asked of him on the
subject. The meeting is scheduled to
start at 7:30, and is held under the
auspices of the Euphronia Club. All
members of the Daily Palo Alto staff
are especially requested to be present
at the meeting and all others inter
ested in journalism are cordially in
vited.
