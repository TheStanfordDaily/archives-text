# Sequoia Freshmen Will Organize Athletically
## 
### 
A meeting of the freshmen in Se
quoia was held Tuesday evening, to
urge all the newcomers to get out for
some activity and to assure them of
the support of the upper classmen.
The meeting was addresesed by War
ren Lewis, who spoke on literary act
ivities, and by Junior Collins, vice
president of the Oval Club, the Se
quoia hortor society. B. F. Haley, in
whose room the meeting was held,
acted as chairman.
