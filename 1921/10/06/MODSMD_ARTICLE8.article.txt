# ROBLE ENTERTAINS AT FANCY DRESS DINNER
## 
### 
Roble Club entertained last night
at a fancy dress party and dinner,
given at the hall, for the freshmen
and new students living there. Miss
Mary Yost, dean of women, and Miss
Margaret Woolsey, secretary of the
Y. W. C. A., were the guests of honor,
and attended the affair in costume.
Following dinner there was a short
entertainment, after which a grand
march and dancing completed the
evening.
