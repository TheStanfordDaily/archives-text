# SEVERAL SCHOLARSHIPS ARE STILL AVAILABLE
## 
### 
There are several University grad
uate scholarships and the Stanford Me
morial Scholarship still open, according
to an announcement made by the Com
mittee on Scholarship today.
In 1900, Mrs. Stanford established the
Stanford Memorial Scholarship in mem
ory of her son. Living expenses, cost
of books, fees, and other necessary ex
penses of the holder are paid. A room
in Encina, in addition, for which there
is no charge is set aside.
Applications for appointment to these
scholarships may he filed with Professor
P. A. Martin, room 229, History Build
ing, up to Tuesday noon, October 11.
