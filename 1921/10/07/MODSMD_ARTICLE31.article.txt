# BARBARIANS TO MEET LOCAL LEGION SOCCER TEAM HERE TOMORROW
## 
### 
The first game of the University and
Club Soccer league will be played on
the Community grounds in Palo Alto
next Saturday afternoon at 3 o'clock,
when the Stanford American Legion
team will meet the Barbarians of San
Francisco.
All men of the American Legion and
any others who are interested in the
spcrt are urged to attend. Most of last
year's men are out again this year, and
as the Barbarians have a strong aggre
gation, the game promises to be an ex
citing one. Stanford's team will be
composed of the following men : Quimby,
Clark, Dr. Browne, Maloney, Todd,
Gardner, McGillvary, Price, Mattison,
J. Adams and Smith.
