# Professor Bassett Will Speak in Los Angeles
## 
### 
Professor Lee Emerson Bassctt of
the English Department will leave today
for Los Angeles where lie will speak at
teachers' institutes in the various small
towns of Los Angeles county. Next
Wednesday and Thursday he will talk
before the Northern Section of the Cali
fornia Teachers' Association at Sacra
mento.
