THETA SIGMA PHl—Meets in the
Women's Clubhouse, at 4 o'clock.
Important meeting.
SUNDAY
ALL new students in Little Theater,
at 10:45, to attend Matriculation
Services.
MONDAY
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB — Special
meeting, in the Women's Clubhouse,
at 7:30 p.m. Members only.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Commuters! Get your Daily Palo
Alto at the Southern Pacific news
stand. Paper will be sent home on
request.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Due to other work the , class of
Faculty Equitation has been changed
to Monday and Wednesday, at 2 p.m.
The first meeting will be Monday.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Courses in Secretarial Training are
still open. Either shorthand or typing
or both may be taken. Students will
please register as soon as possible.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
English Club members are request
ed to turn sonnets over to Miss Buck
ingham, Room 211, or send them to
the secretary, Miss Dorothy Pierce,
by Saturday, October Bth.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Managerial tryoutees are needed on
the Daily. Candidates should report
as soon as possible to the business
office, between 11 and 12 o'clock in
the morning and 1 and 3 o'clock aft
ernoons, on any day the paper is is
sued.
