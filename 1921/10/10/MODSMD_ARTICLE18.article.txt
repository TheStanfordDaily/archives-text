# Will Accept Big Game Applications This Week
## 
### 
Applications for Big Game tickets
will be received at Encina Gymnasium
any time after October 15, until the
closing date, to be announced later.
No seats will be sold before that date.
Script issued by California in exchange
for stadium contributions is not to
be considered in the seat sale. All
applications for the 1921 Big Game
must be made under a separate agree
ment through the graduate manager's
offices of both universities.
