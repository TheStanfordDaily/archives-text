# FORMER CREW MEN TO MEET TUESDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
The subject of crew prospects is to
be revived tomorrow night when an im
portant meeting of all former crew men
will be held at the Chi Psi house prompt
ly at 8 o'clock. This meeting will take
up the question of the crew this year,
and important business will be discussed.
This announcement includes all men
who rowed on varsity, second varsity,
and freshmen crews.
