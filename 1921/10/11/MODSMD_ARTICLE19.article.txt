# On'n Off the Campus

### 
Frances Willis, '20,.
Returns to Campus
Frances Willis, '20, has returned
to the campus after spending the
past year in Belgium, where she had
a Fellowship to the University of
Brussels. Miss Willis, who is a mem
ber of Roble Club, was formerly presi
dent of Y. W. C. A., and of Cap and

Gown. She has been awarded another
Fellowship in Political Science, and
is now doing research work, under
Professor West, in the Hoover War
Collection.
