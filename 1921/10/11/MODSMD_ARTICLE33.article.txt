TUESDAY
LENS AND PLATE—Tuesday, at 7
p.m., in the Dark Room.
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON—Meets at
7:15, in Room 60.
ENGLISH CLUB—Meets at the resi
dence of Professor Smith, 12 Alva
rado, at 8 p.m.
CIRCLE S SOCIETY—WiII hold an
important business meeting at the
Zeta Psi House, at 7.30 p.m. Only
old members.
FORMER CREW MEN—Meet at Chi
Psi house, at 8:00 p.m. IMPORT
ANT. This includes all men who
have rowed on varsity, second var
sity and freshman crews.
WEDNESDAY
M. E. SOCIETY —Meets in Union,
at 7:45 p.m.
CIVIL ENGINEERING SOCIETY—
Meets at Beta Theta Pi house, at
7:30 p.m.
Second rehearsal of the class in
orchestra practice (one unit of
credit) at 7:30 p.m., Room 84 (Or
ganist's office). Any further enrol
ment should take place not later
than this time.
THURSDAY
SKULL AND SNAKES will meet in
the Union at 12:45.
The members of CAP AND GOWN,
women's honorary society, will hold
the first meeting of the year at Mrs.
Bailey Willis* home on Lasuen at 4
o'clock.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Commuters! Get your Daily Palo
Alto at the Southern Pacific news
stand. Paper will be sent home on
request.
NOTICE!
Make-up examinations which were
announced for October thirteenth to
fifteenth, inclusive, have been post
poned until later on in the quarter,
possibly November sixteenth to eigh
teenth, inclusive. These are make
ups in the Law School for last quar
ter and other law courses not given
last quarter, including law 1. Further
details will be announced later.
