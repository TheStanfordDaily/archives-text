# Alumni Season Tickets Can Be Obtained Now
## 
### 
Cards admitting the faculty and
alumni of Stanford University to all
athletic games throughout the year are
now on hand at the Graduate Manager's
office and can be obtained by applica
tion. The cards admit the holders to all
athletic contests held at Stanford dur
ing the year with the exception of all
conference games. The regular charge
of $5 is being made for the season
tickets.
