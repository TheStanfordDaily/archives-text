# DR. JORDAN WILL GIVE ADDRESS TO NESTORIA
## 
### 
Disarmament in its past and present
phases is to be the subject oi an ad
dress to be given by Dr. Jordan to the
I Nestoria Debating Society this evening
I at 7 :30 in Room 12 of the Law Building.
Dr. Jordan's knowledge of the subject
