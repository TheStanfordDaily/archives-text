# Encina Courtiers Hold Ceremonies; Back to Normalcy
## 
### 
The first court fire of the year was
held yesterday evening at 11 .23 in the
Encina east wing. The lire was the first
of a series to be given, according to cus
tom, at regular intervals throughout the
year. The time was spent in reviewing
the future activities of the participants,
and a detailed discussion of the business
of all present was entered into. Plans for
personal conferences were made, and
aqueous refreshments served to all.
Those present declared the affair a
howling success.
