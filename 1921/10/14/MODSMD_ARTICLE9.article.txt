# Stanford Dames Hold Get-Together Meeting
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames Club held an
open meeting yesterday afternoon in
the Women's Clubhouse, which was
attended by sixty of the members.
There were many new ones among
this number, and the club hopes be
fore long to increase their member
ship to one hundred. Most of the
time was spent in getting acquainted,
hut plans were also made for the
coming year regarding some charity
work which the club is taking up.
