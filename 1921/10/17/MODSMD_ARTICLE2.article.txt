# Dr. Gardner Will Conduct Freshman Tour Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow at 4:45 p. m. Dr. Gardner
will conduct the first group of freshmen
and new students on a tour of the Me
morial Church. All those whose names
come between A and E in the alphabet
will meet at the hour mentioned in the
vestibule of the church where Dr. Gard
ner will meet them. Owing to the size
of the freshman class it has been neces
sary to make a division .into three
groups, the second of which will visit
the Memorial Church 011 Wednesday
and the third on Thursday.
