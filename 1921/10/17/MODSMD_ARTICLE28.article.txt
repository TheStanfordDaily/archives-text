# WOMEN TO ELECT NEW HOCKEY MANAGER
## 
### 
Carey Baillard, 22, president of the
Women's Athletic Association, an
nounces the election of a women's
hockey manager to be held on the hockey
field Wednesday at 5 o'clock. She will
manage all business concerning the
freshman, sophomore, junior and senior
teams. All women are asked to attend.
Am election was held this afternoon
on the hockey field for captain of the
senior team.
