# PLAYS CONSIDERED BY "SOPH" COMMITTEE
## 
### 
A meeting of the Sophomore Play
Committee was held in room 203 yes
terday in order to select a play to he
given by the class of '24 during the
early part of December. A spectular
play is being looked for so as to secure
elaborate and distinctive stage settings
and lighting effects.
A score of plays have been under
consideration by the committee, among
which are "Prunella" by Barker and
Houseman; "The Little Princess" by
Sheldon, and "A Kiss for Cinderella"
by Barry. Some plays have been sent
for from the East. The committee con
sists of Helen Whitney, E. H. Greppin
and M. o.| Tobriner, chairman.
