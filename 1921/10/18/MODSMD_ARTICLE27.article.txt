# Classical Club Members Will Elect New Officers
## 
### 
The Classical Club will hold its first
meeting of the year in the Women's
Clubhouse Wednesday night at 8 o'clock.
Professor H. R. Fairclough will speak
on "Some Personal Reminiscences of
Switzerland and the Balkans."
Officers for the current year will be
elected and general plans discussed. The
Classical Club is one of the oldest cam
pus organizations, having been initiated
iu 1908. All those majoring in the clas
sics and any others that are interested
are eligible for membership.
A general social hour and refresh
ments will follow the business meeting.
