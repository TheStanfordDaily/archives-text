# Members of Y. W. C. A. Cabinet Will Meet
## 
### 
There is to be no joint meeting of
the Y. W. C. A. and Y. M. C. A. cabi
nets as was planned for Thursday, the
combined conference having been post
poned to some future time. The Y. W.
cabinet members will nevertheless meet
at 5 o'clock on Thursday, in the Wo
men's Clubhouse, to make final arrange
ments for the cabinet house party which
is scheduled to be held in Saratoga this
week-end. No supper is to be served
at the meeting.
