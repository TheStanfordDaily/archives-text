# SENIORS WILL MEET TOMORROW AFTERNOON
## 
### 
A meeting of the senior class will be
held Thursday at 1 o'clock in room 214.
The president urges all members to be
present since important business is to
lie transacted. Among the questions to
be decided is that of senior pictures in
the 1923 Quad. Wylie Kilpatrick, editor
of the Quad, will explain the reasons
and plan for the substitution of senior
photographs for those of the juniors.
The clas sof 1923 has voted for the
change, and it is now up to the senior
class to voice its opinion.
