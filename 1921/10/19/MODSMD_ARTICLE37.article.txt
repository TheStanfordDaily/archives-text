# Four New Members Chosen At Schubert Club Meeting
## 
### 
Four tryoutees were admitted to
Shubert Club, the women's glee club,
In the meeting held Tuesday evening,
from 8 to 9 o'clock, in Room 84. Maria
Anderson, Katherine Beswick, Ruth
Barten, and Sara Meskimmons, were
those chosen.
Tryouts will be held again Tuesday,
from 4:30 to 6, and from 8 to 9, at
the Chi Omega house, 7 Lasuen.
Everyone who is interested is asked
to try out as the club wishes to have
seventy-five members this year.
