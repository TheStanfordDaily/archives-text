# Women Commuters Elect Two Conference Members
## 
### 
At a meeting of about fifty of the
Stanford women commuters held yes
terday noon at the clubhouse on the cam
pus, the following members were elect
ed to Women's conference: Grace
Swithenback of San Jose and Dorothy
Cramer of Palo Alto. These two will
represent the women at large on the
governing group of the five-hundred.
The newly elected members, with
Mary Mathews, president of the Asso
ciated Women Students, will form a
committee for the organization of a
Commuters Club which will meet, prob
ably each Tuesday noon at the club
house, to consider the interest of that
group and to express their organized
opinion regarding the activities of the
women students.
