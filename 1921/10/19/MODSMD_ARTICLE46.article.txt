# Music Club Elects Three New Members at Meeting
## 
### 
Three members were elected to the
Stanford Music Club, last night, at a
meeting held in the Women's Club
house. They are Maria Anderson, '25,
Muribeth Cameron, '25, and Clarence
Burnette, '25.
Miss Anderson, who is a soprano
soloist, sank two numbers, and Miss
Cameron rendered two classical num
bers on the violin. Burnette gave the
club members bis interpretation of
two piano solos.
All the tryoutees were not given an
opportunity to perform one account
of the limited time. Other tryouts
are to be held soon. The definite time
will be announced later.
