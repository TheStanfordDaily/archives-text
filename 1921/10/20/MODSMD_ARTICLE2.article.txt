# Lacrosse Squad Entry List Open at Gymnasium
## 
### 
All men interested in lacrosse, the
Canadian sport, will have the chance
to sign up for this activity, which is
to be added to the list of minor sports,
now open at Stanford. Full credit is
to be given to all on the entry list.
Plans for matches with the University
of California are at present being
formulated.
LaCrosse sticks and complete equip
ment for two full teams are now on
hand at the Gymnasium. Matches are
to be played on the polo field.
