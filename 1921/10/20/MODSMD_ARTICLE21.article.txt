# Be There
## 
### 
You can hear the story of the successes of the
Cardinal eleven from the coaches tonight. You

will be told the prospects for victories during the
balance of the season and in the Big Game. You
will hear for the first time this season the new
coaches who are building the Stanford team. You
will have a chance to voice your approval of your
coaches and your team at the mammoth football
rally in Encina tonight. Any one of these features
should attract you. All together they make it im
perative that you be among those present at the
football rally of the year.

THOSE TRYOUTEES WHO WERE unsuc
cessful in these tryouts should not be disheartened.
Because it was necessary to confine the final selec
tion to fifteen, several men and women who showed
distinct promise were not included. Their failure
to finish among the first fifteen this time should
not discourage them from trying out again. Several
of the most successful members of the present staff
did not get on the paper until their second and even
third trials.
