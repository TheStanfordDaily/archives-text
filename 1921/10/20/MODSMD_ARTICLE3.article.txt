# Rooters to Loosen Encina Hall Roof At Rally Tonight

The Encina clubrooms at 7:30 is set
as the time and place for the football
rally this evening. This is the time at

season will be fully gone into, yells and
songs to be given at the game Saturday
practiced, and complete bop on the
