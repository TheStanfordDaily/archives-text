# SPANISH CLUB WILL HEAR DR. ESPINOSA
## 
### 
Spanish Club will hold its lirst meet
ing of the year in the Women's club
house this evening at 8 o'clock. Dr.
Espinosa of the Spanish Department will
talk to the members about the club and
its possibilities. Officers for this quarter
will be elected. All students who are in
terested in Spanish are invited to attend.
