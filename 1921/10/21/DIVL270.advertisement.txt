Are You Homesick?
If you arc, even a wee bit, you'll find that there are, after all,
things one can do to make one's room look more like home,
and less like the strange place one's room-mate has scattered
her belongings about in!
LET THE LIVINGSTON SHOP SHOW YOU!
For instance —bright cushions, of flowery cretonne, or vivid
silk taffeta, as gay as your gayest dance frock!

doir lamps, handkerchief cases, and many other delightful
things dear to the feminine heart! You'll like them!
