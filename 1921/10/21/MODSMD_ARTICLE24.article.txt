# Glee Club Begins Season With Large Membership
## 
### 
Interest in the work being under
taken by the Glee Club, recently aug
mented by sixty-five new members, is at
a high pitch, the many new men start
ing their club careers with great enthu
siasm. Owing to the large number now
in the club,, competition will be keen
for places on the Glee Club team which
will make concert tours during the year.
Director Allen is well pleased with the
showing that so far has been made by
the club, and is very optimistic over its
prospects.
The club lias been divided into two
sections, one meeting on Mondays and
Wednesdays and the other on Tuesdays
and Thursdays.
