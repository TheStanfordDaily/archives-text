# Maniac Meanders Madly; Sequoia is Severely Shaken
## 
### 
Shots. Wild excitement mingled with
cries of "get him, hold him. Where is
lie?'' More excitement. "Where'd he
go?" Dashing here and elsewhere of
pajama-clad figures. Shots again, wound
ed brought in. Several speedy sentinels
save sadly shaken sacrifice. Maniac
makes much malicious mischief, finally
fleeing from ferociously lighting fresh
men.
Calm. Much barricading of doors.
Several sophomores seen silently snick
ering in isolated corners of Sequoia.
Lights out. More calm. Joke? Uh-huh.
