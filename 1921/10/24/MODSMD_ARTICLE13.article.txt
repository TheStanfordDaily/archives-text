# Seventy Vacancies For Ushers, Ticket Takers For Big Game Still Open
## 
### 
There are still seventy vacancies
for ushers and ticket takers at the Big
Game and application for same may
be made to Ernest Wilson in the Ad
ministration Building, from 1 to 5
p.m., Tuesday, October 25th. All ush
ers and ticket takers will receive 50
cents per hour in addition to admit
tance to the game.
All men who made application at
the Graduate Manager's Office for
ushering and ticket takers positions
have been accepted and will be notified
in regard to their duties at a later
date.
