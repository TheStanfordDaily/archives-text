# RALLY ON THURSDAY TO OPEN TRAINING TABLE FOR VARSITY
## 
### 
The training table rally is to be held
on Thursday night at the Delta Upsilon
house, where the training table is to be
conducted. At this time each member
of the varsity squad is slated to give a
short talk to the students.
The institution of the training table
for Stanford's varsity teams is looked
upon as marking the beginning of the
final lap in preparation for the Big
Game with the University of California.
Yell Leader Connolly urges every one
possible to bq on hand 011 this occasion
to give the team his support. Tll this
connection, he also requests that all liv
ing groups practice as much as possible
the Stanford songs and yells so as to
be able to take a full part in the rooting
on November 19.
It is announced that according to
present plans, the bonfire rally will be
hold as usual in the Assembly Hall, hut
that several rallies during this week will
be held in the stadium.
