# Stanford Red Book to Give Complete Details of Student Government
## 
### 
The Stanford Red Book for 1921-22
will be off the presses next week. This
book, which will be twenty-four pages,
is published every year under the au
spices of the executive committee, and
gives the officers and details of student
government.
This issue will contain the constitu
tion of the Associated Students, rules
and methods of enforcing the honor
system, the constitution of the Men's
Council, and of the Women's Athletic
Association. The names and duties of
all important committees are cited, Skull
and Snakes' resolutions in regard to
athletic awards are explained, the rules
given governing Student Body publica
tions, and all details of the election sys
tem set forth. A complete exposition of
Stanford traditions will be included.
