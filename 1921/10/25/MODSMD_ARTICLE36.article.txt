# ELECT NEW OFFICERS FOR CLASSICAL CLUB
## 
### 
New officers have been announced
for the Stanford Classical Club, fol
lowing a meeting held for the pur
pose of naming those who will head
the organization during the year.
Those elected were: F. L. Farley,
president; Blanche Sullivan, vice
president; F. C. Hutchens, secretary;
and W. E. Tenimore, treasurer.
Professor H. R. Fairclough brought
the meetting to a close by giving some
interesting reminiscences of his Red
Cross work in Europe
