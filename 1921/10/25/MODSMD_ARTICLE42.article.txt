# PHI DELTA PHI TAKES SEVEN NEW MEMBERS
## 
### 
Miller Inn of Phi Delta Phi, national
legal fraternity, today announced the
election to membership of seven law
majors, Roland Kinney, '22, Richard
Kelly, '22, John Boucher, '22, Ralph
Cowing, '22, Robert Meyers, '20, George
Taubman, '20, and Percy Heckendorf,
'23.
Initiation will take place before the
District Appellate Court in the near
future. Judges of the Circut Court
who are members will participate.
