# Cap and Gown Will Give Dance on November 12
## 
### 
A dance will be given by Cap and
Gown, according to action taken at their
regular meeting held yesterday at Mrs.
Branner's home. The date of the dance
is November 12, which will be a closed
date.
Many different plans were discussed
at the meeting. No definite action has
been decided upon as yet, but tlie mem
bers of the Cap and Gown are looking
forward to an unusually active year.
