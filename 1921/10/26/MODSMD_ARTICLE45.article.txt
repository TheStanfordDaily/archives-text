# SPANISH CLUB WILL HEAR DR. C. G. ALLEN
## 
### 
Di> C. G. Allen of the Spanish De
partment will speak to El Club Espa
nol, tomorrow evening, at the meet
ing which will be held at 8 o'clock
on the second floor of the Women's
Clubhouse. Dr. Espinosa will address
the club.
Students who are interested in
Spanish are invited to be present.
All Spanish majors are automatically
members of the organization and
should pay their dues to Truman
Donoho, '22, treasurer. This meeting
is principally for the purpose of help
ing the members to get acquainted
with each other.
