# Y. W. C A. WILL HOLD RECOGNITION SERVICE
## 
### 
A recognition service to receive into
its active membership all members
of the Y. W. C. A. will be held at 5
o'clock tomorrow afternoon, in the
Memorial Church. Miss Helen Solt,
the student secretary for the Pacific
Coast Field, will come down from
San Francisco to speak on "The Pur
pose of the Y. W. C. A. Throughout
the World." Miss Solt has only re
cently entered her work and the Y.
W. C. A. is anxious that all old as well
as new members com* out to meet
her at the recognition service.
