# FIVE MEMBERS TO ENTER SCHUBERT CLUB
## 
### 
Five members will be taken into
Schubert Club as the results of tryouts
held at the Women's Clubhouse yes
terday afternoon and evening. The
successful tryoutees are: Dorothy
Hunt, '23, Carol Klink, '25, Frances
Ilobinsou, '25, Geraldine Watt, '24, and
Lura Spangler, '25.
The first regular meeting of the
Schubert Club will be held next Tues
day afternoon, at 5 p.m. The place
of the meeting has not yet been de
cided upon, but will be announced
later. Roth old and new members are
requested to be present as some very
important business will come up.
