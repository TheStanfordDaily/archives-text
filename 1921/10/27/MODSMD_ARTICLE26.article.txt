# ENCINA CLUB TO GIVE DANCE SATURDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
The first Encina dance of the quarter
will he given Saturday evening in the
Encina Club rooms. The decorations and
refreshments will carry out the Hal
lowe'en idea. Good music lias been pro
vided. There will be fourteen dances as
the dance will be a 12 o'clock affair.
Programs were issued yesterday by
George Drake, '22, head of the com
mittee. The dance will begin at 8:30.
