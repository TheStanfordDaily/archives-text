# Rally at Delta Upsilon House Will Inaugurate Varsity Training Table
## 
### 
Speeches by Coach Van Gent, Cap
tain Jack Patrick and other members of
the Stanford varsity are on the program
of the training table rally to be held
this evening at 7 :30 at the Delta Upsilon
house, where the training table for this
season will be conducted. Two start
ing points for rooters is the plan of
Yell Leader Connolly, one group to go
down the row from the Sigma Nu
house, the other to start at Encina. The
1). U. lawn will be the meeting place.
The opening of the training table
marks the final heat in the preliminaries
to the Big Game, according to custom.
Tt is the purpose of the annual train
ing table rally to give the players a send
off on this course, and Yell Leader Con
nolly urges everyone to be present to
help the cause along.
