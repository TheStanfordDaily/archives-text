# WEDNESDAY ALUMNAE TEA WELL ATTENDED
## 
### 
The alumnae tea held in the Women's
Clubhouse yesterday afternoon from 4
until 6 o'clock was one of the best at
tended of the teas given this quarter.
The women of Roble Hall acted as hos
tesses. Those who received were: Miss
Gardiner, Mrs. Franklin, Mary Mathews,
'22, Katherine Donald, '22, and Mrs. C.
A. White.
Numerous small parties enjoyed a cup
of tea and a social time. Among these
was the table at which Alice Hayes, '95,
was hostess.
