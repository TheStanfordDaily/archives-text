# Dr. Allen Epeaks at Spanish Club Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. C. G. Allen of the Spanish De
partment addressed the members of El
Club Espanol in a meeting held last
evening. Dr. A. M. Espinosa told tales
of his last trip to Spain.
Plans were made for the presentation
of a short Spanish comedy at the next
meeting, which will be held in two
weeks.
