# IOTA SIGMA PI WILL MEET TUESDAY NIGHT
##

lota Sigma Pi will meet in the Women's
Clubhouse. The report of the national
convention is to be voted upon, and plans
arc to be made for furnishing a club
room in the Chemistry building, to be
used by all the women in the department.
