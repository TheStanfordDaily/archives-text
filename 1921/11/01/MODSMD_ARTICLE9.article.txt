# BLEACHER SECTION IS RESERVED FOR WOMEN
## 
### 
A special section of 500 seats will
hereafter be reserved for Stanford
women at all athletic contests. This rul
ing is effective at once, and will be used
at the send-off rally Wednesday after
noon. By reserving the full 500 seats,
the opportunity is given to every Stan
ford woman to have a place at all
games.
Women students are to meet at the
Women's Clubhouse at 3:45 tomorrow
and march in a body to the bleachers.
