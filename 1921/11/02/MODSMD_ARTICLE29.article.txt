# STUNT DANCE TO BE HELD FRIDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
A dance for the benefit of the
bleacher stunt fund will be held in
the Women's Clubhouse, on Friday
night. Whitmer's orchestra will fur
nish the music.
The men in charge are very anxious
for a large crowd to attend. The
bleacher stunts in the new stadium
will be bigger than ever and an in
creased amount of money will be
necessary to carry out the plans pro
perly. v
