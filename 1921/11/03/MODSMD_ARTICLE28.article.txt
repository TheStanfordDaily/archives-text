# STAFF OF 1923 QUAD WILL GIVE BANQUET IN UNION TOMORROW
## 
### 
A banquet of the staff of the 1923
Quad will take place tonight at 6:30
o'clock in the curtained room of the
Union. The entire editorial and mana
gerial staffs, as well as the Quad Board
of Control, will he present, and have as
their guests a number of persons who
are to be associated with the production
of the Quad during the coming year.
Henry C. Harvey, a representative of
Schwabacher, Frye & Company, of San
Francisco, the publishers of the 1923
Quad, will speak concerning the pub
lishing end of the annual. Harvey has
been intimately connected with the pub
lishing of college year books for some
years.
