# SYLLABUS FEES WILL BE MAILED ON FRIDAY
## 
### 
Syllabus and other fees will be mailed
to students of the University on Friday.
The bills are payable at the comptroller's
office on Monday. Tuesday and Wednes
day of next week. No payment will be
received at an earlier date. A list of all
syllabus and other fees will be posted at
the registrar's window. It is requested
that students consult this list of fees
before asking for a change in the bills.
