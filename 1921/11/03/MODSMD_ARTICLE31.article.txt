# New Stanford Song Written By Alumnus
## 
### 
A new Stanford song entitled, "Alma
Mater," has been written by Weston
Wilson, '13, a former yell leader of
Stanford. Wilson is at present con
nected with a firm of music publish
ers in San Francisco.
Wilson has distributed a large num
ber of copies to the Glee Club and
band in addition to a number of copies
that have been sent to the various liv
ing groups on the campus. Additional
copies were distributed yesterday at the
first bleacher rally.
The words of the song are:
Hail! Hail! Hail! Alma Mater, Hail!
Stanford, Hail!
Thy fame shall ere prevail,
Thy sons thy glory shall acclaim.
And ever honor thy fair name,
So Hail! Hail! Hail!
Alma Mater, Hail.
Hail! Hail! Hail! Alma Mater Hail!
Stanford, Hail!
Thy fame shall ere prevail,
With hearts that ever true shall be,
We pledge our love anew to thee,
So, Hail! Hail! Hail!
Alma Mater, Hail!
The Stunt Committee and the yell
leaders have asked that every fratern
ity, sorority, club and living group
practice this song as well as the other
songs that were printed on the song
sheets that were donated by Wide
man find Sons' store, of Palo Alto, and
which were given out at the football
game last Saturday. For all those who
did not receive copies at that time
the yell leader will distribute addi
tion copies at the next bleacher rally.
