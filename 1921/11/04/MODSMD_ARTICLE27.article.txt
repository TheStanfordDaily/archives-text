# Quad Banquet Speakers Explain Production of University Year-Book
## 
### 
"Not merely a photographic and sta
tistical tabulation, but an interesting
chronicle and interpretation of a year's
life at Stanford, should be the aim of
the 1923 Quad," said Editor Wylie Kil
patrick, to the twenty-five members
of the Quad staff and guests gathered
at the Stanford Union last night, in
the annual Quad banquet.
