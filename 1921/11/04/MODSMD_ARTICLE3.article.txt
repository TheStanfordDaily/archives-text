# Ten New Men Admitted to M. E. Society, Wednesday
## 
### 
Ten new men were voted in the Stan
ford Student Chapter of the American
Society of Mechanical Engineers at a
meeting 1 held Wednesday night in the
Men's Union. These men are: E. D.
Small, A. F. Semino, R. H. Hartman,
L. S. Bush, C. L. Meyer, V. S. Esconrt,
A. IT. Herndon, R. E. Johnson, C. A.
Wilcox and D. S. Atwood.
A regular business session was held,
and tentative plans were formulated for
initiation night, which was set for No
vember 10.
