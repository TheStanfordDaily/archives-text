SUNDAY
"WHY I AM NOT GOING TO ENTER
THEf MINISTRY" will be the topic
for discussion at the Fireside Dis
cussion Club meeting, Sunday even
ing, at 7:30. Meetings are held in
the clubroonis of the Congregational
Church, Hamilton and Woverley.
All are welcome.
MONDAY
Professor Francis W. Hirst will de
liver a lecture on "Lloyd George as
Prime Minister," at 4:15 p.m., in the
Little Theatre.
TUESDAY
NESTORIA—Mid-quarter banquet, in
the Union, at 6:30 p.m.
"THE TAILOR-MADE MAN" cast
will not have its dance, which was
planned for Tuesday, November Bth,
owing to the fact that many of the
cast are rehearsing in the Football
Show.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Beginning at once, we will make
one FREE DAILY DELIVERY to the
campus, leaving our respective stores
at 3:30 p.m.
