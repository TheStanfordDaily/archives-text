# CLIP AND LEARN TONIGHT
## 
### 
Alma Mater
(By Weston Wilson, '13)
Hail! Hail! Hail! Alma Mater Hail!
Stanford, Hail!
Thy fame shall ere prevail.
Thy sons thy glory shall acclaim,
And ever honor thy fair name.
So, Hail! Hail! Hail! Hail!
Alma Mater, Hail!
Hail! Hail! Hail! Alma Mater, Hail!
Stanford, Hail!
Thy fame shall ere prevail.
With hearts that even true shall be,
We pledge our love anew to thee,
So, Hail! Hail! Hail!
Alma Mater, Hail!
Cardinal Fighting Song
Fight, men of Stanford,
Victory's in sight,
Trail Berkeley's banner,
And for the Cardinal fight, fight, fight,
fight.
Win, men of Stanford,
Win the game today.
Drive the Golden Bear,
Back to his lair,
Across the Bay.
Fight, men of Stanford,
Down them in your might,
Now rush them off the field,
And for the Cardinal fight, fight, fight,
fight.
Win, men of Stanford!
Win the game today.
Drive the Golden Bear,
Back to the lair,
Across the Bay.
