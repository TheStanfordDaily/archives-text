# SEVENTEEN NATIONS ON INTERNATIONAL NIGHT ACT PROGRAM
## 
### 
A variety of entertainment is planned
for International Night in the Women's
Clubhouse November 14. The Cosmo
politan Club will put on acts representa
tive of seventeen different nationalities.
Hints given out to date promise a pro
gram on a larger scale than ever before
attempted.
Life at the University of California
will be interpreted by a group of black
face comedians to be sent down by the
Areopagus Club, U. C.'s cosmopolitan
organization. A revelation of Hindu
magic is to be put on by club members.
Chuck and Chu will give an exposition
of Hawaiian music on Chinese mando
lins.
