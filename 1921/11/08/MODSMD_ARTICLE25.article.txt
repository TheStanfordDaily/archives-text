# Quad Editorial Tryoutees To Have Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
Editorial tryoutees of the 1923 Quad
will meet in the office of Editor Wylie
Kilpatrick, at 2 o'clock, tomorrow
afternoon. Important phases of the
work of tryoutees for the coming
edition of the year-book will be out
lined by the editor, and assignments
will be given the various candidates,
