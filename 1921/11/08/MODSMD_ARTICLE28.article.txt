# Plans Discussed at Y. W. And Y. M. Joint Meeting
## 
### 
Plans to send fifty men to the Y. M.
C. A. Conference at Asilomar during
Christmas vacation was the chief topic
discussed at the joint meeting of the cab
inets of Y. W. C. A. and Y. M. C. A.
last week, in the Women'sS Club
house. Hugo iiecker, '21, who spoke
on Asilomar, said that this year fully
fifty Stanford men are expected to at
tend.
