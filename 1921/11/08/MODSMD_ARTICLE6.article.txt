# NESTORIA DEBATERS TO BANQUET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Nestoria Debating Society will
gather around the banquet table at
6:30 this evening in the Union, for
mid-quarter festivities. Professor J.
G. Emerson, debating coach, will give
the address of the evening to the
members, candidate members, and
guests assembled. The customary dis
play of humor and oratorical pyro
technics will be indulged in by the
members.
