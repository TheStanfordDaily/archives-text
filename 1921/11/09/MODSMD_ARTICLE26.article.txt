# Freshmen Hold Drawing For Tennis Tournament
## 
### 
Thirty-two first-year men have en
tered the freshmen singles tennis tour
nament which will start on Thursday.
The drawings have been made and the
following matches have been scheduled:
Thursday at 2 o'clock—T. Bielefeldt
vs. H. A. Ashly; H. Auerfeldt vs. E.
Douglas; D. F. Forster vs. D. D. Suit.
Three o'clock—G. A. Marks vs. S. S.
Miliken; Walter Starr vs. D. H. Peery;
A. Wenrick vs. S. B. Pratt; H. Porter
field vs. E. Hawkins.
Four o'clock—Charles Holmes vs.
Walter Maas; J. C. Wright vs. Glenn
Kramer; Davidson vs. Doyle; L. Du-
Rois vs. K. R. Swartz; H. Rowland vs.
Charles Foye; R. Rowland vs. P. Man
ger; H. A. Miller vs. J. Aydellott.
