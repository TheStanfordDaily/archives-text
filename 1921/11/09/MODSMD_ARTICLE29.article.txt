# BIG GAME RETURNS AT FAIRMONT HOTEL
## 
### 
The second annual Stanford dinner
dance scheduled for November 19, in
celebration of the Big Game, will take
place at the .Fairmont Hotel, San
Francisco. Reservations accompanied
by check or money order must be
sent to J. E. McDowell, Alumni Sec
retary at Stanford, not later than No
vember 18. Tables at $2.75 per plate
may be arranged for, two, four,- six, or
eight persons, though special rates are
offered to out-of-town guests. The
dinner is scheduled for 7:30 p.m.
