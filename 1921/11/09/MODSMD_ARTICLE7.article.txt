# INTRAMURAL TENNIS SEMI-FINALS PLAYED
## 
### 
Semi-finals in the men's singles tennis
tournament were played off last night.
Phil Neer, varsity captain, had little diffi
culty in eliminating his two opponents.
The first match, with A. H. Goldstonc,
was won 6-1, 6-0. J. A. Craig was de
feated by a 6-1, 6-1 score.
Ford Tussing defeated J. Neer after
two sets of long volleying, 6-4, 6-2.
Many deuce games were played, consist
ent chopping by both players resulting in
long rallies.
