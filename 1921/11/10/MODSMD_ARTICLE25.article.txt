# Cap and Gown to Have Dance Saturday Night
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, the senior women's
honor society, will give a dance Satur
day night at the Women's Clubhouse.
Comptroller and Mrs. A. E. Roth. Coach
and Mrs. Walter Powell, and Major
and Mrs. Leroy Collins will be the pa
trons and patronesses.
Music for the dance will be fur
nished by Owen Grimmstead's orches
tra. All of the proceeds from the dance
will go to the Women's Clubhouse fund.
