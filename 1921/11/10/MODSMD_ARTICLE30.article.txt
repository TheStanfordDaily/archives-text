# Dr. Morgan to Lecture on The "Origin of Life"
## 
### 
"The Origin of Life" is to be the sub
ject of the discussion of Dr. Morgan
next Sunday morning at 10 o'clock in
the Memorial Church.
Dr. Morgan is conducting a course at
this hour every Sunday, with the gen
eral title of "The Modern Approach to
Religion." The aim of the discussion
is to show that religion does not con
flict with science, but that it is the out
growth of natural processes.
Everyone interested in the subject is
invited to attend, especially upper class
men. A knowledge of biology, psychol
ogy, sociology, and philosophy is pre
supposed.
