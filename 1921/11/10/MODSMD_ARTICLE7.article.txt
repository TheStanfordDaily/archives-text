# Professor Smith Talks To G. & M. Society
## 
### 
Profesor J. P. Smith of the Geology
department gave a short talk before a
meeting of the Geology and Mining So
ciety in the Breakers clubroom last night,
in which lie outlined the history of the
society since its foundation in 1892, and
pointed out the careers of several
former members who have become na
tionally prominent in geology circles.
The men included Herbert Hoover,
Theodore Hoover, professor of mining
here, Ralph Arnold, and George Ashley,
the first man to receive a degree in min
ing at Stanford.
A short business meeting followed,
during which ice cream was served and
cigars were passed around.
