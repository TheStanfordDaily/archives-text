# Cercle Francais Changes Usual Plan of Meetings
## 
### 
At a rccent business meeting of La
Cercle Francais, it was decided to have
only one meeting a month instead of
two, with an off-campus gathering in
addition to the regular meetings. This
quarter it will take the form of a
Christmas meeting.
Twenty new members have been en
rolled this year which, added to the old
membership of the. club, promises to
make it one of the largest memberships
it has ever had. The date of the next
meeting of the club will be announced
later.
