# SERVICES ARE HELD BY OVERSEAS LEAGUE
## 
### 
A memorial service for all American
women who gave their lives in overseas
service was held after the regular morn
ing worship Sunday in the Memorial
Church by the Western division of the
American Women's Overseas League.
This service, conducted by the Reverend
Francis Van Horn of the First Congre
gational Church, Oakland, concluded the
annual convention of the Overseas
League which met last week at the Fair
mont Hotel in San Francisco.
