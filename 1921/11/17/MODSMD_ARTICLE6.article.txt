# FOOTBALL ISSUE OF DAILY TO APPEAR ON THE CAMPUS FRIDAY
## 
### 
Tomorrow's issue of the Daily Palo
Alto will be the annual football edition.
In addition to articles by the regular
sport writers of the paper, sport writers
of the San Francisco papers are to give
their opinion of the game, and the Card
inal chances. "Brick" Morse, Warren
Brown and Ed Hughes are some of
those scheduled for articles.
A short history of each of the Card
inal players and coaches will also be
included, with many cuts and illustra
tions to enliven the stories.
A six-column picture of the team
lined up for action is one of the fea
tures of the edition.
