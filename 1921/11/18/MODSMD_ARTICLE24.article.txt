# "TINY" HARTRANFT
## 
### 
S. Glenn Hartranft, number 23, is an
other of the players from last year's
freshman aggregation. He plays any line
position, and when be lias entered the
game has made his presence felt by
smearing end runs and busting things
up in general for the enemy. Hartranft
weighs 192 pounds, and is one of the
fastest men on the squad. He was timed

at 101-5 seconds in the 100-yard dash
last year in track. He calls Fullerton his
home, and is a member of Kappa Sigma.
Hartranft is 19 years old, and is 6 feet
3 inches tall.
