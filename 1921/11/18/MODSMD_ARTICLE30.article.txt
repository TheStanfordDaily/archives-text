# "MIKE" REED
## 
### 
Myron Nelson Reed is a member of the
class of 1923 and won his numerals on
Art Acker's freshman team two years
ago. He played quarterback and holds
a similar position on this year's varsity
squad. He was not enrolled in the uni
versity last fall, so he did not have an
opportunity to play football. "Mike's"
number is 27. He is 22 years old,
weighs 180 pounds, and is five feet eleven
inches tall. He is from Portland, Ore.,
and registers in the Economics Depart
ment. Reed is a member of Beta Theta
Pi.
