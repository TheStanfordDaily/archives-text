# HUGH FULLERTON
## 
### 
Hugh Thompson Fullerton will be
ready to jump in at half in the clash
tomorrow. Fullerton has had two years'
experience on the varsity squad, and
only his lack of weight has kept him off
the first team. He tips the weights at
153, and stands 5 feet 10 inches. Fuller
ton belongs to the class of 1921, coming
from Lowell High School of San Fran
cisco, and is a Law major. He is a
member of Beta Theta Pi fraternity. His
number is 33.
