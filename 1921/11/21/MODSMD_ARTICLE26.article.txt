# ALTERNATING SEATS TO HELP CALIFORNIA HONOR SPIRIT
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA,
Nov. 21 (P. I. N. S.)—ln order to
make the observance of the Honor
Spirit in examinations are easy as pos
sible for the student body, the Student
and Faculty Welfare Committees have
decided to revive the pre-war custom of
alternating the seating. This is made
possible in large classes by the forma
tion of smaller groups taking the ex
aminations in places.
