# Schubert Club Holds Tryouts For Concert
## 
### 
Tryouts for Schubert Club arc being
held every Tuesday from 5 to 6 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse. All women
with vocal ability are urged to tryout.
The Schubert Club is planning to give
some concerts later in the year, and in
order to make these a success, a full
membership is necessary. Three new
members have recently been taken into
the club. They are: Mabel Ferry, Vic
toria Gunby and Catherine Ilarvey.
