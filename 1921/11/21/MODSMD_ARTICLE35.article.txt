# BASKETBALL LEAGUES TO START NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Intramural basketball will start the
first of next week, according to C. S.
Botsford, director of intramural ath
letics. Twenty-two fraternities and
eight other organizations have entered
teams. It is planned to have the com
petition in the various leagues finished
by the Christmas vacation. As soon as
the new basketball pavilion is completed,
some of the games will be played there.
A mounted bronze basketball plaque
will be presented to the winner of each
league, and a championship plaque to
the campus champions.
