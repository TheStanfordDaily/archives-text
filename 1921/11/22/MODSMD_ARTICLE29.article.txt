# DIVORCE LAWS TO BE TOPIC OF WOMEN'S DEBATING TEAM
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Nov. 22. —"Resolved, That laws
governing divorce should be made uni
form by Congress," will be the sub
ject for the O. A. C. women's varsity
debate tryout. Debates will be held
with the University of Oregon, and one
California college. Eight women will
be chosen for the squad from which
four will later be chosen for the team.
Three college credits will be given the
girls who work on the squad.
