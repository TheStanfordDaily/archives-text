# La
						Crosse Management Arranges Canadian Tour
## 
### 
Negotiations are taking place with
British Columbia clubs for a series of
mid-summer lacrosse meets to be played
in Canada, according to the team's man
agement. If this tour is arranged for, it
will be conducted during the summer
quarter, commencing about June 25 and
ending at the beginning of the autumn
quarter.
Competition will soon be offered the
local boys by the University of Califor
nia, where lacrosse has lately been in
troduced. It is the hope of the Stanford
team that the organization of a Califor
nia squad will be completed in time to
arrange for a number of games either
this fall or early next spring.
