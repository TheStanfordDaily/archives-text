# Improvements in San Juan Hill District Being Made
## 
### 
As part of the improvements on the
San Juan subdivision, to be used for
faculty homes, lighting, domestic and
searville water, and sewer lines are now
being laid back of the Sigma Nu house.
The lines will be laid down to Alvarado
Row, and will be large enough to ac
commodate all future needs of the sec
tion. No overhead wires are being in
stalled, as part of a plan to eliminate
telephone poles from the district.
