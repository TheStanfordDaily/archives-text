# SOUTHERN STUDENTS MUST ATTEND CHAPEL SERVICES
## 
### 
U. S. C., Los Angeles, Nov. 23 (P. I.
N. S.) —Chapel service assembly has be
come compulsory at the University of
Southern California. On Tuesday, regu
lar chapel exercises are held, student as
semblies for rallies and lectures coming
on Thursday. Attendance is checked by
seat location.
