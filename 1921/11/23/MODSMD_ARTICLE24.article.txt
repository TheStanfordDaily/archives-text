# O. A. C. PRIZE-WINNING HENS GET FREE TRIP TO ENGLAND
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, No. 23 (P. I. N. S.)
—Eight prize-winning liens of the O. A.
C. experiment station have gone to Eng
land to be entered in what is said to
be the largest international egg-laying
contest ever held, according to authori
ties. Their journey is a 6,000-mile one,
to Bently, Suffolk, England. The birds
are all of the famous Oregon variety,
developed at the agricultural college.
