# WILLAMETTE UNIVERSITY ADOPTS ARMS RESOLUTION
## 
### 
WILLAMETTE UNIVERSITY, Sa
lem, Ore., Nov. 23 (P. I. N. S.)—The
Williamette student body has adopted
a resolution favoring international arms
limitation, which was taken by Professor
W. H. Hertzog, official representative of
Williamette, to Chicago, where the Dis
armament Convocation of Universities
and Colleges has convened.
