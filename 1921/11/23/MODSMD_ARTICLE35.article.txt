# DR. EXNER GIVES TALK BEFORE MEN STUDENTS
## 
### 
Dr. M. J. Exner, director of physical
education of the American Social
Hygiene Association, spoke before a
large crowd of men last night in the
Assembly Hall.
His topic was "The Rational Sex
Life," and in his speech he stressed
the importance of young men living
up to their ideals. Dr. Exner is at
present on a speaking tour of the Pa
cific Coast. The talk was given under
the auspices of the local Y. M. C. A.
