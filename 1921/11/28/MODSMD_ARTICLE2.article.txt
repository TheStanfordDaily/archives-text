# STANFORD DAMES ARE HOSTESSES AT PARTY
## 
### 
One hundred students, their wives,
mothers, and faculty members were en
tertained at a Thanksgiving party given
by the Stanford Dames, at the Women's
Clubhouse last Wednesday evening.
A musical program followed the in
formal reception. The numbers included
trumpet solos with piano accompani
ments; piano recital by Mrs. Charles
Moser; vocal solos by Mesdames J. W.
Mansfield, E. O. McCormick and W. H.
Powell; violin and piano duet by Mr.
and Mrs. C. D. Weaver; Paul Laurence
Dunbar selections read by Mrs. Fordvce
Stewart; and piano numbers by Mrs. J.
W. Mansfield.
