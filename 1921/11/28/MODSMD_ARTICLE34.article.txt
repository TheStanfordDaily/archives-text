# THANKSGIVING DINNERS PROVIDED FOR STUDENTS
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Nov. 28 (P. I. N. S.)
—Invitation to Thanksgiving dinners
for all O. A. C. stude-nts not able to go
home for the holidays were issued by
the Y. M. and Y. W. associations. Cor
vallis families offered to open their
homes and entertain the students.
