# NORTHERN GLEE CLUB HAS TWO TOURS MAPPED OUT
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Nov. 28 (P. I. N. S.)
—The O. A. C. Glee Club will make two
tours during the winter term. The first
will go to Portland and Vancouver in
January, and the second will include
Seattle, Tacoma, Olympia, Aberdeen and
Centralia, in March. Later the girls of
the Madrigal Club will tour southern
Oregon.
