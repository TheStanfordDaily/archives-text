# Cardinal Manager Issues Call For New Tryoutees
## 
### 
Ivan Deach, manager of the Stan
ford Cardinal, has issued a call for
managerial tryoutees. They may call
at his office in the Student Body office
between the hours of 8 a.m. and 10
a.m., or 1 p.m. and 2 p.m. Any one
wishing to try out for manager should
call as soon as possible, in order to
get acquainted with work at once.
