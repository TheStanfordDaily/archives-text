# NESTORIA TO DISCUSS EXCESS PROFITS TAX
## 
### 
Nestoria debaters will discuss the
proposition, "Resolved, That the United
States Senate Was Justified in Repeal
ing the Excess Profits Tax,"'at the reg
ular meeting tomorrow evening in room
12 of the Law building. The affirmative
will be supported by H. W. Dryden, '24.
and P. W. Harris, '24, while 11. A. Bu
tree., '25, and O. C. Barbee, '24, will
speak on the opposite side. A free dis
cussion will follow the debate, and heck
ling is to be in order.
