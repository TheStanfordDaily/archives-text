# HALL MEN ASKED TO RESERVE ROOMS SOON
## 
### 
Encina and Sequoia men should not
fail to reserve tlieir rooms for next
quarter if they plan to remain in the
dormitories, according to an announce
ment by George B. Culver, dean of men.
The new Union building will not be
open before February, and there is al
ready a waiting list for both Encina and
Sequoia.
Reservations should be made at the
dean of men's office on the following
dates: Encina, December sth, 6th and
7th. Sequoia: December 12th and 13th.
Drawings for rooms will be at Encina
December Bth and at Sequoia Decem
ber 14th.
