# "COLLEGE NIGHTS" PROVE SUCCESS AT CALIFORNIA
## 
### 
Berkeley, Ca., Nov. 29. —(P. I. N.
S.) —"College Nights" was held for the
last time this semester in Hearst hall,
University of California. "The Per
fect Lover," a one-act play which was
presented made up the main part of
the entertainment.
"College Nights," attempted for the
first time this semester, have proven
very successful. Socially they have
succeeded in bringing the students
closer together. Financially they
have more than paid for themselves.
