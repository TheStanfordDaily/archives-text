# Chemical Processes Are To Be Shown in Pictures
## 
### 
Motion pictures showing the manu
facture of dynamite, of zinc oxide, and
the smelting of iron ore are to be shown
in the Little Theater Thursday evening
at 7 :30, under the auspices of the Chem
istry Club. Professor W. J. Crook, Dr.
E. C. Franklin, and Professor N. W.
Rakestraw will give descriptive talks
accompanying the films. The processes
shown in these pictures are said to be
of especial interest to all engineering,
chemistry, and geology majors. The
meeting will be open to the public.
