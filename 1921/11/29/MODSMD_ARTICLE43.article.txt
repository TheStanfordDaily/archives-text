# COMMUNITY PLAYERS TO GIVE "BILLETED"
## 
### 
On the evening of December 15, in
the Assembly Hall, the Palo Alto Com
munity Players will present their first
plav, "Billeted," which is a recent success
of Margaret Anglin. The production
will be directed by Coach Gordon Davis.
Among the amateurs taking leading
parts arc William Brownless Owens,
associate professor of law, and Barbara
Stettheimer, '24, of Atherton. Others in
the cast are Mrs. A. T. Murray and E.
P. Lesley of the Mechanical Engineering
Department at Stanford.
