# OLYMPIC COMMITTEE ISSUES 1920 LIST
## 
### 
Harry Maloney announces that a
book containing an offcial list of all
the events of the Olympic Games of
1920 has been issued by the Amer
ican Olympic Committee. The book
is very elaborate and the details are
interestingly carried out. Mr. Maloney
has conceived the plan of giving one
of these books to each fraternity on
the campus, upon request by an au
thorized representative from each
house.
