# ONLY POET LAUREATE IN U. S. SPEAKS AT WHITMAN
## 
### 
Whitman College, Walla Walla,
Wash., Nov. 30— (P. I. N. S).—John
D. Neidliardt, poet laureate of Neb
raska, addressed the Whitman stu
dent body yesterday. Known as "The
Poet of the Prairies" he was appointed
laureate by a special enactment of the
Nebraska legislature, and he is the
first man in this country to be awarded
this honor. He speaks at Oregon,
Washington, and Idaho also.
