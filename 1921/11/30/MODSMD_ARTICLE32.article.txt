# TWO "FLUNKS" MEAN A PERSONAL CONFERENCE HERE
## 
### 
Willamette University, Salem, Ore.,
Nov. 30. — (P. I. N. S.) —A personal
conference with the president con
fronts any student who has two or
more "flunks," according to a new
faculty ruling, and a seething schol
astic mob surged about a long flunk
list of names tacked on President
Doney's door, early last week. Many
upperclassmen were among those who
received the call.
