# History Club Meets To Discuss Irish Question
## 
### 
At a meeting of the History Club held
night at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse, the problem of the Irish
Question was discussed. Lucy Wilcox,
'22. led in the discussion.
Tlic next meeting of the club will be
held next Thursday at the home of Pro
fessor E. D. Adams.
