# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## U. S. C. CAPTAIN MAY NOT PLAY IN SATURDAY'S GAME 
### 
U. S. C., Los Angeles, Dec. 1 (P. I.
N. S.) —In all probability, Charley Dean
will not play during all of the Univer
sity of Southern California-Oregon Ag
gies' game, if he plays at all. He is
still ill with a severe cold, and he may
be unable to play.
