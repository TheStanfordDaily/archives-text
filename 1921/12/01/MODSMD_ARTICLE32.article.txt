# FIRST DEBATERS' RALLY IN HISTORY TO BE AT U. C.
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA.
Berkeley, Dec. 1 (P. I. N. S.) —For the
first time in the history of the University
of California a debaters' rally will be
held. The team that will represent the
University in the debate with Stanford,
December 2, will be given a send-off on
Wednesday evening. Several prominent
speakers, among them President David
P. Barrows, will address the meeting.
