# ENGINEERING STUDENTS PUT OUT OWN MAGAZINE
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Nov. 30 (P. I. N. S.)
—Students in the electrical engineering
department here arc editing a magazine
of their own, known as the "Student
Engineer." The publication includes a
two-color design and many half-tone
engravings.
