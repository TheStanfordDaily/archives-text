# DEAN MARY YOST TO LEAD DISCUSSION ON "SOCIAL STANDARDS"
## 
### 
Miss Mary Yost, dean of women, will
lead a discussion on "Social Standards
in tlie University" at the Y. W. C. A.
meeting next Tuesday afternoon at 5
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Some of the questions on the subject
which will be answered arc:
"What is a social standard?" "How
are standards of conduct formulated?"
"What part do women play in form
ing standards of conduct in the Univer
sity?" "Do men or women have the
larger share in establishing standards?"
"In what ways do the general social
standards fall short of the desirable at
Stanford?" In what way may the cur
rent standards of conduct be either
raised or lowered?"
