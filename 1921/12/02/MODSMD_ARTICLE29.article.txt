# WHITMAN COLLEGE ELEVEN WINS NORTHWEST TITLE
## 
### 
WHITMAN COLLEGE, Walla Wal
la, Wash., Dec. 2 (P. I. N. S.)—The
Whitman College football team, coached
by "Mig" Borleske, a graduate-coach,
won the 1922 northwest conference
football championship this fall with a
record of three victories and no de
feats. W. S. C. finished with two vic
tories and one tie, while the University
of Oregon, with one. victory and three
tie games, took third place. The cham
pionship is the first football title that
Whitman has won.
