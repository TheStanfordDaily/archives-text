# TENNIS EXHIBITIONS WILL BE NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Owing to rain the exhibition match
scheduled to open the new varsity ten
nis courts yesterday afternoon was post
poned until next Thursday. For the
match next Thursday, in addition to the
singles players secured, two of the best
doubles teams in the United States will
be seen in action. Davis and Roberts
have given their consent to play next
week and, in all probability, the Kinsey
brothers' team will also make its ap
pearance.
Johnston and "Peck" Griffin will fur
nish most of the excitement in the sin
gles matches, with Davis and one of the
Kinsey brothers completing the bill.
