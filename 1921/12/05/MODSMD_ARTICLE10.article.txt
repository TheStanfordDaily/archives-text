# ORGAN RECITAL TO BE REPEATED TUESDAY
## 
### 
The organ recital which was given
Sunday afternoon, consisting of the J
Symphony in G minor, by Kdward
Barnes, will be repeated Tuesday after
noon in Memorial Church at 4:15.

hearsals for the. oratorio next Sunday
will be held Wednesday evening at 7 :30
and Thursday at 7:30. There will also
be one Sunday afternoon at 3 o'clock.
