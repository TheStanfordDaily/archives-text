# Large Attendance Is Expected at Alumnae Tea
## 
### 
Mrs. Slater and Mrs. A. D. Browne
will hold a bridge party of fourteen
tables at the Alumnae Tea at the
Women's Clubhouse, Wednesday aft
ernoon from 4 to 6. At the same time
Mrs. Parkinson and Mrs. Dwyer will
also entertain.
Tho tea is expected to be an unusu
ally large one as about 150 people are
expected to attend. The hostesses
will be: Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur, Mrs.
Timothy Hopkins, Mrs. T. T. C. Gre
gory, Mrs. John T. Nourse, and Miss
Mary Yost.
