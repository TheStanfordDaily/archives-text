# Civil Eugineers Will Hold Smoker Saturday
## 
### 
The Civil Engineers will hold a de
partmental smoker in the Faculty Club
house next Saturday night at 8 o'clock.
Prominate graduates of the department
will speak as will several of the under
graduates. The meeting is intended for
a general get-together, and eats and
smokes will be served. All engineering
majors are invited.
