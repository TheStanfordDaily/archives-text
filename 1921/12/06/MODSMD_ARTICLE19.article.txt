# Dr. Carl L. Alsberg Will Address Classical Club
## 
### 
Dr. Carl L. Alsberg will address the
Classical Club on Wednesday evening
at the Women's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock.
Although his subject has not yet been
definitely announced, he will discuss
some phase of the Food Institute's work.
Dr. Alsberg is director of the Food In
stitute in the University. The discussion
is open to all interested.
