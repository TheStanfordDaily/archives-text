# MEMBERS APPOINTED ON OPERA COMMITTEE
## 
### 
Five members of the class of 1923
have been appointed to the Junior Opera
Committee by Chairman Dean Ross.
They are Margaret Lies, Ivo Copeland,
Florence Pixley, Richard Malaby, and
Charles Midgley.
In order that work on the opera may
be done during the Christmas holidays,
Chairman Ross desires to meet all those
interested in writing lyrics and plots to
be in Gordon Davis' office at 7:15 to
morrow night> The general plan of the
opera and the type of songs to be used
will be discussed.
