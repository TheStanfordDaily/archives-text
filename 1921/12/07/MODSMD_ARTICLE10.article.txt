# Dave Wallace, '20, Elected Skipper of Rugby Varsity
## 
### 
Dave M. Wallace, '20, was elected
captain of the Stanford rugby team
Monday night. He ]is one of the
Stanford men who played on the All-
California rugby team that won the
world's championship at Antwerp, in
1920. Wallace is a member of the
Chi Psi fraternity and registers in the
Geology department. This is his third
year on the rugby varsity.
