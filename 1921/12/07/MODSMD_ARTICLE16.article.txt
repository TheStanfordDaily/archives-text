# CAP AND GOWN ELECTS TWO NEW MEMBERS
## 
### 
Mary Jane Clark, '22, and Kathryn
Donald, '22, were elected to Cap and
Gown, the senior women's honor so
ciety, at a meeting held yesterday
afternoon, at the home of Mrs. Elsie
Branner Fowler, in Palo Alto.
Miss Clark is the present womens'
editor of the Daily Palo Alto, and was
a member of the 1922 Quad staff, and
has taken part in several dramatic
productions. She is a member of
Theta Sigma Phi and of Kappa Alpha
Theta. Miss Donald is president of
Roble Club, and is on the Y. W. C. A.
cabinet.
One hundred and forty dollars was
turned over to the football men from
the sale of tags for the benefit of the
varsity training table, according to
the final report given at the meeting.
