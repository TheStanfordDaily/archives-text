# OREGON AD CLUB HOST TO ORGANIZATION PRESIDENT
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OE OREGON. Eu
gene, Dec. 7 (P. I. N. S.) —Charles
Henry Mackintosh, president of the As
sociated Advertising Clubs of the World,
will visit the University of Oregon cam
pus as the guest of the University Ad
Club, which is a member of the world
organization. He is to address the stu
dent body at the.weekly assembly and
will be entertained by members of the
Ad Club while on the campus.
