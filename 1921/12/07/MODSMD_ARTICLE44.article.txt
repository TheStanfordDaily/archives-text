# Staff of Daily Palo Alto To Hold Banquet Thursday
## 
### 
Members of the Daily staff will hold
their quarterly banquet at the Kappa
Alpha Theta house on Thursday evening
at 8 o'clock. Dancing will be from 6
to 8.
Decorations have been planned by the
committee in charge to carry out the
newspaper idea, although their exact na
ture has not been divulged.
Each quarter the new members of the
staff give a banquet for the older mem
bers.
