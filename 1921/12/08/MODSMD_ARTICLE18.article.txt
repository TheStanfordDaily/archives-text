# Decorations Are Being Made For Union; Dining Room Tile Work Started
## 
### 
Ornamental molds for the arcade of the
new Union have arrived, and work has
begun on the manufacture of the deco
rations. Owing to the variety of de
sign, the kitchen of the Union where
the pieces are being set resembles a
curio shop.
Flooring on the third story is practi
cally completed, and work on the second
floor has started. Setting of the quarry
tile in the small dining room on the
main floor has also begun. This tile
which will be used in the three dining
rooms and the kitchen of a dark red
color.
The tile roof of the pavilion is almost
finished. Lumber for the. galleries has
just arrived, and construction is being
started at once. The preliminary work
in laying the maple flooring of the pa
vilion has also commenced.
