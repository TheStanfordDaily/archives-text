# NEW MEMBERS PLEDGED BY THETA SIGMA PHI
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi pledged two new
members at the regular meeting yes
terday afternoon, at 4 o'clock, in the
Women's Clubhouse, Maurine Voerge,
'23, and Marion Potter, '22. The pled
ges will be initiated the early part of
next quarter.
Both Miss Voerge and Miss Potter
are members of the Daily Palo Alto
staff.
