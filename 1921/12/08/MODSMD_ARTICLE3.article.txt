# ENCINA TO END TERM WITH DANCE, FRIDAY
## 
### 
Encina Club's dance of the quarter
will be Riven tomorrow night in the En
cina clubrooms. An attractive color
scheme is beinp carried out under the
direction of Bob Connolly, '22, chair
man of the committee, on decorations.
This is to be a football dance and all
members of the 1921 football squad have
been invited. The programs, conceived
by George Drake., '22, chairman of the
committee for that purpose, carry out
the football idea.
Paul McCoy's six-piece orchestra
from the Hotel Oakland will furnish the
music. Dancing will start at 9 o'clock
and last until 1 o'clock.
