# RUTH ST. DENIS BOOKS RECEIVED BY LIBRARY
## 
### 
Ruth St. Denis and Ted Shawn have
presented to the Library two illustrated
volues, "Ruth St. Denis: Pioneer and
Prophet." These are from a 350-copy
edition. They are the history of Ruth
St. Denis' cycle o Oriental dances, the
text being written by Ted Shawn.
He tells of "how they came into being,
how they developed, flowered and multi
plied." Volume I is the text and Vol
ume II is devoted to plat'es of the
dances. These were given to Stanford
in remembrance of "an evening full of
stimulus on April 21, 1921." Ted Shawn
has written on the fly-leaf that "it was
an audience that made us wish we could
carry it with us always."
