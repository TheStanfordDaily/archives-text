# MASQUER MAIDS MAKE MERRY OVER MYSTIC MIRTHFUL MANUSCRIPT
## 
### 
Tryouts for Masquers' play will be
held next Tuesday.
Shrouded in mystery is the name of
the forthcoming production and all
other information concerning it. Suffice
it to say that that enterprising coterie
of dramatic maids have received the
manuscript of one of the best and most
sought-for successes of the present
season —a knock-out in story, movie, and
on the stage.
The matter of acquiring it was pure
luck, and the Masquers promise a play
which will take the campus by storm
when it appears the first part of next
quarter.
This—and this alone will they di
vulge—the cast of characters offers rare
and unusual opportunity for feminine
ability and charm. There are. many
types and everyone dramatically in
clined, with the exception of freshman
women, may tryout next Tuesday, at a
time and place to be announced later.
