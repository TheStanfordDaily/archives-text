# Schubert Club Will Give Short Concert at Roble
## 
### 
A short concert will be given by Schu
bert Club at Roblc Hall Tuesday even
ing at 7 o'clock under the direction of
Warren Waters. The program will be
about half an hour in length, and every
one is invited to attend. The numbers
to be given are:
Piano solo—Prelude Mendelssohn
Marjoric McDonald.
Soprano solo—Song of the Robin
si una Case
Maria Anderson.
Violin solo —Playera Sara sate
Maribeth Cameron.
Baritone solo—Vision Fugitive
Massenet
Warren Waters.
Selections—
When Daddy Sings Victor Harris
To a Wild Rose MaeDoivell
Merry June Charles Vincent
Schubert Club.
