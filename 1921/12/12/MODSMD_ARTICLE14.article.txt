# Polo Handicap Title of Campus Will Be Decided
## 
### 
The finals of the fall quarter polo
handicap tournament will start on
Wednesday. The Reds will meet the
Whites, and the Blues play the Browns,
in the regulation alternate period
matches of six minutes each. The win
ners of these contests will decide the
campus title in the final event on Fri
day afternoon.
The Whites are rated the strongest
team, with a handicap of seven. The
Reds come next, with five, and the Blues
have three. The Browns, composed
largely of new men, have no handicap
assigned.
In practice matches last Friday the
Reds defeated the Browns 10-5. by scor
ing three goals, and the Browns held the
Blues to a tie by only allowing three
goals.
