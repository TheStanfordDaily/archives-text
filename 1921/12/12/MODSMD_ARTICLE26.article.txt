# Permanent Shrubs Are Set Out About Quad Palisades
## 
### 
Four varieties of berry shrubs are be
ing set out as permanent plantings about
the palisade in front of the Quad. These
plants are to take the place of the an
nuals recently killed by frost, although
it is planned to set a few annuals in
place next spring.
Two shades of red and two shades of
orange are the colors of the berries.
The shrubs being planted are : Pyracan
tha Lalandi, Crataegus Yumanenis, Ca
toneaster Pannosa, and Catoneaster
Horizontalis. Most of the plants have
been raised in the University nursery.
