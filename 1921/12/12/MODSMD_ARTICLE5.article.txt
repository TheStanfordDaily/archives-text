# MURPHY WINS OPENING RACE AT SAN CARLOS
## 
### 
Jimmy Murphy, a San Francisco
boy, set a new world's record for the
250 mile distance at the opening race
of the San Carlos Speedway, yester
day afternoon. Murphy averaged 111.8
miles an hour for about 2 hours and
15 minutes.
About 35,000 people saw automobile
racing make its debut in the bay regi
ons. Frank Elliott crashed into the
rail at the top of the track, but he
and his mechanician escaped unin
jured.
