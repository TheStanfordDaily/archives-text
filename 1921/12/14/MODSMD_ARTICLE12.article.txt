# Fletcher Appoints Junior Assistants For Football
## 
### 
Upon the recommendation of Roland
T. Kinney, football manager for the
1021 season, Graduate Manager Wm.
Fletcher has appointed the following
men to serve as junior assistants to
the football manager, for the 1922
football season. Leslie Dills, Thomas
Bannon, Elliott Ayers, John Mace,
Donald Stewart, and Simeon Zane.
On the failure of any of these men
to report next year, the following men
will be substituted in the order nam
ed: William Lucas, Jerome Draper,
and Alfred Patton.
