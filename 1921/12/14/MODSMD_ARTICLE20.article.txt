# Putty Work on Chemistry Windows Being Gone Over
## 
### 
Putty work on every window of the
Chemistry Ruilding is being renewed.
Tn several places the putty had cracked
off to such an extent as to endanger
the panes, especially on the. north side,
which is most exposed to the elements.
All cracked places are being reinforced
thoroughly. Workers are at present
going over the windows of the Qualita
tive Chemistry Laboratory.
