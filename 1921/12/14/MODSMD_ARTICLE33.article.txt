# AGGiES WILL NOT CHANGE COACH, IS PRESENT REPORT
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Dec. 14. (P. I. N. S.) —
Coach R. B. Rutherford will remain at
the head of the Aggie coaching staff
notwithstanding rumors to the contrary
recently printed in a Portland paper.
The board of control in its meeting yes
terday voted its entire confidence and
united support to the coach.
