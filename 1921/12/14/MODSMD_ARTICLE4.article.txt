# CHICAGO IS POSSIBLE OPPONENT FOR COACH TEMPLETON'S VARSITY
## 
### 
Negotiations are under way for a
track meet between the University
of Chicago and Stanford, according to
an announcement from the Graduate
Manager's office today. The proposed
meet would take place here during
Stanford's preliminary season, prob
ably in the last week of March.
Coach Stagg. of the University of
Chicago has written to the Graduate
Manager requesting information on
the possibilites of such a meet. It is
expected that plans for the proposed
meet will be announced defiinitely in
the near future. The move for such
a meet is in line with the attempt to
promote more intersectional athletic
contests in the future.
