# STUDENT HANDBOOK WILL APPEAR SOON
## 
### 
The handbook of the Associated Stu
dents of Stanford University has been
revised and will appear on the campus
within a short time. The handbook
contains information of the Associated
Students, and short articles on the
Board of Athletic Control, Daily Palo
Alto, the Men's Council for Student Af
fairs, graduate manager, student man
ager, senior manager system, athletic
eligibility, and other committees and
systems of the campus. The duties, re
sponsibilities. and privileges of members
of the student body are included in the
book.
The book is published by the Execu
tive Committee and is free to all mem
bers of the Associated Students.
