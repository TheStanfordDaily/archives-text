# PROFESSOR COTTRELL LEAVES FOR HARVARD
## 
### 
Professor Edwin A. Cottrell, of the
Political Science Department, has taken
out a leave of absence for six months
in order to take the place of Professor
Monroe in the Harvard Department of
Government. At present he is lecturing
in southern California.
Professor Cottrell has been at Stan
ford three years, and has lectured chief-
ly in the administrative branch of po-
litical science. He will return in June.
