# LUCY WILCOX LEAVES FOR LONDON POSITION
## 
### 
Lucy Wilcox, '21, will leave Sunday
for a visit in the East prior to sailing for
London at the end of the month. She
will do newspaper correspondence work
in connection with Francis W. Hirst,
who was a visiting professor in the Eco
nomics Department during the summer
and autumn quarters and gave a series
of Monday lectures on Lloyd George and
the present British political situation.
Hirst has been connected with English
newspaper work for some time and was
editor of "The Economist" before com
ing to the United States. He will handle
news to be published in numerous Amer
ican journals including the Philadel
phia Ledger.
Miss Wilcox was secretary to Hirst
during his stay at Stanford.
