# Athletic Situation To Be Skull and Snakes Subject
## 
### 
Skull and Snakes is to convene to
morrow evening at 7:30 in the Union.
The importance of the subjects for dis
cussion makes it imperative that mem
bers be on hand, announces President
Tauzer. Matters of special interest, in
cluding details of the athletic situation,
and a tenative proposal for an old-time
"Jolly Up," will come up at this time.
