# Special Meeting of Music Club to Be Held Tuesday
## 
### 
A special meeting of the Stanford
Music Club will be held at 7:15 to
morrow night in the Women's Club
house. As important business will
come up at this time all members are
requested to attend this meeting.
Tryouts will be held at the regular
meeting which will be held on Tues
day, January 16, in the Women's
Clubhouse. The club hopes to in
crease its membership to twenty-five.
There are now twenty members in
the club.
