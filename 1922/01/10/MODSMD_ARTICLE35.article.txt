# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## GLEE CLUB PLANS EXTENSIVE TOUR THROUGH THREE STATES OREGON AGRICULTURAL COLEGE, Corvallis, Jan. 9. (P. I. N. S.) 
### 
LEGE, Corvallis, Jan. 9. (P. I. N. S.)—
A trip to Portland and Vancouver,
January 13 and 14, and a tour through
southern Washington—the first ap
pearance outside the state —are some
of the activities planned for the col
lege glee club this year.
A singing personnel of 24 men, a
director and accompanist, make a
better balanced organization than
ever before has been formed, accord
