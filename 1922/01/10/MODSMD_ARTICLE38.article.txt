# EDITORIAL BOARD TO HELP RUN O. A. C. PUBLICATION
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Jan. 9. (P. I. N. S.) —
The editorial stall' of the "Barometer,"
student paper of O. A. C., has been
strengthened through the addition of
an editorial board which will act in an
advisory capacity to the editor and
assist in preparing the editorial. The
members of this board are for the
most part seniors who have a thorough
knowledge of campus conditions. The
editorial board will conduct research
into desirable methods and customs
in use 011 other campuses and endeavor
to transmit its findings to O. A. C.
