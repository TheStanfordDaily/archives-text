# Encina Club Votes To Reduce Cost of Dances in Future
## 
### 
Dance expenses of Encina Club will
be reduced hereafter, according to a
vote cast at a meeting of the club last
night. Tlie motion passed provides that
the maximum amount of money to be
expended will be $75. In view of the
newly launched endowment campaign, a
resolution was passed which stated that
all surplus money derived from dances
shall be given to the endowment fund
for the University. To make possible
a surplus it was decided that in the fu
ture no refreshments other than punch
shall be served at dances, and that only
one program shall be issued with each
ticket. Hereafter no ticket will exceed
$3, and it was recommended that an av
erage of $2 be .maintained.
