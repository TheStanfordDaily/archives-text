# Professor Robinson To Speak To History Club
## 
### 
The History Club will hold its first
meeting for the quarter Thursday
evening, at S o'clock, in the Women's
Clubhouse. Professor E. E. Robinson,
who has recently returned from the
Disarmament Conference at Washing
ton. 1).C.. will speak.
A short business meeting will also
be held, for the purpose of electing
a new president to fill the vacancy
left by Lucy Wilcox, '22, who is not
in the university this quarter.
