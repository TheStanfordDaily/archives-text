# Local Students Invited To Hear Harvard Dean Speak
## 
### 
The Harvard Club of San Francisco
will entertain Doctor Donald K. David,
Assistant Dean of Harvard School of
Business Administration, at dinner at
the St. Francis Hotel on Monday, Jan
uary 16.
Stanford students interested in the
school are cordially invited to attend the
dinner and Invar Doctor Davis talk of
the school. Those intending to come
will kindly notify George 11. Lent, 503
Market street. San Francisco, and seats
will he reserved for them. Dinner will
he in the Colonial Room at 7 p. in.
Cost. $2.00.
