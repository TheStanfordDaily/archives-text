# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## MORE LENIENT TREATMENT COMING TO U. C. FRESHMEN 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA,
BERKELEY, Cal., Jan. 12.—(P. 1. N.
S.) —More lenient treatment of the fresh
men at the hands of the second-year
men was the sentiment expressed by the
Senior Peace committee at a meeting
Monday. Class tradTMons must be ob
served. The sophomores have been
cautioned to molest no one who is not a
frosh, especially the entrants. Undue
hazing and unbecoming conduct has been
discouraged. It is the duty uf the senior
class to see that these sentiments are ob
served.
