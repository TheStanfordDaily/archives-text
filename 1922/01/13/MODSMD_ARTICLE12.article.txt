# David Starr Jordan to Celebrate Anniversary At Home Next Thursday
## 
### 
On Thursday, January 19, Dr. David
Starr Jordan will celebrate his seven
ty-first birthday, at his home, Xazmin
House, on the campus. He will en
tertain the Jordan Club, at 4 o'clock
in the afternoon. The Jordan Club
was founded on Dr. Jordan's sixtieth
birthday. In the evening Dr. Jordan
will entertain a number of his old
friends. Among them will be the Old
Guard. The Old Guard is composed
of the original members of the Stan
ford faculty. Because of this celebra
tion Dr. Jordan will not be able to
have the Students' Conference at his
home that evening.
