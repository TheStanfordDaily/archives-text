# Oberle Will Tour Europe With Party This Summer
## 
### 
Professor K. J. Oberle, formerly an
instructor in French at Stanford, now
at Rice Institute, Texas, will act as
guide for a party of people through Eu
rope thi* summer on a six weeks' tour.
The party, which was organized under
the Temple Tours of New York, will
visit Switzerland, Germany, and France,
seeing the battle-fields and other points
of interest, and returning through Eng
land. They expect to leave early in
July, and will return to the United States
about the middle of August.
