# ALL FRESHMAN RULES ARE CALLED OFF AT WHITMAN
## 
### 
! WHITMAN COLLEGE, WALLA
! WALLA, Wash., Jan. 17.—(P. 1. N. S.)
—All freshman rules, with the excep
tion of wearing the green "dinks," have
been called off at Whitman College,
while the green caps will he burned in
; a huge bontire following the first home
| varsity basketball game.
