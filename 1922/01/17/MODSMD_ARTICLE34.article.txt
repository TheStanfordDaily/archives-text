# SWIMMING PROVES POPULAR PASTIME AMONG CO-EDS
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, CORVALLIS, Ore., Jan. 17.—
(P. I. N. S.) —Four hundred girls at
O. A. C. are registered in swimming. The
instructors in the sport are planning a
general swimming meet. They believe
that there should be some "Annette Kel
lermans" in such a large number of
swimming students.
