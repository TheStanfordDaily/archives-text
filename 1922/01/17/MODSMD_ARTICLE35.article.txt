# HEAVY ATHLETIC SCHEDULE AHEAD OF WHITMAN TEAMS
## 
### 
WHITMAN COLLEGE. WALL A
WALLA, Wash., Jan. 17.—(P. 1. N. S.)
—The most pretentious athletic program
in the history of the college is the 1922
schedule of varsity sports lined up by the
graduate manager. "Nig" Borleske.
Seventeen conference basketball games,
21 baseball games, four track meets, two
tennis meets, and six football contests
I will be played and participated in by
J Whitman teams.
