# DISARMAMENT DISCUSSED IN INTERNATIONAL DEBATE
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
SEATTLE, Wash., Jan. 17.— (P. I. \.
S.) —Disarmament without the League
of Nations is the subject of the debate
Friday with the University of British
Columbia. The U. of W. will support
the affirmative here, and the negative in !
Vancouver.
