# PADDOCK TO TRY LOWERING FOUR-FORTY YARD RECORD
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN
| CALIFORNIA, Jan, 19. (P. J. N. S.)—j
| Charlie Paddock, the world famous
I sprinter, will compete for the IT. S. C. j
track team during the coming season. 1
The speed marvel will probably try
Ito lower the 100-yard dash record
as well at the quarter-mile record.
