# ALMOST THREE HUNDRED IN O. A. C. BOXING CLASSES
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Ore., Jan. 20.—Box
ing is becoming more and more popu
lar at O. A. C., as latest statistics show.
Two hundred and seventy-five are en
rolled in the boxing classes, and a
tournament for seven weights, from
flyweights to heavyweights, is being
arranged.
