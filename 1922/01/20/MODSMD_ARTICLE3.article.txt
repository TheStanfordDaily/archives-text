# PIE-EATING FEATURES ENCINA CLUB SMOKER
## 
### 
Custard pic was the order of the
evening at the Encina Club smoker last
night, when six freshmen entered a
pie-citing contest.
Glen Cramer, '25, and Ed Strong, '24,
Sequoia's representatives, gave several
popular tunes harmonicas, which
were heartily applauded and encored.
The dramatic phase of the program was
interpreted by the. supreme dansant, Fa
tima, aided by four assistants fresh from
the royal palace of Bagdad.
Cigars and cigarettes wore passed
around, and refreshments were served
at the close of the program.
