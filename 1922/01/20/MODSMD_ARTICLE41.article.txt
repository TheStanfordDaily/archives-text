# Scholarships Are Now Ready For Applicants
## 
### 
Professor P. A. Martin has made the
announcement that applications for
the four scholarships now available
from the Converse Scholarship Fund,
will not be accepted if filed later than
Tuesday, January 24. These scholar
ships carry an annual stipend of $250,
and undergraduate students from all
departments are eligible for candidacy.
All applicatinos are to be filed at the
office of Professor P. A. Martin. Room
229, History building.
