# June 23 Is Date Set For The Harvard-Yale Regatta
## 
### 
June 23 has been definitely announced
as the date for the Harvard-Vale re
gatta Which will be held on the Thames
river at Xew London. The big event
of the day, the varsity eight-oared race,
will be rowed up stream and will, lie
held at 6 o'clock its the tide turns at
about that time.
