# Rolling Pebbles Gather No Moss On Faculty Links
## 
### 
Fifteen men on the faculty links,
what ho, and a game of golf! That
was the spirit with which fifteen mag
nimous souls drifted over to the facul
ty golf links yesterday, filled with re
formatory zeal and the charitable in
tent of making the green once again
green.
When is a links not a links, was the
great question to be solved, and the
solution resulted in a vast expenditure
of muscular effort, and extravagant
vernacular expression on the part of
the self-instituted rockcrushing crew.
The solemn Sabbath stillness was
broken by the sounds of rolling stones,
whose moss-gathering days under the
feet of the frolicking faculty are over.
"The better the day the better the
deed," quoth the fastidious fifteen.
Now the links have taken on the ap
pearance of a newly shaven cranium,
and all that is needed is a little hair
tonic of the verdant variety to pro
duce that promising playground on
which the most meticulous member
of the intellectual contingent may
freely frolic.
