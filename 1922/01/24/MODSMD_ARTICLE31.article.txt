# "ORANGE OWL" GROWING RAPIDLY IN INFLUENCE
## 
### 
I OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Jan. 24 (P. I. N. S.)—
"The Orange Owl," official O. A. C.
comic magazine, is coming into its own.
The publication, which appeared just
before Christmas, is attracting favorable
comment from larger humorous publi
cations in the East including Life. Judge,
! and the Collegiate, World. Second class
, mailing privileges were recently ac
; corded the publication.
