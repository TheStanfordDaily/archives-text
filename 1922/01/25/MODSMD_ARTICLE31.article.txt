# O. A. C. ALUMNUS TRAVELS 13,650 MILES TO REGISTER
##

OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Ore., Jan. 25. (P. I.
N. S.) —G. A. Roberts, '07, has return
ed from Rhodesia, South Africa, 13,-
650 miles, to again register as a stu
dent at O. A. C.
