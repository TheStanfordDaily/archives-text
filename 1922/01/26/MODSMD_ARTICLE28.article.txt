# OREGON UNIVERSITIES TO EXCHANGE WIRELESS NEWS
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Ore., Jan. 26. (P. I.
N. S.) —The exchange of news between
O. A. C. and the University of Oregon
in the future will be made by wire
less. Steps have been taken to con
nect all the colleges on the coast using
tlife Pacific Intercollegiate News Ser
vice, by means of the wireless.
