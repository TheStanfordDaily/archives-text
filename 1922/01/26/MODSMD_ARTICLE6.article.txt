# ENGLISH CLUB WILL STAGE ANNUAL PLAY
## 
### 
March 8, the evening before Foun
ders' Day, ha-- been as the date for
the annual "English Club play, tryouts
for which will be held early next week,
according to announcement made this
morning.
The play has already been chosen. It
will be a spectacular production, even
surpassing the record set by the club's
presentation of the Chinese drama, "Yel
low Jacket," last year.
The cast includes thirty people, with
unusual opportunity for all lines of
dramatic talent, including two women
leads. As usual, tryouts will be open
to all but first-year women students.
