# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## PARTICIPATION, NOT POINTS TO BE BASIS OF AWARDS 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
Seattle, Jan. 27 (P. I. N. S.)—That the
requirements for football letters be in
creased, for basketball, slightly de
creased, and that track and tennis
awards be made on a participation in
stead of a winning basis, were the
changes agreed upon here by a joint
committee of the athletic coaches and
members of the board of athletic control.
