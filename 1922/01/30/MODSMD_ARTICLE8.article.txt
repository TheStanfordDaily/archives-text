# Miss Marguerite Wales Will Talk to Y. W. C. A.
## 
### 
Miss Marguerite Wales, director of
social work for the Stanford Clinics
Auxiliary, will speak on "Medical So
cial Work," at the Y. W. C. A. meet
ing in the Women's Clubhouse, Thurs
day, February 2. at 5 o'clock One
feature of Miss Wales' social service
work is the Stanford Home for Conva
lescent Children, which is in the old
Stanford residence on the campus.
