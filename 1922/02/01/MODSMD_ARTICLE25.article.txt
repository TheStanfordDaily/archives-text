# Albert Gregersen, '23, Is Elected to Music Club
## 
### 
One new member, Albert Gregersen,
'23, was admitted to Music Club, as
the result of tryouts held last night
in the Women's Clubhouse. Greger
sen has a baritone voice.
A special meeting of the club will
be held next Tuesday evening in the
Women's Clubhouse. At that time try
outs will be held again. There are
still four places open in the club. After
the meeting, the club will visit the
chapel organ with Warren D. Allen.
