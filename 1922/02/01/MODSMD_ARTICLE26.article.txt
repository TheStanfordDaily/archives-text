# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## OVER 200 MINISTERS WILL ASSEMBLE AT WHITMAN 
### 
WHITMAN COLLEGE, WALLA
WALLA, Wash., Feb. I.—(P. I. N. S.)
—Invitations are now being sent out by
Whitman College to over 200 ministers
living in Washington, Oregon, and
Idaho, within a radius of 100 miles of
Whitman, inviting them to attend an
annual interdenominational religious
conference at Whitman College, Febru
ary 28 to March 3.
Speakers of prominence will be in at
tendance at the conference, and students
will be given the opportunity to bear
short "sermonettes" by them during the
regular chapel periods.
