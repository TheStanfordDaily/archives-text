# OREGON MUSICIANS MAY TOUR EUROPE DURING NEXT SUMMER
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF OREGON, EU
GENE, Ore., Feb. I.—(P. I. N. S.) —
Plans for a summer tour of Europe by
Oregon music students are developing
under the leadership of Dean John
Landsbury. Seven students have already
expressed their desire to go, says Dean
Landsbury.
