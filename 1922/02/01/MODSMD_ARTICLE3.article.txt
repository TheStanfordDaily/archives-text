# Professor Robinson Will Talk to Sigma Delta Chi On Arms Meet Press Work
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi will hear Professor
E. E. Robinson discuss the disarmament
conference, particularly in relation to
the press, at a dinner tomorrow evening
at 6:15 in the curtained room of the
Union. Professor Robinson will also
give some of the side-lights on the work
of newspaper men in Washington, as he
saw them while there.
