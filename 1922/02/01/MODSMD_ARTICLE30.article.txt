# PROSPECTORS' COURSE OPEN TO STUDENTS AT NEVADA
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF NEVADA,
RENO, Nev., Feb. I.—(P. I. N. S.) —
Twenty-two men are registered in the
prospector's short course which ha,s
opened its eighth regular four weeks' ses
sion in the Mackay School of Mines. The
crew from a U. S. Bureau of Mines
rescue car is aiding in practical instruc
tion.
