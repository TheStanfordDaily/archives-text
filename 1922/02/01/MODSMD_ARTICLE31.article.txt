# SERVICE STRIPES TO MARK W. S. C. SECOND-YEAR MEN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON STATE COLLEGE.
PULLMAN, Wash., Feb. I.—(P. I. N.
S.)—A new type of varsity sweater for
second-year men will be awarded in the
future at Washington State College. The
sweater will have a V-neck and will have
two service stripes woven in the left
arm. 1 Three-year letter men will now
receive three types of sweaters, as the
service sweater awarded the last year is
of the coat style.
