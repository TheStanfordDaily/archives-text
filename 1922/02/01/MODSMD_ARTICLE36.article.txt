# NEW POWER EQUIPMENT INSTALLED AT O. A. C.
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE. CORYALLIS, Ore., Feb. I.—(P.
I. N. S.) —Installation of new power
equipment in the electrical engineering
laboratory was completed last week. The
addition of this new equipment places the
laboratory with the best of its kind on,
the coast.
