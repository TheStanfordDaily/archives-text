# JOHN HALL CHOSEN FRESHMAN PRESIDENT
## 
### 
John Hall, president; Elsa Barber,
vice-president; Russell Merritt, secre
tary; Horace Swing, treasurer, were
elected officers of the freshman class
at a meeting in room 214, today. The
officers were elected for the rest of
the college year.
The fight for president was closely
contested for between Hall and Don
ald Graham. Merritt defeated William
Bielfedt in the race for secretary, by
only four votes.
Votes were cast as follows: Presi
dent. CJraham, 31; Hall, 30; vice-presi
dent, Barber, 43, Leslie Peters, 17,
Swing was elected by a unanimous
vote.
