# General Mid-Year Election will be Held February 8
## 
### 
A. A. Carrey, chairman of the elec
tion board, announced yesterday that the
regular mid-year quad election would be
held Wednesday, February 8.
At this time two men will be elected
to the Men's Council ,one junior and one
senior. One member will be elected to
the Board of Athletic Control. One
junior man and one junior woman and
one sophomore will be chosen for the
Executive Committee. All class officers
will also be elected.
Beginning today, nominations may be
placed in the box at the Romanic Lan
guage Building. Carrey desires that
nominations be made early in order that
the election may be of greater interest to
the students than has been the case in the
past.
