# STUDENT GOVERNMENT TO BE REVISED AT CALIFORNIA
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA,
Berkeley, Feb. 2 (P. 1. N. S.)—The
committee for revision of the student
government of the University of Cali
fornia today considered three types of
government. The relative merits of (1)
an English Democratic parliamentary
system; (2) a commission form, and
(3) one modeled after that of the Fed
eral government were discussed, but no
definite decision was reached.
