# WASHINGTON DANCE TO BE MODELLED ON CIRCUS PLAN
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
SEATTLE, Wash., Feb. 3.—(P. I. N.
S.) —The Sophomore Glee to be held in
the R. O. T. C. Armory Friday will be
one of the most unique parties given at
the university this year.' The armory is
to lie transformed into a huge modern
circus. Between dances circus stunts
will be featured.
