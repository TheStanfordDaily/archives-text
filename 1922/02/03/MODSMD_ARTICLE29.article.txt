# THREE COLLEGE ATHLETES HAVE DUAL ROLE TO PLAY
## 
### 
WHITMAN COLLEGE. WALLA
WALLA. Wash., Feb. 3.—(P. I. N. S.)
—It is a peculiar coincidence that at the
University of Idaho, Washington State
College, and Whitman College the same
athlete who is basketball captain this year
is also captain-elect of the baseball team.
