# Y. DIRECTOR IS GUEST OF STANFORD CABINET
## 
### 
Y. W. C. A. cabinet had Miss Mar
guerite Wales as their guest at dinner
Thursday evening in the Women's Club
house after the regular Y. W. C. A. meet*
ing. Miss Wales, who is director of so
cial service work for the Stanford Clinics
Auxiliary, conducted an impromptu ques
tion and answer meeting on medical
social service, and explained her work
more specifically than she had been able
to do in her talk at the 5 o'clock meeting.
