# CHANGES MADE IN HALL APPEARANCE
## 
### 
Dinner will not be served and there
will je no charge for the open meeting
of the Journalists' Association at which
Wilbur Hall will speak next Tuesday
evening, it was announced today by the
Executive Committee of the chapter.
The meeting will be held at 7 o'clock
at the Alpha Sigma Phi House. 6 Sal
vatierra, and all interested in writ
ing, which will be discussed by Mr.
Hall, will be welcome. Reservations are
not neccssary.
Following the usual custom, the even
ing will be decidedly informal and op
portunity will be given those who attend
to become personally acquainted with a
man who has been successful in jour
nalism, particularly in the writing of
short stories.
