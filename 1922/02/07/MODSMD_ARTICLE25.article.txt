# CHAIRMAN IS CHOSEN FOR ALUMNI REUNION
## 
### 
At a meeting of the executive com
mittee of the Alumni Association last
week, Charles K. Field, '95, was made
chairman of the alumni committee in
charge of University Day, which is to
be May 20 this year.
Field held a preliminary meeting
Monday with students and alumni to
discuss the plans for University Day.
The other members of the committee
are to be announced later.
