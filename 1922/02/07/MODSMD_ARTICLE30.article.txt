# THREE THOUSAND DOLLAR GAS MACHINE INSTALLED
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Are., Feb. 7. (P. I.
N. S.) —A new gas machine, costing
$3,000, is to be installed to take the
place of the present one used in the
manufacture of gas for the chemistry
laboratories at O. A. C. The new ma
chine is provided with an electrically
driven blower and has a capacity of
20,000 cubic feet of gas per hour. It
will use a high-grade of gas—7s to 86
per cent gravity. The installation of
the machine will take place during
the spring vacation.
