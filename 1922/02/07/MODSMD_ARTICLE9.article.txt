# Grading of New Sequoia Athletic Field Is Begun
## 
### 
Work was begun yesterday on the
new athletic held for the use of the
members of Sequoia 11 all. Grading is
proceeding rapidly, as the field was re
cently plowed for planting.
Provision is being made for a base
ball diamond, a football field, and for
other minor activities. The work is
being done by the University.
