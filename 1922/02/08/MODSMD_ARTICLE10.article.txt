# ALUMNI TEAM WILL NOT PLAY IRELAND AS PER SCHEDULE
## 
### 
Due to the heavy rains recently, it
has been decided to call off the base
ball game scheduled for tomorrow
afternoon, between Cliff Ireland's Inde
pendents and the Alumni team. The
field will not be in good shape for a
game, and arrangements were made
beforehand that in case weather con
ditions became bad, the game would
be called off. Announcement regard
ing the playing of the game Satur
day between the varsity and the Alum
ni will be made later.
