# SPANISH CLUB GIVES CASTILIAN ROMANCE
## 
### 
Spanish Club presented as a part of its
program last evening a one-act play,'
"The Old Maids." The action of the
play involved many humorous scencs in
which the characters got themselves into
amusing situations. The members of
the cast were: Dorothy Quigley, '22,
Anita Osuna, '21, Elizabeth Brown, '21,
Rafael Meza, '24, and Carlos Guerra, '24.
Several musical selections concludcd
the program. These were given by
Trueman Donoho, '22, at the piano, Clyde
Gunter, '22, violin, and Ivan Hoalton,
'22, who played the cello.
