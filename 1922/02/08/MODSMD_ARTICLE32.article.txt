# Captains Chosen In First District Alumni Campaign
## 
### 
Appointment of captains for the.
alumni campaign in District No. 1,
which includes San Mateo. Santa Clara,
and Santa Cruz counties, have been
completed and acceptances tiled. The
captains are to have 100 alumni under
their jurisdiction. Canvassing in each
group will be divided among ten lieu
tenants. A report made, at a meeting
of the Executive Committee in the of
fices of Alumni Secretary McDowell
showed that a majority of the lieuten
ants have already been chosen. The
campaign in these three counties is to
be the first among the alumni, and will
immediately follow that on the campus.
