# Nestoria's Mid-Quarter Banquet Is Set Tonight
## 
### 
Ncstoria's mid-quarter banquet will he
licit! tonight at 6 o'clock in the Union.
Besides the regular festivities, a presi
dent will he elected to take the place of
S. G. Trimble, who is connected with
business interests outside which make it
impossible for him to continue in office.
The banquet will be adjourned in time
for the basketball game this evening.
