# BOOKLET EXPLAINING ENDOWMENT PUBLISHED
## 
### 
A fourteen-page booklet on "The First
Million For Stanford, and The Three
Million Dollar Program" has been pub
lished by the Endowment Committee,
and will be sent out to alumni as their
district is reached in the nation-wide
campaign. The booklet, which is of
pocket size, contains a statement by the
Board of Trustees, explanation of what
the money to be raised in the tirst, sec
ond, and third million will be used for,
and an explanation of reasons leading up
to the appeal, by President Wilbur.
