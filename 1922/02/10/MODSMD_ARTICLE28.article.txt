# STANFORD TO HAVE TWO SOCCER TEAMS ON FIELD TOMORROW
## 
### 
Stanford will have two soccer teams
on the field tomorrow. The varsity
will meet the Palo Alto Post of the
American Legion, while the freshmen
will compete with the California year
lings. Both teams are booked to win.
The varsity will play on the Palo Alto
field and the freshmen will play on
the local turf. Both games are at
2:30 p.m.
