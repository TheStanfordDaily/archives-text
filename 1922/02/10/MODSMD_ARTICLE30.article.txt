# FRESHMEN TO AWAIT SOPHOMORE ATTITUDE ON BASEBALL FIGHT
## 
### 
Freshman attitude toward the com
ing baseball fight will be determined
by the desire of the *24 class. This
was the general opinion expressed by
the first-year men in their meeting
in Room 214, this morning. If the
class fight is to be held, the freshmen
resolved to abide by all Executive
Council regulations.

freshmen to arrange for the first class
jolly-up: Women's committee, Gale
