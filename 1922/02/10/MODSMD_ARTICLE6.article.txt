# TWO "Y" ASSOCIATIONS MEET AT JOINT DINNER
## 
### 
The Stanford Y. M. C. A. was host to
the Y. W. C. A. last night at a joint din
ner held in the Women's Clubhouse. The
members of both the Y. W. C. A. cabinet
and their freshman cabinet were present,
and the Y. M. was represented by the
members of the cabinet and heads of
committees.
Dean Houston gave a short talk on the
work on the campus which confronted
both organizations. Following this, a
general discussion was held, and ideas on
the best plans for co-operation were ex
changed.
