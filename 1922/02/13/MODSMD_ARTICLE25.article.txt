# "CREW INFORMAL" GIVES $620 PROFIT FOR TRAINING MONEY
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA,
BERKELEY, Cal., Feb. 13.—(P. I. N.
S.) —Results of the "Crew Informal"
held February 3 at the University of
California show a profit of $620, which
will be used to help defray training table
expenses for the university crew. The
training table will start next week.
