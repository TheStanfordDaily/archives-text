# WOMEN AVERAGE HIGHER THAN MEN; GRADS ARE BEST
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF OREGON, EU
GENE, Ore.. Feb. 13.—( P. I. N. S.) —
Women averaged higher than men and
graduates higher than undergraduates
for the fall term, according to recent
statistics from the registrar's office. The
all-university average including both men
and women, was 3.53, the men averaging
3.75 and the women 3.29. The average
for the graduate men was 2.22, while
that of the graduate women was 2.13.
The general average of the graduate stu
dents was 2.19.
