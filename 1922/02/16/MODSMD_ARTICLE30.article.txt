# HAMMER AND COFFIN HAS ANOTHER CHAPTER INSTALLED
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Ore., Feb. 16.— ( P.I.
N.S.) —A chapter in Hammer and
Coffin society, national organization
for college comics, with its parent
chapter at Stanford, has been granted
to the Orange Owl club .of O. A. C.,
which puts out the "Orange Owl,' the
O. A. C. comic. This makes the fourth
chapter of Hammer and Coffin. The
other three being at Stanford, Oregon,
and Washington.
