# OARSMEN MUST KEEP STRICT TRAINING RULES THREE MONTHS
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
Seattle, Wash., Feb. 16.— (P.1.N.5.) —
Training rules, with a close observ
ance of sleep and diet, will go into
force next week at the University of
Washington crew house, where the
oarsmen live. Nearly three months
will be required of the men to con
dition for the California race.
