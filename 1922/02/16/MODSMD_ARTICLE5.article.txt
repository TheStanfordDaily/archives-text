# CAP AND GOWN PLANS FOR ALUMNAE BANQUET
## 
### 
Plans for the Alumnae Banquet to lie
held in May were discussed at the regu
lar meeting of Cap and Gown held at the
home of Mrs. Ryan Tuesday afternoon.
The next meeting of Cap and Gown will
he at Mrs. A. E. Roth's house, Feb
ruary 28.
Mrs. David Starr Jordan will now he
an honorary member of Cap and Gown.
Mrs. Jordan has not been able in the
past to take part in the activities of the
organization, as all her time has been
taken up in assisting Dr. Jordan in his
writing.
