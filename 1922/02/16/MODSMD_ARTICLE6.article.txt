# DOROTHY ADAMS, '25, IS SCHUBERT CLUB ELECT
## 
###

of the Schubert Club after try outs held
Tuesday night in the Women's Club
house.

will be held next Thursday afternoon
from 5 to 6 in the Women's Clubhouse.
