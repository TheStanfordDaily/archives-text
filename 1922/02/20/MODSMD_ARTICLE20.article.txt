# Palo Alto Endowment Fund Workers To Hold Banquet
## 
### 
A banquet for all workers in the Palo
Alto endowment campaign will be held
tonight at 6:30 in the Stanford Union.
The Palo Alto work starts tomorrow and
will run to Thursday, coincident with the
alumni campaign. Alfred Seale, general
chairman of the Chamber of Commerce
committee, will preside, and short talks
are to be given by President Wilbur and
Comptroller Roth. Another banquet will
be given on the conclusion of the canvass
Thursday night. In the meantime, the
workers are to interview over seven hun
dred people selected from the Palo Alto
directory list.
