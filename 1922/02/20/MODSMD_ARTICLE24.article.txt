# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## WILLAMETTE BASKET TEAM COMPOSED OF YOUNGSTERS 
### 
WILLAMETTE UNIVERSITY,
Salem, Ore., Feb. 20. (P.I.N.S.) —The
university is represented this year by
one of the youngest basketball teams
In the history of the school, the aver
age age being 20, and the youngest
Bearcat tosser, 18.
