# MENDELSSOHN MUSIC TO BE HEARD TUESDAY
## 
### 
The Sonata No. 6, in D Minor, by
Mendelssohn, will be played in the or
gan recital Tuesday, February 21. at
4:15 by Warren D. Allen. Mendelssohn's
six organ sonatas form a link between
the older music of Bach's day and the
modern organ pieces. They are classic
in form, and romantic in sentiment. In
this sonata there are four well contrasted
variations on the Chorale.
