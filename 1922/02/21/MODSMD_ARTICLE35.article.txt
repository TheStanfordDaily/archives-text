# Alpha Chi Sigma to Tour San Francisco Factories
## 
### 
A tour of the Fuller Paint Company,
and of the Illinois Glass Works, of San
Francisco, will he made by Alpha Chi
Sigma Thursday morning and after
noon. About twenty members are ex
pected to go, leaving on the 7:50 bus
and taking in the Fuller Paint Com
pany plant, which covers several acres
in South San Francisco, in the morn
ing. The party will have luncheon at
the Illinois Glass Works at one o'clock,
and will then inspect the plant, which
was rebuilt, after a tire last year, with
modern types of machinery and facili
ties.
