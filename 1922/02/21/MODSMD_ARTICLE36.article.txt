# Kenneth Ritchie, '21, To Address Lens and Plate
## 
### 
Kenneth Ritchie, '21, will speak on
"Picture Taking" at the open meeting of
Lens and Plate at 7:30 this evening.
He will take up different methods of
taking pictures in order to obtain the
best results, and will illustrate the use
of different types of cameras. The meet
ing will be held in the Lens and Plate
dark room in the Union, and will he open
to everyone interested in this subject.
