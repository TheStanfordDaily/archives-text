# FRATERNITY FRESHMEN TO GATHER AT SMOKER
## 
### 
An interfraternity freshman smoker
will be held Thursday evening in the
Men's Union at 7:00. The purpose of
the meeting is to give the fraternity
first-year men an opportunity to become
better acquainted.
Several members of the faculty will
address the freshmen. Entertainment in
the form of boxing, wrestling, and mu
sic will take a large part of the evening.
Plenty of good food is expected to bring
smiles of contentment to the faces of
the freshmen.
