# ALLAN McFARLAND IS KILLED IN AIR CRASH
## 
### 
Allan T. McFarland, '09, nephew of
Professor F. M. McFarland of the de
partment of Histology, was killed in a
recent dirigible balloon crash at Langley
Field, Virginia.
McFarland was a law major, and at
tended the University of Chicago his
first three years, completing his coursc
at Stanford. He was captain of one of
the first observation balloon companies,
and went over to France in one of the
first squadrons. He was in action at
Chateau Thierry, in the second battle of
the Marne, St. Mihiel, Argonne, and the
Meuse. McFarland had been an aviator
for several years.
