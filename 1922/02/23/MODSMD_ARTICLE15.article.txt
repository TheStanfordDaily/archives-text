# Film Showing Manufacture of Sugar To Be Exhibited
## 
### 
A film showing the complete process of
manufacturing sugar is to be exhibited
by the Chemistry Club next Wednesday
evening in the Little Theater. The pic
ture shows the methods used in the Cali
fornia-Hawiian Sugar Corporation's
plant at Crockett, and was shown at the
Industrial Exposition recently held in
San Francisco. Arrangements arc being
completed to get a man from the
Crockett plant to explain the processes
as they are shown.
