# A. LAKEY ADDRESSES MECHANICAL ENGINEERS
## 
### 
A. B. Lakey of the Kingsbury Machine
Company of San Francisco addressed
the Mechanical Engineering Society on
the "Theory and Practice of the Kings
bury Thrust Bearing" at a meeting last
night. Lakey used slides in illustrating
his lecture.
