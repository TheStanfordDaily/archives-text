# Y.W.C.A. Dress-Up Party Is Scheduled for Tuesday
## 
### 
The Y. W. C. A. annual dress-up party
will he given in the Women's Clubhouse
next Tuesday evening. This get-togetlicr
party for all the women of the university
is preceded by a dress-up party at Roble
Hall. Music, dancing, and refreshments
will be the entertainment, and the party
will close promptly at 9 o'clock, for the
sake of those having to study or having
other engagements.
