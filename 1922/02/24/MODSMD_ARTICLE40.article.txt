# Announcements!
## 
### 
MONDAY
SCHUBERT CLUB — Members will
meet at 12 :45 for Quad picture on Li
brary steps.
NESTORIA DEBATING SOCIETY—
Will meet in front of the Library Mon
day at 1 o'clock to have its picture
taken.
TUESDAY
CAP AND GOWN will meet at 4:15 at
the home of Mrs. A. E. Roth.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Will the students who have kept the
first set of health cards and have failed
to turn them in please file them at once
at my office. MARY YOST,
Dean of Women.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Owing to the prevalence of influenza
on the campus, the Midwinter Y. W. C.
A. Conference announced for February
25 has been postponed until the first part
of April.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
The first quarter of Mathematics 3.
prerequisite one year of high school
mathematics, will be offered next quar
ter if enough students desire the course.
All who think they would like this
course fihould leave their names with
the Mathematics Department within the
next two or three days.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
March 4 is the closing date for wom
en's living groups to have their pictures
taken for the Quad. A large proportion
have already been taken, and it is neces
sary to clean up the rest next week in
order to close the living group section on
schedule time.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Notice la hereby given that there
will he a meeting of the Union Board
of the Stanford Union, at 4:15 p.m.,
Monday, February 27, 1922, at the
Union.
GEO. B. CULVER, Secretary.
