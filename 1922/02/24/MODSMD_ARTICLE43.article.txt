# Fifty Dollars Offered By English Department
## 
### 
An annual literary prize of $50 has
been created by the English department,
to be awarded for prose or verse. No
certain type of literature is stipulated in
the announcement, except that if prose is
given the prize one year poetry will be
awarded the next, and vice versa. De
tails of the offer are given in the Feb
ruary Cardinal, coming out Monday.
