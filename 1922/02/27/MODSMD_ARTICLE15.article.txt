# Lens and Plate to Hold Open Meeting on Tuesday
## 
### 
"Picture-Taking," is the subject of
Kenneth Ritchie's talk to be given at
the open meeting of Lens and Plate,
tomorrow night, at 7:30, in the Lens
and Plate Dark Room.
Ritchie will describe the different
methods of taking .photographs and
comment on the various shutters and
lenses now in use on the ordinary
camera.
