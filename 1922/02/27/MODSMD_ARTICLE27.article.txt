# SUGAR PLANT MANAGER TO EXPLAIN FILMS OF REFINING PROCESSES
## 
### 
E. B. Caster, assistant plant manager
of the California-Hawaiian Sugar Re
fining Corporation, has been secured
to lecture in connection with the films
showing the completed process of
sugar refining, to be exhibited Wed
nesday evening, in the Little Theatre.
These motion pictures will show the
process of preparing sugar for table
use, from the time the raw material
is delivered at the docks from Hawaii,
through to the bagging and packing
for shipment. The chemical labora
tory control work related to each step
is pictured. The quadruple vacuum
evaporators used in drying the sugar,
and the method of making lump and
granulated forms, are depicted as part
of the process.
