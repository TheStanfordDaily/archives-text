# ARMY CHIEF HERE TO INSPECT R.O.T.C.
## 
### 
Major General G. C. Morton, com
manding the Ninth Corps area of the
army, visited the campus this afternoon
for the purpose of inspecting the army
detachment here and to view the R. O. T.
C. Upon the result of this inspection
will hinge the rating of the local unit.
Except in number enrolled, the Stanford
Field Artillery has consistently headed
the list of all colleges maintaining similar
units throughout the country.
General Morton was received by the
officers of the military department, and
conferred with President Wilbur before
returning to San Francisco late this
afternoon.
