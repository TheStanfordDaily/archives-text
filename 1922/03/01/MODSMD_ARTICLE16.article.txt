# C.E. Walton to Address Renaissance Club Tonight

### 
Clarence Elton Walton will speak to
the Renaissance Club tonight on the

with special reference to his comedy,
philosophy and satire. The meeting is
to he held in the Women's Clubhouse at
7:30. Elton is at present doing graduate
