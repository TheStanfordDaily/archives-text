# SANTA CRUZ ALUMNI PLEDGE $4700 IN THE FIRST FOUR HOURS
## 
### 
Working from noon until 4 o'clock
yesterday, alumni endowment canvassers
in Santa Cruz secured $4700. One man
pledged himself to a contribution of
$2500. This was the record of the first
<!;iy the work was able to be carried on,
owing to the prevalence of influenza in
that region.
The business men's campaign in Palo
Alto will hold a second report meeting
tomorrow night, at which it is expected
that most of the 800 people selected will
have been canvassed. Over $30,000 lias
been pledged so far. Reports from other
districts are expected in by tomorrow,
when meetings of workers will be held
in the three sections.
