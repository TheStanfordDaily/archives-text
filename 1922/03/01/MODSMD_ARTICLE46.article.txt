# Nestoria to Debate on Industrial Court Plan
## 
### 
A debate on the question, "Resolved,
that California should have an industrial
court of appeals similar to that of Kan
sas," will constitute the main event of
Nestoria's meeting tonight at 7.30 in
room 12 of the Law Building. Speakers
on the affirmative are W. W. Davis and
O. C. Barby, opposing L. R. Avilla and
Zeimar Hawkins.
Plans will be made at this time for the
end-quarter banquet and election of of
ficers, which will be the next meeting,
to be held in two weeks.
