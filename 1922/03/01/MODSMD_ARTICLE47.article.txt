# Dormitory Men Asked to Make Reservations
## 
### 
All men living in Encina and Squoia
halls are requested to make their room
reservations for the ensuing quarter
in accordance with the following an
nouncement.
Men living in Sequoia Hall who
wish to reserve their present rooms
for the Spring Quarter, should do so
at the Dean of Men's office, on March
6th and 7th. The room drawing will
take place Wednesday, Marth Bth. at
7 o'clock p.m., at the Sequoia Hall
office.
Reservations of the present rooms
in Encina Hall, for the Spring Quar
ter should be made on March 13th,
14th, and 15th. The drawing will
take place Thursday, March 16th, at
7:30 p.m., at the Encina Hall office.
