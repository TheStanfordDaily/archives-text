Announcing
A Change of Ownership of the Music Shop
The Music Shop formerly lyiown as Ostrander's Music
Shop has been purchased from F. M. Ostrander and
Mrs. Ostrander, by C. E. Martin. As the new owner.
I wish to thank the people of Palo Alto for the patron
age that they have accorded this store. I hope to con
tinue to merit this business and for the convenience of
patrons, am making a few changes in the store and
making the policy of the store more liberal.
The Music Shop
