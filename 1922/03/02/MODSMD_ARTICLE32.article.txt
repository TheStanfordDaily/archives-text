# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## O.A.C. IS PLEASED WITH HAMMER AND COFFIN CHAPTER 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Ore., March 1. (P.I.
N.S.)—The fact that the O. A. C.
"Orange Owl" has been granted a
chapter in Hammer and Coffin society,
national organization for college
comics, ranks it among the leading
humorous publications of the nation.
