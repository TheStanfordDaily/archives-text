# POINT SYSTEM GIVES GIRLS CHANCE TO WIN SWEATER
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
Seattle, Wash., March 2. (P.I.N.S.)—
The point system operating in the
Women's Athletic Association gives
the girl with all-around but not stellar
ability a chance to win a "W" sweater.
Formerly the only method by which
a sweater could be won was by mak
ing four first teams, not more than
two being in one sport.
