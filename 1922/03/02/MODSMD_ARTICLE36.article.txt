# U.S.C. OPENS DOORS TO "NO TOBACCO" LEAGUE
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN
CALIFORNIA, LOS ANGELES, Cal.,
Mar. 2.— (P. I. N. S.) —According to the
No Tobacco Journal, U. S. C. is the first
great university to open its doors to the
meetings of the No Tobacco League;
also the first to offer a scholarship for
the winning oration on the subject of
"Tobacco." The third annual No To
bacco League convention convened at
the Trojan institution this year.
