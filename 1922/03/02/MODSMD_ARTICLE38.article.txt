# OREGON COMMENCES SPRING FOOTBALL PRACTICE SOON
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF OREGON, EU
GENE, Ore., Mar. 2.—(P. I. N. S.) —
Spring football training will start with
the third quarter, according to Head
Coach Shy Huntington. This year
Huntington intends not only to supervise
the work of the varsity squad but will
also handle several gym classes in rudi
mentary gridiron training, such as pass
ing and kicking.
