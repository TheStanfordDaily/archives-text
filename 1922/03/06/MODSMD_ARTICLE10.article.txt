# Funeral of President-Emeritus Branner Held From Residence Friday
## 
### 
The funeral of President-Emeritus
John Casper Branner was held at the
Branner residence, 13 Avlarado Row,
at two o'clock Friday afternoon. Dr.
D. Charles Gardner had charge of the
ceremony. The body was buried at
Cypress Lawn Cemetery. The pall
bearers were: Mr. J. F. Newsom,
Mr. J. P. Smith, Mr. C. F. Tolman, Jr.,
Mr. E. C. Franklin, Mr. E. B. Kimball,
and Dr. T. M. Williams.
