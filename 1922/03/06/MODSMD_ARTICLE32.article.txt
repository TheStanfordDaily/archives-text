ANNOUNCEMENT!
Warren D. Allen announces that
there will be no organ recital on Tues
day, March 7. 011 account of the Glee
Club Concert in San Francisco that,
evening. Also on Thursday, March fl, j
the regular recital has been post
poned 011 account of its being Found
ers' Day. That evening the San Fran
cisco Symphony will give a sacred
concert for the trustees, faculty, stu
dents and employees of the university,
in the Memorial Church.
