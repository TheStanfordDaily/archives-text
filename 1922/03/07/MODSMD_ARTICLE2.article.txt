# HERTZ ORCHESTRA TO GIVE CONCERT IN MEMORIAL CHAPEL
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony Or
chestra under the direction of Alfred
Hertz will give a concert in the Me
morial Church, at 8 o'clock on the
evening of Founders' Day. Admission
will be by ticket only, on account o£
the limited capacity of the Church.
The program will include the Sym
phony No. 2, in D major, by Brahms,
a Russian Easter Overture by Rimsky-
Korsakov, an air from D major suite
by Bach, and a symphonic poem.
"Death and Transfiguration," by
Strauss.
