# PROFESSOR HOOVER IS SPEAKER AT SMOKER
## 
### 
Over a hundred men were entertained
at the Geology and Mining Department
supper, which was given for Majors of
the department and Lower Division stu
dents who contemplate taking up Geol
ogy and Mining work, last night in the
Geology Building. The faculty of the
department were hosts at this occasion.
Professor Theodore Hoover outlined to
the new men the purpose and aims of
the six years' course in the department.
Professors John D. Hyde and Welton J.
Crook also spoke.
