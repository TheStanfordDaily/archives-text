# Dr. H.A. Lorentz to Speak Before Faculty Members
## 
### 
The distinguished physicist, Dr. H. A.
Lorentz, of the University of Leyden,
Holland, will speak at the Faculty Club
house. Friday evening, March 10, at 7:30
o'clock on "Sub-atomic Mechanics."
The regular meeting of the Faculty
Club has been called for this same eve
ning, members being asked to come
either at 7:30 or at 9 (or thereafter) ac
cording to choice.
