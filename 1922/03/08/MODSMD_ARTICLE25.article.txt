# MUSIC CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS FOR YEAR
## 
### 
At the election of officers held by
Music Club last night in the Women's
Clubhouse, the following were chosen:
President, Marjorie McDonald, '23;
vice president, Dorothy Grant, '24;
secretary, Meribeth Cameron, '25;
treasurer, Warren Lewis, '21. They
will hold office for the rest of this
year.
