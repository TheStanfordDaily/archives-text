SUNDAY
FIRESIDE DISCUSSION GROUP—
Meeting at *7:30 p.m. Election of
officers. Suggestions for improve
ment of discussions.
MONDAY
PRESS CLUB—WiII meet at 12:50 in
the Press Building.
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB—WiII meet
in the Women's Clubhouse, at 7.45
FACULTY WOMEN'S CLUB—WiII
meet Monday afternoon, at 4 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Tomorrow afternoon, the Stanford
team will play a return match with
Johnston and Griffin in San Fran
cisco. The bout will be played on the
California Club courts.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Miss Handy will hold office hours
in the office of the new Stanford
Union, daily, 8:30-10:30 a.m. Appoint
ments may be made for other hours.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Enrollment in the women's equi
tation classes will be conducted at
Roble Gymnasium, from 4 to 5 o'clock.
All senior and graduate women are
qualified to register.
