# Sequoia Holds Second Dance of the Quarter
## 
### 
Sequoia's second dance of the quar
ter was held last Friday night. The
hall was decorated with Stanford
banners and pennants in order to
give the clubrooms a collegiate at
mosphere. Palms and greenery were
also used for decorations.
Fifty couples enjoyed the dance.
Music was furnished by Grinstead's
orchestra.
