# STUNTS PRESENTED AT FRESHMAN JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
Freshmen, upperclassmen, roughs,
and snakes, men and women, all enter
ed into the spirit of the occasion and
helped to make the '25 jolly-up, Satur
day night, a success.
In addition to the dancing the fresh
men women and men each put on a
stunt. The girls presented ft skit
which was featured by Madame Fa
tima, "The psychic marvel." The
men's act consisted of a comic negro
monologue.
Refreshments consisting of ice
cream and cookies were served, as
well as punch.
