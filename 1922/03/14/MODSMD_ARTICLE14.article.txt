# Herbert Hoover to Be on Campus Two Days Next Week
## 
### 
Herbert Hoover will be on the cam
pus and in San Francisco March 24
and 25. He is now 011 a tour of Colo
rado, Utah, Wyoming, Arizona, New
Mexico, California, and Nevada, as
head of the commission which is hold
ing hearings 011 the Colorado river
water power and irrigation project.
His visit is to be unofficial, as there
is no hearing by the commission in
this region.
Hoover will spend one of his two
days in this district at his home 011
the campus. He is to lay a wreath 011
the grave of Dr. Branner, and will at
tend a meeting of the Board of Trus
tees. On March 25 he will be the
guest of honor at a dinner given by
San Francisco business men.
The commission will stop for three
days at Phoenix, Arizona, tomorrow,
and also in Los Angeles, March 20,
21, and 22. Following his stay here
Hoover will go with the other mem
bers of the commission to Salt Lake
City.
