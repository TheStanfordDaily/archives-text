# Applications Due For Research Fellowships
## 
### 
Application for the Bishop Museuh Fel
lowships in Anthropology, Botany, Zo
ology, Geology, or Geography must be
filed with the Dean of the Graduate School
of Yale University before April 1. 1922.
These fellowships, of the value of $I,(XK)
each, are open to both men and women
who have completed at least one year
of graduate study. Preference will be
given to candidates who have already
obtained their Doctorate and who have
demonstrated their ability to carry on
original research.
