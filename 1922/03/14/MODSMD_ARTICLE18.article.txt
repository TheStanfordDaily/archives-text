# FIFTY DOLLAR PRIZE TO BE AWARDED FOR BEST ORIGINAL POEM
## 
### 
The prize of fifty dollars offered by
tlie members of the English Department
will be awarded this year for the best
original poem submitted by an under
graduate member of the University.
Contestants should submit manu
scripts either by mail, addressed to the
Department of English, or by leaving
them with the secretary of the depart
ment in Room 207a, on or before the
10th day of May. They should be
signed by an assumed name and be ac
companied by a sealed envelope con
taining the assumed name and the real
name of the author.
Any contestant may submit two or
more manuscripts in competition.
The committee of judges will be in
structed to withhold the award in case
no manuscript is submitted which they
deem worthy of recognition.
