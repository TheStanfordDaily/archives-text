# LE
						HANE TO LEAD BEAR QUINTET NEXT SEASON
## 
### 
Louis F. Le Hane has been elected
captain of the 1923 California basket
ball team. Le Hane has played stand
ing guard on the Bear team for two
years and many experts rate him as
one of the best guards on the coast.
He registers from Berkeley, and will
be a senior next year.
