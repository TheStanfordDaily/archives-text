# Theta Sigma Phi to Sell Doughnuts For Benefit of Women's Pageant
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi's annual doughnut
sale will be conducted on April 12,
for the benefit of the women's spring
pageant, according to a decision made
at a meeting of the organization, yes
terday afternoon.
The doughnut sale has become a
time-honored custom of the journalistic
society. The recipe used has been
handed down from several generations
of women journalists on the campus
and is one of the prized possessions
of the organization.
All profits made from the sale will
be turned over to the Pageant com
mittee to be used in covering the ex
penses incidental to the production.
