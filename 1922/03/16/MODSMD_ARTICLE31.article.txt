# BATTERY B LEADS IN R.O.T.C. PISTOL SHOOT
## 
### 
Inter-battery shooting matches held
by the pistolmen of the R. O. T. C.
have been finished, Battery A lead
ing with a percentage of 56.39 against
the 51.39 scored by Battery B. The
battalion staff had a considerable
higher average, that of 63.83, raising
the general average of the battalion
to 54.36.
