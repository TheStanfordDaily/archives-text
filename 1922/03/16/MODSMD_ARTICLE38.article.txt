# RECORD AMOUNT OF MILK GIVEN BY IDAHO VIOLET
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF IDAHO. MOS
COW. Idaho, Mar. 17.—(P. I. N. S.) —
Idaho Violet, a Holstein cow owned by
the University of Idaho, has recently fin
ished a record which surpasses all others
in the state for all ages and breeds. Dur
ing the year she gave 21,379.3 pounds of
milk, and 1,805.9 pounds of butter fat.
