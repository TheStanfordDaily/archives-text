# Envelopes Must Be Left With Registrar For Grades
## 
### 
No study cards will be given out
by the Registrar's Office during the
spring vacation, according to an an
nouncement made today by Registrar
O. L. Elliott. Those who wish to re
ceive their grades during vacation
should leave self-addressed stamped
envelopes at the Registrar's window
before the close of this quarter.
Monday, April 3, will be registra
tion day for all students.
