# P.I.P.A. RADIO STATION RECEIVES FIRST MESSAGE
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON.
SEATTLE, Wash., Mar. 17.—(P. I. N.
S.) —The first message to be taken by
the radio station of the P. T. P. A. was
received from the University of Cali
fornia. It is the plas to supply coast
schools with detailed information in fu
ture athletic contests.
