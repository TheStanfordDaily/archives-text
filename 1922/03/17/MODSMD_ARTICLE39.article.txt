# HICKS HAVE HOP—BRAVE BONFIRE IN BEEF BARRACKS
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, CORVALLIS, Ore., Mar. 17.—
(P. I. N. S.) —Jazz jarred the peaceful
slumbers of the bovine beauties as it re
sounded through the O. T. C. beef barn
on the occasion of the annual haymow
dance.
Cowboys, rubes, and milkmaids were
dolled up in their best overalls and calico
gowns, gathered in the hayrack, and a
lunch of rustic variety was eaten around
a bonfire in the stock judging pavilion.
