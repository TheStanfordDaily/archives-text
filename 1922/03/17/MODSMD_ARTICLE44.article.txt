# SALE OF SWEETS TO RAISE FUNDS FOR WHITMAN ANNUAL
## 
### 
WHITMAN COL LEGE, WALLA
WALLA, Wash., Mar. 17.—(P. T. N. S.)
—The sale of homemade candy bars
every Friday following chapel is the
latest method found for raising funds
for the Whitman Junior Annual.
