# OARS RECEIVE INITIAL BATH IN CLASS REGATTA
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
SEATTLE, Wash., Mar. 17.—(P. I. N.
S.) — Shells will have their first wetting
here this afternoon, when classes meet
in the annual crew regatta. Predictions
are that the Juniors, having five men
from the varsity shell, will win. with the
Sophomores a close second.
