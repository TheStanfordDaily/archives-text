# O.A.C. ENGINEERS TO BURN SNOW AT EXHIBIT
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, CORVALLIS, Ore., Mar. 17.—
(P. I. N. S.) —Burning hose with the
running water, and running a steam en
gine without steam will be some of the
novel features at the mechanical exhibit
of the Engineering Show. The miners
will put on a saloon representing the
frontier days of '49, which will be prop
erly garnished with 45s and bad men.
