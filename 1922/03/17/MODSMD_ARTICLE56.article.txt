# Mrs. A.M. Espinosa Will Entertain at Alumnae Tea
## 
### 
Mrs. A. M. Espinosa will entertain
about forty friends at the last Alumnae
Tea of the quarter, which will be held
in the Women's Clubhouse, next Wed
nesday afternoon, from 4 to G. Miss
Ruth Seibird, who is visitor on the
campus and Dorothy Grant, '24, will
give a few vocal and piano numbers.
The hostesses of the tea will be:
Mrs. O. M. Johnston, Mrs. George
Parkinson, Mrs. Stanley Smith, Mrs.
A. M. Espinosa, Mrs. P. A. Martin,
Mers. Henry Stuart, Mrs. S. D. Town
ley, and Mrs. A. W. Meyer.
