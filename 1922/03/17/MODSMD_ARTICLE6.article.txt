# BRAWNIE BESSIES TAKE PLEASURE IN SWATTING THE PELLET
## 
### 
Due perhaps to recent publicity in the
Daily Palo Alto, the old faculty golf
course has lately been enlivened hy a
large number of gentlemen plodding
from hence to whence across the kopjes
in quest of the elusive elastic. All rec
ords were mangled recently, however,
when three and entrancing and brawny
500's bore a professional-looking outfit
of clubs out the Country Road. Beset
with curiosity, a journalist followed in
his subtle way, and beheld from ambush
one earnest young lady swing diligently
(but fruitlessly) at the gobbet for five
minutes, and then crash out a screaming
drive ten feet down the fairway. At this
crisis, being in the nature of a golf-bug
himself, he broke down under the strain,
so the medal score was not recorded.
Flapper golf team this way; come right
ahead, girls who said knickers?
