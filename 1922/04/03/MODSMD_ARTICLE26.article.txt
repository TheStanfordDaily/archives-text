# JAPANESE QUINTET VICTORIOUS IN SOUTH
## 
### 
Stanford's all-Japanese basketball
squad, one of the eight Card athletic
teams to make the trip to southern Cali
fornia the past week, went on a rampage
last Tuesday night, winning from the
Mikados of the University of Southern
California in the Trojan pavilion by a
score of 43-3. Fred Koba, as in pre
vious tilts, was the star flash of this game.
He was successful in making 20 of the
Cards' points.
