# EUPHRONIANS TO HOLD DEBATE TRYOUTS SOON
## 
### 
A team of three men will be chosen
for the coming debate with the Senate
Society of California, at the Euphronia
Society meeting Thursday, at 7:30.
The question for debate, as decided
upon by the two societies, is "Re
solved, That the United States should
recognize the defacto government of
Mexico." Tryouts for places on the
team will consist merely of an inform
al discussion of the affirmative side
of the question, and speeches will be
limited to five minutes in length.
