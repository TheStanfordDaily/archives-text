# Registration Dance Held Tonight By English Club
## 
### 
The English Club will hold a Registra
tion Dance in the Women's Clubhouse
tonight at 9 o'clock. Admission is $1.10.
Hackett's orchestra will play.
During the spring vacation the dance
floor of the clubhouse has been repaired
and rcfinished. and it is now in excellent
condition for dances.
