# University Trucks Will Be Given Old-Fashioned Endurance Test Saturday
## 
### 
Skull and Snakes will inaugurate a
new custom next Saturday, besides re
viving the old-time Jolly-U# at the pa
vilion. Replacing and obliterating the
old-fashioned taxicab. reinforced and up
holstered trucks, built for service, will
be used to escort sorority and Koble
women to the front. The affair will be
conducted under the auspices of Mose
Grinstead's six-piece syncopation. All
prominent members of the society will
be in the receiving line at the box office,
and will deal in an old-fashioned man
ner with any who try to enter in other
than campus clothes.
