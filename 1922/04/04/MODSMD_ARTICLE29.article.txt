# Dr. Lanz Plays at Musicale
## 
### 
Dr. Henry Lanz of the Slavic depart
ment participated in a musicale held yes
terday afternoon at the Palo Alto Com
munity House. Dr. Lanz was at the
piano, and. besides acting as accompanist
to Mrs. J. W. Mansfield, soprano, he
played the third movement from Bee 4
thoven's Sonata. Opus 57, and Rachman
inoff's Prelude in C sharp minor.
