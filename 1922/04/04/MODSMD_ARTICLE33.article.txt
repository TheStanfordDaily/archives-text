# Alumnae Tea to be Given Wednesday
## 
### 
The following women will act as
hostesses at the first resident Alumnae
Tea of this quarter, which will he
given in the Women's Clubhouse, Wed
nesday afternoon, from 4 until 6 p.m.:
Mesdames A. E. Peck. William Darsie,
Jerome Thomas, Jacob Kreps, A. W.
Johnson, Laura Pinney, Bailey Willis,
and John Jago.
