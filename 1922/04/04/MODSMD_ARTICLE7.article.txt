# PROFESSOR TREAT TO SPEAK AT ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
Professor Payson J. Treat, of the
History Department, will be the speak
er at the first student assembly of
the quarter at 9 o'clock next Thurs
day morning.
Professor Treat lias just returned to
Stanford from a seven months' trip
through the Orient and will speak next
Thursday particularly of his observa
tions in Japan, where he met states
men, university people and others.
