Shirley Charles, '23,
Announces Engagement
Shirley Charles, '23, has recently
announced her engagement to John
Ralph Nichols, son of Mr. and Mrs.
Walter Nichols of Palo Alto. Nichols
attended Stanford before going Over
seas with the A. E. F., but will gradu
ate at the Oregon Agricultural Col
lege this spring. The home of Miss
Charles is also in Palo Alto.
No date has yet been set for the
wedding.
