JUNIOR OPERA—
Campus talent interested in writing
Lyrics or composing music for the
Junior Opera will meet at the Kappa
Alpha Theta house, tomorrow after
noon, at 3 o'clock.
The Junior Opera committee and
Les Seib, director of the production,
will be present to discuss phases of
this part of the dramatic work. Seib
desires all those having contributions
to bring them to the meeting.
