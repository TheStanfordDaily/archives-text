# One-Tenth of U.C. Students Vote For Self Government
## 
### 
With little more than one-tenth of the
student body voting, University of Cali
fornia undergraduates adopted student
self-government in an election yesterday.
The total count showed 1026 votes, out
of 9000 or more students registered, the
measure passing by a majority of 963
to 63.
A mass meeting of students was called
last Thursday to discuss the new con
stitution, at which 61 were present.
