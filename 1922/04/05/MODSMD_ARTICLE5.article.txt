# SHERMUND ELECTED CAPTAIN OF FIRSTYEAR TRACK SQUAD
## 
### 
Ralph Shermund has been elected
to captain the babe track team. He
is one of the most consistent point
gainers on the freshman squad. Re
cently he made twelve and one-fourth
points in a meet against the com
bined schools of the Peninsula. He
is from Lowell High in San Francisco
where he has been running sprints
for several years.* The 100 and 220 are
his events primarily but he is look
ing good for a place in the quarter.
Shermund is a member of Kappa
Alpha.
