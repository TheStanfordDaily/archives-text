# Inter-Society Jolly-up To Be Held in Palo Alto
## 
### 
An intcr-society jolly-up, under the
auspices of the executive council of the
young people's societies of Palo Alto,
will be held Friday night at 7:30 o'clock
at the First Presbyterian Church. Part
of the evening will be devoted to a pro
gram. and the remainder to a general
good time. Admission is fifty cents.
