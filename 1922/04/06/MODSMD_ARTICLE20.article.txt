# NEW YELL LEADERS
## 
### 
Slips and falls marked the attempts of the new
yell princes to put over their stuff at the meet
yesterday. Some fine comedy was inadvertently
furnished the bleachers and everyone had a good
time laughing at the aspirants' efforts. But we
have something new with all this competition in yell
business. At other universities much is made of
the job of yell king.
Here the position has languished for some years
and only in the last couple has there been an honest
try to bring it back into its own. It is something
that means more than mere cheer direction at games.
The yell leader is one of the important figures in
campus life if he wishes it so. Close competition
will bring out the man for the job who will be able
to handle it in the shape that it merits.
