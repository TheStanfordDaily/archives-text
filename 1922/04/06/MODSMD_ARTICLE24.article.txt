# JOURNALISTS OUTLINE PROGRAM THIS EVENING
## 
### 
The part which the Stanford chapter
of the American Journalists' Association
will take in the press conference to be
held here April 17, 18, and 19, the pro
gram for the quarter, membership, and
other masters of importance will be
taken up at the first meeting of the chap
ter this evening.
The meeting will be held at 7 o'clock
in the Alpha Sigma Phi house and will
be brief in order that the members may
attend the spring sports rally.
