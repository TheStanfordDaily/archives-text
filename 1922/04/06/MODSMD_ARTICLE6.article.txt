# DICK CRANDALL CHOSEN BASKETBALL MANAGER
## 
### 
Dick Crandall, '23, has been selected
as next year's basketball manager.
This appointment was made under the
new competitive system of chosing
all athletic managers. Crandall is a
member of Phi Kappa Sigma. Three
sophomores, Robert Playter, Hugh
Wallace, and D. C. Rowland, were
chosen as assistants. Tryouts were
conducted during the entire basket
ball season and the selection was
made by W. D. Fletcher, Manager
Ernie Wilson, Coach Van Gent, and
Captain Davies
