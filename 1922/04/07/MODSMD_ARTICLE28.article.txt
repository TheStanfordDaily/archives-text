# NOTED VIOLINISTS TO APPEAR IN PALM SUNDAY PROGRAM
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. Samuel Savannah, vio
linists, will assist Warren 1). Allen in
the opening recital of the spring quar
ter to be given Palm Sunday, April 9,
at 4 o'clock. Mr. Savannah, who
teaches in San Francisco and Palo Alto,
and has appeared at a number of con
certs here before, will play solos, and
both artists will appear in works for
two violins with the organ. Composi
tions by Bach, Mendelssohn, and
Rimsky-Korsakov will compose the pro
gram for Palm Sunday.
