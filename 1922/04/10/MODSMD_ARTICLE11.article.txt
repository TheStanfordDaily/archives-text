# UNION WILL OPEN NEW DINING FACILITIES
## 
### 
Eating facilities for 108 additional stu
dents will be available tomorrow morn
ing when the large dining room of the
new Stanford Union is to be opened to
the public. This room will take care of
all transient trade and will be run on an
a la carte and table d'hote basis. Meal
hours are as follows: Morning, 7-9;
noon, 11:45-1; evening, 5:45-6:45.
Miss Handy states that the price for
board for club members has been re
duced from $90 a quarter to a strict $1
a day basis, although the method of pay
ment will remain the same. Those men
entering late will have their board bills
reduced accordingly.
