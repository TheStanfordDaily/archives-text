# DESERVING OF ANOTHER
## 
### 
Skull and Snakes put over its jolly-up on Friday
night iu a manner that left nothing to be desired.
Much work was put on the dance by members of
the society and they are to be credited for thpir ef
fort to revive old times. The plan of having the
trucks to call for the women was iu keeping with
the spirit of the evening and carried but the theme
of the dance. More such parties will be good for
us all.
