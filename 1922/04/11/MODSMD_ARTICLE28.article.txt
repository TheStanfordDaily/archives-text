# Entire Cast of Pageant to Hold First Practice
## 
### 
The entire cast of the women's
spring pageant will rehearse for the
first time Thursday afternoon, at 4:30
in Roble gymnasium. Dates and hours
for future rehearsals will be decided
upon at this meeting. The general
outline of the pageant will be pre
sented and a short rehearsal will be
held.
The practices for dances will con
tinue as scheduled. There will be
only five more rehearsals for each of
the dances.
