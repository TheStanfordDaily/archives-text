# CARNIVAL OFFICES TO OPEN IN OLD UNION
## 
### 
Offices for the 1922 Carnival will be
opened tomorrow in the clubroom of
the V. M. C. A. in the old Union, and
will lie under the supervision of Paul
Davis, chairman of the financial commit
tee. Office hours will be from 1 to
2 o'clock every day cxccpt I luirsday,
when there will lie someone on hand
from 11 to 12.
C. V. Smith, '19. will take charge of
securing supplies, as purchasing agent.
Helen Whitney, '23, will he financial
secretary ,and will attend to all requisi
tions.
