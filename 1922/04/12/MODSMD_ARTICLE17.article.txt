# YEARLY MEETING OF Y.M.C.A. MEMBERS TO BE ON THURSDAY
## 
### 
The yearly meeting of all student
members of the Stanford Y. M. C. A.
will he held Thursday night at the
Union, beginning at 7 :30.
At this meeting elections for officers
for next-year will he held. The entire
group of offices, from the president
down, will have to he filled by electing
new men or re-electing the present oc
cupants.
In addition, there will be given at
this time special reports by the various
cabinet members and chairmen, dealing
with their phases of the "Y" work
throughout the present college year.
These reports arc to be made out in
written form and kept on tile as per
manent records.
