# STANFORD TO MEET O.A.C. AT POLO
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE April 12. (P. I. N. S.)—O.
A. C. will open the polo season by
playing Stanford some time in May,
on the Stanford fiield. The college
team consisting of six men lias not
yet been chosen.
