TEACHERS!

April, Mr. W. W. Marquardt, Philip
pine educational agent, will call at
Stanford University to interview per
sonally anyone at Stanford who might
be interested in teaching in the Philip
pine Islands. Full details regarding
opportunities there can be obtained
at the Appointment Office. Those in
terested should notify the Appoint
ment Secretary immediately so that
arrangements can be made for them
to meet Mr. Marquardt while he is
here.
