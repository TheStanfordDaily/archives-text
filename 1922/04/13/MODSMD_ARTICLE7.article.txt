# Change in Dining Schedule at New Union Announced
## 
### 
Miss Etta Handy, director of the new
Union, announces tliat breakfast hours
for the transients' dining room will here
after he from 7 td 8:30, instead of from
7 to 9, as formerly. The change has
been made necessary because of the lack
of student waiters for the morning shift.
According to Miss Handy, the a la carte
system at the Union has been removed
entirely for a week or so, and table
d'hote service is all that can be obtained
at the present time. Both plans will he
permanently established, however, when
further supplies have been received.
