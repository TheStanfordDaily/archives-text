# PREPAID FOR SURE
## 
### 
Support in a very material form was given by
members of the administrative body of the University
to the Firemen's night fund. Money was needed to
day to defray certain expenses that have arisen in
connection with transportation, and advertising the
show. Marshal Ed Lamb took a slip around and had
no trouble in signing up the dwellers of the campus
for 110 mean sum. These people realize the benefit
of an active tire force and have not been slow to show
their appreciation in good hard iron men.
