# FRATERNITY RUSHING CLOSES SATURDAY
## 
### 
Formal rushing of freshmen by fra
ternities will close at midnight, Sat
urday evening. From that time until
after the freshmen consult with the
Interfraternity Conference lawyer on
Monday, there will be a closed period
during which no fraternity may have
any communication with them. After
the consultation on Monday, there
will be informal rushing and bidding.
Bids are to be turned into Heaton
Wrenn, president of the Conference,
at the Phi Delta house, by Saturday
noon.
