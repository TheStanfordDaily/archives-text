# VISITORS WELCOME
##

The Western Intercollegiate Press Association
extends an invitation to all who are interested in
journalism to attend its meetings during the conven
tion here. On account of the limited space available
only the active members of the staffs of the publica
tions can be admitted to the banquet and dance. In
vitations to the banquet tonight and to the dance
tomorrow night can be secured from he heads of
the various journalistic organizations or from any
member of (Ik; convention committee.
