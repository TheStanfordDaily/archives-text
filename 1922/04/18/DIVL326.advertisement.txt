There are undoubtedly a number of men in
this year's graduating class, whose character,
ability and personality give promise of a suc
cessful career in the investment banking busi
ness, should they be given opportunity of gaining
the requisite experience.
It is the purpose of Blyth, Witter & Co. to
receive applications from those desirous of such
a career and to select three or four applicants for
special training and for positions in our organ
ization.
1 112 you wish to start your business career under
our supervision, write us giving in detail all in
formation necessary for a true appraisal of your
ability. Information will be held confidential.
No applications by telephone or personal call
will be considered.
