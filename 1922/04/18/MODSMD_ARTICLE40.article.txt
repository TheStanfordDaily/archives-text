# Y.W.C.A. WILL HEAR MISS ETHEL HIGGINS
## 
### 
Miss Ethel Higgins will speak at the
first Y. W. C. A. meeting of the quar
ter Thursday afternoon at 5 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Miss Higgins
is assistant superintendent of the Pres
byterian Home for Chinese Girls in San
Francisco and will describe her work
to the Y. W. C. A. She spoke at a
meeting on the campus five years ago.
Miss Donaldine Cameron, superintend
ent of the Home for Chinese Girls in
San Francisco, was scheduled to speak
to the local association, but an unex
pected trip to the East made it impos
sible for her to come.
The World Fellowship Committee 011
the Y. W. C. A. cabin has charge of
the meeting. Maria Anderson, '25, will
sing; Marjorie Mac Donald, '22, will ac
company her; and Meribeth Cameron,
'25, will play a violin obligata. Refresh
ments will be served.
