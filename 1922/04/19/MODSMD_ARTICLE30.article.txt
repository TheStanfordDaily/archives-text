# Martin d'Andrea Elected Member of Music Club
## 
### 
Martin d'Andrea, '25, was elected as
a new member to the Music Club, which
met Tuesday evening in the Women's
Clubhouse for the purpose of holding
trvouts for the present quarter. A short
program was presented, in which Charles
Foye, '25, played several numbers on
the violin: accompanied by Maria An
derson, '25.
