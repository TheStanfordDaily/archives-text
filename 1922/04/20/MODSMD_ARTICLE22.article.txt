# THE
						WORTH OF A ROUGH
## 
### 
Evidently the price of roughs is not six hits. A
few ardent supporters got out to the firemen's benefit
last night, hut the showing was small. The men
who worked hard to make the thing a success deserve
credit, however, for their efforts were strenuous and
devoted to a cause that is of the most vital interest to
all of us.
