# INTRAMURAL BASEBALL PROGRAM POSTPONED
## 
### 
On account of the match between
Stanford 1925 and the California Presli
Men, the intramural baseball program
for Thursday afternoon was called off,
and will be played later. Friday is al
ways an off day, and the series will lie
resumed Monday with games between
Delta Chi and Alpha Sigma Phi, Encina
Two meets Beta I beta Pi, El Toro plays
Alpha Delta Phi, Kappa Sigma opposes
Theta Chi, and Delta Tau Delta tangles
with El Canipo.
