# A 						TRIBUTE TO WALT
## 
### 
Walt Christie had a Dodge auto presented to him
at the track on Saturday as a token of the regard in
which the alumni hold the old mentor. An alumnus
made a fine speech on how much the alumni loved
\V alt and how they appreciated the work that he had
done in making victories for California on the track.
When Christie answered he said simply that the relay
was yet to come and he had his mind too full of that
to think of anything else.
It is this devotion to his sport that has won for
Walt so many meets. He is one of the honorable
figures of the athletic world. We hand him much
and are glad to see such worth-while support by an
alumni body of a man who merits all praise that he
gets as does Walt Christie.
