# FRESHMEN INJURED IN AUTO CRASH WITH CAR
## 
### 
George Rawlins and John Hunt, fresh
men, were slightly injured last Friday
evening when their automobile collided
with a street car near the service station
at the junction of the State Highway
and University Avenue and Palm Drive.
Both were cut by broken glass, and
Hunt's back was wrenched. They were
taken to the Palo Alto Hospital for treat
ment.
