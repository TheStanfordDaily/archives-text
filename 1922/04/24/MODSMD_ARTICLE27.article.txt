# SENATE SOCIETY WILL DEBATE EUPHRONIANS
## 
### 
The championship of the Intersociety
Debating League will be decided when
Euphronia meets Senate Society of Cali
fornia next Wednesday evening. Eu
phronia will uphold the affirmative side of
the question, "Resolved, That the United
States should recognize the de facto gov
ernment of Mexico." Mathew Tobriner,
1 24, Edward Landels, '22, and William
Bielefeldt, '25, will represent Euphronia.
Judges have not as yet been chosen.
