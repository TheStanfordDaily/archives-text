# Hist! Hostile Huskies Hysterically Hasten to Hoist Hefty Horse-Shoes
## 
### 
At last! Village blacksmiths have
their glittering opportunity to throw
something besides bull. Shining golden
horseshoes await the two meanest wield
ers of the equine slipper, the livery
stable sneaker, and the jackass oxford.
The world's champion horseshoe slinger
of Stanford- will he crowned (gently)
with a golden mule-kick. The horse
shoe trust announces that no paper
ishoes may the thrown, and the S. P. C. A.
adds that it is not necessary to kill
horses in order to get the cavalry
vFlorsheims.
It is possible that a Feminine Fling
,'may be staged, using Shetland pony
pumps. Hay-racks will be sent to bring
'the Five Hundred.
The University horseshoe tournament
at Encina Gymnasium is off to a start
next week, and matches in the first round
must be held before May 6.
