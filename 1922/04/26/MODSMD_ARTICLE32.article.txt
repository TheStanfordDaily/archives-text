# BERKELEY MAYOR WILL SPEAK HERE THURSDAY
## 
### 
"How California Can Be Made as Ef
ficient as Palo Alto in Its Public Utili
ties" is the subject of a lecture to be
given by Louis Bartlett, mayor of Berke
ley, in Room 12 of the Law Building,
at 7:30 o'clock, tomorrow.
Bartlett. who is president of the
League of California Municipalities, is
credited with bringing the next conven
tion of this organization to Stanford.
He is a member of the State Water and
Power Act Campaign Committee.
The meeting is under the auspices of
Euphronia Debating Society. The pub
lic is invited to attend.
Professor A. M. Cathcart, mayor of
Palo Alto, will preside.
