# DEBATE TRYOUTS TO BE HELD ON MAY 3
## 
### 
Tryouts for the freshman-sophomore
debate which will take place next month
will be held in the Little Theater on
Wednesday, May 3. The subject is,
"Resolved, That the coal mines of the
United States should be nationalized."
Tryouts will consist of a direct speech
for five minutes, and a three-minute re
buttal.
Freshmen and sophomores who in
tend to try out are asked to sign up in
Debating Coach Emerson' office, Room
2038 of the English Building. Women,
as well as men, are eligible to compete.
