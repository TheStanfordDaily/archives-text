# INTRAMURAL NINES RESUME SCHEDULES
## 
### 
After a lapse in the schedule of two
clays, during which only a few chal
lenge games were played, intramural
baseball is resumed today, with the
opening of the third round. Sequoia No.
One meets Delta Chi, Encina No. Two
tangles with Breakers, Sequoia No. Two
plays El Toro, Alpha Tau Omega mixes
with Kappa Sigma, and Delta Kappa
lipsilon crosses bats with Delta Tau
Delta.
At the end of the second round, all
teams which entered the competition arc
still in the running, and there have been
no failures. The brand of baseball
served up by the intramural is
improving with each succeeding round.
