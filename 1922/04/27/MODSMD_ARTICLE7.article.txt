# Paddock Not To Run In Eastern Track Carnivals
## 
### 
Charles Paddock, world's champion
sprinter, from the University of South
ern California, will not compete in the
i. C. 4-A. or any other meet in the East
this year. It was only recently an
nounced that Paddock would enter in
the Pennsylvania relay carnival and
other events. Hugh Hirshon, president
of an Eastern athletic association, on
sending an invitation to Paddock, re
ceived this reply: "Paddock will not
come East to compete in any games.
Final!"
