# WORLD FELLOWSHIP MEETING IS TO BE HELD SUNDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
A World Fellowship meeting, under
the joint auspiccs of the young people's
societies of Palo Alto and the Stanford
Y. M. 6. A., will he held next Sunday
evening, from 5:30 to 7:30 o'clock, in
the Palo Alto Congregational Church, at
Hamilton and Waverley streets.
According to the committee in charge,
the purpose of this meeting is to acquaint
the American students and the students
from foreign lands with one another.
Seven nationalities will take part in the
evening's program.
