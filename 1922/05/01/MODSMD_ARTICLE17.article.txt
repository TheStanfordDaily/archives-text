# BAR ASSOCIATION TO AWARD ESSAY PRIZES
## 
### 
The Public Welfare Bureau of the
California Bar Association has offered
prizes of $250 and $100 for the first
and second best papers on the Sample
Bill, written by law students in this
state.
The competition is restricted to those
students who have not received their
law degrees prior to May, 1922. The
maximum length of any paper submitted
has been set at 6.000 words, but there
is no restriction as to brevity. Manu
scripts must be submitted not later than
August 22, 1922.
