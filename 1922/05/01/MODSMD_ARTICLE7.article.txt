# FRESHMAN NET TEAM DEFEATS BERKELEY
## 
### 
The Stanford Freshman tennis team
won six out of the seven matches from
the Berkeley High School squad, Sat
urday morning, on the varsity courts.
The babe net men played a much bet
ter brand of tennis than they have dis
played any time this season.
The first singles was the only match
which the prep school players were able
to take. Chandler winning from Holmes,
6-1, 6-1.
Archie Logan won a hard-fought
match from Hanscom of Berkeley. Lo
gan took the first set, 6-4, but the high
school lad rallied in the second set and
won, 6-4. Logan came back strong and
took the last set, 6-3.
The most interesting doubles match
was played between Holmes and Willis
of Stanford, and Chandler and Hans
com of Berkeley. Stanford won in
three sets, 7-5, 4-6, 6-3.
The results are as follows:
SINGLES
Chandler (B.), defeated Holmes (S.),
6-1, 6-1; Logan (S.) defeated Holmes
(B.), 6-4, 4-6, 6-3; Overfelt (S.) de
feated Rhodes (B.), 6-2, 3-6, 6-2; Willis
(S.) defeated Graham (B.), 6-2, 6-2;
Milliken (S.) defeated Grettin (B.), 6-4,
6-4.
DOUBLES
Holmes and Willis (S.) defeated
Chandler and Hanscom (B.), 7-5, 4-6.
6-3; Logan and Overfelt (S.) defeated
Graham and Rhodes (B.), 6-4, 6-1.
