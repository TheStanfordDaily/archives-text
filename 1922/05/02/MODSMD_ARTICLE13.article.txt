# Civil Engineers To Hear H.J. Ryan on Water Power
## 
### 
"Possibilities for the development
of water power on the Pacific Coast,"
will be the subject of a talk by Pro
fessor Harris J. Ryan, head of the
electrical engineering department, to
the members of the Civil Engineering
Society, tomorrow evening, in Room
283 of the Engineering Building. Pro
fessor Ryan will demonstrate the im
portant part that the engineer must
play in developing the water resources
of the coast states. The lecture will
be illustrated with lantern views.
Following the speech a short busi
ness meeting will be held at the Phi
Delta Theta fraternity house.
