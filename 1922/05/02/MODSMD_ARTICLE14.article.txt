# TWELVE SOCCER MEN ARE AWARDED MEDALS
## 
### 
Twelve members of the 1922 soccer
varsity have been awarded individual
gold medals by the California Soccer
Football Association. The awarding
of the medals came as the result of
Coach Harry Maloney's men winning
the championship of the University
and Club Soccer League this year,
this being the third consecutive year
that a Stafford team has been given
these trophies. In the near future a
medal will be given to the man who
has rendered the varsity the most valu
able service during the past season.
The men who received the awards are
Captain Gorman, Dwiglit, Franklin,
Elmore, Walthers, Hayes, Masters,
Mendonca, Robinson, Peavy, G. Clark,
and Taylor.
