# MUSIC CLUB WILL PRESENT CONCERT PROGRAM TONIGHT
## 
### 
The annual concert of the Stanford
Music Club will be presented tonight
in the Little Theater, at 7:30. This
concert takes the place of the Tuesday
night lecture. The program will be
as follows:
Violin solo— Uliss Meribeth Cameron:
a. Romanza Rubinstein
b. Playera Sarasatti
Vocal solo —Glenn Russ:
a. 11 mio dolce ardor Gluck
b. Vale ..„ Kennedy
Piano solo —Miss Marjorie MacDouald:
a. Prelude —E minor..Mendelssohn
b. Rhapsodie —B minor Brahms
Vocal solo —Martin D'Andrea:
Selected
Violin solo —Charles Foye:
a. The Old Refrain Kreisler
b. Serenade Espagnole
Chaminade-Kreisler
Piano solo —Richard Malaby:
a. Scherzo Carl Preyer
b. May Night Palmgren
c. Country Gardens
Percy Grainger
