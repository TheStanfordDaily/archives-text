# NESTORIANS TO HOLD MID-QUARTER BANQUET
## 
### 
Nestoria Debating Society will hold its
regular mid-quarter banquet in the new
Union tomorrow night at 6 o'clock. This
affair is a preliminary to the initiations
which will be held the latter'part of this
week.
Speeches will be heard from older
members of the society and President E.
K. Baum requests that all Nestorians be
present.
