# Botsford Asks That Net Matches Be Played Soon
## 
### 
Play in the spring intramural ten
nis tournament is progressing slowly.
C. S. Botsford, director of intramural
athletics, asks that the matches in the
first round be played off as soon as
possible in order that the second round
can be started.
