# ROSA PONSELLE MAY TOUR PACIFIC SLOPE
## 
### 
Miss Rosa Ponselle, the first prima
donna of the Metropolitan Company to
be horn and educated in the United
States, is contemplating an extended
tour of the Pacific slope next season.
Miss Ponselle rose in a short time
from the vaudeville stage to the Metro
politan Opera House stage, and her voice
has provoked favorable comment from
leading critics in New York and else
where. Next year will be her fifth con
secutive season on the opera stage under
the direction of Mr. Gatti-Casazza. Miss
Ponselle's proposed transcontinental tour
is to begin at the close of the 1922 opi-ra
season. .
