# Palo Alto to Send Four Men to State Track Meet
## 
### 
Four athletes will be sent from the
Palo Alto Union High School to the
eighth annual California Federation
Track and Field Meet to be held at
Stockton next Saturday. Forty-four
prep schools in all parts of the state
will be represented.
In the Peninsula Athletic League meet
held on the Stanford oval last Saturday,
Palo Alto took third with a sum total
of 25 points, San Jose and San Mateo
having placed first and second, respect
ively.
