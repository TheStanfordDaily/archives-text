# Kirk Elected President Stanford Montana Club
## 
### 
H. M. Kirk, '24, was clected president
of the Stanford Montana Club at a
meeting held last night. Other officers
elected were: P. E. Hoffman, '25, vice
president; Sam Mendenhall, '24, record
ing secretary; H. S. Thompson, '24,
treasurer; R. E. Booth, secretary.
Plans were discussed for a banquet to
be given the last of this month or early
next month, at which time it is hoped
to have J. E. Richards, former governor
of Montana, speak to the club.
