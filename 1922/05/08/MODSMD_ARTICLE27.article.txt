# FEDERAL STUDENTS TO MEET TUESDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
Elmer Shirrell, chief of rehabilitation
for the twelfth district, and Harry Ma
loney, director of minor sports at Stan
ford, will be the chief speakers at a
meeting of the Associated Federal Stu
dents to be held tomorrow night at 7 :30
in the Women's Clubhouse. Music and
refreshments will complete the program.
All Federal Board students, and par
ticularly those who have come to Stan
ford for the first time this quarter, are
requested to be present at this first get
together of the quarter.
