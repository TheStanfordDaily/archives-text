# GLEE CLUB WILL GIVE CONCERT AT LOS GATOS
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club of forty
voices, under the direction of Warren
D. Allen, will give a choral concert
in the motion picture house at Los
Gatos, tomorrow night. The selections
to be given, comprise practically all
of the numbers sung recently at con
certs in San Jose and San Francisco.
Marsden Argall, '23, baritone, and Mar
shall Monroe, '23, tenor, will be the
soloists for the concert.
AM Glee Club men have been re
quested to meet at the car stop by
Greene's, Wednesday evening at 7. A
special car will carry the men to Los
Gatos.
