# OFFICERS ARE CHOSEN BY DELTA SIGMA RHO
## 
### 
Knox Miller, '23, and James Anglim,
'23, were elected last night president and
vice-president, respectively, of Delta
Sigma Rho, national debating fraternity.
Both men have done considerable
forensic work at Stanford. Miller was
the winner of the recent Joffre debate
with California, and was a member of
the team that met California last fall in
the annual debate. Anglim is also a
varsity debater.
Plans for a smoker in the near future
were discussed at last night's meeting.
