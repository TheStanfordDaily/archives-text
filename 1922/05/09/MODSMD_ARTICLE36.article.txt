# STUDENTS MAY ENTER THREE PLAYS A YEAR
## 
### 
By a recent action of the Dramatic
Council, students may now participate in
three plays during the regular academic
year. Participation in two of these plays
may be included in one quarter.
After being passed upon by the Dra
matic Council, this new ruling was sanc
tioned by the Deans of Men and Women
as well as by the Academic Council.
This ruling is applicable this year. Stu
dents who have already participated in
two plays this year may try out for the
Senior Farce.
