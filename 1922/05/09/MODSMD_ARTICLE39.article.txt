# STUDENT VOLUNTEERS ENTERTAIN VISITORS
## 
### 
The Stanford Student Volunteers were
the hosts Saturday to Volunteers from
San Jose Teachers' College and the Col
lege of the Pacific.
At the meeting in the afternoon Har
old Clark of San Anselmo Seminary
and Gladys Howard of the University of
California, president and vice-president,
respectively, of the Student Volunteer
Union of Northern California, gave
short addresses. Clark and Miss How
ard have recently returned from the na
tional convention of Student Volunteers
held at Yonkers, N. Y. Following the
meeting a tour of the campus was made.
A picnic was held in the evening.
