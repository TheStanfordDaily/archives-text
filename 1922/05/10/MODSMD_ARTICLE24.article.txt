# REPRESENTATIVES TO ATTEND CONFERENCE
## 
### 
Miss Helen Masters Bunting, faculty
representative of the Women's Athletic
Association, Carey Bailard, '22, acting
president, and Eunice Biddle, '24, presi
dent-elect of the Association, left today
to attend a conference of the Western
Section of the Athletic Conference of
American College Women which will
open May 12 at the .University of Ore
gon at Eugene.
Miss Bunting will meet with other
physical education groups which are hav
ing sessions at Eugene this week. She
is a member of the National Women's
Basketball Committee of the American
Physical Education Association. The
Western Society of College Directors of
Physical Education for Women, an or
ganization of which she is secretary and
treasurer, will meet at this time also.
