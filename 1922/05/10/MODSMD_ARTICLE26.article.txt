# GLEE CLUB WILL GIVE CONCERT AT LOS GATOS
## 
### 
Glee Club men are giving a concert to
night at Los Gatos, the first since the
successful spring tour. All members
will leave on a special car which will
start at 6 o'clock from the Stanford
Bookstore.
According to present plans concerts
may be given on Alumni Day and at
Lane Hospital in San Francisco before
the end of the quarter.
