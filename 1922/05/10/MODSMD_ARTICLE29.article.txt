# ALUMNI WILL REVIEW MILITARY IN ACTION
## 
### 
As part of the ceremonies of Alumni
Day on May 20. the Stanford R. O. T. C.
unit will maneuver before President Wil
bur, the faculty and the alumni at 9:30
a. m. Commissions as second lieutenants
in the United States Army, Reserve
Corps, will be awarded at that time to
the fifteen men who have qualified, by
President Wilbur, acting for the Presi
dent of the United States. The review
will be followed by a four-period polo
match.
