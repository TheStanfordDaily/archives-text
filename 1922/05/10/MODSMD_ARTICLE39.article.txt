# Credit Will Be Given For Summer Training
## 
### 
Men who attend the field artillery
camp at Camp Lewis this summer as ad
ditional military training will be allowed
five units of university credit for the
work, according to a decision of the
committee on advanced standing. Major
Leroy P. Collins, head of the local mili
tary department, will be in command of
the field artillery activities at the north
ern camp, which will open on June 15.
Thirty-two men will attend from Stan
ford, all expenses being paid by the gov
ernment, and the advanced course men
receiving pay in their respective ratings.
