# G. and M. Society to Plan Initiations at Meeting
## 
### 
Plans for the initiation of new mem
bers will be discussed at a meeting of
Geology and Mining Society, to be held
this evening at 7 :30 o'clock at the Kappa
Sigma house. Initiations will probably
be held this week-end. Other important
business matters will be taken up at the
meeting this evening, and President
Robert Spiers requests that all members
be present.
