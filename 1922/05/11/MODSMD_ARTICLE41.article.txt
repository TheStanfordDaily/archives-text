# CAP AND GOWN WILL GIVE ANNUAL BANQUET
## 
### 
Miss Elisabeth Buckingham of the
English department will be toastmistress
at the Cap and Gown annual reunion
banquet in the new Union Friday eve
ning, May 19, at 6:30 o'clock. Dean
Mary Yost, Mrs. Payson J. Treat, Mrs.
Theresa Russell, and Margaret Shedd,
'20, will speak at the banquet.
Two hundred and fifty invitations have
been sent to Cap and Gown alumnae.
The reunion banquet is given annually by
Cap and Gown the night preceding Uni
versity Day, as it is customary to hold all
alumni gatherings at this time.
