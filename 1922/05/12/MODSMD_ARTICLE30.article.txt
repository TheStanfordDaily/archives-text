# FRENCH MUSIC TO BE FEATURE OF RECITAL
## 
### 
Compositions by two well-known
French composers, Guilmant and Mas
senet, will be the feature of the organ
recital Sunday afternoon, May 14, at
4, in Memorial Church. The number
by Massenet has Tieen arranged for the
organ by Clarence Dickinson, an Amer
ican composer.
Mr. Allen will give the following pro
gram :
Prelude and Dugue in G Minor
John Ilaraden Pratt
Scene Religieuse Massenet
Symphonie No. 1, in D Minor
Louis Vierne
Allegro Vivace.
Andante.
Finale.
