# Famous Instructor May Coach U.C. Swimming
## 
### 
Tommy Whittaker, former swimming'
coach at the Naval Academy at Annapo
lis, former director of aquatics at the
Missouri Athletic Club, and one of the
foremost swimming experts in the Unitecf
States, may become swimming coach at
the University of California. This be
came known yesterday when Lute Nich
ols, graduate manager at California, said
that it might be arranged to have Whit
taker take charge of aquatic affairs at
Berkeley.
