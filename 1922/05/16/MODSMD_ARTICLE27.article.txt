# Regular Nestoria Meeting Postponed to Next Week
## 
### 
The regular meeting of Nestoria De
bating Society, announced for Wednes
day night ,has been postponed to next
week because of the Labor Day pro
gram.
It is rumored that a well-known mem
ber of the society, whose name is being
withheld, will be tried in the near future
for certain heinous crimes which he is
accused of committing. If found guilty,
a fitting punishment will be meted out by
a Nestoria court.
