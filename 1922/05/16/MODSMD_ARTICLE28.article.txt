# Packing Company Men Will Interview Students
## 
### 
Representatives of the California
Packing Company will be at the appoint
ment office Wednesday, May 17, at 3:30
to interview Stanford students who are
interested in obtaining positions with
their company for the sufnmer. The
large size of this company causes them
to have many vacancies. It is very im
portant that men who wish to get these
positions be at the meeting.
