# Mrs. Thomas Appointed Stanford Postmistress
## 
### 
Mrs. Chester Thomas, '98, of Oakland,
has been appointed postmistress of the
Stanford post office. Mrs. Thomas, with
her three children, will make her home
on the campus this summer and hopes to
locate here permanently.
Chester Thomas, '98, who died in 1920,
was a graduate of the Civil Engineering
department and had business interests in
the Yukon Territory.
