# GLEE CLUB WILL SING ON UNIVERSITY DAY
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club of thirty
voices under the direction of Warren D.
Allen, university organist, will give a
concert as part of the University Day
program for returning alumni. It will be
held in the Memorial Church this com
ing Saturday afternoon from 5 to 5:30.
Instead of the usual program that has
been given in concerts this season, sev
eral sacred numbers will be introduced.
In order to rehearse the program, all
Glee Club men have been asked to meet
in room 84, Wednesday afternoon at
4:30.
