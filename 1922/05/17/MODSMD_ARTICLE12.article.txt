# Miss Smith, Y.W. Finance Secretary, Visits Campus
## 
### 
Miss Ellen Smith, national Y. W. C. A.
finance secretary, was on the campus
today to discuss with Marjorie Woolsey,
secretary of the local association, and
Edna Hawkenson, '22, the financial
problems which will be brought up at the
Asilomar conference next August. Miss
Smith proposed the percentage basis for
financing student associations which was
adopted at the Y. W. C. A. national con
vention held last April at Hot Springs.
Miss Smith's office is in San Francisco.
