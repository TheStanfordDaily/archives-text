# D.R. LANE TO TALK AT JOURNIALLSTS' DINNER
## 
### 
D. R. Lane, night manager for the As
sociated Press in San Francisco, will
address a meeting of the American Jour
nalists' Association at dinner in the new
Unio.n at 6 o'clock tomorrow evening.
Everyone interested in journalism is in
vited to attend the meeting, but reserva
tions must be made by noon tomorrow.
There will be a ten-minute discussion
by the association members on matters
of general campus interest following
Lane's talk. The dinner will be con
cluded by 8 o'clock on account of the
joint meeting to be held at that time at
which David Prescott Barrows will
speak.
