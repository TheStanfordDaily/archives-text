# F.W. Coker Will Give Courses Next Quarter
## 
### 
Francis William Coker, professor of
political science in Ohio State University,
will give two courses here during the
summer quarter. He received his A.B.
from Harvard in 1902 and his Ph.D.
from Columbia in 1910. He was lecturer
at Yale and at Columbia during 1917
and 1918.
Professor Coker served on Golonel
House's commission which gathered ma
terial for use in the peace conference.
He is one of the editors of the "Political
Science Review," and is the author of
"Organismic Theories of the State" and
of "Readings in Political Philosophy.''
