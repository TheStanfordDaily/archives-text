# Professor Cooper Will Spend Summer Abroad
## 
### 
Professor W. A. Cooper of the Ger
man department expects to leave the
campus at the beginning of the summer
quarter, for a period of travel and study
in Europe, and will be away until Christ
mas.
During this time he will visit various
points of interest, especially Weimar,
Germany, and other places of classical
and historical importance. His travels
will also include Holland, Belgium and
Switzerland. Professor Cooper intends
to make a study of some of the old his
torical cities in southwestern Germany
for purposes of research.
Professor Cooper plans to see the
Passion Play at Oberamergau in Au
gust, as during that month and the
month of July there will be nine per
formances.
