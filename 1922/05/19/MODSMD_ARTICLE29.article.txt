# One Hundred Dollars Made At Benefit Bridge Tea
## 
### 
Over one hundred dollars was clearcd
at the benefit bridge-tea held yesterday,
May 18, at the Women's Clubhouse, for
the purpose of raising money to pay off
the clubhouse debt. Wives of faculty
men donated candy, which was sold by
children during the afternoon. Miss
Marjorie McDonald, at the piano, and
Miss Margaret Macky, violin, furnished
music during the serving of tea.
Thanks is due to those who supported
the tea and those whose work was
largely responsible for the success are
Mrs. G. B. Culver, Mrs. A. E. Roth,
Mrs. J. S. Tatlock, and members of the
Board of Directors.
