# F.J. Thompson Will Speak To Electrical Engineers
## 
### 
"Automatic Substations" wil be the
subject of a lecture by T. J. Thompson of
San Francisco before the members of
the Electrical Engineering Society next
Thursday evening at 7:30 o'clock in
Room 283 of the Engineering Building.
Thompson is a western representative of
the General Electric Company.
In his talk Thompson will demonstrate
the uses of electric automatic substations
for various types of machinery. Auto
matic substations have already been in
stalled by some of the leading electric
companies in the country, and their use
is rapidly extending. The processes of
manufacture will also be explained.
The lecturc will be illustrated by lan
tern views. All engineering students arc
invited to attend.
