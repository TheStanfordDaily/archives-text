# University of Washington Nine Defeats W.S.C. Team
## 
### 
The University of Washington evened
up the baseball series with Washington
State College yesterday by taking the
second and final game by a 9 to 6 score.
With three men on bases in the last
inning, Washington State sent in Zeigler
as pinch hitter. He was fanned by Stet
ser, the third pitcher used in the game
by the University of Washington.
