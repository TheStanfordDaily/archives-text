# BENEFIT PLANNED FOR CONVALESCENT HOME
## 
### 
"The Dragon," a play by Lady Gre
gory, will be given in the gardens of
Miss Harker's school, the evening of
June 2, for the benefit of the Stan
ford Home of Convalescent Children.
Mrs. A. D. MacLaine, who recently
coached a successful one-act play
given by the Community Players, is
directing the production. The parts
will be taken by members of the senior
class.
