# Dean Huston Will Discuss Administration of Justice
## 
### 
"Non-professional Administration of
Justice," will be discussed by Dean
Huston of the Law School at an open
meeting of Alpha Pi Zeta to be held
tomorrow evening on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
All students who are interested in
the discussion of political problems,
and all Lower Division students who
are intending to major in Political Sci
ence are invited to attend.
