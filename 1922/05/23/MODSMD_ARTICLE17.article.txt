# Intramural Baseball Players Striving For Coat of Tan in Contests
## 
### 
Intramural baseball is organized, ac
cording to the authoroties, for the pur
pose of improving wind and limb; but
a new benefit has come to light, and
is now offered to an eager public. In
one of the recent matches the umpire
was clad in a blue one-piece bathing
suit, and strolled around the diamond
under the California sunshine with an
exultant expression. The arbiter de
clared that athletes should come out
for intramural baseball and have a
summer coat of tan made to order.
It is believed by unreliable sources
that the Military Department will
order the bathing suit to take the place
of the regulation drill uniform, in
order that Stanford may send forth a
bevy of nut-brown men into an admir
ing world.
