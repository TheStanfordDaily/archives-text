# Professor B.O. Foster Will Tell of Greek Swimmers
## 
### 
"Some Greek and Roman Swimmers"
will be the subject of a lecture to be
given by Professor B. O. Foster of the
Latin department, to the Classical
Club, tomorrow evening, at 8 o'clock,
in the Women's Clubhouse. The ma
terial for the talk was gathered from
representations on Greek vases and
from allusions made by Greek and
Latin writers, and will include a de
scription of the various strokes used,
an account of swimming as adapted
to ancient warfare, and some famous
Greek and Roman swimmers and div
ers. The public is invited.
