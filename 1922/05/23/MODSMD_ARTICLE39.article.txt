# Franklin Hichborn, '92, To Talk in Palo Alto
## 
### 
"The Case of Charlotte Anita Whit
ney" will be the subject of a lecture
to be delivered by Franklin Hichborn,
'92, in the Community House, Thurs
day evening, at 8 o'clock. Hichborn
is now employed by the prohibition
forces in this state in the interest of
law enfonement.
In his lecture, Hichborn will outline
tho reasons for the arrest and convic
tions of Mrs. Whitney. The public is
invited.
