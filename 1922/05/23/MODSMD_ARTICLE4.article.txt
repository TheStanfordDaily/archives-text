# PALO ALTO LOSES TO SAN JOSE ON TRACK
## 
### 
Palo Alto Union High School finish
ed on the short end of a 109 to 4ft
score in the annual dual meet with
San Jose held on the Stanford oval
last Saturday. Palo Alto placed first
in the half mile and the relay. Fehren,
San Jose hurdler, was high point man
with a total of 13 points.
