# MAJOR COLLINS TALKS AT PALO ALTO HIGH
## 
### 
Major Leroy P. Collins, talked yes
terday at an assembly at the Palo
Alto Union High School, on Citizens'
Military Training Camps.
The Citizens' Military Training
Camp at the Presidio this year will
open July 6 and a large group of Palo
Alto men and boys is expected to
attend.
