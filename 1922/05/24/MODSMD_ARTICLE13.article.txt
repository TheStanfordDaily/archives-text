# PRINCETON DEFEATS U.S.C. TENNIS TEAM
## 
### 
Princeton defeated the University of
Southern California's two-man tennis
team yesterday, taking two out of three
matchcs.
Green, U. S. C., won from Taylor,
Princeton, 7-5, 6-3; and Howard, Prince
ton, defeated Welch, U. S. C., 6-1, 6-3, in
the singles. In the doubles Bickinson
and Shipway, Princeton, defeated Green
and Welch, 7-5, 1-6, 6-2.
