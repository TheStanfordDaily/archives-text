# MYSTIC MEDICINE BOOK RECEIVED AT LIBRARY
## 
### 
Robert Flud's book, a publication of
the seventeenth centry entitled "Utrusque
Cosmi Metaphysica, Physica Atque
Technica Historia,'' has just been added
to the Stanford Library. Printed in
1617 at Oppenhemii, this volume is val
uable because of its beautiful binding
and letter-press. Flud's book contains
many engravings of Theodore De Rry, a
famous engraver of the early seventeenth
century.
Robert Flud was the chief English rep
resentative of mystic medicine. His book
is of interest because of the 1 historical in
formation it gives about the development
of modern physical science.
