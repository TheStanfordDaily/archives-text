# Harry Maloney Will Give Talk Tomorrow
## 
### 
Harry Maloney, associate director
of athletics, will give a talk tomor
row afternoon at 3 o'clock in the San
Francisco Emporium on "The Benefits
Derived from Minor Sports." This
week is sport week at the Emporium
and each day someone of prominence
in the athletic field will give a short
speech. At the close of Maloney's
talk he will put on an exhibition fen
cing bout with Bob Willis, captain of
this year's fencing team.
