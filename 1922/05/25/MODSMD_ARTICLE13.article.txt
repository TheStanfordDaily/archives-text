# Freshman Class to Hold Last Meeting of Quarter
## 
### 
The freshman class will meet to
morrow morning at 10 o'clock in Room
214, for the last meeting of the quar
ter. Final arrangements for next
year's hats will be made, and the post
er fight, for next year will be discussed.
The question of regulation of system
atic freshman discipline for the '20
class will be brought up for discussion.
