# HARDING IS INITIATED IN PHI ALPHA DELTA
## 
### 
President Harding was initiated yes
terday in Phi Alpha Delta, a national
legal fraternity with a chapter at Stan
ford. The initiation took place at the
White House. Chief Justice Taft and
General Pershing were among the mem
bers of the fraternity present at the cere
monies.
