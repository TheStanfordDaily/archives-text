# FRESHMEN HOLD LAST MEETING OF QUARTER
## 
### 
The freshmen class held their final
meeting of the .quarter this morning at
10 o'clock. Final arrangements for next
year's hats were made and freshmen
who desire to obtain hats for the summer
quarter are requested to hand their
names to Treasurer Everett Swing. Hats
arc to be purchased at Wideman's, and
will cost $2.25 each. The question of
discipline for the class of '26 was brought
up. but no decisions were arrived at.
Contrary to expectation, the election
for sophomore officers will take place
with the general quad election as a large
group of the freshmen were unable to
attend the meeting this morning.
