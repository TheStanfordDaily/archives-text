# G.H. ELMORE NAMED JOURNALISTS' HEAD
## 
### 
Garrett H. Elmore, '23, was elected
president of the Stanford Chapter of
the American Journalists' Association
for the coming year at a meeting held
last night at the Alpha Sigma Phi
house. Elmore is a desk editor on tho
Daily Palo Alto, member of the Quad
staff, Press Club and Phi Alpha Delta.
The officers chosen were: Vice-presi
dent, Pearl Bundick, '23; secretary,
Maurine Voerge, '23; treasurer, Leon
David, '25.
