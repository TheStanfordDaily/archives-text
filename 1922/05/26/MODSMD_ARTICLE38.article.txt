# R.O.T.C. Is Preparing For Exercises May 30th
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford R. O. T.
C. have definitely decided to take a
part in the Memorial Day exercises
in Palo Alto next Tuesday, which are
to be held under the auspices of the
Fremont Post No. 52, of the American
Legion. The Stanford Batteries will
parade with their equipment, along
with other patriotic organizations and
the Palo Alto military schools. Some
equipment may be borrowed by the
Redwood City Legion post for use in
the exercises at that city.
