# ACCIDENT INQUEST IS POSTPONED TWO WEEKS
## 
### 
The inquest in connection with the
death of Ruby Strachan ("Jimmie")
Moore, '23, who was killed in an auto
mobile accident on the campus on the
night of May 19, has been postponed by
Coroner Amos Williams for two weeks.
The postponement was made because of
the illness of Miss Elizabeth Mayhcw of
Palo Alto, who was in the automobile at
the time Moore was killed, and who is
to be a witness at the inquest.
