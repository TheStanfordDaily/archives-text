# TICKETS FOR SENIOR BALL TO BE PLACED ON SALE THURSDAY
## 
### 
Tickets for the Senior Ball, to be held
on June 16. may be purchased by the
public on Thursday. At present the
tickets are on sale at the Bookstore to
members of the '22 class only.
Accommodations for the dinner and
dance are limited, and, according to the
Senior Ball Committee, seniors must
buy their tickets before Thursday in or
der to be sure of a place. Mission-style
decorations will be used in the gardens
of the courtyard, where the dinner is to
be served, and in the Women's Club
house for the dance.
