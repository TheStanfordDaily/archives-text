# STOCKTON SWIMMERS WIN NORTHERN TITLE
## 
### 
Stockton High School won the north
ern California title of the California In
terscholastic Federation Saturday at
Neptune Beach, Alameda, by defeating
Berkeley High by a score of 53 to 24.
This win qualifies Stockton to meet the
winner of the southern division for the
state title which Stockton holds at the
present time.
Hardman and Gold water of Stockton
were the individual stars of the meet,
Hardman making eleven points by win
ning the fancy diving and taking second
in both the 50 and 100 yards; Goldwater
winning both the 220 and the 440 yards
from Glasson of Berkeley, who was con
sidered the favorite in both rdces.
