# PERMANENT OFFICERS TO BE ELECTED BY '22
## 
### 
Permanent officers for the class of
1922 will be elected at the senior meet
ing tomorrow evening at 7 :15 in Room
214 of the Economics Building. Accord
ing to Alumni Secretary McDowell, the
secretary is the only permanent officer
who has been elected by any of the
classes so far. It is his business to call
the class together for reunion and to rep
resent the class among the alumni.
The members of the class will be told
the details of the arrangements for Se
nior Week and the Senior Picnic activ
ities.
President A1 Taylor desires that every
member of the class be present.
