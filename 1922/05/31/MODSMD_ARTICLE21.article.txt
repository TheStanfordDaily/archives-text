# SOPHOMORES TO HAVE LAST SMOKER TONIGHT
## 
### 
Sophomores will gather for a final
smoker and get-together this evening
at 7:30, in the old Union. There will
be food, smokes and entertainment
and business for next year will be dis
cussed. This will be the last oppor
tunity for the '24 men to get together
in a body before next year and Presi
dent "Nip" McHose urges a full at
tendance.
