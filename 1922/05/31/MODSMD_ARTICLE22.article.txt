# Professor A.M. Espinosa Talks to Spanish Teachers
## 
### 
Professor A. M. Espinosa of the
Romanic Languages Department was
one of the speakers at the annual ban
quet of the California Chapter of the
American Association of Teachers of
Spanish, which was held at the Whit
comb Hotel, San Francisco, last Satur
day.
Professor Espinosa outlined some of
the problems In connection with the
teaching of Spanish in the various uni
versities and secondary schools of the
United States.
