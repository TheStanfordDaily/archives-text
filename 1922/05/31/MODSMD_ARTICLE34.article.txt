# EUPHRONIA TO ELECT OFFICERS TOMORROW
## 
### 
Officers for the autumn quarter will
be elected at a meeting of Euphronia
Debating Society, to be held tomorrow
evening, at 7:30 o'clock, in Room 12
of the Law Building. The meeting
will be only fifteen minutes in length,
and President Herbert Alexander, re
quests that all members be present.
The banquet scheduled for tomorrow
has been cancelled.
Euphronians are asked to co-operate
with the Law department in the prac
tice trial which will be held this
evening in the Law Lecture Room.
They act as jurors.
