# Delta Sigma Rho Grants Oregon Aggie Charter
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE (P. I. N. S.) —Delta Sigma Rho,
national honorary forensic organization,
granted a chapter at O. A. C. to Delta
Pi Sigma, a local forensic fraternity.
Stanley B. Houck, attorney of Minne
apolis, Minn., and national president,
was the installing officer of the local
chapter.
Delta Pi Sigma has set a high stand
ard in grade averages, although the or
ganization is primarily not for scholship.
The weighted average for all members
for their entire college career is 90.5.
