# Wesleyan Grants Honorary Degree to Centenarian
## 
### 
Cornelius Cole, former United States
Senator from California, who will be
100 years old next September, has ac
cepted an invitation from Wesleyan
University at Middleton, Conn., to at
tend the institution's commencement
exercises next month and receive a de
gree of LL. D.
Senator Cole graduated from Wes
leyan seventy-five years ago, and is
said to be the only member of the class
of 1847 still living. The University of
Southern California recently conferred
upon him the degree of LL. D.
