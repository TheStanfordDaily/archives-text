# JUNIOR POLO SQUAD DEFEATS SENIORS IN CONTEST FOR TITLE
## 
### 
Junior polo players defeated the se
nior team by a score of 3 to 2 in a fast
game yesterday afternoon, leaving the
sophomores and the juniors to contest
for the interclass championship Friday.
Yesterday's game was close through
out, and went the full length of three
periods. The deciding match on Fri
day will be of four periods, unless a tie
results, in which extra periods may be
played, or a return game arranged later.
Those participating yesterday were:
Juniors: Edwards, Vanderburg, French,
Nash; Seniors: Jennings, Binns, Condit,
Bruce.
