# MAJOR COLLINS TAKES COMMAND AT CAMP LEWIS
## Stanford R.O.T.C. Commandant Will Have Charge of Artillery Units 
### 
Majod Leroy P. Collins, head of the
military department, has left for Camp
Lewis, Washington, where he will make
preparations for the field artillery school,
which will be conducted there this sum
mer. Major Collins will be in charge
throughout the periods when reserve of
ficers and members of the Reserve Of
ficers' Training Corps receive advanced
instruction.
Captain Charles Boyle and Captain
Harold Cooney arc in charge of the
Stanford Military department for the
remainder of the quarter.
