# Lester Seib is Directing Local High School Play
## 
### 
Lester Seib is directing "Captain
Kidd, Jr.," the three-act comedy which
will be presented by the senior class
of the Palo Alto Union High School,
on June 9. Mary Pickford starred in
the "movie" version of the story. The
cast includes a group of promising
actors from the senior class.
Tickets can be purchased for 50
cents at Crandall's in Palo Alto, and
at the Bookstore on the campus.
