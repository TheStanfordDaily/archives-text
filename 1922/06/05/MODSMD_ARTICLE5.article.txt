# DR. HUGH BLACK WILL TELL SCOTCHMAN'S VIEWS OE AMERICA
## 
### 
Reverend Hugh Black of the Union
Theological Seminary of New York will
deliver a lecture tomorrow evening in
the Little Theater. "America Through
Scottish Spectacles" will he the subject
of his address.
Dr. Black preached in the Memorial
Church at the regular service yesterday,
and will conduct the Chapel service ev
ery morning for the remainder of the
quarter. He will also preach next Sun
day on the campus, and will deliver the
Baccalaureate sermon on June 18.
Among the books which Dr. Black
has written dealing with theological sub
jects are, ''The Dream of Youth,"
"Friendship," "Clirist's Service of Love,"
and "The Open Door."
