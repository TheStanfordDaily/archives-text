# RECEIPT OF WOMEN'S PAGEANT TOTAL $438
## 
### 
Net receipts totaling $438 were cleared
by the Women's Pageant given at Lake
Lagunita on May 6 under the auspices
of the Women's Athletic Association.
This money will he donated to the Stan
ford Endowment Fund. It was originally
intended to present the pageant every
two years, but on account of the great
success scored this spring it has been
decided to make the production an an
nual affair.
