# SOPHOMORES HANDLE TRANSFER BUSINESS
## 
### 
As a final project to remove the soph
omore class debt, Manager Charles
White lias made an agreement with the
Palo Alto Transfer Company whereby
all business solicited by members of the
class for moving trunks, suitcases, and
boxes, during the days of finals, will be
divided by the transfer company with
the class on a 20 per cent basis.
The rates to those using the sopho
more's servicc will be the same as ever,
ranging from fifty cents to one dollar,
depending upon the distance from the
station.
