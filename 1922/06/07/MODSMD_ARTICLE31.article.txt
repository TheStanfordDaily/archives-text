# MARION DWIGHT TO HEAD CAP AND GOWN
## 
### 
Marion Dwight, '22, was elected pres
ident of Cap and Gown at the last meet
ing of the society yesterday, at the
Delta Gamma house.
The other officers elected were: Vice-
President, Helen Greene, '23; secretary,
Frances Fish. '23; treasurer, Marion
Potter, '22; representative on the Wo
men's Clubhouse Board, Margaret
White, '23.
The members of Cap and Gown will
be hostesses at the Resident Alumnae
Tea in the Women's Clubhouse this af
ternoon. Proceeds of the tea will go to
the Clubhouse fund.
