# STANFORD MEN MAY LEAD SUMMER CAMPS
## 
### 
The annual Y. M. C. A. camps as
conducted by the Santa Clara County
Association will be held this year
rear La Honda beginning June 26 and
lasting through until July 18. It is
expected that a few Stanford stu
dents will act as leaders of the groups
of boys who are to attend this sum
mer camp from the various Y. M. C.
A. centers in the county.
