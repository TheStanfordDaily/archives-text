# David Elliott, 17, and Wife Visit on Campus
## 
### 
David Elliott, '17, son of Dr. O. L.
Elliott, and his wife (Catherine Hunt
ington, '17) were campus visitors this
week from Washington, D. C. Elliott is
secretary to the president of the Eederal
Reserve Bank Board in Washington.
Mrs. Elliott is a member of Chi Omega
and Elliott is a member of Alpha Sigma
Phi.
