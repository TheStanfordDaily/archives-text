AMONG THE MANY STANFORD traditions, too numerous to be
set forth in a body for the benefit of students now here not familiar with
them, is the privilege of wearing corduroy trousers accorded men of
junior or more advanced standing. The campus doesn't present the cor
rect atmosphere with the limited number now visible, and as a matter of
economy, comfort, and tradition, students with the required standing are
advised to appear in cords.
