# Professor Birkhoff Will Give Series of Lectures
## 
### 
Professor G. D. Birkhoff of Harvard
University will give a course of four lec
tures on the "Problem of n-bodies" in
Room 72 (inner quadrangle), Friday
afternoon, June 30, at 3:15 and Saturday
morning at 10:15 o'clock. He will also
give four lectures on the "Theory of
Relativity" sometime during July, the
exact dates to be announced later.
These lectures are open to the general
public. Only a fair amount of mathe
matics will be necessary for both courses.
The first two lectures of each course
will be conducted with a minimum of
mathematical technicality.
