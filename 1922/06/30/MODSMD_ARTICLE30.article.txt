# Marjorie Woolsey Y.W. Secretary, Returns East
## 
### 
Miss Marjorie Woolsey, general sec
retary of the Y. W. C. A. at Stanford,
left Friday for her home in New York
City She will spend the summer in
New York and the Adirondacks and will
return to Stanford next October.
