# JUNIOR CLASS WILL GIVE DANCE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Tlie class of '24 will give the first
dance of the quarter tonight in the Wom
en's Clubhouse on the campus. Grin
stead's orchestra will play for the occa
sion. Tickets will be on sale at the door
for one dollar. According to Fred Fisher,
chairman of the dance committee, if this
dance is a success, two more will be
given later in the quarter, one on July 23
and the other on August 15.
The money raised will be utilized by
the class to pay off a large debt that was
incurred by an automobile accident dur
ing the preliminaries of the baseball fight
in 1920.
