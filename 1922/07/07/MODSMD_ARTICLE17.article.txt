# JUST FOR A CHANGE
## 
### 
Little life is presented by a summer quarter, and some students
probably resent any suggestion that there should be life. Yet, if anybody
wanted to do it, a big jolly-up at the Pavilion of the nature of the affair
staged by Skull and Snakes a few months ago might go well. All the
women of the campus are now at Roble, which would simplify the matter
of taking them from their home to the pavilion by truck, and if this were
properly handled, a sufficient number of men might find a way of their
own to arrive on the scene.
Such an affair would afford at least one chance to see who is and
who isn't here this summer. It would afford one opportunity for summer
students to get acquainted. A nominal admission charge would pay
expenses and leave a surplus.
