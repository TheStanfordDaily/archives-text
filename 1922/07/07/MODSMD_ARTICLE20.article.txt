# Professor Birkhoff of Harvard to Lecture Here
## 
### 
The first two lectures of a course
of four lectures by Professor G. D.
Birkhoff of Harvard University, on the
"Theory of Relativity," will be given
on the afternoon of Tuesday. July 11,
and Thursday, July 13, at 2:30, in
Room 72, of the Mathematics build
ing (inner quadrangle). The two re
maining lectures will be given on
Tuesday, July 18, and Thursday, July
20. at 2:30 p.m.
These lectures are open to the gen
eral public. Only a fair amount of
mathematics will be necessary for the
complete set of lectures; the two first
lectures Will be conducted with a
minimum mathematical technicality.
