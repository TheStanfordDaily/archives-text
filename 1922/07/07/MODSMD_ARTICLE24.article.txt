# E.W. Hadley, '19, Elected Head of Law Association
## 
### 
E. W. Hadley, '19, was elected presi
dent of the Law Association of the
Stanford Law School, at a meeting
held last Thursday evening. Plans
were made at that time for a Law
smoker, which will be held next
Thursday at 7:30, in the Old Union.
H. A. Davis, '20, chairman of the Fi
nance Committee requests that all
dues be paid before July 13, the date
of the smoker. Several of the visit
ing law professors, including Profes
sors Aigler, Woodward, and Wright,
will Rive talks based upon their ex
periences.
