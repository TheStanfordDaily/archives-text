# STANFORD DAMES TO WELCOME NEWCOMERS
## 
### 
The second meeting of the Stanford
Dames will be held in the Women's
Clubhouse at 3 o'clock, next Thurs
day afternoon. July 13. The wives
of all the newcomers at Stanford are
invited to attend the meeting. There
approximately 200 new women eligible
for membership, according to Mrs.
Peirce, president of the Stanford
Dames. The organization meets the
second and fourth Thursdays of every
month when a program is given.
All children of the members and
visitors will be taken care of at the
Clubhouse during the meeting.
