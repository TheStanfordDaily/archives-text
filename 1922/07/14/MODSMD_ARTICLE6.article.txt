# Junior Class Will Give Second Dance on June 21
## 
### 
The Junior Class will give its second
dance of the quarter, next Friday even
ing. July 21, at the Women's Club
house. As these dances are practically
the only social gatherings of the sum
mer session, the committee in charge
of the dance, hopes that everybody will
attend. "Mose" Grinstead's orchestra
will furnish the music. The admis
sion price will be one dollar.
