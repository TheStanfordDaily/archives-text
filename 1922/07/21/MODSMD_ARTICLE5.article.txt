# STANFORD DAMES TO ENTERTAIN HUSBANDS
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will entertain
their husbands at an informal jolly
up, Saturday evening, at 8 o'clock, in
the Women's Clubhouse. The even
ing's entertainment will include a pro
gram of music and games, according
to Mrs. Leo Pierce, president of the
Stanford Dames.
The regular meeting of the society
will be held next Thursday afternoon,
July 28, in the Women's Clubhouse.
All women on the campus, especially
newcomers, who are wives of Stan
ford men, are invited to attend the
meeting, whether they are members
of the society or not.
