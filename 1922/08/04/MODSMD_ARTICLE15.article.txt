# THIRTY-NINE NAMED ON PROVISIONAL LIST FOR OCTOBER DEGREES
## 
### 
Thirty-nine candidates are named on
the first provisional list for graduation
in October, 1922, according to a roll re
leased by the Registrar's office this
morning. The names are :
BACHELOR OF ARTS
Chemistry—Faw Yap Chuck, Kenneth
A. Gagos, Harry Bernard Gidal, Dorothy
Duncan Pierce.
Economics—Joseph Paul Burns, Mar
vin George Chamberlain, Jean J. Del
pech, John Gilbert Fall, Will M. Fawcett,
Dorothy Elizabeth Hunt, Harbin Michon
Rousselot.
Education—Kathryn Jarman Williams.
Education (Graphic Art)—Estella
Hoisholt.
Engineering (Civil Engineering) —
Homer Franklin Tate.
Mechanical Engineering — Vivian F."
Estcourt.
English—Ethel Bontz Appleton, Rus
sell B. Mann, Anna Frances Ramsey,
Harry Albert Wadsworth.
English - Journalism — Harry Phelps
Gates, Walter M. Warren.
Geology—Ralph Omer Rhoades, Re
mond W. Richardson, Paul Louis Yau
doit, Davis McOuat Wallace.
Germanic Languages — Mary Frances
Young.
History — Herman Henry Chrisman,
William Clyde Condit.
Mathematics—Beatrice Alethea Fen
tier, Ralph Emerson Weston.
Pre-Legal Curriculum — William
Hooper Combs, Ralph Huffaker Cow
ing. Julien Francis Goux. Philip Mechem.
