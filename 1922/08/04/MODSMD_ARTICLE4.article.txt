# Education Department Shows Book Exhibit
## 
### 
An exhibit of textbooks and pro
fessional books arranged by the Allyn
and Bacon Publishing Company will
be on exhibit in Itoom 4u of the Edu
cation Building next week from Au
gust 7 to 11.
This exhibit, corresponds somewhat
in nature and purpose to that held last
month under the auspices of a repre
sentative of the Houghton, Mifflin
Company.
The exhibit is open to the general
public.
