# GROUP WOULD FORM NEW POLITICAL PARTY
## 
### 
All citizens who believe that the Re
publican and Democratic parties are
not controlled by, nor are they in sym
pathy with, the masses of the people;
that present economic conditions and
the welfare of the nation demand the
organization of a third party (The
People's Party) to supplant the two old
parties, are requested to send name,
address, and contribution, if any, to
J. H. Chandler. National Organizer, 500
Fifth Avenue, New York City. Salary
paid to organizers who qualify.
