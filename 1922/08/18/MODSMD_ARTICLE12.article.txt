# BOOK OF SIGNATURES RECEIVED BY HOOVER
## 
### 
A "book of signatures" containing sev
eral hundred pages of names, the product
of the handwriting of thousands of Pol
ish children, has been addressed to Her
bert Hoover in the name of those whose
actual signatures arc included in the
volume.
Marshal Pilsudski, the Polish chief of
state, wrote the first page, and other
pages were written by members of the
Polish parliament, the cabinet. Cardinal
Kakowski, the Polish Protestant clergy,
the Jewish rabbis, students and profes
sors of the universities, and representa
tives of all the arts, sciences, crafts and
professions of Poland.
