OFFICIAL NOTICES

MEMORIAL CHURCH
Divine Service and Sermon, Sunday, 11:00 a.m. Preach
ers:
August 20—The Reverend Merle Smith, First Methodist
Church, Pasadena.
August 27—Rabbi Martin A. Meyer, Temple Emanu-El,
San Francisco.
Half Hour of Music: Sunday, 4:00 p.m.
SOCIAL HYGIENE LECTURES
A course of lectures on School and Community Prob
lems in Social Hygiene will be given throughout the
quarter in Room 50, Monday and Wednesday ovenings
at 7:15. The course is given by Mrs. Bertha Chapman
Cady under the co-operative research fund of the United
States Interdepartmental Social Hygiene Board and
Stanford University. Visitors are welcome. Two units
credit for registered students.
TUESDAY EVENING MEETINGS
August 22—Lecture by Albert L. Guerard, "The Inter
national Language Problem."

LECTURES ON EVOLUTION
By Dr. David Starr Jordan, in the Little Theatre, at
4:15 p. m.
August 23—Subject: "Factors in Evolution" (second
lecture)
August 30 —Subject: "Evolution of Man" (third lecture).
END QUARTER EXAMINATIONS
8:00 a.m. Thursday, August 31, to 6:00 p.m. Saturday,
September 2.
BOARD OF TRUSTEES
August 22 —Meeting of the University Committee, at
11:00 a.m.
August 22—Meeting of the Finance Committee, at 1:45
p. m.
August 25—Meeting of the Board of Trustees, at 1:45
p. m.
THOMAS WELTON STANFORD ART GALLERY
The Art Gallery is open to the public, without charge,
during the following hours: 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.,
daily, including Sunday.
