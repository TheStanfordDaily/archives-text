# SAM M'DONALD WILL ENTERTAIN COACHES OF CARDS AND BEARS
## 
### 
Sam Mac Donald, veteran caretaker,
who has been in charge of Stanford
athletic fields, will be host, to Univer
sity of California and Stanford foot
ball coaching staffs at a date early in
September to be fixed soon.
Among the guests will be Andy
Smith, Andy Kerr, Boles Rosenthall,
Nibs Orice, Claude Thornhill, Comp
trollers Sproule of California and Roth
of Stanford, Lute Nichols, R. W. Cor
telyou, William Fletcher, and Paul
Davis.
Prominent on the program of enter
tainment will be one of Sam's famous
barbecues.
