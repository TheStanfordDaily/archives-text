# ACCIDENT INCURRED BY ROLAND HOFFMAN WHILE USING PNEUMATIC DRILL
## 
### 
Roland Hoffman, captain of last
year's freshmen basketball team,
and candidate for a backfield berth
on the 1922 Stanford football var
sity, is now laid up in the Fabiola
Hospital, Oakland, with a bursted
appendix, as a consequence of
which he will probably be out of
athletics for the coming year.
Hoffman incurred the injury
while handling a pneumatic drill.
He is said to be making reasonably
good progress in recovery.
