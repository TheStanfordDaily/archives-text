# M.M. BALLARD BEATS A. MILLER 6-1, 6-4 IN SUMMER TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
M. M. Rallard, defeating Art Miller,
6-1, 6-4, in the final round of the Stan
ford summer tennis tournament, earned
the title of summer champion last Sat
urday. Ballard is victor in a field of
more than fifty candidates.
In the doubles tourney E. H. Rashor
and Art Miller turned hack the other
finalist pair, Hodgson and Rankin, 6-0,
6-3.
The summer tournament was scheduled
and run off under direction of the physi
cal education department.
