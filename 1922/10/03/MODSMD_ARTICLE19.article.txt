# DR. WILBUR WILL ADDRESS ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
The first assembly of the year will be
held Thursday morning at 11 o'clock in
the Assembly Hall, when the new stu
dents will be welcomed to the University
and the old students will have their first
chance for a get-together.
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will ad
dress the student body. He will speak
on the present situation at Stanford,
particularly its bearing in relation to the
new students.
While this assembly is primarily for
the purpose of welcoming newcomers,
the whole student body is expected to
attend. The first regular session of the
student body will be held within the next
week or two.
