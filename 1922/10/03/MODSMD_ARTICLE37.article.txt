# Paddock Receives Olympic Medals After Two Years
## 
### 
Charles W. Paddock, world's sprint
champion, is in receipt of three medals
and a certificate of his accomplishments
at the Olympic games in 1920 from the
Belgian Olympic committee, according
to a dispatch which appeared recently.
He received the medals for placing in the
100-meter race, winning the 200-meter
race, and running in the relay.
Six other California athletes, including
"Pesky" Sprott, formerly of the Univer
sity of California, received medals and
certificates.
