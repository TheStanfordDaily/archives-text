# SPITEFUL WOMAN CORNERS 42,000 COPPER PENNIES
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 2.—"Paid in Full"
was marked today on a society wom
an's taxi bill when she got angry be
cause two collectors hounded her. For
spite she delivered a keg containing
42,000 pennies at the company's office.
