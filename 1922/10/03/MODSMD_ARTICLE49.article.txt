# PROFESSOR BASSETT WILL SPEAK IN SOUTH
## 
### 
Professor Bassett of the English
department will take a trip to the
southern part of the state to address
the teachers' institute, which is being
held in Los Angeles,, Pasadena and
Redlands. He is to lecture on the
teaching of English and poetry.
The week of the twentieth he will
address the Central-Coast section of
the Associated Teachers, at Watson
ville.
