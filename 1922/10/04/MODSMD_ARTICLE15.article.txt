# NEW STUDENTS TO BE WELCOMED AT FIRST ASSEMBLY
## President Wilbur Will Speak on Stanford Traditions and Customs Before Students 
### 
Eleven o'clock is the hour set for the
first assembly of the year to be held to
morrow in the Assembly Hall. The
prime purpose of this assembly is to give
the faculty and the student body of the
University an opportunity to formally
welcome the new students. At the same
time it will be the first chance for the
old students to get together.
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
make the address of welcome to the stu
dent body. The ideals of the University,
what it requires of its members, and the
present situation with its relation to the
incoming students will form the basis of
the address.
This assembly will be followed next
week by the first session of the student
body, at which Phil Landis, '23, the new
student body president, will make his
first talk to the students. Besides Lan
dis, other men and women active in un
dergraduate affairs will make addresses.
