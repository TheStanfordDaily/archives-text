# Euphronia Holds First Meeting Next Wednesday
## 
### 
Tentative plans for a program of
interest to freshmen, are now being
arranged for the first meeting of Eu
phronia. Debating Society, next Wed
nesday night, October 11, at 7:30, in
Room 12 of the Low Building. At this
time Euphronia yill welcome all fresh
men and other students who are in
terested in all forensic activities.
The regular time of meeting of the
society has been changed from Thurs
day to Wednesday night. This was
thought necessary so that freshmen
could more easily attend, as there is
usually less preparation for examina
tions on the latter night.
