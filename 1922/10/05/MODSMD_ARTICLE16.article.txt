# Faculty Equitation Class Is Organized for Quarter
## 
### 
The faculty equitation class, given
| by the Military Science department,
1 will be held again this quarter. This
is open to all members of the faculty.
The class will he held on Tuesday
and Thursday afternoons, at 3 o'clock,
and on Saturday mornings at 9 o'clock.
The first meeting will be Tuesday,
October 10. All desirous of joining
should see Major Collins as soon as
possible or telephone their names to
the Military Department, local 45.
