# ALICE HOYT WILL BE FIRST Y.W. SPEAKER
## 
### 
Miss Alice Hoyt. the new Pacific coast
field secretary, will be the speaker at the
first regular meeting of the Y. W. C. A.
next Thursday afternoon at 5 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Miss Hoyt was
appointed last spring shortly before col
lege closed and this will be her first ap
pearance on the campus.
Senior cabinet of the Y. W. C. A. met
for the first time this quarter Wednesday
afternoon t ) discuss plans for the meet
ing Thursday and the picnic for the new
women to be given by the freshman and
senior cabinets Tuesday afternoon. The
social committees of the two cabinets ask
that all women attend the picnic in the
costumes of the 49ers. The place of the
round-up will be announced later.
