# KAMPUS KLOTHES AND KAMPUS QUEENS KILL KARES AT JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
All followers of the ancient and
honorable Art of Terpischore will
scrape the mud from their Sheik shoes,
knock the ashes out of their hods, and
stamp into the pavilion for the first
Jolly-LTp of the year on Friday night.
Inside they will find the fairest
queens of the Stanford Campus gath
ered in mobs and trying to regain their
breath after the death-defying ride
from Roble and the Row in the Uni
versity trucks.
When the seven-piece orchestra pro
cured by Skull and Snakes hits the
first note there will be a grand rush
and the fight will be on. Woe be unto
him who comes in other but every
day clothes, for "Come as you are" is
the only condition imposed by Skull
and Snakes.
