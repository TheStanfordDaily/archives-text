# IF YOU MUST DIE WAIT UNTIL 1923
## 
### (By United Press 
COLUMBUS, 0., Oct. 11.—If you
must die, wait until 1923. The conven
tion of National Selected Morticians
said the cost of dying will be approx
imately $5 less next year.
