# GIANT ARMY DIRIGIBLE ON WAY EAST TODAY
## 
### (By United Press 
ROSS FIELD. Arcadia, Calif., Oct.
11. —(United Press)— The army dirig
ible C-2, commanded by Major H. A.
Strauss, today was humming its way
eastward, somewhere between here
and El Paso, Texas.
The big dirigible pulled away from
the balloon'field here at 8:45 p. m. yes
terday.
