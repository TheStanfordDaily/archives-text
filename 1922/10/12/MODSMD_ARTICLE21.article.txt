# Free Entrance to Dance Given Football Players
## 
### 
First-varsity football men will be ad
mitted free to Skull and Snakes Jolly-Up
in the basketball pavilion tomorrow night
according to announcement this morning
by the dance committee.
Campus clothes without embellish
ments is the order for both men and
women. Two university trucks have
been loaned for the occasion, and will
call for the women at their living groups
to transport its quota to the pavilion.
