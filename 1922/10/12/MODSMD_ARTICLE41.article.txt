# Alice Hoyt to Speak At Y.W.C.A. Meeting
## 
### 
Miss Alice Hoyt, student secretary
for the Pacific Coast field will be the
speaker at the first regular Y. W. C. A.
meeting which will be held tomorrow
at 5 in the Women's Club House. Miss
Hoyt will speak on the Y. W. C. A, in
relation to university life. Maria Ander
son, '25, will give a vocal solo. Re
freshments will be served after the pro
gram.
