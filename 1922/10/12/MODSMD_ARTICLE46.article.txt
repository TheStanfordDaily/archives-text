THURSDAY
PI KAPPA TAU—Will meet at 7 p. in.
in the Stanford Union.
ZOOLOGY CLUB meets tonight at 8
o'clock in the Reading Room of the
Women's Clubhouse.
SKULL AND SNAKES will meet at
7:30 in old Union.
There will be an important meeting
of Chemistry Club on Thursday, Octo
ber 12th, in Room 20 ofNlhe Chemistry
Building at 4:20 p. m. All majors and
prospective majors are urged to be
present.
History Club will meet at S p. m. in
Women's Clubhouse.
The Stanford Band will meet in the
old band room at engineer corner at
4:15.
Wranglers—Regular meeting at 7:15
in the Women's Club House.
PHI DELTA PHI will meet at 1:30
in Room 11 of the Law Building.
Geology and Mining Society meets
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY—
will hold its first regular testimon
ial meeitng at 7:30 011 the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
All students cordially invited.
FRIDAY
Preliminary tryouts for the Stan
ford-California debate will be from 2
to 5 o'clock in the Little Theater.
Question: "Resolved that the United
States should recognize the Soviet
government of Russia." Former varsity
debaters need not present themselves
for this preliminary tryout.
Preliminary tryouts for the Stan
ford-California debate will be from 2
to 5 o'clock in the Little Theater.
There will be an important meeting
of the Masonic Club at a supper in
the Stanford Union Friday at 8 p. m.
TUESDAY
De Molay Meeting—All members
of the Order of De Molay are re
quested to attend a meeting at the
Old Union Tuesday, October 17, at
7:30 p. m.
All candidates for charter member
ship in Palo Alto Chapter De Molay
must have petitions in hands of Ben
Allen, Palo Alto Hardware Co., before
Tuesday, October 17.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
Paul H. Davis, local coordinator
of the California Veterans Welfare
Bureau, will keep office hours in, the
Alumni Secretary Office in the Ad
ministartion Building, from 1 p. m. to
2:30 p. m. on Mondays and Thursdays.
NOTICE
Sophomore tryoutees for tennis man
ager report to Nowell at Encina Gym
nasium this week.
ANNOUNCEMENT!
All those who are interested in try
ing out for the position of business
manager of the Dramatic Council meet
in the Green Room at 2 o'clock tomor
row afternoon.
P. D. Richer, '24, Dramatic Man
ager, will outline to the tryoutees the
work to be done in trying out for this
position. All men students of the Uni
versity are eligible to try out.
