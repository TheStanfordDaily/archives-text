# STATE GOVERNORS WILL CONFER ON UNIFORM LAWS
## 
### 
OLYMPIA, Wash., Oct, 13. —(United
Press) —For the purpose of establish
ing uniform speed laws, gasoline tax
legislation and uniformity in other
laws, Governor Louis F. Hart has is
sued an invitation to governors of all
states west of the Rocky mountains
to meet with him in a conference at
San Francisco on November 16 to dis
cuss the questions.
