## 
###

cisely," declared Debate Coach J. G.

12 of the Law Building. Coach Emer
son also outlined the debates in which
Stanford will engage this year and

Emmet McCaffery, '24, was elected
president of the society to succeed
Sam Dcrws, '22, who failed to return
