# Reverend John H. Melish Will Be Memorial Preacher
## 
### 
The Reverend John Howard Melish
of Brooklyn, New York, will he the spec
ial preacher at the Memorial Church for
the next two Sundays and the speaker
at morning chapel on the days interven
ing.
Students who are interested in the
problems of Religion and Society may
consult with Mr. Melish after morning
chapel or by appointment.
