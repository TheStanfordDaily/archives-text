# MASQUERS SOCIETY TO MEET IN NEW UNION
## 
### 
Masquers Society will take luncheon at
the New Union Wednesday at 12 o'clock
for the first meeting this quarter. R. C.
Bentinck, Stanford's new dramatic coach,
will be present and will give an informal
talk. A definite time for holding regu
lar meetings of the club will also be de
cided on.
