# SPANISH CLUB ELECTS OFFICERS FOR YEAR
## 
### 
Spanish Club elected officers at the
first meeting of the quarter, which was
held last night in the Women's Club
house. Myrtle Klahn, '22, was chosen
vice-president; Irma Bley, '23, secre
tary; S. E. Aguerrevere, '24, treasurer.
A one-act comedy entitled, "The Sat
urday Without a Sun," written by the
Quintero brothers, will be presented
by some of the members of the Span
ish Club at its next meeting.
The club voted to hold its meetings
once a month.
