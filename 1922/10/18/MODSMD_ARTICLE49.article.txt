# Masonic Club Dance To Be Given on Campus Friday
## 
### 
The Masonic Club will give a dance
at the Women's Clubhouse on the campus
Friday evening at 9 o'clock. All Masons
and those of Masonic home affiliations
are invited to attend. Whitmer's orches
tra will provide music for the occasion.
