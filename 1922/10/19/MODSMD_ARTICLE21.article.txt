# SMOKER TO BE GIVEN BY ALPHA CHI SIGMA
## 
### 
Alpha Chi Sigma will act as host
to all chemistry majors at a smoker in
the Union next Wednesday evening.
Plans were completed for the occasion
at a meeting held last night.
A trip will be taken next Thursday
by the club to San Francisco and
Sausalito where they will pay visits
to the Western Meat Company and
the Mason By-Products Company.
Chemistry and its practical applica
tion to industry will be the object of
this trip.
