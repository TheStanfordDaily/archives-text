# Conference Will Select Western Grid Team Soon
## 
### 
Selection of the Pacific Coast Con
ference football team which is to rep
resent the West in an intersectional
game at Pasadena 011 New Year's Day
under the auspices of the Conference,
will be made within the next two or
three weeks. The Western team will
be named as soon as the grid season
has advanced far enough to permit
the officials to pick a representative
eleven.
California has represented the West
for the past two years and they will
probably be in the New Year's Game
again if some other Conference team
does not. nose them out.
Penn State may be the Eastern rep
resentative although 110 official state
ment has as yet been announced. The
team is coached by Uugo Bezdek, for
mer Universitv 01 Oregon star.
