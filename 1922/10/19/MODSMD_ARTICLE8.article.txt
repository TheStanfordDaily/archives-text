# Stanford Women's Club To Give Tea on Saturday
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Club of San
Francisco will give a tea Saturday after
noon from 3 to 6 in the parlors of the
Stanford Nurses Home, located at Web
st:i" and Clay streets in San Francisco.
All Stanford women, faculty and guests
are cordially invited to attend. Mrs.
Ray Lyman Wilbur and Dean Mary Yost
will be among those in the receiving line.
