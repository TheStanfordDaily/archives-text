### 
As a send-off to the varsity on its
invasion of the North, a bleacher rally
in the stadium rooting section has
been scheduled for Tuesday afternoon.
The squad's last scrimmage before
lining up against O. A. C. will be run
off before the crowd, a team of Goofs
supplyiug opposition.
Three special songs, to be sprung
in the Big Game, will be learned. Sev
eral of the Stanford yells have been
shortened by Yell Leader Lou Minsky,
and changed to fit the stadium echo.
The short, sharp cadence of yelling
