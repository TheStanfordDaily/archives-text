# GARAGE KEEPER ROBBED AND SUSPENDER BUTTONS CLIPPED
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Oct. 20. —Two bandits
robbed Arthur Koenigsburg, garage
keeper, of $1000 and then clipped their
victim's suspender buttons so that he
was helpless to pursue.
