# MASONIC DANCE AT WOMEN'S CLUBHOUSE
## 
### 
Student speakers will address the
gathering on subjects relating to Masonic
activities at a preliminary program from
eight-thirty to nine at the Women's
Clubhouse tonight.
There will be dancing. This is a "no
queening" function to which all women
and men of Masonic home affiliations or
aspirations arc cordially invited.
"The air of mystery attached to last
night's publicity will be made evident
to the men tonight," it was declared.
