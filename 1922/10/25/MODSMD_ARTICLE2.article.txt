# ELEVEN MEN HELD FOR CRIMINAL SYNDICALISM
## 
### 
(By United Press)

SACRAMENTO, Calif., Oct. 25 â€”
Eleven men with I. W. W. literature on
them were arrested in a police raid
in the lower end of the city last night
and are being held on charges of
criminal syndicalism.
W. E. Townsend, chief witness for
the prosecution at yesterday's court
session, testified to sabotage practiced
in every branch of industry by the
I. W. W. He said that during the war
members of the organization, acting
as food inspectors in a Chicago pack
ing house, purposely passed cans of
spoiled food in order that troops
might be poisoned.
