# INTERCOLLEGIATE KNIGHTS PLEDGED AT U. OF IDAHO
## 
### 
Pledging of new men for Intercol
legiate Knights was recently completed
at the University of Idaho, eleven
candidates being given the gold and
white pledge ribbon.
One freshman from each organiza
tion was pledged as well as two soph
omores to take the place of two uten
who did not return to the University
this year. The new men began work
immediately by policing the first
freshman game.
