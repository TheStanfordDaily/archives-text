# Chemistry Majors to Be Entertained at Smoker
## 
### 
A smoker for all chemistry majors will
be given at 7:30 this evening in the
Union by Alpha Chi Sigma. Speeches,
entertainment, and smokes will be the
program of the occasion.
Tomorrow the club will visit several
by-products plants in San Francisco and
Sausalito where the members will see
the practical uses to which chemistry
may be put in modern industry.
