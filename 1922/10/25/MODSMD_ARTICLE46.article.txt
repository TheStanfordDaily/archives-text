# Margaret Lothrop Talks To Welfare Commission
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Lothrop of the Stan
ford Economics department was the
speaker at the meeting of the Social Wel
fare Commission yesterday. The com
mission, which has been known until
this time as the Social Service Board,
is taking over the work formerly done
by the Y.W.C.A. and the Woman's Ser
vice Board. The Social Welfare Com
mission plans to do social service work
at the Base Hospital, the Convalescent
Home, Five Points near Redwood City,
and in Mayfield. it also intends to con
duct nutrition classes in the schools.
