# GOLF SQUAD MEETS TO DISCUSS TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Members of the golf squad will meet
in the Men's Union tonight at 7:15 to
discuss the prospects for the handicap
golf tournament, to be played over
the San Jose Country Club links Octo
ber 30. Twenty-five men have signed
up for the tournament and several
others have signified their intention of
entering the competition.
A clock putting green is being in
stalled on the freshman baseball field
which will offer opportunity for prac
tice before the match. Men who wish
can also play on the San Jose links,
providing they pay a fee of fifty cents.
