# SOPHOMORES OPPOSE "WATER-CURE" METHOD
## 
### 
The Sophomore class went on record
as officially opposed to all tubbing
and pledged strict observation of the
University's regulations against the
"water-cure" at a meeting held yester
day afternoon in the Little Theater.
Other measures for the upholding of
Freshman traditions were provided
for
Interclass football was discussed and
a sign-up sheet was passed around.
Candidates for the Sophomore "var
sity" were urged to come out for first
practice this afternoon. Suits will be
provided. Men who play in the inter
class contests for the class will be
granted 1925 letters.
