Gordon Davis and Lester Seib Sail From Honolulu
Gordon Davis and Lester Seib, '22, sailed from Hono
lulu Wednesday for Shanghai with the Daniel Frawley
theatrical company. Davis, who was dramatic coach at
Stanford, is directing the plays which are being given
&nd is also taking a few character parts in some of the
plays.
The company has been in Honolulu since early in
September. It will remain in Shanghai for a month
and will then play in Canton and Peking.
