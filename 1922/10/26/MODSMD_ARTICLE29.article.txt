# Dr. A.W. Meyer Talks on Proposed Chiropractic Act
## 
### 
Dr. A. W. Meyer talked to the mem
bers of the Stanford Dames on the
Vivisection and Chiropractic bills at their
regular meeting held yesterday afternoon
in the Women's Club House. Follow
ing his talk, Robert Swain of the Palo
Alto High School gave two piano solos.
Bridge and five hundred were played the
latter part of the afternoon.
The Dames are planning an evening
entertainment and party to be given Nov
ember 4 for the immediate members of
their families.
