# Stanford Dames Will Give Husbands and Sons Party
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will give a party for
their husbands and sons at the Women's
Clubhouse on the campus November 4
at 8 p.m. All Dames are cordially in
vited to be present on that date with
their respective quests.
The regular meeting of the Dames
will be held at the clubhouse Thursday
at 3 p.m.
