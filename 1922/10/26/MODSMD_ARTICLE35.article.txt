# LAW STUDENTS TO SEE FENCING EXHIBITION
## 
###

as one of the events on the program
form are Sandretta, Richardson, Mon
oca, and Coach Maloney. All but Ma
loney are on the fencing team which
appeared at the exhibition at Cali
fornia Monday night. The same team
will probably represent the University
at the next exhibition match to be
held here November 6 against Cali
fornia, Olympic Club, and the Army
and Navy Club.
