# New Book on War Acquired By the Hoover Collection
## 
### 
"Let France Explain," by Frederick
Bausman, is a new book on the war re
cently acquired by the Hoover War
Library.
This is the first American book which
attempts to exhonerate Germany's actions
in the late war by trying to place respon
sibility upon France and Russia.
Mr. Bausman was formerly a member
of the Supreme Court of the State of
Washington.
