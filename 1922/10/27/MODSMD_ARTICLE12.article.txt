# INTRAMURAL TENNIS ENTERS THIRD ROUND AT NOON TOMORROW
## 
### 
All tennis matches in the first and
secund rounds of the intramural tourna
ment which remain unplayed at noon
Saturday will be automatically cancelled
and the players dropped from the entry
list in order that the Advanced rounds
may proceed as scheduled. This ruling
will affect many of the contestants as
nearly half of the bouts are not yet
decided. Yesterday's battles were for the
most part one-sided with the exception
of Mueller's defeat of Hoe, 10-8, 10-8,
and Sah's victory over Huncke.
The results of Thursday's contests are:
seniors, Mueller defeated Hoe, 10-8, 10-
8; juniors, Bragg defeated Hanrahan,
6-3, 6-3, Darhanian defeated 6-1,
6-1; sophs, Davidson defeated Johnson,
6-2, 6-1, Ev Smith defeated A. Smith,
6-2, 6-3, Price defeated Sims, 6-0, 6-1;
freshmen, Bledsoe defeated Clifford, 6-0.
6-1, Fairchild defeated Peery, 6-3, 6-0,
Shillock defeated Friedlander, 6-0, 11-9,
Otis defeated Hess, 8-10, 6-3, 6-3, Engel
ecke defeated Gillette, 6-3, 5-7, 6-0. Bailey
defeated Windsor, 6-2, 6-1, Coffin de
feated Frasse, 6-0, 6-0, Forker defeated
Angell, 6-0, 7-5.
