# GEORGE WARREN WILL TALK TO JOURNALISTS
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the Amer
ican Journalists Association will meet
tonight at 7:15 at the home of Profes
sor and Mrs. E. W. Smith, 12 Alvarado
Row. George C. Warren, dramatic
critic of the San Francisco Chronicle,
will be the speaker of the evening.
Warren will talk on matters con
cerning the stage and the general
business of a dramatic critic. The
meeting, which is to be of informal
nature, is open to all interested in
journalism or dramatics.
