# EIGHT CARD NET MEN IN CITY TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Eight Stanford players arc entered in
the San Francisco tennis championships,
which opened Saturday on the courts of
the California Lawn Tennis Club, Brush
and Scott streets, San Francisco. Jimmy
Davies, Dick Hinckley, Ted Mertz, and
Ford Tussing are varsity men in the
contest.
In addition to these veterans, H. Wil
liard Everett Smith, Harry Coffin and A.
Darhanian are entered. The latter meets
Howard Kinsey in the first round, while
Ted Mertz will have a hard match with
Herbert Suhr. Among the other net
stars competing are "Peck" Griffen, Willis
Davis. Wallace Bates, and Phil Neer.
