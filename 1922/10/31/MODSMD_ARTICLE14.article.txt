# COLUMBIA UNIVERSITY GETS TOTAL GIFTS OF $50,000
## 
### 
Gifts amounting to more than $50,000
were recently received by Columbia Uni
versity for the use in research work and
the construction of needed buildings.
The Class of 1897 gave an additional
donation for the construction of a boat
house on Baker field. The Borden Com
pany gave $12,000 for research in food
chemistry and nutrition. A gift of $5000
was made by Dwight W. Morrow for
publication of "The History of the
American Colonies," by Profesor Osgood.
A number of scholarships and donations
for research work were also made.
