# Student Volunteers Will Hold Annual Conference
## 
### 
One hundred delegates from Bay region
colleges will meet at a conference of the
Student Volunteer Union to be held at
Stanford November 11 and 12. World
problems and movements of the present
day will be the general topic for dis
cussion at the conference.
The Conference will hold its meetings
in the Women's Clubhouse. Dr. W. G.
Fletcher, who has spent many years in
the Near East, will address the opening
session Saturday. November 11.
Delegates will be present from the Stu
dent Volunteers of the University of Cali
fornia, College of the Pacific, Stanford
and other colleges of Northern California.
