# Professor West to Head Social Science Academy
## 
### 
Professor Victor J. West of the Politi
cal Science department was clected
president of the California Academy of
Social Sciences which was organized at
a dinner held at the University of Cali
fornia Faculty Club last Saturday.
Marion M. Kirkwood, also of Stanford,
was named as secretary-treasurer.
The organization is non-political and
aims to establish a forum for the dis
cussion and publication of material in
social science and practical government.
Membership is limited to teachers and
practitioners of law and the social
sciences, administrators of government,
and boards of social service and
charities.
