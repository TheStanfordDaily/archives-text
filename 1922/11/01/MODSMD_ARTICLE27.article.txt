# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## RECORD BOOKS FOR ALL STUDENTS AT WASHINGTON 
### 
Dean Gould of the University of
Washington has introduced a course
and record book to be used for re
cording accomplishments of the stu
dents.
Each book will contain the high
school records of students and the
credit received at the University. Each
quarter the books will be presented to
the registrar for the additional credit
received. The books also contain a
copy of the rules and regulations of
academic life and each student will
be held responsible for a knowledge
of these facts.
