# U. OF C. ENGINEERS RANK LOWEST IN MID-TERM GRADES
## 
### 
University of California mid-term
grade reports issued for the last term
show that of the six colleges in the
university the engineering students
had the lowest marks with 27.7 per
cent below the 3.25 named as the index
figure. The best record was made by
the pre-medical students who fell only
12.8 per cent below the satisfactory
standard.
The index figure of 3.25 was set as
a basis for determining satisfactory
freshman scholarship and was chosen
to be the first average above the low
est third of the university freshman
class.
