# LAW ASSOCIATION AT U. OF C. TO WORK FOR SAMPLE BILL
## 
### 
The law association of Boalt Hall.
University of California, is making an
active campaign to further the inter
ests of the Sample bill which numbers
among the fifty amendments to be
voted upon this fall. The bill, if en
acted, will prevent unlawful practice
of law by trust companies and indi
viduals.
