# STANFORD BAND WILL PLAY AT NEVADA GAME
## 
### 
\The Stanford Band will play for the
first time this season at the Nevada
game Saturday. The band organization
is complete with sixty men with the
exception of one bassoon and one flute.
Anyone who is capable of playing
these instruments is requested to try
out. Practice meetings are held Tues
days and Thursdays from 4:30 to 5:30.
Finances of the band have been
taken over by the Board of Athletic
Control who will now have charge of
the instruments, equipment, and li
brary formerly owned by the Student
Body. The schedules are prepared by
William Fletcher through his office.
Trips, probably to the South, are
planned for both Christmas and Spring
vacations.
Elections for officers will be held
next week.
