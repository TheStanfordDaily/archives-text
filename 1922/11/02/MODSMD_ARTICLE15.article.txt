# CALIFORNIA WRESTLERS OUT TO DEFEAT STANFORD TEAM
## 
### 
"We have some excellent material
this year and expect to clean up on
Stanford." Coach Andrews said in a
recent interview as to the wrestling
prospects of the University of Cali
fornia.
Weekly competitions are held to
keep up the interest and to condition
the men more thoroughly. Andrews is
also planning to obtain the services
and advice occasionally of wrestlers
in the amateur and professional com
petitions on the Coast. Ad. Santell,
world light-heavyweight champion, has
offered his services.
