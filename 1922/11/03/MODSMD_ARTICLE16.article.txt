# Y.W.C.A. Holds Welcome Service For Members
## 
### 
New members of the Y. W. C. A.
were received into the association at
the recognition service held yesterday
afternoon in the Memorial Church.
Mrs. J. P. Mitchell, president of the
Advisory Board, had charge of the
meeting and made a short talk on the
need and place of fellowship in the
Y. W. C. A. Marion Potter, "22, presi
dent of the local association, wel
comed the new members and spoke
briefly on the place of the Y. W. C. A.
on the campus.
Ruth Garten, '23, sang "The Ros
ary" and Myrtle Klahn, '22, played
Schubert's "Litany" on the organ.
