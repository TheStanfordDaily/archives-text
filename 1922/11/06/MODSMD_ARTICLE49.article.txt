# Sophomore Play Tryouts Will Begin Wednesday
## 
### 
Tryouts for the 1925 sophomore play,
to be given December 9, will be held
Wednesday'afternoon from 3 to 5:30 in
the Assembly hall; Wednesday night
from 7 to 10 in the Little Theatre, and
Thursday afternoon from 3 to 5 :30 in the
Assembly hall.
The cast will be selected from sopho
mores only. Tryouts will be handled by
Director Bentinck and the Sophomore
Play committee. The personnel of the
committee is : Lloyd Davies, chairman ;
Edith Gronen, Charles Parks, and Louis
Hitchcock.
