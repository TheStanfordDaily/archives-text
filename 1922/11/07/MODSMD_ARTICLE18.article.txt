# Fraternities Cooperate With Leaders to Obtain Well Organized Rooting
## 
### 
A committee consisting of Gene
Trago, Jim Clark, and Don Hancock
was appointed to cooperate with the
yell leaders in the matter of new yells
and innovations in organized rooting
by the Interfraternity Conference at
its meeting last night.
The committee in charge of the
interfraternity smoker program was
revised to include Dick Taylor, Gene
Trago, and Hollis Chalmers. A tenta
tive date for the smoker was set for
the first Monday after the Thanksgiv
ing holidays, December 4. It will be
held in the basketball pavilion if this
can be secured.
There will be no meeting of the con
ference until Monday, November 20.
