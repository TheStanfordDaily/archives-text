# Girls Scout Captain's Corps Meet With Miss Woolsey
## 
### 
The Girls Scout captains training corps
will have their second meeting in the
Women's Clubhouse at 5 o'clock on Tues
day with their trainer, Marjorie Woolsey,
general secretary of the Y. W. C. A.
The women in the corps will take their
examinations early in December and those
who pass will be granted a captaincy by
the Girls Scouts. Each captain will then
be given a group of Girl Scouts in Palo
Alto, Redwood City or Runnymede.
Miss Woolsey says that anyone inter
ested in the work may still be admitted
to the class.
