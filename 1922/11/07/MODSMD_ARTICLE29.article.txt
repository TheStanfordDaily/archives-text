# BACKING THE BABES
## 
### 
That rally welcoming home the freshmen last
night was a shouting success considering the rain
and other dampening agencies that always show up
to mar affairs of this kind. It is too bad, though,
that the student body has to be divided in its support
of two teams, each with a big game this week-end.
Nolxxly knows which way to jump. For the fresh
men to follow their team to Berkeley seems to be
about the only solution.
