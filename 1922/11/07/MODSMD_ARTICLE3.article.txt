# FIFTY-ONE BODIES RECOVERED FROM WRECKED MINE SHAFT
## 
### (By United Press 
JOHNSTOWN, Pa., Nov. 7.—Fifty
one bodies have been recovered from
the Reilly mine wrecked yesterday by
an explosion, it was announced today
as rescue workers continued to tear
away debris. Fifty more bodies are
believed lying in the ruined workings
of the shaft.
