# IN OTHER UNIVERSITIES
## CALIFORNIA STUDENTS RALLY TO SUPPORT OF PRESIDENT 
### 
Students at the University of Califor
nia held a rally Wednesday to crystal
ize their feelings concerning the re
tention of President David P. Barrows
as the head of the University.
Petitions will be circulated among the
students and presented to the Board of
Regents in an effort to retain President
Barrows, whose resignation is rumored.
