# Vocational Information News Bulletin Is Issued
## 
### 
Dean Yost announces that a Voca
tional Information News Bulletin may
be found on the Bulletin Board in her
office. This bulletin states where train
ing for a particular vocation may be ob
tained, where opportunities are open and
how many women there are in any par
ticular occupation.
The Bureau of Vocational Information
issues the Bulletin twice a month, each
copy dealing with a different subject,
which is compiled by a trained board of
investigators
"Women in Chemistry" is the subject
of the November issue.
