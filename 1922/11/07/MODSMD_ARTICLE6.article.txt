# INTERCLASS TOURNE YWILL OPEN U.S.C. HOOP SEASON
## 
### (By United Press 
The U. S. C. hoop artists have
started basketball practice with a
bang. A new pavilion together with
some fine material have given the Tro
jans big ambitions for this year. Ac
cording to Coach Lee Turner, a ten
day interclass tourney will be staged
at once in order to get a line on new
material, the first college game being
scheduled for January 26. U. S. C. will
play four games each with Stanford
and California.
