# Y.M.C.A. PLANS STAG PARTY FOR NEXT WEEK
## 
###

be held Thursday, November 16, in the
Men's Union, are being arranged and will
be completed within a few days. Stag
will be the big meeting of the autumn
quarter. New members wil lbe intro
duced.
Professor C. D. Marx, head of the Civil
Engineering department, will talk at this
meeting.
