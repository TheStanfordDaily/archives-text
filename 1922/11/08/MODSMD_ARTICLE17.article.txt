# Rally For Two Teams in Pavilion Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
A rally to send the varsity and fresh
men grid teams into their respective
games Saturday will be held in the Pa
vilion tomorrow at 7 o'clock. This will
take the place of the scheduled bleacher
rally, according to Yell Leader Minsky.
Varsity and Babe coaches, and some of
the players will talk. Lines will leave
the Sigma Nu House, Encina, and Se
quoia at 6:45. Grinstead's orchestra will
be on hand to accompany the songs.
