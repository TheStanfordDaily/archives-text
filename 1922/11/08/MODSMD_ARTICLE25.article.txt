# MORE FUNDS ASKED FOR U. OF C. MEDICAL SCHOOL
## 
### 
To enable, the University of Cali
fornia to build a mammoth medical
school of the most modern type and
with the most recent equipment,
friends of the University, backed by
some of the most prominent men of
the state, have become interested in
raising an additional $2,000,000 to the
Hooper Foundation.
In 1919 Mrs. George William Hooper
gave .$2,000,000 to the University. With
the addition of the $500,000 recently
donated and the $2,000,000 to be raised
by a drive, the total sum available for
the Medical School in San Francisco
will be $4,500,000.
