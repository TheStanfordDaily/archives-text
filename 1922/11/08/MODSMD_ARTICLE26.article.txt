# STUDENT BODY PRESIDENTS TO MEET AT CALIFORNIA
## 
### 
An intercollegiate conference of
student body presidents will be held
at the University of California over
the week-end of November 18. At least
twenty western colleges and universi
ties are expected to send representa
tives from among their student body
officials. Definite acceptance of the
invitation to attend the conference
has been made by the president of the
University of Washington Student As
sociation.
Student problems will be discussed
and means for furthering the more
general adoption of the honor spirit
will be considered. Special interest
will be called to the visiting delegates
in regard to the operation of the pres
ent A. S. U. C. government under the
recently adopted constitution.
