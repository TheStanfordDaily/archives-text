# PLANS COMPLETE FOR NEW WHITMAN COLLEGE DORMITORY
## 
### 
Plans for the new men's dormitory
at Whitman College are complete, and
a call has been issued for bids.
Beginning next year, all freshmen
not living at home will live in the
dormitory, which will accommodate
one hundred men. It will be built of
red brick and stucco at a cost of
$100,000.
