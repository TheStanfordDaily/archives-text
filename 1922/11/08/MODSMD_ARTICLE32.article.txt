# TWO WILLAMETTE WOMEN POETS GET RECOGNITION
## 
### 
"Poets of the Future," a college
anthology, published by the Stratford
Company, Boston, will contain the
names of two Willamette University
women poets, Miss Audred Bunch, a
junior, and Miss Irene Berg, a sopho
more.
One of Miss Bunch's poems, "Val
ues." will be included in this year's
anthology and Miss Berg's poem, "The
Goal." will be listed in the supple
mentary roll of honor. Poems chosen
from the United States, Canada, and
Mexico.
