# Stanford Dames Entertain
## 
### 
Husbands of the Stanford Dames
were entertained at a party given Sat
urday evening in the Women's Club
house in their honor. A vaudeville act
by P. H. Talbert, '22, and a Spanish
dance by Jessie Herron, '23, were on
the program. Frank Stuart and Mrs.
L. M. Terman were the prize winners
at the games which were played after
the program.
