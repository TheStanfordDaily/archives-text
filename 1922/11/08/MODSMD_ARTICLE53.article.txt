# A.S. Raubenheimer Will Describe South African Educational System
## 
### 
Albert S. Raubenheimer, a graduate of
Cape Town University, will speak on
South African education at the first
meeting of the Education Club, to be
held tonight at 7:30 in Room 42. Mr.
Raubenheimer, who holds the Buckel
Fellowship in psychology, is studying for
his Doctor's Degree here. All education
majors and others interested are asked
to come.
