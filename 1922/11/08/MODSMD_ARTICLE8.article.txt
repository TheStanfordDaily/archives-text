# Roth, McDowell, Will Speak to Freshmen In Assembly Tonight
## 
### 
Comptroller A. E. Roth and Alumni
Secretary J. E. McDowell will ad
dress the freshmen at 7:30 tonight
in the last On-the-Boat assembly at
the Assembly hall.
Opportunities for students at Stan
ford and something of the past de
velopment of the University is the
subject of Roth's talk. The work of
the alumni, especially as it affects in
coming students, will be told by Mc-
Dowell.
Yell Leader Lou Minsky will tell
of arrangements made for the Fresh
man Class at the California Babe
game this Saturday.
