# Raubenheimer Describes South African Education
## 
### 
South Africa and its educational sys
tem was the subject of a talk by A. S.
Raubenheimer at the first meeting of the
Education Club Wednesday evening.
Raubenheimer, who is a graduate of Cape
Town University and holder of the
Buckel Fellowship in Psychology at
Stanford, pointed out certain special fea
tures of the educational system such as
the separation of school finances from
school supervision, ai,id the presence of a
Minister of Education in the Cabinet.
