# Bicycle Riders Rejoice Over Improved Crossing
## 
### 
The hearts of campus bicycle riders
have been gladdened and their .vays made
smoother by a recent action of the Uni
versity. A load of dirt was carried to
the car track spur behind the Library
and dumped on the crossing of the foot
path leading to Encina. Now the wheel
riders can sail gayly over the obstruction
where formerly they had to get off and
carry.
