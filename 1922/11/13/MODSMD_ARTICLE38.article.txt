# Visiting Rotarians Are Entertained on Campus
## 
### 
Rotarians from San Jose and San Fran
cisco were entertained on the campus
Saturday by the Rotary Club of Palo
Alto. In the morning Comptroller Roth
conducted a tour of the Farm which ended
in an organ recital at the Memorial
Church. The committee gathered in the
New Union for luncheon and were en
tertained between courses with a bur
lesque organ concert by Arnold Bailey, '23.
