# Norman Dole Is Head of Sophomore Honor Society
## 
### 
Norman Dole was elected president of
Scalpers' Society, sophomore honorary
organization, at a meeting last week.
Other officers are: Harry Shipkey, vice
president; Roland Hoffman, secretary
treasurer ; Charles Fletcher, Bob Janssen,
junior advisers; C. J. Tauzer, senior ad
viser.-
Scalpers Socicty was organized last
year by the class of '24, to see that all
men on visiting athletic teams are well
cared for and are given a favorable im
pression of the University.
