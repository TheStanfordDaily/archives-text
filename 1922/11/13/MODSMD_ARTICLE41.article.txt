# Glenn Kenyon to Explain Commercial Photography
## 
### 
Glenn Kenyon, of Kenyon & Crow of
Palo Alto, will speak at the second open
meeting of Lens and Plate Society in
the reading room of the Y. M. C. A. in
the Old Union at 7:15 tomorrow on
"Commercial Handling of Amateur
Films."
Kenyon & Crow have the contract for
pictures for the Quad this year, which
Lens and Plate Society held last year.
