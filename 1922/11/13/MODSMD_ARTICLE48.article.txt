# Announcements
## 
### 
MONDAY
ALL WOMEN ENROLLED in the
hockey classes must begin to keep
training rules in preparation for the
big game with California.
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB will hold its
regular meeting Monday at 7:45 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
ENTIRE CAST of Football Show
will meet tonight at 7 o'clock in Jor
dan Hall.
TUESDAY
SOCIAL WELFARE COMMISSION
meets at 4 o'clock in the Y. W. C. A.
rooms in the Women's Clubhouse.
PAN-HELLENIC meets in Women's
Clubhouse at 5 o'clock.
WEDNESDAY
ALPHA CHI SIGMA meets at 7:30
in the old Union.
ALPHA PI ZETA—WiII meet in
Women's Clubhouse at 7:30. See Polit
ical Science department bulletin board
for announcement of topic.
