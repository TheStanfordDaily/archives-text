# NEVADA UNIVERSITY ENGINEERS TO VISIT FACTORIES ON COAST
## 
### 
Engineering students at the Univer
sity of Nevada are planning an excur
sion to the Coast for the purpose of
studying factory and power house con
ditions. The trips will be supervised by
Professor F. H. Sibley, Dean of the
College of Engineering. It is planned
to visit such plants as the Union Iron
Works, Hawaiian Sugar Refinery, and
the Oakland station of the Pacific Gas
& Electric Company.
