# WASHINGTON STATE EVERGREEN TO BECOME TRI-WEEKLY PAPER
## 
### 
"The Evergreen," State College of
Washington's twice-a-week newspaper,
will be issued three times weekly be
ginning December 1. Clayton V. Bern
hard, editor of the paper, will attend
the Pacific Intercollegiate Press Asso
ciation convention in Berkeley Novem
ber 24 and 25. While there he plans to
investigate the operations of other col
lege newspapers in order to increase
"The Evergreen" in quality as well as
quantity. The rapid growth of the col
lege makes necessary the third issue
of the paper each week.
