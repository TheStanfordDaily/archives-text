# NO EXAMINATIONS REQUIRED FOR W.S.C. SHORT FARM COURSE
## 
### 
Any person who can read and write
and who is sixteen years of age may
enroll for study in the agricultural
short course at the State College of
Washington which is held from Janu
ary 8 to March 3. Tuition is free ex
cept for the gas engine and tractor
courses, and the creamerymen's course,
for which a small fee is charged.
