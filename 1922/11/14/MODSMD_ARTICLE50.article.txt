# STUDENTS FROM 130 STATE HIGH SCHOOLS ON OREGON ROSTER
## 
### 
University of Oregon's student roster
this year is composed of representa
tives from 130 high schools in the
state, sixteen of which are located in
Portland, according to a compilation
issued by the Registrar's office in an
attempt to learn the geographical dis
tribution of entering students. The
largest number of new students com
ing from any one place is 216, which
is the representation of Portland.
Eugene ranks second with eighty and
Pendleton third with sixteen. Foreign
students registered come from Canada,
China, Germany, Italy, Korea, Peru,
and the Philippines.
