# FRATERNITY COUNCIL TO PLEDGE ON SUNDAY
## 
### 
The extended rushing period of the
Fraternity Council, consisting of Alpha
Delta Phi. Beta Theta Pi, and Delta
Kappa Epsilon, will be concluded this
week.
Formal rushing will be followed by
a closed season lasting until after the
freshmen have seen the lawyer Sun
day morning.
The procedure of bidding will be
the same as used before. The fraterni
ties will give the names of the men
bid to their attorney, who will mail
notices to appear before him. After
stating their preferences the men will
proceed to the houses chosen.
