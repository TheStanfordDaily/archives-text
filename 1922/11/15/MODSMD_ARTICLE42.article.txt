ALPHA CHI SIGMA meets at 7:30
in the old Union.

Women's Clubhouse at 7:30. See Polit
ical Science department bulletin board
for announcement of topic.

Y. M. C. A. MEMBERS will meet at
a Stag Party given by the Association
in the Old Union at 7:30.
Y. M. C. A. MEMBERS will meet at
a stag party to be given by the Asso
ciation in the Old Union Thursday at
7:30.
A LIST OF Big Game ushers, police,
and ticket takers has been posted in
front of the Graduate Manager's office
at Encina Gym. Thirty more ushers
are needed. Anyone desiring a job
should see Chesley Douglas at the
Graduate Manager's office any after
noon this week between 4* and 5:30.
