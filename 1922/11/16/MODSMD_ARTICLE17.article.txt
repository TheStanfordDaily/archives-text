# Fraternity Council Will Turn in Lists of Men Bid to Lawyer Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Fraternity Council
will turn in their lists of men bid to
the fraternity lawyer this evening.
Notices will be mailed out tomorrow
by the attorney to all men bid.
Formal rushing by the three frater
nities, Beta Theta Pi, yelta Kappa
Epsilon, and Alpha Delta Phi, will be
concluded Friday after the noon meal.
There will be a closed period from
tomorrow afternoon until Sunday
morning.
The men bid will appear before the
fraternity lawyer Sunday morning and
after stating their preferences they
will proceed to the house chosen.
