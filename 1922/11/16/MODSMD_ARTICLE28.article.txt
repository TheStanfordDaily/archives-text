Professor Martin Will Speak to Zoology Club
Professor E. G. Martin, of the physiology department,
will speak on "The Brine Shrimp, Artemis," at a meet
ing of the Zoology Club to be held at the home of Pro
fessor J. O. Snyder, 8 Alvarado, this evening at 8 o'clock.
All students who are interested in zoology, botany, or
bionomics are invited to be present.
