# SOPHOMORE POSTERS ARE STILL ON SALE
## 
### 
There are still a few 1925 class posters
that have not been sold, according to
John marble, '25, class treasurer. The
tirst five hundred posters printed were
sold out the morning after the water
tight and about 150 more were ordered
for those who still wanted posters.
These posters can be obtained for 25
cents from Talbot Bielefeldt, Room 445,
Encina; Frank Munroe, Room 50, Se
quoia, or John Marble, at the Alpha
Delta Phi House.
