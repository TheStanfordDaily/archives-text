# Nestorians To Argue On Ku Klux Problem Tonight
## 
### 
Whether or not the Ku Klux Klan
should be abolished will be argued by
Nestoria Debating Society at their
regular meeting tonight in Room 12
of the Law building. Bertrand L. Com
pared '24, will favor the Klansmen,
while R. L. Walter, '24, will argue for
their abolition. An open discussion
will follow the debate.
