# Pi Kappa Tau Will Have Club Room For Meetings
## 
### 
Future meetings of Pi Kappa Tau will
he held in the new room soon to be fin
ished for it in the Military headquarters.
This announcement was made last night
at a meeting of the fraternity held in
the Old Union.
This room has been set aside for the
use of the members of the organization.
Maps will be found on the walls when
the room is finished and the room in gen
eral will be fitted for military meetings
and conferences.
