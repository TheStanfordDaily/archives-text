# Women Organize Riding Class and Polo Club
## 
### 
Twenty-three women have regis
tered for the Women's Riding and
Polo Club, which held its first equita
tion class yesterday afternoon.
The class is in charge of Captain
Boyle and meets on Tuesday ami
Thursday afternoons at 3 o'clock for
an hour and a half. Upperclass women
are eligible for the club, and may sign
up at Dean Yost's office. When soma
proficiency in riding has been at
tained it is planned to give paper
chases and other cross country riding
sports.
Military trucks call for the class at
Roble and the Women's Clubhouse.
