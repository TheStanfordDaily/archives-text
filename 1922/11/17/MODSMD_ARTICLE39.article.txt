# Stanford Challenged by Cornell to Pistol Meet
## 
### 
Challenge to a telegraphic pistol
meet, to be held during the spring
quarter, has been received by the
military department from Cornell Uni
versity. According to Major Collins,
the challenge will be accepted.
In the past such challenges have
been received annually from Cornell,
Missouri, Alabama, and Oregon Agri
cultural College, and occasionally from
Yale and Harvard. Stanford usually
stands well at the top of the list, but
the strength of the pistol squad this
year is unknown.
