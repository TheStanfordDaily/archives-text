# Professor Lutz To Speak Before Classical Club
## 
### 
Professor R. H. Lutz of the History
department will speak on "Modern
Greece and the Question of the Near
East" at the meeting of the Classical
Club to be held at 8 o'clock tomorrow
night in the Women's Clubhouse.
This is the second meeting of this year
year and all students arc invited to at
tend. Membership in the Classical Club
is open to all students interested, whether
they are majors in the Greek and Latin
departments or not.
Refreshments will be served after the
program.
