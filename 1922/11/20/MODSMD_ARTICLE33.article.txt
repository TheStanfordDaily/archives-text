# Entomological Society Of Coast Meets Here
## 
### 
Various entomological problems
were discussed at the meeting of the
Pacific Coast Entomological society
held here Saturday. The informal dis
cussions which were held Saturday
afternoon and evening in the entomo
logical laboratory were attended by
teachers of entomology from colleges
in the Coast states.
