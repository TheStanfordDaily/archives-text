# B.T. Higley Represents Stanford at Syracuse
## 
### 
Brodie G. Higley, '99, was the official
representative of Stanford at the installa
tion of Dr. Charles Wesley Flint as
Chancellor of Syracuse University last
Friday.
Higley is a past president of the Stan
ford Club of New York City and was
the donor of one of the first scholar
ships ever established at Stanford, and
is senior member of the law firm of
Higley, Sherman & Booth in New York
Citv.
