# Governor-Elect And Party Take Section in Stadium
## 
### 
Governor-Elect Friend W. Richard
son, 150 California editors, and mem
bers of the Three Cities Chamber of
Commerce will occupy an entire sec
tion of the Stanford stadium at the
Big Game on November 25.
Richardson will make a tour of the
peninsula together with members of
the California Press Association of
which he is president. The party will
visit the Pacific Studios at San Ma
teo, be guests at a banquet served at
San Mateo at noon, motor to Palo
Alto, inspect the business section of
the city and then drive to the stadium.
