# San Francisco Alumni Plan Reunion Dinner
## 
### 
A reunion dinner for the evening be
fore the J.ig Game, Friday, November
24, will be held by the San Francisco
Stanford Club. Following the usual
custom, this will take place at the Press
Club, Powell street, near Sutter, at 6:30.
Entertainment will be supplied by both
alumni and campus talent. Reservations
for the dinner are to be made not later
than November 23.
