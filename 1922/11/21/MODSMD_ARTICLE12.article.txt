# COFFIN ADVANCES TO TENNIS FINALS BY WIN OVER McCLEAVE
## 
### 
In a fast game, H. T. Coffin succcedcd
yesterday in defeating T. C. McCleave,
6-3 and 8-6 for first honors in the
freshman elimination contest of the in
tramural tennis tournament.
Play started Friday but darkness
halted the match with one set to the
credit of each player. McCleave led at
first, winning the first set 6-3 and lead
ing the second one at 5-0 when Coffin
tightened up and won 9-7. Yesterday the
match was begun again and Coffin drove
through to victory in two sets.
