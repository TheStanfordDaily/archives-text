# $350,000 Is Subscribed To The Third Million
## 
### 
Approximately $350,(XX1, or a little
over one-third of Stanford's Third Mil
lion for the Medical school, has been
raised as a result of the campaign which
has been conducted in San Francisco.
President Wilbur will be the chief
speaker next Friday at the meeting of
teams and divisions engaged in the so
licitation work. He is scheduled to re
turn from the East Thursday.
