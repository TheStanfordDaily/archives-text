# Junior Class Will Have Two Hot Dog Stands For Bonfire Rally Thursday
## 
### 
Two hot-dog stands at the bonfire
have been arranged for by Dan Gage
of the Junior Class, and both stands
will be running to full capacity Thurs
day night after the rally in the pavilion
in an effort to satisfy the several
thousand appetites of those who will
be present for the big fire. The profits
011 the sales will go towards the Junior
Class debt.
The committee in charge of the
stands Thursday night are: Wayne
Mulford, chairman; Jack Badham. De-
Witt Rowland. Francis Kauffman, Kirk
HOward, and Orville Tucker.
