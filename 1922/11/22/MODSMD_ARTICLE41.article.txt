# Euphronia To Hear Talk On Educational System
## 
### 
"The Primary and Secondary School
System of the United States and their
Control by the Federal Government,"
will be the subject of a talk to be
given by Professor P. E. Davidson of
the Education department, at the reg
ular meeting of Euphronia tonight at
7:30 in Room 12 of the Law building.
The topic is the same as the one
which will be debated by Euphronia
and the California Congress Friday
night. Euphronia's speakers, Dick Jen
kins, '25, Percy Spencer, '25, and Stan
ley Weigel, '26, alternate, will present
their arguments on the question in a
forum immediately after Professor
Davidson's talk.
