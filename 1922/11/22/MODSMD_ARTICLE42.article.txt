men's Clubhouse at 3.

at the Delta Delta Delta house. All
interested are invited to be present.

Union will be open from 11 a. m. to

teria will be open continuously from

day in Encina Gymnasium from 4 to
5. Report to your manager on the
floor before starting to play.

which do not stop at Palo Alto.
