# Dr. Cubberley to Address Legion Education Meeting
## 
### 
At an open meeting of the American
Legion December 7, Dr. Elvvood P. Cub
berley, head of the School of Education,
will speak on National Education for the
benefit of National Education Week,
which is set for the week of December
3. Parents of children of school age will
meet at the Palo Alto Community House,
where the lecture will be given.
The membership drive of the Legion
will open December 1 and plans for con
tinuing the drive to the end of the year
will be discussed at the meeting.
