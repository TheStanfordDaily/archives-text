# Special Meeting Is Called Monday For English Club
## 
### 
English Club will come to a decision
on the title of the drama for Club
production that is given during the
winter quarter, at a special meeting
called for all members on Monday
evening, November 27, at 8 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse.
This extension of time was given
at a regular meeting held last evening.
Dramatic Coach Richard Bentinck and
Miss Margery Bailey of the English
department were present and took
part in the general discussion of plays.
An extra supply of editions of the
Stanford Book of Verse, bought on
special rates, has been obtained by the
English Club. The question of the dis
posal of these books at the Stanford
Book Store for a reduced price will
be brought up before the meeting on
Monday.
