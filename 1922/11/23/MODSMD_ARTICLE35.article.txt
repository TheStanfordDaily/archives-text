# Decorations Planned For Big Game Dinner Dance
## 
### 
Decorations for the dinner dance in
the Pavilion on the night of the Big
Game will be carried out in red carna
tions and poinsettias with a background
of greenery, and J. A. Hulwr of San
Jose has been secured to do the catering.
Attention is called to the fact that
general admission after 10:30 has been
changed from $1 to 75 cents. Applica
tions for reservations at the dinner dance
can still be made through J. E.
McDowell.
