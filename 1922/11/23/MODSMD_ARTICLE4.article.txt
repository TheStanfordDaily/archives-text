# EIGHTY-THREE MINERS LOSE LIVES IN MINE EXPLOSION
## 
### (By United Press 
BIRMINGHAM, Ala.. Nov. 23.—
Eighty-three miners lost their lives in an
explosion in the Dolomite mine late yes
terday, officials of the Woodward Iron
Company announced today.
The victims included twenty-two
whites. Seventy-five workers were in
jured, forty seriously.
