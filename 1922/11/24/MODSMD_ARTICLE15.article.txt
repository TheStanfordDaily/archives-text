# ON and OFF the CAMPUS
## 
### 
Dick Pershing Is on Campus
Dick Pershing, '21, arrived on the campus this morn
ing for the Big Game. He has been working for the
Red River Lumber company at Westwood. Pershing is
a member of Chi Psi.
♦ • •
Marriage of W. L. Schwartz to Take Place Soon
The marriage of W. L. Schwartz, instructor in the
French department, to Miss Austrice Bryant, will be
solemnized November 28 in San Jose at the Hotel
Vendome. The services will be simple, with only the
relatives and a few intimate friends present.
