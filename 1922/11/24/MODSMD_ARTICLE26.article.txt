## 
###

as the second largest in the United States.

45,000; Pasadena, 45,000; University of
Michigan, 40,(XX); Princeton, 42,000; Ta
coma, 35,000; San Diego, 35,000; Uni
versity of Washington, 30,000; Polo
Grounds, 30,000; Syracuse, 20,000;
College of New York, 20,000. The
Athens stadium, where the Olympic
