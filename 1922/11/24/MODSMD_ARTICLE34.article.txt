# Euphronia and Congress Meet in Debate Tonight
## 
### 
Euphronia and U. C. Congress meet
in their annual debate tonight in the Lit
tle Theater at 7:30, on the question,
Resolved, That the primary and sec
ondary schools of the United States
should be placed under the jurisdiction
of the national government.
Euphronia., arguing in favor of the
measure, will be represented by Dick
Jenkins, '25, Percy Spencer, '25, and
Stanley Weigel, '26, alternate. Jenkins
is the only man on the team with varsity
experience.
The California Congress team will
consist of Royal Jumper, '24, and Har
old Batter, '24, both fluent speakers.
