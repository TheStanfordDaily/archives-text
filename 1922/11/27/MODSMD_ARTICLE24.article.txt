# Nichols to Captain Bears
## 
### 
Don Nichols, halfback of the Blue
and Gold team, was elected captain
for the 1923 season at a banquet of
the team in the Hotel Clift after the
Big Game. Nichols was a member of
the 1924 freshman team and has just
completed two years' service in the
backfield of the California Varsity. He
is the brother of Lute Nichols, the
graduate manager of the University
of California.
