# ADMISSION TICKETS NEEDED TO BELGIAN ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
Students must obtain spccial tickets
in order to gain admission to the ex
ercises in the Memorial Church on
Monday, December 4, in connection
with the presentation by the Belgian
people of a statue in recognition of
the work of Herbert Hoover, '95, on
behalf of that nation during the war.
Tickets of admission to this Uni
versity Assembly, for students and
members of the faculty, will be dis
tributed from the ticket booth in the
administration building all day to
morrow and Wednesday and ON
THOSE DAYS ONLY.
Students must present their student
body cards and will then be entitled
to one ticket each.
