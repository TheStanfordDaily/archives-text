# Don Nichols Will Lead 1923 California Squad
## 
### 
Don Nichols, left halfback of the Bruin
Varsity, was elected captain of the 1923
eleven at the Football banquet in San
Francisco after the game Saturday.
Nichols entered the University of Cali
fornia from Pomona High School, and
played in the fall of 1920 with the fresh
man football team. He is a member of
Sigma Phi fraternity and belongs to
Skull and Keys and the Winged Helmet,
honorary fraternities.
