# ASILOMAR DELEGATES TO BE GIVEN BANQUET
## 
### 
Aailomar delegates will hold a ban
quet In the main dining room of the
New Union at 7 o'clock Tuesday De
cember 5, according to the announce
ment of the committee in charge of
the Asilomar delegation from Stan
ford.
Anyone who is interested in the Y.
M. C. A. conference to be held at
Asilomar-by-the-Sea, December 26 to
January 2, is invited to this banquet
and may obtain reservations froth the
Association office or from the ticket
sellers.
Comptroller A. E. Roth will be the
principle speaker at the banquet and
an effort is being made to have E. L.
Devendorf, secretary of the Y. M. C.
A., at Berkeley, present. Devendorf has
just returned from the international
convention of the Association.
The Stanford Y. M. C. A. has issued
a challenge to the University of Cali
fornia claiming that it will send one
delegate for every two from California
and the plan has been accepted.
