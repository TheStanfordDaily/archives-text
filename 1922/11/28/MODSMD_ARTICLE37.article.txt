# Scholarships Offered by U.P. to Washington State
## 
### 
Fifteen scholarships to the State Col
lege of Washington have been offered
by the Union Pacific Railroad to boys
in the counties that the Union Pacific
touches. The scholarships consists of
$75 cash and railroad fare to and from
Pullman. The boys are chosen by the
county agent on the basis of activities
in the community and scholarship. The
Union Pacific intends to increase the
number of scholarships from fifteen to
twenty-five.
