# NUMBER OF COTILLION TICKETS IS LIMITED
## 
### 
There are a limited number of tick
ets to the sophomore cotillion in the
Pavilion on December 8 and they are
going fast. These facts should lead
everyone who intends to go to the
cotillion to get his tickets as soon
as possible, according to John Hall,
'25, chairman of the cotillion commit
tee.
The tickets are $5 and they can be
obtained at the pen counter in the
bookstore.
Novel ideas for decorations and pro
grams are being worked out by the
committee, composed of John Hall, '25,
chairman; Meribeth Cameron, '25; Gail
Thompson, '25; John Marble, '25;
"Tex" Middleton, '25, and George Had
ley, '25.
