# PROFESSOR SWAIN TO DELIVER CHEMISTRY LECTURES IN EAST
## 
### 
R. E. Swain, head of the Department
of Chemistry, has received the dis
tinction of being chosen to lecture on
chemistry at Columbia University this
winter.
The Charles Frederick Chandler
Foundation provides for a yearly lec
ture by an eminent chemist, who is
also presented with a medal as further
recognition of his achievements in
science.
Professor Swain's subject will be
"Atmospheric Pollution by Industrial
Wastes." The lecture will be at Have
raeyer Hall, Columbia University, on
January 9.
Previous lecturers on this foundation
were L. H. Baekland, W. F. Hilde
brand, W. R. Whitney, F. G. Hopkins,
and Edgar F. Smith.
