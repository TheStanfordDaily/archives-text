# SCREEN STARS OF FUTURE COME FROM UNIVERSITIES
## 
### (By United Press 
LOS ANGELES. —Screen stars of the
future will come from the universities
of the country, Marshall Neilan, direc
tor and producer, believes.
Neilan is now corresponding with uni
versity officials seeking co-operation im
finding screen talent. *
"More breeding, good looks and cul
ture arc found among university stu
dents than among any other classes,""
said Neilan, "and it is these qualities
that the pictures most need."
