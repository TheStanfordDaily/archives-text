# CLINICS AUXILIARY START BENEFIT SALE
## 
### 
The Stanford Clinics Auxiliary is
making its annual sale of Christmas
greeting cards for the benefit of the
clinics of the Stanford University Hos
pitals and San Francisco Maternity.
These cards are on sale in sets of ten
with envelopes and attractively boxed.
All the proceeds go to the benefit of
the work of the Stanford Clinics Aux
iliary. They arc on sale in President
Wilbur's office.
