# Professor E.C. Starks to Talk on Marine Mammals
## 
### 
"Marine Mammals of California" will
be the subject of Professor E. C.
Starks's talk to the Zoology Club this
evening at his home in Palo Alto. Pro
fessor Starks has been engaged in re
search work on the seal islands off the
Pacific coast for several years. This
summer lie devoted largely to the study
of California seals and sea lions. His
experiences and discoveries of the sum
mer will be the main theme of tonight's
lecture.
