TUESDAY
ZOOLOGY CLUB meets at 8 o'clock
at 1115 Ramona in Palo Alto.
MECHANICAL ENGINEERING SO
CIETY meets at 6 o'clock in the Phi
Kappa Psi house.
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON meets in
the Union at. 7:30. Important.
M. E. SOCIETY INITIATION, Phi
Kappa Psi house, 6 o'clock. All mem
bers urged to be present.
SENIOR BASKETBALL SQUAD
practice in Encina Gym, 7 p. m.
MUSIC CLUB will meet at 7:15 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
PANHELLENIC will meet at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse. Dean Yost
will be present to discuss current
questions regarding the rushing sys
tem.
WEDNESDAY
ENGLISH CLUB will meet at 8
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
THERE WILL BE a special meeting
of Cap and Gown Wednesday at 4
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
THURSDAY
WRANGLERS will meet at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
ALPHA OMEGA CHAPTER of the
honorary economic fraternity of
Shifters will meet at the usual time
and place.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY
will hold its regular testimonial meet
ing on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 p. m. Students and
faculty members are invited.
Notice to Roble Students
All undergraduate women and Stan
ford graduates taking their fifth year
who plan to return for the winter
quarter, must make their room deposit
by mail or at the Dean of Women's
Office December 6-8. Failure to make
the deposit this time may mean for
feiture of the room.
MARY YOST, Dean of Women.
