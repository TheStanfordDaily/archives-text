# MRS. SNELL WILL TALK ON JOBS TO Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Mrs. Elizabeth Snell, the appoint
ment secretary, will speak on what a
job demands of a woman and what a
woman should expect of her job at
the Y. W. C. A. meeting to be held
Thursday at 5 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. The meeting is in charge
of the newly-organized vocational com
mittee of which Marion Leffingwell,
'23, is chairman. Miss Woolsey, Y. W.
C. A. secretary, asks especially those
juniors and sophomores who have not
decided on their vocations to take this
opportunity of getting information on
employment.
