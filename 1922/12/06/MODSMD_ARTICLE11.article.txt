# HARRY B. SMITH WILL SPEAK TO JOURNALISTS
## 
### 
Harry B. Smith, sporting editor of the
San Francisco Chronicle, will address
the Stanford chapter of the American
Journalists' Association at an open meet
ing which will be held at the Alpha Tau
Omega house at 7:30 tomorrow evening.
Smith has edited sports for the Chron
icle for many years and runs a special
column called "The Double-0 on
Sports," in the athletic section of the
paper.
No definite subject has been announced
but Smith will tell of his duties, how the
Sporting Green is put out, and how ath
letic events are covered by reporters and
dispatches.
Everyone interested in athletics as con
nected with journalism is invited to at
tend the meeting.
