IFORNIA —Staring into the adolescent,
freckled, smiling, cackle-raising face
of Wesley Barry as he peered out
from the front cover of the Wampus,

;of university humor of the year.

lowed the introduction of Wesley
Barry, verging on the 99 44-100 per
