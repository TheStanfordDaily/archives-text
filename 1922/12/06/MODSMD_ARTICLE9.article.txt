# Dr. True Gives Organ Program For Music Club
## 
### 
Dr. Latham True, now acting as Uni
versity Organist, gave illustrations on
the organ of how various moods and
emotions such as jealousy can be ex
pressed by music, at the meeting of
Music Club held yesterday evening. He
also gave imitations of other musical
instruments to show the wide variation
of range and tone quality in the organ.
This was the last meeting of the club
this quarter.
