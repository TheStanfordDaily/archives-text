# Appointment Secretary Will Speak to Local Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Jobs for women will be the subject of
the talk to be given by Mrs. Elizabeth
Snell, the appointment secretary, at the
Y. W. C. A. meeting to be held at 5
o'clock today in the Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. Snell will discuss what one may
expect when she takes a position and
what a position will demand of her. Ar
rangements for the meeting are in
charge of the vocational committee
which was organized this year with
Marion Lcfifingwell, '23, as chairman.
