# BUREAU OF CHEMISTRY FINDS NEW MEANS OF PREPARING TEA
## 
### 
The government Bureau of Chem
istry lias found that the leaves of a
variety of holly, or "Christmas berry
tree," when properly prepared, make
an excellent tea.
The, leaves contain nearly two per
cent, of caffeine, which is the active
stimulant In both coffee and tea. The
holly tree grows wild in all Atlantic
and Gulf states from Virginia to the
Rio Grande. It is commonly used for
ornamental hedges, being green all the
year and containing bright red berries.
