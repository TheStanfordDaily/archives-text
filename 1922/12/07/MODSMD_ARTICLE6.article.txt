# Meeting of Intramural Basketball Teams Soon
## 
### 
Intramural basketball will follow the
interclass court games now being held
at Stanford, it was announced today by
Charles W. Davis, director of Intra
mural Athletics. A meeting for the
purpose of organizing leagues will be
held kin the Encina Gymnasium at 4:30
Tuesday afternoon. Representatives
from each group which intends to enter
a team will meet at this time. A sched
ule will be drawn up during the Christ
mas holidays and play will begin early
next quarter.
Fraternities, clubs, eating clubs and
various hall organizations will compete.
More than forty teams arc expected to
enter. Last year thirty-six teams played
in six leagues.
