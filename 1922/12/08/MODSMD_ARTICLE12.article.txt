# Alpha Chi Sigma Holds Banquet And Initiation
## 
### 
A banquet at the Theta Xi house
followed by a preliminary initiation in
the Chemistry building was Alpha Chi
Sigma's order of procedure last eve
ning. Thirty-six members and honorary
faculty members were present. Short,
talks were given at the dinner by Pro
fessors R. E. Swain, John Russell and
A. F. O. Germann of the Chemistry
department. The final initiation cere
mony will take place Saturday after
noon.
