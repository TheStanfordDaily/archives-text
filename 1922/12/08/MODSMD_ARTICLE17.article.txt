# Pi Kappa Tau Initiates Thirteen New Members
## 
### 
Thirteen members were initiated
Wednesday night by Pi Kappa Tau. The
new members of the military fraternity
arc. O. C. Barby, R. E. Barby, Norman
are: D. C. Barby, R. E. Barby, Norman
Greene Jr., E. B. Harper, E. W. Laub,
Adolph Newman, W. W. Patterson. D.
K. Stewart, H. R. Trcmaine, L. C. Van
derburgh, and M. A. Ward.
