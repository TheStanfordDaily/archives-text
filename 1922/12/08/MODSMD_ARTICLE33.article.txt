# R.O.T.C. Polo Team To Play Reserve Officers
## 
### 
The Stanford polo team will play its
second outside game of the season
when it meets the Reserve Officers
of the 91st Division on the highway
field tomorrow morning at 10 o'clock.
Thanksgiving Day the polo team
was defeated after a hard-fought con
test by the officers of the 31st in
fantry.
The lineup has been changed; the
squad will play as follows: Barby, No.
1, Edwards, No. 2, Bruce, No. 3, Van
derburgh, No. 4.
