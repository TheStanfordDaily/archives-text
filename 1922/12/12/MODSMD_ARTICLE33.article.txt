# Resident Alumnae Tea Will Be Held Tomorrow
## 
### 
The regular weekly Resident Alum
nae tea will be held at three o'clock
tomorrow afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse. There will be tables for
bridge and tea will be served later in
the afternoon. The hostesses this week
are: Mrs. V. V. Harrier, Mrs. Max
Hopper, Mrs. C. A. Chaquette, Mrs. D.
R. Bonliam, Mrs. A. W. Johnson, Mrs.
Burke Corbet, Mrs. William Darsie.
The last tea of the autumn quarter
will be held next Wednesday after
noon in the Women's Clubhouse.
