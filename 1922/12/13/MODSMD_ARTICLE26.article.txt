# New Orchestra Club Plans For Tryouts Next Quarter
## 
### 
A Stanford Orchestra Club was or
ganized last Saturday evening. Tryouts
for membership will be held early next
quarter.
The object of the club, founded in the
interest of orchestra music, is to meet
regularly for the purpose of acquiring
a large repertoire of music.
Officers were elected and C. W. Midg
ley was chosen president; E. C. Moore,
vice-president; O. W. Johnson, secre
tary-treasurer.
The founders are as follows: D. B.
Hackett, '23, E. C. Moore, '23, V. J.
Minner, '25, C. W. Midgley, '23, M.
Davidson, and O. W. Johnson.
