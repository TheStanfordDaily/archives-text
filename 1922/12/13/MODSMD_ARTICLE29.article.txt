WEDNESDAY
CIRCLE "S" SOCIETY meets at
12:45 in Old Union. Short and import
ant.
RESIDENT ALUMNAE TEA will be
held at 3 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.
lOTA SIGMA PI will meet at 7:30
at Roble hall.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE meets at
7:15 in the Women's Clubhouse.
WEDNESDAY EXAMINATIONS of
men students scheduled for Thurs
day, December 14. are postponed.
W. H. BARROW,
Medical Adviser of Men.
THURSDAY
HISTORY CLUB will meet at 7:30
in the Women's Clubhouse.
WRANGLERS will meet at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
JUNIOR CLASS will meet in Room
214 at 1 o'clock. Phil Landis will talk
on "Senior Control."
STANFORD DAMES meet in -Wo
men's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock. There
will be a feature for the children.
NOTICE!
Men who desire to take out tuition
notes for the winter quarter must
make applications at Dean Culvpr's of
fice on Thursday and Friday, Decem
ber 14 and 15.
Men who desire to renew their tui
tion notes for next quarter should do
so at the office of the Dean of Men
next Thursday, Friday and Saturday.
Notice to Roble Students
All undergraduate women and Stan
ford graduates taking their fifth year
who plan to return for the winter
qt&rter, must make their room deposit
by mail or at the Dean of Women's
Office December 6-8. Failure to make
the deposit this time may mean for
feiture of the room.
MARY YOST, Dean of Women.
NOTICE TO WOMEN STUDENTS
OF THE UNIVERSITY
Attention is called to the fact that
unless a woman student registers on
Registration Day she forfeits her place
in the University. In case a student is
unavoidably detained either at home
or in transit to the University, she
must write or telegraph the Registrar
so that he gets the communication on
Registration Day, Jan. 2.
(Signed): MARY YOST,
Dean of Women.
NOTICE TO LOWER DIVISION WO
MEN: Lower division women should
sign up for their conferences with
their advisers Wednesday at the
Dean of Women's ofEice.

New Union will remain open on reg
ular schedule during the Christmas
holidays. The cafeteria will be closed
at the end of the quarter, but the
club dining rooms will be operated
for any students who make arrange
ments with Miss Handy for meals
there during the vacation.
The regular schedule for meals is
as follows:
Breakfast —7 to 9:30 o'clock.
Lunch—ll:4s to 1 o'clock.
Dinner —5:45 to 6:45 o'clock.

Notice to Roble Students
All undergraduate women and Stan

qt&rter, must make their room deposit

NOTICE TO WOMEN STUDENTS
OF THE UNIVERSITY
Attention is called to the fact that
unless a woman student registers on
Registration Day she forfeits her place
in the University. In case a student is
unavoidably detained either at home
or in transit to the University, she
must write or telegraph the Registrar
so that he gets the communication on
Registration Day, Jan. 2.
(Signed): MARY YOST,
Dean of Women.
NOTICE TO LOWER DIVISION WO
