# BENTLEY FUNERAL IS HELD THIS AFTERNOON
## 
### 
The funeral of Charles H. Bentley,
, who died of an attack of apoplexy while
1 attending the Stanford-Pittsburgh game
Saturday, will be held this afternoon.
Bentley was vice-president of the Cali
fornia Packing Corporation and one of
the well-known business men of the Pa
cific Coast. He aided Herbert Hoover
in the organization of the Food Admin
istration and occupied other important
commercial posts. At one time he was
president of the San Francisco Chamber
of Commerce.
Bentley's son. Wilder, a student at
Vale, was with him when lie died. Bent
ley was a graduate of the University of
California.
