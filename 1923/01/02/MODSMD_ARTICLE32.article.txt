# FALLING TREE INJURES CAMPUS EMPLOYE
## 
### 
C. C. Crippen, for many years em
ployed on the campus, was seriously
injured last Thursday when he was
struck by a falling tree located near
the Stanford Union. He was taken
to the Palo Alto hospital for treatment
where it was discovered that he had
suffered a fractured leg and possibly
internal injuries. Crippen lives at 372
Lincoln street, Mayfield.
