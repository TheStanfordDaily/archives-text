# BAND WILL ORGANIZE UNDER NOVEL SYSTEM
## 
### 
The Stanford band will hold its organ
ization meeting for winter quarter
on Thursday afternoon of this week.
Members will report in the band room
in the Engineering building at 4 p. m.
The usual University credit will be given
for band work, and a trip will be taken
during the spring vacation, definite ar
rangements for which will be completed
within a few weeks. There are openings
for several new members, and men with
ability will be welcome at this meeting.
A slight change in the organization
of the band is planned for this quarter.
The members will form a club, and a
selection of officers will probably be made
next week.
