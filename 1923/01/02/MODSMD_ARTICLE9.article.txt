# U.C. Stadium Contracts Have Not Been Awarded
## 
### 
Contracts for building the initial units
of the California Stadium have not yet
been awarded. The Board of Regents
have delayed awarding the contract be
cause they are endeavoring to determine
which is the "lowest responsible" bidder
in order to insure the completion of the
work within the required 135 days, ac
cording to Secretary Robert Sproul.
