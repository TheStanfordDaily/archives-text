# Mrs. Miller, Secretary in Esthonia Will Talk at Y.W.C.A. Meeting Thursday
## 
### 
All new women in the University are
invited to attend the first Y. W. C. A.
meeting of the quarter to be held Thurs
day afternoon at 4:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse. Mrs. Olive Gordon Miller,
who spent four years as "Y" secretary
in Esthonia, will be the speaker.
Mrs. Miller returned to the United
States about six months ago. Esthonia,
as one of the Baltic states created by tne
war, has been organizing its government
and in general going through a strenuous
period of change. Mrs. Miller had many
exciting and interesting experiences,
which will be related in her talk tomor
row night.
