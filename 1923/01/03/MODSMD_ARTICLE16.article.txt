# PROFESSOR E.W. SMITH RETURNS FROM EAST
## 
### 
Professor E. W. Smith returned to the
campus yesterday from Chicago, where
he has been attending a convention of
the American Association of Teachers
of Journalism, held at Northwestern
University from December 28 to 31.
Professor Smith, in his address de
livered before the association, laid "par
ticular emphasis on the professional and
ethical responsibilities of journalists and
teachers of journalism."
The association adopted by vote a
resolution which called for unified ef
forts on the part of teachers of journal
ism to define clearly to students of jour
nalism the ethics of the profession.
