# FLOYD KALLAM WINS ENGINEERING PRIZE
## 
### 
Floyd L. Kallam, '21, has won the an
nual prize of the American Society of
Mechanical Engineers for the best paper
submitted by a junior member of the
organization with an article entitled,
"Preliminary Report on the Investigation
of Thermal Conductivity of Liquids."
This is the fourth time such an honor
has been bestowed upon a Stanford stu
dent, being a distinction no other college
has earned more than once.
Kallam, who was the University base
ball captain in 1921 and freshman base
ball coach in 1922, graduated from the
Department of Mechanical Engineering

with one of the highest standings in re
cent years.
