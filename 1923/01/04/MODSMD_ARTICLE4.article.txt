# ROBINSON’S DEMAND WILL REOPEN SENATE DEBATE
## 
### (By United Press 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 4. —Senator
Robinson's demand that the United
States be officially represented on the
reparations commission was expected
to reopen Senate debate today on the
international situation. Robinson was
scheduled to explain his bill in a brief
speech and so great is the interest
now in the Senate on this and kindred
questions that general discussion was
expected to follow.
Meanwhile, amid great secrecy,
Ambassador George Harvey is carry
ing on his conference on the European
situation with President Harding, Sec
retary of State Hughes and other ad
ministration officials and leaders in
Congress.
