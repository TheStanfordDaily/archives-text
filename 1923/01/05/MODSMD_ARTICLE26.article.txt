# ON and OFF the CAMPUS
## 
### 
Faculty Women Will Meet
The Faculty Women's Club will hold its first meeting
of the quarter at 3 o'clock Monday afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. Robert Guryea will present a program of songs,
accompanied by Mrs. E. C. Franklin on the piano.
♦ ♦ *
Stanford Dames Help at Childrens' Home
The mothers' branch of the Stanford Dames met
Tuesday afternoon in the Women's Clubhouse. The
members spent the time sewing garments and piecing
quilts for the Stanford Home for Convalescent Children.
