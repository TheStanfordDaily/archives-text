# HARVARD AUTHORITIES GIVE OUT 1923 FOOTBALL SCHEDULE
## 
### 
Harvard University athletic authori
ties have announced a tentative foot
ball schedule for 1923. For the first
time since the war the intersectional
element 'was lacking in the schedule.
The seven games which have been ar
ranged to date are: Oct. 6, pending;
Oct. 13, Middlebury; Oct. 20, Holy
Cross; Oct. 27, Dartmouth; Nov. 3,
Tufts; Nov. 10, Princeton at Prince
ton; Nov. 17, Brown; and Nov. 24,
Yale.
