# STADIUM IS PLANNED FOR WASHINGTON, D.C.
## 
### 
A huge stadium to seat 75,000 per
sons may be built in Washington, D.
C., if plans now being considered by
the National Amateur Athletic Federa
tion are carried out.
Colonel VVaite C. Johnson,- United
States Army, a member of the federa
tions committee, visualized such a
stadium as an ideal place for the
annual Army-Navy football contest and
suggested that periodically sectional
fixtures in different branches of sport
be staged at the capitol.
