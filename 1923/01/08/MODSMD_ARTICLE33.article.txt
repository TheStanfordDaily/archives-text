# Professor Swain Lectures At Columbia University
## 
### 
Professor Robert Eckles Swain of the
Chemistry department will speak tomor
row at Columbia University, New York,
on "Atmospheric Pollution by Industrial
Wastes." Professor Swain was chosen
as lecturer under the Charles Frederick
Chandler foundation. The income from
this foundation is used each year to pro
vide for a lecture by an eminent chemist
and for a medal to be presented to the
lecturer.
Professor Swain and his family will
sail from New York January 18, to spend
six months traveling.
