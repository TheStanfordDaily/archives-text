# WELCOME SIGNS ARE PUT UP IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Erection of two signs welcoming
visitors to Stanford University and
Palo Alto, to be placed on the State
highway at the two entrances to the
Palo Alto limits, was authorized by the
Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce.
These signs are a part of the adver
tising program carried on by the
Chamber of Commerce in their
"Greater Palo Alto" movement.
