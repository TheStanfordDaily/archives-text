# Alumni of New York Print Stanford News Pamphlet
## 
### 
Stanford alumni in New York start
ed the new year with a monthly pam
phlet of Stanford news which is mailed
free of charge to more than five hun
dred alumni on the East coast.
During the Pittsburgh-Stanford game
the same organization followed the
play by radio. This was the first oc
casion when a West Coast contest was
made audible on the other side of the
continent.
