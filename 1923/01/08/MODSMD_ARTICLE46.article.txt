# Professor Burlingame Is In East for Conferences
## 
### 
Professor L. L. Burlingame of the
Botany department is now In the East
attending a number of scientific con
ferences, among them the American
Association for the Advancement of
Science, the Botanical Society of
America, and the American Society of
Naturalists. Professor Burlingame will
report on his experiments on the
clarkia and other plants which he has
been raising in the experimental gar
dens here. He is expected back on
the campus January 13.
