# Schubert Club Will Hold Tryouts for Membership
## 
### 
Schubert Club tryouts will be held in
the music room at Roble Hall this even
ing from 5 to 6 and 7 to 8 o'clock.
Mabel Ferry and Katherine Beswick are
in charge. Ten or more women may be
taken in, according to the parts they sing.
The club is especially in need of women
who can sing alto.
