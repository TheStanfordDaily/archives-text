# TREASURY PLANS TO FLOAT A NEW OFFERING OF NOTES
## 
### (By United Press 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 9.—The treas
ury will float a new offering of 4 1 / £
per cent treasury notes, totalling $300,-
000,000, on January 15. These notes
mature December 15, 1927.
The offering is intended, with bal
ance already on hand, to provide for
the called Victory notes and 1918 war
savings certificates which still remain
to be presented, and at the same time
to cover the treasury's current cash
requirments between now and the
March installment of taxes.
