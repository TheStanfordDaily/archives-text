# TROJANS WILL HAVE A “ROW” FOR FRATERNITIES
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN
CALIFORNIA —One of the first prac
tical steps in the setablishment of a
Fraternity Row at the University of
Southern California has been made by
the Xi Psi Phi dental fraternity, which
plans to erect a house on the site of
the proposed row. Tentative plans call
for a gymnasium, plunge, and other
elaborate equipment seldom found in
fraternity residences.
