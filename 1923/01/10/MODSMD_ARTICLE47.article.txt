WEDNESDAY
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE will meet
in usual place at 7 o'clock.
ZOOLOGY CLUB meets at the Kap
pa Alpha Theta house at 8 o'clock.
SOPHOMORES wishing to try out for
Baseball Manager will report in the
Graduate Manager's office at 4:15
Wednesday, January 10.
AD CLUB meets at 12:01 on Library
steps to have Quad picture taken. Be
there on time.
CLASSICAL CLUB will meet in Wo
men's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock.

will meet at the S. A. E. house
Wednesday evening at 7:30.
THURSDAY
WRANGLERS will meet at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
AMERICAN JOURNALISTS' ASSO
CIATION meeting at 2:00 In Room 3
of Press Building.
MUSIC WRITERS wishing to sub
mit songs for the Ram's Head show
will meet with Arnold Bailey in the
old Union Thursday at 1 o'clock. Com
positions already written should be
turned in then.
CHEMISTRY CLUB meets at 4:20
p. m. in Room 20, Chemistry Building.
HISTORY CLUB will meet at 8:00
in the Women's Clubhouse.
PHI KAPPA TAU will meet at Mili
tary headquarters at 7:30i p. m.
ALPHA PI ZETA meets on Library
steps at 12:55 for picture for 1924
Quad.
FRIDAY

Law steps for Quad picture at 12:50
sharp.

the Library steps to have picture taken
for Quad.

o'clock in Room 214 instead of Room
414.
