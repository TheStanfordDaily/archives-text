# Professor Bassett Will Lead Discussion Group
## 
### 
Starting this Sunday, Professor Lee
I Emerson Bassett of the Department of
Public Speaking will lead a discussion
group at 10 a.m. in the Memorial Church
on "The Social Principles of Jesus."
Professor Bassett will take up Chris
tianity in relation to modern industrial
and social problems. This group is un
der the joint direction of the Y. W. C. A.
and the Y. M. C. A., and all men and
women who wish to attend are wclcomc.
