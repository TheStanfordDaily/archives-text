# SANTA CLARA DEFEATS SAN JOSE Y.M.C.A. TEAM
## 
### 
The University of Santa Clara de
feated the basketball team of the San
Jose Y. M. C. A. last night 34-S. The
game was played on the Santa Clara
floor. A fast passing game with accur
ate basket shooting kept Santa Clara
in the lead from the start. The San
Jose players seldom got the ball down
to the Santa Clara basket.
