# NESTORIA TO DISCUSS GRADE POINT SYSTEM
## 
### 
Whether the present point system of
reckoning a student's scholastic standing
in the University is practicable, will be
discussed at an opening meeting of Nes
toria tonight at 7:30 in Room 12 of the
Law building. The recent disqualification
of eighty-five Stanford athletes brought
the discussion to a head. All who are
interested are invited to attend.
Nestoria members are urged to be pres
ent to make arrangements for taking the
society's Quad picture.
