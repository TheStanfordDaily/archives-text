# DOUG AND MARY ARE OUTBID FOR JACKIE COUGAN
## 
### (By United Press 
LOS ANGELES. Jan. 12.—Douglas
Fairbanks and Mary Pickford were
unsuccessful today in their efforts to
rbtain the services of Jackie Coogan,
eight-year-old millionaire film star.
Metro Film corporation with a last
minute offer outbid Doug and Mary
by a considerable margin.
Under the terms of the new offer
Jackie will get $500,000 outright and
a 60-40« share of the profits, and
Jackie's father will also produce the
film. Metro will advance production
costs.
