# Classical Club Hears Professor L.E. Bassett
## 
### 
Professor Lee Emerson Bassett of
the English department read before
the Classical Club last evening, selec
tions from the sonnets of the late
Lloyd Mifflin. Mifflin, considered by
many the greatest, of American sonnet
ists, composed more than six hundred
poems of the type, most of them on
classical subjects.
