# CAP AND GOWN WILL GIVE DANCE FRIDAY
## 
### 
A dance for the benefit of the Wo
men's Clubhouse fund will be given
next Friday evening under the au
spices of Cap and Gown in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Grinstead's orches
tra will furnish the music.
Dancing will not begin until after
the Stanford-Nevada game. This is
Cap and Gown's annual dance to aid
in paying the Clubhouse debt.
