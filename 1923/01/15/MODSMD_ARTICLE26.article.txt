# Skull and Snakes Propose Sophomore Honor Society
## 
### 
An Axe Society to guard the Stan
ford Axe and composed of Sophomores
who have made their numerals in
some freshman major sport will be
recommended to the student body by
Skull and Snakes. The plan is still in
definite, having first been suggested
at the meeting of Skull and Snakes
last night.
New members were elected to the
society and their initiation will take
place next Wednesday after a dinner
at the Zeta Psi house.
The recent scholastic disaster which
struck the campus was discussed from
various angles and plans were pro
posed for preventing its recurrence.
Dr. Williams and Judge Nourse, hon
orary members of the society, were
