# English Majors Refuse Citric Refreshments
## 
### 
There are oranges and oranges. This
particular type of the species flour
ishes into a latent state of ripeness
; about this time every year in the rear
of the English building. And every
time this happens a few inexperienced
members of the student body decide
to sample the Farm's product. Accord
ing to appearances in the immediate
vicinity these adventures have not met
with much success. The ground is
strewn with partially mutilated speci
mens of California's prize product,
while the sour looks on the faces of
those passing through the area aren't
due entirely to returned Econ. papers.
