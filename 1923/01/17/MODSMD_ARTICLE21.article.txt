# Lightweights Will Play Mountain View Tonight
## 
### 
The Cardinal 130-pound basketball
team will start their second game of
the season against the Mountain View
High School quintet in the Pavilion
at 7:30 tonight.
In their game with the Palo Alto
High School five Monday night, the
lightweights showed exceptional abil
ity and strength, which was aided by
the experience of the four veterans
from last year's championship squad.
The men who will probably take their
places In tonight's game are: Hobro
and Meyer, forwards; Blosser .and
Young, guards, with Shaw and Hol
lingsworth alternating at center.
