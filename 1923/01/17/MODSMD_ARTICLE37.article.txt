# Committee Chosen at Chemistry Mass Meeting
## 
### 
A committee was appointed to care
for the room in the Chemistry build
ing which was recently given to the
women in the Chemistry department
at the mass meeting of all women in
chemistry held yesterday afternoon.
The committee includes Ruth Fulton,
chairman; Ruth Newlin, secretary;
Evelyn Howard, Margaret McDowell,
and Miss Boulware, secretary of the
department as faculty member. Miss
Yost, Mrs. Sponslor, and Miss Boul
ware were the speakers.
