# SCHUBERT CLUB MAKES PLANS FOR CONCERT
## 
### 
Schubert Club will meet Thursday at
5 to practice for the concert which is to
be given by the club January 30. The
program has not definitely been arranged
as yet, but is to be announced soon. At
the meeting of the club held yesterday
it was decided to hold rehearsals every
Tuesday and Thursday until the concert.
