# Tryouts for Euphronia Freshman Team Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for Euphronia freshmen to
argue Wrangler freshmen in a debate
scheduled for the end of the month,
will be the principal part of Euphro
nia's regular meeting this evening at
7:30 in Room 12 of the Law building.
The subject of these tryouts, and of
the debate, is: Resolved, That Stan
ford should abolish professional
coaches for athletic competition. Try
out speeches will be limited to three
minutes.
According to Burt Palmer, president,
anyone who wishes to try out for mem
bership in the society may do so at
this meeting. Such tryoutees should
speak upon the same subject as above.
