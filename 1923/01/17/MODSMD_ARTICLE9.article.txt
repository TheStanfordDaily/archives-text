# Alumnae Tea Held Today In Women's Clubhouse
## 
### 
The second Resident Alumnae tea
of the quarter was held at 3 o'clock
this afternoon in the Women's Club
house. The hostesses were: Mrs. H.
Clay Miller, Mrs. Harry Haehl, Mrs.
R. B. Cherington, Mrs. J. P. Mitchell,
Mrs. Robert Kirkwood, Mrs. Wager
Bradford, Mrs. F. N. Fowler, and Miss
Elizabeth Thompson.
