# YOUNG TO COACH FOOTBALL AT U. OF PENNSYLVANIA
## 
### 
Louis Young has been named as the
new mentor of the University of
Pennsylvania football team, according
to a recent announcement made by
that Institution. Young, who was cap
tain of the 1913 team, succeeds John
W. Heisman, now with Washington
and Jefferson supplanting "Greasy"
Neale.
According to an announcement made
at the college, Young will have an en
tirely new situation to face. A call has
been sent out to scores of Penn stars
to go to Philadelphia for the purpose
of conferring with the coaching staff
In an effort to develop a concrete pro
gram for next year.
