# University of California Announces Fellowships
## 
### 
The College of Commerce of the Uni
versity of California has announced two
fellowships on the Flood Foundation
which will be available annually.
These fellowships are open to gradu
ates of institutions of recognized stand
ing, and are given for encouraging re
search in the field of business and re
lated subjects.
Applications for the Flood Fellowships
should be addressed to the Recorder of
the Faculties, University of California,
Berkeley, and should be filed not later
than the first of March.
