# PLAYERS MAY SIGN UP FOR TENNIS TOURNEY UNTIL NEXT MONDAY
## 
### 
Tennis players desiring to sign up for
the tennis tournament, which will com
mence next week, will be given until
Monday to enter their names, according
to Nels Nowell, tennis manager.
It was at first intended to close the
entries yesterday, but it was decided that
it would be advisable to allow more time.
Nowell expects to have nearly sixty
entries for the tournament by Monday.
Play in the first round will commence
next week as soon as the drawings can
be held.
On the basis of the results of this
tournament a ranking of the ten best
players in the University is to be pre
pared, and a freshman and a doubles
rating will also be made up.
