# AD CLUB ELECTS NEW OFFICERS AT MEETING
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Stanford Adver
tising Club Thursday afternoon, T. F.
Faucette, '23, was elected president to
fill the vacancy left by W. R. Gage, '24,
who resigned. R. L. Rothschild, '25, was
elected secretary and treasurer of the
club to replace C. I. Haley, '24,
Besides election of officers, members
discussed the policies and programs to
be pursued during the ensuing year.
