# Chester Hartlett to Talk With Student Volunteers
## 
### 
Chester Hartlett, traveling secretary of
the Student Volunteer Movement, will
be on the campus tonight and tomorrow.
Hartlett is giving a talk in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7 :30 this evening, and ev
ery one who is interested in foreign
Christian service is invited to come.
Tomorrow afternoon Hartlett is keep
ing his time free for individual interviews
with students who are interested in Vol
unteer work or in work similar fields. He
will be in the Y. M. C. A. office and ap
pointments should be secured by those
wishing to see him.
Hartlett is about to complete his tour of
visiting all the universities and colleges
in California. He graduated from Lau
rence College, Wisconsin, in 1921.
