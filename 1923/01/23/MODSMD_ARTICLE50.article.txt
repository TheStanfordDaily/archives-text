# Professor Davidson to Discuss Race Problem At Euphronia Meeting
## 
### 
"The Race Problem in America" is
the, topic to be discussed under the
leadership of Professor Davidson of
the Education department at the
meeting of Euphronia tonight at 7:30
in Room 12 of the Law building. The
meeting is to be held tonight owing
to the basketball game on Wednesday.
The freshman debaters of Euphronia
are preparing for the coming debate
with Wrangler's team. Rumors from
the camp of the Wranglers are that
the Wrangler freshmen are also in the
midst of great preparations. The ques
tion concerns the abolition of profes
sional coaches at Stanford.
