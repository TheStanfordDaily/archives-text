# Second Highway From San Francisco Is Now Assured
## 
### 
The legal obstruction to the proposed
highway to go down the .east side of the
peninsula, was removed yesterday when
District Attorney Swart told the San
Mateo Supervisors they could proceed
with the work. The agreement with San
Francisco county had been held up pend
ing the report of Swart on the legality
of the compact.
The last remaining details of the agree
ment will be worked out between the San
Francisco and San Mateo boards within
the next week.
