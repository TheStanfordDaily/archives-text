# One Campaign Per Quarter Ruling Is Still in Force
## 
### 
The Social Service Board has called
attention to the regulation providing
that there shall be only one general
appeal to members of the student body
for funds each quarter. So far as is
known the only organizations planning
to make a campaign this quarter are
the Y. M. C. A.and the Y. W. C. A. for
their local work.
The regulation in force prohibits
general, campaigns with personal so
licitation among the students except at
the one time approved by the Social
Service Board each quarter. There are
no restrictions on appeals made by
mail or on any general appeals not ac
companied by personal solicitation;
the regulation is interpreted as barring
out tag days. Appeals for small indi
vidual sums for student purposes are
under the jurisdiction of the Execu
tive Committee of the Associated Stu
dents and may be authorized by that
committee.
