# Case of Finnish Documents Is Added to War Library
## 
### 
A case of Finnish documents, bearing
on the government of Fnlaind and pre
sented by the Ministry of Foreign Af
fairs in that country, came into the
Hoover War Library this week. This
case is in addition to other material
wheh the Library has previously received
from the same country.
The large Finnish collection, which
this recent case supplements, was given
to Herbert Hoover himself two years
ago, and he had it placed on the shelves
of both the University Library and the
Hoover War Library.
