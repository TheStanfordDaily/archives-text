# Three Cardinal Soccer Teams to Play Saturday
## 
### 
Three Stanford soccer teams will be
engaged in contests Saturday, January
27. The Varsity will meet the Vikings
and the freshmen will clash with the
Palo Alto High School team on the
campus. The Cardinal second team goes
to San Francisco to play the Clan Mc-
Donald.
On the same day, the following games
will be played: California vs. Trafalgar
at San Mateo; Olympics vs. Barbarians
at San Francisco; and Trafalgar vs.
American Legion at Palo Alto. Advance
information indicates fast games in all
cases.
