# NO WILD CONSTERNATION OVER OFFER FOR DEMPSEY
## 
### (By United Press 
NEW YORK, Jan. 25— No wild con
sternation prevailed in Tin Ear Alley
here today over the million-dollar of
fer made to Jack Dempsey by Tom
O'Ronrke for three fights.
Habitues of the cauliflower patch
are skeptical. Million-dollar offers have
been heard in the past. They will be
heard in the future. Interest in the
current offer centered in—"where did
Tom ever get that much dough?"
