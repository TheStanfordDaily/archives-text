# Jupiter Pluvius Halts Outdoor Sports
## 
### 
To the heartless actions of Jupiter
Pluvius is due the desertion of the
tennis courts, the track field, and the
soccer fields during the last three
days. Swimming is the only outdoor
sport that is apparently unaffected.
Outdoor classes have been meeting in
the Gym and have larkely taken pos
session of the handball courts and the
basketball facilities. Indoor sports are
unimpaired so a great congestion has
resulted. No one has as yet approached
the various coaches as to their opin
ions regarding the wily Jupiter—no
one darep.
