# BEBE DANIELS DOING WELL AFTER APPENDIX REMOVAL
## 
### (By United Press 
NEW YORK, Jan. 29. —Bebe Dan
iels, movie star, underwent a success
ful operation for appendicitis at
Roosevelt hospital today.
It was stated at the hospital soon
after the operation that she was
"doing very well."
