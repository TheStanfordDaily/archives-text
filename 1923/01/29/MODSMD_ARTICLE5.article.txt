# POLA NEGRI IS VAMPED BY KING OF SLAPSTICK
## 
### (By United Press 
DEL MONTE, Cal., Jan. 29— Pola
Negri, who spurned the love of
princes, has been vamped at last —by
the king of slapstick comedians.
It will be the second matrimonial
venture of each of them. Chaplin was
formerly married to Mildred Harris,
while Pola's first husband was a Pol
ish count.
