# ENGLISH CLUB WILL START WORK ON PLAY
## 
### 
Committees to carry on the work of
"Will Shakespeare," the four-act drama
by Clemence Dane to be presented by the
English Club on April 6, will be organ
ized at a regular meeting of the organi
zation to be held Wednesday evening in
the Women's Clubhouse. Besides the
regular costume, tryout, and publicity
work, there will be certain other research
work on costumes and stagecraft to be
done. Director Richard Bentinck will be
present to assist the club in the prelim
inary organizing.
