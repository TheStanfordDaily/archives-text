# INITIAL ROUND OF TENNIS TOURNAMENT STARTS TOMORROW

All-University championship tennis
tournament will start tomorrow on the
Encina courts,
Over sixty men signed up for the
tourney and there will be plenty of com
petition for the championship.
A ranking of all the tennis players in
the University is to be prepared from the
results of this tournament. The best sin
gles, doubles, and freshmen teams will
be rated.

until next week.
