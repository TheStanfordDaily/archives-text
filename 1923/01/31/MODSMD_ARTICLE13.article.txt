# WOMEN WILL HEAR TURKISH TEACHER
## 
### 
Miss Henrietta Thompson, regional
secretary of the National Foreign Stu
dents League, will speak at the Y. W.
C. A. meeting at 5 o'clock Thursday
afternoon in the Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Thompson will speak of her
work with foreign students in San
Francisco, and of her personal work
with the students in Turkey. Refresh
ments wll be served after the talk.
