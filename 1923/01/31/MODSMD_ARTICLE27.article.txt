# Alumnae Tea to Be Held Tomorrow in Clubhouse
## 
### 
Reservations for bridge and tea have
been made for the regular weekly Resi
dent Alumnae tea to be held at 3 o'clock
tomorrow afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Hostesses for the afternoon include:
Mrs. Charles A. Shurtleff, Mrs. Frank
Hall, Mrs. R. R. Agnew, Mrs. Harry
Douglas, Mrs. Joseph Lorber, Mrs. Ed
win Tucker, and Mrs. James Sharp.
