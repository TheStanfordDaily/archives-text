# Tanforan Opens November 3
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO —Announcement
than Tanforan race track near San
Francisco will have its initial opening
November 3 with a 25-day racing pro
gram, exclusive of Sundays, was made
today. It marks the first plans for re
sumption of racing near San Francisco
in more than ten years.
