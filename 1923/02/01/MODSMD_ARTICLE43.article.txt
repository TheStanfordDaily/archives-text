# Sticky to Let Campus Buy in New Stock Issue
## 
### 
Campus investors will have a chance
soon to be stockholders in the biggest
business concern on the campus. The
Ernest Wilson Company is contem
plating an increase in its capital stock
because of its expansion in opening
new stores in San Francisco and
Stockton.
It is planned to increase the capi
talization to $600,000 to be divided into
4500 shares of common stock and
1500 shares of preferred stock at a
par value of $100 each. This is to in
clude 50 shares of preferred stock
which Palo Alto people will be given
an opportunity to buy.
A meeting of the Stockholders will
be held in Palo Alto, April 2, for the
purpose of authorizing the increase.
