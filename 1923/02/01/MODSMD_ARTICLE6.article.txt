# Colleges for Moonshiners Advocated
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Feb. I.—Government col
leges for training of moonshiners were
advocated today by Dr. Ben L. Reit
man, famous welfare worker, as a so
lution to the prohibition problem.
Doctor Reitman said lie based his
conclusions 011 findings he made in
connection with his work on the Chi
cago Board of Health. "More people
are dying daily in Chicago from poison
liquor than from any other cause." he
said.
SAN FRANCISCO. Feb. I.—Chief of
Police O'Brien today transmitted to
district commanders in the police de
partment a list of 480 places where he
has been informed liquor is sold, and
ordered that they be "cleaned up."
