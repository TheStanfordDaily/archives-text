# University Club Women Offer Loan Scholarship
## 
### 
An annual loan scholarship of $300
is offered by the Women's University
Club of Los Angeles to women of Los
Angeles county who wish to study at
Stanford University, the University of
California, Pomona College, Mills Col
lege, or Occidental College.
Application must be made through the
head of the school or college the candi
date is attending, and should be accom
panied by recommendations from three
teachers. The points to be considered
in selection arc health, character, schol
arship and financial need.
