# FRESHMAN JOLLY-UP TOMORROW EVENING
## 
### 
The members of, the freshman basket
ball team arc to be guests of the class
at the Freshman Jolly-Up. to be given
at the Women's Clubhouse tomorrow
evening. The music will start a few
minutes after the game with U. S. C.
The dancing will commence with a
grand march, and the dances are to be
alternately tag and date dances. An en
tertainment will be given during inter
mission. The dance is to be formal.
At the meeting of the class, it was de
cided to give the freshmen the privilege
of wearing numerals for fencing and
boxing.
Lists of those going to the freshman
game at Berkeley February 10
