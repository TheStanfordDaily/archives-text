# Big Joke on Uncle Sam
## 
### (By United Press 
SAN FRANCISCO—Big joke on
Uncle Sam. He sold the gunboat York
town of Dewey's Manila Bay fleet, for
junk. Wreckers, dismantling her here,
found $5000 in Spanish gold pieces
hidden in the engine room.
PITTSBURGH, Pa., Feb. I.—Penn
sylvania crude oil and other grades
in the Eastern market were advanced
ten cents a barrel today for the fourth
time this year. Prediction was made
that $4 oil will be reached within 30
days.
