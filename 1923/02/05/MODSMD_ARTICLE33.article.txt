# ELEVEN MATCHES IN NET TOURNEY PLAYED
## 
### 
Eleven matches were played last
week in the first round of the Lindley
Murray tennis tournament.
Most of the sets were taken by lop
sided scores and no fast tennis re
sulted. All of the varsity tennis men
who played came through without dif
ficulty.
The result of the matches were as
follows:
Anderson defeated Freidenrich, 6-1,
2-6, 6-4.
Hinckley defeated Huneke, 6-1, 6-3.
Hess defeated Willis by default.
Oatman defeated Weigel, 6-1, 6-4.
Bledsoe defeated Eller, 6-4, 6-1.
Stiles defeated Helm, 6-1, 6-1.
De Bach defeated Steffen, 6-1, 6-3.
Ho defeated Rowles, 6-4, 4-6, 6-3.
Overfeldt defeated Hofflund, 6-1, 6-3.
Davidson defeated Otis, 6-0, 6-2.
Fairchild defeated Welch, 6-1, 6-0.
