# Fellowship Committee Will Hold Candy Sale
## 
### 
The World Fellowship Committee will
sell candy between 9 and 12 o'clock to
morrow morning for the purpose of rais
ing money to buy a share in a college of
the Orient.
Vassar, Smith, and Bryn Mawr have
"sister colleges" in the Far East and Stan
ford needs $28 for a share in one. The
Fellowship Committee raises all of its
money by candy sales on the campus.
