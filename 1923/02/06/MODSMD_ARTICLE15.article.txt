# Euphronia Holds Try-Outs For Second Round Debates
## 
### 
Tryouts for the second round of de
bates in the League of College Debating
Societies will occupy the main portion
of the program for the meeting of Eu
phronia tomorrow evening in Room 12
of the Law building. Euphronia will
meet the Santa Clara "Philhistorians" at
Stanford, February 24.
The subject for the tryouts will be the
same as that of the debate: Resolved,
That California should adopt a court of
industrial relations with duties and pow
ers similar to that of the Kansas In
dustrial Court. Speeches will be limited
to five minutes, with three minutes for
rebuttal.
