# Industrial Relations Court Discussed at Meeting of Euphronia
## 
### 
With the debate with the Santa
Clara "Philliistorians" scheduled for
February 24, Euphronia will hold try
outs for a team at their meeting this
evening in Room 12 of the Law build
ing. The subject will be: Resolved,
That California should adopt a court
of industrial relations with duties and
powers similar to that of the Kansas
Industrial Court. Speeches will be live
minutes, with three minutes for re
buttal.
This subject has attracted a great
deal of attention throughout the Uni
ted States, as well as California. The
question of the establishment of in
dustrial courts has been widely dis
cussed by both capital and labor.
