Trustee Buys Land
Leland Cutler, '06, trustee of Stanford University, has
purchased a large tract of the Oro Rancho at Gridley.
The land is to be planted to peaches, prunes and wal
nuts.

Elizabeth McGee, Paul Stewart Engaged
The engagement of Elizabeth McGee, '21, to Paul
Stewart, '22, was announced at a tea given by Frances
Merrill, '22, at the Menlo Park Country Club Saturday.
Miss McGee is a member of Cap and Gown and Delta
Gamma. Stewart belongs to the local chapter of Phi
Delta Theta.
