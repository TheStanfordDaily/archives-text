# M.E. Society Will Visit San Francisco Refinery
## 
### 
The Mechanical Engineering Society
is planning a trip of inspection to the
Western Sugar Refinery in San Fran
cisco next Saturday, February 17. All
members desiring to go should hand
in their names to Claude Peavy.
From now on meetings of the society
will be held regularly every second
Tuesday, beginning February 13, from
7 to 8 p. m. It is the plan to have
members of the faculty and other
speakers give talks at each meeting.
Tuesday, P. Sandretto and H. John
will address the society.
