# Professor Mitchell Will Give Vocational Lecture
## 
### 
"Possibilities for Women in the
Physical Sciences" will be the subject
of a talk by Professor John Pearce
Mitchell of the Chemistry department
next Thursday afternoon in the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 5:30.
This is the second of a series of lec
tures which are planned to assist wo
men of the University in deciding upon
suitable vocations.
