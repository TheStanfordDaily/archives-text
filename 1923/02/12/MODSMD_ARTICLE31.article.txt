# THEATER WORKSHOP CLASS HEARS TALK ONSTAGE TECHNIQUE
## 
### 
Dickson Morgan, '16, scenic director
of four Pacific Coast theaters, lectured
to Richard Bentinck's Theater Work
shop class on the technique of the the
ater at the regular meeting of the class
in the Little Theater Thursday after
noon. Morgan is connected with the
Alcazar Theater of San Francisco and
the Majestic Theater of Los Angeles.
According to Morgan, the college
man's best opportunity to work into
technical theatrical work is by getting a
job as an assistant scenic director. Mor
gan, who had his first professional ex
perience with the movies, got his start
as assistant scenic director of the Ma
jectic Theater.
