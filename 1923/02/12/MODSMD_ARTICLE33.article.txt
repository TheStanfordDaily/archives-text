# Development of Socialism Discussed by History Club
## 
### 
A discussion of the development of
Socialism in various countries was held
at the meeting of the History Club
Thursday evening in the Women's Club
house.
The discussion on the rise of Socialism
in Russia was led by Mervin Crobaugh,
on the development in England, by Row
land Harvey, and on movements in Hun
gary by Robert Binkley.
