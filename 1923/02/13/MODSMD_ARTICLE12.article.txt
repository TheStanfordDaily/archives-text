# BOY SCOUT TROOP IS FORMED BY Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
A Boy Scout troop has lately been or
ganized on the campus by the Y. M. C. A.
The group is composed of twenty-two
boys, nearly all of whom are professors'
sons. James Sparling, '22, has been ap
pointed scoutmaster.
This troop has had three meetings al
ready, all of which took place on the
regular week-end bikes back into the
foothills. Last Saturday they hiked to
Searsville lake and spent a few hours
learning scoutcraft under the instruction
of tbe scoutmaster. Preliminary tests
will begin very soon.
