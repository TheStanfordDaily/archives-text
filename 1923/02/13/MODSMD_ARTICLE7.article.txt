# SENIORS WILL GIVE FIRST JOLLY-UP OF QUARTER TONIGHT
##

ter is scheduled for this evening from 7
j until 9 in the Women's Clubhouse. This
is the first of a series of get-togethers
that the senior class is planning.
Air is supposed to be the only free
commodity on this campus, but the
seniors have added to this promising list
the price of admission to the jolly-up.
