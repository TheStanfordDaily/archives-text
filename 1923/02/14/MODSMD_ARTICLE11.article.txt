# TEMPLETON APPOINTS MARATHON CAPTAINS
## 
### 
Coach Templeton has appointed the
class team captains for the annual Irish
Marathon to he held on March 3.
Blink Williamson will lead the senior
tracksters, Charley Daley is to captain
the juniors, Ralph Shermund is in
charge of the sophomores and Rill Rich
ardson will pilot the '26 men.
The team captains would like to have
their men report to them as soon as pos
sible in order that they may see what
material they are going to have.
