# Scholarship Is Reward for Essay on Colonial History
## 
### 
A scholarship carrying a stipend of
$100, given by the Colonial Dames of
America, resident in California, will be
awarded to a student of Stanford for
the best essay on a subject in American
Colonial history.
The subject for the present year is
"Life on the Colonial Frontier in the
Middle of the Eighteenth Century." The
award is open to any student who has
had college work in American Colonial
History. This is History 21. Only un
dergraduates may be candidates. The
papers must be in before April 5.
The jury of awards will consist of
three members of the Stanford faculty.
For further information, see Professor
Egdar E. Robinson, Room 237, History
building.
