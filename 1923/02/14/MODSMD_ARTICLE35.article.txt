# President Wilbur to Speak In Community Chest Drive
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
speak in behalf of the Community Chest
drive to be launched in Palo Alto at a
meeting to be held at 8 o clock tomor
row night in the Community House.
The Community Chest drive is now
being carried on in San Francisco, San
Jose, and other cities about the bay. To
morrow night will begin the drive in
Palo Alto.
