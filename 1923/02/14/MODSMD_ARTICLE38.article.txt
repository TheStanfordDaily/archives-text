# Dr. Henry B. Learned Goes To Congress for Stanford
## 
### 
Dr. Henry Barrett Learned has ac
cepted an appointment to represent Stan
ford at the Fifth International Congress
of the Historical Sciences at Brussels
in April. Dr. Learned has been con
nected with the History department as
an acting professor on several occasions.
At Brussels he will act with Professor
F. A. Colder as joint representative of
Stanford.
