# MANAGERS OF INTERCLASS BASEBALL TO ARRANGE SCHEDULE
## 
### 
Plans for an interclass baseball tourna
ment will be made at a meeting of the
class baseball managers Jo be held to
morrow afternoon at 4 with Intramural
Director C. W. Davis in Encina Gym.
Practice will start Monday and the
schedules will be opened the first of
March and continue for two weeks. By
this arrangement the contests will be
over before examination week.
The class managers will appoint a
committee to draw up the rules and
regulations for the conduct of the inter
class games or else undertake the work
themselves. A schedule of games will
be made and eligibility rules worked out.
