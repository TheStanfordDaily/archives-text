# CAP AND GOWN WILL STAGE SECOND FRISK
## 
### 
Cap and Gown will stage the second
of the Friday "frisks" tomorrow after
noon in the Women's Clubhouse at 4 :30.
Grinsiead's Orchestra will furnish the
music for the hour dance.
Cap .and Gown is sponsoring these in
formal dances on alternate Friday after
noons to give the students a chance to
get acquainted. The success of the. first
"frisk" two weeks ago indicate their
becoming an institution on the campus.
Admission is always 15 cents and Quad
clothes only will be worn.
