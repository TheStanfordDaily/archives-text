# Women's Economics Club Elects New Committee
## 
### 
A program committee for the Wo
men's Economics Club was appointed
at a special meeting held Monday in
the Women's Clubhouse. The commit
tee consists of Mabel Jones, '23, chair
man; Louise Wilber, '23, and Joseph
ine Scott. '23.
The club is planning to help enter
tain at a Washington party with the
Americanization class, at the Commun
ity House, February 20, and a pro
gram for this event is being arranged.
