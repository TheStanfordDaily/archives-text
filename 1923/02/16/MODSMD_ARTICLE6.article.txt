# Professor Alden Gives Talks to School Children
## 
### 
Professor Raymond Macdonald Al
den. of the English department, who
is in the East, gave talks to the chil
dren of the elementary schools and
the students of Cornell University, at
Ithaca, New York, last week. Accord
ing to the paper of that city, the
school children were delighted with
the talk and the stories which he read
to them from his new book of chil
dren's stories, "The Boy Who Found
the King." Professor Alden is also
the author of "Why the Chimes
Rang," a Christmas allegory.
