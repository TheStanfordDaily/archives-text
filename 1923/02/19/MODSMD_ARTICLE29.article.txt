# PROFESSOR TREAT TO LECTURE IN PASADENA
## 
### 
Professor Payson Treat, head of the
History department, left for Pasadena
today where he will give an address
on "Japan Since the Washington
Conference" before members of the
Pasadena Lecture Course. The Pasa
dena Lecture Course is a series of
lectures delivered by different au
thorities on subjects of national im
portance. Professor Treat will return
to the campus Thursday evening.
