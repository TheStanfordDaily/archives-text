# PRESIDENT WILBUR LEAVES UNIVERSITY ON EASTERN TRIP
## 
### 
President Wilbur has gone East on
a trip which will take him as far as
New York on various items of Uni
versity business. On his return west
ward he will attend the meeting of
the Association of Medical Colleges
at the University of Michigan, on
March 1 and 2 and a meeting of the
Council on Medical Education at Chi
cago a few days later.
