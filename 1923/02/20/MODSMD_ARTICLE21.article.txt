# WE ARE OFFERING—
## 
### 
Thursday is a University holiday and studies will
not interfere with pleasure for those who go to
Berkeley Wednesday night. Jf you saw Saturday
night's battle you don't have to he told that the next
will provide its qdota of thrills. Jf you didn't, you
are foolish to let another such opportunity go by.
The score was close enough to insure loyal sup
port for the Californians. Stanford should have a
large enough representation at least to drown out
the shouting of the Hears—but that will mean get
ting on the scene early.
