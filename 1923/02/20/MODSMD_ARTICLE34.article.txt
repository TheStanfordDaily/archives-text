# Dean Yost to Return From Conference in March
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost, who has been in the
East since the last of January, is ex
pected back on March 12. Miss Yost
attended a Conference of Deans of
Women at Cleveland, where she read a
paper on the extra-mural activities of a
Dean. She also visited several universi
ties and is stopping at her home in
Staunton, Virginia, on her return trip.
