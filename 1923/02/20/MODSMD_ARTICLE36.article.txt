# Professor Alden Returns Home From Eastern Trip
## 
### 
Professor Raymond Mcdonald Alden
of the English department arrived home
Saturday after a prolonged trip East,
where he attended a meeting of the As
sociation of Modern Languages at Phila
delphia. Professor Alden visited a num
ber of Eastern colleges on his trip, and
spent several days in Florida. He will
resume his work at the University next
quarter.
