# PROFESSOR COTTRELL GIVES TALK AT VISALIA
## 
### 
Professor E. A. Cottrell of the Politi
cal Science department gave a talk to
the men and women of Visalia who are
of age at an evening session on Wash
ington's birthday.
When speaking of the trip he said,
"This is the first time a talk on the
duties and privileges of citizenship has
been planned for the benefit of the home
citizens, although many such talks have
been thought necessary for the nat
uralized citizens."
