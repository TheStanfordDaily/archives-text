# Head of Marine Station Will Return to Campus
## 
### 
Walter K. Fisher, director of the
Hopkins Marine Station and associate
professor of Zoology, will return to the
campus February 28, according to word
received recently by the Zoology de
partment. Prof. Fisher has been con
ducting investigation at the Marine
Station which is situated at Pacific
Grove along Monterey Bay. In the
Summer Quarter Prof. Fisher is to give
courses in the Classification and Ecolo
ogy of Marine Invertebrates and in
Zoology Research that the Hopkins Ma
rine Station.
