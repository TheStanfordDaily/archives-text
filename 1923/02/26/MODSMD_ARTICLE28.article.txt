# No More Music Murder
## 
### 
Singing of Stanford songs has declined
to a stage where it no longer exists. This
horrible realization was brought out at
the last basketball game and something
must be done to establish such singing
in the University. With our teams fight
ing all of the time, we must help them
achieve victory, and it is here that proper
singing, which inspires enthusiasm and
confidence can be of great value. I am
working out a plan whereby the student
body will learn Stanford songs and act
ually sing them. The band will cooper
ate and we should all do so for our own
good.
Yours for less lead and more pepper,
LOU MINSKY,
Yell Leader.
