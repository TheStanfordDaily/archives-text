# "NIP" McHOSE WLIL CAPTAIN BASKETBALL VARSITY NEXT YEAR
## 
### 
J. C. ("Nip") McHose, '24, was
elected captain of next year's bas
ketball varsity yesterday.
McHose played forward on the
1924 freshman quintet and has
played the same position on the var
sity for the past two seasons. He
has been the most important cog in
the scoring machine during the past
season.
He is a member of Skull and
Snakes, Hammer and Coffin, and
Phi Delta Theta.
