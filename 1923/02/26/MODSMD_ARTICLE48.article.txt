# Professor Coester Will Speak to Spanish Club
## 
### 
The Spanish Club meeting which will
be held at the Women's Clubhouse Feb
ruary 28 will include dancing from 7 to
8, and an illustrated lecture on Spain by
Professor Alfred Coester of the Romanic
Languages department.
Professor Coester's lecture on the peo
ple and the customs of Spain will be in
English and any one who is interested
in the subject is invited to attend. Re
freshments will be served at the close
of the program.
