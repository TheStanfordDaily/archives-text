# Senator Jones to Speak On Legislative Problems
## 
### 
Senator Herbert C. Jones, chairman
of the State Judiciary Committee, and
chairman of the Jones Committee for
investigation of election expenditures,
will speak on "Legislative Problems"
at 7:30 o'clock Thursday night in the
Little Theater.
Senator Jones graduated from Stan
ford University in 1902, and received

his degree of LL.D. from the Univer
sity in 1914. He is now an attorney-at
law in San Jose.
The lecture Thursday night is given
under the auspices of the Law depart
ment, but is open to all students inter
ested in the subject.
