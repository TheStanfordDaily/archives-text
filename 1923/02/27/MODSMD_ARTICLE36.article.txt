# ENCINA CLUB TO STAGE BASKETBALL DANCE
## 
### 
At the regular Monday night meeting
of the Encina Club, President Johnnie
Lane announced the second Encina dance
of the quarter to be given by the club
on March 9. The dance is especially for
upperclassmen. In accordance with the
usual custom, the basketball team has
been invited and will be the guests of
the club. The dance will l>e the last
dance before the exams, the club limiting
itself to two dances a quarter.
