# MARATHON RUNNERS TO REPORT TODAY
## 
### 
All men who have competed for
their class in previous Irish Marathons
are expected to compete again this
year, according to Track Manager Sam
j Applewhite.
Those who intend to enter the event
should see their class captains this
'afternoon at 4:15 in front of the
track house. Tryouts for the class team
will be held every day this week on
the Stanford track and the class cap
tains are desirous that prospective
members of the various teams should
work out every afternoon this week.
