# Roth Goes to Los Angeles In Interest of Endowment
## 
### 
Comptroller Roth left yesterday for
Los Angeles in the interest of the en
dowment fund. He is to speak at a
luncheon today before a group made
up largely of Stanford alumni. Comp
troller Roth will return to the campus
tomorrow.
