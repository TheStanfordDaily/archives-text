# Chemists to Give Smoker For Guests This Evening
## 
### 
An invitational smoker and entertain
ment will be given tonight by Alpha Chi
Sigma at 7:30 in the old Union. The
members of the society will treat them
selves and their guests to smokes, cats
and humorous stunts by outside talent.
Several talks are to be given on various
subjects of chemical nature. Professor
E. C. Franklin of the Chemistry de
partment will be the main speaker of the
evening.
