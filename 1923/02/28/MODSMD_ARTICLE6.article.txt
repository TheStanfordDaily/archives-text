# Student Secretary Will Address Women Thursday
## 
### 
Miss Elsie Heller, national student
secretary of the Y. W. C. A. for the
Northwest, will speak at the Y. W. C.
A. meeting to be held Thursday at
5 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse on
women in public affairs. Miss Heller
has been attending the mid-winter con
ference of the Y. W. C. A. in Berkeley.
The nominating committee will pre
sent the following nominations: Helen
Schardin, '24, president; Helen Whit
ney, '24, vice president; Ruth Lee
Spilman, '24, secretary; Ruth Newlin,
'24, treasurer; Eunice Biddle. '24,
undergraduate field representative.
Those who desire to make nominations
from the floor will be given a chance
to do so.
