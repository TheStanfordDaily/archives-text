# GREENLY GIVES TALK ON HAWAIIAN AFFAIRS
## 
### 
Maurice G. Greenly addressed the Edu-'
cation Club last evening on "Meeting !
Our Oriental Neighbors in Honolulu."
Greenly, who is now a student in the;
Education department, taught school in
Honolulu for several years and there
fore understands conditions in Hawaii.
He also outlined the Japanese question
briefly. After the formal talk, a gen
eral discussion was held and Greenly
discussed the difficulties and opportuni-:
ties of the teacher in Hawaii.
There will be no more meetings of the
Education Club this quarter.
