# Organ Recitals
##

Mrs. J. L. Cowger, soprano, will assist
Warren D. Allen, University organist, in
the organ recital at 4 o'clock Sunday
afternoon in Memorial Church. Mrs.
Cowger will sing two solos. Following
is the program:
Solemn Prelude T. Tertius Noble
I Chorale-Prelude, "O Man, Bewail Thy
Fearful Sin" Bach
I Three Bible Songs Dvorak
Abendlied (Evening Song).... Schumann
(Carrillon Vierne
