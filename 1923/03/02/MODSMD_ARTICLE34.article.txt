# SIX MEN CHOSEN TO COMPETE FOR YELL LEADER ASSISTANTS
## 
### 
"Dutch" McCallister, "Red" Bell, Les
lie Peter, "Cal" Conron, Oscar Trippit,
and Howard Booth were selected unani
mously by the rally committee last night
to run as candidates for assistant yell
leaders in a student body election to be
held some time soon.
After a try-out period lasting for six
weeks, during which time the squad of
candidates was put through three weeks
of instruction, and workouts at three
basketball games, the trvoutees were all
at their best yesterday evening. Accord
ing to "Lou" Minsky, yell leader, it was
with some difficulty that the committee
was able to choose the six best prospects.
