# Women Will Hear Talks On Education, Psychology
## 
### 
The possibilities for positions after
college for women who major in psy
chology or education will be discussed
by Miss Margaret Leme of the Psy
chology department and Professor John
Almack of the Education department,
respectively, at the Vocational Confer
ence meeting tomorrow at 5 in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. These meetings, which
are under the direction of the vocational
committee of the Y. W. C. A., are in
tended to give those women who have not
yet selected a vocation an idea of the
preparation which the various depart
ments here give for work after gradu
ation from college.
