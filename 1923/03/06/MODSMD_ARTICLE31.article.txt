# Professor Coester Will Speak to Spanish Club
## 
### 
Professor Alfred Coester of the Ro
manic Languages department will give
an illustrated lecture on Spain at the
Spanish Club meeting tomorrow night
at the Women's Clubhouse. The pro
gram will include dancing from 7 to 8.
Professor Coester's lecture on the peo
ple and the customs of Spain will be in
English, and any one who is interested
is invited to attend. Professor Coester
was decorated by the Spanish government
when he was traveling in Spain, where
he collected the pictures which will be
used in the lecture. Refreshments will
be served at the close of the program.
