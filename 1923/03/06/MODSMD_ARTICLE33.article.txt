# Newly-Elected Officers to Preside at Lens and Plate
## 
### 
At the Wednesday evening meeting
of the Lens and Plate Society, the re
cently elected officers will preside. The
officers at the last meeting were:
President, Kenneth Ritchie; vice presi
dent, Philip Hooker; secretary-treas
urer, Gale Griswold. Ritchie was re
elected to his office, while the others
take office for the first time. Griswold
announces that a photographic tour of
the campus is being planned but the
complete arrangements have not been
formulated.
