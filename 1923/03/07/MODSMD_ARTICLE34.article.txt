# DR. JORDAN TO ADDRESS ENCINA CLUB SUNDAY
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will speak before Encina
Club at 7 :30 Sunday evening, according
to the announcement by John Lane,
president of Encina. Dr. Jordan has been
accustomed for several years to giving
addresses on certain Sunday evenings to
the club, when he is at the University.
Talks by Dr. Jordan usually occur every
other Sunday. The last address to En
cina was on February 18.
