# Nominations Are Open For Y.W.C.A. Officers
## 
### 
Nominations for the officers of the
Y. W. C. A. will be received until to
morrow evening. All nominations must
be signed and may be given either to
Miss Woolsey or to Marion Potter, '22.
Voting will be held next Monday
and Tuesday by means of ballot boxes
in the Women's Clubhouse in which
voters may place their ballots. Only
members of the Y. W. C. A. are en
titled to vote.
