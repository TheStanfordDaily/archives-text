# MARINE STATION HEAD LECTURES ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
Professor Walter K. Fisher of the
Zoology Department, director of the
Hopkins Marine Station at Monterey
Bay, left San Francisco yesterday af
ternoon after a visit on the campus of
only a few hours. Professor Fisher ar
rived here in time to give his lecture
on the "Geographical Distribution of
Animals" to the advanced Biology
class at 8 o'clock Wednesday morning.
Early in the afternoon, he took the
train for San Francisco. Professor
Fisher will return to the campus Fri
day morning.
