# J.L. Baker, '25, Will Play World Billiard Champion
## 
### 
Ralph Grcenleaf, world's champion
pocket billiard player, will engage in two
exhibition matches at the United Cigar
Stores' tables in Palo Alto this after
noon and evening. The champion is said
to be in good form, after recent matches
in San Francisco, in which he several
times made runs above the 100 mark.
J. L. Baker, '25, an engineering stu
dent. has been asked to hold the other
cue during the evening game, which will
start at 8 o'clock. Rack pool will be
played.
