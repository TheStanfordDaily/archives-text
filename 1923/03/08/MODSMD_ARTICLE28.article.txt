# Tomorrow Is Final Day To Submit Opera Plots
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the final day for submit
ting Junior Opera plots, according to
John Richer, '24, chairman of the com
mittee. Manuscripts should be turned in
to him at the A. T. O. house.
Five plots have been received to date

by the committee. Their authors are
Hal Rorke, '24, and Carl Shoup, '24, Nor
ris James, '24, Charles Cross, '24, Mau
rine Voerge, '22, and Mildred Dannen
baum, '24.
The opera will be chosen this week
end by the committee, and announcement
of the successful manuscript made either
Monday or Tuesday.
