# COACH WOLTER MAKES FIRST BASEBALL CUT
## 
### 
Coach Harry Wolter got out the pro
verbial axe yesterday afternoon and
lopped fifteen men from the varsity base
ball squad. It was the first cut of the
season, and takes effect tomorrow.
The men surviving the cut are: Wood
ward, Peavy, Roberts, Heckendorf, Mul
chay, Deffebach, Carver, McCandless,
Parker, Lawson, May, Follansbee, Oliva,
Patterson, Fuller, Huneke, Teague,
Clark, Solomon, Lowenstein, and Kol
berg.
