# Organ Recitals
## 
### 
Charles Bulotti, tenor, and Austin
Sperry, baritone, will assist Warren D.
Allen, University organist, in the organ
recital Sunday afternoon at 4 o'clock
in Memorial Church. Each will sing a
solo and they will sing one duet. Fol
lowing is the program:
Chorale-Prelude, "We All Believe in
One God, Creator" Bach
Agnus Dei Henry Hadley
Mr. Sperry
The Crucifix Faure
Mr. Bulotti and Mr. Sperry
"My Hope Is in the Everlasting" (from
"The Daughter of Jairus" Stainer
Mr. Bulotti.
Told at Sunset (from the "Woodland
Sketches") MacDowell
Chant de printempe (Song of Spring)
Joseph Bonnet
