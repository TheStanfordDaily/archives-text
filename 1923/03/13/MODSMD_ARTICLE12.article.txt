# Boxers and Wrestlers to Meet Southern California
## 
### 
The members of the boxing and
wrestling teams are working out every
night in preparation for the matches
with the University of Southern Cali
fornia which will be held at Los An
geles on March 24. Nine boxers and
six wrestlers will participate in the
tourney with the Trojans. It is possible
that a meet will be arranged with the
Los Angeles Athletic club while the
Stanford men are in the southern city.
The boxers who were picked to meet
California will probably make the trip,
although Coach Harry Maloney has not
definitely decided upon the men he will
use. Wrestling Coach Vaughn Hose,
pian will announce his team this after
noon.
