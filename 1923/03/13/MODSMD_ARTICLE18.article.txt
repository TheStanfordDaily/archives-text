# CARD TENNIS PLAYERS DEFEAT SUTTER CLUB
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity tennis team defeated
the Sutter Tennis Club of Sacramento
on the local courts Sunday afternoon
by winning seven of the nine matches
played. The loss of two matches Sunday
was due to the absence of de Bach and
Tussing, ranking third and first on the
varsity squad.
Harold Overfelt, '25, surprised every
one by defeating Iveuchler in straight
sets, 6-3, 6-3. Leeds of Stanford was
beaten, 3-6, 6-2. 6-2, in the singles and
in the doubles Davis and McCleave were
defeated. 9-7, 6-8, 2-6.
The men who played for Stanford
were Davis, Mertz, Hinckley, Williard,
Overfelt, Leeds, and McCleave.
