# Professor West to Give Talk on Work of Congress
## 
### 
"The achievements of the Sixty-Sev
enth congress in the light of the prom
ises made by the Republican party in
the campaign of 1920" will be the main
topic of Professor Victor J. West's
lecture that is to be given this evening
in the Little Theater. The lecture will
start at 7:30.
Professor West will also discuss the
organization of congress in relation to
the question of leadership in legisla
tion. The Budget Law and how the
business of the Sixty-Seventh congress
differs from that performed by pre
ceding legislatures will be some of the
other main points brought nut in the
lecture.
