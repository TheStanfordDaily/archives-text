# Robert Meyers, '25, Will Captain Water Polo Team
## 
### 
Robert P. Meyers, '25, was elected
captain of the water polo team for
next season at a meeting held imme
diately after the California game last
Friday.
Meyers won his numerals in water
polo and swimming last year 011 the
freshman teams and this year played
forward 011 the varsity. He scored
three of the seven points made against
the Rears last week and is expected to
do most of the scoring in the games
to be played at Chicago next month.
