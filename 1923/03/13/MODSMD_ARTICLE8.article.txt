# Resident Alumnae Meet Tomorrow in Clubhouse
## 
### 
Resident Alumnae will hold an im
portant business meeting at 4:30 to
morrow afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse, following the regular
Wednesday tea, at which Miss Harker,
assisted by her teachers, will be the
hostess.
The question of the use of the tea
fund money will be discussed at the
meeting, and it will be decided wheth
er to use it to help pay off the Club
house debt or for further improve
ments.
