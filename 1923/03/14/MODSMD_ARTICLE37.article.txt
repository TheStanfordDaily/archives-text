# ENDOWMENT CAMPAIGN NEAR END AT SOUTHERN COLLEGE
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN
CALIFORNIA—The Endowment cam
paign among University students has
nearly reached the 100 per cent point, ac
cording to reports from the treasurer.
The net subscriptions of the campaign
are over $100,000. Ten million dollars
is the amount to be subscribed in the nexe
few years by both students and alumni
in an Endowment Drive originated here
a few weeks ago.
