# French Foreign Policy Is Joffre Debate Topic
## 
### 
"French Foreign Policy on the Con
tinent of Europe since the Treaty of
Versailles" is the general topic which
has been accepted for the annual
Joffre debate with California. The con
test will be held in the Assembly hall
this year on Friday, April 13.
The Stanford speakers will be
Thomas Bailey, '24 Mathew Tobriner,
'24; Bertrand Comparet, '24, and Bert
Levit, '24, alternate.
