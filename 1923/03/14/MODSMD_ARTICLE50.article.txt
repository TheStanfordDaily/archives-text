# Euphronia Elects Gross As President for Spring
## 
### 
Euphronia elected officers for the
spring quarter at their weekly meeting
in Room 12 of the Law building last
Wednesday night. Dick Gross, '24,
was elected president; Percy Spen
cer, '25. vice president; Frank Crist,
'24, secretary, and Walter Odemar,
'26, treasurer.
