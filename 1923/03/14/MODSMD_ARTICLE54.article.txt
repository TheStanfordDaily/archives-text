# Chicago Professor to Give Celtic Course Here
## 
### 
Tom Pe-ete Cross, widely known as
a scholar in Celtic ltierature, will be
a member of the Stanford English De
partment next quarter and will give
two courses in his special field. One
will be a general course for graduate
students on the origins of the Roman
tic Movement.
This is not Professor Cross's first
experience at Stanford since he was a
member of the English faculty here in
the summer quarter of 1920.
He is connected with the University
of Chicago and at that time had the
title of Professor of English and Celtic
Literature there. Since then he has
been placed in charge of the work in
Comparative Literature at Chicago.
