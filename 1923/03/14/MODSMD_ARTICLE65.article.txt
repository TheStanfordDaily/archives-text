# Japanese Doctors Visit Here
## 
### 
A commission of Japanese medical
scientists who are touring the United
States under the auspices of the
Rockefeller Foundation were guests
on the Stanford campus yesterday.
Thoy were entertained at luncheon at
the president's house by President and

Mrs. Wilbur, and inspected the Uni
versity plant and equipment, especially
the departments having to do with
medicine.
Miss Stoner, Director of Athletics
at Mills, Mary Leonard, Eunice Biddle,
Dorothy Williams, and Virginia Hall
also spoke.
