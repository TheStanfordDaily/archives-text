# Memorial Library Fund Receives Subscriptions
## 
### 
Subscriptions to the Charles Andrews
Huston Memorial Library hind have
reached the sum of $1,268, according to
reports of the committee in charge of
the campaign. This amount represents
the contributions of 194 subscribers.
Letters are now being sent out to about
eight hundred Law school alumni, in an
effort to reach all graduates interested
in subscribing to the fund. Contribu
tions are secured only from past and
present Stanford law students.
