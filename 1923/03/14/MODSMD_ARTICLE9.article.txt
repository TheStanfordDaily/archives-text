# Research Club to Meet; Two Papers Will Be Read
## 
### 
The Research Club will meet in the
Faculty Clubhouse on Wednesday even
ing at 8 o'clock to hear Professor H. R.
Fairclough read a paper on "The Au
thorship of the So-called Minor Poems
of Virgil," while Dr. J. M. Stillman will
take as his subject, "Pctrus Bonus and
Certain Chemical Forgeries."
Dr. Stillman is Professor Emeritus of
the Department of Chemistry, and is the
only professor who camc to Stanford
University at the request of Senator
Stanford. Professor H. R. Fairclough
has conducted research work in classical
literature for a number of years.
