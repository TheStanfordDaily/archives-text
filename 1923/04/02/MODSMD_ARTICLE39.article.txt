# Stanford Clubs Will Give Track Meet Dinner Dance
## 
### 
After the track meet with the Uni
versity of California on April 21, Stan
ford people will gather at the Hotel
Fairmont in San Francisco for dinner
and dancing under the auspices of the
Stanford Clubs of San Francisco.
Table reservations may be sent to the
hotel now with the request to be placed
in the Stanford group. The south end of
the main dining room and the Gray
Room will be used for this occasion.
Those who wish to dine at home may
come to the hotel later to visit and dance
with their Stanford friends.
