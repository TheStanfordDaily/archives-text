# President Wilbur to Talk At Masonic Meeting
## 
### 
President Wilbur, Fred Prince Jr.,
'12, and Francis Keesling, '98, will be
the speakers at the meeting of the
Masonic Club at the Thcta Xi house to
morrow evening at 7:30.
Old times at Stanford will be the sub
ject of the talk by Prince. Keesling will
speak on the relation of Masonry to
education. After the talks, the usual re
freshments and smokes will be in order.
