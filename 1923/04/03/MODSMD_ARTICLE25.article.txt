# Dr. Marie Reimer to Give Organic Chemistry Course
## 
### 
Dr. Marie Reimer, head of the De
partment of Organic Chemistry at
Barnard college, has been appointed
lecturer in Chemistry for the spring
quarter. Dr. Reimer will offer a two
unit lecture course in advanced or
ganic chemistry during the coming
quarter. This course is designated as
Chemistry 25 and is open to students
who have completed courses 7 and 8
or 10.
