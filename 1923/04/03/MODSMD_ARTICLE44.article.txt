# Tryouts Begin Tomorrow For Women's Swimming Meet With California
## 
### 
All women interested In making the
swimming teams for the interclass
meet with California April 21 are re
quested to report to Doris Stevenson,
*22, manager, in Rohle Gymnasium this
afternoon or tomorrow.
The swimming meet will be held
the morning of the Stanford-California
track meet. Since the four classes will
compete against their respective Blue
and Gold opponents, Manager Steven
son urges that every woman turn out
for her class team.
Credit will not be given for taking
part in the meet unless the partici
pant is registered for a class in the
Physical Education department. Prac
tice will be held every afternoon at
4:20.
Captains will be appointed soon.
