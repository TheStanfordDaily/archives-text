# Small Miss Templeton Arrives Easter Sunday
## 
### 
"Dink" Templeton walked out onto
the track this afternoon with an ex
pansive grin on his face.
There was a reason. Yesterday af
ternoon at 4 o'clock old man stork
stopped at the Templeton home in
Palo Alto and left a four-pound baby
girl.
"Dink" is known on the Stanford
campus as one of the greatest ath
letes that ever competed for the Card
inal. Mrs. Templeton was formerly
Miss Cathryn Williams, '19. She is a
member of Pi Beta Phi.
