# Y.W.C.A. Will Install Officers, Cabinet Members
## 
### 
The new officers and cabinet mem
bers of the Y. W. C. A. will be for
mally installed at a meeting of the
association Thursday at 5 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Miss VVollsey,
secretary of the Y. W. C. A., Marion
Potter, '22, retiring president, and
Helen Schardin, '24, who was elected
to that office at the end of the last
quarter, will give short talks. Refresh
ments will be served.
