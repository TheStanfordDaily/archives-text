# CONFERENCE PARTY TO BE GIVEN THURSDAY
## 
### 
Freshman women will have charge
of the Conference Party to be given
for new women Thursday evening
from 7:15 to 9:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse. All women are invited and
sponsors for the new women are to
bring their sponsettes.
Miss Yost will welcome the new
women in a short talk, and Dr. Mo
sher will give some statistics from the
health investigations made here last
year. Ruth Sneddon will be in charge
of two stunts put on by the freshmen.
After the program, refreshments,
planned by Millison Hardy, will be
served, and Grinstead will furnish the
music for dancing.
