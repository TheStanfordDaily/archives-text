# Euphronians Will Make Plans For New Quarter
## 
### 
Euphronia will hold its first meeting
of the quarter in Room 12 of the Law
building, at 7:30 tonight. Dick Gross,
the new president, will lead the club
in considering plans for the spring I
quarter. The society will also set. a
date for the annual Euphronia-Nes-1
toria debate which is to be held this
month. Tryouts for this debate will be
held at the next meeting of Euphronia
a week from today. >
