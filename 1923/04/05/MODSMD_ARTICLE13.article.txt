# Joseph C. Chamberlin Granted Fellowship
## 
### 
Joseph C. Chamberlain, who will
graduate from the department of en
tomology at Stanford at the end of
this year, has been granted a Univer
sity Fellowship carrying a stipend of
$750 for advanced work in entomology
at Stanford next year.
Chamberlain began his college work
at the University of Utah and has
made an exceptionally high scholar
ship recerd since entering Stanford.
Eight of these University Fellow
ships with a value of $750 each for
graduate work at Stanford next year
have been awarded recently by the
Committee on Graduate Study.
