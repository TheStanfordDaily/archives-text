### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur was
elected yesterday to the new execu

Community Chest. The Community
Chest is an organization through
which the various public charities of
San Francisco are financed. Dr. Wil
bur has been connected with it from
its inception. The Community Chest
provides $5,000 for the Stanford Home
for Convalescent Children on the
Stanford campus, and .$lO,OOO for the
auxiliary to the Stanford clinics in
