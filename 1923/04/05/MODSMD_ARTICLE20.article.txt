# WHIPPING POST IS LATEST WAY OF PUNISHING BABES
##

UNIVERSITY OF NEVADA—The
sophomore class is making plans for
the erection of a whipping post con
structed of concrete to resemble the
old New England stocks. Instead of
the debtors, witches, horse thieves,
and the like, those brought to task
before the post will be freshmen vio
lating the University traditions.
