# NEW AMENDMENT CAUSE OF MUCH STIR AT U. OF O.
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF OREGON-Much
discussion is being held over an
amendment to the new University con
stitution proposing the abolition of
letter awards to the members of the
mile relay team. Arguments in favor
of the amendment say that in a meet
where four teams are competing a
letter is easily and unjustly earned by
simply placing in the event.
