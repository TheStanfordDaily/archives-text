# MEN AT U. OF P. MAINTAIN WOMEN LACKING IN BEAUTY
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF PENNSYLVANIA
—There are no beautiful girls at the
University of .Pennsylvania but there
are many handsome men. according
to the results of a contest to determine
the most beautiful girl and handsome
man, which was held recently by the
Red and Islue, the university magazine.
"The girls at the university have
brains and ability and —brains and
ability do not mix with beauty," one
student said. The women say that the
men are crazy, assert superiority, and
take no notice of the matter.
