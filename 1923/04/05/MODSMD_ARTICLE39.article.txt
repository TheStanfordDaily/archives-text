# PREPARATIONS BEGUN FOR SOPHOMORE DANCE
## 
### 
Preparations for the "new step" dance
of the sophomore class to be held some
time this quarter, were commenced this
week.
The following committee was ap
pointed to take charge of work on the en
tertainment : James Aydelott, Dave
Oliva, finance; C. A. Londahl, decora
tion ; Dorothy Dahler, Evelyn Van Horn,
refreshments; Allan Charles, publicity.
