# Card Fencers to Compete In Coast Championships
## 
### 
Stanford will enter a team consisting
of Sandnetto, Bugge and Gardner in the
Pacific Coast fencing championship con
tests at the Unione Sportiva Italia club
in San Francisco Monday evening. Ag
guerrevere will be alternate.
Other entrants in the championship in
clude teams from the Olympic club, Uni
versity of California, Italia Virtus,
Unione Sportiva Italia, and the Army and
Navy club. Following this meet Coach
Harry Maloney will pick the Stanford
entrants in the individual championship
contests which will be held the following
Wednesday.
