# VETERANS WILL PUT ON BOUTS AND SONG ACTS
## 
### 
A vaudeville production, consisting of
wrestling, boxing and fencing exhibi
tions and several song and dance acts,
will be given at the De Luxe Hall at
Palo Alto, Friday evening, April 13, un
der the auspices of the Foreign War
Veterans and the two local American
Legion posts, Fremont and Allen
Nichols, in an effort to raise funds for
the purchase of post colors for the Fre
mont Post of Palo Alto.
Coach Harry Maloney has charge of
the bouts and the music and acts will be
furnished by members of the three organ
izations giving the performance.
