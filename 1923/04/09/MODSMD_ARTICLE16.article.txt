# QUAD STAFF SETS FRIDAY, THIRTEENTH, FOR DANCE
## 
### 
Friday, the thirteenth, all supersti
tions to the contrary, is the date set
by the 1924 Quad staff for a dance to
be held in the Women's Clubhouse for
the benefit of the Endowment Fund.
The dance will be a closed date.
