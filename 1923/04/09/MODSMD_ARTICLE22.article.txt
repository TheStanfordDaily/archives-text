# OLYMPIC CLUB STARS DEFEAT CARD TENNIS TEAM BY 6-3 SCORE
## 
### 
The Olympic Club tennis stars suc
ceeded in carrying off six of the nine
matches played with the Stanford team
i Sunday on the Stanford courts. The
matches were part of the interclub tennis
series. This defeat leaves Stanford in
third place, trailing the Olympic Club
team, and the California Lawn Tennis
Association.
The upsets of the day occurred when
Captain Hinkley of the Cardinal team
defeated Bob Kinsey, 6-4, 6-3, and when
Overfelt, another Card player, ,defeated
Roberts of the Olympic Club, 6-1, 4-6,
8-6. These two matches were harcl
' fought, and were both won over more
experienced players.
In the other singles matches, Howard
Kinsey of the Olympic Club defeated
Davies, 6-1, 7-5; Fottrcll, Olympic Club,
defeated Tussing, Stanford, 9-7, 6-3;

i and Fottrell, Olympic Club, 6-3, 6-3;
