# Music Club Will Hold Tryouts and Elections
## 
### 
Music Club will hold its only period
of tryouts for this quarter tomorrow
evening at 7:15 in the Women's Club
house. All students who are either
vocal or instrumental soloists are
asked to come prepared to give two
numbers.
After the tryouts, election of officers
for the coming year will be held. The
retiring officers are: Marjorie McDon
ald. '23. president; Meribeth Cameron,
'25, secretary; Albert Gregersen, '23,
treasurer.
