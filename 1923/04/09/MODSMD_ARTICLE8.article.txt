# Organization to Control Women's Athletics Is Formed by Mrs. Hoover
## 
### 
A national organization to investigate
and control physical education of wom
en and girls in America has been formed
under the leadership of Mrs. Herbert
Hoover (Louise Henry, '98) at a meet
ing of the physical training instructors,
and experts from all parts of the coun
try, which is being held at Washington,
D. C., this week.
This organization will affiliate with the
National Amateur Athletic Federation of
America and will include in its member
ship all women's organizations and
bodies interested directly in physical
training.
The aim of the body is to stand as a
deliberative, investigating, advising, leg
islating, and finally controlling body of
physical education problems.
