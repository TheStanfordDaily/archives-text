# WARREN D. ALLEN TO LEAD SINGING IN STUDENT ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist and musical director, will talk
and, with the Glee Club, will lead
Stanford songs at a student assem
bly Thursday morning at 9 o'clock.
Comptroller A. E. Roth will also
speak.
Acting upon the suggestion that
University singing can be materially
improved, the Senior Ccntrol Com
mittee decided upon this subject for
the first of a series of assemblies.
"There is no reason why Stanford
should not make as fine an impres
sion as the University of California,
in spite of the difference in num
bers," said Professor Allen in dis
cussing singing during athletic
meets.
