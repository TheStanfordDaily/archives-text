# Mabel Cory Scholarship Is Offered in History
## 
### 
Applicants for the Mabey Hyde Cory
scholarship for the year 1923-24, worth
$500 annually, and awarded preferably
to a needy young woman in the Depart
ment of History, are requested to con
sult with Professor Treat, Room 236.
The scholarship was first established in
1921 by Miss Emma L. Martin, '97, and
her sister, in memory of Mabel Hyde
Cory, '96, who died February 15, 1921.
