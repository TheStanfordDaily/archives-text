# SPRING SPORTS RALLY WILL BE HELD TONIGHT
## 
### 
Harry Wolter, varsity baseball
coach, will be the main speaker
at the annual spring sports rally
to be held in Encina hall tonight
at 7:30. All the latest dope on
track, baseball, and tennis will
be available, according to Lou
Minsky, yell leader.
The rally will be short, lasting
not more than a half-hour. Those
students living on the Row will
join the parade starting from
the Delta Tau Delta house at 7
o'clock under the direction of Al
Huneke. Sequoia men will leave
at 7:15 under the guidance of
Burt Lapp.
