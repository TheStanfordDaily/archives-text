# Y.M.C.A. WILL HOLD STAG; ELECT OFFICERS
## 
### 
Y. M. C. A. members will hold their
annual stag in the Old Union at 7:15
tonight. New officers are to be elected
and a short program will be given.
President Wilbur will address the
members on some topic of interest to

the association and A1 Gregerson and
D'Andrea will sing. Refreshments will
be served and it la planned to have
the meeting over within an hour.
