# Lafayette College Gives Fellowships in Chemistry
## 
### 
Lafayette College has announced
fellowships in chemistry paying $500
and $700 each to students who are
graduates of colleges with a degree
approximately equivalent to the B. S.
in chemistry or chemical engineering.
Applicants are required to submit
with their application a record of
work done, one or more letters of
recommendation, references, and a j
photograph. No special application
form is required.
The holder of a fellowship is not
required to do any teaching and is!
exempt from the regular tuition fee.
The work done may count towards an
advanced degree, but the greater
amount of time will be devoted to in
vestigations.
