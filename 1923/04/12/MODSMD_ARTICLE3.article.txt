# WILBUR SPEAKS AT Y.M.C.A. SPRING STAG
## 
### 
President Wilbur addressed the Y.
M. C. A. members at their spring stag
in the Old Union last night.
Officers for the spring quarter elect
ed at a business meeting held earlier
in the evening, are: Don Robertson
president; Dave Harris, vice presi
dent; Allen White, secretary; and
Cole Griswold, treasurer.
