# "Hot or Cold" Will Be Battle Cry at Jolly-Up Saturday Evening
## 
### 
Hot Dogs and Eskimo Pies will be
served for refreshments Saturday
night at the Skull and Snakes Jolly-
Up. This is intended to meet the vari
ous tastes of the jolliers and also aB
an inducement to hungry Ruffs. Those
who care to dance will be encouraged
in that line by Grinstead's eight-piece
orchestra. Every other dance is to be
a tag dance, according to a statement
issued by the chairman of the Jolly-
Up committee.
