# VARIETY OF DANCES PROMISED AT GRAND BRAWL IN PAVILION
## 
### 
Tag dances, straight dances, and hot
dog dances will l>e the order of the
evening tomorrow at the Jolly-Up.
Something in the way of a sparkle, jig
or reel may also he expected if former
Skull and Snakes parties are to be taken
as a criterion. The merry-making will
begin when the trucks start making their
rounds on the Row and at Roble. They
will start operations about 8:15 and the
women are promised a real joy ride to
the pavilion, where the roughs will be
waiting with both eyes open.
Tickets can be obtained from Skull
and Snakes men or at the door of the
Pavilion.
