# Dr. Jordan's Son to Form Local Naval Battalion
## 
### 
Knight Starr Jordan, son of Dr. Jor
dan, has been detailed by Rear
Admiral A. S. Halstead to form a bat
talion of naval reservists at Palo Alto.
The mission of Knight Jordan was
announced yesterday at Twelfth Naval
District Headquarters in San Fran
cisco. According to the report received
from the former lieutenant in the
navy, twenty former navy men have
already signified their intention of
joining the battalion.
Lieutenant Jordan saw service with
the U. S. S. Iroquois which convoyed
American transports across the At
lantic.
