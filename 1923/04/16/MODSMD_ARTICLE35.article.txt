# "Pop" Warner to Talk at Senior Smoker Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Pop" Warner is the main speaker for
the senior smoker tomorrow evening at
7:30 in the Old Union. Coach Andy
Kerr and "Dink" Templeton will prob
ably talk also.
Other entertainment will be furnished
and Grinstead's orchestra will play.
An admission of 25 cents will be
charged to cover costs of refreshments.
