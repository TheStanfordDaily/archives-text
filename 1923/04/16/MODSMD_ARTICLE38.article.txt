# UNIVERSITY OF UTAH GIVES OPERA "SAMSON ET DELILAH"
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF UTAH—The uni
versity opera was presented for the
sixth successive year on April 9 and
10. The piece given was the famous
opera by St. Saens, "Samson et Deli
lah," and was given entire without
any cuts. A cast and chorus of 150
took part.
