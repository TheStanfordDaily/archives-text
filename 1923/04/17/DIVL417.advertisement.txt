FAIR DEALINGS OUR BIGGEST ASSET
Did you ever come to McClatchie's with your tire troubles and fail to receive honest, helpful attention ? If you failed there must have been some misunderstanding for fairness to
all is the biggest asset in our business. Unless otherwise specified every repair we make and every tire we sell is fully guaranteed and backed by us to the limit. You cannot be
the loser if you deal with us and come to us frankly with your troubles.
