# Swimming Instructors Win Life-Saving Honors
## 
### 
Mrs. Ernst Brandsten and Miss Mil
dred Maurer, instructors in swimming
at the Stanford women's pool, passed
with high honors in the Red Cross life
saving- tests, and are now qualified to
rescue the drowning under any circum
stances, according to T. W. Sheffield,
who gave the tests at the University this
week.
Mrs. Brandsten, Sheffield said, came
through the tests with one of the highest
scores in the state. Sheffield is life-saving
representative of the Red Cross.
