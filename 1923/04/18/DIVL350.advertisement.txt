The Study of Expressive 'Dress
WHILE you may not find it in the curriculum, the
study of how to select garments that interpret
effectively your own personality is one of the most im
portant things to learn.
When you have discovered what HOUSE OF YOUTH
Suits, Wraps, and Dresses can do toward helping you
produce the impression of grace, poise and attractive
ness you will be a post-graduate in the art of Successful
Dressing.
