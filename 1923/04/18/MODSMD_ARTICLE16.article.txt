# Glee Club Is Arranging San Francisco Concert
## 
### 
The Men's Glee Club is completing
arrangements for a concert to be
given in San Francisco on Sunday,
April 29. The concert will be held in
the Granada Theater from 12:30
to 1 o'clock. Further particulars will
be announced later.
