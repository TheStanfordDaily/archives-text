# INTERNATIONAL DAY TO BE OBSERVED MONDAY
## 
### 
International Day, which is observed
annually by the Cosmopolitan Club, will
be on Monday, April 23. Plans were
completed in the last cabinet meeting.
There will be a banquet in the New
Union at 7 o'clock. Dr. David Starr
Jordan has consented to act as toast
master, and several foreign consuls from
San Francisco have been asked to speak.
International Day has become an an
nual affair here in the efforts to promote
good will and friendship among the for
eign students in the University.
Tickets for the banquet are now on
sale at the Bookstore.
