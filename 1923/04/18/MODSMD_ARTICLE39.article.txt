# Y.W. Student Volunteer Secretary Visits Campus
## 
### 
Miss Edith Sanderson, student volun
teer secretary of the Y. W. C. A., will
speak to the Y. W. C. A. cabinet and the
world fellowship committee tomorrow
on the student volunteer movement, with
which she is closely connected. Miss
Sanderson, who graduated from the
University of California last year, will
he on the campus tomorrow and Friday.
