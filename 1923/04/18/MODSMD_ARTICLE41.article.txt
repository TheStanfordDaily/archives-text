# University Scholarships To Be Awarded in May
## 
### 
University scholarships for 1923, both
graduate and undergraduate, will be
awarded early in May, according to no
tices sent out by the scholarship com
mittee this week. All candidates, in
cluding present holders of scholarships,
should file applications with the secre
tary of the committee, Margaret Hous
ton, before May 1. Blanks may be ob
tained at the office.
