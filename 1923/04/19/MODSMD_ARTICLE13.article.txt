# WOMEN WILL ELECT PRESIDENT, COUNCIL ON QUAD TOMORROW
## 
### 
Election booths on Quad will be
open tomorrow from 9 to 2 o'clock for
voting on the president of Associated
Women and members of the women's
council. The newly elected officers will
be installed the first part of May and
serve for the coming year.
Alice Roth and Lillian Cottrel are
the two junior members of council up
for president of Associated Women.
Eunice . Biddle, Margaret Hood, and
Alice Dodds have been nominated for
senior representative. Only one of the
three will be electd tomorrow. Two
junior rpresentatives will be chosen
from among the following sophomores:
Carolan Strouse, Elizabeth Simmons,
Margaret Watson, and Ruth Lee Spil
man.
