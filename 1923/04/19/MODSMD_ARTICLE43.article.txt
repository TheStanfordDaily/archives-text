# Professor C.P. Stone Will Address Psychology Club
## 
### 
"The Significance of Experimental
Neurology to Psychology" is the sub
ject of a lecture to be given by Pro
fessor Calvin P. Stone, of the Psy
chology department, at 7:30 tonight,
in Room 460 of the Botany building.
This is the fourth of a series of lec
tures presented under the auspices of
the Psychology Club, on the general
subject of the relation of psychology
to other fields of knowledge.
Professor Stone will touch upon the
historical development of experiment
al psychology and neurology, and the
growth of relationships between them.
He will illustrate his lecture by prac
tical experiments upon the nervous
systems of animals.
