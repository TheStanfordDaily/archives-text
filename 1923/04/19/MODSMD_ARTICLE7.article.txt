# "WILL SHAKESPEARE" HAS FINAL REHEARSAL FOR SAN JOSE DATE
## 
### 
Filial rehearsals for the English Club
production. "Will Shakespeare," which
will play in the auditorium of the State
Teachers' College at San Jose Saturday
night, were held yesterday afternoon and
evening in the Assembly hall.
The play was the first to be taken off
campus for many years, and it was pre
sented in San Francisco on April 6, at
the Plaza Theater. z
The advance sale of tickets indicates
a full house when the curtain rises Sat
urday evening.
Delmer Daves, who plays the role of
Kit Marlowe, has been ill in the hospital,
but he will be out in time to go with the
cast to San Jose for Saturday's perform
ance.
