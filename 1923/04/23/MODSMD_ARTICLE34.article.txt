# Cap and Gown to Hold Food Sale Wednesday
## 
### 
Cap and Gown will hold a food sale
next Wednesday morning from 9 to 12
in front of the Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. A. E. Roth has charge of the food
which will be prepared by faculty mem
bers of Cap and Gown. Candy and
cookies will be sold, the proceeds going
to the fund needed to send two dele
gates to the national convention of presi
dents of associated women students at
Columbus, Ohio.
