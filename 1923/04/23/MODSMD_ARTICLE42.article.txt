# Military Tournament to Be Horsemanship Display
## 
### 
A combination horse show arid gym
khana, with Roman riding, military jump
ing, and other horsemanship specialties,
will comprise the greater part of the,
fourth annual Military Tournament on
Saturday, May 26.
Battalion review, battery maneuvers, a
tractor race, broad sword contest, mili
tary jumping, horsemanship exhibitions,
obstacle race, steeplechase, and a polo
game were some of the events last year
which showed the sensational as well as
practical- sides of the work done by the
R. O. T. C. unit. It is expected the I
program will be similar this year.
