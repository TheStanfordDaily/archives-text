# "Pat" Patterson Is Elected Captain of 1924 Baseball Nine
## 
### 
Edwin Alexander Patterson, '23,
of Oakland, has been elected cap
tain of the 1924 baseball team. Pat
terson won his numerals in base
ball, and performed at first base on
the 1921 varsity. He was not in
college last year.
Durnig the past season "Pat*' has
been an absolute essential behind
the bat. He gets the most out of
his pitchers and has a remarkably
accurate throw. He hits well, and
is an all-around, heady, ball-player.
Patterson is a member of Phi
K Psi.
