# A 						LESSON LEARNED
## 
### 
The men in charge of the student movie programs
presented on the campus at regular intervals learned
early that the word of the press agent is not always
reliable, and after being taken in, along with the
rest of the student-body by the picture persented
-last time, they have taken unusual precaution to
secure a feature for next Saturday night that has
an element of modernity to it. An attendance of at
least six hundred is necessary to maintain the lower
prices which they are offering.
