# Carnival Masque Offers Quad Prize for Idea
## 
### 
Have you a little idea in your dome?
A grand prize of 10,000 rubles, payable
j in Patagonian rubber securities, will bei
awarded the man, woman or Phi Bete
who offers the best suggestion for a skit'
or dance number to be given at the Carni
val Masque. Something short and
snappy, for individual or group talent,
is needed. Post your replies to the
Carnival Masque Committee, Box L,
Campus.
