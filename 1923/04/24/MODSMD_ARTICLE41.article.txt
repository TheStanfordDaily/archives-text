# G. & M. Students Print Directory of Alumni
## 
### 
The 1923 Geology and Mining Year
Book and Directory went to press yes
terday, it is announced by Bradford
Bishop, '21, the managing editor of the
volume.
The year book is one of international
circulation, and goes wherever mining
and oil men are found. It is virtually a
catalogue of the addresses and activities
of all the graduates of the Geology and
Mining department at Stanford.
It is expected that the directory will be
ready for circulation about May 10.
