# Physical Education Heads To Hold Conference Here
## 
### 
Western Socicty of College Directors
of Physical Education for Women will
hold its second annual conference at
Stanford April 27 and 28 at the same
time the western section of W. A. A.
meets for its convention on the campus.
The directors plan to hold several
joint meetings with the delegates of W.
A. A. Miss Helen Masters Bunting,
head of the Women's Physical Education
department, will give a report on the three
eastern conventions of athletic asso
ciations which she attended during the
first part of April.
