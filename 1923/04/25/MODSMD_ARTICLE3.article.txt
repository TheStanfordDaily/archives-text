# COMPTROLLER ATTENDS U. OF A. INAUGURATION
## 
### 
Comptroller Almon E. Roth is of
ficially representing Stanford at the
inauguration of Cloyd Heck Marvin as
president of the University of Arizona.
Marvin was a student at Stanford in
1909-1910. Later he was graduated
from the University of Southern Cali
fornia and, after receiving advanced
degrees at Harvard, he became assist
ant director of the Southern Branch
of the University of California in Los
Angeles.
Comptroller Roth bore the official
greetings of Stanford to the Univer
sity of Arizona for the inauguration
yesterday. He was also one of the
speakers at the dinner given to the
official delegates the evening before.
