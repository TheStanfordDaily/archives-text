# ANNOUNCEMENTS

WEDNESDAY
CIVIL ENGINEERING SOCIETY
will meet tonight at 7:30 In the Old
Union.
lOTA SIGMA PI will hold an open
meeting Thursday, at 4:10, in the wo
men's rest room in the Chemistry
building. All women who are interest
ed in chemistry are invited to attend.
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON will meet
tonight at 7:30 in the Old Union.
THURSDAY
GERMAN CLUB will meet at 7:30
p. m. at the Women's Clubhouse.
CARNIVAL MASQUE COMMITTEE
meets at 12:45 on the second floor of
the Women's Clubhouse. It is impera
tive that everyone be there promptly.
RIFLE CLUB will meet in Room
214, Thursday, at 7:30 p. m.
WRANGLERS will meet at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
VARSITY AND FRESHMAN TEN
NIS MEN will meet on varsity courts
at 5 o'clock for Quad pictures.
GEOLOGY AND MINING smoker
will be held at 7:30 in the Mining
building.
There will be an illustrated lecture
on Electrical ""Engineering Problems
in the Engineering building, Room
283, at 4 p. m.
QUAD STAFF PICTURE will be
taken in the Quad office at 3 o'clock.
VARSITY and FRESHMAN TRACK
MEN will have Quad pictures taken at
four o'clock on the varsity track.
FRIDAY
WRESTLING, FENCING, AND
CROSS-COUNTRY SQUADS will have
Quad pictures taken at 4:30 o'clock
at Encina Gym.
WATER POLO AND SWIMMING
SQUADS will have Quad pictures
taken at 4 o'clock at Encina pool.
