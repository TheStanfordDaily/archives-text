# PAUL WILBUR ELECTED CAPTAIN OF GYM TEAM
## 
### 
Paul Wilbur, '24, has been elected
captain of next year's gymn team.
At present the team is composed of
Don Snedden, captain, A1 White, Paul
Wilbur, Wayne Smith. Max Hopper,
and Ed Meyers.
The team will probably take a trip
to Los Angeles during spring vacation
next year.
