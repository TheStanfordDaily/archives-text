# RIFLE CLUB TO HOLD MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
Arrangements for the use of the out
door rifle range will be made at a meet
ing of the Rifle Club to be held this
evening at 7:30 in Room 214. All mem
bers and others interested are asked to
attend.
The Rifle Club has several matches in
view and expects to hold its first infor
mal shoot next Saturday. Most of the
shooting this quarter will be on the out
door range south of San Juan hill
The Stanford Club is a member of the
National Rifle Association, ammunition
being furnished free of charge.
Challenges for matches have been re
ceived from University of California.
Yale University, and Fresno.
