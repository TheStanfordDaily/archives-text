# COURSES IN TRAINING BLIND ARE INSTITUTED
## 
### 
Training and teaching the blind is a
new field of teaching and social ser
vice combined, which is open to
everyone interested in the project.
Specialized training courses to pre
pare the student for this vocation are
now being given at the universities of
California, Harvard. Columbia, Penn
sylvania, and the George Peabody Col
lege for Teachers.
The courses offered give special at
tention to the semi-blind, the class of
people who are not actually blind but
need specialized training.
Salaries of $700 to $1200 are paid
to teachers. Anyone interested in this
work should write to the universities
at which the courses are given for
further information.
