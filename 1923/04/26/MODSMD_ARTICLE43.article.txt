# Geology-Mining Smoker To Be Held Tonight
## 
### 
An informal Geology and Mining
smoker for the purpose of drawing to
gether members of the two departments
and lower division men, who plan to
to major in either department, will be
given Thursday evening at 7:30 near the
Mining building. .
The men will gather around a bonfire
adjacent to the Mining building. Short
talks by Professors Blackwelder and J.
P. Smith will be followed by musical
numbers, refreshments and light enter
tainment.
