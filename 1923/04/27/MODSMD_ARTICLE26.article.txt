# Students of Santa Clara To Present Passion Play
## 
### 
Students of the University of Santa
Clara will present the Passion Play
in the University Auditorium, Santa
Clara, on Friday, Saturday, and Sun
day evenings at 8 o'clock, and Sunday
afternoon at 1:30 o'clock.
The Passion Play, which is pre
sented annually by the Associated Stu
dents of the University of Santa Clara,
is written by Clay M. Greene, Santa
Clara, '69, and produced under the di
rection of Martin V. Merle, Santa
Clara, '06.
A performance, given Tuesday as a
pre-view for the press, was attended
by thousands of parochial school chil
dren of the Santa Clara Valley, and
today it will be repeated for public
school children.
Tickets for the regular performance
may be secured at Weingartner's in
Palo Alto. Prices range from $1 to
$2.50.
