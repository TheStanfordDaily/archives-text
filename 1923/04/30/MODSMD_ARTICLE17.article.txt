# Professor E.P. Cubberley Attends Iowa Anniversary
## 
### 
Professor E. P. Cubberley, head of the
school of education, will return on Tues
day from lowa, where he attended the
fiftieth anniversary of the founding of
the Education department of the Uni
versity of lowa.
The first permanent school of educa
tion in the United States was at lowa,
and this anniversary celebration was a
event of national importance,'with rep
resentatives of universities of the entire
nation attending. The Dean of Colum
bia presided.
Dr. Cubberley spoke at the meeting,
the title of his paper being '"The College
of Education and the Superintendent."
