# WOMEN OF FACULTY TO ENTERTAIN HUSBANDS
## 
### 
"Off Again, On Again," a two-act
farce written by Mrs. C. A. Huston, will
be presented at the annual party given
by the Faculty Women's Club, this eve
ning at the Women's Clubhouse. The
husbands of the members will be the
guests of the evening.
Those who will take part in the play
include Mrs. James Kendall, Mrs. L. T.
Collins, Mrs. L. B. Wallis, Professor E.
B. Professor L. E. Bassett, and
Professor W. B. Owens. Old-fashioned
dancing, bridge and mah jongg will fol
low the program.
