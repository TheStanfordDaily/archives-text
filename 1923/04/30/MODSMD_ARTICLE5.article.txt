# HUNDRED WOMEN TRAIN FOR IOWA STATE TRACK MEET
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF lOWA—Over 100
women are working for the track and
field meet to be held soon on the cam
pus. Many of the women are becoming
efficient in the shot put and discus, as
well as in high and broad jumping,
hurdles, and dashes.
