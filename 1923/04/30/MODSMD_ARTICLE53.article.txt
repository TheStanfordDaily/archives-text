# Secretary Will Talk on Social Service Tuesday
## 
### 
Miss Helen Allan, social service
secretary at the University of Califor
nia, will lead a discussion on social
service work at the meeting of the
Social Welfare Commission to be held
in the Women's Clubhouse at 4:30"
next Tuesday. Miss Allan will expound
the philosophy underlying such work,
and will explain some of the condi
tions which make social service work
necessary.
Leona Mayer, '23, president of the
Social Welfare Commission, will give
a brief survey of the work that the
commission has been doing in the
vicinity of Stanford. Refreshments
will be served. All women who are
interested are invited to attend.
