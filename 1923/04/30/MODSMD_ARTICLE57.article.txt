# Comptroller Roth Back From Arizona University
## 
### 
Comptroller A. E. Roth returned to
the campus on Friday from Arizona,
where he was Stanford's representative
at the inauguration of Cloyd Heck Mar
vin as president of the University of
Arizona.
During his trip Roth studied mission
architecture, of which there are several
fine examples in Arizona. Roth is con
sidering the incorporation of a few spe
cial features of the Arizona type in the
new University buildings.
