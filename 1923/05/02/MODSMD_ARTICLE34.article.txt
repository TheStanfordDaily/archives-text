# First Edition of Famous Encyclopedia Arrives
## 
### 
The first edition of "La Grande En
cyclopedic," a valuable set of seventeen
volumes, edited by Diderot, has just been
received by the Library. The set belongs
to the Couzon collection recently pur
chased by the Library, and arrived in
the last shipment of books.
The Encyclopedia was published in
Paris and Neufchatel, France, from 1751
to 1765. During the French Revolution
the volumes were put under a ban be
cause of the revolutionary theories ex
pressed. The ban was first imposed by
the church and later by the French gov
ernment.
The Encyclopedia, sponsored by Louis
XIV, later proved instrumental in bring
ing about the death of Louis XVI dur
ing the French Revolution.
The books are now in the Reference
room of the Library.
