# CORNELL SENIORS TO WEAR SENIOR COSTUME AT GAME
## 
### 
CORNELL —A solid section at the
first baseball game of the season in
Ithaca will be filled with a mass of
blue, dotted here and there with
specks of scarlet, when the class of
1923 dons its senior costume. As the
annual garb, which is worn each year
by the graduating class during Its last
few weeks of student life, seniors this
year selected dark blue worsted sport
jackets with scarlet stoles antl pocket
lapels.
