# Education Club Will Hear Professor Kelley
## 
### 
"How Educational Ideals Have
Weathered the War" will be the sub
ject of an address by Professor T. L.
Kelley, of the Education department
at a meeting of the Education Club
tonight at 7:30 in Room 50 of the
Education building.
Professor Kelley spent last year in
England, and is well qualified to com
ment on the educational system there
as well as in America. He was also
connected with the psychological test
ing department of the United States
army during the war.
Anyone interested is invited to at
tend the meeting.
