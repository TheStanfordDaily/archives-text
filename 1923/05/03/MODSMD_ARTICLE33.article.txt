# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
All court scene and Carnival
Masque entertainers be at Pavilion at
7 o'clock tonight. Costumes will be
distributed.
S. MENDENHALL.
CLASSICAL CLUB will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 8 p. m.
WRANGLERS will meet in Room
214 at 7:30.
PROFESSOR WALTER R. MILES
of the Psychology department will lec
ture on "The Significance of Recent
Advances in Physiology to Psycho
logy." The lecture is given under the
auspices of the Psychology Club in
Room 460 of the Botany building at
7:30 p. m.
HISTORY CLUB meets in Women's
Clubhouse at 8 o'clock. Professor E. A.
Cottrell will speak.
RALLY COMMITTEE meets in
Council Room at 4 p. m.
STANFORD STUDENT FORUM
AND EUPHRONIA will discuss "The
Goose-Step" at 7:30 p. m. in Room 12
of the Law building. Any student may
take part in the discussion.
NOTICE
VOLUNTEERS are wanted to assist
in decorating the Pavilion Thursday
and Friday for the Masque Ball, to
work between the hours of 1 to 6.
