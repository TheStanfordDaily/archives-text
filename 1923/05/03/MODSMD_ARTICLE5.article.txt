# Professor W.R. Miles to Address Psychology Club
## 
### 
"The Significance of Recent Advances
in Physiology to Psychology" will be the
subject of an address to be given by Pro
fessor W. R. Miles, professor of experi
mental psychology, before the Psychology
Club, at 7:30 tonight, in Room 460 of
the Rotany building.
Professor Miles' lecture is the sixth of
a series'being presented this quarter un
der the auspices of the Psychology Club.
The general topic of discussion is the
relation of psychology to other fields of
knowledge, and the lectures arc open to
students in all departments.
