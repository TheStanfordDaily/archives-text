# "Earthquake Weather," Warns Dad; Watch For Preliminary Quivers
## 
### 
"Earthquake weather," says Dad.
Hold yourself in breathless waiting
for the prejiminary qu.vver- and keep
away from tall building . This innocent
springtide is treacherous and deceiving,
according to Dad, guardian of the En
cina portals ; and as more than one bill
board advertises, he knows.
"It was just like this before the Big
Quake in "00." he says, "flowers bloom
ing all over the place—purtiest June
weather you ever saw. Just a few days
before :t became sultry and clouded over
a bit. and then came a few little shivers.
All the animals were lying close to the
ground all the time—they knew what
wps coming. And then the big earth
quake broke loose."
Ihe majority of animals observed
recently have believed in four-square
support as far as we know; but just the
same the next few days won't be too
comfortable. One can't be too careful
in earthquake weather.
