Marian Brown, '20, of Los Angeles,

house.

Mark Evans, *23, announced his en
gagement to Miss Frances Sumner at
the Masonic Club dinner Wednesday
evening. Miss Sumner is a graduate
nurse employed in the offices of Drs.
Williams and Barnett in Palo Alto.
