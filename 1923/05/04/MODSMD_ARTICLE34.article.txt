# WEST POINT TO MEET RIFLE TEAM MAY 26
## 
### 
The United States Military Academy
at West Point will meet the Stanford
Rifle Club in a telegraphic rifle match
on May 26. The events will be: Slow
fire from standing position at 200 yards,
slow fire from the kneeling position at
300 yards, rapid fire at 300 yards, and

slow fire from a sandbag rest at 000
yards. Phones have been installed on
the Stanford rifle range to establish com
munication between the butts and tbe
firing line.
All men who have fired the service
rifle or who think they can shoot are
urged to meet at tbe post-office at 11
a. m. tomorrow and go to the range
with the members of the rifle team for
practice.
