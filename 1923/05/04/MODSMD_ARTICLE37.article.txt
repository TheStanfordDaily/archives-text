# Research Bureau Opening Announced by Institute
## 
### 
Openings in the Research Bureau for
Retail Training have been announced by
the Carnegie Institute of Technology.
The bureau is directing its attention
to personnel problems in the field of re
tailing. It trains for positions in the
employment and training department of
mercantile establishments and for teach
ing retailing in the public schools. It
also conducts research in this field, half
of the students time being spent in the
stores.
The training class is limited to fifteen
students in order to assure varied and
practical experience in the co-operating
stores. Eight fellowships of $500 are
open to mature men or women who are
college graduates or who have had store
experience and who wish to prepare for
personal work in stores or for teaching
retailing in the public schools.
