# Dr. O.L. Elliott Elected Chairman of Association Of College Registrars
## 
### 
Dr. O. L. Elliott was elected chairman
of the Pacific branch of the National
Association of Collegiate Registrars at
the convention held in the Women's
Clubhouse Friday and Saturday. Other
officers elected were: Dr. J. H. Mont
gomery of the University of Southern
California, vice-chairman, and Mrs. Es
telle Greathead of the San Jose State
Teachers' College, secretary-treasurer.
Thirty-eight institutions, fifteen of
which were represented at the convention
last week, are members of the Pacific
branch. The National Association meets
every two years and the Pacific division
will meet hereafter in the alternate years.
