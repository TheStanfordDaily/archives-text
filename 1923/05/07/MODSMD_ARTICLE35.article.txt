# BANQUET TO BE GIVEN FOR FOREIGN STUDENTS
## 
### 
The San Francisco Chamber of Com
merce will give a banquet for foreign
students at the Hotel St. Francis on
May 10. Invitations have been for
warded to all foreign students at Stan
ford who have already turned in their
petition for graduation.
However, the Chamber of Com
merce is anxious that all foreign stu
dents attend this banquet and those
students who have not received invita
tions and wish to attend are asked to
communicate with the Registrar or
A. T. Hubbard, secretary of the for
eign trade bureau of the Chamber of
Commerce.
