# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
INTERFRATERNITY CONFER
ENCE will meet tonight at 9:30 in the
Old Union.
There will be a meeting of the Wo
men's Clubhouse Board at 5 o'clock.
IMPORTANT MEETING of new
sorority women at the Women's Club
liouse at 7 o'clock.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE LECTURE
by John M. Tutt, C. S. 8., in the Wom
en's Clubhouse at 8 p. m.
TUESDAY
SCHUBERT CLUB will meet Tues
day at 5 in Room 84.
WRANGLERS will meet at 5 o'clock
in Room 214.
NOTICE
SPRING FOOTBALL PRACTICE will
be continued until May 11 for those
men who are enrolled in physical
training for fulfillment of require
ments. At that time those men will
be excused from further required
work for the quarter and need not
transfer to any other activity. Men
to whom this applies will report for
practice Monday afternoon. Any
others on the squad who wish to
may also continue to report. Work
will be under the supervision of
Coaches Kerr, Hunt, and Post.
