# Women's Tennis Tourney Will Commence This Week
## 
### 
Preliminary matches to decide the
women's class tennis championship will
start this week. The games will be
played during the gymnasium class
hours, and the first tilts will be run off
to eliminate some of the contestants in
order to determine the players who will
represent the four classes.
Semi-finals are scheduled for May 25,
and the finals will be played on Field
Day, May 26. The tennis awards will be
made at the Field Day events.
