# Prof. W.M. Proctor Granted Lectureship at Harvard
## 
### 
Proefossor William M. Proctor has
received word of his appointment to a
lectureship in the Graduate School of
Education at Harvard University for the
year 1923-24. Professor Proctor has
been granted salibatical leave by Stanford
and will take his family to Cambridge
in September. In addition to lecturing
on secondary education at Harvard, he
will complete the manuscript for a book
on educational and vocational guidance
for the Riverside Text-book Series.
