# BIG PARADE PLANNED FOR MINNESOTA STADIUM DRIVE
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF MINNESOTA —
Great preparations are being made for
(he opening of the stadium drive which
will be set off by a monster parade of
the entire student body through the
streets of Minneapolis. A noise com
mittee has been appointed to liven
the celebration. Aeroplanes, tanks, ar
tillery and machine guns will help to
make the opening a long-remembered
event.
