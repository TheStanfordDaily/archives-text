# DR. JORDAN TO TALK WITH STUDENTS ON "THINGS THAT ARE"
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will talk on the subject
which he has announced as "The Things
That Are," at his Thursday evening con
ference, which he will hold in his home
tomorrow evening at 7:30 o'clock.
The conferences arc informal and all
students, as well as the members of the
faculty, are invited to come.
