# MARIA ANDERSON TALKS TO STANFORD MUSIC CLUB
## 
### 
A talk on the life and works of
Frederic Chopin was given by Maria
Anderson, '25, at the meeting of the
Music Club held yesterday evening at
the Women's Clubhouse. Piano num
bers illustrating the talk were given
by Eugene Poncelet, '22, Marjorie Mc-
Donald, '24, and Sidney Hawkins, '24.
After the program, plans wej-e dis
cussed for the joint concert of Schu
bert Club, Men's Glee Club, Orchestra
Club, and Music Club, which will be
given May 29 to raise money for a
new piano for the Assembly hall.
