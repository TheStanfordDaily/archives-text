# JUNIOR SOCCER MEN TO MEET SOPHOMORES IN FINAL GAME TODAY
## 
### 
Junior soccer men will meet the
sophomore interclass team on the var
sity soccer field this afternoon in the
final game of the interclass round
robin. The sophomores have the title
cinched already with three victories
and no defeats but the juniors who
are tied with the freshmen for second
place must win today to beat out the
first year men.
The freshmen and juniors have each
won one and lost one game. The sen
iors have lost all three of their starts,
forfeiting their last game Monday to
the sophomores. Last Friday the fresh
men beat them 1-0 and on April 30> the
juniors defeated them 1-0.
