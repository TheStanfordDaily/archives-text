# Student Appointed to Be Superintendent at Lodi
## 
### 
Jesse R. Overturf, who will receive
his Master of Arts degree this quarter,
has been appointed superintendent of
schools at Lodi, California, for the com
ing year.
Overturf comes from College View.
Nebraska. He held the position of
superintendent of schools there also.
Overturf received notice of his ap
pointment to the position in Lodi last
Tuesday. He will leave to take up his
position at the completion of this quarter.
