# Halfdan Gregersen Plans To Study in Copenhagen
## 
### 
Halfdan Gregersen, '22, who is an as
sistant in the Spanish department, has
announced his plans for next year. He
will go to Copenhagen as a result of his
receiving the American Scandinavian
Fellowship in New York City.
He expects to leave California in Au
gust, and will remain in Denmark until
next May, when he will tour France and
Spain. 'Part of the time in Denmark he
will attend the University of Copenhagen
and study the Scandinavian language and
literature. During the remainder of, his
visit he will be in the country, where he
will study the Danish high schools.
