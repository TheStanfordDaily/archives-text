# Henry Higgs Addresses Political Science Class
## 
### 
Henry Higgs of England addressed
the Political Science class last Tues
day on the criticism of the bureau
cracy system in England. He pointed
out that the evils include corruption,
arrogance, and lack of efficiency.
Higgs expressed his hope of improving
the civil-service system with which he
has been working in Egypt. The visi
tor expressed his approval of the in
terest taken in the American univer
sities in political and social sciences.
