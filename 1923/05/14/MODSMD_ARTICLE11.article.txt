# Candidates For Head Yell Leader Must Enter Names
## 
### 
Upperclassmen who will not graduate
until next year must hand their names in
to Ed Butler, chairman of the Rally
Committee, before noon tomorrow if
they wish to try out for head yell leader.
According to the student body constitu
tion, any upperclassman is eligible to run
in the May election, provided he is not to
graduate before next year. Candidates
will be examined by the Rally Commit
tee as to their qualifications, and their
names will be placed before the student
body in the coming election.
