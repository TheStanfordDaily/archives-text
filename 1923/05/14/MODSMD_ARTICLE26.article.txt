H. L. Beaver, '15, Married
The marriage of Miss Florence
George and Harold Lovell Beaver, '15,
was solemnized Thursday evening in
the First Presbyterian Church of San
Francisco by Rev. W. K. Guthrie. Mrs.
William A. Mudgett of Palo Alto was
matron of honor and Miss Mary Beav
er, sister of the bridegroom, was maid
of honor. Bridesmaids were Miss Mil
dred Beaver and Mrs. Thomas Wal
lace. Gerald Gill of Palo Alto was best
man. William Mudgett and Harold Jay
Beaver were ushers.
