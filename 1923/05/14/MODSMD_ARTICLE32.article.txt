# Library Tick-Tock Is Guaranteed to Catch Overdue Literature
## 
### 
Strong words will not be uttered so
loudly in the future if the new machine
in the Reserved Book Room of the Li
brary works as it should. Careless ones
will moderate their voices and more or
less severe punishment based only on the
evidence of this little instrument will
be meted out to any who are negligent.
No, it is not a dictaphone, merely a type
of time clock to stamp the hour of re
turning books and prevent any argu
ments on the correctness of Western
Union.
