# Stanford Men Elected on Palo Alto City Council
## 
### 
Professor C. B. Wing of the Civil
Engineering department, and Professor
E. A. Cottrell, of the Political Science
department, were elected members of the
Palo Alto City Council at an election
held yesterday. Wing received more
votes than any other candidate for coun
cilman.
Dr. T. M. Williams, '97, chairman of
the Board of Athletic Control, was also
elected to the council. All three of the
Stanford men were elected for the long
term.
