# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
QUAD TRYOUTEES will meet at
7:15 o'clock in Quad office.
M. E. SOCIETY meets at 7:15 p. m.
in Room 263.
JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB meets in"
Women's Clubhouse at 7 o'clock. Dance
from 7 to 8.

WEDNESDAY
WOMEN'S "S" SOCIETY meets to
night at 6 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at
7:15 in the Union, for the purpose of
nominating officers.
STUDENT FORUM will meet in the
Old Union at 4:15. Dr. Charles F. Dole
will speak.
PI KAPPA TAU meets in Old Union
at 7 o'clock.

5:30 to start for Roble bridge.

men's Clubhouse at 7:30.
