# J.O. Hayes Appointed Regent Of University of California
## 
### 
J. O. Hayes of Santa Clara has been
named to succeed the late Charles
Wheeler as regent of Hie University of
California. Hayes, who is one of the
proprietors of the San Jose Mercury,
is well known in the newspaper world.
The appointment gives the agricultural
interest representation on the board, as
Hayes is also an orchardist and farmer.
Governor Richardson, on commenting
on this appointment, stated that he con
sidered the position of regent of the
University as one of the most important
and honorable services to the state.
