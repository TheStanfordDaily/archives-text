# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
WEDNESDAY
WOMEN'S "S" SOCIETY meets to
night at 6 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at
7:15 in the Union, for the purpose of
nominating officers.
PI KAPPA TAU meets in Old Union
at 7 o'clock.
THURSDAY
HAMMER AND COFFIN will meet
at 7:30 in the Press building.
THE LAW ASSOCIATION will
hold a smoker at 7:30 in the Old Union.
SCHUBERT CLUB will meet at 8:30
in Room 84, to rehearse for the concert
May 24. All members not present will
be dropped from the club.
HISTORY CLUB PICNICKERS will
meet at the Women's Clubhouse at
5:30 to start for Roble bridge.
GERMAN CLUB meets in the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 7:30.
