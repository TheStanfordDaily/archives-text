# CAMPUS SCRIBES TO BANQUET TONIGHT
## Tri-Delt House Will Be Scene of Conflict 
### 
Campus scribes on the Daily Palo
Alto staff will cease to shake the wick
ed pen for a few hours this evening
and take up the exercise of the pedal
extremities at the Delta Delta Delta
house in their quarterly staff banquet-
New members on the staff are giv
ing this banquet for the benefit of the
older members and they have also
promised to provide entertainment for
their seniors.
Dancing will start promptly at 6 and
continue until 8 when the banquet will
be served.
Marjorie Allen is chairman of the
banquet committee and has had gen
eral charge of the arrangements for
the affair.
