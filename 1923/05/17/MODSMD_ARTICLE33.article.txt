# RESCUE AT ARGONAUT WILL BE DISCUSSED
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Geology
and Mining Society will be held this
evening at 7:30 at the Theta Xi house,
2 Dolores. Mark Evans will give an
illustrated talk on Alaska. B. F.
Hickox and R. B. McDonald of the
United States Bureau of Mines will
give a lecture on the methods used
and the conditions found in the rescue
work after the Argonaut mine disaster.
