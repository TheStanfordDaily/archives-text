# Chemistry Student Speaks At Education Club Meeting
## 
###

ing the action of different chemicals,
William L. Burdick. a graduate stu
dent in chemistry, demonstrated the
subject of his speech, "Recreational
Chemistry," at a meeting of the Edu
cation Club in R6om 50 of the Educa
tion building last night.
Burdick's speech was illus

Among the experiments shown by

ment with sulphur. Burdick also ex
hibited a so-called silver sponge which

amalgam.
