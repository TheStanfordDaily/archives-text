# "HIT FRONT DOOR" IS OREGON STAFF WARNING
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE—"Warning To the Satire Staff
of the Beaver: You have hit the torea
dor—now hit the front door. Corvallis
can't hold you and us. This is the last
warning. You will be given the duration
of the Junior Week-end to pack up your
nightshirts like the Arab and as silently
steal away," is a signed and decorated
ultimatum in the "Co-Ed" number of the
Barometer.
