# Theta Sigma Phi Will Hear Convention Report
## 
### 
Miss Marion Brandt, a junior at the
University of California, will be the
guest of the Stanford chapter of Theta
Sigma Phi, women's national journalistic
fraternity, at their dinner in the new
Union this evening. Miss Brandt will
give a report on the national convention
of Theta Sigma Phi, which was held
at the University of Oklahoma last week.
