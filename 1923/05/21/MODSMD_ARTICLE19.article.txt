# Charles Merrill Elected Head of G. and M. Society
## 
### 
Charles Merrill was elected president
of the Geology Mining Society at the
annual election at the Theta Xi house
Thursday. Don Knowlton was elected
vice-president, Henry Mulryan. secretary,
and Dale Sparks, treasurer of the so
ciety.
Mark Evans gave an illustrated talk
on the ukon and the methods of min
ing there. R. B. McDonald of the United
States Bureau of Mines gave a lecture
on the methods used and the conditions
found after the Argonaut disaster.
