# Engineers to Visit Hetch-Hetchy Project
## 
### 
Fifty-five engineers, members of the
class in hydraulic machinery and power
stations, will leave early Saturday morn
ing by automobile on a four-day trip to
the Hetch-Hetchy project, near Yosemite.

Only those enrolled in the class will
be allowed to go, and Professor William
F. Durand and Professor William R.
Eckart will represent the faculty. Rich
ard Jalinson, '23, will have charge.
The tour has been made possible
through the kindness of the city engineer
of the City of San Francisco.
