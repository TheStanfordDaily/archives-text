# SOPHOMORES TO GIVE DANCE IN CLUBHOUSE SATURDAY EVENING
## 
### 
Snakes will be given an opportunity
to don the festive stacomb and perform
on the floor of the Women's Clubhouse
next Saturday evening, as the guests of
the Sophomore Class. Whitmer's six
piece orchestra will supply the music
for the affair, and refreshments will be
served, as well as punch, by the second
year men. Admission is $1.
