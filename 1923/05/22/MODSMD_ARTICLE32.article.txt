TUESDAY
RADIO CLUB meets in "Radio
Shack" in Corporation Yard at 7
o'clock.
SCHUBERT CLUB will meet in
Room 84 at 5 o'clock.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY
meets at 7:30 in Women's Clubhouse.
SIGMA DELTA CHI meets in Room
8 of Old Union at 9 p. m. Election and
other important business.
WEDNESDAY
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON meets at
7:15 p. m. in the Old Union for election
of officers.
THURSDAY
SCHUBERT CLUB will rehearse
with the choir at 7:30 o'clock in the
chapel.
REPTILE AND BIRD exhibit in
Jordan hall at 2:30 by Zoology depart
ment.
WRANGLERS meet at 7 :30 in Wom
en's Clubhouse, to hold elections.
NOTICE
The basement and first floor of En
cina will remain open for student i
use during the summer. Men now
living on those floors who want to
reserve their present rooms for the
summer quarter, call at the Dean of,
Men's office Thursday, May 24.
Other men living in Encina may be j
able to reserve rooms on Friday, j
May 25. On Monday, May 28, and j
thereafter, assignments will be made j
all applicants.
