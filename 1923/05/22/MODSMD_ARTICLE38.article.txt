# Clubhouse Board Will Be In Charge of Alumnae Tea
## 
### 
Members of the clubhouse board will
be in charge of the regular Wednesday
alumnae tea at the Women's Clubhouse
from 4to 6. The board is composed of
both students and graduates.
Among those who have made bridge
and tea reservations are Mrs. George
Morrell, Mrs. Price, and Miss Alice
Reppy.
