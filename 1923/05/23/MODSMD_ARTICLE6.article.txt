# Lens and Plate Chooses Fall Quarter Officers
## 
### 
Gale Griswold, '25, was elected presi
dent of Lens and Plate at a meeting
held in the dark room of the old Union
last night. He succeeds Kenneth S.
Ritchie, '21. Marshall Mason, '25, was
chosen vice president and Ed Strong,
'25, secretary-treasurer.
Arrangements for the art exhibit to be
given in the Art Museum in a week or
two were discussed and it was decided
to hold another meeting next Tuesday to
complete the plans. The art exhibit will
consist of enlargements of photographs
taken by members of Lens and Plate.
The meeting next Tuesday will be open
to visitors.
