# STANFORD DAMES WILL ELECT NEW OFFICERS
## 
### 
Election of officers for the coming year
1 will be the chief business of the Stanford
| Dames meeting this afternoon at 3
o'clock in the Woman's Clubhouse.
The meeting will be the last of the
quarter. Mrs. C. R. Pierce, the retiring
president, will be in charge of the session.
The yearly picnic of the organization
is scheduled for Saturday afternoon at
i Lagunita. Members and their families
and friends will attend. Mrs. Arthur
Kent is in charge of the picnic.
