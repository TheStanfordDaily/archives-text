# Carnival Entertainers Will Give Cast Dance In Clubhouse Tuesday
## 
### 
An informal dance, given by the
cast of the Carnival Masque, will be
held Tuesday night in the Women's
Clubhouse. As Wednesday is a Uni
versity holiday, a 1 o'clock date has
been granted.
Music for the dancing will be fur
nished by Jerry Powell and a five
piece orchestra. There will be special
programs. and refreshments. Contrary
to current rumors the dance will not
be a masquerade.
Tickets can be purchased only by
presenting an invitation card at the
Bookstore, and only one hundred cou
ples will be admitted.
