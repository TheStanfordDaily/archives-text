# NESTORIA TO DEBATE WORLD COURT QUESTION
## 
### 
Nestoria will hold a debate on the
world court question at its weekly meet
ing in Room 12 of the Law building to
night. The question is, "Resolved, That
President Harding's proposal for Amer
ican participation in the world court
should be adopted."
Leon Brown, '26, and Dave Wood, '26,
will uphold the affirmative side against
Russ Walters, '24, and "Bill" Northway,
'26, of the negative side.
