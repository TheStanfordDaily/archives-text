# Dr. Jordan to Address Education Club Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will address the Education
Club at its last meeting of the quarter
tonight at 7:30 in Room 50 of the Edu
cation building. His subject will be
"Some Aspects of Higher Education."
Many new phases in the subject of
higher education will be brought out in
the talk. Chief among the factors of
stirring up consideration of higher edu
cation has been the recent book by Up
ton Sinclair, "The Goose-step."
