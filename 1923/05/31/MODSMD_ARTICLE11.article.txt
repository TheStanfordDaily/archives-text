# PROFESSOR RYAN TO BE GUEST OF E.E. SOCIETY
## 
### 
A dinner in honor of Professor H. J.
Ryan, who was recently elected national
president of the American Institute of
Electrical Engineers, will be given by
the Stanford Electrical Engineering So
ciety in the New Union at 7 tonight.
Ryan has gained national prominence
in the electrical field by his widely
known experiments and researches.
Officers elected recently by the society
for next year are as follows: Hal Bum
haugh, '22, president; Lemuel Smith, '23.
vice-president; George Stray, '23, secre
tary and treasurer.
