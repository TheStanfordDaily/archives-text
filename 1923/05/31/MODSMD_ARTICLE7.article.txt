# Lewis Will Be Stanford's Speaker at Debating Meet
## 
###

ford at the oratorical contest to be held
at the Bovart Auditorium in Los An
geles today. Representatives of the
University of California, University of
Southern California, Redlands, Pomona,
Occidental, and Stanford will partici

auspices of the Southern California Citi

contest on the subject of "The Consti
