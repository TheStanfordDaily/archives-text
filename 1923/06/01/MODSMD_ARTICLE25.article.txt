# Y.W.C.A. Will Give Tea For Dr. and Mrs. Fitch
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Albert P. Fitch will be
guests of honor at a tea to be given
by the Y. W. C. A. Monday from four
to six in the Women's Clubhouse. Dr.
Fitch is preaching in the Memorial
Church during June, and will give the
baccalaureate sermon this year. He
has been head of Andover Theological
College.
