# SENIOR PROGRAMS TO GO ON SALE MONDAY
## 
### 
Senior programs, containing a com
plete list of graduates, with art work
by Pedro Lemos and J. E. T. Stoll. will
be placed on sale at the Bookstore Mon
day morning. At the same time mem
bers of the graduating class may obtain
Commencement Week invitations there
in any quantity desired.
The programs are bound in grey sheep
skin, with a special cover design by
Lemos. Sketches of the campus by
Stoll, who did a great deal of the art
work for the 1923 Quad, arc scattered
throughout the booklet. The program
of the events of Senior Week, which
lasts from Wednesday, June 13, until
Monday,' June 18. is included at the
front.
