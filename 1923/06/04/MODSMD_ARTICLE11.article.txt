# Eighteen Lieutenants Are Commissioned at Review
## 
### 
Eighteen men received their com
missions as second lieutenants in the
Officers' Reserve Corps Saturday morn
ing from President Wilbur at the R. O.
T. C. review. These men are:
W. H. Taylor, '23, S. E. Vaughan, '23,
W. W. Patterson, '23. T. G. Greene, Jr.,
'23, J. B. Hurd, '23, R. W. Reticker, '23,
J. J. Hartley. '23. W. R. Wilson, '23, R.
W. Faville, '23, H. C. Stoddard, '23. J.
P. Price, '23, A. W. Nash, '23, Don
Witherbee, '23, M. A. Ward, '23, Nor
man Cleaveland, '23, H. H. Patterson.
'23. H. R. Tremaine, '23. J. C. Muir, '25.
