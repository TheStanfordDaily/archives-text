# Hazel Pedlar Faulkner Will Explain Alumnae Association to Seniors
## 
### 
Hazel Pedlar Faulkner, '06, will
speak to the senior women tomorrow
afternoon at 4:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse on the work and plans of
the American Association of Univer
sity Women. Mrs. Faulkner is presi
dent o fthe San Francisco branch of
the A. A. U. W. and is a special writer
on the San Francisco Examiner.
Following Mrs. Faulkner's talk, Dean
Yost will entertain the senior women
at a garden party at 2 Lasuen in order
that they may meet the speaker. Mrs.
Ray Lyman Wilbur, Mrs. George B.
Culver, and Mrs. Almon E. Roth will
receive with Miss Yost.
The senior women will meet for
their annual dinner at the new Union
after the garden party.
The meeting in the clubhouse is
open to all who are interested; gradu
ate and faculty women are especially
invited to be present.
