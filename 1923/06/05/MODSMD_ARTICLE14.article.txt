# ROBERT EDWARDS, '24 MADE HEAD OF A.J.A.
## 
### 
Robert Edwards, 24, was elected
president, and Archie Steele, '24, was
chosen secretary and treasurer, at the
last meeting of the quarter of the
American Journalists Association this
afternoon. Garrett Elmore, '23, the re
tiring president, gave an informal fare
well talk to the members.
Edwards is a member of Sigma Delta
Chi, American Journalists Association,
Alpha Tau Omega, is a desk editor on
the Daily Palo Alto, and an assistant
editor of the 1924 Quad. Steele is a
member of American Journalists As
sociation and Encina Club.
