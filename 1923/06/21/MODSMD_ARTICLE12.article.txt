# Forty Stanford Students Will Usher at Circus
## 
### 
About forty Stanford men, under the
direction of Paul H. Davis, assistant
graduating manager, will act as police,
ushers, and ticket takers at the Circus
and Horse Show to he given at Menlo
Park Saturday, June 23. The Circus
i<- in the nature of a charitable activity,
sponsored by Menlo Park residents.
Stanford men assisted in the work last
year, and were so successful that they
have again been called upon.
