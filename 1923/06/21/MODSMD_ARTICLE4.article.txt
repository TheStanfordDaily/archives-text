# Dr. Wilbur Will Speak At S.F. Medical Meeting
## 
###

head of the American Medical Associa
tion, will speak at the open meeting of
that association at the Dreamland Pa
vilion, San Francisco, Sunday evening,
June 24. Dr. Wilbur's subject is "The
Public and the Organized Medical Pro
fession."
