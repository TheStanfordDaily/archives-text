# New Women Outnumber New Men Summer Registration Figures Grow
## 
### 
Latest figures from the Regis
trar's Office show that 861 students
have completed registration, as com
pared with 796 matriculated at the
same date last year. Of this number
all but 21 at the Hopkins Marine
Station are on the campus.
The men number 593; the women,
268.
Of the men registered 93 are in
their first quarter at Stanford .The
new women number 125.
Students are still registering at
the rate of five or six each day.and
with the entrants at mid-quarter it
is expected that the summer quar
ter figures will run to approximate
