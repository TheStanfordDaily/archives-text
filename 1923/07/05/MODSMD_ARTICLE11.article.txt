# President Wilbur Entertains Doctors
## 
### 
On Friday, many doctors from all
parts of the United States visited the
Stanford campus with their families.
Previous to this, they had attended
the convention of the American Medi
cal Association which sat in San Fran
cisco. President Wilbur, president
elect of the association, entertained
the visitors at his home.
