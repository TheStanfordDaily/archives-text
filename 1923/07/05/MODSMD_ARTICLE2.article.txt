# DR. SARGENT APPROVES CONSOLIDATED SCHOOLS
## 
### 
Dr. C. G. Sargent, of the State Agri
culture College of Colorado, showed
In strong contrast the relative results
which are secured by consolidation of
numerous small rural schools, and by
non-cooperative efforts, in his illus
trated lectures Monday afternoon and
evening in the Little Theatre.
By consolidation school plants are
secured which rival those in the large
cities. Dr. Sargent lias had great suc
cess in the organization and carrying
out of this work in Colorado, which
state today is nationally recognized as
the most progressive along this line.
