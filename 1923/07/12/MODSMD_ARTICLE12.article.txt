# HARDING COMES TO PALO ALTO AUGUST 1
## 
### 
President Harding and his party will
come to Palo Alto on August 1, at
4:30 p. m., to visit the Base Hospital.
It is possible that the President may
deliver an address in the Stanford
Stadium. Such a plan would eliminate
mobs and disorder. The presidential
part will be in San Francisco from
July 29 to August 1, whence it pro
ceeds to Los Angeles by way of Palo
Alto.
