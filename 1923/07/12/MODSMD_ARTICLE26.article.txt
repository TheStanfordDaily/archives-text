# "NO FLAPPEPRS AT RADCLIFFE" IS CLAIM
## 
### 
RADCLIFFE COLLEGE (lowa)—lt
is the boast of authorities at Radcliffe
that there are 110 flappers 011 its roster.
No women in the college use rouge or
powder their noses in public, and high
heels are never seen on the campus,
it is stated.
