FRIDAY, JULY 13
8:00 p.m. AT HOME. The faculty of the Summer Quarter and their wives to the students and their families.
Women's Clubhouse.
SUNDAY, JULY 15
11:00 a.m. DIVINE "WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: The Reverend G. A. Johnston Ross, D.D., of Union
Theological Seminary, New York City.

"Come Unto Him" David S. Smith
Mr. William Griswold Smith, tenor
Anthem: "Grant Us Thy Peace" Gounod
MONDAY, JULY 16
4:15 p.m. LECTURE on "Evolution," by Dr. David Starr Jordan; in the Little Theatre.
TUESDAY, JULY 17
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen; in the Memorial Church.
Beethoven Program
Nature's Praise of God
Adagio from the "Moonlight" Sonata, Op. 27, No. 2
Andante Cantabile from the Fifth Symphony
Minuet in G major
Hallelujah Chorus, from "The Mount of Olives"

7:30 p.m. LECTURE on "Racial Diversities and Social Progress," by Professor Julius Draschsler, of the College
of the City of New York; in the Little Theatre.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 18
4:15 p.m. LECTURE on "Evolution of Man," by Dr. David Starr Jordan; in the Little Theatre.
THURSDAY, JULY 19
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen; in the Memorial Church.
Fantasia Jose Maria Beobide
Adagio from the "Suite in ancient style" Georges Enesco
Humoresque, "L'organo primitivo" Pietro A. Yon
Theme, Arabesques and Fughetta....Van Denman Thompson
7:30 p.m. THURSDAY EVENING CONFERENCE, at the home of Dr. David Starr Jordan. Subject: "The Case
at Amherst College.

7:30 p.m. "BROTHERS UNDER THE SKIN," Moving Pictures, under the auspices of the English Club; in the
Assembly Hall.
