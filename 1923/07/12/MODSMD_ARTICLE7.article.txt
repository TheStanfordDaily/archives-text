# Cosmopolitan Club to Hold Social Friday
## 
### 
The Cosmopolitan Club will hold a
business meeting and social Friday
evening at the home of Carol Davis
at Cooley and Runnymede avenues in
Palo Alto. Arrangements have been
made to meet, at the other end of the
line, all those who take the car leav
ing the campus at 7:20. Miss Amy
Davis, who has recently returned from
the Philippines, will tell something of
the Islands and her experiences there.
All those who are interested in Cos
mopolitanism are invited to come as
well as all the regular members of
the club.
