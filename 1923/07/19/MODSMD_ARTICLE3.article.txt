# Dr. Jordan Will Speak of Agassiz
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will speak in the Little
Theatre at 4:15 p.m.. Wednesday, July
25, taking for his subject, "Agassiz as
a Teacher." The occasion is the fiftieth
anniversary of the founding of the
Agassiz school at Penikefe. Dr. Jordan
was a student of Agassiz fifty years
ago, and has always been a close fol
lower and admirer of him. He will tell
of the work done that memorable sum
mer at the school, and what it meant
in the history of scientific education.
