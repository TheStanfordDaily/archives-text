# Tennis Winner Will Receive New Racquet
## 
### 
A. G. Spalding & Bros, have announced
that they will give a tennis racket to the
winner of the summer singles tourna
ment now in progress. This award will
take the place of the cup which was
previously offered to the winner.
The third round of the tournament will
commence July 30th, fourteen men being
still in the running. On the completion
of play in this event, a doubles tourna
ment will be scheduled.
