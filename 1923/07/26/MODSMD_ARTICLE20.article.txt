# STUDENT ESCAPES INJURIES
## 
### 
H. E. Fast, a graduate student at
the University this quarter, narrowly
escaped serious injury last Friday eve
ning when the machine he was driving
collided with one driven by N. Faitos
of Palo Alto. The crash occurred at
the intersection of Palm Drive and the
State Highway. Two of the occupants
of Faitos' car, which turned on its side,
were hurt.
