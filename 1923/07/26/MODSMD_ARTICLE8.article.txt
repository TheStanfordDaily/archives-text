# NEW MAN ASSUMES DUTIES OF FIRE MARSHAL AT STANFORD
## 
### 
Former Fire Marshal Ed Lamb
has been succeeded by Fire Chief
Dugan, a retired member of the San
Francisco Fire Department. Dugan
comes to Stanford with the highest
recommendations. He was injured
while in the service in San Fran
cisco and has several times been
cited for courageous conduct while
on active duty.
Lamb left Stanford to work in the
fire prevention division of the Stan
dard Oil Company.
