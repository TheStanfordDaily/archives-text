# HOOVER UNABLE TO SPEAK IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Secretary Herbert C. Hoover of the
United States Department of Com
merce will be unable to accept the
invitation of the Palo Alto Chamber
of Commerce to make an address at a
luncheon here. Pressure of Govern
ment business was stated by Secretary
Hoover as the reason for his refusal
when the invitation was delivered.
