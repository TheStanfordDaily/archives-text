# TEXTBOOK EXHIBIT
##

exhibit, which is held in Room 264
during the summer quarter, the
Houghton Mifflin Company and the
World Book Company will both have
a large display of books. This will be
of unusual interest because the World
Book Company will have its full line of
mental and educational tests as well
as textbooks, August 6 to 11.
