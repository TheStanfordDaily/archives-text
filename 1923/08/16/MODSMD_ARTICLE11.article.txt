# EUREKA LOSES SCOTCH CAMPBELL TO CAMPUS
## 
### 
J. D. (Scotch) Campbell, captain of
the 1923 football varsity, arrived on the
campus Tuesday after spending most
of the summer in Eureka. He will
spend the next few weeks in looking
over the football situation and in going
over the plans for the season with
Andy Kerr. Together they will see that
everything is in readiness for the first
practice of the season on September
15.
