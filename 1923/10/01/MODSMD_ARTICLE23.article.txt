# FIRE MARSHAL ED LAMB SUCCEEDED BY NEW CHIEF
## 
### 
Former Fire Marshall Fd Lamb has
been succeeded by Fire Chief Dugan, a
retired member of the San Francisco fire
department. Dugan came to Stanford
highly recommended, and has several
times been cited for courageous conduct
while on active duty. Lamb is now work
ing in the first prevention division of the
Standard Oil Company.
