# 1926 Quad Candidates for Editor, Manager to Meet
## 
### 
Sophomore candidates for manager and
editor of next year's Quad will meet at
7:15 o'clock Thursday evening in the
Quad office, on the second floor of the
Administration building. All sophomores
are eligible.
The tryoutec period continues from
now until May, at which time the Soph
omore class elects an editor and a man
ager from those candidates who have
shown themselves qualified for the posi
tions.
