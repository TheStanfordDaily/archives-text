DAILY MANAGER
ISSUES CALL FOR
NEW TRYOUTEES
Selection of men to fill the mana
gerial ranks of the Daily Palo Alto
will begin Wednesday afternoon at
1 o'clock, at the Press Building. An
unusual opportunity is offered in
practical business experience and
also a chance to serve the Univer
sity on one of its most important
activities. All those who have pre
viously worked on the "Daily," or
who have worked on the Business
Board of their high school papers,
are requested to attend.
