"Why do you take the trouble
to look us up in every city
where one of our Confectau
ranta is located?"
We asked that question of a
guest who travels a good deal
thoughout the State. His an
swer was:
"I like the home-cooked food
at Wilson's and the friendly
spirit shown by the employes
of your stores. I feel at home
with you, and if you have
ever moved around much you
know what that means to one
who is away from his family
and friends."
