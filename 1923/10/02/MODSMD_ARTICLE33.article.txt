# Donaldine Cameron Married
## 
### 
The marriage of Donaldine Cameron,
cx-'23, to Captain Frank Pinney Helm,
U. S. N., took place Saturday evening in
the presence of a few relatives and
friends. When at Stanford, Mrs. Helm
had the lead in a large number of campus
dramatic productions, and was a member
of Masquers and Kappa Kappa Gamma.
Captain and Mrs. Helm will live in San
Francisco.
