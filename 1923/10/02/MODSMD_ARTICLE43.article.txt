# Cubberleys Will Travel in East
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Ellwood P. Cub
berley have left for a six weeks' trip
in the East, where Professor Cubber
ley will address a number of meetings.
Professor Cubberley is head of the
Education department at Stanford.
The Cubberleys will go first to New
York, and later will be the guests of
the Proctors in the Berkshire Hills.
Professor Proctor is also in the Edu
cation department here.
