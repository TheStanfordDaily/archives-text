# IDAHO WILL OFFER NEW ARCHITECTURAL COURSE
## 
### 
The University of Idaho becomes the
third college in the northwest to offer a
degree in architecture, since the institu
tion of a four year course in architecture
this fall. Rudolph Weaver, formerly head
of the department of architecture at
Washington State College, will be the
head of the new department. Weaver,
as university architect, will also have
charge of the erection of the new $200,000
science hall and the $100,000 men's dor
mitory.
Students registering in the architecture :
department will be given practical expe-'
rience in preparing specifications for the :
new buildings.
