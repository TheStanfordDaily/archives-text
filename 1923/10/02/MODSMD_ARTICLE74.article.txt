# President Will Welcome New Students on Saturday
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur
will be at home to all the new students
of the University on Saturday, October
6, at 8 p. m., at their home on Lagunita
Knoll. The purpose of the reception is to
welcome the new students and to give
them an opportunity to become acquaint
ed. Music will be furnished for those
who care to dance, and refreshments will
be served.
