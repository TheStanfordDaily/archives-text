# OKLAHOMA FRESHMEN ARE ASSESSED PARADE DAMAGES
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF OKLAHOMA —
Freshmen of the university are as
sessed $2 to pay for damages done to
local business firms in the annual
parade. Tags are given as receipts and
all frosh who appear without them are
warned to expect a "warm reception."
