# Student Patronage Urged By University Gas Station
## 
### 
The University has completed the in-J
stallation of a fully-equipped gasoline
service station which is located next
to the Stanford fire department. Gaso
line and olis may be obtained at the
lowest market price. The station is
in charge of the firemen on duty at
the lire house, and students are urged
to give it their patronage.
