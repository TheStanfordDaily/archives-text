WEDNESDAY
ENGLISH 183 will not meet today.
—Professor Seward.
SWORD AND SANDALS meets in
Green Room at 7:30 p.m.
GLEE CLUB will meet in Room 84 at
4:15 p.m., sharp. Tryouts for new
men will be held Monday at 4:15
and 7:15 p.m.
STUDENTS FORUM meets at 7 p.m.,
in the Old Union. All members are
expected to be present. Important
business.
THURSDAY
There will be a meeting of ALL
CLASS PRESIDENTS or CLASS
REPRESENTATIVES on Thursday,
at 2:30, in the Encina Gym.

at 7:15 in the Old Union.

Women's Clubhouse. Very important.
THE BAND will meet at 4:15 in the
band room. Old and new members
are urged to be present.

are requested to meet in Encina
Gym in the fencing room at 4 p. m.
SIGMA DELTA CHI meets at 7:30 up
stairs in Press Building.
