# GOLF MEN TO MEET IN GYM TOMORROW
## 
### 
A meeting of those interested in.
golf will be held in the fencing room
of Encina Gym tomorrow at 4 p. m.,|
and plans will be laid for an elimina
tion tournament to be held this fall.
The Stanford golfers have obtained
permirvion to use the Burlingame
course.
Only three members of last year's
team returned to the campus this fall.
Ditzler, Hines, and Hirschman, who
was recently elected captain of the
team, are practically sure of places on
the six-man team. Hinckley and Lang
are also expected to give some keen
competition for two of the remaining
places.
