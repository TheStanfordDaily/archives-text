During the past few days hundreds of men from the
campus have stepped inside the store to exchange this
friendly greeting.

new Kuppenheimer suits and topcoats, cords and flan
nel shirts, classy caps and hats, smart socks, clever ties,
nobby sweaters, and scores of other things to wear.

the fall suits and o'coats —Eng-
