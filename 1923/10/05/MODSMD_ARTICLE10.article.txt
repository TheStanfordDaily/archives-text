# PRESIDENT TO HOLD RECEPTION TOMORROW
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur will be at home to new students
on Saturday, October 6. at 8:30 p.m.
at their home on Lagunita Knoll. The
reception is given to welcome the new
students and to help them become ac
quainted. Those assisting the hostess
are Alice Roth, Margaret Richardson,
Helen Schardin, Eunice Biddle, Dorothy
Brenholtz, Helen Green, Lillian Cott
rell, and Caroline Strouse. Music will
be furnished for those who care to
dance.

University of Michigan has a total
registration this fall of 8852, an increase
of 5 per cent over the registration made
last year.
