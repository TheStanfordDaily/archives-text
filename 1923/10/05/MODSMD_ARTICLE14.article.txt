# Professor Murray to Give Tuesday Evening Lecture
## 
### 
"The Greek Revolution of Septem
ber, 1922" is the topic chosen by Pro
fessor A. T. Murray of the Classical
Literature Department for the first
lecture of the Tuesday Evening series
next Tuesday at 7:30 o'clock in the
Assembly hall.
This series of lectures is given under
the auspices of the Committee on Pub
lic Exercises appointed by President
Wilbur. The topics chosen are of a
popular character, covering dramatic,
musical, historical, political, sociologi
cal, artistic, and other fields.
