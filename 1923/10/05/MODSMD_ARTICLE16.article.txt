# STANFORD GOLF TEAM TO HAVE TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Stanford golfers will start the sea
son on October 15 when they will tee
off probably at the Burlingame club
in the first tournament of the year.
The winner of this tournament will be
presented with a plaque, similar to
those given for intramural sports, and
symbolic of winning the Stanford
championship.
About twenty-two men were in at
tendance at the golf meeting held yes
terday to discuss plans for the com
ing year. The Burlingame club will be
the scene of most of the golf team's
work. Reg Hirschman, '24, is captain
and manager of the team, which will
be selected later. There is an entry
blank posted in Encina Gym, and men
may sign up until next Friday.
