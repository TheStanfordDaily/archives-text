# FASHION
## 
### 
It is now fashionable to condemn reformers. In
such case it is still more fashionable to praise them,
according to the psychology of those who are de
termined to be unfettered by conventions. But even
the defenders of reform are becoming a little numer
ous. Which situation again furnishes the proper ex
cuse for expressing one's feelings aliout persons who
concern themselves over other people's morals.
