# 1100 NEW STUDENTS ARE EXPECTED AT PENN STATE
## 
### 
New students entering Pennsylvania
State College this fall were expected
to number more than 1100, according
to figures given out by W. S. Hoffman,
registrar. Among the new students are
949 freshmen, 84 of them women, 44
sophomores, 17 juniors, and two sen
iors. There are five graduates, 13 spe
cial students, and 52 two-year agricul
tural course students.
The total enrollment at Penn State
has not been announced as yet, but it
is expected to reach the 3000 capacity
figure.
