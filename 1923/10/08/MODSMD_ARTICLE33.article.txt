# Euphronia Opens Year With Meet Wednesday
## 
### 
Euphronia, the campus legal discussion
group, will hold its first meeting of the
year Wednesday night at 7 o'clock in the
Trophy room of the Old Union. An
open forum will be held on the question
of Governor Walton and the Oklahoma
situation. New students as well as mem
bers of the organization will be allowed
to take part in the discussion.
