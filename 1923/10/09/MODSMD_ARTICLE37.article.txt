# Faculty Women's Club Holds First Meeting
## 
### 
Members of the Faculty Women's Club
met for the first time this year Monday
afternoon at the woman's clubhouse. The
organization regularly meets the second
Monday of each month, at 3.
According to the usual custom, two of
the members were elected as representa
tives on the clubhouse board. They are
Mrs. A. M. Cathcart and Mrs. J. P.
Smith.
