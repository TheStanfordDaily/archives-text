It has never been a matter of any particular importance to us as to
whether we could claim to be "the biggest" men's store in San Fran
cisco, or brag of having the largest stock of clothing—although we
devote four full floors to serving thousands of men and boys to
whom this institution is Clothing Headquarters.
Rather we have striven to have well-chosen stocks in sufficient
quantities to allow satisfactory selection by our customers.
Our fall suits include the new low-waisted norfolk—3 and 4 button
Brooks types —the popular new long-roll lapel English style—plenty
of double-breasted models —in domestic and imported fabrics.
Hastings has been Clothing Headquarters for hundreds of Stanford
men every since there has been a Stanford University. We're
prepared to serve you even better this year than ever before.
