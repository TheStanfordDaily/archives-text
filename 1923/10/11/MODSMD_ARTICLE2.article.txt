# Puzzling Skylight Mystery Searched Out; Sky Lit By Searchlight
## 
### 
That which you see in the sky is
not the finger of God, nor is it even
the long arm of the law at last
reaching to the Stanford campus.
Some of the brothers thought it was
a bootlegger's guide to California,
but they were disappointed. It is
only some trick advertising for the
Mountain View Prune and Apricot
Festival.
