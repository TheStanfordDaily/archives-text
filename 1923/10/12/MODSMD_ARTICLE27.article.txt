# "Literary Glory of Bible" Is Memorial Church Topic
## 
### 
Dr. Kerr Boyce Tupper, D.D., LL.D.,
of Philadelphia, who will speak at the
Sunday morning services of the Me
morial Church, will lecture on "The
Literary Glory of the Bible" Sunday
night at 7:30 in the Memorial Church.
Dr. Tupper recently arrived from
Denver where he delivered a course
of seven lectures on the great re
formers, Martin Luthpr, John Knox,
Jonathan Edwards, and John Wesley.
Dr. Tupper was known in Denver
for his excellent lectures and Chautau
qua addresses. He has addressed the
cadets at Annapolis for six successive
years and is a well-known speaker at
Cornell.
