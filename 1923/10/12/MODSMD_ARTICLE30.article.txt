# Department of English to Give $50 for Prize Essay
## 
### 
The English department prize of $50
for excellence in writing will this year
he offered for the best essay written
by an undergraduate. The prize was
started two years ago by members of
the English faculty.
The essay may be formal or infor-
are no limitations regard
ing length and topics, and the contest
is open to all registered undergradu
ates. Manuscripts will be called for in
April, and the prize awarded later.
Last year the prize was won by
Charles McNichols, '24. The judges for
the last contest were Wilbur Hall, W.
J. Nldig, '96, and Robert Duffus, '10.
