# Woodward Made Head of Skull and Snakes
## 
### 
"Bud" Woodward was elected presi
dent and "Dud" DeGroot secretary
treasurer at a meeting of Skull and
Snakes last night. October 27 was the
date set for a jolly-up under the au
spices of the society. This is on the
eve of the varsity football tussle with
U. S. C.
