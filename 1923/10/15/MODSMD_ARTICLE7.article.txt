# Dr. Jordan Will Address New Students at Assembly
## 
### 
Dr. David Starr Jordan will address
all freshmen and new students this eve
ning at 7:30 in the Encina clubroom at
the second of the series of "On the
Boat" assemblies, being given this quar
ter under the auspices of the Stanford
Y. M. C. A.
The Chancellor Emeritus will take as
his subject, "Stanford Traditions and
Ideals," and his talk will include remi
nisccnce of the early days on the Farm
and the origin of many campus customs.
It will be the first opportunity entering
students have had to hear the first presi
dent of the University.
Norman Ambrose, '25, will be in
chargc of the meeting. The assemblies
arc for all new students, not merely those
living in Encina.
