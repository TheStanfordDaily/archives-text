# INTRAMURAL SPORT SCHEDULE FOR FALL GIVEN OUT BY DAVIS
## 
### 
C. W. Davis, director of intramural
and interclass athletics, has announced
a complete schedule of fall activities.
This schedule has been worked out so
that every man in the school who is ath
leticly inclined will be able to compete
in some i sport. Included in the list are
golf, handball, football, basketball, cross
country running, and tennis.
The schedule follows:
Golf tournament —Qualifying round,
Monday, October 15, at the Burlingame
Country Club.
Tennis singles tournament — Monday,
October 22. Entries close Wednesday,
October 17. All men eligible to compcte.
Handball tournament—Monday, Octo
ber 22. Entries close Wednesday, Octo
ber 17. Singles only.
Interclass football—Freshmen vs. Jun
iors, October 27; 9:30 a. m.
Sophomores vs. seniors, October 27;
10:30 a. m.
Freshmen vs. seniors, November 3;
9 :30 a. m.
Juniors vs. sophomores, November 3;
10:30 a. m.
Freshmen vs. sophomores, November
10; 9:30 a. m.
Seniors vs. juniors, November 10;
10:30 a. m.
Games will be arranged for second
class teams if enough men turn out for
interclass football.
Intramural cross-country run—Novem
ber 5 (tentative).
Interclass basketball—December 3 to
15. Round robin games for first and
second teams.
