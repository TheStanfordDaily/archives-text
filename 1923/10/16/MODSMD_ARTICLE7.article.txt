# Applications for Rhodes Scholarships Due at Once
## 
### 
Students desiring to apply for places
as candidates for the Rhodes scholarship
should file their applications with Pro
fessor A. T. Murray in Room 250 im
mediately. Representatives from Stan
ford will be selected within the next few
days.
Stanford will be entitled to five candi
dates. The applicants must be at least
nineteen years old and not older than
twenty-five, and have full junior stand
ing.
The Rhodes scholarship is tenable for
three years and the stipend is 350 pounds
per year. There is no restriction placed
upon a Rhodes scholar's choice of
studies. The men chosen are appointed
without examination on the basis of their
record in school and college.
