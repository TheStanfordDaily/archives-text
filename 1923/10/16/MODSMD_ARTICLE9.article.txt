# New Y. W. C. A. Members To Be Formally Received
## 
### 
New members of the Y. W. C. A.
will be formally received itno the as
sociation at the recognition service to
be held tomorrow at 5 o'clock in the
Memorial Church. This service, which
is an annual affair, is intended to
bring clearly before the new women
the ideals of the organization which
they have joined.
Dean Mary Yost will give a short
talk after which the traditional candle
light service will take place. Members
of last year are urged to come to help
in the welcome to those entering the
Y. W. C. A. for the first time.
