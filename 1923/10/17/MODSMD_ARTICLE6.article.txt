# Horrors! Lion Runs Wild on Stage; Refuses to Roar
## 
### 
Trouble and disruption almost leading
to assault and battery were discovered
last night in the cast of ''Androcles
and the Lion" just in time to prevent
it from spreading to the rest .of the
campus. Arnold Bayley had a tantrum
and nearly clawed the rest of the cast
to pieces. He said that just because he
was a lion everybody needn't accuse
him of it all the time. He was finally
pacified by "Androcles" Chalmers and
led to his cage before he could hurt
any of the brand new actors.
