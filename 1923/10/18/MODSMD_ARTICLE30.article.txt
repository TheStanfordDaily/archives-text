# CROSS COUNTRY RUN TO BE PRELIMINARY TO IDAHO CONTEST
## 
### 
The annual cross country run is
scheduled for Saturday, November 17,
at 1:30, as a preliminary to the Idaho
football game, announced C. W.
Davis, director of intramural sports,
yesterday. Entries for the run will
close November 14. Rules and regula
tions as well as the course to be run
will be the same as last year. All men
are eligible.
Davis urges that all entrants take
the necessary physical examination
at once. Individuals may enter for any
of the several prizes offered. A bronze
plaque will be given the winning
team.
