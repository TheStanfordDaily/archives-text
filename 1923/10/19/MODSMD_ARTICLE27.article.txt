# Under the Sun
##

<3> C. N. H. S>
ILLUSION
At precisely eight o'clock
every morning, the old
fashioned president of
the Excelsior Fire
Extinguisher
Manufacturing Company
arrived at the office and
f* began the day's work. And
exactly at the same time
the factory machinery
started to hum. The old
man came to believe that
the wheels wouldn't turn
until he got there.
But one morning the familiar
figure did not come at
8 o'clock. It didn't
come at all. But the cogs
began to grind away at the
usual time, just the same.

Keith Lloyd, cousin of Harold Lloyd,
the film comedian, and star sprinter on
the Nebraska team last year, has regis
tered at the University of Southern Cali
fornia. He is the holder of several Mis
souri valley dash records and will be
eligible to represent the University of
Southern California in A. A. U. meets
next spring.
