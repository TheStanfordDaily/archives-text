# Coaches to Address Freshman Class at Assembly in Encina
## 
### 
Members of the University's
coaching staff will be the speakers
at the "On-the-Boat" Assembly this
evening at 7:30 in the Encina club
rooms. Football Coaches Kerr and
Thornhill, Harry Wolter, baseball
mentor, and Dink Templeton, track
coach, will outline the athletic situ
ation at Stanford.
This assembly is the third of the
series conducted for the benefit of
entering students by the Stanford
Y. M. C. A. Norman Ambrose, '25,
will be in charge.
