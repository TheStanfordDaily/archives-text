# Transfers Will Be Given Picnic by Y. W. C. A. Cabinet
## 
### 
Senior Cabinet of the Y. W. C. A.
will be hostess to all new women
other than freshmen at a picnic to
morrow afternoon. Graduates who
have transferred here are also invited.
The new women will meet the sen
ior cabinet at the Women's Clubhouse
at 5:30> Wednesday afternoon. From
there they will go into the hills where
they will be served food. A huge bon
fire and entertainment will be fur
nished for the amusement of those
who attend.
