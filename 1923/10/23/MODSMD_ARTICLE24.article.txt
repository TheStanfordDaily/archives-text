# Skull and Snakes to Give Jolly-Up Saturday Night
## 
### 
Skull and Snakes will stage the first
jollv-up of the quarter next Saturday
in the pavilion. "Nip" McHose, who
will have charge of the affair, prom
ises plenty of good music.
According to tradition, the women
will be transported to the pavilion in
trucks. Most of the dances will be
cut-ins.
