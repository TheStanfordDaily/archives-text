# Many Reservations Are Made for Alumnae Tea
## 
### 
An unusually large number of reser
vations have been made for the Alum
nae Tea at the Women's Clubhouse
Wednesday, from 4 to 6.
Those who will entertain include
Mrs. Wagner Bradford, Mrs. J. F.
Davis, and Mrs. F. Hall. Mrs. J. S. P.
Tatlock is hostess for the day. *
