# Sunday Night Meals To Continue at Roble
## 
### 
Sunday night suppers will continue
to be served at Roble as the result of
a ballot taken on the question yester
day. The vote was 110 to 34.
An extra course at dinner of bread
and milk in the evening were the two
alternatives which were to have been
voted upon if the proposition to abol
ish suppers had passed.
