# Botany Tea Hounds Meet Each Week for Drink-Fest
## 
### 
Botany Tea ! Sounds like boot-leg, but
it's simply an informal gathering of the
faculty and students of the Botany de
partment at 4 o'clock every Friday in
the Botany library.
Tea is served, and the students claim
that Botany tea is just as good as any
other. All students interested are in
vited to try it out.
