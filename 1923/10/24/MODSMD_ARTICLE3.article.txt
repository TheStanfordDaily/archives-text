# Professor Fairclough to Attend Divinity Convention
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough has been
delegated to represent Stanford at the
ceremony marking the thirtieth anni
versary of the Church Divinity School
of the Pacific in San Francisco tomor
row afternoon. Dr. I). Charles Gardner,
chaplain of the University, who is an
alumnus and trustee of the Divinity
school, will also attend.
The exercises will also mark the in
stallation of the Rev. Herbert Harry
Powell as dean of the school, follow
ing the retirement of Bishop William
Ford Nichols.
