# STANFORD POLO TEAM TRIMS CLUBMEN, 6-4
## 
### 
The Stanford Polo team won from
the Palo Alto Polo Club by a 6-4 score
on Highway Field yesterday afternoon.
Tremaine led in the scoring with
three Cardinal goals, while Barby and
Irwin accounted for two and one re
spectively. Tooney located the posts
three times and Thompson once for
the Palo Alto aggregation.
Laub was missing from the Stanford
line-up due to a fair sustained in prac
tice last week. Reticker took his place.
