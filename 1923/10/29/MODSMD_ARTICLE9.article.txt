# AUTOGRAPHED WORKS OF JUSSERAND FOUND
## 
### 
Three author's presentation copies auto
graphed by J. J. Jusserand, French ambas
sador to the United States for the last
20 years, have been found in the library.
They are in the 5000 volume French
library of Senateur Eugene Lintilhac,
purchased in 1921.
The autographed volumes are "Histoire
Litteraire du Peuple Anglais," "Shake
speare en France sous L'Ancien Regime,"
and "Les Sports et Jeus D'Exercice dans
L'Ancienne France."
