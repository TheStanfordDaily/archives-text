# Marian Roads Married
## 
### 
Marian Roads, *24, and Osgood
Lovekin, '23, were married October 19
in the Stanford Memorial Church. Dr.
Gardner officiated at the ceremony.
Mr. and Mrs. Lovekin returned from
their honeymoon October 26 and are
at home to their friends at 281 Embar
cadero, Palo Alto.
Mrs. Lovekin is a member of Kappa
Kappa Gamma. She transferred to
Stanford from the University of Cali
fornia last spring quarter. Lovekin be
longs to Encina Club.
