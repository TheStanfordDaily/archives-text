# Tryouts for Speaking Contest to Be on Monday
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Pacific Coast Ex
temporaneous Speaking Contest, to be
held in Eugene, Ore., on November 16,
will be held in the Little Theater Mon
day at 2:30. The specific wording of
the general subject for debate, "The
Criminal Syndicalism Laws," will be
assigned at that time and one hour
will be allowed to prepare speeches
before the actual tryouts will begin.
The winner of these tryouts will go
north to represent Stanford in Novem
ber.
