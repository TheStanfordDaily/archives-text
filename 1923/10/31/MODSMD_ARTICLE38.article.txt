# Russian Student Life Is Topic for Y. W. Talks
## 
### 
Student life in Russia will be dis
cussed by two students from that
country at the Y. W. C. A. meeting to
be held Thursday at 4:30 in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. The two students
are Miss Gasenvush and Mr. Kovaleff,
both of whom have studied in Russian
universities. Miss Gasenvush is now
registered at the University of Cali
fornia.
The Student Volunteers, the Y. M.
C. A., the Cosmopolitan Club, the Stu
dent Forum, and of course, all Y. W.
C. A. members, are urged to attend.
The meeting Thursday will be the
first regular one held by the associa
tion this year. Open meetings will be
held every month hereafter.
