# Candidates for Rhodes Scholarship Are Chosen
## 
### 
Five candidates for the Rhodes
Scholarship Fund have been chosen to
represent Stanford in the final selec
tion, which will be held in San Fran
cisco this month. Professor R. A. Mur
ray of the Classical department and
chairman of the Stanford Committee,
gives the five names in the following
order: Dudley DeGroot, Magnus Gre
gerson, A. A. Hill, Thomas Bailey,
Northcutt Ely.
The Rhodes Scholarship is offered
to students in American universities
and enables them to attend Oxford.
Two scholarships are assigned to each
state. *
