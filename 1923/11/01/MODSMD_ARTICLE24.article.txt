# Cotillion Tickets on Sale Next Week; 350 Limit Set
## 
### 
Three hundred and fifty tickets will be
sold in the early part of next week for
the sophomore cotillion in the Pavilion
November 16, according to an announce
ment nude recently by Ray Kay, general
chairman of the cotillion committee.
The Fairmont hotel eight-piece orches
tra in San Francisco has been engaged
for the dance.
Although no definite plan has been
reached by the decoration committee as
to the arrangement of the pavilion, that
body claims that the decorations will be
far more elaborate than those displayed
last year.
