# Wranglers Society Will Choose New President
## 
### 
Wranglers society will elect officers for
the year at the meeting \o be held tonight
at 7:3() in the Women's Clubhouse.
Neither Elsa Barber, who was to have
been president, or Ruth Lee Spilman,
secretary, is registered in the University
this quarter. Linda Van Norden is re
signing from the position of treasurer.
Plans will be made for the organization
of a freshman women's debating society
under the direction of Wranglers. This
group will be similar to the Freshmen
Wranglers society of last year.
