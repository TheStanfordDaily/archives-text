# SENIOR CLASS TO PUBLISH BLUE AND GOLD THIS YEAR

BERKELEY, Cal. —Publication of
the Blue and Gold, the University of
California year book, will henceforth |
be directly under the control of the
Associated Students with seniors as,
editor and manager. This publication
has previously been handled by the
Junior Class.
