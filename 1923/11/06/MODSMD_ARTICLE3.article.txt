# TOYON CLUB MEMBERS DISCUSS YEAR'S PLANS
## 
### 
Plans for Toyon during the present
year were discussed by members at a
meeting last night in the club lobby. Sid
president of Sequoia club, ex
tended the greetings of that organization
to the new living group, and expressed a
desire for co-operation between the two
groups. Charles Beardsley gave details
regarding the furnishing of the new dor
mitory and announced the club's opening
dance for members on December 8.
Beardsley stated that the finishing
touches will be made in time for the
first dance.
