# Notice
## 
### 
Syllabus tees are all to be paid on
one day this year, Tuesday, November
13, instead of the customary three-day
'period. Bills are to be mailed this week
and any person wishing more time to
meet the obligation should see the
Registrar. The office will be open from
; 7:30 Tuesday morning to 5 in the
! afternoon.
