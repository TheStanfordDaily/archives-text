# RAY TREMAINE CHOSEN PI KAPPA TAU HEAD
## 
### 
Ray Tremaine was elected commandant
of Pi Kappa Tau at the first meeting of
the quarter held at military headquarters
last night.
Other elections included Ralph Barby,
lieutenant commandant; Adolph Neumati,
adjutant, and Edward Laub, financial of
ficer. Plans for .the year were outlined
at the meeting.
