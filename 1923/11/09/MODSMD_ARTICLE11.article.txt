# STANFORD MAN DIES IN JAPAN DISASTER
## 
### 
Chuzabura Tsukamoto, '02, was the
only Stanford man killed in the Japan
disaster, according to word received yes
terday by J. E. McDowell, alumni sec
retary.
There were about one hundred alumni
in Japan at the time of the earthquake,
Secretary McDowell says. Immediately
after the quake, a committee of three
started a search for the Stanford alumni
known to be in the islands. All were ac
counted for but Tsukamoto, who was
killed in Tokio.
