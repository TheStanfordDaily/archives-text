# O.S. McCALL TO TALK ABOUT BIG BUSINESS
## 
### 
"Big Business and the Blood of
Men" will be discussed by Dr. Oswald
W. S. McCall in his second lecture in
a series of six tomorrow at 7:30 in the
Memorial Church.
Dr. McCall has been asked to repeat,
the talks which he is giving here in
London, where he has lectured before.
He is an Australian and came to
Berkeley two years ago from Tas
mania. He served as a chaplain dur
ing the war.
