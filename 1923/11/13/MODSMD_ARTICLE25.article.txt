TUESDAY
ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING SOCI
ETY will meet in Room 263 in the
Engineering building at 7:15.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY will
meet upstairs in the Women's Club
house at 7:30 p.m. All students in
terested in Christian Science are in
vited.
TRYOUTEES FOR QUAD MANAGER
meet at 7:15 in Quad office.
JUNIOR CLASS meets in Little Thea
ter at 12:50.
DRAMATIC MANAGER TRYOUTEES
meet at dramatic manager's office in
Administration building at 7 p.m.
SPANISH CLUB members and those
intending to become members meet
at pavilion for Quad picture at 1
o'clock. Men please wear dark
clothes.
WEDNESDAY
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at 6:15
in the lobby of the New Union for
dinner.
RADIO CLUB will meet at 7:15 p.m.
in the Corporation Yard.
SCHUBERT CLUB will meet in the
Grey room on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse at 7:15.
EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY
meets at 7:30 in Trophy room of old
Union. Subject for discussion: Cali
fornia's Criminal Syndicalism Law.

SOPHOMORE TRYOUTEES for Ten
uis Manager report to H. J. Willis

gymnasium at four o'clock.

hall at 7:30 this evening.
