# CABBAGES and THINGS
## 
### 
I -—c. s. s.—
To
I think that I shall never see
A human lovelier than he.
His prowess, to the world, attest
Ten honour pins upon his vest.
'Tis he who may in autumn wear
A football helmet on his hair.
A big "S" sweater, red and white,
Assures us all that might is right.
Poems are made by fools like me
But only MEN make the Varsity.
