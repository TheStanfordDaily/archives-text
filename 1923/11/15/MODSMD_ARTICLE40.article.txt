# Mrs. T. J. Hoover Presides At Alumnae Bridge Tea
## 
### 
Mrs. T. J. Hoover was chairman of the
alumnae tea yesterday afternoon at the
Women's Clubhouse. She was assisted by
five other hostesses.
Among those who entertained at bridge
and tea were Mrs. John Sclby, Mrs.
Wager Bradford and Mrs. D. C. Gardner.
