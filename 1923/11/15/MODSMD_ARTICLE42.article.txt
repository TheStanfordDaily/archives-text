# Thirty Glee Club Members To Make Thanksgiving Tour
## 
### 
Thirty members of the Glee club
will make a Thanksgiving trip to
Marysville, Colusa, and Chico. The
club will sing Thursday, November
29, in the Marysville high school, Fri
day, November 30, at Colusa, and Sat
urday at the Normal school at Chico.
Dances for the club will be given at
both Colusa ana Chico.
Members for the trip have been
picked from the eighty-five men on
the club roster by competition.
