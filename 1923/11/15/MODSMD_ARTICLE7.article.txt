# Professor Strong Speaks Before S. F. Business Men
## 
### 
"The Technique of Advertising and
Selling" was the subject of an address by
Professor Edward K. Strong, of the
Psychology department, given last night
at Bellevue, San Francisco, before
a meeting of the Business Men's Re
search Association.
Professor Strong discussed the under
lying elements of selling and advertising
the latest methods of magazines and text
book advertising. He followed this with
a theory of his own for the better adapta
tion of advertising to selling needs.
