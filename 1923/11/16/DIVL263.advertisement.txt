A Talcum for Men
M
U
Talcum Powder is so cooling and refreshing to men
after shaving, after the bath or swim, and for hot,
perspiring and burning feet, it's strange how few
talcums there are that really suit the average man.
We have a talcum which men like. We know so
because they tell us. It's called GENTLEMEN'S
TALC, comes in a tall can of convenient shape for
the suitcase or grip, is smooth and fine in texture,
and is only slightly perfumed.
It's a man's talc, and this is the only store in town
t-hat carries it.
