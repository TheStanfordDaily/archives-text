# Under the Sun
## 
### 
<§> C. N. II <•>
A biologist
and a civil
engineer were
looking out
the window
of a New York
skyscraper at
a long, misty
shape dimly
outlined far
below —
Brooklyn
Bridge.
The engineer
spoke: "Great
feat of
engineering,
that thing
down there."
"So is the
orb web of the
garden spider,"
observed his
scientist
friend.
