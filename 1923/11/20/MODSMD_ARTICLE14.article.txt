# Aurelio Espinosa, Jr., Elected Spanish Club Vice-President for Year
## 
### 
At the Spanish Club meeting last;
night, held in the Women's Clubhouse.
Aurelio Espinosa, Jr., was elected
vice-president for the current year. In
the program that followed, B. A. Bley
gave a talk and Mary Borton sang.
Refreshments were served after the
program.
