# 600 Places Reserved For Big Game Dinner Dance
## 
### 
Over six hundred reservations have
been received by J. E. McDowell for
the fourth annual Big Game Dinner
Dance at the Fairmont Hotel Novem
ber 24.
Today is the last day that reserva
tions will be taken. The affair is to
be held in the Gold Ball and Red
rooms of the Fairmont, from 7:30 to 1.
Students, both men and women, and
alumni, may attend, and tables for
special parties may be arranged. The
plate charge is $2.50.
