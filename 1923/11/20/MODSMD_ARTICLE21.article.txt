# Sellards Appointed to Department of Commerce

### 
John A. Seiiards, instructor of
French at. Stanford last year, has re
ceived a position with the Department
of Commerce at Washington, D. C.,
according to word received by Profes
sor O. W. Johnston of the Romanic
Languages department.
Seiiards took his masters degree at
Stanford in 191 *5. Last year, he re
placed S. A. Smith, associate profes
sor of French, who was absent on
sabatical leave.
Seiiards joined the Stanford ambu
lance unit in the war, and went to
France. There he transferred to the
navy. He was promoted to second
lieutenant under Admiral Wilson in
