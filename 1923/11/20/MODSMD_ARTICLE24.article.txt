# Professor Treat Writes Monthly History Survey
## 
### 
Professor Treat, head of the History
department, is one of a group of
American historians who is contribut
ing a monthly survey of world events
to the Current History magazine. Pro
fessor Treat is in charge of the sec
tion covering the Far East. The first
contribution appeared in the Novem
ber issue. The contributors are twelve
men from twelve leading universities.

Ohio Northern University's new law
building, now nearing completion, will be
named in honor of the late President
Harding, who held the degree of doctor
of law from that university.
