# First Greek War Papers Are Received at Library
## 
### 
The Hoover War library has just
received its first important collection
of war documents from the Greek
government. The shipment includes
two cases of reports from the Greek
parliament and ministry, government
war documents and propaganda litera
ture. Two cases of war documents
from the Italian government were in
cluded in this shipment.
