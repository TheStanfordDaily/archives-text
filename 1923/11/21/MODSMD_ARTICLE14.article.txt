# Sophomores Will Meet Mills in Hockey Game
## 
### 
After defeating the freshman hockey
women 5-0 yesterday afternoon in an
elimination practice, the sophomore
team was chosen to play against the
Mills underclass team Saturday morn
ing at Mills.
The juniors and seniors played a
match at the same time. The score
was 5-2 in favor of the juniors. As
neither team showed up well they will
play another game this afternoon be
fore the coaches announce which team
will meet Mills.
In elimination matches last week
for the California games the sopho
mores and seniors were much the
stronger teams in the upperclass and
underclass matches.
