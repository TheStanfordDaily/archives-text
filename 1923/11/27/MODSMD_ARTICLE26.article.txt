# LITTLE WAVES
## 
### 
Littlq waves among the rushes —
Marcel waves by Madame Musy.
Since the birth of time the rushes
Have been waving, waving, waving,
For, without a wave, the rushes,
Rumor says, will cease their rushing,
Silken, tawney, flaxen, golden,
Henna, bandoline, peroxide,
Just a dollar, cash and carry.
Little waves among the rushes!
Flitter, glimmer, dazzle, sparkle—
Little waves are social virtues;
Outer darkness yawns to gather
Waveless, unconsidered women . . .
Stark, uncouth, and rank pariahs
Scorning waves and Madame Musy
They shall perish unelightened*
Standard of success or failure!
Badge of beauty, badge of culture!
Little waves among the rushes!
