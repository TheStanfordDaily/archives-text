# Thornhill May Be Given Three Year Coaching Contract
## 
### 
"Tiny" Thornhill, Stanford line
coach, will be offered a three-year con
tract to continue his present posi
tion, according to anouncement made
in the Graduate Manager's office to
day. The Board of Athletic Control,
which confirms all appointments, will
meet tomorrow noon.
Andy Kerr's contract has three
more years to run, and "Pop" Warner;
has a five-year contract starting next
year. The expected appointment of
Thornhill will insure a football coach
ing staff consisting of Warner, Kerr,
and Thornhill, for at least three years.
Thornhill is a graduate of Pittsburg,
where he was trained under Warner,
and came to Stanford from Center col
lege, where he coached the line.
