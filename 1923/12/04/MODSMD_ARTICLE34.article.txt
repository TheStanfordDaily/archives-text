# Panorama of U. C. Stadium For Sale at Bookstore
## 
###

University of Caifornia stadium may
now be purchased at the Stanford Book
store for $1.25. The picture is three
feet long and includes everyone who
was in the stadium that day, -and those
on the hill besides.
