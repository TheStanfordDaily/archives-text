# SIGMA DELTA CHI GRANTS CHARTER TO W. S. C. LOCAL
## 
### 
Pullman, Wash. —Sigma Delta Chi,
national journalistic fraternity, has
granted a charter to Alpha Delta Mu,
local petitioning group, at Washing
ton State College. Installation will be
held some time after the first of the
year.
