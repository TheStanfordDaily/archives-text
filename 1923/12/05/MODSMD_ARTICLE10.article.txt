# 1926 Comedy Boosted At Sophomore Meeting
## 
### 
"Captain Applejack," the sophomore
play to be presented Friday evening, was,
boosted at yesterday's meeting of the:
Class of '26. Delmer Daves, chairman,
of the sophomore play committee, spoke;
briefly on the merits of the comedy.
"'Captain Applejack' is worthy of
your support and will meet the approval!
of the campus," said Daves. "I am
echoing the sentiments of Gordon Davis,
who said at last night's rehearsal that he
was well satisfied with the production.!
The cast is unusually representative of j
all types of campus living groups."
Stephen O'Donnell and Millison Hardy |
were appointed to select sophomores to
visit the Convalescent Home each week. |
