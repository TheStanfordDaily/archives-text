# Constance Tamplin Engaged
## 
### 
Constance Tamplin, '24, has an
nounced her engagement to E! Huston
Hart of Holly Hill, South Carolina. He
is a graduate of Wofford College and

at present holds a position as head of
the science department at the William
Warren School in Menlo Park.
