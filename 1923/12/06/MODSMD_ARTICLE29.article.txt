# Examinations for Second Lieutenants Next Spring
## 
### 
Examinations for appointment of
second lieutenants in the regular army
will be held the week commencing
April 24, 1924, according to a letter
received by Major William D. Geary
of the Military department.
Both the preliminary and final ex
aminations will probably be held at
the Presidio in San Francisco.
The information comes from the
headquarters of the Ninth Army area.
