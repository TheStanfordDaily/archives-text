# Announcement
## 
### 
CLASSES IN PHYSICAL education
and athlctics for men will close Friday,
December 7. Make-up classes will be
held in Encina gymnasium at 4:30 daily
for men who have taken more than the
allowed number of cuts. Men are re
quested to report and work off cuts to
prevent their receiving incompletes.
Make-ups will be worked off in special
classes, not in sports in which men are
enrolled.
(Signed) W.H.BARROW.
