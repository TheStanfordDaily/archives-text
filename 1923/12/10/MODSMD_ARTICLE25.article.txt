# U. of Idaho Commends Stanford Grid Victory
## 
### 
A1 Masters, president of the A. S.
S. U., recently received a letter from
the president of the University of
Idaho student body congratulating
Stanford on her football victory over
Idaho and expressing a wish for a
continuance of athletic relations be
tween the two universities.
The recent football contest marked
the first time that Stanford had met
Idaho in any sport.
