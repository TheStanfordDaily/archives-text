# Notice
## 
### 
ALL WOMEN of the Lower Division
are requested to come to the office
of the Dean of Women Thursday from
8:30 to 12, or from 2 to 5 p.m., Friday,
the same hours, or Saturday morning
from 8:30 to 12, December 13, 14, 15,
to arrange their schedules for winter
quarter. The earlier part of the week
appointments may be made for the
last three days of the week. Time
schedules will be out the early part
of the week, so that they may be con
sulted before the appointments.
