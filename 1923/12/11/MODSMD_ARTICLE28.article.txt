# Women May Register For Physical Education Early
## 
### 
All women who plan to take courses
in physical education, either required
or elective,* during winter quarter, may
register in advance Saturday morning
at Roble gymnasium from 8:30 to L!
o'clock, according to the announce
ment of Helen Masters Bunting, physi
cal education director.
Registration will also be carried on
as usual on Registration Day.
Basketball is the major sport for
winter quarter. All sports will be of
fered, however, and the present plan
of giving gymnastics one day a week
and some sport the other two days
will be continued.

Postoffices were first established in
1484.
