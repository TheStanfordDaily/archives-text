# Christmas Card Sales At President's Office For Stanford Clinics
## 
### 
Christmas cards and Christmas
seals, attractively boxed, and a spe
cial Robert Louis Stevenson illuminat
ed Christmas card on parchment paper
are being sold at the President's office
for the benefit of the Stanford Home
for Convalescent Children.
The boxes of Christmas cards and
those of seals and labels are 50 cents
apiece, while the Stevenson Christmas
cards are sold for 25 cents eaoh. Ten
cards are contained In the 50-cent
boxes. A gold seal of the University is
stamped on the cover of each box, and
the Stevenson Christmas cards are
inclosed in envelopes for mailing.
