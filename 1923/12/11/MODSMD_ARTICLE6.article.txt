# Scholarship Offered For Colonial Essay
## 
### 
A scholarship prize of $100 is of
fered by the National Society of Colo
nal Dames resident in the state of
California, to the woman undergradu
ate student who has, in History 21,
presented the best essay in the field
of American colonial history.
This is the second year the scholar
ship has been offered, and it is probable
that the society will make it perma
nent. Last year the contest was won
by Dorothy Shepherd with an essay
on "The Frontier Woman."
Some weeks ago, Professor E. E.
Robinson, who has the matter in
charge, attended the annual meeting
of the society in San Francisco; where
he addressed the members on the re
cent revival of interest in colonial his
tory.
