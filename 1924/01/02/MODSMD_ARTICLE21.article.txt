# GLEE CLUB TO GIVE DANCE IN CLUBHOUSE
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club will give a
dance in the Women's Clubhouse next
Saturday night for the purpose of raising
money for the trip to Los Angeles dur
ing the spring vacation. Whitmer's or
chestra will furnish the music. The dance
will start at 9 o'clock. Tickets are being
sold for one dollar.
