# Tryouts For Masquers Play Will Continue in Assembly Hall Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for Masquers Play will con
tinue tonight in the Assembly hall
from 7 until 10. All students in the Uni
versity are eligible to try out except
Freshman women.
A list of those successful in these
preliminary trials will be posted out
side the green room tomorrow. All on
the list will report for second tryouts
tomorrow from 3 to 5 and 7 to 10.
First tryouts will also be conducted
tomorrow for those who were unable
to appear today.
Masquers have secured February 1
as the date for the production of their
play. Rehersals will commence the
first of next week.
