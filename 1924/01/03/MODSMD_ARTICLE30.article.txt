# DEMOCRACY LEAGUE WILL AWARD ECONOMICS PRIZES
## 
### 
Two Economics prizes have been
awarded to college students during 1924
by the League for Industrial Demo
cracy. The prizes were offered in
memory of Dr. Charles P. Steinmetz,
late vice president of the League. A
committee of five professors and pro
fessional men has been appointed to
make the awards which are for $200
and $100 for first and second places
