# HEBER TILDEN IS SERIOUSLY INJURED IN AUTO ACCIDENT
## 
### 
Heber Tilden, '20, was run down
and seriously injured in San Francisco
last night by an automobile driven by
George A. Rooth, wealthy grain
broker. Tilden was alighting from a
street car as the machine struck him.
He was abandoned by the driver of
the car who fled to his hotel, where he
shot and seriously wounded himself
as detectives were coming to arrest
him. Tilden was taken to the French
hospital where his condition was pro
nounced dangerous.
While at Stanford Tilden was a
member of Phi Delta Tlieta. He served
in the Fourth Stanford Ambulance
unit during the great war.
