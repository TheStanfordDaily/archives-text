# Bill Evans, 1922 Editor Of Daily Palo Alto, Is Engaged to Be Married
## 
### 
The engagement of Bill Evans, for
mer editor of the Daily Palo Alto, to
Miss Harriet Hurd, has recently been
announced. Evans is now living at
Riverside. It is understood that the
marriage will take place soon.
Evans was editor of the Daily Palo
Alto during the spring of 1922. He
was a major in economics and a mem
ber of Beta Theta Pi. He is now as
sociated with his father in business at
Riverside. Miss Hurd is a graduate of
Knox College, Galesburg, Illinois, and
a member of Pi Beta Phi.
