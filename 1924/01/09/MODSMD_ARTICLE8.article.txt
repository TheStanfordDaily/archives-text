# Heat Of Rushed Rushing Blamed For Fire Alarm
## 
### 
Excitement over the rushing invi
tations, which were issued today to
the new women, reached such a white
heat last night that the Kappa Sigma
house, was rumored to be on fire. The
engine arrived, as usual, after practi
cally the entire row population made
an appearance in front of No. 14
Lasuen to witness the debacle. A vic
torious serpentine was formed by the
occupants when they found that the
alarm was false.
