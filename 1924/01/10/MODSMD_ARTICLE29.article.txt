# STANFORD-IDAHO GRID CONTEST MAY BE PLAYED AT SPOKANE
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF IDAHO — The
Idaho Vandals will meet five Pacific
Coast Conference teams next season,
three of them being home games, with
Stanford University, Washington State
College, and University of Oregon.
Although the Stanford-Idaho game
is scheduled to be played in Moscow,
it is possible that it will be played at
Spokane, Washington.

PARIS, France —The Chamber of
Deputies has just passed a bill provid
ing for the issuance of postage stamps
commemorative of the Olympic games
to be held in France next summer.
The Senate already has voted favor
ably upon the measure.
