# FRESHMEN OF BRIGHAM YOUNG UNIVERSITY FINED
## 
###

disobedient freshmen has been adopt
ed by the senior class of Brigham
Young University. If the recreant is
■unable to pay immediately, he is to
be set to work shining shoes in the
various dormitories.
money collected is to be held
