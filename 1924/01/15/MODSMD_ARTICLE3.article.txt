"Economy is best in the long run," ac
cording to the rushing system in force
for the benefit of the new women. When
the new inhabitants of Roble Hall dis
covered that there would be a week of
rushing at the first of the quarter, con
sternation was rife at the lack of fine
plumage, Easter is the deadline date for
new togs, and that day of celebration
is three months in the offing. .
However, as the festivities will con
tinue for one week only, the new women
got out the trusty iron, pressed the
wrinkles out of their brows as well as
their dresses, and gaily sallied forth yes
terday to sample the provender of the
nine celestials who preside in a like num
ber of GcceX' kitchens. So it's "Excel
sior" until Sunday evening, when they
will again pursue the even tenor of their
ways.
