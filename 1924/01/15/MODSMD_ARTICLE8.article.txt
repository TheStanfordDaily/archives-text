# FENCERS PREPARING FOR COMING MATCHES
## 
### 
Coach Harry Maloiley is drilling his
fencers in preparation for a number of
matches with San Francisco teams the
latter part of the month and dyring
February.
Although no definite dates have been
secured, the Stanford men will meet the
Italia Virtus, Olympic Club, and Army
and Navy club teams during the season.
During spring vacation it is probable
that the fencers will take a trip to Los
Angeles, where they will meet the Los
Angeles Athletic Club.
