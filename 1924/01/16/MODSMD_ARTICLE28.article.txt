# MUSIC CLUB ELECTS FOUR NEW MEMBERS
## 
### 
Four new members were elected to
Music Club Tuesday evening. Those
admitted to the club were: Charles
Pratt, pianist; Robert Rathbun, sing
er; Allan Porter, pianist; Winifred
Estabrook, singer.
It has been the policy of the club
to limits its membership to about 25
people. Tryouts are held quarterly.
Plans were discussed for the joint
concert to be given on February 8
by the Schubert Club, Music Club, and
the Glee Club.
