# Junipero Serra Club To Give Shoe-Scuttle In Clubhouse Friday
## 
### 
Junipero Serra club will be host at
the usual Friday evening dance at the
Women's Clubhouse this week. The
wind-jamming will begin at 9 o'clock,
inaugurated by Alabaster's orchestra
The club plans to make it an open
affair, with an invitation list including
the campus and stipulating only the
usual entrance fee. Ordinary store
clothes will strike a happy medium
between tuxedos and numeral sweat
ers.
