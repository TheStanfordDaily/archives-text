# DR. JORDAN TO TALK ON BOK PEACE PLAN TOMORROW EVENING
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will speak on the Bok
Peace Prize at his home tomorrow
evening at 7:30.
"It is a well-written document, and
I cast my vote for the plan without
question," says Dr. Jordan. "It repre
sents a new league of nations, divest
ing all the objectionable features of
the league now in operation. I am
sure, when it is put to the vote of the
people, the Bok Peace Prize plan will
be carried by a large majority."
