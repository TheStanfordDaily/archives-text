# Classical Club Hears Murray's Experiences
## 
### 
Experiences in Greece during the
time of the overthrow of King Con
stantino were recounted by Professor
A. T. Murray of the Classical depart
ment at the meeting of the Classical
Club at his home yesterday evening.
Officers were elected for the re
mainder of the year as follows: Linda
Van Norden, president; Joseph Bur
net, vice-president; Madeleine Eren
nan, secretary; and Arthur Wild,
treasurer. Professor Fairclough was
chairman of the meeting.
