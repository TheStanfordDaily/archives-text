# IDAHO WILL NOT HAVE USUAL SPRING VACATION
## 
### 
MOSCOW, Idaho — The regular
spring vacation at the University of
Idaho from April 2 to 8 has been sus
pended by a recent faculty ruling.
Consequently, the college term will
end a week earlier, allowing students
from southern Idaho to return home
sooner.
