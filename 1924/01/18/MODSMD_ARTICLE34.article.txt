# San Francisco Stanford Club Selects Officers
## 
### 
Eugene F. Kern, '19, was elected
president of the Stanford Men's Club
of San Francisco, at a meeting held in
the Palace hotel Wednesday. Cyril
Nunan, '12, was chosen vice-president,
Wheeler Thayer, ex-'2O, secretary, and
Warren Hastings, '14, treasurer.
A committee was appointed to in
vestigate the possibility of the club es
tablishing its own club rooms in San
Francisco.
