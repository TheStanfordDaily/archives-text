# Professor Gray Lectures In San Jose on Drama
## 
### 
Dr. Henry David Gray of the Eng
lish department will give a series of
lectures on "Present Day Drama" in
San Jose, starting today at 3:15. The
first is called "Drama in Spain."
The lectures will be held at San Jose
High school, but arc open to the gen
eral public. A fee of $5 is charged for
the series.
