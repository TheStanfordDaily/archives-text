# Campus Gas Station Bids For Patronage With Reduced Rates
## 
### 
Reduced rates on gas and lubri
cating products may be enjoyed by
anyone connected with the Univer
sity at the gas station located next
to the firehouse and run by the Uni
versity.
All profits realized from sales go
toward the expenses of the fire de
partment. The firemen are in
charge of the station. Gasoline is
on sale for 15 cents a gallon, one
cent below market price. Oils and
greases are offered at marked re
ductions from the regular prices.
Fuel oil is also carried.
The firemen solicit the patronage
of students and faculty.
