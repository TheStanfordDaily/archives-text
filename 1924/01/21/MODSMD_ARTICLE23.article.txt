# $205,000 WINS DEMOCRATIC CONVENTION FOR NEW YORK
## 
### 
New York nosed out San Francisco
by 17 votes in the contest for the
Democratic national convention at a
meeting of the Democratic National
Committee held recently at Washing
ton. San Francisco offered $200,000
and all convention expenses only to
have New York come back with a
$205,000 bid which clinched the vic
tory.
When Chicago dropped out of the
race the Illinois delegation switched
their support to Manhattan, thus pro
viding the necessary majority. The
final choice is considered unfavorable
to the McAdoo candidacy.
