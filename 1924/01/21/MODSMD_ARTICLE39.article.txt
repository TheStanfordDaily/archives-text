# On'n Off Campus
## Stanford Graduates Wed 
### 
—M. V. D.—

The marriage of Dorothy Bernard,
'20, and Wallace A. Craig, '22, was
solemnized in the Stanford Memorial
Church on December 22.
Mrs. Craig will continue her work
as a member of the San Francisco
State Teachers College faculty. Craig
is a chemical engineer for the Union
Oil Company at Eoleum. He and Mrs.
Craig will live in Oakland.
