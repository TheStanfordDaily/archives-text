THURSDAY
STANFORD DAMES meet at 3 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse. Mrs. G.
D. Merner will tell of lier recent
visit to the German Passion Play.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA meet in New
Union for dinner at 6:15. Meeting to
follow at 7:15.
FRESHMAN WRANGLERS meet at
7 o'clock in Room 358 Roble.
WRANGLERS will meet at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
SATURDAY

C. assembles at Military Headquar
ters Saturday morning at 8:30 for
Quad pictures.
