# FENCING TEAM WINS FROM ITALIAN CLUB
## 
### 
The Stanford fencing squad won six
matches out of nine when it crossed
swords with the Unione Sportiva I tali
ana, Italian sports club of San Francisco,
last evening in the first matches of the
season.
The Cardinal team was composed of
John Wallace, who won two matches;
Michel DeCazotte, winner of three sets;
and George Smith, who won one. Coach
Harry Maloney accompanied the men.
Last night's matches were the first in
a series conducted in the American Fenc
ing League Association.
Stanford meets the University of Cali
fornia fencers on March 14.

Veneers as thin as 106 to the inch
can be cut with present-day machinery.
