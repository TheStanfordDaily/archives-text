# Paul Davis Given 3-Year Contract as Graduate Manager
## 
### 
Paul Davis, '19, will continue to
be Graduate Manager for another
three years, by action of the Board
of Trustees at noon today in con
firming the contract recommended
by the Board of Athletic Control.
Davis took charge of Stanford
athletics last year after having
worked a year under W. F. Fletch
er. His appointment for 1924-25 was
recommended at the last meeting
of the Board of Athletic Control.
Under Davis' management the
work of the Graduate Manager's of
fice has been systematized along
the same lines started by Fletcher,
and several improvements in the
gymnasium, pavilion, and arrange
ment of athletic fields inaugurated.
