# Ku Klux Klan on Campus To Be Euphronia Subject
## 
### 
"Resolved, That the Ku Klux Klan
movement on the campus should be
repressed" is the subject to be de-
bated by Euphronia tomorrow night
in the Trophy room of the Union.
The meeting is open to all inter-
ested.
Any, who wish to try out for the
society may do so at the meeting to
morrow night, by giving a prepared
speech on some aspect of the Klan
topic.
