# FRESHMEN GUESTS AT FIRST TOYON SMOKER
## 
### 
Freshmen were the guests of Toyon
Club last night at the first smoker
held in the club room of the new
dormitory. More than 150 first-year
men were present. Speeches by sev
eral students, musical and oral enter
tainment, gymnastics, and a burlesque
act were included in the program.
The underclassmen were shown
through the new building and made
acquainted with the plan to be fol
lowed in the future regarding the
housing situation at Stanford.
Pretzels and near beer proved to be
a unique feature in refreshments serv
ed at the smoker. Bill Gage, president
of Toyon, presided, with Dan Gage
acting as chairman of the program
committee.
