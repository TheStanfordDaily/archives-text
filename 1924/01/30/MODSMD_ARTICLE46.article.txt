# 1927 Debate Team Tryouts Take Place on February 6
## 
### 
Tryouts for the freshman debating
team will be held next Wednesday
evening, February G, at 7 o'clock in
the Little Theatre. These trials will
be held under the auspices of Nes
toria, Euphronia, and Delta Sigma
Rho. Each freshman will hav>e five
minutes in which to speak and three
minutes for the rebuttal on the ques
tion, "Should the United States recog
nize the present government in Rus
sia?"
Plans are being made for freshman
intercollegiate debates with California
and the College of the Pacific which
will be held early next quarter.
