# Theta Sigma Phi Dance Follows California Game
## 
### 
Open house to all comers—a dance
in the Women's Clubhouse after the
first Stanford-California game on Sat
urday, February 9 —will be managed
by Theta Sigma Phi, women's journal
istic fraternity.
Music by Alabaster's orchestra will
follow the chalking up of the final
score, after a short intermission. Ad
mission will be $1.
