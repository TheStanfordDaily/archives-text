# Sophomore Women Will Picnic in Lake Bottom
## 
### 
"Sophomore Rain Riot" is the title
given to the picnic for sophomore wo
men which will be held in the lake bot
tom next Tuesday afternoon. In case
of rainy weather, the picnic will be
transferred to the Women's Clubhouse.
The gathering, which will last from
5:30 to 8, is for the purpose of getting
together all women of the sophomore
class. Stunts will be given and a 50-
cent supper served. Tickets are being
sold by sophomores in the women's
living groups.
