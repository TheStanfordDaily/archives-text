# WOMEN'S INTERCLASS HOOP COMPETITION STARTS FEBRUARY 19
## 
### 
Woman's inter-ciasa basketball com
petition will open with the senior
freshman game February 19, accord
ing to a schedule issued today by
Grace Strobel, basketball manager.
Training will begin next Tuesday.
There will be no outside competition
this quarter.
With more than 100 women out
for the sport, the coaches. Miss Helen
Bunting, and Miss Isabel Capps, re
port a wealth of excellent material
and a promise of close competition.
The freshmen are expected to make
a particularly good showing with a
number of experienced players. The
seniors, who won the championship
last year, anticipate retaining it.
