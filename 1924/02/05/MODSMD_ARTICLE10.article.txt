# FRESHMAN SWIMMERS TO MEET JUNIOR OLYMPICS
## 
### 
The Stanford freshman swimming
team has accepted the challenge of
the junior Olympic Club for a meet
to he held sometime this month. The
date for the meet will be decided this
week.
Freshman swimmers have not had
a chance to demonstrate their ability
so far. Rob Williams swims the 220-
vard free style in 2:3S flat, and clips
the 100 in better than a minute flat.
Phil Brooks does 1:01 in the century.
"Dutch" Fletcher, younger brother of
Charlie, swims the 100-yard back
stroke in 1:15 and the 50 in 25 3-5.
Jimmy Ilanley, freshman captain,
and John Kenney can do 1:18 jn the
breast stroke.
