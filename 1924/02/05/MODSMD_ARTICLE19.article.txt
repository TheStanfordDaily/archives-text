TODAY'S HELPFUL HOUSE HINT
If by chance the soup at the evening meal is too rich you may Immediately
deduct that there is too much fat in it. If the cold soup is poured through
| the table cloth with another soup dish below the cloth to catch the flowing
! liquid, the superfluous fat will be strained off. —D. A.
