# WISCONSIN MAY COMPETE IN POUGHKEEPSIE REGATTA
## 
### 
University of Wisconsin has ac
cepted tentatively an invitation to
compete in the intercollegiate regatta
on the Hudson river at Poughkeepsie,
June 17, for the first time in a decade.
Princeton is doubtful about enter
ing the regatta, while the Naval Acad
emy has definitely decided not to com
pete again, states Maxwell Stevenson,
chairman of the board of stewards of
the Intercollegiate Rowing Asociation.
