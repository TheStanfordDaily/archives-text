# ON AN EMPTY POSTOFFICE BOX
## 
### 
OH THE LIGHT THAT'S GLANCING GLANCING
SO ENTRANCING—DANCING DANCING
ON THE EMPTY EMPTY BOTTOM
OF MY OLD POSTOFFICE BOX
MORNING EVENING MEETS ME MEETS ME
TWINKLINK TWINKLING GREETS ME GREETS ME—
OH THE LIGHT THAT'S DANCING DANCING
SO ENTRANCING GLANCING GLANCING
ON THE EMPTY EMPTY BOTTOM
OF MY OLD POSTOFFICE BOX
