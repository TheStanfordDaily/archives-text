# Grinstead To Play For Dance After California Game
## 
### 
Following the game with California
Saturday evening, a celebration will
be in order at the Clubhouse, where
Theta Sigma Phi has obtained Grin
stead's orchestra to furnish music for
a campus dance.
The dance will last from 9:30 until
12. Decorations will consist of the
usual band of roughs around the walls.
Admission charge of $1.00 will admit
any couple regardless of age, race, or
bawl-out average.
