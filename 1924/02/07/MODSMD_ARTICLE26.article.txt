# CREW PRACTICE BEGINS AT U. OF CALIFORNIA
## 
### 
One hundred and thirty crew men
reported to "Ky" Ebright, new U. C.
: coach, at the sign-up rally recently.
Only two months of practice remain
until the annual meet with Washing
ton in April. California will probably
go East and possibly compete in the
Olympic game tryouts if she turns the
this year. The varsity will start its
trick on the crack Washington crew
workout today on the Oakland Estu
i ary.
