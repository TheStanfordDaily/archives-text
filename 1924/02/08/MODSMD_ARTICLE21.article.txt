# "Young Man and Life" Will Be Sermon Topic
## 
### 
"What shall a young man do with
his life?" is the subject of the sermon
to be delivered by the Rev. W. K.
Guthrie of the First Presbyterian
Church in San Francisco, Sunday
morning in Memorial Church.
The Rev. Mr. Guthrie will make
his talk a personal story of how he
came to go into the ministry. He will
give his opinions of the religious
situation and the church of today,
with suggestions for saving this great
heritage for the future.
