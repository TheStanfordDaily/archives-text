# INTEREST SHOWN IN MID-YEAR ELECTION
## 
### 
Twenty-six new nominations for
various Student Body and Class offices
had been made by noon today when
nominations for the mid-year election
were closed. Twenty of these nomina
tions were for class offices, and six
were for Student Body positions.
Three men declined their nomina
tions.
Unless there is a large number of
nominees that decline before tomor
row noon, the final hour for declining,
this election will witness the strongest
competition that has been displayed in
several years.
The polls will open at 8:45 Tuesday
morning and remain open continuous
ly until 2 p.m.

have been made since yesterday noon:
