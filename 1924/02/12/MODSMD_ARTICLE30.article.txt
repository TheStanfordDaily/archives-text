# Helen Thoburn, '07, to Talk On China at Y. W. Meeting
## 
### 
Helen Thoburn, '07, will speak at a
special Y. W. C. A. meeting tomorrow
afternoon at 4 o'clock on the second
floor of the Women's Clubhouse. Miss
Thoburn has just returned on a fur
lough from China, where she has been
doing Y. W. C. A. work for the past
five years. She will speak on phases
of her work in China, telling her ex
periences in that country.
Miss Thoburn was a member of Cap
and Gown, English Club, and took part
in Y. W. and other campus activities.
She is a member of Gamma Phi Beta.
Her father was well-known as chap
lain and professor of biology.
All women are urged to attend the
meeting Thursday.
