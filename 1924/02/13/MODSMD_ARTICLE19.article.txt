# Dr. Jordan Will Talk On Roosevelt, Wilson
## 
### 
"Roosevelt and Wilson As I Knew
Them" is the subject that Dr. Jordan
will discuss at his regular Thursday
evening lecture tomorrow at 7:30 at
his home, Serra house.
Dr. Jordan will tell of several per
sonal experiences during his acquain
tance with former President Roosevelt
in the middle eighties. His talk will
also include incidents in both his per
sonal and official relationship with
the late President Wilson while he
was a young professor at Princeton.
