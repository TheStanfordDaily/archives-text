# Ram's Head Will Give Campus Dance Friday
## 
### 
A super-feature orchestra composed
of a portion of Ram's Head musical
talent and the Mertz-Alabaster synco
symplionists, will provide the music
for the campus dance in the Women's
Clubhouse Friday night after the
Ram's Head show.
The admission charge will be 75c,
ami the festivities will end at 1 o'clock
Saturday morning.
