# SEQUOIA CLUB ELECTS KAUFFMAN PRESIDENT
## 
### 
F. M. "Dutch" Kauffman, '24, was
elected president of the Sequoia Club
yesterday on a white ballot.
He is a member of the Scalpers and
ran on the '24 freshman track team.
He was also president of the '24
sophomore class.
Stephen O'Donnell, '26, was chosen
secretary for the coming term in the
only contest marked by any opposi
tion.
Byron Reinemund, '25, and Russell
Walter, '24, were elected vice-presi
dent and treasurer respectively on
white ballots.
