# Euphronians Discuss Place Of Debating at Stanford
## 
### 
"Place and Purpose of Debating on
j the Stanford Campus" was the topic of
• discussion at last night's meeting of
i the Euphronia debating society.
Ways and means to arouse general
interest in debating here were proposed.
: it is the aim of the society to establish
' debating on as firm a footing at Stan
' ford as it holds at other universities.
