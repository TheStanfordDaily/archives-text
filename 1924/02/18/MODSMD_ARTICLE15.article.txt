# STANFORD DIAMOND SQUAD DEFEA TS IRELANDS 6 TO 4
## Varsity Showers Shamrock Aggregation Under Melee of Runs and Hits 
### 
Stretching its winning streak to five
straight games, the Cardinal varsity
beat Cliff Ireland's ball club Saturday
! afternoon, 6-4.
The visitors got away to a two-run
lead in the first ir.ning on a combina
tion of hits and errors, and added an
other run in the third.
Stanford scored one run in the sec
ond and counted again in the third.
In the fourth stanza, the varsity, on
five clean hits and a boot on the part
of the umpire, pushed across four
runs, enough to put the game on ice.
Woodward. Mulchay, and Lowen
stein singled in rapid succession,
Woodward scoring. Roberts was hit
by the pitcher and the bases were
loaded. Cuddeback and McCandless
i came through with another pair of
hits, driving in three more scores. |
Woodward came to bat for the second
time in the inning, and grounded to
! first, base for the third out.
Schorr relieved Pierotti for Ireland's
i team, and held the varsity to two hits
during the remaining innings.
I Stanford played a poor game in the
field, making five errors behind Low
i enstein. Mulchay, at short, had an off,

day and pulled Woodward off the bag
with several throws.
The hitting of the varsity was all
that could be desired. McCandless got
three hits. Roberts, Cuddeback, and
Johnston each connected twice, and
the rest of the team accounted for
three more bingles.
Cbrrigan, in right for Ireland, holder
of several world war medals, among
which are decorations of foreign gov
ernments and the Distinguished Ser
vice Cross, brought a lot of fight back
from the war with him and collected
a couple of hits.
