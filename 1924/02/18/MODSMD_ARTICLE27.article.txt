# TRYOUTS FOR WOMEN'S DEBATE TO BE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Tryouts for the women's intercolle
giate debate will be held tonight at
7:15 in the Women's Clubhouse. Each
candidate will be required to give an
extemporaneous speech on some local
subject.
Stanford women will compete with
the University of California and Mills
College in the triangular debate on
March 12. The debate will be under
the auspices of Wranglers Society.
All University women are urged to
try out for it. The places at which
the debates will be held are as fol
lows: Stanford vs. Mills at Stanford,
California vs. Stanford at California,
Mills vs. California at Mills.
