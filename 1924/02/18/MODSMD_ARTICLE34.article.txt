# Home of J. R. Nourse, '97, Destroyed by Fire Friday
## 
### 
The home of James R. Nourse,
Stanford '97, in Washington, D. C.,
was destroyed by fire last Friday eve
ning, according to dispatches from
Washington yesterday. Nourse is serv
ing as a correspondent for California
newspapers. Mrs. Nourse and the chil
dren were home alone at the time
of the fire, but all escaped without
injury.
At Stanford Nourse was editor of
the Daily Palo Alto and was active in
student affairs. He is a brother of
Judge John T. Nourse, of Palo Alto,
who is a member of the University
Board of Trustees.
The pulp left after extracting oil
from olives is burned as fuel in Spain.
