# SENIOR ASSESSMENT FOR 1925 QUAD DUE
## 
### 
The second installment of the Quad
senior assessment is now due and
payable at the Quad office in the
Administration building any afternoon
from 1 to 4 o'clock. March 1 is the
deadline date for the payment of the
$4.00, and anyone who fails to turn
in that amount will forfeit the $3.00
already paid and be unable to secure
a Quad. A fine of 25 cents will be
charged for lost receipts.
