# Dr. Jordan Will Talk on How War Weakens Nation
## 
###

subject that Dr. Jordan will discuss
at his regular Thursday evening con
ference this week.

fare destroys the best elements in
every nation. War condemns a coun
try to backwardness physically, patri
otically, and intellectuallj', and is the
only way that any nation has ever
fallen by self-destruction.
