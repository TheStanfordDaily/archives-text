# Freshman Nine Beats William Warren, 4-3
## 
### 
The freshman baseball nine defeated
the William Warren school team yes
terday afternoon on the local diamond
by the score of 4 to 3.
Steady improvement is being shown
by the freshmen with every practice
game and Coach Harry Wolter expects
to turn over a squad of potential win
ners to Coach "Husky" Hunt when the
basketball season closes.
