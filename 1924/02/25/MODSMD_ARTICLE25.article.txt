# Varsity Tennis Rankings Announced by H. M. Davis
## 
### 
After a week of play, the varsity
tennis rankings have been anounced
by H. M. Davis, assistant director of
Encina gymnasium.
The rankings are as follows:
1. V. T. Mertz 9. F. B. Leeds
2. N. J. deßack 10. W. O. Davidson
3. F. M. Tussing 11. R. R. Murphy
4. R. H. Hinkley 12. F. D. Mannoccir
5. C. H. Overfelt 13. S. S. Millikan
6. P. L. Fairchild 14. H. J. Willis
7. T. C. McCleave 15. M. A. Row
S. H. T. Coffin 16. E. W. Strong
Davis says that each man is ex
pected to play two challenges this
week —one with the man ranked be
low him, and one with the man ranked
above him.
