# Professor Swain Leaves For Trip Through East
## 
### 
Professor Robert E. Swain of the
Chemistry department left last Thurs
day for a trip to the East. He will go
to Boston and return about the middle
of March.
In addition to several speaking en
gagements Professor Swain is plan-'
ning to visit a number of the eastern
and middle western universities in
order to look over the work which is j
being accomplished in chemistry. He
expects to see many Stanford people
while he is away.
