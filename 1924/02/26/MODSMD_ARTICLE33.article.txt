# ADDITIONAL NOMINEES PLACED ON Y. W. LIST
## 
### 
Several names have been added to
the list of nominations for Y. W. C. A.
offices. The nominations will close
Tuesday evening, and voting will take
place on Quad on Thursday, 9-12, and
on Friday, 9-11.
The full list of nominations is as
follows: President, Anna Fitzhugh;
vice-president, Susan Hyde, Dorothy
Dunlavy; secretary, Bertha Shedd,
Margaret Watson: treasurer, Frances
Lawrence, Doris Swayze; undergradu
ate representative, Ruth Snedden.
