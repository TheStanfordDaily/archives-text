# Coover to Describe Mind Reading Before Euphronia
## 
### 
"Mind Reading, Mental Telepathy,
and Hypnotism" is the subject of a
talk to be given by Professor J. E.
Coover of the Psychology department
at a meeting of the Euphronia debat
ing society Wednesday night.
Professor Coover will give a short
demonstration of mind reading and
hypnotism after his lecture. Discus
sion of the subject by members of the
society will follow.
The meeting will start promptly at
7:30 in the trophy room of the Old
Union.
