# Water Polo Teams Meet California Here Friday
## 
### 
Varsity and freshman water polo
teams will play California in Encina
pool Friday afternoon at 4:15. The
varsity lineup will probably be O'Con
nor, Fletcher, Meyers (C), forwards;
McCallister, Schmeider and Allen,
backs; and Collett, goal.
For the freshmen, Stewart (C),
Brooks and Fletcher will play forward;
Sibbett, Graham and Herth, backs, and
either Paulson or H inkle at goal.
Women will be admitted to the pool
for the first time this year at Friday's
meet. General admission is fifty cents
and tickets for students with cards are
twenty-five cents.
