# Sixty-Eight Graduate Scholarships Are Open For Stanford Women
## 
### 
Over sixty-eight scholarships for
graduate work in all fields in various
institutions of America are open to
Stanford women. The scholarships
range from $200 to $1500 per year.
Applications for the scholarships
must be in by March 15. Any graduate
or senior woman interested in apply
ing for the awards should get details
from 'the Dean of Women.
Fifty of the scholarships are offered
through Bryn Mawr. They are open to
graduate women of any recognized in
stitution. The announcement of the
scholarships states that holders of fel
lowships are not permitted to teach
or hold paid positions. Scholarship
holders are allowed to do a small
amount of teaching.
