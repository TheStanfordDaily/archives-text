# OREGON INITIATES "O" MEN AT BASKETBALL GAME
## 
### 
Between the halves of the recent
Oregon-O. A. C. basketball game the
Order of the "O" initiated the men
who won their letters in football,
track, baseball, and tennis. Dancers,
prima donnas, and poseurs were dis
covered among Oregon's choice ath
letes, to the intense delight of the
spectators.
