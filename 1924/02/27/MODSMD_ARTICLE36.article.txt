# Lincoln and Darwin Will Be Subject of Dr. Jordan
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will discuss "Darwin and
Lincoln," the two greatest men of
their century, at his regular Thursday
evening conference this week.
"The two men were born on the
same day," says Dr. Jordan, "125 years
ago. Darwin was the'greatest investi
gator of his time, while Lincoln had
the broadest knowledge and greatest
sympathy with democracy of any man
of his day."
