# Lincoln and Darwin Is Jordan's Topic Tonight
## 
### 
Incidents in the lives of Darwin and
Lincoln, two of the foremost men of
their century, will be the chief topic of
Dr. Jordan's lecture at 7:30 this evening
at Serra house.
Dr. Jordan will discuss Darwin as a
scientist and a man as contrasted with
Lincoln, the foremost thinker along po
litical and democratic lines of any man
of his century.
The men were both born on February
12, 1899, and each reached the pinnacle
in his line of thought and action.
