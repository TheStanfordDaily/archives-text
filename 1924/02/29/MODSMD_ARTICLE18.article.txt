# STANFORD TO DEBATE TECHNOLOGY SCHOOL AT REDWOOD MONDAY
## 
### 
Stanford debaters meet the Cali
fornia Institute of Technology of Pasa
dena at S o'clock Monday evening in
the Redwood City high school audi
torium. It will be the first off-campus
debate of the year for the Cardinals.
"Resolved, that the United States
should enter the World Court" is the
question. The discussion may touch
points discussed in the Bok Peace
Plan arguments held here recently.
George Crocker and Percy Spencer
will uphold the affirmative for Stan
ford. Both men have bad previous ex
perience on varsity teams. The same
men will oppose the University of
Oregon in the tri-state debate in the
Little Theatre on March 6.
