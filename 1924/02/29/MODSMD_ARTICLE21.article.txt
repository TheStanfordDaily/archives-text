# Marjorie Woolsey Engaged To Yale Alumnus
## 
### 
Marjorie Woolsey, Y. W. C. A. sec
retary, announced her engagement to
John C. Kittle last night at the weekly
dinner of Y. W. cabinet. They will be
married next summer.
Miss Woolsey has occupied her pres
ent position at Stanford for three
years. She is a Vassar graduate, of
the class of '16. Kittle is a San Fran
cisco man, and they plan to make that
city their home. He was graduated
from Yale in 1904.
