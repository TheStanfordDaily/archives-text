# FRESHMAN BALL NINE PLAYS ALAMEDA HIGH
## 
### 
"Husky" Hunt's freshman ball nine
will stage nine innings with Alameda
high school on the babe field at 3:30 to
morrow afternoon. The trans-bay
school is reputed to have a powerful
aggregation.
Collins will probably start the game
in the pitcher's box.
