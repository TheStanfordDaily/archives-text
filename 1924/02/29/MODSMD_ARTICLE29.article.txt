# Fiesta
## 
### 
Women are offered an opportunity to try out for the Fiesta Monday
afternoon and evening in the Assembly Hall.
One of the main purposes of the pageant is to provide an activity in
which many women may participate. Seventy women will be used in the
various group dances alone. In there will be speaking parts to
be taken. For those who are not in the cast, there is a chance to take part
in costuming, designing scenery, and helping with the administrative end.
The pageant last year was very creditable and every woman in the
university should make an effort to have some hand in this year's produc
tion. The committees in charge are capable and the Fiesta, which is to be
presented on May 3, should even eclipse former presentations .
