# LEAP YEAR DANCE IS GIVEN BY WASHINGTON STATE WOMEN
## 
###

lege are staging an elaborate Leap
Year dance tonight. The women are
purchasing the tickets and filling out
the programs. Some of the hostesses
will taxi for their dates and others are
said to deem that treats after the
dance will make a hit.
