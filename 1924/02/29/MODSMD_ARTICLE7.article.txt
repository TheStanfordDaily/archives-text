# STANFORD DAMES DECIDE ON PARTY
## 
### 
March 8 was set at a meeting of
the Stanford Dames yesterday after
noon as the date for their quarterly
party which will be given in the Club
house.
The program yesterday consisted of
a group of violin solos played by Mrs.
Eliot Blackwelder to the accompani
ment by Mrs. C. E. Franklin. Mrs.
Graves gave some dramatic readings.
