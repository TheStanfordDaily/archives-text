# Wallace O'Connor Elected 1925 Swimming Captain
## 
### 
Wally O'Connor was electcd captain
of the 1925 swimming team at a recent
meeting of the men on the squad.
O'Connor was captain of the 1926
freshmen team and this year has been
one of the most consistent point win
ners on the varsity. In the meet with
California this year he swam on the
winning relay squad and took first in
the and 100-yard free style.
He is Pacifiic Coast A. A. U. 880-
yard free style champion and holder of
the Olympic Club trophy for the 440-
yard free style. O'Connor is a sure
I prospect for the Olympic games.
