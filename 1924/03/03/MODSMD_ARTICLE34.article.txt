# INDOOR RECORD FOR FIVE HUNDRED YARDS BROKEN
## 
### 
Joseph Tierney, Holy Cross distance
star, broke the world's indoor record
for 500 yards when he broke the tape
in 58 seconds flat in the N. Y. A. C.
games, eclipsing by 2-5 of a second the
former record made by J. W. Driscoll
of the Boston Athletic Association two
years ago.
