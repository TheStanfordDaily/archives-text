# Notice
## 
### 
Men now living in Encina hall who
wish to reserve their rooms for the
Spring quarter should call at the of
fice of the Dean of Men on Monday,
Tuesday, or Wednesday, March 3, 4,
and 5.
Drawings will be held at 7 p.m.,
Thursday, March G, at the Encina of
fice. Reservation dates for the other
halls will be announced later.
