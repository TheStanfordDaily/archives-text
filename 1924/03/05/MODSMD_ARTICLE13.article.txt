# "DUTCH" McCALLISTER ELECTED CAPTAIN OF WATER POLO TEAM
## 
### 
"Dutch" McCallister was elected cap
tain of the 1925 water polo team at a
recent meeting of the water polo seven.
McCallister was the logical man for the
position having held down a regular
berth, at guard, on the varsity for the
last two years. He also played on the
1925 freshman team.
In the California game last Friday,
he was one of the outstanding stars.
McCallister is also president of the
class of 1925, assistant yell-leader, a
member of the winning relay swimming
team against California.
