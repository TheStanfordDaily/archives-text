# Bolton White Elected Member of Music Club
## 
### 
Bolton White was elected a member
of Music Club at its meeting last night.
The club is planning to give a program
on Tuesday, April 15, in the Little
Theater to take the place of the Tues
day evening lecture. A tentative pro
gram was dran up.
