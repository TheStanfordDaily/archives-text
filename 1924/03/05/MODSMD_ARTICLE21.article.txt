# O. A. C. CO-EDS DO NOT OVERDO PRIMPING
## 
### 
Primping is not overdone by O. A. C.
co-eds according to the time reports
recorded by thirty women in the home
management class. The time spent on
self is not in excess of that required
for health. Nineteen of the women re
corded an average of eight hours of
steep, but only three girls spent suf
ficient time in the mastication of their
food. Only one girl indicated no time
wasted, while three reported no social
activity and three no time in develop
ment
