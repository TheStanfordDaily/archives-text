# HEAD OF NEW TEXAS TECH GATHERS DATA ON STANFORD CAMPUS
## 
### 
Paul Whitfield Horn, president of
the Texas Technical College which is
to he located at Lubbock, Texas, and
Carl G. Staats, who is an architect, were
on the campus Monday afternoon and
made a thorough inspection of the Uni
versity in all its phases.
The Texas Technical College is a
new institution and has not as yet erect
ed its buildings, but is to be completed
in the near future.
President Horn and Mr. Staats are
making an extended tour of the entire
country, visiting all the prominent uni
versities in an attempt to gather all
the data and information possible on
just how they are built and conducted.
Both expressed the opinion that Stan
ford compared very favorably with any
institution that they had yet visited.
