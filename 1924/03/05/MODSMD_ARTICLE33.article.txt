# PRESIDENT BIRD OF OCCIDENTAL IS GUEST ON STANFORD CAMPUS
## 
### 
As a result of receiving a gift for
the construction of a new women's
dormitory at Occidental College in
Los Angeles, President Bird, together
with several of the trustees of that
institution were on the campus yester
day for the purpose of making a thor
ough inspection of the dormitory sys
tem employed at Stanford. Included in
the party was Mr. William Benson,
donor of the gift.
The party of nine was the guest of
Mr. W. Mayo Newhall, President of
the Stanford Board of Trustees. After
lunching at Roble hall, they were con
ducted about the campus, paying par
ticular attention to both the men's
and women's dormitories.
