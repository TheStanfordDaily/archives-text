# Glee Club Dance Ends Quarter's Duties For Women's Clubhouse Floor
## 
### 
Be it known that the Glee Club
dance in the Women's Clubhouse ter
minates promptly at 1 a.m., Saturday.
And be it known that after this occa
sion, it will be more fitting to bask in
the light of midnight oil rather than
the warm glow of social satellites. For
there shall be plenty of work to per
form in these last few weeks of the
quarter, and that in view of the plenti
tude, no more dates have been
granted.
If the end of the quarter brings nu
merous bills, reflect that fifty cents
plus a stub from a Glee Club concert
ticket will secure admission to the
clubhouse. Otherwise, one dollar must
be forthcoming.
The Mertz-Alabaster orchestra will
occupy the position of prominence on
the stage. More will be heard from
them tomorrow night.
