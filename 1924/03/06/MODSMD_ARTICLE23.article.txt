# Basketball Captain
## 
### 
The varsity basketball men are to be congratulated upon their choice of
a captain for next year.
Dave Oliva is an ideal man for the position. He is one of the best
standing guards on the Pacific Coast and is a steady and consistent basket
ball player. His absence after his injury this year was sorely felt in the
California series.
Next year should be his best on the court. He should be a fine leader.

Interested persons may get the results of the California-Washington
game tonight by calling Palo Alto 1074, the Daily Palo Alto office, any
time after 9:30.

Business men may go back to their work, students to their books, and
professional men to their clients. Jack Dempsey and Babe Ruth are on
the road to recovery.

Having a picture taken for the 1925 Quad is a comparatively simple
matter. The victim only has to please a photographer or two, a couple
tryoutees, and possibly a spectator or two.
