# New Woman's Scholarship Founded by Cap and Gown
## 
### 
Plans for raising money for a new
woman's scholarship were discuss-ed
at a meeting of Cap and Gown yester
day. The scholarship will be awarded
for the first time next fall quarter and
will amount to at least $150.
Letters to alumnae members inform
ing them of the banquet to be held
May 3 will include a plea for money J
for the scholarship. The proceeds of
the banquet will go toward the fund.
Doughnut sales are to be held and
dances will be given to raise money.
According to Miss Yost the new
Cap and Gown scholarship will help
to meet a very urgent demand.
