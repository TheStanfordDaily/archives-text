# Chemistry Journal Editor To Talk Before Chemists
## 
### 
Harrison E. Howe, editor of the
Journal of Industrial and Engineering
Chemistry, is to be the speaker at
the 146 th regular meeting of the Cali
fornia Section of the American Chem
ical Society which is to be held at the
Engineers' Club, 57 Post St., San Fran
cisco, tonight at 8 o'clock. His talk will
be about the activities of the Ameri
can Chemical Society and the Journal
of Industrial and Engineering Chem
istry. All chemistry students who are
interested are invited to attend this
meeting.
