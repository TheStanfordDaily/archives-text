# FROSH TENNIS TEAM DEFEATED IN MEET BY LOWELL PLAYERS
## 
### 
Freshman tennis players divided the
singles and lost both sets of doubles
' to LiOwell High last Saturday.
Captain Holman had 110 difficulty
in downing Seller, 6-4, 6-4 in the first
; match, and Kurihara took two sets
from Gold, 6-3, 6-0 in the third. Gard-
I ner and Baer were less fortunate, los
j ing to Heilbron and Stewart respec
tively by scores of 6-1, 6-4, and 6-4, 7-5.
In the first match of doubles, Men
zies and Anawalt lost to Gold and
Stewart, 6-3, 7-5, while Holman and
! Kurihara were defeated by Seller and
J Heilbron, 6-3, 6-4.
The loss of their first meeting should
| not reflect any discredit on the fresh
men as they played a brand of tennis
which gives promise of a winning
team after a little more development.
