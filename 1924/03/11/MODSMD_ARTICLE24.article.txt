# CABBAGES and Things
## 
### 
A symphonic noise,
A dramatic crash,
To your seat you numbly hold,
Your very flesh creeps,
Your heart's in leaps.
And your blood runs hot and cold.
—That's Fritz Kreisler's fault.
A symphonic noise,
A dramatic crash,
To your B. V. D.'s you numbly hold,
Your very flesh creeps,
Your old heart sleeps,
And your blood runs hot and cold.
—That's the furnace man's fault.
