# Queening Classes Get Off to Early Start This Evening
## 
### 
Registration for the course in ad
vanced Queening begins this evening
at 9 o'clock when the doors of ihe
Women's Clubhouse will be thrown
open to all snakes that have the
proper prerequisites, blonde or sun
burned. Theta Sigma Phi has charge
of the first three-hour lab period deal
ing with the theory and practice cf
modern dancing, while the Mertz-Ala
baster orchestra furnishes the neces
sary working materials. Distinctive ef
forts will be immediately rewarded by
the organized Roughs' corrector sys
tem. No examinations have been
scheruled, and the tuition fee is only
one dollar. It's a pipe, try to get in!
