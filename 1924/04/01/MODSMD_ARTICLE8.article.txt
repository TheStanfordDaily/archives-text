Rules governing the play:
1. All games shall start promptly at
4:15 p. m.
[ 2. The Baseball diamonds are num
bered and located between the old polo
field and the military headquarters.
3. Report to your instructor in ad
! vance if you wish to be excused from
physical training classes to play base
ball.
4. The games shall be of seven in
nings.
5. Games may be played before, but
not after, the scheduled date. Games

may he postponed only if the field is
unavailable on the day when the game
has been scheduled. This postponement
must in every case be sanctioned by the
Director of Intramural Athletics.
6. The championship shall be deter
mined on a percentage basis; any team
failing to appear forfeits; if both teams
tail to appear, both teams are declared
losers on the percentage basis.
7. Any player engaging in any rough
ness or retaliation shall be put out of
that particular game.
8. Men dropped from varsity and
freshman squads are eligible for intra
mural games. The varsity and fresh
man coaches shall post a list, after each
cut has been made in their teams, of
the men who are ineligible to play in
tramural baseball. The men who ap
pear on the final list cannot play intra
mural baseball should the varsity or
freshman season end before the intra
mural season is over.
9. A man who starts out on one team
shall continue on that team for the rest
of the season.
10. Seven men shall constitute a
team.
11. Each team shall furnish a good
standard ball for each game.
12. Teams must report at the sched
uled time.
13. Only students registered in the
University are eligible to play.
