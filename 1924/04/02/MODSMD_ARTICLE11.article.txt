# PRINCIPALS, CHORUS ARE ANNOUNCED FOR PAGEANT
## Daphne Hodgson and Velma Randall Are Leads in "Legend of Laurel" 
### 
Daphne Hodgson and Velma Randall
as Apollo and Daphnp respectively,
will carry the leading roles in "The
Legend of the Laurel," the women's
fiesta pageant to be presented May 10.
Other principal members of the cast
are as follows: Latonia, Grace Peter
son; Cupid, Marjorie Allen; Tmolus,
Anne McAnear; Artemis, Marcia Mor
ton; Pan, Caroian Strouse; Soothsay
er, Gertrude Bylef. About 150 women
will participate in the dances.
Rehearsals Begin at Once
Rehearsals for the fiesta will begin
at once. The dramatic cast, priestesses,
and peasants will report for work at
7 o'clock tonight in the Women's Club
house.
