# Two New Members Added to Anatomy Department Staff
## 
### 
Two additions have been made to
the teaching staff of the Anatomy de
partment. Both men will serve for this
quarter only.
Dr. Ernest Huber has been em
ployed as acting assistant professor.
He holds degrees from the Johns Hop
kins medical school and from the Uni
versity of Zurich. He has made spe
cial investigations of the muscles of
the face and of racial differences.
Dr. Herlwin R. Green of Palo Alto
has been appointed teaching assistant.
He was awarded M.D. degrees at the
University of Pennsylvania and at
Brown University.
