# F. E. Morgan Holds Class In Religion Next Sunday
## 
### 
F. E. Morgan, general secretary of
the Y. M. C. A., will hold the first meet
ing of his class in "Science and Re
ligion" this quarter in the east balcony
of the Memorial Church at 10 o'clock
Sunday morning.
He will summarize the arguments i

given so far this year on "Science and
the Belief in God." Other topics which
will be taken up in the spring quarter
are "Science and Human Personality,"
"Free Will," "The Bible," and "Immor
tality."
The class is open to all upper classmen.
