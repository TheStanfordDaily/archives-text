# Encina Golf Club Plans Tourney Monday Afternoon
## 
### 
With fifty entries assured, Encina
Golf club's tournament next Monday
afternoon on the Lincoln Park courts
in San Francisco seems certain of suc
cess.
After the contest the players will meet
for dinner at the Hotel Whitcomb. Wal
ter Rounevel, assistant manager of the
hotel and a golfer of note, will talk on
the line points of the game. Efforts are
being made to secure Harold Sampson,
Lincoln Park professional, as an addi
tional speaker. Sampson was formerly
professional at Del Monte, and he is
considered one of the foremost players
in California.
