# Mary Sweet to Handle Women's Registration
## 
### 
Miss Mary C. Sweet will have charge
of the supervision of changes of pro
gram for the University women during
the absence of Dean Mary Yost this
month.
Dean Yost left the campus Friday on
a trip to Tucson, Los Angeles, and
Denver. She will return to Stanford
the latter part of the month.
