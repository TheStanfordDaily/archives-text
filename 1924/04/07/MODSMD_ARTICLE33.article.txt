# Sigma Delta Chi Will Give Dance Friday Night
## 
### 
On Friday night, April 11, Sigma
Delta Chi will be sponsor to an All-
University dunce, to be given in the
Women's Clubhouse.
As it is a closed date, there will be
no other dances on the campus that
night. The regular admission price of
11.10 will be charged, and the Ala
baster-Mertz orchestra will supply the
music.
