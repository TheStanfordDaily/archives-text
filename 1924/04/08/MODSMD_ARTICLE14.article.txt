# CARDINAL THREE-MAN FENCING TEAM SEEKS PACIFIC COAST TITLE
## 
### 
Cardinal fencers will make a bid for
the Pacific Coast title tonight at the
Olympic Club at 8 o'clock when they
will enter a three-man team to com
pete against the duellers entered by
the Olympic Club and Unione Spor
tiva Italione. Some other teams may
enter the meet at the lust minute.
The Stanford outfit will be made up
of Captain Gardner, Bugge, and Smith
or Wallace. The varsity and the Olym
pic Club will be the principle con
tenders for the title.
