# Urner, Hunkins Elected To Glee Club Offices
## 
### 
Philip Urner was elected president
of the Glee Club at the election yes
terday afternoon. Charles Stratton
was elected vice-president, Wilfred
Hunkins, manager, Leonard Dobson,
assistant manager, and Kenneth
Thompson, librarian.
A double quartet from the Glee Club
will sing at the San Jose Stanford
Club banquet in the Vendome hotel
next Tuesday. A trip to Watsonville
and Carmel for the first week-end in
May has been tentatively arranged,
and the club is scheduled to broadcast
from Hale Bros, radio station KPO
later in the quarter.
