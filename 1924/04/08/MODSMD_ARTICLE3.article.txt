# Michaelis' Three Biology Lectures Are Postponed
## 
### 
His steamer from Japan having been
delayed, Professor Leonor Michaelis
of Bonn University will be unable to
give his lecture series on "Modern
Theories and Their Relation to Bio
logy" which had been scheduled for
this week in the Little Theatre.
The lectures have been postponed
Tintil his arival. Specific dates will be
announced later.
Professor Michaelis was supposed
to have spoken at 7:30 p.m., Wednes
day, at 4:15 p.m., Thursday, and at
7:30 p.m., Friday.
