# Dr. Sisson to Speak on Education and Society
## 
### 
"Education and the Great Society," is
the subject of a talk to be given by Dr.
E. O. Sisson, Professor of Education at
the University of California, tomorrow
evening at a meeting of the Education
Club in Room 50 at 7:45 p. m.
Dr. Sisson was formerly connected
with the Department of Education at the
University of Washington. He has held
the positions of president of Reed Col
lege, Portland, Oregon; Commissioner
of Education in Idaho, and president of
the University of Montana.
