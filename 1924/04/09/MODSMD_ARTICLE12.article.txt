# Dr. Mehrtens Will Talk on Psychology and Medicine
## 
### 
Dr. H. G. Mehrtens M. D., assistant
professor of medicine in the Stanford
Medical school will lecture on "The
Relation of Psychology to Medicine"
Friday at 7.30 p. m. in room 414.
Dr. Mehrtens is a specialist on
nuero-psychiatry, and his speech will
include a comparison of the two
sciences. The lecture is given under
the auspices of Tau Psi Epsilon,
honorary psychology fraternity, and
is open to the public.
