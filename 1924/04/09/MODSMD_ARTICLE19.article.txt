# DEAN YOST PRESENTS TOPIC AT CONFERENCE
## 
### 
"Promotion of Health in Student
Communities" was the topic presented
by Dean Mary Yost today at the West
ern Conference of Deans of Women,
in session at the University of Ari
zona, Tuscon, April 9 to 11.
Besides the business sessions, which
include discussions of the honor sys
tem, vocational guidance, the relation
of the dean to the campus, to the
students, and to the dormitories, en
tertainment has been planned for the
visitors.
A desert picnic is arranged for this
evening and a pageant, "The Magic
Fountain," is to be given by the mem
bers of the Department of Physical
Education tomorrow. A trip to San
Xavier mission is on the calendar for
Friday.
