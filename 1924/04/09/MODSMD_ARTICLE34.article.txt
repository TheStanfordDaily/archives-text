# Mrs. Josephine R. Rogers Broadcasts Lecture Today
## 
### 
Mrs. Josephine Rand Rogers, wife
of Professor P. A. Rogers of the Phy
sics department, broadcasted a lec
ture on "The Need of a Child Re
search Station" from the General
Electric company's radio station KGO

Child Research Station of Palo Alto,
and has been interested in this type
of work for several years.
