# Delta Sigma Rho Chapters Initiate in San Francisco
## 
### 
Seven Stanford students were initi
ated into Delta Sigma Rho at a joint
meeting with the California chapter
held Monday night at the Clift hotel,
San Francisco. Among the prominent
speakers was S. A. Stokes, national
president of the fraternity. J. G. Emer
son, debating coach at Stanford, was
toastmaster.
