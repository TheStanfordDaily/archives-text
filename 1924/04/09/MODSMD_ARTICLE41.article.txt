WEDNESDAY
ORATORIO TENORS AND BASSES
rehearse in Memorial Church at
7:30.
SCHUBERT CLUB meets at 7:15 in
Women's Clubhouse. Fiesta practice.
RADIO CLUB meets In the clubroom
at 7:30. All members are requested
to be present.
THURSDAY
GERMAN CLUB will hold important
meeting at 7:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse.
RALLY COMMITTEE will meet at
7:15 in Old Union.—AL HUNEKE.
ALL WOMEN see Daily article for
fiesta dancing rehearsals schedule.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet in the
New Union at 6:15 for dinner. Busi
ness meeting to follow at 7:15.
CHOIR rehearses in Memorial Church
at 7:30.
ORATORIO REHEARSAL in Memo
rial Church at 8:30.
JORDAN CLUB will meet at 7:30 p.m.
in the Botany Library, Room 461.
Mr. V. E. Hall will speak on "The
Effect of Sections of the Vagus and
Pelvic Nerves on Muscular Effi
ciency."
SIGMA DELTA CHI-ENGLISH CLUB
Publication Committee will meet in
Spectator office at 7 p.m.
WRANGLERS will meet at 7:30 in
Women's Clubhouse.
