# Lane Clinic Lecturer to Address Parent-Teachers
## 
### 
Miss Adelaide Brown. M D., member
of the State Board of Health, will
speak on "Keeping Children Well" at
the Community House in Palo Alto
tomorrow afternoon at 3 o'clock. The
lecture is given under the auspices of
the Palo Alto Parent-Teachers associa
tion.
Dr. Brown is the lecturer on childv.
hygiene from the Lane hospital in
San Francisco. Her speech will be
based on experiments carried out in
the Lane clinic.
