THE GENERAL CORD
The more we see of General Cord Service the prouder
we are of selling them.
Cord separation and loose treads, with their resultant
blowouts, are unknown in Generals—at least we have
our first ones to see and we have sold Generals for
nearly a year.
We have Diamond tires at a slightly lower price and
Diamond tires are good tires.
