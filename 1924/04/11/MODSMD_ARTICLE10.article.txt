# WILBUR ENGAGEMENT DISCLOSED AT DINNER
## 
### 
Announcement of the engagement of
Lois Wilbur to Max Hopper was made
at a dinner party yesterday at the home
of Miss Wilbur's parents, Dr. and Mrs.
Ray Lyman Wilbur.
Miss Wilbur is a member of the
sophomore class, while Hopper is a
junior, majoring in Chemistry. No date
has been set for the wedding.
