# Daily Palo Alto to Run Dope Sheets on Big Meet
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto intends to run
a series of dope sheets on the Big
Meet prior to April 26. Those who
desire may mail their ideas of the
probable outcome of the track contest
to the Daily office immediately. A com
posite dope sheet will also probably
be published.
