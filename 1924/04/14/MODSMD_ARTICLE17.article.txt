# CORSAGES BANNED AT U. S. C. INTER-FRATERNITY FORMAL
## 
### 
No corsages will be allowed at the
IT. S. C. Interfraternity Formal to be
given this Friday at the California
Country Club. The proceeds from the
dance will be used to purchase a schol
arship cup to be awarded the fratern
ity having the highest scholarship for
the past semester.
