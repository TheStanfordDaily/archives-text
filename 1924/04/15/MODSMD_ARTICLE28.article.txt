# O. A. C. WOMEN TO TAKE PART IN STYLE SHOW
## 
### 
Ten O. A. C. women will display ap
propriate costume# for school, street,
afternoon, summer, and evening wear,
In a style show given at the Multno
mah hotel, Portland, Oregon, March
20. All the dresses have been made by
students in the regular household arts
classes.
