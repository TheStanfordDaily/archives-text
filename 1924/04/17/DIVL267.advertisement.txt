NOTICE
Candidates for the degree of Bache
lor of Arts in June should file applica
tion at the Registrar's office without
delay. No applications will be received
after Tuesday, May 13. Application
blanks may be obtained at the Regis
trar's office.

registered for their second quarter of

complete their requirements by the
end of summer or fall quarters, should
file application at the Registrar's of
fice not later than Thursday, May 1.
