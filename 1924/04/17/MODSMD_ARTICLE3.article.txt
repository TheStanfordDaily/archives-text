# OREGON LEADS STATES IN COLLEGE STUDENTS
## 
### 
A survey of the data In the latest
number of the World Almanac shows
that Oregon leads all the other states
in the Union in regard to the number
of college students in the state on the
basis of population. In Oregon, there
are 112 persons for each college stu
dent, while the nearest competitor on
the list of states, lowa, has 128 per
sons in the state for each student en
rolled in college. The District of
Columbia has a higher record than any
of the states in this regard with one
college student for every 96 persons
in the state.
