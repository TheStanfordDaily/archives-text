# Vanity Fair Beauty Shoppe Started by Well Known Barbers
## 
### 
Ted Hausch and J. T. Fate, famous
tonsorial artists of the Block S Bar
ber Shop, have gone into the business
of beautifying the fair sex. The new
establishment will be known as the
Vanity Fair Beauty Shoppe, and is
located at 539 Bryant street. The for
mal opening was at noon today.
Mrs. Helen Jones is manager of the
shop which will make a specialty of
bobbing, besides purveying the usual
aids to pulchritude. All bobbing will
be done by appointment, and will be
under the direct charge of Mr. Tate,
who lias had extensive experience
along this line. Ted Hausch will re
main at his old post in the Block S
shop.
