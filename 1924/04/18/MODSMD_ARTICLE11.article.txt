# Spring Handball Tourney Entries Close Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day in which
to sign up for the spring handball
singles tournament which begins next
week.
In order to make the matches more
interesting for the less expert players.
Director C. W. Davis intends to run
the tourney at handicap.
Nineteen entries have been recorded
to date. Among them are Richards,
Rutter, Reticker, and Murray, who
played for the Cardinal against Cali
fornia last quarter. Other low handi
cap men are Kahn, Welch, and Mc-
Donald.
