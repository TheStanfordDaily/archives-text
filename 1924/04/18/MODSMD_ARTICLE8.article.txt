# Bobbed Hair Race Won By Alpha Omicron Pi; All Sisters Shingled
## 
### 
The classic race of the year is
over. Alpha Omicron Pi wins by a head
in the much-talked-of women's handi
cap.
After running second for two quar
ters the sisters launched into a fast
spurt at the beginning of the third lap
and last week went over the top of
the last girl's head with a pair of
shears.
Now the only one left to be shingled
is the house mother and the whole
house is concentrating in this direc
tion.
