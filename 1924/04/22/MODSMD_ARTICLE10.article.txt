# Mrs. Barrow to Be Hostess On Wednesday Afternoon
## 
### 
Mrs. Barrow, assisted by the wives
of the faculty of the Physical Educa
tion Department, will be hostess at
the regular Alumnae Tea to be held
in the Women's Clubhouse on Wednes
day afternoon, from four to six. Reser
vations for tea and bridge may be
made with Mrs. Ewell at the Club
house. Everyone is invited, and Quad
clothes will be worn.
