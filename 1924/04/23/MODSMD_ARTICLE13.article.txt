# Theta Sigma Phi Elects Meribeth Cameron President
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women's journal
ism society, elected Meribeth Cameron
president for next year. Elizabeth
Roper was chosen vice-president.
The following four new members
were initiated yesterday afternoon:
Beatrice Flesher, Helen Broughall,
Delia Taylor, and Esther Greenaere.
The society decided to take a con
cession at the Fiesta, and Elizabeth
Roper was appointed chairman of the
committee in charge.
