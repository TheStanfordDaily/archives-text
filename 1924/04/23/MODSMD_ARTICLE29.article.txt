# Stanford Dames to Meet At Clubhouse Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will meet at
the Women's Clubhouse Thursday af
ternoon at 3 o'clock. The program will
include a talk by Mrs. A. D. Elmer,
who has recently returned from
Hawaii, on "Hawaiian Customs and
Education," and piano solos by Mr.
Vetlesfen of Palo Alto.
