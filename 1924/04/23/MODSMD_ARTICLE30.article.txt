# Education Club Will Hear Of Phoenix School Survey
## 
### 
Dr. J. B. Sears of the School of
Education will be the speaker this
evening at a meeting of the Education
Club to be held in Room 50 at 7:45.
Dr. Sears will speak on "The Phoenix,
Arizona, School Survey." The meeting
will be opened to all who are inter
ested in the survey and its results.
