# One-Act Play Is Planned At Spanish Club Meeting
## 
### 
"Las Solteronas," a one-act play, 1
will be given by the Spanish Club
meeting in the Women's Clubhouse,
8 o'clock tomorrow evening.
The cast for the play is: Procopio—
Angel D. Aguerrever; Claudio —Aure-
lio Espinosa, Jr.; Sandalia —Elizabeth
Borton; Pura —Elizabeth Meyers;
Casta —Rhoda Lewis.
Everyone interested is invited to
attend.
