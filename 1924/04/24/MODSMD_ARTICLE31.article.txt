# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
IMPORTANT MEETING of the Jun
ior Class in Little Theatre at 12:50.
Junior Week announcements and
group picture for 1925 Quad.
SIGMA DELTA CHI eats at Alpha
Delt house at 6:15.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet in the
New Union at 6:15 for dinner. A
business meeting will follow at 7:15.
JSPANISH CLUB will meet at 8 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
JUNIOR OPERA REHEARSALS—
Ponies, 8:30; Beauties, 9; Men, 9:15.
At Women's Clubhouse.
WRANGLERS will hold their weekly
meeting at 7:15 in the Women's
Clubhouse. After the business meet
ing there will be a brief discussion
of new books.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA, chemistry honor
fraternity, will meet in the New
Union at 6:15 for dinner. Hay Gene
reaux, president, will preside at the
regular business meeting which fol
lows at 7:15.
TUESDAY
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE will meet
at 7:30.
PAGEANT CAST and priestesses re
hearse at 8 p.m. in Women's Club
house.
FIESTA COMMITTTEE meets at 7:15
in Women's Clubhouse.
