# Birdhouses Attract New Varieties on Campus
## 
### 
Who says he can catch birds by salting
their tails? The Zoology Club claims it
has discovered a more effective means
of attracting feathered flocks.
Last year the zoologists built eight
small bird houses, which are now ready
for occupation. These wooden nests
have lured several different varieties of
winged creatures to make their homes on
the campus this spring.
