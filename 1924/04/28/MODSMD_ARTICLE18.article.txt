# Charles Lowry Awarded Traveling Fellowship
## 
### 
Charles Lowry, Instructor in Organic
Chemistry, has been awarded the Shel
don Traveling Fellowship by Harvard
University. This offers the opportunity
to travel abroad and study in foreign
countries during the school year of
1924-1925.
The award has been accepted by
Lowry and, although he has 110 definite
plans, he expects to leave next fall and
will travel and study in France, England,
and Germany.
