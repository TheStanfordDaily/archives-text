# Rosemary Drachman Back On Campus for Week's Visit
## 
### 
Rosemary Drachman, '22, of Tuscon,
Arizona, is visiting at the home of Dean
Yost for a week. She will go to Denver,
Colorado, from here. Miss Drachman
was Miss Yost's secretary (luring the
year following her graduation. She is
a member of Cap and Gown, Masquers,
English Club, Roble Club, co-author of
the 1922 Junior Opera, and author of the
1922 women's pageant.
