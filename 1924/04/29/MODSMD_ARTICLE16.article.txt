# Mrs. H. C. Benedict to Be Hostess at Alumnae Tea
## 
### 
Mrs. H. C. Benedict will be the host
ess at the Resident Alumnae Tea to
morrow afternoon at 3 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. She will be as
sisted by Mrs. E. A. Cottrell, Mrs,
Joseph Taff. Mrs. John Buchanan, Mrs.
W. B. Allen, and Mrs. David Evans.
Reservations for card parties or for
tea should be made through Mrs.
Ewell by calling P. A. 177.
