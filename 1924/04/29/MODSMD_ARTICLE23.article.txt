# UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS VOTES ON MOST "GIRL SHY" MAN
## 
### 
The University of Texas is holding
a student body election to discover
who appears the most "girl shy" man
on this year's football team. The one
selected is to escort eleven girls to a
local theater where is showing Harold
Lloyd's picture, "Girl Shy."
