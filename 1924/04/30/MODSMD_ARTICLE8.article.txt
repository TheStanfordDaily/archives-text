# SPECIAL ORCHESTRA TO PLAY AT FIESTA
## Pageant Performers To Have First Musical Rehearsal Tomorrow 
### Esther Greenacre 
IWUSIC for the "Legend of the Laurel," the spring Fiesta to be staged at
4 o'clock next Saturday afternoon by the Stanford women, will be supplied
by a twenty-five piece orchestra. The entire orchestra will practice with the
pageant cast for the first time tomorrow afternoon at the ensemble dress
rehearsal to be held at Lagunita from 1 to 6 o'clock. All participants in the
Fiesta are asked to report tomorrow at 1 o'clock sharp.
