# History Professors Hosts To Graduates at Banquet
## 
### 
Graduate students of the History
department were guests of the faculty
of that branch of the University at a
banquet held in the Union last night.
The teaching staff stages a similar '
affair annually in order to promote i
friendly feeling between professors
and the holders of degrees.
H. H. Fisher and Professor F. O.
Colder spoke briefly on the material
in the Hoover War Library, discussing
particularly the recent acquisitions of
importance to students of history.
Thirty members of the department
were present.
