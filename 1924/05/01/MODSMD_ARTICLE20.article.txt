# Norris James Is Elected Head of Sigma Delta Chi
## 
### 
Norris James was elected president
of Sigma Delta Chi at a meeting held
in the Delta Chi house last night. The
other new officers are: Vice-president,
William L. Wright; secretary, Donald
McKay; treasurer, David C. Meikle-
John; "Quill" correspondent/ Ray
Spangler. Although this year's officers
have formally resigned, the now of
ficers will not assume complete con
trol until after the convention of high
school journalists which will he held
this week-end under the auspices of
Sigma Delta Chi.
