# CHARLES GAMBELL TO BE BASEBALL MANAGER
## 
### 
Charles Gambell received the ap
pointment of varsity baseball mana
ger for next year at the meeting of the
executive committee Tuesday night.
Gambell was given his appointment
because of his knowledge of the game
and because of his work during the
two year tryout period. He is a mem
ber of Theta Delta Chi.
The four men who were chosen as
junior baseball managers are Thomas
Quinn, Francis Wingard, Rupert
McCook, and Edward Kurtz.
