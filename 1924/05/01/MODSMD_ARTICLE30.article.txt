# CALIFORNIA RANKS FIRST IN NUMBER OF STUDENTS
## 
### 
The rankings of the universities of
the country in registration of full-time
students was recently published in
"School and Society." California was
listed first with 13,276 and Columbia
second with 11,530. Illinois was third,
having 9,353, and Michigan a close
fourth. Minnesota, Ohio State, Wis
consin, Pennsylvania, Harvard, New
York, and Nebraska follow in the order
named.
