# Cap and Gown Will Give First Benefit Dance of Quarter Saturday Night
## 
### 
Nymphs, satyrs, and sunbeams of
the afternoon will resume their mortal
characters Saturday night at a dance
sponsored by the Cap and Gown so
ciety in the Women's Clubhouse.
Syncopation will start at 9 o'clock
and music for the event will be fur
nished by the Mertz-Alabaster orches
tra. This will be the first Cap and

The usual admission price of $1.10
