# Corded Juniors to Sup And Dance With Queens In Old Union Thursday
## 
### 
Instead of being held in the Wo
men's Clubhouse this year the Cordu
roy Ball will be staged in the Men's
Union Thursday evening. Dancing be
gins at five o'clock sharp to the tune
of a five-piece orchestra and will con
tinue until eight o'clock. Supper will
be served duriug the intermissions.
Tickets for this party went on sale
this afternoon in the Administration
building. If there are any left over
they can be obtained from Robert
Triest at Sequoia by Junior men and
others who intend to take Junior wo
men. A charge of $1.75 will be made
for the dance and the four-course sup
per.
