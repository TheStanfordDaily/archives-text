# Coolidge Leads Johnson In Final Campus Returns
## 
### 
Pinal returns of the campus vote in
the presidential primaries yesterday
gave Coolidge a lead of 146 over John
son. Of the total vote cast, Coolidge
received 191, Johnson 45, and McAdoo
29. Eighteen ballots were cast "with
out preference."
Of the 282 votes cast, 237 were re
publican and only 45 democrat. Nei
ther the socialist or prohibition par
ties received a vote.
