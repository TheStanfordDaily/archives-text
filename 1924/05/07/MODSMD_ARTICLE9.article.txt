# Tickets to Corduroy Ball Are Open to All Classes
## 
### 
A few remaining tickets to the Cordu
roy Ball tomorrow evening arc now on
sale and can be obtained by seniors,
juniors and sophomores from Robert
Triest, John Driscoll, Tom Grace, Bill
Wright and James Muir. Not all the
100 tickets were sold yesterday after
noon at the sale for juniors only, so the
rest have been thrown open to all
classes with the exception of the fresh
men.
