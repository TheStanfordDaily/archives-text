# Helen Whitney Chosen New Cap and Gown President
## 
### 
Helen Whitney was elected presi
dent of Cap and Gown for the coming
year at the annual supper of the or
ganization, given by faculty members
at the home of Mrs. Bailey Willis last
night.
Miss Whitney will succeed Helen
Green. Other officers chosen were:
Violet Knowles, vice-president; Meri
beth Cameron, secretary; and Mar
garet Watson, treasurer.
