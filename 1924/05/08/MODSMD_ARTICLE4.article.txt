# Tangora, Typist Champion, Gives Demonstration of Speed in Assembly Hall
## 
### 
Albert Tangora, world's champion
typist, gave demonstration tests yes
terday afternoon in the Economics
building.
Tangora has an official record of
147 words a minute for an hour, or
11 1 strokes a second. He attributes
his speed and accuracy to his almost
perfect rhythm in writing and his
ability to concentrate.
Tangora comes from Patterson, New
Jersey, and received his training in
a private business school of that city.
He is now twenty years old and began
typing seven years ago.
