# Scientific Body to Meet At Stanford Next Month
## 
### 
The Pacific Division of the Ameri
can Association for Advancement of
Science will meet at Stanford June
24-28. Saturday, June 28, will be de
voted to excursions to the coast, La
Honda, Big Basin, and Lick Observa
tory.
Arrangements are being made by the
science departments for the entertain
ment of the wives of members as well
as for the members themselves.

Congress has proved that it is al
most impossible to keep her nose on
the grindstone while keeping the ear
near the ground.
