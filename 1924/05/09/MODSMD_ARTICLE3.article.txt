# Stanford Radio Club to Visit KGO This Saturday
## 
### 
The Stanford University Radio Club
will visit KGO, the General Electric
broadcasting station at Oakland, on
Saturday, May 10.
Those desiring to go should take the
7:4(5 a.m. train for San Francisco.
Then take the Key Route System to
Key Route Inn, and ask the conductor
which street car to board for KGO.
Everyone is invited.
