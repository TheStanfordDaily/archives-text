# PRESIDENT WILBUR TO SPEAK IN SOUTH
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
apeak tomorrow morning at the an
nual meeting of the Medical Society
of California in Los Angeles, and he
will also be the guest of honor and
chief speaker at a luncheon given by
the Southern California Regional com
mittee of the organization which is
carrying on a survey of race relations
on the Pacific Coast. Dr. Wilbur is
chairman of the organization for
which Dr. Robert E. Park is conduct
ing investigations, and his luncheon
talk is announced as a report of the
recent developments of this investiga
tion and an interpretation of their
meaning.
President Wilbur will return to the
campus tomorrow night.
