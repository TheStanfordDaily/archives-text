# PROFESSOR HULME TO SPEAK BEFORE EUPHRONIA TONIGHT
## 
### 
Professor E. M. Hulme, of the His
tory department, will address the Eu
phronia society at 7:30 on the subject
of "Some Old Euphronians and Oth
ers."
Hulme was a student on the Farm
at the time Wallace and Will Irwin,
Herbert Hoover, and Charles K. Field
were registered. Hulme was the editor
of the 1895 Quad.
After Professor Hulme graduated he
studied at Harvard and took his Mas
ter's degree from Cornell. The meet
ing will be held in the trophy room of
the New Union, and everyone inter
ested is invited to attend.
