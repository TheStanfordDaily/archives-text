# UNIVERSITY HOLIDAY IS CANCELLED; DATES GRANTED ON MAY 29
## 
### 
In spite of the fact that there is to
be no University holiday on May 30
the dates granted for May 29 will not
be taken away, according to the deci
sion of the Executive Committee last
night.
M. S. C. That the dates granted for
May 29 be allowed to stand, since the
cancellation of the holiday for May 30
took place since the granting of the
dates.
