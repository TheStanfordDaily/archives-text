# LAW CLASS TO TRY MOCK CASE TONIGHT
## 
### 
The California Practice class of the
Law school will hold a mock trial in
the Assembly hall tonight to decide
the case of Sebring vs. Harris on a
charge of arrest and false imprison
ment.
Edward Landels and Harry Linder
smith will be attorneys for the prose
cution. while Phil Smith and P. Eu
gene Glenn will defend the case.
The prosecution contends that
Emma Sebring, a boarding-house keep
er, represented by Helen Whitney,
has been arrested and falsely impris
oned on a charge of larceny and of
disturbing the peace, by S. Harris,
who is to be represented by Howard
Wilson.
