# Carl Wilhelmson Receives English Department Prize
## 
### 
The English department prize for
the best essay by an undergraduate
has been awarded to Mr. Carl Wil
helmson for an essay entitled "Work."
Honorable ipention is given to the
essay, "Sun Dogs," by Mr. David Lam
son. The judges in the contest were
Bruce Bliven, '11, Mrs. Frances War
ner Hersey, and Robert Paufrey Utter.
