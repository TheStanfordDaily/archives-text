# LANDELS, LINDERSMITH WIN MOCK TRIAL CASE
## 
### 
The prosecution, represented by Ed
ward Landels and Harry Lindersmith,
was awarded damages to the extent of
one hundred dollars in the mock trial
of Sebring vs. Harris held by the Cali
fornia Practice Class of the Law
School in the Assembly Hall night.
The attorneys for the defense were
Phil Smith and P. Eugene Glenn, who
were handling the case of S. Harris,
who was represented by Howard Wil
son. Harris was charged with having
falsely arrested Emma Sebring, who
was represented by Helen Whitney,
on a 'charge of larceny and disturbing
the peace. Mrs. Sebring sued for
damages, and recovered.
