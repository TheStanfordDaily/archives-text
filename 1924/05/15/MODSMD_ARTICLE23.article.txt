# NOTICE
## 
### 
Men now living in Toyon and Bran
ner halls who will not be graduates
on October 1 can reserve their rooms
for the fall quarter at the Dean of
Men's office on Monday and Tuesday,
May 19 and 20. Drawings will be held
in the offices of the respective halls
at 7 p.m. on May 21.

Students at the University of Indi
ana are celebrating the local clean-up
week. They have the co-operation of
the mayor of Bloomington in their ef
forts to have a clean campus.
