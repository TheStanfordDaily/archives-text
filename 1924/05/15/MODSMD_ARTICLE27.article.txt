# MAZARIN BIBLE BOUGHT FOR HUGE SUM BY NEW YORK MAN
## 
### 
A Bible owned by the famous Cardi
nal Mazarin of "Twenty Years After,"
sold recently for $43,225. It was print
ed in 1455, one of the earliest made
with movable type. It passed succes
sively to a foreign monastery, to Mr.
Perry, former proprietor of the Morn
ing Chronicle; the duke of Sussex, the
bishop of Cassel, the earl of Crawford,
and the earle of Carysfort. It was
bought by a New Yorker.

The Kent college of law in Chicago
has in the June graduating class a
woman who is 58 years of age. She
has just completed a four-year course.
