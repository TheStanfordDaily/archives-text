# NOMINATIONS ARE CLOSED WITH 43 NEW CANDIDATES
## Large Number of Nominees Vie for Positions at Election Tuesday 
### 
Nominations closed at noon today
with forty-three new names to be put
on the ballot next Tuesday. Ten men
are running for the Men's Council.
Only two have been nominated for
the Board of Athletic Control, and two
for the presidency of the student body.
