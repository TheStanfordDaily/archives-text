# Officers Elected at Annual Alumni Meeting in Japan
## 
### 
The Stanford Alumni of Tokyo and
Yokohama held their annual business
meeting and dinner on April 17 at the
Chutotei Restaurant in Tokyo. The
meeting was attended by twenty-thiee
alumni. J. F. Abbot, '99, was elected
president; Y. Fukukita, '04,' and T.
Kimura, '07, vice-presidents; T. Miwa,
'19, and T. Mitoma, '08, members of
the executive committee.
