# Y. W. C. A. Picnic Marks Re-Entrance of Women Into Campus Activities
## 
### 
Now that the Great Struggle of 1924
is over, the rush-worn women are an
nouncing their re-entrance into cam
pus activities by staging a Y. W. C. A.
picnic tomorrow night.
All women are invited to assemble
at 6 o'clock on the south side of the
lake where supper will be served for
25 cents. The prime purpose of the
picnic is to arouse enthusiasm for the
Student Conference to be held at
Asilomar June 18-28.
Appropriate entertainment is being
arranged by Virginia Hyde. Those in
charge of the affair are Ruth Snedden,
Margaret Dietrich, and Esther Green
acre.
