# OVERFELT LOSER IN BAY COUNTIES MEET
## 
### 
Harold Overfelt was eliminated from
the Bay Counties tennis championship
after reaching the round of eight.
After defeating Ray Greenberg in the
fourth round, 6-2, 6-2, he lost to Bowie
Detrick, 7-5, 6-3.
Overfelt and Deßack were also de
feated in the doubles in the same
round. Having defeated Gruber and
Burke 6-4, 6-3, they lost to Randall
and Jensen 2-6, 6-3, 7-5. Both matches
were hard fought, Overfelt having his
opponent 5-1 before the latter got
started, and Overfelt and Deßack win
ning their first set and forcing the
last set to deuce.
