# WALKING TO BE ADDED TO SPORT LIST AT U. OF W.

### 
Walking is to be added to the al
ready lengthy list of sports at Wis
consin. Walking matches will be ar

event put on the same basis as other
minor sports if it proves popular. Wis
consin is the first school to foster
walking in the middle west, although
it is quite common among eastern
colleges.
