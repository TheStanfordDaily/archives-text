# Dr. C. H. Gilbert to Make Study of Alaskan Salmon
## 
### 
Dr. C. H. Gilbert of the Zoology
department, is leaving Saturday for
Alaska where he will investigate the
salmon situation in the interests of
the United States Bureau of Fisheries.
He plans to make a scientific study of
the different species of salmon with
respect to their life habits and his
tories. to obtain information which is
essential for the conservation of the
salmon supply.
Robert A. Welsh. Jr., '27, will ac
company Dr. Gilbert and assist him
in his investigations. They will work
in the waters about Kodiak Island, in
| the streams of the southern coast of
the Alaskan Peninsula, and in the
Bering Sea.
