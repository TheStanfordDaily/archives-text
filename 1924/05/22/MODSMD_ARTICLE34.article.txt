# Fifty Women Gather For Y. W. Picnic Last Night
## 
### 
About fifty women attended the Y.
W. C. A. picnic on the lake bank last
night.
After a supper served around a bon
fire, talks were given by Isabel Capps,
Anna Fitzhugh and Ruth Snedden, on
the Student Conference to be held at
Asilomar June 18-28. A short stunt
written by Virginia Hyde was staged
by Ethelind Thompson, Barbara Pro
basco, and Esther Greenacre.
Patrice Nauman was in charge of
the food. Pamphlets advertising the
picnic were designed by Charlotte
Lovekin.
