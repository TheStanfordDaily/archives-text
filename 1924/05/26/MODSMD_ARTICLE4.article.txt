# Delta Sigma Rho Dance Tryouts Continued to Next Saturday Evening
## 
### 
On account of the large number of
enti jus, t.ie dances sponsored by Delta
Sigma Rho will be run oil in two
heats.
The first heat, which took place last
Saturday night, resulted in some good
times. Entrants for the second heat,
scheduled for Saturday night, May 31,
are unlimited.
Experience and training are unne
cessary for those who participate in
the second heat. Qualifications will be
entirely mercenary.
Those who have qualified in the
first two heats will be sent east to
try out for the Follies. Later some of
these entrants will enter the Olympic
Dance tryouts.
