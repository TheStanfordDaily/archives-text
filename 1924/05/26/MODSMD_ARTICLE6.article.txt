# PRESS OFFICIAL TO ADDRESS STANFORD JOURNALIST CLUB
## 
### 
Morris De H. Tracy, manager of the
Pacific Division of the United Press,
will speak to the Stanford Journalists
Club next Wednesday evening. As
usual, the meeting is open to all who
are interested in the speaker or his
talk on aspects of newspaper work,
especially that of the United Press.
At a business meeting of the Stan
ford Chapter of the American Journal
ists Association, the organization
changed its name to the Stanford
Journalists Club.
The place of the meeting Wednes
day evening will be announced later.
