# MEMORIAL SERVICES TO BE HELD FRIDAY
## 
### 
In commemoration of Memorial Day,
a short service has been arranged by
Dr. David C. Gardner, which will take
place In the Memorial Church at 11:45
Friday morning.
Classes will be dismissed at 11:35
so that the students may attend the
twenty-minute exercises. They will be
resumed in the afternoon.
A hymn, the national anthem, and a
soloist, supported by the choir, will
be included in the program.
