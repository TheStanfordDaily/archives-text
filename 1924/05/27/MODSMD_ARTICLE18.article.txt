# SURPLUS OF LABOR DAY MONEY TO GO TO STANFORD HOME
## 
### 
The Stanford Convalescent Home is
to receive the surplus from the money
which was collected from the men's
dormitories for Labor Day, according
to the action taken at the meeting of
the Executive Committee last night.
This is to help in the drive which is
being held for the benefit of the Home.
M. S. C. That the surplus from the
money for Labor Day be turned over
to the Convalescent Home.
M. S. C. That the election results
be approved.
M. S. C. That Marian Gower be
the member at large on the Quad
Board.
