# Tickets on Sale for Mendelssohn's "Elijah"
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale for Men
delssohn's "Elijah," the oratorio which
is to be presented Sunday, June 8, in
the stadium.
General admission is $1, and re
served seats in the center sections
are $1.50. Tickets may be obtained at
the Administration building, at Cran
dall's, and at the Music Shop in Palo
Alto, at Sherman Clay & Co., San
Francisco and San Jose, and at Roos
Bros, stores in San Francisco.
