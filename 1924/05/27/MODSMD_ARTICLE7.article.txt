# Daily Needs $25 Donation To Obtain Track Results
## 
### 
THE DAILY PALO ALTO is asking
for donations to the extent of $25 to
help defray the expenses of carrying
on a telegram communication with the
I. C. A. A. A. A. meet at Boston next
Friday and Saturday and the P. C. C.
meet to be held at Eugene, Washing
ton, next Saturday. A donation box
will be placed in the UNION STORE
TONIGHT.
The Student Body will be invited
to the Daily office Friday and Satur
day afternoons to get results from the
two meets in which Stanford has en
tered her first and second track teams.
Results will be received here at about
the middle of the afternoon, the exact
time to be announced tomorrow.
