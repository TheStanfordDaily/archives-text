# Cardinal Loyalty
## 
### 
Seven days ago the best track team in the country left the campus for
the J. C. A. A. A. A. meet in the East. Yesterday another crack Stanford
squad boarded the train for the P. C. C. contest in Oregon.
1 omorrow without the support of a howling cardinal rooting section
fifteen Redshirts will take their places on a strange eastern field, and begin
their efforts to make the cardinal colors wave in a triumphant victory.
But three thousand miles to the West there will be a rooting section
though it will not be in one place, yelling itself hoarse obeying the com
mands of a yell leader. Every student on the Farm will be anxioush
waiting to hear the results of the meet and doing enough mental rooting
to drown out the roar of a regiment of canons.
Even in case of some unforeseen event —the appearance of Hercules or
Mercury at the meet—which might cause the Redshirts to be defeated.
Stanford will be just as proud of her athletes, who have done their best.
