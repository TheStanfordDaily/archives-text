# CHAPPIE MAKES FINAL SPLURGE ON MONDAY
## 
### 
Chappie busts out Monday with the
final splurge of the year. Featuring
a three-color halftone cover by none
other than George Thompson, and ab
breviated stories by Chappie's new in
tercollegiate champion rib vibrator,
Carl Shoup, Chappie spreads himself
this time with a display of art work
that has caused the business manager
little or no pleasure.
"Gutter Stories ror the Little Ones,"
"The Eskimo as a Field for Commer
cial Exploitation," "The Summer Ad
vertising Season Gets Under Way,"
"My Interview with the Fizz-Remover
King," are titles over the initials of
Shoup, Newill, and Ely. Bill Wright
offers revised dope on Horatius in a
syllabus sheet that would make H. G.
Wells blush.
