# CLEAN POLITICS
## 
### 
Senior to group of frosh—Now get me straight, men. Stanford stands for
lean politics. It's the right man we're after. Dirty work's taboo. Election
eering s all right tor the one-horse college but we can that stuff here. Get me?
Now the whole bunch of you will march over to the polls with me and
ote for X lor president. As you all agree, I'm sure, he's the only man for
he job. As to other offices, I'll check 'em off for you. I'm glad to help you
'Ut and give you a good, clean start on the road of Stanford ideals.
