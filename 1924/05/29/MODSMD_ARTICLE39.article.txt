THURSDAY
UNIVERSITY PHILOLOGICAL AS
SOCIATION meets at 4 p.m. in Wo
men's Clubhouse.
SATURDAY
ZOOLOGY CLUB will meet at 7:00
p.m. at Stephens Creek in the camp
of Mr. and Mrs. Norman Scofield.
WEDNESDAY
RESEARCH CLUB meets at 8 p.m. in
Faculty Clubhouse. Papers to be
given are: "Shakespeare's Share in
Heywood's Pericles," by Henry D.
Gray, and "Gravity as a Formative
Influence in Plants," by George J.
Peirce.
