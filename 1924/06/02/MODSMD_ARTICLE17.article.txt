# Daily Receives Track Results Over U. P. Wire
## 
### 
Results of the I. C. A. A. A. A. and
the P. C. C. meet were received by the
Daily Palo Alto Friday and Saturday
through the courtesy of the United
Press in San Francisco.
The Daily was enabled to bring the
returns to the campus largely through
the cooperation of the following or
ganizations: Alpha Delta Phi, Alpha
Kappa Lambda, Alpha Sigma Phi,
Beta Theta Pi, Breakers Club. El Toro
Club, Kappa Kappa Gamma, and Phi
Sigma Kappa.
The Daily received a total of $13.10,
which includes the amount dropped
in the box in the Union Store, con
tributed by unknown Individuals and
groups.
