# BRANDSTEN LEAVES FOR INDIANAPOLIS
## 
### 
Ernst Brandsten, Stanford, swim
ming coach, left for Indianapolis Sat
urday to take up his duties as diving
mentor for the American Olympic
team.
Brandsten was selected because of
his national reputation aB a diving
coach. He will go to Paris shortly
after the conclusion of the tryouts.
Final tryouts for the American team
will be held at Board Ripple pool in
Indianapolis June 5 to 7, when more
than two hundred survivors of the
sectional tryouts will compete. The
Stanford entrants, Wally O'Connor, A1
White, and Clarita Hunsberger, left
for the East last week.
