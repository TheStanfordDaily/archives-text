# Philological Association Holds Meeting Thursday
## 
### 
The Stanford Philological Associa
tion will hold a meeting downstairs in
the Women's Clubhouse Thursday
afternoon at 4 o'clock. This is the last
meeting of the association this quarter.
Two papers will be read: one by Pro
fessor S. A. Smith of the Romanic
Languages department on Giacosa's
"Diritti dell Anima" and Ibsen's "The
Doll's House." He will draw attention
to certain parallels between the two
plays. The other paper will be by Pro
fessor H. D. Gray on "The Occasion
of Joel's Prophecy."
