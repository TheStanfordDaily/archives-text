# Dr. Carruth Given Dinner By English Department
## 
### 
Dr. W. H. Carruth, head of the Eng
lish department, who is retiring at the
end of this quarter, was given a sur
prise dinner at his home 011 Roble
Ridge by the members of the English
department.
The affair was in the form of a
picnic supper informal enter
tainment. Congratulatory and remini
scent poems composed for the occa
sion, together with a ballad written by
Dr. Margery Bailey, were read. Pro
fessor Samuel S. Seward was master
of ceremonies.
Dr. Carruth has been with the Uni
versity for a number of years. He has
taught verse writing as well as com
parative literature, his specialty.
