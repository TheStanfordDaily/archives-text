# Introduction to Economics
## 
### 
Harvey L. Lutz—//. F. Stanton
This book was written, according to
the authors' preface, for the purpose
of creating a greater appreciation of
the duties and privileges of citizen
ship.
It is composed of five parts: the first
part sets forth the principles under
lying the present economic system and
explains their development; the sec
ond, third, and fourth parts deal with
the production, distribution, and con
sumption of wealth, and the regula
tions of international trade; in the
fifth part, the problems of economic
and social organization are treated in
their relations to the development of
the science of economics.
