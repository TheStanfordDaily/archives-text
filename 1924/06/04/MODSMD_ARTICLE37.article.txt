# JAPAN CHOSES PENN STATE ATHLETE TO ATTEND OLYMPICS
## 
### 
Japan has, appointed Dasutoshi
Naito, Penn State wrestling captain and
intercollegiate champion, to represent
his native land in the Olympic games
this summer. Naito will be the only
Japanese to take part in wrestling,
and he has shown unusual ability as a
college grapple on Penn State's cham
pionship team.
