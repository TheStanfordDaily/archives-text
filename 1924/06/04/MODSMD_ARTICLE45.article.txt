# 10 DAYS TO GO
## 
### 
Only 10 days before finals start.
| Better get in shape; drink plenty of
those delicious and healthful choc
malts at the University Creamery, next
to the Stanford Theatre. —Adv.
A birthday cake, the frosting of
which, alone required sixty-five pounds
of sugar, was served at the celebra
tion of the seventy-fifth anniversary
of the University of Wisconsin.
