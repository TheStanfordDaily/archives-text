# H. L. White Elected Head Of Civil Engineers Club
## 
### 
Harold White was elected president
of the Stanford University chapter of
American Society of Civil Engineers
at a meeting held at the Alpha Tau
Omega house Tuesday evening.
Fred Ingram was elected vice-presi
dent; Harry Williams, secretary; Ted
Hazeltine, treasurer; and Allen White,
corresponding secretary.
A summary of the year's work was
given by Elmer Nelson, retiring presi
dent, and in this was included the
publishing of an alumni directory.
This contains the name, address, and
occupation of every civil engineer
graduate of Stanford.
