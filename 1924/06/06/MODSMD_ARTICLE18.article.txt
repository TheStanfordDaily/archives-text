# A 						Confession
##

Be prepared for a great shock. Loath as we are to break the news,
we feel constrained to tell you that this is the annual FRESHMAN
EDITION of the Daily Palo Alto. Now you can settle back and light
up, the suspense is over. Incidentally, the Freshmen are greatly indebted
to many of the older members of the staff for their help in getting this
paper out. With but six members of 1927 to call upon, it would have
been an impossible task without the help of the older heads.
