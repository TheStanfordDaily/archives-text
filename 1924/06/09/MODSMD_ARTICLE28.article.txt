# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
QUAD RAZZ BOARD meets at 7:15
in Room 201.
EDUCATIONAL PICTURES will be
shown tonight at 7:30 to members
of Civil Engineering Society in the
Little Theatre.
REHEARSAL of entire cast for 1924
Senior Farce at 7 o'clock in the As
sembly hall.
TUESDAY
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY will
hold its final meeting at 7:30 up
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse.
Members of the Mother Church are
especially urged to attend.
COMMUTERS CLUB will meet at the
Women's Clubhouse. Election of of
ficers. Very important.
WEDNESDAY
SCIENCE ASSOCIATION will meet at
Faculty Clubhouse Wednesday, at
8 p.m. Address by Dr. Schultz on
"The Richettsia, a New Group of
Micro-Organisms."
