# Summer Daily
## 
### 
The Daily Palo Alto will be conducted as a weekly during the summer
quarter. Any students who will be in the University will be welcomed on
the staff. Those interested should communicate with the editor immedi
ately.
For the bnefit of students who wish to have the paper sent to them
during the summer, a special mailing service will be conducted. Names
should be left with the Business Manager immediately.
