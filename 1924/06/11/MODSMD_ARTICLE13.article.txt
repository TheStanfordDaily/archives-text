# H. McDONALD WINS HANDBALL TOURNEY
## 
### 
H. McDonald won the spring singles
handicap handball tournament when
he defeated Francis Murray in the
final match. The tournament has been
going on all quarter.
McDonald had a handicap of three,
and Murray, who is a varsity player,
was scratch. Murray took the first
game 21 to 18, but McDonald came
through and won the next two games,
21 to 10, and 21 to 4.
