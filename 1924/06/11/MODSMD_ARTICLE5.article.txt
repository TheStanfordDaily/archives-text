# ENVELOPE SALE FOR GRADES ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The Registrar's office announces
that envelopes for report cards are
now on sale. The proceeds will go
for the benefit of the Stanford Conva
lescent Home, as is customary.
Report cards for students receiving
degrees will be ready for distribution
at 10:30 a. m. Friday, June 20. Report
cards for other students will be mailed
as soon as possible after the close of
the quarter. No cards will be given
out at the Registrar's desk except to
candidates for degrees.
