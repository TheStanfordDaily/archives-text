# O. A. C. HAS ANNUAL WOMEN'S ACTIVITY DAY
## 
### 
A day devoted entirely to the ac
tivities of women was inaugurated as
an annual event at the Oregon Agri
cultural College recently. Mothers and
guests from all parts of the state at
tended the all-day program, planned
for their entertainment. Breakfasts
for the visitors in the halls and
houses, a women's track meet, and a
banquet in the college tea room were
features on the program.
