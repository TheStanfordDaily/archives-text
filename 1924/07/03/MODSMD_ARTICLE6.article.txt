# President Wilbur Leaves For Stay at Summer Home
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur left the campus last Saturday for
their summer home on the north fork
of the American river. They were
accompanied by their two younger
sons, Dwight and Ray, Jr, Dr. Wilbur
and his sons plan to enjoy a vacation
by taking various trips from their
camp into the mountains.
