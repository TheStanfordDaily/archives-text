# PRESIDENT, FACULTY, INVITED TO VISIT DEL MONTE CAMP
## 
### 
President Wilbur and the entire fac
ulty of the University have been in
vited to visit the Citizens' Military
Training Camp at Del Monte, July 19.
The invitation is as follows:
"The Commandant and Corps of In
structors of the Citizens' Military
Training Camp, Del Monte, California,
cordially invite you to be present at
a Military Tournament to be held in
camp on July 19, 1924, Visitors' Day.
"The program will include events
which demonstrate fully the character
of the instruction given to students.
Progress in physical development,
alertness and quality of leadership,
will be shown by exercises and dem
onstrations.
"Special provision has been bade
for entertainment of guests on Vis
itors' Day.
"Inspection of the camp and of the
work by those who are interested in
the building of sturdy American man
hood will be welcomed at any time;
but on Visitors' Day the routine of in
struction will be subordinated to the
entertainment of relatives and friends
of the students."
