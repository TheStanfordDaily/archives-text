# Toyon Club to Give All University Dance July 25
## 
### 
Toyon Club will be the host at an |
all-university dance Friday, July 25, J
in Toyon hall. Tickets, which will
sell at $2, are on sale at the Book- !
store. The dance will be a one o'clock
and Trago's orchestra will furnish the :
music.
