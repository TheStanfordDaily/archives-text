# Dean Yost Leaves Campus For Outing at Inverness
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost, accompanied by
her sister, Miss Buckingham, and Mrs.
Russell, left Tuesday for a week's va
cation at Iverness, During Miss
Yost's absence, the office of the Dean
of Women will remain closed. Dean
Yost expects to return to the campus
the first of next week.

"Founders' Day" is an annual event
at VV. S. C.
