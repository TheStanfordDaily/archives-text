# STANLEY MORRISON ADDED TO FACULTY OF LAW DEPARTMENT
## 
### 
Stanley Morrison, a graduate of
Yale University, has been appointed
to the faculty of the Stanford Law
School.
Morrison received his A.B. degree
from Yale in 1915 and his LL.B. from
Harvard in 1919. After finishing his
law course, he was secretary to As
sociate Justice Oliver Wendel Holmes,
of the United States Supreme Court
for a year.
Since 1920, he has been associated
with the law firm of McCutcheon. Ol
ney, Mannon, and Greene, of San Fran
cisco. Morrison will give courses in
Municipal Corporations, Taxation, and
Admiralty. His appointment as a lec
turer increases the staff of resident
instructors in the Law School, to nine.
