# SIR BERNARD PARES TO TALK ON RUSSIA
## 
### 
Sir Bernard Pares, Director of the
School of Slavonic Studies at the Uni
versity of London, will speak on "The
Inside Diplomacy of the War and
Revolution in Russia" in the Little
Theatre next Tuesday evening at 7:30.
Sir Bernard Pares is professor or
Russian Language, Literature and
History at Liverpool and King's Col
lege, London. He is editor of the
Russian Review and of the Slavonic
Review. He is lecturing at the sum
mer session of the University of Cali
fornia.
