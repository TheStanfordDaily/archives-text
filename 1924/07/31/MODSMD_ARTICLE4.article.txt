# PRESIDENT WILBUR TO BE SPEAKER AT STUDENT ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
address a University assembly at 10
o'clock Wednesday morning, August 6.
His subject is announced as "Stanford
University," and it is probable that
his message will be directed in par
ticular to students of the summer ses
sion. President Wilbur returned to the
campus Tuesday from a month's vaca
tion at his summer home on the north
fork of the American river.
