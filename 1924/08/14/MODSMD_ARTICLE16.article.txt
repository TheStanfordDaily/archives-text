# PRINCETON FILMS ATHLETIC EVENTS, STUDENT ACTIVITIES
##

the vogue at Princeton. Interclass and
varsity athletics have been filmed and
film was taken of university activities.

record of student life. Films were
taken of all the leading athletic events.
In addition, reels were taken of stu
dent body officers, editors and mana
gers of student publications, and dra
matists.
