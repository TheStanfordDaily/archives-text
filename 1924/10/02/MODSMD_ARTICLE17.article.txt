# WILBUR TO GREET ASSEMBLY MONDAY
## 
### 
Monday morning at 9 o'clock the
first assembly of the year will be held
in the Assembly Hall. An assembly of
this nature is held at this time every
year, and is in the form of a formal
welcoming of the new freshman class,
and other new students, to the Uni
versity. While the principal address,
delivered by Dr. Wilbur, president of
the University, is directed to the new
students, the entire student body is
urged and expected to attend.
