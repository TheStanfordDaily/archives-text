# Captain E. C. Boyle Now Stationed at Monterey
## 
### 
Captain E. C. Boyle, who has been
connected with the Stanford unit of
the R. O. T. C.for the last four years,
has been transferred recently to the
7Gth Field Artillery at Monterey.
While at Stanford Captain Boyle was
instrumental in turning out several
Polo teams of championship caliber.
