# NOTICE
## 
### 
Encina and Branner dining halls are
open to any men students who wish
to obtain board on the co-operative
plan. October board is $31.00, payable
in advance at the Custodian's office
in the respective halls.

Pamphlets on Vocational Guidance
at Stanford may be obtained at the
Cashier's Office in the Administration
Building at a cost of 25c each.
