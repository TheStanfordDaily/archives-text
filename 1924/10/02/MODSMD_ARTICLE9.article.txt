# D. P. A. Management Will Begin Tryouts Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tryouts for the business stall' of
1 the Daily Palo Alto will commence
tomorrow and will last through the
following Friday. An opportunity is
offered for n limited number of under
classmen. especially freshmen, to be
appointed to this staff. The work is
entirely of a business nature, and good
training in business methods is to be
had. All who wfsh to try out should
see the business manager of the Daily
between 1 and 2 o'clock tomorrow at
- the Press building
