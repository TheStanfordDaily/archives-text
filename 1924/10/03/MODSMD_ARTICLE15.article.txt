# Chaparral Announces Tryouts for Manager
## 
### 
The Chaparral announces to the
classes of 1927 and 1928 that tryouts
for the business staff will begin on
Monday. Tryoutees should see the
business manager of Chaparral on the
second floor of the press building to
morrow between 10 and 12, or Monday
ot 1 o'clock.
