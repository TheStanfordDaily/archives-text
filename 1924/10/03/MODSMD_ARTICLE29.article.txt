# Large Turnout Expected For 1924 Soccer Season
## 
### 
More than 100 men are expected to
turn out for the 1924 'soccer team, ac
cording to early indications yesterday
in the Encina Gym registration. Ralph
Howe, Stanford captain, and veteran
of three years, will assume the reins
of the freshman, varsity, and reserve
squads until the return of Harry Ma
loney who is not expected until Octo
ber 15. Howe was last year adjudged
the best halfback in the West. Among
the better-known veterans who have
signified their intention of practicing
this season are: Hoover, Franklin,
Tucker, and Miron.
