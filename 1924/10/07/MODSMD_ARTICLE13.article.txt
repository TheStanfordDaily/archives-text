# HANDBALL MEN WILL STAGE TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Campus handball enthusiasts are I
signing up now for the University i
singles tournament which will start j
next week.
It is expected that the four new i
handball courts constructed during I
the summer will stimulate the already
active interest in the game and result j
in a record Held of entrants.
Whiting Welch romped away with
first honors last year and will be out
to duplicate the feat. Since freshmen
as well as upper-classmen are eligible
for the varsity in this sport., they will
probably turn out in large numbers |
for the tourney in an effort to earn a
berth on the squad. Sign-up sheets will i
be posted in the Enema gym until ,
October 13.
