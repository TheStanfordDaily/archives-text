# Stars Will Rub Elbows With Audience in Cast Dance for "Dover Road"
## 
### 
The whole cast will be there. This,
when translated, means that at the
cast dance to be given in the Wo
men's Clubhouse following the per
formance of "The Dover Road" next
Friday night, the campus will be able
to see at close range, and even rub
shoulders with, the members of the
I cast. Admission to the dance will be
only 75c a couple, and will last until
j one o'clock.
Gordon Davis, the star and director,
: has promised to dance with every lady
who will wear a red geranium pinned
on her" left shoulder. Three carloads
of this flower are on the way here,
Tyroler's orchestra will officiate.
