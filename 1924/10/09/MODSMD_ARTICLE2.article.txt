# THIRTY-SEVEN MEN SELECTED IN FIRST GLEE CLUB TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Thirty-seven men have successfully
passed the Glee Club tryouts and will
report for their first meeting, at which
old members will also be present, next
Monday in Room 84.
The new men requested to report
are: first tenors, George Eardley, A. S.
Grady, J. W. Dallavo; second tenors,
L. H. Mathers, J. K. Moore, A. G.
Winterbotham, T. H. Acres, R. Hef
ner, Jr., L. O. Hasom, D. H. Alden,
E. J. Wilson, Clyde Smith, Jr., B.
