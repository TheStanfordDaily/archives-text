it, Cabbages and Things will be served
»/» in the Daily Palo .11 to on Tuesdays
and Thursdays (7*77/ 12:05 H. Aclte\
121 I. Grove Pay, Date Meiklejohn,
Jack Hardy, and Miss Esther Cireenacre j
will mix the olive oil and vinegar in the
order named. Other contributors "who be- \
lieve they have something to add to the
mixture will be given the salad fork 1
from time to time. Miss Green acre:

Sophomores' reign of terror falls j
flat. Next day frosh declared champion |
mud-slingers. Retaliation planned. Cab
bages suggests that sophs move into
Encina courts and make as much noise
as. they did last year.
* * *
Frosh found lampblack in sophs'
mud-hole. One soph found lamp black
after light. Had picture taken with
dark lantern. Evidence to be presented
to Ku Klux Klan.
# * *
Mud bath declared aid to beauty. I
'Sright. Know a guy who had a face
like a horse. After fight horse would
have lost face to be seen with him.
* * *
Mud diet said to be a necessity.
Sophs ought to be full of vitamines. |
Tough on frosh. Would recommend
following a diet at Encina dining
halls; mud pie, pie a lu mud, mud j
pudding, puddle mudding, fried mud
jacks. Maybe some of this isn't new.
• * *
Three women walk up law steps.
Law majors surprised. Discover art of
blushing not. lost. One woman, inter
viewed afterward, denied it. Said it, I
was epidermic spasm. No doubt.
