# Theta Sigma Phi to Give Dance Saturday In Women's Clubhouse
## 
### 
According to Theta Sigma Phi, an
nouncement has been made that the
postoffice, library, and administrative
oft ices will close their doors from 9
to 12 o'clock Saturday evening so that
queeners may gather at the Women's
Clubhouse.
Tyroler's orchestra will furnish the
music, and the University has con
sented to provide lights in the Club
house in spite of the fact that the
moon will be full. Further information
than that the admission will be one
dollar and ten cents a couple, provid
ing that it isn't a "couple of roughs,"
cannot be divulged until tmorrow
night. At that time notice concerning
the entrance of aforementioned roughs
will be given out.
