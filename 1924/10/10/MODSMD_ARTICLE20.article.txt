# Golden-Paved Detours to Be Sold for "Dover Road"
## 
### 
A mass of golden-paved detours to
"The Dover Road" will be opened to
the roughs at fifty cents per passage
tonight at 7 o'clock in the Assembly
hall box-office. These choice bits will
be on sale until 7:45 only, at which
time they will resume their disguise as
through boulevards to the best comedy
ever revealed on the local campus.
Line up. boys, 7 p.m.
