# PHYSICAL EDUCATION CLASSES SCHEDULED TO MEET NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
All men enrolled in Physical Educa
tion classes are requested to report
for the first time Monday, October 13.
according to an announcement issued
today by Dr. Barrow, head of the Phys
ical Education department.
The roll will be called then for the
first time and any cut or failure to
report without the proper suit will be
counted as an unexcused absence and
will have to be made up.
Six quarters of physical education
is a required subject for all lower
division students and without these
units one cannot graduate. Any course
in the department may be taken by
upper division men and one hour
credit will be given.
