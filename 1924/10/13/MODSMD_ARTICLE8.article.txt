# Sword and Sandals Will Hold Try-Outs for Play
## 
### 
Sword and Sandals, men's dramatic
society, will hold try-outs for its an
nual play tomorrow night and Wednes
day night from* 7 to 9 o'clock. There
will also be an extra try-out period
Wednesday afternoon from 2:30 to 4
o'clock.
"R. IT. R.," the sensational melo
dramatic success of the Theatre Guild,
will be this year's Sword and Sandals
play. Although the society is a man's
organization, women are needed in the
action of the play. Everyone is eligible
except freshman women.
