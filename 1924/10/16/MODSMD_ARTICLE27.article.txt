# Edgar E. Robinson Speaks To Francis Drake Society
## 
### 
Professor Edgar E. Robinson of the
History department spoke on "Drake
and Empire" at a recent meeting of
the Sir Francis Drake Association. The
meeting was held at the San Rafael
Golf and Country Club.
The Sir Francis Drake Association,
which was started in 1914, was formed
with the purpose of stimulating inter
est in the landing of Sir Francis Drake
on the Coast of California, June 17,
1579, and in the consequences of that
event.
