# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
VARSITY AND FRESHMAN candi
dates for swimming and water polo
meet at Enclna pool at 5 p.m.
RALLY COMMITTEE members will
meet at the Sigma Chi house at 5:45
p.m. Following supper, there will be
a dance at the Kappa house until
8 o'clock. Members are requested
to secure partners for the dance.
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON meets in the
Old Union at 7:15.
HISTORY CLUB meets at 8 o'clock in
Women's Clubhouse. Dr. C. F.
Brand, new faculty member, will
speak.
SOPHOMORES will meet in Room 214
at 12:40.
FRIDAY
THE BONFIRE meeting of the Stu
dent Volunteers and Fellowship will
be held at the farther end of Lake
Lagunita Friday evening at 7:30.
