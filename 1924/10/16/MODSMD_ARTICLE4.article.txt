# CHAPPIE HEAD TO SET DEADLINE FOR TOMORROW AT NOON
## 
### 
Aspiring literati of the campus are
officially notified that all literary con
tributions for the "Vocal Number" of
Chaparral must be in the hands of the
editor by tomorrow noon. Copy may
be mailed to The Chaparral, Box 15,
Campus, or shoved under the door of
the Old Boy's office on the second floor
of the Press building.
Although this is labeled the Vocal
Number, material need not be restrict
ed to this theme. The only requisites
are that, copy be funny, original, and
not overly Rabelaisian. Also, the An
cient One requests that contributors
respect his aversion for jokes concern
ing co-eds and prohibition.
