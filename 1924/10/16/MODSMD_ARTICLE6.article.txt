# Talk, "Man," Delivered to Freshmen by Dr. Seerley
## 
### 
Dr. F. N. Seerley, a national social
hygiene lecturer, who is touring the
country under the auspices of the
American Social Hygiene Association,
gave one of his addresses, "Man," to
an audience of more than 400 fresh
men at Encina last evening. The
speaker was introduced by Dr. Mor
gan. secretary of the Stanford Y. M.
C. A.
"It is your job to make that brain
of yours capable of doing what you
want it to do; no one else can do that
for you." were his words of caution to
the first-year men.
