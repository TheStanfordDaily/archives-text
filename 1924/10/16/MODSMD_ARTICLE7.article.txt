# Women's Equitation Class Discontinued for Quarter
## 
### 
Announcement has come from Major
William D, Geary, head of the Mili
tary department, that there will be no
equitation class for upperclass women
this quarter.
According to the department, it has
been impossible to arrange the sche
dule for the class, as in the past, but
effort will be made to resume it winter
quarter.
