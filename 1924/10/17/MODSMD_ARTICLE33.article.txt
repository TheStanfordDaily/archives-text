# F. W. Peek, '05, to Address Engineers on Lightning
## 
### 
F. W. Peek, Jr., '05, consulting en
gineer of the General Electric Com
pany, will address engineers and phys
icists on "Lightning in the Laboratory
and in Nature" at 11 o'clock Tuesday
morning in the Little Theatre.
Peek is known internationally for
his researches in high voltage pheno
mena. His lecture will include lantern
slides and motion pictures.
The University of Idaho football
team has traveled more miles than
any other team in Pacific Conference
football.
