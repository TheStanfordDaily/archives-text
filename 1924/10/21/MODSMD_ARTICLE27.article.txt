# Y. M. C. A. HAS LIMITED NUMBER OF "BIBLES"
## 
### 
Freshmen and new students who
have not yet received a copy of the
freshman "Bible" are urged to call at
the office of the Y. M. C. A. on the
campus for one of the booklets. A
limited number are on hand and will
be issued to students for their own
use.
Thirteen hundred booklets were
printed for the opening of the new
year and have been issued to a large
uumber of students during the past
three weeks.
The "Bibles" contain interesting in
formation regarding activities on the
campus and a directory of student or
ganizations with the officers.
