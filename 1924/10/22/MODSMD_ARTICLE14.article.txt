# Victor J. West Will Speak Before Stanford Dames
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will hold their
first meeting o the year tomorrow af
ternoon at the Women's Clubhouse.
This will be a get-together meeting
and after a short business session
there will be a program and a social
meeting. Professor Victor J. West is
to give a short talk on the present
political conditions. Tea will be served
and wives of all Stanford students are
invited.
