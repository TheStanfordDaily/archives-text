# M. K. BENNETT BACK FROM INVESTIGATIONS
## 
### 
Merrill K. Bennett, Junior Research
Associate of the Food Research Insti
tute, returned to Palo Alto Monday
from a two months' business trip in ,
which he visited the more important !
institutions where investigations into
farm costs of production are being
conducted.
Among them were the state colleges
or universities in Kansas, lowa, Illi
nois, Ohio, New York, Wisconsin, Min
nesota, and North Dakota, as well as
the Bureau of Agricultural Economics
at Washington, D. C. Bennett reports
that extensive interest is displayed in
the determination of farm costs and
in the forecasting of prices of agri
cultural products, considerable pro
gress being evident in both lines of
research.
