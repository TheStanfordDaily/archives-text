# Spanish Club Members In First Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Spanish Club members will gather
tonight at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse for the first meeting of the
year. Mr. Ernest Templin, Mr. E. P.
Guerra, and Professor Aurelio Es
pinosa will address the members in
Spanish.
Mr. Templin, who has recently re
turned from Spain, will speak on the
economic and political situation there
at the present time. Conditions in
Mexico will*be discussed by Mr.
Guerra, last year's president of the
club.
