# Toyon Will Entertain At Open House Sunday
## 
### 
Toyon Club will hold open house
Sunday, October 26. This is the first
social event of the year at Toyon and
the club members have planned spe
cial entertainment for the evening and
invite the entire campus to be pres
ent.
The hall will be officially open to

visitors during the hours of 7 and 9 in
the evening and at 7:30 a program of
classical music arranged by Martin
D'Andrea, University tenor, will be
presented.
Those on the program include James
Arthur, banjo; Dr. Moreno, guitar;
Maria Anderson, soprano; Malcolm
Davidson, 'cello; Elizabeth Peirce,
violin; Martin D'Andrea, tenor; and
Marjorie Mac Donald, piano.
