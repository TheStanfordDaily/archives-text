# NOTICE
## Swimming for Women 
### 
The following swimming classes are
open to women of the Stanford faculty,
faculty children, Stanford alumnae,
Stanford Dames, and friends of the
University: intermediate and ad
vanced swimming, Tuesdays and
Thursdays at 3 o'clock; elementary
swimming, Wednesdays and Fridays
at 2 o'clock. Registration will close
on Wednesday, October 29.
Signed: Helen Masters Bunting.
