# Instructions for Voting
## 
### 
Any person connected with Stanford is eligible to vote.
Clip out the above ballot and place it in one of the following
ballot boxes.
English corner.
Political Science Building.
Engineering corner.
Designation your affiliation and give your address.
Any ballot incomplete or defaced will not be counted.
Above all, Vote! Once! Because of a late change in plans, this
straw vote will be conducted under the Honor System.
