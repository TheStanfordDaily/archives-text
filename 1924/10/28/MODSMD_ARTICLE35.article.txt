# STUDENT ASSEMBLIES SET FOR THURSDAY
## 
### 
Women of the University will give
their views on campus problems and
how to solve them at 9 o'clock Thurs
day morning at a student assembly
strictly for the women, in .the Little
Theatre.
At the same time, a meeting will be
conducted by the men of the Univer
sity in the Assembly hall.
