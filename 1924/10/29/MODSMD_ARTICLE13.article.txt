# Officers of Freshman Class Will Be Elected At Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
Election of the freshman class of
ficers will take place in the Little
Theutre at a meeting of the class
tomorrow noon. At yesterday's meet
ing. because of the small attendance
due to the rain, it was decided to defer
the election two days. Nominees for
the office of president of the class
are Ken Faulkner and Lee Bering, Wil
liam Pabst having withdrawn his
nomination.
A rally of the class will be held
Thursday evening in the lobby of En
cina. Plans for a dance and a smoker
will be discussed and the report of
the House Committee will be read.
Stanford yells and songs will be under
the direction of "Dutch" McAllister.
