Say, Guy:
I liave just finished reading "Chair
man of Roble Reception's" remarks in
tonight's Daily, and I am pretty darn
mad. I think her remarks are impu
dent, untrue, uncalled for, and awfully
inconsiderate.
I'm the guy that said I was a cripple.
I was, too. I had just been dancing
with a frail, and she had said I danced
like a clown. So I bit her on the ear.
She kicked me on the shins, and there
I was.
So along conies this dame, and says:
"I want you to meet Miss Awk." I
says: "I don't want to meet no dames,
the last dame I met kicked me in the
shins."
She says: "She did, did she, huh?" ;
So she kicked me in the face, and
there 1 was. Yours truly,
ABE SNABE.
