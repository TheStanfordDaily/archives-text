# Graduate Manager to Sell Athletic Event Pictures
## 
###

of the Board of Athletic Control arc
on sale in the graduate manager's of
fice. These pictures include action, in
dividual, and group photographs of
athletes. The Board of Athletic Con
trol places these pictures on sale
merely as an accommodation to the
students, and makes no profit on them.
