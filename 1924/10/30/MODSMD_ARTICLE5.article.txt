# H. J. HALL COLLECTS, EDITS NEW VOLUME OF AMERICAN VERSE
## 
### 
The Library has recently received
an addition to its Stanford Collection,
a collection of all the works published;
by Stanford graduates and professors.
This work, entitled "Benjamin Tomp
son," is a volume of poems of Benja
min Tompson, the first native-born
poet of America, collected with an
introduction by Professor H. J. Hall,
of the English department.
The volume was published this year
by the Houghton-Mifflin Company.
