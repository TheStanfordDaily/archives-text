# Encina Club Discusses Plans for Dance, Smoker
## 
### 
Plans for a dance and a smoker
were outlined at a meeting of Encina
Club last night. The dance will be held
on November 15 if the date can be
secured, and the smoker, which will
be tendered to the sophomores, will
be held a week from next Tuesday.
John Long, chairman of the House
Committee, read the rules and regula
tions which the freshmen are expected
to follow during their stay at Encina.
The committee for the dance will be
appointed sometime within the com
ing week.
