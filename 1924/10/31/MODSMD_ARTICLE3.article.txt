# Rally Committee Passes Out Hammer and Coffin Stickers for U. S. C. Game
## 
### 
Windshield stickers bearing an axe
and coffin have been printed by Ham
mer and Coffin Society for use at the
U. S. C. game. The design was drawn
by Elizabeth Roper from a "hunch"
furnished by Bill Wright.
Distribution of tlie stickers will start
at the Santa Clara game this after
noon, when members of the Rally
committee plan to decorate the wind
shield* of all cars parked around the
stadium. On Saturday morning, from
10 to 12, the stickers will be given
out at the Daily Palo Alto office. Ad
ditional copies can be procured all
next week at the Daily office.
