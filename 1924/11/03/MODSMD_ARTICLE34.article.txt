# DR. A. S. EDDINGTON TO TALK OF STARS
## 
### 
Dr. A. S. Eddington, Plumian Profes
sor of Astronomy at Cambridge, Eng
land, since 1913, will talk on "The In
terior of a Star," at 4:15 Wednesday
afternoon in the Little Theatre.
Dr. Eddington is the author of "Space,
Time, and Gravitation," a simple expo
sition of the principles of relativity, and
numerous other books dealing with
physical and mathematical astronomy.
He was president of the Astronomy
Society of England from 1921 to 1923,
and has been very active in research in
this field.
"Captain Applejack" will be given
some time this fall by the University
of Colorado.
