# Gil Wheat Delegate to Interfraternity Council
## 
### 
Gil Wheat was elected to represent
Stanford at the National Interfratern
ity Conference at the regular meeting
of the Interfraternity Council last
night.
The national conference will be held
in New York on November 28 and 29.
At this time representatives from all
the college interfraternity organiza
tions will, gather.
Wheat will leave here after the Big
Game and will spend two weeks on the
trip.
