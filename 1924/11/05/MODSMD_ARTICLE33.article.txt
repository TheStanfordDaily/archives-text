# Judge Ralston to Speak At P. A. Community House
## 
### 
An address on "Why Nations Fight"
will be given in the Palo Alto Com
munity House at 8 p.m. tomorrow by
Judge Jackson H. Ralston. Judge Ral
ston is considered an authority on
international law and has served on
several boards. Some of his books are
in the files of the Stanford library.
