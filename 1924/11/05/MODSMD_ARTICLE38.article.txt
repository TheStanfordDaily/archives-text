# Olympiad Diving Mentor Visitor at Encina Pool
## 
### 
Fred Spohnberg, who assisted Ernie
Brandsten in coaching the American
divers at the Olympic Games, was a
visitor at Encina pool yesterday. He
discussed Cardinal swimming pros
pects with his erstwhile chief.
Spohnberg will serve as diving in
structor at Miami, Florida, during the
coming winter.
