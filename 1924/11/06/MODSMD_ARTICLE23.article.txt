# Seven Men Elected to Masonic Club Last Night
## 
### 
Seven new men were elected to
membership in the Stanford Masonic
held last night in the trophy room of
Club at its second regular meeting
the Old Union. The candidates passed
were the following: Guy Holliday,
Lloyd E. Graham, William G. Lee, Jr.,
Carl L. Johnson, Floyd B. Pierson,
Maurice Nielsen, and Sidney W. Rob
inson.
An invitation was extended to all
Master Masons on the campus to at
tend the club's regular meetings which
are held on the first Wednesday of
each month.
