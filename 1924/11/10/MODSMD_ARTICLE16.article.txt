# "DULCY" TRYOUTS BEGIN TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tryouts for the sophomore play,
"Dulcy," will be held tomorrow after
noon in the Little Theater from 3 to 5
o'clock.
"Dulcy" will require quite a large cast,
and should prove to be an opportunity
for all sophomores who are interested
in dramatics to take part in their class
play. Gordon Davis will supervise the
tryouts, with the sophomore play com
mittee assisting him. The sophomore
play will be the climax of the Sophomore
Week-End, which will be celebrated on
December 5 and 6.
