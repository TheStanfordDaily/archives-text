# Journal Club to Present Exhibition of Specimens
## 
### 
An exhibition of specimens and book
reviews will be given tonight at a meet
ing of the Geology Journal Club in the
Geology library.
Professor Frederick Buss will talk on
'"Observations of Streams and Valleys in
the Santa Cruz Range." Arthur L.
Crawford will discuss "The Origin of
Igneous Pebbles." Theodore K. Sawyer
will present "Blackburn on Oil Shale
Extraction."
