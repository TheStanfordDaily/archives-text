# Edward Vail Will Speak At Cosmopolitan Club
## 
### 
Edward Vail, assistant secretary of the
Stanford Y. M. C. A., will speak before
an open meeting of the Cosmopolitan
club at 7:30 tonight in the Women's
clubhouse on his experiences in Russia
during eighteen months of refugee work.
The Chinese members of the club will
give an entertainment consisting of
Chinese music.
