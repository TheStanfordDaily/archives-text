# De Molay Planning Dance To Be Given December 6
## 
### 
Plans for a dance were discussed at
the De Molay meeting held last night
at the Masonic Temple in Palo Alto.
The dance, which is to be an invita
tional affair, will be given on Decem
ber 6 at the Masonic Temple. Elabo
rate plans for decorations and refresh
ments are being made. A campus or
chestra will supply tunes for the occa
sion.
The business of the meeting also
included voting on candidates. After
the meeting, the members adjourned
to the Stanford Theatre to see D. W.
Griffith's latest film, "America."
