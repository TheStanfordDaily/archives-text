# Y. W. C. A. Women to Hear World Fellowship Talks
## 
### 
World fellowship is the subject of
an open Y. W. C. A. meeting in the
Roble library tomorrow at 7 o'clock,
to observe World Student Friendship
week.
The purpose of the World Student
Friendship week is the promotion of
a better feeling of internationalism.
Student speakers will bring campus
women into relation with the move
ment with talks on their experiences
in foreign countries and on student
conditions abroad. All women are in
vited.
