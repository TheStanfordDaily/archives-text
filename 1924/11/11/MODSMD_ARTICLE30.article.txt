# Encina Club Gives Smoker For Sophomores Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the sophomore class
will be entertained tonight at a
smoker given by the members of En
cina hall. A special program of stunts,
boxing matches, and music has been
arranged and it is planned to serve a
light luncheon. The smoker will serve
as a means of cementing the good feel
ing between the freshman and sopho
more classes.
John E. Munholland is the chairman
of the committee in charge of arrange
ments.
