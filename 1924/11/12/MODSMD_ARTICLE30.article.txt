# Faculty Research Club Will Meet at 8 Tonight
## 
### 
The Faculty Research Club will
meet in the Faculty Clubhouse at 8
o'clock tonight. Besides the regular
business of the meeting Professor
Lawrence Becking of the Law depart
ment will read a paper on "The
Growth of Lower Organisms," and
Professor Henry Lanz of the Slavic
Literature department will read an
article on "The Evolution of Educa
tion in Soviet Russia."
