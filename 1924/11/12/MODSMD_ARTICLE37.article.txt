# Ed Vail to Give impressions Of Russia Before Euphronia
## 
### 
Ed Vail, recently returned from a
visit to Russia, will talk on his ex
periences and observations there at
the meeting of the Euphronia Club,
7:30 p.m., Thursday, in the Women's
Clubhouse. An open discussion will
follow the talk. The public is invited.
The date of the meeting has been
changed from Wednesday to Thurs
day in order to avoid interference with
the Law Smoker this week.
