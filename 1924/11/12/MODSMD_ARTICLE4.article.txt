# Encina Club Smoker Draws Crowd of Underclassmen
## 
### 
Two hundred freshmen and sopho
mores packed the Encina hall lobby
last night for the smoker given by
Encina club. A varied program of
jokes, recitations, and humorous skits
by Frank Sansone, '23, and William
Pabst, '28, and two boxing matches
furnished entertainment. Music was
given by the Hess-Young band, which
will play for the Encina club dance
next Saturday night.
-Hud" McGonigle and John Munhol
land had charge of arrangements for
the smoker.
