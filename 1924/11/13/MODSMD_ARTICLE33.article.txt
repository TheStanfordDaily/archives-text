# PROFESSOR ROBINSON AUTHOR OF BOOK ON POLITICAL PARTIES
## 
### 
Professor Edgar E. Robinson, of the
History department, chairman of the
Comittee on Public Exercises, is "the
author of "The Evolution of American
Political Parties," which has been pub
lished this month by Harcourt, Brace
& Company.
The book deals with the origin,
growth, and change of political parties
in the United States from pre-revolu
tionary days to the present time.
Robinson has reviewed the succes
sion of parties which have been agen
cies for the expression of public opin
ion in the United States. He has also
considered the development of a three
party system, similar to the present
English system, ill the United States.
