# Cotillion Ticket Sale Monday for Sophomores
## 
### 
Tickets for the Sophomore Cotillion
and the tea dance will go on sale at 1
o'clock Monday in the Administration
building. Tickets will be sold only to
those sophomores who have paid their
class dues.
Cotillion tickets will sell for $5
while the dansant will be 25 cents.
Admission to the Saturday afternoon
tea is limited to purchasers of Cotillion
tickets.
