# THURSDAY LAST DAY STUDENTS MAY GET BIG GAME TICKETS
## 
### 
Big Game tickets that were ordered j
by students will be distributed at the j
ticket booths on the first floor of the i
Administration building until 5 p.m., j
Thursday. The booths are open from
8 to 12 and 1 to 5 daily. Each student,
must bring his own student body card
and sign for his own tickets.
The ticket department of the Board
of Athletic Control calls attention to
the following statemens:
1. Each student is entitled to
apply for one rooters' ticket at
$1.50. This ticket is not transfer
able and if used by anyone but the
original purchaser is subject to
confiscation.
2. Every applicant is responsible
for the tickets allotted to him.
Anyone whose tickets are sold or
offered for sale at a premium will
be refused the privilege of appli
cation in the future. Violation of
either of the above statements will
constitute scalping. In the past the
Students' Councils have dealt with
all cases of scalping. Three men
have boen expelled from the Uni
versity for this offense. It is hoped
that circumstances will not arise
this year which will cause action
to be taken by the Students' Coun
cil.
