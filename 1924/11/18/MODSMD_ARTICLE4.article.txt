# Near East Relief Plans To Be Discussed at Tea
## 
### 
Plans for Golden Rule Sunday will
be discussed at tea which Mrs. Jordan
is giving at her home tomorrow after
noon. A small group of women from
Stanford, Palo Alto, Menlo Park, and
Atherton are meet at this time to
make plans for the Golden Rule Sun
day which is under the auspices of the
Near East Relief Commission.
