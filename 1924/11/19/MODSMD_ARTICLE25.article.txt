# Dr. Bailey Talks on Folk Songs at Redwood School
## 
### 
Dr. Margery Bailey of the English
department spoke at the Sequoia High
School in Redwood City last night on
"Folk Songs," of which she gave sev
eral examples supplementary to her
lecture. She addressed the Oakland
alumni on the same subject last Fri
day evening.
Dr. Bailey also lectured to the Mon
day Club in Monterey on "The Double
Renaissance in England," in which she
described the Chaucer and Tudor
Renaissance.
