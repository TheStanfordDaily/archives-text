# Bahai Meeting for Unity Of Religions to Be Held
## 
### 
"The Foundation of All Religions Is
One" is the subject to be discussed
tonight at the Bahai meeting at
7:15 in the Women's Clubhouse.
"The Bahai cause is an inclusive
movement; the of all reli
gions and societies are found here,"
said Abdul Baha, the leader of the
movement. "Christians, Jews, Bud
dhists, Mohammedans, Zoroastrians,
Theosophists, Free Masons, and all
others find their highest aim in this
cause. Religions are many, but Reli
gion is one." The meeting is open to
the public.
