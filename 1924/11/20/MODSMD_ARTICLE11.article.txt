# Wranglers to Be Guests of Euphronia Society Tonight
## 
### 
Wranglers, women's discussion club,
will be the guest of Euphronia debat
ing society, at a meeting at 7:30 to
night. The two groups will discuss, in
an informal way, the subject of the
value of college activities, with a view
to getting both the women's and the
men's' viewpoints.
