# ALDEN HONORED AT PHILOLOGICAL CLUB MEETING YESTERDAY
## 
### 
"Raymond McDonald Alden was a
successful teacher because he led his
students to a keener and a finer ap
preciation," said Professor Henry
David Gray of the English depart
ment, at the memorial meeting held
in honor of Dr. Alden by the Philo
logical Association yesterday.
Professor Gray revealed Dr. Alden
as having had a fundamental sense of
chivalry, and love of beauty, balanced
by a delicate and keen sense of humor.
