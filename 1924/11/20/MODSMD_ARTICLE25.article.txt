# 1928 Interclass Cagers To Begin Work on Monday
## 
### 
With an eye to copping the inter
class basketball championship candi
dates for the freshman intramural
cage quintet will begin workouts next
Monday afternoon. A large number is
expected to turn out for the early
practices.
A call has been issued for would-be
managers of this team and sixteen
have put in a bid for the position. The
manager will be selected by the end
of this week and the freshmen will be
organized and ready for practice work.
