# BIG GAME

Crack, crack!
Two thieving aborigines, caught in
their nefarious design, leaped into the
air and then hit the dust.
With a smile of self-assurance on

his handsome features, Harold Har
greaves wiped the barrel of his trusty
Winchester.
"You may remove the bodies, Wat
kins," he ordered Ills faithful valet.
"If these mice keep gnawing their way
into this apartment a fellow won't be
able to call his soul his own!"
