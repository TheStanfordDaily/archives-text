# "Mayer's Condition Same," Says Dr. T. M. Williams
## 
### 
Walter A. Mayer, Jr., who is at the
Palo Alto Hospital, rested easily last
night, according to Dr. T. M. Williams
of Palo Alto who has charge of the
case. Mayer was thrown from a horse
at the R. O. T. C. field Thursday after
noon and it is believed that his spine
was broken. Mayer has been paralyzed
from the waist down since the acci
dent and his condition has remained
practically unchanged.
W. A. Mayer, Sr., in response to
word of the accident, arrived in Palo
Alto last night from Chicago.
