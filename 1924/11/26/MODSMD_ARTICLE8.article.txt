# Davidson and Fairchiid Win Class Tennis Honors
## 
### 
Bill Davidson and-Paul Faircliild are
senior and junior class champions in
the intramural tennis tournament, and
will compete with the sophomore and
freshman title-holders for the Univer
sity championship next month.
Davidson defeated Milliken 6-3, 6-4,
in the finals in his division. Fairchild
eliminated Ogden 6-1, 6-1. Both victors
are members of the varsity squad.
