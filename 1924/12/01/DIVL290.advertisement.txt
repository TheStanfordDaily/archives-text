"Resolved: That the Constitution should be amended to provide for a popular referendum on war.
except in case of actual attack, or in case of a formal declaration of war against the United States."
(Stanford affirmative speakers: J. C. Duniway and W. L. Gosslin.) Each speaker may be interrupted
twice by the audience with times out for qiiestion and answer.

Southern California, Reed College, Willamette University, Whitman College, and Stanford University.
Question: "The Power of the Supreme Court to Override Congressional Legislation." The public
is invited.
