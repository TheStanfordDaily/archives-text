# GOLFERS WILL FORM ALL-UNIVERSITY CLUB
## 
### 
Stanford golfers will complete the
organization of an all-University links
club at a meeting in the trophy room
of the old Union tomorrow night.
At a preliminary gathering held last
week general plans for the association
were outlined. Tomorrow's meeting
will be devoted chiefly to the election
of officers and the adoption of a con
stitution. The membership will in
clude all men above first-year stand
ing who are interested in golf, regard
less of their playing ability.
Announcements of the meeting have
been mailed to all the living groups on
the campus, and a large turnout of
enthusiasts is anticipated.
