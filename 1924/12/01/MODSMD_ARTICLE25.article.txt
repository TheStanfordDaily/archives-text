# Six Hundred Will Attend Y. M. C. A. Asilomar Meeting
## 
### 
Six hundred delegates from Pacific
Coast universities will attend the an
nual Y. M. C. A. conference which
begins on December 27 at Asilomar.
The conference will be the largest
of its kind ever held, and will include
men from seven western states, Brit
ish Columbia, Mexico, and Hawaii.
Outstanding moral and civic issues
will be discussed under the leadership
of prominent churchmen, who will en
deavor to locate the responsibilities of
college students and to consider voca
tion and Christian service.
