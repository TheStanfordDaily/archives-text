# Y. W. C. A. Workers Will Address Women in Roble
## 
### 
Miss Maude Russel, Y. W. C. A.
worker supported in China by Stan
ford, and Miss Henrietta Thompson,
foreign student secretary of the West
Coast, will speak at a general meeting
for all women in the Roble lobby at
7:15 Wednesday evening.
Miss Russel and Miss Thompson will
arrive on the campus tomorrow, and
will be the guests of Miss Isabel
Capps, Y. M. C. A. secretary, until
Friday.
