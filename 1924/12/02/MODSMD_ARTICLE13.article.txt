# Chappie to Publish Cross-Word Number
## 
### 
Whenever anything foolish happens
the Old Lsoy is always all for it —yes
you guessed it, children, the next ef
fort is to be a crossword puzzle num
ber.
The deadline has been set for De
cember 8. Artistic hunches should be
In before that time, if possible, in
order to give the staff artists a chance
to work up the ideas.
Anyone wlio has ever worked a
crossword puzzle has thought of some
thing funny to say about it. If your
opinions on the Subject are printable
send them in.
