# Surprise Dance Will Be Given Hard Workers
## 
### 
All sophomores are putting aside the j
cross-word puzzles this week and are |
trying to figure out just what night j
"Chubby" Tyroler and his gang of j
syncopators will be at the pavilion.
The chairman of the decoration com-1
mittee has planned a "Suprise Dance \
Night" for the industrious second-year j
students who lenditheir time to aid j
in decorating the pavilion for the j
Cotillion.
Some night, during the week of
decorating, from 7 to 10 o'clock, Ty- j
roler will appear on the scene and
the workers will be treated to an hour
or so of dancing. Consequently the j
wise birds in the class of '27 are put- j
ting in a little time every night this
week.
Both men and women are needed for
the work of decorating. This evening
will be an especially busy one because
all of the material for decorating and
the properties rented from San Fran
cisco have arrived on the campus.
These will have to be assorted and
made ready to put in place.
