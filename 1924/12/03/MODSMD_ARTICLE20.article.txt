# Edith Mirrielees Speaks At High School Assembly
## 
### 
Edith R. Mirrielees, assistant pro
fessor of English, addressed an as
sembly at the Sequoia ' Union High
School in Redwood City yesterday
morning on the subject, "Reasons for
Required Reading in High School and
College." ,
The lecture was given to explain
the need for comprehensive literature
courses which have become a require
ment in so many of the high schools
of the country.
