# German Club Has Special Christmas Session Tonight
## 
### 
The German Club will hold a Christ-;
mas meeting of the organization to
night at 7:30 on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. Dr. Walter Gie
seeke will speak on German Christmas
customs, and Mrs. E. H. Allen will
I
sing.
A Christmas tree with the custom
ary surprises for all those present is
also being planned. A few German
games and refreshments will complete
the program. Everyone interested is
invited to attend.
