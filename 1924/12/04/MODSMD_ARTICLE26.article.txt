# TROTTER PUBLISHES HISTORY OF CANADA
## 
### 
Professor Reginald G. Trotter, re
cently assistant professor of History
at Stanford and now associate profes
sor of British and Canadian History at
Queen's University, Kingston, Ontario,
has just had published "The Canadian
Federation."
The book is a development of Pro
fessor Trotter's doctor's thesis. It was
published by J. M. Dent of London and
Toronto and printed in Great Britain.
The volume is fully documented with
extensive bibliography.
