# Freshmen at Encina to Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
###

siding at Encina hall will hold a meet
ing tonight at 7 o'clock in the lobby
of the hall. This will be the last meet
ing of the class during this quarter
and matters of regular business yet
on hand will be cleared up at the
gathering.
