# Encina Club Will Give Smoker Tuesday Night
## 
### 
A smoker will be given next Tuesday
night at Encina hall by the members
of Encina club, according to announce
ment made today by Lawrence Mc-
Gonigle, the president. Arrangements
for the affairs have not yet been for
mulated but there is a possibility that
wrestling or jui-jitsu matches will
take place of the boxing matches that
have been the attractions of other
"get together" affairs.
