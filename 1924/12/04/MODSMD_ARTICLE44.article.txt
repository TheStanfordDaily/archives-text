# PROFESSOR ADAMS WILL RETURN SOON
## 
### 
Professor E. D. Adams of the His
tory Department will arrive in New
York from Europe soon, according to
word received on the campus this
week. Professor Adams, who has been
acquiring material for the Hoover War
Library in Paris, London, Brussels,
Berlin, Warsaw, Prague, Vienna, and
Berne, will attend the annual meet
ing of the American Historical As
sociation at Richmond, Virginia, dur
ing the week following Christmas.
There he will represent the Pacific
Coast division of the association, and
will return to the campus on Jan
uary 5.

A series of lectures on educational
psychology given over the radio will
earn credit for listeners at the Uni
versity of Kansas.
