# "Bahai Temple" Is Topic For Discussion Tonight
## 
### 
"The Bahai Temple" will be the
subject for discussion at the regular
Bahai meeting at 7:15 tonight in the
Women's Clubhouse.
The Bahai Temple, the Mashriqui'
ladkar, is being constructed on the
shores of Lake Michigan in Chicago.
The Temple is being built so that
it will be representative of the great
religions of the world. No services
will be held, but the books of all re
ligions will be kept there.
The meeting is open to the public.
