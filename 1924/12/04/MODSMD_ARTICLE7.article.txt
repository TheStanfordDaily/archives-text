# Stanford Dames Prepare To Entertain Tomorrow
## 
### 
At a party to be given by the Stan
ford Dames in the Women's Club
house at 8 o'clock tomorrow night,
Professor O. E. Norton of the Public
Speaking department will give a
reading, and Mrs. Herbert R. Laslett
will offer two songs.
Dancing, both old fashioned and
modern, will occupy the remainder
of the evening, according to Mrs.W.
E. Wiley, who is in charge of the pro
cram.
