# L. D. Students May Prepare Winter Study Lists Early
## 
### 
Lower Division advisors have set
aside the mornings of Thursday, Fri
day, and Saturday next week to assist
men and women in Lower Division in
preparing their winter quarter sched
ules.
Students are urged to sign any time
next week for appointments with their
advisors. The arrangement has been
made in order that each student may
be given special aid in making out his
program. Time schedules will be on
sale by Wednesday.
