# Maloney to Give Talk in San Francisco Wednesday
## 
### 
Harry Maloney will address the Tide
Water Oil Sales Corporation at their
annual convention next Wednesday at
the Hotel Whitcomb in San Francisco.
He will speak on the "Importance of
Keeping Fit."
On "Hello Day" at Northwestern,
each girl was expected to say "Hello"
to every other girl she met on the cam
pus.
