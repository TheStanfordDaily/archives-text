# Women's Clubhouse Will Be Scene of Cast Dance After "Dulcy" Tomorrow
## 
### 
A special meeting of the Step
pers' Society will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse immediately
after tomorrow night's production
of "Dulcy." Members of the cast
will be the guests of honor, and
the -entertainment will consist of
music, laughter and lemonade.
Active members of the-society
will be admitted upon payment of
the regular weekly dues. New mem
bers will be initiated upon applica
tion at the door until 12 o'clock,
at which time the meeting will ad
journ to reconvene at some future
date.
As a special attraction, Tyroler's
orchestra will play some of the
latest compositions of such masters
as Irving Berlin and Ernest R. Hall.
