# BABE SOCCER TEAM TO MEET SEMINARY PLAYERS TOMORROW
## 
### 
The freshman soccer team will play
the San Anselmo Theological Semi
nary tomorrow afternoon in San An
selmo. This is the last game of the
season for the freshmen.
With a rest since the second Cali
fornia freshmen game, the babes
should be in excellent condition. The
varsity soccer team defeated the semi
nary squad recently by a scor.e of 7 to
0 in an easy game played on the sda
dium field. From all reports the San
Anselmo aggregation play a better
game on their home field than when
they travel.
The regular team that started the
California game will play, while Font-
Reaulx and Byron Hay will be taken
up as substitutes. Theron Hooker is
now playing outside right due to his
demonstration of ability in the second
California freshman game.
