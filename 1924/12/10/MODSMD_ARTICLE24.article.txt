# Rey Will Read Selections From Maeterlinck Tonight
## 
### 
Maurice Rey, graduate student at
the University of Liege, Belgium, who
is visiting Stanford University, will
read various selections from Maeter
linck, and Elizabeth Bentley will play
a number of piano selections at a
meeting of the French Club at 7:30
tonight in the Women's Clubhouse.
This will be the last meeting of the
club this quarter.
