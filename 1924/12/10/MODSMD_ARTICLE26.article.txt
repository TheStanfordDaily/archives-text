# Cottrell Re-elected to Municipal League Council
## 
### 
Professor Edwin A. Cottrell, of the
Political Science department, was re
elected a member of the council of
the National Municipal League at its
annual convention held in Cambridge.
This is the third three-year term
which Professor Cottrell has served.
Other members elected were Dr.
Charles A. Beard of New York, Pro
fessor T. H. Reed of the University of
Michigan, Louis Brownlow, city man
ager of Knoxville, Tenn.; Dean Som
mer of New York University Law
School, and Mrs. Slade, president of
the League of Women Voters of New
York.
