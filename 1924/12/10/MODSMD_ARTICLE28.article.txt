# Campus Makes Favorable Red Cross Drive Showing
## 
### 
Six hundred and seventy-nine dol
lars was the total amount raised on
the campus in the annual Red Cross
Roll Call. The results here compared
very favorable with the rest of the
district, according to Red Cross head
quarters.
Palo Alto joined to the extent of
$1,435. Receipts from the entire dis
trict have been $2,841.25 up to date.
As some places have not yet reported,
the mark of $3,272 made last year will
probably be covered, according to
Mayor Edwin A. Cottrell, who com
plimented the campus on its showing.
