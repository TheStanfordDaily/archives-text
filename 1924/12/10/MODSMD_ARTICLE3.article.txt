# DAILY STAFF WILL HOLD SEMI-ANNUAL BANQUET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Press representatives will assemble
at the Alpha O house tonight for the
semi-annual banquet of the Daily Palo
Alto, given entirely at the expense of
the neophyte news hounds. The nose
bag will be tied on at 8 p. m., but pre
vious to this riotous, rollicking, rous
ing entertainment will be accorded by
way of the approved dancing custom.
Inspiration for this will be supplied
by a three-fifths portion of Tyroler's
Terspischorean Tantalizers.
It is rumored that the neophytes
will render a short skit, which will be
completely received by George Jean
Nathan in the next issue of Whiz
Bang.
