# Registration Dance Will Be Given By Junior Class
## 
### 
Next quarter's registration dance is
under the auspices of the junior class,
and will be held in the Women's Club
house. As the date of the students'
homecoming will be January 5, the
date of the dance will be identical.
The customary admission charge will
be collected and Tyroler's orchestra
will furnish the music.
