# Professor Franklin Wins Nichols Medal for 1925
## 
### 
Professor Edward C. Franklin of the
Chemistry department has been awarded
the Nichols medal for 1925. Professor
Franklin received the medal for his
paper, "Systems of Acids, Bases,' and
Salts," which was considered by the jury
as the best chemical research paper pub
lished during the past year.
The medal was established by Dr. Wil
liam H. Nichols in order to advance
chemical research, and the American
Chemical Society will present the award
to Professor Franklin on March 6 in
New York City. Professor Franklin
was elected a director of the American
Chemical Society Wednesday at is an
nual meeting, which was held in New
York.
