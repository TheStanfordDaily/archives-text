# Campus Panorama Placed In Ferry Building Here
## 
### 
A bird's eye view painting of Stan
ford University was unveiled in the
nave of the San Francisco Ferry build
ing on Tuesday by the California De
velopment Association.
This panoramic scene of the Univer
sity is three feet wide and five feet
high and shows clearly the buildings
and grounds in their exact location.
With this panorama and in the same
large educational panel, are a miniature
model of the University of California
campus and a panorama of the Univer
sity of Southern California grounds.
