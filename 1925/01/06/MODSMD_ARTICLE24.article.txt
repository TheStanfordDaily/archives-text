# SYRACUSE FACULTY TO PRESENT EXTRAVAGANZA
## 
### 
"Faculty Follies" will Wo presented
this year by the faculty of Syracuse
University. This "professorial extrava
ganza" will be in the form of a three
ring indoor circus, and will contain
both sideshows and vaudeville. Talent
for the annal professor's frolic will be
drawn from among the 500 members
of the Syracuse faculty.
The 1924 "Faculty Follies" is for the
benefit of the new faculty clubhouse.
