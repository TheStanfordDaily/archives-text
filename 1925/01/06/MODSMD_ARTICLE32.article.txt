# H. D. Gray Begins Course of Lectures at Sacred Heart
## 
### 
Professor H. D. Gray of the English
department, who was recently elected
vice-president of the Philological Asso
ciation of the Pacific Coast, began a
course of lectures on "English Fiction"
at the College of the Sacred Hetyt to
day.
The series of lectures will be given
throughout the entire winter quarter.

Three hundred boxes of apples will
be on display at the annual apple show
to be held at Ohio State University,
February 2 to 6.
