# Class of '28 Urged to Pay Dues as Soon as Possible
## 
### 
Members of the freshman class must
pay up their dues soon, declared "Jim"
Sapero, frosh president, today. The
decision made -by the class during the
last quarter to present each member
of the 1028 grid squad with a silver
cup if it is to go through must bo
backed with the fifty cents due from
each and every freshman.
William B. Johnston, treasurer of
the class has begun active work in col
lecting the money.
