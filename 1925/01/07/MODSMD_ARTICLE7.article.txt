# Freshman Cabinet Holds Y. W. Tea for New Women
## 
### 
Freshman cabinet of the Y. W. C. A.
entertained the new women at a tea
yesterday afternoon in the Iloble
lobby.
Thirteen new women are living at
the hall. The Y. W. C. A. tea is a
quarterly affair, at which flew and old
women meet.
