# Professor Treat Writes Monthly Far East Review
## 
### 
Profesor Payson J. Treat, executive
head of the History department, is the
author of monthly articles on the Far
East, published in Current History.
In the January issue Professor Treat
writes of the establishment of the new
government of China under Tuan Chi-jui
at Peking. A review of the history of
Japan during the month of November is
also included in the January review of
the Far East.
