# SPRINGBOARD DIVING FOR WOMEN
## 
### 
A course in diving will be given at
2 on Wednesdays and Fridays at Roble
Swimming Pool, under the instruction
of Mrs. Brandsten, provided at least
ten persons sign up for the course. A
fee of $5 will be charged. The course
is open to all university women and
their friends.
Signed: Helen Masters Bunting,
Director Physical Education
for Women.
