# New Business Staff Members
## 
### 
Four were successful in the tryout period of one week for the busi
ness staff of the Daily Palo Alto. Those who are to be congratulated
are: N. R. Morgan, R. M. Gunn, J. Crebs, and W. H. Levit.
There will be a meeting of both old and new members of the business
staff at 1 o'clock sharp, tomorrow.

DR. DURAND has been chosen to help determine the country's
engineering policy in the air. The committee has a rich field for action,
and Stanford's great aeronautical authority is to be congratulated upon
this opportunity for advancing the world of flight.
