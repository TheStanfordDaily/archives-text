# "Peace and War" Will Be Discussion Topic at Union
## 
### 
"Peace War" will be the subject
of discussion at the first meeting for this
quarter at the Round Table Discussion
Grotip at the Union Thursday noon.
Professor Theodore Hoover of the Min
ing and Metallurgy department will
take the preparedness side of the ques
tion while several students who have
been selected will oppose him by sup
porting the various peace plans.
This meeting will be held Thursday
but the other regular meetings held each
week will be on Wednesdays.
