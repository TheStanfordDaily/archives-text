# Twenty-Two Students Try For Daily Staff Position
## 
### 
Fifteen undergraduate men and
seven undergraduate women signified
their desire to become affiliated with
the editorial staff of the Daily Palo
Alto at the first tryoutee meeting held
in the Press building at 12:50 p.m.
today. Of this number two were mem
bers of the class of '26, five were
members of the class of '27, and fifteen
were freshmen.
Anyone who did not attend today's
meeting may report at the meeting to
morrow at the same time and place
and signify his or her intention. The
work that was assigned today must be
made up by the latecomers.
