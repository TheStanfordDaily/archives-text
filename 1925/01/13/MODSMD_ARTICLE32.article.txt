# "Human Fly" to Scale Palo Alto Skyscraper Tomorrow
## 
### 
Babe White, the "Human Fly," will
scale one of the skyscrapers of Palo Alto
tomorrow afternoon at 2:30 and at 7:30
in the evening. He has selected the Ra
mona Hotel as the scene of his climbing.
Before performing the feat White will
pass the hat and, according to advance
notices, must have at least fifty silver
coins before he will undertake to do the
act.
