# Encina Candy Store Moves To Location in Basement
## 
### 
The management of the Encina
Candy Store has anounced a change
of location from the first floor to the
basement. The store is now just to
the right of the door as one comes in
at the west entrance to the basement.
This new location, while not so cen
trally placed as the older one was,
is much larger and permits a better
display.
