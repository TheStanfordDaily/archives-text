# Rally Committee Will Gather for Banquet at Alpha Sigma Phi House
## 
### 
The Rally Commitee will gather to
morrow night at the Alpha Sigma Phi
house to pass judgment on a several
course dinner.
There is no special business to come
up, there will be no awards of any
kind made, and it is hard to tell just
who will do the speaking, according
to an announcement made late last
night by Les Peter, chairman of the
committee.
The close of a successful season
under a new constitution is the only
special feature that is being cele
brated.
