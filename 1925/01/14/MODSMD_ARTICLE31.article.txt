# Jiu Jitsu Exhibition to Be Given Tonight at Gym
## 
### 
A demonstration of Jiu Jitsu will be
given by Sergeant D. R. Hamilton of
the Chicago Police Force at the En
cina Gym, Wednesday night at 8
o'clock.
The exhibition will include a num
ber of self-defense measures suitable
to practice on a thug, or over-jubilant
roommate, and will be open to all men
students.
