# Freshman Women Will Banquet Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Y. W. C. A. freshman discussion
groups will have a banquet at 6 o'clock
tomorrow night in the New Union.
New freshman women are invited.
The program, in charge of Rosalind
Coverley, will be conducted by Miss
Isabel Capps, Y. W. C. A. secretary.
Tickets are on sale -in the various
discussion groups at 75 cents.
