# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
GEOLOGY AND MINING SOCIETY
meets at the Kappa Sigma house at
7:30 p.m.
RADIO CLUB meets in the Corpora
tion Yard at 7:30.
STANFORD JOURNALISTS CLUB
meets at 7:30 at the home of Profes
sor E. W. Smith, 12 Alvarado.
STANFORD GOLF CLUB meeting,
scheduled at 7:15 p.m., postponed
until next Tuesday.
CIVIL ENGINEERING SOCIETY will
meet at 7:15 Thursday evening in
the Senior Directory Room.
SCABBARD A&D BLADE will hold a
short meeting at Military Headquar
ters at 4:45 o'clock.
GERMAN CLUB meets at 7:30 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
FRIDAY
JUNIORS WANTED for boxing mana
gers, Friday afternoon, 4: 30l See
/ Harry Noland in Hary Maloney's
office.
WEDNESDAY
AN OPEN FORUM DISCUSSION will
be held in the lobby of the Old Union
at 7:30 p.m. on the question: "To
what extent is government regula
tion against liquor drinking violated
by Stanford students?" Speakers:
Dean Culver, Professor H. F. Lutz,
and J. C. Duniway. Meeting open to
all students, members of Faculty
and Administration, and residents
of Palo Alto and vicinity.
