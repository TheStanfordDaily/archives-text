# Junipero Serra Club to Be Host to Campus in Clubhouse Friday Night
## 
### 
The Junipero Serra Club will give
its annual dance Friday night at 9
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse. The
afft'.ir will be open to the campus pub
lic.
Roughs are cordially invited, nay,
urged to contribute themselves by way
of decoration for the windows. No tax
will be levied upon these knights of
the corduroy. They will, in fact, be
as free as the March winds.
The admission will be one dollar and
ten cents, which includes the privilege
of listening, as intently as you wish,
to the melodious strains of Tyroler's
orchestra.
