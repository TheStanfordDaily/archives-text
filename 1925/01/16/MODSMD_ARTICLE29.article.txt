# Dr. E. D. Adams Scheduled To Address Education Club
## 
### 
Dr. E. D. Adams of the History de
partment is to be the speaker of the
evening at a meeting of the Education
club next Monday. It will be held in
the Women's Clubhouse at 8 p.m.
Dr. Adams' address is on the sub
ject: "Some of the Present-Day Refu
gees from Europe." He has just re
turned from a tour of the continent
and has gathered a fund of experi
ences and first-hand observation's for
the address.
Pennsylvania University, founded in
1740, is one of the four oldest in the
country.
Mortimer L. Schiff made a gift of
$25,000 to the Jewish Theological
Seminary at New York.
