# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
JUNIORS WANTED for boxing mana
gers, Friday afternoon, 4:30c See
Harry Noland in Hary Maloney's
off-ice.
THE EPWORTH LEAGUE of the
Methodist Episcopal church invites
all students to a social to be given
at the church, corner Hamilton and
Webster streets, at 8 p.m.
TUESDAY
JUNFPERO SERRA CLUB meets at
7:30 in the Women's Clubhouse.
WEDNESDAY
AN OPEN FORUM DISCUSSION will
be held in the lobby of the Old Union
at 7:30 p.m. on the question: "To
what extent is government regula
tion against liquor drinking violated
by Stanford students?" Speakers:
Dean Culver, Professor H. F. Lutz,
and J. C. Duniway. Meeting open to
all students, members of Faculty
and Administration, and residents
of Palo Alto and vicinity.
