# Directory of Alumni in Japan Given to McDowell
## 
### 
Jack McDowell, alumni secretary, re
ceived a booklet of local interest from
T. Miwa, the secretary of the Stanford
Club of Japan. It is a Stanford direc
tory of all the alumni in Japan, Japanese
as well as American, and contains 96
names. Opposite each name is the class
and major subject of the person. There
is more data written in Tapancse, the
meaning of which not vt been
ascerta'ned. Six of the alumni listed
were Americans.
