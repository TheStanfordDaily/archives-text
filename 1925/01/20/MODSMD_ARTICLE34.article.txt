# Masonic Club Will Hold Special Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
A special meeting of the Masonic
Club has been called for tomorrow
night at 7:15 in the Trophy Room of
the Old Union. The programs for the
Masonic Club dance to be given on
the evening of February 7 after the
Stanford-California basketball game,
can be obtained by members of the
club and their friends at this meeting.
