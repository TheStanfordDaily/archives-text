# W. G. HAYS LEADS IN FREE THROW CONTEST
## 
### 
At the end of the first day of com
petition in the intramural free-throw
contest, W. G. Hays led the field by
making 18 goals out of a possible 25.
Carl Ankele and D. A. Mowers were
tied for second with 17 throws.
The contest continues today and to
morrow. All those intending to com
pete may report in the gymnasium at
any time and take their throws. A
large number have already entered
this event, which is run in conjunction
with the intramural basketball series.
