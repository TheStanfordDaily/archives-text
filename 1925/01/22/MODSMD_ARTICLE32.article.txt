# Condition of 1928 Football Star Reported Improving
## 
### 
Lawrence Dorcy, a freshman student,
has been lying in a critical condition
in the Letterman hospital in San Fran
cisco, as a result of a blow in the
stomach he received from a flying dis
cus last week.
The San Francisco doctors say
that Dorcy has passed the danger
ous stage, and is rapidly regaining his
strength. He is still confined in the
hospital where he will be for another
week.
Dorcy was a member of the 1928
football team and was practicing for
the coming track season when the acci
dent happened. He was hit Avhen one
of his teammates allowed the discus
to slip from his hand. The plate hit
Dorcy in the center of the stomach,
knocking him unconscious.
