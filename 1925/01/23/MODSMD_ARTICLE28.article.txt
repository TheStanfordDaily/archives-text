# Schubert Club Dissolves For Remainder of Quarter
## 
### 
The Schubert Club has been discon
tinued for winter quarter because of
the difficulty encountered in obtaining
a director. In addition the club did not
believe that enough was accomplished
last quarter to warrant its present
existence.
The University of Michigan is con
ducting an investigation among the
students to see how much the average
man spends while in college.
The University of Berlin will give a
coarse of lectures on "The United
States and Canada; their position in
World Economy and World Politics."

Motion pictures of the life at the
University of Florida will be shown
in that state as an advertisement for
the institution.
Compulsory attendance at student
organization meetings has been tem
porarily adopted at New York Univer
sity on account of the recent lack of
interest.
