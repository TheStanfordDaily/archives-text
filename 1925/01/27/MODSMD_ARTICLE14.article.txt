# Emerson Anounces Topic For Annual Joffre Debate
## 
### 
Faculty Debate Advisor J. G. Emer
son anounces that the tryouts for the
Joffre Debate will be held on February
6. The general topic for the tryout Is
"France and the Colored Races." The
specific topic will be given out one
hour before the debate.
The squad system which is being
tried out in the University this quar
ter will be followed in this debate,
according to Robert Littler. The pur
pose of the squad system is to Rive
more students an opportunity to avail
themselves of debate training and ac
tual practice.
