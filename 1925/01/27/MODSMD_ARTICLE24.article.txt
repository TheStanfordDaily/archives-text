# Y.W.C.A.TOSELL CANDY TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Sophomore Cabinet of the Y.
W. C. A. will conduct a candy sale in
front of the Women's Clubhouse to
morrow morning from 10 to 12:30.
The candy is to be home-made, and
will sell for ten cents a bag, the pro
ceeds to be devoted to the Y. W. C. A.
funds.
