# Boys' Gymnasium Classes Begun by Harry Maloney
## 
### 
Shouts of laughter and yells of de
light arose from the Enclna Gymna
sium on Saturday morning while Harry
Maloney held his first hoys' gymna
sium class of the year.
Forty young athletes turned out for
an hour and a half of recreation and
instruction, which was followed by a
swim in the Enclna pool. These classes
will be held every Saturday morning
and are open to boys who live In the
vicinity of the University.
