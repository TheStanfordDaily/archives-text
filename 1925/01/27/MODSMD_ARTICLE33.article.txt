# Roble, Encina Halls to Share in Dinner Dances
## 
### 
Students living at Roble and Encina
halls will entertain for their mutual
benefit with a "Jolly-up" party in the
form of two dinner dances some time
next week. The plan is to have the
freshman girls entertained at Encina
while an equal number of men of the
freshman class will be entertained at
Roble hall the same evening.
A definite date has not been an
nounced for the affair, but according
to Anne Bancroft and James Sapero,
who are in charge of the plans, the
most probable date is Tuesday, Feb
ruary 3.
