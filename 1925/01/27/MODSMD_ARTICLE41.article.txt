# English Professor Edits Novel by Samuel Butler
## 
### 
The honor of editing Samuel Butler's
book, "The Way of All Flesh," has
been given to Mrs. Frances Theresa
Russell, assistant professor of Eng
lish at Stanford, now in the East. Mrs.
Russell is a specialist in the field of
satire as shown by her book, "Satire
in the Victorian Novel," and by her
articles in various magazines in the
last few years.
Mrs. Russell is also doing some re
search work on the history of common
wealths while in New York City.
