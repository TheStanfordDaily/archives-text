# Bahai Meeting Will Deal With Life of Baha'U'llah
## 
### 
The Bahai meeting at 7:15 tonight
in the Women's Clubhouse will take
up the subject of "The Life and Teach
ings of Baha'u'llah."
Baha'u'llah is the founder of the
Bahai movement. He began his work
and his teachings in Persia, from
where they have spread over the East ;
and to the West. The name Baha'u'llah
means "Glory of God," and was adopt
ed by the teacher to signify his mis
sion. The public is invited.
