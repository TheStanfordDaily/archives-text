WEDNESDAY
MEMBERS OF THE MASONIC CLUB
degree team will meet at 7:15 at the
Sciot's Club, Palo Alto.
BAHAI MEETING at 7:15 in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Subject: "The Life
and Teachings of Baha'u'llah."
THE FORUM will meet at noon in
the Union Banquet Room. Gregario
Patacsil, political science graduate,
will talk in defense of Philippine
independence. The forum has no
regular membership, and is open to
all interested.
CHESS CLUB meets at 12 o'clock
sharp in Basketball Pavilion for
Quad pictures.
GERMAN CLUB will have important
business meeting at 3 o'clock in the
German building.
WRANGLERS SOCIETY meets at 7:15
at the Chi Omega house.
SIGMA DELTA CHI meets in the
Press building at 12:50 p.m.
THURSDAY
RALLY COMMITTEE meets in the
Old Union at 7:15 tonight.
RADIO CLUB meets in Corporation
Yard at 7:30 p.m.
USHERS report for the Peninsula
Musical Concert.
1:50 P. M.—All members of the Eu
phronia Debating Society meet in
basketball pavilion to take Quad pic
ture.
GEOLOGY AND MINING SOCIETY
meets at 7:30 in D. U. house.
RALLY COMMITTEE picture will be
taken for the Quad at 12 o'clock in
the pavilion.
RALLY COMMITTEE will meet at Old
Union at 7:15.
THETA SIGMA PHI will meet at 5:30
in the Women's Clubhouse.
STANFORD AD CLUB of Palo Alto
will meet at 6:15 at the Cardinal
Hotel. Professor B. O. Brown will
continue his course.
FRIDAY
INTERFRATERNITY BOARD OF
CONTROL meets at noon in the
Zeta Psi house. Luncheon will be
served.
USHERS report for "You and I."
WINTER FOOTBALL—All men who
are planning to come out for winter
football practice should report to

room at Encina gymnasium between
