# U. C. Botanist Visits Stanford Campus
## 
### 
Professor Rudolph, of the University
of California Fruit Experiment Station
at Mountain View, was a visitor at the
Botanical Gardens here yesterday. Pro
fessor Rudolph made use of the steriliz
ing apparatus-at the gardens in preparing
his machine for a new series of experi
ments at the U. of C. branch station.
