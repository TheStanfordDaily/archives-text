# Stanford Club of S. F. Will Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
Aileen Brown was elected tennis
manager at the W. A. A. meeting last
night, to take the place of Laura
Gardiner, who has taken out leave of
absence on account of illness.
Grace Judd, basketball manager, pre
sented the schedule of interclass
games. The sort of sweater to be used
for awards this year was discussed.
