# Masonic Club Will Give Dance on February 7
## 
### 
The Masonic Club will give their quar
terly dance on Saturday, February 7, in
the Women's Clubhouse. Plans for the
dance are being made by the dance com
mittee under the direction of M. L. Niel
sen. The Masons expect to celebrate the
defeat of the Bruin quintet at their dance
which will follow the game.
