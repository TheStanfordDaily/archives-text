Opening Dance
Saturday
Aux Gourmets, a delightful new place to dance and to dine, opens
at Atherton Saturday. Plan to come; you will find your Stanford
friends here.
You will 'be greeted by old acquaintances—Joseph Gere, of Joe's
Place, Palo Alto, and Camille Lacombe, of Caf6 de Paris, Atherton.
A campus combination will furnish dance music.
If you are going to the basketball game, dine here before the game,
and return to dance. No extra charge.
Dinner, the plate (including cover until 12), $1.50. Cover after 9, 50c.
Phone reservations (Redwood 625-J) by Thursday.
