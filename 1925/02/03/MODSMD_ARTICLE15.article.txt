# CIRCLE '27 WINNERS GIVEN RED JERSEYS
## 
### 
All sophomores who won a circle '27
in athletics last year have just been
awarded their jerseys by the class of
1927.'
Twenty-eight men are included in
this list who won their numeral in
either soccer, swimming, or water
polo in the events last year. All those
who have already purchased a jersey
will have t*his money refunded.
Cranston Holman is handling the
business for the class of '27 and may
be reached at the Alpha Delt house.
