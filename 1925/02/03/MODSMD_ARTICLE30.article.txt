# Boxers, Fencers, Wrestlers Entertain at Game Saturday
## 
### 
As entertainment between the halves
of the basketball game with California
Saturday night, Harry Maloney an
nounces an "athletic trio." Six bouts
of boxing, fencing, and wrestling will
he carried on simultaneously on the
floor of the pavilion.
