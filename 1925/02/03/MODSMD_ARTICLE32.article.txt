# E. P. Cubberly Speaks at San Jose Commencement
## 
### 
Dr. Elwood P. Cubberly, dean of the
School of Education of Stanford, de
livered the commencement address at
graduation exercises of the San Jose
State Teachers College held Friday.
He stressed the point that develop
ment of the individual rather than an
attempt to force all minds into a single
mold, was the ideal of true education.
One hundred and fifteen students re
ceived diplomas.
