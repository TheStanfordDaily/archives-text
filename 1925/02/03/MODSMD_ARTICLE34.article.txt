# Freshmen Sign to Try Out For Grid Managerial Job
## 
### 
With the start of winter football'
practice, Frank Frye, manager of the
team, has issued a call for freshmen
to try out for the managerial position.
A few first-year men have already
signed up for the job and are out work
ing with the team but many more
candidates are needed. All freshmen
interested should report to one of the
junior managers on the practice field
tomorrow afternoon.
