# Ex Committee Grants "Daily" Permission to Purchase Typewriter
## 
### 
R. E. Rotschiltf, business manager of
the Daily Palo Alto, was authorized to
withdraw $24.78 from the Daily Palo
Alto Improvement Fund for the pur
chase of a typewriter at a meeting of the
Executive Committee last night.
The following dates were granted:
Rally Committee, Feb. 21—Informal.
Sigma Delta Chi, Feb. 21 —Informal.
