# Arne Borg, Swedish Swimmer, Arrives Here February 15
## 
### 
Coach Ernie Brandsten was notified
recently that Arne Borg, Europe's
greatest swimmer, will be in San Fran
cisco on February 15 to begin training
for the national swimming champion
ships to be held here in the latter part
of April.
Arne Borg is at present being enter
tained by the Illinois Athletic Club.
