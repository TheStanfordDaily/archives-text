# Campus at Large Urged To Contribute to Razz Section of 1926 Quad
## 
### 
The razz editors of the 1926 Quad
have departed from a long established
custom and are soliciting contributions
from the campus at large. According
to A. Grove Day, head of the razz
board, the main object is to gain the
confidence of the student body in gen
eral in matters pertaining to scandal
and common ordinary razzing.
All desiring to contribute Should
mail their hunches or completed manu
scripts to Box 1857, Campus, or de
liver them personally to Room 110,
Toyon. The members of the board
have promised to keep all the scandal
confidential. The deadline will be Feb
ruary 10.
