# Joffre Debate Tryouts Postponed to February 12
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Joffre debate will be
held Thursday, at 2 o'clock in the after
noon at the Little theatre, according to
an announcement by Robert Littler, de
bate manager.
The general topic for the debate will
be "France and the Colored Races." The
specific topic will be announced at the
trials and the actual speaking will begin
one hour later. Speeches will be limited
to five minutes with three minutes for
rebuttal.
