FRIDAY
WOMEN'S EDUCATION CLUB meets
at 4:15 on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. Closed meeting.
SATURDAY
HAND MEMBERS meet at pavilion at
7 p.m. Coats and caps.
SUNDAY
STANFORD JOURNALISTS CLUB
meets at 10:50 a.m. at pavilion for
Quad picture.
JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB Quad pic
ture will be taken at the pavilion
at 11:30 a.m. All members be present.
SCABBARD AND BLADE picture for
Quad will be taken Sunday at 1:30
p.m. in the pavilion. Appear in uni
form.
FIRESIDE CLUB meets at 7:30 in
Congregational church, corner Ham
ilton and Waverley streets. "The
Meiklejohn Idea of a University
Education" will be the topic of dis
cussion.

WOMEN'S ECONOMIC CLUB meets
at 5 p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.

EUPHRONIA DEBATING TEAM try
outs will be held in the lobby of the
Old Union at 7:30 on the subject:

United States should be nominated
by the direct vote of the people.
