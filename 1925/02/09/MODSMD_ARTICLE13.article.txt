# Schmeider Named Captain Of Card Swimming Team
## 
### 
"Dutch" Schmeider, stellar 100 yard
breast stroke man, was elected captain of
the Cardinal varsity swimming team Fri
day afternoon by the circle "S" men of
the aquatic sports. Schmeider is one of
the best bets Stanford has in the coming
swimming meet with California.
Two other captaincies were filled when
the freshman squad elected their swim
ming and water polo leaders. Vandcn
Akker and Luster were given the two
positions. Vanden Akker swims the 220
yard free style event and Luster plays
sprint on the babe water polo team.
