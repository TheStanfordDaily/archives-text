# Roth, McDowell, Confer With Eating Club Men In Toyon Hall Tonight
## 
### 
Comptroller A. E. Roth and Alumni
Secretary J. E. McDowell will address
the members of El Toro, Breakers, and
El Campo eating clubs tonight in the
lobby of Toyon hall on the dormitory
eating situation.
According to an announcement by
Roth today, the settlement of the prob
lem will be delayed until the middle
of next quarter. Until that time, he
will gather as many facts as possible
on the situation.
Roth has already addressed Toyon
and Branner halls, and is endeavoring
to settle the problem in the most satis
factory manner to all the men con
cerned.
