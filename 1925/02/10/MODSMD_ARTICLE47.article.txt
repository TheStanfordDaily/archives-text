# Italian Operas Presented In San Francisco This Week
## 
### 
The San Carlos Opera Company,
under the direction of Fortune Gallo,
impressario and manager, is present
ing a repertoire of Italian operas in
San Francisco this week. Many noted
artists are in the company including
Alice Gentle, the famous America so
prano. Matinee performances are to
be given on Thursday and Saturday
the last day. The schedule is as fol
lows :
Tuesday, Feb. 10—La Traviata.
Wednesday, Feb. 11—Carmen.
Thursday matinee—Lohengrin.
Thursday night—Faust.
Friday, Feb. 13—Lucia di Lamer
moor.
Saturday matinee—Madame Butter
fly.
Saturday night—AWa.
