# MASQUE BALL DATE SET FOR APRIL 24 BY EX COMMITTEE
## 
### 
The Masque Ball wil\, be held April
24 under the supervision of the Rally
Committee, according to a motion
passed by the Executive Committee
last uiglit.
The complete action of the commit
tee was as follows:
M. S. C. That the election returns
submitted by the Election Board be
accepted.
M. S. C. That Tuesday, February 17,
be set as the date for the next elec
tion.
M. S. C. That the Rally Committee
be given permission to stage a masque
ball spring quarter, the purpose being
to raise funds for the committee.
M. S. C. That the following date be
granted: Masque Ball, April 24, 1925.
Closed date.
Orville Tucker submitted a financial
report of the Student Organization
Fund.
