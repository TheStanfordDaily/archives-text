# Professor W. J. Crook to Lecture on Steel Tonight
## 
### 
Professor W. J. Crook, of the Min
ing and Metallurgy department, will
lecture on "Recalescence and Decale
scence Points of Steel" tonight at 7:30
in Room 550, Mining building.
The subject matter is elementary
and will illustrate the reasons for the
great use and adaptability of the ele
ment iron in our present industrial
generation. Professor Crook is also
Consulting Metallurgist for the Pacific
Coast Steel Company.
