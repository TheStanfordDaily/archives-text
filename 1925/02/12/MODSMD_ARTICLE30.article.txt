# FENCERS COMPETE IN SAN FRANCISCO
## 
### 
Five members of Harry Maloney's
first fencing squad showed excellent
form when they crossed foils with
teams from Olympic Club, Unione
Sportive Italiana, California, and the
San Francisco De Molay in an informal
match held in San Francisco Tuesday
night.
The Cardinal fencers won nineteen
out of twenty-five matches. E. L.
Bugge and Captain Aguerrevere com
pletely shut out their opponents by
winning all their bouts. The latter won
six matches out of six, while Bugge
won four out of four.
The other men who competed were:
Wallace, winning three out of six;
and Noack, two out of three.
