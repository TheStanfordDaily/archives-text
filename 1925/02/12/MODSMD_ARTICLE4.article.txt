# CRANSTON HOLMAN, ALAN HERRINGTON RANKED NATIONALLY
## 
### 
Cranston Holman and Alan Herring
ton have been ranked No. 2 and No. 6
respectively on the National Junior
tennis ranking list which has just
been published. Holman is second only
to George Lott of Chicago, and is No.
1 on the Stanford varsity list. Recently
he won the San Francisco city title,
and is considered by many the best
collegiate player in the United States.
Herrington, who is first on the
freshman ranking, for the past two
years has been one of the outstanding
players in the state. Tilden, during his
recent trip to the coast mentioned
Herrington as one of the West's best
tennis prospects.
