# Marion Delaney to Speak Before Wranglers Club
## 
### 
Miss Marion Delaney of the Califor
nia League of Women Voters will
speak to the Wranglers Society in the
Women's Clubhouse tonight at 7:15.
In her talk, Miss Delaney will place
special emphasis on the part which
the college woman can take in campus
activities.
Beginning with tonight's meeting,
the general public is invited to attend
all Wranglers Society meetings.
