# Graduate Receives Post In Naval Department
## 
### 
From the Appointment Office comes
the announcement that W. R. Ruther
ford, '19, is to become Director of
Education in the Naval Department of
the United States government. His
headquarters will he at Washington,
D. C.
For several years Rutherford has
been Director of Education in a de
stroyer fleet at. Charleston, S. C. He
was at Stanford in 1919-20 and is a
graduate of the Department of Educa
tion.
