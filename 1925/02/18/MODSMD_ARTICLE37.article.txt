# Wife of Former English Head Dies in Palo Alto
## 
### 
Mrs. Melville Best Anderson, wife
of Melville Best Anderson, professor
of English at Stanford from 1891 until
his retirement in 1910, and mother of
the late Malcolm P. Anderson, '04, and
of Robert V. Anderson, '06, died this
morning in Palo Alto, where she has
been for some time in a sanitarium.
Her health has been poor for a num
ber of years.
