# Greenwich Village Subject Of Talk by Dr. H. D. Gray
## 
### 
"Greenwich Village" was the topic of a
talk by Dr. Henry David Gray of the
English Department, who spoke before
the Education Club last evening in the
Women's Clubhouse. Dr. Gray has vis
ited Greenwich Village several times, and
at various stages in its career. He there
fore could give a birdseye view of its his
tory, telling his own impressions.
There will be another meeting of the
club next Monday, the speaker for which
has not yet been announced.
