# Stanford Women Hold Y. W. C. A. Rally Tonight In Women's Clubhouse
## 
### 
Miss Henrietta Thompson, Foreign i
Student Secretary of the Pacific Coast,
I will talk before a meeting of Stanford
women this evening at 8:15 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
"The National Y. W. C. A." will be
the theme of the talk. Miss Thompson
is well qualified to speak on anything
collected with the Y. W. C. A., since
she is continually visiting universities I
all over the country and is in touch j
| with the latest developments in the j
j work.
This rally is an opportunity to get
lin on the work of the Y. W. C. A.
There will be songs and yells on the
| program in addition to the speeches.
