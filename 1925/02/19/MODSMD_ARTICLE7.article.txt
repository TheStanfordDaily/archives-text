# California Professor to Speak on Student Morals
## 
### 
"The Morals of the Undergraduate
and Why" will be the subject of the
Sunday evening sermon to be given
by Professor E. C. Rugh at the Pres
byterian church at 7:30 o'clock.
Professor Rugh is of the Educational
department at the University of Cali
fornia and is addressing his lecture as
an explanation of the question which
is deeply concerning the public of the
day.
Special music by a mixed quartet as
well as vocal solos will complete the
i proaram.
