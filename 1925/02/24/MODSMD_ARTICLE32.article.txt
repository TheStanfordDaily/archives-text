# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY OF
STANFORD will hold its regular bi
monthly meeting this evening at
7:30 o'clock on the second floor of
the Women's Clubhouse.
CHEMISTRY DEPARTMENT smoker
will be given tonight at 7:15 in the
Old Union. All Chemistry students
are invited to attend.
CHESS CLUB meets at 7:30 in the
Women's Clubhouse. Important.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S CLUB will
hold a meeting in the Women's Club
house at 4:30.
THE FRESHMAN CLASS will meet
in the Assembly hall at 10 o'clock.
Important business will be taken up.
M. E. SOCIETY meets at 6:15 in Theta
Xi house for a banquet in honor of
Dr. Durand, president of the A.S.M.E.
CHEMISTRY SMOKER to be held in
Old Union at 7:30. All chemistry ma
jors and sophomores interested in
chemistry invited.
THURSDAY
SCABBARD AND BLADE SOCIETY
will meet, on Thursday at 3:45 in the
Military Headquarters.
