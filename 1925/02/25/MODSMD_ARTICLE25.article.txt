# Mrs. Webster to Entertain Stanford Dames Tomorrow
## 
### 
The meeting of the Stanford Dames
will be held at the home of Mr. David
Webster, corner of Dolores and Web
ster streets, at 3:15 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon.
All wives of Stanford students are
Invited to attend.
