# RADIO CLUB ELECTS NEW TERM OFFICERS
## 
### 
Radio Club officers for the second
term were elected at a recent meeting.
The club made plans for the remod
eling of the present station in the
corporation yard. New equipment has
been ordered, and work on the im
provements will commence upon its
arrival.
The following men were elected:
President —Nelson P. Case
Vice-President—William Wells
Secretary-Treasurer—C. E. Hesthel
