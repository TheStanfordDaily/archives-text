# Colonial History Prize Given to Elsa Barber
## 
### 
The Colonial History scholarship for
1925 has been awarded to Elsa Barber
for her essay, "Colonial Military Lead
ership."
This scholarship is given yearly by
the Colonial Dames of America resi
dent in California to the undergradu
ate student who writes the best paper
on a subject relating to American
Colonial history. W. C. Harris won the
award last year for his paper on
"Medicine in Colonial America."
All undergraduates who have taken
History 21 or who have completed a
course in American Colonial history in
some other university are eligible to
enter the contest.
