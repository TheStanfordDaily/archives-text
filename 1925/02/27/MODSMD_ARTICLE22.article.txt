# Ray Spangler Will Head Stanford Journalist Club
## 
### 
New officers were elected by the
Stanford Journalists Club in a meeting
last night at the home of Professor
Everett W. Smith, 12 Alvarado Row.
Those to take office are: Ray Spangler,
president; Allene Thorpe, vice-presi
dent; Art Duncomb, secretary, and
John Levi, treasurer. Plans were made
for a lecture In the near future, by
an official of one of the metropolitan
dailies.
