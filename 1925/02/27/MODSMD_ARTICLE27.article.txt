# Stanford Alumnus Will Head Teachers College
## 
### 
H. F. Minssen has been appointed
acting president of the San Jose
Teachers College by Will C. Wood,
executive officer in control of all the
teachers colleges in the state.
Minssen received his A. B. degree at
Stanford in 1916, and in 1920 was
awarded his Master's degree in the
School of Education under Dr. Cub*
berley.
