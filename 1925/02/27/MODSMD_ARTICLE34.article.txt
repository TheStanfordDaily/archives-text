FRIDAY
USHERS report for Glee Club concert.

FIRESIDE CLUB will meet at 7:30
p.m. in the Congregational Church,

officers and athletic managers will

1:40, in the basketball pavilion.

o'clock. Please make appointment.

meeting at 7:30 in the Shack.
