# John Munholland Named Encina Club President
## 
### 
John Munholland was elected presi
dent of the Encina Club at its meeting
last night in the lobby of Encina hall.
Other officers named were Francis
Whitney, vice-president, and Gomer
Thomas, secretary-treasurer.
Members of the house committee
were also elected at the meeting.
These were Bill Price, Bill Hollings
worth, Jimmie Rae, and Clif Moore.
