# THREE APPOINTMENTS MADE ON 1926 QUAD
## 
### 
Appointment of three new members
to the staff of the 1926 Quad has been
announced by Leon B. Brown, editor.
The position of literary editor is to be
filled by Don Carlos Hines. Hines will
have charge of all copy for the book.
John Levi has been added to the
staff handling the athletic section, and
Robert Philippi, a freshman, will join
the staff as cartoonist.

A rule at Yale forbids a student to
marry during his college course on
penalty of dismissal.
