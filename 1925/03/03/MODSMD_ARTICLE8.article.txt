# NEW YORK PAPER TO START PLAY CONTEST
## 
### 
The Morning Telegraph of New York
has started a Prize Play Award Con
test, open to amateurs only. The con
test opens April 1 and closes August 7.
An advance royalty of $1000 will be
given to the writer of the best play
submitted, as determined by the Board
of Judges, seven in all, to be selected
from leading people of the literary and
stage world. The Morning Telegraph
guarantees the public production of
the play during the season of 1925-26.
This production will be made under
the auspices of The Morning Tele
graph, and will be given the benefit
of the best professional talent obtain
able.
