# MILITARY BALL WILL BE HELD ON APRIL 3
## 
### 
The Military Ball will be held on
Friday, April 3. Tickets will go on
sale Monday, March 9, for members of
the Military department only. On Tues
day they will be on open sale in the
Bookstore. The price is $4.
Art Weidner's orchestra will furnish
the music, and members of the orches
tra will put in special numbers be
tween dances.
An interior decorator from San Fran
cisco will arrange the settings.

Although fifty-two universities use a
combination containing orange for
their colors, Syracuse is the only uni
versity in the United States to use
orange alone.
