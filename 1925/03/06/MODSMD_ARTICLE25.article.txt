# Riley Breaks Hurdle Record
## 
### 
Ivan Riley of the Illinois Athletic
Club, former Kansas Agricultural Col

record in the 50-yard high hurdles at
the annual invitational meet of the
K. C. A. C. He made the distance in
6 2-5 seconds, lowering the former
world's record by 1-5 second.
