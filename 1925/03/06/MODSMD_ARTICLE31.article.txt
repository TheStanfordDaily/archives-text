# WOMEN'S CLUB OF SAN FRANCISCO TO HAVE CONCERT, DANCE
## 
### 
The Glee Club concert, under the
auspices of the Stanford Women's Club
of San Francisco, will be held in the
Ball Room of the Palace hotel at 8:30
o'clock on Tuesday evening, March 10.
Immediately following* the concert
there will be a dance, during which
an a la carte supper will be served in
the dining room adjoining. Tickets,
which are $1, are on sale at the office
of the Alumni Secretary, Crandall's
in Palo Alto, and in San Francisco at
Sherman & Clay's and at. the informa
tion desk at the Palace hotel.
Patrons and patronesses will be as
follows: Dr. and Mrs. David Starr Jor
dan, President Ray Lyman Wilbur,
Timothy Hopkins, Judge and Mrs. J. T.
Nourse, Mr. and Mrs. Richard Barrett,
Charles Field, Mr. and Mrs. Hugh
Brown, Mr. and Mrs. Wallace Alexan
der, Mr. and Mrs. Paul Shoup, Judge
and Mrs. Marcus Sloss, Mr. and Mrs.
Charles Shurtleff, Mr. and Mrs. George
I Clark, Mr. and Mrs. Frank Anderson,
Mr. and Mrs. Ira Lillick, Mr. and Mrs.
Joseph Grant, Mr. and Mrs. T. T.
Gregory, Mr. and Mrs. Milton Esberg,
Mr. and Mrs. Leland Cutler, Mr. and
Mrs. Almon Roth, Mr. and Mrs. W. H.
Fowler, Mr. and Mrs. Charles "Wil
liams, Mr. and Mrs. Newton Cleave
land, Mr. and Mrs. Warren Allen, and
| Mr. and Mrs. Orrin Elliott.
