# ANNOUNCEMENTS
##

MONDAY
CHESS CLUB electa officers at meet
ing in Clubhouse at 7:16.
ANDY KER'S CLASS in basketball
will meet as usual.
TUESDAY
ECONOMICS CLUB to meet at Phi
Kappa Sigma house for dinner at
6:30. Talk by Rollo E. Fay of Aetna
Insurance Co.
STANFORD GOLF CLUB meets at
7:15 in Trophy Room of Old Union.
QUAD MANAGERIAL TRYOUTEES
meet at 7:15 in Room 209.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY will
houd its regular bi-monthly meeting
Tuesday evening at 7:30 on the sec
ond floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
M. VI. SOCIETY meets at 7:15 p.m.
Election of officers.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S TEA will be
held in the Women's Clubhouse on
Tuesday afternoon, March 10, at
4:30. This will be the last tea of the
quarter.
EUPHRONIA DEBATING TEAM will
meet Mills College on question, "Re
solved, that the president of the Uni
ted States should be nominated by
direct vote of the people," In the
Little Theatre at 8:40 o'clock.
WEDNESDAY
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB meets tonight
at 7:30 at the Chinese Club. Pro
gram of Chinese music and lantern
slides. Cabinet meets Monday,
March 11, at 4:30 in Women's Club
house.
OPEN FORUM in lobby of Old Union
at 7:30 o'clock on question, "Should
fraternities and sororities be abol
lished from the Stanford campus?"
Dean Yost will speak.
THURSDAY
EUPHRONIA BANQUET in Union at
6 o'clock. Election of officers. Tick
ets can be obtained from W. L. Goss
lin, G. N. Crocker, H. W. Peterson,
and J. A. Leiter.
