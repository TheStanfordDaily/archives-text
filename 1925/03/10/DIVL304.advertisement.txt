The SPRING IDEA
It comprises principally,
two developments: New,
easy draping fabrics— Deau-
ville flannels, the worsted
cheviots, and Shetlands;
new, light colorings— fog
_ greys, biscuit buffs, sky
blues, London lavenders
We doubt whether you can find
anywhere so fine a representation
of spring ideas as the one we have
assembled for you Stanford.men
