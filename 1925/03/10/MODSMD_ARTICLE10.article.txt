# Dr. E. C. Franklin Awarded Medal for Research Work
## 
### 
Professor Edward C. Franklin, pro
fessor of Organic Chemistry, was pre
sented with the Nichols medal for
1925 by the New York section of the
American Chemical Society in New
York City last Friday.
Professor Franklin was awarded the
medal for his paper, "Systems of
Acids, Bases, and Salts," which was
considered by the jury as the best
chemical research paper published
during the past year. Professor Frank
lin is expected to return to the campus
before March 21.
