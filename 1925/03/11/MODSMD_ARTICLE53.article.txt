# Varsity, Freshmen Play Matches for Elimination
## 
### 
The following matches were played
yesterday in the elimination tourna
ment of the varsity and freshman ten
nis teams:
Varsity
Holman defeated Pairchild, 6-0, 6-2.
Overfelt defeated Coffin, G-2, 3-6, 6-3.
Davidson defeated Williard, 6-4, 1-6, 6-2
Freshmen
Thomas defeated McElvenny, 4-6, 6-8,
6-3.
Castellanos defeated Charles, 6-3, 6-3.
The first annual Texas Relay (James
will be held at the new Texas Memor
ial Stadium on March 27. Athletes of
all universities, colleges, normal
schools, and high schools of the South
west will be invited to attend. The
university has purchased special equip
ment for the occasion.
