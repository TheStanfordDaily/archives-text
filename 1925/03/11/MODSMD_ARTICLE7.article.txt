# Dr. Hubner, Touring West, Visits German Department
## 
### 
Dr. Hubner of the University of Bres
lrui visited the Stanford German de
partment recently. He is studying la
bor problems in the United States, and
in order to get the laborer's point of
view he has done all sorts of manual
labor, such as working in lumber
camps and mines.
Dr. Hubner is from one of the many
German universities that send men all
over the world to study political and
labor problems.
