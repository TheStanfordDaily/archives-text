# MOTORBOAT RACING LATEST DIVERSION AT LAKE LAGUNITA
## 
### 
Another sport will soon be added to
the already long list of competitive
activities which feature life on the
Stanford "farm." Motorboat racing is
the latest divertissment to be offered
to a sport-loving campus, and the ini
tial attempt at King Neptune's favor
ite relaxation is scheduled for the end
of next week.
Boats are now being constructed by
two fraternities, and sundry wagers
are said to have been made as to the
outcome. The course will be once
around the lake, and in case of acci
dent, the boat which has the most men
saved will be declared the winner.
Rooting sections, armed with cream
pit's, will gecide the issue in case of
a tie.
