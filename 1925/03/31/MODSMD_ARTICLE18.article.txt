# WAYNE SMITH NAMED AS GYM TEAM CAPTAIN
## 
### 
Wayne Smith was chosen captain
of the 1926 varsity gymnastics team
at a meeting held hy the squad imme
diately after the match with California
at the end of the winter quarter.
Smith is a member of the class of
1926 and has competed for three years
on the gym varsity. In the California
match two years ago he was second
high point man, and he placed in
several events this season.
