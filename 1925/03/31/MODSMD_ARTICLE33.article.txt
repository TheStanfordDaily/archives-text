# J. E. McDowell Speaks to San Diego Stanford Club
## 
### 
J. E. McDowell, Alumni Secretary,
was speaker and guest of honor at a
meeting of the Stanford Club of San
Diego on Friday evening, March 27.
Edgar Luce, '05, of San Diego, a mem
ber of the Stanford National Board,
also spoke.
The new officers elected are as fol
lows: Ralph Whitaker, *14, president;
Edgar Luce, '05, vice-president; J. L.
Russell, '21, secretary.
