# Study in Mexico
## 
### 
An announcement of the Patison 1925
summer tour to Mexico, including six
weeks in Mexico City during the sum
mer session of the National University,
may be obtained by addressing G. M.
Patison, Box 1181, San Antonio, Texas.
The Patison 1924 Mexico party num
bered fifty-seven students and teach
ers. Every berth in the two chartered
Pullmans used by the party was re
served several weeks before date of
departure.
