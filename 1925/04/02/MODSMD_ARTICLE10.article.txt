# Three to Represent Cards At High School Convention
## 
### 
Mrs. Elizabeth Snoll, Miss Lillian C.
Owen, and Miss Kathryn Krogh, of the
university appointment office, will repre
sent Stanford University at the annual
convention of the California High School
Principals, which will be held at the
Hotel Arlington, at Santa Barbara, April
6 to 10 inclusive.
Monday night all principals who have
attended Stanford will meet for their an
nual banquet. Last year the banquet was
attended by nearly 100 men and women,
in addition to 40 graduates living in the
community.
The annual principals' convention was
called this year by A. C. Oleny, State
Commissioner of Secondary Schools.
