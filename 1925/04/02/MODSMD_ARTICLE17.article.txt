# Thermionic Tubes Course Is Given by Dr. H. S. Read
## 
### 
Dr. H. S. Read, National Research
Fellow at Stanford, will give a two
unit course on Thermionic Tubes this
quarter. Dr. Read has a very wide
field of knowledge on the subject, hav
ing spent, three years in the research
laboratories of the Western Electric
Company.
The course will cover a study of
the theory and uses of tubes and of the
associated electric circuits. Lectures
will deal with thermionic emissions,
tube circuit characteristics, and prob
lems in using tubes.
Dr. Reed gave a course similar to
this last year at Cornell.
