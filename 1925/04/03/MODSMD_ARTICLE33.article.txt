# Encina Candy Store Adds Coffee, Snails to Menu
## 
### 
The Encina Candy Store introduced
several new services to its patrons
with the opening of this quarter. Cof
fee and snails are being served at noon
and during the late afternoon.
A greater variety of beverages hat?
been made available and more chairs
and a table have been placed in the
room. The store is located near the
west basement entrance to Encina
hall.
