# CLUB DONATES CUP FOR ATHLETE WITH HIGH SCHOLARSHIP
## 
### 
A "Scholarship Cup," to be awarded
by Skull and Snakes to the Block "S"
athlete who in his junior year has the
highest scholastic average is now on
exhibition in the Bookstore. The pre
sentation will be made annually by a
committee composed of the president
of the Skull and Snakes society and
representatives from the Registrar's
office and the office of the Graduate
Manager.
Transfers from other schools who
have completed sixty hours' credit at
Stanford will also be eligible for the
cup.
