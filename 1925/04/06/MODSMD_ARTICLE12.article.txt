# MINT HOWELL '23 DIES IN SAN JOSE

###

late Saturday night in his home in San
Jose. Three years ago lie participated in
the Stanford-California track meet
against the doctor's orders because Stan
ford needed his half point to tie the meet.
The final score, due to his efforts was
