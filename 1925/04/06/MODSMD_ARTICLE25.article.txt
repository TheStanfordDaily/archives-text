# President Wilbur Speaks At Education Celebration
## 
### 
President Wilbur spoke last Thursday
on "The Experimental Method in Edu
cation" at the College of the Pacific
in Stockton. His speech was part of
the "Dedication to Education" program
at the college which is part of a week's
celebration dedicating the buildings
and the Harriet M. Smith campus.
Will C. Wood, state superintendent of
public instruction, was also a speaker
on the program.
