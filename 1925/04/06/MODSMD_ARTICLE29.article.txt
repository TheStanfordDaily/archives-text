# Euphronia to Hold Oxford Union Meeting on April 22
## 
### 
At the last meeting of the Euphronia
Debating Society a meeting in the Ox
ford Union style is planned for Thurs
day, April 22. Every attempt will be
made to follow the regulations of the
Oxford system. The question is, "Re
solved, That the present foreign policy of
the United States is a disgrace to the
nation."
The question is to be moved by J. C.
Dunniway, who was one of the Joffre
debaters, and seconded by W. J. Nelson.
The motion will be opposed by W. D.
Cathcart, and Jack Leiter.
