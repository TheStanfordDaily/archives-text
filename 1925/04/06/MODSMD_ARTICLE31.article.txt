# ACTIVITIES SUBJECT OF THURSDAY FORUM
## 
### 
The Euphronia Society will sponsor
its most important open forum of the
year next Thursday at 7:30 in Room 12
of the Law Building, on the question of
the place student organizations and activ
ities should occupy on the Stanford
campus.
Dean Yost will be the principal speaker
of the forum. Following her talk the
Campus Organizations Committee, ap
pointed last fall will make a report on
one hundred organizations and commit
tees investigated. Several suggestions
will be offered.
This will be the first forum of the
quarter. The ones to be sponsored by
the Euphronia Society this quarter will
be similar to the series conducted last
quarter. All organization and committee
heads are urged to be present and to take
part in the meeting.
