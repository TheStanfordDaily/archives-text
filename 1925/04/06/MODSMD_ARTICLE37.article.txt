# Students, Faculty Invited To Naval Art Exhibition
## 
### 
An invitation to visit the Naval Art
exhibition that is being held this week
in the St. Francis Hotel, San Francisco,
has been extended to the students and
faculty of the University by the Cali
fornia Historical Society, which is spon
soring the exhibit.

This exhibition which is being held in
conjunction with the Citizen's Committee
for the entertainment of the American
Fleet, April 6-15, stresses the activities
of the United States Navy in the acquisi
tion of California and features naval
views in the harbor of San Francisco.

Paintings, prints, and etchings comprise
the maritime views which are on exhibit
in suite 261-262 of the Hotel St. Francis
from 10 a. m. to 10 p. m. daily.
