# French Club to Present Two Productions in May
## 
### 
Two French plays. "Petite Pluie," a
comedy in one act by E. Pailleron, and
"La Comedie de celui qui Epousa Une
Femme Muette," by Anatole France, are
being prepared for presentation early
in May.

Easter parties are being planned for
the girls at the University of Okla
homa whose first names begin with
"L."

A chess tournament is being held
between students and faculty members
at McGill University.
