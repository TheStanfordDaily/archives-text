# Junior Class Will Meet in English Building Tomorrow
## 
### 
There will be a short meeting of
the Junior Class in Room 214 of the
English building at 1 p.m. tomorrow.
According to Charles Stratton, presi
dent, there is some agitation among
the class to do away with the week
program that is usually published for
Junior Week. This, along with other
issues concerning the week-end of May
6 to 9, will be discussed tomorrow.
