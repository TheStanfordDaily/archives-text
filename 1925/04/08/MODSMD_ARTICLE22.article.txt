# PROFESSOR ECKHART HEADS OIL DIVISION
## 
### 
W. R. Eckart, professor of experi
mental engineering, has been made
chairman of the Northern California
sub-division of the Petroleum division
of the .American Society of Mechanical
Engineers. He will attend a meeting
of the chairmen of all the sub-divisions
at Thlsa, Oklahoma. April 24. The pur
pose of the meeting is to perfect the
organization of the Petroleum division.
