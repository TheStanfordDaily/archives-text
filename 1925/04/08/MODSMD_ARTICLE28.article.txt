# CABBAGES and THINGS
## 
### Steve O'Donnell 
Erroneous News item
"The first step has been taken to
ward the emancipation of women. Stu
dents at Vassar are permitted to
smoke openly on the campus.
Which inspires the following:
The Emancipated Co-ed
"I'm free," the woman student said.
"I'll spread my wings and flutter."
And then she took a chaw of Star
And spat into the gutter.
