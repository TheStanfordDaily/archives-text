# POLO ASSOCIATION FORMED TO FOSTER INTEREST IN SPORT
## 
### 
At a meeting of the polo squad in
the R. O. T. C. headquarters the Stan
ford Polo Association was formed for
the purpose of providing an organiza
tion for the friends of Stanford polo.
Captain John M. Jenkins, Jr., was
elected honorary president, Fred Trot
ter president, and Roscoe Thomas sec
retary-treasurer.
The association will include active
members who are on the polo squad,
and supporting members composed of
Stanford and peninsula residents in
terested in polo as a collegiate sport.
