# STANFORD GRADUATE WINS SCHOLARSHIP
## 
### 
Mary Katherine Chase has been
awarded one of the six fellowships
given annually in the United States by
the .Committee on Belgium Relief. Miss
Chase graduated in 1922 in Econ. Law.
She was granted an exchange scholar
ship at Toulouse the following year
and since that time she has been in
structing in the Romantic Language
department at Stanford. She is a mem
ber of Phi Beta Kappa.
