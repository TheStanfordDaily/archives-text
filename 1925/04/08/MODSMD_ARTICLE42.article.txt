# Sophomore Class Meets Tomorrow for Election
## 
### 
The sophomore class will meet in
Room 214 tomorrow at 12:45 o'clock
for the purpose of electing a class
vice-president. Eugene Cox, who was
named to that office in mid-year elec
tions, is not registered this quarter.
Charles Eckart and Ruth Jordan are
candidates for this office, botli having
failed to obtain a majority vote in the
election held last Monday.
