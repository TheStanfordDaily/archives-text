# Glee Club to Have New System of Manager Tryouts
## 
### 
A system of managerial tryouts
similar to that used in sports will be
incorporated in the new constitution
of the Stanford Glee Club, according
to an anouncement made yesterday.
The club also announced that the
spring tryouts will be held next month.
