# NORTHWEST DEANS OF MEN WILL MEET
## 
### 
Deans of Men of the universities of
the Northwest, including the states as
far east as Utah, are to hold their sec
ond annual convention on the Stan
ford campus between the dates of
April 1G and 18.
Twenty universities will be repre
sented. and the purpose is to discuss
the problems of the colleges and to in
terchange ideas which may be benefi
cial to all.
Dean Culver will present a paper on
"Stanford's Fraternity System." Presi
dent Wilbur and Professor Terman are
also to give talks. The men will have
a barbecue at Searsville lake and be
entertained at Dean Culver's house
one evening.
