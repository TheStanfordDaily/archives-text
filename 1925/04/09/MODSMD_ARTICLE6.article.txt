# HALL SMOKER DRAWS FIVE HUNDRED MEN
## 
### 
Five hundred dormitory men attend
ed the smoker of the Inter-hall Asso
ciation, which was held last night in
the Encina lobby in the initial meeting
of the new hall federation.
Professor E. M. Hulme gave a short
talk in which he stressed the obliga
tion of the hall men to assist athletes
to keep up their scholastic standing.
Professor Hulme also advised the as
sociation members to develop a taste
for art and music while still at college
in order that they may enjoy their
leisure time in later years.
