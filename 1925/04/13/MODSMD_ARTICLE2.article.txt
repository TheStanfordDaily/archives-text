# THREE-DAY CHARITY DRIVE BEGINS TODAY
## 
### 
Canvassing for subscriptions to the
spring quarter Charity Drive began this
morning and will continue today, Tues
day, and Wednesday. Funds are being
solicited, by recommendation of the
Social Service Board, for a twofold
cause: the Students' Friendship Fund
and the Stanford Convalescent Home.

committee. Others who are aiding in
