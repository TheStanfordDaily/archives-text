# Far East Relations to Be Subject at Open Meeting
## 
### 
Professor Yamato Ichihashi, of the
History department, will speak on
"Far Eastern Relations to the League"
at an open meeting of the Stanford
Branch of the League of Nations Non
partisan Association, which will be
held tonight at 7:30 in the Little
Theatre.
The constitution for the branch will
be presented by the committee, and
work of the association for the quar
ter will be. announced.
The immediate purpose of the asso
ciation is to secure the entrance of
the United States into the League of
Nations and the World Court, through
educational means. All interested in
the support of the league are invited
to attend this meeting.
