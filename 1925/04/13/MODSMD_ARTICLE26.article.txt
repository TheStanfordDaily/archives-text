# Freshmen to Hold Meeting At Encina Hall Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Freshman Class will
hold a meeting tonight in the lobby of
Encina at 6:45 to discuss the purchase
of sophomore hats next fall. Several
other items of business, notably plans
for a picnic in the near future, will be
brought up.
Following the meeting, members of
the dance committee will meet at Roble
hall at 7:30.
After the meeting Dr. Jordan will ad
dress the freshmen on the topic, "Short
Cuts to Happiness."

Yale University is to have a School
of Fine Arts fully equipped to study
all phases of the drama.
