# CARD PUGILISTS MAY MEET CALIFORNIANS
## 
### 
The Stanford boxing team will meet
California next week at Berkeley, if ne
gotiations now being made are com
pleted.
Dr. Barrow, director of the Depart
ment of Physical Education, has drawn
up a plan to govern future boxing meets
with the California team. The details of
the proposal have not been divulged at
present, but the associated students at
Berkeley will vote on the suggestions
Wednesday. If the plan is approved, the
boxing match will probably be scheduled
on either April 23 or 24.
