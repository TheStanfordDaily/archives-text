# Chapter Officers Elected By Palo Alto De Molays
## 
### 
N. P. Case was elected Master Coun
cilor of the Palo Alto chapter of De
Molay at the quarterly election held
in the Masonic Temple last night.
The following were also elected: F.
A. Senn, Senior Councilor; D. P. Krotz,
Junior Councilor; R. C. Powell, Senior
Deacon; H. A. Neff, Junior Deacon;
F. W. Gowell, Senior Steward; E. W.
Hamilton, Marshall; R. H. Mosher,
Almoner; L. F. Harms, Scribe; J. Y.
Springer, Chaplain.
