# STUDENTS MUST NOW PAY TWO CENTS TO SEND POSTAL CARDS
## 
### 
* The increase in postal rates which
was recently adopted by Congress goes
into effect tomorrow. The main in
crease is that affecting postcards,
which will now be sent for two cents
instead of one cent as before.
Heretofore no fee has been exacted
for return receipts for registered mail.
Under the new rates a fee of 3 cents
will be charged. A fee of 3 cents also
will be charged for return receipts on
parcel post insured packages.
