# RYFKOGEL NAMED ON HEALTH COMMITTEE
## 
### 
Dr. H. A. L. Ryfkogel, San Fran
cisco physician and member of the
Stanford medical school faculty has
been named to represent San Fran
cisco county in a national program of
the Gorgas Memorial Institute for the
prevention of disease and prolongation
of life. Efforts will be made to in
crease the average life from fifty-eight
to seventy years.
The Gorgas Institute was formed in
memory of the work done by General
W. C. Gorgas in the World War and
in the Panama canal district. Its pur
pose is to carry on the work of pre
ventative measures such as he prac
ticed during his connection with the
army.
