# "ALUMNI HOP IN" TO BE SLOGAN OF CARS ON UNIVERSITY DAY
## 
### 
J. E. McDowell, alumni secretary,
has appointed a committee to print
and distribute windshield posters for
University Day bearing the slogan,
"Alumni Hop In."

According to C. E. Beardsley, these
stickers should make the "old grads"
feel that the students welcome them,
and wish to do their utmost to make
University Day a success.
