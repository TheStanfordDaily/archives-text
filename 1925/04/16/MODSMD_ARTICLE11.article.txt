# HOLMAN, CHANDLER, JUNIOR NET ACES, TO MEET SATURDAY
## 
### 
Cranston W. Holman, Stanford ten
nis ace, will meet Captain Chandler
of California on the varsity tennis
courts at 10 o'clock Saturday in what
promises to be one of the best inter
collegiate matches of the year. This
match is one of the most important in
the annual tournament between the
two institutions, and many believe that
the winner will take the national col
legiate title this year. .
Chandler was national junior runner
up the previous year. As ranking man
on the California varsity, the match
with Holman in the annual tourna
ment between California and Stanford
promises to be of more than usual
interest.
