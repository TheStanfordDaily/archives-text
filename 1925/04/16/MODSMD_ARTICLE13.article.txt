# Nominations For King, Queen Of Masque Ball Open Today
## 
### 
Nominations for the King and Queen of the 1925 Masque Ball open tonight.
Voting will start next week, the contest ending at 3 p.m., Friday, April 24.
Nomination blanks are printed in tonight's paper, and should be mailed to
the Rally Committee before 9 p.m., Saturday. A signed nomination will
entitle the nominee to 1,000 votes. No one nominated will be allowed to
resign.
E. E. Wells states that the contest will be conducted with the utmost
honesty. Votes will be sold daily next week between the hours of 9 a.m. and
3 p.m. in front of the Romanic Languages Building. All ballots will be printed
and numbered, and will be carefully checked after they are dispensed. Votes
will be sold in denominations of 10, 100, and 1,000, at one cent a vote.
