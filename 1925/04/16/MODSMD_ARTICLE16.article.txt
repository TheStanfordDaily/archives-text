# STUDENT GEOLOGIST LEAVES FOR MEXICO
## 
### 
Eric Jordan, son of Dr. David Starr
Jordan and a major in the Geology
department, left the campus on a
scientific expedition to Mexico, which
is being sent out by the California
Academy of Sciences in San Francisco.
The members of the expedition will
explore the islapds of the western
coast off Lower California and Mex
ico, and will be gone two months. The
geological division of the work will be
under the leadership of G. Dallas
Hanna. Zoology, botany, and other
sciences will also have part in the ex
ploration and discovery.
