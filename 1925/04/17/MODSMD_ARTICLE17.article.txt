# LAW ASSOCIATION TO HOLD ANNUAL DINNER
## 
### 
The annual dinner of the Stanford Law
Association will be held tomorrow night
in the Union at 6 o'clock. According to
Dean Kirkwood of the law school, at
least 100 members of the association
will be present.
The speakers will be the president of
the association, L. H. Cromwell, Dean
Kirkwood, Professor McCaskel of the
Cornell Law School, and Judge W. A.
Bcasley, former Superior Court judge
in Santa Clara County.
