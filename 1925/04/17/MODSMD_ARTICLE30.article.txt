# Women Formally Organize Stanford Debating Group
## 
### 
Tryouts for the open forum debate
between the newly-formed Women's
Debating Society and the men will be
held Monday, April 27. The question
for the debate, will be, Resolved; that
the women should bear half the ex
penses on a "date." The debate will be
held May 6.
The Women's Debating Society was
formally organized at a meeting held
early in the week. Kathleen Wood
ward was elected president of the or
ganization and a constitutional com
mittee appointed. Dean Mary Yost and
Miss Elizabeth L. Buckingham were
elected honorary members.
