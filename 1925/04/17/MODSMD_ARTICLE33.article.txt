# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
A MEETING of all those who are to
police or usher at the track meet
Saturday will be held at 3:45 p.m. in
Room 214 of the Economics building.
ALL USHERS for the California Glee
Club concert please report at the
Assembly hall at 7:30.
THE GERMAN CLUB will meet this
evening at 7:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse.
THE WOMEN'S EDUCATION CLUB
meets on the third floor of the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 4:15. Important
business meeting.
SOCIAL MEETING will be held to
night at 8 o'clock in the Presbyterian
Church In Palo Alto to which all
young people are invited.
SUNDAY
THE CHRISTIAN ENDEAVOR of the
Presbyterian Church meets for light
supper at 6 pm. Discussion on the
question, "Do You Believe in Evan
gelism," will follow the supper.
THE MASONIC CLUB DEGREE team
meets at Redwood City Lodge, at
9:30 a.m.
THE CONGREGATIONAL DISCUS
SION CLUB will hold its regular
meeting at the Congregational
church at 7:30 p.m.
MONDAY
ALL SENIORS OR HOLDOVERS
wishing to play on the senior class
soccer team report to soccer field,
Monday, 4:15 p.m.
TUESDAY
M. E. SOCIETY meets at 7:15. Pro
fessor Tickell will speak on "The
Transportation of Oil."
