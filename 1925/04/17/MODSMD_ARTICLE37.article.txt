# Dean G. B. Culver Acting as Host to Visiting Officials
## 
### 
Dean Culver is acting as host to a
convention of deans of men being held
on the campus from April 16 to 18.
Last night a dinner was held at which
the deans, representing colleges all
along the Pacific coast, were enter
tained and welcomed to Stanford by
President Wilbur.
They were guests at luncheon today
at the home of Dean Culver, following
which a session was held at which
Dr. Lewis M. Terman of the Depart
ment of Psychology was the chief
speaker. This afternoon they are being
shown the various points of interest
on the campus and neighborhood.
