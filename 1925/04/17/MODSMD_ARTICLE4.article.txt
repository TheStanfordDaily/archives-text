# English Literature Study Published by Dr. H. D. Gray
## 
### 
Dr. Gray of the English department
has published a book entitled "English
Literature Since 189(1." The book is a
survey of modern English literature and
is divided into three sections, the poetry,
the drama, and the novel. Several noted
men have contributed to the book.
Among them are Brander Matthews,
Hamilton Mahie, and Albert S. Cooke.
