# W. W. ELLSWORTH TO TALK OF OLD LONDON
## 
### 
"Shakespeare and Old London" is to be
the subject of a lecture delivered by Wil
liam Webster Ellsworth in the Assembly
Hall tomorrow evening at 7 :30.
Ellsworth, former president of The
Century Company, has lectured at Stan
ford before. He is the author of "A
Golden Age of Authors" and many lit
erary and historical works.
The lecture tomorrow night will repro
duce the London of Sliakespeare's time by
means of pictures in color from the model
of the city in the London Museum. In
cluded will be portraits, prints, water
color drawings by famous artists, plans
of the old theaters, and interesting docu
ments.
