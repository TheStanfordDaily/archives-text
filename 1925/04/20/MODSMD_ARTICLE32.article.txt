# Industrial Revolution Is Lecture Topic by Hulme
## 
### 
Professor Hulme, of the History de
partment, will lecture in the Paul Elder
gallery in San Francisco tomorrow at
2:30 o'clock on "The Industrial Revolu
tion in England."
Professor Hulme has been giving a
series of six lectures at the Elder Gallery
of which this is the fifth.

Three O. A. C. students are making
a 10,000 mile tour of the U. S.
