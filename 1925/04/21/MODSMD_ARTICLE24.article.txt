# Professor Findlay Will Talk at Community Lunch
## 
### 
"Science and Industry" will be the
subject of an address by Professor
Alexander Findlay at the Community
luncheon at 12:10 o'clock tomorrow at
the Russell Cafeteria.
Professor Findlay, who is visiting at
Stanford, is a professor of chemistry
at Aberdeen University, and an au
thority on this subject.
Professor R. E. Swain will preside.
The luncheon will be 60 cents a plate.
