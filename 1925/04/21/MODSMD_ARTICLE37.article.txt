# Flint Donates Loving Cup for Card Fencers
## 
### 
J. Flint, a former member of the fenc
ing team, and a lightweight boxer, has
donated a loving cup for the fencing
championship of Stanford.
Under the conditions which he has at
tached to the award, the trophy may be
competed for only once a year and will
be emblematic of the University fencing
championship. The cup will be the per
manent possession of the man who first
wins it two years in succession.
