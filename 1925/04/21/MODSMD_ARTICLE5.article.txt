# LIBRARY EXHIBITS EARTHQUAKE SCENES
## 
### 
Photographs and magazine illustra
tions showing Stanford before and
after the earthquake, April 18. 1906,
are now on display in the show cases
of the library.
Views of the Memorial Church, the
library, and the men's gymnasium are
shown. The special edition of the Daily
Palo Alto on April 18, 1906, with the
story of the catastrophe, occupies a
prominent position in the exhibit. Is
sues of the Stanford Sequoia show pic
tures of the rebuilding process.
