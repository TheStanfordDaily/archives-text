The women of the University
are desirous that hiddinp; on Fri
day night be accomplished in as
inconspicuous and quite a way
as possible. Confident that the
men will help to cary out this
wish for privacy, Women's Coun
cil and Panhellenic are asking
that no demonstration of any
kind be made.
CAROLAN STROUSE,
President Associated Women.
KATHRYN McCAHAN,
President Panhellenic.
