# Marion Gower-Beardsley Announce Engagement
## 
### 
The engagement of Marion R. Gower,
'24, to Charles E. Beardsley, '25, was
announced last night at the Delta
Delta Delta house and at El Campo.
While at Stanford Miss Gower was
prominent in student activities, hold
ing the position of Women's Manager
of the Quad. She was also a member
of Phi Beta Kappa. Beardsley is Presi
dent of the Associated Students and a
member of El Campo, Phi Alpha Delta,
legal fraternity, and of the Quadrangle
Club.
