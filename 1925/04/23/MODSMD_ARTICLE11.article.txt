# Civil Engineers Fund Pays $300 Annually
## 
### 
Student initiative in the Civil Engi
neering department, started several
years ago, has borne fruit in the estab
lishment. of a scholarship bearing an
annual stipend of $300, it was an
nounced today. A check for $6,075.02
has been delivered to the University,
the income from which will be used
for this purpose.
Students in the Civil Engineering
department collected approximately
$3,000 of this sum. Other alumni of
the Civil Engineering department
made stadium subscriptions of vary
ing sums, with the provision that these
should be made available for this
scholarship fund.
