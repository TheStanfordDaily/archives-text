# Sled Added to Collection Of Crafts on Lagunita
## 
### 
Lagunita's fleet has been further in
creased by the addition of a motor
boat of the sea-sled type. The boat has
been in the process of construction
for the last two weeks, Tim Colvin of
the Fijis acting as construction engi
neer and head mechanic for the craft
An examination of the turbulent
Henry, which the builders allege is
to cause the boat to oscillate and ad
vance in Lagunita, solves the secret
of the mysterious sound of airplane
motors that' has harassed the campus
of late.

Queeners and snakes note! After the
show it's great to cross the street and
enjoy a sandwich and malt at the
Altomont Creamery (across from the
Varsity).—Adv.
