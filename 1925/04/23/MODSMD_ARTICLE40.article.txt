# Professor Ichihashi to Discuss Japanese Methods
## 
### 
"Historical Methods in Japanese
Universities" will be the subject of a
talk by Professor Yamato Ichihashi to
be given at the History Club meeting
which will be held at 7:45 tonight in
the Women's Clubhouse.
This will be the first meeting of the
quarter. The officers elected for this
quarter are: L. H. Crawford, presilent;
Esther Caukin, vice-president; D. W.
Harris, secretary; Mary Tingley, treas
urer. Plans for the annual picnic of
the organization will be made tonight.
Men at the University of Oregon
are not allowed to queen to any offi
cial athletic contest or rally.
