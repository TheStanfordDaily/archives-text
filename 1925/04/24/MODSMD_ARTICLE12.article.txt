# Fund Established to Aid Students by Women's Club
## 
### 
The East Bay Stanford Women's
Cluh has established a loan fund for
needy and worthy students from the
East Bay district. No interest will at
tach to tho loans, repayment of which
will not be called for until two or
three years after graduation. The
fund contains now $100. Mrs. Harold
Knowles of Berkeley is secretary of
the club.
