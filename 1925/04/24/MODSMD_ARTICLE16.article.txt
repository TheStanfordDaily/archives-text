# Member of Prince School To Talk on Store Service
## 
### 
Mrs. Lucretia Prince of the Prince
School for Store Service, Boston,
Mass., will be at Stanford Tuesday,
April 28. She will give a talk to those
interested in store service at an hour
to be announced later.
Those who desire personal confer
ences with her may make arrange
ments with Mrs. Elizabeth Snell of
the Stanford Appointment office.
