# F. E. Morgan Departs for Ranch in Upper California
## 
### 
F. E. Morgan, retiring secretary of
the Stanford Y. M. C. A., left this
morning for northern California, ac
companied by his wife and his father,
Mr. H. J. Morgan.
Mr. Morgan expects to spend the
rest of the spring on his ranch, Monte
Vista, thirty miles north of Sacra
ment, and this summer he and his
wife are planning an extended auto
mobile and fishing trip through Oregon
and over to Yellowstone Park.
