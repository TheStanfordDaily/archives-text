# Order of Coif Announces Election of Three Men
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the Order
of the Coif, national legal scholarship
fraternity, election to which is limited
to the highest 10 per cent of the
graduating clafs, announces election
of Arthur H. Kent, Palo Alto; Lewis
Hayd Leve, Palo Alto, and Bertram
W. Levit, San Francisco. Dean M.
Kirkwood of the Stanford Law School
is at present president of the national
order.
