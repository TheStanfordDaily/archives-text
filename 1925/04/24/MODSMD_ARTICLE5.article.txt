# Professor Strong Made Advertising Club Head
## 
### 
Professor F. K. Strong of the Psy
chology department was elected presi
dent. of the Stanford Advertising Club
of Palo Alto at the annual election of
officers which was held at the Cardi
nal hotel last night.
Ed Zwierlein was named vice-presi
dent, J. Paul Moore was elected secre
tary. G. A. Montell, the retiring presi
dent, will represent the club at the
Pacific Coast Advertising Clubs As
sociation's convention. The newly
elected officers will be installed at the
first meeting in May. Lloyd Spencer,
president of the association, will visit
the club next Thursday night.
