# Dr. W. A. Cannon to Make Botanical Studies Here
## 
### 
Dr.' William Austin Cannon, research
associate of the Carnegie Institute, who
is now stationed on the Stanford campus,
is preparing to take up extensive botani
cal study here. Dr. Cannon is a gradu
ate of Stanford, and of Columbia, and
for many years has been connected with
the Carnegie Institute. He has been
stationed at Tucson, Arizona, and later
at Carmel, California.
