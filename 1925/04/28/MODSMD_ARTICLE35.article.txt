# Salinas Alumni Meeting Held to Elect Officers
## 
### 
Stanford alumni from Monterey, the
Peninsula, and Salinas recently met at
the Caminos hotel. The speaker of the
evening was Comptroller Roth, who gave
a talk on Stanford conditions. He ex
plained the changes in the faculty, the
importance of the special departments,
the research work and its growing in
dustrial importance to California and
national business, and the physical im
provements on the campus.
In the election of officers for the asso
ciation, Jorgensen was re-elected presi
dent; Ralph Huges, '10, vice-president;
and Winifred Tuttle, ex-'IS, of Pacific
Grove, re-elected secretary-treasurer.
