# High School Principal to Address Education Club
## 
### 
Walter H. Nichols, president of the
Palo Alto Union high school, will
speak at an open meeting of the Wo
men's Education Club on Friday aft
ernoon at 4:15. His subject will be
"What a principal expects of his
teachers."
Nichols has taught in summer ses
sions of the Education department at
Stanford. About a year ago his book,
entitled "Trust a Boy," was published,
and recently "Measure of a Boy" ap
peared. He is a member of the Phi
Delta Kappa, an educational fraternity.
