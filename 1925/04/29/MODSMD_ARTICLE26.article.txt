# HAYSTACKS BURN AS FRESHMEN YELL AT UNIVERSITY FIREMEN
## 
### 
A real fire in two of the haystacks j
on the lot west of Encina called out'
the fire department at 11 o'clock last
night. The majority of the crowd 1
j watched the valiant workers from the j
j windows of the building, but one or
two of the more hardy ventured to go
out on the field to give the firemen
(advice as to the theory of fire-fighting.
For a while there was fear that the
! second stack would have burned itself
out before the men had extinguished
j the first, but when the fighters finally
I decided to let the fire take its own
time, and ceased hindering its work,
the onlookers were able once more
: to really enjoy the blaze.
