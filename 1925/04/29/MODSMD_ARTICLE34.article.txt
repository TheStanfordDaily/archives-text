WEDNESDAY
BUSINESS STAFF OF D. P. A. meets
at 7:15 p.m. sharp.
BAHAI MEETING in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:15. Subject: "The

the Women's Clubhouse.

Women's Clubhouse.

the Old Union.

WOMEN'S EDUCATION CLUB meets
at 4:15 in the Women's Clubhouse.
Open meeting.
SUNDAY
CHRISTIAN ENDEAVOR meets at the

light supper and discussion. Topic:
"How the Minor Prophets Help Us."
