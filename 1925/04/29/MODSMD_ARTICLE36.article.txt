# Civil Engineering Smoker Will Take Place Thursday
## 
### 
All freshmen and sophomores inter
ested in engineering are invited to
attend the smoker to be given by the
Civil Engineering Society Thursday
night at 7:15 in the Old Union. The
principal speaker of the evening will
be J. D. Galloway, consulting engineer
in San Francisco for the Pacific Gas
& Electric Company and for the Great
Western Power Company. A short talk
will also be given by Professor C. D.
Marx. Elizalde's orchestra will furnish
music for the occasion.
