# QUEENERS DISCOVER TANDEM CYCLING AS CO-OPERATIVE DATE
## 
### 
With all the exhilaration of flying
through the air, all the thrill of physical
exertion, all the pleasure of companion
ship and cooperation, a new sport may
become the queeners' delight.
Down the Row they came, hair flying,
garments fluttering in the wind, both
pumping as hard as they could, she sit
ting in front, he behind her—on a tandem
bicycle! Around the corner they sped at
breakneck speed and vanished.

expenses should note this new way of
queening where the woman can at least
do her share of the work.
