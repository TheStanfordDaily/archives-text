# SENIORS WIN FROM 1927 TENNIS TEAM IN CLASS MATCHES
## 
### 
The seniors and freshmen are now
tied for first in the intramural tennis
matches as a result of the seniors'
"25 to 10 win over the sophomores yes
terday afternoon. The two teams will
meet to decide the championship next
Monday.
The two best matches in yesterday's
play came in the first doubles and
singles. McCray of the sophomores de
feated Pike, senior star player, in a
close three set contest, 6-4, 4-6, 6-3.
In the doubles match Leeds and David-
won from Osthaus and McCray,
6-3, 0-6, 6-1. The seniors won five of
the seven matches played.
Complete results follow:
McCrav (Soph) defeated Pike (Sr),
6-4, 4-6, 6-3.
Davidson (Sr) defeated Todd (Soph)
6-0, 6-4.
Leeds (Sr) defeated Smith (Soph),
6-4, 6-4.
Leeds and Davidson (Sr) defeated
McCray and Osthaus (Soph), 6-3, 0-6,
6-1.
Willis and Millikin (Sr) defeated
Smith and Todd (Soph), 6-2, 6-4.
Bledsoe and Cramer (Sr) defeated
Menzies and Roberts (Soph), 6-4, 8-6.
Hofflund and Israel (Soph) defeated
Selenger and Israel (Sr), 6-3, 4-6, 8-6.
Tomorrow the juniors and the sopho
mores battle for third place in the
class tennis standing. The sophomores
are favored to win by a safe margin,
but the juniors may pull out of last
position by coming through with a
win over their opponents. The juniors
have failed to win a match so far this
year, while the sophomores have given
both of their opponents a close run.
