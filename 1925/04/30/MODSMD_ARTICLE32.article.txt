# Sequoia Dance Programs Are Available Tonight
## 
### 
Sequoia Hall will give its spring
informal tomorrow night and programs
for the affair will be available from
any member of the dance committee
tonight.
Bob Beale's orchestra of Berkeley
will supply the music for the hop
which is to be a one o'clock dance.
A mountain country club will be repre
sented by the decorative scheme
which has been planned by L. L. Hunt,
chairman of the decorations commit
tee.
