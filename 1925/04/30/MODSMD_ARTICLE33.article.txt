# Stanford Ad Club Hears Branch President Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Advertising Club of
Palo Alto will hold its regular dinner
and meeting tonight at the Cardinal
Hotel in Palo Alto. Dinner will be
served at G: 15 and will be followed
by the meeting which will commence
at 6:45.
The program will consist of an ad
dress by Lloyd Spencer, advertising
manager of the Seattle Post-Intelli
gencer and president of the Pacific
Coast branch of the National Advertis
ing Clubs. All students interested in
journalism or advertising are welcome
to attend the meeting.
Classes in the afternoon have been
resumed at Princeton, after a period
of twenty years in which no afternoon
classes have been held.
