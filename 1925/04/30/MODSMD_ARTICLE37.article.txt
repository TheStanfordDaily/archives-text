# Student Presidents Hold Conclave on Campus Soon
## 
### 
The annual convention of the Pacific
Student Presidents Association will be
held this year at Stanford in the latter
part of May, according to an an
nouncement by C. E. Beardsley, Presi
dent of the Associated Students of
Stanford.
The convention will last for a period
of from three to four days. Presidents
and presjdents-elect of colleges in the
association attend as representatives
of their schools. Problems of student
government and systems used in dif
ferent schools will be discussed.
