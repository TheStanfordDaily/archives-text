# LAST PAYMENT DUE ON BOARD AT ENCINA
## 
### 
According to an anonuncement from
the custodian's office at Encina hall,
the second and last installment of $35
in the board bill of all residents who
have not already paid in full, is due
not later than next Tuesday. Late
payments will be penalized 25 cents a
day, unless an extension of time is
secured from the office.
