# Advertising Clubs Asked To Aid College Students
## 
### 
"Advertising clubs should extend a
helping hand to the students of our
colleges and help them to get a good
start in business life," declared Lloyd
Spencer, advertising manager of the
Seattle Post-Intelligencer, at the Stan
ford Advertising Club meeting held in
the Cardinal hotel last night.
"To make the printed word believed
by the reading public is the principle
object of organized advertising," he
said. Spencer discussed the work of
the Pacific Coast Advertising Clubs
Association of which he is president.
Miss Florence Gardner, executive sec
retary of the San Francisco club,
pledged the co-operation of that or
ganization. The newly elected officers
of the club will be installed at the
May 8 meeting.
