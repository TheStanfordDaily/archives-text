# Y. W. C. A. Plans Barbecue At Lagunita Wednesday
## 
### 
A campfire and barbecue will be
held on the shore of Lagunita at six
o'clock Wednesday, under the auspices
of the Y. W. C. A. The purpose of this
get-together is to arouse interest in
the Asilomar Y. W. C. A. conference
which will take place this June.
