# CABBAGES and THINGS
## 
### Steve O'Donnell 
THIS OUGHT TO MAKE IT
CLEAR
"The Committee on Scholarship, in
order to prevent misunderstandings re
garding deficiencies, honors, and scholar
ship awards has issued a twenty-page
pamphlet explaining these subjects in de
tail."
Only the worst kind of a rum-dum
could misunderstand the regulations after
they had been reduced to a trifling matter
of twenty pages.
♦ * *
A demon statistician reports, "The
graduates of Yale University, if gath
ered together, would equal a city of
40,000."
It would surely be tough if, after all
this figuring, the graduates would not
gather together and thus upset the
figures.
♦ * *
"This year's rushing rules for men will
be explained in the lobby of Encina Hall
at 6 :45 Monday evening."
It is expected that Tuesday eight o'clock
classes will be delayed long enough to
allow the '28 men to comprehend the sys
tem fully.
