# PROFESSOR COESTER WILL STUDY ABROAD
## 
### 
Professor Alfred Cocster of the Span
ish department will be on leave during
the next year. He will spend some time
in the libraries of New York, doing re
search work. From there he plans on
going to South America, where he will
do research work in the field of Span
ish American literature.
A Spanish translation of the History
of Spanish-American Literature, which
Professor Cocster wrote several years
ago, has just appeared in Madrid.
