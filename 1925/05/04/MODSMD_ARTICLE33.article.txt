# PROFESSOR ECKART ELECTED CHAIRMAN
## 
### 
Professor W. R. Eckart of the Me
chanical Engineering department was
elected national chairman of the Petro
leum division of the American Society
of Mechanical Engineers at a conference
held at Tulsa, Oklahoma, April 24.
The convention was attended by the
presidents and secretaries of the branch
sections throughout the country. Pro
fessor Eckart went as the chairman of
the San Francisco section of the Petro
leum division. He returned to the
campus last Friday.
