# W. M. Proctor Has Volume Accepted for Publication
## 
### 
"Education and Vocational Guidance,"
by Professor W. W. Proctor of the
Education department has been accepted
by Houghton-Mifflin for publication.
The book is for the use of high school
teachers in helping students determine
what they are best fitted for. It will
also be used as a text, as thirty uni
versities are now giving courses in vo
cational guidance for education majors.
