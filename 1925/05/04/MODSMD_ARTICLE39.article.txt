# Fencers Give Exhibition Saturday at Sacramento
## 
### 
The Stanford fencing team, under
the leadership of Harry Maloney, gave
instruction to the Sacramento Fencing
Club Friday and Saturday at Sacra
mentp.
Maloney and Bugge gave an exhibi
tion bout with H. F. Warner as judge
before an audience of 2,000 at the
Senator Theatre Saturday afternoon.
Both class and individual coaching
was given by R. E. Anderson, T. B.
Criley, S. E. Aguerrevere, G. M. Zucco,
H. F. Warner, Bugge, and Maloney.
While in Sacramento the fencers
were the guests of the fencing club,
J. H. Lubin, and the West Coast Thea
tre Corporation.
