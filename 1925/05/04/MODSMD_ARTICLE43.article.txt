# Chamber of Commerce to Banquet Foreign Students
## 
### 
The Chamber of Commerce of San
Francisco will hold the annual dinner for
foreign students of Stanford and Cali
fornia on Wednesday evening at the Com
mercial Club of San Francisco. Charles
McCallister, Stanford yell leader, and
George Gaw, California yell leader, will
be present at the dinner.

The Oregon Agricultural College de
bate team lost but one debate on their
recent tour of the East.
