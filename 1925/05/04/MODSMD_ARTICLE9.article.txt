# MILLS ALUMNAE TO HEAR DR. H. D. GRAY
## 
### 
"The Attic Spirit in the Modern
Age" is the title of the lecture to be
given by Professor H. D. Gray of the
English department, Friday, May 8,
before the alumnae of Mills College.
Dr. Gray will be the guest of honor
at the banquet, which is to be given
at the Oakland hotel preceding his ad
dress.
