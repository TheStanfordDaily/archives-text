# Y. W. C. A. CAMPFIRE RALLY WILL BE HELD AT LAKE TOMORROW
## 
### 
A campfire rally at Lagunita will be
hold by the Y. W. C. A. at 6 o'clock to
morrow. The program will include
talks about the Asilomar conference
whit his to be held in June.

Miss A lit e Hoyt, National Secretary
of the Y. W. C. A., will explain the
conference work, and Miss Bull, a
student of the University of California,
will also speak. A one-act Russian play
will be given, the cast consisting of
Pauline Hoffman, Ethelind Thomson,
Edith Mattsen, Winifred Huntington,
and Virginia Hyde.

Esther Greenacre, president of the
Y. W. C. A., will preside. Committees
in charge of the arrangements are:
Marian Wood, food; Dorothy Hack and
Lillian Force, entertainment; Eleanor
Davidson, general chairman.
