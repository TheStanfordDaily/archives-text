# INTERIM TENNIS TITLE WON BY 1925; 1928 TAKES SECOND
## Seniors Win From Freshmen In Interclass Tennis Finals Yesterday 
### Kenneth Dow 
Showing decided superiority in both
singles and doubles the senior inter
class tennis team won from the fresh
men yesterday afternoon in the final
match to determine the championship.
The seniors won all of the seven
matches played.
In the first singles Davidson (S) de
feated Dunn (F) 6-1, 6-4. The winner
proved too steady for his erratic op
ponent. Pike (S) won from Charles
(F) in easy fashion in the second set
after a close game in the first set.
In third singles Willis (S) won over
Mansfield (F) 6-1, 6-3.
Freshmen Take Sets
The only matches in which the
freshmen took sets were in the dou
bles where Breeden and Duval (F)
ran Willis and Millikin (S) to a three
close set contest, 6-4, 4-6, 6-2. Dow and
Way (F) took the first set of their
match against Bledsoe and Cramer
(S) at S-6, only to be defeated in the
next two, 6-1, 6-2.
