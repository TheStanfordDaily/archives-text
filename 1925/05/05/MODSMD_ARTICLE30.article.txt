# M. E. SOCIETY WILL SPONSOR LECTURE
## 
### 
"Accident Prevention" will be the sub
ject of the lecture to be given by E. G.
Sheibley tonight before the student
chapter of the American Society of
Mechanical Engineers. The meeting will
be held at 7:15 in room 550 of the Min
ing and Metallurgy building.
Mr. Sheibley, who graduated from
Stanford in 1908, is chief engineer and
superintendent of the State Industrial
Accident Commission. . Any one inter
ested is invited.
