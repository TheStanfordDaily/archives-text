# Miss Ida Stauf Plans Trip Around the World
## 
### 
At the end of spring quarter Miss Ida
Stauf of the Romance Language depart
ment will leave the University for an
extended trip around the world.
Miss Stauf will visit Japan by way of
Honolulu and will make a study of the
schools in that country. In France she
intends to do research work for four
months. In England she will transact
business and visit friends. Other stops
include Italy, Spain, China, and South
America.
Miss Stauf intends to be gone a year.

ning a new ehapel to have a seating

the university by John D. Rockefeller
providing $1,500,000 for the chapel.
