# CORDUROY BALL TO RE HELD THURSDAY
## 
### 
Three hours of dancing will be pre
pared for the chosen few who were able
to secure tickets to the Corduroy Ball
which will be held Thursday afternoon.
From 3 to 6 King Jazz will hold sway in
the Woman's Club House.

Whitmer's orchestra will furnish music
for the event. R. K. Hubbard, who is in
charge of the Ball, has arranged light
refreshments to be served during the
dance. The "rough's" will be excluded
from the Ball until 5 o'clock when the
dance will be thrown open.
