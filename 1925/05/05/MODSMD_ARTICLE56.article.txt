# W. C. Smith to Lecture At E. E. Club Wednesday
## 
### 
W. C. Smith will talk on transformers
at the meeting of the Stanford Branch
of the American Institute of Electrical
Engineers to be held Wednesday night
at 7:15 in room 280.

"The Manufacture of Transformers
and Transformer Theory and Practice"
is the complete title of the lecture. Mr.
Sheibley is transformer engineer for the
General Electric Company at San Fran
