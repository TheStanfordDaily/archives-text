# STANFORD FRESHMAN NET PLAYER STARS
## 
### 
Gomer Thomas, Stanford freshman
tennis star, created a sensation among
the followers of tennis in San Fran
cisco last week-end by his perform
ance in winning from Waldron Thain
and John Murio, stellar junior players,
in the first two rounds of the San
Francisco city tournament which is
now in progress.
Thomas' form on the court was the
big attraction, and gained for him the
commendation of city tennis fans and
the favorable criticism of Miss Ash
baugh, Chronicle tennis critic. Thomas
won second singles against California
in the recent freshman matches.
