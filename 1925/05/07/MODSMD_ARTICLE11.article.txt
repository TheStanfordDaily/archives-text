# Catherine M. Cox Returns For Psychological Study
## 
### 
Dr. Catherine M. Cox, who has been
making psychological studies at the
Whittier State School for Boys, has
returned to the campus for two weeks.
Dr. Cox is preparing material to be
used in the second volume of Dr.
Terman's "Genetic Studies of Genius,"
the first volume of which has just
come from the press. Her survey will
be published under the title, "Early
Mental Traits of 300 Geniuses."
