# CASTILLEJA HOLDS MAY GARDEN FETE
## 
### 
The annual May Fete of the Castil
leja school will be held in the school
garden tomorrow morning at 10:30.
It will be in the nature of an old
English May festival with May poles
and May dances. The program will
include old country, Morris, gypsy,
and Maypole dances.
