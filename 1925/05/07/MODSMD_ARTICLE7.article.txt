# NOLAN TO BEGIN WORK WITH ARNOLD BAILEY
## 
### 
Lloyd Nolan, '2G, is leaving for the
East, Thursday, to play with Arnold
Bailey, '24, in liis stunt called "The
Radio Robot." He plans to meet Bailey
in Troy, New York, where the show
is on the road. At the end of its road
run, it will probably be taken to New
York City for a second successful
showing.
Nolan contemplates staying in New
York for the summer. At the end of
that time he will probably play on
the legitimate stage.
