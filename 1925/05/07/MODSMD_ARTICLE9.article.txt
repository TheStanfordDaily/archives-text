# ERNEST NEVERS WINS SCHOLARSHIP AWARD DONATED BY ALUMNI
## Fred R. Muhs Receives Skull And Snakes Trophy at Student Assembly 
### 
Ernest Nevers was given the Alumni
scholarship of $225 at the presentation
of activity awards in the A. S. S. U.
assembly this morning as the student
receiving the highest scholastic record
and making the greatest contribution
to the University in activities.
F. R. Muhs was awarded the Skull
and Snakes cup which is given annu
ally to the athlete who in his junior
year has won a block "S" and who
has made the highest average for the
last five quarters. The winner has cus
tody of the cup for one year.
The Joffre debate medal, awarded
annually to the winner of the Joffre
debate on French foreign policy, was
presented to J. C. Duniway, who was
adjudged the winner in the debate
with California last month.

of activity awards in the A. S. S. U.
assembly this morning as the student
receiving the highest scholastic record
and making the greatest contribution
to the University in activities.
F. R. Muhs was awarded the Skull
and Snakes cup which is given annu
ally to the athlete who in his junior
year has won a block "S" and who
has made the highest average for the
last five quarters. The winner has cus
tody of the cup for one year.
The Joffre debate medal, awarded
annually to the winner of the Joffre
debate on French foreign policy, was
presented to J. C. Duniway, who was
adjudged the winner in the debate
with California last month.
