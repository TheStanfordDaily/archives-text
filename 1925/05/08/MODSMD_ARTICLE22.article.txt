# Announcement of Courses Booklet Now on Press
## 
### 
The Announcement of Courses for
1925-26, booklet issued each year by the
Registrar's office, is now on the press
and will be ready for distribution some
time next week.
The Summer School Course booklet
now available for distribution contains
a section devoted to the summer school
of physical education and athletic coach
ing which this year the University will
conduct for the first time.
