# CABBAGES and THINGS
## . SIMPLE POETRY FOR SIMPLE PEOPLE 
### Steve O'Donnell 
Harold loved a maiden fair,
And how his heart did flutter.
He {isked her to become his bride:
She knocked him in the gutter.
So Harold worked and worked and
worked
And made a lot of money.
He told his love a second time.
She said, "Come closer, honey."
—F. E. H.
