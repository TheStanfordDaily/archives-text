# New Y. W. C. A. Cabinet Installation Sunday
## 
### 
Installation services for the new mem
bers of the Y. W. C. A. cabinet will be
held in the Memorial Church at four
o'clock Sunday. The recognition of all
members and candle-lighting service will
be combined with the installation this
year. The Advisory board, both old and
new Senior cabinets, and all members are
expected to take part in the service.
