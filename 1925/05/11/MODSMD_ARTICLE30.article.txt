# Junipero Serra Club Will Hold Annual Picnic May 19
## 
### 
The Junipero Scrra Club will hold its
annual picnic Tuesday, May 19.
The members of the club will meet in
front of the Women's Clubhouse at 5
o'clock that afternoon and travel to the
picnic grounds in automobiles provided
by the club.
All Catholic students intending to go
on the picnic should communicate as soon
as possible with W. C. Espy, Jr., 1052
High Street, Palo Alto; Dorothy Dahler,
203 Roble; or S. C. Cypher, Delta Chi
House.
