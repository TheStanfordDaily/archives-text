# Archery Meet Scheduled For Women's Field Day
## 
### 
The archery meet scheduled for
Field Day, May 30, promises to be one
of the most interesting events of the
day. Many women are out for the
sport and those showing the most
promise will be allowed to shoot on
Field Day when they will hold a regu
lar Colombia round of thirty, forty,
and fifty yards.
Those women who have already
showed ability are as follows: Una
Stafford, Pauline Hoffman, Lillian
Force, Doris Bonner, Myrtle de Vaux,
Elizabeth Jenks, Mary Harwood, Lu
cille Snyder, Winifred Huntington, and
Hope Williams.
Last year the archery cup was won
by Rosalind Coverly.
