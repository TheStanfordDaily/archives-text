# FINNISH BOOKS IN HOOVER COLLECTION
## 
### 
Two new Hoover War Library exhibits
arc in the show cases in the library.
One case shows examples of delega
tion propaganda that was acquired at
the Paris Peace Conference in 1919,
when the Hoover Library was first or
ganized. The government of Sweden and
Finland presented the War Library with
a collection of official documents at this
time. In this case arc also League of
Nations documents, and one of the 50,-
000 books purchased for the library
through European agents, aided by schol
ars in the cities of Europe.

In the other case arc specimens from
the collection of Finnish scientific and
literary work which was a gift of the
Republic of Finland to the Hoover War
Library. This library owns the most
complete collection of this kind in the
United States.
