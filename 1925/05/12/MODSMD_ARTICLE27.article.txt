# Stanford Women's Club Holds Monthly Meeting
## 
### 
The regular monthly meeting of the
Stanford Women's Club of San Fran
cisco was held Saturday afternoon.
Mrs. H. O. Close, '12, was the speaker
and guest of honor. This was the last
meeting of the club until next Septem
ber.
On May 26 the Board of Directors
and all chairmen of committees will
meet at the Y. W. C. A.
The regular monthly dinner, which
is held at the Women's Athletic Club
at 6:15 p.m., is scheduled for May 27.
