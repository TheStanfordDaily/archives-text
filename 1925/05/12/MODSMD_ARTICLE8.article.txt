# CURATOR PLANS VISIT TO EASTERN MUSEUMS
## 
### 
Mrs. Ida S. Oldroyd, curator of the
Geology Museum, will leave the cam
pus on July 20 for her annual trip
East.
Mrs. Oldroyd will visit the Field
Museum in Chicago for three days,
and will stop in Washington to work
in the conchology section of the Uni
ted States National Museum for one
week. She will spend much of her
time at the American Museum of New
York where Mrs. Oldroyd will arrange
and classify the shell collection of the
museum. On the return trip she will
visit the New York State Museum at
Albany and the Boston Museum.
