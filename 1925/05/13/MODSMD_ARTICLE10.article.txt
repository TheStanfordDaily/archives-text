# Water Supply Class Plans Trip to San Leandro Dam
## 
### 
Professor L. B. Reynold's class in
water supply will inspect the property
of the East Bay Water Company on
a trip to be made next Saturday. The
members of the class will be given
lunch by the company.
The upper San Leandro dam will be
the first object of the visit. The dam,
now in process of construction, is of
the hydraulic fill type.
