# MISS SPROUL GIVEN FAREWELL PRESENT
## 
### 
Employes of the Encina Dining Halls
presented a leather traveling case and
a large box of chocolates to Miss Annie
B. Sproul in appreciation of her help
during the past year.
Miss Sproul left the campus this
morning for the Yosemite Valley
where she will become director of the
Camp Curry cafeteria which is being
opened for the first time this summer.
Miss Eva McKinnon, former director
of the Branner Dining Hall, and Miss
Etta H. Handy, director of the Uni
versity dining halls, are now managing
the Encina Dining Halls.
