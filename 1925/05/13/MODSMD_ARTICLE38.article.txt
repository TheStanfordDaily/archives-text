# E. E. Club Calls Meeting To Discuss Oakland Trip
## 
### 
All who intend to go on th Electrical
Engineering Society's trip to the Gen
eral Electric Company's plant Satur
day are requested to come to a meet
ing to discuss plans which will be held
tomororw night at 7 o'clock in Room
263.
The society plans to go to Oakland
in automobiles, if enough can be se
cured for the trip. The start will be
made about 7 o'clock in order to reach
the plant by 8:30. Anyone having a
machine which could be used for the
trip is urged to communicate with
Fred Crever or come to the meeting
Thursday.
