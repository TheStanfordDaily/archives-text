# POLO TEAM ELECTS TROTTER CAPTAIN
## 
### 
F. E. Trotter, who has played num
ber four on the polo team for the past
season, was yesterday elected varsity
captain for 1926.
Trotter has played seven years with
the Honolulu Club team and has a
handicap of one goal in the American
Polo Association. During the past
son he has scored thirty-one goals in
twelve games, making over one-third
of Stanford's tallies. Trotter is a
sophomore transfer from the Univer
sity of Hawaii.
