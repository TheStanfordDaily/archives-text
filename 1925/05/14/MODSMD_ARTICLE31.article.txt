# Dean Mary Yost to Attend Convention at Sacramento
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost is leaving today for
Sacramento, where she will attend the
fourth annual convention of the state
division of the American Assocaition of
University Women. Miss Yost is vice
president of this organization and will
preside at several of the conferences.
The meetings will he held Friday eve
'ning and all day Saturday of this week.
Branch problems of the association will
be discussed.
Miss Yost will return to the campus
Sunday.
