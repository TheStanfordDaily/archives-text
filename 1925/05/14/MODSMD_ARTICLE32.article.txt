# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
FRESHMAN TRYOUTEES for next
year's Quad editorial and manager
ial staffs will meet in Room 355, En
cina, from 3:30 to 4 o'clock.
SPANISH CLUB meets at 8 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse.
CONVENTION STAFF of the Califor
nia Interscholastic Press Associa
tion will hold a final meeting at 1:30.
Complete instructions will be issued
to convention workers at this time.
ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING CLUB
urges all who plan to visit the Gen
eral Electric Company next Satur
day to come to Room 263 at 7 p.m. to
make plans.
C. E. SOCIETY dinner at 6:15 tonight
at El Campo Club. E. A. Reinke of
California State Board of Health will
speak on Public Health Engineering.
SPANISH CLUB meets tonight at 8
o'clock in Women's Clubhouse.
GEOLOGY AND MINING SOCIETY
meets at 7:30 in the Mining build
ing. Election of officers.
FRIDAY
FACULTY WOMEN'S CLUB will en
tertain men of the faculty in the
Women's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock.
MASONIC CLUB DEGREE TEAM will
meet at 7 p.m., Masonic Temple,
Palo Alto.
SUNDAY *
CHRISTIAN ENDEAVOR meets at
Presbyterian church in Palo Alto at
6 o'clock for supper. Discussion on
"Idols" will follow.
MONDAY
LAW I examination will be held Mon
day, May 18, at 11 o'clock in the
Little Theatre.
