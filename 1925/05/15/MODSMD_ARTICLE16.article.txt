# Charles J. Woodbury to Talk on Emerson Tuesday
## 
### 
Charles J. Woodbury will deliver the
Tuesday evening lecture next week
in the Little Theatre. "Emerson and
Ilia Friends" is to be the subject of
the talk. Woodbury is the only author
and lecturer now living who knew
Ralph Waldo Emerson personally.
Woodbury has lectured at many
schools and colleges in the United
States about Emerson and other Con
cord personalities.
