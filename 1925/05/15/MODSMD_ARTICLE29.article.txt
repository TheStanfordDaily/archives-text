# SENIORS WIN CLASS CHAMPIONSHIP TITLE
## 
### 
With double wins in tennis and soccer
the seniors came from behind to nose
out the sophomores for first place in the
University all-round championship. The
triple tie in soccer for second place gave
the 1925 men a substantial lead.
Not only did the winners lead in the
number of points, but also in the num
ber of first places, copping track, soccer,
and tennis. The 1927 men took firsts in
football and basketball and tie for first
with the upperclassmen in swimming.
Results of the all-round championship
contest by points is as follows:
Class fb. bkb. sw. tr. bb. so. ten.Total
