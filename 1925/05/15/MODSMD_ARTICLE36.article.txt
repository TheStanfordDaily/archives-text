# LAKE ARROWHEAD
## CALIFORNIA'S FINEST PLAYGROUND 
### 
The Village of Lake Arrowhead has a particular appeal to college
men and women. There are the annual events —the trap shoot, "mile
hi" diving meet and regatia, besides the golf and tennis finals, swim
ming and track meets, archery, handball, and various other athletic
events. You'll find college men from all over the world at Lake
Arrowhead this summer. %
