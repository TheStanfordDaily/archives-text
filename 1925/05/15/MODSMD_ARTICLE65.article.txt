# ANNOUNCEMENTS

FRIDAY
FACULTY WOMEN'S CLUB will en
tertain men of the faculty in the
Women's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock.
MASONIC, CLUB DEGREE TEAM will
meet at 7 p.m., Masonic Temple,
Palo Alto.
SATURDAY
ELECTRICAL ENGINEERING Society
members going on the Oakland trip
will meet at 6:40 a.m. in front of
the Bookstore.
SUNDAY
EPWORTH LEAGUE of First M. E.
Church will hold a devotion meeting
at 6:15. Topic: "What It Means to
Be a Christian."
CHRISTIAN ENDEAVOR meets at
Presbyterian church in Palo Alto at
6 o'clock for supper. Discussion on
"Idols" will follow.

day, May 18, at 11 o'clock in the
Little Theatre.
