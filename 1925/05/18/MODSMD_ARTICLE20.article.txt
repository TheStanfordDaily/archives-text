# Junipero Serra Club Holds Picnic Tomorrow Evening
## 
### 
The Junipero Serra Club will hold its
annual picnic tomorrow evening at a
spot beyond Frenchman's Tower.
The members of the club will meet in
front of the Women's .Clubhouse be
tween 4:30 and 5:30 tomorrow after
noon and will travel to the picnic
grounds in automobiles provided by the
club. Thirty have already signed up
to go.
All Catholic students intending to go
on the picnic should communicate as
soon as possible with W. C. Espy, 1052
High Street, Palo Alto; Dorothy Dahler,
Roble; or S. C. Cypher. Delta Chi.
