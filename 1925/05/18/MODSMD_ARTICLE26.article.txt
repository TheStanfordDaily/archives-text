# HARRY MALONEY WILL GIVE TALK OVER KGO
## 
### 
Harry Maloney is scheduled to give a
talk over KGO this evening at 8 o'clock
on "Common Sense Exercise for Wom
en," together with the importance of
(especially the business woman) keeping
physically fit.
The talk is a preliminary to a series
of specially selected exercises for women
which are to be given in the near future
by Harry Maloney for the benefits of
the patrons of KGO.

Thieves entered the University
bookstore at the University of Colo
rado last week and escaped with $75
in cash.
