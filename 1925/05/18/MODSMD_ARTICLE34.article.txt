# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
LAW I examination will be held Mon
day, May 18, at 11 o'clock in the
Little Theatre.
CARDINAL CHESS CLTTB meets at
7:15 in the Trophy Room of the
Old Union.
MECHANICAL ENGINEERING SO
CIETY will meet at 7:15 in Room
280. Professor Eckart will speak on
"Oil."
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at 7:15
in the Old Union.
JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB meets at
4:30 in front of the Women's Club
house to leave on annual picnic.
WOMEN'S COUNCIL will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA meets in Old
Union at 7:15 o'clock.
THURSDAY
SKULL AND SNAKES will hold its
initiation at 6 o'clock at the Theta
Xi house. All members are requested
to be present.
WEDNESDAY
PUBLIC DISCUSSION, sponsored by
Euphronia and the San Jose State
Teachers College in the Lobby of
the Old Union at 7:30 i on the ques
tion: "Resolved, that women should
be given equal smoking privileges
with men."
