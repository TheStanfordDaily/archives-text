# Aquaplaner Loses Bet, Gets Clothes Wet By "Accident"
## 
### 
Aquaplaning while fully dressed is the
latest form of amusement at Lagunita,
according to eye-witnesses of such an
event yesterday afternoon.
A prominent member of the student
body made a bet that he could ride
around the lake on an aquaplane without
getting wet. All went well until time
for disembarking, when the intrepid ad
venturer took two steps, one of which
went on a float, but the other was into
the lake. Cheers from onlookers greeted
the unfortunate student as he drew him
self, shivering and shrunken, onto the
n .. „
