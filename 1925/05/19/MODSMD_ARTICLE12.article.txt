# R. 0. T. C. to Participate In Memorial Exercises
## 
### 
The Stanford artillery unit will take
an active part in the Memorial Day
exercises in Palo Alto on Saturday,
May 30. As the University is planning
no particular observance of the day,
the Stanford R. O. T. C. will join with
the various organizations of Palo Alto
and march in the parade there.
Major W. D. Geary, head of the de
partment of military science, is in
charge of the program.
