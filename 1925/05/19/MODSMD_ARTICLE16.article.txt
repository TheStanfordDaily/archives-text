# Rushing Rules Discussed By Fraternity Council At Meeting Last Night
## 
### 
Men's rushing rules were the main
topic of discussion at the interfratern
ity council meeting last night. The
week day dates for this week and next
week will be from 11:30 until 2 o'clock
for lunch, and from 5:30 to 8 o'clock
for dinner.
Friday rushing will be from 11:30
in the morning until 1 o'clock the next
morning, and Saturday will be from
8 o'clock until 1 a.m. Rushing is from
8 in the morning until 8 in the evening
on Sunday.
Cards for next week will be given
out by. L. H. Peter in the Old Union
from 1:30 to 1:45 Thursday, May 21.
The freshmen may accept as many
dates as he wishes with the fraterni
ties this week and next.
The bids must be turned in to the
lawyer between 4 and 6 o'clock on
Thursday, May 28. Pledging will be
on Sunday, May 31.
