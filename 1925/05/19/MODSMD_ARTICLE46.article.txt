# Carnival Spring Dance Planned by Toyon Club
## 
### 
Toyon Club will give a carnival
spring dance Friday night. A few
tickets are still available and may be
obtained in Room 216, Toyon hall.
Balloon prize dances, colored spot
lights, and indirect lighting effects are
among the features promised by M. D.
McPherson, chairman of the dance
committee.
Serpentines, confetti, and 600 bal
loons have been secured for the affair.
Original leather programs will be in
harmony with the sport atmosphere.
