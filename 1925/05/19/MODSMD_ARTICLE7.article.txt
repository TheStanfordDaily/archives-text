# AIR MAIL SCHEDULE CHANGED FOR SOUTH
## 
### 
Changes in the air mail schedule
from New York to San Francisco have
gone into effect on May 1. The east
bound plane now leaves San Francisco
at 7 a.m. instead of at G a.m. The final
hop is made from Concord, California,
at 8:45 a.m. This change enables the
southern California and San Joaquiil
Valley mails which come north in the
"Owl" to be transferred to Concord
via Bay Points and reach Chicago in
one day less time than that taken on
the old dispatch. Now all mail accumu
lating at San Diego until 2 p.m. and
at Los Angeles until 5 p.m. Mondays
will arrive in Chicago at 7:35 a.m.
Wednesday, and New York at 5 p.m.
Air mail dropped in the local post
office before 5 p.m. on any day will
leave on the Eastbound Air Mail Dis
patch the next morning.
