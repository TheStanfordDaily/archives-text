# Dr. Jacques Hadamard Will Discuss "Huygen's Principle"
## 
### 
Dr. Jacques Hadamard, professor of
analytical mechanics at College de
France and professor of analysis at
l'Ecole Polytechnique de Paris, will
lecture here this week on "Huygen's
Principle" in Room 72 of the Mathe
matics building.
These lectures will be held at 4 p.m.
on Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday,
and at 10 a.m. on Saturday.
