# Professor Eckart Speaks Before Engineers' Club
## 
### 
Professor W. R. Eckart spoke to the
Stanford student chapter of the Ameri
can Society of Mechanical Engineers
last night on "Specific Heat of Oil in
Relation to Specific Gravity and Tem
perature."
This same talk was given by Pro
fessor Eckart before the national con
ference of the Petroleum Division of
the American Society of Mechanical
Engineers last month at Tulsa, when
he was elected national chairman. The
lecture was illustrated with lantern
slides.
