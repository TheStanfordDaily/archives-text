A man of parts is Teddy Cleek,
The Lord made him six-sevenths cheek,
He's used ray ties and shirts and hat
And queened my girl—and add to that
When he goes out, that famous line
That's wholly his now, once was mine—
So, fellows cheer for Teddy Cleek
(I'd like to land once on his beak!)
—L. A. B.
