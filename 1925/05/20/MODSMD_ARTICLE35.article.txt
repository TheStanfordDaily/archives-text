# Y. M. C. A. Campaign Nets Thousand Dollars in Week
## 
### 
Palo Alto business men are conducting
a drive for funds for the combined Stan
ford and Palo Alto Y. M. C. A., and a
week's campaigning has resulted in an
accumulation of over a thousand dollars.
The drive will continue until the quota
of $2100 is proceured.
