# Radio Listeners to Hear Stanford Band Over KPO
## 
### 
Campus radio listeners will be able
to hear the Stanford Band over radio
station KPO, San Francisco, from 9
to 10 o'clock on Monday night. The
band, under the direction of Professor
E. W. Martin, will present a varied
musical program. Dean Oeorge B. Cul
ver is scheduled to deliver a ten-min
ute talk during the hour.

One-third of the living members of
Phi Beta Kappa are women.
