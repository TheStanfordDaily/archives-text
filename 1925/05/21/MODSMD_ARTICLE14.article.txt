# H. H. BLEE DISCUSSES DIRIGIBLES TUESDAY
## 
### 
H. H. Blee, '10, will speak in the
Little Theatre Tuesday afternoon at 4
o'clock on "The Development of Bal
loons and Dirigibles and Their Place
in Commercial Aeronautics." Blee
holds a lieutenant colonel's commis
sion in the Air Service Reserve. He
also holds an international license as
spherical balloon pilot and dirigible
pilot.
The lecture, which will be illustrated
with lantern slides, will include a brief
resume of aerial photography and its
application to the work of the engi
neer, and will be of general interest
rather than of a highly technical char
acter. The general public is invited
to attend.
