# J. I. LUBIN PRESENTS PERPETUAL TROPHY
## 
### 
Jess I. Lubin, president of the Sacra
mento Fencers' Club, who was host to
the Stanford fencing team on its re
cent trip to Sacramento to instruct
the club, has offered the Jess I. Lubin
perpetual fencing trophy for annual
competition between the men students
of the University.
The cup will be open for competi
tion at the University championships
to be held tomorow night, and the
winner each year will have his name
inscribed on the trophy.
