# CONDITION OF CAPTAIN JENKINS IS IMPROVED
## 
### 
The condition of Captain J. S. Jen
kins, assistant professor of Military
Science and Tactics, is reported im
proved, according to the report re
ceived from the military department
yesterday. Captain Jenkins was oper
ated on yesterday afternoon in a San
Francisco hospital. He will not return
to Stanford until fall quarter.
Captain Jenkins has been coaching
the polo team since fall quarter, when
he first became connected with the
military department at Stanford. In
addition to this he also instructed in
some of the military classes.
