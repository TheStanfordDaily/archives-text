# Stanford Student Appears In Alfred Savoir Comedy
## 
### 
Burt Harger, a Stanford student who
is a member of Theodore Kosloff's
ballet, is appearing in a special num
ber in the third act of "The Grand
Duchess the Floor-Waiter," a com
edy by Alfred Savoir, with Elsie Fer
guson, which is now running in San
Francisco at the Columbia theatre.
