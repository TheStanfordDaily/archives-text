# SCOUTS PLAN JINKS IN ASSEMBLY HALL
## 
### 
Boy and Girl Scouts of Palo Alto
and vicinity will hold their annual
Scout Jinks in the Stanford Assembly
hall Wednesday evening, May 27. Ten
events have been arranged for the pro
gram, including vocal solos by Charles
Bulotti, tenor; cartoons by James G.
Swinnerton, who is known for his
work on the Chaparral and as the
originator of Little Jimmie; six se
lected vaudeville acts by Stanford stu
dents, whose names will be announced
later, and several choral numbers by
the Morris Club of Palo Alto.
