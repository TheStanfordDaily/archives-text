# A. D. Aguerrevere Elected President of Spanish Club
## 
### 
A. D. Aguerrevcre was elccted presi
dent of the Spanish Club at a recent
meeting. A. M. Espinosa, Jr., vice-presi
dent, and E. H. Templin, secretary
treasurer. The club has adopted a new
constitution providing for the election of
officers in the spring quarter instead of
the fall quarter, so that at the opening
of the new year there will be officers to
assume the work of the organization.
