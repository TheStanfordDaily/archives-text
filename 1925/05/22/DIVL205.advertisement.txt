LAKE ARROWHEAD
CALIFORNIA'S FINEST PLAYGROUND
The uniqueness of Lake Arrowhead and its quaint Village lies in the
wholesome, homelike atmosphere of the Village and Woods. You will
like the picturesque Village Common with the tiny shops, the invigorat
ing bathing, good boating and fishing, moonlight dancing, open air
theatre, the outdoor grill and the brisk horseback rides.
