# BERKELEY PLAYERS PLAN PERFORMANCE IN ASSEMBLY HALL
## 
### 
Irving Picliel and his Berkeley Play
ers will present Bernard Shaw's
"Devil's Disciple" in the Assembly hall
Friday night, May 29 under the au
spices of the Palo Alto Community
Players, an organization that has been
inactive for the last two years.
The Berkeley Players are moving
down to Stanford for the night with
their own scenes, settings, and furni
ture. Everything will be brought down
in a large moving van earlier in the
week.
