# Transfer of Territorial Y. M. C. A. Office Planned
## 
### 
Next September the headquarters of
the Y. M. C. A. for this territory which
are now in San Jose will be moved to
Stanford, according to Ed Vail, acting
secretary of the campus Y. M. C. A.
The new headquarters will have juris
diction over Palo Alto, Mayfleld, and
Runnymede, as well as Stanford. A
Y. M. C. A. specialist in boys' work
will have its offices here on the cam
pus.
