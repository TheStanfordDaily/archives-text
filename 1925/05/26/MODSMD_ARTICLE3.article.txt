# Stanford Faculty to Be Speakers at Conference
## 
### 
The seventeenth annual meeting of
the California Conference of Social
Workers opened yesterday at Sacra
mento for a period of Tour days.
Among the speakers scheduled is
Professor E. C. Mears of the Stanford
Economics department. His subject
will be "A Race Relation Survey of
the Pacific Coast." N. F. Cummings,
director of public health at the Stan
ford Hospitals, will speak on "Extend
ing the Hospital's Usefulness." Miss
Margaret Lothrop of the Economics de
partment will also speak.
