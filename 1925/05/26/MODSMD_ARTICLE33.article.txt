# H. F. SMITH CHOSEN CHESS CLUB'S HEAD
## 
### 
H. F. Smith was elected president
of the Cardinal Chess Club at its last
meeting of the year held last night in
the Trophy room of the Old Union. The
other officers elected were: vice-presi
dent, J. L. Blackfort; secretary, E. H.
Bramhall (re-elected); treasurer, W.
H. Jacobs; manager, M. M. Kornfeld.
A match with California and a pos
sible preliminary contest with Davis
Agricultural College were outlined as
plans for the fall quarter. The corres
pondence match being carried on with
California will be continued then also.
This meeting completed the first year
of the club's existence. Its members
now number twenty-five.
