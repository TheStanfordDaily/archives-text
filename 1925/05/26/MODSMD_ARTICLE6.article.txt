# PROFESSOR FEARING TO ATTEND MEETING
## 
### 
Professor Franklin Smith Fearing
of the Psychology department will
leave the University early in June to
attend the 19th annual meeting of the
Pacific Division of the American As
sociation for the Advancement of
Science.
At the convention Professor Fear
ing will read several papers which are
the outcome of his collaboration with
Professor Clara S. Stoltenberg of the
Anatomy department on the study of
mystagmus in pigeons.
The meeting will be held at Reed
College, Portland, and will last from
June 17 to June 20.
