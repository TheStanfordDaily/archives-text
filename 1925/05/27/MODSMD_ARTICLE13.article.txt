# 1925 Ball Set for June 22 Ticket Sale Starts Monday Seniors Get Service First
## 
### 
Tickets for the Senior Ball go on
sale tomorrow downstairs in the
Administration building. The sale is
open to seniors only for the first
week. After June 4 any remaining
tickets will be sold to members of
the student body. Tickets for the
ball are ten dollars.
The 1925 Ball will be held at
Toyon hall on June 22. Music is to
be furnished by Horace Heidt's or
chestra from the Claremont Hotel.
Dancing will begin at 9:30 o'clock
and end at 3 o'clock when there
will be breakfast served in the
Union.
