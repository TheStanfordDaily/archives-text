# NEW THEATRE WILL BE OPENED IN PALO ALTO WITH SPECIAL PICTURE
## 
### 
The New Stanford Theatre will open
with a special picture, Tuesday, June 9.
This theatre is one of the largest in
the vicinity, the seating capacity being
1500, including first floor, loge, and bal
cony scats.
The stage equipment is run on the new
counter-weight system of operating cur
tains and drapes on cables. The building
is heated and ventilated by the mushroom
system, with the hot or cold air coming
up through the floor and going out
through the roof. The latest in power
machines arc being installed in the
operating room.
The theatre is equipped for vaudeville,
but no plans have been made for it yet.
Daily matinees and evening performances
will be held.
