# STUDENT PRESIDENTS ENTERTAINED AT 7-8
## 
### 
The student body heads of all the Pa
| cific Coast Colleges and universities were
entertained last night at the Kappa Kappa
Gamma House at a 7 to 8 dance,
Tyroler's orchestra furnished the music.
Today will be taken up in discussions
and lectures in the lobby of Toyon Hall.
This evening a banquet will be held in
the Union. The delegates leave Saturday
to visit the University of California be
fore returning home.
