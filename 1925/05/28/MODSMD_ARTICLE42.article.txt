# NEW WOMEN WILL BE GIVEN PICNIC BY "Y"
## 
### 
At a recent meeting of the Y. W. C. A.
senior cabinet it was decided that on the
Saturday after the Intelligence test and
before registration, the Y. W. would
sponsor a picnic for the new women who
enter Stanford next fall.
It was also decided to hold a tea for
the new women on the first Tuesday
after registration.
