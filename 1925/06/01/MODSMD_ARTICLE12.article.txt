# SENIOR BALL TICKET SALE TO OPEN IN AD BUILDING TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tickets for the Senior Ball will go
on sale in the Administration building
tomorrow from 1 to 2:30 o'clock to
seniors who have not obtained their
tickets and also juniors and under
classmen will be able to purchase the
remaining pasteboards.
The date of the Senior Ball has been
changed from Monday, June 22, to Fri
day, June L 9. Because of housing diffi
culties at Roble which would arise at.
the later date from summer quarter
registration, the committee has de
cided to hold it the previous Friday.
