# A 						MESSAGE TO EPICUREANS
## 
### 
Those of you who are planning
on attending Summer Quarter
here will have to solve the prob
lem of where to eat. Practically
every table which you will be
asked to join will charge the
same rate, $30.00 per month.
Therefore you will have to con
sider chiefly the kind of cooking
to be had at the various places.
The Beta House table this sum
mer will again have for its cooks
Mr. and Mrs. Daugherty, whose
culinary skill will be vouched for
by anyone who has ever tasted
their food. Ernie Daugherty was
formerly head chef at one of the
Leighton Cafeterias, and his wife
is famous for her pies and salads.
Accommodations will be limit
ed to thirty-five, and many have
already signed up. If you are
going to summer school and
want to be well fed, eat at the
Beta table.
