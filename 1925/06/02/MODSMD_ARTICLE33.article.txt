# Frye Nominated for Head Of Interfraternity Council
## 
### 
F. A. Fryc was unanimously nominated
for the position of president of the Inter
fraternity Council at the regular weekly
meeting of the conference last night. Frye
is next season's football manager, and a
member of Phi Kappa Sigma.
H. C. Oatman was nominated for sec
retary to take the place of L. H. Peter,
the present secretary. F. M. Chase and
D. D. Graves were nominated for posi
tions on the board of control. Two men
will be picked for the board this quarter.
Nominations will be held open until noon
Thursday.
