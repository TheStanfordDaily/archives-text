# Soccer Men Hold Annual Banquet in Union Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the soccer squad will hold
their annual banquet in the Union at 6:15
o'clock tomorrow evening.
Plans for next season will be. made, and
the trophy for the individual on the squad
who, in the estimation of the captain and
coach, has been the most valuable man,
will be awarded. This year a gold soccer
ball with a fob has been donated by Phil
Wilson, '01.
