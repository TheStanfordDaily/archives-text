# Freshmen to Hold Last Roble "Jolly-Up" Tonight
## 
### 
The freshman class will hold the
last "Jolly-Up" of the year from 6:30
to 8 o'clock at Roble hall. N. G. Lar
son's four-piece orchestra will furnish
music for the occasion.
The committee on arrangements is
headed by Carolyn Grossman. Other
members of the committee are P. A.
Barertt, Anne Hartwell, and W. H.
Hubbard.
