# LOWER DIVISION WOMEN
## 
### 
As announced in the statement
from the Lower Division Office,
Advance Registration for next au
tumn quarter for Lower Division
students will be held June 5, 6,
and 8. In order to save the time
of women on these days, it will
be possible for them to sign up
for their appointments in the
Dean of Women's Office, Tuesday,
Wednesday, and Thursday of this
week.
All Lower Division women are
urged to study the autumn quar
ter time schedule before coming
to their appointments, and to
think seriously over their choice
of major department, even if they
are in their freshman year.
