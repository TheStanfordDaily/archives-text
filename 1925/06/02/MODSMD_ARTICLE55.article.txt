# 1926 QUADS WILL BE DISTRIBUTED MONDAY
## 
### 
Distribution of the 1926 Quad will
begin at 1 o'clock next Monday in the
Administration building. Absolutely
none of the yearbooks will be given
out until that time. Purchasers of
Quads who are unable to present their
ticket stub at the window will have
to pay an additional twenty-five cents.
