# TOYON CLUB ELECTS W. A. HILL AS NEW EXECUTIVE HEAD
## 
### 
W. A. Hill was elected president of the
Toyon club for next year at the election
of officers held last night. Hill has been
manager of Toyon club for the past three
months and is a member of El Campo.
Others officers named were J. B. Man
non, vice-president; B. M. Fraser, man
ager; T. F. Conroy, secretary. Fraser
and Conroy belong to El Cuadro while
Mannon is a member of El Toro.
