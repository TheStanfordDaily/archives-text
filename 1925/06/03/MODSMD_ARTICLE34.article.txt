# QUAD DISTRIBUTION COMMENCES MONDAY
## 
### 
Distribution of the 1926 Quad will be
gin at 1:00 p. m., Monday afternoon in
the Administration Building, according to
R. M. Kay, manager. The Quad will be
given out for several days, but the man
agement will not hold books for those
who defer calling for them for a period
longer llian a week.
Every effort will be made to enable
those with tickets to get their books with
the least possible delay. Absolutely no
books will be given out before the start
ing of the official distribution. Ticket
stubs must be presented or a fine of 25
cents will be levied.
