# C. J. KINNISON MADE HEAD OF M. E. CLUB
## 
### 
Court J. Kinnison, '24, was elected
president of the Mechanical Engineering
Society at the last meeting of the quarter
held last night. Professor G. H. Marx,
head of the Mechanical Engineering de
partment, was named honorary chairman
for the year.
H. N. Swift, '26, was elected vice-presi
dent for the next half-year term, while
H. W. Powers will fill the office of sec
retary-treasurer of the society.
