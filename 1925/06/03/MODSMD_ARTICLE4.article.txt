# SENIOR WEEK PLANS WILL BE COMPLETED AT CLASS MEETING
## 
### 
Professor E. G. Robinson will ex
plain plans for the Commencement
exercises at a meeting of the senior
class to be held in Itoom 214 at 1
o'clock tomorrow. Several other prob
lems will be discussed regarding
Senior Week, according to W. J.
Dickey, class president.
Senior Week plans are rapidly being
completed. The Senior Ball, which will
take place Friday evening, June 19,
will be given Wednesday
June 17, in the Assembly hall. With
the return of Gordon Davis, the cast
will hold final rehearsals.
