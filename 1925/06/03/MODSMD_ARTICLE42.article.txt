# NOTICE
## 
### 
Registration for Choir for the sum
mer and autumn quarters began this
morning. Mr. Allen will be in his
office, Room 84, tomorrow from 8:30
to 12 to hear others who wish to sing
in the church next year. Those unable
to come in the morning may come to
the rehearsal in the church tomorrow
evening at 7 :30, or to Room 84 Tues
day morning, June 9th, the last day on
which registrations will be received.
Mr. Allen will be unable to keep office
hours on Friday of this week, as pre
viously announced.
