# Crever Elected Chairman Of Electrical Engineers
## 
### 
Fred F.. Crever was elected chairman
of the Stanford Student Branch of the
American Society of Electrical Engineers
last night'. Crever has been secretary
treasurer of the organization this year.
The other officers elected were: vice
chairman, W. B. Wells; secretary-treas
urer, C. R. Walling. No other meeting of |
the club will be held this quarter.
