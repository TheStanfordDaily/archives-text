# Dramas, Life of Sheridan Will Be Lecture Subject
## 
### 
"Sheridan, His Plays and Place in
the Theatre," will be the subject of
an address by Clayton Hamilton at
4:15 tomorrow afternoon in the Little
Theatre.
Hamilton has made a special study
of the life and work of the Irish dra
matist, and will discuss such plays as
"The Rivals," "The School for Scan
dal," and "The Critic."
Hamilton is known as a dramatic
critic, having served in that capacity
and as associate editor of The Forum,
The Bookman, Everybody's Magazine,
and Vogue. He is also the author of
several books on drama and dramatists
such as "The Theory of the Theatre,"
and "Studies in Stagecraft."
