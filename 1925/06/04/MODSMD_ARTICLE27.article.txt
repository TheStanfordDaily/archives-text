# Donald Clark Awarded Medal at Soccer Dinner
## 
### 
Donald Clark was awarded the gold
football given to the most valuable man
on the soccer squad each year at the
soccer banquet in the Union last night.
Clark has played on the varsity for
three years and was one of the out
standing stars of the team last fall.
Philip Wilson, '01, donated the charm
this year.
Plans for next year's team were dis
cussed at the banquet. Harry Maloney
outlined the prospects of the season.

The King's Crown Board of Gover
nors has awarded fifty-seven crowns
for non-athletic work by students of
Columbia University. Twenty-five of
the crowns awarded were gold and
thirty-two were silver.
