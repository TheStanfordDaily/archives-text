# Tau Psi Epsilon Elects Officers for Next Year
## 
### 
Tau Psi Epsilon, national psycho
logical fraternity, elected officers for
the coming year at its luncheon at the
Union Wednesday noon.
The new officers are: president,
Cecil Broyler; vice-president, Barbara
Burks; secretary, Mildred Burlingame;
treasurer, Grace Peterson.
