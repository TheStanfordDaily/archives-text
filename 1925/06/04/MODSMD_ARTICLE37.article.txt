# Career as M. D. Is Topic Of Dr. Jordan's Jecture
## 
### 
"My Career in the Medical Profes
sion" is to be the title of Dr. Jordan's
regular Thursday evening lecture to
night at his home at 7:30 o'clock.
Dr. Jordan will deal with the pro
fession forty or forty-five years ago
and compare it to the present state
of the science of medicine. He will
speak especially of the founding and
the development of the Stanford Lane
hospital and medical school.
