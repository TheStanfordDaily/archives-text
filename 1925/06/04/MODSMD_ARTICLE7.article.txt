# Final Banquet of Euphronia Society to Be Held Tonight
## 
### 
Euphronia debating society will hold
its final banquet of the year in the
banquet room of the Stanford Union
tonight at 6 o'clock.
Officers for the autumn quarter will
be elected, and reports of all present
officers will be made. Talks will be
given by Professor L. E. Bassett. of
the English department, and by W. L.
Gosslin, the president of the society,
who will speak on the progress of the
society during the last two quarters.
All new members of the society are
expecterl to be present and old mem
bers are urged to attend. Tickets can
be obtained from W. L. Gosslin, or
from any other officer of the society.
