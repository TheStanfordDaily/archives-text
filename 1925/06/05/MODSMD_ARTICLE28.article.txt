# Tuesday Organ Recital To Have Franck Selection
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen will give organ re
citals on Tuesday and Thursday of
next week.
On both programs he will play com
positions of Cesar Franck. Tuesday, he
will play the Cantabile, and on Thurs
day, the Piece Heroique.
A collection of compositions by
Grieg, the Nocturne, Peasant Song,
and Festival of the King (Sigurd Jor
salfur) will also be on the Tuesday
program.
He will play Widor's Scherzo from
the Fourth Symphony on Tuesday and
his Andante Cantabile from the
Fourth Symphony.
