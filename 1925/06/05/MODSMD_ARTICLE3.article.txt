# Freshman Bridge Player Draws Thirteen Spades
## 
### 
"I'll bid seven spades," so spake a
certain freshman who was playing
bridge in the Encina Hall lobby the
other day. He got the bid, and he
made it without losing a single trick.
And here is the secret:
This reckless bidder picked up his
hand and in it were thirteen black
cards, all of them spades. He made a
grand slam.
