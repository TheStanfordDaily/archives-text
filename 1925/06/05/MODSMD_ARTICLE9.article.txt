# Removal of Biological Collections Makes Room For New Laboratories
## 
### 
The vacant space left by the re
moval of biological collections, and the
Dudley herbarium from the Zoology
building will be utilized by remod
eling it into offices and laboratories.
The large empty room of the
Zoology building is to be built into a
room to be used as a library for all
the biological departments.
The basement space below will be
a stock room for all departments in
biology, and at the back of the present
lobby will be a stock room window
and counter for the supply of appa
ratus to students.
