# PICNIC HELD IN HONOR OF PROFESSOR MARTIN
## 
### 
The History club and faculty mem
bers of the history department, gave
a picnic Sunday evening in honor of
Professor Percy A. Martin who will
leave the University this summer for
a European trip.
The club and faculty members met
at the Women's clubhouse, and drove
to the old zoology cabin which is now
leased by a San Francisco firm.
After the picnic, the party gathered
around the bonfire and Professor Mar
tin told of his intended trip.
