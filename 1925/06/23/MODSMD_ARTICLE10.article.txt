# Trist, Mulryan Nuptials Held in Memorial Church
## 
### 
The first of many weddings sched
uled for the summer took place yester
day afternoon, when Marian Trist, '25,
was married to Henry Mulryan, '23,
in Memorial Church. Dr. D. Charles
Gardner, University Chaplain, offi
ciated.
Miss Trist graduated this year In
French, and is the permanent Class of
1925 Secretary. She is a member of
Phi Beta Kappa and Chi Omega. Mul
ryan received his degree in Geology,
and is a member of Theta Chi. The
couple will live in Wickerville, Ari
zona.
