# HOLMAN ENTERS NET TOURNEY IN EAST
## 
### 
Cranston Holman, who played num
ber one man on the Stanford tennis
team last season, is now at Phila
delphia where he is entered in the
National Championship tournament to
be played at the Metion Cricket Club.
Holman was defeated by
Fritz Mercur, of Lehigh, in the Dela
ware State singles, but will have an
opportunity to again meet Mercur in
the present tournament. He plans to
remain in the East during the rest of
the summer, competing in the various
tournaments which are scheduled.

Six small colleges of New York
State have recently formed a small
college athletic conference.

"Dear Brutus" by Sir James Barrie
was recently produced by the English
Club of Syracuse University.
