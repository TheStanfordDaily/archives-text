# WATSON. HOOVER WEDDING THURSDAY
## 
### 
A wedding of much interest to Stan
ford will he solemnized in Memorial
Church Thursday night when Miss
Margaret Watson, '25, will be married
by Dr. Gardner to Herbert Hoover, Jr.,
also of the class of 1925.
Both Miss Watson and Hoover have
been prominent in university activ
ities during their careers at Stanford,
and have a host of friends on and off
the campus. The wedding will be wit
nessed by the immediate families, with
a reception to be held afterwards at
the Hoover home.
