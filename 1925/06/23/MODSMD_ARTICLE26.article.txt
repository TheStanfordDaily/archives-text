# SUMMER AUTO TRIPS COMMENCE SATURDAY
## 
### 
The first of a series of auto trips
to nearby points of interest will take
place next Saturday, when an excur
sion will be made to Half Moon Bay
and La Honda. These trips are under
the auspices of the university and are
arranged by John A. Sellards, Direc
tor of the summer quarter.
Cars will leave from in front of the
Stanford Union at ten o'clock Satur
day morning, and will return to the
campus at four in the afternoon. A
charge of $2.50 will be made for trans
portation, and those planning to go
will take their own lunches.

Professor William H. Norton of
Cornell College, lowa, is making a
special study of college novels.
