# Francena Henderson and Roscoe Thomas Married
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Francena Hen
derson, '26, and Roscoe Thomas, '27,
has just been announced. They were
married near Del Monte in January of
this year. Mrs. Thomas is a member
of Kappa Alpha Theta and Thomas is
a member of the polo team and Beta
Theta Pl.' *
