# AGAMEMNON TRYOUTS CONTINUE THIS WEEK
## 
### 
Tryouts for Agamemnon, the Greek
drama to be produced August 18 under
the combined direction of Evalyn
Thomas and Gordon Davis, may be
made by reporting to Miss Thomas at
Room 200 from 2 to 4 p.m. on Mondays,
Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays,
and from 7:30 to 9 p.m. every day ex
cept Thursday.
There are six leading characters in
the play, which will offer unlimited
opportunity for campus talent. Miss
Thomas has already found a number
in the class who are doing very good
work and who will prove invaluable in
the production, but there is a shortage
of men to carry the predominating
male roles. «
