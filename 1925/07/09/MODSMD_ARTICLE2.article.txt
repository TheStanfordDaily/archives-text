# COACHING SCHOOL TO STAGE DANCE JULY 17
## 
### 
The students and faculty are invited
to don their spiked shoes and shin
guards and attend the sport dance and
reception to be given by the Physical
Education Department on Friday, July
17, at Toyon hall. This will give the
members of the coaching school an
opportunity to become better acquain
ted at Stanford.
As a special attraction at the dance,
gymnastic Btunts and fancy dancing
will be offered. Andy Kerr is in charge
