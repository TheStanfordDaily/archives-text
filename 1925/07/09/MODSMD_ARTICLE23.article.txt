# FORMER EDITOR OF DAILY VISITS CAMPUS
## 
### 
Frank J. Taylor, a former editor of
the Daily Palo Alto, returned to Stan
ford on a visit last week while on a
business trip to the Bay region. Tay
lor has been prominent as a journal
ist both in the East and in the West.
Taylor is accompanied by his wife,
formerly Miss Katherine Ames, a
member of Delta Delta Delta sorority
at Stanford. The Taylors have recently
returned from Washington, D. C., and
New York City, where Taylor was en
gaged in newspaper work. They plan
to make their home near Stanford fol
lowing this summer in Yosemite Park,
where Taylor is being employed by
the Yosemite Park and Curry Com
pany as publicity director.
