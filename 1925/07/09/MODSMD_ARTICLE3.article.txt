# DARTMOUTH MINISTER TO PREACH SUNDAY
## 
### 
The Reverend Mr. Ambrose White
Vernon, minister of Christ Church,
Dartmouth, and famous academician,
will deliver the sermon at the Chapel
services of Sunday, July 12.
Dr. Vernon has studied abroad, has
been a professor at Dartmouth and
Yale, and was intimately connected
with Harvard. He is noted not only for
his theological research, but also as a
lecturer and an author.
