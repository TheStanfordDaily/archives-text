# BAHAI MEETING AT CLUBHOUSE JULY 15
## 
### 
Elimination of racial, religious, ami
national prejudice will be the subject
for open discussion of the Bahai meet
ing to be held at 7 p.m. on Wednesday,
July 15, at the Women's Clubhouse.
Everyone is cordially invited to attend.
The Stanford organization of Bahai
is based on the great international
Bahai movement dating apecifictally to
the middle of the nineteenth century.
The purpose of this society is univer
sal brotherhood.
The only membership qualification
of Bahai is interest in its purpose, and
students in sympathy with such a
movement are urged to attend its
meetings.
