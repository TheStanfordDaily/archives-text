# Cast Dance in Clubhouse To Follow Performance
## 
### 
The regular cast dance will be given
in the Women's Clubhouse tomorrow
night, following the performance of
"Hell-Bent fer Heaven."
Frank Hornkohl's Cardinals will pro
vide the music, and admission will be
75 cents. The dance will be a one
o'clock.
