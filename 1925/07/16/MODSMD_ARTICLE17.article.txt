# MALONEY GRANTED LEAVE OF ABSENCE
## 
### 
Harry Maloney, coach of boxing,
fencing, wrestling, and soccer, has
been granted a leave of absence for a
year to take a course in medicine at
Lane Hospital. Maloney has been at
Stanford for a period of approximately
twenty years. Aside from his duties as
coach at Stanford, he was head coach
of the American Track Team at the
Interallied games, and coach of the
American Rugby Football Team at the
Olympic Games in 1920.
