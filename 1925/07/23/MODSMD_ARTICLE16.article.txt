# FULLERTON BUILDS NEW CAR; SAYS IT'S FINE—IF IT RUNS
## 
### 
Henry Ford is surpassed. Dick Ful
lerton, '26, has built himself a four
lunged motor car for a total cost of
twenty-five dollars. The motor is of
one make, the chassis another, and —
"What are you going to call it?"
asked the reporter.
"I dunno. Think I'll wait and see if
it runs."
