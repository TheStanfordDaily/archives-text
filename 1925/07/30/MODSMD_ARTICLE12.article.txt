# FORMER PROFESSOR MARRIES PRINCESS
## 
### 
Charles H. Huberich, professor of
law at Stanfodd from 1905 to 1912,
was recently married at Oxford to the
Princess Nina Mdivani, daughter of
Prince Zacliarias Mdivani, aide of the
late Czar.
Mr. Huberich is a graduate of Yale
'99. The engagement had. been an
nounced in London during April.
