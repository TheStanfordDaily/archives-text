# FAIRCLOUGH GIVES ILLUSTRATED TALK
## 
### 
Professor H. R. Fairclough, of the
Department of Classical Literature,
will give an illustrated lecture on
"Art and Architecture of the Dalma
tian Coast" on Wednesday, August 5,
at 4:15 in the Little Theatre. During
the World War Professor Fairclough
was lieutenant-colonel in the Ameri
can Red Cross in Montenegro. As his
base of supplies was in Dalmatia he
had many opportunities to inspect the
remains of ancient civilization there.
