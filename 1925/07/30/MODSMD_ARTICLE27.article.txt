# STANFORD PICTURE RETURNS TO CAMPUS
## 
### 
"Stanford Days," a moving picture
film of campus life, taken during the
year 1923-24, will be presented by the
Stanford Alumni Association on Wed
nesday evening, August 5, at 7:30 in
the Assembly hall.
The scenario was written by Robert
Fitzgerald, a graduate student in Eng

lish. The leading parts are taken by
Hal Bumbaugh, Lloyd Nolan, and Mar
garet Richardson. All but a few scenes
were taken on the campus. Admission
will be 50 cents at the door.

Archery will be introduced as a
sport for women at Temple University
next year.
