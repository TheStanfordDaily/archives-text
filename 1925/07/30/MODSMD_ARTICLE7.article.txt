# JIM LAWSON SECURES MARRIAGE LICENSE IN REDWOOD CITY
## 
### 
Lawson is rated as one of the best
football players in the West and holds
the title of All-American end. During
the past year he led the Stanford
squad through one of the most suc
cessful seasons they have had for sev-1
eral years.
He is a member of the Delta Kappa
Epsilon fraternity and Skull and
Snakes and Block "S" societies.


Jim Lawson, '25, captain of the 1924
football team, applied for a marriage
license in Redwood City last week.
The bride-to-be is Miss Isabel Evans
of Long Beach, which is Jim's home
town.
