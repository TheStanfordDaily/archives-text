# Little Big Game Here November 7
## 
### 
The 1925 Little By? Game will be
played in the Stanford stadium on
November 7. According to custom the
game should be played at Berkeley
for the next two years, but this season
California plays Washington State in
the Berkeley stadium on that date.
The Stanford varsity will play Wash
ington University in the North the
same day.
