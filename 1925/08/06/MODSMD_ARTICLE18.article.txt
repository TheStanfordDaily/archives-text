# BUCKINGHAM PLAYS PRESENTED AT 8:15
## 
### 
"Voices" and "Enter the Hero" are
the plays to be presented by Miss
Buckingham's class in reading and
presenting plays, at 8:15 .tonight in
the Little Theatre.
In "Voices," Yvonne will be played
by Miss Frances Blomquist, and the
Other One by Miss Evelyn Brownell.
Miss Grace Russell directs the play,
and Herbert Smith is stage manager.
Miss June Clemmons is the director
of "Enter the Hero." The characters
are: Harold, played by Edmund Spel
lacy; Anne, by Marie Camp; Ruth, by
Katherine Austin; Mother, by Erma
Luce. Frances Keach is the stage
manager.
