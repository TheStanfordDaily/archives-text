# CHARLES K. FIELD'S MOTHER DIES SUNDAY
## 
### 
Mrs. Henry Field, the mother of
Charles Field, '95, died Sunday eve
ning, August 9, at the Hillcrest Hotel,
San Francisco, after a long illness.
Private services were held Monday
afternoon at Cypress Lawn cemetery,
where cremation took place.
Mrs. Field was born in Vermont but
had made California her home during
most of her married life. Since her hus
band's death several years ago Mrs.
Field spent most of her time at Los
Altos where the family had formerly
maintained a summer home.
Surviving her are four sons and a
daughter: Charles K. Field, Allen
Field, Russell Field, and Mrs. Harry
Sharp. Charles and Allen Field are
alumni of Stanford.
