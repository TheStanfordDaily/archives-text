# Jordan Lectures on "Meaning of Heredity"
## 
###

lecture this week on August 13 on
"The Meaning of Heredity." This is
the third of Dr. Jordan's lectures on
evolution. On August 20 at 4:15 in the
Assembly hall he will give a lecture
on the "Evolution of Nations."
Dr. Jordan is also giving his regular
weekly conference today at his home
on "The Human Harvest." Next week's
subject is:"Is War Eternal?"
