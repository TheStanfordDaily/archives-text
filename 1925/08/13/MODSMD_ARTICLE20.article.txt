# DAVID FALL TAKES SIX POINTS AT A.A.U.
## 
### 
Contrary to reports circulated last
week, Dave Fall, Stanford aquatic
star, placed in the national A. A. U.
swimming championships held at Seat
tle on August 1. Fall took second in

both the high fancy diving and high
plain diving events, giving him a total
of six points*
The other Stanford entrants placed
as follows: Arden Allen, third place
in the medley, one point; Carl Steven
son, third place in the half-mile, one
point; A1 White, third in the high
fancy diving and second in spring

board diving, four points. White was
competing for the Olympic Club.
