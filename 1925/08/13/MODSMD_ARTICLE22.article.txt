# HOTCHKISS SPENDS SUMMER IN CHICAGO
## 
### 
Willard E. Hotchkiss, dean of the
Graduate School of Business, is visit
ing in Chicago. He will remain there
until late in September. His assistant,
Professor V. H. Pelz, is temporarily
occupying President Wilbur's office in
the Administration building.
Those wishing information concern
ing the courses offered are requested
to communicate with the secretary,
Miss Owen.

The Home Economics Department of
Temple University holds an annual
fashion show where the garments, de
signed, made, or remodeled by the girls
themselves, are displayed so as to em
phasize the personality aspect.

A loan of $3,000i,000 cash from Dallas,
Texas, banking institutions were re
cently negotiated entirely by telephone.
