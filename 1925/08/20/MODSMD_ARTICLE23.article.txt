# JORDANS ENTERTAIN CORNELL WOMEN
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. David Starr Jordan en
tertained the members of the Cornell
Women's Club of Northern California
at a luncheon in Palo Alto on Satur
day, August 15. Mrs. Harris J. Ryan
was joint hostess with Mrs. Jordan.
Among the Cornell songs sung at the
gathering was one written by Dr. Jor
dan during his undergraduate years at
Cornell.
Mrs. Jordan and Mrs. Ryan give such
an entertainment every year.
