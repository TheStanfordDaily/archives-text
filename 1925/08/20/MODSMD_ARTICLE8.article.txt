# FULL QUOTA ENTER BUSINESS SCHOOL
## 
### 
Twenty-five graduate students, the
full quota allowable, have applied for
registration for the new school of busi
ness administration, according to an
announcement of Professor Victor H.
Pelz, who is organizing the school in
the absence of Dean Willard Hotch
kiss.
"The degree of according
to Mr. Pelz, "will be conferred when
the student has completed satisfac
torily the required study in residence
and has demonstrated his ability to
occupy a business position demanding
some executive capacity." This is the
first announcement of the policy of
the business school in conferring their
degree of Master of Business Adminis
tration.
