# Rex C. Bell Appointed New Y. M. C. A. Secretary
## 
### 
Rex C. Bell has been selected secre
tary of the Y. M. C. A. to succed F. E.
Morgan who resigned last spring after
seven years' work on the campus. Ben
J. Culver will be the assistant secre
tary.
Bell has been general secretary of
South Dakota, tri-state student secre
tary of Minnesota and the Dakotas,
and for the past three years associate
secretary of the Central Region of the
United States.
