# FRESHMEN MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Student leaders of college activities
will address the freshmen at the first
assembly which will be held at 6:30
p.m. in the Encina lobby. This is the
first of. the weekly series of "On the
boat" meetings which are sponsored
by the Y. M. C. A. President Wilbur,
Dean Culver, Registrar Mitchell, and
Comptroller Roth will be some of the
speakers at future assemblies.
