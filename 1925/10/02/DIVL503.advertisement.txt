Dramatic Council Dance Tonight
The second registration dance of the
quarter will be given tonight in the
j Women's Clubhouse by the Dramatic
j Council. The Hess-Young band, which
furnished the music last night, will
play again at tonight's affair. Dancing
will be from 9 o'clock until 1, with aTti
admission charge of $1.10.
