# HAMMER AND COFFIN TO NAME EDITOR OF CHAPPIE THURSDAY
## 
### 
Stanford chapter of the Hammer and
Coffin society will meet Thursday
night to choose an editor for Chaparral
for this year. Arthur Bragg, who was
elected to fill the office last June, is
at present in Schenectady and will not
register in the University this year.
The fli'st issue of Chaparral, which
went on sale on the Quad registration
day was edited by David A. Lamson,
who headed the Chappie staff last year.
George Thompson, '22, former art
editor, did the cover of the current
issue.
