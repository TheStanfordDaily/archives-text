# HATFIELD, '11, NAMED ATTORNEY
## 
### 
George J. Hatfield, '11, was named
attorney for the northern district of
California, by President Coolidge on
Saturday, October 3. Hatfield received
his A.B. degree from Stanford in 1911,
as well as his A.M. the following year,
into law practice in San Francisco he
and his J.D. in 1913. Since his entrance
has been prominent in state and
county Republican committees, and a
member of the State Veterans' Welfare
Board. In his appointment he succeeds
Sterling D. Carr.
