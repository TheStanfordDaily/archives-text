# MARX LEAVES CAMPUS
## 
### 
C. D. Marx, professor emeritus of
civil engineering, left Sunday for New
York on his way to attend the centen
inial of Karlsruhe Polytechnic in Ger
many. At the celebration Professor
.Marx, a graduate of Karlsruhe, will
represent both Stanford University
and the American Society of Mechani
cal Engineers, of which he is honor
ary vice-president. He will sail for
Hamburg on October 14 on the "Ber
lin" of the Hamburg-American line.
