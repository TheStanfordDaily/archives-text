# LESLIE KILER TO BE DRAMATIC ASSISTANT
## 
### 
Leslie Kiler has been appointed by
the University to the position of as
sistant to the director of dramatics,
according to Gordon Davis, dramatic
coach.
In this capacity Kiler will hold
classes in stage design, color, practical
methods, and costuming, in connection
with the class in theatrical workshop.
He will also be the technical assistant
in the production of plays.
An interesting group of plays, to be
announced later, has been chosen for
this year's dramatic season. There are
still a few places vacant in the class
in theatrical workshop for students
interested in play production, ajid it is
not too late to enroll.
