# STANFORD DAMES MEET
## 
### 
Wives and mothers of Stanford stu
dents are cordially invited to attend
the meeting of the Stanford Dames
tomorrow which will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3:15 o'clock.
As this meeting has been called to
acquaint the guests with work of the
Stanford Dames, the constitution will
be read, and matters of a business na
ture discussed. Refreshments will be
served following the meeting.
