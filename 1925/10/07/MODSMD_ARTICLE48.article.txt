# Enrollment in Oratorio Class Closes Tomorrow
## 
### 
Warren I). Allen, director of the
Stanford Glee Club and the choir, an
nounces that tomorrow will be the last
day on which it will be possible to
enroll in the oratorio class being
formed this quarter. Membership in
the choir will be a prerequisite to en
rollment in the class which will take
up the study of the oratorio "Stabat
Mater" by Anton Dvorak during the
autumn and winter quarters. The class
will meet each Tuesday at 7:30.
Places are still open in the Univer
sity choir for anyone wishing to join.
