# ANNOUNCEMENTS

WEDNESDAY
MASONIC CLUB meets at 7:30 o'clock
in the Old Union. All Masons are
cordially invited to attend.
EUPHRONIA will hold its first meet
ing in the lobby of Old Union at
7:30 p.m. "The Freshman and His
Choice of College Activities" will be
the subject of discussion. All fresh
men invited. •
ZOOLOGY" CLUB will meet at the
home of Mr. and Mrs. Snyder, 8
Alvarado. Dr. Taylor will speak.

RETURNING USHERS and any new
men interested in ushering for As
sembly Hall productions will meet
at 7 o'clock tonight in Room 200,
English building.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at 7
p.m. in the Old Union.
RADIO PARTY at First Presbyterian
church, Waverley and Homer streets,
at 8 o'clock.
