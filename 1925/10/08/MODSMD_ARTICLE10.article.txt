# STUDENT ASSEMBLY SLATED FOR MONDAY
## 
### 
A student body assembly will be
held at 11 o'clock, Monday, October
12, in the Assembly hall. The speakers
will be Robert E. Lewis, president of
the Associated Students, Dean Yost,
Dean Culver, Robert P. Myers, presi
dent of Men's Council, and Miss Mil
dred Worswick, president of Women's
Council.
Warren D. Allen will lead in singing
the Stanford hymn, the words of which
have been slightly changed. Printed
copies of the revised hymn will be
handed out prior to the assembly.
