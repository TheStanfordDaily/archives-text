# Stanford Journalists to Discuss Plans for Year
## 
### 
The Stanford Journalists Club will
meet tonight after the rally at tho
home of Professor Everett W. Smith,
12 Alvarado Row, to elect officers for
the coming year.
All old members are requested to
attend and new members are invited
to be present. Plans are being made to
have a number of prominent editors
and newspaper men address the club
during the fall.
