# Enrollment for Class In Oratorio to Close
## 
### 
Today is the last day in which stu
dents may enroll for the class in Ora
torio to be given by Warren D. Allen,
University organist and choir director,
during both the autumn and winter
quarters. The oratorio "Stabat Mater"
by Anton Dvorak will be studied. Mem
bership in the University choir will be
a prerequisite for the course, which
will be given every Thursday evening
at 7:30 o'clock.
Membership is still open in the Uni
versity Choir to anyone wishing to
join.
