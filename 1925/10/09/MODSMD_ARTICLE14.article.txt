# ART GALLERY HAS GERMAN DRAWINGS, DESIGNS DISPLAYED
## 
### 
Drawings and designs made by stu
dents of the Artcrafts School of Dus
seldorf, Germany, are now on display
at the Art Gallery. The pictures con
tain many interesting nature studies,
with unique ideas in posters and ink
silhouettes, as well as perspective and
constructive drawings.
Sketches of both common and un
usual species of California Wild Flow
ers are portrayed in the collection by
Ethel Wickes. A series of block prints
and striking etchings by Helen Hyde
presents a great variety of Japanese
subjects.
These pictures will remain on dis
play until November 1.
