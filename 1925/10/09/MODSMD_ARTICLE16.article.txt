# SPONSORS IN EATING HALLS
## 
### 
A sponsor will be placed in each
freshman eating hall from now on to
maintain quiet. This plan was decided
upon at a meeting of Comptroller Roth,
Dean Culver, Mrs. Handy, and Jacob
C. Irwin, the head sponsor, to con
sider means of keeping better order
in the eating halls. The sponsors ap
pointed to this position are Irwin,
Charles W. Dickenson, William Hobro,
and Stanley Weigle.
