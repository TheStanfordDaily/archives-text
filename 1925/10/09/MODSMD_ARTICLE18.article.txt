# Dr. Gardner to Address Freshmen Sunday Night
## 
### 
Dr. Gardner, chaplain of the Univer
sity, will address members of the
freshman class on the subject, "His
tory and Architecture of the Memorial
Church," on Sunday evening at 7:30
in the Memorial Church.
This meeting is sponsored by the
Y. M. C. A. and the Y. W. C. A. or
ganizations and a committee consist- j
ing of W. Leslie Gosslin and Esther
Greenacre is in charge of the affair.
After the talk of the evening, Dr.
Gardner will meet the freshman men
and women informally.
