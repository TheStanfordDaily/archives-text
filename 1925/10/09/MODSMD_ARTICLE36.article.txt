# Levi Elected New Head Of Stanford Journalists
## 
### 
John N. Levi, '26, was elected presi
dent of the Stanford Journalists Club
at a meeting held last evening at the
home of Professor E. W. Smith. Other
officers of the organization were elect
ed as follows: Beatrice Flesher, vice
president; Harry M. Bennett, secre
tary; and Victor Greisser, treasurer.
Levi is the managing editor of the
Daily Palo Alto, sport editor of the
Quad, and a member of Sigma Delta
Chi.
