# Mrs. Snell Attends L. A. Superintendents' Meeting
## 
### 
Mrs. Elizabeth Snell, secretary of
the Stanford appointment office, will
represent the University at the annual
state convention of Superintendents of
Public Schools, ordered by Will C.
Wood, state superintendent. The con
vention opens today in Los Angeles.

Mrs. Snell will be the principal
speaker at tomorrow's luncheon meet
ing of the convention. She will speak
on "The Problem of Teacher Place
ment."
While in the south Mrs. Snell will
also attend the meetings of the West
ern Association of Appointment Secre
taries, of which organization she is
secretary.
