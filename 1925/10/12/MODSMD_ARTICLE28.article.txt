# SOCIETY TO ENTERTAIN
## 
### 
New women will be the guests of
the Cap and Gown society which will
entertain at the home of President
Wilbur tomorrow afternoon from three
to six. It is the custom of the Cap
and Gown society to entertain the new
women each year. Cap and Gown id
an honorary society, selecting its mem
bers from upperclass women who have
'been active in student affairs.
