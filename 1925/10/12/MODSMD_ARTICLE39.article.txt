# Junipero Serra to Meet At Women's Clubhouse
## 
### 
The Junipero Serra Club will hold
its first meeting of the year at 7:30
o'clock tomorrow night on the first
floor of the Women's Clubhouse. Invi
tations have been sent to all freshmen
registered as Catholic students. Enter
tainment for the evening is planned
by the committee in charge.
The president of the club for this
year is A. M. Espinosa, Jr.
