# Guerard to Give Address On "France and America"
## 
### 
"France and America" will be the
subject of a lecture by Albert Leon
Guerard at 7:30 o'clock Tuesday eve
ning in the Assembly hall.
Professor Guerard has returned to
the Stanford faculty after an absence
of twelve years during which he has
been at the Rice Institute and at the
Southern Branch of the University of
California. He studied extensively in
Europe and served as a professor of
literature in the Paris Normal School
before coming to Stanford as an as
sistant professor of French in 1907. He
is now giving courses in general and
comparative literature here.
Professor Guerard has made a spe
cial study of French civilization and
is the author of several books on the
subject. He is a member of the Modern
Language Association of America.
