# "FRANCE AND AMERICA" SUBJECT OF TALK BY GUERARD
## IS NAPOLEON AUTHORITY Speaker Returns to Campus After Absence of Twelve Years 
### 
"France and America" is the sub
ject of a lecture by Professor Albert
Leon Guerard to be given at 7:30
o'clock tonight in the Assembly hall.
Professor Guerard has just returned
to the Stanford faculty after an ab
sence of twelve years, during which
he has been at Rice Institute, Houston,
Texas, and at the Southern Branch
of the University of California.
He was formerly assistant professor
of French here, but is now associated
with the English department, and is
giving courses in Napoleonic legend,
literature, and civilization. He is here
as a professor of general literature,
and will be a permanent member of
the faculty.
Professor Guerard has made a spe
cial study of French civilization, and
has published several books on the
subject. "Napoleon," his latest work,
appeared last year, and has received
much favorable comment. He is a
member of the Modern Language As
sociation of America.
