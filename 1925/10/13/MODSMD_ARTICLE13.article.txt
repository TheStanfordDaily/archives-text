# RESEARCH CLUB TO HEAR W. E. HOTCHKISS
## 
### 
"Research in Business" is the sub
ject which Professor Willarcl E. Hotch
kiss, of the Graduate School of Busi
ness Administration, will present to
the Research Club meeting in the Fac
ulty Clubhouse at 8 o'clock tomorrow
evening. Professor Hotchkiss will
show the two different kinds of re
search in business; first, work directed
toward adding knowledge primarily
for the sake of the business man, and,
second, work directed toward building
up courses for the student.
Franklin S. Fearing, of the Psy
chology department, will also address
the club on "Head Nystagmus in Adult
Pigeons."
