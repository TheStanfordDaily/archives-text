# J. J. Donnay Will Address First French Club Meeting
## 
### 
The French Club will meet tomor
row evening at 8 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse, for its first meeting
this year. Mr. J. J. Donnay, a graduate
of the University of Liege in Belgium,
will speak on "Une University Beige,"
and will compare Belgian and Ameri
can universities. Frederic E. Hines
will render vocal selections.
