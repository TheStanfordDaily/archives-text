# TAYLOR GOES EAST
## 
### 
Dr. Alonzo E. Taylor, director of the
Food Research Institute, has left for
the East on business for the Institute. !
He expects to interview department
officials in Washington and grain I
growers and operators in Minneapolis.
He will also visit in Chicago and New
York.
