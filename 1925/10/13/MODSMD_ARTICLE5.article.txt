# Theta Sigma Phi Gets Idea Woman Scribes Plan Sale Will Peddle Hot Doughnuts
## 
### 
Doughnuts, hot, sugary, light,' and
delicious, will be distributed to all for
tunate possessors of a nickel on
Wednesday morning in front of the
Women's Clubhouse.
The sale will start at eight o'clock
and will last as long as the doughnuts
do. Theta Sigma Phi sponsors the sale
and guarantees the super quality of
the doughnuts.
