# Wagner Given Electric Laboratory Contract
## 
### 
The contract for the Ryan Electrical
Laboratory was let to George Wagner
for $100,000 last Saturday, according
to Comptroller Roth. Work is to be
stated immediately.
The building will be constructed of
steel reinforced concrete and corru
gated asbestos, and is expected to be
finished next January, when the trans
formers, the largest ever built, will be
installed. The laboratory will be lo
cated near the Frenchman's Dam, on
the south side of the University
grounds.
