# Secretaries to Assemble At Stanford Next Summer
## 
### 
Ray W. Smith, secretary of the Palo
Alto Chamber of Commerce, has been
named business manager of the West
ern School for Commercial Organiza
tion Secretaries, which meets at Stan
ford next year from July 11 to 17, in
clusive.
At a meeting of the state advisory
council in Oakland last Saturday, the
summer school for the coming year
was discussed and a committee ap
pointed to draft a permanent organiza
tion.
Professor Victor West is dean of the
school. Smith will have charge of the
organization of the business details.
