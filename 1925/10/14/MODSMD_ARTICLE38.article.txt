# Drawings Posted for Tennis Tournament
## 
### 
Entries* for the tennis tournament
closed Monday und drawings have
been posted in Encina gymnasium.
Matches between the men paired are
to be arranged by the person placed
i in the upper part of the bracket. Con
testants not appearing at the agreed
time default their match. The first and
second rounds must be completed by
i October 2G.
