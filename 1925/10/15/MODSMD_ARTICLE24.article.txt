# CAIN, '23, TALKS
## 
### 
Brighton C. Cain, '23, gave a short
talk last night before Los Arcos Club
on his experiences as field naturalist
for the Oakland Council of the Boy
Scouts of America, a position which
is unique In America. He told also of
the active interest in Stanford affairs
which is retained by the East Bay
Stanford Men's Club, now meeting
every Tuesday in the new htiilding of
the Athens Athletic Club in Oakland.
