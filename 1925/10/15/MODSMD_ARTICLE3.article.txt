# BIG GAME TICKETS
## 
### 
Student applications for Big
Game tickets were mailed out to
day to all undergraduate and gradu
ate students who have paid under
graduate fees.
The requests for tickets must be
returned to the ticket department
of the Board of Athletic Control
before 5 o'clock, Wednesday, Octo
ber 21. Tickets will not be mailed
out this year, but the students will
have to call for them. They will be
ready for distribution sometime
early next month.
If any undergraduates or gradu
ates who have paid their under
graduate fees do not receive appli
cation blanks, they should com
municate with the ticket depart
ment. Each student may get a
rooters' ticket at $1.50 and two gen
eral admission tickets at $5 each.
