The uther daie I rings up 1 of those I
soroarity cisters and askter for a dait.
I sez wot are yew doing on saturdaie
nighte and sez yes so I askter to go to
the Wist Party witch the Paly Alta
fier dep is giving. She sorta cleers her
throte an dthen sez alrite so I shutz
downe thinkin of coarse everythin is
0 K. But wait til I tell yew.
Wellcer, Jake, on sat nighte I dresses
alup in my paten lether Bhews, stiff
collar and everythin. I even borrys
the januters dimon stick pin and cain,
and I goz up to the soroarity howse all
a shinin and swell like a new hearse.
1 wuz so swell Jake, that evrybuddy
just cudent help stairing at me. Well
I goz up to the dore of hte soroarity
howse and getz let in. Their wuz a lotta
guize settin aroun and prettie soon
my dait cumz rushin downe the stares
but she dozent see me at first and runz
over and talkt to anuther gie. Then
she seen me and smiels very much and
I smiels and the uther gie smiels and
then they cumz over and she inter
dooses me to the uther gie.
Wen we wuz all interdoost she sez
"I have to taik some potaitos to my
aunt in Paly Alta becuz she deerlie
luvs potaitos for brekfest and she
hazent got anie for tomorro morning.
Will yew cum out and hold the sack
while I get them. I wonderz for a sec
ond and then I asks the uther gie if
he wants to hold the sack two and he
laffs at me jake, he laffs rite in my
face. That kinda aggervaited me so I
desided I wuz goin to hold that sack
aloan no matter how many potaitos she
put in it. Well we goz owt on the back
poarch and she hands me the sack and
tells me to hold it rite out infrunta me
wile she goz and gets the potaitos.
Well I stands their for sometime and
finale the howse muther cumz owt and
tells me not to cum for the garbage
at nite animore cuz it waiks her up.
Jake, Ime thru with wimmen.
