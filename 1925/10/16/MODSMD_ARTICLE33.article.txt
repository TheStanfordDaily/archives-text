# WARREN D. ALLEN TO PRESENT "PASTORALE"
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will be heard in his regular after
noon recital next Sunday at 4 o'clock
in the Memorial Church. The opening
selection is a piece by Alexander Rus
sell, the organist of Princeton Univer
sity, entitled "Bells of Saiut Anne de
Beaupre."
Others are "Pastorale," a movement
from tlie Second Symphony by C. M.
Widor; two "Nocturnes," by the popu
lar composer, Robert Schuman; and a
finale, the "Coronation March," from
the opera, "The Prophet," by Giacomo
Meyerbeer.
