# RICHARD NEUSTADT ADDRESSES AD CLUB
## 
### 
Richard Neustadt, a director of the
San Francisco Advertising Club, ad
dressed the Stanford Advertising Club
of Palo Alto last night on the subject,
"Work of the Better Business Bureau,"
at a meeting held in the Cardinal hotel.
With sixty business men present, the
meeting was the largest ever held in
the history of the club. Theodore F.
Baer spoke of the membership cam
paign and fifteen new members were
admitted.
