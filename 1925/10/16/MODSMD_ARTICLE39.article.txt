# U. C. WOMEN PROTEST
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women journalists'
honor society of the University of Cali
fornia, asserts in the "The Dill Pickle"
that many a bright and shining star
in campus life carries gravy stains on
his vest. This accusation has caused
some concern among male campus
leaders, for by "gravy" is meant graft
in the form of favoritism.
