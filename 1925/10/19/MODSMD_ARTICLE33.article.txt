# CLUB TO ELECT
## 
###

requested to assemble in the Women's
Clubhouse for an important business
meeting at 7:30 o'clock Thursday eve
ning. Election of officers will take
place. At 8 o'clock all those interested
in the club will be welcome to join the
meeting.
