# Cubberly Will Address Education Club Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Ellwood P. Cubberly of the De
partment of Education will address
the Education Club which will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock
tonight. Dr. Cubberly has just re
turned from Wyoming where he spoke
before the State Teachers Convention.
Efforts are being made to secure
several distinguished speakers for the
Education Club. Programs for the
various meetings will be announced
later in the quarter.
