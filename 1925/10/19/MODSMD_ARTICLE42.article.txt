# EUPHRONIA TO MEET
## 
### 
An open forum under the auspices of
the Euphronia Debating Society will
be held in the lobby of the Old Union
at 7:30' o'clock Wednesday night.
"Campus Traditions" will be the sub
ject of discussion. An audience vote,
as a means of determing the campus
attitude 011 each tradition, will be
taken. The meeting is open to all who
care to attend.
