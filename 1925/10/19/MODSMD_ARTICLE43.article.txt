# WILBUR LEAVES FOR EAST
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur left Friday for a short trip in the
East, where Dr. Wilbur will attend a
series of meetings. The most import
ant of these will be the meeting of
the Association of American Univer
sities at New Haven, Connecticut. He
will return in time for the Big Game
November 21.
