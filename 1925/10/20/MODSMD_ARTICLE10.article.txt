# FIRST SOPHOMORE JOLLY-UP PLANNED
## 
### 
Members of the sophomore class will;
hold the first of a series of "Jolly-ups"
tomorrow evening from 6:30 to 8
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Members of the class will assemble at
the Clubhouse from the various living
groups of the campus at this time.
A series of "Jolly-ups" will be held
this year due to the success of those j
given by the class of twenty-eight dur
ing their freshman year. Music for the
affairs will be arranged by the commit
tee headed by Carolyn Grossman, who
is in charge of the "Jolly-ups."
