# BUREAU ESTABLISHED
## 
### 
The Albert Ilussel Erskine Bureau
for Traffic Research has been estab
lished recently at the Southern Branch
of the University of California, through
a donation of $10,000 a year by the
Studebaker Company. This is to ex
tend over a period of three years. Dr.
Miller McClintock, a professor of the
university and a nationally known
traffic authority, was made director
of the new bureau.
