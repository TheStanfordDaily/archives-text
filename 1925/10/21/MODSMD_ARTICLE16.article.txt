# Roble Musicians' Society Includes Forty Members
## 
### 
Membership in Roble Music Club,
which was organized recently, approxi
mates forty. The club is divided into
a "uke" section, a singing group, and
a jazz orchestra division.
Plans are being made to present pro
grams for various functions of the
hall.
