# Sigma Delta Chi Chapter Will Elect New Members
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi will meet this eve
ning at 7:15 in the trophy room of the
Old Union to elect new members.
Raymond L. Spangler, president of
the professional journalism fraternity,
will outline and explain the organiza
tion's program for the coming year.
All members are requested to be pres
ent as the meeting is one of vital im
portance.
