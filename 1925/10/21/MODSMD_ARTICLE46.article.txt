# FRESHMAN "Y" ELECTS
## 
### 
Stewart Way was elected president of
the Y.M.C.A. Freshman Council at a
meeting held last sight in the "Y" build
ing. Don Wright was named vice-presi
dent and Allan Van Nuys was made sec
retary of the organization. A hike and
campfire program to be held for fresh
men Saturday afternoon after the O.A.C.
game will be sponsored by the council.
