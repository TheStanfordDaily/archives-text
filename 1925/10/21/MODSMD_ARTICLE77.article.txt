# DONATIONS ACCEPTED
## 
### 
University of Minnesota has decided
to accept any donations or gifts at any
time so long as they come from legiti
mate sources and are donated for the
purpose of advancing and promoting
the cause of education.
Justice, in the form of duckings in
the icy waters of a nearby lake, was
meted to the unruly freshmen of the
University of Colorado. Twenty-two re
ceived this kind of punishment and
four were obliged to sing in chapel.
The head of the public speaking
department of the University of Den
ver advises students to relax at foot
ball games in order to get the maxi
mum amount of their voices for root
ing.
A portable phonograph and fifty of the
latest dance records were donated to the
University of Washington football team
by a Seattle merchant.
