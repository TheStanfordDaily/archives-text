# Skull and Snakes to Stop Noise at Show
## 
### 
A resolution was passed at a
meeting of Skull and Snakes, honor
ary athletic society, today, whereby
every member of the society is obli
gated to prevent to his fullest
power the spirit of rowdiness that
has been so noticeable at the Thurs
day night vaudeville performance at
the New Stanford theatre.
Dave Oliva, president of the or
ganization, requested that all mem
bers be present at, the show this
evening to take action if it is neces
sary to do so. Arrangements are
being made to secure the sanction
of the theatre management to this
resolution.
