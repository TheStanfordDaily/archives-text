# SKULL AND SNAKES WILL GIVE JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
At a meeting of Skull and Snakes
held yesterday in Breakers Club, the
society decided to give a University
Jolly-up sometime next quarter. Ted
Miller was authorized to request the
Executive Committee for a definite
date for the affair, which wilLprobably
be given early in the quarter.
A1 R. Masters. '23, graduate manager
of the University and former president
of the A. S. S. U., was elected to
membership.
