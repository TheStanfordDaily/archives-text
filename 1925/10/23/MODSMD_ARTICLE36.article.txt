# GRADUATE WOMEN OFFICERS ELECTED
## 
### 
At recent meetings held at Manza
nita and Madrono halls, the graduate
women selected their house officers for
the year.
The officers of Manzanita Club are:
President, Ruth M. Stucky; Secretary-
Treasurer, Fannie Kelton; Members
of the Committee on Standards, Helen
P. Davidson and Louise Carew; Cus
todian, Katherine Ewell. The constitu
tion used last year was adopted, with
the exception that the office of Cus
todian was created this year.
The Madrono Club women chose Dor
othy I. Dahler as their president and
representative in Women's Conference
