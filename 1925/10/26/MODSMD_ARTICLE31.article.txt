# STEINECKE MARRIES
## 
### 
Max Steinecke, '21, now head of the
Geology Research Department of the
Standard Oil Company in Colombia,
South America, was married to Miss
Florence Smith, '21, last Saturday in
San Francisco. Steinecke and his wife
will return to South America after a
three months' automobile trip.

Freshmen at the University of Wash
ington take an oath of allegiance to the
university. This is one of the oldest
traditions there.
