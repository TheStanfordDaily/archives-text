# First Y. W. C. A. Picnic Is Given at Roble Bridge
## 
### 
The first of a series of Saturday
morning picnics given by Y.W.C.A.
was held last week-end at Roble Bridge.
It has been planned to start from Roble
Hall at 7:15 o'clock Saturday morn
ings, go somewhere within easy walk
ing distance, and cook breakfast over a
campfire. The breakfasts cost 25 cents.
All freshmen and uppcrclass women arc
invited to attend.
Anyone interested in these picnics
should communicate with Marietta Fry
at the Kappa Kappa Gamma house or
Hulda Hoover at Roble Ilall. Those
intending to go must turn in their
names by the preceding Thursday even
ing. A hike of five miles or over will
count for W.A.A. points.
