# DAUGHTER BORN
## 
### 
A daughter, Beatrice Dale, has been
born to Mr. and Mrs. Carl Shoup in
New York. Shoup received his A.B. in
law in '24. Mrs. Shoup (Ruth Snedden)
is an ex-member of the class of '26.
Shoup is now attending Columbia
University, taking graduate work in
economics. He is also writing for the
New York World.
