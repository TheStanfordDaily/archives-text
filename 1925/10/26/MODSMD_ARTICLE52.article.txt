# TOWNLEY APPOINTED TO HARVARD POSITION
## 
### 
Professor Sidney D. Townley, pro
fessor of Applied Mathematics, has
been appointed to take charge of the
astronomical laboratory and to teach
astronomy at Harvard University. He
will also lecture in astronomy at Rad
cliffe College.
Professor Townley will be at Har
vard from November to March during
the absence of Professor Harlan T.
Stetson. Professor Stetson is traveling
to Sumatra to study the total eclipse
of the sun on January 14.
