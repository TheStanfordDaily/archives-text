# "WALK-OUT" PARTY PLANNED BY Y. W.C.A.
## 
### 
A "walk-out" was planned for Satur
day morning by the Y. W. C. A. com
mittee on meetings yesterday after
noon in the Women's Clubhouse. The
hike will start from Roble hall at 7
o'clock Saturday and all intending to
go should inform Hulda Hoover before
Thursday.
The Y. W. C. A. will give a party
similar to the Women's Conference
affair in the near future, according to
decision made by the committee yes
terday. Announcement of the date for
the first of these quarterly parties will
be announced soon.
