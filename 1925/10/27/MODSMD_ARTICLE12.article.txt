Tickets are now on sale for the

The banquet is a part of the observ

Foreign relations will be the theme

from the University of California will
be guests of the Stanford Y. W. C. A.
at the banquet. Professor Yamato Ichi
hashi of the History department will
speak on international relations in the
Pacific basin, especially as they con

Professor Graham H. Stuart of the
Political Science department will speak
on our relations with Europe, particu
larly in their connection with student
movements.
Ruth Klettle will sing some foreign
songs, accompanied by Doris Higgins.
Eleanor Davidson will be toastmistress.
