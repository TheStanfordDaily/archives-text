<$» Ted Elias, Jr. <s
Things are happening all around us,
Things are happening, that is true,
And the ones who read the Daily,
Know who they are happening to.
Daily makes a change in office,
Now we'll have a better sheet,
For we've added light and comfort,
And a place to park our feet.
Tuesday's lecture is a concert,
Complicated though it seems,
Indian Love Song and a Flutist,
Bring us near the land of dreams (not
literally).
Women on the Stanford campus,
Hold assembly to decide,
If the point should run the school,
Or if the "line" should still abide.
Men will also hold assembly,
Talking campus problems o'er,
"Dinks" is one of many subjects,
That will occupy the floor.
If the thing came off per schedule,
Songs were sung today at noon,
If, however, something happened,
This announcement's made too soon.
Football show commands attention,
Stunts must all be in this week,
Let us know about it, students,
If you play or dance or speak.
