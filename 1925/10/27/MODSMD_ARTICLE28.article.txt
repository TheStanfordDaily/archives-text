# CANDIDATES FOR DEGREES
## 
### 
Bachelor of Arts
Candidates for the degree of Bache
lor of Arts at the close of the present
quarter should file application at the
Registrar's Office as soon as possible.
Application blanks may be obtained at
the Registrar's Office.

Candidates for the degrees of Mas
ter of Arts and Engineer, who are now
registered for their second quarter of
advanced work, and who expect to
complete their requirements by the
end of the Winter quarter, should file
application at the Registrar's Office
not later than Monday, November 2.
