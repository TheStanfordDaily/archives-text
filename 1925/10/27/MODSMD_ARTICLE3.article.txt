# Campus Service Station Stocks Auto Accessories
## 
### 
A complete line of "Whiz" liquid
auto accessories has been put in stock
by the campus service station beside
the fire-house. Every necessity from
auto enamel to neat's-foot oil can now
be obtained there.
Molds are ready for pouring the
concrete in the new pit being con
structed for crank-case service. The
pit will he used solely for this purpose,
and will not be available to student
mechanics.
