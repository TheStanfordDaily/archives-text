# EX-SOLDIERS STUDY
## 
### 
Only 175 ex-service men are regis
tered in the University of Texas this
semester, whereas a few years ago
over a thousand men who took part in
the World War were enrolled, accord
ing to information given out recently
in the auditor's office. Most of the
ex-service men are registered for
graduate work or courses in the
School of Law.
