# Campus Search Fails to Reveal Missing Cut-Down
## 
### 
A Ford cut-down parked by the li
brary yesterday morning was stolen
while its owner was in class. The Palo
Alto police are working on the case
but have as yet been unable to recover
the automobile.
The Ford, which was highly tuned
up. was first supposed to have been
taken for the morning only. But a
thorough search of the campus failed
to reveal its whereabouts, and the
theft was reported to the Palo Alto
police.
