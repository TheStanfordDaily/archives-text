TUESDAY
RADIO CLUB meets at 8 o'clock in
Corporation Yard. All interested in
amateur radio are invited to attend.
QUAD, Volume 33, Managerial tryoutee
meeting tonight at 7:15.

Union at 7 o'clock.

office.

Stanford will hold its regular bi
monthly testimonial meeting Tues
day evening at 7:30 o'clock on the
second floor of the Women's Club
house. Members of the faculty, stu
dents past and present, and others
associated with the University are
invited to attend.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE will meet
at 7:15 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.

building, 7:30 p.m. Presence of all
members essential.

in order to nominate students for
class offices.

except freshman women.

with Mills College and the College
