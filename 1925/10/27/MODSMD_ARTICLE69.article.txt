# SEQUOIA PLANS DANCE
## 
### 
Sequoia Club will hold its first dance
of the year Friday, October 30; under
the direction of W. Conrad Fernelius,
chairman of the dance committee. No
announcement of the decoration
scheme has been made by James B.
McKim, who will be in charge of the
decorations for the dance. Stanley
Miller will take care of the tickets. It
will be a one o'clock dance.
