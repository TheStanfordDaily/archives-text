# JUNIOR HOCKEY TEAM PLAYS AGAINST 1928
## 
### 
Junior and sophomore women will
play in the first hockey game of the
season tomorrow afternoon, instead of
juniors and seniors, as was announced
yesterday. The game will be played on
the Roble gymnasium field, and will
start at 4:15 o'clock.
Seniors and freshmen will meet in
a practice game tomorrow afternoon
on the Roble gymnasium field, at the
same hour.
