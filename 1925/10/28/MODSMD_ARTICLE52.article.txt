# News-Writing Class Will Visit Chronicle Plant
## 
### 
Twenty members of the newswriting
class will visit the plant of the San
Francisco Chronicle tomorrow afternoon
and Friday night under the direction of
Professor Everett W. Smith of the Jour
nalism division.
The trips have been arranged through
the courtesy of W. H. B. Fowler, '06,
who is the assistant publisher of the
Chronicle. Anyone wishing to visit the
plant with the class may -meet in front
of the Chronicle building, Fifth and
Mission streets, San Francisco, on Thcrs
day afternoon at 2 o'clock, or on Friday
night at 7 o'clock.
