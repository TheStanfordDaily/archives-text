# CARD GOLFERS PLAY SAN JOSE SATURDAY
## 
### 
Ten members of the Stanford Golf
club will meet representatives of the San
Jose Country Club in a match to be
played Saturday, on the San Jose links.
Members selected for the Stanford team
are Dick Lang, captain, Fritz Ditzler,
Eddie Meyberg, Neil Miller, Bob Ray
mond, Bob Cendening, Jim Wilson,
Bob Syer, Ward Edmunds, and Tom
Condit.
Eddie Meyberg is leading in the golf
tournament which the golf club is con
ducting at the Burlingame links. Mey
berg has turned in two scores of 78 for
the eighteen-hole course.
