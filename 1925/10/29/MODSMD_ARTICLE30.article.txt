# DAY'S DOINGS
## Ted Elias, Jr. 
### 
Things are happening all around us,
Winter, Summer, Spring, and Fall,
Read the Daily Palo Alto,
Get the low-down on it all.
Oregon and Stanford tangle,
In a crucial conference fray,
Dopesters doping out the contest,
For a change have lots to say.
They think Stanford ought to score,
As often as the Gold and Blue,
I Provided Nevers, Bogue, and Murphy,
| Hyland, and the rest come through.
! Word comes from "Encina Kennels,"
Of the fire they will make,
And the hopes of California
We intend to burn at stake. 1
Euphronia has made selections
j For a new debating bunch,
I Mills announced as our opponent,
Thousands come and bring their lunch.
And some changes have been published
In the cast of "Goose Hangs High."
"Does College really make you snob
bish?"
Will be answered by and by.
