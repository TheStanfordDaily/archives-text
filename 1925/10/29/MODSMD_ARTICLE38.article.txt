# WOMEN TO SEND FLOWERS
## 
### 
A flower committee has been ap
pointed by the Associated Students to
send flowers and a note to each woman
in the University who is ill more than
one day. A list of the women who are
sick is taken from the Dean of Wo
men's office each day, and flowers are
sent to them in behalf of Associated
Women. Jean Stewart is the chairman
of the committee, which consists of
Virginia Moulton and Matie McLaren.
