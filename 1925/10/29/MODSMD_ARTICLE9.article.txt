# FRESHMEN NOMINATE OFFICERS FOR TERM
## 
### 
Nominations for the officers of the
freshman class were made yesterday
under the supervision of Robert E.
Lewis, President of Associated Stu
dents.
Those nominated for president were
Victor E. Walker, Harry F. Dietrich,
and Samuel S. Hayes; for vice presi
dent, Sidney C. Madden, E. Howard
Marx, nnd Sylva T. Weaver; for secre
tary, James O. Post, Helen E. Bell,
and Richard J. O'Brien; and for treas
urer, John B. Wheatley, Lloyd M.
Smith, and Chester R. Arthur.
President Lewis announced that the
election would take place as soon as
possible after the ballots are printed.
