# STANFORD GOLFERS PREPARE FOR MATCH WITH SAN JOSE CLUB
## 
### 
Stanford golfers, led by Captain
Dick Lang, will meet ten members of
the San Jose Country Club tomorrow
at the San Jose links. Dick Dang, Fritz
Ditzler, Eddie Meyburg, Neil Miller,
Dob Raymond, Jim Wilson, Dob Clen
dening, Ward Edmunds, Dob Syer, and
Tom Condit will represent the Stan
ford Golf Club, while the San Jose
team has not been named.
Play in the fall quarter tournament
being conducted by the golf club will
be completed this week. Eddie Mey
burg, playing his first, year nt Stan
ford, is leading the field in the first
rounds.
