# SEQUOIA CLUB DANCE PLANNED FOR TONIGHT
## 
### 
The annual fall dance of Sequoia
Club will be held tonight at Sequoia
hall. The decoration scheme, to be in
the school colors, has not been re
vealed, but the committee under Con
ard Fernelius has prepared something
new for campus dance attenders.
Tickets for fifty couples have been
sold, and the dance promises to be a
gala affair. Hess' orchestra is engaged
to furnish the music.
A special announcement was made
that punch would not be furnished
for the roughs. The dance is not a
Hallowe'en affair, and will start at
nine o'clock and be over at one.
