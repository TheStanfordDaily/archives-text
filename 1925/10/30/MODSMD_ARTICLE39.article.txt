# WOMEN'S "S" SOCIETY MAKES GAME POMPOMS
## 
### 
Red pompoms for the Big Game will
be made by Women's "S" Society, and
will go on sale a few days before the
Big Game at fifteen cents each.
Last year the society made red and
white pompoms. Because these showed
pink at a distance, the society has de ;
cided to make all red ones this year.
The society will make enough pom
poms for all the women, and ask that
everyone shall buy one.
