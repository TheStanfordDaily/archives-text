MANUFACTURERS
Today, Tomorrow and
Wednesday
Nov. 2, 3 and 4
In these three days we will offer you an opportunity to
buy that long-wanted fur-piece or coat. Or perhaps
you intended getting it this winter. Don't wait! You
will never get this opportunity again this season. Think
of it: a clean, new, large stock to select from, and at a
saving from one-fourth to one-third of what you would
have to pay should we carry them in stock. The furs
in this sale ar supplied to us on consignment by one
of the largest manufacturers on the coast.
Through this arrangement we are able to give you
many thousands of dollars' worth of highest quality
furs from a new stock, and this at prices that will
startle you. Whether they are from the Hudson Bay
or from some far-off iceland country, you will know
just what you are getting when you buy during these
three days.
