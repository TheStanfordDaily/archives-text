# Peninsula Tennis Title Won by Stanford Player
## 
### 
Neer and Lansdale became the
Peninsula Club tennis champions yes
terday by easily* defeating Plymire
and Tussing 0-0, 6-1 in the final round
of the Club's invitational tournament.
Tussing and ISeer both graduated from
Stanford in 1922. Neer played on the
varsity and won the intercollegiate
singles championship in 1921 and, with
Jim Davies, the doubles championship
in 1922. Tussing played on the Stanford
doubles team in 1923 and 1924. The
vistory of Neer and Lansdale yester
day was due largely to the playing of
the former.
