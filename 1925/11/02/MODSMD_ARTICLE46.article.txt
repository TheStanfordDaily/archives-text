# ANNOUNCEMENTS

MONDAY
SOPHOMORES will meet at 12:50 in
Room 214 for the purpose of dis
cussing financial obligations and
plans for Sophomore Week. Attend
ance urgent.
THETA SIGMA PHI will meet Mon
day, November 2, at 4:45 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. All mem
bers are urged to attend.
SOPHOMORE TRYOUTEES for the
Chaparral business staff will meet in
the Chappie office at 8 o'clock to
night.
TRYOUTEES for the business staff of
the "Stanford Literary Magazine"
will meet at 8:15 in the lobby of the
New Union.
