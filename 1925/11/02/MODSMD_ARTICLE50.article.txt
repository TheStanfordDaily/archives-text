# Matthay Announces Sale Of 500 Senior Year Books
## 
### 
More than 500 senior Quads were
sold in the campaign which ended Fri
day. According to Manager F. Lowell
Matthay, this is a record. Almost all
of the senior class was covered in the
sale.
Appointments for senior pictures
are being made at the rate of 25 every
day. Matthay says that all senior pic
tures will he taken within the next
two weeks.
Seniors may make their appoint
ments personally with Stone's, on the
Circle, by telephone. His number is
Palo Alto 51-M.
