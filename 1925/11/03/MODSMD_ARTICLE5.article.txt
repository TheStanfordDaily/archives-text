# WORLD FELLOWSHIP WILL BE DISCUSSED AT Y.W.C.A. DINNER
## 
### 
World fellowship will be the
theme of the banquet to be given
by the Y. W. C. A. at 6:15 o'clock
tonight at the Union. Professor
Yamato Ichihashi of the History
department and Professor Graham
H. Stuart of the Political Science
department will speak on various
phases of the United States' foreign
relations. Six foreign students from
the University of California will be
guests of the Y. W. C. A. at the
banquet.
World Fellowship Week is an in
ternational institution of the Y. W.
C. A. Students throughout the
world observe it through their Y. W.
C. A. organizations.
Eleanor Davidson will be toast
mistress at the banquet tonight.
Ruth Klette will sing, with Doris
Higgins as her accompanist.
