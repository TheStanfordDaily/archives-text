# A. C. Mahr Will Speak About Recent German Paintings
## 
### 
Dr. A. C. Mahr will talk ou "German
Paintings of the Nineteenth Century"
at a meeting of the German Club to
be held tomorrow night in the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock.
The meetings of the organization
will be held regularly hereafter on the
first and third Thursdays of each
month, under the direction of Walter
Gieseke, president. The other officers
for this quarter are Rose Trautz, vice
president, and Hartley Devlin, secre
tary and treasurer.
