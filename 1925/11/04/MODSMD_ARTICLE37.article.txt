# University Weekly Calendar

WEDNESDAY, NOVEMBER 4
3:00 p.m. Residential Alumnae Tea,
in the Women's Clubhouse.
7:30 p.m. Open Forum, under the
auspices of Euphronia Debating
Society, in the lobby of the Old
Union. Address on "Can Good
ness Be Legislated?" by Mr. John
L. McNab. The public is invited.
THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 5
4:15 p.m. Organ Recital, by Mr. Al
len, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. German Club, in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Program in Ger
man. All interested are invited
to attend.
8:15 p.m. Concert, by Anna Case,
soprano, under the auspices of
the Peninsula Musical Associa
tion; in the Assembly hall.
FRIDAY, NOVEMBER 6
8:00 p.m. Faculty Club, in the Fac
ulty Clubhouse.
8:15 p.m. "The Goose Hangs High,"
drama by Lewis Beach, presented
by Sword and Sandals Society;
in the Assembly hall.
