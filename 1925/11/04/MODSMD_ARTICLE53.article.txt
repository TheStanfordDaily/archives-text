WEDNESDAY
RADIO CLUB will meet at 8 o'clock
in the Corporation Yard shack.
SIGMA DELTA CHI will meet at 7:30
o'clock upstairs in the Press build
ing.
THURSDAY
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY of
Stanford will give a free lecture on
Christian Science by Peter V. Ross,
C.5.8., of San Francisco, member of
the board of lectureship of the
Mother Church, The First Church of
Christ, Scientist, in Boston, Mass.,
in the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30
o'clock. Faculty, students, alumni,
and their friends are invited to
attend.
SKULL AND SNAKES meeting has
been indefinitely postponed.
ALL USHERS are to report at the
Assembly hall at 7:30 o'clock tonight
for the Anna Case concert.

for "The Goose Hangs High."
