# JOURNALISTS CLUB HAS OPEN MEETING FOR TAYLOR TALK
## 
### 
Frank J. Taylor will discuss "The Job
of the Reporter" at an open meeting of
the Stanford Journalists' Club tonight at
the home of Professor Everett W. Smith,
12 Alvarado Row. He plans to introduce
j the various types of reporters' jobs, and
discuss their similarities and differences.
Taylor began his newspaper career here
at Stanford, and was elected editor of the
j Daily Palo Alto in 1917. After his war
experiences he returned to the United
States and became engaged in building up
the United News service. Taylor is now
advertising manager of the Yoscmite
Park and Camp Curry Company.
