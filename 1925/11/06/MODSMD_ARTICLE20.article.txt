# CARDS BEGIN POLO SEASON WITH ARMY CONTEST TOMORROW
## 
### 
Stanford will begin its polo season
tomorrow with a contest with the 30th
Infantry of San Francisco. The game
will be held at 1:30 on the highway
field at Stanford.
The starting line-up for Stanford
will be: No. 1. Krohn; No. 2, Dorcy;
No. 3, Vanderbugh; No. 4, Captain
Trotter. The San Francisco team is re
ported to be very strong, and a closely
contested match is expected according
to Major John Keliher, who has been
coaching the Stanford squad this year.
Stanford will play a return match
with the 30th Infantry next Monday
at the highway field.
