### 
Endorsing as individuals the en
trance of the United States into the
World Court, the cabinet of the Y. M.
C. A., at its last meeting, voted to
co-operate with such organizations as
the League of Nations Non-Partisan
Association and Alpha Pi Zeta which
are planning meotings and forums for
the discussion of the World Court is
sue.
This is a part of a nation-wide move

fostered by the National Council of
Christian Associations looking toward
effective registry of student opinion at
the time of the Senate debate on the
World Court December 17.
