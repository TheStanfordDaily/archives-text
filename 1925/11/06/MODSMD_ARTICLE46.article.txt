# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
ALL USHERS are to report at the
Assembly hall at 7:30 o'clock tonight
for "The Goose Hangs High."
SUNDAY
FRESHMAN STUDENTS will meet
this evening at G:3O o'clock in En
cina Lobby to hear Charles 13. Wing,
professor of Civil Engineering,
speak.
PROFESSOR EUGENE W. LYMAN of
the Theological Seminary of New
York City will be the speakor at
vesper service in the Presbyterian
church in Palo Alto.
MONDAY
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30.
MANAGERIAL TRYOUTEES of the
Stanford Literary Magazine meet at
8:15 o'clock in the lobby of the
Union.
TUESDAY
SKULL AND SNAKES will meet at
noon at the Kappa Sigma house. New
members will be elected.
1929 CLASS MEMBERS will elect of
ficers tonight. Men will vote at En
cina from 7 to 8:15 and the women
at Roble from 7:15 to 8.
