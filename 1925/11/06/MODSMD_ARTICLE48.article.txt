# DOCTOR OF THEOLOGY TO SPEAK ON "FAITH"
## 
### 
Eugene W. Lyman, professor of
Philosophy of Religion in the Union
Theological Seminary, New York City,
will speak at the vesper service of the
Presbyterian Church in Palo Alto on
Sunday afternoon. The subject of his
address will be: "Faith; that by which
men live."
In the Young People's meeting to be
held after the service Professor Isaac
J. Cox of the History department will
speak on student life in Latin lands.
An open forum on the question of the
"Value of Foreign Missions" will close
the meeting.
