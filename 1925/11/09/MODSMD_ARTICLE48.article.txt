# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30.
MANAGERIAL TRYOUTEES of the
Stanford Literary Magazine meet at
8:15 o'clock in the lobby of the
Union.
CHESS CLUB dinner and smoker in
the Trophy room of the Old Union
at 7:15.
DE MOLAY meeting will be held in
Palo Alto Masonic Temple at 8
o'clock tonight.
TUESDAY
SKULL AND SNAKES will meet at
noon at the Kappa Sigma house. New
members will be elected.
1929 CLASS MEMBERS will elect of
ficers tonight. Men will vote at En
cina from 7 to 8:15 and the women
at Roble from 7:15 to 8.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at 6:45'
o'clock tonight in the Trophy room
of the Old Union.
WOMEN'S ECONOMICS CLUB will;
meet at 5:15 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. It is important that all
members attend.
SIGMA DELTA CHI meets at 7:30 up
stairs in the Press building.
DR. RICHARD LARUE SWAIN will
speak at morning chapel today at
7:50 o'clock.
MUSIC CLUB tryoutees will meet at
8 o'clock tonight in the Women's'
Clubhouse.
WEDNESDAY
ALPHA KAPPA DELTA will meet at
8:*30 tonight at the Women's Club
house.

QUAD editorial tryoutee meetings will!
hereafter be held at 1:15 o'clock Fri-!
