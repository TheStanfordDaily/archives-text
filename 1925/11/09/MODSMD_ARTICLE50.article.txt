# ST. MARY'S TO BUILD
## 
### 
Buildings for St. Mary's College will
be erected at a cost of $3,000,000 on
the Foothill Boulevard near San Lean
dro. Plans have been approved which
provide for a faculty clubhouse, an ad
ministration building, a students' hall,
two dormitories, a union building, a
power plant, and a gymnasium. Six
additional buildings will be construct
ed at a later date.
