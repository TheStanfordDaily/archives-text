# Y. W. C. A. Starts Campaign To Enlarge Membership
## 
### 
Women of the Y. W. C. A. member
ship committee canvassed women's liv
ing groups today in a campaign for
new members. The campaign will end
Wednesday evening.
The Y. W. C. A. plans to acquaint
the women of the campus with its
work, and to expand its membership
so as to take in most of the women
at Stanford this year.
