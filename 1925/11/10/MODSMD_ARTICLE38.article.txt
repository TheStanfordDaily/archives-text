# DAY'S DOINGS
## 
### Ted Elias, Jr 
Snakes discovered in Sequoia,
Frosh is found who wears his dink.
Studying for raid-term Ex's
Must be driving us to drink.
Law will throw its annual smoker,
It is set for Wednesday night.
There will gather future lawyers,
Who'll determine wrong from right.
And tonight there is a lecture
For the students of our College,
Who have serious intentions
Of absorbing extra knowledge.
Team returns from northern journey
And receives a great ovation;
Stanford rooters show some spirit
In a rousing demonstration.
Sophs announce that their production
Is to be a mystery;
Thursday starts the sale of tickets.
Here's a play you ought to see.
All automobiles parked on the Kan
sas Agricultural College campus must
have a parking tag.
