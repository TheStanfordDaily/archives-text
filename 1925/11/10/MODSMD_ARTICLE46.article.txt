# PLANS FOR MATCHES MADE BY CHESS CLUB
## 
### 
Chess matches with the University
of Oklahoma, Columbia University, and
California Agricultural College will he
held in the near future, according to an
announcement made at a meeting of the
Cardinal Chess Club last night in the
Union. The regular meeting was fol
lowed by refreshments and informal
chess matches.
The match with Oklahoma is assured,
and the various moves will be ex
changed by correspondence, commenc
ing immediately. The two other matches
arc only tentative at present. The regu
lar varsity matches with California will
not be held until spring according to
present plans.
