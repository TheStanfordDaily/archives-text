TUESDAY
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY of
Stanford will hold its regular bi
monthly testimonial meeting this
evening at 7:30 o'clock on the sec
ond floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
Faculty members, students, and oth
ers associated with the University
are invited to attend.
ALL RESERVES are to report from
now on for scrimmage against the
varsity.
SKIM A DELTA CHI will meet at 7:30
upstairs in the Press building. At
tendance important.

spea.k tomorrow at morning chapel
at 7:40 o'clock.
QUAD managerial tryoutees will meet
in the Quad office in the Administra
tion building tonight at 7:15.
TRYOUTS for the Sophomore Play
will be held in the Assembly hall
from 3 to 5 o'clock this afternoon,
and from 7 to 9 o'clock this evening
ip the Little Theatre.
SKULL AND SNAKES will meet at
noon at the Kappa Sigma house. New
members will be elected.

ficers tonight. Men will vote at En
cina from 7 to 8:15 and the women
at Roble from 7:15 to 8.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet at 6:45
o'clock tonight in the Trophy room
of the Old Union.
WOMEN'S ECONOMICS CLUB will
meet at 5:15 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. It is important that all
itiembers attend.
SIGMA DELTA CHI meets at 7:30 up
stairs in the Press building.

speak at morning chapel today at
7:50 o'clock.

8 o'clock tonight in the Women's
Clubhouse.
