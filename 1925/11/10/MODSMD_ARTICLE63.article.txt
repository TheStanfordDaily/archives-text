# MUSIC CLUB TO HOLD MEMBERSHIP TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Music Club
will be held tonight at 8 o'clock on the
main floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
Those trying out should be prepared
with two selections, either vocal or in
strumental.
The purpose of the music club is
to promote better music among the
students. Concerts are given yearly,
and preparations are under way for
this year's program.
The officers for this year are: Lois
McQuistion, president; Robert Hefner,
vice-president; Phyllis Hogan, secre
tary and treasurer.
