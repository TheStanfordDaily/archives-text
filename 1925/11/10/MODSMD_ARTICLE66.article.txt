# MOREY'S RENDEZVOUS TO OPEN NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Morey's Smoke-shop will make its
first step in the formation of a "Smoke-
Shop" chain with the opening of the
new "Morey's Rendezvous" next Mon
day. The new shop will be located on
University Avenue near the Stanford
Theatre.
Hot lunches are to be served at all
times, in addition to the regular foun
tain service, according to "Morey." The
management will cater to both men and
women of Palo Alto and the campus.
In the rear of a front room will be a
pool room containing a snooker table,
a billiard table, and three pool tables.
A stock of pipes and tobacco similar to
that of the present "Smoke Shop" will
be carried in the new "Rendezvous."
