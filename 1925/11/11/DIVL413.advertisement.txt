I! Who? John Doe? I
Most everyone despises the writer of an anonymous
letter. But did you ever stop to think that the maker
or seller of unadvertised merchandise is handing you
an anonymous product?
Those merchants who are not afraid of the quality of
their goods do not hesitate to recommend them to the
public through advertisements. Thus the are bound to
sell only high-class goods in order to retain their trade |
I and reputation. They could not afford to advertise
products and then offer for sale goods of an inferior
quality.
The advertisement is your insurance against poor
merchandise. 1
The Daily Palo Alto
