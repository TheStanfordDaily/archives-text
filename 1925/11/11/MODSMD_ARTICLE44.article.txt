# Skull and Snakes Plans Winter Quarter Jolly-Up
## 
### 
Discussion of plans for a jolly-up to
be given next quarter was the chief
business of the Skull and Snakes meet :
ing held yesterday noon at the Kappa
Sigma house. The date has not yet
been set.
Election of new members, which
was to have been completed yesterday,
was postponed until after football sea
son, when it is felt a more representa
tive group of members can be chosen.
