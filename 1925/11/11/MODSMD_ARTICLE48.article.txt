# CARDINAL ORCHESTRA WILL HOLD REHEARSAL
## 
### 
The newly formed Stanford orches
tra will hold a rehearsal tonight at
7:30 o'clock in Room 200 of the Eng
lish building. Helen Engel Atkinson is
directing the orchestra and instructing
the musicians in the fundamentals of
ensemble playing, sight reading of
scores, and interpretation.
Mrs. Atkinson is the only woman
member of the first violin section of
the San Francisco Symphony Orches
tra, directed by Alfred Hertz. She is
beginning her third year in the orches
tra, and is well versed in the require
ments of ensemble playing.
