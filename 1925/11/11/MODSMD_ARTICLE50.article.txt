# 1928 DUES PAYABLE
## 
### 
Sophomore dues for the first half of
the college year will be payable to
morrow at 1 o'clock in the Administra
tion building. Dues will be one dollar,
as assessed at a recent meeting of the
sophomore class, and must be paid
before class members may purchase
tickets to the Cotillion.
William H. Hubbard, Treasurer '28.
