# STANFORD DAMES TO GIVE ONE-ACT PLAY
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will present a one
act play, "The Antidote," at the Wo
men's Clubhouse tomorrow evening at
8 o'clock. Readings by Mrs. C. G. Os
borne, vocal selections by Mrs. S. Han
sen, and piano selections by Mrs. H.
C. Pepper are also included in the
program.
The next regular meeting of the
Stanford Dames is scheduled for next
Thursday afternoon at 3 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
