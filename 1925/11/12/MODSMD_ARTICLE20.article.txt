# KNOX, ROGERS ATTEND CONVENTION IN NORTH
## 
### 
Wallace W. Knox and Hershey Rog
ers, editor and business manager of
; the Daily Palo Alto, left the campus
last night to attend the annual con
vention of the Pacific Intercollegiate
Press Association. The convention is
being held this year on the campus
of the University of Oregon at. Eugene.
The convention will be in session
Friday and Saturday. Knox and Rogers
expect to return to Stanford Sunday
night.
