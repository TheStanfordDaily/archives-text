Branch; in the Little Theatre.

gins of Modern International
Law," by Mr. Jackson H. Ralston.

of Professor and Mrs. E. D.
Adams, 7 Cabrillo, Campus. All
graduate students in History are
invited.

Dr. Vittorio Putti. Subject: "New

Francisco.
