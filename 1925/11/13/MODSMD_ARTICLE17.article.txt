# BASSETT WILL SPEAK TO FRESHMAN CLASS
## 
### 
Professor L. E. Bassett, of the De
partment of English and Public Speak
ing, will address members of the
freshman class Sunday evening at 6:30
in the Encina lobby.
The meeting will be the third of a
series arranged by officials of the
class for the purpose of acquainting
freshmen with the members of the
University faculty.
These meetings are all made as in
fomal as possible, and in carrying out
this idea, the professors announce no
set subject for their talks. Professor
Bassett will speak of his experiences
as instructor of public speaking here.
