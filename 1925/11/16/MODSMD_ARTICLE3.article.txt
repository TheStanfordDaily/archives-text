# Test Course Applications Available to Engineers
## 
### 
All mechanical and electrical engi
neering students who are interested in
taking the testing course offered by
the General Electric Company, Sche
nectady, New York, are urged to file
their applications immediately at the
Appointment office. These interview
blanks are for statistical purposes only
and do not bind the students in any
way, according to Mrs. Elizabeth Snell.
These applications are for next fall
and early applications will be given
priority of claim. The representatives
sent out by the General Electric Com
pany to interview applicants will be
at Stanford early in the winter quar
ter.
