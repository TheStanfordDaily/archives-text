# Professor Guerard Will Give Address Tonight
## 
### 
Albert Leon Guerard, professor of
General Literature, will speak on the
French educational system at a meet
ing of the Education Club at 8 o'clock
tonight in the Women's Clubhouse. His
lecture is the second in a series of
programs on foreign educational work.
The first was on phases of Philippine
Island education. Tonight's meeting is
open to all interested in the teaching
profession and its branches. Harold
Benjamin, president, will preside.
