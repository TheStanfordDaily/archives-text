# CLUB INVITES STUDENTS
## 
### 
Visiting Stanford students and
alumni who will be in San Francisco
before the Big Game are invited to
be guests of the San Francisco Stan
ford Women's Club, which will hold
open house at 233 Post street, Novem
ber 20. Mrs. James Lanagan, assisted
by wives of former football stars, will
be the hostesses. This will be the last
meeting of the club at the present
headquarters, as the Nurses' Training
School of the Stanford Hospital will
be used in the future.
