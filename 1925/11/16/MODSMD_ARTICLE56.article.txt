# 400,000 EXAM BOOKS FOR OHIO
## 
### 
Four hundred thousand examination
books have been ordered by Ohio State
University in preparation for a siege
of mid-term and final examinations.
One-half of these are four-leaf and the
other are six-leaf pamphlets. Two mil
lion sheets of paper are used every
year by the university, and the ap
proximate cost of the books is $2500.
