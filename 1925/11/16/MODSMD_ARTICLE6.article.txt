# Women Rooters' Pompoms To Go on Sale Tomorrow
## 
### 
i
Pompoms made under the auspices |
of the Women's "S" Society for the j
Big Game will go on sale in Roble I
hall and the various women's living
groups tomorrow. Four hundred pom- j
poms were made and will be sold for '
fifteen cents each.
Since pompoms made of red and i
white last year produced the effect of !
pink at a distance, the ones this year :
are made entirely of red. Millison
Hardy Is in charge of making the pom- \
poms and Jean Ward will conduct the
sale of them.
