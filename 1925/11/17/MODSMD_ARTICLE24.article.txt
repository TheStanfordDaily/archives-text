# ROBLE WILL BE HOST AT TEA TOMORROW
## 
### 
Roble Club will be hostess at the
clubhouse tea tomorrow afternoon. All
women in the University are cordially
invited to be present. It. is hoped that
girls will plan to engage bridge tables
before tea. These may be had for $1.50
for the whole table, including tea ser
vice. Tea for individuals will be 25
cents.
This is one of the series of teas that
are given every quarter to help remove
the Women's Clubhouse debt, and to
give undergraduate students, graduate
students, and women members of the
faculty a chance to meet and become
better acquainted.
University of Missouri is now offer
ing a course in canoeing.
