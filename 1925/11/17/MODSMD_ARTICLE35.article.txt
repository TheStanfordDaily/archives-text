# COVERING PLANNED
## 
### 
The football playing field of the Uni
versity of Illinois at Champaign will
be covered with a rubberized material,
protecting it from rain. George Huff,
athletic director, is planning to install
this covering at a cost of $10,000 or
$12,000. A similar covering is at pres
ent used by the major league baseball
clubs.
