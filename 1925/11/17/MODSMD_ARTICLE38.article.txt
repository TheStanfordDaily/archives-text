# TWO CHESS MATCHES BEGUN BY CARDINALS
## 
### 
Correspondence chess matches with
the University of Oklahoma and the
University of California began this
evening when the first official moves
were mailed by the Stanford teams, as
a part of the meeting of the Cardinal
Chess Club in the Union.
In the match with Oklahoma two
teams are playing. Fred W. Speers
and Samuel W. French will maneuver
with the whites, while the blacks will
be under the direction of Adin D. Hen
derson and Ervin H. Bramhall.
A chess board will be posted in the
Bookstore window and all moves in the
California match will be recorded there.
Moves will be decided upon by the
club as a whole.
