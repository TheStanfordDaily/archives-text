# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
JNIPERO SERRA will give a 7 to 8
o'clock dance in the Women's Club
house.
PROGRAM COMMITTEE for the
Sophomore Cotillion will meet at 7
o'clock at Roble.
QUAD editorial tryoutees will hold an
important meeting tonight at 7:30
o'clock.
THURSDAY
FINAL SALE of tickets for the Sopho
more Cotillion will be held from 1
to 4 in the Administration building.
Tickets will be sold to all Stanford
students regardless of class affilia
tion.
SALESWOMEN for Chaparral will
meet at the Kappa Kappa Gamma
house at 12:45 o'clock.
