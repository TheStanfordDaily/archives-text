# BOARD OF CONTROL SEVERELY CENSURES RUSHING VIOLATIONS
## 
### 
Christmas and Thanksgiving holiday
rushing was severely censured last
night, in the report of the Board of
Control given before the regular meet
ing of the Interfratcrnity Council. Be
lieving rushing during the holidays
contrary to the spirit of the rushing
system, the Board asked all houses to
refrain from rushing parties until the
official opening of the rushing season.
The Board also heartily approved
the action of the houses in reporting
any violations of the rushing system to
the council for punishment, but it was
urged that should any violations be
discovered before spring, that they be
reported to the council now and not
next spring.
