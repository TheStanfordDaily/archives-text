# Women's Council Sanctions Hotels for Big Game Eve
## 
###

night of the Big Game, Women's Coun
cil recommends the following hotels
for Stanford women in San Francisco:
Fairmont, St. Francis, Palace; Oak
land, Oakland; in San Jose, Vendome;
in Palo Alto, Cardinal; and all campus
dances.
Late leave up to 2:30< for the Rig
Game night may be granted on appli
