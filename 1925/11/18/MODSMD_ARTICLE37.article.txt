# STANFORD CLUBS OF EAST PLAN DINNER
## 
### 
The Stanford University Club of
New York, New Jersey, and Connecti
cut will hold its Big Game dinner Satur
day at the International House, 500
Riverside Drive, New York City. The
club had first intended to use the Town
Tavern, but it was not large enough.
KYW, Chicago and KDKA, Pitts
burgh will broadcast the game.
The International House is the first
building in the world given over to the
promotion of peace through fellowship.
It is a magnificent building having as
its occupant-members men and women
graduate students. These students,
from 71 countries, number 1100, and
have included Stanford men and women.
