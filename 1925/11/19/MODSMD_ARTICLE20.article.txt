# Lucie Bentley Will Play Julia in 1929 Production
## 
### 
The name of Lucie Bentley, who is
to have the part of Julia in "In the
Next Room/' the sophomore play to
he presented December 6, was omitted
from the cast published yesterday.
Miss Bentley created favorable com
ment by her interpretation of the role
of Mrs. Coade in "Dear Brutus," both
in the original production given last
summer and the repeat performance
presented here October 23. She is a
transfer from the University of Ore
gon.
