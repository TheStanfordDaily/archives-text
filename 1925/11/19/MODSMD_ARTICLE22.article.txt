# San Mateo Man Follows Team 5,000 Miles in Year
## 
### 
D. A. Raybould, San Mateo realtor,
has followed the Stanford football
team over five thousand miles this
year. He saw the Stanford-Notre Dame
game at Los Angeles on New Year's
Day, and he has followed them
throughout this season, taking in the
University of Southern California and
Washington games, together with
every game that the team has played
in its own stadium.
