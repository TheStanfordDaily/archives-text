# House Party Scheduled By Y.W.C.A. for January
## 
###

at Asilomar for Stanford women will
be held late in January, according to
an announcement made last evening
! by the senior cabinet of the associa
! tion. Earlier in the same month the
; three cabinets, freshman, sophomore,
; and senior, will have a house party

jonly. There is no junior cabinet. Jun
ior women work on the senior cabinet.
