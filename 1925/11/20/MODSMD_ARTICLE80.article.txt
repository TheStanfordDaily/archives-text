# Denny, Varsity Trainer, Is Full-Blooded Indian
## 
### 
Trainer Wallace Denny, the man en
trusted with the physical well-being
of the Cardinal football machine, is a
full-blooded Oneida Indian. Denny
came to Stanford with the new regime
under Coach "Pop" Warner.
Denny has been the trainer for
"Pop" for many years, first working for
him at Carlisle Indian School when
Warner was turning out his famous
Indian teams which attracted so much
attention because of their victories
over the largest universities. Denny
himself is a former football player,
Vaving been one of Warner's Carlisle
players about a quarter of a century
ago.
