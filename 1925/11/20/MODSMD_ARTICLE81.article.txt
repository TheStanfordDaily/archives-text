# BEQUEATHS ESTATE
## 
### 
Miss Esther Ann Horwood has be
queathed half of her estate of $13,000
to the Stanford Convalescent Home,
according to a will admitted to pro
bate yesterday by Superior Judge
Frank H. Dunne of San Francisco.
Miss Horwood died October 19 at the
age of 70 years. Provision for a war
memorial of $500 near Miss Horwood's
old home in England was also made in
the will.
