# FINAL TICKET SALE HELD FOR COTILLION
## 
### 
A final sale of tickets for the Sopho
more Cotillion and Yachting Party will
be held next Tuesday. Several tickets
were left over from sale yesterday.
Contracts for the decorations and
music for the Cotillion have been let
this week. Rudy Seiger, who has re
cently returned from an European
tour, will furnish the music for the
occasion with his concert orchestra.
The decoration committee for the
Cotillion has contracted with a San
Francisco decorator.
