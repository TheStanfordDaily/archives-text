# DATES ARE SUPPLIED
## 
### 
Dates are to be supplied for the
University of lowa football players
after their game with the University
of Southern California next Saturday,
November 21. The committee in charge
of getting the girls for the warriors
from the East claim that they will
have the very keenest girls on the
campus. The fellows at Southern Cali
fornia do not appreciate this plan very
much, and are planning on retaliating
by stepping out with girls from South
ern Branch, University of California.
