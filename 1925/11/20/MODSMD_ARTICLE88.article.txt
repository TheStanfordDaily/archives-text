# ALUMNI TO SPONSOR ANNUAL DINNER DANCE
## 
### 
Alumni of Stanford are sponsoring
the sixth annual dinner dance which
will be held at the Fairmont Hotel fol
lowing the Big Game. The Stanford
team and coaches will be present at
the dinner.
Fifteen hundred guests are expected
to attend the dinner. Arrangements for
the affair have been made under the
direction of John E. MacDowell, secre
tary of the Stanford Alumni Associa
tion.
