# BUFFET LUNCHEON HELD
## 
### 
Students living in Encina, Toyon,
and Brainier halls, who are not regu
larly boarding at Encina Commons,
are invited to bring their guests to a
buffet luncheon which will be served
in Dining Room 8, from 11:30 to noon
tomorrow. A charge of 50 cents a plate
will be charged. The number of tickets
is limited, and early reservations are
advised.
