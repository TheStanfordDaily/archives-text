# WARNER GOES EAST
## 
### 
Coach Warner left for the East yes
terday on the Overland Limited where
he will attend the Cornell-Pennsylvania
clash. Andy Smith departed on the
same train, meeting Pop as the limited
left the Berkeley station. The two
rival coaches made the first bet for
the 1928 game, at Sacramento, each
man advancing $100 in support of the
merits of his respective team.
