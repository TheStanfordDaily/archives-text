# JAPANESE FISHERIES DIRECTOR ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
Dr. Yojiro Wakiya, director of fish
eries for Korea, returned with Dr. Jor
dan from the Hawaiian Islands. He is
here for the purpose of studying and
comparing Hawaiian and American
forms of marine life.
Dr. Wakiya has written a book on
the Garangidae of Japan, a large group
of semi-tropical food fishes.
Dr. Jordan brought back with him
from Hawaii ten species of fish, some
of them new and unclassified. He left
them at the Academy of Sciences at
Golden Gate Park, to be classified
and written up by Dr. Evermann and
Eric Jordan.
