# GRADUATE WOMEN TO HOLD TEA TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Graduate Women's Club will hold
its regular weekly tea from 4:30 to 6
o'clock tomorrow afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse. Music will be fur
nished by the Graduate Women's quartet,
and a discussion of plans for the Christ
mas party will be held.
The Christmas party will be in the form
of a supper held in the Women's Club
house from 6 to 7:30 o'clock December 8.
Each member will bring a box supper, and
these will be sold by auction to those pres
ent. The object of this sale is to pro
vide for an informal gathering of the
members, and to add to the fund for the
club's weekly teas. The committee in
charge is composed of Ruth M. Stucky,
chairman; Bertha L. Shedd, Meribeth E.
Cameron, and Dorothy G. Burgess.
