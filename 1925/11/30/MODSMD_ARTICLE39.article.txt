# Past Assistant Registrar Will Leave for New Post
## 
### 
Charles Baldwin Goddard, assistant
registrar at Stanford for the past four
years, leaves tomorrow to take up
his new duties as chief registrar at
the San Jose State Teachers' College,
to which position he was appointed
a month ago.
Goddard's successor at Stanford has
been appointed, it is announced, hut
the name of appointee has not yet
been made public by University au
thorities.
