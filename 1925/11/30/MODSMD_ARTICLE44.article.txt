# High Voltage Laboratory Is Subject of Pamphlet
## 
### 
"The High-Voltage Laboratory, a Rec
ognition of Public Service" is the subject
of the November issue of the "Concerning
Stanford" leaflet issued by the University.
Copies of this bulletin may be obtained at
the president's office or from the endow
ment secretary.
" The Harris J. Ryan Laboratory at
Stanford, a gift of a number of the great
electric and power companies of the coun
try tu the University, is a monument to
the realization of the mutual benefits of
co-operation between pure science and the
practical application of that science in
modern life," declares the article.
