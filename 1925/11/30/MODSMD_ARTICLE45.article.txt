# EXPEDITION PLANNED
## 
### 
An American expedition backcd by John
D. Rockefeller, Jr., will excavate Arma
geddon, the most famous battlefield of an
cient times. Plans are being made at the
Oriental Museum of the University of
Chicago by Professor James H. Breasted,
famous Chicago archaeologist, to begin
work in the near future. More than 400
workmen will be employed, directed by
Professors Breasted and D. F. Higgins
of the University of Chicago, and C. S.
Fisher, formerly of the University of
Pennsylvania. The three professors will
leave for Palestine soon to make prelim
inary surveys of the ground and to select
suitable camp sites.
