# Dr. Bailey Will Speak at Y. W. C. A. Open Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. Margery Bailey, instructor in
English, will give some ballad read
ings at the open social meeting of the
Y. W. C. A., to be held at 7:15 o'clock
the evening of December 9. The meet
ing is being given as a get-together
for all women of the University before
vacation.
Three stunts will be presented in
the second-floor room of the Club
house, and afterward the meeting will
move to the clubroom downstairs for
the readings. Refreshments will bo
served later in the evening.
