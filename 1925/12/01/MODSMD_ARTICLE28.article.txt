# DAY'S DOINGS
## Ted Elias, Jr. 
### 
Yachting party is called off, for
Reasons that I hereby state,
Insufficient sale of tickets
And we could not get a date.
Bassett speaks on Rudyard Kipling,
At the lecture Tuesday night,
If you miss these cultured meetings,
You are proving Barnum's right.
Sale of tickets starts on Wednesday
For the Soph'more mystery play.
Who committed all the murders?
Advertising posters say.
Study plan has been suggested
Whereby Seniors cut their classes.
This will be installed at Stanford
When the ocean's green molasses.
And the lads of '27
Beat the boys of '26,
Thereby leaving Senior backers
In a bad financial fix.
