WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 2

the Women's Clubhouse.

ing Society vs. Mills College, in
the lobby of the Old Union. Sub
ject: "Resolved, that the new
freedom of the younger genera

ulty Clubhouse. Papers: "Practi
cal Problems in Price Forecast
ing," by Mr. Holbrook Working;

Other New Resins," by Professor
George B. Frankforter.
