# MILITARY SOCIETY TO HOLD SMOKER TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Scabbard and Blade military so
ociety will hold a smoker at 7 o'clock to
night in honor of the commissioned cadet
officers of the Stanford R.O.T.C. and the
new instructors. The smoker will be held
at the military headquarters.
There are thirty commissioned officers
in the cadet corps this quarter. The new
instructors are Major John Keliher, Cap
tain Frank J. Atwood, and Lieutenant
Charles S. Whitmore.
