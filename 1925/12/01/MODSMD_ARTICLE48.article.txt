# Stanford Band Will Play At Christmas Party Friday
## 
### 
The Stanford Band will march and play
at the Palo Alto merchants' annual Christ
mas party from 7:30 to 9:30 o'clock on
Friday night. Money received for this
service will be used to help pay for the
new uniforms purchased for the band this
year.
Seven hundred dollars was expended to
buy new uniforms for the California
game. Of this amount $400 was given by
the Board of Athletic Control and the
band will pay for the remainder of the ex
pense. The band will appear in Palo Alto
Friday night in uniform and all ninety
six members are expected to be in line.
