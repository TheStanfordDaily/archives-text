# "DISRAELI" SUCCESSFUL
## 
### 
"Disraeli," presented at the Wash
ington State College, met with such
success that the extension department
of that institution sponsored its pre
sentation in Spokane a short time
later.

A student ballot at Yale University
recently showed that campus senti
ment was against compulsory chapel,
one of the oldest traditions of the
school.

Northwestern University plans the
erection of a $1,000,000 three-decked
stadium with a seating capacity of
75,000 persons.
