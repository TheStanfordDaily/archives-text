# NOTICE
## 
### 
Men now living in Toyon, Brauner,
and Sequoia halls who wish to re
serve their rooms for winter quarter
should call at the Dean of Men's office
Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday, De
cember 7, 8, or 9, and pay deposits.
Drawings will be held at the officer
of the respective halls at 7:30 p.m.,
Thursday, December 10.

Sixty-five years is the limit set by
trustees of Liverpool University for
the age of professors.
