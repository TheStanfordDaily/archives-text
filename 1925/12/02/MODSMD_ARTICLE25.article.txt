# University Weekly Calendar

THURSDAY, DECEMBER 3
3:15 p.m.—Stanford Dames, in the
Women's Clubhouse.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital, by Mr.
Allen, in the Memorial Church.
4:15 p.m.—Jordan Club, Room 460,
Botany Building. Seniors, gradu
ates, and faculty in Biology are
invited.
7:30 p.m.—Public Meeting, in the As
sembly Hall. Subject: "The
World Court." Speakers: Advo
cates and opponents of American
adherence.
FRIDAY, DECEMBER 4
9:00 a.m. —University Assembly, in
the Assembly Hall, Address by
President William L. Bryan of
the University of Indiana.
4:15 p.m.—Women's Education Club,
in the Women's Clubhouse.
4:30 p.m.—Cap and Gown, at the
home of Mrs. D. Charles Gardner,
la Alvarado.
8:00 p.m.—Faculty Club, in the Fac
ulty Clubhouse.
SATURDAY, DECEMBER 5
S: 15 p.m.—"ln the Next Room," Mys
tery Play by Eleanor Robson Bel
mont and Harriet Ford, under
the direction of Gordon Davis.
Seat sale Wednesday, December
2, at 1 o'clock in the lobby of
the Administration building.
Tickets, 75c and $1 at the A. S.
S. U. office, Administration build
ing, and at Crandall's in Palo
Alto.
