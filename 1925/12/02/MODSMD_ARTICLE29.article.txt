# BURNS' LETTERS SOLD
## 
### 
At a book sale recently held in Ed In
burgh, two of Robert Burns' person
ally signed letters sold for 35 pounds
apiece, while a third sold for 5t
pounds. A characteristic letter from
Robert Louis Stevenson went at 16
pounds, and several other letters by
noted men such as Sir Walter Scott,
Gladstone, Disraeli, and Longfellow,
sold for various smaller sums.
