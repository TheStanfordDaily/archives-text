# DATE FOR WINTER CONFERENCE PARTY SET AS JANUARY 13
## 
### 
The quarterly Women's Conference
party will be given January 13. The
committee in charge has been selected
by Mildred Worswick, president of
Associated Women Students, as fol
lows: Helen Huneke, chairman; Bea
trice Flesher, Harriet Oliver, Helen
von Damm, and Alice Sohlinger.
Seniors and sophomores will be in
charge of the stunts and entertain
ment for the party. At the autumn
quarter party, at which the juniors
and freshmen were in charge, decora
tions were introduced for the first
time. They were Dutch in design and
helped to give the atmosphere for the
main skit.
