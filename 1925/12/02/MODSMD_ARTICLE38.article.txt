# STANFORD DAMES TO MEET
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold a meeting
in the Women's Clubhouse tomorrow
afternoon at 3:15 o'clock. Mrs. War
ren D. Allen will sing and Mrs. H. H.
Beckwith will give readings. Refresh
ments will be served.

a great bonfire in honor of their vic
torious football team. In the pile were
two Ford cars, a piano, and several
wagons.
