# AWARDS COMMITTEE TO CLASSIFY SPORTS
## 
### 
Classification of sports into major
and minor groups will probably bo
concluded at a meeting of the Awards
Committee to be held in Room 204 at
7:15 o'clock tonight. Discussion of the
requitements for awards in each sport
will probably be commenced.
Donald J. Kropp has been appointed
by the Executive Committee to fill the
vacancy caused by the death of Leslie
P. Herth, who had been chairman of
the committee, and Theodore F. Mil
ler has been named chairman.
