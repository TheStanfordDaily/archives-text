# MRS. SHURTLEFF DIES
## 
### 
Mrs. Ada West Shurtleff, wife of Judge
Charles A, Shurtleff, who is a member of
the Stanford National Board, passed
away at her home Sunday in Menlo Park,
following a three-day attack of pneu
monia. Mrs. Shurtleff, besides being en
gaged in philanthropic work in San Fran
cisco, was also active as a member of the
hoard of directors of the Stanford Con
valescent Home.
