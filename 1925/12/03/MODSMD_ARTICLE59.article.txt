# MASONS MEET
## 
### 
Plans for the Masonic clubhouse on
the campus were discussed at a meet
ing of the Masonic Club at the Alpha
Kappa Lambda house last night. It
was decided that a formal dance would
be given the first of next quarter at
the Cardinal Hotel in Palo Alto. Early
next quarter the club will send a de
gree team to the University of Cali
fornia to enter ritual competition.
