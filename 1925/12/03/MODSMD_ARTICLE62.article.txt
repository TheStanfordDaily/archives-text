# Framework Being Erected For Ryan Electrical Plant
## 
### 
The laying of the concrete for the
foundation of the new Ryan Electrical
Laboratory has been completed, and
the framework of steel columns is now
being erected. According to Comptrol
ler Roth, the field work should all be
completed in about three weeks.
The steel columns are sixty feet in
length, and an? being shipped to May
field. Each column lies across the
length of two flat cars. Trucks will
bring them to the building site.
