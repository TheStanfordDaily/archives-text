# Many Manuscripts Turned In For Fiesta Lagunita
## 
### 
Eleven manuscripts have been
turned in for the Fiesta, according to
Jean Ward, president of W. A. A. The
committee for choosing of the plot for
the Fiesta is composed of five faculty
advisors, Miss Bunting, Miss Yost,
Miss Mirrielees, Mrs. Wentwortli, and
Mrs. Calvin, and five students, Mar
jorie Allen, Patrice Nauman, Margaret
Schaufelberger, Margaret Gemmell,
and Martha Montgomery. The com
mittee will meet tomorrow evening.
The board has recommended to the
executive committee of the Fiesta that
the pageant be given at night. A defi
nite decision will be made tomorrow
evening at the meeting.
