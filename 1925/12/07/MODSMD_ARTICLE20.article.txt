# Le 						Conte Society Holds Geology Meeting, Banquet
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford and the Uni
versity geology departments met on the
campus Saturday at the monthly Le Conte
club meeting. After the meeting a ban
quet was held in the Union.

Dr. Solon Shedd, curator of the Bran
ner Geological Library, presented a paper
on "Some Late Events in the Geological
History of Eastern Washington and their
Economic Importance." Professor Cyrus
F. Tolman, Jr., of the Geology depart
ment, gave a preliminary account of "Si
liceous Sediments of the Santa Maria
| Region."
