# ASSOCIATION TO MEET
## 
### 
The Stanford Philological Association
will meet at the Women's Clubhouse,
Thursday, December 10. Dr. Alwin
Thaler, of the English department, will
read a paper on "Shakespeare's Unhappy
Endings." Professor Aurelio Espinosa,
of the Romanic Language Department,
will read "Notes on Verso de Arte
Mayor," and Professor Frederick Ander
son, of the Romanic Language depart
ment will read a paper 011 "Pascal's Acci
dent at Neuilly Bridge."
