# Mail Orders Taken For "No, No, Nanette" Tickets
## 
### 
Tickets for "No, No, Nanette," which
will play at the Now Stanford Theatre
December 19, may be secured by mail
orders now or at the box office after
December 15.
Seats on the lower floor will be
priced at $2.50 and $2.75; the balcony:
lopes $2.75, first three rows $2.20, next
three rows $1.65, the balance of the
balcony $1.10.
Applications by mail should be made
as soon as possible. Check should be
enclosed, with a self-addressed stamp
ed envelope with all orders by mail.
