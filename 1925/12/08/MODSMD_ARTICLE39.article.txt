# Publication Completed by Food Research Institute
## 
### 
The first volume of "Wheat Studies,"
published by the Stanford Food Re
search department, has recently been
completed. The first number of the sec
ond volume is now being printed, and
it is expected that it will be finished
by the end of the week.
This publication is in the form of
statistical reports on the work done
by the Food Research Institute in the
field of wheat and wheat production.
It is a periodical having ten numbers
published each year.
