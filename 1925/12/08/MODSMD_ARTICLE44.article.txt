# Medical School Professor Gives Sewage Lectures
## 
### 
Professor Leon B. Reynolds of the
Stanford Medical School is giving a
series of lectures before the class in
preventative medicine and public
health on the subject of "Sewerage
and Sewage Treatment." The first of
these lectures was given yesterday
and the other two will come on De
cember 10 and 14.
For use with these lectures sixty
flies have been prepared showing vari
ous types of sewage treatment in
California.
