# "Gels" Is Topic Discussed Before Chemistry Seminar
## 
### 
The Chemistry Seminar held the
last meeting of the quarter last night
in Room 20 of the Chemistry building.
Dr. Carl L. Alsberg, director of the
Food Research Institute, led the discus
sion on the subject of "Gels," dealing
with the progress made through re
search work on the knowledge of the
structure and composition of this
matter.
Continuing an annual custom, the
Columbia University Christian Asso
ciation distributed 400 copies of the
Bible to the first students who applied.
