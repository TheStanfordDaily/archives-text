WEDNESDAY, DECEMBER 9
7:30 p.m.—Wednesday Evening Con
ference, at the home of Dr. David
Starr Jordan.
8:00 p.m.—Science Association, in
the Faculty Clubhouse. Address
on "The Decay of Agnosticism"
by Professor Stewart W. Young
of the Department of Chemistry.
THURSDAY, DECEMBER 10
1:45 p.m.—Board of Trustees. Regu
lar monthly meeting in the offices
of the Board, Balfour Building,
San Francisco.
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital, by Mr. Al
len, in the Memorial Church.
4:15 p.m. — University Philological
Association, in the Women's
Clubhouse. Papers: "Shakes
peare's Unhappy Endings," by
Professor Alwin Thaler; and
"Notes on the History of the
Verso de Arte Mayor," by Profes
sor Aurelio M. Espinosa.
7:30 p.m.—English Review Club, at
the home of Professor Arthur G. j
Kennedy, 435 Coleridge.
SUNDAY, DECEMBER 13
11:00 a.m. — Christmas Service.'
Preacher: Reverend D. Charles
Gardner.
