# Dean Yost Joins Women In Keeping Health Rules
## 
### 
Dean Yost has joined the ranks of
the army of women who are keeping
training rules during the week previ
ous to final examinations. "I promise
to live up to the rules beginning to
night and ending next Monday night,"
declares Dean Yost.
An army enlisting all the women of
the campus in the campaign to keep
training rules has been organized.
Majors, captains, and lieutenants have
been appointed to check up on those
keeping training.
Eight consecutive hours of sleep
are required for those in training.
