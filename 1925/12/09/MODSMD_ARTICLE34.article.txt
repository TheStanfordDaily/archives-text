# ALLEN PLANS MUSIC FOR VESPER SERVICES
## 
### 
Warren I). Allen has arranged the
musical program to be presented by
Schubert Club at the Christmas ves
per services planned by the Y. W.
C. A. for Sunday afternoon at 4:45
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Besides the Christmas music rendered
by the club, carols will be sung by
the entire assemblage.
Mrs. Ewell, manager of the Wo
men's Clubhouse, invited the Y. W.
C. A. to hold its Christmas services
in the clubhouse as her guests. The
Christmas vespers are open to every
one.
