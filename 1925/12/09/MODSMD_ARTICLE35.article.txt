# DINNER TRADED FOR GAME
## 
###

the Pennsylvania-Cornell football

that any girl wishing to attend the
game needed only to invite him to a
reyl Thanksgiving dinner, including
cranberry sauce, and the ticket was
hers. Its author declares that he re
