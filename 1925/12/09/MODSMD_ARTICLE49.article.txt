# DEAN W. E. HOTCHKISS LEAVES FOR SOUTH
## 
### 
Dean Willard E. Hotchkiss of the
Graduate School of Business is leav
ing tonight for Los Angeles, where he
will remain until the first of ne-ct, week
transacting business for the new
school.
From Los Angeles he will go to San
Luis Obispo where he will give three
lectures under the general title of
"Business Education" on December 10,
16, and 17. These lectures are being
delivered under the auspices of the
Central Coast Section of the Califor
nia Teachers Association.
Hotchkiss plans to return to the
campus December 18. He will be here
a day or two before leaving for Chi
cago.
