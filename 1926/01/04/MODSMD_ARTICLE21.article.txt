# Tractor Fatally Injures Former Stanford Student
## 
### 
Ellsworth W. Eager, ex-'23, died in
the hospital at Red Bluff from injuries
which he received when a tractor belt
slipped off, circled his leg, and drew
him into the moving machinery.
Eager attended Stanford from 1917
to 1919, but did not graduate, He was
with the Engineers Corps during tlio
war.
He lived at Fairfield and was 28
years of age.
