# Alumni Secretary Sends December Review Issue To All Former Students
## 
### 
Eighteen thousand copies of the spe
cial Christmas number of the Illus
trated Review were distributed among
all graduates and former students
whose name and address are on record
in the office of the Alumni Secretary.
The Alumni Association spent $2000
in addition to the regular expenses of
the magazine in printing and circulat
ing the extra number of copies. A
drive is being made to bring as many
alumni as possible into more direct
contact with the University through
the association and the Illustrated He
view.
