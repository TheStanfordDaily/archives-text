# ATTEND ASILOMAR PARLEY
## 
### 
Thirty Stanford men attended the
Western America Student convention
held at Asilomar from December 26
to January 3 under the auspices of
the Y. M. C. A. World problems of
international relations, industry,
peace, race questions, and problems
of the Western Coast were discussed.
Some of the well-known leaders were
Thomas W. Graham, Kirby Page, Paul
Meng, Lynn T. White, and J. Stitt
