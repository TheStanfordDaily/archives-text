# Nobel Peace Committee Opens Work in February
## 
### 
All proposals of candidates for the
Nobel Peace Prize must be laid before
the Nobel Committee of the Norwe
gian Parliament before the first of
February of this year. At that time
the Nobel Committee will take these
proposals into consideration.

The only candidates for the Nobel
Peace Prize that are considered as
eligible are members of the Nobel
Committee, of the Norwegian Parlia
ment, members of the parliaments or
government of the different states,
members of international arbitration
court at The Hague, or university pro
fessors of political science, of law, of
history, and of philosophy. Every writ
ten work must have appeared in
printed form before it may be submit
ted. The prize will be awarded De
cember 10, 1926.
