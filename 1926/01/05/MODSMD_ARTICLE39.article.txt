# MASONIC CLUBHOUSE
## 
### 
An $80,000 Masonic clubhouse to be
built on the new campus of the Uni
versity of California, Southern Branch,
at Westwood, is being planned. The
proposed structure will be of Spanish
architecture and up-to-date in every
respect. An organization to raise
funds has been outlined with the help
of Judge Ira F. Thompson, sponsor for
a similar clubhouse at Berkeley.
