# The 						Charleston Takes Its Toll
## 
### 
A tight-rope walker was seriously in
jured recently when he fell while at
tempting to walk a wire strung be
tween two twelve-story buildings, be
cause a jazz orchestra nearby started
to play "Yes, Sir, That's My Baby."

because it is believed that the students
