# Professor Adams Returns From History Convention
## 
### 
Professor E. D. Adams, of the History
department, returned to the campus Mon
day after a trip to the Middle West where
he attended the annual meeting of the
American Histoical Society held in Ann
Arbor, Michigan.
Attending the convention were several
former Stanford instructors, including
Professor R. G. Trotter, a member of the
faculty at Queen's University, Ontario;
Dr. Paul H. Clyde, who is lecturing in Eu
ropean history at Ohio State University;
and Dr. Thomas Marshall, professor at
Stanford in 1915-16. "The Open Door in
Manchuria" was the title of a paper read
at the convention by Dr. Clyde.
