# GEOLOGIC LITERATURE COURSE TO BE GIVEN
## 
### 
Dr. Solon Shedd, curator of the
Branner Geological library, will give
a new course in Geologic Literature —
Geology 114 —this quarter. The course
will be an introduction to the principal
sources of written information, biblio
graphic methods, abstracting and also
a study of important general works
on Geology.
Enrollment in this course is limited
to seniors and graduate students and
the class is limited. Two units' credit
will be given and hours are to be ar
ranged by Dr. Shedd.
