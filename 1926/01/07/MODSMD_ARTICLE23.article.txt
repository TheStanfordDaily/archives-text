# WINTER REGISTRATION SETS NEW RECORD
## 
### 
Failing to take warning from the
fate of the young men and women who
have previously been drowned in the
sea of class average, 2,921.2 students
again rushed heedlessly into the break
ers of the academic ocean where man
eating scholarship committee sharks
and deadly examination books abound.
Some in order to increase the cer
tainty of their destruction tied mill
stones weighing as much as 17 to 20
units about their necks.
E pluribus unum (which means there
is one born every minute).

We see that the Palo Alto Humane
Society is taking action on the ever
increasing scarcity of cats in Palo
Alto. Fortunately the course in Ma
mallian Anatomy will not be given
until the next autumn quarter.
