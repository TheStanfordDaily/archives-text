# HELEN BELL ELECTED FRESHMAN SECRETARY
## 
### 
After two tie votes, Helen E. Bell
was elected secretary of the Freshman
Class as a result of a third ballot cast
last night at a 1929 class meeting in
the Little Theatre. Samuel S. Hayes,
class president, presided at the meet
ing.
Powell W. Carpenter was elected
chairman of the dance committee by a
safe majority. He plans to whip a com
mittee into shape which will start pre
parations soon for a large 1929 class
dance. "
