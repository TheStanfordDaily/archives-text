# Wesley U. Benepe Named Branner Club Secretary
## 
### 
Wesley U. Benepe was appointed
secretary of Branner Club by the hall
executive committee last night to fill
the vacancy left by the graduation of
Paul R. Jackson. John L. Blackford
was appointed junior representative
of the club, filling the position left
vacant by Ralph Mossman. The com
mittee named John A. Leiter chair
man of the six-thirty to eight o'clock
dance to be given in the near future.
