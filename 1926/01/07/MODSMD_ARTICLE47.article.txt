# Two Comedies to Be Given By French Club Wednesday
## 
### 
!
I The French Club will present two
; comedies in the Little Theatre next
Wednesday evening at 8:15 o'clock.
Proceeds will go to the French war
! orphans. The ticket sale will be an
nounced tomorrow.
The plays which are to be produced
are "La Paix Chez Soi" by Georges
Courteline, and "La Plaisanterie," by
Mare Soual. Benefit performances by
the French Club for the French war
orphans have been held every year
since the war.
