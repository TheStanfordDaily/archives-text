# Washington Freshmen Paint Numerals on Buildings
## 
### 
The freshman class at the Univer
sity of Washington painted their class
numerals on the memorial arch and
library roof of that institution. The
Senior Council has decided to take
drastic action on the matter.
If the perpetrators of the crime are
not discovered in due time the first
year students will not be permitted
to participate in any university activ
ity including publications, debate
clubs, Knights of the Hook, A.S.U.W.
committee, etc. Moreover class officers
will be removed and all social privi
leges will be taken away.
