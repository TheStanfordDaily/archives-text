MEMBERS OF THE GAMMA PHI

who will assist in selling Chaparrals
meet today at 12:45 o'clock at the
Chi Omega house.

out for boxing manager meet at 4:30
p.m., Friday, in the boxing room at
the gym.

out for tennis manager should leave
(heir names and telephone numbers
on a slip in the sport manager's of
fice before Tuesday.
