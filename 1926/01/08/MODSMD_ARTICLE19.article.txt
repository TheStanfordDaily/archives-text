# WOMEN PLAN PARTY WEDNESDAY EVENING
## 
### 
The quarterly Women's Conference
party will be given next Wednesday
night in the Clubhouse. Helen Huneke
is chairman of the program which
promises many interesting features.
Mildred Worswick, who has recently
returned from the East where she at
tended the National Collegiate World
Court Conference, will speak to the
women of her experiences at the Con
ference, and her visits to other col
leges in the country.
The party will give the new girls
an opportunity to meet all the women.
