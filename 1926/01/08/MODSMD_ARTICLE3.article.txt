# GEORGE BAKER SIGNS TO PLAY WITH TIGERS
## 
### 
George Baker, Stanford center on
the 1923 and 1924 elevens, has signed
tn play professional football with the
Los Angeles Tigers, captained by
fJeorge Wilson, Washington All-Amer
ican halfback. The Tigers will meet
Rod Grange's Chicago Bears at Los
Angeles January 16.
While at Stanford Baker took a
prominent part in student activities,
being defeated by a close margin for
president of the A.S.S.U.
