# TOYON CLUB INFORMAL COMMITTEE REPORTS
## 
### 
A meeting of the Toyon Club was held
last night presided over by Ward Hill,
president. Reports were heard from the
committee which is arranging the "all
campus women" dance to be held in the
Toyon lobby in two weeks, A Fairmont
hotel orchestra has been obtained for the
dance which is a one o'clock affair.
Plans were set under way to repeat the
Toyon Musicale which was so successful
last year. Herb Klein was appointed to
take charge of the musical and appoint an
assisting committee.
It was decided to make use of the Toyon
fireplace on Sunday afternoon and other
times when the weather made it desirable.
