# Fairclough Elected Head Of Philological Group
## 
### 
Henry Rushton Fairclough, Profes
sor of Classical Literature at Stanford,
who is on a leave of absence and is
teaching at Harvard this year, was
elected president of the American
Philological Association at an annual
meeting held at Cornell the latter part
of December. This is the first, time for
a number of years that anyone west
of the Rockies has been president of
this association.
