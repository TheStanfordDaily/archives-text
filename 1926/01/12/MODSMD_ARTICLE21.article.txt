# YELL LEADERS ISSUE CALL FOR TRYOUTEES
## 
### 
Tryouts for assistant yell leader will
be opened to sophomores at a meeting
called by Bob Fraser and Jimmy Farr,
junior yell leaders, which will be held
in the pavilion tomorrow afternoon at
2 o'clock.
Candidates will be put through a
course of intensive training, under the
tutelage of Fraser and Farr. These
instructions will last throughout the
quarter. Tryoutees will appear at vari
ous athletic events during the season.
Two of the candidates will be chosen
for junior yell leaders at the spring
student body elections.
