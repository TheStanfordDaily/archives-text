# DAY'S DOINGS
## 
### 
<§> Ted Ellas. Jr. <?>
Intramural basketballers
Got off to a flying start.
In the basketball pavilion,
Tearing everyone apart.
Tryoutees have been selected
For the Ram's Head annual noise;
Dave Manoccir is director
Of the Stanford Rover boys.
Cards are set to meet the Saints
Upon the Grid in '27,
Then we'll see if Palo Alto
Is a better place than heaven.
In the lobby of the Union,
Sale of Glee Club tickets starts;
If you miss this neat performance,
You are "blah" on finer arts.
Lewis Terman's big endowment
Fills the editorial page;
His examination system
Is at present all the rage.
