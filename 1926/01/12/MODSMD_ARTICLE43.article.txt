# WOMEN'S EQUITATION CLASS
## 
### 
There will be a class in equitation
for women this quarter given under
the Military department. Those desir
mg to enter the class should give
their names immediately to the secre
tary of the Dean of Women. As the
class is limited, preference will be
given to graduate and senior women.
The class will meet on Tuesdays and
Thursdays at 10:30 o'clock.

Students competed for the honor of
being Santa Claus at a fraternity-sor
ority Christmas party given for one
hundred poor children at the Univer
sity of Minnesota.
