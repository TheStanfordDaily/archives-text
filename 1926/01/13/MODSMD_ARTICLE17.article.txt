# WOMEN'S ATHLETIC FIELDS VISITED BY PLAY DAY DIRECTOR
## 
### 
Miss Hannah Mathews of the Cas
tilleja School for Girls, who heads the
committee which is making plans for
the Bay schools play-day to be held on
the campus in March, visited Iloble
gymnasium Monday afternoon to look
over the women's athletic equipment.
Miss Helen Masters Bunting, head of
the Women's Physical Education de
partment, is also a member of the
program committee.
The play-day, in which girls from
about ten schools will participate, is
sponsored by Women's Athletic Asso
ciation. Stanford women will act as
officials for the play-day games, and
will be hostesses to the girls during
the day and help with arrangements
beforehand.
