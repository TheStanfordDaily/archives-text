# Gym Class for Sons of Faculty Opens Saturday
## 
### 
Sons of Stanford faculty members
may register for a boys' gymnasium
class which will meet at 9 o'clock Sat
urday morning and thereafter every
Saturday morning in the Encina gym
nasium, according to announcement
made by C. W. Davis who will Instruct
the class. Application blanks may be
obtained from the Graduate Manager's
office. Blanks must be signed by par
-onts and returned by next Saturday
morning.

McClure Hall and the Journalism
building at the University of Oregon
are undergoing extensive remodeling
in order to make more classrooms, and
also to provide new offices for The
Emerald.

Notre Dame's new gymnasium annex
will seat 6000 spectators.
