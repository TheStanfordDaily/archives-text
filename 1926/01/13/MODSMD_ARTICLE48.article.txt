# Juniperro Serra Dance to Be Held Saturday Night
## 
###

tion, will hold its "Campus Dance"
Saturday evening in the Women's
Clubhouse following the basketball
game. One dollar will be charged for
the affair which will last from 9 to 12
o'clock.
