# Sigma Delta Chi Meets to Elect President Tonight
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi, professional jour
nalistic fraternity, will hold its first
meeting of the winter quarter at 7:15
o'clock tonight in the Daily Palo Alto
office. A new president will be elected
to replace Ray L. Spangler, who gradu
ated last quarter, and other important
business will be transacted. All mem
bers are urged to be present.
