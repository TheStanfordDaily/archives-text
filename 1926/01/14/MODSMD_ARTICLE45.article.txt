# Interclass Polo Play Will Start With Spring Quarter
## 
### 
Polo will be started for the winter
quarter by a tourney for the members
of Squad B. This tourney will consist
of matches as played during the tour
nament held in the fall quarter.
During the sping quarter, the annual
interclass polo matches will be held.
These tourneys were not held last
year, but during the past years they
have developed several good polo
players. Play is for the Stanley Don
ough cup, a perpetual trophy, which
has been won twice before, by the
class of 1924.
