Daily glorious new things for Spring wear are arriving at Hale's.
Everything is color —color —color! From renewed Nature herself
comes the inspiration for soft greens and blues, brilliant pinks and
reds, and radiant yellows and oranges.
Coats boast gayly colorful plaids or novelty mixtures. Dresses are,
for the most part, delightful printed effects in many colors. And —
here's good news for the co-ed —even the smartest of the new arrivals
are moderately priced.
