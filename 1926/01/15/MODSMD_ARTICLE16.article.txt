# CONFERENCE PROGRAM PLANNED FOR MONDAY
## 
### 
The Women's Conference party for
this quarter will be held Monday eve
ning at 7 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. The program which was an
nounced last week will be given.
The party was to have taken place
last Wednesday, but was postponed in
memory of Laura Gardiner. The din
ner, which was planned as part of the
program, was held at that time be
cause women's living groups had plan
ned not to serve dinner that evening.
The Conference party serves as a
general get-together for all campus
women and is given every quarter.
