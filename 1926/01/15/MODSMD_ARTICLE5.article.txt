# ALLEN ORGAN RECITAL PLANNED FOR SUNDAY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, in his regular Sun
day afternoon concert to be given in
the Memorial Church at 4 o'clock, will
play "Rhapsody" by Edward Shippen
Barnes of New York. Barnes is in the
front rank of American composers,
and has composed two symphonies and
many smaller works for the organ.
"Rhapsody" is from his second sym
phony.
The program will open with "Al
legro" from the First Sonata by Alex
ander Guilmant. Other numbers are
"Triumphal March" by Edward Grieg,
and "Will-o'-the-Wisp" by Gordon B.
Nevin.
