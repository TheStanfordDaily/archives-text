# Stanford Advertising Club Meets at Cardinal Hotel
## 
### 
Three members of the Los Angeles
Advertising Club will have charge of
the program tonight at the meeting
of the Stanford Advertising Club of
Palo Alto to be held at 6 o'clock in
the Trellis room of the Cardinal hotel.
Don Thomas, secretary of the Los
Angeles club, will speak on the sub
ject, "Business Men in the Advertis
ing Club." Approximately one hundred
members and guests are expected to
attend.
