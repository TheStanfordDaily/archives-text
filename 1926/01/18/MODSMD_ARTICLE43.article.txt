# 250 Women at Idaho Are Earning Own Expense Money
## 
### 
More than 250 women at the Uni
versity of lowa are earning their way
through school this year by working
outside of school hours. Most of the
girls are doing various kinds of house
work for board or room, but many
have clerical positions; some are so
cial secretaries, and others work in
the mailing department. A few girls
even hold down jobs as chemistry
technicians.
