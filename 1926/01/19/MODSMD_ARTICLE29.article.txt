TUESDAY, JANUARY 19
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital, by Mr. Al
len, in the Memorial Church.
4:30 p.m.—Graduate Women's Club, j
Informal Tea. in the Women's
Clubhouse. All graduate women
are invited to attend.
7:30 p.m.—Lecture on "Informal Im
pressions of Germany," by Pro
fessor C. D. Marx; in the Little
Theatre. The public is invited.

3:00 p.m.—Resident Alumnae Tea, in
the Women's Clubhouse.
7:30 p.m.—Y.M.C.A. Members Assem
bly, in the Y.M.C.A. building.

Secretary, Committee on Friend
ly Relations Among Foreign Stu
dents in America; former secre
tary World's Student Christian
Federation.

7:30 p.m.—Euphronia Debating So
ciety, open meeting, in the lobby
of the Old Union. Dr. Jordan will
speak on his recent visit to
Hawaii.
