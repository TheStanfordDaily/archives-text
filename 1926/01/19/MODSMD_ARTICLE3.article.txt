# Mears Author of Article On Immigration in "Times"
## 
### 
Professor Eliot G. Mears, of the Eco
nomics department, is the author of a
series of articles on "Immigrants in
the West," which is appearing in the
Palo Alto Times this week. All phases
of this particular immigration prob
lem are being presented by Dr. Mears,
who takes up the effects of the immi
gration of different nationalities into
the Western states.
