# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
MUSIC CLUB will meet at 7:30 o'clock
at the home of Warren D. Allen.
QUAD PICTURE of Phi Alpha Delta
to be taken on the Art Gallery steps
at 1 o'clock.
JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB will meet
Tuesday, January 19, at 7:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
MOTHERS CLUB will meet at 3
o'clock in the Stanford Women's
Clubhouse. Mrs. Parker Maddux will
speak on "Women in International
Affairs." Members of the club may
bring guests.
BRANNER 6:30 to 8 dance. Admission
35c.
WEDNESDAY
SCABBARD AND BLADE meets at
6:15 in New Union for a dinner in
honor of new pledges. Initiation will
be held afterwards at Military head
quarters.
Y.M.C.A. members meeting, "Y" build
ing, 7:15 o'clock. Charles D. Hurrey
will speak. Refreshments will be
served.
ZOOLOGY CLUB will meet at the Wo
men's Clubhouse tonight at 8 o'clock.
Professor Danforth will talk on
"Human Heredity." Music and re
freshments. All interested are in
vited.
THURSDAY
GERMAN CLUB will meet at 7:30
o'clock tonight in the Women's Club
house.
