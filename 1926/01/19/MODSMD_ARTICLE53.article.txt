# Summer Courses Announced In Bulletin to Appear Soon
## 
### 
The Announcement of Courses for
summer quarter are now on the press
and will be ready for distribution in
February, several weeks earlier than
the usual date.

As only a limited number of copies
is being printed this year, members of
the student body wishing to send bul
letins to off-campus friends are in
structed to leave their names and ad
dresses with Director of the Summer
Quarter or at the Registrar's office as
soon as possible.
