# WOMEN WILL START BASKETBALL SEASON BY PRACTICE SERIES
## 
### 
The first game of the women's bas
ball season will be a practice game on
February 2. It is customary for the
championship series to be preceded
by a practice series, in which all
players in each class are given a
chance to play regardless of ranking.
The practice series determines the
ranking.
The championship series will begin
with a junior-freshman game on Feb
ruary 23. All games will be played on
Tuesdays and Wednesdays this sea
son, leaving Thursdays and Fridays
open for practice.
Practice Games
February 2 —Juniors vs. Freshmen.
February 3 —Seniors vs. Sophomores.
February 9 —Seniors vs. Freshmen.
February 10—Juniors vs. Sophomores.
February 16 —Juniors vs. Seniors.
February 17 —Sophomores vs. Fresh
men.

First-Team Games

March 9—Juniors vs. Seniors.
March 10 —Freshmen vs. Sophomores.
