# "HELL WEEKS" OPPOSED
## 
### 
Claiming that long "Hell Weeks"
are disastrous to the grades of the
candidates, members of the faculty
of Ohio State university are advocat
ing the shortening of the period to
three days.
On returning from Christmas vaca
tion the women of Morse Hall, Em
poria Teachers' College, found small
parcels of chocolates in their mail
boxes, gifts of a local taxi company.
