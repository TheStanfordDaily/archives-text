# ENGINEERING SCHEDULE PREPARED BY FACULTY
## 
### 
Reports on the new engineering cur
riculum which will replace the present
study program of the Engineering
School next year were given by the
curriculum committee at a meeting of
the faculty of the School of Engineer
ing Monday night. The schedule which
is being prepared by a committee of
faculty members in the various engi
neering departments will be ready for
printing in the 1926-1927 Announce
ment of Courses which comes out in
Juae.
A football player at the University
of Chicago that falls below scholastic
grade during the season does not get
his "C" even though he has played
enough to earn it.
