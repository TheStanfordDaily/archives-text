# ROOTERS WILL HAVE SECOND CHANCE TO GET U.S.C. TICKETS
## 
### 
Tickets for the U.S.C. basket
ball game Saturday night will be
on sale to students at the 50-cent
rate until Saturday noon in the
Board of Athletic Control ticket
offices in the Old Union. This
additional rooters' ticket sale is
being held because only 340 root
ers purchased pasteboards at the
regular sale.
Public sale of tickets will be
held at Palo Alto and San Fran
cisco today. After Saturday noon,
students will have to pay the
general admission rate of one
dollar.
