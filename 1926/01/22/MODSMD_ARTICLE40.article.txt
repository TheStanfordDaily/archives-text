# GERMAN CLUB PLANS TRIP TO SANTA CRUZ
## 
### 
Plans for an automobile excursion
to Santa Cruz on Washington's birth
day were made at the German Club
meeting held last night in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. The club is also con
sidering the presentation of a comedy
and a committee was appointed to
make arrangements for its production.
Professor W. A. Cooper, Dr. A. C.
Mahr, and Clara L. Ophuls were
named as the committee. Those in
tending to participate in the comedy
should send their name and address
to Hartley Devlin, box 1306, campus.
