# Research Director Talks On Chemistry of Gasoline
## 
### 
"The Cracking of Hydrocarbon Oils
Into Gasoline" will be discussed by
Dr. Gustave Egloff Monday evening at
7:30 o'clock in the Chemistry build
ing, Room 20. Dr. Egloff is the Director
of Research of the Universal Oil
Company, Chicago.
The lecture, which is being given
under the auspices of the Chemistry
department, is open to the public.
