# VARSITY CHESS MEN TO PLAY FRESHMEN
## 
### 
The four ranking varsity players
will play the four beat freshmen in
simultaneous exhibition matches at
the open meeting of the Cardinal Chess
Club at 7:15 o'clock this evening in
the Y.M.C.A. building. Ratings of the
freshmen were made at the last meet
ing of the club.
Matches with other colleges are
being arranged for the freshmen. The
varsity has played several correspon
dence matches. At present competition
among the varsity men is for places
on the seven-man team to be picked
for the California matches in the
spring. A round-robin tournament will
be held later to determine rankings.
