# ELLIOTT WINS PRIZE
## 
### 
David C. Elliott, '18, has been award
ed the second prize of $150 in the
recent Bankers Magazine prize essay
contest. The title of his essay is:
"What About Branch Banking?" El
liott is in the statistical department of
the Federal Reserve Bank of Cleve
land, Uino. Mrs. Elliott was Katherine
Huntington, '19.
