# Graduate Women Will Be Guests at Tea Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Graduate Women's Club will be
guests at a tea to be given at Roble
Hall tomorrow afternoon, from 4 to 6
o'clock, when Roble Club and Mrs.
Stearns will be hostesses to all the
graduate women students of the Uni
versity.
The regular Tuesday meeting of the
Graduate Women's Club will not take
place this week.
