# Washington Will Hold Vocational Conference
## 
### 
All students at the University
of Washington will be given an oppor
tunity to receive valuable first-hand
information regarding various phases
of business at a vocational conference
to be held here this month under the
auspices of the College of Business.
Men representing outside fields will
present their viewpoints, giving practi
cal advice concerning the choice of a
business career.
Printed copies of the papers read at
the conference will be preserved for
advisory information in the future.
