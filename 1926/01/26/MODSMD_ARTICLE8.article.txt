# Cap and Gown Society Plans Bridge Tea Soon
## 
### 
Plans for a bridge tea to be given
February 6 are being made by Cap
and Gown. The proceeds will go to
the Cap and Gown scholarship fund.
Reservations for the tea, which is
to be held at the Women's Clubhouse
may be made through Peggy Detrich
at Roble hall. Tables may be reserved
for $2 and arrangements may be made
for singles. Prizes are planned.
