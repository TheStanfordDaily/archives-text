# Tonight's Conference to Discuss Effects of War
## 
### 
The subject of Dr. Jordan's Thurs
day evening conference for this week
is "The Wood of the Nation." The dis
cussion will consider the various ef
fects that are produced on the race
through the loss of its best men in
war.
The conference will be held at Dr.
Jordan's home at 7:30 o'clock tonight.
Everybody is invited.
