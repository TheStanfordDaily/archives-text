# WATER SUPPLY CLASS TO VISIT AQUEDUCTS
## 
### 
Leon B. Reynolds, professor of hy
draulic engineering, will conduct a sec
ond field trip for his class in Water
Supply next Saturday. Places on the
west side of the bay that are of inter
est to hydraulic engineers will be
visited by the class. The first visit will
be made to the rivet steel pipe con
nection at Irvington, which Joins the
pipe lines of the Spring Valley Water
Company and the Hetch-Hetchy pipe
line.
Other points of interest will be the
large storage reservoir in Niles Can
yon, the tunnel and flume through the
canyon, the underground water col
lector at Sunol, and the Calaveras dam
which now collects a large part of the
San Francisco water supply.
