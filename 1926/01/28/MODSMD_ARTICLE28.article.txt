# ODE TO ST. PATRICK AND THE SNAKES
## 
### 
"Me frinds, Oi've psint me loif wid ye
A-tipplin' an' a-boozin'
An' whin Oi'm gone from all of ye
Plaze look ye to me snoozin'.
"Oi've drunk me share of whuskey,
byes,
Oi've pickled all me innards,
An' so Saint Peter knows me not,
Oi'm listed wid the sinners.
"So down Oi go to worrk a bit —
Oi've dodged it all me loif —
But, byes, rajoice to see me lave,
'Cause Oi'll not miss me woif!
"An' when Oi'm past the Border, byes,
An' laid out nate an* slick,
Jist pour me in thim bottles there,
An' stopper me up quick."
