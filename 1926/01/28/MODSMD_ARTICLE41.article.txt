# ZOOLOGY CLUB TO VISIT STEINHARDT AQUARIUM
## 
### 
The Zoology Club will visit the
Steinhardt Aquarium and the Academy
of Sciences next Saturday. The mem
bers who intend taking the trip will
meet at the Women's Clubhouse at 8
o'clock.
Dr. E. C. Starks of the Zoology
department will lecture on the life,
habits, and care of the various fish
found in the Aquarium. The animal
and bird collections at the Academy
ot Sciences will also be visited by the
club.
