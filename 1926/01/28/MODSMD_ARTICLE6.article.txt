# Branner Club Planning Thoroughly Oriental Hop
## 
### 
Tickets for the Chinese dance to be
given February 5 by the Branner Club
will be placed on sale tonight to the
general student body, and can be ob
tained from W. IT. Jacobs or any mem
ber of the dance committee.
Futuristic panel effects, designed by
Marvin Park, and lighting features,
, arranged by Werner J. Raab, have
been secured for the dance. Ev Young
and his seven-piece orchestra will fur
nish the music, and two stunts have
been planned for intermissions by the
committee.
