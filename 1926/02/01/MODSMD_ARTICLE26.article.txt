# Professor Bruce Summer Dean for Berkeley Session
## 
### 
Professor H. L. Bruce, former asso
ciate professor in English and dean of
the summer session in Los Angeles,
has been appointed to the office of
dean of the summer session at Berke
ley in place of Dean J. P. Buwalda,
who resigned.
The University of California Board
of Regents also appointed Professor
T. M. Putnam, dean of the under
graduate division, to succeed Professor
Bruce at his post in Los Angeles.

There will be a dance given in the
Paly Woman's Club house Wednesday
night, from 8:30 to 11. Music by an
eight-piece campus orchestra.
