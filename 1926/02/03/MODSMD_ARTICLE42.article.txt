# Epidemic Sweeps Campus 500 Falls for Galoshes Gore Spilled on New Craze
## 
### 
The first symptom of an epiremic
of golashes has been detected on the
campus. (Florida papers, do not
copy.) No loss than ten pairs of the
protective overshoes were seen this
morning in this hitherto unmolested
community and a similar number of
women looked indifferently on the rain
clouds looming on the distant horizon.
Galusha Goolash, the eastern import,
claims that the utility of the boots ex
tend beyond their protective powers.
The lacing "doojigger" all the way up
indicates that the lady already has a
date for the evening, all the way down
that she has none, and half way up
that she has a date but is open to
persuasion.
