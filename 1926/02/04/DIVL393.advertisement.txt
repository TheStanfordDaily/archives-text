# Adv. 12 Page 3

### 
"Tell them for me," said Dick, the eldest Rover, "that the
ROVER BOYS have too often reached the box office only
to find the milk skimmed, the last car gone, the gas tank
empty. Friends of the ROVER BOYS shall not view our
splendid performance (here Dick blushed modestly) from
behind a pillar. We will save (insert name of) University
both Friday and Saturday, Feb. 12th and 13th."
ONLY 4 more days and the ROVER BOYS arrive on the
Campus, go into training for two days and then—BE
WARE, JACK DALTON—you bully!
