# NOMINATIONS FOR ALL OFFICES WILL CLOSE TOMORROW NOON
## Candidates Fail to Appear in Race for Freshman Class Offices 
### 
Tomorrow at 12 o'clock the ballot
box at the Law steps for student body
and class officer nominations will be
removed, and no more candidates can
be added after that time. Seventeen
additional nominations have been re
ceived today, bringing the total up to
forty-one. Candidates may decline
their nominations not later than 11
o'clock Saturday morning. The elec
tion will be held next Tuesday.

No nominations have been for the
1929 class offices, and only one can
didate has been named for the Union
Board of Governers.
