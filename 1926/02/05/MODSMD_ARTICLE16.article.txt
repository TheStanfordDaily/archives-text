# OVER EIGHTY ARE NAMED FOR STUDENT BODY, CLASS OFFICERS
## Seven in Race for Freshman Presidency; Election Tuesday 
### 
Nominations for student body and
class officers closed today at 12
o'clock with a grand total of eighty
nominees. The freshmen sent in
twelve nominations yesterday, seven
of which were for president. Candi
dates have until 11 o'clock tomorrow
morning to withdraw.
A complete list of candidates group
ed according to the offices for which
they are running will be published
Monday after all withdrawals have
been handed in.
