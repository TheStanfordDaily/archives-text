# 1925 Football Men Receive Awards for Defeating Bears
## 
### 
Members of the 1925 varsity foot
ball team were awarded gold footballs
yesterday as a result of their victory
over California last fall. The footballs
are made of hammered gold. On one
side of the ball there is a raised
cardinal block "S." Under this is in
scribed the year 1925. On the reverse
side of the ball the player's name and
the score of the game is engraved.
