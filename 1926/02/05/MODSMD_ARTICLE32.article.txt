# Y.M.C.A. Conference Men Plan All-Day Outing Sunday
## 
### 
Twenty-two members of the delega
tion which went to the last Y.M.C.A.
Asilomar conference (luring Christmas
vacation are planning to spend Sunday
at "Dutch" Roth's cabin in the Santa
Cruz mountains. They are leaving at
8:15 in the morning, and plan to re
turn late in the evening, spending the
day in discussion and games. Rex
Bell, general secretary of the Y.M.C.A.,
will accompany them.
