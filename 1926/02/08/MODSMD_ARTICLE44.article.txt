# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30
o'clock. New members will have
charge of the program.
TUESDAY _
BUSINESS STAFF of the Da'ily Palo
Alto will meet this evening at 7
o'clock.
SPANISH CLUB Quad picture will
be taken on the Art Gallery steps
at 12:55 o'clock Tuesday.
MUSIC CLUB meets 7:30 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Professor
Mahr will lecture on popular Ger
man music.
GLEE CLUB is invited to meet with
the Music Club at 7:30 in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
TRYOUTEES for assistant managers
who met with Gordon Davis last
week meet in the Green Room this
evening at 7 o'clock.
FRESHMAN WOMEN will meet this
noon immediately after lunch in
Roble to discuss selling the Chapar
ral.
WEDNESDAY
JIJNIPERO SERRA CLUB Quad pic
ture will be taken on the Art Gallery
steps at 12:15 o'clock Wednesday.
SIGMA DELTA Cljl will hold an im
portant meeting at the Daily office
at 7 o'clock tonight.
THURSDAY
FRENCH CLUB meets at 8:30 at
Kappa Alpha Theta House.
