# Professor Hulme to Talk At Community Luncheon
## 
### 
Professor Edward M. Hulme, of the
History department, will speak on the
subject, "The Big Things of Life," at
the weekly Palo Alto Community
Luncheon of the Chamber of Com
merce tomorrow. The affair will bo
held In the Russell Cafeteria at 12:10
o'clock and the general public Is In
vited to attend.
