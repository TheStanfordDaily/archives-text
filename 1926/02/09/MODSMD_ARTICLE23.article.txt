# Freshman Players Defeat Varsity in Chess Matches
## 
### 
Freshman chess players defeated
the varsity 3 to 1 in matches played
at the Chess Club meeting last night
in the Y.M.C.A. building. The only
varsity man to win his match was
Adin Henderson, president of the
club, who defeated Jack Hodges, fresh
man player.
Kenneth Howard, freshman, won
from John Blackford, varsity; Edwin
J. Simon defeated Ervin H. Bramhall,
and Douglas R. Fuller defeated Ger
ald O. Vaughn.
