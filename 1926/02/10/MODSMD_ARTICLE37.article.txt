# INTELLIGENCE STERILIZED
## 
### 
"Examinations are ruining the in
telligence for all practical purposes,"
according to Professor H. E. Arm
strong at the conference of the Sci
ence Masters' Association in London.
"I am a good deal in contact with tech
nical men," he continued, "and know
the kind of material sent up. Under
this examination system intelligence
is being absolutely sterilized."
