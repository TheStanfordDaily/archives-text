# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
WEDNESDAY
RADIO CLUB meets at 8 o'clock to
night in corporation yard shack.
Election of officers will be held.
WOMEN'S DEBATE tryouts will be
held at 7 o'clock on the second floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
UNIVERSITY ORCHESTRA will not
rehearse tonight.
MASONIC CLUB will meet at the
Theta Chi house at 7:30. All Master
Masons invited.
JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB Quad pic
ture will be taken on the Art Gallery
steps at 12:15 o'clock Wednesday.
STGMA DELTA CHI will hold an im
portant meeting at the Daily office
at 7 o'clook tonight.
SCHUBERT CLUB will meet at 7:15
o'clock in F.oom 84. Tryouts at 8
o'clock.
YHURSJAY
FRENCH CLUB meets at 8:30 at
Kappa Alpha Theta House.
THURSDAY
UNIVERSITY ORCHESTRA will meet
at 7:30 tonight in Room 84.
Y.M.C.A. CABINET to meet at 6:45
o'clock in "Y" building. All mem
bers please be present.
ALL FRESHMAN WOMEN who sign
ed up to sell Chaparrals and any
more who wish to meet after lunch
this noon in the Roble Library.
