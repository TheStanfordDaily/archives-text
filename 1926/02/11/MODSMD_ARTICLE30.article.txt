# "MODERN LITERATURE" FRENCH CLUB TOPIC
## 
### 
Mrs. Marie de Mare Stein will speak
on "Tendances nouvelles de la littera
ture raoderne," at a meeting of the
French Club to be held at 8:30 o'clock
this evening at the Kappa Alpha
Theta sorority, 15 Lasuen.
Mrs. Stein has toured this country
frequently, giving Conferences and
lectures under the auspices of the
"Alliance francaise de Paris," and is
an accomplished and entertaining
speaker. She gave a series of three
lectures here this summer. All who
are interested are invited to attend.
Refreshments will be served. This will
be the last meeting of the present
quarter.
