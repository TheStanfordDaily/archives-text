# HANDBALL MEN START TRYOUTS FOR VARSITY
## 
### 
First rounds of a preliminary hand
ball tournament are now being played
on the Peninsular Club court to select
men to fill the vacancies left on the
varsity squad by Whiting Welch and
Edson Waterhouse. Both of these men
were letter men and expert handball
players. Sam Joseph, Arnold Rumwell,
Francis Harold, and Dick Richards
are playing good handball and are ex
pected to strengthen the team.
A tentative schedule has been
drawn up calling for games with the
San Jose Y.M.C.A., the Alumni Club
of San Jose, and the Peninsular Club.
The Stanford-California match is to
be played March 5 at the Athens Club
in Oakland.
