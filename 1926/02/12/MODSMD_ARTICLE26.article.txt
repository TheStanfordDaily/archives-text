# PROFESSOR WING TALKS OF STANFORD STADIUM
## 
### 
Charles B. Wing, professor of struc
tural engineering, will address the
freshmen at Encina Sunday evening
at 6:30 o'clock on the subject, "The
Stanford Stadium." This is one of the
regular Sunday evening lectures de
livered to the freshmen by representa
tive professors for the purpose of
making the students and professors
better acquainted. The series is spon
sored by the Y.M.C.A.
Professor Wing was the designer of
the stadium, and of other University
athletic fields. Further work he has
done is the designing of the campus
wireless tower, 620 feet in height, and
several Shanghai towers of 1000 feet,
as well as the Columbia River bridge.
