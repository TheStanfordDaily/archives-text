# COMMITTEE CHOSEN FOR MILITARY BALL
## 
### 
The committee for the 1926 Military
Ball, to be given April 9, has been an
nounced by Homer Dombaugh, chairman
in charge of the plans for the dance.

N. Chantry, S. Clark Cypher, Juniors;
Theodore B. Criley, Robert L. Grutz
macher, Alfred Virag; Sophomores:
George A. Connolly, Henry L. Flood,
John S. Speer; Freshmen: Theodore T.

McKey.
