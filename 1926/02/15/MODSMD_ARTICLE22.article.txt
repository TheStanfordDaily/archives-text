# Former Dean of Stanford Law School Promoted
## 
### 
Announcement has recently come oi
the promotion of Professor Frederit
C. Woodward, former head of the
Stanford Law School, to the position
of Vice-President of the University of
Chicago. Professor Woodward has
been connected with the Chicago in
stitution since 1916, and has been a
very popular and successful instructor.
He was dean of the law school at
Stanford for eight years, having been
a law professor here for nine years in
all. Until his recent promotion, Wood
ward has been Professor of Law at
the University of Chicago.
