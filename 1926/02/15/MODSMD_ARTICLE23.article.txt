# NEVADA SOCIETIES MANY
## 
### 
University of Nevada, Reno, Nev.,
Feb. 11. —P.I.P.) —A recent survey
shows a total of 775 students on the
Nevada campus, with 82 organizations
in all. This is the percentage of one
organization for every ten students.
The organizations counted include so
cial, professional, and honor fraterni
ties, standing student committees,
clubs, musical and service organiza
tions.
