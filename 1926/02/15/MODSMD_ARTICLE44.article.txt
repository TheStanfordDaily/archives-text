# Robinson Will Give Series Of Lectures at Columbia
## 
### 
Professor Edgar E. Robinson of the
History department will lecture at
Columbia University, New York City,
this summer. He will give two gradu
ate courses, one on "Political Parties,"
and one on "The Westward Move
ment." The department of history at
Columbia has a practice of adding to
its staff in the summer, men from the
West and South and usually from the
Middiewest.
