# CABBAGES and THINGS
## 
### Monroe Eaton 
THE GOAT'S WHISKERS
SOCIETY PUTS OFF A SKIT
We agree that the Ram's Head show
was a howling, hair-raising success
but we suspect that the complexions
of some of the actors were decidedly
off color. Anyway, we are very much
opposed to the use of rouge.
♦ * *
Out of fear that the matter may
not be given due consideration by the
staff, Cabbages is reserving until
called for this space for the JOKE in
the Rover Boy contest, which was to
be preceded by a Row of Bums or
something like that:

From a careful consideration of the
behavior of Drucilla, Dora, Caroline,
and others, we have been inspired with
a solution (non-alcoholic) for the cam
pus date problem.
