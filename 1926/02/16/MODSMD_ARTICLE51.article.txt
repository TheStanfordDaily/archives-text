# Gordon Elected Branner President in Heavy Vote
## 
### 
Leslie O. Gordon was elected presi
dent of Branner Club at the second
election held in the hall last night.
One hundred and twenty-seven mem
bers voted at the polls which is the
largest number ever recorded in an
election at Branner.
In closely contested ballotting John
C. McDermott was named treasurer
of the club. Other new officers for the
remainder of the year are: Orvin P.
Fry, vice-president; Malcolm Jones,
secretary; Floyd Forker, Interhall
representative; A. Perry Banta, senior
representative; John E. Truman, jun
ior representative; Paul Forker, sopho
more representative.
