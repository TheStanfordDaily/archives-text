# Stanley Weigel Chosen To Head Men's Council
## 
### 
Stanley A. Weigel was chosen chair
man of the Men's Council at a meet
ing held yesterday afternoon, to suc
ceed Robert P. Myers, chairman for
the past half year. Merrill Armour
was chosen secretary, succeeding
Weigel in the position.
Personnel of the Council includes
Stanley Weigel, Merrill Armour, Wil
liam Hobro, Charles Stratton, and
Horace Edmondson.
