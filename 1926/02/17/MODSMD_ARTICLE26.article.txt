# EX COMMITTEE NOTES
## 
### 
A new roll was called last night at
the weekly Executive Committee meet
ing when Biff Hoffmann, George
Bogue, Don Robesky, and Louis Gas
par were ushered into the positions
left vacant by the retiring members,
Karl Doer, Bob Bailard, Bill Kerr, and
Don Kropp. Bailard did not serve his
full term.
Introductions and an informal dis
! cussion preceded a short business ses
sion in which the following minutes
were recorded:
M.S.C. That the election returns of
Tuesday, February IG, as submitted
by Jack Sutro, Chairman of the Elec
i tion Board, be accepted.
M.S.C. That Helen Rittenliouse be
appointed as Student Member at
Large on the Quad Board.
M.S.C. That the following date be
granted: Phi Kappa Psi, informal,
! May 28.
