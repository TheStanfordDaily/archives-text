# STANFORD DAMES MEET
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames Club will meet
tomorrow afternoon at 3:15 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse for an "All-
Dame" program presented by seven
members of tho club. Those taking
part in the program include Mrs. Stan
ley Marvin, Mrs. L. Bolenbaugh, Mrs.
C. R. Sattgast, Mrs. Fred Buss, Mrs.
Charles Burch, Mrs. F. S. Balyeat, Mrs.
W. L. Bradshaw. Refreshmens will be
served following the program.
