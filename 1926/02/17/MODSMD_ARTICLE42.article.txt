# U.C. to Choose Speakers For Joffre Medal Debate
## 
### 
University of California, Feb. 17. —
(P.I.P.) —Three speakers and one al
ternate will be chosen tonight at the
tryout for the Joffre Medal Debate
team which will meet Stanford in
April.
The subject of tonight's debate will
be announced three hours before the
tryout is'held, and will be connected
with the general subject of "France
and the Locarno Agreement."
