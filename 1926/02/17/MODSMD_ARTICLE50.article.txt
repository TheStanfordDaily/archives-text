# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON meets at 7
o'clock tonight in the Chemistry
building. Tomorrow at 1 o'clock the
fraternity will have its Quad picture
taken at the Art Gallery steps.
PUBLICITY COMMITTEE for the
Fiesta will meet at 8:15 Tuesday
evening downstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse, It is imperative that all
(members of the committee be pres
ent.
ALL USHERS will please report at
the Assembly hall promptly at 7:30
o'clock for the San Francisco Sym
phony concert.
WOMEN'S ECONOMICS CLUB will
hold an important meeting at 4:45
downstairs in the Women's Club
bouse to elect officers and adopt a
constitution.
WEDNESDAY
ZOOLOGY CLUB will meet at the
Women's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock to
night.
SIGMA XI AN 10 SCIENCE ASSOCIA
TION meet tonight at 8 o'clock in
the Faculty Clubhouse.
COSMOPOLITAN CLUB Quad, picture
will be taken at 12:45 o'clock in
front of the Art Gallery. All mem
bers are requested to be present.
RADIO CLUB meets tonight at 8
o'clock in the Corporation Yard
shack.

THURSDAY
AI.L USHRRS will please report at
Ike Assembly hall promptly at 7:30
o'clock for the Peninsula Music As
sociation concert.
