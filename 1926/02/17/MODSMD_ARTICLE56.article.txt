# Shrine Temple Secured For University Dances
## 
### 
Southern California, Feb. 16. —(P.1.P.)
—A1 Malaikah Shrine Temple will be
dedicated for university use at the
second all-university dance of the year
on March 5. Approximately 6500 can
be accommodated and the proximity
of the building makes it a splendid
location for all future social functions.
