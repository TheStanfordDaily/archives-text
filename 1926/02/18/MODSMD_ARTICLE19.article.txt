# Fletcher Walker Chosen Chairman of M.E. Society
## 
### 
Fletcher Walker was chosen to head the
Stanford student branch, American So
ciety of Mechanical Engineers, at a meet
ing of the society held last night for the
election of officers for the rest of the
year. Walker will succeed Court J. Ken
niston as chairman of the organization.
Other officers elected by the engineers
arc: vice-chairman, George Hulstede;
secretary-treasurer, John Carriere.
