# FEBRUARY ISSUE OF LITERARY MAGAZINE APPEARS TOMORROW
## 
### 
February's issue of the Stanford Lit I
will appear on the campus tomorrow. |
Subscribers' copies will be distributed
in the postoffice boxes tonight. There |
will be no Quad sale, but extra maga
zines may be purchased at the Book
store and at Morey's smokeshops.
Appearing in this month's magazine
are "Heradias," a modern story, by
Anne Ilussell; a satirical essay, |
"Smoke Rings," by Theodore C. Achil
les; and "The Doom of Penuria," a
parody play by A. Grove Day. One
of the attractions of the Lit is its
extended review section of campus
tiramatics.
