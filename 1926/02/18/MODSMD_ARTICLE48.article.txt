# WOMEN LEARN TOE-WRITING
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF NEVADA (P.1.P.)

—Feb. 16.—Women in one of the phys
ical education classes of the university
are being coached in the art of writing
with their toes by their instructor.
She requires that each of the eight
women write her name with chalk
each time before the class is dis
missed. After several periods of prac
tice in toe-writing, the majority of the
class has become quite proficient.
