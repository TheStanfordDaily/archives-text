# TRYOUTS FOR FIESTA SCHEDULED TUESDAY
## 
### 
First tryouts for the Fiesta will be
held Tuesday evening at 7 o'clock in
Roble gymnasium. Women will try
out mainly for dancing parts, and are
asked if possible to wear dancing cos
tumes such as are worn in the classes
at the gymnasium. A few costumes
will be provided at the gymnasium for
those who do not have them.
It is not necessary for women to
have been enrolled in dancing classes
to try out for the Fiesta. All women
are urged by the Fiesta heads to try
out.
Further tryouts will be held later,
and definite decisions as to dramatic
leads for the pageant will be deferred
until spring quarter.
