# STANFORD GLEE CLUB BROADCASTS TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club will give a
concert tonight from 8 to 9 o'clock
over radio station KPO of San Fran
cisco. Several numbers which were
given in the "Novelties" will be broad
casted.
Ensemble as well as quartet num
bers are included in the program, and
the Hawaiian trio will play several
selections. Clarence Young, Tom
Drake, Lew Foote, Sheldon Faye, Jack
Ross, Charles Moore, Clarence McCue,
Robert Reed, Charles Stratton, Leon
ard Dobson, Louis Root, Carlton
Byrne, and John Palstine are among
those who will take part.
