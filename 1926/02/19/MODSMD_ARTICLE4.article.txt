# SENIORS MEET 1927 IN CLASS BASEBALL OPENER WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Senior and junior baseball teams
meet Wednesday in the opening game
of the interclass baseball series. The
tournament will be a round-robin af
fair, each class playing the other
classes before the competition ends.
The complete six-game schedule is
as follows:
Feb. 24 —Seniors vs. Juniors.
Feb. 25 —Freshmen vs. Sophomores.
Feb. 26 —Juniors vs. Freshmen.
Mar. I—Seniors1 —Seniors vs. Sophomores.
Mar. 2 —Juniors vs. Sophomores.
Mar. 3 —Seniors vs. Freshmen.
C. W. Davis announced that class
numerals will be given to the winning
team as was done in interclass foot
ball. The teams are expected to be
unusually strong this year because any
man released from the varsity base
ball squad by Coach Wolter is eligible
to compete.
The games are scheduled to start
at four o'clock and will be six innings
in length. The classes are required
to furnish their own balls, but catch
er's equipment will be supplied by the
Intramural department. Practices may
be held on any intramural field.
Dick McKey has been appointed
manager of the freshman baseball
nine, Kenneth Graham is managing the
juniors, and Gilbert McDermont the
seniors. The sophomore manager has
not been chosen yet.
