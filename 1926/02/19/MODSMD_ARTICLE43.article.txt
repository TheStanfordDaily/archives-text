# U.C. SENIORS PUBLISH MEMOIRS OF WHEELER
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA,
Feb. 18. — (P.1.P.) — Choosing "The

Abundant Life" as the title for the
memoirs of President Emeritus Ben
jamin Ide Wheeler, the class of 1926
has completed all arrangements for
the publication of the volume. The
book is to be the graduation gift of
the class to the university.
