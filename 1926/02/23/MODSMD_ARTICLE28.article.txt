# Toyon Club Will Hold Musicale, Open House
## 
### 
Toyon Club will give a musicale on
Sunday evening, March 7, at 7:30
o'clock. Several well-known artists
have been secured for the program
which will include violin, piano, and
vocal solos. Open house will follow
the program.
