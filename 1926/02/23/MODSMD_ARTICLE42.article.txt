# 12-SCENE PLAY GIVEN
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
SEATTLE, Feb. 22. — (P.l.P.)—The
first collegiate presentation of "The
Beggar on Horseback," a play in 12
scenes by Marc Connelly and George
Kaufman, and requiring the efforts of
150 people, was made here Friday and
Saturday evenings. This was the third
non-professional presentation of the
play.
