# ANNOUNCEMENTS

TUESDAY
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE SOCIETY will
hold is regular bi-monthly testimo

associated with the University are
invited to attend.

Chemistry building at 7 o'clock.

hearse with the women of the ora
torio chorus.
