# Professor Campbell Will Attend Oxford Meeting
## 
### 
Douglas H. Campbell, professor
emeritus of botany, will leave for Eng
land sometime in the middle of sum
mer to attend a meeting of the British
Association for the Advancement of
Science at Oxford. The meeting will
take place toward the end of July and
the beginning of August.
After leaving England, Dr. Campbell
will spend some time in travel on the
continent. He intends returning to
Stanford early in the winter.
