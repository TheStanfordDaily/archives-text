# Martin Completes Work On Manuscript of Book
## 
### 
Professor Ernest Gale Martin of the
Department of Physiology has com
pleted his manuscript for the eleventh
edition of his book, "The Human
Body." The book is being published
by Henry Holt and Company and is
on the press.
Proof on the book has not yet been
read, but it should appear in about
six or eight weeks. It will be used as
a textbook not only at Stanford but
at several colleges throughout the
United States.
