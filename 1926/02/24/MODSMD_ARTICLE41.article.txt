# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
WEDNESDAY
W.A.A. BOARD meets tonight at 7:15
o'clock. The open meeting of W.A.A.
has been postponed.
JUNIOR CLASS meets at 12:50 o'clock
in Room 214. All juniors are urged
to be present.
HISTORY CLUB meets at 7:45 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
SCHUBERT CLUB will meet at 7:15
o'clock in the organ gallery to re
hearse with the women of the ora
torio chorus.
THURSDAY
ALL STUDENTS in the Military de
partment who expect to attend the
Advance Course Camp next summer
are requested to meet in the class
room of the Military Department at
4:30 o'clock Thursday afternoon.
ALPHA PI ZETA Quad picture will be
taken at 1:15 o'clock today at the
Art Gallery.
