# Professor A.C. Guerard To Lecture on "Esperanto"
## 
### 
"Esperanto" will be the subject of
a lecture by Professor Albert L. Guer
ard, professor of General Literature,
as a part of the exercises planned by
the World Goodwill Day committee
of Palo Alto. The program is to be
given in the Palo Alto Community
House tomorrow night.

The amateur national wrestling meet
will be held at O.A.C. in April.
