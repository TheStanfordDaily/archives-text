# University of Washington Women Sponsor Girls' Day
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
SEATTLE, Feb. 22— (P.I.P.)— Involu
tions for the first Sports Day for girls
on March 6, sponsored by the Wo
men's Athletic Association, have been
sent to all high schools within 100
miles of Seattle. The purpose of the
day is to get high school girls to
gether for a day of athletic competi
tion. The association hopes to make it
an annual affair.
