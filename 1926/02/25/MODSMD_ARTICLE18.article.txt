# Riverside Poloists Unable To Meet Cards in Match
## 
###

Riverside polo squad were unsuccess
ful, according to an announcement
made by the Military department. The
team could not remain in the north
to play the Stanford four. A game with
the Arizona team in the future is cer
tain. This team is one of the best
polo aggregations in the West. Ari
zona's squad defended the West in
the Eastern Intercollegiate matches
last year. The game will be played
at Stanford, according to present
plans, but no exact date for the con
test has been set.
