# Students May Attend Opera at Low Prices
## 
### 
Students wishing to see perform
ances of the San Carlo Grand Opera
Company given in San Francisco at
the Civic Auditorium Friday evening.
Saturday afternoon, and Saturday eve
ning, may secure seats at fifty cents
each upon presentation of their stu
dent body cards.
