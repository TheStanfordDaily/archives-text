# Junior Sport Managers Named at Class Meeting
## 
### 
Managers of the junior interclass
athletic teams were named at the
meeting of the junior class held yes
terday afternoon by Kenneth C. Gra
ham, president of 1927.
The managers chosen were: Howard
A. Sheets, baseball manager; Robert
E. Williams, swimming manager;
Cranston Holman, tennis manager;
Lawrence T. Babcock, track manager.
