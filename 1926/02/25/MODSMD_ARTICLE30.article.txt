# University Weekly Calendar
## 
### 
THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 25
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital, by Mr. Al*
lent, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—Thursday Evening Confer
ence, at the home of Dr. David
Starr Jordan. Subject: "The
Ethics of Foreign Trade."
FRIDAY, FEBRUARY 26
4:30 p.m.—Women's Education Club,
at the Campus School. Discussion
on "The Teacher's Place in the
Community." Open meeting.
4:15 p.m.—Seventh Lecture on Pat
ent Law, by Mr. Leonard S. Lyon
of the Los Angeles Bar. Topic:
"The Ownership and Transfer of
Patent Rights." Room 12.
SATURDAY, FEBRUARY 27
10:00 a.m. —Eighth Lecture on Patent
Law, by Mr. Lyon. Topic: "Vio
lation of Patent Rights, The Par
ties Liable, The Liability Incur
red." Room 12.

For selling the greatest number of
copies of Cactus, the University of
Texas yearbook, a student was
awarded a free round trip to Europe.
Other prizes were trips to New York
and New Orleans.
