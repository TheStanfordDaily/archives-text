# Louis Untermeyer Will Talk on American Poetry
## 
### 
Louis Untermeyer, one of America's
leading poets, will lecture on "The
New Era in American Poetry" in the
Assembly hall at 7:30 o'clock Tuesday
night. Untermeyer is well known for
his two anthologies, Modern American
Poetry and Modern as
well as for his poems.
This is the first transcontinental lec
ture tour ever made by the poet, who
is now offering several lecture courses,
including "The Critic's Half-Holiday,"
"This Singing World," "The Younger
Voices," "The New Era in American
Poetry," and "Modern British and
American Poetry: A Contrast."
