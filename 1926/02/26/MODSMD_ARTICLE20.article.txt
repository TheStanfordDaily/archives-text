## 
### 
Alpha Kappa Lambda and Branner
I entered the semi-final round of the
intramural tennis tournament yester
day with straight set victories over
Los Arcos and the Breakers.
James Mansfield defeated M. J.
Shannon, Los Arcos, 6-0, 6-0, and Shel
don Milliken won decisively from Les
lie Dobbins, 6-0, 6-1, to give the A.K.L.
team its fourth straight victory in tho
tournament.
In a hard-fought match Harold Way,
Branner, gave Stanley Cook, Breakers,
a beating, 9-7, 6-4, while Webster Otis
beat Rodney George easily, 6-0, 6-3.
This gives the hall team the right to
play in the semi-finals next week.
