# PHI LAMBDA UPSILON WILL MEET THURSDAY
## 
### 
The first meeting of the informal
research conference, recently spon
sored by the Phi Lambda Upsilon, hon
orary chemistry fraternity, held its
first meeting last Thursday at which
W. C. Fernelius, chemistry graduate,
spoke. Next Thursday, Dana Burks,
Jr., will be the speaker.
The purpose of the conference is
primarily to bring those persons in
terested in, or doing, research work
together, to show them the problems
which are having to be solved, and let
them know what is being done in the
different parts of the Chemistry de
partment.
