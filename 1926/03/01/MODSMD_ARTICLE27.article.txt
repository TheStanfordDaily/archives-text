# "Young China in Action" Is Topic of Arnold's Talk
## 
### 
"Young China in Action" is the topic
of a lecture to be given by Julean
Arnold in the Little Theatre at 4:15
o'clock Wednesday afternoon. Arnold
is the Commercial at San
Francisco for the Bureau of Foreign
and Domestic Commerce of the United
States Department.
Arnold, who is a graduate of the
University of California, has been in
China for about twenty-five years. He
is at present on a six months' furlough
and is touring the United States,
speaking on China.
