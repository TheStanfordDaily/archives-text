# O.A.C. FRATERNITY IS BOTHERED BY RODENT
## 
### 
Finding the buttons of their clothes
missing in the morning when they are
in a hurry to dress for class has
been the latest trouble of one of the
O.A.C. fraternities. The thief was par
ticular, for he only selected bone but
tons on clothes that rested on tables
and chairs.
Investigation of the mysterious dis
appearances led to the discovery that
a huge rat had carried off the buttons
after' he had nibbled them from the
clothes. Poison and traps have been
set to put a stop to the infamous be
havior of the culprit.
