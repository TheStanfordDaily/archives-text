# Freshmen, Senior Women Vie for Basketball Title
## 
### 
Senior and freshman women will
tangle in the third game of the cham
pionship basketball series tomorrow
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock on Roble
field. Each of the teams has lost one
game already, the seniors having
dropped a game to the sophomores,
and the freshmen having been downed
by the juniors.
The seniors have the advantage of
more experience as a team, and are
favored to win.
