# CARD DEBATERS WILL TALK AT M.E. CHURCH
## 
### 
Theodore F. Baer and Stanley A.
Weigel, members of the debate team
which recently returned from a tour
of Utah universities, will discuss "The
Freedom of the Modern Generation"
tomorrow night at the Methodist
church of San Bruno.
Weigel will defend the status of
the present age while Baer will talk
against the freedom of the modern
generation. Leland G. Stanford, Stan
ford law graduate, is pastor of the
San Bruno church.
