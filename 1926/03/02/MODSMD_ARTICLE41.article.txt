# Stanford Leaflet Deals With School of Biology
## 
### 
Copies of the "Concerning Stanford"
leaflet were mailed to alumni and
friends of the University this week by
the registrar's office. "The School of
Biology Unites Related Science" is
the subject of the March issue of
Stanford's informational bulletin.
Students wishing to secure a copy
of the leaflet may obtain it in the
President's office.
