# Robertson, Dolin Secured For Club Program April 16
## 
### 
Harrv Robertson, San Francisco
tenor, has been obtained as a soloist
for the Glee Club's home concert,
which will be given in the Assembly
hall April IG. Max Dolin, violinist of
the California Theatre, and his trio,
have already been secured by the
singers as special attractions for the
recital in which the club presents its
music for the year.
