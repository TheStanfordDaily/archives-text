# INTRAMURAL MEETING WILL DISCUSS RULES
## 
### 
Athletic managers of campus living
groups will meet at 4:15 o'clock to
morrow afternoon in Encina gymna
sium with C. W. Davis, director of
intramural sports, to settle questions
regarding intramural eligibility rules
and schedules.
The managers are to elect three
representatives to form a committee
to handle intramural athletics and
make drawings for next quarter's ac
tivities. Sports which are scheduled
for spring quarter are baseball, golf,
handball, relay, and track.
All managers of teams must get
their entries into the intramural of
fice before Monday.
