# ROLAND S. HAUCK TO ADDRESS ADVERTISERS
## 
### 
"Putting: on the Oliver Fellow's
Shoes" will he the subject of a talk
'to be given tonight by Roland L.
Hauck, former Palo Alto advertising
man, at the meeting of the Stanford
Advertising Club of Palo Alto to be
held in the Cardinal hotel at 6:15
o'clock.
Hauck, who is a member of the class
of 1927, is now associated with the
J. Walter Thompson Advertising
! Agency in San Francisco.
