# LETTERMEN ELECT DAVID TO CAPTAIN 1927 VARSITY FIVE
## 
### 
Nelson David was chosen cap
tain of next year's basketball
team by the ten letter men at an
election held at Encina gym yes
terday afternoon at 4 o'clock.
David is a forward and has played
two years on the varsity besides
a year on the 1927 freshman Ave.
This season he was kept out
of a number of games in mid
season by illness, but came back
to take part in the Nevada games
and the California series. He is
generally credited with having the
best shooting eye of any player
on the varsity.
David is registered from Al
hambra, California, and is a mem
ber of Phi Delta Theta.
