# COMMITTEES BEGIN WORK ON ACTIVITIES FOR UNIVERSITY DAY
## 
### 
The general committee and sub-com
mittees under Dudley S. DeGroot, '24,
have met several times and are at
work on the plans for University Day,
May 15. University Day is the ob
servance of the 30th anniversary of
the class of '96 and the 10th anniver
sary of the installation of President
Wilbur, '96. There will be reunions
of the classes of '96, '01, '06, '11, '16,
'21. and '24.
The sororities, fraternities, and
clubs will give dinners for the alumni
on Friday, May 14, which will be fol
lowed by La Fiesta Lagunita. Satur
day, May 15, there will be luncheons
of the different classes, a girls' hockey
game, a military review, a polo game,
an organ recital in the afternoon, and
a dinner with entertainment and dance
in the evening. There is a possibility
of the Pacific Coast Conference Track
Meet being changed from May 22 to
May 15. If so, the meet will be held
the afternoon of University Day.

meeting yesterday afternoon are as
follows:
