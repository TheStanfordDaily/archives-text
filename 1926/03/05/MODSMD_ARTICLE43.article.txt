# Nathan A. Rhine Receives Commission in U. S. Army
## 
### 
Nathan A. Rhine, '27, took the oath
of office at the Presidio, San Fran
cisco, this afternoon, and received his
commission in the United States Army
Reserve as second lieutenant in the
Air Service of the Ninth Corps Area.
He recently passed a flying test and
Army examination at Crissy Field.
Rhine has been flying several years
and was responsible for the letters
dropped from the air above the Stan
ford stadium during the California
track meet last April.
