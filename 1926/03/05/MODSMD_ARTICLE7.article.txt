# Babe Chess Players to Meet California Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford's seven-man freshman
chess team will play the California
first-year men tomorrow at Berkeley.
The Cardinal men held a final drill
last evening, under the direction of
Coach Louis B. Zapoleon.
Douglas R. Fuller is first man on
the Stanford freshman squad. The
other men are rated as follows: sec
ond, Kenneth W. Howard; third, Mil
ton M. Hartman; fourth, Jack O.
Hodges; fifth, Ronald R. Greene; sixth,
John M. Reily; seventh, Louis I.
Mandell.
