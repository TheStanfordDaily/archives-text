# PHYSICAL SOCIETY HEARS REPORTS AT SATURDAY SESSION
## 
### 
Seventy California physicists, repre
senting Stanford, the California Insti
tute of Technology, the University of
California, and the Southern Branch,
met Saturday in the physics depart
ment lecture room for an all-day ses
sion of the Pacific Coast section of
the American Physical Society.
The program of the day comprised
twenty-three ten-minute papers on re
search work in abstract physics prob
lems and a supplementary program
for additional presentations and for
discussion. Professors P. Ashton Ross
and Maurice L. Huggins of Stanford
were included among the speakers.
