# CHESS CLUB NAMES OFFICERS AT MEETING
## 
### 
Officers for the remainder of the
year were elected at the meeting of
the Cardinal Chess Club last night.
They are: president, M Marion Korn
feld; vice-president, Ervin H. Bram
hall; secretary, Douglas R. Fuller;
manager, Kenneth W. Howard; treas
urer, Astrid Arnoldson.
Meetings of the varsity players are
to be held twice a week, on Wednes
days and Mondays, until the California
match on April 8, so that the men may
have more practice. These meetings
will begin at once.
