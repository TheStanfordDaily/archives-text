# Lorna Downes Announces Marriage to Hal Morgan
## 
### 
Lorna K. Downes and Harold G. Mor
gan were married last Saturday. The
ceremony took place in San Jose. Mor
gan is a member of the Alpha Tau
Omega fraternity and Miss Downes
of the Berkeley chapter of the Chi
Omega sorority. She is also a member
of Roble Club on the campus.
