# FENCERS MEET BRANCH, U.S.C. DURING VACATION
## 
### 
A three-man varsity fencing team
will leave for Los Angeles Monday,
March 22, for matches with U.S.C. and
the Southern Branch. Coach Hal Davis
will select the men for the trip this
afternoon, making his choice from
among the following members of the
squad: Ted Criley, Carl Anderson,
Gaeton Zucco, Paul Boodberg, and
Angel Aguerrevere.
