Easter Proclaims Its Springtime Spirit
to the World
At last, all the things that are to be said about spring modes
have been uttered by Paris modistes, couterieres, and milliners. The
spring collection stands complete—with a few changes, perhaps, but
essentially unchanged. In this Easter showing you will find every
thing to make your new Easter wardrobe a success —smart apparel,
with every necessary article of accessory to complete the outfit.
A visit is certainly worth your time. It will especially be worth your
while to visit our beautiful Easter Windows this week.
As a special Easter offering we will give a ten-per-cent discount
on all ladies' dresses and coats from now until Easter. Here is an
opportunity to save on your outfit, and yet not sacrifice quality or
style.
