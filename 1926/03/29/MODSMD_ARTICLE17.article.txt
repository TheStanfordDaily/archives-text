# PASTEBOARDS TO GO ON SALE TOMORROW FOR MILITARY BALL
## 
### 
Tickets for the Military Ball
which will be held on April 9 will
go on sale tomorrow at the Hook
store and can also be obtained
from any member of the Military
department.
The entire staff is making care
ful preparations for the event.
The pavilion will be decorated in
a strictly military tone, and a
martial atmosphere will prevail
throughout.
Homer Dombaugh is general
chairman; George Baker is in
charge of the music; Clark Cy
pher is arranging for the deco
rations; and Kenneth Chantry
has charge of the programs and
tickets.
Tickets will cost $3.50 apiece.
