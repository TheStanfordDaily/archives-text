# Miss Yost Will Attend Western Deans Conclave
## 
### 
Miss Yost is planning to attend the
Western Conference of Deans which
will be held at the University of Cali
fornia, Southern Branch, from April
12 to 15, according to a statement by
her today.
The convention will consist of a
joint meeting of the Western Division
of Deans of Women, and the Western
Division of Associated Women Stu
dents. The program will be announced
later.
