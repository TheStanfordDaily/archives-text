# Dr. Wilbur to Address Graduate Women's Club
## 
### 
President Wilbur will speak on "The
Trend of Graduate Study" at a meet
ing of the Graduate Women's Club to
morrow afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse. The meeting is open to all
graduate women. Tea will be served
at 4 as Dr. Wilbur will speak at 5
o'clock.
