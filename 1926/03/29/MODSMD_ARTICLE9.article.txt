# REGISTRATION SPORT DANCE SPONSORED BY LITERARY BOARD
## 
### 
With Picadilly punch and three
prize dances promised, the Stan
ford Lit sponsors a registration
sport dance tonight in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Tyroler's or
chestra will provide music for
the affair which will commence
at 9 and end at 1 o'clock.
Red-headed men will be admit
ted at half price tonight and a
large representation of the origi
nal Cardinals is expected. Large
prizes are expected to be donated
for the three special dances.
