# STANFORD R. O .T. C. WILL BE INSPECTED BY ARMY OFFICRS
## 
### 
Inspection of the Stanford Univer
sity R.O.T.C. unit by the officers from
the War Department General Staff will
be carried on Thursday, April 1. Lieu
tenant-Colonel Lewis Brown and Major
Martin Wise from the Washington of
fice will go over the work of the Mili
tary department with Major William
D. Geary, head of the Stanford depart
ment. The inspection is for the pur
pose of determining whether or not
Stanford shall receive the rating of
distinguished college.
Regular classes in military work will
be covered Thursday morning, and
practical drill will be inspected in the
afternoon when all military depart
ment students will attend, stated Ma
jor Geary yesterday.
