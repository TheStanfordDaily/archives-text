# Wilbur Addresses Graduate Women on Pacific Relations
## 
### 
President Wilbur spoke on the "Pacific
Relations Commission" at the Graduate
Women's Club tea, which was given in
the Women's Clubhouse yesterday after
noon. He told of his personal experiences
at the Pacific Relations Conference held
in Honolulu last summer, of which con
vention he was the presiding officer.
About fifty members were present. Mrs.
Wilbur and Miss Yost were honor guests.
A short business meeting was held. Elec
tion of new officers will take place at the
next meeting which will be held in the
Clubhouse Tuesday afternoon, April 6.
A nominating committee will be appointed
by the president, Miss Helen Marburg.
