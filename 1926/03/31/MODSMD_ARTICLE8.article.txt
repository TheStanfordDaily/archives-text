# All-University Boxing Tourney Begins in April
## 
### 
The All-University boxing tourna
ment will be held in the latter part of
April. Every man in the University is
eligible to enter and varsity boxer 3
will not be excluded.
Winners of the seven divisions will
be given medals. Preliminary bouts
are to be held throughout the week
previous to the date of final matches.
In the tournament held last year the
following received medals: Floyd Tull,
heavyweight; R. Farmin, light-heavy
weight; M. R. Schall, middleweight;
F. L. Martin, welterweight; Jack Par
sons, lightweight; C. A. Miron, feath
erweight.
