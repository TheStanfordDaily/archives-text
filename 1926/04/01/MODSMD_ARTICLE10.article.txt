# BABE TRACK SQUAD ELECTS McKINNON TO CAPTAIN TEAM
## 
### 
Alex M. McKinnon of Los An
geles was elected captain of the
freshman track team yesterday
afternoon at a meeting of the
babe squad.
McKinnon was captain of the
Polytechnic High School track
team in Los Angeles last year
and is the holder of the state
championship for the 880-yard
run. He is the best half-miler on
the present freshman team and
also a good quarter-miler and re
lay man.
