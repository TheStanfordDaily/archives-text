# JOFFRE DEBATE DATE ANNOUNCED BY BAER
## 
### 
April 20 has been set as the date for
the annua] Joffre debate, according to
Theodore F. IJaer, debate manager.
Stanley A. Weigel, George N. Crocker,
Fred Combellack, and C. Craven Scott
will represent Stanford against the
University of California in Berkeley.
"France and the Locarno Treaties"
will be the general subject for the de
bate this year, and a specific question
will be submitted to the debaters two
hours before the contest. The question
asked in the Stanford tryouts was,
"Are the guarantees of the Locarno
Pacts such that France is justified in
effecting a radical reduction of arma
ment?"
