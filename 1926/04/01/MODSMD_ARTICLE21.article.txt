# Professor Anderson Gives General Phonetics Course
## 
### 
A three-unit course in General Pho
netics will be given by Professor
Frederick Anderson, of the Romanic
Languages department, this quarter.
The course, number E2Ol, will meet
from 3 to 4 o'clock on Mondays and
Fridays. Those interested should see
Professor Anderson and sign up as
soon as possible.
