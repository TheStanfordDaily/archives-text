# Doris Overman, '16, Weds Santa Barbara Resident
## 
### 
Miss Doris Overman, *16, was mar
ried last week in Santa Barbara to Ed
ward MacLaren Howard. Mrs. How
ard, who is the youngest daughter of
Mrs. Edwin Cooke Overman and the
late Judge Overman, had a studio in
Santa Barbara where she practiced
painting and crafts for several years.
