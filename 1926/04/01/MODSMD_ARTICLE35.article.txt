# HARTRANFT'S AUTO IN PALO ALTO COLLISION
## 
### 
Automobiles driven by Glenn Hart
ranft, Palo Alto, and E. E. George,
San Francisco, collided at Emerson
street and Homer avenue Tuesday
afternoon. The latter's car incurred a
damaged right front fender. Both driv
ers were going along Emerson street
in opposite directions. The accident
occurred when George attempted to
turn into a garage. Hartranft is a
Stanford student and was last year's
track captain.
