# Art Department Offering Course in Metal Craft
## 
### 
For the first time in three years the
Art department is offering a course in
metal craft. Twelve students are regis
tered in the course, which meets every
afternoon this quarter under the di
rection of Professor Clark, head of
the department.
Work is done on copper and silver.
Among the articles made are book
ends, bowls, napkin rings, desk-sets,
and jewelry.
