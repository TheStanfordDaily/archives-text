# Easter Sunrise Service Will Be Held at Lagunita
## 
### 
An Easter sunrise service will be
held at 7 o'clock Sunday morning be
side the Lagunita boathouse with Miss
Isabel Capps, secretary of the Stan
ford Y.W.C.A., as chairman of the af
fair.
The young people's societies of the
Palo Alto churches are sponsoring the
service, which is an annual event. Rex
Bell, the Y.M.C.A. secretary, will be
the speaker of the hour, while Baylor
Brooks will be music director. Instru
mental selections and several numbers
rendered by the Y.M.C.A, quartet will
be given Sunday morning.
