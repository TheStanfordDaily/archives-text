# Nevada Graduating Class Is Largest in History
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF NEVADA— (P.1.P.)
—The largest class in the history of
this university will be graduated in
June when ninety seniors of the vari
ous colleges of this campus will re
ceive their diplomas. The College of
Arts and Science ranks first with sixty
eight graduates to its credit, and the
remaining are divided between Engi
neering and Agriculture.
