# Harold R. Gaw Appointed Branner Hall Custodian
## 
### 
Harold P. Gaw, former custodian of
Sequoia hall, lias succeeded Percival
I. V. Spencer, '25, as custodian of
Brannor hall and Harry W. Call has
been appointed to fill the vacancy left
by Gaw at Sequoia.
Spencer was the first University ad
ministrative representative in Branner
and was in charge of the dormitory
during the days of its early existence.
He is engaged in law work at Ingle
wood, leaving the campus Friday after
noon.

A prize will be awarded to the best
woman dancer at the University of
Ohio to be chosen by the tryout
method.
