# Practice House Used for Work in Home Economics
## 
### 
Girls of the Home Economics depart
ment at the University of Texas are
preparing for a "Better Homes Cam
paign" to be carried on the latter part
of April, using the practice house as
a model.
The practice house is operated by
the department and serves as a means
of unifying home economics work and
gives an opportunity of practicing on
a fatnily schedule the work learned in
the laboratory. It is managed by girls
majoring in the department.
A monogrammed "Il T " appeared re
recently on the right trouser-leg of a
new blue suit worn by a student of
the University of Indiana.
The University of Kansas now offers
a course in pistol and revolver shoot
ing.

A course in elementary Japanese is
being offered at the University ol
Washington to meet the demand foi
more work in oriental languages.
