# CHESS PLAYERS POINT TO CALIFORNIA SERIES
## 
### 
Consultation matches to strengthen
the play of the chess varsity were
held at the meeting of the Cardinal
I Chess Club last night. The team for
the California match was definitely
announced as including Gerald O.
Vaughn, Hugo Leistner, Gregory M.
Kranzthor, John L. Blackford, Paul
Boodborg, M. Marion Kornfeld, and
Adin Henderson.
The match will be held Thursday
evening in the Union, and will be pre
ceded by a banquet which will start I
at 6 o'clock.
