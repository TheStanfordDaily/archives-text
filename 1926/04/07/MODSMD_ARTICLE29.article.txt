# Marjorie Treat Announces Engagement to Schofield
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Marjorie
Treat, '28, to Eugene Schofieid, '26, was
announced this week. Miss Treat, whose
home is in Salinas, is not registered this
quarter.
Schofieid lives in Palo Alto and has
been prominent in campus athletics. He
is a member of Theta Xi.
