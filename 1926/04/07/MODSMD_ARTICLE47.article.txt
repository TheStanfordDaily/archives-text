# TOYON PLANS SMOKER FOR FRESHMAN MEN
## 
### 
The third annual Toyon Club-Fresh
man smoker will be featured as a part
of the "Friendship Week" that is being
observed this week by the freshman class.
The smoker, which is to be given in the
Toyon Lobby Wednesday evening at 7:15
is expected to draw a large crowd from
Encina Hall because of the very success
ful affairs held the past two years.
Jack Sutro, chairman for the smoker,
has promised refreshments as well as a
novel program for the freshman guests.
