# Chicago Art Institute Man To Give Talk on Etchings
## 
### 
Frank van Sloan, formerly of the
Chicago Art Institute, will give a lec
ture on etchings at the Palo Alto
Public Library tonight at S o'clock.
The lecture is under the auspices of
the Palo Alto Art Club and will be
of general interest to art students.
