# STANFORD TO DEBATE UTAH AGGIES IN MAY
## 
### 
Stanford debaters will meet the
Utah Agricultural College team in a
return debate on the prohibition ques
tion. The forensic contest will be held
on the campus in the middle of May.

No definite date has been set for
the Stanford tryouts, which will be
held within .two weeks. Only those
men who have participated in one
varsity debate or in none will be
eligible to try out. Among the debaters
eligible the following will make a
strong bid for the tenm: Philip Cava
lero, Raymond R. Bruce, Harold W.
Peterson, and Barrel It. Parker.
