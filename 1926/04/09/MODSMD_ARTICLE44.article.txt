# BENNETT ELECTED NEW PRESIDENT OF JOURNALISTS CLUB
## 
### 
Harry M. Bennett, managing editor
of the Daily Palo Alto, was elected
president of the Stanford Journalists
Club at an election held last night.
Frederic W. Specrs was named vice
president of the organization, Florence
Gallagher was elected secretary, and
Francis W. Mosher was selected as
treasurer. The club voted to co-operate
with the Journalism department in
publishing the California Scholastic
Association quarterly magazine.
The club appointed Alfred B. Post
and Speers joint editors of the pub
lication, while Herbert A. Klein will
be circulation manager for the next
issue.
