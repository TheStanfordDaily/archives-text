# EUPHRONIA MEMBERS TRY OUT FOR DEBATE
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Euphronia debate
with Mills College scheduled for April
29 will be held tonight at 7:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
"Resolved, That this house is op
posed to co-education in American col
leges" is the subject for the tryouts.
Three members of the society will be
selected to compose the team.
