# EXPENSES FOUND LOW IN EUROPEAN COLLEGE
## 
### 
Expenses at the University of Paris
are very low, according to American
students enrolled there. A yearly ex
penditure of $600 is found to be the
usual cost per student.
Tuition is at present $2.80 a year,
and even for courses requiring an un
usual number of books, this item rare
ly exceeds $15. Two dollars a week
will provide rooms in the Latin quar
ter, and meals can be obtained for
15 or 20 cents in many restaurants.
Nearly 3500 American students, of
whom more than half are women, are
enrolled in the University of Paris.
