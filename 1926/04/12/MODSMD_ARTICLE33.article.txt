# Eliot Mears to Address San Jose College Women
## 
### 
"Rcent Changes in the Aegean" is
the topic of an address Professor Eliot
G. Mears of the Economics department
and the Graduate School of Business
gave before the San Jose branch
of the American Association of Uni
versity Women Saturday afternoon at
3 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Professor Mears discussed the de
velopments in Turxey, Greece, Pales
tine, Syria, and other countries around
the Aegean Sea since the World War.
He is the author of "Modern Turkey,"
and has been American Trade Com
missioner in the Near East.
The afternoon's program included
a group of songs by Mrs. Warren D.
Allen. The public is invited.
