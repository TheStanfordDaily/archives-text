# Juniors Are Asked to Pay Class Dues This Week
## 
### 
Juniors are requested to pay their
class dues this week either to L. B.
Lundborg, the treasurer of the class,
or to the collectors in the various
living groups. Class dues must be paid
in order to obtain a ticket to the
Junior Prom.
