# Scholarship to Radcliffe College Offered by Club
## 
### 
The Radcliffe Club of San Francisco
is offering a scholarship of $300 toward
a year's study at Radcliffe College, to
any California girl whose qualifications
are acceptable to the club. The schol
arship is for the year 1920-1927, and
applications for it should be made in
writing to Miss Maude Fellows, 2
Tamalpais lload, Berkeley, before
April 15. Further information on this
subject may be obtained at the office
of the Dean of Women.
