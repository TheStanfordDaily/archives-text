# Stanford Club of New York Plans "Big Meet Dinner"
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford University
Club of New York, New Jersey, and
Connecticut will celebrate the Califor
nia-Stanford meet with a dinner at the
National Vaudeville Artists' Club, New
York, Saturday evening.
News of the meet and the baseball
game will be sent by telegram direct
to the alumni by Don Liebendorfer.
