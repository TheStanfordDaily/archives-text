# Zoology Club Will Meet Tonight to Elect Head
## 
### 
Because of the Rooters' Rally to
morrow night, the Zoology Club will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse at
7:30 o'clock tonight for the election of
officers. This election is necessary be
cause of the withdrawal of both Sey
mour Smith, '26, and Ludwig Herz,
'25, president and treasurer respec
tively of the organization.
Smith is in Alaska at present where
he is assisting Rich of the United
States Bureau of Fisheries, while Herz
is engaged in advanced work at the
Hopkins Marine Station at Pacific
Grove.
As the meeting will be important all
members are urged to be present.
