# Wellesley Class Makes Profit of $25,000 in Week
## 
### 
The advent of the American girl
into the world of finance was heralded
lately at Wellesley, where a generous
professor donated $1000 to each of his
students on the condition that they
invested the sum in Wall Street. The
profits of the class at the end of one
week amounted to $25,000.
