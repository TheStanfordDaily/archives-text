# Jessie E. Smith Lectures At French Club Meeting
## 
### 
The French Club will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 8 o'clock to
morrow night, immediately following
the Big Meet Rally.
Miss Jessie E. Smith, a member of
the French department, will be the
speaker of the evening. She will lec
ture on Theodore Botrel, a modern
Briton songwriter and poet.
All members of the club are urged
to attend. Refreshments will be served
following the meeting.
