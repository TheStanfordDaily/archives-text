# Weekly Clubhouse Tea to Be Sponsored by Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
The weekly Clubhouse Tea tomorrow
will be sponsored by the Y.W.C.A.,
proceeds to be used on the Clubhouse
debt. Jean Stewart will be in charge.
Esther Greenacre and Pauline Hoff
man will pour tea.
Tables may be secured for $1. Wo
men are urged to drop in and have
tea, whether they have made previous
arrangements or not. Tea will be 25
cents. Eighty-two have already en
gaged bridge tables.
