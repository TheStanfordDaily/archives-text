TUESDAY
ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in
the Memorial Church, at 4.15 o'clock.

is invited.

portant that all members be present.

and others associated with the Uni
versity, are invited.

7:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.

o'clock tonight in Room 84.

the Memorial Church, at 4.15 o'clock.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S CLUB, Infor
mal Tea, in the Women's Clubhouse
at 4:30 o'clock. All graduate women
are invited to attend.

Motion Picture," in the Little Thea
tre, at 7:30 o'clock, by Edward Lang
ley, technical director of the Pick
ford-Fairbanks Studios. The public
is invited.

portant that all members be present.

at 7:30 o'clock tonight in the* Wo

hold its regular bi-monthly testimo
nial meeting at 7:30 on the second
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
Members of the faculty, students,
and others associated with the Uni
versity, are invited.

7:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.

SCHUBERT CLUB will meet at 7:15
o'clock tonight in Room 84.

poration Yard. Nelson P. Case will

tonight in the Women's Clubhouse.
