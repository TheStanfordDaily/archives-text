# Mathematics Professors To Attend Conventions
## 
### 
Professors H. F. Blichfeldt and R. L.
Green of the Department of Mathe
matics, will leave Friday for a short
trip East. They will attend the meeting
of the National Academy of Science at
Washington, D.C., and also that of the
American Mathematical Society, at
New York. They expect to return to
the campus within two weeks. Their
classes will he in charge of assistants
during their absence.
