# Ray W. Smith to Speak On Commercial School
## 
### 
Kay W. Smith, secretary of the Palo
Alto Chamber of Commerce, has been
appointed by the advisory council of the
Summer School for Commercial Secre
taries to speak before seven sectional
meetings in the state of California on the
school. The school for commercial sec
retaries will be held on the campus from
July 25 to July 31. Smith is the manager
of the new school.
