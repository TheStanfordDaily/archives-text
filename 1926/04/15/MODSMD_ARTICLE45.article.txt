# Stanford Graduate Named French University Fellow
## 
### 
Raoul M. May, who served with the
Stanford unit in France during the
World War, and who later received his
A.B. degree here, has been awarded
the American Field Service Fellowship
for French Universities, for the year
1926-27.
Application blanks for the next
award will be ready for distribution in
October, according to Stephen P. Dug
gan, Secretary, New York City.
