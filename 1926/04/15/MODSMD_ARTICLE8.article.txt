# Sequoia Club Conducts Two Tennis Tournaments
## 
### 
Tennis matches in the two tournaments
being conducted by the Sequoia Club
opened Monday. Prizes are offered for
the winner and runnerup of each contest.
Through the efforts of the members of
the club, the Sequoia courts have been
re-marked and supplied with new nets.
Arrangements for the tournaments and
for the improvement of the courts, arc
in charge of David A. Wood, president
of the club.
