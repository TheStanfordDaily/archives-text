# STANFORD GRADUATES ANNOUNCE MARRIAGE
## 
### 
Following a University romance, Miss
Dorothy Marston and Herbert Finlay
Ormsby, both formerly of Stanford, were
married at the. Presbyterian church in
San Rafael Saturday night. Many of
their college friends were present.
A popularity contest, not essentially
for the most beautiful, nor the most in
telligent, nor the most athletic girl,
but for the typical co-ed, has been in
stituted at New York University. The
winner will be crowned at the Wash
ington Square College Spring Fete,

|and will be presented with a silver
loving cup.
