# Washington Limits Sorority Summer Rushing Expenses
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON—
(P.I.P.) —Sororities at the University
of Washington will hereafter he lim
ited in the amount of money to be
spent in summer rushing, in accord
ance with a ruling made by Panhel
lenic at a recent, meeting.
The allowance was fixed at $125, and
will include all expenses for rushes,
hut not for members attending the
parties.
