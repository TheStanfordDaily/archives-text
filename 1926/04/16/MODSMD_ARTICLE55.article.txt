# APPLY FOR DEGREES
## 
### 
Candidates for the degrees of Master
of Arts and Engineer, who are now
registered for their second quarter of
advanced work, and who expect to
complete their requirements by the
end of the summer or autumn quarters,
should file application at the Regis
trar's Office not later than Friday,
April 30. Application blanks may be
obtained there.

the country. At Johns Hopkins they
are holding a contest to determine the
"boiler" that most resembles a wreck.
