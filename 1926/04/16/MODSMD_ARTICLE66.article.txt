# PRESIDENT WILBUR TO PRESIDE AT MEETINGS
## 
### 
President Kay Lyman Wilbur will
preside at the Saturday conference on
"The Awakening Responsibility of
Parents," which is being held by fifty
representative organizations of Ala
meda County in Oakland, April 15, 16,
17, and 24. The Saturday evening sub
ject is "The Teacher and the Chang
ing Education."
Wilbur and Dr. D. Charles Gardner
will also speak at the Sunday evening
services at the dedication of the new
Temple Emanu-El in San Francisco,
The dedication will start Friday night
and last until Sunday evening when
the "Fellowship Services" will be held.
