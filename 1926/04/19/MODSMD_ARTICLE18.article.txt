# WOODWARDS GUESTS OF DINK TEMPLETON
## 
### 
Mrs. Reggie Woodward of Vancouver
and her daughter visited at the home
of Dink Templeton last week. Mr.
Woodward has been instrumental
in arranging trips for Stanford rugby
teams io Vancouver for many years,
and is known to several generations
of Stanford athletes. He is an enthu
siastic follower of all amateur sports.
