# FRATERNITIES SEND OUT RUSHING CARDS FOR SECOND PERIOD
## 
### 
Cards were given out today for the
second period of rushing which begins
at noon on Sunday, April 25, and ends
Friday night, April 30. The first period
ends on Wednesday. The time allotted
for rushing during this second period
is as follows: Sunday, 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.;
Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday, and
Thursday, 11:30 a.m. to 2 p.m., and
5:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.; Friday, 11:30 a.m.
to 1 a.m. Saturday.
The final period begins on Saturday,
May 1, and extends till Tuesday eve
ning, May 4. The time allotted for
rushing during this third period is as
follows: Saturday, 8 a.m. to 1 a.m.,
Sunday; Sunday, 8 a.m. to 8 p.m.;
Monday and Tuesday, 11:30 a.m. to 2
p.m., and 5:30 p.m. to 8 p.m.
Dates with freshmen will be made
only by means of form cards. Dates
for giving out the third set will be
announced at Interfraternity Council
meetings.
These cards, which will be self-ex
planatory, are to be sent to each
freshman whom the house desires to
invite, with a list of dates from which
he may accept one (either a luncheon
or dinner) during the first period. On
cards covering the second and third
periods a freshman may accept as
many of the dates offered by the house
as he desires.
His signature in the proper place
signifies his acceptance and is bind
ing. If a house permits a visit by a
freshman who has not signed for that
date, it has violated this rule, and it
will be denied pledging privileges as
a penalty. However, on the last eve
ning of rushing, Tuesday, May 4, fresh
men may visit any house or houses
regardless of signatures on the date
cards.
