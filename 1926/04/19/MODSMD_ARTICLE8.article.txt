# CHESS CLUB OPENS TOURNAMENT TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Cardinal Chess Club will start
a tournament among its members to
night at 7:15 o'clock in the Y.M.C.A.
The tournament will be based to some
extent on the use of openings which
will be explained to the club members
each week, and which they will be ex
pected to master.
Tonight Gerald O. Vaughn will ex
plain the first of these openings to the
club, and afterward Coach Louis B.
Zapoleon will drill the members on it.
A short business meeting will be held
also. \
