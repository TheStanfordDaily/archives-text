# University of Washington Graduate Manager Resigns
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON—
(P.I.P.) —Roscoe "Torchy" Torrance,
who has served the University of
Washington in the capacity of assist
ant graduate manager for the past
three years, has handed his resigna
tion to the Board of Control. Before
his graduation from the university
Torrance was a star baseball player,
performing on the varsity nine for
three years.
