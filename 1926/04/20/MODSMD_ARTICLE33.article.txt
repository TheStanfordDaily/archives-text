# MATHEWS RESIGNS
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF IDAHO—(P.I.P.)
—R. L. Mathews, coach and director
of the athletic department, has re
signed to accept a coaching position
with the University of St. Louis.

Mathews plans to leave for his eastern
post as soon as possible, after finding
a competent coach to take care of
track practice. Mathews was here four
years. No successor has been con
sidered.
