TUESDAY
BUSINESS STAFF of the Daily Palo
Alto will meet Tuesday evening at
7 o'clock in the Business Manager's
office.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet in the
Chemistry building at 7 o'clock.
JUNIOR PROM COMMITTEE will
meet in the lobby of the New Union
at 2 o'clock.
MOTHER'S CLUB will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
President Wilbur will speak on
"Stanford."
ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in
the Memorial church, at 4:15 o'clock.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S CLUB infor
mal tea will be held in the Women's
Clubhouse at 4:30 o'clock. All gradu
ate women are invited to attend.
LECTURE on "Europe and America,"
in the Little Theatre, at 7:30 o'clock,
by Frank A. Golder, of the Depart
ment of History.
QUAD PICTURES of the varsity and
freshman swimming and water polo
teams will be taken at the Encina
pool Tuesday at 4 o'clock. All mem
bers of the squads are requested to
be present.
THE JUNIOR PROM COMMITTEE
meets in the New Union at 2 o'clock.
WEDNESDAY
SCHUBERT CLUB will rehearse at
7:15 o'clock tonight in Room 84.
IMPORTANT MEETING of the Glee
Club at 4:15 in lobby of Old Union.
Election of officers and important
business.
RESIDENT ALUMNAE TEA, in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
Hostesses: Mrs. A. M. Espinosa, Mrs.
O. M. Johnston, Mrs. William Sel
lards, Mrs. Albert Guerard, Mrs.
Lawrence Cutter, Mrs. Solon Shedd,
Mrs. H. H. Beckith, Mrs. Ernest S.
Erwin, Mrs. Ward B. Kindy.
ANNUAL DEBATE at 7:30 o'clock be
tween Euphronia Debating Society
and the Senate Debating Society of
the University of California. Sub
ject: "Resolved. That this house
condemn the policy of barring for
eign radicals from entering the
United States. Lobby of the Old
Union. The public is invited.

7 o'clock in the Business Manager's
office.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet in the
Chemistry building at 7 o'clock.
JUNIOR PROM COMMITTEE will
meet in the lobby of the New Union
at 2 o'clock.
MOTHER'S CLUB will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
President Wilbur will speak on
"Stanford."
ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in
the Memorial church, at 4:15 o'clock.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S CLUB infor
mal tea will be held in the Women's
Clubhouse at 4:30 o'clock. All gradu
ate women are invited to attend.
LECTURE on "Europe and America,"
in the Little Theatre, at 7:30 o'clock,
by Frank A. Golder, of the Depart
ment of History.
QUAD PICTURES of the varsity and
freshman swimming and water polo
teams will be taken at the Encina
pool Tuesday at 4 o'clock. All mem
bers of the squads are requested to
be present.
THE JUNIOR PROM COMMITTEE
meets in the New Union at 2 o'clock.
WEDNESDAY
SCHUBERT CLUB will rehearse at
7:15 o'clock tonight in Room 84.
IMPORTANT MEETING of the Glee
Club at 4:15 in lobby of Old Union.
Election of officers and important
business.
RESIDENT ALUMNAE TEA, in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
Hostesses: Mrs. A. M. Espinosa, Mrs.
O. M. Johnston, Mrs. William Sel
lards, Mrs. Albert Guerard, Mrs.
Lawrence Cutter, Mrs. Solon Shedd,
Mrs. H. H. Beckith, Mrs. Ernest S.
Erwin, Mrs. Ward B. Kindy.

tween Euphronia Debating Society
and the Senate Debating Society of
the University of California. Sub
ject: "Resolved. That this house
condemn the policy of barring for
eign radicals from entering the
United States. Lobby of the Old
Union. The public is invited.
