# McDowell Returns Sunday From Trip Through East
## 
### 
John E. McDowell, alumni secretary,
will return to the campus Sunday fol
lowing a month's trip in the East.
During April 15, 16, and 17, McDowell
attended the annual National Confer
ence of Alumni Secretaries held at
Columbus, Ohio.

The alumni secretary, who left
March 19, visited various cities on his
journey East to speak before meetings
of the alumni in these vicinities. His
itinerary included such cities as Phoe
nix, Washington, D.C., New Orleans,
New York. Buffalo, and Salt Lake City.
