# BAND NOTICE
## 
###

are announced for the band which will
record for the Victor Company: Thurs
day, 8:30 to 9; Friday, 7 to 8; Satur
day, 8 to 10 o'clock. The full band
will rehearse tonight from 7 to 8:30
o'clock. The recording band leaves
Palo Alto at 7 o'clock Sunday morn
ing. Members should consult band bul
letin board.
