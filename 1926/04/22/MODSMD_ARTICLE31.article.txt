# Hulme Presents Series of Talks on Art in Berkeley
## 
### 
Professor Edward M. Hulme of the
Stanford history department com
pleted Sunday a series of talks at the
First Unitarian Church at Berkeley.
His general theme has been "Art" and
on three consecutive Sundays he has
spoken on "Art as an Expression of
the Spirit of the Age," "Art as an Ex
pression of the Spirit of the Nation,"
and "Art as an Expression of the
Spirit of the Individual."
