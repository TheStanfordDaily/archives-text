# Early Days of Stanford Told at D.A.R. Meeting
## 
### 
At a recent meeting of the Palo Alto
chapter of the Daughters of the Ameri
can Revolution, Mrs. John Casper
Branner read a paper on "Early Days
at Stanford," which disclosed many
experiences of the days of '91 hitherto
unwritten.
Mrs. Branner's paper and one re
cently written by Mrs. O. P. Jenkins
on personal reminiscences of Jane
Lathrop Stanford will be the begin
ning of a series of memoirs the chap
ter is planning to have put into per
manent form.
