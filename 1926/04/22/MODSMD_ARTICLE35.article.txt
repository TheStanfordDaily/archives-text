# Stanford Graduate Will Build Palo Alto School
## 
### 
B. A. Stephenson, Stanford '13, head
of the newly-formed Stephenson Con
struction Company, has been awarded
the contract to build the first unit of
the proposed South Palo Alto school.
The bid specifies that the two-class
room unit is to be completed August
15. The building will be located on
a tract recently purchased by the
board.
