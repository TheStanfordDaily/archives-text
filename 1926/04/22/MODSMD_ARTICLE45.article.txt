# Washington Votes Higher Salaries for Six Coaches
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON—
(P.I.P.) —Salaries of six of Washing
ton's coaches were raised by the Board
of Control at its last meeting, setting
a new high mark for coaches' salaries
in this institution. The increases will
take effect September 1.
Tubby Graves, baseball coach, will
receive the raise when his new two
year contract starts. His colleagues,
Enoch Bagshaw, football mentor;
Rusty Callow, crew coach; Hec Ed
mundson, basketball and track coach;
and Bart Spellman and Wayne Sutton,
assistants on the football staff, will
be benefited by the rise immediately,
although their contracts do not expire
this year.
