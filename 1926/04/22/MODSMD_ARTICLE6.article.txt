# T. W. Tully, T. S. Scribner Take Positions in Arizona
## 
### 
Charles W. Tully and Thornton S.
Scribner, seniors in mining and metal
lurgy at Stanford, have left school to
lake positions as laborers with the
Copper Queen branch of the Pheops,
Dodge Corporation, at Ilisbee, Arizona.
They will resume their studies next
year, after having gained experience
in actual mining in these positions.
