# WILBUR HALL HOLDS FIRST FIESTA CAST GENERAL REHEARSAL
## 
### 
First rehearsal of the Fiesta was
held last night with the full east under
the direction of Wilbur Hall of Los
Gatos. general director of the pageant.
The cast went through the entire pa-1
I
geant, using the second revision of j
the manuscript. Several changes have
been made in the action of the plot.
Hall expressed satisfaction with the
work done at the rehearsal. He said,
however, that he regretted that the
dances could not be done last night,
as he desired to get the general effect
of their sequence. The dance director, j
Adelaide Kelly, announces that the :
dances will probably not be performed
in general rehearsals for a couple of
weeks.
