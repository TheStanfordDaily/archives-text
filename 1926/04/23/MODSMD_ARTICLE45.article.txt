# Charles G. Hyde Succeeds Hildebrand as U.C. Dean
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA —
(P.I.P.) —Charles G. Hyde, professor
of sanitary engineering, will succeed
Joel H. Hildebrand in the office of
dean of men, as a result of the recom
mendation of President W. W. Camp
bell to the Board of Regents yester
day. Hyde received his B.S. at the
Massachusetts Institute of Technology
in 1896, became active in water purifi
cation in the East, and in 1902 became
a professor at this university.
