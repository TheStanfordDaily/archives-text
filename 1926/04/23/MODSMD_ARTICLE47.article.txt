o'clock.

will he held at Memorial Church at
11 o'clock. Rev. D. Charles Gardner,
Chaplain.

Memorial Church by Warren D.
Allen, University Organist.
MONDAY
GLEE CLUB tryouts for next year, in
Room 84, Inner Quad, at 4 o'clock.
Open to all students interested in
Glee Club work.

ming and water polo team pictures
for the Quad will be retaken at 4
o'clock at Encina Pool, as it has
been decided to use a larger size
picture.

Church at 4:15 o'clock, by Warren
D. Allen, University Organist.

the Century Company, will be held
at 7:30 o'clock in the Assembly hall.
The public is invited.
