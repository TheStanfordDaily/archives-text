# ZOOLOGY CLUB PLANS FIELD TRIP SATURDAY
## 
### 
The Zoology Club is planning its
first field trip of the quarter for next
Saturday evening at the Zoology cabin.
Those who intend to take the trip
will meet in front ot" the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock Saturday
evening.
Members who wish to take the trip
should sign up on the bulletin board
in the Zoology building before tomor
row night. The meeting at the Cabin
will be informal. Refreshments will be
served, and entertainment has been
provided for. This trip is one of many
that the Zoology Club is planning.
