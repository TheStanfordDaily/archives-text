# TRAVELER'S LECTURES ARRANGED AT LONDON
## 
### 
Travelers in Great Britain this sum
mer may enlarge their view of Eng
lish literature and history by a series
of lectures which has been arranged
especially for them by the University
of London. The course, which is to be
given in London from July 16 to Au
gust 12, will be of particular interest
to those who are preparing for the
teaching profession.
There will be lectures on "Some
Contemporary English Writers," "The
History of London," "Some British
Contributions to the History of Eu
rope," and many other subjects.
A number of entertainments have
been arranged and places of interest
in and around London will be visited.
