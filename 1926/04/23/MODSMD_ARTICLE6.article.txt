# GREENACRE IS NAMED EDITOR OF HANDBOOK
## 
### 
Esther Greenacre has been appoint
ed editor-in-chief of the next annual
handbook issued jointly by the Y.M.
C.A., Y.W.C.A., and Associated Stu
dents. The book, which is to be issued
next fall, is to be a compendium of
useful information about the Univer
sity and its activities, prepared espe
cially for freshmen and new students.
Yale Maxon is business manager,
and the rest of the staff includes Anne
Hartwell, Lucia Champlin, Edward
White, and William Christian. Work
has already been started on the hand
book, according to Miss Greenacre.
