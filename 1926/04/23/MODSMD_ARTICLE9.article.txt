# ASSOCIATED WOMEN WILL INSTALL NEW OFFICERS THURSDAY
## 
### 
New officers of the Associated Wo
men Students will be installed Thurs
day night at the quarterly Conference
Party to be held in the Clubhouse.
President Wilbur will attend the
meeting to perform the formal duties
of installation in the same manner
that he did last year.

Charlotte Lovekin will become presi
dent in the place of Mildred Worswick.
Other women to take the oath of office
are Barbara Perkins, vice-president;
Eleanor Davidson, secretary; Kath
reen Clock, and Anne Bancroft.

Dinner will be served to all the wo
men before the Conference Party in
the Clubhouse before the program.
Dancing will follow the regular meet
ing of the Conference.
