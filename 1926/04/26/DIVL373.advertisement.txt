Three days of laughter and mer
riment! Reginald Denny in
"Skinner's Dress Suit"
With Laura La Plante. The kind
of picture that will not only
make you laugh but will send
you home with the smile that
won't come off. The merry mirth
quake of a white collar man —a

first dress suit. A breezy farce!
Nothing but laughs! Don't miss
it for fun!
