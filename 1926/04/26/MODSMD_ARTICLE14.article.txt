# SENIORS TO ANSWER QUESTIONNAIRE AT MEETING OF CLASS
## 
### 
The senior class will hold a
meeting at 8:15 o'clock tomorrow
night at the Women's Clubhouse
to discuss matters pertaining to
the class endowment and gift.
Men and women of the class will
be given a questionnaire at the
meeting.
This questionnaire, which has
been compiled by Delmer Daves,
contains fifty questions, some
serious and some humorous. It
is similar to senior questionnaires
that are given to seniors at col
leges throughout the country, no
tably at Columbia.
The meeting is being held at
8:15 in order that the fraternity
men who are rushing may attend.
This will be one of the most im
portant meetings of the year.
