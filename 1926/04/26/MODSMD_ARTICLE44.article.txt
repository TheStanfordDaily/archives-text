# Nebraska Tracksters Break World's Record in Relay
## 
### 
The University of Nebraska half
mile, four-man relay team broke the
world's record in this event in the
annual Kansas Relays, running the
distance in 1 minute 28.6 seconds. This
is four-tenths of a second better than
the former mark.
Roland Locke, a member of the rec
ord-breaking Nebraska team, ran the
100-yard dash in 9.6 seconds on the
same day, tying the world's record.
