# BERKELEY TEAMS TO TRAVEL
## 
###

from the University of California to
the I.C.A.A.A.A. meet and the Prince
ton meet this summer. Two crews will
ho sent to the Poughkeepsle regatta,
and the baseball team will sail to
Hawaii, also.
