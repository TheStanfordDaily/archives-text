# CABBAGES and THINGS
## MCCUBBINS HEAD-WRITING CONTEST WINNERS ANNOUNCED 
### Steve O'Donnell 
SUBJECT 1
Hi-diddle-diddle, the eat and the fiddle,
The cow jumped oVer the moon,
The little dog laughed to see such sport,
And the dish ran away with the spoon.

head by
Alcibiades Maine

STRATFORD-ON-AVON, Eng., April 26. —Winners in a nation-wide contest
of copyreaders have have been announced by Prof. Amos McCubbin, journalism
instructor at Northclift'e University, judge of the $5,000,000 prize competition
held under the Hearst-Scripps foundation.
The lucky authors and their winning efforts:

down beside her,
