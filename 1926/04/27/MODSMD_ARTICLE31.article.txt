# ERNIE NEVERS BATS FOR ST. LOUIS TEAM
## 
### 
Appearing for the second time this
season, Ernie Nevers was put in the
game between the St. Louis Browns
and Cleveland Indians yesterday.
Nevers was used as a pinch hitter in
the ninth inning for Pitcher Ballou of
the Browns, but failed to get on base.
The Indians won the game 12 to 1.
