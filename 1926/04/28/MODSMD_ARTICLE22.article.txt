# SENIORS BEAT 1928 CLASS TENNIS TEAM IN FIVE MATCHES
## 
### 
Winning five out of seven matches
yesterday afternoon from the 1928 ten
nis squad, the seniors are now tied
with the juniors for the interclass ten
nis championship.
James V. Mansfield defeated Ken
neth A. Rogers in straight sets, 6-2, 6-1
for the sophomores, while Gomer
Thomas handed Leighton M. Bledsoe
a beating, 6-2, 2-6, 6-2 for the other
1928 victory.
Fred B. Leeds' win over Frank Dunn
was the big upset of the afternoon.
Dunn took the first set, 2-6, but was
tired, due to his strenuous playing in
the doubles match, and Leeds took the
next two, 6-1, 6-3.
The deciding match between the
juniors and sophomores will be played
tomorrow afternoon. If the second
year men take the match the sopho
mores will be tied with the juniors and
seniors for the interclass champion
ship. Thomas and Dunn, sophomores,
will meet Gardner and Todd, seniors,
to decide the battle between the two
classes.
