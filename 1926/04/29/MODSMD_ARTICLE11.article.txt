# JUNIOR CLASS WILL RETAIN CORD DANCE IN WEEK'S ACTIVITY
## 
### 
Contrary to previous announce
ments, the annual Cord Ball will
be held Saturday afternoon, May
8, from 3:30 to 5:30 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse, according to
announcement made today by
Kenneth Graham, president of
the junior class.
Since it is impossible to hold
the Lake Sports Carnival, accord
ing to custom, which usually fol
lows the Corduroy Ball on Thurs
day afternoon of Junior Week,
plans for the Cord Ball were not
made until recently.
Tickets costing $1 will be
placed on sale at the Stanford
Bookstore when Prom programs
are distributed. The time will be
announced later.
