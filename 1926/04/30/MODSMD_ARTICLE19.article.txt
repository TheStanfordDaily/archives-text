# THREE STORIES SENT TO HARPER'S CONTEST
## 
### 
Three manuscripts have been sent
from Stanford to the Harpers Maga
zine competition, an intercollegiate
short story and play contest having
a first prize of five hundred dollars.
As each university is. allowed only
three entries, a preliminary contest
was held from which three stories
were selected: "The Drifting Ship," a
short story by Margaret Lee; "Ren
dezvous," a play by Rowena Mason;
"Anchors," a short story by Francis
Gill. The results of the contest will
be published in a June issue of Har
pers Weekly. Contributions not se
lected for the contest may be obtained
by the writers at the English depart
ment office.
