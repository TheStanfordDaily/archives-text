# Rev. Gleason Will Deliver Memorial Church Sermon
## 
### 
Monsignor Joseph M. Gleason, of
the St. Thomas Aquinas church, Palo
Alto, will deliver the sermon Sunday
morning at 11 o'clock in the Memorial
Church, taking as his subject, "The
Engrafted Word."
The Stanford Glee Club will sing a
chorale, "How Bright Appears the
Morning Star," and the anthem, "Ado
ramus te," is to be presented by the
University choir under the direction
of Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist.
