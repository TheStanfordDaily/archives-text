# Oregon Students Must Pass Swim Test to Graduate
## 
### 
Orgon State College has started a
new innovation in the swimming test
which all students are required to take
before they are given their diplomas.
One unit of credit is given with a
grade of 1) to those who pass the test
by swimming the required 100 feet.
C's and B's are given for proportion
ately better performances.
To get an A the student must be
, able to swim 150 feet in good time,
' swim back stroke, do several dives,
and swim forty feet under water.
