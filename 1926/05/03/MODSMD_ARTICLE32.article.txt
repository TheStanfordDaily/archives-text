# SCOFIELD APPOINTED TO NEW COMMISSION
## 
### 
Norman B. Scofield, '95, graduate of
the Zoology department, was appoint
ed a few days ago by President Cool
idge to the recently created Interna
tional Fisheries Commission. Scolield
is considered one of the best fisheries
authorities on the Pacific Coast.
He is chief of the Department of
Commercial Fisheries with the Cali
fornia Commission and will continue
his duties with the California body as
well as assuming his new work as a
member of the International Fisheries
Commission.
