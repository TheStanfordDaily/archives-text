# Sholz, Olympic Champion, Visits Stanford Campus
## 
### 
Jackson Sholz, the 1924 Olympic
Games champion in the 200 meter
dash, is staying on the campus at the
Kappa Alpha house, while training
here for the P.A.A. meet at Kezar
stadium, San Francisco, this week.
Sholz, who is running under the
colors of the New York Athletic Club,
has just returned from a tour of New
Zealand and Honolulu, which he made
with Lloyd Hahn.
