# Tournament of Cardinal Chess Club Opens Tonight
## 
### 
The Cardinal Chess Club's novice
tournament, which was postponed
from last week, will open tonight at
7:30 o'clock in the Y.M.C.A. building.
Anyone not a member of the freshman
or varsity chess teams may compete.
The club will hold a short business
meeting before the tournament opens.
