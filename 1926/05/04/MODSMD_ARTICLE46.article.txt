# Espinosa Leaves Campus For Language Conference
## 
### 
Professor Aurelio M. Espinosa, of
the Department of Romanic Lan
guages, left last night for Mesil Park,
New Mexico, to attend the Southwest
ern Language Conference, which is to
be held under the auspices of the New
Mexico College of Agriculture. The
convention will be in session May 6,
7, and 8.
Professor Espinosa iS to address the
Conference on the subject of Spanish
Literature. He will also give a talk on
language tests before a group session,
as well as an informal discussion, the
topic to be decided upon later. He ex
pects to return to the campus within
two weeks.
