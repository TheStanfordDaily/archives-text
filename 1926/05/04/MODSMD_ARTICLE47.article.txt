# Jeanette Booskin Married To Washington Graduate
## 
### 
Jeanette Louise Booskin, '25, was
married to Dale Hilbert Hollenback
in the Stanford Memorial Chapel last
Sunday. Hollenback is a graduate of
the University of Washington where
he was a member of Delta Kappa Ep
silon. Miss Booskin is a resident of
San Jose.
