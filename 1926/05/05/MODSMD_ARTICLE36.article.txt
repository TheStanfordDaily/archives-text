# Salmon Packers Present Husky Crew With Shell
## 
### 
A new racing shell, to be known as
"The Salmon King," was recently pre
sented to the University of Washing
ton crews by the Association of Sal
mon Packers. The gift is the second of
its kind this year, and the third to be
made in the last two years.
Construction of the boat is being
rushed in hopes that it will be in
condition to be used in the coming
Poughkeepsie race.
