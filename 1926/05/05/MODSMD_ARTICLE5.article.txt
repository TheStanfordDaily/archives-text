# Roble Hall to Entertain At Open House Next Week
## 
### 
In accordance with the plan spon
sored by the Y.W.C.A. to create great
er harmony and understanding be
tween the hall and sororities, Roble
Hall will hold open house Tuesday,
May 18, from 3 to 6 o'clock.
The open house tradition was initi
ated by the Pi Beta Phi house which
entertained all women of the campus
at a tea on Thursday, April 8. Roble
Hall will follow the precedent by giv
ing the second such tea.
