# "Y" PLANS ASILOMAR BEACH RALLY SUNDAY
## 
### 
A roundup of men interested in the
Asilomar Conferences will be held on
the Pescadero beach Sunday under
the auspices of the Stanford Y.M.C.A.
chapter. The party will leave the cam
pus at 6:30 o'clock Sunday morning
and return at 4:30 o'clock in the after
noon.
The Reverend J. Stitt Wilson, who
has been a lecturer at Asilomar for
the past several years, will be the
principal speaker at the beach round
up. Arrangements for the trip are
being made by Leonard P. Wilbur,
Sterling Beckwitli, and Stewart Way.
