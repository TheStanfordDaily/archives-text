# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
FACULTY CLUB will meet in the
Faculty Clubhouse at 8 o'clock.
ALL USHERS will please report to
the Assembly hall promptly at 7:30
for the Junior Opera.
SATURDAY
DELTA GAMMA Association will hold
its annual meeting at 10 o'clock Sat
urday morning at the chapter house.
ALL USHERS will please report to
the Assembly hall promptly at 7:30
for the Junior Opera.
ANNUAL MEETING. CALIFORNIA
ACADEMY OF SOCIAL SCIENCES,
Law Building, commencing at 10
o'clock.

SECOND PERFORMANCE of Junior
Opera, "Ride a Cock Horse," in the
Assembly hall at 8:15 o'clock.
SUNDAY
STANFORD EPISCOPAL CLUB will
meet at 6 o'clock in the Parish
House. Professor Albert Guerard
will speak.
SPECIAL CHOIR REHEARSAL Sun
day morning at 10:30 o'clock in
Room 84.
DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON in
the Memorial Church at 11 o'clock.
Preacher, the Reverend G. A. John
ston Ross, D.D., of New York.
ORGAN RECITAL in the Memorial
Church at 4 o'clock by Mr. Wheeler
Becket.

MONDAY
ANNUAL MEETING of Faculty Wo
men's Club will be held in the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
ENGLISH REVIEW CLUB will meet
at the home of Professor Arthur G.
Kennedy, 433 Coleridge Ave., Palo
Alto, at 7:30 o'clock.
TUESDAY
ORGAN RECITAL by Warren D. Al
len, University Organist, in the Me
morial Church, at 4:15 o'clock.
