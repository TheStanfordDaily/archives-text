# FACULTY COMMITTEE MAKES SCHOLARSHIP AWARDS FOR 1926-27
## 
### 
The fatuity committee of scholarship
voted at its meeting yesterday to grant
the fololwing undergraduate scholar
ship awards for 1926-27:
Braly Scholarship — Louise Dawes
Shedd.
Cap and Gown —Margaret Elizabeth
Olsen.
Converse —Jean M. Ballou.
Frank Bernard Carrol—Robert Ar
thur Hume.
Malcolm Nixon Jones —Louise Bil
lings Lundborg.
Edward Arthur White, Laura Sher
man Webb (autumn quarter).

M. Watanbe.
