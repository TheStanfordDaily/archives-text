# Dean Culver Leaves to Attend Eastern Meeting
## 
### 
Dean George B. Culver is leaving
tonight to attend a conference of
Deans and Advisors of Men, which
will be held the latter part of this
week and the first .of next week at the
University of Minnesota, Minneapolis.
Culver expects to be gone about a
month, during which time he will make
a tour of all the principal colleges in
the East and Middle-West.
