# Harvard Freshmen Rebel At Tradition; Throw Eggs
## 
### 
When the Harvard seniors started
their annual custom of begging money
from underclassmen for the senior
picnic, they were greeted by a shower
of eggs and tomatoes from the fresh
man class. Captain M. A. Cheek of the
Harvard football team and first mar
shal of the senior class was the ini
tial target for the vegetables and
produce.
