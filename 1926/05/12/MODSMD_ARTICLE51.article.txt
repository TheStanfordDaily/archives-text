# Rivero Wins Masters Soccer Decathlon Cup
## 
### 
Manuel Rivero, '27, won the annual
soccer decathlon for the Masters
trophy as a result of the competition
held Monday and Tuesday of this
week. Rivero scored high in each of
the ten events on the program. Other
men high in the ranking for the trophy
were Baillard, Schirm, and Miron.
