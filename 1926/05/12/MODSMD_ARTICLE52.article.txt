# McCOLLOCH APPOINTED TO FLOWER COMMITTEE
## 
### 
Leland J. McColloch has been ap
pointed as the second member of the
1927 flower committee by Kenenth C.
Graham, president of the junior class.
McColloch was selected to fill the
position left vacant by Francis J.
Moore who has taken out a leave of
absence. Aileen Brown is the other
member of the flower committee which
places blossoms every Sunday morn
ing at the Mausoleum. The committee
will act until Senior Day next year.
