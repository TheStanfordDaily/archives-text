# Indianans Plan Reunion To Celebrate Anniversary
## 
### 
Alumni, former students and teach
ers, and friends of Indiana University
at Stanford and in the vicinity will
celebrate the one hundred sixth anni
versary of Indiana at the Stanford
Union Monday evening at 6:15 o'clock,
and not on the date announced in
previous issues. Dr. David Starr Jor
dan wil' speak. Those desiring to at
tend are requested to signify their
intention to Professor Carl F. Brand.
