# EBRIGHT AND NAGLER CONTRACT
## 
### 
"Ky" Ebright, rowing coach at the
University of California, and Russ Nag
ler, freshman crew coach, recently
signed contracts to continue two more
years in charge of the Bear oarsmen.
Ebright and Nagler came from the
University of Washington two years
ago to take over California's rowing
destinies.

A debate was recently delivered en
tirely in French by American-born
students of Boston and Holy Cross
colleges.
