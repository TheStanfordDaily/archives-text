# Y.M.C.A. Representatives Hold San Jose Meeting
## 
### 
Six representatives of the Stanford
Y.M.C.A. took charge of the San Jose
Hi-Y meeting last night in San Jose.
Ben Culver presided at the meeting
while Sterling Beckwith and Neal E.
Sooy spoke. Another part of the pro
gram was a saxaphone duet given by
Hughes Brewster and A. Hale Dins
moor, accompanied by Reidar B. Tor
jussen.
