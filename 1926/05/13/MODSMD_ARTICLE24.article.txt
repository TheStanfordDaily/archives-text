# NOTICE
## 
### 
Freshmen who wish to reserve
rooms in Toyon, Branner, or Sequoia,
for the fall quarter, should call at the
Dean of Men's office, Wednesday,
Thursday, or Friday, May 19, 20, or 21.
Reservations will be given to those
who are high enough on the waiting
list, and deposits will be payable at
that time.
