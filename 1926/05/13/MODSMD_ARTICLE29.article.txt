# Six Survive First Round Of Branner Net Tourney
## 
### 
First-round matches in the Branner
Hall tennis tournament have been
completed with six men surviving. The
second round must be completed by
May 20, according to John A. Leiter,
manager of the tourney.
Those surviving the initial rounds
are: Harold F. Way, Theodore F. Baer,
Stanley J. Cook, Samuel Miller, Henry
Gibbons, John A. Leiter.
