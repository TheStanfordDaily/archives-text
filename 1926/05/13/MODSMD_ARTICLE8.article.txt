# MILITARY REVIEW TO FEATURE ALUMNI DAY
## 
### 
The Reserve Officers Training Corps
unit of Stanford University will review
before President Wilbur on Saturday
morning as part of the Alumni Day
exercises. The President's review will
be held on the Encina polo field, start
ing at 10:20 o'clock.
A polo exhibition game has been
planned between two classes of polo
to take place before and after the ex
hibition. Bleachers for this and the
events of the following week have
been erected at the field, and will be
ready.
