# TOYON CLUB TO GIVE INFORMAL AFTER MEET
## 
### 
Toyon Club will be host to returning
alumni, students, and visitors tomor
row afternoon at an informal dance
to be given in the hall clubroom after
the Pacific Coast Conference track
meet.
Phil Brooks' orchestra will provide
the music for the four-thirty to six
informal. No admission charge will bo
made, and everyone is invited to at
tend by the Club dance committee.
John D. Van Amringe is in charge of
arrangements for the affair.
