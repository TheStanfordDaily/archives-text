# ALUMNI ELECT THREE MEMBERS TO COUNCIL
## 
### 
Three new members, Mrs. Ethel
, Bowden Burbank, '13, Paul M. Down
ing, '95, and George Osborne Wilson,
'14. were elected to the Alumni Coun
cil at the meeting of the Alumni As
sociation in the Law building Saturday
morning.
The retiring members of the Coun
cil are President Hubert H. Hall, Vice-
President Harriet Pugh Barrett, and
William H. Harrelson.
