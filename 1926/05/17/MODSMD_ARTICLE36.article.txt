# ROBLE TO HOLD OPEN HOUSE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Roble Hall will hold open house from
3 to 6 o'clock tomorrow afternoon in
accordance with the plan sponsored by
the Y.W.C.A. to create harmony and
understanding between the hall and
sororites.
A Chinaman eight and one-half feet
high recently arrived in San Francisco.
His name is Liu Yu-Ching, and he is
the tallest known Chinese. He weighs
about 600 pounds.
A new basketball court at the Uni
versity of Pensylvania will seat 10,000
people.
