# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
ALUMNI and FORMER STUDENTS
of Indiana University hold anniver
sary banquet in Stanford Union at
6:15 o'clock.
A CAPELLA CHOIR, John Smallman,
conductor, will give a concert in the
Assembly hall at 8:15 o'clock under
the auspices of the Stanford Glee
Club.
CHESS CLUB meet'; at 7:15 o'clock
in the Y.M.C.A. building.
ALL USHERS will please report to
the Assembly hall promptly at 7:30
for the Smallman A Cappella Choir
concert.
TUESDAY
MOTHERS CLUB will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock. Dr.
Gardner will speak on "Religion in
the University."
ORGAN RECITAL in the Memorial
Church at 4:15 by Warren D. Allen,
University Organist.
INFORMAL TEA will be held by the
Graduate Women's Club in the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 4:30 o'clock.
ILLUSTRATED LECTURE on "Al
geria—Part of the French North
African Empire," by Mr. Robert Van
V.leck Anderson, '06, in the Little
Theatre at 7:30' o'clock. The public
is invited.
JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB will meet
at 7:30 p.m. in the Women's Club
/ house.
ALL ENGINEERS will meet at 7:30 in
Room 268 to discuss the advisability
for publishing a Stanford engineer
ing journal.
WEDNESDAY
ORCHESTRA will meet Wednesday
evening in Room 84 at 7:15 o'clock.
RESIDENT ALUMNA TEA in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
EUPHRONIA DEBATING SOCIETY
will meet in the Women's Clubhouse
at 7:30 o'clock.
JOINT MEETING of the Science As
sentation and Sigma Xi will be held
in the Faculty Clubhouse at 8
o'clock. Professor L. M. Huggins of
the Department of Chemistry will
speak on "The Structure of Solids
as Revealed by X-Rays."
THURSDAY
ORGAN RECITAL in the Memorial
Church at 4:15 o'clock by Wallace
Rolls, organist.
THURSDAY EVENING Conference
will be held at 7:30 o'clock at the
home of Dr. David Starr Jordan. Sub
ject: "The British Strike."
DEBATE between Utah Agricultural
College and Stanford will be held at
8 o'clock in the Little Theatre. Ques
tion: "Resolved, that the present
prohibition law should not be
amended. Stanford will take the af
firmative side. Audience-judge deci
sion. The public is invited.

PI LAMBDA THETA will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 4:15 o'clock.
