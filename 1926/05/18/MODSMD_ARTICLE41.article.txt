# Dr. John Burnet to Speak On "Plato" to Chi Nu Kappa
## 
### 
Dr. John Burnet, of Saint Andrews
College, Scotland, will discuss "Plato"
at the meeting of Chi Nu Kappa, class
ical literature society, which will be
held at 8 o'clock tomorrow in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Burnet received the only honor
ary degree bestowed by the University
of California this year and holds sev
eral degrees from foreign universi
ties. Dr. Burnet was the Sather pro
fessor of classical literature this year
