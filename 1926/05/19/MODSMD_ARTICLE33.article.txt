# Mississippi Repeals Law Prohibiting Fraternities
## 
### 
Fraternities will again be allowed
in the universities of Mississippi fol
lowing the recent repeal of the Lee
Russell law prohibiting fraternities.
The law was passed in 1912 after some
fraternity scandals.
Chapters will soon be installed under
control of a faculty committee but no
house? may be built for five years.
