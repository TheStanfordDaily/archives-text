WEDNESDAY
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will meet in the
Chemistry building at 7:15 o'clock
tonight.
THE FRESHMAN CLASS will give a
Jolly-Up at Roble tonight from 6:30
to 8 o'clock. All members of the
freshman class are invited to attend.
MASONIC DEGREE TEAM will meet
for rehearsal at 6:30 o'clock tonight
at the Masonic Temple in Palo Alto.
CHI NU KAPPA meets at 8 o'clock
tonight in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. John Burnet of St. Andrews Col
lege, Scotland, will speak. The pub
lic is cordially invited.
ORCHESTRA will meet Wednesday
evening in Room 84 at 7:15 o'clock.
RESIDENT ALUMNA TEA in the
Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
EUPHRONIA DERATING SOCIETY
will meet in the Women's Clubhouse
at 7:30 o'clock.
JOINT MEETING of the Science As
sociation and Sigma Xi will be held
in the Faculty Clubhouse at 8
o'clock. Professor L. M. Huggins of
the Department of Chemistry will
speak on "The Structure of Solids
as Revealed by X-Rays."
