# Houser Will Broadcast Book Reviews Over KFWI
## 
### 
Lionel L. Houser, '27, will give a
series of weekly literary addresses and
book reviews over radio station KFWI,
of San Francisco, beginning tomorrow
night at 8:30 o'clock.

If a member of the Student Council
at the University of Illinois is absent
from the meetings more than twice in
succession, and does not send a proxy,
he is barred from further meetings.

A poor handwriting betrays the fact,
of hidden genius, says a scientist at
the University of Pittsburgh.
