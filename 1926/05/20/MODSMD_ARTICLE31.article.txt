# ASILOMAR ROUND-UP PLANNED BY Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
An Asilomar round-up will be held
by the Stanford Y.M.C.A. at Pescadero
beach Sunday for men interested in
attending the annual student conven
tion. The Reverend J. Stitt Wilson,
well-known California clergyman, will
be the principal speaker.

All students intending to make the
trip should see Leonard F. Wilbur,
chairman of the committee in charge,
immediately. The party will leave the
"Y" building at 6:30 o'clock Sunday
morning and is to return by 6 o'clock
in the evening. Transportation and
two meals will be provided by the
committee.
