# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
1927 QUAD managerial tryoutees will
meet at 7:30 o'clock in the Quad
Office in the Old Union. All fresh
men are eligible to try out for the
managerial staff. Those interested
are invited to attend.

GERMAN CLUB meets tonight at 7:20
o'clock at the Women's Clubhouse.
Hunter Abend —Program. Refresh
ments.
SIGMA DELTA CHI will meet tonight
at J o'clock in the Daily office. Im
portant.

ORGAN RECITAL in the Memorial
Church at 4:15 o'clock by Wallace
Rolls, organist.
THURSDAY 1 "" EVENING Conference
will be held at 7:30 o'clock at the
home of Dr. David Starr Jordan. Sub
ject: "The British Strike."
DEBATE between Utah Agricultural
College and Stanford will be held at
8 o'clock in the Little Theatre. Ques
tion: "Resolved, that the present
prohibition law should not be
amended. Stanford will take the af
firmative side. Audience-judge deci
i sion. The public is invited.
FRIDAY
PI LAMBDA THETA will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 4:15 o'clock.
MASONIC CLUB and Degree Team
will go to Santa Clara tonight. For
further information call Ambrose
Martin at P.A. 270-R or P.A. 54.
SUNDAY
STANFORD EPISCOPAL CLUB will
meet at 6 o'clock in the parish house.
Herndon Ray will uphold the affir
mative and Ned .Wheldon the nega
tive of the question, "Resolved, That
a life devoted to the Ministry brings
a higher return than one devoted to
the Law.
