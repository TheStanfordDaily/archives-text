# Commercial Club Members Hear President Wilbur
## 
### 
President Wilbur was a speaker at
a banquet given at the Commercial
Club last night in honor of Takashi
Komatsu by the Chamber of Com
merce of San Francisco.
Komatsu is manager of the Toyo
Kisen Kaisha steamship line. He has
been in the United States for four
years and is now returning to Japan.
He was a member of the Institute of
Pacific Relations, which was presided
over by Dr. Wilbur last summer.
