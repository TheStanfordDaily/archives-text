# Junipero Serra Club to Meet S.A.E. Nine for Title
## 
### 
Finals of the intramural baseball
series this afternoon bring together
Sigma Alpha Epsilon, champions of
League VII and Junipero Serra Club,
League I title-holders, at 4 o'clock on
the Varsity diamond.
Serra club boasts a consistent infield,
a creditable outfield, and a clever bat
tery. Against this, the S.A.E.'s have
to offer a stellar twirler in La Rue, and
a powerful lineup of hitters. Ihe title
at stake today is held by El Catnpo,
which team won it last year from El
Tigre.
