# Palo Alto Boys to Hold Track Meet on Old Oval
## 
### 
A track and field meet in which over
100 boys will participate will be held
Saturday. May 29, on the campus. The
meet is sponsored and overseen by the
Boys' Work Commission of the Stan
ford Y.M.C.A.
Final arrangements for the affair
will be completed at a meeting of the
commission at a lunch in the Union to
morrow. The commission has success
fully managed a baseball league this
year. It is composed of six members of
the Y.M.C.A. with B. F. Culver acting
as advisor.
