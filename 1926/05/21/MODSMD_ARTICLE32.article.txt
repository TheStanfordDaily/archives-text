FRIDAY
PI LAMBDA THETA will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse at 4:15 o'clock.
MASONIC CLUB and Degree Team
wili go to Santa Clara tonight. For
further information call Ambrose
Martin at P.A. 270-R or P.A. 54.
SATURDAY
STANFORD BAND leaves the band
house at 1:15 p.m. for the San Jose
Fiesta de las Rosas. Private cars
meet the band at Seifert Gymna
sium, Santa Clara University, be
fore 2:30 p.m.

STANFORD EPISCOPAL CLUB will
meet at 6 o'clock in the parish house.
Herndon Ray will uphold the affir
mative and Ned Wheldon the nega
tive of the question, "Resolved, That
a life devoted to the Ministry brings
a higher return than one devoted to
the Law.
