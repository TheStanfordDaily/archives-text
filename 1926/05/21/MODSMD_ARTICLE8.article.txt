# Dr. August Mahr Speaks At German Club Meeting
## 
### 
The last meeting of the Stanford
German Club this year was held last
night in the Women's Clubhouse, Dr.
August Mahr addressing the members
on "German Architecture." He illus
trated his talk with several photo
graphs.
The picnic which was to be held at
La Honda this week-end was called
off. Several German songs were sung
at the meeting.
