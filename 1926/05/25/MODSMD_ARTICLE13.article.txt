# Stanford Professor to Talk at Western Reserve
## 
### 
Professor Edward C. Franklin, of the
Chemistry department, will deliver a
series of thirty lectures on the general
subject, "The Ammonia System of
Compounds," at the summer session
from June 21 to July 30 of Western
Reserve University, Ohio.
Professor Franklin was awarded the
Nichols Medal for 1925 by the Ameri
can Chemical Society of which he has
been a director.
