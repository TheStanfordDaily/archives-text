# Spanish Honor Fraternity Plans Initiation Tomorrow
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Pi, national Spanish
honorary fraternity, held a meeting
yesterday afternoon in Room 243 of
the Romanic Language Department
building, for the purpose of electing
new members. Initiation will be held
at I) o'clock tomorrow night at the
home of Professor Aurelio M. Espi
nosa, 16 Salvatierra Street.
