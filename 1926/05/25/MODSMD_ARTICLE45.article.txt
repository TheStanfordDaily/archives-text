# Electrical Engineers to Visit R.C.A. Radio Station
## 
### 
Arrangements for a trip to the Radio
Corporation of America broadcasting
station will be made at a meeting of
the Stanford chapter of the American
Institute of Electrical Engineers to be
held in Room 263 at 7:15 o'clock to
night.
Officers of the society are Fred E.
Crever, president; Cecil R. Benson,
vice-president; and Curtis R. Walling,
secretary.
