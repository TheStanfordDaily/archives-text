# Delta Epsilon Will Meet At Professor Stark's Home
## 
### 
Delta Epsilon, national art. frater
nity, will meet from 7:30 to 10 o'clock
tomorrow night at the home of Profes
sor and Mrs. Edwin C. Starks, 1115
Ramona Street, Palo Alto. Mrs. Starks
is on the faculty of the art depart
ment.
Professor Starks, of the Zoology de
partment, will give a lecture on Italian
Architecture and Building, illustrated
by slides including views from Rome
and Venice.
