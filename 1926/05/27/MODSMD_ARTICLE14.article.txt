# GEOLOGY INSTRUCTOR WANTED AT KANSAS
## 
### 
A position as instructor in general
geology and one course in economic
geology for the school year 1926-27 is
open at Kansas State Agricultural
College, according to a letter received
by the Stanford department from Ar
thur Sperry, of the K.S.A.C. geology
faculty. A man with some teaching ex
perience and with a Master of Science
degree is preferred.
The position will last only for a
year, according to present indications,
but there is a possibility of its being
permanent. The salary will be $2000
for nine months. All interested should
see Dr. Eliot Blaekwelder of the Geo
logy department for further informa
tion.
