farce, "Le Pendu Dependu Se Repent,"
at its meeting last night. Marcel Tou

young man determined to commit sui
cide. The role of the maid, whose
timely entrances foil his numerous at
tempts to hang himself ahd who finally
reconciles him to continuing life, was
presented by Marie Louise Frapper of
Palo Alto. Clarita Hunsberger repre
