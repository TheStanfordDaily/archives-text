# ICHIHASHI TO TALK AT STUDENT CONFERENCE
## 
### 
Stanford University will play a large
part in the annual Student Conference
to be held at Asilomar from June 17
to June 26. Professor Y. Ichihashi of
the History department, Rex Bell,
secretary of the Stanford Y.M.C.A.,
and Leslie Blanchard, '12, Executive
Secretary of the National Student
Council of the Y.W.C.A., will act as
three of the leaders of the Conference
groups.
Professor Ichihashi, who will be one
of the main speakers of the conven
tion, will organize his lectures under
the heading of "Facts About the
Pacific," discussing China, Japan,
India, and Korea.
