# MASONIC DEGREE TEAM MEETS AT SAN MATEO
## 
### 
Bringing a successful season to a |
close last night, the Masonic degree
team of Stanford University met at
Sau Mateo, where it conferred a third
degree at the Masonic Temple. Next
Wednesday evening, June 2, the Ma
sonic Club and degree team will leave
for Dacota, the site of the Home for
Aged, maintained by Masonic bodies
for needy Masons.
The last regular meeting of the club
for this year will be held the follow
ing Wednesday, June 9. All members
are requested to be present. Electiou
of officers for the succeeding year will
be held. All campus Masons not mem
bers of the club are invited to attend
this meeting and become affiliated
with it.
