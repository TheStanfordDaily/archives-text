# Spanish Fraternity Meets Initiate Five Members
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Pi, national honorary
Spanish fraternity, held a meeting
last night at the home of Professor
Aurelio M. Espinosa, for the initiation
of live new members. After the cere
mony, members enjoyed a social hour,
at which various forms of entertain
ment were offered by the initiates.
Sinclair Lewis visited the University
of Kansas recently and donated $100
to the "Dove," an independent student
publication.
