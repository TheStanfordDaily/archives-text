# QUAD RE-ELECTION TO FILL FOUR OFFICES
## 
### 
Re-elections for junior and senior
men representatives on the Executive
Committee, president of the class of
1928, and treasurer of the class of
1929 will be held on Quad Tuesday
from 8:45 until 2 o'clock.
William A. Glen and John A. Leiter
will run again for the office of senior
representative on the executive com
mittee, and the 1928 representative
will be chosen from Albert Biaggini
and Murray Draper. Cecil Doll and
Rufus Ilanney are the re-vote candi
dates for the presidency of the class
of 1928, and the 1929 treasurer will be
selected from Joe Carroll and John
Newbegin.
