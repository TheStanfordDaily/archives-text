# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
FIFTH ANNUAL CONFERENCE on
Educational Research of the Cali
fornia Educational Research Asso
ciation, Northern Section, will be
held as follows: afternoon session,
1:30 o'clock; evening session, 7:30
o'clock; both sessions being held in
the Little Theatre.
SATU R DAY
CALIFORNIA EDUCATIONAL CON
FERENCE will meet as follows:
morning session at 9:30 o'clock, and
afternoon session at 1:15 o'clock, in
the Little Theatre. A banquet will
be held in the Stanford Union at 6:15
o'clock under the auspices of the
men's and women's educational fra
ternities, Phi Delta Kappa and Pi
Lambda Theta.
SUNDAY
EPISCOPAL CLUB will meet at 6
o'clock in the parish house. Profes
sor Graham H. Stuart of the Politi
cal Science Department will speak.
DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON at
Memorial Church at 11 o'clock.
Preacher, Right Reverend Edward
Lambe Parsons, Bishop of California.
PROFESSOR GRAHAM H. STUART
of the Political Science department
will speak at the Stanford Episcopal
Club meeting Sunday evening at
6:30 o'clock in the parish house.
ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Benjamin
S. Moore, Trinity Church, San Fran
cisco, in the Memorial Church at 4
o'clock.
TUESDAY
MEETING OF ALL ENGINEERS and
Mining majors and graduates at
7:30, Room 268, for the purpose of
electing staff officers and for the
new Stanford Engineering Journal.
Important.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S CLUB will be
entertained by Mrs. Wilbur, at the
President's House, at 4 o'clock.
LECTURE on "Impressions of Rus
sia," by Professor Henry Lanz, at
7:30 o'clock, in the Little Theatre.
The public iB invited.
WEDNESDAY
UNIVERSITY ORCHESTRA will meet
in Room 84 tonight at 7:15 o'clock.
