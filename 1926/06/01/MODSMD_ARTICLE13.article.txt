# Chemistry Fraternities Will Hold Joint Meeting
## 
### 
Phi Lambda Upsilon and Alpha Chi
Sigma, chemistry fraternities, will hold
a joint smoker for the Chemistry de
partment in the Lobby of the Old
Union at 7:15 o'clock tomorrow night.
Dr. G. B. Frankforter, a professor
of the department, will give a talk
and demonstration on explosives. A
skit will also be given by the pre
medical students who will endeavor to
show "how the young doctor does his
stuff."
All chemistry students are invited.
