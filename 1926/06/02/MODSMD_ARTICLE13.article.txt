# PHI LAMBDA UPSILON TO SHOW BAKELIGHT FILM
## 
### 
"The Story of Bakelite," a film, will
be shown tomorrow evening at 7:30
o'clock in the Little Theatre by Phi
Lambda Upsilon, honorary chcmical
fraternity. Dr. Franklin of the Chem
istry department will give a short talk
on Bakeland, the inventor of the Bake
light process, as he knows him.

Bakelight is a synthetic phenol-for
maldehyde resin which is being put to
numerous uses. The film will show the
process of manufacture of Bakelite and
many of its industrial applications. The
public is invited to attend.
