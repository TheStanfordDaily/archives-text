# Sophomore Class Plans Last Jolly-Up of Quarter
## 
### 
The Sophomore Class will hold its
last jolly-up of the quarter this eve
ning from 7 to 8 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Music for the occa
sion will be furnished by Norm Lar
sen's orchestra.
All members of the class of '28 are
invited to attend.
