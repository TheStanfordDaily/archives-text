# New Art Museum Planned By Washington University
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON—
(P.I.P.) —Ground was broken recently
for Washington's $100,000 art museum
which will house the half-million dollar
art collection recently donated to the
university by Horace C. Henry, Seattle
banker.
One hundred and eighty-five paint
ings, etchings, and water colors, by
famous masters of both the old and
modern school of painting, represent
ing the efforts of 33 years by Mr.
Henry, compose the collection. Art
masterpieces of 137 painters from five
nations are included.
