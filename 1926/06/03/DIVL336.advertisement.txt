FOOT COMFORT EXPERT
from Chicago will be at our store
FRIDAY AND SATURDAY
JUNE 4 AND 5
It is no longer necessary for you to suffer
the tortures of tired, aching, broken-down
feet. For the benefit of persons so afflicted
this store will conduct, on the above dates, a
Special Foot Comfort Demonstration. In
charge of this important work is a foot com
fort expert from the personal staff of Dr.
Wm. M. Scholl, the recognized foot authority.
