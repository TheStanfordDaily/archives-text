# Pi Sigma Alpha Elects Officers at S. F. Banquet
## 
### 
Pi Sigma Alpha, national political
science fraternity, held a banquet at
i the Stewart hotel in San Francisco
recently, at which time Paul E. Jack
son was initiated into the fraternity
and officers for the ensuing year
were re-elected. J. A. Clifford was re
elected president, Hubert D. Swim,
vice-president, and Robert Rapp, secre
tary-treasurer.
