# Hal H. King Will Speak Before Advertising Club
## 
### 
Hal H. King, president of the San
Francisco Advertising Club, will dis
cuss plans for the twenty-third annual
convention of the Pacific Coast Adver
tising Club Association at the meeting
of the Stanford Advertising Club to
be held in the Cardinal hotel at 6
o'clock tonight.
A $1,000,000 gift from an anonymous
friend of Dartmouth College, to be
used to construct a new library, has
been received.
