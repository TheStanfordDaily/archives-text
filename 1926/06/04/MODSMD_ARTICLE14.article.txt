# "ART AND LIFE" TOPIC OF HULME'S LECTURE
## 
### 
"Art and Life" is the topic of a lec
ture by Professor Edward M. Hulme
of the History department who will
speak in the Little Theatre at 7:30
o'clock Tuesday night.
Professor Hulme, who graduated
from Stanford in 1897, has been at
Stanford since 1921. He is the author
of a number of books on historical
subjects.
"Art and Life" is the concluding
Tuesday evening lecture of the pres-
I ent quarter.
