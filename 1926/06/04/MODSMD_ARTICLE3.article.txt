# STUDENT PEDALIST RACES TOMORROW TO SMASH RECORD
## 
### 
After four weeks of road work,
John A. Leiter will leave Branner
' Hall at 5 o'clock tomorrow morn
ing in an attempt to break the
Stanford-to-San Francisco bicycle
record of two hours and thirty
five minutes now held by Harold
W. Peterson.
The finish will be at the Nine
teenth avenue and Lincoln Way
gates to Golden Gate Park.
George N. Crocker, varsity de
bater, is to be judge of the race,
and will follow the cyclist in an
auto.
In order to hang up a new mark
Leiter will have to cover the
thirty-three miles at an average
speed of over thirteen miles an
hour.
