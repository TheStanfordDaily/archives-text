# Officers of Sigma Xi Will Be Elected Monday
## 
### 
Officers for the coming year will be
elected by Sigma Xi which will meet
in the faculty clubhouse at 8 o'clock
Monday evening. New members will
be initiated during the evening.
Dr. David L. Webster, professor of
physics, who is president of the Stan
ford chapter of Sigma Xi, will give a
brief talk.

A complete aerial survey of Pomona
College campus an dthe town of Clare
mont has been ordered by the authori
ties of the college.
