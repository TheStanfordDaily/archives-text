# Pi Lambda Theta Elects Officers for Next Year
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta will initiate new
members this afternoon in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. The ceremony will
be followed by the installation of new
officers: president, Gretchen Wulfing;
vice-president, Helen Vogel; recording
; secretary and keeper of records, Dor
othy Knowles; corresponding secre
tary, Mrs. Georgia Benjamin; treas
urer, Hannah Foote.
A tea for faculty members of the
education department and friends of
Pi Lambda Theta will conclude the
afternoon's program.
