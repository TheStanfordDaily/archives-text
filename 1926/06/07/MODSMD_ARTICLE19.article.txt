# MEMORIAL PLANNED FOR WALTER CAMP
## 
### 
Of special interest to the University
is the national memorial planned for
Walter Camp, who was Stanford's first
football coach. Although the memorial
will stand in the midst of the Yale
Athletic Fields in the form of a monu
mental gateway, contributions for the
\ monument will be made by his friends
and admirers and by universities and
schools of the United States.
