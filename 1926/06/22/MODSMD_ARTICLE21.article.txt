# The 						Cellar
## 
### 
Last summer The Cellar had to be closed because it fell so far short
of meeting expenses. This summer it will be kept open if there is
enough patronage to permit. It is not suggested that you surfeit yourself
on sundaes when you don't want them in order that they may be available
when you do, but it would be Avell to remember that the decision of
whether we have a cooling Cellar a week or two from now depends on
whether it has patronage.
