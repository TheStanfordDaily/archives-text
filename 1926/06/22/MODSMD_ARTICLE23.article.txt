## 
###

the School of Business of the Univer
sity of Minnesota, will join the faculty
of the Stanford Graduate School of
Business as Professor of Finance next
year, it was announced by President
Wilbur. He is the third man of nota
ble achievement in both educational
and business activities who will take
up their duties with the Stanford
school next year.

Professor J. Hugh Jackson, new pro
fessor of accounting in the Harvard
Graduate School of Business Adminis
tration, will take the same position at
Stanford, and Paul E. Holden leaves
the United States Chamber of Com
merce at Washington, D.C., where he

ment of Commerce in developing sim
