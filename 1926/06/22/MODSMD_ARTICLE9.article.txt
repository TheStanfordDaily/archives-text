# SECOND AWARD MADE BY PARENTS-TEACHERS
## 
### 
The Palo Alto Union Federation of
Parent-Teacher Associations is giving
a second scholarship for the year
1926-27 in addition to the one previ
ously announced. John Young Springer
has been awarded the first scholar
ship, and Wayne Echard Jeffers the
other.
The second award has been made
from the proceeds of the production of
the children's play, "Li Chen," in the
Assembly Hall Saturday, June 12.
