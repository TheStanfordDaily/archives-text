# A 						Business Chance
## 
### 
The Business Manager offers an exceptional opportunity for general
business training and for preliminary work on the business staff of The
Daliy Palo Alto to freshmen who will be here in the Fall and others
who may be interested in the business side of Stanford publications. Ik
sides the excellent training for work on the paper, these summer tryouts
offer a fund of general knowledge which will be invaluable to all who con
template trying out for the business staffs of any other of the activities of
the Associated Students. Those interested should see Harry Bennett at
the office of The Daily Palo Alto.
