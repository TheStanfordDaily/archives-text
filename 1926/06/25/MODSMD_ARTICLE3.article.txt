# SUMMER FACULTY MEN GREETED AT SMOKER
## 
### 
The Faculty Club was sponsor for
an informal meeting in its clubhouse
Tuesday evening at which the mem
bers of the regular teaching staff be
came acquainted with the visiting pro
fessors and they with each other.
The attendance was so large that
the refreshments gave out at precisely
the time when the last of the line
reached the tables at which the sand
wiches, apple tarts a la mode and
coffee and chocolate were being
served.
The men spent the evening in smok
ing and conversation about everything
that professors talk about when they
get together, which for some of them
continued until midnight or so.
All members of the summer faculty
are eligible for membership In the
club.
