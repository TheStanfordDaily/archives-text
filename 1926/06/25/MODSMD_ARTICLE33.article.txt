# TOLMAN BOY IS HURT IN AUTO COLLISION
## 
### 
"Tombie" Tolman, 12-year-old son
of C. F. Tolman, professor of geology,
received scalp and face wounds when
the car in which lie was riding collided
with another at the postoffice corner
last night. The boy was thrown against
the windshield of the car by the im
pact of the two machines. Dr. F. V. A.
Lee closed the scalp wound with six
stitches.
The car in which the boy was riding
was driven by his sister, Miss Hilde
garde Tolman, and the other by Miss
Frances Hadenfelt. Neither of the
women was seriously hurt. Both cars
were damaged.
