# ANDY KERR LEAVING IN AUGUST TO COACH EASTERN FOOTBALL
## 
### 
Andy Kerr, who for four years has
been a prominent member of Stanford's
coaching staff, is leaving about the mid
dle of August to assume his new duties
as football coach at Washington and
Jefferson University. Football prac
tice starts at the Eastern institution on
the first of September while the official
school year commences on September 20.
Coach Kerr is largely responsible for
building up Stanford's football machine
to the point where Pop Warner was
able to turn in a win the second year of
his presence on the local athletic staff.
Kerr introduced the Warner system at
Stanford and deserves a large share of
the credit for the fine showings of the
team in the last two years.
While a coach at Stanford Andy Kerr
also handled the local basketball team.
