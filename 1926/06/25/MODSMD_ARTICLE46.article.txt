# DEAN YOST WELCOMES NEW WOMEN AT ROBLE
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost welcomed the wo
men of Roble hall last night at the first
house meeting of the summer quar
ter. For the benefit of the women who
have not attended Stanford hereto
fore, she told of some of the customs
that are observed on the campus and
in the hall, among these being that
men do not smoke in the lobby of
Roble.
