# NEW MEN AND WIVES TO BE RECEIVED BY DIRECTOR SELLARDS
## 
### 
The visiting professors of the sum
mer quarter faculty and their wives
will be the guests at an informal re
ception in the Union court and arcades
tendered by Director John A. Sellards
Sunday afternoon, July 4, from 4 to 6.
The purpose of the reception is to pro
mote acquaintance among the visiting
faculty families and those who live
here.
All members of the regular faculty,
whether they are on duty this quar
ter or not, and their wives are invited,
and are urged to attend and help wel
come the visitors. No cards will be
sent.
