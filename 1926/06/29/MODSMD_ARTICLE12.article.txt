# Educational Department To Hold Alumni Dinner
## 
### 
The annual dinner of the men of the
Education department will be held
Thursday evening at G:3O at the Stan
ford Union. Dr. Elwood P. Cubberley
will be the principal speaker.
At this meeting an attempt will be
made to organize a luncheon club
which will meet once a week. The pur
pose of the club will be to promote
good fellowship among the members
of the department and to provide
worthwhile programs dealing with sub
jects of general interest.
Walter Baclirodt, Superintendent of
San Jose Schools, who will preside at
the meeting Thursday evening, states
that all men in the Education depart
ment and others interested in educa
tional work will be most welcome at
this dinner.
