# Miller-Fuller Marriage Is Held in Los Angeles
## 
### 
Miss Maxine Miller and Dana L.
Fuller, both graduates from the De
partment of Economics at Stanford
this year, were married yesterday at
St. John's Episcopal Church in Los
Angeles. The wedding was followed
by a reception at the home of the
bride's parents, Mr. and Mrs. Clyde
Eugene Miller.
