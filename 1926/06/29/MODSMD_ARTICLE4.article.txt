# DIRECTOR SELLARDS DECLARES JULY 5 UNIVERSITY HOLIDAY
## 
### 
John A. Sellards, director of the
summer quarter, has announced
that no classes will be held July
5. As Independence Day falls on
Sunday, the University will ob
serve the following Monday as a
holiday.
A special service will be given
in the Memorial Church, Sunday,
July 4.
The announcement made by
Sellards is as follows:
"As Independence Day comes
this year on Sunday, the Univer
sity will observe Monday, July 5,
as a holiday. There will be no
classes on that day."
JOHN A. SELLARDS,
Director, Summer Quarter.
