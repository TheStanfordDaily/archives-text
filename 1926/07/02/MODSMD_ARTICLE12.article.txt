# 130 New Students Given Aptitude Tests by Cowdery
## 
### 
One hundred and ninety students
have been given the college aptitude
tests for entering summer quarter
students; by Karl Montague Cowdery.
Director of Research and Personnel.
One hundred and thirty-five were
given June 19 and 55 on June 26. This
includes those students who were un
able to take the test in February and
March at which time approximately
1400 were given in various places
throughout the state. Mr. Cowdery
was assisted by Robert Bernreuter, re
search assistant.
