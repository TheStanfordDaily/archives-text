# MISS BUNTING IS NOW ON SABBATICAL LEAVE
## 
### 
Miss Helen Masters Bunting, head
of the Women's Physical Education
Department at Stanford, is on sab
batical leave for next year and will
take graduate study at Columbia Uni
versity in New York. She is at present
enjoying a vacation at Carmel-by-the-
Sea, where she will remain until July
15, when she will leave for the East.
Mrs. Ruth A. Calvin is acting head of
the department in Miss Bunting's ab
sence.
