# DEAN YOST TALKS ON COLLEGE ACTIVITIES AT THETA MEETING
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost spoke Wednesday
afternoon on "Citizenship and Extra
Curricula Activities" at the opening of
the national convention of the Kappa
Alpha Theta sorority at the Clift Hotel
in San Francisco. Mayor Rolph wel
comed the 1000 delegates to the city.
Among the topics for discussion at
the convention will be the scholarship
fund and the "Bussey" system of budg
eting, which the sorority has tried for a
year. The National Presdient Mrs. Hal
Lebrecht of Kansas City, presides over
the meetings.
