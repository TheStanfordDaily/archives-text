# Dr. R. E. Swain Takes Part In Medical Examinations
## 
### 
Dr. Robert E. Swain, professor in
the Chemistry department, was in San
Francisco Tuesday where he took part
in giving examinations to candidates
to practice medicine in California.
The examinations were held by the
National Board of Medical Examiners.
Fifteen candidates took the tests in
various medical subjects.
