# PROFESSOR WEBSTER YACHTING IN NORTH
## 
### 
Professor Webster, head of the
Physics department, is at present at
Half Moon Bay Postoffice, British
Columbia.
He is an expert yachtsman, and
used to sail boats in the East, where
he had intended to return this sum
mer. Instead, however, he has bought
a yacht in Canada to experience sail
ing in new waters.
Professor Webster will be back at
Stanford sometime in August.
