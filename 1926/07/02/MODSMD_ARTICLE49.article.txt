# Sarah McNair, '23, Married To George K. MacHamer
## 
### 
Announcements have reached the
campus of the marriage of Sarah Mc-
Nair, '23, to George W. MacHamer on
June 3 at Prescott, Arizona. The young
couple will live in Golden, Colorado.
While on the campus Mrs. Mac-
Hamer was a member of the Kappa
Kappa Gamma sorority. Her home is
in Great Falls, Montana, but recently
she has been teaching school at Pres
cott.
