# Sorbonne Professor to Speak on American Poet
## 
### 
Charles Cestre, professor of English
literature at the Sorbonne, will lecture
at eight o'clock Tuesday night in the
Little Theatre. His topic will be the
poetry of Edward Arlington Robinson.
Professor Cestre is an eminent schol
ar and an authority on French and
English literature of the nineteenth
century. During the past year he has
given a series of lectures on American
poets, at the University of Michigan.
This summer he is a visiting professor
at the University of California. He
speaks excellent English, and com
pleted part of his advanced work at
Harvard.
