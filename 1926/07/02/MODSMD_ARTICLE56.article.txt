# 58 Disqualified at Session Of Scholarship Comittee
## 
### 
Scholarship reports for the spring
quarter show that fifty-eight students
flunked out. Of these sixteen were
freshmen while fifty-nine other fresh
men went on probation. Action in the
above cases was taken at a recent
meeting of the Scholarship Committee.
Changes were also made with re
gard to two scholarships. Don Mar
quis resigned the Memorial which
was given to Robert A. Hume. Mar
quis was then awarded a Converse
Scholarship.
