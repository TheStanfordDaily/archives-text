### 
COMMENTS, QUERIES,
WANTED BY D. P. A.
Contributions, comments, and
queries for publication in the
I Daily Palo Alto may be handed
to any member of the staff or left
at the D.P.A. office, upstairs in
the press building. Those which
merit publication will be printed.
All communications must be
signed by the writer, but will be
printed under a pen name if the
author so desires.
