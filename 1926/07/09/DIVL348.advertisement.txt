Stanford University Weekly Calendar
VOLUME XXXVIII, NO. 4 PUBLISHED EVERY FRIDAY JULY 11-17
SUNDAY, JULY 11
11:00 a.m.—DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: Rev. Charles R. Brown, D.D., Dean of Yale University
Divinity School.
Anthems:
"Blessed Art Thou" Martin
"Grant Us Thy Peace" Gounod
4:00 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Samuel A. Baldwin, Acting Organist; in the Memorial Church.
MONDAY, JULY 12
7:30 p.m.—ENGLISH REVIEW CLUB, at the home of Professor Kennedy, 435 Coleridge Avenue. All graduate Eng
lish students are invited to attend.
TUESDAY, JULY 13
8:00 p.m.—PIANO RECITAL, by Katharine Vander Roest Clarke, in the Assembly Hall. The public is invited.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 14
4 15 p m—ILLUSTRATED LECTURE on "Fashionable Europe in the Age of Baroque and Rococo." by Dr. Johannes
L. Hoops, Professor of English Philology, Heidelberg, Exchange Professor at the University of Cali
fornia; in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
THURSDAY, JULY 15
3:00 p.m.—STANFORD DAMES, in the Women's Clubhouse.
4:15 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Samuel A. Baldwin, Acting Organist; in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—THURSDAY EVENING CONFERENCE, at the home of Dr. David Starr Jordan. Subject: "The Ascent
of the Matterhorn."
FRIDAY, JULY 16
8:20 p.m.—"HAMLET" (Modern Dress), presented by Dramatic Council, in the Assembly Hall. Direction of Gor
don Davis.
SATURDAY, JULY 17
4:00 p.m.—EXCURSION to Lick Observatory, Mount Hamilton. Return about midnight. Fare $3.00.
