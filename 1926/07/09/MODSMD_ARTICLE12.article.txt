# ENCINA POOL WILL BE CLOSED ON SATURDAYS
## 
### 
The men's swimming pool is to be
closed every Saturday during this
quarter.
This ruling was made at the request
of A1 Masters, the graduate manager,
in ordet to give those men who clean
out the pool an opportunity to rest on
Sunday. Should the pool remain open
these men would have to get up early
on Sunday morning to do their work.
