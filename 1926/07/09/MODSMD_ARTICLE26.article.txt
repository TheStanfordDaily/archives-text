# SELLARDS WELCOMES FACULTY ON SUNDAY
## 
### 
John A. Sellards, Director of the
summer quarter, was assisted by Mrs.
John Casper Branner, Mrs. E. P. Cub
berley, and Mrs. Bailey Willis in giv
ing his outdoor reception to the sum
mer quarter faculty in the Union court
last Sunday afternoon. Dean Mary
Yost was unable to be present on ac
count of illness. Music for the occa
sion was furnished by Phil Brooks'
campus orchestra.
Dean and Mrs. Harold L. Bruce of
Berkeley were among the guests. Dean
Bruce is director of the summer ses
sion at the University of California.
