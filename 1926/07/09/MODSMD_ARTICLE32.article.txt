# Buford O. Brown Speaks To Editorial Association
## 
### 
Professor Buford O. Brown of the
Division of Journalism at Stanford and
head of the journalism department at
the University of California Southern
Branch for the summer session, spoke
on "What the Schools of Journalism
are doing for the Weekly Newspaper"
before 300 delegates attending the
forty-first annual meeting of the Na
tional Editorial Association held in
the Ambassador Hotel, Los Angeles.
