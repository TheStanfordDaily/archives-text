# FACULTY CLUB WILL MEET THIS EVENING
## 
### 
The Stanford Faculty Club will hold ;
its next regular monthly meeting in |
the Faculty Clubhouse tonight at 8
o'clock.
All members of the summer faculty ;
are especially urged to attend in order
to promote a spirit of congeniality
between them and the regular faculty
members.
The meeting was changed from its
regular Friday date to avoid conflict '
with Mr. Sellards' reception.
