# PROFESSOR COESTER RECEIVES DIPLOMA
## 
### 
Word has been received from Argen
tina that Professor Alfred Coester of
the Romanic Languages department of
Stanford University has been given a
diploma as honorary member of the
"Junta de Historia y numismatica
americana" of Buenos Aires. Professor
Coester has been in Argentina for
some time and in May he was asked
by the National University of La Plata
to give a series of lectures there on
"The History of Spanish-American cul
ture in its relations with politics."
