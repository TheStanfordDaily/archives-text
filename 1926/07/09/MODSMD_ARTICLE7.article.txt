# Holman and Chandler Among Davis Cup Team Prospects
## 
### 
Cranston Holman of Stanford and
Edward Chandler of the University of
California were among the nine men
selected by the United States Lawn
Tennis Association from which the
American Davis Cup team will be
picked.
Final selection of the four men who
will defend the international title of
the United States will take place on
or before August 19. The Association's
selection was as follows: William T.
Tilden, William M. Johnson, Vincent
Richards, R. Norris Williams, Howard
Kinsey, Cranston Holman, Edward
Chandler, Wray Brown, and George
Lott, Jr.
