# B. E. Howard Holds Reception Honoring Doctor Bullock
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Charles Jesse Bullock
and Professor and Mrs. Ralph William
Aigler were honored by a reception
held by Mrs. Burt Estes Howard on
the lawn of the Kingscote Apartments
Thursday afternoon.
Dr. Bullock has come from Harvard
University to join the summer Eco
nomics staff, while Professor Aigler is
a member of the Law faculty for this
quarter, coming from the University
of Michigan.
