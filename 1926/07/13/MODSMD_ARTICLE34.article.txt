# HALL'S POETRY BOOK READY FOR PUBLICATION
## 
### 
Howard Judson Hall, Associate Pro
fessor of English at Stanford, has just
finished a book entitled "Types of
Poetry," to be published by Ginn and
Company. This book is an introduction
to the study of poetry and will be
used by Professor Hall in one of his
English courses in the future. Another
book by the same name has just been
published by the Macmillan Company.
