# Joseph Glenn Elected High School Principal at Eureka
## 
###

graduate work in the University the
past year, has been elected principal
of the high school at Eureka, Califor
nia. Mr. Glenn came to Stanford from
Eugene, Oregon, where he had been
superintendent of schools.
Mr. George Jensen, the former prin
cipal, who developed the school to the
point where it is considered one of
the best in the state, resigned this
spring to become the Research Secre
