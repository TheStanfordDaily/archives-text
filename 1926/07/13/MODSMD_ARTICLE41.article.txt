# Professor and Wife Back From Automobile Trip North
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. George E. Os
borne have recently returned to Palo
Alto after a short, vacation trip. The
Osbornes have been motoring in the
North for the past two weeks. They
traveled as far as Eureka. Professor
Osborne is not teaching this quarter.
