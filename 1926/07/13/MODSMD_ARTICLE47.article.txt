# GABRILOWITSCH TO CONDUCT
## 
### 
The fourth of the series of concerts
given by the San Francisco Symphony
Orchestra will be held next Sunday
afternoon at the Hillsborough school
grounds. This concert will mark the
first Pacific Coast appearance as or
chestral conductor of Ossip Garbilo
witsch, the world-renowned pianist and
director of the Detroit Symphony Or
chestra. Gabrilowitsch has long been
admired as one of the outstanding
pianists of the day.
