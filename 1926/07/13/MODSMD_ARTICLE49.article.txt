# Stanford's Prestige Great Says Eastern Alumnus
## 
### 
Wilson C. Price, '97, as a representa
tive of Stanford University, attended
the inauguration of Dr. James A.
Beebe as president of Allegheny Col
lege. In a letter to President Wilbur
Mr. Price says, "I found that the pres
tige of Stanford was very great, and
because I represented that University,
I found myself for that day a person
of considerable importance."
Mr. Price took his LL.B. from Har
vard in '00 and is at present practicing
law at Jamestown, New York.
