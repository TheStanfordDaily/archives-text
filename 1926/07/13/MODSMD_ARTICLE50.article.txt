# Ted Baer Places First in Advertising Clubs Contest
## 
### 
Theodore F. Baer, '27, won first
prize in a coast-wide three-minute
speaking contest held before the Par
cific Coast Advertising Clubs Associa
tion in San Francisco last week.
Baer spoke on the question, "Can
Advertising Stabilize Prosperity?"
During the past school year he was
debating manager for Stanford, and
participated in the Utah and U.S.C.
debates. He is at present doing adver
tising work in Palo Alto.
