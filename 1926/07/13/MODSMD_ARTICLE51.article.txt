# Dink Templeton Awarded High Position in Legion
## 
### 
"Dink" Templeton, track and field
coach of Stanford, has been appointed
regional athletic director of the Pacific
Coast for the American Legion.
His selection was made by the na
tional officers of the order. He is
shortly to take part in a conference
relative to the Legion's plans for ath
letics to be carried on by the various
posts of the organization.
