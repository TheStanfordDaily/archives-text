# Hertz Presents Brilliant Concert at Hillsborough
## 
### 
The most brilliant program of the
symphony concert series was given
under the direction of Alfred Hertz,
regular conductor of the orchestra, at
Hillsborough, Sunday, July 11. Four
numbers were presented: Tannhauser
Overture (Wagner), Symphony in B
Minor (Schubert), Hungarian Rhap
sody No. 2 (Liszt), and Symphonic
Suite "Scheherazade" (Rimsky-Korsa
kov).
An audience of about 4000 persons
attended the concert.
