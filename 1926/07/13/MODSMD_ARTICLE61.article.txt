# Art Gallery to Exhibit Rhenstrand Landscapes
## 
### 
An exhibition of landscapes done in
pastel by Jane Rhenstrand will soon
replace the present collection of Isa
bel Snelgrove's oil paintings in one of
the small rooms at the Stanford Art
Gallery. Mr. Leraos, director of the
gallery, plans to have the new display
open to the public about July 14.
