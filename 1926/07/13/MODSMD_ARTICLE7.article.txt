# HOLD PLAY TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Tryonts for the two remaining plays
of the summer, "Hay Fever" and
"Aren't We All," will be held this
afternoon and tomorrow afternoon
and tomorrow afternoon from three to
five o'clock in the Assembly hall. All
students are eligible to take part in
these plays with the exception of
freshman women, and everyone is
urged to try out.
