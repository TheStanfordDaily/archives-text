# DIRECTOR OF CONCERT ARRIVES ON GROUND
## 
### 
Osslp Gabrilowitsch, Director of the
Detroit Symphony Orchestra, who is to
conduct the concert of the San Fran
cisco Symphony Orchestra next Sun
day afternoon at the Hillsborough
school grounds, arrived yesterday. He
was accompanied by his wife and
daughter, and they are guests at the
home of Mrs. Samuel Knight in Bur
lingame. Mrs. Gabrilowitsch, known
on the concert stage as Clara Clemens,
Is a daughter of the late Mark Twain.
