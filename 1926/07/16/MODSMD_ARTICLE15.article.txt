# By Force of Circumstance
## 
### 
The sledding is tough when there is no snow. There is no snow in
the summertime. Consequently sledding is tough.
Commencing with this issue the summer edition of the Daily Palo-Alto
reduces its size from six to five columns. Advertising does not warrant
the continuation of the larger issue.
The Daily Alto has decided to reduce in size rather than in the
number of issues. Notwithstanding the reduction in size the news will
he fully covered so that the readers will not lose by the change.
