# ESPINOSA TO GIVE ADDRESS
## 
### 
Aurelio Macedonio Espinosa, profes
sor of Romanic Languages at Stan
ford, will speak on "Picturesque Cities
of Southern Spain," in the Little Thea
tre, July 20, at eight o'clock.
Confining the examples to views of
buildings in Granada, Seville; Cor
dova, and Toledo, the lecturer will
trace development of Moorish architec
ture from the 9th to the 15th century.'
