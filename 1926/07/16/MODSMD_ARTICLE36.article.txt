# MEETING IN CLUBHOUSE OF STANFORD DAMES
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames, an organiza
tion of mothers and wives of Stanford
students, held a meeting in the Wo
men's Clubhouse yesterday afternoon
at 3 o'clock, at which Gordon A. Davis,
Director of Dramatics at Stanford
spoke.
Mrs. M. S. Stark had charge of the
musical program.
Refreshments were served.
