# WEIGEL WORKS FOR TRIBUNE
## 
### 
Stanley A. Weigel, recently elected
president of the Associated Students,
has been spending the summer work
ing on the editorial staff of the Oak
land Tribune. During the past year
Weigel acted as the Stanford corres
pondent for the Tribune, and it is in
recognition of excellent work in this
capacity that he has been given a
place for the summer in the offine.
