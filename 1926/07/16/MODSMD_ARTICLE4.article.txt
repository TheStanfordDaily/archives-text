# SAMUEL A. BALDWIN WILL GIVE RECITAL
## 
### 
Samuel A. Baldwin, organist, will
give his regular recital Sunday after
noon, July 18, in Memorial Church at
4 p.m. The program reads as follows:
Prelude in E Flat J. S. Bach
Adagio; Sonata, Opus 27 No. 2
(moonlight)....Ludwig van Beethoven
(a) Les Jongleurs, (b) La Zingara,
from Sonata, "A Pageant"
Harry B. Jepson
Largo from symphony "From the
New World" Anton Dvorak
Finalle from first symphony
Louis Vierne
