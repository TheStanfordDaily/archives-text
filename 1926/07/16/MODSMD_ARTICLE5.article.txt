# Summer School Students in San Francisco Auto Wreck
## 
### 
Miss Frances Hadenfeldt of Oakland
and Charles McAllister of San Fran
cisco were injured Saturday evening,
July 10, when Mr. McAllister's four
passenger Buick in which they were
riding was wrecked.
The couple were driving in a heavy
fog in San Francisco and the car
skidded as they were coming down
from Twin Peaks. Miss Hadenfeldt is
suffering from a cut on the head and
a bruised back, while Mr. McAllister
had to have two stitches taken in his
leg. He also had bruises and cuts on
the head.
Miss Hadenfeldt is a member of
Alpha Omlcron Pi and Mr. McAllister
is a Kappa Sigma.
