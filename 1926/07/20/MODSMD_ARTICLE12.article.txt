# French Scholar on Campus To Write History of U.S.
## 
### 
An eminent French scholar, Dr. D.
Pasquet, who is professor of American
History at the Sorbonne in Paris, is
now at Stanford gathering material for
a second volume which he is writing
on "The Political and Social History
of American People."
He is here on a fellowship of the
Laura Spelman Foundation. The first
volume has already been printed and
his present visit to many parts of the
United States is being made by Dr.
Pasquet for additional material which
he will incorporate in the second vol
ume. He does not intend to deliver any
lectures while here.
