SUNDAY, JULY 25
11:00 a.m.—DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: Rev. Charles R. Brown, D.D., Dean of Yale University
Divinity School.
4:00 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Samuel A. Baldwin, Acting Organist; in the Memorial Church.
TUESDAY, JULY 27
4-5:30 p.m.—ROBLE CLUB TEA, at Roble Hall. All faculty and students are cordially invited.
6:00 p.m.—INFORMAL DINNER for all students and faculty from the state of Washington, in the Stanford Uniou.
Dr. Cubberley will speak.
8:00 p.m.—LECTURE on "Austria on the Eve of the Great War," by Dr. Alfred Francis Pribram of the University
of Vienna, Acting Professor of History; in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 28
4:30 p.m.—LECTURE on "Scholarship and Its Rivals," by Professor Hardin Craig, Professor of English, University
of Iowa; in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
THURSDAY, JULY 29
12:00 m. —EDUCATION MEN'S LUNCHEON CLUB, in the Stanford Union. Leader: Mr. C. A. Nelson. Speakers:
Dr. Fred Ayer, University of Washington; Supt. C. L. Littel, Centralia, Washington.
4:15 p.m.—LECTURE on "The Truth of Transformism," by Dr. David Starr Jordan; in the Little Theatre. The public
is invited.
4:15 p.m.—ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Samuel A. Baldwin, Acting Organist; in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—THURSDAY EVENING CONFERENCE, at the home of Dr. David Starr Jordan. Subject: "Alsace and
Lorraine."
SATURDAY, JULY 31
9:00 a.m. —EXCURSION to San Francisco, including Presidio. Cliff House, Golden Gate Park, Legion of Honor
Palace, and many other places of interest. Return about 4 p.m. Fare $3.00.
