# NEER LEAVES TO PLAY IN EASTERN TOURNAMENTS
## 
### 
Phil Neer and Jim Davies, ex-Stan
ford tennis stars and winners of the
national intercollegiate doubles in
1922, left for the East, Monday.
Neer is entered in five tournaments,
starting next Tuesday with the Metro
politan Championships held at Brook
lyn, followed by tournaments at South
ampton and Seabright, the National
Championships at Forest Hills, and
one at Newport.
Neer predicts that Cranston Holman
will place fourth in the ranking for
this year; while either Chandler, of
California, or the Frenchman, Borotra,
will win the Nationals.
