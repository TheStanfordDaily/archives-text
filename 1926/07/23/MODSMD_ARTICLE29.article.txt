# BIG BASIN TRIP NEXT
## 
### 
The Big Basin, located in the heart
of Santa Cruz county, is the objective
of this week's excursion, which prom
ises to be one of the most interesting
trips of the quarter. The party, made
up of summer session students, will
leave the campus at 8:30, Saturday
morning, and travel in automobiles
over the long winding highway
through the Santa Cruz mountains to
the big tree country. This road runs
through some of northern California's
most beautiful scenery, and pictur
esque views confront the tourist on
every hand.
