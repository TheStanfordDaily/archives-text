# VISCOUNT ASTOR VISITS CAMPUS
## 
### 
Viscount Astor, who with his son is
traveling in the United States, will
visit the campus at 3 o'clock this
afternoon. He is the son of William
Waldorf Astor, who renounced his
American citizenship to accept the
British title. He has been a member
of parliament and is owner of "The
Observor." Viscount Astor's wife, Lady
Mary Astor, who is American-born, is
the first woman ever elected to the
British parliament
