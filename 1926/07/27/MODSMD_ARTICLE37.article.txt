# DR. ELLIOTT, FORMER REGISTRAR, IN EUROPE
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. O. L. Elliott are now
in Europe. Dr. Elliott was formerly
registrar of Stanford University and
is now a member of the emeritus
faculty.

In a letter recently received from
Mrs. Elliott she stated that some time
had been spent in Paris and that the
return trip would be made by way of
fiondon, arriving home December 20.
She described churches, people, and
attitudes of the serving classes as be
ing interesting and unusual as com
pared with those of the United States.
