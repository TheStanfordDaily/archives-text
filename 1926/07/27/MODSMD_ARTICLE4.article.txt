# Royalty Will Visit Stanford Thursday
## 
### 
Crown Prince Gustav Adolph and
Princess Louise of Sweden will visit
the Stanford campus Thursday morn
ing entering the Memorial Court at
9:30. While on the Peninsula they stay
at the home of William H. Crocker at
Hillsborough. The prince and princess
are at present touring the United
States and are expected to arrive in
San Francisco today.

The Crocker estate is one of the
points of interest to be visited by the
excursion under the leadership of Pro
fessor Clark on Saturday.
