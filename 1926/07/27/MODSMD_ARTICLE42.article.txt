# Stanford Man Escapes Injury in Auto Mishap
## 
### 
Harold D. Towers, '27, and Miss
Marion Shea of San Francisco, narrow
ly escaped injury Wednesday after
noon when Tower's automobile col
lided with a Municipal Railway car at
Union and Fillmore streets, San Fran
cisco. Miss Shea was driving when
another automobile tried to pass at
the intersection and cut off her view.
