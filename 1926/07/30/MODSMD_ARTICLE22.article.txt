# Visiting Savants Talk to Tau Psi Epsilon Members
## 
### 
Tau Psi Epsilon, the Psychological
Fraternity, held a get-together Tues
day evening in the main hall of the
Psychology department. Supper was
served after which the visiting mem
bers of the faculty gave informal
talks.
Dr. Kate Gordon of the University
of California, Southern Branch, told of
the work of the psychology depart
ment at the Southern Branch in con
nection with the Los Angeles police
department. One aspect of the gestalt
theory of psychology was presented
by Professor Karl Duncker of the Uni
versity of Berlin and Clark Univer
sity, who pointed out certain relation
ships between the configuration and
intelligent behavior.
