# Destructiveness
## 
### 
It is hard to believe that it should become necessary to lock the
occupants of Toyon Hall out of their own billiard room because of their
destructiveness. Toyon is not a freshman dormitory, but evidently the
house committee has a few wards who have not yet outgrown the romper
age.
Pride in their hall has led some of the men to declare that the fault
lies with irresponsible outsiders, but observation of the habits of some
unthinking college'men would tend to discredit Sue If a simple explanation.
