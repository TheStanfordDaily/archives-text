# MISS HANDY GOES TO HONOLULU
## 
### 
Miss E. H. Handy, Director of Stan
ford Union, will spend her vacation in
Honolulu.'Miss Handy expects to sail
for the islands August 4 and will be
away the entire month. During her ab
sence Stanford Union will be in charge
of Miss Hilda Longyear, House Direc
tor of Roble Hall.
