# DR. FARNSWORTH TO WED
## 
### 
Dr. Paul R. Farnsworth, instructor
in Psychology, left last Sunday for
Columbus, Ohio, where he will marry
Miss Helen E. Cherington, at present
teaching assistant in Economics at
Ohio State University.
Mr. and Mrs. Farnsworth will spend
their honeymoon in the Canadian
Rockies and arrive at Stanford about
September 15. They will make their
home in the vicinity of the University.
