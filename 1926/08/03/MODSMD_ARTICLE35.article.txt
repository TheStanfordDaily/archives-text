# LAURA WEBB IS ROBLE HEAD
## 
### 
Laura Webb has been chosen presi
dent of Roble Club, as Mrs. Elliott
was here only for the first half of the
quarter. Two new members, Muriel
McCutchen and Gretchen Wulfing,
have been chosen for the Committee
of Five, to take the places of Mrs.
Elliott, who is leaving, and of Pauline
Peipers, who has resigned.

A bathing beauty parade, showing
styles past and present in bathing
suits, was part of a final exhibition of
the summer swimming class at the
University of California.
