# RECENT GRADUATES MARRY
## 
### 
The wecttiing of Miss Harriet Oliver
of Loh Altos and Leslie H. Peter, Jr.,
took place in the Ng Tong garden, ad
joining the Oliver residence, Saturday
night. July 31. The maid of honor and
bridesmaids were all sorority sisters
of Miss Oliver, who is a Delta Gamma
and a Stanford graduate of the class
of '26. Peter, who received his A.B, at
Stanford in 1925, is an Alpha Kappa
Lambda.
