# SECOND TOYON DANCE PLANNED FOR TONIGHT
## 
### 
The second Toyon dance will be held
this evening in the lobby of the hall.
Unique lighting will be featured, and
Ev Young and his orchestra will fur
nish the music.
Patrons and patronesses for the af
fair will be: Professor and Mrs. Mur
ray Shipley Wildman,. Professor and
Mrs. Charles Reynolds, Professor and
Mrs. James Gordon Emerson, Mr.
Elliot Ayres, and Mr. John A. Sellards.
It is to be a program dance, and ad
mission is $2. The committee in charge
consists of Victor Walker, chairman;
Henry Harris, Carvel Brown, and Ed
ward Frank.
