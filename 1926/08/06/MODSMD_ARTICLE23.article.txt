# R. W. AIGLER TO SPEAK TO MICHIGAN ALUMNAE
## 
### 
Professor Ralph William Aigler, a
visiting law professor from the Uni
versity of Michigan, will give an ad
dress Friday afternoon before the
Pacific Coast Alumnae Association of
the University of Michigan.

The alumnae association of Michi
gan is the largest of any college in
the United States. The Pacific Coast
Division holds a tri-annual convention
which will be held in San Francisco
this year at the Palace Hotel. Profes
sor Aigler's topic will be "Education
and The University of Michigan."
