# Telephone "Service" Again
## 
### 
Authenticated reports of two unsuccessful attempts to secure tele
phone communication from the 2900 exchange, the switchboard which
handles telephone "service" for campus dormitories, have been received
by The Daily Palo Alto. At :31 Saturday morning an attempt was
made to communicate with the Union. The report was, "2900 does not
answer."
Sunday morning at 11 :43 a person with a broken hand attempted to
call a doctor, using - a telephone in the Union, lie could get no response
from the switchboard operator.
