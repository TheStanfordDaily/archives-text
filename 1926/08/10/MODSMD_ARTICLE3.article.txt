# FINAL GABRILOWITSCH CONCERT APPRECIATED
## 
### 
Ossip Gabrilowitsch, the famous
Detroit Symphony director, acted as
leader of the San Francisco Symphony
Orchestra for the last time at the
Hillsborough concert Sunday. The pro
gram was as follows:
Overture to "Der Freischutz"....Weber
Symphony No. 4 in D minor Schumann
Prelude to "Die Meistersinger"
Wagner
"'Peer Gynt" Suite No. 1 Grieg
Morning
Ase's Death
Anitra's Dance
In the Hall of the Mountain King
Oriental Dances from Prince Igor
Borodin
