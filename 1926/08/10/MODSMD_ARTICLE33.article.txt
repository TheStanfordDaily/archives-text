# STEVENSON WINS SWIM
## 
### 
Carl Bering Stevenson, '27, won the
men's Silver Gate channel swim at 1
San Diego Sunday.
Stevenson covered the 600-yard
course in 7:25 2-5. Wallace O'Connor,
also of Stanford, placed second in the
race. Both men have been prominent
students of Ernie Brandsten and both
have made countrywide reputations as
swimmers.
