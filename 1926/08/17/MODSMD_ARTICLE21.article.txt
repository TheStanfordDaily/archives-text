# ATWOOD BACK FROM CAMP
## 
### 
Captain Frank J. Atwood, assistant
professor of military science and tac
tics, returned to the campus last week
from Camp Lewis, Washington, -where
he was in charge of the ordnance de
partment for the Pacific Coast Annual
Encampment of the Reserve Officers
Training Corps. Major John Keliher,
assistant professor of military science
and tactics at Stanford, was in charge
of field artillery at the encampment.
Thirty-one students from Stanford
went to the six-weeks camp this year,
which ended July 31.
