# BINGHAM TO GO TO COLUMBIA
## 
### 
Joseph Walter Bingham, professor
of Law at Stanford, will go to New
York on August 25 where he will teach
in the law school of Columbia Univer
sity. He will give courses in Conflicts,
Trusts and Wills, and Equity, and will
remain East for a year. Professor
Bingham, with his wife and son, is
vacationing at Fallen Leaf near Lake
Tahoe, where they will remain until
they leave for New York.
