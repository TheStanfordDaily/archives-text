# DAMES TO HOLD FINAL AFFAIR
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will hold their
last meeting for summer quarter on
Thursday afternoon at 3 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. Mrs. Charles
Reynolds, the president, has urged all
members to attend. The meeting will
be an informal social affair, with no
program.
