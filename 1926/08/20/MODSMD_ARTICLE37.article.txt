# MEDICAL STUDENT MARRIES
## 
### 
Marshall Irwin Mason, a student at
the Stanford Medical School in San
Francisco, and Bertha Labaree Shedd,
'25, niece of Ray Lyman Wilbur and
Secretary of the Navy Curtis D. Wil
bur, were married in the Memorial
Church last Saturday. After the honey
moon the couple will live in San Fran
cisco.
