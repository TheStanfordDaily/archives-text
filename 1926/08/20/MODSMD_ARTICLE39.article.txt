# ROBLE GIVES SURPRISE PARTY
## 
### 
Roble Club gave a surprise party
for Mrs. Stearns, the Social Director,
Thursday night at 10 o'clock in the
Roble Library. The girls served cider,
doughnuts, and popcorn. Mrs. Stearns
is leaving Monday for a vacation in
Oregon. She will return the last of
September.
