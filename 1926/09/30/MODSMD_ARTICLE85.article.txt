# Allen Returns to Play Stanford Organ Sunday
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University Organ
ist, will return to the campus from an
extended trip in Europe in time to
play for the first service and recital
Sunday morning at Memorial Church.
He desires all students and mem
bers of the community who wish to be
enrolled in the choir for the coming
year to be present at Room 84 on
Sunday morning at 10:15 o'clock to
rehearse the hymns for the matricula
tion service.
The voices of all students wishing
credit in choir and oratorio will be
heard by Mr. Allen at Room 84, Pale
ontology building, between the hours
8:30 to 12 o'clock every morning next
week, except Friday.
