# Stanford Man Appointed to Montana University Faculty
## 
### 
Robert B. Fitzgerald, M.A. '24, has
been appointed professor of English at
the University of Montana this year. He
takes the place of Sidney H. Cox, who
resigned to take a position at Dartmouth.
Miss Lucia Mirrilies, sister of Miss Edith
Mirrilics of the Stanford English De
partment. is also on the Montana Eng
lish faculty.
