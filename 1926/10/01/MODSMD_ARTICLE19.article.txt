# REGISTRATION LAGS BEHIND 1925 RECORD
## 
### 
Total registration for yesterday was
1271, two behind that of the first day
of the first day of fall quarter which
last year was 1273, was the report
given out from the registrar's office
last night.
Most of those who filed registration
yesterday were members of the class
of '30. Those who had fully completed
their registering by last night ex
ceeded the number of those that had
completed their registering a year ago
by twenty-seven. The remainder of
both are those who had taken out
blanks but failed to fill out all the
requirements by closing time.
