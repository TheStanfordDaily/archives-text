# 1927 Stanford Quad Sells At Reduced Price Today
## 
### 
An opportunity to get the 1927
Stanford Quad at a reduced price will
be offered today when the yearbook
will be sold at $5.50' instead of the
regular price of $6. Salesmen from
the Quad managerial staff will be in
front of the Administration building
all day.
The books may be purchased in one
installment, or may be reserved by
paying the first installment of $2.50
and the other $3 by February 1, ac
cording to Robert Paradise, manager
of the book.
