File your ticket application today—don't wait until tomorrow.
As far as the student is concerned no wiser advice was ever passed on,
mainly for the one big reason that if his application is not sent in to the
Board of Athletic Control before October 15—there is a perfectly good
chance that he may not receive any Big Game tickets.
However, every student is practically assured of a rooter ticket and
two general admission tickets if the application is filed promptly. A few
moments spent today in filling out the blank you receive while register
ing, writing the check, and mailing both to the Board are bound to save
'much grief later Oil.
It is virtually impossible, however, that everyone comply with these
limitations, and someone is going to do a lot of moaning and wailing to
no avail. Insure yourself against this possibility and act today.
