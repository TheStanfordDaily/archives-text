# Jim's Barber Shop Offers Prizes in Grid Contest
## 
### 
Jim's Barber Shop in the new Union
announces a football score contest open
to all students and professors in Stan
ford. Contestants will turn in their
guess for the score of each Stanford
game before noon on the day of the con
test.
On the following Monday the winner
will be announced, and a prize of $2.00
will be awarded. The contest opens each
week on Monday at 8:00 a.m., and closes
Saturday at noon.
