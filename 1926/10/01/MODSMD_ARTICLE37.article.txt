# STUDENTS MAINTAIN SAME AVERAGE FOR TWO YEARS
## 
### 
Stanford students maintained the same average during the year 1925-20
that they held in 1924-25. In the ratio of grade points to registered units, the
average for the entire University was 1.500 for both years, according to
figures released yesterday afternoon by Mrs. M. D. Huston, chairman of the
Scholarship Committee. This is midway between a Band a C. It is especially
conclusive when it Is considered that the tabulated results extend over a
period of two years.
Phi Beta kappa leads the list of organizations, with an average of 2.358.
Non-sorority women made better grades than sorority women. Both groups
scored more honor points than the men, with non-fraternity men ranking above
the members of the Row.
