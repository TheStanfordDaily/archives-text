# SIDNEY DEBATE TRYOUTS WILL BE HELD SATURDAY
## 
### 
Tryoutees for the University of Sidney
debate are asked to report to the Little
Theater at 9:30 Saturday morning, when
the tryouts are to be held, according to
Theodore F. Baer, debate manager.
The question for discussion is "Re
solved, That we have more to fear than
to hope from science." The speeches will
be judged by. Professor James Gordon
Emerson, faculty debate adviser, Profes
sor Lee Emerson Bassett, and Robert
Littler.
One graduate and two undergraduate
students, together with one alternate, will
be chosen to represent the University.
