# Dean Hotchkiss to Give Talk at Oregon Meeting
## 
### 
Willard E. Hotchkiss, dean of the
Graduate School of business, will take
part in one of the largest educational
celebrations ever planned on the Pacific
Coast when a number of leading educa
tors of the United States meet at Eu
gene, Oregon, October 18-23. Universi
ties and colleges from all over the world
will send representatives.
During the conference Dean Hotchkiss
will discuss the topic "Human Relations
in Industry." Other important addresses
will be given by Dr. John Buwalda of
the California Institute of Technology;
Dr. E. T. Williams, professor of Orien
tal languages at the University of Cali
fornia; and Dr. Claiborne M. Hill, presi
dent of the Berkeley Baptist Divinity
School.
