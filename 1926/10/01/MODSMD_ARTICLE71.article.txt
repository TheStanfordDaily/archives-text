# A.W.S. Schedules Assembly, Barbecue for New Women
## 
### 
An assembly for all new women will
be held tomorrow at 11 o'clock in the
Little Theater. Charlotte Lovekin, presi
dent of Associated Women Students, will
preside at the meeting, and will explain
traditions and regulations on the campus.
Dean Mary Yost will also welcome the
new women to the campus.
At noon a barbecue will be given in
the field near Roble gymnasium where
the usual Field Day meeting is held.
