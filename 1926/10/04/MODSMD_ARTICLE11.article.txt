# BUSINESS STAFF TO HOLD TRYOUTS TODAY
## 
### 
Tryouts for the business staff of the
i Stanford Daily will begin this after
noon at 1 o'clock in the Press build
ing. All freshmen interested in obtain
ing practical business experience and
working toward a salaried position
! are urged to sign up for the tryouts.
The activities of the business de
partment give the tryoutee a chance to
learn actual advertising and merchan
dising work. The merchandising de
i partment of the staff studios the needs
and possibilities of the local market
in relation to the commodity or ser
vice of the merchant, and writes ad
vertising ropy. This copy, as well as
space in the paper, is sold by the
space salesman. In this way the try
outee may become familiar with both
the preparation and sale of advertis
ing copy.
The freshman may become familiar
with business problems and talk with
successful business men. Moreover it
gives an opportunity to serve the stu
dent body in one of its most important
j functions.
