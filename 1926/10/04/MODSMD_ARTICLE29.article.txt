# POSITIONS ON BAND NEARLY FILLED; 12 MUSICIANS NEEDED
## 
### 
Although most of the University
Band personnel has been chosen in
the tryouts held the last few days,
there is still need for a few more men,
according to Ernest Whitney Martin,
Director.
Professor Martin still has places
open for three or four clarinets, one
flute, two saxaphones, one trumpet,
one bass, and one drum. He also de
sires to secure two pianists for bell
parts.
Men who play these instruments are
asked to make appointments with Pro
cessor Martin for their tryouts.
