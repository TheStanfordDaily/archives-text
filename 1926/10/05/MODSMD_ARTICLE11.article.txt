# EX COMMITTEE WILL NAME SECRETARY TO REPLACE R.S. BOREN
## 
### 
Filling the vacancy left by the fail
ure of Raitt Boren to return to school
will occupy the main attention of the
Executive Committee at the meeting
In the Associated Students Office in
the Old Union at 7 o'clock this eve
ning. Boren was to serve as secretary
of the committee had he registered
this fall.
The only other scheduled matter for
the meeting is the arrangement of a
large number of requests for dates.
