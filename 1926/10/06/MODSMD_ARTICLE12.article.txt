# Women's Conference Party Planned for Clubhouse
## 
### 
Women's Conference Party will be
held at 7 o'clock tonight in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Charlotte Lovekin,
president of Associated Women Stu
dents, Marie Manchee, president of
Women's Athletic Association, Ethe
lind Thompson, president of the
Y.W.C.A., and Dean Mary Yost will
give short talks of welcome to the
freshmen.
Miss Lovekin announced that tho
party was for all Stanford women and
that graduate students and commuters
were particularly asked to come.
Masquers Society is taking charge of
the entertainment for the party.
