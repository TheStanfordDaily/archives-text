# Quad Tryoutees Will Meet Today in Press Building
## 
### 
Quad staff tryoutees who wish to
begin work now are asked to meet
Ruth Jordan, Women's Editor of the
1927 magazine, at the Quad office at
1 o'clock today when those who have
already signed up for the tryout
period will hold their second meeting.
Photographic as well as managerial
and editorial tryouts are scheduled to
start now. During this week the edi
torial staff will assist the managerial
staff in selling Quad subscriptions.
Subscriptions may be obtained for
$5.50 now whereas $6 will be charged
later. Seniors may secure their sub
scriptions at the Engineers' Corner.
Picture appointments will be made
there also.
