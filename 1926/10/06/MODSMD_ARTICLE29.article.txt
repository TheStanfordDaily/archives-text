# "insanity Defense" to Be Topic of Women's Debate
## 
### 
"Resolved, That insanity as a de
fense in criminal cases be abolished,"
is the question chosen for the triangle
women's debate to be held sometime
in the end of November or the be
ginning of December between Stan
ford, the University of California, and
Mills College. Plans for this debute
will be discussed at a meeting of the
Women's Debating Society tomorrow
evening at 7:15 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
All women who are interested are
invited to attend the meeting. Fresh
man and new women are especially
urged to come. Anyone who has had
previous debating experience will be
given an opportunity for showing her
ability.
