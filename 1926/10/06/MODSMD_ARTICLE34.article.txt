# President Wilbur to Talk At Bankers' Convention
## 
### 
President Wilbur left Monday eve
ning at 8 o'clock on the Lark to speak
at the American Bankers Association
at Los Angeles. President Wilbur was
accompanied by Mrs. Wilbur. This is
the only meeting that he will attend
and he will return to the campus later
in the week.
