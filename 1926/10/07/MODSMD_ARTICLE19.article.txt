# EIGHTY-SEVEN REPORT FOR SOCCER PRACTICE
## 
### 
Establishing a record for soeccr turn
out, eighty-seven men have reported to
Coach Harry Maloney seeking positions
on the squad this year. Fifty-seven of
this group are out for the varsity team,
and thirty are seeking berths on the
freshman aggregation.
Coach Maloney stated last night that
the majority of men now out will he re
tained, and three full teams will be or
ganized, including first and second var
sity teams and a freshman team. The
season will officially open this Saturday
