# Program for Review Club Meeting Includes Readings
## 
### 
The English Review club will meet
Monday evening at the home of Dr. Ken
nedy, of the English department. The
program will consist of readings and
reviews of various books and magazines.
The cluh was formed a year ago by
Dr. Kennedy, and is composed wholly of
graduate members of the English de
partment. It meets regularly every second
Monday at the home of Dr. Kennedy
throughout the school year.
