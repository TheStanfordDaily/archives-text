# CHESS CLUB MEMBERS CALLED TO MEETING
## 
### 
All old members of the Chess club
who are on the campus this quarter are
expected to attend the meeting of the
organization in the Y.M.C.A. building at
7:30 o'clock tonight, according to Kenneth
Howard, president. Intensive practice, he
says, will begin at once, with a permanent
ladder ranking beginning as soon as all
members have played matches for posi
tion. The meeting is for members only.
The program of the Chess club for this
year includes also a discussion at each
meeting of a separate game in chess.
During the year each member will be
given an opportunity to study some par
ticular move and demonstrate it to the
club. These discussions will be continued
up to the time of the match with Cali
fornia.
