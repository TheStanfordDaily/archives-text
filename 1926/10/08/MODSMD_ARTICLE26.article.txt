# Ellington Needs More Men For 1930 Interclass Team
## 
### 
"More men will have to turn out
before we will have anything like a
championship team," says Coach El
lington of the freshman interclass
squad. Practice was held Wednesday
and yesterday when about fifteen men
turned out. Practice is held every day
at 4 p.m. at Field 4, opposite Encina
Hall.
