# Women's Debating Society Chooses Year's Officials
## 
### 
Rhoda Lewis was elected debating
manager, and Marian Clocksen was
chosen secretary at a meeting of the
Women's Debating Society last night
the Women's Clubhouse.
Plans for the coming triangle de
bate between Stanford, the University
of California, and Mills College were
discussed. The debate will take place
either late in November or early in
December.
Although the date for the tryouts
has not been set yet the club will
meet next week to make further plans.
