# New Students to Attend Matriculation Service
## 
### 
Matriculation service for new stu
dents will be held at the Memorial
; Church Sunday, October 10, at 11
o'clock. The services are under the
direction of Dr. Gardner, and ad
dresses will be made by President
Wilbur, the Dean of Women, and the
Dean of Men.
All new lower division men will
meet at the Little Theatre at 10:45
and, accompanied by their sponsors,
march in procession to the church.
The new women and their sponsors
will assemble in the west arcade of
the inner quad.
