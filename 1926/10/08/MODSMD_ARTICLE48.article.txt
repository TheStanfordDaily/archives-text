# Maloney Requests Golfers To Meet in Encina Gym
## 
### 
Golfers and golf enthusiasts are re
quested by Harry Maloney, Director
of Minor Sports, to meet him in his
office in the Encina gym at 12:45
today. This meeting will concern all
those interested in golf whether they
are proficient in the sport or not Both
golf and handball have been placed
under the supervision of Maloney this
year.
