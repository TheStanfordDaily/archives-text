# Sophomores to Pay for Broken Encina Windows
## 
### 
Damage to the amount of twelve
dollars will be charged to the class
of 1929 for the breaking of ten Encina
hall windows Wednesday night fol
lowing the mud fight, according to
Elliot H. Ayres, manager of men's dor
mitories.
Approximately $600 indebtedness has
been Incurred by the sophomore class
for wanton destruction of University
property. Included in this amount is
$350 for broken windows in Encina
last spring quarter, $188 for pillows
destroyed, $42 for removing ink stains
from the freshman hall, and $20 for
balustrades.
