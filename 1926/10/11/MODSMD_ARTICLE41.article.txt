# ELIGIBLE TRANSFER LIST ANNOUNCED BY FRATERNITY COUNCIL

###

has prepared a list of transfers that
are eligible for fraternity rushing to
morrow morning. The list, which fol
lows, is composed of all transfers that
have 90 or more units of credit. The
list includes students of graduate
standing. Some of the men in the list
are already members of fraternities.
