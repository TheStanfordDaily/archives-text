# Stanford Rifle Club Names New Officers
## 
### 
Thomas R. Barnes was elected presi
dent of the Rifle Club, Preston W.
Morris, vice-president, and John W.
Crawford, secretary-treasurer, at a
meeting held last Friday. Lawrence V.
Weymouth was elected executive,
whose duty is to schedule matches
and pick the teams.
Arrangements .for the rifle shoots
with California and U.S.C. were dis
cussed. In these meets there will be
two teams, a varsity and a freshman.
Plans for entering in the National
Intercollegiate meet were also formu
lated.
It was announced that the indoor
rifle range, under the Administration
building, would be open from 2 to 5
o'clock on Thursday and Friday after
noos, and during the noon hour every
day.
