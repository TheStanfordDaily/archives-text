# More Students Registered Than Last Autumn Quarter
## 
### 
Completed registrations for this
quarter show an increase over last
year of 131 students. As yet 58 stu
dents have not finished registering,
and when this number is completed
the total for the quarter will be over
3300. The total as it now stands for
October 8 of this year is 3248, while
the total a year ago on the same date
was 3117.
The number of freshmen, transfers,
and graduate students who are attend
ing Stanford for the first time now
reaches a total of 869, with 21 other
now students still completing registra
tion. The final total will approximate
890.
