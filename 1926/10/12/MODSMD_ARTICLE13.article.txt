# Stanford Music Club to Hold Tryouts Tomorrow
## 
### 
The first meeting for the year of
the Stanford Music Club will be held
at 7:30 o'clock tonight in the Women's
Clubhouse. As tryouts for new mem
bers will be held, all those interested
n music and capable of solo work
should come. Old members are also
to attend the meeting, as business
matters will be discussed.
