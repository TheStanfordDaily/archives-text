# DANCE DRAMA COURSE OFFERED FOR WOMEN
## 
### 
As a supplement for the Fiesta, a
dance Urania will be given in the
winter quarter, according: to the plans
of Mrs. Ruth Calvin, acting head of
the Women's Physical Education de
partment. Mrs. Calvin requesfs that
anyone interested in submitting manu
scripts see her this week.
Problems which will arise in the
production of this drama will be
studied in the Dance Composition
class to be given this quarter for all
women who have completed the Ad
vanced Dancing course. Those who
wish to take the course are to meet
with the class in Advanced Dancing
at 4 o'clock this afternoon when Mrs.
Calvin will arrange for meetings of
the new class. The class will meet one
evening a week for an hour and a half.
