# INDIVIDUAL PICTURES SCHEDULED BY QUAD
## 
### 
The first individual pictures for the
1927 Quad were taken in the trophy
room of the Old Union yesterday by
H. P. Fisher of San Francisco. No
photographs will be made without ap
pointments, as sittings are being
scheduled in advance by Quad edi
torial tryoutoes.
Appointments have been arranged at
aeven-ininute intervals from 9 to 11:30
o'clock in the morning and from 12:30
to 5:30 in the afternoon, and from
S to 12 o'clock in the morning to 12
o'clock noon on Saturdays. Sitters are
requested by Allan S. Klauber, Quad
editor, to meet their appointments
promptly, to come neatly attired, and
to bring appointment slips.
