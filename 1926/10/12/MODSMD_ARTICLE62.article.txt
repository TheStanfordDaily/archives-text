# Large Number of Women Enter Texas University
## 
### 
Approximately 1714 women students
have registered in the University of
Texas this fall, according to reports from
the office of the dean of women. Of these
475 are freshmen, about 400 sophomores,
399 juniors, and 280 seniors. There are
also three women students registered in
the School of Law, 138 enrolled in the
Graduate School, and 19 special students
listed.
