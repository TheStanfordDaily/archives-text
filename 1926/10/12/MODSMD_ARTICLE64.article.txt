# Women With Most Pep Will Receive Award From Tulane
## 
### 
A Tavan Key will be awarded by the
Associated Women Students at Tulane
University to the woman who shows the
most enthusiasm, pep, and school spirit
during the year. Popular election will
determine the winner from a selected
group chosen by the faculty.
