# SWIMMERS TO ELECT CAPTAINS THURSDAY
## 
### 
Election of captains for the varsity
swimming and water polo teams will
take place on Thursday at the Encina
pool at 4 o'clock. At this time there
will be a swimming rally of all men
trying out for the swimming and
water polo teams. Everyone who is
interested in trying out for either
am is urged by Coach Ernst Brand
sten to attend the rally.
