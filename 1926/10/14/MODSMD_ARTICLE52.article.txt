# Encina Club Nominations To Close Saturday Night
## 
### 
Nominations by ballot of Encina
Club officers must be made before 7
o'clock Saturday night, according to
Dick Stewart, election board chairman.
The managership, four elective mem
bers of the house committee, and
chairmen of the entertainment and
smoker committees are the offices for
which men must be named.
Following closing of the nomina
tions a list of nominees will be posted
Monday, and a meeting, held Tuesday
night at which the candidates will be
presented, will proceed the ballot elec
tion Wednesday.
A box for nomination ballots has
been provided in the lobby of Encina
Hall.
