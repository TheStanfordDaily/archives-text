# Weekly Resident Alumnae Tea Given at Clubhouse
## 
### 
The regular weekly social tea of tlie
Resident Alumnae Association was
held yesterday at 3 o'clock. The
hostesses for afternoon were: Mrs.
John Branner, chairman; Mrs. J. W.
Mitchell, Mrs. J. W. Stillman, Mrs.
David Starr Jordan, Mrs. Charles B.
Wing, Mrs. Eliot Blackwelder, Mrs.
Augustus T. Murray, and Mrs. Henry
R. Fairclough.
