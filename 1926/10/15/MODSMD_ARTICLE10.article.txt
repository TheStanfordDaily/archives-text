# Students Will Appear in Pendragon Play Tomorrow
## 
### 
Barbara Eubanks, *27, Edwina
Ewing, '29, and Sidney Lewis, '27,
have parts in the Pendragon Players'
production of "The Beggar on Horse
back" to be given at the Palo Alto
Woman's Club House this evening at
8:15 o'clock. The play is a fantastic
comedy of a young composer driven
mad by jazz.
Miss Eubanks and Lewis have both
appeared on the campus stage. Tickets
for the play are on sale at Crandall's
at $1.10 each. After the play thero
will be a 1 o'clock dance at the club
house.
