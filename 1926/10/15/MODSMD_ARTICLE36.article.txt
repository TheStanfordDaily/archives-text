# Last Chance Saturday to Nominate Encina Officers
## 
### 
All nomination ballots for officers
of Encina Club must be deposited in
the box provided in the Encina lobby
before 7 o'clock Saturday night, in
order to have the candidate's name
placed on the ballot for Wednesday's
election, according to Dick Stewart,
election board chairman.
Officers to be nominated are the
lour elective members of the house
committee, chairmen of the entertain
ment and smoker committees, and the
club manager.
