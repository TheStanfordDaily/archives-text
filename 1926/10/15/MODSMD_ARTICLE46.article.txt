# Phliological Club Elects Officials for New Term
## 
### 
Professor William Leonard Schwartz
was elected president of the Philological
Society at a meeting held yesterday af
ternoon. Mrs. Frances T. Russell was
chosen vice-president, Miss Margery
Bailey was re-elected secretary, and Pro
fessor Henry D. Gray made treasurer.
Professor Gray read a discussion of
Webster's play, "Appias and Virginia."
