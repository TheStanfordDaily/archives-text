# DANCE DRAMA PLOTS DUE ON OCTOBER 22
## 
### 
All manuscripts for the dance drama
to be given next quarter must be in
by Friday, according to Mrs. Ruth
Calvin, acting head of the Women's
Physical Education department, and
sponsor of the production.
The manuscript will be chosen the
first part of next week by a committee
appointed by Mrs. Calvin. Many plots
have already been turned in.
The selection committee consists of
Mrs. Calvin, Dr. Margery Bailey, Dean
Mary Yost, Margaret Olsen, Olga
Spirito, Velma Randall, Helen von
Damm, and Madeline Prick.
