# German Club Will Meet To Elect New Officials
## 
### 
Election of officers and appoint
ment of program committees will con
stitute the first business session of
the German Club in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock Thursday.
Membership in the club is open to
all students interested in the German
language, literature, life, and customs.
After the first meeting, regular ses
sions will be held the first and third
Thursday of each month.
Last year's officers were Walter
Gieseke, president; Rose E. L. Trautz,
vice-president; and Hartley W. Devlin,
secretary-treasurer.
