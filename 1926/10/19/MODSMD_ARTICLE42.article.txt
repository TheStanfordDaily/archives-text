# New Rushing Restrictions Issued by U.S.C. Council
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN
CALIFORNIA, Oct. 14. — (P.1.P.) —
Regulations passed by the Interfra
ternity Council Tuesday evening pro
vide that fraternity rushing will be
prohibited during Freshman Week,
and that it shall be unlawful for any
fraternity to extend bids before the
sixth day of classes at the beginning
of a semester. These new rushing re
strictions were drawn up as a tangible
basis to work from, pending further
investigation into systems used in
other colleges.
