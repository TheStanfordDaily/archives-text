# LIT TRYOUTEES WILL HOLD MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
An important meeting of all mana
gerial tryoutees for the Stanford Liter
ary Magazine will be held in the Lit
office in the Press building at 7:30
tonight. As a cut will be made very
soon, it is necessary that all be pres
ent to find out their standing. The
Lit will continue its subscription cam
paign throughout next week until the
required quota is filled. At present the
total is far short of this, and better
student support is needed.
