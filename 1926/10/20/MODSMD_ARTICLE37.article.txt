# Professor H.R. Fairclough Will Address Chi Nu Alpha
## 
### 
Professor Henry R. Fairclough will
talk to the meeting of Chi Nu Kappa,
honorary classical fraternity, at its
regular meeting tonight in the Wo
men's Clubhouse, at 8 o'clock. Profes
sor Fairclough will speak on "My
Harvard Experiences." He was a visit
ing professor in Latin and Greek at
Harvard, in 1925-26.
