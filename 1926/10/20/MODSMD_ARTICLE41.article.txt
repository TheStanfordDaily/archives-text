# Theta Sigma Phi to Hold Doughnut Sale Thursday
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women's honor
ary national journalistic fraternity, an
nounces that its second doughnut sale
of the quarter is scheduled for Thurs
day morning, October 21, in front of
the Women's Clubhouse.
The sale will begin at S o'clock and
will last as long as the doughnuts
hold out.
