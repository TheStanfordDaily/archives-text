# Skyline Road Name Change Advocated in Palo Alto
## 
### 
"Peninsula Ridge Route" is the name
favored by the highway and bridgi
committee of the Palo Alto Chamber
ot Commerce for the present Skyline
Boulevard, as this road is apt to be
confused with the same-named route
In the East Bay region.
The committee also recommended
that Harvey Toy be retained as high
way commissioner.
