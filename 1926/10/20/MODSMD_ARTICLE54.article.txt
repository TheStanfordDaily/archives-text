# Thirty Golfers Complete Preliminary Tournament
## 
### 
From twenty-five to thirty golfer#
yesterday finished the preliminaries
of the golf tournaments now being
held at the Beresford Country Club.
A. number of good scores were turned
in, Dick Lang, the only one to play
thirty-six holes, making a 78 and a
77. Eddie Meyberg turned in a score
of 84.
The Beresford Country Club course
will be open to students except on
Wednesday mornings, according to
Horace Hindry, golf manager.
