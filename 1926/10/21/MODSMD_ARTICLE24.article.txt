# SOPHOMORE WOMEN TO HOLD PICNIC TONIGHT
## 
### 
All sophomore women arc invited
, to a picnic supper being given tonight
under the auspices of the Y.W.C.A.
The picnickers will meet in front of
Roble gymnasium at 5:30 o'clock and
proceed to a spot a short distance
from the campus where supper will bo
served. The exact location has not
i been decided upon as yet, but it will be
; within easy walking distance.
"Wienies" served in the "roast >our
own" style, and a full moon, are ex
pected to bo the main attractions.
Though the excursion was planned
rather suddenly, the sophomore cabi
net hopes that as many as possible
will attend. Supper will cost twenty
five cents.
