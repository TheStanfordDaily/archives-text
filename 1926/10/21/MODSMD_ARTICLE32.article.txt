# Y.M.C.A. to Give Stag for Transfers Sunday Evening
## 
### 
The "Transfer Students' Stag," given
by the Y.M.C.A., will be held in the
Y.M.C.A. building Sunday evening.
October 24, at 7:30 o'clock. Dr. Jordan
will speak on "Stanford Ideals." All
new men except freshmen are invited.
