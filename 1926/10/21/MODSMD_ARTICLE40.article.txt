# DAVIS TO DISCUSS EXHIBIT OF PRINTING, BOOKMAKING
## 
### 
"Fifty Books of the Year" and
"Printing for Commerce," the two ex
hibits that will be on display in the
Stanford Art Gallery Monday, will be
discussed in an illustrated lecture by
W. H. Davis, editor of the Stanford
University Press, in the Little Theatre
at 7:30 o'clock Monday evening.
The exhibits comprise displays of
American printing and bookmaking.
The exhibition, which is being held
through the arrangement of the
American Institute of Graphic Arts,
will be held all week with a special
showing Monday evening immediately
following the lecture. The lecture is
open to the general public.
