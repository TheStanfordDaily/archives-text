THE PENINSULA MUSICAL ASSOCIATION
Palo Alto, California
ANNOUNCEMENT OF CONCERTS
FOR FIFTEENTH SEASON
(1) THURSDAY, NOVEMBER 11, 1926. Marcel Grandjany, French
harpist, with Denise Daverni, lyric soprano.
(2) THURSDAY, DECEMBER 9, 1926. Albert Spalding, American
violinist
(3) THURSDAY, FEBRUARY 10, 1927. Kathryn Meisle, contralto,
Chicago Opera Association.
(4) TUESDAY, APRIL 12, 1927. Roland Hayes, negro tenor.
The object of the association is to provide programs by some of the
best artists who visit Stan Francisco each season, at a popular
price, and in our own assembly hall.
Membership dues are $6.00 a year, entitling the subscriber to two
tickets for each of the four concerts, or 75 cents a ticket.
Make checks payable to the Peninsula Musical Association.
Dues may be paid at any time, but are delinquent October 20th.
For the convenience of campus residents, subscriptions will be received
and information furnished by the Stanford Bookstore. Applications
may also be made at Crandall's or directly to the Secretary.
MISS ALICE W. KIMBALL
Stanford University
Tel. P.A. 644 (P.M. only)
