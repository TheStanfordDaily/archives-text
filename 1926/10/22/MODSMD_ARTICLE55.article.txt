# Freshman Women Hold "Get Acquainted" Meeting
## 
### 
The first meeting of the freshman
women was held Wednesday in Roble
Hall to enable the freshmen to be
come acquainted with each other. Alice
Ingraham, Chairman of Freshman
Women, conducted the meeting.
Plans for two parties, one to be a
get-acquainted party for the freshman
women, and the other ap entertain
ment for upperclass women, were dis
cussed and committees appointed.
Dorothea Wyatt was appointed chair
man of the committee for the get
together party with Martha McDowell
and Helen Thompson assistants. Ada
Pennington, chairman, Motaa Dutton,
and Marilyn Powers were appointed to
arrange for the entertainment for
upperclass women.
