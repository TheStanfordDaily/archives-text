# California Professor to Address Phi Delta Kappa
## 
### 
Dr. G. M. Ruch, Professor of Educa
tion at the University of California,
will address Phi Delta Kappa, men's
educational society, on "The New
Type Examination" at a banquet at
6 o'clock this evening in the New
Union.
Dr. Ruch got his Ph.D. at Stanford,
and was later a professor at the Uni
versity of Idaho. A specialist on
examinations, he is now writing his
second book on the subject.
