# Women Commuters Meet Today Noon to Form Club
## 
###

to the University will be held today
at noon downstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse. The purpose of the meet
ing is to organize a commuters' club
which it is planned to have meet once
a week.
All women interested are Invited'to
come and bring their lunches.
