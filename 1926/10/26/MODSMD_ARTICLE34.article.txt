TUESDAY
9:00 A.M. —Student Body Assembly,
men in the Assembly Hall, women in
the Little Theater.
9:00 A.M. —All members of the Glee
Club must be at the stage entrance
of the Assembly Hall at 9 o'clock
this morning, to sing at the assem
bly. White shirts and dark bow ties
will be worn.
4:15 P.M. —Organ recital by Warren D.
Allen, in the Memorial Church.
4:30 P. M. —Graduate Women's Club
informal tea in the Women's Club
house. All graduate women are in-
vited.
4:30 P.M. —Graduate Women's Club
will entertain graduate men students
of the Education and Psychology
departments at tea in the Women's
Clubhouse.
7:00 P.M. —Distribution of tickets for
the Stanford Concert Series in the
lobby of the Old Union. All students
who purchased season tickets are
asked to get them then.
7:15 P.M. —Junior class officers and
committee heads meet upstairs in
Press building (Quad office).
7:15 P.M. —Orchestra will meet at the
home of Warren D. Allen at 31 Alva-
rado Row.
7:15 P.M. —Phi Lambda Upsilon meets
in the Conference Room of the
Chemistry building. It is requested
that all members please be present.
7:30 P.M. —Christian Science Society
of Stanford University will hold its
bi-monthly meeting in the Women's
Clubhouse. Students, faculty, and
graduates are invited to attend.
7:30 P.M. —Quad editorial staff mem
bers meet upstairs in the Press
building. All members must be pres
ent.
7:30 P.M. —Stanford Music Club will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse.
Second tryouts will be held.
7:30 P.M. —Lecture on "Russia," by
Chester H. Rowell, Lecturer in the
Department of Political Science, in
the Assembly Hall.
