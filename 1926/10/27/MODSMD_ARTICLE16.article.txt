# HAMMER AND COFFIN STICKERS WILL BE DISTRIBUTED TODAY
## 
### 
Hammer and Coffin stickers
bearing the legend, "Trouble
Ahead, Trojans!" will be dis
tributed on the Quad at the
Engineers' Corner this noon.
They may also be obtained at
the Bookstore, the Union, and
after 1 o'clock at the Chaparral
office in the Press building.
All rooters who are planning
to drive to the U.S.C. game are
urged to secure one or more of
these stickers for the windshields
of their cars. A sufficient number
has been printed to furnish every
Stanford car.
