# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
12:00 M.—Circle "S" Society meets to
dav at Delta Tau Delta house.
4:15 P.M.—Organ recital by Warren D.
Allen, in the Memorial Church.
7:00 P.M. —Concert Series ushers, both
men and women, report to the bas
ketball pavilion. Important to be
there on time.
7:00 P.M. —All ushers for tonight's
concert, both me nand women, will
meet in the pavilion.
7:30 P.M.—Thursday evening confer
ence at the home of Dr. Jordan.
Subject, "Is This a Christian Na
tion?"
8:15 P.M. —Concert by Louis Graveure,
baritone, under the auspices of the
Associated Students of Stanford
University, in the Basketball Pav
ilion.
