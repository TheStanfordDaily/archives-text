# Freshmen Will Pick Class Officers at Quad Election
## 
### 
Election of officers of the class of
1930 will be held next week on the
Quad under the direction of the elec
tion board.
At a meeting of the class yesterday
the following nominations were made:
for president, Harold M. Horn and
Clarence E. Bush; for vice-president,
Don R. Winegar, Rosamund Clarke,
John E. McDowell, and Robert S.
Crowl; for secretary, Alice Louise
Ingraham and Betty Brown; for treas
urer, William B. Freeman. Richard R.
Stewart, and Boyd McNaughton.
