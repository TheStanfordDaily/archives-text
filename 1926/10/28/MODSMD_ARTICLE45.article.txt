# STANFORD DAILY HAS CIRCULATION INCREASE
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily has increased its
circulation by 507 copies over last
year's figure and shows an increase of
077 since 1924. Of the 3520 copied now
circulated daily, 3520 copies are de
livered each day by carriers on the
campus and in Palo Alto. The present
mailing list includes the names of 300
alumni and others throughout the
country.
The circulation staff consists of the
manager, one mail clerk, a wrapper,
four carriers in Palo Alto, an increase
of one over last year, and three car
riers on the campus.
