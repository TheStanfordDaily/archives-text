# Women Arrange Tri-College Sports Day on November 6
## 
### 
Arrangements for a Triangular
Sports Day at Berkeley on November
6 were completed by representatives
of the women of Stanford, Mills Col
lege, and the University of California
late Monday evening. Hockey, swim
ming, and tennis were decided upon
as the sports for the gathering, which
will take place in the morning. After
the contests are finished, there will
be a luncheon at Stephens Union
which will be over in time for the
women to attend the Little Big Game
in the California stadium.
The Stanford women will leave the
campus in busses at 6 o'clock in the
morning and reach Berkeley at 9
o'clock. Lists for women who wish to
go to sign up have been posted at
Roble gymnasium.
