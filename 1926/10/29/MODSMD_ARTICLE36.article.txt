# Memorial Church Service Will Observe Saints' Day
## 
### 
The Reverend D. Charles Gardner
will deliver the sermon for All Saints
Day Sunday morning at 11 o'clock in
the Stanford Memorial Church. The
anthems will be: "Call to Worship"
and "For Thee, Oh Dear, Dear Coun
try." The soloist will be Miss Winifred
Eslabrook.
An organ recital will be given by
Warren D. Allen at 4 o'clock Sunday
afternoon. Morning chapel is sched
uled at 7:50 o'clock sharp.
