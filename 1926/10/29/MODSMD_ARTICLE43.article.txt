# U.C. CHOOSES NEW MAN AS GRADUATE MANAGER
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA —
(P.I.P.) —William W. Monahan, '24,
has been chosen to succeed Luther A.
Nichols as graduate manager of the
A.S.U.C. by the Executive Committee
meeting last week.
While in college, Monahan was pres
ident of the A.S.U.C. and also pres
ident of the Pacific Coast delegation
of student body presidents. His term
of office was characterized by several
forward steps in student administra
tion and by the establishment of many
new student, traditions.
