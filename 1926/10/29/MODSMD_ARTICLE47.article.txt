# Schubert Club Will Reorganize on New Basis
## 
### 
Schubert Club, which was disbanded
last spring quarter, is being reorgan
ized, according to Charlotte Lovekin,
wI;o spoke at the Women's Assembly
Tuesday morning. The club, which for
merly sang only classical numbers,
will now be more on the order of a
women's Glee Club, according to pres
ent plans.
Twenty women have signed for the
club, which will be made up on an
entirely new basis. Tryouts will be
held in the near future, as soon as a
new director has been appointed. Any
one interested in joining the club may
sign up with Kathreen Clock.
