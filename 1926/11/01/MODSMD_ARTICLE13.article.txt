# Stanford Man Re-Elected Language Association Head
## 
### 
Professor Oliver Martin Johnston of
the Romanic Languages department
has been elected president of the Mod
ern Language Association of Northern
and Central California for the second
consecutive year.
Four other members of the Stanford
faculty were present at the meeting,
which was held October 2.1 in the San
Francisco library. They were Profess
ors William A. Cooper, Stanley A.
Smith, William L. Schwartz, and So
phie Cramer of the Education depart
ment.
