# COMMERCIAL TESTS SET FOR JANUARY 10
## 
### 
The two semi-annual commercial
examinations of the A. Barton Hep
burn Foundation, given by the Cham-'
ber of Commerce of the State of New
York, will be held next at New York
City, January 10-21, 1927.
One examination leads to the award
of a Junior Credential, and the other
leads to a Senior Credential, both of
which awards represent high attain
ment. Those who pass the examina
tions below the award level will re
ceive certificates of proficiency.
Further information about the ex
aminations may be obtained on re
quest from R. C. McCrea, 65 Liberty
Street, New York City.
