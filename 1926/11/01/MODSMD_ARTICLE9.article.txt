# Education Club to Hold Open Forum on Teaching
## 
### 
"Should Married Teachers Be Em
ployed in Our Schools" will be the
question thrown open for discussion
before a meeting of the Education
Club to be held at 7:30 o'clock this
evening in the Women's Clubhouse.
William C. Mathews, Willard Mor
ris, and William S. Briscoe will speak
briefly on this topic.
