# ENCINA HONORS 1930 TEAM WITH INFORMAL
## 
### 
In honor of the freshman football
team, the Enclna Club will hold an
informal dance on the evening of
November 6, following the Stanford-
California yearling "Rig Game." The
members of the team and those who
attend the game will have ample time
to return for the evening's affair.
Featuring a San Francisco orchestra
and many clover novelties, the dance
is designed to show the appreciation
of the class for their team. The num
ber of bids is limited to two hundred
and fifty. These may be secured any
time this week from Henry A. Gersten
korn, chairman of the Encina Club
Entertainment Committee, who is in
charge of the dance.
