# FRESHMAN JOLLY-UP SET FOR TOMORROW
## 
### 
The new women at Roble, including
those in the freshman class and trans
fers, will entertain at the first jolly-up
of the season tomorrow evening, ac
cording to an announcement by Mar
garet McOmie, president of Roble
Club. Freshmen and other new men
students are invited to the dance,
which will begin promptly at 6:45
o'clock and last until 8 o'clock.
This is the initial jolly-up of a series
planned by the women, the date hav
ing been changed from last week on
account of the U.S.C. rally.
