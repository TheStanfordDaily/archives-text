# Handball Teams Meet in Semi-Final Matches Today
## 
### 
The intramural handball tournament
will enter into the semi-finals this
afternoon when the Kappa Signla team
meets the Delta Tau Delta pair, and
the Chinese Club and Alpha Kappa
Lambda teams oppose each other. The
winners of today's matches will meet
tomorrow in the finals.
Handball will be the first sport to
count for the intramural athletic
championship. Cross-country will be
the next activity, followed by track
and field in the spring. Twenty-eight
teams representing the clubs, halls,
and fraternities of the campus partici
pated in the tournament which ends
tomorrow.
