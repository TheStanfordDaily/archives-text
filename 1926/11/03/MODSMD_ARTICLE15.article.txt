# SOPHOMORE CABINET LEADS Y.W.C.A. DRIVE FOR NEW MEMBERS
## 
### 
Isabel Capps, secretary of the
Y.W.C.A., announced, at a meeting of
the Senior Cabinet last night, in the
Women's Clubhouse, that her office
hours for all campus women would be
from 10 to 12 o'clock in the morning,
and from 1 to 4 o'clock in the after
noon. Visiting hours are to be set from
4 to 6 o'clock every day and persons
wishing to see Miss Capps should
make appointments ahead of time.
The membership drive of the
Y.W.C.A., which is held every year,
is now under way. This drive is under
the leadership of Eleanor Patten and
the Sophomore Cabinet. Kathreen
Clock is to be in charge of the fresh
man discussion groups, which will
start next week. These groups were
started two years ago and have been
found to be successful in aiding the
women to get a better understanding
of university life.
At the meeting last night it was
decided to send Louise Shedd to the
Montezuma conference at Los Gatos
over Thanksgiving vacation.
The Senior Cabinet will have its
next meeting at a supper on Novem
ber 10.
