# Dr. Jordan Will Speak on Lessons of the Election
## 
### 
"Democracy and the Lessons of the
Election" will be the topic which Dr.
Jordan will discuss at his Thursday
Evening Conference at 7:30 o'clock
tomorrow at his home.
Following a general discussion of
Democracy, Dr. Jordan will use yester
day's election as a source from which
he will draw object lessons ns to its
actual workings.
