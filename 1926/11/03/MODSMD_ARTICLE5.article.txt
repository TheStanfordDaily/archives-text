# INTERCLASS SERIES SCHEDULE RELEASED BY HOCKEY COACHES
## 
### 
The schedule of interclass hockey
games for women was released yester
day by Miss Marion Woehnert and
Miss Marguerite McGowen, coaches
for the teams.
The first of the practice games be
tween the classes was played yester
day between the juniors and the fresh
men. The regular series which will
decide the championship will start
November 22. The results of the prac
tice series do not count towards de
termining the interclass championship.
The schedule follows:
Practice games—
Nov. 2 —Freshmen vs. Juniors.
Nov. 4 —Sophomores vs. Seniors.
Nov. B—Sophomores8—Sophomores vs. Juniors.
Nov. 10 —Freshmen vs. Seniors.
Nov. 16—Freshmen vs. Sophomores.
Nov. 18 —Juniors vs. Seniors.
Championship games—
Nov. 22 —Freshmen vs. Juniors.
Nov. 23 —Sophomores vs. Seniors.
Nov. 30 —Sophomores vs. Juniors.
Dec. I—Seniors1 —Seniors vs. Freshmen.
Dec. 7 —Sophomores vs. Freshmen.
Dec, 9 —Seniors vs. Juniors.
