# New Commuters Club Makes Hike, Picnic Arrangements
## 
### 
Plans for social meetings for the
quarter were discussed at. the meeting
of the Commuters Club yesterday noon
in the Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
Hikes and picnics will bo features of
the social program. A week-end at the
VV.A.A. Lodge at Searsville Lake was
also discussed. It was decided to meet
in Palo Alto some night next week to
go to a show.
