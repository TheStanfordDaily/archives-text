# New Women's Scholarship Planned by Cap and Gown
## 
### 
Plans for collection of Bookstore
receipts were discussed at the meet
ing of Cap and Gown held yesterday
at 4 o'clock in Miss Yost's home. The
society, which is planning to slart. a
new scholarship for women in the
coming year, will collect the money
lor the receipts and put it into the
fund.
