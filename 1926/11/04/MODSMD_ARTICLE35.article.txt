THURSDAY
3:00 p.m.—Stanford Dames, in the
Women's Clubhouse.
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital by Warren
D Allen in the Memorial Church.
7:00 P.M. —Stanford Lit managerial
tryoutees will meet in the Press
building tonight.
7:30 p.m.—German Club in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
7:30 p.m.—History Club, in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Mr. Robert Binkley
will speak. .
7:30 p.m.—Thursday Evening Confer
ence, at the home of Dr. David Starr
Jordan.
FRIDAY
7:30 P.M. —All ushers for the perform
ance of "John Ferguson" are asked
to report promptly in the Assembly
Hall.
8:20 p.m.—"John Ferguson," drama by
St. John Ervine, presented by Sword
and Sandals Society, in the Assem
bly Hall.
SATURDAY
2:30 p.m. — Intercollegiate Football,
Santa Clara vs. Stanford, in the
Stadium.
SUNDAY
G :00 P.M. —The Stanford Episcopal
Club will meet in the parish house.
The Rev. Charles P. Otis, S.S.J.E.,
of the Church of the Advent, San
Francisco, will speak informally

about his recent trip to the Hawaiian
t Islands.
