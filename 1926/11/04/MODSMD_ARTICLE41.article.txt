# Y.M.C.A. Will Give Banquet For Encina Club Members
## 
### 
The first Y.M.C.A. banquet for the
members of the Encina Club will be
held tonight in Room 3 of the Univer
sity Dining Halls at 6:15 o'clock.
A program composed of musical
numbers, a boxing match, and talks,
has been arranged by William Free
man, chairman of the committee in
charge. Over sixty have signed up to
attend and there is still room for
more. Anyone wishing to attend should
sign up in Room 3 this noon.
There will be no extra charge for
the banquet and it will last little
longer than the regular meal. These
meetings will be held monthly.
