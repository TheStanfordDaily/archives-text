# Three Stanford Students Slightly Hurt in Wreck
## 
### 
John E. Munholland, Lawrence E.
MeGonigle, and John S. Speer, mem
bers of Pbi Kappa Psi, received slight,
injuries yesterday when Munholland's
car went over an embankment on the
Portola Road near Redwood City.
Although the machine in which they
were riding was badly damaged, John
Munholland is the only occupant of
the car who received severe cuts.
