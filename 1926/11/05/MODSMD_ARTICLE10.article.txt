# RETURN OF OVERDUE JOURNALISTIC BOOKS REQUESTED BY SMITH
## 
### 
Professor Everett W. Smith, head
of the Journalism Division, declared
yesterday that any Journalism books
that were taken out of the library last
year or that are found lying around
should be returned immediately to the
journalistic library.
This library is located in Room 3 of
the journalism division and contains
161 copies of bibliography, fiction, es
says, textbooks, histories, etc., all re
lating to newspaper work. It was
started by Professor Smith who do
nated his own private collection. Pro
fessor Brown and former classes have
added to the list.
