# Dr. G.W. Dowrie Speaks At Y.M.C.A. Frosh Dinner
## 
### 
Dr. George W. Dowrie, professor of
Finance in the Graduate School of
Business, was the principal speaker at
the first Y.M.C.A. freshman dinner
held last night in Encina Dining Hall.
Two piano selections and a two
round boxing exhibition completed
the program.
