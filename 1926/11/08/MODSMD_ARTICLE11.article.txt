# MRS. HELLER TO TELL OF AFRICAN TRAVELS
## 
### 
Mrs. Hilda Hempl Heller will talk
on the domestic problems arising from
"Traveling in Sasari," this afternoon,
at the regular monthly meeting of the
Faculty Women's Club, at 3 o'clock in
the Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. Heller recently returned from
a scientific expedition under the aus
pices of the Field Museum of Natural
History in Chicago, to the interior of
South Africa.
She will outline the various prob
lems of food obtainable in the country,
methods of transportation, and the
proper way to handle the native
"boys."
