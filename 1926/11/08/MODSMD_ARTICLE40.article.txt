# Y.M.C.A. Reading Room Has Ten State Dailies on File
## 
### 
Ten newspapers from towns in Cali
fornia are being received in the
Y.M.C.A. reading room daily and are
put on files for the use of Stanford
Students. The papers are: Los Angeles
Times, daily and Sunday; Oakland
Tribune, Santa Barbara Morning
Press, Long Beach Press-Telegram,
Fresno Republican, Modesto News-
Herald, San Diego Sun, Stockton Rec
ord, Palo Alto Times, and Stanford
Daily. The Y.M.C.A. will send for any
other home-town papers if requested.
