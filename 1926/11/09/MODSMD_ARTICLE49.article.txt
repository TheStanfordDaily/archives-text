# RIFLE CLUB MEETS TO ARRANGE MATCHES
## 
### 
] Every one, including both men and
| women, who is interested in rifle
| shooting, is invited to attend a meet
ing of the Stanford Rifle Club to be
held this evening at 7:30 in Room 214.
Freshmen are particularly urged to
come, and varsity members will be
expected to be present, as several
matches must be arranged for.
Schedules for women's shooting will
be announced by Helen Carpenter,
manager of the women's section. Final
arrangements for the women's prac
tice hours have been made, but any
who have not been assigned a regular
time may still bo worked into the
present plan.
