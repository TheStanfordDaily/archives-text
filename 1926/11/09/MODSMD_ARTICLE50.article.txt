# U.S.C. Student Drowns In Los Angeles Harbor
## 
### 
William Cutler, student at Southern
California, was drowned Sunday eve
ning when the skiff in which he and a
companion, Clair Le Black, were sail
ing in Los Angeles Harbor, capsized.
They were within the breakwater,
stated Black, when the keel of the
skiff became loosened. While swim
ming to shore Cutler was seized with
cramps, and despite all his efforts to
aid his friend, Black was unable to
rescue him.
