# EUPHRONIA WILL HOLD TRYOUTS TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Euphronia debate,
to be held in the early part of Decem
ber, will take place tomorrow night at
7:45 in the lobby of the Old Union,
it was announced yesterday by Har
old W. Peterson, club president.
Although the subject of the Decem
ber debate has not been definitely de
cided upon the tryout subject will be,
"Rosolved, That the world has more
to hope for than to fear from science."
Tryoutee speeches will be three min
utes in length with a five-minute re
buttal. The definite subject of the
December debate will be announced
later. The speakers may take either
side in the tryout.
