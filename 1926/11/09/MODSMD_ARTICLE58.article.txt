# Dick Crandall, '24, Married In South, Visits Campus
## 
### 
Dick Crandall, '24, and Irella Fly,
of San Diego, were married on Novem
ber 3 in Los Angeles. Crandall is a
member of Phi Kappa Sigma. Mrs.
Crandall is a Chi Omega from the
University of Oregon. They stopped
