# San Jose Alumni to Give Banquet for Captain Swan
## 
### 
It was announced yesterday by
Thomas H. Coughran, football man
ager, that Pop Warner and the coach
ing staff, Captain Fred Swan, and Dick
| Hyland will attend a banquet tonight
given in their honor by the San Jose
Alumni. Coughran also stated that he
was attempting to schedule a pre
liminary game between the Stanford
Grays and the Antlers to take place
Saturday at 12:30 o'clock. No word of
acceptance has been received from
the Antlers as yet.
