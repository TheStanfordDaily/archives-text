# Alice Russell Is Elected Commuters Club Secretary
## 
### 
Alice Russell was elected secretary
of the Commuters Club at the regular
weekly meeting yesterday noon in the
Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
All women commuting to the Uni
versity are invited to come to the
meeings on Tuesday noons, in the
Clubhouse, and bring their lunches.
Various social "get-togethers" are be
ing planned for the quarter.
