# Roos Brothers Schedule Big Game Rooters' Train
## 
### 
Rooters who desire rapid transporta
tion to Berkeley for the Big Game
will have an opportunity to ride in a
rooters' special train scheduled by
Roos Brothers for Stanford students.
The train will leave Palo Alto at 11
| o'clock Saturday and will transfer at
Redwood City at 11:40 o'clock. From
there it will, by special concession of
the railroad company, go direct to
Berkeley, stopping within walking dis
tance of the campus.
The train will have a diner, an ob
servation car, and all other standard
equipment. An orchestra will provide
entertainment for the passengers.
The fare has been set at $1.70 round
trip if the return journey is made on
the same day.
