# New Oxford Regulations to Prohibit Bags, Plus-Fours
## 
### 
Oxford bass and plus-fours are now
forbidden in the dining halls in all
the colleges of Oxford University, Eng
land, states a press report from that
university.
The notices posted in the halls read:
"Gentlemen are reminded that they
are not allowed to dine in this hall
wearing what are vulgarly known as
plus-fours and Oxford bags, or any
other unseemly or improper article of
dress."
Servants are instructed not to give
food to undergraduates clad in the
blacklisted attire.
