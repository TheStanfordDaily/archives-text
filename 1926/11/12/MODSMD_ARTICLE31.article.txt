# CONCERT TICKETS
## 
### 
Tickets for the Mischa Elman
string quartet concert Tuesday
night, November IG, and the San
Francisco Symphony concert on
December 8, will be distributed
today.
If the weather permits, the
tickets will be given out from a
table in front of the Old Union;
if not, they can be had in the Old
Union.
