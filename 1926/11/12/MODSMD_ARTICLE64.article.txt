# DAVIS CALLS TRYOUTS FOR SOPHOMORE PLAY
## 
### 
Postponed because of the failure of
the manuscripts to arrive from New
York, the tryouts for the Sophomore
I'lay, to be produced December 3 in
connection with the autumn quarter
Sophomore Week, will be held today
from 3 to 5, and from 7 to 9 o'clock
in the Assembly Hall. The manuscripts
have been sent by air-mail and are
definitely expected to arrive this morn
ing, according to Gordon Davis, direc
tor of dramatics.
The production for the Sophomore
Week has not been definitely an
nounced, although Davis has declared
it to be a recent Broadway comedy.
The play is the only Dramatic Council
production of the year which is limited
to students with sophomore standing.
