# Committee Heads Chosen For A.W.S. Dance Drama
## 
### 
Heads of the committees which will
take charge of the Dance Drama to be
given the latter part of winter quarter
were announced yesterday by Mrs.
Ruth Calvin, director of Roble Gymna-
sium.
Madeline Frick will be chairman of
the Dance Committee. Other members
are: Velma Randall, Olga Spirito, and
Margaret Olsen. This committee will
work on the manuscript which has
been selected for the drama, and will
organize the various dances.
Anne Russell and Helen Stanford
are to take charge of the program
committee, Frances Stanford, music,
llelene von Damm, costumes, Marga
ret Olsen, publicity, and Ruth Minskey,
properties.
