# Dean Kirkwood Speaks At Freshman Assembly
## 
### 
Dean Marion R. Kirkwood of the
Law School spoke at the fourth
Y.M.C.A. freshman assembly in the
lobby of Eneina last, night. He dis
cussed the choice of a life work and
the means afforded at Stanford for
helping students to make the choice.
Dean Kirkwood is a member of the
vocational guidance committee and
has had wide experience in this line.
The Y.M.C.A. assemblies are de
signed to give opportunity for students
to meet faculty men informally out
side the classroom.
