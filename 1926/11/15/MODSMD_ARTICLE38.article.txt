# Second Tryouts for Light Opera Concert Scheduled
## 
### 
Further tryouts for the light opera
to be given next quarter will be held
this week. Women who wish to try
out will report tomorrow night be
tween 7 and 8 o'clock in Room 84 of
the Inner Quad, and second tryouts for
men will be announced later.
About thirty-six men and women
passed the first tryouts held last Fri
day and their names will be announced
later together with the dates for fur
ther meetings.
