# Student Life Subject of Dean Yost's Talk Tuesday
## 
### 
"Student Life at Stanford" will be
the subject of a talk by Dean Mary
Yost, to be given at the Stanford
Mothers Club meeting at 3 o'clock to
morrow afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Hostesses for the afternoon will be
Mrs. William Cranston, Mrs. William
Proctor, Mrs. Charles Gray, Mrs. Wil
liam Proctor, Mrs. J. M. Denny, Mrs.
M. V. Adams, Mrs. H. A. Dutton, Mrs.
A. E. Fowle, and Mrs. Charles Spinks.
