# STUDENT BASEBALL UMPIRES WANTED AT CONVALESCENT HOME
## 
### 
"Have a heart and help a child
to find the home plate!"
This was the appeal issued yes
terday by Mrs. Olive Dawson,
educational director of the Stan
ford Convalescent Home. The
playground director solicits the
help of student baseball players
in umpiring and playing ball with
twenty or more children of the
home who are able to engage in
the national pastime.
The playground at the Home is
open from 2:30 to 4 o'clock each
afternoon of the week, and all
who are willing to assist in or
ganizing indoor baseball nines
are asked to report at the Home
any day at that time.
