# Stanford Oldest Rival Of Washington Huskies On Football Gridiron
## 
### 
Stanford is the oldest rival of the
Washington Huskies. The first college
football team ever played by an eleven
from the University of Washington
was against Stanford University De
cember 29, 1893, in Seattle, on the old
West Seattle grounds.
This was also the first year Wash
ington was able to procure a real
schedule, playing five games in all.
That first game the Cards won by a
40 to 0 score.
Then for twenty-seven years the two
teams did not meet on the gridiron.
The second game was played in 1920,
with Stanford taking the game with
a field goal.
