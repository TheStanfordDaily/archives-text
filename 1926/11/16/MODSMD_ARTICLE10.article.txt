# Football Squad, Coaches Will Be Theatre's Guests
## 
### 
Tonight will be Stanford night at
the New Stanford theatre, according
to an announcement of Harry C. Sei
pel, manager. Richard Dix will appear
in "The Quarterback," and news reels
of the Trojan-Card battle of two weeks
age and of the Big Game last year will
be shown.
A section has been set aside for
the varsity squad and the coaches,
who will be guests of the management
for the evening.
