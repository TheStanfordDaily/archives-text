# Freshman Women Aided in Activities by A.W.S., W.A.A.
## 
### 
Freshman women (his year are be
ing organized under the combined
jurisdiction of the A.W.S., the
Y.W.C.A., nnd W.A.A., in order to fur
ther unity in the class. The girls have
been urged to attend the organ re
citals, concerts, Tuesday night lec
tures, and Dr. Jordan's lectures.
A program for the whole year has
been mapped out and includes a picnic
which is planned for December 2. The
women will take their supper out in
the hills, where stunts and a bonfire
will be chief attractions.
