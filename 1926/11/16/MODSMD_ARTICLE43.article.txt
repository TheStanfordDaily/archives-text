# Allen Will Meet Women Interested in Light Opera
## 
### 
Women interested in the proposed pro
duction of a light opera, to be
produced under the auspices of the Dra
matic Council and under the combined
direction of Warren D. Allen and Gor
don Davis, are asked to meet Allen today
from 5 to 6 o'clock or from 7 to 8 o'clock
in Room 84.
