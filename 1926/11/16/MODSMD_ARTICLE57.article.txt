# Junior Opera Committee Chosen; Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Junior Opera committee will meet
at 7 o'clock tomorrow evening in Room
214. There will be a discussion of the
policy of the opera this year, and an
outline of plans for getting the work
under way.
The members of the committee are as
follows: Marian Farr, Sarah Margaret
Burns, Marion McCardle, William Pabst,
Murray Draper, Carl E. Green, Henry
Silverman, Joe Smith, Herbert Klein,
Edward White, Frank Baker, Charles
Mynard, and Philip Cox.
