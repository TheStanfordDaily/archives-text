# U.S.C.-Stanford Student Presidents Will Deliver Annual Exchange Talk
## 
###

the two student bodies, University of
Southern California and Stanford, will
he a yearly affair during the week pre
ceding the Card-Trojan game, it was
decided last night by the Ex Committee.

versity of Southern California to ex
change student body presidents to address
student assemblies during the week pre
ceding the Stanford-Trojan football game
each year be acccpted.

tion board on the Prohibition poll be
approved.

ray Draper and John Duniway to va
cancies on the Rally Committee be ap
