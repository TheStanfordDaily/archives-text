# SECOND PERFORMANCE OF "LILIOM" OFFERED
## 
### 
"Liliom," after being well-received at
its initial performance last Saturday, will
he presented again tonight in the Wom
en's Clubhouse in Palo Alto at 8:15
o'clock under the auspices of the Pen
dragon Players. Six Stanford students
are members of the cast, which will give
an intelligent interpretation of Franz
Molner's seven scenes of the life of the
main character, Liliom. These are Lynn
Bramkamp, Kenneth McDougall, Robert
Sears, David Stollery, Reidar Torjussen,
and Garth Winslow.
