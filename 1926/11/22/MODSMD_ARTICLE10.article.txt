# Stanford's Band First in U.S. to Use Glockenspiel
## 
### 
The Stanford Band claims the dis
tinction of being the first musical or
ganization in America to use the
Glockenspiel, lyre-sliaped instrument
carried at its head in the Ilig Game,
Saturday.
Frank P. Hornkohl played the unique
piece, which is of the percussion type,
resembling orchestra bells.
