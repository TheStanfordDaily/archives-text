# SUB-COMMITTEES FOR JUNIOR OPERA NAMED
## 
### 
To facilitate the handling of the
Junior Opera work on hand, the 1929
committee has been divided into three
sub-committees: manuscript, direction,
and music.
The manuscript committee is com
posed of It. Frank Baker, chairman,
Marian McCardle, Murray Draper, Carl
E. Green, Wesly I'. Cox. William D.
Pabst was named chairman of the di
rection committee and the following
as members: Marian Farr, Sara Mar
garet Burns, Henry E. Silverman, Jo
seph O. Smith. The music committee
includes Herbert A. Klein, chairman,
Katherine Kanim, Charles E. Mynard,
Edward A. White.
