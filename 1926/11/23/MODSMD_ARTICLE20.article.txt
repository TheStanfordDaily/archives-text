# Scabbard and Blade Elects Officers; Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
At a meeting of Scabbard and Blade,
national military fraternity, held last
week at Military Headquarters, the
following officers were elected: C. O.
Thompson, captain; Glenn H. Stock
hridge, first lieutenant; Alfred Virag,
second lieutenant; and Dixon Chub
buck, first sergeant.
The second meeting of the organiza
j (ion will be held tomorrow at 4:30 p.m.
at Military Headquarters. All members
j are urged to be present.
