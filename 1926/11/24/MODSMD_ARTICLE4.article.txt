# Meyberg Asks Juniors to Report for Basketball
## 
### 
All juniors are requested by Edwin
J. Meyberg, class basketball manager,
to turn out for basketball practice
tomorrow afternoon In Encina gym
at 4 o'clock. Wry few men have come
out as yet, and with the first game
scheduled for Tuesday, more material
is needed, said Meyberg.
