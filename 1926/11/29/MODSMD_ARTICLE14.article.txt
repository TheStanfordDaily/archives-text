# Huskies Plan Rifle Team For Naval R.O.T.C. Unit
## 
### 
The Naval R.0.T.C., established at
the University of Washington this
year, will have a rifle team to compete
with the regular Washington and
Army R.O.T.C. teams. Thirty-five out
of sixty men enrolled in the unit ex
pressed their intention of trying out
for the team. The unit is composed of
freshmen men only.
