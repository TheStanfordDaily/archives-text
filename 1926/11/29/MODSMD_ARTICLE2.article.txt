# Nevers Scores All Points For Duluth Team's Victory
## 
### 
Scoring all of the points for his
team, Ernie Nevers. former Stanford
and AU-American fullback, led his Du
luth Eskimos to a 1(5-0 victory over
the Hartford team Saturday.
The former Cardinal ace kicked
three field goals, plunged over for a
touchdown, and successfully made the
try for point. Besides this, he did the
punting, threw all complete passes,
and did most of the defense work.
