# Basketball Attendance at Big Ten Games Increases
## 
### 
Basketball attendance in the Big
Ten Conference has increased to such
an extent that several of the colleges
have found it necessary to raise the
seating capacity of their gymnasiums,
and in some cases to construct field
houses for handling the increasing de
mand for tickets.
Ohio State has been having crowds
of 13,000 at all its big games and Illi
nois claims audiences of 8500. lowa
University is completing a new field
house that will accommodate 15,000
people and Northwestern is doubling
the present seating capacity.
