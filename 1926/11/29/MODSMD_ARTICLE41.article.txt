# SOLOMON MARRIES
## 
### 
Fred Solomon, '25, former varsity
quarterback and now assistant foot
hall coach at Stockton high school,
was married to Violet Knowles, '26, of
Los Angeles, on November 20t, immedi
ately following the Big Game. The
ceremony took place at the home of
Rev. Cumming.s in San Francisco. Mr.
and Mrs. Solomon will make their
home in Stockton.
