# FRESHMAN-1929 CAGE TILT SCHEDULED TODAY
## 
### 
Interclass basketball starts today
when the freshman team meets the
sophomores at 4:15 o'clock in Encina
gymnasium, announces Charles W.
Davis, director of Intramural athletics.
The junior-senior contest will he
played tomorrow at the same time.
Each class is represented by two
teams, the second-team game starting
at 4:15 o'clock and continuing for two
fifteen-minute periods. The first-team
contest follows, the playing periods
being the same.
All students, with the exception of
varsity men, are eligible, Davis states.
