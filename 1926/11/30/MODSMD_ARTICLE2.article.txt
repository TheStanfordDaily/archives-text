# Undergraduate Problems Discussed at Convention
## 
### 
The annual conclave of college fra
ternity men, which began November
2fi and was held under the auspices
of the Interfraternity Conference at
the Hotel Pennsylvania, New York
City, consisted in the main of a two
day discussion of problems concerning
undergraduate life. More than two
hundred officials of nearly all the so
cieties of the United States and Can
ada were present at this meeting,
which is the eighteenth in the history
of the Conference.
