# Sigma Delta Chi to Hear Report on Convention
## 
### 
_______
Sigma Delta Chi, men's professional ;
journalistic fraternity, will meet for
dinner at the Alpha Kappa Lambda
house at 6 o'clock tonight.
Wallace W. Knox, president of the
Stanford chapter, will give a report,
on the recent national convention of
the fraternity which he attended as a :
delegate. Other important business ;
will be transacted. All members are
requested to be present.
