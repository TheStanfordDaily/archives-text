# League of Nations Will Be Dr. Jordan's Subject
## 
### 
"Petrich find the League of Nations"
will be the subject of Dr. Jordan's
lecture at his Thursday Evening Con
ference to be held tomorrow at 7:30
o'clock at his home.
Dr. Jordan will discuss the work of
the League, and the influence that it
wields in world affairs. He will also
give his personal reminiscences of the
little town of Petrich, which Dr. Jor
dan considers to have had a decided
influence on the work of the League.
