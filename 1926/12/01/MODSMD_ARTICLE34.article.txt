# Zoology Club Will Meet With Dr. Martin Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Zoology Club will
meet at the home of Dr. E. G. Martin,
1 Cabrillo, on the hill, tonight.
The business meeting starts at 7:30,
followed by the regular meeting at 8.
Mrs. Helen Heller, recently returned
from Africa, will continue her talk on
mammal collecting in the tropics.
