# Author of Stanford Song Will Speak to Teachers
## 
### 
Geoffrey F. Morgan, '10, well known
as author of "Sons of the Stanford
Red," will be one of the chief speakers
at the annual meeting of the State
Teachers Association in San Francisco
next month. He will deliver a series of
four addresses.
Morgan has been engaged in educa
tional work for a number of years
since graduating. He lias taught in
California, Ohio, and New York. Dur
ing the past five years he has been
doing lecture work, speamng to high
schools, colleges, clubs, and civic or
ganizations. He now lives in Santa
Monica.
