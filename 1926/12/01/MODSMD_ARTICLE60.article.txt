# Minnesota Journalism Head Organizes Tour of Europe
## 
### 
Professor Marion, head of the De
partment of Journalism at the Univer
sity of Minnesota, is organizing a tour
of Europe for journalists, college stu
dents of journalism, and journalism
teachers. His purpose is to acquaint
students of journalism with some of
the more important European news
centers and to give them a better con
ception of European interests and
points of view, so that they will be able
to interpret and edit European affairs
more intelligently.
