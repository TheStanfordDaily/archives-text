# Four Papers Will Be Read Before Geological Club
## 
### 
A paper on "The Recognition of
Fault Scarps" by Eliot Blackwelder,
professor of geology at Stanford, is
one of four to be presented at the
meeting of the Le Conte Geological
Club, association of bay region geolo
gists, Saturday at 3:30 p.m. in the
Geology building. Other papers will be
given by Warren J. Mead, William F.
Barbat, and Bruce L. Clark. After the
program will be a dinner at the Stan
ford Union.
