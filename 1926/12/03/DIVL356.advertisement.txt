Going Home For The Holidays
Reduced Fares
—to Pacific Coast Points
After exams, get aboard a train for home! A
roomy, comfortable seat. Your friends nearby,
also homeward-bound. A swift, joyous journey.
And at low cost.
Roundtrip fares specially reduced for student
travel. Tickets on sale Dec. 8 to Dec. 22, with re
turn limit Jan. 11,1927.
$22.75 to Los Angeles and back. Fares to other
points atsimilar reductions. 9 trains in day and night
service between San Francisco and Los Angeles.
