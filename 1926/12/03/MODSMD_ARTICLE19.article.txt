# Women's Glee Club Will Sing at Vesper Services
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Glee Club
will sing at the Christmas vesper ser
vices, which is to be held under the
direction of the Y.W.C.A. on the after
noon of Sunday, December 12, in the
Women's Clubhouse. The club sang at
these services last year and will sing
the same sort of program consisting
of Christmas music and carols.
Starting with the meeting held hist
night, the club will hereafter meet
every Wednesday evening at 7:30
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
