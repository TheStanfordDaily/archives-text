# Graduate Students, Wives Invited by Stanford Dames
## 
### 
All graduate students and their
wives are invited to the party the
Stanford Dames Club is Riving at 8:15
o'clock tonight in the Women's Club
house in the New Union. Dancing,
bridge, and other card games will con
stitute the evening's entertainment.
