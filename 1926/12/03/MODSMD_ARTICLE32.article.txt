# San Francisco Rabbi Will Conduct Chapel Services
## 
### 
Rabbi Louis J. Newman, of the Tem
ple Emanu-El, San Francisco, will con
duct the regular services in the Me
morial Church Sunday. The Temple
service will be used, the music being
interpreted by Cantor Reuben R.
Rinder and the University Choir.
The organ recital will be held at 4
o'clock Sunday afternoon.
