# PROFESSOR CUBBERLEY RETURNS FROM EAST
## 
### 
Professor Ellwood P. Cubberley,
head of the Stanford School of Edu
cation, recently returned from three
months' travel in the East where he
lectured before many of the colleges.
Tie gave twelve lectures on educa
tion to the State Teachers' Associa
tion meting in Michigan, and spoke at
a teachers' college in Itaca, N.Y.
Cubberley also talked before the
teaching department of Columbia Uni
versity, and the teachers' college at
Kalamazoo, Michigan.
Seven weeks of his time was spent,
on an automobile trip through New
York, New England. Eastern Canada.
Pennsylvania, Maryland, Ohio, Indi
ana. and Michigan.
