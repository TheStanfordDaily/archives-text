# Phi Sigma Alpha to Hear Lutz Lecture on Poland
## 
### 
Post-war conditions in Poland will
be discussed by Professor Harley L.
Lutz, of the Economics department,
before a luncheon of Phi Sigma Alpha,
honorary political science society, in
the alcove room of the Stanford Union
at 12:15 today. Having assisted in the
reorganization of finances of that
country, he is an authority on its pres
ent condition. All members of the
fraternity are asked to attend today's
meeting.
