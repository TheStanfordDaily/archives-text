# SCIENTIFIC RESEARCH FELLOWSHIPS OPEN
## 
### 
The President's office received no
tice on Saturday from the Imperial
College of Science and Technology,
South Kensington, London, that three
Beit Fellowships for scientific re
search will be awarded at the four
teenth election of fellows which will
take place on or about July 15th, 1927.
Applications for these fellowships
must be received by April 15), 1927.
Further information and application
blanks may be obtained, by letter only,
from the Rector, Imperial College,
South Kensington, London, S.W.
