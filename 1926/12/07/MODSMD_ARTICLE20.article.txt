# Fifty Teams Enrolled in Intramural Cage Schedule
## 
### 
The intramural basketball schedule
for next quarter will be drawn up to
morrow night by the Intramural com
mittee, C. W. Davis announced last
night. Fifty teams are now signed up
for the coming tourney. A squad wish
ing to onter must notify Davis before
the meeting tomorrow night.
