# Deadline Set for Filing Low Scholarship Excuses
## 
### 
Students whose work has been in
terrupted during the present quarter
by illness or other causes, and who
wish to have the special circumstances
taken into consideration by the Com
mittee on Scholarship, may file a state
ment with Mrs. Margaret Huston, sec
retary of the committee, any morn
ing between 9 and 12, in the Regis
trar's office. Excuses submitted later
than December 10th cannot be assured
of consideration before action is taken
on the end-quarter reports, the com
mittee announces.
Requests for allowance on the
ground of illness must be accompa
nied, it is stated, by the special physi
cian's certificate furnished by the com
mittee.
