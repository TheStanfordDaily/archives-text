# Eight Hostesses Selected For Wednesday Alumni Tea
## 
### 
Hostesses who will preside at the
Resident Alumnae Association Tea
Wednesday afternoon at 3 o'clock in
the Stanford Women's Clubhouse are:
Mrs. John Noble Blair, Mrs. B. O.
Foster, Mrs. Henry David Gray, Mrs.
George Hempl, Mrs. C. S. Jones, Mrs.
Anne E. Peck, Mrs. George J. Pierce,
and Mrs. Jerome Thomas.
