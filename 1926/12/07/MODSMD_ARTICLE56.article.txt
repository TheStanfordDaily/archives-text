# Warren D. Allen to Play Parts of "The Messiah"
## 
### 
Two portions of Handel's great ora
torio, "The Messiah," will be played
by Warren D. Allen as opening,num
bers of his organ recital at 4:15 o'clock
this afternoon in the Memorial Church.
Other selections to be given are:
"Resonet in Laudibus," Karg-Elert;
"Shepherds in the Fields," Otto Mail
ing; "Christmas," Gaston Dethier.
