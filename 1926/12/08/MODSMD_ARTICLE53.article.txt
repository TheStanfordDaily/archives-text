# War Memorial Erected At Durban, South Africa
## 
### 
"Tell it to the Generation Following"
is the inscription on a brightly colored
war memorial recently erected in Dur
ban, South Africa, bearing a great
bronze candelabra with a light which
is always to be kept burning. On the
monument are found the names of
1000 Durban soldiers who died in the
war.
The monument is of granite, fifty-six
feet high, costing about $125,000.
Sculptured figures relating to the war
occupy a space twenty-one by eleven
feet on the side and are colored vividly
in white, blue, yellow, orange, green,
and gold.
