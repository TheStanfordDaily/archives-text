# Professor Buckingham to Go East for Conference
## 
### 
Miss E. L. Buckingham, of the Eng
lish department, will leave December
24 for tho meeting of the National As
sociation of Teachers of Speech, to
be held in Chicago. Miss Buckingham,
who is vice-president of the organiza
tion, will read a paper before the
meeting on "Type-Casting in Relation
to Development Value In Amateur
Dramatics."
