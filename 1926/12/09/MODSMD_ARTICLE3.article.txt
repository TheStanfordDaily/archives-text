# Women Hockey Stars to Be Announced at Banquet
## 
### 
Women all-star hockey selections
will be announced at the hockey ban
quet tonight at 0 o'clock in the Union.
The all-star team will then be called
on to givo an exhibition of team-work
in a stunt. Class teams will also fur
nish part of the program.
Tickets for the banquet may still
bo obtained today from the class team
captains or from Helen Lease.
