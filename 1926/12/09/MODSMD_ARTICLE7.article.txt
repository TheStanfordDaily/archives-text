# Grade Envelopes on Sale In Administration Building
## 
### 
All students desirous of learning
their grades before registration day
of next quarter must leave a stamped,
addressed envelope at the registrar's
window in the administration building.
For those who wish, stamped envelopes I
will be supplied at the window at a
charge of five cents apiece, the bal- \
ance over cost being given to the
Convalescent Home.
