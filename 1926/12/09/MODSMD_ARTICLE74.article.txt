# Memorial Church to Hold Christmas Service Sunday
## 
### 
Christmas services will be held in
the Stanford Memorial Church, Sun
day, December 12, at 11 o'clock. The
Hormon will be preached by Rev. D.
Charles Gardner and will be followed
l)y a short musical program.
At 4 o'clock an organ recital by
Warren D. Allen, the University or
ganist, will be given.
