# W.W. STILSON DEAD AFTER BRIEF ILLNESS
## 
### 
William W. Stilson, '29, succumbed
to an attack of double pneumonia in
the St. Francis hospital in San Fran
cisco on December 10. Stilson was a
member of the Phi Gamma Delta fra
ternity and was a member of last
year's freshman track squad.
Stilson became ill during the
Thanksgiving holidays and was at once
taken to the hospital for medical at
tention.
