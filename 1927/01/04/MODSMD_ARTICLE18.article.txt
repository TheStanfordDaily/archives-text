# Registration Total for Winter Quarter Exceeds 1926 Figures by Seventy
## 
### 
Figures at the registrar's office for the
first day of winter quarter registration
yesterday show a total of 3024 students
registering in the University, of which
number 2931 have completed the process
of registration. This total represents an
increase of 70 over the registration the
first day of last winter quarter, when only
2954 registered. Ninety-three new stu
dents entered Stanford yesterday, as com
pared with 88 last year at this time.
