# Appointment Office Asks Return of Library Books
## 
### 
Books borrowed from the Appoint
ment Office library last quarter are
now due and should be returned imme
diately, in the announcement made by
Mrs. Snell, employment bureau secre
tary.
Self-supporting students entering
this quarter are invited to make use of
the free library maintained at the ap
pointment office, which consists of
over a hundred volumes of text and
reference books.
