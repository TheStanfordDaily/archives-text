# NESTOR OULIE KILLED IN AUTOMOBILE WRECK
## 
### 
Nestor Oulie, a graduate student in the
Stanford Medical School, was killed in
an automobile collision nineteen miles
south of Bakersfield last Sunday morn
ing. His brother, Bernhard Oulie, who
was with him, was slightly injured.
The parents, Mr. and Mrs. C. N. Oulie,
live at 1.341 Twenty-fourth street, Los
Angeles. _
