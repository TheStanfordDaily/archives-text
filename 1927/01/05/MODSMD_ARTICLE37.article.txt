# SPECIAL ENGINEERING COURSE IS OFFERED
## 
### 
A course of lectures covering recent
developments in the design of concrete
mixtures, fineness modulus, water ce
ment ratio, use of admixtures and proper
curing, will be given under the auspices
of the department of Civil Engineering
by Mr. R. C. Buell of the Portland
Cement Association, in Room 521, on the
dates following:
Tuesday, January 4, 1 to 4 p. m.
Wednesday, January 5, 1 to 4 p. m.
Monday, January 10. 1 to 4 p. m.
Tuesday, January 11, 1 to 4 p. m.
Wednesday, January 12, 1 to 4 p. m.
Any who may be interested are invited
to attend.
