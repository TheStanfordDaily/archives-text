# DR. AND MRS. WILBUR HOLD INFORMAL AT HOME THIS EVENING
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Wilbur will be at home
i informally Thursday evening to stu
dents, faculty, and friends, contrary to
the announcement of yesterday's Daily
that Wednesday evening was the cor
rect time.
This reception is for the purpose of
greeting the faculty and students, and
making newcomers welcome to the
campus, and is held the beginning of
every quarter at the President's home.
Dr. and Mrs. Wilbur are at home
after a long absence from the campus,
during which time they visited the
Orient.
