# STANFORD DEBATERS RECEIVE DECISION IN CONTEST AT BOWDOIN
## 
### 
Stanford's debating team, which is
now touring the East, won a victory
over Bowdoin College by an audience
vote of 92 to 69. The team composed of
Fred Cobellack and Harry Turkel up
held the affirmative of the question,
"Resolved, That the world has more to
fear than to hope from science."
The tour will also include Yale, Har
vard, Princeton, the University of De
troit, the University of Chicago, Bates
College, Maine.
