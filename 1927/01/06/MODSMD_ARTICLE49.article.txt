# Professor Harley L. Lutz Heads Tax Association
## 
### 
Professor Harley L. Lutz of the Eco
nomics department, who was a dele
gate from the University to the Na
tional Tax Conference hold in Phila
delphia the week of November 15, has
been elected vice-president of the Na
tional Tax Association.
Professor Lutz, who is recognized as
one of the leading tax authorities in
the country, was selected on the com
mission of American tax and economic
experts which recently made a study
of Polish economic conditions, with a
view of putting that country on a firm
financial basis. He made a similar
study of Peru a year ago at the re
quest of that government.
