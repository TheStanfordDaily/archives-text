January
Clearance
Sale—-
A busy year, topped by Christmas, stocks incomplete
in size or color. Some slightly ruffled from handling.
And so we have gathered them together and now offer
them to you at greatly lowered prices for this January
sale. There are still many months of service in a Winter
coat several season's wear in a new silk frock—all of
excellent quality at lowered prices. You will start the
new year with considerable savings if you avail yourself
of these bargains.
Now is the time to buy the coat or dress which
you felt was a luxury—the undergarments for
the year—the decorative touch for your home
—at much below their regular prices. In most
cases, the high quality remains unchanged.
Some are a trifle mussed from handling, but all
are capable of prolonged service, and are wise
purchases.
During our January clearance sale it is our rule to
allow a ten per cent (10 r /o) discount on everything
not already reduced. This assures you of a saving on
everything you buy. Many of our patrons supply their
home and personal needs for months ahead during
these sales.
Another of the big worth-while features of
this event is that our entire stock of silk,
wool, and cotton remnants are put at your
disposal at one-half their regular price.
