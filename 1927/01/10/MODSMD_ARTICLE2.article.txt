# IRENE HARDY POETRY PRIZE OFFERED AGAIN BY WRITER'S FRIENDS
## 
### 
The Trcnc Hardy Poetry Prize offered
biennially for the best poem by an un
dergraduate student will be awarded in
the spring quarter of this year.
The fifty dollar prize is drawn from
a fund established by friends in mem
ory of Irene Hardy, a former member
of the Stanford faculty and the author
of several volumes of verse. The com
petition, which is conducted under the
supervision of the English department,
is open to all undergraduates. Infor
mation concerning the form of manu
scripts and the date for the closing of
the contest will be found posted on the
English department bulletin board.
