# GIRL SCOUT LEADER TO SPEAK ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
Agnos Wavman, director of physical
education at Columbia University, will
speak tomorrow afternoon before the
Girl Scout Training Class on the
"Scouts' Health Program." The lecture
will be given in Room 56 of the Educa
tion building and is open to the public.
Miss Wayman will also talk tomorrow
evening at 7:30 o'clock in the Palo
Alto Girl Scout House.
Miss Wayman, who is connected
with the national Scout headquarters
in New York, is a Stanford graduate
and is at present engaged in organiz
ing the Girl Scouts of California.
