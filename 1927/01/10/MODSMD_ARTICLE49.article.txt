# MEMBER OF '95 CLASS DIES AT SAN MATEO
## 
### 
John Mellgren Lewis, a member of
the first graduating class of Stanford,
that of 1895, passed away at his home
in San Mateo on January 4. He had
long been an extensive traveler, and at
one time practiced law in the Philippines.
With his brother, he had also made
large investments in California property.
