# University of Texas May Return to Semester Plan
## 
### 
Beginning with the long session of
1927, courses in the University of
Texas will probably be conducted un
der the semester system. Adaptation
of the system was urged at the south
ern school on the basis that it would
prove of great benefit to high school
students who are graduated in Febru
ary. It is also claimed that the new
system is in use in most of the larger
universities at this time.
