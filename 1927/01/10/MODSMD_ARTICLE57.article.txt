# CASTING COMPLETED FOR A.W.S. PLAYLET
## 
### 
Casting has been completed of the
playlet to be given on the A.W.S. pro
gram Wednesday night from seven to
nine in the Women's clubhouse.
Parts will be taken by Catherine Co
man as the heroine, Frances Shoup as the
villain, Maxine Cushing as the hero, and
Marion Strong, Martha McDowell, Ann
Wheeler, and Benetta Merner as chil
dren. Elizabeth Ann Lynch is reader
and director.
The playlet is to be a costume panto
mime of Colonial days, containing ele
ments of dark-dyed villainy, romance,
and Paris fashions, and hinging on the
heroine's success at "vamping" the vil
lain.
