# MUSICAL PARIS OF PRESENT DAY TOPIC FOR TUESDAY TALK
## 
### 
"Present Day Musical Paris" will bo
the subject of a. talk to be given by
Miss Jeanne de Mare tomorrow eve
ning at 7:30 o'clock in the Little
Theatre. The lecturer will illustrate
her talk on the piano.
Born and brought up in Paris, Miss
de Mare knows personally most of the
present-day composers, us well as
contemporary authors and painters,
who keep her well-informed regarding
their work. This opportunity makes it
possible for her to give her lectures
a vivid personal touch.
