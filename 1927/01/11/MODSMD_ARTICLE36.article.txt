# First-Year Men to Hold Tryouts for Golf Squad
## 
### 
A meeting of all freshmen interested
in trying out for the yearling golf
team will be held in the Eneina lobby
at 7 o'clock tomorrow evening.
The manager of the golf team and
C. W. Davis, director of minor sports,
are to be present at that time, and
will announce the plans for competi
tion, as well as for the team match
which is planned for Saturday. It is
important that all freshmen that have
any aspiration to make the team
should be at the meeting.
