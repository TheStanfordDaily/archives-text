# Y.W.C.A. COUNCILS HOLD SUPPER MEETING
## 
### 
The Senior Cabinet and the Advi
sory Board of the Y.W.C.A. will meet
tonight at 6 o'clock for dinner at the
home of Mrs. Fred Wickett in Palo
Alto, to hear the reports of Margaret
Owen and May Hardy concerning
their trip to the student conference
at Milwaukee.
The Advisory Board will meet first
as a body in order to hear the reports
of the house party which was ar
ranged for last week-end by members
of the Y.M.C.A. and the Y.W.C.A. in
the Santa Cruz mountains. Later both
organizations are to meet for dinner.
