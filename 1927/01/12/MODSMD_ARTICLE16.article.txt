# FELLOWSHIPS OFFERED FOR STUDY IN FRANCE
## 
### 
Announcement has been made of a
limited number of fellowships for ad
vanced study in France for the year
1927-28. Each will carry a stipend of
$1200 and will be tenable for one year,
with the possibility of renewal for a
Recond year if circumstances are
favorable.
At present these fellowships are
open only to men. In the absence of
an absolute rule as to age, preference
will be given In all cases to candi
dates between the jircs of twenty and
thirty years. Further details may be
secured from the French department.
