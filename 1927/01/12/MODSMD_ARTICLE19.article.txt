# High School Boys Will Learn Home-Making Art
## 
### 
Thirteen boys have voluntarily en
rolled In the home economics depart
ment at Inglewood Union High School
and will undergo instruction in the art
of home-planning, the cooking of well
balanced meals, and the construction
of clothing. The course will be estab
lished as a permanent major for boys
if they continue to manifest sufficient
interest In the subjects offered.
