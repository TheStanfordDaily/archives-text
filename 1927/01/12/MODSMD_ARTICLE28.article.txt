# WOMEN'S GLEE CLUB MEETING POSTPONED
## 
### 
On account of the Conference Party
at the Women's Clubhouse, the Stan
ford Women's Glee Club will not hold
its regular weekly meeting tonight.
Beginning next week the club will
meet every Wednesday evening up
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse.
The organization needs new mem
bers in order to continue its existence.
No tryouts are required for member
ship and everyone interested is urged
to join.
