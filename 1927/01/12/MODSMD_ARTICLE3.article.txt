# Westinghouse Electric Representative Speaks To Engineering Majors
## 
### 
Engineering students will have an
opportunity today to acquaint them
selves with the work open to them
with the Westinghouse Electric and
Manufacturing Company when A. W.
Copley, a representative of that firm,
will address a meeting for such stu
dents at 10 o'clock this morning in
Room 268. He will outline possible
openings, and answer any questions on
the subject.
Copley will spend the afternoon at
the appointment office giving private
interviews to all interested students.
Mrs. Elizabeth 13. Snell, appointment
secretary, requests that all those
wishing such interviews make arrange
ments with her office, preferably be
fore the 10 o'clock lecture.
