# Portland Editor Speaker At Combined Club Meeting
## 
### 
Marshall N. Dana, associate editor
of the Oregon Journal, of Portland,
Oregon, and president of the Pacific
i Coast Advertising Clubs Association,
was the honor guest and speaker last
night at a combined dinner meeting
of the Stanford Advertising Club of
; Palo Alto and the Palo Alto Exchange
; Club, held in the Trellis room of the
I Cardinal Hotel. George A. Montell,
president of the Exchange Club,
shared the honors of chairmanship.
