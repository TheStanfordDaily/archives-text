# MISS OWEN TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Owen, who was one
of the Stanford delegates to the Na
tional Student Christian Conference at
Milwaukee during the holidays, has
been selected as the speaker at the
community luncheon of the chamber
of commerce today at the Russell
Cafeteria in Palo Alto. She will speak
of her experiences and observations at
this conference attended by college
men and women from throughout the
country.
