# Jordan to Speak at Meeting of Women's Club
## 
### 
The regular monthly meeting of the
Mothers Club of Stanford University
will be held Tuesday, January 18, at 3
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse. Dr.
David Starr Jordan will speak on
"The Founders of the University."
Guests are welcome.
The hostesses for the afternoon are:
Mrs. Alfred Davidson, Mrs. 11. M.
Fischer, Mrs. L. L. Burlingame, Mrs.
N. L. Vermilya, Mrs. A. J. Clyde, Mrs.
W. H. Kelly, Mrs. J. A. Frey, and Mrs.
W. H. Spotsford.
