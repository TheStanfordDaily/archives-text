# MARTIN WILL SPEAK ON TOUR IMPRESSIONS
## 
### 
"Fugitive Impressions of the Near
East and South America" is the sub
ject chosen by Professor Percy A.
Martin of the History department to
speak on in the Little Theatre at 7:30
o'clock Tuesday evening.
Professor Martin returned this week
trom a year and a half spent in travel
in Europe, South America, and the
Near East. He spent the last eight
months in South America, principally
in Brazil, Uruguay, Chili, and Peru.
While in Uruguay he visited for a
month with ex-President Brum on his
cattle ranch, which is one of the larg
est in the state.
