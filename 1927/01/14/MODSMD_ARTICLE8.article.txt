# Eectrical Engineers Will Convene Here Tomorrow
## 
### 
The conference of the student
branch of the American Institute of
Electrical Engineers will open here
tomorrow with nearly one hundred
delegates expected to be in attend
ance.
During the meeting, representatives
of California and Santa Clara will
read a number of papers relative to
this division of engineering. Other
features of the program will include
addresses by men prominent in this
field, among them being P. M. Down
ing. vice-president of the Eighth Geo
graphical District of the A.I.E E., and
Dr. Harris J. Ryan, professor of elec
trical engineering at Stanford.
The conference will be concluded by
a special demonstration in the Ryan
Laboratory for the convention guests,
preceding which a dinner will be held
in the Stanford Union.
