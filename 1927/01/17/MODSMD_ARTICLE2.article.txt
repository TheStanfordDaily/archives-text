# ADVISERS APPOINTED TO INTERFRATERNITY BOARD OF CONTROL
## 
### 
Professor William B. Owens, Delta
Chi, and Professor Edwin A. Cottrell,
Phi Delta Theta, were appointed by Pres
ident Wilbur last week to serve as ad
visory members on the Inter fraternity
Board of Control. Chosen from a list of
six faculty members submitted by the
Interfraternity Council, according to its
constitution, these two men will act in
this official capacity for the coming year.
The Interfraternity Council will meet
this evening at 9 o'clock in the Trophy
room of the Old Union. Both fraternity
delegates and house managers have been
asked to be present tonight.
