# BRANDSTEN EXPECTED IN PALO ALTO TODAY
## 
### 
Ernst Brandsten, swimming coach, is
expected to arrive in Palo Alto today
from Los Angeles, where he has spent
the last two weeks as trainer and pilot
for Norman Ross, former Stanford
swimmer, in the Catalina Channel race.
During Brandsten's absence swimming
and water polo practice has gone on every
night under the direction of Wally
O'Connor. He has been assisted by Cap
tain Williams of the water polo team and
Captain Stevenson of the swimming team.
