Announcement has been made of the
engagement of Theodore Carter Achilles,
'24, of Morgan Hill to Miss Louise Lord
Coleman of Los Gatos. Miss Coleman is
a graduate of the University of Califor
nia, where she is a member of Kappa
Kappa Gamma sorority.
Achilles is a member of Beta Theta Pi
fraternity. He is now taking a graduate
course in play writing at Yale.
* * *
Dorothy Dahler, '25, is now teaching at
the San Juan Junior High School.

engagement of Mary Elizabeth Bcedy,
'27, of San Francisco to Lorimer Har
rell, '25, of Merced. Miss Beedy is a
member of Kappa Alpha Theta sorority.
Harrell is a Sigma Alpha Epsilon. The
wedding will take place on February 8.
