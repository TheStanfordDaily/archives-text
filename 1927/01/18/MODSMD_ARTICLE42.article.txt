# HONORARY FRATERNITY TO INITIATE MEMBERS
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Pi, national honorary
Spanish fraternity, will meet at 8:30
o'clock tonight, al the Women's Club
house, for the initiation of new mem
bers. Associate Professor Alfred Coos
ter, of the Romanic Language Depart
ment, who is editor of "Kispania," and
who has just returned from a year of
travel in South America and Spain,
will be made an honorary member of
the society.
After the business meeting, a pro
gram will be presented by the new
members.
