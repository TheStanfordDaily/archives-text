# Zoology Professor Will Speak at Club Meeting
## 
### 
Professor J. O. Snyder of the
Zoology Department will speak on
"Experimental Study of Young Sal
mon in the Klamath River" at the
regular meeting of the Zoology Club
tomorrow night at the Women's Club
house. A special business session will
be held at 7:30 while the regular meet
ing will start at 8 o'clock.
