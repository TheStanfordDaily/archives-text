# Resident Alumnae to Give Bridge Tea at Clubhouse
## 
### 
Resident Alumnae will give a bridge
tea in the Women's Clubhouse at 3
o'clock this afternoon. Hostesses for
the occasion are: Mrs. Clay Miller,
Mrs. Frederick Fowler, Mrs. Harry
Haehl, Mrs. Wager Bradford, Mrs.
Reed Cherington, Mrs. D. Charles
Gardner, Mrs. J/ Pearce Mitchell, Mrs.
Frank W. Erlin, Mrs. Thomas Rolph,
and Miss Beth Thompson.
