# Senior Committee Meets To Consider Endowment
## 
### 
The Senior Endowment Committee
will meet at the Chi Omega house
today at 1 o'clock to discuss plans
for the proposed endowment insur
ance for the Class of 1927.
John Menzies is chairman of the
committee, the other members of
which are Kenneth Graham, Caroline
Moore, Theodore Baer, Monroe Eaton,
Robert Duncan, Margaret McOmie,
Stanlus Natcher, and Velma Randall.
