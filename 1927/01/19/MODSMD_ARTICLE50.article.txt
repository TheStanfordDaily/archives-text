# DEADLINE FOR QUAD PICTURES SET TODAY
## 
### 
Today is the final date on which
individual pictures for the 1928 Quad
may be taken on the campus, is the
announcement made by those in
charge of the photographic work of
the annual.
The Quad photographer will be in
the Trophy Room of the Old Union
between 9 and 11 o'clock in the morn
ing and from 12:30 to 5 in the after
noon, at which times those who are to
have pictures in the annual but who
have not yet had them taken may
report.
After today, individual pictures will
be taken only at the H. P. Fisher
studios in San Francisco.
