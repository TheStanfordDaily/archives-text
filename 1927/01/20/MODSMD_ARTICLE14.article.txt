# COLD VACCINE EFFECT OBSERVED ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
In an effort to prevent colds and to
eliminate man's susceptibility to this
threat, Dr. W. A. Barrows, medical
adviser to men, and Dr. Arthur Bloom
field, professor of medicine, have iu
noculated thirty men with an immune
serum prepared from human blood.
These, with thirty others who were
not innoculated, are being observed to
determine the efficacy of the vaccine.
As yet there is no report of the suc
cess of the experiment.
