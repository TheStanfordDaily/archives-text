# Food Research Director Elected Vice-President Of Statistical Society
## 
### 
Dr. Joseph Stancliffe Davis, Director
of the Stanford Food Research Insti
tute, has received notice of his elec
tion to the vice-presidency of the
American Statistical Association as its
annual business meeting held recently.
This association has a membership of
between 1500 and 2000 men interested
in various phases of statistics and
statistical methods.
