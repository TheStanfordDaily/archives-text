# ANNOUNCEMENTS
##

THURSDAY
10:00 A.M.—Student Body Assembly;
In the Assembly Hall. Address on
"Observations in the Orient," by
President Wilbur.
12:00 M. —There will be a meeting of
the Circle "S" society at the Dolta
Kappa Rpsilon house.
3:00 P.M. —Stanford Dames, in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
4:IF) P.M. —Organ Recital by Mr. Allen
in the Memorial Church.
7:15 P.M. —A.S.C.E. meeting in Room
276. Nomination of officers and other
important business.
7:30 P.M. —A business meeting of the
Zoology Club will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse. The regular
meeting will start at 8 o'clock.
7:30 P.M. —The Masonic Club degree
team will meet at the Masonic Tem
ple. All Masons are invited to attend.
7:30 P.M. —Thursday Evening Confer
ence, at the homo of Dr. David Starr
Jordan. Subject: "A Night in Balti
more."

7:30 P.M. —German Club will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse.
SATURDAY
7:30 P.M. —All ushers report to the
Assembly Hall, instead of the Has
ketball Pavilion, for the Glee Club
concert.
T' M —California-Stanford joint
glee club concert, in the basketball
pavilion.
SUNDAY
6:00 P.M. —The Stanford Episcopal
Club will meet in the parish house.
Professor Walter G. Beach will
speak.
