# STANFORD MEN WORK ON X-RAY AT CORNELL
## 
### 
Professor Perley A. Ross of the Phys
ics department ,and Dr. Warren Nichols,
who received his Ph.D. degree at Stan
ford last June, are now at Cornell Uni
versity where, for the next year, they
will work with Professor Richtmeyer on
problems in X-ray research.
Professor Ross and his Cornell col
league have already completed consider
able work on the subject, as the latter
spent some time here a year ago last
summer.
In addition to his experimental work
Professor Ross will conduct classes dur
ing his stay at Ithaca.
