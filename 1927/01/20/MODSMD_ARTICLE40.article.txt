# O.A.C. TRACK SQUAD NOT TO LEAVE COAST
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Jan. 16. —Eor the first
time since 1923, the O.A.C. four man
relay team will not enter any of the
eastern or middle-western relay carni
vals, announced the board of control to
day.
