# LOUBAT PRIZE AWARDS TO BE GIVEN IN 1926
## 
### 
Two prizes known as the Loubat
Prizes, of the value respectively of $1,000
and $400 are awarded at commencement
at the close of every five year period,
dating from July 1, 1898, for the best
work printed and published in the Eng
lish language by any person on the his
tory, geography, archaeology, etmology,
philology, or numismatics of North
America, according to announcement just
received by the president's office; the
next award will be in 1928.
Communications and works submitted
in competition for the Loubat Prizes
should be addressed to the Secretary of
Columbia University, New York City.
