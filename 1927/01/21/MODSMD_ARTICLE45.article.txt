# European Folk Songs on Tuesday Evening Program
## 
### 
European folk songs, all given in
their original language, will lend va
riety to the regular Tuesday evening
program next Tuesday night at 7:30
o'clock in the Little Theatre when
the Misses Peronne and Constance
Arntzenius, twin sisters who
won a coterie of friends by their in
terpretative work in the United States
and Europe, will present the program
in native costumes.
