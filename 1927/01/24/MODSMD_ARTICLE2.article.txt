# COMMITTEE ASKS FOR MORE MANUSCRIPTS FOR JUNIOR OPERA
## 
### 
Junior Opera manuscripts will here
after be submitted to Fenton Barrett,
chairman of the Junior Opera com
mittee, it was announced yesterday.
The committee is desirous of receiv
ing a number of contributions before
the deadline, January 31. Several plots
of a light nature have been turned in,
but Barrett hopes for a few last
minute offerings to widen the field of
choice.
Musical scores are being received
by Herbert Klein, and the deadline for
these contributions is January 31, as
for the books. Books need not be ac
companied by music nor scores by
plot ideas.
