# DEGREE APPLICATIONS ISSUED THIS WEEK AT REGISTRAR'S OFFICE
## 
### 
The Registrar's office has issued an
announcement to the effect that candi
dates for the A.B. degree this quarter
and candidates for advanced degrees
next quarter should file applications
this week.
Students who expect to get their
degree of Rachelor of Arts at the close
of the present quarter should file ap
plication at the Registrar's office as
soon as possible. Application blanks
may be obtained from that office.
Candidates for the degree of Master
of Arts and Engineer, who are now
registered for their second quarter of
graduate work, and who expect to
complete their requirements by the
end of spring quarter, should file ap
plication at the Registrar's office not
later than Friday, January 28.
