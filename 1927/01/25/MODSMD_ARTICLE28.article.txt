# Washington Punishes Twelve For Cheating in Finals
## 
### 
The Senior Council at the Univer
sity of Washington has taken action
unequalled in the history of the school
by giving one hundred hours in penal
ties to twelve students convicted of
cheating in final examinations last
quarter. This punishment is the most
drastic ever exacted by student of
ficials in the northern university.
