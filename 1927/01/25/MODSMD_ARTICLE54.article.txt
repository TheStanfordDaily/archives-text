# Members of "Princess Ida'' Cast Meet for Rehearsal
## 
### 
Members of the "Princess Ida" cast
will meet tonight in the Assembly
Hall at 7 o'clock for an important re
hearsal and instructions and appoint
ments for further rehearsals.
Dramatic rehearsals will be carried
on from 7 to 10 o'clock. Those not
needed from time to time on the stage
will review the musical score in
Room 84.
