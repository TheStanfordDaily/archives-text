# JACKSON WILL SPEAK AT METHODIST CHURCH
## 
### 
"Young Islam on Trek" is the gen
oral title of a series of talks being
given by Professor J. H. Jackson for
the Mission Study class at the Method
ist Church in Palo Alto.
He uses as a basis for Ihe study
Pasil Matthews' book of the same
name. The second meeting of the
series will be Sunday night at 6:30
o'clock.
Professor Jackson will develop his
subject from the social, economic, geo
graphical, and intellectual standpoints,
emphasizing the relations between the
Mohammedan and Christian peoples,
and the importance which this move
ment of young Islam merits in the
eyes of the world.
