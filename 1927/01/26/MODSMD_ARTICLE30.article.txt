# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
WEDNESDAY, JANUARY 26
3:00 p.m.—Resident Alumnae Tea, in
the Women's Clubhouse.
8:00 P.M. —The Radio Club will meet
in the club shack in the Corporation
Yard.
8:00 p.m.—Science Association, in the
Faculty Clubhouse. Professor Arthur
B. Domonoske, of the Department
of Mechanical Engineering, will
speak on "Automotive Work at the
University of California."
THURSDAY, JANUARY 27
12:45 P.M. —Meeting of Junior Class
in Room 214 to discuss class affairs
including assessment of dues.
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital, by Mr. Al-
len, in the Memorial Church.
4:15 p.m.—Philological Association, in
the Women's Clubhouse.
<7:30 p.m.—Thursday Evening Confer
ence, at the home of Dr. David Starr
Jordan. Subject: "The War Debts."
FRIDAY, JANUARY 28
8:00 p.m.—Popular Medical Lecture,
Lane Hall, San Francisco. Subject:
"The Role of Heredity in Disease,"
by Professor C. H. Danforth.
SUNDAY
6:00 P.M. —The Stanford Episcopal
Club will meet in the parish house.
The speaker will be the Rev. Leslie
E. Learned, D.D., of All Saints
Church, Pasadena.
