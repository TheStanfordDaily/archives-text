## 
### 
BERKELEY, Jan. 26—"Nibs" Hill,
star California pole-vaulter, has been
declared eligible for competition this ,
year. There had been some controversy j
as to his eligibility on the varsity, it
having been charged that he had al
ready competed for more than three ,
varsity years.
Hill is the holder of the California- j
Stanford pole vault record, having
established a mark of 12 feet 11 7-8
inches in 1926, Having anticipated a
favorable reply upon his status, Hill
has been working out consistently and
■will be in good condition for the Bears'
first meet here with the Los Angeles
Athletic Club on March 19.
