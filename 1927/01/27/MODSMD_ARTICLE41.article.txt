# Stock Exchange Director Will Speak at Assembly
## 
### 
Cameron Beck, personnel director
of the New York Stock Exchange, will
address the University assembly Mon
day, January 31, at 10 o'clock.
Beck is making a speaking tour
> throughout California under the auspi
ces of the Long Beach Rotary Club,
1 talking on "Youth and its relation to
Industry" and"The responsibility
which the business man should feel
toward the youth of America." While
in Palo Alto, Beck will address also
the high school assembly and the
Rotary Club meeting.
