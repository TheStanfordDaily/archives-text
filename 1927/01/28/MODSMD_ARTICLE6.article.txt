# DEKES, DELTA CHIS, BETAS VICTORIOUS IN TENNIS MATCHES
## 
### 
Delta Kappa Epsilon 2, Encina 3 0
Beta Theta Pi 2, Phi Kappa Psi 0
Delta Chi 2, Sigma Chi 0
Delta Kappa Epsilon defeated En
cina 3 yesterday in the first match
played yesterday afternoon, when
James Fleming was defeated by Whit
ing Welch 6-1, 6-0. Welch's net shots
and excellent placing in general
gained him his win. In the other match
Alan MeCray defeated John Morris
6-3, 6-0, making excellent use of smash
ing drives and serves.
Beta Theta Pi took Phi Kappa Psi's
measure, Gomer Thomas trimming
Joseph Bitterlin 6-1, 6-0, while Doug
Lewis defeated Ward Edmonds 6-2, 6-3.
Each of these matches was character
ized by long rallies and good drives.
The Delta Chi-Sigma Chi match
ended in victory for the former house,
6-0, 6-1. Norman Springer defeated his
opponent 6-1, Ross Goodcell defeated
Ramsey Smith 6-4.
