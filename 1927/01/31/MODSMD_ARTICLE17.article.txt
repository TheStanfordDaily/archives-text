# ENCINA STORE, CELLAR START LATER HOURS
## 
### 
Students who burn the midnight oil
can now obtain -hot food at the Encina
Store and Cellar until 12 o'clock every
night.
New equipment has been installed
in both places and spaghetti, beef,
and hot meat sandwiches are on the
now menu. The Cellar will begin to
serve this food at noon, hut the En
cina Store not until 7 o'clock in the
evening.
This innovation was allowed by
Comptroller Roth in response to stu
dent demand.
