# 1928 OPERA AUTHORS PROMISED PAYMENT
## 
### 
That the author or authors of the
Junior Opera will receive a substan
tial remuneration for their work is
the announcement, made by Fenton
Barrett, chairman of the Junior Opera
Committee. This will not be intended
as a prize, but will serve to pay the
writers for the work they have put
in on their plots.
The deadline for the receipt of manu
scripts has been moved forward from
today until Friday.
