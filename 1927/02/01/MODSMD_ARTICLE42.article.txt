# FORTY-FIVE TURN OUT FOR WINTER PRACTICE
## 
### 
Forty-five candidates reported for
the first day of winter,practice yester
day under the supervision of Head
Coach Warner. These aspirants for
Stanford's 1927 eleven were put
through an afternoon of running sig
nals, and the group was divided into
four teams and given plays upon which
to work.
Practice starts daily except Satur
days at 4 p.m. If the weather condi
tions do not permit outdoor work, the
time will be spent with blackboard lec
tures in the gymnasium. Winter prac
tice will continue throughout the
month of February.
