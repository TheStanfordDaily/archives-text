# HAIR-BY-HAIR TALE OF WHISKER MEET WILL RUN IN DAILY
## 
### 
Several new contestants yes
terday entered the running in
Chappie's mammoth whiskerino
contest, and the race is on! As
yet there is no perceptible change
in the close-shaven visages of
aspiring roughs. All the men start
at scratch.
In a few days, however, the Old
Roy anticipates some close com
petition; a hair-by-hair account
will be carried by the "Daily."
Betting cards with odds will also
be printed to assist tlie bleachers
iti making selections of favorites.
