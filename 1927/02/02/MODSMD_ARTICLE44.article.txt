# Starks Gives Illustrated Lecture to Zoology Club
## 
### 
Speaking on his experiences while
collecting specimens in the Philippine
Islands, Professor E. C. Starks -will
entertain members of the Zoology
t'lub at 8 o'clock this evening in his
home, 1115 Ramona Street, Palo Alto.
He will illustrate the talk with lantern
slides.
