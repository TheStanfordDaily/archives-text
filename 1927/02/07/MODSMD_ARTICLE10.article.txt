# THREE SELECTED FOR JOFFRE DEBATE TEAM
## 
### 
Frederic M. Combellack, John E.
Truman, and Heverly McAnear were
chosen as members of the Joffre Medal
debating team at the tryouts held last
Friday afternoon.
The question on which the tryoutees
had to present argument was "French
problems in relation to Italy since the
Armistice." This is the same question
to be debnted by the teams of Stanford
and California at the Joffre debates to
be held here April 14.
