# Physiography of Southern Continents Topic for Talk
## 
### 
"Physiography of the Southern Con
tinents" will be the subject of a paper
by P. V. Duhoux at the meeting of
the Journal Club, to be held this even
ing at 7:30 o'clock in the Geology
building. The other speaker on the
program will be G. E. Ekblaw, who
will give an account of the Los Ange
les meeting of the Cordilleran Section
of the Geological Society of America.
