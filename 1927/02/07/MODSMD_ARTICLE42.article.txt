# Freshman Debate Squad Tryouts Scheduled Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for places on the freshman
debating squad will be held at 1:30
o'clock today in the Little Theatre.
The tryoutees are to speak five min
utes with a three-minute rebuttal
speech on either side of the question.
"Resolved, That modern methods of
advertising are harmful to the con
sumer." Any freshmen who have not
signed up for the tryouts or who can
not be present today are asked to
see Ed Dillon, debate manager, or Johu
Duniway, freshman debating coach,
before the tryouts.
