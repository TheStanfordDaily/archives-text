# Fred Olsen Calls Meeting Of Encina Club Tomorrow
## 
### 
Fred Olsen, president of the Encina
Club, has called a meeting of that or
ganization for tomorrow night at 7
o'clock In the hall lobby.
Definite announcements regarding
breakage fees, club finances, and in
tramural games will be made. As
inter-Encina basketball series starts
in another ten clays, details concerning
individual trophies and the system of
playing games will be outlined at this
meeting.
