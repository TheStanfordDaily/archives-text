# Palo Alto Boy Scouts Seek Contributions for Goodwill
## 
### 
Palo Alto Boy Scouts are securing
names of those who will contribute dis
carded articles to be used in the Good
will Industries Plant.
The date that articles are to he col
lected is set for Saturday morning. Those
who have any articles to contribute arc
asked to leave them on the sidewalks for
boys to pick up.
