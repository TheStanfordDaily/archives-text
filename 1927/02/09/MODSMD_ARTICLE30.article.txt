# U.C. Club Will Sing In East on Concert Tour
## 
### 
The University of California Glee
Club will leave Berkeley on March 4
for an Eastern tour, lasting two weeks.
They wi)l give concerts in seven
cities, and will participate in the an
nual Intercollegiate Glee Club contest
in New York City.
Twelve Eastern and Middlewestern
universities will send groups to com
pete in the national contest. This is
the first time a Coast institution has
been asked to participate.
