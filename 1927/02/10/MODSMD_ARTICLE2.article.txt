# PALO ALTO ARRANGES DEMONSTRATION OF RADIO INTERFERENCE
## 
### 
Demonstrations of the sources of
radio interference and practical meth
ods of eliminating certain types of in
terference by receivers will be pre
sented at a mass meeting to be held
by the radio committee of the Palo
Alto Chamber of Commerce next
"Wednesday night.
The committee requests that all
radio listeners in the vicinity send in
their names, together with the name
of the set used and the number of
tubes, to the Chamber of Commerce
office, as that information will be help
ful to the committee in its efforts to
eliminate local interference.
