Supper Dance
, \j Tomorrow Night
DANCE to the enchanting music of the ISrooks-Worthington Stanford
Orchestra tomorrow night in the beautiful dining room of the
Hotel Sainte Claire.
Supper and dancing every Saturday evening from 7 to 9 —51.50 a
