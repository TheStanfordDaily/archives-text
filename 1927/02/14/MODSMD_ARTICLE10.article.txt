# APPOINTMENT OFFICE FINDS SUMMER JOBS
## 
### 
Summer positions are already being
found for students by the Appoint
ment Office. Students are being placed
as swimming instructors, general util
ity men at summer resorts, tutors in
homes, traveling tutors, and salesmen.
Last week a large number of re
quests for gardeners were filled by
the office. All applications already in
were used and extra men called in.
