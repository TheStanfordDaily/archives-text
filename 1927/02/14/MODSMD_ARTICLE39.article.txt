# SAN JOSE CLUB MEN DEFEAT VARSITY GOLF TEAM BY 9-7 SCORE
## 
### 
San Jose Country Club players
evened up the score with the Stanford
golf varsity in the second meeting of
the two teams on Saturday, nine to
seven.
The contest was played over the
San Jose links. Robert Syer, Jr., man
ager of the Cardinal golf team, was
low-score man, turning in a 75 for the
eighteen holes. Three other team
mates scored 77's; Eddie Meyberg,
Douglas White, and Ward Edmunds
all breaking the 80 mark. Dick Rich
mond of the Country Club golfers also
shot a 77.
