###

season the Stanford freshman tennis
"team overwhelmed the Palo Alto High
School aggregation by a score of 4
matches to 1. on the Stanford courts,
Saturday morning, losing only one
singles match.

defeat before Plymire of Palo Alto by
a score of 6-1, 7-5, in the first singles
match. Ben James flifeated Small, and
Ed Weyl beat Hale 6-2, 6-2.
Stanford made a clean sweep in
i the doubles. Lee and James won their

The second encounter was the most.

j and Perin eventually triumphing over
