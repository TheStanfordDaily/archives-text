# SPANISH HONOR CLUB PRESENTS TWO PLAYS
## 
### 
Two Spanish plays will be presented
by Sigma Delta Pi at the open meet
ing at 8:30 o'clock tonight in the
Women's Clubhouse. "La Dimision Ir
revocable," the first play, includes in
its cast: Josefa, Berneice Little; Un
Senor, Charles C. Montgomery; Una
Senora, Hannah F. Foote; Un ladron,
Kenneth M. Park; Ysidro, Aurelio M.
Espinosa, Jr.
"Una Tragedia dp the other
plav, is cast as follows: La Pastora,
Dorothy E. Webster; La Reina, Ruth
E. Slevers; El Pastor. Charles C. Mont
gomery; El Rey. Kenneth M. Park.
