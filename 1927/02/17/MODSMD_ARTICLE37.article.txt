# DEBATERS SELECTED FOR CONTEST WITH CALIFORNIA WOMEN
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society selected
a four-man team last night to meet
the Women's Parliament of the Uni
versity of California in a debate sched
uled for March 10 in Berkeley. The
following men will represent Stan
ford: Harold Peterson, Wallace Cath
cart, John Cranston, and Marvin
Adams, alternate.
The University men will take the
negative side of the question, "Re
solved: That this house deplores the
practice of social lying."
