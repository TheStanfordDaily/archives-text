# ORGANIST WILL PLAY WORKS BY SCHUBERT
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will give a Franz Schubert pro
gram, incorporating a number of that
composer's best-known works, at 4:15
o'clock this afternoon in the Memorial
Church.
"Die Allmacht," which is the first
number to be played, will be followed
by the famous "Moment Musical," Op.
94, No. 6, and by "Ave Maria." The
final selections for the recital consist
of "Du Bist die Ruh" and "Marche
Militaire."
