# Swimming Team to Meet Hollywood Athletic Club
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity swimming
team will meet its second opponent
this season when it enters against the
Hollywood Athletic Club on Tuesday,
February 22, in the Encina pool.
Coach Brandsten will enter practically
the same team that started against
the Oregon Webfooters last Saturday.
The Hollywood team includes many
national champions, as well as sev
eral Stanford graduates.
