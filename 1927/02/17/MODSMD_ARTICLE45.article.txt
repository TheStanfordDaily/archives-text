# U.C. DOGS BANNED BY PRESIDENTIAL ORDER
## 
### 
Dogs have been banned from the
University of California by an order
of President W. W. Campbell.
"The campus has been too much a
focus for dog life," says President
Campbell. "These dogs have been mak
ing nuisances of themselves in vari
ous ways. Owners of dogs are in
structed to keep their pets at home,
as from now on all stray dogs on the
campus will be transported to the
pound."
