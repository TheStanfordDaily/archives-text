One business
That Serves sill Others
yl SINGLE investment house may be the investment banker
jL V_ for scores of borrowers in the municipal, public utility,
industrial and real estate fields. It must know how to judge
securities, and, moreover, how to sell them so that the invest
ment will be serviceable to the investor in each case.
A dependable organization, comprising diversified talent,
is essential to maintaining an investment banking service that
will serve completely both borrowers and investors.

field for well trained minds. Knowledge, personality, energy

field of business.
