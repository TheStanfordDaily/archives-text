# ORGANIST WILL PLAY CLASSICAL NUMBERS
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will play a varied program of
works by modern and classical au
thors in his organ recital at 4 o'clock
Sunday afternoon at the Memorial
Church.
Mozart's Larghetto for the "Clarinet
Quintet" opens the program. The or
gan arrangement of this number is an
adoption of the original theme which
was written for a clarinet and string
quartet.
Next will follow a work of "Camille
St.. Saens, the "Fantasie," Op. 101, in
I) flat, and the "Angel Scene" from
Engelbert Humperdinck's "Hansel and
Gretel."
Theodore Dubois' Toccata in G
major completes the program.
