# Rosa Ponselle to Appear In Season's First Concert
## 
### 
Rosa Ponselle, dramatic soprano of
the Metropolitan Opera Company, will
appear here on the night of March 3
in the first concert of the Associated
Students' Series to be given in 1927.
Her appearance here will mark the
first student body recital to be given
here since December 8, when the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra under
the direction of Alfred Hertz gave its
concert in the Basketball Pavilion.
