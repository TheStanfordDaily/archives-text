# REPRESENTATIVES OF TELEPHONE COMPANY TO TALK ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
Students wishing to obtain perma
nent or summer jobs with the Bell
Telephone Company will have an op
portunity to make applications in per
son to representatives of the com
pany this week, during their annual
visit to the campus, starting Wednes
day.
Talks will be given at 5:15 o'clock
and 7:30 o'clock on Wednesday, in
Room 521, Materials* Laboratory. The
nature of the telephone business,
functions of the respective depart
ments, and varied types of work of
fered for the non-technically as well
as the technically trained man, will
be explained.

Interviews with the representatives
may be had from 9 o'clock until 5 o'-
clock on Thursday and Friday in
Room 532, Engineering School.
