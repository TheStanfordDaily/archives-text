# HANDBALL MEN LOSE TO ATHENS PLAYERS
## 
### 
The Stanford handball varsity went
down to defeat 3 to 0 at the hands
of the Athens Athletic Club Friday
at the Athens courts, when the Car
dinal players lost three closely fought
doubles matches.
Frank Harold and Carroll McGetti
gan were defeated 21-9, 20-21, 21-10 by
Rowel and Nelson of the Athens team.
Van der Ende and Theiss, also of the
Winged Acorns, won two straight
pames from Waldo Ashby and James
Melton. In the last contest, Rowel and
Nelson downed Clifford Figel and
Cyril Chappellet 2 to 0.
