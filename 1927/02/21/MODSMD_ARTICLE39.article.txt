# Harold Downs Rumwell in Semi-Final Handball Match
## 
### 
Frank Harold defeated Reg Rum
well 16-21, 21-2, Friday in the
last handball match before the finals
of the tournament. Rumwell, who had
an early start, took the first game
easily. In the second, he could not
find himself, losing out 21-2, and in
the third. Harold played a game that
kept Rumwell on the run all the time.
