# Psychologist will Speak Before Pi Lambda Theta
## 
### 
"Psychological Work in Connection
with the Juvenile Court" is* the topic
of the talk to be given by Dr. Maud
A. Merrill, assistant professor of psy
chology, at the meeting of Pi Lambda
Theta, women's honorary education
society, in the Women's Clubhouse at
5 o'clock this afternoon.
Dr. Merrill is Santa Clara County
Juvenile Court psychologist and is a
member of the staff of the Santa
Clara County Health Center.
