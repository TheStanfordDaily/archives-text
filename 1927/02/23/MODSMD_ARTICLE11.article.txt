# SECOND REVOTE ON FIVE CLASS OFFICES OPENS
## Election of All 1929 Heads Scheduled in Today's Special Ballot POLLS AT LAW STEPS Position of Vice-President For Juniors Also To Be Filled 
### 
Polls for the second special mid
year election to determine the vice
president of the Junior Class and all
sophomore class officers open this
morning at 8:30 o'clock and close at
2 p.m. •
Men will cast their ballots at the
Law steps, while the women's polls
are located in the arcade directly
across from the main entrance to the
Library. Women vote at the men's
polls from 12 until 1 o'clock.
Two candidates who received the
highest number of votes at the special
election last Tuesday for the respec
tive offices for which they are run
ning are on the ballot for each va
cancy. They are as follows:
Vice-President Class of 1928
Lucia Champlin
Anne Hartwell
President Class of 1929
Joe Carroll
Ted Krough
Vice-President Class of 1929
Jane Plumb
John B. Wheatley
Secretary Class of 1929
Elizabeth Cramer
Ruth Lansburg-h
Treasurer of Class of 1929
Homer Craig
Frederic N. Clark
