# HOLMAN PLAYS OGDEN FOR TENNIS RANKING
## 
### 
Cranston Holman and Lionel Ogden
will play this noon at 12:15, weather
permitting, for first place on the var
sity tennis team. Ogden was ranked
fourth on the squad at the beginning
of the team competition a month ago,
but by victories over Ralph McElvenny
and Alan Herrington attained his pres
ent position of second.
