WEDNESDAY
7:15 P.M. —Alpha Chi Sigma meets in
Conference Room of Chemistry
building. Very' important.
3:00 P.M. —Resident Alumni Tea, in
the Women's Clubhouse. Hostesses:
Chapter D.D. of P.E.O. Sisterhood
of Palo Alto.
7:00 P.M. —Stanford Women's Glee
Club meets upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
THURSDAY
4:15 P.M. —Organ Recital by Mr. Al
len, in the Memorial Church.
4:15 P.M. —University Philological As
sociation, in the Women's Club
house. Paper on "Classics and Our
Twentieth Century Poets," by Dr.
Henry Rushton Fairclough.
7:30 P.M. —Thursday evening confer
ence, at the home of Dr. Jordan.
Subject: "Why Jefferson Trembled."
,8:00 P.M. —History Club, in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Professor Ichihashi
will speak on "Art as an Aid to His
torical Research." Faculty members,
graduates, and seniors in the De
partments of History and Political
Science are invited to attend.
