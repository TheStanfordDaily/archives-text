# EXHIBITION AT SANTA CRUZ ENDS TRAINING OF CARD GYM TEAM
## 
### 
With but nine days remaining be
fore the annual match with the Uni
versity of California, the Stanford
Gym team under Coach Charles W.
Davis is winding up a long pre-season
practice period in an exhibition to be
staged at Santa Cruz High School
between the halves of a basketball
game there tomorrow evening.
Coach Davis will take ten men to
perform in individual and team tum
bling, pyramid stunts, and parallel bar
work. The following will make the
trip: Taylor, Beckwith, Strang, Naff.
Lucy, Freeman, Smith, King, and
Allerdice.
