# ANNUAL MEETING HELD BY CITY ALUMNI CLUB
## 
### 
Wheeler Taylor, '20, was elected presi
dent of the Stanford Alumni Club of San
Francisco at the annual meeting held
February 23. Carl G. Brown, '00, gave a
report on the activities of the past year.
The membership in the San Francisco
club lias increased from 250 to 500, and
fifteen scholarships are now maintained
by them for Stanford students.
