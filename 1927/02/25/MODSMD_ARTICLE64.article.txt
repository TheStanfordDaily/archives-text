# U.S. ATTITUDE TOWARD LATIN AMERICA TOPIC FOR WOMEN'S DEBATE
## 
### 
"Resolved, That the Policy of the
United States Toward Latin America
Should Be Condemned" is the question of
the debates on March 2 between the Stan
ford women's debating teams and the
University of California in Los Angeles
and Mills College.
Fifteen-minute speeches will be given
and cach side will be allowed a twelve
minute rebuttal. The decision will be
made by the audience. Ballots will be
taken up before and after the debates.
