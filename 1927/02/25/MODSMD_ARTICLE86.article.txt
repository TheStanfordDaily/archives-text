# Interfraternity Bodies To Meet Next Monday
## 
### 
Interfraternity Council will meet
Monday night at 9 o'clock in the
Trophy Room in the Old Union, when
it is expected that the final rushing
rules for the 1927 season will be com
pleted.
Monday noon at the Beta Theta Pi
house the Interfraternity Board of
Control will meet to finish drawing up
the rules to be presented that night at
the meeting of the Council.
Rushnig season starts the third
week in spring quarter according to
the present rules.
