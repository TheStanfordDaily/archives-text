# Freshmen, Sophomores Open Class Baseball Play
## 
### 
Interclass baseball will begin this
afternoon when the FRESHMEN face
the sophomore team in the initial
game of the series. Junior and senior
nines will meet tomorrow and on
Thursday the championship is to be
decided in a game between the win
ners ol' the preliminary contests. The
diamond in front of the military de
partment will be used and the starting
time has been set for 4 o'clock.
1930
