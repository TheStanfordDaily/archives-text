# TOYON CLUB SMOKER IS PLANNED TONIGHT
## 
### 
Toyon will hold its annual smoker
for the freshmen tonight at 7:30
o'clock in the hall. There will be
smokes and food, and a program of
speeches, music, stunts, and two con
tests for the freshmen. The contests
will be a milk-drinking competition
and a cracker and whistle contest.
Prizes will be given for each one._
The program will consist of a talk
by Dink Templeton, a pantomime of
a dope fiend by Archie Rauta, a stunt
by Togo Shlma and his revelers, and
singing by George Bogue, accompanied
by Rainey Hancock on the piano.
