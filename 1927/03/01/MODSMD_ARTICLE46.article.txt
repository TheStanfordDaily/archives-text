# Resident Alumnae Meet Tomorrow at Weekly Tea
## 
### 
Members of the Resident Alumnae
Association will meet tomorrow after
noon at 3 o'clock in the Stanford Wo
men's Clubhouse, for their regular
weekly tea. Hostesses for the after
noon are: Mrs. William Huston, Mrs.
Harry Todd, Mrs. Browning Smith,
Mrs. A. T. Cluff, Mrs. Edward Harri
son, Mrs. Alex Esenberg, and Miss
Geneva White.
