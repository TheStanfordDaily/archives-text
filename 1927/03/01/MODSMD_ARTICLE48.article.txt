# Esther Caulkin Hostess At Graduate Women's Tea
## 
### 
Esther Caulkin, A.M. '25, will be
the hostess for the regular weekly tea
of the Graduate Women's Club this
afternoon at 4:30 o'clock in the Stan
ford Women's Clubhouse. Discussion
will be continued on graduate social
life.
