# R.O.T.C. BASKETBALL TOURNEY PLAY STARTS
## 
### 
Initial play-offs in the R.O.T.C. bas
ketball tourney will be held in Encina
gymnasium at 4:15 o'clock this after
noon when the Company C quintet
meets the Company D five. At 5 o'clock
the teams of Companies A and B will
play the second bracket.
The winner of the C and D game
will meet Company E Thursday after
noon at 4:15 o'clock. The winner of
this match will contest the winner
of the A and B game for the cham-
pionship Monday.
