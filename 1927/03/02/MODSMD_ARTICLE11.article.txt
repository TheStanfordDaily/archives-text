# LAST IRISH MARATHON TRYOUTS SCHEDULED
## 
### 
Last chance to try out for places in the
Irish Marathon, which is to be run oif
Saturday, will be given this afternoon.
Track managers will be at the old oval
from 3:30 to 5:30 to time those who
wish to try out.
Teams for the Irish marathon are se
lected from the members of each class
making the best times for the quarter
mile in the tryouts. Kach of the twenty
four men in the teams runs a quarter
mile in the race itself.
