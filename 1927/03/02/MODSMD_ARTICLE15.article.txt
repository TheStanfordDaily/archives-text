# COMPANY D DEFEATS OPPONENTS IN R.O.T.C. BASKETBALL TOURNEY
## 
### 
Company D won the first game in
the R.O.T.C. basketball tournament
yesterday afternoon by decisively de
feating the Company C squad by the
overwhelming score of 36 to 13. Com
pany A advanced one notch by the
default of Company B.
The outcome of the game was never
in doubt as the D quintet ran in an
early lead which was augmented as
the game progressed. The, score at
half time was 15 to 5 in favor of the
winners.
Tomorrow afternoon Company D
will play the Company E five in En
cina gymnasium at 4:15 o'clock. Mon
day the unit championship will be
played off when the winner of D vs.
E. plays Company A.
