# Women's Glee Club Will Broadcast Radio Concert
## 
### 
Arrangements have been made with
Sherman and Clay of San Francisco
|by Miss Natalie Macrum, director of
: the Stanford Women's Glee Club, for
concerts to be given by the club next
! quarter over KMRC, Sherman and
Clay sending station. Proceeds from
these will go towards paying off the
club's debts. The society will hold its
regular woekly choral practice today
at 7 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
