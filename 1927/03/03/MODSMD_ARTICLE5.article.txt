# COLLEGIATE LITERARY CONTEST BY HARPER'S ENDS NEXT SATURDAY
## 
### 
A first prize of $500 and a second
and third prize of $300 and $200, re
spectively, are being offered to Ameri
can undergraduates for pieces of prose
writing by the Harper's Intercollegiate
Literary Contest, conducted by Har
per's Magazine.
Contestants may submit only one
manuscript in the form of prose writ
ing, short story, play, essay, or article.
All manuscripts must be mailed not
later than Saturday, March 5, to the
Harper's Intercollegiate Literary Con
test, Harper's Magazine, 49 East 33d
Street, New York City. Previous pub
lication will not exclude 1t from the
competition.
