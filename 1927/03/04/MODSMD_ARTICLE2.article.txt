# Dinner Meeting Scheduled By German Club Saturday
## 
### 
A dinner meeting will be held
by the German Club Saturday night in
the Women's Union, starting promptly
at 6 o'clock. Women members are to
bring box dinners, which will be sold
to the men.
Hugo Leistner, 224 Toyon, and
Helene von Damra, 369 Roble, are
taking reservations for the men and
women, respectively. Anyone who
speaks, understands, or is interested
in German may attend.
