# FRESHMAN DIAMOND MEN PLAY HAYWARD HIGH SCHOOL TODAY
## 
### 
If weather conditions permit, the
babe baseball team will play the Hay
ward High School nine at 9 o'clock
tomorrow morning. The freshmen have
lost out considerably on account of
the recent rains, being forced to can
cel three of their scheduled games.
The freshman batterly will be com
posed of either Simpkins or Leverenz
pitching and Sparks catching. The
other men whom Coach Husky Hunt
will probably start are: Levin, first
base; Story, second base; Bianchi,
shortstop; Laird, third base; Murphy,
right field; McCoy or Peace, center
field.
