# Authority on Practical Economics to Speak on U.S. in World Business
## 
### 
Sir George Paish, termed by David
Starr Jordan as "one of the highest
world authorities on practical econo
mics and a very interesting speaker,"
will lecture in the Little Theatre to
morrow afternoon at 4:15 o'clock. The
topic of his talk is "Will America Re
main a World Banker and Trader?"
This lecture will he of particular value
to those interested in business prob
lems.
Sir George is a noted British econo
mist and is well-known as an English
Liberal and Free Trader. He is mak
ing a lecture tour which includes the
University of California, Pasadena, and
San Diego.
