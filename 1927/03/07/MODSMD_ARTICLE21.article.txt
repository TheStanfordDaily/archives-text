# Reviews to Be Presented At Journal Club Meeting
## 
### 
Two reviews will be presented at
the meeting of the Journal Club of the
Department of Geology at 7:30 o'clock
tonight in the Geology building. Davis'
"The Lesser Antilles" will be the sub
ject of a review by Robert R. Wilson,
and A. L. Christenson will review
Barren's "Marine and Terrestial Con
glomerates."
The meeting will be open to the
public. *
