# EXCUSES FOR LOW SCHOLARSHIP
## 
### 
Students whose work has been in
terrupted during the present quarter
by illness or other causes, and who
wish to have the special circumstances
taken into consideration by the Com
mittee on Scholarship, may file a state
ment with Mrs. Huston, secretary of
the committee, any morning between
9 and 12, in the Registrar's office. Ex
cuses submitted later than March 11
cannot be assured of consideration be
fore action is taken on the end-quarter
reports.
Requests for allowance on the
ground of illness must be accompanied
by the special physician's certificate
furnished by the committee.
