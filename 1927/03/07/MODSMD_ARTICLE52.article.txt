# CALIFORNIA DEFEATS CARD HANDBALL MEN
## 
### 
Stanford's handball team was com
pletely smothered by California in the
Intercollegiate matches last Saturday
afternoon at the Athens Club, losing
in every event.
J. C. Melton and W. Ashby lost in
the first doubles match; C. Figel and
It. Rurawell lost in the second doubles;
F. Harold lost in the first singles; and
C. McGettigan lost in the second sin
gles.
