# AWARD OF SCHOLARSHIPS
## 
### 
All University scholarships, except
those reserved for entering students,
will bo awarded during the spring
quarter. Applications should be filed
at the office of the Committee on
Scholarship before April 15. Candi
dates for 1926-27, including present
holders of scholarships who wish to
apply for renewals, should file new
applications at this time. Application
blanks and further information may be
obtained at the office of the commit
tee.
