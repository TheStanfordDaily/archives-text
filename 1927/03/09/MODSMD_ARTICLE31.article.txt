# Redlands Women's Glee Club to Sing on Campus
## 
### 
The University of Redlands Women's
Glee Club will give a public concert
here on the first Friday of spring
quarter, April 1. Members of the visit
ing club will be guests of the Stanford
Women's Glee Club at luncheon prior
to the presentation of their program.
The last meeting for this quarter of
the local women's organization will be
held tonight at 7 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
