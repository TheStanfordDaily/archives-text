# KANSAS ATHLETIC COACH OFFERS CAGE COURSE USING MAIL
## 
### 
"Basketball for Men" will be offered
as a correspondence course by Dr. F. C.
Allen, director of athletics .'.t the Uni
versity of Kansas.
The course is designed for men teach
ing basketball who need to know some
thing about the technique of basketball
coaching, men in other professions who
desire the same knowledge, and college
students majoring in physical education.
The fundamentals of basketball will
be taken up. Such subjects as training,
conditioning, practicing, details and rules
of the game will be outlined fully.
The course has been prepared to fill
a demand for correspondence courses in
recreative sports. Other similar courses,
including some for women, are being
worked upon and will be ready soon.
