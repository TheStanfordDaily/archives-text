# EUPHRONIA TO HOLD ELECTION TOMORROW
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society will hold
its quarterly banquet and election of offi
cers in the New Union at 0:15 o'clock
tomorrow night.
The same evening Wallace Cathcart
and John Cranston will go to Berkeley to
debate with the U.C. Women's Parlia
ment on the negative side of the question,
"Resolved, That this house deplores the
jiracticc of social lying." The debate
.starts at 8:15 o'clock, and will be held in
Wheeler Hall, on the state university
campus.
