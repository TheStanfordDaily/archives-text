# Washington Students Begin Search for Hidden Garter
## 
### 
Amateur sleuths and treastfre hunt
ers are offered a $25 prize by a Seattle
theatre if they succeed in recovering
a dainty, jeweled garter entrusted to a
woman student here for safe-keeping.
The thrill of "Getting Gertie's Garter,"
combined with the monetary reward,
is creating wide interest among all the
men in the hunt for the elusive elastic.
