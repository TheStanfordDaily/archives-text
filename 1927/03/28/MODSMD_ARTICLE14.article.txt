# FOUR-HOUR COURSE IN JAZZING TONIGHT; TUITION ONE DOLLAR
## 
### 
The best place to forget your
newly-departed ninety-six dollars,
according to all available authority,
will not be in San Francisco or way
points, but at the Women's Club
house this evening.
There the quarterly registration
dance, sponsored by the Stanford
Women's Athletic Association for
the benefit of the W.A.A. lodge,
will be held.
The Brooks-Worthington orches
tra will start in at 9 o'clock and
will hold forth until 1. Admission
will be one dollar per couple.
