# CASH, PROPERTY GIFTS ANNOUNCED BY U.C.
## 
### 
Gifts to the University of California
totalling over $405,000 in cash and
several million dollars in property and
equipment, during the past year, were
announced by President W. W. Camp
bell at the fifty-ninth Charter Day ob
servances in the Greek Theatre on
March 22.
Leading the list of gifts were the
William A. Clark, Jr., estate in Los
Angeles, consisting of property, labora
tories, an observatory, and a library,
valued at four or five million dollars;
and a fund of $106,000 created by Mrs.
Clara Hellman Heller of San Fran
cisco for the support of the Emanuel
S. Heller professorship of law, and of
research in social economics.
