# U.C. Professor to Talk on "Greece Before the Greeks"
## 
### 
Professor John Linton Myres, Wyke
ham Professor of Ancient History at
Oxford and now Sather Professor of
Classical Literature at the University
of California, will give a lecture
Thursday evening on the subject,
"Greece Before the Greeks."
Professor Myres' talk will be given
under the auspices of the Department
of Classical Literature; it will begin
at 7:30' in the Little Theatre, and will
be open to the public.
