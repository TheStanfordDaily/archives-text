# JOLLY-UP PLANNED FOR ALL STUDENTS BY HONOR GROUPS
##

Skulls and Caps, Gowns and
Snakes, and a jolly-up. No, it's
Skull and Snakes and Cap and
Gown, but the jolly-up is right.
An all-campus jolly-up to be
held at the Basketball Pavilion
Friday night is being planned by
Skull and Snakes, men's honor
ary society, and Cap and Gown,
Senior Women's honorary or
ganization.
"There'll be plenty of men at
50 cents apiece," noise the
Skullers and Snakers.
"And there'll be more than a
plenty of women at nothing
apiece," repartee the Cappers and
Gowners.
