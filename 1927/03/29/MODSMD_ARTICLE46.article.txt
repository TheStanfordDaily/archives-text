# SEMINAR PLANNED TO AID IN BRIAND SPEECH
## 
### 
SEMINAR PLANNED
William L. Schwartz, Professor of
French, will meet students interested in
the Aristide Briand Speech Contest, an
nounced last quarter in the Daily, in Room
242 at 1 o'clock Wednesday. Distribution
of the official French text of the cele
brated statesman's speech before the
League of Nations Assembly upon the
entrance of Germany into the League and
of necessary blanks to be filled out will
be made at that time.
A class will be formed among those
competing in the contest for the purpose
of giving assistance in translating the
text. Explanation of obscure idioms only
will be made; the competitors themselves
will translate the speech.
