# Bathing Suits Used in New Type of College Surveying
## 
### 
Engineering classes al the Collect
of the Pacific: have discovered the
value of the bathing suit as an aid in
the preparation ot classwork. When
told to make a profile drawing of the
Calaveras River, which runs to the
rear of the college grounds, the men
donned bathing suits as proper attire
for locating all holes and depressions
in the river bed.
