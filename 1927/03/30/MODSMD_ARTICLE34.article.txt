# U.C. REQUIRES PLEDGE SIGNATURE ON EVERY EXAMINATION PAPER
## 
### 
Written pledges are now required of
all students in minor tests and final
examinations at the University of
California, according to an official
notice published by the University
President, W. W. Campbell, following
a request for action by the Executive
Committee of the Associated Students.
To make an examination valid it is
necessary that each student partici
pating therein shall, at the end of his
examination paper, write and sign his
or her name to the following state
ment:
"I hereby certify upon my honor
that I have neither given nor received
unauthorised assistance in this exam
ination."
Any examination paper not closing
with this certification will be given a
grade of "P."
