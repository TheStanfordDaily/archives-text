# OXFORD INSTRUCTOR LECTURES ON GREECE IN LITTLE THEATRE
## 
### 
"Greece Before the Greeks," an il
lustrated lecture about pre-historic
Greek times, will be given this after
noon by Professor John Linton Myres,
Oxford instructor now teaching at the
University of California. The lecture
will be in the Little Theatre at 4:15
o'clock.
Professor Myres will talk particu
larly about the ancient Minoan civiliza
tion and will give details of Sir Arthur
Kvans' excavations in Create. The
speaker is well acquainted with archeo
logical work in that district, having
spent several years with expeditions
on the island.
