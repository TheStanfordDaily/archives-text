# ITALIAN SOCIETY TO OFFER FELLOWSHIP
## 
### 
The Italy America Society an
nounces "The Eleanora Duse Fellow
ship" for the year 1927-28, which car
ries an annual stipend of $1200 to be
awarded to a student of either sex
who was born in the United States or
Canada, and who has a speaking and
reading knowledge of Italian.
The fellowship is for study in Italian
universities in the fields of literature,'
history, philosophy, mathematics, and
the social sciences, and is for the pur
pose of furthering intellectual rela
tions between Italy and the United
Btates. Applications must, be filed be
fore May 1, 1927, with the Secretary
of the Italy American Society, 25 West
43rd Street, New York.
