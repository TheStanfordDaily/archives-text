# Women's Glee Club to Honor Visitors at Lunch
## 
### 
All members of the Stanford Wo
men's Glee Club who can possibly be
present are requested by Betty Thomp
son, president, to attend a luncheon
given at 12:30 o'clock tomorrow in the
dining room of the Old Union for the
Women's Choral Club of the Univer
sity of Redlands.
A public concert will be given at.
1:30 o'clock by the visiting Glee Club
in the Women's Clubhouse following
the luncheon.
