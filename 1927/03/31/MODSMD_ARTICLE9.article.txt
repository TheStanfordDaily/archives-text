# PRESS MEN TO HEAR SURVEYS BY STUDENTS
## 
### 
Among the outstanding features of
the conference of local professional
newspapermen which is to be held
here tomorrow and Saturday will be
the reading of two journalistic reports
prepared by Stanford students.
Allan Porter, a former student, has
prepared a survey of the opinions of
the Burlingame Advance readers,
while Walter M. Campbell, of the
Journalism department will read a
survey on "Newspaper Merchandis
ing."
