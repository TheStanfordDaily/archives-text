### 
Ping-pong comes in on the rising tide of intercollegiate athletics. The
Tulane Hullabaloo editorially comments;
"Today the ping-pong players from Tulane journey to Baton Rouge where
they will play the table tennis players of Louisiana State University for the
championship of Louisiana. Tulane, it is believed, will win the contest and
gain much by her victories. Here's hoping Pat. Ryan and Johnny Gilmour
paddle the Tiger's claws, and win the series by means of love-sets!"
