# 1930 DEBATE SQUAD WILL START SEASON AGAINST CALIFORNIA
## 
### 
Participating in their first debate of
the season, the Stanford freshman debat
ers will meet the University of California
beginners Thursday evening in the Little
Theater. The question for discussion is
"Resolved, That the modern methods of
advertising are harmful to the consum
ers."
The affirmative and negative squads,
composed of three members each, have
not yet been chosen. The tryoutees for
the team were rated some time ago and
before the final selection of the two
trios will be made they will speak before
another set of judges and the two ratings
averaged to determine the squads.
