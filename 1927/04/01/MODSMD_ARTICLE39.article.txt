# SENIOR CLASS WILL ORGANIZE DRIVE FOR COLLECTION OF DUES
## 
### 
Collection of senior class dues to carry
on the class activities and buy the senior
class gift to the University will start on
Monday of next week and end Wednes
day. To make the collection of the money
easier, the finance committee will appoint
a member in each living group to help
them. Seniors who do not live in the
halls or any living group are asked to
mail their dues to Don E. Fritts, Box
1304, Campus.
Those who do not pay class dues will
not he given Senior Ball tickets or Sen
ior Week booklets, nor will they he al
lowed to sign the class roster.
