# Women Elect Captains for Spring Quarter Swimming
## 
### 
Election of freshman, sophomore,
and junior women's swimming cap
tains for the coming season was held
last week by Wilma Fenher, swimming
manager, with the following results:
freshman captain, Maxine Cushing;
sophomore. Mary Barstow; and junior,
Jean Wakefield.
The senior election will be held
later in the season when more swim
mers in that class will have reported
for practice.
