# SEVEN MEN WILL GET BOXING MEDALS
## 
### 
Seven men, the winners of the several
classes in the Novice Boxing Tourna
ment held on February 1, will be enter
tained tomorrow noon at a luncheon in
their honor held at the Delta Chi house.
At the luncheon they will be presented
with the medals offered by the Circle
"S" Society.
Those who will receive the awards are :
V. L. Shoemaker, bantamweight; Les
Cowan, featherweight; H. R. Skeltch,
lightweight; A1 Charles, welterweight;
Strcnt Hanna, middleweight; W. Hol
lingsworth, light-heavyweight; and Dave
Oliva, heavyweight.
