# NEW HEADS ELECTED BY PHI ALPHA DELTA
## 
### 
Holding its regular business meeting tor
the spring quarter last night at the
Tau Omega house at 7:30 o clock, Phi
Alpha Delta, national honorary legal fra
ternity, elected officers for the quarter.
The new officers are Stanley Lowell,
Justice; Milton M. Hogle, Vice-Justice;
Wallace Cathcart, Clerk; Kenneth Fur
guson, Treasurer; and Charles E.
Beardsley, Marshall.
Besides regular business and the elec
tion of officers, plans for the quarter
were discussed.
