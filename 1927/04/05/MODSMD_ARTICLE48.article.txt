# BEARS FACE TROJAN TENNIS MEN SATURDAY
## 
### 
BERKELEY, April 5. — (Radio Dis
patch)— Tennis teams from University
of California and University of South
ern California will meet next Saturday
as one of the features of University
Day.
The California squad, as it now
stands, includes, in order of rank, Cap
tain Tom Stow, John Risso, Dick
Hoogs, and Andy Burke. Nebo Cchas
seur advanced to fifth position yes
terday by defeating Shirley Ward, who
previously held that place.
