# RUBE WOLF SAYS JAZZ MUST FIT INSTRUMENT
## 
### 
"Modern syncopations and rhyth
modics are arranged to fit the type of
instruments popular today," says Rube
Wolf, popular San Francisco musical
director, who will bring his orchestra
here the night of April 22 for the Mili
tary Ball. "Especially for organiza
tions playing in cafes, dance halls, on
the stage, and in novelty bands does
the syncopation in the fox-trot, the
one-step, and the Charleston make it
possible to conceive the various
dances," he declared.
Committees working on the dance
report that plans are progressing well.
Bids may still he secured at th.>
A.S.S.U. office in the Stanford Union
for four dollars.
