Students are urged to make
their reservations for the All-
Stanford supper to be given in
Memorial Court, April 16, as soon
as possible. Reservations should
be made through the office of
the alumni secretary.
Dinner is scheduled for 6:30.
Tickets will bo two dollars, and
will admit holders to the enter
tainment and dance following the
supper. Tickets for one dollar
may be secured which will admit
holders to the entertainment and
dance.
