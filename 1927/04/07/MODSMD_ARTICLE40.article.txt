# DINK TEMPLETON WILL GIVE TALK OVER KPO
## 
### 
As the first track and field radio
talk of the season, Coach "Dink" Tem
pleton will broadcast an interview
over KPO tomorrow night for Sports
on-the-Air listeners, from 6:15 to 6:30
o'clock.
Templeton will discuss the track
prospects for the close of the season
and will compare Stanford with Cali
fornia and University of Southern Cali
fornia. whose track teams are yet to
meet the Cardinals.
