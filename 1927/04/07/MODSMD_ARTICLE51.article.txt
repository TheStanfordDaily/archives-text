# Faculty Women's Club Will Entertain Male Colleagues
## 
### 
Members of the Faculty Women's
Club will entertain the men of the
faculty on Saturday night, April i), at
8 o'clock in the Stanford Women's
Clubhouse, in their annual party to the
faculty men. Mrs. P. A Huston has
written a skit entitled "The Wind of
Freedom," which will be presented by
members of the club.
