# Training Slips for Women Filed Each Week at Gym
## 
###

petitive athletics during this quarter,
including swimming, track, and tennis,
must be filled out each" week, accord
ing to a gymnasium announcement.
The slips, which are to be deposited in
ti special box immediately inside the
Kym door, will check on compliance
with the regulations for sleep, diet, ex
ercise, bathing, and smoking.
