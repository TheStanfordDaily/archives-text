# STANFORD CHEMISTS WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Stanford chemists who are members
of the American Chemical Society will
meet in San Francisco this evening at
8 o'clock to attend the regular meet
ing of that organization. It will be held
in the Engineers' Club at 57 Post
Street, a dinner being served, previ
ous to the business session, at 6:15
o'clock.
"The Chemist in the Tannery" will
bo the subject of the principal paper
for the evening, with Mr. C. B. Kinney,
chemist for the Sawyer Tannery Com
pany, giving it. Another feature of the
meeting will be a short talk on ' Oil
Evaporation Losses" by P. S. Williams,
of the Standard Oil Company.
