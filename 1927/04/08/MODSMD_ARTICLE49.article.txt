# University of Nevada to Radio Glee Club Program
## 
### 
Thirty-five singers and an orchestra of
eight pieces, comprising the University
of Nevada Glee Club, en tour, are sched
uled to sing before the microphone of
Radio KPO on Saturday night, April
16, from 7 to 8 o'clock.
The program will include a baritone
and a piano solo. After the broadcast,
local alumni will fete the glee club at
the Whitcome hotel.
