# FACULTY WOMEN WILL PRESENT ORIGINAL SKIT
## 
### 
"The Wind of Freedom," a skit writ
ten by Mrs. C. A. Huston, chairman of
the scholarship committee, will be pre
sented at the regular annual party
giveu the men of the faculty by the
Faculty Women's Club, Saturday eve
ning at 8 o'clock in the Stanford Wo
men's Clubhouse. The skit will be pre
sented at 8:30 o'clock.
Mrs. E. C. Cubberly, Miss Mary
Wildmau, Mrs. Horatio Stebbins, Mrs.
Carl Weutdorff, Mrs. Joseph Taylor,
and Mrs. C. A. Huston will compose
the cast, and Mrs. Huston will direct
the playlet. The music will be played
between scenes.
Bridge will follow the presentation
of the skit.
