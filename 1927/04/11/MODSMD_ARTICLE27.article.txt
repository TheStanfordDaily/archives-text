Georgetown University juniors enjoy a game of restful basketball,
winning in their sleep. The 1/oya declares:
"After Beemingly enjoying a late sleep throughout tho first half, the
Juniors came back in the second half of their basketball game with the
Frosh 8.5.1, on Sunday morning. In the first half baskets were scarcer than
athletes getting A in Geology. The store at half time was o to 2. It .sounds
like a baseball game without the scandals."
