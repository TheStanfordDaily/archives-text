# GRADUATE APPOINTED COLLEGE PRESIDENT
## 
### 
Appointment of Thomas William
MacQuarrie, '22. who received his A.M.
in 1923 and Ph.D. in 1924 at Stanford,
as president of the San Jose State
Teachers College was made last week
by the State Board of Education at
Sacramento. Dr. MacQuarrie was for
merly president of Metropolitan Col
lege.
Another man, Ralph W. Swetman,
who received his graduate training at
Stanford several years ago, was named
president of the Areata Teachers Col
lege.
