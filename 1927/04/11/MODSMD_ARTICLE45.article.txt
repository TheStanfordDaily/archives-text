# TICKETS FOR CONCERT CONTINUE ON SALE
## 
### 
Seats will be constructed on the
stage of the Assembly Hall to accom
modate the numerous applicants for
tickets to the recital of Roland Hayes,
celebrated negro tenor, who will sins
here tomorrow night under the auspi
ces of the Peninsula Musical Associa
tion. No roughs' seats will be available.
An additional row built at the rear
of the hall, together with the seats on
the stage, will place the number of
unsold seats at 150, tickets for which
will be on sale at Crandall's today and
tomorrow for $1.50.
