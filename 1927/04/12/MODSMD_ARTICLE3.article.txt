# EUPHRONIA WILL HOLD MEMBERSHIP TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Quarterly tryouts for the Euphronia
Debating Society will be held tomor
row evening at 7:30 o'clock in the
lobby of the Old Union. Membership
is open to all men registered in the
University.
All those wishing to try out must
give five-minute speeches before the
society on any subject that they de
sire.
