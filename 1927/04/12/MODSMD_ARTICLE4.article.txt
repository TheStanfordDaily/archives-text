# SEVENTY NINE ACCEPT BIDS EROM CAMPUS SORORITIES
## 
### 
Seventy-nine women were pledged
to the ten campus sororities last night,
as compared with the sixty-two who
pledged last. year.
Bidding began in Roble at 5:45
o'clock, and the new women were
taken directly in cars to their respec
tive houses. Five of the women were
undecided.
