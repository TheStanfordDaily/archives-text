# PROMINENT LAWYERS WILL JUDGE DEBATE
## 
### 
Three prominent San Francisco at
torneys have been selected to judge
the Medaille Joft're debate, which will
be held Thursday evening, at 8 o'clock
in the Assembly Hall. Marcus C\ Sloss,
retired Justice of the Supreme Court,
John L. McNab, and Percy V. Long

California Debating League and vice
