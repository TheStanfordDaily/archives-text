# Stanford-Nevada Debate In San Jose Tonight
## 
### 
Suppression of criminal trial news
paper reports is the subject of the
Stanford-Nevada debate to be held this
evening at San Jose at 8 o'clock in the
Kan Jose High School auditorium.
Robert A. Hume and John Franklin
will uphold the negative for Stanford.
Nevada will be represented by Emer
son Wilson, debate manager, and Er
nest Bingham freshman debate coach.
