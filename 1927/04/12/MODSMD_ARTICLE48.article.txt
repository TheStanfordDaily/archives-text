# CREATOR OF "JIMMIE" GIVES CARTOON SERIES
## 
### 
James Swinnerton, painter and car
toonist, creator of the "Little Jimmie"
series of cartoons, is starting a car
tooning series tonight at 8 o'clock in
the Palo Alto Elks' Club building, on
the corner of Melbourne and Embarca
dero avenues.
The series is under the auspices of
the Palo Alto Art Club, which is giving
a,course including sketching from life
and cartooning. It is advised that all
intending to take the course be pres
ent at the first meeting tonight.
