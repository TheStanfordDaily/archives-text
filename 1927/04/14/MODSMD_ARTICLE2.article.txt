# TWO AUTO ACCIDENTS REPORTED YESTERDAY ON STANFORD CAMPUS
## 
### 
Two automobile accidents occurred
on the campus yesterday, one of which
was between Stanford people; the
other involved visitors. Miss Etta H.
Handy, director of University dining
halls, and Cecil Doll, former president
of the junior class, collided in front
of the Chi Psi house on Salvatierra.
No serious damage was done.
R. E. Pelton, of Menlo Park, was
taken to the Palo Alto Hospital suf
fering from minor sprains and bruises,
after he had piloted his motorcycle
into the side of a machine driven by
C. J. Foge of San Francisco. He was
apparently blinded by the headlights
of another car.
