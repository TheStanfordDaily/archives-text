THURSDAY
1:45 P.M. —Board of Trustees, regular
monthly meeting in the offices of
the Board, Balfour Building, San
Francisco.
4:15 P.M. —Organ Recital by Warren j
D. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 P.M. —Thursday Evening Confer
ence at the home of Dr. David Stan-
Jordan on "The Meaning of Democ
racy."
7:30 P.M. —Tryouts for the Euphronia
Debating Society in the lobby of the
Old Union. Tryoutees must deliver a
five-minute speech on any topic of
current interest.
7:30 P.M. —Choir rehearsal for Easter
program in Memorial Church. Or
chestra report at 7:15.
8:00 P.M. —Radio Club will meet in
Radio Shack in Corporation Yard.
8:00 P.M.—Annual Jotrre Medal De-!
bate between the University of Call-1
fornia and Stanford in the Assembly
Hall. General question: "French
Problems in Relation to Italy since
the Armistice."• (Specific questions
to be announced at the debate.)
8:00 P.M. —History Club meets in the
Women's Clubhouse. Miss Esther
Caukin will speak.
FRIDAY
3:00 P.M. —Joint Meeting of the Board
of Trustees and the Stanford Na
tional Board, in the President's Of
fice.
4:15 P.M. —Organ recital by Mr. Allen
in the Memorial Church. A special
Good Friday program of chorale-pre- j
ludes by Bach.
SUNDAY
6:30 P.M. —The Stanford Episcopal
Club will meet in the new Parish j
House. The speaker will be the Rev. |
I). Charles Gardner.
