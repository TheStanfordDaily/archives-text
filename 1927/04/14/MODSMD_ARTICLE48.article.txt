# Sam Hays Given Lead in Mount Tamalpais Pageant
## 
### 
Sam Hayes, prominent in Stanford
dramatics, has been given the lead
part in the Mount Tamalpais Pageant,
to be given May 22. He will play the
part of Agmar, a beggar, the chief
character in "Gods of the Mountain"
by Dunsany.
Garnett Holme, who is directing the
pageant, will collect most of the mob
extras from the University of Cali
fornia.
Sam Hayes will be remembered for
his work in "The Book of Charm," and
for his characterization of the tutor
in "The Swan."
