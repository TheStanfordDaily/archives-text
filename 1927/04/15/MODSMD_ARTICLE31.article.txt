FRIDAY
3:00 r.M. —Joint Meeting of the Board
of Trustees and the Stanford Na
tional Board, in the President's Of
fice.
4:15 P.M. —Organ recital by Mr. Allen
in the Memorial Church. A special
Good Friday program of chorale-pre
ludes by Bach. *•

10:30 A.M.—Drill for R.0.T.0. students
will be dismissed so that the men
may see the activities of the day.
6:30P.M. —All members of the Glee
Club will meet in front of the Bas
ketball pavilion.
SUNDAY
6:30 P.M. —The Stanford Episcopal
Club will meet in the new Parish
House. The speaker will be the Rev.
D. Charles Gardner.
