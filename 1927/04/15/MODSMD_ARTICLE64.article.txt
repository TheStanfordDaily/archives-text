# HATS SUBSTITUTE FOR STUDENTS AT LECTURE
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, D.C., April 14. —
(I.P.) —After waiting for the arrival of
their instructor for fifteen minutes one
day recently, members of a class at
George Washington university ad
journed. The professor then an
nounced that he hud been present, in
asmuch as he had left his hat on his
desk. At the next meeting of the
class, the professor entered his class
room to find the students' seats occu
pied by hats but no students. Leaving
his own hat on the desk, he secured
an extra hour of sleep.

The New York Evening Post is con
ducting a campaign to discover the
cause of mental gloom said to be sweep
