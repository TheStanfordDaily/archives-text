# CARDS FOR SECOND PERIOD OF RUSHING ISSUED WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Fraternities will close their
first period of freshman rushing
Wednesday evening of this week,
and there will be no entertaining
by any of the houses until Sunday
noon, April 24, when the second
period begins. The Interfraternity
Conference will meet at 9 o'clock
tonight in the Old Union, and all
fraternities are requested to have
representatives present to order
form cards for the second period.
These cards will be printed and
ready for distribution to the Row
men between 9 and 10 o'clock,
Wednesday evening.
