# Physician Warns Students Against Eating Hurriedly
## 
### 
Columbia University students are eating
themselves out of an education, in the
opinion of Dr. William McCartline, medi
cal chief of that school. He says that
actual tests show that the average stu
dent bolts his lunch in six minutes, three
seconds. Such haste may ruin the mind
as well as the stomach, he warns.
