# HARVEY LYTEL CHOSEN LABOR DAY CHAIRMAN
## 
### 
Harvey Lytel was yesterday ap
pointed chairman of Labor Day at the
Convalescent Home, which falls on
Wednesday, May 4, by Stanley Weigel,
president of the A.S.S.U. Lytel was
chairman of the Labor Day last year.
Weigel and Lytel are working on the
organization of committees and expect
to announce appointments this week.
An attempt will be made to make
ibis Labor Day more successful than
the last, which was declared to be one
of the very best ever held on the
campus. Classes will be dismissed at
noon, as they were last year, and work
will be carried on during the after
noon. *
