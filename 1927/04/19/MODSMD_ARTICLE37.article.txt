# Marriage of Robert Stice, Stanford '22, Announced
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Ethel Ber
mingham of San Francisco, a Univer
sity of California alumna, to Robert
11. Stice. Stanford '22, took place Mon
day night at St. Luke's Episcopal
Church, San Francisco. Miss Berming
ham is a member of the Delta Delta
Delta sorority. Mr. Stice is a graduate
of Stanford and of the Harvard Gradu
ate School of Business Administration.
His fraternity is Phi Kappa Sigma.
