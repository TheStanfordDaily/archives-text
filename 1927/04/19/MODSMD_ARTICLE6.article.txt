# TENNIS. SOCCER CLASS MANAGERS APPOINTED
## 
### 
Class managers for tennis and soc
cer were yesterday appointed by class
presidents. Soccer managers are re
quested by C. W. Davis, director of
intramural athletics, to start picking
their teams, as interclass soccer play
will start soon. The interclass tennis
play started yesterday. The sophomore
managers are: tennis, Robert P. West;
soccer, Walter J. Fell; junior mana
gers: tennis, Harold F. Way; soccer,
Allen W. Strowger; senior managers:
tennis, Edwin Gardner; soccer, Don
ald W. McCarty.
