# ORGANIST TO INCLUDE POPULAR SELECTIONS IN REGULAR RECITAL
## 
### 
Two selections of popular appeal
will be played at the organ recital by
Warren I). Allen, University organist,
at 4:15 o'clock tomorrow. Mendels
sohn's "Spring Song," and "Berceuse,"
by William Spence, are the lighter
numbers included on the recital pro
gram, the latter for the first time.
The first composition on the pro
gram will be a C6sar Franck Choral,
followed by "Adagio in Ancient Style,"
by Enesco. The Finale from the Fourth
Symphony by Widor will be the con
cluding selection.
