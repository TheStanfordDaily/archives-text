# EUPHRONIA TO STAGE CRIME SQUAD DEBATE AT MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
Newspaper reports of criminal trials
will be on the stand tonight when
three members of the Euphronia De
bating Society face representatives
from the Crime Squad of debaters at
7:45 o'clock in the lobby of the Old
Union on the question, "Resolved,
That newspaper reports of criminal
trials should be censored by the
state." This is the second of a series
of debates sponsored by Euphronia.
Dan Smith, Fred Thon, and Gordon
Shallenberger of the Crime Squad will
take the affirmative, while Harold
Peterson, Lewis Baer, and Laurence
Heiges will take the negative for
Euphronia.
