# CHOIR AND ORATORIO REHEARSAL TONIGHT
## 
### 
Students in Choir and Oratorio will
report for a short rehearsal with or
chestra tonight at 7:30 o'clock in the
Memorial Church to prepare the Halle
lujah Chorus by Beethoven for next
Sunday's service. The regular choir
rehearsal will be held tomorrow eve
ning at 7:30 o'clock, and work will
commence at 8 o'clock on the music
for the special service to be given at
the end of the quarter.
