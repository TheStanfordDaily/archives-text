# O.A.C. Museum Gets Tooth, Tusk of Oregon Elephant
## 
### 
OREGON AGRICULTURAL COL
LEGE, Corvallis, Ore., April 19. —
(P.I.P.) —A tooth and a tusk of an
elephant excavated in southern Ore
gon have been brought to the college
museum by Dr. John B. Horner, di
rector of "Oregon historical research.
The specimens were uncovered at a
hydraulic placer mine in blue clay at
a depth of 40 feet.
