# JUNIOR PROM BIDS CONTINUE ON SALE TO CLASS OF 1928
## 
### 
Tickets for the 1928 Junior
Prom will be continued on sale
to Juniors today and tomorrow
from 1 to 5 o'clock. These will
be reserved for third-year men
only on these days, after which
other upperclassmen and gradu
ates will be allowed to purchase
them. Bids will be $5 apiece.
Class dues must be paid before
Juniors get bids. Dues are now
being collected. Only 300 bids
are to be placed o nsale.
