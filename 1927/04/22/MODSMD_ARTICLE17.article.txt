# REV. BLACK TO GIVE SERMON ON SUNDAY
## 
### 
Reverend Hugh Black, D.D., of New
York will give the sermon in the Me
morial Church, Sunday. Reverend
Black is professor of Practical Theo
logy at the Union Theological Semi
nary in New York and is this year's
speaker for thfe West Memorial series
of lectures. Selections to he sung by
the choir at Sunday's services are:
"In Death's Strong Grasp the Savior
Lay," by J. S. Bach, and "Hallelujah
Chorus" from"The Mount of Olives,"
by Beethoven. An organ recital by
Warren I). Allen will be given at 4
o'clock Sunday afternoon.
