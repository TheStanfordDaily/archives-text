# Barrymore to Broadcast Soliloquy from "Hamlet"
## 
### 
In honor of the three hundred and
sixty-third anniversary of Shakes
peare's birth on April 22, 1564, John
Barrymore will broadcast the soliloquy
from "Hamlet," and passages from
"Richard III" between eight and nine
o'clock this evening from a New York
radio station.
The program can be received by
tuning in on the Pacific broadcasting
chain.
