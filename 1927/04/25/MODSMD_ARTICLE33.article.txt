# DECAY OF EDUCATION IN HOME BLAMED ON MOVIES, AUTOMOBILES
## 
### 
"Movies, automobiles, and dancing
are partly to blame for the 'decay of
education in the home'," President W.
W. Campbell, of the University of Cali
fornia,, told the final session of the
convention of state high school prin
cipals in Sacramento Saturday.
"American parents are paying less
attention to their children than ever
before," he claimed, "although home
conditions may improve in the next
few years." President Campbell fin
ished with a plea for the awakening of
parental consciousness.
