# ECONOMICS STUDENTS LEAD CANDIDATE LIST FOR GRADUATION
## Engineering and Pre-Legal Departments Follow Second and Third

One hundred ninety-nine people
were named on the first provisional
list of candidates for graduation in
June which has just been released by
the Registrar's Office. Economics
leads in the number of candidates with
forty-two students, Engineering takes
second with forty, Pre-legal has twen
ty-three, Chemistry 14, Pre-clinical
thirteen, Romantic Languages twelve,
History eleven, English eight. Mining
and Geology each have six, Education
and Zoology 5, Social and Political
Sciences each four, Psychology and
Mathematics threfe. Classical Litera
ture two, while Bacteriology, Germanic
Languages, Philosophy, and Physio
logy have only one each.
May 10 Is Deadline
Additional applications for gradua
tion must, be filed with the reigstrar
not later than May 10.
