# SENIOR TENNIS TEAM DEFEATS FRESHMEN; TITLE TILT IS TODAY
## 
### 
With a victory Friday afternoon the
senior interclass tennis team definitely
eliminated the freshman players in
the class racket tourney and set the
stage for the title to be decided today
at 3:30 o'clock when the sophomores
and juniors meet on the new courts.
The 192S men hold a victory over the
seniors and the 1929 squad has beaten
the freshmen.
The senior margin of victory Friday
was four matches to two, the first
doubles remaining to be completed
this afternoon. The doubles were
again the seniors' strong point, all
matches played going to them, while
the yearlings showed better in the
singles, winning two to one.
Both teams played ragged tennis,
the doubles being particularly slow
and marked by errors. The results:
Singles—
Lee ('3O) defeated Wheldon ('27),
6-4. 4-6, 6-3.
Murphy ('27) defeated Weyl ('3O),
6-4, 6-1.
Nyquist ('3O) defeated Bledsoe ('27),
6-2, 2-6, 6-3.
Doubles —
Gardner and Bledsoe ('27) defeated
Weyl and Gilmour ('3O), 6-2, 6-2.
Roberts and McCray ('27) defeated
Perin and Sorenson ('3O), 5-7, 6-3, 6-3.
Leeds and Rowland ('27) defeated
Smith and Richardson ('3O), 4-6, 6-1,
7-5.
