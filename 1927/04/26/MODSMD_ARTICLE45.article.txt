# ROTH ELECTED HEAD OF ROTARY DISTRICT
## 
### 
Comptroller Almon E. Roth was
unanimously elected to the district
governorship of the second district of
Rotary International during a three
day convention held recently at Stock
ton.
The district convention will be held
at Long Beach as it is the policy or
the Rotarians to share this favor be
tween the northern and southern parts
of the state, Roth stated.
