# STANFORD MEN WILL HUNT WILD ANIMALS
## 
### 
Hunting wild animals and photo
graphing African wild life for Stan
ford University will be the purpose of
an exploring party of which two Stan
ford men are members. A letter re
ceived from Kenneth It. Walker, *27,
who with his brother, Fletcher L.
Walker, Jr., '26, states that the group
will soon travel into the heart of
Africa to gather material.
