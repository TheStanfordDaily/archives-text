# FRATERNITY ALUMNI OF BAY REGION MEET TONIGHT FOR BANQUET
## 
### 
Greek letter alumni of the bay re
gion will gather at the Mark Hopkins
Hotel in San Francisco for their an
nual interfraternity banquet tonight.
T. T. C. Gregory, Stanford alumnus
and member of Beta Theta Pi fra
ternity, the only fraternity member of
the Stanford Board of Trustees, will
discuss the proposal to abolish the
lower division and its effect on the
fraternity situation.
A cup will be awarded to the frater
nity group having the largest attend
ance at the banquet.
