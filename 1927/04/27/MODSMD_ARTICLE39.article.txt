# SIGMA XI TO AWARD FELLOWSHIPS IN JUNE
## 
### 
Sigma Xi, Society for the Promotion
of Research, will award fellowships
1 for 1927-1928 in June. Applica
tion blanks, which should be made out
before May 20, may be obtained from
jean Edward Ellery, Union College,
; Schenectady, N.Y. These fellowships
are available for workers in all fields
of science. There are no restrictions
as to the university or the country in
which the holder wishes to work.
