# LEWIS' BOOK OKEYED
## 
### 
PROVIDENCE. R. 1., April 25.—(1.P.)
—When the city of Providence, Rhode
Island, proposed to ban Sinclair
Lewis' latest book, President W. H. P.
Faunce of Brown University protested
that such an action would be contrary
to the history and spirit of the state
that was the home of Roger Williams.
