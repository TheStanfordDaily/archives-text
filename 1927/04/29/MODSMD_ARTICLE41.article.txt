# DR. BLACK TO PREACH IN CHAPEL SERVICES
## 
### 
Reverend D. D. Black of New York
City, who has been visiting the Uni
versity for the last week, will conduct
morning services in the Memorial
Church, Sunday. Divine worship and
sermon will commence at 11 o'clock.
The anthems to be sung are: "God
Be in Thy Head," by Davies, and
"Benedictus es Domine," by Martin.
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will give his usual Sunday after
noon recital at 4 o'clock.
