# WING TAKES OROVILLE GIRL AS EASTER BRIDE
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Dorothy Steyd
man of Oroville, and Robert Lewis
Wing, '21, son of Professor and Mrs.
Charles E. Wing of Palo Alto, was
solemnized on Easter Sunday at the
bride's home in Oroville.
Wing is a member of Chi Psi fra
ternity, and Mrs. Wing is a graduate
of San Jose State Teachers College.
Wing has been doing water construc
tion work for the State Department of
Public Works for some time and was
formerly an engineer for the Southern
Pacific Company. The couple will live
in Sacramento.
Football paid the University of
Pennsylvania $214,996 in profits in the
season just closed.
