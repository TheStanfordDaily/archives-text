# WILBUR WILL DISCUSS JUNIOR COLLEGE PLAN IN LECTURE TONIGHT
## 
### 
"Junior Colleges" will be the sub
ject of a talk by President Wilbur to
night before a meeting of the Educa
tion Club at 7:30 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. The lecture will be
open to the public.
Dr. Wilbur will discuss the advan
tages of the junior college plan and
the effect that doing away with the
lower division at Stanford is..expected
to have on the development of the
junior college.
