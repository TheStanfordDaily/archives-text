# Ackerman Briggs Reveals Betrothal to Californian
##

Ackerman Briggs to Miss Elizabeth
Schriber of Santa Monica was made
last night at Beta Theta Pi, Briggs*
fraternity. He is a member of the
Class of '27. Miss Schriber is a mem
ber of Alpha Delta Pi sorority at the
University of California and is a sen
