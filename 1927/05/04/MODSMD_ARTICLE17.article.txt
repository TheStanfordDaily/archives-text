# TRUSTEES HOSTS TO ACADEMIC COUNCIL

at a dinner given the Academic Coun
cil at Branner Hall tomorrow night
at 7 o'clock.

Board of Trustees, will preside at the
gathering. Problems of mutual interest
to faculty members and alumni will
be discussed.
