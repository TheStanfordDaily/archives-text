# SOPHOMORE WOMEN WIN FIRST SWIMMING CONTEST FROM 1930
## 
### 
Sophomore women downed the juniors
by a 56-11 score in the first women's
interclass swimming meet of the season
yesterday afternoon, and won the first
round toward the championship.
In the seven events two firsts, one
third, and two fourths fell to the four
junior swimmers; all other places were
taken by the winners. The sophomores
won the relay by half a second, Jean
Wakefield almost tying for the juniors
after closing up a five-yard lead.
Katherine Doyle was high-point win
ner, taking a first in the 25-yard free
style, 25-yard backstroke, and 75-yard
112 rcestyle.
