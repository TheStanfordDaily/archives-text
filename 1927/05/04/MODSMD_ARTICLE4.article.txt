# FRATERNITIES' BIDS DUE AT OLD UNION DURING AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Fraternity bids must be handed
in to the secretary of the Inter
fraternity Hoard at the Old Union
between 2 and 4 o'clock this after
noon.
Bids must be typed on the of
ficial cards furnished tho houses,
with the name of the fraternity
on the bottom of the card and the
freshman's on the top, and must
be submitted in alphabetical
order.
Pledging will be from 1 to 4
o'clock on Friday afternoon, also
at the Old Union, under a lawynr
to bo appointed by the board.
