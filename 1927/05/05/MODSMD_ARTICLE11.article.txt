# PHYSICAL EDUCATION EXPERTS VISIT ROBLE
## 
### 
Dr. Jesse Feiring Williams of Colum
bia University, together with Dunkard
Neilson, State Superintendent of
Physical Education, Miss Rosamond
Cassady, head of the Gymnasium De
partment at Mills College, and Miss
Violet Marshall, director of Physical
Education at the University of Cali
fornia, visited the Stanford campus
yesterday. The purpose of the visit
was to inspect the equipment and
work of Roble Gymnasium and its
staff. Dr. Williams is in the West to
dedicate the now women's gymnasium
at Oregon Agricultural College, and
is studying the work in physical edu
cation for women on the Pacific Coast
while he is here.
