# TODAY MARKS OFFICIAL TRADITIONAL CORD BALL START OF JUNIOR WEEK
## 
### 
Junior Week officially begins at
2:30 o'clock this afternoon when
Hu Berner and his orchestra will
open the annual Cord Ball in the
Women's Clubhouse.
This traditional event is entirely
limited to upperclassmen, who are
requested to appear in their ordi
nary garb, cord trousers.
Dancing will begin at 2:30 and
will last till 5 o'clock. Tickets may
be obtained at the door for 75
cents.
