# ACADEMIC COUNCIL, TRUSTEES MEET AT DINNER IN BRANNER
## 
### 
At the dinner gathering of the mem
bers of the Board of Trustees and the
Academic Council at Branner Hall at
7 o'clock tonight the abolition of the
lower division at Stanford will be the
principal subject under discussion.
W. M. Newhall, president of the
Board of Trustees, and Dr. Wilbur will
be among the speakers who will pre
sent their opinions upon elimination
of the freshman and sophomore years.
Since the Board of Trustees has not
yet indicated officially its sentiment
toward the plan, its meeting with the
Academic Council will give it the per
spective of professional educator on
the situation before making a final
decision.
