# FOUR TEAMS CHOSEN FOR WOMEN'S TRACK
## 
### 
Four independent teams have been
chosen to practice for women's track. The
first meei of the season took place yes
terday, when Marian Holley's team placed
first with 56.3 points. The nearest com
petition was given by Harriet Sutton's
team, which placed second with 15.3
points. The teams headed by Dorothy
Fordyce and Ruth Lansburgh came out
with 6 and 3.3 points respectively. All
the women entered each event. Out of
nine events, Marian Holley took seven
first places, and tied for an eighth.
