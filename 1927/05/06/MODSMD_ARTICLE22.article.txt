# FRESHMAN WOMEN WIN FROM 1927 SWIMMERS
## 
### 
Freshman women defeated the senior
swimming team 47-6 in Thursday's inter
class encounter. The 1930 women took
first in all the events with the exception
of the diving contest. Three former Stan
ford records were broken. Maxinc Gush
ing broke both the records for the 75-yard
and the 25-yard swim, and Mariana
Evans broke the 50-yard mark. These
two women were the high point winners
of the day, taking two firsts and a second,
and one first and two seconds, respec
tively.
