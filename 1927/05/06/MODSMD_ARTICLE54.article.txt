# FRENCH LECTURER TO TALK ON LITERATURE
## 
### 
Early in the Summer Quarter, Madame
Marie De Mare Stein will give a series of
three lectures, delivered in French, on
contemporary French literature.
Madame Stein is at present giving a
cries of lectures in Washington, D.C.,
and has lectured widely throughout the
United States under the auspices of the
Alliance Francaise.
