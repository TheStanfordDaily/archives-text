# DRAKE ELECTED NEW PRESIDENT OF LOCAL Y.M.C.A. ORGANIZATION
## 
### 
Unanimity prevailed in the Stanford
University Young Men's Christian As
sociation yesterday as the voters in the
annual election cast their ballots solidly
in favor of Thomas Drake for president;
Arthur Holmes, vice-president; Robert
Scares, recording secretary; and Wallace
Cathcart, treasurer, Rex C. Bell, general
secretary, announced last night when the
votes were counted.
There were no other candidates. The
total vote was eighteen.
"That no other nominations were made
indicates either lack of rivalry for the
positions or lack of interest," Bell stated.
Two hundred twenty-five letters were
mailed out permitting nominations by
petition. None of the letters were an
swered.
The successful candidates will take of
fice immediately.
