# FRATERNITIES PLEDGE 218 MEN TO SET NEW RECORD NUMBER
## Formal Rushing for Class Of 1930 Ends With Bidding Friday ENTIRE LIST ANNOUNCED Twenty Men Hold Out for Further Consideration Before Deciding 
### 
Surpassing all former records in the
matter of formal pledging, 218 men
accepted fraternity bids at the con
clusion of the organized rushing period
Friday afternoon. Twenty men who
received bids signified their intention
of holding out for further considera
tion.
This year's number exceeds that of
last year by sixteen of those that
pledged on the official day and by one
that held out for a later date. In 1925,
IS3 men accepted bids to fraternities
and in 1924, 176 men.
Sigma Alpha Epsilon announced the
largest number of pledges totaling
nineteen, while Theta Xi was second
with eighteen. No pledges were an
nounced by Delta Upsilon and Phi
Gamma Delta announced only two.
