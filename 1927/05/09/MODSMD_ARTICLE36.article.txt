# MADAME STEIN WILL ADDRESS FRENCH CLUB
## 
### 
Madame De Mare Stein, well-known
French lecturer, will speak on "Na
poleon and Josephine" at the meeting
of the French Club to be held at 8:15
o'clock Wednesday night at the Alpha
Phi house.
Madame Stein, who has been sent to
the United States by the Alliance
Francaise to lecture on literary and
artistic subjects, has spoken to the
club before on "Modern French Lit
erature." She is now a resident of
Palo Alto and has just returned from
a tour of the East.
An election of officers for next year
will be held during the evening.
