# Dr. Jordan Will Speak to Zoologists on "Evolution"
## 
### 
"Evolution" will be the subject of
the talk to be given by Doctor Jordan
before a meeting of the Zoology Club
at his home at 7:30 o'clock tomorrow
night. He will discuss the question as
an outdoor subject as contrasted to
the minute, laboratory science.
