# EXPERT GIVES TALK ON CARE OF EYES
## 
### 
Care of the eyes will be the sub
stance of a talk by Mrs. E. C. Lierman,
eye authority, at the Unitarian Hall at
8 o'clock Thursday night.
Mrs. Lierman, who was connected
with Dr. W. 11. Bates, prominent in
clinical work in New York City, will
illustrate her lecture with moving pic
tures.
