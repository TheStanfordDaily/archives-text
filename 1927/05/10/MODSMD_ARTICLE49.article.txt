# STANFORD PROFESSOR HEADS BUSINESS GROUP
## 
### 
Professor George W. Dowrie, in
charge of the work in finance in the
Stanford Graduate School of Business,
was elected president of the American
Association of Collegiate Schools of
Business at the annual meeting held
May 5, 6, and 7 in Cambridge, Massa
chusetts.
Professor Dowrie has served as vice
president of this association during
the past year and is also a member
of the executive committee of the
American Economic Association.
