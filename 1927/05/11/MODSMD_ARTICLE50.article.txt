# FOUR STANFORD LAW STUDENTS SELECTED FOR ORDER OF COIF
## 
### 
Four Stanford law students, Paul T.
Wolf, Richard W. Fennemore, James E.
Brenner, and Robert H. Rinn, have
been chosen for membership in the
Order of the Coif, a national organiza
tion, the highest award made for
scholastic attainments in the law de
partment.
The men were selected from forty
eight candidates on the basis of the
records made during their work on the
local campus.
Wolf, Fennemore, and Brenner will
receive their degrees in June. Rinn
has already been given his and is
doing graduate work.
