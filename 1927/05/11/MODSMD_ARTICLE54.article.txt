# Roller Skating Forbidden At University of Illinois
## 
### 
URBANA, 111—(I.P.)—Because of
the increased danger to students as a
result of the roller skating craze at
the University of Illinois, the mayor
of Urbana has issued a decree forbid
ding anyone to skate in the streets.
The mayor has declared that his ac
tion is taken to protect the rights of
motorists as well as the lives of the
skaters.
