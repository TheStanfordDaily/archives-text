# REVEREND BEAL OF LOS ANGELES WILL PRESIDE AT CHAPEL
## 
### 
The Rev. Harry Beal of St. Paul's
Cathedral, Los Angeles, will preach at
the service in the Memorial Churcli
at 11 o'clock Sunday morning.
Carol Lasater is the soloist, and the
anthems will be "I Sought the Lord,"
by Gaul, and "Teach Us, Good Lord,"
by Stevenson.
At the Monday morning chapel ser
vice at 7:50 o'clock, Mrs. Leila L.
Thompson, of the Unitarian Church in
Palo Alto, will be the speaker.
