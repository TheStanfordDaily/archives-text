# BRITISH AMBASSADOR VISITOR AT STANFORD
## 
### 
Sir Esme Howard, the British am
bassador to the United States, visited
the campus Tuesday afternoon and
was officially welcomed by John A.
Sellards, Director of the Summer
Quarter, in the absence of President
Wilbur.
The ambassador was accompanied
by H. L. Hopkinson, secretary of the
British embassy in Washington, and
the two wore shown the Memorial
Chapel, the Hoover War Library, and
other buildings of interest.
Ten fraternities at the Penn State
College conducted a radio program,
which was broadcasted over the sta
tion of the college, to determine which
house had the best songs and singers.
