# SUPERSTITION DANCE SCHEDULED BY TOYON
## 
### 
Toyon Club will give an informal Su
perstition Dance tomorrow night, John
Van Amringe, president of the club, stated
yesterday. The decorations will be appro
priate for Friday the thirteenth. The
committee in charge of the dance is:
Donald Robesky, chairman, Edward B.
Krough, Dick C. Warden, Marshall Som
erville, and Eugene C. McKnight.
