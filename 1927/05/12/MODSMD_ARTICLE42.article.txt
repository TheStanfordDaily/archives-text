# SECOND-ROUND PLAY IN HORSESHOE MEET SCHEDULED BY DAVIS
## 
### 
Horseshoe teams will meet today in
the final play of the second round of
the intramural tournament, C. W.
Davis, director of intramural sports,
announced yesterday. All living groups
that held tournaments are asked to
check up on the number playing.
Today's games:
Alpha Sigma Phi and Phi Kappa
Sigma will meet at 3 o'clock to play
their postponed match.
Hall, Delta Chi, vs. Lathrop, Alpha
Kappa Lambda, at 4 o'clock.
Biaggini, Delta Chi, vs. Jones, Bran
ner I, at 4 o'clock.
H. Rowland, Branner 11, vs. winner
of Alpha Sig-Phi Kap match at 4
o'clock.
Robinson, Encina, vs. Varian, Inde
pendents, at 5 o'clock.
Cavalero, El Tigre, vs. Bradley, En
cina IV, at 5 o'clock.
Gillette, Theta Xi, vs. Troyer, Phi
Kappa Psi, at 5 o'clock.
Todd, Sequoia 11, vs. Doane, Phi
Kappa Psi, at 5 o'clock.
