# Bears to Send Two Men East for Net Tournament
## 
### 
Tom Stowe and Dick Hoog were
named Tuesday to defend the national
intercollegiate tennis singles and doubles
title held by the University of California.
The match will be held at the Merriam
Cricket Club, Philadelphia.
Edward "Bud" Chandler, now gradu
ated, won the singles championship last
year, and he and Stowe the doubles.
