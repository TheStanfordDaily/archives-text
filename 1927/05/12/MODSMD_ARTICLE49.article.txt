# GRADUATE STUDENTS' CLUB MEETS TONIGHT
## 
### 
Dancing from 7 to 8 o'clock, card play
ing, refreshments, and a short business
session will make up the second meeting
of the new Graduate Students' Club set
for tonight at 7 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. A prize dance will be a
feature of the evening.
The purpose of the new organization is
social, and all graduate students are eli
gible for membership. Tonight's meeting
will be open to all, the committee in
charge announces, organization of the
club not being complete as yet. After a
few introductory meetings the details and
structure will be determined.
