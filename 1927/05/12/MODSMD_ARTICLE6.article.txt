# OVER TWO THOUSAND STUDENTS GRADUATE AT U.C. CEREMONIES
## 
### 
Two thousand and eighty-three sen
iors at the University of California re
ceived their graduation degrees from
President W. W. Campbell in an elabo
rate ceremony staged yesterday morn
ing in the Berkeley memorial stadium.
The graduate .degrees showed a gain
of ninety over those conferred last
year, but the total number, graduate
and undergraduate, was one hundred
and nineteen less than in 1926.
Of the regular diplomas four hun
dred and forty were symbolical of the
completion of professional and gradu
ate courses, while the remainder were
granted to those completing bachelor
of arts and bachelor of science de
grees. The honorary doctorate of laws
was given to three distinguished scien
tists.
