# COLLEGE FOR WOMEN ADDED AT CLAREMONT
## 
### 
Fifty women will have the oppor
tunity of attending a new exclusive
college for women at Claremont next
September.
Scripps' College for Women, the
second unit of Claremont colleges, is
located at Claremont within a few
blocks of Pomona College. Dr. Ernest
J. Jaqua will be president of the new
institution.
