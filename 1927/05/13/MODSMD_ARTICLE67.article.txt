# GLEE CLUB TRYOUTS SCHEDULED MONDAY
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Men's Glee Club will
be held in Room 84, Inner Quad, Mon
day afternoon, beginning at 4 o'clock.
All men interested in group chorus
singing are urged by Arthur Holmes,
manager, to attend the meeting at that
time. Although Warren D. Allen will
be the advisor of the Group, Albert C.
Kullman, student director, will be the
active head of the organization.
