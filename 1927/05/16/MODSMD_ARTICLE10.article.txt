# Men's Glee Club Tryouts Are Scheduled for Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Men's Glee Club for
all those interested in group chorus
singing will be held this afternoon
beginning at 4 o'clock in Room 84, in
the Inner Quad, announces Arthur
Holmes, manager. Warren D. Allen
will be the advisor of the group, while
Alber C. Kullman, student director,
will be the active head of the organiza
tion.
