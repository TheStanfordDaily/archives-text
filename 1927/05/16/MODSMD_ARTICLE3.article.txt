# LETTER WRITTEN BY MARKHAM INCLUDED IN LIBRARY EXHIBIT
## 
### 
Included in the exhibition of the
Bender Collection of printed books in
the display cases of the University
Library is a private letter by Edwin
Markam, well-known poet, to Mr. A. M.
Bender of San Francisco. The letter
was written August 14, 1915, in antici
pation of the reprinting of Markam's
celebrated poem, "The Man With the
Hoe," which was reprinted in San
Francisco in 191G under Bender's
supervision. Mr. Markam requested
the change of the word "menace" in
one place in the poem to "danger."
A copy of Millet's famous painting of
the same name which inspired the
poem is also on display.
