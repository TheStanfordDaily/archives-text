# Rugby Dropped from 1928 Program of Olympic Games
## 
### 
Rugby has been dropped from the
program of the 1928 Olympic games,
was the statement of a letter received
yesterday by Harry Maloney, member
of the Olympic Committee, from Fred
erick Rubien, secretary of the Ameri
can Olympic Committee. In view of
this, Maloney stated that it was prob
lematical now as to whether it will be
advisable for Stanford to continue
rugby. However, he said the matter
was in the hands of the Board of Ath
letic Control.
