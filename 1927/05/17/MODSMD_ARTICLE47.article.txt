# POSTERS EXHIBITED BY HOOVER WAR LIBRARY
## 
### 
A collection of Victory Loan Posters
are on exhibit in the Hoover War
Library display cases of the Stanford
Library. These are facsimilies of all
French, English, Italian, and Ameri
can Victory Loan Posters issued to
raise loans during and after the World
War.
The Hoover War Library has the
originals of most of these posters in
its possession. The library also has
some rare French and Hungarian
posters.
