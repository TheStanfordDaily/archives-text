# SENIORS POSTPONE JOLLY-UP ONE WEEK
## 
### 
The senior jolly-up planned for to
night has been postponed until next
Wednesday night from 7 to 8 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse because
the clubhouse could not be obtained
tonight, George R. Bogue, class presi
dent, announced yesterday. The affair
will be in charge of Phil Brooks, who
will furnish the music. Transportation
after the jolly-up will be provided for
all senior women, who are asked to be
there at 7 o'clock.
