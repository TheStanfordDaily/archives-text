# Location Doubtful
## 
### 
The exact location of Stanford University is evidently not known by
many people living in the East, Sonth, and in the Middle West, if the
addresses attached to exchanged papers indicate anything. Every day
many papers and news letters come addressed to Stanford at San Fran
cisco, at Berkeley, and at Los Angeles. But worst of all is the Wisconsin
Sport News which comes week after week mailed to "Leland Stanford,
Pasadena, California." None have couie addressed to the Rose Bowl
as yet.
