# DOMONOSKE GOES TO ENGINEERING MEETING
## 
### 
Professor Arthur B. Doinonoske, of
the Mechanical Engineering depart
ment, left last night for the University
of Illinois, where he will inspect the
engineering shops and visit with old
acquaintances. Later he will look over
the latest mechanical developments in
Milwaukee, and then will attend the
summer meeting of the Society of
Automotive Engineers in French Lick
Springs, Indiana. He will be gone for
two weeks.
