# Roble Women Will Meet to Plan Asilomar Conference
## 
### 
At 6 o'clock tonight Stanford women
will participate in an Asilomar walk
out, leaving Roble Hall for Roble
Bridge. Plans will then be discussed
for the coming Asilomar Student Con
ference which will be held from June
16 to June 25. U.S.C., California, and
U.C.L.A. will each send fifty delegates
to the conference.
