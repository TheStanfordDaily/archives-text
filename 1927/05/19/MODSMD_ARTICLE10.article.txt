# C.D. MARX TO ADDRESS ENGINEERS' ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
Professor Emeritus Charles D.
Marx will address a general engineer
ing assembly In the Little Theatre at
4 o'clock this afternoon on the
subject of the Stevens Creek Test
Dam. The explanation of the progress
of the work will be illustrated by mo
tion pictures.
The assembly is under the auspices
of the student chapter of the American
Society of Civil Engineers, and is
open to the general public.
