# FRESHMEN BUSINESS TRYOUTEES DESIRED FOR CHAPPIE STAFF
## 
### 
Freshmen, both men and women,
who desire to try out for positions on
the business staff of Chaparral, are
requested by Norman Jlanak, business
manager, to meet in the Chaparral
business office in the Press building
this afternoon from 1 to 2 o'clock.
Ilanak says that freshmen who work
on the publication the rest of this
month and the first part of June will
be eligible for advertising manager
next fall, and, in their junior year,
for business manager.
