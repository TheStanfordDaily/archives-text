# Y.W.C.A. Senior Cabinet To Hold Spring Training
## 
### 
The new Senior Cabinet of the
Y.W.C.A. will hold its annual Spring
Cabinet training week-end at Asilomar,
from May 20 to May 22. Miss L. M.
Sherman of the Berkeley Y.W.C.A.
and Miss Henrietta Thomson, national
student secretary, will help with the
cabinet training. Installation services
for the new cabinet members will be
held Sunday morning as the culmina
tion of the week end.
