# CUBBERLEY TEXTBOOK IS PUBLISHED AS ONE OF RIVERSIDE SERIES
## 
### 
"State School Administration" is the
title of Dr. Elwood P. Cubberlcy's latest
book, which was published recently by
Houghton-Mifflin Company as one of
the Riverside Textbooks in Education
series.
The volume, which is designed for use
in normal schools and universities, pre
sents a thoroughly organized develop
ment of the topic of the American state
school system. It deals with the origins,
policies, and practices of school adminis
tration and discusses the probable lines
along which evolution will occur. The
text is the outgrowth of more than twen
ty years of instruction at Stanford in the
subject of state school administration.
