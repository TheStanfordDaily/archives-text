# Another Little Drink
## 
### 
College editors from fourteen eastern colleges and universities gath
ered ut Amherst last week, and went on record unanimously as condemn
ing drunkenness among college men as conduct unbecoming a gentleman.
This severe condemnation was a part of the official business of the East
ern Intercollegiate Newspaper Association's Convention.
Now that the editors have decided to condemn drunkenness, what are
they going to do about it ?
