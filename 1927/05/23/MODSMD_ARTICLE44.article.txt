# Allen to Conduct Chorus At New Theatre Dedication
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will conduct the Philharmonic
chorus at the dedication of the new
Woodland Theatre at Hillsborough,
which has been built by the school

board of that community, on Sunday
afternoon. May 29, at 3:15 o'clock. The
dedication will be under the auspices
of the Philharmonic Society of San
Mateo.
