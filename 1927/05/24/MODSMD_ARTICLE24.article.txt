# TRACK MENTOR GOES EAST TO I.C. 4A. MEET
## Squad of Fifteen Men Will Arrive at Chicago for Workout Today RECOVERY ALLOWS TRIP Illness of Four Cardinals May Hamper Showing in Eastern Contest 
### 
OGDEN, Utah, May 23.—(Spe
cial dispatch)—The team is in
good shape and everyone is happy
and confident. At every stop the
boys have been a three-ring circus
for the passengers. Sent a tele
gram to "Dink" Templeton last
night. Glad to hear he is improv
ing. We had a good work-out here.
Fleishhacker was nearly lost in
Reno.
