# Dr. Wilbur Offers Services To New Diagnostic Center
## 
### 
President Wilbur, who is permanent
chairman of the medical advisory
council of the United States Veterans
Bureau, has volunteered his services
as supervisor in the institution and
management of the' diagnostic center
planned for the Veterans Hospital
near Palo Alto. Nervous and mental
rases only are treated at this base
now, but word has come from Wash
ington, D.C., that fifty additional beds
are to be installed and devoted to
diagnostic work.
