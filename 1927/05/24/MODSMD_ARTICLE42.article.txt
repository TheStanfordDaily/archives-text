# Girl Scout Classes to Begin in Palo Alto Today
## 
### 
Classes in Girl Scout leadership will
start again today at the Palo Alto
Scout House, states Miss Phillipson,
Girl Scout Master. Instruction in troop
leadership will be given at 1 o'clock
every Tuesday from now on, and
those successfully completing the
course may qualify for scoutmasters
next year. Classes are open to the
public.
