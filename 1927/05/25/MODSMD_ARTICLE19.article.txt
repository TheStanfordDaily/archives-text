# ALLEN SETS TIMES FOR FUTURE RECITALS
## 
### 
Announcement that after next Sun
day the usual organ recitals given in
the Memorial Chapel at 4:30 o'clock
would be rendered at 7:30 o'clock in
the evening, was made yesterday by
Warren D. Allen. University organist.
This change will be effective during
the months of the summer quarter,
June, July, and August.
There will be a recital every Sunday
evening up to and including August 1!>.
except there will be no program given
the Sunday of June 19, which is
Baccalaureate Sunday.
