# Graduate Group Meets to Select Officers Tonight
## 
###

the newly formed Graduate Students'
Association will meet tonight at 7:30
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
There will be no assessments. Bridge
as entertainment will be played after
ward. Louise Snyder, who is the tem
porary chairman of the group, is at
present in the hospital.
