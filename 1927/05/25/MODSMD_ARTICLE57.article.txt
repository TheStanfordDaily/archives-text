# EX COMMITTEE NOTES
## 
### 
M.S.C. That the election returns on
the new constitution, as submitted by
the Election Board, be approved.
M.S.C. That the following dates be
granted: May 27, Delta Gamma formal
at Los Altos Country Club; May 28,
Alpha Tau Omega, sport dance.
M.S.C. That declinations be closed
Friday, May 27, at 6 o'clock p.m., and
that nominations be closed on Thurs
day. May 26, at 6 o'clock p.m.
M.S.C. That the election of Fred
Speers as editor of the Stanford Daily
be approved.
M.S.C. That the following men be
appointed junior track managers for
1927-2S: Donald Hamblin. John Dugan,
Paul Lloyd, Paul Harwood, Ted Rath
man. Alternates: Leslie W. Garner
and Harlow Alabaster.
M.S.C. That Murray Draper be ap
pointed senior track manager for
1927-28.
M.S.C. That all applications for per
formances of a dramatic nature on the
campus must be filed three weeks in
advance of the date requested with
the Dramatic Council and the Execu
tive Committee.

tee expresses confidence in the pres

of the Y.M.C.A.
