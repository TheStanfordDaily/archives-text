# CLYDE KING TO COACH OLYMPIC CLUB TEAM
## 
### 
Clyde King, former stroke of the
Navy crew, and football star of the
Eastern eleven, has been selected as
coach of the Olympic Club football
team. King wiil take the place of Babe
Hollingsberry, who was appointed
coach at Washington State. Officials
of the club, in naming King, say they
expect him to raise the standard of
the Olympic team, which has fallen
considerably during past seasons.
