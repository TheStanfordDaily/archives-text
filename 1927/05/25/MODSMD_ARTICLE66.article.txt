# W.C. SMITH TO SPEAK TO ENGINEERS' GROUP
## 
### 
W. C. Smith, General Electric Com
pany expert, will address the meeting
of the Stanford branch of the Ameri
can Institute of Electrical Engineers
in the Little Theatre at 7:30 o'clock
tonight on the subject, "Transformers
and Their Construction."
Smith will illustrate his lecture with
motion pictures of the methods of
transformer construction used by the
General Electric Company.
