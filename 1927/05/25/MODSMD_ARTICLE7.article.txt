# Commercial Secretaries To Attend Campus School
## 
### 
More than one hundred chamber of
commerce secretaries from eleven
western states will attend the summer
session school for commercial secre
taries to be held here July 17 to 23.
This school is endorsed by the most
prominent western business men, and
the United States Chamber of Com
merce. The board of governors is now
arranging for the faculty and cur
riculum.
