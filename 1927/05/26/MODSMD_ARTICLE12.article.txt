# SIX FRESHMEN GIVEN DISCIPLINE BY ENCINA SPONSOR COMMITTEE
## 
### 
Six freshmen were summoned be
fore the Encina Hall sponsors at their
meeting Tuesday night, and the fol
lowing punishment was given:
One freshman, ten hours at the Con
valescent Home for general disorder.
One freshman, six hours at the Con
valescent Home for general disorder.
Three freshmen, four hours each at
the Convalescent Home for damaging
their room.
One freshman must pay for damage
in wanton destruction of University
property and see Dean of Men.
One freshman warned for general
disorder and noise.
