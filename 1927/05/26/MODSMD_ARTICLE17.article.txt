## 
### 
Two thousand three hundred sing
ers took part in a huge music fete ex
tending over one week held near Chi
cago under the auspices of the North
Shore organization. A feature of the
program was a children's chorus of
1500 voices. The A Cappella choir con
sisting entirely of Northwestern Uni
versity students, the Chicago sym
phony orchestra, 10 operatic stars,
and a chorus of 600' people, also par
ticipated in the festival. This was one
of the outstanding musical events of
the year and was attended by many
prominent people living in Chicago
