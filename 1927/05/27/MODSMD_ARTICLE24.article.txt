# HELEN VOGEL CHOSEN AS NEXT YEAR'S HEAD OF PILAMBDA THETA
## 
### 
Helen Vogel, one of the founders of
the Stanford chapter of Pi Lambda
Theta, women's national educational
fraternity, was elected to the presi
dency of that organization at a meet
ing Tuesday night.
Guinevere Hotter, who has just com
pleted a term as president, was chosen
as vice-president, and Louise Snyder
and Miriam Gat ley were elected to
the positions of secretary and cor
responding secretary, respectively.
Mrs. Harold P. Benjamin, who will
serve as treasurer during the coming
year, has also been chosen to repre
sent the local chapter at the national
Pi Lambda Theta convention which
will be held at Seattle the second week
