# CAREWORN SERPENT SEEKING SURCEASE ENTERS ROBLE GYM
## 
### 
Great suffering snakes! Even
the worn n are seeing them now!
But the Mia Ice that the women
saw wasn't any relation to the
well-known green lizard, nor did
it seem to be suffering severely.
In fact it looked perfectly happy
curled up there on the cool ce
ment floor of Roble gym.
It was a friendly, innocent sort
of snake, without even an apple
tucked up its sleeve, but alas,
like so many of us, it was mis
understood. Screeching women
hurled shoes at it from every
side, as the poor creature, search
ing only for cool and oblivion,
glided swiftly from dressing
room to dressing room.
And then came its savior. With
dulcet tones and sinuous gestures
slf'e lured it from its retreat in
the depths of a shower room,
and- taking it in her hands de
posited it gently on the archery
field where it might once more
pursue the even tenor of its way.
Rumor has it that this reptile
has been decorated by its broth
ers for Bravery in the Face of
Females and has furthermore re
ceived a medal of distinction as
the only snake that has ever in
vaded Roble gym.
