# STRONG GOES EAST TO ATTEND CONFERENCES
## 
### 
Dr. Edward K. Strong, Jr., professor
of applied psychology in the Graduate
School of Business, will leave tomorrow
for Chicago and New York City, where
he will represent Stanford at national
conferences. Li Chicago he will meet
with other educators to discuss personnel
problems of the Y.M.C.A. Later he will
attend a committee meeting of the Ameri
can Council of Education in New York.
This group is composed of twenty-five
educators and business men representing
the entire country who are to consider
personnel procedure in both colleges and
business.
