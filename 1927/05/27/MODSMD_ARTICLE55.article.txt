# STANFORD DAILY NOT TO APPEAR MONDAY
## 
### 
The Daily will not appear on Mon
day because the University Press will
be closed for the entire week-end of
Decoration Day. The Daily's press
room staff will resume work Monday
night in order to get out the women's
edition on Tuesday.
Other workers of the Press will not
return until Tuesday morning.
