# Texas Exhibitions Receive Mention of Art Institute
## 
### 
AUSTIN, Texas, May 26.—Mention
was given five architectural students
of the University of Texas on designs
submitted to the Beaux Arts Institute
of New York, according to C. P.
Reming, instructor in architectural
engineering.
