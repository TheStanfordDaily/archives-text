# MARJORIE ALLEN TO WED WILLIAM PABST
## 
### 
| Invitations are being issued to the
wedding of Marjorie S. Allen, '26, and
1 William Pabst, '28, which will be
! solemnized at 5 o'clock on Wednes
day, June 15, in the Memorial Chapel.
Marjorie Allen is a member of Chi
Omega and of Cap and Gown. Pabst is
a Phi Kappa Psi. Both have been
prominent in campus dramatics, Mar
jorie Allen belonging to Masquers and
j Pabst being a member of Ram's Head.
