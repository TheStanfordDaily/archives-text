# DAVIS' CLASS TO GIVE EIGHT ONE-ACT PLAYS
## 
### 
Public performances of eight or
more of the one-act plays prepared in
Public Speaking 119b will be given at
1:15 o'clock on Friday and Monday in
the Little Theatre. Gordon Davis is in
charge of the course for the first time
this quarter.
"Simoom," "Meet the Missus," "The
Managers," and "A Question of Prin
ciple" will be given on Friday. Mon
day,"The Prairie Doll," "The Same
Old Thing," "Finders Keepers," and
"The Dickey Bird" will be presented
There are possible additions to both
these programs.
