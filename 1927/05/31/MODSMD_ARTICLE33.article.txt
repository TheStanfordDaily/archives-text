# CAROLINE MOORE, '27, REVEALS ENGAGEMENT
## 
### 
Announcement of the engagement
of Caroline Moore, '27, anil Allan E.
Charles, '25, was made Saturday night
at the Chi Omega supper dance in
Los Altos.
Caroline Moore, whose home is in
Stockton, is a member of Cap and
Gown and Chi Omega sorority.
Charles, from Palo Alto, is a graduate
in the Stanford Law School. He is a
member of Quadrangle Club, Skull and
Snakes, and Phi Delta Theta. While
an undergraduate he was president of
Men's Council and distinguished hint
self in the two-mile in the Stanford-
California track meets of '24 and '25.
