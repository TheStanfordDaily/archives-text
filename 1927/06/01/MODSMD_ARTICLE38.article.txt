M.S.C. That the following date be
granted: June 11, Theta Sigma Phi.
M.S.C. That the election returns as
presented by the chairman of the Election
Board be approved.

awarded a Block "S" for football on the
recommendation of Coach Warner, Cap
tain Swan, and Graduate Manager Mas
ters.

held Tuesday, June 7.

Conn as Business Manager of the Daily

of the Stanford Daily Improvement Fund
to purchase a new typewriter.

Arthur Holmes be appointed Assistant
Concert Manager at a salary of $100 for
the scries be approved. An additional $25
is payable for each extra concert.

to appoint a committee of three to look
into the concert series and make a rec
ommendation to the Executive Committee
at the next meeting.
M.S.C. That the report of the Concert
Manager for 1926-27 be approved.
M.S.C. That the recommendation that
Miss Gertrude Baker be appointed Man
ager of the concert scries be approved at
a salary of $50 per concert. No money
may be spent for tiie handling of tickets.

a recount>of the election of May 31, 1927,
must ask for it in writing by noon of
Wednesday, June 1, 1927, at the Account
ing Office of the Old Union. In addition
each candidate requesting a recount must
supply their students at Room 20 at 2
p.m. Wednesday, June 15. Other candi
dates for the same office may at their
option supply three assistants also.

authorized to sign a note for the A.S.S.U.
indebtedness to the University for their
share of the cost of the Basketball Pavil
ion upon his receiving a statement from
the University which is satisfactory to
him.
