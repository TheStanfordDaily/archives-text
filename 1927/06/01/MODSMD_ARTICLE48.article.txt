# R.H. LUTZ TO TALK AT NORTHERN UNIVERSITY
## 
### 
Ralph H. Lutz, professor of History,
will address the University of Wash
ington chapter of Phi Beta Kappa at
Pullman, Wash., on June 10. His sub
ject will be "The Idealism of the
Great War."
Professor Lutz took his LL.B. de
gree at the University of Washington.
According to the University of Wash
ington Daily, Professor Lutz has been
made an honorary member of Phi
Beta Kappa at the northern institu
tion.
