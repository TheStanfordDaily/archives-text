# ENCINA CLUB TO HOLD LAST SMOKER TONIGHT
## 
### 
Encina Club will hold its last smoker
of the school year tonight at 7 o'clock
in the lobby of Encina Ilall.
Professor Ralph Lutz will be the speak
er of the evening and will give a little
talk on his experiences as a freshman as
well as give a few reminiscences of the
European trip which he took last summer.
Warner Hobdy will give a few solos
on the ukulele, anil along with Charles
Greenfield and Gordon Griffith will play
a few trios. Further entertainment will
be supplied by Allen Campbell, who will
again give one of his famous little piano
logues, and Dick McCurdy and his banjo.
