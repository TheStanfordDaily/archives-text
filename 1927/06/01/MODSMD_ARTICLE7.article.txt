# MOORE APPOINTED TO BE TENNIS MANAGER
## 
### 
Clifton Moore was appointed Varsity
Tennis Manager for next year at a meet- '
ing of the Board of Athletic Control
Monday. The appointment is subject to
approval by the Executive Committee.
The following Junior Tennis Managers
were appointed: Lyle Olscn, Martin
Woodward, Antoine Bovet, and Eminel
Halstead.
