# SENIOR BALL TICKETS GO ON SALE AT UNION
## 
### 
Senior Ball tickets will be placed on
sale for the last time from 1 to 2
o'clock Monday in the Associated Stu
dents office at the Old Union. This
will also be the last day that tickets
can be returned. All seniors who have
had tickets reserved must call for
them at that time or their tickets will
be sold.
