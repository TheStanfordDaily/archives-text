# GRADUATE STUDENTS WILL HOLD JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
Graduate students will hold their second
jolly-up of the quarter tonight from 7 to
8 o'clock at the Women's Clubhouse. A
ten-cent assessment will be laid on the
dancers in order to cover expenses for
the music.
Officers of the new graduate student
association, under whose arrangement the
jolly-up will be held, are: Randolph Karr,
president; Louise Snyder, vice-president;
Richard W. Husband, secretary-treasurer.
