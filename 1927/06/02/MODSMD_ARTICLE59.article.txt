# MISSOURI MAN WILL JOIN SUMMER STAFF
## 
### 
Professor Frederick A. Middlebush,
chairman of the Department of Political
Science at the University of Missouri, is
to lie a member of the summer quarter
faculty at Stanford. He is giving two
courses: "History of Political Theory,"
and "International Relations."
Middlebush, a graduate of Michigan
University, spent three years as a student
at The Hague. He is the author of
"Anglo-Dutch Relations and Diplomatic
Documents." In 1926 he was a member
of the Carnegie party at Geneva, and was
a leader of one of the Round Table
groups of instruction there. His spe
cialty is political theory and international
relations.
