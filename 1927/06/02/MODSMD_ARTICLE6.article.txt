# DAILY STAFF WILL BANQUET TONIGHT AT CHI PSI FRATERNITY
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Daily edi
torial staff will hold their annual spring
banquet at the Chi Psi house this after
noon fjjom 5:30 o'clock to 8 o'clock. The
dinner and entertainment will be fur
nished by the new members of the staff,
headed by Robert Brownell and Gale
Wallace. Wilbur F. Adams, retiring
Daily editor, will act as toastmaster.
Music for dancing and tunes for the
dinner will be furnished by an orchestra
headed by Phil Brooks. Dinner will be
promptly at 6 o'clock, with the enter
tainment and dancing before and after
the banquet.
