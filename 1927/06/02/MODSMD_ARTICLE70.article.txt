# Dudley C. Chambers Gets Engineer Office
## 
### 
Dudley E. Chambers was elected
chairman of the Stanford chapter of
the American Institute of Electrical
Engineers last night at its final spring
quarter meeting. N. R. Morgan was
chosen vice-chairman and T. L. Len
zen secretary.
Arrangements were made at the
meeling for an inspection trip to the
KGO broadcasting station in Oakland
Saturday morning. The transformer
plant will be examined especially. A
request for papers was also made in
preparation for the Del Monte con
vention of Pacific Coast A.I.E.E. chap
ters on September 13.
