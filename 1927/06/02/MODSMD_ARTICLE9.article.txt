# NORMAN LARSON IS SELECTED DRAMATIC MANAGER BY BOARD
## 
### 
Norman G. Larson, '28, was chosen
next year's dramatic manager by the Dra
matic Council last night at the yearly
election.
Competing against fourteen tryoutces
and working for three quarters, Larson
and Richard J. O'Brien were selected for
the final appointment. The decision of
the council was based 011 the amount of
time spent in trying out and the quality
and ability of work shown.
Larson is registered from Berkeley and
is enrolled in the School of Engineering,
lie took a minor part in "Macbeth" in
his freshman year, lie is a member of
Phi Kappa Psi.
