# KINGMAN TO SPEAK AT SUMMER BANQUET OF STANFORD Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
Harry Kingman, who is now on his
way to the United States from China,
will be the principal speaker at the
annual Y.M.C.A. dinner in the Union
next Tuesday night.
Kingman has been in the Orient for
several years serving as a Y.M.C.A.
secretary in various cities there dur
ing his stay. He is a graduate of
Pomona College and at one time
played baseball for the New York
Yankees. He will speak on the present
situation in China.
Officers for the coming year will be
installed at (he meeting, and the an
nual election of board members will
take place.
