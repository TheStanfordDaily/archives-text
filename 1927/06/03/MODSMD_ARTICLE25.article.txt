# TRIALS REMAIN OPEN FOR BUSINESS STAFF OF STANFORD DAILY
## 
### 
Tryouts for the business staff of the
Stanford Daily will continue until next
Wednesday. Freshmen interested
should report at a meeting to be held
at 12:45 o'clock today in the business
office located next to the Press Build
ing, states Claude Conn, manager.
The first tryout period will acquaint
the aspirants with (he general duties
of the staff, and the fields for advance
ment open in the work.
The following men, who are seeking
positions in the business department,
have been assigned to preliminary
duties, beginning Saturday morning at
10 o'clock: Elvan Cobb, Walter Den
nis, Raymond Perin, Charles Shreve,
Ward Smith.
