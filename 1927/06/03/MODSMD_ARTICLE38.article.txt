# Michigan State Students Collect Disaster Funds
## 
### 
EAST LANSING, Mich.—(LP.)—
Students of Michigan State College
here have raised more than two hun
dred dollars for the relief of the fami
lies stricken by the recent dynamite
disaster at the Bath Consolidated
school.
After the catastrophe occurred sev
eral members of the staff of the Michi
gan State News aided metropolitan
newspaper reporters in getting the
latest facts on the affair.
