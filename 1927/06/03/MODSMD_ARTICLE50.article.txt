# SERVICE TO BE HELD IN CHAPEL ON SUNDAY
## 
### 
Divine worship and sermon will be
at 11 o'clock in the Memorial Church
Sunday morning, with Reverend D.
Charles Gardner officiating. There is
to be no morning chapel, but at 7:30
o'clock an organ recital is scheduled.
In the morning service anthems will
be "Ave Maria," Arcadelt, and "Na
ture's Praise of God," Beethoven. This
latter number will be sung by the
Stanford Male Choir.
