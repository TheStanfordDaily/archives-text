# WARNER LEAVES FOR VACATION TRIP EAST
## 
### 
Glenn S. Warner. Stanford grid coach,
left the campus Saturday for Laramie,
Wyoming, where he will spend a week on
a fishing trip.
From there he will go to Colorado Uni
versity to give a coaching course.
Warner will then spend a week of in
tensive instructing at a coaching 'school
in Superior, Wisconsin; and after visit
ing his home in the East, will return to
Stanford about August 1.
