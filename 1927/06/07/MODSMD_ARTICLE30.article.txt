# Antioch Students Vote To Abolish Varsity Teams
## 
### 
YELLOW SPRINGS, Ohio.—(l.P.)—
Students at Antioch College here have
petitioned for the abolition of varsity
athletic teams and for the substitu
tion of an extensive intramural pro
gram. No action has yet been taken
by the student council or the college
authorities on the petition, which was
signed by ninety-four of the students.
