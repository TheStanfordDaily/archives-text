# SIGMA DELTA PI WILL GIVE 'LOS PANTALONES'
## 
### 
"Los Pantaloncs" is the title of a play (
to be given by Sigma Delta Pi, honorary
Spanish society, at 8:30 o'clock tonight in
the Women's Clubhouse.
Marvin Park, taking the part of Don
Juan, and Ruth Sievers, as Dona Paula
Paula, head the cast.
Aurelio M. Espinosa, Jr., will act the
part of Felipe and Dorothy Webster that
of Luisa, while Lucile Burk will be Pepa,
the servant.
The play, which is a light comedy, will
be given entirely in Spanish. It will im
mediately follow the initiation of the
newly electcd members.
