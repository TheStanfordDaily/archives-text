# WISCONSIN PROFESSOR SECURED FOR SUMMER
## 
### 
Professor Alexander R. Hohfeld, head
of the German department at the Uni
versity of Wisconsin, will give two
courses here during the first half of sum
mer quarter. Courses offered are both
two-unit subpects, one in the German
drama of the nineteenth century, and the
other in Goethe's "Faust."
Professor William A. Cooper, of the
local German Department, says Hohfeld
is considered the dean of Germanists in
this country. He declares that the Wis
consin man, who has been at that uni
versity for twenty-six years, is one of the
most. noted graduate teachers in the
United States.
