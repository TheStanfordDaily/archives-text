# TWO WORLD ATHLETIC CHAMPIONS TO ENTER STANFORD NEXT FALL
## 
### 
Pete Desjardins, world's champion
high diver, and Fidel La Barba,
world's champion flyweight boxer,
both have passed the entrance require
ments and will register at Stanford in
the fall quarter.
Desjardines will be eligible for the
swimming team, but because of his
professional standing, La Barba will
not be permitted to compete with the
Stanford boxers. Desjardins is coming
from Florida, where he has been com
peting, and La Barba from Los An
geles. The latter was president of the
student body at Los Angeles High
School previous to his graduation
there.
