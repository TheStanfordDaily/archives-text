# Ninety Terms Required 'To Finish Ohio Classes
## 
### 
DELAWARE, Ohio —(I.P.) —The fol
lowing enlightening statistics were
published in the Ohio Wesleyan
Transcript:
"Forty-five years is a long time to
go to Ohio Wesleyan, yet it would

take a student ninety semesters to
complete the curriculum from Astron
omy to Zoology at the usual rate of
sixteen hours a semester.
"The students of this university
spend over one million hours a year in
classes at the rate of over 30,000 hours
a week. In other words, it would take
a student 52 years to spend as much
time in class as the student body does
in one week."
