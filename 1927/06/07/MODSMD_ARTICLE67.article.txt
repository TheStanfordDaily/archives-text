# BERNARD FAY TO GIVE CONCLUDING LECTURE OF TUESDAY SERIES
## 
### 
The last Tuesday evening lecture of
the quarter will be given at 7:30 o'clock
tonight in the Little Theater when Pro
fessor Bernard Fay, visiting professor
at Columbia University, speaks on "In
ternationalism vs. Cosmopolitanism in
Contemporary French Literature."
Professor Fay, eminent French histo
rian, has specialized in the intellectual
development of France, especially during
the eighteenth century. At present he is
writing a book on Benjamin Franklin as
a significant representative of that period.
Modern French literature is in the scope
of his interests, and his subject for to
night's address has been selected from
that field.
The speaker came to this country from
the University of Clermont-Ferrand,
France.
