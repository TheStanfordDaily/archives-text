# THIRTY-SIXTH ANNUAL COMMENCEMENT CLOSES SENIOR ACTIVITIES ON JUNE 20
## Three Hundred Ninety-One Will Receive A.B. Degrees; Wilbur Chief Speaker of Final Exercises 
### 
OTHREE hundred ninety-one seniors of the June graduating class will receive
A.B. degrees in the thirty-sixth annual commencement which will be held
at 10:30 o'clock Monday, June 20, in the Memorial Church. In addition 175
students will receive advanced degrees. President Ray Lyman Wilbur will be
the commencement speaker.
Senior Week activities will be opened by the Senior Farce in the Assembly
I-lall and will close with the commencement exercises in the Memorial Church.
Other features of the week will be the Alumni Dinner, the Senior Ball, the
Class Day exercises, and the Baccalaureate services.
