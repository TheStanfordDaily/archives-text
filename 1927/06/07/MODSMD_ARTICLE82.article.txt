# BARNES NAMED NEW U.S.C. TRACK CAPTAIN
## 
### 
Lee Barnes, crack pole vaulter of 1
U.S.C., who held the American record
until Sabin Carr of Yale made a new
record of fourteen feet at the recent
1.C.4-A. meet, was elected to head
the Trojan track and field squad for
the coming season at a meeting of the
team last Saturday.

He was the only man nominated for
the position and the election was
unanimous. The new captain is a
graduate of Hollywood High School
