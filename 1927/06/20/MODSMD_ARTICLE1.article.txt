# STANFORD GIVES 868 DEGREES AT COMMENCEMENT EXERCISES
## 
### 
Eight hundred sixty-eight degrees
were conferred by the University at
the thirty-sixth annual commencement
exercises this morning with six hun
dred fifteen students in the graduating
class.
Of the 1927 Class thirty-two stu
dents were graduated with great dis
tinction, sixty-two also earning dis
tinction.
More than a hundred students were
granted A.M. degrees, 113 receiving
their Master's degree. Forty-four
Ph.D.'s were conferred, twenty-five
M.D.'s, forty-two J.D.'s. Only two stu
dents received the degree, of LL.D.,
while twenty-seven degrees were given
engineers.
