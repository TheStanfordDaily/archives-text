Wednesday, June Fifteenth
6:30 P.M. —Club and Fraternity Dinners.
S:l5 P.M. —Senior Farce.

6:30 P.M. —Complimentary Dinner to the Seniors, by the Executive
Committee of the Stanford Alumni Association in the
Union.
S:00' P.M. —Senior Promenade in the Court of the Stanford Union.
Friday, June Seventeenth
9:00 P.M. —Meeting of the Academic Council in Room 12.
4:00 P.M. —President and Mrs. Wilbur's Reception, at the President's
House.
0:00 P.M. —Senior Ball, in Toyon Hall.
Saturday, June Eighteenth
2:00 P.M. —Class Day Exercises in the Memorial Church.
3:00 P.M. —Dedication of the 1927 Class Plate, in Inner Quadrangle.
4:00 P.M. —Pilgrimage to the Mausoleum. Transference of the Flower
Privilege from the Senior to the Junior Class.
4:30 P.M. —Annual Open Meeting of the Phi Beta Kappa, in the Little
Theatre.
6:30 P.M. —Phi Beta Kappa Reception, Dinner, and Initiation, in the
Old Union.

11:00 A.M. —Baccalaureate Service in the Memorial Church. Sermon
by Reverend Howard Meliah, Trinity Church, Brooklyn,
New York.
4:00 P.M. —Faculty at Home.
