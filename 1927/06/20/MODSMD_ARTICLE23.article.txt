# Leo Harris Receives Job Coaching at Fresno High
## 
### 
Leo Harris, for three years a tackle
on the Stanford Varsity football team,
will coach athletics at the Fresno High
School next year. Harris is a Santa
Cruz man. He will receive his degree
in the education department. In addi
tion to his work in football at Stan
ford, he has played guard on the bas
ketball team.
