Stanford University Weekly Calendar
Volume XLII, No. 1 PUBLISHED EVERY FRIDAY June 21-25
Tuesday, June 21
REGISTRATION DAY
7:30 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Organist, in the Memorial Church. Program of
Early Organ Music before Bach.
8:00 p.m. INFORMAL SMOKER to visiting members of the Summer Quarter Faculty, by the Men's Faculty Club,
at the Faculty Clubhouse.
Wednesday, June 22
4:15 p.m. LECTURE on "Goethe and England," by Professor Alexander Hohlfeld, of the Department of German,
University of Wisconsin, in the Little Theatre. The public is invited to attend.
Thursday, June 23
4:15 p.m. LECTURE-RECITAL, "Johannes Sebastian Bach," by Mr. Warren D. Allen, assisted by Mr. Orley See,
Violinist, in the Assembly Hall. The public is invited to attend.
Friday, June 24
8:00 p.m. President and Mrs. Wilbur will be at home informally to all faculty and students of the Summer Quarter, at
the President's House.
Saturday, June 25
9:00 p.m. *ROBLE OPEN HOUSE. All Summer Quarter students are invited. Cards and dancing.
