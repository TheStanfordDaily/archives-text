# ROBLE HALL WOMEN HOLD OPEN HOUSE SATURDAY EVENING
## 
### 
Following the custom of former sum
mer quarters, Roble Hall will begin
its social activity for the summer
term with an open house at 0 o'clock
Saturday evening. All students at
tending the quarter are invited' to be
present.
There will be cards and dancing.
Roble, the women's dormitory, will be
the scene of many student gatherings
throughout the quarter.
