# Charlier to Give Course In Renaissance French
## 
### 
Professor Gustav Charlier, whose
course, Romanic Languages A 144, is
listed as "Histoire de la litterature
francaise," at 11 o'clock daily, will
make it a course on French literature
of the Renaissance. Professor Charlier
is a specialist in this particular field.
The course will be pi von entirely in
French and a syllabus will facilitate
the work of those taking it.
