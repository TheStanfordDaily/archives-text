# STUDENTS OF SPANISH COME FOR ADVANCED WORK WITH NAVARRO
## 
### 
Attracted by the opportunity to work
under Professor Navarro Tomas of
Madrid, authority on phonetics, and
by the other advanced courses offered,
many students in Spanish have come
to Stanford for the summer quarter,
it has been announced.
The Spanish Club is making plans
for a lively season. The work of the
club will be arranged to provide prac
tice in speaking. Students taking ele
mentary and second-year Spanish will
be welcomed to the meetings of the
club, it was announced.
In addition to the classes under Pro
fessor Tomas, the advanced courses
