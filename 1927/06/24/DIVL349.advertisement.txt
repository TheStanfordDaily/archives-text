Start the New Quarter
With the American Trust
Whether you are a new student, coming to
Stanford for the first time, or an old student
returning for the new quarter's work, we're
standing ready to welcome you.
We invite you to start a checking account.
Deposit all your income. Pay all your bills by
check. It's a simple, easy, thoroughly satis
factory way to keep track of funds.
And call on us, if there is any other way we
may serve you.
