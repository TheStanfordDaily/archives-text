# REPORTS SHOW MORE MEN ON CAMPUS THAN IN PRESENT QUARTER
## 
### 
With the fraternity houses virtually
full, more men are living on the cam
pus this quarter than the previous
summer quarter, according to reports.
Stanford Union and Toyon are full.
Encina is open for the first time for
the summer quarter, and has seventy
students lodged there already. Roble
Hall is accommodating a number near
capacity. Contrary to last, week's be
lief, Manzanita will not be opened.
