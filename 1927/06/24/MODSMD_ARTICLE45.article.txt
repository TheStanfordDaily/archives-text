# Stanford University Weekly Calendar

Volume XLII, No. 2 PUBLISHED EVERY FRIDAY June 26-July 2
Sunday, June 26
11:00 a.m. DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: The Right Reverend Charles Wesley Burns, Bishop of
the Methodist Episcopal Church in California.
Music:
"I Sought the Lord" Stevenson
Carol Lasater, Contralto Soloist
7:30 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Organist, in the Memorial Church. Program of
Organ Music by Bach.
Monday, June 27
3:00 p.m. FACULTY WOMEN'S CLUB, in the Women's Clubhouse. Special meeting for visiting faculty women.
8:00 p.m. ILLUSTRATED LECTURE on "La Espana de Hoy," by Professor Tomas Navarro Tomas, Visiting Pro
fessor of Romanic Languages, from Centro de Estudios Historicos, Madrid; in Room 244, Romanic
Languages Building. Open to the public.
Tuesday, June 28
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in the Memorial Church. Compositions by Mozart, Haydn, and other
predecessors of Beethoven.
4:30 p.m. ROBLE TEA. All women students and faculty are invited.
8:00 p.m. LECTURE on"The Present Status of the League of Nations," by Professor Charles K. Webster, Wilson
Professor of International Politics, University of Wales; in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
Wednesday, June 29
4:15 p.m. LECTURE on "Life in the Fifteenth Century as Illustrated in Tapestry," by Dr. Phyllis Ackerman, Lec
turer in American Art Museums; in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
8:30 p.m. RECEPTION by the Director of the Summer Quarter to all members of the Faculty, at Branner Hall.
Thursday, June 30
4:15 p.m. LECTURE-RECITAL, "Ludwig von Beethoven," by Mr. Warren D. Allen, assisted by Mr. Orley See,
Violinist, and Mr. W. Villalpando, Violoncellist; in the Assembly Hall. The public is invited.
7:30 p.m. THURSDAY EVENING CONFERENCE, at the home of Dr. David Starr Jordan. Subject: "The Method
of Evolution."
Friday, July 1
8:15 p.m. OUTDOOR CONCERT, in the Court of the Union. The public is invited.
9:30 p.m. RECEPTION by the Director of the Summer Quarter to all registered students, in Branner Hall.
Dancing.
Saturday, July 2
8:00 a.m. AUTOMOBILE EXCURSION, to Berkeley and the University of California, via Dumbarton Bridge.
Leave from the Stanford Union, Main Entrance, returning about 4 p.m. Fare $2.70. Reservations must
be made and fare deposited two days in advance, in the office of the Director of the Summer Quarter,
Room 173, Administration Building.
