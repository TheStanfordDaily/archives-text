# Seats for "Craig's Wife" Go on Sale Thursday
## 
### 
Seat sale for "Craig's Wife," to
be presented July 8, opens at 1
o'clock Thursday in the lobby of
the Old Union. Those desiring
the best seats are urged by Dra
matic Manager Norman Larson
to buy their tickets Thursday af
ternoon. All campus residents
who are not students may have
the privilege of obtaining their
tickets by mail if they will write
or see Norman Larson, Dramatic
Manager.
