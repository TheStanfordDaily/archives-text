# WIFE OF STUDENT KILLED
## 
### 
Mrs. Wray Cameron Greenwood,
wife of Vernon Greenwood, a student
In the Stanford Education Department,
was instantly killed yesterday when
she stepped in front of an interurban
electric: ear while boarding a bus for
Mayfleld, where she lived.
