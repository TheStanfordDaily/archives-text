# TOYON HALL MEN ORGANIZE CLUB FOR SUMMER MONTHS
## Cypher Elected President; Ehrmann Chosen to Be Club Manager 
### 
At the meeting last evening of the
men in Toyon Hall the organization of
a Summer Toyon Club was completed.
Cook Cypher was elected president and
Alexis Ehrmann was the choice for
manager. Both of these are old men
111 the hall and well known in Univer
sity athletics.
An informal dance will be held on
July 15, as the first of several social
events at Toyon.
