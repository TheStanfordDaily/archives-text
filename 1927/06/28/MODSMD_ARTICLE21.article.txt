# Director Sellards Will Hold Faculty Reception
## 
### 
Director John Sellards will entertain
members of the faculty tomorrow night
at an informal reception to meet the
visiting members of the summer quarter
staff. The reception will be at half-past
eight, in Brainier Hall. Any members
of the regular faculty who are not on
duty, but who are on the campus, arc
invited to attend. No cards are being
sent out.
