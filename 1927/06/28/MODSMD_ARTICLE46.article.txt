# R.F. Sandwick, Senior, Killed in Collision
## 
### 
Richard F. Sandwick, a senior at
Stanford, was fatally injured in a col
lision at Wlnnetka, Illinois, Saturday.
Sandwick was the son of Richard L.
Sandwick, '95. He was born in High
land Park, Illinois, and was 22 years
of age. At Stanford he was in the
School of Biology, specializing ih bac
teriology. He was a member of Chi
Psi fraternity and was to have com
pleted his studies here next quarter.
