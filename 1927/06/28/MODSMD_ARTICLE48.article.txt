# Charles Kenyon, Author Of "Kindling," Married
## 
### 
Charles A. Kenyon, playwright, was
married last Monday in Los Angeles
to Jane Winton, movie actress. Judge
Crawford performed the ceremony.
Kenyon attended Stanford during
the college year 1899-1900. His first
successful play was 'Kindling," which
was produced in New York in 1911.
