# Daily Subscribers May Obtain Paper at Union
## 
### 
All students automatically be
come subscribers to the Stanford
Daily upon registration. For the
convenience of readers, the sys
tem followed during other quar
ters is continued in the summer
so far as residents of dormitories
and student-occupied fraternity
houses are concerned, and the
paper is distributed to these
houses.
Other students may obtain their
papers at the student office ni
the main lobby of the Old Union.
The paper will be sent to com
muters among the students and
to subscribers outside the student
body through the postoffice.
Complaints about receipt of the
Daily may be telephoned to the
business office of the paper, Palo
Alto 1047, or to the editorial de
partment, Palo Alto 900, local 93.
