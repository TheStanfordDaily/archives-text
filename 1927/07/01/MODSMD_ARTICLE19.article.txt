# Open Forum Tonight On Civil Liberties
## 
### 
"Do Americans want civil liberties?"
is the title of an address which will
be given by Professor Harry F. Ward
of the Union Theological Seminary,
New York City, in the Palo Alto Com
munity House, at 7:30 this evening. An
open forum will be held, to which all
are invited.
Professor Ward is secretary of the
Methodist Federation of Social Ser
vices, and of the Federal Council of
Churches of Christ in America, as well
as chairman of the American Civil
Liberties Union.
