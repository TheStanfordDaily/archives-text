# AUSTRALIANS VISIT CAMPUS
## 
### 
J. C. Leverrier, K.C., of Sydney,
Australia, accompanied by his son and
two daughters, was a visitor on the
campus yesterday, calling on Profes
sor Harris J. Ryan to inspect the high
voltage laboratory. Mr. Leverrier is an
attorney and is interested in electrical
development. He is making a brief
tour of the Pacific Coast states. He is
a member of the senate of the Uni
versity of Sydney. With the party was
J. E. Morwood, who is returning honte
after completing a traveling fellow
ship from the University of Queens
land at Brisbane. He has spent the
time chiefly in England and in the
works of the General Electric Com
pany in this country.
