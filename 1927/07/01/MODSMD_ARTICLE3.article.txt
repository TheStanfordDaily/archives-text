# Elections Take Place At Roble Hall Today
## 
### 
Election of two new members to the
Committee of Five is being held today
in the lobby of Roble Hall. Nominations
were held at the first meeting of the
Roble Club held Tuesday evening. Those
nominated were Miss Mary I?. Nelson.
Miss Betty Chase, and Miss Peggy
Waite.
The Committee of Five is a committee
elected by the residents of Roble Hall
and has charge of "lock-outs," conduct,
and anything in regard to social disci
pline.
