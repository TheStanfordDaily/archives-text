# Roble House Committee Completed by Election
## 
### 
Roble's Committee of Five was com
pleted by the election last Friday of
Mary B. Nelson and Betty Chase. The
other three members are Esther
Brown, chairman, appointed, in ac
cordance with the constitution of
Roble Club, by last quarter's club
president, and Arbie Stewart and Mary
Virginia Cooper, appointed, as the con
stitution provides, by the summer
quarter chairman and the Dean of
Women.
The Committee of Five has charge
of the enforcement of the house rules
of the club as adopted by the members.
