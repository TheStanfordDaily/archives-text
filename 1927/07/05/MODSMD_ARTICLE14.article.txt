# STANFORD GRADUATES WED
## 
### 
Helen A. Gale and Charles R. Betts
were married last Saturday afternoon
at the home of the bride's mother,
Mrs. Julia M. Gale.
The newly married couple are both
Stanford graduates. Mrs. Betts has
for several years been chief of the
serial department of the Stanford
library. They will make tlieir home at
Gladstone, near Portland, Oregon.
