# Appointment Office Asks Students to Get Records
## 
###

all students take advantage of its
registration for record, whether they
I wish service for next year or not.

| hence, a student needs recommenda
tion on work finished this summer at
' Stanford. If that information is col
lected now while the student is known,
, it can be sent, out from the secretary's
! file upon request.
