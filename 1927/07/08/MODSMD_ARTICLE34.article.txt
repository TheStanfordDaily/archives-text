# Novarro to Lecture on Spain Monday Evening
## 
### 
An illustrated lecture on "La Espana
de Hoy" (The Spain of Today), will
be given in Spanish by Professor
Tomas Navarro Tonnls, visiting Pro
fessor of Romanic Languages, at 8
o'clock next Monday evening at the
Little Theatre.
Professor Navarro will illustrate his
discussion with lantern slides.
