# Alumnus Appointed Dean Of U. of C. Law School
## 
### 
R. Justin Miller, who recetved his
L.L.B. from the University of Montana
in 1013 and his J.D. from Stanford in
1914, has been appointed Dean of the
School of Law at the University of
California. The appointment was ef
fective July 1.
During the past year Dr. Miller has
been professor of law at the Univer
sity of Minnesota. Before coming to
Minnesota he was a professor of law
at the University of Oregon.
