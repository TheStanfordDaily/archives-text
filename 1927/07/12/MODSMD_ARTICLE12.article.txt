# Dr. Bailey Is Hostess To Reviewers Club Members
## 
### 
Dr. Margery Bailey was hostess to
a meeting of the Reviewers Club last
night. At the meeting Mrs. It. C. Stoker
reviewed several current articles.
Arduis Dean reviewed his Master's
thesis for the English department, and
Dr. Bailey spoke.
Refreshments were served. Most of
those attending were new members of
the club from the summer student
body.
