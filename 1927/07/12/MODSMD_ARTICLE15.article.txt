# Half Moon Bay Outing Entries Close Friday
## 
### 
Parties wishing to go as a group on
the trip to Half Moon Bay next Satur
day morning at 10 o'clock should make
such arrangements when the tickets
are bought, it was announced at the
office of the Director of the Summer
Quarter today.
Reservations may be made any time
up to 4 o'clock Friday afternoon. Tick
ets cost $2.50 apiece, and are obtain
able at 173 Administration Building.
No reservations whatsoever may be
made after the time specified.
The cars will follow the road out
from Woodside over the Santa Cruz
mountains. Many groves of redwoods
and beautiful forests are passed, and
the last part of the trip is along the
shore.
