# Another Show Case Given For Library Exhibits
## 
### 
Stanford was presented with a glass
covered show case for the library by
;m anonymous friend last week. The
show case will be added to the four
others in the balcony of the library
for exhibits of various kinds.
At the present time the Albert Ben
der collection of fine books is being
shown. This collection was given to
the library by Albert Bender of San
Francisco, and by other friends of the
University. Books from all of the mod
ern presses specializing in fine print
ing are to be found in the collection.
Besides those volumes from the mod
ern presses, the collection contains
copies of books printed as early as
1499.
