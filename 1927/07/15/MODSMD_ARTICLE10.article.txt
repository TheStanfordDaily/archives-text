# Roble Club Holds Another Informal Tuesday Tea
## 
### 
Members of the Roble Club were
again hostesses to the students of the
summer quarter at their weekly tea
on Roble lawn Tuesday afternoon.
Tea was served by Mrs. Mary H.
Stearns, social director of Roble.
According to custom, guests came
in school clothes. Notebooks and
papers were stacked on the table and
lawn.
All students of the quarter, men as
well as women, are invited to these
informal teas. A table is carried on
the lawn about which the guests
gather. The teas start at four and con
tinue to five-thirty.
These affairs are held every Tues
day.
