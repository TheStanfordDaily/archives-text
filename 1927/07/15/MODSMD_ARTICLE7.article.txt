# O.A.C Students and Alumni To Give Dance and Picnic
## 
### 
Students and alumni of the Oregon
Agricultural College will give a dance
on Saturday, July 23, at Stephens
Union, on the University of California
campus, and a picnic at Stinson Beach
on July 30 and 31.. All former O.A.C.
students at Stanford are invited to
both.
The tickets for the dance are fifty
cents apiece. No arrangements need
be made. Those desiring additional in
formation may write to Iloy R. Hamer
sley, formerly president of the Golden
Gate O.A.C. Club, who is in charge.
The Stinson Beach picnic on July
30 and 31 is in charge of W. H. Misson.
