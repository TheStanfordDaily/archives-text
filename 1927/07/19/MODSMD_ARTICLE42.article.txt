# EDUCATION SCHOOL WILL HOLD BANQUET AT UNION TOMORROW
## 
### 
Members of the Education depart
ment will hold an Education Dinner
at the Stanford Union tomorrow eve
ning at 6. The dinner is open to
anyone enrolled or interested in any
way in education courses.
Cantaloupe, creamed chicken patty,
potatoes, peas, tomato salad, ice
cream, cake, and coffee will make up
the menu.
Dr. Truman L. Kelley, professor of
education, will act as toastmaster. A
program of music and speeches is
being arranged by the committee in
charge.
The price of the dinner will be
seventy-five cents for each person.
Those interested are requested to sign
their names on the list posted outside
Room 55.
