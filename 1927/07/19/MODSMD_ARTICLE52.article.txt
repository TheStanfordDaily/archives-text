# Roble Rummage Sale to Aid Convalescent Home
## 
### 
Today is the day when the members
of the 500 have an opportunity to get
rid of misfits and to acquire longed-for
articles at Roble's mammoth rummage
sale.
Some will get rid of white elephants
and others will be delighted to secure
them. The promoters assure astonish
ing bargains, a social event worth at
tending, and a charitable enterprise of
sterling worth.
The sale will last all day. Refresh
ments will be served.
Monetary proceeds of this sale,
which is under the direction of the
Y.W.C.A., will go to the Stanford Con
valescent Home.
