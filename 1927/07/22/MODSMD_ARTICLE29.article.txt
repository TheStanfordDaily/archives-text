# Pi Lambda Theta Holds Open Tea at Clubhouse
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Thota held open tea to
the women of the Stanford campus
Wednesday afternoon at the Women's
Clubhouse from four to five-thirty.
Mrs. Harold Benjamin explained that
this is the Education honor sorority,
told its requirements for membership,
and listed some of its activities. Mrs.
Edith Pattee spoke of the activities of
the campus chapter at the University
of Oregon. Miss Isabel Caps mentioned
activities of Pi Lambda Theta at other
colleges which she has attended.
