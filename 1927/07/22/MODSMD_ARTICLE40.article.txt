# Three Physical Directors Visit Professor Storey
## 
### 
Three physical education directors have
visited the Stanford campus recently as
the guests of Dr. Thomas A. Storey,
Director of Physical Education at Stan
ford.
Dr. James H. McCurdy, Professor and
Director of Physical Education at the
Y.M.C.A. College in Springlield, Massa
chusetts, who is now spending his sum
mer in charge of the Physical Education
Department at the University of Cali
fornia, was at Stanford Wednesday.
Dr. Paul Phillips, Director of Physi
cal Education at Amherst, and probably,
according to Dr. Storey, the most promi
nent in his line, passed through here
while spending his sabbatical year travel
ing-
Professor F. A. Wall, Director of Hy
giene at the College of the City of New
York, who succeeded Dr. Storey there
when the latter came to Stanford, spent
Tuesday with him.
