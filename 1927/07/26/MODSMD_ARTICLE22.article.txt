# HELEN LATHROP GUEST HERE
## 
### 
Helen Lathrop, Stanford '02. is the
guest of Dr. and Mrs. Leroy Abrams.
For many years Miss Lathrop lias held
the position of head librarian in the
American Library at Paris, Her six
months' vacation in the United States
will come to an end on August 19,
when she will sail for France.
