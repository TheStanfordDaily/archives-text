# FORMER STUDENT MARRIED
## 
### 
Miss Lina S. Jacob, A.B. '14 and
A.M. '15, and Marcelino Guerrero of
Palo Alto were married last week in
San Francisco by the Rev. Frederick
Morgan, former pastor of the First
Congregational Church in Palo Alto.
Mrs. Guerrero before her marriage
had been a teacher in the Palo Alto
Union High School.
