# NEBRASKANS ARE ENTERTAINED
## 
### 
Summer Quarter students from Hast
ings, Nebraska, were guests of the
Reverend and Mrs. Luther E. Stein of
San Francisco, at Los Gatos on Friday
evening. Eight Nebraskans were pres
ent. The guests were Mrs. W. F. Buck,
Miss Dorothy Buck, Mr. and Mrs.
R. W. Taylor, R. W. Krestinger, and
George G. Bruntz.
