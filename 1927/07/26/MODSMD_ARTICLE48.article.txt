# GARDNER-CRAMER MARRIAGE
## 
### 
Dr. Kenneth Gardner of the Stan
ford Hospital in San Francisco and
Miss Dorothy Cramer, '22, were mar
ried in the Memorial Church last
week. Dr. Gardner received his A.B.
at Stanford in 1923 and his M.D. in
San Francisco last month. The mar
riage ceremony was performed by the
Rev. John S. Thomas of Berkeley.
