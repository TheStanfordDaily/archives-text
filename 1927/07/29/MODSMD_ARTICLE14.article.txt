# Beefsteak Fry at Lake Held by Classical Club
## 
### 
Members of the Classical Club gave
a beefsteak fry at Searsville Lake
Wednesday afternoon at which about
fifteen men and women were present.
The gathering was arranged at this
date so as to have Miss Hazel D. Wil
liams, who is leaving for Greece soon,
present at the picnic.
Professor E. D. Perry of Columbia
University, who is visiting the Uni
versity as the guest of Professor A. T.
Murray, was present.
