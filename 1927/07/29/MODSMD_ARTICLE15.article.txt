# Relative of University Founder to Wed at Church
## 
### 
Leland Stanford, grand nephew of
Leland Stanford, the founder of Stan
ford University, will be married in the
Memorial Church here on August 2
to Miss Bernice Marie Barthold of
San Mateo.
The bride-to-be is the daughter of
Mr. and Mrs. E. M. Barthold of San
M-ateo.
