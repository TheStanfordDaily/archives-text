# Arthur Loesser Will Give Piano Recital August 5
## 
### 
Arthur Loesser, noted pianist and
accompanist, will give a recital in the
Basketball Pavilion on Friday evening,
August 5. No admission tickets are
necessary.
Loesser is at present in Portland,
Oregon. A number of years ago he
toured the coast as the accompanist
for Maud Powell, violinist. He has a
considerable reputation as accompan
ist, and is now appearing as a concert
pianist.
