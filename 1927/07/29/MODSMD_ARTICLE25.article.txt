# MADE CITY ATTORNEY
## 
### 
Preston L. Higgins, a former Stan
ford student, was appointed city at
torney of Oakland. Wednesday.
Higgins was secretary to Mayor
Davis of Oakland from 1915 to 1919
after leaving Stanford, and was later
deputy district attorney under District
Attorney Ezra Decato.
