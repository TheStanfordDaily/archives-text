# Roble Club Votes Against Dance for This Quarter
## 
### 
At an informal vote Sunday, Roble
approved a dance later in this quarter.
A further survey showed that not
enough would back it to make it a big
success.
Sentiment on the subject has been
agitated pro and con and this survey
finally settles the subject, at least so
•far as Roble is concerned.
