# Kaulfers Defeats Strayer In Toyon Net Tournament
## 
### 
Walter Kaulfers defeated A. D. Stray
er 6-1, 6-8, 6-2, in their first round match
of the Toyon Club tennis tournament.
Kaulfers will next meet Jack Leiter in

Leiter defeated George Lazar last week.
