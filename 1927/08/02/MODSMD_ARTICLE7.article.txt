# Dr. Jensen Continues Tests For Education Students
## 
### 
Vocational tests for lines of teach
ing work will be given again next
Thursday evening at 7 o'clock by Dr.
Milton Jensen. Sixty took the tests
last week.
Dr. Jensen has worked out these
tests under careful supervision and is
offering his time and training in test
ing to the Summer Quarter Education
students. A fee of 25 cents is charged
to cover printing and paper expenses.
The test will be given in Room 414
of the Psychology building.
