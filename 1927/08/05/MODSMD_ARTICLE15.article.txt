# Allen, University Organist, To Leave on Vacation
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen will leave for his
vacation next week accompanied by
his wife and family. They will take a
trip along the Redwood Highway first,
and later intend spending some time
in Carmel.
Mr. Wallace Rolls, who is a gradu
ate of the College of the Pacific, and
has studied in England, will take Mr.
Allen's place while he is away.
