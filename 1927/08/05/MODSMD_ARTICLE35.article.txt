# Roth to Attend Rotary Gathering in Chicago
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, comptroller of the
University, left Wednesday for Chi
cago, where he will attend the meeting
of the Rotary District Governors'
Council, which will be in session Au
gust 7 to 12.
Mr. Roth, who will make the Chi
cago trip while on his vacation, will
return to the campus in about three
weeks. Before returning to Stanford,
he will visit several Rotary clubs in
Nevada.
