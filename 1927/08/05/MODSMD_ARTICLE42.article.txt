# Work Progressing Rapidly On Offices of Men's Gym
## 
### 
Work is progressing rapidly on the
new building which will house the ad
ministration offices of the men's gym
nasium.
The brick construction will be com
pleted today, and work on the sub
flooring of the second floor will com
mence the middle of next week. Al
ready the studs are being put up on
the inside for plastering the first floor.
The latter part of next week will see
the steel trusses in place to hold the
roof. A large fireproof concrete safe
has been installed in the basement for
records and tickets.
The Graduate Manager's office stat
ed that the building will be ready for
occupancy by the first of October.
