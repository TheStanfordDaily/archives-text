# Physics Department Held Annual Outing Saturday
## 
### 
Stanford's Physics department held its
annual outing at Searsville Lake last
Saturday. Cars left the Physics building
at 11:30 and returned at 5:30. Hiking,
swimming, and the traditional baseball
game were the diversions of the outing.
Fifteen students, and six faculty mem
bers with their wives enjoyed the sports
and the barbecued meat. Drs. David L.
Webster and P. A. Ross were in charge,
Miss lola Page attending to the food.
