# Stanford Woman to Wed And Make Home in Ireland
## 
### 
Miss Fidela E. Conard, '20, and Doug
las Hanna of Belfast, Ireland will marry
next week, August 17, before sailing from
New York for Mr. Hanna's home in Ire
land where they will make their residence.
Miss Conard, who registered from San
Diego, was a member of Pi Beta Phi
sorority while at Stanford.
