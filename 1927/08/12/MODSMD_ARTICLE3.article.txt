# Assembly Hall Ceiling Will Be of Sugar Cane
## 
### 
The new ceiling in the Assem
bly Hall will be made of sugar
cane.
Celotex, which will be used, ex
plains J. W. McDonnell, Assist
ant Superintendent of Buildings
and Grounds, is a by-product of
the manufacture of sugar and
syrup from cane.
This material is valuable for
absorbing sound, and will thus be
used to better the acoustics.
