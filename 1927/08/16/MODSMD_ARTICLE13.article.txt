# Kirkwood, Law School Dean, Returns Here September 15
## 
### 
Dean M. R. Kirkwood of the Stan
ford Law School, who has been acting
professor of law at Cornell University
during the summer session there, re
turned from the East recently and will
remain in Santa Barbara until about
September 15, when he will come back
to the campus.
The subject which Dean Kirkwood
taught while at. Cornell was Real Prop
erty. He concluded his course there
July 2H.
During the absence of Dean Kirk
wood, Professor W. B. Owens is acting
dean of the Stanford Law School.
