# Stanford Dames Plan Bridge Party Thursday Afternoon
## 
### 
A bridge party is being planned for
the Thursday meeting of the Stanford
Dames. Those who do not care to play
will sew. A nursery will be maintained
as usual where the members' children
may be cared for during the afternoon.
The meeting will be at 3 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
