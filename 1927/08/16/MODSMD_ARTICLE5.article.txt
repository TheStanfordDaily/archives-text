# Stanford Man Dies in Attempt to Save Woman
## 
### 
Dr. Albert E. Butterfield, who re
ceived his medical degree from Stan
ford last spring, drowned at Moss
Landing Sunday after saving a girl
companion.
He brought Miss Dorcas Worsley to
safety, and, exhausted, was pulled
down by the undertow. The body was
not recovered until last night.
Dr. Butterfield was a recently li
censed practicing physician in San
Francisco. He was graduated from the
University of Southern California in
1922, and from the Stanford Medical
School in 1927.
