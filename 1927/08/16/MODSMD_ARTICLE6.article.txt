# Roble Club to Serve Tea At Four o'Clock Today
## 
### 
Roble Club will serve tea this after
noon from 4 to 5 :30 o'clock. All students
and faculty arc invited. Mrs. Frances T.
Russell, associate professor of English,
will pour. Miss Norma Wright will be
in charge.
The last Roble tea this quarter will be
a week from today. Roble Club has been
serving these teas as an opportunity for
Summer Quarter students to meet infor
mally and to make new friendships. In
order to rotate charge of the teas, Roble
Hall was taken wing by wing and oor
by floor. Girls in these sections each had
a turn being hostess.
