# TENNIS TOURNAMENT TO INTRODUCE FALL INTRAMURAL SPORTS
## 
### 
Intramural sports for the year will
begin with the open tennis tourna
ment which will begin shortly, an
nounced C. W. Davis, director of in
tramural athletics. All men students
are urged by Mr. Davis to sign up for
the tournament which will be divided
into class groups.
Freshmen in particular will find it
advantageous to take part in the com
petition, as the data gained on their
performances in matches will be used
by the tennis coaches. Last year 170
men took part in the tournament.
