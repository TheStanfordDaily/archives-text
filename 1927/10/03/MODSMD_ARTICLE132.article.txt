# SCHOLARSHIP OF STUDENTS GOES BELOW 1500 MARK
## 
### 
The University standing for the past scholastic year dropped below the
3.500 figure which it had maintained for two years previous, according to
figures released Saturday by Mrs. M. D. Huston, chairman of the Scholarship
Committee. Three main groups fell below their previous averages and thus
the general University figure was lowered. The groups which dropped were
the men, non-sorority, and fraternity while women, sorority, and non-fraternity
showed an increase.

units. Plus signs indicate a rise over the previous year's standing and minus
