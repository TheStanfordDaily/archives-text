# Alumnus Appointed Dean Of U.C. Law School
## 
### 
R. Justin Miller, who received his
L.L.B. from the University of Mon
tana in 1913 and his J.D. from Stan
ford in 1914, has been appointed Dean
of the School of Law at the University
of California. The appointment was ef
fective July 1. Dr. Miller has been a
member of the legal faculties at the
University of Minnesota and at the
University of Oregon. ,
