# STANFORD GRADUATES WED
## 
### 
Helen A. Gale and Charles R. I3etts
were married July 2 at the home of
the bride's mother, Mrs. Julia M. Gale.
The newly married couple are both
Stanford graduates. Mrs. Betts has
for several years been chief of the
serial department of the Stanford
library. They will make their home
at Gladstone, near Portland, Oregon.
