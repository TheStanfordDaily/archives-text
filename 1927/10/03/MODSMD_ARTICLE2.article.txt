# EX COMMITTEE HOLDS OPEN MEETING OF QUARTER TOMORROW
## 
### 
Functioning for the first time this
year, the Executive Committee meets
at 7:15 o'clock tomorrow night in the
A.S.S.U. office. Nine of the fourteen
committeemen will be present for the
first time.
Considering dates submitted will oc
cupy the major part of the meeting.
The plan of granting seven dates for
campus plays or productions for the
whole school year will be discussed.
This plan allows more open dates, as
heretofore the plays and productions
have required closed dates on the
nights of their presentations.
