# PI PHI WEDS
## 
### 
Miss Fidelia E. Conard, '20, and
Douglas Hanna of Belfast, Ireland,
were married on August 17, before
sailing from New York for Mr. Hanna's
home in Ireland where they will make
their residence. Miss Conard, who
registered from San Diego, was a
member of Pi Beta Phi sorority while
at Stanford.
