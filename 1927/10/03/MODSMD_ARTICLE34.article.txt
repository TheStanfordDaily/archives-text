# REWIEW EDITOR WILL GIVE WRITING COURSE
## 
### 
Frank Taylor, editor of the Illus
trated Review and a man of varied ex
perience in writing, will offer a three
unit course in article writing this
quarter. The conference will meet
Wednesday night at 7 o'clock in the
Press Building.
This course presupposes consider
able ability in writing which students
must demonstrate before admission to
the class will be granted. A large
amount of article writing will be re
quired during the quarter and articles
suitable for popular magazines and
Sunday magazines will be assigned.
