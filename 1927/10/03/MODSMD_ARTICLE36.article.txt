# EX-STUDENT COMMITS SUICIDE IN SAN JOSE
## 
### 
John Rankin Rogers, ex-'29, commit
ted suicide at his home in San Jose,
June 28. He had not been registered
since June 1920, having spent the past
year studying art and music in San
Jose.
Rogers was the son of Professor
Frederick J. Rogers, of the Stanford
Physics department. His mother, Mrs.
Josephine Rand Rogers, is well known
for her leadership in civic and wel
fare work in the state.
