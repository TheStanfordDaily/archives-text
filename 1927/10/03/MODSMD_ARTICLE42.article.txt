# STEVENSON WINS
## 
### 
Carl Stevenson, a member of the
San Francisco Olympic Club and cap
tain of the Stanford swimming team,
won the eighth annual Silver Gate
Swim at San Diego on June 26, with a
time of seven minutes and three sec
onds. He has won this swim for two
consecutive years.
The annual Healdsburg mile swim
on the Russian River was the next
event Stevenson entered, he having
won the event twice before. The race,
which was held July 1, was won by
Art Hargrave, also of the Olympic
Club, with the Cardinal merman sec
ond.
