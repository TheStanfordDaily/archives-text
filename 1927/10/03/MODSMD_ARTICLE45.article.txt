# R.F. SANDWICK KILLED
## 
### 
Richard F. Sandwick, a senior at
Stanford, was fatally injured in a colli
sion at Winnetka, Illinois, on June 25.
Sandwick was the son of Richard L.
Sandwick, '95. He was born in High
land Park, Illinois, and was 22 years
of age. At Stanford he was in the
School of Biology, specializing in bac
teriology. He was a member of Chi Psi
fraternity and was to have completed
his studies here next quarter.
