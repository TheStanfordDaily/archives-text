# SENIOR KILLED
## 
### 
Garth Winslow, Stanford student
noted in campus dramatics and a mem
ber of the Sierra Hiking Club, plunged
to his death July 19 while attempting
to scale Black Kaweali Peak in the
Mount Whitney region of the High
Sierra.
Winslow was 21 years of age and is
survived by his father and mother,
Dr. and Mrs. Thomas H. Winslow, and
a sister, Yvonne, all of Piedmont. He
\> as a member of Alpha Tau Omega.
