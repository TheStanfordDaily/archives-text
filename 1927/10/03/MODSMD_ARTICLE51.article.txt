# STUDENT KILLED IN CRASH
## 
### 
Henry K. Axton, '29, was killed Au
gust 5 while flying an airplane at
Alameda Airport. Axton was taking
lessons in flying at a private school.
He had taken leave of absence last
April to enter the aviation school. Col
lapse of a wing of his wasp monoplane
at the height of seventy-five feet
caused the accident.
