# FOOTBALL MANAGER TRYOUTEES WANTED
## 
### 
A call for more sophomores to try
out for the six junior football manager
ships has been issued by Dick Lowry,
senior football manager. Candidates
for these positions should report to
(he men's gym. Tryoutees for senior
and junior football managers have
been working since September 15,
when the conference season opened.
Managers will be selected at the close
of the season by a committee com
posed of the present football captain,
manager, the captain and manager
elect, and the coach and graduate
manager.
