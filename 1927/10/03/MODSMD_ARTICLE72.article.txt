# POLITICAL SCIENCE 1 GIVEN ONLY IN FALL
## 
### 
Political Science 1, prerequisite to
all other courses in the department,
will be given in the fall quarter only,
it has been announced. The course
will come at 8 o'clock, five hours a
week.
Professor Graham H. Stuart of the
Political Science' Department will not
he in residence during the autumn
quarter. His courses in International
Relations and the League of Nations
will be given either in the winter or
summer quarters.
