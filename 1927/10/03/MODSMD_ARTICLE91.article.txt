# Nearly 200 New Students At President's Reception
## 
### 
Approximately 200 students attended
the reception and dance given Satur
day evening at the home of President
Ray Lyman Wilbur for freshmen and
other new students.
In the receiving line were President
and Mrs. Wilbur, Miss Mary Yost,
Dean of Women, and George Culver,
Dean of Men.
