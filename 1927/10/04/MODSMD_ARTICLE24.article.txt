# REGISTRATION SWELLS AS 3358 MARK IS HIT
## 
### 
Enrollment figures for autumn quar
ter have continued to swell, and the
present total of registered students
stands at 3358. Of this number only
3274 have completed their registra
tion. This year's total shows consider
able increase over last year's, which
was 3190.
The number of students entering for
the first time is 910 to date, an in
crease of twenty-seven over the 192G
fall enrollment.
