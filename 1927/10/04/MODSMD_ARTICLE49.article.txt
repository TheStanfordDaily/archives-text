# U.C.L.A. REGISTRATION REACHES NEW RECORD
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 3. —Registra-
tion for the fall semester at the Uni
versity of California at Los Angeles
closed formally last week with enroll
ment figures for the state institution
here at a new record peak.
A total of 1850 freshmen, represent
j ing virtually every section of the state
and nation, are included in the enroll
ment to date .The remainder are ad
vanced students, some of them enter
ing the local institution from other
colleges and universities.
