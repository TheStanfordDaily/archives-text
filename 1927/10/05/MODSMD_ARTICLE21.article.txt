# LUTZ TO ATTEND TAX MEETING IN TORONTO
## 
### 
Professor Harvey L .Lutz of the
Department of Economics will leave
today for Toronto, Canada, where he
will attend the convention of the
United States National Tax Associa
tion. Professor Lutz is vice-president
of the Association, and while he is
there, will have charge of the com
mittee to report on revenue from pub
lic lands.
The duration of the convention will
be one week, and Professor Lutz will
return to Stanford around the twenty
first of October.
