# TRYOUTS SCHEDULED THIS AFTERNOON FOR DRAMATICS MANAGER
## 
### 
Tryouts for the 1927 dramatic man
agership, a salaried position, will be
held in the dramatic office of the Old
Union this afternoon at 2 o'clock, and
Thursday evening at 7:15, states Nor
man Larsen, present manager.
Sophomore and junior men are eligi
ble for the position. There will be a
meeting of all those who appear today,
and all others interested, in the Old
Union Thursday evening, where all
duties will be explained.
Larsen announces three plays for
the ensuing quarter, and states that
work for the tryoutees will commence
immediately.
