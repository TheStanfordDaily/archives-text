# STANFORD BAND WILL PLAY NEXT SATURDAY FOR NEVADA CONTEST
## 
### 
The Stanford Band will make its ini
tial appearance of the college year at
the Nevada game Saturday, announced
Dr. Ernest W. Martin, director, at the
first rehearsal of the organization yes
terday. Nearly 100 men were present
to begin the intensive practive which
will be required to attain the proper
degree of proficiency before the band
will take the field. Music will be
played from the stands only next Sat
urday.
It is planned to have a band of 104
pieces this season with top form at
the Big Game. At the U.S.C. game the
band will give its first full dress
parade.
