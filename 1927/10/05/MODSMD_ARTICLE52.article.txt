# Frank Taylor to Conduct Course in Article Writing
## 
### 
A three-unit course in article writ
ing will be presented this quarter by
Frank Taylor, Editor of the Illustrated
Review. Meetings will be held each
Wednesday evening at 7 o'clock in the
Pres3 Building.
Students must demonstrate their
ability in writing before being admit
ted to the course. The composition of
articles suitable for popular magazines
and Sunday magazines will constitute
a large amount of the work of the
class.
