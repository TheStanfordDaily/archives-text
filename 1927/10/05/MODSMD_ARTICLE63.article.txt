# HONOR SOCIETY WILL HOLD FIRST MEETING
## 
### 
Chi Nu Kappa, Classical Literature
honor society, will hold its first meet
ing of the quarter tomorrow night at
8 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Officers will be elected, and plans
made for the coming year.
