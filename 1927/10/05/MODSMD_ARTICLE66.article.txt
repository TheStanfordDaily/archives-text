WEDNESDAY
10:00 a.m. — University Assembly, in
the Assembly Hall. Address by Pres
ident Ray Lyman Wilbur.
1:00 p.m.—All sophomore men will
meet in Room 214.

the 1930 Quad are requested to meet
at the Manager's office in the Old
Union.

Encina Hall.

in the forthcoming tryout for the

sity debates are requested to meet
tonight in Room 201.

Club meets in Women's Clubhouse.

mittee meeting at the Old Union to
night at 7:15. All old and new mem
bers must be present.

agerial positions on the Associated
Students' Concert Series in the
A.S.S.U. office in the Old Union.
8:00 p.m.—Chi Nu Kappa will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse.
8:00 p.m.—Meeting of the Stanford
Radio Club in the "shack" in the
corporation yard. All students inter
ested in radio are requested to at
tend.
THURSDAY
7:30 p.m.—Thursday Evening Confer
ence, at the home of Dr. David Starr
Jordan. Forum: "How It Looks to
Me."

an informal luncheon in the Union

are cordially invited to be present.
