# Roble Gymnasium to Have New Instructor in Dancing
## 
### 
Mrs. Helen Cramer, '28, will take
the place of Mrs. Iluth Calvin as di
rector of dancing at the Roble Gym
nasium. Mrs. Calvin, who has been
with the department since the fall of
1924 resigned at the beginning of this
quarter.
Mrs. Cramer has studied dancing
under Miss E. D'Houbler at the
University of Wisconsin for the past
year, and it is Miss D'Houbler's type
of dancing that she will teach at Stan
ford.
