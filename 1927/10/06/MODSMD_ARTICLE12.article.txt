# DR. JORDAN RENEWS INFORMAL MEETINGS
## Will Answer Students' Queries At First Gathering of Quarter Tonight 
### 
Dr. Jordan will open his regular
series of Thursday evening lectures
tonight with an open forum meeting.
He will speak on whatever topics may
be brought before him, following the
general topic, "How It Looks to Me."
As lias been his custom during the
past few years, Dr. Jordan will hold
these weekly conferences at his home
Thursday nights at o'clock. All
talks are open to the public. His dis
cussions will cover matters of local
import, as well as national and inter
national topics of world interest.
