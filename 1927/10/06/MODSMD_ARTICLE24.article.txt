# BLACKWELDER NAMED HEAD OF DEPARTMENT OF PHYSICAL SCIENCE
## 
### 
Stanford is attempting something
new this year in a Department of
Physical Sciences, headed by Profes
sor Eliot Blackwelder, formerly pro
fessor of geology, which combines
chemistry, physics, geology, and as
tronomy.
Under the theory that all sciences
are related, this combination course
is being given in three divisions, In
troduction to Physical Sciences la,
lb, and lc, to be given autumn, winter,
and spring quarters respectively.
Professor Blackwelder will deal
with astronomy and geology this quar
ter; with chemistry and physics win
ter quarter, stressing physics; and
with the same two in the spring, with
the emphasis on chemistry.
