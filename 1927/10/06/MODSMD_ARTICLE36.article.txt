# GOEBEL NAMED COLONEL
## 
### 
OKLAHOMA CITY, Okla., Oct. 5.—
In official recognition of his flight
across the Pacific in an Oklahoma
owned airplane, Governor Henry S.
Johnson has commissioned Art Goebel
a colonel. Goebel will serve on the
Governor's staff.
