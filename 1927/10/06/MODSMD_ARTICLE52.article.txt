# Buford O. Brown Treats Of Newspaper Business Problems in New Book
## 
### 
"Business Problems of the Newspaper"
is the title of a book soon to be published
by Harpers, written by Buford O. Brown,
associate processor of journalism.
Revisions have already been finished
and the manuscript is almost completed.
Harpers will publish the book as soon as
it deems it advisable to do so.
The location of a newspaper, the finan
cing of its equipment, and the co-ordina
tion of its various departments for ef
ficient production, including matters of
income and expenditure as well as cir
culation promotion, are sonic of the
principal subjects with which Professor
Mrown deals. Newspaper law, especially
libel and contempt, are the legal aspects
which the author considers.
