# MISS WILBUR WITH RAMBEAU
## 
### 
Elizabeth Wilbur, who graduated
from Stanford last spring, is now en
gaged by the Ebey Players at the Ful
ton Theatre in Oakland. Miss Wilbur
has been playing with Marjorie Ram
beau in "Daddy's Gone A-hunting,"
and in "Compromising Sadie." She
also played with Ruth Renick in "The
Last of Mrs. Cheney."
Prominent in campus dramatics,
Elizabeth Wilbur will be remembered
for her work in such productions as
"Hay Fever" and "So This Is London."
