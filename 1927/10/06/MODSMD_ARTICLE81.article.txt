THURSDAY
1:00 p.m.—Men of the sophomore class
are to meet in Room 214 to organize
for the mud fight Friday.
12:10 p.m.—Masonic Club will meet for
an informal luncheon in the Union
Dining Room. All Master Masons
are cordially invited to be present.
4:oop.m.—Any student who has not
already completed arrangements for
gym suits report at Roble.
4:15 p.m.—Warren D. Allen will play
an informal organ program today.
Regular recitals will begin Sunday
afternoon at 4 o'clock.
7:3op.m.—There will be a rehearsal
of the Choir and Oratorio at the
Memorial Church. Those unable to
come to Monday and Wednesday
meetings may register tonight.
7:30 p.m.—Thursday Evening Confer
ence, at the home of Dr. David Starr
Jordan. Forum: "How It Looks to
Me."
ALL SUBSCRIBERS to the Chaparral
who do not receive their copies of
the first issue by noon today may
obtain it by bringing their receipt
to the Chaparral office, above the
Press Building,

ulty Clubhouse. All faculty men are
invited.
