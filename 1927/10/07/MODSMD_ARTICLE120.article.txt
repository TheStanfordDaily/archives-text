# O.A.C. Debaters Will Make Long Transcontinental Trip
## 
### 
OREGON STATE COLLEGE, Cor
vallis, Oct. 6.—(P.l.P.A.) —Plans for the
O.A.C. transcontinental debate trip this
winter are nearly completed and travel
for this year will cover from 15,000 to
18,000 miles. The debaters will meet
eighteen of the largest Pacific Coast,
Middle West and Eastern colleges and
universities.
