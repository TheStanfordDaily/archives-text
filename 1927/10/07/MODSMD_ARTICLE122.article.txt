# Novel on College Life Is Written by Berkeley Man
## 
### 
A novel entitled "The Lordly Ones,"
written by Professor Benjamin Lehman
of the English department of the Uni
versity of California, was published early
in October by Harper Brothers.
It shows college life, as does his "Wild
Marriage," which he published some
time ago. With university life as a back
ground, it is cast in some anonymous
Western university.
