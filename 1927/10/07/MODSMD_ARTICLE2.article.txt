# MEETING OF MURAL ATHLETIC MANAGERS SCHEDULED TUESDAY
## 
### 
On Tuesday afternoon, October 11,
there will be a meeting of the intramural
athletic managers with C. W. Davis,
intramural director, at 4 o'clock in En
cina Gym.
This meeting marks the beginning of
the intramural season. It is to the inter
ests of the living groups, dormitories,
and eating clubs that they send their
representatives. This committee will
have full power.
