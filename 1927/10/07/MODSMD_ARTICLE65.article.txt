# ROOTERS MAY SECURE TICKETS AGAIN MONDAY
## 
### 
A second sale of U. S. C. root
ers' tickets will be open to students in
the Old Union from 1 o'clock to 5 o'clock
Monday, announces the Board of Athletic j
Control. Preferred tickets may also be
purchased at that time.
As preparation for the rooting section
for the U.S.C. games, A1 Cass, varsity i
cheerleader, requests that all students
wear rooters' caps for the Nevada game
tomorrow. Caps may be obtained at Roos
Brothers in Palo Alto.
