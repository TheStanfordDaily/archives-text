# Women to Stage Football Rally at U.C. Next Month
## 
### 
At the Women's Football Rally, which
will be held at the University of Cali
fornia in the Greek Theatre November
17. a women's orchestra will make its
debut. This orchestra will lie open to all
women of the University, and is replac
ing the one composed of red-headed
women which was organized some time
ago to play at some of the houses.
