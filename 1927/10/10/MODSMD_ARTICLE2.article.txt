# TWENTY-SIX TRY OUT FOR YEARLING SOCCER TEAM UNDER MALONEY
## 
### 
With twenty-six signed up for fresh
man soccer, Harry Maloney, soccer
coach, is trying to get a first-year team
lined up to meet the California fresh
men on November 5. Since some of
the men have never played soccer be
fore, Maloney, with tho help of several
varsity men, has been busy teaching
them the fundamentals of the game.
The babes will have their first
tryout this week, when they will scrim
mage the varsity. Next Saturday they
will play the Pescadero High School
team, which lost to the varsity by one
goal.
