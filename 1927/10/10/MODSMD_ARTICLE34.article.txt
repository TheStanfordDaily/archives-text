# COMMITTEE DISCUSSES U.S.C. RALLY PLANS
## 
### 
Plans for the U.S.C. game rally
Wednesday night were discussed at a
meeting of the Rally Committee yes
terday afternoon in the Old Union.
The winning U.S.C. rooting section
stunt has not yet been chosen, but the
committee expects to be able to pre
sent the cup offered as a prize at the
rally Wednesday night.
Rally Committee members will meet
again tonight at 8:30 o'clock in the
Old Union.
