# MARY HARWOOD MARRIES
## 
### 
Mary Harwood, ex-'27, and John
Marble, '25, were married on June 28
at the home of the bride's parents
near Pasadena. Mrs. Marble is a mem
ber of Kappa Kappa Gamma, and her
husband is an Alpha Delta Phi. The
couple are now living in Boston, where
Marble is connected with the Harvard
Graduate School of Business.
