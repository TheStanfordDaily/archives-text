# PLANS COMPLETED FOR U.S.C. RALLY TOMORROW NIGHT
## 
### 
With final plans completed last
night by the Rally Committee,
everything is now in readiness
for the U.S.C. game rally in the
Pavilion at 7:15 o'clock tomor
row. The winning rooting sec
tion stunt has been chosen, but
the winner's name will be with
held till the rally, when the cup
will be awarded.
Dink Templeton, as the lead
ing speaker, has agreed to sum
up Stanford's chances for Satur
day, and the Stanford Band will
be there to see that action does
not lag. The complete program
will be announced tomorrow
morning.
