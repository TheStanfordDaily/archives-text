# MURRAY DRAPER WILL PARTICIPATE IN U.S.C. GOODWILL ASSEMBLY
## 
### 
Murray Draper, A.S.S.U. president,
will deliver a message of goodwill in
behalf of Stanford students at a U.S.C.
assembly to be held in Los Angeles
Thursday morning.
Draper's talk before the U.S.C. as
sembly will parallel that of the southern
university's student body president who
will speak here Friday morning.
This is the second successive year that
Stanford and U.S.C. presidents have de
livered exchange speeches on the eve of
the Cardinal-Trojan football game.
