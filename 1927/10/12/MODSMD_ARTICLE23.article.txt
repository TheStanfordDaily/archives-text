# EMERALD LAKE GOLF COURSE SECURED FOR INTRAMURAL TOURNEY
## 
### 
The Emerald Lake Golf and Coun
try Club course has been secured for
the Intramural Golf Tournament
which will probably be held on Satur
day morning, October 22, it was an
nounced yesterday by Charles Davis,
director of intramural sports.
Twenty-two two-man teams have
signed for the meet, and several more
are expected before the date of the
tournament. Participants will be al
lowed to practice at Emerald Lake.
Golf. Davis pointed out, is the open
ing event on the year's intramural
schedule, and will have considerable
bearing on the later showing of the
campus organizations.
