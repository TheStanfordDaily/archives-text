# PLAN LUNCHEON FOR NATIONAL EXECUTIVE OF STUDENT Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
In honor of David R. Porter, national
executive secretary of the student divi
sion of the Y.M.C.A., a luncheon will be
given at 12:15 o'clock today in the small
dining room of the Old Union.
Porter, who has been associated with
Y.M.C.A. work for twenty-eight years,
will speak on"The Student Movement
in the United States." Tickets arc on sale
at the Y.W.C.A. and Y.M.C.A. for fifty
cents cach. All students interested are
invited to attend the luncheon, which will
be over in time for one-o'clock classes.
