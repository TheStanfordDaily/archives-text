# SEQUOIA CLUB ELECTS NEW SET OF OFFICERS
## 
### 
With all votes in yesterday, Francis
Morgan was elected president of Sequoia
Club for the autumn quarter. He will
be assisted by Don Murray, vice-presi
dent; Jack How, secretary; and Chester
Luther, treasurer.
The house committee elected included
Moroni Jameson, Dallas Ayers, Eric Hill,
John O'Sullivan, and Howard Piatt. For
the social committee were chosen: Stan
ley Swain, chairman; Bob Harkness,
Russ Waite, Don Ruppe, Verne King.
Lawrence Johnson was chosen to head
the athletic committee. Other members
are Verne King and Morton Flint.
