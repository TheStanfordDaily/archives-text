# HUTCHINSON, COLDER COLLECTING DATA ON SITUATION IN RUSSIA
## 
### 
Professor Frank A. Golder and Dr.
Lincoln Hutchinson are now in Russia
in connection with studies which they are
undertaking for a period of years and
the outcome of which is scheduled for
publication through the Stanford Uni
versity Press. They are expected to re
turn sometime this winter.
"On the Trail of the Russian Famine,"
published recently by the University
Press, was the result of Professor
Golder's and Dr. Hutchinson's work in
Russia during the post-war years. Much
valuable source material is contained in
the book.
