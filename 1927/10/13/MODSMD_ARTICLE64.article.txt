# FRESHMEN WILL MEET TO FORM ENCINA CLUB
## 
### 
Freshmen men will meet in the
lobby of Encina next Tuesday eve-
ning to organize into Encina Club.
Clark Cypher, head sponsor, will act
as chairman, since no president has
been elected. Stanford Steinbeck,
president of the Sophomore Class, and
of Encina Club last quar
ter, will speak concerning elections
and other things of interest to Encina
Club. Officers will not be elected until
a later meeting.
