# PUBLICATIONS HEADS DISCUSS PLANS FOR PROPOSED CHARTERS
## 
### 
Plans for the formulation of char
ters for the various publications on
the campus were discussed yesterday
afternoon at the meeting of the Pub
lications Council. The Stanford Daily,
The Literary Magazine, The Chapar
ral, and the Stanford Quad, are repre
sented on this Council by the respec
tive editors and business managers
of each.
The members of the Publications
Council are: Professor Everett W.
Smith, head of the Division of Jour
nalism, Fred Speers, Claude Conn,
Herbert Reynolds, Mac Lewis, Mar
garet Olsen, Norman Walsh, Winston
Norman, and Dick Guggenheim.
