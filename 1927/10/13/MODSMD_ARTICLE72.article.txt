# ZOOLOGY CLUB HEARS TALK ON SHELL FISH
## 
### 
"Shell Fish of Alaska" was the topic
of a lecture given last night before the
Stanford Zoology Club by Professor
Frank Walter Weymouth of the Depart
ment of Physiology. The meeting was
held at the home of Professor and Mrs.
John Otterbein Snyder, 8 Alvarado Road.
Dr. Weymouth spoke of his own ex
periences in Alaska while making a study
of clams and other shell fish there which
arc of economic importance.
The Zoology Club consists of zoology
students of the University and mefets two
or three times during the quarter for
social and scientific purposes. Richard S.
Croker is president of the organization
for the current year.
