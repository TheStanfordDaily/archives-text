# Women's Chorus Practice Postponed Until Tuesday
## 
### 
Rehearsal for the Women's Chorus of
the University Choir, scheduled for to
night, will be postponed to Tuesday eve
ning, October 18, in Room N4. This will
permit the women of the choir to attend
the Women's Conference party tonight in
the Women's Clubhouse.
All men of the choir and oratorio are
expected tonight at 7 :30 o'clock, announ
ces Warren D. Allen, choir director.
