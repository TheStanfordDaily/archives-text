# FINE ENTERTAINMENT FEATURES PROMISED AT BRANNER SMOKER
## 
### 
Entertainment de luxe will bo the
special feature of the first Branner
Club Smoker of the year tonight at
7:15. John Clark, who is in charge of
the program, has arranged some in
teresting numbers.
A1 Cass will favor the club with
some of his songs, and Adelbert Cul
ling will give a vocal solo. John Tru
man will give an informal speech, and
a piano solo will be rendered by Law
rence Weinberg. The festivities will
be concluded by a piano and banjo
number given by W. C. Seaman and
Bill Leavitt.
