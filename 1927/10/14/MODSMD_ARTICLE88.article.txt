# STANFORD CALENDARS SOLD AT BOOKSTORE
## 
### 
A complete calendar of University
administrative events for the aca
demic year, together with a regular
calendar of the months, has heen
printed and placed on sale this quar
ter at the Comptroller's office and at
the Stanford Bookstore.
Printed with a view to aiding stu
dents and faculty members in the
knowledge of University dates, the
calendar gives official dates of events
of each quarter, telling the time set
for changing study lists, filing degree
applications, vacations, and examina
tions.
