# TWO W.S.C. MUSICIANS VIE FOR $5000 PRIZE
## 
### 
Two music students of Washington
State College have been chosen to
represent the college in the Atwater-
Kent Radio Company's national audi
tion contests for singers.
State elimination contests will take
place at Seattle and Spokane and a
district contest at San Francisco.
The district winners, a man and wo
man, will go to New York where $5000
and a two-year scholarship in a well
known musical school will be pre
sented to the winners of the two first
prizes. The second, third, and fourth
prizes are $2000, $1000', and $500, re
spectively.
Atwater-Kent judges and radio
audiences will each have half the
votes. Names of contestants will not
be disclosed during the trial.
