# MARY CHASE AWARDED PHILOSOPHY DEGREE
## 
### 
Mary Katherine Chase, '22, was
given the degree of doctor of philo
sophy and literature with the greatest
distinction from the University of
Brussels last week, according to word
received here Saturday.
Miss Chase received her A.B. from
Stanford in 1922 and took graduate
work the following year, serving as a
teaching assistant in her department.
She is the daughter of Mrs. Mary
Chase of Palo Alto and the sister of
Miss Elizabeth Chase.
