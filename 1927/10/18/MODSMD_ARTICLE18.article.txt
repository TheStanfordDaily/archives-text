### 
Ruth Tinsley, women's debate man
ager, announces that tryouts for a de
bate with the University of California
scheduled for the latter part of No
vember, will be held at 7:30 o'clock
Thursday night in the Little Theatre. ;
Speeches should be from three to ]
five minutes in length, and may be :
made on either of the two questions:
"Resolved, That under the present so
cial and economic system women can
never attain a status of equality," or:
"Resolved, That women are inferior
to men." The subject of the California
