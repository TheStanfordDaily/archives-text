# German Club Will Hold First Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
A reorganization meeting of the |
German Club will be held at 7:30
o'clock tomorrow night in the tea
room of the Women's Clubhouse. Pro
fessor William A. Cooper of the Ger
man department will preside over the
meeting, which will be for the pur
pose of outlining plans for the com
ing year and deciding on the night
for regular meetings.
