# Freshmen Plan Jolly-Up At Roble Hall Tonight
## 
### 
The first freshman jolly-up of the
year will be held tonight from 6:30
to 8 o'clock at Roble. A freshman 7-
piece orchestra will furnish music.
Betty Alden, chairman of the fresh
man women, is in charge of the jolly
up. Dinner will be served early to
allow all the Encina men to get to
Roble by 6:30 o'clock.
