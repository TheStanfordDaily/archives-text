# EDITORIAL, BUSINESS TRYOUTEES OF "LIT" WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Tryoutees for both the editorial and
managerial staffs of the Stanford Lit
erary Magazine will meet in the of
fice of the publication this evening
at 5:15 o'clock.
The meeting is to be an important
one at which the exact organization
of the magazine and of the tryoutee
system will be discussed. Plans for a
banquet to be held in the near future
will be discussed also. One of the
purposes of the meeting is to foster
a spirit of co-operation between the
two staffs.
