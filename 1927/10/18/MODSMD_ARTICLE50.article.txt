# Mothers' Club Will Hear Women's Activity Leaders
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost, Harriet Cogswell,
Y.W.C.A. secretary, and leaders of the
various women's activities will speak
before a meeting of the Stanford
Mothers' Club at 3 o'clock this after
noon in the Women's Clubhouse.
Eleanor Davidson, president of the
A.W.S.; Aileen Brown, president of
Cap and Gown; Barbara Fenwick,
president of W.A.A.; and Winifred
Huntington, president of the Y.W.C.A.,
will be the student speakers on the
program.
