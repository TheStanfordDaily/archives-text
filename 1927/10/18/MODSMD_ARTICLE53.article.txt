# GIRL SCOUTING TOPIC OF TALK TOMORROW
## 
### 
Girl Scouting will be the subject of
an open meeting tomorrow night at
7:15 o'clock in the Y.W.C.A. rooms
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse. Miss Philipson, Girl Scout
leader for the Palo Alto section of tho
Girl Scouts, will lead the meeting.
Every Thursday, beginning this
week, she will conduct a class in
scouting from 1:30 to 3:15 o'clock in
the main room of the Clubhouse.
