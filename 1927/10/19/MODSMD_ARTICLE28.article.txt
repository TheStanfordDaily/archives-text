# AMERICAN FEDERATION OF LABOR HEAD WILL SPEAK AT SAN JOSE
## 
### 
William Creen, president of the
American Federation of Labor, will
speak at the San Jose High School
Auditorium Saturday evening at 8
o'clock.
Mr. Green is making a tour of Cali
fornia after a two weeks' convention
held in Los Angeles, where he was
re-elected head of the labor organiza
tion.
The meeting Saturday night will be
open to the public. No admission will
be charged.
