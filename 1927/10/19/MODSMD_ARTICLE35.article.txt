# Getting It Straight
## 
### 
Questions may be mailed to the "In
formation Editor," left at the Daily
office, or phoned to the paper at PA
1074. They need not be signed, but
must concern University or student
affairs.

Question: Where do the Lagunita
frogs go in the winter?
Answer by Professor Charles V.
: Taylor, professor of biology:
It is their habit to hibernate. They
; burrow into the mud two or three feet
; and lie dormant until spring. Then
; they revive and croak in the evenings.
: Follows mating season and pollywogs.

Question: Why must postage due be
paid on the Stanford Daily when it
is received through the Campus P. 0.?
Answer by James Frank, advertis
ing manager of the Daily:
As the P.O. is now a branch of the
Palo Alto office additional postage
has to be paid on Campus mail. The
extra cent will be paid by the Daily
; as soon as the mailing list can be re
| arranged tomorrow.
