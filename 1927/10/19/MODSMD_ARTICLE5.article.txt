# MISS PHILIPSON WILL TALK ON GIRL SCOUT WORK AS PROFESSION
## 
### 
Freshman women and any older stu
dents interested in Girl Scout work
will be addressed by Miss Alice Phil
ipson at 7:15 o'clock tonight in the
Y.W.C.A. rooms on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse. Miss Philip
son is Girl Scout leader for the Palo
Alto section of the Girl Scouts.
Miss Philipson will speak on Girl
Scouting in general and as a profes
sion.
Every Thursday, beginning this
week, she will conduct a class in
scouting from 1:30 to 3:15 o'clock in
the main room of the clubhouse.
