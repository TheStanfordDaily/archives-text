# DR. WILLIS TO SPEAK ON EARTHQUAKE LURE
## 
### 
Dr. Bailey Willis, professor emeritus
of geology, will speak on"The Lure
of the Earthquake" today at a com
munity luncheon sponsored by the
Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce. The
luncheon, which is to be held in the
Sunset Cafeteria at 12:10 o'clock, is
open to the public.
Dr. Willis will describe his recently
completed trip to various parts of the
world in quest of earthquakes.
