# DR. MARX HONORED BY AMERICAN ENGINEERS
## 
### 
The election of Dr. C. D. Marx, Pro
fessor Emeritus of Civil Engineering, to
honorary membership in the American
Society of Civil Engineers, was an
nounced Tuesday evening at a dinner of
the San Francisco section. Dr. Marx
was formerly National President of the
organization.
Honorary membership is limited to
twenty, and but fifteen men hold this
distinction at the present time.
