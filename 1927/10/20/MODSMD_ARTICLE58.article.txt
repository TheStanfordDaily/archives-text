# "NO MILITARY DRILL" IS WISCONSIN PLEA
## 
### 
Complete abolition of military drill is
being advocated by the Daily Cardinal,
student newspaper at the University of
Wisconsin. Already the state legislature
of Wisconsin has made R.O.T.C. work
at the institution voluntary. It is stated
that Northwestern University is contem
plating complete elimination of R.O.T.C.
courscs from its Curriculum.
