# DR. JORDAN TO SPEAK ON "ORIGIN OF MAN"
## 
### 
Dr. Jordan will discuss "The Origin
of Man" tonight at his regular Thurs
day Evening Conference. He will ex
plain that although some details of
the subject are uncertain, there is no
doubt about the main fact that man
and all members of the ape family are
of a common origin. When Dr. Jordan
was a professor here he gave a course
of lectures on this subject.
The talk, open to the public, will be
held at Dr. Jordan's home at 7:30
o'clock.
