# Men's Glee Club Tryouts Scheduled Next Monday
## 
### 
Tryouts for Men's Glee Club will
be held at 4:15 o'clock Monday in
Room 2GOC, Engineering Building.
C. Albert Kuhlmann, director, in an
nouncing the first meeting of the or
ganization, requests that freshmen
and other new* men attend.
The club will include in its reper
toire this season an increased num
ber of modern selections, at the same
time maintaining a high standard of
music, says Kuhlmann.
Tryouts for accompanist will be
held in Room 2GOC at 4:15 o'clock
today.
