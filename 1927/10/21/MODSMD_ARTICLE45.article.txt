# MRS. BRANDSTEN TO LEAVE DURING WEEK FOR EUROPEAN TRIP
## 
### 
Mrs. Greta Brandsten, director at
Roble Gymnasium, will leave next
week for an extended tour of Europe.
After spending Christmas with rela
tives in Sweden, she will travel on
the Continent, probably not returning
to the campus until next March.
Miss Marjorie Landon, from the
Boston School of Physical Education,
will take Mrs. Brandsten's place until
her return. Miss Landon has been
teaching at normal school in Keen,
New Hampshire, where she special
ized largely in swimming. She is
qualified by the Red Cross for life
saving.
Ernst Brahdsten, swimming coach,
will not accompany Mrs. Brandsten
on her European tour.
