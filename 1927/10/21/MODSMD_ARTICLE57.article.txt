# NEW WOMEN INVITED TO LEARN PURPOSES OF CAMPUS Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Stanford Y.W.C.A. will hold its first
open meeting of the quarter next
Tuesday night at 7:15 o'clock in the
tearoom of the Women's Clubhouse,
announces Winifred Huntington, pres
ident of the organization.
Although this meeting will be chief
ly for the purpose of acquainting new
women with the purposes of the
Y.W.C.A., all University women are
invited.
Addresses will be made by Harriet
M. Cogswell, Stanford Y.W.C.A. secre
tary, and Winifred Huntington. Mem
bers of Senior Cabinet will explain
various phases of the association's
work, and the freshmen and other
new women will be given an oppor
tunity to sign up for activities in
which they are interested.
