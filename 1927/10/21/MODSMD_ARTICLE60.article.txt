# OREGON WILL ADOPT CALIFORNIA SYSTEM OF GRID MANAGERS
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., Oct. 20.—(P.1.P.A.)
—California's system of football man
agership has proved so successful as
to warrant its installation at Oregon,
in Ihe opinion of Frank German, foot
ball manager.
Under the California system there
is a senior manager and under him
are five junior assistants. Bach junior
assistant has five sophomore helpers.
The work in managing a football sea
son is divided into five parts: training
quarters, care of field, visiting teams,
field practice, and general manage
ment.
