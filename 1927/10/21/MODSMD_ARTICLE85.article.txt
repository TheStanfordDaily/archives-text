# CARROLL RECEIVES PAROLE
## 
### 
ATLANTA, Oct. 20.—Earl Carroll's
parole papers arrived at the Federal Pen
itentiary tonight. The theatrical producer
was released immediately to join his
wife and brother in a mountain hotel.
