# Dietrich Speaks Before U.C. Mining Association
## 
### 
Waldemar F. Dietrich, associate pro
fessor of mining, addressed the Min
ing Association of the University of
California Tuesday night on the "Clay
Deposits and Ceramic Industry of
California."
Thomas Berray, President of the
Mining Association, suggested that
Professor Dietrich speak on this topic
since it was one which was not cov
ered by courses offered at the Univer
sity of California.
