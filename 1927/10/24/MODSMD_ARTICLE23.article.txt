# SPECIALTIES WANTED FOR FOOTBALL SHOW; TRYOUTS TOMORROW
## 
### 
All kinds of specialty actors are
urged to come out for Football Show
tryouts, tomorrow at 3 o'clock, at the
Assembly Hall. Monologuists, pairs
with vaudeville skits, and specialties
in musical instruments are particu
larly wanted.
Frances Boone will have charge of
the chorus work, and will announce
the date for chorus tryoutees later.
Women who wish to try out for the
chorus are, however, asked to report
torqorrow so that some idea of the
material on the campus this year may
be gained.
Original ideas for skits and stunts
are also wanted. In case men and
women with such ideas are unable to
report at the Assembly Hall, manu
scripts may be sent to A1 Cass, Zeta
Psi house.
