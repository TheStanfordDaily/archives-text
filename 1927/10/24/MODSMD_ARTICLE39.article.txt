# ISSUE SCHEDULE FOR LAW LIBRARY HOURS
## 
### 
The following hours are announced
as the new schedule in operation at
the Law Library: week days from 8
o'clock at night; Saturday mornings
the evening, and from 7 o'clock to 10
o'clock at night! Saturday mornings
from 8 to 10 o'clock; and Sundays
from 2 to 5 o'clock in the afternoon.
Students will have free access as
before to the stacks and to all reports,
but all books used in current courses
will be held on reserve.
