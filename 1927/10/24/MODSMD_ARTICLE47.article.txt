# SCHOLARSHIP BRIDGE TEA TO BE TONIGHT

### 
Miss Dorothy Putnam, secretary
of the School of Education, will be
joint hostess with Mrs. Margaret
Wells at the Annual Scholarship

this evening at the Business and Pro
fessional Women's Clubhouse.
Tables may be secured by tele
phoning the Community House before
noon, Miss Putnam announced.
Proceeds of the tea will go to pro
vide a scholarship for a girl gradu
ating from the Palo Alto High School.
Susan Hays, now a student at 0.A.C.,
