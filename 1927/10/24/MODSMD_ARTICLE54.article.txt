# Fog Causes Ship to Go Aground in Golden Gale
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 23. —
Grounded in a heavy fog, the Coos
Bay, largest lumber carrier of the
Pacific Coast lumber fleet, was hard
and fast upon the rocky reefs off
Land's End in the Golden Gate, Sun
day.
