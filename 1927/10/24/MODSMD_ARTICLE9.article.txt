# PROFESSOR ICHIHASHI WILL SPEAK AT OPEN MEETING OF Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Professor Yamato Ichihashi will
speak on "Foreign Interests Repre
sented by the Y.W.C.A." at the open
meeting of the Y.W.C.A. tomorrow
evening at 7:15 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
Other speakers will be Winifred
Huntington, president of the Stanford
organization, and Anna Fitzhugh, '25,
member of the local advisory board.
The topic of Miss Fitzhugli's talk will
be "The Lasting Influence of the
Y.W.C.A." Commission leaders will
explain their various commissions to
the new women. These include: So
cial Service, Religious Life, World
Education, Discussion Groups, Fo
rums, Finance, and Girls' Work.
