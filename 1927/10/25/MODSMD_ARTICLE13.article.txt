# Graduate Women's Club To Meet for Tea Today
## 
### 
The Graduate Women's Club will
meet today from 4:30 to 6 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. There will
be a short business meeting, and tea
will be served by the hostesses, Ber
neise Little, Hannah Foote, and Patri
cia Harrison. All graduate women stu
dents on and off the campus are in
vited to attend.
