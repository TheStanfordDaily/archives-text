# HUGH BROWN TO SPEAK AT LAW CLUB SMOKER
## 
### 
Hugh Brown, '96, a San Francisco
attorney, will be the speaker of the
evening at the Law Association
smoker to be held at 7:30 o'clock to
night in the Old Union.
A boxing bout between Strent
Hanna and Ed Dillon and several
musical numbers including selections
by a male quartet and banjo solos will
be other features of the program.
