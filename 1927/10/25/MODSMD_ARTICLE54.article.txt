# Sophomore Cabinet Will Give Lunch to New Women
## 
### 
New sophomore women will be the
guests of Sophomore Cabinet at lunch
Thursday in the Y.W.C.A. office at the
Women's Clubhouse, announces Ben
etta Merner, president of Sophmore
Cabinet.
Aims of the Cabinet will be ex
plained to the new sophomores and
plans for a reorganization of the body
will be discussed.
