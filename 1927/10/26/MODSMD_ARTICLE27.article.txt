# FOWLER WILL SPEAK ON COLORADO RIVER PROJECT AT MEETING
## 
### 
"The Colorado River Project" is the
subject of a lecture to be given by
Frederick Hall Fowler, '05, before a
meeting of the student section of the
American Society of Civil Engineers
tonight at 7:45 o'clock at the home of
Professor Reynolds, 553 Middlefield
Road, Palo Alto.
Fowler, who is a prominent consult
ing engineer of San Francisco, is
especially interested in hydro-electric
engineering which will prove a big
factor in the development of the river
project. His lecture will be illustrated
with moving pictures and slides.
The meeting will be held at 7:45
o'clock at the home of Professor Rey
nolds, 833 Middlefield Road, Palo Alto.
