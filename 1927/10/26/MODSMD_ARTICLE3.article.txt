# W.A.A. Refers Intramural Competition to Societies
## 
### 
Intramural competition for women will
be referred to the sororities, and W.A.A.
will base its final decision on their ad
vice, it was decided at a meeting of
W.A.A. Board last night. If it meets
the approval of the campus living
groups, competition will start in winter
quarter with basketball.
In the discussion of training rules, the
restriction on smoking was removed, hut
the Board strongly advises that women
taking part in athletic competition do not
smoke.
