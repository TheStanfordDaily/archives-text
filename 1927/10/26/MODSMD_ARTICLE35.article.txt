# Women's Glee Club Holds Regular Practice Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Glee Club
will hold regular practice tonight at 7
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Members are urged to be prompt,
since the singing lasts only till 8
o'clock. Interested women may join
the club at this meeting. There are
no tryouts.
