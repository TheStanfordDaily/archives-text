# Belgian Student Will Be Speaker for French Club
## 
### 
Robert E. De Strycker, scholarship
student from Belgium, will be the
speaker at the meeting of the French
Club in the Women's Clubhouse at 8
o'clock tonight. This is the first meet
ing of the club for the year.
He will compare Belgian and Ameri
can universities. De Strycker, who is
a graduate student in mining engi
neering, is one of the three Belgians
here on scholarships this year.
All those interested in French are
invited to the meeting.
