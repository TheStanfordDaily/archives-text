# COMMISSION LAWYER TALKS TO STUDENTS ON TRANSPORTATION
## 
### 
Carl Wheat, the chief attorney of
the California Railroad Commission,
spoke yesterday morning to the class
in Railway Transportation on the sub
ject of State Regulation. He described
the work of the California Commis
sion, and discussed some of the prob
lems with which it has to deal.
Mr. Wheat took his law degree at
Harvard University. He has been con
nected with the Commission for five
years, and during that time has fre
quently argued cases before the Inter
state Commerce Commission and the
Supreme Court of the United States.
