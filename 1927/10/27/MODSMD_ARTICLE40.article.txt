# Trojan Women Rooters to Wear Cardinal, Gold Hats
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN
CALIFORNIA, Oct. 26 (P.1.P.A.). —
Trojan women rooters will don special
cardinal and gold hats, similar to those
of the men's rooting section, for the first
time Saturday at the California football
game.
