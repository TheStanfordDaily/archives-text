# Y.W.C.A. CABINET TO ENTERTAIN AT LUNCH
## 
### 
Sophomore Cabinet of the Y.W.C.A.
will entertain all new sophomore women
at lunch today in the Y.W.C.A. rooms
on the third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Benetta Merner, president of the cabi
net, will explain the aims of the organi
zation. Plans for a possible division of
the cabinet will be considered.
