# LAW LIBRARY POSTS SCHEDULE OF HOURS
## 
### 
The corrected schedule for the
hours the Law Library is open is as
follows: week days from 8 a.m. to
C p.m., and from 7 p.m. to 10 p.m.;
Saturdays from 8 a.m. to noon; and
Sundays from 12 p.m. to 5 p.m.
Books used in current courses will
be held on reserve, but free access to
the stacks and to all reports will be
given as before.
