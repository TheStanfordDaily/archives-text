# Al Cass Issues Call for Yell Leader Tryoutees
## 
### 
All sophomores wishing to try out for
yell leader are to meet in the Old Union
Monday night at 8 :45 o'clock, announces
A1 Cass, yell leader. Classes in yell
leading will be conducted, and tryoutees
will work for the Rally Committee this
quarter. Spring quarter, six tryoutees
will be picked by the Rally Committee to
appear on the ballot at the regular spring
Quad election. The two receiving the
most votes will be assistant yell leaders
next year, and one of the two will be
yell leader the following year.
