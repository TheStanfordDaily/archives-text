# CLASSMATES MARRY
## 
### 
Kathryn von Stein, '26, and Edmund
Barker, '26, were married on August 28
at the bride's home in Ontario. They
sailed for New York via the Panama
Canal on their honeymoon. Mrs. Barker
plans to take her Master's degree in
Spanish at Radcliffe and Barker will
continue his study of law in the East.
