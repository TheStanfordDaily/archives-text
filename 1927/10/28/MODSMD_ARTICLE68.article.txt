# RUTH ELDER LEAVES MADRID
## 
### 
MADRID, Spain, Oct. 27.—Miss Ruth
Elder and Captain George Ilaldeman left
Madrid for Bayonne, France, at 10 p.m.
The American fliers were scheduled to
go to Paris before sailing for the United
States Saturday.
