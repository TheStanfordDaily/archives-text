# DISEASE CONTRACTED IN HOSPITAL CAUSES DEATH OF GRINSTEAD
## 
### 
Dr. Marion Owen Grinstead, who
completed the pre-medical course at
Stanford in 1923, died in San Fran
cisco Friday night following an at
tack of scarlet fever which he con
tracted while attending Dr. Henry
Francis Wagner o the San Francisco
Hospital.
Grinstead received his A.B. from
the University in 1923 and completed
his course at the University of Cali
fornia last June. He joined the staff
of the San Francisco Hospital in July
and was specializing in infectious
diseases.
