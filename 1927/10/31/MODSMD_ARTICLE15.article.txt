# Stanford "Lit" Postpones Deadline for Contributions
## 
### 
The deadline for contributions for
the Stanford Literary Magazine has
been postponed to 2 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon, announces Margaret Olsen,
editor.
Contributions from freshmen and
sophomores are especially requested.
Light short stories and essays con
cerning student opinion are wanted.
Plans for a banquet of the staff and
tryoutees of the magazine will be
onsidered at a meeting at 5:15 o'clock
today in the Press building. The sub
scriptions will be checked on at this
meeting and further work assigned.
