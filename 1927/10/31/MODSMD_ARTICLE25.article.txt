# CARDS DEFEAT MENLO CIRCUS CLUB BY 16-0 SCORE IN POLO GAME
## 
### 
Playing against men that lacked
teamwork Stanford's polo squad
raced up and down the local field last
Friday to beat the Menlo Circus Club
by a 16-0 score. While the opposition
was slight, the Cardinal men showed
both by their defensive and offensive
work that they have a good chance
to annex again the coast champion
ship.
McDougal was high-point man with
nine goals to his credit. ' Captain
Thompson, playing number four, was
a stone wall on the defense. Wey
mouth and Connolly were at one and
three respectively.
The next game is scheduled on the
campus for November 15 with the 30th
Infantry quartet from Monterey.
