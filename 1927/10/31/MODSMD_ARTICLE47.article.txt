Notices for this column should be handed in
at the Stanford Daily office by 9 o clock of
the evening preceding publication.
MONDAY
5:15 p.m.—All statf members and try
outees for the Stanford Literary
Magazine will meet at the office of
the publication.
9:00 p.m.—Rally Committee meeting
in Oitii Union.
8:45 p.m.—Tryoutees for assistant
yell leader will meet at the Old
Union.
TUESDAY
7:30 p.m. — Sophomore Cabinet of
Y.W.C.A. will meet upstairs in the
Women's Clubhouse.
MONDAY
5:15 p.m.Pan-Hellenic meeting in the
Women's Clubhouse.
TUESDAY
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital by Mr. Allen
in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—Lecture by Abb<§ Ernest
Dimnet in the Little Theater. Sub
ject: "From a Paris Balcony."
WEDNESDAY
4:00 to G:00 p.m.—Miss Yost will be
at home to the women of the Uni
versity, informally.
8:00 p.m.—Zoology Club in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
THURSDAY
3:15 p.m. — Stanford Dames in the
Women's Clubhouse. Wives of new
students are especially invited.
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital by Mr. Allen
in the Memorial Church.
8:00 p.m.—Concert by Ignaz Fried
man, pianist, in the Pavilion.
