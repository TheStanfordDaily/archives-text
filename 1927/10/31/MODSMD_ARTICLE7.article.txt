# 1931 FOOTBALL MAN IN ACCIDENT AS CAR SKIDS IN HEAVY FOG
## 
### 
Paul Magee, freshman football
player, was cut and bruised about the
head in an automobile accident last
Saturday morning at Larkin and Ful
ton streets, San Francisco.
Magee was riding in the front seat,
of an open car with Marion O. Cavitt,
'31, and Bruce Magee, '29, who was
driving. In the heavy fog, the car
skidded on the wet pavement and
crashed into a lamp post, catapulting
him through the windshield. The
other two students were not hurt,
but Magee may be Incapacitated for
the rest of the football season.
