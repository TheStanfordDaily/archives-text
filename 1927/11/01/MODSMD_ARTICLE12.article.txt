# Menlo Rummage Sale to Benefit Convalescent Home
## 
### 
A rummage sale to raise money for
the Convalescent Home building fund
will be held today and tomorrow in
the Menlo Park Bank building on the
state highway. The sale will open at
9 o'clock in the morning.
Articles on sale will include cut
glass, toys, vases, men's clothing, wo
men's and children's garments, books,
ornaments, and pictures.
