# PUBLICATIONS COUNCIL CONSIDERS PLANS FOR DISPOSITION OF FUND
## 
### 
Question of handling publication
funds will be discussed at a meeting
of the Publications Council this after
noon at 4 o'clock in the Press Build
ing. Robert Cecil, student manager,
and Murray Draper, A.S.S.U. presi
dent, together with a sub-committee
appointed by the Executive Committee
from its own members, consisting of
John Van Amringe, Robert Fraser, and
Edward Krough, will meet with the
Council.
The plan of putting publication
funds under the Students' Organiza
tions Fund to be controlled by the
Executive Committee was taken up by
the Publications Council at last Wed
nesday's meeting, and will be further
discussed tomorrow.
