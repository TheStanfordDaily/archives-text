# Y.W.C.A. World Education Committee Meets Sunday
## 
### 
The World Education Commission
of the Y.W.C.A. met for supper at the
home of Mrs. R. B. Cherington in
Palo Alto Sunday evening, and dis
cussed "Movies and International Re
lations." They talked of the effect of
certain scenes and thrillers on public
opinion and on racial feeling. The
commission expects to make a study
of this problem during the year.
