# ALMACK REVISION OF TERMAN TEXTBOOK IS PREPARED FOR PRESS
## 
### 
"Hygiene of the School Child," a
book written in 1914 by Dr. Lewis M.
Terman, professor of psychology, was
recently revised and enlarged by Dr.
John C. Almack under the supervision
of Dr. Terman.
The war and the recent progress of
knowledge in this field have insti
gated this revision. The book is ready
for publication now and will be off
the press in July. It is being published
by the Houghton Mifflin Company of
Boston in the Riverside Education
Series of which Ellwood P. Cubberley,
professor of education and dean of
the school of education, is the editor.
