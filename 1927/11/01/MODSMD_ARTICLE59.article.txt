# PAN-HELLENIC AMENDS RUSHING REGULATIONS
## 
### 
The privilege enabling sisters who live
in Rohle and on the Row to he together
at all times in any place was extended
by Panhellenic Society at its regular
meeting last night. The movement was
provided to amend the previous rule re
quiring sisters to he together only in the
presence of members of a different so
rority or of Rohle.
The question of allowing a greater
variety in table decorations was also dis
cussed and given for consideration to
members of each sorority.
