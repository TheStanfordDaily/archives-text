Mammy's Log Cabin
When you're speeding down the highway
And I/linking where you'll stray
Why just stop in at MAMMY'S,
You'll never regret the day.
The food, the music, the dancing,
Oh Hoy! It's a Cabin of fun,
So just come in to MAMMY'S
Where you'll find everyone.
