# HONORARY ART SOCIETY TO HOLD PICNIC TODAY
## 
### 
All art majors are invited to at
tend a picnic given this afternoon in
Hie Santa Cruz mountains by Delta
Epsilon, honorary art fraternity, at
the ranch of Elizabeth Williams. The
picnickers will meet at the Art De
partment at 4 o'clock, and start for
the ranch. A charge of 25 cents will be
made.
Officers of Delta Epsilon are: Lu
cille Lyon, president; Mary Denny,
secretary, and Bob Philippi, treasurer.
