# SOCCER SQUAD PLAYS LAST CONTEST TODAY BEFORE BEAR SERIES
## 
### 
Playing their last practice contest
beford the three-game series with
TT.C., varsity soccer men will meet the
San Francisco Theological Seminary
at 4 o'clock today on the varsity field.
Since the defeat by the Teutonians
last Saturday, the men have been
shifted to different positions to
strengthen the forward line. The team
has a strong defense, but must de
velop a scoring machine if it is to
beat California, said Coach Harry
Maloney yesterday.
