# Piano Solo Will Feature Stanford Dames Program
## 
### 
A piano solo by Miss Victoria Par
son and a dance by Miss Marian Lewis
will be the features on the program at
a meeting of the Stanford Dames to
morrow afternoon at 3:15 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. A short business
meeting will precede the entertainment,
states Mrs. G. A. Duncan, president.
