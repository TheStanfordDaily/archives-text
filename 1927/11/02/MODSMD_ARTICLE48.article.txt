# Sophomore Cabinet Flans To Meet Every Thursday
## 
### 
At a meeting of Sophomore Cabinet
of Y.W.C.A. last night it was decided
to meet every Thursday from 7 to 8
o'clock, and that they will nut meet for
supper as has been the custom previously.
A picnic or dinner will probably be held
every quarter.
Plans for having round table discus
sions with different professors of the
University were discussed.
