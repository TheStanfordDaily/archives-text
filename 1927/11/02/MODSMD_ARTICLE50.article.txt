# Chinese Revolution Chosen Subject of Y.W.C.A. Forum
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. will hold an open forum
at 8 o'clock tonight in its rooms up
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse. The
subject will be, "A Brief Survey of the
Second Revolution in China." Two Chi
nese students will be present to discuss
this subjcct and to answer questions
concerning China.
May Hardy, chairman of the World
Education Commission of the Y.W.C.A.,
and Margaret Owen, of the forums com
mittee, are in charge of the meeting.
