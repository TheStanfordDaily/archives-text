# Myers, Fry Will Address Zoology Club on Rare Fish
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of the
Zoology Club this evening at
8 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
George S. Myers will talk about some
little-known South American fish, and
Donald 11. Fry will tell about Cali
fornia fish and fishing. All students
and faculty members interested in
zoology are invited.
