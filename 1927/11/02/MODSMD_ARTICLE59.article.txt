# Daily Business Staff Will Hold Confernce Tonight
## 
###

in the various departments of the Stan
ford Daily business staff at a meeting
of the staff Wednesday evening at 7
o'clock in the business office of the paper.
The organization of the staff and its
plans for the future will be discussed.
The entire staff will be present.
