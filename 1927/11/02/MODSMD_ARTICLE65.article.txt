# Dean Yost Holds First Tea Of Quarter This Afternoon
## 
### 
After having been prevented for
several weeks from holding her regu
lar Wednesday afternoon teas because
of the press of office work, Dean Mary
Yost will be at home to her friends
again this afternoon from four until
six o'clock.
Miss Yost plans to hold informal
teas every Wednesday afternoon dur
ing the rest of the quarter and in
vites all campus women to attend.
