# RADIO CLUB WILL GO TO LOCAL PLANT OF FEDERAL TELEGRAPH
## 
### 
The Stanford Radio Club at a meet
ing in the radio shack last night decided
to get speakers on non-technical sub
jects for future meetings and to make
an inspection trip to the Federal Tele
graph Company's local station, provid
ing the increase in membership of the
club warrants the trip, according to Fos
ter B. Detrick, president of the club.
The next meeting of the club will be
held Wednesday, November 9, at 7:30
o'clock in Room 268. All interested stu
dents, especially freshmen, are invited
to attend.
