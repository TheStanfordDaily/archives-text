# Masons Will Exchange Conferring of Degrees
## 
### 
Arrangements were completed at the
first meeting of the Stanford Masonic
Club last night to exchange conferring
of degrees with the Masonic Club of
U.C. and Masonic lodges in the bay dis
trict.
The Club plans to continue the weekly
luncheons held at the Union on Mondays
in order to promote fellowship among
the Masons of the campus. After the
meeting refreshments were served.
