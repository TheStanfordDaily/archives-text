# ARTERIAL STOP SIGNS PLACED AT LASUEN, PALO ALTO STREETS
## 
### 
Automobilists on the campus were
greeted yesterday afternoon by bright
red and yellow warning signs posted to
announce that Lasuen Street and Palo
Alto Avenue had become arterial stops.
The boundaries of these arterials ex
tend southward on the two drives from
the junction at University Avenue in
front of the Oval.
Drivers will be compelled to make
complete stops where the notices ap
pear, according to the traffic division of
the Automobile Association of Califor
nia, which posted the signs.
