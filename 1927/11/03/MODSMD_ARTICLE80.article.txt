# RUMMAGE SALE NETS LARGE SUM FOR HOME
## 
### 
Over $900 was cleared for the Stanford
Convalescent Home in the two days'
rummage sale which ended yesterday,
it was announced by Mrs. Timothy Hop
kins. The sale was conducted under the
auspices of the Ladies' Auxiliary for
the Stanford Convalescent Home, of
which Mrs. Robert Aloorc of Menlo Park
is the president.
