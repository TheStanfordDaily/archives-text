# Cass to Organize Rooting Section at Northern Tilt
## 
### 
A1 Cass, Cardinal yell leader, will
be at the Washington-Stanford foot
ball game tomorrow to organize a
rooting section among fans who made
the journey north.
Sergius Klotz and Scott Webster,
assistants, will lead cheers at the
Little Big Game in the stadium here.
Freshmen are requested to wear
white shirts and rooters' hats and sit
in the rooting section for the contest.
