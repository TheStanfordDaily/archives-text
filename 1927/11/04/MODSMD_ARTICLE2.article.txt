# CLASS PLAY TRYOUTS TO BE HELD MONDAY AFTERNOON, EVENING
## 
### 
Tryouts for the sophomore play wil
be held next Monday afternoon in the
Assembly Hall between the hours of 3
and 5 o'clock in the afternoon and
again in the evening at 7 o'clock.
Both men and women of the sopho
more class are urged to try out for
the play, which will be directed by
Anna May Price.
Two plays have been selected from
which to make a choice. The names
of these plays have not yet been di
vulged but both have well-balanced
casts which will give each actor am
ple opportunity to display talent, ac
cording to Paul Speegle, a member of
the Sophomore Play committee.
