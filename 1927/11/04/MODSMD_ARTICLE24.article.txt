# SWAIN WILL SPEAK ON 'CENTURY AFTER VOLTA'
## 
### 
"The Century After Volta" is the
subject on which Professor Robert E.
Swain of the Department of Chem
istry will address a'joint meeting of
the American Institute of Electrical
Engineers and the Italy America So
ciety tonight at the Engineers' Club
of San Francisco.
Professor Swain's talk will deal of
the major advances of science since
the death of the eminent Italian
scientist, Alesandro Volta, whose con
tributions to science are being com
memorated tonight, a century after
his death.
