# Graduate Women's Club Tea Will Be Held Today
## 
### 
Stanford Graduate Women's Club
will hold a tea this afternoon from
4:30 to G o'clock at the Women's Club
house. Laura Hartman Peniwell and
Nesta Thompson will be among the
hostesses. A tea is given by the
Graduate Women's Club on the sec
ond and fourth Tuesdays of every
month.
