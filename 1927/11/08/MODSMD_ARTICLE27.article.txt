# DE MOLAY MEMBERS GUESTS AT LUNCHEON
## 
### 
With the purpose of bringing to
gether the members of two organiza
tions, members of the Order of Do
Molay were guests of the Masonic
Club yesterday at luncheon in the
Union. For the benefit of the new men
on the campus these get-together
luncheons will be Riven periodically
by the Masonic Club, according to
Ambrose Martin, president.
