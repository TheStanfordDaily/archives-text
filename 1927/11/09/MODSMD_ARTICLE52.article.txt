# International Problems Topic of Talk by Kerr
## 
### 
"International Problems Confront
ing the Twentieth Century" is the
subject of a lecture by Mr. Philip H.
Kerr, secretary of the Rhodes Trust,
to be given at 7:30 o'clock tomorrow
evening in the Assembly Hall.
Kerr has been secretary of the
Rhodes Trust since 1925. He is a
well-known figure in international af
fairs.
