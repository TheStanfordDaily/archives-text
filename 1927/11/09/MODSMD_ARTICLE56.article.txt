# German Club to Present Play at Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
A one-act play by Wilhelmi, the Ger
man author, will be presented by
members of the Stanford German
Club on their program at 7:30 o'clock
tonight in the Women's Clubhouse.
Several musical numbers will com
plete the program, which will be pre
ceded by a short business meeting.
Betty Ford and Coit Coolidge are in
charge of the entertainment.
