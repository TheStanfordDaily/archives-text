# THETA SIGMA PHI'S TO HOLD IMMENSE DOUGHNUT BARTER
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi's new initi
ates, headed by Aileen Hicks as
grand dispenser, will hold the
first doughnut sale of the year
tomorrow morning. This annual
traditional habit of Theta Sig,
women's honorary journalistic
society, has been modified this
year to allow the new members
to have all the honors of the
first sale.
Doughnuts will go on sale at
S o'clock in front of the Women's
Clubhouse, and the sale will con
tinue through till noon. The price
will be five cents apiece, and the
doughnuts will be guaranteed to
be hot.
