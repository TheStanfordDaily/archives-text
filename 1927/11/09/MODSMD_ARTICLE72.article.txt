# Fall From Triple-Decked Bed Is Fatal to Freshman
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY CITY, Miss., Nov. S.
— (LP.) —E. L. Motlierhead, 19, fresh
man at the University of Mississippi,
went to bod the other night on the top
of a triple-decked bed. Since lie was
planning to get up early the next
morning and catch a train for a foot
ball game, he placed his alarm clock
right at his car. When the alarm went
off it frightened him so that he fell
from the bed, and received injuries
which caused his death.
