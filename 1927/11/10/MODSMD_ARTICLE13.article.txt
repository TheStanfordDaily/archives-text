# PLAY ELEVEN GAMES IN FALL UNIVERSITY TENNIS TOURNAMENT
## Other Matches in Semi-Final Round Held Up Because Of Rainy Weather 
### 
Eleven of the sixteen matches to
decide the semi-final winners of the
Fall University Tennis Tournament
have been completed, wet courts
holding up the other five matches to
be played before the deadline set for
tomorrow.
In the senior bracket Duval ad
vanced to the semi-finals by winning
over Dobson in the hardest-fought
match of the tournament, 6-4, 10-12,
6-4. Milliken won over Otis 6-1, 6-2,
to gain a place.
All four matches have been played
in the junior group. Hall defeated
Lewis 6-1, 6-4. Farwell defeated
Sperry 6-4, 6-3. Renzel won over
Hainan 6-1, 6-2, and Driscoll beat
West 9-7, 6-3.
In the sophomore bracket, Lee won
over Ricketts 6-4, 6-4; Smith defeated
Dietrich 6-4, 6-2; and Weyl beat Sor
enson 6-0, 0-6, 6-2. One more match
remains to be played.
Weesner, freshman, advanced to
the semi-finals in his division by
beating Harrington 6-1, 6-1.
