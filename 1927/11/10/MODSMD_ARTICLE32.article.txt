# Getting It Straight
## 
### 
Questions may be mailed to the "In
formation Editor," left at the Daily
office, or phoned to the paper at PA
1074. They need not be signed, but
must concern University or student
affairs.

Question: Where may one obtain a
list of the Campus speed laws?
Answer by A. E. Roth, Comptroller:
There is such a list posted on the
left bulletin board next to the east
entrance to the Administration Build
ing.
Question: Is it necessary to obey
the "arterial stop" sign at Lasuen and
Alvarado when the former street is
roped off during class hours?
Answer by Comptroller A. E. Roth:
The matter is now under the con
sideration of a committee composed
of slVlents and the Comptroller. An
announcement regarding the commit
tee's decision will be made in a few
days. Until that time the ruling must
be obeyed.
Question: What is the "corpora
tion," that it may have a "yard" on
the campus?
Answer by Comptroller A. E. Roth:
The term "corporation" is a trade
name that applies to all service yards
of municipalities and other large or
ganizations. It has no reference to a
business house.
