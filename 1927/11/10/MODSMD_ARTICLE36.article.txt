# THREE NEW MEMBERS ELECTED TO Y.M.C.A. BOARD OF DIRECTORS
## 
### 
Three new members were elected
to the Stanford Y.M.C.A. Board of
Directors at a regular meeting ot' the
board last night.
The new directors am: Rev. Oscar
Green, Rector, All Saints Episcopal
Church, Palo Alto; Professor Harold
Shopard, Stanford Law School; and
Professor J. Hugh Jackson, Stanford
Graduate School of Business. These
men will serve until the close of the
association year which is April 1, 1928.
Professor A. M. Cathcart presented
the report of the Finance Committee,
and the budget for 1927-1928 was
adopted.
