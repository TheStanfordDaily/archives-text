# Captain Eagle Will Speak Before Cosmopolitan Club
## 
### 
Captain A. I. Eagle of the United
States Army will give an illustrated
lecture under the auspices of the Cos
mopolitan Club Monday night at 7:301
o'clock in Room 320, the Geology
lecture room. The public is invited
to attend.
The lecturer explored and made a i
map of Mindoro, the fourth largest
island of the Philippine Archipelago.
The International Luncheon Club
will meet again next Wednesday in
the dining hall of the Union.
