# Concert Tickets Placed On Sale
## Sophie Breslau to Be First Artist on Program 
### 
Season and individual concert tick
ets for the Peninsula Musical Associa
tion Series, which will be held in the
Stanford Assembly Hall beginning
next Thursday, are on sale at Cran
dall's in Palo Alto this week.
Sophie Breslau, American contralto,
will be the first artist to appear here
under the auspices of the association.
Fresh from successes in the bay dis
trict, she has assembled a program
selected from her best received songs.
