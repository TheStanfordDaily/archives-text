# Anne Hartwell's Mother Killed in Motor Accident
## 
### 
Word was received yesterday that
Anne Hartwell's mother was instantly
killed in an automobile accident near
Ventura yesterday afternoon. Mrs.
Hartwell was on her way here when
the accident happened. Particulars
are not known.
Anne Hartwell is president of Roble
Club, and a member of Cap and Gown.
She and Elizabeth Bakewell left for
Beaumont, where her father is.
