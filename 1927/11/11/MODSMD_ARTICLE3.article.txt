# VEND CHAPARRAL'S ANNUAL BIG GAME ISSUE WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
"Far and above the fact that
it looks like a bumper year for
the asparagus crop, not to speak
of sweet peas, and lima beans,"
said the Old I3oy this morning:,
while he stroked his beard grown
grayer since October, "and not
to mention that it is idiotic to
go around this weather with
short sleeves, the most import
ant announcement for the Uni
versity and student body inclu
sive is that Chaparral will take
issue with any possessor of
thirty cents next Wednesday,
the 16th inst.
"Ted Krough has designed a
cover for the campaign which is
absolutely unique in Big Game
numbers. Moreover, by diligent
search we have unearthed some
personal facts about Coach Glenn
S. Warner which the public
should know. These, with the
true story at last of the Hammer
and Coffin-Scribes football game,
will be disclosed to our campus
with the Big Game number of
Chaparral, Quad sale of which
begins at 8 o'clock Wednesday.
