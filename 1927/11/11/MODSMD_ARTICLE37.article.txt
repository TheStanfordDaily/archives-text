# ENCINA CLUB TO HEAR COMPTROLLER 'AL' ROTH
## 
### 
Encina Club will hear Comptroller
"Al" Roth next Sunday evening at
6:45 o'clock when Mr. Roth will ap
pear as the speaker for the second
Freshman "Y" informal hour. Roth
will talk on the early history of Stan
ford, the damage done to the Univer
sity by the 1906 earthquake, and will
even delve back into the little-known
period when Stanford was famous as
a world renowned stock-farm. The
speaker will conclude his talk with a
short discussion of the Stanford tra
ditions that are worthwhile.
