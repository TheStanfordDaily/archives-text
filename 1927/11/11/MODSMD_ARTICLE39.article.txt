# Kansas Fraternity Stands For Improved Serenading
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF KANSAS, Nov. 9.
—(I.P.) —Phi Kappa Theta, serenade
fraternity, has been organized at the
University of Oklahoma, in order that
better serenades may be had during
the night.
