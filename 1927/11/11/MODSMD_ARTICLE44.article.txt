# Oldest Fraternity Pin Is Found on Field of Battle
## 
### 
TIIORNBURY, Pa., Nov. 10—(LP.)—
The oldest fraternity pin in America, a
Phi Beta Kappa key presented by Wil
liam and Mary's College of Virginia
in 177G to John Graham, a Revolu
tionary soldier, has been found on the
site of the battle of Brandywine.
