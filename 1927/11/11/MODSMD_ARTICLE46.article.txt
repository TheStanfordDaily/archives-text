# Announce Postponement of Tonight's Joint "Y" Party
## 
### 
Postponement of the freshman
Y.M.C.A. - Y.W.C.A. informal party
which was scheduled to be held at
Dr. Robert E. Swain's residence to
night was announced yesterday by
Fred Burlew, chairman of the Fresh
man "Y" social committee. The
get-together will be held sometime
near the end of the quarter at a date
to be announced later.
