# Dink Templeton Announces Arrival of Baby Daughter
## 
### 
Dink Templeton, Stanford varsity
track coach, is the father of a third
child, a baby daughter born at the
Palo Alto Hospital Thursday after
noon.
The child, who is to bo named
Robin, weighed eight pounds and thir
teen ounces at birth. Mrs. Templeton
is reported as "doing fine."
