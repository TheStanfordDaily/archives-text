# LOOFBOUROW FIRST IN CROSS-COUNTRY RACE, EL TORO CLUB WINS
## 
### 
By a last-minuie spurt which car
ried him into the lead not 20 yards
from the finishing line, Robert Loof
bourow of the Sequoia Hall team
won the intramural cross-country
race last Saturday in the fast time of
21 minutes, despite the handicap of
unfavorable weather conditions. Team
honors went to Carley, Bush, and
Crandall of El Toro Club who finished
in third, ninth, and twelfth positions,
respectively, giving them a total of
24 points.
