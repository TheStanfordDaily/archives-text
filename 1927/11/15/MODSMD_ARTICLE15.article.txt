# BRUCE GRAY TO HOLD STUDENT CONFERENCES
## 
### 
Druce G. Gray, traveling secretary
of the Student Volunteer Movement
for Foreign Missions, will hold con
| ferences with students this afternoon.
, He will be at the Y.M.C.A. building
from 1 to 4 o'clock, and at the
Y.W.C.A. rooms in the Women's Club
house from 4 to 5 o'clock. Appoint
ments may be made through Harriet
Cogswell, Y.W.C.A. secretary, and
Dean Culver.
Mr. Gray talked to the Senior Cabi
nets Sunday night, telling them about
the Student Volunteer convention to
be held in Detroit at Christmas time.
Three questions to be brought up in
j Detroit are: "Materialism versus
j Christianity"; "Is the Day in Missions
! Done?"; "What Shall the Foreign
j Policy of the United States Be, and
What Part Shall Students Have in In
' fluencing It?"
