# 'EXPRESSING WILLIE' REHEARSAL TRYOUTS WILL BE HELD TODAY
## 
### 
Rehearsal tryouts for "Expressing
Willie," the sophomore farce, will be
held again today at the Little Theatre
at 4 o'clock. Thirteen have been
eliminated from the previous list of
tryouts, and further cutting will be
done at this time, since the cast of
the play includes only ten roles.
The following women are asked to
report at this time: Phoebe Plate,
Patricia Rhodes, Maxine Cushing,
Helen Thompson, Edith Jayne, Claire
Pierce, Mariana Evans. Men re
quested to report are: Ernest Page,
Harold Castberg, Paul Speegle, Alan
McCone, Stanley Mullin, Alfred Leon
hardt, Herbert Wenig.
