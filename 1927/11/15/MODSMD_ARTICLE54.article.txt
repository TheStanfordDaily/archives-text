# Espinosa to Read Tonight On Spanish Club Program
## 
### 
Reading of the story "Sanroque"
by A. M. Espinosa, Jr., will he the
principal feature on the program of
the Spanish Club meeting at 8:15
o'clock tonight in the Women's Club
house. There will be a short musical
program followed by the business
meeting.
Committees for the year, including
a play committee, will be appointed.
All students interested in Spanish are
urged to attend by Berneice M. Little,
president.
