# Lawrence Lazarus Given Henry Windt Scholarship
## 
### 
Lawrence Lazarus has just been
awarded the Henry Windt Junior Me
morial Scholarship, announces the
Scholarship Committee.
Students in chemical engineering
are eligible for this scholarship.
The income is from the fund of $8500
established by Henry and Viola Windt
in memory of their son, Henry Windt,
Junior.
