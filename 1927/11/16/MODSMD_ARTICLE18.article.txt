# ANNOUNCE CHANGE IN DEBATE TRYOUTS FOR S.C.. SQUAD CONTESTS
## 
### 
Changes have been made in the
tryout schedule for the University of
Southern California and squad de
bates, continuing preliminary tryouts
tomorrow from 2 to 4 o'clock in the
Little Theatre, and placing the date
for final tryouts for Monday, Novem
ber 28, announces Professor James
Gordon Emerson.
The subject for the tryouts is: "Re
solved, That the results of the Great
War have tended toward the peace
of the World." Professor Emerson,
Professor Anthony F. Blanks, and Pro
fessor William Hawley Davis will be
tbe judges in the final tryouts. Pro
fessor Emerson is judging the pre
liminaries.
