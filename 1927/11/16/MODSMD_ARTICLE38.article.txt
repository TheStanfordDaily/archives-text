# 'Science and Religion' Topic Of Freshman 'Y' Discussion
## 
### 
Science and Religion, the topic of last
night's Frosh "Y" discusison group,
proved so popular that Dr. C. P. Morgan,
the leader, has agreed to hold three or
four more sessions on more particular
branches of the main topic. The next
meeting will he held next Wednesday to
clear some of the differences of opinion
formed at the first session.
