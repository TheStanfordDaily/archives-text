# FRENCH PROTEST OLYMPICS
## 
### 
PARIS, Nov. 15.—Despite energetic
protest on the part of French farmers,
the Senate today voted 2,000,000 francs
in credits to finance France's participa
tion in the 1928 Olympic Games at Am
sterdam. The vote was 279 to 1.
