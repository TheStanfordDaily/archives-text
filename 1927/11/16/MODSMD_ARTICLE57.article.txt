# DE MOLAYS DECIDE TO FORM SOCIAL GROUP
## 
### 
DeMolavs on the campus held their
first meeting for organization pur
poses last night. They will form a
group which will act as a social club
with possibilities of functioning as a
Degree Team later 011 in the year.
The DeMolays will meet next Tues
day at 7:30 o'clock to consider and
adopt a constitutional by-laws for the
club.
