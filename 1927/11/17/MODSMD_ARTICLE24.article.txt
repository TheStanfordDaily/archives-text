# Allen Includes Classics In Today's Organ Recital
## 
### 
Dvorak's "Largo" from the "New
World Symphony" will be included by
Warren D. Allen in the regular organ
recital program at 4:15 o'clock to
day in the Memorial Church.
Other numbers to be played today
will be Guilmant's "Scherzo from the
Fifth Sonata," "Adagio from the Suite
in Ancient Style" by Enesco, and
"Concert Overture in B Minor" by
James H. Rogers.
