# GUESTS OF FRESHMEN CAN OBTAIN LUNCHEON AT ENCINA COMMONS
## 
### 
Freshmen who have guests on the
campus at noon on Big Game clay may
obtain a buffet luncheon in dining i
hall 8 of the Encina Commons. A j
special menu is being prepared for ;
the occasion. The Board of Control i
lias decided to hold the hall open
from noon until 12:30 o'clock. A

Those who do not have guests will
