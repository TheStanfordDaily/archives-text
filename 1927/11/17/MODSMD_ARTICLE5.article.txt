# Y.M.C.A. TO DISCUSS COMING CONFERENCE AT LUNCHEON TODAY
## 
### 
Gale Seaman, Y.M.C.A. student
secretary for the Pacific Southwest,
will be the principal speaker at a
luncheon to be held today to discuss
the plans for the coming Asilomar
Student Conference. Lunch will be
served in the Union dining room and
is open to all interested.
The Asilomar Conference is an an
nual affair and will be held from De
cember 26 until January 2 this year.
