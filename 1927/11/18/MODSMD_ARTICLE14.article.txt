# S.A.E. HANDBALL MEN DEFEAT KAPPA SIGMA TEAM; FINALS TODAY
## 
### 
Sigma Alpha Epsilon yesterday
reached the finals of the intramural
handball tournament which is being
played in Encina gymnasium when it
defeated Kappa Sigma, two love.
Sobieski won a long, hard match
from Ashby with a score of 21-16, 9-21,
and 21-19. Duval took a straight match
from Farewell, 21-6, 21-17.
