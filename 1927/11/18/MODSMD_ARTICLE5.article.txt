# Dr. Grinstead Honored for Services by Health Board
## 
### 
Resolutions passed by the San Fran
cisco Board of Health pay high trib
ute to the memory of the late Marion
O. Grinstead, M.D., in recognition of
the valor shown by the sacrifice of his
life while caring for another physi
cian.
The young man was the son of Mr.
and Mrs. J. R. Grinstead of Palo Alto
and a graduate of the University in
1923. His death came after contract
ing the malady of the physician he
was caring for.
