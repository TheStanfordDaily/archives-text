# HOWARD PEASE WILL GIVE LECTURE SERIES
## 
### 
Howard Pease, '23, has been chosen
as one of the six authors who will
speak in a series of lectures given
in San Francisco this week under the
auspices of Paul Elder of Paul Elder
and Co.'s Bookstore.
Pease is the author of several boys'
adventure books, including "The
Tatooed Man" and"The Jinx Ship."
The talks will be given in the Boys'
and Girls' Book Room, 239 Post street.
