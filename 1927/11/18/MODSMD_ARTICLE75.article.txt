# VIOLINIST, TENOR WILL GIVE TUESDAY RECITAL
## 
### 
Miss Marian Nicholson, violinist, and
William Pilcher, tenor, accompanied by
Margo Hughes at the piano, will give a
recital in the Little Theatre at 7:30
o'clock Tuesday evening under the aus
pices of the Public Exercises Committee.
During the past season Pilcher has
been associated with the Los Angeles
Grand Opera Association and the San
Francisco Opera Association. Miss Nicho
son is one of the favorite artists of the
bay region, her home being in Piedmont.
