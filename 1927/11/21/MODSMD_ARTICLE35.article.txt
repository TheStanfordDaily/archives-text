# LAST OF INTRAMURAL HANDBALL CONTESTS DELAYED TILL TODAY
## 
### 
Both of the remaining intramural
handball matches scheduled for the
past week-end were postponed be
cause of Big Game activities. They
will be played today and tomorrow.
Kappa Alpha will meet El Toro on
Courts one and two at 5 o'clock today
to battle for third and fourth places.
The Deke's play Kappa Sigma tomor
row for first and second honors.
Five points will be awarded toward
the year's intramural standing of the
twenty or more living groups on The
campus who entered handball teams
in the contest two weeks ago. First
place earns an additional 15 points;
second place, 10; third place, 8;
fourth place, 5.
