Suedes are Smart!
Smartness in Dress is often decided by the Shoes
and Hosiery one wears.
%
SUEDE PUMPS, either black or brown, arc particu
larly favored by Fashion this season. Sometimes
ornamented with buckles of Cut Steel, Bronze, or
Rhinestones, at other times worn plain.
For each style and occasion special care must be *
taken in the selection of Hosiery or Smartness will
be lost. And therefore Fashion brings forth New
Shades in Dainty Chiffon: Twilight, Evenglow, and
Blue Fox for Black Suede; Dusk, Roselle, and Can- i'
yon for the Brown. ;
