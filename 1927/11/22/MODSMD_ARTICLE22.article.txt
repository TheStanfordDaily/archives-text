# Y.W.C.A. GROUP READS AT MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
Senior Y.W.C.A. Cabinet will meet
for 6 o'clock supper tonight in the
Women's Clubhouse. A v eading party
will be held. All literature about for
eign as well as national Interests of
the Y.W.C.A. have been collected, and
each person will read for ten or fif
teen minutes after supper.
Membership, the Asilomar house
party to be held in January, and
Christmas vespers will be discussed.
The World Commission of the
Y.W.C.A. attended the California-
Stanford women's debate last night in
a group, and held a short meeting
afterwards.
