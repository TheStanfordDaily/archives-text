# FIFTY SENIORS, 1930 CAGE MEN TURN OUT FOR FIRST PRACTICE
## 
### 
Fifty men reported to the first practice
of tlie senior and sophomore basketball
teams which was held yesterday after
noon at Encina Gym.
This afternoon from 4 to 5 o'clock
practice will be held for the juniors and
seniors. Juniors and sophs will hold prac
tice from 5 to 6 o'clock tomorrow after
noon and sophs and seniors on Monday
at the same hour.
The first interclass cage game will be
held on Tuesday, November 29, at En
cina Gym between the freshmen and the
sophomores. The first game, betwen the
second teams, will be called promptly at
7:30 o'clock and the varsity game will
begin at 8:15 o'clock.
