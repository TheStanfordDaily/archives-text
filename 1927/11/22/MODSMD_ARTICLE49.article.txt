# Sophomores Will Meet in Women's Clubhouse Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Sophomore Class
will meet tonight at 7 o'clock in the
basement of the Women's Clubhouse
to consider plans for Sophomore
Week, November 30 to December 3.
During Sophomore Week a jolly-up
and the Sophomore Cotillion will be
given, and the Sophomore Play, "Ex
pressing Willie," will be presented.
