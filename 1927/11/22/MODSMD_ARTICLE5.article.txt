# CASTELLANOS DOWNS WEYL IN SEMI-FINALS OF NET TOURNAMENT
## Match to Decide University Champion Scheduled For Tomorrow 
### 
Joseph Castellanos won from Ed
Weyl yesterday in the semi-final ten
nis tournament for the University
championship. The score was 6-0, 6-3.
Castellanos earned the Senior Class
title Saturday by beating Sheldon
Milliken 2-6, 6-3, 7-5. Weyl won the
sophomore championship from Frank
Lee by the score 6-1, 1-6, 6-3.
Charles Weesner, who took the top
place of the Freshman Class from
Ted Easton Saturday, 6-4, 6-2, will
play the second semi-final match to
day with Lawrence Hall. Hall won
from Lawrence Driscoll in the final
play for the junior title.
The winner of the finals which is
to be held tomorrow afternoon will
be awarded a gold medal as Univer
sity champion.
Castellanos, Hall, Weyl, and Wees
ner, as winners in their respective
classes, were awarded tennis racquets
donated by J. H. Matravers of the
Tennis Shop of Palo Alto.
