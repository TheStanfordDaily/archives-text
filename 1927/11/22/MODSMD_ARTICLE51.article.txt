# Mrs. Grace Caukin Speaks On S.F. Political Situation
## 
### 
"Political bosses are seldom the hide
ous monsters that the public imagines,"
declared Mrs. Caukin, former
secretary of the Democratic State Cen
tral Committee, at a banquet of Pi
Sigma Alpha, honorary political science
society, last night. Mrs. Caukin spoke on
the general political situation in San
Francisco. Much of her talk was from
her own personal experiences.
