# YALE CAPTAIN ELECTED
## 
### 
NEW HAVEN, Conn., Nov. 22.—
Maxon Hunter Eddy, of Middlebnry, Vt.,
was elected captain of the 1928 Yale
football eleven at the annual football
banquet tonight. Eddy was a tackle on
this year's team.
