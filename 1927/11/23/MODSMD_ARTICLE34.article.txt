# MORE COPY NEEDED; OLD BOY POSTPONES DEADLINE TILL TODAY
## 
### 
Owing to the fullness of activity of
the last week-end the copy deadline for
the San Francisco number of Chaparral
has been postponed until today. Both
art and literary copy is wanted, and al
though material pertinent to San Fran
cisco is especially desired, any jokes,
epigrams, or cartoons 011 other subjects
will be accepted.
The Old Boy is anxious for some of
the Los Angeleans 011 the campus to ex
press their ideas concerning San Fran
cisco. He also suggests that earthquakes,
hills, fog, sailors, traffic troubles, cable
cars and the waterfront should stimulate
humorous comment.
