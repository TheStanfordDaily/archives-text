# MURAL TRACK TRIALS SCHEDULED FOR NEXT THURSDAY-C.W. DAVIS
## 
### 
Trials for the Intramural Track
Meet. will he held on December 1 at
4 o'clock, according to Charles W.
Davis, Director of Intramural Sports.
Trials will be in the 100-yard dash,
220-yard dash, 120-yard high hurdles,
220-yard low hurdles, high jump, shot
put, discus, and four-man 220-yard
relay.
The Intramural meet will be held
on December 2 and the regular bronze
plaque will be awarded to the winning
team.
