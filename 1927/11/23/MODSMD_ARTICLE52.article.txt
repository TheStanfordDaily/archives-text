# ROY LEE TO ADDRESS CABINET OF Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Roy Lee, secretary of the Interna
tional Students Service, will talk to
Senior Y.W.C.A. Cabinet next Tues
day night, it was announced at a
meeting of the group last night. Lee
will picture the work of the I.S.S.
telling actual experiences in helping
students.
The Y.W.C.A. membership drive
will be held the week before finals,
and a special commission for this
drive with Sallie Kinsman as chair
man has been appointed. It will hold
a meeting Tuesday night at 8 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
