# KEATING TAKES OVER SENIOR PRESIDENTIAL DUTIES; KING RESIGNS
## 
### 
Presidency of the Senior Class will
be assumed by Hallie Keating, vice
president, as a result of the resigna
tion of Bob King, who has taken out
a leave of absence for the rest of the
quarter following an illness. King ex
pects to return winter quarter.
Officers and committee chairmen
are requested by the new president to
attend a meeting to discuss class plans
at 8:30 o'clock tonight in the Press
Building. Those asked to be present
are Fred Speers, Henry Keyes, Louis
Creveling, and Harry Call.
