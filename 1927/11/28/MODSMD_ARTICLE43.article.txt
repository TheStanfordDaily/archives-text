# Ruth L. Savage Chosen To Give Organ Recital
## 
### 
Miss Ruth L. Savage, formerly of
Pittsfield, Massachusetts, will give the
regular organ recital tomorrow after
noon at 4:15 o'clock in the Memorial
Church.
Miss Savage made her home In
Palo Alto for the last two or three
years, but at present is living in San
Jose.
