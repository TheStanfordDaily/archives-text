# COTTRELL ANNOUNCES WILLIAM H. BALDWIN PRIZE ESSAY CONTEST
## 
### 
Announcement of the William H.
Baldwin Municipal Government prize
for 1928 was made last night by Pro
fessor E. A. Cottrell of the political
science department. One hundred dol
lars will be paid the winner of the
contest who writes the best essay on
the general subject of Municipal Gov
ernment.
Essays are limited this year to
10,000 words and must be in the
hands of the judging committee be
fore May 15, 1928. The contest is
sponsored by the National Municipal
League with headquarters in New
"York. Professor Cottrell has been se
lected as chairman of the committee
on prizes. Interested students should
see him.
