# Professor Mears Leaves For Meeting at Riverside
## 
### 
Professor Eliot G. Mears, of the
Graduate School of Business, left the
campus last week to attend the sec
ond Institute of International Rela
tions which is being held this week
at Mission Inn in Riverside.
The Institute program consists of
special lectures, general conferences,
and daily round tables for discussion
of trade, social and race relations,
international debts, labor, and other
similar questions. Professor Mears is
conducting one of the round table
groups.
