# Mrs. Anderson to Address Stanford Dames on Russia
## 
### 
Mrs. Anderson will tell of her per
sonal experiences in a talk on "Rus
sia" before the Stanford Dames' Club
Thursday at 3:15 o'clock in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Following this Mrs.
J. Ogg will give vocal selections.
Plans for an informal party to be
given Saturday, December 3, will be
discussed at a short business meeting.
