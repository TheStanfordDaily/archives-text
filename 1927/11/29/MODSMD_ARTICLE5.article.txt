# DRAPER REQUESTS WILBUR TO DEFER REGISTRATION DAY
## 
### 
Murray Draper, president of
the Associated Students, will ask
President Wilbur today to post
pone registration for the Winter
Quarter one day to avoid con
flict with the New Year's Day
game in Pasadena. He will ask
that the registration be deferred
from Tuesday, January 4, to
Wednesday, January 5.
The game will be over at
about 5 o'clock on Monday,
January 2, making it almost im
possible to arrive at Stanford
for morning registration on Tues
day.
