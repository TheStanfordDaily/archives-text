# PASADENA CONTEST TICKET BLANKS ARE OBTAINABLE TODAY
## 
### 
Applications for tickets to the
New Year's game between Stan
ford and Pittsburgh may be se
cured in the Old Union from 1
to 5:30 o'clock today upon pre
sentation of Student Body cards.
Students may apply for four pre
ferred tickets at $5 and as many
extra seats at $3 as they wish.
Application should not be
made for rooters' tickets which
will be on sale December 7 and
8 in the Old Union. This will be
the only sale of tickets held on
the campus.
After the student application
period today, the general public
may apply for tickets at the
same prices.
