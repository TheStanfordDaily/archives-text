# ADVISORY BOARD WILL MEET TO ELECT NEW "LIT" BUSINESS HEAD
## 
### 
Appointment of a new business
manager for the Stanford Literary
Magazine will be made tonight at a
meeting of the Stanford Literary
Board in the Lit office at 7:15 o'clock.
Norman Walsh, present manager, is
leaving school for the remainder of
the year. The Board will discuss gen
eral policies of the publication and
lay plans toward the writing of a
charter.
The staff is now collecting material
for the January issue which will ap
pear January 15. No deadline has been
announced yet. The December edition
is closed and will be on the Quad the
fifteenth of the month.
