# Freshman Y.W.C.A. Cabinet Meets at Supper Tonight
## 
### 
Freshman Y.W.C.A. Cabinet will
meet tonight upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse for 6 o'clock supper. They
will choose subjects for discussions
for the year.
Freshman discussion groups will
meet Thursday night from 6:45 to
7:30 o'clock for an open discussion.
Group 1 will convene in Katherine
Keho's room, group 2 in Gladys Gil
lig's room, and group 3 with Sally
Miller.
