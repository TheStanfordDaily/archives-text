# Fellowships Offered by American College Women
## 
### 
American Association of University
Women Fellowships have been an
nounced for 1928. Applications and
recommendations for these fellow
ships, of which there are a large
number, must be received not later
than January 1, 1928, by Dr. Agnes L.
Rogers, Bryn Mawr College, in Penn
sylvania.
Further information regarding fel
lowships offered by the American As
sociation of University Women may
be obtained from Dean Mary Yost.
