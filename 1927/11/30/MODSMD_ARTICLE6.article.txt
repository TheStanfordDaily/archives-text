# COPIES OF SCHEDULE FOR WINTER QUARTER ARE PLACED ON SALE
## 
### 
Copies of the time schedule for win
ter quarter are now available at the
Accounting Department office in the
Administration building. Among the
courses listed is "Modern China,"
which has never been given before at
Stanford. It will be given by Obed S.
Johnson, Instructor in Chinese Lan
guage and Civilization, under the His
tory department.
The price of the time schedule is
ten cents a copy. It is sold under the
honor system plan, buyers helping
themselves from the stack of copies
and leaving the money in a box
nearby.
