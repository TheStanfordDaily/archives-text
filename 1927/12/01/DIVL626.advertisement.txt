train. It offers advantages that
can be matched by no other
means of transportation.
First there's economy!
$21*50 Los Angeles and back.
San Diego and back.
Special reductions to other
Southern California points.
Can't beat those fares and your
ticket is good until Jan. 9.
Next there's speed! Only
an airplane can beat those S. P.

might just as well have all the

home. Don't waste it enroute!
And then there's pleasure!

by train; and the girls too. You
can travel with your own crowd.

in. Comfortable club and ob
servation cars.
