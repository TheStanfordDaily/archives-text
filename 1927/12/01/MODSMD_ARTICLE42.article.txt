# Oregon Debaters Arrested In Japan for Photography
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., Nov. 30—(P.1.P.) —
Word has been received that two of
the University of Oregon around-the
world debaters were arrested in Naga
saki, Japan, on a charge of photo
graphing a fortified zone. On a plea
or '"gnorance of the law the men were
released, but their negatives were
confiscated.
