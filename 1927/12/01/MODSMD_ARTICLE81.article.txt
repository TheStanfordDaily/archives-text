# HARRELL LAW WARD REPORTED IMPROVING
## 
### 
Harrell Law Ward, Stanford fresh
man, who was struck by an automobile
while crossing the road on the west side
of Kncina Hall early Tuesday evening, is
reported by Palo Alto hospital authori
ties to be recovering nicely. He was un
conscious until 2 o'clock Wednesday
morning, but bis injuries are confined to
minor cuts around the head, there being
no internal injuries as it was first be
lieved. Although his condition is good,
the doctors in charge cannot say how
soon he will be able to leave the hospital.
