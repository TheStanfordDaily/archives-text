## FROM UNITED PRESS Cholera Epidemic Results In 3,000 Indian Deaths 
### 
KARACHI, India, Dec. 1. — More
than 3000 natives are known to have
died in the worst Indian cholera epi
demic in many decades.
In Bengal alone, according to latest
advices today, 2139 persons died over
the last week-end out of 3703 cases
which were under treatment.
The epidemic was particularly
acute in the Mahla district where 600
deaths were reported. A shortage o£
water added to distress of the suf
ferers.
