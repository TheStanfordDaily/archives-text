# BRIDGE TEA TO RAISE STUDENT FUND QUOTA PLANNED BY Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Stanford's share of the funds for the
International Student Service is to he
raised by a bridge tea which will be held
tomorrow in the Women's Clubhouse
from 3 to 5 o'clock. This tea is given
under the auspices of the Y.W.C.A.
One hundred and fifty dollars is Stan
ford's quota. America, through the Stu
dent Friendship fund will contribute $12,-
000, which will go to help students in
France and other countries to build sani
tariums for students suffering from tu
berculosis, and to further interest among
students of different nations.
Tickets are 50 cents, and may be ob
tained from Sylva Weaver. The decora
tions will carry out an international idea.
