# Handel, Le Begue Numbers Included in Organ Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organist,
will give an organ recital in the Me
morial Church on Sunday at 4 o'clock,
the program consisting of: Handel's
"Overture and Pastoral Symphony," Le
Begue's "Une vierge pucelle," Daquin's
I "Noel sur les Flutes," and Guilmant's
I "Offertory on Two Christmas Themes."
Also, Claire Harrington, soprano, will
sing Schubert's "The Omnipotence" and
Cowen's "Be of Good Comfort."
