# SOPHOMORE WRITING COURSE OFFERED TO QUALIFIED FRESHMEN
## 
### 
A special section of English 2a will
be given winter quarter to freshman
students interested in writing who
are judged eligible from specimens
of their work. Those wishing to en
roll should submit samples of their
writing to the secretary of the Eng
lish Department not later than Fri
day, December 9.
Writing already done in school or
college courses is as suitable for sub
mission as work especially written.
This course is designed for students
particularly interested in English
work, and will be followed by a 2b
course in the spring.
