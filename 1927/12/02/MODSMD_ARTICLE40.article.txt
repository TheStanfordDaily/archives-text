# U. OF C. DAILY RUNS EDITION OF BUSINESS NEWS AND FEATURES
## 
### 
Something different in college journal
ism was shown Wednesday by the Daily
Californian, which published a Business
and Professional issue instead of the
regular paper.
This issue contained articles and in
terviews from the leading business and
professional men of San Francisco, Oak
land, and Berkeley explaining what they
believed is required of the college man
and woman in the business and profes
sional world. The paper was sponsored
by the Occupations Bureau of the Cali
fornia Alumni Association.
