# REGISTRATION BEGINS FOR LOWER DIVISION WOMEN NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Advance Winter Quarter registra
tion of Lower Division women will be
arranged for on Thursday, Friday, and
Saturday of next week in Dean Yost's
office, it was announced yesterday.
Office hours on Thursday and Fri
day, December Sand 9, will ne from
!) to 12 o'clock in the morning and
from 1:30' to 5 o'clock in the after
noon. On Saturday, December 10,
they will be from 9 o'clock until noon.
Appointments may be made now,
and Dean Yost urges all women to
attend to their registration promptly.
