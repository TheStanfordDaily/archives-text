# Daily Announces Grantland Rice's Grid Immortals
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Dec. l.— (U.P.)—Col
lier's Weekly will announce this
morning the thirty-ninth official
American football team picked from
among all college grid aggregations
in the country. Grantland Rice, of the
Chicago Tribune, succeeds the late
Walter Camp, famous Yale coach, as
namer of the mythical eleven.
In making his selections, Rice re
ceived the co-operation of numerous
well-known coaches. These included
Warner of Stanford, Zuppke of Illi
nois, Rockne of Notre Dame, Wade of
Alabama, McEwan of Oregon, Jones
of Southern California, Madigan of
St. Mary's, and McGuigan of Vander
bilt.
