# Y.W.C.A. TO STAGE MEMBERSHIP DRIVE DURING NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Y.W.C.A. membership drive will
take place next Wednesday, Thurs
day, and Friday, it was decided at a
meeting of the Membership Commis
sion last night. This body will con
vene to make final plans Tuesday
night at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Sophomore Cabinet will serve at
the International Student Service tea
tomorrow, it was decided at their
meeting last night. Freshman Cabi
net will decorate the clubrooms. This
group is planning to have a meeting
with the Freshman Y.M.C.A. Cabinet
