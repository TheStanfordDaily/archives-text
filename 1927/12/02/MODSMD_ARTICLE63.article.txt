# MEN'S ROOMS MAY BE RESERVED NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Room reservations for the winter
quarter for Encina, Sequoia, and the
Union will be made next week, ac
cording to Vivienna Church, secretary
to Dean of Men. Men living in Toyon
and Branner must call at the Dean
of Men's office today and pay deposits.
Men now living in Encina Hall who
wish to reserve their rooms for winter
quarter must call at the office on
Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday,
December 5, 6, or 7, and pay deposits.
On Thursday or Friday, December 8
or 9, men from Sequoia and the
Union must make deposits. Failure to
meet these requirements will mean
the loss of room for the winter quar
ter.
