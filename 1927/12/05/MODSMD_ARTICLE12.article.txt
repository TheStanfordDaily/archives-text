# SOPHOMORE QUINTETS FACE SENIOR TEAMS IN PAVILION TONIGHT
## 
### 
First and second team sophomore
quintets face the undefeated senior
teams tonight in the fourth round of
the interclass basketball tournament.
The second teams meet at 7:30 o'clock
and the first team's game is sched
uled to start immediately afterwards.
The sophomores and seniors have
each played once. Both senior hoop
teams triumphed over the juniors. The
first and second team sophomores
were each defeated by the freshmen
in the first interclass game of the
season.
The two divisions of the sophomore
team play again tomorrow night when
they meet the juniors.
