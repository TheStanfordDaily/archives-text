# ACTUAL WORK BEGINS ON SMITH MEMORIAL
## 
### 
Actual work on the Andrew Latham
Smith Memorial Bench began last
week when the first chips of granite
flew from the sculptor's chisel. The
bench which commemorates the for
mer California coach will be forty-five
feet long and slightly bowed in the
center. It is to be constructed of
granite and teakwood. The bench will
cost approximately $5,000; which was
donated by the students, alumni, and
friends.
