at the Stanford Daily office by 9 o'clock of

6:15 p.m.—Pan-Htllenic will meet in

len, In the Memorial Church.

invited.

of the Y.W.C.A. meets in the Wom

national luncheon at the Stanford
Union. All persons interested are
invited.

versity.

ulty Clubhouse.

sion of coming Asilomar Confer
ence.

Phi joint meeting. Professor Freder
ick Anderson, Romanic Languages
department, to speak. Home of E.
P. Owens, GB6 Columbia Avenue,
East Palo Alto.
