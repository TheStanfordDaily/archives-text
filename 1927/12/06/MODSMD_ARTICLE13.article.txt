# KIRKWOOD, MORRISON TO ATTEND MEETING OF LAW ASSOCIATION
## 
### 
Dean M. R. Kirkwood and Professor
Stanley Morrison of the Stanford
Law School will attend the annual
convention of the Association of
American Law Schools in Chicago on
December 29, 30, and 31. Dean Kirk
wood is to act as chairman of a coun
cil on property and status.
Over sixty-five American law
schools have membership in the As
sociation, which meets to discuss
problems arising in legal work.
