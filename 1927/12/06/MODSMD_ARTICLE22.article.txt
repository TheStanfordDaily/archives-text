# FINAL ENGLISH CLUB MEETING WILL HEAR FREDERICK ANDERSON
## 
### 
Tlieta Sigma Phi, honorary women's
journalistic society, will be guests of
the English Club at a joint meeting
tomorrow evening, at 8:30 o'clock, in
the home of John Breck. Professor
Frederick Anderson of the Romanic
Languages department will speak on
"The Future of Literature."
All members not having rides or
wishing directions are to meet at the
Women's Clubhouse at 8:15 o'clock
where transportation will be pro
vided. The meeting was changed to
the Breck home near the Woodside-
Portola road from the home of Mr.
and Mrs. E. P. Owens as was formerly
announced, because of more adequate
facilities.
