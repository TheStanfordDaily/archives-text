# Y.W.C.A. CABINET TO PREPARE CHRISTMAS CAROLS AT MEETING
## 
### 
Senior Y.W.C.A. Cabinet will sing
Christmas carols in preparation for
Christmas vespers when they meet
from 6 (o 7 o'clock tonight in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
Helen Bailey and Katherine Teach
are in charge of Christmas vespers
which will be held at 5 o'clock Sun
day in the Women's Clubhouse. Pro
fessor William Hawley Davis of the
English department will read "The
Tumbler of Our Lady," a medieval
Christmas Legend, and a chorus com
posed of members of the men's and
women's glee clubs will sing Christ
mas carols.
Membership Commission of the
Y.W.C.A. will meet at 7 o'clock to
night in the Women's Clubhouse to
make final plans for the membership
drive Wednesday, Thursday, and Fri
day.
