# PLANS FOR PASADENA STUNT SECTION MADE BY RALLY COMMITTEE
## 
### 
Rooting section arrangements for
the Pasadena game and provisions for
a reception committee to meet the
California Student Presidents Associa
tion delegates Friday occupied the
Rally Committee at a short meeting
last night.
Stunt ideas are in readiness and
the committee is waiting only for as
surance of an adequate rooting sec
tion by the sale of enough rooters'
tickets. Announcement that the band
will make the trip south is expected
soon.
Rally Committee members will meet
the junior college presidents' associa
tion delegates when they arrive Fri
day, and will also show them around
the campus Saturday morning. Thirty
or forty delegates are expected.
