# SPECIAL COURSE OPEN TO MANAGERIAL STAFF
## 
### 
All lower division men on the
sports managerial staff taking physi
cal education for fulfillment of lower
division requirements may enroll in
physical education course 1, special,
which is limited to managers, accord
ing to announcement made yesterday
by Steve Clarke, junior manager.
These men must enroll this week
with Ford Tussing, or John Van Am
ringe, in the staff office in the En
cina gymnasium.
Clarke stated that this applies to
all freshmen intending to try out for
sophomore football manager during
the winter quarter.
