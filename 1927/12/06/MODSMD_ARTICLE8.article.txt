# MASONS, DE MOLAYS WILL HOLD COMBINED GATHERING AT UNION
## 
### 
Invitation has been extended to the
Stanford DeMolays to attend the Ma
sonic Club dinner and stated meeting
in the Union at G:3O o'clock tonight.
President Ambrose Martin urges that
all Master Masons in the University,
whether members of the campus or
ganization or not, attend the banquet.
The joint meeting is the first to be
held to promote better fellowship be
tween the Masons and their junior
organization. Professor A. F. Blanks,
English department, is scheduled to
make a short address and a fine pro
gram of entertainment is being ar
ranged by A. P. Mills, chairman of
the program committee.
