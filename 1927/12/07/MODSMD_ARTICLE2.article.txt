# DEADLINE ANNOUNCED FOR INITIAL ISSUE OF NEXT YEAR'S CHAPPIE
## 
### 
Deadline for the January issue of
Chaparral has been set for December
16. This issue will not be specialized,
and copy and drawings on any sub
ject are asked for. Since this edition
must go to press before the end of
the quarter early copy will be wel
come, according to Winston Norman,
editor.
The December issue of Chaparral,
featuring San Francisco, will be on
sale Friday. Subscription copies will
be mailed to reach subscribers by
this time.
