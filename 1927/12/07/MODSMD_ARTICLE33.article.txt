# Women's Glee Club Will Hold Last Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
All members of the Women's Glee
Club will meet in the Women's Club
house at 8 o'clock tonight for final
practice before the broadcast over
KPO this Saturday. Women who in
tend to take part in the program must
be present at the meeting, announces
Maxine Cushing. Those who are un
able to come tonight, but attended
the practice Monday, may also sing
on the program.
