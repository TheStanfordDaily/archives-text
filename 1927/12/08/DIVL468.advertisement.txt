Y.W.C.A. Commission Work
Outlined for 1930 Cabinet
Sophomore Cabinet of Y.W.C.A.
will meet from 7 to S o'clock tonight
upstairs in the Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Harriet Cogswell will outline the
work going on in the various commis
sions, and general discussion will be
held.
All but five members of the 1927
Cornell varsity football squad will be
available for the 1928 season.
