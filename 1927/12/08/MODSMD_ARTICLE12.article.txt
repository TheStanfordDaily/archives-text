# DR. JORDAN WILL TELL "STORY OF STANFORD"
##

Evening Conferences, Dr. Jordan will
speak this evening on"The Story of
Stanford." This subject was chosen
in compliance with a request of the
Y.M.C.A. The conference will be held
at Dr. Jordan's home at 7:30 o'clock.
