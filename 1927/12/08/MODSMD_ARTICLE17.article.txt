WINNIPEG, Man., Dec. 7. — The
blizzard which swept in from the West

today was exceptionally severe even
for this territory. Highways and rail
roads were blocked by snow.
DENVER, Colo., Dec. 7—The cold
est December weather since 1924 has
gripped the Rocky Mountain region
and no relief is in sight until Thurs
day.
Sub-zero temperatures were record
ed in Montana, Wyoming, and Colo
rado today, the lowest here being 12
degrees below zero at 7:30 u.m. The
mercury reached zero at noon but
toward nightfall started another rapid
dive.
