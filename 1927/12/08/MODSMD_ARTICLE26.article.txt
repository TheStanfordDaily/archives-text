# P.C.C. ATHLETIC HEADS OPEN MEETING TODAY
## 
### 
PORTLAND, Ore., Dec. 7. — (United
Press) —Representing ten colleges of
the Pacific Coast Intercollegiate Con
ference, 30 football coaches, graduate
managers, and faculty advisors regis
tered here tonight for the annual
meeting of the conference which
starts tomorrow.
As the rivals of the gridiron chatted
together it was evident that the com
ing session would be a peaceful one.
Arrangements of games and discus
sion of eligibility rules will be the
main subjects before the conclave.
