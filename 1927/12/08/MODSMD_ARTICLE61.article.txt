Notices for this column should be handed in
it the Stanford Daily office by 9 o'clock of
he evening preceding publication.
THURSDAY
12:15 p.m.—Meeting of Skull and
Snakes at the Breakers Club for
election of new members.
1:45 p.m.—Board of Trustees. Regu
lar monthly meeting, in the offices
of the Board, Balfour Building, San
Francisco.
4:00 p.m.—Dr. William Morris Davis
will lecture on "Physiographic
Bases of the Regulation of the Mis
sissippi River and Great Lakes"
in Room 334 of the Geology build
ing.
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital, by Mr. Al
len, in the Memorial Church.
6:45 p.m.—Freshman Y.W.C.A. dis
cussion groups meet.
7:00 p.m.—Sophomore Y.W.C.A. Cabi
net meet upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
7:15 p.m.—Branner Club smoker. Ex
hibition boxing bouts and music se
lectians will be presented.
7:30 p.m.—Thursday evening confer
ence, at the home of Dr. David
Starr Jordan. Subject, "The Story
of Stanford," with particular refer
ence to customs and ideals.
7:4sp.m.—Debate: Mills College vs.
Euphronia, in the lobby of the Old
Union. Subject, "Resolved, That It
Is Right to Break Unjust Laws."
3:00 p.m.—Chi Nu Kappa will meet
in the Women's Clubhouse. Profes
sor Murray will speak on "Delphi."
FRIDAY
3:00 p.m.—The Stanford Dames Club
will hold an informal party, with
cards and dancing, in the Women's
Clubhouse.
SATURDAY
7:30 p.m.—Ushers report in Assembly
Hall for Sophomore Play.
3:15 p.m.—"Expressing Willie," by
Rachel Crotliers, in the Assembly
Hall, under the auspices of the
sophomore class. Direction of Anna
May Price.
SUNDAY
6:15 p.m.—The last meeting of the
Stanford Episcopal Club for the
quarter will be held in the Parish
House. Professor Samuel S. Seward
of the English department will be
the speaker. Everybody welcome.
MONDAY
2:00 p.m.—Meeting of all those inter
ested in a 130-pound independent
basketball team in the Pavilion.
MISCELLANEOUS
ANYONE WISHING TO SELL pro
grams at the Pasadena game, Janu
ary 2, see Murray Draper, 319 En
cina. A commission of two cents per
program will be paid, with a $1
bonus for 300 sales and an addition
al bonus for each 100 over that
figure.

to see Cook Svpher, 112 Toyon.
