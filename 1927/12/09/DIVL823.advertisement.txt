Will
Investment Banking
be YOUR Career?
Various careers are open to you men who
are approaching mid'year graduation.
Each year the investment banking field
draws many of you.
To those of you who plan to enter the
the bond business we suggest that you
communicate with us. We are in a posi'
tion now to consider two or three men
just graduating.
Make your first contact by writing us a
letter giving us facts about yourself.
Address communications to
loom 1729
hi SUTTER STREET
San Francisco, California
