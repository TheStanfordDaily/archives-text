# Dartmouth Trustees Plan For Big Ice-Hockey Arena
## 
### 
Hanover, N. H. Dec. I—(IP) —A one
| hundred thousand dollar arena especially
adopted for the playing of ice hockey, is
being planned by the board of trustees
of Dartmouth college here. It is expected
! that the structure will be as fine as any
building of its kind in the country. Work
will commence next spring.
