# Hindu Student to Lecture Before Y.W.C.A. Committee
## 
### 
"Ghandi" will be the subject of a
talk by Bixit, a Hindu student at Stan
ford, at a meeting of the Comparative
Religion Commission of the Y.W.C.A.
Sunday morning at 9:45 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. A discussion of
this famous leader will follow the
talk.
Ruth Tinsley, chairman of the com
mission, invites anyone interested to
attend.
