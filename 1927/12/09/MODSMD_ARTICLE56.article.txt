# DAILY WRITER'S WORK TAKEN BY MAGAZINE
## 
### 
Ben Maddox, Jr., who was on the
Daily staff while a member of the Fresh
man class last year, recently sold a series
of feature stories on the various moving
picture studios in Hollywood to the Col
lege Stories Magazine, a national pub
lication.
The January issue of this magazine
contains his first story, entitled "A Peep
into the Lasky Studio."
Maddox is at the present time living
in Visalia. He has written a series of
features 011 the Hollywood studios for
the Daily, some of which have been pub
lished this quarter.
