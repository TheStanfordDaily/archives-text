# Class Dues of Freshmen Payable on January 3, 4
## 
### 
All members of the Freshman Class
not living in Encina must pay dues of
$2 on January 3 or 4 at the Adminis
tration Building, announces Jack C. Sny
der, treasurer. Those who paid their
board at Encina for the Autumn Quarter
arc considered as having paid their dues,
|he said.
