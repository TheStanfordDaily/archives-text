# ENVELOPES FOR GRADES AT REGISTRAR'S OFFICE
## 
### 
Stamped envelopes in which
quarterly grade reports will be
sent to students at their homes
will go on sale this morning in
the Registrar's Office. The price
of the envelopes is five cents, out
of which comes the postage, the
rest being given to the Conva
lescent Home.
It is necessary to leave an ad
dressed envelope in the Regis
trar's Office if a student wishes
to know his grades, as no grades
will be given out from the win
dows of the office until Registra
tion Day of Winter Quarter. Re
ports will be mailed from the Uni
versity about December 28.
