# Mrs. Lindbergh Completes Mexico City-Detroit Flight
## 
### (By Untied Press 
DETROIT, Mich., Jan. 2. — Mrs.
Evangeline Lodge Lindbergh com
pleted her 4000-mile trip by air from
Detroit to Mexico City and return
today, landing in Detroit at 4:50» p.m.,
Eastern time.
The flight from St. Louis to Detroit
took three days, and two forced land
ings were made. Mrs. Lindbergh,
however, refused to consider taking
a train after either mishap and waited
until her pilot, Harry Brooks, suc
ceeded in digging the huge Ford plane
out of a snow bank.
