# Democrats Will Receive San Francisco Letters
## 
### (By United Press 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 2.—Letters
to each member of the Democratic
National Committee urging them to
accept San Francisco's invitation for
the 1928 National Convention have
been sent by H. H. McPike, chairman
of the Democratic State Central Com
mittee.
The letter said California "has no
presidential 'candidate" and speaks of
the climate and conveniences of the
Western City.
