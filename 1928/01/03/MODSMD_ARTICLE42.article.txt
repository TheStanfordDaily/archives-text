# Kemnitzer, '24, Married to Frances Robson Stowell
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Frances Rob
son Stowell and Luis Emmett Kem
nitzer, '24, in San Francisco last
Wednesday, has been announced.
Kemnitzer was a varsity track man.
He is a member of Delta Upsilon fra
ternity. Mrs. Kemnitzer is the daugh
ter of Dr. John Matson Stowell, '95.
She is a graduate of the University
of California.
