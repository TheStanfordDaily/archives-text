# 1000 Marines Ordered South
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 3—(U.P.) —
One thousand marines have been or
dered to Nicaragua in an attempt to
wipe out the rebel General Sandino.
The force was ordered to the South
American country after Rear Admiral
Sellers, commander of the Special
Service Squadron, learned that San
dino's men were well-trained and well
equipped.
