# BROWN TO REPRESENT STANFORD IN STUDENT FIELD COUNCIL OF "Y"
## 
### 
Stanford University will be repre
sented for the coming year in the
Y.M.C.A. Student Field Council for
this section by Morden Brown, ac
cording to Ben Culver, Secretary of
the local "Y." Brown's election took
place at the recent Asilomar Inter
collegiate Conference.
Direction of all intercollegiate af
fairs in the southwestern division is
handled by this council. Brown is a
junior and has been prominent in
Stanford Y.M.C.A. circles. He is at
present a member of the Executive
Committee of the local "Y."
