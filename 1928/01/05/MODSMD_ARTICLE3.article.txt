# Morrison, Dean Kirkwood Return From Eastern Trip
## 
### 
Dean Marion R. Kirkwood, dean of
the School of Law, and Stanley Mor
rison, Associate Professor of Law,
returned yesterday from a ten-day
trip in the East. Dean Kirkwood and
Professor Morrison spent three days
in Chicago where they attended the
Association of American Law Schools
convention. Dean Kirkwood presided
as chairman of the conference on law
of property. They left for the East on
Christmas Day.
