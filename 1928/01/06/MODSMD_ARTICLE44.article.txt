# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
Notices for this column should be handed in
*t the Stanford Dailv office by 9 o'clock of
the evening preceding publication.
FRIDAY
1:45 p.m. — Tryoutees for baseball
manager are asked to report at The
manager's office in the administra
tion building of Encina gym.
4:15 p.m.—Academic Council. Regular
meeting in Room 12, Law Bulilding.
Conferring of degrees.
8:00 p.m.—Faculty Club, in the Fac
ulty Club House.
9:00 p.m.—Registration Dance, under
the management of the Glee Club.
Music by Brooks-Worthington. Ad
mission $1. Roughs excluded.
SUNDAY
2:00 p.m.—Oratorio rehearsal in Me
morial Church instead of 3 p.m., as
previously announced.
MONDAY
12:00 m.—Senior Cabinet of Y.W.C.A.
meets upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
2:00 p.m.—First practice of the 145-
pound independent basketball team
in Encina Gymnasium.
5:00 p.m. — The Stanford Literary
will meet in the A.S.S.U.
office.
3:00 p.m.—Freshmen desiring to try
out for sophomore football manager
next fall should meet the senior
and junior managers in the admin
istration building of Encina Gym.
3:00 p.m.—Faculty Women's Club will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse for
a musical program to be presented
by Mrs. Samuel S. Seward, Miss
Elizabeth Peirce, and Miss Mabel
Marble.
5:00 p.m.—Phi Lambda Theta will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse.
Practice teachers are invited to at
tend.
9:00 p.m.—The Interfraternity Coun
cil will meet in the Old Union.
WEDNESDAY
6:15 p.m.—The Board of Control of
the Interfraternity Council will
meet in the Union dining room.
MISCELLANEOUS
4:00 p.m.—Tuesdays and Thursdays
of the winter quarter a swimming
class for faculty and outside women
will be offered at Roble Gym.
Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday, 9
a.m. to 12 noon. Registration of new
members for choir and oratorio un
der Mr. Allen in Room 84, Inner
Quad.

FRIDAY
1:45 p.m. — Tryoutees for baseball
manager are asked to report at The
manager's office in the administra
tion building of Encina gym.
4:15 p.m.—Academic Council. Regular
meeting in Room 12, Law Bulilding.
Conferring of degrees.
8:00 p.m.—Faculty Club, in the Fac
ulty Club House.
9:00 p.m.—Registration Dance, under
the management of the Glee Club.
Music by Brooks-Worthington. Ad
mission $1. Roughs excluded.
SUNDAY
2:00 p.m.—Oratorio rehearsal in Me
morial Church instead of 3 p.m., as
previously announced.
MONDAY
12:00 m.—Senior Cabinet of Y.W.C.A.
meets upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
2:00 p.m.—First practice of the 145-
pound independent basketball team
in Encina Gymnasium.
5:00 p.m. — The Stanford Literary
will meet in the A.S.S.U.
office.
3:00 p.m.—Freshmen desiring to try
out for sophomore football manager
next fall should meet the senior
and junior managers in the admin
istration building of Encina Gym.
3:00 p.m.—Faculty Women's Club will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse for
a musical program to be presented
by Mrs. Samuel S. Seward, Miss
Elizabeth Peirce, and Miss Mabel
Marble.
5:00 p.m.—Phi Lambda Theta will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse.
Practice teachers are invited to at
tend.
9:00 p.m.—The Interfraternity Coun
cil will meet in the Old Union.
WEDNESDAY
6:15 p.m.—The Board of Control of
the Interfraternity Council will
meet in the Union dining room.
MISCELLANEOUS
4:00 p.m.—Tuesdays and Thursdays
of the winter quarter a swimming
class for faculty and outside women
will be offered at Roble Gym.
Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday, 9
