# WILLIS ELECTED HEAD OF GEOLOGICAL GROUP
## 
### 
Announcement has just been re
ceived of the election of Dr. Bailey
Willis, Professor Emeritus of Geology
to the presidency of the Geological
Society of America which held its an
nual convention in Cleveland last
week. Dr. Willis read a paper on"The
Origin of the Red Sea" before that
body.
Dr. Willis does not expect to return
to the University until February and
will spend this month traveling in the
East.
