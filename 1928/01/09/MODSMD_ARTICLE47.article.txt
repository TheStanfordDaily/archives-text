# FOOTBALL MANAGER TRYOUTS ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Freshmen desiring to try out for
the position of sophomore football
manager next year will meet the sen
ior and junior managers in the ad
ministration building of Encina Gym
nasium this afternoon at 3 o'clock.
All freshmen are eligible to enter
the competition. Credit in physical
education may be obtained by taking
fifteen minutes of gymnasium work
three times a week while trying out
for the football managership.
!
