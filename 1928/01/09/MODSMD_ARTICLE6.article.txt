# THETA SIGMA PHI TO HOLD MEETING TODAY
## 
### 
Disccussion of a national essay con
test to be held by Theta Sigma Phi,
women's national professional jour
nalism fraternity, and plans for the
quarter, will be the purpose of the
first meeting of the organization this
afternoon at 4:30 o'clock in the Press
building.
The essay contest will be open to
all journalism students on the cam
pus. Further details of the contest will
be printed in a later issue of the
Daily.
