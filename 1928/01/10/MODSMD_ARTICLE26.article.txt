# Whoops for the Hoopsters
## 
### 
With basketball claiming- the attention of the fans, intramural athlctics
start the second half of the year. The absence of weight basketball is
likely to boom the stock of intramural play and make this season the most
exciting - on record. The presence in the schedule of some new teams is
an encouraging indication of the interest being taken in this winter sport.
The necessity of playing the games in the early evenings and the
consequent interference to the study programs of the players, gives rise
to a suggestion that the schedule will he best maintained and waiting
will be reduced to a minimum if teams will make an effort to start the
games promptly and play with as much dispatch as possible.
