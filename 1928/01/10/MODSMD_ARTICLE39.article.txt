# DISCUSSION OF BAHAI LED BY RUHI EFFENDI
## 
### 
Discussion of the Bahai teachings
and the subject of religion in general
will continue tonight and tomorrow
night under the direction of Ruhi
Efl'endi, Guardian of the Bahai Move
ment. The groups will meet at 7
o'clock in the reading room on the
second floor of the Women's Club
house.
Nearly thirty men and women par
ticipated in the discussion last night
and many points of definition and in
terpretation were clearod; tonight it
is expected to progress well into the
fundamentals of the subject.
