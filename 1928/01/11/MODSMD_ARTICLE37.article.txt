# Driscoll Downs Farwell In Varsity Tennis Match
## 
### 
Lawrason Driscoll defeated Lyndon
Farwell in straight sets yesterday in
the varsity tennis challenge play.
Ralph McElvenny will play Alan Her
rington this afternoon for first place
ranking. The elimination will continue
through the quarter to choose the
varsity squad that will play against
California, in the early part of April.
