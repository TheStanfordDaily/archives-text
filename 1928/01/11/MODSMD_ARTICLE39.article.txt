# Quad Will Start Work On Fraternity Pictures
## 
### 
Fraternity photographs for the
Quad will he taken Thursday, Friday,
and Monday, Quad officials announced
yesterday. Ten pictures will be made

daily, starting at 1 o'clock.
Mac Lewis, manager, urges the men
to be prompt and asks them to wear
dark coats. All appointments must be
kept, he stated. A complete schedule
of appointments will be announced
i later.
