# CAPTAINS NAMED BY VARSITY SWIMMING, WATER POLO TEAMS
## 
### 
Cal Strong was elected captain of
the varsity water-polo team at the
first scrimmage of the poloists in En
cilia Pool yesterday afternoon, and
Kryne Vanden Akker was chosen by
the swimming team to head them for
the season.
Strong is an outstanding member of
the polo team, playing either guard
or forward position, and holding a
reputation of never losing the ball
once he gets his long arms on it.
Vanden Akker is a fast sprinter, swim
ming the 50- and 100-yard dashes, and
also plays forward on the polo team.
All further plans for the acquatic
season are held up pending announce
ments of results for Eastern engage
ments.
