# TODAY'S SCHEDULE OF YEARBOOK FRATERNITY PICTURES ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Starting today, fraternity pictures
will be taken for the 1928 Quad. Pho
tographs will also be taken tomorrow
and Monday. Herb Reynolds, editor,
urges that all men be present when
the photographer calls, as it will be
their only chance to be snapped.
Again it is asked that the men wear
dark suits, and try for a hundred per
cent turnout.
Today's schedule is as follows:
Sigma Alpha Bpsilon 1:00 p.m.
Phi Delta Theta 1:20 p.m.
Kappa Alpha 2:20 p.m.
Phi Sigma Kappa 4:00 p.m.
Other organizations that might be
able to arrange for a Quad picture at
times intervening between the above
may make appointments for this af
ternoon by calling the Quad Editorial
office.
