# SUGGEST STANFORD AS ARMY OPPONENT IN PLACE OF NAVY
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 11.— (U P.) —
—Possibility that Stanford may
play the Army on the date when
the Army-Navy game was to have
taken place, was expressed today
by Chairman John M. Morin, of
the House Military Affairs Com
mittee. Morin recommended to
Secretary of War Davis that West
Point Military Academy schedule
a football game with a western
university on the date it would
have played the Navy. The two
academies recently severed rela
tions.
The University of Minnesota
also desires a game with the
Army in 1928. I
