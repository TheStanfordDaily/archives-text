# NEW ELIGIBILITY RULE IN EFFECT AT OXFORD
## 
### 
OXFORD, England—(l.P.)—lt is ex
pected that a new athletic ruling at
Oxford, prohibiting from taking part
in athletics any students who are
above 23 years of age, will have a
disastrous effect on the athletic
chances of American Rhodes schol
ars, most of whom matriculate at
Oxford at an age above the limit.
