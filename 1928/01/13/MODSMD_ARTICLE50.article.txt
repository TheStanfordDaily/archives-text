# Navy Plane Lands at Miami During Flight to Nicaragua
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 12.—(U.P.)—
Mayor L. B. Bourne and two marine
companions on a one-stop flight from
here to Managua, Nicaragua, landed
at Miami, Florida, at 4:30 p.m., the
Navy Department was advised. The j
plane will be refuled and probably
leave Miami for Managua tomorrow,
the Department said.
