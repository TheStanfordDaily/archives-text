# Y.M.C.A. TO CONDUCT EVENING SERVICES AT PALO ALTO CHURCH
## 
### 
The Y.M.C.A. Deputation Team,
composed of students, will conduct
the evening services at the Palo Alto
Presbyterian Church next Sunday, an
nounces Ray Griset, chairman of the
committee.
Griset and Lyle Cook will deliver
an address entitled "We Would See
Jesus." Griset will open the subject,
and Cook will conclude it. Their talks
will center around the tendency of
Christian students to draw aside the
veil of tradition surrounding Christ to
consider the man, it was stated.
