# Asks Students Not Getting "Daily" to Notify Office
## 
### 
Students, who through changes of
address or any other reason are not
obtaining their copies of the Stanford
Daily are asked to notify the circu
lation department at once. This may
be done by mailing a notice to the
Daily circulation department or phon
ing Palo Alto 1074 after 1 o'clock in
the afternoon.
