# CARD FIVE WARNED TO BEWARE FRIDAY THIRTEENTH HOODOO
## 
### 
Avast! Polygasters of the un
seen and bugbears of the super
natural. What abracadabra or
dained Friday the Thirteenth for
the first conference basketball
game? The team is earnestly ad
vised to throw salt one way or
the other, keep clear of leaning
ladders, and peer at the moon
over its left shoulder.
April and July also are cursed
with ominous Fridays this —still
worse —leap year. Campus dolls
are warned to employ mirrors
gingerly. The Sears, Roebuck
Catalogue for lt)2S guarantees a
set of high-grade penalties to all
those breaking mirrors under
such circumstances. And as for
pledges who raise umbrellas in
the house —quoth the Raven
"Nevermore!"
