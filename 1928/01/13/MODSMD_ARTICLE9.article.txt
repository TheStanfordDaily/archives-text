# Annual Intramural Free Throw Contest Schedule
## 
### 
The annual intramural free throw
contest will be held January 24, 25,
and 26 from 2 to 4 o'clock in Encina
gym. Each man will be allowed twenty
throws. If he has a perfect record up
to twenty, he will be allowed to throw
until he misses. C. B. Clyne won the
contest last year, shooting seventeen
baskets out of twenty tries.
