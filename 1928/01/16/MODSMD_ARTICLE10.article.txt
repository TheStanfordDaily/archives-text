# Kentucky Meeting Honors Two Stanford Professors
## 
### 
Dr. William A. Cooper, Professor
of German, and Dr. Arthur G. Ken
nedy, Associate Professor of English
philology, were elected chairmen of
sections of the Modern Language As
sociation of America which met at
Louisville, Kentucky, during the holi
days.
Dr. Cooper was elected chairman of
the section on Goethe for the confer
ence of the association in Toronto,
Canada during the Christmas holiday
season next year. Dr. Kennedy was
chosen chairman of the section on
Middle English for the same confer
ence.
