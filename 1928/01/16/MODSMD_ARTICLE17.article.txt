# UNIVERSITY SPONSORS KIRBY PAGE LECTURE ON CAMPUS TUESDAY
## 
### 
A communication received yester
day from Ben F. Culver, secretary of
the Y.M.C.A., states that Kirhy Page,
who will speak at the regular Tues
day evening lecture in the Assembly
Hall on January 24, is coming to the
University under the auspices of the
Committee on Public Exercises and
not under the auspices of the
Y.M.C.A.
Culver said that many students
were under tlie impression that the
Y.M.C.A. had invited Page here be
en use he had appeared on other cam
puses under the auspices of the
Y.M.C.A. The Stanford Fellowship
will merely co-operate in a luncheon
and dinner meeting for Page while
he is on the Stanford campus.
