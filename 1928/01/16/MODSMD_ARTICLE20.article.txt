# WAMPUS CLUB NAMES BABY STARS FOR 1928
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Jan. 15—(U.P.) —
Designating them the most promising
of the thousands of young actresses in
Hollywood and predicting certain star
dom for the entire group, Wampus to
night named its baby stars for 1928.
For 1928, Wampus, organization of
Hollywood press agents, announced
as its baby stars, Lina Basquette,
Lupe Velez, Flora Bramley, Sue Carol,
Ann Christy, June Collyer, Sally
Filers, Alice Day, Audrey Ferris, Dor
othy Gulliver, Gwen Lee, Molly O'Day,
and Ruth Taylor.
