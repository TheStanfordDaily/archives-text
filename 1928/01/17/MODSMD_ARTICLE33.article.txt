## 
### 
Five men remain on the list of try
outees for dramatic manager that was
announced last night by Norm Lar
sen, manager for this year. Of the
original seventeen that tried out for
the position, they are the only sur
vivors.
Sidney Lippitt, Dean Morrison, and
James Wehb have been put in charge
of the Masquers play, "The Romantic
Young Lady," while Frank Sloss and
Edward Smith will work on the Eng

Three of the men on the list will
be dropped at the end of the winter
quarter; one of the others will be ap
pointed manager for 1928-29 just be
fore the Senior Farce at the end of
