# Moser Loses to Dunlap In College Golf Final
## 
### 
DEL MONTE, Jan. 16.—( U.P.) —
Gibson Dunlap, former Michigan and
Illinois state golf champion, today
added the California Intercollegiate
golf title to his list by defeating Allan
Moser of the University of Southern
Californai, 5 and 4 on the 32 hole of
the final match play.
