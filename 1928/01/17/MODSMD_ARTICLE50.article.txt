# Berkeley, U.C.L.A. Alumni Given Ticket Privileges
## 
### 
U.C.L.A., LOS ANGELES, Jan. 16.
—(P.l.P.) —Hereafter, all alumni of
Berkeley as well as alumni of the
Los Angeles campus of the Univer
sity of California, will be accorded
the privileges in purchasing athletic
tickets of the other university.
