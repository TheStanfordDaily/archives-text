# GENE TUNNEY SIGNS WITH DEMPSEY FOR BOUT NEXT SUMMER
## 
### 
NEY YORK, Jan. 10.—(U.P.)—Gene
Tunney and Jack Dempsey will meet
for a third time in a heavyweight
championship bout at Yankee Stadium
in June, Tex Rickard announced to
day.
The winner will meet the survivor
of Rickard's heavyweight elimination
tournament in another heavyweight
title bout in September.
MIAMI BEACH, Fla., Jan. 1G. —"Tt
suits me," was Gene Tunney's com
ment here today when informed that
Tex Rickard had announced that
Tunney would meet Jack Dempsey in
the first heavyweight title bout of the
summer at Yankee Stadium.
