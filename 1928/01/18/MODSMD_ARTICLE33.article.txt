# BLUE AND GOLD KEPT AS COLORS OF BRUINS
## 
### 
U.C.L.A., LOS ANGELES, Jan. 17.
— (P.I.P.) —Blue and gold, the colors
of the University of California at
Berkeley and Los Angeles, will he re
tained by the latter, according to a
report received from that university
yesterday. This definitely was the de
cision of the A.S.U.C. Council at its
meeting last night.
