# MEX REBELS KILL AMERICAN
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Jan. 17.—(U.P.)—
Frank Edward Lake, Jr., an American,
was killed Monday during a rebel at
tack on the Las Esperanza ranch near
Colima, the American embassy was
informed today. The embassy made
representation direct to Minister of
War Amaro who is personally con
ducting the Federal campaign against
the rebels.
