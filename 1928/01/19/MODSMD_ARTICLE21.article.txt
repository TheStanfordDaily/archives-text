# Ruth Tinsley Awarded Colonial Dames Prize
## 
### 
Ruth E. Tinsley, '29. has been an
nounced as the winner of the year's
Colonial Dames Scholarship prize of
$100 for an essay on Colonial history.
The prize is given annually to a
Stanford student by the San Fran
cisco branch of the Colonial Dames
of America, a society whose member
ship is composed of direct descend
ants of residents of the original thir
teen colonies.
