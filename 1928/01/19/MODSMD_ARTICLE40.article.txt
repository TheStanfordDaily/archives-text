# French Society at U.C.L.A. Makes Plans for Building
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Jan. 17.—(P.1.P.) —
A clubhouse to be used exclusively by
French-speaking students is the aim
of the campus French club which has
started a campaign for funds to erect
such a building on the new university
campus.
