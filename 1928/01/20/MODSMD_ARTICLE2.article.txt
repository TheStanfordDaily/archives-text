# ANNOUNCE PLANS FOR WOMEN'S HOUSE PARTY
## 
### 
Plans for the Stanford women's house
party to be held at Asilomar February
3, 4, and 5, were announced at the sorori
ties and Roble yesterday noon. Rates for
board and room per day are scheduled
as $2.50, and anyone wishing to go may
get in touch with Bcnetta Merner, chair
man of the committee making plans, it is
announced.
For the past few years a house party
has been held at Asilomar during winter
quarter. Asilomar is between Pacific
Grove and Carmel; it is a camp including
a dining hall, Scripps Ilall, and living
quarters. The purpose of the week-end is
recreational.
