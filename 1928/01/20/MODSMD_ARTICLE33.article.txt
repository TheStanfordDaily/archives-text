# STUDENTS MAY BUY GROUP PHOTOGRAPHS STATES QUAD EDITOR
## 
### 
Arrangements for purchasing group
photographs now being taken for the
Quad have been announced by Quad
officials. All pictures will be on sale
for one dollar and presidents of all
organizations will have a sample as
soon as pictures are taken. Those
members wanting one will sign the
sample. Money will be collected and
photographs distributed by the presi
dents. If more than twenty pictures
are bought by any group, that or
ganization will receive gratis ciiv
seven-dollar enlargement.
The Quad photographer will finish
all work by the end of the month, it
is announced.
