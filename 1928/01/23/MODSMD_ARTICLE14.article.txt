# J.P. Mitchell, Registrar, Returns From Convention
## 
### 
J. Pearce Mitchell, registrar, re
turned last night from Atlantic City
where he attended the fourteenth an
nual convention of the Association of
American Colleges. Mitchell served
as the Stanford delegate to the ses
sion.
President A. Lawrence Lowell of
Harvard made an address before the
meeting on"The Outlook of the Col
lege." Much of the time this year was
spent in a consideration of university
finances and student scholarship.
