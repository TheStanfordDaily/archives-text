# FREE THROW CONTEST TO START TOMORROW
## 
### 
Competition in the annual free
throw contest will hegin tomorrow
afternoon at 2 o'clock in Encina gym,
and will continue through Thursday.
Every afternoon of the three days
there will be a scorer on hand to
handle the competitors. Each person
is allowed twenty throws from the
foul line and the one with the best
score will have his name engraved
on a plaque which is kept in the
trophy room of the gym. In any case
anyone should make twenty baskets
without a miss he will be allowed to
continue until he misses.
