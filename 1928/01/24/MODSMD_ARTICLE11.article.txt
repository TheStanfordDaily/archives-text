# Memorial Coin Issue Marks Cook's Hawaiian Discovery
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 23.—(U.P.)—
The House Committee on coinage,
weights, and measures today ap
proved a bill by Delegate Houston of
Hawaii providing for coinage of
10,000 memorial fifty-cent pieces to be
issued in connection with the sesqui
centennlal celebration next August of
the discovery of the Hawaiian Islands
by Captain Cook.
