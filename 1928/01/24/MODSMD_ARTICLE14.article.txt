# NASH WILL ADDRESS ENGLISH CLUB GROUP ON BOOKMAKING ART
## 
### 
John Henry Nash, typographer and
printer, will speak on fine books and
their making at an open meeting of
the English Club in the reading room
of the Women's Clubhouse tomorrow
night at 8:15 o'clock.
Nash, who is one of the best-known
typographers in the West, will bring
some specimens of his prinling to the
meeting. He planned the typography
for the California Blue and Gold for
several years and for the Stanford
Quad one year.
Mr. Albert Bender, donor of this
collection, has been invited to attend
the meeting.
