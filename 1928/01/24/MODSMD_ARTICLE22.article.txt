# Alabama Senator Renews Campaign Against Smith
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 23.—(U.P.)—
Senator Heflin of Alabama today re
newed his fight to prevent the nomi
nation of Governor A1 Smith by the
Democratic Party.
"Alabama isn't going for A1 Smith,"
he said. "Neither is any other South
ern state except possibly Louisiana.
He is a Tammanvite, wringing wet,
and a Roman Catholic. I would vote
against him for all three reasons."
