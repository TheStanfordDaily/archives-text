# SECOND INSTALLMENTS ON QUADS DUE—LEWIS
## 
### 
All second installments on Senior
and general Quads are now due and
payable, announces Macßoyle Lewis,
manager. The amount due on Senior
Quads is $4.00, while that due on gen
eral Quads is $3.00. Checks made out
to the 1928 Quad may be mailed to
Box 100, Campus, or payments may
be made in the Old Union at either
the Quad office or Ihe Students' Or
ganization Fund office.
It is announced that unless these
second installments are paid on or
before February 1, the management
will be forced to charge a delinquency
fine of 50 cents.
