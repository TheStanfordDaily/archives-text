# JUDGES, TRYOUTS IN DEBATE WITH MILLS COLLEGE ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
"Resolved: That intolerance makes for
progress," will be the question discussed
in the women's Mills-Stanford debate
tryouts tomorrow night at 7 :30 o'clock in
the Little Theatre. A three to five min
ute talk on this subject will be presented
by those interested in competing.
Professors' J. G. Emerson and A. F.
Blanks will act as judges for the tryouts.
All women are eligible for the debate. A
subject for the Mills debate has not yet
been selected but will be announced
shortly, states Ruth Tinsley, debate man
ager.
