# Pi Lambda Theta Plans Student Tea Today
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, women's educa
tion fraternity, will hold a tea for
students interested in teaching today
in the Women's Clubhouse from 4 to
6 o'clock. The hostesses will be Dean
Mary Yost, Mrs. Snell, and Miss Put
nam.
Invitations have been sent to all
women who are registered for winter
quarter practice teaching.
