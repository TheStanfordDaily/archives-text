# COUNCIL FINES THREE ON TRAFFIC OFFENSES
## 
### 
Three students were fined by the
Men's Council yesterday for campus
traffic violations in the second series
of fines for such offenses.
One student was sentenced to pay
a fine of twenty-one dollars for pass
ing an intersection on Palm Drive at
the rate of thirty-six miles per hour.
The rate of the fine was one dollar
per mile over the fifteen-mile inter
section limit, as established last
quarter by the adopted traffic regula
tions. Two other students were each
fined one dollar for improper parking.
