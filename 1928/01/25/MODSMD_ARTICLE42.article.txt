# "ASHLEY CREED" TOPIC OF JORDAN'S LECTURE
## 
### 
"The Ashley Creed" will be the
topic of Dr. Jordan's regular Thurs
day evening lecture given tomorrow
evening at 7:30 o'clock at his resi
dence.
Dr. George Hall Ashley, who re
ceived his Ph.D. at Stanford in 1804,
and is one of the country's leading
geologists at the present time, gath
ered a group of students from the Uni
versity of Pittsburgh around him on
Sunday afternoons to discuss reli
gion. The result of these discussions
has been arranged and is called the
Ashley Creed.
