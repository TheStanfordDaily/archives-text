# HALIBURTON WILL GIVE LECTURE IN SAN JOSE
## 
### 
Richard Haliburton, well known for
his two books, "The Glorious Adven
ture" and "Royal Road to Romance,"
will give a lecture at 2:30 o'clock next
Saturday afternoon in the Banquet
Room of the Sainte Claire Hotel in
San Jose. Haliburton will be in San
Jose under the auspices of the local
branch of the League of American
Pen Women of which Ruth Comfort
Mitchell is president.
One of the most noted of Halibur
ton's adventures is his feat of swim
ming the Hellespont, also accom
plished by Lord Byron a century
earlier. Students wishing to attend
should procure tickets in advance at
any bookstore in San Jose.
