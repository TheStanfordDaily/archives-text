# NEW POLICY ADOPTED BY JOURNALISTS CLUB
## 
### 
Several important changes were
made in the character and policy of
the Journalists Club, Stanford Uni
versity's honorary journalistic society,
at a meeting held in the Daily office
yesterday afternoon, the most im
portant of which was the vesting of
the control of the club in the junior
and sophomore members. Also, in
accordance ith the new policy, sopho
more standing will be necessary to
membership.
The following new officers were
elected for the coming administra
tion: Milton Silverman, president;
Templeton Peck, vice-president; Mar
garet Cummings, secretary; and Cal
vin Wood, treasurer.
