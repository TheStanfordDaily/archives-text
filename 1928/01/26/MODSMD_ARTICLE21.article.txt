### 
Fellowships and scholarships open
to American students for study in
foreign countries have just been an
nounced by the Institute of Interna
tional Education.
Applications for study in France
must be submitted on or before Feb
ruary 1 to Archie M. Palmer, Assist
ant Director of the Institute of Inter
national Education at 2 West 45th
Street, New York, N.Y.
Scholarships and fellowships for
study in Czechoslovakia, Germany,

be sent to Palmer before March 1.
Students wishing to apply for scholar
