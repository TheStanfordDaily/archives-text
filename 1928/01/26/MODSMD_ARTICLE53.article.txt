# San Francisco Baby Falls From High Window; Unhurt
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Jan. 25—(U.P.)
— Russell Overstreet, aged 2, leaned
out of a third-story window here to
day to watch children playing in the
street. He leaned too far and fell to
the pavement below. Much to the
astonishment of his mother and
others who ran to pick him up he was
entirely uninjured.
