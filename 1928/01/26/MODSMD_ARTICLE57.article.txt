# Senate Committee Votes To Dismiss Vare Contest
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 25.—( U.P.) —
The Senate Privileges and Elections
Committee voted 8 to 6 today to drop
the contest brought by William B.
Wilson, Pennsylvania Democrat,
against Senator-Elect William S.
Vare, Pennsylvania Republican.
The vote was on party lines, with
Republicans favoring dismissal of the
contest and Democrats upholding it.
