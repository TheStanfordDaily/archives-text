# FRESHMAN AQUATIC SQUADS HEADED BY DESJARDINS, BURNS
## 
### 
Pete Desjardins, three times na
tional (living champion and freshman
swimmer, was elected captain of the
freshman swimming team yesterday.
Milton Burns, diver, water-polo man,
and breast-stroke wonder, was elected
captain of the water-poloists.
Desjardins will go to the national
diving meet at Chicago this summer
to defend his title, and come back
here to try out for the Olympic
Games.
Burns was named by Coach Ernst
Brandsten as the most promising
breast-stroker in the school.
