# PLAY FOUR MATCHES IN INITIAL ROUND OF TENNIS TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Four matches of the scheduled thir
teen were played off yesterday in the
second and final frame of the first
round intramural tennis tournament. The
other matches were either partly played
or postponed.
In the first match of yesterday's play
Phi Gamma Delta lost to the Toyon net
men in two straight matches. Esberg de
feated Tate 6-0, 6-2, and Sorenson
cinched the match by taking down Web
ster by two easy sets 6-0, 6-1. In the
second match Alpha Sigma Phi advanced
by default of the Fighting Phi Betes.
Norton and Nock, playing for Encina
Basement, downed Phi Sigma Kappa
with little trouble in two single matchcs
of straight sets, 6-2, 6-2, and 6-0, 6-1. In
the final match of the day Theta Chi
defeated the El Cuadro tennis men by
winning one set 6-2, 6-3; and the other
on a forfeit.
A new team entered into the tourna
ment competition when the El Tigre ten
nis team substituted for Blum and Stone
of Encina 3W. They will meet Delta
Tau Delta tomorrow.
