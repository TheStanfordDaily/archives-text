# G.W. HAWLEY TO TALK TO ENGINEERING CLASS
## 
### 
George W. Hawley, A.B. in Civil
Engineering, '13, C.E. '16, now engi
neer in charge of water supply, in
vestigation, and construction for the
East Bay Water Company, will speak
to Professor Leon B. Reynolds' class,
Water Supply, at the regular hour of
class meeting this morning.
This afternoon he will accompany
the class on an inspection trip of some
of the East Bay Water Company
properties including the lower San
Leandro earth fill dam, the upper San
Leandro hydraulic fill dam, the Chabot
filter (thirty-five years old), and the
new filter plant finished last year.
