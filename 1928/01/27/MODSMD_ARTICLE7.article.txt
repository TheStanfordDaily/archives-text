# DURAND TO LECTURE AT A.S.M.E. MEETING
## 
### 
Dr. William F .Durand, Professor
Emeritus of Mechanical Engineering
at Stanford, will give a lecture before
a meeting of the San Francisco branch
of the American Society of Mechan
ical Engineers to be held at 7:30
i o'clock tonight in the Concert Room
of the Palace Hotel, San Francisco.
The topic of the lecture will be "Some
Problems of the Colorado River."
Dr. Durand is past president of the
Society he will address, and is a
member of the Federal Fact Finding
Bureau in which a great deal of his
time has been devoted to the study
of the interesting and vital problems
of the Colorado River project.
