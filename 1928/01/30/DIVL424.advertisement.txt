Look at all the cars at the Auto Show in the San Francisco
Auditorium (open daily till Feb. 4th) —then leave your
orders with us so you get home service instead of having to
run up to Frisco every time a nut needs tightening. Of
course we are big-hearted and don't care for the commissions.
We are just unloading a carload and will have a fair
representation on our floor for a few days.
