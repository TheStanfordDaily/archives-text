# Rigden Will Demonstrate Latest Electric Receivers
## 
### 
"Latest Electric Receivers" will be
the subject of a lecture by W. Rigden
of the Federal Telegraph Company,
Palo Alto, at a joint meeting of the
University Radio Club and the Stu
dent Branch of the A.I.E.E. Wednes
day night at 7:30 o'clock in Room
283, Engineering Corner. One of these
receivers will be demonstrated.
