# Freshman Y.W.C.A. Cabinet Will Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
###

meet in Katherine Keho's room in
Roble at 7 o'clock tonight. Both the
old members and those who have just
been elected are to be present, it has
been announced. This is the first
meeting of the cabinet this quarter.
