# PELTZER TO COMPETE IN NEW YORK RACES
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Jan. 29—(U.P.)—It is
practically assured that Dr. Otto
Peltzer, German middle distance run
ning champion, will make his first
American appearance at the Millrose
Games in New York Thursday, W.
Gibbons Uffendell, chairman of the
Illinois Athletic Club Athletic Com
mittee, announced tonight.
