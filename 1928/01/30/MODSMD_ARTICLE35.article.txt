# Campus, Palo Alto Voters Register With McDowell
## 
### 
Registration blanks for voting are
now being received, and all bona fide
residents of the Stanford campus and
Palo Alto community who desire may
register at the office of John E. Mc-
Dowell, alumni secretary.
"Ordinarily students at residence in
the University are not eligible to vote,
and the privilege is only extended to
those students who have their legal
residence in the community. All oth
er students must arrange with their
county clerk to vote at their legal
residence," the alumni secretary
stated.
