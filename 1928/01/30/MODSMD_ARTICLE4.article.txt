# Y.W.C.A. Discussion Groups Meet Tonight in Clubhouse
## 
### 
Freshman Y.W.C.A. discussion
groups will meet tomorrow night, at 7
o'clock downstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse. Dr. Ethel Owens, women's
medical adviser here last summer,
and Olga Bridgeman of Oakland will
meet with them. This is the first dis
cussion of the quarter, and the three
groups are meeting together.
