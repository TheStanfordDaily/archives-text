# Build $190,000 Women's Gymnasium at Colorado
## 
### 
BOULDER, Colo., Jan. 29.—(1.P.)—
A hair-drying room so constructed
that 100 women may come in from
the showers and dry their hair in
fifteen minutes is one of the main
features of the new $11)0,000 women's
gymnasium at the University of Colo
