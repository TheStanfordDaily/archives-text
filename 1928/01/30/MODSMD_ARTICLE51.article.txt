4:00 p.m.—Tuesdays and Thursdays
of the winter quarter a swimming
class for faculty and outside women
will be offered at Roble Gym.
SOPHOMORES wishing to try out for
TRACK MANAGER are asked to!
report on the track any day after 4
o'clock. Tryoutees may get physical
education credit by working out in
the gym for twenty minutes, three
times a week.
Mid-quarter make-up examination in
M&M 105, Pyrometallurgy of Iron
and Steel, will be held Thursday,!
January 26, at 4 o'clock, in Room 11
550, Mining Building.
All second installments on Senior and
general Quads are due and payable
now. ($4.00 on Senior Quads, $3,00 !
on general Quads.) Payment must
be made by check to Box 100, Cam-
PUS, or at the Quad office or Stu-1
dents' Organizations Fund office in J
the Old Union. A fine of fifty cents i
will be charged for installments not j
paid by February 1.
