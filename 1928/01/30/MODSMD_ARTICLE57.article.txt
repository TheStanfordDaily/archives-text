# Morley Drury Appointed U.S.C. Baseball Manager
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Jan. 29.— (P.1.P.) —
Morley Drury, sensational All-Ameri
can halfback, was appointed U.S.C.
Baseball Manager for the 1928 season
by the university Board of Athletic
Governors. Drury is a senior and is
nationally known for his work on the
gridiron last fall.
