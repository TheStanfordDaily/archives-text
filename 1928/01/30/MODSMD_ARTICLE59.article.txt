# HELEN LEWIS IS MARRIED
## 
### 
Helen Marion Lewis, '26, was married
to Myron Leroy Hyman of Buffalo, New
York, last Tuesday in the Room of the
Dons at the Mark Hopkins Hotel. San
Francisco. Mrs. Hyman took a promi
nent part in campus activities, especially
in dramatics, and was a member of Cap
and Gown. The couple will make their
home in Buffalo.
