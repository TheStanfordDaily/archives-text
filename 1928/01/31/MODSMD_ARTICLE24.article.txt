# Freshman Y.W.C.A. Groups To Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Freshman Y.W.C.A. discussion
groups will hold their first meeting
of the quarter tonight at 7:15 o'clock
downstairs in the Women's Club
house. Dr. Ethel Owens, women's
medical advisor here last summer
quarter, and Dr. Olga Bridgeman, of
Oakland, will meet with them. The
groups will hold their discussion joint
ly. Thursday night the groups will
meet again with Drs. Owens and
Bridgeman.
