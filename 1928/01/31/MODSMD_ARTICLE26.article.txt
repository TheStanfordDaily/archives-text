# 'Y' Party Spends Week-End In Yosemite National Park
## 
### 
The twenty students who, accom
panied by Ben Culver, "Y" secretary,
and Mrs. Culver, spent the week-end
at Yosemite, had an enjoyable trip,
according to Culver. Snow, on the
north side of the valley, was more
than two feet deep, and the whole
party passed the time in skiing, to
: bogganing, and hiking.
