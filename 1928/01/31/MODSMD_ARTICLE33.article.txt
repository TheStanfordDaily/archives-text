# JOLLY-UP POSTPONED
## 
### 
The Sophomore Jolly-up, scheduled
for tonight from 7 to 8 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse, has been post
poned until some later date, accord
ing to announcement made last night
by Stanford Steinbeck, sophomore
president. The jolly-up, it was stated,
will be given at the earliest possible
date.
