# TRAFFIC FIGURES FOR DUMBARTON ARE GIVEN
## 
### 
More than 35,000 cars crossed the
Dumbarton Bridge during "Big Game"
month, according to a report sent out
by Dumbarton Bridge officials re
cently. The heaviest month was Octo
ber, when 35,402 motor cars used the
structure. In July the total number
was 35,126.
At least 400,000 passenger cars and
trucks (and 947,288 passengers)
crossed the span during the year, the
compilation of traffic points out.
These figures, officials say, were far
above expectations.
