# Rigden Will Demonstrate Latest Electric Receivers
## 
### 
"Latest Electrical Receivers" will
be the subject of a lecture by W.
Rigden of the Federal Telegraph Com
pany, Palo Alto, at a joint meeting
of the University Radio Club and the
Student Branch of the A.I.E.E. to
night. at 7:30 o'clock in Room 283,
Engineering Corner. One of these re
ceivers will be demonstrated.
