# INTRAMURAL GAMES OF HANDBALL BEGUN
## 
### 
Competition began yesterday in tlie
winter quarter intramural handball
tournament when two matches were
played off in the Peninsula Club at
Palo Alto. The games are played on
regulation four-walled courts.
In the matches scheduled for to
day, Price will meet R. Richards at
4 o'clock and Finley Thompson will
play A. J. Marvin at 5 o'clock. If the
appointed times are unsatisfactory,
new arrangements can be made by
notifying C. W. Davis immediately.
Second-round competition will begin
next Monday if all matches are played
on time.
