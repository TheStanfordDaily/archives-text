# FRESHMEN WOMEN TO HOLD "Y" DISCUSSIONS
## 
### 
Freshman Y.W.C.A. discussion groups
will meet with Dr. Ethel Owens, wom
en's medical adviser here last summer
quarter, and Olga Bridgman of Oakland,
tomorrow night at 7:15 o'clock down
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse. This
meeting will be a followup of the one
held last night.
Freshman Cabinet will entertain Dr.
Owens and Dr. Bridgman at dinner be
fore the meeting tomorrow night, it was
announced by Katherine Keho, chairman
of the cabinet.
