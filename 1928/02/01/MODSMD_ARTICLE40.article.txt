# CHANGE GERMAN CLUB PICTURE APPOINTMENT
## 
### 
Time for taking the German Club
Quad photograph has been changed
from 12:45 to 1:10 o'clock today,
Egon M. Petersen, club president, an
nounced yesterday.
The next regular meeting of the
German Club will be held next Wed
nesday night, February 8, at the
Women's Clubhouse. Several of the
members will present a German play
in the original. Dr. E. Wiehl, German
consul-general at San Francisco, has
been invited to attend the meeting.
