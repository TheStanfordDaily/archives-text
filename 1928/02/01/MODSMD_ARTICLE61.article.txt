# CHILEAN BOOKS GIVEN U. OF TEXAS LIBRARY
## 
### 
AUSTIN, Texas, Jan. 31— (I.P.)—
A gift of 150 books from various de
partments of government in Chile has
recently been received by the Univer

sity of Texas library, according to
C. E. Castaneda, librarian of the
Latin-American collection. The ma
terial will be of particular interest to
students interested in the develop
ment of Chile during the past fifty
