# Freshman Y.W.C.A. Groups To Hear Medical Advisors
## 
### 
Dr. Ethel Owens, women's medical
advisor here last summer, and Olga
Bridgeman, of Oakland, will meet
with freshman Y.W.C.A. discussion
groups tonight at 7:15 o'clock down
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse.
This discussion will be a follow-up of
the one held Tuesday night by the
groups meeting jointly.
