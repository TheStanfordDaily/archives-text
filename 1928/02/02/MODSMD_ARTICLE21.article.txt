# Prof. Niles Prepares Book On "Airplane Structures"
## 
### 
Alfred S. Niles, Professor of Aero
nautic Engineering, is preparing a
text on "Airplane Structures" in col
laboration with a professor at Massa
chusetts Institute of Technology. The
book is intended for use in classes of
structural design.
At present there is no text on the
subject written in English.
