# Pi Sigma Alpha Will Meet To Initiate New Members
## 
### 
Pi Sigma Alpha, honorary political
science society, will hold a banquet
dinner meeting tonight in the Union
at 6:15 o'clock for the purpose of
initiation of new members. Professor
Graham H. Stuart, national vice-presi
dent of the organization, will address
the club.
This is the first and only initiation
of the year, and ten new members will
be taken in. Ziemer Hawkins will
have charge of the proceedings.
