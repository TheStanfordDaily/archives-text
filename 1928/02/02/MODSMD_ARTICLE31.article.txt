# DEMPSEY ANNOUNCES HE IS THROUGH WITH BOXING PERMANENTLY
## 
### 
MIAMI BEACH, Fla., Feb. 1. —
(U.P.) —Jack Dempsey has retired
from the ring permanently and Gene
Tunney's opponent in the next heavy
weight championship fight is still to
be selected, Tex Rickard announced
today.
