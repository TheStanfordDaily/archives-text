# COMPTROLLER ROTH RETURNS TO CAMPUS
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, comptroller, returns
today from the Sacramento Valley
region where bo has been making a
series of addresses before Rotary
Clubs in Modesto, Stockton, and other
cities.
Roth, who Is District Governor of
the Rotary Clubs in California, Ne
vada, and the Hawaiian Islands, has
made a series of tours to speak at
the several clubs in the district since
his election to the office last July.
