# General Obregon Rumored Assassinated at Nogales
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Feb. 2—(U.P.) — An
entirely unconfirmed report reached
New York today from Mexican
sources that General Alvaro Obregon,
candidate for the Mexican presidency,
has been shot at Nogales.
A few months ago an attempt was
made to assassinate Obregon in Mex
ico City but the bombs missed their
mark. Four Catholics wore arrested
and executed in connection with the
attempt.
