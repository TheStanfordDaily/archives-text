# INTERCOLLEGIATE GRID RULES COMMITTEE MEET
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Feb. 2.—(U.P.)—The
National Intercollegiate Football
Rules Committee will hold its annual
meeting Saturday at the Seaview Golf
Club, Absecon, N.J., to adopt rules for
1928, Chairman E. K. Hall announced
today.
The meeting will be held more than
a month earlier than usual this year
so that the rules may be published in
advance of the football season.
Despite considerable criticism from
some quarters over the 1927 code, the
coaches at their annual meeting here
in December went on record as favor
ing no more changes in the rules.
