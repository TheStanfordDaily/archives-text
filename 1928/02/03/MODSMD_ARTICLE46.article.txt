# FRESHMAN "Y" WOMEN MEET FOR DISCUSSION
## 
### 
: Dr. Ethel Owens, women's medical
advisor here summer quarter, and
Olga Bridgeman, met with the fresh
man Y.W.C.A. discussion groups last
night to continue the discussion upon
psychological questions held Tuesday
night. Before the meeting they were
entertained at dinner by freshman
cabinet at Roble. The discussion
groups will meet again next Thurs
day night, it was decided.
