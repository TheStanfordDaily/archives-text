# Senator Walsh Suggests Continental Trading Taxes
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. S—(U.P.) —
Senator Thomas J. Walsh, of the Sen
ate Committee investigating the Tea
pot Dome affair, made a new move
tonight when he directed inquiries to
Secretary of Treasury Mellon as to
how the Government could obtain
back taxes on the $3,000,000 in profits
realized by the Continental Trading
Company.
