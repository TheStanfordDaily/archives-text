# PALO ALTO NOT PART OF STANFORD ADDRESS
## 
### 
Patrons of the Stanford postoffice
are requested by Superintendent V. P.
Mason to have all their mail ad
dressed to Stanford University, Cali
fornia, not to Stanford University,
Palo Alto, California.
First-class mail is not all distri
buted to the boxes until 9 o'clock in
the morning. Patrons should allow
from thirty minutes to an hour and a
half after mail arrives for distribution
to boxes, depending on the quantity
of mail.
