# Huerta Trial Starts in Los Angeles Tomorrow
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 6.— (U.P.) —
Trial of Adolfo de la Huerta, former
provisional president of Mexico, and
six arrested associates, charged with
conspiracy to violate United States
neutrality laws, is scheduled to open
here tomorrow.
According to federal investigations,
agents for De la Huerta were seized
while attempting to run munitions
across the Arizona border into
Mexico.
