NEW REGULATIONS FOR WOMEN 1
OF STANFORD UNIVERSITY
A. General: The following rules ap
ply to all Stanford women:
112. The regulations shall apply to |
all Stanford women residing on
the campus during vacations
and all four quarters.
11. Week-end privileges shall apply
to all nights during vacation!
periods.
111. Stanford women are warned
against entering places of pub- i
lie resort not recommended by;
Associated Women Students, i
Disregard of this warning, re-1
suiting in bringing disrepute on,
the name of the University,:
will be dealt with by Women's j
Council.
IV. Respect for the sentiment of
the University against women
smoking in public gatherings |
on (he campus, on the streets
of the campus, or in the vicin- j
ity of University buildings, is I
upheld by A.W.S. Whether or
not women shall smoke in liv-1
ing groups is left to the yearly
decision of the individual living
groups. It is recommended that
women do not smoke in public
places in Palo Alto or the vi- j
cinity.

V. Stanford women shall not at
tend an off-campus dance given
by or for a living group, unless
that group has complied with
off-campus regulations passed
by Executive Committee.
VI. Women students leaving their
living groups for over night or
for a period of time shall leave
with the person responsible the
name of their hostess, the ad
dress, arid the telephone num
ber. It is regarded as against
the spirit of the rules to stay
in Palo Alto or the vicinity in
order to evade the 1:30 regula
tion on the campus.
VII. By applying to a member of
Women's Council, one may ob
tain late leave for special oc
casions.
VIII. No Stanford woman shall go
into a men's living group un
less another woman is present.
IX. Men and women visitors are
not allowed in undergraduate
houses after 10:30 on week
nights, and 1:30 on Friday and
Saturday nights.
X. Guests shall abide by all Uni
versity regulations.
B. Freshmen. The following rules ap
ply to Freshman specifically:
I. Houses of residence shall close
for Freshmen at 10:30 o'clock}
every night, including Friday
and Saturday.
11. By signing name and destina
tion, and time of return in
registration book in their liv
ing groups, Freshmen may be J
out as follows on Friday and i
Saturday nights:
1. Until 11:30 for second show!
in Palo Alto, and until 12:30 1
and 1:30 for campus dances!
lasting until 12:00 and 1:00
respectively.
2. Until 1:30 for dancing and
theatre in San Francisco,
San Jose, and San Mateo.
3. Until one half hour after the J
end, for such campus and :
Palo Alto plays and concerts,,
etc., whose time of ending is
indefinite. This does not ap- j
ply to the second show in
Palo Alto. I
111. Freshmen women may not mo
tor after dark in groups of less
than four unless accompanied |
by a chaperon, or by a senior '
woman, except as a means of
conveyance between their!
homes and the campus.
IV. Freshmen women shall not
participate in Dramatic produc
tions.
C. Sophomores, Juniors, and Seniors.
I. Houses in which Sophomore, !
Junior, and Senior women re
side shall tjose for them at
10:30 on Sunday, Monday, Tues
day, Wednesday, and Thursday ,
nights.
11. Sophomores, Juniors, and Sen
iors may have 1:30 leave on
Friday and Saturday nights by j
signing name and destination. |
D. Graduates—Specifically.
I. Houses for graduate women
shall close at 10:30, but gradu
ate women may be out after
that time if they register time
of return and reason for late
ness. The regulation of this
privilege shall be in the hands
of a committee of graduate wo- i
men residing in the house.
11. Graduate women residing in
