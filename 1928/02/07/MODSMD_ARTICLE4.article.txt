# Announce Engagement of Two Stanford Graduates
## 
### 
Announcement was made yesterday of
the engagement of Miss Elizabeth Patti
ani and Edward Athelstone Howard, both
Stanford graduates. The marriage will
take place this summer and the couple
will live iu Hollywood.
Miss Pattiani is a daughter of Mr. and
Mrs. William Louis Pattiani of Berkeley.
She was a member of the Delta Gamma
sorority. Howard was a member of the
Zeta Psi fraternity. He served in the
World War with the engineering corps.
