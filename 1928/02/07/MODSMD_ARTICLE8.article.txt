# CHARLES WING NAMED CHIEF OF STATE PARKS
## 
### 
Professor Charles B. Wing, for
many years head of the Department
of Civil Engineering, was yesterday
appointed chief of the state division
of parks by Governor Young, acting
upon the recommendation of the park
commission.
Professor Wing has been active for
some time in developing and preserv
ing state parks. He will be remem
bered as the developer of State Red
wood Park in the Santa Cruz Moun
tains. The newly appointed park chief
left for Sacramento yesterday where
he will confer with state authorities
there. It is not definite yet as to
whether Wing will resign his Univer
sity position, or apply for a leave of
absence as was reported yesterday.
