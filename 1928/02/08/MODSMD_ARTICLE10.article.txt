# SEVEN TENNIS TEAMS ADVANCE UNDER NEW SCHEDULE OF GAMES
## 
### 
Seven teams advanced to the third
and fourth competition in matches of
the intramural tennis tournament yes
terday afternoon. This was the first
play under the new schedule arranged
Sunday by C. W. Davis, Director of
Intramural Sports. All but four teams
have advanced to the quarter-finals
round; these four teams will advance
after tomorrow's matches.
Today play will continue in the
consolation tournament to determine
fifth place winner. There has been
no regular schedule arranged for con
solation competition. All third-round
competition must be over by Monday.
Only three matches have been played
off so far, leaving seven unplayed
matches.
In the first match of yesterday's
competition Independent 4 lost to
Evans and Smith of Phi Kappa Sigma
in two straight sets. Following this
match Phi Kappa Sigma lost to Sigma
Alpha Epsilon by matches of 6-0, 3-6,
6-2, and 7-5, 6-3. Sequoia won the next
match, defeating Los Arcos in two
straight 6-2, 6-1 and 2-6, 6-4, 6-4. In
third-round play Theta Chi defeated
Branner Club in a match that went
to the doubles sets. In the first match
Way defeated Morey of Theta Chi by
a score of 6-1, 6-2. Fredrickson came
back in the second match and evened
things up. In the doubles Theta Chi
won by a score of 4-6, 7-5, 7-5.
In the final matches Beta Theta Pi
won over Encina 2E by a default.
Alpha Delta Phi won the final match
when Dunn and Kendel defeated the
Toyon tennis men by scores of 6-1,
6-4 and 6-1, 3-6, 6-2.
The schedule for tomorrow is as
follows:
Sequoia 1 vs. Beta Theta Pi, NTC
4 and 5.
Sigma Nu vs. Sequoia 3, NTC 8
and 9.
Encina Basement vs. Delta Chi,
NTC 10 and 11.
Breakers vs. Alpha Sigma Phi, OVC
1 and 2.
