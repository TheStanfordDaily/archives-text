# NO NEW POLIOMYELITIS CASES, STATES REPORT
## 
### 
Health Report, Tuesday Evening,
February 7, 1928
No new cases of poliomyelitis
or other contagious disease today.
All the patients in the Isolation
Hospital (eight poliomyelitis
cases, three German measles, and
one case of mumps) are continu
ing their uneventful convale
scence. .
Thirty-four men students re
ported to the offices of the Medi
cal Adviser of Men today for ad
vice. One of them was sent to
the Palo Alto Hospital because
of an infected foot.
DR. THOMAS A. STOREY,
University Health Officer.
