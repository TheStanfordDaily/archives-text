# LAGUNITA FULL-NOW OPEN FOR CANOEING
## 
### 
Lagunita is now full, anil canoes
may be rented at the boathouse any
time from 10 o'clock in the morning
to 10 o'clock at night from the care
takers, announces Swimming Coach
Ernst Brandsten,
All canoeists must have a permit.
These can be obtained by anyone
passing a swimming test, given at
fhe Encina and lioble pools. The test
cannot be taken until paralysis quar
antine regulations are removed, but
permits from last year will suffice.
