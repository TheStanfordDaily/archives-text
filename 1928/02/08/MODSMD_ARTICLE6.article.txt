# CALL MEETING OF ALL MEMBERS OF Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Senior Y.W.C.A. Cabinet voted last
night to hold a short business meet
ing of the entire membership a week
from tomorrow night to explain the
budget in preparation for the annual
finance drive. It also decided to have
the Asilomar house party the week
end of May 4, 5, and 6.
Miss Olivia Boezinger will meet
with the Senior and Freshman Cabi
nets next Tuesday night at 7:15
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse to
discuss vocations for women, an
nounces Miss Harriet Cogswell,
Y.W.C.A. secretary.
