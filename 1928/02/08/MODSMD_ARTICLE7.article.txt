# Individual Picture Proofs Obtainable at Quad Office
## 
### 
Proofs of the individual pictures
taken for the Quad last Wednesday
and Thursday must he obtained at
the business office of the Quad in the
Old Union tomorrow. This is the only
chance students will have to Ret their
proofs, as the photographer's repre
sentatives will be here only one day.
Proofs must be returned next Mon
day or Tuesday.
