# Re-Vote on Branner Club President Set For Tonight
## 
### 
Branner Club members will vote for
club president tonight in the Branner
Lobby. At the election last Thursday
night Harold Way and Paul Forker
both tied for president. The other
officers elected were: Wareham Sea
man, vice-president; John Clark, sec
retary; Adelbert. Culling, treasurer;
Wesly Cox, senior representative;
David Rytand, junior representative;
and James Springer, sophomore rep
resentative.
