# DEAN MARY YOST TO ATTEND CONFERENCE IN EAST THIS MONTH
## 
### 
Miss Mary Yost, whose position as
Dean of Women will be filled during
her vacation by Olivia Boezinger, will
leave soon to attend a conference of
deans to be held in Boston the latter
part of this month at which she will
deliver two papers. While in the East
Miss Yost will visit friends in Boston
and New York.
In April she will come as far West
as Denver, and from there will drive
with friends to New Mexico. Return
ing here the latter part of April, she
will sail, accompanied by her parents,
to the Hawaiian Islands where she
will spend the month of May, return
ing to Stanford in time to resume
her work on June 1.
