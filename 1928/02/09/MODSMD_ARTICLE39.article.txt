# League Council Meeting Called by Secretariat
## 
### 
GENEVA, Feb. B—(U.P.) — The
Forty-Ninth Session of the League of
Nations Council will be convoked
March 5, the League Secretariat an
nounced today. The Agenda comprises
thirty questions of which the most
important is the Little Entente de
mand that the League investigate
shipment of machine guns to Hun
gary.
M. Benes of Czechoslovakia, Presi
dent of the League's new Security
Commission, notified the Secretariat
the commission would hold a public
session in Geneva February 20. It
will consider suggestions of several
governments, notably England, Swe
den, and Norway, who favor launch
ing of a world-wide Locarno Pact as
a model security treaty.
