# NO MORE PARALYSIS REPORTED YESTERDAY
## 
### 
Health Report, Wednesday Evening,
February 8, 1928
No new cases of poliomyelitis or
other contagious disease today. All
the patients in the Isolation Hospital
(8 poliomyelitis cases, 3 German
measles, and 1 case of mumps) are
continuing their uneventful convale
scence.
Fifty-two men students reported to
the offices of the Medical Adviser for
Men today for advice. No one was
found in need of special medical
treatment.
(Signed): THOMAS A. STOREY,
University Health Officer.
