# Paul Forker Is Elected Branner Club President
## 
### 
Paul Forker was elected president of
Branner Club in the second election last
night in the Branner lobby. Forker re
ceived 61 votes to 41 for Harold Way.
At the election of officers last week no
president was elected because of a tie
vote but for the other officers: Wareham
Seaman was elected vice-president, John
Clark, secretary; Adelbert Culling, treas
urer; Wesly Cox, senior representative;
David Rytand, junior representative; and
James Springer, sophomore representative.
