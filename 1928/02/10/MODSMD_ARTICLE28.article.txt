# Windstorm in Southern California Causes Death
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 9.—(U.P.)—
' A savage windstorm tore through
I southern California today causing one
death, spreading serious property
damage, and fanning two forest fires
in the vicinity of Los Angeles.
Chester Hammock, 14, was electro
cuted when he touched wires blown
down by the force of the wind.
A blaze in the Verdugo Hills north
of Glendale still was burning furious
ly but was under control tonight
while a fire in the Arroyo Seco near
Pasadena was almost extinguished
after destroying valuable property.
