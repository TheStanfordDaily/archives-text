# S. Leslie Grow to Present Castilleja Organ Recital
## 
### 
S. Leslie Grow, '28, will play an
organ recital at Castilleja School next
Sunday evening at 6 o'clock, under
the auspices of the music department
of that school. The concert will fea
ture famous composers and a new
suite for piano and organ planned

I by Professor Closky, organist at Po
mona College. In this number Mr.
Grow will be assisted by Dr. Latham
True, dean of music at Castilleja.
The recital will be open to the public.
