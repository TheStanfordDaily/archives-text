# Dr. Storey Raises Voluntary Ban On Campus Activities
## 
### 
After consultation with the
State Health Officer yesterday,
Dr. Thomas A. Storey, Univer
sity Health Officer, declared the
period of special precaution re
quired by the state for the pro
tection of campus people and the
public against the further dis
semination of poliomyelitis ended.
Those students who have been
confined to the Isolation Hospital
with the disease will remain
there until the three weeks' quar
antine after their admission has
passed.
All activities, social and ath
letic, will resume their normal
course.
