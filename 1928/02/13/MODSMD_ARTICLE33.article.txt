# EUPHRONIA DEBATERS TO OPPOSE MEMBERS OF FRESHMAN SQUAD
## 
### 
Euphronia debating society's nega
tive team will meet three members
of tbe freshman debate squad at 8
o'clock Wednesday night in the lobby
of the Old Union on the subject, "Re
solved, That the present effort in
America to give college education to
the masses should be abandoned."
Members of the Euphronia team
are: Victor Burns, Louis Mercado,
and Howard Custer. The freshman
affirmative team is composed of
Wadieh S. Shibley, Sturgis Riddle,
and Harry Warmke.
The freshman team will debate with
the O.S.C. freshmen on the same sub
ject at some date early in April. Hugh
Lindsay, former Stanford debater,
will assist Darrel Parker in coaching
the first-year debaters.
