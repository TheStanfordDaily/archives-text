# Acting Dean of Women to Address Y.W.C.A. Cabinets
## 
### 
Miss Olivia Boezinger will meet
with the Senior and Freshman Y.W.
C.A. cabinets at 7:15 o'clock tomor
row night in the Women's Clubhouse
to discuss "Vocations for Women,"
announces Harriet Cogswell, Y.W.C.A.
secretary. Miss Boezinger, who takes
the place of Dean of Women here
today, was enrolled in the Teachers'
College at Columbia University last
fall.
