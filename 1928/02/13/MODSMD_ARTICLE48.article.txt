# KENNETH WRIGHT, '21 LEAVES FOR TRINIDAD
## 
### 
Kenenth Wright, '21, geologist with
the Pan-American Petroleum Corpora
tion, left February 1 for Trinidad, it
was learned Saturday by the secre

tary of the Geology department.
He went to join the staff of the
British Controlled Leaseholds, Ltd.
This company holds concessions and
freehold properties covering 226,000
acres in Trinidad as well as extensive
properties in Venezuela, Colombia,
Peru, and Guatemala.
