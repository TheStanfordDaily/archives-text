# ALLEN WILL PRESENT RAVEL COMPOSITIONS ON RECITAL PROGRAM
## 
### 
Modern music will be featured in
the organ recital this afternoon in
the Memorial Church at 4:00 o'clock.
Three compositions of Maurice Ravel,
who is an outstanding genius in mod
ern music, will be given. Ravel is
now in California and his visit is an
event of great interest in musical
circles.
The program is as follows:
Fantasia in G Major J. S. Bach
The Enchanted Garden
Minuet from the Sonatina
Passacaille from the Trio
Maurice Ravel
Paradise Zdenko Fibich
March from "Die Meistersinger"
Wagner
