# STANFORD TO DEBATE MILLS ON PAGANISM
## 
### 
"Resolved, That this house regrets
the present tendencies of American
College Students towards Paganism"
is the question chosen for the Stan
ford-Mills Debate to be held on March
1, announces Ruth Tinslev, Women's
Debating Manager.
Women's Debating Society will hold
a business meeting tomorrow night
at 7:30 o'clock downstairs in the
Women's Clubhouse.
