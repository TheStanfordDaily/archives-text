# PRICE WILL TALK ON "UNIVERSITY AFLOAT"
## 
### 
"Around the World on the Univer
sity Afloat" will be the subject of an
address by James F. Price at the
meeting of the Education Club next
Monday evening at 8 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. Mr. Price made
the world tour on the S.S. Ryndam
from September 1926 to May 1927.
The student affairs on board and
on shore were in the hands of a stu
dent body organization of which Mr.
Price was president. The oddresß will
be followed by a discussion.
