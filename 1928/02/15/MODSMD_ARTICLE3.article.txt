# EUPHRONIA TO MEET FRESHMAN DEBATERS
## 
### 
"Resolved, That the present effort
in America to give college education
to the masses should be abandoned"
is the subject of a debate between
Euphronia debating society and a
freshman debating team, to be held
in the lobby of the Old Union at 8
o'clock tonight.
Euphronia debaters who will speak
on the negative side of the topic are
Victor Burns, Louis Mercado, and
Howard Custer. Wadieh Sliibley,
Sturgis Riddle, and Harry Warmke
are the freshmen upholding the af
firmative.
The freshman team will meet the
O.S.C. frosh early in April on tho
same subject.
