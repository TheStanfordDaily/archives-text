# ENTIRE MEMBERSHIP OF Y.W.C.A. TO MEET
## 
### 
The entire membership of the
Y.W.C.A. will hold a short meeting
at 6:40 o'clock tomorrow night down
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse, an
nounces Miss Harriet Cogswell, Stan
ford secretary. Florence Cooper, treas
urer, will submit a plan for a finan
cial drive to be held in the near
future.
Directly following this, the Fresh
man Cabinet will meet to discuss
plans for the quarter.
