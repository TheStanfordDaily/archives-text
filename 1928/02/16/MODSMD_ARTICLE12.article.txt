# DeMolays May Affiliate Before Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Officers of the Stanford DeMolay
Club have announced that prior to
their meeting tonight at 7:30 o'clock
in the basement of the Women's Club
house, is not too late for DeMolays
to become affiliated with the Stan
ford Club. At the meeting tonight the
question of initiation and the possi
bilities of organizing a DeMolay De
gree Team will be discussed.
