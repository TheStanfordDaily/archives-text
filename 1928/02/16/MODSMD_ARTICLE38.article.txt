# Budget to Be Put Before Y.W.C.A. Members Tonight
## 
### 
Florence Cooper, Y.W.C.A. treas
urer, will submit for approval a new
budget at a meeting of the entire
Y.W.C.A. membership in the Women's
Clubhouse tonight at 6:50 o'clock.
Barbara Lee will explain plans for
collecting membership dues, an
nounces Miss Harriet Cogswell, Stan
ford secretary.
Immediately following this meet
ing. Freshman Cabinet will convene.
The business will be done in a very
short while so that members may at
tend the Pro Arte concert.
