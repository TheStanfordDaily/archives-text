# SMALLPOX EPIDEMIC CLAIMS EIGHT LIVES ON PACIFIC STEAMER
## 
### 
HONOLULU, T.H., Feb. 15.—( U.P.)
—With eight persons already dead
and an epidemic of smallpox and
pneumonia raging among the passen
gers and crew, the steamship Presi
dent Cleveland was making a dra
matic race with death tonight, and
will arrive here early Thursday, ac
cording to radio advices.
Most of the deaths have thus far
been among the steerage passengers,
according to the wireless dispatches
and, although it was known that the
epidemic had reached the cabiu,
whether or not any first-class passen
gers were dangerously stricken was
not learned.
