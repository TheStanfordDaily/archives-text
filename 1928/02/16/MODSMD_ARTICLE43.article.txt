# Nicholas De Grigny's Work To Feature Recital Today
## 
### 
The work of Nicholas de Grigny,
who was the organist at the Cathe
dral at Rheims during the latter part
of the seventeenth century, will be
played this afternoon by Warren D.
Allen in the organ recital at the
Chapel at 4 o'clock.
The program for this afternoon is
as follows:
Prelude to "Lohengrin"
Richard Wagner
Minuet in C, from the "Jupiter"
Symphony Mozart
Recit de tierce en taille
Nicholas de Grigny
Lullaby Brahms
A Ground ("Evening Hymn")
Henry Purcell
This program will be repeated Sun
day afternoon at 4 o'clock.
