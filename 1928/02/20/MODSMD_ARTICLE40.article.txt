# NEW Y.W.C.A. BUDGET ELIMINATES NEED OF EXTRA FINANCE DRIVE
## 
### 
The new Y.W.C.A. budget for $550
was approved at a meeting of the
members Tuesday night. It is to cover
all demands which it will have to meet
so that no extra drives will be needed.
Collection of membership dues, which
' will be combined with the financial
' drive, will be made from February 24
to 28. Pledges obtained at this time
will be collected April 5 and May 5.
Barbara Lee is chairman of a com
mittee of representatives from all the
living groups who are to collect the
dues. Florence Cooper, treasurer, and
I Winifred Huntington, president,
i spoke concerning the financial drive.
