# Professor Clark to Speak At Mothers' Club Meeting
## 
### 
A meeting of the Mothers' Club
will be held tomorrow at 3 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Professor
Arthur B. Clark, of the Department
of Education, will give a talk on the
"Art Work done in the University."
A short business meeting will pre
cede the lecture.
The Red Cross has a membership
of over 18,000,000 people, represent
ing fifty nations.
