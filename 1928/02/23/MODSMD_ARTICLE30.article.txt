# HANDBALL CANDIDATES TO HOLD MEET TODAY
## 
### 
Candidates for the varsity handball
squad will meet this afternoon
at 2 o'clock on the courts of the
Peninsula Club, Palo Alto. The team
has scheduled two meets in the com
ing month. The first appearance will
be against the Athens Club on March
5, the second against California the
following week.
C. W. Davis announced that there
is room for eight men on the squad
and that all men interested should
try out for the positions.
