# TO TOONERVILLE
## 
### 
Intrepidly perambulating,
Seldom ever hesitating,
Dauntless, braving gloom
Made gloomier by trees,
'Tis funny—ever ludicrous
That on this glorious omnibus,

Some folk prefer the tail-light
To paying six-cent fees.
—"Crom," '31.
♦ ♦ *
Esther is a clever girl,
Maggie not at all.
Esther is a lank type,
Maggie's petite and small.
Maggie parks easily
Within a Ford couoe —
Esther's best on a davenport,
Because she's built that way.
—Oscar, '31.
What college comics do you read,
Oscar? We've already met that poem,
or else its little sister. —Ed.
