# TRYOUTS ARE PLANNED FOR MURAL SWIMMING
## 
### 
Tryouts for the intramural swim
ming meet, to be held on Monday,
will be held this afternoon at 4:15
o'clock, according to an announce
ment made by C. W. Davis yesterday.
Six men will be chosen in each
event to compete in the finals. Entry
points will be given to only those
organizations that qualify four men.
Points will be given for the first four
places in each event. A plaque will
be awarded the high-point man.
