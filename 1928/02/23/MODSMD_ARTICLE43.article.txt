# ALUMNI WILL OBSERVE FOUNDERS' DAY MAY 9
## 
### 
Meeting of all Stanford alumni and
former Stanford students will be h >11
May 9 in observance of Founders'
Day. Luncheon will be served at 12
o'clock in the Concert Room of the
Palace Hotel.
Speakers for the meeting include
Richard W. Barret, Leland Cutler,
Comptroller Almon E. Roth, Mrs.
Hazel P. Faulkner, Coach "Pop" War
ner, and Coach "Dink" Templeton.
Reservations will be $1.25 per plate
and may be secured through Mrs.
Triatam I). Connelly, 1369 Green St.,
San Francisco.
