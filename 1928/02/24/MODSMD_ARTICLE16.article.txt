# Australia Flight Proves Economy of Air Travel
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 23.—(U.P.)—
The economy of travel by air is demon
strated by the flight of Bert Hinkler,
English aviator, who flew from England
to Australia at an average cost of 2 cents
a mile, Trubee Davison, assistant secre
tary of war in charge of aviation, said
tonight.
"This courageous and skillful British
airman flew 12,(WK) miles in 15 days at a
cost of about 2 cents a mile according to
press reports," Davison said. "The facts
and figures speak for themselves."
