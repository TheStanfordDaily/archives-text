# Two Former U.C. Oarsmen May Row on Husky Crew
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash. — (P.I.P.) — Two
former California oarsmen, Jack and
Stanley Valentine, promise to make
strong bids for the Huskies' varsity
crew this year.
The first turnout of the season in
cluded five varsity lettermen, seven
junior lettermen from the champion
| ship crew of 1927, and six oarsmen
from the freshman eight of last year.
