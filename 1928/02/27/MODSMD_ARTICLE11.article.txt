# SEATS FOR CARD-U.S.C. GAME ON SALE TODAY
## 
### 
| Students' tickets for the Stanford-
U.S.C. basketball game to be played
! in the Pavilion Wednesday night will
| bo on sale at the Old Union between
1 and 5 o'clock today and tomorrow.
In order to obtain tickets, which are
fifty cents, students must show their
A.S.S.U. cards. General admission for
the game will be one dollar.
