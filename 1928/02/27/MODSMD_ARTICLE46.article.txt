# WOMEN WILL DEBATE WITH MILLS COLLEGE
## 
### 
"Resolved, That this house regrets
the present tendency of the college
students toward paganism," will be
the subject of a debate in which Stan
ford will meet Mills College on Feb
ruary 29 and 30. The negative of the
question will be upheld at Mills by
Violet Crosbie and Dorothy Fordyce
from Stanford on Wednesday. Rose
Brecher and Katharine Teach, also
from Stanford, will discuss the affirm
ative side at the Little Theatre at
7:30 o'clock the following night.
