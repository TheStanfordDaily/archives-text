# Soviets Sentence Five To Death for Espionage
## 
### 
ODESSA, U.S.S.R, Feb. 26.—( U.P.)
—Five persons were sentenced to
death today when they were con
victed of espionage on behalf of Ru
mania. Twenty men and one woman
were prosecuted on this charge in
three simultaneous trials. Those who
did not receive the death sentence
were given varying terms of impris
onment. The woman was sentenced
to serve three years in jail.
