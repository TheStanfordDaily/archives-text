# SENIORS WILL HOLD JOLLY-UP THURSDAY
## 
### 
A senior jolly-up to be followed by
a class meeting will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse on Thursday
night from 7 to 8 o'clock, announces
Louis Creveling, chairman of the
committee.
The class meeting will be devoted
to a consideration of the proposition
of an endowment fund for the Class
of 1028. An announcement will bn
made of appointment to several com
mittees.
