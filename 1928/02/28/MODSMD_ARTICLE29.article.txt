# Is She Really?
## 
### 
The only dramatic production of the Winter Quarter will be given
Friday night. Due to "Ippy" and various mixiips the show has necessarily
been delayed several times, but the management assures us that this date
is definite. So those students who have been waiting to purchase their
tickets when they were sure the money wasn't merely being tied up, can
visit the office in the Old Union and dole out with safety.
There is an unusually experienced cast in "'I he Romantic Young
Lady," and though the name gives the connotation of a somewhat soulful
affair, we are assured that it is not fdled with auras, but rather with a lot
. of good, clever lines. We hope so, and we wonder if the Young Lady
really is.
