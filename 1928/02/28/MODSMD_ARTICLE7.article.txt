# French Club Offers Two Benefit Plays Tomorrow
## 
### 
Two benefit plays will be presented
by the French Club at 8:15 o'clock
tomorrow night in the Little Theatre,
the proceeds of which will go toward
the reconstruction of a convent occu
pied by American troops in France.
The first play will be "Deux Con
verts," by Guitry, and the second,
"La Maine Leste," Labriche. Tickets,
which are 75 cents, are on sale at
the A.S.S.U. office.
