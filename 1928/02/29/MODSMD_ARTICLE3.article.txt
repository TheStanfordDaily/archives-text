# Girls' Reserve Work Head Will Visit Campus Today
## 
### 
Women interested in securing positions
as camp councilors for the summer, are
asked to meet Marjorie Grant, head of
the Girls' Reserve Work in San Fran
cisco, who will visit the campus today.
Miss Grant will interview women from
3:15 to 6 o'clock this afternoon at 532
Lasuen. Appointments may be made
through the Dean of Women's office to
sec her in the evening.
