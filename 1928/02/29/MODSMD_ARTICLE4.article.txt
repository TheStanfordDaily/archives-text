# New Chairman Plans Encina Club Smoker for Thursday
## 
###

smoker in the Hall lobby at G:45
o'clock Thursday night. This will be
the first smoker planned by A1 Miller,
the newly elected chairman of the
smoker committee. Plenty of enter
tainment, food, and smokes have been
promised.
