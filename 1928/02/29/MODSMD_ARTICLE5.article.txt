# SEAT SALE CONTINUES FOR S.C. TILT TONIGHT
## 
### 
Seat sale for the Stanford-US.C.
basketball game to be played in the
Pavilion tonight at 8 o'clock will con
tinue from 1 to 5 o'clock at the Old
Union.
Rooters' tickets can be obtained at
the A.S.S.U. office for 50 cents by
showing student body cards. General
admission tickets can be obtained at
the Board of Athletic Control office
in the Administration Building of En
cina Gym until 5 o'clock.

Starting with today's sale the new
policy adopted for the sale of unre
served rooters' tickets will come into
use. All sale of rooters' tickets will
continue up to 5 o'clock of the night
of the game from now on.
