# STUDENT BREAKERS OF TRAFFIC RULES FINED
## 
### 
Nine violators of the University
traffic regulations were fined by the
Men's Council at a meeting Tuesday
afternoon. No other cases were
brought up. The fines are as follows:
Five arterial stop violations, $1
each.
Two prohibitive parking cases, $1
each.
Two speed cases for intersections
on Palm Drive, one fined $25 and one
$17.
Money derived from fines goes to
pay for the traffic officer and for I
maintenance of University roads.
