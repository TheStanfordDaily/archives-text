# THE 						QUIPPING POST
## 
### 
—University of Minnesota Daily.
Herr Editor:
Py golly, von, we ah havink ein lot
of schnee. Mein outomobile is hardt
for gettink stardtet de mornink in.
Mein frau cranks for maype it iss
ain hour before we can get it movink.
problem to? I am arrivink too often
Iss it any solushun you know such a
late dem mornink classes for. Mein
poss, Herr Schlenker, is me madt on
Maype I should ride ein horse and
already. How can I dis ting up fix?
puggy. Yes, no? Py golly I am in a
maze! —Oscar Burkhardt.
