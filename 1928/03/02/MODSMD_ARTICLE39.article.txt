# STANFORD MASONS BID TO ANNUAL PILGRIMAGE
## 
### 
Joe Catanich, Elvin Hoy, and Harry
Franks, members of the Stanford
Masonic Club degree team, have been
invited by S. L. Hawken, District
Masonic Club Inspector, to accom
pany the officers of the annual Dis
trict Pilgrimage to Sacramento on
April 21. This invitation was extended
after Hawken saw the local team
confer degrees at Burlingame Lodge
last Tuesday night.
Hawken declared that the work of
the Stanford team was comparable to
that of the best lodges in the state;
he would have no hesitation in send
ing the team to any lodge in the dis
trict with his highest recommenda
tion.
