# Y.M.C.A. to Conduct Hike To Feldt Lake Saturday
## 
### 
All students wishing to go on the
Y.M.C.A. hike to Feldt Lake tomorrow
afternoon are asked to communicate
with the "Y" secretary, Larry Sohmitl,
or Don Purvis, before noon today.
The hikers will leave the Y.M.C.A.
building at 1:30 o'clock tomorrow and
will return sometime after dinner.
They will cook their supper at the
lake.
