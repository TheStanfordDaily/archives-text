# NORTHWESTERN TENNIS CAPTAIN IS INELIGIBLE
## 
### 
EVANSTON, 111.— (I.P.)—lneligibil
ity landed its fifth blow of the sea
son upon Northwestern's athletic cap
tains when John Phillips, tennis lead
er, received a notice of a condition in
one of his subjects.
Net followers of the Purple are
hopeful, however, that he may be able
to clear his record by the time the
outdoor season begins.
Phillips is the fifth captain of the
season to get into similar difficulties.
Track, swimming, wrestling, and foot
ball leaders have all been under a
like cloud this year, although the grid
captain had finished his season be
fore the semester examinations.
