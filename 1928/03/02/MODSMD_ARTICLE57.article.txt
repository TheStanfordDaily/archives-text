# TWO GIRLS KILLED BY FLAMES, FALLING TREE
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., March 1— (U.P.)
—Fire and a falling tree claimed the
lives of two small girls near here
today.
The five-months-old daughter of
Mrs. It. L. Ziegelman perished in a
fire that destroyed a house near
Riverton. Phyllis Nelson, aged five,
was killed when she was hit by a tree
her father cad cut down near Zenith.
