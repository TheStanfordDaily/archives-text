# FLIERS HOP OFF ON FIRST LAP OF TOUR
## 
### 
CURTISS FIELD, L.1., March 4 —
(U.P.) —Colonel Arthur C. Goebel,
winner of the Dole transpacific flight,
and Ernest Robison, vice-president of
the Fairchild Aviation Co., took off
at 3:25 p.m. today in a monoplane on
the first lap of a good-will tour of the
Orient.
WASHINGTON, March 4—Colonel
Arthur C. Goebel and Ernest Robison
arrived at Rolling field at 6:30 to
night from New York.
