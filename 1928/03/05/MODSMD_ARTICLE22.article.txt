# FORCED LANDING MADE IN FRONT OF BRANNER
## 
### 
Forced down by engine trouble.
Move Stephens, flying a Waco plane
from the Palo Alto School of Avia
tion, made a perfect landing on the
rough field in front of Branner Hall
yesterday afternoon. It was found
that a defective float in the carburetor
caused the engine to slow down.
Lieutenant. Ooddard, with his as
sistants. made necessary repairs and
the plane was able to take off again.
Stephens is a Stanford student and
an experienced pilot.
