# CANDIDATES DREAM OF PRESIDENTIAL PILLOW
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, March 4.— (U.P.) —
Presidential candidates probably were
dreaming sweet dreams tonight. For
exactly one year hence one of them
is likely to pillow his head on a
White House bed, as the new chief
of the nation—unless President Cool
idge, contrary to the expectation of
most politicians, is renominated and
re-elected.
