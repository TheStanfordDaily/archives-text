# MISCELLANEOUS
## 
### 
Men, now living in Encina, who wish
to reserve their rooms for Spring
Quarter, should do so at the Dean |
of Men's Office today.
Men now living in Toyon and Branner,
halls who wish to reserve their
rooms for spring quarter should
call at the Dean of Men's office to
day or tomorrow and pay deposits.
Room drawings will be held in the
offices of the respective halls on
Wednesday at 7:30 o'clock.
Men now living in the Union or Se
quoia Hall who wish to reserve
their rooms for the spring quarter
should call at the Dean of Men's
office Thursday or Friday, March
8 and 9, and pay deposits.

nalism from 1920 to 1922, has become

ly he has been an executive of the j
Yellow Cab Company In San Fran-1
cisco.
