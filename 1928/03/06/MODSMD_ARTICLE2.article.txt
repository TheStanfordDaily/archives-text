# NATURE POSTERS BY SCHOOL CHILDREN ON DISPLAY AT GALLERY
## 
### 
Nature posters made by the school
children of California are on exhibi
tion at the Stanford Art Gallery from
now until March 13 under the auspi
ces of the California Wild Flower
Conservation League.
The work of school children over
the state, particularly the Bay Dis
trict, is shown in the exhibition.
From the first grade to the last year
in high school the students are taught
the principles of conservation through
the Wild Flower League, and these
students make posters with appropri
ate slogans on them.
Representative work is shown from ]
the following schools: Fremont, j
Berkeley, Palo Alto, San Jose, and j
