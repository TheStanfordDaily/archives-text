# NO SEX IN POLITICS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, March 5— (U.P.)
—Sox has no place In politics, ac
cording to Mrs. Florence P. Kahn,
Republican member of Congress from
California.
"We should not support a man for
office because he is a man, nor a
woman because she is a woman," she
said.
"There is no sex in citizenship and
there should be none In politics."
