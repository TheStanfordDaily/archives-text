# ICHIHASHI WILL SPEAK BEFORE DEBATE CLUB
## 
### 
Dr. Yamato Ichihashi, Professor of
Japanese History, will talk on
"Japanese Influence in China" tomor
row niglit at 8 o'clock in the Old
Union Lobby before an open meeting
of the Euphronia Debating Society.
On the following Wednesday Eu
phronia will hold its quarterly ban
quet, after which a team representing
the Stanford society will meet the
debate team of the Parliament Wo
men's Society of the University of
California.
