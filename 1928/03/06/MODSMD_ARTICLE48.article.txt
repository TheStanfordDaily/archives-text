# Scholarship Applications To Be Filed Before April
## 
### 
All University scholarships for
1928-29, except those reserved for en
tering students, will be awarded dur
ing the spring quarter. Applications
should be filed at the office of the
Committee on Scholarship before
April 1 for graduate, and April 15 for
undergraduate, scholarships. Candi
dates for 1927-28, including present
holders of scholarships who wish to
apply for renewals, should file new
applications at this time.
