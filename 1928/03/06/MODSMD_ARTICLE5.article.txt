# TICKETS AVAILABLE FOR FOUNDERS' DAY SYMPHONY CONCERT
## 
### 
Student and faculty tickets for
the Founders' Day concert to be
given Friday evening in the Me
morial Church will be distribu
ted today from 9 to 12 o'clock
and from 1:30 to 4:30 o'clock in
the lobby of the Administration
Building.
Admission to the concert will
be by ticket only. Students may
obtain tickets upon presentation
of student body cards.
The San Francisco Symphony
Orchestra, conducted by Alfred
Hertz, has been secured to pre
sent a program of three selec
tions Friday night at 8:15 o'clock
in commemoration of Mr. and
Mrs. Stanford, founders of the
University.
