# Cosmopolitan Club Plans Picnic For Next Sunday
## 
### 
Cosmopolitan Club is planning a
picnic to be held at Big Basin next
Sunday, and everyone intending to
go should inform Dixie Stall, presi
dent, before Saturday. Transportation
will be provided. The regular Inter
national luncheon of the club will be
held tomorrow noon in the alcove of
the Union dining room.
