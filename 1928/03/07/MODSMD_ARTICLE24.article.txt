# MEN'S COUNCIL ELECTS VINCENTI AS CHAIRMAN
## 
### 
Louis Vincenti was elected to suc
ceed Fred Wool as Chairman of the
Men's Council for the remainder of
the University year at the meeting of
the council held yesterday afternoon.
Norman Fain was elected secretary
and Fred Wool, secretary to handle
traffic cases.
Two students were fined $1 each
for failing to make arterial stops, and
one was fined $1 for parking in a pro
hibited area. One N.S.F. check was
ordered paid at once.
