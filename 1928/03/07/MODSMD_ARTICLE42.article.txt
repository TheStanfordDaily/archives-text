# FRESHMEN TO HOLD JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
The first freshman jolly-up of the
year will be held tomorrow night
from 7 to 8 o'clock at Roble Hall.
This is one of. several jolly-ups being
planned by the freshmen for this
quarter and the spring quarter.
U.C. held its ninth quadrennial Uni
versity Labor Day and third post-war
Big "C" Sirkus on February 20.
