# Eldeison Will Speak at Masonic Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
"Masonry in Other Lands" will be
the subject of a talk by G. A. Eldei
son, of the Palo Alto Masonic Lodge,
at a meeting of the Stanford Masonic
Club tonight at 6:30 o'clock in the
special dining room of the Union.
Eldeison is assistant-manager of the
Sunset Cafeteria.
Plans for the Los Angeles trip for
the degree team will be discussed.
The meeting will be open to all De-
Molays and Masons.
