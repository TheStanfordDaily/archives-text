# SENIORS MUST ALTER QUAD RECORDS TODAY
## 
### 
All seniors who wish to add any
thing to their senior records in the
Quad must mail these changes today
to the Quad Editorial Office, Box 100,
declares Herb Reynolds, editor. Un
less these are altered at once, the
records will be printed as they were
formerly submitted.
Any organization which has elected
new members, or has any other
change to make, should notify the
Quad imdiediately.
