# La 						Verne Corbin Is Elected California Captain for '29
## 
### 
BERKELEY—(I.P.)—LaVerne Cor
bin, leading scorer and center of the
University of California basketball
team, has been elected captain of the
Bears for the 1029 season.
Corbin, who is a junior, made the
Boar varsity last year as a sopho
more and was selected as second all
coast center.
This season his choice as first all
coast center was practically unani
mous.
