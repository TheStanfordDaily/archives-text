# Theta Sigma Phi to Hold Business Meeting Today
## 
### 
An important meeting of all active
and alumni members of Theta Sigma
Phi will be held at 3 o'clock today
in the home of Mrs. A. Stevenson (Isa
bel Schanek) at 1327 Byron, in Palo
Alto. There will be discussion of new
members, and activities for the rest
of the year, according to Hallie Keat
ing, president. A car will be in front
of the Daily Office at 2:30 o'clock to
take those who wish to go.
