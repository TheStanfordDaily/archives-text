# Students On Riot; Policemen Killed
## 
### 
CUCUTA, Colombia, March 8. —
(TJ.P.) —Latest reports from Caracas,
Venezuela, received here today, said
thirty persons had been killed in
Venezuelan disorders. This does not
include the six policemen who were
lynched by students.
Students arrested in connection
with the disorders' are still being
held in their dungeon-jail although
the government has offered to liber
ate them under bail. The students
decline to negotiate with the govern
ment, it was said.
