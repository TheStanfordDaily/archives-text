# Professor James Will Give English 130 Spring Quarter
## 
### 
English 130, Advanced Composition,
will be given in the spring quarter by
Professor E. O. James. The class is
limited to twenty-five. Applicants should
be prepared to show Mr. James something
of their work on Registration Day. Those
who filed the petition for such a course
will be given preference, so far as the
size is concerned, provided they will hand
in their names at once to the Secretary of
the English Department, ofiicc 207 a.
