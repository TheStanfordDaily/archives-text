# Student Protest Aroused By Advancing Tuition Rate
## 
### 
EVANSTON, 111., Mar. B.— (P.I P.)
—Violent student protest was aroused on
the Northwestern University campus re
cently when a raise in tuition was an
nounced. The new rate will be $25 higher
than that for this year. Poor equipment
011 the Evanston campus was given by
prominent students as the chief reasons
for denouncing the new rates.
