# MRS. KISICH WILL GIVE COURSE NEXT QUARTER
## 
### 
Mrs. Margaret Shedd Kisich of the
Palo Alto Pendragon Players will give a
course next quarter at Stanford in Out
door Play Production, Public Speaking
126.
This course will be play giving out of
doors with especial attention laid 011 the
mounting of a play for public presenta
tion. It will be given on Tuesdays and
Thursdays from 3:05 to 5:05. Students
interested should consult Mrs. Kisich at
Palo Alto 3367.

All Princeton seniors are being per
mitted an unlimited number of lecture
cuts for the rest of this year.
