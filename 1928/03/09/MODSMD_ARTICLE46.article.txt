# MASTERS ANNOUNCES 1929 HOOP SCHEDULE
## 
### 
Stanford's 1929 conference basket
ball schedule was announced yester
day by Graduate Manager A1 Masters,
who returned from the schedule
meeting in Los Angeles.
The conference schedule is as fol
lows:
Jan. 12 —U.C.L.A. at Stanford.
Jan. 19 —U.S.C. at Los Angeles.
Jan. 26 —California.
Feb. I—U.C.L.A. at Los Angeles.
Feb. 2 —U.C.L.A. at Los Angeles.
Feb. 15 —U.S.C. at Stanford.
Feb. 16 —U.S.C. at Stanford.
Feb. 23 —California.
No decision has been made as to
where the Stanford-California games
will be played. In all probability, one
will be played in the Stanford Pa
vilion, one at Oakland Auditorium,
and one at Kezar Pavilion.
In the United States there is an
automobile for every five persons; in
China there is one for every 23,000.
