# Indicted Oil Magnate Posts $5000 Bond for Appearance
## 
### 
CHICAGO, March B.—(U.P.)—Colo
nel Robert Stewart, chairman of the
board of the Standard Oil Company of
Indiana, indicted recently for contempt of
the United States Senate, appeared before
United States Commissioner James R.
Glass late today and furnished bond of
$5000 for his appearance on the charge.

U.S.C. plans to organize a polo
team.
