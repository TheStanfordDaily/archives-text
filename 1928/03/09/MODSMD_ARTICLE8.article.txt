# Women Will Register For Spring Quarter Next Week
## 
### 
Advance registration of women for
the spring quarter will be arranged
in the Dean of Women's office hy
Miss Boeziriger on Wednesday, Thurs
day, and Friday of next week from
6:30 to 12 o'clock and from 2 to 5
o'clock, and on Saturday front 8:30
to 12 o'clock.
Beginning Monday, students may
sign for appointments in the Dean of
Women's office. They are urged to
attend to registration promptly 1n
order to avoid delays on Registration
Day.
