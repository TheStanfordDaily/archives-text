# VARSITY RACKET MEN DEFEAT U.C.L.A. TEAM IN EASY 7-0 VICTORY
## 
### 
Defeating the U.C.L.A. tennis team
to the score of 7-0, the varsity won
its third successive match Saturday
afternoon. The first five Stanford men
won their singles matches in two
straight sets with the exception of
Ralph McElvenny, whose match with
Smith, the U.C.L.A. third ranking
player, went three sets.
A1 Herrington, first man on the
Card varsity, defeated Bob Laird of
the corresponding position on the
U.C.L.A. varsity in two evenly match
ed sets with a score of 6-4, 6-4. John
Wheatley easily downed Rod Houser,
U.C.L.A. second man,- 6-1, 6-3. Mc-
Elvenny, losing his first set to Smith,
6-3, staged a comeback, taking the
second set 6-0, and winning the third
set 6-2. Renzel, number five man, won
from Struble 6-4, 6-1.
In the doubles matches Herrington
and McElvenny defeated Laird and
Bledsoe, 6-4, 6-4, and Wheatley and
Thomas downed Strubel and West
smith, 6-3, 6-2.
