# FROSH TENNIS TEAM GAINS 6-0 WIN FROM SAINT IGNATIUS HIGH
## 
### 
Another victory went to the class
of 1931 Saturday morning when the
freshman tennis team won all of the
six matches played against the St.
Ignatius High School team on the
varsity courts here.
John Doeg, freshman, took straight
sets from Captain Joe Coughlin of St.
Ignatius, 6-4, 6-2. Easton iiad more
difficulty* in downing the diminutive
star, Hyde, the match going three
sets, 6-3, 4-6, 6-1. Stephens won from
Dunn, 6-3, 6-3; and Forbes defeated
Bentley, 6-0, 6-4.
In the freshman matches Easton
and Stephens downed Coughlin and
Hyde, 6-1, 6-3; while Gantner and
Forbes won against Dunn and Bent
ley in a close match, 7-5, 7-5.
