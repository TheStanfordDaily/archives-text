# BABE GOLFERS HAVE SUCCESSFUL SEASON
## 
### 
The 1931 golf squad has shown itself
'o be one of the best that Stanford has
had during the past few years by
easily defeating the varsity players in

two regularly arranged meets, taking
7 out of 12 matches in the first, and
taking 8, tieing 2 and losing 2 in the
second.
Burnaby, Stevenson, Lewis, White,
Johnson and Edmonds are included on
the freshman team. Freeman, Brink,
Sutphen, Allen, Bonner and Scott also
played in the varsity meets. The '31
golfers will play California at Berke
ley, April 7.
