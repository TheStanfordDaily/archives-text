# FOUNDERS' DAY OBSERVED
## 
### 
The Stanford Men's Club and the
Stanford Women's Club jointly spon
sored a Founders' Day luncheon in
the Palace Hotel, Saturday. Leland
W. Cutler, alumnus of the class of
1906 and present trustee of the Uni
versity, was toastmaster. President
Ray Lyman Wilbur was the chief
speaker of the occasion.
