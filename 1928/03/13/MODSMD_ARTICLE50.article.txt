# EUPHRONIA QUARTERLY BANQUET IS SET FOR TOMORROW IN UNION
## 
### 
Euphronia, Stanford's men's debat
ing society, will hold its quarterly
banquet in the New Union at 6 o'clock
Wednesday night, announces Leon
Carley, president.
Guests at the banquet will be wo
men of the Parliament Society of the
University of California, a debating
organization, who will oppose a
Euphronia team following the ban
quet in a debate on the question, "Re
solved, That this house approves of
educational qualifications for voters."
This debate will be held in the lobby
of the Old Union.
