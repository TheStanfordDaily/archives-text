# ORDERS FOR SENIOR PROGRAMS TO BE IN FIRST OF QUARTER
## 
### 
Senior Week programs, which
will probably be made of black
leather and decorated with wood
cuts, and graduation announce-'
ments may be obtained only by
advanced orders placed during
the first two weeks of next quar
ter, according to an announce
ment made by Claude Conn,
president of the senior class.
All seniors should decide this
quarter the number of programs
and announcements they will re
quire as provisions for ordering
will only be made at the speci
fied time, Conn stated.
