# PALO ALTO TO GIVE UP JUNIOR COLLEGE WORK
## 
### 
Discontinuance of the junior college
department of the Palo Alto Union
High School at the close of the cur
rent school year will be made neces
sary by lack of funds, it was an
nounced Saturday by the board of
trustees.
The junior college department of
the school was established last Sep
tember, and only first-year college
work has been conducted. The board
will continue efforts to form a union
with neighboring high school dis
tricts.

Included in the enrollment of the Uni
versity of Washington are fifty Russian
students who were exiled from the nation
of their birth after having fought under
the flag of the "White Armies."
