# N.Y. STOCK EXCHANGE HAS GREATEST DAY'S BUSINESS IN HISTORY
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Mar. 13.—(U.P.)—The
general public picked today to join the
millionaires in the business of specu
lating on the New York stock ex
change and by their combined efforts
gave the exchange the greatest day's
business in its history.
A total of 4,057,930 shares of stocks
changed hands in live hours of frantic
trading. The greatest previous day's
business was March 3, 1926, when
3,873,700 shares were bought and sold.
Yesterday that record was approach
ed with total dealings of 3,810,000
shares. Today it was exceeded by 184,-
230 shares.
No exchange anywhere ever saw
such a day.
