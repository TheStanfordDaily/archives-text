# Award Airplane Rides for Best Work in R.O.T.C. Unit
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., Mar. 12.—(P.1.P.)
—The five sophomores doing the best
work in the R.O.T.C. unit at the Uni
versity of Washington will be awarded
airplane rides as prizes. Deportment, at
tendance at drills and classroom work,
and the handling of squads will all count
in the competition. First prize will be
a 75-minute ride, second 60 minutes, third
45 minutes, and fourth and fifth a 30-
minute ride each.
