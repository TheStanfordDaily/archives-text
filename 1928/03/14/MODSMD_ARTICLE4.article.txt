# LIBRARY BUYS BOOKS ON BOTANICAL LIFE
## 
### 
The Library has recently purchased,
for more than $700, a seven-volume
set on plant life: "Voyage de Hum
boldt et Bonpjland Botanique; Nova
Genera et Species Plantarium." The
work, containing many plates, con
cerns the travels of the two French
men and their botanical discoveries.
It was published in Paris 1815-1825.
It has been acquired for the use of
the Dudley Herbarium, a collection
founded in memory of a former Bot
any professor.
