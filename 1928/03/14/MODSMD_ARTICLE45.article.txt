# S.C. CHARTERS SPECIAL TRAIN FOR EXPOSITION
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Mar. 13.—(P.1.P.)—
A special train was chartered by the
University of Southern California to
take the band and the men's and women's
glee clubs to San Bernardino, where they
took part in the Trojan Day program of
the National Orange show.
President R. B. vonKleinsmid of the
University of Southern California de
livered the principal address of the day.
