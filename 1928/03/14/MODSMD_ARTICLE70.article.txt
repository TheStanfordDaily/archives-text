# Seven Men of Degree Teams Get Awards for Service
## 
### 
Seven men of the Stanford Masonic
Club Degree Team have recently been
awarded the meritorious Rervice
award pins for outstanding work on
the degree team. Harry Vandervoort,
representative of the Past Masters
Association of the Palo Alto Lodge,
presented the pins at a dinner in the
Union to the following: John Cowan,
Harry Frank, Elwin Hoy, Ray Frank
lin, Fred Miller, Warren Evans, and
John Bacher.
