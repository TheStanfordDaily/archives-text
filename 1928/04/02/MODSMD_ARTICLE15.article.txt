# ALUMNA IS APPOINTED TO DIPLOMATIC POST
## 
### 
Frances Willis, '20, who is the
third woman to be appointed to the
diplomatic corps, is in Washington
preparing to go to her first diplomatic
post, at Valparaiso, Chile, where she
will be vice-consul. She will sail
from New Orleans on April 13.

After graduating from Stanford,
Miss Willis took a postgraduate
course at the University of Brussels.
She was instructor in political science
at Vassar, and, before that, teacher
of history at Goucher.
