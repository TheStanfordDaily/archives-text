# STANFORD GRADUATES MARRIED IN LOS GATOS
## 
### 
Sallie McNair, '24, and Kenneth
Carnahan, '25, were married in Los
Gatos on March 16. Mrs. Carnahan is
a graduate student in Spanish and
belongs to Kappa Kappa Gamma
sorority. Carnahan is a Chi Psi. He
is manager of The Alcove, Ramona
street bookshop.
