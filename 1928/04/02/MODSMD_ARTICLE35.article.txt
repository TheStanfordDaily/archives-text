# One of the Five Hundred
## 
### 
Miss Frances Willis, a graduate of Stanford in 1920, has recently
been appointed a member of the Diplomatic Corps to go to Valparaiso,
Chile. In gaining this post she is the third to have been appointed to
such a position, and is to be congratulated.

ALL MEMBERS OF THE EDITORIAL STAFF must be present
at a meeting to lie held in the Daily office at 1 o'clock today.
