# U.C.L.A. TO HOLD MOCK POLITICAL CONVENTIONS
## 
### 
U.C.L.A., Los Angeles, April 1. —
(P.I.P.) —Not being content with the
straw votes conducted at numerous
universities throughout the nation,
students of the University of Cali
fornia at Los Angeles will hold mock
political conventions for all parties,
April 16 and 17.

Delegates from classes in political
science, history, economics, and pub
lic speaking will form Republican,
Democratic, and Progressive parties.

A straw vote for the presidential
choice will be determined following
the conventions' nominations.
