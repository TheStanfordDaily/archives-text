# Lafayette College Council Limits Prices for Dances
## 
### 
EASTON, Pa.—(l.P.)—By a vote of
twelve to three the Student Council
at Lafayette College has passed a
ruling placing a maximum fee for
undergraduate social events on the
campus. Under the provisions of the
new rule no dance admission may be
placed at more than $5 a couple, and
tickets for various banquets must not
be sold for more than $2, $3, or $5,
according to the event.
