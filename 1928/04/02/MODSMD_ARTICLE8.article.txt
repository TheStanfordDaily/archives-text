# PROFESSOR E. J. FISHER GOES ON LECTURE TOUR
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Edgar J. Fisher
and son, Edgar Jr., left the campus
March 23. Professor Fisher, who is
the head of the history department at
Robert College, Constantinople, has
been 'Acting Professor of History at
Stanford during the winter quarter.

Professor Fisher will speak in vari
ous Southern California universities
and colleges, at the University of Ari
zona in Tucson, at schools through
out the Middle West and East, and at
Syracuse University, where he will
give a course in the School of Citizen
ship during the summer quarter.

The Fishers will sail for Constan
tinople about the middle of August
