ANONYMOUS DONOR
GIVES PRESENT FOR
RESEARCH OF 'IPPY'
Six $100 bills and a brief note
bearing a San Francisco date line
were received by President Wil
bur yesterday from an anony
mous donor. The note explained
that the money was to be used
for the purpose of research to
ward the combating and preven
tion of infantile paralysis.
President Wilbur believes that
the gift is from the same person
who sent a similar contribution
a year ago and who prefers to
remain unknown.
The University now has a fund
of $IGOO for the study of polio
myelitis received during the last
week.
