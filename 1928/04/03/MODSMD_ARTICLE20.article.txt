# RELATIONS BETWEEN NATIONS TO BE TOPIC OF FACULTY MEETING
## 
### 
"International Relations" will be the
program topic at the monthly meeting
of the Faculty Research Club which will
be held at the faculty clubhouse at 8
o'clock this evening.
President Wilbur will read a paper on
"Conference Methods in International
Relations." Professor Carl L. Alsberg
will read a paper on the "Scientific As
pects of Conference Method." And the
third paper, "Technique of the Honolulu
Conference," will be read by Professor
Eliot G. Mears.
