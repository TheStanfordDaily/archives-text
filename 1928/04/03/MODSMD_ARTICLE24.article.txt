# THIRTY-THREE VOTES ADDED TO HOOVER'S TOTAL BY MICHIGAN
## 
### 
LANSING, Mich., April 2—(U.P.)—
Herbert C. Hoover added thirty-three
Republican Convention votes to his
strength in the presidential race and
A1 Smith of New York gained thirty
votes for the Houston gathering, it
was indicated today in the Michigan
preferential primary.
