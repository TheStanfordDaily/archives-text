# ALLEN SETS REHEARSAL OF ORATORIO TONIGHT
## 
### 
Rehearsal for the Dubois oratorio,
"Seven Last Words," will be held tonight
in the Memorial Church at 7:15 o'clock
for the choir and at 8 o'clock for the
University orchestra, announced Warren
D. Allen yesterday. The oratorio will be
given in the Memorial Church at 4:30
o'clock on Good Friday.
Tomorrow night rehearsal for the or
chestra and soloists will be held at 7:15
o'clock, and on Thursday night at 7:30
o'clock rehearsal for the Easter services
is scheduled for the choir.
