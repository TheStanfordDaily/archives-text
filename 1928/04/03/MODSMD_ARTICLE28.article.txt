# OFFICERS FOR Y.W.C.A. TO BE ELECTED TODAY
## 
### 
Voting for Y.W.C.A. officers will be
carried on until 5 o'clock today in the
Y.W.C.A. room upstairs in the Wo
men's Clubhouse, announces Harriet
Cogswell, secretary.
Senior cabinet will meet tonight at
G o'clock for supper to decide upon
delegates to the national Y.W.C.A.
convention to be held in Sacramento
from April 13 to 20. May 4, 5, and 6
have been chosen as the dates for the
Asilomar house party for Stanford
women.
