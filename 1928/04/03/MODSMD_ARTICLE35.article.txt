# U.C.L.A. Five Receives Merit Letter Award
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 2.—(P.1.P.)
—Ten members of Coach Leo B. Cal
land's University of Southern Califor
nia basketball team, winners of the
Pacific Coast Conference champion
ship from Coach C. S. "Hec" Edmund
son's University of Washington five,
were awarded "Merit Letters," the
highest athletic honor in the power
of the university.
Only one other instance of a like
award to an entire team has been
noted at the Trojan institution. This
honor came to Calland's 1922 football
team, which went through an unde
feated season and which defeated
Penn State in a post-season game.
Calland was captain of the team,
playing guard.
