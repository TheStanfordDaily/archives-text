# Washington State to Build New $125,000 Field House
## 
### 
PULLMAN, Wash., April 2—(P.1.P.)
—An athletic expansion program
calling for a new $125,000 field house
was announced recently at Washing
ton State College by Graduate Mana
ger Earl V. Foster.
The proposed field house will be a
huge shell, 125 feet by 300 feet with
overhead clearance of 60 feet, con
sisting simply of brick walls and roof
with earth floor. It will provide for
football, track, and baseball practice
at any time of the year without re
gard to weather.
