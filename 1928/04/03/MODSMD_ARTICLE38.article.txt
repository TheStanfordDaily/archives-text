# WASHINGTON 'COLUMNS' EDITOR IS DISMISSED
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., April 2—(P.1.P.)
—The editor of the University of
Washington Columns, monthly comic
magazine, was dismissed March 28
by the board of control of that insti
tution. The board declared him guilty
of "gross bad taste that cannot be
tolerated in a college paper" for writ
ing and publishing an article on the
"History of Expectoration."
This is the third editor to be thus
removed in three years.
