# EXHIBIT OF CALIFORNIA LANDSCAPES IS SHOWN
## 
### 
California landscapes by F. H. Cut
ting are on exhibition until April 10
in the Stanford Art Gallery. Typical
subjects of California, such as Ra
raona's Marriage Place and La Jolla,
are portrayed by this California art
ist in a picturesque style.
Thirty-seven paintings represent his
work. Some of the other subjects are:
Montezuma School, Capistrano, Yo
semite, Mission Door, and El Capitan.
