# KIRKPATRICK ELECTED EUPHRONIA PRESIDENT
## 
### 
Leonard Kirkpatrick was elected
president of Euphronia, men's debat
ing society, at the annual banquet of
that organization held during the final
week of the winter quarter. John
Cranston was elected vice-president,
Wagner d' Alessio, secretary, and
Henry Haussamen, treasurer.

Roughs at Pomona College are now in
their glory while that school is holding
its annual whisker-growing contest.
