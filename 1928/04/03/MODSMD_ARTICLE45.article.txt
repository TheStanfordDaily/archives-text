# SPRING SOCCER TRIALS TO START WEDNESDAY, SAYS COACH MALONEY
## 
### 
Spring quarter soccer practice will
commence immediately and be regularly
held on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fri
days at 4:30 o'clock, Coach Harry Ma
loney announced yesterday.
Maloney will give soccer his personal
attention this spring, he declared. All
men who expect to try out for the varsity
next fall should be out now. Coach Ma
loney will meet aspirants tomorrow at
4 :30 o'clock on the soccer field.
The soccer decathlon, an annual contest
for the exhibition of proficiency in soc
cer, is an event toward which the men
may look forward to this quarter, he
stated.
