# MISS SIBYL WALCUTT MARRIES DR. TERMAN
## 
### 
The wedding of Sibyl Walcutt and
Dr. Frederick E. Terman took place
on March 22 in the Memorial Church.
Mrs. Terman, a graduate of the
University of Arizona, is working for
a doctorate in psychology at Stan
ford. She belongs to Alpha Phi soror
ity.
Her husband is a son of Professor
Lewis M. Terman, head of the Stan
ford psychology department, and is
now on the engineering faculty. Ter
man's fraternity is Theta Xi.

The University of North Carolina
is holding a series of winter football
contests between organizations of the
school.
