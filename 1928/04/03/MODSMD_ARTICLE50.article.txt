# Trojan Baseball Players Start 14,000 Mile Journey
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 2.—(P.1.P.) —
"Wahoo Sam" Crawford, University
of Southorn California baseball coach,
is preparing his men for their 14,000-
inile journey. The Trojan nine will
make a trip to Japan during the sum
mer months, which will take them
over 12,000 miles. Trips to Stanford
and Berkeley for games will make up
the rest of the journey.
