# Y.M.C.A. Cabinet Members Gather for Last Meeting
## 
### 
The present Y.M.C.A. Cabinet, whose
members have been in office for the past
several months, held its last meeting last
night at the home of Pen Culver, "Y"
secretary.
Plans were completed for the annual
Y.M.C.A. dinner to be held April 11. J.
Stitt Wilson, lecturer and traveler, will
be the principal speaker at this dinner.
