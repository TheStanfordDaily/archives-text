# Cross Country Runners to Enter Fourth State Today
## 
### 
GLEN RIO, N.M., April 3.—(U.P.)—
The Pylc caravan of cross-country run
ners was camped on the New Mexico-
Texas border tonight, ready for an in
vasion of the fourth state to be encoun
tered in the 3400-mile marathon from Los
Angeles to New York.

Ollie Wanttinen, the 90-pound New
York Finn, and Ed Gardner, the Seattle
negro, finished today's run in another of
the dead heats which have featured the
"bunion derby" thus far. They made the
44.1 miles from Tucumcari in six hours,
eight minutes, and 15 seconds, or an aver
age of better than seven miles an hour.
