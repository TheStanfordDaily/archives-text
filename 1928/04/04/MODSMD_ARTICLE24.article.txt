# REPORT HEAVY SALE ON MILITARY BALL TICKETS
## 
### 
Bids for the Military Ball will continue
on sale all this week at the office of the
Associated Students in the Union. A
heavy sale was reported yesterday, and
half the bids were sold to members of the
unit during registration. In the mean
time, the plans for the ball are progress
ing and Craig, decorator from Oakland,
is working on a spectacular motif for
decoration, that of "War of Mars."
