# DAVIS CUP CAPTAIN IS DEFEATED IN PRACTICE
## 
### 
MICXICO CITY, April 3.—(U.P.)—
The defeat of William Tildeti by John
Hennessey featured the practice session
of the United States Davis up team at
the Deportivo Club here today.
The Indianapolis youth played brisk
tennis, downing Tildcn 6-4, 3-6, 6-2.
The first two singles matches in the
opening American zone match between
the United States and Mexico will be
played here Friday. Hennessey and Til
den will represent the United States.
