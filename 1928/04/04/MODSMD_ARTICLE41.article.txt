# Stanford Dames Will Hold First Meeting of Quarter
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold their first
I meeting of the quarter tomorrow after
j noon at 3 o'clock in the Women's Club
| house. Wives of new students as well as

old members arc invited to attend. Fol
lowing the business meeting, the Stan
ford Dames will spend a social hour.
Alumni of the University of Michi
gan have collected $1,000,000 for the
erection of a new women's building.
