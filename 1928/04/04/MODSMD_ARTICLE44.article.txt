# Aero Club Organized by U.S.C. Student Aviators
## 
### 
U)S ANGELES, April 3.—(P.1.P.)—
An Aero Club for students interested in
aviation has been inaugurated in the Uni
versity of Southern California recently.
S. F. Bliss, secretary of the club, states
that the rapid advance in aviation during
the past year has been accomplished al
most entirely by younger men.
