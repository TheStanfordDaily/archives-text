# Ex-Professor at Stanford Gets State Appointment
## 
### 
Mrs. Mary Roberts Coolidge, whose
appointment to the State Board of Edu
cation was announced in yesterday's

morning papers, was associate professor
of Social Science at Stanford from 1894
to 1904. She is the composer of the music
of "Hail, Stanford, Hail." Mrs. Cool
idge is a sister of Roger Roberts of Palo
Alto, and she is married to Dane Cool
idge, '98, novelist and short story writer.

A fifteen-year-old freshman woman
s listed among the forty-eight honor
students of the University of Texas.
