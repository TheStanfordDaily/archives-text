# RUTH TINSLEY CHOSEN PRESIDENT OF Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Officers for the Y.W.C.A. were
voted on at the Women's Clubhouse
yesterday. Results are as follows:

President, Ruth Tinsley; vice-presi
dent, Barbara Lee; secretary, Betty
Alden; treasurer, Editha Wright; un
dergraduate representative, Alice
Ingraham.

There is to be a meeting of the
senior cabinet with the advisory
board for supper at the home of Miss
Gamble, Palo Alto, at G o'clock this
evening.
