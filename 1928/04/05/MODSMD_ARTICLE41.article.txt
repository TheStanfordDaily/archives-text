# Men's Tuesday Gym Classes Meet for First Time Today.
## 
### 
Men's gym classes that meet regularly
on Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday will
meet for the first time this quarter today
at the usual hours, according to Royce
Reed Long, associate director of Hygiene
and Physical Education. All other men's
gym classes have already had their in
itial meetings this quarter.
Freshman scholastic competition has
been inaugurated in organized houses at
Washington.
