# Meeting of Grid Managers Today
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of all men
trying out for football manager in Room
47 of the new Physical Education build
ing today at 3:30 o'clock according to
John Long, senior football manager.
Twelve of the men who were signed
up for sophomore managers during the
winter quarter have already signed again
for the spring quarter. New men trying
out should sign their names on the chart
in the manager's office in the new Physi
cal Education building, where a schedule
of the spring quarter's work has been
posted. The junior managers are sched
uled to be out once a week, while the
sophomores must report twice a week.
