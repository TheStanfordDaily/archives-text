# COLLEGE OF PACIFIC TO GIVE AERONAUTICS
## 
### 
STOCKTON, April 5. — (P.1.P.) —
Plans for an aeronautics course at the
College of the Pacific are almost com

flying field. Several members of the fac
ulty, as well as a large number of the
students, have shown interest in the enter
prise. Besides Stanford, the College of
the Pacific is the only institution on the
Pacific Coast offering such a course.
