# Charles C. Stratton Weds Kathleen Ardis
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Kathleen Ardis
of Downey, Calif., to Charles C. Stratton,
'26, took place at the bride's home Tues
day, March 27. Stratton is a graduate
law student at Stanford, and a member of
Alpha Tau Omega and Phi Alpha Delta
fraternities. While an undergraduate, he
was twice class president, a member of
the varsity football squad, and on the
Men's Council.
