# Seats on L.A. Exchange Sell at New High Figure
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 5— (U.P.)—
Establishing a new all time high rec
ord, two seats on the Los Angeles
Stock Exchange were sold today for
$50,000 each.
Names of the parties to the transac
tions were not made public pending
approval of the Exchange by the
Board of Directors.
The previous high for a stock ex
change seat was $40,000.
