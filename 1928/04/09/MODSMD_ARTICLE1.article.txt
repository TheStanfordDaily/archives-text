# HOUSES START FIRST RUSHING PERIOD TODAY
## Twenty – Four Fraternities Divided in Two Groups For First Week Four Houses Penalized First Period by Council for Improper Rushing 
### 
Stanford's fraternities start their
rushing season today when first-year
men are allowed in the houses on the
Row for the firat time this year.
The twenty-four fraternities have
been divided into two groups for the
first period which extends over this
week. The houses of one group rush
on one day, and those of the other
entertain on the next.
Allow One Date
During this first period only one
date is allowed for each house from
one freshman. In the second and the
final periods, however, ja firßt-year
man may go to the fraternities of his
preference as many times as desired.
Four houses were penalized recent
ly, to the extent of losing the first
period rushing, by the Board of Con
trol of the Interfraternity Council,
following charges of improper rush
ing. Another house suffered the loss
of one luncheon date.
Houses in Groups
The houses are divided into two
groups. Following are those in the
first group: Alpha Kappa Lambda,
Alpha Tau Omega, Beta Theta Pi,
Delta Upsilon, Kappa Alpha, Kappa
Sigma, Phi Gamma Delta, Phi Kappa
Psi, Sigma Alpha Epsilon, Sigma Chi,
Zeta Psi.
Those in group two are: Alpha
Delta Phi, Alpha Sigma Phi, Chi Psi,
Delta Kappa Epsilon, Delta Tau
Delta, Phi Kappa Sigma, Phi Sigma
Kappa, Phi Delta Theta, Sigma Nu,
Theta Chi, Theta Delta Chi, Theta Xi.
