# Run Off Trials for Track Meet With Bear Freshmen
## 
### 
Trials for the Stanford-California
freshman track and field meet, set for
Friday, April 13, were partly run off
on the Old Varsity Track last Friday.
Will Tl. Ellington, assistant track
coach, announced last night that no
results of the qualification would be
made public until after the final heats
in the 440 and half-mile run on Tues
day.
