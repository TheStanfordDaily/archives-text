# TO OPEN MEN'S GLEE CLUB TRYOUTS TODAY
## 
### 
Tryouts for the men's glee club will
be held at 4:15 o'clock today and to
morrow in Room 260, Engineer Build
ing, under the supervision of C. Al
bert Kulmann, director.
Previous experience in group sing
ing is not necessary to become a can
didate for Glee Club membership.
Those who pass the voice test, will be
given vocal instruction and training
for chorus work.
The club gave a concert at the
California Theatre in San Jose yes
terday, and future appearances in
clude a full day of engagements in
the vicinity of Los Gatos on April 25,
and the home concert to be given in
the Assembly Hall on April 27.
