# U.S.C. Court Leader Most Popular "Leap Week' Man
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April B.— (P.1.P.)
—Jack Bruner, captain of the Univer
sity of Southern California basketball
team, proved his popularity with Tro
jan women during "Leap Week." Six
nights and six dates was his record,
and he was awarded a silver cup for
his perfect score. The men on the
campus are beginning to realize that
athletic hero stuff really means some
thing after all.

LOCKOUT THREATENED
BERLIN, April 8— (U.P.)— Lockout
Thursday of 300,000 Saxony metal
workers was threatened today unless
a wage agreement is reached.
