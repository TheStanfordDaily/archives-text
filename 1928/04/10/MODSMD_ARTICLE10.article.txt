# Berkeley, San Jose to See 'Deep River' by Pendragons
## 
### 
"Deep River," negro war play, present
ed in Palo Alto last quarter by the Pen
dragon Players, is to be given by practi
cally the same cast in the Berkeley
Playhouse tomorrow and at the San Jose
State Teachers College on April 18.
James A. Quimby will replace Professor
Everett P. Lesley of the original cast as
Samba Sarr, a Senegalese soldier.
