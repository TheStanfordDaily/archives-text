# DOWNING WILL SPEAK AT ENGINEER SMOKER
## 
### 
Paul M. Downing, '95, president of the
Stanford Alumni Association and vice
president of the Pacific Gas and Electric
Company, will speak at the engineers' an
nual smoker, which is given under the
auspices of the Stanford branches of the
American Society of Civil Engineers, and
the Geological and Mining Society of
American Universities, tomorrow night
at 7 :30 o'clock in the Old Union.

The undertaking is sponsored by the
recently formed Engineering Council, the
committee of which consists of L. S.
Tuttle, A. P. Maradudin, and T. L. Lcn
rcn.
