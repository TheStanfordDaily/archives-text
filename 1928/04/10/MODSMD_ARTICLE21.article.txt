# BIG MEET TICKETS GO ON SALE TODAY AT A.S.S.U. OFFICE
## 
### 
Tickets to the Big Meet for
alumni and stadium subscribers
were mailed from the graduate
manager's office yesterday. Root
ers' tickets will be on sale to
day, tomorrow, and Thursday,
from 1 o'clock until 5 o'clock, at
the A.S.S.U. office in the Old
Union. These tickets will cost
$1.00.
General admission tickets,
which will sell for $1.50, will go
on sale at Wideman's and Roos
Brothers tomorrow. Roos Broth
ers San Francisco and San Jose
stores will also handle tickets.
All seats will be reserved. The
meet will be held in the Stanford
stadium April 21.
