# MOOT COURT SESSION TO BE HEARD TUESDAY
## 
### 
The semi-final round of the Moot
Court sessions for the $200 Sloss
award will be heard Tuesday night,
April 17, according to George Crocker,
member Moot Court board. The final
event will take place one week later.
Four partnerships are now left in
the competition, H. Brown-Brunner,
L. Brown-Lloyd, Petersen-Kaseberg,
Brownstone-Long.
