# Hayward Again Selected For Olympic Team Coach
## 
### 
Bill Hayward, track coach at Univer
sity of Oregon, has again been selected
from a large list of coaches as one of the
trainers for the United States Olympic
team. Coach Hayward has not yet fully
decided whether or not he will be able to
accept the position this year. If the Ore
gon entries are successful in the final
tryouts held at Boston, he will undoubt
edly make the trip.
