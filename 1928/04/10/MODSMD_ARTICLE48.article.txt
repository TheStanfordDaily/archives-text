# Texas Faculty Contributes Articles to Campus Paper
## 
### 
AUSTIN, Texas, April 9.—(P.1.P.)—
"The Daily Texan," published by the
students of the University of Texas, is
inviting the members of the faculty to
contribute articles to its columns regard
ing the work in their departments. The
series has begun with an editorial by Dr.
H. J. Benedict, president, on the functions
of a university. All major subjects will
be included in the series.

Sentence was suspended on a Chicago
man for reckless driving because he was
on his honeymoon.
