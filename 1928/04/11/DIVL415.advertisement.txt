Safeguarding Valuables
All the skill of the person that is clever in devising ways to
hide things avails little against the danger of fire and the
operations of thieves. burglary hold no terrors for
those that keep their personal treasures in an American
Trust Company safe deposit vault. The expense is very
small. A good-sized box may be rented for four dollars a
year, and it will protect a fortune in securities and other
evidence of worldly possessions.
