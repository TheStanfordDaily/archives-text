# U.C. Junior Informal Bids Are Placed on Sale Today
## 
### 
Tickets for the Junior Spring In
formal of the University of California
to be held at the Palace Hotel, San
Francisco, after the Stanford-U.C.
track meet, April 21, go on sale for
Stanford students at the A.S.S.U. of
fice in the Old Union today. Bids will
be priced at $8.50, and include punch,
cover charge, and souvenir. Two or
chestras will furnish music, and three
ballrooms will be used for dancing.
