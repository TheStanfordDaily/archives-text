# REGENTS STOP MOVING U.C.L.A. TO NEW SITE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 10. —
(U.P.) —Budget provisions for mov
ing the University of California at
Los Angeles from its present site on
Vermont Avenue to the new campus
at Westwood were not approved by
the regents of the university at their
regular monthly meeting here today.

Pending final decision, expected to
be made at the next meeting of the
regents, provisions predicted upon
moving have been held up.

The remainder of the budget for
the academic year of 1928-29 amount
ing to $10,107,298.47 was approved by
President W. W. Campbell.
