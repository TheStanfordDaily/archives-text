# MEN'S COUNCIL FINES TRAFFIC INFRACTIONS
## 
### 
Continuing its policy of strict en
forcement of campus traffic regula
tions, Men's Council passed judgment
on nine violations heard at the meet
ing yesterday.
Fines and punishment are as fol
lows:
Three cases of illegal parking, $1
each; one arterial stop violation, $1;
one open muffler, $1.
One speeding case, $15; one case $5
and twenty hours' work at Convale
scent Home; one case $10 and twenty
hours' work at Home. One case of
speeding, second offense, revocation
of driving privilege for one quarter.
One case of Honor System violation
continued until next meeting.
