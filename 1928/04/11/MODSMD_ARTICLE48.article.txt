# LADY BAILEY CRASHES AT TABORA, IS UNHURT
## 
### 
CAPETOWN, South Africa, April
10.—(U.P.)—Sir Abe Bailey, diamond
magnate, today received a telegram
from Lady Bailey that she had
crashed at Tabora but was unhurt.
She is flying from London to Cape
town. Her plane, she said, was dam
aged.
"Frailty, thy name is woman," said
Shakespeare once, but he never tried to
feed a co-ed, states the Daily Kansan.
