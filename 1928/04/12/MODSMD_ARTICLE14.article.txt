# CONCLUDING SALE OF BIG MEET ROOTERS' TICKETS SET TODAY
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stanford-
California track meet go on sale for
(he last time today at the A.S.S.U.
office in the Old Union from 1 until
5 o'clock. The tickets are a dollar.
Tickets for alumni and stadium sub
scribers were mailed from the Gradu
ate Manager's office Tuesday. Gen
eral admission seats are available at
Wideman's and Roos Brothers in Palo
Alto for $1.50. Roos Brothers in San
Jose and San Francisco will also offer
the tickets. All seats for the contest,
which will be held in the Stanford
Stadium on April 21, will be reserved.
