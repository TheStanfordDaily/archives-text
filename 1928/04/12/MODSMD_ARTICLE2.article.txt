# MAJOR LEAGUES OPEN 1928 BALL CAMPAIGN
## 
### 
NEW YORK, April 11—(U.P.) —
Baseball burst into bloom in eight
major league cities today.
The 1928 pennant races are under
-way without the scratching of a single
■entry due to the blustery April
weather which prevailed in the East,
with unparalleled cold at Boston, New
York, Brooklyn, and Philadelphia.
