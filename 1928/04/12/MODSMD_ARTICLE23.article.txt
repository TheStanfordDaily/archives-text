# HOLD TRIALS TONIGHT FOR WOMEN'S DEBATE
## 
### 
"Resolved, That American investors
and investments should he protected only
by the government of the country in
which the investment is made," will he
the question used for the women's S.C.-
Stanford debate trials tonight at 7 o'clock
in the Little Theatre.
For those who cannot come at this time
a second tryout will he held Friday after
noon at 1 o'clock in the Little Theatre.
This question will be the topic for the
debate with Southern California, the first
to be held between Stanford women and
a southern college. Stanford will he
host to the visiting debaters in the first
argument on May 17, and the second will
be the following day at Southern Cali
fornia.
