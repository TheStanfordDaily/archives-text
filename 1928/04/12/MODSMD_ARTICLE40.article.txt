# WALKAWAY CONTESTS CLOSE SECOND ROUND OF MURAL BASEBALL
## Phi Kaps Beat Phi Delts in Five-Inning Game by 9 to 5 Score 
### 
Three walkaway games, two of them
by more than ten runs, featured the
third day's contests of the intramural
baseball tourney. Rain halted two
games in the sixth inning and the
third in the fifth.
Evans, Phi Kappa Sigma, held Phi
Delta Theta to five hits when they
defeated them 9 to 5 in a five-inning
game. Phi Kappa Sigma connected
with Frost, Phi Delt pitcher, for four
hits and as many runs in the second
inning. The Phi Delta rallied in the
fourth inning and brought in three
runs but three strikeouts retired the
side.
