# Speaker's Illness Causes "Y" Meet to Be Cancelled
## 
### 
Because of the illness of J. Stitt
Wilson, the principal speaker, the an
nual dinner of the Y.M.C.A. planned
for last night was indefinitely post
poned.
The election of officers for the en
suing year was held as planned, and
the following were elected: Morden
Brown, president; Stewart Way, vice
president; Larry Sclimitt, secretary
treasurer.
