# SENATE AMENDMENT TO FARM BILL MADE
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, DC., April 11. —
(U.P.) —The Senate late today adopted
an amendment to the McNary-llaugen
farm relief hill, raising the appropriation
for the revolving fund from $290,000,000
to $400,000,000. The vote was 42 to 30
and in the face of a new warning that the
bill was not acccptable to President
Coolidge.
