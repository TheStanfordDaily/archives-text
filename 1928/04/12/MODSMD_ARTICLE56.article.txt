# Gene Tunney Signs Tex's Exclusive Fight Contract
## 
### 
MIAMI BEACH, Fla., April 11.—
(U.P.) —Gene Tunney admitted today that
he has signed a contract with Tex Rick
ard, giving the promoter exclusive right
to the heavyweight champion's services
in 1929.
"Of course, if I'm not champion after
the next fight, it doesn't count," explained
Gene. He said he had agreed to fight no
more than once this year.
