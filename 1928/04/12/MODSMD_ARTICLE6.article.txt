# CONCERT TO BE GIVEN BY SYMPHONY TODAY
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony Or
chestra, which gave the final concert
of the A.S.S.U. Series last night, will
appear in a popular concert in the
Pavilion this afternoon at 3 o'clock.
Tickets, which are fifty cents, are on
sale at Crandall's in Palo Alto.
The orchestra, led by Alfred Hertz,
will play the following program:
Demonstration of wood wind instru
ments (flute, oboe, clarinet, and
bassoon).
Unfinished Symphony Schubert
Peer Gynt Suite Grieg
Morning; Death of Asa; Anitra's
Dance; In the Hall of the
Mountain King.
Beauty and the Beast Ravel
In the Village Ippolitof Ivanof
Ilide of the Valkyrie Wagner
The Magic Fire Spell Wagner
