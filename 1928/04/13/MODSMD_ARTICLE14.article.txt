# FURTHER TRYOUTS FOR WOMEN'S DEBATE HELD
## 
### 
Further tryouts for the women's
U.S.C.-Stanford debate will be held at
1 o'clock this afternoon in the Little
Theatre. A three- to five-minute
speech on the question, "Resolved:
That American investors and invest
ments should be protected only by
the government in which the invest
ment is made," will be used as a basis
of trial. This same topic will be the
subject of the debate with Southern
California May 17 and 18.
Announcement of the chosen foren
sic squad will be made in Monday's
Daily.
