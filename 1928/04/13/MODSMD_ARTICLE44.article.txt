# HERRE MADE CURATOR OF ZOOLOGY MUSEUM
## 
### 
Dr. Albert C. Herre was recently
appointed curator of the Stanford
Zoological Museum. Dr. Herre has
been director of fisheries in the Philip
pines for nearly eight years, and has
been carrying on a study of fishes
of the tropical Pacific, especially
those of Japan, Samoa, and East
India.
The new curator was an instructor
at Stanford a number of years ago.
