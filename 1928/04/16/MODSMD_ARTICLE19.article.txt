# Hollywood Bowl Will Be Used for Commencement
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 15.— (P.1.P.)
— Commencement exercises at U.C.
L.A. will be held in the Hollywood
Bowl this year. Lack of room in the
present location of the school was the
cause of the decision. The new audi
torium at Westwood will be used in
the future.
