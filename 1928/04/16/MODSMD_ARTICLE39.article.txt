# WEDDING OF FONTAINE SURPRISE TO FRIENDS
## 
### 
Announcement is made by Mr. and
Mrs. W. W. Wasson of Palo Alto of
the marriage of their daughter, Lorin
Dolores, to George Kingdon Fontaine,
'27, of San Jose. The ceremony took
place on Saturday, March 17, at Reno,
Nevada, with Reverend Moll Case
officiating. News of the wedding
comes as a suprise to the many
lriends of the couple on the peninsula,
and at Stanford.
