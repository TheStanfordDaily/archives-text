# RUSHING CARD ORDERS MUST BE FILED TODAY
## 
### 
Orders and checks for second period
rushing invitations must be turned in
at the Old Union this morning by 9
o'clock, according to the announce
ment Friday night by Willis Wolfe,
president of the Interfraternity Coun
cil.

The cards are distributed at a cost
of three cents apiece, which includes
postage to the freshmen and for re
turn.
