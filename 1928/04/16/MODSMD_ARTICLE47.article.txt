# TICKET SALE FOR U.C. INFORMAL ENDS TODAY
## 
### 
Ticket sale for the U.C. Junior
Spring Informal, to be given at the
Palace Hotel on April 21 following
the Big Meet, will close this afternoon
at 5:00 o'clock. Tickets may be ob
tained all day in the Old Union at
$3.50 per couple.
Both Bill Jones' Country Club or
chestra and the regular Palace Hotel
orchestra will furnish the music.
Punch, cover charge, and souvenirs
are included with the tickets.
