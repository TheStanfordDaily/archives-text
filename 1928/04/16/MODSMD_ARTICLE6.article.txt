# Italian Dirigible in Danger; Fighting Gale Over Europe
## 
### 
BERLIN, April 15.—The Italian
north polar dirigible Italia was fight
ing a desperate battle for life in a
thunderstorm tonight as she felt her
way half blindly over Upper Silesia
on her way to Stolp, on the German
coast, from Milan.
