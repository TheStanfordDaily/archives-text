# Cast Selected for Spanish Play 'El Dia De San Roque'
## 
### 
Final selections of the cast for tlic
Spanish Club play "El Dia de San Roqne"
were made at a meeting of the club last
night. Rehearsals will begin at once and
the play will be presented in the Little
Theatre April 24.
Alice Rose, Raymond Carlson, and
Stanford Steinbeck are the co-authors of
the sketch.
