# U.C. SERIES TICKETS ON SALE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Sale of rooters' tickets for the last
of the baseball series with California,
to be played on the Stanford diamond
Saturday, will take place tomorrow
afternoon in the Old Union from 1 to
5 o'clock. This will be the one and
only chance to obtain rooters' tickets,
as none will be sold at the gate.
Rooters' tickets will sell for thirty
five cents, and general admission will
be seventy-five cents.
