# KEIO UNIVERSITY BALL TEAM WILL TOUR U.S.
## 
### 
BERKELEY, April 15.—(P.1.P.) —
The Keio University baseball team,
intercollegiate champions of Japan,
arrived in San Francisco last week
from the Orient via Honolulu, and
will open their tour of American uni
versities the Bears, whom
they will play here April 18 and 23.
The co-operative bookstore at the
University of Wisconsin has been
proclaimed illegal.
