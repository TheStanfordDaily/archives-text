# CAPTAIN SPENCER WILL SPEAK OVER RADIO KPO
## 
### 
Emerson "Bud" Spencer, captain
and crack quarter-miler of the Stan
ford varsity track team, will talk over
the radio from Station KPO between
7:15 and 7:30 o'clock tomorrow night.

Spencer will discuss the track pros
pects of the Stanford team for the re
mainder of the season with particular
reference to the Stanford-California
annual Big Meet Saturday. This is to
be Spencer's initial talk before the
microphone.
