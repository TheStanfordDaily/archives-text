# U.C. BASEBALL GAME TICKETS SOLD TODAY
## 
### 
"Absolutely no rooters' tickets for
the California baseball game will be
sold after this afternoon," stated Jake
Irwin, ticket manager. The ticket sale
will take place this afternoon from 1
to 5 o'clock in the Old Union, and
will be the only chance to secure
rooters' tickets for the last of the
Bear series, to be played on the Stan
ford diamond Saturday. Rooters'
tickets will be sold, on presentation
of student body card, for thirty-five
cents. General admission will be sev
enty-five cents.
