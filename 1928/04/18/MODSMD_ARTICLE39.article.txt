# ENGLISH CLUB TO HEAR FRIED, S.F. REVIEWER
## 
### 
Alexander Fried, featured music
critic of the San Francisco Chronicle,
will address the English Club, who
will be guests of Miss Mirrielees at
534 Lasuen tonight promptly at 8
o'clock.
Problems, difficulties, and joys of
reviewing will be discussed by Fried
from the viewpoint of the writer. Be
sides being a critic, Fried is an au
thority on music. His column, "The
Sounding Board," appears in the
Chronicle every Sunday, and he is a
frequent contributor to national
periodicals. This will be his first visit
to the Stanford campus, according to
Herbert Klein, president.
