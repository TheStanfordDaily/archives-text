# BAND PROGRAM WILL BE GIVEN AT THEATRE
## 
### 
Appearance of the Stanford Band and
of the varsity track stars will be a feature
of Varsity Night which will be observed
at the New Stanford Theater tonight. A
concert by the band will be added to the
regular program of feature picture and
news-reels. The Rally Committee and
band officials have co-operated with the
theater management to make the pro
gram possible.
