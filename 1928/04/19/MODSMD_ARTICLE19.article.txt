# CAPTAINS FOR WOMEN'S TRACK TEAMS ELECTED
## 
###

sulted in the following captains having
been chosen : senior, Etna Demon ; junior,
I Marion Klockson; sophomore, Harriet
Sutton; freshman, Ruth Clawson. The
j senior and freshman classes voted for
I captains yesterday, and the other two
I were elected Monday on a white ballot.
