# E. A. Cottrell Is Present At San Francisco Hearing
## 
### 
Professor Edwin A. Cottrell of the
Political Science Department was
present and spoke at the hearing held
in San Francisco Tuesday night,
April 17, for the citizens of the city
who are interested in the revision of
the San Francisco charter. Tnese
hearings are being conducted by the
Supervisor's Judiciary Committee.
Three plans are under discussion:
the city manager plan, the commis
sion plan, and a plan for the amend
ment of the existing charter. Profes-

sor Cottrell was present in the inter
est of the city manager plan.
