# ALPHA TAU OMEGA, EL TORO, BREAKERS WIN MURAL BALL GAMES
## A.T.O. Conflict With Theta Chi Nine Best of Day's League Contests 
### 
Breakers, El Toro, and Alpha Tau
Omega won their contests in the in
tramural baseball tournament yester
day afternoon. El Cuadro won over
Zeta Psi by default. Teams are get
ting into form now and fewer errors
and better hitting is shown.
Breakers defeated El Campo in the
first game of yesterday's play by a
11 to 0 score Avhen Perry Taylor al
lowed the El Campo hitters one bin
gle. The Breakers hit George Evans
freely scoring four runs in the open
ing frame. Many of the Breaker runs
were scored on El Campo errors,
which were numerous.
El Toro defeated Alpha Kappa
Lambda 8 to 5 in the second contest.
Holding the lead until the fourth in
ning, Alpha Kappa Lambda was de
feated when El Toro ran up nine
runs in the last three innings. Gal
braith, El Toro pitcher, allowed only
five hits, while his teammates were
collecting seven. Fourteen batters
struck out before the club twirler.
