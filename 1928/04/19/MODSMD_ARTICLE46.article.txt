# Freshman Y.W.C.A. Cabinet Takes on New Duty Today
## 
### 
Freshman Y.W.C.A. Cabinet has taken
on as a new duty the caring for the chil
dren of the members of the Stanford
Dames while the mothers are attending
the meetings of the club in the Women's
Clubhouse. A schedule has been arranged
by which there will he at least two women
in charge of the children during the meet
ing hours. The Y.W.C.A. Cabinet will
function for the first time in the new
duly this afternoon at 3 o'clock when the
Stanford Dames will meet in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
