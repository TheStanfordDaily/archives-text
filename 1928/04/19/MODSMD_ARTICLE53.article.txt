# EUPHRONIA WILL HOLD MEMBERSHIP TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Quarterly tryouts for membership in
Euphronia, men's debating society, will
be conducted at the regular meeting of
the club in tire lobby of the Old Union
tonight at 7:15 o'clock, announces Presi
dent Leonard Kirkpatrick.
Each tryoutee is to oppose or defend
some presidential candidate with a five
minute speech. The meeting will be run
through as quickly as possible in order
that the members may attend the fresh
man debate.
