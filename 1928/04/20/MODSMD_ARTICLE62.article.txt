# Oregon State College May Enter Crew in 1929 Races
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Oregon, April 19. — '
(P.I.P.) —With a new shell on the
water and another in prospect, Oregon
State College may enter a crew in the
regattas held next year. The nucleus
of the crew will be the members of
the Oregon State Rowing Club, com
posed of students who organized in
absence of a varsity crew.
