# GIBBS WILL CAPTAIN 1928 SOCER TEAM
## 
### 
At the annual soccer banquet held
in the Union yesterday, Allison J.
Gibbs was chosen captain of the
1928 eleven. Gibbs is a two-year
varsity letterman and plays
wing position. He is a member of
Delta Chi fraternity.
Herbert M. Holmes and Robert
P. Reynolds were chosen the most
valuable players 011 the 1927 varsity
and freshman teams respectively.
Holmes held down left fullback po
sition on the varsity while Reynolds
captained and played center half
back 011 the frosli aggregation.
