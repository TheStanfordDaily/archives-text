# Book Reviews Are Given at Stanford Dames' Meeting
## 
### 
Stanford Dames held a regular meet
ing yesterday at 3 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. Two book reviews and sev
eral piano selections made up the enter
tainment program. After the business
meeting there was a social meeting and
tea was served. The Freshman Cabinet
of the Y.W.C.A., under the direction of
Katherine Keho, took care of the chil
dren while the mothers attended the
meeting.
