# W. R. MILES RETURNS FROM PSYCHOLOGICAL CONFERENCE IN EAST
## 
### 
Professor Walter R. Miles of the
Psychology department has just re
turned from an extended visit in the
East. He attended the meeting of the
National Research Council of Wash
ington, D,C., which was held in Car
lisle, Pennsylvania, March 29-31. Car
lisle is the city where Pop Warner
wus once football coach before he
came to Stanford. Professor Miles
says the best rooms In the town hotel
were given him "because he was a
friend of Pop Warner's." The con
ference included about thirty psycho
logists from the representative larger
institutions of the country.
