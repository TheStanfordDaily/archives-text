# Student Directory To Be Placed on Sale Monday
## 
### 
Announcement from the Registrar's
Office states that the spring quarter
issue of the Directory of Officers and
Students, more familiarly known as
the Bawl-out, will be placed on sale
Monday. The usual price of fifteen
cents will prevail and copies may be
obtained in the Comptroller's Office
on the second floor of the Adminis
tration Building.
