# PLAYS WILL BE GIVEN BY SPANISH SOCIETIES
## 
### 
"El Dia de San Roque" will be
given by members at a meeting of
the Spanish Club tomorrow evening
at 8 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. "Las Solteronas," a short skit,
will be presented by Sigma Delta Pi,
Spanish honorary society.
The first play, which represents a
village in Spain at the time of the
annual festival in honor of the patron
saint, was written by students in the
Spanish department. The program is
open to the public.
