# MEETING OF Y.W.C.A. SENIOR CABINET HELD AT SHOUP RESIDENCE
## 
### 
A joint meeting of the present Sen
ior Cabinet of the Y.W.C.A. and the
newly appointed Cabinet for next year
was held yesterday at the home of
Paul Shonp at Los Altos. A picnic
luncheon was served at noon, and a
stunt presented by this year's Cabi
net. Plans for next year's program
were discussed, and a report was
made on the recent national conven
tion at Sacramento.
Sally Kinsman, vice-president, an
nounced that arrangements for the
establishment of a lending library
were under way. Decisions as to the
rates and types of books to be loaned
have not yet been made.
