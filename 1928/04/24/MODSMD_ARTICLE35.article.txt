# SPANISH CLUB GIVES TWO PLAYS TONIGHT
## 
### 
Two plays will comprise the pro
gram to be presented at a meeting of
(he Spanish Club in the Women's
Clubhouse at 8 o'clock tonight.
"El Dia de San Roque" will be
given by the following cast: Manuel
Espinosa, Stanford Steinbeck, Allison
Gibbs, Alice Rose, Raymond Carlson,
Lois White, John Reid, Blanch Bar
nett, Finley Gibbs, Andrew Cole, and
C. Rogers. The other part of the pro
gram will be a short skit, "Las Sol
teronas." The meeting will be open
to the public.
