# PHI DELTS, ENCINA 2E WIN DIAMOND CONTESTS IN THIRD ROUND START
## 
### 
Phi Delta Theta and Encina 2E won
in the opening play of third-round in
tramural baseball competition yester
day. Equally strong teams made yes
terday's scores the closest of the
tournament. Better fielding and pitch
ing has been the result of the three
weeks' play.
Phi Delta Theta defeated Encina 3E
in the best-played game of the two
weeks' play by a 2 to 1 score. A home
run by Perkus in the last of the ninth
with the score tied gave the fraternity
team a victory after the Encina team
had tied the score in the first of the
ninth. The only freshman hit of the
game was made by Mears who
stretched it into a run on an over
throw of the Phi Delt third-baseman.
Reynolds, Phi Delt hurler, struck out
fifteen Encina men.
