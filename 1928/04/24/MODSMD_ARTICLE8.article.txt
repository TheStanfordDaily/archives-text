# REINALD WERRENRATH WILL BE LAST ARTIST OF PENINSULA SERIES
## 
### 
In the last of the Peninsula Musical
Association concerts to be given this
year, Reinald Werrenwrath will sing
in the Assembly Hall Thursday eve
ning at 8:15 o'clock.
Werrenrath is a baritone of wide
fame, and is heralded as enjoying a
larger and more faithful following
than any of the non-operatic singers
with the exception of John McCor
mick. His songs from Kipling are
particularly popular.
Tickets for the concert may be ob
tained at Crandall's Bookstore, and
are priced at $1 and $1.50.
