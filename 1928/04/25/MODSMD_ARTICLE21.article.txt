# ESPERANTO STUDENTS MEET, PLAN PROGRAM
## 
### 
International Language students
met last night with the class of Red
wood City Night School under the
direction of E. B. Myers who teaches
Esperanto at Redwood City. The class
is using the direct conversational
method which is particularly adapted
to this composite language.
Exchange programs with other en
thusiasts are being planned. Next
week a special program will be given
by instructors and students of Esper
anto in Montezuma school.
