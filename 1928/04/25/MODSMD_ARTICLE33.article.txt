# German Club Sponsors Card Party in Clubhouse Tonight
## 
### 
German Club members will give a
card party tonight at 7:30 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Although
bridge will be the principal game,
other card games are to be provided.
All interested students, as well as
regular club members, are invited.
Those planning to attend are asked to
sign up on the list posted on the
bulletin board in the German depart
ment.
