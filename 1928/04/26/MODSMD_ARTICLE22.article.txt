# Smith Will Speak Before Stanford Advertising Club
## 
### 
A. Carmen Smith, president of the
Pacific Advertising Club Association,
will speak tonight before the Stan
ford Advertising Club of Palo Alto
in the banquet room of the Sunset
Cafeteria at 6:10 o'clock.
Smith will talk in general on what
an Advertising Club can do in a com
munity and on the advertising con
vention to be held in Hawaii the early
part of June.
In honor of A. Carmen Smith's visit
this will be ladies' night at the club.
It will be an open meeting and stu
dents who do not wish to come for
dinner may attend the address which
will start at 7 o'clock.
