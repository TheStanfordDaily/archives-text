# ROSS NICHOLS WILL CAPTAIN 1929 CARD TRACK, FIELD TEAM
## 
### 
Ross Nichols, high and low
hurdler, was yesterday elected
by the varsity track squad to
captain the 1929 Stanford team.
"Nick," a member of the Class
of 1928, was captain of his fresh
man team in the spring of 1925,
but did not compete on the var
sity in his sophomore year be
cause of an injury. He has won
every high hurdles race in which
he has participated this year,
wifh the exception of that in the
Olympic Club meet, when he fell
and lost out.
The captain-elect is a member
of Delta Tau Delta. His home is
in Huntington Beach.
