# DAILY WILL CONTINUE SUMMER PUBLICATION
## 
### 
A Stanford Daily that comes out
twice a week will be the student
newspaper and workshop for students
in journalism during the Summer
Quarter.
Responsibility for the publication of
the Daily will be in the hands of the
student staff under the supervision
of the instructors of journalism.
