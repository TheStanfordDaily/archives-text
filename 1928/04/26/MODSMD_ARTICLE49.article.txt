# STUDENTS TO SPEAK FRENCH. SPANISH. AT TABLE NEXT QUARTER
## 
### 
French and Spanish conversation
will be fostered this summer for stu
dents and teachers of the two lan
guages by the establishment of spe
cial tables in the Stanford Union.
Conversation in Spanish will be di
rected by a native of Spain, and the
French table will be presided over
by Mademoiselle Suzanne Requet, a
French woman and member of the
University staff of France.

Since the number of these tables
will be necessarily limited, John A.
Sellards, Summer Quarter Director,
urges students desiring to take ad
vantage of this opportunity to notify
the office of Director of Summer Quar
ter, Room 173.
