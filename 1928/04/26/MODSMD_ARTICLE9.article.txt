# Dr. J. P. Smith Is Awarded Mary C. Thompson Medal
## 
### 
For "the most important services to
geology and paleontology" during the
year, Dr. J. P. Smith, professor of pale
ontology in the Stanford Department of
Geology, has been awarded the Mary
Clark Thompson medal by the National
Academy of Sciences, meeting in Wash
ington, D.C., according to word received
on the campus yesterday.
These recogniticns of scholarly achieve
ment by the National Academy are
among the most highly valued by scien
tists. The Thompson medal carries with
it an honorarium of $250.
