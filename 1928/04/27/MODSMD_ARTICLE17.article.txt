# Rushing Violation Charges Must Be Submitted Early
## 
### 
Charges of rushing infractions must
be submitted to the Interfraternity
Council Board of Control within
twenty-four hours after the violation
is discovered.
Thist announcement was made last
night by Willis Wolfe, president of the
Interfraternity Council. Wolfe empha
sized the point that violations will
not be considered if they are more
than a day old.
