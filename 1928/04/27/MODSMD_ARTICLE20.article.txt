# WOMEN TO INDICATE GYMNASIUM UNIFORM PREFERENCE IN VOTE
## 
### 
Women's preference between two
new gymnasium uniforms designed
by the gym department is being de
termined by a general vote which be
gan yesterday among the physical
education classes. Results of the vot
ing will be submitted to the W.A.A.
board Tuesday for final action.
The proposed uniforms are an im
provement over the ones now in use
in that they are of lighter weight, per
mit greater freedom of movement,
and present a trimmer appearance.
Both are two-piece suits, consisting
of a negligee shirt and closely cut
knickers of light tan soisette. Tan
elkskin moccasin shoes, tan stock
ings, and a tan sweater jacket com
plete the outfit.
