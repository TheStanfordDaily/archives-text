# MORRIS TAYLOR WEDS ELIZABETH M. BRAND
## 
### 
The marriage of Morris Taylor, '25,
to Miss Elizabeth Morgan Brand, will
take place tomorrow at noon in the
Memorial Church.
Taylor is the son of Dr. Alonzo E.
Taylor, director of the Food Research
Institute, and is n graduate of the
Department of Mechanical Engineer
ing. Miss Brand has been secretary
to Dr. Taylor in the Food Research
Institute.
