# REPORT U.S. MISSIONARY DEAD
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. April 26.—(TT.P.)—
Unofficial reports on the slaying of
Dr. Seymour, head of the Presby
terian hospital at Tsining, China,
were received by the State Depart
ment today in messages relayed
through Mrs. Charles Ames, wife of
a missionary at Isining, and the
American consul at Isinanfu.
