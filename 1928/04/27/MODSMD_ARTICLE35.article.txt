# RUSSIAN CHOIR SINGS SUNDAY IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
The Russian Cathedral Choir will
give a concert of secular music the
evening of Sunday, April 29, at the
Women's Clubhouse in Palo Alto.
The proceeds from the recital will
go to a fund for destitute Russians.
Tickets for the affair are one dollar,
and may be obtained at the door.
