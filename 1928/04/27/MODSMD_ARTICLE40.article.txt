# FLEET ARRIVES IN HAWAII
## 
### 
HONOLULU, April 26.— (U.P.) —
The battleship Texas arrived at Pearl
Harbor today and will be followed
Saturday by the remainder of the
U.S. battle fleet, which has been en
gaged in maneuvers while en route
from San Francisco.

PESCARA, Italy, April 26.—( U.P.)
—A landslide in the commune of
Civitella Casanova today swept away
twelve buildings. Panic-stricken in
habitants of the district abandoned
other endangered buildings. There
were no victims.
