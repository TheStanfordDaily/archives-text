# Garden Setting To Be Used At Encina Informal Dance
## 
### 
A garden setting will be the theme
of the decorations at the Encina In
formal sport dance which will be held
on May 18. Udell C. Walk will have
charge of the decorations. Programs
in keeping with the occasion have
already been ordered from San Fran
cisco. The dance committee requests
any suggestions to make a bigger and
better affair.
The ticket sale will open Wednes
day, May 3.
