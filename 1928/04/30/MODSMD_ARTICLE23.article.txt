# EARTHQUAKE SHOCKS CONTINUE IN GREECE
## 
### 
ATHENS, April 29. — (U.P.) —
Strong earthquake shocks continued
today in Corinth and lesser shocks
were felt in Western Thrace and
Western Pelophonnesus. A moderate
shock was felt here at 11:40 A.M.,
Saturday.
The Council of Ministers has decid
ed to invite tenders for the construc
tion of 1,000 huts in Corinth and 400
in other districts to care temporarily
for homeless people. A commission
will be formed to study the problem
of reconstructing Corinth.
