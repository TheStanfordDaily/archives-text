# Charley Paddock Sets New Record in 175-Yard Sprint
## 
### 
PHILADELPHIA, April 29.— (TT.P.)
—Fourteen years of wear and tear
have not wrecked the mechanism of
the "world's fastest human."
Charley Paddock's dashing feet car
ried him to one of the most remark
able of his many world's track rec
ords in a special 175-yard exhibition
sprint race at the thirty-fourth annual
University of Pennsylvania Relay
Carnival Saturday.
