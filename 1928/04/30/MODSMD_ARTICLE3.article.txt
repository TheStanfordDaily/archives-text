# FINE FOUR WOMEN $91 FOR SPEED VIOLATIONS
## 
### 
Women's Council imposed fines for
speeding totalling $91 upon four cam
pus women at the regular meeting
Thursday. All four arrests were made
for violations of the speed limit at
intersections along Palm Drive.
Fines included $20 for going 35 in
a 15-mile zone; $15 for going 35 in a
20-mile zone; $21 for going 36 in a
15-mile zone; and $27 for going 42
in a 15-mile zone.
