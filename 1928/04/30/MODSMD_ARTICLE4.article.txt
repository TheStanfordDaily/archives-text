# Irene Way, '29, Injured by Fall From Horse Saturday
## 
### 
Irene Way, '29, was taken to the
Palo Alto Hospital Saturday after
noon in an unconscious state after
falling from her horse while attempt
ing a jump in the riding hal! of the
Military Department.
Miss Way recovered consciousness
in a few hours and was found to have
only a slight concussion of the brain
besides minor bruises. She is recover
ing rapidly.
