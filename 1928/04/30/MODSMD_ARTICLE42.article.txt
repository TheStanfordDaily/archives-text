# Varsity Poloists to Play O.S.C. on Oregon Campus
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Ore., April 29. —
(P.I.P.) —The O.S.C. polo team will
have plenty of competition this year.
Invitations have been received from
the Vancouver Barracks, University
of Utah, and Boise, Idaho, to play on
various week ends in April and May.
Stanford is willing to play at Oregon
State on campus week end, May 17-19.

One thousand tickets have been
sold for the 1928-29 concert series at
the University of Minnesota.
