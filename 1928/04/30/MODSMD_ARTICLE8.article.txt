# GEOLOGY STUDENTS GO TO REDDING TO STUDY
## 
### 
Twenty-one Stanford geology stu
dents, in charge of Professor Eliot
Blackwelder, of the Geology depart
ment, left the campus Saturday for
Redding where they will study geo
logical formations.
The party made a trip the same
morning to Stinking Canyon, near
Bellavista, where oil indications are
prominent. They plan to return to the
campus this evening.
