# SCHOOLS WILL ENTER ORATORICAL CONTEST
## 
### 
Representatives from more than
five of the leading high schools of
the peninsula will take part In the
Peninsula Debating League's annual
oratorical contest to be held in the
Little Theatre at 8 o'clock Thursday
evening under the auspices of Eu
phronia, Stanford men's debating so
ciety.
Among the high schools who will
participate are Redwood City, Palo
Alto, Mountain View, Santa Cruz, and
Los Gatos. Members of Euphronia
will judge the debate, which will be
open to the public.
