# TOYON MUSICALE WILL BE GIVEN NEXT SUNDAY
## 
### 
Musical talent of the first rank will
be represented at the fourth annual
musicale sponsored by Toyon Club on
Sunday, May 13. The program, which
will be given in the lobby of the
dormitory, will be open to the public.
Artists appearing at the musicale
will include Marie de Forest Emry,
Palo Alto soprano, and the Victor
Lichtenstein String Quartet, com
posed of members of the San Fran
cisco Symphony Orchestra. This will
be the first Toyon Musicale which
has San Francisco musicians on the
program.
