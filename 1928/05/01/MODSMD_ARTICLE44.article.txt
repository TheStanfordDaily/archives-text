# MORE TREMORS SHAKE CORINTH, OTHER GREEK TOWNS; DAMAGE SLIGHT
## 
### 
ATHENS, April 30.—(U.P.) —Light
earthquakes continued today in Corinth
and nearby villages. The damage was
negligible.
Hundreds of houses in many villages in
the vicinity of Corinth were razed in
Sunday's strong earthquake shocks. Some
casualties also are reported.
The panic-stricken inhabitants slept
throughout last night in the open, fearing
recurrence of the shocks.
ROME, April 30.—(U.P.)—Pope Pius
has contributed $17,500 for relief in the
earthquake areas of Bulgaria, Greece,
and Smyrna, it was announced today.
