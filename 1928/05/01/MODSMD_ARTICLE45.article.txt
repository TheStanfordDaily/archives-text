# Freshmen Asked to Attend Toyon Club Smoker Tonight
## 
### 
All freshmen are invited to attend
a smoker which the Toyon Club will
give this evening at 7:15 o'clock. The
committee in charge promises "some
thing unusual" in the way of enter
tainment and "eats." A1 Roth, comp
troller of the University, will be the
principal speaker, and several mem
bers of the glee club will give some
