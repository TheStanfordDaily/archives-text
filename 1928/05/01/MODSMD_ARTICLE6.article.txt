# Y.W.C.A. CONVENTION DELEGATES TO SPEAK AT MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
Shizu Ishikawa and Leslie Blanch
ard, delegates to the recent Y.W.C.A.
National Convention at Sacramento,
will speak before an open meeting of
the Y.W.C.A. at 7:15 o'clock tonight
in the Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Ishikawa, who is a member of
the National Committee of Japan,
may take as her topic the student
work of the association in Japan in
which she is greatly interested. This
is her first trip to the United States.
Miss Blanchard, a Stanford graduate
of the Class of '12, plans to talk on
the significance of the new purpose
adopted a short while ago by the
Y.W.C.A.
Miss Blanchard and Miss Ishikawa
will stay two days on the campus.
Mrs. Elbert E. Smith is entertaining
them and the Senior Cabinet at din
ner this evening at her home at 740
Santa Ynez.
