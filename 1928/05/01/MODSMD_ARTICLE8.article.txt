# Harpist, Soprano, Pianist Present Old Classics In Concert Tonight
## Program Will Include Two Selections by Debussy, Schubert Songs 
### 
"The Volga Boatman," composi
tions by Schubert, Beethoven, De
bussy, Brahms, and others will be in
cluded in a concert to be given at
7: UO o'clock tonight in the Little
Theatre by Ruth Lorraine Close,
harpist, Gertrude Weidemann, so
prano, and Margo Hughes, pianist.
Mrs. Close, who graduated from
Stanford in 1915, is solo harpist of
the Portland Symphony Orchestra
and head of the harp department of
the Ellison White Conservatory of
Music. Gertrude Weidemann is a stu
dent of the "Hochschule fiir Musik"
of Berlin, and Margo Hughes is the
accompanist of the Wind Instrument
Ensemble of San Francisco.
Following is the program for the
evening:
Minuet Beethoven
Berceuse Hasselmans
Autumn Thomas
Ruth Lorraine Close
Geheimnis Schubert
Gretchen and Spinnrade .... Schubert
Lachen und Weinen Schubert
Auf dem Wasser su Singer....Schubert
Gertrude Weidemann
La Priere Hasselmans
Volga Boatman Folk Song
Marguerite at the Spinning Wheel
Zabel
Ruth Lorraine Close

Auf dem Kirchofe Brahms
Vergebliches Standchen Brahms
Song of the Open La Forge
Gertrude W'eidemann
