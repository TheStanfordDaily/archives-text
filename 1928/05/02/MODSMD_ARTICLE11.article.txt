# Men's Council Levies Fines On Traffic Rule Violators
## 
### 
Fines totalling $53 were levied by
the Men's Council yesterday after
noon for offenses against the Univer
sity traffic regulations. Two fines of
$3 and $5 respectively were suspend
ed by the Council. Three of the fines
were $1 each for open muffler, park
ing, and arterial stop violations. The
remaining five, including the two sus
pended cases, were for exceeding the
speed limit.
