# Washington University Plan To Illustrate Encyclopedia
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., May 1.—(P.1.P.)
—International recognition of the
plan of its campus was received by
the University of Washington in the
request of the Encyclopedia Britan
nica that a photograph of the plan be
furnished for publication in the world
famous reference work. The plan,
which was designed by Bebb and
Gould, former university architects, is
to illustrate an article on institutional
architecture.
