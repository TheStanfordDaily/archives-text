# Inactive Honorary Societies Ousted by Oregon Council
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., May 1.—(P.1.P.)—
The student council revoked the
charters of fourteen honorary or
ganizations as a result of a careful
investigation of all the honoraries on
the campus as to activity and useful
ness. Among those ousted were sev
eral language clubs, a number of art
societies; Hammer aud Coffin, Hu
morous writers' association; Delta
Theta Phi, law fraternity; and a num
ber of local organizations such as the
California Club, the Oregon Normal
Club, and the Coos County Club.
