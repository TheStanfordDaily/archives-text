# Australian Flier to Leave S.F. on Pacific Hop May 29
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO .May 2.—( U.P.)
—Captain Charles Kingsford - Smith,
Australian aviator, was flying here today
from Los Angeles to make final prepa
rations for his flight across the Pacific
ocean to Australia.
Officials at Mills Field, the municipal
airport here, said they understood he
would start the long flight from their
field on May 29.
