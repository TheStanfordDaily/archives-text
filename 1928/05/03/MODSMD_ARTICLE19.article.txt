# Nobile Starts First Leg Of Flight to North Pole
## 
### 
STOLP, Germany, May 3.—(U.P.)—
General Nobilo in Ills dirigible,
"Italia," in which he will attempt a
flight to the North Pole, left here at
3:10 A.M., today for Vadsoe, Norway.
It had first been announced the
"Italia" would stop at Oslo for re
fuelling and supplies. General Nobile
early this morning announced that
the flight would be made direct to
Vadsoe at nearly the extreme tip of
Norway.
