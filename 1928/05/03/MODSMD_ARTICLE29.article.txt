# WILLIS TO ENTERTAIN STANFORD FELLOWSHIP
## 
### 
Bailey Willis, professor emeritus of
geology, will entertain the Stanford
Fellowship informally at his home
this evening at 8:30 o'clock. The
membership of this group is not
limited since any Stanford student
may belong, but because only a cer
tain number can be accommodated
anyone wishing to go to Professor
Willis* home should notify a Board
member.
The fellowship board is composed
of the following: Sally Miller, Morden
Brown, Don Alden, Harriet Cogswell,
Ben Culver, Maxine Cushing, Tom
Drake, Anne Frick, Helen Huneke,
Winifred Huntington, Margaret Owen,
Stanford Steinbeck, and James Wat
kins.
