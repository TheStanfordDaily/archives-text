# U.C.L.A. Student Delegated To Youth Peace Conference
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 2.—(P.1.P.)— 1
Bringing distinction not only to his
university but to the Pacific Coast as
well, Chester Williams, junior of the
University of California at Los An
geles, has been chosen a delegate to
the World Peace Conference of Youth
in Holland, August 17 to 26. Williams
is the only representative of a Pacific
Coast college to be selected for this
honor.
Mrs. J. M. Hamilton, 65, a grand
mother, is to study journalism at the
University of Arkansas.
