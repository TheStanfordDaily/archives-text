# LINDBERGH AT CURTIS FIELD
## 
### 
CURTISS FIKLD, L.1., May 2. —
(U.l\) —Colonel Charles A. Lindbergh,
accompanied by Frank Robertson, of St.
Louis, Mrs. Robertson, and Lieutenant
Commander P. V. H. Weems, U.S.N.,
landed here at 7:15 p.m. today in Lindy's
new Ryan cabin monoplane.
