# PROFESSOR SCHULTZ ATTENDS PATHOLOGY CONVENTION IN EAST
## 
### 
E. W. Schultz, Professor of Bac
teriology and Experimental Pathology,
attended a convention of the Ameri
can Association of Pathology and
Bacteriology at Washington, D.C.,
which ended yesterday. The first day
of the convention Dr. Schultz read a
paper on "Studies on the Antigenic
Properties of Ultramiscroscopic Vi
ruses." Dr. Schultz will also attend a
conference of the American Associa
tion of Immunologists which is being
held at Washington coincident with
the convention. He is expected to re
turn to Stanford about the middle of
the month.
