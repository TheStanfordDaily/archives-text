# MAKES RECORD BUSINESS TRIP
## 
### 
NEW BRUNSWICK, N.J., May 3 —
(U.P.) —P. G. B. Morris, of New York,
left Hadley Field today in a mail
plane for San Francisco on one of
the fastest business trips ever at
tempted. He is due in San Francisco
Friday and will sail for Honolulu
where he will spend one day and then
return to the United States.
