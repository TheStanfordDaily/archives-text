# APPLEBY TO UMPIRE DAILY-PRESS GAME; STATEMENTS ISSUED
## 
### 
Edwin Appleby, well-known
San Francisco umpire, has been
induced to officiate at the Univer
sity Press-Stanford Daily base
ball classic which will be played
on the varsity diamond at 10
o'clock Sunday morning.
Carl Oliver, manager of the
Press team, in an official state
ment to the Daily said, "My club
is not over-confident of victory;
we are just sure we are going to
win." Asked why, he explained,
"There are nine reasons; they
will be on the field Sunday."
"They will be hard pressed to
beat us!" the Daily team replied
in unison. "They're not the
type."
