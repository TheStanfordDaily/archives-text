# PROFESSOR BINGHAM WILL RETURN TODAY
## 
### 
Professor Joseph W. Bingham of the
Law School will return to the campus
today from Washington, D.C., where he
has been attending annual conferences of
the American Law Institute and Teach
ers of International Law Association.
Professor Bingham is an adviser on the
subject "Conflict of Laws" for the Amer
ican Law Institute and assisted in draw
ing up a restatement of the Institute's
regulations concerning such conflicts.

Professor Graham H. Stuart of the
Political Science department will return
tomorrow from the conference of Teach
ers of International Association.
This association is sponsored by the Car
negie Foundation for World Peace. Pro
fessor Stuart will meet his classes Mon
day.
