# CAMPBELL, '24, HAS SERIOUS AUTO CRASH
## 
### 
J. D. (Scotch) Campbell, *24, star foot
ball player and captain of the 1923 team, 1
narrowly escaped death at an early hour
last Tuesday morning when the car which I
he was driving from San Francisco to
his home in Palo Alto got out of control
and smashed into a gasoline pump of the
Speedway Garage at San Carlos.
The car immediately caught fire but
Campbell and his wife, who was with him,
managed to extricate themselves from
the wreckage without being hurt.
