# EUPHRONIA WILL HEAR BASSETT ON "LURE OF MONACO" WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Professor Lee Emerson Bassett of
the Stanford English department will
be the speaker at a smoker to be
given by the members of Euplironia,
men's debating society, next Wednes
day night at 7:30 o'clock in the lobby
of the Old Union.
His topic will be "The Lure of
Monaco," based on his European trav
el, and will cover particularly the
Monte Carlo gambling houses. Leon
ard Kirkpatrick, president, has also
arranged a musical program, and
urges all members to attend.
The possibility of organizing Eu
plironia into a Stanford chapter of a
national fraternity will also be dis
cussed at the meeting.
