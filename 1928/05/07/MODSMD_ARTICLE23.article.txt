# WILL HONOR WING AT BANQUET TOMORROW
## 
### 
Charles Benjamin Wing, Professor
of Structural Engineering at Stan
ford since 1893, who will retire next
year, is to be honored by a banquet
of the American Society of Civil En
gineers at 6:45 o'clock tomorrow night
in the Stanford Union.
Officiating at the banquet will be
Charles David Marx, Professor Emeri
tus of Civil Engineering, and for
many years a friend of Professor
Wing. President Wilbur and Shirley
Baker, '95, will speak. All alumni of
the Civil Engineering department in
the Bay Region and all of Professor
Wing's friends on the campus are in
vited.
