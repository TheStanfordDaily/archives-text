# Fears of Dam Collapse in South Carolina City Ended
## 
### 
GREENVILLE, S.C., May 7.— (U.P.) !
—Pears of a cave-in of the Green
ville city waterworks dam at Table
Rock Cove, 31 miles from here, vir-

tually ended today when reports were
brought from the dam that sloughing
away of the earthen structure has
stopped and constant rains had
caused no appreciable damage.
