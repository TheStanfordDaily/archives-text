# Spanish Club Holds Picnic West of Campus Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Spanish Club will hold a picnic
in the hills west of the campus to
morrow afternoon, starting at 5:15
o'clock from the Lagunita boathouse.
All those wishing to attend are re
quested to pay 35 cents in advance
to one of the committee, composed
of Bernice Little and Dorothy French
at Roble Hall and Chilton Shirley at
Sequoia.
