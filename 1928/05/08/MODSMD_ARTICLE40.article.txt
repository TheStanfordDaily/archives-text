# U.C.L.A. to Hold University Beach Day Program May 11
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 7.—(1.P.)—
The U.C.L.A. annual All-University
beach day will be held in Santa Mon
ica on May 11. The entire Edgewater
Beach Club has been engaged for the
occasion, and the program will in
clude bathing, exhibition diving, and
dancing.
