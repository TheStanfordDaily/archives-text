# MEXICAN EDUCATIONAL LEADERS ENTERTAINED HERE SUNDAY, MONDAY
## 
### 
Twenty-five educational leaders of
Mexico were on the campus over
Sunday and Monday on an investiga
tion tour of California universities
and secondary schools.

On Sunday the group of educators
attended the service in the Memorial
Church, called informally to pay their
respects to Dr. and Mrs. Jordan, and
heard the organ recital in the church.
Yesterday the Mexican professors and
superintendents met President Wil
bur, drove about the campus, and
visited such classes as they desired.
After luncheon in the Stanford Union
dining room, they continued their tour
to San Jose.
