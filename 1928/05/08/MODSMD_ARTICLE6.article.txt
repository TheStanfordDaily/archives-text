# SATURDAY, SUNDAY SET FOR GOLF TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
The annual Santa Clara County
Golf championship will take place
Saturday and Sunday at the San Jose
Golf and Country Club. Eighteen
holes will be played Saturday after
noon and thirty-six on Sunday.
All Stanford men desiring to enter
are requested to phone Don Ander
son at the Phi Kappa Psi house dur
ing the luncheon hour or any day
before Friday.
