# Resident Alumnae To Give Benefit Bridge Tea May 16
## 
### 
The Resident Alumnae Association
will give a benefit bridge tea Wednes
day afternoon from two to six o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse. The
money raised from this tea will go
for the scholarship of the association.
This is the only tea the Resident
Alumnae on the campus have given
this year, so a large turnout is ex
pected. Reservations for bridge or tea
parties may be made through Mrs.
Ewell at the Clubhouse. The price of
each table is $2.00, and tea is served
for twenty-five cents.
