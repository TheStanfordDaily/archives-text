# PROGRAM GIVEN FOR HOMECOMING WEEK
## 
### 
Following is the program for Home
coming Week:
Friday, May 11
6:00 P.M. —Group Dinners: Depart
ments, Organizations, and Fra
ternities.
Saturday, May 12
9:30 A.M. —Reserve Officers' Train
ing Corps Activities, Encina
Polo Field.
9:30 A.M. —Mounted Artillery Drill.
10:30 A.M. —Polo Game.
12:00 Noon —Luncheon to officers of
the Alumni Association, mem
bers of the Stanford National
Board, members of the Alumni
Council, and members of the
Reunion Classes at the Presi
dent's House.
11:00 A.M. —Annual Meeting of Alum
in Council, Room 10, Law
Building.
4:00 P.M. —Annual Business Meeting
of Alumni Association, Room
12, Law Building.
4:30 P.M. —Tea, Women's Clubhouse,
with local alumnae as hostesses.
6:00 P.M. —Class Reunion Dinners,
1903, 1905, 1906, 1907, 1908,
1924, 1925, 1926, 1927. (See bul
letin boards for assignment of
dining halls to various classes.)
6:00 P.M. —Dinner to Officers of Uni
versity, Deans of Schools, Stan
ford National Board, and Alum
ni Council at Stanford Union.
8:30 P.M. —Junior Opera in Assem
bly Hall.
Sunday, May 13
11:00 A.M. —Special Services, Memor
ial Church.
4:00 P.M. —Special Organ Recital at
Memorial Church. Warren D.
Allen, University Organist.
7:30 P.M.—Toyon Musicale.
