# Bottled Goods
## 
### 
Mother's Day is this coming Sunday. Thousands of mothers' hearts
will I>e gladdened by greetings, presents, and other expressions of love
from their children on this day. But how much joy will it bring to one
who knows that her dutiful son has rushed into a telegraph office, slimmed
down the requisite amount, and ordered a "Mother's Day greeting No. 3"
to be wired ?
It is the message itself she sees, but it is the idea behind the sending,
the love, the atTection, which makes her happy, and if she knows that it is
just a canned message picked from random out of a number of Western
Union boosting campaign slogans, the receipt of the greeting will be just
about like the sending. One is never too busy to compose his own words
to mother.
