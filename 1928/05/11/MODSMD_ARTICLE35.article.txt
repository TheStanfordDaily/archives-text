# Y.M.C.A. Cabinet Will Meet At Taylor's Sunday Night
## 
### 
The Y.M.C.A. Cabinet will meft Sun
day evening at the home of Professor J.
D. Taylor, and will complete cabinet or
ganization for the coming year at this
time. Plans will also be made for the
Y.M.C.A. Conference Retreat to be held
at A1 Roth's in the Santa Cruz Mountains
May 25, 26, and 27.
