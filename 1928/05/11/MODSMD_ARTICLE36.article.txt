# POSTOFFICE SCHEDULE
## 
### 
The heaviest incoming mails at the
Stanford postoffice are at 7:30 o'clock
in the morning and 12:30 in the after
noon. Mail also comes in from the
southern states and southern Califor
nia at 10:30 a.m.
About an hour and a half is re
quired in sorting mail for the boxes
and general delivery. The best times
to call for mail are after 9 a.m., noon,
and 2 p.m.
Special Days
On Sundays and holidays there is
only one incoming mail, at 7:30 a.m..
and one dispatch from the campus, at
G a.m. However, at the downtown
Palo Alto office, there is a dispatch
which closes at 5:50 p.m.
Schedule of arrival and departure
of mails from the Stanford Univer
sity postoffice:
Arrives From
7:30 a.m. All points
10:30 a.m. South
12:30 p.m. North and East
4:00 p.m. S.F. and Bay District
5:00 p.m. S.F. and Hay District
Closing Time To
6:00 a.m. All Points
10:00 a.m. South
1:00 p.m. South, North, East
4:45 p.m. All Points
Sundays and Holidays
Mail arrives at 7:30 p.m. from all
points.
Mail closes at 6:00 p.m. to all
points.
Air mail leaves San Francisco at
7 a.m. Last dispatch to connect with
North and East air mail —5 p.m.
The 6:08 p.m. to San Francisco car
ries a railway postoffice car, and in
emergency, letters may be mailed on
this car at the Southern Pacific depot
for the next morning air mail and for
others first-class mail to points north
and east.
Special delivery mail will be de
livered to halls, houses, and resi
dences, and should be so addressed.
