# Eagle Scouts Installed as Zeta of Alpha Phi Omega
## 
### 
The Stanford Eagle Scout troop
was formally Installed as Zeta chap
ter of Alpha Phi Omega, national hon
orary professional scouting fraternity,
at a meeting in the Union Wednesday
evening. David Schmidt, who has been
serving as senior patrol leader, was
elected grand master; Edward Smith,
deputy grand master; Andrew Por
ter, secretary; and Victor Siegfried,
treasurer. Harrison E. White, scout
executive for the local district, pre
sided at the installation. Sixteen
scouts were present.
