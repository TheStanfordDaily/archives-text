# Julian Case Defendant Ill; Excused To Recover Health
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 10.— (U.P.) —
j Seriously ill, T. P. Conroy, secretary
of the Julian Petroleum Corporation,
one of seven men on trial charged
with conspiracy, left today for Phoe
nix, Arizona, in hope of recovering
his health.
Informed of Conroy's condition,
Superior Judge William C. Doran ex
cused him from the case. The prose
cution offered no objections. Conroy
is under $10,000 bond.
