# Home Of Sapiro Attorney Ruined By Mystery Bomb
## 
### 
DETROIT, Mich., May 13.—( U.P.)
—A bomb explosion early today did
considerable damage to the home of
William Henry (fcillagher, prominent
Detroit attorney. Windows were
i broken in the Gallagher residence
and in neighboring houses. No one
i was injured.
Gallagher was chief counsel for
! Aaron Sapiro in his million dollar
j libel suit against Henry Ford and the
Dearborn Independent last year, but
; police were unable to explain the
bombing.
