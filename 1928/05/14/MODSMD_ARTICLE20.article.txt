# GREEKS FIX DRACHMA VALUE
## 
### 
ATHENS, May 13.—(U.P.)—A de
cree for stabilization of the drachma
I was signed, fixing the monetary unit
of Greece at the following gold value:
151,212.87 drachma! equals 1000 gold
grammes. For bills of exchange the
pound sterling equals 375 drachma!.
The decree becomes effective Mon
day.
