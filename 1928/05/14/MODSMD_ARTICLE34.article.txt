# Y.W.C.A. Will Hold Open Meeting Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
An open meeting of Y.W.C.A. will
be held tomorrow night at 7 o'clock
downstairs in the Women's Club
house to review the events at the
National Convention, including the
Student Assembly, held at Sacra
mento.
Miss Cogswell, secretary of the
Y.W.C.A.; Mrs. Margaret Mudgett,
president of the Advisory Board; and
Ruth Tinslev, president of Y.W.C.A.,
will relate some of the interesting
points and people of the convention.
