# PROFESSOR P. J. TREAT TO STUDY AT CAPITAL
## 
### 
Professor Payson J. Treat of the
History department will be away all
next year. From October to February
he is planning to study diplomatic
material in the Department of State
at Washington, D.C., dealing with
early relations between Japan and
the United States. Professor Treat
published the first part of this study
ten years ago.
From February to June he will be
a visiting professor at Columbia Uni
versity, giving courses on the Far
East.
