# HICHBORN TO DISCUSS MODERN PROPAGANDA
## 
### 
"The Amazing Development of
Propaganda in Public Life" will be
the topic of Franklin Hichborn, noted
California publicist, who will talk be
fore Pi Sigma Alpha, political science
honorary society, Wednesday evening.
The meeting, which will be open to
members and their guests, will begin
at 6:15 o'clock tomorrow night in the
Alcove at the Union.
Hichborn, who has been in con
stant contact with the colorful side
of practical politics in California for
many years, is the author of well
known studies of the legislature at
Sacramento, and writings concerning
the notorious San Francisco graft
prosecution during the period 1906-
1910.

The University of Arkansas plans
a nine-hole golf course for the use
of the students.
