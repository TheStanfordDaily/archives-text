# Sophomore Women Defeat Seniors In Track Contest
## 
### 
The sophomore women defeated the
seniors in an interclass track meet
yesterday afternoon by a score of 52
to 23. One record was broken, that
of the basketball throw, which
Marion Holley won with a heave of
70 feet 10 inches. The old record, set
by Frances Watson, '2S, at Field Day
last year, was 68 feet 3 inches.
