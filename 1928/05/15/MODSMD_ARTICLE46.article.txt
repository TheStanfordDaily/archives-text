# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
2:30 p.m.—Members of Women's Glee
Club meet in front of the Women's
Clubhouse for transportation to the
Los Altos concert.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren D.
Allen in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—Journalists Club meets in
the Press building. William Leiser
and Ernest Hopkins will speak.
7:30 p.m.—Concert by Winifred Esta
brook, soprano, Elizabeth Peirce,
violinist, and Mary Moynihan, pian
ist, in the Little Theatre.
7:30 p.m.—Stanford Radio Club will
hold important meeting in Radio
Shack. All interested Stanford stu
dents urged to come.
WEDNESDAY
12:15 p.m.—Cosmopolitan Club, inter
national luncheon in the alcove of
the Stanford Union dining room.
2:15 p.m.—Resident Alumnae Associ
ation bridge tea in the Women's
Clubhouse for the benefit of the
scholarship fund. Reservations may
be made through Mrs. Ewell at the
Clubhouse.
3:00 p.m. — Tryoutees for baseball
manager meet on varsity diamond.
8:00 p.m.—Science Association and
Sigma Xi will meet jointly at the
Faculty Club House. Professor
Walter Iv. Fisher will speak on the
work of the Hopkins Marine Sta
tion.
8:15 p.m.—French Club in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
THURSDAY
3:00 p.m.—Stanford Dames in the
Women's Clubhouse. Guest Day.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
I"). Allen in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—Thursday Evening Confer
ence at the home of Dr. Jordan.
Subject: "Peralta-Revis, Baron of
Arizona."
7:30 p.m.—Zoology Club will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse to discuss
plans for a trip to Pacific Grove. A
business meeting will follow.
FRIDAY
7:15 p.m.—Women's Debate, Univer
sity of Southern California vs. Stan
ford. Subject: "Resolved, That in
vestors and investments in foreign
countries should be protected only
by the government of the country
in which the investment is made."
