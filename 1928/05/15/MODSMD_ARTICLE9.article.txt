# NATIVE BELGIANS PLAN SOCIETY ORGANIZATION AT MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
Four Belgian members of Le Cercle
Francaise will organize a typical Bel
gian social party at a meeting of the
club to be held in the Women's Club
house tomorrow night at 8:15 o'clock.
J. D. H. Donnay, Carl de Brouwer,
Pierre Van Rysselberge, and Robert
E. de Strycker, who constitute the so
called Belgian Colony in the club, are
arranging the program. The four are
exchange students from Belgian uni
versities and are under the C.R.B.
Education Foundation established by
the Commission for Relief in Bel
gium.
The program includes a ventrilo
quial comedy called "Le Petit Ma
lade" by G. Courteline, Belgian stu
dent songs, monologues, French
games and charades, and classical
piano selections, to be played by Van
Rysselberge.
