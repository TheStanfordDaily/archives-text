# TWO STANFORD TENNIS ACES WILL LEAVE FOR SEATTLE TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Alan Herrington and Ralph McEl
venny will entrain this morning for
Seattle where the two Stanford tennis
aces will compete in the annual
Pacific Coast Intercollegiate Tennis
Tournament. Coach Hal Davis will
accompany the men. The play will
take place tomorrow and Friday.
The Cardinal pair will enter both
singles and doubles competition. They
should have little trouble in winning
the doubles competition and their
real opposition in the singles division
will come from the Oregon team
headed by Howard Neer. Neer has
defeated both of the Stanford racquet
wielders.
