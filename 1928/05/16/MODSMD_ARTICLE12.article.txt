# BELGIAN SOCIAL PARTY WILL BE FEATURE OF FRENCH CLUB MEETING
## 
### 
A typical Belgian social party will
be the feature of a meeting of Le
Cercle Francais in the Women's
Clubhouse tonight at 8:15 o'clock.
The program will include a ventrilo
quial comedy called "Le Petit Malado"
by G. Courteline, Belgian Student
songs, monologues, French games and
charades, and classical piano selec
tions by Pierre Van Rysselberge.
