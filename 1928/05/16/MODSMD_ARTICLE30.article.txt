# FIRST ROUND MATCHES PLAYED YESTERDAY IN MIXED-DOUBLE TENNIS
## 
### 
The second half of the first-round
matches in the all-University mixed
doubles tennis tournament was played
yesterday afternoon at 3 o'clock. This
completes the first round of cham
pionship play.
In the first match of the afternoon
Bell and Wheatley defeated Curtis
and Neighbors, 6-1, 6-1; White and
Dobson won their match from Jago
and Espinosa, 6-4, 6-2; Sanford and
Johnston were defeated by Leachman
and Long, 6-1, 6-0; A. Frick and
Lawshe defaulted to Edwards and
Thomas; Thatcher and Renzel de
feated Lovekin and Springer, 6-3, 6-3;
Powell and Barrett dropped their
match to Williams and Sperry 1-6,
1-6; Patton and Weesner won from
Jones and Wilson, 6-2, 6-2; in the last
match B. Williams and Israel took
their match from Thorner and Carl
smith, 6-3, 6-8.

Twelve U. of W. co-eds contested
for Queen of Junior Day.
