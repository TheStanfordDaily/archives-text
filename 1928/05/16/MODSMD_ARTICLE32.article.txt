# Y.W.C.A. WILL SPONSOR CANDY SALE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Homemade candy will be sold in
the Roble lobby at noon tomorrow,
under the auspices of the Y.W.C.A.
Proceeds of the sale will go toward
buying new fiction for the Y.W.C.A.
loan library, which will be opened on
the campus next fall.
Contributions of candy are being
received from the living groups and
patronesses of the organization, and
if enough is collected, the sale will
extend to the Quad and the Row.
