# Stanford Dames To Hold Guest Day This Afternoon
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold a Guest
Day meeting this afternoon at 3
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse, to
which each member will bring a
guest. Roland B. Dodge will talk on
the Hawaiian Islands, and Mrs. Har
old Benjamin will play several violin
selections.
