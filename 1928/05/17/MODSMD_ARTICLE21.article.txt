# Dempsey Resumes Training For New Stage Appearance
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May IG.—U.P.)—
Jack Dempsey has resumed training,
but it is only for his forthcoming New
York stage appearance, he said. The
former champion, who is to appear in
an Eastern production with his wife,
Estelle Taylor, works out daily on
the road and in the gym.
