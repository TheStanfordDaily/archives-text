# Warren D. Allen To Give Recital In Chapel Today
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen will give his regu
lar organ recital this afternoon at
4:15 o'clock in the Memorial Church.
The program is as follows:
Sonata No. 2, in D minor J. H. Rogers
Choral Arthur Honegger
Toccata on "King's Lynn"
Harvey Grace
Berceuse Harvey Grace
"Evening Star" Romanza "Tann
hauser" Wagner
March "Hail, Bright Abode,"
Tannhauser Wagner
