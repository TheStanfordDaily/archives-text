# DAILY BUSINESS STAFF TRIALS ARE TO CLOSE TOMORROW, SAYS CONN
## 
### 
Tryouts for the business staff of
The Stanford Daily will continue
throughout this week, ending to
morrow, according to Claude C. Conn,
business manager. Those wishing to
try out should see Conn in the Daily
business office as soon as possible.
Students trying out for positions
on the business staff will work until
the end of the Spring Quarter when
appointments will be made for next
year. As sophomores they will com
pete for the three junior manager
ships which are salaried positions.
These three managers, chosen at the
end of the sophomore year, have
charge of advertising in the three
fields —national, local, and San Fran
cisco. From these three the business
manager will be appointed for the
senior year.
Previous experience is not needed
by tryoutees. Their duties will consist
in selling advertising, making sur
veys, merchandising work, bookkeep
ing, office work, and the like. Compe
titive rating will not commence until
next year.
