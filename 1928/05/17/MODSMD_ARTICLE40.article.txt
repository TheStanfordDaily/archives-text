# TWO GAMES TODAY IN MURAL BASEBALL PLAY
## 
### 
Two games will be played today to
complete first-round competition in
the intramural baseball finals. Win
ners of these games will advance to
the semi-final round which will be
played next Tuesday.
Alpha Tau Omega will meet Alpha
Delta Phi at 4 o'clock on the varsity
diamond in the game. Phi Kappa Psi
will meet the winner of the Kappa
Sigma-Phi Delta Theta game, which
derides the championship of League
VI.
The winners of these games will
meet Sigma Alpha Epsilon and Delta
Chi Tuesday to determine the teams
that will enter the finals for the All-
University championship.
