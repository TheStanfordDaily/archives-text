# GYM TEAM WILL GIVE EXHIBITION TOMORROW
## 
### 
A one-hour exhibition of gymnastics
will be given at 7:30 o'clock tomor
row night in the apparatus room of
Encina Gymnasium by the Stanford
Gym team. Pyramid building, hand
balancing, and apparatus work will
comprise the exhibition. Admission
will be free, and ladies are invited
to attend.
