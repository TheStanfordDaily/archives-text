# FRASER LEAVES FOR CONVENTION IN SOUTH
## 
### 
Robert M. Fraser, chairman of the
Rally Committee, left last night for
Los Angeles where he will attend the
sixth annual convention of the Pacific
Association of Yell Leaders Monday
at Occidental College, A1 Cass and
Scott Webster, yell leaders, and
David Jacobson. recently elected
chairman of the Rally Committee for
next year, will leave the campus Sun
day to attend the same convention.
Problems concerning the manage
ment of a rooting section and the
question of selecting stunts will be
the chief topic of this year's conven
tion. The Stanford delegation will in
vite the association to hold its next
meet here.
