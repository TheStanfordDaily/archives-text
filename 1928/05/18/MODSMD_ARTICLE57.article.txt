# MEXICAN ANTHOLOGY PUBLISHED BY PRESS
## 
### 
The second book of the Stanford
Spanish Series, which is being edited
by Professor Aurelio M. Espinosa, has
just been published by the Stanford
University Press. The new text is
"A Brief Anthology of Mexican
Verse," by S. L. Millard Rosenberg,
Ph.D., and Ernest H. Templin, Ph.D.,
both of the University of California
at Los Angeles.
