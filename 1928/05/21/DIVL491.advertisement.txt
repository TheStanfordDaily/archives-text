Second Anniversary
Marking the Anniversary of our Second Year of Shoe
Service to Stanford Students, we are presenting a sale
in which we offer our "best sellers" at extraordinarily
, low prices, in all styles of shoes. At these great savings
it will pay you to stop in and supply youself with shoes
for every occasion.
These shoes are taken from all our lines. They are in a
wide variety of styles and prices. Though not all sizes
are included in every style, all sizes are represented in
this event.
PRICES RANGE |
from
$3.95 to $8.85 '
