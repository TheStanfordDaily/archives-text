# German Club Will Present Heidelberg University Scene
## 
### 
A scene of Heidelberg University,
acted by singing and costumed ac
tors. will be presented by the German
Club Wednesday evening in the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock.
The program will include a variety
of German folk-songs and tunes such
as are sung by university students
of Germany. Tn addition, there will be
a few popular classical songs and a
cornet solo.
Everyone interested is invited to
attend.
