# THIRD ROUND IN MIXED DOUBLES TOURNAMENT IS SLATED TOMORROW
## 
### 
The All-University mixed doubles
tennis tournament has progressed to
the third-round matches, which are
scheduled to be played tomorrow
afternoon at 3 o'clock on the varsity
courts. Losers of the first-round
matches will play a consolation tour
nament at the same time tomorrow.
Seven matches of round two were
played last Thursday. Two went to
three sets: Dunlap, Hall defeated
Richmond, Easton, 7-5, 7-5, 6-4;
Thatcher, Renzel defeated R. Wil
liams, Sperry, 6-4, 4-6, 6-3. The other
matches resulted as follows: Patton,
Weesner defeated B. Williams, Israel,
6-4, 6-2; Edwards, Thomas defeated
Leachman, LAng, 8-6, 6-2; Bell, Wheat
ley defeated White, Dobson, 6-3, 6-0;
Rittler, McElvenny defeated Demond,
Swim, 6-1, 6-0; Willis, Milliken de
feated Silliman, Mansfield, 11-9, 6-3.
Because of the absence of Herring
ton, the scheduled match of David
son, Herrington vs. Deirup, Rickets
was postponed. The winning teams of
the above matches will play tomor
row, two matches will be played
Thursday, and the finals on May 29.
