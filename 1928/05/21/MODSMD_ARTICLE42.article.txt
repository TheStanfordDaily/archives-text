# Collegian Takes N.Y. Lap of Bunion Derby
## 
### 
WAVERLY, N.Y., May 20.—( U.P.)
—Paul Simpson, Burlington, N.C., col
legian, won his first lap of C. C. Pyle's
transcontinental marathon today by
covering the 58.3 miles from Bath to
Waverly in 30 hours, 17 minutes, 10
seconds.
A rainstorm descended on the run
ners again today, but it did not bother
Simpson who did his best running
since the caravan left Los Angeles
March 4.
Simpson is thirty-ninth in elapsed
time standings, but refuses to quit
the race. He has covered the 3155
miles from Los Angeles to this con
■trol in 830:28:42.
