# CARD SWIMMERS WIN IN SAN JOSE CONTEST
## 
### 
Six Stanford men won six medals
in the Fiesta de las Rosas swimming
meet Friday night held in the Ven
dome Hotel plunge in San Jose. The
Cardinal mermen took all three
places in both the 100- and 500-yard
freestyle events.
Reggie Harrison won the century,
followed by Bramel and Thomas. The
time was 56 seconds. The 500-yard
race went to Ogden Driggs, who
swam it in 6:48 3-5, with Lucy and
Brown second and third.
The last edition of the S
Californian Wampus will be
fifty university women.
