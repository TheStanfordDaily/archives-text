# University Of Oregon Has Total Enrollment Of 4087
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., May 20.—(1.P.) —
Full-time enrollment at the Univer
sity of Oregon, including the Eugene
campus, medical school and exten
sion divisions in Portland, and corre
spondence work, has reached a total
of 4087. This shows a marked in
crease over 1924, when the total was
3075.

Regular college work for freshmen
will be emphasized at the University
of Alabama summer school this year.
