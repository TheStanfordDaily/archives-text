# Cincinnati Shows Largest University Growth In U.S.
## 
### 
MINNEAPOLIS, Minn., May 20.—
(I.P.) —The University of Cincinnati
leads all other universities in the
United States in growth since 1922,
according to statistics compiled by
the Association of American Univer
sities. Cincinnati has shown an 80
per cent increase in six years and
now ranks sixteenth among American
colleges, having an enrollment of
9088.
