# Fog Helps Escape of Three Convicts From Prison Dairy
## 
### 
JOLIET, 111., May 20.—(U.P.)—Un
der the protection of a heavy fog,
three convicts escaped from their
guards while on a milk detail at the
prison dairy farm at Stateville on the
outskirts of Joliet today.
Three truckloads of guards armed
with rifles and shotguns were sent
out immediately to search the high
ways for the fugitives, who, it was
believed, had fled in the direction of
Chicago.
