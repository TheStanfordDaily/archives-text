# MOSHER ENGAGEMENT
## 
### 
Announcement of the engagement
of Helen E. Mosher, '28, and. Robert
Rapp, '26, was made Friday night at
the formal dance of the Delta Delta
Delta sorority of which Miss Mosher
is a member. Rapp is a member of Pi
Sigma Alpha and Phi Beta Kappa,
receiving his M.A. last year. He re
sides in San Francisco.

Phi Beta Kappa at the University
of Illinois annually offers a prize of
$25 for the best original essay sub
mitted by a member of the senior
class.
