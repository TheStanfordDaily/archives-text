# BROWN WILL RELATE TROPIC EXPERIENCES
## 
### 
Professor Harold Brown of the De
partment of Philosophy will address
the meeting of the English Club to
morrow night, at 8 o'clock at Jack
Slump's home in Los Altos. Frofessor
Brown will talk about his recent ex
periences in the South Sea Islands.
Election of new members will be
held at this meeting, and plans for
the initiation and picnic to bo given
soon will be discussed. Members of
the club who wish rides should meet
in front of the Women's Clubhouse at
7:45 o'clock and cars will be provided.
