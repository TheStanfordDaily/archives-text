# Stein-Paradise Advance In Handball Tournament
## 
### 
The Stein and Paradise team ad
vanced to the second round of the
1928 Handball Doubles Tournament
yesterday when they defeated Mey
berg and Levit in a close match. The
score was 21-8, 21-16.
The first round of the tourney will
be completed today when McDermott
and Mandell meet Walker and Mahy.
