# Tri-Delts Win Annual Song Contest Trophy At Whitman
## 
### 
WHITMAN COLLEGE, Walla Walla,
Wash., May 21.—(P.I.P.)—The Delta
Delta Delta sorority won the annual
Gena Branscomb contest for the second
time. This is the annual song contest into

which each fraternity and sorority enter.
The groups sing one of their own songs
and one of a general nature. The win
ning group is picked for its interpretation,
quality, and technique. The cup which is
given to the winner was given in honor
of the famous Gena Branscomb, who is
now in New York, composing music.
