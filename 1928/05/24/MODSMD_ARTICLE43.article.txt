# CUBBERLEY WILL TALK FOR EDUCATION CLUBS
## 
### 
Phi Delta Kappa, professional edu-
I cation fraternity, Pi Lambda Theta,
women's educational fraternity, and
the Education Club will hold a joint
social meeting Monday night at 8
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Ellwood P. Cubberley, Dean of
the School of Education, will talk on
"The Doctor of Education Degree."
i Members of Pi Lambda Theta will
put on a musical program, followed
{ by a general mixer.
Flaud C. Wooton, president of the
1 Education Club, urges all education
students, and other interested people,
to come to the meeting, the last of
the year.
