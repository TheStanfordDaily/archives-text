# SPANISH ORGAN MUSIC TO BE CHAPEL PROGRAM
## 
### 
Modern Spanish organ music will
be played this afternoon at 4:15
o'clock in the Memorial Church by
Warren D. Allen. A number of Span
ish compositions have been recently
made accessible to organists over the
world, and Allen will play several of
these. Spain is rich in cathedrals and
fine old organs.
The program for this afternoon is
as follows:
Fantasia Jose Maria Beobide
Pantomime (from "El Amor Brujo")
Manuel de Falla
Intermezzo
Adaigietto
(from the "Suite l'arlesienne")
Bizet
Rhapsody on Spanish Airs
Eugene Gigout
