# CLASS TO GIVE THREE PLAYS DURING SUMMER
## 
### 
Three plays, one of which will be
"The Queen's Husband," by Robert
Sherwood, author of "The Road to
Rome," will be given by the Theatri
cal Workshop class during the sum
mer. One of the other plays which
has been decided upon is "The Chief
Thing," a Russian play, done by the
Theater Guild two years ago.
The unexpected return of Gordon
Davis to take charge of dramatics at
Stanford during the Summer Quarter
insures the continuation of the excel
lent instruction and performances
which have marked former Summer
Sessions. Davis will he assisted on
the technical side by Harold Helven
ston, who lias had much experience in
designing and drawing.
