# PEKING HOLDS OUT
## 
### 
LONDON, May 24.—(UP.)— The
rapid march of the Chinese National
ist armies against Peking, which was
expected to fall at the first battlo
with Chang Tso-Lin's Northern forces,
apparently has been halted outside
the city, where fighting is believed
to be proceeding with the Nationalists
at some placcs in retreat.
