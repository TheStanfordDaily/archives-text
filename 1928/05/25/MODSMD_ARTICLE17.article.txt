# Non-Stop Flight To Capital Of U.S. Planned By Mexican
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, Calif., May 24.— (U.P.)
—Emilio Carranza, 23, the "Lind
bergh of Mexico," took off from here
at 3:30 o'clock, this afternoon on a
non-stop flight to Mexico City.
He planned later to make a non
stop flight to Wasliinglon from Ihe
Mexican capital to return the visit
of Colonel Charles A. Lindbergh to
the Southern Republic.
