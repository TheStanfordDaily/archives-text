# STANFORD DAMES PLAN MEETING FOR TONIGHT
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold a social gath
ering tonight at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse for the members of the club
and their husbands. There will be bridge,
and a musical program. Mrs. G. A. Dun
can, president, will play a violin number,
and Mrs. Ogg will give several vocal
selections. This is the regular quarterly
party, and the last one for this year.
