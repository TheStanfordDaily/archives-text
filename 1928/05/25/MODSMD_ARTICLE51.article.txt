# POLO TEAM WILL PLAY LAST GAME TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity polo team will
have its final game of the season to
morrow when it plays a combined
Burlingame-San Mateo squad on the
Menlo Circus Club grounds at 2:30
o'clock. The public is invited.
The Menlo Club has donated four
' cups which will go to the winners.
| Lieutenant Charles S. Whitmore will
be an umpire. Stanford's line-up will
be: Weymouth or Connolly, Post, Mc
i Dougall, Thompson. The Burlingame
| San Mateo line-up will be: Fuller,
Schwerin, Tevis, Durry.
