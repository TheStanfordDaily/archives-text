# Byron Summers Is Winner Of 14-Mile Swim Marathon
## 
### 
ALAMEDA, May 27.—(U.P.)—An
easy but powerful stroke brought
Myron Summers of Alameda to vic
tory In the second annual Alameda
14-mile marathon swim here today.
Summers negotiated the course in
exactly nine hours. Mark Wheeler,
Venice policeman, finished second, 32
minutes behind Summers. Only eight
of 36 entrants were able to cover
more than 3% miles of the grind.
