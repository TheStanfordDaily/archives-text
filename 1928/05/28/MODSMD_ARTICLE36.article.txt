# OREGON JOURNALISTS TACKLE CITY PAPERS
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., May 27.—(P.1.P.)—
As practical experience in applying
the knowledge learned in class, the
reporting classes in the school of
journalism of the University of Ore
gon put. out the Eugene Register,
morning paper, and the Eugene
Guard, evening paper, on two days
during the past two weeks. The stu
dents wrote all local stories and ed
ited all the wire stories.
