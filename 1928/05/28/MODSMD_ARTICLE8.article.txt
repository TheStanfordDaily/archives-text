# Oregon Wins West Division Of Northwest Ball Series
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., May 27.—(P.1.P.)—
The University of Oregon cinched the
championship in baseball in the west
ern division of the Northwest Confer
ence Friday by defeating the Univer
sity of Washington at Seattle 4-2. So
far the Webfoots have won five and
lost ono game. Oregon will meet the
winner of the eastern section, prob
ably the University of Idaho, for the
Northwest championship.
