# BOROTRA-BRUNGAN DUO WINS TENNIS TOURNEY
## 
### 
ROLAND GARROS STADIUM,
Paris, May 28—(U.P.)—Jean Borotra
and Jacques Brugnon of France won
the international hard courts men's
doubles championship today by defeat
ing their fellow countrymen, Henri
Cochet and Rene Debuzlet, 6-4, 3-6,
6-2, 3-6, 6-4.
Francis T. Hunter, United States
Davis Cup player, advanced in the
men's singles, defeating Combemale
of France, 6-0, 6-3, 6-2. Hunter's ter
rific forehand crashed into Combe
male's court with such force that the
Frenchman seldom was able to re
turn it.
Gerald Patterson ,the Australian,
eliminated Louis Torralva, 3-6, 1-6, 6-3,
7-5, 6-4.
