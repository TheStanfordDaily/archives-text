# Popular Screen Actress Of 1928 Is Shorter, Lighter
## 
### 
The promising young actress of 1928
is an inch shorter, two years younger,
and two pounds lighter than the
promising young screen actresses of
last year.

The statistics are from the Metro-
Goldwyn-Mayer studio which brought
out five feminine players in 1927 and
a similar number this year. The girls
of this year's group average 18 years
in age, five feet three inches in
height, 112 pounds in weight, and
l three of the five are blonde.
