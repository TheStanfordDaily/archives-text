# Washington Enrolls First Woman In Military Course
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., May 27. —
(P.I.P.) —Alyce Wester, a junior in
the liberal arts college, is the first
university woman to enroll in a mili
tary course as far as registrar's sta
tistics show. Miss Wester has been a
member of the women's rifle team,
and entered the course to study the
practical application of rifle shooting
in warfare.
