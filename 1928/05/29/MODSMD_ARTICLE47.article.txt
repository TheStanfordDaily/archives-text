# Junior At Washington Sails On Expedition To Alaska
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., May 27—(P.1.P.)
—Chosen because of his knowledge
of Alaskan natives, Harry Manca,
liberal arts junior at the University,
lias sailed for St. Lawrence Island, off
the coast of Siberia, as a member of
the Smithsonian Institute party which
will spend seven months in the
Arctic, attempting to trace the evolu
tion of the Eskimos.
