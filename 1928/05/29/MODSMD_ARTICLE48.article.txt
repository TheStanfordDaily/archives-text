# NEW TRIAL IN JULIAN CASE ON U.S. CHARGES
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 28.—( U.P.) —
Preliminary hearing for S. G. Lewis,
former president of the Julian Petro
leum Corporation and the Lewis Oil
Corporation, and Jack Bennett, his
aide, today was set for June 8.
The men, charged with using the
mails to defraud, appeared before
United States Commissioner Head and
provided bonds of $25,000 each.
Both were recently acquitted of
conspiracy charges in connection
with the collapse of the Julian com
pany.
The Federal charges were based on
sale of stock in the Lewis company.
