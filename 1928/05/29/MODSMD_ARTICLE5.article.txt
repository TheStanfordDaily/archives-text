# LIBRARY COMMISSION OF Y.W.C.A. STAGES BREAKFAST MEETING
## 
### 
The Library Commission of the
Y.W.C.A. will hold a breakfast meet
ing this morning at 7 o'clock down
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse. The
commission will plan the details of
conducting the new library for next
year. It will also make a final report
of the books which have been col
lected.
This library is being formed by the
Y.W.C.A. to rent books, both fiction
and non-fiction, for the use of the
women. These will be bought, or col
lected from the living groups and
patronesses of the organization.
Miss Harriet Cogswell, secretary of
the Y.W.C.A., and Page Bradshaw,
chairman of the commission, are in
charge of the breakfast. The commis
sion plans to hold another candy sale
Thursday to raise money for buying
new fiction.
