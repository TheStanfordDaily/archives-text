# DR. HANSEN TO ATTEND CONGRESS IN NORWAY
## 
### 
Dr. Hazel Hansen, '20, together with
Dr. H. Barrett Learned, will represent
Stanford at the Sixth International Con
gress of Historical Sciences to be held
in Oslo, Norway, President Wilbur an
nounced recently.
Dr. Hansen, who received her Ph.D.
degree in 1926, will probably be one of
a very few women to attend this meeting,
says Dr. Payson J. Treat of the History
department, such an appointment being
considered a great honor. She has been
spending the present year studying at
Athens and traveling extensively in
Greece. She will read a paper at the
Congress on archeological discoveries in
Thessaly.
