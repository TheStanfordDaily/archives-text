# Patton, Holley Leave For Women's Athletic Meet
## 
### 
Thelma Patron and Marion Holley,
Stanford women track stars, left last
night for Eureka to represent the North
ern California Athletic Club in a track
meet Saturday.
Marion Holley will take part in the
high jump event; in yesterday's meet she
cleared 4 feet 6 7-8 inches.
Patton will run in the 65-yard high
hurdles. Her time in this event yesterday
was 10.4 seconds.
