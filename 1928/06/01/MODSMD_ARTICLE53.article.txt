# MISSION BALL TEAM TAKES COOK SYPHER
## 
### 
Cook Sypher, 1928 captain of varsity
baseball made an agreement today to play
with the Mission ball team of San Fran
cisco, having completed arrangements
with Red Killefer on Wednesday. Sypher
gained pre-collcgiate experience on the
Timber League and Northwest League
teams in Tacoma, Washington.
