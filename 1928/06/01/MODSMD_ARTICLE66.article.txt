# THETA SIGMA PHI HAS DINNER FOR INITIATES
## 
### 
Nine newly elected members of
Theta Sigma Phi, women's national
journalistic fraternity, were enter
tained by the older members at a
dinner at the Pepper Tree Inn Wed
nesday night following the initiation
ceremonies.
Each of the initiates was called
upon to read an original literary ef
fort written for the occasion. The
new members are: Page Bradshaw,
Margaret Cummings, Alice Ingraham,
Ruth Patterson, Phoebe Plate, Janet
Post, Helen Smith, Sylva Weaver, and
Margaret Wood.
