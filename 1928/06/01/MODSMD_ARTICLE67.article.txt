# Alice Rose Is Elected Head Of Women's Education Club
## 
### 
Alice Rose was elected president
of Pi Lambda Theta, honorary wo
men's education society, at a meeting
yesterday afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse. The other officers elected
were Judith Johnson, vice-president;
Gertrude Rendtorff, terasurer; Mrs.
Georgia Benjamin, recording secre
tary; and Ada Roberts, corresponding
secretary.
