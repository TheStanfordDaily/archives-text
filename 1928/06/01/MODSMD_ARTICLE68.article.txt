# Hugo Wall New President Elect of Phi Sigma Alpha
## 
### 
Hugo Wall was elected president
of Phi Sigma Alpha, honorary political
science fraternity, at a recent meet
ing. Other officers elected were
Frank Carroll, vice-president; and
Katharine Newkirk, secretary-treas
urer.

"No Smoking" signs have been dis
tributed throughout campus buildings
