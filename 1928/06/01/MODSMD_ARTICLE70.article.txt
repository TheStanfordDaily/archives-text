# Bomb Explodes in Campaign Headquarters Of Obregon
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, May 31— (U.P.) —
A bomb exploded this evening in the
campaign headquarters of General
Obregon, former president of Mexico,
who is a candidate for the presidency
to succeed President Calles.
One campaign worker was injured
but none was believed killed.
Several days ago a bomb was set
off in the Chamber of Deputies shortly
after passage of a measure sponsored
by Obregon followers.
