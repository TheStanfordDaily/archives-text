# PIGSKIN ARTISTS WILL HOLD PUNTING CONTEST
## 
### 
Cardinal pigskin artists will meet
at 4 o'clock this afternoon on the
varsity practice field to compete in
the annual all-University punting con
test. The competition is open to all
registered students and no prelimi
nary sign-up is necessary.
Lud Fentrup won the title last year
and Captain Biff Hoffman the year be
fore. Harlow Rothert was a possi
bility in the contest this year, but is
in the Kast with the track team. The
winner will have his name engraved
on the perpetual punting trophy.
