# JUNIPERO SERRA CLUB GIVES GLEASON WATCH
## 
### 
Monsignor Joseph M. Gleason,
founder of the Junipero Serra Club
and at present in charge of the St.
Francis de Sales church in Oakland,
was presented with a watch by the
local club at a reception held in his
honor recently.
At the same time Frank B. Carroll
was elected president of the organi
zation for the coming year. Other of
ficers were: William F. Trigerio, vice
president; Van R. Hamilton, secre
tary; and Manuel Espinosa, treasurer.
