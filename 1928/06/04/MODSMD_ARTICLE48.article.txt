# POTOMAC SCHOOL HAS ONE-MAN TRACK TEAM
## 
### 
STATE COLLEGE, Pa., June 3.—
(I.P.) —A one-man track team, made
up of Paul Heydrick, from Potomac
State School, Keyser, West Virginia,
set a record for individual point
scoring here recently at a fourteen
school meet, when he piled up thirty
three points for himself and his alma
mater.
Heydrick took first places in the
high jump, broad jump, pole vault,
hammer throw, discus and javelin
events, and finished second in the
shot put.
