# McDOUGALL, HART TAKE TOUR
## 
### 
James Webster McDougall, '27, and
George David Hart, '31, are leaving
on the steamship "President Adams"
on June 15 to take a trip around the
world and return by October. They
are going through the Orient and are
expecting to spend a month in Eu
rope, touring by means of airplane
while there.
