THURSDAY, JUNE 21
7:30 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Organist, in the Memorial Church. (Choir
rehearsal at 8:15.)
FRIDAY, JUNE 22
4:15 p.m. LECTURE, by Dr. George Barger of the University of Edinburgh, in the Little Theatre. Subject:
"The History of Ergot and Ergotism."
8:00 p.m. AT HOME. President and Mrs. Wilbur will be at home very informally to all students and members
of the faculty of the summer quarter at the President's House.
SATURDAY, JUNE 23
10:00 a.m. AUTOMOBILE EXCURSION, to La Honda and Pebble Beach. Leave from the Stanford Union,
Main Entrance, returning about 4 p.m. Fare $2.50. Reservations must be made and fare deposited
two days in advance, in the office of the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Administra
tion Building.
SUNDAY, JUNE 24
11:00 a.m. DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: Reverend Stanley A. Hunter, D.D., St. Johns
Presbyterian Church, Berkeley, California.
7:30 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
MONDAY, JUNE 25
4:15 p.m. LECTURE RECITAL, by Mr. Charles Koechlin, in Branner Hall. Subject: "Contemporary French
Music since Debussy."
TUESDAY, JUNE 26
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
8:00 p.m. LECTURE, by Mr. Gordon Davis, in the Little Theatre. Subject: "The Trend of the Theatre in
Central Europe Today."
WEDNESDAY, JUNE 27
4:15 p.m. LECTURE, by Mr. A. Feuillerat, in the Little Theatre. Subject: "Shakespeare and Poetry."
THURSDAY, JUNE 28
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
FRIDAY, JUNE 29
9:00 p.m. to 1:00 a.m. RECEPTION TO SUMMER QUARTER STUDENTS, at Roble Hall. Dancing.
SATURDAY, JUNE 30
8:00 a.m. AUTOMOBILE EXCURSION, to Santa Cruz. Leave from the Stanford Union, Main Entrance,
returning about 4:00 p.m. Fare $3.00. Reservations must be made and fare deposited two days in
advance, in the office of the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Administration Building.
OFFICIAL NOTICES
THOMAS WELTON STANFORD ART GALLERY The Art Gallery is open to the public, without
Exhibition of Block Prints in Color, by William S. charge, from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., daily, including
Rice, June 10 to July 1. Sunday.
