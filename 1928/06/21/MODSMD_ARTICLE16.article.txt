# QUARTER'S DIRECTORY NOT TO BE 'BAWL-OUT'
## 
### 
The Summer Directory should be
ready for distribution in about two
weeks, according to an announcement
made by the Registrar's office. The Di
rectory will include the names, ad
dresses, and places of registration of all
professors and students enrolled in the
Summer Quarter.
The Summer Directory is not a "bawl
out." The regular quarter directory in
cludes the honor point standing of all
students. The Summer Directory will
not include any scholastic records.
