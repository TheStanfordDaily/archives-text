# FACULTY CLUB PLANS SMOKER FOR NEW MEN
## 
### 
Visiting summer faculty men will
be welcomed by the Faculty Club at
a smoker at 8 o'clock tonight in the
Faculty Clubhouse. A special meet
ing was called to give the faculty men
this chance to get acquainted.
All men on the summer faculty
will be given guests cards to the
Faculty Club which will entitle them
to the full use of the privilegea of
the club.
