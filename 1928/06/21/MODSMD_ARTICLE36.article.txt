# ALLEN ISSUES CALL FOR CHOIR STUDENTS
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, director of the
University choir, has issued a call for
all who intend joining the choir to
meet him in the organ loft of the
Memorial Church immediately after
the organ recital tliis evening. The
choir sings at the morning service in
Memorial Church each Sunday. A
unit of academic credit is offered for
the training in choral singing.
