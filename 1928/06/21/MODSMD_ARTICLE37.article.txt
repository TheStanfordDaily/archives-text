# Women's Faculty Club to Present Musical Program
## 
### 
A short musical program will be
given by the Women's Faculty Club
at the Women's Clubhouse Monday
at 3 o'clock. The program is planned
to welcome the wives of the guest

professors and the women professors
of the Summer Quarter. A special
meeting of the Women's Faculty Club
has been called Monday for this pur
pose. All women connected with ttlie
faculty are invited.

Smoking by women has nothing to
do with "ladylikeness," says Beloit
College.

The Department of Geology at the
University of Kansas will conduct a
summer Rocky Mountain field trip.
