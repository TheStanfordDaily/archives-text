MASONIC HALL, PALO ALTO
WEDNESDAY, JUNE 27, AT 8 O'CLOCK
After numerous requests for another performance
THE EMINENT SCANDINAVIAN EXPERIMENTER
de Waldoza
will conduct his famous series of unique experiments, demonstrating
the secret subtle power of the human mind, which delves into the un
fathomable depths of the human soul. An attendance at one of these
seances is an unforgettable and unparalleled incident. It's unlikely
that a similar opportunity may ever present itself again.
De Waldoza will take away pains of any kind and if possible will cure
anyone from nervous ailments during the performance.
