# ART EXHIBITION WILL REMAIN UNTIL JULY 1
## 
### 
The Palo Alto Art Club exhibit of
Architecture and Landscape Garden
ing is now to be seen at the Palo
Alto Public Library which is located
at the corner of Hamilton Avenue
and Bryant Street, Palo Alto. The
exhibit will be open to the public
daily until July Ist.
The collection of material in the
exhibit includes fine photographs, pen
and ink drawings, work in water
colors, work in pencil, crayon, and
blue prints, covering interiors and
exteriors of residences as well as gar
den scenes and views of business
blocks.
Growing interest in and respect for
the spirit of the honor system is re
ported by the honor committee of
Oregon State College.
