Saving on Small Wages
Will power, rather than earning ability, is the determining factor
in the universal ambition to win financial success. Some persons are
unable to save on ten thousand a year, and yet other men on far
smaller incomes manage consistently to save some of it. Little sacri
fices may be necessary, but the peace of mind that comes from having
followed a systematic savings plan is worth every bit of the effort. A
modest sum will open a savings account in the American Trust Com
pany. Four per cent interest, compounded semi-annually is paid on
deposits.
