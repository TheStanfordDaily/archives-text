# FEUILLERAT LECTURES WiLL BEGIN JULY 11
## 
### 
The series of lectures on Shake
speare and Poetry which are to be
given by Professor A. Feuillerat. will
start on Wednesday, July 11. Due to
delay en route, Professor Feuillerat
was unable to give his first lecture
which was scheduled for Wednesday
at the Little Theatre. A good-sized
group of interested people were dis
appointed by his non-appearance.
