# Education Department to Give Banquet Monday Eve
## 
### 
Sponsored by the department the
banquet for men in Education will
be held Monday evening at the Stan
ford Union. There will be speeches
and songs. The time will be 6:30
o'clock and the price $1.25. Students
sign up in Room SG, Education De
partment.
