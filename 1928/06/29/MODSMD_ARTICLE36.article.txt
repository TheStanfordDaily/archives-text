# REGISTRATION IS NOW 1313; HALLS FILLED
## 
### 
Registration for the Summer Quar
ter reached 1313 today. Last year at
a corresponding date the total was
1376, finally climbing to 1394.
Of the men's dormitories Toyon and
Union report every vacancy filled,
while Encina will not open this sum
mer.
Roble reports a total registration of
193, which leaves only six vacancies
remaining.

Peking, China, is making an effec
tive campaign against speeders and
has beheaded some chauffeurs.
