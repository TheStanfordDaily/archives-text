# EDUCATION FRATERNITY HOLDS FIRST MEETING
## 
### 
Plans for the summer were made at
last night's meeting of the Stanford
chapter of Phi Delta Kappa. Presi
dent John R. Nichols called the chap
ter to order and the following officers
were elected for the summer quarter.
R. D. Case, Recording Secretary; Rex
Bell, Corresponding Secretary, and
John Aseltine, Treasurer. The re
newal of old acquaintances occupied
a portion of the time and after an
hour's discussion of business the
group adjourned.
