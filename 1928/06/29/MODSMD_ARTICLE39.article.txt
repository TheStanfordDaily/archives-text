# Tennis Tournament Starts July 5; All Men Eligible
## 
### 
A singles tournament and a doubles
tournament for all men who are reg
istered in the University for the Sum
mer Quarter will get under way on
Thursday, July 5. All men who are
interested in entering one or both of
these tournaments are urged by El
wyn Bugge, coach of tennis during
the Summer Quarter, to sign up at
the men's gym before Wednesday,
July 4.

An inventor in Germany has in
vented an electric , dry-cell battery
clock which he asserts will run with
out winding for several years.
