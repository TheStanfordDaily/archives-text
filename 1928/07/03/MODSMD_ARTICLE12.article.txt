# VARIETY OF NUMBERS IN SUNDAY'S RECITAL
## 
### 
The regular Sunday evening organ
recital was played by Warren D. Al
len in the Memorial Cliapel. The
opening number, "The Bells of St.
Anne de Beaupre," by Russell, was
followed by the Lento from the "Lon
don Symphony" whieh was played re
cently at Hillsborough. The Legende
from the "Suite in B Minor," by Er
nest Douglas, and Bairstow's "Eve
ning Song" were also included on the
program. Mr. Allen concluded the re
cital by playing the brilliant Finale
from the "Third Suite" by Edward
Barnes.
