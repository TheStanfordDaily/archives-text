# ROBLE CLUB TO HOLD FIRST AT-HOME TODAY
## 
### 
Roble Club announces the first
of a series of Tuesday afternoon
at-homes will be held today from
3:30 to 5:30 o'clock. No special
invitations will be sent out, but
all faculty or student body mem
bers are invited. Tea will be
served during the afternoon. Miss
Olivia Boezinger, social director
of the hall, will act as hostess.
