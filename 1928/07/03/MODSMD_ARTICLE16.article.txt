# ENTRIES TAKEN FOR TENNIS TOURNAMENTS
## 
### 
Plans have been made by Elwyn
Bugge, instructor in tennis for the
Summer Quarter, for men's singles,
men's doubles, and mixed doubles ten
nis tournaments to start next Mon
day, July 9. These tournaments are
open for all students registered in
the University for the Summer Quar
ter.
Entry lists are now posted in En
cina and Roble gyms, and any inter
ested are requested to sign up before
Saturday, July 9. Those entering the
mixed doubles may choose their own
partners by putting his or her name
down when signing up. For those who
have no. preference, partners will be
assigned them by the committee in
charge.
