# TEA TO BE HELD FOR POMONA DEAN, JULY 12
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, National Honor
ary Educational Fraternity will give
a tea at the Women's Clubhouse
Thursday, July 12, at 7:30 o'clock.
Miss Gibson, dean of women at
Pomona College, will be the speaker
of the evening, and will give her im
pressions of the Pi Lambda Theta
Convention held at Seattle last July.
Those who are interested in mak
ing application for membership in
this fraternity may obtain blanks in
the education library or from mem
bers of Pi Lambda Theta. Require
ments for membership include twelve
hours of education, experience in
teaching, a "B" average, and at least
one previous quarter of work at Stan
ford.
