# Stanford Dames to Meet Next Thursday Afternoon
## 
### 
The first meeting for the quarter
of the Stanford Dames, an organiza
tion open to wives and mothers of
students in any department of the
University, will he held Thursday
afternoon at 3:15 o'clock in the Stan
ford Women's Clubhouse. For the
benefit of those who are new to the
campus it has been explained that
the purpose of the club is the promo
tion of acquaintanceship among these
women, that their temporary resi
dence at the University may be more
agreeable. Those eligible for member-,
ship may become members by attend
ing, without formality of application
or election.
