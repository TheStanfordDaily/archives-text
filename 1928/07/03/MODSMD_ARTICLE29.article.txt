# Encina Gym and Pool Will Be Open Part Time July 4
## 
### 
The Encina gymnasium and pool

will be open part time July fourth.
The gymnasium may be used from 9
to 5 o'clock. The swimming pool will
be open from 9 to 12 in the morning,
and from 2 to 5 in the afternoon. The
gymnasium store and supply room
will be closed all day. Therefore,
those wishing towels and other sup
plies, must secure them today. The
south entrance to the gymnasium will
be the only one that will be open.
