# SEATS FOR QUEEN'S HUSBAND TO GO ON SALE IN OLD UNION
## 
### 
Tickets for "The Queen's Hus
band," which is to be given Fri
day night, can be obtained at
the Associated Students Office
in the Old Union. There will
also be a box office sale on the
night of the performance start
ing at 7 o'clock.
The Dramatic Office announc
es that there are still many good
seats available, but those who
wish them should get them as
soon as possible. The office also
stated that the curtain would
rise at 8:20 o'clock.
