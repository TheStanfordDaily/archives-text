# Ushers For Summer Plays Needed Now, Says Franklin
## 
### 
All men who are interested in usher
ing for the Summer Quarter produc
tions in the Assembly Hall should
report to J. P. Franklin at the Old
Union Thursday night at 7:30 o'clock.
Franklin may also be reached by
telephoning Palo Alto 2311-J.
The ushers for the performance of
"The Queen's Husband" are asked to
report at the Assembly Hall at 7:30
o'clock Friday night.
