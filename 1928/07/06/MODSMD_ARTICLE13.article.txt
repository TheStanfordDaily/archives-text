# TWO STANFORD WOMEN ENTER OLYMPIC GAMES
## 
### 
Two women will represent Stanford
at the 1928 Olympic Games at Am
sterdam. Clarita Hunsberger, '27, will
compete in the fancy high diving and
Marion Holley, '30, in the high jump.
Miss Hunsberger, who was trained
by Coach Ernst Brandsten while at
Stanford, competed also in the Olym
pic Games of 1924. In the Olympic
trials this year she took second place
in the national high diving meet.
Marion Holley tied for second place
in the high jump at 4 feet 11% inches.
