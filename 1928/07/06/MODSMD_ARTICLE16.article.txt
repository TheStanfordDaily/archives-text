# SUMMER BAND ADMITS FIRST WOMEN ARTISTS
## 
### 
For the first time in the history of
the Stanford band women have been
admitted to membership. Four of the
fair sex are now practicing regularly
with the Summer Quarter band. Femi
nine influence is found further in the
fact that the soloist for the first con
cert of the season is to be Miss Flor
ence Mannon, who will play a sousa
phone solo.
The first concert will be held July
15 at !! o'clock in the afternoon at
the band house. The program has not
yet been definitely decided.
