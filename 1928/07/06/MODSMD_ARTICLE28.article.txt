# ROBLE CLUB TO HOLD TEA EVERY TUESDAY
## 
### 
Roble Club announces that a
series of at-homes will be held
every Tuesday afternoon from
3:30 to 5:30. Members of the fac
ulty and friends of the Club are
welcome although no invitations
will be sent out. Tea will be
served during the afternoon, Miss
Olivia Boezinger, Social Director
of the Hall, acting as hostess.
