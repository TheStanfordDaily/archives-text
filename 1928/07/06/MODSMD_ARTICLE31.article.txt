# Education Department Is To Give Dinner Thursday
## 
### 
The first of the Thursday evening
dinners, a summer session institution
of the men in Education, will take
place Thursday evening, July 12, in
the dining room of the Stanford
Union. These gatherings are highly
informal and each may come and do
as he pleases. The dinner will be at
6 o'clock.

The largest battery of typesetting
machines in the world is the govern
ment printing office in Washington.
