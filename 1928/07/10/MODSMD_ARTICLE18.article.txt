# Loder To Be Manager Of Stadium At State Capital
## 
### 
Ed Loder, Engineer and Manager
of Operations for the Board of Ath
letic Control, will leave August 1 to
become Manager of the Municipal
Stadium at Sacramento.
Loder graduated from the School of
Engineering in '23, returning here in
the spring of '25 under Paul Davis
who was then graduate manager. All
plans for new buildings since that
time have been drawn by Loder who
has supervised the building of
them, He has also superintended all
events taking place in the Stadium.
As yet no one has been appointed i
to fill the position vacated by him.
