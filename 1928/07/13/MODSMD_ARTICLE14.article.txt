# SCHEDULE COMPLETED FOR ALL-UNIVERSITY TENNIS TOURNAMENTS
## 
### 
The schedule for the all-University
Summer Quarter tennis tournament
has been completed and is now posted
at the Encina gymnasium.
Forty-five are entered in the men's
singles and thirteen pairs in the men's
doubles. Eight, teams are entered in
the mixed doubles.
Participants are urged to play their
matches as soon as possible as it is
necessary for the first round to be
completed by next. Friday and the
second round by the end of the fol
lowing week, in order that the tourna
ment end in scheduled time. All
matches are to be played on the
courts opposite Encina gymnasium.
Any further information may bo
obtained from Coach Elwyn Uugge in
Harry Maloney's office.
