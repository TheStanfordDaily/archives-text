# RICHFIELD PLANE DUE HERE THIS WEEK-END
## 
### 
The Richfield's twelve-passenger
tri-motor Fokker airplane to be used
by the Hoover reception committee
is expected at the Palo Alto School of
Aviation sometime this week-end.
The huge runway and the two
smaller ones which have been under
construction this week are now com
plete. They will be ready for use as
soon as they settle and the oil dries,
which should be about Saturday.
