# Educational Fraternity To Initiate Class Saturday
## 
### 
Phi Delta Kappa, national profes
sional fraternity for men in Educa
tion, will initiate a class of neophytes
next Saturday afternoon in Palo Alto.
The initiation ceremonies will begin
at 2 o'clock and will be held at the
Native Sons' Hall located at the cor
ner of Hamilton avenue and Emerson
street. At 6 o'clock, following the cere
monies, a banquet will be held at the
Cardinal Hotel.
