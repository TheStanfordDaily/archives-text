# STANFORD DAMES TO MEET NEXT THURSDAY
## 
### 
A meeting of the Stanford
Dames will be held on Thursday
afternoon at 3:15 o'clock in the
Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
Wives of students are cordially
invited .There will be bridge and
sewing.

The college girls of Converse Col
lege are not using lipstick or rouge
any more.
