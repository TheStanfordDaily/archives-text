# TICKET SALE OPENS THIS THURSDAY FOR "THE CHIEF THING"
## 
### 
Tickets for "The Chief Thing"
are to go on sale Thursday at
1 o'clock in front of the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Tickets may
also be obtained at the Student
Manager's Office in the Old
Union and at Crandall's in Palo
Alto.
Those who wish tickets by
mail may obtain them by writ
ing to the Dramatic Manager's
Office, Stanford University.
Ed Smith, dramatic manager,
states that those who wish good
seats should get them early as
many people were unable to get
tickets for "The Queen's Hus
band" because of their delay.
