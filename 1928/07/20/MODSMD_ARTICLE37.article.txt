# LIST FOR HANDBALL IS POSTED AT GYM
## 
### 
Those desiring to enter the
singles and doubles handball
tournament may do so by signing
up on the list posted on the minor
sports board in Encina gymna
sium. All men registered for the
Summer Quarter in the Univer
sity are eligible. Further informa
tion in regards to the tournament
may be had from Harry Maloney.
