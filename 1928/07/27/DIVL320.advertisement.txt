VoL XLIII, No. 39 ~ PUBLISHED EVERY FRIDAY _ July 29-Aug. 4
SUNDAY, JULY 29
11:00 a.m DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: Reverend Albert Joseph McCartney, Presbyterian
Church, Santa Monica.
Anthems:
"O, Bone Jesu" Palestrina
"Hear, Oh My People" Stevenson
Miss Verda Hogan, Soprano soloist
7:30 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Organist, in the Memorial Church.
MONDAY, JULY 30
8:00 p.m. CONCERT, by The Abas String Quartet, in Branner Hall. Open to the public without charge.
TUESDAY, JULY 31
3:30-5:30 p.m. ROBLE CLUB at home to students and faculty.
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
8:00 p.m. LECTURE, by Professor Fernand Baldensperger, in the Little Theatre. Subject: "Young Clemenceaa
on American Reconstruction."
WEDNESDAY, AUGUST 1
4:15 p.m. LECTURE, by Professor A. Feuillerat, in the Little Theatre. Subject: "New Light on Sir Philip Sidney."
THURSDAY, AUGUST 2
3:15 p.m. STANFORD DAMES, in the Women's Clubhouse. Russian program.
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. THURSDAY EVENING CONFERENCE, at the home of Dr. Jordan. Subject: "Kellogg and the Out
lawry of War."
SATURDAY, AUGUST 4
400 pm. AUTOMOBILE EXCURSION, to Lick Observatory, Mount Hamilton. Leave from Stanford Union,
main entrance, returning about midnight. Fare $3.50. Reservations must be made and fare deposited
two days in advance, in the Office of the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Administration
Building.
OFFICIAL NOTICES
THOMAS WELTON STANFORD ART GALLERY The Art Gallery is open to the public, without charge,
American Indian Handicraft Exhibit. from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m., daily, including Sunday.
Australian Landscapes, by J. W. Curtis.
Paintings and Austrian Wood Carvings, being part of
the Thomas Welton Stanford collection.
WEEKLY CALENDAR
Announcements for the Weekly Calendar, in writing, should be in the hands of
the Secretary to the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Administration
Building, not later than 9:00 a.m., THURSDAY
