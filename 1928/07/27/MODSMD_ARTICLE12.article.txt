# Rev. Albert J. McCartney To Preach Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
The Rev. Albert J. McCartney of
the Presbyterian Church at Santa
Monica will preach in the Memorial
Church Sunday. The choir will sins
"Oh, Bone Jobu," of Palestrina, and
Stevenson's "Hear, Oh My People,"
with a soprano solo by Miss Verda
Hoean.
