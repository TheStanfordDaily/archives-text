# Here's How!
## 
### 
The Summer Quarter is flying by rapidly, and the first six weeks will
soon be over. Some students will leave at that time, while others will
merely wish that they could. Summer time is vacation time and the
thought of going to school is really worse than the actual process.
The Daily hopes-that the students that are leaving when the first half
of the quarter is over have had a pleasant time and leave with pleasant
memories, whether they lie regular students or summer students.
To those who are going to remain the last four weeks we do not
believe any consolation is necessary. College isn't nearly as hard as most
students try to make their parents believe, and we would be perfectly wiling
to lead this cruel life the rest of our davs.
