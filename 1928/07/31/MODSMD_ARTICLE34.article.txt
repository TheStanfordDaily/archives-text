# BARBARA LEE, '29, WINS WOMEN'S GOLF TROPHY
## 
### 
Barbara Lee, '29, won the women's
golf championship of San Francisco
at Lincoln Park, Friday. Miss Lee de
feated Mrs. H. P. Braddock, winning
by a margin of 4 and 3. She was pre
sented with the Herbert Fleishacker
trophy.
The match was featured by Miss
Lee's strong drives; tee after tee she
registered an average of 200 yards.
Her score was 39, to her opponent's
43, for a four-hole lead at the ninth,
which is one over women's par for
this course.
