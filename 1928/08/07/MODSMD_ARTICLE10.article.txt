# STUDENTS MAY GET TICKETS TOMORROW FOR NOTIFICATION
## 
### 
Distribution of tickets to fac
ulty and students for the Hoover
notification speech, on August
11, in the Stanford stadium, will
be held Wednesday. Tickets may
be secured in the lobby of the
Administration Building, between'
10 and 12 o'clock, and from 2 to
5 o'clock.
One ticket will be given to
each student ,and two to mem
bers of the faculty. If any tickets
are left over, there will be an
additional distribution on Friday
in Room 173. Although there are
accommodations for everyone,
students and faculty are urged
to insure themselves good seats
for Saturday by getting these
tickets early.
