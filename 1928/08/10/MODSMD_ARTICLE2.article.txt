# HERBERT HOOVER '95
## 
### 

BIRTHDAY ON EVE OF GREAT EVENT
Herbert Hoover will celebrate his fifty-fourth birthday today. He was
born August 10, 1874, in West Branch, lowa. Tomorrow he will be officially
notified of the greatest honor he has yet attained, the nomination for
President of the United States. This will top his many achievements as
mining engineer and food administrator, and various chairmanships and
secretarial positions he has held.
