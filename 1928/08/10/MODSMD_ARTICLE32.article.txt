# SIGNALS ARE ON FOR "BIFF'S" RESTAURANT
## 
### 
Signals are on and all is set to
throw hunger for a twenty-yard loss
in "Biff" Hoffman's new soda fountain
and restaurant.
The famous Stanford athlete has
decided to carry on a business along
with his school work, and has opened
a modernly equipped establishment in
Palo Alto.
"Biff" is a three-letter man in track.
He is captain-elect of the 1928 foot
'ball squad, being a two-year varsity
veteran of this sport, in which he has
gained ail-American distinction.
