# Biography of Hoover by Will Irwin Selling Fast
## 
### 
Will Irwin, the biographer of Her
bert Hoover, was a freshman in 1895
when Mr. Hoover was a senior. Irwin
became a hero-worshiper of Hoover
then and has remained one of his
closest friends.
One hundred fifty copies of "Her
bert Hoover," Will "Irwin's biography,
have been sold on the campus and in
Palo Alto.
