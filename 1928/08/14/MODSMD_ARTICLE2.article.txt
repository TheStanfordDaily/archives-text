# FIRST AIR POLICEMAN KEEPS SKY CLEARED FOR HOOVER ADDRESS
## 
### 
Lieutenant Norman A. Goddard, in
structor at the Palo Alto School of
Aviation, has officially been made the
first, air traffic policeman in the State
of California.
Goddard made the first use of his
authority last Saturday when he sent
orders to all the airports in the state
prohibiting any airplane to fly in the
vicinity of the Stanford Stadium dur
ing the hours between 3 and 5 o'clock.
All planes using the Palo Alto field
during the afternoon were grounded
until the notification ceremonies were
finished.
