# Rev. Charles Gilkey Will Give Last Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Charles Gilkey, visiting
minister from Chicago, will deliver
the last of his three sermons in the
Memorial Church Sunday morning.
"O Saviour of the World," by Goss,
and "Incline Thine Ear," by Himmel,
will be sung by the choir. The latter
anthem contains an alto solo which
will be sung by Mrs. W. P. Clement.
