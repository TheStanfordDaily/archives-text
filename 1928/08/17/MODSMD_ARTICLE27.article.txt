# Adams Will Be On 1929 Summer Quarter Faculty
## 
### 
Joseph Quincy Adams will be a
member of the Stanford faculty for
the Summer Quarter of 1929, as a
visiting member of the Department
of English. Professor Adams is the
author of several well-known books
on Elizabethan literature. He is a
familiar contributor to leading liter
ary and philological journals of this
country and of Europe, and joint
editor of "Cornell Studies in English."
He has been Professor of English at
Cornell University since 1919.
China's unused resources are said
to be similar to those of the United
States 400 years ago.
