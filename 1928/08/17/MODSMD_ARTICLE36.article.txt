# Traffic Violators Fined At Men's Council Session
## 
### 
One parking and three speeding
violations were tried at a meeting of
the Men's Council Tuesday. The total
fines amounted to $22. Although Clark
Cypher, the present chairman, is leav
ing the University, the council did not
elect a new chairman. There will be
but one more routine meeting of the
council during the Summer Quarter.
