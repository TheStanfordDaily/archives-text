# JOHN DOEG IS VICTOR IN EASTERN NET PLAY
## 
### 
John Do eg, '31, captain of last
year's freshman tennis team, won the
men's singles title at Itye, New York,
Sunday. Doeg, who is eighth in the
national ranking, beat Fritz Mercur
of Bethlehem, Pa., the conqueror of
Bill Tilden and George Lott. The
score was 7-5, 8-6, 6-1.
After winning the singles title Doeg
paired with George Lott of Chicago,
to defeat Tilden and Frank Hunter.
This \atter match gave the Western
ers the national doubles title. The
score here was 4-6, 9-11, 7-5, 6-0, 6-3.
