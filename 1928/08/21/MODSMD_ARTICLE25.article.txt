# Appreciation
##

The Summer Quarter Daily for 1928 appears today for the last
time. This has been an unusual Quarter as far as Stanford is concerned,
for during the past two months she has gained world recognition because
of Hoover's short stay here. And while this famous alumnus was making
Stanford's name heard 'round the world, Card athletic teams were ac
complishing the same result in far-off Australia, and in Europe.
But a scant two weeks remain now till the end of the quarter. Those
who are here only for the summer will be returning to the college they
regularly attend or to their professional work. We hope that their stay
here has been as much an aid to them as it has been to us. The Daily
lakes this opportunity to thank the members of the Summer Quarter
Student Body for their co-operation and help in making this paper
possible.
