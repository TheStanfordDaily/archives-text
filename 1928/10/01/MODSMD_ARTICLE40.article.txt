# "LIT" OPENS CAMPAIGN TO GET SUBSCRIPTIONS
## 
### 
Subscriptions are being taken to
day for the Stanford Literary Maga
zine for the year 1928-29 at stands on
the Quad. The price of the book is
one dollar for the year.
Issued regularly through the school
year, the Lit contains stories, verse,
book and theater reviews, and criti
cisms written by Stanford students.
Those not subscribing today can
do so by mailing one dollar to the
Stanford Literary Magazine.
