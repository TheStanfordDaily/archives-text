# APPLICATIONS FOR BIG GAME SEATS GIVEN OUT TODAY
## Tickets For U.S.C. Contest Will Be Sold in Old Union This Week 
### 
More than 500 applications for the
Big Game were given out yesterday
to students registering in upper and
lower division. Applications may be
secured today as soon as registration
is completed by the presentation of
A.S.S.U. cards at the Hoard of Ath
letic Control desk outside of the Ad
ministration Building. J. C. Irwin,
head of the Ticket Department, urges
students to send in their applications
immediately to make sure of securing
seats. Each student may secure one
rooter's ticket and two additional
tickets.
Sale of rooters' tickets and addi
tional seats for the U.S.C. game will
be held in the A.S.S.U. offices at the
Old Union from 2 to 5 o'clock on
Thursday and Friday afternoons of
this week. For this contest each stu
dent is entitled to a rooter's ticket
and four seats in the best section,
Five hundred good seats are available
and will be sold to the first appli
cants. Rooters' tickets for the Trojan
contest will not be on sale after this
week as all tickets that the students
do not use will go to fill the other
applications now on hand.
