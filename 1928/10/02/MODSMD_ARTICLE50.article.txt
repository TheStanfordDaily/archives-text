# THIEVES RAID BORAH'S HOTEL ROOM; GET $700
## 
### 
LINCOLN, Neb., Oct. 1. —(/P) —Ap-
proximately $700 was stolen from
Senator William E. Borah of Idaho
and Sam Jones, his secretary, while
they slept in an unlocked room at a
local hotel Saturday night, it was
learned today. The money was said
to have belonged to the Republican
campaign fund.
Senator Boran retired Sunday night
after his campaign address at the
University of Nebraska Coliseum. He
placed his wallet containing about
$400 under his pillow. Mr. Jones left
his money in the pockets of his trou
sers. When they awoke yesterday
morning their pocketbooks were
where they left them but the money
was gone.
