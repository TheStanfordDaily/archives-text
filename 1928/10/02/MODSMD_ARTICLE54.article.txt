# FIND NIGHT FOOTBALL SUCCESSFUL IN SOUTH
## 
### 
Night football is becoming more than
a passing fad in Dixie.
William and Mary of Virginia played
several games last season at night and
found the experience so successful that
the practice will be continued.
Alabama Polytechnic Institute, of the
Southern conference, will be the first
team to try it in that organization. The
Tigers are to play Birmingham-Southern
College at Montgomery, Ala., on the
night of September 28.
Howard College also is initiating the
practice.

Ten men have been indicted in
Kansas City for transporting stolen
motor cars across state lines.

The Otus, new British submarine,
was launched recently.
