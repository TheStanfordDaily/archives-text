# Three Changes Announced In Autumn Time Schedule

###

fall time schedule. Principles of Eco

from nine o'clock to ten, and will meet
in room 90 under Professor Pagan.

been changed from eleven to nine o'clock.

speaking for engineers, meeting at eight
o'clock on Monday, Wednesday, and Fri
day in room 200e, has been announced.
