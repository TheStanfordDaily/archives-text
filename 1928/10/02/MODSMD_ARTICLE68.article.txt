# PHI BETA KAPPA SECOND IN 1927-28 SCHOLARSHIP LIST
## Chemistry Fraternity Ascends to First Place in Rating as Proverbial Scholars Drop in Grade Standing 
### 
Phi Beta Kappa dropped to second place in the scholarship rating for
1927-28 with an average of 2.492, and Phi Lambda Upsilon, chemistry fra
ternity, won the cherished first place with an average of 2.589 for the year.
No other group under any classification surpassed this rating.
Commuters and students living in Palo Alto were better in scholarship
than students on the campus, and those enrolled at Hopkins Marine Station
placed third in the entire rating with an average of 2.244.
The varsity basketball team averaged 1.659, taking firßt among men's
athletic groups, while among the women's teams the archers were best at
1.967. Varsity football was sixth among the athletic teams and sixty-ninth
as compared to all other groups.
Stanford women outdistanced the men by .362 points, despite the fact that
there were only 394 undergraduate women, the rest of the 500 being graduate
students. Non-sorority and non-fraternity students were better as a whole
than tha fraternity students.
