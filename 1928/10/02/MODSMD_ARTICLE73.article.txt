# SAYS ILLINOIS TO GIVE MAJORITY TO HOOVER
## 
### 
EMPORIA, Kans., Oct. I.—{&)— Roy
O. West, Secretary of the Interior, pre
dicted here today that Illinois, his home
state, would give a majority of 500,000
for Herbert Hoover. Secretary West
made the statement when informed that
Thomas F. Donovan, chairman of the
Illinois Democratic state committee, had
told Governor Smith the state would
give him a 50,000 majority.
Mr. West, with E. C. Finney, a mem
ber of his staff, is on his way to Safford,
Ariz., to conduct a hearing on a Gila
River dam controversy. He also intends
to investigate Indian affairs in Albu
querque, N.M.
