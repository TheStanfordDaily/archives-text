# Y.W.C.A. HAS TEA FOR NEW WOMEN
## 
### 
A registration tea for all new and old
women students held under the auspices
of the Y.W.C.A. at the Women's Club
house yesterday afternoon was well at
tended. Introductions were facilitated by
having each person wear a label bearing
her name.
Dean Mary Yost, Harriet Cogswell,
Y.W.C.A. secretary, Mrs. Mudgett, chair
man of the advisory board, and Ruth
Tinslcy, president, stood in the receiving
line.
