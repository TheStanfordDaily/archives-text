# REQUEST SENIORS TO SIGN UP FOR CLASS FOOTBALL
## 
### 
All seniors wishing to play inter
class football are asked to sign up
before the end of the week in order
that suits may be issued and practice
start promptly on Monday. Credit
may be received by signing up for
gym class 101, Seniors not desiring
credit for this activity are requested
to report to Charles W. Davis, Room
47, New Physical Education Building.
All interclass players have been as
sured complete equipment by Davis.
The senior class manager and coach
will be announced later in the week.
After two weeks of practice, the
games will be played. Further infor
mation may be secured by consulting
Dick Holt, senior class president.
