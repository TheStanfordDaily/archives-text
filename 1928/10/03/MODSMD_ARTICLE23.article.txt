# LAW GROUP MEET TO BE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Consideration of plans for the com
ing Law Smoker will be the chief
business of a meeting of the Stanford
Law Association at 1:15 o'clock to
morrow in Room 12 of the Law Build
ing. According to Harry Rathbun,
president, the election of two mem
bers of the Executive Board and a
minor amendment to the constitution
will be the other scheduled items of
business before the session.
