# S.C. GAME TICKETS GO ON SALE TODAY IN A.S.S.U. OFFICE
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stan
ford-U.S.C. football game, which
will take place November 3 at
Los Angeles, will be on sale this
afternoon and tomorrow after
noon from 2 until 5 o'clock, in
the Old Union, according to J. C.
Irwin. Each student may pur
chase one rooter's ticket and
four extras in the preferred sec
tion, and any number of extras
in the non-preferred section.
Applications for tickets for the
U.S.C. game and the University
of Washington game are being
filled as rapidly as possible. The
closing time for applications for
the Big Game is October 15, and
Irwin's office will begin as soon
as possible after that to mail
the pasteboards.
