# LAW ASSOCIATION WILL DISCUSS FIRST SMOKER
## 
### 
Preparations for a smoker will be
made at a meeting of the Stanford
Law Association which is to be held
Thursday afternoon at 1:15 o'clock.
All students registered in the School
of Law are eligible to attend this
first session of the autumn quarter.
The meeting will be held in Room 12.
Two members of the association
will be elected to posts on the Execu
tive Board and an amendment to the
constitution will be considered. Doug
las L. King, '28, was president of the
Stanford Law Association last year.
