# "DYBBUK" CAST HAS TWO STANFORD MEN
## 
### 
Sam Hayes and Paul Bissinger, both
former Stanford actors, have parts in
"The Dybbuk," a play opening in the
Temple Theatre at San Francisco on
Octobor 29. Hayes has the important
role of the mystic messenger, while
Bissinger will be one of the three
batlons.
The sets of the play were designed
by Harold Helvenston, now a mem
ber of the Stanford English faculty,
Bissinger is also director of the
Tomple Players, and he is assisting
Nahum Zemach of New York to con
duct the rehearsals of the unusual
play.
