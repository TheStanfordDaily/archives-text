# MARK TWAIN IS TOPIC OF GRADUATE'S TALKS
## 
### 
Cyril Clemens, Stanford graduate
student and cousin of Mark Twain, is
broadcasting a series of interpreta
tions of his famous kinsman over
KYA on Wednesday afternoons from
4:30 to 4:45 o'clock. Clemens is presi
dent of the Mark Twain Society of
America.
"Roughing It," one of Twain's ear
lier works, will be the subject of his
talk next week. Yesterday Clemens
discussed "Life on the Mississippi,"
written after the humorist's experi
ences as a river pilot.
