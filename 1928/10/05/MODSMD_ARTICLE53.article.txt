# U.S. REJECTS SEAT ON LEAGUE OPIUM BOARD
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 4. (/P)_The
United States government has declined
to participate in the permanent central
board of the League of Nations, set up
by the 1925 Geneva opium convention,
because the convention does not deal ade
quately with the control of production
and distribution of raw opium and cocoa
leaves and their derivatives.
