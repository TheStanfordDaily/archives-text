# TRIALS CONTINUE FOR DAILY BUSINESS STAFF
## 
### 
Sophomores who wish to try out
for the business staff of the Daily
should see James Frank, business
manager, at the Daily office without
delay. More men are especially
needed. Tryoutees will begin collect
ing advertising copy in Palo Alto
next week. Freshmen who desire an
additional year's experience may also
try out at this time.
