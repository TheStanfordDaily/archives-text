# STANFORD STUDENT IS WITH NEW YORK PLAY
## 
### 
William Thornton, former Stanford
student, is now appearing with Wal
ter Hampden in that actor's Broad
way production of "The Light of
Asia." It is a dramatization of the
life of Buddha and is from the pen
of Georgina Jones Walton.
Thornton, who was prominent in
dramatics at Stanford, recently
.starred in a production of "Hamlet"
in Los Angeles. He has also appeared
on the Orpheum circuit and with the
Pasadena Community Players. At
Stanford, he will be remembered par
ticularly for his work as the king in
"Agamemnon." He belongs to Sword
and Sandals honor society and to
Alpha Kappa Lambda fraternity.
