# WOMEN'S DEBATE IS PLANNED WITH CALIFORNIA TEAM
## 
### 
A debate with California women
this quarter is being considered as
the first contest of the year, an
nounces Lorraine Reeder, women's
debate manager. This would proba
bly take place the first wees in De
cember. The topic to be chosen will
be one of popular college Interest,
rather than some world question.
Coaching will be under the direc
tion of some former varsity debater,
who has not yet been seleeteu. Try
outs for the event will be set later
when definite arrangements are made.
