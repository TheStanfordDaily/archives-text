# Hours Tennis Courts Open Now Announced By Bugge
## 
### 
University tennis courts will be
open to students, according to Elwyn
Bugge, tennis instructor, at the fol
lowing hours: Mondays, Tuesdays,
Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays from
8 to 10 o'clock in the morning, and
from noon to 2 o'clock in the after
noon; Saturdays from 8 to 10 o'clock
in the morning and from noon on
through the afternoon. Tuesdays and
Thursdays a limited number of courts
will be available from 8 to 12 o'clock
in the morning.
