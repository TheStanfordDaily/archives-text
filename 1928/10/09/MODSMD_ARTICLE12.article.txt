# VARSITY SOCCER MEN ELECT WALTER FELL TO CAPTAIN ELEVEN
## 
### 
Walter Fell, two-year letterman
and crack right halfback on the
soccer team, was elected captain
of the varsity soccer squad at a
meeting held yesterday after
noon. Due to the ineligibility of
Captain Allison Gibbs, who
graduated last summer, Fell was
acting oeptain in last Saturday's
game with the Palo Alto Post
of the American Legion. During
the contest his play was out
standing.
