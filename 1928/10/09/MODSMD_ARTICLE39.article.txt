# Ram's Head Society Meets Today In English Building
## 
### 
A meeting of the Ram's Head society
will he held at the Green room in the
English building at 5 o'clock this eve
ning, Boh Cecil, secretary of the organi
zation, announces. Committees will be
appointed and plans made for the foot
ball show sponsored annually by the so
ciety. In the absence of A1 Cass, Presi
dent, Gordon Davis will preside.
Rooters' hats will no longer be worn
by members of the women's rooting sec
tion of the University of Southern Cali
fornia.
