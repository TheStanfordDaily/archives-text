# Orchestra Tryouts Held Tonight At 7: 15 O'Clock
## 
### 
Tryouts for positions in the string
or wind instrument sections in the
University orchestra will be held at
the first rehearsal tonight at 7:15
o'clock in Room 84, announces War
ren D. Allen, University organist. C.
A. Kulmann will assist Mr. Allen this
year in conducting the orchestra.

"The Marseillaise" was played pub
licly in Berlin recently for the first
time since the World War.
