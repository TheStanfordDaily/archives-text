# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB TO HOLD LUNCHEONS
## 
### 
Leo Schumacher, president of the
Cosmopolitan Club, announces that
the club will continue this year its
weekly luncheons, to be held at 12:15
o'clock each Wednesday in the alcovfe
of the Stanford Union dining room.
The first luncheon will be held this
noon. All interested persons are in
vited to membership in the club which
is social in its purpose. No dues are
assessed, each participant in any func
tion pays his own share of the ex
pense.
