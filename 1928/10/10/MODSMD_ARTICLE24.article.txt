# First Women's Conference Meeting Scheduled Tonight
## 
### 
Women's Conference will hold its
first meeting of the year at 7:30
o'clock tonight at the home of Dean
Yost.
The meeting will be an important
one, as the offices of treasurer and
secretary are to be elected, and Dean
Yost will have a special message for
the members after the summer vaca
tion, making it essential that every
member be present, says Janet Har
ris, president of Women's Conference.
