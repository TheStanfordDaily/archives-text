Football and
Malted Milks
Any player—varsity or
freshman — who scores
points or blocks a kick in
a scheduled game re
ceives one of Wilson's
famous malts without
charge.
Famous, we say, yet we
have never claimed to
give the biggest malt but
always the best. This
isn't hard to understand
when you know that our
ice cream is 75% richer
in butterfat than the law
requires.
There are three scoops
of ice cream put in every
malt served at the Foun
tain.
