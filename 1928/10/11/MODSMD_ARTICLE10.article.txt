# JONES SELECTED AS MANAGER OF NEXT SPORT DAY
## 
### 
At the first W.A.A. Board meeting
of the year, held Tuesday night, Mary
Edith Jones was appointed manager
of the next Triangular Sports Day,
which will lake place at Mills later
in the quarter. A conference with the
other managers and with the heads
; of the physical education department
will be held in San Francisco next
i Saturday.
The board resolved to buy more
hockey equipment In order that each
woman may have her own stick
I throughout the season. Open hours in
the Roble swimming pool were de
cided upon as follows: tomorrow after
noon from 4:30 to 6:30, and Saturday
morning from 10:30 to 11:30 o'clock.
