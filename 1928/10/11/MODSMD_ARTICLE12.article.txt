# ELECT WILTON HEAD OF SKULL AND SNAKES
## 
### 
Frank Wilton, captain of the base
ball team and varsity halfback, was
elected president of Skull and Snakes,
men's honor society, at a meeting
held yesterday noon at Breakers Club.
Spud Harder, varsity end and out
fielder, was named secretary.
Yesterday's meeting marked the re
tirement of Biff Hoffman as president
of the society. New members are to
be elected at a meeting to be held
sometime within two weeks, it was
said yesterday.
