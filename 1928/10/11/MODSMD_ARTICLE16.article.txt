# GERTRUDE ATHERTON AT MEETING TODAY
## 
### 
Gertrude Atherton, author of "Black
Oxen" and "Immortal Marriage," and
four well-known political leaders will
be present at an open-discussion meet
ing which is to be held at 2:30 this
afternoon at 4G2 University avenue.
All Stanford women are invited to at
tend this meet.
Mrs. Maude Fay Farmington, world
famous prima donna, will be the main
speaker at the round-table discussion
of important questions.
Mrs. Charles Donohoe, Democratic
national committeewoman, Cecelia
Casserly, San Mateo club leader, and
Mrs. Charles Spinks, prominent in
California political circles, are to be
present at the discussions.
