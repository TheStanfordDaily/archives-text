# PROGRAM ANNOUNCED FOR USE OF COURTS
## 
### 
Every effort is being made to pro
vide tennis courts for players for as
large a part of every day as is pos
sible, according to Elwyn Bugge, ten
nis instructor.
Courts for men Btudents outside of
the tennis classes are available from
8 to 10 and from noon till 2 o'clock
on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays,
Thursdays, and Fridays, and on Sat
urdays from 8 to 10 o'clock and all
afternoon. On Tuesdays and Thurs
days a limited number of courts are
available from 2 to 6 o'clock.
