# DEBATERS MEET TONIGHT TO PLAN FOR U.C. CONTEST
## 
### 
In order to prepare particularly for
a debate with the University of Cali
fornia, tentatively scheduled to occur
in Berkeley on the evening of Novem
ber Ist, all members of the varsity
debate squad, including men who won
positions in the recent tryouts, are
urged by Steve Halsted, manager, to
be present at a meeting in Room 201
tonight at 7 o'clock.
A split team will debate on a politi
cal topic at California, it has been
announced, but further details of the
contest are not yet available as nego
tiations have not been completed.
Several other colleges and universi
ties have communicated with Halsted
regarding debates for the near future
but definite developments have not
yet emerged. Plans for immediate pre
paration for possible contests are to
be laid at tonight's meeting, which is
regarded as highly important by the
manager.
Hereafter the debate squad will
meet regularly on Monday evenings
at 7:30 o'clock.
