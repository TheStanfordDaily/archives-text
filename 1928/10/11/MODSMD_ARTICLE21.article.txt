# STORIES ABOUT HOOVER PRESENTED TO LIBRARY
## 
### 
The Stanford Library has received
a collection of stories and anecdotes
concerning Herbert Hoover. Archie
Rice, '95, is the author. He was presi
dent of Hoover's class during his
sophomore year, yell leader during his
first two years, Hoover's predecessor
as student-body treasurer, and editor
of the Daily. Since his graduation, Mr.
Rice has been a journalist and pub
licist in California and New York.
Fifty-six anecdotes are included in
the collection, comprising the first re
sults of a world-wide search for such
i material concerning Mr. Hoover.
Many of the stories are by Hoover's
I classmates, and the majority of them
concern his life at Stanford.
