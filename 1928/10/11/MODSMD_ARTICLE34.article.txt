# 'STANFORD RED' AUTHOR WRITES NEW OPERETTA
## 
### 
Geoffrey F. Morgan, '10, author of
the well-known song, "Sons of the
Stanford Red," has written a new
operetta entitled "Riding Down the
Sky." Geoffrey O'Hara has composed
a musical setting for the new produc
tion, the theme of which is oil, air
planes, romance, and revolution in
Central America.
While at Stanford, Morgan was
editor of the Chaparral, besides being
listed prominently in other activities.
He is also author of "The Belle of
Bagdad," "Tulip Time," "Peggy and
the Piratp," and other operettas
"Riding Down the Sky" Is being
published by C. C. Birchard & Co.,
Boston, Massachusetts.
