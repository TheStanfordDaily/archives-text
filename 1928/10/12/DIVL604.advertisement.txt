THAT IS —
There is that quite irresistible
quality in the music of Gordon
Henderson and his Palm Court
Orchestra which spells Dance
Appeal!
A certain dash... or flair... not readily
found in any other dance aggregation#
The fascination is completed by the
new spring dance floor...the lighting
...in fact, the whole atmosphere.
