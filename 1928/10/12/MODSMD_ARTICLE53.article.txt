# STANFORD RADIO CLUB PLANS STATION CHANGE
## 
### 
At the first meeting of the Stanford
Radio Club Wednesday night plans
were made for changing the station
to a better location. The present
equipment o ftwo 5-watt tubes will be
replaced by a 100-watt transmitter
operating from a 1500-volt motor gen
erator.
All radio amateurs not in the club
are asked to get in touch with Jack
Low, 328 Union, for information.
The next weekly meeting of the
club will be held Monday at 7:30
o'clock at the shack in the Corpora
tion Yard.
