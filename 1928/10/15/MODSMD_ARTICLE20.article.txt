# Sequoia To Give "Winter Palace" Dance On Friday
## 
### 
Members of Sequoia Club are start
ing preparations today for their first
dance of the quarter which is to be
held In Sequoia Hall next Friday
night, October 19.
The dance is to be informal and a
"Winter Palace" theme is to be car
ried out in lighting and decorating.
Don Ruppe is chairman of the com
mittee in charge which consists of
Eric Hill, Marshall Kimball, George
F. Mott, Jr., and John O'Sullivan.
