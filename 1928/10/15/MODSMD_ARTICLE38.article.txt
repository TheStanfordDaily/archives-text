# Freshman I.Q. Found Equal To That Of Army Majors
## 
### 
Dr. W. R. Atkinson, psychology in
structor at Southwestern College, re
ports that the average intelligence of
its freshman class is equivalent to
that of a United States army major.
Relatively speaking, this year's class
is an improvement over former fresh
man classes.
"Two years ago the freshman was
equal to an army sergeant in mental
faculty, last year equal to that of a
captain, and this year equal to a ma
jor," states Atkinson.
