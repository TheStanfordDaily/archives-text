PRESTO!
AND THE UGLY CREATURE CHANGES TO A
BEAUTIFUL CREATION
The Armored skin of the Repulsive Reptile has
been converted by the skillful hands of American
Craftsmen to the most fashionable of street shoes.
Shoes of Service as well as Style and particularly
appropriate for winter wear.
The oxford illustrated is made of genuine Alli
gator, of a rich brown shade. The soles are hand
turned—very flexible.
The price $13.50
