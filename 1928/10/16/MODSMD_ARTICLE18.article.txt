# ENCINA CLUB TO HOLD RE-ELECTIONS TONIGHT
## 
### 
Encina Club will hold a re-election
this evening from 6:30 to 8 o'clock for
all offices except president. Austin Clapp,
quarter-mile swimmer, who was elected
president of the club last Wednesday
evening, was the only candidate to re
ceive a majority.
The candidates who survived the elimi
nations of Wednesday's balloting follow:
For vice-president, Allen and Quigley:
for secretary, Topke and Thigpen; fot
manager, Eichelberger and Kelley; for
dance committee chairman, Rintala and
Terrell; and for smoker committee chair
man, Caddell and Crawford.
