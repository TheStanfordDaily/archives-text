# STANFORD 'LIT' COPY DEADLINE OCTOBER 22
## 
### 
Copy deadline for the Stanford Lit
erary Magazine will be October 22,
according to an announcement yester
day by James Tucker. Short stories,
poetry, book reviews, and other type
of copy of interest to the campus may
be submitted.
Tucker announces that anyone who
wishes to confer with him about
writing or a review may find him in
the Lit office any afternoon.
Woodcutb of four by six inches or
smaller are desired for a frontispiece.
