# Dramatic Office Tryoutees Meet Tonight In Old Union
## 
### 
Ed Smith, dramatic manager, an- '
nounces there will be a meeting of the '
tryoutees this evening at 7 :30 o'clock in '
the dramatic manager's office, Old Union, j
Any sophomore and junior students
who have not previously tried out, and
are interested, are invited to attend this
meeting. Women as well as men may
try out for the position of dramatic man
ager, Smith says.
