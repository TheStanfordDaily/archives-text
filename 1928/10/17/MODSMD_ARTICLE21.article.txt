# STANFORD DAMES PLAN FIRST FALL MEET TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames Club will hold
it 3 first meeting of the fall quarter at
3 o'clock tomorrow afternoon in the
Stanford Women's Clubhouse. All
wives of students are invited to at
tend.
The business program of the meet
ing includes the election of officers
for the year. Following this a musical
program will be given. Mrs. Magni,
who appeared recently in a recital at
Stanford, has been secured to play
the piano. Tea will be served at the
conclusion of the program.
For the particular accommodation
of members who have children, ar
rangements have been made for a
nurse to take care of them during
the meeting.
