# Norman Fain Announces Betrothal To Texas Girl
## 
### 
Norman W. Fain, '28, last year's
boxing captain and light-heavyweight
intercollegiate champion in the Far
Western Conference for two years,
has announced his marriage on Octo
ber 7 to Miss Johnie Lee Parsons of
Fort Worth, Texas.
Fain was president of the Theta Xi
fraternity, and member of the Men's
Council. The marriage ceremony was
performed at the bride's home in
Fort Worth, and the couple will re
side in Prescott, Arizona, where Fain
has a sheep ranch.

So that no one would build next to
him John I). Rockefeller recently
bought 115 acres for $800,000.
