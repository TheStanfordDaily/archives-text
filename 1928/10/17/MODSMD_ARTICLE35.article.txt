# '32 DEBATE TRYOUT IS SCHEDULED NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Tryouts for the freshman debating
team for the autumn and winter quar
ters will be held a week from today
at 4 o'clock in the Little Theatre, ac
cording to an announcement made
yesterday by Leonard Kirkpatrick,
freshman debate coach.
Following the tryouts six men will
be chosen to represent Stanford in
two debates. Men trying out will come
prepared to deliver a six-minute talk
on either Hoover or Smith. Written
briefs are necessary and will be col
lected at the close of the meeting.
Several men of the varsity debating
team and faculty members will be
chosen for judges whose selection has
not been made at present.
