# Science, Religion Will Be Women's Debate Subject
## 
### 
"Resolved, That Advanced Science
Tends to Destroy Theistic Belief" has
been chosen as the subject of the
California-Stanford women's debate
which will be held November 26. Try
outs for the debate have been sched
uled for the 23rd of this month. They
will be held in the Little Theatre, and
tryoutees will give a five-minute talk
on the same subject. As this is the
only intercollegiate activity for wo
men, a large turnout is expected.
