# JUNIPERO SERRA WILL HOLD FIRST MEETING TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Junipero Serra Club, an or
ganization composed of Catholic stu
dents at Stanford, will hold the first
of a series of three meetings tonight
at 7:15 in the Women's Clubhouse.
All students interested are requested
to attend.
According to Frank Carroll, presi
dent of the club, "three short, snappy
meetings" a quarter are to be held in
stead of six as formerly.
The purpose of the meeting tonight
is to get the new members ac
quainted with the old. An effort is
being made to obtain the president
of the Newman Club at the Univer
sity of California as the principle
speaker of the evening.
