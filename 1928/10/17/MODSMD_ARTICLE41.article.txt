# Swain Discusses European Tour At Chemical Meeting
## 
### 
"Rambling Comments on a Summer
in Europe" was the subject of Profes
sor R. E. Swain's talk at the Phi
Lambda Upsilon Research Conference
yesterday afternoon.
He spoke of his travels in France,
Germany, Switzerland, and Italy. Dur
ing this time he spent several days
at the International Union of Pure
and Applied Chemistry at The Hague.
He also spoke of the meeting of the
Eighth Congress of Industrial Chem
ists which he attended while at
Strassburg.
