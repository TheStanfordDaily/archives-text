# Post Drawings In Gym For Annual Tennis Tournament
## 
### 
Drawings for the annual interclass ten
nis tournament have been posted on the
Gymnasium bulletin board. The first
round must be played by Saturday, Octo
ber 20. The player whose name appears
on top of each pairing is responsible for
scheduling the time of the match and the
winner is to post the results.
All men in the second and third var
sity squads are required to play in the
tournament.
This year there are entered forty-eight
seniors, forty-nine juniors, thirty-seven
sophomores, and fifty-five freshmen.
