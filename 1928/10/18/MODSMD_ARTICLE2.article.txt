# ALLEN FEATURES BACH IN RECITAL THIS AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Compositions by Johann Sebastian
Bach only will be played by Warren
I). Allen, University Organist, in his
recital today at 4:15 o'clock in the
Memorial Church. The program is as
follows:

C'horale-Prelude, "A Saving Health to
Us Is Brought."
Andante in B Minor from the Fourth
Trio-Sonata.
Toccata and Fugue in D Minor.
Chorale, "Jesu, Joy of Man's Desir
ing," from the 147 th Cantata.
Sonatina, "God's Time Is the Best."
March from "Dramma per Musica."

The "Andante in B Minor from the
Fourth Trio-Sonata" is one of Bach's
six Sonatas which are chamber music
for the organ, giving the impression
of three instruments playing together.
