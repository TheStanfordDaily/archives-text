# PROFESSOR DAVIS SPEAKS AT MEET OF GEOLOGY CLUB
## 
### 
Professor W. M. Davis, formerly of
Harvard and now of the Stanford
Geology Department, addressed the
Geological and Mining Society Tues
day night on the topic, Remniiscences
of Gilbert and Powell." Gilbert and
Powell, two famous geologists, were
well known by Davis, who had inti
mate contact with them during his
work with the Geological Survey.
Davis gave a resume of his connec
tions with the two men.

Elections for president, vice-presi
dent, and treasurer of the society
were held, the positions being filled
by Robert Loofbourow, Harold L.
Fudge, and Fritz von Estorff, respec
tively. John M. Campbell was elected
secretary last spring quarter.
