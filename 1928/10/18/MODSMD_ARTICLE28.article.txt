# Request First Time Voters To Sign With Hoover Club
## 
### 
Each person who is casting his first
vote in the coming election, and who
intends to vote for Hoover, is re
quested to come to the Hoover Head
quarters, located in the Gotham Shop
Building in Palo Alto. The Hoover
Headquarters Is compiling a list of
new voters which, when completed,
will be sent to Hoover.
All who intend to do so are asked
to come down promptly, as the list
must be sent in within a few days.
