# CLUB TO HOLD PICNIC SATURDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
The first Cosmopolitan Club picnic
this year will be held at Sam McDon
ald's cabin near La Honda Saturday
afternoon.
Cars will leave the postoffice at 3:45
o'clock. The picnic is open to all those
interested. Any who wish to attend

and who have not yet signed up may
call Leo Schumacher, Stanford Union,
or Box 2352, or Alice Behner, Roble
Hall, before Friday afternoon. Those
who own cars are asked to bring
them.
