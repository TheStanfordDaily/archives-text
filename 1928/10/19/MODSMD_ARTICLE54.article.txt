# BRING RECORD PRICE
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 18 (/P) — Sale of
three stock exchange scats was announced
today—one equalling the record price of
$480,000 established a few days ago, and
another slightly cheaper at $475,000. The
price of the third was not announced.
This brings total sales for the week to
five.
Names of fifty-three faculty members
and administrators of the University of
Southern California appear in the new
edition of "Who's Who in America."
