# UNIVERSITY'S ANNUAL NET TOURNEY BEGINS
## 
### 
With 199 players paritcipating, Stan
ford's annual university tennis tourna
ment is now under way, several first
round matches having already been play
ed. The tournament is divided into
classes, the winners of each class tourna
ment playing in the finals.
Charles Davis, director of intramural
sports, has announced that the player
whose name appears on top in the scoring
sheets posted in the gym is responsible
for the playing of the match and re
quests that the winners post the results
as soon as possible. First-round matches
must be played before tomorrow.
