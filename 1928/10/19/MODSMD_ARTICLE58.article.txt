# FOREIGN ART EXCELS AT CARNEGIE EXHIBIT
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH, Pa., Oct. 18 (/P). _
European artists scored over American
painters when they were awarded six out
of eight prizes in the twenty-seventh an
nual Carnegie Institute international ex
hibition of paintings, which opened here
today. Three awards were to French art
ists, two to Americans, one to a Spaniard,
one to an English woman, and one to a
Belgian.
A total of 118 artists, 62 Europeans and
54 Americans, submitted 381 paintings.
The exhibition will continue here until
December 9, after which the European
paintings will be exhibited at Cleveland
and Chicago.
