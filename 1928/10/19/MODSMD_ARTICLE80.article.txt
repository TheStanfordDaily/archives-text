# Cosmopolitan Club Will Hold Picnic at La Honda
## 
### 
A barbecue and picnic at Sam Mc-
Donald's cabin near La Honda is planned
for tomorrow afternoon by the Cosmo
politan Club. Cars will leave from the
post office at 3 :45 o'clock.
About thirty persons have already
signed up for the picnic. Any others
who wish to attend may notify Leo
Schumacher, Stanford Union, or Alicc
Behner, Roble Hall, this afternoon.
