# Bakersfield Well Flowing At Rate of 2500 Barrels
## 
### 
BAKERSFIELD, Cal., Oct 18 (/P)—
Haile dby oil men of Kern County as
one of the greatest strikes ever made in
the Bakersfield area the Fruitvale Xo. 5
uvll of the Pacific Eastern Production
Company is flowing refinable paraffine
base crude oil at the rate of more than
2,500 barrels in 24 hours, it was reported
today.
