# TWENTY TEAMS SIGN
## 
### 
Twenty teams have signed up for
intramural handball, according to an
announcement made by Charles W.
Davis, director of intramural sports,
yesterday. Today at 5 o'clock will be
closing time for organizations to enter
the second intramural sport of the
quarter. Winning teams will be given
points toward the intramural cup,
which was won by El Toro last year.
