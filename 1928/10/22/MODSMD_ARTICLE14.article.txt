# U.S.C. STUNT IDEA CONTEST TO CLOSE AT TWELVE TODAY
## 
### 
All rooters' stunt suggestions
for use at the U.S.C. game must
be in the hands of George Ells
worth, chairman of the selec
tion committee, at noon today
when the contest is scheduled
to close. Lapp, Roberts, and
Wright, in conjunction with the
Rally Committee, will present a
silver trophy to the originator of
the winning idea. This will be
awarded at the U.S.C. game rally
to be held in the Pavilion Wed
nesday preceding the game.
Several suggestions of a politi
cal nature have been received
and cannot be used. According
to Ellsworth, stunts compliment
ary to U.S.C. will be particularly
welcome.
