# Advertising Club to Hear Cherington on "Retailing"
## 
### 
Dr. Paul T. Cherington, of the
Graduate School of Business, will ad
dress the Stanford Advertising Club
of Palo Alto on "The Strange Inter
lude of Retailing" Thursday at G
o'clock at the Cardinal Hotel. Dr.
Cherington was formerly director of
research for the J. Walter Thompson
