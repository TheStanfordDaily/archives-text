# NEW BAND UNIFORMS
## 
### 
The University of Southern Califor
nia band appeared at the game Satur
day equipped with new all-gold uni
forms. Tho gold caps modelled after
a helmet, and the sweaters with their
reversible collars, eight-inch cuffs and
deep waist-bands of cardinal are a
distinct change from last year's uni
foi m.
