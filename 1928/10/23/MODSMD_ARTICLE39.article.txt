# FRATERNITIES ROBBED
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., Oct. 22. (1.P.) —
Fraternity houses 011 the campus at
Oregon State College have had rob
beries amounting to $1200. The rob
bers showed remarkable versatility
of choice. Among the articles on the
list of stolen goods were thirty-five
watches, numerous mantel decora
tions, two pair of B.V.D.'s, a bronze
track medal, and a shot-put medal.
