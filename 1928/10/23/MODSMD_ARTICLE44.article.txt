# German Club Meeting Time Changed to Thursday Night
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the German
Club will be held on Thursday night
in the Women's Clubhouse, instead of
Wednesday, on account of the Wo
men's Conference party. The regular
meetings for the rest of the quarter
will be held on Wednesday nights. All
people interested are welcome.
