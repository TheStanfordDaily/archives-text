### PHILIP SPACE 
Several of the sets for "The Devil
in the Cheese" are said to represent
the interior of a modern girl's mind.
Will the actors wear rubber boots?
A high school in California, we un
derstand, is prosecuting the sockless
leg fad among the girls. The principal
will see ten of the offending members.
Five girls.
Headline in Oregon State Barometer:
CO-EDS WILL MAKE
TRIP TO ARBORETUM
That would only be news here
if the arboretum, like Birnam Wood,
came to the co-ed.
