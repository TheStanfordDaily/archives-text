# Professor Davis to Talk At Meeting of Engineers
## 
### 
"Physiographic Aspects of the Regu
lation of the Mississippi River and
the Great Lakes" is the subject which
Professor W. M. Davis of the Geology
Department will talk on Thursday
evening at 8 o'clock before a joint
meeting of the student chapter, Ameri
can Society of Civil Engineers, and
the civil engineering faculty. The
meeting will be held at the home of
Professor L. B. Reynolds, 853 Middle
field Road, Palo Alto.
