# DR. CHERINGTON WILL SPEAK AT LOCAL AD CLUB
## 
### 
Dr. Paul T. Clierington, Director of
Research for the J. Walter Thompson
Company, New York, will address the
Stanford Advertising Club of Palo
Alto tomorrow night at 6 o'clock in
the Cardinal Hotel. His subject of
address will be "The Strange Inter
lude of Advertising."
Dr. Clierington will soon be at the
Stanford Graduate School of Busi
ness for several months to establish
a course in marketing. He was for
merly a professor of Marketing at the
Graduate School of Business at Har
vard and also the editor of commer
cial publications. In addition lie is an
author of "Advertising as a Business
Force" and "The Consumer Looks at
Advertising."
