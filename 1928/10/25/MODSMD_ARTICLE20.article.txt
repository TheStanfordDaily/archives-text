# Seniors Convene To Enforce Honor
## 
### 
A discussion of the Honor System
will take place at the meeting of the
Senior Class to be held at 7 o'clock
tonight in Room 214, announces Dick
Holt, Senior Class president. Phil
Cavalero and class speakers will take
part in the discussion.
The purpose of the discussion is to
discover means of assisting in the
campaign for its strengthening. Mem
oers of the Senior Class plan to adopt
a course of action resulting in stricter
enforcement of the System which they
helped to inaugurate.
Dick Holt will also announce the
chairmen of the various Senior Class
committees.
