# Euphronia Will Debate on Continued Republican Rule
## 
### 
Whether or not the Republican party
should be maintained in power for the
next four years will be the topic of a
debate between the members of Euph
ronia in the Lobby of the Old Union
Tuesday evening at 7:30 o'clock. The
public is invited to attend.
Two teams of two men each will argue
the question. Myron Tower and Bruce
Wright will uphold the Republicans,
while Manuel Espinosa and Hilarion Tal
avera will oppose them.
