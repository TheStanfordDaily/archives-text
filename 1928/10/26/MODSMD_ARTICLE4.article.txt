# JOURNALISTS DANCE FOLLOWS TIBBETT'S PROGRAM TONIGHT
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi, professional
journalistic fraternity, will spon
sor an all-University dance to
take place at Branner Hall to
night, from 10 to 1 o'clock, im
mediately following the A.S.S.U.
concert featuring Lawrence Tib
bett. The faculty of the Division
of Journalism and their wives
will act as patrons and patron
esses.
The Five Furious Fanatics will
furnish the music. Unusual light
ing effects will form part of the
decorations. The hall is large,
providing plenty of floor space.
Couples will be admitted upon
presentation of a bid which may
be secured at the door for one
dollar.
