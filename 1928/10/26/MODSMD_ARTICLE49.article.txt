# Sociology Professor Finds Favored Days For Suicide
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., Oct. 25. (IP) —
Sunday and Monday are the favored
days for suicides in Seattle, according
to statistics compiled by a sociology
professor at the University of Wash
ington. Based on police and coroner
records for the past ten years, his
figures show that there are three men
i suicides to every woman. The ma
! jority of the men used firearms, and
the women, gas.
