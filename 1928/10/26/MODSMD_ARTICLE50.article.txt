# Excess Drinking Causes Ban on Football Dances
## 
### 
MINNEAPOLIS, Minn., Oct. 25. (/P) |
—Post-football dances at Minnesota I
University sororities today were
banned because of disturbances
caused by drinking among students.
The Dean of Women declared that
students from outside schools abused
open house privileges.
