# Stanford Graduate Dies While East on Business
## 
### 
John F. Newsome, '92, famous mining
engineer, died suddenly Wednesday while
on business in Washington, D.C.
Newsome received his degree here in
1892, and completed his PhD in 1901.
While an undergraduate he won his
Block S as a member of the varsity base
ball team. From 1898 to 1910 he held the
position of professor in the department
of mining.
Since 1910 Newsome has concerned
himself with many mining projects
throughout this country and abroad. His
home was in San Francisco.
