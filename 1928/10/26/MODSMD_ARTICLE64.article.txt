# SALE OF SEATS FOR NEXT CAMPUS PLAY STARTS WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Seats for "The Devil in the
Cheese" will go on sale Wednesday
at 1 o'clock in front of the Wom
en's Clubhouse, according to Ed
Smith, dramatic manager. Everyone
who gets four tickets to "The Devil
in the Cheese" will get a Big Game
Gaieties ticket application which will
entitle him to purchase as many as
four tickets to the Gaieties from a
preferred allotment of tickets.
In the past, students have com
plained of the difficulty in obtain
ing good seats to campus shows,
and this arrangement is being made
whereby students can secure the
very best seats, said Smith. Tickets
will sell for $1 and $1.25.
