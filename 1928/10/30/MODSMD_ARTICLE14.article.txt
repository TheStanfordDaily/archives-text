# REX BELL TO ADDRESS Y.W.C.A. OPEN MEETING
## 
### 
Rex Bell, of the department of citi
zenship, will talk on the "Student
Institute of Pacific Relations" at an
open meeting of the Y.W.C.A. tonight
at 7:15 o'clock.
"Public Opinion, and How It. is
Formed" is the proposed subject for
discussion at the Institute this year.
The organization, of which Bell is
chairman, meets annually at Monte
zuma during the Thanksgiving holi
days, and is entirely in the hands of
students in northern California. Stan
ford has been represented by several
students in past years and has an
active role in the Institute.
