# Fie, Fie, Bates!
## 
### 
Joseph Bates (going up to a bearded
gent who is carrying a pick on his shoul
der) —Are you a Geology Major?
An Old Fool—No, I'm just an old
Miner.
READERS MAY SEND IN THEIR
SACKCLOTH AND ASHES and any
little dainties they might have in the
icebox TO MR. PHILIP SPACE,, Hid
ing-in-the-Hayloft, Stanford Corporation
Yard.
