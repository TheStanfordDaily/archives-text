# Registrar Collects $96 For Study-List Charges
## 
### 
A total of ninety-six dollars has
been collected by the Registrar's Of
fice as fees for changes In study-lists
since the beginning of the quarter.
Thirty-six petitions were granted
without charge, however; while the
fees paid by the less fortunate ranged
from one to eight dollars per person.
Only one petition for a change in
study-list was denied.
Freshmen at Antioch College, Ohio,
voted overwhelmingly in favor of the
continuance of hazing by the sopho
mores.
