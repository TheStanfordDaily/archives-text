# Euphronia Holds Political Debate in Old Union Lobby
## 
### 
Euphronia's intra-society political
debate will be held in the lobby of
the Old Union at 7:30 o'clock tonight
instead of tomorrow, according to an
announcement made by John Cran
ston last night. All members of the
society are asked to attend.
"Resolved, That the Republican
party should be maintained in power
for the next four years," is the ques
tion selected for the debate. Myron
Tower and Bruce Wright will speak
for the affirmative, while Hilarion
Talavera and Allan Beach will oppose
them.
