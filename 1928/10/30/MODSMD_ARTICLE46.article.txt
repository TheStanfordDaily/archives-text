# AMPHIBIAN HOPS OFF
## 
### 
ATLANTIC CITY, N.J., Oct. 29. (/P)
—Repaired and re-fueled, the Flying
Fish, amphibian biplane, with its
crew of three, made its second start in
as many days for Bermuda at 2:30
o'clock this afternoon from the Curtiss
station at the inlet.
