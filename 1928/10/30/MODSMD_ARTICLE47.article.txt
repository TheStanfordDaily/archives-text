# TICKETS ON SALE FOR WEST POINT GAME TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tickets for the Stanford-Army foot
ball game, to be played in New York
City on December 1, will go on sale
tomorrow at the A.S.S.U. office in
present his own student-body card,
: the Old Union. The hours for the
.sale, which lasts only one day, will be
from 10 to 12 o'clock in the morning,
| and from 1 to 5 o'clock in the after
noon.
Each student is entitled to six tick
ets, which are $4 apiece. He must
and purchase only his own allotment
of tickets. He will not be allowed to
use another's student-body card to
purchase twelve seats.
All students will be held personally
responsible for the tickets they re
ceive. If these are procured by scalp
ers, even though it be indirectly and
unknown to the student, he will still
be held responsible.
