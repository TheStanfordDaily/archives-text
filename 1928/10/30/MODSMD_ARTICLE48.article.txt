# Women's Conference Party Postponed For Second Time
## 
### 
The Women's Conference Party
which was postponed last week to to
morrow lias again been put off until
next Wednesday, it was announced
by Alexia McCarthy, chairman of the
social committee for the party. No
definite reason, other than that it
was considered advisable, was given
for the postponement. Women's Con
ference plans to have the same stunts
and skits as formerly planned for the
affair.
