# ROTH TO DISCUSS FOOD SITUATION WITH CLUBS
## 
### 
Another mass meeting of the clubmen
in the Commons to discuss the board
situation with Comptroller A. E. Roth
has been scheduled for tomorrow night
in the club room of El Tigre. The comp
troller will address the assembled club
men.
After dinner at which Comptroller
Roth will be the guest of El Tigre, the
other clubs will gather in the Tigre club
room for a frank discussion of the whole
matter. The comptroller has made no
comment on the situation but the meeting
will be held at his request.
Saturday afternoon a group of club
leaders conferred with the comptroller
and it was decided to carry the discussion
dircctly before the members.
