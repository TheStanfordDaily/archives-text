# STATE AMENDMENTS WILL BE CONSIDERED
## 
### 
Arguments for and against the twenty
one state constitutional amendments to be
voted on next Tuesday will be presented
in a campus open meeting tonight at 7 :30
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse. Dean
Kirkwood of the Law School will preside.
Amendment one, concerning reappor
tionment of the population quota, will be
discussed by Dr. Orrin Leslie Elliott,
Registrar Emeritus. Professor John C.
Almack of the school of education will
talk on amendment six, dealing with state
education. Other amendments will be
considered in groups by local speakers.
