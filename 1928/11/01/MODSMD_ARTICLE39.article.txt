# Theta Sigma Phi Convenes Today to Set Tea Plans
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi will meet this af
ternoon at 5 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. All members are request
ed to be present to discuss plans for
the tea to he given in honor of the
delegates to the High School Press
Association Convention, which will
meet here a week from Friday. The
high school journalists hold their con
vention at Stanford every year, and
the welcoming tea is an annual affair.
