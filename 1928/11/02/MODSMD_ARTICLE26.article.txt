# YOUNG FLIER AT SALT LAKE
## 
### 
SALT LAKE CITY, Utah, Nov. 1.
(fP) —Richard James, 17-year-old avi
ator, of New York, landed at the Salt
Lake airport at 4:35 P.M., today on a
flight from Los Angeles to New York.
Ho left Reno at 9:20 this morning
for Salt Lake City via Elko.
A chapter of Delta Zeta, national
sorority, has recently been installed
at the University of Utah.
