# FOG DELAYS S. F. FERRIES
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 1 (/P).—A
heavy tule fog disrupted San Francisco
bay traffic today, early morning com
muters arriving about ten minutes late.
All boats were slowed down to fug
schedules.
