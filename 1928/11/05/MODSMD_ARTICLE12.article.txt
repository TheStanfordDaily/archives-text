# DAILY TO PUBLISH ELECTION BULLETIN
## 
### 
A Daily bulletin of the latest avail
able election returns, including na
tional and state balloting, will be dis
tributed at the Pavilion immediately
following the Sousa concert tomor
row night, according to an announce
ment by Templeton Peck, Daily editor.
Last minute returns from all over
the United States will be telephoned
to the Stanford Daily from the San
Francisco office of the Associated
Press, and these will be incorporated
in the bulletin.
