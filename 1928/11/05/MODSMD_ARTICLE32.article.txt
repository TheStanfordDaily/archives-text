# BOOKSTORE TO START PROFITS DISTRIBUTION
## 
### 
A distribution of $9000 by the Stan
ford Bookstore will begin Wednesday
morning, November 7, at 8 o'clock.
This amount represents the total of
the rebates of the profit-sharing cou
pons issued during 1927-28. The hours
of distribution are: Wednesday—B to
5 o'clock, Thursday and Friday—lo
to 12 and 2 to 4 o'clock.
There will be several extra clerks
to handle the distribution in an effort
to dispose of the money on the first
day. The Bookstore requests those
■who turned in coupons last year to
get their rebates on Wednesday.
