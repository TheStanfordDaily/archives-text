# WOMEN IN GLEE CLUB, CHOIR MEET THURSDAY
## 
### 
The Women's Glee Club and the
women members of the choir will
meet together Thursday evening at
7 o'clock in the Memorial Church for
rehearsal of the program to be pre
sented on November 18.
This recital will consist entirely of
the choral works of Franz Joseph
Schubert, who died on November 19,
1828. The centennial anniversary con
cert will be performed jointly by the
choir, the two Glee Clubs, and the
Morris Club of Palo Alto.
Work on "The Dream of Mary" is
also progressing and this work will
probably be presented on December
16, in the Memorial Church.
