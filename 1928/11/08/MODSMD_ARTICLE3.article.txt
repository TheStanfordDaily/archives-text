# FINAL TRYOUT FOR GAIETIES CHORUSES IS SET FOR TODAY
## 
### 
Final tryout for the men's and
women's choruses of the Foot
ball Gaieties will be held this
afternoon in the Little Theatre
from 2 to 5 o'clock. This is the
third and last day of the try
outs.
Gordon Davis urges all stu
dents to try out for the choruses
as they are to be a special fea
ture of this year's show. Every
one except freshman women is
eligible for the Gaieties. For the
first time men and women will
be used together in the chorus.
Frances Boone will have charge
of directing the chorus.
