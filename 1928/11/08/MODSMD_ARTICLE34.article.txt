# ALUMNI TO CELEBRATE BIG GAME IN EAST
## 
### 
Celebrations after the Pig Came
this year will reach as far east as
New York, when the alumni of Stan
ford and California will meet in the
eastern city at the Aldine Club to
dine and dance. Play by play reports
of the big Pacific Coast football game,
moving pictures of campus scenes at
California and Stanford, and old col
lege songs are listed on the program.
Alumni of the two universities are
also sponsoring a joint dinner in Chi
cago at the Allerton Club on the day
of the Stanford-California game. All
Alumni who nappen to be in Chicago
at that time are welcome, according to
"William 13. Snow, 23, secretary of the
Stanford Club of New York.
