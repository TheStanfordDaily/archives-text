# SEIDEL CONCERT THIS EVENING IN ASSEMBLY HALL
## 
### 
Toscha Seidel, violinist, will appear
in a concert under the auspices of
the Peninsula Musical Association at
8:15 o'clock tonight in the Assembly
Hall. Tickets are on sale at Crandall's
in Palo Alto.
Seidel is only 29 years old, has ap
peared in concerts in England, Scandi
navia, France, Hawaii, Australia, and
New Zealand, and has been given
great ovations everywhere. The violin
solo part of Brahms concerto is the
favorite of audiences who near Seidel.
The London Daily Mail said in re
viewing Seidel's concert that Kreisler
at his best did not play this part with
more beauty.
