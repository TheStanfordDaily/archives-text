# VICTORY BRINGS DAY OF REST TO HOOVER FAMILY
## 
### 

Stanford's most eminent alumnus and family at their campus home on San Juan Hill, scanning election reports
and telegrams of congratulation from all over the world. Left to right (front row): the President-elect, Mrs.
Hoover holding her granddaughter; back row: Herbert Hoover, Jr., his wife, and Allan Hoover.
