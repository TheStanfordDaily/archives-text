# Early Editions of Daily, Chappie, Other Campus Publications on Display
## 
### 
A collection of the different student
publications which have appeared on
the Stanford campus is on display in
one of the library showcases. Among
them is the lirst "Daily Palo Alto"
(now "The Stanford Daily"), the first
issue of "Chaparral," and an early
number of "The Stanford Alumnus"
(now "The Illustrated Review.").
One of the most interesting of the
evanescent publications is "Josh,"
which was edited by Dewitt H. Gray
of the University of California and
Charles K. Field of Stanford. It was
published in San Francisco and was
"devoted to the humorous interests
of the men and women of Stanford
and the University of California."
There is also an engineering journal
edited In 1897.
