# Alice Putnam, '30, Dies at University of Minnesota
## 
### 
Alice Putnam, '30, died November
8 at the University of Minnesota of
spinal meningitis after a week's ill
ness. Funeral services were held No
vember 9 at Redwing, Minnesota.
Miss Putnam, who was from Boise,
Idaho, spent her freshman and soph
omore years at Stanford, and trans
ferred to the University of Minnesota
this year. She was very active in
student affairs.
