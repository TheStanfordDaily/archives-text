# KING OBSERVES ARMISTICE DAY
## 
### 
LONDON, Nov. 11. (/P)—Under
lowering skies and surrounded by a
thin curtain of fog, King George
joined with thousands upon thousands
of his subjects in the Armistice Day
ceremonies around the Cenotaph in
Whitehall this morning.
