# ALMACK SPEAKS FOR EDUCATION MEETING
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of Pi Lamb
da Theta, women's educational so
ciety, held its National Founder's
Day meeting yesterday in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Professor J. C. Al
mack delivered the address.
Plans were discussed for meetings
to be held the second and fourth
Thursdays of each month, to hear
lectures on the different countries of
the world. Mrs. Hoh of China will
inaugurate the series on the last
Thursday in November. These will
be open meetings in the Women's
Clubhouse.
