# PETERSEN TO EXHIBIT PHOTOGRAPHIC STUDIES
## 
### 
Albert Petersen will open an exhi
bition of artistic photography in the
Stanford Art Gallery, beginning to
day, announces Pedro Lemos. He is
featuring figure studies and land
scapes as well as portraits. Two
years ago Petersen's works were ex
hibited here.
Professor Carl F. Brand will show
his collection of Persian rugs in the
Gallery next week. He will be there
during the week to talk informally to
visitors about the types and periods
of the rugs.
