# STANFORD DAMES CLUB WILL HEAR DEAN YOST
## 
### 
Stanford Dames Club will meet
Thursday afternoon at 3 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Dean Mary
Yost will speak on "Stanford as I See
It." Mrs. John E. McDowell, President
of the Stanford Mothers Club, will
discuss the history and purpose of
the Mothers Club and its plans for
the future. A nurse will be secured
to care for the children of mothers
who wish to attend.
Saturday night at 8:30 o'clock the
Stanford Dames will give a dinner
for the husbands of the members.
This is the first of two such dinners
held each quarter. Bridge will follow
the program.
