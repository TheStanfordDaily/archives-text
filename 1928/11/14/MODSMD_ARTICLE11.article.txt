# Evans, Harkleroad Beat Coelho, Andrews in Case At Moot Court Contests
## 
### 
In a hotly contested case, Evans
and Harkleroad last night defeated
Coellio and Andrews in the second
Moot Court competition of the year.
The case was in the nature of an ap
peal from a trial court in a damage
suit.
Attorney Edwin Hoffman of San
Mateo, who judged the case, rendered
the decision in favor of the respond
ents, upholding the decision of the
lower court.
Another case is scheduled for to
night at 7:30 in Room 12, with Linne
man and Newton meeting Jackson
and Row before Attorney Lewis Hayde
Leve of Palo Alto.
