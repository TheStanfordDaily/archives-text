# MALONEY LEAVES FOR ATHLETIC CONVENTION
## 
### 
Harry Maloney, Director ot' Minor
Sports, left today to represent Stan
ford at the annual convention of the
National Collegiate Athletic Associa
tion to be held in New York Novem
ber 18. 19, and 20. Business for the
Board of Athletic Control will keep
him in the eastern city for a few
days after the convention.
During Maloney's absence from the
campus, Ward Smith, a member of
last year's varsity soccer squad, will
supervise the work of the soccer
teams.
