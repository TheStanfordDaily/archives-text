WEDNESDAY
12:15 p.m.—lnternational Club lunch
eon in alcove of Stanford Union
dining room.
7:00 p.m.—The Women's Glee Club
will meet in Room 84.
7:15 p.m.—Meeting of the Junipero
Serra Club in the Women's Club
house. Short illustrated lecture and
refreshments. All interested are
welcome.

China," a talk by Obed S. Johnson,
instructor in Chinese language and
civilization, will be given at an open
meeting of Euphronia Debating So
ciety in the Lobby of the Old
Union.

DeMolay Club in the Y.M.C.A.
building. New members will be
initiated. Refreslimeus will be
served.
7:30 p.in.—Christian Science Society
will meet on the second floor of
the Women's Clubhouse.
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of the German
Club at Women's Clubhouse. All
those interested are welcome.
7:30 p.m.—An illustrated Lecture, on
"Dinosaurs and the Age of Rep
tiles," will be given by Dr. William
D. Matthew, Professor of Paleon
tology, University of California; in
the Assembly Hall. The public is
invited.
