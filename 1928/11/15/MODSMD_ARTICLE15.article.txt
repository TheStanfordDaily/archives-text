# TICKETS AVAILABLE AT ONE O'CLOCK FOR BIG GAME GAIETIES
## 
### 
Seat sale for the Big Game
Gaieties will begin this after
noon at 1 o'clock in front of the
Women's Clubhouse, according
to Edwin Smith, dramatic man
ager. Students are urged to
come early as there promises to
be a great demand for tickets.
The Football Show, which is
given every year the night be
fore the Big Game will be pre
sented by Gordon Davis in the
Assembly Hall a week from to
morrow night. The show this
year promises several unusual
features and novelties.
