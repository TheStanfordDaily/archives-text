# Woman Crushed To Death In Barn In Iowa Tornado
## 
### 
MANCHESTER, lowa, Nov. 14. (/P)
—Mrs. Alpha Hankinson, HO, was
crushed to death In a barn which was
demolished late today when a tor- |
nado swept throughout lowa. Meagre
reports of the extent of the storm
indicated it struck in successive sec
tions from the center of the eastern
part of the state to the Minnesota
line.
