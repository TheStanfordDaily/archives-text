# You Want a Thousand? Here, Take Two!
## 
### 
"Bankers are not the cold-blooded things they used to be," declares
Floyd A. Allen, assistant to the president of the General Motors Corpora
tion. "They are getting quite warm hearted."
The time is coming, according to Mr. Allen, when applicants for loans
will probably be told that they must take twice what they ask for or
nothing at all. on the principle that banks will not stand for half-way pro
grams of expansion.
Perhaps so. But there will be a very different type of banker in
authority when that day comes around.
