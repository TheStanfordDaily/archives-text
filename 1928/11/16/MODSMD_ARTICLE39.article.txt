# AVIATION SCHOOLS COMBINE
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Nov. 15 (A*).—Merger of
several middle western aviation schools,
including the one where Col. Charles A.
Lindbergh oncc was an instructor, into
a group to he known as the Universal
Flying Schools, was announced today.
