# Luncheon For Sophomore Women To Be Held Soon
## 
### 
All sophomore women who attended
Stanford in their freshmen year are in
vited to a buffet luncheon which will be
held in the Women's Clubhouse on Fri
day, December 7.
There will be someone in charge of
the lists in each living group, and it is
urged that all who plan to come sign up
immediately, as the number must be
known by Wednesday.
