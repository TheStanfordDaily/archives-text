# STANFORD DINNER AFTER ARMY TILT PLANNED IN EAST
## 
### 
Stanford people attending the Stan
ford-Army football game in the Yan
kee Stadium, New York, on Decem
ber first, will gather afterwards at
the Concourse Plaza Hotel to meet
the team and coaches and to join in
a grand reunion and celebration.
Facilities are being provided for
an immense Stanford people's dinner
at the hotel by the Stanford Club of
New York, New Jersey, and Connecti
cut; and even those who are unable
to attend the dinner are invited to be
present for a while after the game
for the purpose of making and re
newing Stanford acquaintances.
The Graduate Manager of the club
has announced that a block of extra
tickets has been secured for Stanford
people in the East.
