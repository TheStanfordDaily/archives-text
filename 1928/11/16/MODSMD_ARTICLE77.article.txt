# "Japan and Korea" To Be Topic of Tuesday Lecture
## 
### 
"Japan and Korea" is the topic of a
lecture to be given by Frederick Starr at
7 :30 o'clock Tuesday evening in the Lit
tle Theatre. The speaker will illustrate
his talk with lantern slides.
Starr, who is a student of social prob
lems in the Orient, is an anthropologist
of note and has traveled extensively in
Japan, Korea, and Mexico.
