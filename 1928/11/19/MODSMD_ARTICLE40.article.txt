# DeMille, Mayer, Talbot Arrive in Fokker Plane To Visit President-Elect
## 
### 
Cecil B. DeMille, motion picture di
rector, Louis B. Mayer, Metro-Gold
win-Mayer official, and John Talbot,
Richfield Oil Company representa
tive, paid Herbert Hoover a short
call Friday afternoon when they flew
from Los Angeles to the Palo Alto
aviation field in the Richfield Fokker
tri-motored monoplane.
Arriving shortly after 2 o'clock, the
party had a conference with Hoover
and were gone again in less than an
hour's time. They were to spend the
night at the Hearst ranch, and con
tinue their flight to Los Angeles next
morning.
