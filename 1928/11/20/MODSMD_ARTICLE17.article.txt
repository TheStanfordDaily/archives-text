# Students May See Dress Rehearsal of Gaieties On Thursday Afternoon
## 
### 
For the great host of disappointed
ticket seekers to the football Gaieties
Friday night, a special performance will
be given Thursday afternoon in the As
sembly Hall. It will be in the form of a
dress rehearsal, including all of the reg
ular program, and will be practically an
exact duplicate of Friday night's enter
tainment. Tickets to the afternoon per
formance will be seventy-five cents. Ac
cording to Edwin Smith, dramatic man
ager, the whole house for the Gaieties
was sold out by Thursday afternoon of
last week. So many were unable to ob
tain tickcts that a good house is expected
for the rehearsal Thursday afternoon.
