# NEW STANFORD SONGS
## 
### 
At the request of the Rally Commit
tee, the Daily has published the words
to the two newest Stanford songs:
"Giants in Red," and "Stanford For
ever." Students are asked to memo
rize these words as they will both be
:sung at the Rig Game and at the
Rally tomorrow.
GIANTS fN RED
Stanford is ready, ready one and all,
Sturdy and steady, waiting for the
call! rah! rah! rah!
With every Cardinal primed to do or
dier —
Once more the victory song will thun
der through the sky.
CHORUS
Giants in Red, forever fighting arm
in arm,
Definance is bred, in ev'ry heart down
on the Farm (keep fighting),
Vict'ry must come, the Cardinal spirit
will not yield,
Sons of Stanford conquer, and march
triumphant from the field.
—Words and music by
J. B. Leam?.n, '13.
STANFORD FOREVER
On, men of Stanford, the vict'ry
awaits you;
Your foes shall tremble as your valor
they see.
On, loyal sons of the all-conqu'ring
Cardinal,
"Stanford Forever" rings our cry of
victory.
