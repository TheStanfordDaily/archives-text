# Hoover's First Map Will Hang in Geology Library
## 
### 
The first map that Herbert Hoover
made as a beginning student in
geology has been retrieved from the
archives of the Geology department.
It is being framed and will be hung
in the Geology Library. In comment
ing on the map. which is a topogra
phic chart of the Stanford campus,
Doctor Eliot Blackwelder of the Geo
logy department said, "It's a pretty
good map."
