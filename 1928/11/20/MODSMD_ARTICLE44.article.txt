# REGINALD DENNY TO REMARRY
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 19.
nald Denny, film star, and his leading
lady, Betsy Lee, appeared before the
marriage license bureau here today
and filed a notice of intention to
marry.
Denny told reporters that he and
Miss Lee had planned their marriage
for November 24, the day on which
his divorce decree from his wife be
comes final.
