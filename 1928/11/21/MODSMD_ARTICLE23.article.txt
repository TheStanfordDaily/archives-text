# To Hold Sophomore Play Tryouts Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
Sophomore play tryouts will be held
today and tomorrow in the Little
Theatre. Gordon Davis will hold the
tryouts from 4 to 6 o'clock today and
from 7 to 9 o'clock tomorrow evening.
The play, according to Davis, is
one of mystery WJth many comedy
situations and thrills. All sophomores
are urged by him to come out for the
1 tryouts.
