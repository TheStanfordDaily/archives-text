# DAILY TO PUBLISH SPECIAL BIG GAME EDITION TOMORROW
## 
### 
Five hundred extra copies of
the Big Game edition of the
Daily which will appear on the
campus tomorrow morning, will
be printed and sold to those stu
dents desiring them. The charge
will he ten cents apiece.
Tomorrow's paper will be the
largest of the year. It will in
clude four pages of sports fea
turing a story on the Big Game
written by the sport editor of
the Daily Californian, and state
ments from Captain Phillips and
Coach Nibs Price, four pages of
pictorial section, as well as eight
pages of the main news section.
Students wishing to have extra
copies of the Daily are asked by
James Frank, business manager,
to purchase them at the Daily
Office any time tomorrow.
