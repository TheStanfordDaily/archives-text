INDIANAPOLIS, Ind., Nov. 22
(PIP). —The dominant feature of the
new campus of Butler University is the
fact that all the officcs, class-rooms, and
even laboratories are housed in one build
ing. There are fifty-four class-rooms in
addition to the administration offices, the
college of liberal arts and religion, the
library, the science hall, laboratories, do
mestic sciences and arts, and a botanical
conservatory which consists of an her
barium and a greenhouse.
