# POLO PRACTICE AT OREGON
## 
### 
OREGON STATE COLLEGE, Cor
vailis, Nov. 22 (PIP) —Polo practice is
under way here. Cage work is the pre
liminary work schedule for a few weeks,
hour letter men are back from the squad
of last year that won four of five con
tests, losing' one of two games played
with Stanford University.
