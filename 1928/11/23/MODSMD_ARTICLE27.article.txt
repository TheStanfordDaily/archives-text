# MEN'S DORMITORY TO BE OPENED IN JANUARY
## 
### 
Bowles Hall, the first dormitory to be
owned by the University of California,
is practically completed, and will be
opened for men students with the begin
ning of the spring semester in January,
according to Robert G. Sproul, vice
president and comptroller of the Univer
sity, and chairman of the administrative
board for the dormitory.
The dormitory will accommodate ap
proximately 105 men. There will be 46
suites of two bedrooms and study, two
suites of bedroom and study and 11 single
rooms. The rates for the new dormitory
have not been announced, but they will
include both board and room.
