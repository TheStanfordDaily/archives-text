# TRADITIONS UPHELD
## 
### 
UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON,
Nov. 22 (/■P) —Sophomores at the Univer
sity of Washington decided at a recent
meeting that, with the consent of Presi
dent M. Lyle Spencer who is absent from
the Washington campus at the present
time, frosh traditions will be strictly en
forced. Freshmen will all wear'green
hats, will be prohibited from standing on
the steps of the Denny, will not be per
mitted to walk or talk with women on
the campus, and will be allowed to smoke
only corn-cob pipes, if President Spencer
agrees to the sophomore plans.
