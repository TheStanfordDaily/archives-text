# SEAMEN AWARDED DAMAGES
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 25. (/I 1 ) —Throe
seiimon, injured more than two years
ago in an explosion during the filming
of the picture "Old Ironsides," will
receive $28,041 damages from the
Famous Players-Lasky Corporation, it
was learned here today.
