# The 						Proud Old Tree Might Die of Shame
## 
### 
The Daily wants to Ik- one of the first to rise and defend the name
of Palo Alto, as against that of Hoover, as a designation for our neigh
boring village. Some upstart called Bertram M. Bell, a Chicagoan, has
written to Governor Young urging that he ask the next session of the
legislature to pass an act changing the name of what is wrongly called
"Hoover's Home Town."
