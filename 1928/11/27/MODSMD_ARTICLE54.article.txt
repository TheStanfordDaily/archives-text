# Green, Companion Recover From Automobile Injuries
## 
### 
Paul J. Green, senior registered
from Inglewood, and Miss Virginia
Peery, 571 Hamilton Avenue, Palo
Alto, were recovered yesterday from
minor injuries received Saturday
night when their car crashed into a
tree near San Mateo. Both were
treated at the Mills Memorial Hos
pital for slight 6uts and bruises.
Green said the accident occurred
when he temporarily fell asleep while
driving.
