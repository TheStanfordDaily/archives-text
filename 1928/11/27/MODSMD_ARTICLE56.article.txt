# Kappa Alpha, Sequoia To Play For Handball Title
## 
### 
Kappa Alpha and Sequoia I will
meet in the championship match of
the 1928 intramural handball tourna
ment at 1 o'clock this afternoon. At
the same time Phi Sigma Kappa and
Theta Delta Chi will battle for third
place.
The Kappa Alpha team is composed
of Davis and Yoakum, while Souter
and James represent Sequoia. The
winner will be decided by victories
in two out of the three matches, one
doubles and two singles. Awards are
points toward the intramural cham
pionship and medals for the indi
vidual winners.
