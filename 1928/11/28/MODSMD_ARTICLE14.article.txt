# CALIFORNIA WINS SECOND DEBATE FROM STANFORD
## 
### 
Upholding the negative of the ques
tion, "Resolved, That Advanced Sci
ence Tends to Destroy Theistic
Faith," the Stanford women's debate
team last night lost an audience de
cision to California at Berkeley.
On a ballot after the debate the
majority of the audience indi
cated a change of its opinion from
the negative to the affirmative. Violet
Crosbie and Alice Behner made up
the Stanford team, and Carol McCam
mon and Lynn Rountree debated for
the University of California.
Monday nighty a Stanford team,
taking the affirmative of the same
question, won a decision over the
Berkeley team here. Francis Marshall
and Eleanor Davidson coached the
Stanford teams.
