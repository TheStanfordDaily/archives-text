# Intramural Track Tryouts Next Wednesday Afternoon
## 
### 
Tryouts for the intramural track meet
will be held next Wednesday at 4:30
o'clock on the old oval. Teams who in
tend to compete must have four contest
ants at the tryouts in order to he eligible
for intramural points. Those teams who
have not entered their men yet should
see Charles W. Davis at once.
Finals in the intramural meet will be
held Thursday, December 6.
