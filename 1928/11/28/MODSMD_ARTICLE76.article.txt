# Polo Practice Scheduled For Three O'Clock Today
## 
### 
Polo players on both the varsity and
"B" squads are to report for practice to
day at 3 o'clock, announces Captain Rich
ard A. Gordon. Because of Thanksgiv
ing holidays, many team members are
leaving for the week-end, and it is neces
sary to combine the teams to have enough
men to play a few chukkers.
On Friday the two squads will work
out together at 1:30 o'clock.
Not a single man turning out for
freshman and varsity sports at the
University of Washington received
one of the six-week notices of poor
work sent out by the registrar's of
fice.
