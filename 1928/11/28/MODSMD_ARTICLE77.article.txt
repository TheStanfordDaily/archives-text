# FIRST ROUND OF MOOT COURT CASES FINISHED
## 
### 
Moot Court competition in the first
round was finished last night when two
cases were held in the Law building.
Draper and Vincenti were awarded the
decision over Lawson and Scully by At
torney John C. Dunniway. Dunniway is
a Stanford graduate and is with Gushing
and Cushing, attorneys, in San Francisco.
In the case of Baker and Cook vs.
Zitkowski and Truman, under Attorney
Everett S. Laymon of San Francisco,
the decision will be rendered later, as the I
attorney was in doubt about some of the
points of the case. i
