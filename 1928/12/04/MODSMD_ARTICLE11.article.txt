# NINETEEN STUDENTS ELECTED MEMBERS OF PHI BETA KAPPA
## Women Lead Men in Latest Election to National Honor Fraternity 
### 
Phi Beta Kappa, National Honorary
Scholastic Fraternity, yesterday an
nounced the election of nineteen stu
dents to membership in the organiza
tion. There are nine men and ten wo
men among this year's members. Only
twelve students were elected last
year.
The new members and their homes
follow:
Norman W. Applegarth—Palo Alto.
William M. Bradley—San Diego.
Rose Brecher —San Jose.
Lucy M. Evans —Nashille, Mich.
Milton M. Hartman —San Francisco.
Judith V. Hedges—Oregon City, Ore.
Dinifred L. McClatchie —Palo Alto.
Sally Miller —Porterville.
Richard A. Ogg—Palo Alto.
Yoshio Ouchi —Fuknoka, Japan.
Mary E. Raney—Morgan Hill.
Milton Routt —Palo Alto.
Mary L. Schofleld —Palo Alto.
Arthur F. Scotten —Pasadena.
Mary Thygeson—Palo Alto.
Ruth E. Tinsley—Kansas City, Mo.
Irene L. Way—Petaluma.
Orby C. Wheeler —Amarillo, Texas.
