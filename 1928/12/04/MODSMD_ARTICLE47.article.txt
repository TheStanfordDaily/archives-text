# JUNIOR CLASS WOMEN DINE AT LOCAL HOTEL
## 
### 
Women of tlie junior class will hold
their monthly dinner at the Cardinal
Hotel tomorrow night, starting at 6
o'clock, and all those who have signed
up previously for the dinners and can
not attend tomorrow night must let
Frances Shoup know by G o'clock this
evening. The price of the dinner will
be $1.00.
The custom of holding a dinner on
the first Wednesday of every month
was started when 23 junior women
spent a week-end together at Asilo
mar at the beginning of the quarter.
