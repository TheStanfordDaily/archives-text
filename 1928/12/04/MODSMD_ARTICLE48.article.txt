# Third Child Born To Wife Of 1923 Football Leader
## 
### 
A daughter was born to Mrs. D. S.
DeGroot, wife of Dudley S. DeGroot,
captain of Stanford's football team
in 1923, in the Palo Alto Hospital

yesterday.
Mrs. DeGroot was formerly Miss
Alice Roth and the baby is a niece of
Comptroller Roth and of Dr. A. E.
Roth of Palo Alto. It is the DeGroots'
third child.
