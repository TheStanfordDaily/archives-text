# WAGNER PIECES ARE FEATURE OF ORGAN PROGRAM
## 
### 
A Wagner program will be featured
by Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock
in the Memorial Church.
More requests come for Wagner
than any other composer, except
Bach. Evidently some of the magical
effect of the master's orchestration
gets to the listener, for the transcrip
tions of excerpts from the music
dtamas are very difficult. The pro
gram is as follows:
Selections from "Lohengrin"
Prelude
Prayer of the King
Elsa's Bridal Procession to the
Minister
Introduction to Act 111
Traume (Dreams) (A preliminary
study for "Tristan and Isolde"
Act II)
March from "Die Meistersinger"
