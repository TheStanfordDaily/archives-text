# STAGG NOT TO RETIRE
## 
### 
' CHICAGO, Dec. 4. (/P)—"Retire?
Absurd! I'm a young man!"
This is what Coach Amos Alonzo
Stagg, the "Grand Old Man" of foot
ball, told the Associated Press today
i in answer to the persistent rumors
i that he planned to call it a day—his
! last day—on the gridiron.
