# KITTREDGE WILL TALK TO ENGINEERS' SOCIETY
## 
### 
F. A. Kittredge, chief engineer of the
National Park Service, will speak before
the Stanford Student Chapter of the
American Society of Civil Engineers to
night. "Variety of Engineering Prob
lems in the National Park Service" will
be Kittredge's subject at this, the third
social meeting, which is to be held in
the Phi Gamma Delta house at 8 o'clock.

Invitations have been extended by the
Chapter to men eligible for membership,
and any man with upper division or
graduate standing is invited to be pres
ent. Refreshments will be served.
