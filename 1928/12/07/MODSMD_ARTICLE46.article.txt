# CHRISTMAS VESPERS TO BE HELD SUNDAY
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. will sponsor an infor
mal Christmas vesper service for the
men and women of the campus to be
held Sunday at 5 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.

Professor Frederick Anderson of the
Romanic Languages Department will
read a Christmas selection. The Stan
ford Women's Glee Club will offer sev
eral Christmas songs, and carols will be
sung by those in attendance in an in
formal gathering around the lire.

The Y.W.C.A. cordially invites all
men and women to the vespers.
