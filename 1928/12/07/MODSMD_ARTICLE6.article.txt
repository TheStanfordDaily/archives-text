# WET TOWEL NEWEST MICROPHONE DEVICE
## 
### 
LONDON, Dec. 6 (/P). Sir Harry
Lauder has "invented" a device to im
prove the broadcasting of the voice.

It involves nothing more costly than
a towel soaked in water. The towel is
placed over the microphone and lias the
effect of emphasizing the singer's voice,
and at the same time, subduing the sound
of the orchestra.
