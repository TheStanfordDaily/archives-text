# TROJAN MANAGER GOES EAST
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Dec. 6 {/P).—Gwynn
Wilson, general manager of the Univer
sity of Southern California, was en
route east today bound for South Bend
and conferences with Knute Rockne and
Notre Dame officials over the 1929 foot
ball meeting of the two institutions.
