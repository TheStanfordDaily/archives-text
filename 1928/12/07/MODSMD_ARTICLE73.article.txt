# Brockhagen Named Editor Of U.C. Daily Californian
## 
### 
BERKELEY, Dec. 6 (#>).—Frederick
Brockhagen of Oakland was named edi
tor of the Daily Californian, University
of California student newspaper, today.
Frederick J. Lawless of Riverside was
appointed sports editor and Harold L.
Clioate of l'lacerville, manager. Marian
Donnellan was named women's editor.
