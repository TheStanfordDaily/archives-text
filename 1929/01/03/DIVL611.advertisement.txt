CLASSIFIED ADS
All classified advertisements
must be received at the busi
ness office of The Stanford
Daily by 6 p.m. day previous
to publication. Advertisements
must be paid in advance.
EUROPEAN TOI T R —Summer of 1929.
Stanford University party will visit
Italy, Austria, Germany, Switzer
land, France, Belgium, and Eng
land. For particulars see Prof. C. F.
Brand, Office 230c.
I think that we are getting now on
the right track. Herbert Hoover
knows how to do things—and you
seem to know also.
Very truly yours,
David Starr Jordan.
Sixty of the seventy-eight law gradu
ates of the University of Oklahoma last
year are now earning their livings in
some branch of their profession.
