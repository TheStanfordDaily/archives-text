# NATIONAL ASSOCIATION TO DIRECT S.F. SHOW
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 2. (/P) —A
national horse show to be conducted
under the auspices of the San Fran
cisco National Horse Show Associa
tion will be held here February 2 to
9, it was announced here today by
Edward J. Tobin, president. Tobin
said purses and trophies will be pro
vided on a scale that will bring to
San Francisco tho finest animals from
the finest stables in the nation. Horse
shows held here previously have been
lccal or regional.
