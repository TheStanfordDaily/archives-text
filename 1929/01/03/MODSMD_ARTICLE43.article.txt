# OUTSTANDING U.C.-GEORGIA TECH WARRIORS OF FAMOUS NEW YEAR'S ROSE BOWL BATTLE
## 
### 

Outstanding figures of the football year, in the lineups of the Golden Tornado and Golden Bears, which clashed at Pasadena to determine gridiron
supremacy between the two seaboards. Left, Mizel, Tech halfback; large head, Phillips, California end; upper center, Lorn, California half; lower,:
Pund, Tech center; upper right, Watkins, Tech tackle; lower right, Schmidt, California fullback.
