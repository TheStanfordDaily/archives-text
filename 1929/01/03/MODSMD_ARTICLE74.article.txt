# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
2:00 p.m.—Junior football managers
will meet in Room 41 of the Physi
cal Education building. All fresh
men wishing to try out for football
manager should attend the meeting.
7:3op.m.—Stanford Choir will meet
in the Chapel for the first rehearsal
of (he quarter. All students wishing
to join should report.
FRIDAY
5:00 p.m.—Harry Maloney will meet
all men interested in varsity boxing
at the boxing gallery. Plans for the
coming boxing season will be dis
cussed.
WEDNESDAY
3:00 p.m.—Tryouts for winter quarter
debating squad will be held in the
Little Theatre.
MISCELLANEOUS
All sophomores desirini: to try out for
junior baseball managerships meet
at Board of Athletic Control build
ing, Thursday, January 3, at 2 p.m.
