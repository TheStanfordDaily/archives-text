# DOOMED NUN IS ILL
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Jan. 3. (/P)— The
Nun Concepcion, sentenced to twenty
years' imprisonment, in connection
with the assassination last July of
General Alvaro Obregon, was re
ported today to he seriously ill at the
penitentiary here.
