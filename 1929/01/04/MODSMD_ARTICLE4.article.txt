# BUSINESS STAFF OF DAILY TO CONTINUE TRYOUTS FOR WEEK
## 
### 
Freshmen seeking an activity
providing business experience
are urged by James Frank, busi
ness manager of the Daily, to
try out for the managerial staff
of the paper. Winter quarter try
outs will continue through next
week. Experience is offered in
preparing advertising copy and
in sellng space, and members of
the staff also are enabled to
come into contact with business
men of San Francisco and the
Peninsula. Membership on the
staff will lead in two years to a
salaried position.
