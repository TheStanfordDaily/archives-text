# Summer Quarter Courses Are Announced In Booklet
## 
### 
Announcement of courses for the
1t)29 summer quarter have just come
from the press and are now ready for
distribution. They may be obtained at
the office of the Registrar or from
the office of the Summer Quarter Di

list of graduate and undergraduate

summer term. Information concern
