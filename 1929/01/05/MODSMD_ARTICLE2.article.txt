# HOLBROOK TO BE SUMMER SCHOOL LAW PROFESSOR
## 
### 
Evans Holbrook, a graduate of
Stanford University and professor of
law at the University of Michigan
Law School, has been appointed act
ing professor of law for the 1929 sum
mer session, it was announced yester
day.
Holbrook received his A.B. from
Stanford University in 1897 and con
tinued his studies at the Northwestern
University for a year, finally return
ing to Michigan, where he took his
LL.B. in 1900. From 1900 until 1905
he practived law in Chicago. He was
then appointed professor of law to
the University of Michigan, where he
has been since except when in 1918
he taught at the University of Cali
fornia for a year.
