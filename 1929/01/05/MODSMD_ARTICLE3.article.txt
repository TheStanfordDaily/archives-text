# STUDENTS' TICKETS FOR CARD-BEAR TILT GO ON SALE TODAY
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the sec
ond California-Stanford basket
ball game to be held in the
Pavilion Saturday will go on
sale this morning from 9 to 12
o'clock and from 1 to 5 o'clock
in the A.S.S.U. offices in the Old
Union. All seats are to be re
served at the price of 50 cents
and can be procured by the pre
sentation of student body tick
ets.
Because of the limited seating
capacity of the Pavilion there
only will be available seats for
appoximately half of the student
body. No general admission
tickets will be sold. Any remain
ing seats will be sold to students
and alumni for $1. Arrangements
have been made to allow any
student to purchase one rooters'
ticket for each student body card
presented.
