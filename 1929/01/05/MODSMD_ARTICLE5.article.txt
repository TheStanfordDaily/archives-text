# CAP AND GOWN PLANS BRIDGE TEA SATURDAY
## 
### 
A bridge tea to raise money for the
Cap and Gown scholarships will be
given by Cap and Gown at 2 o'clock
Saturday in the Women's Clubhouse.
Refreshments will be served and
fresh dates, candy, and nuts will be
sold. Prizes may be obtained from a
grab bag In order that each table may
have one.
The scholarships for which the
money is being raised has been spon
sored by the organization for four
years. Two scholarships of $300 are
given each year to defray the tuition
expenses of two women whom the
Cap and Gown consider in need of aid.
