# Debate Aspirants To Give Five-Minute Tryout Talks
## 
### 
Five-minute talks by men who wish
to try out for places on the debating
squad will be heard in Room 201
Wednesday afternoon between 3 and
5 o'clock, according to an announce
ment made by Wheeler Barger, as
sistant debate coach.
Debaters aspiring to squad posi
tions will speak on the topic, "Re
solved: That the plea of temporary
insanity in defense of crime should
be prohibited by law."
