# CALIFORNIA ELECTORS CAST THIRTEEN VOTES FOR HOOVER
## 
### 

The electors of Herbert Hoover's home state cast their ballots for him for president at the state capitol in
Sacramento. The votes will be forwarded to Washington in this specially designed box. They are, left to right:
John A. O'Connell, Ben R. Meyer, C. P. Rendon, Fred H. Schauer, Rolfe Thompson, Harry Woolff, Mrs. Harriet
W. Works, A. W. McPherson, Gov. C. C. Young, Ry. Williams, Miss Aurelia Reinhardt, Marshall Demotte, James
F. Collins, State Senator L. L. Demett, Frank F. Merriam, Chairman Republican State Central Committee.
