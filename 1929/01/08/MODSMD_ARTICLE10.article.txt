# PRESS SALES MANAGER GOES EAST FOR MEET
## 
### 
David Lamson, sales manager for
Stanford University Press, left yes
terday for New York, where he will
attend a meeting of university press
representatives and the annual meet
ing of the National Association of
Book Publishers, of which the Stan
ford press is a member. He will also
visit Chicago, Buffalo, Boston, New
Haven, Philadelphia, and Washington,
D.C., in the interest of books pub
lished by the Press, returning to the
campus about mid-February. Mrs.
Lamson is accompanying him on the
trip.
