# Will Give Out Invitations For Record Rush Period
## 
### 
The first period of rushing ended
yesterday, and invitations for the sec
ond period will be distributed at Roble
tomorrow morning. Today and tomor
row there will be no rushing, and
the second and last period will begin
with a dinner date Thursday evening,
lasting until Tuesday night, January
15. No date will be considered a pre
ference date, according to custom.
Bidding and pledging will take place
on January 16.
