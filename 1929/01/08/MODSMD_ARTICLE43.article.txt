# TRYOUTEES ASKED FOR BOXING MANAGERSHIPS
## 
### 
Sophomores interested in trying out
for the position of manager of the
varsity boxing squad are asked by
Wareham Seaman, boxing manager,
to report to the boxing room in En
cina gym tomorrow afternoon at 5
o'clock. The work for this year will
consist of caring for the equipment,
taking care of visiting teams, and
other such duties. Two junior man
agers will be chosen from the sopho
more tryoutees, and one of these will
be manager of the team in his senior
year.
The boxing squad increased to 60
men this afternoon when fifteen new
men reported to Coach Harry Ma
loney. Maloney expects to divide his
squad into two sections in order to
handle the men more efficiently.
