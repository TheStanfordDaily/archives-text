# ASSISTANTS IN RECORD-SMASHING FLIGHT
## 
### 

These four men manned the refuelling ship which transferred gasoline
and oil to the Question Mark while it circled over southern California to
shatter all air endurance records. Left to right: Lieut. Odas Moon, "Scotty"
Duthie, Lieut. Frederick Salter, and Lieut. J. G. Hopkins.
