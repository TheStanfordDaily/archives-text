# LAST PERIOD OF RUSHING SEASON BEGINS TONIGHT
## 
### 
The second and last period of wo
men's rushing will begin with dinner
tonight, and will continue until Tues
day night, with bidding taking place
on Wednesday. There will be no pre
ference dates.
Invitations for the second period
were distributed at Uoble yesterday.
The first period ended Monday night,
and Tuesday and yesterday there was
no rushing. Luncheon dates are from
12:15 to 1:30 o'clock, and dinner from
6 to 8:15 p.m., except on Sunday
when dinner will be from 1 to 2:30
o'clock, and supper from 6 to 8:15.
Wednesday, January 16, will be a
day of silence, when no sorority wo
man may communicate with any
freshman. Bidding last year took
place between 7 and 9 p.m. It is not
known at what hours it has been
planned this year.
