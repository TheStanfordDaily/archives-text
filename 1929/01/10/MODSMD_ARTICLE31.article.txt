# Fifteen Matches Planned By Rifle Club in Winter Quarter; Two This Week
## 
### 
Since winter quarter is to be the busi
est for the Stanford Rifle Club, there
being fifteen matches scheduled within
this period, members arc requested to do
as much rifle practice as possible. This
was the announcement made Tuesday
night at the club meeting by Jack
Crawford, president.
Saturday noon, January 12, has been
set as the final limit for completing the
matches with Pennsylvania and Califor
nia Tech. One match will be fired next
'week, and three the following week.
Monday, Tuesday, and Wednesday af
ternoons from 1 to 4 o'clock have been
designated as practice periods for women,
while Tuesday and Friday afternoons and
Saturday morning have been set aside for
the men.
