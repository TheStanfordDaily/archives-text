# FROSH, FRATERNITY MEN PLAN SMOKER TONIGHT IN ENCINA
## 
### 
All freshmen and fraternity
men are urged to attend a smoker
to be held in the lobby of En
cina Hall from 7 to 9 o'clock to
night. The affair is sponsored by
the Interfraternity Council.
The purpose of the smoker, as
outlined previously, is to better
acquaintanceship between fresh
men and Row men before the
regular rushing season begins
Spring Quarter. According to ad
vice received from the Council,
there is to be no actual rushing
at affairs of this kind.
An orchestra has been ob
tained to provide entertainment
for those who attend.
