# CAP AND GOWN PLANS BENEFIT BRIDGE PARTY
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, national women's
honor society, will give its annual
benefit bridge in the Women's Club
house on Saturday, February 9, ac
cording to an announcement made
today.
The proceeds from the bridge party
will be used to est.iblish two scholar
ships for women. Reservations at two
dollars per table may be obtained
from Blanch Barnett at the Gamma
Phi house, Edith Wright at Roble,
Mrs. John C. Branner, and Mrs. C. G.
Allen in Palo Alto.
