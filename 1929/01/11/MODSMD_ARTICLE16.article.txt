# GEOLOGY SOCIETY TO HOLD SMOKER NEXT WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
The Geological and Mining Society
invites all undergraduate students of
the departments of Geology, Mining,
and Metallurgy, as well as all others
interested, to attend a smoker in the
lobby of the Old Union at 7:30 o'clock
on Wednesday, January 16.
Mr. Edward Nutter, chief engineer
of the Minerals Separation, North
America Corporation, will speak on
approaches to modern metallurgical
problems. Father Hubbard of the Uni
versity of Santa Clara will give an
illustrated lecture on the geology of
Alaska, which country he has recent
ly visited. A couple of bouts will be
staged by members of the varsity
boxing squad between speeches. Ad
mission will be free.
