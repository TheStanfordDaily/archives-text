# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
9:00 a.m.—All University Assembly.
Classes at this hour dismissed.
SUNDAY
6:00 p.m.—Regular meeting of Stan
ford Episcopal Club to be held in
the parish house, 425 Hamilton
avenue. All students invited to at
tend.
6:15 p.m.—Dr. A. T. Murray of the
Department of Classical Literature
will address the College Discussion
Group of the First Presbyterian
Church, corner of Waverley St. and
Forest Ave., Palo Alto.
SATURDAY
5:15 p.m.—Yell leader tryoutees will
meet in the Pavilion.
TUESDAY
3:00 p.m.—Mothers' Club meeting in
the Women's Clubhouse. Dean Cul
ver will speak on the work of his
department.
MISCELLANEOUS
The Costume Department of Stanford
Dramatics is in need of any article
of wearing apparel of the period
between 1900 and 1915. Anyone
wishing to lend anything of this
character to the department kindly
communicate with Harold Helven
ston by January 19.
All Sophomores interested in signing
up for the position of Sophomore
Baseball Munager report at the
manager's office in the Physical
Education building Friday after
noon. If unable to sign then, report
on the following Monday at 2 p.m.
at the varsity diamond.
All freshman and varsity men with
soccer equipment must return it to
the equipment room in Encina Gym
immediately. All men who wish to
use the same shoes next quarter
put their names on such
