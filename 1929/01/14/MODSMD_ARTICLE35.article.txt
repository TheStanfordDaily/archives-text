# TWO POINTS REPORT QUAKE
## 
### 
BRUSSELS, Belgium, Jan. 13. (/P)
—A violent earthquake, with its epi
center about 5000 miles distant, pos
sibly in northorn Japan, was recorded
at the Uccle observatory shortly after
midnight last night.
CAIRO, Egypt, Jan. 13. (/P) — An
earthquake shock of great intensity
with its epicenter about 5600 miles
away, was recorded at the Helouan
observatory beginning at 2:15 A.M.
today.
