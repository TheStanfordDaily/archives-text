# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
3:00 p.m.—Quarter's first meeting of
the Stanford Dames in the Women's
i Clubhouse. All wives of students
are invited.
MISCELLANEOUS
j The Costume Department of Stanford
Dramatics is in need of any article
of wearing apparel of the period
between 1900 and 1915. Anyone
wishing to lend anything of this
character to the department kindly
communicate with Harold Helven
ston by January 19.
| All Sophomores interested in signing
up for the position of Sophomore
i Baseball Manager report at the
j manager's office in the Physical
Education building Friday after
noon. If unable to sign then, report
on the following Monday at 2 p.m.
at the varsity diamond.
All freshman and varsity men with
soccer equipment must return it to
the equipment room in Encina Gym
immediately. All men who wish to
use the same shoes next quarter
put their names on such
