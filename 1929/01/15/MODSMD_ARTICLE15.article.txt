# QUAD PHOTOS MUST BE COMPLETED THIS WEEK SAYS BEEMER
## 
### 
"This week is positively the
last in which individual Quad
pictures will be taken," an
nounced H. Lawrence Beemer,
editor of the 1929 yearbook, yes
terday afternoon. "Seniors who
fail to secure photographs dur
ing the final week absolutely
will not have their records pub
lished in the book."
Appointments may be made in
the Women's Clubhouse between
the hours of 9 and 12 and 1 and
4:30 o'clock every day this week.
Pictures will be taken also dur
ing these hours.
Proofs of pictures already
taken may be secured tomorrow
and any day until the end of
the week.
