# Marshal Foch Stricken With Severe Heart Attack
## 
### 
PARIS, Jan. 14. (£>)-— Marshal Ferdi
nand Foch, Generalissimo in the
World War and beloved of the French
people, has been stricken with a se
vere heart attack. Today he fought
for his life with the same courage
that characterized him as leader of
the Allied Armies.
The man who commanded millions
of soldiers in the great struggle was
under the orders of six physicians,
who have imposed upon him the ne
cessity of absolute rest.
