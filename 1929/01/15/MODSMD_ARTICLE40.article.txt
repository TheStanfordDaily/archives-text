# STANFORD DAMES MEET
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames Club will hold
its first meeting of the winter quarter
Thursday at 3 o'clock in the Stan
ford Women's Clubhouse. Mrs. Wil
liam Roach will give readings, and
Mrs. Sears will sing. Following the
program there will be a social hour
and tea. Wives of students are espe
cially invited.
