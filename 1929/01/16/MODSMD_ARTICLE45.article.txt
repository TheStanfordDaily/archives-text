Glover, U. S. Grant, 111, chairman; Dr. Hubert Work, Mrs. Virginia White Speel. Standing: C. C.
Glover, jr., Robert Fleming, district commissioner; Corcoran Thom, Theodore P. Noyes, Edward C.
Graham, Joseph C. Hines, David Lynn, Sidney Toiavero, district commissioner, and Joshua Evans, jr.
