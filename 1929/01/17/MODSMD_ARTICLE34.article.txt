# Airplane Companies Form Merger To Direct Patents
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 16. (£>)—Curtiss
Aeroplane and Motor Company is re
ported in Wall Street to have formed
an affiliation with the Caproni Avia
tion Company of Italy, one of the
largest European manufacturers of
airplanes. A new company, it is un
derstood, is to be formed to control
all the patents and designs for ex
clusive manufacture and sale in the
United States of Caproni seaplanes
and airplanes.
A 135 passenger airplane for use in
long distance transport is now under
construction in Germany.
