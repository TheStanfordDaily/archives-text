# STANFORD DAMES TO MEET
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will meet for
the first time this quarter this after
noon at 3 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. On the program will be
readings by Mrs. William Roach and
songs by Mrs. Sears. Tea and a social
hour will follow the meeting, and
wives of students are especially in
vited to attend.
