# SEVEN MATCHES ARE COMPLETED IN TENNIS PLAY
## 
### 
Three matches, one doubles and
two singles, were played off yesterday
in the varsity tennis competition with
one default; while the freshmen
played three singles matches. Follow
ing are the results:
' Varsity: Castellanos d. Forbes for
10th place by default; Farwell d.
Woodruff for 15th place 6-1, 6-4; Rob
ertson d. Eaton 6-4, 1-6, 8-6; and Mc-
Kellop and Castellanos d. Farwell and
Gantner 7-5, 6-3; Stephens d. Brem
for 16th place 7-5, 6-2; and Guard d.
Eisbee for 20th place 6-2, 6-4.
The following matches are sched
uled for this afternoon: lienzel vs.
Hall for 4th place at 2 o'clock; Mc-
Kellip vs. Driscoll for 6th place at
2 o'clock; Doeg and Wheatley vs.
Weesner and lla.ll for Ist place at 3
o'clock; Renzel and Easton vs. Forbes
and Stephens for 3rd place at 4
o'clock; and Sorensen and Gibbs vs. I
Erlin and Woodruff for 7th place at
3 o'clock.
