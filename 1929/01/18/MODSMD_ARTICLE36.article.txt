# Appointment Secretary Attends Council At U.S.C.
## 
### 
Mrs. Elizabeth B. Snell, Appointment
Secretary, left yesterday morning to at
tend a meeting of District IX of the
American Alumni Council at the Uni
versity of Southern California. She will
preside Saturday afternoon at a session
on Student and Alumni Employment, and
will visit various schools during her trip.
