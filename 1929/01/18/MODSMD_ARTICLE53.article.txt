# Tennis Manager Trials Are Still Open To Sophomores
## 
### 
Martin Woodward, senior tennis man
ager, announces that sophomores wishing
to try out for tennis manager may still
do so. The work consists of routine co
incident with the playing of varsity and
freshman matches and of matches be
tween Stanford and other teams. The
work is interesting; no previous knowl
edge is necessary; and tryoutces report
ing now will not he handicapped by not
having been out before. Anyone inter
ested may see Woodward this afternoon
on the varsity courts.

A six-day week has been started at
the University of Oklahoma in order
to relieve classroom congestion.
