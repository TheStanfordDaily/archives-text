# Initiation Banquet Planned For Glee Club Members
## 
### 
The initiation banquet of the Men's
Glee Club will be held on Wednesday
night, January 23, in the dining room of
the Sunset Cafeteria, according to an
announcement made last night by Rus
sell Waite, president of the organization.
A program of entertainment will fol
low the dinner. Warren D. Allen, Me
morial Church organist and past director
of the glee club, Albert Kulman, present
director, and John Ezra McDowell,
alumni secretary, will be the principal
speakers of the evening. Twenty-five
new members will be formally initiated
into the club.
