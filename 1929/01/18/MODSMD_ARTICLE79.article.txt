# Fenton Barrett Elected Sword And Sandals Head
## 
### 
At a meeting of Sword and Sandals,
honorary dramatic society, held yesterday
in the Green Room in the Knglisli build
ing, officers were elected for the coming
term. Fenton Barrett, '30, was chosen
president and Kenneth Funk, '29, secre
tary-treasurer. At the same time, plans
were discussed concerning the initiation
which will take place in the near future.
