# IMPORTANT TOPICS SET FOR SENIORS' MEETING
## 
### 
Senior Class will hold its first meet
ing of the quarter on Wednesday,
January 23 at 7 o'clock in Room 214.
Dick Holt, class president, urges all
members of the '29 class to be pres
ent to discuss several matters of im
portance.
The more important matters of dis
cussion that will be presented at the
meeting are as follows:
The advisability of a questionnaire
to be sent to all members of the
graduating class, and to be sponsored
by the Stanford Daily; designs for a
proposed Senior ring; the Memorial
Fund contribution as well as the Sen
ior endowment
