# San Francisco Engineer Speaks At Union Tonight
## 
### 
"Safety and Human Engineering" is
the subject on which J. J. Ilosedale,
consulting safety engineer of San
Francisco, will address the first so
cial meeting of the Stanford Branch,
American Society of Civil Engineers,
tonight at 8 o'clock in the Old Union.
The talk will be illustrated, showing
how a safety engineer directs con
struction work on a building project
in order to secure the maximum pro
tection for workmen and bystanders.
