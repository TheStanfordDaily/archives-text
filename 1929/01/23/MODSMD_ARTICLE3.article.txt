# Electrical Engineers Will Hold Op en Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford Branch, American Insti
tute of Electrical Engineers, will hold
its second open meeting of the quar
ter at 7:15 o'clock in the Little Thea
tre, at which time there will he four
reels of motion pictures shown. One
reel will show the manufacture of the
largest examples of electrical equip
ment, while the three other reels
will give a survey of the development
of steam and electrical power.
