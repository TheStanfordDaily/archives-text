# CHEMICAL MOVIE TO BE SHOWN
## 
### 
A motion picture, "The Manufacture
of Rayon," will be shown tonight in
the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30
o'clock, sponsored by Stanford honor
ary chemical societies. This is the
first of several similar films to be
shown during the winter and spring.
Anyone interested is urged to attend.
Refreshments are to be served.
