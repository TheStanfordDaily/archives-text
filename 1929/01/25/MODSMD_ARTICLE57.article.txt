# AVIATION SCHOOL DANCE
## 
### 
The Palo Alto School of Aviation
is giving its first annual Masque Ball
at the Palo Alto Woman's Club House
on February 9. The Staley-Spauldlng
orchestra will play. Tickets, which
will sell for $1.50 a couple, will be
on sale at the flying school, Biff Hoff
man's, Morey'B, Crandall's, and the
Old Union.
