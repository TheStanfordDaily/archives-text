# Second Quad Payments Are Due Before Feb. 1
## 
### 
All students buying Quads must pay
. their second installments before February
1 1 if they wish to avoid a fifty-cent fine
for late payment, announces Marjorie
Rice, circulation manager. The amount
of the installment is four dollars for sen
iors and three dollars for other students.
! Payments are being received every after
noon from 1 to 2 o'clock at the Quad
i office in the Old Union, or at the table on
j the Engineering corner.
