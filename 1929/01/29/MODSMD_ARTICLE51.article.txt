# ROOS WILL SPEAK TO SCABBARD AND BLADE
## 
### 
Colonel Robert A. Roos, prominent
San Franciscan and leading reserve
officer in the United States, will ad
dress the advanced classes of the
Stanford R.O.T.C. and the Scabbard
and Blade Society at 7:30 o'clock
tonight in Room 214, History Build
ing.
Colonel Roos has recently returned
from a short stay at the War College
at Washington. He will speak on the
Reserve Corps, a subject that should
be of interest to all enrolled in the
military courses.
The meeting is being held under
the auspices of Scabbard and Blade.
