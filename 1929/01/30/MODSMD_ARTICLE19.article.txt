# Colonel Roos Gives Talk To Scabbard And Blade
## 
### 
Colonel Robert Roos, reserve officer of
the United States Army, last night ad
dressed the members of Scabbard and
Blade Society and the advanced classes
of the Stanford R.O.T.C.
Colonel Rons spoke of his experiences
in the reserve corps, and at the War
College in Washington. He emphasized
the point that in an emergency students
commissioned in the R.O.T.C. will sup
plant the nucleus of regular officers in
training an army for national defense.
