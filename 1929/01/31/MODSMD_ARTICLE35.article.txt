# FORTY-FIVE WOMEN TO MAKE ASILOMAR TRIP
## 
### 
The women of *the sophomore class
have planned a trip to Asilomar for
this coming week-end. The purpose
of this trip is to keep up a closer
contact between the women of the
class. So far about forty-five women
have signified their intention of mak
ing- the trip. Some are going down
Friday and planning to stay through
Sunday, while others are arriving on
Saturday.
The cost of the trip for the week
end will be $5.50, not counting trans
portation, which will be provided by
the women who have cars. All sopho
more women, both new and old, are
urged to come.
Anyone who is interested in making
the trip should notify Elizabeth Alden,
P.A. 1014.
