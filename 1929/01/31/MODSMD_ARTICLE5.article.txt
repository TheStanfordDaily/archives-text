# Toyon Dance Programs To Be Given Out Today
## 
### 
Programs for the Toyon Club dance
to be Riven Friday night, will be dis
tributed this afternoon by Herb
Wenig, Toyon Club manager.
The programs are bound in green
leather with a specially designed seal
on the cover. The "hunting lodge"
motif which is being carried out in
decorating for the dance is also evi
denced in the design of the programs.
