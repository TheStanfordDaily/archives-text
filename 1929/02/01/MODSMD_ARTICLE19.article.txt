# Dr. A. T. Murray Conducts Campus Vesper Services
## 
### 
Dr. Augustus T. Murray, in a sennon
during the first vesper service which he
led yesterday afternoon ill the Memorial
Church, stressed seeking for truth, open
ing one's mind to new facts, and thinking
things out for one's self.

He conducted the prayers and Warren
D. Allen accompanied the singing. These
services will he held immediately after
the organ recitals at 4:15 o'clock every
Thursday afternoon.
