# SOPHOMORE WOMEN TO LEAVE FOR ASILOMAR
## 
### 
Forty women have made arrange
ments to go on the trip to Asilomar
which has been planned by the sopho
more women for this week-end* Most
of them are intending to leave this
afternoon, while the others will leave
tomorrow. Those who have cars are
providing the transportation for those
who have no way of getting there.
The purpose of the trip is to bring
the women of the class together; all
sophomore women are urged to go.
Anyone interested in taking the
trip should notify Elizabeth Alden,
P.A. 1014, immediately.
