# SHIP FIRE KILLS TWO
## 
### 
JERSEY CITY, N.J., Jan. 31. (fP)—
Two unidentified members of the
I crew of the Dollar liner President
Johnson, scheduled to sail this after
noon on a world cruise, were burned
to death today when fire damaged the
port berth deck amidships in the
white crew's quarters.
