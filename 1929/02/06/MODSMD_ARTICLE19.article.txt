# ESSAYS, POETRY FOR NEXT "LIT" WANTED
## 
### 
Contributions for the next issue of
the Stanford Literary Magazine are
requested now, announces James
Tucker, editor. The deadline for the
submitting of manuscripts, which
should he brought to the Lit office in
the Press Building or mailed to Box
27, Campus, has been set for the nine
teenth of this month.
Short stories, essays, and poetry by
students and faculty are particularly
desired.
