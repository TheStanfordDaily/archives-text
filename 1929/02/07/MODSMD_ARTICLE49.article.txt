# VESPERS FOLLOW ORGAN PROGRAM IN CHURCH TODAY
## 
### 
Vespers will follow the organ re
cital by Warren D. Allen, University
organist, this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock
in the Memorial Church. The program
is as follows:
Andante con moto, from the
"Unfinished Symphony"
Schubert
Angel Pantomime, from "Hansel
and Gretel" Humperdinck
Sonata, No. 4, in B Flat (last two
movements) Mendelssohn
Allegretto
Finale
Ave Maria Arcadelt
Mrs. Esther H. Allen will sing Mac-
Dermid's solo: "He that dwelleth in
the secret place of the most high" at
the Vesper service following the or
gan recital. The service will be con
ducted by Dr. I). C. Gardner, Univer
sity chaplain, who will give a short
devotional address! This is the second
of the Vesper Services which are held
every Thursday at 4:45 o'clock.
