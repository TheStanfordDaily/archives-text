# Doeg Downs Wheatley; Retains Ranking Position
## 
### 
Johnny Doeg retained first position
on the varsity tennis squad by defeat
ing Captain John Wheatley yesterday
in one of the weekly ranking matches.
The score was 6-2, 6-4, a clear-cut
victory.
Other varsity singles were decided
when Castellanos beat Erlin, 6-2, 3-6,
6-4, for eleventh position, and Far
well won over Gantner, 6-3, 3-6, 6-3,
for thirteen tli place.
On the freshman team, Hickman de
feated McCoy, 6-1, 6-1, Leib defeated
Webb, 6-4, 8-6, and Eichelberger de
feated Bergstrom. In doubles, the
Eichelberger-Bergstrom team scored
a victory over Leib-Leib, 6-4, 6-2.
