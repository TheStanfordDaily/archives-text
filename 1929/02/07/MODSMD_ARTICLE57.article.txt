# Cardinal Golfers To Meet Club Players On Saturday
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity golf team will
play a practice match with the Los
Altos Country Club Saturday after
noon at 1 o'clock, Dean Sweeney, golf
manager, announced yesterday. The
ten Cardinal golfers have not yet been
chosen, and the only member of the
club team known is Dink Templeton,
Stanford track coach.
Sweeney has asked the freshman
and varsity golfers to wear golf
knickers when they meet in front of
Encina gym this afternoon at 3:45 for
the Quad picture.
