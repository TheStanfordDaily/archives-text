# TRAVELERS ARE INJURED
## 
### 
HAVANA, Cuba, Feb. 10. (JP) —Two
passengers who arrived here on the liner
Mongolia, en route from New York to j
California, were in a hospital in a serious
condition here today as a result of an i
automobile accident yesterday.
