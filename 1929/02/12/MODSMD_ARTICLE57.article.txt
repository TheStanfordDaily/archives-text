TUESDAY
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of the Christian
Science Society in the Women's
Clubhouse.
WEDNESDAY
12:15 p.m.—Cosmopolitan Club lunch
eon in the alcove of the Stanford
Union dining room.
7:00 p.m.—Women's Glee Club will
meet in Room 84.
7:15 p.m.—A meeting of the Junipero
Serra Club will be held in the
Union for discussion of business
matters. Refreshments will be
served.
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of the German
Club in the Women's Clubhouse. All
members are urged to be present.
8:30 p.m.—Gordon Davis will see all
faculty members and their wives
who would like to act in a faculty
play, to be given next quarter.
THURSDAY
7:30 p.m.—DeMolay meeting in the
Y.M.C.A. Initiation and refresh
ments.
MISCELLANEOUS
All individual proofs for the '29 Quad
must be turned in immediately to
the trophy room in the Old Union.

played tonight. El Campo meets

clashes with Phi Gamma Delta to
determine the championship of
League 1.

tomorrow night in the gym. Sigma
Chi meets Sequoia 1 at 5:10 in a
game that will decide the title win
ner of League 4. At 7:50 El Toro will

pionship against the winner of the
Kappa Alpha-Fiji fracas. Another
quarter-final game will be played on

with either Sigma Chi or Sequoia 1.

performance.
