# HAWKS, WEST-EAST AIR RECORD HOLDER
## 
### 

Frank Hawk established a new transcontinental non-stop
record from Los Angeles to tyew York when he landed at Roosevelt
field, New York, just 18 hours, 21 minutes and 59 seconds after his
takeoff in Los Angeles. He bettered the record of Art Goebel by 36
minutes and one second.
