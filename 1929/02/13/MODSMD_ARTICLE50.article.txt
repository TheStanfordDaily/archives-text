# Military Ball Bids To Go On Sale At Union Today
## 
### 
Bids for the Military Ball which
will take place Friday night, March 8,
instead of March 9, as was erroneous-
Ily announced in yesterday's Daily,
will go on sale at 1 o'clock this after
noon at the A.S.S.U. offices in the
Old Union for $5 each.
The Ball this year will be built
about a theme inspired by the life
and environment of the French For
i eign Legion. In keeping with this idea
(he invitations are copies of French
I Mobilization orders, issued at the
j command of Scabbard and Blade.
