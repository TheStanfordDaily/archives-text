# WEESNER-HALL DOWN DOEG-WHEATLEY, WIN FIRST VARSITY RANK
## 
### 
In a closely contested match during
which both teams played exception
ally good tennis, Weesner and Hall
yesterday afternoon defeated Doeg
and Wheatley 6-2, 4-G, S-6 for first var
sity doubles position.
The other varsity matches played
yesterday resulted as follows: Easton
u. Stephens 4-G, 7-5, 6-4 for seventh
place; Forbes d. McKellop 9-7, 5-7, 6-1
for ninth place; and Lee d. Wilson
6-4, 6-2 for fifteenth.
Freshman results were as follows:
Bancroft d. Forney for first; Robinson
d. Johnston 6-2, 8-6 for third; Berg
strom d. Peck 6-2, 6-2 for sixteenth;
C. B. Leib d. Cherry 6-2, 6-1 for eighth;
Hiikman and Forney d. Logan and
Eichelberger 7-5, 6-8, 6-4 for first dou
bles; and Johnson and Robinson d.
Leib and Leib 6-3, 3-6, 6-4, for second.
The following matches are sched
uled for tomorrow: Logan vs. Hick
man for fifth freshman singles at 9
and Renzel and Easton vs. McKellip
and Forbes, varsity doubles.
