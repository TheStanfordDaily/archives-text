# Engineer Society Names Officers For Half Year
## 
### 
After a lengthy meeting closing at
12 o'clock Tuesday night, the Ameri
can Society of Mechanical Engineers
elected the following officers for the
next half year; John Wheatley, presi
dent, Edmond Wagner, vice-president,
Maynard Born, corresponding secre
tary, and Houston Rehrig, recording
secretary.
Earlier in the evening, at a pro
gram open to the public, the Society
gave four reels of motion pictures
made by the Carborundum Company,
showing plants of the company at
Niagara Falls.
