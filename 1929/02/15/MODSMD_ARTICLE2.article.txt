# Night Flying At Palo Alto Field Will Be Inaugurated
## 
### 
Night flying at the Palo Alto School
of Aviation will be inaugurated about
March 1, with the installation of land
ing lights at the school, according to
Mrs. N. A. Goddard, secretary of the
institution. The lights, with the new
turf runways now being installed, will
make this field one of the best for its
.size in the state.
A Fokker Universial monoplane
from Whittier was a visitor at the
field yesterday morning. It is owned
by the Stoodui Company of Whittier.
