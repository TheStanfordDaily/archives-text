# BISHOP EDWARD CROSS SCHEDULED TO SPEAK IN MEMORIAL CHURCH
## 
### 
Bishop Edward M. Cross will be
the special speaker at the services
Sunday at 11 o'clock in the Memorial
Church. Bishop Cross' jurisdiction
comprises a large portion of the state
ot Washington and he is building a
cathedral in Spokane. He spoke here
iast Sunday.
The Anthems will be "We Praise
Thee" by Tschaikowsky, and "Jesu,
Dulcis Memoria" by Vittoris.
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will give his recital at 7:30 p.m.
in the Memorial Church. Waldo Fer
gnson, baritone, will be featured.
Frances Lee, violinist, will play at
the evening recital of Warren D. Al
len, University organist, at 7:30
o'clock in the Memorial Church.
