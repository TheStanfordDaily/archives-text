# SEAGRAVE PLANS NEW SPEED ATTEMPT IN U.S.
## 
### 

Maj. H. O. D. Segrave, British racing driver, is bringing his Golden Arrow to the United States to
seek a new automobile speed record at Daytona, Fla., in March. Upper picture shows beach at
Daytona with artist's drawing of the new car. Inset is rear view of the new car taken before Major
Segrave sailed from Enflland.
