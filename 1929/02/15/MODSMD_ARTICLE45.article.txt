# Kedroff Quartet To Sing At Stanford Next Month
## 
### 
The Kedroff quartet of Russian
singers will present a concert in the
Assembly Hall on the evening of
March S. The quartet, which is com
posed of a baritone, basso, and first
and second tenors, is touring America, j

They were famous singers in Russia
before the war, and four months
after their arrival in America last
winter they were being favorably dis
cussed throughout this country.
The concert is being presented
under the auspices of the Committee
on Public Exercises, and there is no
admission charge.
