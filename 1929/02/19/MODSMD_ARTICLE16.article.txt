# Library Director To Talk On "Union Lists" Tonight
## 
### 
" " - -"-3""
Nathan van Patten, director of the
Stanford University library, will talk
before the Special Libraries Associa
tion of San Francisco tonight, at the
Bellevue Hotel, on the subject, "Union
Lists." His speech will be in connec
tion with preparations made by the
association to publish a new list of
publications. This special list will be
of particular benefit to business and
technical men, and is expected to
come off the press early this summer.
