A DRINK OF SPARKLING WATERS THAT
ARE VERY KIND TO ONE, OF LIMES
AND FRESH-CRUSHED GINGER
J/ IMPORTED
VIUCML
DRY GINGER ALE
You can try it at your club today, or wherever
you may go to dine or dance tonight—a ginger ale
from the Philippines. Everywhere it's being poured;
so quickly has the word gone 'round since the first
cargo of Isuan reached America.
How you will enjoy your first sensuous sip of it.
How you'll welcome that virtue of Isuan that thirsty
men of Manila and of the humid China Coast have
especially esteemed. You can drink Isuan far into
the night, yet in the morning feel as fresh as tropic
sunshine!
IN MANILA THEY SAY "E-SWAN"
The Isuan Corporation, Isuan Bldg., San Francisco
