# 'SPUD' LEWIS SIGNED TO COACH FOOTBALL AT NORTHWESTERN U.
## 
### 
The backfield coach of Northwest
ern University's 1929 football team
will be Lawrence "Spud" Lewis, it
was announced in Chicago Saturday.
Dick Hanley, head coach, talked with
Lewis last month while lie was in
Palo Alto.
The Northwestern system is closely
patterned after that of "Pop" Warner,
and Hanley wants an expert in War
ner backfield technique. "Spud" has
been a first-class player in all four
backfield positions, and climaxed his
varsity career with his exhibition
against the Army last December.
