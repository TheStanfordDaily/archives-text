# Symphony Concert To Be Given Saturday, March 9
## 
### 
The Founders Day concert of the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra
will be given Saturday, March 9, in
stead of on the following Sunday, as
had been announced. The concert will
take place in the Memorial Church at
8:15 o'clock.
Admission is free. Tickets may be
obtained only by presentation of stu
dent body card in the lobby of the
Administration building from 8 to 5
o'clock Wednesday, March G.
