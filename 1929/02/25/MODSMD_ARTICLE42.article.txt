# ENDURANCE FLIGHT DELAYED
## 
### 
BAY CITY, Mich., Feb. 24.
cause of adverse weather conditions,
Eddie Stinson and Randolph G. Page,
veteran Detroit aviators, postponed
their proposed endurance flight which
was to have been started at 9 o'clock
this morning from a runway on the
ice of Saginaw Bay here. The flight
will be attempted later this week.
