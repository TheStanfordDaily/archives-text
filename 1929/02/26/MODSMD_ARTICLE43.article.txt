# Schedule Swimming Trials For Tomorrow Afternoon
## 
### 
Preliminary trials for the intra
mural swimming meet will be held
tomorrow afternoon at 4 o'clock
in the Encina pool. Swimmers quali
fying in the trials will splash it out
for the finals on the following clay.
The meet will be a close one, accord
ing to Charles Davis, director of
mural sports, as twelve teams have
signed up for the event to date.
