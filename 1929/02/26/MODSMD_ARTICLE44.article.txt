# S.C. Offers Fellowships In School Of Philosophy
## 
### 
Two fellowships, one of $1500 and
one of $1000, besides several scholar
ships ranging from $150 to $500, are
offered by the University of South
ern California in the School of Philo
sophy for the coming year to distin
guished graduates of U.S.C. and of
other universities and colleges. The
purpose of the fellowships and schol
arships, given only to graduate stu
dents, is to encourage research work
in philosophy.
Applications for scholarships and
fellowships must be sent in by April
25, and should be accompanied by a
complete statement of the candidate's
previous training, recommendations
from professors, and, if possible, spe
cimens of written or published work.
