# Geology Class To Visit Hunter's Point Saturday
## 
### 
Students in the Geology I class will
make an all-day field trip Saturday
morning to Hunter's Point near San
Francisco, leaving the campus at 8
o'clock and returning at 5 o'clock. Be
sides observing points of geological
interest en route, the class will study
the Munter's Point formations of sedi
mentary, igenous, and metamorphic
rocks, and will have the opportunity
of studying the ocean wave and cur
rent action at the point.
