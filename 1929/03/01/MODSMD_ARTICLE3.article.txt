# Acting President
## 
### 

Dr. Robert Eckles Swain, acting
head of Stanford University since
President Ray Lyman Wilbur left for
Washington two weeks ago ,is slated
to become permanent head if Wilbur
accepts a post in the Hoover cabinet,
it is believed. Rumor has it that the
latter will be made Secretary of the
Interior during the Hoover regime.
