# IDAHO CONSIDERS CALLAND
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 28 (/P).—Leo
Calland, freshman football coach at the
University of Southern California, is
mentioned as being in conference with
University of Idaho authorities over the
position of gridiron coach.
