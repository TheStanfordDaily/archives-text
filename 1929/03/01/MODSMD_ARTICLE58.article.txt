# Mothers Club Is To Give Inaugural Tea On March 4
## 
### 
The Mothers Club will give an In
augxtral Tea on March 4 to raise
money for two scholarships.
Mr. Martin, leader of the Stanford
Band, has offered the services of that
organization for the afternoon. Fea
tures of the program will be "The
President's March," "Hail to the
Chief," some Stanford music, and as
a special number the band will play
"The Star Spangled Banner." The pro
gram will be presented in the Union
Court.
Tickets still may be obtained from
Mrs. E. C. Baker at Kingscote. Mrs.
Ewell at the Women's Clubhouse will
reserve tables for any who desire
them.
