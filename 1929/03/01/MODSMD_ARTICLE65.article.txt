# Varsity Handball Team To Meet Athens Club Tonight
## 
### 
An inexperienced Stanford varsity
handball team will play an important
practice match tonight in Oakland
with the Athens Club's veteran squad.
Stanford has played only one match
so far this season, defeating the San
Jose Y.M.C.A. '3 to 2 February 2.
The lineup of the Cardinal team to
night will consist of Herb Mayer and
John Tampeke as first and second
singles players respectively; Paul
Magee and George Richards as the
first doubles team, and Gordon White
and Lincoln Rode as the second dou
bles. Ted Starr will be alternate.
