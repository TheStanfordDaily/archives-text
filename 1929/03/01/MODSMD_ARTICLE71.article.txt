# FRIED GETS VALOR MEDAL
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Feb. 28 (£•).—Captain
George Fried, of the United States liner
"America," today received the valor
medal of the American Bureau of Ship
ping for the rescue of the crews of the
Italian steamer "Florida," last January,
and the British steamer "Antinoe," in
1927. The medal was presented at a
luncheon at which Captain Fried was the
guest of the Bureau of Shipping.
