# WILLS PLANS SUMMER PLAY
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 28 (/P).—
Helen Wills, champion, and Edith Cross,
third in the national ranking of women
tennis players, have accepted invitations
from the United States Lawn Tennis As
sociation to play team matches in Berlin
and France.
