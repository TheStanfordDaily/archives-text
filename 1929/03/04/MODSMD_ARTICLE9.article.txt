# KYA IS TO BROADCAST PROGRAM OF SONGS BY STANFORD'S GLEE CLUB
## 
### 
] A program of songs by the Stanford
| Men's Glee Club will be broadcast
from station KYA, San Francisco at
9 o'clock tonight. KYA will be con
nected in a Western hook-up under
the control of the American Broad
casting Company which is sponsoring
the program. Stations as far east, as
! Chicago are to be included in the
string of broadcasting units.
The program by the glee club will
' consist largely of favorite Stanford
songs., "Sons of the Stanford Red,"
"The Cardinal Is Waving," and
"Come Join the Band," being featured.
The program will be a half hour in
I length.
