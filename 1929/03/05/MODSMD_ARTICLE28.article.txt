# Golf Architect Inspects Proposed Campus Course
## 
### 
After spending yesterday tramping
through the hills back of the campus,
William Bell, famous golf course
architect, said that he was favorably
Impressed with the topographical pos
sibilities of Stanford's proposed golf
course.
Accompanied by A1 Masters, gradu
ate manager, and Comptroller Almon
E. Roth, Bell made a preliminary sur
vey of the course yesterday. He has
been employed by the University to
ascertain the feasibility of construct
ing the links on the land set apart by
the Board of Trustees.
