# PASSES BOULDER COMPACT
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, March 4. (/p)—With
extraordinary dispatch two measures,
ratifying the six state Boulder dam
compact, excluding Arizona, and limit
ing to those states agreeing use of
Colorado river waters were passed by
the California legislature and signed
by Governor C. C. Young today.
