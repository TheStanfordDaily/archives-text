# EXPLOSION IN SOFIA
## 
### 
SOFIA, Bulgaria, March 4. (>P) —
Thirty persons were killed and 20
seriously injured today in a terrific !
explosion at the Sotia arsenal. Heavy
material damage was caused by the
blast which was in the fuse section.
