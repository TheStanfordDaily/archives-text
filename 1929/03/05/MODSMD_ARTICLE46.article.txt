# DR. REDFIELD NAMED ASSISTANT PROFESSOR IN SUMMER QUARTER
## 
### 
Dr. Robert Redfield of the Univer
sity of Chicago has been appointed as
acting assistant professor of psychol
ogy for the coming summer quarter,
Summer Quarter Director John A. Sel
lards announced recently.
A young psychologist, Redfield is
himself a former student of the Uni
versity of Chicago, where he received
his Ph.D. last year. Before he became
an instructor in anthropology at the
University of Chicago in 1927 he was
an instructor in sociology and anthro
pology at the University of Colorado
in 1925 and a research fellow of the
Social Science Research Council the
following year. At present he is as
sistant professor of anthropology at
the University of Chicago.
