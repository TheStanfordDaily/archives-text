# Sir Bernard Pares Speaks On Russia Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
"Contemporary Russia" will be the
subject of a lecture to be given by
Sir Bernard Pares tomorrow evening
in the Assembly Hall at 7:30 o'clock.
Sir Bernard's experiences has given
him a wide knowledge of conditions
in Russia, as a considerable part of
his life has been spent in that coun
try. From 1904 to 1919 he was in al
most constant touch with the leaders
of Russian affairs, and later on he
was a participant in the Revolution
during the early months. He has pub
lished several books on the Russian
situation, and is editor of the Russian
Review and the Slavonic Review.
