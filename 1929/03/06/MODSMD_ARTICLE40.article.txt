# First Jolly-Up Of Toyon Club To Be Held Tonight
## 
### 
A new event in Stanford's social
world will be initiated tonight when
Toyon Club gives its first seven-to
eight dance in the Toyon lobby.
An invitation has been extended to
all Stanford co-eds to attend. Tom
Murphy's Stanford Orchestra has been
engaged to furnish the music and I
novel refreshments will be served.
A committee composed of Stanford
Steinbeck, Irwin Warner, Dave Wil-:
liamson, Joe Grant, and Bob Gooden
have been working on the details of
the dance.
