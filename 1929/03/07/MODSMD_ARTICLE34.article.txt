# Encina Club To Hold Last Election Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Walter Cooper and Dick Broderick
were selected last night as presiden
tial candidates for a final Encina Club
election to be held tomorrow night.
As none of the candidates in the
preliminary election received a ma
jority vote, the following names will
appear on the ballot tomorrow: Carl
Cartwright and Ferd Fletcher, vice
president; Frances Bates and Ernest
Hatch, secretary; James Reed and
Tom Aitken, dance committee; and
Don Bedford and Russ Had ley, smoker
committee.
