# SEEK NATIONAL AMATEUR BASKETBALL HONORS
## 
### 

St. Ignatius college hoop team of San Francisco has entered the national tournament at Kansas
City after winning 21 of its 22 games this season, including the one with the Pacific Coast Conference
champion, California. Left to right: Vally Cameron, guard; Jay Hale, center; Ray Maloney, captain
and guard; Rene Bareilles, guard, and Bob Kleckner, forward.
