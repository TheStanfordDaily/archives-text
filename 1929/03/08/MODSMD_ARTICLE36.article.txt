# Professor Percy Martin To Spend Year In Travel
## 
### 
Professor Percy Alvin Martin, acting
head of the history department, will
spend next year in travel and study in
Europe. He intends to engage in re
search work most of the winter in the
libraries and archives in Seville.
During Professor Martin's absence his
courses will be taken over by Professor
Charles W. Hackett of the University of
Texas. Professor Hackett is a leading
authority on Latin American history in
the United States.
