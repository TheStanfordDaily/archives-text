# MADEIRA ISLAND LANDSLIDE
## 
### 
LONDON, Mar. 7 (/P). —Details were
being sought today of a disaster in the
Madeira Islands. Messages from Fun
chal said that 100 persons had lost their
lives in a great landslide. Reuter advices j
were that five had been killed and 100'
made homeless. Reliable information was [
