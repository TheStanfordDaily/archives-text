# N.B.C. Official Speaks On Advertising In Broadcasts
## 
### 
Frank A. Arnold, Director of Devel
opment for the National Broadcasting
Company of New York, told the story of
the phenomenal development of the use
of radio broadcasting for commercial
purposes in an address before lirst and
second year students of the Graduate
School of Business Wednesday after
noon.
Arnold stated that there has not
so much public interest in this branch of
radio development but it is important as
the financial basis for all broadcasting.
