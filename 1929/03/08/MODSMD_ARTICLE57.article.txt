# JUDGES' SALARIES INCREASED
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, Mar. 7 (/P).—The
assembly passed two bills today increas
ing the salaries of Los Angeles and San
Francisco county superior judges from
$9,000 to $12,(300 a year.
