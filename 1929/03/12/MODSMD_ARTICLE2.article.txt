# TIRE INDUSTRY HEAD INTERVIEWS SENIORS INTERESTED IN FIRM
## 
### 
A representative of the Firestone Tire
Company of Akron, Ohio, will be on the
campus today to consult with Stanford
seniors who are interested in salesman
and production manager positions with
the automobile tire concern.
The Firestone Tire Company offers a
six weeks' training course in Akron to
university graduates desirous of entering
the tire industry.
Students desiring to interview the Fire
stone representative are urged to make
arrangements this morning through the
Appointment office.
