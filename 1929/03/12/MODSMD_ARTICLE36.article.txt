# L. B. Reynolds Will Speak In Conference At Fresno
## 
### 
Professor Leon B. Reynolds of the
Civil Engineering department leaves the
campus on March 19 to attend a two-day
spring conference of the California Sew
age Works Association in Fresno March
20 and 21. Reynolds, president of the
association, will make the response to the
Fresno mayor's address of welcome. The
conference will include presentation of
papers, and inspection trips to local sew
age plants.
Police at Flagstaff, Arizona, have
bought an airplane equipped with a
machine gun.
