# AUTO FERRIES CONSOLIDATE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 1. (JP)—
Tlie consolidation of all auto ferry
services on San Francisco Bay under
one company to be known as the
Southern Pacific-Golden Gate Ferries
Ltd., was authorized by the State
Railroad Commission here today.
