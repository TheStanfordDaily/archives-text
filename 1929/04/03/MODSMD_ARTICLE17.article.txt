# Hall Telephone Violators To Be Traced By Company
## 
### 
In an effort to curb the misuse of
dormitory telephones, the telephone
company lias made it possible to trace
all long distance calls placed over
Palo Alto 2900, Louis It. Vincenti, stu
dent. manager, announced yesterday.
Several calls placed through 2900
last quarter and left unpaid by the
phone users were traced by the new
system and the names of the violators
were turned over to the Men's Coun
cil. The cases will be considered at
the next meeting of the council.
