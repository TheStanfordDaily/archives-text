# Alden To Take Position As 1929-30 Y.W.C.A. President
## 
### 
Hetty Allien was elected president
of the Y.W.C.A. for the coming year
at elections held during the Registra
tion Day Tea. She replaces Itutli Tin
sley. Harriet Burroughs was chosen
vice-president; Miriam Miller, treas
urer; and Elizabeth Jackson, secre
tary. The new Senior Cabinet will be
appointed by the two presidents and
the secretary sometime during this
week and the old Senior Cabinet
meets for the last time next Tuesday
night.
