# Colonial Dames Offer Annual Contest Award
## 
### 
Announcing their annual essay contest,
the Colonial Dames of America Resident
in California offer a prize of $100 to the
Stanford undergraduate who writes the
best essay on "Colonial Leadership."
This is the sixth year that this contest
has been held at Stanford. The winners
in the past have been three Stanford wom
en and two Stanford men.
Contestants will file their names on or
before April 8. Essays are due on May
15. The decision of the judges will be
announced about May 22. All those desir
ing to enter should get in touch with
Professor Edgar E. Robinson, 172 Ad
ministration Building, where further de
tails concerning the contest may be pro
cured.
