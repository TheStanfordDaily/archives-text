# GLENN-DINSMORE ENGAGEMENT
## 
### 
The engagement of Margaret Glenn,
'25, and Treat Dinsmore, '28, was an
nounced simultaneously Monday eve
ning at the Chi Omega House, the
Phi Kappa Sigma House, and the
U.C.L.A. Chi Omega House, of which
Margaret Glenn was formerly a mem
ber. Dinsmore is now in Riverside,
Calif. No definite date has been set
for the marriage.
