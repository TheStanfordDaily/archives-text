# CANADA SUFFERS IN STORMS
## 
### 
TORONTO, April 2. With re
storation today of crippled lines of
communication indications were that
property damage from yesterday's ter
rific windstorm would reach $1,000,000.
Scarcely a town or city in the storm
area escaped damage either through
flood waters or the wind which ap
proached hurricane force. Rain,
snow, and sleet accompanied the
storm.
