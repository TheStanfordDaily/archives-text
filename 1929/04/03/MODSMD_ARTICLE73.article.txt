# APPLY FOR SCHOLARSHIPS
## 
### 
Applications for 1929-30 scholarships
should be submitted as soon as pos
sible and not later than April 10, ac
cording to an announcement just made
by Mrs. Margaret Huston, Secretary
of Committee on Scholarship.
This announcement applies to all
students applying for scholarships ex
cept undergraduates who are entering
next fall. Applications for scholarships
by entering undergraduates will be
accepted until the latter part of June.
