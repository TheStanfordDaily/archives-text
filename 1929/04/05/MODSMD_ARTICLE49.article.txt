# NEW DAVIS CUP TEAM CAPTAIN
## 
### 
PHILADELPHIA, April 4. (/P)Fitz
Eugene Dixon, of Philadelphia, chair
man of the Davis Cup selection com
mittee, has been named captain of
the Davis Cup tennis team. The se
lection was made by the Davis Cup
committee of which W. Wear, Phila
delphia, is chairman.
