# SWIM CLASS REGISTRATION
## 
### 
Registration which is now open for
the swimming class for Faculty Wo
men and outsiders will close Friday,
April 12. All desiring to take this
course should sign up at once as it
will not be given unless at least ten
register for it. The class is held at
Roble Pool at 11 o'clock on Mon
days and Wednesdays during spring
quarter.
The Alcove Bookshop, Ramona
Street, Library memberships for the
quarter—sl.so.—Adv.
