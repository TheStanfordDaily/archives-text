# POETRY PRIZE
## 
### 
Entries for the Irene Hardy poetry
prize must be left with the secretary or
any member of the English Department
before May 1. Poems should be handed
in in triplicate, typewritten if possible.
Each manuscript should not bear the
name of the author, hut should be accom
panied by a sealed envelope containing
the writer's name and bearing on the out
side the name of the poem.
A Bridgeport, Conn., man used a mir
ror to kill himself with after he missed
himself with the first two shots.
