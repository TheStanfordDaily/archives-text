# DOES "OUTSIDE LOOP"
## 
### 
MINNEAPOLIS, April 9. (/P)—
Charles "Speed" Holman, winner of
various competitive air flights, suc
cessfully negotiated an outside loop in
a biplane here this afternoon. He is
said to be the first flier to perform
this feat with a commercial plane.
