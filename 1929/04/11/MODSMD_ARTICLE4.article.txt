# SENIORS MAY ORDER PROGRAMS NOW AT CAMPUS BOOKSTORE
## 
### 
Orders for Senior Week pro
grams and announcements can
be placed now at the Stanford
Bookstore. The programs, which
will arrive in June, are being
made by Elliot Co. of Pennsyl
vania, and will be sold for $1.00
each. Commencement announce
ments are priced at two for
twenty-five cents.
Senior Week programs contain
the list of candidates for de
grees, the Senior Class officers
and committees, and the program
of events during Senior Week.
Covers are to be of gray suede
embossed with an archway and
Inlaid with silver. A sample is
on display in the Bookstore.
