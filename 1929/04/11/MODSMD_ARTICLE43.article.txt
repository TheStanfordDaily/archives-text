# Choose Men For Varsity, Freshman Tennis Squads
## 
### 
Names of the ten varsity and twelve
freshman players who will make up
the varsity and yearling tennis squads
during the spring quarter, as released
yesterday, are as follows:
Varsity—Doeg, Driseoll, Easton,
Farwell, Forbes, Hall, McKellip, Ste
phens, Weesner, Wheatley.
Freshmen — Bancroft, Bergstrom,
Forney, Hickman, Johnston, C. B.
Leib, S. F. Leib, Logan, McCoy, Rob
inson, Williams, Lambert.
