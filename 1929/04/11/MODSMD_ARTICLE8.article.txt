# SANTA FE TRAIN DERAILED
## 
### 
HOLBROOK, Arizona, April 10. (/p)
I —Santa Fe train No. 10, eastbound,
was delayed four hours early today by
derailment of three coaches because
of spreading track west of here. No
one was injured.
