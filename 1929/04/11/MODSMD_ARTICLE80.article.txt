# Law Association Elects McDougal New President
## 
### 
Kenneth McDougal was , elected
president of the Stanford Law Asso
ciation yesterday afternoon at a spe
cial meeting which was held at 1:15
in Room 12 of the Law building. He
succeeds Harry J. Rathburn, who com
pleted work for his J.D. degree last
quarter.
McDougal was a member of the
executive board of the law associa
tion before becoming president of that
organization. His place on the board
is to be filled by Harry Call, who was
elected at yesterday's meeting.
