# WASHINGTON CREW VETERANS MEET BEARS SATURDAY
## 
### 

Here are Coach A| Ulbrickson's rowing veterans who meet the famed California crew on the Oakland estuary
in the first regatta of the 1929 season Saturday morning. Left to right: Capt. Ellis McDonald, Ronald Wails, Stanley
Valentine, George Oistad, Bert Kauffman, Warren Davis, and Bob Schoettler.
