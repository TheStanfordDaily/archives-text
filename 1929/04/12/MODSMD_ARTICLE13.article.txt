# BIG MEET ROOTERS' TICKETS TO GO ON SALE NEXT MONDAY
## 
### 
The sale of rooters' tickets for
the annual Big Meet between
Stanford and the University of
California will start Monday
morning at the A.S.S.U. offices
in the Old Union, according to
an announcement made by Jake
Irwin, manager of the ticket de
partment.
The ducats will be sold only
on Monday and Tuesday and the
office hours are 0-12 and 1-5.
Rooters' tickets are $1.00 each.
Additional tickets for the meet,
may lie obtained at the ticket
window for $1.50.
