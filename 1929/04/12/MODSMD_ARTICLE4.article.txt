# RUSHING HOURS FOR FIRST PERIOD DATES LISTED BY COUNCIL
## 
### 
Rushing hours for the first
period as announced by Doug
Lewis, president of the Interfra
ternity Council, yesterday, are
as follows:
The Sunday noon date is from
12:30 to 3 o'clock. All other noon
dates are from 11:30 in the morn
ing till 2:30 in the afternoon.
Night dates, for every evening
including Sunday, are from 5:30
to 8 o'clock.
The first rushing period begins
at noon, Sunday, April 14, and
extends to Friday night, April 19,
Any additional information may
be obtained from Waldo Ashby,
secretary of the Interfraternity
Council.
