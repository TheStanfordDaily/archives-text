# BIG MEET ROOTERS' TICKET SALE STARTS TODAY IN OLD UNION
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the annual
Big Meet between Stanford and
the University of California will
be placed on sale today and to
morrow at the A.S.S.U. offices
in the Old Union. The meet is
to be held Saturday, April 20, in
the Stanford Stadium at 1:45
o'clock.
The ticket office will be open
from 9 to 12 and 1 to 5. The
price of the rooters' tickets will
be $1.00 and additional tickets
may be purchased for $1.50. Af
ter five o'clock Tuesday night
all tickets will be $1.50, accord
ing to Jake Irwin, manager of
the B.A.C. ticket department.
Rooters' tickets for the Cali
fornia-Stanford baseball game
will be sold for 35 cents at the
same time and will not be sold
at the game. General Admission
tickets for the game may be
bought either at the A.S.S.U. of
fices or at the gate for 75 cents.
It is expected that the track
meet will be over before the first
inning of the ball game has been
played.
The policy of allowing each
student to purchase as many
rooters' tickets as he has stu
dent body cards will be main
tained in this ticket sale.
