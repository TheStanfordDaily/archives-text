# AIRWAY CONTROL CHANGED
## 
### 
NEW YORK, April 14. (/P)—Control
of the Colonial Airways System, hold
ers of the original government air
mail contracts, is expected to pass
shortly to the Aviation Corporation,
it was made known today by Graham
E. Grosvenor, president of Aviation
Corporation.
