# Musicale To Be Given By Toyon Club Next Sunday
## 
### 
Toyon Club will give its annual
musicale next Sunday evening, and
the committee headed by Lloyd Smith
has sent out invitations to all faculty
members, and to all sororities and
fraternities. All Stanford students are
invited.
This year a particularly good pro
pram has been secured. Mrs. Samuel
Swayze Seward, Jr., contralto, will be
the soloist of the evening. A fine
string trio of campus musicians will
bo another feature. Elwyn B. liugge,
violiuist; Leland R. Long, celloist;
and Frances Sanford, pianist, are
arranging a varied program.
