# Games Are Scheduled For Mural Soft Ball Rounds
## 
### 
The second round of intramural
soft ball will be held this Thursday
and Friday, April 18 and 19. The
games are scheduled for the regular
intramural diamonds, and are to begin
at. 4:15 o'clock.
The games Thursday are; Encina
3W vs. Toyon, Sequoia II vs. Tlieta
Xi, Phi Kappa Sigma vs. Encina Base
ment, and Kappa Alpha vs. Encina
IW. Friday's games are: Kappa Sigma
vs. Zeta Psi, and Encina 4E vs. Phi
Kappa Psi.
