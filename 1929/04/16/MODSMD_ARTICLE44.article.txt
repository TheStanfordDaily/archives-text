# Doeg, Wheatley, Weesner, And Hall, Tennis Victors
## 
### 
Doeg, Whcatley, Weesner, and Hall
were victors in the first round of the var
sity's round-robin tennis tournament
which came to a close last week. The
second round started yesterday and will
last the entire week.
Following are the results of the first
round: Doeg d. Driscoll, 8-6, 8-6;
Whcatley d. Easton, 8-6, 2-6, 6-4; Wees
ner d. Farwell, 6-3, 6-3; and Hall d.
McKellip, 6-2, 6-3. The results of the
Forbes-Stephens match were not posted.
The schedule of second round matches
follows: Doeg vs. Easton, Farwell vs.
Wheatley, Forbes vs. Weesner, and Hall
vs. Stephens.
