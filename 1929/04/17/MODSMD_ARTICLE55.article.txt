# INVESTIGATE MAIL THEFT
## 
### 
SAN JOSE, April 16. (£>) Federal j
agents today were investigating the j
theft of three sacks of registered j
mail, which, they revealed, had been
stolen from the branch postoffice here J
several days ago. The empty pouches, ;
which had contained 53 pieces of j
registered mail, were found on a i
ranch near here yesterday.
