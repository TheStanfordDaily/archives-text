# Dr. McCowan To Lecture Sunday About Christianity
## 
### 
Dr. C. C. McCowan, dean of the
Theological department of the College
of the Pacific, will give the third of
tlie series of lectures on "Origins and
Early History of Christianity" which
are sponsored by the Stanford
Y.M.C.A., at 7:30 p.m., on Sunday,
April 21.
In his lecture, entitled "The Story
of the Gospels," Dr. McCowan will
open that part of the series which is
devoted to the life of Christ and the
growth of the new religion.
