# HOOVER GIVES OFFICE TO KELLOGG, FORMER STANFORD PROFESSOR
## 
### 
Vernon Kellogg, former professor of
biology at Stanford and at present sec
retary of the National Research Council,
has been appointed by President Herbert
Iloover as a member of an informal ad
visory group to aid the director of the
national park service in the development
of national parks.

An old friend of President Hoover.
Mr. Kellogg was a member of the Stan
ford faculty from 1894 tn 1920, except
for several years during the war when
he assisted Hoover, then food adminis
trator in Europe. They worked together
from 1915 to 1919.
