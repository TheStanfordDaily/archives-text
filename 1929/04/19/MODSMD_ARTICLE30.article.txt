# Euphronia Team Debates Centuriata Club Tonight
## 
### 
Euphronia Debating Society will meet
Centuriata Debating Society of the Uni
versity of California at Berkeley tonight,
when the question, "Resolved, That a
popular referendum on the declaration of
war would promote world peace," will be
debated. The Oregon style of debating
is to be used, and Euphronia will defend
the negative of the question. Edmond
Jones will give the constructive speech
for Euphronia, and George Shibley will
cross-examine the affirmative speaker and
later give the rebuttal for the negative.
