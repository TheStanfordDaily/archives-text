# GLEE CLUB TICKET SALE BEGINS TODAY
## 
### 
The advance tickets sale for
the Stanford Glee Club Annual
Home Concert and Theatre Fund
Benefit, which is to be given Fri
day night, April 26, in the As
sembly Hall, commences today
at the A.S.S.U. offices in the Old
Union. The hours for distribu
tion are from S-12 every morning,
and 1-5 each afternoon through
Friday. Choice reserved seat
ducats will be offered at 75 cents,
and 50 cents is the price of gen
eral admission tickets. One-half
of the gross proceeds is to be
turned into the Theatre Fund,
