# Valianos Elected To Lead 1930 Bear Baseball Team
## 
### 
John Valianos, junior at California,
and shortstop of the championship
California baseball varsity, was elect
ed Saturday night to lead the Bears
in the 19U0 baseball season. He was
the shining light of Saturday's Bear-
Card game by getting four hits in five
trips to the plate, besides figuring in
double plays and two spectacular
catches.
