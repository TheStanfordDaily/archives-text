# RIVER LEVEE BREAKS
## 
### 
CANTON, Missouri, April 21. (/P)~
Tne Mississippi River levee crumbled
under the pressure of flood waters
from tributary streams here today.
More than 200 homes were flooded and
rail tiafflc was paralyzed. There was
no loss of life as far as could be ascer
tained.

DARWIN, Australia, April 21. (Jp)—
The airplane Kookaburra which dis
appeared ten days ago with Lieut.
Keith Anderson and Robert Hitchcock,
was found in the Tanimi desert today.
