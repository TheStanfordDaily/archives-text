TUESDAY
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Mr. Pois
ter in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.in.—lllustrated lecture on "Can
the Movies Teach?" by Mr. George
E. Stone, in the Assembly Hall. The
public is invited.
WEDNESDAY
7:30 p.m.—Zoology Club meeting at
the home of Prof, and Mrs. E. G.
Martin, 770 Santa Ynez St.
7:30 p.m.—There will be a re-showing
of the film "Beautiful Germany" in
the Little Theatre. Everyone is
urged to see these views. No admis
sion.
THURSDAY
4:15 p.m.—Lecture on "National-mind
edness and International-minded
ness," by Professor George Herbert
Mead, of the University of Chicago;
in the Little Theatre. Open to the
public.

ter, in the Memorial Church.

morial Church. Speaker, Rev. Sidney
Snow. Baritone soloist, Major F. H.
Forbes.
8:15 p.m.—Stanford Glee Club Annual
Home Concert, in the Assembly
Hall. For benefit of Theatre Fund.
