# German Film To Be Shown For Second Time Wednesday
## 
### 
Due to numerous requests, there
will he a second showing of the film
"Beautiful Germany" in the Little
Theatre next Wednesday night, April
17, at 7:30 o'clock.
The film shows many interesting
views of Germany and is being shown
for the benefit of those who were
unable to see the picture at its previ
ous showing last week. There is no
admission charge.
