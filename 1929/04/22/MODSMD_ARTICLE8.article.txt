# University World Cruise Announced For October 10
## 
### 
The Third University World Cruise,
sailing from New York October 10
for a seven months' world tour during
which students and professors will
carry on a college year of study has
just been announced. The Cunard
Cruise-Ship, "Letitia," has been char
tered. Dr. James E. Lough will have
charge of all educational matters to
gether with professors from leading
American universities.
The first semester is spent on a
three months' European tour; the
second is held on board the "letitia."
A student may enroll for either of the
semesters, thus missing only one-half
year. Arrangements have been made
with many universities whereby the
courses taken may be accepted for
credits toward a degree.
