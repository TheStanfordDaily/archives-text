# C. H. Delaney, Electrical Engineer, Speaks Tonight
## 
### 
O. H. Delaney, assistant operation
engineer for the Pacific Gas and Elec
tric Company will lecture on steam
I power plants before the Stanford
branch of the American Institute of
Electrical Engineers at a meeting In
Room 283 of the Engineering building
at 7:30 p.m. Special reference will be
given to the equipment of Station C
of the Pacific Gas and Electric Com
pany at Oakland. The talk will be
illustrated by lantern slides.
After the lecture, a short businena
meeting will be held to discuss a
smoker scheduled for May 8. Saturday
the members of the chapter will make
au inspection trip to Station C.
