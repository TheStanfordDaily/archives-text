# SINCLAIR'S SENTENCE UPHELD
##

WASHINGTON, April 22. (JP)— The
Supreme Court ruled today that Harry
F Sinclair, unless pardoned, must
serve three months in jail for refusal
to answer questions before the Senate
Teapot Dome Committee.
