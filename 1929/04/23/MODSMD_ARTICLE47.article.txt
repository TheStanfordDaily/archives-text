# COUZENS APPOINTED CHAIRMAN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 22.
ator Couzens, Republican, Michigan,
was given the chairmanship of the
Senate Interstate Commerce Commit
tee today by the Republican caucus of
the Senate which ratified the new as
signments of members of the com
mittees.
The University of California campus
covers an area of 535 acres.
