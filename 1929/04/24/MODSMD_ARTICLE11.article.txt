# PRESIDENT-ELECT OF A.W.S. RESIGNS FROM W.A.A. BOARD OFFICE
## 
### 
Benetta Merner, junior representa
tive on W.A.A. Board, tendered her
resignation from the Board at its
meeting last night. She was recently
elected President of Associated Wo
men Students for next year.
The committee to make nominations
for W.A.A. officers for next year was
appointed. Lucy Ititter, Editha Wright,
! Rosamund Clarke, and Gertrude Laid
law were named. Those nominated by
the committee will be voted upon at
the time of the A.S.S.U. spring elec
tions.
