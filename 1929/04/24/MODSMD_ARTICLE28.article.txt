# Library Director Speaks Tonight At English Club
## 
### 
Nathan Van Patten, director of the
Library, will be the featured speaker
at a meeting of the English Club to
night. A new treasurer will also be
elected. The meeting is to be held at
the home of Hallie Keating in Los
Altos.
All members who wish transporta
tion should be at the Women's Club
house promptly at 7:30 o'clock.
