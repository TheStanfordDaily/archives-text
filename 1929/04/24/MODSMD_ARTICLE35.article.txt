# GLEE CLUB MEETS
## 
### 
The Women's Glee Club will meet
tonight at 7 o'clock in Room 84 to
plan a tentative radio concert to be
given over KPO the latter part of
May. Dr. Griggs, conductor of the Glee
Club choruses, requests full attend
ance in order that complete arrange
ments may be made. Women inter
ested in the work may join at this
time.
