# Frosh And Sophs To Meet In First Class Net Play
## 
### 
The freshmen will clash with the
sophomores at 3 p.m. tomorrow in the
first match of the annual Interclass
Tenuis Tournament. The four classes
will play a round robin, the final
standing to count toward the Inter
class Trophy. Class numerals will be
awarded to the members of the win
ning team.
Men on the sophomore and fresh
man team have not been announced.
There will be eight men on a team
and any other member of the class
may challenge any one of the men
for a place.
