# KATHERINE DOYLE WINS CAPTAINCY OF SENIOR CLASS TRACK WOMEN
## 
### 
Captains of women's class track
teams were elected yesterday at tho
regular track practice. Dorothy
Sweet, Margaret Bullard, Katharine
Sobey, and Katherine Doyle will cap
tain the freshman, sophomore, junior,
and senior squads respectively.
All four captains have been out
standing players in the late basketball
and hockey seasons of fall and winter
quarters. Katherine Doyle will take
the leadership of the senior team for
the second time this year. She was
hockey captain for the fourth-year
women in the fall.
