# BRANNER SMOKER
## 
### 
The members of Branner Club will
liold their first smoker of the spring
quarter tonight at 7:30 o'clock in the
< lub lobby. The program will be short,
and will include a discussion of the
coming Branner dance. Harry School
field, club president, will preside.

The gold pen with which President
Lincoln signed the proclamation
emancipating the slaves January 1,
1863, is being offered for sale.
