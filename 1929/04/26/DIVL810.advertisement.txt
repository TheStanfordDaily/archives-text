If your house
should be
ransacked today—
Or should he burned to the ground
—what would you miss most—
negotiable papers that cannot be re
placed? Important contracts? Per
sonal treasures that cannot be re
stored? The very fire and burglary
insurance policies that would prove
so helpful in this emergency? Why
take a chance on losing your valu
ables when you can place them in a
safe deposit box in this bank—safe
from fire and theft—and at little
cost?
