# The 						Daily Congratulates-
## 
### 
Rigorous tryouts for the Daily staff in progress since the first of the
Quarter ended last night with the addition of fourteen names to the repor
torial list in the masthead. The Daily congratulates the new reporters
and believes they will find the time expended in work on the paper well
worth while. The new staff members are Beatrice Bishop, Elizabeth
Clarke, John Gillingham, Jr., Genevieve Goddard, Ellis Gait, William
Ilartsough, Arthur McGarr, Ruth Miner, Elisabeth Nourse, Jean Rosen
berg. Catherine Spaulding, Willis Sullivan, Robert VanEtten, Isabell
Warren.
