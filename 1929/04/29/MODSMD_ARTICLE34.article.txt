# GANGSTERS STAGE SHOOTING
## 
### 
CHICAGO, April 28. (Jp)—South '
Side gangsters staged two shooting f
forays just before dawn today. A
saloonkeeper was killed in one; in
the other two detectives interfered
and brought down a gunman-member
of a decadent beer gang, wounding
him seriously.
