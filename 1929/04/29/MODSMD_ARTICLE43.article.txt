MONDAY
3:3op.m.—Tennis men who played in
California matches, both frosh and
varsity, meet in Room 45, B.A.C.
building. Very important; wear your
playing outfits and bring racquets.

20, Chemistry building. Professor
James M. Luck will discuss "The
Biochemistry of Urea."

I). Allen in Memorial Church.

scheduled for 7 o'clock will be held
in the Roble library immediately
after the installation banquet.

in the lobby of the Old Union. Plans
for the remainder of the quarter
will be discussed.

"La Reja," a Spanish comedy by
Quintero, in the Women's Club
bouse. Public invited.

ton, John Bunyan, and Their Times,"
by W. W. Ellsworth, in the Assem
bly Hall.
THURSDAY
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren 1).
Allen in the Memorial Church.

morial Church. Speaker, Reverend
Ernest Bradley, San Rafael.

men's Clubhouse. Wives of students
are invited.
