# ROBINSON SUMMER ORGANIST
## 
### 
Raymond Robinson, organist at
King's Chapel in Boston, will be or
ganist here (luring August while War
ren I). Allen is in the East. Allen is
to take Robinson's work at King's
Chapel during the time Robinson is
here.
Organ recitals will be given several
afternoons each week by Robinson,
as is the custom each summer.
