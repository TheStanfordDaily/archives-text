# Temporary Insanity Plea Is Women's Debate Topic
## 
### 
"Resolved, That the plea of tem
porary insanity as a defense for crime
should be prohibited by law," is the
subject to be debated by University
of Southern California and Stanford
women on Thursday.
Hallien Hickman and Jean Magnus
are to uphold the negative side of the
question here in the Little Theatre,
while Alice Behner and Isabell War
! ren will make up the affirmative team
which goes to Los Angeles. Mr. Justus
Wheeler Barger of the English De
partment has been coaching the Stan
ford women.
