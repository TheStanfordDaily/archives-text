# "Monte Carlo" Theme Will Be Used At Encina Formal
## 
### 
Encina Club will give Its only for
mal of the school year on Friday,
May 17. The inspiration of the dance
is Monte Carlo, and this idea will be
carried out in the decorations. Free
dom of the Latin temperament will be
especially emphasized. Bids, costing
four dollars apieco, will be placed on
sale in a short time.
