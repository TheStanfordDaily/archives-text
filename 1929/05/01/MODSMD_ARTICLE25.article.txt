# Quad Art Work Declared Excellent By S.F. Artists
## 
### 
The art work on the 1929 Quad has
been met with favor by the San Fran
cisco artists. The color plates, which
were done by Rauschnabel, and the
black and whites which were done by
Kegg have been declared very excel
lent pieces of work.
The opener for the Razz section is
also said to be very good. This work,
which was done by Ted Krough, has
been declared unexcelled by the Ster
ling Engraving Co. The title for the
Razz section is to be the "Let Down."
The object of this name is to be ex
plained in the Quad.
