# Branner Club To Sponsor Sport Dance Friday Night
## 
### 
Branner Club is to entertain Friday
night with an informal sport dance.
Bids are on sale this week, and may
be obtained from all members of the
Club's executive committee, and from
Harry Schoolfield, Leonard Bartlett,
Larry Heiges, Wareham Seaman, Tex
Cox, Arthur Angel, and John Clark.
Sport clothes will be in vogue for
the evening's entertainment. Special
favors in keeping with the general
scheme of dance will be given out.
Tom Murphy's Springtime Serenaders
have been secured for the "Spring is
here" festivities.
