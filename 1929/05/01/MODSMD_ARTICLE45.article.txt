# HEARST CONTEST CLOSES
## 
### 
NEW YORK, April 30. (>P)— Be
tween 40,000 and 50,000 competitors
have submitted plans in the $25,000
temperance prize contest, sponsored
by William Randolph Hearst, which
closes at midnight tonight.

United States has 406 cities under
the city manager plan.
