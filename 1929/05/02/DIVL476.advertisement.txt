Announcement
THE TREND OF THE
AMERICAN UNIVERSITY
By
David Starr Jordan
will be published by Stanford University Press on Tues
day, May 7, in an edition limited to 250 copies, signed
and numbered, of which 240 are for sale. The book con
tains three essays by Dr. Jordan, presenting a panorama
of the development of higher education from 1888 to
1928. The edition is printed in large, clear type on white,
laid finish, deckle-edge paper, bound in dark cardinal
suede leather. The price is $7.50, net.
STANFORD UNIVERSITY PRESS
Stanford University, California
