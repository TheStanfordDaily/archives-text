# REV. ERNEST BRADLEY OF SAN RAFAEL WILL LEAD VESPER SERVICE
## 
### 
Vesper services in the Memorial
Church at 4:45 this afternoon will be
led by Rev. Ernest Bradley of San Ra
fael. Mrs. R. F. Duryea, soprano, will
sing a solo by Gounod.
The organ recital, which lasts from
4:15 until the vesper services at 4:45,
will include the following numbers played
by Warren D. Allen, University organist:
Processional Stanza Th. Dubois
Scherzo Edward S. Barnes
Toccata on Gregorian Theme
Edward S. Barnes
Nocturne in E Flat Chopin
Sonata, No. 6, in D Minor. .Mendelssohn
1. Choral with variations
2. Fugue
3. Andante
The same program will be given on
Sunday at 4 p.m.
