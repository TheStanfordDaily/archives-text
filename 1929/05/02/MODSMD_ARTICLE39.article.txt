# Debate Society Will Hold Tryouts For Team Tonight
## 
### 
At a meeting of Euphronia Debating
Society which will be held at 7:30 to
night in the lobby of the Old Union, the
team will be chosen which will represent
the society in the debate with Wranglers
on the question, "Resolved, That Athlet
ics are overemphasized at Stanford."
Tryoutees may speak for five minutes
upon any subject. Business plans for the
quarter, including the annual banquet of
the society, will also be discussed at this
meeting.
