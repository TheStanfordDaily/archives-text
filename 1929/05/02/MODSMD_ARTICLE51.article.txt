# Women Debaters To Face U.S.C. Team This Evening
## 
### 
Alice Behner and Isabell Warren left
last night for Angeles where they
will debate with University of Southern
California women tonight on the ques
tion, "Resolved, That the plea of tem
porary insanity as a defense for crime
should be prohibited by law." They will
uphold the affirmative side of the ques
tion, while at the same time Hallien
Hickman and Jean Magnus will take the
negative against U.S.C. here in the Little
Theatre at 8 o'clock.
