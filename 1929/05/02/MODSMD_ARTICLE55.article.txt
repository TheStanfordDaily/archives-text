# AVIATOR DIES IN CRASH
## 
### 
SAN ANTONIO, Texas, May 1. (£>)—
Lieut. J. C. Banta, Brooks Field flier,
was killed today when an army plane
crashed near here. Authorities at the
field said the flier was attempting to
make a 180 degree turn for a landing
at the field when his ship went into
a spin at an altitude of 400 feet. He
was flying alone.
