# Glee Club Tryouts To Be Held Monday, Wednesday
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Men's Glee Club,
scheduled to be held last night, were
postponed on account of the Labor
Day activities. Club Manager Earl
Reed announced yesterday that new
dates are Monday, May 0, and Wednes
day, May 8. They will be held in the
music room in the basement of the
Engineering building.
Reed urges all students who have
the ability and desire to sing to try
out for membership in the club. In
joining the organization now, one will
become acquainted with the various
selections which have been chosen for
the early autumn quarter appearances.
Freshmen and sophomores are given
a special Invitation to attend the try
outs.
