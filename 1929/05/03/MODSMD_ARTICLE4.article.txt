# Draper And Vincenti Win Final Moot Court Contest
## 
### 
Contending that the 1923 Amend
ment to the California Constitution
does give the wife a vested interest
in community property, Murray Dra
per and Louis Vincenti won the final
round of the annual Moot Court tour
nament held last night in Room 12
of the Law building.
Vincenti and Draper, besides win
ning the contest, will receive a $200
prize from Charles Eels, a retired at
torney of Carmel. Call and Armour,
the opposing team, will receive a set
ol Professor Owens' Form Books.
Judges at the trial were former
Supreme Court Justice Charles A.
Shurtleff, Frank M. Angelotti, and
Frank Monroe.
