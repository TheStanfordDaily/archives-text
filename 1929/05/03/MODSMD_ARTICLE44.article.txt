# AWAIT AGREEMENT

WASHINGTON, D.C., May 2 (>P). —
The Peruvian and Chilean ambassadors
today remained within call expecting an
announcement from Lima, Peru, that the
two governments concerned had reached
an agreement on the Tacna-Arica dispute.
