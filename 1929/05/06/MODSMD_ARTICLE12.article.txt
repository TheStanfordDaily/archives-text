# ENTRY LISTS POSTED FOR ANNUAL R.O.T.C. HORSE EXHIBIT-STONE
## 
### 
Entry lists for Scabbard and
Blade's tenth annual R.O.T.C. horse
show, which will be held at 1:30
o'clock Friday, May 31, will be posted
all this week at the Military Depart
ment stables, Nevdone Stone, chair
man, announced Friday.
Anyone desiring to participate must
sign up for his events before Satur
day. Drawings for horses will be held
at 1:30 o'clock Saturday. Persons
signing up for events must be present
at the drawing or appoint someone to
represent them.
The show this year will be limited
to members of the R.0.T.C., and will
be held in the new riding hall near
the highway field. The list of classes
includes: Mounted Tug of War, Jump
ing in Pairs, Musical Chairs, Mounted
Wrestling, Military Jumping, Horse
manship Contest, Polo Bending Race,
Polo Stake Race, Touch and Out
Jumping, and an exhibition Monkey
Drill by the members of the enlisted
detail.
