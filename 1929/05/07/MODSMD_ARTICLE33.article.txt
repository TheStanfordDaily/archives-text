# Allen Plays New Pieces In Today's Organ Recital
## 
### 
Two new American compositions
will be played by Warren D. Allen,
University organist, in his recital at
4:15 this afternoon in the Memorial
Church. "Cantilena and Musetta" is
by Dr. Mauro-Cottone, of New York;
"Cortege Triumphal," by H. J. Stew
art of San Diego.
Mr. Allen will play in addition the
following numbers:
Chant de printemps Joseph Bonnet
To Spring Edvard Grieg
May Night Selim Palmgren
Faith in Spring Franz Schubert
