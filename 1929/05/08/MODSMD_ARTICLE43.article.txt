# Professors To Speak At A.I.E.E. Smoker Thursday
## 
### 
In order to get the engineers ac
quainted with the well-known men
on the campus, the American Insti
tute of Electrical Engineers will hold
a smoker tomorrow night at 7:30
o'clock in the Old Union, when three
famous Stanford engineers will speak.
Election of new officers will also be
held at this time.
Charles David Marx, Emeritus Pro
fessor of Civil Engineering, will de
scribe Stanford as he knew it "in the
old days." Theodore J. Hoover, Dean
of the Stanford School of Engineering,
will give a brief talk on engineering
in foreign countries. Professor Harris
J. Ryan will also speak.
