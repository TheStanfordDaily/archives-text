# Fifteen Junior Prom Bids To Be Put On Sale Today
## 
### 
Fifteen remaining bids for the
Junior Prom will be put on sale at
1 o'clock this afternoon in the
A.S.S.U. office. Three hundred bids
have already been sold, according to
Dean Morrison, general chairman of
Junior Week.
Programs will be distributed from
1 o'clock until 3 o'clock today, tomor
row, and Friday upon presentation of
an admission ticket at the A.S.S.U.
office in the Old Union.
The work of decorating will begin
this afternoon, announces Saff Nye,
chairman of decorations. Jack Swales
and his University of California or
chestra will furnish the music for the
dance. Swales and his musicians have
orchestrated several of the numbers
from the junior opera, "Moving On,"
for this occasion.
