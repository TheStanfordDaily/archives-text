# Encina Formal To Be May 17; Bids Going Fast At $4
## 
### 
Bids for the Encina Formal on May
17 are now on sale at the A.S.S.U.
offices in the Old Union. Latest price
quotations show the bids to be hold
ing their own at four dollars per.

Hub Morgan's orchestra from Cali
fornia will play for the dance. The
floor will be decorated with scenes
and draperies to carry out the Monte
Carlo idea. Jim Reed is chairman of
the dance committee composed of
Spencer Davis, Berk Anthony, Reed
Briggs, Carl Cartwright, and Walter
Cooper. Bids may be secured from
any one of the committee members.
