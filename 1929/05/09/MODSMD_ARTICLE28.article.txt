# MIX ACCUSED BY GRAND JURY
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 8.
Mix, who rode to fame and wealth in
filmdom's Wild West, tonight was ac
cused in one federal grand jury in
dictment of falsifying his income tax
returns, and in two more of conspir
ing with others to withhold from the
government $112,114.11 in taxes due
for the years of 1925, 1926, and 1927.
