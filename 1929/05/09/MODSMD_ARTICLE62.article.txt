# Perpetuation Of Theatre Fund Committee Planned
## 
### 
Plans for the organization of a
standing committee to perpetuate the
work of the Theatre Fund were dis
cussed yesterday at a meeting held
by Jack Shoup, chairman of the drive.
Suggestions for the members to com
pose the committee were considered.
Suggestions for the organization of
an alumni committee were considered
so that the campaign for the New
Theatre can be carried on among the
former students of the University.
