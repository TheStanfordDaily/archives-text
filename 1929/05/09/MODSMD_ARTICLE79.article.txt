# Congressman Takes Stand In Trial On Liquor Charge
## 
### 
KEY WEST, Fla., May 8
gressman Alfred M. Michaelson of Chi
cago took the witness stand late today in
his trial for alleged prohibition law vio
lation and testified that three of the six
trunks he brought into Key West from
Cuba oil an order granting him freedom
