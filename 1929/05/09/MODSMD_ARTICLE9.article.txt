# DELIVERY OF PROM PROGRAMS TO BEGIN IN OLD UNION TODAY
## 
### 
Because of the San Francisco
engravers' strike, the junior
prom programs were not de
livered yesterday as promised.
The programs will be distributed
today and tomorrow, upon pre
sentation of an admission ticket,
from 1 o'clock to 5 o'clock at the
A.S.S.IT. office in the Old Union.
Tho eight remaining bids for
the prom will be put on sale at
1 o'clock today at the A.S.S.IT.
office. Dean Morrison, general
chairman of Junior week, an
nounces that no extra bids will
be offered for sale.
