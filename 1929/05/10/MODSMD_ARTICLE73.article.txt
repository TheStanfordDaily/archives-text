# EVACUATE CHINESE PROVINCE
## 
### 
HONGKONG, May 9. (Jp) —Consular
instructions have been given Ameri
can women and children to evacuate
that part of Kwangsi Province near
Kweilin. For the prosent tliey are
being stopped at Wuchow, on the
Kwansi-Kwantung border to await the
British gunboat Moorhen.

A system of deferred pledging is
now being considered by the Univer
sity of Oregon. At present pledging
takes place at the beginning of the
fall term.

Just arrived from London—a new
line of Dunhill, Sasieni, and Comoy
pipes. See them at Morey's.—Adv.
