# Glee Club Appointments To End Two-Week Trials
## 
### 
Appointments to membership in the
Stanford Men's Glee Club will be
made at the end of two weeks' trial
Hinging with the club at the regular
rehearsals, according to an announce
ment made last night by Earl Reed,
manager of the Glee Club.
With the appearance of twelve new
men at the meeting last night, the
number of tryoutees was swelled to
thirty-four. Director C. Albert Kul
mann is well pleased with the turn
out and quality of the prospective new
material.
