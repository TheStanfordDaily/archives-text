# Allen Features American Melodies This Afternoon
## 
### 
Compositions of 11. C. Nearing and
Carl McKinley, who belong to the ris
ing school of American organ com
posers, will be Included in the pro
gram of Warren D. Allen, University
organist, at 4:15 p.m. today in the
Memorial Church. Beginning on Juno
16, organ recitals will be given at
7:30 p.m. instead of in the afternoon.
Today's program is as follows:
Cortege Triumphal H. J. Stewart
Nature Sketches Joseph W. Clokoy
a) Whispering Moth
b) Dripping Spring
Mirage
The Old Mission
From "Scenes from the Mexican
Desert" H. C. Nearing
Cantilena ...Carl McKinley
Tales from the Arabian Nights
R. S. Stoughton
The Fisherman and the Genie
Julamar of the Sea
A Joyous March Leo Sowerby
