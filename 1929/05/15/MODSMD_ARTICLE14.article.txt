# Campus Officer Resumes Work After Broken Ankle
## 
### 
After a week's layoff from his duties
because a fractured ankle sustained
last Thursday, John Olsen, campus
traffic officer, returned to his post
yesterday to resume work.
For a few weeks ho will be forced
to attend to patrol duties in an auto
mobile because tho broken bone has
not healed onough to permit riding a
motorcycle.
