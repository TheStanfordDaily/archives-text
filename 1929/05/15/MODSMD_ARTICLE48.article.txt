# Encina Formal Bid Sale Opened To Public Today
## 
### 
Tickets for the Encina Club Formal,
to be held Friday, go on sale in the
Old Union at 9 o'clock this morning.
Encina men will be able to purchase
their bids at the hall.
Jimmy Reed is in charge of the
dance which will carry out the idea
of Monte Carlo. The California Deco
rating Company is making the Encina
lobby resemble a gambling hall.
Painted plates with scenes of the
original Monte Carlo are to be hung
around the walls. The programs will
help to convey the impression. Hud
Morgan's orchestra from U.C. will fur
nish the music.
