# Euphronia And Wranglers Will Debate This Evening
## 
### 
Euphronia and Wranglers Societies
will meet in the Women's Clubhouse
tonight in the debate on "Resolved,
That Athletics Are Over-emphasized
at Stanford" which was postponed
from last Friday.
Violet Crosbie and Helen Smith of
Wranglers will oppose Leon Carley
and Donald Galbraith, who will uphold
the negative. Carley is a varsity de
bater and coach of the freshman de
bate team, while Galbraith will make
his first appearance tonight. The pub
lic is invited to attend the debate.
