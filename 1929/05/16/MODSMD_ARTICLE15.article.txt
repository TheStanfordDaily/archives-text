# Rev. McCash Will Preside At Vesper Service Today
## 
### 
At the vesper service immediately
after the organ recital this afternoon
at 4:45 p.m., the Rev. Wellington
McCash will preside, and Mrs. R. J.
Jungermann will sing "Adore and Be
Still," by Gounod. The organ recital
at 4:15 will be as follows:
Preludio and Adagio
Alexander Guilmant
From the Sonata, No. 3 in C Minor
Pastorale in E Major C6sar Franck
Andante con moto, from the "Fifth
Symphony" Beethoven
Vision Rheinberger
