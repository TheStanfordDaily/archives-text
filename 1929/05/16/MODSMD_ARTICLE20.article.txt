# Engineering Departments Given Motor For Tests
## 
### 
Through the efforts of W. S. James
and H. S. Vance of the Studebaker
Corporation, Stanford's Mechanical
Engineering department has recently
been presented with a Studebaker
President Eight automobile engine.
According to Professor Domonoske,
of the Engineering School, this motor
is one of the latest in the newer de
velopments of automobile engineering,
it being an eight in line, 337 cubic
inch displacement, L head, and de
veloping 114 horsepower at 3200 revo
lutions per minute.
