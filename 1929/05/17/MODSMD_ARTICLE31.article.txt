# Newman Club Adopted As Name Of Junipero Serras
## 
### 
Junipero Serra Club, organization of
Catholic students, changed its name to
Newman Club at the meeting held Wed
nesday night. Affiliation with the national
Newman Club, which has chapters in
all large universities, is the purpose of
the change.
Election of new officers for the coming
year were held. Joseph De Lucclii was
elected president; Mary Jane Pollock,
vice-president; Virginia Daugherty, sec
retary; and Manuel Espinosa, treasurer.
A committee to draw up a new con
stitution for Newman Club was appoint
ed, and consists of the following: Robert
Bilafer, chairman, Frank Carroll, Joseph
De Lucchi, and Manuel Espinosa.
