# LEGISLATURE ADJOURNS
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, May 16 (£>).—The
forty-eighth biennial session of the state
legislature adjourned sine die at 1:20
a.m. today. The senate, last to finish its
file, notified the assembly at that hour
that it had completed its work, where
upon the two houses were declared offi
cially and actually adjourned.
