# Reverend Beal Will Lead Services At Chapel Sunday
## 
### 
Reverend Dean Beal of St. Paul's Ca
thedral, Los Angeles, will speak at the
eleven o'clock chapel services Sunday.
Music will include the anthems The Flour
Cometh by Rogers and O Bone Jesu by
Palestrina. Warren D. Allen, University
Organist, will give his usual Sunday af
ternoon recital at four o'clock.
