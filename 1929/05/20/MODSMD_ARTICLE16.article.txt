# Hotel Alameda Orchestra To Play At Toyon Dance
## 
### 
Gordon Heche and his Hotel Ala
meda Orchestra have been engaged
to play at the Toyon Club Sport Dance
Friday night, May 24. This orchestra
played at the fall dance given by
Toyon Club.
The dance committee under the di
rection of I-.es Garner are planning to
make full use of the Toyon court by
placing the furniture out of doors
and giving between-dance entertain
ment there.
The remaining bids for the dance
are being sold by Herb Wenig in
Room 226, Toyon, at $2.76 each. An
engraved invitation goes with each
bid.
