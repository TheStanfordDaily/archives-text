# Inter-Hall Smoker To Be Held In Branner Tonight
## 
### 
Members of Branner, Toyon, and
Sequoia halls, fifth floor Encina resi
dents, and freshmen not pledged to
fraternities, are invited to attend the
spring inter-hall smoker to be held
in Branner Hall lobby at 7:15 o'clock
tonight.
Speeches by Jfilce Irwin, former
Stanford boxing captain and inter
collegiate middleweight champion,
and by Herb Wenig, varsity debater,
will feature the program. Additional
features of the entertainment will be
songs by the Glee Club quartet and
piano solos.
