# LEACHMAN, HAWKINS, PATTON ARE WINNERS OF TENNIS MATCHES
## 
### 
Upperclass titles in the women's
tenuis tournament were decided as
a result of play on the Roble courts
last week. Thelma Patton annexed
the senior championship, while Mar
ian Leachman won the junior compe
tion. The freshman tournament was
completed yesterday when Catherine
Cartlidge lost to Betts Hawkins in a
three-set match.
Patton had little difficulty in van
quishing her opponents. In the first
round she won from Lydia Bowen 6-2,
6-0, and in the finals defeated Sue
Curtis 6-3, 6-1.
In the junior matches Betty Shupp
defaulted to Nan Siegfried, and Mar
ian Leachman drew a bye. Leachman
in the deciding match eliminated Nan
Siegfried, other contestant for the
junior title, 6-0, 6-2.
Sue Betty Dunlap and Barbara
Young are the sophomore finalists.
The closest competition was in the
freshman division. Cartlidge took part
in the only tournament matches which
went to three sets. She played a con
sistent game to win from Eloanor
Ophuls 3-6, 8-6, 6-3, and later lost to
Betts Hawkins 6-4, 1-6, 2-6.
