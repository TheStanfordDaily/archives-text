# EX COMMITTEE NOTES
## 
### 
Special meeting of Ex Committee,
May 20, 1929.
M.S.C. That the following names as
selected by the Rally Committee be
placed on the ballot as nominees for
assistant yell leader: Charles Brink,
Walter Craig, John Hunken, George
McKee, Bob Reynolds, Stewart War
ner.
M.S.C. That W.A.A. ballots be dis
tributed at the A.S.S.U. polls at the
regular election of May 21, 1929.
Respectfully submitted,
Harlow Rothert,
Secretary pro. tem.
