SNAKESKIN SHOES ARE SMART
WITH EVERY DAYTIME COSTUME!
A pump of soft Sunburn Beige Snakeskin is exactly right
to wear with any color, any costume for any daytime
hour of the Summer Season. The beautiful markings of
Watersnake raise it far above the ordinary reptile leath
ers, and its neutral colorings harmonizing with so many
apparel shades, expands its usefulness to a most remark
able degree.
The shoe we illustrate is a hand-turned pump, made from
selected skins not pieced, and each pair is nicely matched
in vamp and quarter. In all it is a most attractive shoe
and for such quality very moderately priced.
