Section 1. The Convalescent Home
Committee.
a. The Convalescent Home Commit
tee shall be a body of five students
who shall have charge of the rela
tions of the Student Body with the
Stanford Convalescent Home.
b. The Committee shall be appointed
each year by the President of the
A.S.S.U.
c. The duties of the Committee shall
be:
(1) To make an annual appeal to
the Faculty and Student Body
of the University for funds
for the maintenance of the
Home.
(2) To take charge of the Labor
Day each year at the Home.
(3) To appoint appropriate man
agers and committees for the
above-mentioned activities.
(4) To take charge of all other
affairs of the Student Body
in relation to the Home.
