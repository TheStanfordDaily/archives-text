# BURNING SHIP REACHES PORT
## 
### 
J SAN FRANCISCO, May 21. (/P)—
With a dangerous fire smouldering in
her hold and her crew of 36 men near
ly exhausted after a two-day battle
against the flames, the British steamer
l Trevanion, en route to Victoria, 8.C.,
from Antilla, Cuba, found a haven in
this port shortly before noon today.

j BRIONI, Italy, May 21. (JP) —James
J. Tunney exclaimed "I know abso
lutely nothing of the report" when
informed today that papers in a suit
for alleged breach of promise of mar
riage had been sent to Bridgeport,
Conn., for filing. "It must be another
joke," declared the former heavy
weight boxing champion.
