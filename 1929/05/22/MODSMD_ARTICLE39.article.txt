# Entire Campus Canvassed For Concert Subscriptions
## 
### 
Canvassing of the campus for
A.S.S.U. Concert Series subscriptions
began last night and the drive will
continue for this week. Halls, sorori
ties, and fraternities will be visited
or tickets may be ordered by mail ad
dressed to Box 1151,• campus. The
A.S.S.U. office in the Old Union is
open between 8:80 and 12 o'clock and
from 1 to 5 daily.
The tentative program of artists is
as follows: Will Rogers, Efrem Zim
balist, San Francisco Symphony Or
chestra, and two others to be chosen
from the following: John Charles
Thomas, Lawrence Tibbett, Sigrid
Onegin, and the Kedrof Quartet.
