# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
WEDNESDAY
2:30 p.m.—Annual Chemistry Depart
ment Picnic wiil be held at the end
of Foothill Road.
4:00 p.m.—All junior contestants who
did not compete in the fencing pre
liminaries Tuesday afternoon be on
hand at Encina Gym promptly.
7:30 p.m.—There will be a tryoutee's
meeting in the A.S.S.U. office in the
Old Union. Tryoutees already signed
up and others interested report
there at that time.
7:30 p.m.—There will be a meeting of
the tryoutees for the A.S.S.U. Con
cert Series Staff in the A.S.S.U. of
fice at the Old Union.
7:30 p.m.->-Zo6logy Club meeting at
the home of Professor and Mrs. R.
W. Doane, 762 Dolores St., Stanford.
8:00 p.m.—French Club in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Election of officers.
8:00 p.m.—Le Cercle Francais will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse.
THURSDAY
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital in the Memo
rial Church.
4:00 p.m.—Finals of the University
fencing championships will be held
in Encina gym.
6:15 p.m.—Euphronia Debating Society
will hold its annual end-of-the-year
banquet in the main dining room of
the Stanford Union. Colonel McCabe
will speak. Election of next year's
officers.
FRIDAY
12:15 p.m.—Luncheon for all sopho
more women, honoring Dean Yost,
at the Women's Clubhouse. There
will be no charge for the luncheon.
MISCELLANEOUS
Freshmen who wish to reserve dormi
tory rooms for fall quarter should
call at Dean of Men's office Monday,
Tuesday, or Wednesday, May 21,
21, or 22. Deposits will be accepted
for those high enough on waiting
lists to secure rooms.
Men who wish to take out or renew
tuition notes should call at the Dean
of Men's office between May 30 and
June 7.
