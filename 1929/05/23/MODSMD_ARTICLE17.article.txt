# Golf Club Offers Special Monthly Rate To Players
## 
### 
The Capuchino Golf and Country
Club, endeavoring to encourage Penin
sula golf players, is offering a special
monthly rate. A card will be issued
for the price of $3 per month. The
course and clubhouse will be open to
holders of these cards on Mondays,
Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays,
and Fridays. Cards may be obtained
at the club. The Capuchino course is
about fifteen miles from Stanford.
