# Yale Poloist Holds First Ten Goal Handicap Rating
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 22. (£>)—Winston
Guest, former Yale captain, today be
came the first and only player in the
history of American indoor polo to be
given a handicap rating of ten goals.
Previously sharing a nine-goal han
dicap with two other stars, Guest was
raised to sole possession of the new
top rating at the annual spring meet
ing of the Indoor Polo Association of
the United States.

BELFAST, Northern Ireland, May
22. (/P) —Great excitement prevailed
in thousands of Ulster homes today
as for the first time in the history of
the British Isles "flappers" were en
abled to vote.
