# Oakland Portrait Artist Plans Dr. Smith Picture
## 
### 
Peter Van Valkenhurgh, who had an
exhibition of charcoal portraits in the
Art Gallery last year and is well
known on the campus for his life
drawings of faculty members, is now
to do the portrait of Dr. James Perrin
Smith, Professor of Paleontology.

Mr. Van Valkenburgh has completed
portraits of over one hundred leading
Californians, including many in each
of the great universities. Last year
the Oakland artist did approximately
fifteen portraits of men on this cam
pus.
