# Cathcart, Gardner Speak At Sequoia Club Tonight
## 
### 
The ninth annual banquet of the
Sequoia Club will be held in the
Cardinal Hotel tonight with Professor
A. M. Cathcart of the Law depart
ment, and Reverend Charles Gardner
as speakers of the evening.
Professors from the various depart
ments of the University will be guests
of the club. William A. Glen will pre
side as toastmaster.
The club's most popular hasher, who
has been selectod by ballot but whose
name has not been announced, will
be awarded a prize during the evening.
