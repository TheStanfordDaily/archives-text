# PALO ALTO DOG SHOW OPENS THIS MORNING IN STANFORD STADIUM
## 
### 
The Palo Alto Kennel Club will open
its first annual Dog Show this morning |
at 10 o'clock in the Stadium. Between ;
four and five hundred dogs are entered, j
Prominent entries include two Great j
Danes belonging to Mr. H. B. Hostetter J
of Pasadena, and Mrs. Getchell's famous j
"war veteran" "Lupo."
Judging will begin this morning at 10
o'clock, with the sporting dog class. Fi
nal judging will take place Saturday
afternoon at 5 p.m., when the best dog j
of the show will be selected.
Gates will be open Friday and Satur- j
day from 10 a.m. to 7 :30 p.m. Admission ,
is fifty cents, but University students will :
be admitted at half price upon presen- J
tation of student body cards.
