# MERCADO IS ELECTED EUPHRONIA PRESIDENT AT ANNUAL BANQUET
## 
### 
Euphronia debating society held its
annual banquet and election of officers
last night in the Union dining hall. Louis
Mercado was chosen president; Art An
gel, vice-president; Don Galbraith, treas
urer; and George Shibley, secretary.

Colonel E. R. Warner McCabe, who
was the speaker of the evening, discussed
the subject of human nature, showing
that it is universally the same. He re
lated a number of his experiences as
Lieutenant Governor in the Philippines
and as military attache to Germany, Italy,
and Czccho-Slovakia.

The final meeting of of Euphronia will
be held next Wednesday night, when
tentative arrangements for debates for
next year will be made.
