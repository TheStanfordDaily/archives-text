# MacMurray Appointment Hinted By Chinese Press
## 
### 
SHANGHAI, China, May 23 (TP). —
The British North China News printed
a special Tokio dispatch today stating it
had learned that President Hoover was
"seriously thinking of appointing John
Van A. Mac Murray, minister at Peking,
as ambassador af Tokio."
The dispatch added, however, that
there was some doubt whether the ap
pointment would go through bccause of
the attitude of the Japanese government.

PADUCAII, Ky„ May 23. (/p)—John
W. Keller, 67, capitalist and retired
distiller, died today. He had been out
of active business for several years,
spending most of his time at his win
ter home in California.
