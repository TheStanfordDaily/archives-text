# SENIORS WILL HOLD IMPORTANT MEETING TOMORROW EVENING
## 
### 
An Important Senior Class
meeting will be held tomorrow
night at 7:15 o'clock in room 214
according to Johnny Lauritzen,
class president.
"It will probably be our last
class meeting," said Lauritzen
yesterday."I hope there will be
a large turnout as there are
many important announcements
and reports to be given to the
class."
Plans for the Senior Week pro
gram will be discussed. "Come
on time," said Lauritzen, "so we
can finish the business of the
meeting quickly."
