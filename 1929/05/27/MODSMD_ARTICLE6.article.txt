# Doubles Title Of Tourney Won By Borotra, LaCoste
## 
### 
ROLAND GARROS STADIUM,
PARIS, May 26. (/P) —Jean Borotra and
Rone LaCoste, of France, won (he
doubles title in the French tennis
championships today, defeating Henri
Cochet and Jacques Brugnon, another
French pair, in a hard, five-set, two
hour match. The scores were 6-3, 3-6,
6-3, 3-6, 3-6.
Helen Wills and Francis T. Hunter,
of the United States, reached (he
finals of the mixed doubles by defeat
ing Elsie Goldsack and D. O. C. Col
lins of England, 6-2, 6-2.
May Sutton Bundy, veteran Cali
fornian, disposed of her first round
opponent in the singles, Mile. Rornerta
Peyre, of France, 7-5, 7-5.
