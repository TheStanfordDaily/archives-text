# Chesterlyn Thomas, '29, Weds Alan Robertson, '28
## 
### 
Chesterlyn Antoinette Thomas was
married to Alan Hazelton Robertson
In Palo Alto yesterday afternoon.
Miss Thomas was a member of Kappa
Kappa Gamma sorority and was presi
dent of the Women's Athletic Asso
ciation. She belonged to Cap and
Gown, senior women's honorary so
ciety. Robertson is a Stanford gradu
ate of the class of 1928 and belongs
to Phi Kappa Psi fraternity.
