# "A Trip Through Spain" To Be Shown To Spanish Club
## 
### 
"A Trip Through Spain" is the title
of the moving pictures that Leonard
Crary is exhibiting to the Spanish
Club tonight at its meeting in the
Women's Clubhouse at 7:15. Pictures
of Spain and Spanish America will be
shown.
Officers for next year will be elect
ed and all members are requested to
be present.
