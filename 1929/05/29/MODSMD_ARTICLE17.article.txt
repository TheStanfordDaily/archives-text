# Edmonds Leads Amateur Tourney First Round Play
## 
### 
DEL MONTE, May 28. (/P)Warner
Edmonds, Jr., of San Diego took the
lead among the early finishers of the
36th hole qualifying round for the
State Amateur Golf Championship
when ho shot a 79 for today's round
and posted a total of 156 for his two
oighteens. Young Edmonds went out
in 38 and took 41 for the incoming
nine.
