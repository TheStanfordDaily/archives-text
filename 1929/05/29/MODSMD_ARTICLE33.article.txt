# Bell, Wheatley Play Hall, Dunlap In Finals Today
## 
### 
The Helen Bell and John Wheatley
mixed doubles team will meet the Sue
Betty Dunlap and Larry Hall combina
tion in the finals of the all-University
mixed doubles tennis tournament this
afternoon at 4 o'clock on the Encina
courts.
These two teams met in the finals
of the contests last spring; Sue Betty
Dunlap and Larry Hall came out on
top, 6-3, 6-3.
Both teams won their positions in
the finals by three-set matches with
their opponents. Dunlap and Hall de
feated Ted and Betts Hawkins yester
day, 6-3, 1-6, 8-6.
Helen Bell and John Wheatley
achieved the finals position by defeat
ing Thelma Patton and Jack Hick
man, 9-7, 6-8, 6-3. It was Wheatley's
smash and crack service along with
Helen Bell's coming through in the
pinches that won the match for them.
