# Adv. 13 Page 5
## 
### 
SENIOR WEEK
Wednesday, June 12
Senior Farce, "Not Herbert," in the Assembly Hall at 8:20 o clock.
Thursday, June 13
Senior Dinner, given by Executive Committee of Alumni Association at Union
at 6 o'clock.
Senior Promenade, in Union Court after Alumni Dinner.
Dance in Women's Clubhouse, 9 to 12 o'clock.
Friday, June 14
Tea given by Dr. and Mrs. Wilbur for seniors, 4 to 6 o'clock.
Senior Ball in Toyon Club.
Saturday, June 15
Class Day exercises in Memorial Chapel.
Presentation of Senior gift.
Pilgrimage to Mausoleum.
Sunday, June 16
Baccalaureate services in Memorial Chapel, 11 o'clock.
Monday, June 17
Commencement Exercises, in Memorial Chapel, 10:30 o'clock.
