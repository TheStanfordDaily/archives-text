# Rally Committee Donates Ball Profits To Theatre
## 
### 
Four hundred and twenty-five dol
lars was the sum given to the Theatre
Fund by the Rally Committee yester
day and represents the profits from
the King contest and the Masque Ball
held early this quarter.
Unofficial figures now place the to
tal amount raised at $4 t 408.27 which
shows that the new Theatre Fund
Committee is well on its way to reach
the $5,000 quota set by it to be raised
by the end of the quarter.
