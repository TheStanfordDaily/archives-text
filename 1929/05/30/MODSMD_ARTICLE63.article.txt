# Special Services To Mark Decoration Day Tomorrow
## 
### 
Special services in the Memorial
Church will commemorate Decoration
Day at 4:15 o'clock tomorrow after
noon.
The Reverend John Stephens of the
First Methodist Church of Palo Alto
will deliver the address, and the choir
will sing "Sanctus" by Gounod with
Siemon Muller as tenor soloist, and
also "The Supreme Sacrifice" by Har
ris. At the opening of the program,
Warren D. Allen will play "Piece
Heroique" by Cesar Pranck, and
"Prayer for Peace" by Paul Held, and
at the end "Chant for Dead Heroes"
by Harvey Gaul. Colonel Warner Mc-
Cabe will read the Scripture Lesson
and flowers, the gift of friends of the
late Dr. Colder, will be placed on the
altar. The services will close with
Taps.
This service will be the last Thurs
day vesper of the year.
Immediately following the Decora
tion Day program, the choir will have
its regular weekly rehearsal at 5
o'clock, instead of in the evening, and
will have dinner at the Union at 6:15.
After the dinner, Mr. and Mrs. Allen
will be at home to the singers at 075
Alvarado Row.
