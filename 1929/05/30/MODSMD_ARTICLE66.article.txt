# Y.W.C.A. BREAKFAST TO BE HELD TODAY IN COURT OF UNION
## 
### 
Inaugurating a new Stanford custom,
the Y.W.C.A. will sponsor a May break
fast this morning from 7 : 30 to 10: 30 in !
the Union Court. Strawberry shortcake
smothered in whipped cream and coffee
make up the succulent bill of fare to be
served to campus connoisseurs of the best
in breakfasts for 25 cents. The Y.W.C.A.
announces that they will attempt to have
accommodations for all, but students are
warned to come early and avoid the rush.
