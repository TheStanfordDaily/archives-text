# PHI BETA KAPPA RELEASES LIST OF ELECTIONS
## Fifteen Women, Forty-Four Men New Members of Honorary Society 
###

the Stanford chapter of Phi Beta
Kappa, national honorary scholastic
fraternity, last Tuesday night. Of
these, fifteen are women and forty
four are men.
