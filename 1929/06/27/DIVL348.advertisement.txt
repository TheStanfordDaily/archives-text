Building.

THURSDAY, JUNE 27
11:00 a.m. to 12 m. and 2:30 to 4:30 p.m. SOCIAL INSTITUTE—The American Social Hygiene Asso
ciation, in Room 214.
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Organist, in the Memorial Church.
FRIDAY, JUNE 28
9:00 p.m. to 1:00 a.m. RECEPTION to Summer Quarter Students, at Roble Hall. Dancing.
SATURDAY, JUNE 29
8:00 a.m. AUTOMOBILE EXCURSION to Santa Cruz. Leave from the Stanford Union, Main En
trance, returning about 4:00 p.m. Fare $3.00. Reservations must be made and fare deposited two
days in advance, in the office of the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Administration
Building.
SUNDAY, JUNE 30
11:00 a.m. DIVINE WORSHIP AND SERMON. Preacher: The Right Reverend William Hall More
land, D.D., Bishop of Sacramento. Subject: "If God Is Love, Why Crime and Evil in the
I World."
Duet, "The Lord Is My Shepherd" Smart
{ Madell Gille Allen, Soprano
Esther Houk Allen, Contralto
| Anthem, Choral from the 147 th Cantata Bach
7:30 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Organist, assisted by Madell Gille
Allen, Soprano, in the Memorial Church. i
TUESDAY, JULY 2
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, in the Memorial Church.
| 8:00 p.m. CONCERT, by the Abas String Quartet, in Branner Hall.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 3
4:15 p.m. LECTURE on "German Universities—Old and New," by Professor Friedrich von der Leyen of
the University of Cologne, Germany, in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
