# WILBUR TO SPEAK AT CONCLAVE OF SOCIAL SPECIALISTS TONIGHT
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
address the National Conference of
Social Work in the Exposition Audi
torium at San Francisco at 8 o'clock
this evening. His subject is "Pacific
Relations." Tomorrow morning he will
preside at the section meeting of the
conference dealing with the economic
aspect of medical care, a subject to
which he has given particular atten
tion for a number of years. Dr. Wilbur
is chairman of the advisory council
to the San Francisco committee of the
National Conference now meeting in
the city.
