# ORGANIST'S SISTER TO SING SUNDAY EVENING AT CHURCH PROGRAM
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, lias selected for the organ recital
this afternoon at 4:15 in the Memorial
Chapel:
Aria from the Concerto grosso,
No. 12 Handel
Symphony in B Minor (Unfinished)
Schubert
Allegro moderato
Andante con moto
Piece heroi'que C6sar Franck
The lecture on the Woodland Con
certs, previously announced, will not
he given.
Madell Gille Allen of Philadelphia
will be the soprano soloist at the or
gan recital Sunday evening at 7:30 in
the Memorial Church. Mrs. Allen is a
sister-in-law of Allen and is here with
her husband, Dr. Frederick H. Allen,
for the National Conference of Social
Workers which is being held in San
Francisco and on the campus.
Mrs. Allen will sing:
Allelulia Mozart
"Arise, Shine, for Thy Light Has
Come" James G. McDonald
Morning Hymn Georg Ilenschel
The selections which Dr. Allen will
play are:
Passacaglia and Fugue in C Minor
J. S. Bach
In the Steppes of Central Asia
Borodin
Partita in E Major, Opus 100
Sigfrid Karg-Elert
Entrata
Canzona
Finale quasi ritorno
On Tuesday, July 2, at 4:15 o'clock,
Allen will repeat his organ selections
for the Sunday recital.
