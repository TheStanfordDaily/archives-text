# Bishop Moreland To Give Sunday Morning Sermon
## 
### 
"If God Is Love Why Crime and !
Evil in the World?" will be the sub
ject of the sermon to be delivered by :
Dr. William Hall Moreland, Bishop of
Sacramento, in the Memorial Church
next Sunday morning at 11 o'clock.
Bishop Moreland stressed the knowing
of God and His Love last Sunday, and
this sermon is an answer to the first.
Madell Gille Allen, soprano, and
Esther Houck Allen, contralto, will
sing a duet.
