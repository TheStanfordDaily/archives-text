# Students, Faculty, Needed By Allen At Choir Tonight
## 
### 
A call has been issued by Warren
D. Allen, organist of Stanford Univer
sity, for all members of the commun
ity, faculty, students, and others who
have had some experience in choral
work, to join the University choir.

The first rehearsal will be held to
night at 7:30 o'clock in the organ loft
of the Memorial Church, and will last
for about an hour. A unit of Univer
sity credit is allowed the students for
their choir work.
