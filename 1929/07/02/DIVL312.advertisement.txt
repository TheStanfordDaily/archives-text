Coleridge Avenue, Palo Alto. All graduate students of English are invited. Professor

8:00 p.m. CONCERT, by the Abas String Quartet, in Branner Hall.
TUESDAY, JULY 9
1:00 p.m. SEAT SALE for "The Adding Machine" by Elmer Rice will take place in front of the
Women's Clubhouse. Prices $1.25 and $1.00.
4:15 p.m. ORGAN RECITAL, by Miss Ruth L. Savage, in the Memorial Church.
B*oo pm LECTURE, by Professor Albert Guerard, Professor of General Literature at Stanford
University, on "Balzac," in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
WEDNESDAY, JULY 10
4:15 p.m. LECTURE, by Father George Lacombe, in the Little Theatre. The public is invited.
OFFICIAL NOTICES
