# FIREMEN HURRY TO ASSEMBLY HALL FOR IMPROMPTU AFFAIR
## 
### 
An impromptu gathering was
held in the Assembly Hall yes
terday morning. The affair was
of especial interest to firemen,
who had gathered in the base
ment of the hall to take action
on certain urgent business, name
ly, the extinction of a small
blaze caused by a cigaret dropped
in some trash.
Other matters disposed of by
the firemen were a few burning
shingles on the roof of the Pi
Beta Phi house, caused by a de
fective flue, and several small
grass fires, all within the last
few days.
Two of the fires were at the
Gilmore picnic grounds, one of
which burned itself out before
the firemen arrived.
