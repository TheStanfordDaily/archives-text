# BLAZE ON GOLF LINKS BURNS OVER 25 ACRES
## 
###

fought by nearly twenty men, ap
proximately twenty-five acres of
the Palo Alto Stock Farm was
burned over.
The blaze started when a fire
which was burning grass off the
first fairway of the new Stan
ford Golf Links got out of control
and spread into one of the pad
docks of the stock farm.
