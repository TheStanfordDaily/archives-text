# ESSAY WINNERS ON CAMPUS
## 
### 
Winners of the Hearst flag essay
contest visited the campus last Thurs
day. The four victors were accom
panied by George Hearst, son of Wil
liam Randolph Hearst and publisher
of the San Francisco Examiner. They
were received by President Wilbur,
Acting-president Robert E. Swain, and
John A. Sellards, director of the sum
mer quarter.


Dr. Clyde A. Duniway
