# STANFORD WEEK-END HOST TO CONFERENCE OF SOCIAL WORKERS
## 
### 
Stanford was again host to mem
bers of the National Conference of
Social Workers on Saturday evening
and Sunday afternoon.

Saturday evening eighty guests were
entertained at dinner at the Union
dining room. This group was com
posed chiefly of members of the
American Red Cross Hospital Work
ers. The dinner followed a visit to the
Palo Alto Veterans' Base Hospital,
which has a national reputation for
psychiatric work.

Between fifty and sixty visitors were
entertained at tea on Sunday after
noon at the Union. Before tea was
served the visitors were driven about
the campus on a brief inspection tour.
