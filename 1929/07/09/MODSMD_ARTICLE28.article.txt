# Letters From Hoover War Library Shown In England
## 
### 
The presentation of two auto
graphed letters of Cecil Rhodes which
were found here in the Hoover War
Library was the feature of a dinner
at Rhodes House, Oxford University,
last Friday which was attended by
the Prince of Wales, Prime Minister
Stanley Baldwin, and other promi
nent persons. On behalf of the Ameri
can Rhodes scholars, Vincent Butler
of California presented the letters at
the dinner, which celebrated the
twenty-fifth anniversary of the scholar-
Hhips. The letters, written by Rhodes
to his solicitor, concerned the found
ing of the Rhodes trust and were se
cured by President Hoover, '95.
