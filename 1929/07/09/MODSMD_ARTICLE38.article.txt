# PIANIST TO PRESENT PROGRAM AT TOYON HALL MONDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
Marcus Gordon, an eminent pianist
and holder of various musical honors,
will give a recital at 8:15 Monday, in
Toyon Hall, which will be open to all
students and faculty. Gordon has had
an interesting and brilliant musical
career during which he won a scholar
ship with Josef Lhevinne for the sum
mer of 1925, and also won the Julliard
Musical Foundation for four consecu
tive years. He returned to New York
to finish his fourth year with Lhe
vinne after which he filled numerous
engagements in New York and New
Jersey. He will also appear in San
Francisco for a recital in September.
