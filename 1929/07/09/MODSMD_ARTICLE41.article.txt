# MENLO CIRCUS FUNDS TO GO TO STANFORD CONVALESCENT HOME
## 
### 
The proceeds from "The Circus," a
production to be staged by the Menlo
Circus Club, will provide the funds
for the club's annual gift to the Stan
ford Convalescent Home. The pageant,
under the direction of Clarence Wal
ter, will be given 011 July 27. Mem
bers of the younger society of Ather
ton, Woodside, and Menlo Park make
up the cast.

There will be a secret number, a
lancer's drill, and about seven other
numbers in the circus. Two contest
ants, Florence Douglass and Edith
Walkut, are competing for the title of
"Queen of the Circus."
