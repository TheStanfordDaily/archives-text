A Vacation Message!
—Whether your vacation leads to the
mountains, the seashore, or the popular
resort, we are certain that our unlimited
stock can furnish you with just the
clothes you need. A smart sport dress
... a frilly dance frock ... a swagger
coat or sweater ... or a chic hat, we have
them all, and fit them to you without
charge. Also our convenient credit ac
count is at your service . . . payments to
begin after you return from your vaca
tion if you wish. Wishing you a pleasant
vacation,
Maurice Samuels
