# LYTLE TO WED
## 
### 
Aten Lee Lytle, a former Stanford
man, and Miss Helen Hardy will be
married July 19. The ceremony will
take place at the bride's home in San
Francisco. Lytle is a member of Delta
Kappa Epsilon and Miss Hardy is a
graduate of Miss Ransome's School
and Mount Vernon Seminary in Wash
ington, D.C.
