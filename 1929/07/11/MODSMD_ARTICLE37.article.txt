# Travelogue Of India Latest Book By Dhan Mukerji, '14
## 
### 
Dhan Gopal Mukerji, Stanford *14,
has recently finished a new book,
"Visit India With Me," an imaginary
tour in which he shows India, his
native land, to an American friend.
The book is to be released in October.
Mukerji is also the author of an an
swer to the much-discussed "Mother
India," in which he defends India and
brands many of Katherine Mayo's
statements as untrue.
Besides these books, he has written
"Caste and Outcast," "The Face of
Silence," "Gay Neck," and others.
