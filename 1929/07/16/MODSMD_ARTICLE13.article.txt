# Last Of Three Musketeers Visits Stanford Air School
## 
### 
Lieutenant A. F. Woodring, last of
the "Three Musketeers" who com
peted at the National Air Races last
year, and Lieutenant Salter, landed at
the Palo Alto aviation field Saturday
afternoon in the new Boeing P. 12's,
powered with Pratt and Whitney
Wasps. After filling their gas tanks,
the two continued their journey from
Rockwell Field to Crissy. Their planes
were the fastest models of pursuit
ships in the army.
