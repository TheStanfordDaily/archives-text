# H. S. DODDS AIDED WOMEN
## 
### 
The death of Henry G. Dodds has
revealed, through Dean Yost's ofiice,
that he had for a good many years
given large sums of money for the
benefit of needy Stanford women.
Twice every year Mr. Dodds had sent
a check or this purpose, but would not
permit his name to be revealed.
