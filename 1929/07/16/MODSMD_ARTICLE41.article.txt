# Torrence Burned In Fire At Aviation School Field
## 
### 
Dean Torrence, Beta Theta Pi, while
working for the Standard Oil Com
pany, was severely burned as a result
of a gasoline fire accident last Satur
day afternoon at the Palo Alto Avia
tion grounds.
Torrence was filling one of the
underground storage tanks at the field
when the barrel which he was han
dling ignited from static electricity.
The flames which followed burned
both of his legs before he was able
to extricate himself from the blaze.
