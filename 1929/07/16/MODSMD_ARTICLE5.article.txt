# Terman Receives Honorary Degree From Alma Mater
## 
### 
Professor Lewis M. Terman, head
of the department of psychology, and
editor of the Genetic Studies of Genius
series published by the Stanford Uni
versity Press, has been awarded the
degree of honorary doctor of laws by
the University of Indiana, his alma
mater. This is the highest gift of the
University from which Dr. Terman
graduated in 1903.
