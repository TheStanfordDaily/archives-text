# MOORE MARRIED
## 
### 
Robert Griffen Moore, a Stanford
graduate, was married June 29 to
Miss Florence Townsend. Because of
illness in the bride's family a quiet
wedding replaced the large ceremony
planned to be held in the Memorial
Church. Mrs. Moore is an alumna of
the San Jose State Teachers College.

The first sandwich is said to have
been made in the 17th century.

Andrew Carnegie founded more than
2800 libraries in the United States.
