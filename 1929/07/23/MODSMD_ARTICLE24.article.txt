# HANDBALL TO START
## 
### 
Drawings for the annual summer
handball tournament have been com
pleted, and the first-round matches
will be played today. With an entry
list of ten evenly matched teams the
competition should prove to be lively.
The teams composed of A 1 Masters
and Don Liebendorfer and of Profes
sor C. F. Tolman and Myron Sprague
are conceded to be favorites in the
tournament.
