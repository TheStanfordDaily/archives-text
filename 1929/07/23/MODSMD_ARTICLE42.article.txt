# Far Eastern Visitor Will Talk On "Oriental Music"
## 
### 
Henry Eickheim, the composer, who
has recently returned from a tour of
the Far East, will give a lecture on
"Oriental Music" at 4:15 o'clock next
Monday afternoon in the clubroom of
Toyon Hall.
The lecture will be illustrated by
selections on the piano by Mrs. Eick
heim, herself an accomplished musi
cian.
Mr. Eickheim is not only a com
poser but an understanding student of
music and is known here as an orches
tra director through his appearances
as guest conductor of the San Fran
cisco Symphony.
