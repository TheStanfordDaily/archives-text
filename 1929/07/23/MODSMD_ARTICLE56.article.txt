# Professor Green And Wife Return From Trip Abroad
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. R. L. Green re
turned Thursday from a year spent
mostly in Italy, France, and England,
and are now visiting their son and
daughter-in-law, Mr. and Mrs. Thomas
E. Green of Palo Alto "before their
i departure for Yosemite, where they
will remain until September 1. While
in Washington they were entertained
at luncheon by President and Mrs.
Hoover. Green is professor emeritus of
mathematics at Stanford.
