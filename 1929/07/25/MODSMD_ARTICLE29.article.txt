# Faculty, Students Guests At Roble Club Weekly Tea
## 
### 
More than 200 people, including
members of the faculty and their
wives, as well as University students,
were guests of Roble Club at the
weekly at-home given in the lobby
Tuesday afternoon from 2:30 to 5.
Mrs. Stearns received. She was as
sisted by Elizabeth Paine, who was
in charge of the tea, and by Marion
Schumacher, who poured.
