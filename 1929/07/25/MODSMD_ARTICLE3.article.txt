# SUZZALLO TO SPEAK AT EDUCATION DINNER
## 
### 
All students in the Education de
partment and their wives, husbands,
and friends, are invited to the annual
education banquet at 6:30 Saturday
evening in the Union dining room.
Admission will be by ticket only.
Reservations, which are limited,
should be made at once in front of
the Education building.
Dr. Henry Suzzallo, '99, of the Car
negie Foundation, ex-president of the
University of Washington and former
member of the Stanford education
faculty, Vierling Kersey, state super
intendent of schools, and Dean E. P.
Cubberley of the Stanford School of
Education, will be the speakers.
The Marguerite MacManus String
Quartet of Oregon State College will
play.
