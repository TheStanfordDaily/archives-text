# 'New China' Subject of Dr. Koo Tomorrow in Theater Lecture
## 
### 
"The Emergence of China from the
Old Life Into the New" will be the
subject of a lecture to be given by
Dr. T. Z. Koo from Shanghai, China,
at 4:15 tomorrow afternoon in the
Little Theatre.
Dr. Koo is secretary of the World's
Student Christian Federation and
comes as its representative from the
national committee in China.
A movement of international under
standing in China was founded by
Dr. Koo a few years ago, and he has
been lecturing extensively in both
Europe and America in connection
with its development.
Dr. Koo's lecture here tomorrow
will be one of his last to be given in
America this year, as he is now on
his return trip to China.
