# Ten Contestants Remain In Campus Tennis Matches
## 
### 
The all-university singles tennis
tournament goes into its second
round of play this week with only
ten contestants left in the running.

Coach E. B. Bugge, sponsor of the
tournament, announced that those
failing to play off their scheduled
matches would automatically default.
The dates of the third round matches
will be posted on the Encina gym bul
letin board.
Those remaining in the tourney are
Edwards, Coughlin, Mockett, Blethen,
Woodruff, Ballard, Bradbent, Rich
ardson, Long, and Bradshaw.
