# Handball Semi-Finals Set For Tomorrow Afternoon
## 
### 
Tomorrow the semi-finals of the
handball tournament will start. There
are three teams ready to play their
semi-finals now: Norris and Sullivan
defeated Mott and Ruffa in a hard
fought contest, 20-22, 21-12, 21-6; and
Masters and Liebendorfer defeated
Levit and Hamlyn 21-13, 21-6. Sprague
and Tolman reached the semi-finals
last week.
