# Dr. Alonzo E. Taylor To Assist In Census Work
## 
### 
Dr. Alonzo E. Taylor, director of
the Stanford Food Research Institute,
will assist in the census work next
year. Dr. Taylor was named by Secre
tary Lamont of the department of
commerce to help in planning for the
manufacturers' census, which will be
taken next year in connection with
the decennial census of population.
