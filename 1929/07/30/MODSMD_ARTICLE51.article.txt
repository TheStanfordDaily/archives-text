# Dean Wildman Leaves For Two Conventions In East
## 
### 
Professor M. S. Wildman, head of
the Stanford economics department,
will leave immediately to attend the
ninth annual session of the Institute
of Politics to be held at Williams
town, Massachusetts, from August 1
to 20.
While in the East Professor Wild
man will also attend a meeting of the
Social Science Research Council at
Hanover, New Hampshire.
