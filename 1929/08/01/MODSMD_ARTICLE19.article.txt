# SEASON TICKETS FOR FOOTBALL WILL SAVE QUARTER OF THE COST
## 
### 
A $12 season ticket for all football
games except the Big Game is now
being sold to alumni, stadium sub
scribers, script holders, and the gen
eral public. These tickets guarantee
the purchaser centrally located seats
on either side of the stadium besides
insuring good seats for the University
of Southern California game and the
West Point game in December. This
season book affords a saving of 25
per cent in the regular admission
price.
