# Report of Wedding Bells Exaggeration Says Salt
## 
### 
William Haslem Salt, Jr.'s mar
riage to a young lady of San Fran
cisco was announced in this paper
Tuesday. He isn't married. Further
more, he never has been married. And
to complete the confusion, he has
never even seen his supposed wife.
Some practical "joker" turned the
notice in at the Daily office. Both Salt
and the staff extend their apprecia
tion.
