# Harold Griffin Entertains Roble With Piano Recital
## 
### 
Harold Griffin, a former Stanford
student, presented a program of piano
numbers at Roble Hall Tuesday after
noon. The recital was attended by
many faculty members and students.
After the recital, which lasted about
an hour, tea was served for those in
attendance.
Griffin has recently studied in
Paris, and plans to return there soon
to continue his work. His playing has
received high consideration from
critics of music.
