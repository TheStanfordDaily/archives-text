# Maloney-Bradshaw Win In Handball Semi-Finals Play
## 
### 
Maloney and Bradshaw defeated
their opponents 21-7, 21-4 yesterday
to enter the semi-finals of the hand
ball tournament. Their next match
will be against Sprague and Tolman
today at noon. The winners of this
playoff will meet the winners of the
Sullivan-Norris, Masters-Liebendorfer
match next week.
Because of the interest centered in
the present tournament another tour
nament will run starting next week.
The provision has been made that
those entered in the present tourna
ment who wish to compete must
change partners.
