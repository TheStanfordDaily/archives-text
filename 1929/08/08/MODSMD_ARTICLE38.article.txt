# Lantern Lecture Will Show Modern German Stage Sets
## 
### 
"Modern Stage Settings in Ger
many" will be the subject of an Illus
trated lecture by Professor Friedrich
von der Leyen next Wednesday, Au
gust 14, in the Little Theatre. With
the aid of lantern slides, Professor
von der Leyen will show settings used
by Schiller, Shakespeare, Mozart, ani
Richard Wagner, and will compare
these with those in use in the modern
German drama.
