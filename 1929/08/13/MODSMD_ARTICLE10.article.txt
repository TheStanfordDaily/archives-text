# WILBUR EXPECTED ON CAMPUS VISIT TODAY TO ATTEND MEETINGS
## 
### 
Ray Lyman Wilbur, Secretary of
the Interior and president of Stanford
University, is expected to arrive on
the campus this afternoon from Wash
ington, D.C., for a brief stay.
He will attend a committee meet
ing of the Stanford board of trustees
today and a regular meeting of the
board on Thursday.
