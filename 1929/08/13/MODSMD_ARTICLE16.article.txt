# Raymond Drake Marries Hollis Yerington, Both '28
## 
### 
Raymond Drake, '28, and Hollia
Yerington, also '28, were married Fri
day noon at the Yerington home,
1550 Middlefield Road. Mrs. Drake is
a daughter of Professor and Mrs.
Yerington, she is a member of Gamma
Phi Beta sorority and Drake is a
member of Theta Delta Chi frater
nity. His home is in Long Beach.
