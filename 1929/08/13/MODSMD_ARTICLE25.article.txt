# Philological Association Meeting To Hear Talks
## 
### 
Dr. Freidrich von der Leyen, acting
professor of German, and Dr. Damaso
Alonso, lecturer in Spanish literature,
will be the speakers at the meeting
of the University Philological Asso
ciation Thursday at 7:30 in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.

Dr. von der Leyen, who is professor
of philology at the University of
Cologne, will talk in German on the
"Origin of Fairy Tales." "Some As
pects of Modern Spanish Literature"
will be Dr. Alonso's topic. He will
speak in English.
