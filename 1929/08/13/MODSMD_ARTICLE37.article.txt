# University of Nevada To Receive Mackay Donation
## 
### 
Clarence H. Mackay, son of John
W. Mackay, has given approximately
$500,000 to the University of Nevada
to erect and equip a building in com
memoration of his father's name. The
building will be known as the Mackay
Science School and will supplement
the Mackay School of Mines. When
the group is completed. Nevada will
have the world's greatest school for
training engineers in mining, geology,
and metallurgy.
Since 1908, the family of Mackay,
who worked as a miner in the famous
Comstock lode and later founded the
Postal Telegraph Company, has been
making a series of gifts to the Uni
versity in his memory.
