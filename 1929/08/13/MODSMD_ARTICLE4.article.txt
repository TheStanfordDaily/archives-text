# Stanford Medical Men To Attend Conference
## 
### 
Professor Emmet Rixford, Emile
Frederick Holman, Leo Elocsser, and
Edward Bancroft Towne left for Hono
lulu Saturday on the liner Malolo to
attend the first Pan-Pacific Surgical
Conference to be held in Hawaii Au
gust 14 to 24.
These professors of the Medical
School will be part of a San Fran
cisco delegation of 14 well-known
Pacific Coast medical men.
