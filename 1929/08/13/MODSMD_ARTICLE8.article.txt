# LAST OF AFTERNOON RECITALS SCHEDULED FOR MEMORIAL CHURCH
## 
### 
The organ recital next Thursday
afternoon will conclude the series of
Thursday organ recitals for the sum
mer. Raymond C. Robinson, acting
organist, will give the recital. The
program follows:
Pontifical March from the
First Symphony C. M. Widor
Chorale Prelude "A Rose Breaks
Into Bloom" Brahms
Passacaglia Bach
Sketch in D Flat Schumann
Fantasia in E Flat Salnt-Saens
Evensong Johnson
Piece heroique Cesar Franck
The recital will be given at 4:15 in
the Memorial Church.
