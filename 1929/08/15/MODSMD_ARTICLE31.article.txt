# September 7 Chosen For Overfelt-Wilcox Wedding
## 
### 
The marriage of Hal Overfelt, '25,
to Miss Alberta Wilcox of Santa Clara
has been set for September 7 in the
Stanford Memorial Church. Since his
graduation Overfelt has been manag
ing a number of ranches near Hol
lister. While at Stanford Overfelt
played on the varsity tennis team
and belonged to Skull and Snakes. He
was also a member of the Sigma Nu
fraternity.
