# 'LA RECOMMENDATION' TO BE SUPPLEMENTED WITH FOREIGN TALENT
## 
### 
A galaxy of foreign entertainment
will lighten the minds of the partici
pants in the evening program of tlie
summer language tables Wednesday
in the Little Theatre.
The main feature of the short pro
gram will be the presentation of the
famous French one-act comedy "La
Recommendation," acted by students
of the French classes and directed by
Mile. Broquet of the Romanic lan
guage department faculty.
Assisted by the members of the
Spanish language table the group will
join in singing several folk songs
from Brltany.
Admission is free to the Wednesday
evening program and all outsiders are
cordially invited to attend.
