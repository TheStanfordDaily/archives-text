# Howard Richardson, '30, Weds Nadine Nellis
## 
### 
Announcements have been received
that Howard C. Richardson, '30, Phi
Kappa Psi, and Nadine Nellis were
married at Green Lake, Wisconsin,
Saturday, August 10.
Richardson is employed by Blythe
and Company, San Francisco brokers.
The couple will be at home to friends
after the first of September in San
Francisco.
