# DAMES HOLD FINAL SUMMER GATHERING
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames held their final
meeting for the summer quarter
Thursday afternoon in the Women's
Clubhouse. A program of music and
dancing was given.
Mrs. L. T. Harvey played a piano,
solo and Mrs. H. G. Copeland and
Mrs. Harvey sang a duet number.
Dances by pupils of Miss Maizie
Roddy completed the program.
Those taking part in the dancing
were: Minette Meyer, Elizabeth Mae
Pittock, Georgia Northon, Verna
Scarpa, Alta Bell, and Elsie Anderson.
