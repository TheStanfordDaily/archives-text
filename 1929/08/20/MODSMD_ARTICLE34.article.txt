# FACULTY HONORED BY ROBLE CLUB IN LAST BANQUET OF SUMMER
## 
### 
Roble Club entertained the mem
bers of the faculty and their wives
at a formal banquet last evening.
After the dinner, served by candle
light, they adjourned to the lobby
where coffee was served. The guests
then played bridge for the remainder
of the evening.
There were places laid for 54 pro
fessors and their wives. Approximate
ly two hundred were present at this
social affair, the last of the summer
quarter.
