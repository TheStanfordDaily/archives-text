# August 28 Announced For Cypher-Farrell Wedding
## 
### 
The date of the marriage of S. Clark
Cypher, '20, to Miss Mary B. Farrell
of Burlingame has been set for Au
gust 28. Cypher, who at the present
time is an instructor in the Palo Alto
Union High School, was prominent in
athletics and student body affairs
while at Stanford. He was a member
of the Delta Chi fraternity.
