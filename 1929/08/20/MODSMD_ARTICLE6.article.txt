# DAILY WILL APPEAR FOR LAST TIME ON CAMPUS THURSDAY
## 
### 
The next issue of the Daily
will be the final edition of the
summer quarter. This will bring
to a close volume number 75a
which is published by the classes
in Journalism and is under the
direction of Professors Everett
W. Smith and Buford O. Brown.
According to custom the paper
makes its final appearance on
the campus one week before the
finals commence in order that
students working on the paper
can find time to study for their
examinations.
All announcements concerning
events coming after August 22
should be in the office of the
Daily by 6 p.m.-, Wednesday, Au
gust 21.
