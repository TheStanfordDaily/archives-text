# REV. LESLIE LEARNED TO PREACH AT FINAL SERVICE OF SUMMER
## 
### 
The services to be held next Sunday
in the Memorial Church will be the
last until Matriculation Sunday, Octo
ber 6. Rev. Leslie Learned of Pasa
dena will deliver the sermon, the sub
ject of which is to be "Leaders." This
sermon is a postlude of that given
last Sunday. The musical program will
be:
"Incline Thy Ear" Himmel
Baritone Solo by Major F. H. Forbes
"O God, Our Help in Ages Past"
Williams
Ruth L. Savage will be at the organ.
