# QUARTERBACK INJURED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Sept. 30 (/P).—
Listed among the first football casualties
of the season is Norwood Jaqua, Santa
Clara University quarterback, who had
a rib broken in the game with the Uni
versity of California last Saturday. He
will be out for the rest of the season.
Jaqua is in a hospital here.
