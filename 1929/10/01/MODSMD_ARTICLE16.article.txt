# Northcutt Ely Marries Louise Shoup August 31
## 
### 
Louise Shoup, '29, and Northcutt Ely,
'24, were married at the home of Paul
Shoup, the bride's father, on August 31.,
Mrs. Kly is a member of Kappa Kappa
Gamma sorority, and the bridegroom,
who was graduated from the law school,
was editor of Chaparral.
