# Dr. Jordan Resting Easily, Though Unimproved, Report
## 
### 
Doctor David Starr Jordan, Chancel
lor Emeritus of the University, who has
been seriously ill during the summer
months, was reported as resting easily at
his home at 330 Serra Street late yes
terday afternoon. His condition, how
ever, was characterized by Mrs. Jordan
as unimproved.
Although confined to his bed most of
the time, Doctor Jordan is now able to
spend about an hour in the sunshine each
morning. Between eleven and twelve o'-
clock he is placed in a wheel chair for
his daily trip out into the fresh air.
