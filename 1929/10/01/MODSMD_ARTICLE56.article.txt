# AIR TRAVEL INCREASES
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 1 W.—Air
passenger travel broke all local records
during the week ending September 7,
with a total of 1,018 passengers arriving
and departing on scheduled transport
planes at San Francisco Bay airports.
