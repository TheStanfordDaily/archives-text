# STANFORD SCHEDULE OF AIR, TRAIN MAIL REVEALED BY WERRY
## 
### 
Air mail and train schedules to and
from the University are announced by
Postmaster W. C. Werry as follows:
Air mail for the East leaves San
Francisco morning and evening each
day except that there is no flight on
Monday mornings. Mail deposited be
tween 4:00 a.m. and 3:30 p.m. will con
nect with the night plane of the same
day. Mail deposited between 3:30 p.m.
and 4:00 a.m. will connect with the
morning plane.
Air mail for Seattle must be de
posited before 6:00 p.m. to connect
with plane leaving San Francisco
each morning.
Air mail for Los Angeles is good
only between 6:00 p.m. and 4:00 a.m.
For quickest service to Los Angeles
use special delivery.
