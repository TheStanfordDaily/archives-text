# Lansburgh, Helvenston Married on September 2
## 
### 
Ruth Lansburgh, '29, prominent horse
woman and swimmer of San Francisco,
and Harold Helvenston, technical direc
tor of dramatics, were married on Sep
tember 2 at the home of G. A. Lansburgh
in San Francisco.
Mrs. Helvenston was a member of Phi
Beta Kappa, Cap and Gown, and was a
member of the Executive Committee last
year.
