August 3, the Third Annual Palo
Alto Horse Show opened, attracting
a brilliant list of entrants from all
parts of the United States.
Ruth Lansburgh, Stanford '29, on
Dark Laughter, won the Charles S.
Howard $500 Stake for five-gaited
mares, with Mrs. W. P. Roth's My Joy
taking second prize.
In the $500 Stake given by Miss
Elinor Getz for fine harness horses,
Ruth Lansburg, driving Dark Laugh
ter, won first, and Mrs. Roth's Charm
ing Gypsy took third.
