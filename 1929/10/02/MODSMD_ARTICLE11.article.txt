# First University Assembly Called For Friday Morning
## 
### 
Stanford's first All-University As
sembly takes place Friday morning at
eleven o'clock. Acting-President Rob
ert E. Swain will preside. He will also
deliver a welcome to the students on
behalf of the Administration.
Stanford Steinbeck, President of the
Associated Students, will address the
assembly, stressing in his talk the
honor system, Stanford spirit, and
student activities. Benetta Merner,
president of A.W.S., will also talk,
addressing her message mainly to the
women.
