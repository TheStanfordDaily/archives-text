A Big Year
Ahead
THE VARSITY has been work
ing hard the last three weeks in
preparation for a big year in foot
ball. We, too, have been prepar
ing for a big year. In order that
Stanford men and women might
have the latest styles in footwear
to choose from we have added
greatly to our stock in anticipa
tion of their wants.
Come in and see the footwear
we have just received. It is well
worth the trip to Paly to see our
Fall display.
