# FACES BURGLARY CHARGE
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 2 (£>).—Jack
Noonan, brother of Sally O'Neill and
Mollie O'Day, screen actresses, faced
preliminary hearing today on charges of
burglarizing the home of Ted Lewis,
band leader. He was returned here yes*-
terday from New York, where he went
shortly before being secretly indicted by
the county grand jury.
