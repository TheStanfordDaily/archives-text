# Track Manager Tryoutees Meet Today At Old Oval
## 
### 
All sophomore men wishing to try
out for track manager are asked to
report to Senior Manager "Rusty" Bell
at the Old Oval today. Three after
noons' work a week is required of
each tryoutee, and this work will be
given regular gym credit.
At the end of the year the five
highest ranking candidates will be
come junior track managers, and the
year following, one of these five will
be given the senior managership.
