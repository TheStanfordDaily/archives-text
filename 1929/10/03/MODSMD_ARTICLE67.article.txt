##

OAKLAND, Cal., Oct. 2 (#>).â€”Nine
persons bailed out of the city jail by Miss
Anita Whitney, radical leader, following
their arrest for passing out handbills
in violation of a city ordinance, had their
hearing continued today when no com
plaining witness appeared.
The nine were arrested last night when
police broke up an open-air meeting of
several hundred persons.
After providing $10 bail each, Miss
Whitney led the group to their original
meeting place, with waving banners and
placards, where they resumed their ses
sion, which was called to protest the trial
of twenty-three Gaston, N.C., mill work
ers charged with the murder of a police
man during recent labor riots.
