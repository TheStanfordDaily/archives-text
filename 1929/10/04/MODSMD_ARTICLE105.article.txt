# Mormon Football Squad Sees Stanford Work Out
## 
### 
Led by Captain Thorn, twenty-five
members of the Brigham Young foot
ball squad were guests of Stanford
yesterday afternoon. The Mormons
spent most of their time watching the
Cardinal frosh and varsity squads
work out. Following their defeat of
Nevada at Reno last Saturday tho
B.Y. men have been quartered at
Berkeley, where they have been scrim
maging on the California field in pre
paration for their game with the
California Aggies to be held Saturday
at Sacramento.
