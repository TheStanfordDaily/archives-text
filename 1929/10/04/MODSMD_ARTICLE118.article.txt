# GOVERNOR ACQUITTED
## 
### 
FRANKFORT, Ky., Oct. 3 (/P).—The
first trial resulting from the recent in
dictment of Governor Sampson, seven
members of his textbook commission, and
twenty-three publishers, came to an
abrupt end today with an instructed ver
dict for acquittal of the governor.
