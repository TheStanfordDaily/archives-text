# Martinez Oil Station Fire Does $50,000 In Damage
## 
### 
MARTINEZ, Oct. 3 (#•). — Damage
approximating $50,000 resulted today
when a fire of undetermined origin de
stroyed the light oil filling station at
the Shell Oil Company's plant near here.
For a time the flames threatened to
spread to nearby tanks containing 55,000
barrels of oil each.
