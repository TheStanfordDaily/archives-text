# Matriculation Services To Be Held In Church Sunday
## 
### 
Freshman matriculation services
will be held in the Memorial Church
Sunday morning, October 6, at eleven
o'clock. Only on that day and again
on Baccalaureate Sunday will the en
tire class be together in the Church.
Dr. Swain, Dean Culver, and Dean
Mary Yost will speak on various
phases of campus life. Dr. J. Pierce
Mitchell will read the lesson. All fresh
men will meet on the Inner Quad and
go into the Church in a body.
