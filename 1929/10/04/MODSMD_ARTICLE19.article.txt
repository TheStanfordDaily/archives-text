# ELECTED PRESIDENT
## 
### 
ANNAPOLIS, Md., Oct. 3. (/p) —
John W. Gannon, Lodi, California,
halfback on the football team, today
was selected president of the second
or junior class, United States Naval
Academy. He was president, of the
"Youngsters" or sophomores last
year.
