# DR., MRS. SWAIN HOLD RECEPTION
## 
### 
Acting President and Mrs. Swain arc
entertaining all new students at the Pres
ident's house on Lagunita Knoll tonight
between 8:00 and 11:00 o'clock. Those
receiving will include, besides Dr. and
Mrs. Swain, Dean Culver, Dean Yost,
and the faculty members of the Lower
Division.
In addition to meeting the above fac
ulty members, the new students will
have the opportunity of becoming ac
quainted with each other, as there will be
dancing all evening.
Sponsors will be the only old students
present. New men are to go directly to
the President's house. All women are to
meet at Roble and go to the reception.
