# Missions Defeat Seattle; Tie Hollywood For First
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. .3 (£>).—'The
San Francisco Mission Reds went into a
tie with Hollywood for first place in the
Coast League race today by defeating
Seattle 13-1 while Hollywood was losing
to Portland. Cole and Hubbell held Se
attle to six scattered hits while the Reds
pounded Smith for sixteen safeties. Bor
reani's homer accounted for the only
tally for the visitors.
