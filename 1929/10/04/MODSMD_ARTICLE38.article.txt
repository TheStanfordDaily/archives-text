# Three Guards Die As 1000 Colorado Convicts Mutiny
## 
### 
CANON CITY, Colo., Oct. 3. (/P)—
Three guards were reported killed in
a mutiny of approximately 1,000 pris
oners at the state penitentiary here
today, occasioned when several hun
-1 dred prisoners in an unaccountable
way gained access to the prison ar
i senal and obtained a large quantity
of ammunition and guns. The out
! break occurred in the mess hall at
! the noon hour and was still under
| way an hour and a half later.
