# WILL HOLD TRYOUTS FOR OXFORD DEBATE TEAM ON WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Tryouts for membership on the
Stanford team that will debate Ox
ford University have been changed
from Tuesday afternoon, as previous
ly announced, to Wednesday. They
will be held in the Little Theatre at
1:30.
"Resolved, That this is the best of
all possible worlds" is the question
which will be discussed in these trials.
Speeches of five minutes each will
be given by those trying out. A spe
cial committee will judge and will
choose the eight or ten best speakers,
who will speak again on the following
Friday, at which ame the three mem
bers of the team will be chosen defi
nitely. There will be no rebuttals
after Tuesday's speeches, but there
will be after Friday's.
