# Japanese Davis Cup Tennis Player Enrolls In Pomona
## 
### 
CLAREMONT, Cal., Oct. 3
Tamio Abe, second player of the Jap
anese Davis Cup tennis team, yesterday
enrolled as a special student in Pomona
College. Abe was graduated from Wa
seda University several years ago. lie
expects to spend his time in Pomona
College improving his knowledge of the
English language.
