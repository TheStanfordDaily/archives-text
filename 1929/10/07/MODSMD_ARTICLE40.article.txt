# Junior Women To Entertain New Members of '31 Class
## 
### 
A welcoming luncheon for the new
junior women will be held tomorrow
at 12 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. Dean Mary Yost and all Row
and Roble old junior women are to
greet the thirty juniors who have en
tered this quarter. The junior women
now number ninety in all.
The junior women who were for
merly members of the freshman cabi
net in the Y.W.C.A. organization will
be in charge of the affair. The lunch
eon is a class function.
