# CAP AND GOWN TEA
## 
### 
Invitations for a Cap and Gown
tea, to be held at Dean Yost's home
tomorrow afternoon from 3:30 to 5:30,
are being sent cut to the new Stanford
women. All new women are invited
to come, however, regardless of
whether or not they have yet received
invitations. The tea tomorrow after
noon is the welcoming tea given an
nually by Cap and Gown for new
women.
