# MASQUERS TO ENTERTAIN
## 
### 
Masquers Society will entertain at
the Women's Conference masquerade
party to be at the Women's
Clubhouse on Wednesday night be
tween 7:15 and 9:30. In addition,
Betty Alden, president of the
Y.W.C.A., will give a short talk and
the freshman women will present a
stunt. After the program there will be
dancing and refreshments. No charge
is being made for the Conference
party this year. Arrangements are in
the hands of Marian Strong, chairman
of the A.W.S. social committee.
