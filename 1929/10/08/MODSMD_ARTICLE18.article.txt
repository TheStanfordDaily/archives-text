# LONG PLANS REVIVAL OF WRESTLING HERE
## 
### 
Wrestling at Stanford will be re
vived this quarter, after a relapse of
several years If ten more men enroll
this week for the class, announced
Dr. Royce R. Long yesterday. In that
event an instructor will be secured to
coach the men in the fundamental
points.
Men with some experience are espe
cially wanted, and should enough capa
ble men report to make a team, out
side competition may be permanently
incorporated in the University ath
letic programs.
