# Berlin Burgomaster Guest Of Professor W.H. Cooper
## 
### 
This afternoon Burgemeister Boess
of Berlin will be the guest of Profes
sor and Mrs. Cooper here on the cam
pus. The office of the Burgemeister
in Berlin is similar to that of the
Lord Mayor of London. Boess and his
party, after a short visit at the
Cooper home, will return to San Fran
cisco.
