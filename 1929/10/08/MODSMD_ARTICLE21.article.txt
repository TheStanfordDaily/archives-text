# ANNOUNCE RALLY CONTEST PRIZES
## 
### 
Twenty-five dollars in prizes are
being offered by the Rally Committee
for the winners of the three contests
now being sponsored by them. Ideas
for rooting stunts for both the U.S.C.
and California games are needed. The
deadline for the former contest will
be 5 o'clock, Tuesday, October 15; for
the California game, Sunday, October
20, at the same hour. Prizes in these
contests will be ten dollars each.
New yells are needed by the com
mittee; and five dollars will be given
for the best yell submitted before 5
o'clock on November 9. Decisions will
be made by a committee composed of
Helen Thompson, Paul Speegle, and
Charles Fox. Material should be
mailed to C. M. Fox, Box B, Campus.
