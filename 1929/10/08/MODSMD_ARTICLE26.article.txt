# WOMEN'S CONFERENCE PLANS COSTUME FETE FOR TOMORROW NIGHT
## 
### 
An informal costume party, given
by Women's Conference between 7:15
and 9:30 tomorrow evening in the
Women's Clubhouse, will be the first
occasion undergraduate women have
had to gather together. Entertainment
and stunts are being prepared by
Masquers Society and by the fresh
man women.
After the program there will be
dancing, for which an orchestra is
being provided, and refreshments.
There is to be no admission cost this
year and all arrangements are under
the charge of the A.W.S. social com
mittee, which includes Billie Kalin,
Juliet Tliorner, Jean Utt, Elizabeth
Erlin, and Marian Strong, chairman.
