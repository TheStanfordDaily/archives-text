# AFRICAN TORNADO
## 
### 
JOHANNESBURG, Union of South
Afgrica, Oct. 7. (>P) —A tornado of un
precedented violence struck the sub
urbs of Turffontein and Kenilworth
yesterday. Roofs were blown off hun
dreds of houses, damaging Solly
Joel's stables and valuable horses.
