# LAW GROUP SMOKER WILL BE ADDRESSED BY ROTH TOMORROW
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, Comptroller of the
University, will be the principal speak
er at the annual smoker of the Stan
ford Law Association to be held at
the Old Union tomorrow evening at
7:30. Roth's subject will be "The
Legal Status of Stanford University,"
concerning the founding of Stanford,
its charter, provisions in the Califor
nia Constitution and Political Code
regarding Stanford, the legal signifi
cance of the limitation of women stu
dents, and other details of the legal
status.
Dean Marion Rice Kirkwood will
give a talk on the work in the Law
School for the coming year with a
special word of welcome to the enter
ing law students.
