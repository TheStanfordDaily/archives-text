# Open Swimming Class For Faculty Women And Wives
## 
### 
The swimming class for faculty
women and wives of the faculty will
be given this quarter at 11 o'clock on
Monday and Wednesday at the Roble
pool. Anyone desiring to register for
this course should sign up at once at
the swimming pool office in the Roble
gymnasium. The class was given last
year, and proved popular. A faculty
children's course will be offered, the
time of which will be announced later.
