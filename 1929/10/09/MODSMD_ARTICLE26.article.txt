# ROTH TO ADDRESS NEW MEN AT Y.M.C.A. MEET ON IDEALS, TRADITIONS
## 
### 
Comptroller A. E. Roth will address
the new men students at the Y.M.C.A.
reception Thursday night, 7:30 to 9
o'clock, in the Old Union lobby on
"Stanford Ideals and Traditions."
Stanford Steinbeck, President of
the A.S.S.U., Merle Emry, editor of
the Stanford Daily, and Scott Web
ster, president of the Interfraternity
Council, will make short talks. Musi
cal numbers and refreshments will
complete the program.
Morden G. Brown, '29, was elected
student president of the Y.M.C.A., at
a special election, taking the place of
Francis Knapp, who did not return to
school. Brown was also president in
1928.
