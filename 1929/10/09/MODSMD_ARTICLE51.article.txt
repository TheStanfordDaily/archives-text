# Tryoutees For Quad Staff Meet Tomorrow At Union
## 
### 
All tryoutees for the business staff
of the Quad are to meet at one o'clock
tomorrow in the Old Union. Any
sophomores who would like to try
out for any of the four salaried posi
tions open are urged to come. If
anyone is unable to attend the meet
ing he should see Jerry Richardson
as soon as possible.
