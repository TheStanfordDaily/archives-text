# Interclass Tennis Entries End Saturday, Says Bugge
## 
### 
Entries for the interclass tennis
tourney will close Saturday, October
12, announces Elwyn Bugge, director
of tennis. Drawings will be prepared
and posted by Monday, October 14.
Initial ranking of varsity and fresh
man tennis players will be influenced
considerably by the showing made in
this tourney, states Bugge. Anyone is
eligible to enter; and approximately
85 have signed up to date.
