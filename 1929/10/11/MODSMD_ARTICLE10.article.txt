# TRAINS CRASH HEAD-ON
## 
### 
PORTAGE, Pa., Oct. 10. (/P) — An
east-bound passenger train and a west
bound freight train crashed head-on,
west of the Pennsylvania passenger
station here late last night, killing
three trainmen.
