# Thirteenth Victim Of Riot In Canon City Prison Dies
## 
### 
CANON CITY, Colo., Oct. 10 (#»).—
Myron Goodwin, a guard in the Colorado
state penitentiary, who was one of the
first men to be shot in the riot of a week
ago, died from his wounds today. Good
win's death brings the death toll of the
riot to thirteen, eight of the dead having
been guards.
