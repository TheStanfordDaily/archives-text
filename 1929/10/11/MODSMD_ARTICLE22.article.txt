# Theta Sigma Phi Outlines Plans At Initial Meeting
## 
### 
Thcta Sigma Phi, women's honorary
journalistic fraternity, organized for the
lirst time this year at their meeting yes
terday afternoon. Plans for the year's
work were discussed but, according to
President Phoebe Plate, "None of these
are yet ready for publication." Meetings
will be held every other Thursday at 5
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse, tea
being served. This year's officers are:
Phoebe Plate, president; Sylva Weaver,
vice-president; Jessie Fleming, archivist;
and Ruth Patterson, secretary.
