# Stanford Alumnus Writes Of California Universities
## 
### 
Laurence L. Hill, '11, is compiling
a volume devoted to the history of
institutions of higher learning in
southern California. Photographs and
material concerning Claremont, Occi
dental, Loyola colleges, and Redlands
University, the University of Southern
California, and the University of Cali
fornia at Los Angeles will be in
cluded.
During his University Days Hill
was a member of the Quad staff and
edited the Stanford Daily during his
senior year. He is at present con
nected with the First Security Na
tional Bank of California.
