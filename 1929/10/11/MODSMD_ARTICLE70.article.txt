# Dr. Clyde Visits Stanford On Way Back From Orient
## 
### 
Dr. Paul Clyde, who was visiting pro
fessor of Far Eastern history at Stanford
last year, stopped at the campus yesterday
on his way home from a three months'
tour of the Orient, where he visited the
principal cities of Manchuria, Korea, and
japan. Dr. Clyde is a member of the
history department of Ohio State Univer
sity, at Columbus, Ohio.
