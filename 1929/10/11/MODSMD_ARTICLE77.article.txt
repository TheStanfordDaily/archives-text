# Dr. Clark Injured In Auto Wreck; Recovering Rapidly
## 
### 
Professor Arthur B. Clark, head of
the Art Department, last night was
reported recovering rapidly from in
juries suffered in an automobile acci
dent Monday. While on the way to
Berkeley, Clark's car was grazed by
a stage, knocking a wheel off and
throwing it off the road. Clark suf
fered two broken ribs and was taken
to the Merritt Hospital in Oakland
for treatment.
