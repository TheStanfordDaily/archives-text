# WILLIAMS, McINTIRE ADDED TO STAFF OF WOMEN'S GYMNASIUM
## 
### 
Miss E. B. Williams of New Jersey
and Miss C. T. Mclntire of Lakeport
have been added to the staff of the
physical education department for
women this quarter.
Miss Williams is a graduate of El
mira College, New Jersey, and took a
postgraduate course for two years at
Wellesley College. She is at present
in charge of the swimming, diving,
and junior and senior hockey classes.
She has replaced Miss Marjory Lan
don in this work.
Miss Mclntire is a Mills graduate
and has had in addition two years at
the University of California. She lias
replaced Mrs. Helen Cramer as di
rector of dancing.
