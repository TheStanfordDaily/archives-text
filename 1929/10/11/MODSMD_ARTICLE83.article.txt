# Professor Domonoske, M.E. Head, Promoted To Major
## 
### 
According to official communication
received here yesterday from the Ad
jutant General in Washington, Pro
fessor A. B. Domonoske, Head of the
Mechanical Engineering Department,
has been promoted from the rank of
Captain in the Ordnance Reserves to
the rank of Major. He has been as
signed to the San Francisco Procure
ment District.
