# GEORGE YOUNG, NOTED DIPLOMAT, WILL GIVE SERIES OF LECTURES
## 
### 
A series of lectures, under the au
spices of the Committee on Public
Exercises, will be delivered by Mr.
George Young, distinguished British
diplomat, journalist, and scholar, on
the evenings of October 15, 16, and
17, at 7:30 o'clock in the Assembly
Hall.
Mr. Young, who was a leading
speaker at the recent Institute of
Politics at Williamstown, will speak
on "Labor Party Personalities and
Policies," "Post-War Constitutional
Changes in Europe," and "Command
and Freedom of the Seas," on Tues
day, Wednesday, and Thursday nights
respectively.
Mr. Young was for some years Pro
fessor of Portuguese Literature in
London University, and is Examiner
in Ottoman Law there. He is well
known as the author of numerous
revolutionary reports, and is a Fellow
of the Royal Historical Society.
