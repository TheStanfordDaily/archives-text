# Varsity Debate Tryouts In Little Theatre Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for membership on the var
sity debate squad will be held at 1:30
o'clock today in the Little Theater.
The subject of the four-minute tryout
speeches will be: "Resolved, That
modern science tends to destroy the
istic faith." Each speaker may take
the affirmative or negative side of this
question, and will be called upon tor
a three-minute rebuttal speech.
From the membership of this squad
the speakers for all intercollegiate de
bates in which Stanford is to partici
pate will be chosen.
