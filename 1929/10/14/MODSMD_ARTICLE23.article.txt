# Stanford Couple Wins Dancing Contest Cup
## 
### 
Jean Jamison, Kappa Kappa
Gamma, and Eminel Halsted, Kappa
Sigma, won first place in the dancing
contest sponsored Friday night by the
Hotel St. Francis, as a feature of the
initiation of their "College Night"
festivities.
Constance Morse and Stuart War
ner were among the last three couples
on the floor, but were cut in favor of
the other two. A silver loving cup was
presented to Miss Jamison and her
partner at the close of the contest,
which was judged by popular decision.
