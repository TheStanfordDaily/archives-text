# WEEK-END AT ASILOMAR
## 
### 
Celebrating their third annual Y.W.
C.A. Trek, 20 junior girls spent the
week-end at Asilomar, the Y.W.C.A.
camp located on the Monterey coast.
Recreation included swimming, horse
back riding, tennis, and hiking. Ar
rangements were in charge of a com
mittee with Gladys Gillig as chair
man.
