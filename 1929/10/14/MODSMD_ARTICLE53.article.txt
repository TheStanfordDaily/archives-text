# SUN MOTOR INVENTED
## 
### 
WORCESTER, Mass., Oct. 13 (/P).—
Robert H. Goddard, Clark University
physicist, has invented a sun motor
which he suggests can be used by
dirigibles and enable them to stay in
the air indefinitely.
