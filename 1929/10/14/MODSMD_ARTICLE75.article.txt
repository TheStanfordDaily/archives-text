# WRESTLERS BEGIN WORK
## 
### 
Wrestling classes begun last Friday
in the apparatus room of ICncina Gym
nasium with twelve men enrolled for
work. R. L. Nicholson, former West
ern Intercollegiate Light Heavyweight
champion, is instructing the group.
The wrestling period is scheduled for
4:20 p.m. every afternoon, and anyone
interested in the sport is requested to
register for the course with the Proc
tor in the gymnasium.
