# "FOLK SONGS" TOPIC OF TONIGHT'S TALK BY WARREN D. ALLEN
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will speak on "Folk Songs" in his
lecture Tuesday evening, October 22,
one of the regular series of lectures
given during autumn quarter. Another
address on "Melody in the Church"
has been scheduled for Wednesday,
November 13.
These are, in a way, preliminary to
the carol service of religious folk
music which is to be given Sunday,
December 8, under the direction of
Allen. Later in the year the Commit
tee on Public Exercises plans to bring
two distinguished musical scholars,
now residing in California, to the
University.
