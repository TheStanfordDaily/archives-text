# University Calendar Now Being Sold At Bookstore
## 
### 
University calendars of the wall
type for the academic year 1929-30
are now on sale at the Bookstore and
the Comptroller's Office in the Ad
ministration Building. These calen
dars not only contain the regular
monthly leaves but also a list of the
important dates during the quarters
such as final examination dates, etc.
The price is ten cents.
