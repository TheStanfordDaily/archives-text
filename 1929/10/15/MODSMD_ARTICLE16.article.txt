# Forty-One Tryoutees For Daily Survive First Cut
## 
### 
At a meeting of tryoutees for the
editorial staff of the Daily, held yes
terday afternoon, names of those sur
viving: the first cut were posted. The
number has been reduced from 75 to
41.
Tryoutees are now starting on a
trial period of real work, of an indefi
nite length, before the final cut which
will place those remaining on the
