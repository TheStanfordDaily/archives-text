# Pete Horner Will Furnish Music For Sequoia Dance
## 
### 
Leo Shapovalov, chairman of the
dance committee, announced that Pete
Horner and four of his most versatile
men will furnish music for the Se
quoia Radio Dance, to be held Friday.
Horner and his orchestra played at
Rio Nido this summer, and proved to
be one of the most popular musical
organizations in the Russian River
district. A feature of past dances at
Sequoia, the broadcasting of the dance
music by remote control through a
phonograph located in the lobby, will
be continued Friday .
