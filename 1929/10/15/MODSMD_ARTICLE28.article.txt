# SUPREME COURT WILL DECIDE AUTHORITY OF RADIO HOURS CONTROL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 14. The
authority of the Federal Radio Coin
mission to control the hours during
which radio broadcasting stations may
be on the air, a controversy of great
importance to the radio industry, will
be decided by the Supreme Court.
The highest tribunal today an
nounced it would review the decision
of the District of Columbia Court of
Appeals which had held that the com
mission could not compel the General
Electric Company to comply with an
order regulating the time its broad
casting station WGY at Schenectady,
N.Y., could operate. The Supreme
Court will consider the case on its
merits, and in the opinion of many its
decision will be vital to the life of
the commission.
