# California And Oxford To Debate On Talking Pictures
## 
### 
"Talkies" will be the topic under
discussion when the University of
California meets the Oxford debating
squad on November 13, the night fol
lowing the Stanford contest with the
English team.
Arrangements are being made to
broadcast the debate from the Oak
land Municipal Auditorium. The ques
tion will be: "Resolved, that talking
pictures will strike a deadly blow on
Western culture."
