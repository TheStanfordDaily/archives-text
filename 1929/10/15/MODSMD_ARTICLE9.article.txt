# Hammer And Coffin Elects Halderman And Thompson
## 
### 
Helen Halderman, Women's Editor
of Chaparral, and Joe Thompson, fre
quent contributor, were elected to
Hammer and Coffin at a meeting last
Thursday noon at the Kappa Sigma
house, according to Nelson Carter,
president. Helen Halderman's election
is honorary.
Plans were discussed for the meet
ing of the Western Association of Col
lege Comics, to be held here Novem
ber 7, 8, and 9. Carter, president of
the association, states that there will
be elaborate entertainment provided
for association members at morning
and afternoon sesions on each of the
three days.
