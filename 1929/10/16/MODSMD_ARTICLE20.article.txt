# Dr. E.C. Franklin Stops At Genoa En Route to London
## 
### 
Dr. Edward Curtis Franklin, who
is retiring this year as professor of
chemistry at Stanford, is at Genoa on
his way to London, according to a
letter received by Dr. Swain yester
day.
Dr. Franklin has spent the summer
abroad, having been selected guest
of honor from America to the annual
meeting of the British Association for
the Advancement of Sciences held this
year at Cape Town, South Africa. He
will speak at several educational in
stitutions in England and Scotland
before returning to his home on the
campus.
