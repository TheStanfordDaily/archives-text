# WOULDN'T IT JAR YOU?
## 
### 
Just when we complacently began to congratulate ourselves that Stan
ford was a University of national prominence because of the positions
held in world affairs by distinguished alumni, and the brilliant football
victory over the Army last year, along comes something to spoil the
illusion. For it must have been an illusion.
From the University of Wisconsin comes a letter addressed to "Editor
of the Stanford Daily, University of California, Palo Alto."
