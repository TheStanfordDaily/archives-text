# McClelland Will Talk Of Own Life At English Club
## 
### 
John McClelland, instructor in Eng
lish composition, will speak at a
meeting of the English Club tomor
row evening at 7:30 in the basement
of the Women's Clubhouse. He will
talk about his own life and experi
ences. Walter Ferguson, baritone, will
sing at the conclusion of the lecture.
Marshall Kirby is in charge of the
program, which is open to the public.
This is the second meeting of the
club this year and will be conducted
by Ward Foultz, the new president.
