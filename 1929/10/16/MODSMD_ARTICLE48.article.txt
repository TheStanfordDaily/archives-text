# Drawing For Interclass Tournament Posted Today
## 
### 
Drawings for the interclass tennis
singles tournament will be posted to
day in Encina Gymnasium by Coach
Elwyn Bugge, director of interclass
tennis.
The entries, which closed last Fri
day, listed 33 freshmen, 24 sopho
mores, 41 juniors, and 55 seniors.
Bugge set October 23 as the final date
for playing off the first two rounds of
the drawings.
