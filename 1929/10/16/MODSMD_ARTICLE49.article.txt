# Opening Smoker Will Be Given At Branner Tonight
## 
### 
Branner Club holds its opening
smoker in the lobby tonight at 7:30.
Leonard Bartlett, president, will wel
come new members and outline club
activities for the fall quarter, with
particular reference to the Branner
Informal to be held October 25th. Tom
Murphy's orchestra will furnish musi
cal entertainment.
New members are particularly
urged to attend this smoker. Cigars,
cigarettes, and refreshments will be
handed out during the evening under
direction of "Brick" Clark, smoker
chairman.
