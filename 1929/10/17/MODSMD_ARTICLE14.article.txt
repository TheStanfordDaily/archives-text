# Leland Cutler, Trustee, Joins S.F. Brokerage Firm
## 
### 
Leland Cutler, a member of the
Board of Trustees of Stanford, has
become a member of the firm of
Brayton, Cutler, and Cooke, members
of the San Francisco Stock Exchange
and San Francisco Curb Exchange.
He has also been made a director
and vice-president of the Fidelity and
Deposit Co. of Maryland and is a trus

director of San Francisco Chamber of
