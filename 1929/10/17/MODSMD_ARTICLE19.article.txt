# ENROLLMENT SHOWS ROBLE WOMEN ARE FROM MANY STATES
## 
### 
Twenty-two states of the union
are represented at Roble Hall
this quarter, according to a sur
vey made today. California, as
may well be expected, leads the
lists with 175 students of the
total enrollment of 225. Two
other Pacific Coast states, Wash
ington and Oregon, follow with
five and six women respectively.
Arizona, Colorado, Indiana, Illi
nois, Idaho, lowa, Massachusetts,
Michigan, Minnesota, Mississip
pi, Nebraska, New Jersey, New
York, Ohio, Oklahoma, Pennsyl
vania, South Dakota, and Wyom
ing are also represented. Besides
the 45 students from other states,
there are two women from China
and one from Alaska.
