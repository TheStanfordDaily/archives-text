# CELLAR TO OPEN NEXT WEEK; NEW COUNTER, BOOTHS TO BE READY
## 
### 
Every effort is being made to re
open the "Cellar," Stanford's centrally
located confectionery and dispensary
of light lunches, by Tuesday or Wed
nesday of next week, according to
Miss Etta Handy, director of the Uni
versity dining halls.
The old "Cellar" has been complete
ly remodeled and the facilities greatly
increased, during the summer. A new
counter that will seat 40 people and
booths that will accommodate 100 as
well as other new equipment have
been installed in an attempt to
quicken and enlarge the service given
to students who desire lunches at odd
times.
