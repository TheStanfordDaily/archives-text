# YOUNG TO SPEAK ON POSITION OF WOMEN FOLLOWING LECTURE
## 
### 
An opportunity to meet informally
for discussion will be offered to Stan
ford women tonight at the Women's
Clubhouse when George Young, Brit
ish diplomat, will talk on the rising
status of British women in public life.
The meeting will be held directly after
Mr. Young's lecture in the Assembly
Hall.
As a member of the Labor Party
now in control of the English govern
ment, the lecturer is interested in the
position in politics now being occu
pied by women. Mr. Young is familiar
with intimate stories concerning the
political careers of women members
of the British Parliament.
Refreshments will be served by
members of the faculty.
