# Rabbi Newman Will Speak In Chapel Sunday Morning
## 
### 
Rabbi Louis I. Newman, of Temple
Emanu-El, San Francisco, will speak
at the 11 o'clock Chapel services Sun
day morning. The music will be the
temple service Liturgy under the di
rection of Cantor Ruben R. Rinder,
who. has been preparing the Stanford
choir for the service.
Rabbi Newman spoke on the rela
tion of religion and science in the
Memorial Chapel during November of
last year. He has also addressed the
campus in evening lectures on reli
gious subjects.
Warren D. Allen, will give his Sun
day afternoon organ recital at 4
o'clock. The music will be the same
as in the recital Thursday.
