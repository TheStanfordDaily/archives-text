# Stanford Seal Carved On New N.C. University Union
## 
### 
Stanford is the only University
west of the Mississippi to have its
seal carved on the Union Building at
the new Duke University in Durham,
North Carolina.
The top of the building is decorated
with stone shields from twenty-five
universities. Oxford, Cambridge, Paris,
Harvard, Yale, Princeton, Pennsylva
nia, Dartmouth, Virginia, Navy, Chi
cago, Michigan, Cornell, and Wiscon
sin are aamong the universities repre
sented.
