# Denver Policemen Act To Stop Chinese In Tong War
## 
### 
DENVER, Colo., Oct. 17 (£>).—Forty
Chinese, twenty of them said to have been
"imported" from Portland, Oregon, were
arrested in a police drive last night which
was said to have narrowly averted a tong
war between two rival Chinese factions.
After questioning, all but three of the
men were released. The three were held
for invstigation and the 20 Portland men
were ordered to leave town.
