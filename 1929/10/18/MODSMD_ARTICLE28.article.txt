# Mary Isabel Fry Elected Secretary Of Review Club
## 
### 
Mary Isabel Fry was elected the
new secretary of the English Review
Club, which held its first meeting of
the year in the home of Dr. Kennedy,
acting head of the English depart
ment. Mabel Vanderzee, English
graduate, addressed the club.
The object of the club is to keep
English majors, chiefly graduate stu
dents, in touch with source material
for book reviewing.
