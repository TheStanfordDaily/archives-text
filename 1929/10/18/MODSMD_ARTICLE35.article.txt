# Branner Grid Dance Ticket Sale To Commence Today
## 
###

Dance, to be held October 25, will be sold
to members of Branner Club exclusively
today and tomorrow, according to Art
Angel, who told of plans for the dance
at a smoker held Wednesday night by
Branner Club.
