# Pi Sigma Alpha To Hear British Diplomat Tonight
## 
### 
George Young, noted British diplomat
and visiting lecturer on the campus, will
talk informally to Pi Sigma Alpha, hon
orary political science fraternity, after a
dinner to be given in his honor tonight
in the Union at 6: 15 o'clock.
Young will answer questions raised by
his recent lectures in the Assembly Hall
and by the discusions. All members of
Pi Sigma Alpha and specially invited
guests are to attend the dinner.
