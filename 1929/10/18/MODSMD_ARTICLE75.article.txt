# Noach And Snyder Wed In San Francisco Yesterday
## 
### 
Jeannette Snyder, '30, of San Jose,
and William Raymond Noack, '27, of
Oakland, were married yesterday af
ternoon at 3:30 in the Supreme Court
room of the State Building in San
Francisco. The civil ceremony was
performed by Judge William F. Lang
don of the Supreme Court. Mr. and
Mrs. Noack expect to live at Kingscote
for the remainder of the year.
