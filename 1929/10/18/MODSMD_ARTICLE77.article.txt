# Prowlers Arc Fired At By Canon City Prison Guard
## 
### 
CANON CITY, Colo., Oct. 17 (/P).—
Two mysterious prowlers were fired upon
by a guard of the Colorado state peni
tentiary last night when they were seen
on the Skyline, a mountain forming the
northwest wall of the prison.
The men apparently escaped without
injury and nothing further was seen of
them.
