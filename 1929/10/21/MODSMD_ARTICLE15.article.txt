# Concert Series Tickets To Be Distributed November 1
## 
### 
Season tickets for the A.S.S.U. Con
cert Series will be distributed about
November 1. They are on sale at the
A.S.S.U. office in the Old Union at any
time, according to Carl Mather, man
ager.
The San Francisco Symphony, with
Ignaz Friedman as piano soloist, will
appear on the first pfogram December
4. They will be followed at intervals
of about a month by Efrem Zimbalist,
violinist, Lawrence Tibbett, baritone,
Mme. Sigrid Onegin, contralto, and
the Kedroff Quartet, Russian singers.
Season tickets for the entire season
of five concerts may be obtained for
as low at $2.50.
