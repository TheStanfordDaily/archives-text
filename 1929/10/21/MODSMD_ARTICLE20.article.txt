# Hall Announces All-Roble Jolly-Up Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
An all-Roble jolly-up will be given
| at Roble Hull tomorrow night from 7
| to 8 o'clock. This is the first jolly-up
! which has been given for the whole
hall in several years, since they are
usually given under the auspices of
the freshman class.
Phoebe Plate is in charge of the
dance, and the sponsors will act as a
reception committee.
