# Intramural Tournament Handball Entries Close
## 
### 
Today is the last day for entering
the intramural one-wall handball
tournament. Drawings are expected to
he posted tomorrow, and match play
will start this week.
So far thirty-two teams have signi
fied their intention of taking part in
this sport. Last year only twenty-four
groups entered the tournament. All
matches will be played in Encina gym
under direction of Myron Sprague.
