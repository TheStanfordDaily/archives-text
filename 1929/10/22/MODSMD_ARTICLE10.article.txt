# Bugge Announces Schedule For Use Of Tennis Courts
## 
### 
The old tennis courts adjacent to
the basketball pavilion will not be
available to general use between 4:30
and 5 o'clock from Monday to Friday,
due to occupation by regular physical
education classes, announces Elwyn
Bugge, director of tennis. With this
exception, the schedule of court use
ig as follows:
Daily until 2 p.m.—All courts.
Monday to Friday after 4 p.m.—Old
courts.
Tuesday and Thursday, all day—
Old courts.
Saturday all day—All courts.
