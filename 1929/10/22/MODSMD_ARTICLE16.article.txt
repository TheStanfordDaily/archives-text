# Press Editor To Speak On Publication Of Researches
## 
### 
Professor William Hawley Davis,
editor of the University Press, will
speak tomorrow night at the first
regular meeting of the Science Asso
ciation at 8 o'clock in the Faculty
Club House.
Davis' subject will be "Publication"
and he will discuss and analyze the
situation in the United States, and
especially at Stanford, in regard to
the publication of the results of scien
tific research, particularly considering
the viewpoint of the research worker.
