# "DINK" TRIES HAND AT WRITING PLAYS
## 
### 
"Dink" Templeton, track coach of
national fame, has entered a new field
of activity. He is author of a play
called "S-O-S," and has submitted the
manuscript in the San Francisco Com
munity Chest drama-writing contest
that closes November 1. The plays
are supposed to deal with welfare
work, and a number already submitted
have been passed on to the judges by
Mrs. Sanford S. Wendell, head of the
committee unit in charge of manu
scripts.
Mrs. Helen H. Wilbur of Stanford
University has also written a play for
the contest. It is entitled "The Boy
Business."
