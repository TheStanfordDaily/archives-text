# DR. SURFACE SPEAKS TO POLITICAL SCIENCE DEPT. THIS AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Dr. Frank M. Surface, Professor of
Marketing in the Graduate School of
Busines, will speak to faculty mem
bers and graduate students of the
Political Science department in an
assembly in the Little Theatre at 4
o'clock today.
Dr. Surface's subject will be "Re
cent Trends in the Relation of the
Federal Government to Business." He
is well qualified to speak on this sub
ject as he is now on leave from the
United States Department of Com
merce where he is Director of the
Domestic Commerce Division of the
Bureau of Foreign and Domestic Com
merce.
