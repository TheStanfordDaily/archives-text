# CAMPUS MECHANIC DISCOVERS MISSING BILL ROTH DONKEY
## 
### 
Leo Walsh, mechanic in the Cor
poration Yard, today is being con
sidered for the post of volunteer de
tective under Fred Frehe, foreman of
outside properties, following Walsh's
successful sleuthing in discovering a
lost donkey belonging to young Bill
Roth, son of Comptroller Almon E.
Roth.
Walsh was detailed by Frehe to lo
cate the missing animal after it was
reported lost by the Palo Alto freight
station. The burro, in travelling from
Mexico to Palo Alto, decided to get
off in San Jose and it was several
days before Walsh found the donkey.
Henry Maier, chief gardener, present
ed the animal to young Roth.
