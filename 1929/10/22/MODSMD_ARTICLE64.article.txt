TUESDAY
4:00 p.m.—Graduate School of Busi
ness assembly in Little Theatre. Dr.
Frank M. Surface will speak on "Re

morial Church.

orary debating society, will be held

speak on "Resolved, that science
is tending to destroy theistic faith."
