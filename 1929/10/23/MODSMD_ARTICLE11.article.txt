# New Roble Social Director To Act During Fall Quarter
## 
### 
Mrs. Walter Alden Shockley, newly
appointed social director of Roble
Hall, was introduced at the Roble
house meeting last night. Mrs. Shock
ley will hold the position until Mrs.
Mary H. Stearns, former director, re
turns from Oregon at the beginning
of winter quarter.
Dr. Dyment spoke on the rest prob
lem of Roble Hall at the meeting.
Billie Kahn was elected to fill the
Place vacated by Jeannette Snyder
Noack on Women's Conference.
