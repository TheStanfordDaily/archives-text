# Women's Glee Club Meets At Seven O'Clock Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Glee Club
will meet tonight at 7:15 o'clock on
the top floor of the Women's Club
house, announces Dorothy Bogen,
president of the club.
New members are especially urged
to attend; no tryouts are to be held
and previous experience is not neces- j
sary.
