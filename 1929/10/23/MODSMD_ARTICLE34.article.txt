# Nathan Van Patten Gives Address To Library Club
## 
### 
Speaking informally on "The Use
of Imagination in Bibliographical Re
search," Nathan van Patten, Univer
sity Libraries director, talked to the
Stanford Libraries Professional Club
yesterday afternoon at 4 o'clock in
the Felton Room. Nina Almond, Hoo
ver War Librarian, who is chairman
of the club this year, presided.
