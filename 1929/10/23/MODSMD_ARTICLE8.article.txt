# Guerard Will Be Speaker At First French Club Meeting
## 
### 
Professor Guerard will be the
speaker at the meeting of the Cercle
Francaise to be held Wednesday eve
ning at 8:30 o'clock in the basement
of the Women'a Clubhouse. This meet
ing, which is the first of the quarter,
will be open to the public. Members
are especially urged to attend.
