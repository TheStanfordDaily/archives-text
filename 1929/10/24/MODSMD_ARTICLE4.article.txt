# SEAT SALE OPENS AT 1 O'CLOCK FOR "THE IVORY DOOR"
## 
### 
Tickets for the first dramatic
production of the year, "The
Ivory Door," a satirical comedy
by A. A. Milne, to be given No
vember 1, go on sale today at
one o'clock in front of the Old
Union. This will be the first time
that "The Ivory Door" has been
presented on the Pacific Coast.
A large seat sale is expected.
Prices will be $1.00 and $1.25.
Tickets may be obtained at Roble
Hall at 12:30 o'clock, and at Sher
man, Clay Co. in Palo Alto this
afternoon, as well as in front of
the Union at one o'clock.
