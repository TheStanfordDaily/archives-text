# Organ Recital To Feature "Vesper Program" Today
## 
### 
Under the title of "Vesper Program"
combined organ recital and vesper
services will be given each Thursday,
starting at 4:15 this afternoon in the
Memorial Chapel. The organ recital
will begin at 4:15 and the vesper serv
ice at 4:45.
The scripture reading and short talk,
which are to be given by Acting Chap
lain Reed B. Cherington, will follow
Warren D. Allen's third offering and
be succeeded by the "Solemn Pre
lude," from the "Gloria Domini" of T.
Tertius Noble. Noble was formerly at
Yorkminster, England, but is now or
ganist at St. Thomas' Church, New
York.
