# Football Show Skits Due To Committee By Monday
## 
### 
All songs and ideas and copy for
skits for the football Gaieties must
be handed in by next Monday. These
ideas may be given to Stan Mullin,
Paul Speegle, Doug Gregg, or Burnell
Gould, members of the committee in
charge of the Gaieties.
Tryouts for the musical acts, skits,
and specialty stunts will take place
Tuesday afternoon. The Gaieties are
under the direction of Ram's Head So
ciety, men's honorary dramatic so
ciety.
