# November 1 Set Final Day For First-Round Handball
## 
### 
First round matches of intramural
handball must be played by November 1,
according to Myron Sprague, in charge
of the sport. Drawings of the thirty-one
teams entered in the tournament are post
ed in Encina gym. Matches will consist
of two singles and one doubles game. No
attempt has been made to provide officials,
but opposing teams are free to select them
by mutual consent if deemed necessary.
Groups may enter two, three, or four
man teams.
