# WEDDING DATE SET
## 
### 
The marriage of Elizabeth Cutten
of San Francisco to William Maguire,
'29, of Stanford, has been set for the
afternoon of November 14 at the
home of the bride's parents, at four
o'clock. Guido Caglieri, '31, has been
selected as best man. Caglieri and
Maguire are both members of Phi
Kappa Psi fraternity.
