# Newman Club Welcomes New Catholic Students
## 
### 
At the first meeting of the Newman
Club to welcome new students Presi
dent Jess de Lucchi last night out
lined briefly plans for the coming
year. Rev. A. J. Carrol gave a short
talk on the purpose of the club. Danc
ing followed and refreshments were
served.
Another short business meeting fol
lowed by a regular dance will be held
Tuesday, November 5. Those who did
not receive invitations this time are
requested to notify the secretary of
their mailing address, Box 1366, Cam
pus, as soon as possible.
