# Dr. H.J. Ryan Will Speak To E.E. Students Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Harris J. Ryan will be the prin
cipal speaker at tonight's meeting of
the Stanford chapter, American Insti
tute of Electrical Engineers. The
meeting, which is the first social gath
ering of the year for the Institute, will
be held at 7:30 o'clock in the Y.M.C.A.
reception room in the lobby of the
Old Union.
Dr. Ryan will talk on the purposes
of the electrical engineering society,
its aims, ideals, and of some of the
projects that the society is sponsor
ing.
Douglas Ring is the newly elected
president of the Stanford chapter, tak
ing the place vacated by Donald Mur
ray, who is not registered in school
this quarter.
