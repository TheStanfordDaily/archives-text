# PERFECT CAMERA
## 
### 
BERKELEY, Oct. 23 (/P).—University
of California scientists announced per
fection of a camera shutter with an oper
ating speed of one-millionth of a second.
The purpose of the invention is to facili
tate the study of electric sparks.
