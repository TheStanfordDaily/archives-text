# Nomination For A.S.S.U. Ex Committee Vacancy Opens
## 
### 
Nominations for the office of senior
representative on the executive com
mittee of A.S.S.U. and of president,
vice-president, secretary, and treas
urer of the freshman class will be
open this morning and continue until
Thursday at 6:00 p.m., according to
Bob Clarke, chairman of the election
board.
Blanks will be placed at the law
steps and should bo put in the box
provided for that purpose.
Class nomination blanks will be
furnished for the freshman office, and
regular A.S.S.U. blanks will be used
for the executive committee office.
