# Stanford DeMolay Initiates New Members At Old Union
## 
### 
Ten new members were initiated at
a special meeting of the Stanford
DeMolay Club Monday evening in the
Y.M.C.A. room of the Old Union. The
initiates were Donald Anderson, Sid
ney Brash, Louis Ehret, Louise Fer
rino, William Eberwine, Melvin Peter
son, Arnold Steiner, Lawrence Baker,
Harry Edwards, and Noel Eldred.
Bob Triplett, club president, pre
sided at the meeting. It was decided
that an informal dance be given by
the group in San Francisco on or
about December 6.
