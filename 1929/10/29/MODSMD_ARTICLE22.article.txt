# Herb Wenig Outlines Plans For Convention Of N.F.S.A.
## 
### 
Organization plans for the coming
National Students Federation of
America convention were outlined last
night to committee chairmen by Herb
Wenig, general chairman. The con
gress will meet January 1, 2, 3, and 4
Avhen Stanford will be host to two
hundred delegates from as many
American student-bodies.
Complete committee personnel will
meet at 7:15 o'clock tomorrow night
in (he Women's Clubhouse to continue
the work of organization.
