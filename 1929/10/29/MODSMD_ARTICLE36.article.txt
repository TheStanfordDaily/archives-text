# Dean Yost Will Speak To Women Debaters Tonight
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will speak to
Wranglers at 6:45 tonight in the Wo
men's Clubhouse concerning possible
activities on the Stanford campus for
women interested in either debating
or public speaking.
Officers for the year will be elected
at this time, and topics for the annual
intercollegiate debate with the Uni
versity of California women will be
submitted.
Refreshments will be served at the
conclusion of the meeting, which will
terminate about seven-thirty o'clock.
