# Al Roth's Mountain Cabin Goal Of Y.M.C.A. Climbers
## 
### 
Comptroller Al Roth's cabin in the
Santa Cruz Mountains will be the goal
of the Y.M.C.A.'s first week-end trip
on November 2 and 3.
All interested are invited to take
part in the trip, and those planning
to go on the first outing should leave
their names and addresses at the
Y.M.C.A. office in the Old Union be
fore October 31 so that provisions for
food and transportation may bo ar
ranged.
