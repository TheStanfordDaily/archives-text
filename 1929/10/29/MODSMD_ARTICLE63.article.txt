# ENCINA INFORMAL ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The Encina informal will be Riven
Saturday night, December 7, the dance
committee of the Encina Club an
nounced at a meeting last evening.
Bids well sell at $2.50 each. An orches
tra will be imported from San Fran
cisco. The dance will be closed to all
except members of the Encina Club.
