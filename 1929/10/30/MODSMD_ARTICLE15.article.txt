# Final Gaieties Tryouts Will Be Held At 2:30 P.M.
## 
### 
Remaining tryouts for specialty acts
for the Big Game Gaieties will be held
this afternoon at 2:30 o'clock in the
Little Theatre, Ram's Head officials
announced.
Yesterday's tryouts uncovered much
hidden talent for the forthcoming re
vue, it was revealed. Singing, danc
ing, and comedy possibilities are seen
for the program. The show, which will
be directed by Harold Helvenston, is
presented on the eve of the Big Game
under the auspices of the Ram's Head
Society.
