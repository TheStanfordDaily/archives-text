# Henrietta Thomson Speaks Informally Before Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Henrietta Thomson, well-known on
the Pacific Coast for her work in the
field of international relations, will
speak informally at the Y.W.C.A. tea
held Friday afternoon from 3:30 to
5:30 o'clock at the Women's Club
house. Miss Thomson will also be on
the campus on Thursday.
This is the first of a series of
group discussions to be given at vari
ous times during the war for those
interested in special branches of
Y.W.C.A. work.
