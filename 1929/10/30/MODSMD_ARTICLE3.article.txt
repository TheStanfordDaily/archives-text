# Chi Nu Kappa Will Meet At Classical Lit Library
## 
### 
Chi Nu Kappa, honorary classical
literature society, will meet tonight
at 7:30 o'clock in the library of the
classical literature building.
The main purpose is to suggest and
discuss possible new members. Fol
lowing this, plans for the programs
throughout the year will be discussed.
