# Curator Herre Will Speak Today On New Guinea Life
## 
### 
Dr. A. W. Herre, curator of the
Stanford Museum of Natural History,
who has recently returned from a
year's sojourn in the South Seas, will
address the Palo Alto chamber of
commerce at their noon luncheon to
day at the Sunset Cafeteria. He will
speak on "Some Aspects of Life in
New Guinea." The general public is
invited.
