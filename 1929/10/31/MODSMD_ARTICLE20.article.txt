# Radio Club Meets Tonight For Election Of Officers
## 
### 
Plans for the quarter will be discussed
and officers elected at a meeting of the
Stanford Radio Club at 7:30 tonight in
room 263 of the Civil Engineering Build
ing.
Activities of the club this term will in
clude talks by authorities on radio, one
of which will be given by Dr. Frederick
E. Terman, of the Electrical Engineer
ing Department. Trips to some of the
large commercial and marine broadcast
ing stations in the San Francisco district
are also planned.
Students interested may get in touch
with Jack Low, president, at room 305
Union.
