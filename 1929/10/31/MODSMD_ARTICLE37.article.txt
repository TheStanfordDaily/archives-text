# Sign Now For Y.M.C.A. Outing At Roth's Cabin
## 
### 
All interested in participating in the
Y.M.C.A. outing at Comptroller A 1
Roth's log cabin in the Santa Cruz moun
tains arc asked to sign up today at the
Y.M.C.A. office.
This trip to Roth's home among the
redwoods is the first sponsored by the
organization this year. Transportation
will be furnished for all who need it. All
students interested arc invited to take part.
