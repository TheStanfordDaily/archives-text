# TO OVERHAUL GRAF
## 
### 
FRIEDRICSCHAFEN, Germany,
Oct. 30. (>P) —The dirigible Graf Zep
pelin will make one more trip on No
vember 6 after which it will be taken
out of service and thoroughly over
hauled. The November flight will be
to Frankfordt on Main.
