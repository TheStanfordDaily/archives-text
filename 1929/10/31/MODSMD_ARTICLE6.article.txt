# Cosmopolitan Club Picnic At La Honda On Saturday
## 
### 
At the luncheon of the Cosmopolitan
Club yesterday noon it was announced
that a trip of club members to La
Honda will start from the postoffice
at 3 p.m. Saturday. It is planned that
a picnic supper will be served around
campfires, after which the party will
return to the campus.
Miss Wakako Domoto, secretary of
the club, requests that members who
wish to attend sign up with her in
order that transportation and food
may be secured for all.
