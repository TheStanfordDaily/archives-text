# Philip Cavalero Cleared Of Auto Accident Blame
## 
### 
Phillip Cavalero, president of the
A.S.S.U. last year, was cleared of
blame for an automobile crash that
occurred last March opposite Mills
Field by the superior court in Red
wood City yesterday. Guiseppina and
Angelo Gianoli, guest of Frank Casa
granda, whose auto was involved in
the crash with Cavalero's, were
awarded $2800 damages.
Cavalero and Casagranda were de
fendants in a $26,631 suit filed by
Gianolis. The judgment was against
Casagranda, the jury giving Mrs.
Gianoli $2500 for a fractured skull
and her husband $300 for injuries.
