# Polo Manager Tryoutees Wanted; To Receive Units
## 
### 
A sccond call for tryoutees for polo
manager was issued today by Norton T.
Faus, last year's manager. Men who try
out will receive a unit of credit per quar
ter, reporting at the polo field to look
after the polo balls, shirts, and cooling
the ponies. The manager is to be chosen
in the spring quarter.
Men interested get in touch with Cap
tain Richard A. Gordon, coach, or Faus.
