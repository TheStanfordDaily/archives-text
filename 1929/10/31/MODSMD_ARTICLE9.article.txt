# Saturday Last Day For Seniors' Quad Pictures
## 
### 
Seniors must have their Quad pictures
taken before Saturday noon. November
first at twelve o'clock is the absolute
deadline. Appointments can be made at
the Women's Clubhouse between nine and
twelve or one and five, or by calling the
editorial oflice of the Quad, Palo Alto
6161.
Before having his picture taken, each
senior must have purchased a senior
Quad, seven dollars, or paid a deposit of
three dollars, the balance due later.
