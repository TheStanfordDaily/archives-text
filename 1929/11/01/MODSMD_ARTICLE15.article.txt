# University Press Releases Volume On Belgian Relief
## 
### 
"Documents of the Public Relations
Commission for the Relief in Belgium"
has just been released by the Univer
sity Press.
This work, consisting of two vol
umes, was compiled by H. H. Fisher,
lecturer in History at Stanford, and
George I. Gay, of the Commission for
the Relief in Belgium. Herbert Hoover
is chairman of the commission.
The material contained in these
volumes may be found in the Hoover
War Library of Stanford University.
