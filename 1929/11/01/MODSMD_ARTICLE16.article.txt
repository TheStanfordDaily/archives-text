# Sorority Teas To Be Held For New Women On Sunday
## 
### 
The first sorority teas for new wo
men are to be given Sunday by Alpha
Phi, Gamma Phi Beta, and Kappa
Alpha Theta.
These teas are given annually by
every house for the purpose of getting
sorority women and now women ac
quainted. There are to be three days
for teas this year: November 3, 10,
and 17, with three teas on the third,
four on the tenth, and three on the
seventeenth.
