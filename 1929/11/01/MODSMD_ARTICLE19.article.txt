# "JOURNEY'S END," WAR PLAY, WILL BE READ ON TUESDAY EVENING
## 
### 
A reading by Mrs. Guy Stevens Far
rington of the war play, "Journey's
End," written by R. C. Sherriff, will
constitute the Tuesday evening pro
gram which will be given at 7:30 in
the Assembly Hall November 5. The
public will be welcome.
Mrs. Farrington is a graduate of the
University of California and a mem
ber of the faculty of the Extension
Division. Her particular field is the
interpretation of drama. The play has
been successful in London and New
York, and will probably come to the
west coast soon.
