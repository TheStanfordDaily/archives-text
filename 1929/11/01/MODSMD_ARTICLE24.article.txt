# Noted French Organist To Give Recital Here Tuesday
## 
### 
M. Marcel Dupre, noted French organ
ist, will play at the University assembly,
held Tuesday morning in the Memorial
Church at 11:00 o'clock. M. Dupre is
now head of the organ department at the
Paris Conservatory, the American Con
servatory in Fontainebleu, and at the
Ecole Normalc de Musique in Paris.
