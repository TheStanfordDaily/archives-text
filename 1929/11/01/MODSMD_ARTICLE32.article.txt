# A.S.S.U. Concert Tickets Ready In Old Union Today
## 
### 
Season tickets to the A.S.S.U. con
certs are ready for distribution today,
according to Carl Mather, manager of
the series. They may bo obtained at
the A.S.S.U. offices in the Old Union,
between the hours of 8:30 and 12 or

1 and 5.
The first concert of the series will
be presented December 4 by the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra, with
Alfred Hertz, conductor, and Ignaz
Friedman, pianist. The Orchestra will
piny its first popular concert of the
season in San Francisco next Sunday
afternoon.
