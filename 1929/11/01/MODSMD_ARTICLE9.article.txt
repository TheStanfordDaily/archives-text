# Harriers Asked To Start Cross-Country Training
## 
### 
Cross-country enthusiasts are asked
by Rill Ellington, director of intra
mural sports, to get in shape for the
annual intramural cross-country race
to be held November 16th just before
the Santa Clara game. Entries may be
posted in Encina gym until November
15th.
Gold, silver, and bronze medals will
be awarded to first, second, and third
place Individual winners. In addition,
points toward the intramural cup will
be given to teams entered in this
event.
