# A.S.S.U. TO HOLD INITIAL VOTE OF YEAR TOMORROW
## Senior Executive Committee Member, 1933 Officers Will Be Chosen 
### 
Four freshman class officers and
men's senior member of the executive
committee will be chosen at the polls
tomorrow at the first student election
of the year. The voting place is lo
cated on the Quad near the law steps
and will be open from 8 until 2 o'clock.
Three declinations have been re
ceived. Herbert Wenig, candidate for
the ex committee position, has de
clined his nomination. Willard De-
Weese, nominated for both president
and secretary of the Class of 1933,
will run for the presidency only. The
declination of Carl Kusliman, nomi
nated for secretary of the freshman
class, has been previously published.
Robert M. Clarke, chairman of the
election board, announces that there
will be a brief meeting of the Class
of 1933 at 1:15 o'clock today in Room
214, English Building. At this time
the several candidates for class of
fices will be introduced.

didates:
