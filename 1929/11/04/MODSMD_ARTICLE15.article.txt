# Funeral Held Friday For Holsclaw, Stanford Grad
## 
### 
The funeral of Dr. Florence M. Hols
claw, '94, and a graduate of the
Cooper Medical College (now the
Stanford Medical College) in 1907,
was hold Friday in San Francisco.
Prior to her death Dr. Holsclaw, who
was chief of the Children's Hospital
staff in the city, had the position of
clinical professor of pediatrics at the
University of California.
