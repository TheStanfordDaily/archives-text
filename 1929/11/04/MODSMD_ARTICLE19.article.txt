# Cosmopolitan Club Holds Picnic Supper At La Honda
## 
### 
The first trip of the Cosmopolitan
Club for this year took place Satur
day, when members of the club held
a picnic at La Honda. A supper was
served around a large bonfire, and the
party remained at La Honda for sev
eral hours before returning to the
campus later in the evening. It is
hoped that more such picnics, with
resulting fellowship of American and
foreign students of the University,
may be held by the Cosmopolitan Club
this quarter, announces Secretary
Wakako Domoto.
