# Glee Club Presents Short Program At City Theatre
## 
### 
Lending an authentic touch of real
ism to a collegiate program, the Stan
ford Glee Club made its first public
appearance of the fall quarter last
Friday night before a San Francisco
audience at the Granada Theater. Led
by Director C. Albert Kulmann, 43
members of the club presented a
short program at each of the two
evening performances. The following
songs were presented: Bleacher Med
ley (arranged by Kulmann), Old King
Cole (Forsythe), Passing By (Pur
cell), Oh Hail Us Ye Free (Verdi).
