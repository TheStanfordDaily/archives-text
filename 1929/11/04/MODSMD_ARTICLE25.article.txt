# Three Elected To Sequoia Club Executive Committee
## 
### 
Chid Knaebel, H. Pitts Mack, and
Fred Dietz were elected members of
the executive committee of Sequoia
Eating Club at the regular election
held Friday noon. The seven mem
bers of the executive committee have
control over the menu of Sequoia
Club, and the election was hotly con
tested. Fred Dietz fills the position of
sophomore member, while Mack and
Knaebel were chosen members-at
large.
