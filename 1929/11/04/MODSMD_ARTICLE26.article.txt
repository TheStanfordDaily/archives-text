# Masonic Club Will Meet In Encina At 7:30 Tonight
## 
### 
All Masons in the University are
urged to attend a meeting of the Ma
sonic Club tonight, according to Dick
Adams, president. The meeting will
be at 7:30 in Room 425 Encina.
A luncheon given at the Union last
Friday was the first of a series of
social events to be given by the
newly organized club, whose aim is
to bring together fellow members of
the lodge attending the University.
