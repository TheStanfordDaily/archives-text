# Chemical Society To Open Freshman Essay Contest
## 
### 
An essay contest open to freshman
students and offering valuable prizes
is being conducted by the American
Chemical Society.
Six subjects, each with prises of
$500, $300, and $200 for first, second,
and third place, may be used. The
contest closes March 1, 1930.
Any freshman students interested
may get in touch with Professor W.
H. Sloan, in the Chemistry building.
