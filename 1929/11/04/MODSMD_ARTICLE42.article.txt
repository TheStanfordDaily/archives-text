# Distribution Continues For Tickets To Concert Series
## 
### 
The distribution of season tickets
for the A.S.S.U. Concert Series will
continue all this week, according to
Carl Mather, manager of the concerts.
The tickets may be obtained by call
ing at the A.S.S.U. office in the Old
Union, between 8:30 and 12 every
morning and between 1 and 5 every
afternoon.
The first concert of the series will
be December 4, with Ignaz Friedman,
Polish pianist, playing with the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra.
