# Autumn Quarter Syllabus Fees Are Due On Thursday
## 
### 
Syllabus fees must be paid by
Thursday, November 7, according to
announcement made today by the
cashier's office. Checks may be sent
by mail to the cashier's office or may
be paid there.
If there are any errors in the syl
labus fee bills, students are asked to
adjust the mistakes at the registrar's
office before paying the fees.
The bills were mailed Friday night
and should have been received by
