# FOX SELLS F.N. STOCK
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 3. (/P)—William
Fox, president of Fox Film Corpora
tion, today announced completion of
the sale of his company's stock hold
ings in First National Pictures Cor
poration to Warner Brothers for a
sum in excess of $10,000,000.
