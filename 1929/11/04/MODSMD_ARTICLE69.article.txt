# STANFORD STUDENTS TO SEE ITALIAN BOOK FAIR IN S.F. LIBRARY
## 
### 
All Stanford students are Invited
by the Executive Committee of the
Italian Book Exhibition to attend a
Book Fair to be held at the San Fran
cisco Public Library during the sec
ond half of November. In order to
make it more pleasant for tho stu
dents, a special day, November 21st,
has been set aside for their visit.
Students wishing to attend the fair
are urged to register early so as to
secure seats in the motor cars, which
will be placed at their disposal. Pro
fessor Cooper of the German Depart
ment will be able to give more details
of the plans for the Fair.
