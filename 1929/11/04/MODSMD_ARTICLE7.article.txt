# BASSETT TO PRESIDE OVER OXFORD DEBATE, ANNOUNCES EMERSON
## 
### 
Professor Lee Emerson Bassett will
be chairman of the evening when
Stanford debates Oxford University
November 12 in the Assembly Hall,
according to announcement by J. G.
Emerson, debate coach, yesterday.
The Stanford speakers, Lawrence
Kimpton, Robert McClintock, and
Henry Harris, will present the affirm
ative side of the question, which is,
"Resolved, That this is the best of all
possible worlds."
Preceding the debate a banquet will
be given in the Union at which the
visiting Oxford debaters will be
guests of honor.
Students will be admitted free when
student body cards are shown; gen
eral admission will be fifty cents.
