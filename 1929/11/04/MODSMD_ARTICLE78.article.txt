# Davis Appointed To Direct Economics Of Farm Board
## 
### 
Dr. J. S. Davis, of the Food Research
Institute, has been appointed by the
Federal Farm Board in Washington
to the task of organizing and direct
ing the economic staff of the Board.
Dr. Davis has requested a leave of
absence of indefinite length, to begin
December 1, in order to take up his
new duties.
Dr. Davis' book, "The Export De
benture Bill," soon to be published,
has attracted favorable attention from
authorities on the subject.
