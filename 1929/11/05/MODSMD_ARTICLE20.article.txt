# P.A. Philatelists Welcome Student Stamp Collectors
## 
### 
"The Palo Alto Philatelic Society
was organized a year ago to stimu
late the fascinating hobby of collect
ing postage stamps," declared George
V. L. Haskell, president, at the regu
lar bi-monthly meeting last night in
the Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce
building.
"Stanford students are always wel
come to our meetings. There are sev
eral of them in our membership at
present and we hope to have many
more in the future."
Plans were discussed for an auction
to be held November 18, in which
anyone may enter stamps.
