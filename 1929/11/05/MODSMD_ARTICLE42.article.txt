# Euphronia Will Meet To Plan California Debates
## 
### 
A meeting of Euphronia Debating
Society will be held at 7:15 o'clock
in room 214 of the English building
for the purpose of accepting dates and
subjects for the debates planned with
Parliament and Sophomore debating
societies of the University of Cali
fornia, announces Louis Mercado,
president of Euphronia.
At this meeting the teams to repre
sent Euphronia in these debates will
be chosen, and plans will be laid for
the proposed banquet of Euphronia
and Parliament societies to be held
sometime this month.
