# Bids For Toyon Informal To Go On Sale Tomorrow
## 
### 
Bids for the Toyon Dutch Dance, to
be held November 15th, will go on
sale to members of Toyon Club for
$2.75 tomorrow night in room 258
Toyon.
Music for the informal will be fur
nished by Leo Gerckens' St. Francis
Night Club orchestra. Solo and trio
vocal numbers by members of the
orchestra will feature the evening's
entertainment.
Decorations are being planned by
the dance committee which will trans
form the classic Spanish lines of
Toyon hall into a veritable scene from
the Netherlands.
