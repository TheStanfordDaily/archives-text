# Translation Of 'De Oculis' Published By Local Press
## 
### 
"De Oculis," the first book ever
printed on the eye and. its diseases,
will be released today by the Stan
ford University Press.
Benventus of Jerusalem, eye sur
geon of the tenth century, was the
author of "De Oculis," which for 500
years was the most popular opthalmic
manual of the Middle Ages. This first
English translation, by Casey A.
Wood, M.D., LL.D., is from the Fer
rara incunable, printed in 1374 A.D.
