# Women's Debate Society To Hold Supper Meeting
## 
### 
Wranglers, women's debating so
ciety, will meet for supper at 5:45
o'clock tomorrow evening in the Wo
men's Clubhouse, announces Jean
Magnus, president. Immediately fol
lowing the supper, tryouts will be
held for the intercollegiate debating
teams.
A charge of twenty-five cents per
plate will be made for the supper,
reservations for which should be made
by tonight with Anne Hightower in
Roble, Magnus declared.
