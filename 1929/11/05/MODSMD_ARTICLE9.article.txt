# W.A.A. Board Will Discuss Competitive Sports Plans
## 
### 
Arrangement of team competition
at Sports Day, the annual triangular
athletic meet between Mills, Califor
nia, and Stanford, will be discussed
at a meeting of the W.A.A. Board to
be held tonight at 7 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Two new board members will be
chosen to fill the positions of fresh
man representative and hiking man
ager.
