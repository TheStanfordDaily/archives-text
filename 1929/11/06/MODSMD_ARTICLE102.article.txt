# Walter Shaw To Decorate For Roble Formal On 15th
## 
### 
The Roble Formal will be given on
November 15, according to Annie Lou
Carmichael, chairman of the commit
tee in charge. Walter Shaw of San
Francisco is to provide the decora
tions which wlil be fantastically mod
ern, and is also to furnish the pro
grams and favors. Neal Spaulding's
orchestra has been engaged for the
evening.
