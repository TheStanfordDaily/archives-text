# MARION STOREY IMPROVING
## 
### 
Marion Storey, 15, daughter of Dr.
T. A. Storey, men's medical advisor,
who was badly cut around the head
when the horse she was riding on the
campus last Saturday stumbled and
fell, is doing very well, according to
latest reports from the Palo Alto Hos
pital, where she is being confined for
a few days.
