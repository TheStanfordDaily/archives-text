# 'League' Publications Are Discussed By Cataloguer
## 
### 
"League of Nations Publications," es
pecially in regard to the Hoover War
Library, was the subject of Miss Louise
Katz, cataloguer for the Hoover Library,
in an informal talk before the Stanford
Libraries' Professional Club yesterday af
ternoon in the Felton room.
Together with exhibiting documents,
Miss Katz discussed the classification and
cataloguing of the publications from a
library and technical point of view.
