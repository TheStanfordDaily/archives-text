# BOSTON CRASH KILLS TWO
## 
### 
BOSTON, Nov. 5 (yP).—George John
son, federal air inspector, and "Hank"
Carter, pilot for Skyways, Inc., were
killed today when their airplane crashed
after going into a tailspin over the East
Boston airport.
