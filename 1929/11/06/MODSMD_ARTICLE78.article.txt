# Sale Of Toyon Informal Bids Start This Evening
## 
### 
Due to the illness of Parker Reed, club
manager, sale of bids to the Toyon Dutch
informal will be handled by Bert Weyl,
room 112 Toyon. Tickets will go on sale
this evening for $2.75.
According to plans for the Dutch dance,
to be held November 15, Toyon hall will
he converted into a veritable sccne from
Holland. Entrance will be through a
windmill being built to cover the main
entrance. Tulips, the leading product of
the Netherlands, windmills, dikes, Dutch
girls, and wooden shoes will convey the
foreign atmosphere.
