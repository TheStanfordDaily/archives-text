# Frosh Debate Tryoutees Will Meet Next Monday
## 
### 
At the meeting of Euphronia Debat
ing Society held last night, Leon Car
ley, freshman debate coach, an
nounced that a meeting of all fresh
men wishing to try out for debating
would be held in the Encina Lobby at
G:45 p.m., Monday. He explained that
a squad will be chosen from the try
outees which will culminate its activi
ties in the debate with the University
of California freshmen late in the
winter or early in spring quarter. Pre
liminary debates will probably be held
with Mills College and San Mateo
Junior College.
