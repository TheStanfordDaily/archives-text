# Many Men Will Compete In Novice Boxing Tourney
## 
### 
Lists for the Novice Boxing Tourna
ment posted in the boxing room show
marked interest in the coming bouts.
While the tournament will not be held
until December, many have signified
their intention to compete. The med
als which will be awarded to the win
ners have been ordered, and will be
on display soon. The tournament is
off to a good start, but all men inter
ested in competing should sign up at
once and begin training for the bouts.
