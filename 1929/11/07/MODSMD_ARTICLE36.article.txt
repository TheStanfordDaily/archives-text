# Waiting Room Completed At Appointment Offices
## 
### 
Improvements in the Appointment
Office rooms and facilities have been
completed. A waiting room, outside
the general office, is the greatest
single addition. It is a convenient
place to wait for interviews, and in
sures greater privacy in the inner of
fice. About 100 feet of space have
been added to the main room, and a
complete set of steel files, providing
for more adequate systematization of
the work, has been installed.
