# Col. McCabe To Address Military Smoker Tonight
## 
### 
Colonel E. R. W. McCabe, head of
the military department, will speak
on "The Command of Men in the Mili
tary Service" as the chief feature of
the Scabbard and Blade smoker for
advanced military students to be held
tonight at 7:30 o'clock in El Cuadro
clubroom, Room 3 Encina Annex.
From 80 to 90 military students and
Scabbard and Blade members are ex
pected to be present.
Some musical entertainment is on
the program, and the latter part of
the evening will bo taken up by a
"bull-session," according to Russell
Robinson, president of the society.
