# S.F. Supervisor Post Won By Former Stanford Man
## 
### 
Franck R. Havenner, ex-'OB, Theta
Delta Chi, and present chairman of
the San Francisco Board of Super
visors' finance committee, received
42,824 votes for re-election, it was
learned yesterday after another count
had been made. This was the second
highest sum cast for supervisor, An
gelo Rossi receiving 50,812.
C. I. Haley, '24, also a Theta Delt,
was not so successful as his frater
nity brother and received 15,250 in
the contest. Haley was business man
ager of "The Daily Palo Alto" (now
"The Daily") during his senior year
and held several class offices.
