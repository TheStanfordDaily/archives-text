# Campus Football Traffic Problem Is Topic Of Meet
## 
### 
Eugene Biscailuz, superintendent of
the California State Highway Patrol,
will attend the meeting of University
officials and traffic experts to be held
tomorrow noon at the Cardinal hotel
for the purpose of solving the Stan
ford traffic problems during big
games.
Final plans for traffic control during
the California game will be made at
the meeting tomorrow. In the group
are Charles Reed, state inspector of
traffic of San Francisco; Captain Jack
Pachco of the Santa Clara county
traffic squad; A 1 Masters, general
manager of the 8.A.C.; Orville Tuck
er, B.A.C. engineer; Chief of Police
Zink of Palo Alto; and A. E. Roth,
comptroller.
