# SECOND LECTURE ON 'MUSIC' TO BE GIVEN BY WARREN D. ALLEN
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will give the second lecture in his
series on Elements of Music, entitled,
"Melodies and Instruments," Wednes
day evening, November 13, in the
Little Theatre at 7:30 o'clock.
Allen will review briefly the sub
ject-matter of the first lecture on
"Melody" and take up the subject of
instruments, their evolution, peculiar
character, and their effect on musical
art. The various families of instru
ments will be described, with empha
sis on the character and the types of
melody best suited to each. His points
will be illustrated by selections on
the various musical instruments.
