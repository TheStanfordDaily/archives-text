# Parachute Jumping Exhibit To Be Shown Here Sunday
## 
### 
A demonstration in the art of para
chute jumping will be given by Leo
F. Root, flying instructor at the Palo
Alto School of Aviation, at the local
airport at 1:30 o'clock Sunday after
noon.
Root, who was test pilot for the
MacClatchie Manufacturing Company,
airplane motor builders, until two
months ago, and instructor in flying
and parachute-jumping at the Eagle
Airport in Los Angeles before that,
will jump from an altitude of 2000
feet, opening his chute after falling
clear of the ship. Before his jump,
Root will give a demonstration in the
use and operation of a parachute, on
the ground, to all who are interested.
