# Stanford To Hold Special Armistice Day Celebration
## 
### 
Stanford's Armistice Day service
will be celebrated in the Memorial
Chuch at 11:45 o'clock, Monday morn
ing. The program will begin with
Guilmant's "Funeral March," played
by Warren D. Allen. Colonel E. R. W.
McCabe will read a passage from
Scripture which will be followed by a
hymn, "The Supreme Sacrifice." The
commemoration will close with two
minutes of silent prayer at twelve
o'clock and the benediction.
All classes will be dismissed at
11:40 o'clock.
