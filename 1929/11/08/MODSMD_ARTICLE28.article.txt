# Encina Smoker To Aid In Organization For Bonfire
## 
### 
Lysle Bardo's popular Berkeley
orchestra will furnish entertainment
at the Encina Club smoker Wednesday
evening, November 13. Other features
of the program have not been defi
nitely arranged, hut Ray Force, chair
man, announced yesterday that the
smoker will serve in organizing co
operation for the erection of the Big
Game bonfire.
