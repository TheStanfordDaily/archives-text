# GOODWILL RAIDS
## 
### 
Apropos of the exaggerated spirit of goodwill and courtesy iti inter
collegiate rivalry conies the thought of its furthered application to the field
of campus raiding.
Although this very droll practice has almost died out, the Daily sees
an excellent reason why it should be continued. Think of the wonderful
opportunity it could afford the rival universities for showing goodwill!
The Rally Committee, with red caps put aside for proper Tuxedos,
could welcome the visitors from a rival institution and escort them with
honor to the Bonfire. If, peradventure, the guests chose to ignite it, Stan
ford boy-minds could express their appreciation and goodwill by retali
ating in a most original manner. There is a large letter on a hill that could
be painted red!
Imagine how pleased the rival campus will be at the exchange cour
tesy. Really, this old idea of daring and jocose sprees on each others'
campuses seems to be an opportunity too good to be missed!
