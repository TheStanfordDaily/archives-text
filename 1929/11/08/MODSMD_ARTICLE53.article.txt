# Phi Lambda Upsilon Holds Initial Smoker Wednesday
## 
### 
Phi Lambda Upsilon, honorary
chemical society, wlil hold its first
smoker Wednesday, November 13, at
7:30 o'clock in the Old Union, an
nounced H. W. Leverenz, chairman.
Professor Emeritus E. C. Franklin,
of the chemistry department, and Act
ing President Robert E. Swain will
speak. Franklin has just returned
from South Africa where he has been
on a tour of inspection for the British
government. In addition, a program of
music and vaudeville has been ar
ranged. Refreshments and smokes
will be served. All interested are in
vited to attend.
