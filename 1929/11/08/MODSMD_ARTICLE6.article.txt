# Annual Football Tea Will Be Given This Afternoon
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club will
give its annual football tea this after
noon in the Women's Clubhouse from
2 to 5:30 o'clock. Bridge will be
played. Proceeds from the affair will
go toward the club's scholarship fund.
The clubhouse will be decorated in
cardinal and white, and the football,
autographed by the members of the
Stanford team, to be given away as
a door prize, will further the football
motif.
Hostesses in the receiving line will
be Mrs. Robert E. Swain, Mrs. John
C. Branner, Mrs. Glenn S. Warner, Mrs.
Thomas A. Storey, and Mrs. John E.
McDowell.
Active members of Cap and Gown
will sell candy and stuffed dates, made
by the alumnae, for the benefit of the
Cap and Gown scholarship fund.
