# First W.A.A. Hike Of Year To Take Place On Sunday
## 
### 
The W.A.A. Board will sponsor the
first hike of this quarter on Sunday
morning at 9:30 o'clock, according to
an announcement made by Catherine
Cartlidge, recently elected hiking
manager.
All women registered in the Uni
versity are invited to participate in
the hike which will have as its des
tination the Frenchman's Tower,
familiar landmark on the Page Mill
Road. Hikers are asked to meet in
the lobby of Roble Hall on Sunday
morning.
Points toward a block letter may be
gained through hikes sponsored by
the W.A.A. Each mile counts as one
point and nothing less than a five
mile hike can be taken.
