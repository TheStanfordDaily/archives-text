# American Church Council President To Preach Here
## 
### 
Bishop Francis J. McConnell, Presi
dent of the Federal Council of
Churches in America, will speak at
the 11 o'clock service at the Stanford
Chapel Sunday morning. Bishop Mc-
Connell is from New York City and
is one of the leading modern thinkers
in Protestantism.
Anthems prepared by Warren D.
Allen, University organist, are "Cast
Thy Burdens on the Lord" from Men
delssohn's "Elijah" and "In Heavenly
Love Abiding" with Mrs. Kathleen
Wrenn as soprano soloist.
At 4 p.m. Warren D. Allen will give
his regular Sunday organ recital.
