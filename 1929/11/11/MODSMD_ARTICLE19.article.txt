# Women's Team Chosen To Debate U.C. On Marriage
## 
### 
To debate the question of marriage,
a success or a failure, Barbara Bailey,
Marie Baker, Esther Kelly, and Jean
Magnus were selected Saturday to
compose the women's team which will
engage in a forensic encounter with
the women's debating society of the
University of California during the
latter part of this month.
Anne Hightower, Mary Catherine
Morris, Adele McMannus, and Isabell
Warren will compose the regular in
tercollegiate team which is scheduled
to meet to meet the University of
California women formally on Decem
ber 2 and 3. The question for this de
bate is to be, "Resolved, That the
breakdown of conventions Is detri
mental to the youth of today."
