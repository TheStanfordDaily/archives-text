# MIRRIELEES TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Dr. Edith R. Mirrielees, associate
professor of English, will speak at the
Women's City Club in San Francisco
tomorrow at 11 o'clock. Dr. Mirrielees
has just returned from Bread Loaf,
Vermont, where she conducted a
course on the short story in the fa
mous school of that place. Her sub
ject Tuesday will be "The Short
Story."
