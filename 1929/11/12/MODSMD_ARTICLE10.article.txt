# Arthur Whomes, '30, Died At San Marino Saturday
## 
### 
C. Arthur Whomes, '30, died Satur
day morning at his home in San
Marino, California, as the result of an
operation performed last Monday in
Los Angeles.
Whomes, who was a transfer in
Economics from Occidental and was
in his first quarter at Stanford, was
suddenly taken ill while on a trip
home last week, and the operation
was performed immediately.
