# FROSH HOLD REVOTE FOR CLASS OFFICERS FROM 8 TO 2 TODAY
## 
### 
Final vote will be taken today to
decide three freshman class offices.
The poll is located at its usual place
on the Quad near the law steps and
will be open from 8 until 2 o'clock.
Robert M. Clarke, chairman of the
election board, urges freshmen to fol
low voting instructions carefully and
to mark ballots with ink.
Roberta Tempest received a major
ity last week to win the vice-presi
dency on the first ballot. Candidates
for president are Homer Boushey and
Jillson Peattie; for secretary, Ray
mond Hanson and Mary-Katherine
Moris; for treasurer, John Allen and
Bob Chickering.
