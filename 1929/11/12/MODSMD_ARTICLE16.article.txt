# Sophomore Women Defeat Juniors In Hockey Contest
## 
### 
The sophomore women defeated
the junior women's hockey team yes
terday afternoon in a game that com
pleted the first half of the practice
season. The sophs won by a score of
7 to 0.
The junior defense failed to hold
during the major part of the game.
Sue Betty Dunlap and Edith Badger
of the third-year women's squad were
outstanding. The sophs as a whole
scored five of the seven points. Sandy
Leib and Jacqueline Bugge chalked up
the other two. Three of the seven
goals were made during the first half.
The frosli-senior game will be held
tomorrow afternoon.
