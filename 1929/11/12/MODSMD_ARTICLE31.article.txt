# Freshman Debate Tryouts Held In Encina Yesterday
## 
### 
More than twenty freshmen at
tended the debate tryouts which were
held in the Encina Lobby yesterday
evening. A number of proposed de
bate subjects were taken under con
sideration in order that material
might be gathered upon them soon.
Leon Carley, freshman debate coach,
announced that no debates would be
scheduled until early next quarter.
It was decided that another meet
ing of the squad would be held next
Monday at 7 p.m. in Room 201 of the
English Building.
