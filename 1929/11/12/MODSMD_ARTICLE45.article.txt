# Stanford Y.W. Secretary Improving After Accident
## 
### 
The condition of Miss Mary Custus
Foster, secretary of the Stanford
Y.W.C.A., who received injuries to her
leg and knee in an automobile acci
dent Saturday morning, is improving,
according to statements made yester
day by attendants at the Palo Alto
Hospital. Her injuries were sustained
when, as she was passing through
Mountain View, a large touring car
skidded on the road and collided with
the roadster in which she was riding.
Miss Foster, with Miss Lily Sher
man, of the University of California
Y.W.C.A., was on her way to Carmel
when the accident occurred.
