# Anniversary Of Armistice Commemorated At Chapel
## 
### 
Stanford observed the eleventh an
niversary of the signing of the Armis
tice yesterday morning at 11:45 with
a short service in the Memorial
Church.
Guilmant's "Funeral March," played
by Warren D. Allen on the organ, be
gan the program. This was followed
by the reading of a Scripture passage
by Colonel E. R. W. McCabe and the
singing of a hymn, "The Supreme
Sacrifice."
The service ended with three min
utes of silent prayer, observed in com
mon with those throughout the nation
who were commemorating Armistice
Day. Acting Chaplain Reed B. Cher
ington gave the benediction.
