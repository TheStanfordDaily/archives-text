# Stanford Orchestra Will Become Student Activity
## 
### 
The Stanford University orchestra,
which has previously been carried on
as a regular class for which credit
has been given, has been organized
this year simply as a student activity,
according to Warren D. Allen, head
of the musical department.
Louis Weichselfelder of San Jose is
the student director of the organiza
tion and Donald Pratt is managor.
Anyone who is interested in playing
in the orchestra should get in touch
with Donald Pratt in Room 407 Se
quoia as soon as possible.
