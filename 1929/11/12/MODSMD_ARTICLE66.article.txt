# Warren Allen To Feature Mozart At Recital Today
## 
### 
Larghetto from the Clarinet Quintet
by Mozart will be featured by Warren
D. Allen, University organist, In the
regular Tuesday organ recital at 4:15
in the Chapel.
The program is as follows:
Chant for Dead Heroes
Harvey B. Gaul
A Prayer for Peace Paul Held
Larghetto from the Clarinet
Quintet Mozart
Symphony IV C. M. Widor
Andante cantabile —Scherzo —Finale
