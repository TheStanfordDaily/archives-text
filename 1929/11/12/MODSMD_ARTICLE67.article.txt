# Oriental Religion, Magis, To Be English Club Topic
## 
### 
"Voodooism and Magic" is to be the
subject of Mrs. Jolin Fletcher's talk
before the members of the English
Club and their guests tomorrowy eve
ning at. 7:30 o'clock. The meeting will
be held on the first floor of the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Mrs. Fletcher is a
magazine writer and was last year
sponsor of several lecture tours.
Chester Blackman will play a num
ber of piano solos, several of the
compositions by modern composers.
