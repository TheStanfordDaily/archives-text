# SAN FRANCISCO MEN WILL BE GUESTS OF STANFORD ENGINEERS
## 
### 
More than thirty engineers from the
San Francisco section of the Ameri
can Institute of Mining Engineering
will come to the campus tonight to
meet with the Stanford division of the
Geology and Mining Society of Ameri
can Universities, according to Alden
B. Greniger, president of the Stanford
organization, who will welcome the
guests.
A banquet will be held in the Union
at 6:30, and at 7:30 the meeting will
take place. Dean Hoover of the En
gineering Department, C. F. Tolman,
professor of economic geology, and
Welton J. Crook, professor of metal
lurgy, will speak.
