THE SOCIAL LIFE OF SAN FRANCISCO CENTERS AT THE
Famous Hotel St. Francis
"Embassy Room "
Announcement Extraordinary!
Overshadowing all entertainment
events of the season
THE INCOMPARABLE LAUGHNER-HARRIS
AND THEIR MUSICAL CLOWNS
Direct from a Triumphant and Successful Season in Los Angeles,
will open an extended engagement at the Hotel St. Francis
"Embassy Room" on Friday Evening, November 15.
GALA OPENING NIGHT PARTY
Welcoming Laughner-Harris and their Entertainers
Friday Night, November 15.
Dinner De Luxe $2.50 per person—Couvert Included
For Reservations Phone the Maltre 'Hotel —DOuglas 1000
