# Announce New Additions To Big Game Football Show
## 
### 
Additions have been made for new
skits for the Football Gaieties, ac
cording to Harold Helvenston, acting
director of dramatics.
Marshall Kirby, who was author of,
and chief actor in last year's hit,
"The Mauve Moth," will be seen in an
act involving more people but again
starring the author. Jean Jamison will
be heard in a musical act, and a wo
men's vocal trio will be announced
this week. Phil Brooks will do a spe
cialty act, the character of which has
not been revealed.
Among the skits which have been
accepted by Ram's Head Society for
the 1929 show is a satire of local in
terest by Nels Carter and Joe Thomp
son.
