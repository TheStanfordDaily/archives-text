# Stanford Graduate Will Speak At S.F. Auditorium
## 
### 
Dr. Wilfred Osgood, '99, will tell
the story of his 2000-mile caravan
journey through Abyssinia Friday
night in the Scottish Rite Auditorium,
San Francisco, illustrating his talk
with several reels of motion pictures
showing the characteristics of the
country and its inhabitants.
While at Stanford, Osgood was in
spired by David Starr Jordan to be
come a zoologist and naturalist, and
started his scientific career which has
taken him all over the world. At the
present time he is curator in charge
of the department of zoology of the
Field Museum in Chicago.
