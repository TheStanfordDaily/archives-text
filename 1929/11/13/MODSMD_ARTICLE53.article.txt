# Cables Will Keep Autos Off Plants At Big Game
## 
### 
Cables to prevent automobiles from
running over the plants along Palm
Drive will be installed by the corpora
tion yard next week prior to the "Big
Game."
Iron poles, on which cables will be
stretched, are to be mounted in per
manent sockets. The cables will be
put up during all important games in
the future.
Approximately 3200 damage was
done to the plants during the U.S.C.
game traffic congestion.
