# English Club Is To Hear Talk On Magic At Meeting
## 
### 
"Voodooism and Magic" will be the
subject of a talk to be given by Mrs.
John Fletcher before members of the
English Club and their guests at a
meeting on the first floor of the Wo
men's Clubhouse this evening at 8
o'clock. Mrs. Fletcher is a writer of
magazine articles and she was the
sponsor of several lecture tours last
year.
Chester Blackman will play several
piano solos composed by modern
writers.
