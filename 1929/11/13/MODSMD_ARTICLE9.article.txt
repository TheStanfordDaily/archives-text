# Four Sororities Achieve Goal In Red Cross Drive
## 
### 
Four sororities have achieved 100
per cent membership so far in the
annual Red Cross drive on the cam
pus, according to Sarah Grant, wo
men's chairman. These houses are
Delta Gamma, Alpha Omicron Pi,
Gamma Phi Beta, and Pi Beta Phi.
Other living groups and Roble Hall
have registered approximately fifty
per cent of their members on the Roll
Call lists.
