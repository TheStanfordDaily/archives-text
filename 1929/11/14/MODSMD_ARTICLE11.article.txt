# Sports Writers To Vote On Football Championship
## 
### 
A jury of 250 sporting writers and
editors will select a 1929 football
championship team, it was announced
yesterday. A consensus of opinion
among these representatives will de
cide the leader, according to definite
standards, of American gridiron play
ing.
W. O. McGeehan, of the "New York
Herald-Tribune" and formerly a San
Francisco sports writer, will be chair
man of the Albert Ilussell Erskine
Award, as the honors for football
glory will be called. The preliminary
ballot will be taken on November 30,
seven days after the Big Game, the
final ballot following late in Decem
ber. Six hundred colleges will be con
sidered.
