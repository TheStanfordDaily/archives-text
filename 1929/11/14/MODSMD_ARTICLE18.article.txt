# William Maguire, '29, To Be Married in S.F. Today
## 
### 
Elizabeth Cutten of San Francisco
and William Maguire, '29, will be
married this afternoon at 4 o'clock at
the Cutten home in the city. The
bride-elect is a graduate of Miss
Burke's School and attended eastern
finishing schools. Maguire, a member
of Phi Kappa Psi, has chosen his fra
ternity brother, Guido Caglieri, '31, to
be best man.
