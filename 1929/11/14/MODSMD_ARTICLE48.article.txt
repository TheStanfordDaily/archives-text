# Cashier's Office Is Location Of Lost, Found Department
## 
### 
A Lost and Found department is being
operated, for the convenience of students,
at the cashier's window in the Adminis
tration Building. All lost articles should
be reported and all found articles sent in
to this office.
At present there are several pairs of
glasses and various other articles which
have not been claimed, and a number of
lost articles reported which have not been
turned in. Students are urged to make
more use of this department.
