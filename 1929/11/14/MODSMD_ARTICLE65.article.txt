# Sonoma County Teachers Hear Education Professor
## 
### 
Professor John C. Almack of the
education department returned yester
day from Santa Rosa, where he ad
dressed the Sonoma County Teachers
Association on the question of the im
provement of high-school teaching.
There were about five hundred
teachers present at the meeting,
among whom there were several Stan
ford graduates who are now teaching
school in Sonoma County.
