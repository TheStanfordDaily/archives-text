# Owens Called To Decide Upon Bronco Eligibility
## 
### 
Professor William Brownlee Owens,
professor of law, and president of the
Pacific Coast Intercollegiate Athletic
Conference, as well as the representa
tive of the Stanford faculty on the
executive board of the sports associa
tion, was called upon at the last min
ute Saturday before the St. Mary's-
Santa Clara football game to decide
the eligibility of "Al" Terremere,
Bronco fullback, it became known yes
terday. Although officials of St.
Mary's admitted filing the protest,
rumors of strained relations between
the two schools were denied as false.
