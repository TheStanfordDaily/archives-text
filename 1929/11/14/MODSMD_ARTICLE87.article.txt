THURBDAY
1:45 p.m.— Meeting of the Board of
Trustees.
3:00 p.m.—Oral Examination for the
Doctorate, Ira J. Condit (Major
Botany; Minor Biology), Room 308,
Library.
4:00 p.m.—Neophytes of Geology and
Mining Society meet In room 320
of the Geology Building.
4:80 to 5:30 —Stanford Fellowship tea
in the Women's Clubhouse, third
floor.
4:15 p.m.—Vesper recital in Memorial
Church. Speaker: Rev. George H.
Whisler.
7:00 p.m.—Regular rehearsal of Stan
ford University Orchestra.
T :15 p.m.—Ushers for the French mo
tion picture, "The Passion of Joan
of Arc," will meet in the Assembly
Hall.
7:30 p.m.—The Radio Club will meet
this evening in room 2G3 of the
Civil Engineering Building.
7:30 p.m.—Regular meeting of the
Christian Science Society on the
second floor of the Women's Club
house.
8:00 p.m.—French movie film, "The
Passion of Joan of Arc," presented
by Dramatic Council in Assembly
Hall. All seats reserved at 75 cents.
Tickets at Sherman, Clay & Co.,
and Old Union. Student sale 1 p.m.,
Nov. 11, in front of Women's Club
house.
