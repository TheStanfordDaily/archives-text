# "Professional Discipline" Subject Of Law Lecture
## 
### 
"Professional Discipline" will be
the subject of a lecture to be given
by O. K. Cushing, San Francisco at
torney and former member of the
Board of Governors of the California
Bar, on November 17 at 7:15 p.m. in
room 12 of the Law building.
The talk will deal with the ethics
of the legal profession. It will be of
interest primarily to students taking
Law 254, but the public will be wel
come.
