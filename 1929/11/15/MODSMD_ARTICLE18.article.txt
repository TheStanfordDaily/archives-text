# Toyon's Informal Tonight To Use Dutch Decorations
## 
### 
Dutch atmosphere will be carried out
in the decorations for the Toyon in
formal tonight at 9 o'clock. Tulips,
steins, a dike, wooden shoes will be
utilized. Entrance to the hall will be
through a windmill. Costumes of Hol
land will be worn by the orchestra.
Bob Van Etten is chairman of the
dance committee.
