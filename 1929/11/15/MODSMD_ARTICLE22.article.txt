# Fellowship Meeting Will Be Held At Swain's Home
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford fellowship
and other students interested, will
visit the home of Dr. Robert E. Swain
Sunday evening at 8 o'clock. This is
the second meeting of its kind held by
the fellowship this year. Visits to the
homes of faculty members are planned
twice a month to afford the instruc
tors and students of Stanford an in
formal contact with one another.
Those interested in attending Sun
day evening are asked to see Fred
Carter at the Zeta Psi house.
