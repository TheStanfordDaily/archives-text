# JOURNALIST SOCIETY OFFICER LEAVES FOR MISSOURI CONVENTION
## 
### 
Kenneth Mansfield, '30, left last night
for Columbia, Missouri, where he will
attend the fifteenth national convention
of Sigma Delta Chi, national professional
journalistic fraternity. Official headquar
ters are at the University of Missouri.
Mansfield, a night editor on the Stan
ford Daily, is vice-president of the Stan
ford chapter of Sigma Delta Chi. The
convention will be held from Monday,
November 18, to Wednesday, November
20.
