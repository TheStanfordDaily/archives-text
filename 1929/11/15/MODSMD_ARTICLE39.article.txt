# Publicity Sheet For N.S.F.A. Meet Now In Preparation
## 
### 
A bulletin advertising the National
Students Federation of America con
vention to be held at Stanford Janu
ary 1-4, is now being prepared under
the direction of Leonard Bartlett,
chairman of publicity for the confer
ence.
It will be a four-page pamphlet con
taining pictures of Stanford and tell
ing various phases of Stanford's life,
to be written by Burnell Gould, Tem
pleton Peck, and Sylva Weaver. The
publication will be sent out Dec. 4
to 533 college leaders throughout the
United States.
