# Stanford Engineers Hold Joint Meet With S.F. Men
## 
### 
The Stanford section of the Geo
logical and Mining Engineers of
American Universities and the San
Francisco section of the In
stitute of Mining and Metallurgical
Engineers held a joint meeting at
Stanford this week.
The speakers included Theodore J.
Hoover, Dean of the School of Engi
neering, C. P. Tolman, Professor of
Economic Geology, and Weltoa J.
Crook, Professor of Metallurgy.
A feature of the meeting was a trip
of inspection through the mining and
metallurgical laboratories.
