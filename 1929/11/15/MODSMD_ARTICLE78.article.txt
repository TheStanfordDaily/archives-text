# Sophomore Women Elect Snyder As Cabinet Head
## 
### 
Jane Snyder was elected chairman of
the Sophomore Cabinet at the first meet
ing of the group. Beatrice dyne was
elected secretary-treasurer.
Organization of sophomore women is
to be handled through the cabinet, which
is composed of a representative from
each sorority house, three, from Roble,
and one from the women living off
campus. Arrangements were made to
have the cabinet meet every other Tues
day night at 7 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
