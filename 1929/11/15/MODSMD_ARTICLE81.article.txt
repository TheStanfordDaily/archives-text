# Miller, Field Engagement Announced Monday Evening
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Sally Mil
ler, '29, to Dr. John Field 11, assistant
professor of physiology, was an
nounced Monday night at Cap and
Gown. Miss Miller, who has been
prominent in campus activities, is
now doing graduate work in the physi
ology department. Dr. Field was also
graduated from Stanford and received
his advanced degrees here.
