# Toyon Club To Stage Open House Dance Big Game Nite
## 
### 
Immediately following the Big
Gume, Toyon Club will hold a 5:30 to
7:00 open house dance in the Toyon
lobby, according to Bob Freeman,
chairman of the committee in charge
of the dance. Everyone is invited.
"The dance will furnish a good
place to Bpend the time until the Big
Game traffic has cleared up," said
Freeman.
