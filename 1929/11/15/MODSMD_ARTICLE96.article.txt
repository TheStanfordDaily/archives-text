# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
SUNDAY
6:00 p.m.—Meeting of tho Stanford
Npiscopul Club at the Parish House,
corner Wavorloy street and Hamil
ton avenue. Col. McCabe will speak.
A buffet supper will be served.
TUESDAY
7:30 p.m.—Dr. Louis C. Cornish will
Apeak on "Treatment of Minorities
in the Balkans" In the Assembly
Hall.
MISCELLANEOUS
CANDIDATES FOR DEGREE OF
BACHELOR OF ARTS at the close
of the present quarter should file
application at the Registrar's Office
as Boon as possible. Application
blanks may be obtained at the
Registrar's Office.
THE ENTOMOLOGY CLUB, being
not formally organized, has an
nounced that more students are not
being considered for membership
at this time.
