# Stanford Press To Honor White, Sequoia Park Head
## 
### 
In honor of Colonel John R. White,
Superintendent of Sequoia National
Park, the Stanford University Press
will give its first author's tea next
Monday evening at 7:30 o'clock in
the Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
Motion pictures of a buffalo roundup
held in Yellowstone National Park on
the occasion of Dr. Ray Lyman Wil
bur's visit to the Park last September
will be shown.
Colonel White is the co-author, with
Judge Walter Fry, of "Big Trees,"
manuscript for which was accept 1
by the Press this fall for publication
in the spring.
