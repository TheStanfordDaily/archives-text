# Stanford Student Injured In Alum Rock Auto Mishap
## 
### 
Palo Alto Hospital officials reported
last night that Frank B. Lee, Stanford
'30, fractured his leg when the car he was
driving went over a bank last Saturday
night near Alum Rock Park outside of
San Jose. A badly cut lip and a severe
case of poison oak were other injuries
sustained by Lee. X-rays are being taken
to ascertain the exact nature of the frac
ture, but had not been completed yester
day.
Crystallization of the spindle on the
car he was driving caused the accident in
which Lee was the onyl person hurt.
