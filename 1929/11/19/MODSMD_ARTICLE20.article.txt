# STANFORD RED CROSS CONTRIBUTIONS LEAD ALL LOCAL DISTRICTS
## 
### 
Stanford to date leads all other dis
tricts in this locality In the Red Cross
Drive with a total of 914 member
ships, and has increased its own rec
ord 400 per cent over that of last
year, according to George Bodle,
chairman of the University drive.
Thirteen houses have so far turned
in 100 per cent membership: Alpha
Omicron Pi, Alpha Phi, Kappa Alpha
Theta, Gamma Phi Beta, Delta
Gamma, Kappa Kappa Gamma, PI
Beta Phi, Delta Delta Delta, Delta
Kappa Epsilon, Phi Gamma Delta,
Phi Delta Theta, Theta XI, Theta
Delta Chi.
Roble Hall has reported $163.75, the
Union dormitories $51.00, Branner
$28.50, Toyon $20.00, Encina incom
plete $163.75, Sequoia returns also in
complete.
