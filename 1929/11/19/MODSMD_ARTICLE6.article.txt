# Mather Requests Owners To Claim Concert Tickets
## 
### 
Approximately 250 paid season tick
ets for the A.S.S.U. Concert Series
remain unclaimed at the offices in
the Old Union, according to Carl
Mather, manager of the series. Mather
requests that tickets be called for im
mediately. They may be obtained
every morning between 8:30 and 12
and every afternoon from 1 to 5.
The first concert of the series will
be December 4. Ignaz Friedman, Pol
ish pianist, will play with the San
Fjancisco Symphony Orchestra.
