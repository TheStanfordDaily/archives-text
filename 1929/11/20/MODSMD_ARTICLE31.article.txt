# Wilbur Misses Hutchins' Installation Ceremonies
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, secretary
of the Interior and president of Stan
ford, was unable to attend the instal
lation ceremonies of Robert Maynard
Hutchins as president of the Univer
sity of Chicago yesterday because cf
Secretary of War James W. Good's
death. Upon Dr. Wilbur was to be
conferred the honorary degree of doc
tor of laws, and a prepared speech
that he was to deliver was handed in
manuscript to the vice-president of
the university.
