# Y.M.C.A. Plans Excursion To Roth's Mountain Camp
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth will again be host
in his Santa Cruz cabin when the
Y.M.C.A. repeats its recent trip to his
mountain home the week-end of De
cember 7. Plans for the outing having
been completed, registration for the
trip is now open, and all students in
terested should sign up at the
Y.M.C.A. office as soon as possible.
The trip is open to all. Costs will be
approximately $1.50.
