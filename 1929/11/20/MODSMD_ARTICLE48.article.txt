# Columbia Awards Willis Honorary Doctor's Degree
## 
### 
Professor Bailey Willis, professor
emeritus and former executive head of
the Stanford Department of Geology,
was recently awarded the honorary de
gree of Doctor of Science by Columbia
University. The award was made at the
celebration of the one hundred seventy
fifth anniversary of the founding of the
institution.
Professor Willis succeeded Dr. Bran
ner as head of the department, when the
latter became President of the Univer
sity, in 1915. He became professor emeri
tus in 1922. Since then he has been active
in geological research in various parts of
the earth, and has recently returned
from central Africa, where he has been
studying the action of earthquakes.
