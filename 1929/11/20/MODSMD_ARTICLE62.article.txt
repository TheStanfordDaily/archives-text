# Reorganization Of Club On Foreign Relations Planned
## 
### 
Reorganization of the International
Relations Club, sponsored by the Car
negie Foundation for International
Peace, is to be undertaken shortly
for the college year, with a program
consisting of informal meetings and
addresses by well-known speakers.
All persons interested in interna
tional affairs are eligible for member
ship and are requested to sign today
or tomorrow on the list posted on the
Political Science bulletin board. No
tices of the meetings will be sent to
them.
