# COPIES OF SPECIAL DAILY EDITION WILL BE SOLD TOMORROW
## 
### 
Students may secure extra copies
of the Big Game edition of the Daily
tomorrow at the business office of the
paper, for ten cents each. The special
edition will be published tomorrow,
and will contain 16 pages, with a four
page pictorial section.
Postage will be prepaid on copies
to any address, if the addresses and
payments are left at the Daily office
today. Postage will be included in the
special price of 10 cents a copy.
