# DEATH OF HUBERT CHILDS
## 
### 
News was received yesterday of
the death of Dr. Hubert G. Childs, of
Bloomington, Indiana. Childs received
his Master's Degree in Education at
Stanford in 1911, having received his
Bachelor's Degree from the Univer
sity of Michigan in 1897. For some
time he had been a professor in the
University of Indiana.
