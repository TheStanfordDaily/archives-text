# Judge Withholds Decision Upon Case In Moot Court
## 
### 
Decision on the moot court case
of Parker and Badger vs. Cathcart
and Holbrook, tried last night tn
room 12, was withheld by the acting
judge, Professor Sigvald Nielson, un
til 7 o'clock tonight. Professor Niel
son explained that in consideration of
the amount of work expended by both
teams he did not feel able to grant
a hasty decision. The case concerned
the question of the validity of a
"spendthrift" trust.
