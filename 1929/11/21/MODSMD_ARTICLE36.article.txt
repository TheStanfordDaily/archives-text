# Eight Remain In Tryoutee Period For Concert Series
## 
### 
At the present time there are still
eight tryoutees competing for the
managership of the 1930-31 A.S.S.U.
Concert Series. These are Jerome
Cramer, Dave Edwards, Charles Ham
man, Carl McDowell, Albert Makle,
Antoinette Schulte, Walter Radius,
and Barbara Beach Thompson.
Selection of Concert Manager,
House Manager, and Office Manager
for next year will be made from this
group based primarily upon the hours
of work that each puts in and the
amount of tickets that each sells. The
appointments will be made next
March after the final concert of this
year's series.
