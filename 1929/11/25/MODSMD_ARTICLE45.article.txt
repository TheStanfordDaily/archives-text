# Encina Hall Men To Make Room Deposits Next Week
## 
###

rooms for winter quarter should call
at the Dean of Men's office Monday,
Tuesday, or Wednesday, December 2,
3, and 4, to pay deposits, announces
Dean of Men George B. Culver.
