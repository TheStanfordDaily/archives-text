# STAMP THIEF SENTENCED
## 
### 
George L. Harper, charged with the
theft of $375 worth of stamps from
the Stanford Food Research Institute
recently, pleaded guilty Friday morn
ing when arraigned in Palo Alto court
before Superior Judge J. R. Welch,
who sentenced him to spend three
months in jail as well as paying a $250
fine. Part of the loot has been recov
ered.
