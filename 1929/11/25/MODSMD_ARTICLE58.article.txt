# Revised A.W.S. Constitution Approved By Dean Of Law
## 
### 
Dean M. R. Kirkwood of the Law
School is returning the draft of the
revised Associated Women's Students
constitution to the Constitutional Com
mittee today, after having approved
the legal validity of the changes.
The draft will be presented to Wo
men's Council tomorrow night for ap
proval, and will be read before all
women's living groups next Monday.
It will be voted on Tuesday, Decem
ber 3, at an A.W.S. assembly.
