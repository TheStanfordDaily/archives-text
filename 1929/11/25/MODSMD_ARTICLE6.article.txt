# Airplanes Carry 150 Fans To "Big Game" Saturday
## 
### 
California airways carried approxi
mately 150 football fans to the annual
Stanford-California football classic
Saturday, according to reports re
leased at the Palo Alto School of Avia
tion yesterday.
Maddux, Western Air Express, and
Overland Airways accounted for most
of this number, the last mentioned
carrying on an airplane taxi service
between the local port and Mills Field
in San Francisco. Several tri-motored
planes flew up from southern Califor
nia. Thirty planes landed at the local
improvised field. No accidents were
reported.
