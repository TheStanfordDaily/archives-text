# Allen Features Program Of Popular Music Today
## 
### 
With a program consisting mainly of
popular numbers, Warren D. Allen, Uni
versity organist, will play today at 4:15
o'clock in the Chapel. This will be the
only recital this week, Thursday and Sun
day services being omitted because of
Thanksgiving vacation.
The program is as follows:
Largo from the "New World Sym
phony" Dvorak
In Autumn MacDowtll
Told at Sunset MacDozvcll
Autumn Song Gretchamnoff
Marche de Rogations ... Eugene Gigout
Pilgrim's Chorus (from "Tann
hauser") Wagner
