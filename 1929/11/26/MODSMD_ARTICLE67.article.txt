# Fellowship Board Meeting This Afternoon After Tea
## 
### 
Members of the Fellowship Board
will meet this afternoon at 5:30 on
the third floor of the Women's Club
house following the regular daily tea.
Hostesses for the week were chosen
by the Board at its last meeting. The
five women selected will serve one
day each week and will take complete
charge of the tea for the day. They
are: Monday, Elizabeth Jackson;
Tuesday, Sylva Weaver; Wednesday,
Joan Nourse; Thursday, Kate Pins
dorff; Friday, Maxine Cushing.
All students and members of the
faculty are invited to attend the daily
teas which are held every day from
4:30 to 5:30 o'clock in the Clubhouse.
