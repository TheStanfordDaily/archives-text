# Gordon Davis Will Direct Play For Everett Morton
## 
### 
Gordon Davis, Stanford Dramatic
Manager on leave, has been engaged
by Edward Everett Horton, prominent
theatrical producer of Los Angeles, to
direct his next play for one week.
Davis has been writing a play himself,
and as soon as this week's engage
ment has been completed he will con
tinue work on it.
The play to be directed is "Among
The Married," by Lawrence Vincent.;
it has recently had a successful run
on Broadway. The cast will include
Horton, Florence Eldridge, Mary As
ton, and others.
