# Sophomore Women's Group Will Meet, Plan Banquet
## 
### 
Members of the Sophomore Wo
men's Cabinet will meet tomorrow
night on the third floor of the Wo
men's Clubhouse at 7 o'clock to make
plans for a banquet and hockey rally,
according to Jane Snyder, chairman
of the cabinet.
"We want to have all the sopho
more women meet at a banquet in the
Old Union on Tuesday, December 3,"
said Miss Snyder. "The chief purpose
in having the dinner is to have a
rally for our sophomore hockey team
and to help foster the spirit of unity
which now exists in the class."
