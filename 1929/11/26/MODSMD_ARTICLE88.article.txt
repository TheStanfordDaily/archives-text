# Iowa Votes Road Program Of Thirty Million Dollars
## 
### 
DES MOINES, lowa, Nov. 25. (£>)—
A $30,000,000 road building program,
calling for paving 1,000 miles of high
way and grading 400 additional miles,
was lowa's response to the request
of President Hoover that states co
operate with the federal government
in a program of public improvement
as an aid to business conditions.
The program was outlined by Gov
ernor John Hammill in a telegram
sent to the President late today.
