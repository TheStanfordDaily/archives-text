# Freshman Chess Squad Is Reorganized, To Meet U.C.
## 
### 
Stanford's once-famous freshman
chess squad has again been organ
ized, according to Angelo May, acting
secretary of the Cardinal Chess Club.
The Cardinal team, defunct since
1926, will probably first combat the
strong Bear pawn-shovers.
Tex Allan, Keith Wilhelm, Russel
Nielson, Ed Fisher, Angelo May, Rob
ert "Gunboat" Smith, and Samuel
Clark will form the team.
There will be a meeting of the
Cardinal Chess Club tonight at 7:00
in the Y.M.C.A. lobby.
