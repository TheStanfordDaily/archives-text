# ENGAGEMENT ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The engagement of Lily Chloupek,
'30, and Pierre van Rysselberge was
announced last night at a Fouuders'
Day banquet at the Delta Delta Delta
house. Van Rysselberge came here in
1927 as a C.R.B. Fellow from the Uni
versity of Brussels and took his doc
tor's degree here last year.
