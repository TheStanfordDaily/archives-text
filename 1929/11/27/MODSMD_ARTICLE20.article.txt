# Men's Debate Teams Will Travel On Road Next Week
## 
### 
Stanford debate teams will go on
the road next week, when they travel
to meet St. Mary's and Fresno State
College on Wednesday and Thursday,
respectively. To Maraga for the con
test with St. Mary's December 4 will
go Norman Tyre, Smith Stevens, and
Howard Conn. They will uphold the
negative of "Resolved, That modern
science tends to destroy theistic
faith," the question of last night's de
bate.
Glenn Rusk, Edwin Gerhardt, and
James Bednar will represent Stanford
against Fresno State December 5,
when they will argue on the affirma
tive of the same question.
