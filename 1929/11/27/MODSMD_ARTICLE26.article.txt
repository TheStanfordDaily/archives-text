# Blade Elected President Of Stanford Chess Club
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Cardinal Chess
Club held in the Y.M.C.A. lobby last
night, Ellis H. Blade was elected to
the office of president. John Dayton
was elected to the vice-presidency,
and Nielson defeated "Gunboat" Smith
for treasurer. Angelo May was the
sole candidate for secretary.
The members of the freshman
squad, now organized, will hold their
first meeting next Tuesday, although
extensive practice is now being car
ried on preparatory to the probable
contest with the California freshmen
this quarter.
