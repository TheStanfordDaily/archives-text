# Library Will Remain Open During Thanksgiving Period
## 
### 
Vacation hours will prevail at the
Stanford Library over Thanksgiving
to accommodate students remaining
on the campus and desiring to study
there.
The library closes at 6 o'clock to
night and will remain closed Thanks
giving Day. On Friday it will be open
from 8:30 to 4:30; Saturday, 8:30 to
12:30; Sunday, from 2 to 6 o'clock.
