# Date Is Set For Glee Club Concert At Bohemian Club
## 
### 
Definite announcement of Thurs
day, January 23, as the date set for
the Men's Glee Club to appear at the
1 Bohemian Club in San Francisco to
j present a concert was made yesterday
by Earl Reed, manager of the Glee
Club. Arrangements for the Stanford
club to sing at the Bohemian were
made several weeks ago, but no date
was settled upon until recently. This
will be the 'second time that the Glee
Club has appeared before the San
Francisco organization; it filled a
similar engagement last year, accord
nig to Reed.
