# ARRANGEMENTS MADE FOR HOCKEY BANQUET BY SOPHOMORE WOMEN
## 
### 
Final arrangements were completed
for a banquet and hockey rally to be
held on Wednesday, December 4, at
6:15 o'clock In the Old Union, accord
ing to Jane Snyder, chairman of the
Sophomore Cabinet.
Members of the cabinet, which met
last night, approved the plan of hav
ing the dinner as a hockey rally to
precede the Junior-Sophomore game.
"We want to keep up class unity,"
said Jane Snyder. "If possible we
would like to have every sophomore
woman present at the banquet."
Tickets for the dinner, which will
cost 75c, will be sold in the living
groups.
