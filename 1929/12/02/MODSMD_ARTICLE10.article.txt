# Hockey Schedule Change Is Announced By Manager
## 
### 
Women's interclass hockey games
during the remainder of the season
will be played on Wednesdays and
Thursdays, announces Elizabeth
Woodyatt, hockey manager. Games
have been held previously on Mon
days and Wednesdays.
The frosh-senior contest on Wednes
day will be the next regular inter
class competition to take place. Sec
ond-team games between the frosh
and sophomore teams are being car
ried on in addition to the regular
first-team combats.
