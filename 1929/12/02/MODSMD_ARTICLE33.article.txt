# Over 200 Delegates Will Attend N.S.F.A. Convention
## 
### 
More than two hundred delegates
from all parts of the country will con
vene at Stanford January 1 to 4 for
the fifth annual congress of the Na
tional Student Federation of Amer
ica. This year's convention will be
the first on the Pacific Coast and
representatives to the conference will
be guests of the A.S.S.U. Stan Weigle,
formed student body president and
present western regional representa
tive of the N.S.F.A., is in charge of
the conference.
