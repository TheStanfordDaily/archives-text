# Society Meets Tonight To Discuss Stamp Exhibition
## 
### 
Plans for a rare postage stamp ex
hibition to be held early in January
will be the principal topic of discus
sion at the regular bi-monthly meet
ing of the Palo Alto Philatelic Society
tonight at 7:30 o'clock in the Palo
Alto Chamber of Commerce Building,
540 Ramona St., Palo Alto, according
to George V. L, Haskell, president.
Recent stamp issues will also be dis
cussed.
