# Few Bids Left For Encina Club Informal December 7
## 
### 
Only a few bids are left for the
Encina Club informal December 7,
according to James Keesling, chair
man, over 100 having been sold by
Monday evening. Bids are $2.75, and
may be obtained at Room 232 Encina
or at the A.S.S.U. office. This infor
mal, which will be the last hall dance
of the quarter, is open to all members
of Encina, Toyon, Branner, and Se
quoia clubs.
