# Wilson And Adams Speak To Geology Journal Club
## 
### 
Robert R. Wilson and Bradford C.
Adams spoke before the Geology Jour
nal Club yesterday afternoon on the
subjects of "Geology of the Upper
Salinas Valley" and "Problems in
Petroleum Drilling." A second meeting
is slated for Thursday afternoon to re
place last week's regular meeting
which was postponed in order that
Dr. Robin Willis might deliver a series
of geological lectures.
