# Schuck And Mullen Heads Of Christmas Stamp Sale
## 
### 
Organization for the annual sale of
Tuberculosis Association Christmas
stamps on the Campus is being under
taken by Lillian Schuck, campus chair
man, and Stanley Mullen, men's chair
man. The drive will begin Thursday
and last a week, aiming for a goal of
100 stamps, at one cent apiece, for
each person, Schuck stated.
The proceeds of the drive go to the
Stanford Rest Homes, created for stu
dents in need of special rest and care.
The homes are used when hospital
care is unnecessary or when hospital
accommodations are not available.
