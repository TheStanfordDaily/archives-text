# ROBLE JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
A jolly-up will be given by the
freshman women at Roble, on Thurs
day evening from seven to eight. A
campus orchestra is to be engaged.
Emilie Dohrmann, Anne Adams, and
Mary Louise Bouelle, who are in
charge of arrangements, have an
nounced that the dance will not be
limited to freshmen.
