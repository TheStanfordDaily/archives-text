# Afflicted Leader of Glee Club Improves Slightly
## 
### 
Slight improvement is reported in
the condition of C. Albert Kulmann,
director of the Men's Glee Club, who
is seriously ill with an attack of in
flammatory rheumatism. Kulmann is
now in the Palo Alto base hospital,
having been taken there last Wednes
day. He has been ill for the past ten
days. Adelbert Culling, president of
the Glee Club, is leading the club in
Kulmann's absence
