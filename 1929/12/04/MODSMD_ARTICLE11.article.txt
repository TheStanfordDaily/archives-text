# Glee Club Will Rehearse Tonight For Next Recital
## 
### 
In preparation for its appearance in
the Memorial Church next Sunday,
the Men's Glee Club will rehearse in
the choir loft of the chapel tonight at
7 o'clock. For the past two weeks the
club has been practicing hymns to
be sung Sunday. This program will be
a continuation of the annual chapel
custom of the Glee Club.
