# CARD DEBATERS FACE SAINTS' TEAM TODAY IN FORENSIC BATTLE
## 
### 
A Stanford debating team composed
of Norman Tyre, Smith Stevens, and
Howard Conn, will meet St. Mary's
today on their home platform in a
debate on the question, "Resolved,
That modern science tends to destroy
theistic faith."
The St. Mary's team is headed by
Leonard Thomas, a senior and stu
dent body president of that institu
tion, as well as being winner of the
Cottle medal oratorical contest held
there. His colleagues will be John
Fitzgerald and Harold Dobbins.
Stanford representatives will be
guests of the St. Mary's team at a
dinner before the debate.
