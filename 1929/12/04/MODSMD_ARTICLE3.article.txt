# Fellowship Board Holds Last Meeting Of Quarter
## 
### 
The Stanford Fellowship Board at
its last meeting of the quarter yester
day afternoon in the Women's Club
house, decided to continue the regular
daily teas next week from 4:30 to
5:30, it was announced by officials.
Five women were chosen to take
charge of the teas on their respective
days: Monday, Elizabeth Jackson;
Tuesday, Sylva Weaver; Wednesday,
Joan Nourse; Thursday, Kate Pins
darff: Friday, Maxine Gushing.
