# Songs And Skit Feature Sophomore Hockey Dinner
## 
### 
Miss Helen Bunting, head of the
women's physical education depart
ment, and Miss Georgina Burk, sopho
more hockey coach, will be the guests
of honor at a sophomore class rally
banquet tonight at 6:15 o'clock in the
Old Union, according to Jane Snyder,
chairman of the class cabinet.
Four songs and a skit, written by
Catherine Cartlidge, have been
planned for entertainment. Betty
Scott and Jane Townley will act in
the skit.
Tickets for the dinner will be 75
cents and may be purchased just be
fore the dinner. Reservations may be
made through members of the indi
vidual living groups.
