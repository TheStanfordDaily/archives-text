# Collection Of Bookplates Shown In Library Exhibit
## 
### 
Bookplates of Maxiraillian I of Mex
ico, belonging to President Ray Ly
man Wilbur, and to many universities,
are on exhibit in the Stanford Library
as part of the collection of famous
bookplates.
One of Nikolai Mikailovich, 1859,
Grand Duke, and one of Sydney Smith
are especially featured. Among Stan
ford bookplates are those of the Stan
ford English Club, of Eliot Grinnell
Mears, and one of negro literature be
longing to Librarian Nathan van Pat
ten.
