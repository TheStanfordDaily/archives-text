# Lida Rittler Married To William Price Saturday
## 
### 
The marriage of Lida Rittler, '29,
to William Clay Price, also of the
class of '2!), took place at the Piret
Presbyterian Church in San Francisco
last Saturday afternoon. Rev. William
Kirk Guthrie performed the ceremony,
and only members of the immediate
family were present.
Mrs. Price is a member of Chi
Omega sorority, and Price belonged
to Phi Kappa Psi. Benton Sparks, Phi
Kappa Psi, was best, man at the cere
mony. The couple will live in San
Francisco after their return from their
wedding trip.
