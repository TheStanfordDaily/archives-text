# NEGRO DENIES SLIGHTS
## 
### 
WEST POINT, N.Y., Dec. 3. In
the first published interview permit
ted a West Point undergraduate in
more than 100 years, Alonzo Parham,
negro cadet, today spiked rumors that
he had been ostracized at the military
academy.
