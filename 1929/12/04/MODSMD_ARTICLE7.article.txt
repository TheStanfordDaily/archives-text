# Y.M.C.A. Convention Meets At Asilomar December 26
## 
### 
Stanford will send approximately
thirty-five men to the Y.M.C.A. con
vention to be held at Asilomar from
December 26 to January 2. The main
object of the Asilomar conference is
to bring together students of all the
western colleges and aid them in de
veloping better ways of living, at the
same time affording them a vacation
among recreational advantages. All
students interested in going should
enroll at the Y.M.C.A. office.
