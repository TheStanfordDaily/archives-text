Should University Fashions
Be set at a Clothing
Manufacturers' Conference•
Highgate Tailors believe they should not. ,
Working within the shadow of a great Univer
sity, catering to University men exclusively, High
gate Tailors«look to the University Man alone for
style inspiration. Thus, their fashion observers cover
the important campi of the nation, from Princeton
and Harvard to California and Stanford. . . . What
they see and learn you'll find reflected in Highgate
suits and topcoats. Naturally, you'll like them —
instantly!
Highgate Clothes are tailored from beautiful Uni
versity fabrics which are woven especially and
exclusively for Highgate Tailors. &
