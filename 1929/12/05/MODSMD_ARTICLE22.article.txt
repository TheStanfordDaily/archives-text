# Bryant Will Talk On War At Relations Club Tonight
## 
### 
Lieutenant Commander Stewart Fred
erick Bryant, U.S.N., will deliver a brief
address on "Limiting the Causes of War"
at the first meeting of the International
Relations Club at 7:15 tonight in the
West Memorial Library. The talk will be
based partially on Lt. Commander Bry
ant's experience in the Far East, where
he was intelligence officer of the fleet, and
on the Limitation of Armaments Con
ference in Washington, where he was also
on duty. He is at present doing special
graduate study at Stanford in interna
tional relations.
The address will be followed by an
open discussion. The club's relation to
the Carnegie Foundation for Internation
al Peace will be explained, and a chair
man and a secretary will be elected.
