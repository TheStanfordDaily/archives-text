# Varsity, Freshman Golfers' Green Fees At Menlo Due
## 
### 
All varsity and freshman golfers
who are to practice at the Menlo Golf
and Country Club should pay their
preen fees immediately, announces
Coach Bill Ellington. The fee is $5,
and may be paid to Ellington in room
17, B.A.C. building, between 10 and
11:30 o'clock in the morning or be
tween 1 and 2:30 o'clock in the after
noon.
