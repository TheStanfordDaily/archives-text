# AIRPLANES FOR CHINA
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Dec. 4 (/P). —A second
shipment of three fighting planes, part of
an order of twelve placed by the Chinese
Nationalist government, was on its way
to China today. The total order amounts
to nearly $1,000,000.
