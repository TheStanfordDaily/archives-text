# Economics Professors To Attend Eastern Meetings
## 
### 
Two members of the Economics De
partment, Professor Murray S. Wild
man and Professor A. C. Whittaker,
are going East during the Christmas
holidays to attend meetings.
Professor Wildman will attend a
meeting at the University of Chicago,
where research in social sciences will
be discussed and a new building for
research will be dedicated. He will
speak at the meeting, which will be
held December 16.
Then both Wildman and Whittaker
will attend the meeting of the Ameri
can Economical Association in Wash
ington, D.C., from December 27 to 30.
Professor Whittaker will present a
paper on "The Federal Reserve Rank
ing System."
