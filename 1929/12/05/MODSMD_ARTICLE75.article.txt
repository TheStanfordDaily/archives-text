# Aircraft Carrier May Give Power To Northern Cities
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, D.C Dec. 4 (JP).~
Assurances were given by Secretary Ad
ams today that the naval aircraft carrier
Lexington would be permitted to furnish
electric power to Tacoma and Seattle,
Washington, if an administrator were ap
pointed by Governor Hartley to allocate
the supply from the ship's generators.
Secretary Adams said that the cities
must also agree to conditions of distri
bution to be made by the Navy.
