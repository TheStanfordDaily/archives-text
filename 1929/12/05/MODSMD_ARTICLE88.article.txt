# Y.W.C.A. Group Deplores Passing Of Old Traditions
## 
### 
"The passing of Stanford traditions"
was deplored by the Tuesday night dis
cussion group of the Y.W.C.A., which
met in the Roble library yesterday eve
ning. Next week's meeting will be the
last of the quarter, according to President
Betty Alden. However, the "organized
bull sessions" have proved so popular that
they will be continued during the coming
winter.
