# Stanford Mothers' Club Meets Tuesday Afternoon
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club will meet
at 3 o'clock Tuesday afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse. Envelopes solicit
ing a silver Christmas offering for the
benefit of the Rest Homes will be distrib
uted to tie on the Christmas tree. Mr. and
Mrs. Warren D. Allen will furnish a
musical program illustrating Christmas
carols.
