# HERE IS CONVENTION SCHEDULE
## 
### 
The following Is the program of events for the California Scholastic Press
Association convention today and tomorrow:
FRIDAY
9:00 —12:00 a.m. —Registration of Delegates—Administration Building.
Assignment of lodging.
Collection of fees.
Distribution of labels, group programs, tickets.
12:00 — 1:00 p.m.—Faculty luncheon —Old Union.
1:00 — 2:00 p.m.—Opening Meeting—Little Theatre.
Welcome to Delegates by Stanford Steinbeck, president
of A.S.S.U.
Introduction of Herman Wittenberg, president of C.S.P.A.
Speaker: Nancy Barr Mavity, Oakland Tribune.
2:00 — 4:30 p.m.—Round Tables.
4:30 — 5:30 p.m.—Theta Sigma Phi tea dance —Women's Clubhouse.
6:30 — 8:00 p.m.—Convention Banquet—Union Dining Room.
Toastmaster: Herman Wittenberg.
Speaker: R. L. Burgess, San Francisco Examiner.
8:20—11:00 p.m.—"Cock Robin" —Assembly Hall.
11:00 — 1:00 p.m.—Convention Dance —Women's Clubhouse.
SATURDAY
8:30 —11:00 a.m. —Round Tables.
11:00 —11:15 a.m. —Group Picture —Memorial Church.
11:15 a.m. —12:00 noon —C.S.P.A. Executive Committee meeting—A.S.S.U. Of
fice, Old Union.
1:00 — 2:30 p.m.—Faculty Meeting—Little Theatre.
Visits to Publications Offices.
Examination of Entry Exhibits —Press Building.
2:30 — 4:00 p.m.—Closing Meeting—Little Theatre.
Election of Officers.
Awarding of Prizes.
