# THREE HIKES PLANNED BY W.A.A. HIKING HEAD FOR COMING WEEK-END
## 
### 
Three W.A.A. hikes for the purpose
of getting points for block letters
have been arranged for this week-end
by Catherine Cartlidge, W.A.A. hiking
manager.
A five-mile hike to the quarry near
the Stanford Isolation Hospital will
start at 1 o'clock this afternoon from
Roble Hall. A fifteen-mile bike to
Searsville Lake is being arranged for
tomorrow. Anyone wishing to go may
find out the starting time by calling
Catherine Cartlidge tonight.
Sunday morning at 9:30 o'clock
there will be a hike to Jasper Ridge.
This will probably be seven miles in
length.
