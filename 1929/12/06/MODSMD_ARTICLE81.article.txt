# L.A. BANK ROBBED
## 
### 
LOS Angeles, Dec. 5 (/H). —Three rob
bers held up a branch of the Citizens Na
tional Bank here today and escaped with
$2,500 after overpowering two employees,
who were alone at the time. They fled
in an automobile.
