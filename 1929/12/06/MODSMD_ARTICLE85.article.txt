# Aide Of Former Secretary Of Interior Attests Fraud
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Dec. 5 (£>).—Ed
ward C. Finney, assistant to former Sec
retary of the Interior Albert B. Fall,
testified today that he awarded leases to
660 acres in the Kern County Naval Oil
Reserve to Edward L. Doheny and the
Pan-American petroleum interests in 1921
at his chief's direction without competi
tive bidding.
Finney has been on the witness stand
almost continuously since testimony tak
ing in the government's suit in federal
court to cancel the $15,000,000 leases on
the grounds that they resulted from fraud
and conspiracy.
