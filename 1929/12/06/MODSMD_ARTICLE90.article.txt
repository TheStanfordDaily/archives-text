# MINER KILLED
## 
### 
REDDING, Dec. 5. (/P) —Struck by
a 200-pound rock in a tunnel of the
Iron Mountain mine, 12 miles north
east of here, Nikolo Ugankovich, 34,
was almost instantly killed today. He
was struck in the head.
