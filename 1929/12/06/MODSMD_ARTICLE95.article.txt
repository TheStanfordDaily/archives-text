# Theta Sigma Phi To Act As Tea Dance Sponsors
## 
### 
Tlicta Sigma Phi, women's journalistic
fraternity, will entertain delegates of the'
California Scholastic Press Association
at a tea dance to be given downstairs in
the Women's Clubhouse from 4:30 until
5 :30 o'clock this afternoon. Phoebe Plate,
president of Theta Sigma Phi, will he in
charge. Tom Murphy's orchestra is to
play.
