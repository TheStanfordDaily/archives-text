# W.A.A. Hikers Make Trips To Searsville And Quarry
## 
### 
W.A.A. hikers covered twenty miles
during the week-end in a hike to the
quarry near the Isolation Hospital
Friday afternoon and one to Searsville
Lake Saturday morning. A hike
planned for Sunday morning was can
celled.
Catherine Cartlidge, W.A.A. hiking
manager, announces that there will
he a five-mile hike to Felt Lake next
Friday, leaving Roble at 2 o'clock. All
those working for points toward a
block letter are urged to come. One
point is given for each mile. So far
Roble is considerably ahead of the
Row in hiking points.
