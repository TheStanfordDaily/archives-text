# Sophs Play Frosh Hockey Second-String Game Today
## 
### 
Having already won two contests of
the three-game hockey series between
the freshman and sophomore second
string players, and having virtually
cinched the series, the sophomore
eleven will meet the freshman squad
this afternoon in a third game, which
will be purely for practice purposes,
according to Elizabeth Woodyatt,
hockey manager.
The regular championship series
will be finished this week. The sopho
more-fresliman first-team combat is
scheduled for Wednesday and the
junior-senior for Thursday. The
W.A.A. hockey banquet Thursday eve
ning wlil close the '29 season. Present
rating of teams puts the sophs and
juniors on equal footing, and the frosh
and seniors in third and fourth places
respectively.
