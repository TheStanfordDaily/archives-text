# Episcopal Club Students Entertain Bishop Dallas
## 
### 
Reverend John Thomson Dallas,
D.D., Bishop of New Hampshire, who
spoke at the Memorial Chapel morn
ing services yesterday, will be the
guest of honor at a dinner tonight at
6:30 in the All Saints' parish house,
425 Hamilton Avenue, Palo Alto. All
Episcopal students registered at Stan
ford are invited.
Bishop Dallas' sermon was his in
troduction to the campus. He will re
main here for eight days during which
time he will confer with students on
Tuesday and Thursday evenings at
7:30 in the vestry of the Chapel. On
Thursday he will speak at vesper
services and on Sunday he will de
liver the sermon at Chapel services.
