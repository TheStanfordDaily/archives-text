# Renewal Of Tuition Notes Scheduled On December 16
## 
### 
Men who wish to take out or x'enew
tuition notes for the winter quarter
are reminded by the secretary of Dean
Culver that they should call at the
office of the Dean of Men between De
cember 16 and December 20.
It is important that these notes
should be attended to before the stu
dents leave for the holidays, the secre
tary announces.
