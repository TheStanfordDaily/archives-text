# Dr. Mahr To Lecture On Romans To Chi-Mu-Kappa
## 
### 
Dr. August Carl Mahr of the Stan
ford German Department will talk at
a meeting of Chi Nu Kappa, honorary
classical literature society, which will
be held at 8 o'clock Wednesday eve
ning in room 119 a. The lecture will be
on "Romans at the Rhine" and will
be illustrated by lantern slides.
Following the entertainment, the
club will adjourn to the Women's Club
house for refreshments.
