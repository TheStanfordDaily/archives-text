# Illinois Guard Mobilized For Mine Strike Service
## 
### 
SPRINGFIELD, 111., Dec. 9. (£>)—
Orders for immediate mobilization of
the 130 th Infantry and the lOGth Cav
alry, Illinois National Guard, for ser
vice in connection with the mine
strike at Taylorville, were issued late
today by Adjutant General Carlos M.
Black.
