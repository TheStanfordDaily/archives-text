# Classical Lit Society To Meet Tomorrow Night At 8
## 
### 
At a meeting of Chi Nu Kappa, hon
orary classical literature society,
which is to be held at 8 o'clock on
Wednesday in room 119a, Dr. August
Carl Mahr of the Stanford German
Department will talk on "Romans at
the Rhine." This talk will be illus
trated by lantern slides.
The group will adjourn to the Wo
men's Clubhouse after the entertain
ment, and refreshments will be
served there.
