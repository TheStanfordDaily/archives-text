# Winter Quarter Schedule Sunday Chapel Services
## 
### 
During the winter quarter when the
Rev. G. A. Johnston Ross of New
York City will be acting chaplain of
the Memorial Church the preaching
schedule will be as follows: January
5 and 12, Rev. Charles W. Gilkey of
Chicago; January 19 and 26, and Feb
ruary 2 and 9, Rev. G. A. Johnston
Ross; February 16, Rabbi Newman;
February 23 and March 2 and 9, Rev.
G. A. Johnston Ross; March 16, Dr.
Soares of Chicago.
