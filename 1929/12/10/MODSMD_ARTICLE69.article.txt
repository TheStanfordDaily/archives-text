# Amelia Earhart Will Be Guest of Women's Groups
## 
### 
ALAMEDA, Dec. 9. (#») —Amelia
Eearhart, noted aviatrix, arrived at
Alameda airport today after piloting
a T.A.T.-Maddux air liner carrying
seven others from Los Angeles.
Tomorrow <JHiss Earhart will be the
guest of honor at a luncheon in the
Mark Hopkins Hotel sponsored by
the San Francisco Federation of Wo
men's Clubs, the San Francisco Cen
ter, Junior League, and the Western
Women's Club.
