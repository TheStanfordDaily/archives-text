# "Christmas" Subject Of Dallas' Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
"Christmas" will be the subject of
Bishop John Thomson Dallas' sermon
Sunday morning at 11 o'clock in the
Memorial Church. No Sunday services
are to be held during the Christmas
vacation.
There will be no Sunday afternoon
organ recital but at 7:30 that evening
the University Choir and other sing
ers of the community, with the aid
of a chorus of children's voices, will
present a "Service of Christmas
Carols, Old and New."
