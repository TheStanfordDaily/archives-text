# FRESHMAN DEBATERS FOR WINTER QUARTER TO TRY OUT THURSDAY
## 
### 
Try outs for next quarters freshman
debates will be held in Room 214 of the
English building at 7 o'clock Thursday
night, December 12, announces Leon Car
ley, freshman debate coach. Five-minute
speeches and three-minute rebuttals on
the question "Resolved, That newspaper
sensationalism tends to increase crime"
will form the basis for judging the men
who try out.
At present a debate with Mills College
has been scheduled for early in February,
and negotiations are being conducted for
other possible debates for the freshman
team.
