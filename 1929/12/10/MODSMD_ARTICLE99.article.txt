# FINALS OF INTERCLASS TENNIS TOURNEY WILL BE PLAYED THURSDAY
## 
### 
Keith Gledhill, winner of the fresh
man-sophomore interclass tennis singles
championship, will play the winner of the
junior-senior division of the tournament
at 2 o'clock Thursday afternoon, Decem
ber 12, on the varsity courts to determine
the interclass singles championship.
Among the senior entrants, the winner
of the McKellip-Osborne match in the
semi-finals must meet Matthews in the
class finals. The senior finalist will then
play Harry Plymire, junior finalist, and
the resulting winner is scheduled to face
Gledhill Thursday.
