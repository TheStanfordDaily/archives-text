# N.S.F.A. Auto Stickers Available At Old Union
## 
### 
Auto stickers reading "N.S.F.A.,
Happy New Year!" are available
' for all who wish to display them
! on their ears. They may he secured
at the Administration Building any
time today, as well as at the
N.S.F.A. office, which is located in
the Old Union, below the A.S.S.C.
office.
