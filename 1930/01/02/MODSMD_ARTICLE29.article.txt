# YOUTH NOT COMPLETELY DEGENERATE YET
## 
### 
The Stanford campus and Palo Alto were football hothouses
during the Christmas holidays. With the Cadet gridders, the East
team, and the Card football men training here, All-Americans were
under foot at every turn. The three squads made a fine cross
section of this country's youth from east to west. It would have
been good if those who lament tlie present state of the younger
generation could have inspected that cross-section. Those football
men were about as fine an argument for youth as could be pre
sented.
