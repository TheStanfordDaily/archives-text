# CINCINNATI HONORS TAFT
## 
### 
CINCINNATI, Jan. 2. (/p)—Cin
cinnati today paid final tribute to
Charles P. Taft, philanthropist and
! publisher, who died Tuesday at the
age of 8(5. Mr. Taft, founder of the
Cincinnati Times-Star and its pub
lisher until his death, had been ill
for more than o year.
