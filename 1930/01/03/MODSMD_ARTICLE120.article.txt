# LINDBERGH IN K.C.
## 
### 
KANSAS CITY, Jan. 2 (/P). -
Colonel Charles A. Lindbergh and
Mrs. Lindbergh, who is accompany
ing her husband on an inspection trip
over lines of the Transcontinental Air
Transport, arrived here at 12:40 o
clock today from St. Louis. They de
parted at 1 .05 p.m. for Wichita, Kan.,
the next stop on their trip.
