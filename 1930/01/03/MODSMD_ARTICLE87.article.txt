# FIFTEEN COLLEGES IN FORTY SANCTION WOMEN'S SMOKING
## 
### 
Only fifteen out of forty colleges
allow women to smoke in dormi
tories, it. was discovered at a meet
ing of women representatives dis
cussing student government under
the leadership of Cornelia Ander
son of Vassar in the Women's Club
house yesterday afternoon. The
other twenty-five colleges prohibit
women's smoking. The greater
number of these women are from
the leading Women's colleges in the
United States.
The question came up in regard
to enforcing rules against smoking.
