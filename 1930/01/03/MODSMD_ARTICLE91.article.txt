# Farmer, Art Instructor, Is Back From N.Y. Study
## 
### 
Edward M. Farmer, instructor in
education in the graphic art depart
ment, has recently returned to the
campus from a six months' study
at the Art Students League in New
York City. While there he studied
painting and portrait work.
Mrs. Farmer, formerly Mabel Mc-
Kibben, accompanied him and also
studied at the League. Elizabeth
Roper Jordan, and Rhona and
Elizabeth Williams are other Stan
fordites who are members of the
same colony.
