# Oregon State Professor Wins Association Office
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Jan. 2. (fP)—Prof.
! John F*. Dollman of the University
! of Pennsylvania, was elected presi
dent of the National Association
of Teachers of Speech at the an
nual meeting held yesterday. Prof.
W. 10. Wells, Oregon State College,
was elected a vice-president. The
next, convention will be held in
1 Chicago in December 1930.
