# JOHNNY DOEG WILL NOT BE REGISTERED REMAINDER OF YEAR
## 
### 
That Johnny Doeg, Card tennis
captain, will not register in the
University at all this year, was
confirmed by members of the Delta
Kappa Epsilon fraternity yester
day,
Doeg at present is competing in
the Midwick tennis tourney at
Pasadena. He expects to work out
frequently with John Van Ryn in
the Southland.
In the spring Doeg plans to com
mence definite practice with the
members of the U.S. Davis Cup
team, and will leave for the East,
in April or May. He is expected lo
return to the campus next autumn.
