# NEGRO FILM STAR FREED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Jan. (I. (yip)
Stepin Fetchit, Negro film come
dian. proved to the satisfaction of
a municipal court jury today that
he was just "powerful sleepy"
when he was arrested last Decem
ber 11 on a charge of driving while
intoxicated.
