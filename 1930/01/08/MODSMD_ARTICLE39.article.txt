# LARGEST LAND PLANE TESTED IN GERMANY
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
The Junkers plane "G-38," said to be the largest land plane in the world, was recently successfully
tested at Dessau, Germany. Accommodations for passengers, luxuriously appointed cabins and an
electric kitchen have been installed in the wings. Inset shows one of the four wheels in the landing gear
