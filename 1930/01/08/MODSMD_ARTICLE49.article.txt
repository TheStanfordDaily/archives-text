# ONE CANDIDATE IS ON BALLOT TODAY FOR Y.M.C.A. VOTE
## 
### 
j John W. Way, Jr., will be sole
candidate on the Stanford Y.M.C.A.
presidential ballot today. He was
nominated at a cabinet meeting
: yesterday by the Y.M.C.A. nominat
ing committee. No candidates were
nominated from the floor.
Balloting, which is open to all
members, will take place in the
Y.M.C.A. lobby all day today. The
boxes will be closed at 4 o'clock.
The election is being held at this
time because of a vacancy caused
I by the failure of Francis Knapp,
elected president, to return to
! school. Morden Brown has been
j substituting during the fall quar
ter. All members are urged to vote.
Way has distinguished himself
both in his work in the Stanford
Industrial Group, an outgrowth of
i the Y.M.C.A., and in the Cosmo
politan Club. He has also been
1 prominent in Y.M.C.A. affairs.
