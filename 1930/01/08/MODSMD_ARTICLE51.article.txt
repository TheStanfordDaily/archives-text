# Fellowship Tea Will Be Held In Clubhouse Today
## 
### 
Continuing the plan successfully
instituted last quarter, the Stan
ford Fellowship will give the first
tea of the quarter this afternoon at
the Women's Clubhouse, beginning
at 4:30 o'clock. The teas were well
attended last quarter.
A meeting of the fellowship
board will be held at 5:30 o'clock
this afternoon on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse. All
members of the board are urged to
attend.
