# A.S.S.U. CONCERT TICKETS ON SALE THIS AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Tickets to the second
A.S.S.IT. Concert of the 1929-
30 season go on sale this after
noon at 1 'oclock in the Asso
ciated Students' Office in the
Old Union, according to Carl
Mather, manager. The concert
will be in the Pavilion next
Wednesday night, January 15.
Until that time the tickets
will remain on sale in the
A.S.S.U. Offices. They are
priced at $1, $1.50, and $2.
Efrem Zimbalist, noted Rus
sian violinist, will play his
Stradivarius, considered one
of the four best made by the
master. Emanuel Bay will be
the accompanist.
