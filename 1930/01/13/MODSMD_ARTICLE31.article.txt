# TWO MEN FROZEN IN ARIZONA
## 
###

Reports were received here tonight
that two men had been found
frozen to death, and several others
were missing, on the Jerome Pres
cott Highway, as there. nit of one
of tlie worst snowstorms in, the his
tory of Yavapai county.
