# "Full Measure" Written By Hans Otto Storm, '20
## 
### 
Hans Otto Storm, whose book,
"Full Measure," was reviewed by
the Daily recently, is a Stanford
graduate of the class of 1920. He
majored in mechanical engineering,
and shortly after graduation was
employed by the Federal Telegraph
Company. He has worked at the
Federal station near Palo Alt.o, and
it. was. in that line of work he
gained the experience he employed
in writing his book.
