# Golf Tourney Postponed To Next Weekend-Bennett
## 
### 
The elimination 36-hole medal tour
nament scheduled for today and to
morrow to cut down the varsity jjolf
squad to twenty men was postponed
on account of rain until Friday and
Saturday of next week, stated Coach
Garry Bennett late last night.
The tourney will start at 1 o'clock
next Friday atternoon when 18 holes
will be played. The other IK will be
shot on Saturday morning beginning
at 9 o'clock.
