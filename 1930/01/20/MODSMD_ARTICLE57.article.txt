# Brown, Small Announce Engagement At House
## 
### 
The engagement of Aileen Brown,
'2S, to Francis McCauley Small,
'2!*. was announced at the Alpha
Omicron Pi sorority, of which Miss
Brown is a member. She is an ex
president of Cap and Gown and a
former woman's editor of the Daily.
Small is a member of Theta Xi.
