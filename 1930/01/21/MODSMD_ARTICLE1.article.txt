# EIGHTY WOMEN ARE PLEDGED BY SORORITIES
## Delta Gamma Leads Houses With 16; Chi O's Next; Pi Phi, Gamma Phi in Tie Increase of Four Pledges Over Number Last Year; Three Women Undecided 
### 
Eighty women pledged sororities
at the formal bidding last night, an
increase of four over last year.
Three women were listed by Pan-
Hellenic as undecided when bid
ding closed at !• o'clock last eve
ning. This number was nearly ap
proached in IWI7 when seventy-nine
women pledged.
In numbers Helta Gamma led
with sixteen. Chi Omega came
next with thirteen; Gamma Phi
Beta and Pi Phi tied with eleven
each.
