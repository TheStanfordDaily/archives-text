# Oregon Rancher Caught In Wire Endures Cold
## 
### 
AMITY, Ore., Jan. 21 UP).
Caught by barbed wire while crossing
a field, Steve MeKinney, 7X, rancher,
was in a hospital here today suffer
ing from exposure and frozen hands
after he spent a night in sub-zero
temperature.
MeKinney, who was unconscious
when found by his brother-in-law,
Peter Burton, said he was crossing a
field to a neighbor's home to replen
ish his supply of matches. He said he
exhausted himself endeavoring to
loosen his clothing from the barbs.
