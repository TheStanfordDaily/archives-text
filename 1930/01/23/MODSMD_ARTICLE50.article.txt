# Chinese Envoy To Cuba Is Stanford Graduate
## 
### 
Dr. Ping Ling, Stanford '16, who
was recently appointed Chinese
minister to Cuba, reached San
Francisco yesterday morning on
the steamship President Madison.
He is accompanied by his wife, a
graduate of the University of Cali
fornia, his three children, and Miss
Yoeh E. Wang who is the daughter
of C. T. Wang, the foreign minis
ter of China. A secretarial staff is
included in his retinue.
