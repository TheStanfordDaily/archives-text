# SHOTS STOP RIOT
## 
### 
WATSON VILLI-;, Cal., Jan. 11
(A 3 ). —Shotguns fired directly into -a
group of charging men saved Fili
pinos from possible violence at a
Palm Beach resort, near here, tonight.
The attack tonight climaxed several
days of growing tension here, origi
nating when white girls were import
ed as dancing partners in a Filipino
social club at Palm Beach.
