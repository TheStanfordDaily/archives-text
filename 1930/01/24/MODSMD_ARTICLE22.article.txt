# GLEE CLUB ENTERTAINS
## 
### 
The Men's Glee Club entertained
the members ot the Bohemian Club
in San Francisco last night at the
Post Street clubhouse of the Sail
Francisco organization. Thirty
eight Glee Club singles had dinner
at the club and presented a pro
gram of songs afterward.
