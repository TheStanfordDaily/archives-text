# Eminent Physicist Will Deliver Lecture Series
## 
### 
Four lectures on "Band Spectra"
will be given at Stanford by Dr.
Raymond T. Birge, professor of
physics at the University of Cali
fornia, beginning Monday, January
27, and continuing on successive
Mondays.
Dr. Birge is an internationally
known authority in the field of
band spectra and has done a great
deal of research in that line.
The lectures will be given under
the auspices of the Chemistry
Seminar at 4:15 o'clock, in Room
20, in the Chemistry Building.
