# Wilburs Depart For Oil Fields And Washington
## 
### 
Secretary and Mrs. Wilbur left
the campus yesterday afternoon
after a reception at which they
were hosts io the Stanford faculty
members and their wives. Mem
bers of Cap and Gown served at
the reception which was the only
social event of the Wilbur's visit
here.
Today, Secretary of the Interior
Wilbur will confer with the con
servation committee of the Kettle
man Hills operators after a visit
to the Kettleman Hills fields. This
is the last step in Dr. Wilbur's in
vestigation of the oil situation in
the West on his present visit. The
Wilburs expect to arrive in Wash
ington February 2.
