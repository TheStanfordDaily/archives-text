# BANDIT CAPTURED
## 
### 
SAN RAFAEL, Jan. 2f>. (/!>) —
Captured hiding in a ditch in thick-
Is-wooded country five miles east
of Novato. a man who gave his
name as Jack Lewis, was identified
today as the bandit who last Thurs
day robbed' the Fairfax branch of
the Bank of Italy of $2,000.
