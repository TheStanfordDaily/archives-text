# Fine Of 50 Cents Will Be Added To Quad Payments
## 
### 
Second installments on the IIKSO
Quad are payable before Saturday,
February 1, according to Hildur
Richardson, business manager of
the Quad. After this date, a fine of
fifty cents will be added to the
amount due. Payments are made
at the Quad business office in the
Old Union which is open from 1
to a o'clock daily.
