# Cosmopolitan Club Will Entertain On Saturday
## 
### 
The Women's Clubhouse will be
the scene of the Cosmopolitan Clubs
entertainment Saturday night, accord
ing to Mill West, president. At 8:15
p.m. a program of amateur movies,
entertainment, and refreshment will
start which is open to all interested.
The object of the affair is to encour
age relations with foreign students on
the campus.
