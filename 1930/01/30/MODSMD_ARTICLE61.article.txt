# Mizota Named Secretary To Naval Arms Delegate
## 
### 
Chosen for his unusual ability,
George Mizota, '25, is secretary to
Admiral Takarabe, Japan's second
delegate to the Naval Conference
in London. Mizota took his A.B. in
the pre-legal curriculum at Stan
ford in 1925. He is a relative of
Professor Yamato lehihashi, of the
History I )epartment.
Professor A. J. Cathcart, of the
Law School, said of Mizota yester
day:"He was a good student and
his grasp of the American point of
view will help him greatly in his
present position."
