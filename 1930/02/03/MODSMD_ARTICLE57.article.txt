# Chi Omega Housemother Dies Suddenly Saturday
## 
### 
Mrs. Nancy K. Ivev, for 18 years
housemother at the Chi Omega
sorority, passed away suddenly
Saturday morning at the Palo Alto
Hospital following a heart attack.
Funeral services will he held this
afternoon at 3:150 o'clock at the
Roller and Hapgood chapel.
Mrs. Ivey came to Stanford 18
years-ago as housemother for the
Longworth Club, which later he
came the Chi Omega sorority. Her
final illness was brief, and her
death was unexpected.
