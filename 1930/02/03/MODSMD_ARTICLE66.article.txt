# VOCATIONAL SCHOOL
## 
### 
BERLIN, Feb. 2. (/P)— What is
said will be the world's largest and
most modern vocational school
will be built here shortly at a cost
of nearly $2,000,000. Facilities for
the instruction of more than 8,000
students in 2;)7 classrooms and
workshops will be provided.
