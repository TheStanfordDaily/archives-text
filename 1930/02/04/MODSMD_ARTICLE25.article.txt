# Son Spanks Father For Taking Car, Girl Friend
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Feb. 3. (yp) Albert
Fiemerman's father borrowed Al
bert' girl friend and Albert's auto
mobile, and got spanked for it.
The spanking, done by Albert,
who is 21, attracted so much at
tention oi# North LaSalle street
yesterday that police were called
and arrested Albert, his father.
David, and the girl friend, Miss
Sheron Daniels.
At the station, it was cleared up.
and Albert gave his dad a good
talking to.
