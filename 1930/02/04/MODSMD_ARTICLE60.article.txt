# CARD BOXING TEAM MEETS U.C. AGGIES IN SEASON OPENER
## 
### 
Opening the intercollegiate box
ing season with a meet with Davis
Farm Friday night, the varsity box
ing squad is hard at work again
following the completion of the all
-I'niversity tournament last Thurs
day. Coach Harry Maloney reports
that all the men came through the
tourney in line style, and that they
will be in line shape for Davis.
Ten bouts will be on tap in the
Davis meet, since three exhibitions,
besides the regular seven matches,
have been scheduled. As yet the
team for the coining engagement
has not been selected, and the men
will not be named until Wednesday
night following the regular work
out of the squad.
