# International Group To Gather At Nine Tonight
## 
### 
The International group, which
usually meets in the Women's
Clubhouse at 0:45 o'clock, will meet
there tonight at it o'clock instead.
Entertainment will be planned for
Miss Helen Thoburn, secretary of
international interests of the
Y.W.C.A.
Miss Thoburn, who will visit the
campus on Friday, February 14,
has recently attended the Cause
and Cure for War Conference held
at Washington, D.C.
An Industrial Group Study will
also meet tonight at 7 o'clock in
the Y.W.C.A. room of the Women's
Clubhouse.
