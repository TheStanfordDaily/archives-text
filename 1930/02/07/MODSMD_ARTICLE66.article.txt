# Bear Wrestler
## 
### 

BERKELEY, Feb. 7. (£>)—'The
heaviest and lightest men on the
University of California wrestling
team are both captains. Carl
Handy, captain-elect of the football
team wrestles in the unlimited di
vision while not playing guard. Ed
gar Nomir, wrestling captain, is
the smallest man on the squad. He
wrestles at 130 pounds.
