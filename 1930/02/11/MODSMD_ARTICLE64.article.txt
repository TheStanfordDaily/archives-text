# RUBIO WOUNDED BY ASSASSIN
## 
### 

I Pascual Ortiz Rubio, newly inaugurated president of Mexico, is
recovering from a bulelt wound made by one of six bullets fired by a
youthful assailant two hours after Rubio had been sworn into the
chief executive position. One bullet grazed the face of Senora Ortiz
Rubio, wife of the president.
