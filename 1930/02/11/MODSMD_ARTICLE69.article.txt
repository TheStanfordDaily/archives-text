# WOMEN REPLACE MEN
## 
### 
CONSTANTINOPLE, Feb. 10. (/p)
—Turkish women's most recent
conquest is the music market. Old
'I urkish orchestras are replacing
jazz bands, and women compose
more than half the singing and
playing talent.
