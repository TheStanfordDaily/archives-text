# HALL AND DRISCOLL DEFEATED IN MATCH BY WEESNER, EASTON
## 
### 
Playing a fast exhibition match on
the varsity courts yesterday after
noon, Weesner and Easton defeated
Hall and Driscoll, 7-5, 6-4, in one of
the contests being held to work out a
good doubles combination for the
Stanford team.
In the singles competition, Hall and
Weesner struggled through a slow
match for second place ranking of the
varsity squad, with Weesner losing by
a score of 8-6, 6-4. For seventh place
ranking, Forbes defeated McKellip,
7-5, 6-1.
In the other matches played yester
day, Lee defeated Erlin, 6-4, (>-4 ; Mil
ler won from Brock, 6-3, 6-3; Strong
beat Burnett, 2-6, 6-4, 8-6; Hayman
defeated Catlin, 6-8, 6-2, 6-3; and
Mansser beat Johnston.
