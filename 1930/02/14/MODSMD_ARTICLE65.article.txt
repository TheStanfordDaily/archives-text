# SCAFFOLDING COLLAPSES
## 
### 
TUCSON, Ariz., Feb. 13. t/P) —Ed
Murphey, laborer from Vermont,
was killed and three other men
were injured when a scaffolding
collapsed and dropped them 35
fee to tin! floor of a theatre under
construction.
