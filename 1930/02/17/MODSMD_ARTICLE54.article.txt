# ROOTERS' TICKET SALE TODAY FOR BEAR HOOP TILT
## 
### 
Student rooters' tickets to
the third California-Stanford
basketball game, which is to
be played in the Stanford
Pavilion Saturday, February
22, will go on sale at the
A.S.S.U, offices in the Old
Union this morning at 10
o'clock, according to an an
nouncement yesterday by Don
Liebendorfer.
The rooters' tickets, which
sell for 50 cents, will be ob
tainable only today and to
morrow.
