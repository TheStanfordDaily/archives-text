# A.S.S.U., CLASS REVOTING WILL BE HELD TODAY
## Polling Will Take Place From 8 to 2 O'clock At Law Steps on Quad All Candidates to Run for Junior Secretaryship and Sophomore Presidency 
### 
Polls for the A.S.S.C. and class
election revote will be open from
S to 2 o'clock today on the Outer
Quad .near the Law Steps.
The officers to be chosen are the
junior and sophomore representa
tives on the Executive Committee;
president of the Class of '30; and
president, vice-president, and secre
tary of the Junior, Sophomore, and
Freshman classes.
With the exception of the junior
secretaryship and the sophomore
presidency, the candidates for the
above offices are the two contest
ants who received the highest num
ber of voles in last Tuesday's pri
mary election. The full quota, of
candidates are recotnpeting for
junior secretary and sophomore
president, because of the fact that
four names were omitted from the
ballots for these offices on Tues
day. If no one candidate for these
two offices receives enough votes
to give him a plurality, a third
vote will be taken a week from
today.
Hob Clarke, chairman of the
election board, urges voters to fol
low instructions printed on the bal
lot explicitly in order that their
ballots may not be voided.
