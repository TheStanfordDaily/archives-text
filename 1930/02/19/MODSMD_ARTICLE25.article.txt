# MEN'S COUNCIL FINES
## 
### 
Three $1 parking fines am! one
$5 speeding penalty were levied at
the regular Tuesday meeting of the
Men's Council in the Old I'nion
yesterday afternoon. Virgil Howell
was unanimously chosen secretary
of the Council for the balance of
the college year. Harlow Rothert
will continue in his position its
chairman.
