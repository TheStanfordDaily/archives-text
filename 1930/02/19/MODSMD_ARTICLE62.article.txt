# HELVENSTON HORSE ENTRY WINS FIRST
## 
### 
With her horse, Jean's Jewel,
Mrs. Harold Helvenston, '29, won
the .$5OO prize offered by Alex J.
Young, Jr., for the best three-gaited
saddle horse entered in the San
Francisco National Horse Show at
the State Armory Monday night.
Another of Mrs. Helvenston's
horses, Dark Laughter, placed third
in the five-gaited mare class.
