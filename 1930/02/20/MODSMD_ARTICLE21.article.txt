# CLASSMATES MAKE AEROBATIC RECORD
## 
### 
ST. LOUIS, Feb. 19. (fP) An out
side loop with a plane carrying a
passenger was performed today for
the first time in aerobatie history.
Phil Love, well-known St. Louis
flier, accomplished that feat when
he successfully completed three
outside loops with Eugene Stevens,
of Tallulah, La,
The pair, classmates once in the
Army flying school at Kelly Field,
Texas, took off unannounced from
Lambert Field and performed the
unprecedented feat before a small
group of visitors here for the In
ternational Aircraft Exposition.
