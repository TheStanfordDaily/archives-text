# MEET OF AMERICAN GEOLOGISTS IS HELD
## 
### 
Meeting in joint session at
Berkeley, the western sections of
the Geological Society of America,
the Paleontological Society of
America, and the Le Conte Geologi
cal Club of Berkeley today begin a
three-day series of discussions and
speeches.
Among Stanford people who will
participate in the programs are
Professors Elliot Blackwelder, Aus
tin F. Rogers, and Hubert' G.
Schenck; assistants and graduate
students, Slemon W. Muller, IT. S.
Grant IV, J. C. Ray, William C.
Putnam, George L. Richards, and
M. M. Kornfeld.
