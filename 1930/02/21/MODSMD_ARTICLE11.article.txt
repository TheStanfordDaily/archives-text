# DR. GARDNER TO RETURN MARCH 23
## 
### 
Dr. Charles I). Gardner, chaplain
of the University, now tournig Ku
rope, will return to Hie campus on
March 23, according to Dr. John
ston Ross, acting chaplain. Dr.
Gardner is scheduled to sail from
Italy on March 1.
Dr. Ross will deliver the sermon
Sunday morning at. the regular ser
vices in the Memorial Church. Two
anthems, "In Thee, Oh Lord, I I'ut
My Trust" by Kalinnikoff, and
Palestrina's "Adoramuu Te" will
be supplemented by Handel's
"Holy, Holy, Lord God Almighty,"
a tenor solo to be rendered by Ot is
