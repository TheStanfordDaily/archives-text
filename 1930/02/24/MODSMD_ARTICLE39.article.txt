# CANTILEVER SPAN OF BRIDGE JOINED
## 
### 

/hiurtalrU I''rt) I'hoto
Longview, Wash., and Rainier, Ore., on opposite banks of the
Columbia river, will be joined by the Longview bridge to be opened
for traffic in April. Its cantilever span, 1200 feet, will be the longest
in the United States, the total length of the structure being 3900 feet.
