# SWENDSON TO GUIDE U.C.L.A. SWIMMERS
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 2:5. (/!>)
Clyde Swendson, famous Hollywood
Athletic Club swimming instructor,
is the latest addition to the
C.C.L.A. coaching ranks. Swendson
will handle the Bruin swimmers as
well as the water polo team.
"While at Hollywood A.C., Swend
son led the* Movie City Mermen to
the Far West, Pacific Coast, and
National Championships in recent,
years and aims to put the U.C.L.A.
swimmers in the national spotlight
along with his Hollywood stars.
