# RUMANIAN PRINCESS, COUNT STAY ENGAGED
## 
### 
LONDON, Feb. 24. f/P) A Cannes
dispatch to the London Daily Mail
today quoted Count Alexander von
Hochberg as saying stories from
Bucharest that his engagement,
with Princess Henna of Rumania
had been broken were "fabrications
of the Rumanian government,
which wants the Princess to marry
a crown prince."
