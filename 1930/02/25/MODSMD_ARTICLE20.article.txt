# GLIDER RECORD FALLS IN NINE-HOUR FLIGHT
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, Feb. 24. (/P) Har
vey Bowlus, who early today set
a new American glider record of
nine hours, live minutes, 27 sec
onds, assisted Charles A. Lind
bergh this afternoon in the first of
a new series of glider tests.
Bowlus, although he remained in
the air until 2:4:! a.m., failed to
break the German-held world's rec
ord of more than 14 hours.
