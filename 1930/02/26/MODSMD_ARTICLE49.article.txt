# BOYCOTT PROPOSED
## 
### 
MANILA, Feb. 25. (/P)—A boy
cott* of all American and other for
eign linns that oppose Philippine
independence was proposed in a
resolution unanimously adopted to
day by the labor section of the
Philippine Independence Congress
which is holding sessions to pro
mote the islands' freedom.
