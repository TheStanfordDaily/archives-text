# WOMEN'S GLEE CLUB TO START PRACTICE
## 
### 
At a meeting tonight; at 7 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse, thy]
Women's Glee Club will start prac- j
lice oil some new songs, under the
direction of Miss Natalie Macrumm.
The songs are to be offered by the
Glee Club at the A.W.S. installation
banquet, in April.
Miss Macrumm, the new director,
will begin her work tonight. She
was at Stanford several years ago,
directing the Glee Club in the year |
1920-1927.
