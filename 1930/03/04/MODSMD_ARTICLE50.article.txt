# PROFESSOR JACKSON IN SCHOOL ELECTION
## 
### 
Professor J. Hugh Jackson, of
the Graduate School of Business,
will be a candidate for the Palo
Alto elementary school board in
the election to be held for the
board April 19, nomination papers
qualifying him as a candidate hav
ing been filed Friday.
Professor Jackson is running for
the place at present held by Dr.
Willard B. Thorp whose term ex
pires soon. Dean Marion Kirkwood
of the School of Law is also a
member of the school board.
