# WARREN IS ELECTED PRESIDENT OF ENCINA
## 
### 
William Warren was elected
president of Encina Club for the
second term at the election last
night in the hall lobby. Voting was
done on a preferential ballot, to
avoid the necessity of a revote be
fore the end of the quarter.
Other officers elected are as fol
lows: Payne Palmer, vice-presi
dent; Robert Polhamus, secretary;
Irwin Tway, manager; Bradford
Pease, chairman of the cmoker com
mittee; and Carl Lucas, dance com
mittee chairman.
