# BASEBALL ENTRIES MUST BE POSTED
## 
### 
Groups wishing to compete in in
tramural baseball next quarter
must post their entries in Encina
gymnasium by tomorrow afternoon,
according to Coach Murray Cudde
back. Drawing of leagues and
schedules will be handled by the
intramural board during spring va
cation.
Points won in baseball are dis
tributed toward the all-sport intra
mural championship on the same
basis as in football and basketball,
with the University champions
adding fifty points to their total.
A cabaret in Berlin requires the
wearing of black masks by its pa
trons to give tired business men a
chance to escape detection.
