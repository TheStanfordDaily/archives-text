# BLACKWELDER IS ON LEAVE NEXT QUARTER
## 
### 
Professor Eliot Blackwelder, head
of the Department of Geology, on
sabbatical leave during the coming
spring quarter, yesterday announced
plans for continuing his research
work in desert physiography in the
southern part of the state.
With Death Valley and the Mojavc
Desert his objective, Dr. Blackwelder
will leave Stanford during the spring
vacation and will return about June 1.
For a number of years, he has been
concerned with desert geology.
