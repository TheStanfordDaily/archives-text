# TESTS FOR CANOEISTS MAY BE TAKEN DAILY
## 
### 
Tests required of all canoeists on
Lagunita are being given daily at En
cina poool from 10 to 4 o'clock, an
nounces Ernst Brandsten, water
sports director.
Applicants are required to pass a
test consisting of: a 200-yard free
style swim, a 40-foot swim under
water from a start in the water, and
retrieval of a Ford rim from the cen
ter of the tank.
