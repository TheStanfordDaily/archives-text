# STAMPED ENVELOPES FOR GRADES ON SALE
## 
### 
Stamped envelopes for students who
want their grades sent to them this
vacation will be placed on sale today
at the office of the Registrar for 5
cents each. The sale will be for the
benefit of the Convalescent Home.
Students wishing to receive their
grades before registration day of next
quarter must have them sent by mail
according to the Registrar's office.
