# KATHERINE SNODGRASS IS GIVEN FELLOWSHIP
## 
### 
Katherine Suodgrass, a research
assistant at the Stanford Food Re
search Institute, lias been awarded
a fellowship by the John Simon
Guggenheim Memorial Foundation,
according to an announcement
made last week by Henry Allen
Moe, secretary of the foundation.
Miss Suodgrass, receiving one of
the eight scholarships given in the
West, will be enabled to study the
replacements of dairy fats by vege
table fats in northern Europe.
