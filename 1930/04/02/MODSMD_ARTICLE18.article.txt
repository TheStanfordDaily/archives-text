# FIND BODY IN BAY
## 
### 
EUREKA, Calif., April 1 (/P). —
\\ eighted down by two heavy iron
railroad plates, the body of an un
identified man was found today on the
shore of Humboldt Bay. The plates
were fastened to the body with wire.
