# THE 						POWER OF THE PRESS
## 
### 
Just to show that modern civilization is run by newspapers, and
that international relations are determined by reporters pounding
out copy for the press of the world, late last night an assiduous
Daily staff member, in writing up the story of the Irish Marathon,
discovered to his amazement that the juniors had amassed the high
est number of points. This was contrary to the announcement made
at the meet, which declared that the class of \>o had won the banner
of victory.
So all the seniors who spent last night smugly gloating over their
athletic triumph will wake up to the cold clear light of truth th
morning. Sic transit gloria mundi, oh class of 1930.
