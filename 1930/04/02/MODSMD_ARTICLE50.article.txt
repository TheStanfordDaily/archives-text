# DIGNITARIES DEDICATE COLUMBIA SPAN
## 
### 

Opening of the new $5,800,000 highway span over the Columbia river, linking Rainier, Washington, and
Longview, Oregon, was attended by state and municipal officials including (left to right): Mayors Frank
Baker of Portland, Frank Edwards of Seattle, James A. Rolph of San Francisco, W. W. Comer, Seattle
financier, and Mrs. J. J. Tynan, Jr., sponsor of the 1200-foot structure.
