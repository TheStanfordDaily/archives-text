# EXPLOSION SLIGHTLY INJURES INSTRUCTOR
## 
### 
While performing an experiment
during a chemistry lecture yester
dav morning Robert Dußois, lec
turer and instructor in chemistry,
received minor injuries to his face
and hands as the result of a vio
lent explosion. A. bottle containing
acetylene and oxygen was placed
near a bunsen fiame and the explo
sion, which was more violent than
anticipated, resulted in the break
ing of the bottle and the scattering
of the glass around the lecture
room.
