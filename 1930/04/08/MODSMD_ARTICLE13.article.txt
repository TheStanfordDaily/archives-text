# SEND-OFF RALLY TO BE HELD THURSDAY
## 
### 
A rousing send-off rally for the
varsity track team and Coach Dink
Templeton is being planned for
Thursday night, as they leave for
Los Angeles and the meet with
I'.S.C. on Saturday, according to
Paul Speegle, head yell-leader.
The rally will be held at the
Southern Pacific depot in*' Palo
Alio at 6:45 o'clock Thursday. It is
suggested that fraternities and eat
ing clubs plan early dinners for
that evening, in order that a huge
group may attend the rally.
