# INTRAMURAL BALL SCHEDULE STARTS; 5 LEAGUES PLAT
## All Games To Be Played With Regulation Hard Ball; 7 Innings 
### 
Intramural baseball will begin
tomorrow, according to Murray
Cuddeback, assistant coach. All
games will begin at 3 o'clock on
the intramural diamonds, and will
be played with the regulation hard
ball, both teams being required to
bring a new one for each of their
contests. Seven innings will con
stitute a game, though a contest of
live periods will be counted.
Varsity baseball letter men will
be ineligible. Games may be played
before but not after the scheduled
date, and may be postponed only
if the field is unavailable, or in
case of rain, at the decision of the
umpire. A team will be forced to
forfeit if at least, seven men do
not show up fifteen minutes before
starting time.
