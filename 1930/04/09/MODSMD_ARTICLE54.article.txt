# ROUSING SEND-OFF TO BE GIVEN TRACK MEN AT S.P. DEPOT
## 
### 
A rousing send-off rally for the
varsity track team as the men
leave tomorrow for Los Angeles
and the dual meet with Southern
California on Saturday is being
planned by the Rally Committee,
according to Paul Speegle, head
yell-leader.
The rally will occur at 6:45
o'clock sharp, tomorrow evening at
the Southern Pacific depot in Palo
Alto. Speegle urges that all loyal
Cardinal supporters come to cheer
the team and Coach Dink Temple
ton as they board the train.
It is suggested by the Rally Com
miltee that fraternities and eating
clubs plan early dinners in order
that everybody will be able to at
tend the rally.
