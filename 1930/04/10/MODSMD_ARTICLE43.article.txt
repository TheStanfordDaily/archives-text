# REV. GARDNER WILL LEAD VESPERS THIS AFTERNOON AT 4:15
## 
### 
Rev. D. C. Gardner, University
Chaplain, will lead the regular
Thursday afternoon vesper service
in the Chapel at 4:15 o'clock. War
ren D. Allen. University organist,
will he assisted by Lewis New
bauer, baritone soloist.
The program will he composed
of chorale preludes by J. S. Bach,
and is as follows: "O Sacred Head,

Now Wounded," "A Saving Health
to Us Is Brought," "Lord, Hear the
Voice of My Complaint," "We All
Believe in One God, Creator,"
"Have Mercy on Me, Lord," "in
Thee Is Gladness." .
