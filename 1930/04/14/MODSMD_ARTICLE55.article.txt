# JUNIOR GOLF TEAM NOSES OUT SENIORS
## 
### 
The Junior class defeated the
Seniors in a final count taken at
the close of the annual interclass
golf tournament last Friday, grab
bing a low score of 1501 to 1545
in an aggregate 0f.324 holes played
by nine-man teams. Neither the
sophomores nor yearlings had nine
men entered and consequently do
not earn points toward the inter
class trophy.
