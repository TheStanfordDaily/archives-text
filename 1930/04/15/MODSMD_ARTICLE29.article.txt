# LARGE FEMALE SNAKE MISSING FROM MUSEUM
## 
### 
LONG BKACH, April It. (/P)
Somebody stole a female snake
three and a half times as long as
a sedan. • ,
That today was the belief of po
lice who were called in to help
hunt for Powerful Katrinka, thirty
five foot python, missing since last
night from a beach museum here.
Katrinka eats nothing but live
pigs, her owner said. But nobody
believed tliat so thoroughly as to
engage in-the hunt, without guns,
clubs, and other weapons.
News that the snake was missing
reduced Jo zero attendance at the
amusement zone.
