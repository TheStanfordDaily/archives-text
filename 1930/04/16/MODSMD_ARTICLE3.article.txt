# CAP AND GOWN PLANS SANTA CRUZ OUTING
## 
### 
At the first spring quarter meet
in.U of Cap and down, held at the
home of Mrs. Elsie Fowler Monday
afternoon, plans were made for the
Cap and Gown Week-end which
will be spent at the Thomas and
Roth cabins near Santa Cruz, April
2-V27. Cars will leave both Friday
and Saturday.
