# SHAKESPEARE SCHOLAR DIES
## 
### 
PHILADELPHIA, April ir». (/P) —
Dr. Horace Howard Furness Jr., <15,
noted Shakespearean editor and
scholar, died suddenly at. his home
here today. Dr. Furness' father
was a world-famous authority on
Shakespeare and the son took up
his work when the elder Furness
died.
