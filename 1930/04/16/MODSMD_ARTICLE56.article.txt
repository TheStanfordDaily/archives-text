# FRIDAY LUNCHEON TO HONOR CLUB'S GUESTS
## 
### 
The Upsilon chapter of Pi
Lambda Tlieta, women's education
al fraternity, is giving a luncheon
for Miss Jessie Hill, formerly na
tional visitor of that fraternity, on
Friday noon at the Union.
The luncheon is open to all mem
bers of the organization for the
purpose of meeting Miss Hill. Miss
Isabel Watkins, president, will pre
side.
After the luncheon a conference
will take place in which only mem
bers of the executive committee of
the chapter will participate.
