# NEW 'CHAPARRAL' WILL BE SOLD ON QUAD TODAY, 25c
## 
### 
Chappies out today! "Get
it out of your mail box. before
your roommate sees it! Buy it.
on Quad for 25c «o you will
'own your own!'" says the
Athletic Board of Control.
Taking upon itself part of
the burden of student theo
logical education, this issue of
Chaparral lias added to the
frosh bible a feature entitled
"Gospel according to St. Hor
ner." This is for the general
A.S.S.U. (and interested alum
ni) enlightenment as to what
really happened when Un
kind-hearted U.C. men "gave
'em the Axe."
Secrets of men's rushing
will be laid bare to student
eyes and the watchful Inter
fraternity Council this morn
ing. Both those being rushed
and those rushing who want
to get 011 the boat should
know how (and why) the
other fellows are doing it.
Needless to say, the women
may come in for a little edu
cation on the subject, too.
