# AXE SONGS CONTINUE TO BE SENT A.S.S.U.
## 
### 
Manuscript contributions continue
to pour in in response to the Execu
tive Committee's request for a new
Stanford song commemorating the
recovery of Stanford's Axe, to be sung
at rallies and athletic contests,
No definite time limit has as yet
been set upon the contest. Manu
scripts should he sent to the Asso
ciated Students office in the Old In -
ion. The winner of the contest will
have his name down in the annals
of Stanford history as the author of
the Stanford Axe Song.
