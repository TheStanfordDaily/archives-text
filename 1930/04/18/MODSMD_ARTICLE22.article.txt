# SIX BANKS FORCED SHUT IN NEBRASKA
## 
### 
LINCOLN, Neb., April 17. (/p> -
Six small hanks in Saunders conn
ty, with deposits aggregating $1
400,00(1, have been Hosed within
the last two days.
They were part of a chain in
which F. «T. Kirehman had the eon
trolling interest.
The break came when the Saun
ders County National Bank, with
deposits of half a million dollars,
atid the Nebraska State Savings
Bank, both in Wahoo, shut their
doors.
