# WOMEN'S GLEE CLUB WILL SING TUESDAY
## 
### 
Under the direction of Miss Mac-
Crumm the Women's Glee Club is
working on songs to be given at the
installation of the new A.VY.S. offi
cers next Tuesday. The selections to
be sung are: "La Golondrina," by
Narciso Serradell: "Japanese Love
Song," Clayton Thomas; "To a Wild
Rose,' by McDowell.
