# LEWIS DENIED WRIT
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., April 2Ji.
—Federal Judge Edward E. Cush
man today denied the petition of
S. C. Lewis, former president of
the Julian Petroleum Corporation
| of Los Angeles, for a writ of habeas
corpus to prevent his removal from
McNeil island penitentiary to Cali
fornia.
