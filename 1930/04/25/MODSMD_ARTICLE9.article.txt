# FIVE NEW TRAINING PLANES BOUGHT BY SCHOOL OF AVIATION
## 
### 
Five new Fleet training planes,
painted in Stanford colors, will be
added to the equipment of the Palo
Alto School of Aviation as soon as
the shipment arrives from the East,
according to S. G. Whittelscy, vice
president of fhe Associated Air
Services, the new owners of the
school. The planes are of the same
type used for army und navy train
ing purposes.
Work on the new steel-frame
hangar will be speeded up so that
the structure will be ready for the
new ships when they arrive. The
new building will be capable of
housing 25 training planes for a
Iri-motor ship, Whittelscy stated.
