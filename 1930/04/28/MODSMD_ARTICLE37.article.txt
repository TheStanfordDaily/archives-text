# TRACK STARS IN RAISIN DAY RELAYS
## 
### 

Al Pogolotti, captain of California's track team who tied Podge Smith in the low hurdles in 24.6; Rus
sell Sweet, sprinting ace of the Olympic Club; Jesse Mortenson, javelin heaver of the University of South
ern California; and Herman Brix, shot-put star of the Los Angeles Athletic Club. These athletes were seen
in action in the West Coast Relays at Fresno.
