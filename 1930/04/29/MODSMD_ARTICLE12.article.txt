# REHEARSAL TONIGHT FOR JUNIOR OPERA
## 
### 
Full rehearsal for the Junior
Opera will be held tonight in the
Assembly Hull at. 7 o'clock. This
practice requires the full cast in
cluding both the principals and
(lionises for the first and second
acls. Because of the Ireture selied
-11 lod for 8 o'clock, rehearsal will
only last about half an hour.
The Stevedore and Gangster male
choruses will meet at 4 o'clock to
morrow afternoon in tlie Assembly
