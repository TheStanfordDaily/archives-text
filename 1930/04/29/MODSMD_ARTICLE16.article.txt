# MAY 1 LAST DAY TO PLACE SENIOR PROGRAM ORDERS
## 
### 
Thursday, May Ist, is the
last day on which orders for
Senior Programs and An
nouncements may be placed
at the Bookstore.
Programs will cost one dol
lar, and graduation announce
ments ten cents each. Orders
may be placed from 8 to 5
o'clock at the stationery coun
ter in the Bookstore until
Thursday night.
