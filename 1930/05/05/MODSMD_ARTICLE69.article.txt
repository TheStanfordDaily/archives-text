liar and Professor of Chemistry,
was elected to the city council ot'
Palo Alto by tin* city's novenunent
Thursday night to replace Dr.
llarry U. Reynolds, who resigned
the position recently.
Dr. Mitchell received eight votes
out ot' the total thirteen cast. Rey
nold's resignation was tendered in
order that he might make an ex
