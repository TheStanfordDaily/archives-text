# TICKETS ON SALE FOR JUNIOR PROM TODAY AT UNION
## 
### 
Tickets for the Junior Prom
to be given by the Class of
1931 in the Pavilion on Friday
night, May 16, go on sale this
morning in the A.S.S.U. office.
The price is $5.00 and the sale
for the first two days will be
limited to juniors only.
No dues are being collected
from the members of the jun
ior class this year, and conse
quently all students who have
between 90 and units to
their credit are eligible to pur
chase prom tickets at the ad
vance sale.
All tickets not purchased by
members of the class of '31
will lie placed on general sale
Thursday morning and will re
main on sale until all are sold.
The number of tickets avail
able is limited to 800.
The A.S.S.U. office in the
Old Union is open from 8 to
12 a.m. and from I to 5 p.m.
