# KING LAYS WAGER
## 
### 
NEWMARKET, Eng., May 6. (/P)
King George placed a wager on
a horse today, for the novelty of
working one of the new betting
machines. When His Majesty ar
rived for the races he became
greatly interested in the machines
which were only recently installed.
After listening to the explanation
of how betting is done through the
machine the King purchased a
ticket.
