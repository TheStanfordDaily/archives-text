# CANDY, PEANUTS TO BE SOLD TO WORKERS TODAY
## 
### 
Labor Day committees have
not neglected refreshments
for the workers, and those
who come out today will find
cigarettes, ice cream, gum,
frozen orange juice, and pea
nuts on sale.
Booths will be placed in the
old Stanford l-loine and on the
grounds. Women have been
appointed to canvass the
workers and supply them all.
Donations have been made
by Wilson's, Peninsula Cream
ery, Home Dairy, Stanford
Union, Nipa Hut, and Camel
and Lucky Strike manufac
turers.
Proceeds will be given to
the Home, to which about
$100 was donated in this man
ner last year.
