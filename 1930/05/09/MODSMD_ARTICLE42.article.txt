# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
5:30 p.m.—Cosmopolitan Club will
meet at Shanghai Low Restau
rant, 532 Grant Avenue, San Fran
cisco, for dinner and tour of
Chinatown.
7:80 p.m. An examination for the
removal of conditions incurred
in Mining Engineering 105 will
be held in room 550, Mining
building, from 7:30 to 51:30
o'clock. I
8:30 p.m- Cosmopolitan Club as
sembles at Capitol Theatre in
San Francisco as guests of Mei
La n-Fang.
SATURDAY
C :30 p.m. Stanford Dames party
for their husbands at Women's
Clubhouse. Cards and program
will follow.
SUNDAY
COSMOPOLITAN CLUR postpones
Sunday dinner.
MISCELLANEOUS
STUDENTS wishing to try out for
managerial positions on the
A.S.S.U. Concert Series will meet
at 8 o'clock Thursday evening at
the Concert Series office in the
Old Union.
MEN NOW LIVING in Toyon and
Rranner halls who wish to re
serve their rooms for fall quar
ter should call at the Dean of
Men's office Monday or Tuesday,
May 12 or 13, and pay deposits.
Room drawings will be held in
the offices of the respective halls
Wednesday, May 14, at 7:30 p.m.
ALL STUDENTS who expect to
complete the work required for a
teaching credential at the end of
spring or summer quarter should
have formal application on file
with the Secretary of the Edu
cation Department, Room 52, not
later than May 17.
