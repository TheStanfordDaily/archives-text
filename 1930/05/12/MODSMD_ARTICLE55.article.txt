# SENIOR CLASS DUES COLLECTION BEGUN
## 
### 
Collection of senior duos from
men living iu Toyon Hall began
loday under the direction of Carl
Conradi, Ralph Malcolm, and Alex
Morris, members of the finance
committee of the Class of 1930.
Seniors residing in Toyon are
urged to get in touch with these
men immediately so that prompt
payment of dues may be made.
All seniors are urged to send a
check for $1.50 for duos to "Senior
Class Treasurer, Box 2336, Stan
ford University," or make payment
at the A.S.S.U. office in the Old
Union.
