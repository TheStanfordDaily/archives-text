# GOLFERS COMPETE
## 
### 
EUGENE, Oregon, May 12. (TP) —
George Will, captain of Oregon's
northern division championship
golf team, and Vincent Dolph, a
member of the team, will leave to
morrow for Los Angeles where
they will compete in the Pacific
Coast Conference title play at Los
Angeles Thursday, Friday, and
Saturday.
