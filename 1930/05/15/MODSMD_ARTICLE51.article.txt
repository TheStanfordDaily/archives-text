# SOCCER TEAM TO ELECT LEADER AT LUNCHEON TODAY
## 
### 
Members of the soccer squad
will hold their annual banquet
at noon today in the Sigma
Chi house. Next year's captain
is to be chosen at the meet
ing, and another feature will
be the presentation of the
award for the man who has
contributed the lflost to the
success of the team.
This award, a gold medal, is
part of the new group of
minor sports awards donated
this year by James B. Howell
of Atherton. The recipient will
not be announced until the
banquet.
