# GLEE CLUB
## 
### 
The Stanford Glee Club, under
the direction of C. Albert Kulmann,
will appear at the New Stanford
Theatre Saturday night, according
to Earl Reed, manager. The Glee
Club members will form a part of
the regular Saturday evening
vaudeville program.
