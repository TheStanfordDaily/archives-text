# ROBLE TO GIVE LAST JOLLY-UP TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Freshman Class will hold its
last official jolly-up of the quarter
tomorrow night from 7-8 at Roble.
The orchestra has not as yet been
engaged. Edith Neff will be in
charge of the dance. The commit-
tee which has planned the jolly-ups
of this quarter has consisted of
Mary Kay Morris, Belly Lawton,
Barbara Balfour, and Mary Roche-
fort, chairman.
