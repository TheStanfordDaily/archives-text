# SHAMROCK WINS
## 
### 
SOUTH END, ISngland. May 21.
(/p) - Winning her fourth successive
race. Sir Thomas Lipton's new
yacht, Shamrock V, finished first
today In a 40-mile sail off this port
by a margin of about 12 minute.
