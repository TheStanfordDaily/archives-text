# PORTLAND CITIZENS GO ON GOOD WILL TOUR IN LAUNCHES
## 
### 
LEWISTON, Idaho, May 21. </P)
A "good will" delegation of Port
land, Oregon, citizens, traveling in
a fleet of launches up the Colum
bia and Snake rivers, is expected
here Saturday.
The boats will leave Portland
early Friday morning, according to
v. ord received here, and will spend
Friday night at Pasco. The trip is
intended to promote public interest
in the restoration of navigation on
the Columbia and Snake rivers.
An Elaborate program of enter
tainment for the visitors i.s being
planned here.
