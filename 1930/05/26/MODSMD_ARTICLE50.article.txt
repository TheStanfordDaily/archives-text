# FORBID SUNDAY ROLLS
## 
### 
NEW BRUNSWICK. N.May
25. (/P) — There will be 110 hot rolls
and bread for this blue law abiding
city on Sundays. Cnder New Jer
sey's old "blue law" only milk can
be distributed on the sabbath.
