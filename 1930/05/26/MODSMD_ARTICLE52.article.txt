# CLASS VIEWS HOME FOR FEEBLE-MINDED
## 
### 
A clinic and inspection of the
grounds and inmates of the Sonoma
Slate Home near Sonoma was
given for :!5 members of Dr. M. A.
Merrill's class in Mental Deficiency
and Delinquency Saturday. The
clinic, which was held in the morn
ing, was given by Drs. Smith and
Ordahl and consisted of the demon
stration and explanation of differ
ent types of feebleminded and phys
ical defective individuals.
The afternoon was devoted to a
lour of the institution and further
inspection of clinical types.
