# COSMOPOLITAN CLUB TO HOLD BARBECUE FRIDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
As the concluding social event of
the school year, the Cosmopolitan
Club will hold its annual picnic and
barbecue at Soarsville Lake Friday
afternoon at 4:30.
According to the committee in
charge, there will be swimming,
boating, volley-ball, and baseball
games. Sam McDonald, honorary
member of the organization, will
prepare and serve the barbecued
meal.
All members are urged to get in
touch with Pardee Lowe, John
Way, Alice Behner, Denny Wood
worth, or Margaret Murray if they
care to attend. Transportation will
be provided.
