# WOMEN TO HAVE FIELD DAY JUNE 4
## 
### 
The women's Field Day will be
held on June 4 and will last all
afternoon. Among the drawing
cards will be tennis matches, swim
ming events, archery rounds, and
fencing, exhibitions. It is expected
that some of the previous swim
ming records may he broken, and,
as this is the first time in several
years that there has been a fenc
ing exhibition, the even! will he of
unusual interest.
A barbecue supper will he served
afterwards in the gymnasium at
which the all-star teams will be an
nounced and next year's managers
will be elected.
