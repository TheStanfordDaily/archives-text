# DENY CIGARETTE TO 6 FOOT 6 INCH BOY
## 
### 
OMAHA, Neb., May 28. (/P) -Carl
Pratt raised his 235 pounds and
stood up Id his six feet six inches
til height from a hard jail bench to
ask for a cigarette.
"You can't have one," ruled
Chief of Detectives Franks.
There is no rule against prison
ers smoking in their cells. Franks
explained, but Pratt is only sixteen
and didn't have a note from his
parents.
