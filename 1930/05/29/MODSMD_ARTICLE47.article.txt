THURSDAY
3:30 p.m. The Stanford Dames will
not meet this week. Next week
Mrs. Elizabeth B. Snell of the ap
pointment office will speak.
7:15 p.m.— I The Walrus Club meets
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
7:MO p.m.—Last meeting of the
Stanford Newman Club in the
Women's Clubhouse downstairs.
FRIDAY
4 to 4:30 p.m. Cosmopolitan Club
meets in front of Students' Union
for trip to Searsville Lake picnic
and barbecue.
MISCELLANEOUS
MEN who wish to take out or re
new tuition notes for the summer
and fall quarters should call at
the Dean of Men's Office during
the week of June 2 to <1.
TRACK CLASSES meet. Monday,
Wednesday, and Friday.
STUDENTS who expect to enter
Law School either at the begin
ning of summer or autumn quar
ters should file application blanks
with the Secretary of the Law '
School, Room 1, immediately.
