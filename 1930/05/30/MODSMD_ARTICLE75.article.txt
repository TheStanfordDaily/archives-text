# COMMENCEMENT WEEK PROGRAM AS ANNOUNCED LATE YESTERDAY
## 
### 
Following is the complete program of Commencement Week
events announced last night. The Class Day program and the annual
meeting of Phi Beta Kappa on Saturday and the Organ Recital on
Sunday will be the only part of the week's activities open to the
public. On Baccalaureate Sunday and on Commencement Day, ad
mission to the Memorial Church will be by ticket only.
