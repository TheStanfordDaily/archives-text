# SMUGGLES DOPE
## 
### 
SAN I-RANG I SCO, May 29—(/P) :
»—All Yuen, seaman on the Dutch!
tanker Phoebus, arrested for smug
gling narcotics, would have Federal
authorities believe Honorable bate
played a cruel trick upon him.
Through an interpreter, he said he in
tended to take the narcotics from
Shanghai to Swatow, but instead of
going to Swatow the Phoebus came
