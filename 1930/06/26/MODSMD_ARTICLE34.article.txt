# ADVISORS CLUB PLANS DISCUSSION MEETINGS
## 
### 
The second regular meeting of the
Advisors Club, whose purpose is for
the study of deanships and problems
in the education of girls and women,
will be held 7:20 Monday evening in
the Women's Clubhouse. The club is
composed of members of the dean's
course under Miss Jessie Gibson of
Pomona.
At the first meeting last Monday a
program committee was elected.
Plans were made for informal discus
sions and for speakers on educational
problems.
All those interested are invited to
[attend the meetings.
