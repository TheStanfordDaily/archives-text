# ORCHESTRA SECURES CONDUCTOR; HOLDS REHEARSAL TONIGHT
## 
### 
Mrs. Margaret Me Man us, conductor
of flic Oregon State College symphony
orchestra, has been secured to act as
Kuest conductor of the Stanford or
chestra, according to Elwyn Bugge,
director.
The first rehearsal of the orchestra
will be hold at 7:30 o'clock tonight in
Room 84 Inner Quadrangle. Bugge
urges that all players of symphonic
instruments come out. He particularly
wants players of string instruments.
Work will be directed toward a con
cert to be given at the end of the
quarter.
