# TICKETS FOR PLAY TO GO ON SALE THIS AFTERNOON
## 
### 
The seat sale for "Pygmalion,"
to be given Friday evening at
8:30, will begin at 1 o'clock to
day at the desk in front of the
Women's Clubhouse. After the
preliminary sale, tickets can be
procured at the A.S.S.I!, in the
Old Union. They will also be
sold in the Roble lobby after
lunch today.
Tickets, which vary in price
from 75 cents to $1.25, will also
be available at Dodson's in Palo
Alto.
Men who wish to usher at the
play are asked to report to Vic
tor Siegfried in the Assembly
Hall at 7:30 Friday evening.
