# DR. FISH TO TELL OF NEW WISCONSIN PLAN
## 
### 
Dr. C. R. Fish, visiting professor of
history, will he the speaker at the
regular Wednesday education lunch
eon which will be held at noon in
the Union cafeteria. Professor Fish
will speak on the plan of the Univer
sity of Wisconsin whereby a superior
student will be enabled to receive his
master's degree after four years of
college work. Professor Fish is chair
man of the faculty committee at Wis
consin which is working on the plan.
