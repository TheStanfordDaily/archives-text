# BROADWAY TICKET SALE TO BEGIN ON CAMPUS THIS NOON
## 
### 
Tickets for "Broadway," which
is to be given Friday evening,
will go on sale at 1 o'clock this
afternoon both in front of the
Women's Clubhouse and at Dod
son's in Palo Alto. Prices of
seats will be as follows: main
floor, $1.25; center balcony, $1;
and side balcony, 75c. There will
also be unreserved balcony seats
sold at tlie box office on the eve
ning of the performance for 50c.
There will be a special sale in
the Roble lobby after lunch to
day. Tickets can be bought
throughout the week at. the
A.S.S.IJ. offices in the Old Union.
The box office will open at 7
o'clock Friday evening.
