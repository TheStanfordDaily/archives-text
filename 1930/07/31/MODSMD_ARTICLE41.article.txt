# ALUMNUS TO MARRY
## 
### 
I Miss Frances Moore of Palo Alto
and James A. Watson, '28, are to be
1 married Saturday, August 2, in the
Stanford Memorial Church. Miss
Moore,'who is the daughter of Mr.
and Mrs. George B. Moore of Palo
Alto, is a graduate of San Jose State
College. Watson is the son of Mrs.
J. A. Watson of Palo Alto.
