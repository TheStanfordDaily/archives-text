# STUDENTS MARRIED IN HOLLISTER THURSDAY
## 
### 
Miss E. Verna Hannah and Harris
R. Skelton, enrolled for graduate work
in the summer quarter, sprang a sur
prise on their friends by going to
Hollister Thursday and being married.
The ceremony was solemnized at the
Christian Church of that place by the
pastor.
Mrs. Skelton is taking her master's
degree in education this quarter, and
will return to Sacramento in Septem
ber where she is a member of the
high-school faculty, while Mr. Skelton
continues graduate work in economics
at Stanford.
