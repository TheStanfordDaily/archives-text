# WILBUR WILL ADDRESS COMMONWEALTH CLUB
## 
### 
Dr. Hay Lyman Wilbur, Secretary
of the Interior, who has been at Fort
Duquesne. Utah, attending a confer
ence on Indian affairs, will return in
time to address the San Francisco
Commonwealth Club tomorrow noon.
"A Progress Report" is the title of Ills
talk.
While here he will confer with di
rectors of the Imperial Irrigation dis
trict and the Coachella Valley water
users relative to financing the all-
American canal.
