# EDWARD KELLY WILL GIVE ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Edward Kelly, a pupil of Warren I>.
Allen, University organist, will give
a recital in the Memorial Church at
4:15 Tuesday afternoon. Kelly has
just returned from a year's work in
Paris with Marcel Dupre, famous or
gan composer who played on the cam
pus last spring at a student assembly.
The following program has been
selected: four selections by J. S.
Bach, including Prelude and Fugue
in E Minor, Prelude in C Major,
and two choral preludes, "A Saving
Health to Us Is Brought" and "Lord,
Hear the Voice of My Complaint";
"Cantilena," Carl McKinkey; and
"Piece lleroique," Cesar Franck.
