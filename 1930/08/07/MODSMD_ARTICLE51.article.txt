# MORE WOMEN NEEDED FOR TENNIS MATCHES
## 
### 
Enough women have indicated their
intention of participating to insure
that the swimming meet and the
archery tournament will be held Tues
day, August 19. Margaret MacDougall,
chairman of tlie program, will make
definite announcements next week'
concerning the events.
The tennis tournament will not be
held unless eight more women sign
up on the notices posted in the wo
men's gymnasium and on the Roble
bulletin boards. The lists will be left
up until four o'clock tonight, and
other women students who are inter
ested are urged to sign up.
