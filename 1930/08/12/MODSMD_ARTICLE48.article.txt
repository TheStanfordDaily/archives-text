# HORST TRAINS BALLET FOR MENLO PAGEANT
## 
### 
Vincent Duffoy, who lias done work
in connection with the Bohemian and
Family Club productions of San
Francisco, will direct the lighting of
the pageant to he given at the Menlo
Park Circus Club August 23. Betty
Horst, San Francisco dancer, former
ly associated with Ruth St. Denis and
Ted Shawn, will be in charge of the
diinees for "A Midsummer Night's
Dream," the play to be presented. An
orchestra of twenty-five pieces will
he directed by Carol Weston.
Shakespeare's fantasy is the first
of a series of outdoor productions
which the auxiliary board or the
Stanford Convalescent Home now
plans to give for its annual benefits
instead of the traditional circus.
