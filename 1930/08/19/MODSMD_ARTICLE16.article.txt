# FLEISHHACKER TO BE COACH
## 
### 
Herb Fleishhacker, '30, has been
appointed assistant coach of the
Menlo Junior College football team.
As quarterback on the varsity, Fleish
hacker was for three years one of
the outstanding backs on the coast.
At Menlo he will assist Dud DeGroot,
who is a former Stanford football
captain.
