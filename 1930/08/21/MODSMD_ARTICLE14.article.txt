# POSTMASTER ASKS FOR ADDRESSES AS QUARTER CLOSES
## 
### 
All summer quarter students
who plan to leave the campus at
the end of the month should file
a forwarding order a day or so
before departure, according to
an announcement from the post
office. Publishers of magazines
or newspapers from whom litera
ture is being received should
also be notified of change of ad
dress.
Those who wish to reserve
their box for next quarter may
do so at the money order win
dow. Money will not be accepted
until after the middle of next
month. Present box leases will
expire September 30.
