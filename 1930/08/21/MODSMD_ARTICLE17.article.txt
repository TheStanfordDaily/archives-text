## 
###

men's Gymnasium will he kept open
for recreation purposes only after the
new gymnasium is completed, accord
ing to present plans. The pool is still
in good condition and will he used in
;lliis manner until a time when a spe
cial recreation pool and a diving pool
can he installed in tlie new gym.

ately in the new gym will be used for
the most part for instruction purposes
only.
