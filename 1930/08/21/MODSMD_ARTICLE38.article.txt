# EXCUSES FOR LOW SCHOLARSHIP
## 
### 
Students whose work has been
interfered with during the cur
rent quarter by illness, or other
causes for which allowance can
he made by the committee on
scholarship, may report the spe
cial circumstances to Mrs. Mar
garet I>. Huston .secretary of the
committee, at the Registrar's Of
fice. Excuses should be submitted
not later than August 30 in order
to be assured of consideration
before action is taken on the end
quarter reports.
Requests for allowance on ac
count of illness must be supple
mented by the special physician's
certificate furnished by the com
mittee.
