# HULME TO ADDRESS MEETING
## 
### 
Professor Edward Maslin Hulme of
the Department of History will ad
dress the women's committee of the
San Francisco Opera Association at
the Fairmont Hotel at 2:30 o'clock
Tuesday afternoon. His subject, will
be "Art and Its Relation to Life," with
special reference to opera. The meet
ing is open to all who are interested
in music.
