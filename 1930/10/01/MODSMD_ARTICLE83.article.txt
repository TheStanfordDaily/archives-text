# HELVENSTON PLANS THREE-ACT COMEDY NEXT CAMPUS PLAY
## 
### 
"Mrs. Bumpstead-Leigh," a com
edy in three acts by Harry James
Smith, will he the opening produc
tion of the Stanford dramatic sea
son.
Everyone who is interested in
acting is urged by Harold Helven
ston, acting director of campus
dramatics, to try out for the cast
of the play. First tryouts will be
held tonight at 7:15 o'clock in the
Assembly Hall. Second tryouts wili
he tomorrow afternoon at 4 o'clock
and finals tomorrow evening at
7:15 o'clock. These will also take
place in the Assembly Hall. Cata
loguing of the results of the try
outs will he made for reference in
casting future plays.
This comedy calls for a variety
of ages and types. The roles range
from a pseudo-society matron to a
rancher from New Mexico. There
are twelve parts to be filled.
