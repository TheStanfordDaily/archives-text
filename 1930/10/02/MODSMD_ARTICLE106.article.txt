# GERMANS LOSE TO ITALIANS IN TENNIS
## 
### 
FLORENCE, Italy, Oct. 1 (Asso
ciated Press). —Italy got the jump
on Germany today in the opening
play of their tennis tournament,
winning two of the three matches.
Sertorio of Italy defeated Zulmann
jf Germany, 6-4, 6-4, 6-8, 6-4, while
De Stefani, Italian Davis cup player,
downed his German opponent,
Frenz, 6-0, 4-6, 6-0, 6-0. Buss won
the only match for the Germans, de
feating De Minerbi, 7-5, 6-4, 6-3.
