# FIRST ASSEMBLY AT 11 TOMORROW
## 
### 
All students of the Univer
sity, old and new, will meet
for the first time at the first
general assembly of the year,
which will be held tomorrow
at 11 o'clock in the Assembly
Hall.
Acting-President Swain will
make a speech in which he will
welcome new members to the
University. All students are
urged to attend.
