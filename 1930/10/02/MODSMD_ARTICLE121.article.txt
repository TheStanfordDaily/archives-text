# RECEPTION FOR NEW STUDENTS TOMORROW
## 
### 
Freshmen and all other new mem
bers of the Stanford student body
will have their first chance to get
acquainted with each other tomor
row night when Acting-President
and Mrs. Robert Eckles Swain will
be at home informally to all new
students at 8 o'clock at the Presi
dent's House, Lagunita Knoll.
All students who are entering
upon their first quarter in the Uni
versity are invited to be present.
There will be music for those who
care to dance.
