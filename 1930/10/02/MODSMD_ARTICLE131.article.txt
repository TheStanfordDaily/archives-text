# LARGEST DIRIGIBLE
## 
### 
CARDINGTON, Eng., Oct. 1 (/P).
—The Dirigible R-101, which is the
iargeti. airship in (he world since it
recently was lengthened, left its
mooring mast at 4:40 p.m. today, on
a short trial trip preparatory to a
voyage to India.
