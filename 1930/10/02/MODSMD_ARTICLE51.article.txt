# FIRST Y.W.C.A. TEA OF SERIES IS HELD
## 
### 
The new women were introduced
to the old yesterday at a tea given
in the Women's Clubhouse from 4
to (J o'clock. Some 130 new women
attended the affair, the first of its
kind this year. Ruth Lockett and
Mary Edith Jones are in charge of
the teas which will be given during
the year in the Y.W.C.A. rooms on
the third floor of the Clubhouse,
according to Lillian Schuck, presi
dent of the Y.W.C.A.
