# DRAMATIC MANAGER TRYOUT INTERVIEWS ON FRIDAY-DENNEY
## 
### 
All sophomores and juniors in
terested in trying out for dramatic
manager will be interviewed by A 1
Denney, dramatic manager, in the
dramatic office, Old Union, at 1:30
o'clock Friday afternoon.
Tryout period will last one year
after which one man will be selected
as manager from June 1931 to June
1932. The dramatic managership
pays on a straight salary basis for
eleven months out of the year of
incumbency.
No special requirements are de
manded except interest in the finan
cial management of amateur play
production, and some knowledge of
accounting is desirable.
