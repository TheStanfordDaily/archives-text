# LIGHTH GRANDCHILD IS BORN TO WILBURS
## 
### 
Word has been received of the
birth of a son on September 21 to
Dr. and Mis. Dwight Locke Wilbur,
at Rochester, Minnesota,, where Dr.
Wilbur is connected with the Mayo
Clinic. The baby is the eighth
grandchild of Dr. and Mrs. Ray Ly
man Wilbur, and has been named
Dwight Locke Wilbur 111 for its
father and great-grandfather
Dr. and Mrs. Wilbur are both
Stanford graduates. They were mar
ried two years ago in the Memorial
Chapel. Mrs. Wilbur was formerly
Ruth Jordan.
