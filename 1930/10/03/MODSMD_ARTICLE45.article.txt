# WOMEN BETTER STUDENTS THAN STANFORD ROUGHS
## 
### 
Women students on the campus lead the men in scholastic ranking
by .521 point, according to the 1929-30 report rating the various organiza
tions and living groups on the campus. Non-fraternity and non-sorority
groups lead sorority and fraternity by a slight margin. This new report
institutes a new method of rating whereby the organizations are classified
according to their averages as compared with the University average.
In the report that follows, the plus and minus figures in the left-hand
margin show the status of the group in relation to the University average,
—1 just below. The range between average and the highest possible mark
has been divided into ten equal divisions. Negative ranks extend below
the University average by divisions of the same size as those used for the
positive ranking.
The University average is 1.545; the highest possible mark is 3.000.
