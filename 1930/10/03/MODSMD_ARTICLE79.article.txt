# CALL IS ISSUED FOR MANAGER OF TENNIS
## 
### 
First call for sophomore tennis
managers was issued yesterday by
Herb Hazeltine, senior tennis man
ager. All second year men desir
ing to tryout may report to Hazel -
tine today from two to two-thirty
o'clock at the varsity tennis courts.
Working as a sophomore tennis
manager will fulfill the lower di
vision physical education require
ment. Men already enrolled in gym
will be transfered upon reporting
to Hazeltine. The sophomore tennis
managership requires three after
