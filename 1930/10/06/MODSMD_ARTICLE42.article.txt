# NEGRO ACTOR SHOT
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH. Oct. 5. (Associ
ated Press) —Bill Robinson,
old Negro theatrical star, was shot
and wounded today by a policeman
who mistook him for the Negro
purse-snatcher Robinson was pur
suing.
