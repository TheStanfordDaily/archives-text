# EXECUTIVES' MEETING SET FOR TOMORROW
## 
### 
Meeting in the Council Room of
the Old Union at 7 o'clock tomorrow
night, the Executive Committee of
the Associated Students will gather
lor the first time of the 1930-31
scholastic year.
All business to come up before
the Committee should be referred
to the A.S.S.U. offices today or to
morrow. The Executive Committee
consists this year of: Thomas Pike,
chairman: Robert Brown, vice
chairman; Robert Perry, secretary;
Virgil Dowell, graduate member;
Surah Grant, senior member; and
Austin Clapp, Harold Allen, and
Miriam Strout, junior members.
