# THETA SIGMA PHI WILL MEET TODAY
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi. women's nation
al honorary journalistic society, will
hold a meeting at 5 o'clock this
afternoon in the Women's Club
house.
The purpose of the meeting is the
election of a new president and a
new treasurer for the coming year,
according to Mary Edith Jones. All
members are requested to attend.
