# CARD CHESS TEAM MEETS TO DISCUSS SEASON'S PROGRAM
## 
### 
Stanford Chess Club will hold its
second meeting this quarter Thurs
day evening in the lobby of the Old
Union. Angelo May, captain of the
club, urges all Stanford men and
women to attend.
The Varsity Chess team is to hold
a practice game in preparation for
games with St. Ignatius and Cali
fornia. The first move of the game
with California is to be determined.
In addition, a Freshman team to
engage in competitive Freshman
matches is to be chosen.
The Chess Club now boasts a
membership of almost fifty, having
been reorganized last year by an
enterprising group of young pawn
pushers.
