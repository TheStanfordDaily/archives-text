# RAGAN SECURED AS FULL TIME VARSITY TENNIS INSTRUCTOR
## 
### 
Services of Frank Ragan as full
time varsity tennis coach have been
secured by the University for the
coming year. Ragan, who formerly
was professional at the California
Tennis Club in San Francisco and
was able to put in only a part of
his time on the Stanford courts,
will now be in position to devote
all his efforts to the Cardinal var
sity.
Besides team coaching Ragan'
intends to give private lessons to
students. He enjoys a widespread
reputation as tennis teacher and has
developed many fine players. Dur
ing the past summer season he was
the professional at the famous old
Gcrmantown Cricket Club near
Philadelphia.
