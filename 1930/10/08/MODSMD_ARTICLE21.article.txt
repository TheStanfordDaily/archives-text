# DEMOLAY CLUB WILL HOLD OPEN MEETING
## 
### 
Members of (he Stanford DeMolay
club will hold their first meeting
tonight in the Y.M.C.A. lobby in the
Old Union at 7:30 o'clock.
Plans for activities of the coming
year will be discussed with Noel
Eldred, elected president last year,
presiding. All new men who arc
DeMolays are invited to attend.
