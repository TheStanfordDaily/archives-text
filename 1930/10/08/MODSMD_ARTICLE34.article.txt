# IMAGINE 3,000 STAFF MEMBERS
## 
### 
There are more than 3,000 staff members of The Stanford
Daily ; each member of the student body is a member of the staff.
The Daily exists for the students. Its columns are open to the
students who have something to contribute that might be of interest
in the remainder of the students.
The Daily's Campus Opinion column is usually the point of
mntact most students have with this publication. Students are
urged to express their views through this medium. There are no
restrictions on comment except that the subject must be judged by
the editor as being of sufficient value as to warrant publication and
all letters of personal criticism must be published with the writer's
name.
