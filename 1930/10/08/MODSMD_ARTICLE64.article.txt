# TRACKMEN PRACTICE FOR FRIDAY'S MEET
## 
### 
With Iho first of this fall's weekly
track meets scheduled for Friday
afternoon, the practice oval is a
scene of activity t his week as 120
track and field athletes round them
selves into condition.
Friday's meet will be strictly in
formal, and all distances will be
shortened, according to Assistant
Coach Bill Ellington, since it is so
early in the season.
The high hurdles will be short
ened to 75 yards, the low barriers
to 120 yards, the mile to three laps,
and other events in proportion.
