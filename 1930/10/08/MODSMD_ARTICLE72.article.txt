# WOMEN'S DEBATE TRYOUTS WILL BE HELD TOMORROW
## 
### 
Tryouts for the women's debate
team will be held tomorrow after
noon at 3:15 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. AH women are eligible
and one unit of credit will be given
those who make the squad. New
women are urged to take part.
Everyone interested in trying out
is asked to prepare arguments for
one side of the question: "Resolved
that the individual should not go to
Junior College in preparation for
University."
The first debate will be with Cali
fornia on November 7. The dates
for the rest have not been set.
