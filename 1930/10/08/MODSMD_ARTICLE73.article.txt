# FIRST MEETING OF RIFLE CLUB TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Stanford Rifle Club will hold
its first meeting for this year tonight
in room 231 in the English Building
at 7:30 o'clock. Officers are to be
elected and plans for the coming
season will be discussed.
Steps have been taken toward se
curing a coach for the team and it
has been suggested that shoulder
to-shoulder matches be held with
the University of California. All
men interested in rifle shooting are
; invited to be present.
