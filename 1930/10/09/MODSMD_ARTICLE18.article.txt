# CHESS TEAMS WILL BE CHOSEN TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Chess Club will meet in the
Y.M.C.A. rooms in the Old Union at
7:30 o'clock tonight to elect officers
for the coming year and choose a
varsity and a freshman team. Each
team will consist of seven members.
A match with St. Ignatius is
scheduled as the first one of the
season to lie played on October 2H.
