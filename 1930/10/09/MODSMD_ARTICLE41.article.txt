# MEETING PLANNED FOR OFF-CAMPUS WOMEN MONDAY
## 
### 
All women students who are not
living in Roble or on the Row this
quarter are cordially invited to at
tend a luncheon meeting to be held
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse at 12 o'clock Monday.
This' will be the first gathering of
the quarter for those women who
do not belong to campus living
groups.
New students are especially urged
to attend. Regular meetings will be
held throughout the year in order
that such women may be organized
for social and business purposes.
Those who plan to go Monday are
asked to make reservations for
luncheon with Lois Ruth Bailey, by
phoning P A. 3934 before Saturday
morning.
