# STUDENT ROLL TO DATE IS IDENTICAL WITH LAST YEAR'S
## 
### 
Exactly as many students have
completed registration in the Uni
versity to date as composed the final
grand total for the autumn quarter
a year ago, and more will be added
in the next few days.
When the Registrar's office closed
yesterday 3,556 names were on the
roll of students who have met all
requirements, while 21 others were
provisionally registered with some
details yet to be finished. The grand
total on the same day last year was
3,503, with 53 more provisional
registrants. In this total for the
quarter were included names of all
students who had registered, among
them some who dropped out of the
University before the end of the
quarter.
