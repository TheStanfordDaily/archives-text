# WEDDING PLACE SET

marriage of Princess Giovanna,
daughter of the king and queen of
Italy, lo Boris, "bachelor king" of
Bulgaria, will be performed at San
Kossore, near Pisa, summer home of
the royal family, on Nov. 15.
