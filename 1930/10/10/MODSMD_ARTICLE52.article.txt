# FELLOWSHIP BOARD WILL SPONSOR TEAS
## 
### 
Stewart Warner, chairman of the
Stanford Fellowship, announced
Tuesday at a meeting of the Fellow
ship Executive Council that the
afternoon teas would begin again on
Monday and will be held as usual
from 4:30 to 5:30 o'clock on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse
every afternoon. The regular hos
tesses for each day have not yet
been definitely appointed.
Visiting professors, new and for
eign students will be especially wel
come at these teas and will find that
they offer an opportunity to get
better acquainted on the campus.
