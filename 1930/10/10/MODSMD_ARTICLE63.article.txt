# DR. GARDNER TALKS AT SUNDAY SERVICE
## 
### 
Reverend Dr. Charles Gardner,
University chaplain, will deliver the
sermon and preside at the service
of divine worship at 11 o'clock Sun
day, October 11, at the Memorial
Church. Music, under the direction
of Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will consist of two anthems,
'Oh. All Ye Nations," by Schutz, and
"Clod Be In My Heart," by Davies.
Mr! Allen will present his semi
weekly organ recital at 4 o'clock in
the afternoon.
