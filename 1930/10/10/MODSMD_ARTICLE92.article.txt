# KRENZ TURNS DOWN NEW ZEALAND TRIP
## 
### 
Eric Krenz, former Cardinal track
and field captain, will not make a
trip to New Zealand in December,
because of business interests, ac
cording to the announcement made
by Krenz yesterday. The world's
record discus tosser was to lead a
team of picked men on an invasion
of the land down under this winter,
but instead has been forced to turn
down the offer.
Harlow Rothert, Intercollegiate
shot-put champion, may take Eric's
place, although definite announce
ment to this effect cannot be made
until Rothert's return from Minne
sota.
