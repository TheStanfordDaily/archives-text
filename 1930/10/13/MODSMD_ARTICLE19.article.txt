# MISS BLACKWELDER ENGAGED TO BAKER
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Eliot Black
welder have announced the engage
ment of their daughter Gertrude to
C. Everett Baker of Long Beach.
Miss Blackwelder is in her second
year at Stanford. Her father is head
of the geology department here.
Baker is now in his fourth year at
the University, and plans to go on
with his studies in law.
