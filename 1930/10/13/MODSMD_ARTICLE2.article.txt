# C.J. CRARY ELECTED PRESIDENT OF BANK
## 
### 
Announcement of the election of
Charles Judson Crary, '03, as presi
dent of the Palo Alto National Bank
to succeed John L. McNab, was
made by the bank Friday. Crary,
who is very prominent in campus
affairs, has had much experience in
banking, having held positions in
the First National Bank of Rich
mond and in the American Trust
Company of San Francisco.
He is a member of the Interfra
ternity Board of Control, president
of the Stanford Alumni Association
of San Francisco, and a member of
Delta Tavi Delta fraternity.
