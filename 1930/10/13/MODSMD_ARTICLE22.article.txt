# TENORS, SOPRANOS REHEARSE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will hold a rehearsal of all
sopranos and tenors of the oratorio
chorus tonight at 8 o'clock in room
84.
The chorus is preparing Brahm's
"Requiem," which is to be sung with
a full orchestra on Tuesday eve
ning, November 11, Armistice Day,
in the Memorial Church.
One unit oratorio credit will be
given to all students who attend the
12 rehearsals, ending November 11.
All who have had any singing ex
perience and can attend the rehear
sals specified may apply for mem
bership in both the oratorio and the
University choir.
