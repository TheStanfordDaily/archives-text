# Y.M.C.A. NAMES MEN OFFICERS, DIRECTORS
## 
### 
At the meeting of the board of
directors of the Stanford Young
Men's Christian Association Friday
Professor C. N. Reynolds, acting
head of the economics department,
was elected chairman of the board
to fill the vacancy made by the res
ignation of Dr. Karl M. Cowdory.
Three new members were added
to the board: O. H. Blackmail, Reg
inald Bell, and Rev. P. L. Offen
heiser.
Frank Hoge was elected chairman
of the finance committee. Ben F.
Culver was re-elected to serve as
executive secretary for the ensuing
year.
