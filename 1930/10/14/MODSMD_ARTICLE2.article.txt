# JORDAN WILL NOT DISCUSS FISH AND GAME AMENDMENT
## 
### 
Because, ill health has kept him
from careful and critical study of
current topics, Dr. David Starr Jor
dan is unable to take part in the
present discussion over the fish and
game initiative measure which will
appear on the November ballot, ac
cording to an authorized statement
from his home. The statement fol
lows:
"As a member of the advisory
board of California Conservation
ists, Dr. Jordan is greatly interested
in the conservation of the fish and
game resources of our state. At the
same time he explains that he is
taking no part in the public contro
versy over the merits of Amendment
No. 11, because his health has un
fortunately not permitted him to
make a thorough study of the points
under critical discussion."
