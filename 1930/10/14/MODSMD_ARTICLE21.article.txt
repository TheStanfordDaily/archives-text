# EMPLOYEE INJURED IN BOILER EXPLOSION
## 
### 
John Curran, employee at the
j Stanford power house, suffered
burns about the face and hands yes
terday morning at 8 o'clock as a re
sult of a backfiash in boiler number
i six. according to Edward Water
man, chief engineer of the power
house.
The backfiash came when the
flame in the oil furnace, which had
gone out, was being re-ignited. The
flame may have gone out from one
of several causes such as the failure
of one of the oil pumps or a dirty
burner, stated employees at the
power house.
Curran is resting easily, accord
ing to the latest report from the
Palo Alto Hospital.
