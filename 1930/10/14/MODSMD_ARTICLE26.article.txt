# AVIATRIX ATTEMPTS TO LOWER RECORD
## 
### 
VALLEY STREAM, N.Y., Oct. 13.
(/P) —Mrs. Keith Miller, British
aviatrix, took off from Curtiss Field
at 6:30 a.m. today for a flight to
Los Angeles in an effort to lower
the women's transcontinental record
recently established by Miss Laura
Ingalls. She planned to make her
first stop at Indianapolis and stop
overnight at Wichita, Kansas.
