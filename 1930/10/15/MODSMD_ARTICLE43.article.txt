# PERSIAN DELEGATE INSPECTS FARMING
## 
### 
HANFOED, Oct. 14. (/!')—M. H.
Farchi, member of the Persian min
istry of agriculture, recently visited
Hanford in the course of a survey
of California agricultural conditions
and marketing problems. He was
much impressed with California
methods of farming and ways of
marketing fruit and other products.
