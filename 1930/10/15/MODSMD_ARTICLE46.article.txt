# CARDINAL GLEE CLUB PRESENTS PROGRAM
## 
### 
Presenting a varied program of
college songs and general music, the
Stanford Glee Club gave its first
concert of the season last night at
a dinner-dance at the Hotel Mark
Hopkins in San Francisco.
A group of 30 selected men took
part in the entertainment, which
was the first of what is expected to
be a series of off-campus engage
ments for the organization, accord
ing to William Dienstein, manager
of the club.
