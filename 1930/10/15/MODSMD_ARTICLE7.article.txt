# MEETING OF DAILY TRYOUTEES TONIGHT
## 
### 
There will be an important meet
ing of tryoutees for the Daily edi
torial staff at 8 o'clock tonight in the
Daily office. All tryoutees must at
tend.
With competition very keen for
places on the staff, all tryoutees who
are not at the meeting will be com
pletely and unconditionally dropped,
according to Elisabeth Lars h,
women's editor.
