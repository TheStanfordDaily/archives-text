# POLO TEAM BEGINS REGULAR PRACTICE FOR FALL SEASON
## 
### 
With the ponies and players be
ginning to round into shape, Captain
R. A. Gordan's polo squad is settling
down to the serious business of pre
paring lor the fall season.
After two weeks devoted to con
ditioning the ponies, the mallet
wielders are engaging in their first
stick practices this week.
The opening game will be played
in about a month, according to Cap
tain Gordan. The crack Monterey
presidio four will probably furnish
the opposition.
Prior to the Monterey game the
squad will engage in several practice
scrimmages.
