# ALLEN TO RENDER BACH PRELUDES AT REGULAR CONCERT
## 
### 
Bach's three chorale-preludes on
"In Dulei Jubilo" will be an un
usual feature of the organ recital
to be presented by Warren D. Allen,
University organist, in the regular
Thursday afternoon recital today in
the Memorial Church at 4:15 o'clock.
The preludes are three variations
on a familiar Christmas carol dating
from the Middle Ages and .show the
master's versatility To a marked de
gree. Allen will also offer his own
arrangement of a movement from
Handel's Suite for the Royal Fire
works.
