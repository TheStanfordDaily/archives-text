# NEWMAN CLUB WILL HOLD FIRST MEETING
## 
### 
Professor Aurelio Espinosa of the
Romanic Languages department,
Harry Maloney, and Harry Wolters,
directors in the Men's Gymnasium,
will be guests of honor at the first
meeting of the Stanford Newman
Club to be hold in Iho Women's
Clubhouse at 7 30 o'clock tonight.
The Rev. Andrew Carroll and the
Rev. James Cronin of Palo Alto will
speak of the purpose of the club
and will extend an official welcome
to the new members.
