# UNEMPLOYMENT AIDED
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 16. (fP) — In
order to provide 10,000 idle men
with jobs during the winter months,
a number of financial and business
leaders have pledged themselves to
raise a $150,000 payroll weekly.
