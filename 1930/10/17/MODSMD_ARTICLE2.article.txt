# BAWLOUTS ON SALE EARLY ON SATURDAY
## 
### 
"Bawlouts" for autumn quarter
will go on sale Saturday morning at
8 o'clock, according to an announce
ment given out by the cashier's of
fice yesterday afternoon. They may
be bought for fifteen cents in the
cashier's office on the second floor of
the administration building.
A "bawlout" is officially known
as a Student Directory. Students,
faculty members and officers of the
University are listed in it. The book
is clubbed "bawlout" because it pro
vides a quarterly announcement of
tirade points.
