# SEVEN ARE CHOSEN FOR ENGLISH CLUB
## 
### 
The English Club announces the
election of the following: Nathan
van Patten and Beunichi Kagawa as
honorary members; Rcid Briggs,
Elizabeth Stuart Cooper, John Sit
ing, Grenville Jones, Bill Palmer,
Claude Peeiy, and Joan Reppy, as
active members; Helena Maxwell as
associate member.
The English Club will hold its
initiation and breakfast at 9:30
o'clock Sunday morning, at the home
of Milton Rosenfield, near Atherton.
The neophytes will be required to
give stunts and must bring with
them, for the perusal of the club
members, the literary masterpieces
of their youth.
