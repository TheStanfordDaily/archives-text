# DAILY TELEPHONE NUMBER CHANGED; IS NOW 4176, 4177
## 
### 
The Stanford Daily editorial
office announces the acquision
of an additional phone, and the
changing of its old number.
The new numbers are 4176 and
4177.
This telephone arrangement
will enable the editorial depart
ment to handle its business
with more facility, for if one
number is called and found
busy, the call will automati
cally be switched to the other
line.
