# ROBLE GYM POOL TO BE OPEN EVERY DAY
## 
### 
The Roble Gymnasium pool will
be open to all Stanford women
every day at 3 o'clock, Tuesday and
Thursday, at 4, and Saturday from
10:30 to 12 o'clock, according to an
announcement made by Miss Haw
ley, head of women's sports.
At all times swimming will be
supervised by Mrs. Botts or Miss
Linscott. swimming instructors.
