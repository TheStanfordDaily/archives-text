Two airmen crashed to their death
today when their planes collided
during a free-for-all race, the clos
ing event of the all-Eastern States
air meet at Mercer Airport.
The victims, Richard W. Mackie,
Valley Stream, N.Y., and George
Zinn, Rydal, Pa., were among a
score of contestants in the final race
of a two-day meet. They collided
as they rounded the first pylon and
their deaths were witnessed by
thousands of spectators.
