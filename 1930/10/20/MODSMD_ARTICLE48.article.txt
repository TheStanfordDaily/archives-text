# CHORUS REHEARSAL TO BE HELD TONIGHT
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will hold a rehearsal of all
altos and tenors of the oratorio
chorus in room 84 at 8 o'clock to
night.
No students who have not at
tended all of the previous rehearsals
can enroll in the chorus at this late
date because of the short length of
time remaining for practice.
