# DINNER TO BE GIVEN DR. HUNTER TONIGHT
## 
### 
A dinner for Dr. Allan Hunter,
who preached in the Chapel yester
day, will be given in the Union to
night at 6:15 o'clock under the aus
pices of the Y.W.C.A. and the
Y.M.C.A.
Dr. Hunter will speak on ''The
Challenge to the Christian Associa
tions on the Stanford Campus."
Student and faculty friends of Dr.
Hunter are cordially invited to at
tend this dinner. Reservations must
be made at the office of the Y.M.C.A.
or Y.W.C.A. by Monday noon.
