# RICHARDS-HOLTHAM WEDDING ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The marriage of Miss Irish Aud
rey Richards, of Australia, and Earl
A. Holtham of New York, took placo
at Del Monte Chapel on Tuesday.
The Rev. Ernest Bradley officiated.
Holtham was a member of the
class of '19 at Stanford and belongs
to Phi Kappa Psi fraternity. During
the World War he served as a naval
aviator.
