# WIFE OF STANFORD MINING PROFESSOR FILES CROSS-BILL
## 
### 
Mrs. Ida Velma Eaton Dietrich
filed divorce proceedings in San Jose
against Waldemar F. Dietrich, pro
fessor of mining and ceramics at
Stanford, charging cruelty, it was
learned Saturday. Mrs. Dietrich's
suit is one answering a suit filed
by her husband in Reno a short time
ago.
Professor Dietrich is not on the
campus this quarter, having estab
lished residence in Nevada.
The Dietrichs were married in
Stockton in September 1913, and
separated in March of this year.
