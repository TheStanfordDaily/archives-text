# NEW MEMBER ADDED TO GERMAN FACULTY
## 
### 
Miss Elizabeth Helen Rohden has
been added to the faculty of the
German department to occupy the
position of assistant in instruction,
according to the announcement of
Dr. William A. Cooper, head of the
department.
Miss Rohden is a graduate of the
University of Washington, and for
the past year has been teaching in
a high school in Pascoe, Washington.
She has studied at Stanford for the
last two summer quarters, and this
fall received her appointment as an
instructor here.
