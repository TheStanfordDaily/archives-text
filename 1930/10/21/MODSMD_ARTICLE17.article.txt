# WOMEN PICKED FOR CARD DEBATE SQUAD
## 
### 
Eight women were chosen for the
women's debate squad yesterday
morning by Coach J. C. Duniway.
Those chosen were Esther Kelley,
Mary K. Norris, Thelma Hogevoll,
Margaret Strong, Marian Reed, Isa
belle Wilty, Eleanor Furst, and Jean
Adams.
The team which will meet with
the California women will be com
posed of Esther Kelley, Mary K.
Morris, and Marian Reed. The date
of the debate has not yet been de
cided.
