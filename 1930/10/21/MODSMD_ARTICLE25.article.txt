# COMPTROLLER ROTH TO SPEAK TONIGHT
## 
### 
Comptroller A. E. Roth will be the
guest of honor and speaker at the
"Kick-off" dinner of the San Jose
Community Chest this evening. Mrs.
Roth will also be a guest.
The dinner is to be held in the
Scottish Rite Temple with approxi
mately 1,000 workers to be mobilized
for the campaign to raise $149,613
for philanthropic work during the
year. The principal speaker will be
Dr. Herman F. Schwartz, president
of the Berkeley Community Chest
and of the California Conference of
Social Works.
