# ENCINA WILL SERVE LUNCH TO GUESTS
## 
### 
Students who eat at Encina Com
mons may invite guests to eat a
light luncheon with them this Sat
urday noon before the U.S.C. game.
The special luncheon will be fifty
cents a plate, and will be served
from 11:45 to 12:15 o'clock, according
to the announcement by Mrs. An
derson, manager of Encina Com
mons.
Over 150 places arc already re
served, but a large number more
can be accommodated.
