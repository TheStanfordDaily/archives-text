# POLO MANAGERS TO TRY OUT ON FRIDAY
## 
### 
Tryouts for sophomore and junior
managers for the polo squad will
be opened Friday, it was announced
yesterday by Edwin Clarke, senior
manager.
Two junior manager posts will be
filled, and probably six sophomores
will be taken cn, Clarke said. All
those interested in trying out should
report at Encina polo field at 1
o'clock Friday afternoon.
