# COMEDY TICKETS GO ON SALE AT 1 O'CLOCK TODAY
## 
### 
Tickets for "Mrs. Bumpstead-
Leigh," first play of the cam
pus dramatic season, will go
on sale today at 1 o'clock in
front of the Women's Club
house. Prices will be $1.25 for
seats downstairs; $1 for center
balcony; and $.75 for side bal
cony.
There will be a special ticket
sale in Roble during the noon
hour today, and tickets can also
be procured at Keebles in Palo
Alto after 1 o'clock. Following
today's sale, tickets will be sold
until Friday at the A.S.S.U.
offices in the Old Union.
