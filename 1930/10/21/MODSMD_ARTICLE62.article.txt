# FRENCH SOCIETY TO MEET WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
Le Cercle Francais will hold its
first meeting of the quarter Wednes
day evening at 8:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse. The hour of the meeting
has been changed from 7:30 to 8:30
o'clock in order that those who
wished to do so could first attend
the U.S.C. rally.
Edmond Berard, C.R.B. Educa
tional Foundation Fellow, will speak.
Berard, who is a Belgian by birth)
is an electrical engineer and is
working this year in connection
with the Ryan Laboratory.
Catherine Musante will give some
vocal numbers. In the absence of
Alice Brett, president of the Cercle,
Professor William Leonard Schwartz
of the French department will pre
side.
