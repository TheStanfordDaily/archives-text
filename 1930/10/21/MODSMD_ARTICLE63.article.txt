# DR. HALL CONDUCTS RESEARCH IN EAST
## 
### 
Dr. Howard Judson Hall, profes
sor of English at Stanford, has been
for the past two quarters engaged
in historical research in the East.
Professor Hall, whose special line is
American Literature, is examining
the municipal archives of many
cities of the Atlantic Coast especially
with reference to Colonial culture.
He is expected to return to the
campus by Christmas.
