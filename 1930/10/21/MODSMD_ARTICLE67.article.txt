# ALLEN TO REHEARSE ORATORIO SECTIONS
## 
### 
All sopranos and basses of the
oratorio chorus will meet in room
84 at 8 o'clock tonight for a re
hearsal of Brahm's "Requiem" under
the direction of Warren D. Allen,
University organist.
The full chorus will hold a re
hearsal as usual Thursday night in
the Memorial Church at 8:20 o'clock.
Only twelve more rehearsals re
main before the performance Tues
day evening, November 11, in the
Chapel, and all members of the
chorus are required to attend them.
