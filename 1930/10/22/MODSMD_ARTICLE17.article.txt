# SERIES OF VESPER SERVICES TO BEGIN SUNDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
A series of Sunday vespers will
be started this week, under the
auspices of the Y.W.C.A., according
to Olive Mott, cabinet member. All
women interested are invited to
meet in the Y.VV.C.A. rooms, which
arc on the top floor of the Women's
Clubhouse, Sunday, from 5:15 to
5:30 o'clock.
Betty Moser will play selections
on the violin, and Betty Alden will
read poetry.
The vespers .-ire being started to
fulfill several requests for a Sunday
group which would meet for a short
while for music and quiet.
