# BIDS FOR COTILLION GO ON SALE MONDAY
## 
### 
Bids for the Sophomore Cotillion
to be held Friday, November 14,
will go on sale Monday' in the
A.S.S.U. office in the Old Union.
The price of the bids is $5. and the
number to-be sold is limited to 250.
The Cotillion, the only campus
formal of fall quarter, will be held
in the Pavilion. It is an annual all
campus dance sponsored by the
sophomore class.
