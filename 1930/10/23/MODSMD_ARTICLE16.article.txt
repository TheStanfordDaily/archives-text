# CARDINALS MATCH WITS WITH U.C. IN FIRST CHESS TILT
## 
### 
In a tournament in which Stan
ford will oppose California and St.
Mary's will oppose St. Ignatius,
Stanford chessmen will play their
first intercollegiate matches of the
year at Stephens Union tomorrow
night, according to Angelo May,
president of the Cardinal Chess
Club.
Stanford will be represented by
Winston Strong, Harvey Holcomb,
E. Fisher, Angelo May, Ellen Blade,
Keith Wilhelm, and Norman Han
sen.
The contestants will dine at Ste
phens Union at 6:30 o'clock before
Ihe tournament.
