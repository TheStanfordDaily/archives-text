# PLANS FOR TRIANGLE SPORTS DAY TALKED OF BY W.A.A. BOARD
## 
### 
Plans for the annual triangular
sports day held with Mills and Cali
fornia, were discussed at the meeting
of the W.A.A. board last night. It
was decided to postpone the event
until spring quarter, when the new
gym will be completed.
Catherine Cartlidge handed in her
resignation as hiking manager. It
i was agreed that this position shall
henceforth include direction of out
ings. The new manager will be
elected at the next meeting of the
board.
The resignation of Barbara Beach
Thompson as Junior representative
was accepted.
Plans for raising money for the
furnishings of the new gym were
discussed. Margaret Ball was put in
charge of the committee to look into
means of doing this.
