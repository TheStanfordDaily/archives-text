# TODAY IS LAST FOR ALL SENIOR PICTURES
## 
### 
All seniors who have not had pic
tures taken for the Quad must re
port sometime today. The offices in
the Old Union will be open from 9
until 11:45 o'clock this morning, and
from 12:45 to 4 o'clock this after
noon.
Seniors that cannot possibly re
port today should make arrange
ments immediately with the Quad
office. Fraternity and sorority pic
tures will be taken next week.
