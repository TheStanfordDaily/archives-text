# GANG CHIEF SEIZED BY FEDERAL AGENTS
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Oct. 23. (/-P) —George
! (Bugs) Moran, gang chief, was
seized by federal agents as he ap
peared in municipal court today for
hearing on a vagrancy charge. His
arrest was believed to have con
nection with alleged income tax
frauds.
