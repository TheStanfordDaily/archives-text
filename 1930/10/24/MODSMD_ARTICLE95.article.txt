# REV. SMITH TO BE SUNDAY PREACHER
## 
### 
Reverend Herbert Booth Smith,
pastor of the Immanuel Presbyter
ian Church of Los Angeles, will be
the visiting preacher at the Memor
ial Church services Sunday morn
ing at 11 o'clock. The anthem, "In
Thee, O Lord, I Put My Trust," by
Kalinnikoff, will be sung by the
choir. Harry Piobertson will render
a tenor solo, "If With All Your
Hearts," by Mendelssohn.
After graduating from Washington
University, Dr. Smith obtained his
A.M. from Princeton and his D.D.
from Occidental. He is a contributor
to several church papers, and has
published three volumes of sermons.
