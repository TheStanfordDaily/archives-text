# CRASH KILLS FLYERS
## 
### 
VALLEY STREAM, N.Y., Oct. 26
(A 3 ).—A biplane rented for a train
ing ilight nose-dived to the links of
the Cold Stream Golf Club here to
day, killing Major Philip Watson,
Army Reserve officer, and Julius
Nester, 34, student flier.
