# STANFORD COURSE RECORD IS BROKEN BY WOMAN STARS
## 
### 
In exhibition matches played be
fore a gallery of several hundred
persons Saturday morning, three
women golf stars broke the Stan
ford University course record.
Maurine Orcutt scored 80 for the
eighteen holes. The course record
was 86; par is 84. Helen Hicks' score
was 83, and Mrs. O. S. Hill turned
in a card of 85.
Other players in the matches in
cluded Mrs. Marian Hollins. Mrs.
Edith Quier, and Peggy Wattles.
