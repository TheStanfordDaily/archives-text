# OFF-CAMPUS WOMEN WILL MEET AT NOON
## 
### 
Oil-campus women will hold their
bi-monthly luncheon meeting this
noon on the third floor of the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Mrs. Ben Allen,
general secretary of the campus
Y.W.C.A., will be the guest of honor.
Women of all classes who do not
live at Roble or on the Row are
urged by Dorothy Cook, president
of the group, to attend the meeting,
as several matters of interest to
them will be taken up.
