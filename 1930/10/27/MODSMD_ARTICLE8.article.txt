# WOMAN INJURED IN AUTOMOBILE WRECK
## 
### 
Driving north from Los Angeles
with her son and a friend to attend
the Stanford-Trojan football game.
Mrs. M. C. Davis, 57, 1517 Angdon
Drive, Los Angeles, suffered several
broken ribs and bruises and lacera
tions Friday when her car crashed
into a telephone pole in San Jose.
The accident occurred on the
Schutzen Park crossing on the Mon
terey road. The car skidded on the
slippery road, and out of control,
careened into a telegraph pole, and
was badly wrecked.
