# ENGLISH CLUB WILL POSTPONE MEETING UNTIL NEXT WEEK
## 
### 
Because of the Conference party
lo be held Wednesday night, the
round table meeting of the English
Club will be postponed to a week
from Wednesday and will be held at
7:30 o'clock at the home of Webster
Street, 590 Embarcadero, in Palo
Alto.
A. Grove Day, Stanford '26, former
editor of the Stanford Lit, will pre
side at the meeting.
The English Club will offer a prize
of $5 for the best literary work sub
mitted to it before December 1. All
students are eligible to compete and
manuscripts should be handed to
Mrs. Murphy, secretary of the Eng
lish Department or placed in the
cabinet in room* 200.
