# FOUR FOR ONE
## 
### 
PARIS, Oct. 27. (/I 3 ) —The airplane
taxis have now adopted an idea
from their ground brothers, the au
tomobile taxieabs. Plane taxis are
now hired by a fiat I'ate, and four
can ride in a plane for the price of
one.
