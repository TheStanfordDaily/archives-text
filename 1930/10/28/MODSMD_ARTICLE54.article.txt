# GREY WILL SPEAK TO INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS GROUP
## 
### 
"The Present Attitude of England
Toward America" is the title of a
talk to be given by Professor Henry
David Grey tomorrow evening at
7:30 in the West Memorial Library
in the Political Science building.
This program, which is open to all
interested students, is given under
the auspices of the International
Relations Club.
Professor Grey has just returned
from attending meetings of ihe
Naval Parity Conference in London.
While there he was also a director
of the American Universities Union.
The talk tomorrow will be the
first of a series to be given at the
monthly meetings of the Interna
tional Relations Club. The club is
sponsored here and in all other
American universities by the Car
negie Institute for International
Peace.
