# CERCLE FRANCAIS SPONSORS WEEKLY LUNCHEON TABLE
## 
### 
All professors and students who
are interested in speaking French
are invited to luncheon at the
French Table at the Union every
Wednesday. These weekly lunch
eons, sponsored by the Cercle Fran
eais, will afford opportunity for
meeting people with cosmopolitan
interests as well as for practicing
French conversation. Jt will not be
necessary to reserve places in ad
vance.
Monsieur Edmond Berard, C.R.B.
Fellow in Electrical Engineering,
gave a short talk on the mines and
miners of the Borinage Basin in
Belgium at the first meeting of the
Cercle Frangais which was held last
Wednesday night.
