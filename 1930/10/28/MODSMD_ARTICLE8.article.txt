# ALDEN ELECTED TO ENCINA PRESIDENCY
## 
### 
John Aldcn was chosen president
of Encina Club in the elections held
last week, John Lauritzen, head
sponsor, announced yesterday.
Dick Gould was elected vicc
president; A1 Dremer, secretary;
Joe Chamberlin, manager; Bill
Stark, chairman of dance committee:
and Bill Hathaway, chairman of
smoker committee.
