# JUNIORS WILL GIVE JOLLY-UP TONIGHT
## 
### 
With transportation arrangements
for all University women, a junior
class jolly-up will be held tonight
at the Women's Clubhouse from 7
to 8 o'clock, according to Bob
Thornton, class president.
A personal invitation is extended
to all house presidents, he an
nounced, and automobiles will be
J sent to the sororities and Roble Hall
at 7 o'clock to take those desiring
to attend.
The jolly-up was arranged by the
class committee, composed of Patty
Edwards, Helen Osborne, Larry
Harris, Berk Anthony, Art Katzev,
and Austin Clapp. Music will be
furnished by Joe Thompson's live
piece band.
