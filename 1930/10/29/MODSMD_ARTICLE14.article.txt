# CREW MUTINEES
## 
### 
BUCHAREST, Rumania, Oct. 28.
(/?')—Newspapers here have learned
that the crew of the Soviet steamer
Lenin mutinied at Soukoum Har
bor, killing Captain Sokoloff and
two officers.
