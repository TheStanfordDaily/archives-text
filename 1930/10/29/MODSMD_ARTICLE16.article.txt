# VARSITY DEBATERS TO OPPOSE ENGLISH TEAM IN NOVEMBER
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity debate squad
will meet a team representing Eng
lish universities here on November
17, in the first major contest of the
autumn forensic season, announces
John Pascoe, manager.
"Resolved: That the principle of
democracy has been tried and found
wanting" will be the question for
the debate. This topic, one of a
group of live submitted by the Eng
lish team, was selected at a recent
meeting of the Stanford squad.
Speakers for the British team will
be B. J. Crehan of the University
of Liverpool and B. Hope Elletson
of St. John's College, Oxford Uni
versity. The Stanford speakers have
not yet been chosen.
