# GERMAN AIR GIANT TO FLY ATLANTIC
## 
### 
The huge Dornier plane, DO-X, will begin flight across the Atlantic from Friedrichshafen, Germany, to
New York early in November. Capt. Friedrich Christiansen (upper right) will be in command and Lieut.
11. Schildhauer, American naval officer, will serve as pilot. The navigation and control rooms are shown below.
