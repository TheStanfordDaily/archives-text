# CARD BOY SCOUTS TO BE VOTED INTO ALPHA PHI OMEGA
## 
### 
Stanford students who belong to
the Boy Scouts of America, and who
have attained the rank of Eagle
Scout, are eligible to be voted on
for membership in Alpha Phi
Omega, an organization whose mem
bers are all Eagle Scouts, according
to an announcement by President
John Tarr.
The membership now comprises
25 Stanford students. The activities
of the organization include volun
tary service work, helping Palo Alto
troops pass tests, and frequent meet
ings and trips.
Interested eligible students should
see Bill Corum, 308 Branner, Felix
Juda, 142 To-yon, or John Tarr, 431
En c in a.
