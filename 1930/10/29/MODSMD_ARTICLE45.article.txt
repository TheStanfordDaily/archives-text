# STEAMER IS FLOATED
## 
### 
ASTORIA, Ore., Oct. 28. </P)—'The
McCormick steamer Munmystic,
which grounded on Meins Sands on
the Washington side of the lower
Columbia, was floated this morning
and left up river. The ship ran
aground in a pea soup fog.
