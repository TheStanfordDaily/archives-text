# COOPER PRESIDES AT LANGUAGE MEETING
## 
### 
Stanford's foreign language de
partments were well represented at
a meeting of the Modern Language
Association of Central and Northern
California held last Saturday after
noon in the San Francisco Public
Library Assembly Hall.
Dr. William A. Cooper, professor
of Germanic languages at Stanford
University, presided at the meeting.
Associate Professor William L.
Schwartz of Stanford's Romanic
language department read a paper
on the question of the value con
temporary literature should play in
teaching of modern foreign lan
guages.
