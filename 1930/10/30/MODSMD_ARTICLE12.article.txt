# STANFORD GRADUATE MADE ECON EXPERT
## 
### 
Dr. Thomas Ham, Stanford gradu
ate, has been appointed one of four
economic experts to assist the city
of Boston in solving the problem of
unemployment, advices here say.
After taking his M.A. degree at
Stanford, he studied for a doctorate
at Harvard and is now on the fac
ulty of that institution.
The other members of the Boston
survey body are Henry Le Favour,
president of Simmons College; Hugh
Bancroft of the Financial News
Agency, and R. W. McNeel of the
McNeel Financial Agency.
