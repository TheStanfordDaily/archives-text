Of special interest to Stanford
professors is the announcement of
the publication of the first volume
of "Mediterranean Studies" by the
late Dr. George Hcmpl, professor of
Germanic Philology at Stanford
from 190(i until his death in 1921.
This treatise, edited by Frederick
Anderson, asociate professor of Ro
manic Languages at Stanford, con
tains two papers by Dr. Hcmpl: The
Genesis of European Alphabetic
Writing, and Minonan Seals. Also
included arc a memoir to the author,
his portrait, and a list of his many
published writings.
The volume, published and print

issued today as one of the Univer
