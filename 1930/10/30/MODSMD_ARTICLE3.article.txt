# ALLEN TO FEATURE MOZART'S "ANDANTE"
## 
### 
Mozart's "Andante and Varia
tions'' will be a special feature of
the organ recital to be given by
Warren D. Allen in the Memorial
Church at 4:15 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon. In addition to the Ger
man composer's music, Allen will
play selections from the work of
musicians in .Russia, England,
France, and America, all of a com
paratively modern period.
The program is as follows:
Andante and Variations... .Mozart
(1756-1791)
Intermezzo in B Minor. Moussorgsky
(Russian)
Nocturne in A F. H. Shera
(Modern English)
Invocation H. Mcsserer
(Modern French)
Ancient Phoenician Procession
R. S. Stoughton
(American)
