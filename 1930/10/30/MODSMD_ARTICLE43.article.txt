THURSDAY
12 noon—Meeting of the Stanford
Masonic Club at Jane's Tea Room,
Palo Alto. i
12 noon—Alpha Chi Sigma will hold
its customary luncheon in the
Union Alcove. All chemistry ma
jors are cordially invited.
12:10 p.m.—lota Sigma Pi will meet
at noon today in the Chemistry
Building.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen, University organist, in
the Memorial Church.
5:00 p.m.—Theta Sigma Phi will hold
a meeting upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
0:15 p.m.—The Stanford Men's De- .
bate Squad and the Freshman
Squad will meet for dinner at the
Stanford Union. Robert MeClin
tock will speak. Per plate 65c.
7:45 p.m.—Meeting of the Cardinal
Chess Club in the lobby of the
Old Union. All frosh trying out
for the freshman team must at
tend.
FRIDAY
3:30 p.m.—Professors R. Ladenburg
and M. von Laue, prominent Ger
man physicists, will lecture in
room 20 of the Chemistry Build
ing.
4:15 p.m.—Lecfbre recital on "Rela
tion of Overtones to Musical
Pleasure," by Henry Cowell. Lit
tle Theatre. Public invited.
SUNDAY
6:20 p.m.—Gilbert Wrenn will speak
and lead discussion on success at
college, in the Encina lobby. All
freshmen are invited.

Bugge to play off their matches
