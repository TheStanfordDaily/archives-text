# HAYRIDE POSTPONED TO TUESDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
The sophomore women'?; hayride
and barbecue dinner, which was
originally scheduled for tonight, has
been postponed until Tuesday, No
vember 4, according to announce
ment by Nancy Jones, president of
the Sophomore Women's Cabinet.
Women will assemble near Roblc
Gymnasium at 5 o'clock, and it is
expected that the trip and barbecue,
which will be held at the quarry,
will consume most of the evening.
Tickets will be sold for less than
50 cents in the various living groups
next week, and all sophomores are
urged to attend.
