# TERWILLUGER WILL PLAY AT VESPERS
## 
### 
Vesper services will be held on
Sunday at 5:15 o'clock in the
Y.W.C.A. rooms on the top floor of
the Women's Clubhouse. All of the
faculty and woman students are in
vited to attend.
Marian Terwilliger will play the
violin, and Mrs. Ben Allen will read
poetry, the selections to be chosen
on the theme "Trees."
