# ABYSSINIAN RULERS AWAIT CORONATION DAY
## 
### 

Careful and elaborate preparations were completed in Addi Aha ha for the enthronement of the Negus Ras
Tafari and his queen, Waizeru Menen, who, on Sunday, will he crowned emperor and empress of Abyssinia.
