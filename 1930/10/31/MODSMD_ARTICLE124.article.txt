# ULTRA-MODERN TALK GIVEN TODAY AT 4
## 
### 
"Relations of Overtones to Musi
cal Pleasure" is the title of a lec
ture recital to be given in the Little
Theatre, at 4:15 o'clock today, by
Ilenry Cowell, ultra modern com
poser. This is to be the last of a
series of three musical talks given
on the campus this week by Cowell,
under the general title of "Human
Response to Musical Pleasure."
Long a resident of this region,
Cowell is well known for his un
usual compositions and new methods
of piano technique. The settings for
many of his unique rhythms arc
furnished by Irish mythology.
