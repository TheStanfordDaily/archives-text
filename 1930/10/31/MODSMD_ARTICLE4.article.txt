# WIFE KEEPS VOW TO IGNORE HUBBY WHEN WAGE FAILS
## 
### 
DENVER, Colo., Oct. 30. I«
—Mammon dealt Hymen a
mortal blow so far as the mar
riage of Fred R. Jenner and
his May bride are concerned.
Jenner and his bride agreed
before the marriage ceremony
that they would not live to
gether as man and wife unless
Fred got an expected raise in
pay. The increase was expected
in two weeks.
The raise never came and
Jenner stated in his suit of an
nulment that his wife carried
out the agreement to the let
ter.
