# GOLF PLAYERS MAY NOT WEAR SKATES
## 
### 
RIVERSIDE, 111., Oct. 30. (/»').—
Chasing a little pill around holes on
a city lot may be O.K. But in the
opinion of the village fathers, when
the chasers put on l'oller skates it's
a different matter.
They obtained a temporary in
junction barring a , combination
roller skating and miniature golf
course from Riverside on the ground
it would cause objectionable noise
and injure public morals.
