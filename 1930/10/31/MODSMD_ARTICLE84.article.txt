SACRAMENTO, Oct. 30. (AP).—
The fifth earthquake at Redding in
18 hours shook the northern Califor
nia town at 8:30 o'clock tonight. The
tremor was felt in residence districts
but was not noticed in downtown
sections. The shock lasted about
eight seconds. No damage was re
ported.
