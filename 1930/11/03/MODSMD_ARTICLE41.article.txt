# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
MONDAY
8:00 pm.—A meeting of Stanford
voters interested in the discussion
of the measures on Tuesday's elec
tion ballot will be held in the tea
room of the Women's Clubhouse.
TUESDAY
4:15 p.m.—Lecture on Brahms' "Re
quiem" by Warren D. Allen. Or
gan gallery, Memorial Church.
Public invited.
7:30 p.m.—Ernest Fowles, fellow of
the Royal Academy of Music, will
speak on "Music and Life." Little
Theatre.
WEDNESDAY
8:0") p.m.—Sigma Xi will meet in
the Faculty Clubhouse. Professor
Perry Byerly of the University of
California will speak on "The In
terior of the Earth."
FRIDAY
9 a.m.-4 p.m.—Tickets for Brahm's
"Requiem" will be distributed to
faculty members and students in
the lobby of the Administration
Building. Tickets may be obtained
on presentation of student body
cards.
8:00 p.m.—Faculty Club meeting in
the Faculty Clubhouse.
MISCELLANEOUS
MEN entered in the interclass tennis
matches are urged by Coach
Bugge to play off their matches
immediately.
