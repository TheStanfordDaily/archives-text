# PSYCHOLOGY TENNIS TOURNEY ADVANCES
## 
### 
First round of the Tau Psi Epsi
lon's tennis tournament for psych
ologists was completed Thursday
afternoon and the second round will
be played this afternoon.
According to Robert Gillon Bern
reuter, president of the society, the
losers will play oil in consolation
tournament simultaneously with the
regular second round matches.
