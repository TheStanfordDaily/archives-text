# STORMS STRIKE PARIS
## 
### 
PARIS, Nov. !! (TP) —Fierce storms
.struck Paris today, causing the
death of two men and injuries to
many. One man was killed by a
falling electric light pole and an
other in a different part of the city
by a falling chimney. Seven injured
were taken to hospitals. Property
damage was heavy.
