# YOUNG TO ADDRESS EDUCATORS TONIGHT
## 
### 
Dr. S. W. Young, professor of
physical chemistry, will speak to
night before Phi Delta Kappa, pro
fessional educational fraternity, on
the subject "Dogmatism and Agnos
ticism." This meeting will be held
at 7:15 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.
The society will act on the resig
nation of Wyatt Hale as secretary
treasurer at a short business meeting
to be held before Professor Young's
talk. An election will be held for the
position left vacant by the resigna
tion of Hale.
