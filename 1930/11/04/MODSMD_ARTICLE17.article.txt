# MUSIC LECTURE GIVEN TONIGHT
## Ernest Fowles Will Speak In Assembly Hall 
### 
"Music and Life" will be the sub
; ject of Ernest Fowles's lecture at
7:30 o'clock tonight in the Assembly
: Hall, which he is to illustrate at the
piano.
The subject chosen reveals the
intimate connection between the art
j of music and the general needs of
| human life. Fowles is a Fellow of
j the Royal Academy of Music. This
is his third lecture tour of America.
He has previously lectured at
Yale, Cornell, and at many other
colleges, private schools and insti
tutions throughout the country.
Fowles's lecture is presented under
the auspices of the Committee on
Public Exercises. The public is in
vited.
