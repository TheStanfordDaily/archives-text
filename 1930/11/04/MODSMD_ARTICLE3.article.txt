# EXTRA ROOTERS' TICKETS TO U.W. GAME AVAILABLE
## 
### 
Thirty complimentary rooters'
tickets for the Washington
game will be given out from
the Board of Athletic Control
office, starting at 8 o'clock this
morning, according to Jake Ir
win, manager of the ticket de
partment.
The free pasteboards will be
presented only to those that
have not already applied for
tickets, Irwin stated. Band
members, rally committee
members, and those working
at the game need not apply for
the tickets.
Good seats on both the Stan
ford and Washington sides of
the Stadium are still available
at Roos Bros, and Wideman's
in Palo Alto. Tickets are priced
at $2.50 each.
