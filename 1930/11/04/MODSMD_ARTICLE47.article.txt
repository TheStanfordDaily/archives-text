# PLAY WILL BE READ
## 
### 
"Meteor," a play by S. M. Behr
man, author of the "Second Man,"
will be read by Mrs. Hugh Brown,
Thursday afternoon at 2:15 o'clock
in the Palo Alto Woman's Club
House. "Meteor" is a Theatre Guild
play presented last spring in New
York and deals with modern busi
ness ethics.
