# MASSEY INJURED IN AUTOMOBILE CRASH
## 
### 
Another accident was added to
the long list of collisions at the in
tersection of University avenue and
the state highway when James Mas
sey. '34, was injured in an automo
bile crash there Sunday night.
Massey, a member of the freshman
football squad, was treated in the
Palo Alto Hospital for minor in
juries to his right leg received when
the car on the running board of
which he was standing, collided with
another.
