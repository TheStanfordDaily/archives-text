# FACULTY MEMBERS BACK FROM HEALTH ASSOCIATION MEET
## 
### 
Dr. T. A. Storey, head of the
School of Hygiene and Physical Edu
cation. and Dr. Walter Henry Brown,
director of the hygiene department
of the University Health Service, re
turned Sunday from the convention
of the American Public Health As
sociation in Ft. Worth, Texas.
Dr. Brown addressed the conven
tion twice, once on the subject of
"The Health of the School Child" and
once regarding "Social Hygiene in
the College Curricula." Dr. Storey
attended as an observer.
The convention, which lasted three
days, from October 27-3(3. was made
up largely of public health officers
connected with state and municipal
governments.
