# ROSES PLANTED ON BORDER OF HIGHWAY
## 
###

be planted in the next few days
along the highway from San Fran
cisquito bridge to Mayfield, a dis
tance of more than two miles, Henry
Maier. head gardener for Stanford,
reported yesterday. Work on the
planting was begun yesterday morn
ing and is expected to occupy a ;
small force of gardeners most of this 1
week.
