# PEEWEE GOLF ROBBED
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Nov. 3. </P)—Peewee
burglars have made their first ap
pearance in Chicago. The tower
town miniature golf course was
their victim. They took the Saturday
night receipts and left nothing in
their place but some fingerprints.
