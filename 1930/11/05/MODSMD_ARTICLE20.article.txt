# COMMUTERS MEET FOR LUNCH MONDAY
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will be the guest
of honor at the regular hi-weekly
luncheon meeting of woman com
muters next Monday. All women
who do not live at Roble or on the
Row are eligible to attend the
gathering. New women arc espe
cially urged to be present to meet
Miss Yost.
The luncheon will be served on
the third floor of the Women's Club
house. Reservations must be made
with Lois Ruth Bailey, P.A. 3934, be
fore Saturday.
