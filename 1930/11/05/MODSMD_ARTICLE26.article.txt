# ADVISORY BOARD TO ENTERTAIN Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Y.W.C.A. cabinet will meet with
the advisory board tomorrow night
for dinner at the home of Mrs. A.
W. Johnson. The joint meeting is
held once a quarter for the purpose
of exchanging reports on the com
mittees' work.
Dinner tomorrow is a social affair
and cabinet is requested lo meet
first in the Y.W.C.A. rooms in the
Women's Clubhouse at 5:45 o'clock.
