# DAMES WILL ELECT OFFICERS TODAY
## 
### 
Election of Stanford Dames Club
officers for the coming year will
take place today from 3 to 5 o'clock
in the basement of the Women's
Clubhouse. Voting will precede the
regular semi-monthly meeting of
the club.
Following the casting of ballots a
program will be presented by Mrs.
E. L. Potter and Mrs. Marguerite
McManus. Mrs. Potter will give a
reading and Mrs. McManus will pro
vide music for the program. Both
these members of the club have
come to Stanford from Oregon State
College, where they were on the
faculty.
