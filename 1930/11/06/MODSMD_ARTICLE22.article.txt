# PAJAMA-CLAD FROSH TO PARADE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Fashions in latest evening attire
will be displayed when the fresh
men parade down the Row to Roble
tonight.
The freshmen will meet in front
of Encina at 7:15 o'clock, according
to George MeK.ee, yell-leader. They
will be accompanied by the cheer
leaders and members of the Rally
Committee.
The Pajamarino is annually con
ducted as a rally for the Little Big
Game, which will take place on
Saturday.
