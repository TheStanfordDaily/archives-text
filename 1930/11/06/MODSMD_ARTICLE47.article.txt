# FRANCE RECEIVES GIFT
## 
### 
I PARIS, Nov. 5. (/P)—Art objects
I valued at more than $1,000,000,
gathered by Edward M. Tuck,
wealthy former Bostonian, over a
period of 40 years, were presented
to the French government today and
placed in a new museum set aside
| in the Petit Palais.
