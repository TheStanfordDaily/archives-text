# NEW W.A.A. BOARD MEMBERS NAMED
## 
### 
Jeanne Millard was elected fresh
man Thom
as junior representative, and Mar
garet Abrams hiking manager at a
meeting of the W.A.A. board Wed
nesday night.
Margaret Ball was put in charge
of a commitee to plan a jolly-up
from 7 to 8 o'clock which will be
held in the new gym on the day it
is opened. Admission will be charged
and the money used to help furnish
the building.
