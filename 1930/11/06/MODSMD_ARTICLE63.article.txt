# OFF-CAMPUS WOMEN TO GATHER MONDAY
## 
### 
Women commuters will gather to
gether for their third bi-weekly
luncheon meeting Monday noon on
the top floor of the Women's Club
house. Dean Yost is to be the guest
of honor. Girls who are new on the
campus are especially urged to be
present.
Women of all classes who live in
Palo Alto, surrounding vicinities, or'
anywhere on the campus but the
Row or Roble, are cordially invited
to attend. Reservations must be
made with Lois Ruth Bailey, P.A.
3934, before Saturday.
