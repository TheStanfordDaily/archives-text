# ALLEN WILL SPEAK ON 'REQUIEM' TODAY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give a second lecture on
Brahms' "Requiem" this afternoon
a( 4:15 o'clock in the organ gallery
of the Memorial Church.
The lecture is to take the place
of the regular Thursday afternoon
organ recital and is to sketch briefly
the history and themes of the re
quiem which is to be given by the
oratorio chorus on Armistice Day.
