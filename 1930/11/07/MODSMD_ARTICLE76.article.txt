# TRACK-FIELD MEET HELD TODAY AT 4
## 
### 
Events above the 100-yard dash
will again be shortened to three
quarters of their usual length in the
regular weekly track and field meet
to be held at 4:20 o'clock this after
noon at the practice oval.
Last week full distances were run
for the first time this sason. Only
one more full-distance meet will be
run off this quarter, in December.
