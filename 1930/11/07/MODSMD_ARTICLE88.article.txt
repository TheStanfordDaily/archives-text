# YOST IS GUEST OF WOMAN COMMUTERS
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will meet as
guest of honor with the woman com
muters at their regular luncheon
gathering on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse Monday noon.
All woman students who do not
live on the Row or in Roble, espe
cially those who are new this quar
ter, arc urged by Dorothy Cook,
president of the group, to take ad
vantage of this opportunity to meet
Miss Yost informally. Miss Yost will
be very glad to answer any ques
tions which the commuters may
wish to ask her.
Reservations for luncheon must
be made with Lois Ruth Bailey,
P.A. 3934, before Saturday.
