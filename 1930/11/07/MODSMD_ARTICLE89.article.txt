# REQUIEM REHEARSAL TO SUPPLANT RECITAL
## 
### 
Because of the rehearsal for the
Armistice Day performance of
Brahms' "Requiem," Warren D.
Allen, University organist, will not
present his usual Sunday afternoon
organ recital this week.
The Reverend D. Charles Gard
ner, University chaplain, will de
liver the sermon at the regular
services in the Memorial Church
Sunday morning at 11 o'clock.
The music which will be given
during the services includes the
chorale, "Jesu, Joy of Man's Desir
ing." by Bach, and the anthem. "In
Thee, O Lord, I Put My Trust," by
Kalinnikoff.
