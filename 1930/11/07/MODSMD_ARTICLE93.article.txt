# NEW ENGLANDERS WILL MEET TUESDAY
## 
### 
Professor Leon Reynolds of the
Engineering Department will be the
speaker at the next meeting of the
New England Society of Palo Alto,
which will be held in the Com
muiity House Tuesday evening. No
vember 11, at 8 o'clock. The talk
will concern sewerage and garbage
disposal arid will be illustrated with
stereoptican slides.
At the end of the meeting an old
fashioned New England dinner will
be served to New Englanders now
residing in California. The meeting
is open to all. but the dinner only
to New Englanders.
