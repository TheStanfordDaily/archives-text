# RALLY PLANS TO BE DISCUSSED TONIGHT
## 
### 
Plans for the Big Game rally will
be discussed at a Rally Committee
meeting at 9 o'clock tonight in the
Y.M.C.A. room of the Old Union.
The rally will be held a week from
Wednesday. Early plans arc neces
sary because arrangements must be
made with the National Broadcast
ing Company, according to Ken Mfc-
Rae, chairman.
