# BECKING RESIGNS POSITION; ACCEPTS POST IN HOLLAND
## 
### 
Resignation of Professor Laurence
B. Becking as Herzstein professor
of botany at Stanford, to accept the
directorship of the Botanical Insti
tute at the University of Leyden,
Holland, and the chair of botany at
that university was announced Fri
day.
Becking, who is a native of Hol
land, has been at Stanford since
1919, connected chiefly with the
Jacques Loeb Laboratory of the
Hopkins Marine Station.
