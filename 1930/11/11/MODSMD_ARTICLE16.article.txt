# SECRETARY LAMONT SUGGESTS REMEDY FOR UNEMPLOYMENT
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 10 (/P).—
Improvements to facilitate street and
highway traffic were suggested to
day by Secretary Lament as a means
of increasing employment.
The commerce secretary also ad
vocated the changes to promote
safety rind for economic reasons. He
pointed out that traffic accidents cost
approximately $1,000,000,000 a year,
while ' the losses, delays and other
inefficiency resulting from the traf
fic congestion and inadequacy of
traffic facilities arc estimated at more
than $2,000,000,000 per annum."
The recommendations included
improvement in traffic signs, re
moval of obstructions to view, snow
removal, pedestrian safety islands
and clearing away street obstruc
tions.
