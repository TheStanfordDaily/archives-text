# CULVER INVITED TO SPEAK OVER KPO
## 
### 
George B. Culver, Dean of Men,
has been asked to be present at a
Stanford-Dartmouth broadcast from
KPO, Tuesday evening, November
25, at 8 o'clock, in connection with
the Stanford-Dartmouth game on
November 29. Dean Laycock of
Dartmouth is to be present to say a
few words in response to an official
greeting from officers of Stanford.
The Stanford dean has as yet
taken no action concerning the in
vitation.
