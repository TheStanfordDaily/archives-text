# PLASTER WORK ON NEW P.A. HOSPITAL IS TO BE STARTED
## 
### 
Plasterers are now preparing the
plaster for the second story of the
new Palo Alto Hospital, the work
on which is being carried out on
schedule up to the present time.
This job is expected to be finished
about December 10.
The pouring of concrete has been
finished and the plumbing work is
nearly complete. Lathing on the
third and fourth floors is now being
done, and the tiling on the exterior
walls is nearly set.

the Southern Pacific right-of-way,
