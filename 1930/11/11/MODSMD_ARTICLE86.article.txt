# PROFESSOR GRAY TO GIVE TALK ON IBSEN
## 
### 
Professor David Henry Gray, of
the English Department, will speak
on Hendrik Ibsen at 8 o'clock Wed
nesday evening in the Women's
Clubhouse in an open meeting spon
sored by the English Club, accord
ing to Ruth Lockett, president of the
organization.
Special attention will be paid by
the speaker to "Ghosts," a play which
the English Club is going to attend
in San Francisco, Friday evening.
Mrs. Patrick Campbell, a well
known actress, is taking the lead in
the production.
