# ENGLISH CLUB WILL HEAR IBSEN SPEECH
## 
### 
Sponsored by the English Club, a
meeting for club members and their
friends will be held tonight at 8
i o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Professor David Henry Gray, of the
English department, will speak on
Hendrik Ibsen, according to the an
nouncement of Ruth Lockett, presi
! dent of the organization.
The speaker will pay particular
attention to "Ghosts," which the
English Club will attend next Fri
day in San Francisco.
