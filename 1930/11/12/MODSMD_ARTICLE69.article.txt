# LOCAL BIOLOGICAL LIBRARY HAS 100 SCIENCE PORTRAITS
## 
### 
More than one hundred portraits
of leading zoologists and physical
scientists adorn the walls surround
ing the local biological laboratory.
Especially interesting in this
group of pictures are those of mod
ern scientists that have been per
sonally presented to members of the
University faculty. Several local
professors have presented these
portraits to the University.
Included in the group of zoolo
gists are pictures of Karl von Baer,
Arthur Shipley, and Alfred R. Wal
lace. All of these men were in their
times leaders in their respective
fields.
