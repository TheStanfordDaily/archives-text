# LEAGUE OF NATIONS STUDENT CONTEST THESIS ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The League of Nations Associa
tion has recently announced the
first national contest for college
students. Theses for the contest, are
to be written on disarmament,
studies of the league, or surveys of
political and economical aspects of
proposed federation of European
i status. Scripts are to be mailed to
the contest editor, not later than
March 2.
A trip to Europe, including a stay
in Geneva and an opportunity to
study the League of Nations at
work, is offered as the first prize.
One hundred dollars will constitute
the second prize, and the third prize
will be fifty dollars.
Further information for those in
terested can be obtained in the of
fice of the Dean of Women.
