# MILLIONS SAVED
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 12. (/P)—lt will
be a merry Christmas. Some 11,-
000,000 members of Christmas clubs
run by 8.000 banks in the United
States are to have $012,000,000 avail
able shortly, it is estimated by Her
bert R. Rawll, banker. The total
saved is the largest ever.
