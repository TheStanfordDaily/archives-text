# SOPHS, JUNIORS WIN FIRST W.A.A. GAMES
## 
### 
In tho first games of the W.A.A.
basketball practice series being
played this week in preparation for
the regular series which start soon,
the sophomore women won from
the freshmen by a big margin and
the juniors won from the seniors.
First-string lineups have not been
announced as yet, and these practice
games will in a great measure de
termine the teams to play through
out the season, according to Jane
Snyder, basketball manager. In the
practice games all possibilities for
first and second teams are on the
court for at least a pari of the game.
