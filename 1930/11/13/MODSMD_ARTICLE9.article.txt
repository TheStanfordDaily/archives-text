# DAMES WILL SERVE "POT-LUCK" SUPPER
## 
### 
Honoring husbands and sons,
members of the Stanford Dames
Club will serve them a "pot-luck"
supper in the basement of the Wo

6:30 o'clock. A short program will
be followed by an evening of bridge.
Preparations for the supper are
being made under the direction of
Mrjf. G. D. Wood, who has planned
for each member of the club to
bring a part of the menu. Guests are
asked to meet at the clubhouse at
