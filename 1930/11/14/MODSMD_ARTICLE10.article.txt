# VIOLINIST TO PLAY IN CHURCH SERVICE
## 
### 
Samuel Savannah, violinist, will
be the feature artist on the after
noon program of organ and violin
music to be given in the Memorial
Church this Sunday at 4 o'clock.
Warren D. Allen, organist, will open
the program with "Toccata and Fu
gue in D Minor" of J. S. Bach.
Antonin Dvorak's ever-popular
Largo from his "New World Sym
phony" will be redered in an ar
rangement for violin and organ. A
violin solo, "Andantino from the
Concerto, Opus 61" of C. Saint
Saens, and an organ selection by
Seth Bingham, "Roulade in D
Minor," will complete the program.
