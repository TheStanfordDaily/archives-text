# BUZZARD STEALS HALL
## 
### 
PALM BEACH, Fla., Nov. 13. (/P)
—Tough on Mike Rider! He hit a
straight drive of 225 yards. A buz
zard swooped down and flew off
with the ball. Mike learned his golf
in Scotland.
