# DEAN QUAENTIN IS VISITING PREACHER
## 
### 
This Sunday (he Very Reverend
Cecil S. Quaintin, Dean of the
Cathedral in Victoria, British Col
umbia, will be the visiting preacher
at the Memorial Church services at
11 o'clock in the morning.
The anthems which will be sung
are: "In Thee, O Lord, I Put My
Trust," by KalinikofT. and "Bene
dictus es Domine," by Martin.
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present his usual organ
recital at 4 o'clock in the afternoon.
