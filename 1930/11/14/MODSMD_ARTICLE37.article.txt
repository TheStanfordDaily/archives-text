# Y.W.C.A. WILL HOLD OPEN HOUSE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Mrs. F. J. Perry and Mrs. J. B.
Bellamy will be hostesses at the
"open house" of the Y.W.C.A. from
7:30 to 10 o'clock tonight in the as
sociation's rooms on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
According to Elizabeth Sumner,
chairman of the program committee,
there will be card playing and sing
ing. Afterwards they will pop corn
in the fireplace. All Stanford women
( are invited to come and have a jolly
evening together.
