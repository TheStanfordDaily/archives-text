# DUELISTS MAKE UP
## 
### 
MONTEVIDEO. Uruguay, Nov.
13. (/I s ) —Having engaged in an af
fair involving pistols for two, Pro
fessor Pedro Baridon and Alberto
Demichelli, minister of instruction,
are friends again. They had a duel.
Both missed. Then they shook hands.
