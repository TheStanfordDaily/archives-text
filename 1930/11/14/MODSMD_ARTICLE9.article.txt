# SEWARD WILL READ AT VESPERS SERVICE
## 
### 
Professor Samuel Seward of the
Stanford English Department will
read at a short vesper service to be
held this Sunday afternoon in the
Y.W.C.A. room on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse. Miss Rhoda
Manning will play the cello.
Maria Lynch is in charge of these
meetings which are open to all
Stanford women and held every
Sunday afternoon from 5:15 to 5:30
o'clock. The Vesper meetings are
sponsored by the Committee on Re
ligion of the Y.W.C.A. of which
Olive Mott is chairman.
