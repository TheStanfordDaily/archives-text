# BOARD AUTHORIZES GAS SYSTEM SALE
## 
### 
Purchase of the Stanford Univer
sity gas system by the Pacific Gas
and Electric Company for $27,000
was authored Friday by the Cali
fornia State Railroad Commission.
The City of Palo Alto intervened
on the ground that it owns 1,000
feet of the campus and should be
reimbursed. The decision made no
provision for this.
