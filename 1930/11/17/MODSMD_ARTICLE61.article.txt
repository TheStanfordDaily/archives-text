# SCOUTS WILL HEAR SCHOOL EXECUTIVE
## 
### 
Walter L. Bachrodt, superintend-
ent of schools at San Jose, will be
the principal speaker at Alpha Phi
Sigma's dinner at the Union 6:15
o'clock tomorrow evening.
All Eagle scouts are invited to at
tend. The price of the dinner will
be 65 cents. Bachrodt's subject has
not been announced.
