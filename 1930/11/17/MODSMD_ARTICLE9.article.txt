# NEW WOMEN HOLD JUNIOR PARTY AT ROBLE TOMORROW
## 
### 
Junior women living at Roble and
of! the campus will hold their sec
ond get-together tomorrow night at
10 o'clock in the Roble library.
Arrangements arc being made un
der the direction of Virginia Lloyd.
Skits and entertainment will be fur
nished, and refreshments served. A
small charge of 10 cents will be
made for refreshments.
Off-campus juniors who plan to
attend the gathering arc asked to
make reservations with Lois Ruth
Bailey, P A. 3934, according to Jane
Snyder, Y.W.C.A. cabinet member
in charge of the party.
