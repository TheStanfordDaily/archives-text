# OWENS TO SPEAK TO MOTHERS CLUB
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers Club will
meet this afternoon at 3 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse instead of
Monday, as stated in yesterday's
Daily.
Professor William B. Owens will
speak on the athletic side of uni
versity life. Ten will be served after
the program.
