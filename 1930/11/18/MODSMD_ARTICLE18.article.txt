# STANFORD ALUMNUS KILLED IN ACCIDENT
## 
### 
Judge Elias Victor Rosenkranz,
Stanford '10, was killed in an auto
mobile accident in Los Angeles, ac
cording to Associated Press dis
patches from that city. His machine
skidded on wet pavements.
Judge Rosenkranz was graduated
from Stanford in 1910 and received
a degree two years later from the
Southern California Law School. In
1926 he was appointed to the muni
cipal court of Los Angeles.
