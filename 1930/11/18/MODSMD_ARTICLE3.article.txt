# GAIETIES TICKETS GO ON SALE AT 1 O'CLOCK TODAY
## 
### 
TICKETS for the 1930 Big
Game Gaieties, to be held
Friday night in the Assembly
Hall, go on sale today. Stan
ford students wishing to make
reservations for tickets in ad
vance can do so from 9 to 12
o'clock this morning at the
Dramatic Council office.
Regular sale will begin at 1
o'clock this afternoon in front
of the Women's Clubhouse and
at Keeble's in Palo Alto. Pre
ferred orchestra seats and those
in the first 12 rows sell at
$1.50: all other downstairs seats
and those in the center balcony
are $1.25; and side balcony
tickets are $1.
