# ORCHESTRA TO HOLD REHEARSAL TONIGHT
## 
### 
Rehearsal of the Stanford Sym
phony Orchestra will take place at
7:15 o'clock tonight in Roble Gym
nasium, instead of tomorrow night,
as was announced at last week's re
hearsal, according to Bourne Eaton,
manager of the orchestra.
The orchestra has definitely de
cided to present its concert on the
campus on December 9, the second
Tuesday of next month. Practice is
being directed on the classic pieces
which will be presented at that
time.
