# WOMEN TO DISCUSS GYMNASIUM PLANS
## 
### 
I
Suggestions concerning the new
gymnasium will be discussed at a
meeting of the Women's "S" Society
in the Women's Clubhouse at 5
o'clock this afternoon.
The society is planning to put on
a stunt at the Carnival in December
and also to furnish either the
Lounge Room or the Court of the
, v new gym when completed.
