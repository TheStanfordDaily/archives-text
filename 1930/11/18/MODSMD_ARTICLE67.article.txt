# LOCAL EDUCATORS ATTEND J.C. MEET
## 
### 
Stanford will be represented by
five educators at the eleventu con
vention of the American Association
of Junior Colleges which opens this
morning for a three-day session at
the Claremont hotel, Berkeley.
Lowry Howard, '17, Dr. W. W.
Kemp, '98, Wyatt Hale, graduate
student, and Drs. Walter Eells and
W. M. Proctor of the Stanford School
of Education are to speak.
