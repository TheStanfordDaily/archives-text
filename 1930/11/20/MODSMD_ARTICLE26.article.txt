# A 						TRIBUTE TO DR. MARTIN
## 
### 
Stanford's famed E flat cornet player, Professor Ernest Whitney
Martin, band leader for generations of students, last night, on the
eve of his last appearance as director, gracefully took his bow from
active work before the assembled hundreds present to witness the
annual Big Game Bonfire rally.
While the students, alumni, and friends of the University gath
ered to celebrate the burning of the Campanile and the cremation of
the Golden Bear's coffin, friends and supporters of Dr. Martin
tendered the 74-year-old professor a lasting memento of appreciative
letters for his efforts vyitli the Stanford Band.
The DAILY joins with the students in wishing Dr. Martin that he
may long witness the triumph of the Stanford colors amidst the
stirring strains of the Cardinal band.
