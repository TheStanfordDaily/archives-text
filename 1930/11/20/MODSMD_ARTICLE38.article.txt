##

cashier of the Stockton Savings and
Loan Bank, has been named comp
troller at the College of the Pacific,
Stockton. Mrs. Ritter was Lucy
Alma Corker, 'O2. Their children,
Lucy Ritter, now doing' graduate
work in the political science de
partment, and Ovid Ritter, were
graduated with the Class of '3O.
