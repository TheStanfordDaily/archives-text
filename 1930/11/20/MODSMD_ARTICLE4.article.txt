# DAILY EDITORS TO HOLD THIRD REUNION TOMORROW EVENING
## 
### 
The third annual reunion of Stan
ford Daily editors will be held at
Camille's Restaurant in San Fran
cisco tomorrow evening at 6:30
o'clock.
Templeton Peck of the San Fran
cisco News, who was Daily editor in
1929, will be toastmaster. Thirty peo
ple are expected to attend this din
ner which is the third of its kind to
be held, the custom being started in
1928.
Those attending are: Scott Cal
houn, '95; C. P. Cutten, '99; E. W.
Smith, '99; James L. Nourse, '00;
Frank Hinman, 'O2; Alfred A.'Hamp
son, 'O2; B. C. Dey, 'O5; Ben S. Allen,
'O6.
Dave Walker, 'O9; W. P. Fuller,
Jr., '10; C. H. Hails, '10; E. H.
(Squire) Behrens, 'l4; Paul Stam
ford, 'l6; M. C. Hall, '18; Frank J.
Taylor, 'lB.
Ruth Taylor White, 'l9; Lloyd W.
Dinkelspiel, 'l9; Landis O. Weaver,
'2O; Ellen Calhoun Van Every, '2O;
William F. Leiser, '2l; Harry J. Bor
ba, '22; Tom Irwin, '23.
Don E. Liebendorfer, '24; Wallace
W. Knox, '26; Harry M. Bennett, '27;

Bishop, '3l, and s 'ale Wallace, '3l.
