# P.A. SHOP TO HAVE FORMER OPERATOR
## 
### 
James H. Warner, radio operator
on the 7500-mile flight of the South
ern Cross from Oakland to Bris
bane, has been appointed head of
the service department of a Palo
Alto radio shop. Prior to his epoch
making hop with Major Charles
Kingsford-Smith, Warner served 16
years in the U.S. Navy.
