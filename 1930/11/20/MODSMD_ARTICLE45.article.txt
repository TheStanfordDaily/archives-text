# DARTMOUTH GAME PUBLICITY ARRANGED
## 
### 
Publicity for the Dartmouth-
Stanford game will be handled by
the San Francisco Junior Chamber
of Commerce, according to an an
nouncement 'from the offices of the
Board of Athletic Control. Arrange
ments for radio and newspaper ad
vertising have been made, and tick
ets have been put on sale at all
agencies in the east-bay. Approxi
mately 20,000 tickets have already
been sold.
Wideman's and Roos Brothers will
have tickets on sale for those buying
them in Palo Alto.
