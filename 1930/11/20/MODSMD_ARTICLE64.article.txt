# MRS. HOOVER'S VOTE DECLARED ILLEGAL
## 
### 
County supervisors meeting yes
terday morning in San Jose to can
vass the vote of the election of
November 4 rejected as illegal the
absent voter's ballot cast by Mrs.
Herbert Hoover, because it was
mailed in advance of election day.
It was sent from Washington by air
mail on November 1 and reached
the office of the county clerk on
election day. (
The absent-voter's ballots cast by
President Hoover and Secretary and
Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur were for
warded on election day and were
pronounced legal by the super
visors.
