# FORMER STANFORD ASSOCIATE WRITEES MARGARINE STUDY
## 
### 
As the fourth book in a series of
fats and oils studies, the Food Re
search Institute is publishing a book
entitled "Margarine as a Butter
Substitute," by Katherine Snod
grass, former research associate at
Stanford. The manuscript is at the
Press now.
Material for the book was gath
ered by Miss Snodgrass irom the
leading food research authorities in
the country, and advance orders
have been received from libraries,
universities, schools, and dealers in
fats and oils.
Dr. Alonzo E. Taylor, director of
the Institute, is writing the fifth
study in the series, now in manu
script form.
