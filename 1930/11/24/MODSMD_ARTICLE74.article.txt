###

Stanford will take part in the thir

Philological Association of the Pa
cific Coast to be held in the lobby of

urday, November 28 and 29.
Professors Jefferson Elmore and

first session on Friday, whiPe Pro
fessor Edith R. Mirrielees will take

Papers of those members of the

Leonaud Schwartz, and Benjamin O.
Foster, who is a member of the ex
