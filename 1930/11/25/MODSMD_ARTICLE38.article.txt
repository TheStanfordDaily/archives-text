# TIME SCHEDULES FOR NEXT QUARTER READY
## 
### 
Time schedules for the Winter
Quarter may be procured on the
payment of a dime at the Cashier's
office on the second floor of the Ad
ministration building, today.
Those wishing to take advance
registration for the Winter Quarter
should purchase a copy, and then
consult their adviser as to their
courses, according to J. P. Mitchell,
chairman of the committee on
Lower Division administration.
