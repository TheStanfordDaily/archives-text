# MISSIONARIES KILLED
## 
### 
HANKOW, China, Nov. 25. (/I 1 ) —It
was reported here today that the
two Italian priests, Father Noveli
and Father Luciano, were killed by
bandits at Hinganfu in south Shensi,
325 miles northwest of here.
