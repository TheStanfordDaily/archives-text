# QUARTET WILL SING FOR NBC OVER KPO SATURDAY MORNING
## 
### 
The Cardinal Quartette has been
asked by the National Broadcasting
Company to sing over its Pacific
Coast network from KPO during the
Shell Happy Time hour from 8 to 9
o'clock the Saturday morning of
the Dartmouth game.
The program will be intended tc
serve as a pre-game rally for the
public, and to arouse interest in the
East-West clash, proceeds of which
will go to the aid of the unemployed.
Everett Baker, '31; John Huneke.
\ r !2; George McKee, '31; and Ralph
Wallace, '30, compose the quartette.
