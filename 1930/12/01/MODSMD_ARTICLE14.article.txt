# RUSSIAN PRINCESS TO BE RECEIVED BY DR. ROBERT SWAIN
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Robert Eckles Swain
will hold an informal reception Fri
day afternoon for Princess Agreneva
Slavionsky and the Royal" Russian
Chorus who will appear at the Palo
Alto High School on the evening of
Tuesday, December 0, under the
auspices of the American Legion.
According to Lieutenant R. E.
Lawrance of the Palo Alto Ameri
can Legion, the Royal Chorus is
presenting a program to aid the
needy people of Palo Alto and the
unemployed.
The Royal Chorus is composed of
vocal artists who at one time sang
before the Czar of Russia and re
cently completed a run in New York
City.'
