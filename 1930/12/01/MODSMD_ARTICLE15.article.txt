# VESPER SERVICE HAS READING ON PROGRAM
## 
### 
Featuring the half-hour program
of yesterday afternoon's Y.W.C.A.
vesper service at the Women's Club
house, Francis Thompson's "The
Hound of Heaven" was read by Miss
Anastasia Doyle, Social Director at
Roble. Marion Terwilliger, in charge
of the service, played a violin solo.
Community singing of several verses
of "Now the Day Is Over" concluded
the gathering.
