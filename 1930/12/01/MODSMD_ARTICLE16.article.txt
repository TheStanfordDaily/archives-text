# PAPA PRESERVES NEUTRALITY WITH END SEAT CHOICE
## 
### 
Dr. Howard Clarke, of Hono
lulu, saw the Stanford-Dart
mouth game from a seat behind
the goal posts and preserved
family neutrality Saturday.
Howard Clarke, Jr., in the
Dartmouth rooting section, and
Samuel Clarke on the Stanford
side, with Howard Clarke, Sr.,
wavering between the two,
That was the family lineup.
Dr. Clarke, a physician and
father of the boys, one of whom
is a student at Hanover and
the other at Stanford, was in
a quandary as to which In
dians to whoop for in the con
test, and decided on both. He
is himself a former football
star, having played for Tulane
University in 1905.
