# DISCUSSION MEETINGS TO BE HELD IN ROBLE
## 
### 
Freshman women's discussion
groups will meet tomorrow evening
from 7 o'clock to 8 o'clock. One
group, under the leadership of Eliza
beth Jackson and Sarah Grant, will
discuss relations between men and
women, in the Roble library.
The vocational guidance group
will meet in room 216 at Roble.
Marriage, secretarial work, teach
ing, personnel work, dramatics, and
writing will be taken up. Geraldine
Kinnc and Margaret Kcmpenich will
lead this group.
