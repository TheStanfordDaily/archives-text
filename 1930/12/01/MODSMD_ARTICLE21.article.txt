# DONNAY WILL SPEAK ON SPHERULITE OPTICS
## 
### 
Members of Ihe Mathematics Club
will hear a talk on the subject of
"Spherulite Optics" by Dr. J. H.
Donnay, research associate in geo
logy, at a meeting to be held tomor
row afternoon in room 75 in the
Mathematics building.
Dr. Donnay will illustrate his
paper with lantern slides and
Lumiere plates. Tea will be served
before the talk. Anyone interested
in the subject is invited to attend
the meeting.
