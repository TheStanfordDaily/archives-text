# BOUTS FOR NOVICE BOXERS SCHEDULED TO BE HELD TODAY
## Finals In Tournament To Be Held In Pavilion Wednesday Night 
### 
Preliminary bouts in the all-Uni
versity novice boxing tournament
to be held December 1 to 3 will be
fought out today and Tuesday in
the boxing gallery. The finals will
be fought in the Pavilion Wednesday
i night. Admission will be by student
body card.
Thirty-two men have signed up
for the tourney and all weight lists
have been filled with the exception
of light-heavies.
The complete list of entries fol
lows:
Bantamweight: John Allen, Rob
ert C. Case, and Yuji Imai.
Featherweight: Bruce McCallum,
Dave Goodloe, Griffith Page, Lewis
Page, and Victor Shoemaker.
Lightweight: James Arthur, Jess
Benton, Cecil Bryan, Cecil Cutting,
! Robert Dearborn, Don Stewart, and
Jack Wilson.
j Welterweight: Tom Decker, Mor
-1 gan Evans, Don Frick, Fred Glover,
James Kelley, John Livingstone, and
Jerry Wood.
Middleweight: Alfred Barstow,
James Bell, William Brown, Leland
j Guth, Andrew Morthland, Jack
j Perego, Lewis Sterry, and Fred Van
Dusen.
Heavyweight: Bill Harding and
| Oscar Odale.
