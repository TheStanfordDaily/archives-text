# INSURANCE MAN WILL MEET STUDENTS HERE
## 
### 
For the purpose of talking to men
interested in receiving training as
insurance salesmen, a representative
of the Pacific Mutual Insurance
Company will be at the Appoint
ment Service Office in the Admin
istration Building tomorrow after
noon, according to an announcement
made by Ruth Tracksell, executive
assistant in the Appointment Ser
vice.
Appointments should be made
with Miss Tracksell this morning or
tomorrow morning to meet the com
pany's representative, who will out
line plans whereby salary as well as
a commission will be paid during
the training period.
