# CARD CHESS CLUB MEETS ST. MARY'S IN LEAGUE TOURNEY
## 
### 
Seven members of the Cardinal
Chess Club will clash with an op
posing team from Saint Mary's Col
lege Friday evening at, 7:45 o'clock
in the lobby of the Old Union. This
will be the second match of the
season with a member of the Cali
fornia Intercollegiate Chess League.
The men who will take part in the
play are: Angelo May, '33, captfyn;
Edward Fisher, Norman Hansen,
Trelfa Lund, Norman Sawyer, Win
ston Strong, and Keith Wilhelm.
The club was organized last year
and already boasts a membership of
50. All students interested in chess
are invited to attend Friday's game.
