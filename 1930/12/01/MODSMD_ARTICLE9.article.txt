# TOYON, BRANNER ROOM FEES PAYABLE TODAY
## 
### 
Deposits on rooms in Toyon and
Branner halls for the winter quarter
will be payable today and tomorrow,
according to Vivienne Church, sec
retary to the dean of men. Drawings
for the rooms will be held in the
offices of the two halls Wednesday
evening at 7:30 o'clock.
The reservations should be made
with Miss Church at the office of
the dean of men.
