# WOMEN'S ADVANCE REGISTRATION WILL BE HELD THIS WEEK
## 
### 
Women's advance registration for
Winter Quarter will take place on
Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday
of this week in the Dean of Women's
office. Although Dean Mary Yost
is in Virginia, appointments should
be made today for interviews with
Helen Huneke who will take charge
of registration.
Miss Huneke's office hours will
l>c from 10 to 12 o'clock on Wednes
day; and from 9 to 12 o'clock and
from 2 to 4 o'clock on Thursday and
Friday.
Women are urged by Miss Huneke
to make their appointments today
because of the importance of at
tending to programs before the end
of the quarter.
