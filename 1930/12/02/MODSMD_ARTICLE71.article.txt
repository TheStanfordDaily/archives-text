# YOUNGEST GIRL POLO TEAM IN THE UNITED STATES
## 
### 

These girls of the Douglas school, Pebble Beach, Cal., averaging 14 years of age, comprise what is
said to be the youngest girl polo team in the United States. Left to right: Caroline and Nancy Stan
baugh of Youngstown, Ohio; Jean Hobbs, captain, of Cohasset, Mass.; Louise Parke of Pebble Beach,
and Jane Pitou, substitute, of Chicago.
