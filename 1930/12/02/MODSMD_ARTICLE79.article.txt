# ROBLE JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
1 An all-Roble jolly-up will be held
from 7 to 8 o'clock this evening in
Roble Hall. Bessie Falk is in charge
of arrangements and freshmen
women will act as hostesses. Music
will be furnished by members of
Henry Shapiro's orchestra.
