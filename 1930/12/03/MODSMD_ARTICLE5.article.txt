# WOMEN'S VARSITY DEBATERS TO MEET CALIFORNIA TONIGHT
## 
### 
"Resolved: that an individual
should not go to junior college in
preparation for further work in a
university," will be the subject of
the debate tonight between the var
sity women's debating teams of Stan
ford and California at 7:30 o'clock in
the Little Theatre. There will be no
charge for admission, according to
the announcement by the women's
debate manager.
Two members of last year's team,
Mary Catherine Morris and Esther
Kelly, will uphold the affirmative for
Stanford. The University of Califor
nia negative team is composed of
Mary Shumard and Margaret Ward.
The Stanford negative team, Mar
garet Strong and Marion Reed, will
debate the California affirmative
team at Berkeley Thursday.
