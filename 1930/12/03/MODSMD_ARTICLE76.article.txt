# XMAS FOREIGN MAIL MUST BE SENT NOW, SAYS POSTMASTER
## 
### 
Christmas mail addressed to for
eign countries should be mailed at
once to insure delivery on or before
Christmas Day. It is already too late
for mail being sent to some coun
tries to arrive on time, and all for
eign mail should be sent within the
next few days. Information regard
ing sending mail may be obtained
by calling at the campus post office.
In wrapping parcels, the address
should be put on one side only, and
the complete return address should
be placed in the upper left hand
corner. This uniformity in address
ing, W. C. Werry, postmaster, an
nounces, is asked by the post office
to facilitate handling the mail.
