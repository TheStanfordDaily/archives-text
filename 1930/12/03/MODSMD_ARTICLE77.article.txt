# KIMBALL, '96, WILL ADDRESS ENGINEERS IN JOINT MEETING
## 
### 
Acting-Professor Dexter S. Kim
j ball, "96, of the Graduate School of
Business, will address a joint meet
ing of the Stanford branches of the
American Institute of Electrical En
j gineers and the American Society of
Mechanical Engineers next Tuesday
levelling at 7:30 o'clock in the lobby
of the Old Union.
Professor Kimball will speak on
! the early days of Stanford, particu
larly of the engineering school from
which he x-eceived his engineering
degree in 1913. He is on leave from
Cornell University this quarter,
where he holds the position of the
Dean of Sibley College.
All students who are interested are
invited to attend. Refreshments will
be served following the meeting.
