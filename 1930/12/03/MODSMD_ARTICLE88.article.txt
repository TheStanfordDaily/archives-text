# DRAWINGS FOR HALL ROOMS HELD TONIGHT
## 
### 
Drawings for the choice of un
reserved rooms in Toyon and Bran
ner halls will be held tonight at 7:30
o'clock in the offices of each hall,
announces Miss Vivienne Church,
secretary to the dean of men.
Men who have been living in the
halls this quarter and who desire
to change rooms are eligible to par
ticipate in the drawing.
