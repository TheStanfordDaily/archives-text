# TRAFFIC COURT JUDGE DEALS WITH STUDENTS
## 
### 
Traffic violations of six Stanford
students were dealt with by Judge
John E. Springer in the Palo Alto
traffic court on Tuesday afternoon.
Fines for speeding were imposed
upon the following students: John W.
Mitchell, $5; Wirth A. Brown, $2.50;
Harold A. White, $15; Willard A.
Miller, suspension of license for 30
days.
In addition to these, James B.
Fredericks and Margaret D. Whi
taker were each fined $1 for minor
violations.
