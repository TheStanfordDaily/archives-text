Why Shiver ?
Chase the Chill from Any Corner with
this Cheery Warmth
There is no excuse for giving up a part of your house in
cold weather to Old Man Chill.
A Perfection oil-burning room heater will put you in full
possession of every room in your house even in the cold
spells. A heater you can carry from place to place as you
j need the heat.
There are large ones and small ones—heaters finished in
bright colored porcelain enamel and nickel or in plain
black japan—and the new PERFECTION FIRELIGHT
Heater —with the Pyrex fire-bowl, transparent as glass
that gives a cozy radiant glow as bright and cheery as
an open fire.
Prices $6.25, $7.75, $10.75 up to $17.75
