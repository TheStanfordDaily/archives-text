# FRESNO PREACHER TO SPEAK SUNDAY
## 
### 
The preacher for the 11 o'clock
service in the Memorial Church
Sunday morning will be the Rev
Norman B. Henderson of the Firsl
Congregational Church in Fresno
His subject will be "Life as a Prob
lem or as an Experience." There
will be a chorale, Bach's "My Soul
Awake," and the Stanford Glee
Club, under the direction of C. A
. Kulmann, will sing a Netherlands
Folk Song, "Song of Thanksgiving.'
