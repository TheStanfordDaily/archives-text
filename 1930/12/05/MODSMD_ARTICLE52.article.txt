# REDSHIRT POLOISTS FACE OAK KNOLL IN INITIAL APPEARANCE
## 
### 
Polo enthusiasts will have their
first opportunity to see the Stanford
polo squad in action tomorrow af
ternoon, when the Cardinal riders
will clash with the Oak Knoll club
of Napa.
The game will be played on the
Encina field. The ball will be thrown
in at 2 o'clock.
Captain R. A. Gordan, Stanford
coach, will use two full teams in the
contest, each to play three chukkers
of the six-period game.
The starting quartet will be com
posed of Fred Carter, No. 1; "Chuck"
Lewis, No. 2: Captain Jack McDou
gal. No. 3; Rufus Spaulding, No. 4.
