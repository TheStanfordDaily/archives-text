# "S" SOCIETY PLANS WAFFLE BREAKFAST
## 
### 
Members of Women's "S" Society
will be entertained at a waffle
breakfast Sunday morning at the
home of Helen Masters Bunting, 736
Coronado. The affair will take place
at 10 o'clock, according 1o Mai*y
Edith Jones, president, who requests
that she be notified if any members
are unable to attend.
