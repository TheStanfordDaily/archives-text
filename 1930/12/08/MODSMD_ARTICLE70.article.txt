# GIANT PLANE FOR PROPOSED ATLANTIC SERVICE
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
Giant multimotored 40-passenger land plane, the first of a fleet to be constructed for the Imperial
Airways, is being tested in England for possible use on the proposed trans-Atlantic airmail, express
and passenger service between England and the United States. The plane has a speed of 100 miles an
hour and a cruising range of 3,000 miles.
