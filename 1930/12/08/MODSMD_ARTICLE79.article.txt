# COTTRELL NAMED ON C. OF C. COMMITTEE
## 
### 
Professor E. A. Cottrell of the
political science department has been
chosen a member of the Program of
Work Committee of the Palo Alto
Chamber of Commerce, it was an
nounced Friday.
It is the object of the committee to
recommend to the board of directors
projects which may be undertaken
by the Chamber of Commerce.
