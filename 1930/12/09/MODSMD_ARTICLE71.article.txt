# GRADE ENVELOPES ON SALE AT REGISTRAR'S
## 
### 
Students who wish to receive
grades at the end of the quarter
should leave stamped, addressed en
velopes at the Registrar's office for
that purpose, announces Karl M.
Cowdery, assistant registrar. Usual
envelopes for the benefit of the Con
valescent Home will be on sale for
five cents.
Those who do not leave envelopes
will not be able to receive their
grades until registration day next
quarter.
