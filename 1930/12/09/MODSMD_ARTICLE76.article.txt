# HELVENSTON SPEAKS TO MOTHERS TODAY
## 
### 
"Playmaking at Stanford" will be
the subject of a speech by Harold
Helvenston, dramatics director, be
fore the regular meeting of the Stan
ford Mothers' Club this afternoon at
3 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
After a short business meeting
with Mrs. T. A. Storey presiding,
Helvenston will discuss the work of
the student in dramatic productions
and the organization of casts and
selection of plays for production at
Stanford. Tea will be served after
the program.
