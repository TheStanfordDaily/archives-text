# PROFESSOR KIMBALL WILL SPEAK BEFORE ENGINEERS TONIGHT
## 
### 
Professor Dexter S. Kimball,
of the Graduate School of Business,
will address a joint meeting of the
Stanford Branches of the American
Institute of Electrical Engineers and
the American Society of Mechanical
Engineers at 7:30 o'clock tonight, in
the lobby of the Old Union.
Professor Kimball will speak on
the early days of Stanford, particu
larly concerning the engineering
the engineering school from which
he received his degree of engineering
in 1913. He is on leave this quar
ter from Cornell University, where
he is the dean of Sibley College.
All students interested are invited
to attend the meeting tonight, which
will be the last of either society for
this quarter. Refreshments will be
served following the meeting.
