# MEXICO CITY STREET NAMED AFTER FORD
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Jan. 5. </b- Mex
ico City is to have a Henry Ford
avenue.
The name plates have been
stamped and construction has been
started on "La Avenida Henry
Ford," which will bisect the Calzado
Guadalupe, where the new Ford
automobile plant will be built with
in the next few months.
