# CHAPPIE TRYOUTEES RATED FOR QUARTER
## 
### 
Ratings to date for the fall quar
ter tryoutees were announced yes
terday by Reidar Winther, circula
tion manager of Chaparral. They
are: Albert M. Russell, first; Robert
de Roos, second; Henry Reigh, third;
and William Engquist, fourth. Rat
ings are based on the merits of the
work done during the past quarter.
The circulation manager for 1931
and 1932 will be chosen in May from
these competing men.
