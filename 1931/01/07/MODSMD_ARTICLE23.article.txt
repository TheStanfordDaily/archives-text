# GOVERNMENT TO HALT ISLAND STRIKE SPREAD
## 
### 
MANILA, Jan. 6 (A 3 )—'The Philip
pine government took measures to
day to prevent development of seri
ous labor troubles on Negros Island
as an outgrowth of the strike of dock
workers of the Victorias sugar cen
tral for higher pay.
Reports indicated the strike might
spread to all dock workers of Negros
and eventually cause a general strike
throughout the island.
