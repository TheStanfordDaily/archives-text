# FELLOWSHIP BOARD TO HOLD FIRST TEA OF QUARTER TODAY
## 
### 
Pauline Wilson and Caroline Hall
will be joint hostesses at the first
Fellowship Tea of the quarter, which
will be held this afternoon from 4:30
to 5:30 o'clock in the Fellowship
Board room on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. Everyone is
invited to attend.
The Stanford Fellowship Board,
which promotes closer acquaintance
ship and friendship between pro
fessors and students, sponsors these
afternoon teas, which are held every
day except Saturday and Sunday
throughout the quarter.
Tomorrow Susan Noble and Nancy
Jones will be hostesses; on Friday,
"Elizabeth West and Lucille Morgan.
The hostesses for next week will be
announced later.
