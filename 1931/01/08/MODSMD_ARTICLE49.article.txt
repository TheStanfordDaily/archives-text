# BIRD CENSUS TAKEN
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 7 (/P)—
The wild bird census is over, and
in spite of non-co-operation of many
of the fowl-population, the Audubon
Association of the Pacific reported
counting 11,784 birds this year
within a 15-mile radius of San Fran
cisco.
