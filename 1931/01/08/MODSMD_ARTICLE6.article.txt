# SOPH ASPIRANTS FOR YELL-LEADER TO MEET TODAY
## 
### 
Sophomores desiring to try
out for assistant yell leader
should report at 1:30 o'clock to
day in the Pavilion, according
to an announcement made by
George McKee, yell leader.
Tryoutees will be given ele
mentary instruction in cheer
leading and exhibited at games,
as provided in the student body
constitution. A final cut, one
month before the May elections,
will be made. Two assistants
will be elected by the students
from this group.
