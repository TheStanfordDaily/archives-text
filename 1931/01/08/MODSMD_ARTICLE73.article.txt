# SALT LAKE FREEZES
## 
### 
SALT LAKE CITY, Jan. 7 f/P)—
Great Salt Lake, one of the saltiest
bodies of water in the world, has
succumbed to the cold—long regard
ed as next to impossible. Ice was
found on the lake yesterday for the
first time in the history of the
weather bureau here.
