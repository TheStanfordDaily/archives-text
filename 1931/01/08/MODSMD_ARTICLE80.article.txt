# GLEE CLUB ARRANGES FOR ANNUAL BANQUET
## 
### 
At its first meeting of the winter
quarter the Stanford Glee Club last
night made tentative plans to hold
the annual initiation banquet for the
newly elected members on Wednes
day evening, January 21.
Members have been arranging
dates for the club's spring trip to
Los Angeles and environs, which
will be held at the close of the
present quarter. It is possible that
the club will make a short sound
subject for a Hollywood film com
pany sometime before the summer
vacation.
