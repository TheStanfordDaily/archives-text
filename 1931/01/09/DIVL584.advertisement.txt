STANFORD UNIVERSITY MEMORIAL CHURCH
Without ideals, life is mean—
Without purpose, it is flat—
Without inspiring power, it will fail.
The Church can give to man ideals, purposes, power.
In the lives of prophets and heroes and in the life
of jesus Christ, ihe Church holds up the ideals by which
character and achievement must be measured.
In the call to help build the Kingdom of Cod on earth
the Church presents the purpose which can give new
meaning to everything men do.
In common faith and co operation of purpose, the
Church releases through human lives the transforming
power of Cod.
You .ire cordially invited to attend the church services
