# WOMEN'S TRYOUTS FOR DEBATING TO BE HELD MONDAY
## 
### 
Winter quarter tryouts for wo
men's debate will be held Monday
afternoon at 1:15 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. All campus
women are eligible.
Old debaters should call Ben
Duniway, Local 151; all new women
interested, even though they are
unable to attend tryouts on Monday,
should get in touch with Marie
Baker, women's debate manager, at
Palo Alto 5189.
Debates have already been sched
uled with Oregon State College in
February on the Ghandi in India
question. Additional forensic meets
have been arranged with San Fran
cisco State Teachers College, U.S.C.,
U.C.L.A., and with Parliament, wo
men's debating society at California.
