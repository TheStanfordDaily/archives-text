# JANUARY CHAPPIE TELLS TALES OF STANFORD ROUGH
## 
### 
TIIF Stanford rough, popular
through mythical campus
character, will he glorified by
the coming issue of the Chap
arral, according to press no
tices seeping from the publica
tion's offices today.
Burnell Gouid, editor, and
his staff are even amusing
themselves with the riotous
drawings and articles sched
uled to appear on January 15.
The cover is drawn by Thor
ington Putnam, and depicts a
touching scene on the Law
Steps. Other artists represented
are Berk Anthony and Louis
Rogers, art director of the Sun
set Press. Literary gems are
from the pens, or better, type
writers, of Burned Gould, Paul
Lorton. and Joe Thompson.
