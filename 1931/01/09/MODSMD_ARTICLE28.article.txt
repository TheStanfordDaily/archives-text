# MRS. SABINA BURKS CLAIMED BY DEATH
## 
### 
Mrs. Sabina D. Burks, mother of
Mrs. George B. Culver, died last
night, after a brief illness, at the
home of her daughter, 715 Santa
Ynez. Mrs. Burks was a resident of
Los Angeles until 10 years ago when
she came to Palo Alto to live.
Funeral services will be held to
morrow morning at 9:30 o'clock at
the chapel of Roller and Hapgood.
They will be strictly private and
friends are asked not to send flow
ers. The Rev. Paul Offenhiser will
officiate.
