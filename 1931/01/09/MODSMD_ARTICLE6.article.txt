# ALDEN TO READ
## 
### 
Betty Alden will read this Sunday
at a short vespers service to be held
from 5:15 to 5:30 o'clock in the
Y.W.C.A. rooms on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse. Rhoda
Manning will play the 'cello. All
Stanford women are invited to at
tend. This Sunday, Maria Lynch
will be in charge of the service.
