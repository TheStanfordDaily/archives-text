# PEIRCE, ALLEN WILL GIVE ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Winter quarter organ recitals at
the Memorial Church will be re
sumed Sunday afternoon at four
o'clock. Warren D. Allen will be as
sisted in his first program by Miss
Elizabeth Peirce, violinist, who will
play the first movement of Mozart's j
Concerto and Shubert's Litany.
The organ numbers will include a
new arrangement of Graneville Ban
tock's Processional from the "Song
of Songs." Chorale Preludes by
Bach, and an arrangement of "Ring .
Out, Wild Bells," by Gounod.
