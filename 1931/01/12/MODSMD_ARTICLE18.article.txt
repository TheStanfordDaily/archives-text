# ARRANGEMENTS MADE FOR RIFLE PRACTICING
## 
### 
Starting tomorrow afternoon, Cap
tain Ittai Luke of the ordnance de
partment will coach any students
desiring instruction in rifle and pis
tol shooting in the rifle range in the
basement of the Administration
Building. This decision was made
following a meeting of the Rifle Club
last week.
The range will be open every af
ternoon from 2 to 5 o'clock, and
Saturday morning from 9 to 12
o'clock. Captain Luke will coach the
Rifle Club on Monday, Tuesday, and
Wednesday from 4 to 5 o'clock. Stu
dents interested in either pistol or
rifle instruction may come to the
range any afternoon. Rifles will be
furnished and ammunition provided
at nominal cost.
