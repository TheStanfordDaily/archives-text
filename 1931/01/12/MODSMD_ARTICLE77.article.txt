# WOMEN WILL ENROLL FOR NOON SWIMMING
## 
### 
Campus women interested in a
noon open hour daily for swimming
at Roble Pool are requested by the
secretary of the Department of
Physical Education for Women to
enroll immediately; for unless a suf
ficiently large number desire to
swim daily the pool will be open
only twice a week at the lunch hour.
Regular open hours for swimming
will be daily at 3 o'clock, and Sat
urday morning from 10:30 until 12
o'clock. Phyllis Linscott will be in
charge of the pool and a series of
five cent tov/el tickets may be pur
chased at the Cashier's office in the
Administration building.
