# WOMEN'S GYM FUND SWELLED BY HOPKINS
## 
### 
Timothy Hopkins of San Fran
cisco, member of the Board of Trus
tees, today contributed $50 to the
Women's Gymnasium Fund, by
which, together with contributions
made last quarter, and receipts from
the carnival, the fund is raised to
approximately $3000.
Donors during the autumn quar
ter were Mrs. Harris J. Ryan, Dr.
Bertha Dyment, Miss Gertrude
Hawley, and J. E. McDowell.
Timothy Hopkins, for whom the
reading room in the Stanford library
is named, has been a generous con
tributor to the Convalescent Home
as well.
The sum still to be raised by the
Women's Gymnasium Fund amounts
to $17,000.
