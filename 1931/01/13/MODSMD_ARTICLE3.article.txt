# WOMEN'S RUSHING FOR FIRST PERIOD OVER
## 
### 
Tonight will terminate the first
period of women's rushing after
four days of luncheon and dinner
dates. The second period starts Fri
day after two days of silence.
Last period invitations will be
distributed tomorrow night and re
plies will be made on Thursday.
Rushing during this period will ex
tend from dinner dates on Friday
through dinner on the following
Wednesday. Pledging will take place
a week from Thursday.
