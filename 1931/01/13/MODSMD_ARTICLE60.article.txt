# COSTS OF DIVORCE MAY BE INCREASED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Jan. 12. (JP) A
further penalty on divorce by in
creasing the fees for the filing of
actions to provide more money for
the care of destitute children was
recommended to the state legisla
ture in a resolution adopted today
by the county boar dol" supervisors.
Tiie resolution, by the board's ac
tion, will be put in the form of a
bill and formally olft red to the leg
islature for adoption.
