# SWIMMERS TO TAKE STROKE SPEED TESTS
## 
### 
Varsity and freshman swimmers
will participate in a series of speed
tests to be held under the supervi
sion of Coach Ernst Brandsten and
Assistant Coach Ted Smith Thurs
day afternoon in the Encina pool.
Season marks in the various free
style events, and also in the back
and breast stroke races are expected
to be lowered during the tests,
which will be held at intervals
throughout the winter quarter.
Among varsity swimmers will be
Austin Clapp, Tuppy Gardner, Jack
McKelvey, John Trotter, and Frank
Booth. Ted Wiget will be the out
standing freshman in the tests, ac
cording to Brandsten.
