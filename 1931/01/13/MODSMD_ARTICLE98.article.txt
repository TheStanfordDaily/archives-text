# TOWNSEND TO SPEAK ON HUNTING IN INDIA
## 
### 
W. A. Townsend, graduate stu
dent in education, will speak on his
hunting experiences in India at a
meeting of the Palo Alto American
Legion post Friday evening. The
meeting will be held at the Com
munity House at 8 o'clock.
Townsend spent three years hunt
ing big game in India, and his talk
is expected to contain much of in
terest.
While in India Townsend killed
an 11-foot tiger, one of the largest
ever bagged by a white man. The
skin of this tiger and other interest
ing trophies will be on exhibit.
Stanford Legionnaires arc invited
to attend the meeting.
