# APPOINTMENT HEAD ANNOUNCES REVISION OF OFFICE SCHEDULE
## 
### 
Announcing a new set of office
hours, Mis. Elizabeth Snell, appoint
ment secretary, yesterday listed her
new schedule as follows: For inter
views with students, 1:30 to 3:30,
Monday to Friday inclusive; for in
terviews impossible to be placed at
another time, 10:30 to 11:30, Mon
day through Friday; for very brief
talks, 9:30 to 10:30 daily except Sat
urday. Interviews with people off
the campus will be held Saturday,
and this day will be reserved for
the purpose.
Twenty-four hours' notice must
be given Mrs. Snell to obtain ap
pointments, in order that records
may be consulted.
,"We must have schedule cards of
students registered in the Appoint
ment Service," Mrs. Snell stated
yesterday. "Leisure time as well as
hours in class should be given, in
order that we may be able to locate
students quickly."
