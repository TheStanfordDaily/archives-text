# BRANNER WILL HOLD ELECTION THURSDAY
## 
### 
Branner Club will hold its regu
lar semi-annual election of officers
next Thursday evening, January 22,
in the club lobby from 7 to 9
o'clock.
Nominations for the vacant offices
must bear the signatures of 15
members of Branner Club and must
he posted on the club bulletin board
when completely filled out. All
nominations close Monday evening,
January 19, at 8 o'clock.
The following seven positions,
which bear office for the ensuing
five months, arc declared open:
president, vice-president, secretary,
manager, and senior, junior, and
sophomore representatives.
