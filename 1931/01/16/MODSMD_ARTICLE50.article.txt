# MELISH TO PREACH ON CAMPUS SUNDAY
## 
### 
Reverend Howard Melish, D.D.,
preacher of the Church of the Holy
Trinity at Brooklyn, New York, will
preside at the regular 11 o'clock
worship services of the Memorial
Church, Sunday, January 18. The
musical program accompanying the
sermon will include two organ se
lections from Handel, the "Largo in
E Major," and parts from"The Mes
siah," played by Warren D. Allen. .
"Come Unto Him" will be sung
by the soloist, Noel Walster, and the
chorus is to give "And the Glory
of the Lord."
