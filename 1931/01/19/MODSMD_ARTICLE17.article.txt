# KITCHELL TO SPEAK ON INDIAN SUBJECT IN CAMPUS LECTURE
## Famous Traveler And Lecturer To Describe Glacier Park 
### 
"Glacier National Park and the
Blackfoot Indians" will be the sub
ject of the regular Tuesday evening
lecture to bp given in the Little
Theatre at 7:30 o'clock Tuesday
night by Laurence D. Kitchell, noted
traveler and lecturer.
Kitchell is well-known to uni
versity audiencos all over the United
States. He has lectured at Harvard,
Yale, Princeton, Cornell, and many
other colleges and universities. His
talk will be illustrated with colored
motion pictures and stereoptican
views.
The program is being sponsored
by the Committee on Public Exer
cises. The public is invited.
