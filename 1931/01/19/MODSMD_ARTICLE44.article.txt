# NOMINATIONS CLOSE FOR BRANNER CLUB POSITIONS TONIGHT
## 
### 
Nominations of candidates for the
seven positions declared open in
Branner Club will close tonight at
8 o'clock. The nominations must
bear the signatures of 15 club mem
bers, and when completely filled out
must be posted on the club bulletin
board.
The vacant positions which are to
be filled for the ensuing five months'
period are: president, vice-presi
dent, secretary, manager, and senior,
junior, and sophomore representa
tives.
The election for these offices will
be held next Thursday evening from
7 to 9 o'clock in the club lobby.
