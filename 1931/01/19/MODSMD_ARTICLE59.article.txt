# GRADUATES' WEDDING ANNOUNCED RECENTLY
## 
### 
The marriage of the former Flor
ence Lee Ogier, '30. and Abraham
L. Hawley, Jr., '30, which took place
December 30 in Alamgorde, N.M.,
was revealed only last Friday eve
ning when it was announced by the
bride at a Chi Omega rushing party
and by the groom at a dinner given
at the Sigma Chi house.
Florence Hawley, graduate in
political science, is the daughter of
James Lee Ogier, a member of a
pioneer San Jose family; and Abra
ham Hawley, Jr., graduate in min
ing engineering, is the son of an
El Paso, Texas, retired capitalist.
Both are registered at the Univer
sity this quarter.
