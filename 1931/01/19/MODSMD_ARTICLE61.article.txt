# STAMP COLLECTORS WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Palo Alto Philatelic Society
extends an invitation to all Stanford
students, both men and women, who
are interested in stamp collecting,
to attend its regular semi-monthly
meeting tonight at 7:30 o'clock in
the Community House.
Tonight's meeting will be given
over to swapping and a discussion
of current news of the hobby. Stu
dents who have been attending the
gatherings which have been held
on the first and third Monday eve
nings of each month for the past
two years at the Chamber of Com
merse should notice the change in
meeting place.
