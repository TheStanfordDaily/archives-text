# SOPHOMORE DIAMOND MANAGER JOBS OPEN
## 
### 
Sophomores interested in baseball
managerships must turn out this
week at the latest, Richard Grant,
senior manager, announces. Try
outees may report to Grant at the
Board of Athletic Control offices
any afternoon between 2 and 4
o'clock, preferably on Wednesday.
Lower Division physical education
requirements are fulfilled by the
managerial activity, Grant states.
Sophomore managers are expected
to report for baseball practice three
days each week for the remainder
of the season, with a chance for pro
motion to junior manager
