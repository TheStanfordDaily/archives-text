# STUDENT FUND LEFT BY PHILANTHROPIST
## 
### 
PORTLAND, Ore., Jan. 20. <7P»—
Ben Selling, Portland pioneer and
philanthropist, who died here Janu
ary 15, provided in his will that a
$100,000 trust fund be established to
aid needy and worthy students in
obtaining an education.
Under the terms of the will, re
vealed today, the scholarship fund
would bo administered by a bank
here as a perpetual trust.
