# HOUSE OF DAVID CASH WILL BE UNDISTURBED
## 
### 
ST. JOSEPH, Mich., Jan. 20. (JP)
—Prospects of court action to force j
the disclosure of the hiding place
of the reputed wealth of the late
"King" Benjamin Purnell, founder
of the House of David, faded today I
as H. T. Dewhirst, present leader
of the colony, withdrew his suit for ;
a writ of discovery.
