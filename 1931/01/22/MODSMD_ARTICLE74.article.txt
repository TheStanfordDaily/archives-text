# BROWN IN LIST FOR COUNTY GRAND JURY
## 
### 
Buford Otis Brown, associate pro
fessor of journalism, is in the venire
from which the county grand jury
for the current year will be se
lected. The list announced today in
San Jose was prepared by Superior
Judges J. R. Welch, F. B. Brown,
and P. F. G. Gosbey.
Nineteen grand jurors will be
chosen from the list some time in
the future when the grand jury
which is now impaneled is dis
charged by Judge Welch.
