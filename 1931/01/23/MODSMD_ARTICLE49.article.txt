# WRENN TO SPEAK ON CHOICE OF VOCATIONS
## 
### 
Speaking on the general subject
of "Choosing a Vocation,"' Charles
Wrenn, executive secretary of the
Vocational Guidance Committee,
will talk to seniors of Palo Alto
High School next Tuesday afternoon.
One of the main objects of the
Vocational Guidance Committee is
to help high school graduates and
also local University students to get
definitely in mind some vocation for
which they Can prepare during their
college career.
