# CHAMP DOGS ENTER
## 
### 
TRUCKEE, Jan. 25 (/P). — Eight
teams already have been entered for
the Tahoe-Sierra dog derby begin
ning here February 14, Wilbur May
nard, committee chairman, an
nounced. Roy Stover, Sacramento,
who won the cup last year, has en
tered his winning team.
