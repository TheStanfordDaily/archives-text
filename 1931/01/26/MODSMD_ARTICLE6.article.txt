# READING TO BE GIVEN AT VESPERS SERVICE
## 
### 
Barbara Boggs will read from
Kahlil Gibran't book, "The Prophet,"
at Vespers next Sunday. Catherine
Spaulding will play the piano. The
service will be held as usual from
5:15 to 5:30 o'clock in the Y.W.C.A.
rooms on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. Olive Mott will
be in charge. All Stanford women
are invited to attend.
