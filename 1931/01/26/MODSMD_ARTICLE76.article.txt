# CAMPUS GARDENER RECOVERING AFTER FALL INJURES HIP
## 
### 
Following a fall which cost him
a broken hip, Joshua Davis, cam
pus gardener, is slowly recovering in
the Palo Alto Hospital.
Davis is a well-known figure on
the campus where ho has served for
over 39 years. In the early days he
was Mrs. Stanford's gardener and
following that position he became
gardener to the Zeta Psi house. This
was in '95, when the house was on
Lasuen, in what old-timers will re
member as the "old Oyster place."
Previous to his fall Davis was
gardener to Dean Mary Yost, and
to the Alpha Delta Phi, Delta
Gamma, Sigma Chi, and Kappa
Kappa Gamma houses.
