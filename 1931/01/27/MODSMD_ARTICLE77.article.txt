# FELLOWSHIP BODY TO CONVENE TOMORROW
## 
### 
The Stanford Fellowship Board
will meet in the board's room on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house at 8 o'clock tomorrow night
to elect a chairman to succeed
Stewart Warner, who is not regis
tered in the University this quarter.
Plans for visits to professors'
houses on Sunday evenings and for
the support yf the regular afternoon
teas sponsored by the Fellowship
will be discussed, according to the
executive board, and the list of
regular hostesses will be revised.
All hostesses and other members of
the board are urged to be present at
this meeting.
