# ITALIAN CONSUL SHOT
## 
### 
ZURICH, Switzerland, Jan. 27. (/P)
—M. V. Bianchi, Italian constil gen
eral here, was shot three times today
by a former Italian soldier named
Bassi, whom Bianchi had declined
to give financial aid.
