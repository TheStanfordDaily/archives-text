# JONES ELECTED HEAD OF FELLOWSHIP BOARD
## 
### 
Piobert Jones was elected Presi
dent. of the Stanford Fellowship
Board at a meeting of the board
last night in the Women's Clubhouse.
Df. and Mrs. Gardner, Dr. and Mrs.
Treat were elected faculty advisors
to the group.
The Fellowship will no longer hold
tea on Friday this quarter but will
cooperate with the Y.W.C.A. in their
regular Friday afternoon faculty
teas.
A committee to arrange for Sun
day evening visits to professors'
houses was appointed and the date
of the first visit was set for Sunday,
February 8. A publicity committee
and a committee for provisions for
the teas were also named.
