# MARGARET ABRAMS ELECTED TO HEAD ALL JUNIOR WOMEN
## 
### 
Margaret Abrams was elected pres
ident of the junior women yesterday
noon at a meeting held in the Wom
en's Clubhouse by the class for the
purpose of re-ftrganizing.
The class voted to go to Asilomar
on the weekend of February 27, and
tentative plans for a roller-skating
party and outdoor picnics were made.
A cabinet consisting of four mem
bers from Roble, and one from each
of the houses and the Commuters
Club will be elected by the respec
tive groups today. The first meeting
of the cabinet will be held Wednes
day night at 7 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
