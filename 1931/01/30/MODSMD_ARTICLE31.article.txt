# THOMAS PRESIDENT OF CERCLE FRANCAIS
## 
### 
Helen Thomas was unanimously
named president of the Cerele Fran
cais at a meeting of the club the
Delta Gamma house last. Miss
Thomas immediately took the chair
find led a lively discussion of Euro
pean travel problems. Professor W.
L. Schwartz, Joseph Donnay and
others contributed interesting talks
concerning how to get around in
France.
