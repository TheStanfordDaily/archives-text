# ZONA OWENS LEADS VESPERS ON SUNDAY
## 
### 
Zona Owens and Emily Chase will
take part in the Y.W.C.A. vespers
to be held Sunday afternoon from
5:15 to 5:30 o'clock. According to
Winona Woon, who is in charge of
the services, they will be on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house. The public is invited.
Reading by Zona Owens will con
sist of poetry having the ocean as
its theme. Emily Chase will offer
piano selections.
