# PASSENGERS RESCUED
## 
### 
TOKYO, Feb. 1. (/P) —Passengers
and crew of the liner Arabia Maru,
from Vancouver, 8.C., to Tokyo,
successfully were transferred to the
steamer Takao Maru after the two
steamers collided at the entrance of
Tokyo Bay this afternoon.
