# BUSINESS EXPERT WILL LECTURE ON INDUSTRY
## 
### 
Dr. Earl Dean Howard, professor
of economics at Northwestern Uni
versity and vice-president of Hail,
Schaffner and Marx Company, will
arrive here today to give a series
of lectures in the courses in the
Graduate School of Business on the
problems of industrial relations.
Dr. Howard has made a careful
study of this subject and has had
much practical experience as labor
manager for Hart, Schaffner and
Marx. He will remain here during
the month of February.
