# GORDY WHITE MAKES HOLE IN ONE ON LINKS
## 
### 
It can be done! A hole in one was
the record of Gordy White on the
seventeenth hole, when he was
playing on the Stanford golf course
Saturday morning. The seventeenth
hole is 165 yards with a par score
of 3. White was playing in a four
some which consisted of Bob Gold
water, Herman Hellman, and Stew
art Hanley.
