# AVIATION ADVANTAGE IS SUBJECT OF TALKS
## 
### 
Advantages of aeronautical in
struction in this era will be the
subject of two short talks to be
given by Captain W. H. Royle and
Les McMurtry, field directors of the
Palo Alto School of Aviation, at an
open meeting of the school to be
held tomorrow night at 7:30 o'clock
in the flying field classroom on the
campus.
A general invitation to the meet
ing is extended to all students in
terested in aviation. In addition to
the talks, motion pictures will be
shown illustrating certain points of
aviation instruction.
