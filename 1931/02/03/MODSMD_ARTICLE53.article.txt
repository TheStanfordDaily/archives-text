# THOMAS WILLIAMS DIES AFTER SHORT ILLNESS
## 
### 
Thomas Burton Williams, '31. died
of pneumonia in the Palo Alto Hos
pital early yesterday afternoon, fol
lowing an illness of several days.
Williams was a geology major,
making a special study of micro
paleontology under Dr. Schenk, and
was a member of the Geological and
Mining Society. He roomed in Toyon
Hall.
His mother, Mrs. J. B. Williams
of Los Angeles, has been here for
several days and was with him when
he passed away. Interment will be
in Los Angeles.
