# ENGLISH CLUB WILL DISCUSS SHORT FILM
## 
### 
To assist those who are planning
to write plots for the English Club
motion picture, which is to be filmed
next quarter, the Club will present
"The Fall of the House of Usher"
tomorrow night at 7:45 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse.
This one-reel picture will be dis
cussed, and the kind of story de
sired for the projected picture will
be explained. The motion picture to
be made next quarter will be the
first of two three-reel cinemas which
will be produced by the English
Club.
