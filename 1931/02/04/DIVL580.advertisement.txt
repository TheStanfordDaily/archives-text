CLASSIFIED ADVERTISING
All classified advertisements must be received at the business office of
The Stanford Daily by 5 p.m., day previous to publication. Advertisements
must be paid in advance. Office hours: 12 a.m. and 1-5 p.m. lMioue 4717.
RATES: 15 line minimum: for one issue, 35e; two issues, (JOc; three issues,
75c; four issues, 90c; five issues, $1.00. For each issue thereafter 15c.
Five cents per day for each additional line. Count live words to the line.
