# BISHOP CANNON FILES ANSWER TO CHARGES
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 3 </P). Ec
clesiastical fireworks, with Bishop
James Cannon, Jr., at their center,
were expected today as Southern
Methodism's most widely known
prelate answered charges filed aginst
him by four of his fellow clergymen.
The display, however, were not to
be heard or seen by lay ears or eyes,
for word has gone forth from those
in charge that the church will veil
the proceedings with all the secrecy
in its powev.
