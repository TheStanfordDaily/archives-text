LAKEHURST, N.J., Feb. 4 (/P).-- -
Just as tin sun was setting today the
navy dirigible Los Angeles, with 48
men aboard, took off from the uuval
air station to join the United States
fleet in war maneuvers off Panama.
ON 3 WEEK-ENDS*
Eoundtrip

Southern Pacific offers round
trips to Tahoe and Truckec for
less than the one-way fare! All

jump and toboggan slide at
Truckce,.
Overnight Pullmans direct to
Truckce anil Tahoe Tavern
every night from San Francisco.
Also day service.

day and Sunday). Famous pro

f.ire, you can leave Feb. 6 or 7.
