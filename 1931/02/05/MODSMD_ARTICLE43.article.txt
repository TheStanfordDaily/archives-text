# DR. LYMAN TO GIVE THIRD MEDICAL TALK
## 
### 
''The Odyssey of John Marsh: The
First American Physician and Sur
geon in California" is the talk to
be given Friday by George D. Ly
man, M.D., lecturer in pediatrics of
the Stanford School of Medicine.
This will be third of the popular
medical lectures delivered on al
ternate Friday evenings at 8 o'clock
in Lane Hall, Sacraniento street,
near Webster, San Francisco.
