# OWEN D. YOUNG NOT SEEKING PRESIDENCY
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 4 (#>).—
Owen D. Young today told news
paper correspondents he was not a
candidate for the Presidency.
Author of the Young reparations
plan, a powerful industrialist and
prominent Democrat, he has been
mentioned frequently for the Presi
dential nomination of that party next
year.
He made the statement after testi
fying before the Senate committee
investigating banking conditions and
the House Ways and Means Com
mittee which is looking into the
question of cashing the soldier's
compensation certificates.
