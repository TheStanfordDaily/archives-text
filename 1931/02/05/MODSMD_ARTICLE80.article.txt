# BOYS GET CO-ED RITZ What! No Women? Passe BALK DANCE CRASHERS
## 
### 
WAYNESBURG, Pa., Feb. 4 (/P)
' Powdered noses were held high at
Waynesburg College today, as dain
ty co-eds snubbed college boys and
I the "zero hour" in this school's
j campus "war" drew nigh.
I Tonight's the night of the girls'
! "manless dance"—the penalty im
; posed by the co-eds because the
j boys escorted non-college girls to
I a recent campus social event.
| There was much whispering
| among the boys. Listen! Some of
! them are going to attempt to crash
j the dance disguised as girls.
There was much giggling in the
camp of girls. Sh-h-h—there'll be a
1 couple of husky policemen stationed
on the inside.
