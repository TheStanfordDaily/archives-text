# SPRING GRID TRAINING ABOLISHED BY ARMY
## 
### 
WEST POINT, N. Y„ Feb. 5 (/P).—
Spring training of Army's football
squad has been abolished, Major
Philip B. Fleming, graduate man
ager of athletics at the United States
Military Academy, announced today
following a meeting of the Athletic
Council.
The action of the council was
based on the theory that, each sport
has a definite season and that the
spring months should be devoted
to activities appropriate to that pe
riod of the year, Major Fleming
said.
