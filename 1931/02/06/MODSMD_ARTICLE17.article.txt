# SILLIMAN ELECTED TO HEAD CIVIL ENGINEERS
## 
### 
Julian W. Silliman, graduate stu
dent in the School of Engineering,
was elected president of the Stan
ford chapter of the American So
ciety of Civil Engineers at the regu
lar mid-year elections held Wednes
day night. At the same time, Cyril
Thomas was elected vice-president
and Tom Bedford treasurer. J. G.
Sutherland was named to the post
of recording secretary, and Michael
Kcrner was elected to the post of
corresponding secretary.
The chapter decided not to hold
the regular social meeting next
month, but instead to attend the
meeting of the San* Francisco section
in San Francisco Tuesday, February
17. Larry Bryan, outgoing president,
presided at the chapter meeting.
