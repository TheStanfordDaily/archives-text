# AUTHORITIES WARN STUDENTS TO PAY FOR PHONE CALLS
## 
### 
Students who have made any un
authorized long distance telephone
calls over the local dormitory ex
change, Palo Alto 3121, which sup
plies Encina, Toyon, Branner, and
the Union, are advised to pay for
them at the A.S.S.U. office immedi
ately. Unpaid calls will be traced by
the telephone company and names
will be given to Men's Council for
action.
The University was forced to
cover bills amounting to $200 for
long distance calls made during May
and from September to November.
The privilege of night dial phones in
the dormitories may be withdrawn
if it continues to be abused by resi
dents, authorities indicated.
