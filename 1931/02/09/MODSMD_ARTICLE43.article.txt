# ‘PAULINE PATRIOTISM’ SUBJECT OF SERMON
## 
### 
"We shall never get rid of war
until we get rid of race prejudice
and false nationalism," said (he
Reverend Edgar A. Lowther, minis
ter of the First Methodist Episcopal
Church of Oakland, in his sermon
in the Memorial Church yesterday.
Dr. Lowther, taking as the sub
ject of his sermon "Pauline Patriot
ism Up To Date," emphasized the
need of a patriotism not limited by
the boundaries of one country, but
meaning esesntially a love for all
peoples and all countries.
