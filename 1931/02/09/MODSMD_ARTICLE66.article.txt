# RODE, SAUTER ENTER IN HANDBALL FINALS
## 
### 
John Rode, a long odds "dark
horse," defeated Clive Warner Fri
day, 22-20, 21-17, in the semi-finals
of the annual University hand-ball
tournament. In the second match of
the semi-finals John Sauter defeated
Harlan Reyburn, 21-10. 21-15.
The final match in the tournament
will be played off Wednesday after
