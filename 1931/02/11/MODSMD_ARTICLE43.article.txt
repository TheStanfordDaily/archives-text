# MARTIN WILL HOLD OPEN HOUSE FOR FELLOWSHIP BOARD
## 
### 
Percy Alvin Martin, Professor of
European and Spanish-American
History, has granted the request of
the Stanford Fellowship Board to
hold open house from 8 to 9 o'clock
this Sunday evening for members
of the Fellowship and others who
are interested.
The visit of Dr. Martin is the first
of a series of similar Sunday eve
ning visits to professors' houses to
be sponsored by the Stanford Fel
lowship during the quarter.
A list will be posted in the Fel
lowship Room on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse and those
who desire to visit Dr. Martin next
Sunday are urged to sign up there

promptly, as no more than twenty
can be conveniently accommodated
