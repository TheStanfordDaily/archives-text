# SAUTER FAVORED FOR HANDBALL CHAMPION
## 
### 
John Sauter and Lincoln Rode
meet this afternoon on the D.U.
court to decide the handball cham
pionship of the school. Rode, dark
horse of the recent tournament, is
expected to give Sauter a hard fight
for this honor. Sauter is a last year's
varsity veteran and is favored to
win the match.
