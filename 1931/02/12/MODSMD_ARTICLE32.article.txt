# NEW PET FOR WHITE HOUSE
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
This little pup, a two-months-old pedigreed Norwegian elkhound,
came ail the way from the kennels of Dr. Dyrlage T. Hensen in Norway
to be a pet of President Hoover. The dog, photographed in New York,
will replace the President's shepherd. King Tut, which died.
