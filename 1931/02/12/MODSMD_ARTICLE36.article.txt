# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
12 noon—French Table in dining
room of Union.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of the Christian
Science Society on the top floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
FRIDAY
12:10 p.m.—Roger Williams Club
luncheon in the Stanford Union
Alcove.
3 p.m.—Aptitude test for medical
colleges in room 214. Fee of $1.
necessary at that time.
4-6 p.m.—Tea for the Political Sci
ence Department in the Y.W.C.A.
rooms.
SUNDAY
5:15 to 5:30 p.m.—Vespers service in
the Y.W.C.A. rooms in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
8 to 9 p.m.—The Stanford Fellow

ship visits Professor Percy Alvin
Martin at his home, 593 Salva
tierra.
About 1000 employes will be need
| ed to lay the new cable between Eu
rope and the United States.
