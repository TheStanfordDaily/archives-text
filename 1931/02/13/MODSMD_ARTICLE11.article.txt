# ALUMNAE PLAN FOR SPRING PARTY HERE
## 
### 
Plans for a get-together for Stan
ford alumnae living in the vicinity
to be held some time in the spring
were made at the quarterly meeting
of the Stanford Resident Alumnae
Association Wednesday afternoon.
Only $121.50 has been contributed
so far toward the $315 required for
the association's scholarship, accord
ing to Mrs. Caroline Hall, member
of the committee. Members were
urged to send in their contributions
as soon as possible.
