# STANFORD GLEE CLUB TO SING TOMORROW
## 
### 
Thirty members of the Stanford
Glee Club will appear tomorrow eve
ning at a Valentine supper dance
engagement at the Whitcomb Hotel
in San Francisco. The men were se
lected at a meeting of the glee club
last week on the basis of attendance.
Among the numbers which the
club will be prepared to sing are in
cluded two medleys of college songs.
Two light, humorous numbers, two
unaccompanied harmony numbers,
and a selection from a Verdi opera
complete the repertoire.
