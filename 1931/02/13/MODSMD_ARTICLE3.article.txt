# SYLVA WEAVER WEDS STANFORD MAN SOON
## 
### 
Invitations have been sent out for
the wedding of Sylva Weaver, Kappa
Kappa Gamma, to Edgar Tevis Smith
which will take place in the Beverly
Hills Community Church Friday
evening, February 27.
The bride-to-be was graduated
from Stanford last June and was
women's editor of the Daily. Smith
is an alumnus of the 3925 class.
