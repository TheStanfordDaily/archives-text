# ALPHA OMICRON PI OFFERS FELLOWSHIP
## 
### 
Alpha Omicron Pi offers a $1,000
fellowship for the college year 1931-
1932 to graduate students who are
not members of the sorority.
The successful applicant will not
be limited to any particular field of
work. She will be chosen on the
basis of her fitness for her chosen
profession, her attitude toward life
and her general needs and qualifi
cations. Announcement of the award
will be made May 1, 1931.
Application blanks and any fur
ther information desired can be ob
tained from the office of the Dean
of Women. To be considered, an ap
plication must be mailed before
Pdarch 1, 1931.
