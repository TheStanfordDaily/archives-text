# TICKETS GO ON SALE TODAY FOR U.C. GAME
## 
### 
Students must purchase tickets
for the third game of the Stanford-
California basketball series either
today or tomorrow, according to
Jake Irwin, ticket manager of the
Board of Athletic Control. Tickets
for this encounter, which will be
staged in the Oakland Auditorium
next Saturday, may be bought for
50 cents upon presentation of stu
dent body cards between the hours
of 9 and 12 o'clock and 1 and 5
o'clock at either the A.S.S.U. office
or the B.A.C. building.
