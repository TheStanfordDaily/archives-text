# THREE APPOINTMENTS MADE FOR FACULTY
## 
### 
Three appointments to the Stan
ford faculty were approved Friday
at the regular meeting of the .Stan
ford Board of Trustees. The new
members of the faculty are Dr.
Jessie W. M. DuMond, William C.
Putnam, and Mrs. Greta Johannsen
Brandsten.
Dr. DuMond has been appointed
Acting Associate Professor of Phys
ics. He is, at present, Research As
sociate in Physics at the California
Institute of Technology. Mr. Putnam
has been appointed Acting Instruc
tor in Geology, and Mrs. Brandsten,
Instructor in Swimming and Diving.
