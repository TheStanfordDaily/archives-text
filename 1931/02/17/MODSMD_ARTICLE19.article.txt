# MANAGEMENT ADDS EXTRA TELEPHONES FOR ENCINA HALL
## 
### 
It's a long, long journey from the
far rooms in the wings of Encina
Hall to the middle of the halls, as
many freshmen and former Encina
residents know.
The journey has for years seemed
exceptionally long when answering
a telephone call has been the object
of the trip. So long, in many cases,
that the traveler has lost a high
percentage of his phone calls.
Well, it seems that too many calls
were being lost to suit the manage
ment, so a force of electricians and
carpenters were called in. Now En
cina has a grand total of sixteen
telephones—one at each end of
every floor, one for the accommoda
tion of inhabitants of rooms over
the lobby, one in the middle of the
hall on the first floor, and two long
distance booths, also on the first
floor, a net increase of six tele
phones.
