# MEDICAL ALUMNUS DIES OF PNEUMONIA
## 
### 
Dr. Frederick C. Keck, graduate
of the Stanford Medical School, class
of 1892, died of pneumonia in San
Francisco yesterday after a brief
illness. Dr. Keck was a member of
the Stanford Alumni Association,
Union League Club, Crockett Lodge,
and the Olympic Club. Funeral ser
vices will be held tomorrow.
