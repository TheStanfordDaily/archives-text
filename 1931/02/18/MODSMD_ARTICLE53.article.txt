gineers.

third floor of Women's Clubhouse.

invited.

at the home of M. S. Rosenfiold,
Camino por los Arboles, Atherton.
7:30 p.m.—Two lectures under the
auspices of the Vocational Guid
ance Committee, in room 90,
Philosophy Building. "Opportuni
ties in Private Foreign Trade" by
Carl W. Kley, and "Opportunities
in Government Foreign Service"
by Alvin C. Eicholz.

ary' band fraternity, will hold a
smoker at the bandhouse. The

band is invited. Dr. Martin will
speak.
