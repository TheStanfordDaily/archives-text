# SOPHOMORE REUNION AT LUNCH THURSDAY
## 
### 
Another sophomore class reunion
will be held on Thursday noon when
the women students will meet for
lunch at 12:15 o'clock at the Union.
This affair, which is being planned
by the Sophomore Cabinet, is Ihe
third of a series of class parties held
throughout the year. All sophomore
women are urged to be present, for
the new chairman of the cabinet will
be introduced.
