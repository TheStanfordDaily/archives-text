# KLEY AND EICHHOLZ SPEAK AT CONFERENCE
## 
### 
Carl W. Kley, export manager of
the Crown Willamette Paper Com
pany, and Alvin C. Eichholz, as
sistant manager of the San Fran
cisco branch of the United States
Bureau of Foreign and Domestic
Commerce, were the speakers at an
informal vocational guidance con
ference held last night in Room 90
of the Philosophy Building.

Kley spoke on "Opportunities in
Private Foreign Trade," while Eich
holz devoted himself to a discussion
of the governmental and diplomatic
divisions of the general conference
subjcct, "Foreign Trade."
The conference was one of a series
being given to aid students in the
selection of a profession.
