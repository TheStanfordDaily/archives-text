# DAILY TRYOUTEE CUT WILL BE MADE SOON
## 
### 
Tryoutees for the Daily staff will
number approximately 20 after the
first cut Monday afternoon, accord
ing to Betty Clarke, women's editor
of the Daily.
Quality of writing, attendance at
meetings, and office work from the
beginning of tryouts last Monday
until next Monday afternoon are
considered in judging the tryoutees
who now number 26.
The next meeting will be this af
ternoon at 5 o'clock. Anyone unable
to comc must see Betty Clarke be
fore then.
