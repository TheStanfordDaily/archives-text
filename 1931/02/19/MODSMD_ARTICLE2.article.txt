# DR. MES TO SPEAK ON FELLOWSHIPS IN CLUBHOUSE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Dr. Margaretha Mes, who holds an
international scholarship awarded by
the American Association of Uni
versity Women at the University of
California, will talk in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock tonight on
the subject of Fellowships.
Dr. Mes, a former student at the
University of Utrecht in Holland, is
a Dutch woman from South Africa.
She is one of a very few who have
been awarded an international A.A.
U.W. scholarship.
The time of the talk has been set
ahead because of the concert at 8:15
o'clock.
