# GURLEY LECTURES IN ENGINEERS' MEETING
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Stan
ford student chapter of the Ameri
can Society of Civil Engineers, Lc
roy Gurley, editor of the national
trade journal "Welding," lectured
yesterday afternoon in the Little
Theatre before a group of engineer
ing students on the subject of weld
ing, emphasizing especially its ap
plication to structural 'engineering.
Gurley stressed the future need of
welding in the erection of steel
buildings, outlining its advantages
and the obstacles to be overcome be
fore it can entirely replace the pres
ent,method of steel riveting.
