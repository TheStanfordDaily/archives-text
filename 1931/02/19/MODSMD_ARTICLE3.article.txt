# WAGNER SELECTIONS TO FEATURE RECITAL
## 
### 
Compositions of Richard Wagner
will feature the regular Thursday
organ recital which Warren D. Allen,
University organist, will give this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock, in the
Memorial Church.
The complete program will be as
follows:
Adagio from the Sonata, Opus 108
Johannes Brahms
Prayer of the King (from "Lohen
grin") Richard Wagner
Scherzo in G Minor Schumann
Litany Franz Schubert
March from "Die Miestersingcr"..
Wagner
There will be no organ recital on
Washington's birthday, Sunday.
