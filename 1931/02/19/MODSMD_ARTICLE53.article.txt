# NOTRE DAME COACH UNABLE TO ACCEPT WARNER INVITATION
## 
### 
Knute K. Rockne, football coach
at Notre Dame, will be unable to
satisfy the hopes of Pop Warner and
the Stanford students with a visit
to the campus. Warner had previ
ously invited the South Bend wiz
ard to spend what time he could in
Palo Alto.
Notre Dame's illustrious coach in
an address over station KPO last
night extended some advice to boys
from 6 to 60 on just how to play
football. Rockne's remarks were
particularly pointed to players on
the coast with a consideration of the
special problems they have to face.
Rockne will address the California
student body this morning and then
give a few talks in certain San Joa
quin Valley points, before going to
Seattle where he is due for another
banquet in his Studebaker tour on
Monday.
