# TOYON DANCE WILL HAVE HECHE'S BAND
## 
### 
Gordon Heche's Rhythm Band has
been chosen to provide the music
for the Toyon Formal to be held in
Toyon Lobby tomorrow night, ac
cording to Myron Tower, chairman
of the dance committee.
The decorations of black and sil
ver in the futuristic style will be
executed by Barker Bates, while
Joe Gordon will provide the light
ing effects.
A limited number of bids are
available from Charles Coit, in 245
Toyon. Programs will be ready for
distribution today and may be ob
tained from Joe Gordon in 222
Toyon.
