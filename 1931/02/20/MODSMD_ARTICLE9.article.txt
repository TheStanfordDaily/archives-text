# COUNTY COMMITTEE GETS FACULTY MEN
## 
### 
Emeritus Professors R. L. Green
and Charles B. Wing are Stanford's
representatives on a committee of 50
persons appointed to meet with the
Santa Clara county board of super
visors and make recommendations to
it. The body was named by the
chairman of the board. Yesterday it
met in San Jose to consider a county
civic* center, court house, and jail.
