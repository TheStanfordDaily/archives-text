# CONSIDER 18TH AMENDMENT
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 23. (/P)—The
Supreme Court will return tomor
row from a three weeks' vacation
with a decision pending on the
validity of the Eighteenth Amend
ment. Its opinion in the Govern
ment's appeal may be handed down
during the day.
