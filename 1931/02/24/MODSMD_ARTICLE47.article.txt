# FRENCH CLUB MEETS
## 
### 
The Cercle Franca is, composed of
all students who are interested in
French, will meet at 7:30 o'clock
tomorrow night at Ihe Alpha Phi
house. Professor Albert Guerard
will be the guest of the club and
will speak on "French Attacks on
American Civilization." There will
be a short business meeting imme
diately after the address.
