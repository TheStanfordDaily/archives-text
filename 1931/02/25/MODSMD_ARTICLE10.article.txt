# SEATS ON SALE FOR COMING PRODUCTION
## 
### 
Tickets for "The Royal Family,
which will be presented in the As
sembly Hall on Friday, February
27, under the direction of Harold
Helvenston, may be obtained today.
The scat sale began yesterday,
and will continue at 1 o'clock today
in front of the Women's Clubhouse.
Tickets may also be obtained at
Roble during the noon hour and at
David Kecblc's in Palo Alto.
Tickets sell for $1.25 for the
downstairs section, $1.00 for the cen
ter balcony, and 75 cents for the
side balcony.
