# Y.W.C.A. WILL TEACH CONTRACT AT PARTY
## 
### 
Contract, as well as auction bridge,
will be taught again this Friday at
the regular Friday evening party of
the Y.W.C.A. For those who do not
care for bridge, other entertainment
will be provided. All Stanford
women are invited to the party,
which is from 7 to 9 o'clock in the
Y.W.C.A. rooms of the Women's Club
House.
