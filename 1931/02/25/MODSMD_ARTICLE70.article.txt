# GUERARD TO SPEAK ON NOVEL SUBJECT AT FRENCH CIRCLE
## 
### 
Albert L. Guerard, professor of
general and comparative literature,
will give a short talk tonight before
the Cercle Francais on the subject
"French Attacks on American Civili
zation." Professor Guerard is a na
tive of France but regrets the recent
tendency of French novelists to be
little America.

The Cercle Francais is a club
formed of students and professors
interested in French. The meeting
tonight, the second of the quarter,
will take place at 7:30 o'clock, at the
Alpha Phi House, 543 Lasuen. Any
one interested is cordially invited to
attend the meeting.
