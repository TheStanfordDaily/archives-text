# NOTED WRITER WILL TALK HERE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Henry F. Pringle, noted political
science writer and press correspond
ent, will speak on "Politicians and
the Press" before the Phi Sigma
Alpha, Honorary Political Science
fraternity, at a dinner meeting to
night ill the Union at 6:15 o'clock.
Pringle is a well known figure in
the political world and is considered
to be one of the eminent authori
ties on matters of Political Science.
He writes regularly for Harper's
magazine, and also has written
many books; the most popular of
which is his "Intimate Biography
of Alfred E. Smith," which came
out late last spring.
