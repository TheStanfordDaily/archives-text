# REGULAR DOUGHNUT SALE SLATED TODAY
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, national hon
orary journalistic society for women
will hold their weekly doughnut
sale this morning in front of the
Women's Club house and the Li
brary from 10 until 12 o'clock.
The sale is held for the purpose
of raising funds for the scholarship
which is presented each year to a
woman interested in the journalism
department. Last year the scholar
ship was awarded to Margaret Main.
