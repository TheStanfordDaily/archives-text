# DANCE DRAMA USES TRAGEDY AS THEME
## 
### 
Anatole France's picturesque and
tragic tale of "The Juggler of Notre
Dance," set to music and presented
in dance and pantomime by mem
bers of the advanced dancing class
under the direction of Gretchen K.
Botts, will be the feature of the
Dance Drama to be presented on
March 5 at 8 o'clock in the Assem
bly Hall.

Tickets for the Drama will be sold
under the auspices of the Women's
Athletic Association at the A.S.S.U.
offices, at Sherman and Clay in
Palo Alto, at Roble, and in the vari
ous houses. Beatrice Bishop, chair
man of the ticket committee, has an
nounced that the tickets will go on
sale Monday for 25 ceyts on pre
sentation of student body cards, and
for 50 cents without them.
