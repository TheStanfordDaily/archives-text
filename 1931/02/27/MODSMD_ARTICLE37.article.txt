# CAST SELECTED FOR NOTED GERMAN PLAY
## 
### 
Leads in the famous one-act Ger
man comedy, "Einer Mus Heiraten,"
will be taken by Eleanor Page,
Victoria Siegfried, Jack Gordon, and
Robert L. Piercy.
The play will be given under the
direction of S. L. Sharp in the Little
Theatre on March 5.
After the play the film, "A Zep
pelin Trip Over the Rhine," will be
shown. The public is invited to at
tend both performances.
