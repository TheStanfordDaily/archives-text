# GOLFERS COMPETE FOR CLASS TITLE TOMORROW MORN
## 
### 
Teeing off tomorrow morning at 9
o'clock, six foursomes of Indian
golfers will compete in 18 holes of
medal play for the interclass title.
The number one man of each six
man team will make up the first
foursome to leave the initial tee,
with the remaining groups following
in the order of their ranking.
Near-par golf is expected from
the first foursome, with MacNaugh
ton representing the seniors, Hell

man shooting for the juniors, Haw
ley for the sophomores, and Little
for the freshmen.
The second quartet should also
furnish some good divot digging
with Edmonds, Eichelberg, Rea, and
Brown competing. I
