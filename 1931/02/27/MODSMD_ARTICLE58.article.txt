# Y.W. TEA HONORING HISTORY FACULTY HELD TODAY AT 4
## 
### 
Students interested in history or
in meeting the faculty and gradu
ate students of the History Depart
ment are invited to a tea this after
noon which is being given in honor
of the History Department by the
Y.W.C.A. This is the second faculty
tea to be given by the Y.W.C.A. this
quarter. The first was in honor of
the Political Science Department.

Among those who have already
accepted invitations to the tea are
Carl Brand, C. G. Osborn, E. A.
White, P. A. Martin, and P. J. Treat.
Both men, as well as women, stu
dents are included in the invitation
to the tea, which will be held from
4 to 6 o'clock in the Y.W.C.A. rooms
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
