# S.F. NINE GOES SOUTH
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 26. (TP)—
The University of San Francisco
baseball squad, 20 strong, left here
tonight for Los Angeles to meet the
Southern California Trojans and
U.C.L.A. Bruins in four California
intercollegiate league games.

A new high altar is being installed
in the Pisa Cathedral.
