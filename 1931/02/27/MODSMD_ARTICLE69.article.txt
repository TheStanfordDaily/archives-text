# HERRE TO SPEAK ON ART EXHIBIT FRIDAY
## 
### 
Dr. Albert Herre will talk in
formally on the articles, now on
exhibition, which he has collected
from the Philippines, Borneo, and
New Guinea on Friday afternoon in
the Art Gallery.
This collection will be removed
soon, and it is the last opportunity
for the general public to see the
collection or to hear Herre speak.
