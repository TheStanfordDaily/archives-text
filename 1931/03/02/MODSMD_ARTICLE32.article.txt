# BEARS START GRID PRACTICE IN WEEK UNDER "NAVY BILL"
## 
### 
One week from today spring
football practice will begin at Cali
fornia. when 15 returning lettermen
and a large number of reserves will
go through their paces for the first
time under "Navy Bill" Ingram.
Stub Allison and Frank Wick
horst, whom Ingram recently named
as assistants, will not be on hand
for the opening practice, but will
report before the end of the week.
Ingram has announced that the
greater part of spring practice will
be devoted to blocking, tackling,
and other fundamentals.

Five brothers married five sister
in Lewis County, Mo.
