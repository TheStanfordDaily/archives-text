# SNEAK THIEF RAIDS DELTA TAU DELTA
## 
### 
Entering the Delta Tau Delta
house sometime last night, a sneak
thief stole a suit and an overcoat.
According to members, the thief was
evidently hurried, as he entered only
one room and took nothing of fur
ther value. It is also reported that
the same man entered the Delt
house at California sometime Sat
urday night and made away with
one overcoat.
