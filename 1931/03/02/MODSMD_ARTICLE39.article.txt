# OPERA STARS COME TO COAST
## 
### 

Tito Schipa, tenor, of the Chicago Civic Opera Company, one of the
many singers who will appear at the Civic Auditorium in San Francisco
for the week starting today. Performances will be given nightly, with a
matinee on Saturday. The season opens tonight with Verdi's "La Traviata"
featuring Claudia Muzio, John Charles Thomas, and Schipa.
