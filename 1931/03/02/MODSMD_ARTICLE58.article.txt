# FAIRCLOUGH PRAISED BY ARCHAEOLOGISTS
## 
### 
Dr. Henry Rushton Fairclough,
professor emeritus of classical liter
ature at Stanford University, has
won much favorable comment from
scholars in Rome on a lecture he
delivered before the British and
American Archaeological Society
there this month, according to word
j received yesterday. Dr. and Mrs.
Fairclough are spending the winter
i in Rome.
