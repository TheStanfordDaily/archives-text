# CAMP COUNSELORS ANNUAL CONVENTION SET FOR MARCH 26
## 
### 
Emphasizing creative leadership !
in the training of camp counselors j
for skilled groups, such as swim- j
ming, handicrafts, and nature, the j
annual conference of the Pacific
Section of Camp Directors' Associ
ation will be held at Asilomar from
March 26 to 29, according to a let
ter received at the Roble Gymna
sium.
The conference will be under the
direction of Bernard S. Mason, pro- j
fessor of sociology at Ohio State
University and an authority on the
subject of camping in connection
with education.
All Stanford women interested in
receiving training for camping lead
ership are urged to attend the con
ference, which in past years has
proved very successful.
