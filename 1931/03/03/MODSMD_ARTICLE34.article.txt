# LOTT RETAINS TITLE
## 
### 
! MIAMI BEACH, Fla., March 2.
I (/P)—George Lott, Chicago, won the
singles title in the Pan-American
tennis championships today for the
second successive year, defeating
• John Van Ryn, East Orange, N.J.,
j 6-2, 1-6, 6-2, 6-4 in the finals.
