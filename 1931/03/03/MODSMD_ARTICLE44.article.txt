# WINGS OVER EUROPE WILL BE PRESENTED AT BOHEMIAN CLUB
## 
### 
"Wings Over Europe," one of the
most outstanding successes ever
given on the Stanford stage, will be
repeated at a special request per
formance Thursday night at the
Bohemian Club in San Francisco.
Harold Helvenston, campus dra
matics director, was asked to re
produce this stirring play, written
by Maurice Browne and Robert
Nichols, as a favor to members of
the club who were unable to at
tend either performance here.
Special music has been arranged
for the prelude of the presentation
in San Francisco, said Helvenston.
He has requested all members of
the cast to meet for rehearsal in the
Assembly Hall tomorrow and Wed
nesday from 2 to 4 o'clock.
