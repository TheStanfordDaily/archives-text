# TRYOUTEES FOR QUAD PHOTO STAFF TO MEET
## 
### 
Freshmen and sophomores who
are interested in photography are
I asked to come to the meeting of
tryoutees for the Quad photography
staff to be held this evening in the
Quad office at 7 o'clock upstairs in
the Press building.
The staff manager announces that
there was but a feeble response to
his first call for tryoutees so that
' the field is wide open to local ama
teur photographers. Women espe
cially are asked to come to the
meeting, though anyone in the
; Lower Division will be welcomed.
