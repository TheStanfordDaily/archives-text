# TEA WILL BE GIVEN FOR ENGLISH STAFF
## 
### 
Honoring the English and the pub
lic speaking departments, the Y.W.-
C.A. will hold its third faculty tea
Friday from 4 to 6 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Majors, graduates, and lower di
vision students intex'ested in the de
partments are invited to attend to
meet and talk with the professors.
Mary Edith Jones* and Isabel Hop
kins axe in charge of the tea, as
sisted by the social and personnel
committees.
