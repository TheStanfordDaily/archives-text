# LOW SCHOLARSHIP EXCUSES DUE SOON
## 
### 
Students whose work has been
interfered with during the currcnt
quarter by illness, or other causes
for which allowance can be made
by the Committee on Scholarship,
may report the special circum
stances to Mrs. Huston, secretary
of the committee, at the Registrar's
Office. Excuses should be submitted
not later than March 13 in order to
be assured of consideration before
action is taken on the end-quarter
reports.
Requests for allowance on ac
count of illness must be supple
mented by the special physician's
certificate furnished by the commit
tee and approved by the medical
adviser.
