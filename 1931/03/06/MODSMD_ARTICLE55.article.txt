# SPEECH ON ORIENTAL MEDICINE SCHEDULED
## 
### 
Dr. Emilc Holman, professor of
surgery in the School of Medicine,
will speak on "Past and Present
Concepts in Oriental Medicine" to
night at 8 o'clock in Lane Hall,
Sacramento street near Webster,
San Francisco. The lecture is the
fifth of a series of six popular talks
presented by the school. '"Indiges
tion, Its Meaning and Management,"
by Dr. Arthur L. Bloomfield, will be
given March 20.
