# WOMEN JOURNALISTS TO HONOR NOVELIST
## 
### 
Ruth Comfort Mitchell, noted
woman author, will be the guest of
honor at the annual Theta Sigma
Phi banquet which will be held to
night in the mural room of the
Woman's City Club at 6:30 p.m.
The dinner will be attended by the
combined active chapters of Stan
ford and California and the mem
bers of the bay region.

The meeting which is the most
important of the Matrix round
tables which the women journalists
hold monthly will be followed by
an informal discussion in which the
novelist will participate. Tickets
may be procured from Allene Lam
son for $1.25.
