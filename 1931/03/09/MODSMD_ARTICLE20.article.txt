# EDUCATION LEADERS RETURN FROM EAST
## 
### 
Professor W. M. Proctor and Wal
ter C. Eells of the education school
have returned from the department
of superintendence convention of
the National Education Association
held February 21-26 at Detroit.
Dr. Eells spoke on junior college
finance before the Secondary Prin
cipals Association. "Working To
gether for the Children of America"
was the theme of the meetings,
which were addressed by Rear-Ad
miral Richard E. Byrd.
