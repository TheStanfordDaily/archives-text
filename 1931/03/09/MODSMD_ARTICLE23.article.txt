# CARD BALL TEAM TOPS CONFERENCE
## 
###

St. Mary's nine, 13-1, to ring up its
sixth straight conference win, Cali
fornia maintained an unbeaten rec
ord by defeating San Francisco Uni
versity. Down South Santa Clara
and U.C.L.A. broke even, the for
mer to get its first win in seven
starts, and the latter to keep a .500
percentage.
