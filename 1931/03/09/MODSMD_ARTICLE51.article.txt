# FROSH TENNIS STARS WIN PIEDMONT TILT
## 
### 
Without the loss of a single match,
the freshman racket wielders made
a clean sweep of their tilt with Pied
mont High Saturday. Taking all of
the nine matches except one in
straight sets, the local netsters gave
no quarter to the high school outfit.
Complete results: Otis (S) d. But
ler (P), 6-3, 6-4; Shwayder (S) d.
Evans (P), 6-3, 6-0; Ellis S) d. Hay
nor (P), 6-1, 6-0; Bogan (S) d. Lerch
(P), 6-2, 6-2; Thorpe (S) d. Red
dall (P), 6-1, 6-1; Torrence S) d.
Lerch (P), 6-1, 6-1; Otis-Eilis (S)
d. Butler-Haynor (P), 6-2, 6-0; Bo
gan-Shwayder (S) d. Lerch-Evans
(P), 6-3, 2-6, 6-4; Sadler-Thorpe (S)
d. Lerch-Reddall (P), 6-1, 6-4.
