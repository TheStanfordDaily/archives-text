# WOMEN'S RUSHING TO BEGIN MARCH 31
## 
### 
Women's spring quarter rushing
will begin with dinner on the night
of Tuesday, March 31, the day after
registration, and will continue until
dinner on the night of Friday, April
3, according to Jean Utt, president
of the local Pan-Hellenic group.
Pledging is to take place Satur
day afternoon, April 4. Rushing in
the spring is always of an informal
nature, and Pan-Hellenic favors ad
herence to this custom.
