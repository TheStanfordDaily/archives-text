# EAKER WILL ATTEMPT NEW FLIGHT RECORD
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, March 9.
vided weather conditions are deemed
favorable, Captain Ira C. Eaker,
United States Army, probably will
take off from Rockwell Field about
midnight tonight on a contemplated
flight to New York City that may
result in lowering the transcontin
ental record now held by Captain
Frank Hawkes, according to infor
mation from Rockwell Field this af
ternoon.
Captain Eaker will pilot a new
type Lockheed army plane with
which he has been conducting a
series of tests at Rockwell Field for
the past 10 days. This plane, it is
declared, has a speed in excess of
200 miles an hour.
