# COURT DISBAR U.S. DISTRICT ATTORNEY
## 
### 
DENVER, March 9. (/P) The
Supreme Court today disbarred
Granby Hillyer, former United
States District Attorney, on charges
of unprofessional conduct preferred
by the Denver Bar Association.
Hillyer was found guilty of mis
appropriating funds intrusted to his
care by former clients, and of neg
lect of the interests of a client. For
a number of years Hillyer practiced
law here but he is now located at
Lamar, Colorado.
