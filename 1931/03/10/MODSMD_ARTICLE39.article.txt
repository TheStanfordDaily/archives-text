TUESDAY
4:15 p.m.—Organ Recital by Warren
D. Allen, University Organist, in
the Memorial Church.
6 p.m.—The annual W.A.A. hockey
banquet will be held at the Presi
dent Hotel.
7:30 p.m. Lecture on "Political
Problems in Post-War Europe,"
by Professor Graham H. Stuart.
In the Little Theatre.
WEDNESDAY
8 p.m. —Research Club' in the Fac
ulty Clubhouse. Speakers: Pro
fessor Yamato Ichihashi on "Some
Aspects of the Second Generation
Japanese Problem"; Professor
Harvey M. Ball on the "Influence
of Environment and Heredity as
Tested by Botanical Transplants."
THURSDAY
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of the Christian
Science Society on the top floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
FRIDAY
8 p.m.—Faculty Women's Club will
entertain the men of the faculty
in the Women's Clubhouse.
