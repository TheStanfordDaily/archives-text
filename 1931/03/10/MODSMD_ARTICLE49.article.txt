# WOMEN TO GIVE UP GYM LOCKERS TODAY
## 
### 
All lockers in the women's gym
must be cleared by 6 o'clock this
evening. The lockers are soon to
be moved to the new gym and this
necessitates their being emptied...
There will be no more women's
gymnasium classes this quarter after
today, according to Dr. Bertha Stuart
Dyment, head of the physical edu
cation department for women.
