# FRANCES JOHNSON ELECTED HOSTESS FOR THURSDAY TEA
## 
### 
The Fellowship Committee has
chosen Frances Johnson as a new
hostess for Thursday's Fellowship
■ tea.
Fellowship teas will be given as
i usual this week on Monday, Tues
day, Wednesday, and Thursday,
from 4:30 to 5:30 o'clock, in the
Fellowship Board room on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse. All
students and faculty members are
invited. Thursday will conclude the
teas for this quarter, but they will
be resumed next quarter.
Hostesses for this week, Monday:
Ruth Beede, Elizabeth McCullough;
Tuesday: Lucille Morgan, Elizabeth
West; Wednesday: Betty Hewit, Ann
Adams, Sue Noble; Thursday: Fran
ces Johnson, Dolores Van Patten.
