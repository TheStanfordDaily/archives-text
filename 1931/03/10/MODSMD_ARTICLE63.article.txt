# ROSH RACQUETMEN GAIN NEW PLACES ON RANKING LADDER
## 
### 
Alden Sadler yesterday defeated
George Torrence 2-6, 6-4, 7-5, in the
only high-ranking tennis ladder
match to be settled among the fresh
men in the last few days. By vir
tue of this victory, Sadler regains
his sixth position on the list.
Fred Hills jumped two rungs in
the course of last week's play, first
removing Norman Wright, 3-6, 8-6,
6-3, then winning over Jim Shea
han by a score of 6-4, 6-3. Wright
dropped another rung at the hands
of Leighton Johnson yesterday, 6-2,
6-3.
In the lower positions, Al Wood
ward moved up at the expense of
John Lohman, 6-3, 6-0, and Paul
Fries took down Bob Quinn by a
6-1, 6-1 score to climb a step. Fries
later failed to gain another place,
however, losing to Kendall Elling
wood 4-6, 6-4, 6-4.
Holding their second-place laurels,
Monte Shwayder and Al Bogan
downed Jim Sheahan and Norman
Wright, 6-0, 10-8, on Saturday.
