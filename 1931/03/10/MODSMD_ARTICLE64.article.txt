# KALMAN WILL TALK ON 'DAM ANALYSIS'
## 
### 
Two special lectures on "Dam
Analysis" will be given by Eugene
Kalman in room 273 this morning
at 9 and 11 o'clock, under the aus
pices of Professor Fish of the civil
engineering department.
The lecturer received his training
in the universities of Budapest,
Berlin, and Gottingen, and taught
several years in the University of
Milan in Italy. At present he is en
gaged in special research work at
the California Institute of Technol
ogy.
