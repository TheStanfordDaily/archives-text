# BARTON WILL SPEAK ON SOVIET SCHOOLS
## 
### 
Returned to the United States
after an extensive tour of Russia
during which he inspected that
country's schools, Dr. William Bar
ton, professor of psychology in the
University of Chicago, will speak
at an open meeting of the Education
Club Wednesday at 7:30 o'clock, in
the Women's Clubhouse. His sub
ject will be, "What the Soviets Are
Doing for the Schools."
Dr. Barton is an authority on
school supervision, having written
a book entitled "Supervision of In
struction." He is spending two weeks
here writing, and will teach at the
University of California this sum
mer.
