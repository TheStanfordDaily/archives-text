# REGISTRATION DANCE IS FOR GYM BENEFIT
## 
### 
Joe Thompson's campus orchestra
will furnish Ihe music for the spring
quarter registration dance which will
be held tonight from 9 to 12 o'clock
at Branner Hall. Bids for the dance
are now on sale for $1.25, and all
proceeds will go toward the women's
gymnasium fund.
Patrons and patronesses for the
dance will be as follows: President
and Mrs. Robert E. Swain, Mr. and
Mrs. Edwin Thomas, Dean John
Sellards, and Mrs. "Frank Findley.
