# PRIZE SPRINTERS TO RACE AT TANFORAN
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, March 29. (/P)
- Sprintehs will usher in the spring
racing season at Tanforance track
(morrow afternoon. The Inaugural
Handicap, over the sohrt A. B.
Spreekels course, attracted fifteen
to the lists of battle, including the
cream of the speed horses now on
I the Pacific Coast.
