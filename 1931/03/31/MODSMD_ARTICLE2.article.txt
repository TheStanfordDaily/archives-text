# A.I.E.E. WILL MEET TOMORROW NIGHT TO DISCUSS PLANS
## 
### 
The Stanford University Branch
of the American Institute of Elec
trical Engineers will meet at 7:30
o'clock Thursday evening in room
280 of the Engineering Building to
discuss plans for holding a joint
meeting with the Santa Clara and
California University branches of
the San Francisco Section of the
Institute.
B. D. Dexter, Pacific Gas and
Electric Corporation engineer, will
be present at the meeting and will
speak on the corporation's new sub
station at Newark which the club
will visit Saturday morning.
All members and guests of the
society who intend to go on the in
spection trip are asked to sign their
names on the list which has been
placed on the bulletin board of the
Engineering Building. Thg trip will
begin promptly at 9:30 o'clock from
the engineering corner.
