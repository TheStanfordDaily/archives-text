# WOMEN'S RUSHING WILL START TODAY
## 
### 
Spring quarter rushing for women
will begin with dinner tonight and
will continue until dinner on the'
night of Friday, April 3, according
to Jean Utt, president of the local
Pan-Hellenic group.
Pledging will take place Saturday
afternoon, April 4. Pan-Hellenic has
always favored informal rushing in
the spring quarter and this custom
will be followed this year as usual.
