# SIGMA XI TO HEAR PAULING TOMORROW
## 
### 
Professor Linus Pauling, speaking
on the subject, "The Nature of the
Chemical Bond," at 8 o'clock tomor
row night in room 20, Chemistry
Building, will be the next lecturer
in the series now being sponsored
by the Stanford chapter of Sigma
Xi.
Professor Pauling is from the
California Institute of Technology
and will be guest of honor at an
informal dinner at 6:15 o'clock to
morrow night in the Stanford Union.
