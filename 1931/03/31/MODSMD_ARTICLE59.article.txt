# IMPORTANT MEETING OF SIGMA DELTA CHI
## 
### 
For the purpose of presenting
shingles and pins to new members,
and also for the consideration of
very important business, Sigma
Delta Chi, professional journalistic
fraternity, will meet at 1 o'clock
Wednesday afternoon in Room 9 of
the Press Building. The business to
be considered is very important, ac
cording to John Isaacs, president.
