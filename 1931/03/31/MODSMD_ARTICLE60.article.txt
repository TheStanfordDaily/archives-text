# SWAIN ANNOUNCES CLINICAL STAFF OF MEDICAL SCHOOL
## Eleven Doctors Named To Full Professorships For 1931-32 
###

the Stanford medical school for the
year 1931-32\ were announced by
Acting President Robert E. Swain
yesterday. The names follow:
Department of Medicine
Clinical professors: Doctors H. E.
Alderson, W. E. Cheney, W. R. P.

tors W., W. Boardman, Horace Gray',

A. A. O'Neill, and J. M. Rehfisch.
Clinical instructors: Doctors D. A.
Carson, Garnett Cheney, J. C.
Cuneo, H. H. Detrick, L. H. Gar
land, W. E. Kay, T. H. Kelly, A. C.
McKenney, R. B. McKenzie, L. M.
Morris, E. C. Nicholls, W. S. Pol
land, P. S. Pouppirt, Ann Purdy,
Walter Schilling, D. E. Shepardson,
Julius Sherman, E. F. Stadtherr, and
P. A. Taylor.
Clinic dentist: Dr. J. L. Campbell.
Obstetrics and Gynecology
Associate clinical professors: Doc
tors L. A. Emge, K. L. Schaupp,

tors H. vanGeldern, A. V. Pettit, and

Parrott.

Brown, lecturer on child hygiene,
