# LARGE REGISTRATION
## 
### 
When the office of the Registrar
closed last night the number of stu
dents registered for spring quarter
totalled 3145 of whom 3077 were
complete registrations and 68 were
incomplete. The figures show a
slight increase over last year when
the total number was 3068 of which
3019 were complete and only 49 in
complete.
