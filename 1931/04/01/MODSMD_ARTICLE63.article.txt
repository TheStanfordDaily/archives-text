# STANFORD GRADUATE RECEIVES LAW HONOR
## 
### 
Richard E. Guggenhime, '29, has
recently been elected president of
the Harvard Legal Aid Bureau, ac
cording to Professor Thomas S.
Barclay of the political science de
partment, receiving one of the
highest honors given by the Harvard
law school. Guggenhime was a poli
tical science major at Stanford and
graduated with great distinction. He
is now in his second year at the
Harvard School of, Law.
The Harvard Legal Aid Bureau is
an organization giving law students
training in legal practice and gives
legal aid gratuitously.
