# TICKETS ON SALE NOW FOR PUPPET PLAY WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
TICKETS for the production
of Eugene O'Neill's negro
play, "Emperor Jones," which
will be presented by Ralph
Chesse's puppet performers,
will go on sale today at 1
o'clock at stands in both the
Old Union, and at David Kce
ble's store in Palo Alto.' The
performance is scheduled for
next Wednesday evening, April
S. Tickets are priced at seventy
five cents.
This production is being
by the women's gym
committee, under the chair
manship of Elisabeth Larsh,
Proceeds from the play will be
added to the total of the Wo
men's Gym Fund.
