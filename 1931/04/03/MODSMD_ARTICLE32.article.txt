# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
FRIDAY
7 p.m.—Chorus tryouts for the Jun
ior Opera will be held in the As
sembly Hall.
SATURDAY
9:30 a.m.—Members and guests of
the A.I.E.E. will leave from the
engineering corner on an inspec
tion trip to the Newark sub-sta
tion of the P. G. and E.
SUNDAY
5:15 p.m.—Vespers will be held in
the Women's Clubhouse.
MONDAY
8:30 p.m.—Frosh track men meet in
room 45 of the B.A.C. building to
elect a new captain. Ellington asks
that all men be present.
MISCELLANEOUS
ALL FRESHMEN wishing to be
sophomore football managers meet
on the varsity practice field at 4
o'clock this afternoon and Friday.
This wiil fill physical education
requirements.
PROGRAM SALESMEN for Big
Meet report to Ray Clark at
B.A.C. as soon as possible.
Students at the University of Illi
nois will pay increased fees next
year.
