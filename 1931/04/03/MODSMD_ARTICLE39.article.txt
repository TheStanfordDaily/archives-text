# CARDINAL CHESSMEN WILL MEET FACULTY
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Stanford
Chess Club last night plans were
made for the rest of the quarter.
Matches arc to be held with the
faculty, the University of San Fran
cisco, and the Stanislaus County
Chess Club. Individual tourna

quarter.
All students interested in chess
are urged to attend the meetings of
this club.
