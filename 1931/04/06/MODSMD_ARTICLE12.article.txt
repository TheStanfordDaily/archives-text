# GORHAM IS ELECTED POLO SQUAD CAPTAIN
## 
### 
"Chuck" Gorham, two-year let
terman, has been elected captain of
the polo squad, succeeding John Mc-
Dougall, who graduated last quar
ter. Gorham has played polo here
for three years.
The new captain has had a great
deal of riding experience, appearing
in the larger horse shows all over
the country, and is an expert horse
man, according to Captain Gordon,
polo coach.
