# MEN'S RUSHING SEASON BEGINS ON ROW TODAY
## Houses May Have Only One Date With Freshmen In Initial Period 
### 
With the chiming of the clock
tower bells at 11:30 o'clock this
morning begins that most frenzied
.of spring quarter sports, men's rush
ing.
Starting with luncheon today, the
Greek letter fraternities, divided
into two groups, will rush mem
bers of the class of '34 until April
1(5. During this time, designated the
first period by the Interfraternity
Council, houses may have only one
date, either for luncheon or dinner,
with each prospective pledge.
First Group Houses
Fraternities in the first group,
which will rush today, April 8, 10,
13, and 15, arc Alpha Kappa Lamb
da, Alpha Tau Omega, Beta Theta
Pi, Delta Chi, Delta Upsilon, Kappa
Alpha, Phi Delta Theta, Phi Gamma
Delta, Phi Kappa Psi, Sigma Alpha
Epsilon, Sigma Chi, and Zeta Psi.
Second group houses, rushing to
morrow, April 9, 12, 14, and 16,
are Alpha Delta Phi, Alpha Sigma
Phi, Chi Psi, Delta Kappa Epsilon,
Delta Tau Delta, Kappa Sigma, Phi
Kappa Sigma, Phi Sigma Kappa,
Sigma Nu, Theta Chi, Theta Delta
Chi, and Theta Xi.
Interfraternity Rules
During this first rushing period
luncheon dates will begin at 11:30
o'clock and end at 2 o'clock. Din
ner dates will last from 5:30 until 8
o'clock. On Sunday, April 12, the
times will be from 12:30 to 3 o'-
clock for luncheon, and from 6 to
8:30 for dinner.

Friday and Saturday, April 17 and
18, will be quiet days, in which
houses in neither group will be al
lowed to rush. The second period is
to begin Sunday, April 19, and will
last until 10 o'clock Thursday eve
ning, April 23. During this time a
house may have as many dates with
a rushee as he desires. Formal
pledging will take place before the
Interfraternity lawyer on April 24.
