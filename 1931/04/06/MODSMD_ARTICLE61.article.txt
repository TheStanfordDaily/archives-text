# GRUNSKY TO LECTURE ON 'TRADE JOURNALS'
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, national hono
rary journalistic society for women,
will hold their monthly meeting in
the mural room of the Women's
City Club at 6:30 o'clock tonight.
The main speaker for the evening
will be Clothilde Grunsky of the
McGraw-Hill publishing bureaus
who will speak on the subject of
"Trade Journals."
