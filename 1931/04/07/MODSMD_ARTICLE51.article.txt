# POWERS ENGAGED
## 
### 
The engagement of Marylyn Pow
ers, '32, to John G. Sobieski, '28,
was announced yesterday. Sobieski,
a law graduate, is practicing his pro
fession in Los Angeles. While in
Stanford he was affiliated with the
Sigma Alpha Epsilon fraternity, and
was a pitcher on the varsity baseball
team. The announcement was made
by Marylyn Powers at the Kappa
Alpha Theta house.
