# WOMEN JOURNALISTS MEET AT CITY CLUB
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, national honor
ary journalistic society for women,
held their monthly meeting last
night in the mural room of the
Women's City Club at 6:30 o'clock.
The main speaker of the evening
was Clothilde Grunsky of the Mc-
Graw-Hill publishing bureaus, who
discussed the place of trade journals
and their relation to the woman
journalist.
