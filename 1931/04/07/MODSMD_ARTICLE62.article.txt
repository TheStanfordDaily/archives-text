# ECONOMICS FACULTY WILL BE GUESTS OF 'Y' AT TEA THURSDAY
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. will entertain
Thursday in honor of the economics
department with a faculty tea from
4 to 6 o'clock in the Y.W.C.A. rooms
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse, Ann Adams acting as
hostess.
Students who are interested in
getting acquainted with professors,
graduate students, and correctors of
Ihe department are invited to come.
The wives of the faculty members
ai'e also invited.
This tea is the third of a series of
faculty teas being given under the
auspices of the Y, those for the
political science and history depart
ments having already taken place.
The economics tea is scheduled fox-
Thursday due to the fact that the
Y cabinet will be unable to be pres
ent on Friday, the usual day for
faculty teas.
