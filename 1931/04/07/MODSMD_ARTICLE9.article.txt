# OFFICERS OF S.C.A. WILL HAVE ELECTION
## 
### 
Election of officers for the Stan
ford Christian Association will be
held today, according to announce
ment last night by John W. Way,
Jr., president of the organization.
Nominees for the offices to be
filled arc as follows: For president,
Tom Vasey; for vice-president, Den
ny Woodworth; for secretary, Gil
bert Smith and Ed Fisher.
Members of the Association may
vote at any time today at the As
sociation offices in the Old Union.
Results will be tabulated tonight,
Way announced.
New cabinet members will be in
stalled soon, and will attend the an
nual spring conference of Northern
California Christian Association of
ficers at Yosemite, April 18 to 19.
