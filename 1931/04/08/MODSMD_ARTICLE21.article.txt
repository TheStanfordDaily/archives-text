# OPERA TICKETS MAY BE RESERVED TODAY
## 
### 
Students' ticket reservations for
the Junior Opera, "Proletaria Plus,"
to be presented under the auspices
of the Dramatic Council on April 17,
may be made today from 8 to 11:45
o'clock and from 1 to 3 at the dra
matic manager's office in the Old
Union. Tickets are priced from $1
to 51.50.
Advance sale of tickets will begin
April 14, according to A1 Denny,
dramatic manager.
