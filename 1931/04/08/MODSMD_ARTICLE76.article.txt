# 'MISSING' U.C. CO-ED BACK AT HOME HERE
## 
### 
Miss Esther B. Lovrien, former
University of California co-ed, re-

ported missing since March 27 from
her home at Mayfield, near here,
returned yesterday with the an
nouncement she had been visiting
friends in Berkeley.

The girl left the Berkeley institu
tion last February because of a
nervous breakdown, and has been
undergoing medical treatment at
Mayfield.
