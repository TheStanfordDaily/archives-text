# CONTEST OPENS FOR PREVENTION SLOGANS
## 
### 
Prizes for the best slogans en
couraging fire prevention are offered
in a contest open to students in
California universities, colleges, and
high schools, it was announced yes
terday.

Slogans must not exceed 15 words
and must be mailed before mid
night, April 30, to the Stop Forest
Fires Committee of California, 150
Van Ness Avenue, San Francisco.
Further details may be obtained by
writing the secretary of the com
mittee.
