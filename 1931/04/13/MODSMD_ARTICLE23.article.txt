# McGINNIS, POYNTER HURT WHEN THROWN BY MILITARY HORSES
## 
### 
James E. McGinnis, '34, was seri
ously injured yesterday when he was
thrown from the horse he was rid
ing near Searsville Lake. He was
found unconscious by F. H. Dennis
of San Mateo and rushed to the
Palo Alto Hospital where he is
under observation until the exact
extent of his injuries can be diag
nosed.
William F. Poynter, '32, is suffer
ing from shock caused Saturday af
ternoon when his horse threw him
on the Page Mill road. His condition
is not serious.
