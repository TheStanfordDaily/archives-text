# Y.M.C.A. TO SPONSOR LEADERSHIP COURSE
## 
### 
Professor John Almack, prominent
educator and member of the local
Y.M.C.A. Committee, will give the
initial talk of a series to be given
as a leaders' training course for
t those interested in learning about
the various programs of boys' work
under the Y.M.C.A.
The course, which will run for
four consecutive Monday evenings,
will be held in the Association Room
in the Stanford Union, beginning the
evening of April 13, at 7:30. Further
information can be secured by call
ing Roy A. Hohberger, the Y.M.C.A.
secretary, at the Community House.
