# CARD, TROJAN POLO QUARTETS TO MEET IN CONTESTS HERE
## 
### 
Because the water shortage on the
campus makes it impossible to keep
the highway polo field in proper
condition, the Stanford polo four
will meet the U.S.C. quartet on En
cina field this week-end.
The first contest of a two-game
series will be played tomorrow af
ternoon at 3:30 o'clock, with the
second scheduled for Saturday af
ternoon.
Captain P. A. Gordan v/ill start
the second team tomorrow, but has
not announced his plans for the final
fray.
