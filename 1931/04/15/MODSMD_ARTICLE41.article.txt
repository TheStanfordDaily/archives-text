# STARS IN NATIONAL SWIM MEET
## 
### 

These champions will defend their titles in the national indoor
swimming championships in New York City in which the nation's lead
ing women swimmers will compete. Helene Madison wears the 100, 220
and 500-yard free style crown, Eleanor Holm is 100-yards backstroke
and 300-yards medley champion, Georgia Colenien is the 10-foot spring
board champion and Jane Fauntz holds the low board title.
