# CREATE NEW SCHOOLS
##

SACRAMENTO, April 14. (/P) —
The Assembly adopted today bills
creating four-year colleges-in Sacra
mento and Fresno. The vote on the
Fresno bill was 41 to 34, and on
Sacramento 41 to 33.
