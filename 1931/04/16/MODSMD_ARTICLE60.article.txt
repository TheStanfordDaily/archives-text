# SWAIN GIVES RADIO TALK ON CHEMISTRY
## 
### 
Speaking on "Chemistry in Our
Everyday Life,"' Acting-President
Robert E. Swain was on the air yes
terday over the National Broadcast
ing system as a part of its national
education program. These programs
are held from 1:30 to 2 o'clock every
Wednesday afternoon.
Dr. Swain believes that this move
ment for education by means of the
radio has a great future. He says,
"The movement is spreading and
more people are taking an interest,
more men are being drawn into it.'
