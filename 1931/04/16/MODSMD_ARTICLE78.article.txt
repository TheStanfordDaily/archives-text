# STANFORD STUDENT WILL SPEND TERM AT GERMAN SCHOOL
## 
### 
Ernest Gieseckc, '31, has been
chosen as an exchange student by
the International Institute of Educa
tion to spend a year of study at a
German university which will be
named at a later date.
Giesecke, who is a member of
Sigma Nu, will pursue his studies
abroad in the field of Germanic
Literature and Culture. The Inter
national Institute of Education will
likewise choose a scholar from one
of the German universities to spend
a year on this campus.
