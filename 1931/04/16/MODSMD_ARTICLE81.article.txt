# BRANDSTEN SELECTED 1932 OLYMPIC COACH
## 
### 
Ernst Brandsten, Advisory Direc
tor of Water Sports at Stanford, was
named last Tuesday as coach of the
American Diving Team to compete
at Los Angeles in the 1932 Olympic
Games.
Brandsten, who has an interna
tional reputation as a trainer of
champion divers, has coached the
American team since 1920, and has
brought three successive titles back
to the Stanford campus, A1 White in
1920, Clarence Pinkston in 1924, and
"Pete" Desjardins in 1928.
