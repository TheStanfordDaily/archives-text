# BABE FOILSMEN TO OPPOSE BEAR CUBS IN ANNUAL CLASHES
## 
### 
With high hopes of emulating the
varsity fencers who recently de
feated California, the Indian fresh
man foilsmen travel to Berkeley to
night for their annual meet with the
California yearlings in Harmon
gym.
A five-man squad and Head Coach
Harry Maloney, with his assistant,
Elwyn Bugge, will go to Berkeley
for the clash. Richard Criley is the
most experienced man on the babe
team. He came to Stanford from
Paris where he received much fenc
ing instruction. Others on the team
include A1 Snyder, Ed Munger, Gor
don McKenzie, and Willard Stone.
