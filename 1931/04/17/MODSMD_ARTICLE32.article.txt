# TWO MINOR SPORTS BEGIN COMPETITION IN SPRING PRACTICE
## 
### 
With the annual doubles handball
tourney starting tomorrow, and the
University intramural horseshoe
pitching competition slated to begin
next Monday, two spring sport fea
tures get under way.
In both of these events a large
number of teams have entered and
close competition is expected. Coach
Myron Sprague, who is in charge of
the handball matches has posted a
complete list of the games to be
played. Each team is urged to ap
pear exactly when the game is
scheduled.
