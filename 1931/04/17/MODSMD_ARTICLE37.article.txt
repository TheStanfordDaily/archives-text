# NEW YORK MINISTER WILL PREACH SUNDAY
## 
### 
Rev. J. V. Moldenhawer, of the
First Presbyterian Church of New
York City, will deliver the sermon
at 11 o'clock, Sunday, April 19, in
the Memorial Church.
The program of music follows:
Organ Prelude: Allegretto Bach
From the Seventh Symphony
Bass Solo and Chorus:
"Peace Be Unto You"... .Bach
Henry Shefoff, bass
"Praise Ye the Lord of Hosts"
St. Saens
University Choir
Holy Communion will be held at
8 o'clock.
