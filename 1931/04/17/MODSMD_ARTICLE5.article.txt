# A.I.E.E. WILL MEET HERE; 70 TO VISIT RYAN VOLTAGE LAB
## 
### 
A joint meeting of the California,
Santa Clara, and Stanford Univer
sity student branches and the San
Francisco section of the American
Institute of Electrical Engineers will
be held on the campus this afternoon
and evening. Approximately 70 elec
trical engineering students are ex
pected to be present.
Meeting in the Union court at 2
o'clock, the members of the society
will proceed on an inspection trip
through the shops and laboratories
of the School of Engineering, and
then through the Ryan Laboratory,
where they will witness a high-vol
tage demonstration at 5 o'clock.
At the dinner which will be held
in the Union at 6:15 o'clock, papers
by students from the three univer
sity branches represented will be
read.
