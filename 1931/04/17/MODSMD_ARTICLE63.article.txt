# LOCKWOOD, KINSEY WILL COACH TENNIS TEAM NEXT SEASON
## 
### 
Sherman Lockwood and Howard
Kinsey, nationally known tennis
players, have been appointed acting
instructor and advisor, respectively,
of the Stanford tennis team, ft was
announced yesterday.
Lockwood was runner-up for the
national intercollegiate doubles ti
tle last year, and has won numerous
state singles and doubles champion
ships.
Kinsey was finalist in the singles,
doubles and mixed doubles at Wim
bledon in 1928, and besides winning
many other titles, has participated
in Davis Cup play.
Both men have been coaching at
the Berkeley Tennis club for the last
six months.
