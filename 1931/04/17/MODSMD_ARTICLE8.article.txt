# FINAL RUSHING BEGINS SUNDAY
## Freshmen Can Accept Any Dates They Wish 
### 
Following two days of silence, to
day and tomorrow, men's rushing
for the second period will begin Sun
day noon. Freshmen may accept as
many of the dates indicated on fra
ternity invitation cards as they see
fit.
On Sunday and Monday luncheon
dates will begin at 12:30 o'clock and
last until 3. Dinner dates will be
from 6 to 8:30. Luncheons on Tues
day and Wednesday will last from
11:30 to 2, and dinners from 5:30 to
8 o'clock.
Luncheon dates for Thursday, the
last day, will be the same as those
on Tuesday and Wednesday. Thurs
day night is preference night, and
houses may rush in Palo Alto from
5 to 10 o'clock.
