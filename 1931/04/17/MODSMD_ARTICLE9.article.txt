# MORRIS CHOSEN MEDAL DEBATER
## Is First Stanford Woman On Joffre Squad 
### 
Mary Katherine Morris wins the
distinction of being the first woman
ever chosen to represent Stanford
in the nationally known Joffre Med
al debate with California.
This selection competes the trium
virate of speakers for Stanford, the
other two being Irving Rosenblatt
and George Shibley, both debaters
of proven ability in Pacific intercol
legiate forensic contests.
The debating classic will be held
in the Assembly Hall next Tues
day evening. The specific subject on
which the six speakers will talk is
to be chosen two hours before the
opening of the debate. Only the
general topic of discussion is known
which is this year to be "The French
Colonial and Mandate Policy."
