# SECOND PERIOD OF MEN'S RUSHING IN FULL SWING TODAY
## 
### 
Second period rushing started at
the men's houses on the Row yester
day, and will continue until prefer
ence night, Thursday. The period is
one of comparatively open rushing,
freshmen being allowed as many en
gagements with each house as they
desire.
On Thursday night, houses will be
permitted to rush on the campus or
in Palo Alto until 10 o'clock. The
dinner dates will start at 5 o'clock
on that day.
Pledging will take place before the
interfraternity lawyer in the Old
Union Friday afternoon, following
the custom that has been used dur
ing the past several years.
