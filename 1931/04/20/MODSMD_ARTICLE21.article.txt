# GLEE CLUB TO HOLD SPRING TRYOUTS IN GYMNASIUM ROOMS
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Glee Club will be
held tonight and Wednesday at 7:30
o'clock in the club's new temporary
practice rooms in the women's old i
gymnasium. The club is undergoing
intensive practice in preparation for
its Bohemian Club concert in San
Francisco.
Any man is eligible to try out for
the club at either of these times,
according to William Dienstoin, man
ager. Dienstcin is not yet certain
of the date of the Bohemian Club i
concert, but it is probable that it i
will take place early in May, if not
held the last Thursday in April.
