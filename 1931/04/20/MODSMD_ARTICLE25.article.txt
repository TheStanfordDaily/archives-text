# ARGELL WINS PRAISE IN SUNDAY'S RECITAL
## 
### 
Marsden Argell, baritone, was Ihe
soloist 1 assisting Warren D. Allen

University organist, in the Univer
sity Day recital yesterday afternoon
in the Memorial Church.
An aria from "The Seasons'' by
Haydn, and the "Gloria" by Buzzi-
Peccia were rendered with fine feel
ing by Argell.
As a student at Stanford Argell
distinguished himself in vocal work.
He has since become prominent on
the concert stage and is. now asso
ciated with the forthcoming Pacific
opera season.
