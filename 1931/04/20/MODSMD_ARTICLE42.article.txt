# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
TUESDAY
2 p.m.—Stanford Mothers Club in
Clubhouse. Miss Chris
tine Ricker will speak on "Man
agement of Dining Halls and
Preparation of Foods."
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Alien in Memorial Church.
8:15 p.m.—Annual Joffre Medal de
bate, between University of Cali
fornia and Stanford, in Assembly
Hall. Public invited.
WEDNESDAY
12 noon—The International Table
will meet for all those interested
in the Union Court.
8 p.m.—Research Club, at Faculty
Clubhouse. Professor Jefferson El
more will speak on "Horace's Art
of Poetry"; Professor Edward K.
Strong will speak on "Interests
and Vocational Choice."
THURSDAY
4:15 p.m.—Philological Association in
Women's Clubhouse. Paper: "The
Vocabulary of the American Negro
as Recorded in Recent Fiction,"
by Nathan Van Patten.
4:1;3 p.m.—Lecture in Spanish by
Professor Fedelino Figuereido, of
University of Madrid, in Little
Theatre. Public invited.
4:15 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen in Memorial Church.
