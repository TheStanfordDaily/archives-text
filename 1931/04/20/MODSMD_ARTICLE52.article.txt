# BREAKERS-SIGMA NU WILL CLASH TODAY
## 
### 
Breakers' baseball team, league
winner in last year's intramural
tournament, swings into action this
afternoon at 4 o'clock, when it
clashes with Sigma Nu on the new
intramural diamond.
This field is located between the
Grays' gridiron and the polo field.
Phi Kappa Sigma, v/hich has a
reputation for strong intramural
teams, is scheduled to meet Alpha
Tau Omega on the Sequoia diamond
in the second game of the day.
