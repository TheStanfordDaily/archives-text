# FOOTBALL COACH DIES
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Apr. 19 (/P).—Joe Prel
li, Notre Dame football halfback in
1925, 1926, and 1927 and coach of Mt.
Carmel high school here, died last
night of influenza.
