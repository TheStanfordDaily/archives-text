# ANDERSON TO SPEAK AT CERCLE FRANCAIS
## 
### 
Members of the Cercle Francais
will hold their first meeting of the
spring quarter Thursday night at
7:30 o'clock in the Delta Gamma
house.
Professor Frederick Anderson, as
sociate professor of the romanic
languages department, will give a
short talk on "Les Philosophes Fran
cais." In addition to this, community
singing of well-known French folk
songs will be a feature of the eve
ning.
An invitation is extended espe
cially to students now in the second
and third-year French classes.
