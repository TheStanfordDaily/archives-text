# SUMMER QUARTER PROFESSORS
## 
### 


Professor Romanzo Colfax Adams (left), University of Hawaii research
expert in race relations in the Pacific area, will offer two courses in that
field here next quarter. At the shme time, Professor Jean-Albert Bede,
of Princeton University, will give courses in the contemporary French
novel, and in advanced French composition. He will also give a course
in French interpreting texts illustrating doctrines of Romanticism.
