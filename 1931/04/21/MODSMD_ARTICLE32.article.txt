TUESDAY
2:30 p.m. — Stanford Mothers Club in
Women's Clubhouse. Miss Chris
tine Ricker will speak on "Man
agement of Dining Halls and
Preparation of Foods."
12 noon — The Stanford Masonic Club
will meet for luncheon with all
i Stanford Masons in the Union din
ing room. Paul Merner will speak.
4:15 p.m. — Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen in Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m. — Rehearsal of the Stanford
Symphony Orchestra in the old
Roble gymnasium.
8:15 p.m.—Annual Joffre Medal de
bate, between University of Cali
fornia and Stanford, in Assembly
Hall. Public invited.
WEDNESDAY
12 noon — The International Table
will meet for all those interested
in the Union Court.
12 noon — International Table in the
Alcove of the Union dining hall
will be open to all interested.
8 p.m. — Research Club, at Faculty
Clubhouse. Professor Jefferson El
more will speak on "Horace's Art
of Poetry"'; Professor Edward K.
Strong will speak on "Interests
and Vocational Choice."
8:45 p.m. — Radio Club will meet in
room 263 in the Engineering
Building.
