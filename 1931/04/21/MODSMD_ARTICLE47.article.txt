# VARSITY TENNIS MEN HAVE LITTLE ACTION ON RANKING LADDER
## 
### 
Varsity ladder matches among
players below team rank are pro
gressing slowly with but one singles
encounter settled since the new sys
tem began. Wallace, number 11 on
the new list, held his position against
Young in three sets, 3-6, 6-3, 6-3,
last week.
Joe Melczer kept himself from
slipping another rung on the fresh
man ladder by virtue of a 0-6, 6-4,
6-2 win over Fred Hills, remaining
number eight. Al Woodward jumped
two places so far this quarter, de
feating first Frank Barnes 6-3, 8-6,
and then Norman Wright 6-2, 6-0.
He now rests in twelfth position.
A sensational freshman doubles
pail - , Phil Kneisley and Leighton
Johnson, has climbed into second
position below Otis and Ellis by
beating out both the Sheahan-
Wright and Bogan-Shwnyder com
binations. The former were van
quished 4-6, 6-3, 6-3, and the latter
6-3, 4-6, 6-2.
Four million persons voted in the
muncipal elections of Spain.
