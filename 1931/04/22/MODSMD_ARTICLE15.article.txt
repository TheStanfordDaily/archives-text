# ROTHERT GETS NICHE IN "HALL OF FAME"
## 
### 
Appearing among the "Interesting
Collegians" in the "Hall of Fame"
of the May issue of College Humor
is a picture of Harlow Rothert, "Pop"
Warner's star fullback of last year,
and former president of Men's Coun
cil.

Rothert's prominence in athletics
and in campus activities are the mer
its on which the "Hall of Fame" com
mittee chose him as one of the most
outstanding university students in
the United States. He is at present
on his way back from a trip to the
Orient, where he broke many shot
put records.
