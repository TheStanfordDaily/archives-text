# BOWMAN WILL GIVE SERMON IN CHAPEL
## 
### 
Rev. Harold Leonard Bowman, of
the First Presbyterian Church, Port
land, Oregon, will give the sermon
in the Memorial Church Sunday,
April 2(>.
The visiting preacher is considered
one of the best speakers of the
Northwest. He is President of the
Westminster Foundation Synod of
Oregon and is interested in student
work.
