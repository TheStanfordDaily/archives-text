# SPANISH PROFESSOR WILL SPEAK TODAY ON SOUTH AMERICA
## 
### 
Speaking on South American cul
ture, Professor Fedelino Figuereido '
of the University of Madrid, con
sidered the best literary and his
torical critic in Portugal, will give
an address in Spanish in the Little '
Theatre this afternoon at 4:15
o'clock.
* I
For this semester, Professor Fi- I
guereido is a visiting professor at
the University of California. He is |
conducting courses covering all
phases of Portuguese civilization, in
cluding exploration, medieval cul
ture, science, and economic life. He i
has been invited to lecture at
Columbia University during the
summer, and at the National Uni
versity of Mexico next year.
Professor Figuereido has written
several books on the different
periods of Portuguese literature.
The lecture, which is being given
under the auspices of the Commit
tee on Public Exercises, is open to
everyone.
