# CARD-TROJAN MEET DUCATS TO BE SOLD
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stanford-
Southern California track meet in
the Stanford Stadium, Saturday,
May 2, will be on sale Monday,
Tuesday, and Wednesday at the
A.S.S.U. offices in the Union and
the Board of Athletic Control office
from 9 to 12 and 1:30 to 5 o'clock.
Each student may buy one rooter's
ticket for 50 cents upon presentation
of a student body card and as many
i general admission tickets at $1 as he
wishes.
