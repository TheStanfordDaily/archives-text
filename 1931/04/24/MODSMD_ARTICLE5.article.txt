# APRIL 30 DEADLINE SET FOR ORDERING SENIOR PROGRAMS
## 
### 
With the deadline for ordering
I senior programs and announcements
1 set for April 30, Robert Perry, chair
man of the committee in charge of
procuring them, announced yester
day that absolutely no orders would
be accepted after that date since it
will be impossible to fill them then.
Programs are on order for one
dollar apiece. They are bound in
; brown suede with a picture of the
Memorial Church embossed upon
them in silver, serving as a souvenir
of Stanford as well as a calendar of
senior activities.
Announcements, which serve as
engraved notifications of the send
er's pending graduation, are 10 cents
each.
