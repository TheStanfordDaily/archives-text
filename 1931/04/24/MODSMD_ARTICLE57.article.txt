# SCHOLARSHIP LOAN APPLICATIONS FOR WOMEN DUE MAY 1
## 
### 
The Scholarship Committee of the
Women's University Club has an
nounced that applications for its
Scholarship Loan Fund will be due
on May 1.
Individual loans are not to exceed
$300 a year or $600 during the entire
course. The money will be loaned
without interest during the college
course and for three years after
graduation. If the loan is not paid
at the expiration of that time, 4 per
cent interest will be charged.
Awards will be based on charac
ter, scholarship, health, and need.
Preference will be given students of
junior and senior standing. Awards
will be made only to residents of
Los Angeles county.
Further information may be ob
tained in the Dean of Women's of
fice.
