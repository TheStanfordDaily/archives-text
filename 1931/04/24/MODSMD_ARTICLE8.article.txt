# TO LEAD SYMPHONY
##

LONDON. April 23. l/P) — Sir
Hamilton Harty, conductor of the
Halle orchestra, will leave England
in June to conduct 11 concerts in
California. The concerts are to be
given by the San Francisco Sym
phony Orchestra and the Hollywood
Howl Orchestra, three by the former
land eight by thu latter.
