# 205 PLEDGED ON FRIDAY TO ROW HOUSES
## D.K.E.'s Signed Largest Number With 16; Alpha Delts Second With 15 
### 
Men's spring rushing season came
to a close Friday afternoon, when
205 freshmen marched out of the
interfraternity lawyer's office in the
Old Union and up to the Row to the
houses they wished to pledge.
Delta Kappa Epsilon filled the
greatest number of bids, 16, while
Alpha Delta Phi emerged from the
pledging melee with the next high
est number, 15.
No additional pledges were re
ported to the Interfraternity Council
over the week-end, other than those
made at the lawyer's office, accord
ing to Richard Grant, president.
Pledging and rushing were illegal
on Saturday and yesterday, Grant
states.

Men's spring rushing season came
to a close Friday afternoon, when
205 freshmen marched out of the
interfraternity lawyer's office in the
Old Union and up to the Row to the
houses they wished to pledge.
Delta Kappa Epsilon filled the
greatest number of bids, 16, while
Alpha Delta Phi emerged from the
pledging melee with the next high
est number, 15.
No additional pledges were re
ported to the Interfraternity Council
over the week-end, other than those
made at the lawyer's office, accord
ing to Richard Grant, president.
Pledging and rushing were illegal
on Saturday and yesterday, Grant
states.
