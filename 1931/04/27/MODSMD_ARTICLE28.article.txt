# RENAISSANCE MUSIC TOPIC FOR LECTURE
## 
### 
A Iccturc recital, "Music of the
Renaissance," will be given by Elio
Gianturco, associate professor of
Italian at the University of Califor
nia, tomorrow night in the Little
Theatre at 7:30 o'clock. Professor
Gianturco will illustrate his lecture
with piano selections.
Gianturco came to the United
States from Italy a year ago. Since
his stay in the bay region, Professor
Gianturco has frequently delivered
lectures on music, and is known for
his musical ability.
