# POP WARNER, BUNN PLAN FOR SUMMER GRID, HOOP SCHOOL
## 
### 
Football and basketball coaches of
prep schools and colleges all over
the country are expected to be at
tracted to Stanford this summer by
a coaching school to be conducted
by Pop Warner and John Bunn.
The school, to be held from June
23 to July 3, will be the only one
in which Warner will participate
this* year. He will be assisted in the
grid classes by Tiny Thornhiil and
Chuck Winterburn, while Bunn wiil
handle the basketball classes.
It is planned that three days a
week will be devoted to football,
with the other two days occupied
by basketball. Four hours of class
room instruction will be held each
morning, with a two-hour period of
field demonstration each afternoon.
