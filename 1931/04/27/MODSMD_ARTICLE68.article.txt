# COUNCIL PENALIZES TOYON SOPHOMORES FOR ROW IN ENCINA
## 
### 
Two sophomore residents of
Toyon Hall were given sentences of
25 hours of work each at the Con
valescent Home when they were
convicted by Men's Council Friday
of disturbing the peace at Encina
Hall.
Alleged to have thrown firecrack
ers from Encina windows and other
wise to have created a disturbance,
Ihe sophomores were arraigned be
fore Men's Council rather than the
Encina Sponsors because of lack of
jurisdiction of the latter body.
A total of approximately $40 in
lines was levied by the council
against five speeders and several
parking violators. 0
