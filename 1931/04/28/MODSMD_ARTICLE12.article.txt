# FRESHMAN DEBATERS TO BE GIVEN DINNER
## 
### 
The entire Stanford freshman de
bating squad of 15 men will hold a
banquet in the Union at 6:30 o'clock
tomorrow night. James C. Emerson,
varsity coach, will be present at the
dinner.
Those of the squad who will be
able to be at the dinner are asked to
sign up with Wardell Duggan. The
freshmen will meet on Eneina steps
at 6:15 o'.clock before the banquet.
