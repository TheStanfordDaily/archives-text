# EDUCATOR TO SPEAK TO PI LAMBDA THETA
## 
### 
Mrs. Frank B. Duveneck, princi
pal of the Peninsula School of Cre
ative Education in Menlo Park, will
speak before the members of Pi
Lambda Theta Thursday afternoon
at 4:15 o'clock in the Women's Club
house, and will discuss the work
which she is carrying on at the
school.
All women interested in education
are invited to attend the meeting.
Mrs. Duveneck will give her listen
ers an opportunity to ask questions.
