# POSITIONS AT CAMPS ARE OPEN TO WOMEN
## 
### 
Stanford women interested in po
sitions at summer camps this year
may obtain detailed information
concerning them from Miss Gertrude
Hawley in her office in the new
Roble Gymnasium from 4 to 5
o'clock daily.
Requests for three or four v/omen
to act as camp counselors have come
to Miss Hawley and there are vari
ous other positions open. Those in
terested especially in Girl Scout
camps may also obtain information
from Miss Green, head of the Palo
Alto Girl Scout troop, in the Scout
headquarters in Palo Alto.
