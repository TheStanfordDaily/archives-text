# REGISTRAR RETURNS FROM TRIP IN EAST
## 
### 
John Pearce Mitchell, Registrar,
returned to the campus yesterday
morning from Buffalo, New York,
where he has been attending the
annual meeting of the American As
sociation of Collegiate Registrars.

The convention was held from
April 21 to 23 and included approxi
mately two hundred and thirty
members from the United States
and Canada. The association meets
every year to discuss methods and
policies of universities throughout
the United States.
