# SENT TO HONDURAS TO PROTECT AMERICANS
## 
### 

Associated I'ress I'hoio
The Memphis, one of three of the navy's swiftest cruisers sent to protect American citizens on the
north coast of Honduras where a revolution has broken out against the regime of President Mejia
Colindres. Outbreaks occurred in north coast ports where American fruit companies have large inter
ests and scores of American citizens live.
