# EPISCOPALIAN BISHOP TO PREACH SUNDAY
## 
### 
The Right Reverend Edward
Lambe Parsons, Episcopalian Bishop
of California, will deliver the ser
mon at the 11 o'clock service in the
Memorial Church Sunday, May 3.
The service also includes:
Welsh Hymn, "Lovely"
Organ Prelude; Prelude on a
Vaughan-Williams
Introit; "As for Me, I will come into
Thy House"
Jubilate; Plaint Chant
Anthem: "Let Our Mouths Be
Filled with Thy Praise"
'Rachmaninoff
Sanctus; XIV Century Plain Song
