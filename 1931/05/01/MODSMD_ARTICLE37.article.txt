# EASTERN COLLEGE HANDS PROMOTION TO STANFORD MAN
## 
### 
Notice of the promotion of William
C. Johnstone, Jr., at George Wash
ington University, Washington, D.C.,
has been received by the political
science department. Johnstone has
been advanced from the position of
instructor to assistant professor in
political science at the eastern insti
tution. He is at present completing
work on his thesis, ''The Adminis
tration of the International Settle
ment of Shanghai, China," in order
to obtain a doctor's degree from
Stanford.
Mrs. Johnstone, formerly Anne
Hartwell, '28, is finishing her mas
ter's thesis, "Comparative Study of
the Governments of Eastern and
Western Samoa." Both Mr. and Mrs.
Johnstone are particularly interested
in international relations.
