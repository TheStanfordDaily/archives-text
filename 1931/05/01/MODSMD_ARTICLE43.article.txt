# JUNIOR, SOPHOMORE SOCCERITES TO MEET
## 
### 
The junior and sophomore class
soccer teams will meet on the soc
cer field at 4 o'clock today to de
cide" who shall occupy the cellar
position in the interclass soccer
tournament.

The juniors put up an excellent
fight Wednesday against the strong
senior team which is composed
mostly of varsity players. The jun
iors should manage to direct the
sophomores into the basement, un
less the teamwork of the second year
men has greatly improved in the last
week.

The final game of the series will
be played Monday, when the seniors
tangle with the freshmen for the in
terclass soccer championship of the
school.
