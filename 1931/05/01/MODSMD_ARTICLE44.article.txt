# W.A.A. GOLFERS WILL MEET IN GYMNASIUM
## 
### 
With over fifteen women signed
up for the W.A.A. golf tournament,
a meeting of all entrants will be
held Monday afternoon at 4 o'clock
in the locker room of the women's
gymnasium.

All women who play golf are
urged to enter the tournament,
which is being held to arouse en
thusiasm for the sport and to tee if
there is enough interest to merit the
granting of points for competition.
The first round will be played next
week.
