# CLUB ISSUES FINAL CALL FOR WRITINGS
## 
### 
Today is the last day for submit
ting manuscripts for the English
Club yearbook of Stanford writing.
The book, which will be published
this quarter, will contain what the
English Club committee considers
the best campus writing of the past
year. A prize will be awarded to
the writer of the best piece of work.

Contributions should be deposited
in the cabinet in room 200, or given
to the English Department secretary.
At the meeting of the English Clpb
held last Wednesday evening, all the
manuscripts submitted at that time
were read and considered.
