# TRANSATLANTIC CROSSING
## 
### 
FRIEDRICHSHAFEN, Germany,
April 30. (/P)—The Graf Zeppelin
will make a second transatlantic
crossing to Brazil late in August,
Captain Hans von Schiller said today.
