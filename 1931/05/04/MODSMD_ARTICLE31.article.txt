# FINAL MOOT TRIAL TO BE DECIDED BY THREE S.F. JUDGES
## 
### 
When the decision in Thursday's
moot trial, the last of the season, is
handed down, it will be the work of
three prominent San Francisco jur
ists.
Judge John T. Nourse, presiding
justice of the California District
Court of Appeal; Judge Walter Per
ry Johnson, presiding justice of the
' appellate department of the San
Francisco Supei'ior Court; and Judge
Homer R. Spence, associate justice
of the California District Court of
Appeal, will be on the bench for
this trial.
John Cranston and Robert Hume
will oppose James Engebretsen and
W. L. Skanchy in the trial, which
will follow immediately after the
annual Law Association banquet.
