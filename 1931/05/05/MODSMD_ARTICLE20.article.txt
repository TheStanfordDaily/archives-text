# Y.W.C.A. CABINET TO DISCUSS CONFERENCE
## 
### 
Plans for this year's Y.W.C.A.
summer conference at Asilomar will
be discussed at the regular business
meeting and dinner for the Y.W.C.A.
cabinet tonight at 6 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. The conference
includes colleges and universities
throughout northern and southern
California, Oregon, Nevada, Wash
ington, and Arizona.
The net profit from the May
breakfast will also be announced at
this meeting. An unofficial estimate
places the amount near $60.
