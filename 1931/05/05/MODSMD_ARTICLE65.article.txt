# CHESS CLUB MEETS FACULTY TEAM IN IMPORTANT CONTEST
## 
### 
Tonight will be the big night for
the Stanford Chess Club, with the
long-talked-of match between the
faculty and the Chess Club taking
place at 8 o'clock at the home of one
of its members.
All members of the Stanford Chess
Club must meet at 7:30 o'clock in
the lobby of the Union. A match
with the University of San Francisco
has been arranged for Friday night
at 7:30 o'clock in the Union lobby.
Two official California Intercolle
giate Chess League matches are to
be played at this time. Ed Fisher
and Winston Strong are the two
players who will represent Stanford.
Students from San Francisco Uni
versity and the University of Cali
fornia have entered.
