# SHOVE ENGAGEMENT TO McCLEAVE TOLD
## 
### 
Mary McCleave, '31, Gamma Phi
Beta, announced her engagement to
Maynor Shove, '31, Phi Delta Theta,
at the initiation banquet held by the
Gamma Phi Beta sorority Sunday
evening at Wilson's.
Miss McCleave was prominent on
the campus in music and graduated
in December. Shove is an outstand
ing track man and will graduate in
June.
