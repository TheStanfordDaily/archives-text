# QUAKE HITS ALABAMA
## 
### 
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., May 5. (/P)
—An earth tremor of sufficient force
to arouse sleeping residents and
rattle dishes and windows was felt
in Birmingham and in sections with
in a radius of approximately 75
miles early today.
