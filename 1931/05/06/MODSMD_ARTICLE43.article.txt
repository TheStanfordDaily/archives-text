# Y.W. TEA TO HONOR FACULTY MEMBERS
## 
### 
A tea in honor of the members
of the psychology department will
be given under the auspices of the
Y.W.C.A. from. 4 to G o'clock Friday
afternoon in the "Y" rooms on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
All students, both men and wom
en, interested in psychology and de
siring to meet the professors, cor
rectors, and graduate students of
the department are invited to at
tend the tea. Wives of professors in
the department have also been in
vited.
Marion Jones, Catherine Guerard,
and Ann Adams will be hostesses in
charge of the tea which is the fourth
in a series of departmental teas
which the Y.W.C.A. is sponsoring
this quarter.
