# POLA NEGRI RETURNS
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, May 5. (/P) -
After an absence of two years, Pola
Negri, Polish film star, was back in
Hollywood today "inspired again,"
talking of an American husband, the
superiority of American styles, and
a new career in the talkies.
