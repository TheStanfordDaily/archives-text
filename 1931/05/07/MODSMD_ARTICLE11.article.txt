# LA 						MER LECTURES ON MODERN CHEMISTRY
## 
### 
Speaking under the auspices of
the Stanford chapter of Sigma Xi,
national honorary scientific society,
Professor Victor K. LaMer of Col
umbia University, last night de
livered a lecture on "A Modern
Theory of Electrolyte Solutions."
The lecture was given before a
large group of faculty members,
graduate students, and undergradu
ates in room 20 of the Chemistry
Building, and dwelt largely on the
result of Professor LaMer's own re
searches in the field of physical
chemistry.
