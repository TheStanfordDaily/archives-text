# CHESS CLUB TEAMS WILL MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
There will be an important meet
ing of the Stanford Chess Club to
night in the lobby of the Union at
7:30 o'clock. The seven men who
will represent Stanford in the match
with the University of San Fran
cisco will be ehosen. This match
will be played in the lobby of the
Union Friday night at 7:30 o'clock.
The last match of the Stanford
Chess Club played with the faculty
last Tuesday night ended in a tie,
each club winning six games.
