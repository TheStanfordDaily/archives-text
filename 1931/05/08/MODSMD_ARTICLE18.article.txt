# NEW RECORD MADE IN ANNOUNCEMENT, PROGRAM CAMPAIGN
## 
### 
Setting a new record for the total
number of orders placed, the senior
program and announcement sale
closed last night with 527 programs
on order and 6298 announcements.
The programs, which are bound
in brown suede with the Memorial
Church embossed in silver on the
cover, will contain the names of the
graduating students, those receiving
advanced degrees, and the schedule
of Senior Week activities which is
as follows:
Promenade, Thursday, June 11;
Senior Ball, Friday, June 12; Class
Day, Saturday, June 13; Baccalau
reate Sunday, June 14; Commence
ment, Monday, June 15.
