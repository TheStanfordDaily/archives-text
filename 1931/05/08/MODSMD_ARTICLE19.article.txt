# PSYCHOLOGY DEPT. IS HONORED GUEST AT 'Y' TEA TODAY
## 
### 
Members of the psychology de-
will be guests this after
noon at a tea being given in their
honor by the Y.W.C.A. from 4 to 0
o'clock in the "Y" rooms on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Professors and their wives, cor
rectors, and graduate students work
ing for their master's and doctor's
degrees in the psychology depart
ment have been especially invited.
All students, both men and women,
wishing to meet these individuals or
interested in psychology are invited
to attend the tea.
Ann Adams, Marian Jones, and
Catherine Guerard will be hostesses
for this tea which is the fourth in a
series of departmental tens being
given by the Y.W.C.A. to foster bet
ter student-faculty interest and re
lationship.
