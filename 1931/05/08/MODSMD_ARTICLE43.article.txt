# JORDAN UNCHANGED
## 
###

change all week in the condition of
David Starr Jordan, chancellor
emeritus, Doctor Russell V. A. Lee
announced yesterday. Dr. Jordan
has been critically ill for several
weeks, but has shown some im
provement for the last ten days.

The United States still refuses to
recognize the Russian Government.
