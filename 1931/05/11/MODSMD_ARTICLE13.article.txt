# CONCERT GIVEN IN CITY BY STANFORD SYMPHONIC GROUP
## 
### 
Upon the invitation of Chester
Rosekranz, general director of Music
Week in San Francisco, the Stan
ford Symphony Orchestra gave a
concert in the Civic Auditorium last
night, climaxing the Music Week
celebration.
Selections for the concert, which
was presented under the direction of
Daniel P. Bryant, were taken from
the program offered here on Tues
day under the auspices of the Com
mittee on Public Exercises. Von
Weber's "Oberon Overture," pre
sented with finish and originality of
interpretation, was enthusiastically
received.
