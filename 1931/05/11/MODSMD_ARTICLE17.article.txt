# ESPINOSA TO GIVE TUESDAY LECTURE ON SPANISH CITIES
## 
### 
An illustrated lecture, "Cities of
Southern Spain," will be given by
Professor Aurelio Espinosa of the
Romanic languages department to
morrow night at 7:30 o'clock in the
Little Theater.
Professor Espinosa has been con
nected with Stanford since 1910. He
has also taught at the Universities of
Colorado, Mexico, Chicago, and Cal
ifornia.
He is associate editor of the
Journal of American Folklore, a
member of the advisory editorial
board of Inter-America, and a cor
responding member of the Royal
Spanish Academy.
