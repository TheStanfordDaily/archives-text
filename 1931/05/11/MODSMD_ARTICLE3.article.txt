# PEN HOUSE DAY AT HOSPITAL ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
1' richiy, May 22, has been set as
cn house day for the Stanford
ispital instead of Tuesday, May 12,
cording to Vesta Nickerson, chair
in of the Y.W.C.A. Hospital Ser
:e Committee.
Stanford Y.W.C.A. women will act
hostesses for the open house and
II show all visitors through the
spital. The open hours will be
>m 2:30 to 5 o'clock in the after
on and from 7:30 to 10 o'clock in
2 evening.
