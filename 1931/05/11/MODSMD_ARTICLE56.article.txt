# STANFORD NURSING SCHOOL TO HONOR 48 WITH DIPLOMAS
## 
### 
The Stanford School of Nursing
will hold graduation exercises next
Wednesday evening in the San
Francisco Auditorium for a class of
48.
Following is the graduation list
for 1931: Anita Augusta Adams,
Karin Bertha Anderson, Charlotte
Lucille Bacon, Nell Baxter, Dorothy
Helen Bomberger, Rena May Cas
well, Esmee Ethel Cheney, Elsie
Fern Compston, Maxine M. Corne
lius, Lorene Evelyn Cusick, Gert
rude Eleanor Dawson, Margaret
Esther Dawson, Ellen Eisner, Lois
Adelaide Ewing, Beatrice Harrison,
Mary Bruce Harvey.
Helen Hedberg, Prudence Hylen,
K. Elizabeth Jackson, Dorothy Eliza
beth Jepson, Florence Kelting, Helen
Jeannette KlefYman, Carmen Koehne,
Florence Alma Knoles, Ino Zarella
Knopf, Violette G. Latta, Maude
Elizabeth Leflang, Alice Elizabeth
Lyser, Alice Lucille Martin, Rose
Ann Nulty, Elsie Virginia Olson,
Florence M. Pickerill, Edna Rich
ardson, Margaret M. Ross, Carol
Nadine Rumsey, Helen Ruth, Eliza
beth L. Sanwald, Doris Sibbald.
Kirstine Smith, Betty J. Spekain,
Mary Mildred Stack, Alice Stamm,
Beatrice H. Starr, Rhoda Louise
Tibbetts, Katherine Torjussen, Wan
da Claire Truman, and Orma Whyte.
In addition, six 1930 graduates will
take their degrees as public health
nurses this year. They are Ramona
Borree, Edith Jenks, Vera Johnson,
Helen McClellan, Rachel Parker, and
Hilda Willard.
