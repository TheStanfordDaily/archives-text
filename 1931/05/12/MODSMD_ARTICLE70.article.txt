# FORMER VICTORS ENTERED IN AUTO CLASSIC
## 
### 

Associated I'ress Photo
Four previous winners are among the drivers named to handle cars in the annual 500-mile race to
oe run at the Indianapolis motor speedway, May 30. They are Billy Arnold, Chicago, who won last year;
Ralph De Palma, victor 16 years ago; Louis Meyer, Southgate, Cal., who finished first in 1928, and
Peter De Paolo, Hollywood, Cal., who led the way in 1925. Arnold is shown below with his mechanic.
