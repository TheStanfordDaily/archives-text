# FINAL DATE FOR LISTING ROOMS IN TOYON, BRANNER
## 
### 
THODAY is the last opportun
ity for men now living in
Toyon or Branner Hall to re
serve their rooms for next fail
quarter. The deposits of $5 may
he paid up until 5 o'clock to
Miss Vivienne Church in the
office of the Dean of Men.
All rooms for which no reser
vations have been made by to
day will be placed in the draw
ings to be held Wednesday eve
ning at 7:30 o'clock in the of
fices of the two halls.
