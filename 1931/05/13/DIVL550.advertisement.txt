The Swimming Racket

advertisements—the best this and the*
finest that. And we've come to the con
clusion it's quite a racket. After all the
only real test of a swim suit is the way

Swim Suits pass with flying colors. Of
course, you can pay fancier prices for
muchly advertised suits —but when you
go to the smart beaches and pools and the
ones who really know will be wearing
Gantner Swim Spits because they're right
in the van of fashion—and they cost you
less, and serve you better.
