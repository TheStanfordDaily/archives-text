# DOLLAR HOLDS OWN IN SERIOUS ILLNESS
## 
### 
SAN RAFAEL, May 12. UP)—Cap
tain Robert Dollar held his ground
today in his fight against a kidney
ailment. He has been critically ill at
his home here for a week.
Dr. R. C. Dufficy said the 87-year
old shipping magnate's temperature
was 98 this evening, his pulse 88,
and his respiration 24—the same as
this morning. This meant the Cap
tain had held the slight gain ac
complished earlier in the week.
