# EUROPEAN TOUR FOR SUMMER ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
For the purpose of studying land
scape architecture in Europe this
summer, as well as gaining a his
torical and cultural background, the
Copley Landscape Architecture tour
has been planned by the Bureau of
University Travel.
The tour will be under the leader
ship of G. Leslie Lynch, professor of
landscape architecture at Ohio State
University. Specialized scholars will
conduct the studies. Included in the
itinerary are England, France, Bel
gium, Italy, and Switzerland. The
group will leave New York June 19,

and will return August 30. Further
information may be obtained by
writing to the Bureau at Newton.
Massachusetts.
