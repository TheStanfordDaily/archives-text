# BUCHAREST TO HONOR ILEANA'S BETROTHAL
## 
### 

Bucharest has started plans for elaborate functions to honor the return of Princess lleana of Rumania
and Archduke Anton of Austria whose betrothal recently was announced. A mutual fascination for avia
tion led to the romance, lleana is shown in center in her flying costume, at right among her library
books, while at the left is a recent portrait of her.
