FRIDAY
1:10 p.m.—Sigma Del(a Chi will
meet in the Press Building.
3 p.m.—Stanford Dames sewing
group meets with Mrs. Duncan
Macruer, 610 California, Mayfield.
4:30 p.m.—Members of the Cosmo
politan Club will meet in -the
Union court for a t,rip to La
Honda. An election will be held
for new officers.

MISCELLANEOUS

Union, Sequoia, and Encina 'men
who wish rooms for fall quarter
should call at the Dean of Men's
office Thursday or Friday to make
reservations.
No military will be held Saturday
or Monday.
