# URMY POETRY PRIZE DEADLINE IS MAY 21
## 
### 
The Clarence Urmy Award for
lyric poetry, offered yearly through
the English department, will be open
this year to registered graduate as
well as undergraduate students. The
deadline for entries in the contest
has been set at the evening of May
21, the winner to be announced as
soon thereafter as possible.
Conditions and rules of the con-:
test have been posted on the bulletin
board of the English department;
manuscripts may be turned in to the
office of the department any time
before May 21.
