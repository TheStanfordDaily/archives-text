# PROGRAM PRESENTED BY PI LAMBDA THETA
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, national
women's educational fraternity, hon
ored the wives of the faculty mem
bers of the education department at
a tea at the Women's Clubhouse
yesterday afternoon. Lilly Nordgren
sang several classical selections and
an informal discussion took place.
There will be an important busi
ness meeting of Pi Lambda Theta
Tuesday afternoon at 5 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse for the pur
pose of electing officers for the com
ing year. It is urgent that all mem
bers and new initiates be present.
