# PI LAMBDA THETA TO ELECT NEW OFFICERS
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, national wom
en's honorary education society, will
hold a business meeting in the
Women's Clubhouse at 5 o'clock
Tuesday for the purpose of electing
next year's officers.
Isabell Warren, vice-president, i
will preside since Dorothea Wyatt,
president, has left for a trip around
the world.
