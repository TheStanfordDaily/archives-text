# SCHOOL OF BIOLOGY TO HONOR FACULTY
## 
### 
Professor Isabel McCracken and
James Rollin Slonaker will be hon
ored at a dinner to be sponsored by
the School of Biological Sciences at
the Stanford Union on Thursday,
May 21, at 6:30 o'clock. All the fac
ulty and their wives are invited to
attend.

Notice of acceptance should' be
sent to Dr. E. G. Martin, Department
of Physiology, not later than Wed
nesday, May 20. The price of the
subscriptions will be $1.25 per plate.
