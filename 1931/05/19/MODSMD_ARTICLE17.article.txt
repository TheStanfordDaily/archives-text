# CUPID MARKS PEACE
## 
### 
ADDIS ABABA, May 18. (/P> —
Cupid is making peace in Abyssinia.
Princess Senaba-Auorq, daughter of
the Emperor, is engaged to a son of
Has Hailou, governor of Godjam
province, who has been hostile to the
throne.
