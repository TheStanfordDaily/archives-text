# ADMINISTRATIVE NEGLIGENCE
## 
### 
A clear example of carelessness and neglect was re
vealed yesterday in I lie lire at Cedro Cottage, located on
the campus, ('edro Cottage, being on the campus, is sup
posed to he protected by the University in case of tire. How
ever, on arriving at the scene of the blaze yesterday the
Stanford lire department found the water supply there
inadequate to do much good. Fortunately, the lire de
stroyed only the outhouses in which it started.
About three years ago the University was advised lo put
in ;i four-inch water connection to Cedro Cottage. Instead,
:i two-inch pipeline was installed. Yesterday's incident may
convince the University that all of the campus is not sat
isfactorily protected against lire hazards.
