# PALO ALTO HOSPITAL OPEN TO PUBLIC ON FRIDAY AFTERNOON
## 
### 
Finishing touches on the new
$465,000 Palo Alto Hospital will be
completed in time for the opening
which will take place Friday, May
22, after many postponements.
Open house will be held from 3
p.m. to 9 o'clock in the evening.
During the afternoon officials of the
City of Palo Alto and the Univer
sity will receive with the Auxiliary
to the Palo Alto Hospital. Dr. Ralph
S. Seem, physician superintendent
of the University hospitals will be
a guest of honor. |
Friday's opening will be for public
inspection only. The business open
ing will not be until June 1.
