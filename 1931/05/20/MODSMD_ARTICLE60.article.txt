# SILVER CUP TO BE PRESENTED WINNER OF SALES CONTEST
## 
### 
The woman who has sold the
greatest number of Chaparrals this
year will be awarded the Chapar
ral's silver loving cup. The cup will
be on display on the Quad tomor
row.
The highest "four women in the
race for this honor are Marie Ba
ker, Janet Kempenich, Mildred
West, and Bernice Woodard. These
women will vie for the highest sales
record for the last time with the
next issue of the Chaparral.
The cup will be awarded imme
diately after the publishing of the
June issue of the Chaparral, which
will go on sale about June 2.
Last year's cup was won by Jean
Carson.
