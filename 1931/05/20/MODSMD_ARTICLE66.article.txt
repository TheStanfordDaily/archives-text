# MODERN SPAIN TOPIC OF ESPINOSA SPEECH
## 
### 
The dictatorship and revolution in
modern Spain will be discussed by
Professor Aurelio M. Espinosa of the
Romanic Languages Department at
a meeting of the International Rela
tions Club tonight at 7:30 o'clock in
the West Memorial Library. One or
two students will also be prepared
to take a leading part in the dis
cussion.
Election of officers for the coming
year will also take place at the
meeting.
