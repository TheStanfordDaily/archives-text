# COLLEGE WOMEN TO MEET IN BERKELEY
## 
### 
BERKELEY. May 20 (/P) — The
American Association of University
Women will meet here May 22 and
23 with the East Bay Branch of the
American Association of University
Women. The College Women's Club
of Berkeley will be the hostess
branch.
