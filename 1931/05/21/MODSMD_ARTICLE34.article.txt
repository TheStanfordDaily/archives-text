THURSDAY
3:30 p.m.—Max Montor will read
Goethe's Faust in German in the
Little Theatre, under the auspices
of the German department.
4 p.m. — Chemophysical Biology
Seminar in Room 427.
4:30-5:30 p.m.—Fellowship tea on
Ihe third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse. Faculty and students
are cordially invited.
5:15 p.m. —Organ recital to be given
by Warren D. Allen, University
organist, in the Memorial Church.
6:15 p.m.—Pi Sigma Alpha will meet
for the last time this quarter.
Franklin Hickborn, legislative re
porter, will speak. Election of of
ficers.
FRIDAY
2 p.m.—Oral examination for the
Doctorate, Charles Craven Scott
(Major, History), in Room 308, Li
brary.
2 p.m.—Oral examination for the
Doctorate, Stuart Cuthbertson
(Major, Romanic Languages), in
Room 310, Library.
