# SMALL GRASS BLAZE PUT OUT BY FIREMEN
## 
### 
Campus fires yesterday included
one small grass fire between the
anatomy building and the nursery.
No damage was done and the blaze
was extinguished immediately.
Grass fires in Menlo Park con
tinued to be set yesterday. A small
fire was reported about 3:30 o'clock
along Teskalusa avenue. Authorities
report evidences that firebugs are
at work in this district, but as yet
no one has been apprehended. Last
year at this time incendiaries caused
considerable trouble to the authori
ties.
