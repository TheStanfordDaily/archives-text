# JUNIOR TENNIS TEAM TAKES CLASS TITLE
## 
### 
The juniors took the interclass
tennis crown of the University yes
terday afternoon when they defeated
the senior team 5-0.
The scores were as follows: singles,
Blethen d. Weesner 5-7, 7-5, 8-6;
singles, Petterson d. Roberts 3-6,
6-3, 6-2; doubles, Petterson and
Gordon d. Weesner and Roberts,
6-3, 8-G. And in the second round,
doubles, Robinson and Blethen of
the junior team won by default. In
the third doubles, Strong and Bur
nett defeated Eastman and Gantner,
6-4, 6-4.
