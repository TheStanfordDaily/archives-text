# 'ELITE' STANFORD ROUGHS OFFERED GUIDE TO DANCES
## 
### 
UOR the benefit of the Stan
ford rough who might other
wise be bored this week-end,
the following guide to campus
functions is published:
The elite might start the eve
ning off at the Alpha Phi for
mal. following this with either
the Kappa Alpha Theta formal,
or the Alpha" Sigma Phi formal
—it doesn't make any difference
which. Leave for the Phi Kappa
Sigma sport dance as soon as
the food gives out.
On Saturday evening the fare
is less complicated, as only the
Delta Gamma formal is sched
uled. Remember to get there
before midnight as University
regulation prohibits any danc
ing on Sunday.
