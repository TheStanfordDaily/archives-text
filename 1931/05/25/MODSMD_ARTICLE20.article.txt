# WOMEN'S REGULATIONS SUBMITTED TO DEAN
## 
### 
Further discussion of women's
lockout regulations occupied the
larger part of the meeting of Wom
en's Conference Friday afternoon in
the Women's Clubhouse, when Con
ference prepared a draft for sub
mission to Dean Mary Yost and
Acting President Robert E. Swain.
Dean Yost and President Swain
are expected to take action on the
draft today.
