# FISHERMEN DIE
## 
### 
TOKYO, May 24 (.4'). —Twenty-
three fishermen are believed to have
been drowned after a trawler with
a crew of 31 overturned in a storm
near Yokohama today. Other ves
sels picked up eight of the men.
