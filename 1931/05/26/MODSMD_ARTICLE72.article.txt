# DAILY WILL CHOOSE NEW EDITOR TODAY
## 
### 
Voting for the editor of the Stan
ford Daily, Volume 80, will take
place between 1 and 2 o'clock today
at the Daily office. Nominees for the
position are Reid Briggs and Rich
ard J. Friendlich. Lists of the mem
bers of the staff who hold votes
have been posted, with the number
of votes each one possesses.
