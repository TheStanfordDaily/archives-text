# SOCIETY NOMINATES OFFICERS FOR YEAR
## 
### 
Edith Badger was nominated for
the presidency of Women's "S" So
ciety, honorary organization for the
recognition of outstanding women
athletes, at a meeting of the group
yesterday afternoon. Elizabeth Mc-
Cullough was the only nominee to
date for secretary-treasurer.
New officers and newly elected
members of the society will be an
nounced during Field Day, which
has been changed to next Wednes
day, Mary Edith Jones, outgoing
president, stated last night.
Except in the case of outstanding
senior athletes, new members are
elected only at the end of the year,
continued Miss Jones.
Edith Badger is the outgoing sec
retary-treasurer.
