# BRYANT NAMED HEAD OF SCOUTING GROUP
## 
### 
At a meeting of the Stanford Eagle
Scout chapter of Alpha Phi Omega,
national scouting fraternity, Bruce
Bryant was elected and installed as
president, Masura Nakayama as sec
retary, and Robert Kemp as treas
urer, last night.
The out-going officers are Victor
Siegfried, president; Jack Lowson,
vice-president; Charles McGee, sec
retary, and William Bryant, treas
urer.
Besides the election .and installa
tion of officers, a report of the na
tional scouting convention was pre
sented by Jack Tarr.
