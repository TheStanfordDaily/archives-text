# DE MOLAY CLUB TO HOLD LAST MEETING
## 
### 
Officers will be installed at to
night's meeting of the Stanford De
Molay Club, to be held at the Y.M.
C.A. club rooms at 8 o'clock. Cap
tain S. F. Miller will be the speaker
of the evening. As the meeting will
be the last of the year, President
Noel Eldred urges a large attend
ance.. Refreshments will be served.
