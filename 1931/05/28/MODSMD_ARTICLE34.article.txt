# BINGHAM WILL TALK AT BERKELEY HOUSE
## 
### 
Professor J. W. Bingham, of the
law school, will give a report on
"Piracy" at a meeting at Internation
al House, Berkeley," today and to
morrow, sponsored by the Research
in International Law of the Harvard
Law School.
The report is part of an unofficial
attempt to codify international law.
Professor Bingham, in the prepara
tion of the report, has had an ad
visory committee consisting of: Pro
fessors Graham H. Stdart, Jackson
H. Ralston, and Harold H. Sprout,
of the Stanford political science de
partment.
