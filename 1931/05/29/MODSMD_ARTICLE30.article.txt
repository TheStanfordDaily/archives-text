# VAN PATTEN TO TALK ON CANADA WRITERS
## 
### 
Nathan van Patten, director of
the University libraries, will lecture
in the Little Theater Tuesday eve
ning at 7:30 o'clock. He will speak
on the "Poets and Prose Writers of
Canada."
During four years in Canada van
Patten had an opportunity to meet
many of the important contempo
rary writers of that country. His
bibliography of the works of Bliss
Carman, written in collaboration
with R. H. Hathaway of Toronto,
will be published in New York City
later in the year.
