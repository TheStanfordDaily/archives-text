# JUNIOR WOMEN'S SUPPER
## 
### 
Barbara Beach Thompson has in
vited the Junior women to a barbe
cue supper on Tuesday, June 2, at
her home at 445 Lowell avenue,
Palo Alto. Anyone planning to go
who has not yet notified Margaret
Abrams, please do so at once. Supper
will be at a quarter to six.
Stockholm police have been re
armed with swords.
