# GRADUATE STUDENTS GET GYM PRIVILEGES
## 
### 
All men graduate students
in Stanford for the Summer Quarter
have all the privileges of the men's
gym, announces Coach Harry Maloney.
Two classes are given, one at 11:20
a.m. and one at 4:00 p.m. Handball,
swimming, tennis, volleyball and other
sports are included in these classes.
There will be no costs other than
the regular gym fee.
