# ROTH PRESIDES OVER ROTARY INTERNATIONAL
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, comptroller of the
University, called to order the 1931
convention of Rotary International in
Vienna yesterday. The convention con
tinues until Friday.
Roth was elected president of Rotary
International in Chicago last year. Prior
to this election he was district governor
of Rotary.
Paul Harris of Chicago, founder of
Rotary, will be unable to attend the
convention, but has sent greetings.
Among the speakers will be .Lord Rob
ert Cecil of England, Dr. Louis Grote
of Germany, and Edward A. Filene of
Boston.
