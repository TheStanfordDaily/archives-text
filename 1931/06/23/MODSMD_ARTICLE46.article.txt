# STANFORD GRADUATE GETS APPOINTMENT
## 
### 
Mrs. Fannie Carruthers, who received
her M.A. degree and her certificate of
administration from Stanford, has been
selected as dean of women and vice
principal of the Mountain View Union
High School.
Mrs. Carruthers was a member of
the faculty of the San Jose High School
for a number of years. She formerly
served on the Petaluma High School
staff.
