# FIRST STUDENT TOUR GOES TO SANTA CRUZ AT 8 A.M. SATURDAY
## 
### 
Cars will leave the Union main
entrance at 8 o'clock Saturday
morning for the all-day trip to
Santa Cruz, the first of a series
of short trips planned by tv/o
Palo Alto taxi companies. Fare
for this trip will be $2.50, and
luncheons can either be taken
by the excursionists or be pur
chased in Santa Cruz.
The drive will be over the Santa
Cruz Mountains and will include
the Cliff Drive along the Pacific
Ocean. The party will probably
return to the campus about 4
o'clock in the afternoon.
