# SUNDAY'S SERMON TO BE GIVEN BY SOARES
## 
### 
The Rev. Theodore G. Soares, Min
ister of Community Church in Pasa
dent, will preach at the services held
in the Memorial Church on Sunday at
11 o'clock. The service will also in
clude the organ prelude, "Lord, Hear
the Voice of My Prayer," by Bach,
Noble's choral prelude on "St. Ann,"
the chants "Venite" and "Sanctus," and
Bach's anthem, "My God, I Give My
self to Thee."

At 7:30 in the evening, there will be
an organ recital by Warren D. Allen,
University Organist.
