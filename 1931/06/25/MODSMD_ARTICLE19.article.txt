# SCHENCK ELECTED TO GEOLOGICAL SOCIETY
## 
### 
Professor Hubert G. Schenck of the
Geology Department has been elected
to membership of the Geological So
ciety of France. This association was
founded in 1830 and corresponds to the
Geological Society of America. The
members are among the best known
geologists and paleontologists of Eu
rope.
With the exception of Professor
Schenck, there are only three mem
bers of the Pacific coast.
