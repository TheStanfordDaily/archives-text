# FACULTY WIVES MAY SWIM
## 
### 
Wives of faculty men and their
daughters may avail themselves of
swimming privileges at the new Roble
gymnasium during the summer on pay
ment of a fee of $5. For women not
connected with the university the toe
is $10. Classes in instruction will be
held at 10 a.m. on Tuesdays and Thurs
days, beginning today.
