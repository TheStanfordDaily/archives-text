# PUPIL OF ALLEN IN RECITAL HERE TODAY
## 
### 
Edward T. Kelly, organist at St. Pat
rick's Church in San Francisco, will
give an organ recital in the Memorial
Church at 4:15 this afternoon. He has
been a pupil of Warren D. Allen and
of Marcel Dupre in Paris.
His program will be composed of the
following numbers:
Prelude in C Minor—J. S. Bach.
Toccata per l'elevazione—Frescobaldi
Minuet from the suite "L'Arlesienne"
—Bizet.
Lied, Carillon—Louis Vierne.
