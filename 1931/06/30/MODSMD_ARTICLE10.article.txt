# COMTROLLER ROTH AWARDED GOLD ORDER
## 
### 
Almon Edward Roth, comptroller of
Stanford University and retiring presi
dent of the Rotary International has
been awarded the great gold order of
honor from the President of Austria,
according to an Associated Press dis
patch from Vienna, where Rotarians
have been in convention. M. Eugene
Ncusom, of Durham, N.C., former
president, received the great silver or
der.
The gold order was also conferred
upon Chesley R. Perry, of Chicago,
general secretary, and the silver order
of merit for services to the Austrian
Republic was awarded to Howard H.
Feighner, manager of this year's Ro
tary convention at Vienna.
