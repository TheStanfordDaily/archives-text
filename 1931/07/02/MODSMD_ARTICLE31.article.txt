# STANFORD SWIMMERS ENTER AMATEUR MEET
## 
### 
The Amateur Athletic Union open
swimming meet, which is being spon
sored by the Redwood City Exchange
Club, will be held July 14 in the Se
quoia High School tank, according to
an announcement made today by Nor
ton Thornton, swimming coach.
James Benet, entering Stanford
freshman and also former Northern
California private school champion, and
Harry Baskerville, transfer from Ore
gon State College, where he was a
freshman star, will both enter the div
ing competition unattached. These men
have been working out daily under the
supervision of Coach Thornton. Bill
Erskine, of last year's yearling squad,
will compete for the Athens Athletic
Club in the 220 yards, free style race.
