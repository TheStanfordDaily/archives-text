# EIGHTY WOMEN MEET AT EDUCATION TEA
## 
### 
More than eighty women students in
education attended a tea given by Pi
Lambda Theta Tuesday afternoon.
Prominent among the out-of-town
guests were Dr. D. J. Kaucher, Alpha
chapter, Missouri, who is now con
nected with the State Teachers College
at San Jose, and Miss Emily DeVore,
also of San Jose, a field member of the
local chapter. Mrs. Ellwood P. Cubber
ley, honorary member, Mrs. George K.
Benjamin, Miss Elisabeth Buckingham,
and Mrs. Elizabeth B. Snell, associate
members, received the guests.
