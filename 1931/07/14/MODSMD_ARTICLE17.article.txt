# CAMPUS DEPARTMENT FIGHTS FIRE HAZARD
## 
### 
Dry grass which might cause a seri
ous conflagration in the built-up resi
dential areas of the campus, is being
burned by the University fire depart
ment under the directiton of Chief R.
E. Dugan.
Work along Lasuen street from Santa
Ynez to ,Mayfield is being done and
when that is completed the firemen ex
pect to burn grass on Mayfield avenue
from Lasuen to the Lathrop residence
near San Francisquito creek, and on
Foothill boulevard where there are a
number of large residences.
