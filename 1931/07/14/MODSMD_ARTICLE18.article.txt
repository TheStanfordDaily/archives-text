# DAMES READING GROUP WILL MEET ON FRIDAY
## 
### 
The reading group of the Stanford
Dames met Thursday afternoon at the
Women's Clubhouse under the direc
tion of Mrs. John O. Moseley. They
plan to read and review at each meet
ing one book of fiction and one of non
fiction, alternating with drama, poetry,
and current events.
The next meeting will be held at 3
o'clock Friday afternoon at the home
of Mrs. Moseley, 1375 College Avenue,
who has planned a very interesting
program.
