# ANOTHER JOLLY-UP AT ROBLE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Another Jolly-Up will be held be
tween 7 and 8 o'clock tonight at Roble
Hall. Virginia Chamberlain and Annie
Pauline Moore, who are in charge of
arrangements, will be assisted by a
committee selected from Roble and
Hoyon halls.
All students on the campus are in
vited to attend.
