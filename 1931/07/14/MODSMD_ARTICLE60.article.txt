# PH.D. EXAMINATIONS TODAY
## 
### 
The oral examination of Benjamin
Sacks for the degree of Doctor of
Philosophy will be held in Room 308,
Library, at 2 o'clock this afternoon. His
major is history, and his minor, politi
cal science. Announcement of this ex
amination was omitted from the Week
ly Calendar.

A vat containing 50,000 gallons of
beer was poured into the Thames near
London recently, when it was dis
covered that a workman had drowned
in it.
