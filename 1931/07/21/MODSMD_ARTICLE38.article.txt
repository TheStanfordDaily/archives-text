# EAT WIENIES AT LIBE LUNCH
## 
### 
The Library Club will hold an in
formal supper party at 6:30 o'clock this
evening in the Library gardens. Wil
liam Owens is in charge of arrange
ments. The supper will consist of
wienies, salad, rolls, watermelon, and
ice cream, and it is not expected to
last more than an hour. All members
of the library staff are invited and it
is hoped that a group of at least 50
will attend.
