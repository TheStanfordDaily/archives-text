# GLEDHILL AND VINES DEFEAT DOEG, COEN
## 
### 
Keith Gledhill, Stanford's intercolle
giate tennis champion, and Ellsworth
Vines of Southern California, added
another title to their rapidly growing
list by capturing the historic Longwood
Bowl doubles championship at Brook
line, Massachusetts, Saturday. Four sets
were necessary to down Johnny Doeg
and Wilbur F. Coen, 3-6, 6-1, 6-3, 6-3.
Ellsworth Vines won the singles title
by defeating Doeg in a fast five-set
match, 4-6, 6-3, 6-3, 3-6, 6-3. Vines won
46 out of the last 48 matches he played
in tournaments.
