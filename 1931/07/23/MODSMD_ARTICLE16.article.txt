# SORORITY LUNCH TO BE SERVED TUESDAY
## 
### 
Members of the international P.E.O.
sisterhood chapter in Palo Alto are to
be hostesses to those of their organiza
tion who are visiting here or are un
affiliated with the local chapter at a
12 o'clock luncheon Tuesday in the
Union dining room.
The sorority is a social and educa
tional group which provides a number
of scholarships and a general fund to
help women students.
Reservations for the luncheon may
be made with Mrs. J. E. Springer of
I Palo Alto.
