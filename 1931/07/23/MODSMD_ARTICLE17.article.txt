# McFARLAND-OHNHAUS BETROTHAL REVEALED
## 
### 
The engagement of Anne Ohnhaus,
'31, to Joseph Q. McFarland, '31, was
announced recently. This engagement is
the growth of a campus romance which
started when McFarland met Miss Ohn
haus at a Jolly-up last fall.
McFarland is a member ot Alpha
Sigma Phi fraternity, and while at
Stanford was closely connected with
campus publications. At present ho is
connected with a publicity organization
in Santa Barbara, where he makes his
home. Miss Ohnhaus. a member of Pi
Beta Phi sorority, is registered in
school for the Summer Quarter. Her
home is in Cheyenne, Wyoming.
The date for the marriage has not
vet been announced.
