# ANDERSON CONTINUES EDUCATIONAL SURVEY
## 
### 
Word has been received here that
Mr. and Mrs. H. D. Anderson of the
School of Education are en route to
the Pribiloff Islands from Dutch Har
bor, Alaska, on the Coast Guard cutler
Itska.
Anderson is starting his second year's
survey of native educational problems
for the United States Bureau of Educa
tion in co-operation with Dr. Henry
Suzallo of the Carnegie Institution. Dr.
Suzallo is also a Stanford alumnus.
The survey will cover the health and
ability of the native children, the pres
ent educational methods, and the de
sirability of continuing the system now
in use.
