# STANFORD SORORITY ENTERTAINS VISITORS
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the P.E.0.,
international social and educational
sorority, will give a luncheon today at
12 o'clock in the Union dining room for
visiting P.E.O.'s. From 35 to 40 mem
bers are expected to attend.

Entertainment will consist of a musi
cal program which will include a group
of violin solos by James H. C. Smith
and numbers by the Nanon Instru
mental Trio.

After luncheon the guests will ad
journ to Mrs. A. F. Rogers' home to
play bridge.
