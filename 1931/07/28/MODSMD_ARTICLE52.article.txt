# MILITARY STUDENTS RETURN FROM CAMP
## 
### 
The summer R.O.T.C. camps at Mon
terey ended Saturday and Stanford's
unit is once more on home soil. It was
the only Field Artillery unit at the
summer camp. Infantry bodies wore
represented by California, U.C.L.A.,
Nevada, and Pomona.

Approximately fifty Stanford men at
tended the camp and were under the
instruction of the local captains, E. C.
Williams, S. F. Miller, and R. A. Gor
don, who directed them during the six
weeks period.

Men who returned to the campus re
port they enjoyed attending the camp
in spite of being turned out of bed at
5:30 o'clock. They said they missed
sleeping through an eight o'clock class.
