# ROBLE TEA WELL ATTENDED
## 
### 
Students and faculty gathered around
a cheerful fire at Roble Tuesday after
noon where the weekly "Roble at Heme
Tea" was served. The physical science
department faculty were special guests
of the afternoon and a large crowd at
tended. Hilda Haven was hostess of the
occasion with Catharine Cartlidge act
ing as assistant.
