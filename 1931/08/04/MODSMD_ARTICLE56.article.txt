# RUSSIAN POETRY TO BE READ THURSDAY
## 
### 
A "soiree" of Russian poetry and
I song will be presented in the Little
I Theatre on Thursday evening at 8
o'clock. The entertainment is open to
the public.
An introductory talk will be given
by Professor Henry Lanz of the Slavic-
Language Department. Professor Henri
Gregoire will recite several poems
which he has translated into French,
and Mrs. Hugh Brown of Palo Alto
will present several recitations.
Music will be furnished by Mrs.
Anita O. Barrett, violinist, with Grace
Corinna Sharp at the piano; Nina Ver
nadsky, soprano, Philip T. McLoan,
tenor, and Claude A. Shull, baritone.
