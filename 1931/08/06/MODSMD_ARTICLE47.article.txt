# LAST JOLLYUP ON AUGUST 13
## 
### 
The last Jollyup of the quarter will
be given at Ruble Hall from 7 to 8
o'clock on August 13. It was previously
announced it would be held on August
18. George Starbird's orchestra has
been engaged for the dance.
