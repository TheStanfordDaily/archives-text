# ENGLISH CLUB MEETS TONITE
## 
### 
The English Club will have a dinner
meeting at 6 o'clock tonight in the al
cove of the Union. The English trans
lation of Moliere's play "Les Precieuses
Ridicules" will be read and Professor
Jean-Albert Bede of the French de
partment will give an informal talk on
Moliere's life and work.
