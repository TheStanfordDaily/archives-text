# FINAL JOLLY-UP AUGUST 20
## 
### 
The last Jolly-Up of the quarter will
' be given at Roble "Hall from 7 to 8
o'clock on August 20. It was previously
announced that the Jolly-Up would be
' held on August 13, which date was can
celled because of final examinations be
ing given by the Education depart
ment. George Starbird's orchestra has
| been engaged for the dance.
