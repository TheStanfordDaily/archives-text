# FLEISHHACKER WINS TAHOE CHAMPIONSHIP
## 
### 
Herbert Fleishhacker, Jr., former
Stanford gridiron star, won the Lake
Tahoe championship in the finals of
the power boat regatta on a seven-mile
course last Sunday with his '"Maybe
I Not." He captured all three of the sea
son's races to take the Shield perpet
ual trophy.
His time was nine minutes and six
seconds, which was two minutes better
than the time of his nearest competi
tor, Stanley Dollar, Jr.
