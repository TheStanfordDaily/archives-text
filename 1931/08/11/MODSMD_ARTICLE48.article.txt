# GLEDHILL, VINES WIN MEADOW CLUB TITLE
## 
### 
Keith Gledhill, of Stanford, and his
net partner, Ellsworth Vines, of Pasa
dena, added another title to their long
list of victories when they captured
the doubles championship of the
Meadow Club at Southampton, New
York, last Saturday. Their victory was
gained at the expense of Gregory S.
Mangin of Newark, New Jersey and
Berkeley Bell of New York? The score
was: 17-15, 6-2, 6-4.
George M. Lott, Jr., of Philadelphia
won the singles title by defeating Cliff
Sutter, ot New Orleans, 6-3, 3-6, 2-6,
6-3, 6-1.
