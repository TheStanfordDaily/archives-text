# DEAN MARY YOST GOES TO DENVER FOR VISIT
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost left the campus Sat
urday morning for Denver, Colorado,
to visit friends until the last week in
August. She will be on the campus for
a week during the first part of Septem
ber, and will leave again until the first
of October, though her plans for Sep
tember are not known.

During Miss Yost's absence the of
fice routine is being carried on by
Huneke, who is keeping office
hours from 9:30 o'clock in the morn
ing until noon.

Alice Ingraham, Stanford, '30, who
is to take the place of Helen Huneke
as secretary to Dean Yost in Octo
ber, has arrived on the campus.
