# HARPER PRIZE WINNER GETS AWARD
## 
### 

Robert Raynolds, 2!), of Georgetown, Conn., winner of the $10,000 Harper
Prize, Novel Contest, receiving the lirst copy of his novel. "Brothers in the
Wesl." from Eugene F. Saxton, editor in chief of Harper and Brothers. Although
Mr. Raynolds has been writing for fourteen years, the prize-winning novel is
his first published work. He is married and has two children. "Brothers in the
West" was published on August 17. -
