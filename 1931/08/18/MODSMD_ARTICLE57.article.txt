# GLADYS M. McLEOD BETROTHAL ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The engagement of Miss Gladys Ma
rion McLcod to Anthony James Ken
nedy was announced at a tea given
Saturday afternoon at the Woman's
Athletic Club in San Francisco.
Miss McLeod is a daughter of Mr.
and Mrs. William Francis McLeod of
San Francisco and is a graduate of
Stanford. Kennedy is a graduate of
the University of California and the
University of California law school and
lives in Sacramento.
No wedding plans have been an
nounced.
