# LENIENCY PETITIONS DUE IN REGISTRAR'S OFFICE AUGUST 29
## 
### 
Students whose work has been
interfered with during the current
qua Her by illness, or other causes
for which allowance can be made
by the Committee on Scholarship,
may report the special circum
stances to Mrs. C. A. Huston, sec
retary of the committee, at the
Registrar's office, it is announced.
Excuses should be submitted
not later than August 29 in order
to be assured of consideration be
fore action is taken on the end
quarter reports.
Requests for allowance on ac
count of illness must be supple
mented by the special physician's
certificate furnished by the com
mittee.
