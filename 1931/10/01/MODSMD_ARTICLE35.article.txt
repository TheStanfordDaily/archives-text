# PRESIDENT SWAIN TO MEET NEW STUDENTS
## 
### 
The annual receptions for faculty
members and for new students in
the University will be given by
President and Mrs. Robert E. Swain
tomorrow and Saturday evenings
The faculty will be entertained to
morrow from 8:30 p.m. to 11 p.m.
New students will gather at the
President's home the following
night at 8 p.m. and will enjoy an
evening of dancing.
