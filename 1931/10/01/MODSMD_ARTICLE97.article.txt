# WILBUR HAILS L.A. AQUEDUCT PROJECT
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Sept. 30
Secretary Wilbur said today the
voting of a $220,000,000 bond issue
to build an aqueduct to carry Colo
rado river water into the Los An
geles section was "good news."
The five-to-one vote, he said, was
a demonstration of "a complete and
justified confidence in the financial
future of the West."
The aqueduct will run from Hoo
ver Dam to the metropolitan area of
I Los Angeles, which includes fifteen
cities. It will be 226 miles long and
