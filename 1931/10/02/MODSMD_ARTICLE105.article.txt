# PREPARE PLANS FOR MONTERREY CASINO
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Oct. 1 (/P)—Plans
J for building a casino and tourist
| resort at Monterrey, modeled after
! the resort at Agua Caliente, were
j being prepared today by James M.
i Crofton, president of the Agua
Caliente Jockey Club.
1 Work on a 200-room hotel, Casino
and golf course will be started
within ten days at Topo Chico, just
outside Monterrey, it was announced.
The hotel will be one of a string
that Mr. Crofton plans to build in
; Mexico at an expenditure of several
1 million dollars.
