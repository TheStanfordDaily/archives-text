# 2500 ENROLLED AT SAN JOSE COLLEGE
## 
### 
SAN JOSE, Oct. I—San1 —San Jose State
College has the largest enrollment
of any state college in California
with 2,500 students, a report from
the office of Vierling Kersey, state
superintendent of public instruction,
reveals. San Francisco State Col
lege is second with 1,297.
The latter led all other state col
leges in growth during the year, en
rollment increasing 27 per cent for
the fall semester of 1931. San Jose
led in numerical increases, 380 more.
