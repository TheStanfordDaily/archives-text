# IL DUCE IS GRANDAD
## 
### 
SHANGHAI, China, Oct. 1 {/¥).
Premier Mussolini of Italy became a
grandfather today when a son was
born to his daughter, Countess Edda
Clano, and Count Galeazzo Ciano,
Italian Consul General here.
