# TITLE ANNOUNCED FOR FIRST PLAY
## Tryouts To Be Held Monday Night At 7 o'Clock 
### 
"Berkeley Square" is the title of
the first play to be presented this
season, according to Harold Helven
ston, director of dramatics. October
.>0 is the date set for the production.
John L. Balderston is the author
of this most entertaining play in
three acts. "Berkeley Square" re
cently ran for two hundred nights in
London, and the London Times
comments on the fact that this is
the first instance on record of an
American play by an American au
thor enjoying a long London run
before being produced in New York.
Tryouts will be held Monday eve
ning at 7 o'clock in the Assembly
Hall. Everyone is eligible except
freshman women.
