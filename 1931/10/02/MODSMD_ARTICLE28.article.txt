# REGISTRATION DANCE OPENS FALL SEASON
## 
### 
The social season was opened last
night with the Registration Dance
held in the women's gym under the
auspices of the professional journal
istic society, Sigma Delta Chi.
Music was provided by Craig's
Hotel Sainte Claire orchestra for
this first Registration Dance to be
held in the new building. Formerly
this traditional dance has taken
place in Branner Hall.'
