# NEW CO-EDS TO BE ENTERTAINED SUNDAY
## 
### 
A group of women residents of"
Palo Alto, including members of the
Y.W.C.A. advisory board and wives
of some of the Stanford professors
will act as hostesses at dinner par
ties being given at their homes for
the new women students Sunday
evening.
The new women will be enter
tained in groups of six. Assignments
have been made in alphabetical
order. The hostesses will be aided
by members of the cabinet of the
Stanford Y.W.C.A. which is sponsor
ing the dinners to give the new
women an opportunity to become
acquainted with some older persons
in Palo Alto who may act as older
sponsors for them.
