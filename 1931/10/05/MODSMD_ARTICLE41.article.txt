# SOCCER MEN IN FIRST PRACTICE
## 
### 
In the first practice of the year', j
Harry Maloney's soccer I squad
turned out G3 strong on Friday when
they were given a short workout ,
finished off by a snappy scrimmago
between three teams, each team i
playing two periods.
With a great many positions to be 1
decided on this year, Maloney urges
anyone who is interested in soccer
to sign up for the sport. The fresh
man squad could use a good many
more men who would have an ex
cellent chance of making the varsity
later on.
