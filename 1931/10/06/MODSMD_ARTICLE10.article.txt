# BOMB PLOT PERILS CAMPUS GARDENER IN FEUD FLARE-UP
## 
### 
Charged with placing a bomb in
the automobile of Sam Teluccis,
Stanford University gardener,
George Kebeles of Mountain View
today is in the Santa Clara jail at
San Jose.
He will be held to answer before
the Superior Court-. He was unable
to furnish $1,000 bail, following his
arrest by Constable Fred Jarvis last
week.
Kebeles, it is charged, placed a
high explosive bomb in the rear of
Teluccis' automobile while the ve
hicle was standing in the garage. It
was discovered before any damage
was done.
A grudge against Teluccis is
thought to have been the motive for
the act, Jarvis said.
