# ROOTERS' TICKETS SALE TO CONTINUE FOR GOPHER GAME
## 
### 
Sale of rooters' tickets for the
Minnesota game Saturday will con
tinue today and tomorrow, October
(i and 7, at the A.S.S.U. office in the
Old Union from 9 to 12 and from
1 to 5 o'clock.
Personal presentation of a student
body card and $1 must be made
before Wednesday evening in order
to secure a place in the rooters'
section.
Student tickets for the Southern
California game will be sold on
October 12, 13, and 14. Big Game
tickets go on sale October 19 : 20,
and 21, at $1.50.
