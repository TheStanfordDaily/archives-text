# GLEE CLUB TRYOUTS FOR FROSH TONIGHT
## 
### 
Men's Glee Club tryouts for
freshmen only will be held at 9
o'clock tonight in the women's old
gym, it was announced by Ralph
Wallace, president. Men with spe
cialty entertainments numbers are
particularly desired, he said.
The glee club quartet has been
newly organized, with Ralph Wal
lace as first tenor, Bob Stoddard,
second tenor, Everett Baker, bari
tone, and John Huneke, bass.
The club already has signed for
several engagements, Wallace said,
the first to be early in November.
