# A.K.L.'S Again Lead Fraternities In Residence Scholarship Rating
## General Average of C Is Same as in Previous Years; Two Grade Points for One in New System; Phi Lambda Upsilon Leads Phi Betes 
### 
THOUGH indications point to the average scholastic standing of last year
equaling that of ihe year before, definite figures can not be given
owing to the change in grade point computations. The relative standing
of the groups for the past two years is shown below.
Under "General Classification" fraternities rank 1 while in the classifica
tion "by Residence" it is 2. Under the new system of rating, a 1.00, or C,
average of last year equals a 2.00 average this year. The first'grouping
includes all fraternity men whether living in fraternity houses or not.
Autumn quarter rating, as usual, is generally lower than the other quar
ters, as weak students are weeded out at the end of this quarter.
Alpha Kappa Lambda heads the list of fraternities for the sixth con
secutive year. Phi Lambda Upsilon, which leads Phi Beta Kappa among
the honor societies, has many Phi Beta Kappas on their own membership
roll.
