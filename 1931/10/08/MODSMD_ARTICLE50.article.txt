# WRESTLING MATERIAL FORMED INTO CLASS
## 
### 
Up to last night 14 candidates had
signed up with Coach Ted Hill for
wrestling this fall, among whom
some very good material was avail
able, according to the instructor.
At the present time a class has
been formed giving the members
instruction in special holds. As the
season develops an all-University
novice tournament will be held. The
class meets Monday, Wednesday,
and Friday from 4 to 6 o'clock and
Coach Hi 11 is in the gym for special
aid everv day at that time.
