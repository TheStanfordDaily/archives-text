# MEN'S INTRAMURAL HANDBALL TOURNEY TO BE THIS MONTH
## 
### 
Entries are now open for the an
nual intramural men's handball
tournament, which will begin on the
Encina gym courts October 19.
Coach Myron Sprague said yester
day. All living groups and inde
pendent organizations may enter
teams, but no men on the varsity
team or who have played in a
California series will be eligible.
The teams must play three match
es, one doubles and two singles, in
each competition. Gold medals will
be awarded the winners, and silver
medals will console the runners-up.
Two, three, or four men may be
used to make up a team.
