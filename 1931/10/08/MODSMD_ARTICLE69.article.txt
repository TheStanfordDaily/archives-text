# QUAD CAMERA MEN WILL MEET TODAY TO DISCUSS PLANS
## 
### 
A general call for student pho
tographers for this year's Quad was
issued yesterday by Tom Aitken,
editor of the 1932 yearbook. All in
terested in taking photographs for
the Annual are asked to meet in the
Quad editorial office in the Press
Building at 1:30 o'clock this after
noon.
Plans will be formulated for the
photography for the year. Pictures
to be taken by the student pho
tographers are informal snaps,
mainly for the life and athletic sec
tions, stated Aitken.
