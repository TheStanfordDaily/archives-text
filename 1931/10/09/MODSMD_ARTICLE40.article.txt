# JAIL BIRDS JEER AS AMOS 'N ANDY HOUR IS CHANGED
## 
### 
LEAVENWORTH, Kan., Oct. 8.
(A 3 ) —On top of all their other trou
bles, Anions 'n Andy were blamed
by authorities of the Federal peni
tentiary here for a disturbance
among the convicts which ceased
early today after jeering, catcalling,
;>nd the smashing of window lights.
Abandonment of daylight saving
time in the East was a teammate
of the pair in causing the disorder,
the authorities said.
Heretofore the prisoners have
been permitted to follow the vicissi
tudes of the proprietors of the
Fresh Air Taxi Company," which
began at 9, central standard time.
However, the abandonment of
daylight saving time, the program
does not go on the air until 10
p.m., 45 minutes after the radio
must be shut off according to rules.
