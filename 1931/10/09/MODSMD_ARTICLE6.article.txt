# AUTUMN INFORMAL AT ROBLE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Roble Club will hold an informal
dance this evening from 8:30 to 1
o'clock.
Patrons and patronesses for the
affair will be Comptroller and Mrs.
Almon E. Roth, Dr. and Mrs.
Thomas A. Storey, Dean Mary Yost,
Miss Christine Ricker, Miss Anasta
sia Doyle, and Professor Boggs.
Decorations will follow an autumn
motif. Margaret Abrams and Ruth
Kofal are in charge of the dance.
