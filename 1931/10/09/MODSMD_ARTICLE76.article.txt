# JAPANESE PROFESSOR SEES FOSSIL EXHIBIT
## 
### 
/ Teiichi Kobayashi, professor of
paleontology at the Imperial Uni
versity of Tokio, was a visitor in
the geology department yesterday.
The purpose of his visit was pri
marily to view the collection of
Mesozoic fossil shells made by the
late Professor J. P. Smith. This col
lection is the best of its kind in the
United States.

Professor Kobayashi, accompanied
by his wife, is on his way to Wash
ington to study in the National Mu
seum. Later he will go on to Ger
many for further study.
