# FACULTY WOMEN SET FIRST MEETING DATE
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Faculty
Women's Club will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse on Monday af
ternoon at 3 o'clock.
All members are urgently re
quested to be present at this first
meeting at which Mrs. L. L. Bur
lingame, the new president, will
preside, and at which new business
will be discussed.
