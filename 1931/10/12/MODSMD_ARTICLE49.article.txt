# ENGLISH CLUB WILL HOLD OPEN MEETING
## 
### 
The English Club will hold its
first open meeting of the quarter
Wednesday night at 8 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. All students
interested in the activities of the
club are invited.
"The Man with the Flower in His
Mouth," a one-act play, will be pre
sented, and the prospectus of a new
intercollegiate magazine will bo dis
cussed.
