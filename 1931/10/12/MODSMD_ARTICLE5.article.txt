# LAW SMOKER TO BE HELD TOMORROW IN OLD UNION LOBBY
## 
### 
The annual Law Association
Smoker will be held tomorrow night
at 8 o'clock in the lobby of the Old
Union.
George Presley will be the prin
cipal speaker of the evening. Pres
ley is the Executive Secretary of
the San Francisco Chamber of Com
merce, attorney for the Stanford
Board of Trustees of which he was
formerly a member, and in the past
was a Stanford football coach.
James C. Ingebretson, president
oi the Association, will preside at
the smoker, and Arnold Rumwell is
in charge of arrangements.
All members of the Law Associa
tion are invited to attend.
