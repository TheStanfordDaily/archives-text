# GERMAN HONOR CLUB TO MEET THURSDAY
## 
### 
The first meeting of the German
Honor Society, which is composed
of advanced students, faculty mem
bers, and a few guests of honor,
will be held Thursday afternoon at
4 o'clock at the home of Dr. and
Mrs. K. F. Reinhardt, 712 Salvatierra
street. Dr. Reinhardt is acting ex
ecutive head of the Department of
Germanic Languages.
On the program will be the elec
tion of officers, varied musical en
tertainment including group singing,
a brief travelogue, and the sewing
of refreshments.
