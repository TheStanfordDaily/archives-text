# ICHIHASHI TO SPEAK ON RECENT TRENDS IN MODERN JAPAN
## 
### 
Professor Ichihashi of the history
department will be the speaker of
the evening at the meeting of the
International Relations Club to
held in the West Memorial Library
Thursday evening at 7:.'50 o'clock,
Joseph Nutt, president of the or
ganization, announced today.
Professor Ichihashi will have as
his topic: "The Social and Political
Trends in Japan Today." He will
discuss particularly the more recent
and radical movements, especially
as ihey affect the university student.
The meeting will be open to all
those interested in political science
and foreign relations.
