# FIRST JOLLY-UP SET FOR ROBLE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Roble lobby will be the setting of
the first jolly-up of the quarter to
night from 7 to 8 o'clock.
Margaret Abrams and Dorothy
Wehe are in charge of arrange
ments. A campus orchestra will play
for the dance. Hostesses will be the
Roble sponsors and Jane Desenberg.
