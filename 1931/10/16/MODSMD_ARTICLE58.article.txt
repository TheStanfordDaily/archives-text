# HANDBALL ENTRANTS MUST SIGN UP TODAY
## 
### 
According to Coach Charles
Sprague, in charge of the event, all
entries for the intramural one-wall
handball championship tournament
must be in by 6 o'clock this after
noon. Drawings will be posted Mon
day, and the first round must be
played by the end of next week.
Varsity lettermen or men in the
California series are ineligible for
competition. Gold medals will be
awarded to the members of the
winning squad, and silver medals to
the runner-up.
