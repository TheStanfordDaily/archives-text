# Y.W.C.A. WILL GIVE TEA FOR DRAMATICS DEPARTMENT TODAY
## 
### 
A tea honoring the dramatics de
partment is being given under the
auspices of the Y.W.C.A. from 4 to
6 o'clock this afternoon in the "Y"
rooms on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. All students,
both men and women, interested in
dramatics, are invited.
This is the first in the series of
activity and departmental teas
sponsored by the Y.W.C.A. each
year to help new students to be
come acquainted with the leaders
in campus activities and to encour
age their own active participation in
these extra-curricular activities.
Members of the various dramatic
societies, Ram's Head, Masquers,
Sword and Sandals, and the cast of
"Berkeley Square," to be given on
the campus soon, have been espe
cially invited, so that the new stu
dents may meet those who have al
ready become outstanding in Stan
ford dramatics.
Hostesses at the tea will be Marion
Jones and Therina Guerard, assisted
by the members of the Y.W.C.A.
cabinet.
