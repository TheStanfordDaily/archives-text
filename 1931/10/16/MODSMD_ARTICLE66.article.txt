# STANFORD CHRISTIAN ASSOCIATION MEETS
## 
### 
Gilbert Wrenn, chairman of the
vocational guidance committee of
the Stanford Christian Association,
will lead an informal discussion on
"College and the Job" Sunday eve
ning at 7 o'clock in the Encina
lobby. Everyone interested is in
vited.

This program is the first of a
series being arranged by a new
student committee of the Stanford
Christian Association, which was
formerly known as the Stanford
Y.M.C.A. This committee consists
of Don Erskine, chairman, Bob
Dickinson, and Ed Fisher.
