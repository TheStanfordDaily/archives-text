# STANFORD FENCERS QUALIFY FOR FINALS IN S.F. TOURNAMENT
## 
### 
Competing in the Bay Division
Handicap Foils Tournament at the
Olympic Club last Friday night,
Fred Pearman and Alfred Snyder
of the Stanford varsity team quali
fied easily for the finals, losing only
one match between them.
Pearman, who is president of the
Stanford Fencing Society, had a
handicap of minus one, but won all
of his matches to place first on his
strip. Snyder, last year's frosh star,
fought at plus zero, and dropped
only one contest to place second on
his strip.
