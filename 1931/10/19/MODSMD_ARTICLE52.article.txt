# FIRST OF WOMEN'S RACQUET TOURNEY MATCHES SETTLED
## 
### 
The first play-offs to select the
three best freshman women tennis
players for the triangular sports day
to be held October 21, were finished
during the week-end. The three
chosen players will represent the
Stanford freshmen in the meet with
the University of California women
and Mills College.
The results so far are: Mabel
Overton d. Joyce Campion, 6-1, 6-3;
Katherine Woodward d. Mary
Rechif, 6-1, 6-0, and Mabel Over
lon, 6-2, 6-4; Marjorie Buettner d.
Eleanor Cross, 6-3, 6-2; Marcia Cass
d. Edwine Montague, 6-1, 9-7.
