# CHINESE RELIEF
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 18 (A>).—
The Chinese Famine Relief Commit
tee of California announced receipt
yesterday of a donation of 50 tons
of canned pineapple from the Dole
Hawaiian Pineapple Company. The
fruit will be shipped to China as
soon as possible.
