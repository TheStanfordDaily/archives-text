# SWAIN, YOST WILL ADDRESS MOTHERS' CLUB MEET TODAY
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Mothers
Club will hold their lirst meeting
this quarter at 2:30 o'clock this af
ternoon in the Women's Clubhouse.
Acting-President Robert E. Swain
will extend a welcome to the or
ganization, and Dean Mary Yost will
speak on the relation between the
club and the students.
Committees will then be selected
for the annual "Chrysanthemum
Tea" to be held November 4 at
Mrs. Herbert Hoover's home on San
Juan Hill. General arrangements for
the tea are in Ihe hands of Mrs. Al
mon E. Roth. Mrs. Frederick Hall
Fowler, Mrs. Payson J. Treat, and
Mrs. Jean Henry Large, sister of
Mrs. Hoover. Mrs. T. A. Storey,
president of the Mothers Club, and
others on the board, are assisting.
