# WOMEN'S DEBATING TRYOUTEES TO TALK ON SOCIAL PROBLEM
## 
### 
Tryouts for the women's debating
squad will be held at 8 o'clock to
night in the Women's Clubhouse,
Marie Baker, debate manager, an
nounces.
The topic for the tryout will be
the subject chosen for the meet with
the University of California: "Re
solved, that the care of children
should be the sole basis for granting
of alimony." The tryout will con
sist of a four-minute speech and a
three-minute rebuttal.
All undergraduate women are
eligible and are urged by Marie
Baker to try out. Activities of the
season will include a tour of South
ern California colleges and a debate
with the men of the University of
Nevada.
