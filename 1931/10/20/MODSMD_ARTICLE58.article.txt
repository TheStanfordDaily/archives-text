# DYMENT ANNOUNCES SCHEDULE OF HOURS FOR CONSULTATIONS
## 
### 
In order that the routine of the
office may not be interrupted by
queries, Dr. Bertha Dyment, wom
en's medical adviser, requests that
all students desiring to see her, con
fine themselves to the hours listed
below:
Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday,
Friday, from 10 to. 12 o'clock in the
morning, and 3 to 4:15 o'clock in
the afternoon. There will be no
hours held on Thursday, and the
office will be open from 10 to 12
o'clock in the morning on Saturdays.
The nurse will be in attendance
at the office daily except on Satur
day from 8 to 12 o'clock and 2:15
to 5:15 o'clock.
