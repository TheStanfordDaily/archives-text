# GRADS TO BE GUESTS OF PI LAMBDA THETA
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, women's na
tional honor education fraternity,
will entertain new graduate women
as honor guests at a tea Friday
afternoon between 4 and 5:30 o'clock
Hildegarde Kaufteld is in charge
of the tea, which will be given at
Dean Mary Yost's home.
The hard times of the middle
nineties modified, but did not stop,
the growth of the university.
