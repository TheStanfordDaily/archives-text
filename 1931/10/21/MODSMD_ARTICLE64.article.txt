# PROFESSORS' HOMES THREATENED BY FIRE
## 
### 
Summer homes of Professor W. B.
Owens and Lewis M. Terman, which
are situated in the Alpine district
behind Redwood City, were threat
ened by a serious brush fire Mon
day.
The fire began just after noon and
blazed for several hours until the
San Mateo County fire warden's
forces, assisted by volunteers, suc
ceeded in quenching it.
