# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
12 noon—French Table will meet
for lunch in the Union dining
room. All students are invited.
1:15 p.m.—Members of Ram's Head
will meet in the Green Room to
discuss plans for the Big Game
Gaieties.
1:30 p.m.—A meeting of the varsity
tennis men is scheduled for today
on the tennis courts from 1:30
until 5 o'clock.
2:30 p.m.—Meeting of the reading
group of the Stanford Dames at
the home of Mrs. Ellwood Cub
berley, 610 Cabrillo, Campus. All
members of the organization in
vited.
7:15 p.m.—Tryoutee meeting for the
Concert Series in the old Concert
Scries office at the Union.
8 p.m.—Daily tryoutee meeting will
be held Thursday evening at 8
o'clock instead of Wednesday eve
ning, as previously announced.
7:30 p.m.—The Christian Science
Society will meet on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
FRIDAY
8 p.m.-—Cosmopolitan Club is hold
ing a party in the Old Union. All
persons interested arc cordially
invited.
