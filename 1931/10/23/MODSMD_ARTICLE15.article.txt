# HECHE'S ORCHESTRA AT TOYON TONIGHT
## 
### 
The first hall dance of the year, the
Toyon Informal, will be held tonight
from 9 to 1 o'clock. The music will
be by Gordon Heche's seven-piece
orchestra which has just closed a
summer engagement at a resort on
the Russian River. Members of the
orchestra will furnish entertainment
during intermissions. Special light
ing effects by Joe Gordon will be
featured.
Bids, priced at $2.50, and programs
may be secured at the A.S.S.U. of
fice in the Old Union or from Bots
Parker, room 235 Toyon Hall.
