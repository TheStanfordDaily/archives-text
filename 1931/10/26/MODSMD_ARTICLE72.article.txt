# FELLOWSHIP BOARD TO ELECT OFFICERS
## 
### 
Reorganization of the Fellowship
Board will be the purpose of a meet
ing to be held In the Women's Club
house tomorrow night at 7:15 o'-
clock, according to Robert F. Jones,
president of the board.
Representatives from each living
group are expected to be present at
the meeting Tuesday at which oew
officers will be elected to replace
Catherine Guerard, secretary, and
Ann Adams, treasurer, who have
resigned.
Plans for continuing the acquaint
ance visits of students to
professors' homes on Sunday eve
nings will be discussed.
