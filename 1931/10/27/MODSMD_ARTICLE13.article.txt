# LIFE SAVING CORPS MEETS WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
The first meeting of the proposed
Stanford Life Saving Corps will be
held at the Encina pool at 5 o'clock
Wednesday afternoon. The group
will be a branch member of the
American Red Cross Life Saving
Corps and it will be open only to
senior members and examiners of
that, organization.
The club will bo under the direc
tion of Charles Sprague, associate
director of physical education. Its
object is to further the theory and
technique of life saving.
