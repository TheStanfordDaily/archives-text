# FIRST TRYOUTS HELD IN BIG GAME GAIETIES
## 
### 
Preliminary tryouts for the an
nual Ram's Head Football Gaieties
were held yesterday afternoon in
the Little Theatre. With the next
official tryouts scheduled for Tues
day, November 3, and so allowing
another week for preparation, skits
and song lyrics should be in finished
form for the second tryouts.

Several promising songs and skits
were submitted as well as some
dance acts. All songs and lyrics
should be given to Roger Sumner
as soon as possible. Aspirants de
sirous of turning in special acts
should see Harold Helvenston, who
is in charge of the entire /produc
