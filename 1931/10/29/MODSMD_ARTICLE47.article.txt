# BEST BAND IN CARD HISTORY, ACCORDING TO MANAGER COLES
## 
### 
Several former soloists have re
turned to the Stanford Band after a
summer spent in professional play
ing. Ed Coles, band manager, says
the number of experienced players
this year cheers him greatly, and
he thinks he has the best material
in Stanford Band history.
When tryouts were over, 112 men
were chosen for the band, 104 ofl
whom are regular players, while
eight others serve as supernumer
aries. These act as symbolic char
acters at football games, represent
ing Indians, Bears, and Trojans, but
play when occasion demands.
Byron Snow, '26, has been chosen
associate director of the band this
year.
