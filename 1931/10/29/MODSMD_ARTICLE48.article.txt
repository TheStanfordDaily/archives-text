# FAIRMONT HOTEL IS SCENE OF BIG GAME DINNER NOVEMBER 21
## 
### 
John E. McDowell, secretary of
the Alumni Association, yesterday
announced that the annual Big Game
dinner is to be held this year on the
evening of November 21 at the Fair
mont Hotel, San Francisco.
All Stanford students and alumni
are invited to attend. The dinner has
been held at the Fairmont for the
past twelve years. This year the
price has been reduced from $6 to $5.
Those desiring to attend should
register with Mr. McDowell at the
alumni office in the Administration
Building.
