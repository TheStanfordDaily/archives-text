# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
5:10 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen. Memorial Church.
4-6 p.m.—Mrs. Robert Eckles Swain
will be at home to members of the
faculty and their wives at 634
Mirada.
7:30 p.m.—Zoology Club will meet
at home of Dr. G. C. Price, 532
Alvat'ado. Dr. Albert Herre will
speak.
FRIDAY
3 p.m.—Polo manager tryouts. En
cina polo field.
8:30 p.m.—"Berkeley Square" pre
sented by Dramatic Council. As
sembly Hall. Tickets at 75c-$1.25.
7:20 p.m.—All ushers for "Berkeley
Square" will report to J. P.
Franklin at the Assembly Hall.
MISCELLANEOUS
Freshman and junior transfers
who have not yet secured their
A.S.S.U. handbooks may obtain them
by calling at the A.S.S.U. offices at
the Old Union.
Thursday is last day to file vac
cination certificates. Fine of $2 if
not filed on time.
