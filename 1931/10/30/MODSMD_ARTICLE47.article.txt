# LUCILLE DAY GUEST OF "Y" TEA TODAY
## 
### 
Lucille D;iy, who has had five
years of interesting experience in
work in Turkey, will be the guest
of honor at an international tea
being given under the auspices of
the Y.W.C.A. this afternoon from 4
to 6 o'clock in the "Y" rooms on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house. All students interested are
invited to attend.

Miss Day will discuss the oppor
tunities for Christian service abroad
and relate some of her experiences
there. Her work has included teach
ing in a kindergarten, conducting a
social center at Brousa, teaching
English to members of the national
club, and leading a summer "Y"
camp for girls at Istanbul.
