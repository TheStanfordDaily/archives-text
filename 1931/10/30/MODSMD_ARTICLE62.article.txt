# COACH SCHALL SAYS BOXING INTEREST GOOD
## 
### 
Rounding his proteges into shape
for the all-University novice boxing
tournament which is coming up
about a week after Thanksgiving
vacation. Coach Myron Schall said
last night that his material is shap
ing up very well in daily workouts
from 4 to G o'clock.
Judging from past tournaments,
Coach Schall stated that from 4 to
8 contestants are expected to enroll
in each of the 7 weight divisions of
the affair. All students are now
working hard and many are receiv
ing special instruction from the
coach in various features of the
sport.
Coach Schall announced that men
wishing to improve in any particu
lar way should see him for special
attention.
