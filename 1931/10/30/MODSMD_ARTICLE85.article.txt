# A.F. OF L. PRESIDENT URGES SIX-HOUR DAY
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 29. —
Adoption of the six-hour day was
urged by President William Green
of the American Federation of La
bor, in a statement today reporting
a further increase of unemployment
among trade unions during this
month.
INDEPENDENCE, Mo., Oct. 29.
(/P) —Edith Fern Wright, 11, died
here today, the fourth child fatally
burned in their efforts to start a
cookstove fire with kerosene last
night.
