# GERMAN SOCIETY TO MEET IN PALO ALTO
## 
### 
Members of the German Honor
Society are invited to attend the
regular meeting of the society at
the home of Miss Helena M. Nye,
2SI Addison street, Palo Alto, Wed
nesday afternoon at 4 o'clock.
Dr. Friedrich W. Strothmann will
present a resume of the Christmas
program which is to be given by
students and faculty members of
the German department. Vocal solos
will be given by Miss Claire Mit
chell and Robert T. Clark.
