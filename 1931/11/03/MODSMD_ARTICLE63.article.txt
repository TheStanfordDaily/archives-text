# WRESTLERS CALLED
## 
### 
Coach Ted Hill today issued a
special call to members of the frosh
football squad who are interested in
wrestling to enroll in that sport
upon the completion of their season
this week. Coach Hill intends to
form a special elementary class for
the yearlings enrolling in this ac
tivity in order that they may gradu
ally catch up with the other classes
formed at the beginning of the
quarter.
