# 'G' TO 'M' SENIORS FACE LENS TODAY FOR QUAD PHOTOS
## 
### 
"G" to '"M" seniors are listed
for Quad photographic sittings
at ihe Watson Studios campus
shop across the street from the
Daily office between 9 and 12
o'clock and 1 and 4:30 o'clock
today. Those from "N" to "Z"
are scheduled for tomorrow at
the same hours.
The only group picture set
for today is Toyon Club in the
Toyon Court at 12:45 o'clock.
Branner Club will be photo
graphed at 12:45 o'clock in the
Branner Court tomorrow. Both
the varsity and freshman soccer
squads will face the lens on the
soccer practice field at 4 o'clock
tomorrow.
Proofs are now ready for all
seniors who were photographed
last week. Quad officials urge
them to call for their proofs as
pictures cannot be put into the
Quad unless proofs are called
for and the picture chosen.
Seniors possessing Quad re
ceipts are asked to bring them
to the studio.
