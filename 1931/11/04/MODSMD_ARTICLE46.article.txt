# THIRD ROUND TO BE PLAYED THIS WEEK IN MURAL HANDBALL
## 
### 
The third round in the intramural
handball tournament must be played
and results recorded this week, it
was announced today. The follow
ing brackets are affected by this
statement: Breakers vs. Delta Up
silon, Kappa Alpha vs. El Tigre, in
dependent I vs. bye, Beta Theta Pi
vs. bye.
Results of last week's round are:
Independent I defeated El Campo
by default; Beta Theta Pi, Breakers,
Delta Upsilon, Kappa Alpha, and El
Tigre defeated Deleta Chi, Theta
Delt Chi, Rho Dammit Rho, Delta
Tau Delta, and El Toro, in that
order.
Teams desiring a referee may
choose one or see Myron Sprague,
who will get one or referee himself
if needed.
