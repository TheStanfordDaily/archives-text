sity Life Saving Corps at Encina
Pool. All senior members and ex
aminers are invited.

poned until next Wednesday.

orary scouting fraternity, will hold
open meeting in the Women's
Clubhouse. All Eagle Scouts in
vited.

Quad meets in the Press Building.

Professor Kyoji Suyehiro on "Vi
bration of Buildings in an Earth
quake," in the Little Theatre.

Faculty Club House. Papers by
Professors C. H. Danforth and H.
M. Clokie.
THURSDAY
5:10 p.m.—Organ recital in Memor
ial Church by Warren D. Allen,
University organist.

Little Theatre by Reginald G.
Fisher, head of the State Archaeo
logical Survey of New Mexico, on
"The Rio Grande, the Nile of

12 noon —Spanish Tabic in Union
dining room.
