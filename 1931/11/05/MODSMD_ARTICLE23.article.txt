# MEDICAL FRATERNITY WILL HOLD BANQUET
## 
### 
Omega Upsilon Phi, Stanford
medical school fraternity, will hold
its annual Big Game banquet Fri
day night, November 20, at their
house 011 Washington Boulevard in
San Francisco. Many alumni of
California. Oregon, and other west
ern states will he present, and a
prominent member of the Stanford
faculty is to be the speaker, ac
cording to plans announced by
Clyde Conger, Junior Master.
