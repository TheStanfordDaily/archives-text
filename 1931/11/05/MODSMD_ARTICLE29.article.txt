# SMOKE AND DON'T BE GRADUATED
## 
### 
Once again bluenoses and high-hats collaborate lo build
a hail 011 smoking by women. The latest offering on this
point comes from Michigan Slate Normal School, where
tlie authorities have firmly determined not to permit any
woman to he graduated who makes a hahit of smoking.
It' smoking is to he considered degrading to women and
conducive to lax morals, then it is equally so for men, yet
there are men held in greatest esteem and respect by all
with whom they associate who smoke and smoke freely on
;t variety of occasions or on no occasion at all.
The whole thing resolves itself into an effort 011 the
part of somebody or other to raise the matter of smoking
lo the dignity of an issue when common sense and accepted
standards indicate that no issue exists.
t he Daily longs for the lime when artificial agitation
(.n smoking will die out from lack of fuel.
