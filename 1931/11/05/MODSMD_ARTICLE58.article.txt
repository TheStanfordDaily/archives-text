# BOOK RECEIPTS WILL AID SCHOLARS FUND
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, women's honor
society, would appreciate any Stan
ford students, men or women, sign
ing their Bookstore receipts over to
Cap and Gown. All that is neces
sary is to sign Cap and Gown in
place of your own name.
Proceeds from the Bookstore re
ceipts will be used for the Cap and
Gown scholarship fund. Money is
also being raised by selling pictures
of David Starr Jordan in Dean
Yost's office, and by selling maps of
the campus at Roble.
