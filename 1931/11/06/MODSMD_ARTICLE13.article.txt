# AUTO NOISEMAKERS WILL BE PENALIZED
## 
### 
Excessively noisy cutouts, unnec
essary backfiring, and other "ver
boten" forms of noise produced by
automobiles are to be penalized
hereafter, Men's Council announced
after its regular weekly meeting
yesterday.
Speeding cost students $13 when
they appeared before the Council
yesterday, and parking violations
accounted for $15 more. One muf
fler violator was haled before the
Council, but he was discharged with
an admonition.
