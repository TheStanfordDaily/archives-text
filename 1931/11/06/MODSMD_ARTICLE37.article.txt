# BALCHEN U.S. CITIZEN
## 
### 
HACKENSACK, N.J., Nov. 5 (/P).
—Bernt Balchen, transatlantic flyer
and pilot for Admiral Byrd on the
flight to little America, -today be
came a citizen of the United States.
His wife, Sorlie, also was given her
citizenship.
