# CAP AND GOWN HOLDS PALO ALTO MEETING
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, women's honor
society, will meet this afternoon at
4:30 o'clock at the home of Mrs.
Rouveral in Palo Alto. Mrs. Rouveral
is a graduate of Stanford, and for
the past few years has been engaged
in writing scenarios in Hollywood.
One of her best-known scenarios is
"Dance, Fools, Dance," in which
Joan Crawford was starred.
There will be a short business
meeting followed by refreshments.
