# Seniors Have Final Chance For Photo's
## 
### 
Seniors will have their last op
portunity to sit fbr their Quad pho
tographs today and tomorrow in
front of the Daily office, between
the hours of 9 to 12 o'clock and 1 to
4:30 o'clock.
The schedule necessitates the ter
mination of senior sittings tomorrow
to make way for Roble and living
group pictures, starting Wednesday.
Various group pictures will prob
ably be taken during the rest of
the week. Full announcement will
appear tomorrow.
