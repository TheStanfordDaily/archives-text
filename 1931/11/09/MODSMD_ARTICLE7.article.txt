# EDUCATION GROUP HOLDS RECEPTION IN WOMEN'S CLUB
## 
### 
Dean Patterson Cubberley, head
of the School of Elucation, will be
the main speaker at the first meet
ing of the Education Club v/hen it
convenes tonight at 7:30 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. The
changes that have taken place in the
School and the changes that may be
expected in the next decade will be
the subject of Cubberley's talk.
A short business meeting to elect
officers for the current year will
precede the speaker while a musical
program will follow. The music will
be provided by Francis Michener,
tenor, and the Blackmarr trio. All
students enrolled in the school and
faculty members and their wives are
invited to attend the meeting to
night.
