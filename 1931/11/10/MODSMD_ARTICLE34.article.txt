# MAT COURSE OPEN TO FROSH GRIDDERS
## 
### 
There is still lime left for a few
more members of the frosh football
squad which was disbanded Friday
on the completion of its season to
sign up in the special elementary
class in wrestling being formed for
their benefit, Coach Ted Ilill an
nounced today. Any men interested
in this announcement may see Coach
Hill in Encina Gym after 4:20
o'clock.

In order to allow for the other
classes formed at the beginning of
the quarter, members of this class
will be given special instruction to
enable them to catch up with the
rest, Coach Hill said. The class will
meet three times a week from 4:20
to 6 o'clock.
