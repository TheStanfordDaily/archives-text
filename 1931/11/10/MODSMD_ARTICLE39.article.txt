# "THE ART TEACHER" IS NEW LEMOS TEXT
## 
### 
Pedro J. Lemos, director of the |
Stanford Museum and Art Gallery,
is the author of a new book for art
instruction entitled "The Art Teach
er," which illustrates the results
of art teaching with examples from
24 states and five foreign countries.
Lemos is also the author of "Ap
plied Art," the best Seller among
art texts for the past seven years.
His other publications are: "Art
Simplified," "Color Cement Handi
craft," and "Correlated Art Text
books," besides more than sixty art
instruction portfolios. He is also edi
tor of the School Arts Magazine,
which is used by art teachers in
America and England.
