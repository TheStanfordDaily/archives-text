# COXEY STAGES COMEBACK
## 
### 

Jacob S. Coxey, who 37 years ago led his famous army of unem
ployed on Washington, D. C., has won his latest battle. He was elected
mayor of Massilon, Ohio, on a piatform advocating municipal owner
ship of utilities and flotation of a bond issue to aid the city's unem
ployed. Here he is with his wife, Henrietta, and his son, David. He is
now 77 years old.
