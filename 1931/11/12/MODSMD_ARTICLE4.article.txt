# GRID STARS OF '94 GATHER FOR REUNION
## 
### 
Grid stars of Stanford's 1804 foot
ball team will convene this evening
at a reunion dinner given by Presi
dent Hoover in the White House.
Hoover was manager of the team.
Charles M. Fiekert, Los Angeles
attorney, will be the last to arrive,
having left San Francisco by plane
on Tuesday morning. Other mem
bers of the team have been arriving
in Washington for the past week.
