# RULES PLANNED FOR BIG GAME TRAFFIC
## 
### 
Plans for the regulation of the
heavy traffic on Big Game Saturday
were discussed by the traffic officers
of the vicinity at a luncheon meeting
held in the Union yesterday noon.
Walter Greer, State Superintend
ent of Highway Traffic, heard the
reports and requests from the sec
tions represented. Inspector Living
ston of Santa Clara county, Inspec
tor Read of San Mateo county, the
chiefs of police of the towns between
Palo Alto and San Francisco, and
several representatives from the
California Automobile Association
and the Southern Pacific were pres
ent at the meeting.
