# ENCINA COMMONS TO LUNCH ON SATURDAY
## 
### 
Encina Commons will serve
; luncheon on Saturday to all fresh
men. club members, and their
guests, it was announced yesterday
by Mrs. Hilda Longyear Watson,
director of the Commons. The charge
will be 50 cents per person.
The meal will be served buffet
style to freshmen and their guests
in Dining Room 8 from 11:45 to
12:30 o'clock. Club men and their
guests will be served in their re
, .spective halls.
