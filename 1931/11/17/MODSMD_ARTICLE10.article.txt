# EELL'S CLASS CALLS ON JUNIOR COLLEGE
## 
### 
Twenty members of Dr. Walter C.
Eolls' class in "Junior College Ad
ministration" visited the Marin
Junior College at Kent field last Fri
day to study its history, policies,
and plans for the future.
They were guests of President Ol
ney and the faculty of the junior
college for luncheon and made a
tour of inspection of the entire
plant.
