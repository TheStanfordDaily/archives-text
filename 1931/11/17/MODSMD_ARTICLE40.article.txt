# LABOR CLASH JAILS NINE
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 16. (JP)—Eight
men and a woman, fur workers,
representing right and • left wing
union factions, were arrested today
at Seventh avenue and Thirtieth
street after a fifteen-minute fight,
in which sticks, sawed-off billiard
cues, and at least one knife were
vised.
