# WOMEN GET CHANCE FOR EUROPEAN TOUR
## 
###

1 woman who will organize student
l tours of Europe has been received
at Dean Mary Yosts office.
This second offer is similar to the
| first in rewarding a successful or
ganizer a free trip to Europe. Any
woman interested in doing the work
| may obtain details in the Dean s
| office. Alice Ingraham, Miss Yost's
'secretary, said yesterday that seven
women have inquired about the
first offer.
