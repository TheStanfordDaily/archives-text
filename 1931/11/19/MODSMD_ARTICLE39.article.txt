# QUIT GOLD STANDARD
## 
### 
COPENHAGEN, Denmark, Nov.
18. (/P) —Urgent measures to halt
the flight of Danish capital abroad
and to extend suspension of the
gold standard for another three
months were passed in both houses
of the Riksdag today.
