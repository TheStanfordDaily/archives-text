# FRENCH CIRCLE TO MEET FOR REGULAR WEEKLY LUNCHEON
## 
### 
Opportunity to converse in French
will be given at the luncheon to be
held today in the Union dining room
under the auspices of the Cercle
Francais, Stanford French Club.
Andrew Burneit, cldb president,
announces that all conversation will
be carried on in French, giving
those who attend an opportunity to
gain experience in the practical use
of the language. At the same time,
students will have a chance to be
come better acquainted with their
professors and instructors.
Everyone, including those who are
not enrolled in regular language
courses, is invited to attend. The
regular Union luncheon price of 50
cents will be charged for the meal.
