# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
12 noon —French Table will meet in
the Union dining room. All stu
dents interested in speaking the
language are urged to attend.
12:15 p.m.—Lc Cercle Francais will
hold is regular weekly meeting in
the Stanford Union. Everyone in
terested is invited to attend.
5:10 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen in Memorial Church.
3 p.m.—An open meeting of the
English Club will be held at the
home of Jean Magnus, 510 Lowell.
FRIDAY
12 noon—Spanish Table will meet
in the alcove of the Union dining
room. All students who are in
terested are asked to attend.
7:30 p.m.—Big Game Gaieties ushers
meet with J. P. Franklin in the
Assembly Hall.
3 p.m. — Stanford Faculty Club
Housewarming with President
Swain as speaker. Teaching fac
ulty and officers of administration
invited.
3:30 p.m.—Big Game Gaieties in As
sembly Hall; tickets $1.25-75c.
