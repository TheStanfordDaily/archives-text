# BROTHER PLUNGERS IN CARD BACKFIELD
## 
### 

THE HILLMANS, Harry and Jack, who supply a large portion of the power of Warner's machine.
Captain Harry, the veteran, was expected to show his little brother a few things stout packing the pig
skin, hut Jack, a reserve fullback, promptly demonstrated that he needs little, if any, tutoring. Harry's a
quartet hock, of course, but'full and quarter are one and the same things on a Warner eleven.
