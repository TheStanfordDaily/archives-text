# JUST ANOTHER OF POP'S INTERESTS
## 
### 

THE OLD FOX himself,
but there's no football in this
picture. Yes, Pop Warner is
something of a golfer, but
still more important, he
makes several of his own
clubs. Here you see him gaz
ing fondly upon a weapon of
his own design. But it's just
another of Pop's interests,
for he also is known as a
landscape painter, and in
ventor, and general all-'round
handiman.
