# JOLLY-UP FOLLOWS BIG GAME SATURDAY
## 
### 
After the Big Game on Saturday,
there will be an all university jolly
up at Toyon from 5 to 6:15 o'clock,
it was announced yesterday by Bill
Mearns, president of the hall. Sha
piro's campus orchestra will supply
the music for the affair, which will
be held in the Toyon lobby. Ron
Kennedy, is in charge of the dance
and is working to make it a great
success.
The distance from Pensacola to
Key West by rail is approximately
the same as that from New York to
Chicago.
