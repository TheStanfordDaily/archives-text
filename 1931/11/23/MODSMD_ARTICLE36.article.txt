# ANNOUNCEMENTS

MONDAY
12:15 p.m.—lnternational Table will
meet in the alcove of the Union.
5:30 p.m.—An important meeting of
the executive committee of the
Cosmopolitan Club will be he'd
in the lobby of the Union.
7:30 p.m.—The Rev. H. H. Silver
thorn will speak before the Stan
ford Cosmopolitan Club in the
Y.W.C.A. rooms of the Women's
Clubhouse on missionary work in
the Malay peninsula.
TUESDAY
5:10 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren
D. Allen in the Memorial Church.
7:30 p.m.—Dramatic reading by Mrs.
Guy Stevens Farrington, "The
Barretts of Wimpole Street," by
Rudolf Besier. Assembly Hall.
WEDNESDAY
4:15 p.m. —Herr M. Fischer will
speak before the International
Relations Club in the Little Thea
tre on the subject of "Internation
ality of Railways."
SUNDAY
4 p.m.—Organ recital by Warren D.
Allen in the Memorial Church.
MISCELLANEOUS
Thanksgiving recess will be from
6 p.m., November 25, to 8 a.m., No
vember 30.
New tuition notes for the winter
quarter may be applied for at the
i Dean of Men's office November 30-
