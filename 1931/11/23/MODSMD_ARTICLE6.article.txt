# Three Row Groups See 'Birdie' Today
## 
### 
Members of Sigma Alpha Epsilon,
Sigma Chi. and Thcta Xi fraterni
ties will face the eye of the local
camera today for Quad living
group sittings at Watson's studio
across the street from the Press
Building. Hours are 9 to 12 and
1 to 4:30 o'clock. A photographic
fee of 50 cents is charged.
The only group picture listed for
today is Masquers at 4 o'clock in
the Union Court. Hammer and Cof
fin members will be photographed
in the court at 1 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon and the varsity soccer
squad will be retaken at 4:30 o'clock
tomorrow.
Seniors are urgently requested to
get their proofs at the studio, ac
cording to Ray Hanson, Quad pho
tography chief. No picture can be
placed in the Quad until the proofs
are examined and the picture
chosen.
