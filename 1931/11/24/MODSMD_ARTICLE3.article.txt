# SOUTH PLEADS FOR BAN ON ITINERANTS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 23.—(VP)—
A proclamation warning itinerants
they can expect no employment in
California and that they will face
arrest on vagrancy charges if they
appear at California's borders with
out visible means of support was
sought from Governor James Rolph,
Jr., here this morning by a delega
tion of Southern California Chamber
of Commerce men.

A. J. Hill, Los Angeles Chamber
of Commerce member, who urged
ihe gubernatorial proclamation simi
lar to one issued by Florida's chief
executive recently, declared it would
be a ' drastic remedy for a drastic
situation."
