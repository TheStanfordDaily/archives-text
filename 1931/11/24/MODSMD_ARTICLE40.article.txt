# LIBRARY TO REMAIN OPEN FOR VACATION
## 
### 
Stanford Library will observe its I
regular vacation hours during the
Thanksgiving recess, it was an
nounced yesterday by Library of
ficials. Library hours will be: Wed
nesday, 8 to 5 o'clock, Thursday,
closed all day, Friday, 8:30 to 4:30
o'clock, Saturday, 8:30 to 12:30
o'clock, and Sunday, 2 to 4 o'clock.
Reserved books may be with
drawn on Wednesday afternoon and
returned Friday morning, or with
drawn Saturday morning and re
turned Monday morning.
