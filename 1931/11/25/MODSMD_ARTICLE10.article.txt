# EVANS WILL SPEAK AT SIGMA XI OPEN MEETING ON DEC. 3
## 
### 
Speaking on "The Functions of
the Anterior Hypophysis," Dr. Her
bert M. Evans, head of the Depart
ment of Anatomy at the University
of California, will appear before an
open meeting of the Sigma Xi so
ciety on December 3, at 4:15 o'clock
in the Little Theatre.
Dr. Evans is a noted authority on
glands of internal secretion. With
his collaborators he has made im
portant contributions to the present
knowledge of the growth promoting
and sex regulating functions of the
anterior hypophysis. The University,
public is invited to hear Dr. Evans.
