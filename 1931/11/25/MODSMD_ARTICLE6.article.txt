# Quad Studio Open Until Noon Today
## 
### 
In deference to the general exodus
from the Farm sometime today,
Watson Studios' campus shop across
the street from the Daily office will
close at 12 noon today. As a conse
quence, this last day set aside for
taking of senior, Roble Club, and
fraternity photographs will be cat
to the hours 9 to 12 o'clock.
A photographic fee of 50 cents is
charged to the members of living
groups and $1.50 to seniors, which
sum may be applied to the purchase
of a senior Quad.
Individual photography will be
resumed Monday with the taking of
members of Alpha Omicron Pi,
Alpha Phi, Chi Omega, Delta Delta
Delta, and Delta Gamma sororities.
Gamma Phi Beta, Kappa Alpha
Theta, Kappa Kappa Gamma, and
Pi Beta Phi members will face the
lens on Tuesday.
