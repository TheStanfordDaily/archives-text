# EXPLANATION
## 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
Answering your question printed in
the Editorial Column of your pub
lication issued today, please be ad
vised that the letter box formerly
located at the corner of LaSuen and
Santa Ynez was damaged beyond
repair by some person or persons
unknown to the writer, and the box
was removed at my request; a new
letter box will be erected at that
location as soon as the same is
received from the contractor sup
plying letter box equipment.
Very truly yours,
W. C. Werry, Postmaster.
