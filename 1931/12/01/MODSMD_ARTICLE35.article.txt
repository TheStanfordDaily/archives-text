# ROBLE JOLLY-UP
## 
### 
There will be a Roble jolly-up
this evening held at the Hall from
7 to 8 o'clock. The Freshman Com
mittee is in charge of the affair, the
second of the year sponsored by
Roble. Bob Jones' orchestra will
furnish the music.
