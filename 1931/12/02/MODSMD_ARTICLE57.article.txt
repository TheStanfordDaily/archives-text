# BACH ORGAN MUSIC SERIES COMMENCES
## 
### 
Of particular interest to lovers of
Bach is the series of seven recitals
which Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will commence tomorrow
with his program at 5:10 o'clock in
the Memorial Church.
Tomorrow's program follows:
Three Chorale Preludes..J. S. Bach
We All Believe in One God,
Creator Have Mercy on
Me, a Saving Health to Us
Is Brought
Andante Cantabile from the
Fourth Symphony. C. M. Widor
Kyrie Eleison (from "Cathedral
Windows"') S. Karg-Elert
Benedictus M. Reger
