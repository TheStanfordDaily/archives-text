# GROWTH GLANDS TO BE SPEECH SUBJECT TODAY OF DR. EVANS
## 
### 
An astounding gland that trans
poses little mammals into big ones
and regulates the growth processes
of the body will be described this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in the
Little Theatre by Professor Her
bert M. Evans.
Under the auspices of the Stan
ford chapter of Sigma Xi, national
honorary scientific society, Dr.
Evans will tell of the "Anterior
Hypothysis," a little gland at the
base of the brain which possesses
Herculean power to modify and
regulate the body growth.
Following the lecture a dinner,
tendered by the School of Biology,
will be given at G: 15 o'clock in the
Old Union in honor of the speaker.
