# ENGLISH CLUB WILL DISCUSS CHILDREN'S BOOKS AT MEETING
## 
### 
An open meeting' of the English
Club will be held next Monday at
8 o'elock in the evening in the
Women's Clubhouse. Children's
books will be the subject of dis
cussion. Dr. Margery Bailey, Mrs.
Webster Street, A. Grove Day, and
Howard Pease will be the speakers.
A feature of the meeting will be
a display of children's books.
