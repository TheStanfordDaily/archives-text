# WOMEN TO GIVE FALL DANCE DRAMA DEC. 10
## 
### 
The annual fall dance drama v/ill
bo held Thursday, December 10, at
five o'clock in the dance studio of
iho Roble Gymnasium.
Eleanor Furst, dance manager,
announces that a brief review of
(he quarter's, work will be given,
chiefly by members of the advanced
class. The program will be held im
mediately after the hockey games
and will be open to the public.
Jugoslavia has reduced salaries
of government employees.
