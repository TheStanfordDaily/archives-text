# NEWMAN CLUB TO HEAR TOBIN SPEAK
## 
### 
Speaking on the subject of "Cath
olic Lay Life in Modern Europe,"
Richard M. Tobin of San Francisco
will address the members of the
Stanford Newman Club at their
quarterly communion breakfast
which is to be held at 8:45 o'clock
Sunday morning at the President
Hotel in Palo Alto.
The breakfast will follow a com
munion service at 8:15 mass in St.
Thomas Aquinas Church.
