# Final Photo Week Announced By Quad
## 
### 
Quad officials have announced
this week to be the last opportunity
offered to living-group members to
have their Quad photographs taken.
All pictures will be taken at the
Watson Studios across the street
from the Press Building between
the hours 9 to 12 and 1 to 4:30
o'clock.
Today will be the last day for
members of Alpha Phi, Alpha
Omicron Pi, and Chi Omega .sorori
ties, and Alpha Delta Phi, Alpha
Tau Omega, Alpha Kappa Lambda,
Alpha Sigma Phi, Beta Theta Pi,
Chi Psi, Delta Chi, and Delta Kappa
Epsilon fraternities.
Those scheduled for Tuesday are
Delta Delta Delta, Delta Gamma,
and Gamma Phi Beta sororities, and
Delta Tau Delta, Delta Upsilon,
Kappa Alpha, Kappa Sigma, Phi
Delta Theta, Phi Gamma Delta, Phi
Kappa Psi, and Phi Kappa Sigma
fraternities.
The fact is stressed that this is
the final opportunity afforded to
these house members to have their
photographs taken.
