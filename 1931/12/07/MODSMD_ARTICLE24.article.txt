# GERMAN SOCIETY TO HOLD PARTY TONIGHT
## 
### 
The annual Christmas Party of
the German Honor Society will lie
held tonight in the Women's Club
house at 7 o'clock.
Dr. Friedrich Wilhelm Stroth
mann will be toastmaster. A quartet
consisting of John Peck, Donald
Morgan, Robert Clark, and Gerrit
deJong will sing some German
.songs. A violin solo by Johann Jur
gensen, a vocal solo by Robert
Clark, and a reading in German by
Peter Vellenga will conclude the
entertainment.
