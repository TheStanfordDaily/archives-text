# XMAS DOLLS TO BE DISPLAY AT 'Y' TEA
## 
### 
Christmas dolls to be given to the
Convalescent Home will be on dis
play at the Y.W.C.A. tea to be held
this Friday in the 'Y' rooms from
4 to 6 o'clock.
The 24 dolls dressed by the soror
ity and Roble women will be judged
and a prize awarded for the best
dressed. Tea will be served, and
the visitors are asked to bring a
ten-cent toy to fill the 100 Christ
mas stockings, which will go to
bring happiness to the poor children
in Palo Alto. The stockings will be
distributed at a party to be given
by Mrs. J. P. Mitchell during
Christmas week.
