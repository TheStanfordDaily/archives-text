##

Library hours for the Christmas J
vacation were announced yesterday
by Nathan Van Patten, director of
the University Library.
The Library will be closed on
Christmas Day, New Year's, Sun
days, and the Saturday after Christ- j
mas. On December 18, last day of j
finals, and January 4, registration !
day, the open hours will be from 8 j
o'clock to 6 o'clock. On week days ■
open hours will be from 8:30 o'clock j
o 4:30 o'clock, and on Saturday's j
f rom 8:30 to 12:30 in (he mornings, i
Regular hours will be resumed on I
January 5.
