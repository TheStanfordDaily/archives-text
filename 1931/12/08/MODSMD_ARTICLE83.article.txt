# CHOIR TO PRESENT CANTATA SUNDAY IN MEMORIAL CHURCH
## 
### 
The University Choir will present
a cantata, ''High Heaven's King,"
by Walford Davies, at the Memorial
Church, Sunday afternoon at 4
o'clock.
This cantata, the musical setting
of a portion of Edmund Spenser's
"Hymn of Heavenly Love," is being
sung for the first time in this part
of the country. The choir, directed
by Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will have as soloists Verda
Hogan, soprano; Henry ShefofT,
bass; and Annie Louise David,
harpist.
