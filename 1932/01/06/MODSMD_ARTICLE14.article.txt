# VARSITY YELL LEADER TRYOUTS TOMORROW
## 
### 
Men desiring to try out for the
position of varsity yell leader have
been asked to meet tomorrow after
noon at 1 o'clock in the Pavilion.
AH sophomores are eligible.
Tryoutees will work all through
the winter quarter, and at the end
of that period the six best will be
chosen by the Rally Committee.
Three of these will be elected to
the position of yell leaders by the
student body.
