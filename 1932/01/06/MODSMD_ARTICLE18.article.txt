# MEXICAN JUDICIARY WEIGHS CLERGY LAW
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Jan. 5. UP)
Hearings on applications for re
straining orders against enforce
ment of the new law limiting the
clergy in Mexico City were sched
uled today by various courts.
Although the First District Court
announced its denial of a request
for a writ of amparo, or restraining
order, four other courts delayed
their final decision and another gave
a priest three days in which to
clarify his petition.
