# SORORITY RUSHING TO START ON SATURDAY
## 
### 
Sorority rushing bids for winter
quarter will be out Friday morning,
it was announced by Pan-Hellenic.
Rushing will start Saturday noon,
January 9, and will last from then
till January 12, inclusive. Silence
days will come on January 13 and
14.
The second period of rushing will
be from the fifteenth to the twen
tieth, inclusive. There will be a
period of silence all day on the
twenty-first. Bidding will take
place on the night of the twenty
first.
