# PLANS VISIT TO PHILIPPINES
## 
### 
BRUSSELS, Jan. 6. (A 3 ) — The
Duke and Duchess of Brabant, on
their coming trip to the French In
dies, may visit the Philippine islands
on the way from Indo China to
Madagascar. A definite decision is
to be made later.
