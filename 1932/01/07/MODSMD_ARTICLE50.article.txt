# MATMEN SET FOR COLLEGE MATCHES
## 
### 
Opening mat practice yesterday
with a wealth of untried material,
Wrestling Coach Ted Hill looks for
ward to lots of competition in the
all-University wrestling tournament
which has been scheduled for Janu
ary 29. Hill announced that 3G be
ginners had signed up for coach
ing and that 14 veterans were back
on the mat this quarter.

Practice is held daily from 4:20
to 6 o'clock, and any men desiring
coaching for the coming events and
who hi ve not yet enrolled may see
Hill daring these hours in Encina
gym. Men entering the tournament
should register at once on the lists
posted there in order that matches
may be drawn up as soon as pos
sible.
