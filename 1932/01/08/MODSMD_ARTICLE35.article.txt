# FIRST CAP AND GOWN MEETING ON MONDAY
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, women's honor
society, will hold its first regular
meeting for this quarter at the
home of Mrs. Fredrick Fowler on
Kingsley avenue in Palo Alto next
Monday at 4:30 o'clock.
Plans for the quarter will be dis
cussed, including the Scholarship
tea which will be held on February
13. Lillian Schuck, president of Cap
and Gown, announced that .Cap and
Gown would very much appreciate
any Bookstore receipts which ihe
students can give. All that is neces
sary is to sign "Cap and Gown" in
stead of your own name.
