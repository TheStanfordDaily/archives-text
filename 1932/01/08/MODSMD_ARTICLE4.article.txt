# BIG STADIUM TO HOUSE 1932 G.O.P. CONVENTION
## 
### 

When the members of the Grand Old Party gather in Chicago next June to choose their presidential
standard-bearers, this Chicago Stadium, with a seating capacity of more than 25.000 will be the battleground.
It is here that the G.O.P. will decide the fate of its once-tried Hoover regime.
