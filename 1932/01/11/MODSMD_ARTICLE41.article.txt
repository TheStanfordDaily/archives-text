# BOTANICAL EXHIBIT ON VIEW IN LIBRARY
## 
### 
Botanical publications of the 17th
and 18th centuries are featured in
the cunent exhibition in the lobby
of the University Library. The ex
hibit includes descriptive floras,
botanical texts and histories, reports
of botanical explorers and collect
ors, and plant anatomies printed in
English, French, Italian, Dutch, and
German. The books are from the
general and valuable collections of
the Library.
Among the English publications
on display are "The Posthumous
Works of Robert Hooge," 1705, and
Geraide's "Herball: or, General His
toric of Plants," 1633.
