# MANUSCRIPTS TO BE READ AT CONVENTION
## 
###

of the English Club will bo read
before the organization this evening
when the English Club holds its
first mooting of the autumn quarter.
The meeting is lo convene at 8
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Only members of the club are in
vited to attend.
