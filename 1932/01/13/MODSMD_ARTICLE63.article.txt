# GARBAGE IN BRICK
## 
### 
BERLIN, Jan. 12. (/P)—Bricks
made of garbage will be used in
building houses for those unem
ployed in Berlin, who are to be
settled down on the land in accord
ance with the scheme recently
adopted for relief of unemployed.
