# PROMINENT ITALIANS TARGETS OF BOMBERS

Associated Press Photo
Count Ugo Berni Canani (upper right), Detroit Italian vice consul, received an express package
believed to have been sent as part of a nation-wide anti-fascist plot. Count Cesare Buzzi-Grandenigo
(lower right), Italian consul at Clevelnd, was the target of a similar attempt. Both bombs were disposed
of harmlessly. Above is the label from the package addressed to the Cleveland consul. Below Detroit
police are shown examining the bomb sent to Count Canani.
