# WOMEN SELECT BAND FOR DEPRESSION HOP
## 
### 
The W.A.A. has secured the serv
ices of Herb Shapiro's 14-piece band
for its Depression dance, January
30, it was announced last night by
Marian Hewlett, chairman of the
committee in charge.
The admission will be 25 cents per
couple, 20 cents per man and 10
cents per woman. The affair will
be held in the dance salon of the
new women's gym, and will last
from 9 o'clock to 12.
Refreshments of a sort will prob
ably be served, it was said.
