# MOB BURNS CHURCH
## 
### 
MADRID, Jan. 18. (A>)—'The Min
ister of the Interior reported today
that a mob had set fire to the church
in Santurce, near Bilbao. There was
fighting in the streets and Iwo men
were seriously wounded. The fire
was extinguished.
