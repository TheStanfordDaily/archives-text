# NEVERS' GRID TEAM TO PRACTICE TODAY ON STANFORD TURF
## 
### 
Ernie Nevers and 22 members of
the Chicago Cardinals, professional
football stars, arrived here late last
night to begin practice in the Sta
dium today in preparation for a
charity football game with Frank
Carideo's All-Stars at San Francisco
next Sunday afternoon.
The Nevers squad arrived by auto
from Los Angeles where they de
feated Barrager's All-Stars 14 to 0
in a game Sunday.
Nevers intends to use the Stan
ford Stadium today and tomorrow
and move his outfit to San Francisco
for the final practice sessions at
Kezar Stadium where the game is to
be held.
