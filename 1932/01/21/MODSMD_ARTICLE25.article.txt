# COMPULSORY SERVICE FOR CAVALRYMAN
## 
###

strong feeling in Belgium on the
question of conscientious objection
to military servicc will be intensi
fied by the sentence of eight months'
imprisonment passed on a cavalry
man who refused to obey orders
on principle.
