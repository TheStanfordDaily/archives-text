# DAMES MEET TODAY TO HEAR TRAVELER
## 
### 
Mrs. Albert M. Chesley, noted
traveller, lecturer, and writer, will
speak on a "Winter in Rome" at a
meeting of the Stanford Dames in
the Women's Clubhouse at 3 o'clock
this afternoon.
Suvilla Hilmer and Dorothy Fife
Johnson, lyric soprano and contral
to, will give a program of Italian
music, including Brahms' "Les Bo
hemiennes," of "The Gypsies";
Blumenlhal's "Venetian Boat Song,"
and Brahms' "Santa Lucia."
