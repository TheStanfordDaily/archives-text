# MEETINGS OF GLEE CLUB ARE ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Next week's schedule of Glee
Club meetings has been announced.
Monday evening the basses meet in
El Capitan and baritones in the
women's old gym. Tuesday evening
sees both the first and second tenors
in El Tigre at the usual time, while
Wednesday night the initiation ban
quet will be held, the time and
place as yet undecided.
Neophytes should phone Girvin
Whitney for instructions as to
preparation for the banquet, with all
its attendant program.
