# KEHO GIVEN LEAD IN BAKERSFIELD DRAMA
## 
### 
Katherine Keho Pike, well-known
Stanford actress, remembered here
for many leading roles as well as
for being starred by the Dramatic
Council in "Granite," is playing the
title role in "The Last of Mrs.
Cheney" at the Bakersfield Com
munity Theatre.
Mrs. Pike's last appearance on the
stage here in May was as Fanny in
the campus production of "The
Royal Family." According to re
ports, the Bakersfield Theatre is
giving, "The Last of Mrs. Cheney"
a very professional production and
is seeking national recognition.
