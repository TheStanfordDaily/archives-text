# RAZZ AMERICAN JAZZ
## 
### 
PARIS, Jan. 20. (/P)—Unemployed
French musicians, irked by the
strains of American jazz or Russian
gypsy bands as they tram from cafe
to bar in search of work, have a
' new form of protest. Customers are
| asked to demand French music from
French bands.
Gene McEver, Tennessee football
ace, has spiked rumors that he will
l play pro football next year.
