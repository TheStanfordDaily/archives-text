# Legal Fraternities To Face Camera
## 
### 
Photography of members of the
honorary legal fraternities, seniors,
and living group members will con
tinue today at Watson Studios
across the street from the Press
Building. Studio hours are 9 to 12
and 1 to 4:30 o'clock.
The following members of the
Executive Committee or Men's
Council have not arranged for their
Quad photos as yet and are re
quested to do so at once: Gilbert
Carr, William Doub, Milton Hand,
and Arthur Miller.
For the benefit of the.students, a
sales service is maintained at the
studio. Copies of the '32 Quad may
be purchased for $6.
