# BLAZE HITS GARAGE OF JOURNALISM HEAD
## 
### 
Fire, believed to have been started
by a candle which fell into a pile
of rags, last night attacked the gar
age of Professor Everett W. Smith,
head of the journalism department,
556 Alvarado.
Reported at 10:20 o'clock. Ihe
blaze was extinguished with but
slight damage.
