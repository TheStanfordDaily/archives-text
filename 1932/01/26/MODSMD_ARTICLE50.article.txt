# NOW THE PINOCHLE BATTLE IS ON
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
This scenc, folks, with its rolled up shirt sleeves, slanting derbies
and smoldering cigars is a peep at the "pinochle battle of the century.
The locality mayors of New York vs. the champjons of Toledo, Ohio,
left to right, at table: John Leppig (wearing derby), New York: Elmer
Dupree, Toledo; "Stitch" McCarthy, New York, and Louis Abrams,
Toledo. In foreground is Irving Abalov, official scorekeeper. Behind
Dupree is Frederick Groehl, referee.
