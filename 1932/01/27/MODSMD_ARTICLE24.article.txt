# CLARKE IS ELECTED MEMBER OF W.A.A.
## 
### 
Rosamund Clarke was elected
graduate member of the W.A.A.
Board al a meeting held last night,
filling the place left open by the
resignation of Katherine Sobey.
Plans for the Depression Dance
to be held Saturday night from 0
to 12 o'clock were completed. Ad
mission was definitely set at 25
cents a couple, 10 ccnts for a woman
and 20 cents for a man. Herb Sha
piro's orchestra will furnish the
music for the dance, the proceeds
of which will go to the Women's
Gymnasium Fund.
