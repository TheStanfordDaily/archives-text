# GLEE CLUB INITIATES FIFTEEN NEW SINGERS
## 
### 
Fifteen men were initiated into
the Glee Club at last night's ban
quet at the Sunset Cafeteria, which
was attended by Dr. Gardner and
Jack McDowell, as well as Director
Kulmann. All three addressed the
members, and Don Morgan sang
two bass solos between paddling
sessions.
Kenneth Addicott, Oscar Baer,
Philips Bronson, Charles Dana, Da
vid Dunning, Carroll Green, Clyde
Greenlee, John Huston, Russ Hux
sol, Fran Johnston, John Schwafel,
Bob Stoddard, Dick Van Wagenen,
and A1 Woodward were the fifteen
new members.
