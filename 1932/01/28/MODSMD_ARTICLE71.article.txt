# GAR WOOD SHATTERS SPEEDBOAT RECORD
## 
### 
MIAMI BEACH, Fla., Jan. 27 <»'>
—Gar Wood, veteran American
speedboat racer, today set a mark of
110.785 miles an hour in his Miss
America IX, to claim the world's
speeboat record.
On the second of his runs today,
Wood pushed his long twenty-four
cylinder boat at a rate of 110.49
miles an hour southward over the
measured nautical miles course in
Indian creek following a northbound
run at a speed of 111.08 miles an
