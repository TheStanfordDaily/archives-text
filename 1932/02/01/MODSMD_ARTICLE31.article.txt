# SPONSORS THANKED AT SORORITY SOCIAL FOR AID IN RUSHING
## 
### 
The Roble sponsors were enter
tained at a delightful tea at the
Kappa Kappa Gamma house yes
terday afternoon in honor of the
success of the sorority in the past
rushing season. Both the president
of the house and the president of the
Pan-Hellenic Council, also a Kappa,
made speeches thanking the spon
sors for the invaluable assistance
which they gave the house during
rushing, both girls claiming that
without the hard work of the spon
sors. the Kappas would not have
pledged a single girl.
Each sponsor was presented with
a small gold charm with number
"18" engraved on it in recognition
of their services.
