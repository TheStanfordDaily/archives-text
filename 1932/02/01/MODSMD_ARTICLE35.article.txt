# REFEREE DUNN LIKES JOB, ANSWER SHOWS
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, —an. 31. (As
sisted Press) —William Dunn, noted
basketball referee and protege of
Sam Barry, U.S.C. hoop mentor, has
denied rumors that he will officiate
at no more games alter this year.
"A job that pays well and takes no
mental effort should not be cast
aside without consideration" was
his excuse for remaining in the
refereeing game.
