# SEQUOIA WILL HOLD BENEFIT TEA FRIDAY
## 
### 
Comic valentines to be given to
the Convalescent Home will be on
display at the Sequoia Club tea to
be held Friday in the hall lobby.
Sixty-four types of these cheery
messages will be judged by Roble
women and a prize awarded to the
best. The prize has not been an
nounced.
Tea will be served, and guests are
asked to bring packages of fire
crackers for the Convalescent
Home's Fourth-of-July party. The
party is to be held early this year
to get it out of the way, it is an
nounced.
