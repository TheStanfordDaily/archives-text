# ROBBERS TAKE $300,000 IN GEMS
## 
### 

Three robbers posing as bootleggers entered the New York home
of Harry Glemby, wealthy manufacturer, and scooped up more than
$300,000 worth of jewels. They bound Mr. and Mrs. Glemby and ser
vants before taking the jewelry from the safe. Mrs. Glemby is shown
detailing incidents of the robbery to police. With her are Detective
Murphy (left) and her son, David.
