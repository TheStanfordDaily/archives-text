# INTRAMURAL SWIM TRIALS TO BE TODAY
## 
### 
Intramural swimming tournament
trials will be held at Encina pool at
3 o'clock this afternoon, according
to a recent announcement. Finals
will be held Thursday afternoon.
In order for a contestant to enter
the finals, he must place in one of
the trials which will be held this
afternoon.
