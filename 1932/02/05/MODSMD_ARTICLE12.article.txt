# DEPRESSION OUT WHILE STANFORD FROLICS CHEAPLY
## 
### 
Depression will take another
knockout blow to the chin to
morrow night in the Women's
Gym at the all-University two
bit dance. Twenty-five cents a
couple, 20 cents for stags, and
15c for unescorted women are
the tariffs. All profits over the
price of the orchestra will go
to the Student's Emergency
Employment Fund.
Music will be furnished by
the "Royal Cardinals." The
dance is sponsored by Kappa
Kappa Psi, national honorary
band fraternity.
