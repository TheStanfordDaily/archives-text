# TODAY LAST DAY TO OBTAIN QUAD PROOFS
## 
### 
Today is the last opportunity to
obtain proofs of Quad pictures to
insure inclusion of them in the 1932
annual. No picture can be placed in
the Quad until the proofs are ex
amined and the picture chosen.
About seventy proofs still remain
at the studio which will be open
from 9 to 12 o'clock and 1 to 4:30
o'clock today only.
