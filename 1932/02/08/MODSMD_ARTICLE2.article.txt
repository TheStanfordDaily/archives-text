Steinhart aquarium. For months
the inhabitants of the aquar
ium, fish, turtles, seals, and
whatnot, have eaten "off of"
the city and county of San
Francisco. Now comes the new
charter, with ils itemizations,
and through Controller Leavy,
insists on knowing just what
each one eats.

head of the directing academy,
and his scientific associates,
are now faced with the task
of itemizing animal appetites.
