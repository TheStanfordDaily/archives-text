# FACULTY WOMEN TO PRESENT IRISH MUSIC
## 
### 
A program of vocal music will be
presented at the meeting of the
Stanford Faculty Women's Club
Monday afternoon. The selections
will be given by Mrs. S. S. Seward,
Jr., contralto; Stanley P. King, bari
tone; Grosvenor Cooper, pianist; and
Mrs. Stanley P. King, accompanist.
Irish music will be prominently
featured. Mr. King will sing a group
arranged by William Armes Fisher,
including "The Leprechaun," "The
Blatherskite," and "Down the Sally
Gardens." Cooper will sing the
"Irish Tune from County Derry"
arranged by Percy Grainger. Sev
eral German numbers will also be
