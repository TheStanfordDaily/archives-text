# FINES TO PENALIZE LATE PAYMENT OF SYLLABUS CHARGES
## 
### 
For failure to pay syllabus fees
before tonight, students will be sub
ject to a tine of $2 for delinquent
payment except in cases where fees
are less than $2. In such instances
the tine equals the assessment.
Fees are payable from 8 to 12
o'clock and Irom 1 to 5 o'clock today
to the cashier in the Administration
Building. Registration will be sub
ject to cancellation if fees are not
paid by next Tuesday.
In regard to any questions con
cerning charges, students are re
quested to inquire at the Registrar's
office.
