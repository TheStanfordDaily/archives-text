# DE MOLAY CLUB TO MEET HERE TONIGHT
## 
### 
Plans for conferring a degree in
the near future will be made at
the regular monthly meeting of the
Stanford DeMolay Club, to be held
in the Y.M.C.A. lobby at 7:20 o'clock
this evening. All campus DeMolays,
whether members of the club or not,
are invited to attend the meeting.
The club Quad picture will be
taken in the Union court at 1 o'clock
this afternoon. Members are re
quested to wear suits.
