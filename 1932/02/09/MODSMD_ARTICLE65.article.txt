# SAFFORD TO FEATURE MASTERS IN PROGRAM
## 
### 
Charles L. Safford, visiting or
ganist, will give ;in organ recital
this afternoon at 5:10 o'clock in the
Stanford Memorial Church. He will
play some of the better-known
compositions of the old masters.
One of the more interesting of
these masters is Tschaikowsky, a
Russian. Before beginning his career
of music, he studied law and en
tered the government service. Like
most of the .Russians of his time, he
was intensely nationalistic in his
music and very dramatic.
