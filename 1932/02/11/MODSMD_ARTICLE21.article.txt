# B.A.C. DECIDES TO CUT GREEN FEES FOR LOCAL COURSE
## 
### 
Green fees for outsiders on the
Stanford golf course were reduced
from $3 to $2.50 on week-ends and
holidays and from 32 to $1.50 on
week days at a meeting of the Board
of Athletic Control last night.
It was also voted to donate 24
free rounds of golf during the week
and 12 rounds on week-ends dur
ing the period April 2f> to May 11 to
U.S. naval officers at which time
the fleet will be in port.
Ii was also decided to construct
a permanent roof over the press box
at the Stadium.
