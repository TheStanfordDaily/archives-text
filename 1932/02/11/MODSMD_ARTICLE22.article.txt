# FOUNDERS' PROGRAM FEATURES SYMPHONY
## 
### 
Founders' Day, March 9, will be
observed by a program of the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra in
the Memorial Church, in accordance
with the custom of the past few
years, according to an announce
ment made yesterday by the Com
mittee on Public Exercises.
The orchestra, with Basil Cam
iron conducting, will offer the fol
lowing program:
Overture, "Manfried".. . .Schumann
A Tone Poem, "Tintagel" Bax
Symphony No. 3, "Eroica"
