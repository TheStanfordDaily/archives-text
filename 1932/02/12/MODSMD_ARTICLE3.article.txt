# SUZUKI DESERTS HOSPITAL; OUT ON BOTH FEET NOW
## 
### 
Kamenosuke Suzuki is out
of the hospital!
For the past five months his
name has headed the list of
hospital patients in the Daily
every day. For the last half
year, every twenty-four hours
has seen fresh ink on Kameno
suke Suzuki's name. But the
familiar sight is a thing of the
past.
Tlie cause for his compelled
stay in the hospital was an
automobile accident which oc
curred late last October. A pro
fessor, driving frantically
around the campus, deep in
thought, evidently did not see
that which he hit with impact
and ossed into the air. "That"
happened to baft the surprised
and displeased Kamenosuke
Suzuki,
For four months, then, Mr.
Suzuki lay in a six-foot cot
and pondered upon the prob
lems of life. What conclusions
he has arrived at, we leave to
you to discover. The main
thing is—all physical errors
are rectified, and once more
Kamenosuke Suzuki is in a
vertical position.
