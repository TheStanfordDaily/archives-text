# GLEE CLUB TO SING
## 
### 
Approximately 25 members of the
Stanford Glee Club will journey
north to Cloverdale Saturday to give
a pair of concerts at the Sonomo
County Fair. The group will leave
the Bookstore at 9:30 o'clock Satur
day morning, arriving in time to
sing at 2 o'clock and again in Ihe
evening.
