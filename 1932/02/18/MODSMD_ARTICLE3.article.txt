# GRAF TO VISIT U.S.
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 17. (/P)—
Dr. Hugo Eckener, commander of
the Graf Zeppelin, commanded here
today, saying if weather conditions
permit he will fly to the United
States this week.
