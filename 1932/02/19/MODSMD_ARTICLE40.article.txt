# GLEE CLUB SINGS AT SONOMA COUNTY FAIR
## 
### 
Scheduled to sing once in the af
ternoon at 2 o'clock and again in
the evening, the Stanford Glee Club
will leave the campus tomorrow
morning for Cloverdale.
Manager Jack Peck ha's arranged
the trip as an all-day .affair, with
the two informal concerts to take
place at the Sonoma County Fair in
Cloverdale.
