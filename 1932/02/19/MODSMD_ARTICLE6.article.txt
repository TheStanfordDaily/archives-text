# COLORADO BISHOP TO PREACH HERE SUNDAY
## 
### 
In commemoration of Washing
ton's birthday, the Reverend Irving
P. Johnson, Bishop of Colorado, will
speak in the Stanford Memorial
Church Sunday morning at 11
o'clock. Johnson is one of the most
famous bishops in the country. His
experience has ranged from profes
sor of Ecclesiastical literature to
editorship of "The Witness."
