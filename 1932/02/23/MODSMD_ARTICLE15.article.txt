# Watson Studios To Re-Open Tomorrow
## 
### 
Watson Studios, official photo
graphers for the 1932 Quad, re
open their campus shop tomorrow
for the photography of the new class
and A.S.S.U. officers elected in the
recent Quad poll, as well as the
presidents and vice-presidents of
the campus halls, the debating
coach, members of the Japanese and
Chinese clubs and any other indi
viduals scheduled for special photos
but who have not had them taken
to date.
The studio is located across the
street from the Daily office. Hours
are 9 to 12 o'clock and 1 to 4:30
o'clock. Proofs of all pictures taken
previously may also be had at this
time.
Secretaries and treasurers of the
various classes will be taken in the
Union court from 1 1o 1:30 o'clock.
