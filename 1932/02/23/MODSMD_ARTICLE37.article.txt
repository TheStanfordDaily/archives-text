# FIGHTING GLEDHILL DEFEATED BY VINES
## 
### 
Keith Gledhill, Stanford net cap
tain, failed to make the grade Sun
day at the California Tennis Club
when he bowed to Ellsworth Vines
in straight sets, 6-3, 6-3. Gledhill
showed plenty of fight, however, in
coming up from a 5-0 and 40-15
hole to take three games before
Vines could put over the winning
strokes in the second set.
The fortune of the two together
thai afternoon was better. They
took Neer and Levy in three sets,
6-3, 2-6, 8-6. However, the top
notch Vines-Gledhill doubles com
bination suffered defeat at the
hands of Stratford and Brown, 3-6,
6-1. 6-2.
