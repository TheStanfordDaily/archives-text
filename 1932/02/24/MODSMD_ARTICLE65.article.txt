# WRECKAGE OF BLIMP IN WHICH ONE WAS KILLED
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
John Blair of Rockford, 111., a mechanic aboard, was killed when the Goodyear blimp Columbia
became unmanageable in a 40-mile wind and crashed on a freight shed near the Holmes airport,
Flushing, N. Y. The pilot and a passenger escaped serious injury.
