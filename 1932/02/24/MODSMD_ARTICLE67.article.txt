# SIGMA DELTA CHI TO INITIATE CALL EDITOR
## 
### 
Members of Sigma Delta Chi,
men's professional journalism fra
ternity, will meet today at 1
o'clock in the Journalism library to
discuss plans with the California
chapter for the joint initiation of
Fremont Older, editor of the San
Francisco Call-Bulletin.
Older, together with pledges from
the two university chapters, will be
initiated Saturday at a luncheon
held at the Press Club in San Fran
cisco. All members of the local
chapter are asked to be present at
today's meeting, which will be in
the nature of an executive session.
