# CLUB OFFERS PRIZE FOR ENGLISH ESSAY
## 
### 
With a prize of $50 offered for the
best manuscript turned in, the Eng
lish Club is asking for essays, short
stories, poems, and short plays for
its yearbook. Manuscripts may be
turned in to Professor Margery
Bailey, Edwin Williams, or placed
in the cabinet in Room 200 after
tcday.
Besides the $50 prize offered for
the best article, a special edition of
the winning entry will be printed
by the University Press. Smith
Dawless was the winner of the con
test last year.
