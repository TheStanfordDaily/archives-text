# BIG EATER DIES
## 
### 
BUDAPEST, Feb. 25. I/P)—Steph
en Kiss, Hungary's champion eater,
is dead. Kiss, who was 6 feet 6
inches tall and weighed 3GO pounds,
had as his daily luncheon thirty
eggs, bacon, thirty dumplings, and
two gallons of wine.

to be swell read it
a smiling young lady of nigcr
took a ride on the back of a tiger
they came back from the ride
with the lady inside
and the smile on the face of the
tiger
