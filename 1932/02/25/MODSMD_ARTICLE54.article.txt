# WHITE IN FINALS OF HANDBALL MATCHES
## 
### 
With but three matches to be run
off, competition in the all-Univer
sity, four-wall, singles handball
championship is drawing to a close.
By virtue of his victories over
Winston Strong and Walt Cooper,
Gordon White will enter the finals.
In the lower bracket Joe Wheeler
will play the winner of the Francis
Bates-Bill Graham match, and the
victor will tackle White in the
finals next week for the University
championship.
