# NEW GLIDER COUCH FOSTERS STRETCHING
## 
### 
If a pair of Toyon roommates de
decide to dust up the old suite a bit,
and get some of that furniture that
has been vaguely planned since first
quarjer freshman year, this will
prove a good time to purchase it.
Among the new idebs in the shops
is a smart new glider coueh uphol
stered in horizon blue Basque cloth,
gaily striped. It is a singularly oy
ous blue, conducive to full-length
stretching, with an ethics book in
one hand and a modest volume of
international law in the other.
New bamboo pieces, always popu
lar, show chairs and settees designed
from the old tropical bamboo chair:..
Large swirls at the side give these
pieces a sort of irresponsible, enjoy
yourself expression that fits the
study mood.
