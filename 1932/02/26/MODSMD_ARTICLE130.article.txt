# WEAR ABILITY ONLY CRITERIA FOR DRESS
## 
### 
Along with the general trend to
color and individuality comes the
news from Paris that period effects
are the last things that the fashion
conscious female will take seriously.
It won't matter at all whether
sleeve interest is concentrated at the
elbow or at the wrist. The only re
quirement is that the whole dress
must achieve a harmonious whole.
Becomingness and wearability will
be the criteria by which it will be
judged.
