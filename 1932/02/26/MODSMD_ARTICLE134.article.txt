# SQUASH EVENTS ARE SCHEDULED FOR '500'
## 
### 
An elimination tournament in
squash will start Thursday at the
Roble Gym, it was announced yes
terday by Miss Gertrude Hawley.
She hopes all women who have
been playing squash this year, in
cluding undergraduates, graduates,
and faculty members, will take part.
Anyone wishing to enter must
phone or see Miss Hawley before
Tuesday night. Drawings will be
made Tuesday night and posted on
the bulletin board Wednesday.
