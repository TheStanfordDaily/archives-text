# Y.W.C.A. ORGANIZES NEW CHURCH GROUP TO STUDY RELIGIONS
## 
### 
A student group interested in
broadening its experience and
knowledge of the worship services
and creeds of different churches and
religions will be organized Sunday
under the auspices of the Stanford
Y.W.C.A. to begin a tour of the
various churches represented in
Palo Alto and San Francisco.
All students, both men and
women, and all faculty members
interested in such a project are in
vited to meet in the Y.W.C.A. rooms
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse Sunday morning at 9:30
o'clock. The Catholic church in Palo
Alto will be attended for high mass
at that time. An explanation of the
meaning and significance of this
service will be given preceding the
actual service.
