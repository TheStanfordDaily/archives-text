# SANTA ANA MINISTER WILL PREACH SUNDAY
## 
### 
The Rev. George A. Warmer,
A.M., D.D., from the First Method
ist Episcopal Church of Santa Ana
will preach in the Stanford Me
morial Church next Sunday at 11
o'clock on the subject, "Elements of
a Workable Program." Warmer is
one of the best-known Methodist
ministers on the coast.
Special music which will accom
pany the service will be:
O Saviour of the World
Sir John Goss
How Lovely Is Thy Duelling
Place Johannes Brahms
