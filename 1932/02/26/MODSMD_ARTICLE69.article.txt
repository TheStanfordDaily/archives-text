# SCARFS WILL PLAY IMPORTANT ROLE IN SUMMER FASHIONS
## 
### 
Resorts will see many scarfs in
conjunction with fashionable wom
an's outfits this coming summer and
in a wide variety of uses. Knotted
kerchiefs to go with collarless sports
coats are already well on their way
to established popularity, while ten
nis and other sporting frocks are fit
tingly topped off with a similar or
nament.
Formed into the very latest type
of bathing suit top, the kerchiefs are
also employed as the upper sections
of beach pajamas. They will be
much worn around the waistline of
the new mannish flannel slacks.
The cotton handkerchief of the
workingman type is a favorite ex
pression of the mode. It is offered it«
red and white, navy blue and white,
with polka dots, in plaids, and with
conventional designs.
