# DANCE DRAMA TO BE PRESENTED MARCH 8
## 
### 
Individual and group dances of
one general theme, as in previous
years, will feature the dance drama
to be presented by the dancing
classes in the women's gymnasium,
March 8, at 8:15 o'clock.
Pierrot, columbine and harlequin
dance to the strains of Tschaikow
sky in the introductory number.
Sprightly wood nymphs present
"Tales of Viennese Woods." In
"Dendezvous" two statues, one a
girl and the other a boy, are stand
ing in a garden when the gardener
comes in to water his plants.
