# SQUASH TOURNAMENT BEGINS THURSDAY
## 
### 
Women who have been playing
squash this year are invited to enter
the squash tournament which will
start Thursday at Roble Gym.
Graduate, undergraduate, and fac
ulty women may enter the contest.
Anyone wishing to enter must
telephone or see Miss Gertrude
Hawley before tonight. Drawings
will be held tonight and the results
posted on the bulletin board in the
gym on Wednesday.
