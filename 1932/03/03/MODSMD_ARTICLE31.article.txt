# Scholarships Will Be Given Spring Quarter
## 
### 
All University scholarships for
1932-33, except those reserved for
entering graduates, will be awarded
during the spring quarter. Applica
tions for graduate scholarships
should be filed by April 1, for un
dergraduate scholarships by April
15.
Application blanks and further
information may be obtained at the
office of the Committee on Scholar
ships.
