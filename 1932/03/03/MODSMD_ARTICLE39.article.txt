# BAY FOILSWOMEN WILL MEET HERE SATURDAY NIGHT
## 
### 
In the first women's fencing meet
to be held at Stanford in many
years, Indian swordswomen will
compete in the Bay Division
women's open foils championships
Saturday at 8 o'clock in Roble Gym.
The meet is being conducted by the
Amateur Fencers' League of Amer
ica, and the three fencers who place
highest in the final standings will
be eligible for the Pacific Coast
championships.
Marian Terwilliger, who finished
second last year in the coast finals,
heads the list of Stanford women.
She has a good chance to make the
Unitd States Olympic team.
