# PARENTS, HOME OF WORLD-FAMOUS LINDBERGH BABY WHO WAS KIDNAPED
## 
### 

Charles Augustus Lindbergh, Jr., 20 months old. was kidnaped from the Lindbergh home in Hopewell. New Jersey (pictured above),
between 7:30 and 10 o'clock Tuesday night. Col. Lindbergh and his wife, Anne Morrow Lindbergh, are shown with their home. The baby was
kidnaped from the nursery through an open window. He was carried away in a speeding car, believed to have been a stolen green Chrysler
sedan seen in the vicinity of the house early in the evening.
