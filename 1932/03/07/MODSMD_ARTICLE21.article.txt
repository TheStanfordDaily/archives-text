# WOMEN VOTE FUNDS FOR GYM EQUIPMENT
## 
### 
Purchases of equipment for the
Roble Gymnasium, which are soon
to be made, were financially covered
at a recent meeting of the Women's
Gym Committee, announces Jane
Snyder, chairman.
Furnishings for the coaches's of
fices; cots, mattresses, blankets,
covers, and pillows for the rest
room; plates and silverware for the
kitchenette; and a ping pong set, a
quoits set, and other games for
recreation purposes will be ac
quired.
The above improvements are only
part of many more that are planned
for the women's gymnasium. Fur
ther expenditures will be made
when more money can be voted.
