# DRIFTS MAROON TRAINS IN ROCKIES
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo.
A powerful rotary snow plow, pushed by three locomotives, is
shown bucking 20-foot drifts to go to the rescue of passenger trains
marooned by a blizzard in the Rockies of Colorado. Passengers on
the marooned trains lived on turkey stew made with water from
snow and an express shipment of turkeys.
