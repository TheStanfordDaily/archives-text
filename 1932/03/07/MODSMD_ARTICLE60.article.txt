# ACTRESS QUIETS CROWD IN FAMOUS THEATER FIRE
## 
### 

Associated Press Plioto
New York's Palace theater, the goal of every vaudeville actor, was damaged by a fire that made a
wreck of the rear of the stage. The 1,700 patrons of the theater escaped quietly from the house. Sophie
Tucker, billed as "the last of the red hot mammas," had just finished her turn when the blaze started
She stayed before the footlights and her gay chatter helped to quiet the crowd. This picture shows the
damage backstage.
