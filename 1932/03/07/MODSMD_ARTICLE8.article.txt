# DR. EELLS BACK FROM JAYSEE CONVENTION
## 
### 
Dr. Walter C. Eells of the School
of Education has returned from the
East, where he attended the na
tional junior college convention in
Richmond, Virginia, and visited
junior colleges in Texas, Tennessee,
Virginia, and Washington, D.C.
He also represented Stanford
University at the convocation of the
George Washington University in
Washington, DC., and spoke at the
Educational Press Association in
that city.
