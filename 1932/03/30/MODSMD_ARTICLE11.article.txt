# Sunday Excursion To Monterey Scheduled
## 
### 
An all-expense, one-day trip be
tween Palo Alto and points on Mon
terey Bay has been announced for
Sunday, April 3, by C. A. Stone,
local passenger agent for the South
ern Pacific Company.
The charge will include the round
trip fare between Del Monte and
Palo Alto, a motor trip over the
J7 Mile Drive, lunch at the Del
Monte Hotel, admission to the
steeplechase meet scheduled for
Sunday afternoon at Del Monte,
and transportation between the
hotel and the race track.
The excursion train will leave
Palo Alto at 8:25 a.m. and return at
7:55 p.m.
