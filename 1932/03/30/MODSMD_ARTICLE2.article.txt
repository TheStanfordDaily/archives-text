# Y.W. To Rent Texts For Spring Quarter
## 
### 
Text and reference books for a
majority of the courses being given
this quarter may be rented from the
Y.W.C.A. library at 25 cents each
for the quarter. Among the books
of which there is a large supply are
those for citizenship, ethics, eco
nomics, mathematics, biology, his
tory, education, political science,
classical literature, psychology,
philosophy, and readers used in
German, French, and Spanish
coji rses.
Rental of these books may be
made through Anna Louise Aynes
worth, librarian for the Y.W.C.A.,
or through Mrs. Ben Allen, secre
tary, at the latter's office on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
