# GRAVES, '34, SAVES 2 LIVES
## 
### 
Circumstances made Gordon
Graves, '34, Kappa Alpha and a
member of the Stanford swimming
team, a hero last Saturday, when
he saved two young girls from prob
able death by drowning at Santa
Cruz. The two girls, about 13 years
old, were caught under a pier by a
rise in the tide. Graves, attracted
by frantic screams, pulled them out.
Occupants of a motor car which
turned over six times after leaving
a road near Springfield,Mo.,emerged
unscathed.
