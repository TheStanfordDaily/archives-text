# EIGHT TEAMS STILL PLAYING AS MURAL TENNIS NEARS END
## 
### 
With play in the lower bracket
already completed, the intramural
tennis tournament is gradually
drawing to a close. Sequoia I, with
victories over Phi Kappa Sigma,
Sigma Nu, Sequoia 111, and Theta
Delta Chi, has won its way to the
finals and will meet the team that
comes out on top in the upper divi
sion of the pairings.
There are several matches yet to
be played off in the upper brack
ets. Sigma Chi will meet El Campo,
the winner to tackle Breakers in the
semi-finals. Delta Tau Delta has a
match to play with Chi Psi, and the
victor in this encounter will pair up
with Sequoia 11.
