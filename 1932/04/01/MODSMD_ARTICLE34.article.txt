# GIRL BANDIT SENTENCED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. March 31. (£>)—
! Bernice Parker, 23, dance hall girl,
| who sat in an automobile last Feb
! ruary while two men robbed a mar
ket of $23, was sentenced to a term
of five years to life in San Quentin
1 prison today.

In order of population the largest
| cities in Italy are Milan, Naples,
! Rome, Turin Palermo, Genoa, Flor
| ence, Catania, Trieste and Venice.
Gandhi was known as the best
| dressed man when at Oxford.
