# Debaters Will Meet Pomona On April 7
## 
### 
Debating for the last time in the
current school year on a Stanford
platform, the Stanford men's varsity
debate squad will oppose represent
atives of Pomona College in the
Women's Clubhouse on Thursday
evening, April 7, at 8 o'clock.
The question to be debated is:
'"Resolved, that the Republican Party
is a major cause of the present eco
nomic crisis." Stanford will defend
the Republican party.
