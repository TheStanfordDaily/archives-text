# COPENHAZER WILL SPEAK AT Y.W.C.A. DINNER WEDNESDAY
## 
### 
At the Y.W.C.A. open membership
dinner next Wednesday night at 6:15
in the Women's Clubhouse Miss El
eanor Copenhazer, who is industrial
assistant to the National Services
Board of New York City, will speak
in the place of Miss II Macrae, as
was previously announced.
As Miss Copenhazer is a noted
speaker, the Y.W.C.A. is exceedingly
fortunate in being able to obtain her.
She will speak on "College Women
in the Community," and after the
talk will discuss with the women
phases of social service work.
The Social Service Committee,
headed by Margaret Abrams, will
put on a skit immediately after the
talk. This is the fir I open meeting
and dinner combined which the
Y.W.C.A. has had, bui it intends to
make this a quarterly practice.
Tickets can be bought at any of the
living groups or at the Y.W.C.A.
headquarters in the Women's Club
House for 35 cents each. All tickets
must be bought by Tuesday after
noon.
