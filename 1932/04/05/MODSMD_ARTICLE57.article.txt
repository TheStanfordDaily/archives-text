# Famed Organic Chemist Offers Two Unit Course
## 
### 
Professor C. K. Ingold, D.Sc.,
F.R.S., of the University of London,
well known in the field of organic
chemistry, is visiting the University
this quarter.
Professor Ingold was forced to
come to California for his health
and while he is here he will give a ;
two-unit course in organic reactions !
on Mondays and Tuesdays at ten. '
It is expected that he will give an
open lecture later in the quarter
but the exact date is not known yet.
