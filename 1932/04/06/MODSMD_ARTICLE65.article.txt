# GRAD BECOMES PROF
## 
### 
A Stanford graduate will return
next fall as a professor in the
Graduate School of Business. David
E. Faville, who graduated with dis
tinction from here in '22 and re
ceived his M.B.A. from Harvard in
'25, will come here as professor of
marketing. He has been dean of the
School of Business Administration
and director of business research for
four years at the University of
Oregon.
