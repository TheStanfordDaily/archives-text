# Stanford Dames Hold First Meet Of Quarter
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will meet for
the first time during spring quarter
this afternoon at 3 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
A complete musical program has
been planned consisting of a group
of violin selections by Mrs. H. C.
Smith, several piano solos by Jean
StcfTens, and vocal solos by Mr.
Francis Michener.
