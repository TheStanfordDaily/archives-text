# Date Of Sigma Delta Chi Initiation Decided
## 
### 
Pledges to Sigma Delta Chi, a pro
[ fessional journalistic society, will
lace the ordeal of initiation on the
afternoon of Thursday, April 14, the
organization decided at their meet
ing yesterday. Plans for the affair
were also made at that time, which
will include a banquet after the ini
tiation.
Both members and pledges are to
pose for the camera at 1 o'clock on
Monday, April 11, at the Union
i court.
