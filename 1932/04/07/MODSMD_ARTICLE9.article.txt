# DELEGATES OF "500" LEAVE TO ATTEND ATHLETIC CONCLAVE
## 
### 
Margaret McDougall and Betty
Lewton left the campus last night
to attend a regional convention of
the Athletic Conference of American
College Women which will be held
at the University of Southern Cali
fornia.
The convention will start tonight,
continuing until Saturday night.
Representatives from women's ath
letic associations in California, Ne
vada, and Arizona are to attend.
Athletic policies for college
women, including such topics and
problems as are encountered in
play-days, sports, finances, and the
place of W.A.A. on the campus will
be discussed in round-table meet
ings. Any decisions reached arc re
ferred to the national convention
held every three years, and then in
corporated in its recommendations.
