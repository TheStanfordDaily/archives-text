DIPLOMATIC AND CONSULAR
SERVICE
Examinations will be held in Sep
tember to fill vacancies in the Consular
and Diplomatic Scrvice.
Since 1907 we have specialized in
preparing candidates for the Foreign
Service.
32 of the 34 passing the last exami
nation attended this school.
Courses start in June to prepare for
the September Examinations.
