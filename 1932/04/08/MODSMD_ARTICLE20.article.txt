# Deadline For Baseball Sign-Ups Set For Today
## 
### 
With intramural baseball leagues
scheduled to swing into action
sometime next week, today has
been definitely set as final for all
organizations to sign up for either
the hardball or indoor leagues, but
not both.
The soft ball games will be held
on the freshman and Gray football
fields, while the hardball encounters
will bo run off on the varsity and
freshman baseball fields, with the
space between the baseball fields and
the corporation yards being used as
an auxiliary. Games will begin at
4 o'clock and will be of seven in
nings length.
The list of ineligible men will be
posted in the near future.
