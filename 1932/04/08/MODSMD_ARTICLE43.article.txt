# Card Frosh Golfers To Meet Menlo J.C.
## 
### 
Menlo Junior College will furnish
the opposition for the Stanford
freshman golfers tomorrow morning
on the local course.
The match will be a best ball
foursome affair, with five encounters
.scheduled. Graves, Holle, Richard
son, Roberts, Cox, Allen, Hill, Sc'ne
line, Brock, and either McCaffee or
Kencht will represent '35.
In a similar contest last week,
with the Polytechnic golfers, the
frosh split even, 2-2.
