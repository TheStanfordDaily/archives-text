# COAST LEAGUE SEASON GETS GOING
## 
### 


Two action pictures of opening games in the Pacific Coast League
which show (top) Dressen, Seattle third baseman, hitting the first ball
out to Seal Stadium's centerfield. San Francisco won this one, 2-1.
Lower: Monroe, Portland second baseman, scoring the season's first
run against Los Angeles, but Portland lost, 14-11.
