## 
### 
Results on the Panhellenic voting
held last night to decide the or
ganization's president for the com
ing year show a tally giving Barbara

three, and Roberta Tempest two
votes.
Next Monday the sororities will
revote on the two receiving the
highest number in this election and
the one winning will become presi

position of vice-president. Roberta
Tempest automatically succeeds Lu
