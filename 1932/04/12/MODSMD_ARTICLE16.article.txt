# Visiting Geologist To Lecture On Faults
## 
### 
Chester W. Washburne, of New
York, will give a special lecture on
faults in Room 334 of the Geology
Building at 4:10 o'clock this after
noon. Washburn has been associated
with the late J. S. Diller in the
work of the U.S. Geological Survey
in Oregon and northern California.
Recently he has been a consulting
geologist in South America, Asia,
and other parts of the world, making
the subject of faulting his primary
interest.
