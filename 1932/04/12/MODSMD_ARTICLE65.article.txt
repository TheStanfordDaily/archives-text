# GLIDING BOAT TO TRY ATLANTIC
## 
### 

This gliding boat, resembling a huge mechanical water-bug, Is the
Invention of Herr Nicholas O. Bellin, Berlin engineer, who says he will
attempt to negotiate an Atlantic crossing from Liverpool to New York
in 70 hours. The motor is 267 horsepower. .The wa s mad
at Potsdam, Germany.
