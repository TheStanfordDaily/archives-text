# Y.W.C.A. To Sponsor Tea For German Department
## 
### 
Members of the Germanic lan
guage department will be guests of
honor at this week's departmental
tea being sponsored by the Y.W.C.A.
in their rooms on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse from 4 to
G o'clock tomorrow afternoon.
Professors Cooper and Reinhardt,
and the instructors in the depart
ment, Sharp, Sokol, Strothmann,
Byrne, Cl.irk. Eberhard, Nye, and
Klaustermeyer, have been issued
special invitations. All students, ma
jors, and correctors in the depart
ment are also invited to be present
at the tea.
