# GRAY LECTURE ON SHAKESPEARE SET FOR MARK HOPKINS
## 
### 
Professor Henry David Gray, of
the English department, will speak
on "Shakespeare: A Person," at the
meeting o fthe California Branch of
the English-Speaking Union, to be
held at the Mark Hopkins Hotel on
Thursday, April 21, at 12:15 o'clock.
The characterization of Shakes
peare as a personality is something
which has not been done before.
Professor Gray hopes to bring him
to life for the modern age as an
interesting man as well as a master
dramatist.
Reservations for the luncheon and
lecture may be made by obtaining a
reservation card at the office of the
English department. The charge will
be $1.25 per person.
