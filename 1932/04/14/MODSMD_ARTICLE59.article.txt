# GERMAN SOCIETY MEETS
## 
### 
For its first meeting in spring
squarter the German Honor Society
will meet at the home of Dr. W. F.
Strothmann today at 4 o'clock. The
program will consist of a comic
reading of the Third Part of "Faust"
by Walter Milthaler, and a recita
tion by Robert Clark.
George S. Bond, 5 feet 6 inches
tall and weighing 125 pounds, and
W. P. Fullington, 6 feet 6, weight
275, were opposing candidates for
county judge at Crosbyton, Tex.
