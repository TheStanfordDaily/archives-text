# CHILD SHOOTS SELF
## 
### 
PASO ROBLES, April 13. (/P)—
Mary, 7-year-old daughter of Mr.
and Mrs. J. T. McCann, accidentally
shot herself through the right breast
and heart yesterday evening while
playing with a .22 rifle in a shed at
her home near here.
