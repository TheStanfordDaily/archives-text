# [not clear] Game Ducat Price Remains At Five Dollars
## 
### 
The price of tickets for the Big
Game next fall will remain at $5,
it was announced yesterday by the
executive council of the Associated
dents of the University of Cali
fornia.
Most of the California games have
been reduced from $2 to $1, but the
Big Game remains at $5, the St.
Mary's game at $2, and the game
in Washington State has been only
reduced to $1.50. A 5 per cent cut
to all salaries, except those of
coaches under contract, was also
approved.
