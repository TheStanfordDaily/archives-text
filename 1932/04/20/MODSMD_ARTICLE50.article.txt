# STUDENT STRIKE ENDS IN RIOT AT COLUMBIA
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
A series of clashes and fist fights broke out on the Columbia university campus when a group of
students called a strike in protest against the expulsion of Reed Harris, former editor of the university
paper. Athletes, aroused by Harris' editorials hitting the school's athletics, led the fight against tha
strikers. The picture above shows a striker in the hands of a group of football huskies.
