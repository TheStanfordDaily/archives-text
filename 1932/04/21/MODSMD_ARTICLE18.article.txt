# CHESSMEN TO HOLD TOURNAMENT; OPEN FOR ALL STUDENTS
## 
### 
Chess enthusiasts may still sign
up for the all-University tournament
sponsored by the Cardinal Chess
Club, according to Winston Strong,
manager. Point scores are to be
kept and the person rating highest
will be the winner. A win counts
one point and a draw is to count
one-half.
A tournament for freshmen also
was announced last night and mem
bers of the class of '35 of either sex
are invited to participate. All per
sons interested should register on
the sign-up list in the Encina Lobby
or see Bob Schneider, Room 110, En
cina.

Chess Club officers were chosen:
president, Angelo May; and sccre
tary-treasurer, Andy Harris.
