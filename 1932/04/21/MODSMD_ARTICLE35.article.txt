# ENGINEERS TO GIVE DANCE PROFITS TO EMPLOYMENT FUND
## 
### 
Any profits from the first all-Uni
versity Engineers' Dance to be given
Friday, April 29, in the Women's
Gym, will be donated to the
Students' Emergency Employment
Fund, Cyril Thomas, committee
chairman, announced yesterday.
Plans for the most interesting dec
orations ever seen at a University
dance are being formulated. With
engineering as the theme, machines
from the various laboratories will be
featured.
The committee in charge, headed
by Thomas, consists of Julian Silli
man, Edith NefT, Ronald Born, Eliot
Stoner, Edward Soares, and Ben
Frost. All of the campus engineer
ing societies are represented.
