# FORMER STUDENTS PRAISE CUBBERLEY IN RECENT ARTICLE
## 
### 
Occupying the first few pages of
the April issue of School Executives
Magazine is an article entitled
"Cubberley—As His Friends Know
Him." The characterization of Dr. •
Ellwood P. Cubberley is composed
of the recollections and observations
of men, now successful educators
themselves, who studied at Stanford
under him.
In its editorial columns the maga
zine observes:
'"Any adequate appraisal of the
leadership in American education in
the epochal first three decades of
this century must include the name
of Cubberley among the half-dozen
or so creative minds of the period. |
