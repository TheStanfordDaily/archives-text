# ANNUAL MUSICALE TO BE PRESENTED BY TOYON MAY 8
## 
### 
Toyon Club will present its an
nual Spring Musicale in the lobby
of Toyon Hall on the evening of
Sunday, May 8, it was announced
yesterday by George T. Stump,
president of Toyon Club.
Among the artists appearing in
the recital will bo Otto Allen Will,
flutist in the Stanford Band. Will is
one of the foremost collegiate flut
ists in the United States. Other .solo
ists have not yet been announced.
