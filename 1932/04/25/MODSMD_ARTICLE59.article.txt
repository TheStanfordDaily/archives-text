# GRIDIRON HERO IN CAMPUS ROMANCE
## 
### 

Associated Press Photo
When Dallas Marvil, tackle and captain of Northwestern univer
sity's 1931 football team, goes to the coast this summer to coach at San
Francisco university, he will be accompanied by Dorothy Florence Ames
of LaGrange, lll.—but her name will be Mrs. Marvil then. The wedding
is set for June.
