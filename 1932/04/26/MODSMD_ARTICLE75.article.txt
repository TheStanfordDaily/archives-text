# Toyon Names May 6 As Date For Spring Dance
## 
### 
With the urge of spring dances
hanging over the campus, Toyon is
the latest hall to announce its Sport
Informal dance.
The dance will be held on Friday,
May 6, with dancing from 9 to 1
o'clock. Chairman Russell McDonald
announces that the sale of bids will
commence in Rooms 222 and 237
Toyon, and also at the A.S.S.U. of
fice tomorrow afternoon. The price
per couple will be $2.50.
Later details of the dance, its
theme, and the excellent music and
entertainment of a well-known or
chestra will be announced at an
early date.
