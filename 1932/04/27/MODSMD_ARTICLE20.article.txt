# STEAMSHIP RATES CUT
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 2(5. (/P)
—Cuts of ten per cent or more in
water passenger rates from the
Pacific Coast to Europe were made
effective today by nine lines oper
ating seventy ships in the Pacific
Coast-Europe Conference, the com
panies announced.
