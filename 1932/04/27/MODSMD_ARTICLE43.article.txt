# TORNADO HITS GEORGIA
## 
### 
NASHVILLE. Ga., April 26. (/P)—
A tornado dipped into a farming
community seven miles southeast of
here today and killed five persons,
injured several others, and de
stroyed three homes. The victims
were Mrs. Everett Gaskins and two
children, the young son of Clarence
Padgett and the small son of Joe
Harrell.
