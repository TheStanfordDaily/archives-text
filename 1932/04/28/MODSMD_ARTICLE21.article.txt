# Memorial Church To Have Archibald Sinclair Sunday
## 
### 
The special preacher at the Me
morial Church for the next two
Sundays will be the Reverend
Archibald G. Sinclair, minister of
the First Presbyterian Church,
Bloomfield, New Jersey.
Dr. Sinclair is a graduate of Tor
onto University, Canada. He took
postgraduate work in Edinburgh,
Berlin, and Heidelberg, receiving his
Ph.D. in philosophy from Ihe Uni
versity of Heidelberg and his doc
tor's degree in theology in Toronto.
He was for many years missionary
in Ihe Klondike and later had
charge of a large church in Win
nipeg.
