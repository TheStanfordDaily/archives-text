# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
5 3
THURSDAY
12 noon —Spanish Club will meet in
the Union dining hall.
4 p.m.—Theta Sigma Phi meeting in
the Press Building.
4:30 p.m.—Fellowship tea. "Leisure
Hour" in the Women's Clubhouse.
Faculty and students are invited.
5:10 p.m.—Organ recital in Memorial
Church by Charles Safford, acting
University organist.
7:30 p.m.—Christian Science Club
will meet in the Women's Club
house.
8 p.m.—Women's Debate, Stanford
vs. U.S.C., in the Little Theatre.
Subject impromptu.
MISCELLANEOUS
All sophomores who are inter
ested in playing interclass tennis
will see Chuck Otis in 145 Toyon
before Thursday. Lettermen are eli
gible for competition.

All sophomores who are interested
in playing interclass tennis will see
Chuck Otis in 145 Toyon before
Thursday. Lettermen are eligible for
competition.
