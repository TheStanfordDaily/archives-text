# HERE AND THERE WITH THE NEWS CAMERA
## STRIKE SYMPATHIZERS MARCH ON OHIO MINE 
###

ing a riot in which one man was killed and scores hurt. Here is pact of the crowd gathered on a road
leading to the mine. A similar riot near the Harmon creek mine resulted in a second death.
