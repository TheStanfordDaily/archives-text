# Tuesday Evening Series To Present Danish Film
## 
### 
Illustrating the life of Denmark,
| and centers of art, trade, and com
merce, The Public Exercises Com
mittee will present an official Dan
ish film Tuesday evening in the
Assembly Hall at 7:30 o'clock. The
pictures, which are among a group
shown by the Extension Division of
the University of California, will
take the place of the regular Tues
day evening lecture.
