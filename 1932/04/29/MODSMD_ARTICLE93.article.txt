# Y.W.C.A. TO SERVE ANNUAL BREAKFAST ON MONDAY, MAY 2
## 
### 
Strawberries heaped high, whip
ped cream, hot coffee, will feature
the annual May breakfast to be
given under the auspices of the
Y.W.C.A. next Monday, May 2,
from 7 to 12 o'clock in the Union
Court.
Tables will be arranged around
the fountain in the Union Court,
where individuals or parties will be
served by members of the various
committees of the Y.W.C.A.
Tickets for the event may be pur
chased at any of the women's liv
ing groups, the Women's Clubhouse,
or at the entrance to the court, on
Monday, for 35 cents each.
Flans to make this the biggest
and best May breakfast in the his
tory of Stanford have been com
pleted by Kay Spaulding, president
of the "Y," assisted by the cabinet
and Mrs. Ben S. Allen, secretary.
