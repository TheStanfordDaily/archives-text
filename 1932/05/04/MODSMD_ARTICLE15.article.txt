# BRANNER DANCE
## 
###

mal dance on the night of May 27,
according to an announcement by
Lawrence Wallace, dance chairman.
Gordon Heche's orchestra will pre
side. A night club idea will be the
theme for the dance, it was stated,
and it is planned to have enter
tainers for the occasion.
