# Tentative List Of Candidates For B. A. Issued

students declaring their candidacy
for the degree of Bachelor of Arts
in June 1932 named, the second
provisional list of candidates was
announced by the Registrar's office
yesterday afternoon.

on which applications for grada

whose names should appear of! the
