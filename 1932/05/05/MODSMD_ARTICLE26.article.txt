# Bids On Sale At A.S.S.U. Office For Toyon Dance
## 
### 
Bids for Toyon Club's annual
spring yport dance, to be held Fri
day evening from 9 to 1 o'clock, are
on sale in Rooms 222 and 237 Toyon
Hall, and at the A.S.S.U. office.
The bids, which sell for $2.50, are
already in much demand. Russ Mc-
Donald, chairman of the dance com
mittee, announces that plans feature
lighting by Gordon, music by Heche,
and several novelty numbers .
