# ATTENTION, SENIORS!
## 
###

tivities being carried on by the class and the plans for the
last approaching Senior Week will have an opportunity to
hear a complete explanation of the program at the class
meeting tonight in Room 21 1 at o'clock.
President Tarr urges all who contemplate graduating
this spring to attend ihat they may have clearly in mind
the pleasures and obligations in store for them.

given each senior, not for the purpose of peeping, AVinchell
like, into the affairs of individuals, but to obtain the
opinion of this group thai lias been exposed to four years
of higher education on questions of general interest.
The Daily sincerely hopes that the seniors will till out
the questionnaires with regard for accuracy that the result
mn\ have some foundation in truth.
