# Naval War With Japan Hinted By U.S. Senators
## 
### 
GENEVA. May 5. (/P)- A future
naval war between the United States
and Japan loomed as a possibility
today following a speech by Senator
Claude A. Swanson of Virginia in
which he told Ihe naval commission
of the disarmament conference that
the United States would establish a
powerful submarine base on the
Philippine Islands if the submarine
was not abolished by the conference.
