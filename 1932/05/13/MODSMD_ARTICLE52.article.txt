# Gardner Will Preach At Memorial Church Sunday
## 
### 
The sermon in the Memorial
Church at 11 o'clock Sunday morn
ing will be preached by the Rever
end D. Charles Gardner, Chaplain.
The choir will sing "God Is a
Spirit" by Bennett and "Behold the
Lamb of God" by Handel.
Immediately after the service
there will be a celebration of Holy
Communion.
