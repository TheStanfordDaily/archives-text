# BIDS FOR BRANNER NIGHT CLUB DANCE GO ON SALE TODAY
## 
### 
Planning one of the novel affairs
of the social year, Branner Club an
nounces that bids for its annual
Night Club Dance go on sale today
at the A.S.S.U. office and Room 305
Branner. The dance is to be held in
the Hall on Friday, May 27.
Early purchasers of bids, which
sell for $2 per couple, will have a
decided advantage, as they will be
first in line for table reservations.
In addition to Heche's orchestra,
Dance Committee Chairman Law
rence Wallace has arranged a pro
gram of entertainers that includes
the noted Cardinal quartet.
