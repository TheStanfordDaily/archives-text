# ROBLE OPEN HOUSE
## 
### 
Roble entertained with an open
house yesterday afternoon from 3:30
to 5 o'clock. Refreshments were
served under the direction of a
committee headed by Ruth Kofahl.
The reception committee consisted
of Margaret Abrams, Helen Steve
not, and Elizabeth McCollum.

Students at the University of
Detroit want more co-eds in classes
or none at all. This was the an
nouncement made by the Varsity
News, the weekly students' publica
tion. The university now has an
enrollment of 3,000 of whom but
77 are women.
