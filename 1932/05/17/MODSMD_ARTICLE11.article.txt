# A.S.S.U. SELECTS NOMINEES FOR 24 JOBS TODAY
## Blanks For Nomination Are Available Today at 8; Due Tomorrow 
### 
Opening this morning at 8 o'clock,
nominations for A.S.S.U. posts will
continue today and tomorrow, with
24 offices to be filled.
Nominating blanks will be avail
able on the Quad, near the Law
Steps, from 8 o'clock in the morn
ing to 5:30 o'clock in the afternoon
of both days. Fifteen signatures are
necessary on each blank.
In answer to a query prevalent
throughout the student body, it is
not necessary that a student be a
member of the same class as the
candidate whom he desires to pro
pose for an office, according to Ray
L. Wilbur, Jr., election board chair
man.
Offices open to nomination are:
A.S.S.U. — President, vice-presi
dent, and secretary.
Executive Committee—One grad
uate member, one senior woman,
one junior woman, one junior man,
and one sophomore man. (It is
pointed out that these candidates
must be members next year of the
classes which they are nominated to
represent.)
Board of Athletic Control — One
member.
Men's Council—Three members
for the long term —one year. (To
be eligible for Men's Council, can
didates must have had 120 hours
of University work.)
Classes of '33, '34, and '35—Presi
dent, vice-president, secretary, and
treasurer.
