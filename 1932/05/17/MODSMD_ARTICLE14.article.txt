# Mothers' Club Will Hear Student Leaders Today
## 
### 
Heads of various student organi
zations on will address
the Stanford Mothers' Club at its
monthly meeting this afternoon in
Toyon Hall at 2:30 o'clock.
Following the meeting at Toyon
members of the club will go to En
cina Commons to inspect the kit
chen and dining rooms. Tea is to
be served to them in the court out
side the dining rooms. Open house
will be held at both Toyon and En
ema halls so that the Mothers' Club
may visit the student rooms in the
halls.
