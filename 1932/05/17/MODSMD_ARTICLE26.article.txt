# individual Quad Photos Ready For Distribution
## 
### 
Individual pictures taken in the
fall for the Quad are now back
from the engraver's and may be ob
tained in the yearbook's business
office for 25 cents. Prints may be
secured during the afternoon.
Not only may one's own picture
be obtained, but also that of any
other individual in the University
whose picture has not yet been
asked for. Pictures of all living
groups are now ready for disposal.
