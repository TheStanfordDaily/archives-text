# Stager Leaves To Attend Bookstore Men's Meet
## 
### 
As one of the delegates from the
Western States, R. M. Stager, of the
Stanford Bookstore, has gone to
New York to attend the annual na
tional convention of college book
store managers.
At the conference Stager will
compare the methods, merchandise,
and prices of the campus bookstore
with those of colleges all over the
United States. He is to meet repre
sentatives of manufacturers, and
may return with lower prices and
better goods for next year.
Members of this association buy
collectively and decide at these an
nual conferences what brands of
goods the stores will handle during
the coming year.
