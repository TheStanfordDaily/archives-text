# Life Saving instruction To Be Given On Campus
## 
### 
Men students wishing to become
examiners in the life saving service
of the Red Cross may get instruc
tion from 10:15 to 11:05 o'clock Mon
day, Wednesday, and Friday morn
ings and from 3:30 to 5:30 o'clock
| every Wednesday at meetings of the
Stanford Life Saving Corps, Felix
Juda, president, announced yester
j day.
MUNICH, Germany, May 16. (/P)
—Five were killed and five badly
injured in a series of mountaineer
! ing accidents in the Bavarian Alps
) today.
