# Mrs. Porter Made Head In Stanford Dames' Vote
## 
### 
Mrs. Howard Porter was elected
president of the Stanford Dames for
the summer quarter at the meeting
of the organization yesterday after
noon. Others chosen were Mrs.
Homer King, vice-president; Mrs.
L. Brown, secretary; Mrs. David
Votaw, corresponding secretary; and
Mrs. A. R. Giese, treasurer.
This was the last regular meet
ing for the quarter. Mrs. J. B. Sears
sang a group of solos and the rest
of the hour was spent as a social
hour.
