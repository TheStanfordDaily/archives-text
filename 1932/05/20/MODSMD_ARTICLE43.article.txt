# W.A.A. Invites Students, Faculty To Open House
## 
### 
Both students and faculty mem
bers will be guests of the Women's
Athletic Association open house in
the new Roble Gymnasium to be
held tomorrow afternoon from 3 to
6 o'clock.
Exhibitions of swimming will be
given at 3:30, fencing at 4:15, and
dancing at 5 o'clock, announces
Elaine Dear, chairman of the com
mittee in charge. Members of W.A.A.
Board and Women's "S" Society
will show visitors through the gym
nasium all afternoon.
