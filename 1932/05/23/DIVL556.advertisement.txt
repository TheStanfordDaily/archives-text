Summer Meals
In a quiet attractive dining room the
Stanford Union will serve summer
meals at special board rates. The
equipment of the Union is such that
it can take care of your eating needs
perfectly.
FRENCH AND SPANISH TABLES
Special tables for Spanish and French
conversation, five days a week at spe
cial board rates will be led by natives
of Spain and Rrance.
CAFETERIA SERVICE
The cafeteria will feature meals for
35c. Breakfast, lunch, dinner.
"CELLAR"
Soda Fountain and Coffee Shop Service
continuous.
