# QUAD SALES TO END ON FRIDAY, MAY 27
## 
### 
With the date of distribution set
at June 1, sale of the 1932 Quad
will enter its last week today. Sales
men will be stationed at the En
gineering corner from 8 o'clock to
noon to receive orders or final pay
ments on deposit slips.
Quad officials reiterale the state
ment that Quads will be delivered
on June 1 and 2 only to those pos
sessing fully-paid receipts and no
Quads may be purchased or paid in
full at the distribution counter dur
ing those two first days.
