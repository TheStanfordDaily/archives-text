# Latin America Society Fetes Brazilian Council
## 
### 
The Brazilian council in San
Francisco, the Honorable Pedro de
Alcantara Nabuco de Abreu, and
his wife will be guests of honor at
a dinner given by the Latin Amer
ica Club on Thursday night. The
dinner will be held in the Union
dining room at 6:30 o'clock.
After the dinner an informal
meeting will be held in the Women's
Clubhouse where the council will
speak on conditions in modern Bra
zil followed by an open forum dis
cussion, This meeting is open to all
who are interested and reservations
are to be made.
