# MITCHELL STARS IN GERMAN CLASS ONE ACT PLAY BY THOMA
## 
### 
A one-act German comedy, "Die
Kleinen Verwandten" (The Poor
Relations), by Ludwig Thoma was
presented yesterday afternoon by
the second year German class.
Claire Mitchell, outstanding in her
portrayal of the leading comedy
character, headed the cast. Other
players who took part include Alex
Scheer, Ben De Roy, Dorothy Whea
ton, James Beckerley, and Margaret
Loeb.
Preceding the play, Don Morgan
rendered vocal selections, accom
panied by Girvin Whitney. This was
the last official meeting of the Ger
rrtnn Honor Society of the year, pre
sented under the direction of Karl
Fa lk.
