# Law School Association To Name Officers Today
## 
### 
The Stanford Law School Asso
ciation will elect officers for the
coming year at 4 o'clock this after
noon, according to an announce
ment made today by James Inge
bretson, incumbent.
The election will be held in Room
12 of the Law Building. A presi
dent, secretary, and treasurer will
be elected. Nominees for these po

election time.
