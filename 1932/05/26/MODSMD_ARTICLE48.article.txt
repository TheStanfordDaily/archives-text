# Brazilian Consul To Be Campus Guest Tonight
## 
### 
The Honorable Pedro de Alcan
tara Nabuco, Brazilian consul of
San Francisco, will be the guest of
honor and speaker tonight at the
dinner of the Latin American Club
in the Union dining room. In honor
of the visitor, Charles L. Safford
will feature a Portuguese folksong
in his organ recital this afternoon
at the Memorial Church.
