# MARINES BOMB SANDINO
## 
### 
MANAGUA, Nicaragua, May 25.
(TP)—National Guard headquarters
reported today that United States
marine airmen serving with the
guard killed seventeen Sandinistas
with bombs in an action Monday in
the Department of Prinzapolka.
