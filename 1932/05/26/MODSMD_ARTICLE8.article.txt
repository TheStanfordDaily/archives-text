# B.A.C. Offers Jobs At Olympic Tryouts Here
## 
### 
The Board of Athletic Control is
offering a number of jobs during
the Olympic tryouts to be held here
July 15 and 16. Those holding such
positions will be allowed to see the
games as well as earn a salary. Stu
dents desiring this employment
should leave their names with the
telephone operator at the B.A.C. as
soon as possible.
