# GERMAN SCIENTIST TO BEGIN LECTURE SERIES HERE TODAY
## 
### 
Giving the first of a series of
talks under the auspices of the
mathematics department, Professor
W. Blaschke, of the University of
Hamburg, will speak today in Room
72 at 2:30 o'clock.
The topic of the lecture has not
yet been announced.
This famous German scientist is
in the midst of a lecture tour dur
ing which he has nearly circled the
globe. Since leaving the university
at Hamburg, Germany, he has lec
tured at such places as Calcutta,
Peiping, and in Japan. From here
he will proceed to the University of
Chicago, where he will teach during
the summer session there.
During the next week Professor
Blaschke will give three more lec
tures here.
