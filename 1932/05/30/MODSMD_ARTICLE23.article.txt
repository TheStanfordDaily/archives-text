# STUDENTS HONORED AT FACULTY DINNER
## 
### 
Mrs. Margaret Huston and Dean
Mary Yosl entertained the winners
of undergraduate scholarships for
Stanford for the year 1932-1933 at
an informal dinner Saturday eve
ning at. the home of Mrs. Huston.
More than 30 undergraduate stu
dents were present at the affair.
The recipients of the honor partici
pated in games after the dinner.
