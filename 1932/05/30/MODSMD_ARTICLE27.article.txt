# GARDNER DISCUSSES TEMPTATIONS IN LIFE
## 
### 
Choosing as the topic of his ser
mon in the Memorial Church yes
terday morning, "The Temptation
in Modern Life," the Reverend D.
Charles Gardner said, "The sin is
not in feeling temptation, but in
failing to resist it."
Modern youth, Dr. Gardner said,
does not fully realize the serious
ness of the moral situation.
The Stanford Glee Club sang two
special anthems during the service:
"Hymn of Thanksgiving" by Kiem
ser, and "O God, Who Hast Pre
pared" by Gaul.
