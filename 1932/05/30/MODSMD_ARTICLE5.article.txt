# AKRON SOARS EASTWARD
## 
### 
Below is shown the Navy dirigible Akron, which is slated to leave
her mooring mast at the Sunnyvale base early this morning for an
"unannounced destination." This destination is understood to be the
United States battle force ofT Southern California, where the Akron will
participate in a three-day program of maneuvers. Following the
maneuvers the Akron will return to the East.
