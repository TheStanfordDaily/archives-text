# BOOKSTORE REBATES WILL BE PAID TODAY
## 
### 
Rebates on purchases made at the
Stanford Bookstore between Decem
ber and May 18 may be collected
after 8 o'clock this morrfing at the
office of the Bookstore.
Three per cent on all purchases
of more than fifty cents is to be re
funded to students who deposited
their cash register receipts. Last
fall four per cent was paid, hut the
reduction was found necessary be
cause of decreased sales.
