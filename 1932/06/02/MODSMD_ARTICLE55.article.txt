MISCELLANEOUS
Men now living in Toyon and the
Union who wish to reserve their
rooms for summer quarter should
call at the Dean of Men's office
June 6 or 7, and pay deposits.
Men who wish to apply for tui
tion notes call at Dean of Men's
office between May 30 and June 3.
Reservations may be made at the
Concert Series office for 1932-33
Concert Series season tickets.
Students expecting to enter the
Law School at the beginning of the
summer or autumn quarters should
file applications for admission with
the secretaray of the school in Room
1 of the Law Library immediately.
Students wishing to apply for
tickets to the Southern California
and California games must leave
their addresses at the Board of Ath
letic Control office before leaving
the campus at the end of this quar
ter. Applications will be mailed the
latter part of July.
All men who wish to apply for
tuition notes for summer or fall
quarters are asked to call at the
Dean of Men's office in the Admin
istration Building between May 3ft
and June 3.
