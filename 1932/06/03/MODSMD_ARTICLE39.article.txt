# Winston Strong Elected President Sequoia Club
## 
### 
Winston Strong was last night
elected president of Sequoia Club
for the first term next year. The
ping pong champion won the office
by a small majority over Clair Hill.
Fred Hines was elected vice
president, while Bill Schenk was
made treasurer. A secretary will be
elected at the first of autumn quar
ter. The following committees were
announced: house committee, Jack
Pierce, Howard Russell, Dick Eaton
and Dick Hale; social committee,
Robert Ayre, James Minor, Jim
Seth, Ed Oshier and Frank Zimmer
man; athletic committee, Winston
Strong, Truman Gould and Bill
Nelson.
