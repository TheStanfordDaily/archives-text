# UNIVERSITY ORGANIST RETURNS FROM LEAVE
## 
### 
The University organist, Warren D.
Allen, who, with Mrs. Allen, has just
returned from a five months leave of
absence, hus been visiting organist and
professor of music at Cornell Univer
sity during his absence.
In addition to the regular recitals at
Cornell, Mr. and Mrs. Allen appeared
at the University of California at Los
Angeles, the University of Michigan,
Colgate University, and in St. Louis,
Akron, Philadelphia, and New York
City while they were away from Stan
ford.
