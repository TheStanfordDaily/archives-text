# Stanford Tennis Men Annex Title At Eastern Meet
## Gledhill, Coughlin Win Cup At Intercollegiates 
### 
Stanford's crack doubles team, Cap
tain Keith Gledhill and Joe Coughlin,
won the national intercollegiate tennis
championship last week at Haverford,
Pennsylvania. The Indians defeated
Jack Tidbali and Elbert Lewis of
U.C.L.A. in the final match, G-0, 6-4,
6-1. Gledhill and Coughlin went
through the tournament without los
ing a set in any of their matches.
The singles crown was won by Cliff
Sutter, Tulane star, who held the title
in 1930. Gledhill, 1931 champion, was
eliminated in the semi-finals by Byron
Grant, University of North Carolina,
who was beaten by Sutter, 7-5, 6-2,
6-4, for the title. Coughlin was put out
in the second round of play.
