# Cubberley To Speak At Education Club Luncheon
## 
### 
Dr. E. P. Cubberley, dean of the
School of Education, will speak in
formally at the luncheon meeting of
the Education Club in the Union din
ing room at 12:05 o'clock Wednesday.
Members of the faculty and students
of the School of Education are invited.
Homer P. King, acting president of
the club, announces that these weekly
luncheons will be held regularly.
