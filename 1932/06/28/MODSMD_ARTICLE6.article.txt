# TENNIS COURSES TO DEVELOP TECHNIQUE OPEN FOR WOMEN
## 
### 
Two courses in tennis for women,
intended to develop technique, are be
ing offered this summer by Miss Marie
Manehec.
The mixed class for elementary and
intermediate players will be held at
2 o'clock Mondays, and at 3 o'clock
on Wednesdays and Fridays.
The instruction period for the ad
vanced course will be at 2 o'clock on
Wednesdays. The other two hours re
quired of those in ihe advanced class
may be played off at any time dur
ing the week.
