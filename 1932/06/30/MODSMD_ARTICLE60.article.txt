# POPULAR PLAY TO BE GIVEN AGAIN IN P.A. HIGH SCHOOL TONIGHT
## 
### 
"The Last of Mrs. Cheyney," a com
edy by Frederick Lonsdale, will be re
peated by popular demand in the Palo
Alto High School Little Theatre, at 8
o'clock this evening.
Included in the cast are several
prominent Stanford people. The role
of "Lord Elton" will be played by
Professor Samuel S. Seward of the
English department; "Mrs. Cheyney
by Atha Hillback; "Lord Hilling" by
George Ebey, '29; and "Charles by
Norris James, '26.
