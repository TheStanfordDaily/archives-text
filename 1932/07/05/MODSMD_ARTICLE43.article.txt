# Mrs. J. W. Bingham Chosen Sorority Vice-President
## 
### 
Mrs. Walter Bingham, Stanford '07
and wife of Professor Bingham of the
Law School, was elected first vice
president of the Delta Gamma sorority
at the 26th biennial convention held in
Victoria. British Columbia, last week.
Miss Margaret Winant, New York
City, was chosen as president. The elec
tion of Mrs. Bingham as first vice
president makes her the probable
choice for president in 1933.
