# QUINBY TAKES LEAD AS VILLAGE PLAYERS OFFER "DOVER ROAD"
## 
### 
"The Dover Road," a farce I>y A. A.
Milna and directed by William Pabst,
will be presented by the Village Play
ers at 8:15 o'clock tomorrow evening,
in the Palo Alto Union High School
auditorium.
In the cast of Milne's fantastic com
edy are James Quinby, former Stan
ford favorite, playing the role created
by Gordon Davis here several years
ago; Mrs. Anna Gardner Weyland, also
of Stanford; and Grove Day, who was
in the former production.
Admission is forty cents. Seats are
on sale at Keeble's.
