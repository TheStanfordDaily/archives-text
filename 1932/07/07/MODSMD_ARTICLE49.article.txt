# Former Stanford Captain Wins Monterey Net Title
## 
### 
Lan-y Hall, former Stanford tennis
captain, won the Monterey Peninsula
singles championship this week by de
feating Lloyd Budge in the finals, 6-3,
6-2, 8-6. In the semi-finals Hall elimi
nated another former Indian net cap
tain, Phil Ncer, by a score of 1-6, 6-4,
6-0.

Hall later paired with Wallace Bates
and won the doubles title over Budge
and John Murio.
