# Arctic Explorer Returns From Alaska With Films
## 
### 
Father Bernard R. Hubbard, S.J.,
Santa Clara University geologist who
is known as the "Glacier Priest," will
return from Alaska on August 1.
Father Hubbard has been exploring
unmapped Arctic regions, and is bring
ing back 20,000 feet of motion picture
film of Alaskan volcano districts, the
Unga battlefield, and the buried vil
lages on Unimak island.
