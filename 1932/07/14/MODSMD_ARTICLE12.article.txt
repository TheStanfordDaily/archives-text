# St. Aubyn's Pastor Will Speak In Chapel Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. William James Shergold,
D.D., of the St. Aubyn's Congrega
tional Church, London, England, will
preach in the Memorial Church at 11
o'clock Sunday.
Music will include the following
compositions:
Prelude: "Largo"—Chopin
Introit: "Summer Carol"—Old French
Anthem: "Ave Verum"—Mozart
