# BUY TICKETS FOR OLYMPIC TRIALS NOW; AVOID RUSH
## 
### 
Tickets for the final Olympic
tryouts, to be held in the Stanford
Stadium tomorrow and Saturday,
are on sale at the Board of Ath
letic Control offices.
According to A1 Masters, gen
eral manager, there are no re
served seats, and he advises early
purchase of tickets in order to
avoid standing in line. Students
will be admitted for 25 cents (no
lax) on Friday and $1.10 (includ
ing tax) on Saturday. Student
body cards must be shown when
buying tickets.
Prices for general admission arc
50 cents for Friday and $1.50 on
Saturday, plus 10 per cent federal
tax.
