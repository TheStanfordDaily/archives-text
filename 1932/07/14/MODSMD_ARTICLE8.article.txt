# July 21 Last Chance To Register For Primaries
## 
### 
All voters who have not previously
registered, or who have moved to a
different precinct since the last elec
tion, must register before July 21 if
they wish to qualify for voting in the
August primaries, according to a state
ment made by the Alumni Office yes
terday.
Anyone connected with the Univer
sity may register at the Alumni Office
in Room 174 in the Administration
Building.
