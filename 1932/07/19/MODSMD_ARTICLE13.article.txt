# SECRETARY WILBUR DEDICATES OWYHEE DAM ON WAY HERE
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, Secretary of
the Interior and President of Stanford,
gave the principal address at the dedi
cation-Sunday of Owyhee Dam.
The new dam, which is located at
Nyssa, Oregon, is the highest structure
of its kind in America, and cost
$G,000.000. It will irrigate 125,000 acres
of land and is part of the Vale-Owyhec
$18,000,000 development plan, which
was started four years ago.
Dr. Wilbur is inspecting various I
other Pacific Coast projects, and will :
visit Stanford before returning to
Washington. Mrs. Wilbur is accom
panying him.
