# ANDY KERR, FORMER STANFORD COACH, IS HERE FOR VACATION
## 
### 
Andrew Kerr, former football and
basketball coach at Stanford, is visit
ing with his family at the home of his
mother in Palo Alto.

Kerr came to Stanford in 1923 to in
troduce the Warner system and as
coach that year he was instrumental
in developing Ernie Nevers, who was
then a sophomore. After "Pop's" ar
rival in 1924 "Andy" was made back
field coach and head basketball mentor.
Kerr left Stanford in 192G, going to
Washington and Jefferson University,
and from there to Colgate, where he
is now head football coach.
