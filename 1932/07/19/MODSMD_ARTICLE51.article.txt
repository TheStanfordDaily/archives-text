# Tour To Salinas Rodeo Scheduled For July 23
## 
### 
The second of the tours arranged for
the summer quarter students is a trip
to the Salinas Rodeo, Saturday, July
28. Cars will leave here at 8 o'clock in
the morning, and return about 12 hours
later. The cost of the trip is $4.
Reservations and fare deposits must
be made two days in advance, either
wilh the director of the summer quar
ter, Room 173 Administration Building,
or at the Peninsula Yellow Cab office,
116 Circle, Palo Alto. Further informa
tion may be had at either of these
places.
