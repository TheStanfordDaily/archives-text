# Program Announced For Thursday Organ Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University rogan
ist, will present another of his summer
organ recitals at 4:15 o'clock Thurs
day afternoon in Memorial Church.
The program will be as follows:
Passacaglia and Fugue in C Minor
J. S. Bach
Allegro Vivace from the First
Trio-Sonata J. S. Bach
My Love's an Arbutus
Old Irish Melody
The Night Richard Strauss
Toccata in B Minor. .Augustin Barie
