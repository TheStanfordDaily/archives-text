# SECOND OF SERIES OF MUSIC LECTURES TO BE GIVEN TONIGHT
## 
###

Russia," second of a series of 12 lec
tures by Olin Dowries, music critic, will
be given at 7:30 o'clock Thursday eve
ning in the Little Theatre. Downes'
first lecture dealt with Russia, empha
sizing the part of music in the life of
the people of the Caucasus.

day afternoons and Thursday evenings.

belius, Composer Laureate of Finland
at 4:15 o'clock in the Little Theatre.
