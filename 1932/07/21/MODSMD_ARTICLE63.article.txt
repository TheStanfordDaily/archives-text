# Yale Dean Of Divinity To Preach In Chapel Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Charles R. Brown, dean of
the Divinity School, emeritus, of Yale
University, will preach in the Memorial
Church at 11 o'clock on Sunday. Dean
Brown has appeared many times in
the Memorial Church, and is very
popular wilh Stanford audiences.
Selections to be played at the organ
recital at 7:30 o'clock Sunday night by
Warren D. Allen, University organist,
are:
Prelude: "A Saving Health to Us Is
Brought" Bach
Introit: 'Sanctus" Plain Chant
Choral: "Jesus, Priceless Treasure"
, Bach
Palestine's population has increased
over 30 per cent within ten years.
