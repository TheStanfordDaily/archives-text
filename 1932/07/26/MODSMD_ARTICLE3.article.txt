# Gold Prospecting Lures Rubens to Nevada Fields
## 
### 
Answering the lure of America's last
frontier, Bill Rubens, '32, left for Win
nemucca, Nevada, at dawn this morn
ing to prospect for gold.
News dispatches from the desert city
indicate that gold is being panned in
paying quantities by prospectors there.
Rubens plans to return to the cam
pus within a month. His party merely
hopes to meet expenses with the gold
panned.
