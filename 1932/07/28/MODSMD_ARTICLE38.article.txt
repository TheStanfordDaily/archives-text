# WARNER HELPS IN WHIPPING WESTERN ELEVEN INTO SHAPE
## 
### 
Senior members of the California,
Southern California, and Stanford var
sities will form a western eleven to
play against a Harvard, Yale, and
Princeton aggregation, in the Olympic
Stadium August 8.
Charles Ehrhorn, Mike Reynolds,
Red Hand, Ray Hulen, Pete Heiser, Ed
Marks, Ray Dawson, Phil Noi 11, Phil
Wilson, and Rudy Rintala represent the
Stanford interests. Pop Warner, How
ard Jones, and Bill Ingram will form
the western coaching staff.
