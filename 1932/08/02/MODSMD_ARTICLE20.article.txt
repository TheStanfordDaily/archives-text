# Third Summer Excursion To Visit Big Basin, Trees
## 
### 
The third of the tours arranged by
the Peninsula Yellow Cab Company for
summer quarter students will be a
trip to Big Basin, including a visit to
the Big Trees, on Saturday, August (j.
The cars will leave here at 8 o'clock
in the morning and return at about
5 o'clock in the afternoon. Fares, which
will be $3, must be paid, and reserva
tions made two days in advance. This
can be done at the Yellow Cab office
on the Circle, or at the summer quar
ter office in the Administration Build
ing.
