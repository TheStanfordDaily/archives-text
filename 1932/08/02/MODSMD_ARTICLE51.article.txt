# Collegians To Use Food Products For Tuition Fee
## 
### 
Students at the William Jennings
Bryan University in Dayton, Tennes
see, will be able to pay for their tuition
and board next year with farm pro
duce, canned foods, and other edibles.
Dean S. M. Quigley has announced
that current prices will be paid for all
foodstuffs which can be used in the
co-operative boarding house. Scrip will
be issued in payment, and will be
honored by the university for all fees.
The university was founded as a me
morial to the late "Great Commoner,"
who died in Dayton shortly after the
famous Scopes anti-evolution trial in
1925, in which Bryan assisted the
prosecution.
