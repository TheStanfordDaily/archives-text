# History Of Stanford To Be First Complete Work
## 
### 
Dr. Orrin Leslie Elliott does not ex
pect to finish his history of Stanford
University for some time. The book,
when finished, will be the first to give
complete data on all phases of the
beginnings, growth, and future of the
University.
Included in the work will be a his
tory of the activities of the Stanford
family from the time a University was
thought of, to its present state.
