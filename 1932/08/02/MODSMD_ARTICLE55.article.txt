# SAIL FOR RUSSIA
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. Alan Kinkead sailed
on the Pennland last week for Russia
where Kinkead will have a position in
Moscow. The couple were married
shortly before sailing. Kinkead gradu
ated from Stanford in 1931.

From 1858 to 1928, a period of 70
years, Rio de Janeiro grew from a
population of 0850 to more than a
million.
