# FOR VERTICAL RACE
## 
### 

This White Eagle trophy, made
ol' Carrara marble, lias been posted
by the Aero club of Poland as an
award in a proposed vertical race at
the 1932 National Air races in Cleve
land, Ohio, August 2~ to September 5.
The race will be a mile as straight up
as possible and back to the finish line
in front of the grandstand.
