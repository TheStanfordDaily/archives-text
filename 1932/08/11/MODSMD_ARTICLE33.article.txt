# STANFORD NETMEN BEATEN IN EASTERN GRASS COURT PLAY
## 
### 
Keith Gledhill and Joe Coughlin,
Stanford's touring tennis stars, were
both eliminated in the Eastern grass
court championships at Rye, N.Y.
Gledhill, Card captain and ranking
No. 8 nationally, showed the effects of
too much tennis, and lost out in the
first round Monday to Harold Mac-
Guffin, 6-4, 6-2. Coughlin won his first
match, but was defeated Tuesday in
the second round by Edward Jacobs,
6-2, 6-4.
Jack Tidball and Elbert Lewis,
U.C.L.A. stars who have been showing
well in Eastern tournaments this sum
mer, both advanced to the third round
of play.
