# ROUGHS REJOICE, TOYON SLASHES PRICE OF DANCE
## 
### 
Reduced admission prices were
announced today for roughs at
the Toyon Summer Club "depres
sion dance" to be held Saturday
night.
Roughs will be admitted after
10:30 p.m. for 25 cents, the dance
committee announced. Bids for
couples will be soid at the door
for 75 cents. Dancing is scheduled
to be held from 9 to 12 p.m. Re
freshments will be served.
