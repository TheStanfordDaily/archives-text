# RADIR TO REPLACE BOTTS AS SWIMMING, DANCING INSTRUCTOR
## 
### 
Mrs.' Ruth Radir, who has been in
structing physical education at San
Francisco State Teachers' College, will
replace Mrs. Gretchen Botts as in
structor in dancing and swimming for
women at Stanford.
During Miss Helen Masters Bunt
ing's leave of absence several years
ago, Mrs. Radir acted as director of
physical education for women. She
has also taught in Hawaii, and studied
dancing under Miss Marjorie, D'Houb
ler at the University of Wisconsin.

Mrs. Radii - received her B.A. at Reed
College in Portland, Oregon, and her
Master's in the physical education de
partment at Wellesley College.
