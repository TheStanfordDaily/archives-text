# MYRON ROBERTS TO GIVE ORGAN RECITAL IN MEMORIAL CHURCH
## 
### 
Myron J. Roberts, guest organist, will
present a special organ recital at 4:15
o'clock this afternoon in the Memorial
Church.
The program is as follows:
Tocatta and Adagio J. S. Bach
Chorale-Prelude J. S. Bach
Cantilena Karl McKinley
Ronde francaise Leon Boellmann
Eloile du soil*
Carillon de cWstminster. .Louis Vierne
