# SPECTATCULAR OLYMPIC GAMES RACE
## 
### 

Joe Healey of New York, who won a berth on the United States
track and field team in the Stanford tryouts, won his first heat at the
Olympic Games in the 400-meter hurdles, but was eliminated in the
semi-finals. In this picture Healey is beating two French runners,
Adelheim and Areskog, in the time of 54.2 seconds.
