# Lilienthal Enters Finals Of Summer Net Tourney
## 
### 
Theodore Liiienthal will play the
winner of the Joseph Mendelson-Al
fred Bogan match tomorrow afternoon
for the championship of the summer
singles tournament. Mendelson ad
vanced to play Bogan by defeating
Paul Engelcke, 4-G, G-4, 4-5.
All these men will be in school next
autumn and have a chance to place
on the varsity team. Liiienthal, who
will be a junior, has had two years'
expedience at Menlo Junior College
where he captained the team last year.
