# Jackie Coogan Enrolls At University Of Santa Clara
## 
### 
Jackie Coogan, famous a few years
ago as a child movie actor, has en
rolled at the University of Santa Clara
for the coming year.
Coogan is taking the general arts
course, and living in Kenna Hall, one
of the campus dormitories. He is one
of the 125 freshmen who entered Santa
Clara on Monday.
