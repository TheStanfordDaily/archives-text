# TEN-CENT FARES START TODAY ON CAMPUS-PALY BUS
## 
### 
Ten-cent bus fares go into ef
fect today.
The Peninsular Railway Com
pany has designated Thursday as
Motor Coach Day, when the new
rates and routes will be initiated.
Although it will now cost a dime
to ride from the campus to Palo
Alto, full transfer privileges to all
lines in Palo Alto and Mayfieki
are included. Transfers can also
be made to the interurban electric
cars, each bus fare being good for
ten cents on the train ticket.
Busses will run every 10 min
utes between Stanford and the
Southern Pacific depot during the
day, and on a 12-minute schedule
at night.
