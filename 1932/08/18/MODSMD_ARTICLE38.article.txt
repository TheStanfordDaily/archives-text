# GLEDHILL REGAINS OLD TIME FORM TO ELIMINATE SUTTER
## 
### 
Keith Gledhill, Stanford tennis cap
tain, had sweet revenge for his recent
defeat by Cliff Sutter, when he elimi
nated the Tulane star in the third
round of the Newport Casino tourna
ment, 3-6, 6-4, 6-4. Gledhill won his
first match on Monday by default,
while Joe Coughlin, No. 2 man on the
Indian net squad, lost to Eugene Mc-
Auliff, 6-3, 6-3.

In the doubles, Gledhill paired with
Ellsworth Vines, Davis Cup star, to
win the second-round match from W.
C. Thompson and Charles Greene, 6-2,
6-0. Coughlin and Arnold Jones were
beaten by Jiro Satoh and Takeo Kuwa
bara of Japan, 9-11, 6-4, 7-5.
