# Illustrated Lecture To Be Given By F. E. Fritsch
## 
### 
An illustrated lecture on "The Prob
lem of the Origin of the Higher
Plants," by Professor Felix E. Fritsch,
acting professor of botany, will be
given at 4:15 o'clock tomorrow in the
Little Theatre. Professor Fritsch, who
is from the University of London, has
been at the Hopkins Marine Station
during the" summer. He has been head
of the Department of Botany at Lon
don since 1911. Professor Fritsch is an
authority on algae.
