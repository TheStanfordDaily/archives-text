# THREE LEMOS GIRLS EXHIBIT ART WORK IN PALO ALTO HOME
## 
### 
Art work by the three daughters of
Pedro J. Lemos, director of the Stan
ford Museum and Art Gallery, and
editor of the School Arts Magazine,
was exhibited Saturday afternoon in
their Palo Alto home.
Two of the girls, Misses Margaret
and Esther, have studied under Senor
Crespo, Patti Patterson, and John
Paget-Fredericks, noted artists and de
signers. They have also attended art
schools in Oakland and Los Angeles.
