# Mrs. Lester Henderson To Speak Before Dames
## 
### 
"Personal Reminiscences of Alaska"
will be the subject of a talk to be
given by Mrs. Lester Henderson before
the reading section of the Stanford
Dames, Thursday, August 25. The
meeting will be held at the home of
Mrs. Solon Shedd at 663 San Juan on
the campus at 2:45 o'clock.
