# Swain Returns To Campus After Trinity County Trip
## 
### 
Dr. Robert E. Swain, acting presi
dent of Stanford, returned to the cam
pus yesterday from a fishing trip to
Trinity county.
Other members of the party, includ
ing Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, University
president and Secretary of the Interior;
Dr. J. Pearce Mitchell, registrar; and
Dr. Thomas M. Williams, former medi
cal adviser, will remain in the moun
tains for a longer period.
