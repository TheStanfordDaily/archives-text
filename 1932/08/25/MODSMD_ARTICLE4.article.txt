# Stanford Man Is Awarded Mention For News Work
## 
### 
In the grant of the Pugsley award
for the best piece of Washington cor
respondence during the year 1931 hon
orable mention was given to Harry
Frantz, former Stanford student, now
head of the foreign service of the
United Press. Frantz's course at Stan
ford was interrupted by the World
War. He returned to the University
but did not take his degree.
