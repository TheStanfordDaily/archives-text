# Charles B. Goddard Takes Up Duties At San Jose
## 
### 
Charles B. Goddard, assistant regis
trar at Stanford in the period 1021-2 G.
has taken up his duties as Dean of
Men at San Jose State College. He re
cently returned from a two-year Euro
pean tour, the itinerary of which in
cluded Rome, Paris, Berlin, Egypt,
Bagdad, Constantinople, and the Island
of Majorca.
