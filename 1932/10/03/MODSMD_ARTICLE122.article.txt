# Soph Women To Have Picnic At Dean Yost's
## 
### 
Tomorrow from 5 to 7 o'clock the
sophomore women will entertain the
new sophomore women with a picnic
supper to be held in Dean Yost's
back yard.
The affair will be an informal get
together of the class and an intro
duction of the 15 new sophomore
transfers. There will be a charge of
twenty-five cents per plate. It is
being held under the auspices of
the sophomore committee.
