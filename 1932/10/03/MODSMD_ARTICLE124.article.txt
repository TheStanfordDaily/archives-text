# Solar Observatories Closed In Australia
## 
### 
CANBERRA, Australia, Oct. 2. (/P)
—Owing to the cost of operating
them, the Federal government has
decided to close the solar observa
tories in Australia, with the excep
tion of two —-one of which is located
at Canberra and one at Perth.
