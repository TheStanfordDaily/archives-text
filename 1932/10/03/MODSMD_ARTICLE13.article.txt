# Y.W.C.A. Offers Texts For Student Rental
## 
### 
For the benefit of those hard hit
by the depression, and incidentally
to make a little money for the bene
fit of its own library, the Y.W.C.A. is
renting textbooks for 25 cents a
quarter.
Students desiring to rent texts or
to take out books from the free li
brary of fiction and non-fiction, will
find someone in charge from 10
o'clock in the morning until 4 o'clock
at night in the Y rooms on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
The library committee will also wel
come contributions of books of any
kind.
