# College Men Sought For U.S. Wardens' Assistants
## 
### 
Interested in unearthing new vo
cations for college men, C. Gilbert
Wrenn, secretary of the Stanford
Vocational Guidance Committee,
found that men with college degrees
are sought for warden's assistants in
the U.S. penal system. The Depart
ment of Justice announced that Civil
Service examinations are held for
these positions.
