# RUSHING FACTS
## 
### 
1. All house presidents and
student members of the Inter
fraternity Board of Control will
meet tonight at 8 o'clock in the
Old Union.
2. Formal pledging of the
men of the Class of 1935 will
take place Wednesday between
1 and 4 o'clock in the Union.
3. When a fraternity has
taken ten men from the Class
of 1935 it can pledge no more
in that class during the school
year 1932-33.
4. No fraternity will be de
nied the privilege of pledging
sopohomores Wednesday be
cause it has not yet collected
its accounts receivable.
