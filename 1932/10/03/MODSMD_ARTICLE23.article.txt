# Chaparral Invites New Humorists To Tryouts
## 
### 
There will be a meeting for all
those who wish to try out for Chap
arral, Stanford's humor magazine, in
the Press building tonight at 7:30,
according to Bob Savage, business
manager.
Since the editorial side of the mag
azine is handled by contributions,
the positions open to tryoutees are
those of business manager, for which
the tryout period is two or, three
years, and circulation manager, for
which the tryout period is one year.
Anyone may send in contributions to
the magazine and if enough are ac
cepted it is possible to become a
member of Hammer and Coffin.
