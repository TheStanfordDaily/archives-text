# CUBBERLEY GIVES DINNER
## 
### 
Acting President Robert E. Swain
and the men o'f the education fac
ulty were guests of Dr. Ellwood P.
Cubberley, dean of the school of
education, at a stag dinner Friday
in the Union. After dinner they
were entertained for the evening at
Dr. Cubberley's home.
