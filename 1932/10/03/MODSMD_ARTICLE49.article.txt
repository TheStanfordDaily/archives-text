# A.W.S. Groups To Plan Fall Quarter Activities
## 
### 
Plans for A.W.S. activities for fall
quarter and the school year will be
drawn up at meetings of all A.W.S.
organizations this week. Dates for
Council, Conference, Round Table,
and committee meetings will be an
nounced tomorrow by Mary-Kathe
rine Morris, A.W.S. president.
Special work for the Vocational
Guidance Committee is being
planned, in order to aid students in
deciding where their interests lie
before graduation with Mr. Gilbert
Wrenn devoting more of his time
to this problem.
