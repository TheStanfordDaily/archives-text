# Chaplain Urges Students To Analyze Characters
## 
### 
As a subject for his sermon yes
terday in the Stanford Memorial
Church, Dr. D. Charles Gardner
chose "The Worth of Being Good."
For illustrations he took the lives
of famous Christians, and showed
how their religion had made them
great. In conclusion, Dr. Gardner
stated that now, at the beginning of
a new school year, is the time for
every student to analyze himself, so
that he can. make the best of his
qualities.
