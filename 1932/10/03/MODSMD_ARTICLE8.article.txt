# Gordon Davis Directs Belasco Play In S.F.
## 
### 
Gordon Davis, former director of
Stanford drarpatics, is at present di
recting Will Cotton's "The Bride the
Sun Shines On" which Curran and
Belasco are producing.
Davis, who did two plays in Palo
Alto this summer, returned some
time ago from the Detroit Civic
Theatre of Jessie Ronstelle where
he has been directing and acting for
the past year.
