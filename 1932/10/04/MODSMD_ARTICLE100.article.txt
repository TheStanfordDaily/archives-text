# Soph Women To Picnic With Dean Mary Yost
## 
### 
About seventy-five sophomore
women have made* reservations for
the picnic supper to be held in Dean
Yost's back yard thi.s evening at 5
o'clock. The new sophomore women
| will be the guests of honor.
Iris Forsyth is in charge of a
serving committee, and an appetiz
ing hot plate will be served with
coffee and dessert. A charge of 25
cents a plate will be made.
