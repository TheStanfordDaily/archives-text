# Campus Groups Must Register With Manager
## 
### 
Managers of all campus organiza
tions except living and eating groups
are required by the constitution of
the Associated Students to register
with the student manager.
This may be done any day this
week at the student body office in
the Old Union. Student Manager
Charles Karl also requests a per
sonal audience with these managers.
His office hours are from 9 to 10
and- from 4 to 5 o'clock daily, and
also from 1 to 5 o'clock on Tues
days and Thursdays.
