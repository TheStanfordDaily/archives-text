# Books Still Available For Rental At Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Books are still available at the
Y.W.C.A. renting library for 25 cents
each per quarter. A list will be
posted of books now available, and
a list of needed books will be put
in RobJe for those who cannot find
what they want at the Y.W.C.A.
Second-hand books may be ex
changed.
The Y.W.C.A. library is open daily
from 2 to 4 o'clock in the afternoon.
