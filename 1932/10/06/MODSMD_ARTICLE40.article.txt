# Home Auxiliary Board To Hold Rummage Sale
## 
### 
The Auxiliary Board of the Stan
ford Convalescent Home is holding
a rummage sale for the benefit of
the Home today from 8 o'clock in
the morning to 9 o'clock at night: at
the corner of Hamilton and Emerson
streets in Palo Alto.
Students are especially urged to
attend, as it will be possible to ob
tain furnishings, lamps, rugs, books,
for one's room at an extraordinarily
low nrice.
