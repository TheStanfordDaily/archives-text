# Life Guards Needed For Roble Pool Open Hours
## 
### 
Women holding Red Cross Life
Saving Certificates who are inter
ested in acting as life guard during
open hours at the Roble Gymna
sium pool are asked to report to
Mrs. Mildred Vishoot immediately.
Open hours are from 4 to 5 o'clock
on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday,
and 3 to 4 o'clock on Tuesday and
Thursday.
A swimming class for faculty
women and outsiders has been or
ganized and will start tomorrow. It
will meet on Tuesday, Thursday,
and Friday at 10 o'clock. The in
struction fee is $5 for faculty mem
bers and $10 for outsiders.
