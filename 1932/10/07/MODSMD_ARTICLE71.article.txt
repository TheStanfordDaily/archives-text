# Famous Biologist Will Speak On Human Nature
## 
### 
"Biology and Human Nature" is
the general subject of three ad
dresses to be given here by Julian
S. Huxley on October 11, 12, and 13.
The lectures will constitute the
eleventh series of the West Me
morial Lectures.

The subjects of Huxley's three
lectures will be: "Human Nature:
Statics," October 11; "Human Na
ture: Dynamics," October 12, and
"The Uniqueness oi' Man," October
13.

Huxley is the grandson of Thomas
H. Huxley, the great Victorian
apostle of Evolution, and of Dr.
Thomas Arnold of Rugby, and is a
brother of Aldous Huxley, famous
essayist.
