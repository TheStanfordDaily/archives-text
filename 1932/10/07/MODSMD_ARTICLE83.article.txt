# Glee Club Issues Call For Pianist And Singers
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Glee
Club will be held in the women's
old gymnasium Monday night at 7:45
o'clock. Men interested in singing
with the Glee Club or in qualifying
as pianist are requested to attend
the meeting by Donald P. Morgan,
president.
The Club will make its first pub
lic appearance at the Sunday morn
ing service in the Memorial Church,
presenting a program of sacred mu
sic in conjunction with the choir.
Present and past members are asked
to participate in the program.
Rehearsal of the program will take
place at 10 o'clock Sunday morn
ing in the choir loft.
