# Many Rooters' Tickets For U.S.C. Game Unsold
## 
### 
But 30 per cent of the students
eligible to purchase U.S.C. game
rooters' tickets have obtained them
during the regular two-day sales
period announced Jake Irwin, ticket
manager, last night. In order to give
the large remaining group a chance
to procure the' ducats, tickets will
be sold today in the B.A.C. Building
from 8:30 to 12 o'clock and from 1
to 5 o'clock.
The tickets cost $1.10, and will be
sold only to those presenting student
body cards. Any that are left over
after tomorrow will be sold to the
members of the varsity, freshman,
and Gray football squads.
