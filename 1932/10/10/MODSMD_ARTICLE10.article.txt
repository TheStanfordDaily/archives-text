# Hall-Row Rivalry Marks Close Of Y.W.C.A. Drive
## 
### 
As the Y.W.C.A. membership drive
nears an end, the Row. Hall rivalry
bccomes more marked with the Hall
forging slightly ahead. On the Row,
Alpha Omicron Pi and Chi Omega
are fighting it out for first place. It
is a neck-to-neck race, with both
houses having a total membership
Of 12.
In addition to the many interest
ing activities which the Y.W.C.A.
offers to those who are members of
the organization, special entertain
ments are being planned for this
quarter in order to help the old and
new women to get better acquainted.
