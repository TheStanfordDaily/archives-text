# DATES FOR A.W. MEETINGS SET
## Morris Chooses Committee Leaders, Members 
### 
Announcement of the dates for
A.W.S. committee meetings was
made last night by Mary-Katherine
Morris, A.W.S. president.
Today Election Board will meet
at 1 o'clock to familiarize itself with
the new election rules, and the
Guest Committee will meet at 1:30
o'clock to plan entertainment for
visiting celebrities.
Lockout chairmen from all living
groups will meet tomorrow at 1:15
o'clock to review the regulations and
methods which were codified last
year in order to insure uniformity
throughout the living groups. The
Flower Committee will meet tomor
row at 2 o'clock
The newly formulated Emergency
Committee will meet tomorrow at
2:30 o'clock to make plans for rais
ing money which will be turned
over to the University Emergency
Committee for needy students. On
Wednesday evening Conference will
meet at 7 o'clock at Dean Yost's
home to plan their program for the
coming year.
All committee meetings will be
helofr on the second floor of the
Women's Clubhouse, and all mem
bers of committees are asked to be
present.
