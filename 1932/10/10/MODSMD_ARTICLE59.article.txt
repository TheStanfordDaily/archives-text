# ACTRESS GAINING
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 9. (/P)—Lilyan
Tashman, motion picture actress,
was reported "greatly improved" to
day in Harbor sanitarium where
she recently underwent an emer
gency operation for appendicitis.
