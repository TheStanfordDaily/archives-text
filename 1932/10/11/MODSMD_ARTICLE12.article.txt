# Rooters To Be Organized For Stanford-Army Tilt
## 
### 
There will be an organized root
ing section at the Stanford-Army
game next Saturday for the first
time this season.
All students planning to attend
the contest via the 50-yard line
rooters' seat must be wearing an
accepted Stanford red and white
rooter's hat, according to announce
ment last night by rally committee
Chairman Robert Churchill.
For those new students who have
not as yet secured their hats and
other Stanfordites who can not find
last year's model, Churchill asked
that all purchase theni immediately
at Roos Bros., the official agent,
where they are selling at $1 each.
