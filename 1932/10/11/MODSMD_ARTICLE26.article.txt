# Jack Allen Is Made New Daily Night Editor
## 
### 
Jack Allen, a member of the Daily
staff for the past two years, was
yesterday appointed to the position
of night editor.
Allen has been a copy editor for
the past two quarters and his work
in that department resulted in his
promotion to one of the editorships.
