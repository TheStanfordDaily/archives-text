# Select Net, Fool Squads For Triangle Sport Day
## 
### 
Four class tennis and swimming
teams will be selected by Gertrude
Hawley and Marjorie Buettner for
the Women's Triangle Sports Day
to be held at Mills College on Octo
ber 29. Stanford, California, and
Mills College co-eds will participate.
A doubles net team and a singles
player will be selected to represent
each class, probably on the basis of
past experience.
