# A.B.A. FOUNDATION OFFERS LOANS TO WORKING STUDENTS
## Economics Upperclassmen To Get Scholarships 
### 
Three loan scholarships of $250
each are offered to upperclass stu
dents of economics, banking, or re
lated subjects by the American
Bankers Association Foundation for
the current year.
Only students who are supporting
themselves at least, in part are eli
gible. Students who receive these
loans are expected to begin repay
ment on the second January after
graduation; before that date inter
est is not charged; after that time
interest is charged at the rate of 5
per cent. Application blanks and
information may be obtained from
the Committee on Scholarship.
