# KREUGER KIN NABBED
## 
### 
STOCKHOLM. Oct. 13 (/p)—Tor
sion Kreuger, brother of the late Ivar
Kreuger, was taken in custody to
day in connection with a police in
vestigation into the affairs of the
Patebois Hoegbroforsen Company, of
which he is a director. He,was held
for the nigjit at the police station.
