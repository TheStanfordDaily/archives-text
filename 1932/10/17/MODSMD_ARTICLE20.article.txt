# Tryouts For Stanford Orchestra Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford Symphony Orchestra
tryouts will continue tomorrow night
in the old Roble gymnasium at 7:15
o'clock. Daniel P. Bryant, conductor,
announces that he will begin prepa
ration for a concert to have all
members of the orchestra and try
outees present at the rehearsal.
Students who play either the
viola, violoncello, or French horn
are especially urged to try out for
the orchestra.
