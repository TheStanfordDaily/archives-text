# Round Robin Arranged For First Ten Netmen
## 
### 
Announcement by the senior ten
nis manager indicates that the first
ten men on the varsity tennis squad
will be afforded ample competition
among themselves this quarter.
A round robin schedule of
matches has been posted. Instead of
the first ten being inactive while
ladder matches are in progress,
these top ranking netmen will en
gage in matches in their own ranks.
Each will be required to play one
match a week. By the end of the
quarter, each man will have played
a match with each of the others.
