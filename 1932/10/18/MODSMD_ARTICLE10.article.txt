# Pi Lambda Theta Board Meets To Plan Activity
## 
### 
Plans for an extensive program
of activities to be carried on this
quarter will be discussed at a meet
ing of the executive board of Pi
Lambda Theta, national woman's
honor education fraternity, tonight
at 7:15 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.

Members of the board are Mrs.
Louise Good, president; Olive Mott,
vice-president; Ruth Whitehead,
treasurer; Frances Doughty, secre
tary; and Mrs. Inez Boren, alumna
representative.
