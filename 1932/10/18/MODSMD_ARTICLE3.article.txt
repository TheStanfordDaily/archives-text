# Symphony Tryout Tests On Tonight In Roble Gym
## 
### 
Stanford Symphony Orchestra will
continue tryoutee auditions tonight
in the old Roble gymnasium at 7:15
o'clock. According to Daniel P.
Bryant, conductor, the personnel of
the orchestra will be determined as
soon as possible and rehearsal for
a concert early next quarter will be
started.
Students who play viola, violin
cello, or French horn are especially
urged to attend the rehearsal to
night.
