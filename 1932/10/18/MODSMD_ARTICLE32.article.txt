# Stanford Law Students Given Chance To Work
## 
### 
Six students of the Stanford law
school will be given an opportunity
to obtain practical legal work under
the direction of Samuel Wagner,
chief counsel for the Legal Aid So
ciety of Oakland.
This has been done by Augustine
Donovan, graduate of the Stanford
School of Law and a member of the
board of directors of the society.
Students will assist in the prepara
tion of cases handled by the society.
Civil cases only arc handled, all
criminal actions being left to the
Public Defender's office.
