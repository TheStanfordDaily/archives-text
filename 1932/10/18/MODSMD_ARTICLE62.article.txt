# FRENCH CLUB MEETS
## 
### 
Honoring Professor Alexander
Naughton, new acting assistant pro
fessor of Romanic Languages, and
his wife, the French Club will meet
this evening at 7:30 o'clock on the
first floor of the Women's Club
house. Phoebe Seagrave, acting
president, will preside. Refreshments
are to be served.
