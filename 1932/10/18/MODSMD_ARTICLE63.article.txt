# GEOLOGY CLUB MEETS
## 
### 
Graduate students and others in
terested in geology heard talks yes
terday afternoon at the Journal Club
meeting. O. F. Fenton spoke on
"Studies of Evolution in the Genus
Spirifer," while C. A. Dunbar and
G. E. Condra gave the "Revision of
the Family Reproductidae." Don L.
Frizzel and Stanford L. Rox gave
reviews.
