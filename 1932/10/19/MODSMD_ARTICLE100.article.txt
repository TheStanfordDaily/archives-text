# Espinosa Goes To Spain For Atlas Compilation
## 
### 
Aurelio Espinosa, Jr., acting as
sistant professor of Spanish during
summer quarter, returned to Spain
last week to resume his work as
one of the official investigators of
the Spanish linguistic atlas. Last
May he received the degree of doc
tor of philosophy at Madrid Uni
versity.

Do you know that the planet
Venus is visible by daylight during
several weeks of the year, to any
one with normal eyesight?
