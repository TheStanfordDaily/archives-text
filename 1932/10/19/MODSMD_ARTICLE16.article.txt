# Quad Studio to Be Open Till 5
## 
### 
As a special concession to seniors
detained by laboratory periods, the
Quad photographic studios will re
main open until 5 o'clock this after
noon and tomorrow.
Ray Hanson, Quad editor, pro
poses to finish the senior section of
the yearbook during these two days,
in order to start next week on the
photographing of living groups.
The Quad studios, to which sen
iors must hasten to direct their
steps, are at 520 Salvatierra.
