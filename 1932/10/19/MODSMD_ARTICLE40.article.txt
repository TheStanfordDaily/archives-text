# German Society To Hold Meet In Clubhouse Today
## 
### 
All second and third year Ger
man students interested in gaining
practice in speaking the language
are invited to attend the first meet
ing this quarter of the Junior Ger
man Society at 4 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Ira Cross, president, will conduct
the meeting. Following an election
of officers there will be a program.
Emily Ross is in charge of the re
freshments.
