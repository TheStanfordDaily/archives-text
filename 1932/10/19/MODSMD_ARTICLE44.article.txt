# Ask Off-Campus Women To Attend Get-Together
## 
### 
All women who are not in living
groups are invited to attend a get
together at 4:15 o'clock this after
noon in the Y.W.C.A. rooms on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
In past years the off-campus
women have found an organization
with weekly meetings, sometimes
for lunch, in order to keep them
selves more in touch with the life
of the University as a whole, and
today's meeting is designed to
further acquaintance and to make
plans for this year.
Mary Parsons, president of the
group, and Evelyn Schilling are
making arrangements for this spe
cial first meeting.
