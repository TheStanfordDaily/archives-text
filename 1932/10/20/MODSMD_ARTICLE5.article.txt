# Quad Sets '33 Photo Deadline
## 
### 
Photography work on the Senior
section the 1933 Quad will end
tomorrow, it was announced last
night by Ray Hanson, editor. The
studio at 520 Salvatierra will be
open till 5 o'clock today and until
4 o'clock tomorrow to accommodate
those members of the Class of '33
who did not appear for picture-tak
ing on their scheduled days.
"Seniors have been very slow to
respond to the call for pictures,"
Hanson said. "All the rest of the
class can be 'adequately taken care
of today and tomorrow, however,
and on Monday we plan to start on
another section of the book."
