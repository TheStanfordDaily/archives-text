# REPORT EARTHQUAKE
## 
### 
PASADENA, Oct. 19 (/P) — The
Carnegie Institution's seismological
laboratory reported a moderate
earthquake about 200 miles distant,
occurring at 8:41 a.m. today. Lab
oratory officials were unable to place
the location of the tremor.

One of the main streets of Nan
tucket, Mass., which is named Joy,
I leads to the cemetery.
