# Yost, U.C. Worker Will Speak Sunday To Y.W.
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost and Miss Lela
Anderson of the staff of the Univer
sity of California Y.W.C.A., will be
the principal speakers on the pro
gram of the Y.W.C.A. open meeting
to. be held Sunday afternoon at 4:30
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
A presentation of the Y.W.C.A.,
its work, and plans for the year
will be given at the meeting, em
bodying the results of the interest
test which is now in circulation.
Miss Anderson was very promi
nent in her undergraduate career
at the University of Georgia, and
was especially interested in the "Y"
work on race relationships. "She is
welcomed with open arms in all the
colleges up and down the coast, and
we are very fortunate in having her
I with us Sunday," declared Miss
'Phillips, Executive Secretary of the
Stanford Y.W.C.A.
