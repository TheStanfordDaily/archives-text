# Cosmopolitan Club To Fete Foreign Students
## 
### 
Stanford's Cosmopolitan Club will
hold its first party of the fall quar
ter tonight at 8 o'clock at the home
of Professor Austin F. Rogers on
549 Salvatierra street. This get-to
gether is being held for the purpose
of acquainting old foreign students
with the new.
Under the direction of Elizabeth
Loofbourow, '32, and Alice Wolcott,
'32, plans are being made for games,
dancing, and light refreshments % to
feature the evening.
