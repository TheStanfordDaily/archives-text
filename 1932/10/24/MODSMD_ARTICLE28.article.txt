# A 						SNAKE IN THE GRASS
## 
### 
I he subtle combination of the driver of a water wagon
and the campus cop recently dug up a skeleton which
hadn't seen lite light of day since the great tire of 1001. All
these years the Daily staff has gone about its business un
conscious ol this peril in their midst. Kvcn good old mother
nature had done its part to hide the cadaver from the
minions ol the press, hut not, alas, from the water wagon
driver and the campus cop.
Without warning a flood of bids to the Men's Council,
were received by various staff members for parking too
close to a fire plug. With cries of indignation these gentle
men rushed out to see what power of heaven had made a
hydrant where yesterday none had been. After a minute l
and careful search the offending plug was found buried
under thick bushes of long and honorable growth.
It would seem as though there was indeed a snake in
Ihc grass!
