# Law Librarian Named on Council of American Bar
## 
### 
Professor James E. Brenner, li
brarian of the Stanford School of
Law, was elected to the council of
the section on Legal Education and
Admission to the bar of the Ameri
can Bar Association at the annual
meeting held in Washington re
cently, it was announced yesterday.
The council is the executive body
on the section of legal education.
There are 11 members in the United
States elected for four-year terms.
Under their jurisdiction, the bar
standards are administered. Eleven
years ago the bar adopted a set of
minimum educational requirements
for admission to practice and now
19 states have enacted these into
law.
