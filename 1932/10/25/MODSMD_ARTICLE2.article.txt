# Freshman Class Heads To Be Nominated Today
## 
### 
Nominations for officers of the
Class of '36 will be received today
from 8 until 6 o'clock on the Quad
above the Law steps, according to
Ray Wilbur of the election board.
Positions to be filled are those of
president, vice-president, seci'etary,
and treasurer. Petitions must be
signed by at least 15 members of
the Freshman class and any mem
ber of the class is eligible to run
for office.
They may be secured at the Law
.steps. The nominating polls will also
be open tomorrow at the same
hours.
