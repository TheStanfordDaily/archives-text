# Seven Survive Tryouts For Campus Glee Club

### 
Tryouts hist night for the Stan
ford Glee Club eliminated all but
seven aspirants. A range test was
taken by all tryoutees, and those
surviving were placed on the proba
tionary list until further tryouts to
be held the latter part of November.
The following may report to a

Breed. Gunther Dedert, Bill Gwinn.
Donald Hanson, Waldo Munday,
Charles Welch, and John Young. All
members of the club will rehearse
at this meeting, which will termin

A.S.S.U. Bomioli concert.
