# Quad Photographer Calls A-G Roble Women Today
## 
### 
Roble Women whose names run
from A to G in the alphabet are
scheduled to report at the Quad
Watson photography studio at 520
Salvatierra today. Ray Hanson, ed
itor of the year book, has announced
that he expects to finish work on
the women's hall section this week,
and it is essential that the schedule
be observed.
A nominal fee will be charged for
the service, Hanson said. The studio
hours remain unchanged.
