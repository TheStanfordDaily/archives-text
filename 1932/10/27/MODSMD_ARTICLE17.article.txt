# U.C.L.A. May Hold Two Sessions Next Summer
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Oct. 26—(PIPI —
Through a new ruling passed by the
Board of Regents of the University
of California, U.C.L.A. was author
ized to hold a post-sesion term to
open immediately upon the conclu
sion of the summer session. Four
units may be taken in this term to
run from August 10 to September 1.
Another concession granted U.C.-
LA. was the permission given to
allow graduates from other univer
sities to enroll for credit in under
graduate courses. The new rulings
will make it easier for students to
work for higher degrees.
