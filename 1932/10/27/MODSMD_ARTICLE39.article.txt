# BORAH DENIES CHOICE
## 
### 
BOISE, Idaho, Oct. 26 (/P). —Sen-
ator William E. Borah told the As
sociated Press today that he had
"never at any time made any ref
erences as to how I should vote."
A sophomore-senior formal is an
annual event at Pomona College.
