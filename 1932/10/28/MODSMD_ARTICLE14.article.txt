# Engineers Plan Tour Of R.C.A. Plants Tomorrow
## 
### 
More than 25 members of the
Stanford chapter of the American
Institute of Electric Engineers are
expected to take part in the all-day
trip Saturday to visit the San Fran
cisco, Bolinos, and Point Reyes
plants of the Radio Corporation of
America, one of the international
radio circuits operating from the bay
district.
Engineers and others interested in
making the trip have been asked to
be at the Engineering corner at 8
o'clock Saturday morning when the
caravan to San Francisco will leave.
R. R. Beal, '12, an official in the
San Francisco R.C.A. headquarters,
has been in charge of arrangements
for the visit of Stanford students.
