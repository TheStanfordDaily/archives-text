# Rifle Club Opens Range Wednesday Afternoons
## 
### 
In addition to being open on Mon
day and Friday afternoons, the Rifle
Club range in the basement of the
Administration Building will be
open on Wednesday, from 2 to 5
o'clock. At present the club has
three first-class rifles for anyone
wishing to shoot as well as three
older muskets for those who desire
to learn.
