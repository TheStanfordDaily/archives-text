# Bud Nicholas Wins Sixth Spot On Babe Golf Team
## 
### 
In a nip-and-tuck battle ending
with the final tally of one up, Bud
Nicholas, frosh golfer, defeated Bob
McGlashen for sixth place on the
freshman ladder. Both were shoot
ing good golf and showed about
equal ability. For twelfth place Ray
Freer won a lopsided match from
Ray Hornby.
In intramural play, El Capitan
won a decisive victory from the
Kappa Sigs by the final score of four
up and three to play. Theta Delts
beat the Breakers Club by a like
score, four up with three holes re
maining. All of the five intramural
first-round matches are supposed to
be played off by Monday night.
