# Three American Nations Select New Presidents
## 
### 
Three American republics were
completing the count of ballots to
day in elections of new presidents.
Practically complete returns
showed Arturo Alessandri, candidate
of the Center and some Left par
ties, assured of the six-year term as
President of Chile. Martinez Mera,
Liberal, and General Tiburcio Car
ias Andino, Conservative, were lead
ing for the four-year terms in
Ecuador and Honduras, respectively.
