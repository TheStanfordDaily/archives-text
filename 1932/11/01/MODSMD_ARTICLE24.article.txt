# New German Professor To Be Added To Staff
## 
### 
Professor Julius Peterson, one of
the outstanding German scholars in
the literature of his own nation, will
join the faculty of the German de
partment at Stanford next summer.
Professor Peterson, who is head of
the department of German literature
at the University of Berlin, is es
pecially noted for his contributions
to "literary science," the method of
literary criticism which takes into
account all the cultural trends in
the period under criticism.
Peterson is this year president of
the World Goethe Society. He will
offer three courses: History of Ger
man National Theater, Methodology
of Literary Science, and Classical
German Drama.
