# Carmel Artist Exhibits Foreign Sketches Here
## 
### 
A group of 35 water color paint
ings by T. C. Criley of Carfnel
Highlands, are being shown this
month at the Stanford Art Gal
lery. Through the courtesy of Mrs.
Criley, the public has the oppor
tunity of seeing the work of this
artist for the first time. Sketches
of the byways of Spain and Morocco
and two views on the Seine in
France are included, but the rest of
the scenes are from the Carmel
coast and valleys.
The Art Gallery is open every
day to the public between the hours
of 10 and 5 o'clock. No admission
charge.
