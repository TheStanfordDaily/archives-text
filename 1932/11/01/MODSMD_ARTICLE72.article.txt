# HOAX IN CAROLINA
## 
### 
CHAPEL HILL, N.C., Oct. 31—
Leaving the campus of the Univer
sity of North Carolina as mysteri
ously as he appeared, a man pur
porting to be Bert Metzger, All -
American guard from Notre Dame,
perpetrated a successful hoax on
unsuspecting undergraduates here.
The stranger did show some of the
aspiring football candidates how the
game was played in the big-time,
but he refused to make himself
known to Coach Collins of the North
Carolina eleven.
