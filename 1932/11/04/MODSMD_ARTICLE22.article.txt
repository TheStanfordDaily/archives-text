# Navy Lieutenant Found Dead At Norfolk, Va.
## 
### 
NORFOLK. Va.. Nov. 3 (/Pi—Nor
folk police and naval authorities to
day were investigating circum
stances surrounding the death of
Lieutenant Jack W. Ames, U.S.N.,
whose body was found in a hotel
here last night.
Police are awaiting a report on
analysis of some whisky found in a
suit case beside the bod.
