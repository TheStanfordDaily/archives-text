# New Women Entertained At Sorority Teas Sunday
## 
### 
New women will be entertained
by Alpha Omicron Pi. Delta Delta
Delta, and Pi Beta Phi from 4 until
6 o'clock Sunday afternoon at the
second of the fall teas sponsored by
Stanford sororities. On Sunday, No
vember 13, the final tea is scheduled
with Alpha Phi, Gamma Phi Beta,
and Kappa Alpha Theta holding
open house for the new women.
1 ists are being posted in Roble on
which new women will sign their
preference as to order in which they
will visit the houses, the number on
each list being to avoid congestion.
