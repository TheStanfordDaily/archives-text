# Farm Convalescent Home Junior Auxiliary In S.F.
## 
### 
A San Francisco junior auxiliary
unit of the Stanford Convalescent
Home has been organized to raise
additional funds for the Home, it
was announced today.
The new group will sponsor a
benefit dance and fashion show to
morrow night at Hotel St. Francis
in San Francisco, turning a per
centage of the $1 cover charge into
the convalescent fund. Reservations
may be made at the hotel.
