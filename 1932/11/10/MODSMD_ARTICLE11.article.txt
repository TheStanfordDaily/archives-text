# Y.W. Conference Seeks College Religious Head
## 
### 
Trying to find out what, if any, is
the place of religion in the life of
the college student, a philosophy
discussion group will meet tonight
from 5:30 to 7:30 o'clock in (he
Y.W.C.A. rooms on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
This meeting will be devoted to
discovering the basic meaning of
religion, but various well-qualified
people will be asked to speak at
later meetings, according to Flor
ence Jones, chairman of the group.
Supper will be served, and any
one wishing to attend should make
reservations at the Y.W.C.A. rooms
by noon today.
