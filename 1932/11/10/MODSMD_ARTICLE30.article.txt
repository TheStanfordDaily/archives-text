# Rolph Seeks Legal Aid On Rum Convict Status
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, Nov. 9 (/P>
Governor Rolph said today that as
result, of the repeal vote on the
Wright Prohibition Enforcement Act
yesterday he would ask legal advice
as to status of some 300 state peni
tentiary prisoners serving terms for
violation of the law. The query is
understood to include the question
of amnesty for such prisoners.
