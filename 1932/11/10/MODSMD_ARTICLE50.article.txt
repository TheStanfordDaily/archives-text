# "Y" Will Sell Flowers For Disabled Veterans
## 
### 
Selling forget-me-nots in Palo
Alto today and tomorrow for the
benefit of the Disabled Veterans of
America is the present project of
the Social Service Committee of the
Y.W.C.A.
Anyone not or. the committee who
desires to help should telephone the
Y.W.C.A. rooms and make arrange
ments to do so. The Y.W.C.A. will
furnish transportation to the Cardi
nal Hotel, headquarters for the
work.
