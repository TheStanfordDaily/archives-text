# Allen Offers Popular Organ Numbers Today
## 
### 
A program of outstanding organ
music will be offered at 5:10 o'clock
today in the Memorial Church by
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist. The program follows:
Chorale-Prelude, "Rejoice, Ye
Pure in Heart"..Leo Sowerby
"Prelude in E Minor"..A. Scriabine
"Bell-Melody for the Berghall
Church" J. Sibelius
"King Christian Suite"...J. Sibelius
"Traumerei" Richard Strauss
"Berceuse" and "Finale" from
the "Fire-Bird"..l. Stravinsky
