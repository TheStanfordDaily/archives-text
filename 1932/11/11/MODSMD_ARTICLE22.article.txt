# Social Worker To Speak Before Y.W.C.A. Group
## 
### 
"Opportunities for Stanford
Women in the Lane Hospital" will
be the topic of Mrs. F. R. Fletcher,
head of the Social Scrvice Depart
ment in the Stanford Lane Clinic,
for her talk Sunday to the Y.W.C.A.
social service laboratory committee.
The talk, which will be from 3 to
4 o'clock in the afternoon, will be
the principal event of the committee
meeting and is therefore primarily
for the benefit of the committee
members, but any others interested
in the subject are invited to attencf,
according to Blanche Coe, chairman.
Tea will be served.
