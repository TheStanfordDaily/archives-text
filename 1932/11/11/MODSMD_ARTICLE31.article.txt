# Wilbur's Plans Depend On Events At Capital
## 
### 
Ray Lyman Wilbur has not stated,
as yet, what his plan for after the
first of the year will be.
His leave of absence from Stan
ford will expire on January first,
but he has not said whether ho will
resign from the cabinet before
March 4 or not. He said his plans
would depend on a discussion with
University regents and "on the out
tome of events in Washington after
President Hoover returns."
