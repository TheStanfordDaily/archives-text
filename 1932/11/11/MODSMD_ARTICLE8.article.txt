## 
###

scientist-writer, will speak at an
assembly Tuesday, November 15, at
11 o'clock. "Bad Air" will be the
lecture topic and will deal with the
experiences science has recorded of
dangerous gasses in mines, subma
rines, diving bells, aeroplanes, and
laboratories.
