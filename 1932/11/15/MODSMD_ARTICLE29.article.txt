# Y.W. Cabinet Advisory Board To Hold Joint Meet
## 
### 
All Y.W.C.A. Cabinet members
who can attend this quarter's joint
Cabinet-Advisory Board meeting
for dinner at Mrs. D. C. Cubbison's
house on Tuesday, November 22,
should leave word with Mrs. David
Lamson at the "Y" rooms by the
end of the week, according to Ther
ina Guerard, president of the "Y."
All those who will have cars that
night are asked to give the number
of passengers they can take, so that
transportation arrangements can be
made. Those attending the dinner
will meet at 5:45 o'clock in the "Y"
rooms, and will drive from there.
