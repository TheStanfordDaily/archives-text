TUESDAY
11 a.m.—Address on "Bad Air: A
Study of Problems in Human
Physiology in Some Dangerous
Occupations," by J. B. S. Haldane.
12:10 p.m.—German Table in the
main dining room of the Union.
1 p.m.—Seat sale for the Big Game
Gaieties. The Old Union and at
Keeble's in Palo Alto 1 . Tickets,
$1.25, $1, and 75c.

Doctorate: Willis G. Hewatt. Room
308 Library.

Women's Clubhouse. Speaker: Mr.
O. H. Blpokman.
4-G p.m.—Miss Yost will be at
home, 532 Lasuen.
7:30 p.m.—Stanford Orchestra will
hold a rehearsal in the Old Roble
Gymnasium.
7:30 p.m.—Lecture on "The Russian
Worker and His Government," by
H. H. Fisher. Little Theatre. The
public is invited.
5:15 p.m.—Y.W.C.A. Executive Com
mittee meeting in the "Y" rooms.

Faculty Clubhouse. Speaker: Dr.
Maurice L. Tainter. Subject: "The
Pharmacology of Certain Amines:
a Critique of Methodology."
