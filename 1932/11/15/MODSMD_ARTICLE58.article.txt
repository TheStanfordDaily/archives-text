# Crocker To Judge Second Moot Court Trial Tonight
## 
### 
The second trial of the winter j
quarter round of the Moot Court '
of the Stanford School of Law will
be argued tonight before Judge
George Crocker, Stanford law grad- j
uate and practicing San Francisco
attorney.
The case will be argued by j
Wadieh Shibley and George Vargas
against Leon Carley and James!
Robb for the defense. The trial will
be held at 7:15 o'clock tonight in
the lecture room of the Stanford
law building.
