# Aviation Club Organized For Stanford Students
## 
### 
An avitation club composed of
students who are interested in
learning to fly at a minimum cost
was formed at the Stanford airport
last Tuesday evening. The organiza
tion is non-profit making and is
formed for the sole purpose of in
structing. its members.
Instruction will be given in a
standard Fleet trainer by a former
army flyer who has more than 800
hours in the air to his credit. He
has spent considerable time in
structing in the U.S. Army flying
schools.
Anyone interested should see Bill
Barks at the Phi Delt house for
particulars.
