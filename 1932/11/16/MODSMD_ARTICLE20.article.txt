# Alumnae To Give Tea In S.F. Before Big Game
## 
### 
The San Francisco Stanford
Women's Club and the East Bay
Stanford Women's Club will give
their annual Big Game Tea Friday,
November 18, for all Stanford alum
' nae and the wives or mothers of
i Stanford people. The tea will be
I from 3:30 to 6 o'clock and will take
place in the Sir Francis Drake Hotel
in San Francisco.
