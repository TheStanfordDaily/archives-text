# 'Y' Discussion Group To Hold Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
All women who wish to attend
the meteing of the discussion group
on "Ideas of Living,'* to be held in
the Y.W.C.A. rooms from 5:30 to
7:30 o'clock tomorrow, must make
reservations at the "Y" rooms today,
Florence Jones, chairman of the
group, announced yesterday. Supper
will be served at the meeting.
Discussion will continue on the
meaning and value of religion. Par
ticipants are a.sked to look through
the material on the committee table
in the "Y" rooms before coming to
the meeting. Among such material
available are books by Stanley
Jones, Middleton Murray, and
Kagawa, known as "the Ghandi ol
Japan."
