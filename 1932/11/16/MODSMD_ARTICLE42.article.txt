# AVIATOR HALL TO WED
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 15 (/?)- James
Goodwin Hall, aviator and broker
announced today his engagement to
Miss Anne Burnett of Fort Worth
Texas. Hall said no date for the
wedding has been set.
