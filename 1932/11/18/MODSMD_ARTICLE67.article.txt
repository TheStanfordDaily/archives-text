# OUR GAIETIES
## 
### 
Thai annual exhibition of delightful madness will ap
pear once more behind Ihe footlights of the Stanford stage
when Ihe 15M2 Gaieties get under way tonight. This show
represents one of the last strongholds of tradition on the
Farm. All contributions in the form of skits, songs, and
music are original and intended only for Stanford's own
show.
Great dramatical performances are not expected in the
Gaieties, nor will you find the finished smoothness of pro
fessional vaudeville. However, the entertainment offered
tomes in large packages and produces laughs in Ihe most
depressed of Ihe depressed of this Depress—but you get
Ihe idea.
Reliable advanced low-down predicts that Ibis year's
model of Ihe varied hors-d'oeuvres to the main dish al
Berkeley tomorrow will result in more cracked sides, from
excessive laughter, than have ever been recorded before.
Somebody once said, or should have said, that a good
laugh while digesting will make even bear meat seem
lender.
