Palo alto, calif.
TO THE STUDENT BODY,
STANFORD UNIVERSITY, CALIFORNIA,
NOVEMBER 23, 1932.
WE HOPE YOU'RE GOING HOME FOR THANKSGIVING
VACATION. IF NOT, WIRE GOOD CHEER TO THE FAMILY
AND INTERESTED PARTIES AT HOME. PICK UP THE
PHONE—CALL "POSTAL TELEGRAPH"—AND WITHIN A
FEW MINUTES YOUR MESSAGE WILL BE DELIVERED AT
ITS DESTINATION. YOURS FOR A PLEASANT VACATION.
(SIGNED) POSTAL TELEGRAPH
343 UNIVERSITY AVE.
