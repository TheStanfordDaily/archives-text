# ELDER-CAMP FRIENDLY
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Nov. 22 (/P)—Having
obtained her divorce, Ruth Elder
was "homeward" bound from Reno
to New York today for Thanksgiv
ing dinner with Walter Camp Jr.,
who now has the status of ex-hus
band. She said the dinner was at
his invitation.
Commodity prices in Japan are
increasing.
