# Union Cellar To Remain Open Over Thanksgiving
## 
### 
Observing regular vacation hours,
the Union Cellar will be open daily \
from 7:30 a.m. to 8 p.m. during the !
Thanksgiving holidays.
The cafeteria will be closed dur
ing vacation: but a special seventy
five cent Thanksgiving dinner will
be served in the main dining room
of the Union from 12:30 to 2 o'clock
on Thursday, Thanksgiving Day.
The dining room will be open at
noon only on Sunday. ,
