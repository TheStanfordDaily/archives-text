# Dr. Northway, Hygiene Lecturer, Passes Cigars
## 
### 
At 8 o'clock Saturday night an
8-pound boy was born to Dr. and
Mrs. William H. Northway in the
Stanford-Lane Hospital.
Dr. Northway, hygiene instructor
in the School of Hygiene and Phys
ical Education, is assistant physician
to the Stanford football team, under
Dr. E. F. Roth.
