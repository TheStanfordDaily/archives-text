# Reserve Toyon, Branner Rooms Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
Vivicnne Church, secretary of the
Dean of Men, has announced that
all men who are now living in
either Toyon or Branner Halls and
who wish to reserve their rooms for
winted quarter must call at the
Dean of Men's office today or to
morrow and pay a deposit of $5.
Those rooms which are not re
served tomorrow will be disposed
of in the drawings scheduled for
Wednesday evening at 7 o'clock in
the Branner Hall office and at 7:30
o'clock in the Toyon Hall office.
