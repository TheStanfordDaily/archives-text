# A 						SEAT FOR THE PRESS
## 
### 
The feminine powers-that-be put their heads together
just the other day and decided to give a certain little lady
a great big hand. The Y.W.C.A. Cabinet, the A.W.S. Coun
cil, the W.A.A. Hoard, and the Pan-Hellenic Council moved
separately and simultaneously to honor none other than
the women's editor of the Daily with ex-otlicio member
ship.
Already a member in the cx-oflicio capacity of the
A.W.S. Conference, the queen-journalist of the campus
may now bow her head in thought at the secret and serious
confabs of these ruling groups. It's like being assigned a
place at Queen Guinevere's tea-table, and the Daily is
justly proud of the recognition accorded the ascendant
feminine member of the staff.
Greater glory still, these executive bodies have indi
cated that,if the new arrangement "works out" satisfac
torily il will become permanent. Xo more than just, that
Ihe woman who works three years to attain the peak of
the campus linotype-slug heap should be taken in by the
oilier big shots as one of them.
