# Youthful Pro Wins Second Day Of Miami Tournament
## 
### 
MIAMI BILTMORE GOLF
COURSE, CORAL GABLES, Fla.,
28 (/P) —John Revolta, 21-year-old
professional from Menominee, Mich.,
strode home with a glittering 73-
71—144 to win second day honors in
the last few minutes of today's play
in the $10,000 Miami Bill more open
golf tournament.

Until the last few scores were
posted the lead at the tournament's
halfway mark had virtually been
conceded to the 145 registered by
Johnny Fan-ell of New York and
A1 Espinosa of Akron, Ohio.
