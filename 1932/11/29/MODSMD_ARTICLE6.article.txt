# Group To Talk On Study Aids
## 
### 
"Habits of Concentration" will be'
the subject of a lecture-discussion
to be conducted by Dr. C. Gilbert
Wrenn. at 11 and 7 o'clock in Room
201 today. This is the second of a
series of conferences on the art of
study which will be held each Tues
day during the remainder of the
quarter. The subject of today's dis
cussion was chosen by those who
attended the conference last week.
All students are invited to attend
these informal meetings, especially
freshmen and transfers. The eve
ning meeting will be over in time
to allow attendance at the concert.
