# COTTON PRODUCE SMALL
## 
### 
' WASHINGTON, Nov. 28 (/P) -
World cotton production this year
, was estimated today by the Depart
ment of Agriculture at 23,400,000
bales of 478 pounds, the smallest
! since 1923-24.

Eighteen million turkeys were
I raised in the U.S. this year.
