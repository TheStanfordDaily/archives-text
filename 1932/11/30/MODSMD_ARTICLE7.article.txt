# Uncle Tom's Cabin To Go On Boards In Palo Alto
## 
### 
That old tear-jerking melodrama
of the 19th Century, "Uncle Tom's
Cabin," will go on the boards of the
Palo Alto Community House, Friday
evening.
Included in its cast are the fol
lowing Stanford students: Fred
Coonradt, Irving Milner, and Nor
ris James. The production, present
ed by the Palo Alto Community
Theatre, will be under the direction
of William A. 'Pabst.
