# IN SPEAKERSHIP RACE
## 
### 

With the excitement in Congressional circles reaching a fever pitch
concerning the Democratic nomination for the House Speakership when
Vice-President-elect John Garner resigns, these three men stand out in
the race as the most-favored candidates. Representative Henry T. Raincy
(below), floor leader; Representative John Rankin (upper left), chairman
of the veterans' committee, and Representative John McDuffie (upper
right), party whip, will vie for the job. (Associated Press Photo)
