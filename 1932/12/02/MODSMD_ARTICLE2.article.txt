# Women To Dress Dolls For Convalescent Home
## 
### 
As Christmas gifts for the children
at the Convalescent Home, Stan
ford's pet charity, 45 dolls are being
dressed by the women of the cam
pus. Anyone who wishes to dress
one may obtain it at the Y.W.C.A.
rooms.
Under the direction of Jessie
Marie McWilliams the dolls are be
ing distributed to the Row and
Roble, with one doll to each house
and to anyone else who feels the
urge to relax in this fashion, as well
as help out the Christmas cheer at
the Convalescent Home.
