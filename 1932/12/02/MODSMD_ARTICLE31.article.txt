# BOOTLEGGERS TAXED
## 
### 
ALBUQUERQUE, N.M., Dec. 1
(TP)—The Internal Revenue office
here today was requested to collect
taxes from fifty New Mexico boot
leggers. The request came from
bootleggers taxed.
Explosives and coal tar are cited
as chemistry's chief contributions to
highway progress.
