# Twitty Will Speak Before Jordan Seminar Tomorrow
## 
### 
The monthly Jordan seminar will
be held in Jordan Hall tomorrow
afternoon at 4:30 o'clock, according
to Professor C. P. Taylor, executive
head of the zoology department,
who was successful in obtaining as
the speaker one of the outstanding
scientific men of the present day.
He is Professor Twitty, formerly of
the Kaiser Wilhelm Institute, but
now of Stanford, and the subject of
his lecture will be "Transplantation
Among Embryos."
Professor Twitty spent many
months in Berlin with his colleague,
Professor Mangold, in studying the
reaction upon tissues which have
been cut from one embryo and
transplanted into another.
