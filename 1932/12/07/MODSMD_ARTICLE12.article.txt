# Poor Scholarship Excuses Due Before December 15
## 
### 
Students whose work has been
intefered with during the current
quarter by illness, or other causes
for which allowance can be made
by the Committee cn Scholarship,
may report the special circumstances
to Mrs. Huston, Secretary of the
Committee, at the Registrar's Office.
Excuses should be submitted not
later than December 15 in order to
be assured of consideration before
action is taken on the end-quarter
reports.
Requests for allowance on account
of illness must be supplemented by
the special physician's certificate
furnished by the committee.
