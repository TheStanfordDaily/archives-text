# Women Swimmers Take Senior Life Saving Test
## 
### 
Women swimmers will be offered
the opportunity of passing the Red
Cross senior life saving test today
and Monday at 4 o'clock at the
Roble pool.
All candidates are required to
have had eight practice periods
prior to taking the test, and only
those over seventeen are eligible.
The test will be given by an exam
iner from Palo Alto.
