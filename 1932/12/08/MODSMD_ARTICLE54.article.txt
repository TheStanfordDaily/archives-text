# AIDS DEMOCRATS ORGANIZE SENATE
## 
### 

An error by a county clerk who failed to send in the official vote
gave Walter Walker, Colorado democrat, a seat in the senate long
enough to permit the bourbons to organize the upper house at the
"lame duck" session. Walker who was appointed by Gov. W. H. Adams
getting into an airplane in Denver to fly to the capital for the opening
session. (Associated Press Photo)
