# Dolls Dressed By "500" To Be Exhibited Tuesday
## 
### 
The 35 dolls which are now being
dressed by members of the "500''
for Christmas presents to the chil
dren of the Convalescent Home will
be on exhibition at the Y.W.C.A.
Doll Party, to be held from 3:30 to
5:30 o'clock Tuesday afternoon.
A prize will be given to the per
son whose doll is voted best-dressed
by those attending the party. Ad
mission to the event will be a small
Icy, although ten cents will be ac
cepted as substitute. The toys thus
gathered will be used to fill Christ
mas stockings which are distributed
to San Francisco children through
the Stanford Lane Clinic in the city.
