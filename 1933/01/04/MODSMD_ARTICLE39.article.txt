# Y.W. Committee To Outline Winter Activities Today
## 
### 
Plans for the coming quarter will
be discussed at today's meeting of
the Y.W.CA. Executive Committee
to be held at the home of Barbara
Binns from 5 to 7 o'clock. Members
will meet at 4:45 o'clock in the "Y"
rooms and transportation will be ar
ranged, according to Therina Guer
ard, president.
Committee members held a special
meeting during the vacation with
Helen Price, National Student Sec
retary for the Asilomar region, to
discuss general difficulties in the
work of the "Y" as a whole. Also
during vacation invitations were
sent out for the tea which the "Y"
is holding Friday afternoon to wel
come the women newly registered
this quarter.
