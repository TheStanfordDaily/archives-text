# POLICE KILL TWO GUNMEN
## 
### 
CAPE GIRARDEAU, Mo., Jan. 3
(/I I ) — Two unidentified gunmen were
shot to death by seven policemen
in a pistol duel in a second-story
fiat here today. The men were sus
pected of the burglary of a cafe at
Morehouse, Mo., last night.
