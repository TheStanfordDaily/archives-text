# Y.W. Social Committee Plans Tomorrow's Tea
## 
### 
Plans for tomorrow's tea for the
entering women will be the chief
subject discussed at a meeting of
the Y.W.C.A. Social Committee in
the "Y" rooms at 5 o'clock this
afternoon, according to Marion Hew
lett, chairman.
A general program of social af
fairs for winter quarter, in consider
ation of the low budget, will be de
cided at the meeting, so that the
chairman urges all members to be
present at this meeting especially.
