# Professor Blackman To Address Y.W.C.A. Group
## 
### 
"The Meaning of Religion to Me"
will be the subject of a talk given
by Professor O. H. Blackman at a
meeting of the Y.M.C.A. philosophy
group tonight from 5:30 to 7:30
o'clock in the "Y" rooms on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Anyone interested is invited to
join the group, according to Florence
Jones, its leader. Supper will be ten
cents, and reservations should be
made at the "Y" rooms by noon
today if possible.
The first newspaper in America
was established at Boston in 1719.
