# Junior, Senior Squads Start W.A.A. Basketball
## 
### 
Junior and senior W.A.A. basket
ball squads will open the season's
practice this afternoon at 4 o'clock
in the Roble gymnasium. Coaching
the first week will be under the
direction of Eleanor Dennison, while
Marion Downey will take charge of
'it for the remainder of the quarter.
Although most of the members of
last year's teams have signed up,
additional players are needed to
round out the squads for both
classes. Practices have been sched
uled so that all teams may use the
large court three times a week.
Freshmen and sophomores will
practice at 4 and 5 o'clock, respec
tively.
