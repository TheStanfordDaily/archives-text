# Room Hostess Applicants For Y.W.C.A. Meet Today
## 
### 
All women who wish to bo
Y.W.C.A. room hostesses this quar
ter are asked to meet in the "Y"
rooms at 5:30 o'clock this afternoon,
according to Tamaki Nao, head of
the committee.
A schedule of hours will be ar
ranged at the meeting and plans will
be made for seeing that no one has
too much or too little to do. Each
room hostess is in the "Y" rooms one
hour a week, and her varied duties
give her an opportunity for becom
ing acquainted with all the different
work done by the "Y."
It is impossible to concentrate on a
task in a sound-proof room, accord
ing to psychologists.
