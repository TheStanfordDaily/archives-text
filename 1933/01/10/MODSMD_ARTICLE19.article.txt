# American Composers Will Feature Allen's Recital
## 
### 
At his organ recital in the Memo
rial Church at 5:10 o'clock this af
ternoon, the University organist,
Warren D. Allen, will play selections
from four American composers. The
last one honors its composer,
Humphrey J. Stewart, who died a
week ago. The program follows:
Toccata on "O Filii et Filiae"
Lynnwood Farnam
Passacaglia Leo Sowerby
Introspection
Frederick Stanley Smith
Sonata, "The Chambered Nautilus"
Humphrey J. Stewart
