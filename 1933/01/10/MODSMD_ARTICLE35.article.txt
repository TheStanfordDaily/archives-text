# POPE TO PRAY FOR PEACE
## 
### 
VATICAN CFTY, Jan. 9 (A 3 ) —
Pacification of the souls of individu
als and peace and fraternity among
peoples, it is expected, will be the
purposes of worldwide prayers which
Pope Pius will request in a papal
bull to be issued soon promulgating
a holy year.
