# Business School To Hear Shipping Line Executive
## 
### 
Charles L. Wheeler, vice-president
and general manager of a San Fran
cisco steamship company, will be
the principal speaker at the first
winter meeting of the Graduate
School of Business at 6:15 tonight in
the Union dining room.
For some time Wheeler has been
holding special meetings of his vari
ous managers and tonight the gath
ering will be conducted in a similar
form with the speaker presenting
different problems that arise from
time to time and then aiding the club
members in answering the queries.
Carl McDowell, president, will
head the meeting and call for the
nominations of new officers.
