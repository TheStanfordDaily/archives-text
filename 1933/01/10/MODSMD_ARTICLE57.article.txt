# Latin-Americans Hold First Meeting Thursday
## 
### 
First meeting oi the Latin-Ameri
can Club will be held at 7:30 o'clock
Thursday night in the Toyon lobby,
it was announced today by Manoel
Cardoza, chairman in charge of the
event.
The meeting, an informal gather
ing, is open to all Latin-American
students enrolled at Stanford and
those interested in the southern
countries. Two sections will be or
ganized Thursday when all Spanish
speaking students and Portuguese
speaking students will be grouped.
Separate meetings are planned in
the future for these groups.
