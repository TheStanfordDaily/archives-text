# Ask Teaching Candidates To File Job Applications
## 
### 
Candidates for school positions for
the year 1933-34 are invited to reg
ister with Miss Ruth Tracksell of
the Appointment Service now. Re
quests for teachers for ihe following
year are often received as early as
January, and in order to be con
sidered for these opportunities it is
necessary to have at least prelimin
ary records on file at the Appoint
ment Service. Practice teachers who
have not yet started their teaching
assignments are eligible for registra
tion. On the basis of records to date
they may receive tentative nomina
tions for positions until the first re
ports on their practice teaching are
received.
