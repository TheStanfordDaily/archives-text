# German Travelers Heard At Honor Society Meet
## 
### 
At the meeting of the German
Honor Society Tuesday afternoon
five young Germans, who are on a
trip around the world earning their
way as they go, entertained the
members. Mr. Schafer, their leader,
told of their experiences in crossing
the United States in an old car. Sev
eral poems were recited and the five
joined in singing "Wanderlieder."
All of Ihem are natives of the
lower bank of the Rhine. Some of
them have completed their course
in the university while one has not
finished the gymnasium. When in
Washington they met President
Hoover.
