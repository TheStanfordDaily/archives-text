# Library Given Volumes On Stanford Law Suit
## 
### 
The personal set of Judge Jack
son Temple's books of 21 volumes
on the Colton versus Stanford case
were recently presented to the Stan
ford School of Law Library by R. A.
Ogg, of Palo Alto. Temple was the
judge for this case which involved
the distribution of assets when Sen
ator Stanford broke with Hunting
ton, Hopkins, and Colton, his asso
ciates.
