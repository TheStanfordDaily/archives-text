# Heche's Band To Play At Branner Informal
## 
### 
Gordon Heche, campus melody
master, and his orchestra will pro
vide the music for the annual Bran
ner Winter Informal to be held in
Branner Hall on Friday evening,
January 27.
According to Chairman James Al
gar, bids will sell for $2 and may be
had at the A.S.S.U. office or from
various committee members after
today. Members of the committee
include George Irvine, Jack Hanson,
Will Eisner, Marc Krupp, Jim
Blazer, and Algar.
