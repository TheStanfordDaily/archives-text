# Branner Informal Dance Bids Go On Sale Today
## 
### 
Bids for the Branner Winter In
formal to be held Friday, January
27, go on .sale this morning. They
will sell for $2, according to James
Algar, dance chairman, and may be
obtained at the A.S.S.U. office at 211
Branner, or from any of the com
mittee members.
