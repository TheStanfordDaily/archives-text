# MEXICO EXILE RETURNS
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Jan. 17 (/I 3 )— Pr
ofessor Aurelio Manrique, granted
permission to return to Mexico after
four years' exile in Los Angeles,
arrived here yesterday by train from
Laredo.
