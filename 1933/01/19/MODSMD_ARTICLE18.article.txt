# Selden Osborne To Speak Before Palo Alto Meeting
## 
### 
"Should the United Stales recog
nize Soviet Russia?" This question
will be discussed at. an open meet
ing in Palo Alto, Friday, January 20,
at 8 o'clock, in Union Hall, Univer
sity avenue, between Famoria and
Bryant streets.
The speakers of the evening will
be Selden Osborne, Stanford '32,
well known on the campus, and
Carl Ausmus, an electric worker
who visited the Soviet Union in
May of 1932. Osborne will speak on
the subject of recognition of the
Soviet Union and Ausmus will give
an answer to the articles by Will
Durant which have been appearing
recently in the Saturday Evening
Post.
