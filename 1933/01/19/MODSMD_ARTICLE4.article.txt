# HOLY DOOR OPENS APRIL 1
## 
### 
VATICAN CITY, Jan. 18 </P) —
Pope Pius today fixed the date for
the opening of the holy door of St.
Peter's as April 1, the* day before the
date set for the official inaugura
tion of the holy year.
