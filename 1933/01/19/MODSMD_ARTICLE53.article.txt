# Jackson Will Address Dames On Economics
## 
### 
"Present Economic Conditions" is
the topic for a talk by J. Hugh
Jackson, dean of the Graduate
School of Business, at the meeting
of the Stanford Dames at 3 o'clock
this afternoon in the Women's Club
house. Mrs. Henry Burke, violinist,
and Mrs. Charles Hunter, pianist,
will furnish the music for the pro
gram.
