# Comptroller To Address Committee On Housing
## 
### 
A. E. Roth, Comptroller of Stan- |
ford, will speak before the Stanford I
Student Committee to answer ques
tions of that body and give it infor
mation as to conduct of student in
stitutions on the University campus
at its regular meeting at 4:30 o'clock
Monday afternoon.
Roth has, at the committee's re
quest, agreed to give the adminis
tration's view on campus housing
and make suggestions as to proper
procedure for any action.
