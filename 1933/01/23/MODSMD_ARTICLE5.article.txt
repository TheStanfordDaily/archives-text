# Quad Proofs Obtainable Now At Watson Studio
## 
### 
Quad proofs of those who had
their pictures taken last quarter or
at the beginning of this quarter can
be obtained today at the Watson
Studio on Salvatierra.
It will be necessary for students
who have had two pictures taken to
designate a preference, if they de
sire to have any picture in the
Quad.
