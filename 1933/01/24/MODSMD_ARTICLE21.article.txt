# Branner Holds Dance Smoker
## 
### 
A six-minute, one-fall match be
tween Ted Hill, Stanford wrestling
coach, and one of his hefty proteges,
yet to be chosen, will be the feature
of a smoker to be held in Branner
Hall at 9:30 o'clock on Wednesday
evening.
In charge of Phil Williams, the
smoker is to advertise the Branner
Winter Informal which will be held
on Friday evening.
Gordon Heche and members of his
orchestra, headliners on the dance
program, will play during the
smoker. James Algar, dancc chair
man, will speak briefly.
Free cigarettes and cigars will be
distributed and refreshments will be
served.
