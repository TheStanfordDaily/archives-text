# ROOSEVELT, HOOVER CONFER
## 
### 

Negotiations were reopened between President Hoove/ and President
elect Roosevelt recently and resulted in their second private conference
in the White House since the election. Other attempts toward co-operation
over the war debt situation had ended in failure, hut this time a solution
was reached. The two national leaders agreed to deal immediately with
the troublesome world question and President Hoover will begin the
proceedings that Roosevelt will complete after his inauguration, March 4.
