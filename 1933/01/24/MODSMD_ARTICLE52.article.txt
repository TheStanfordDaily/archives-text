# OWNERS OF FAMOUS RADIO VOICES POSE FOR CAMERA
## 
### 

"How do you do, ladies and gentlemen; how DO you DO?" The greeting so familiar to Toyon corridors
and Greek living rooms comes from the dips of the same Norman Ik-okcnshire who is shown here "tuning up"
a few of radio's famous stars. Left to right: Tom Howard, George Shelton, Ruth Etting, Bing Crosby. Broken
shire, and Lennie Hayton.
