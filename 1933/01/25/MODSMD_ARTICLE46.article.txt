# Pi Lambda Theta Sponsor Lecture Next Wednesday
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, national honor
ary women's educational fraternity,
will be the sponsor for a lecture to
be given by Willard Giben in the
Little Theatre at 7:30 o'clock next
Wednesday evening. Giben is the
superintendent of schools in Oak
land and is president of the Cali
fornia Teachers Association.
"Proposed Legislation" will be the
subject of his lecture, and it will
pertain to the school legislation that
is being brought up in the California
Legislature this session. The lecture
will be of vital interest to all those
who are especially interested in
education, and is open to the public.
