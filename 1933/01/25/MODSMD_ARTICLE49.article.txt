# Junior Class To Hold Jolly-Up 7-8 Tonight
## 
### 
Music by the Royal Cardinals will
be the feature of the Junior Class
jolly-up to be held from 7 to 8
o'clock tonight in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Fred Kalenborn president of the
Class of '34, is in charge of the affair.
