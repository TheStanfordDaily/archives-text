# Babe Trackmen To Hold Important Meet Tonight
## 
### 
An important meeting of the en
tire freshman track squad will be
held at 7:15 o'clock tonight in Room
45 of the B.A.C. building, Coach Bill
Ellington announced yesterday.
Every member must be present, as
this will probably be the last meet-'
ing before the first frosh meet, El
lington said.
The frosh will clash with Menlo
and San Mateo junior colleges Feb
ruary 25 in the opening track meet
of the freshman season. These
schools usually have strong teams.
