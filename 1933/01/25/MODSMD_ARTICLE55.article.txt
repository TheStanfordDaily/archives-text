# FAMED ECONOMIST, STUART CHASE, IS BOOKED FOR CITY
## 
### 
Stuart Chase, nationally famous
economist and author of two best
Kellers, will appear in his only San
Francisco lecture when he speaks at
the Tivoli Theatre, Eddy street near
Powell, at 8:30 o'clock Tuesday eve
ning, February 14.
The title of the lecture will prob
ably be, "What Lies Ahead of Us?"
and will deal with the present-day
economic situation. Tickets for the
lecture are available at the Center
Office, 1215 St. Francis Hotel, at
prices ranging from 50 cents to $1.50.
Many good fifty- and seventy-five
cent tickets are available to students.
