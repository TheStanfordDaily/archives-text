WEDNESDAY
12:10 p.m.—French Table meets in
the alcove of the Union. All mem
bers of the University are cordially
invited to attend.
2:30 p.m.—The Reading Group of
the Stanford Dames will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse.
4 p.m.—Graduate Club will meet at
4 o'clock in Miss Barbara Shef
field's apartment (Miss Yost's resi
dence). All graduate students in
vited.
4-6 p.m.—Miss Yost will be at home,
532 Lasuen.
7 p.m.—Executive Committee of the
Stanford Y.W.C.A. will meet in
the clubrooms in the Women's
Clubhouse.
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of the Stanford
chapter of the American Society
of Mechanical Engineers.
7:30 p.m.—The student branch of
A.S.M.E. will meet in Room 280.
William Mason will speak on
"Problems of Fuel Knock."
8 p.m.—Professor Paul Kirkpatrick
will speak to the Science Associa
tion in the Faculty Clubhouse on
"Some Interactions of X-Rays and
Electrons."
