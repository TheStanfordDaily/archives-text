# John Burton To Speak In Palo Alto Tonight
## 
### 
John Burton, traveler and lecturer,
will be the principal speaker at the
Town Meeting to be held tonight at
the Community House.
Burton, who has appeared at
Stanford, has recently returned from
a world tour in which he studied
world problems and interviewed
political and economic leaders on
current universal problems. The
subject of his talk will be "The
Modern World Dilemma."
Stewart F. Bryant will be chair
man of the meeting.
