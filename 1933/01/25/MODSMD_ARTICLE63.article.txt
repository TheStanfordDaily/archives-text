# Y.W. Religious Committee Holds Session Tomorrow
## 
### 
Members of the religious commit
tee of the Stanford Y.W.C.A. will
meet for weekly supper in the "Y"
rooms of the Women's Clubhouse
tomorrow everting at 5:30 o'clock to
continue discussion on the topic,
"Realities of Life."
Florence Jones, chairman, wel
comes any woman student who is
interested in religious discussion.

of paper are used annually by news
papers in the United States.
