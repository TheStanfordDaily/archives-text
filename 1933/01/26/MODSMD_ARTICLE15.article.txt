# Blazing Ben With U.S. Track Stars To Tour Europe
## 
### 
Stanford's own Den Eastman,
holder of the official A.A.U.
world records in the 410- and
880-yard runs, has definitely
decided to accompany an
American track team which
will tour Europe this summer.
The Swedish National Athletic
Association is sponsoring the
trip of the American Olympic
stars who will compete in Swe
den and other European coun
tries for a period of three
weeks.
