# Irene Hardy Poetry Prize Entries Close February 1
## 
### 
Miss Edith Mirrielees of the Eng
lish department has announced that
poems entered in competition for
the prize of $50 in the Irene Hardy
poetry contest for college students
should be left with the secretary of
the department by February 1.
Details of the contest are posted
on the English bulletin board.
For its Sunday issue the Chicago
Tribune uses 35 carloads of paper.
