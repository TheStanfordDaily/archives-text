THURSDAY
2 p.m.—The Bridge Group of the
Stanford Dames will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse.
4:15 p.m. — University Philological
Association will meet in the
Women's Clubhouse. Papers will
be read by Professors Craig and
Coester.
5 p.m.—Pi Lambda Theta will hold
a business meeting on the first
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
5:10 p.m.—Warren D. Allen, Univer
sity organist, will give an organ
recital in the Memorial Church.
5:30 p.m.—Members of the Y.W.C.A. (
religious group will meet for sup
per and discussion.
7:45 p.m.—Stanford Bridgemen will
meet in El Tigre Club, Encina
Commons.
7:30 p.m.—The French Club will
present "Le Medicin Malgre Lui,"
by Moliere, in the Little Theatre.
The public is invited.
7:30 p.m.—Regular meeting of the
Christian Science. Organization on
the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
7:30 p.m.—The Walrus Club will
meet in the second floor of the
Women's Clubhouse to discuss
Technocracy.
7:45 p.m.—Stanford Bridgemen will
meet tonight in El Tigre dining
room to elect officei's.
