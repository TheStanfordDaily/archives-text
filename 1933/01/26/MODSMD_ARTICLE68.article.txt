# STOCKS IN MILD RALLY
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 25 (JP)—Stocks
braced themselves against the rail
shares today and closed atop a mild
rally.
From Wisconsin, comes the theory
that college students should marry,
so they would study rather than
"dash around."
