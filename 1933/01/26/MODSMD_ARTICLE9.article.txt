# Branner Dance Programs Here

### 
Programs for the Branner Winter |
Informal Friday evening may be ob
tained upon presentation or pur
chase of a bid at the A.S.S.U. office
or at 211 Branner Hall, announces
James Algar, chairman.
The programs have been smartly
printed upon parchment paper with
decorative silhouette effects, bear
ing out the decoration motif, on the
cover and flyleaf.
Gordon Heche and his orchestra,
featuring Pete Peck, will provide the
harmonies for the dance. Unique
lighting effects have been planned
by Joe Gordon, whose colored flood
lights will be used to set off decora
tive panels.
