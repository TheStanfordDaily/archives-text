# Schwartz Named Head Of Stanford Bridgemen
## 
### 
As the result of their election
meeting, the Stanford Bridgemen
elected their officers for the coming
year.
Dan Schwartz was elected to the
presidency, Joseph Mendelson vice
president, John Viera, secretary, and
Charles Bacon, treasurer.
With the finals of their tourna
ment to be played next week, the
club will have a busy season. They
have received a challenge from the
East Side Bridge Club of Palo Alto
and plan to play a series of matches
after their own finals for the campus
tourney are over.
