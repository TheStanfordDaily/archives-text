# Scripps Head Talks At Memorial Church Sunday
## 
### 
Rev. John W. Darr, professor of
religion at Scripps' College, will
preach on "Religion as a Way of
Seeing Things" at the regular 11
o'clock service Sunday morning in
the Memorial Church.
Music for the service will be as
follows:
Prelude: "Andante from the Fourth
Sonata" Mendelssohn
Introit: "Call to Worship".. .Rogers
Chant: "Benedictus es Domine"
Anthem: "No Shadows Yonder"
(from the "Holy City") Gaul
