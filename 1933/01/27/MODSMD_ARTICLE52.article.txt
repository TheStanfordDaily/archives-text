# Organist Will Repeat Recital Program Sunday
## 
###

Warren D. Allen will repeat the
program which he gave yesterday.

as follows:

In Paradisum Th. Dubois
Divertissement Louis Vierne
Lied
Finale from the "Symphonic
Etudes" R. Schumann
