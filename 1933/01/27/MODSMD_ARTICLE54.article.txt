# "SNOW BOUND" MAIL BY AIR
## 
### 
> BURBANK, Jan. 26 (TP)—'The first
delivery of air mail to Mount Wil
son, G,OOO-foot peak, was achieved
today by Pilot Allen Russell, flying
a United Air Lines biplane, who
dropped a sack of mail near the
doorstep of the observatory, which
has been snowbound since January
17.
