# SOLO DWELLERS ORGANIZE AS 'HERMITS, LTD.'
## 
### 

These men are the officers—and total membership—of "Hermits, Ltd.," an organization formed in Omah
of solo dwellers who've been out of contact with the world 35 years or more. Left to right: "Dynamit
Pete" Everett, cave dweller near South Bend, Neb.; Clark Watson, 91-year-old president of the grou
who is a shanty dweller near Elkhorn, Neb., and Henry Morris, cave dweller near Louisville, Neb. (A:
sociated Press Photo)
