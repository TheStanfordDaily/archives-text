# Three Women Named On Y.W. Honor Role
## 
### 
Names of three girls who have
earned places on the honor roll for
outstanding Y.W.C.A. work last
week have been announced by the
membership committee. Marian
Hattersley, Zoe Ann Hill, and Eu
nice Force are those who have given
exceptional service. The "Y" will
issue an honor roll each week.
Girls who are interested in poster
work are asked to drop in at the
"Y" rooms and see Mrs. David
Lamson, secretary.
