# STEAL BURIAL DRESSES
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Jan. 29 (/P)—'Thieves
who took two sample cases from Al
bert Eanhardt, a salesman, made a
giave error. The cases were found
today on a sidewalk. They contained
two ends of caskets and six burial
dresses.
