# Parsons To Talk At Y.W. Quarterly Open Meeting
## 
### 
Members of the JJcanlord Y.W.C.A.
will meet together at the quarterly
open meeting which is to be held
Wednesday evening at 7:30 o'clock
on the first floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Under the chairmanship of Elisa
beth Parmley, the International
committee of the "Y" is to furnish
the guest speaker of the evening,
who will be Miss Edith Parsons,
principal of the American Collegiate
Institute for Girls at Smyrna, Tur
key. She will speak on "The Turk
ish Women of Today."
