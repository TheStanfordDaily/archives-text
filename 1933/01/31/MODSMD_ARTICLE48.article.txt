###

Iheir already impressive intramural
basketball records last night when
they swamped Delta Upsilon and
Alpha Sigma Phi, respectively.

up 51 points to their opponents' 7.

Delta Chi 30 to 17 and El Cuadro
nosed out Kappa Sigma 15 to 13.
Sequoia 111 has entered a team in
the competition and will play its
first game against the Los Arcos
squad Wednesday, February 8. The
Phi Kap-El Tigre game, not listed in
the schedule, will be played Feb. 6.
