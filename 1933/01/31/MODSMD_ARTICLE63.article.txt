# Oakland Man Lectures Here Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Taking a subject dealing with pro
posed legislation relating to Cali
fornia schools, Willard E. Givens,
superintendent of schools in Oak
land, will speak here at 7:30 o'clock
tomorrow evening in the Little
Theatre.
The program will be under the
auspices of Upsilon Chapter of. Pi
Lambda Theta, which has issued an
invitation to all persons interested.
An informal reception in the Wo
men's Clubhouse honoring Mr. and
Mrs. Givens will follow the lecture.
