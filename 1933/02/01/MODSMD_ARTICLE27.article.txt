# Call Made For Entrants In Mural Swimming Meet
## 
### 
All campus organizations wishing
to enter teams in the annual intra
mural swimming meet to be held
February 28 and March 2 must turn
in a list of entrants to Coach Myron
Sprague or Gene Mires before Feb
ruary 14. This entry list must in
clude the events each contestant
plans to enter. It is imperative that
entries be turned in on time in order
that physical examinations can be
made two weeks before the meet.
No late entries will be accepted.
For further details concerning this
event Gene Mires, intramural man
ager, or Coach Sprague, should he
consulted.
