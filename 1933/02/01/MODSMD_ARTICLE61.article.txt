# Noted Writer To Speak On Humanism Tomorrow
## 
### 
George Sarton, author, historian,
philosopher, speaks tomorrow night
at 7:30 o'clock to a Little Theatre
audience on "The New Humanism
and the History of Science."
A member of the American Acad
emy of Arts and Science, native of
Belgium, Sarton has a degree from
the University of Ghent. He is an
associate of the Carnegie Institute of
Washington and is editor of "Isis,"
international philosophy review.
The author of "Introduction to
the History of Science," Dr. Sarton
has also written a number of papers,
scientific, humanistic, philosophic.
Sarton is delivering the Hitchcock
lectures at the University of Cali
fornia.
