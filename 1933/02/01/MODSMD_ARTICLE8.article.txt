# Harry Noland, '28, Named Monterey District Attorney
## 
### 
Harry Noland, graduate of the
Stanford School of Law in the Class
of 1928, was recently appointed Dis
trict Attorney of Monterey county
to finish the term of Russell Scott,
who recently resigned.
Noland, who was, prominent while
here on the campus, both in the law
school and in dramatics, ha.; been
deputy district attorney of Monterey
county since he graduated from
Stanford.
