# Student Leaders Meet With Off-Campus Group
## 
### 
Off-campus women are reminded
by Mary Parsons, president, that
there will be an informal meeting
of the organization from 4 to 5:30
o'clock this afternoon in the Y.W.
rooms on the third floor of the wom
en's Clubhouse.
Betts Hawkins, women's editor of
the Stanford Daily, has been added
to the list of guests for the after
noon. which will include Mary-K.
Morris and Jeanne Millard, presi
dent and secretary of the A.W.S.
respectively.
