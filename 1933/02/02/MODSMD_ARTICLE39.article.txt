# Dr. Wrenn To Speak At Stanford Dames Meeting
## 
###

vocational guidance, will speak on

o'clock this afternoon in the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Music for the pro
gram will be furnished by Mrs.
Richard Ogg, who will sing two se
lections. Her accompanist will be
