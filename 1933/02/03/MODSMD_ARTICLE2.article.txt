# Dance Proceeds Will Go To Convalescent Home
## 
### 
Gala celebration will feature a
festive Valentine party at the St.
Francis Hotel next Wednesday night.
The affair will be sponsored by the
Junior Auxiliary of the Stanford
Convalescent Home and proceeds
will go to that organization.
Ted Fio Rita's orchestra will sup
ply the music. For entertainment
there will be a short fashion show,
several professional cabaret dancers,
and a dancing contest. Stanford and
Junior Auxiliary judges will award
a cup to the winners. Cover charge
is $1 per person for dancing and
$2.50 per plate for dinner.
