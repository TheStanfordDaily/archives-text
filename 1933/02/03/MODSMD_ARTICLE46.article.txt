# Frosh Must Report For Tennis Practice Tuesday
## 
### 
All freshman tennis men, either
out for the team or signed up on the
ladder rankings, are asked by Coach
Sherman Lockwood to report to the
varsity tennis courts at 2:30 o'clock
Tuesday afternoon.
Practice will be held, and follow
ing this a frosh captain for the 1933
season will be elected. Lockwood
advises all desirous of remaining on
the squad to turn out.
A Belgian, August Meunier, wrote
17,131 words on a postal card.
