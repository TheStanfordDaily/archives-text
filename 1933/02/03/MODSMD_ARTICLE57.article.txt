# ROGERS' WIFE RECOVERING
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Fob, 2 (/P.) — Mrs.
Will Rogers, wife of the humorist,
was resting easily today in the Cedar
of Lebanon Hospital, and beyond all
danger, following an operation last
Monday for appendicitis.
