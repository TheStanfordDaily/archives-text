# Y.W.C.A. Cabinet, Board Hold Joint Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Y.W.C.A.
cabinet will meet for dinner at the
quarterly advisory board-cabinet
meeting tomorrow night at li o'clock
at the home of Mrs. George W.
Dowric, 421 El Escarpado, Campus.
Rose Terlin, Y.W. member from
the University of California in
Berkeley will be the guest of honor.
Cabinet officers will meet at 5:45
in the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse to arrange transportation.
Members who are unable to attend
are requested by Mrs. David Lam
son, executive secretary, to provide
a substitute.
