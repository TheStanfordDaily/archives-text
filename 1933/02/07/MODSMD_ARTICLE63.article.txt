# "Y" To Appease Roughs' Hunger With Hot Dogs
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Y.W.C.A.
will offer the campus something new
in the way of tempting food tomor
row morning, announces Barbara
Binns, finance chairman. For 10
cents, hot dogs will be sold by Y.W.
women tomorrow from 7:30 o'clock
in the morning until 2 o'clock in the
afternoon, she says.
Booths will be arranged on the
lawn in front of the Women's Club
house to accommodate customers.
