# Y.W. To Honor Romanic Language Faculty At Tea
## 
### 
Honoring faculty members of the
Romanic Language department, the
Stanford Y.W.C.A. will hold a tea
Friday afternoon, February 10, from
3:30 to 5:30 o'clock in the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Invitations have been exte ided to
all the department members and
their wives. Professor Henry Lanz,
associate professor of Slavic lan
guages, and Mis. Lanz have also
been invited.
The tea is under the auspices of
the social committee, of which Ella
mae Dodds and Rose Cubbison,
chairmen of the social committee,
are in charge of the preparations
for the tea.
