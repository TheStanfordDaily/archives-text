# Stanford Rugby Club To Play Olympic Club Squad
## 
### 
The Stanford Rugby Club will
take- on Charlie Austin's Olympic
Club team at Ewing Field in San
Francisco Saturday afternoon.
Austin has the distinction of hav
ing never coached a losing team. In
his career, Berkeley High School,
Santa Clara', Stanford, and the
American 1924 Olympic team have
all been under his supervision, and
not one of his teams have been de
feated.
The winning team in Saturday's
contest will lay claim to the North
ern California title, and will arrange
a game with the Pasadena Reds to
be played in this district for the
state championship. Pasadena has
defeated all the Southern teams, in
cluding the Los Angeles Blue Jays
and the Hollywood Hawks.
