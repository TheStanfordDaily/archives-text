Come One, Come All
BE PRESENT AT THE INAUGURATION OF A NEW
ERA IN PALO ALTO CUISINES
THE TIME: Thursday night (tonight)
THE PLACE: Hotel President Dining Room
THE EVENT: A 10-Course Italian Dinner
75c
MAKE YOUR TABLE RESERVATIONS EARLY
Our staff of waitresses has been increased in order that you may
be served with the maximum of speed and efficiency.
For the benefit of those not desiring the Italian Dinner, we shall serve
our usual 50c and 65c table d'hote dinners.
We now serve a 25c counter lunch which offers hot entree, vegetable,
dessert, coffee or milk.
HOTEL PRESIDENT COFFEE SHOP &
DINING ROOM
