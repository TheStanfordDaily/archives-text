# Y.W.C.A. Tea Will Honor Romanic Language Heads
## 
### 
Faculty members of the Romanic
Language department and their
wives will be the guests of honor
at the Y.W.C.A. tea from 3:30 to
5:30 o'clock tomorrow afternoon on
the third floor of the Women's Club
house. A special invitation has been
extended to Henry Lanz, professor
of Slavic Languages, and Mrs. Lanz.
Ellamae Dodds and Rose Cub
bison, chairmen of the social com
mittee, are in charge of the tea.
All students interested are cordially
invited, according to the chairmen.
