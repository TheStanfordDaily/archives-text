# Faculty Women's Club Plans Meeting Monday
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Faculty
Club will hold its regular meeting
at 3 o'clock next Monday afternoon,
February 12, in the Women's Club
house on the campus.
A musical program, to be an
nounced later, is being arranged by
Mrs. Warren D. Allen, Mrs. Eliza
beth Peirce, Mrs. Harold Elliot, and
Mrs. Alexander Naughton (Ulrich
Cole).
