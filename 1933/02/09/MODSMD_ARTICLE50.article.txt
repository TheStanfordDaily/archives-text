# HOOVER MAY VISIT CANAL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 8 (/P)
President Hoover said today he did
not expect to leave the United States
after March 4, except possibly to
pass through the Panama Canal
Zone on his way home.

Among the freshman command
ments published in the Utah Chron
icle is: "Thou shalt not keep thyself
in the presence of one woman: for
it is not good."
