# Women Begin Interclass Hoop Schedule Today
## 
### 
Women's interclass basketball
games start this afternoon with a
junior-senior tilt and a game be
tween the second freshman team and
the second string sophomores, just
lately organized from the extra
sophomore material. The toss-off oc
curs at 4:20 o'clock in the women's
gym.
On account of a change in sched
ule, the second sophomore and the
second freshman team will play on
Thursdays hereafter instead of on
Fridays. They will play their other
four games on February 16 and 23
and March 2 and 9. There is no
other change in the schedule pre
viously announced.
