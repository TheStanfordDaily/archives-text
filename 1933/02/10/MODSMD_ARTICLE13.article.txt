# Reverend White To Give Church Service Sunday
## 
### 
The Reverend Lynn White, D.D.,
of San Anselmo, will preach the
sermon at the service at 11 o'clock
Sunday morning in the Memorial
Church. Music for the service is as
follows:
Prelude: "Monmouth" Wood
Introit: "God of Our Fathers"
Old English Melody
Canticle: "Benedictus es, Doininc"
Anthem: "Come, Labor On"..Noble
