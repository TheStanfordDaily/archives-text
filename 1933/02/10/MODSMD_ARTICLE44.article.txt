# Stockton Chorus Program To Be Presented Sunday
## 
### 
j
The Troubadours, a chorus under
the direction of Frank Thornton
Smith, and a string quartet led by
Miss Virginia Short, from Stockton
High School, will give the program
at 4 o'clock Sunday afternoon in the
Memorial Church in place of the
regular organ recital. The accom
panist is Kenneth Loornis.
