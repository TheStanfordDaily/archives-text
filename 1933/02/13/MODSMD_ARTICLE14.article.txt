# Seventeen Women Win "Y" Honor Roll Places
## 
###

! the Y.W. honor roll last week.
They are: Helen Hambleton, Mar
tha Hoerr, Adrian Kneass, Helen
McMahon, Rosalie Mauzy, Muriel
Moreton, Ruth Nutting, Molly Phil
lips, Mabel Ponder, Marie Porter,
I Josephine Schlatter, Joyce Smart,
Margaret Thum, Kathleen Thorburn,
Dorothy Wheaton, Elizabeth Wicher,
;.nd Louise Whitaker.
