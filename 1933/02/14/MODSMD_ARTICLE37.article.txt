# "Y" Cabinet Gathers At Dinner Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
Members of the Y.W.C.A. cabinet
will meet for dinner at the home of
Miss Anita Hodgkin tomorrow night
at 5:45 o'clock. Officers will meet in
the third floor of the Women's Club
house at 5:30 o'clock to arrange for
transportation.
Therina Guerard, president, re
quests that each member bring a
report of committee proceedings
since the Christmas session.
