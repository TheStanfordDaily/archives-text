# RUSS FLYER HURT
## 
### 
MOSCOW, Feb. 14 — Vodo
pianov, the aviator, one of Russia's
most famous flyers, was injured and
his mechanic was killed' today in a
crush which wrecked their plane
near the Siberian village of Misso
vaya, on Lake Baikal.

Iceland has neither prisons nor
policemen.
