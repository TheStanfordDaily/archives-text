# Actress Married
## 
### 

The marriage of Josephine Dunn,
actress, to Eugene John Lewis,
Philadelphia attorney, on Jan. 6,
was recently disclosed. Miss Dunn
has appeared in films and Broad
way productions and at present is
in the cast of a New York musical
comedy. (Associated Press Photo)
