# "Australia" Lecture Topic At Dames Meeting Today
## 
### 
'"Australia" will be the subject of
the talk of Dr. J. Stanley Morrison,
minister of the Presbyterian Church
in Palo Alto, when he speaks at the
meeting of the Stanford Dames at
3 o'clock this afternoon in the Wo
men's Clubhouse.
Music for the program will be
furnished by Claude Schaull, soloist.
