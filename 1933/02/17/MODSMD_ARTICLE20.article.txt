# Y.W.C.A. Tea To Honor English Faculty Today
## 
### 
Honoring faculty menjbers of the
English department, the social com
mittee of tine Y.W.C.A. will present
its second academic tea of the quar
ter this afternoon on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse from 3:30
to 5:30 o'clock.
Ellamae Dodds, chairman, urges
both men and women students to
attend. These teas are being given
in order to allow social contact be
tween students and their professors.
Barbara Steinbeck is in charge of
preparations for the tea.
