# Distinguished Historian Addresses Campus Group
## 
### 
Dr. Herbert E. Bolton, University
of California professor, considered
an outstanding authority in the field
of South American history, spoke
before members of the Stanford His
tory Club at its quarterly banquet
meeting held Wednesday evening.
The distinguished speaker spoke
of H. H. Bancroft, who has created
a new field for historical investiga
tion, that of Western North America.
