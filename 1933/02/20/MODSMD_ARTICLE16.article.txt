# Fortenbraugh Speaks On Railroad Electrification
## 
### 
Problems of railroad electrification
were discussed Thursday at a meet
ing of the Stanford Electrical En
gineering Seminar by S. B. Forten
braugh of San Francisco, in charge
of railroad electrifying for the Gen
eral Electric Company.
Experiences in building electric
railroads in the United States,
South America, Europe, and India
were discussed by Fortenbraugh.
