# Humor Publication Irks W.U. Religious Societies
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Feb. 19 (PIP)-Reli
gious societies at the University of
Washington branded as sacrilegious
the February issue of Columns,
campus humor publication.
The cover of the magazine, which
irked the religious groups, depicted
an art gallery in which a portrait
of the Madonna was shown handing
the Beloved Infant to Napoleon with
the words, "You hold him awhile,
I'm tired."
