# Graduate Women Will Stage Jolly-up Tonight
## 
### 
The graduate women will give a
Jolly-up tonight from 7 to 8 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse to pro
vide an opportunity for the gradu
ate students to meet each other so
cially.
There will be dancing and bridge.
Gordon Heche's orchestra will fur
nish the music.
All the women intending to come
are asked to see Lucy Guild be
tween 1 and 2 o'clock today in the
Women's Clubhouse. The women
will be hostesses at the party and
each one is to pay 50 cents.
