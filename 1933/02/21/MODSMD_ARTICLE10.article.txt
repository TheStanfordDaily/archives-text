# California Professor To Address Business School
## 
### 
Henry F. Grady from the Univer
sity of California will be the speaker
of the evening when the Graduate
School of Business meets at 6:15
o'clock tonight in the Union dining
room. Grady is professor of Inter
national Trade and Dean of the Col
lege of Commerce. Political circles
have rumored that he may be named
on the new Roosevelt cabinet.
The speaker has chosen as his
topic, ''The Effect of Technological
Development on Foreign Trade."
