# Huskies Threaten O.S.C. In Northern Hoop Race
## 
### 
Defeating University of Oregon
Saturday night, 29 to 18, Oregon
State's hoopmen established them
selves at the top of the Northern
Division of the Coast Conference.
Washington, in second place with
four games to play this week, can
nose out the Orangemen by winning
all their tilts, provided the latter
drop one of their two 1 remaining
games with the cellar Oregon team.
Captain Ed Lewis, Oregon State
center, continued to lead Northern
Division scorers with 143 points in
13 games.
