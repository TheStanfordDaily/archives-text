# Finals For All-University Wrestling Postponed Week
## 
### 
Preliminary and finals bouts for
all-University wrestling champion
ships in the seven weights have
been postponed until next week.
Under the auspices of the Student
Relief Committee the fight finals
will be held in the Pavilion with an
admission charge for student relief.
As planned, the final bouts will
be Thursday night, March 2, and
the preliminaries, which will be held
in the mat room of the gymnasium,
will take place on Wednesday after
noon at 4 o'clock.
