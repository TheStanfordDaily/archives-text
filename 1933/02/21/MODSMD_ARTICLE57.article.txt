# Babe Water Poloists Will End Season With Cal Tilt
## 
### 
In a finale of the present season,
Stanford's freshman water polo team
battles the California yearling septet
at 3:30 o'clock tomorrow afternoon
in the new Berkeley pool, in what
should be a close, hard-fought tilt.
Six wins, two losses, and one tie
constitute the Babes' record to date,
a 7-2 win over the Olympjc Club
being the most impressive.
