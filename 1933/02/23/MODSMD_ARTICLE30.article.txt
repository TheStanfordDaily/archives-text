# T.R. WIDOW IN SEATTLE
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Feb. 22 (/I 3 ) — Mrs.
Theodore Roosevelt, widow of the
former President, arrived in Seattle
today from the Philippine Islands,
where her son, Theodore Roosevelt,
Jr., is Governor General.
