# Russian Scholar To Talk At S.F. Club Tomorrow
## 
### 
Alexander Kaun, Ph.D., Associate
Professor of Slavic Languages at the
University of California, will speak
at 8:15 o'clock tomorrow night in the
San Francisco Women's City Club
Building, 465 Post Street, on "Maxim
Gorky and His Russia." Kaun has
written several books on Russia,
among them: "Russia Under Nich
olas II," "Tolstoy and Gorky," and
"Byzantine Art."
