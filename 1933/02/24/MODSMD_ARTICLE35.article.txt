Feather leis, especially, or feather
collars. Best on evening things, this
unique trim ranges from genuine coq
feathers to fabrics.
Together with these, the petal
motif especially of organdie or lin
gerie, is evident, and with a similar
effect. Big and light and very flatter
ing and soft, such "boas appear
with the smartest costumes.
