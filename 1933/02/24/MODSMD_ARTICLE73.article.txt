# Tank Poloists Meet Clubmen

### 
Stanford aquatic fans who wish to
see their varsity water polo cham
pions in action on the Farm will
have their last opportunity when
the Indians tangle with the Athens
Club septet in Encina Pools at 3:30
o'clock tomorrow afternoon.
To date Coach Nort Thorn
ton's proteges have been unde
feated and have piled up 42
points against 12 for their op
ponents.

include Wiget, Triolo, and Hirsch,
forwards; Taranik, Graves, and

There will be no admission charge
and women spectators are invited.
