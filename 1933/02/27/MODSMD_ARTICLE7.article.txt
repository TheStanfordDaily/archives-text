# Cap And Gown To Give Benefit Bridge Party
## 
### 
Cap and Gown will give a schol
arship bridge benefit Friday after
noon at 2 o'clock in the Women'.s
i Clubhouse. The Row, Roble, and
, Cap and Gown will donate candy to
be sold during the afternoon.
There will be an entertainment at
4 o'clock during tea consisting of
; songs by "Mills Sisters," Frances
Mosher, Dorothy Willis, and Lucy
I Guild; a song and tap dance by Jane
Hewlett, and a short skit.
