# Get Wrong Car In College Comedy
## 
### 
KEHKELEY, Feb. 27 (/P) —An
automobile waited at the curb
as Elizabeth and Catherine
I'lanncry, sisters, walked from
a university sority house on the
arms of their escorts, J. M. Es
pinosa and Ingel Sleider.
The girls got in. The boys
exchanged pleased looks and
got in. Away they went.
Scene two—police headquar
ters.
A. J. Howell: "It's my car and
I saw them take it."
The girls to the boys: "We
thought it was your car!"
The boys to the girls: "We
thought it was yours!"
The police: "No runs, no hits,
two errors. Go home, every
body."
