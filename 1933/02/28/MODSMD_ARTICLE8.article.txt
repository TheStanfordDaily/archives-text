# Briggs Named On Staff Of Senator W. G. McAdoo
## 
### 
Reid Briggs, '32, former associate
editor of the Daily, left last night for
Washington, D.C., where he will join
the secretarial staff of Senator Wil
liam Gibbs McAdoo.
While at Stanford Briggs was
president of the local chapter of
Sigma Delta Chi, journalistic honor
society, and a member of Sigma
Alpha Epsilon fraternity.
