# Stanford Alumnae Speak At Annual Girlhood Meet
## 
### 
Miss Catherine Spaulding, '32, and
Miss Marian Holly, '30, will be fea
tured speakers on the program of
the Sixth Annual Conference on
Twentieth Century Girlhood to be
conducted by the Los Angeles Girls'
Council on Friday and Saturday,
March 2 and 3, at the University of
Southern California. Miss Spaulding
will discuss the benefits of college
to the girl of today, and Miss Holly
will talk on world cooperation.
