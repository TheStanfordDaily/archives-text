# Visiting Cleric Presents Third Lecture Of Series
## 
### 
"The Art of Meditation" will be
the subject of the Right Reverend
William P. Remington's talk when
he speaks at 5 o'clock today in the
Women's Clubhouse. The Rev. Rem
ington is bishop of Eastern Oregon.
In the third of his five-day series,
the Rev. Remington yesterday after
noon talked on "Straight Thinking."
"To be a really good person," Dr.
Remington said, "one must not
merely refrain from doing evil, but
accept every opportunity to reach
for the higher things of nature."
