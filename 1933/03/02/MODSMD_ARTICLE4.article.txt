# Professor Anderson Will Meet Religious Group
## 
### 
Professor Frederick Anderson of
the Romance Language department
will speak to the religious group of
the Y.W.C.A. at G o'clock tonight in
the third floor of the Women's Club
house at the weekly supper meeting
of the committee. The topic of Pro
fessor Anderson's talk will be "My
Philosophy of Life."
All students interested are re
quested to make reservations with
Mrs. David Lamson at Palo Alto
4301. Dinner charge is 15 cents.
