# Bookstore Investigators Plan To Correlate Work
## 
### 
Members of the investigating com
mittee of the Stanford Bookstore
who have been delving into the
finances of the store for some time
will hold a meeting shortly, Chair
man Abe Mellinkoff announced yes
terday. It is planned to correlate the
results of various angles of the in
vestigation at the meeting.
A number of recommendations are
expected to develop from the meet
ing of the committee. Thus far in
the investigation no results have
been announced.
