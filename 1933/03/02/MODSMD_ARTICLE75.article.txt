# EIFERT TOPS PIPER IN RACE FOR HOOP HIGH POINT HONORS
## 
### 
Hal Eifert, sharpshooting Cali
fornia forward, nosed out Don Piper
! of U.C.L.A. by three points in the
race for high-point honors in the
southern division of the Pacific
i Coast basketball conference.
Eifert scored 131 points to Piper's
128 digits. Keith Topping, with 105
points, was Stanford's leading
scorer and in fourth place in the
rankings, following Lee Guttero,
U.S.C. center.
Stanford placed six men among
the high 24 scorers in the confer
ence. In addition to Topping, Tom
Cordry, John Hancock, Hal Edelen,
Jay Tod, and Bill Schnetka placed.
