# Foreign Acquaintance Tour Held By Y.W. Saturday
## 
### 
Places are slill available to those
who are interested in making the
Y.W.C.A. Acquaintanceship Tour
which is being held in conjunction
with the International Department
of the Y.W. at the University of
California at Berkeley.
The Tour will begin tomorrow af
ternoon at 3 o'clock and will in
clude visitations to the Japanese and
Chinese Y.W.C.A.'s in San Fran
cisco, followed by dinner at either
a Japanese hotel or Chinese restaur
ant.
Anyone wishing to make reserva
tions is asked to inform Mrs. David
Lamson at the "Y" rooms.
