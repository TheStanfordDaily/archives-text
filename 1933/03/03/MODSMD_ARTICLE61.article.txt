# Scholarships For 1933-34 To Be Awarded In Spring
## 
### 
All University scholarships for
1933-34, except those reserved for
undergraduates, will be awarded
during spring quarter. Application
blanks and detailed information may
be obtained at the office of the Com
mittee on Scholarship. Applications
for graduate scholarships are due
April 4, for undergraduate scholar
ships April 15.
