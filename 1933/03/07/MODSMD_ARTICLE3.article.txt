# DeMolay Club Visits Burlingame Initiation
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford DeMolay
Club are invited to an initiation
meeting of the Peninsula chapter
being held in Burlingame tomorrow
night, Douglas Holcomb, president
of the Stanford club, announced
yesterday.
New members of the Stanford club
will be initiated, Holcomb said. De
parture for tlie local group will be
from Toyon lobby, where the mem
bers will gather at 7:30 o'clock.
