# CHURCHES PASS UP PLATE
## 
### 
MILWAUKEE, Wis., March 6 </P)
—Some of the city's churches de
cided to have a moratorium on pass
ing the plate Sunday because of the
bank holiday.
Washington State has an ordinance
preventing co-eds to wear red.
