# Lower Division Women To Confer With Dean Yost
## 
### 
Lower division women should
make appointments today to see
Dean Yost during her advisory
hours on Wednesday, Thursday, and
Friday, March 8, 9, and 10. She will
be in her office from 10 to 12 o'clock
in the morning and from 2 to 4
o'clock in the afternoon to help
lower division women with their
spring quarter programs.
