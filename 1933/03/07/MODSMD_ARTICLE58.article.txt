# Nomad Church Group To Visit Catholic Church
## 
### 
Members of the Nomad Church
'group will travel to the Catholic
Church in Palo Alto Sunday, March
12. at 10:30 o'clock in the morning in
the second of a series of visits to
various religious institutions in the
Bay district.
Kay Thorburn, chairman of the
group, announces that anyone inter
ested in making the visit should
notify Mrs. David Lamson at the "Y"
