# "Y" Cabinet Holds Final Meet At Dinner Tonight
## 
### 
Members of the Y.W.C.A. cabinet
will meet for the last time this
quarter at the weekly supper meet
ing at 5:30 o'clock tonight in the
Women's Clubhouse. Those who will
be unable to attend must notify Mrs.
David Lamson at the "Y" rooms.
