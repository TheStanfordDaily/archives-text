# Women's Phys. Ed. Dept. Adds Theoretical Studies
## 
### 
Three new theoretical courses
have been added to the women's
physical education department for
spring quarter. "Introduction to
Physical Education" will be given
next quarter by Miss Gertrude
Hawley.
An entirely new theoretical
course, "The Philosophy of the
Dance," will be presented by Mrs.
Paul Radir spring quaxier. "Swim
ming Technique" will also be given
by Mrs. Radir. A junior lifesaving
certificates or its equivalent is a
prerequisite for the course.
