WEDNESDAY
9:10 a.m.—There will be a lecture
on "Polarographic Study of Com
plexes; Amphoters, Cyanides, etc."
by Professor J. Heyrovsky in the
Chemistry Building, Room 20.
12:10 p.m.—There will be a meeting
of the French Table in the alcove
of the Union. All members of the
University are cordially invited.
7 p.m.—The newly formed Pre-
Medical Club will meet in Room
430, Jordan Hall. All students in
terested are invited to attend.
7:45 p.m.—Stanford Bridgemen meet
in El Tigre dining room.
