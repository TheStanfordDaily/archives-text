# Spring Registration Hop Slated By Band Group
## 
### 
A Registration Dance will be
given at the Women's Gym on April
3, the opening day of the spring
quarter. A1 Ru.shten's Royal Car
dinals 10-piece augmented orchestra
will provide the music and special
lighting effects will be featured.

The dance will be sponsored by
Kappa Kappa Psi, honorary band
fraternity, which put on the most
successful depression dance last
quarter. A new low will be reached
with an admission charge of $1 per
couple. Checks will be acceptable.
