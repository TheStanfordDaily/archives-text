# Sequoia Club Votes $30 For Baseball Equipment
## 
### 
Meeting for the last time of the
quarter, the Sequoia Social Club
discussed plans for spring quarter
dances and athletics last night in
the lobby of the hall.
The members voted expenditure
of $30 for mural baseball equipment
and set a date for an all-campus
dance to be sponsored by Sequoia.
Douchnut.s and cider were .served.
