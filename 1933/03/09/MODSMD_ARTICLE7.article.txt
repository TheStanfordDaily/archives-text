# Plans For Y.W. Rummage Sale Will Be Made Today
## 
### 
Barbara Binns, chairman of the
Y.W.C.A. finance committee, an
nounces the meeting this afternoon
of members of the rummage sale
committee at 5 o'clock on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
Plans will be formulated for the
second sale, under the sponsorship
of the organization, which is sched
uled for Saturday, April 22, in Palo
Alto. Concerted drives for contribu
tions to the sale will be held in the
early part of April with a committee
under the general chairmanship of
Evelyn Schilling.
