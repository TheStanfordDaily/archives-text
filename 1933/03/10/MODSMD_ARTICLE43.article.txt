# HOTEL GUESTS GET RIDE
## 
### 
CHICAGO, March 9 (TP)—Ten New
Yorkers, stranded in Chicago with
out available cash because of the
bank holiday, were on their way
bark home today in a bus chartered
by the hotel at which they had been
stopping.
