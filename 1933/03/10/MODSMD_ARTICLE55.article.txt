# Stanford Professor To Tour Japan And Russia
## 
### 
Kurt Lewin, acting professor of
psychology, will sail next Monday to
Japan on a lecture tour. He will
travel through Japan and Russia,
lecturing at the universities of
Tokyo and Moscow. He expects to
arrive in Germany early in May
where he will continue his work.
Professor Lewin, originally con
nected with the Institute of Psych
ology at the University of Berlin,
has been for the last 10 months act
ing professor of psychology at Stan
ford. His work in the field of dy
namic problems has attracted at
tention in Europe as well as in the
United States.
