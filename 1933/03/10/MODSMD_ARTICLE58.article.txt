# Schenck Awarded Belgian Fellowship In Geology
## 
### 
Hubert G. Schenck, assistant pro
fessor of geology at Stanford, has
been awarded an advance fellow
ship for study in Belgium during
the year 1933-34, according to word
received here yesterday by Perrin
C. Galpin, secretary of the C.R.B.
Education Foundation established by
the Commission for Relief in Bel
gium.
Schenck, one of seven Americans
to receive an award, will study ma
rine oligocene formation at the
Royal Museum of Natural History
in Belgium and will do field work.
