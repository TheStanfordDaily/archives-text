# Wilbur Will Speak At Tomorrow's Assembly
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur will make
his first appearance before the Stan
ford student body since returning to
the duties of active president when
he will address an all-University
assembly at 10 o'clock Wednesday
morning in the Assembly Hall.
Dr. Wilbur will have as his sub
ject, "Why Be Educated?"
