# Pomona Glee Club Will Sing At Sunday Services
## 
### 
Services at the Memorial Church
on Sunday will include music by the
Pomona College men's glee club.
The thirty-four students and their I
director, Professor Ralph Lyman, a !
cousin of Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, j
have won first place in eight Pacific
Coast contests of college glee clubs,
and last year received first prize in
a national intercollegiate glee club
contest at St. Louis in which Yale
placed second.
The students from Claremont will
be the guests of the University Sun
day noon at dinner in the Union.
