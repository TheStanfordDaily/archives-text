# SHE'S BEAUTY EXPERTS' IDEAL
## 
### 

Thel Hampton was selected as "Miss 1934" by the vote of 32,000
beauty experts In answer to a questionnaire. They voted her as coming
the closest to depicting the trend of American womanhood. She is 20
years old, weighs 115, has auburn hair, hazel eyes, a 33-inch bust, 35-inch
liips and a 26-inch waist. (Associated Press Photo)
