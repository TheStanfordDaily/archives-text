# Reforestation To Employ 25,000 Men Within Week
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 3 (/P)—
President Roosevelt today selected
Robert Fenchner of Massachusetts
as director of unemployment to su
pervise the reforestation program.
Fenchner, who is vice-president
of the International Association of
Machinists, conferred today with
Government experts at the White
House and was given the assign
ment of starting a movement of
25,000 into the forests by the end
of the week.
