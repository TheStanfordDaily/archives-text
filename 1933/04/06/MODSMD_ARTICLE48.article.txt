# Chinese Youths Discuss Far East Developments
## 
### 
Latest developments in Manchu
kuo and advances of the Chinese
Soviet government will be related
by two Chinese youths, Lawrence
Choy and Antonio Kwock, at a pub
lic meeting at 8 o'clock tonight at
363 Melville avenue, Palo Alto.
As representatives of the newly*
organized Chun Sing Chinese Youth
Association of America, Choy and
Kwock are lecturing to Northern
California audiences on Far Eastern
war and economic conditions.
Students and the public have been
invited to attend the meeting free
of charge.
