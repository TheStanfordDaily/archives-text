# Pistol Team To Start Work For Spring Meets
## 
### 
Spring practice for the R.O.T.C.
pi.stol team will begin next week on
the same schedule as last quarter,
according to an announcement yes
terday by Major E. C. Williams.
The first match to be fired will be
the national R.O.T.C. field artillery
match early in May. The team will
consist of five men firing slow, rapid,
and timed fire. Other matches, in
cluding one with the Olympic Club
are scheduled for later in the quar
ter.
