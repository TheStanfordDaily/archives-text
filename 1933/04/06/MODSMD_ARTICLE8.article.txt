# Jimmy Walker Wedding Banns Posted In France
## 
### 
CANNES. France, April 5 (/P> —
Marriage banns were posted today
at the city hall here by former
Mayor Walker of New York and
Betty Compton, the actress. They
| may now be married any time after
nine days have elapsed.
