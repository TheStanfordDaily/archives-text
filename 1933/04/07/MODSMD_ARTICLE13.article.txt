# Rothert Given Alumni Post
## 
### 
Rothert, '30, famed Farm
athlete, today steps into the newly
created position of Field Secretary
of the Stanford Alumni Association.
The appointment was made last
night in San Francisco at the month
ly meeting of the Alumni Council.
As Field Secretary, Rothert will
work in conjunction with John E.
McDowell, present executive secre
tary.
Rothert has been holding the posi
tions of vice-president of the Stan
ford Alumni Club of Los Angeles,
and president of the Los Angeles
group of junior alumni.
