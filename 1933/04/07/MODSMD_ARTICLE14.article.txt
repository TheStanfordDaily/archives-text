# German Honor Society To Give Two Comedies
## 
### 
To start out its activities of the
spring quarter, the German honor
society at Stanford will give two
German comedies (Schroventide) by
Hans Sachs in the Women's Club
house at 5:15 o'clock next Tuesday
afternoon. The plays are entitled
"Das Kalberutes" and "Der Fahr
ende Schuler."
Dr. Kurt Reinhardt of the Ger
man department is directing the pro
ductions. Members of the cast are
Claire Mitchell, Gunther Detcrt,
Henry Lindgrin, Robert Gordon, and
Hans Leschke. The public is invited.
Preceding the plays, there will be
a regular meeting of the German
honor society at 3:30 o'clock.
