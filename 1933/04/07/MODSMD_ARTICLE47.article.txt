# New Swim Hours For Women's Pool Posted
## 
### 
Open hours for the women's pool
have had to be changed because of
the opening of new classes, an
nounced Mrs. Ruth Radir, director
of swimming.
The pool will be open the rest of
the quarter from 2 to 3 o'clock on
Tuesdays and Thursdays, from 5 to
G o'clock on Tuesdays and Fridays,
and on Saturday from 10:30 to 11:30
o'clock.
