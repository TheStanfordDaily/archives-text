# Hoover Munches Peanuts, Views Big Series Game
## 
### 
To see his first athletic con
test on the Stanford campus
since his return from Washing
ton, Private Citizen Herbert
Hoover and his wife went to
the Sunken Diamond Saturday
afternoon.
In company with Jack Mc-
Dowell, alumni secretary, and
Coach Jimmy Bradshaw, Mr.
and Mrs. Hoover saw three
close innings of Stanford-Cali
fornia baseball. Entering in the
sixth inning, they saw Stanford
rally to tie the game lip IJ-H.
They left in the ninth in time
to miss an inglorious defeat.
Mr. Hoover, a well-worn fe
dora on his head, consumed
peanuts with the best of the
fans. Mrs, Hoover smiled
broadly.
