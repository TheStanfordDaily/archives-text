# London Doctor To Talk On Nutritional Research
## 
### 
"Future Progress of Nutritional
Research." a technical subject, will
be specially presented for the lay
man at 7:30 o'clock tomorrow eve
ning by Dr. J. C. Drummond of
University College, University of
London.
Dr. Drummond, who. is giving the
twenty-fourth course of Lane Medi
cal lectures in San Francisco, will
illustrate his lecture with lantern
slides.
