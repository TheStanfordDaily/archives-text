# Card Net Stars Defeat Trojan, U.C.L.A. Teams
## WINS GIVE INDIANS GOOD CHANCE FOR CONFERENCE TITLE 
### Julius Jacobs 
Stanford's net team definitely put
itself back into the running for the
conference throne, as well as pulling
two well-earned victories out of the
bag against both S.C. and U.C.L.A.
Both matches were played on the
Cards' home courts, and both were
played in the face of a particularly
frisky wind, which, however, did not
interfere at all with some spectacu
lar tennis.
Friday and Saturday's matches
marked the return of Captain Joe
Coughlin, to fine playing form.
Coughlin won three of the four
matches in which he competed
over the week-end, and showed
great fight in a hard losing battle
against Jack Tidball. nationally
ranked player.
