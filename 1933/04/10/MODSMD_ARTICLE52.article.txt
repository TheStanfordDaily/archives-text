# S.C. Wins Gymnastics In Minor Sports Carnival
## 
### 
Southern competitors walked oft
with all honors in the gymnastics
meet at the Minor Sports Carnival
Friday night in Berkeley, where
U.S.C. scored 83 points to take first
place and U.C.L.A. finished a strong
second.
Stanford finished fourth, making
15 points to trail the California gym
nasts who were third.
