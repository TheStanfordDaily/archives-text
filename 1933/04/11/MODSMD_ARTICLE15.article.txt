# Gene Brown Wins Minor Sports Golf Title 77-77
## 
### 
In a spurt that culminated in a
birdie and two pars, Gene Brown of
Stanford defeated his teammate and
captain, Bud Eiehelberger, to win
the California minor sports, carnival
golf title two up. The two survivors
ended the first 18 holes of a sched
uled 36 even and see-sawed back
and forth until the sixteenth hole
of the second round where Brown
secured a birdie.
The modal scores were: Brown
77-77, Eiehelberger 76-79.
