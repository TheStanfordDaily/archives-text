# Rugby Club To Sponsor Dinner In Team's Honor
## 
### 
As a reward for playing a game
that is its own reward, Stanford
ruggers will attend a dinner at <>:!!<)
o'clock tomorrow evening at Miller's
on the highway. The Stanford Rugby
Club is sponsoring the affair, and
the list of guests, headed by Dr.
Frank Angell, includes all men who
participated in rugby at Stannnd
last quarter, Harry Maloney, Norm
Cleaveland, and others.
