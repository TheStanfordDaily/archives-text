# First Aid Expert To Show Inhalator Here Tomorrow
## 
### 
Demonstrations of a double-action
inhalator will be given by K. S.
Butler at a meeting of the Stanford
Life Saving Corps at the Eneinu
Pool at 4:15 o'clock tomorrow, ac
cording to Felix Juda, president of
the corps.
Butler is the president of the
American Red Cross First Aid In
structors Club, holding instructor
certificates in both the Red Cross
and the Bureau of Mines.
