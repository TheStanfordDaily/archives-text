# Low Fare To Berkeley Announced For Week-End
## 
### 
Special rates will be available to
;ill .students going to Berkeley over
I lie Easter week-end, it is announced
by C. A. Stone, agent of the South
em Pacific. For the holiday the
round trip between Berkeley and
Palo Alto will be 95 cents.
Tickets will be good to leave on
either Thursday, Friday, Saturday,
or Sunday, and return within ten
days. The trip in either direction
will bo made via San Francisco.
