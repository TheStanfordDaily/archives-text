# Van Wagenen Elected Press Club President
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Press
Club at a meeting yesterday after
noon. elected Dick Van Wagenen
president; Elizabeth Hewit, vice
president; and Ellamae Dodds, sec
retary-treasurer. The club also
adopted a constitution.
Following the precedent set by
inviting Lincoln Steffens to speak
here, the group decided that all
their future speakers will be promi
nent journalists. Meetings will be
held in the evening.
The constitution will be posted
soon for the signatures of the club
members.
