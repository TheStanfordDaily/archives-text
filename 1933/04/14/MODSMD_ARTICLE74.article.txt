# Tickets For U.C. Junior Dance Go On Sale Here
## 
### 
Tickets for the informal dance to
be given by the Junior Class of the
University of California at the Hotel
St. Francis on Saturday, April 15,
will go on sale today at the A.S.S.U.
offices in the Old Union.
The price will be $1.50 per couple,
and all Stanforditos are invited. Lee
Searight's orchestra will furnish the
music.
