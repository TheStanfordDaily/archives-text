# Saling, Olympic Runner, Killed In Auto Accident
## 
### 
George Saling, 23, famous hurdler,
who was on the Stanford campus
during last, summer's Olympic try
outs, was killed Saturday in an
automobile accident near Troy, Mis
souri.
Saling, who ran for the University
of lowa, won first place in the 1932
Olympics in the time of 14.6 seconds
for the 110-meter hurdles. He also
set the intercollegiate low hurdle
record last year, with a time of 23.1
seconds.
