# LAW'S NET WIN SOFTENS CARD FROSH DEFEAT
## 
### 
Captain Johnny Law gave Stan
ford her only claim to glory Satur
day when the Golden Bear fresh
men trounced the Card frosh tennis
squad, 7-2, on the Berkeley courts.
Playing against Charles Hunt,
United States junior doubles finalist,
Law blazed through with a 6-4, 3-6,
6-4 win. Hunt's powerful service
had little effect on Law's steady
playing.
