12:15 p.m. —International Table in
the Alcove of the Union. Faculty
and students invited.

20, Chemistry Building. Speaker:
Professor E. A. Guggenheim. Sub
ject: A New Graphical Treatment
of the Separation of Solids sby
Dissolution and Crystallization.

Women's Clubhouse to honor the
chaplain, the Rev. J. D. Cronin,
who leaves soon for a year's study
at Oxford.
