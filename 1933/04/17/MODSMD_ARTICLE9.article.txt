# Celebrate Easter Here With Chapel Services
## 
### 
Easter Day was commemorated
yesterday by Holy Communion
taken after the regular church ser
vice at which Dr. D. Charles Gard
ner officiated. An organ recital of
Easter anthems was given by War
ren D. Allen during the service and
at a special concert at 4 o'clock in
the afternoon.
