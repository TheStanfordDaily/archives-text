# Parents Of Ruth Judd Believe Daughter Insane
## 
### 
FLORENCE, Ariz., April 17 (/P)—
The aged parents and the husband
of Winnie Ruth Judd, 28-year-old
confessed slayer of two women, tes
tified today that they believe she is
insane, while reports seeped out of
the State Prison here that there is
unrest among the convicts over the
ease.
