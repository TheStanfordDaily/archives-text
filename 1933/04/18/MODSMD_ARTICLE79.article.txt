# Mary Rath Elected Head Of Panhellenic Council
## 
### 
| Mary Rath, Kappa Alpha Theta,
was elected president of Panhellenic
by a majority vote of the sorority
houses last night. Tying in the num
ber of votes received, Alice Klau
ber, Alpha Phi, and Rose Cubbison,
Pi Beta Phi, will be revoted upon
for the offices of vice-president and
secretary-treasurer. The revote will
be taken at a Panhellenic meeting
to be held at some near date.
