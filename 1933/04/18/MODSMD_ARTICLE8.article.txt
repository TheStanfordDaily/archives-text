# Convalescent Home Will Give Benefit Musicale
## 
### 
The Stanford Convalescent Home
will give a benefit musicale and gar
den talk at the Allied Arts Guild
tomorrow afternoon.
The program, which is sponsored
by the Menlo Park and Atherton
groups of the Junior Auxiliary, will
be a presentation of Spanish songs
and dances and a talk on the patio
garden by Mrs. John Sinclair, writer
and landscape gardener.
