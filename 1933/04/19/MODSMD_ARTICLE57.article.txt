# Labor Secretary Plans Production Change Bill
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 18 (/P) —
Secretary Perkins told newspaper
men today that among the changes
she had suggested to the 30-hour
week bill was "a measure of control
of production to eliminate unfair
competition."
The Secretary of Labor said, ;
however, that the change she had
recommended to the Black bill, now
pending before the House Labor
Committee, did not contemplate any
"nation-wide system of production
control."
