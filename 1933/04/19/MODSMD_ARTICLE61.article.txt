# Ruth Judd Insane, Experts Tell Court In Lunacy Trial
## 
### 
FLORENCE, Ariz., April 18 (/P) —
Expert testimony that Winnie Ruth
Judd is insane and is not "sham
ming" her peculiar reactions—her
violent outbursts—was presented at
length today in her lunacy trial,
while the date of her hanging was
pushed up another week to April 28.
The 28-year-old slayer of Agnes
Anne Leroi and Hedvig Samuelson
in the notorious "trunk murder"
case showed no interest in the news
that the State Pardon Board had
granted her another reprieve.
