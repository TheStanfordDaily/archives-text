# A.W.S. Revises Election Plan
## 
### 
A.W.S. nomination procedure is
being revised, announces Mary-K.
Morris, president, following a meet
ing of the A.W.S. Conference last
Monday. Final voting on the revi
sions, which have already been sub
mitted to the sororities, will be held
next Monday.
Nominations for A.W.S. president
will be held tomorrow from 8 to 2
o'clock. All candidates must secure
blanks from the A.S.S.U. offices, and
obtain the signature of at least one
person.
Eligibility for the office consists
of a woman's being registered in the
University and having not less than
98 or more than 138 units. Only eli
gible women voters may nominate.
Declinations of nominations may
be made in writing next Friday be
tween the hours of 8 and 2 o'clock.
Voting will be held next Tuesday
near the Women's Steps.
