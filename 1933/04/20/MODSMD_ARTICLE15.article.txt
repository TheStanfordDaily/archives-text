# Nominations For A.W.S. President Open Today
## 
### 
Nominations for A.W.S. president
arc open today from 8 o'clock in the
morning until 2 o'clock in the after
noon on the Women's Steps. Blanks,
which can be procured at the
A.S.S.U. oflice, should be deposited
in the voting box.
To be eligible for nomination a
woman must be registered in the
University and must have not less
| than 08 or more than 138 units. Only
| eligible women voters may nominate.
! Declinations can be made tomorrow
I between 8 and 2 o'clock.
