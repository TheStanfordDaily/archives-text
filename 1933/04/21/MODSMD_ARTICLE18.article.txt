# Senior Committee Will Hold Jolly-up Wednesday
## 
### 
The first of two jolly-ups for tht
senior eiass will be staged from 7
to 8 o'clock next Wednesday night
in the lobby of Toyon hall, it was
announced last night by Betty Wat
son, '33, chairman of the committee
in charge.
Only seniors will be admitted at
the door, and the lobby will be
blocked off as it is at regular dances,
it was declared. No announcement
has been made as to choice of an
orchestra, but the committee prom
ises good music by a band of at
least seven pieces.
Long Beach students are holding
classes in parks, playgrounds, and
vacant lots.
