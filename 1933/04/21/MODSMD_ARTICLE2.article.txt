# Wrenn Will Address Radio Audience Monday Noon
## 
### 
Dr. Charles Gilbert Wrenn will
give a radio address on the subject
of "advertising as a Vocation" over
station KYA at 12:15 o'clock Mon
day noon.
This talk is one of the Youth
Forum Series being sponsored by
the State Department of Education
and the San Francisco Y.M.C.A.
Both Dr. O. 11. Blackman and Dr.
Wrenn of Stanford are on the Youth
Forum Series Committee, which has
as its purpose the giving of a variety
of vocational information to high
school and college students.
