# Bookstore Heads Submit Reply To Report Monday
## 
### 
Stanford Bookstore Board of Di
rectors, having taken up the report
of the Student Committee, is sub
mitting its comments to Bill Corbus
Monday afternoon in the office of
. Professor Hugh Jackson, of the
Business School.
The Bookstore's report is in the
form of a. reply to charges made by
the committee, although there is no
indication of its content.
Two-thirds of the students at the
University of Paris are Americans.
