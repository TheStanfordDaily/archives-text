# Five Women Nominated For A.W.S. Presidency
## 
### 
A.W.S. nominated five: women for
the office of president yesterday.
The nominees are Ann Brackett,
Jeanne Millard, Emily Ross, Elaine
Dear, and Dorothy Anne Jackson.
Declinations can be made today
from 8 to 2 o'clock by depositing
i written notices in a box near the
j women's steps .
