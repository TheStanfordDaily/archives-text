# TEA TRADER NOSED OUT IN DEBUT
## 
### 

The crack New Zealand sprinter, Tea Trader, lost his racing debut
in this country by a nose in a thrilling finish at Tanforan, Cal., race,
track. The three leaders as they finished were (left to right) Tea
Trader, second; Up, first, and Sarazen 11, third. (Associated Press
Photo)
