# Junior Auxiliary Will Give Benefit Program
## 
### 
A benefit program sponsored by
the San Francisco Junior Auxiliary
featuring a fashion show and dance
contest will be given at the St.
Francis Hotel tomorrow night with
the .proceeds going to the Stanford
Convalescent Home. An additional
charge of 25 cents will be added to
the cover charge at this occasion.
Members of this organization have
presented several programs of this
kind that have contributed material
ly to the charity fund. College stu
dents predominate at these events
and superior entertainment is of
fered. At previous charity programs
the orchestra has played overtime
in order to satisfy the dancers.
