# Adv. 10 Page 3
##

ATTENTION STUDENTS
During the Christmas holidays we gave a New
Year's party to the people of Palo Alto. As it was
vacation time we are giving another party on Friday
the 28th to accommodate all students. All that
is necessary is to present a Student Body Card and
we will double the amount of the purchase. We
are doing this in order that the students of Stanford
and vicinity may become acquainted with our
candy.
PARTRICK'S
314 UNIVERSITY AVE.
P.S. Remember the date —Friday the 28th —as long as the
candy lasts. Present your student body card.
