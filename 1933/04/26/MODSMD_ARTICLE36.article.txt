# I'M FOREVER BLOWING BURBLES
## 
### 
"A Senate is much less trouble
than an orchestra; the Senators
blow their own horns. We were
having trouble getting a quorum
until beer came in, then we had
'full' meetings."—Vic Meyers, for
mer orchestra leader, now lieuten
ant-governor of Washington.
