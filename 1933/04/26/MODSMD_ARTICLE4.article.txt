# WHEN INFLATION HIT WHEAT MART
## 
### 
When the news of the abandonment of the gold standard hy the United States and the lirst reports of
inflation came simultaneously Thursday, the Chicago wheat pit, shown below, reflected the excitement that
swept through the nation. Feverish activity resulted; wheat futures dropped. Yesterday, with reassuring
reports from Washington, where the Senate is still debating administration inflation proposals, priccs steadied.
