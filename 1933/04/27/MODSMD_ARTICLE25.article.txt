# Interfraternity Council President Elected Today
## 
### 
New officers for the Interfraternity
Council will be elected during a
meeting of the present Council to
be held at the Alpha Delta Phi
house at 6:15 o'clock tonight.
Hal Edelen and Don Tatum have
been selected for president by the
nominating committee, although
other names may be proposed on
the floor. The secretaryship is to be
contested by Gordy Graves and
Hinton Howe. There arc no other
officers of Ihe body.
A constitution has been drawn up
for the Council and will be submit
ted to members tonight for their
approval.
