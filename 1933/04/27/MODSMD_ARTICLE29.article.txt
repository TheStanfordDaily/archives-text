# Japanese Students To Be Feted At Y.W.C.A. Tea
## 
### 
Honoring the Japanese students
| on the campus, the social committee
of the Y.W.C.A. will present the
first tea of the spring quarter to
morrow afternoon from 3:30 to 5:30
o'clock in (he "Y" rooms.
Several Japanese dances will be
j presented by young children from
the local district. Refreshments will
consist of tea and Japanese rice
cakes.
The tea is sponsored by the Inter
national Committee of the "Y" and
is open to the campus.
