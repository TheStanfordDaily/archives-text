# Al Roth A.S.S.U. Head Twenty-Five Years Ago
## 
### 
Tuesday was just another day for
most of the carnput, but to Almon E.
Roth, Stanford comptroller, it was
a memorial day.
Twenty-five years ago. he was
elected president of the A.S.S.U. by
a unanimous vote. On the same poll
if. L. Horton, track captain and grid
player, was voted vice-president; J.
E. Stewart, graduate manager.
