# Dr. Marx Resigns Post On Palo Alto City Council
## 
### 
After more than thirty years of
service on the Palo Alto City Coun
cil, Dr. Charles D. Marx, emeritus
professor of engineering, resigned
from the city office yesterday.
His place on the board will be
filled by another Stanford professor,
Eugene L. Grant, also of the en
gineering school. Dr. Marx has re
cently returned to his home in Palo
Alto, following a several months'
absence in Washington. While there
he acted as consulting engineer for
the Reconstruction Finance Corpora
tion.
