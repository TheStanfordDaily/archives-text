# English Club Sponsors Book Mart Near Union
## 
### 
Ten cents and a book, brought to
the Book Mart stand in front of the
Union between 9 and 1 o'clock today
or tomorrow, gives to the customer
in exchange any book on the table
there.
The English Club is sponsoring
this Book Mart, which will flso be
open several days of next week.
Any book, used Jr new, may be
offered in trade.
Fever tickets cost Texas $1,500,000
during the last five years.
