# Stanford Trustees Okeh Proposed Sewer Project

### 
Stanford will pay its share of the
proposed $90,000 sewer which will
serve the town and the University.
Comptroller Almon E. Roth, ap
pearing at a meeting of the Palo
Alto city council on Tuesday said
that the Stanford Board of Trustees

age main.
