WHO STARTED THIS WAVE OF
"-Sit " PAL M BEACH" POPULARITY?
Somehow the news leaked out. We haven't
breathed a word about Palm Beach suits, and
yet someone started the word that we sell
them.
A few men on the campus bought. And now
.... now the fellows are streaming in with
this request—"Show me those Palm Beach
suits."
Little wonder, though, after all. Because they
are good-looking — and practical—and inex
pensive !
Most men are surprised when they find Palm
Beach fabric is made in colors—that it looks
much like a woolen suit. Surprised, too, when
they learn that Palm Beach washes or dry
cleans perfectly .... without shrinking. Slow
to soil, and it holds its style lines and fresh
appearance longer than any other summer suit.
See them—today?
