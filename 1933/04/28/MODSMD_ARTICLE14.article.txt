# Bids For Toyon Informal Continue On Sale At $2
## 
### 
Bids for the Toyon Spring Infor
mal next Friday continue on sale to
Toyon Club members in the A.S.S.U.
offices at the Old Union for $2 each.
Although only a limited number
have been printed, Chairman Don
Hardy announced last night that any
remaining invitations will be placed
before the public tomorrow morning
at the same price.
Burnham Sheedy has been ap
pointed to fill the vacancy on the
committee caused by the resigna
tion of Art Azevedo, who will be in
Los Angeles with the varsity track
squad that week-end.
