# Life Saving Examinations To Be Given This Week
## 
### 
Harold Terwilliger, field repre
sentative of the Red Cross, will be
on the campus today, tomorrow, and
Wednesday to conduct life-saving
examinations and recheck students
whose certificates are expiring. Ter
williger will hold women's exam
inations today only in the Roble pool
between 4 and 6 o'clock. Examina
tions will be held for men in the
Encina pool between 12 and 4 o'clock
all three days.
