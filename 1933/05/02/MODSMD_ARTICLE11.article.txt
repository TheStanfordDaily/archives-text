# Tornado Sweeps 3 States; 3 Killed, Scores Injured
## 
### 
SHREVEPORT, La., May 1 (/P)—
C. E. Harper, a Minden newspaper
man, arriving here at 5:15 p.m., no
tified the Times a Tornado struck
three states at 3:30 p.m., killing at
least three persons, injuring many,
and destroying forty or more houses.
