# EASTERN ROWING OPENS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 1 (/P)—The first
of the East's big rowing fixtures,
the Blackwell Cup regatta, will
bring together oarsmen of Columbia,
Pennsylvania, and Yale at Derby,
Conn., Saturday.
