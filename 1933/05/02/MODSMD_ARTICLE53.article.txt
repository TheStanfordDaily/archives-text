# BEER FOR TENNESSEE
## 
### 
NASHVILLE, Tenn., May 3 (/Pi-
May Day brought Tennessee its first
legal beer in almost 25 years.
Seventeen products, all grown on
a single Geoigia farm, were served
at a luncheon in Atlanta.
