# Kathleen Norris Will Talk To Dames Club Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Some Problems of Women To
day will be discussed by Kathleen
Norris at a meeting of the Stanford
Dames in the Women's Clubhouse
tomorrow afternoon at 2:30 o'clock.
Members may invite guests for the
meeting.
A musical program will be given
by Mrs. F. B. Hathorn of Palo Alto,
dramatic soprano, accompanied by
Mrs. G. L Betts.
