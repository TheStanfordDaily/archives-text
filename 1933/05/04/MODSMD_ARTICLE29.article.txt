# ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
THURSDAY
2:30 p.m.—There will be a meeting
of the Stanford Dames in the
Women's Clubhouse. Kathleen
Norris will speak.
5:10 p.m.—Warren D. Allen, Univer
sity organist, will present his usual
Thursday recital in the Memorial
Chapel.
G p.m.—Meeting of members of Phi
Sigma Alpha in the West Me
morial Library to elect officers.
6:30 p.m.—Phi Sigma Alpha dinner
at Union. Ray Lyman Wilbur will
speak on "Costs of Medical Care."
All members of the fraternity and
those invited to join are cordially
invited to be present.
7:30 p.m.—Sword and Sandals will
present a group reading of "The
Critic or a Tragedy Rehearsed" by
Richard Brinsley Sheridan in the
Little Theatre. The public is in
vited.
