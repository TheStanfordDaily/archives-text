# Former San Jose Editor To Address Press Club
## 
### 
Speaking on the opportunities of
the newsman who free-lances for
magazines, R. L. Burgess, former
editor of the San Jose News, will
address the Stanford Press Club at
7:.")0 o'clock tonight in the north
study room of Toyon Hall.
Burgess, who was also at one time
star reporter on several San Fran
cisco newspapers, has recently re
turned from a year's stay in New
York where he studied the maga
zine field.
