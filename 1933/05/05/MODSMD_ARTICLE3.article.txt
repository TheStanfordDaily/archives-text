# Acheson Named Aide To Secretary Woodin
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 4 (/P) —
Dean G. Acheson of Middletown,
Connecticut, and Washington, D.C.,
was nominated today by President
Roosevelt to bo Undersecretary of
the Treasury—the right-hand-man
of Secretary Woodin.
The Chief Executive also sent to
the Senate the name of Dave Hen
nen Morris of New York, to be am
bassador to Belgium, succeeding the
veteran diplomat, Hugh S. Gibson,
who was confirmed today as ambas
sador to Brazil.
