# HERE'S A CLOSEUP OF IOWA FARM WAR FIGHTING
## 
### 

This Associated Press picture provides a graphic view of fighting between farmers and deputies at
Primghar, lowa, In one of the several outbreaks on the lowa farm front. This view, taken from the third
floor of the O'Brien county courthouse, shows men attempting to take clubs from officers In a scramble
on the second floor court below.
