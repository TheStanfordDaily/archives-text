# Hables Gives All To B.A.C. Curio Hall; Dink Leers
## 
### Winnie Fidgett 
Leslie Hables, well-known lo
cal boy who is but a shell of
his former self, has announced
a gift that will stir the hearts of
the medical world.
He has decided to present his
sinus, appendix, broken ankle,
four fractured ribs, tapeworm,
pulled muscle, and wife and
child to the B.A.C. trophy room.
Mr. Hables, long a prominent
politician, has been known as a
benefactor since he first arrived
in this world, lie has contrib
uted to the support of many
hospitals, and when he hasn't
been able to, either his brother
or one of his parents have.
