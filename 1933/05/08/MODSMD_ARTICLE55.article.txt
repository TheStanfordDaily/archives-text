# EX COMMITTEE NOTES
## 
### 
Present: Marian Hewlett.
M.S.C. That the following dates
be granted: Phi Beta Kappa Spring
Formal, to be held in lobby of Li
brary May 33; Delta Delta Delta
Beer Bust. May 20; Kappa Kappa
Gamma-Theta Chi get together din
ner, May 20; Delta Gamma-Chinese
Club joint dance, May 27; Gamma
Phi-Pi Phi week-end house party,
May 26-27.
M.S.C. That the following men
and women be awarded a 'replica of
the seal of the University on which
shall be embossed the motto, "The
winds of freedom blow": Dean Mary
Yost, Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, Almon
E. Roth, Judge John E. Springer, Dr.
Harvey B. Slater, and each member
of the Men's Council.
M.S.C. That the Dramatic Council
henceforth be allowed to pick its
own methods of losing money.
M.S.C. That something be done
immediately about etaoin etaoin
shrdlu.
