# Stanford Yearbook Will Appear Late This Month
## 
### 
Stanford's Yearbook of Writing,
published by the English Club, will
appear this month with, among
other contributions, stories, poems,
and essays voted upon and accepted
for publication at an English Club
meeting Sunday, May 7.
Don Stanford, Senior Class Poet,
and J. V. Cunningham, much of
whose work has already been pub
lished, are among the poets repre
sented. A play about the Old South
by Waldo Salt and an essay by
James WolfT, two campus dramatic
stars, have been accepted.
Among the short stories included
are several by such authors as Bar
bara Gibbs and Albert Guovard.
The Yearbook will also contain
anonymous parodies.
Harvard has football equipment
for 600 men.
