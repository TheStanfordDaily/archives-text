# Tryouts For Readings Held This Afternoon
## 
### 
Tryouts for the third reading to
be sponsored this quarter by Sword
■ and Sandals, men's honorary dra-j
matic society, will be held at 4
o'clock this afternoon in the Little
Theatre.
The play has not as yet been defi
nitely selected, but will probably
be a ninteenth-century English
drama. Under consideration are
Bulwer-Lytton's "Richelieu" and
■ the same author's "Lady of Lyons,"
both tremendous favorites in their
