# Ex-Chappie Man Issues 2 Western Song Books
## 
### 
John Milton Hagen, '20, has had
two books, "Songs of the Saddle,"
and "Bad Man Songs of the Wild
and Woolly West," accepted for pub
lication by the Sam Fox Company
of New York.
While at Stanford, Hagen was on
the staffs of the Chaparral and the
Quad, and a member of Sigma Delta
Chi.
