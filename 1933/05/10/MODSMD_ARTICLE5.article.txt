# Junior Prom Bids On Sale Tomorrow In Old Union
## 
### 
Bids for the 1933 Junior Prom will
go on saie Thursday morning, Herb
Sanderson, general chairman, an
nounced last night. Tickets for the
dance, to be held May 19 in the
Women's Gym, may be purchased in
the A.S.S.U. office of the Old Union
between the hours of 9 and 5 o'clock.
Bids sell for $3 nnoli.
