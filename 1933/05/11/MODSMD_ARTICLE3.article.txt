# Pete Heiser Chosen For World's Fair Football
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 10 (JP)
Coach Howard Harding Jones, who
will coach a Western all-star foot
ball game for a World's Fair ex
hibition at Chicago August 24 has
lined up a dozen players already,
included in the list named today

Morley Drury, Gaius Shaver, Ray
Sparling, Tay Brown, Stanley Wil
liamson, Johnny Baker, and Ernie
Smith, all of Southern California;
Dave Nesbet, Paul Schwegler, and
O'Brien of Washington, and Pete
Heiser, Stanford, and Bill Beasley,
St. Mary's.
