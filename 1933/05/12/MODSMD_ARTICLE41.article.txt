# Welfare Group To Meet In Y.W. Rooms Today
## 
### 
The Social Service Committee will
meet at 4 o'clock today in the
Y.W.C.A. rooms of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Mrs. Edwin J. Thomas, prominent
welfare worker, will speak, and
many women active in social ser
vice in the community will be pres
ent. Following this, there will be a
business meeting for the purpose of
completing the organization of the
committee.
