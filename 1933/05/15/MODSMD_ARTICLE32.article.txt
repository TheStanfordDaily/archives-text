# FIND LARGE APE BRAIN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 14 (JP) —
Discovery in a gorilla of a brain of
higher type than ever before found
in an animal —nearer the human
brain weight than any similar ape's
brain on record—was announced by
the Smithsonian Institution.
