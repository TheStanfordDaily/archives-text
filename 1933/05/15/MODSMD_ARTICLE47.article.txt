# Rabbi Reichert Gives Sermon On "Atheism"
## 
### 
"Atheism" was the subject of the
sermon preached by Rabbi Irving F.
Reichert of the Congregation
Emanu-El, San Francisco, yester
day morning in Memorial Church.
The Rabbi asserted that the world
contained few true atheists, for
many who called themselves such
differed with ihe accepted orthodox
religions, but had their own con
ceptions.
Cantor Rinder, assisted by Ihe
Church Choir, sang the temple ser
vice in Hebrew.
