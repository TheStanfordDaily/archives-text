# Oregon Trackmen Upset Dope In Huskies' Meet
## 
### 
One Northern Division Pacific
Coast Conference dual meet record
went by the boards Saturday as
University of Washington track men
upset the dope sheet to defeat Uni
versity of Washington 69 to 62.
Warren Demaris, Webfoot javelin
thrower, broke the record when he
threw the spear 203 feet 4 inches,
one inch further than the mark set
by Homer Hein of Washington Slate
in 1929.
