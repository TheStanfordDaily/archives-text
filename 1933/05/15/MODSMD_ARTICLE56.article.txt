# Ralph Metcalf Equals World's Hundred Record
## 
### 
Ralph Metcalf, colored Olympic
sprint star running for Marquette
University, equaled Frank Wykoff's
world record of 9.4 seconds in the
100-yard dash Saturday in a meet
with Western State Normal College
ai Kalamazoo, Michigan. He also
raced through a 20.9 second 220-
yard dash.
