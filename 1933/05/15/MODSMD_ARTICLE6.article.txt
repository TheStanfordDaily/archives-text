# Branner To Hold Night Club Dance On May 26
## 
### 
Promising all the attractions of a
"night in the city," Chairman Jack
Hanson last evening announced that
the annual Branner Night Club in
formal dance will be held on Friday
evening, May 26.
Included on the evening's program
will be a variety of entertainment
numbers, adding appropriately to
the night club atmosphere. Tables
nt which punch will be served will
be arranged around the dance floor;
and reservations will be taken for
places, according to Hanson.
