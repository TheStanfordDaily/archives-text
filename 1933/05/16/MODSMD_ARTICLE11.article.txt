# Glee Club Will Elect New Officers At Dinner Tonight
## 
### 
Closing the 1932-33 season, the
Stanford Glee Club will hold its an
nual end-season banquet and elec
tion of officers tonight at 6:15 o'clock
at the Sunset Cafeteria.
With all initiated members vot
ing, and with the newly chosen un
initiated members in attendance
without a vote, the club will elect
a president, vice-president, secre
tary-librarian, and two members
at-large for the 1933-34 season.
These men, composing the execu
tive board, will fill the important
position of manager by appoint
ment.
