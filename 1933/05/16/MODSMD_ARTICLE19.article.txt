# Women Singers To Plan Club At "Y" Meet Today
## 
### 
Plans for organizing a women's
glee club next year when the new
quota of women is admitted to the
University, will be discussed at 5
o'clock today when the music group
of the Y.W.C.A. meets in the Roble
Clubroom. There will be group
singing, it has been announced.
The Kev. Paul Offenheiser of the
Baptist Church in Palo Alio will
lead the discussion when the group
which is considering "Ideas of Liv
ing" meets from 5:30 to 7:30 o'clock
Wednesday on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse. Dinner reser
vations may be made at the "Y"
rooms.
