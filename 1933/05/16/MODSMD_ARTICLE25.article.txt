# 85 Students Meet With Alumni Field Secretary
## 
### 
A group of approximately 85 stu
dents met recently with Harlow
Rothert, Alumni Field Secretary, to
discuss plans of organization into
local Stanford clubs representing
various cities on the Pacific Coast.
These clubs are being organized in
order to better canvass prospective
Stanford material in their respective
towns. '
Prominent among those who at
tended last week's meeting were:
Pill Miller, Bud Deacon, Dan Mur
phy, Austin Clapp, Ollie Amdahl,
Jack Freeman, Paul Zaches, Chuck
Glasgow, and Earl Hoos.
