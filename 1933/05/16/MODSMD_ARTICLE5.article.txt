# LINDY TAKES STAND IN SWINDLE CASE
## 
### 
Col. Charles A. Lindbergh calmly told the tragic story of the kidnaping and death of his son in a
courtroom in Washington while testifying in the trial of Gaston B. Means and Norman T. Whitaker. The
pair are charged with a conspiracy to defraud Mrs. Evelyn Walsh McLean (left) of $35,000 on the pretext
that they could recover the child. Lindbergh is shown at the right as he entered the courthouse accom
panied by Deputy Marshall John Clarkson. (AP Photo)
