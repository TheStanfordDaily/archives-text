# Industrial Democrats Hear Talk On 'Lobbies' Tonight
## 
### 
The problem of lobbies will be
discussed by Dean McHenry gradu
al student in the political science
department, at a meeting of the
League for Industrial Democracy at
7:30 o'clock tonight, on the first, floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
The evolution of these organized
groups as a factor in legislation will
be considered with particular refer
ence to their development* in Cali
fornia.
McHenry has spent most of his
time since the first of this year in
Sacramento studying the lobby as it
exists in the State Capital. He has
been active in politics in southern
California, and was president of the
U.C.L.A. student body last year.
