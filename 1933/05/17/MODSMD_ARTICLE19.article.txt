# Y.W. Board, Cabinet Meet Scheduled For Thursday
## 
### 
The Stanford Y.W.C.A. will hold
its advisory board and cabinet con
ference next Thursday from 10 to 15
o'clock in the "Y" rooms of the
Women's Clubhouse.
The business of the day includes
a discussion at 10 o'clock on the
"Y.W.C.A., Past, Present, and Fu
ture," led by Mrs. Homer Miller.
