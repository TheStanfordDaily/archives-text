# ROOM RESERVATIONS
## 
### 
Men now living in Toyon and
1 Branner halls who wish to reserve
rooms for the fall quarter should
crll at the Dean of Men's office
Monday or Tuesday, May 22 or 23,
and pay room deposits, Miss Vivi
enne Church announced yesterday.
Loom drawings will be held in the
offices of the two halls at 7 o'clock
j Wednesday night, May 24.
Co-eds at Arizona pay their own
j way when out with men.
