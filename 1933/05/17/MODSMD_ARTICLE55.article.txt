WEDNESDAY
12:10 p.m.—French Table in the al
cove of the Union. All members
of the University invited.

today in the Women's Clubhouse.

rooms of the Women's Clubhouse.

weekly meeting in the "Y" rooms.

a discussion.

cabinet.

Society in Room 4IJO Jordan Hall.
Dr. C. H. Danforth will speak.

5:10 p.m.—Organ recital. Warren D.
Allen, University organist.

will be held in the Old Union.
Tickets 25 cents.

ing, "The Ideal Husband," by
Oscar Wilde. Little Theatre. Pub
lic invited.

Palo Alto.

MISCELLANEOUS

nounced yesterday.
