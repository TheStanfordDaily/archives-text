I Primary Interest
The Stanford Daily is of primary interest to
everyone connected with the University. In its
columns appear not only the current campus
news but a selected and condensed brief of na
tional and world news, furnished by the Asso
ciated Press. |
I The Daily is the official organ of the Associated
Students. The campus paper is an integral part
of every student's Stanford life.
