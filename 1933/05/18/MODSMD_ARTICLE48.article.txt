# Riflemen To Compete In Matches Saturday
## 
### 
Qualifying matches for govern
ment medals will be held by the
Stanford Rifle Club Saturday morn
ing at the Palo Alto Rod and Gun
Club outdoor range, back of the
Ryan Laboratory, it was announced
today by John Pascoe, president of
the club.
All club members are eligible to

enter the matches and may join the
practice meet to be held at 1 o'clock
tomorrow afternoon. Men wishing
tc qualify for medals should meet
on the English Corner at 8 o'clock
Saturday morning.
