# Gaieties Star At Hall Dance
## 
### 
First information on the "head
liners" to be featured at the Bran
ner Night Club dance next Friday
evening was released last night with
the announcement that Crawford
Sullivan will appear with GritT Wil
liams and his orchestra.
Sullivan was a star of the Big
Game Gaieties last fall with his own
composition, "She Doesn't Love Me,
But She Lets Me Keep Her Warm."
Reservations for tables at the
Night Club Dance may be made
with Chairman Jack Hanson or any
of his committee. They include:
Hans Lund, Lloyd Silverstein, Marc
Krupp, Ed Perko, Gilman Gist,
Laurel Lewis, and Joe Melczer.
