# P.A.A. MEET JUST WARMUP FOR DINK AND HIS CHARGES
## 
### 
The eyes of the track-minded nation will he focussed on Kezur Stadium tomorrow, where this quartet of champions will make its final
Coast appearance before leaving for the 1.C.4-A. meet at Boston, May 2H and 27. The men are Captain Ben Eastman (left), Les Hahles (center),
Gordon Dunn (top right), and John Lyman (lower right). The watchful youth at the top is Dink Templeton, genial mentor of the squad.
