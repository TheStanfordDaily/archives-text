# Two U.C.L.A. Trackmen Leave For I.C.4-A. Meet
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 18—George
Jefferson, captain of the U.C.L.A.
track team and winner of points for
the United States in the Olympic
Games pole vault, and James Lu-
Valle, 400-meter dash star, leave
tomorrow night for the I.C.A.A.A.A.
meet at Cambridge, May 26 and 27.
