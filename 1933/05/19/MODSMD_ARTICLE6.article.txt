# Mrs. Hoover To Be Guest At 'Y' Tea This Afternoon
## 
###

ored guest at a Y.W.C.A. tea in the
Women's Clubhouse from 4 until 6
o'clock this afternoon.
Members of ihe advisory board of
the Stanford "Y" will also be spe
cial guests, and short talks will be
given by cabinet members of the
organization.
Since the facilities of the Club
house are limited, invitation to the
tea is extended only to Y.W.C.A.
members.
