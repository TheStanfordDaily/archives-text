# Ink Well Goes Wet Once More, Thanks To Libe
## 
### 
luk will once again bubble,
gurgle, imd flow in its custom
ary place in the Reserve Book
Room after several weeks of
enforced dryness due to an
economy move on the part of
the Bookstore which had for
merly supplied the ink. Here
after the University Library
will provide this convenience as
part of the administration costs,
according to word received
from Walter Owens, chief of
the circulation division, yester
day.
