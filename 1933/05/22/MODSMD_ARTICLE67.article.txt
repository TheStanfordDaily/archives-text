# Post Will Make Nonstop Flight Direct To Berlin
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 21 (/?)- A pro
jected nonstop High! from here to
Berlin will be the first of Wiley
Post's world girding trip next month,
the Oklahoma City aviator said to
day. Post explained he was going
alone and will use an automatic
pilot.
