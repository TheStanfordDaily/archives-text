# Newly Renovated Encina Lobby To Be Dance Locale
## 
### 
"The New Lobby Informal" of
Encina Club will allow any campus
resident several hours of dancing at
the rock-bottom price of $1 a couple, I
as well as a look at the latest of
campus dance-rooms.
Despite the low price, it was an- 1
m.unced last night that Grif Wil
liams' nine-piece orchestra would
supply the music for Saturday
night's dance.
Dance Chairman Bill Drew gave
the following reason for the dance: ,
"After the Encina Formal, the first :
affair in the renovated lobby, so 1
many frosh asked for a cheap, in- 1
formal dance at which the whole
campus could enjoy the new room,
that Saturday's informal was ar
ranged.
Bids go on sale tomorrow at the
A.S.S.U. office in the Old Union.
