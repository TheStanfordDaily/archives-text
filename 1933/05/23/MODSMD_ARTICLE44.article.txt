# Ernie Nevers Will Speak At Branner Club Smoker
## 
### 
Featuring a talk by Ernie Nevers,
the Branner Club will hold a
smoker at 10 o'clock tomorrow
night. An interesting speaker, Nevers
has appeared at previous smokers
on the campus where his talks have
proved very entertaining.
One of the main purposes of the
gathering is to stimulate interest in
the Branner Night Club Dance to
be held Friday evening. Music and
refreshments will also be included
on the evening's entertainment,
which is open to all students.

Fifty instructors at the Univer
sity of lowa have served on the
faculty of that institution for more
than 20 years.
