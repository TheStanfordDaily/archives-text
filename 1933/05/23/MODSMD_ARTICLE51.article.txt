# Latin American Society Plans Consular Dinner
## 
### 
Plans for a banquet honoring the
San Francisco consular corps will
be discussed at a meeting of the
Latin-American Club at 8 o'clock
Thursday night in the Women's
Clubhouse. The banquet will be
given on June 2 in the Stanford
Union. Manuel Cardoza, who will
preside at the meeting, urges all
members of the club to attend.
