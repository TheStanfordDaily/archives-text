# Military Club To Sponsor Smoker And Horse Show
## 
### 
A smoker, sponsored by Scab
bard and Blade, honorary military
society, will bo held in the lobby of
Toyon Hall at 7:30' o'clock tomorrow
evening. Several reserve officers
from San Francisco will speak on
ihe Reserve Officers Association and
the advantages it offers to college
graduates. All advanced course
R.O.T.C. students are encouraged to
attend and all who are interested are
invited.
The R.O.T.C. horse show, also
sponsored by Scabbard and Blade,
will be held Friday afternoon, June
2. Drawings for horses will be held
this Thursday afternoon at 4 o'clock
at the stables. It is planned to have
fewer events than formerly. Full
details will be announced in the next
few days. Elimination trials for .sev
eral of the events will be held this
Friday afternoon.
