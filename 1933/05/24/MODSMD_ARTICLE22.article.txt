# Reserve Officers Speak Before Military Smoker
## 
### 
Scabbard and Blade, honorary
military society, will hold a smoker
tonight at 7:30 o'clock in Ihe Bran
ner lobby. All advanced R.O.T.C.
students and all who are interested
are invited. Several reserve officers
from San Francisco will speak on
the Reserve Officers Association.
Drawings will be held tomorrow
afternoon at 4 o'clock in the
for the R.O.T.C. horse show to be
held Friday afternoon, June 2.
Elimination trials for several of the
events will be held this Friday af
ternoon.
