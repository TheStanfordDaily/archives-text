# LINDBERGH BUTLER DIES
## 
### 
PRINCETON. N.J., May 23 (/P)-
Ollic Wheatley, butler in the home
of Colonel and Mrs. Charles A
Lindbergh, died in Princeton Hos
pital today. He was operated on for
stomach ulcers last Friday.
