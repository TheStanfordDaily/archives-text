# Seniors Gather Tonight For Their Last Jolly-up
## 
### 
Seniors dance tonight at their last
jolly-up, to be staged in the Wo
men's Clubhouse from 7 until 8
o'clock. Tonight's affair, states Betty
Watson, social chairman, marks the
last informal get-together of the
class before Senior Week.
Only senior men will be admitted
to the dance floor, but all campus
women are invited. An announce
ment of special interest to the Class
<m '33 is to be made at the jolly-up.
Gordon Heche's orchestra will play*
