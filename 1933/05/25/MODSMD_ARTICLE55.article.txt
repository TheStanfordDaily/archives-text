# Scout Fraternity Holds Classes In Life-Saving
## 
### 
The local chapter of Alpha Phi
Omega, national honorary scouting
fraternity, is conducting a life-sav
ing course at the Palo Alto High
School pool this week and next.
George Irvine, '33, is in charge of
the instruction which will be given
from 4 to 5 o'clock every Monday
and Wednesday, and from 5 to 6
o'clock on Tuesday, Thursday, and
Friday. Those enrolled are attempt
ing to pass their Scout and Red
Cross requirements for life-saving.
