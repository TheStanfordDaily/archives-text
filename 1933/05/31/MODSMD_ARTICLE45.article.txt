# Women's Athletic Meet Scheduled For Thursday
## 
### 
Deciding that the annual W.A.A.
banquet would be provided free for
all women for the first time in his
tory, the Women's Council met yes
terday to complete the formulation
of plans for the intramural Field
Day to be held on Thursday, June 1.
At the dinner, which will follow
the afternoon's athletic activities,
awards and cups for athletic events
of the entire past year will be pre
sented by Babe Dear, president of
W.A.A.
Featuring such sport celebrities as
Dorothy Swan and Dot Anne Jack
son of swimming fame, and Helen
Fulton, star tennis player, the Field
Day will interest many.
