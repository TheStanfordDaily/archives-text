# Stuart To Lead U.C. Discussion
## 
### 
Dr. Graham Stuart, professor of po
litical science, is leading a seminar in
"Problems of Latin-American Rela
tions," at the Conference on Interna
tional Relations being held at the In
ternational House on the University of
California campus, from June 26 to
August 4.
Four round tables are being con
ducted one evening a week during this
time on various phases of international
relations. Dr. Stuart's discussion is held
each Monday evening at 8 o'clock.
The list of sponsors for this confer
ence includes the names of President
Ray Lyman Wilbur of Stanford, and
Dr. Eliot G. Mears, director of the
summer quarter.
