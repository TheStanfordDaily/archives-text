# Over 250 Use Stanford Golf Course; Record 290
## 
### 
Well over 250 persons played on the
University's golf course last Sunday.
The record mark for attendance is 290.
The fee for golfers connected with
the University is $1 a round until 4
o'clock, after which time it drops to
50 cents. A rate of $20 for the quarter
or $17.50 for 25 rounds is also made.
For those not connected with the Uni
versity, the green fee is $1 on week
days and $2 on week-ends, with a spe
cial rate of $10 a month.
