Vol. LXVI, No. 2 June 29-July 5, 1933
CHURCH SERVICE
Sunday, July 2
11:00 a.m. Preacher: Dr. Charles R. Brown, Dean Emeritus of the Divinity
School, Yale University.
Music
Prelude: Chorale Jongen
Introit: To Thee Our Morning Song of Praise Plain Song
Anthem : God of the Nations Parker
7:30 p.m. Organ recital—Mr. Warren D. Allen
Tuesday, July 4
(UNIVERSITY HOLIDAY)
SPECIAL LECTURES
Thursday, June 29
8:00 p.m. "Fifteenth Century Italian Artists: Donatello, Ghiberti, Masaccio,
Fra 'Lippo Lippi, Signorelli, Botticelli, and Ghirlandajo," with illustra
tions, by Professor A. B. Clark. Little Theatre. The public is invited.
Wednesday, July 5
4:15 p.m. "Urban Attitudes," by Professor H. Woolston, Acting Professor of
Sociology. Little Theatre. The public is invited.
ORGAN RECITALS
Sunday at 7:30 p.m. and Thursday at 4:15 p.m. Warren D. Allen,
University Organist, Memorial Church.
THOMAS WELTON STANFORD ART GALLERY
Exhibit: Oil paintings by William Ritschel until June 30. Collection of water colors
by J. E. Fitzgerald, until July 1. Photographs by the Stanford Univer
sity Camera Club, until July 2. Drawings of scenes at Stanford Uni
versity by Richard Stephens, until July 2. (The Art Gallery is open to
the public without charge from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. daily, including
Sunday.)
UNIVERSITY LIBRARY
Exhibit: Recent gifts to the Felton Library and Rare Book Collections.
ANNOUNCEMENTS
12:10 p.m. Spanish Table in the Cafeteria, daily. All those interested in Spanish
conversation cordially invited to attend.
Wednesday, July 5
12:10 p.m. French Table in the Alcove of the Union. All members of the Univer
sity cordially invited to attend.
COMING EVENTS '
Thursday, July 6
8:00 p.m. Special Lecture: "Sixteenth Century Italian Artists," by Professor
A. B. Clark.
Tuesday, July 11
8:00 p.m. Tuesday Evening Series: Lecture on "What American Composers Are
Doing," by Mr. Henry Cowell.
Wednesday, July 12
4:15 p.m. Special Lecture: "The Idea of Universal Peace in German Saga and
Literature," by Professor Julius Petersen, Acting Professor of German.
Friday, July 14
8:15 p.m. Concert: Pro Arte Quartet. Tickets, $1.
WEEKLY CALENDAR
Announcements for the Weekly Calendar, in writing, should be in the hands of
the Secretary to the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Administration
Building, not later than 9:00 a.m., Tuesday. Calendar subscriptions at $1 for the
year or 50 cents for the quarter may be sent to Jhe Secretary.
