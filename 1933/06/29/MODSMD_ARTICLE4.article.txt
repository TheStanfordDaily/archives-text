# Spanish Table Opens In Union This Noon
## 
### 
The first meeting of the newly or
ganized Spanish Table will take place
today at noon in the Stanford Union.
The group is open to all persons who
are interested in speaking Spanish.
Professor A. M. Espinosa of the Span
ish department is co-operating in the
organization of the group.
This group is open to anyone who
speaks Spanish as a native tongue in
addition to those who have studied or
are interested in speaknig the lan
guage. The Table will meet in the
cafeteria at reserved tables every school
day hereafter.
