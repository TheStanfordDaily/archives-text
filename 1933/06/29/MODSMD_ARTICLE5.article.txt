# Reception For Faculty Will Be Held Saturday
## 
### 
Honoring visiting faculty members
and their wives, Eliot G. Mears, direc
tor of the summer quarter, and Mrs.
Mears will hold an informal reception
at Roble Gymnasium Saturday after
noon from 4 to 6 o'clock.
All members of the regular staff,
whether on duty or not during the
summer, and their wives, are invited,
as the main purpose of the reception
is to allow the visiting faculty and
their families to become acquainted
with the resident members as early as
possible during the quarter.
