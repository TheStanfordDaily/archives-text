# Stanford Dames Meet In Women's Clubhouse Today
## 
### 
The first summir quarter meeting of
the Stanford Dames will take place to
day at 3 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. All wives and mothers of Stan
ford men are invited.
Officers of the group for the summer
are: President, Mrs. Paul W. Klipsch;
Vice-president, Mrs. H. H. Badzley;
Treasurer, Mrs. Carl Linn; Recording
Secretary, Mrs. H. W. Leuenberger;
Corresponding Secretary, Mrs. Howard
Porter.
