# ELLIOTT WILL SPEAK
## 
### 
Dr. O. L. Elliott, registrar emeritus,
will speak to the Faculty Women's Club
Monday afternoon at 3 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. The subject of
the talk will be "Early Days at Stan
ford." Wives of the visiting professors
are especially invited, so that they may
become acquainted with the regular
members of the club.
