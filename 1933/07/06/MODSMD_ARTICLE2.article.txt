# Henry Co well To Lecture On American Composers
## 
### 
Henry Cowell, California composer,
whose recent book, "American Com
posers on American Music," was pub
lished by the Stanford University
Press, will deliver a lecture next Tues
day evening, in the Stanford Assembly
Hall, on the subject, "What American
Composers Are Doing."
Cowell, one of a very modern school
of composers, conducted a series of
lectures in Palo Alto this spring.
