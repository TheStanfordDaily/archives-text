# Repair Work On Phi Delt House Ends Sepember 15
## 
### 
All improvements and additions to
the Phi Delta Theta house will be com
pleted by September 15, according to
Bill Saufley, general manager and
timekeeper of the job.
The walls were left standing but the
inside has been changed and improved.
A library, guest suite, eight new study
rooms, and a sleeping porch have been
added.
Saufley expects the total cost includ
ing furnishing to be around $22,000.
