# Stover Will Hold Tryouts For Play Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Tryouts for the only full-length dra
matic performance of the summer
quarter will be held in the Assembly
Hall tomorrow night at 7:30 o'clock,
Frederick Stover announced today.
All students interested in dramatics
are urged to attend the tryouts, since
the variety of parts necessitates a large
turnout. The selection of the play will
be announced at a later date. Its pro
duction is made possible through the
sponsorship of the Committee on Public
Exercises.
