# Sewing Group Meets Today; Reading Session Tomorrow
## 
### 
The sewing group of the Stanford
Dames will meet at 3 o'clock this after
noon at the home of Mrs. William
Proctor at 747 Dolores street on the
campus.
Mrs. B. E. Hall will be hostess to
the reading group at her home at 735
Middlefield road, Palo Alto, Wednesday
afternoon at 2:30 o'clock.
