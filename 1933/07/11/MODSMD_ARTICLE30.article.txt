# History Instructor Marries San Mateo J.C. Registrar
## 
### 
Albert Russell Buchanan, Stanford
graduate and now an instructor in the
history department, was married Sun
day evening to Miss Ethel O'Keefe, '21,
registrar of San Mateo Junior College,
at the home of the bride's parents in
Menlo Park. Mr. and Mrs. Buchanan
left Monday by airplane for Portland,
where they embarked yesterday on the
General Pershing for a trip around the
world, intending to return to the cam
pus about the first of October.
Houston, Texas, is the world's larg
set inland cotton port.
