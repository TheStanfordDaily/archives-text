# Toyon Election No Contest; Only One For Each Office
## 
### 
Toyon students will have little trou
ble marking their ballots in the elec
tion of club officers today.
This rare situation was presented on
the eve of voting, when it was disclosed
that only one person had been put in
nomination for each office.
Bill Sim is the candidate for presi
dent and Ted Lynn will fill the office
of secretary.
Leonard Bartlett, manager of the
club, promises an early meeting of the
officers to arrange for a summer dance
and other social events.
