# San Mateo Horse Show At Menlo Park, August 5-12
## 
### 
The seventh annual San Mateo
County National Horse Show will be
held at the Menlo Circus Club, Ather
ton, August 5 to 12, it is announced by
Mrs. W. P. Roth, president.
Major A. Mouel-Fenton, well-known
horse show executive, is secretary
manager of the San Mateo County
show and has opened offices in Red
wood City at 232 Hamilton street.
The classification of books and prize
lists have been mailed to hundreds of
horse show exhibitors throughout the
nation.
