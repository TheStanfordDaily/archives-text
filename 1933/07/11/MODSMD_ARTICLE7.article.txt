# Women Conference Lunch Scheduled For Thursday
## 
### 
A guidance conference luncheon for
women, sponsored by Pi Lambda
Theta, will be served in the alcove of
the Union at 12:30 o'clock Thursday.
All women registered in the School of
Education, as well as those attending
the conference, are invited. There will
be a musical program.
Tickets at 50 cents must be secured
today or tomorrow. They may be had
outside the conference rooms and also
at the School of Education library.
