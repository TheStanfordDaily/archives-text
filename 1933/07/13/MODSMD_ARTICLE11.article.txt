# Nation's Best Are Expected At San Mateo Horse Show
## 
### 
More than 300 of the nation's finest
show horses will compete in the sev
enth annual San Mateo County Na
tional Horse Show to be held August
5-12 at the Mcnlo Circus Club.
Entries from stables in all parts of
the country will vie for many valuable
stakes. All classes will show. The road
ster and harness classes will be of
special interest since the Circus Club
grounds afford such fine opportunities
for showing those breeds.
