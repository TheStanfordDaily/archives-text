# Guidance Conference Lunch To Be Held Today In Union
## 
### 
A guidance conference luncheon for
women will be held in the alcove of
the Union at 12:30 o'clock today. The
program is sponsored by Pi Lambda
Theta. Women registered in the School
of Education, as well as those attending
the conference on guidance and per
sonnel, will attend.
