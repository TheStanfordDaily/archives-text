# Dr Brown's Third Sermon To Be Given Next Sunday
## 
### 
The. Rev. Dr. Charles R. Brown, dean
emeritus of the Yale divinity school,
will give his third sermon in the Me
morial Church Sunday.
The musical program for the service
will consist of the following selections
together with hymns to be selected.
Prelude—Jesu Meine Freunde... .Bach
Introit—Jesu Priceless Treasure.. Bach
Canticle—Jubilate Allen
Anthem—l Sought the Lord. Stevenson
The soloist will be Marie Hansen.
