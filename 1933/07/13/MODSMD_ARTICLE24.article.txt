# Stanford Publisher Shows Dr. Jordan's Autobiography
## 
### 
"The Days of A Man," autobiography
of David Starr Jordan, Stanford's first
president, is on exhibit by the World
Book Company at the thirteenth annual
textbook display at Stanford.
The company was organized by Cas
par Hodgson, '96. The book contains,
along with Dr. Jordan's life story, much
early history of Stanford.
The population of Nevada could be
seated in the Stanford Stadium.
