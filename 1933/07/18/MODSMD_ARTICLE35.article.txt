# Pi Lambda Theta Sponsors Guidance Confab Lunch
## 
### 
Sponsored by Pi Lambda Theta, a
guidance conference luncheon for wo
men was held in the alcove of the
Union last Thursday. Women registered
in the School of Education as well as
those attending the conference on guid
ance and personnel attended. A musi
cal program was given.
