# Fast Retort From Cool Coed Daunts Hot Rough
## 
### 
Stanford students, at least one of
them, are succumbing to the summer
sun which addles brains and mixes
metaphors if taken in too large quanti
ties.
The young lady in question, wearing
a cool cotton print, was snatching a
moment's respite from the toils of the
day under the shade of the library tree.
A typical rough approached her and
looked with approbation on her dainty
freshness.
"Say," he ejaculated, "you look nice
and cool!"
"Well," she returned, "you don't look
so hot yourself."
