# Klenze Returns Next Summer
## 
### 
Announcement was made today of
the appointment of Camillo von Klenze,
honorary professor of American litera
ture at the University of Munich, as
acting professor of German for the
summer quarter of 1934. Professor von
Klenze has already spent one quarter
on the Stanford campus, serving in the
German department during the summer
of 1930, when he presented an unusual
series of lectures on the intellectual
life of the United States.
Von Klenze was born in Switzerland,
but received his A.B. degree at Har
vard University, took his Ph.D. from
the University of Marburg, Germany,
and holds a Litt.D. from the Univer
sity of Wisconsin.
