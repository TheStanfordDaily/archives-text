# Unemployed Will Benefit By Show
## 
### 
The Unemployment Relief Commit
tee of San Mateo County will receive
all proceeds above expenses from the
San Mateo County National Horse Show
this year, directors of the show asso
ciation have decided, according to an
announcement of Mrs. W. P. Roth,
president.
The Horse Show will be held again
this year at the Menlo Circus Club,
Atherton, opening on Saturday eve
ning, August 5, and closing August 12.
There will be matinee performances on
Sunday, Wednesday, and Saturdays.
