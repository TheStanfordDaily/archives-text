# Dr. Barnouw Talk Scheduled Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. A. J. Bamouw, Queen Wilhelmina
professor of Dutch language and litera
ture at Columbia University, will speak
this evening on "Race and Language
Problems in South Africa." His lecture,
which is open to the public, will be
given at 8 o'clock in the Assembly Hall.
Dr. Barnouw is an acting professor
at the University of California during
the summer.
