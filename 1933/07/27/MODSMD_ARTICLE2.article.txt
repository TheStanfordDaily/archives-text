# Mears Speaks To Lunch Club
## 
### 
Director Eliot G. Mears, of the sum
mer quarter, addressed the men of the
School of Education at their weekly
luncheon in the Union dining hall yes
terday.
Drawing his material from remini
scences of his service as a visiting pro
fessor to several continental and Near
Eastern universities, Mears described
some of these institutions in relation
to their curricula, history, and students.
Particular attention was paid, by the
speaker, to social sciences by way of
drawing a comparison between the for
eign and American systems.
The address was supplemented by
songs and the usual air of fellowship
prevailed.
