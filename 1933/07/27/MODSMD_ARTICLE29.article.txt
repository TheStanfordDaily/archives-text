# High School Teachers To Gather Here
## 
### 
Professors from the economics, so
ciology, political science, philosophy,
and education departments will parti
cipate in the Regional Conference of
the California Society of Secondary
Education to be held at Stanford on
August 3-4.

The two-day convention of the So
ciety will be devoted to the considera
tion of the topic, "The Challenge of
Social and Economic Conditions to the
Schools." To insure a thorough treat
ment of this timely subject both morn
ing and afternoon sessions will be held.

As no fee is being charged, this con
ference is a gift to the secondary teach
ers by the California Society of Secon
dary Education and Stanford. It is ex
pected that a large number of mem
bers of the Parent-Teachers Association
will be present.
