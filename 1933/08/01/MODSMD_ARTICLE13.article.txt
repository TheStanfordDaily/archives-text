# Prof. Mears Acts As Host To Pro Arte String Quartet
## 
### 
Professor Eliot Grinnell Mears, di
rector of summer quarter, and Mrs.
Mears were hosts representing the Uni
versity at a luncheon yesterday in the
Union Court in honor of the Pro Arte
String Quartet and their sponsor, Mrs.
Elizabeth Sprague Coolidge.
Among those present were: Miss
Persis Coleman, Miss Mabel Pierce,
Mrs. Herbert Hoover, Mrs. Charles D.
Walcott, Mrs. John Richardson, Mr. and
Mrs. Nathan Firestone, Mrs. David
Starr Jordan, Mrs. Sigmund Stern, Mrs.
John W. Mitchell, Dean and Mrs.
Luther B. Marchant, of Mills College,
Mr. and Mrs. Warren D. Allen, Dean
and Mrs. Marion Rice Kirkwood, Dean
and Mrs. J. Hugh Jackson, Professor
and Mrs. Edgar Eugene Robinson, Mr.
and Mrs. Almon E. Roth, President Ray
Lyman Wilbur, Dr. Douglas H. Camp
bell, and Dr. Henry Lanz.
