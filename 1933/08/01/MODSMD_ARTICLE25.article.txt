# Dames Postpone Picnic Planned For Tomorrow
## 
### 
The picnic of the Stanford Dames,
previously announced to be held to
morrow, has been indefinitely post
poned.
The regular meeting of the Dames
will be held at 3 o'clock Thursday
afternoon in the Women's Clubhouse.
The speaker for the afternoon will be
Grayson N. Kefauver, dean-elect of
the Stanford School of Education. Ke
fauver's topic will be "Some Phases of
Educational Guidance."
