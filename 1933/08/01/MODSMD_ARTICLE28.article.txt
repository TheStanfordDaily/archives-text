# Palo Alto Merchants Adhere To Roosevelt's N.I.R.A. Act
## 
### 
At a meeting called yesterday morn
ing by the Chamber of Commerce, Palo
Alto merchants formulated an agree
ment to reorganize their schedule of
business hours in line with the Na
tional Industrial Recovery Act. The
meeting was presided over by Kenneth
Lawler, manager of Roos Bros.
The new hours will be from 9 to 6
o'clock instead of the former opening
hours of 8 or 8:30 o'clock.
