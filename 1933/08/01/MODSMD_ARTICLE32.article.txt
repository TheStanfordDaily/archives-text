# History Club Will Honor Dr. Guy Ford At Banquet
## 
### 
The banquet in honor of Dr. Guy
Stanton Ford, acting professor of His
tory, will be held tomorrow evening at
6:30 o'clock at Bertrand's Inn, Palo
Alto. This announcement was made by
William Harrover, president of the His
tory Club, sponsors of the affair.
Reservations still may be made with
Mrs. Tuxford, history secretary, and
all club members and department ma
jors are asked to do so at once.
