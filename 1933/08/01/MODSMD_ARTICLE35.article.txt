# Ethel Sleeper Brett Plays In Evening Organ Recital
## 
### 
At an organ recital last Sunday eve
ning at 7:30 o'clock in the Stanford
Memorial Church, Ethel Sleeper Brett,
well-known organist, delivered the fol
lowing program:
Chorale in E Major Cesar Franck
Reverie Richard Strauss ;
Allegro (Symphonie VI)
Andante Cantabile
Finale (Symphonie II)... .C. M. Widor
Sketch in D Flat... .Robert Schumann
Finlandia Jan Sibelius ,
