# Jolly-Up To Be Free of Charge
## 
### 
Toyon Summer Club will sponsor a
jolly-up to be given tomorrow night at
the Women's Clubhouse. Students may
dance free of charge from 7 until 8:30
o'clock.
This will be the first dance of the
quarter which has not required an ad
mission charge. President Bill Sim of
Toyon invites all summer quarter stu
dents to attend.
That the Canfield's Gardens orches
tra is growing in popularity is attested
by the fact that tomorrow night will
mark its third campus appearance dur
ing the summer.
