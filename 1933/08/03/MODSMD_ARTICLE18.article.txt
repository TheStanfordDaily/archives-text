# Soprano To Assist Allen In Today's Organ Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University organist,
will give his regular weekly recital this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in the Me
morial Church. He will be assisted by
Elizabeth Hughes, soprano.
The program will be as follows:
Pilgrims' Chorus (Tannhauser). Wagner
Elizabeth's Prayer (Tannhauser)
Wagner
Elizabeth Hughes, soprano
A Ground (Evening Hymn)
Henry Purcell
Kyrie Eleison (Cathedral Windows)
Sigfrid Karg-Elert
Gebet (Prayer)
Verborgenheit (Seci'ecy)
Hugo Wolf
Miss Hughes
Walter's Prize Song (Die Meister
singer) Wagner
Prelude, Act 111 "Lohengrin"
