# Chicago And Northwestern Tell Plans For Co-operation
## 
### 
Plans for the co-operation of the
University of Chicago and Northwest
ern University were told yesterday by
the Associated Press. Provision is made
for a utilization of the resources of each
institution to the advantage of both.
There is no thought of a merger nor
will either university abandon any part
of its plant or curriculum, authorities
said.
President Walter Dill Scott of North
western hailed the plan as "hastening
the day when Chicago shall become the
center of higher learning in America."
