# DR. DYMENT'S HOURS
## 
### 
Dr. Bertha Dyment, director of phys
ical education for women, has an
nounced that she will have no morning
office hours for the remainder of the
quarter. Hereafter, Dr. Dyment's of
fice hours will be from 2 to 4 o'clock on
