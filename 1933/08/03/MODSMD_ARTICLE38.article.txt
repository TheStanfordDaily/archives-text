# Comptroller Wins Position On County Charter Group
## 
### 
Santa Clara county voted Tuesday to
let the Citizens' League draft a county
charter providing for home rule.
The Citizens' League ticket scored a
clean sweep, easily defeating the Econ
omy group. Among the members of
the winning ticket is Almon E. Roth,
comptroller of the University.
Palo Alto, Mayfield, and Stanford
University went more than five to one
in favor of the Citizens' League candi
dates, although the vote was compara
tively light.
