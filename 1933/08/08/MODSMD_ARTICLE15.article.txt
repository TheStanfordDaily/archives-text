# Fair Times Recorded In Pentathlon Swim Friday
## 
### 
Fair times were recorded at the swim
pentathlon Friday afternoon when the
senior group covered 100 yards back
stroke, while the novice paddlers cov
ered 50 yards. James DeLamater led
Jack Clumeck in the senior division to
win in 1:13.5. Close behind Clumeck
came Norman Sears and Ken Living
ston.
John Smith led the novices covering
the two laps in 33.6. Bunched closely
behind Smith were Frank Parker, Bob i
Jenkins, and Dick Buckwalter.
