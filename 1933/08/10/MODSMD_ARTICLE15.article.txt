# Registrar's Daughter Is Wed In Memorial Church
## 
### 
Miss Laura Mitchell, daughter of
John Pearce Mitchell, registrar and
professor of chemistry at Stanford, was
married yesterday by the Reverend
Dr. D. Charles Gardner, University
Chaplain, to Dr. Munro Eaton, who
formerly attended Stanford.
A large number of friends attended
the wedding. A reception was held at
the home of Mr. and Mrs. Mitchell
following the ceremony.
The couple departed for the East on
their honeymoon.
