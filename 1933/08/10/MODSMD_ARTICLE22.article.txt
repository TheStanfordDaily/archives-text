# Russell To Speak On Poetry Before English Review Club
## 
### 
There will be a second meeting of
the English Review Club at the home
of Professor and Mrs. H. D. Gray, 612
Alvarado Row, at 4 o'clock Monday
afternoon.
Professor Frances T. Russell will
speak on "Nineteenth Century Defini
tions of Poetry."
All graduate and advanced students
of the English department are cordially
invited.
