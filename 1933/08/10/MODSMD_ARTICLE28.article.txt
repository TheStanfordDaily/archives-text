ANNOUNCEMENTS
12:10 p.m. Spanish Table in the Cafeteria, daily except Saturday and Sunday. All
those interested in Spanish conversation cordially invited to attend.
Thursday, August 10
2:00 p.m. Oral Examination for the Doctorate: Harrison French Heath. (Major,
Education; Minor, Psychology.) Room 308, Library.
Saturday, August 12
4:00 p.m. Automobile Excursion to Mount Hamilton and Lick Observatory.
Leave from the Stanford Union, Main Entrance, returning about mid
night. Reservations must be made two days in advance. Fare ($3.00)
may be deposited in the office of the Director of the Summer Quarter,
Room 173, Administration Building, or at the Peninsula Yellow Cab Co.
Monday, August 14
2:00 p.m. Oral Examination for the Doctorate: Philip Robert Petsch. (Major,
Germanic Languages; Minor, fulfilled.) Room 308, Library.

"Nineteenth Century Definitions of Poetry." All graduate and ad
vanced students of the English Department are cordially invited.
