# Los Angeles Students In Get-Together Wednesday
## 
### 
A get-together for all students living
in or near Los Angeles who are at
tending summer school has been
planned for Wednesday night from 8
to 9 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Students from all departments living
in Los Angeles county are invited to
attend. F. J. Highfill is in charge of
plans for the affair.
