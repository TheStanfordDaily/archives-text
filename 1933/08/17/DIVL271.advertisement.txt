| 12:10 p.m. Spanish Table in the Cafeteria, daily except Saturday and Sunday. All

2:00 p.m. Oral Examination for the Doctorate: Laurel H. Turk. (Major,
Spanish; Minor, French.) Room 308, Library.

Economics; Minor, Sociology.) Room 311, Library.

2:00 p.m. Oral Examination for the Doctorate: Robert Chester Challam. (Major,

ford Union, Main Entrance, returning about 5:00 p.m. Reservations

the office of the Director of the Summer Quarter, Room 173, Adminis
tration Building, or at the Peninsula Yellow Cab Co.

1:30 p.m. Oral Examination for the Doctorate: Margaret Dorothy Betz. (Major,
fulfilled; Minor, Physics.) Room 18, Chemistry Building.
