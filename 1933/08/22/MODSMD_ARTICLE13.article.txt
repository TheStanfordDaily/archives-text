# PRESENT MELODRAMA
## 
### 
"Uncle Tom's Cabin," famous Civil
War drama, was revived last week by
Ralph Cheese of the Marionette Guild
in San Francisco. Performances will
be repeated on Friday and Saturday
evenings at 8:30 during August and
September, with a special "request"
matinee announced for next Saturday
at 2:30 o'clock.
