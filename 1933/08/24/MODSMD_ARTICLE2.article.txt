# Prof. Guerard Appears In Three September Magazines
## 
### 
Professor Albert Guerard, who has
been giving a course of lectures at Utah
State University during the summer,
has articles in the September issues of
three general magazines.
In the current edition of Scribner's
is "What Is Education For?", the fourth
of a series of articles by Professor
Guerard in Scribner's dealing with the
relation of art and literature to society.
"Redraw the Map of Europe?" is the
subject of an article in the September
"Current History."
Professor Guerard also has an article
in "France-Amerique," as well as
critical articles in "Books," the literary
section of the New York Herald-
Tribune.
