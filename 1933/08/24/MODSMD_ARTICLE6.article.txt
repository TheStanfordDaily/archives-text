# Visiting Pastor To Preach Final Sermon Of Quarter
## 
### 
The Reverend Kerr Boyce Tupper of
Philadelphia will preach Sunday in the
Memorial Church at the last divine
worship for the summer quarter.
The music will include the following
numbers: "Andante" (4th Sonata), and
"Cast Thy Burden on the Lord," by
Mendelssohn; "Jesu, dulcis memoria,"
by Victoria; and Choral Blessing, by
Lutkin.
Warren D. Allen will be the accom
panying organist.
