# Choir and Orchestra Open to New Students
## 
### 
An opportunity for musically
minded students who are just en
tering Stanford to get into the col
lege choir and orchestra will be
offered shortly after registration, ac
cording to Warren D. Allen, head
of the music department.
The first choir practice, he said,
would be held from 7 to 7:30 Thurs
day night, October 5, at the Memo
rial Church. Allen, who will be in
charge, urges all new students to
attend.
He also announced that those who
are interested in the orchestra may
meet at the first rehearsal Tuesday
night, October 3, in the Old Roble
Gym under the direction of W. A.
Fuhrmann.
