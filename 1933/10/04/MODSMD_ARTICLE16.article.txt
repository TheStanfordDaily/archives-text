# Gilman Gist, Chappie Artist, Joins Daily
## 
### 
Gilman Gist, '35, cartoonist de
luxe and a la carte, joins up with
the scribbling scribes of the Stan
ford Daily staff today with, his car
toon on the editorial pace under (he
caption "King Football." This is
the first time in several seasons that
a local cartoon by a Stanford artist
has graced these columns.
Gist comes with a long line of suc
cesses behind him, including a good
deal of outstanding work on the Old
Boy's Chaparral during the past two
years. Products of his pen and knife
