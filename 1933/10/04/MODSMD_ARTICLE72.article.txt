# Tryouts For Orchestra, Choir Groups Scheduled
## 
### 
New students with orchestral and
choial experience are invited to try
out for the University Symphony
Orchestra.
Orchestra rehearsals arc scheduled
for Tuesday nights with the Univer
sity Choir practice being held on
Thursdays. One unit of credit is
offered in each of these organiza
tions. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, is in his office, Room 84,
every morning this week from 9 to
12 o'clock. Singers unable to report
early may attend the first rehearsal
Thursday night in the Memorial
Chapel.
