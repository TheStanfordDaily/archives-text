# English Professors Take Fall Quarter Vacations
## 
### 
Three members of the English de
partment of Stanford are taking
their vacations during autumn quar
ter. They are Associate Professor
Edith Roland Mirrielees, and Pro
fessors Henry David Gray and Har
din Craig. All three will be teach
ing during the winter quarter.
Miss Mirrielees is one of the best
known American authorities, in ac
ademic circles, on the technique of
the novel and the short story; Pro
fessor Gray's expert fields are
Shakespeare, Elizabethan literature,
and the drama; and Professor Craig
specializes in Chaucer, Shakespeare,
and intervening periods.
