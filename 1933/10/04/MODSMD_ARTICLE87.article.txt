# English Club Opens 1933 Activities Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
The first English Club meeting of
the 1933-34 academic year will be
held at the home of Dr. Margery
Eailey at 8 o'clock tomorrow night.
The English Club is an honorary
organization limited to 25 students
interested in creative writing, or the
study of literature. Membership is
invitational, and is usually limited
to Juniors and Seniors.
Among the activities sponsored by
the club are the "Stanford Year
took of Writing," the reading of
plays, and the filming of experi
mental moving pictures.
At tomorrow's meeting, plans for.
the year will be discussed.
