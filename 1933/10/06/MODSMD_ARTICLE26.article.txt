# Jane Humbird, '35, Named New Junior Quad Editor
## 
### 
Appointment of Jane Humbird,
'35, as a junior editor on the Quad
was anounced last night by AI Sad
ler, editor. About 25 students re
ported last night at the tryouts. Sad
ler stated that he is quite pleased
with this turnout, but adds that any
others interested may see him at the
Quad office from 1 to 2 o'clock to
morrow or early next week.
Quad offers an opoprtunity to de
velop ability in copy writing and
reading, art work, book designing,
j and general editorial work.
