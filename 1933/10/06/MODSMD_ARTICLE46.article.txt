# Campus Post Office Gets New Red And Black Floor
## 
### 
With improvements being accom
plished all over the campus, the
Stanford post office was not forgot
ten. A complete new flooring job
was started yesterday and is due to
be finished today or tomorrow.
The material being used is Halico
Mastic asphalt tiles, with red and
black included in the color scheme
of the floor. The total cost of the
job will be $236, said Murray Roth,
Stanford comptroller.
