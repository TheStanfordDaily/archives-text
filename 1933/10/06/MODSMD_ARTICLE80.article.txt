# Paul Lebenbaum To Talk On Railroad Engineering
## 
### 
"Eicctrical Aspects of Railroad
Engineering" will be the subject of
an address by Mr. Paul Lebenbaum,
chief electrical engineer for the
Southern Pacific Co., at 7:30 o'clock,
Wednesday evening, October 11th,
in room 283.
He will speak before the Stanford
Branch of the American Institute
of Electrical Engineers. This .speech
is open to the public. Refreshments
will be served.
