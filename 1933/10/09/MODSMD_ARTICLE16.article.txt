# First Jolly-Up Scheduled For Union Dormitories
## 
### 
Women of the Union unit dormi
tories will hold their first Jolly-up
Wednesday evening from 7 to 8
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Mary Anita Loos, chairman of the
affair, promises a fine orchestra. In
vitations have been extended to
members of all fraternities and resi
dents of Toyon, Brainier and En
cina to meet the new women of the
Union.
