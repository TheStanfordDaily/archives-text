# "Y" TEAS
## 
### 
Miss Elizabeth Lam, newly ap
pointed executive secretary of the
Y.W.C.A., will be at home this after
noon in the "Y" rooms on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse
from 3:30 to 5:30 o'clock at the first
of a series of teas for the new
women.
Those women with initials from
A to G inclusive are welcome to
day; from H to O, tomorrow; P to
Z, Wednesday; and graduates,
Thursday, 4 to 6 p.m. Old members
of the "Y" are cordially invited to
attend any day.
