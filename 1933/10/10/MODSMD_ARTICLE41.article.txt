# ALUSTIZA, HOWARD HEAVE 'EM HARD
## 
### 
The Trojans' new man about the
backfield, Howard, throws such fast
passes that his mates have difficulty
in catching them. The Trojan back
in this respect much resembles our
man Frank Alustiza who throws a
fast, heavy ball that is hard to re
tain.
