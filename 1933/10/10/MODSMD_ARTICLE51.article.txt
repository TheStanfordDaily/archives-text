# R.O.T.C. RECORD
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Wash., Oct. 10 (P.1.P.)
—All-time records in R.O.T.C. en
rollment at the University of Wash
ington were broken as hundreds of
incoming freshmen, as well as up
perclassmen, registered for military
training.
"People are beginning to think
me quite mad."—Joan Crawford.
