# "Y" Music Group to Hold First Meeting Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Music Group of the Y.W.C.A.
will hold its first gathering of the
quarter at 4:15 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon in the Choir Room under
the leadership of Mis. A. L. Trow
bridge, who is a member of the
Y.W.C.A. advisory board and who
ha.* had considerable experience in
the musical life of Stanford and Palo
Alto distiicts.
Marion Hattersley, the chairman,
urges all women, new and old, to
attend the meeting. It is hoped that
this group will form the nucleus for
a Women's Glee Club, an organiza
tion which is yet to be established
on this campus.
