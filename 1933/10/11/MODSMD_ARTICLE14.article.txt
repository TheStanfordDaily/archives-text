# Cubbison Announces Rules For Military Equestrians
## 
### 
"New students enrolled in the
F.O.T.C. Department will be re
quired to use the riding pen for the
first quarter," announces Lt.-Col.
Cubbison. Others, however, will be
allowed to ride in the vicinity of the
campus, keeping to the roads and
observing property rights.
The list of horses available- dur
ing the week-end will be posted
each Tuesday in the military office
where students must sign up. Lt.-
Col. Cubbison also states that the
mounted pass privilege will be good
for two hours on either Saturday
afternoon or Sunday.
