# There Is a Difference
## 
### 
What many people could not visualize in theory, they
are beginning to see in fact. The women are making a
difference in our campus life.
The jolly-up at Roble broke all records and almost did
as well for the windows and doors. The place was packed.
The rest of the Hall women hold a jolly-up tonight. It is
moved to the Women's Gym in order to save the termite-
ridden Women's Clubhouse from destruction at the hands
of the anticipated mob.
