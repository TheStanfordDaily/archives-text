# CALIFORNIA OUTPLAYS ST. MARY'S TO WIN CONTEST
## 
### 

With a last minute rush that thrilled 60,000 fans, California's football eleven came from behind to
beat St. Mary's 14 to 13 in an upset in the Berkeley stadium. Arleigh Williams (60) credited by "Slip"
Madigan, Gael coach as the man who did most to defeat his team, is shown gaining nine yards on a
march to victory for the Bears. Others in the play are (left to right) Carlton (15), Relies (65), Jorgerv
sen Nichelini (76) and Carpenter (63). (Associated Press Photo)
