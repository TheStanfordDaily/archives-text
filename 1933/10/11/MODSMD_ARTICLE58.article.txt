# Intramural Sport Meeting To Be Tonight at B.A.C.
## 
### 
First step in getting the Stanford
intramural sports season under way
will be taken at 8 o'clock tonight
at a meeting in the B.A.C. Building.
Representatives of fraternities, halls
and clubs will be present for the
ice-breaking session.
Election of student members of
the intramural staff will be held,
according to Marvie Kahn, intra
mural manager.
