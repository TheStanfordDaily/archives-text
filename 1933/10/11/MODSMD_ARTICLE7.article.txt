# $25,000 Burial Outfit
## 
### 
Surgeons extracted 111 shotgun
pellets from the hide of defunct Gus
Winkler, Chicago gangster. "I want
Gussie to have the best," said Widow
Winkler, planning to spend $25,000
on casket and gems to rest under
the sod with "Gussie."
New York brewers will pay $5,000
for testimony in court convicting a
beer racketeer.
