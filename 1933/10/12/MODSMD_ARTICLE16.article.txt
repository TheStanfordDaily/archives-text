# Rental Library Opened By Stanford Bookstore
## 
### 
As the latest addition to the li
brary facilities of the campus, the
Stanford Book Store announces the
opening of a circulating library, in
which a limited number of volumes
are now available for the nominal
sum of five cents daily.
Subject matter ranges from the
newest fiction to technical scientific
works. All books are new. Al
though the present stock is not com
plete, a shipment arriving early
next week i.s expected to provide
the most complete library of its kind
on the campus.
