# Students, Faculty Asked To Join in Sunday Hike
## 
### 
Stanford students and faculty
members who enjoy hiking are in
vited to join in a hike next Sunday,
sponsored by the Loma Frieta Chap
ter of the Sierra Club.
The group will leave Palo Alto
at 8:15 o'clock and proceed by pri
vate cars to Villa avenue, near the
Los Gatos town hall. The route
chosen involves a round trip of nine
miles and a climb of 2,000 feet, in
cluding passing through Lime Kiln
Gulch.
Further information about the
trip may be obtained by phoning
Elmo A. Robinson at Palo Alto 5585.
