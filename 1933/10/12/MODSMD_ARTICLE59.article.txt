# Club-Swinging Amazons Prepare for Mills Golfers
## 
### 
In view of a golf tournament with
Mills College scheduled for Novem
ber 5, Stanford women students un
der the instruction of Mrs. Potter,
are preparing to slice and hook the
little pellets to all corners of the
fairway and rough.
Three women are being chosen
from each class, sophomore, junior,
senior, and even frosh. The team
will consist of the low handicap
player from each class and two al
ternates.
All women, regardless of enroll
ment in golfing classes, are urged to
get out and dig up the Stanford
course by getting in five scores * a.s
soon as possible so that handicaps,
not to exceed 105, may be given out.
