# Seven Card R.O.T.C. Men Made U.S. Camp Officers
## 
### 
Seven Stanford R.O.T.C. graduates
are now on regular duty as officers
at Citizens Conservation Camps
throughout the state.
Those now on active duty as lieu
tenants in President Roosevelt's for
est camps are William S. Richards,
Parker M. Holt, William T. Brown
ridge, Jr., Donald J. Stevick, John
H. Revel 1, E. H. Jones, and Jerry
Bettman. Holt and Stevick are on
duty at Marysville, and Richards is
in Santa Paula.
