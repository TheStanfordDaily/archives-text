# Los Angeles Pastor Speaks Here Sunday

###

pastor of the Los Angeles Temple
Baptist Church and one time arch
enemy of famed "Bob" Shuler, will
deliver Sunday's sermon in the
Stanford Memorial Church.

prelude, and "Sing We All Now
With One Accord," by Praetorious
will be the lntroit. The Canticle
scheduled is Jubilate, the anthem,
Christensen's "Beautiful Savious."
