# Golf Minded Women To Meet in Women's Gym
## 
### 
Jane Dearing, women's golf man
ager, announced that all women in*
terested in golf will meet in the
lounge room of the Women's Gym
on Monday, October 16, at 4 o'clock.
It makes no difference if they were
not able to attend the last meeting;
they may still come on Monday.
