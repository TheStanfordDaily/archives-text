# Tomorrow Is Last Day For Study-List Changes
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the* last day students
may change their study lists for
this quarter without fine, announces
the Registrar's office.
After tomorrow changes can be
made only by petition, approved by
the Registration Committee, and
subject to a fine of $2 for each
course added or dropped. Where
one course is substituted for an
other, the fine is $3. All program
changes made since Registration day
should be filed at the Registrar's of
fice on the regular revised study
fist cards.
