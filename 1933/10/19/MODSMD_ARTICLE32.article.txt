# We Love Palo Alto
## 
### 
Xow, Palo Alio is a splendid village. We love il and,
as we contribute to ils upkeep through IralTie fines, and lo
the care of its needy through wood-chopping, we grow to
love il even more.
Hut love has ils limit and that limit was reached yes
terday afternoon at the Thomas lecture. Hundreds of stu
dents stood while more forethinking, probably less occu
pied Palo Alio residents grabbed off the front seals. More
students craned their necks in thai tortuous gallery.
Rarely does Ihe Campus feature a celebrity like
Thomas. When il does, Ihe best seals and all the seats if
necessary should be reserved for the students and faculty
not for well-meaning Paly Villagers.
