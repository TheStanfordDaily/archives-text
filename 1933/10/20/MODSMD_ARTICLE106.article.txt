# Dashing Swagger Suits Show Gain in Popularity
## 
###

j ger suits, very dashing and smart
! with their novel shoulder treat
ments and well-cut coats of three-
I quarters and seven-eighths length.
Although this type of suit has been
in vogue for some time, it is in
creasingly popular, especially in
tweed.
Three-piece sweater suits in un
usual and interesting weaves and
cuts, and matching twin sweater
sets are also good.
