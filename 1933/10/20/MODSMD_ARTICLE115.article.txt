# Dark Skirts Are Featured
## 
### 
Keeping in step with Dame Fash
ion, who has this year decreed that
shirtwaists and blouses are again
ccming back, I. Magnin is em
phasizing dark blouses and dark
skirts.
Dark color combinations are es
pecially good for this season of the
year and to be in the "swim" the
college woman must use the more
somber tones. But even with all of
the dark colors there is still room
in the wardrobe for pastel shade
gowns in the informal or formal
division.
Twin sweaters and sweater com
binations are popular. Magnin's
lias a complete line of sweaters to
tempt the young woman.
