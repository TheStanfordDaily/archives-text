# Velveteen Again Popular For Campus, Sport Wear
## 
### 
Back to the days of grandma have
the fashion designers gone to resur
rect velveteen for their most modish
dresses. >
Velveteen is especially good for
college women because it won't
muss up and can be worn for street,
afternoon, or sports. All colors look
well in this material, though sev
eral outstanding dresses in the new
wine red shades have been draw
ing much favorable comment.
