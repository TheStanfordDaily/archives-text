# LATIN-AMERICANS
## 
### 
The Ijatin - American Club will
meet Monday evening at 8 o'clock
those who speak Spanish or Portu
guese are invited. Officers will be
elected at the first meeting, and the
membership will be closed.
Heavy weight and nimble feet
don't go together often, but Carl
Goode of the North Carolina State
College freshman football squad is
an exception. He weighs 255, but
has run the century in 11 seconds.
