# W. H. Brown Honored By Health Board Position
## 
### 
Stanford's faculty scored another
point when Dr. Walter H. Brown,
professor of Hygiene at Stanford,
was appointed third vice-president
and member of the executive board
of the American Public Health As
sociation at its recent meeting held
at Indianapolis.
Dr. Thomas A. Storey, medical
adviser of men, commenting on this
appointment to membership on the
executive board of this association,
said: "Dr. Brown's long experience
in public health work fits him ex
traordinarily well for such service."
