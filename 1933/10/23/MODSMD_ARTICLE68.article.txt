# Scholastic Press Group Visits Campus Saturday

### 
Delegates from the 217 high
schools to be represented at the
Central California Scholastic Press
Association's tenth annual conven
tion, being held on this campus on
Saturday, November 4th, will regis
ter early that morning before elect
ing officers at the business meeting.
The Stanford-Olympic Club game
being held that afternoon is open
to the delegates through a generous
arrangement of the 8.A.C., as diver
sion from business.
Al the banquet, in the evening, a
well-known speaker, as yet unan
nounced, will address the conferees.
Acting Head of the Journalism De
partment, Buford O. Brown, and
Secretary of the Department, Mrs.
Marjorie Bryant, are handling the
