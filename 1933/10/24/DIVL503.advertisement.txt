dashery for the blank line
of the Lousy Limerick. Bring
it to the Roos Encina Shop
before 6 p.m. Friday. If your
"contribution" is selected
for firsi place . . you win
the article referred to in the
Limerick!!

"Your taste in neckwear's below
zero—

You'd see Roos Pin-dot Ties
