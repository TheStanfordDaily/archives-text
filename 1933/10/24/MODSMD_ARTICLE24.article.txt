# Encina Freshmen to Vote Tonight for New Officers
## 
### 
After stowing away Common's
food tonight, freshmen will rise, fold
their napkins, and go to the lobby,
where they will ballot for the se
lection of president, secretary, and
dance committee chairman of En
cina Club.
Petition-nominated candidates are
Ted B. Geissler and Jim Reynolds
for president; Don Reed, Jesse Wor
ley, and Stanford Zimet, secretary;
George Ferris, Jack Geisen, Web
Jones, Jr., Peter Knecht, and Tom
Stetson, dance committee chairman.
