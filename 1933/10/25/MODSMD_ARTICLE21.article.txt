# Seventeen S.C. Ducats Available for Resale
## 
### 
Seventeen rooters' ducats for the
Southern California game have been
returned to the B.A.C. ticket dis
pensary and are available today for
immediate resale. First come, first
served, says head man Jake Irwin.
Although the ticket sale for the
contest has definitely been closed,
tickets which are returned to the
B.A.C. up to November 4 will be of
fered for resale. Students who have
not yet been able to purchase tick
ets for this game may sign up with
Irwin in the event that any more
are turned in.
