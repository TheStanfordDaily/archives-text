# Faculty Members Present At French Club Meeting
## 
### 
Le Cercle Frangais last night
held their meeting in Los Altos at
the home of Dr. Williams. The
French play "La Clcs" was read by
members of the club, Eleanor Wil
liams, Suzanne Roquet, and Robert
Anderson, after which refreshments
were served.
President Helen Thomas presided
over a group of thirty members,
with faculty of the French Depart
ment also present. They were: Dr.
and Mrs. Espinosa, Dr. and Mrs.
Schwartz, Mr. Maughton, and Mr.
Mason.
