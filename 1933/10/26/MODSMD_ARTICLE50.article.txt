# Rooters' Special to U.S.C. Will Be Mirth, Fun Train
## 
### 
Eat, drink, and be merry will be
the motto for the men and women
making the trip to the USC game at
Los Angeles by train. This was as
sured today when it was announced
that the fraternities and eating
groups of the campus are to furnish
sandwiches and assorted, delicacies
to the persons making the trip.
Coffee and punch will be the or
der of the day for those who feel an
urge to slake their thirst. Music
will be furnished by some of the
campus orchestras and a good time
is guaranteed to those rooters who
are going by train. Tickets for the
rooters' special are to be had at the
A.S.S.U. office in the Union, the only
place where they may be obtained.
Womankind is divided into three
parts. The intelligent, the beauti
ful, and the majority.
Theta Chis have been holding
archery practice. One member hit a
50-cent piece at 60 feet.
