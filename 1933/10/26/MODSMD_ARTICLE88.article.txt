# Daily Football Scouts Beat Byrd to Southern Wastes
## 
### 
Lillle America, Oct. 2(5, Special to
the Daily:
Our scouting party has just ar
rived in this frozen paradise, hours,
days ahead of the Byrd Expedition.
First sight to greet our eyes on
landing: a mammoth polar bear.
He was slightly difficult to talk to,
but a little gift of 10 fish pursuaded
him to travel to the Farm to take
an active part in the Daily-Chappy
gridiron cmbroglio. .
This is but a meagre beginning.
We expect, to do better. This place
is full of strange creatures; but we
will have to go far to beat that
Chappy outfit. However, the pros
pects look good!

One of the chief industries of the
country of Andorra is smuggling
goods between France and Spain.
