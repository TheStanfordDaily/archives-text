# Gaieties Song Hit to Have Conference Party Debut
## 
### 
At Conference Party tonight,
where most of the females on the
campus will gather under direction
of A.W.S. head Jeanne Millard, one
of the new songs recently written
by two campus women is to have
its premiere.
Entitled "I'm Positive That I Love
You." the future Gaieties lyric will
be played by Gordon Heche with
vocalist Mary Louise Stewart ren
dering the words.
