# Coghlan Given Laundry Can Ride to Roble Steps
## 
### 
Clad in shorts and a gay colored
bathrobe, Phil Coghlan received
formal initiation to the clan of "Se
quoia Roughs" as he was trans
ported in a laundry can to the
Roble front porch last night.
Coghlan having "swiped" the cue
ball from the Sequoia pool table,
the Roughs riddled his room, re
moving his bed and bed clothes
Further trouble on his part caused
his transportation to Roble, the
laundry can in which lie was im
prisoned being carried on Coghlan's
bed.
No admittance was gained to
Roble, but heads popped out of
nearly all front-view windows to
watch Coghlan grab his bed and
run when released.
