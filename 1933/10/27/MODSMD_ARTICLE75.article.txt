# Bay Institutions Purchase Blocks of Concert Ducats
## 
### 
Blocks of season tickets for the
A.S.S.U. Student Concert Series
have been taken up by several Bay
region institutions, according to
Manager Prentice Messimer.
They include San Jose State Col
lege, Palo Alto High School, Miss
Barker's Castilleja, Montezuma
Mountain School for Boys, and the
Los Gatos High School. The series
opens November I with Mischa El
irian.
