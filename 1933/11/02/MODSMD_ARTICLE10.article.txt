# Dr. Whitney, Psychologist, To Lead Discussion at 'Y'
## 
### 
Dr. Elizabeth B. Whitney, con
sultant psychologist at Stanford,
will lead a discussion to be set later
at 5 o'clock today in the Women's
Clubhouse, sponsored by the Fam
ily Relations Group of the Y.W.C.A.,
with Wilma Jenkins as chairman.
This is the second of four meetings
under Dr. Whitney's leadership.
All campus women are cordially
invited to join in the discussion this
afternoon with this noted psycholo
gist, who led one of the most, pop
ular groups —that of Family Rela
tions—at the Y.W.C.A. Asilomar
Conference last summer.
