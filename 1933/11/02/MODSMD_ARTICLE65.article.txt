# Wagner's Life Subject Of German Club Meeting
## 
### 
Richard Wagner, German com
poser, will be the subject of this
afternoon's meeting of the German
Club at the home of Miss Helena
Nye in Palo Alto. Wagner's life will
be discussed by Miriam Fanger.
Raymond Lille and Adolph Zech
will illustrate the discussion of Wag
nerian opera with phonograph rec
ords. Group singing and refresh
ments will complete the program.
