# Medical Aptitude Test For School Applicants Dec. 6
## 
### 
Medical Aptitude Test given by
the Association of American Medi
cal Colleges will be held on Wednes
day, December 6, at 2 o'clock for
all students who are planning to
enter medical school next fall quar
ter. This is the one and only time
the test will be given during the
year. A fee of $1 will be charged.
