# Farm Episcopal Group Meets Sunday Evening
## 
### 
Business, enlightenment, and food
will be served Stanford Episcopal
students, who are scheduled to meet
at 6:15 o'clock Sunday night in the
Parish House, 425 Hamilton Avenue,
Palo Alto.
Buffet supper will be followed by
a brief business meeting and a talk
by Dr. E. Hoag, on "Hygiene of the
Mouth."
Donkeys in India are so small their
riders have to tuck up their legs
to keep them from dragging on the
ground.
