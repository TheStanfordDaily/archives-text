# WHERE'S THE WATER
## 
### 
ANN ARBOR, Mich.—Water was
at a special premium at Michigan
yesterday. Harry Kipke ruled no
ptayers could visit the water bucket
except those who intercepted passes
tossed by the freshmen using Illi
nois plays.
