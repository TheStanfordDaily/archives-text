# Circle "S" Society Elects Carl Knecht President
## 
### 
Adding one more honor to his
rapidly accumulating list, soccer
Captain Carl Knecht was elected
to succeed water polo Captain Gor
die Graves as president of Circle
"S" at the society's meeting yester
day noon at the Beta house.
Quadrangle Clubman, Phi Phi,
and all-Conferenca soccer stalwart,
Knecht's first task will be to or
ganize the all - University novice
tournament to be held early in De
cember. Waddy White, also an out
standing soccer man, was chosen to
fill the dual role of Secretary-
Treasurer of the Society.
