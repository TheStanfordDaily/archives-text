# DEAN YOST AT HOME
## 
### 
Resuming an annual custom, Dean
Mary Yost has scheduled the first of
her weekly "at homes" for tomor
row, from 4 to 6 o'clock. It is to be
informal, and students are invited
to drop in at any time. Quad clothes
are proper.
