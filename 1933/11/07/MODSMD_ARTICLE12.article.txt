# Sleeping Porch Rally Ousts Grid Stars
## 
### 
Last Friday night the Phi Delt
fraternity held an impromptu un
der their sleeping porch.
The purpose of this rally was to
urge the Phi Delt grid stars on to
greater heights, but the rally soon
pot out of control and all the foot
ball men moved over into the other
side of the house.
