# Toyon Dance Dedicated To Famed Stanford Axe
## 
### 
When Toyon dwellers, as well as
guests of the dormitory hold their
autumn dance on November 17, en
tire motif will be dedicated to the
symbol of the Big Game, the resur
rected Axe.
Fortunate in that it will be one
of the first dances under the new
policy of decorations and science
edicts recently suggested by Les
Habies, Chairman Art Kehke ex
pects to transfer the ballroom for
the evening into a fitting atmosphere
to celebrate redecoration of the
trophy.
Copper designs will be worked
out and lighting effects in harmony
with these used in illuminating the
dance. Other committee members
are Art Hearn, Pat Goodrich, John
Freeman, and Gilbert Webster.
