# English Club Will Hear Selections from Operas
## 
### 
With the opera in the midst of
its short, popular season in San
Francisco, English Club members
meet tonight at the home of Profes
sor Merrill Clubb to hear composi
tions from the scheduled perfor
mances. This timely opportunity for
the members takes the form, as
usual, of an informal gathering at
8:15 o'clock.
