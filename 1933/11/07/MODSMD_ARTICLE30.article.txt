# Stanford Education Dean Returns from Arizona
## 
### 
Dr. Grayson Kefauver, dean of the
education department, returned
Sunday night from a short trip to
Phoenix, where he attended a meet
ing of the Arizona State Teachers'
Association.
He held conference Friday noon
with former Stanford students of
Ihe School of Education, addressing
them on the present program of the
education department here at Stan
lord.
A graduate of the University of
Arizona, and formerly engaged in
educational work in that state, Dr.
Kefauver was extended a hearty
welcome upon his arrival there Fri
day morning.
