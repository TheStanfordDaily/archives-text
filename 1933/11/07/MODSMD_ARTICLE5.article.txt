# Ballots End Bulling
## 
### 
Democratic National Chairman
James A. Farley has at last come
out openly in favor of Joseph V.
McKee in the New York mayorality
fight.
On the eve of the election O'Brien
said, "I will be re-elected by a
handsome plurality."
Said McKee, "I am certain I will
be elected."
Said LaGuardia, "The people of
New York are on the eve of a
sweeping victory for good govern
ment."
