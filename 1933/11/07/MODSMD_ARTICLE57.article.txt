# Whitman College Gets Carnegie Book Grant
## 
### 
Whitman College, Walla Walla,
W;ish., is to be the recipient of $3,-
000 this year from the Carnegie
Corporation of New York City, as
part of $15,000 grant made for the
purchase of books.
Headed by the librarian of the
University of Michigan, Carnegie
Corporation makes book purchases
amounting to $250,000 a year.
