# Oklahoma Indians Enjoy Raw Meat, Pipe Smoking
## 
### 
Smoking of a peace pipe and
chewing of raw meat were diver
sions entertained by the Indian Club
of the University of Oklahoma at
their annual celebration 011 the Ok
lahoma campus November 4.
Clad in war paint and breech
clothes, with a blanket here and
there, the club whooped around a
teepee and council fire. Club mem
bers smoked the peace pipe. Pledges
chewed raw meat.
