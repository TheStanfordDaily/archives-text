# Call Issued for Men Dancers, Stilt-Walkers
## 
### 
Men who can walk on stilts and
men interested in dancing in the
comedy chorus are on the latest call
list for the Big Game Gaieties. The
former report today to Harold Hel
veiiston and the latter to Miss Ade
laide Kelly, in charge of the cho
ruses.
Miss Kelly has also issued a call
for further women's chorus tryouts
for 7:15 o'clock tonight. The list of
women who are to report tonight
will be posted at 10 o'clock this
morning outside Room 203 in the
English Building.
The compass of human hearing is
about 88 notes, or the range of an
ordinary piano.
