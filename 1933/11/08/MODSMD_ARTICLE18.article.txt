# Frosh Women Will Give Supper Dance Saturday
## 
### 
A good portion of Roble Club is
expected to go supper dancing Sat
urday night at Roble's first dance
of the quarter. Committee Heads
Margaret White and Sue Ward are
working to turn Roble into a proper
atmosphere for the event.
Supper will be served at 6:15
o'clock and dancing will follow un
til 11:30. The bids are now on sale
for 75 cents.
