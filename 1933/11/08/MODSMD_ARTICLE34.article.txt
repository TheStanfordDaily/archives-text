# S.C. Game Bus Tickets Available at Low Prices
## 
### 
Bus tickets to the S.C. game may
still be purchased at the A.S.S.U.
office, says Student Manager Les
Hables. Round-trip fare is $9 in
cluding to and from hotel.
Featuring low prices, A.S.S.U.-
bargained, tickets provide transpor
tation on the Greyhound special,
leaving Friday night at 8 o'clock on
the night coach for Los Angeles, re
turning 12:30 o'clock midnight after
the hilarities are over, and arriving
ai 2 o'clock in the afternoon, in time
io take off the ice-packs and get
ready lor Monday.
