# BARBERS LOWER PRICES
## 
### 
A split in the solidarity of the
local barbering trade was revealed
yesterday when 10 of the 19 shops
in Palo Alto announced a reversion
to the 50-cent charge for hair-cut
ting. to take effect tomorrow. Re
cently all shops in the city adopted
the 65-cent rate when the N.F.A.
code for barbers was signed.
