Tea and Dance in Satin
with Mousseline de Soie
Dorothy lost her heart to the crisp, dainty white mousseline blouse.
And Doris liked the sophistication of the slim tunic jumper. In
fact, they both declared this the cutest jumper dress ever. And,
being clever young things, they realise how many costumes they
can ring up with different blouses. Black or brown satin. Sizes
11 to 17.
