# Y.W.C.A. Group Will Visit Chinese Babies' Shelter
## 
### 
Welfare-minded women are in
vited to attend a tour of the Chi
nese Babies' Home in Atherton, to
be made today by the members of
the Y.W.C.A. Social Service Group.
Transportation will be arranged for
those who wish to go, if they will
meet at the Women's Clubhouse at
2 o'clock.
The Chinese Baby Home, which
was founded by Donaldena Came
ron, is a shelter for orphan and de
serted Chinese children.
A large crowd attended a similar
tour, which was made on Tuesday.
