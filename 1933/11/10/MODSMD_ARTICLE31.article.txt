# Trautman to Speak At Trojan Bonfire Rally
## 
### 
President Jerry Trautman will re
turn S.C. Student Body President
Laurence Pritchard's visit to the
Stanford rally, speaking at the giant
bonfire rally of El Trojan tonight.
Though he will be busy speaking
to alumni groups at luncheons and
dinners, President Trautman will
probably get to see the game.
