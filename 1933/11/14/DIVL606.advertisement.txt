Now is the time for all good men to buy new
DINNER JACKETS
. . . and can you name a single place where
you can get as smart a tuxedo as at Phelps-
Terkel?
Double-breasteds are considered the top o'
the list this year—waistlined. full-chested,
with rich grosgrain lapels. Of course, single
breasteds are every bit as correct.
*
Here, off the rack, either model sells for
$30 and $35. Tailored to your measure, $50
and more. (There's still time to have yours
tailored for Big Came night if you come in
at once.) Better buy that tuxedo now and
be all set for the winter season.
ACCESSORIES, TOO
Waistcoats, ties, studs, shirts — authenti
cally styled—modestly priced. Check over
your needs and come in soon to see these
fine-looking accessories.
