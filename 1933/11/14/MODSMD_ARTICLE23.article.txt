# Barclay Will Speak To Stanford Women's Club
## 
### 
Professor Thomas S. Barclay of
the Political Science Department
will address the evening section, of
the Stanford Women's Club of San
Francisco at its dinner meeting
Thursday at the Women's City Club.
His subject will be, "After the N. R.
A., What?" Miss Nanelia Siegfried
is chairman of the group. -
