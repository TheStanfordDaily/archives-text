# AMERICANS LOVE MUSIC
## 
### 
Americans are Wagner fans.
Figures on the 2,000 foreigners at
tending this year's musical festival
at Bayreuth, Germany, showed
! Americans in the lead as devotees
of Wagnerian music.
Americans numbered more than
1300, while English, Swiss, and
Czechs numbered more than 200
each, and French about 150.
