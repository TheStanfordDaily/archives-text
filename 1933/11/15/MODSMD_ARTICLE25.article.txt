# Concert Office Continues Sale of Season Tickets
## 
### 
Season tickets for the four re
maining concerts of the Concert Se
ries are still being sold, according to
Prentice Messimer. Concert Series

manager.
Opening the old Union office for
the first time since the Elman con
cert, Messimer announces that ticket
statistics are still being compiled and
will be available for publication
soon, but that there are a large num
ber of very good season tickets left.
Teresina, the Spanish dancer with
out a first name, will be the next
artist to appear on the Series.
