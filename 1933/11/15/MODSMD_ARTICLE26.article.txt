# A.I.E.E. to Visit Dam, Mocassin Creek Project
## 
### 
Members of Stanford's branch of
the American Institute of Electrical
Engineers journey this week-end
to inspect the Mocassin Creek Power
House and the Hetch-Hetchy dam.
This trip is the first of several
planned for electrically-minded en
gineers during the current year.
Those planning to go are asked to
sign up immediately on the A.I.E.E.
bulletin board.
