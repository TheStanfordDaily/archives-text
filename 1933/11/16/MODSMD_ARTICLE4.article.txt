# Reconciled?
## 
### 
Potent National Grange, largest
farm fraternity, converged on Boise,
Idaho, nearly swamped the town.
Tobacco-chewing members plumped
squarely for '"controlled inflation and
managed currency." Which brand
they did not say.
Last night delegates ate roast elk,
pondered a telegram just received
from harried Sec. Wallace stressing
need of "retreating from surplus
acres and surplus toil." Also men
tioned was fact that U.S. foreign
trade is gone, farmers must stake
all on reviving industry. NRA and
AAA are thus neatly reconciled.
