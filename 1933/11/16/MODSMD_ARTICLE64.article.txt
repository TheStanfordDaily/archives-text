# EX-PRESIDENT SEES STANFORD WIN
## 
### 

Among the 90,000 who saw Stanford beat Southern California 13.7
in Los Angeles was Former President Hoover who was football man
ager of Stanford in his undergraduate days. Ray Lyman Wilbur, former
secretary of the interior and president of Stanford, was also present.
Left to right: Mrs. Hoover, Mr. Wilbur, Mr. Hoover and Mrs. Wilbur. |
