# The 						Minor Sports Boys
## 
### 
"While we are still dozing in the warm splendor of our
victory over I'.S.C., we should not forget everything else.
Principally, we should not forget those plugging indi
viduals termed the minor sports participants. No great
publicity heralds their comings or their goings. Xo rooter
masses cheer them on. Hut they keep going nevertheless.
These men plav their game for the full fun in it, and
nothing else. Of course, they earn their red and white felt;
hul that and fun is all they get.
There are so many of these minor sports floating
around, some being abolished and others being created
every now and then, that if is diilicult to give any of them
a pat on their sweated back. Hut for soccer, fencing, water
polo, which are now competing, we offer cheery congratu
lations.
