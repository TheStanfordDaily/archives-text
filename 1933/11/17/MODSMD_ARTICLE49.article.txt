# Y.W. Social Service Group Holds Open Meet Today
## 
### 
Welfare-minded women are in
vited to a meeting of the Y.W.C.A.
Social Service Group at 4:30 o'clock
today in the Women's Clubhouse.
Plans for Christmas philanthropic
work will be made, including ar
rangements for collecting and filling
Christmas baskets. Also, dolls will
be dressed for the Convalescent
Home party.
