# Women's Scholarship And Health Chairmen to Meet
## 
### 
Called for Monday at 4 o'clock is
a meeting, the first of the quarter,
of the health and scholarship chair
men of all women's living groups.
The conclave will be held at Dean
Mary Yost's house.
Dean Yost and Miss Alice In
graham will speak to house repre
sentatives and aid the group in for
mulating definite plans to achieve
the year's aims. Adrienne Hawkins
is chairman of the A.W.S. group.
