# Men Forced to Dance At U. of Nevada Socials
## 
### 
Compelling all men attending to
ask some young lady to dance, Uni
versity of Nevada men's upperclass
committee attempts to abolish wall
flowers at weekly social hours.
Dances are cut to live minutes in
stead of the traditional 15 minutes,
special entertainment presented to
add more spice to the dances. The
new policy has been adopted as a
result of unfavorable comment con
cerning the socials.
