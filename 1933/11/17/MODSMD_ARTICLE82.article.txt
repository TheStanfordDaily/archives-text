# Long Beach Celebrates Minus Razors, Lipstick
## 
### 
What with a whiskerino contest
for the men, a hair-braiding and
non-lip-sticking orgy for the wo
men, and a gigantic old-clothes day
celebration, the Long Beach Junior
College is attempting to instill the
old spirit into the local football
eleven in the "Big Game" against
the forces from Pasadena J.C.
