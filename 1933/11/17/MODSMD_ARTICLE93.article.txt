# Liebendorfer to Speak At Toyon Club Smoker
## 
### 
Don Liebendorfer, stadium loud
speaker talker and director of pub
licity for the 8.A.C., will speak to
Toyon men at their club smoker
Tuesday night, telling his experi
ences in game broadcasting and
publicity work.
Others on the program include
pianist Walt Baird and the Three
Royal Cards. Yolls will be led by
A1 Pcache and refreshments will be
served in the outer lobby.
Ira Cross, smoker committee
chairman, is assisted by A1 Abra
hamson, Phil Coleman, Charles Em
crick, and Ray Smith.
