# Dames Meet in Bridge, Reading, Sewing Groups
## 
### 
Three group meetings of the Stan
ford Dames are scheduled for the
coming week. They are as follows:
Sewing circle meets tomorrow
afternoon at 3 o'clock with Mrs. W.
E. Armstrong at 695 Oberlin street,
Palo Alto.
The reading group will hold its
bi-weekly gathering under the di
rection of Mrs. William F. Robertson
and Mrs. M. Hill at Kingscote, Apt.
1, on Wednesday afternoon at 3
o'clock.
The bridge section will meet
Thursday afternoon at 2 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse.
