# Winter Tuition Notes Must Be Filed by Friday
## 
### 
Friday is the deadline for
winter quarter tuition notes.
All those who now feel that
they will have a desire for
these little life-savers should
apply at once to the Dean of
Men's office, upstairs in tin;
Administration Building.
Those applying for these
notes should have at least a
"C" average.
As old-timers well know a
tuition note is a procedure
wherehy needy students sign
do not pay for their tuition;
the University then waives tui
tion payment; the student re
pays the University in the fu
ture when he is more securely
fixed, economically.
Applications will he taken
by Miss Vivicnne Church, sec
rotary to the Dean of Men.
