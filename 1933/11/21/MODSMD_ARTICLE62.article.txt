# Stanford Dames Choose Officers at Recent Meet
## 
### 
Mrs. William G. Hoover was
elected president of the Stanford
Dame#, organization for wives of
Stanford students, at a recent meet
ing of the club. Other officers who
were chosen to direct the affairs of
the society for the present school
year are Mrs. H. E. Wheeler, vice
president; Mrs. Donald Piston, treas
urer; Mrs. C. W. Johnson, recording
secretary; and Mrs. N. M. Daniels,
corresponding secretary.
Also at the meeting were elected
the following women as faculty
sponsors: Mr.s. Geoige W. Dowrie,
Mrs. George E. Osborne, and Mrs.
Ward Kindy whose husbands repre
sent the business, law, and engi
neering schools, respectively.
