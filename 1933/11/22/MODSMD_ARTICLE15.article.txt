# Gaieties Stars Sing At Pre-Big Game Dance
## 
### 
Sam Lowry's 10-piece Gaieties or
chestra, the singing of Gaieties stars,
special lighting and decorations, Big
Game and Gaieties excitement, will
combine to make the Rally Com
mittee's pre-Big Game dance after
the Gaieties Friday night more than
an ordinary two-hour dance.
Another lowest price in history is
hailed, with the announcement of
the tariff for the dance of only 25
cents per couple for those who saw
the Rains' Head show, and 50 cents
I for others. Roughs will be admitted
:to the dance for the full price.
Contrary to a previous announce
ment, tickets will be on sale at the
Women's Gym Friday night, and not
at the A.S.S.U. office during the
1 week.
