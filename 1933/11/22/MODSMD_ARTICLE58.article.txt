# Pom-Poms for Saturday Game On Sale Today
## 
### 
Pom-poms, necessary for all
women entering the Stanford
rooting section Saturday, will
be on sale all day today at the
A.S.S.U. office in the old Union.
Made especially at the order of
the Rally Committee, the em
blems are sold at the cost price
of 25 cents.
Besides the pom-poms, regu
lation equipment for rooters
will include white attire, and
rooters' caps for men. Women
will hold cards and participate
in stunts given by the rooting
section.
