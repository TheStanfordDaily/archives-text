# Dr. Franklin Honored By English Chemical Society
## 
### 
Word was received yesterday from
London that Professor Edward C.
Franklin of Stanford has been se
lected as an honorary Fellow in the
Chemical Society of England.
Signed by G. P. Morgan, the let
ter praises Dr. Franklin for his
meritorious work in all branches of
chemistry.
The organization to which he has
been elected is the highest chem
istry honorary society in Great
Britain.
