# Mexican Engineers Find Rich Deposit of Opals
## 
### 
A rich deposit of opals has been
discovered in the Cerro de la Calce
donia, near the town of Villa Ma
dero, in Mexico, by engineers of the
Geological Institute of Mexico.
The Institute declares that these
opals are the best yet found in Mex
ico. Among them are stones of a
great variety of colors, ranging from
milky white to'vivid red.
