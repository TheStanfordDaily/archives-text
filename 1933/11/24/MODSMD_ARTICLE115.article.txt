# Rev. Charles D. Gardner To Deliver Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
The Reverend D. Charles Gardner
wiil deliver the Thanksgiving service
Sunday morning in the Stanford
Memorial Chapel. Organ music by
Warren D. Allen, official organist,
will include:
Prelude: "Allein Gott in der Hob
sei Ehr" Bach
Introit: Jubilate Deo.. .Mendelssohn
Anthems: "Non Danket Alle Gott"
(from "Hymn of Praise")
Mendelssohn
"From Thee, O Bounteous God"
(The Seasons) Haydn
Soloists: Verda Hogan, soprano,
and W. Madison Taylor, tenor.
Sunday afternoon Allen will pre
sent his usual 4 o'clock organ recital
n the Chapel.
