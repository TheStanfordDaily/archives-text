# Block "S" Imitation Of Coach's Yale Letter
## 
### 
Billy McLaine, varsity football
pitcher for four years and captain
of the team in '96, has the distinc
tion of being the first possessor of a
Stanford block "S."' His letter was
an imitation of a Yale "Y" worn by
"Pop" Bliss, coach of the football
team at the time.
