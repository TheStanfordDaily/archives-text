# Rushton's Band Will Be Feature of Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
Special arrangements of popular
dance tunes by A1 Rushton will be
featured at the Sophomore Cotillion
to be held December 8.
The motif for the dance was not
decided at last night's committee
meeting but will be fixed soon, said
Bill Drew, sophomore class presi
dent. Rushton's Royal Cardinals, a
12-piece organization, will play for
the Cotillion, to be held in the dance
studio of the Women's Gym.
