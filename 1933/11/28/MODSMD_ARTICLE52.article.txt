# Hugh Johnson to Speak Before Western C. of C.
## 
### 
During the opening session of the
Tenth Western Division meeting of
the Chamber of Commerce of the
United States, J. Hugh Jackson, dean
of Stanford's Graduate School of
Business, will address the conven
tion on "The Responsibilities of
Western Organizations." The State
Capitol, Sacramento, will play host
to the westerners during December
3, 4, 5.
Among the topics to be presented
by leading authorities from the
business and university world are
trade relations, codes, recovery, and
public works. Governor Rolph will
welcome the delegates, and Paul
Shoup, vice-president of the national
organization, will act as chairman of
the sessions.
