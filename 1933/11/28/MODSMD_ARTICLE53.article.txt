# '37 Women to Usher At 'Y' Vesper Service Today
## 
### 
Freshman women are to be ushers
at the Y.W.C.A. vesper service this
afternoon at 5 o'clock in the chancel
of the Memorial Church.
This is the first meeting of the
kind to be held this year by the
"Y," and its very unusualness should |
interest, many women in attending.
At the service, of which Dean
Mary Yost is to be the principal j
speaker, organ music is to be sup- i
plied by Warren D. Allen.
The members of the "Y" are to
wear white dresses. All members
are expected to come, and all
women who are interested are in
vited to attend.
