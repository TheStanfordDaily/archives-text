# Card Women Tennis Stars Defeat San Mateo J.C. 3-2
## 
### 
In a tennis match with the San
Mateo Junior College Women's Ten
nis Club yesterday on the Roble
Courts the Stanford Women's Tennis
Club emerged victorious in the three
singles, but the Junior Collegians
were champipns of the two doubles
matches.
Carrie Richter of Stanford played
a beautiful game against San Ma
teo's Virginia Mclver, defeating her
8-10, 6-2, 6-2; Stanfordian Jane
Snow won from Margery McCam
eley of San Mateo 6-1, 8-6, and Betty
Pierce of the Farm defeated Junior
Collegian Helene Hoag by a score
of 6-2. 6-1.
In the doubles, Edwinea Montague
and Mildred Wheatley of Stanford
were vanquished by Lowella Jacque
find Garnet Drewes of San Mateo to
tbe tune of 3-6, 4-6; the Farm part
nership of Gwen Hitt and Margery
Buettner were defeated by Junior
Collegians Grace O'Neill and Etna
Ensch 4-6, 6-2, 5-7.
