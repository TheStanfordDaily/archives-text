# Quad Proofs Must Be In Friday for Yearbook
## 
### 
Twelve hundred out of 1,700 sets
of Quad proofs are still out and must
be returned without fail this week
if they are to go in the yearbook.
It is absolutely imperative that de
cisions be made immediately and the
proofs taken to the Quad office on
the second floor of the Press Build
ing by Friday, December 8, at the
very latest.
Anyone wishing retakes of pic
tures to be used for Christmas pres
ents can make appointments for sit
tings at the Fisher Studios, 165 Post
Street, San Francisco. Rates for this
service will not suffer increase.
