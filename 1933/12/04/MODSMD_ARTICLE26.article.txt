# Food Institute Director To Address Minnesota U.
## 
### 
Dr. Joseph S. Davis, director of
the Food Research Institute at Stan
ford, will address members of the
faculty and student body of the Uni
versity of Minnesota, December 11,
in the Northrop Memorial Audito
rium of that school. His subject will
be the operation of the Agriculture
Adjustment Act. Dr. Davis has been
selected to make a study of the Act
by the Brookings Institute of Wash
ington, D.C.
