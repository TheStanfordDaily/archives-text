# Rose Bowl Game Jobs Available to Students
## 
### 
Stanford students who expect to
make the trek to Pasadena for New
Year's .Day and to find jobs wait
ing for them at the Rose Bowl, must
file applications at the B.A.C. this
week-end, it was announced yester
day.
Jobs available at the present time
include work in any of the many
branches of activity made necessary
by the game, with the exception of
ticket selling. All work is for the
day of the game only, with hours
determined by the individual jobs.
Applicants must sign up at the
B.A.C. any time on Friday, Satur
day, or Monday, December 8, 9, 11.
