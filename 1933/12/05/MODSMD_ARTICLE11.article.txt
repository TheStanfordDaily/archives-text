# Kreps to Speak Tonight To Ideas of Living Group
## 
### 
Professor Theodore Kreps of the
Graduate School of Business will be
the speaker at a meeting of the Ideas
of Living Group, at 4:45 o'clock to
day in the Women's Clubhouse. His
subject will be, "What Is the Basis
of Happiness?"
Tea will be served, and all inter
ested students are invited.
