# Allan Hoover Fined
## 
### 
Speeding along the Bayshore high
way cost Allan H. Hoover, son of
the depression president, $15. Sat
urday in South San Francisco he
was rambling at 58 m.p.ni., was per
ceived, apprehended, pinched by
Captains Perussima, Schmoke, and
Officer Hewitt. Yesterday an un
named attorney appeared before
Justice of the Peace S. S. F. Farrell,
three days before the case was set,
settled for speeder Hoover.
