# Cadets Bombed
## 
### 
Civil War followed closely on the
inauguration of China's latest revo
lutionary government, but as always,
was has not actually been declared.
Strife in the Fukien Province had
yesterday advanced to the stage of
air raids and a steamship blockade.
Bungling, nationalist government
bombers fired on a group of cadets
at rifle practice, killing 10 boys and
wounding 30. The remainder of the
air attack, concentrated on four
towns, resulted in the death of at
least 20 and great property damage.
