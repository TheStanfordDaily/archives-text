# Oregon State's Crew Men Use Dilapidated Boathouse
## 
### 
Crewmen of Oregon State College
suffer all kinds of inconveniences
and handicaps, with their shell
house being nothing more than an
old barn. For five years it has been
gradually deteriorating, until at
present there are no floors, lights,
or water, while the roof leaks and
one wall is missing.
With the removal of a building
fund from the college budget., Port
land business man have begun to
raise a boathouse fund.
