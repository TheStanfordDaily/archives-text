# Kloss Work to Be Displayed At Stanford Art Gallery
## 
### 
Etchings, drypoints, and aquatint
prints by Gene Kloss are on exhibit
at the Stanford Art Gallery this
month.
Critics who viewed the collection
iti the East consider it the artist's
best work to date, and prophesy as
high position in American art for
him.
The background of the prints is
the Southwest Indian country,
where Gene Kloss has established a
studio at Taos. New Mexico. Indian
dancers, pueblo scenes, and desert
landscapes are accompanied by de
scriptive comments on the interest
ing history of the subjects.
See the set any day in the week
between the hours of ten and five.
The Gallery is open to the public
without admission charge.
