# Columbia Easy Opponent For Cards, Says Warner
## 
### 
The "Old Fox" Pop Warner
stopped off at South Bend yesterday
en route to Palo Alto and reiterated
his opinion that Stanford picked an
"easy opponent" in Columbia.
The Associated Press reported that
Warner believes there are "at least
eight better clubs in the East." The
Temple University coach thinks that
Pittsburgh or Nebraska should have
been chosen.
U.S.C.'s school of dentistry plans
to establish free clinics, where chil
dren especially may have dental
work done.
