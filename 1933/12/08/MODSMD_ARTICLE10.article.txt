# Women Given Rushing Date
## 
### 
Women's rushing dates for win
ter quarter will be from January
sixth to the eleventh, Mary Rath,
Pan-Hellenic president, announced
last night. The twelfth will be a day
of silence and bidding will take
place that night.
This week of rushing will lake
the place of the regular second week
rushing that was the custom while
there were only 500 women. The
present series of teas arc taking the
place of the usual first week in win
ter quarter.
Whether the fall teas will continue
is stil! a debatable question and will
not be decided until spring quarter,
Mary Rath stated.
