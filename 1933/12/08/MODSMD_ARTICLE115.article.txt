# VACATION DAYS FOR "OLD FOX"
## 
### 

Glenn Scobey Warner, the "Old Fox," is en route to Palo Alio where lie
will spend a winter vacation, after his first year as mentor of the Temple
University Owls. Warner announced in the East that he did not consider
Columbia in a class with Stanford, and not suitable for Rose Bowl play.
Thereby Warner agrees with California's Bill Ingram, his former rival.
