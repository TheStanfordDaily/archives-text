# New York Sun Names Corbus As All-American
## 
### 
Five Western players are among
the supermen to win a place on the
mythical Ail-American team picked
by staff members of the New York
Sun. Stanford's contribution to the
lineup was Bill Corbus, guard and
ex-president of the student body,
while the rest of the team included:
End Skladany, Pittsburgh
End Larson, Minnesota
Tackle Jorgensen, St. Mary's
Tackle Wistert. Michigan
Guard Corbus, Stanford
Guard Rosenberg, U.S.C
Center Bernard, Michigan
Quarterback Warburton, U.S.C.
Halfback Buckler, Army
Halfback Feathers, Tennessee
Fullback Mikulak, Oregon
