# Oregon State Enters Ten Teams in Debate Tourney
## 
### 
Debaters of Oregon State will
form ten teams entering the annual
Linfield tournament held in Febru
ary. At least five other colleges are
expected to participate in the affair.
Six of the teams from Oregon
State will be made up of women
members. The question is, "Re
solved, that the powers of the Pres
ident of the United States should
be materially increased as a settled
policy."
