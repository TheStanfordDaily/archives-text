# Dr. Vlastos Will Speak Before Y.W.C.A. Sunday
## 
### 
Recognized as one of the Canadian
leaders in the field of' philosophy
and religion, Dr. Gregory Vlastos,
professor of philosophy at Queen's
University, Ontario, will speak be
fore the Y.W.C.A. Sunday afternoon.
He was chief speaker at the recent
Asilomar Conference.
Those who heard Dr. Vlastos at
Asilomar speak with assurance of
the contribution he will bring to the
Farm. His addresses, it is said, are
marked by a penetrating analysis of
the needs of individuals and of the
world today and by a clear and
thought-provoking description of
what religion is and what it does.
