# Engineers Hear Domonoske At Vacational Convention
## 
### 
Professor Domonoske, head of the
mechanical engineering department
of the University, discussed "Clas
sification of Engineers" before the
annual meeting of the Society for
the Promotion of Engineering Edu
cation during the Christmas vaca
tion. The meeting was held at the
University of Southern California,
and took place December 27, 28.
and 29.
D. M. Wilson of U.S.C. was named
as next year's chairman, Professor
Domonoske is to be vice-chairman,
while the nomination for secretary
treasurer was given to F. O. Rose,
Stanford, '17. The meeting for next
year is to be held at the University
of California and will also be held
in December.
