# Retires
## 
### 

E. W. Howe, famed editor
and philosopher of Atchison,
Kan., who recently announced
that he was discontinuing
publication of his celebrated
personal journal, "E. W.
Howe's Monthly." Now 81. he
said that, having never had
leisure, he now wanted it. He
is spending the winter taking
life easy in Florida.
