# Plan Hall-Row Hoop Contest
## 
### 
The transition period in intra
mural athletics is here; therefore
this week's program will be scanty.
Many events are completed, or in
the process of completion, while
Manager Marvie Kahn is preparing
to fire basketball at the Hall-Row
amateurs.
Kalm announced last night that
the basketball list will be posted to
day or tomorrow and play will begin
soon. The manager urged prospec
tive entries to organize their teams
promptly.
This week's activity will center
around the golf course, where
Delta Upsilon finalists meet a
strong Beta Theta Pi squad.
Because of the efforts of Dante
Lembi and Waldo Mundi, Sequoia
Hall eliminated the Chinese Club in
the finals of the handball division
recently.
