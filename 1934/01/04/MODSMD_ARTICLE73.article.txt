# NICHOLS NAMED DEAN
## 
### 
Dr. John Ralph Nichols, holder
of M.A. and Ph.D. degrees at Stan
ford, has been appointed dean of
the southern branch of the Univer
sity of Idaho at Pocatello. Nichols,
son of Walter H. Nichols, principal
at the Palo Alto High School, will
take up his duties February 1.
