# TODAY IS DEADLINE FOR MAILBOX RENT
## Postmaster Werry Gives New P.O. Dope 
### 
Because of an extension of time,
box rents may be paid today until
the postoffice closes at G o'clock. If
they are not paid by then, Post
master Werry said, the mail delivery
service will be discontinued and the
strips of brown paper will be pasted
on the face of the box, closing them
at once.
The schedules for the arrival and
departure of the mail are the same
as those of last year, but for the
benefit, of postoffice patrons, a copy
of this schedule may save them some
trips. The mail is distributed about
one hour after it arrives.
The arrival schedule is as follows:
at 7:30 a.m. from all points; at 9:30
a.m. from the South and Los An
geles; at 11:45 a.m. from the East
and North and South as far as Mon
terey; at 4 p.m. from the South and
Bay districts; and at 5 p.m. from
San Francisco and the Bay cities.
The outgoing mails close at 9:30
a.m., 3 p.m., 4:45 p.m., 6 p.m., and
7:45 p.m. for all points, and for the
j East and North at 3:45 p.m.
