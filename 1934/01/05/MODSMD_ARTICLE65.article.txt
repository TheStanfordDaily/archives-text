# Nevada Will Enforce Strict Rushing Rules
## 
### 
Women's rushing at the Univer
sity of Nevada consists of a two
hour session twice within a six-day
period by each house, the various
houses following an official sched
ule. This is followed by a three-day
period of "silence." A rule that so
rority women must refrain from all
contacts with high school girls is to
he enforced rigidly this year.
