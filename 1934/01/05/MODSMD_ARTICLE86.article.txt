# SOLD BY A'S IN $300,000 DEAL
## 
### 

In one of the most important sales in the history of baseball, Connie
Mack, veteran leader of the Philade.phia Athletics, sold these stars in
deals involving an estimated cash outlay of $300,000. Rube Walberg
(upper left), pitcher; Max Bishop (upper right), infielder, and Lefty
Grove (lower left) went to the Boston Red Sox and George Earnshaw
(lower right), pitcher, was sold to the Chicago White Sox. (Associated
Press Photos)
