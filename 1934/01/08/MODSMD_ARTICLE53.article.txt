# Glee Club to Meet To Plan Banquet Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Men's Glee Club
holds its first meeting of the quarter
tonight at 7:45 o'clock in the
women's old gym to plan for a ban
quet to be given soon for the ini
tiation of new members taken in
last quarter.
Bob Stoddard, manager, is anxious
to have as many as possible pres
ent because the banquet will prob
ably take place in about two weeks.
Announcement of further tryouts
will be made later on this quarter.
