# Tomorrow Deadline To Return Proofs for Quad
## 
### 
Quad proofs must be returned to
the photograph studios on the sec
ond floor of the 'Press Building
either today or tomorrow, declared
Editor A1 Sadler emphatically last
night, pointing out at the same time
that if necessary the studios will
make the choice as to which picture
will appear in the Quad.
There are still about 358 proofs
out. In addition, all photographs or
dered may be obtained on these two
days. The office will be open from
9:30-11:30 o'clock end from 12:30-
4 o'clock.
