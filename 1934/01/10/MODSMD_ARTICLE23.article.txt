# Bawl-Out Proofs Posted From 8 to 5 Tomorrow
## 
### 
Proofs for the Bawl-out, student
directory, will make their appear
ance tomorrow in front of the Ad
ministration Building. They will be
displayed from 8 to 5 o'clock and all
corrections must be reported to the
Registrar's office by the end of the
day.
Students who have not as yet
turned in their addresses arc asked
to do so at once in order to get them
in the Bawl-out,
