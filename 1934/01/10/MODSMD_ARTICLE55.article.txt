# Storey Attends Health Conventions in Chicago
## 
### 
Dr. Thomas A. Storey, head of
the hygiene department, enjoyed
more than his share of holiday con
ventioning during a busy week in
Chicago this year. The three na
tional meets in which he partici
pated were: The College Physical
Educational Society, The National
Athletic Council, and The American
Student Health Association.
Dr. Storey was recently respon
sible for the organization of the Pa
cific Coast Division of the American
Health Association. At one time he
served as president for the national
organization.
