# Alumni to Fete Wilbur At Meetings in South

### 
Dinners and luncheons given by
Stanford alumni await Dr. and Mrs.
Ray Lyman Wilbur in Los Angeles
where they will go on January 17
to attend a joint meeting of the
Stanford Men's and Women's Clubs
of that city given in the University
Club.
Dr. Wilbur and two of the trus
tees, Leland Cutler and Parmer Ful
ler, will be the guests of honor and
chief speakers at the meeting. Fol
lowing this they wili be compli
mented by the Orange County Stan
ford Clubs and will be guests of
the Long Beach Clubs.
