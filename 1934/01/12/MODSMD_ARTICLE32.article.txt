# Housing Plan Statement Withheld By Dean Yost
## 
### 
With the statement, "I do not be
lieve in talking, unless I know
something," Dean Mary Yost yes
terday refused to give her opinion
concerning the much discussed
question of sorority house expansion.
Despite the furore caused by the
Kappa's proposed annex, Dean in
timated that as yet no very definite
plans had been formulated by any
of the sororities. She, also, inferred
that even with the additions there
will be little appreciable difference
in house capacity.
