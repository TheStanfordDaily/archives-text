# Women's Glee Will Meet In Clubhouse Tomorrow
## 
### 
To interest prospective new mem
bers and to entertain older mem
bers, the Women's Glee Club will
meet for tea tomorrow afternoon on
the first floor of the Women's Club
house.
The club, since its organization
last quarter, has realized a member
ship of some 26 members and given
several performances at various Y.
W.C.A. functions.
