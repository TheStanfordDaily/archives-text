# Ray Wilbur, Jr., Elected Head Sponsor at Encina
## 
### 
To replace Red Gribben, who is
not attending school this quarter,
Ray Wilbur was elected head of
the Encina sponsors at the last
meeting of the freshman overseers.
At the same meeting Jimmy Triolo,
'35. was chosen for Gribben's place
in the Hall.
