# Women's Glee Club Tea Given This Afternoon
## 
### 
All girls who are interested in
singing are invited to attend a tea
given by the Women's Glee Club
today from 4:30-5:30 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Members are asked to come, and
bring any girls who are interested
or have had experience in singing
groups. Tryouts will be .held later.
Mrs. A. L. Trowbridge, the direc
tor of the club, who has just re
turned from a vacation, has an
nounced that the singers have en
gagements to perform before the
Stanford Mothers' Club, the San
Francisco Professional Women's
Club, and numerous high schools.
