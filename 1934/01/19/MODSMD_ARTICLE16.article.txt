# History Club Discusses Near East, Sees Rugs
## 
### 
Discussing the Near East at the
last meeting of the History Club
which met at the home of Profes
sor C. F. Brand of the History De
partment, Dr. David Harris read a
short selection suited to the oc
casion.
Following this, Professor Brand
gave the club a talk on Persian
rugs, on which he is an authority.
He briefly traced the important
contributions made by Persia to
our present civilization, and dis
played pictures, photographs, and
literature on this subject, as well
as architecture and tapestry work.
Brand also explained the signifi
cance of his valuable rug collection.
