# Frosh Formal to Follow U.S.C. Game on Feb. 16
## 
### 
Plans for the annual formal of the
Class of '37 are slowly progressing,
with the major details already
worked out. The date will be Fri
day, February 16, immediately fol
lowing the Stanford-U.S.C. basket
ball game, and the place will be the
lobby of Eneina Hall, scene of the
smart Frosh Informal last autumn.
Valentines, the chosen motif, will
decorate the spacious hall in various
and appropriate designs. A com
mittee, whose personnel has not yet
been definitely named, will be or
ganized this week to manage the
affair, including the sale of bids,
which will open Februai'y 1.
