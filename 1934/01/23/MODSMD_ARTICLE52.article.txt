# Stanford Dames to Meet Three Times This Week
## 
### 
Three meetings are scheduled for
this week of the Stanford Dames,
organization for wives of Stanford
.students. This afternoon at 2 o'clock
the sewing group will meet at the
home of Mrs. Earl Armstrong at 695
Obetlin, Palo Alco.
Tomorrow the reading group will
meet with Mrs. Llewelyn McKay at
711 Columbia. Time: 3 o'clock.

The gentlemen of the military de
partment will brush their sabers
for the Scabbard and Blade mili
tary ball, Friday, March 2.
