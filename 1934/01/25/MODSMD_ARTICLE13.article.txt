# Campus Invited to See Lamp Manufacture Film
## 
### 
Students complaining of the high
prices charged for incandescent
lamps will be given a chance to see
why these prices are high, in a
movie to be given by the Electrical
Engineering Department from 10 to
11 o'clock tomorrow morning in the
Little Theatre.
The pictures, dealing wilh the
manufacture of lamps and the var
ious complicated processes involved,
are intended for the class in illumi
nation, but as the class is small, an
open invitation is extended to all
students interested.
