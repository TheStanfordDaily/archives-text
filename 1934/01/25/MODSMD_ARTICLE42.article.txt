### 
With a blanket invitation to the
entire campus, Sigma Delta Pi,
Spanish honorary society, announces
the first open .social meet of the
year for January 30 in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Sigma Delta Pi heads advise that
lovers of Spanish cuisine will not

tured on the menu will be that
famed Castillian dish, the Arroz a
la Valen'ciana. Solo dancing, sing
ing, and group singing will consti

be built around the theme of "Span
