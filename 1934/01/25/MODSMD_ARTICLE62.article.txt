# Y.W. Drama Group To Present One-Act Comedy
## 
### 
Tryouts for a one-act comedy to
be presented by the Y.W.C.A. Drama
Group will be held this afternoon
from 1 to 3 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. All women, including
\ freshmen, are eligible to try out,
and any who are interested but can
; not come this afternoon should com
municate with Benita Calmenson at
Roble.
; Anyone interested in giving dra
j matic or humorous readings should
also appear for tryouts at this time,
bringing something to read.
