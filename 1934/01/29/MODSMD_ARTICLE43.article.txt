# Diminutive Star Adds to Laurels
## 
### 

Bryan Grant (right) of Atlanta, Ga., is congratulated by
Francis X. Shields, ranked as No. 1 amateur tennis star of
the United States, after defeating Shields in the quarter-finals
of the tournament at Miami Beach, Fla. It was the diminu
tive Grant who put Ellsworth Vines out of the national cham
pionships last fall.
