# NEWS DIGEST from The United Press
## F.R. Birthday 
### 
Birthday greetings poured into
White House by phone, wire, mail
as F.R. celebrated his fifty-second
birthday. But he was not too busy
to attend to a lot of work. Yester
day's program: dinner party with
"the gang," radio speech thanking
friends the nation over for their aid
to Warm Springs Foundation for
Crippled Children, nomination of
Hawaii's Poindexter as its governor,
signature of controversial gold bill
("most important legislation so
far"). ,
Meanwhile, all over the U.S. cities
and towns, large and small, cele
brated the day and night in a big
way. Most gave city parties for
F.R.'s Foundation; proceeds ex
pected to around a million.
