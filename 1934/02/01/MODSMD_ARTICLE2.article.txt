# Down too Fast
## 
### 
Soviet stratospherers reached a
height of 12% miles (new world's
record) feeling fine. They reached
land feeling distinctly rotten. Some
thing went askew on the way down
and the balloon was going 200 m.p.h.
when it struck.
Both scientists and instruments
were unfit for further use. Com
munist big shots voted to bury the
martyrs in ihe Kremlin wall.
