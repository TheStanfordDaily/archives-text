# Rehm Will Fill Vacancy Left in Alumni Council
## 
### 
F. R. Rehm of Los Angeles, Stan
ford '15, has been appointed to the
Stanford Alumni Council to fill the
vacancy made by the resignation of
J. W. Howell of San Francisco.
Howell, Stanford '12. is now serving
on the Board of Athletic Control.
Rehm's appointment was made at
a meeting of the Stanford Alumni
Association, held last Thursday in
San Francisco.
