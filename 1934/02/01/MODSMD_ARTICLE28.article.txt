# Y.W.C.A. Group to Hold Discussion Meet Today
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. Ideals of Living
Group will hold a meeting at 4
o'clock this afternoon in the Y
rooms on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse.
The features of the assembly will
be discussions led by Martha Lang
ston and Ada Martin on "What Is
Worth While in Life." All campus
women who are interested will be
welcome.
