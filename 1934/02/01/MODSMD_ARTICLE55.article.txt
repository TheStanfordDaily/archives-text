# STANFORD DAMES MEET
## 
### 
To hear Dr. Ernest Hilgard of the
Psychology Department .speak on
"The Psychology of Personality,"
members of the Stanford Dames
will meet this afternoon at 3 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
The talk will be followed by a
musical program and tea. Plans will
.be made for the husbands' party
I scheduled for February 9.
