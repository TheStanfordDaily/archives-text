# Whale vs. Liner
## 
### 
Whale tried to knock liner Presi
dent Taft out of occan yesterday
and took worst of the deal. Liner
ran into whale, snoozing in sea lane,
with force enough to halt it. Whale
reported badly dented. Liner feel
ing fine.
