# Entries Due Tomorrow For Mural Swim Meet
## 
### 
Entry lists for the annual intra
mural swimming meet must be
turned in to Myron Sprague or Mar
vie Kahn some time tomorrow.
Each organization to compete in
the meet which will be held next
Friday and Saturday, February 8
and 9, should list the name and ini
tial of each entrant and the events
in which each man will participate.
No individual may compete unless
affiliated with some organization.
