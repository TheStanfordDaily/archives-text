# Women Invited to Attend Art Class Meeting Today
## 
### 
All women interested in art are
: invited to attend a meeting of the
art class under the auspices of the
Y.W.C.A., which will be held today
from 5 tx> 6 o'clock on the third floor
s of the Women's Clubhouse.
This is the last time that women
i may sign up for this class in art,
which is given by Miss Matthews, a
former teacher at Castilleja and the
Palo Alto Evening High School.
Training is given in leather tooling,
| printing, and in applied design.
