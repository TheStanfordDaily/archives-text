# Women's Council Office To Be Decided Tomorrow
## 
### 
With declinations received from
Ann Archer and Miriam Kropp, the
three candidates remaining in the
race for junior member of Women's
Council are Lois Bassi, Marjorie
Buettner, and Elinor Kahn.
The election is slated for tomor
row. The polls will be open from 8
to 12 o'clock in the morning and from
1 to 2 o'clock in the afternoon at the
women's steps. All registered women
may vote.
