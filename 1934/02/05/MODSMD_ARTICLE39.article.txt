# TRUSTEES MEET
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of the
University Committee of the Board
of Trustees at 11 o'clock tomorrow
morning. The Finance Committee of
the Board will meet at 1:45 tomorrow
afternoon. The whole body of Trus
tees will meet at 1:45 o'clock Thurs
day.

keener, great numbers of students
left their professors at the University
of Paris and followed Peter Abelard,
whom they considered a great
teacher.
Yours truly,
Forrest Mills, '35
