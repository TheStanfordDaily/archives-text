# Fencer Al Snyder Wins Bay Open Foils Title
## 
### 
A1 Snyder, captain of Stanford
fencers, Friday night won the Bay
Division Championship in open
foils.
Fighting through a field of 17 en
tries, from the Olympic Club, Cali
fornia, and Stanford, he finally beat
out Ferrard Leicester, Winged "O"
man and former Pacific Coast cham
pion, in the finals. Snyder will prob
ably go South in the spring to com
pete in the Coast championship
matches.
Ed Munger and Oliver Bowen of
Stanford both won several bouts.
