## 
### 
Friday marked the sell-out of bids
for the Toyon Formal slated for Feb
ruary 9, disappointing more than 325
men who applied after the 300 bids
were sold.

cludes Julius Jacobs, Bill Cole, and

ocean motif for the formal with nau
