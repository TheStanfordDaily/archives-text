# Publicity
## 
### 
Never publicity-fighters, screen
stars of varying repute got their
names peppered throughout the
day's news yesterday. Doug Fair
banks, tlie old bounder, was named
in a British divorce action filed by
Lord Ashley against his wife, the
former Sylvia Hawkes, actress.
Emoting Miss Garbo was on the
same train en route to New York as
her director, Ruben Mamoulian.
Usual hitching rumors again circu
late.
Sandry Rambo, blonde stage ac
tress, left L.A. for Italy where she
is to meet and wed one Prince Bi.s
hnu, who will be on tho throne of
Nepal in India some dny- if three
men die ofT first.
:J| * *
But Toby Wing in Hollywood
bested them all. when she heroically

left a vine in a movie jungle scene
to pull ashore a young extra girl
whom she accidentally knocked into
a pool. Dick Arlen, clothes and all,
dived in and extricated both women.
