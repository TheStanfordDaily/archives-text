# Bach, Guilmant Will Be Featured by Organist
## 
### 
Works of Bach and Guilmant will
be featured by University Organist
Warren D. Allen in his regular semi
weekly conceit this afternoon in the
Memorial Church at 4:15 o'clock.
Two numbers by each of those com
posers will bo played, and in addi
tion one by Buxtehude.
The program:
Scherzo from ihe Fifth Sonata....
Guilmant
Air in D Major Bach
"Lord God, Now Open Wide Thy
Heaven" Bach
Fugue in C Major Buxtehude
Marche Religieuse Guilmant
The last number is based on a
theme written by Handel.
