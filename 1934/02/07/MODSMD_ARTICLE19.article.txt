# U.C. Home Science Head To Lecture on Dietetics
## 
### 
''Relation of Diet to Health" will be
discussed by Agnes Fay Morgan in
the third of the Popular Medical
Lectures offered by the University
on Friday evening. Miss Morgan is
head of the Home Science Depart
ment of the University of California.
These lectures are given on al
ternate Friday nights at Lane Hall,
on Sacramento Street near Webster,
in San Francisco, at 8 o'clock and
are open to the public.
This series is the fifty-second such
course given by the Stanford School
of Medicine.
