# "Y" Regional Secretary Will Speak at Supper
## 
### 
Miss Helen Price, regional secre
tary of the Y.W.C.A., will be the
honored guest at a supper of the
"Y" cabinet, to be held tonight at 6
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Price, who is prominent in
"Y" circles throughout the country,
has just returned from a month's
visit to a Y.W.C.A. convention in
New York. She brings with her
many suggestions and questions
which were brought up at this na
tional convention. Miss Price leaves
the campus on Thursday.
