# Work of Russian Scholar Now Showing at Library
## 
### 
The woiks of Dr. F. A. Golder
will be exhibited for two weeks in
the Stanford Libxary. Dr. Golder,
who first came to Stanford in 1921,
was a distinguished scholar of Rus
sian history, a collector of the un
paralleled Russian materials in the
Hoover War Library. He is the foun
der of the Russian research project
here, the work of which is being
carried on by Mr. H. H. Fisher.
Miss Sara Golder and Ben Golder
of Philadelphia have presented two
copies of an etching, one of the Hoo
ver War Library and one to the His
tory Department.
