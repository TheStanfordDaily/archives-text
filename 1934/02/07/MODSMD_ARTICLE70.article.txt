# Dames' Reading Group To Hear Book Reviews
## 
### 
Mrs. Joel V. Berreman will review
two books for the members of the
reading group of the Stanford Dames
this afternoon at 3 o'clock. The
bridge group will meet tomorrow at
2 o'clock at the same place.
Friday evening at 7 o'clock the
Stanford Dames' "Husband Party"
will be held at the Women's Club
house.
