# Learn About Stanford from Them
## 
### 
This place called Stanford University intisl he a wild
place. We read in tlie papers that all sorts of things are
happening here. II seems that the college hoys are raising
good old 'nell most all the time. The girls it seems been
doin' the same thing. Don't know hut according to the
papers, expect the faculty to go collegiate on us any mo
ment.
Of course, try as we will, we have been unable to find
the hell-raising women; and not enough of the men to
make things at all interesting.
It just goes to show that you can't believe what you
read in the pajwrs especially about this wild place called
Stanford University.
