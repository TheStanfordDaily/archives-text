# Fellowship Aspirants Asked to File Letters
## 
### 
Students wishing to compete for
fellowships for the 1934-35
must file their applications with
supporting letters of recommenda
tion at the Registrar's Office <3n or
before next Thursday, February 15,
according to word received from
Miss Mary C. Sweet, Secretary to
the Committee on Graduate Study.
Applications for renewal of Royall
and University fellowships must
also be filed at that time.
