# New Dirigible?
## 
### 
From Detroit, plans for construc
tion of a giant all-metal dirigible,
for use of U.S. Department of Com
merce in opening aerial trade routes
to South America, was presented to
PWA at Washington for approval.
Dirigible would be 550 feet long
and 1 11) feet in diameter, comparable
