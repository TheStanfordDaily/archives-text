TODAY S EVENTS
12:10 p.m. —International Table will
meet in the Union Alcove.
3 p.m.—Faculty Women's Club will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse.
American songs by Mrs. G. D. Bar
nett and modern American piano
music by Douglas Thompson.
4 p.m.—Mrs. Kohler will speak in
the Women's Clubhouse on "Child
Problem in San Fiancisco."
4:15 p.m.—Dr. Robert DuEois speaks
on "Pseudo Atoms" before the
Chemistry Seminar in Room 20 of
the Chemistry Building.
5 p.m.—Women's golf meeting in the
lounge room of the Women's Gym.
7:45 p.m.—Edgar Eugene Robinson.
Margaret Byrne, professor of
American History, will speak over
KPO on "Lincoln and American
ism."
