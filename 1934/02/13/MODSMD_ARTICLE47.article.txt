# Kansan Track Star Triumphs Again
## 
### 

Glenn Cunningham, sensational University of Kansas run
ner, crossing the finish line as he again outran Gene Venzke,
University of Pennsylvania star, to repeat his 1933 triumph in
the Wanamaker Mile at the annual Millrose A. C. meet in New
York. He finished 15 yards ahead of the Pennsylvaman, who
holds the indoor mile record but has never been able to win a
race against Cunningham.
