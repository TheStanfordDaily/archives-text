# Models to Show Latest Styles at Women's Show
## 
### 
Mannequins will display the very
latest styles at the Stanford Women's
Club fashion show and tea which is
planned for Tuesday afternoon, Feb
ruary 20, at Capwell's Roof Garden
Tea Room in Oakland.
At the same time, Babette, fashion
editor of the San Francisco Exami
ner, will talk to the group on spring
styles.
The club requests business and
professional women to come and
bring as many guests as possible.
Proceeds will go to the loan fund.
Reservations should be made by next
Monday with Mrs. Matthews at
GL9430.
