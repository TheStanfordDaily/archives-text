# Hoop Ducats Ready Today
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stanford-
U.S.C. hoop contest scheduled for
Friday and Saturday nights are now
available at the A.S.S.U. office for
the sum of 40 cents. Student body
cards must be presented at the time
of purchase.
Placed on sdie late yesterday after
noon, the ducats are selling fast. Stu
dents who have not yet obtained their
tickets are requested to do as soon
as possible, as a large advance seat
sale will eliminate congestion at the
door the nights of the game.
