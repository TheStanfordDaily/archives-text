# CAMPUS CALENDAR
## 
### 
TODAY'S EVENTS
Rooters tickets for the S.C. basket
ball games Friday and Saturday
are on sale at the A.S.S.U. office
for 40 cents..
3 p.m.—Stanford Dames, meeting in
the Women's Clubhouse, will hear
Professor A. B. Clark on "Art in
Italy."
4:15 p.m.—Warren D. Allen will play
organ recital in the Memorial
Chapel.
7:30 p.m.—Meeting of Walrus Club in
Women's Clubhouse. H. F. Fisher
and C. F. Penrose speak, with dis
cussion following. Topic: "Russia
and Japan Confront Each Other."
7:30 p.m.—Pi Sigma Alpha will meet
at the Women's Clubhouse. Pro
fessor Graham H. Stuart will talk
on "The Foi'eign Policy of the
Roosevelt Administration."
8 p.m.—Meeting of the American
Association of University Women
in the Women's Clubhouse. Dr.
Tully C. Knoles will speak on "In
ternational Peace."
TOMORROW S EVENTS
7:45 p.m.—Discussion the Santa
Clara County charter proposal at
the Palo Alto Community Club.
8 p.m.—Varsity basketball team
plays S.C. in the Pavilion.
9 p.m. — Frosh formal in Encina
lobby.
