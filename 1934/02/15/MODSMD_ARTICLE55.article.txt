presented a tasty line. Swimming
and golf, always popular, will attract
p large share of interest while those
who care more for the excitement of
the squared circle will see that My
ron Schall has plenty on tap with the
coming boxing meets. Polo and fenc
ing also come in for a lot of atten
tion.
In addition to these, rugby, an old
graybeard in its second childhood
and exhibiting all the tendencies of
a bouncing baby boy, will get its
share of the limelight with several
possible trips in the offing.

ball squad closes its schedule shortly
with games against U.S.C. and Cali
fornia scheduled for the ensuing two
week-ends.

full course dinner is ready to come
off the stove. Eat hearty.
