# Soph Carnival Committee Discusses Plans Tonight
## 
### 
The committee on arrangements
for the sophomore carnival, to be
held March 10, will meet tonight at
7:15 o'clock in the basement of the
English Building. Among other plans
to be discussed, final selection of the
orchestra that is to play at the affair
will be made.
Bill Drew will be in charge of the
carnival, which promises to be one of
the biggest of the less expensive so
cial affairs of the year.
