# FROSR DECORATE HALL FOR DANCE
## 
### 
The transformation of Encina
Hall's fashionable living room into a
shadowy, smart ballroom for the
long-anticipated spring frosh formal
of tomorrow night continued through
yesterday afternoon, will be well fin
ished by tonight with a crew of en
thusiastic frosh and experienced in
terior decorators working the major
part of today.
According to news received from
the A.S.S.U. offices in the Union late
yesterday, a limited number of bids
were available at the close of the
third day of general campus sale.
Aside from the capacious, plush
lounge and sparkling, soft-lighted
fountain to be placed in the lobby,
I major scenery piece to be erected is
' a crimson, heart-shaped stage on the
orchestra platform for A1 Rushton's
tenors and trio, to be lighted by
: spots and provided with a public
address system. Rex Hardy of the
dance committee and Decorator Mile
Talley are responsible for the deco
rations.
