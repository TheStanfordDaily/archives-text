# Change in Departure Of Last Mails Announced
## 
### 
A recent change in the departure
of the mails is announced by Super
intendent Wheeler of the Stanford
post office. From now on mail must
be deposited in the University post
office by 5:15 p.m. in order to make
the last train out.
Likewise, airmail letters must be
posted by 5:45 p.m. in order to leave
Mills Field at 11 o'clock the same
evening, otherwise they will be ob
liged to wait until the following
morning.
