# Bronson and Morris Win In Branner Club Revote
## 
### 
Branner Club elections were con
cluded late last night with Phillips
Bronson and Robert Morris taking
the disputed positions of junior and
sophomore representatives in the re
vote.
Elected on the first ballot were:
Ned Baker, president; E. Perko, vice
president, Lloyd Silverstein, secre
tary, and Kollin Woodbury, senior
representative.
