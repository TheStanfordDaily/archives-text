# Margaret Ball Talks To German Honor Society
## 
### 
"Carnival Days in Germany" were
discussed at today's meeting of the
German Honor Society by Dr. M.
Margaret Ball, research assistant in
the Political Science Department,
who received the degree of Doctor
of Laws last summer, following a
year's study in Germany. Also fea
tured during the afternoon were
Valentine games, requiring the use
of the German tongue. An an
nouncement was made this afternoon
concerning the society's next meet
ing, March 7.
