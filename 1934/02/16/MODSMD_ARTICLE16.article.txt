# M'CRACKEN SENTENCED
## 
### 

William P. MacCracken, left, cited by a Senate investigation committee
in connection with the airmail fraud inquiry, was yesterday sentenced
to 10 days in jail for contempt. Also sentenced with MacCracken was his
close friend L. H. Brittin. MacCracken is shown with his attorney, Frank
J. Hogan, and Senate Sergeant-at-Arms Chesley Jurney.
