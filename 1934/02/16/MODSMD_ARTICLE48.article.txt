# Board Awaits Return Of Helvenston to Pick Play
## 
### 
As soon as Director Harold Hel
venston returns from the convention
of the National Theatre Conference
in Seattle, the Dramatic Council will
convene to discuss the Dramatic
Council play for next quarter, ac
cording to Ben Cave, dramatic man
ager.
It is not definitely known what
time Mr. Helvenston will be back,
but he is expected early next week.
