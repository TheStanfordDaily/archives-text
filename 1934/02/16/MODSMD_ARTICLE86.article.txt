# Contralto Will Sing At Sunday Concert
## 
### 
The regular Sunday afternoon or
gan concert, scheduled for 4 o'clock
in the Memorial Church, will in
clude several vocal selections by Miss
Maurine Thompson, contralto, teach
er of singing at San Jose State. In
cluded in the list are two numbers
from the Bach cantata, "God's Time
Is the Best."
Solor are "Arioso" and "Into Thy
Hands," from the cantata, and two
modern songs, "The Heart Worships"
by Hoist, and "Ewig," by Eric Wolff.
