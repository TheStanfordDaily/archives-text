# "IT'S A FRAME-UP"
## 
### 
ROSEYILLE, Calif.. Feb. 15 (UP).
—A $50,000 suit filed against Max
Baer, heavyweight title contender,
in New York today was termed a
"frame-up" by Ancil Hoffman, man
ager of the boxer. Baer is charged
by Shirley La Belle as the father of
her unborn child.
