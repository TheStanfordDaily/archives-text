# Jacobs, Clancy Promoted By New Editorial Board
## 
### 
The Editorial Board of Volume 85
of the Daily held its first meeting
yesterday. Only matters considered
by the new body were the advance
ments of Julius Jacobs to alternate
night editor, and Charles Clancy to
United Press copy editor.

"Gold Pepper," junior and senior
women's honorary association at
Purdue, corresponds to Stanford's
"Cap and Gown." i
