# Wildcats to Arrive For Indian Series Thursday
## 
### 
Journeying up from Tucson, a
quartet of hard-riding Westerners
will arrive on the campus Thursday
morning to represent the University
of Arizona in the Wildcats' iwo
game polo series with Stanford this
week. The games will be played on
Thursday and Saturday afternoons.
The same lineup that saw action
against Stanford last Thanksgiving
is expected to perform again this
week. It will consist of Neilson
Brown at number one, Captain Bill
Clark at two, Jack Budlong at three,
and Warren Hargrave at back.

The average age of the Hollywood
actresses is below 25 years, accord
ing to a survey.
