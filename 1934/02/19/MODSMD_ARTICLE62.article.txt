# Students Eligible For Community Play Parts
## 
### 
Tryouts for the 24 men's roles in
"Secret Service," William Gillette's
famous drama of the Southern Con
federacy, to be staged by the Palo
Alto Community Players March 22,
23, and 24, will be held Tuesday eve
ning, February 20, at 8 o'clock in the
rehearsal hall of the new wing of the
Civic Theatre on Middlefield Road.
Stanford students interested are eli
gible to try out.
