# ARMY FLYERS CARRY THE MAIL
## 
### 

Army flyers at midnight stood ready to hop off on the first flight under the new Federal air mail policy that
last week rang the death knell for private aviation companies. The photo, left, shows flyers at March Field load
ing personal baggage for transportation to the Salt Lake City air base. At right, pilots are dismantling machine
guns to make way for mail bags.
