# War Library Will Show Exhibits on Albert I
## 
### 
Honoring the late King Albert of
Belgium, the Hoover War Library
will place an exhibition of books and
pictures in the exhibition case on'
the first floor of the Library, begin
ning today.
In the exhibition are pictures of
the royal family, a reproduction of
an autographed copy of the King's
photograph which was presented to
Herbert Hoover, a reproduction of
the famous painting of King Albert
in the trenches by Maurice Romberg,
and a map indicating the place of
the King's death.
A copy of "King Albert's Book,"
was sold during the war for the Bel
gian Relief Fund, will also be shown.
