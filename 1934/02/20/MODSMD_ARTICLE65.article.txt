# BATTLE MANEUVERS
##

U.S. fleet's battle force and scouting
force steamed seaward today from
San Pedro and San Diego toward a
secret rendezvous where three-day
tactical exercises of the fleet, under
Adin. David F. Sellers, will be con
ducted.
