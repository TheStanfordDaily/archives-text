# UNUSUAL ACCIDENT
## 
### 
ROCHESTER, N.Y., Feb. 1!) (UP).
—An airplane motorcycle accident,
believed to be the first in the history
of aviation, resulted in the death of
the cyclist. Samuel Blondi crashed
his cycle info a wing of an airplane
landing on the ice of the frozen Iron
dequoit bay near here.

Bashful students of McGill Uni
versity will be aided in getting dates
by a special committee which will
use scientific matching of different
personalities.
