# Y.W. Plans Afternoon Picnic-Hike in Foothills
## 
### 
Plans are being formulated for a
Y.W.C.A. Thursday or Friday after
noon hike to be followed by a weenie
roast in the near-by hills. All who
are interested may get details from
Ruth Neupert, Roble, or call the
Y.W.C.A. rooms, 4301. Hours for
the hike will be 4 to 6:30 o'clock.
While President, Washington ac
cepted no salary, but only for ex
penses that were necessary to the
discharge of his duties.
