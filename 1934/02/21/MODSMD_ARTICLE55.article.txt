# CO-ED POLOIST AT U.S.C.
## 
### 

Pretty Marjorie Brock, IT. S. C. coed, has the honor of captaining one
of the first women's collegiate polo squads in the United States. Re
cently enterprising sorority girls of the school invaded the realm of
polo—until now sanctuary of men—organizing a crack lour to compete
with other feminine squads.
