# SALLY AND FAN
## 
### 
OMAHA, Neb., Feb. 22 (UP).—
Sally Rand and her famous fan will
go on exhibition at a local theatre to
morrow, but Catholics of the Omaha
diocese are under injunction of the
church to remain away from the
spectacle.
