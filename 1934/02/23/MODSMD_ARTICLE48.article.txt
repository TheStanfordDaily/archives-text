# OLD DEBT PAID
##

COLORADO SPRINGS, Colo., Feb.
22 (UP). —Henry Eadle came back
to Colorado Springs today to cele
brate Washington's birthday by pay
ing back 80 cents he had taken from
an employer seven years ago when
it lad of 18.
