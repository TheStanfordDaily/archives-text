# New Solo Flight Record Reached by Stanfordite
## 
### 
Breaking all recent records, W. F.
Erskine, Stanford student studying
flying at the Associated Air Services
field in Palo Alto, soloed Wednesday
after but two hours and 25 minutes
of instruction.
Field officials said no one had
come anywhere near Erskine's mark
in three years, all recent records be
ing at least an hour over his time.
The average student solos in be
tween eight and ten hours of dual
instruction.
After completion of his course un
der Lieut. John Preston, former
Stanford end and school instructor,
Erskine plans to take up commer
cial aviation in Alaska.
