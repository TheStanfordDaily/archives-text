# The 						Time Changes

lion may he deeided and passed on lo I Ik* staff on the same
day, the Kditorial Hoard heneeforlh will meet before Hie
staff instead of alter. Starting today, the Kdit Hoard will
convene in the Dmi.y office at 1 o'clock sharp. Staff meet
ing will follow at 1:20. I nexcused absence rules will be
enforced from now on. Drop around.
