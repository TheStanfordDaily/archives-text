# "LITTLE NAPOLEON" STRUCK OUT
## 
### 

The sport world today mourned the death of John Joseph McGraw,
"Little Napoleon" of the Big Leagues, who died Sunday in New York. Mc-
Graw died from uremic poisoning. Believed to he on the road to recovery
after a 10-day seige of poisoning, he sank rapidly into a coma and died
without regaining consciousness.
