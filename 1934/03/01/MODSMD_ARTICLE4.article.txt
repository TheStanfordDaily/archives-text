# NEWS BRIEFS FROM THE UNITED PRESS
## 
### 
MAIIYSVILLE, Feb. 28 (UP).—
(Late dispatch)—Gov. James Rolph,
Jr., is confined to his hotel room in
Marysville, California, with slight
congestion of the lungs and stomach
disorder, resulting from the fatigue
of a speaking tour in the rain and
some poisoned mushrooms which he
ate.
