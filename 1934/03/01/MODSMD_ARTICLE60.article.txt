# Soviet Pays Tribute to Ill-Fated Stratosphere Heroes
## 
### 

Leaders of Soviet Russia —Dictator Josef Stalin (center), War Commissar Voroshilov (left)
and Chairman of the Council Molotov—carrying the ashes in the impressive funeral procession
in Moscow for the three daring airmen recently killed in a record-breaking flight into the
stratosphere. The ashes were buried in the Kremlin wall.
