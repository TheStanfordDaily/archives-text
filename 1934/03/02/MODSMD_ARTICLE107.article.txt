# Women's Fabric Purses Show Peasant Influence
## 
### 
Gay fabric bags show the vivid
influence of Russia and Mexico,
many of them being made of flow
ered peasant shawls. They are usu
ally envelope shaped and unorna
mented. Suede, linen, and shantung
are the most popular materials.
One attractive model is of soft
suede painted in bright Mexican
colors.
These novelty colored bags are
vei-y smart with white summer
dresses as well as with dark spring
frocks, and several should be in
cluded in every up-to-date ward
robe.
