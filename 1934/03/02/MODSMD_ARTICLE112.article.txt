# UNUSUAL SLEEVES
##

have become more normal with re
spect to size, but are of new and un
usual lengths. The simple straight
sleeve cut off a few inches above the
elbow is popular, and in place of the
yards of puffs, slightly draped or
gathered sleeves arc used.
