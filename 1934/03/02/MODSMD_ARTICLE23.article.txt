# Dance Studio Is Scene Of Military Ball Tonight
## 
### 
To the strains of Vera Vincent";
Night Club Ambassadors 250 couple.'
will dance at the Military Ball from
9 to 1 o'clock tonight in the blossom
decorated dance studio of the Wom
en's Gym. There will be two pianc
ducts and a trio to entertain the mu
sically inclined.
The affair is formal but the dark
tuxedos of the civilians will be set
off by the smart uniforms of the
local cadets.
Late yesterday afternoon there
were no more bids available at the
A.S.S.U. office, but some may be
turned in at the last minute, so those
anxious to go may apply there. Bids
are $3.
