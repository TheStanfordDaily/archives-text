# Sequoia Beats El Campo Basketball Team, 36-22
## 
### 
In a torrid practice scrimmage late
Wednesday night in the gymnasium
the Sequoia I intramural basketball
team defeated El Campo, 36 to 32.
The game played in the absence of a
regular league encounter, and ref
ereed by Sammy Lee, was featured
by Campo's fast breaking offense,
and the effective defensive work of
Seaman, Sequoia guard. Lerch led
Campo's scorers with 14. while Bar
bash tallied 16 for Sequoia.
