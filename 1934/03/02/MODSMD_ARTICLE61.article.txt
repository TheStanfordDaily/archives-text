# Frosh Golfers Will Meet St. Ignatius High School
## 
### 
Stanford freshman golfers will
open their competitive season tomor
row against St. Ignatius High School
on the Stanford golf course.
The match, which is the first of
the season for either freshmen or
varsity, will probably see the entire
team in action. Kuklinski, Berkey,
and Henderson are certain starters.
