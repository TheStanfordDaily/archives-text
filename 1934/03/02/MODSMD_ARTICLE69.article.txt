# Quad Installments May Be Mailed to Box 100
## 
### 
Quad second installments may be
mailed to Box 100, and not to Box
110 as announced yesterday. These
payments, which are now due, may
also be made in the Quad business
office on the second floor of the
Press Building.
Manager Hal Whitfield emphasizes
the necessity for prompt payment.
The general installment price is
$2.50, and the senior price is $3.
