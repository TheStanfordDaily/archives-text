# Room Deposits for Next Quarter Due Thursday
## 
### 
"Pay your money end take your
choice" will be "Pay your money
and take your chance" at the Toyon
and Branner offices this Thursday,
March 8, when room drawings will
be held at 5:15 o'clock.
The $5 deposit required by Toyon,
Branner, Sequoia, and the Old Union
must be paid at the office of the
Dean of Men before Thursday to in
sure a place in the hails next quar
ter.
