# Electrical Engineers To Hear Talk on Elevators
## 
### 
The American Institute of Elec
trical Engineers will meet in Room
28" of the Engineering Building at
7:30 o'clock tomorrow evening. Mr.
C. O. Whit lon, engineer with the
Otis Elevator Company, will give an
illustrated lecture on the subject:
"Electrical Elevator Equipment."
Refreshments will be served.
