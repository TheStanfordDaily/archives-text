# Quad Men Will Collect Second Payments Today
## 
### 
Second installment Quad payments
may be made from 8 to 12 o'clock
this morning to Quad representatives
on the Engineering corner. If this
is not convenient, checks may be
mailed to Box IDO, Stanford.
The second installment payment
due is $2.50, while $3 is to be col
lected from seniors.
There arc still Quads available at
$5.65. Manager Hal Whitfield points
out that last year's book was a sell
out, and stresses the advisability of
getting a copy of the 1934 Quad be
fore it reaches that state.
