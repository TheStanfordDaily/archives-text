# Rummage Sale Profit To Aid Convalescent Home
## 
### 
For the benefit of the Stanford
Convalescent Home, the semi-annual
rummage sale will be held on March
14 and 15 at 640 Emerson Street in
Palo Alto. Mrs. George B. Culver
is chairman of the arrangement com
mittee.
The sale, which has been held each
spring and fall for 12 years, will be
open from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. on both
Wednesday and Thursday. A large
number of books and furniture suit
able for students will be offered for
sale.
