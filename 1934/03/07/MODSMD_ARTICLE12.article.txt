# DILLINGER SUSPECTED
## 
### 
CHICAGO, March 6 (UP). —Six
bandits led by a man resembling
John Dillinger raided a bank in,
Sioux Falls, S.D., while extraordi
nary precautions were taken here to
prevent similar crimes by the same
outlaw.
