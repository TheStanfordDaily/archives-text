# ELEVEN LOST
## 
### 
BUCHAREST, Roumania, March 6 ,
(UP). — Eleven persons were re
ported lost when the Italian tanker
Santoni, from Genoa, split in half on
the shoals of Constanta, in the Black
Sea.
