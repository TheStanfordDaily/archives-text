# Room Drawings to Be Held This Afternoon
## 
### 
Room drawings for Toyon and
Branner Halls will be held at the
Toyon and Branner offices at 5:15
o'clock this afternoon.
Men now living in Encina Hall
who wish to reserve their rooms for
spring quarter .should call at the
Dean of Men's office Monday, Tues
day, or Wednesday, March 12, 14, or
14, and pay deposits.
