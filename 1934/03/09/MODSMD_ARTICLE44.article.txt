# English Head to Make Mediterranean Cruise
## 
### 
Dr. W. D. Briggs, executive head
of the English School, and his wife
are leaving March 24 for a several
month's cruise in the Mediterranean.
Having leave of absence during
the whole of the spring quarter, the :
Briggs expect to visit Florence and
other southern ports during their
cruise.
Dr. Arthur Kennedy will act as j
head of the English department dur
ing Dr. Briggs' absence.
