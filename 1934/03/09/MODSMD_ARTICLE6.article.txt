# RUSSIA SEEKS GOODS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, March 8 (UP).—
The Soviet Union is making inquiry
in the U.S. for 500,000 bales of cot
ton, 100,000 tons of copper, and quan
tities of railroad equipment and
heavy machinery to be financed
through the Export-Import Bank of
Washington, Jesse H. Jones, chair
man of the R.F.C., said.
