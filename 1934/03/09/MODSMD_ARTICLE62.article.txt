# Scholarship Applications, Renewals Due in Spring
## 
### 
Since scholarship applications are
not carried over from one year to |
another all holders of scholarships,
as well as candidates for new ones,
should file application blanks with
the scholarship secretary in the Reg
istrar's office before the following
dates: graduates, April 4; under
graduates (old students), Ajiril 15; ,
undergraduates (new students),
June 25.
