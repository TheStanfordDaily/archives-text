# DEMO HEAD MAY QUIT
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, March 8 (UP).—
Political heads are dropping into the
basket so rapidly that there are those
who believe Postmaster - General,
Farley will surrender his Democratic
National Committee Chairmanship
soon. Both he and F.R. arc on record
as opposed to one man's holding both
positions.

WASHINGTON. March 8 (UP).—
The Senate late today adopted an
amendment making peanuts a basic
commodity under the AAA.
