# BETROTHAL ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
Announcement has been made of
the engagement of Miss Idamyrtle
(Teddy) Klepinger, '33, and Dana
Gerald Bing, '31. Miss Klepinger is
a Chi Omega, while her fiance, a
Bakersfield man, belongs to Phi
Sigma Kappa.

A London naturalist reports the
discovery of a finsh that winks.
