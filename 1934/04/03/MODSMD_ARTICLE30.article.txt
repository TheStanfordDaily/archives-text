### PA. MINERS RIOT 
PA. MINERS RIOT

UNIONTOWN, Pa., April 2 (AP).
—Shots rang out at a huge meeting
today of miners being addressed by
Mrs. Governor Clifford Pinchot. In
the evening 1,500 miners were rioting
hi the down-town area.
