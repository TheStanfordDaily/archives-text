# Prof. Brown Sails For Six-Month World Cruise
## 
### 
Sailing on a round - the - world
cruise, Dr. and Mrs. Walter Brown,
professor in the hygiene and physical
education department, and interna
tionally known worker in the field
of public health, left San Francisco
March 22, planning to return Sep
tember 15.
After short stops in the Hawaiian
Islands and the Orient, they will sail
through the Suez Canal to Naples
where they will leave the boat. They
will tour Europe by motor with ar
rangements including a four-day
stop in Geneva where Dr. Brown
will attend sessions of the health
section of the League of Nations.
In Bristol, England, the Stanford
man will represent the United States
at a gathering of ihe Royal Sanitary
Institute, July 9 to 14.
