# Hoover Granddaughter Celebrates Birthday
## 
### 
Peggy Ann Hoover, granddaugh
ter of Mr, und Mrs. Herbert Hoover,
came up to her grandparents' home
here to celebrate her eighth birthday,
March 17. With their governess,
Peggy Ann and her younger sister,
Joan, made the trip from Sierra
Madre, preceding their mother, Mrs.
Herbert Hoover, Jr., and brother,
Herbert 111.
A half-dozen friends gathered at
the home on San Juan fiill to watch
Peggy Ann blow out the candles and
to celebrate with her.
