# THOMAS LEADS JOBLESS IN PROTEST MARCH
## 
### 

Led by Norman Thomas, progressive editor, ex-clergyman, and Socialist candidate for President in 1932
(carrying brief case) unemployed from several large Eastern cities staged a inarch of protest in Washington
against the discontinuance of CWA work projects. La st October Thomas was an Assembly speaker on the
Stanford Campus where he spoke on the trend and influence of new social trends.
