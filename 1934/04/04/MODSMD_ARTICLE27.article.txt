# Park Authority to Talk On Wild Life Thursday
## 
### 
i
Joseph Dixon of the National Park
Service, Wild Life Division, will give
an illustrated talk on wild life of
Mr. McKinley National Park at the
regular monthly meeting of the Coo
per Ornithological Club, in Room
430, Thursday evening, April 5, at
8 o'clock. The public is invited to
attend.
