# Men Should Pay Board Today to Avoid Fines
## 
### 
It will behoove all men students
in Encina, Toyon, and Branner to
pay their room rent today if they
wish to avoid a fine of 50 cents per
day which will accrue thereafter.
Men boarding at Encina will auto
matically be fined for paying their
board late since they will receive
no refund for meals missed.
Room rent for Women living at
Ruble and the Union was due yes
terday, and a fine will be levied on
all those who pay late.
