# Socialist Head Presents Plan for Negroes Tonight
## 
### 
A talk on "A Plan for Negro
America" will be given tonight at
8 o'clock by Frank Crosswaith, New
York editor and labor organizer.
The talk will bo given in the Native
Sens' Hall, 207 Hamilton Avenue,
under the auspices of the Socialist
Party.
Crosswaith is a graduate of Rams
head, a school of social sciences in
New York, and is a professor there
now. He is now on a tour of the
country, which brings him here to
night.
