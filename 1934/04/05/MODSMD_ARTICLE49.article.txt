# Prof. Rogers Collects Minerals on Vacation
## 
### 
Austin F. Rogers, of
mineralogy, spent Easter vacation on
a trip to Riverside, Searles Lake,
and Death Valley, returning -with
specimens of minerals.
Accompanying Professor Rogers
was M. Vonsen, a mineralogist from
Petahima. At Searles Lake the ex
pedition was joined by William E.
Burke, former instructor in chem
istry at Stanford, who is now gen
eral manager for the American Pot
ash and Chemical Corporation. His
brother, Harry E. Burke, is engaged
in reasearch in minerals at Stanford,
for the government.
