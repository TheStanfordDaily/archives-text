# Kathleen Norris Talks To Colored Women's Clubs
## 
### 
At the reciprocity meeting of the
State Federation of Colored Wom
en's Clubs, held at the Community
House at 1:30 o'clock yesterday,
Kathleen Norris talked to the mem
bers of the convention, which in
cluded at least a hundred delegates
from other parts of the state.
The convention included also an
executive meeting at 9:30 o'clock in
the morning, reports from commit
tees, and a luncheon served at noon.
