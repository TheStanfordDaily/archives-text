# Plans Being Made For Convalescent Home Drive
## 
### 
With the appointment of Ira "Red"
Cross to the chairmanship of Conva
lescent Home Drive Committee, yes
terday Student Body President Jerry
Trautman officially started Stan
lord's annual spring housecleaning
and charity.
Plans are now being formulated
for the drive by Chairman Cross,
who announces his committee as
Nortel Baker. Barbara Steinbeck.
Marvie Kahn. Ben Cave, and Frank
lin Curtis.
First meeting of the committee
will be held in the Committee Room
of the Old Union at one o'clock to
day.
