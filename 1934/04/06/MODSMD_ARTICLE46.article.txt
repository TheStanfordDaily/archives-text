# Housing of Tramps To Be Discussed at Meeting
## 
### 
Housing of itinerants and care of
Palo Alto's needy school children
will be discussed at the town meet
ing tonight at 7:45 o'clock in the
Palo Alto Community House. The
discussion is open to the public.
Speakers will be Manager C. W.
Maupin of the Palo Alto Shelter,
and his assistant, Paul Kern, editor
of the newspaper published by the
men at the Shelter.
