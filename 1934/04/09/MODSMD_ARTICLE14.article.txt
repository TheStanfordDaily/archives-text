# KILLER'S CAR FOUND
## 
### 
OTTAWA, Kan., April 8 (/P).—'The
motor car from which Clyde Barrow
is accused of spreading death was
I found abandoned on a farm near
| here today. Barrow is wanted for
' the slaying of at least seven men.
including a constable and two high
way patrolmen.
