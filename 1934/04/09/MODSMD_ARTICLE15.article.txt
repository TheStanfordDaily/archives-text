# JAIL CHURCH RIOTERS
## 
### 
BOSTON. April 8 (&). — Each
screaming at the other accusations
of the murder of Archbishop Leon
Tourain in New York last Decem
ber, two rival factions of the Arme
nian Church met in the streets and
fought with knives and ice picks.
Nine badly-cut combatants were ar
rested.
