# Women Are Invited To Meeting of Art Guild
## 
### 
All girls interested in art are
urged to attend the first meeting of
the quarter of th;_' Arts and Crafts
Guild today from 4:30 to (5 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse.
This club, which is under the aus
pices of the Y.W.C.A., is open to all
Stanford women. Block printing,
leather tooling, and metal work are
taught.
Mrs. Harold Matthews, former art
teacher at Castilleja and night teach
er at Palo Alto High, will instruct
the group. Thi> fee for the entire
quarter of instruction will be $1.
payable during the first meeting.
