# NEWS BRIEFS
## DILLINGER TO YUMA? 
### 
ST. PAUL, Minn., April 9 (/P).—
Hunters of John Dillinger found
his elusive trail, tho' it was cold.
Abandoning a vigil at a Mankato,
Minn., garage, officers disclosed dis
covery of the auto used in his most
recent getaway. Dillinger was be
lieved headed for Yuma, Ariz.
