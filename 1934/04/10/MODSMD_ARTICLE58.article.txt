# NATION'S BIGGEST PASSENGER PLANE TRIES WINGS
## 
### 

The giant flying boat S-42, built by Sikorsky for Pan-American Airways, Is shown as it proved itself
airworthy on a test flight over Bridgeport, Conn. The plane, powered by four engines snd capable of
flying 2,500 miles non-stop with a mail load and crew, was planned as America's largest passenger plane.
It is 76 feet long, has a wing spread of 114 feet, and has a gross weight of 38,000 pounds. It will be fitted
with 32 passenger seats for the South American service. (Associated Press Photo)
