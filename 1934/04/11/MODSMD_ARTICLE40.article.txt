# Brand Dismisses Classes To Welcome Second Son
## 
### 
Monday morning at 7:52 o'clock
was born to Dr. and Mrs. Carl Fre
mont Brand at Palo Alto Hospital
on Stanford University campus a
7.4-pound son, who is to be chris
tened Robert. Both mother and
child are doing splendidly.
This is the second boy of Dr. and
Mrs. Brand. Professor Brand teaches
English history in the History De
partment, and in view of the event
which transpired Monday, declared
a holiday in his history classes.
