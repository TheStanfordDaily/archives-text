# Mural Baseball Teams Must Sign Before Friday
## 
### 
Organizations wishing to enter
teams in intramural baseball will
sign up for either (not both) hard
or soft ball on the intramural bulle
tin bulletin board in Encina gym by
Friday, April 13; horseshoe pitching
begins April 19.
Those interested in refereeing in
tramural baseball must leave their
names at the intramural desk as soon
as possible.
