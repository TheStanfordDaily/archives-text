# Chi Omega Holds Annual Eleusinian at Berkeley
## 
### 
Celebrating the 39th anniversary
of the founding of Chi Omega, chap
ters Nu Alpha of Stanford and Mu
of California held their annual Eleu
sinian in Berkeley last Saturday. In
cluding alumnae, active members,
and pledges, the assembly started
with a luncheon at 12:30 o'clock at
the chapter house in Berkeley.
The feature, of the meeting was
the round-table discussion, where
the various problems of the sorority
on the campus were examined. Bar
bara Binns acted as chairman of the
discussion.
A 7 p.m. a banquet was held at
the Berkeley Women's City Club,
Dr. Bertha Stuart Dyment being
guest speaker from Stanford.
