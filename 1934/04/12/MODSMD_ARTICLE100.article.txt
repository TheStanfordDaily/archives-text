# BAER GOES EAST
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO. April 11 (A*).—
Max Baer is en route East today
to begin serious training at Altantic
City or some place near New York
for his fistic battle with Primo Ca
nera June 14 for the heavyweight
championship.

WASHINGTON, April 11 </}>).—
Representative Sirovich (D., N.Y.)
told the House Merchant Marine
Committee that plans have been
completed by Japan for capture of
the Aleutian islands off Alaska in
case of war between that country
and the United States.
