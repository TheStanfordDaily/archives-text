# Stanford Dames to Play Bridge This Afternoon
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames Bridge Group
will meet this afternoon at 2 o'clock
in the Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
Thursday afternoon, April 19, the
Stanford Dames are being enter
tained at the Allied Arts at a tea
given by their sponsors, Mrs. W. M.
Proctor, Mrs. George W. Dowrie,
Mrs. Ward B. Kindy, Mrs. P. A.
Leighton, and Mrs. C. G. Wrenn. Re
servations can be made with Mrs.
Leighton at P.A. 3260 or Mrs. Wil
liam G. Hoover, 'P.A. 8871, before
Tuesday, April 17.
