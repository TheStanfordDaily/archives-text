# Dean Yost Leaves Sunday For Meeting at Vassar
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will leave next
Sunday night for Poughkeepsie, New
York, where she will attend the local
meeting of the American Association
of University Women at Vassar Col
lege, her alma mater.
First vice-president of the organi
zation, she will attend a national
board meeting at Washington, D.C.,
April 26, 27, and 28, and then return
to the campus early in May.
