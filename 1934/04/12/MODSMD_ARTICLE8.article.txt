## 
###

in installing the Stanford Axe
in the Cellar. A.S.S.U. officials
announced yesterday that the
final placing of the Axe in its
new home will be this morning.
As in previous delays, the
postponement of installation is
due to difficulties in cementing
the steel bar attached to the
Axe in the floor of the Cellar.
The corporation yard has re
signed direction of this opera
tion to a representative of the
San Francisco jewelry company
that made the case. He is sched
