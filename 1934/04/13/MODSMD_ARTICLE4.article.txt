# Monday Deadline For Changes in Study List
## 
### 
Monday is the last day on which
students may file changes of study
list for the current quarter at the
Registrar's office without fine. After
this date all program revisions must
be made by petition to the Registra
tion Committee, subject to the fol
lowing fines: $2 for each course add
ed or dropped, or $3 where one
course is substituted for another.
