# Montague, Orr Named Night Editors of Daily
## 
### 
Edwine Montague, Julius Jacobs,
and Frank Orr were promoted to
Daily night editors last night by the
Editorial Board.
Annalee Whitmore, Ann Van
Wormer, and Maxine Bartlett were
named copy editors on the staff, and
Millard Browne was appointed As
sociated Press copy editor.
