# AS ONE CHAMPION TO ANOTHER
## 
### 

Jack Medica cf the University of Washington, who breaks swim
ming records with apparent abandon, displays medals he won at the
National A. A. U. swimming meet at Columbus, O. Dick Degener of
the University of Michigan, who as A. A. U. diving champion is no
small fry in the swimming world himself, admires them with a gesture
of envy. (Associated Press Photo)
