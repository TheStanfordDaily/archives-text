# Indian-U.C. Track Meet Tickets on Sale Today
## 
### 
With this year's Stanford-Califor
nia track meet destined to go down
as one of the most spectacular in the
history of the sport, B.A.C. officials
announced that tickets for the an
nual affair will go on sale in the
A.S.S.U. offices today and remain
open to student purchase for most
of the week.
As part of the Alumni Homecom
ing celebration, the Big Meet will be
held in the stadium Saturday after
noon; the javelin will open proceed
ings at 1:30 o'clock, with the mile
run following at 1:45 o'clock.
