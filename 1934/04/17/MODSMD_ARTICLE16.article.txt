# Forum Leaders Travel South for Conference
## 
### 
Three Stanford Forumites, Maxine
Yaple, Abe MellinkofT, and Hal von
Breton, journeyed South over the
week-end to view S.C-.'s new Public
Forum.
Beginning Saturday morning at
ihe crack of dawn they attended
round tables until noon, went to
luncheon for the Argentine Consul
at Ea Golondrina, Los Angeles local
color restaurant, participated in the
Forum during the afternoon.
MellinkofT on the platform and
Yaple and von Breton from the floor,
took an active part in the meeting.
