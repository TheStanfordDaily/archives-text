# TWO BREAK JAIL
## 
### 
CANON CITY. Colo.. April Hi (A>).
—Five long-term convicts in the
Colorado penitentiary here made a
desperate bid for freedom this after
noon, using an overseer for a shield.
Two made good their escape.
