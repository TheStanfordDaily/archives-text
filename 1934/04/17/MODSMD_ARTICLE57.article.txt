# MARY MILES MINTER IN DUAL SUIT
## 
### 

Mary Miles Minter, star of the silent films, and known in private
life as Juliet Shelby is shown in the Los Angeles prosecutor's office
where she sought a judgment against Hilda Desey. At same time
Miss Desey sought a disturbance of the peace complaint against the
former actress. Left to right: Miss Minter, Deputy City Attorney Don
ald M. Redwine and Miss Desey. (Associated Press Photo)
