# Essay Competition Offers $1,000 Biennial Award
## 
### 
Aspiring writers of the University
have an opportunity to turn their
written words into gold with the in
auguration of a new essay competi
tion by the United Daughters of the
Confederacy, which is open to all
college students.
A prize of $1,000 is being offered
biennially for an unpublished essay
of high merit in the field of South
ern history. Ten thousand words is
the limit set.
Those interested may obtain fur
ther information from members of
tho English Department.
