# COTTON BILL PASSES
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 17 (JP). —
Congressional action was completed
on the Bankhead Compulsory Cot
ton Control Bill.

There is a new type of jev/el box
on the market that rings like a fire
bell if anyone touches it while you
sleep.
