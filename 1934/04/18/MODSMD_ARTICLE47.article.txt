# Alumni Golf Tournament Planned for Celebration
## 
### 
In connection with the Alumni
Homecoming celebration this week
end, an alumni golf tournament has
been arranged for Sunday, A1 Mas
ters of the B.A.C. announced last
night.
The tourney will be played on the
Stanford Golf Course, and no green
fees will be charged alumni during
the day. Special starting times have
been arranged from 9 to 12 o'clock
Sunday morning, but competition
will be open all day. Prizes will be
given on a basis of medal play.
