# Moliere's Comedy-Ballet Plays in San Francisco
## 
### 
Moliere's comedy-ballet, "Le Bour
geois Gentilhomme" will be pre
sented by Andre Ferrier and his
company with the assistance of
Adolph Bolm and the members of
the San Francisco Opera Ballet
School and an orchestra under the
direction of Gastone Usigli, in the
War Memorial Opera House in San
Francisco next Friday evening and
Saturday afternoon.

Rochester University has abol
ished 8 o'clock classes.
