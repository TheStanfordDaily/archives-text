# Intramural Horseshoe Games Begin April 23
## 
### 
Intramural competition in pitching
horseshoe begin.s Monday, April
23, in an elimination tournament.
Schedules will appear on the bulle
tin board in Eneina gym. All scores
must be kept and turned in on the
official blue blanks to be found in
the top drawer of the intramural
desk in the staff room in the gym.
Rules of the National Horseshoe
Pitching Association shall govern
except in method of scoring, which
shall be determined on the basis of
twenty-one points for a single
game. A team shall consist of four
players, who shall play one singles
match of 21 points against each of
their four opponents. The team
scoring the greatest number of
points on this basis shall be declared
the winner.
