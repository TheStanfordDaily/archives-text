# Dowrie Takes Students To Visit Stock Exchange
## 
### 
George W. Dowrie, Professor of
Finance, took 50 of his students to
the San Francisco Stock Exchange
yesterday for a first-hand view of
stock trading.
After watching trading from the
visitors' gallery, they heard an ex
planation of the technique of trading
by Ross Ambler Curran, vice-presi
dent of the exchange. Curran also
gave the stock exchange official's
view of the economic function per
formed by the exchange in provid
ing facilities for financing Far West
ern industry and commerce.
