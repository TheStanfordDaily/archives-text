# Tickets for Big Track Meet on Sale at A.S.S.U.
## 
### 
Tickets for the Stanford-Califor
nia track meet are now on sale in
the A.S.S.U. office. Part of the
Alumni Homecoming celebration, the
Big Meet, which will be held in the
stadium Saturday afternoon, prom
ises to be one of the most spectacu
lar in the history of the two colleges.
Tickets are 55 cents to students,
general admission, $1.10.
