# Cancel Practice Because Of Soccer Man's Death
## 
### 
Members of Stanford's soccer
teams yesterday signed a letter of
condolence to be sent to the parents
of Milton Newman, who was killed
last Saturday in an automobile ac
cident. No turnout was held yes
terday as a tribute to the deceased
player.
Newman had played soccer for the
last two years and was slated for
first-string goalie in the 1934 season.
