# German Society Plans Holiday in Santa Cruz
## 
### 
Members of the German Honor
Society will picnic in Santa Cruz
April 21. They will meet at 9
o'clock that morning at the home
of Dr. W. A. Cooper, where further
transportation arrangements will be
made. The society's regular meeting
will be held April 26 at Dr. Kurt
Frank Reinhardt's home, from 4 to
6 o'clock.
