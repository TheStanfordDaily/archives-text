# Phi Phi Bids 14 Electees
## 
### 
Phi Phi, national senior honor
society for men, announces the se
lection of 14 new members follow
ing a meeting of the local chapter
at the Chi Psi house yesterday.
Following the present membership:
William H. Starke, Phi Delt;
Lloyd T. Scheen, Kappa Sig; Frank
B. Ingersoll, Beta; Jake A. Butts,
Deke; Robin M. Lindsey, Deke;
Gordon W. Mallatratt, Alpha Delt;
James A. Woolley, Alpha Delt.
Robert F. Foster, Sigma Nu: King
Harris, Chi Psi; John D. Mottram,
Phi Psi; A1 F. B,lackman» D.U.;
Charles B. Boothe, Zeta Psi; Charles
Wheeler Coberly, Zeta Psi; and
Austin H. Peck, Jr., Zeta Psi.
