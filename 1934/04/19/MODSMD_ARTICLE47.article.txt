# IN THE DERBY PADDOCK
## 
### 

On the basis of news that has seeped out of winter quarters and
on his two-year-old performance, William Ziegler, jr.'s Bonanza rates
consideration as one of the dark horses for the Kentucky derby. The
brown colt, a top notch Juvenile, earned $13,650 for his owner last
year. (Associated Fress Photo)
