# Drawing For Free Bid To Masque Bali Today
## 
### 
Drawing for a free Masque Ball
bid will be held at noon today in
front of the post office. The stubs
from all of the Quads purchased
any time this past week, including
those sold this morning from 8-12
o'clock, will be included in the draw
ing. If the fortunate person is not
present at the time, the ducat will
be given to him later.
The Quad this year promises to be
an especially unusual number with
an outstanding cover and striking
photogi'aphy that has never been
used before. The price is $5.65 or
$6.15 with your name on the cover.
