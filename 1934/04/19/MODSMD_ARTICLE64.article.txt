# Dr. Odegard to Address Political Science Majors
## 
### 
A meeting of the faculty and all
majors in the Department of Poli
tical Science will be held in the
basement of the Women's Clubhouse
this evening at 7:30 o'clock.
One of the features of the evening
will be a speech by Dr. Peter Ode
gard, of Ohio State University, on
his impressions of President Roose
velt's "New Deal." The talk will be
followed by an open discussion.
Dr. Odegard, who has made a spe
cial study of politics and public
opinion, is taking Professor Bar
clay's place on the Stanford faculty
during the spring and summer quar
ters.
