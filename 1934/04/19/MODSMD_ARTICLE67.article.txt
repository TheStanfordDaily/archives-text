# A.I.E.E. Meets Tonight, To Make Trip Saturday
## 
### 
W. VV. Stahl of the Pacific Tele
phone and Telegraph Company will
address tonight's meeting of the
Stanford branch of the American In
stitute of Electrical Engineers, to be
held at 7:30 o'clock in Room 283 of
the Engineering Building.
Mr. Stahl will describe and ex
plain equipment used in a telephone
office to speed up modern methods
of communication. On Saturday, the
local engineers are to make an in
spection trip through the Palo Aito
exchange of the P.T.&T. in order to
see this equipment in actual opera
tion.
Any students interested in making
the trip are welcome to join the
engineers, and those wishing to go
should be at the exchange, Bryant
between University and Hamilton, at
9:30 o'clock Saturday morning.
