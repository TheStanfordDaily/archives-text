# Alexander Faye Draws Lucky Number for Ball
## 
### 
Alexander Faye, freshman student
from the Hawaiian Islands, held the
lucky number in the Quad drawing
yesterday noon for the free bid to
the Masque Ball tonight.
The contest proved successful,
says Business Manager Hal Whit
field, in increasing the, number of
Quad sales. The books now left arc
relatively few.
A drive will bo made today and
tomorrow to sell copies to the alumni
who are here for the Homecoming
week. Quad representatives will be
at the table in front of the Union
and the Administration Building on
Friday, and at the Faculty House
between 11-1 o'clock on Saturday
during the barbecue.
