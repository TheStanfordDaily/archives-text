# We Laugh Again!
## 
### 
NOW THAT crazy Old Hoy, Jimmy Algar, lias come
forth ago in with a good hook, the Chaparral, just in time
10 send a whole Hock of homecoming alumni into hysterics.
The Daily, austere and unostentatious, of course resents
the blatant coloring of the front pages now and then, hut
we derive much pleasure from sneaking ofl in a corner
and laughing heartily where nohodv can see us.
