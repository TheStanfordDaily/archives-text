# Toyon Spring Informal Will Be Friday, May 4
## 
### 
With bids to sell at a new low,
Toyon Club will hold its annual
spring informal in the Toyon lobby
from 9 to 1 o'clock, Friday night,
May 4. New and unusual decora
live features will be a special at
traction to this dance, according to
chairman Glenn Martineau of the
dance committee.
