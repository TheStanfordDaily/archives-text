# Bishop Stevens of L.A. To Give Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
The Right Reverend William Ber
trand Stevens, Bishop of Los Ange
les, will give the sermon Sunday
morning in the Memorial Church.
The University Choir, Miss Frances
Booth, contralto soloist, and Warren
D. Allen, organist, will furnish the
music. Services will start at 11
o'clock.
In the afternoon at 4 o'clock the
A Cappella Choir of the Pacific
Union College of St. Helena will give
a sacred concert.
