# Bear Grid Team to Play In Hawaii Next December
## 
### 
BERKELEY, April 19 (/P).—Uni
versity ol' California's football team
will travel to ihe Hawaiian Islands
next December to play two games
in Honolulu. The Beqrs will meet a
club team on Christmas day and
take on the University of Hawaii
on January 1.
