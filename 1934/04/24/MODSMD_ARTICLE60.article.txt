# Allen To Play American Organ Concert Today
## 
### 
A concert of American organ mu
sic will be played today in the Me
morial Chui'ch by Warren D. Allen.
Mr. Allen has arranged some of the
music especially himself.
His program:
Prelude and Fugue in C Minor....
Seth Bingham
Vermeland Howard H. Hanson
Cantilena Carl McKinley
Shining Shore
Edwards Shippen Barnes
Toccata on a Gregorian Theme
Edward Shippen Barnes
