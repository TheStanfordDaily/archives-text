# Toyon Holds Quarter's Second Smoker Tomorrow
## 
### 
Toyon Club's second smoker will
be held tomorrow evening for its
members at U:.'3o o'clock in the Toyon
lobby.
Captain I. A. Luke of the Ordnance
Department of the University R.O.
T.C., will be guest speaker, and
other well-known Stanford men are
expected to speak. Free cigarettes
will be distributed.

Geometry tests at the University
of Chicago are illustrated with foot
ball plays.
