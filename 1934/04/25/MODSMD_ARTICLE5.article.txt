# Tickets on Sale Monday For 'Y' May Day Brunch
## 
### 
Tickets for the May Day Brunch
May 7 will go on sale Monday. Rep
resentatives of the Y.W.C.A. and
members of the ticket committee will
canvass all living groups. Single
ducats entitling the buyer to straw
berry shortcake and coffee will cost
25 cents. A table of four may be re
served for $1. Tables will be avail
able from 8:30 o'clock in the morning
until 5 o'clock at night.
Invitations will be. sent to the
Stanford Mother's Club, the Stanford
Women's Club, the Administration,
and various Palo Alto organizations.
From' 3 until 5 o'clock in the after
noon they will be specially enter
tained.
The May Day Brunch is sponsored
by the Y.W.C.A. in connection with
the Convalescent Home drive.
