# MOB OF 3000 STORMS LOUISIANA COURTHOUSE
## 
### 

This photo shows part of the mob of 3,000 that stormed the million dollar courthouse at Shreveport, La., in an
effort to reach Fred Lockhart, confessed slayer of Mae Griffin, 16-year-old girl. Mob violence swirled for hours
around the building. Two companies of national guardsmen were called when the crowd threatened to over
power the sheriff. (Associated Press Photo)
