# Roble To Give Annual Informal Dance May 26
## 
### 
Plans for its annual informal have
been begun by Roble Club. Slated
to lake place on May 26, the affair
is well under way, with committees
tentatively appointed.
Orchestra, scene, and exact time
are to be announced at a later date.
