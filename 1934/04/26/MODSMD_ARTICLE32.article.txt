# Waldo Salt's Comedy Receives Third Honors
## 
### 
Third honors in the Palo Alto
Community Theatre's original one
act play contest were awarded to
Waldo Salt. Stanford actor and
writer. Salt's comedy, titled '"Van
ished Yesterdays," will be presented
at the Civic Theatre on May 10.
The contest, which closed recent
ly, was judged by a committee of
three, two of its members being
members of the Stanford faculty:
Dr. Margery Bailey and Ransom
Rideout. The contest was under the
general direction of Ralph Emerson
Welles, who is chief of the Civic
Center organization.
