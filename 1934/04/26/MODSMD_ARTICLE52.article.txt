# Allen To Play Schubert, Schumann Compositions
## 
### 
A program of specially arranged
Schumann and Schubert numbers
will be played for this afternoon's
organ recital by Warren D. Allen.
The ever-popular "Serenade" and
"Ave Maria" will be heard.
The program, which starts at 4
o'clock, is as follows:
Sketch in F Minor Schumann
Sketch in D Flat Schumann
Serenade Schubert
The Almighty Schubert
Ave Maria Schubert
Finale, Symphonic Etudes
Schumann
