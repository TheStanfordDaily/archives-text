# WESTERN HIGHWAY CHIEFS FORM ORGANIZATION
## 
### 

The Western Association of State Highway Patrol Officials was organized at a meeting in Phoenix,
Ariz., and C. R. McDowell of that state was elected its first president. The membership is drawn from 12
states. Among those in attendance were (left to right) E. J. House, New Mexico; McDowell; E. Ray
mond Cato, California, vice-president; R. W. Groo, Utah; L. G. Phares, Texas, and George G. Smith,
Wyoming. (Associated Press Photo)
