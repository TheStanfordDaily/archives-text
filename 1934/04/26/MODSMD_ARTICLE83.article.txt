# Toyon Dance Committee Continues Sale of Bids
## 
### 
Bids to the annual Toyon spring
informal will be available today to
members of Toyon Club. They may
be secured from the dance commit
tee. This group consists of Chair
man Glenn Martineau, Julius Ja
cobs, Dan McGettigan, Ed Reames,
Bill McCallum, and John Cotton.
With the dance, which is to be
held in the hall lobby from 9 to 1
o'clock on Friday, May 4, only one
week off, tickets will go on sale to
morrow to the student body as a
whole, either at the A.S.S.U. office
or from members of the dance com
mittee.
