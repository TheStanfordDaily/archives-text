# BOARD PAYMENTS DUE
## 
### 
Collection of the second install
ment of board payments for Roble,
the Union, and all other University
operated women's dormitories, will
be made in the lobby of the Admin
istration Building, Thursday, May 3.
from 8:30 to 4:30 o'clock. There will
be a fine of fifty cents a day for late
payments. , 1
