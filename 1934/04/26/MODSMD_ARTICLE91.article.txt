# "Three Cornered Moon" To Be Given May 11
## 
### 
Ticket sales begin soon for the
current campus theatrical event,
Gertrude Tonkonogy's "Three Cor
nered Moon." To be presented in
the assembly hall, the comedy will
be produced May 11.
Distinctive cast, unusual plot,
combine to make the opus an un
usual presentation. It is under the
direction of Harold Helvenston,
drama head.
