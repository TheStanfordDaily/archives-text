# Painless Quad Buying Method Offered Frosh
## 
### 
A painless method of buying the
1934 Quad is now being offered to
Encina men by Business Manager
Hal Whitfield.
Through the co-operation of As
sistant Comptroller E. S. Erwin the
Quad payment may be deducted
from the winter quarter Encina re
bates.
A thorough canvass of Encina is
now in progress. Arrangements may
be made also with the Quad rep
resentative from 8 to 12 o'clock to
day in front of the post-office.
