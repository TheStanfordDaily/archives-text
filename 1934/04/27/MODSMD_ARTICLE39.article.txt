# Union Club Holds Formal Dinner Dance Tomorrow
## 
### 
The spring motif will be featured
at Union Club's formal dinner
dance to be held next Saturday
night. Dinner will be at 6:45 o'clock
in the main dining room of the
Union and dancing will begin at
8:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.
Red and white hawthorne blos
soms and other spring flowers
blended with soft lighting will deco
rate the tables and the clubhouse.
Jerry Lanigan's orchestra from
San Jose will provide the music.
The regular Saturday night dance
will not be held this week because
of the formal hut will be resumed
the following Saturday evening.
