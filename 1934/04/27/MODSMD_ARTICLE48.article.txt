# WHERE TIDAL WAVE RUINED NORWWAY FISHING TOWNS
## 
### 

This bleak scene gives a glirrpse of destruction experienced at the fishing hamlets of Rafjord and
Fjoraa, on the west coast of Norway, after the disastrous series of tidal waves which followed a crash

of some of the homes washed into the sea. (Associated Press Photo)
