# Soothe Fails to Place In Lakeside Aquatic Meet
## 
### 
Tom Boothe, Indian diver, failed to
place; v/hile Kitty Ahrens, feminine
Stanford swimming star, lost third
place by inches in Junior National
A.A.U. aquatic events at Lakeside
Wednesday night.
Boothe entered the 10-foot spring
board diving event. Miss Ahrens
placed fourth in the Women's 500-
yard free style.
