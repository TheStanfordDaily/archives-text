# Free Copies of Axe Song Obtainable at Bookstore
## 
### 
Free copies of the song, "The
Stanford Axe," are available to
students by presentation of their
student body cards at the Bookstore.
Written by H. D. Walter, '01, and
arranged by E. R. Flint, '01, to the
"Father of Victory" march, the au
thors have made it possible to be
distributed to students at their ex
pense.
