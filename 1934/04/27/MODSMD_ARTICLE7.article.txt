# DRIVERS PLEAD GUILTY
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 26
Charged with assaulting two news
papermen at the funeral of Ernie
Triplett, auto race driver, March 10,
Babe Stapp and Louis Tomai. also
drivers, appeared in court today and
pleaded guilty to simple assault and
asked for probation from Judge
Charles S. Burnell.
