# NEWS BRIEFS
## OHIO CONVICTS ESCAPE 
### 
COLUMBUS, Ohio, April 29 (/P).—
Acting as though they were going to
do repair work on a guard tower,
three Ohio penitentiary convicts
climbed into the turret of the prison
today, covered a guard with pistols,
disarmed him, and escaped over the
wall.
