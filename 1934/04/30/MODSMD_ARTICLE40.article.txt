# THE 						PREXY SENDS A PLEASANT THOUGHT
## 
### 
The following telegram was re
ceived by Dink Templeton on the
morning of the Big Meet:
R TEMPLETON, STANFORD
TRACK TEAM
STANFORD UNIVERSITY
CALIF
REGRET VERY MUCH UNABLE
SEE OUR TRACK TEAM MEET
CALIFORNIA WIN OR LOSE I AM
FOR THEM THEY SHOULD HAVE
MADE FINE RECORDS AS STU
DENTS MEN AND AS COMPETI
TIVE ATHLETES TELL THEM
MRS WILBUR AND I WILL BE
WISHING EACH ONE OF THEM
AS WELL AS YOU BEST OF LUCK
RAY LYMAN WILBUR
Dink posted the message on the
training room bulletin board. It hit
some of the lads right between the
eyes, and they caught the signifi
cance. Maybe Doc Wilbur won the
Big Meet for vis. Who knows?
