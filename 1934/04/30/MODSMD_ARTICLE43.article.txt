# LEAD REVOLT AGAINST VOLIVA
## 
### 

Leaders in the rebellion against the rule of Wilbur Glenn Voliva
in the religious colony of Zion, 111., were Onias W. Farley and his wife
(above). Farley was named president of the school board, in compe
tition with Voliva's candidate. In reprisal. Voliva closed the town's
parochial schools. Mrs. Farley created a sensation by appearing In
front of Voliva's apartment and denouncing him in a sidewalk speech.
(Associated Press Photo)
