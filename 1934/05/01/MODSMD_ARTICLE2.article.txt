# Women's Room Deposits Must Be Paid Tomorrow
## 
### 
Room deposits of $5 for all women
students who are to live in Univer
sity living groups in the autumn
quarter, 1934, must be made at the
Dean of Women's office today and
tomorrow.
The office will be open from 9 to
12 o'clock, and from 2 to 5 o'clock.
If no deposit is made, it will be as
sumed that the student is not return
ing.
Next year Roble will be for fresh
men only, with no former students
except those who are definitely asked
to help in the administration of the
hall and counselling of freshmen.
