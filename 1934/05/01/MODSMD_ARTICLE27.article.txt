## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 30 (/P). —
Senate Democrats were called to
gether tonight for a conference thai
amounted to a party pep rally in be
half of the reciprocal tariff measure
which the President has placed on
the list of bills he wants enacted be
fore Congress goes home.
