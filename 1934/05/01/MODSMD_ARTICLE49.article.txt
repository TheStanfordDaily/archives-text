# Second Year French, Latin Courses Changed
## 
### 
It is no longer necessary for a
student who wishes to pursue the
study of either language more than
two years, to take second year
French and Spanish five times a
week, according to the 1934-35 An
nouncement. of Courses, which came
out yesterday.
Except for the first year, the five
unit courses in the lower division
have been divided up into two- and
three-unit courses.

Two thousand five hundred dol
lars is the goal set in a drive for
money to send the California crew
East.
