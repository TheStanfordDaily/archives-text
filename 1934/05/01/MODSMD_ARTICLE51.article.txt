# ISLAM, SPAIN TOPIC OF PALENCIA TALK
## 
### 
Lecturing in Spanish, Dr. Angel
Gonzales Palencia, acting professor
of Spanish, will speak on "El Islam
y Espana" (Islam and Spain) at 7:30
o'clock Thursday in the Little Thea
tre.
Illustrations will be used in the
talk which will deal with the history
of Mohammedan domination in
Spain, the outstanding features of
Arabic civilization, the influence 01
Arabic culture in the occidental
world, and the material remains of
Arabic civilazation in Spain.
