# Frosh Meet St. Mary's In No-Decision Debate
## 
### 
Frosh Debaters Ed James, Harold
Charters, and Ed Lindblom met St.
Mary's Frosh Val Detling, Thomas
Paddock, and Edward Nevin on the
question: ''Resolved, that France is
responsible for post-war chaos," in
the Women's Clubhouse last night.
The debate was not judged for a
decision.
