# RAIL BUILDER'S GRANDSON WEDS
## 
### 

One rf the important dates on the New York social calendar this
spring was the wedding of Rosanne Hoar and Anson McCook Beard,
who are shown after the ceremony. Mrs. Beard is the daughter of Mr.
and Mrs. Friend Hoar of New York. The bridegroom is the son of Mrs.
Pierre Lorillard of Tuxedo Park, N. Y., and grandson of the late J.
Hill, famous railroad builder. (Associated Press Photo)
