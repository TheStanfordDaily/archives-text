# Neil Petree Elected As New York Alumni Head
## 
### 
Neil Petree, '19, has been elected
president of the Stanford Alumni
Club of New York, New Jersey, and
Connecticut.
While at the University, Petree
was co-editor of the Illustrated Re
view. His wife is the former Vera
Thomas, '18.
Petree is now president and gen
eral manager of James McCreedy
and Co., New York, one of the old
est department stores in the country.
