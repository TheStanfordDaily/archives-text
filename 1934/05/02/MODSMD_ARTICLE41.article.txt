# Peck Receives Injury During Soccer Game
## 
### 
Pete Peck, Zeta Psi, received a
kick in the head during a soccer
game yesterday and as a result is
confined to the hospital. His injuries
were not serious and it is believed
that he will be released today or
tomorrow.
Details regarding the manner in
which he received the injury are not
available, as Peck remembers noth
ing about the circumstances of the
accident, and bystanders did not
happen to be watching him at the
time.
