# Encina Commons To Pay Board Rebates Tomorrow
## 
### 
Encina Commons board refunds
for winter quarter will be made at
the Encina Hall office windows from
1 to 5 o'clock, possibly later, tomor
row and Friday, with the maximum
set at nine dollars, announced as
sistant comptroller E. S. Erwin yes
terday.
A total of $3300 is ready for those
who appear to claim their own
again.
