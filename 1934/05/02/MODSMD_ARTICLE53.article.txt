# Helvenston Will Discuss Dramatics and Camp Life
## 
### 
Dramatics and camp life will be
the subject of a talk by Harold Hel
venston, director of dramatics, at the
fifth session of the Camp Counselor
Training Course at 7:30 o'clock to
night in Room 202 on the English
Corner.
This course is being promoted by
the Y.M.C.A. with the co-operation
of the University and members of
the faculty.
