# KYA Summer Debaters Asked To Meet Today
## 
### 
Announcement and discussion of
a series of summer debates to be
held over radio station KYA will be
made at 4:30 o'clock this afternoon
in Room 201, according to Bob Gros,
assistant debate manager.
Plans for an international debate
with the University of British Co
lumbia next month will also be dis
cussed. as well as arrangements for
the annual debate banquet.
Lee Chapin, debate adviser, has
asked that all men who have par
ticipated in any Stanford debating
activities during the past year be
present at the meeting.
