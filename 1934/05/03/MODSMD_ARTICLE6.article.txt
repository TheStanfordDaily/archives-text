# Encina Commons Board Refunds Obtainable Now
## 
### 
Encina Commons board refunds
for winter quarter will be made at
the Encina Hall office windows from
1 to 6 o'clock, today and tomorrow,
Assistant Comptroller E. S. Erwin
announced yesterday.
Holders of $85 books will re
ceive $9 refund; $70 books, $7.40;
and guest books, no refund. The
refunds are figured on a basic rate
of 10.6 per cent, of a total of $31,400
board bill for winter quarter.
To claim the refund it is neces
sary only to make an appearance at
the proper hours, since there will
be men enough on duty to handle
all claims.
