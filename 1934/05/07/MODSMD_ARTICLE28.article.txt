# Junior Prom Bid Sale will Begin Tomorrow
## 
### 
Bid sale for the Junior Prom will
open tomorrow in the A.S.S.U. of
fices in the Old Union, according to
an announcement made late last
night by the dance committee, which
is headed by Marvie Kahn. Al
though no definite announcement
was made, the tickets were declared
to be "unusually low" in cost.
Preliminary plans have chosen the
location of the annual formal for the
dance studio of the Women's gym,
the choice spot for campus social
affairs.
