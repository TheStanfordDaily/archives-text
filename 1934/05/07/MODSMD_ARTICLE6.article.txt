# THEY HID DILLINGER'S PAL
## 
### 

Ollie Catfish and his wife, Indians living in the Lac du Flambeau dis
trict of Northern Wisconsin, were the unwilling hosts to George '"Bab
Face" Nelson, widely sought Dillinger gangster, for two days. After tli
impromptu visit Nelson forced the Catfish to direct him out of the wood
near the Indian's shack. (Associated Press Photo)
