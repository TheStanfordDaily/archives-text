# R.O.T.C. Men Will Take Physical Examinations
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Militai'y
Department all candidates for sum
mer encampment in the R.O.T.C.
will receive a physical examination.
They will also be innoculated against
typhoid fever axid be vaccinated for
smallpox.
