# WEST COAST ATHLETES COMPETE IN FRESNO RELAYS
## 
### 

The cream of the track and field performers meet in Fiesno under lights Saturday to decide the Western
supremacy. Among the entrants are (left to right) Toy Draper, S.C. sprinter; Walter Marty, Fresno State high
