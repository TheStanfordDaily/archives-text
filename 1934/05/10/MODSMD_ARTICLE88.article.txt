# Roger Williams Club Will Hold Steak Fry in Hills
## 
### 
Roger Williams Club members will
have their final social event Satur
day, May 12, in the form of a steak
fry in Big Basin. All those affiliated
arc cordially invited.
The group will leave from the First
Baptist Church on the corner of
Bryant Street and Hamilton Ave
nue at 1:30 o'clock sharp. A small
assessment will be charged to cover
food and transportation expenses.
Reservations may be made Thursday
and Friday by calling 5223.
