# TORNADO HITS MONTANA
## 
### 
PLENTYWOOD, Mont., May 9 (/P).
—Northeastern Montana today sur
veyed the wreckage left last night by
a tornado which brought injury to
at least three persons and wide
spread property damage to this and
neighboring localities.
