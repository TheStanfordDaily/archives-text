# Stanford Faculty Women Elect Officers May 16
## 
### 
The last meeting of the season of
the Stanford Faculty Women's Club
will be held at 3 p.m. Wednesday,
May 16, in the Women's Clubhouse.
Business of the meeting will in
clude the making of annual reports
and election of officers for next year.
Mrs. A. E. Naughton will give a
short musical program.
