# Harvard Scholarship Entry Time Extended
## 
### 
A spccial time extension has been
granted to students who are late in
turning in their applications for the
scholarships to the Harvard Gradu
ate School of Business for the year
1934-35. Professor T. J. Kreps of
the Stanford Business School will
receive these entry blanks from 8 to
12 o'clock this morning in his office,
Room 422.
The Harvard Business Club of San
Francisco is offering these two schol
arships to students or graduates of
business schools in Northern Cali
fornia. Professor Kreps is a mem
ber of the committee which will
select the successful applicants. In
addition to these scholarships, other
financial arrangements are being
made to help students for the com
ing school year.
