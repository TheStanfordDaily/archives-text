# Change Open Hours For Women's Pool Saturday
## 
### 
In place of the open swimming
hour on Saturdays at 11 o'clock, a
diving class will be held, according
to an announcement yesterday at
the Women's gym.
Open hours at the women's pool
will be from 2 to 5 o'clock.
