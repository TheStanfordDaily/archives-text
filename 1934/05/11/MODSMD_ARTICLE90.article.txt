# ROUT RELIEF STRIKERS
## 
### 
WICHITA, Kan., May 10 i.P).—
Four companies of National Guards
men bore down upon a meeting of
400 unemployed demonstrators to
day and dispersed them with tear
gas. Acting under orders of Gov.
Alf M. Landon, the Guardsmen had
sought to prevent a recurrence, of
clashes Wednesday in which the
demonstrators, protesting against
wage scales on work relief projects,
beat and stabbed policemen.
