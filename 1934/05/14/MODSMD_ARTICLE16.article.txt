# Statistics Show Hables Flat on His Back Again
## 
### 
Recent statistics gathered by im-
I partial observers show that Leslie
I Hables, track star and present stu
j dent manager, has been either flat
on his back, on crutches or attacked
by boils for three hundred thirty
I days during the past year.
Hables is now in the Palo Alto
Hospital suffering from the effects of
a fall caused by a banana peel on
the law steps. Hables, coming down
the steps, hit the peel and according
to observers, described the prettiest
i arc seen in this vicinity for many
j years. He landed painfully on the
| fleshy part of his hips. He is feeling
much better and will be able to see
his friend in a day or so.
