# Culver's Secretary In Bed with Tibia Trubble
## 
### 
Friends and relatives of Miss Vi
vienne Church, secretary in good
I repute under Dean George Bliss
! Culver, were grieved this morning
to hear that she is suffering from a
painful injury of the left tibia. Miss
Church will probably be unable to
handle the business of the Dean of
Men's office for several days, it is
' feared.
