# Miss Mirrielees To Talk At Stanford Dames Meet
## 
### 
The regular meeting of the Stan
ford Dames will be held Thursday
afternoon at 3 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse,
Miss Edith Mirrielees, associate
professor of English, will discuss
current literature. A musical pro
gram will follow, after which there
will be the tea hour.
