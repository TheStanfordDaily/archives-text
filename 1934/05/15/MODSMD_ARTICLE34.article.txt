# University of Heidelberg Gives Three Scholarships
## 
### 
In view of economic conditions in
the United States, the University of
Heidelberg in Germany offers tuition
scholarships to three Stanford stu
dents, graduate or undergraduate,
for the summer session starting June
25 and ending August 4.
In addition to the special courses
for foreigners in German, students
holding these scholarships will be
permitted to attend any regular uni
versity lectures they choose in other
subjects and to participate in the
historic German festivals that occur
in that period.
The Illinois legislature has asked
the attorney general of the state to
investigate the merger of Chicago
and Northwestern Universities.
The Roble Library is to be com
pletely redecorated, with wall fin
ishing begun.
