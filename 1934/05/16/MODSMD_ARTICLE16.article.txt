# Moot Court Competition Finals To End Thursday
## 
### 
The championship of Stanford's
moot court competition will be de
cided Thursday night in Room 12 at
8 o'clock when Phil and Will Ray
appear as counselors for the appel
lant and Robert Cathcart, counsel for
the respondent in the case, Anderson
vs. the State.
Presiding in the court will be
Judge Henry G. Jorgenson, justice in
the Superior Court of Monterey
County.
The Ray brothers and Cathcart
have successfully participated in
preliminary cases in the moot court
competition and the decision tonight
will determine the champion of the
Stanford Law School.
