# Roble To Give Annual Spring Formal May 26
## 
### 
Roble Hall's annual Spring In
formal has been tentatively set for
May 26, with theme as yet unde
cided. The scene will probably be
the dance studio, if bid sale indicates
sufficiently large attendance; other
wise the Roble lobby will be utilized.
