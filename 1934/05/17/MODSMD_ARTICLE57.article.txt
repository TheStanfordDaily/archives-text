# Bronze Plaque Marks Old Home of Stanfords
## 
### 
Sunday the old Stanford Home in
Sacramento is to be marked with a
bronze plaque. The placing of the
plaque, a gift of the Rio Rita Parlor
No. 253, Native Daughters of the
Golden West, and the dedication will
take place at 4 o'clock.
A number of are slated,
with Monsignor Horgan accepting
the plaque, as the Stanford Home is
now the property of the church.
Other speakers include Thomas
Scollan, mayor of Sacramento; Miss
Esther Sullivan, past grand president
of the Native Daughters, and chair
man of History and Landmarks;
Judge Martin Welsh, Sadie Brain
aid, organizer of Rio Rita Parlor
and donor of the plaque.
