# Quad Pictures on Sale Today at Press Building
## 
### 
Quad officials have generously of
fered to leave the room while Stan
ford women take their pick of pic
tures from among all those appearing
in the 1934 Quad, it was announced
yesterday.
Individual photographs may be ob
tained for the sum of 25 cents apiece,
while whole fraternity panels may
be procured at the bargain price of
$2,50. Group pictures are being sold
for 40 cents.
These pictures are now available
on the second floor of the Press
Building on week-day afternoons.
