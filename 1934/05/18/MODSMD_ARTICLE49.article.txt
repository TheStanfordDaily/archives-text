# Department Store Offers Prize for Dress Design
## 
### 
Prizes amounting to $250 are be
ing offered to financially embar
rassed students by a large San
Francisco department store, ac
cording to an announcement made
today by Charles G. Wrenn, as
sistant registrar of the University.
The contest, which closes June 16,
offers a prize of $100 for the best
design for women's, misses', and
college girls' dresses for fall wear.
A second prize of $50 is offered, in
addition to ten additional prizes
of $10 each.
Any students interested should get
in touch with Professor Wrenn at
the Registrar's office.
