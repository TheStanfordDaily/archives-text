# Felt Lake Patrol Boat Gets Annual Repairs
## 
### 
The row-boat which the Univer
sity maintains at Felt Lake for the
purposes of patrolling and taking
depths was hauled into the Corpo
ration Yard yesterday where it is
undei going its annual repairs. It is
being painted and leak-proofed.

A strike is being threatened at
Peking National University because
of an order prohibiting coeds from
visiting men in their dormitories.
