# NEWS BRIEFS
## REPORT SCORES NRA 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 20 (£>).—
Clarence Darrow's review board to
night presented its 50,000-word
critique of the NRA, recommending
a return to the anti-trust laws which
were suspended for the recovery
program and terming the NRA
watchword of "fair competition" an
"illusory phrase."
