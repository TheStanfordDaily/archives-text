# Swain To Give Last Department Lecture
## 
### 
In the last major department lec
ture of the year Wednesday eve
ning, Professor Robert Eckles Swain,
executive head of the School of
Physical Sciences, will speak on
"The Departments of Physics and
Chemistry."
This lecture was scheduled to be
given February 21 but was post
poned. It is the fourteenth of a
series sponsored by the Vocational
Guidance Committee of the lower
division.
Professor Swain, who has been in
Europe for the past few months, is
due back on the campus today.
