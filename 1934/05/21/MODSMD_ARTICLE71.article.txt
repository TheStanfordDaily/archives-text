# "Y" To Tour Mexican Quarter of San Jose
## 
### 
Next Saturday morning at 10
o'clock a "Y"-sponsored tour will
leave the Women's Clubhouse for an
inspection of the Mexican quarter of
San Jose. With Spanish interpreters
and social workers acting as guides,
ihe tour will include trips to la
borers' homes to see the living con
ditions, to factories, and to clinics in
that quarter.
A Mexican lunch will be served,
some of the items of food being en
chiladas, frijoles, and tortillas.
The cost of the trip, including
transportation and lunch, will be 55
cents. All those interested in going
are asked to call at the Women's
Clubhouse before next Thursday
evening. Jean DcVoss is in charge
of the affair.
