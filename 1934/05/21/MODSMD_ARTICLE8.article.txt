# Encina Club Will Hold Spring Informal Friday
## 
### 
Encinamen planning to attend
their Spring Informal in the Encina
lobby 9 to 1 this Friday must se
cure tickets soon at the office of
the A.S.S.U., as they are selling
rapidly, at $1.50 each.
Ernie Hecksher's twinkling banjo,
vocalists, girls' trio, and 12-piece
orchestra, Wes Muller's tasty re
freshments, and Milo Talley's
spring-time decorations have been
arranged for by committeemen Rex
Hardy, Mike Gonzales, and Bob
Hoover.
