# BOMB BURSTS IN MAIL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. May 21 (/P).-—A
bomb which inspectors say was des
tined for the' Balkans exploded in
the post office dead letter office here
today, shattering a clerk's hand and
slightly injuring a Negro messenger.
