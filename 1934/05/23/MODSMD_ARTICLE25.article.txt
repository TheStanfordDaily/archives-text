# SWEDISH PRINCE THROWN
## 
### 
STOCKHOLM, May 22 (£»). —
Crown Prince Gustaf Adolf was
thrown and injured today when his
horse Aida fell while the royal and
daring rider was practicing hurdle
i jumping.
