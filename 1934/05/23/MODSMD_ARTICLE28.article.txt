# School of Business Club To Hear S.F. Controller
## 
### 
Leonard S. Lcavy, controller of the
city and county of San Francisco,
will speak at the weekly dinner of
the Graduate School of Business
Club, to be held at 6:15 o'clock to
night in the main Union dining room
Leavy will speak on the problems
of city and county control connected
with the bay metropolis.
