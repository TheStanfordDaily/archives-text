# JUMPING FROGS COOL OFF BETWEEN CONTESTS
## 
### 

Here are some of the frogs, with their owners, resting after arduous jumping in the Artgels Camp,
Cal., annual leaping 'jubilee, held in commemoration of the Mother Lode gold rush days. A native, reared
in a pond at the mining -amp, won the main event. Standing, left to right: Mary Edwards, Lawrence
Monte Verde, jr.. Mayor Al Mentz, Dave Capello, Jimmie Valente, Maurice Meltzer. Seated: Alice Mills,
Aileen Ryland and Charlec Crespi. The little fellow next to Crespi forgot to give his name to the camera
man. (Associated Press Photo)
