# DARROW SEES JOHNSON
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 23. (JP) —
Amid renewed disputation over
Clarence Darrow's criticism of NRA,
the veteran Chicago lawyer paid a
friendly visit to the office of Hugh
S. Johnson, and the two went for
an automobile ride. There was no
comment from either, except that
the call was described as social.
