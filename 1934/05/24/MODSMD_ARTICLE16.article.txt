# DEMAND DEMOBILIZATION
## 
### 
MINNEAPOLIS, May 23. (/P) —
Demands for demobilization of the
National Guard before they would
consider acceptance of an extension
of the temporary truce were pre
sented to Gov. Floyd B. Olson to
night by a delegation of striking
truck drivers.
