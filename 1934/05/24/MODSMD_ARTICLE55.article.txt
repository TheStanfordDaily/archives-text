# Banquet Planned To Honor Retiring German Head
## 
### 
Honoring Dr. William Alpha Coo
per. present head of the German
Department who will retire in June,
a dinner will be given at 6:15 o'clock
Sunday, June 3.
Invitations have been sent to all
friends and associates of Professor
Cooper. However, anyone who has
not received one but wishing to come
should get in touch with Miss Esther
Klaustermeyer, secretary of the
German Department, before May 28.
