# Hoover Officiates At Dedication of New Dam
## 
### 
Herbert Hoover, former president,
will leave his Farm home tomorrow
for Pasadena where he will officiate
in the dedication of the new Pine
Canyon dam.
The dam will be christened "Mor
ris Dam" in honor of Samuel B.
Morris, Stanford alumnus, who is
chief engineer and general manager
of the Pasadena water department.
Hoover will unveil two tablets,
bearing the names of those who have
worked on the project and relating
facts about the structure, as a part
of the dedication ceremonies.
