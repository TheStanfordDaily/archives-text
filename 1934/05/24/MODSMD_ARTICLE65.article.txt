# Decoration Committee Of Senior Ball Named
## 
### 
Plans for the Senior Ball, to be
held in Toyon lobby Friday evening,
June 15, are rapidly assuming more
tangible form with the appointment
by the committee last night of Girvin
Whitney and Dick Van Etten to take
charge of decorations.
The ball will begin at 10 o'clock
Friday evening and last until 4:30
o'clock the following morning
Breakfast will be served, the menu
of which will consist of tomato juice
cocktails, scrambled eggs on toast,
home-made Swiss sausage, buttered
biscuits, and coffee The orchestra
has not been selected yet.
