# W.A.A. Holds Play-offs For Tennis Championship
## 
### 
An elimination tennis tournament
is now being held among the top
eight players on the W.A.A. ladder
to determine the all-University
champion and the all-star tennis
team of this year.
The first round matches are to be
played by Wednesday, May 30. and
the semi-finals by June 4. The finals
will be played on Field Day, June 5.
