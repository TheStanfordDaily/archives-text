# Gardner Picks Morality As Sermon Topic Sunday
## 
### 
"Present Day Morality" will bo
the subject of the Reverend D.
Charles Gardner's sermon Sunday
morning, and 11 o'clock in the Me
morial Church. Music, offered by
the University choir, under Warren
D. Allen, will be as follows:
Prelude: "Wachet auf, ruft uns
die Stimme" Bach
Introit: For Thee, Dear, Dear
Country A. R. Gaul
Solo from "The Triumph of
David" Dudley Buck
Anthem: Agnus Dei Bizet
Women's chorus and tenor solo. W
Madison Taylor, soloist.
University Organist Allen will
play his regular organ recital in the
Chapel at 4 o'clock.
