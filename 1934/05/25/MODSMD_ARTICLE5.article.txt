# Annual Roble Informal Slated for Tomorrow
## 
### 
Roble Club will hold its annual
spring informal tomorrow night, at
9 o'clock in the Roble lobby. About
fifty couples will attend with their
escorts.
The orchestra has not yet been
announced, according to Lois Bassi,
who is in charge of the arrange
ments. Several committees are
working hard to insure the success
of the dance.
The list of patrons and patron
esses is as follows: Mr. and Mrs.
1 Charles Wrenn, Mr. and Mrs. Alfred
Trowbridge, Mr. and Mrs. Warren
I D. Allen, Mr. and Mrs. Grayson N.
j Kefauver.
