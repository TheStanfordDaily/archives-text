# RECONSTRUCTION BEGINS IN CHICAGO YARD
## 
### 

Almost as soon as the flames of the spectacular blaze that destroyed Chicago's stockyards had died, hun
dreds of workmen began the task of erecting temporary quarters for many of the concerns and stockyard
agencies burned out in the fire. "Business as usual" was the promise of the yard managements who are build
ing up after the second biggest fire in Chicago's history. (Associated Press Photo)
