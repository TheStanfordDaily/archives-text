# Library Tour Planned For Monday Afternoon
## 
### 
For the benefit of students wishing
to visit all departments of the Li
brary, Miss Alice Hays, reference
librarian, will conduct a tour through
the building on Monday afternoon
at 4:30 o'clock.
All those who are interested in
making this tour must meet Miss
Hays outside the main reading room
on the second floor promptly at 4:30
o'clock.
Time Schedules will be on sale
tomorrow for 10 cents apiece.
