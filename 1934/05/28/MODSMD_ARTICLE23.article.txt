# Present-Day Morals Discussed in Sermon
## 
### 
Rev. D. Charles Gardner, Univer
sity chaplain, spoke yesterday on the
subject, "Present-Day Morals." He
stated that although man is inher
ently good, his morals at the present
are not of the highest quality. Yes
terday's sermon is the last Dr. Gard
ner is to give this quarter.
W. Madison Taylor performed as
tenor soloist, with music by the Uni
versity choir and Organist Warren
D. Allen.
