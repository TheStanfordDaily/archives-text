# FORD BUYS FOSTER BIRTHPLACE
## 
### 

Ilenry Ford paused to smile at the steps of the birthplace of the poet,
Stephen Foster, in Pittsburgh, which he purchased and which will be moved
to Ford's museum in Dearborn, Michigan. Left to right: R. F. Leonard, a
Ford employee; Ford, James Gribbon, with whom Ford negotiated; and the
Rev. A. J. Wiglcy, pastor of the St. John the Baptist Church. (A.P. Photo)
