# RALLY TOMORROW
## 
### 
Permission of Dr. Wilbur has
been secured for a special "wel
come home" rally at 11 o'clock
tomorrow morning on the Eng
lish corner in order to greet the
victorious tracksters, according
to Rally Committee Chairman
Stan Beaubaire.
The team arrives at the Palo
Alto depot at 10:28 o'clock, and
committeemen will drive them
out to the campus where Yell
Leader Jimmy Triolo will be
ready to start things promptly.
