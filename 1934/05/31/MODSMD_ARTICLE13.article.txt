# '34 Ball Bids Now on Sale
## 
### 
Bids for the Senior Ball, June 15,
go on sale today at the A.S.S.U. of
fices at .1 o'clock, instead of Friday
as originally announced. The num
ber is limited to 225.
The bids aie $6.50, and a senior
dues card must be presented when
the bid is bought. The card will
be checked so that only one bid
may be bought with that card.
Patrons and patronesses for the
affair will be Dr. and Mrs. Ray Ly
man Wilbur, Dr. and Mrs. Robert
E. Swain, Dean George Bliss Cul
ver, Dean Mary Yost, Mr. and Mrs.
Almon E. Roth, Mr. and Mrs. John
Ezra McDowell, Lieutenant Colonel
and Mrs. Donald C. Cubbison, and
Professor Theodore H. Boggs.
