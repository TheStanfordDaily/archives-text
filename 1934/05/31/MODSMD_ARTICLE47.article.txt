# Yearbook Copies Still On Sale at Bookstore
## 
### 
Several copies of the "Yearbook of
Stanford Writing" are still on sale
at the Book Store. The book was
written, bound, and compiled by
members of the English Club.
The works of several writers
prominent and new in campus lit
erary circles are contained in the
volume, such as the poetry of J. V.
Cunningham, Barbara Gibbs, and
Vesta Nickerson, and the stories of
North Baker, Albert Guerard, Jr.,
and Thomas Sellers. The covcr was
designed by Will Williams.
