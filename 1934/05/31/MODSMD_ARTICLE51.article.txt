# Officers for 1934-1935 Chosen by Union Club
## 
### 
At the elections held yesterday
at the Union Club the following
officers were chosen for 1934-35:
President, Mary Beth Wortman;
vice-president, Dorothea Burgers;
social secretary, Jane Dodge; cal
endar chairman, Enid Olivi; club
house board, Louise Alauzet; late
leaves, health, and scholarship, Al
ice Robinson; and recording secre
tary, Betty Wheatley.
