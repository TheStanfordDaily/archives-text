# Helen Jacobs, S. Palfrey Favored in French Games
## 
### 
PARIS, May 30 (/P). —Favored as
the finalists, Helen Jacobs, United
States champion, and Sarah Palfrey
today won their way to the semi
final round of women's doubles in
the French hard court tennis cham
pionships.
The American pair defeated Co
lete Rosambert and Mme. Metaxa
of France, 6-3, 1-6, 6-4. Tomorrow
they meet Susan Noel and J. Jedrre
zowska, Polish champion.
Elizabeth Ryan, the former Cali
fornian, and Mme. Rene Mathieu of
France, the defending title holders,
also won their way to the semi
finals, defeating the English team of
Nancy Lyle and Miss E. M. Dear
man, 8-10, 6-3, 6-3.
