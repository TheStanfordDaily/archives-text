# Stanford Represented At U.C. Science Meet
## 
### 
Stanford is to be represented at
the meeting of the American Physi
cal Society in Berkeley on June 21
by Professors P. A. Ross and Paul
Kirkpatrick. Both are to read pa
pers at a symposium on physical
constants.
Ross will present a paper entitled
"Measurement of the Coinpton
Shift." Ross has done notable work
in this field, and contributed much
to clarifying the Compton effect.
Kirkpatrick's contribution will be
on "Continuous X-Rray Spectra and
the Value of h/c 4/3."
Professor Robert A. Millikan of
Cal Tech will also present a paper
at this symposium.
