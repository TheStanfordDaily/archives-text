# FOUR OFFICERS WHO GUIDED CLASS OF 1934 DESTINY
## 
### 

This photo shows four of
the officers who guided the
activities of the 1934 Senior
Class under the presidency
of Donn Tatum. From left
to right: Bob Savage, treas
urer; Ollie Ardahl, secre
tary; Dorothy Ann Jack
son, vice-president; and
Franklin Curtis, vice-pres
ident during the first term
under President Bill Morse.
President Tatum named 13
major committees and more
than 150 committeemen to
carry out traditional activi
ties during Senior Week,
June 14-16. (Quad Photo)
