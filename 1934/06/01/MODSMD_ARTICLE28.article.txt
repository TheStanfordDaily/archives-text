# END STRIKE IN MINNEAPOLIS
## 
### 

The Minneapolis truck strike which led to rioting on the streets
and a call for troops was officially ended when the truck drivers'
union agreed to a peace agreement. Shown here are officials of the
union preparing to sign the pact. Left to right: Cliff Hall, business
agent; William Brown, president; S. Haskell, secretary and treasurer.
(Associated Press Photo)
