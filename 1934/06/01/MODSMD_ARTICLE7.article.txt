# LINDY GIVES VIEWS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 31 (/p) —
Colonel Charles A. Lindbergh, a
vigorous critic of the Army Air
Corps' transportation of Air Mail,
has given the special committee sur
veying Air Corps activities his views
on organization, personnel and
equipment of the Army fliers.
