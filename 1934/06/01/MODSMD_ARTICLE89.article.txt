# Commencement Ticket Distribution Date Set
## 
### 
Tickets for the baccalaureate and
commencement exercises will be
distributed June 7, the Committee on
Public Exercises announced yester
day. Only those students who have
made the required arrangements
will be given tickets.
The ducats, two to each senior,
will be issued in the lobby of the
Administration Building from 9 to
12 o'clock and 1 to 5 o'clock on
Thursday, June 7. Students en
titled to the tickets must call for
them in person, the committee
warned. Tickets will not be held
after this date.
