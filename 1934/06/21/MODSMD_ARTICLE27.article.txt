# SOCIALIST TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Milen Dempster, Socialist candidate
for governor, will explain the Socialist
platform in an address this evening at
8 o'clock in the Native Sons' Hall, 207
Hamilton avenue, Palo Alto.

With only about half of the women on
the campus voting, Stanford co-eds this
year expressed their desire to retain
sororities on the campus by a vote of
216 to 103.
