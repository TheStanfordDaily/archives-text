# ALUMNI FETE COACHES
## 
### 
Santa Barbara's Stanford Club has
planned an "alumni round up" Sunday,
on a ranch north of Santa Barbara with
a barbecue and rodeo. Special guests
will be Coaches Tiny Thornhill, Dink
Templeton, and Harry Maloney, and
Graduate Manager A1 Masters.
Of the 604 people listed in the latest
Stanford Bulletin under the heading,
Officers of Instruction and Administra
tion, 279 have one or more degrees from
Stanford University or Cooper Medical
College which is now Stanford Medical
School.
