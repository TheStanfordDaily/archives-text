# ELEANOR CROSS ENGAGED TO WED WARDELL DUGGAN
## 
### 
Eleanor Cross, daughter of the late
Professor Charles N. Cross and Mrs.
Cross, will become the bride of War
dell Duggan after completion of a
course at the Stanford School of Nurs
ing. Miss Cross will first finish her
junior year's work here. She is a
member of Alpha Omicron Pi.
Duggan has been working on a
newspaper in Mt. Vernon, Wash., since
receiving his degree in journalism at
the end of last fall quarter. He was
debate manager in his senior year and
belongs to Theta Xi.
William Parmer Fuller, Jr., '10, a
member of the Stanford Board of
Trustees, was recently elected presi
dent of the Board of Trustees for
Mills College.
