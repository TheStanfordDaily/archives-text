# Tryout Period Announced For Daily Business Staff
## 
### 
Bob Herman, business manager of
the Daily, has issued a call for all stu
dents desiring practical experience to
try out for the Daily this summer. Of
fering selling experience, office train
ing, business contacts, and an oppor
tunity of working up to any one of five
salaried jobs, the business staff furnishes
an exceptionally good field for extra
curricular activities.
Freshmen and sophomore men and
women are particularly urged to try
out now and to continue their work
throughout the regular year. Hours for
the business office are every morning
from 10:30 to 12, and any applicants
can see Herman at that time.
