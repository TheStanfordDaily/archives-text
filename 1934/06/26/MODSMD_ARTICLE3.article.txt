# JOBS AVAILABLE
## 
### 
Mrs. Irene Spenner, in charge of the
Appointment Service, announces that
more than fifty jobs paying board or
room are still available. Several of the
jobs are for women. Applications may
be made in Room 164, Administration.

"Recent Currents in the Intellectual
Life of Germany" will be the subject of
Professor Camillo von Klenze in the
first of the Thursday series on the eve
ning of June 28.
