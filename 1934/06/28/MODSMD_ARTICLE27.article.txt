# Scientists Inspect Physics Laboratory
## 
### 
The Department of Physics was host
last Monday to three distinguished sci
entists, Dr. K. K. Darrow of the Bell
Telephone Laboratories, New York; Dr.
Robert J. Van de Graff of the Massa
chusetts Institute of Technology, the
inventor of M.l.T.'s famous high volt
age artificial lightning machine; and
Dr. Jesse W. M. DuMond of California
Institute of Technology.
The three professors were shown
through the Ryan High Voltage Labora
tory. They were in Northern California
for the meeting of the American Physi
cal Society last week.
