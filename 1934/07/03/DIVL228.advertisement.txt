Thursday, July 5, is the last day
students may change their study
lists for this quarter without fine.
After Thursday all program re
visions must be made by petition
to the Registration Committee,
subject to the following fines: two
dollars for each course added or
dropped, or three dollars where
one course is substituted for an
other. All program changes made
sinoe Registration Day should be
filed at the Registrar's Office on
the regular revised study - list
cards.
