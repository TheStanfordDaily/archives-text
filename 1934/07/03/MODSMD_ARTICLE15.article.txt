# Let's Have a Dance
## 
### 
"All work and no play makes Jack a dull hoy!"
Stanford students art* not all Jacks, but the same may apply to Jills.
Jfave you a little Jill in your home, sorority, or dormitory? Docs she
sit in vacant corners and pine away the lonesome evenings after the
day's studies are done? Such a condition should not l>c tolerated by true
Stanford Jacks. We rise in arms.
There was a day long ago, even in our abbreviated summer quarters,
when weekly Roble .get-toget liens furnished an outlet for the social
yearning. Such is no more, but the solution is not so difficult. A little
initiative on the part/of the student councils would set the ball rolling.
Let's have one dance, and the next will take care of itself!
