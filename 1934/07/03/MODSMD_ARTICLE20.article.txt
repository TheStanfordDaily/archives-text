# Kenneth Spencer to Sing On Campus Next Tuesday
## 
### 
Kenneth Spencer, Negro basso pro
fundo, will be heard next Tuesday
evening, July 10, in a concert in ihe
Women's Gymnasium Dance Studio.
Spencer, who is twenty-one years
oid and more than six feet tall, has
been described as a "mile-high singer
with a mile-deep voice" and as a "dra
matic interpreter of the music and the
poetry of his race."
His program ranges from spirituals
and the reading of Negro sermons to
Mozart arias, and his singing is familiar
to radio audiences.
