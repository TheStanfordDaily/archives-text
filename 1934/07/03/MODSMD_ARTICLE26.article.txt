# STANFORD DAMES ELECT MRS. WARMCKE SECRETARY
## 
### 
Mrs. Leon Warmeke was elected sec
retary of the Stanford Dames at their
initial meeting last Thursday. Mrs. A.
C. Lambert was chosen social chair
man. The first regular meeting of the
Stanford Dames will be held this Thurs
day at 3 p.m. in the Women's Club
house. AH interested are invited to
attend.
The sewing group of the Stanford
Dames will meet this afternoon at 2
o'clock at Mrs. Colonel Adams' home
at 115 Embarcadero.
