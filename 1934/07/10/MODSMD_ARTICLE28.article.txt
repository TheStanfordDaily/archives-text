# EDUCATION MEN HOLD SMOKER, DISCUSSION
## 
### 
The first smoker for the men of the
School of Education was held last niqht
at 8 o'clock at the home of Dean Gray
son N. Kefauver. There were several
talks given on the general subject of
teacher tenure, followed by a panel dis
cussion presided over by A. R. Kennedy,
acting president of Phi Delta Kappa.
There were about 75 men present.
