# Night Swimmers Lose Garments
## 
### 
Three couples, after becoming
terribly bored with themselves one
night this week, decided to take a
midnight plunge in a fraternity
swimming pool. After a few min
utes of paddling, the moonlight
swimmers climbed out of the pool
to, dress.
Alas! The girls' clothes had dis
appeared, and even after thorough
search of the house, and the wak
ing of all the inmates, the dresses
could not be found. Ruffled, dis
gx-untled and upset, the girls fi
nally agreed to be driven home
in their bathing suits.

get another date with the same
three girls?
