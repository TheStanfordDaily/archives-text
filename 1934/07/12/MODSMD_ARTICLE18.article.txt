# Davis, Food Research Head, To Talk on KPO
## 
### 
Dr. Joseph S. Davis, director of the
Stanford Food Research Institute, will
talk to the KPO radio audience next
Monday evening at 7:45 o'clock on
"Planned Agriculture under the A A A."
Dr. Davis was chief economist for the
Federal Farm Board from 1029 to 1931.
He is now co-operating with the Brook
ings Institute, Washington, D.C., on a
concurrent study of the operation of the
A.A.A. In his talk Dr. Davis expects to
discuss Ihe control of acreage in pro
duction and marketing agreements.
