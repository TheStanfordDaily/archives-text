# FILE VACCINATION SLIPS
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due
at the Registrar's Office on Thurs
day, July 19, for all new students,
including those having permit to
attend classes, and all other stu
dents whose certificates have ex
pired. Failure to file certificates by
5 o'clock on Thursday, July 26,
will result in a fine of $2.00; or, if
not filed by August I, registration
in the University will be subject
to cancellation by the Committee
on Public Health.
