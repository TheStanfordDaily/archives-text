# PATTEN, CREVELING TROTH
## 
### 
The engagement of Mary Patten,
Theta and spring graduate, to Louis
Creveling, '28, present time sales and
advertising manager for the Marion R.
Gray Company, was announced yester
day.
One use of the photo-electric cell is
to prevent the kidnaping of children.
The bed or crib is surrounded with the
beam and breaking or interference sets
off an alarm.
