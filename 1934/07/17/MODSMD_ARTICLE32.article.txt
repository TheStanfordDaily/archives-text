# MRS. HARRY WOLTER INJURED
## 
### 
Mrs. Harry Wolter, wife of Stanford's
veteran baseball coach, incurred a rib
fracture and bruises as the result of
a collision between cars operated by
Harry Wolter and Leland Leads, in
San Jose last week. Mrs. Wolter was
thrown against the side of the car by
the impact of the collision. She was
taken to the Palo Alto Hospital for
treatment, after which she was removed
to her home. Since then her condition
has improved rapidly.
