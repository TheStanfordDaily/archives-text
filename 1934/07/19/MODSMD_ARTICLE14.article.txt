# Hedley to Give Second Of Talk Series Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. George P. Hedley will speak
on "Religion and the Call to Act," the
second in his series of sermons on
"For Such a Time as This," next Sunday
in the Memorial Church at 11 o'clock.
The Rev. Mr. Hedley is from the Hart
ford Theological Seminary in Hartford,
Connecticut.
The musical program will include
Cavatina, from the Quartet, Opus 130,
by Beethoven; "In Thee Is Gladness,"
by Caravaggio-Stanford; and "Jesus,
Priceless Treasure," by Bach. Warren
D. Allen will give an organ recital at
7:30 in the evening.
