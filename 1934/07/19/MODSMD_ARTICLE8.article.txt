# "JERRY" TRAUTMAN WILL WED DORIS TUCKER SOON
## 
### 
Woid has just been received on the
campus that Gerald Trautman, 1933-34
student body president, will marry
Doris Tucker, Stanford co-ed and
member of Kappa Kappa Gamma, some
time next month.
At present "Jerry" is touring the
East. In the fall he will attend Harvard
Law School, while Miss Tucker will
enter Radclille.
