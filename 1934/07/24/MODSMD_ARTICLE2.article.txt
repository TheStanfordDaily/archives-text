# Mrs. Emma B. Meier To Sing To Summer German Club
## 
### 
Mrs. Emma B. Meier. German instruc
tor at Reed College, Portland, will sing
a number of German songs at the meet
ing of the Summer German Club in the
Women's Clubhouse, in the left wing of
the Union, on Thursday afternoon at
4:30 o'clock. She will be accompanied
by Grovenor Cooper, son of Professor
William Cooper of Stanford.
The program will consist of poems by
Goethe, set to music by Schubert,
Schumman, and Wolf. Professor Ca
millo von Klenze will discuss briefly
the treatment of Goethe's poems by va
rious composers.
