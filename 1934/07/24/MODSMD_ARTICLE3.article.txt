# Delmas Stars in Initial Season of Pro Baseball
## 
### 
That Bert Dclmas is succeeding as
shortstop for the Albany club of the
American Association is the latest news
from professional baseball circles about
the former Stanford captain. Delmas,
who at the time of Stanford's reign
over the California Collegiate League,
was the leading slugger, was maintain
ing his pace with the bat, having com
piled a .330 average this season; while
his almost errorless fielding was the
feature of his play.
