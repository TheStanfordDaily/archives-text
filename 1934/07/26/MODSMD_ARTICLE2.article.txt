# REV. HEDLEY CONTINUES SUNDAY SERMON TOPIC
## 
### 
The Rev. George P. Hedley will speak
on "Religion and the Chance to Live,"
the third of .his series of talks on "For
Such a Time as This," at 11 o'clock Sun
day in the Memorial Church.
The musical program will include "I
Stand Before the Gate of Heaven," by
Bach, "Jubilate" by Allen, and "In
Heavenly Love Abiding" by Parker, to
be sung by Mrs. Helen Kutch, soprano.
Warren D. Allen will Rive an organ re
cital at 7:30 o'clock in the evening.
