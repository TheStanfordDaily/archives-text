# Librarians Represent Stanford at Convention
## 
### 
Stanford University was represented
| at the recent Montreal meeting of the
American Library Association by Miss
Linda Turn Suden, bibliographer of the
Lane Medical Library, and by Nathan
; Van Patten, director of Stanford Uni
versity libraries.
Mr. Van Patten read a paper on the
use of various photographic processes
in the reproduction of library materials.
This report was written by Dr. Bedikson
of the Huntington Library, Pasadena,
who was unable to attend the confer
ence.
Miss Suden devoted much of her time
i to a study of the famous Osier Library
' at McGill University.
Director Van Patten was elected a
member of the Executive Committee of
the Association of Research Libraries.
