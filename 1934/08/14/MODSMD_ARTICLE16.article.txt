# MISS LILIENTHAL ENGAGED
## 
### 
Miss Elizabeth Lilienthal, who a year
ago deserted Stanford to attend Bryn
Mawr, is now thinking of deserting
Bryn Mawr for James Gerstley Jr.
Announcement was made of the en
gagement of Miss Lilienthal, daughter
of Mr. and Mrs. Samuel Lilienthal of
San Francisco, to Mr, Gerstley, son oi
Mi-, and Mrs. James Gerstley of Lon
don. Gerstley is established in business
in Los Angeles.
