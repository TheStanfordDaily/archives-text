# Harriet Marvin French, '33, To Wed John DeLamater
## 
### 
On September 1 Miss Harriet Mar
vin French will become the bride of
John Newton DeLamater in Pasa
dena. Miss French was graduated
from Stanford in '33 and continued
her work this last year. She is a mem
ber of the Sigma Kappa sorority. Mr.
DeLamater is a pre-medical student
from Montclair, N.J.
On the same day, in the Joseph
Bond chapel of the University of Chi
cago, Miss Dorothy Nelson will be
come the bride of Dr. John William
Devereaux, graduate of Stanford and
Rush Medical School.
