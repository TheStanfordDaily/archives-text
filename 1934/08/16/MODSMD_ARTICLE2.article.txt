# Indian Economist Will Be Heard In Tuesday Lecture
## 
### 
Drawing his illustrations "primarily
from the great experiment in India of
the last three years, Dr. P. A. Wadia,
of Bombay, will talk on "Religion and
Politics" next Tuesday evening at 8
o'clock in the concluding Tuesday eve
ning series of the summer quarter.
Dr. Wadia, an economist, is dean of
the faculty of arts and chairman of
the board of economics, political sci
ence, and philosophy in the University
of Bombay. He has written "The
Wealth of India," and "Money and
Money Market in India."
The Indian professor is a man of
affairs in India. He is a leader in the
"International Fellowship." A Parsee
by birth, he is consulted by men of all
creeds in India.
