# ORGAN RECITAL TODAY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen will give an organ
recital this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock.
His program will feature toccatas, pre
ludes and fugues. He will play "Pre
lude in D Minor" by Bach, "Toccata for
the Elevation" by Frescobaldi, "Toccata
in C Minor" by Muffat, "Toccata in F"
by Bach, "Prelude in C Major" by Pur
cell, and Prelude from the . Ninth
Violin Sonata" by Corelli.
