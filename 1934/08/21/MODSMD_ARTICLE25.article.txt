# Otis Wins Tennis Doubles In Long Beach Tourney
## 
### 
Charles Otis, Stanford tennis captain,
yesterday paired with Jack Bixler of
Los Angeles to capture the doubles title
of the Long Beach Open Tennis Tourna
ment. The pair beat Charles Carr, Pasa
dena, and Jack Knemeyer, Inglewood,
by the scores 6-3, 6-4.
