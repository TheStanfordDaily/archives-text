# Spanish Lecture To Be In Little Theatre Thursday
## 
### 
Visiting Professor Angel Gonzales-
Palencia, of Ihe University of Madrid,
will conclude the Thursday evening
lecture series with the topic, "The
Legacy of Islam," in the Little Theatre
at 8 o'clock tonight. The lecture was
previously scheduled to be given in the
Women's Gymnasium.
Professor Gonzales-Palencia will out
line the traces of Hispanic-Islam civili
zation still surviving in the western
world, and will show the part played by
this Arabic influence in the transmis
sion of Oriental thought and culture
into European civilization. The lecture
will be illustrated, and in Spanish.
