# Mrs. Brett Gives First Of New Recitals Today
## 
### 
Mrs. Ethel Sleeper Brett, of the First
Methodist Episcopal Church in Sacra
mento, will give a series of organ re
citals this week. Her first recital will
be this afternoon at 4:15.
Mrs. Brett will have charge of the
musical program during the 11 o'clock
service in the Memorial Church Sunday.
The Rev. Carl S. Patton, of the P'irst
Congregational Church in Los Angeles,
will preach the sermon. Mrs. Brett will
play again at 7:30 in Ihe evening. There
will be no church services or organ re
citals until October 7.
