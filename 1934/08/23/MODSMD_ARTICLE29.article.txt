# DR. R. E. SWAIN WILL ADDRESS AMERICAN CHEMICAL SOCIETY
## 
### 
Dr. Robert E. Swain, head of the
Stanford Chemistry Department, will
deliver one of the general addresses at
the opening session of the American
Chemical Society meeting in Cleveland,
to be held from September 10 to 15.
"Chemistry During the Depression"
is the title of Dr. Swain's address. He
will point out that despite the depres
sion, research in the fields of academic
and industrial chemistry has not
dropped but has progressed remark
ably.
