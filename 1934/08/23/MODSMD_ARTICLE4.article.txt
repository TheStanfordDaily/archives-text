# Mears Planning Program Of World Affairs Institute
## 
### 
The program for the twelfth session
of the Institute of World Affairs, to be
held at Riverside, California, during the
second week of December, is being
rounded out, Professor Eliot G. Mears of
the Graduate School of Business, who
is director of the Institute, said yester
day.
Scholars and experts from various
parts of the United States will partici
pate.
The body, which is comparable to the
Institute of Politics, held annually at
Williamstown, Massachusetts, exists
for the discussion of international prob
lems involving politics, trade, finance,
law and communications. A number of
Stanford professors will be on the pro
gram.
