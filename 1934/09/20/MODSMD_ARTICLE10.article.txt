# ART TALENT SOUGHT FOR DAILY STAFF
## 
### 
Special call is issued imme
diately to all artists entering
this year to do cartoons, carica
tures, and linoleum or wood
block cuts for the Daily during
the coming year.
Four positions are open on a
newly-formed Daily art staff,
and no qualifications other than
ability to produce good work
once a week is required.
Aspiring artists are asked to
drop in at the Daily editorial
offices on Monday or Tuesday,
October 1 or 2, for an inter
view.
Tryouts for other editorial po
sitions will be held early in the
autumn quarter, and many op
portunities remain for men and
women interested in journalism,
regardless of previous expe
rience.
