# "Send The Daily Home" Is Advice of Manager
## 
### 
"Want to avoid writer's cramp?"
queried Daily Manager Bob Herman
from his luxurious quarters in the
remodeled Daily shack. "Then have
The Daily sent home."
The far-famed "complete news
coverage" gives a bird's eye view to
the home folks, says Herman, for $4
a year or $1.50 a quarter. "Come
around any afternoon," he grinned,
"or mail us a check; we don't mind."
