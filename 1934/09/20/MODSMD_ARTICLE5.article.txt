# Daily Ad Men Open Tryouts
## 
### 
Business Aspirants! The only
practicable business experience open
to the Stanford student during his
entire college career is available in
the Stanford Daily business oflice.
Tryouts will open for freshman men
and women during registration week.
Freshmen and sophomores, upon
completion of a year of office work
and the writing and selling of ad
vertising, are eligible for promotion
to one of the four advertising man
agerships. From these four man
agers is chosen the business manager
for the ensuing year, all five posi
tions receiving salaries.
Business Manager Bob Herman
will be found in his office in the
newly-remodeled Daily Shyck and
requests those interested to come in
to "talk it over" during the first week
of school.
