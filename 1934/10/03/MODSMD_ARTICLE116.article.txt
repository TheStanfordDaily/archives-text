# PACIFIC MEETS BERKELEY
## 
### 
STOCKTON, Oct. 2. (/P)—Coach
Amos Alonzo Stagg, football's
"Grand Old Man?' whose little Col
lege of the Pacific team last, week
end held the mighty Trojans of the
University of Southern California to
a 6-0 score, today commenced prepa
rations for an even harder battle.
His team meets California at Berke
ley on October 13.
