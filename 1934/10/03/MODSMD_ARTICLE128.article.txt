# Philadelphia Coaches To Buy Portland Club
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, Oct. 2 (£>).—The
Sacramento Union said today that
Bing Miller and Eddie Rommell, for
mer Philadelphia Athletics, will buy
the Portland Club of the Pacific
Coast League.
The Union elid not divulge the
source of its information, but said
the report comes from a source "very
close" to the Portland Club.
Tom Turner, owner of the Beavers,
is known to be completely out of
the baseball picture for next year.
Rommell and Miller were coaches
for Connie Mack this year. They
"quit" their posts this week.
