# Bob Smith Issues Call For Football Managers
## 
### 
Several vacancies exist in the
football managerial staff, Bob Smith,
senior manager, announced yester
day. Smith asked that any interested
sophomores report to him on the
varsity field any afternoon this week.
The senior manager said an award
will be made to the outstanding
sophomore this year, but has not yet
announced the nature of the award.
