# Chekhov Play Will Be First Fall Play Reading
## 
### 
The first fall quarter reading by
Sword and Sandals, men's honorary
dramatic society, will be "Uncle
Vanya," by the Russian playwright,

Anton Chekhov. The date for the
performance has not yet been set.
Tryouts for the roles have been
tentatively scheduled for next week
by James Benet, new president of
the society. Definite time will be
announced soon.
