# American Killed in Crash Over English Channel
## 
### 
FOLKESTONE, England, Oct. 2
(TP).—A mysterious crash of an air
passenger liner into the English
Channel —Britain's third serious air
disaster in eleven days—today took
the lives of all its seven occupants,
' one of them an American. The trag
edy brought to 15 the toll of dead
taken by this blackest period in the
history of British civil aviation.
