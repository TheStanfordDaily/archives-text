# Prepare Coast Highway For S.C. Game Traffic
## 
### 
In order that the job may be fin
ished in time for the U.S.C. game
on October 27. a four-inch gas main
will be installed on the east side of
the coast highway in the "bottle
neck" section just south of San
Francisco.
Men laying water mams and re
placing services in that district are
also working with the maximum
speed, so that within a week the
contractor will be able to start set
ting the base on which the new con
crete roadway will lie.
