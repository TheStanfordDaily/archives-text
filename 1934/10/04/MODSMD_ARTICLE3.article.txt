# Autumn Festival Will Take Place At Jordan Home
## 
### 
At the home of Mrs. David
Starr Jordan a benefit Autumn
Festival for the aid of ihe Chil
dren's Museum of Palo Alto
will take place in the gardens of
the Jordan home on the Stan
ford campus, and is expected
to last from 10 in the morning
till 5 in the evening.
Tickets, which are 50 cents,
are available in advance by
writing to Mrs. Bertha M. Rice,
Poom 230, Cardinal Hotel, Palo
Alto. A special student rate of
15 cents will be in effect from 10
till 2, and tickets at this price
may be purchased at the
A.S.S.U. office.
