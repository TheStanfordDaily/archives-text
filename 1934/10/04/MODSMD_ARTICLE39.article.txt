# FOSTER CALLS TRYOUTS
## 
### 
With fall track getting under way
this week, Louis Foster, senior man
ager, yesterday requested sopho
more managerial tryoutees to report
to him at Angell Field from 4 to 0
o'clock any afternoon during the
next week. Foster also said equip
ment will bo issued to both varsity
and frosh track men this afternoon
at 3 o'clock.
