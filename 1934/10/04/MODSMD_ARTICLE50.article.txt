# New Students Only Ones To Get 1934-35 Manuals
## 
### 
It was erroneously stated in the
Daily yesterday that 1934-35 hand
books would be sold at the A S.S.U.
office to old students for 15 cents.
Handbooks, which cost the admin
istration 37 cents each, are not avail
able to old students at any price.
Freshme'n and new students may ob
tain them free of charge by pre
senting their student body cards at
the A.S.S.U. office today between the
hours of 1 and 3, and tomorrow
from 1 to 4 o'clock.
