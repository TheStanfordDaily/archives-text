# Roger Williams Group Holds Meeting Sunday
## 
### 
The Roger Williams Club will hold
its first meeting of the fall quarter
on Sunday, October 7, at 6:45 p.m.
in the Hamilton avenue room of the
1 First Baptist Church of Palo Alto,
Bryant and Hamilton.
The club is composed of univer
sity students, of any religious denom
ination, meeting together for the
purpose of intelligent and liberal
' discussion of religious problems. At
J each meeting, there is usually a uni
versity professor to lead the discus
sion.
Besides the discussion group, the
club also sponsors a social hour each
Sunday at 9 o'clock at homes on the
campus and in Palo Alto. Visitors
and new members are welcome and
urged to come by James Backerley,
chairman.
