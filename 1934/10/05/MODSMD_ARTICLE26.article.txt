# Exhibition of Chinese Art Will Be Shown at Mills
## 
### 
Many outstanding objects of Chi
nese art from the best museums
and private collections in the United
States will go on exhibition on Oc
tober 12 in the Mills College Art
Gallery.
The public will be admitted daily
from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. through the
closing date, which is November 25.
On October 10 at 8 o'clock the gal
lery will be opened for a special
preview for members of the press.
The Daily editorial staff has been
invited to attend.
