# CONFISCATION DEFEATED
## 
### 
SOUTIIPORT (England), Oct. 4
—(/P) —The British labor conference
overwhelmingly defeated today an
amendment calling for partial con
fiscation of capital property in a
socialization of industry program.
The scheme called for compen
sation to previous owners in the
firm of an income allowance for a
maximum of 25 years, but contained
no provisions for capital repayment.

Having emerged unscathed through
three wars, a Baker, Oregon, man
was struck and fatally injured by a
truck.
