# Hoovers Donate Volumes For Mills College Library
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. Herbert C. Hoover
have donated fifty books of various
types for use in the library of Mills
College.
Among the volumes presented is a
translation made by Mr. and Mrs.
Hoover of an early Latin edition
published originally in 1556. 1
Mr. Hoover, former President of
the United States, is a member of
the Board of Trustees of Mills Col-
I lege.
There will be an important meet
ing of the Graduate Women's Club
at 4:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. Officers announce that it is
imperative that all members be
present. I
