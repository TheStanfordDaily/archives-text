# OKKER WILL TEACH DRUM-MAJOR CLASS
## 
### 
All those interested in becoming
proficient in the fine art of baton
twirling will be given an opportunity
to learn, under the expert tutelage
of six-foot-six Bill Okker, drum
major extraordinary of the Stanford
band, in classes which will start
tomorrow.
Both men and women are eligible
for the class, which will be held at
the bandhouse from 10:30 to 11:30
o'clock.
