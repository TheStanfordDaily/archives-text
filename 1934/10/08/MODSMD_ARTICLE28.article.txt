# Parts in German Play Open to All Students
## 
### 
All students who would like to
take an active part in the production
of a German play are asked to report
today at 4 o'clock in room 119, de
partment of Germanic languages, for
a preliminary conference, according
to an announcement by K. F. Rein
hardt. Those accepted may enroll for
course 134, "Die deutsche Schau
buehne."
