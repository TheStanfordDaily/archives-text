# Sophomore Managers Needed for Basketball
## 
### 
A call for sophomore basketball
managers has been issued by Senior
Manager Willard Hancock. All men
interested are asked to report at the
pavilion any afternoon at 4 o'clock
or call Hancock at the Zete house.
Al the end of the season junior
managers will be picked from the
sophomore candidates. Ultimately
one man will be appointed to the
senior managership. Service as a
manager fulfills lower division phys
ical education requirements.
