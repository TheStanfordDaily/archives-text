# Dean Yost Will Give Tea For All Graduate Women
## 
### 
Tea will be served to all graduate
women at Dean Mary Yost'.s home
tomorrow afternoon from 4 to 6
o'clock, in order to enable them to
become better acquainted with one
another.
No invitations have been sent out
due to the large number enrolled this
quarter but "all graduate women,
both old and new, are most cor
dially invited," according to Miss
Yost.
