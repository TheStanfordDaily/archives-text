# English Club To Meet Tonight at Dr. Bailey's
## 
### 
The English Club will meet at 8
o'clock tonight in the home of Dr.
Margery Bailey, 1416 Tasso Street,
Palo Alto. The business of the meet
ing will be a discussion of new mem
bers. Refreshments and a series of
musical numbers by the club mem
bers will conclude the evening.
