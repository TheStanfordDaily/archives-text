# Quad Sale Continues At Post Office Corner Stall
## 
### 
Quads, 1935 edition, continue on
sale from 8 to 12 o'clock today at the
regular post office corner stall.
Students arc advised by Business
Manager Charles Fontius to get their
yearbooks now, because Tuesday the
special rate which has been in effecl
for the last two weeks will cease.
The reduced rate is $5.15 without,
the student's name engraved on the
cover, and $5.05 with name. The
regular price of $5.65 without name
and $6.15 with name will be charged
for the rest of the year beginning
Tuesday.
