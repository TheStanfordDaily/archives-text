# Telephone Office Open For Public Inspection
## 
### 
Just what happens when a tele
phone call is put through will be
demonstrated at the Palo Alto
branch of the Pacific Telephone'and
Telegraph Company any day this
week between I and 8:30 o'clock in
the evening.
The offices, located on 529 Bryant
street, are open for public inspec
tion. Besides the modern equipment
on view are replicas of the first
telephone and switchboard.
