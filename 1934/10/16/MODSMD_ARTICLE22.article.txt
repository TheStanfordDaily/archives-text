# Chemists Gather For Year's First Seminar
## 
### 
First chemistry department semi
nar of the year was held at the
home of Professor Robert E. Swain
last night at 7:30 o'clock. The speak
ers were Dr. E. C. Franklin and Dr.
P. A. Leighton. Advanced students
in the department and faculty mem
bers attended the meeting.
In addition to the regular pro
gram, this seminar afforded a chance
for all those present, to get ac
quainted. It has been the custom for
several years to hold this opening
meeting at Dr. Swain's home.
