# Men's Novice Tennis Tourney Opens Monday
## 
### 
The date for the men's novice ten
nis tournament has been set for next
Monday, October 22, states John
Dendahl, senior tennis manager. All
those wishing to sign up for the
tournament should register for it on
the bulletin board at the men's
gymnasium.
A list of men ineligible for the
tournament follows: all those who
won a numeral in frosh tennis last
year; former block "S" players; and
the first ten players on the varsity
squad this year.
