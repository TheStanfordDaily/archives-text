# BOY KING LEAVES LONDON
## 
### 

The new boy king of Yugoslavia, Peter 11, is shown in this picture,
cabled to New York from' London, as he left a London notel with his
grandmother, the Dowager Queen Marie of Rumania, prior to being
flown to Paris to join his mother, the widowed queen, Marie. With his
mother, the 11-year-old ruler will depart for Belgrade to attend the
funeral' of his father, the slain King Alexander. (Associated Press
Photo by Bartlane Transmission over Western Union cables)
