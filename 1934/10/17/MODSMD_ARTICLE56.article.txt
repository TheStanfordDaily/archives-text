# Women's Tennis Club Tryouts Set for Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Women's Tennis
club will be held this afternoon at
3 o'clock on the courts in back of the
Women's gym.
Club members will be on hand to
rally with the tryoutees and pass
judgment on their games. Potential
tryoutees wlio cannot arrange to
come this afternoon should com
municate with Amelia Baer, club
secretary, at Roble.
