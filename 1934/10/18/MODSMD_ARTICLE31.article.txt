# ALCATRAZ TROUBLE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Oct. 17 (/P).—
The disclosure that two mystery
speedboats had invaded the forbid
den waters about Alcatraz Island
federal prison twice recently and
had fied before rifle fire of alert
guards was made here today by
Warden James A. Johnston.
