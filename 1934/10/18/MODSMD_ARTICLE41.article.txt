# Students Asked to Sierra Club's Los Gatos Climb
## 
### 
Stanford students and their
friends are invited to participate in
the Sierra Club hike to the summit
f Mt. Umunhum, near Los Gatos.
A. party will leave the Palo Alto
Community House Saturday morn
ing at 7:45 o'clock.
Club members endeavor to fur
nish transportation by private car
to all who wish to go, but cannot
guarantee it. These hikes arc sched
uled about every two weeks. Those
who wish a program of the club's
activities may obtain one by writing
Frank Lewis, 857 Clintonia, San
Jose.
