# FRANCE BURIES BARTHOU WITH HONORS
## 
### 

This Associated Press picture, telephoned from Paris to London, then sent to the United States by radio,
shows the casket containing the body of Louis Barthou. foreign minister slain with King Alexander of Jugo
slavia, being drawn through the streets of Paris in a gun carriage during impressive funeral rites. During
the ceremony elaborate precautions were taken to protect President Lebrun and government members.
