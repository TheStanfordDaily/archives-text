# Rush to the Party!
## 
### 
Tonight lor Iho firsl liino and perhaps the
only time this school year, we, the entire fem
inine student population of Stanford Uni
versity, are to gather under a single roof for
two hours of fun and frolic.
The tradition that every Stanford woman
should know the 499 others has not yet heon
cast aside. It has heen altered, yes, numeri
cally, hut the spirit remains. And what is
tradition bill the spirit?
That there are 1024 Stanford women in
name, the Registrar's office has counted. To
night we tally those who hear the name in
spirit.
