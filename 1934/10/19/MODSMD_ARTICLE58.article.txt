# Co-eds Will Keep Warm In New Pig-Grain Coats
## 
### 
Those who insist on being the first
to wear the smart and different in
apparel, will put a new pig grain
leather jacket on their shopping list
as a rush order, thus causing a sen
sation on the Quad and at the same
time keeping one toasty warm for
8 o'clocks in chilly classrooms.
This new grain leather has it all
over suede, the former favorite, by
not soiling so quickly, being able to
stand more rough and tumble with
out showing it a particle and should
be a favorite with the rapidly van
ishing "rough."
When belted in tightly around the
waist with those new free swing
backs, and action pleats in both front
and back, it is especially smart.
All kinds of metal fasteners are
being used now in the most unique
ways on "piggy" jackets, and inci
dentally add a note to one's time
saving schedule.
