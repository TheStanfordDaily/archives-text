# Stanford Women Invited To Paly League Benefit
## 
### 
All campus women are invited to
attend the garden tea which will
be given from 3 to 5 o'clock this
afternoon at La Casa Abierta, home
of Kathleen Norris. The tea will be
given for the benefit of the Women's
Service League of Palo Alto.
Vivian Kay, Adrienne Johnston,
Virginia Keim, Betty Grover, Betty
Wheeler, and Peggy Woodard will
serve. Tickets, which cost $1, may
be obtained from Lee Stearns at
the Kappa house.
