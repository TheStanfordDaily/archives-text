# Hoopmen To Scrimmage Navy Team Next Week
## 
### 
A pre-season touc hof basketball
will be offered to local hoop fans
next Thursday evening in the pavil
ion when Coach John Bunn's varsity
aspirants will scrimmage with a team
from the battleship U.S.S. Penn
sylvania.
Varsity practice is being held each
afternoon in the pavilion under the
direction of Charlie Glasgow. Some
sixty-odd men are engaged in shoot
ing practice and the learning of
fundamentals.
Regular practice will commence at
the completion of the frosh lootball
schedule.
