# Mothers Choose Oct. 31 For Chrysanthemum Tea
## 
### 
The annual Chrysanthemum Tea,
sponsored by the Stanford Mothers
Club to increase their funds for stu
dent welfare work, will be held Oc
tober 31 at the Women's gymnasium
from 2 to 5:30 o'clock.
Flowers, shellacked gourds, and
candy will be on sale and tea will be
served' throughout the afternoon.
A skit, entitled "Up the Road from
Palo Alto," describing the rise of
Stanford, has been written by Mrs.
Car! Green Wilson and will be pre
sented at 3 o'clock and again at 4
o'clock.
Afternoon classes will be dismissed
in order that no student will have to
miss this charity event.
