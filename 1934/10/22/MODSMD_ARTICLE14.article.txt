# Freshman Badly Injured In Crash Saturday Night
## 
### 
In an automobile accident on the
Bayshore highway late Saturday
night, Irving White, '38, was severely
injured, and Bob Herman, '35, driver
of the car, escaped unscathed, ex
cept for minor bruises.
White is at the Palo Alio hospital,
where he has not yet regain con
sciousness. The extent of his injuries
is unknown. Herman's car was
completely demolished.
