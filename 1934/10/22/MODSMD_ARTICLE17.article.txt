# Reinhardt Picks Cast For "Myrtle Princess" Today
## 
### 
The cast for the "Myrtle Princess,"
German folk drama, will be picked
this afternoon at tryouts from 4 to
6 o'clock in room 119 by Dr. Kurt
Frank Reinhardt. All students in
terested in German drama are urged
to be present.
The cast for the Goethe play,
"The Lovers' Caprice," directed by
Dr. Reinhardt, will include Annelies
Morgan, Cedric Larson, Franclsca
Warlike, and Neil Ruge.
Both plays will be produced early
in December.
