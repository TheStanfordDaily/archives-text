# Orchestra Rehearsing For January Concert
## 
### 
Practice is well under way for the
concert to be given by the Stanford
Symphony Orchestra in January, as
one of the A.S.S.U. series. It will
include Beethoven's "Overture from
Coriolanus" and Smedna's "Over
ture to the Bartered Bride." Julius
Haug, first violinist of the San Fran
cisco Symphony, is director.
As there are still vacancies to be
filled in the personnel of the orches
tra, students playing any type of in
strument, particularly the violin or
violo, may try out at rehearsal Tues
day night at 7:15 o'clock in the Wom
en's old gym. One unit of credit is
given for work in the orchestra.
