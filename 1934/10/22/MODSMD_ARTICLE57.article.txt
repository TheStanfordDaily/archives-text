# READING GROUP MEETS
## 
### 
Reading Group of the Stanford
Dames will meet at 2 o'clock tomor
row afternoon at Cardinal Court, 530
Webster, apartment 10, the home of
Mrs. John Russell. Two books,
"Mary Peters," and "More Power to
You" will be reviewed.
At Whitman College freshmen
women may only go to the library
on Wednesday and Friday nights.

Harvard's baseball team recently
returned from an Oriental tour
where it completed a .series of good
will games.
