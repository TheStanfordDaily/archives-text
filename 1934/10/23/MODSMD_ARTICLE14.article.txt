# Roth Speaks Tonight At First Toyon Club Smoker
## 
### 
Toyon Hall will sponsor its first
smoker of the year tonight at 10
o'clock. Almon E. Roth, comptroller
of the University, will be the guest
speaker for the occasion. He will
give a general talk on Stanford.
Refreshments in the form of cook
ies and ice cream will be served in
addition to the usual cigarettes and
cigars, according to John Sumner,
smoker chairman. Entertainment
also will be given.
