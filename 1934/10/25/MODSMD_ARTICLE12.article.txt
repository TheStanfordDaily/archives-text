# Harriman, Bailey Talk To Philologists Today
## 
### 
The first monthly meeting of the
University Philological Society will
be held at 4:15 o'clock today in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. R. D. Harriman of the class
ical literature department will speak
on "Form or Function." Dr. Mar
gery Bailey of the English depart
ment also will talk. Her subject is
"Tony Lumpkin and the Sentimental
Comedy."
Refreshments will be served and
new officers elected following the
speakers' program. Dr. Aurelio M.
Espinosa, head of the Romanic lan
guages department and a nationally
known philologian, is the retiring
president.
Students and members of the fac
ulty are welcome.
