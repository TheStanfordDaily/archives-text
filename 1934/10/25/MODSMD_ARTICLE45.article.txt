# Dates for Ph.D. Exams In Science Announced
## 
### 
Departmental examinations in
science for the degree of Ph.D. have
been scheduled by the chemistry de
partment for 1 to 5 o'clock in room 18
of the chemistry building on the fol
lowing dates:
Tuesday, November 13—Inorganic
and organic chemistry.
Friday, November 18 — Physical
chemistry, biochemistry, biology,
physics and mathematics.
