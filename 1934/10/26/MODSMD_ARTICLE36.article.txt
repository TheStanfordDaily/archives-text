# WILD ODES
## 
### 
I hide behind a cardboard square,
To make a Trojan Horse, you see;
To guess 1 do not even dart—
Just which part of the horse is ME.
—8.H., '38.
Hot chocolate and cake were
served during a recent examination
at Louisville State College.
