# Rev. Gardner Will Give Sunday Morning's Sermon
## 
### 
The regular morning service will
be held in the Stanford Memorial
Church Sunday at 11 o'clock. This
week's preacher will be the Rev
erend D. Charles Gardner, chaplain ;
of the University.
Philip James' "Meditation" will
be heard as prelude to the service,
and the anthems will be "God Be in
My Head," by Davies, and "Jesu,
Joy of Man's Desiring," by Bach.
There will also be an organ re
cital given by Warren D. Allen at
4 o'clock.
