# Rough Stronghold Ranks High in Food Cleanliness
## 
### 
Inspection of health facilities in
connection with food preparation at
Sequoia hall and eating club showed
the rough stronghold high in clean
liness, it was learned yesterday from
Howard Russell, manager of Se
quoia's eating club.
Of the score of items inspected in
the Sequoia kitchen not a single fail
ure to maintain the best standard
was recorded by Miss Yvonne Fay's
inspecting force from B.A.C. on their
regular round of inspection of all
campus kitchens.
