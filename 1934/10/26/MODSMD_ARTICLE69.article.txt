# Toyon Club To Entertain With After-Game Jolly-Up
## 
### 
To honor visitors from the South
land, to provide an outlet for post
game enthusiasm, and to offer Stan
ford's hospitality to all, Toyon Club
is holding a Jolly-Up Saturday after
the game from 5 to 6 o'clock in the
Toyon lobby.
Offering A1 Rushton and a six
piece band and an hour of enter
tainment free, Toyon will welcome
everyone attending the game, as well
as others.
James Cay ton, manager of the
hall, promises appropriate decora
tions and all the pleasant trifles con
tributing to a good time. -
