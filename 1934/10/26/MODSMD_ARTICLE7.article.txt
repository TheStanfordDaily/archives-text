# Program for Mothers Annual Tea Announced
## 
### 
The days when bicyclists crowded
pedestrians off the walk up Palm
Drive will be recalled at the open
ing of the program for the Chrysan
themum Tea to be given by the
Stanford Mothers' Club Wednesday
afternoon in the Women's Gymna
sium.
Proper attire for cycling in the
'90's as well as for Quad and party
wear will be modeled by the girls
of '34. Costumes reminiscent of the
days intervening will be displayed
while Shelley Smith and Joe Lawry
read descriptive narrative.
Tickets are now on sale for $1 at
the A.S.S.U. office.
