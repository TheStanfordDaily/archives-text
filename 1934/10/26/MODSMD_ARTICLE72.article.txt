# Card Foilsmen To Meet Italians in S.F. Tonight
## 
### 
Varsity fencers will oppose the San
Francisco Uniona Sportiva Italiana,
Italian fencing club, tonight in the
gym of the Italian Club on Columbus
avenue in the City, in a Heron trophy
meet.
The Heron trophy is offered to the
best five-man team in the Bay area,
and is at present held by the Olym
pic Club.
Stanford has been defeated in two
of the five matches by the East Bay
Fencing Club, and Funke Fencing
Academy.
Next week the local fencers hope
to win from the stellar California ag
gregation in a meet to be held in the
Encina gym.
