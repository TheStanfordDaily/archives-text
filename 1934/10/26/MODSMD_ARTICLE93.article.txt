# BEARS WILL MEET WASHINGTON HUSKIES TOMORROW
## 
### 

California's Golden Bears and Washington's Huskies, both undefeated in Coast Conference play, meet in Seattle tomorrow afternoon. Wash
ington has to remember a 33 to 0 drubbing sustained last year, and experts say this may be fully revenged at the Seattle encounter. Left to right:
Calkins, California center developed by Coach Bill Ingram to replace the injured "Red" Christie; Muezynski, hard-hitting Husky fullback; Byron
Haines, sophomore back who has gone places this year; Arleigh Williams. Cal's superstar; and Art Ahonen, who always plays plenty of football
for Coach Jimmy Plielan. (Associated Press photos.)
