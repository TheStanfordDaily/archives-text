# Toyon Informal Bids To Go on Sale Tomorrow
## 
### 
Bids for the Toyon Informal, to
be held Friday, November 9, will go
on sale for two dollars at the A.S.S.U.
office tomorrow, with Toyon resi
dents only eligible to buy them until
this Friday.
Ernie Hecksher, who will provide
the music, intends to feature new
guitar arrangements and a coed
vocalist.
