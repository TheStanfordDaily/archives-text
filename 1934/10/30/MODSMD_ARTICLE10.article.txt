# Stanford Dames Convene Twice During This Week
## 
### 
Sewing group of the Stanford
Dames will meet, at the home of
Mrs. Philip A. Leighton, 455 Cor
nell, this afternoon at 2:30 o'clock.
Regular meeting of the Stanford
Dames will be held Thursday after
noon at 3 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. New officers will be
elected after which tea will be served
end a musical program given.
