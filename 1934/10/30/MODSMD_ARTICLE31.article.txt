# FIRST LADY AND MRS. HOOVER MEET
## 
### 

For the first time since President Roosevelt's inauguration, Mrs.
Franklin D. Roosevelt met Mrs. Herbert Hoover (right), at the confer
ence of Girl Scouts in Boston. Seated between Mrs. Roosevelt and Mrs.
Hoover is Mrs. Frederick Edey, New York, president of the Girl Scouts.
(Associated Press photo.)
