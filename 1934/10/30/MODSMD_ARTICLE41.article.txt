# Cap and Gown To Meet At Branner Home Today
## 
### 
Entertaining with an informal tea,
Mrs. jjohn C. Branner will open her
homes to members of Cap and Gown
for their meeting at 4:30 o'clock this
afternoon. Plans will be formulated
for the Cap and Gown tea honoring
all new women which will be held
at the home of President Ray Lyman
Wilbur on November 9.
A house party at the Thomas cabin
in Santa Cruz mountains is being
planned by members of the organi
zation for this next week-end.
