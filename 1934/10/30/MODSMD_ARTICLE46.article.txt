# Frank Guerena Appointed Lawyer for Trustee Body
## 
### 
Frank L. Guerena, famed coxswain
in the days when Stanford had a
racing crew, and now a well known
San Francisco attorney, yesterday
was appointed attorney for the Uni
versity Board of Trustees, succeeding
the late George Presley.
One of the greatest cox's in the
short crew history of the Farm,
Guerena was known to his fellow
students as "Husky." He was grad
uated in the class of 1911, and since
that date has served on the Alumni
Advisory Board, the Alumni Coun
cil, and the Board of Athletic Con
trol. Guerena also was formerly an
assistant to the state attorney
general.
