# Y.W.C.A. Racial Problems Group To Meet Today
## 
### 
California Racial Problems group
of the Y.W.C.A. will meet this after
noon at 4:30 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. Hobart Young will talk
on the Student Institute of Pacific
Relations and its coming conference.
Jean DeVoss, chairman of the racial
problems group, announces that
plans are to be made today for a tour
to the International Institute in San
Francisco.
Hendrik Van Loon is planning a
University of the Air.
