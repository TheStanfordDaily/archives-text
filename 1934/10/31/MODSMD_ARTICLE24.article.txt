# Big Game Tickets On Sale at B.A.C. Today
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the annual
Stanford-California Big Game are
now on sale at the B.A.C. for $1.65
and will continue to be sold through
Friday, November 2, after which
time no more may be purchased.
Extra tickets for the Big Game
and reserved seats for the Stanford-
Washington game to be played on
Saturday, November 10, may also
be bought at the B.A.C. this week.
Only 270 rooters' tickets for the Cal
game have been bought so far, ac
cording to Don Liebendorfer, man
ager, which is a relatively small
number.
