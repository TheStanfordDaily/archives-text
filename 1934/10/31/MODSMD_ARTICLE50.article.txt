# SCHOLASTIC PRESS ASSOCIATION MEETS HERE ON SATURDAY
## 250 Delegates Register For 11th Annual Meet 
### 
Two hundred and fifty delegates
from 40 schools have registered for
the eleventh annual convention of
the Central California Scholastic
Press Association to be held at Stan
ford University next Saturday.
Newspaper men, typographical ex
perts, and faculty members of the
division of journalism will address
the delegates on the various tech
nical aspects of school publications.
The principal speaker at the banquet
will be Philip Sinnott, western bu
reau manager of the Newspaper En
terprise Association.
The Central California Association
of Journalism Advisers will hold
their semi-annual meeting in con
junction with the student editors'
meeting.
