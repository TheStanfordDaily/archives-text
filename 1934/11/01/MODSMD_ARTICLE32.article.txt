# N.Y. Slickers
## 
### 
In I his up and down game of football your
guess is as good as mine, and the "expert,"
even the Daily's own Sylvestre Drofnats, has
no better chance of picking them than the kids
playing on vacant lots. Though you are
blessed with the gift of the prophets, and can
call 'em as they come, you are apt to run
afoul of the reefs if you back your choices
with your money.
Witness the sad case of those doughty seers
in New York City who were left holding the
well-known sack when the promoters of a
football pool look runout powders and de
parted wilh #130,000 of the customers' spoils.
.lust another major upset, fellows. Next
time, use stage money, or confine your bets
to milk shakes.
