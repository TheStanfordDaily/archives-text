# S.E.R.A. PROGRAM
## 
### 
In the SERA program the Univer
sity of Oregon was allotted $10,000
with which to carry on landscape
gardening, construction of new roads
and walks on the campus athletic
field.

fraternity houses gave dances in two
days last week-end.
