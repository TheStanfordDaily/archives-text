# Fairclough Returns From World Meeting in London
## 
### 
Dr. Henry Fairclough, professor
emeritus of classical literature, and
his wife returned to their campus
home yesterday after attending the
International Congress of Anthro
pological and Ethnological Sciences
in London during July and August.
Before returning to Stanford Dr.
Fairclough was a delegate at the tri
ennial General Convention of the
Episcopal Church in Atlantic City.
Last week he acted as a delegate for
the California branch of the English-
Speaking Union at its annual meet
ing and dinner in New York City. '
