# Education Honor Society To Hold Smoker Monday
## 
### 
Phi Delta Kappa, professional hon
orary fraternity in education, an
nounces a smoker next Monday night
for all men taking courses in the
School of Education. The smoker will
begin at 7:30 at the home of Dean
Grayson N. Kefauver, 610 Cabrillo
Avenue.
Holland D. Roberts will speak on
"Education Abroad." After the ad
dress, time will be set aside for a
discussion on current political events.
