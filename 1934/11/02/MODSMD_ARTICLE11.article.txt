# Wilbur Back On Monday
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur will be
back at his desk Monday, Helen E.
True, his secretary, announced yes
terday.
For the past two weeks Stanford's
president has been attending edu
cational meetings in Chicago and in
Nashville, Tennessee.
At the University of Chicago Dr.
Wilbur attended sessions of a meet
ing of the Association of American
Universities and the Council on
Medical Education 1 and Hospitals of
the American Medical Association.
At Vanderbilt University in Nash
ville he attended a meeting of the
Association of American Medical
Colleges.
Accompanied by Mrs. Wilbur, the
president has been gone since Oc
tober 22.
