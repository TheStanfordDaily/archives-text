# Y.W.C.A. Dinner Nears; Dr. W. J. Sherman Talks
## 
### 
Today is the last chance to buy
tickets for the all-membership din
ner which the Y.W.C.A. is giving to
morrow night at 5:45 o'clock in the
Union dining room. The tickets cost
50 cents, and are on sale at all wom
en's dormitories and sorority houses.
Dr. Walter J. Sherman of the First
Methodist church of Oakland will be
the guest speaker at the banquet.
He will base his talk on conditions
in Europe, on which he is well
versed, having traveled extensively
in Russia and on the rest of the
Continent.
