# Tennis Club Elects New Members After Tryouts
## 
### 
Members of the Women's Tennis
Club elected Katherine Kennedy,
Priscilla Merwin, and Jean Mc-
Dougall to membership, as a result
of tryouts held during the last two
weeks.
The tryouts who were not selected
are urged to practice and come out
again in February.
Seeded in the club tournament,
which will start this week, are Eliza
beth Resting, Betty Pearce, and
Margaret Died rich. The results of
this tournament will be the basis of
the regular tennis ladder.
