# NATION AT POLLS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 5 (/P). —'The
sovereign voters —48,000,000 strong—
rule today on the sway to be granted
the New Deal in Congress for the
next two years, who shall govern
33 states and whether to repudiate
prohibition in seven states.
With Democratic majorities in both
Senate and House a foregone con
clusion, the country contemplated
the unprecedented possibility of one
party dominating both congressional
branches by two-thirds.
