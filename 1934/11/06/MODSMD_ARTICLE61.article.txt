# LaPiere Will Speak At Toyon Smoker Tonight
## 
### 
"What To Do in the Next World
War" is the title of Professor Rich
ard T. LaPiere's talk to be given
before a Toyon smoker audience to
night.

The professor of the sociology de
partment will speak soon after
Toyon's smoker gets under way at
10 o'clock in the hall's lobby. John
Sumner, who has Arranged the en
tertainment, invites all residents of
the hall. Ice cream and cookies, in
addition to the customary cigars,
will be served.

A thief on the UniVersity of Illi
nois campus stole $52 and left a
note of thanks.
