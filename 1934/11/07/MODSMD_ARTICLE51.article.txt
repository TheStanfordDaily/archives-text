# Personnel Office Starts Class in Silent Reading
## 
### 
For students who desire to get
training in more effective reading, a
new section in silent reading is being
organized by the student person
nel oflice under the direction of Dr.
Charles G. Wrenn.
These classes will meet every
Monday night in room 200Q, next to
the Citizenship Reading Room, at
8:30 o'clock. Any student, either low
er or upper division, may enroll by
signing up with William M. Dan
ner, Jr., who will be the instructor
of the class. Dunner will be in
room 200Q tomorrow night at 8:30
to interview anyone who may want
to ask questions regarding the
course, and registration may be made
at that time.
