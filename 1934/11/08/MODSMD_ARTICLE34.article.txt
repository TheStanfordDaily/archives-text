# WHO WERE THEY?
## 
### 
The Editor. Sir:
We should like to use your column
to secure for ourselves certain in
formation, which would otherwise
be unobtainable.
Will the lovely brunette in the
charming white frock, who smiled
i so sweetly upon three young men as

she was walking up the Quad near
the Engineering Corner Tuesday
evening about 5:35, please oblige (if
she be interested), by dropping a
note of identification to a disinter
ested intermediary at Box 1177,
Stanford University.
Very sincerely yours,
—"Three Gentlemen"
