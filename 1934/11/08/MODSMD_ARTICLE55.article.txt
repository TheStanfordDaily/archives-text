# Interclass Tournament Scheduled in Hockey
## 
### 
Interclass hockey for women will
start November 19, reports Mrs.
Margaret Barr, who is directing the
games. A complete round robin will
be played, and if the weather per
mits, another will be played the first
week in December.
The following is the schedule for
the teams:
Nov. 19—Freshmen vs. sophomores
Nov. 21 —Freshmen vs. juniors, and
seniors vs. sophomores
Nov. 20—Juniors vs. seniors
Nov. 22—Sophomores vs. juniors
Nov. 23—Freshmen vs. seniors
