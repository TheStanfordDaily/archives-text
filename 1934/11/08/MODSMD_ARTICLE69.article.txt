# SCOTCH INVENTOR DIES
## 
### 
GLASGOW, Scotland, Nov. 7 (A*).
—Professor James Gordon Gray, one
of whose many inventions was the
inductor compass which assisted
Colonel Charles A. Lindbergh in his
transatlantic solo flight, died today.

' MINNEAPOLIS, Nov. 7 (/P). —
Huey Long's outspoken manner of
challenging Minnesota's football
team on behalf of Louisiana State
failed today to stir up the Gopher's
own coaches.
