# ELLEN McADOO TO WED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 8 (A 3 ).—
Young love found the way when
Miss Ellen Wilson McAdocf, 19-
year-old granddaughted of the
late President Woodrow Wilson,
and Rafael Lopez DeOnate, Span
ish motion picture actor, filed no
tice of their intention to wed here
today.
