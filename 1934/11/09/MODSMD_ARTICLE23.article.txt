# Committee on Theatre Will Discuss Finances
## 
### 
The new theatre committee will
meet Monday afternoon to discuss
finances and the actual beginning of
construction, Marvie Kahn, chair
man of committee, announced today.
Various aspects of the financing
problem will also be considered at
the meeting, Kahn says. Committee
members who have been working on
soliciting outside aid will report and
some decision will be reached as to
when the $1.25 assessment of each
student will be levied. The meeting
will be held in the A.S.S.U. presi
dent's office at 1:15.
