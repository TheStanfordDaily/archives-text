# CHAPLAIN TO SPEAK ON WAR AND PEACE
## 
### 
''The War and Peace," a subject
apropos to the spirit of the day, will
be the theme of the Rev. D. Charles
Gardner's Armistice Day address in
the Memorial Church at 11 o'clock
S.unday. The musical portion of the
program will include: "The Prayer
for Peace" by Paul Held; the chant
ing of the Lord's Prayer; Gounod's
"By Babylon's Wave" sung by the
University Chorus; and Buchanan's
"Wondrous Love" by the A Cappella
Choir.
Warren D. Allen will give an organ
recital in the Memorial Chapel at
4 o'clock Sunday.
