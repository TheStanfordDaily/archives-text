# FIGHTS CAR RACKET
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 8 (/P).—
Describing as a "labor racket" the
bringing of automobiles to California
by caravan, paying drivers only with
free transportation, Arthur L. John
son, attorney for the State Labor
Commission, opened a conference of
state and federal law forces today to
combat the practice.
A good team of reindeer will keep
up a speed of 10 to 12 miles an hour
for a full day in the early winter if
they are in good condition after
summer pasturing.
