# Cap and Gown Fetes New Women at Wilbur Home
## 
### 
All new women,* including gradu
ates, transfers, and freshmen, will
be entertained by members of Cap
and Gown, senior women's honorary,
at the President's house on Lagunita
Knoll from 4 to 6 o'clock this after
noon. The affair is the anual tea
which the organization gives each
fall for incoming women.
Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur, Dean
Mary Yost, Mrs. John C. Branner,
and Iris Forsythe, president of Cap
and Gowq, will form the receiving
line. Assisting about the rooms will
be active and alumnae members of
the chapter.
