# ALLEN GIVES RECITAL
## 
### 
Organist Warren D. Allen will give
a recital at Grace Cathedral in San
Francisco on Tuesday, November
13, at 8 o'clock. The cathedral or
gan, says Mr. Allen, is a remarkably
fine one.
