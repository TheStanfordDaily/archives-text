# International Table Meets in Union Today
## 
### 
The weekly International Table
will meet today in the Union Alcove
at 12 o'clock. This table affords any
student interested in any sort of in
ternational relations to gather and
talk in an informal session every
Monday noon.
No officers, dues, or formality is
observed. Students may drop in at
the table, and with a slight charge,
cat with the group. This phase of
student activity is conducted under
the A.S.S.U. International Commit
tee.
