# Chappies To Pick Ail-Americans In Big Game Issue
## 
### 
Fresh from Saturday's 6-G triumph
over the Daily staff team, the Chap
arral wits are exuberantly preparing
for their Big Game number that will
appear on the Thursday before the
Big Game, November 22.
In this issue the Doddering One,
a kleptomaniac from way back, is
stealing a march on America's fore
most sports writers and issuing
Chaparral's choice for All-Ameri
cans, many days ahead of the usual
selections.
The highest honor that can be
given to any football player, next to
a tuition note, the Chappie Ail-
American football selections will
bring joy and cheer to many a mort
gaged homestead and will fill many
a mother's eyes with tears of joy
and pride.
The manner of choosing the Chap
arral All-American football team
will be very official and importial.
The country's outstanding stars will
be selected by the Ancient one as he
scratches his All-American Beard
of Football.
