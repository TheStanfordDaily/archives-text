# San Francisco Stanford Women's Club To Meet
## 
### 
Russian literature will be dis
cussed at a meeting of the evening
Section of the Stanford Women's
Club of San Francisco on Thursday
evening at 6:15 dinner, to which stu
dents are invited.
The speaker will be Mrs. Elena A.
Varneck of the Hoover War Library.
Mrs. Varneck is a graduate of the
Imperial Educational Society for
Noble Maidens in Petrograd, run
under the auspices of the dowager
empress.
Reservations for the dinner may
be made through Mrs. Carol Greene
Wilson. There will be a charge of
50 cents. The meeting will be held
at "The Club," 1115 Gough Street.
