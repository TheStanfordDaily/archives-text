# Union Club Fall Informal Will Have Football Motif
## 
### 
King Football will be guest of
honor at the Women's Clubhouse to
morrow when the Stanford Union
Club holds its fall informal.
Varicolored streamers are to fea
ture a decoration scheme embodying
Stanford and U.C. colors.
Among patron and patronesses are
Dr. and Mrs. T. A. Storey, Miss Mary
Yost, and Miss Anastasia Doyle.
Ernie Heckscher's orchestra will
provide music.
