# TURNER SAILS FOR U.S.
## 
### 
SYDNEY, N.S.W., Nov. 14 MP).—
I Colonel Roscoe Turner and Clyde
1 Pangborn sailed today for America
i aboard the Mariposa .
. Thea Rasche, German airwoman,
also sailed on the vessel.
