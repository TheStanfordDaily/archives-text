# Skit, Speakers Planned For Rally Wednesday
## 
### 
Stanford rooters will gather at
the Fiji house at 7:15 o'clock Wed
nesday evening and, accompanied by
shouts, tooting, blazing torches, and
band music, will make their way to
the Pavilion for the annual Big
Game Rally.
A hilarious skit is being prepared
for the occasion, and several pz-omi
nent alumni are to speak. A definite
program will be announced early
next week.
Students may secure invitations
for families and friends Monday and
Tuesday at the A.S.S.U. offices. Ad
mission to outsiders will be granted
only by these printed cards.
