# Big Game Stunt Sketches Due by Tuesday Noon
## 
### 
Entrants in the Big Game stunt
contest, sponsored by the Rally Com
mittee, must have their sketches,
graphs, and plans in the hands of
Nancy Brayton, Pi Phi, or Dick
Stark, Phi Delt, by Tuesday noon.
Two men and one woman will be
awarded prizes which have been
donated by three Palo Alto stores.
The winners will be announced
Wednesday night at the Big Game
