# Sophs Overtake Juniors In Friday's Track Meet
## 
### 
Sophs forged from second place
into a strong lead over the juniors
in Friday's interelass track meet,
leaving the standings as follows:
sophs, 141: juniors, 124; frosh, 44;
seniors, 40; and grads, 19.
Best early season marks were reg
istered by Phil Levy with a winning
discus toss of 146 feet 7 inches, and
an illegal throw of 149 feet, and
Humbert Smith's G feet 2 inches in
