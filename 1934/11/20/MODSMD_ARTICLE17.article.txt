# DEAN YOST TO GO EAST
## 
### 
Miss Mary Yost, dean of women,
will leave Sunday for Washington,
D.C., to attend a meeting of the
board of directors of the American
Association of University Women.

Dade County, Georgia, is so cut
off from Ihe remainder of the state
by towering Lookout mountain that
all of its commercial and social con
tacts are with adjacent Tennessee.
