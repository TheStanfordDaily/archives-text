# ICE HOCKEY TEAM STARTS PRACTICE
## 
### 
With a stiff practice session already
behind them and another scheduled
for Sunday at the Oakland ice rink,
Stanford's recently organized winter
sports club is preparing a challenge
for the Hoover trophy to be awarded
intercollegiate champions of ice
hockey, skiing and skating at Yose
mite December 26 to 30 this year.
Stanford men interested in winter
sports are in demand to round out
the team of 16 which will take the
trip, all expenses paid, during Christ
mas vacation. Dick Kelley is pre
pared to arrange transportation to
Oakland for the practice Sunday for
anyone desiring to join the team.
