# Palo Alto Players Plan Barrie's "Mary Rose"
## 
### 
Palo Alto Community Players will
produce as their next play "Mary
Rose," a three-act fantasy by Sir
James M. Barrie, December 13, 14,
and 15. This play has been chosen
because of the popularity of plays
by Barrie or Shaw.
William Pabst, who is directing
the play, announced the following
cast: Mary Kose, Florence Brill; Mrs.
Otcry, Doris Church; Harry, William
A. Kilby; Mr. Morland, Mary Nich
ols; Simon Blake, Shannon Allen;
Mr. Amy, Ernest Dunbar; and Cam
eron, Robert MacKenzie.
