# Women Must Wear White, Carry Pompoms
## 
### 
Women who plan to sit in the
rooting section at the Big Game Sat
urday will be required to wear white
blouses or .sweaters and carry pom
poms, according to announcement
made by the Rally Committee las!
night.
Men must have rooters' hats to be
admitted to the section. They are
available at Wideman's in Palo Alto
for G5 cents.
Pompoms will be on sale this week
in the A.S.S.U. offices. The supply
is limited, and women who do not
have them are urged to buy them
immediately, said Chairman Julius
Jacobs.
