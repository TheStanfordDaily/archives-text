# Green Urges Laborers To Uphold Housing Plan
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 20 (/P).—To
the growing co-operative drive for
economic recovery tonight was added
a group of conservative critical
bankers, the leadership of NRA and
—finally—labor.
William Green, president of the
American Federation of Labor, ap
pealed to working men everywhere
to get behind the administration's
housing plan, which had been en
thusiastically endorsed by the busi
ness men leading the move for a
harmonious working relationship
with the administration.
