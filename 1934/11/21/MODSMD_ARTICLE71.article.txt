# Labor Workers Attempt To Settle Dock Disputes
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 20 (/P).—
The joint longshore labor relations
committee renewed efforts today to
iron out dock disputes as the San
Francisco local of the International
Longshoremen's Union announced
plans for foiming a "Pacific Federa
tion of Maritime Workers."
Harry Bridges, local I.L.A. presi
dent, said a resolution calling for
appointment of a committee to work
for such an organization was adopted
at a general I.L.A. meeting here last
night by an almost unanimous vote.
WARM SPRINGS, Ga., Nov. 20
(/P).—President Roosevelt broke his
quiet routine here today to receive
for a late afternoon call the gov
ernors of the southeastern states.
