# Organ Recital Features 'Autumn' Theme Today
## 
### 
"Autumn" will be the theme of
the recital played at 4:15 o'clock this
afternoon by Warren D. Allen in
the Memorial Chapel. "Leid des
. Chrysanthemes" of Jos. Bonnet and
"In Autumn" by Edward MacDowell
will be the main features of the
program relating to the theme. The
rest is as follows:
Matin provencal
Lied des chrysanthemes Bonnet
(from "Poemes d'autome")
Marche champetre A. J. Boex
Piaeludium Zolton Kodaly
In Autumn Edward MacDowell
Carillon
March for a Children's Festival...
Erie Delmarter
