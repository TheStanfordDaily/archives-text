# RALLYMAN LISTS NECESSITIES FOR ROOTING SECTION
## 
### 
Stanford women sitting in the
rooting section at tomorrow's
game with California will be
required to wear white blouses
or sweaters, according to Julius
Jacobs, chairman of the Rally
Committee.
Coeds must also carry pom
poms. They are available at the
A.S.S.U. offices today for 15
cents.
Men rooters must have root
ers' caps. They may be pur
chased at Wideman's in Palo
Alto for 65 cents.
Students will enter the Me
morial Stadium at Berkeley
through gate GG on the west
side of the bowl.
