# Dr. Carley To Be Honor Guest at Formal Dinner
## 
### 
Honoring Dr. Verna Carley, asso
ciate professor of education, the
Stanford chapter of Pi Lambda
Theta, national educational hon
orary fraternity, announces a formal
open dinner in the Union dining
room to be held Friday evening, De
cember 7, at 6:30 o'clock.
Dr. Carley will speak on "Current
Problems in Education" at the meet
ing. Other guests of honor will be
Miss Mary Yost, dean of women, and
Dr. Grayson Kefauver, dean of the
school of education. Reservations
may be made with Miss Irene Craig,
Stanford University local 51, at any
time between now and noon on De
cember 6. Tickets are 85 cents.
