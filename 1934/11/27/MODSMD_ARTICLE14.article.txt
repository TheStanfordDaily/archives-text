# German Club Members Plan Christmas Banquet
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Ger
man Club will hold their annual
formal Christmas banquet on De
cember Bat G: 15 o'clock in the Union
dining room, announces Claire
Mitchell, who is in charge of gen
eral arrangements for the affair.
Faculty members, students, and
their friends may make reservations
! through Henry Lindgren, secretary
of the club.
