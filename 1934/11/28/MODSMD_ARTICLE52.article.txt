# ANOTHER CAGLE PLAYS
## 
### 
BATON ROUGE. La., Nov. 27 (/P).
— Biff Jones, L.S.U. coach, who
coached the Army when "Red" Cagle
roved the plains, has another Cagle
under his tutelage. Unlike "Red"
this one, Otha. is a guard and Jones
has hopes of developing him into as
famous a player as Army's Cagle.
An air service will be established
between London and Aberdeen,
I Scotland.

| Asia has 140,754,000 acres of land
under irrigation. Thfs is 32 per cent
I of her entire area of 16,217,000 square
; miles and gives her first rank, in this
: respect, of all the continents.
