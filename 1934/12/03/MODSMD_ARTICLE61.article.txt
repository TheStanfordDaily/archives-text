# Allen To Feature Varied Program in Next Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, presents another in his regu
lar series of organ recitals Thursday
at 4:15 o'clock.
The program includes "Veni Em
manuel," prelude, Egerton; "Offer
tory on Two Christmas Carols,"
Guilmant; Christmas pastoral, from
"The Messiah," Handel; "In Dulci
Jubilo" (three versions), Bach; "Va
riations de Concert," Bonner.
