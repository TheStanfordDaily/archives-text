# HUNGARY PAYS 3 CENTS
## 
### 
BUDAPEST, Dec. 2 (/P).-Hun
garian clothing trade workers are
agitating for a 50 per cent raise in
piece-work rates. The pay for sew
ing a pair of trousers is 3 cents and
for a whole suit 35 cents.
