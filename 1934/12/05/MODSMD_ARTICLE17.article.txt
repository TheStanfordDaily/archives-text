# Dates Set for Payment Of Men's Room Deposits
## 
### 
Men now living in the Old Union
and Sequoia Hall who wish to re
serve their rooms for winter quarter
may do so by paying $5 at the office
of the Dean of Men tomorrow or
Friday.
Residents of Encina intending to
reserve rooms must pay their de
posits Monday, Tuesday, or Wed
nesday of next week, according to
Miss Vivienne Church, secretary to
Dean George B. Culver.
Drawings for rooms in Toyon and
Branner Halls will be held at 5:30
o'clock this afternoon in the hall
office.
