# Rushing Begins Friday For Freshman Women
## 
### 
Freshmen women are preparing
for their first official trips up the
Row when the nine sororities en
tertain with rushing teas on Friday,
Saturday, and Sunday afternoons.
Hours from 3 to 5:30 o'clock have
been set by Panhellenic for the
affairs.
Invitations were distributed in
Roble Monday morning and were
answered and returned to the
houses yesterday.
This week's entertaining brings to
a close the first period of rushing
dates, for transfer women were simi
larly entertained on November 16
and 18.
