# Women's Room Deposits Due Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
All room deposits for winter quar
ter must be paid at the office of the
Dean of Women today or tomorrow
between 9 and f2 o'clock, or from 2
to 5 o'clock.
"If a girl fails to pay the $5 de
posit within thii, time, without a
satisfactory explanation, it will be
assumed that she is not returning
winter quarter, and her room will
be re-allocated," said Alice Bodle.
