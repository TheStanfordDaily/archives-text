# Women's Room Deposits For Winter Due Today
## 
### 
All women who are returning win
ter quarter must pay $5 room de
posits at the office of the Dean of
Women today between 9 and 12
o'clock, and from 2 to 5 o'clock.
Alice Bodle announced that unless
a deposit is paid it will be assumed
that the woman is not planning to
return and her room will be re
allocated.
