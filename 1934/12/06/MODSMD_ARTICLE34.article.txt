# MRS. TURNER GREETS AIR ACE
## 
### 

Home again after teaming with Clyde Pangborn and Reed Nichols
to finish third in the London to Melbourne air race, Col. Roscoe Turner
was met by his wife aboard the Mariposa as the steamer came into Los
Angeles from the Antipodes. Colonel Turner is holding a toy lion mas
cot he carried with him throughout the long race. (Associated Press
Photo) ,
