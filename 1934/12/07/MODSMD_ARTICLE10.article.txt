# Grade Envelopes Due At Office of Registrar
## 
### 
Students who wish to receive their
grades for this quarter during the
Christmas vacation should leave a
stamped, addressed envelope at the
Registrar's Office before going home.
No grades will be given out except
by mail until registration day, Jan
uary 3.
Envelopes sold for the benefit of
Ihe Stanford Convalescent Home
may be obtained at the Registrar's
Office for 5 cents each. Grades will
be mailed out about December 2(5.
