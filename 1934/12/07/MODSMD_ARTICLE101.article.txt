# TRAVEL FORBIDDEN
## 
### 
BERLIN, Dcc. 6 (/Pi. — Joseph
Buerckel, the German commissioner
for Saar affairs, today forbade Nazi
storm troopers and members of the
Schutz Staffel (picked guards) to
travel into the Saar basin territory.
