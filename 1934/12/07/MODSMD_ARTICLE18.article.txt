# Silent Reading Courses Offered Next Quarter
## 
### 
Although classes aro\ filling up
rapidly, students who intend to take
the course on Techniques of Silenl
Reading (Psychology R) are re
minded that an entry of this course
be made in the study-cards.
This is a new course given next
quarter for lower division students
who need or want assistance in the
establishment of good reading habits.
A limited number of upper division
students can also be accommodated.
Three sections on this non-credit,
non-fee course are listed in the Time
Schedule: Monday, Wednesday, and
Friday at 11 o'clock; and Monday,
Tuesday, and Thursday at 7:30 and
8:30 o'clock in the evening. Sections
are limited to 20 students.
