# Play To Be Staged At Faculty Club Meeting
## 
### 
Production of a one-act play writ
ten by Mrs. Henry Lanz has been
planned for the meeting of the Fac
ulty Women's Club Monday after
noon at 3 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.

Christmas carols arranged by Mrs.
Charles Barker will be sung at in
tervals during the performance, at
the close of which the carolers will
appear in costume. Tea will be
served after the program.
The cast for the play includes Mrs.
George S. Parks, Mrs. Homer N.
Violette, Mrs. James Bradshaw, and
Professor Everett Parker Leslie.
