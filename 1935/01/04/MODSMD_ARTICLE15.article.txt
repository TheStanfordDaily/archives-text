# Masquers Offer Prize In New Drama Contest
## 
### 
Another contest for Stanford
drama writers has been announced
by Masquers, women's honorary
dramatic society. Similar to the
one held last quarter for three-act
plays, the current contest is for one
act plays only.
The prize-winning play will be
chosen by a committee of prominent
faculty members and students. The
best three plays will be given a
reading by Masquers on February 28.
Manuscripts may be submitted at
any time to Jean Albertson, Mas
quers' president. A deadline for
plays, about three weeks before the
performance, will be definitely an
nounced later.
