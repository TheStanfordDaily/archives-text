# Webster Granted Leave Of Absence for Quarter
## 
### 
Dr. D. L. Webster, head of the
physics department, has been grant
ed a leave of absence for the cur
rent quarter. Dr. P. A. Ross has
been appointed acting head of the
department, and Professor W. W.
Hansen will take charge of Dr. Web
ster's physics 31 class in mechanics
and heat.
Dr. Webster is now in Pasadena
with Mrs. Webster. He will return
to the campus in about ten days and
will then make further plans.
