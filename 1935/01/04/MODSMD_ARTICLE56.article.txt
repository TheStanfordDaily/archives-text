# Army Engineer Advises Rivers, Harbors Outlay
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 3
propriation of $155,000,000 for rivers
and harbors improvements in the
coming fiscal year was suggested to
day to Secretary Dern by Major
General E. M. Markham, chief of
army engineers.
He said a survey had disclosed
$93,816,610 could usefully be ex

Federal Emergency Administration
of Public Works; $47,164,115 on
maintenance and improvement of
projects authorized by Congress, and
