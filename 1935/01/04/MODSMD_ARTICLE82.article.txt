# DAVIDSON RETURNS
## 
### 
Dr. Percy E. Davidson, professor
of education, has returned to the
University after a leave of absence
of two quarters. Dr. Davidson has
been touring Europe the last quar
ter, including Germany, Italy, Rus
sia, and Poland in his itinerary.
During summer quarter he was an
exchange professor with Dr. C. C.
Peters of Pennsylvania State Col
lege.
