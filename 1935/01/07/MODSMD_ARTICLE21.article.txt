# Perry Moerdyke Wins Colonial Dames Contest
## 
### 
Perry Moerdyke, Jr., '3G, has been
adjudged the'winner of this year's
Colonial Dames essay contest, held
yearly by the Stanford Department
of History. His essay was entitled
"The Legal Training of Colonial
Lawyers" and was awarded the prize
of $100.
In 1933 the contest was a tie, and
the prize was awarded jointly to Ann
Archer and Cedric Larson. In 1932
the prize was won by Henry Madden
who is now taking advanced work at
Columbia University.
