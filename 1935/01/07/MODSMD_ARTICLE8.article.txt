# Shakespearean Comedy Roles To Be Cast Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for one of Shakespeare's
most popular comedies, "Twelfth
Night,", are to be held this evening
at 7:30 o'clock in the Little Theatre.
There are 14 roles to be cast which
vary in comic and serious intei-pre
tation.
Robert Balzer is directing the
reading, which will be presented in
about two weeks. Dr. Margery Bailey
will supervise.
