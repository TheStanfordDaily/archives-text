# Twelve Stanford Men On Air with Rushton
## 
### 
Twelve Stanford men are included
in the personnel of Al Rushton's or
chestra, now appearing at the Fair
mont Hotel on Friday and Saturday
nights, with their music being broad
cast over KYA at 10 o'clock. Five of
the men, registered at Stanford this
quarter, are Rushton, Bud Bern
hardt, Stan Brent, Forest Brown,
and Charlie Bubb.
