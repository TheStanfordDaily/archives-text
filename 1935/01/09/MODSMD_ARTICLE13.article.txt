# Rhodes Scholarship Recipients Announced
## 
### 
None of the students selected from
Stanford University were awarded
one of the Rhodes Scholarships.
John Lyman, Leo Cook, Richard Van
Wagenen, and Glenn Martineau were
the Stanford men selected by the
University committee.
Four of the eight scholarships
awarded for the district of Nevada,
Utah, Arizona, Colorado, New Mex
ico, and California went to Herbert
Merillat of the University of Arizona,
John Espey, Occidental College;
Horace Davenport, California Insti
tute of Technology.
Four other candidates were chosen
at Spokane, for the district of Wash
ington, Oregon, Idaho, Wyoming,
Montana, and North Dakota.
The eight scholarship holders will
enter Oxford next October.
