# Medical School Seniors To Study Health Board
## 
### 
Dr. Ernest C. Dickson; of the De
partment of Public Health and Pre
ventive Medicine, Stanford School of
Medicine, will bring his senior class
to Palo Alto soon to investigate the
organization and operation of the
health department.
The Palo Alto Board of Public
Health at its last meeting acceded
to Dr. Dickson's request for permis
sion to show his students the work
ings of the Palo Alto department.
