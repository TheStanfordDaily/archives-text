# DENY KING'S DIVORCE
## 
### 
ROME, Jan. 8 (/P). —A spokesman
for former King Alfonso of Spain
today denied Parisian reports that
a legal separation of Alfonso and his
wife was impending. Alfonso is
spending the winter here.
