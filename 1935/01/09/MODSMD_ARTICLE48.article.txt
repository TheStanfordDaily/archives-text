# AROUND THE DIAL...

Oswald Garrison Willard, noted
journalist, will speak on "The Saar
Valley and the Peace of Europe"
over KFRC at 1.
"Republican Policies in Congress"
will be discussed by Representative
Hamilton Fish. Jr., during a talk over
KPO at 4.
John McCormack returns to the
air for the first of four performances
over KPO at 6:30. William Daly's
orchestra will accompany him.
Frances Maddux will be the guest
star appearing with Lanny Ross and
Harry Salter's orchestra over KPO
at 8:30.
Other good programs: Kate
Smith's Matinee, noon, KFRC; Gould
and Shefter, 4:30, KPO; Andre Kos
telanetz, 6. KFRC; One Man's Fam
ily, 7:30, KPO; Fred Allen, 9, KPO.
Best dance music: Williams-Walsh,
10, KGO; Tom Coakley, 10:15, KPO;
Orville Knapp, 10:30, KFRC; Ted
Fio-Rito, 11, KGO: Dick Jurgens, 11,
KFRC; George Hamilton, 11:30,
KFRC.
