# Thursday Organ Recitals To Feature Bach's Music
## 
### 
Beginning tomorrow afternoon at
4:15 o'clock, Warren D. Allen will
begin a series of Bach programs dur
ing which he will play all the Chor
ale-Preludes, together with the Six
Trio-Sonatas of J. S. Bach.
These programs will be heard
every Thursday at the same time,
while at the Sunday recitals Mr.
Allen will continue to play his usual
varied programs.
