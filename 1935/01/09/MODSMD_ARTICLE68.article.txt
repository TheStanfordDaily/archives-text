# ORGANIZATIONS SIGN FOR ENTRANCE INTO WINTER QUARTER INTRAMURAL ACTIVITIES
## 
### 
Lists are now posted in Enema
Gymnasium for recording entries for
winter quarter intramural activities
in swimming, basketball, and tennis.
Yesterday over 20 teams had
signed in each of the three sports to
comprise the intramural schedule
this quarter. The individuals who
sign for their organizations should

also record telephone numbers and
other data needed for the intramural
managers to contact everyone at the
time intramural play begins.
The absence of Intramural Man
ager Marvie Kahn has delayed ac
tivity slightly, but on his return and
as soon as entry lists are complete,
actual play will begin.
