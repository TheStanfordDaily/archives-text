# Chance for Tri-Power Naval Agreement Grows
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Jan. 8 (A').—Pres
ident Roosevelt and Secretary Hull
have been informed by Norman H.
Davis, American delegate at the
London Naval talks, that there is
a good chance of the United States,
Great Britain, and Japan reaching
an agreement on naval limitation
within another year.
