# Yell Leader Tryoutees Get Final Test Tomorrow
## 
### 
After one and one-half months of
practicing, final tryouts for assistant
yell leaders will be held at 1:15
o'clock tomorrow in the Pavilion, be
fore the Rally Committee.
Six candidates are to be selected
to run on the student body ballot in
February. All Rally Committeemen
and tryoutees are urged to be pres
ent at the final trials.
