# AROUND THE DIAL...
## 
### 
"The Movies In and Out of
School" will be the topic "of a talk
by Dr. W. W. Charters at 2 o'clock
over KGO.
Senator James P. Pope of Idaho
and Professor Manley O. Hudson of
Harvard Law School will discuss the
League of Nations and world affairs
over KGO at 3.
Phil Spitalny's 30-piece orches
tra of women will feature "You're
the Top," "Siboney," and "Vilia" in
their program from KFRC at 5.
Gaetano Merola will conduct the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra
over KPO at 8:15. The program will
include the "Orpheus" overture,
Boccherini's Minuet, the Ballet Mu
sic from "Lakme," Rimsky-Korsa
koff's ' Dance of the Clowns," and the
introduction to Act 111 of "Traviata."
Other good programs: Rudy Val
lee's Variety Hour, 5, KPO; Show
Boat. 6, KPO; Fred Waring, 6:30,
KFRC; Glen Gray, 8:30, KFRC;
Waltz Time, 10:15, KPO.
Best dance music: Richard Him
ber, 4, KGO; Eddie Duchin, 8:30,
KGO; George Olsen, 9, KGO; Leon
Navara, 9:30, KFRC; Orville Knapp,
10:30, KFRC; Ted Fio-Rito, 11, KGO;
Jimmy Grier, 11:30, KGO.
