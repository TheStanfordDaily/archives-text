# Cardinals Will Give Dance After Play Performance
## 
### 
At the meeting of the Cardinals
last night it was decided that a dance
will be held in the Women's Gym
nasium on February 8, following the
Dramatics Council's presentation of
"Her Master's Voice." Admission
will be nominal.
The committee members received
their rings, which are silver with a
led axe on them. Music Manager
Barnes, who was to inform the Card
inals of their duty in publicizing the
Concert Series, was unable to at -
tend the meeting.
