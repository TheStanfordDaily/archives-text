# Campus Thespians Have Leads in Palo Alto Play
## 
### 
Shelley Smith and Neal Berry, two
campus thespians of note, have the
leads in the one-act comedy, "Birds
of a Feather," to be presented this
evening on the workshop program of
the Palo Alto Community Players
at Ihe Civic Theatre on Middlefield ,
Road.
On the same program will be a
group of modern dances, offered by
the Henrietta Greenhood ensemble.
Special lighting and color effects
have been designed by Fred Frey
and Walter Newcomb.
George Kelly's satirical one-act
comedy, "The Flattering Word," :
completes the bill.
