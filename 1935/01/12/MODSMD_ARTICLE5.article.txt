# Rushing Cards To Be Used In Survey
## 
### 
Rushing cards used in this year's
selection of houses and freshmen
for teas and meals will be com
piled by Pan Hellenic, to be used
as a basis for study of rushing
systems, with a view toward im
proving the method used this year.
Kacli year several regulations
are changed, with a view to mak
ing rushing less strenuous for both
the Row and the new freshmen,
and to keep illegal rushing to the
minimum.
Results of the survey, to be be
gun soon, will be used in deter
mining what, if any, changes are to
be made in the system.
