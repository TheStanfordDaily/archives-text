# Applications for Degrees Due Before January 30
## 
### 
Students in their .second quarter
of graduate work, or beyond, who
expect to complete requirements for
the degree of Master of Arts or
Engineer at the close of the spring
quarter, are reminded that applica
tions for admission to candidacy
must be filed with the Registrar
before January 30.
Applicants for these degrees who
are completing all requirements,
including the thesis, at the end of
the present quarter should make
sure that their applications for the
degree are on file in the Registrar's
Office.
