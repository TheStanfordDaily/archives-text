# Six Yell Tryoutees Await Election February 12
## 
### 
With final selection of assistant yell
leader candidates completed, six as
pirants for the two cheer offices must
wait until February 12, election day,
for the final choices to be made by
the student body.
Also to be selected next month is
the head yell king to succeed Jim
Triolo, present cheer director. One of
his two assistants, Dick Stark or Ed
Butterworth, will be elected to the
office.
Picked by Triolo to run for the
assistant positions are Dick Bib
bero, Talbot Shelton, Bill Molineux,
Clem Smoot, Web Jones, and Bob
Razoni.
