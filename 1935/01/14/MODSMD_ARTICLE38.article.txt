# LUPE SEEKS PEACE
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Jan. 13 (/P).—For
i the umpteenth time, Lupe Velez and
Johnny Weismuller are considering i
reconciliation. The muchly disagree
and-makeup movie pair met in a
night club, and Weismuller today
admitted that they had "talked
1 things over" and that Miss Velez'
suit for divorce now on file here may
be dismissed.
