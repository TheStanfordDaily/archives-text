# Encina Club Holds First Smoker of Term Tonight
## 
### 
The Encina Club will hold the
first of its winter quarter smokers
in the lobby of Encina tonight from
9 to 11 o'clock. The gathering will be
somewhat like a basketball lally, ac
cording to Bud Desenberg, chairman
of the club's smoker committee.
Coach John Bunn, whose team reg
istered the exciting victory over the
Trojans last Saturday, will speak.
Jimmy Triolo and Wally Baird
have promised to put on an act for
the freshmen's entertainment. Ten
tative arrangements also call for the
appearance of Doodles Weaver.
Desenberg promises free cigarettes
for all those who attend.
