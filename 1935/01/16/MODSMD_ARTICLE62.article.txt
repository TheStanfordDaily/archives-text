# DEAN YOST AT HOME
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will be at home
this afternoon from 4 to 6 o'clock for
all women, with freshmen especially
invited. Elizabeth Watson, Florence
McCormick, Mary Valentine, and
Margaret Williams will serve as
hostesses.

day at 1:15 o'clock in the Old Union j
to elect new members.
