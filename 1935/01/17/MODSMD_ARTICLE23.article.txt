# Today Last Time To Alter Study Lists Without Fine
## 
### 
Study lists for this quarter may be
changed without fine for the last
time today. After today, students
may make program revisions by pe
tition to the Registration Committee,
subject to the following fines: $2 for
each course added or dropped, or $!>
where one course is substituted for
another.
Any changes made in program
since registration day should be
filed at the Registrar's office on the
regular revised study list cards.
