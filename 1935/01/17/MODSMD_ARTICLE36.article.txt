# Orchids and Brickbats
## 
### 
For yesterday's swill and spectacular
prison break and recapture that ended before
it had a chance to get well started, the citi
zens of California should have mingled feel
ings of disgust and pride.
Disgust for the sorry state of affairs within
prison walls that permitted convicts to make
prize boobies of the custodians, and pride for
the vigilant country police that effected a
quick and decisive termination of the affair.
