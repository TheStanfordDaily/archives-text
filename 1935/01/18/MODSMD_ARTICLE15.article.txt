# Men's Last Rush Cards Ready for Sale Today
## 
### 
Preparation for the last official
period of men's rushing begins to
day with the sale of rush cards
opening in the A.S.S.U. offices at 9
o'clock.
Climaxed by pledging a week from
today, the last men's rushing period
of the year will include lunch and
dinner dates on Tuesday and Wed
nesday and dinner on Thursday.
Representatives of fraternities may
obtain the cards in the student office
in the Old Union between the hours
of 9 and 5 o'clock. *
