# Christian Scientists Will Hear Chicago Lecturer
## 
### 
At 7:30 o'clock on February in
Women's Clubhouse the Christian
Science Organization at Stanford
will present a lecture on Christian
Science by Bicknell Young, C.5.8.,
of Chicago, Illinois.
Lecturer Young is a member of
the Board of Lectureship of tha
Mother Church, The First Church of
Christ, Scientist, in Boston, Mass.
The title of his lecture will be an
nounced shortly.
