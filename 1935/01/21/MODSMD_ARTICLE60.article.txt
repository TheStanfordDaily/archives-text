# Professor B. Q. Morgan Will Lecture Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Pagtimes of a Professor" will be
the subject of a talk by Professor
E. Q. Morgan, executive head of the
Germanic languages department to
morrow evening at 7:30 o'clock in the
Little Tli eat re. The lecture is under
the auspices of the Committee on
Public Exercises and is one of the
Tuesday Lecture Series.
Professor Morgan came to Stan
ford last fall from the University of
Wisconsin, where he had been a
member of the German department
since 1907. Professor Morgan took
his A.B. degree at Trinity College,
Connecticut, in 1904 and received his
Ph.D. at the University of Leipzig
in 1907.
