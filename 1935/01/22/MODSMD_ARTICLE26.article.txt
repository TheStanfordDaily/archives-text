# Spanish Professor Back From Mexican Air Trip
## 
### 
Dr. Alfred Coester, professor of
Spanish American literature, has re
turned to the campus after an air
plane trip to Mexico City, where he
purchased more than 100 volumes of
Mexican literature for the Stanford
Library.
He attended the annual meeting of
the American Teachers of Spanish
during Christmas vacation at Austin,
Texas. He is editor of "Hispania.'
the organization's official publication.
Professor Coester Hew from Austin
to Mexico City via Brownsville. The
route follows the shortline of tho
Gulf of Mexico as far as Tampico,
and then in a straight line rising
above the Sierra Madre Mountains
that encircle the valley of Mexico.
