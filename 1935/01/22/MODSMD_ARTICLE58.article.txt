# Women's Gym Opened To Badminton Players
## 
### 
Every Wednesday night from now
on badminton will be played in the
Women's Gym by both men and
women from 7:30 till 10 o'clock.
All those interested, whether hav
ing rackets or not, are asked to at
tend so that an estimate can be
made of the number wishing to play.
Several Palo Alto players will be
in attendance to help with instruc
tion of those not knowing the rules
of the game.
