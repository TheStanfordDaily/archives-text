# BOETTIGERS RETURN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 21 (4 V ). —
Mrs. Franklin D. Roosevelt has re
ceived word her daughter and new
son-in-law, Mr. and Mrs. John Boe*-
tiger, have returned to New York
from their week-end honeymoon.
