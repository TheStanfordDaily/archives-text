# Sword and Sandals Hold Drama Tryouts Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Fear," a product of the new Sov
iet drama, is to be the next reading
by Sword and Sandals. Tryouts for
roles in this production will be held
from 3 to 5 o'clock tomorrow after
noon in the Little Theatre.
As always, Sword and Sandals
welcomes talented newcomers to
their casts. A variety of interesting
parts provides opportunity for many
aspiring actors. Robert Dwan will
cast and direct the reading.
