# Home Engineering Group To Hear Vocational Talk
## 
### 
Mrs. Alice Ingrim Bodle will talk
to the Y.W.C.A. Home Engineering
Group today at 5 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Mrs. Bodle will talk on the courses
offered at Stanford and other col
leges which will help students in
home-making and in business life
after college, and will be interesting
to upperclassmen, as well as fresh
men and sophomores.
