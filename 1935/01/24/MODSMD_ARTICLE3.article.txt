# Civil Engineering Society Initiates 13 New Members
## 
### 
Stanford chapter of the American
Society of Civil Engineering initiated
the following new members last
week:
Mary Atkins, Arthur Allen, John
Cahill, John Crooker, Nelson Davis,
John Parlett, Phil Prescott, Charles
Richards, George Solnar, G. Helton
Steele, Tod Stice, Morgan Williams,
and Margaret Woolverton.
