# REBELLION QUELLED
## 
### 
ATHENS, Jan. 23 </P). — Greek
newspapers said today many persons
were killed and injured when a re
volt of Greek inhabitants of the
Italian-owned island of Kalymonos
was quelled by aviators and marine
detachments.
