# CALIFORNIA CREW STRETCHES MUSCLES ON ESTUARY
## 
### 

Coach Ky Enbright of California's champion eight-oared boat, has started sending his charges through
their paces on the Oakland, Cal., estuary preparatory to the regatta season. Some of the oarsmen out for
work are (left to right) Richarc Burnley, Laurence Dodge, Ferd Elvin, Ray Andreson, Carrol Brigham,
Kvald Swanson, Jack Yates, Frank Dunlap and (kneeling) Regg Watt coxswa'n. (Associated Press Photo)
