# Union Club Sponsors Radio Dance Saturday
## 
### 
Third of a series of six Saturday
night radio dances sponsored by the
Union Club during the quarter will
be held in the Women's Clubhouse
from 9 to 12 o'clock Saturday eve
ning.
This is the last dance to be given
in January, and as usual is open to
all men on the campus. Floor com
mittee for the dance will be Hester
McMasters, Mary Ellen Leary, and
Constance Wayland.
