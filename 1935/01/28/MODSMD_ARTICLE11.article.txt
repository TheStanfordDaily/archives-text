# Prizes Await Best Writing
## 
### 
Original manuscripts for the sixth
volume of the Yearbook of Stanford
Writing are solicited from students
in any department by the English
Club.
Submitted writings should be
placed in a box in the English de
partment office for that purpose
some time this quarter. Editor Frank
Dor'cey has announced April 1 as the
deadline date.
Two prizes will be awarded the
authors of the works judged best by
a group of judges yet to be selected.
One prize of $25 will be set aside for
poetry; another, for prose, which may
be of either essay or fiction form.
Published every May by the Eng
lish Club, the Yearbook of Stanford
Writing offers the only medium in
the University for student literary
writing in all forms.
