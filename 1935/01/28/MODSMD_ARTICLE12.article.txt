# BOURGEOIS DISAPPEARS
## 
### 
BATON ROUGE, La., Jan. 27 (/P)j
—E. J. Bourgeois, president of the
Square Deal Association, opposing
the dictatorship of U.S. Senator Huey
Long, has disappeared, his adherents
declared today as they pressed a
vigorous search for him.
