# LONG'S FORCES PROTECTED
## 
### 
BATON ROUGE. La., Jan. 27 I/Pi.
—Senator Huey Long's dictatorship
adherents tonight had increased Na
tional Guard protection at the seat of
their government as the Square
Dealers flatly denied responsibility
for last night's armed uprising.
