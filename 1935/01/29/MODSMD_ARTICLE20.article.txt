# Vaccination Certificates Must Be in by Thursday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at
the Registrar's Office Thursday for
all new students, including those
having permits to attend classes, and
al! other students whose certificates
have expired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
o'clock Thursday will result in a fine
of $2; or if not filed by February 7
registration in the University will be
subject to cancellation by the Com
mittee on Public Health.
