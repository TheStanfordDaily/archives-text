# Spanish Group To Honor Mexico University Men
## 
###

honorary society, will entertain three
visitors from the University of Mex -
ico at dinner Thursday evening. The
dinner will be held at Miller's Char
coal Broiler on El Camino Real.
The group will meet in front of
the Women's Clubhouse at 6:15
o'clock. Reservations may be made
with John T. Reid at the Spanish de
partment in room 24i.
