# Frosh Baseball Tossers Drop Practice Tilt, 6-1
## 
### 
The frosh baseballers yesterday
suffered a 6 to 1 defeat at the hands
of the Mountain View Club, in one
of their first practice games.
The game was air-tight as to
pitching, but both teams erred often.
Final statistics revealed the frosh to
have scored 1 run, 3 hits, and 3 er
rors, and Mountain View to have
made 6 runs, 4 hits, and 2 errors.
