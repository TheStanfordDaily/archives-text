# Badminton Players Will Meet Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
All men and women interested in
Badminton are asked to come to the
Women's Gym tomorrow night at
7:30 o'clock, according to Miller
Freeman, president of the Badmin
ton Club.
After a good turnout last week,
says Freeman, it is expected that at
least one more night a week may be
obtainable for playing.
A short meeting of the club will be
held at 8:30 o'clock during the same
evening.
