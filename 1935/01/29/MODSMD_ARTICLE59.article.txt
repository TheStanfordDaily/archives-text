# MOONEY APPEALS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 28 (A I ).—Tom
Mooney will appeal.for a rehearing
of his contention that he has ex
hausted all avenues to freedom
through the California courts, John
F. Finerty, an attorney, announced
today.
Igor Sikorsky, great authority on
aircraft designing, was inspired in
the lifting possibilities of air while
watching his father's shirts flapping
on the line.
