# Lagunita Court To Hold Informal Friday Night
## 
### 
Colored spotlights will illuminate
the fountain in the open court dur
ing Lagunita Court's informal, Fri
day evening, in the Lagunita dining
hall.
Ernie Heckscher and his orches
tra will provide the music and .spe
cialty numbers from 9 to 1 o'clock.
Programs for the dance show a pen
drawing of the entrance of Lagunita
Court designed by Helen McKee.
Refreshments will be served from
11 to 12 o'clock. In the social rooms
of Casas Adelfa and Naranja.
