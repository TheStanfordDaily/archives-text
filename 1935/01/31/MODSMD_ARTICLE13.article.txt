# Slight Rainfall Raises Total to 9.83 Inches
## 
### 
A .slight rainfall continuing for
less than an hour Tuesday night
added .05 of an inch to the Palo
Alto seasonal total, now figured at
9.83 inches.
A year ago the rain total was 5.35
inches, and on the corresponding date
in 1915, the wettest year on record,
it was 12.09 inches.
