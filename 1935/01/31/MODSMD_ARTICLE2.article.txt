# Hold Service Saturday For Professor Brown
## 
### 
Funeral services for Professor Bu
ford O. Brown will be held at 10
o'clock Saturday morning in tha
First Methodist church ot Palo Alto
with the Reverend R. H. Silverton,
assistant pastor, conducting the cere
mony.
Professor Brown was found dead
in his car Tuesday, after having com
mitted suicide.
Active pall bearers will be: Pro
fessor Chilton R. Bush, Clifford
Weigle, Col. Edwin P. Wolfe, A. B.
Clark, Professor Charles Moser, and
Judge E. D. Lakin. Six cousins of
Professor Brown will act as honor
ary pall bearers. Interment will be
in the Alt a Mesa cemetery in San
Jose.
