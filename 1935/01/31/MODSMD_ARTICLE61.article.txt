# Roble Women Will Hold Formal Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Roble's annual invitational formal
will be held tomorrow evening from
9 to 1 o'clock in the Dance Studio of
the Women's Gymnasium.
The theme of the affair is "danc
ing among the stars," with all the
decoration and the lighting carrying
out this motif. Gordon Heche's ten
piece orchestra, including two pianos, I
will supply the music. i
