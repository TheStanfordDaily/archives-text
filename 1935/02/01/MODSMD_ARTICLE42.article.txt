# Control of Obesity Will Be Topic of Medical Lecture
## 
### 
The third in a series of popular
medical lectures will be given at 8
o'clock this evening at Lane Hall,
on the corner of Webster and Sacra
mento Streets, in San Francisco.
The talk will be given by Dr.
Maurice L. Tainter, who will have
as his subject "Dinitrophenol in the
Control of Obesity."
Dr. Tainter is associate professor
of Pharmacology in the Stanford
Medical School, under whose aus
pices the lecture will be given. The
public is invited to attend.
