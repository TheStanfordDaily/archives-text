# LINDY HAS BIRTHDAY
## 
### 
FLEMINCTON, N.J., Feb. 3 (/TV-
Col. Charles A. Lindbergh has his
33rd birthday tomorrow- the 24th
day of the trial of Bruno Richard
Hauptmann on a charge of murder
ing the flier's infant son.
