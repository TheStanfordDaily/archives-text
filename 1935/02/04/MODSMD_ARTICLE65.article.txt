# MACHINE GUN FOUND
## 
### 
RENO, Feb, 3 (/I 5 ). —A sub-ma
chine gun found cached in the
Truckee River near here was sent
today to Washington, where the De
partment of Justice will attempt to
trace its ownership.
Studies of rainfall and erosion
made in California revealed that in
some pine tree areas the trees in
tercepted and gave off through evap -
oration from their crowns about 50
per cent of all rains.
