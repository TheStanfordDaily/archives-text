# Winter Syllabus Fees Must Be Paid Today
## 
### 
Winter quarter syllabus fees must
be paid sometime today in the lobby
jf the Administration Building,
which will be open from 8 to 12 and
From 1 to 5 o'clock.
All those who do not pay their
fees today will be fined two dollars,
and if payment is not made before
February 12, registration will be
subject to 'cancellation.
Checks mailed in an envelope
post-marked February 5 will be
accepted.
