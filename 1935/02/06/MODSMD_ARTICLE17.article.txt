# Bobby Grayson Turns Down 'Pro' Wrestling Offer
## 
### 
Bobby Grayson, Ail-Ameri
can football star, has declined
offers of becoming a profes
sional wrestler, it was an
nounced recently.
Grayson turned down an
offer of a professional wrestling
promoter. The offer guaran
teed among other things that
Grayson would not lose a
match in his first year of com
petition.
In order to keep in training
for his last football season next
fall, Grayson has been work
ing out on the wrestling mat
in the Encina Gymnasium. He
.will confine his wrestling ac
tivities to 1 his extent.
