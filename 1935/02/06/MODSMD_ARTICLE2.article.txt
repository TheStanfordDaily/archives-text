# Farm Rainfall Has Three Inch Edge on Last Year
## 
### 
Rainfall of the last few days has
raised the seasonal total for Stan
ford to 10.41 inches. Seasonal rain
fall at this date a year ago on the
campus totaled 7.08 inches, more
than three inches behind the total
for this season.
In order that the level of Lagu
nita will not rise to a high point,
the flume emptying into the lake
from San Francisquito creek is par
tially closed.
