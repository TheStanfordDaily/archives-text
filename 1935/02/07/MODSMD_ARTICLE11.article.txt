# Ski-Jumps, Tyrol Views Shown in Film Tonight
## 
### 
Ski-jumping, mountain views,
and scenes from Austrian life
will be shown in the Little Theatre
tonight in four reels of motion pic
tures presented under the auspices
of the Department of Germanic
Languages.
The first reel is of outstanding
places and buildings in Vienna,
while the second shows mountains
in the Austrian Tyrol and views of
Salzburg and other Austrian centers.
Winter sports, particularly pic
tures taken at a ski-ing school in
tin- Austrian Tyrol, comprise the
last two reels. Various tricks of ski
jumping are demonstrated by skilled
jumpers.
