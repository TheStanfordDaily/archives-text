# Sale Remains Open On Bids for Toyon Formal
## 
### 
Although a few bids for the Toyon
Club Formal are still obtainable for
$3 a bid, students arc urged by Bob
Elfving, dance committee chairman,
to buy theirs as soon as possible.
Programs, which may be gotten
today at the A.S.S.U. office, feature
a black, gold, and silver color motif.
The cover is of black suede leather
with a gold colored print of a castle
in the upper left hand corner. On
the lower l ight side of the cover is
printed "Toyon Castle" in gold col
oicd letters. A silver paper insert
projects about a quarter of an inch
outside the cover to finish the color
