# 'DIXIE' HOWELL NOW GOING IN FOR MOVIE CAREER
## 
### 

Millard "Dixie" Howell, All-American of Alabama, who created a sensation in the Pasadena, Cal.,
Rose Bowl, was given a screen test by a Hollywood studio. The gridiron ace went to the film colony
secretly and returned immediately after the test. Left to right: Eole Galli, Test Director Jack Foley,
Supervisor Henry Mac Rae and Howell. (Associated Press Photo)
