# Bid Sale Starts For Cap and Gown Dance
## 
### 
Bids go on sale in the A.S.S.U.
office today for the "Perm" dance
in the Roble Gym next Friday night.
This informal is to be sponsored by
Cap and Gown as a part of the cele
bration of the twentieth birthday of
the Women's Clubhouse. Jimmy
Triolo will be master of ceremonies.
Rich Egyptians often sat a skele
ton at their feasts as a reminder of
death.
