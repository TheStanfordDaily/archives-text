# Cellar Lowers Price Of Orange, Tomato Juices
## 
### 
This morning one will be able to
go into the Cellar, order a glass of
orange juice—the kind which for
merly brought about a remittal of
twenty cents —and find that ho is
only being charged fifteen cents for
the same sized glass, according to
Miss Christine Ricker, director of
dining halls and of the Stanford
Union.
There is a corresponding reduc
tion in the price of tomato juice for
those who indulge in this delicacy
