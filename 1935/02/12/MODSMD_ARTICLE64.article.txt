# MAY RESTRICT LIQUOR
## 
### 
PHOENIX, Ariz., Feb. 11 (A>) • A
proposed state liquor code, designed
to place stringent regulations on the
manufacture and sale of alcoholic
beverages in Arizona, and increas
ing license fees, was introduced in
the House of Representatives today.
