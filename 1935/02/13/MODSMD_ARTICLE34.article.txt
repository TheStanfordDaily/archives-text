# Women Will Foil at Mills
## 
### 
Five Stanford women are fencing
in the Prep Individual Foils meet at
Mills College Saturday at 2:30
o'clock. This meet is open to those
never having fenced in meels before
and will include participants from
Mills, California, and Stanford.
Stanford delegates are Beth Doyle,
Barbara Brenk, Birdie Boyles, Mar
jorie Prager, and Mary Agnes King,
who is manager of the team.
Medals for first and second place
will be awarded. The Novice Indi
vidual Foils Tournament is the sec
ond in the series and will be at
Stanford March 2. Berkeley will
be the scene of the third match.
