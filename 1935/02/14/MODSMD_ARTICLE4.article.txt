# Quads on Sale Today In Front of Post Office
## 
### 
Quads will be on $ale from 8 to
12 o'clock today in front of the post
offiee. The 1935 yearbook may be
purchased on the installment plan
with a down payment of $3.15, and
tho remaining $2.50 payable when or
before the book arrives.
Organization pledges, new mem
bers of eating clubs, members of Crip
and Gown, and the debating team
will be photographed today and to
morrow from 9 to 12 and from 1 to
4:30 o'clock in the second floor Press
Building studio.
