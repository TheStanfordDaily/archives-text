# KGO To Feature Choir In Broadcast Monday
## 
### 
Thirty minutes instead of the
usual fifteen minutes allotted to the
Stanford Hour will be given to the
Stanford A Capella Choir program
to be broadcast from Station KGO
Monday evening at 8 o'clock.
Warren D. Allen, director of the
choir, has chosen the program from
Ihe repertoire used in the Stanford
Memorial Church and has made his
selections chiefly from the chorals of
Bach.
Songs prepared for their radio
program will be offered by the choir
Sunday afternoon at 4 o'clock in the
Memorial Church.
