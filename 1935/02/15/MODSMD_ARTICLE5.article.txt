# Tickets for Bear-Card Game Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the California
basketball game here tomorrow
night go on sale this morning and
tomorrow morning from 9 to 12
o'clock in the B.A.C. lobby.
On presentation of A.S.S.U. cards,
admission is 40 ijents, general price
75 cents. Immediately alter the game,
a dance sponsored by the Cardinals
will be held in Ihe Women's Gym in
honor of the Stanford and California
basketball teams, who will be guests.
Admission is free for* women, tickets
for men being 50 cents.
This is the third game of the four
game series to be played with the
Bears.
