# Two Employees Burned In Lagunita Explosion
## 
### 
Two employees of Stanford Uni
versity, Benjamin Facciola, mason
foreman, and Adolf Orlandi, laborer,
were burned in an explosion late
Wednesday in the elevator pit at
Lagunita Court.
Orlandi had gone to the pit to
clean the elevator cable with gaso
line, some of the gasoline dripping
into oil at the bottom of the pit.
Facciola, descending into the pit
later, lit a match whcih caused the
explosion.
