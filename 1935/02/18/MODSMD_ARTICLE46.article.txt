# CARDS LOSE TO SANTA CLARA, 6-5
## 
### 
With a lineup containing five
Bronco grid aces, the Santa Clara
baseball varsity nosed out Stanford
6-5 Saturday on the sunken dia
mond.
Twelve errors on their own part
defeated the Cards; two errors in
the second and three in the fourth
gave the visitors two runs in each
inning. Four more errors were re
sponsible for the remaining Santa
Clara runs in the sixth and- eighth
innings.
Though charged with a defeat,
Fred Heringer pitched excellent ball
for the Indians, allowing but eight
hits.
This afternoon the varsity plays
the U.S.S. Arizona nine on the sunk
en diamond at 3:15 o'clock.
