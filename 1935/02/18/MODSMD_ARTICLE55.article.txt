# COW DERAILS TRAIN
## 
### 
CECIL, Ala., Feb. 17 (/P) — Two
men were killed and five injured
early today when a giant bull step
ped in front of a Seaboard Air Line
freight train causing derailment of
the engine and nine box cars.
