# CAMPUS OPINION
## 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
Reaction to the mercenary expres
sions of those iwo New York Nite
Club GaLs, B.K. and C. O'C.
You're just an old nickle squeezer,
Who's doomed to take it on the
beezer;
If you're hoarding your dough,
Our answer is no;
And sex appeal won't serve as a
teaser.
All in good clean fun.
F.F.
