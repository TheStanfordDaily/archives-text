# Intramural Swim Tests To Be Held February 28
## 
### 
Intramural swimming trials will
be conducted a week from tomorrow.
February 28. Entry lists are due in
th* 1 intramural offices at Encina Gym
tomorrow or before, Marvie Kahn,
intramural manager, announced
today.
Each organization should include
in its lists the names of men ex
pected to compete in the tests pre
liminary to the finals, which will be
held at Encina pools March 1.
