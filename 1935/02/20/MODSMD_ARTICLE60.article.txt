# Encina Gives Out Winter Refunds; More Today
## 
### 
Thirty-four hundred dollars were
paid out at the Encina office yester
day to boarders in Encina com
mons. Three hundred and thirty
people signed receipts for amounts
up to 10 dollars which were given
out for refunds on last quarter's
board charges.
The office will be open from 1
until 6 o'clock today to allow the
remainder to collect their refunds.
A total of $4200 is being refunded
from last quarter.
