# TURK REWARDS JUSTICE
## 
### 
PARIS. Feb. 19—A Turkish ad
vocate, Lufti Fikii Bey, who died
! recently near here, left directions
that an annual prize of $800 be paid
to the Turkish judge who gives the
fairest verdicts and inspires the
greatest respect for law.

"The idea of a United States of
Europe could not win the hearts of
youth, for it possesses no flags, no
songs, no menaces, no self-conceit."
—Emil Ludwig.
