# Alpha Delts Quit at Yale
## 
### 
NEW HAVEN, Conn., Feb. 20 (A 1 )
The Yale News tomorrow will say
that the Yale chapter of Alpha Delta
Phi has formally voted to terminate
its 98-year period of existence on
the campus.
The college publication says the
resolution drawn up by the frater
nity to dissolve follows announce
ment by Alpha Delta Phi represen
tatives last fall the fraternity will
not take in any sophomores.
The future of the house, the Yale
News says, has not been decided.
Alpha Delta Phi was started on the
campus in 1936, two years before
P.si Upsilon, now the fence club, and
eight years before Delta Kappa
Epsilon.
