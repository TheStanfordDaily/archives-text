# Military Ball Bids Sell For $3 at A.S.S.U. Office
## 
### 
Ernie Ileckscher's 13-piece orches
tra featuring two entertainers will
furnish the music for the formal
Military Ball sponsored by Scab
bardy and Blade which will be held
from 9 to 1 o'clock, March 1, in the
Dance Studio. Bids for the dance |
are on sale for $3.00 each at the
A.S.S.U. office.
Four-inch metal sabers pinned on
black leather will make up the cov
ers for the dance programs. Dances
will be named for bugle calls in
stead of being numbered. Each i
dance will start with the playing of j
one of the bugle calls.
