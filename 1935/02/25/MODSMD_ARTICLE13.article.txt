# Chemists Plan Smoker Wednesday in Clubhouse
## 
### 
A program of unusual interest is
promised for this Wednesday eve
ning at the chemistry smoker spon
sored by Phi Lambda Upsilon, hon
orary chemistry fraternity.
Principal speaker for the evening
will be Dr. Joel H. Hildebrand, of
the University of California faculty,
a leader in the field of science. Re
freshments will be served. All in
terested are invited, the program
starting at 7:30 o'clock in the base
ment of the Women's Clubhouse.
