# Daily Inaugurates New Try out Period; Reporters Needed
## 
### 
Those who have cherished a
desire to some time work on a
newspaper will have their op
portunity to tryout for The
Daily staff during the next two
weeks and into the spring
quarter.
Tryouts begin this afternoon
at 5 o'clock in the Journalism
Library of the Press Building.
Charlotte Burridge, women's
editor, will supervise all try
outs.
Tryoutees will be given as
signments throughout the per
iod which will include work
around the office, various sto
ries, and tests from time to time
to determine any journalistic
progress which may have beun
made.
