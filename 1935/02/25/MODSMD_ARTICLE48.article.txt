# Gustav Struve Marries Elinor Castendyk, '36
## 
### 
In a bilingual marriage ceremony
in the Memorial Church, first in
English and <hen in German, Elinor
Castendyk, Stanford '36. became
the bride Friday of Dr. Gustav
Struve, secretary of the German
embassy at Washington.
Dr. D. Charles Gardner, campus
chaplain, read the services in Eng
lish, and Dr. J. J. Raun of San
Francisco read the ceremony in
German. Dr. Struve was especially
trained for the diplomatic service in
Germany, and the couple will live
in Washington.
