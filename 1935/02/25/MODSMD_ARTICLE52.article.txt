# MELLON TRIAL RESUMES
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH, Feb. 24 (/P) —
Opening the second week of Andrew
W. Mellon's income lax hearing, the
government tomorrow is expected to
delve deeper into the transfer of
banking stock to his brother—a deal
it contends was but one of many
"shadow sales" made with the intent
of escaping taxation.
