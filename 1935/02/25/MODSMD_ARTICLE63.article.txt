# Mills Girls To Be Guests Of Women's Conference
## 
### 
On Tuesday, February 20, Wom
en's Conference will have as its
guests fifteen girls from Mills Col
lege in Oakland for a discussion of
orientation for students of all classes.
The meeting will be held from
five to six in the afternoon, and fol
lowing this the Mills girls will be
entertained for dinner in the vari
ous women's residences on campus.
