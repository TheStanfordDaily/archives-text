# CAMPUS OPINION
## GRIEVE FOR BLACK INK 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
We hev notised with amuzment
that the Higgun's Amerikan Draw'n
Ink Ko. is still advertisun in your
gheat. Do you realize that you kan't
bie Higgn's Black Draw'n Ink at the
Book Store? We like Higgn's Ink and
we want to bie Higgn's ink and we
think thu Higgnse Guies is gud fel
lows end we want to help them buck
the Bookie Store trust, and we want
Higgns black ink in the Bookstoie
ai any price. A kash advertizer ought
toe get sufp konsidration — were
plentie mad about the hole thing—
If Mellinkouff wam't geetn a little
tiched in the 'ed he'd hev run onto
this dillema long ago—
Kertiz Barnes
G. D. Williams
