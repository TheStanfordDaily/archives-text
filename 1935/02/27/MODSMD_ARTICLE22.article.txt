# Three Days Left For Tuition Note Renewal
## 
### 
But three days remain in which
men students can renew their tuition
notes or apply for new ones for the
spring quarter. Students wishing to
get notes for next quarter should call
at the Dean of Men's office before
Friday. No application entered after
that date can be considered.
The Dean of Women's office will
start receiving applications begin
ning next Monday, March 4.
