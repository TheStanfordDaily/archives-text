# Dance Program Set For Next Wednesday Evening
## 
### 
A dunce program, the work of
students in the women's dancing :
class and Orchesis, honorary dance
organization, will take place next !
Wednesday evening at 8 o'clock in
the dance studio of the Women's
Gym.
The program i.s under the direc
tion of Mrs. Paul Radir, director of
dancing in the Department of Physi
cal Education. Mrs. Radir obtained
her training under Miss H'Doubler
at the University of Wisconsin and
Anne Murdstock of San Francisco.
