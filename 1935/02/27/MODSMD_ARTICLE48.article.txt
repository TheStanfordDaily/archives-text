# AZUCAR WINS $108,400 IN FIRST SANTA ANITA HANDICAP
## 
### 

In the top photo Anita Baldwin, daughter of the late E. J. "Lucky" Baldwin, presents Azucar with
floral wreaths after the former steeplechase horse won the first Santa Anita handicap at Los Angeles. The
seven-year-old chestnut gelding won $108,400 for his owner by taking the rich race from a classy field of
20 starters, including some of the greatest horses in America. Lower, Azucar finishing in front after trail
ing earlier in the race, with Ladysman second. Time Supply was third and Equipoise, the favorite, finished
far back. (Associated Press Photos)
