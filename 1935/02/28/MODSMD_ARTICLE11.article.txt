# Celebrate Founder's Day On March 10 in Chapel
## 
### 
This year Founder's Day in com
memoration of the birth of Senator
Leland Stanford will be held in
Memorial Church March 10 instead
of March 3, the real anniversary.
Under the direction of Julius Haug
' the Stanford Symphony Orchestra,
together with the Stanford Women's
Choral, directed by Warren D. Allen,
will provide the traditional concert.
Clossita Badger and Winifred E.
Elliott, sopranos, and Kayetan Attl,
harpist, will assist.
