# Freshman Debaters Meet St. Mary's Here Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford frosh debaters will meet
St. Mary's freshman forensic team at
7:30 tonight in the Women's Club
house. The question is, "Resolved,
that Japan's Policies in China Be
Approved."
Bill Clayton and Carl Barrow will
uphold the affirmative side of the
question for Stanford, while the St.

negative.
