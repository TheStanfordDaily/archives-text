# Travel Bureau Plans Contest for Vacation
## 
### 
A vacation cruise contest is being
offered to all undergraduates by the
Bureau of University Travel. The
contest consists of a paper not ex
ceeding 2500 words in length con
cerning "The Educational Values in
Travel.'" All applications must be
submitted before March 15, and all
papers by April 15. Correspondence
should be addressed: Vacation
Cruise Contest, Bureau of University
Travel, Newton, Mass.
The cruise includes a tour of Eng
land. Germany, Holland, Belgium,
France, and the Mediterranean
countries. An experienced staff con
ducts the trip. Further information
may he obtained at Miss Yost's office.
