# Encina Room Deposits Must Be Paid Next Week
## 
### 
Residents of Encina desiring to
keep their present rooms for the
spring quarter should make their
reservations at the dean of men's
office Monday, Tuesday, or Wednes
day of next week.
A deposit of five dollars is re
quired at the time reservations are
made. The deposit will apply on the
rent for the next quarter.

Coeds at the University of Cali
fornia are not allowed to stay out
for movies after 10:15 on week
nights.
