# Phil Bronson Elected President of Glee Club
## 
### 
At a recent meeting of the Stan
ford Glee Club, Phil Bronson was
elected president to take the place of
Dave Bassett, resigned. Royal Ivory
was elected to the club's executive
committee to fill Bronson's vacated
position.
Plans were discussed for the con
certs for the remainder of the sea
sofi, highlight of which will be the
ten-day tour to southern California
during spring vacation.
