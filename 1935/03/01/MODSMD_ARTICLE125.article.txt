# Palm Beach, White Linen Garb Will Be Popular Men's Fashion During 1935
## 
### 
White Palm Beach suits will be the
featured attraction for the man's
wardrobe this summer. Not only is
white to be shown but grey and
brown linens have been introduced
into the linen class.
In Palo Alto this year four hun
dred of the popular whites were sold,
a record, according to local retailers
of men's wear, that was unsurpassed
in northern California.
A grey in a linen suit along with

la colored shirt and light-colored tie
make up a perfect theme in men's
sports wear. White suits with dark
blue shirts, white or yellow ties, and
white shoes make a striking show for
afternoon and beach wear.
