# New Fashions Demand No More Long Hair
## 
### 
Cleverly curled short hair is the
spring fashion. Many college women
sigh, for they are lully aware that
their sleek heads of long hair are
their most becoming feature.
True it is that the new coiffures
are designed to reveal the most
graceful and becoming lines of the
head and neck. And, sadder and
truer than this is the fact that the
new spring rats are made for short
hair.
