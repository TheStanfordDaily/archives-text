# Taffeta To Be Used For Afternoon Coats
## 
### 
Taffeta, which heads the list of
important spring fabrics, is also find
ing its place among coats for street, I
afternoon, and evening wear.
A very intriguing caprice of this
fashion (which may be found in San i
Francisco) is a coat of embroidered ;
black taffeta. Its full sleeves are
down to the wrist for afternoon wear,
or may be pushed up and puffed out I
for evening.
