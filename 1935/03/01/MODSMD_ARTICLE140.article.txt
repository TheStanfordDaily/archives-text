# Calf, Grain, Patent Leather Win Lead For Sport Purses
## 
### 
In the line of purses and hand
bags, grain leathers, calf, and patent
leather are way in the lead, but in a
less formal line cloth purses which
carry out the trim of the suit are in
great demand.
When it comes to the ever impor
tant gloves, a great deal of white is
being worn especially in washable
suedes and rough leathers.
Last summer's fad for embroid
ered initials on handkerchiefs con
tinues again this year. Sport hand
kerchiefs are two to four inches
larger than the plainer afternoon va
viety. Dark brown and navy blue
are popular colors, planned to ac
cent the gay spring plaids.
