# Make-up Prescribed For Blonde, Brunette
## 
### 
For blondes—clear, light tones.
For brunettes—warm, glowing tones.
Thus decree leading cosmeticians in
describing the make-up schemes for
those with light and dark hair. These
schemes apply only to those whose
skin and hair correspond, however.
Blondes are dusting on "peach
bloom" powder and adding color
with geranium rouge and lipstick.
They emphasized their eyes with
soft blue mascara. For brunettes,
however, the face powder is of a
shade blending peach and cream.
Fiouge of a light red and lipstick of
a warm, vivid i - ed are most flattering.
They use black mascara and both
green and brown eye shadow.

The new spring bathing suits may
be found in beautiful bright colors.
In fact, bright colors dominate the
field and as a result the beaches
should be not unlike rainbows.
