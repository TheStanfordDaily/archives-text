# Fashionable Men Seek Smart and Durable Bags
## 
### 
Discriminating men who demand
fashionable, smooth-fitting clothes
are careful to extend their tastes to
garments and equipment for travel.
Practicality and convenience as well
as appearance are always the pri
mary necessities for luggage.
At present men are seeking lug
gage which has touches of color and
which is light in weight. An innova
tion is the rawhide bag which has a
fine, durable finish and is light and
convenient.
