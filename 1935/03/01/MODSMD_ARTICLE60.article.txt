or drag' dance of this quarter in the
Women's Clubhouse Saturday eve
ning from 9 to 12. Informality is
the keynote of the radio dances
sponsored by the unit, which in
cludes Union, Madrono, Mariposa
and Elm. The dance is open to all
campus men.
