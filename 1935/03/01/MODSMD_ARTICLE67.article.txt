# WOMEN MEET CAL, MILLS IN FENCING
## 
### 
Tomorrow afternoon at 2:30
o'clock the Women's Gym will be
the scene of the Novice Individual
Foils Meet between women from
California, Mills, and Stanford who
have fenced in but one previous con
test.
Stanford's representatives are Bir
die Boyles, Beth Doyle, Barbara
Brenk, Mary Agnes King, Marjorie
Prager, and Ruth Mayer. Helene
Mayer, Olympic champion and Mills
coach, will officiate.
This is the second of three meets
between these schools, and medals
will again be awarded by the Wom
en's Division of the Amateur Fenc
ers' League of America.
