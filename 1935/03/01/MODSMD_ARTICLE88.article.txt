# HOOVER. JR.. SUED
## 
### 
BAKERSFIELD, Feb. 28 (/P). —
Herbert Hoover, Jr., son of the for
mer president of the United States,
today was sued for $94,000 damages
as the result of an accident in which
his car figured. The plaintiff is W. E.
Young of Banning.
Only two tickets have been given
for parking violations at the Univer
sity of California this vear.
