# RESCUE PARTY DIES
## 
### 
RABAT, Morocco, Feb. 28 (/P). —
Seven members of a life boat crew
were dashed to death on the rocks
at Casablanca when the boat cap
sized in heavy seas on the way. to a
fishing ship in distress.
