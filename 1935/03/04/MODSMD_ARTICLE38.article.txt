# TROJANS AND BEAVERS TO BATTLE FOR COAST HOOP TITLE
## 
### 

The basketball squads of Southern California and Oregon State will clash at Corvallis, Ore., in a play-off series to determine the Pacific
Coast Conference championship. Troy won the southern division race easily while the Beavers came through to take the northern title. Palm
berg and Hibbard are two of the high scorers on the northern squad. The Southern California team is shown in center. (Associated Press Photo)
