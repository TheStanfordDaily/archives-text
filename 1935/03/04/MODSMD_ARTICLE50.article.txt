# HOLMES WEAKENS; DEATH PROBABLE
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, March 3 UP)
Oliver Wendell Holmes growing
weaker today and a close friend sor
rowfully said, "It is just a matter of
waiting."
Nevertheless, the vitality which
has kept thq aged jurist alive for 94
years—lacking only five days—per
sisted.
Mark Howe, a former secretary to
the justice, who has been at the resi
dence on I street almost constantly
since Holmes was stricken with
bronchial pneumonia, said:
"Mr. Holmes is conscious and he
is not suffering. I do not believe that
