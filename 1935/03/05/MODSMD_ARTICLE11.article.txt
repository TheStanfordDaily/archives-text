# Music Groups To Give Founders Day Concert
## 
### 
Annual Founders Day concert in
commemoration of the birth of Sena
tor Leland Stanford will be held in
the Memorial Church Sunday eve
ning, March 10, at 8 o'clock.
Under Warren D. Allen's direction,
the Stanford Women's Choral com
posed of 60 voices will present the
traditional concert accompanied by
the Stanford Symphony Orchestra,
directed by Julius Haug.
Faculty, students, and friends of
the University are invited. No tick
ets will be required for admission.
The Stanford Symphony Orches
tra will practice in the Memorial
Chapel tonight and tomorrow night
at 7:15 o'clock.
