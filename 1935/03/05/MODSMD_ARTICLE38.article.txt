# TRANSPORTATION STRIKE
## 
### 
DUBLIN, Irish Free State, March
4 -(TP) —Dublin s transportation serv
ices were paralyzed today by a
strike. Business men went to work
on foot, on bicyc'ec and on roller
skates.
