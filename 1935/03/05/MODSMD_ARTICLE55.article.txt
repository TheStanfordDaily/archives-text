# Lagunita Shows 3-Foot Rise Due to Recent Rain
## 
### 
Nearly a three-foot rise in the
water level has been recorded at the
Lagunita Boathouse as a result of
the rains during the past week.
Rainfall over the week-end raised
the season's total to 11.76 inches,
more than four inches more than
was recorded last year at the same
Rainfall Sunday night added
.10 of an inch to the Stanford total.
