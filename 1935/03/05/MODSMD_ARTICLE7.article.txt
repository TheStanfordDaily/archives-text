# Heche Plays Tomorrow Night at Junior Jolly-Up
## 
### 
Class of '36 is holding a jolly-up
tomorrow night at 7 o'clock at the
Women's Clubhouse. Juniors will
dance to the music of Gordon
Heche's eight-piece orchestra and
will be given free cigarettes.
Junior transfers are extended spe
cial invitation to the hour's after
dinner dancing. Wally Brooke,
Jeanne Keesling, Edith Hind, Vir
ginia Johnson, and Joe Vigna head
arrangements.
