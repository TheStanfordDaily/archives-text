# NELSON'S AIDES HIDE FACES FROM CAMERA
## 
### 

Three of the 18 persons in custody in San Francisco on charges of harboring "Baby Face" Nelson,
slain desperado, in California and Nevada concealed their features as they were taken to jail to await
trial March 18. Left to right: Frankie Field, Louis Tambini and Arthur Pratt, Montana jeweler. (Associ
ated Press Photo)
