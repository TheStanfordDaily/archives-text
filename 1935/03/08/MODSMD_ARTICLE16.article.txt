# Envelopes Necessary To Get Grades Mailed
## 
### 
Students who wish to leceive their
grades for this quarter during spring
vacation may leave a stamped, ad
dressed envelope at the Registrar's
office before the end of the quarter.
No guides will be given out except
by mail until registration day,
April 1.
Envelopes sold for the benefit of
the Stanford Convalescent Home
may be obtained at the Registrar's
office for five cents. Grades will be
mailed out after March 27.
