# Well-Known Fan-Dancer Can't Cope With Cupid
## 
### 
NEW YORK, March 7 (£>).—To be
or not to be a Shakespearean actress
—thai is the question now bothering
Sally Rand.
Whether 'tis nobler to tread the
boards as Portia, to go in for high
brow dancing, or maybe to marry
a nice guy and have children—the
shapely blonde whose sensational fan
dance startled the country not so
long ago just can't decide.
