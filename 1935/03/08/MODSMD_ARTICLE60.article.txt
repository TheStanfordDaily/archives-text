# Soccer Squad Arranges Cup for Series Winner
## 
### 
The '34 soccer squad last evening
met for a banquet at the Beta house
and made provisions for a trophy
which will be awarded to the win
ner of the Stanford-California scries
each year.
Waddy White, past team captain,
displayed the trophy, a silver loving
cup, to the members of the team and
put it into the custody of Coach
Harry Maloney who will have charge
of its presentation. Coach Maloney
gave the principal talk of the eve
ning. Others who spoke were Wad
dy White, Winstead Weaver, John
Moore, A1 Navarro, Rudy Gonzales,
Bud Wilson, and Ralph Cotter.
