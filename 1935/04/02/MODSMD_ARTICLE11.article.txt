# Offer Course To Train Counselors For Camps
## 
### 
All interested in developing
themselves for leadership in private
camps or those of various youth
agencies are invited to enroll in the
Camp Counselor Training Course
this Wednesday at 7:30 o'clock in
the Little Theatre. Registration fee
is 50 cents.
Tho course will run for eight con
secutive Wednesday evenings.
Among those who will conduct va
rious sessions are Mrs. Elizabeth
Price of Berkeley, an expert in the
field of nature study; Dr. James C,
De Voss of San Jose State College,
specialist in counselling; and Chief
Eaglewing and Ramona Eaglewing,
who will instruct in Indiancrafts,
lore, and dancing.
