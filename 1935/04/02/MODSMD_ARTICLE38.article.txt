# Dr. Curry To Speak At Religious Seminar
## 
### 
Dr. Bruce Curry, nationally
known leader of student confer
ences, will be on the campus from
Sunday, April 7, until Tuesday for
a three-day seminar on religion.
His first address will be given Sun
day evening at 7:30 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. The remain
ing sessions will be on Monday and
Tuesday afternoons.
The Y.W.C.A. has been instru
mental in bringing Dr. Curry to
Stanford while he is making a four
months' speaking tour in the col
leges and universities of America.
