# MEN'S GLEE CLUB TO RETURN AFTER TOUR OVER STATE
## 
### 
Ending its 1400-mile spring tour,
the Stanford Men's Glee Club will
return to the campus tonight.
The troup includes 22 singers,
"Skipper'' Kulmann, director, Ben
ShensOn, accompanist, and Dwight
Coddington, entertainer.
Programs, presented in about nine
cities throughout California, were
both musically and financially suc
cessful, according to Art Allen. Con
certs were given at inns, high school
assemblies, theatres, and various
community clubs. Two radio broad
casts and two special alumni pro
grams given in Fresno and San Ber
nardino filled out the tour.
Spring quarter tryouts will be
announced soon.
