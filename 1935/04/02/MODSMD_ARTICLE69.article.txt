# Women's Gymnasium Announces Swim Hours
## 
### 
Open hours in the Women's Gym
pool f&r the spring quarter are Tues
day at 3 o'clock; Monday, Tuesday,
Wednesday, and Thursday at 5
o'clock; Saturday at 11 o'clock; and
Sunday afternoons from 2:30 to 5
o'clock. The Sunday afternoon
hours are open to women and their
men guests.
The faculty women and town
women swimming classes will be
held on Tuesday, Thursday, and
Friday mornings at 10 o'clock. A
nominal fee will be charged for en
rollment in the course.

Quad editorial staff is requested
to report for work this afternoon.
