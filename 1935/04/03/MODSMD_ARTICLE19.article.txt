# April Illustrated Review Plans Photolith Section
## 
### 
Tlie Illustrated Review, coming
out April 8, is featuring a photolith
section in the next issue. This sec
tion will contain pictures of Stan
ford track stars, and a center spread
of pictures of the "springtime cam
pus," including scenes of Lake La
tfunita, the golf course, Lagunita
Court, the Quad, and of the dance
drama in thp Women's Gym. These
pictures were taken by Paul Nar
butovskih.
Gord.v Steedman, Chappie editor,
has written on article concerning
the process of phot.olithing.
