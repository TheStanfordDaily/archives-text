# DEMAND MONARCHY
## 
### 
ATHENS, April 2 (/P). — Public
clamor against the government's
clemency to convicted rebels had
Grecian nerves at trigger edge to
night, with talk of another revolu
tion and demands for restoration of
the monarchy in some quarters.
