# Final Tryoutee Tests Set for This Afternoon
## 
### 
The last examination of the try
outees for the Daily will he given this
afternoon at 5 o'clock in the Jour
nalism Library. It is important to
be there as this examination will be
the culminating point of the try
outee period.
The results of the examination
plus the other work already done
will be the basis for the selection of
new Daily reporters. Names of those
who are accepted will be announced
in The Daily this week.
