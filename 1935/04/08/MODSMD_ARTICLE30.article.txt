# GLEE CLUB TO MEET
## 
### 
All members of the Men's Glee
Club are requested to assemble at
the Firehouse at 6:15 o'clock tonight.
Those who cannot appear at this
time will please get in touch with
Don Eddie, 232 Branner, or sec him
at the Glee Club room at 7:15 o'clock.
There will be no regular meeting of
the club tonight, but the usual Wed
nesday meeting will be held at 7:45
o'clock in the Glee Club rooms.
