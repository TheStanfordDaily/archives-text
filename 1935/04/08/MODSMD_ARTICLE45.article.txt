# DINK CALLS PRACTICE
## 
### 
Dink Templeton, Card track men
tor, last night announced that this
afternoon's practice session will be
held on the soccer field if Angell
Field is too wet to hold spiked shoes.
Templeton expressed his wish that
all men turn out unless rain is* ac
tually falling during practice hours.
A Soviet glider is reported to have
stayed in the air in a mountainous
district of the Crimea for 24 hours,
10 minutes.
