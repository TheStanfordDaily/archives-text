# Scouting Fraternity Will Conduct Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
A meeting of the local chapter of
Alpha Phi Omega, national scouting
fraternity, will be held tonight at
7:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. All former scouts on the
campus are invited to attend.
The meeting is planned to or
ganize for the rest of the quarter a
program which started last Saturday
when the group sponsored a meet
ing in honor of Colonel G. Walton,
aide to Lord Robert Baden-Powell,
who was in San Francisco for a two
day stay on his way to England
from Australia.
