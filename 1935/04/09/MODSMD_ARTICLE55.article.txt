# FROSH TRACK MEN TO MEET TONIGHT
## 
### 
Coach Bill Ellington last night is
sued orders for his frosh track team
to report for a meeting in the B.A.C.
Building at 7:30 o'clock tonight. El
lington proposes to advise the men
in each event of improvements that
can be made as the result of last
week's meet with U.S.F. frosh. To
use the coach's own words "so I can
tell 'em what's wrong and what's
light."
This instruction session will serve
to set the squad on the warpath for
Alameda All-Stars, whom they meet
nexi Saturday and for California
irosh the following week-end.
Ellington expects his team to han
dle the Alameda track pounders
without great difficulty but the Bear*
frosh present a different story.
Always a tough customer, California
this year is no exception and will be

out to repeat its victory of last sea
son.
Alameda, defeated last year by the
Card frosh, is reported to be of aver
age strength in all events with extra
power centered in the broad jump.
