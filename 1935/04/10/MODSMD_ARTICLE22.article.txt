# Art Department Shows Mendelowitz Paintings
## 
### 
An exhibition of California, water
colors, painted by acting instructor
in art Dan Mendelowitz, is being
held in the graphic art department.
The pictures portray landscapes, city,
marine, and boat scenes.
Mendelowitz received his training
at Stanford under Professor Clark,
Professor Farmer, and Mrs. Stark.
Afler his graduation in 1926 his
paintings won him a scholarship for
a year's study at the Art Students
League in New York City, after
which he taught art at San Jose
State Teachers College. He is at
present instructing in the graphic
arts department.
