# 'DIZZY' BECOMES GODFATHER
## 
### 

"Dizzy" Dean, ace of the St. Louis Cardinals pitching staff, was
godfather for these five-months-old twins of Mr. and Mrs. Arthur
iMewman. The tots were christened Patricia Ann and Virginia Lee.
Mrs. Mark Koenig, wife of the New York Giants infielder, was god
mother. (Associated Press Photo)
