# Models Named for Daily 'Easter Fashion Revue'
## 
### 
The Daily will hold its first Easter
Fashion Revue next Wednesday
afternoon from 3 to 4 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. Gowns of all
sorts will be modelled from many of
the local and San Francisco stores.
Stanford women who will display
the gowns are: Joan Brandel, Carrol
Arkush, Jean Rouverol, Virginia
Parker, Fritzie KoLster, Peggy Place,
Janet Richter, Marjorie Meyer,
Marjorie McCoy, Mallou Dobbs,
Jeaneite Rosenfeld, and Bette
Brock.
