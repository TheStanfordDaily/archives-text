# Geology Group To Meet Here
## 
### 
The Cordilleran section of the
Geological Society of America will
meet here on Friday and Saturday
under the chairmanship of Dr. Bailey
Willis, Vice-President W. S. Tangier
Smith, and Secretary W. O. Wood
ford. This group is the Pacific Coast
representative o fthe Geological So
ciety of America.
The origin of the crater in Mt.
Mazama will be the topic of one of
the papers read. This crater is the
one in which Crater Lake lies.
The state geologist, Dr. O. P. Jen
kins, will make a report on the prog
ress of a new geological map of Cal
ifornia and the compilation of known
facts of geology, which are very
much needed in the development of
nvning interests.
