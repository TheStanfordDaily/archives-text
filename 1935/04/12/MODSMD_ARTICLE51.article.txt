# Dr. Schultz Leaves For Eastern Science Meet
## 
### 
Dr. E. W. Schultz, professor of
bacteriology, left yesterday for the
East where he will attend several
scientific meetings and visit univer
sity laboratories. He will speak at
the Johns Hopkins Institute and in
Washington, D.C.
His trip will include Detroit,
where Dr. Schultz will give a paper
on infantile paralysis serum and its
effects. In New York he will attend
a joint meeting of the American
Association of Immunologists, he will
talk on the problem of immunizing
against infantile paralysis.
