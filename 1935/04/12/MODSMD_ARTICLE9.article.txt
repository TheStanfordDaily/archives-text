# Daily Fashion Show Will Benefit Campus Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Refreshments at the Daily fashion
show, to be held next Wednesday,
April 17, from 3 to 4 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse, will be sold for
ihe benefit of the Y.W.C.A. Kay
Fitzpatrick will be at the head of
the committee in charge of this fea
ture, and will have as her assistants
Marianne Allen, Frances McCowan,
and Lois Persons.
Gowns of all sorts, including sports
clothes and formal evening attire,
will be modelled by campus women
during the show, and the announcing
will be done by Charlotte Burridge,
women's editor of the Daily.
