# Reynolds Talks On Air Tonight
## 
### 
This evening at 8 o'clock over
station KGO Dr. Harry Reynolds of
Stanford University and president of
Stanford Associates will speak on
the subject "Privately Endowed
Universities."
The purpose of Dr. Reynolds'
speech will be to inform the people
of the radio audience as to the serv
ices performed by the private uni
versities in the line of education both
in former times and at present.
He will also discuss how private
institutions can follow a chosen
course not possible for public, tax
supported institutions, and in this
way will point out the advantages of
the privately endowed institution.
