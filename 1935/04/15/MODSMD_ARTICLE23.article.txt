# Gertrude Stein to Give Second Talk Wednesday
## 
### 
Gertrude Stein, noted authoress,
will make a return appearance here
Wednesday night in the Dance Stu
dio under the auspices of the English
Club. Her lecture will be on paint
ing.
Miss Stein was enthusiastically re
ceived in her visit here last Wed
nesday when she gave a talk on "The
Relation of English to the Ameri
can Language." Tickets will again be
on sale for 50 cents at the A.S.S.U.
office. Last week they were sold out
on Tuesday.
