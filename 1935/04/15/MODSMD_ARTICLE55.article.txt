# Kahn Completes Lists Of Intramural Teams
## 
### 
Lists of organizations entering
teams in intramural sport competi
tion this quarter were completed
Friday, -according to Intramural
Manager Marvie Kahn, with about
35 soft ball teams, 20 hard ball teams,
and 30 horseshoe teams entered.
The different leagues will be
posted on the intramural bulletin
board in Encina Gymnasium today.
