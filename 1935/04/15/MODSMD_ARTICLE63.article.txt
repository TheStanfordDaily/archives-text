# NEW GOWNS, HATS TO BE DISPLAYED AT FASHION SHOW
## 
### 
The newest spring models,
the smartest hats, dresses, and
suits from many Palo Alto and
San Francisco stores, will be
displayed Wednesday from 3 to
4 o'clock at The Daily Easter
Fashion Revue in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Representative women from
hall and row will act as man
nequins, displaying sports
clothes first, then street, and
finally evening clothes. Gordon
Heche's orchestra will furnish
the music.
