# Costume, Bid, Masque, Date Required for Ball
## 
### 
"Any type of costume, a masque, a
date ,and a two-dollar bid" are the
only prerequisites for attendance at
the annual Masque Ball on April 2fi,
according to Chairman Bob Church.
Decorations for the dance have
been arranged according to color ef
fects, which will be carried out in
both drapes and lights. Eddie Strong
and his 12-piece orchestra will pre
sent various novelty medley num
bers, and will play the Royal March
for the king and queen of the eve
ning.
