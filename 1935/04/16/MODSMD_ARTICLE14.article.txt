# Dr. Strong Speaks To Young Japanese in S.F.
## 
### 
Addressing an audience of over
100 Japanese young people, Dr. Ed
ward K. Strong, professor of psy
chology, spoke on "An Analysis of
the Second Generation Japanese" at
the annual bay region conference of
Christian young people held in San
Francisco Sunday.
Last year Dr. Strong published a
book, "The Second Generation Japa
nese Problem," after five years of
study with a grant from the Carnegie
Institute. This book was preceded by
two smaller volumes, "Japanese in
California," and "Vocational Apti
tude of the Second Generation
Japanese."
