# MORE MURDER CHARGES
## 
### 
SANT AFE, N.M., April 15 (/P).—
Seventeen more persons were
charged with, murder in a new com
plaint filed today in District Court
here, the outgrowth of a fatal riot
in Gallup, N.M., in which three per
' sons lost their lives.
