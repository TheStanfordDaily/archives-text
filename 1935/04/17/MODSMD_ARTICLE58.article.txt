# Alumna Will Talk Before Y.W.C.A. Tomorrow
## 
### 
Mrs. Fitzhugh Bell, '25, will be
the guest speaker at a meeting of
the Y.W.C.A. Home Engineering
group tomorrow at 5 o'clock in the
"Y" rooms above the Women's
Clubhouse. Her subject will be
"Budgeting."
While on the campus Mrs. Bell
was a sponsor and a prominent
member of Cap and Gown, honorary
women's socicty. She has also serv
ed as secretary in the dean of wom
en's office and as secretary to Mrs.
Herbert Hoover.
