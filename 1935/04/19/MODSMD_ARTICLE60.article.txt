# Traveling University Sets Sail on June 29
## 
### 
A traveling university sets sail
from Los Angeles harbor June 29.
The 40 students admitted must be
registered with the Inter-Oceanic
University of Los Angeles one month
previous. Accompanied by six pro
fessors and two deans, the group
will attend 20 colleges from England
and France to India and the Orient.
College credentials from each uni
versity will be given to the students.
Classes will also be conducted "en
voyage" by the faculty on board the
steam yacht.
