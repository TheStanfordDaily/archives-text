# Electric Clock Will Be Used in California Meet
## 
### 
Stanford track fans will be ini
tiated to the use of an electric clock
at track meets when they attend the
Big Meet at the Edwards Stadium
tomorrow.
The clock, which is situated at the
head of the 220-yard dash straight
away, is started with the gun for
each race. It has both minute and
second hands, the small red marks
being used to indicate the latter.
