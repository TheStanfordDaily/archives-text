# April 29 Is Date Chosen For Strawberry Brunch
## 
### 
As part of the festivities to be held
during the Convalescent Home Drive
the Strawberry Brunch will be an
important campus event of Monday,
April 29. Held in Union Court, the
brunch will last from 10 to 5 o'clock,
with entertainment from 2 to 4
o'clock. All proceeds will go to the
Convalescent Home. Tickets are be
ing sold in all living groups.
Co-chairmen of the committee in
charge are Jean Kauffman and Vic
Martin, assisted by the following
committee heads: serving, Adrienne
Johnston; entertainment, Marion
Roller; kitchen, Winifrid Manning;
tickets, Betty Weber, Janice Fulton,
and Margaret Johnston; flowers,
Beth Allen; clean-up, Frances Mc-
Cowen.
