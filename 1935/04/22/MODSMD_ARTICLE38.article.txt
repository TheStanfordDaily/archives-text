# FRENCH ACE KILLED
## 
### 
MOSHI, Tanakanyika Territory.
Africa, April 21 </P>.—The French
flier Maurice Finat, who recently
completed a flight from France to
Madagascar, was killed today as his
place crashed into the Sanya moun
tains near Moshi during a storm.
