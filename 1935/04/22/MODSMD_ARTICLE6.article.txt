# West Memorial Lectures Commence Tomorrow
## 
### 
The general topic of the Raymond
Fred West Memorial lectures, to be
' given this year by Dr. Carl L. Beck
er, professor of history at Cornell
University, will be "Progress and
Power." These lectures will be given
Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday
of this week, at 7:30 o'clock in the
Assembly Hall.
The subject of the first talk of the
series will be "Tools and the Man,"
j while the following two will be
given on "The Sword and the Pen"
and "Instruments of Precision." This
.series of lectures is given in alter
nate years by distinguished scholars
from all over the world.
