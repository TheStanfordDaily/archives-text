# Patrons Announced For Toyon Spring Informal
## 
### 
Toyon Hail will give its annual
spring informal Friday evening, May
4, presenting Eddie Stirm's orches
tra. Bids are now on sale at the
A.S.S.U. offices in the Old Union for
$2 per couple.
Patrons and patronesses for the
affair include Professor and Mis.
Edward M. Hulme, Mr. and Mrs.
Ernst Gieseckc, Mr. and Mrs. Clif
ford Weigle, and Mr. Hal Bacon.
