# NAVY ENTERS REGATTA
## 
### 
ITHACA, N.Y., April 20 (/P). —
Cornell University announced today
the varsity oarsmen -of the U. S.
Naval Academy have accepted an in
vitation to compete in the Carnegie
Cup Regatta May 18 against crews
at Cornell, Yale, and Princeton. The
regatta will be rowed on Cayuga
Lake.
