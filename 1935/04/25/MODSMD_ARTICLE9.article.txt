# Cardinals Plan To Aid Grads Homecoming Day
## 
### 
Members of the Cardinals activi
ties committee, in a meeting Tuesday
night with Harlow Rothert, alumni
secretary, laid plans to assist the
alumni association in the annual
Alumni Homecoming Day, Satur
day, May 4,
The Cardinals will help in the re
ception of visiting alumni and will
maintain an information bureau in
the Union Court during the day to
direct them. Members of the com
mittee will also meet incoming
trains at the Palo Alto depot Satur
day morning and conduct alumni to
the campus.
