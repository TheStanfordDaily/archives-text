# Law Alumni Will Hear C. B. Whittier at Dinner
## 
### 
Professor Clark Whittier of the
Stanford Law School will be guest
of honor at the annual banquet of
the Northern Division of the Stan
ford Law Society May 3 in the gold
ball room of the Palace Hotel in San
Francisco.
The law society, composed of
alumni of the Stanford Law School,
is honoring Professor Whittier on
his service for 25 years on the law
school faculty.
