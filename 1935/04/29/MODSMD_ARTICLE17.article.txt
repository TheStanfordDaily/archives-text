# Junior Prom Committee Discusses Dance Theme
## 
### 
Junior Prom committeemen, head
ed by Woody Adams and Wally
Brooke, have not yet decided upon
plans for the dance which Ihe class
of '36 is giving May 24.
Novel motifs and two popular
dance orchestras are as present the
objects of discussion at session of
the group which also includes Dor
othy Lyman, Jean Reynolds, Edith
Hind, Jack Pope, Bill Garth, "Bones"
Hamilton, and Jack Horton.
