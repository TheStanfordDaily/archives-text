# Y.W.C.A. Group To Visit Peon Settlement May 3
## 
### 
In order to consider at close range
the problem of the migratory Mex
ican in California, the Y.W.C.A. is
sponsoring a tour of the Mexican
Settlement in San Jose next Satur
day, May 3. Leaving the Women's
Clubhouse at 8:30 o'clock, the group
will visit iho Mexican Church, ihe
Mexican elementary school, and a
private collection of Mexican art.
Various lectures will be given
throughout.
Reservations, which are available
to all students and faculty members,
can be made by calling the Y.W.C.A.
at 4301 before 4:30 o'clock Thursday.
The cost of the tour is 50 cents,
which will cover transportation and
a box lunch.
