# DIME RACKET SPREADS
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, April 28 (A*).
Despite the frowns of the authori
ties, the "send-a-dime" chain letter
has made its appearance in Holly
wood, and the employes of at least
one major studio were reported to
have taken to the idea enthusiastic
ally.
