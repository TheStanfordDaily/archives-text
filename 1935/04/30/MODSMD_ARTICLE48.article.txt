# FIJI BEATS D.T.D. AT HORSESHOES
## 
### 
Phi Gamma Delta's barnyard art
ists tossed the horseshoes around or
real close to the iron peg 278 times
yesterday while four Delts were
able to ring up only 262 digits. Re
sult—the Fijis take the opener of
the Farm's horseshoe intramurals.
Phi Sigs had even less trouble
with Psi tossers, totaling 302 to the
losers, 158. The scores are the sum
of the scores of 16 games, a game
apiece being played by each of a
team's four members against each
member of the opposition.
