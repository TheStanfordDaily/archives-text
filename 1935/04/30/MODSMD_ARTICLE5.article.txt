# QUAD SALE NEARS END
## 
### 
With 400 more students registered
this year, and Quad sales ahead of
last year at this time, Quad sales
men go into a final flurry of selling
this week, with prospects of a com
plete sellout. Quads will be on sale
this morning from 8 to 12 o'clock at
the usual table in front of the post
office.
