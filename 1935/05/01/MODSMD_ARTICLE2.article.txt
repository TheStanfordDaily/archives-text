# Mitchell Returns From Atlantic Coast Meetings
## 
### 
* Returning to the campus after an
absence of three weeks. Dr. John<
Pearce Mitchell, University regis
trar, was back in his office Monday
after attending two meetings on the
Atlantic Coast.
On April 16 to 18. Dr. Mitchell at
tended the annual meeting of the
American Association of Collegiate
Registrars held at Raleigh, N.C., and
the meeting of the American Chem
ical Society at New York City from
April 22 to 25.
