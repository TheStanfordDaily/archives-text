# Decorative May Display Featured at Art Gallery
## 
### 
With the current exhibition of an
tique decorative maps at the Stan
ford Art Gallery, Director Pedro de
Lemos announces the opening of a
display of decorative cut-paper maps
made by the grade children of Mon
tana public schools.
Subjects include navigation routes
during the Age of Discovery for the
Eastern and Western section of the
United States and North America.
The project represents the results
of a correlation between art, history,
and geography departments. The ex
hibition will remain open to the
public without admission charge un
til May 12.
