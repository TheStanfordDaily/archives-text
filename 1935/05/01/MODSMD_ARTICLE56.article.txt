# YOUNG'S DAUGHTER DIES
## 
### 
SALT LAKE CITY, Utah. April
30 (/P>.—Mrs. Maria Y. Dougall, old
est surviving daughter of Brigham
Young, second president of the Lat
ter Day Saints church and leader of
the Mormon colonists into Utah,
died today.
