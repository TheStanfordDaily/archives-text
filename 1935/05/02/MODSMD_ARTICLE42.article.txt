# Y.W.C.A. Plans Mexican School Visit Saturday
## 
### 
For students interested in social
work among foreign laborers in
California there will be a tour of
the Mexican Settlement in San Jose
i Saturday. Sponsored by the Y.W.
, C.A., and especially by the Social
Service group of that organization,
the tour will leave the Women's
Clubhouse at 8:30 o'clock that
, morning.
Visits to Mexican schools in San
Jose will be made, and talks will be
given by various authorities on Mex
ican settlers in this part of the
country. Arrangements can be made
by phoning the Y.W.C.A. at 4301.
