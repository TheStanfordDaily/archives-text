# Toyon To Use Garden Idea for Sport Dance
## 
### 
An energetic decoration commit
vate Toyon lobby for the Toyon
tee is planning to completely reno
spring sport dance. Vines and flow
ers will be suspended from the
chandeliers to transform the dance
floor into a huge garden. There will
also be a fountain, a vine arbor, and
colored lights to add to the effect.
Eddie Stirm and his popular, or
chestra will play. Bids are on sale
at the A.S.S.U. office for $2. Patrons
for the dance are Professor and Mrs.
Edward Hulme, Mr. and Mrs. Clif
ford Weigle, and Mr. Harold Bacon.
