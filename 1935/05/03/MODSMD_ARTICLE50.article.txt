# Scalpers Society Elects Bob Fullerton President
## 
### 
In the annual election held yester
day, Bob Fullerton was elected to
the presidency of Scalpers, sopho
more men's honor society. Chosen at
the same time to serve under him
were Horace Blackman, secretary,
and Fred Williams, treasurer.
Twelve new members were elected
to the society from the Class of '37:
Bill Blackburn, Jack Clark, Michael
Gonzalez, John Grant, Najeeb Hala
by, Don Moulton, Jack Norcross,
Byron Smith, Clem Smoot, John Sul
livan, Louis Tsoutsouvas, and Howell
Turner.
