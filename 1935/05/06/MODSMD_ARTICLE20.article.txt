# Glee Club To Sing For Bohemian Club in S.F.
## 
### 
Formal concerts in San Francisco
and at Del Monte are included on
this month's schedule of the Stan
ford Glee Club, according to Jack
Ellis, secretary.
The club will sing for members of
the Bohemian Club in San Francisco
on May lfl, while the week-end of
May 25 will find the club's members
appearing at the Hotel Del Monte on
the Monterey peninsula. An appear
ance before the student body of
Lowell High School in San Fran
cisco is also slated as one of this
month's activities.
