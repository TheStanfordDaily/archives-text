# Five Fellowship Awards To Be Given Next Month
## 
### 
Four new fellowships in graduate
research, established on an annual
basis under the will of the late
Abraham Rosenborg, will be award
ed June 1, announces Registrar J. P.
Mitchell. No teaching duties arc re
quired of appointees.
The recipient of the George
Loomis Fellowship in American
Literature, carrying a yearly stipend
of $750, will also be named on the
same date.
