# Bids on Sale Today For Branner Dinner-Dance
## 
### 
Bids are now on sale for Branner
Hall's annual dinner-dance, to be
held May 17th, according to Art
Burnett, club president.
Ed Stirm's orchestra will play
after the banquet, which will be
served in the Branner dining hall.
Bids may be secured from Presi
dent Burnett or Weldon Pascoe,
dance committee chairman, and are
available this week only to Branner
Club members. Public sale will start
on Friday, if any bids are still avail
able at that time.
