# Masquers To Conduct Play Trials Next Week
## 
### 
Tryouts for a reading of "The
Counterfeit Wife," a three-act play
by Henry David Gray, will be held
in the Women's Clubhouse this after
noon at 2 o'clock.
The reading is being given by
Masquers, women's honorary dra
matic society, next Wednesday. Try
outs are open to both men end
women.
