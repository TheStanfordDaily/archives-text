# Dionnes Seek Adoption Of Quintuplet Daughters
## 
### 
CALLANDER, Ont., May 8 (£»).—
A request that they be allowed to
adopt their own quintuplet daugh
ters during the "adopt a child week"
has been forwarded by Mr. and Mrs.
Oliva Dionne to David A. Croll, On
tario Minister to Public Welfare, it
was learned today.
Croll announced last week that, in
honor of the first birthday of the
Dionne quintuplets, the ( week of
May 28 was designated as "adopt a
child week." The five baby sisters
arc special wards of the King under
legislation passed by the Ontaria
legislature.
