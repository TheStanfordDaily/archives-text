# Long Beach Minister Gives Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
The Heverend Richard N. Merrill,
preacher of the First Methodist Epis
copal Church in Long Boach, will be
the guest speaker at the service in
the Memorial Church at 11 o'clock
this Sunday.
Music will be furnished by the
choir, under the direction of War
ren D. Allen. Allen will also play
his regular organ recital at 4 o'clock
the same afternoon.
