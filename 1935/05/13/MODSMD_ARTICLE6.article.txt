# Masquers To Read Play By English Professor
## 
### 
"The Counterfeit Wife," prize
winning play of the recent Masquers
contest, written by Professor Henry
David Gray of Ihe English depart
ment, will be given its first public
hearing at the Masquers' reading
Wednesday night in the Little
Theater.
The cast includes Fritzi-Beth
Bowman, Annelies Morgan, Tom
Seller, and Sidney Head. Masquers
President Jean Albertson is direct
ing the production, for which a
charge of 10 cents admission will be
made.
