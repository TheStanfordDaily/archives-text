# Notice To Daily Hawks
## 
### 
Today is the final stall meeting of Ihe
quarter. All members of the editorial staff
are requested to be present. Promotions arc
in the oiling an election is brewing—it be
hooves everyone, especially new reporters, to
lind themselves enough leisure time to report
promptly at 1:1 a. A special treat is in store
for twenty lucky people, lie present and ye
shall be wise.
