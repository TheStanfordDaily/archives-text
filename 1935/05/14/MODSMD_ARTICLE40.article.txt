# OUR HASHER SEZ:
## 
### 
After deliberations on Our Hasher's form chart ol
yesterday, I note with sorrow that among the organi
zations left out was the smoke house. It is, therefore,
with pardonable pride that I nominate Bamette
i "Leviticus" Greenlee as the loveliest man among the
fire-laddies.
The cutest trick of the drivers of the big red ma
chine is to get the hoses out on hot afternoons and
hold "informal" water fights, dubbed "informal" on
their theory that red men trail no tails.
Gentlemen: *
I point with humility
That such great virility
Wrecks Robles' tranquility.
I have received some more unconfirmed reports on
those cute little devils who have been enjoying their
measles epidemics at the Paly hospital. It would seem
that the latest device of the fiends is to disconnect the
door checks which automatically close the doors. The
result is that the nurses push on the unresisting doors
; and fall fiat on their faces.
Judging by a well-decorated and dilapidated car
that graces the front yard of the Beta Theta Pi house,
I the Betas are getting either religious or blasphemous,
or both, by turns. I would like to print more, but I
suppose it would be indelicate, besides, I am expect
ing to see the Betas go to meet their Maker mighty
soon.
Jeremy Fribble dart Wong, our sage and chef,
I makes the retort courteous to the co-op's recent release
to the press about his refusal of prime baoy beef.
Hasher: "What's the matter, Wong?"
Wong: "Co-opt sendum wrong kind meut. Bum,
; velly bum. You writura in paper. You tellum, Co-opt
sometime sendum 14 poun'. sometime 16 poun'. Hash
i boy no gettum meat; cook no gettum meat; bum, velly
bum. You tellum I no likee velly muehee.
( "And this," said Mr. Wong, holding up 17 pounds
[ of juicy steak, "is the unkindest cut of all."
