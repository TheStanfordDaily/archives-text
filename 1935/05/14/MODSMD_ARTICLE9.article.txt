# Board Names Seventeen To Receive Scholarships
## 
### 
Seventeen Newell scholarships
were awarded recently by the Stan
ford Board of Trustees. The schol
arship fund was instituted in 1932
under the will of the late Henry
Newell.
The following students, some of
whom are now enrolled at Stanford,
received the awards: Fred C. Allen,
Yvonne R. Ashton, Nancy Barker,
William R. Brockbank, Robert Buss,
George Crapo, Oakley Evans, Aus
tin Fife, Henry Finch, Robert Gros,
Joe Kingsbury, Mary E. Leary, Mar
ion Nielson, Mary Osmond, Freder
ick Sorenson, John Stimpson, and
Samuel Thurman.
