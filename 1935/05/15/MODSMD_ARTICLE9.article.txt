# 'Counterfeit Wife' Read By Masquers Tonight
## 
### 
"The Counterfeit Wife," prize
winning play in the recent contest
sponsored by Masquers, women's
honorary dramatic society, will be
read tonight in the Little Theater at
8 o'clock.
The play was written by Professor
Henry David Gray of the English de
partment.. In the cast are Zritzi-
Beth Bowman, Annelios Morgan,
Harriette Hall Kilby, Rosemary Sis
trom, Virginia Swafford, Tom Seller,
Sidney Head, and George Z. Wilson.
A charge of 10 cents will be made
for admission.
