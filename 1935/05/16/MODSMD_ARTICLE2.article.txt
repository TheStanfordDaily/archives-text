# Drama Society To Hold Tryouts for Ibsen Play
## 
### 
Fur their final play reading of the
year, Sword and Sandals, men's hon
orary dramatic society, will present
Hcndrik Ibsen's "Hedda Gabler"
next Monday evening in the Little
Theater. Tryouts for the roles will
be held this afternoon from 4 to G
o'clock in the Little Theater. Parts
for three men and four women will
be cast at that time.
Ibsen was the first of the dramatic
realists and was the father of the
modern problem play. "Hedda Gab
ler" is one of his best known works.
Eva Gallienne included it in her
repertoire when she played in San
Francisco last year.
