# Faculty To Lead Groups In Social Science Meet
## 
### 
Two Stanford faculty members,
Edwin Angell Cottrell, professor ol
political science, and John Bennett
Canning, professor of economics,
will lead committee sessions at the
fifth annual meeting of the Social
Science Research Conference of the
Pacific Coast at San Francisco June
12. 13, and 14.
Cottrell will be chairman of the
group discussing "Co-ordination of
Governmental Units," while Canning
will head the "Agencies of Planning
and Control" session.
Serving on the executive commit
tee of the conference are Edgar E.
Robinson, director of citizenship,
ind Eliot G. Mears. director of the
summer quarter. Carl L. Alsberg,
director of the Food Research Insti
tute, is on the program committee.
