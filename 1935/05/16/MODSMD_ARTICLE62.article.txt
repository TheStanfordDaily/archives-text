The Editor, Sir:
j There have been many ideas of
late for improvements about the
campus, but none of them has been
as justifiable as the recent move on
foot concerning the improving of
Angell Field.
After visiting the California cam-
I pus a few weeks ago and seeing
| the wonderful athletic plant that
J they have over there, it seems only
i natural that the Cal men should gain
! the advantage over us that has been
! so clearly shown in the last few
I weeks. How can our trackmen help
i but be handicapped with the poor
' facilities that they have at the pres
| time for their training?

We don't want to see our track
team lo.se the prestige that has been
theirs for so many years, so let's get
everybody behind this drive for the

improving of our dilapidated Angell
Field.
Sincerely,
E. T.
