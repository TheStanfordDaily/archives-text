# Students Must Sign Up For June SERA Positions
## 
### 
All students desiring to work in
June on SERA funds must sign up
by Saturday noon, announced Mrs.
Irene Spinner, student employment
director. Maximum allotments are
17 hours apiece, and work can be
done up to and including June 17.
If vacancies occur owing to finals,
there may he possibilities of students
working the regular maximum of
30 hours, stated Mrs. Spenner. There
will be no SERA work during the
summer.
