# ESCAPED 'POSSUM HINDERS WORK OF THE DAILY STAFF
## 
### 
Nothing ever surprises a
newspaperman, but a 'possum
in the composing room of The
Daily last night was nonethe
less a disrupting influence.
Found in back of the Uni
versity Press, it was caged in a
cardboard box on the compos
ing stone in The Daily's com
posing room. But, in the midst
of the hurly and burly of put
ting a paper together, the crit
ter escaped. Work ceased while
frantic search was instituted
for the little beastie, who was
finally caught by the linotyper
and imprisoned in a larger,
stronger box.
