# LAMSON JURY DISMISSED WITHOUT VERDICT AFTER FOUR DAYS
## 
### 

The Jury trying David Camson, former Stanford University press sales manager, was dismissed
after four days of deliberation without returning a verdict. Lamson was charged with the murder of his
wife and it was his second trial. The jury is pictured during its debate as it returned to the court after
luncheon oreceedina its dismissal. (Associated Press Photo)
