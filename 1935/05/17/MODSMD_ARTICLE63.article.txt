# JUNIORS WIN OVER SOPHS IN WOMEN'S BASEBALL CONTEST
## 
### 
In the second women's baseball
game the juniors won a close con
test from ihe sophs yesterday, 2 to 1.
Ruth Schmidt, pitching for the jun
iors, struck out nine, walked one,
and allowed but two infield hits in
the two and a half innings played.
Marcia McFarland walked three
juniors and allowed three hits. A
pass, a stolen base, and a single in

both the first arid second innings
accounted for the two runs scored by
the upperclassmen.
Frosh and juniors meet this after
noon at 4 o'clock for the final game
of the first series. The second round
of games will begin next week.
