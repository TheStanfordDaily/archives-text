# LENIN STANFORD'S LI'L RED SQUARE
##

Pictured above is the famous quidrangular bidding •where indians,
parlor pinks, vul just plain reds foregather to shout the praise of Com
munism, bash in each other's skulls, and thumb their proboscusscs at
poleeceinen. This scene shows the calm after the beef preceding the next
whale of a exhortation by Fillem Fuluvit.
