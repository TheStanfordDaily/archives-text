# 'Y' Will Install 1935-36 Officers Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
To install the new officers and
cabinet members, the Y.W.C.A.
membership dinner will be held to
morrow evening at 5:45 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Today is
the last time that tickets may be
secured.
Jo Schlatter, former "Y" president,
will preside over the installation,
and Marion Storey will act as toast
mistress. Speakers will include Mrs.
Alma Halverson, president of the
Y.W.C.A. Advisory Board, and Miss
Mary Cady, secretary of the San
Francisco Y.W.C.A.
