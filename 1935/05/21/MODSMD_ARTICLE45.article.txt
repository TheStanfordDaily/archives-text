# Toyon, Branner Rooms Can Be Reserved Today
## 
### 
Men now living in Toyon and
Branner halls who wish to -reserve
rooms for next year should call at
the Dean of Men's office today 01* to
morrow to make their deposits.
Drawings will be held in the offices
of the hails at 5:30 o'clock tomorrow.
Those present residents of Se
quoia, Old Union, and Encina who
wish to reserve rooms in the same
dormitories for next year should do
so at the dean's office Thursday and
Friday, May 23 and 24. Lower divi
sion students who wish rooms in
different halls should arrange for
them on Tuesday, Wednesday, and
Thursday of next week, May 28. 29,
and 30.

A student speaker at a recent
U.C.L.A. open forum claimed that
Huey Long will be the next presi
dent of the United States.
