# STREAMLINED RACER READY FOR INDIANAPOLIS CLASSIC
## 
### 

Wilbur Shaw, Los Angeles race driver, is shown at the wheel of his new self-designed streamlined
racer that he will enter in the 500-mile Memorial Day grind in Indianapolis. It is powered with a 250
cubic inch four cylinder motor designed by Fred Offenhauer of Los Angeles and operates on a front
wheel drive. (Associated Press Poto)
