# Tonight Set as Deadline For Entering Relay Meet
## 
### 
All teams intended to enter the
annual spring relays, to be staged
tomorrow and Thursday, must sign
up in the gym by 6 o'clock tonight,
according to Leroy Wright, junior
track manager. Three events are
scheduled for each day.
Relays run off tomorrow will in
clude the four-man mile, the four
man mile medley, in which two men
will each run a 220, one a 440, and
one an 880, and the four-man 880.
Thursday's events will consist of
the four-man 440, four-man shuttle
120-yard low Jhurdle, and eight-man
mile relays.
