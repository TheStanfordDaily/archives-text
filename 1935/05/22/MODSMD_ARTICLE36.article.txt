# PLAYS CALLED OFF
## 
### 
; Two French plays, "Le Quatri
eme" and "Les Precieuses Ridicules,''
which were to have been given by
the French department tomorrow
night, have been called off. Two of
the leads were stricken with measles,
and their roles could not be filled at
I such short notice.
