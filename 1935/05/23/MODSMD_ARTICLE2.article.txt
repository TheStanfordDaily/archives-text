# H. D. Gray Will Address Philological Group Today
## 
### 
The University Philological Asso
ciation will hold a meeting this af
ternoon at 4:15 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. The guest speakers
for the afternoon will be Professor
A. E. Sokol, who will speak on
"Liebnitz and the German Lan
guage," and Professor H. D. Gray,
whose subject will be "Shakespeare
and the Rival Poet of the Sonnet."
All graduate students and others
interested in the School of Letters
are invited. Refreshments will be
served.
