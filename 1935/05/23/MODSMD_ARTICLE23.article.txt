# Menlo J.C. To Celebrate Homecoming Day Sunday
## 
### 
Menlo School and Junior College
will hold its annual homecoming on
the Menlo campus Sunday afternoon,
May 26. All former students are in
vited.
Features of the day will be a base
ball game, a track meet, and swim
ming meet. Many Stanford stars are
expected to compete in track, among
them Gordon Dunn,- Arne Ander
son, Bill Miller, and possibly Bud
Deacon. A banquet will be held in
the early evening.
