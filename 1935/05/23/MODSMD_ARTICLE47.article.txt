# Tea, Program Planned For Seniors Tuesday
## 
### 
As a last gathering for the women
of the Class of '35, a tea, sponsored
by Kay Rapp, vice-president of the
class, and Marion Storey, president
of Women's Conference, will be held
next Tuesday in the Women's Club
house promptly at 4 o'clock.
All senior women who are attend
ing are urged to come early, as a
program has been planned for the
afternoon.
