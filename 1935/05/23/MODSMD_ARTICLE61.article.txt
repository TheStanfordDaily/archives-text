# Tripp, Guillotte Named Honor Men of R.O.T.C.
## 
### 
Bob Tripp and Dwight Guillotte
were certified yesterday by Colonel
Donald C. Cubbison as honor grad
uates of the' Stanford R.O.T.C. field
artillery unit.
R.O.T.C. units which are accorded
s rating of "excellent" are entitled
to certify 5 per cent of the second
year advanced students as honor
graduates. After the recent annual
inspection, Stanford was again ac
corded an excellent rating, accord
ing to a letter just received by
President Ray Lyman Wilbur from
Major-General Paul B. Malotie,
commanding officer of the Ninth
corps area.
