# Assembly Votes Limited Exemption on Food Tax
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, May 23 (/P). —A
limited exemption of foodstuffs
from the sales tax was voted late
today by the Assembly to the tunc
of a 75 to 0 ballot.
The vote was cast after the House
had rejected the Rosenthal Amend
ments, which would have exempted
all foodstuffs and meals in restau
rants. The vote was 45 to 34.
