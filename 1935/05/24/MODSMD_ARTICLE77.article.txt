# Senior Soccer Team Favored Over Frosh
## 
### 
Uppercla'ss soccermen meet the
frosh today in the third game of the
interclass series. The older fellows
are pre-game favorites as a result
of their dead heat with the leading
sophomores Monday.
Waddy White and Herb Sawyer
lead the upperelass aggregation,
backed up by Rudy Gonzales, Mike
Gomez, and A1 Velarde.
Tlie derby was introduced as a
fashionable novelty in America in
1874.
