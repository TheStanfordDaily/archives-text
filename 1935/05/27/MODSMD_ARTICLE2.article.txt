# Spud Hamilton To Edit Next Year's Handbook
## 
### 
The appointment of Spud Hamil
ton to the editorship of the student
handbook for 1935-36 was made by
Ex Committee at their last meeting.
Hamilton, chosen out of a group of
three applicants, will supervise
work on the copies, which will con
tinue through the summer to allow
them to be issued next quarter.
Approximately 1200 copies will be
made, with the additional features
of larger pages and type included.
The books, designed primarily to ac
quaint new students with the Uni
versity, will be issued to them first,
with any additional copies going to
the old students.
