# Prize for Goethe Essay Given to Mary Osmond
## 
### 
Mary Irene Osmond was awarded
a six-volume set of Goethe's works
on Friday night at the "Goethe-
Abend'' program of the German de
partment for the best essay on the
poet. Andrew E. Terry was awarded
a German volume for second prize.

ings in German by both students
j and professors of the department.
