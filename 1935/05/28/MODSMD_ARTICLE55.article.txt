# Managers of Baseball, Golf, Gymnastics Named
## 
### 
Appointment of Art Allen to the
senior baseball managership was
announced by Alfred Masters, grad
uate manager, last night. Along with
Allen's appointment came word that
Ed Littlefield will hold down the
senior managerial job for golf and
Hayden Price for gymnastics.
Junior baseball managers will be
Robert Dreisbach, Don Eddie, and
Bill Sumerlin; junior golf managers,
Robert Welch, George Hurst, and
Robert Edwards.
