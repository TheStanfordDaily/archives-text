# German Club Sponsors Searsville Picnic Friday
## 
### 
German Club members, headed by
Claire Mitchell, are sponsoring a
picnic to Searsville Friday after
noon, and invites all students in the
German department and staff mem
bers to attend.
Those planning to participate must
sign immediately on the German
department bulletin board. They
meet, at the Administration Build
ing at 2:30 Friday and bring their
own suppers along with 15 cents
each. Coffee will be supplied by
the German Club.
