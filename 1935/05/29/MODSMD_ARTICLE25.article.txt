# Seniors May Still Sign Scroll in Alumni Office
## 
### 
The senior scroll, which will be
placed under the class plate Senior
Week, is still in the Alumni Office,
where members of the Class of '35
should go to sign it. The Alumni
Office is on the second floor of the
Administration Building.
Only seniors who have paid dues
arc eligible to place their signatures
on the scroll. Class membership
! cards must be presented.
