# WOMEN JUDGED INSANE
## 
### 
TEHACHAPI, May 29 (^P).—Mae
S'ockdale, 35, San Joaquin County
robber, who thrice failed in escape
attempts from the California state
prison for women here, and Mabel
F. Dixon. 52. Humboldt County mur
deress, were ordered committed to
Ukiah State Hospital for the insane
today.
