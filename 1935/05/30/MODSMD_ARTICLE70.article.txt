# Elmore Brown Appointed To U.S. Military Academy
## 
### 
Elmore G. Brown, freshman, re
ceived word yesterday from Con
gressman Frank H. Buck, third dis
trict, that he had been appointed to
the United States Military Academy
at Wesl Point. Brown already has a
first alternate appointment to the
Naval Academy at Annapolis.
The appointee has passed the en
trance examination to Annapolis,
but still faces the West Point exam
ination. Should he pass, Brown will
enter the military academy July 1.
If in the meantime he gains prin
cipal position to Annapolis, how
ever, Brown will enroll in that in
stitution.
