# Bob Brown, Ex-A.S.S.U. President, Is Married
## 
### 
Bob Brown, A.S.S.U. president in
1931-32 and varsity trackman, was
married in Berkeley Wednesday to
Gloria Gillingham, a graduate of the
Anna Head School and Mills Col
lege.
Ben Eastman was the best man at
the wedding and several other Stan
ford men were among the ushers.
The couple are planning to make
their home in Palo Alto after re
turning from a motor trip through
Southern California.
