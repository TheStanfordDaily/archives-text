# Rabbi Reichert To Speak In Chapel Service Sunday
## 
### 
Rabbi Irving F. Reichert of the
Temple Emanu-El in San Francisco
will he guest speaker at the Sunday
Service in the Memorial Chapel at
11 o'clock this Sunday.
The Temple Service will be sung
by Cantor Rinder, assisted by the
University choir.
Warren D. Allen will play an or
gan recital at 4 o'clock the same
afternoon. Thursday, June 5, will be
the last recital of this quarter, and
those for summer quarter will start
Sunday, June 23, at 7:30 o'clock.
