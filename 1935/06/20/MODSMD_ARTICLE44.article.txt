# STANFORD COUPLE MARRIED
## 
### 
Miss Jane Hewlett and George
Wright Fuller, whose marriage is to
take place this afternoon at the San
Mateo home of the bridegroom's par
ents, Mr. and Mrs. William Parmer
Fuller, Jr., will live on Roble Ridge
while the bridegroom takes graduate
work in chemistry at Stanford.
Mr. Fuller received his degree from
Stanford Sunday. His bride-elect is
also a Stanford graduate.
