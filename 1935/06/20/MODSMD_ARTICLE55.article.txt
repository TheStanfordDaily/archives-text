# 27 R.O.T.C. Men Start Six Weeks' Camp at Monterey
## 
### 
Twenty-seven cadets from the Stan
ford Reserve Officers Training Corps
will be encamped at the Monterey Pre
sidio for six weeks, beginning today.
They form an artillery unit commanded
by Lieut. Hayden Y. Grubbs and Lieut.
John F. Bird, both of the field artillery
and on duly at Stanford. The Stanford
cadets in the unit are the following:
Charles R. Baird, Jr., Richard B.
Becker, Kenneth E. Bell, Myron L.
Birnbaum, William Boswell, Won Loy
Chan, Robert O. Coles, Charles R.
Creamer, Robert Fullerton, John H.
Gill, Francis K. Holman, John K. Hub
bard, Hamilton Lawrence, George Q.
Lee, Arthur A. Lundin, Milton I. Ma
cowsky, David H. McKell, Edward J.
McKeon, John F. McPeak, David S.
Mcllinkoff, Robin P. Michelson, John
W. Morse, Samuel T. Pinckney, Rob
ert A. Ranzoni, Monroe R. Rubin, Don
ald W. Smith, Charles ,W. Thomas,
Louis S. Tsoutsauvas, John C. Waller.
