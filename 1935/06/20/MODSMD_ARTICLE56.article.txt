# DR. LUTZ TO SPEAK IN S.F.
## 
### 
"Present Conditions in Germany"
will be the subject .of a talk by Dr.
Ralph H. Lutz at a luncheon meeting
of the Commonwealth Club tomorrow
at the Palace Hotel in San Francisco.
Dr. Lutz, who received his Ph.D. at
Heidelberg in 1910, is a professor of
history and Dean of Graduate Study at
Stanford.
The average mid-day temperature at
Stanford during the summer is 70 de
grees.
