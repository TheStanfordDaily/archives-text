# PI LAMBDA THETA TEA
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, women's national
honorary educational society, will give
a tea Wednesday, June 26. at 4 p.m.
Miss Louise Price is in charge of ar
rangements. This is the first of a series
of entertainments by the group. The
education department faculty and their
wives, and women registered with the
department, are invited to attend.
