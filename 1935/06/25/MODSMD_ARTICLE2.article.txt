# FENTON TO SKETCH ELIZABETHAN PERIOD
## 
### 
Frank Fenton will deliver the first
of the Tudor lectures on "The Com
moner Queen," in which he will stress
the importance of Elizabeth to the Eliz
abethan age. Mr. Fenton will sketch
the diplomatic and political history of
the time, Elizabeth's marriage plans,
and the relationships between Eliza
beth and the citizens.
Tlie lecture will be held at the Wom
en's Clubhouse, Stanford Union, to
morrow at 7:30 o'clock; the public is
invited to attend. The English Club
will serve light refreshments and there
will be an opportunity for the students
and faculty of the course in Shake
spearean production to discuss their
dramatic problems.
