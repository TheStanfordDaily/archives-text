# X-Ray Uses to Be Topic Of Newell Radio Broadcast
## 
### 
"Why and How a Doctor Uses X-
Rays" will be the subject of a radio
talk to be broadcast over Station KGO
Monday night, July 1, from 8 to 8:15
by Dr. Robert Reid Newell, professor
of medicine (radiology) at Stanford.
Dr. Newell received the doctor of
medicine degree at the University of
California Medical School in 1916 and
was named assistant in medicine there
in 1917. He has been a member of the
Stanford School of Medicine faculty
since 1920.
