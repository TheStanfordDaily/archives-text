# First Pro Arte Concert To Be Given on July 2
## 
### 
The Pro Arte Quartet of Brussels
will give four concerts in the Memo
rial Church on Tuesday evenings, July
2, 9, 16, and 23, at 8 o'clock. These
concerts are open to the public with
out admission charge and are made
possible through the generosity of Mrs.
Elizabeth Sprague Coolidge.
The program for July 2 includes a
Bach choral prelude, by M. Wood Hill,
and a fugue from "The Art of the
Fugue" by Roy Harris and Mrs. Her
ter-Norton; Quartet in C, Opus 71, by
David Stanley Smith; and a quartet
by Albert Roussel.
