# Stanford Man Killed In Orient Swimming Mishap
## 
### 
Charles Francis "Chuck" Gambell,
baseball manager at Stanford in 1925
and a member of Theta Delta Chi, was
killed recently in Canton, China, as a
result of a broken neck suffered when
he dove into the shallow end of a swim
ming pool.
Mr. Gambell was Canton manager of
the Dollar Steamship Lines and is sur
vived by his widow, Mrs. Alice Mc-
Donald Gambell.
