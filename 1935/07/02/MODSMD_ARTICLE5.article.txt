# Annual Education Text Exhibit To Open Monday
## 
### 
The annual textbook exhibit of the
School of Education will open Monday
under the direction of Mrs. Margaret
Wells, education librarian. Thirty
eight publishers are co-operating in
this display. Materials will represent,
the elementary, junior, and senior high
schools, and college levels.
The publishers will show texts and
materials representing the new and ad
vanced ideas in education. Other teach
ing helps, such as mimeograph ma
chines and supplies, maps, art mate
rials, and ideas on methods will be
displayed.
The exhibit will be open from 8 to 6
o'clock in Room 300.
