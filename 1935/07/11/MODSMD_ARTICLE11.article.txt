# FRENCH BANQUET SCHEDULED
## 
### 
Pi Delta Phi, national French hon
orary fraternity, will have its initia
tion to be followed by a banquet in the
Union, July 18. at 6:30 o'clock. The
principal speakers will be Professor
Jean-Marie Carre and Mr. Jean Quin
ton.
Those interested in French are cor
dially invited to attend the banquet.
Reservations should be made with
Professor Schwartz.
