# Tuesday Evening Jolly-Up To Be First Social Event
## 
### 
In response to the demand for social
activities, the Union living group, in
cluding Madrona and Mariposa, will
give a jolly-up Tuesday evening. It
will be held in the Women's Clubhouse
from 7 to 8 o'clock.
Bridge tables will be set up for those
who do not care to dance. It is hoped
that the response to this first social
event will be sufficient to make pos
sible the hiring of an orchestra.
