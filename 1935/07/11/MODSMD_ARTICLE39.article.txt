# TOYON DANCE SCHEDULED
## 
### 
The first dance of the quarter will
be given in the Toyon lobby from 10
to 1 o'clock Friday night, July' 26.
Bids will be on sale at $1 starting Tues
day. July 18. Committee members are
Burt Goodman, chairman; Bill Boyd,
Bernie Colley, Bill Smith, and Sid Foor
man.
"Mind your P's and Q's" originated
in the public houses of olden days. "P"
stands for pints and "Q" for quarts.
Horses and carriages used to be driv
en into the Palm Court of the Palace
Hotel in San Francisco.
