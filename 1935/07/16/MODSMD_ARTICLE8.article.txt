# Tudor Lecture Tomorrow Will Be on Lyric Poetry
## 
### 
Dr. A. Yvor Winters, instructor in
English, will speak on "Lyric Poetry in
the Sixteenth Century" Wednesday
night, in the Women's Clubhouse. This
lecture is one of the Tudor series and
will begin promptly at 7:30.
Dr. Winters, poet and critic, has writ
ten several volumes of poems and has
contributed to "Hound and Horn" and
"The Bookman." He took his A.B. and
A.M. at Columbia in 1925 and taught
i at the University of Idaho from 1925 to
1 1927. In 1927 he came to Stanford to
spend a year as a graduate student
and since 1928 has taught here in the
English department.
