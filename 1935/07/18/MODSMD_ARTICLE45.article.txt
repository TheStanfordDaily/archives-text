# Toyon Dance Bids on Sale At Student Body Office
## 
### 
Bids are being sold at the A.S.S.U.
office starting this morning for the
Toyon dance July 26. Bids are priced
at $1. Eddie Stirm's orchestra will play
in the Toyon Lobby from 10 to 1 o'clock.
Attractive decorations featuring indi
rect lighting will be used at this first
all-University dance.
