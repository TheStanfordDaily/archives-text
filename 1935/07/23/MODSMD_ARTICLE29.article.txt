# DR. F. O. KOENIG TO GIVE FIFTH OF TUDOR LECTURES
## 
### 
"Physical Sciences in Tudor Times"
will be the subject of a lecture to be
given by Dr. Frederick Koenig, of the
Department of Chemistry, in the Wom
en's Clubhouse tomorrow night at 7:30
o'clock.
This is the fifth of a series of eight
lectures on Shakespeare's times.. There
is no admission charge and the public
is incited.
