# Illustrated French Talk To Be Given by Dr. Carre
## 
### 
An illustrated lecture in French by
Jean-Marie Carre, acting professor of
Romanic languages, will be given this
evening at 8 o'clock in the Little The
ater. The subject of M. Can re's lecture
will be, "Voyage a Travels l'Egypte
Antique et Moderne."
Professor of comparative literature at
Lyons, M. Carre was a member of
the visiting faculty during the summer
of 1926. Prior to 1933 he served as head
of the French department at the Royal
University of Cairo.
