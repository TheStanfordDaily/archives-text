ponent this I all? Surely a team that has made such a line record
is entitled lo an eastern trip.
Our team this fall is one of Ihc greatest ever assembled at Stan
ford. Three acknowledged all-Americans will be in the line-up as
well as another player that was selected as the outstanding player
of the last season.
In making selections for all-star teams eastern scribes are in
clined to favor men participating in intersectional games. It is
only fair lo the men on the team that they have the chance to display
their prowess to these critics as well as to local experts. Surely it is
not impossible lo find an opponent for pur team.—C. A. M.
