# Dr. Ralph Lutz To Speak At Educational Luncheon
## 
### 
"The European Political Situation" is
the topic to be discussed by Dr. Ralph
Lutz, dean of graduate study and
chairman of the directors of the Hoover
War Library at Stanford, at the 12:10
educational luncheon in the Union to
day.
Dr. Lutz has had close contact with
the situation in Europe, having re
cently returned from his fifth European
trip since the war. He is especially
interested in the problems of Germany
and has written several books con
cerning the history of that country.
