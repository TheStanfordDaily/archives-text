## 
###

dangerous internal hemorrhage Satur
day morning at Bohemian Grove on
the Russian River, where he was spend
ing his vacation. ,
He was rushed to Dante Sanatorium
in San Francisco, where physicians
said the presence of Dr. Harry Wicl,
who treated him when he was "stricken,
may have saved his life.
Mr. Norris, a student at the Stanford
Medical School, will remain in the hos
