# ALLEN TO SPEAK ON MUSIC
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen will speak on the
"Social Significance of Music" at the
final education luncheon which will be
held Thursday at 12:10 o'clock in the
Union.
Mr. Allen is organist and lecturer in
music at Stanford.

The age limits for entrance to the
Stanford School of Nursing are 18 and
30.
The Lagunita boathouse was built
in 1892 for $400.
