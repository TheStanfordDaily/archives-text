# Second Summer Jolly-Up To Be Given in Clubhouse
## 
### 
A jolly-up will be given tonight from
7 to 8 o'clock in the Women's Club
house. The program, arranged for by
members of the Union Club, will in
clude special tables for bridge.
The members of the committee in
clude Dorothy Walker, Ann Waljer,
and Ann Van Wagenen.
