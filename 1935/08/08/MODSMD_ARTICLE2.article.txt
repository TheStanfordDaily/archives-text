# Indian Contralto Presents Many Chants and Legends
## 
### 
Ataloa presented Tuesday night a
program of Indian songs and chants in
Ihe Sioux, Chippewa, Kiowa, Zuni, and
Ojibway tongues. She also offered a
series of Indian legends entitled "How
Things Came To Be."
Ataloa is a Chickasaw Indian, her
name meaning 'kittle Song." She is an
authority on Indian customs and cere
monies and is an instructor at Bacone
College, America's only college for In
dians.
