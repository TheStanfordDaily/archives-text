# National Society Elects Campus Engineering Head
## 
### 
Professor Arthur B. Domonoske, ex
ecutive head of the Department of Me
chanical Engineering at Stanford, has
been elected to membership in the
Council of the Society for Promotion of
Engineering Education for a three-year

sor Domonoske has been at Stanford
