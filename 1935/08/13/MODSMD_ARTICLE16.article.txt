# Education Courses End
## 
### 
This week of school concludes the summer studies of the edu
cation graduates on the Stanford campus as well as the work of
many students who have enrolled for only eight weeks. We of the
Stanioiu) Daily hope that our work has fulfilled the purposes of
keeping them posted on the happenings of the campus as well as
giving an insight into the personalities and careers of visiting pro
fessors whom Stanford is so proud to have here this summer.—-
V. H.
