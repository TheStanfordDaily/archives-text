# Hardin Craig Will Deliver Wednesday Night Lecture
## 
### 
Dr. Hardin Craig of this University
will deliver the eighth of the Tudor
lectures tomorrow evening when he
speaks on "Shakespeare's Metaphysical
System."
Dr. Craig is professor of English and
chairman of the School of Letters. The
lecture will be in the Women's Club
house and will begin at 7:30 o'clock. As
usual, light refreshments will be served
following the talk.
Dr. J. C. Hinsey, professor of anat
omy, is faculty representative on the
Pacific Coast Athletic Conference.
