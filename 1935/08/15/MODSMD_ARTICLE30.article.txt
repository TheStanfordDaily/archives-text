# Stanford Hurdler Weds San Raphael Girl in Reno
## 
### 
A wedding which culminated the
high school romance of a Stanford
track star was solemnized yesterday
when Sam Klopstock and Miss Helen
Aldrick, daughter of Mr. and Mrs.
W. H. Aldrick of San Rafael, were
married in Reno.
Klopstock is the son of Mr. and
Mrs. S. C. Klopstock of San Fran
cisco. He is a graduate of Tamalpais
Military School and San Rafael Hifch
School and will be a senior at Stanford
next year. He won the national hur
dles championship in 1934,
California had possession of the Stan
ford Axe for 31 years.
