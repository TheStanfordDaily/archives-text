# DR. W. M. PROCTER TO ATTEND MEET
## 
### 
Dr. William M. Proctor, professor of
education at Stanford, will attend the
Conference for the Co-opex-ative Study
of Secondary School Standards to be
held at Montreat, near Ashville,
North Carolina, September 2 to 7.
Dr. Proctor will go as the representa
tive of the Western Association of
Colleges and Secondary Schools. The
meeting will be attended by a commit
tee of 21 members who represent all
regional accrediting associations in the
country.
