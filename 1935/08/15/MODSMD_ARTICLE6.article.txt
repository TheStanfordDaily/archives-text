# Visiting Pastor To Talk Sunday on 'Personality'
## 
### 
The Rev. Clarence A. Spaulding will
speak on "Possessing and Inspiring
Personality" at the Memorial Church
Sunday morning at 11 o'clock. Dr.
Spaulding is the pastor of the First
Presbyterian Church of River Forest,
Illinois. The anthem will be sung by
Mrs. H. P. Hansen, soprano soloist.
She will be accompanied by Endicott
Hanson, acting organist.
