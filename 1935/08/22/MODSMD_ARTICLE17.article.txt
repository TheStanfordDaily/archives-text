# Glasgow-Wright Wedding Set for October Twelfth
## 
### 
Christ Church at Sausalito will be
the setting for Miss Janet Wright's
wedding to Charles Stuart Glasgow,
Jr., on October 12. A reception will
follow at the Sausalito home of the
bride's parents, Mr. and Mrs. William
Quinby Wright.
Both Miss Wright and Mr. Glasgow
are Stanford graduates. Their engage
ment was announced at the Alpha Phi
hous« In the spring.
