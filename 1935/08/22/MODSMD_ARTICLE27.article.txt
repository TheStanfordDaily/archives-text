# PLAY LEAD TAKEN BY DOROTHY TULLY
## 
### 
Dorothy Tully, recent Stanford grad
uate, who starred in many campus
dramatic productions during her un
dergraduate years here, is cast in the
leading role of Laura Merrick in "The
Vinegar Tree," farce-comedy, presented
by the Palo Alto Community Players
at the Civic Theater tonight, Friday,
and Saturday at 8:15.
Two attractive sets for the play have
been designed by and constructed un
der the direction of Carroll Alexander,
a Stanford graduate.
