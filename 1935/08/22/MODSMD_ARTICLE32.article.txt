# Miss Brett Will Present Organ Recital This Week
## 
### 
Ethel Sleeper Brett, visiting organist
from Sacramento, will give the organ
recitals this week. Recitals will be re
sumed on Thursday, September 26, by
Raymond Kendall, acting organist.
The program to be presented Thurs
day afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in the
Stanford Memorial Church is as fol
lows:
Introspection. .Frederick Stanley Smith
Bouree in D (in the old style)
Wallace A. Sabin
Ave Maria Arcadelt Liszt
Marche Triomphale Karg-Elert
Toccata (sth Symphony).. C. M. Widor
In Paradisum Dubois
