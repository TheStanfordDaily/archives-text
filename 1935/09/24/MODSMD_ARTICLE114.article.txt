# Engineering Group To Hold Meetings In San Francisco
## 
### 
The American Institute of Mining
and Engineering will convene at the
Palace Hotel in San Francisco from
October 3 to 6. Prominent among
those to attend are Dean Theodore J.
Hoover, chairman of the American
Institute of Mining and Metallurgi
cal groups, and Professor O. C.
Shepard, chairman of the mining
students' section. Several papers
will be presented to the body by
Stanford students.
