# Position of B.A.C. Officials Rearranged
## 
### 
Rearrangement of positions of
B A.C. officials has been announced
by Graduate Manager A1 Masters.
The movements were made to take
care of a vacancy left by O. H. Tuck
er, former manager of operations,
who received a position as construc
tion engineer on the Coyote dam.
Under the new setup Jake Irwin,
former ticket manager, takes over
the managership of operations to re
place Tucker, and Miss Eunice Du-
Prau steps into the ticket handling
position. Miss DuPrau was formerly
Masters' personal secretary.
