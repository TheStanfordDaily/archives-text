# Cross-Country Men Take Physical Exam
## 
### 
Prospective cross-country men
will line up before University phy
sicians Friday at 3 p.m. for medical
examination, and then report to the
gym to receive their track uniforms,
it was announced today by officials
of the Board of Athletic Control.
Pending Completion of the new
track, practice is to be held in tho
stadium.
Although the usual meet with
Cal is planned to take place on
the campus, the line-up is still un
certain, with only Bob Alexander
and Edson Burroughs expected to
return as team members.
