# University of Oregon's Gridiron Hopes
## 
### 

Starting his fourth year as head coach, "Prink" Callison of the
University of Oregon faces the task of building an eleven for a long,
tough schedule. Among the veterans he has to call on are Frank
Michek, veteran line-smashing fullback who can also do the punting,
and Stanley Riordan, pass-catching end from Pasadena, Calif. (Asso
ciated Press Photos)
