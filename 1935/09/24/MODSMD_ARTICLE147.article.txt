# KNIGHT SCORES T.K.O
## 
### 
HAVANA, Sept. 23 (£>). — Joe
Knight of Miami scored a technical
knockout Sunday night over Pepon
Gonzalez of Spain in the eighth
round of a scheduled 10-round bout.
Gonzalez, who claims the light heavy
weight championship of Spain, lost
every round. Kid Sila knocked out
Manolo Gomez of Spain in the sixth.
