# Reinhardt To Give Two U.C. Courses
## 
### 
Dr. Kurt F. Reinhardt, associate
professor of German at Stanford,
will again offer two courses in the
University of California extension
division, it was announced today.
Dr. Reinhardt's courses will be
"Truth in a Changing World" and
"Scholastic Philosophy and Modern
Life." They will begin September
27, meeting at 7 and 8:30 o'clock
every Friday evening.
Six members of the St. Mary's
football team belong to the school's
scholastic society.
