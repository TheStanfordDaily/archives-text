# QUAKE IN PACIFIC
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Sept. 23 (/P). —
An earthquake described as severe
was recorded on the seismograph at
! Georgetown University today.
The disturbance was given at
9500 miles to the northwest, placing
it in the region of New Guinea in
[the South Pacific.
