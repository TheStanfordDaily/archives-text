# Professor Frankel To Teach Classics
## 
### 
Professor Hermann Ferdinand
Frankel, formerly professor extraor
dinarius in the University of Got
tingen, Germany, has come to Stan
ford as acting professor of classics.
Dr. Frankel, with Mrs. Frankel
and their son, Hans, who is enroll
ing as a junior at Stanford, made
the trip from Bremen to San Fran
cisco via the Panama Canal. Inter
nationally known for his books and
articles on Latin and Greek litera
ture, Dr. Frankel is offering ad
vanced courses on Hesiod. Early
Latin Epic, Seneca's Epistles, and
the lyric poets Sappo and Alcaeus.
