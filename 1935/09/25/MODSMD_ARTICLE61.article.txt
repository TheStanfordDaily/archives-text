# German Dramatic Group To Gather
## 
### 
There will be a preliminary meet
ing for all students interested in
participating in the production of
"Jederman," the German version of
the miracle play "Everyman," to be
performed by Dr. Kurt Reinhardt's
class in German drama and stage
craft during winter quarter.
Rehearsals will begin next week,
with the preliminary meeting
scheduled for Friday afternoon at 2
o'clock in room 119 A. The class
is listed in the Time Schedule as
C. 161: Die deutsche Sehaubuhne.
