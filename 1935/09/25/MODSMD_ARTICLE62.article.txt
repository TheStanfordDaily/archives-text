# JAPANS TYPHOON
## 
### 
TOKIO, Sept. 24 (/P). —A typhoon
swept over Japan from Kagoshima
to Tokio today, high winds and tor
rential rains destroying scores of
homes, flooding thousands of others,
disrupting communications and ty
ing up shipping on the Inland Sea.
