# Wilbur Home Scene of Two Social Affairs
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur will entertain at their home
on Lagunita Knoll this evening,
from 8:30 to 11 o'clock, with a fac
ulty reception. The affair is given
annually at the beginning of autumn
quarter and is attended by all mem
bers of the faculty.
Saturday evening at 8 o'clock they
will again be at home informally at
the annual reception for all new
lower division students. Unlike
former years, new upper division
transfers will not attend this affair,
but will be entertained later on in
the quarter.
