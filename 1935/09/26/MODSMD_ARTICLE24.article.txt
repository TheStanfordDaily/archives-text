# Hinkle Recovered From Auto Injuries
## 
### 
George Hinkle, professor of Eng
lish, and his wife were injured last
July when their automobile was
struck by an unlighted train at a
grade crossing near Tahoe City.
Though he suffered a concussion
and a broken nose, the professor is
back on the campus in his teaching
capacity. Mrs. Hinkle is rapidly re
covering from a broken wrist and
superficial bruises and scratches.
The Hinkles' automobile was almost
completely demolished.
