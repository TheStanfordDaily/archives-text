# Autumn Drama Selection Soon
## 
### 
Selection of the fall quarter play
will be made tomorrow at the first
meeting of Dramatic Council. The
first play of the year will be chosen
with a view to student interest, and
will probably be one of the more re
cent dramas.
Dramatic Council is composed of
William Murray Timmons, head of
dramatics; Dr. Lee Emerson Bassett,
faculty member; Frank O'Neil,
Sword and Sandals; Winstead Weav
er, Ram's Head; Rosemary Bcnet,
masquers; James Copp, Chaparral;
Phil Bernheim, Daily; Bob Gros,
student manager; Rex Hardy, dra
matic manager; Jean Albertson,
member at large.
