# River Blaze Blocked By London Firemen
## 
###

! the biggest riverside fires London
has seen in years turned the Colonial
wharves into a raging inferno to
; night.
j More than 200 firemen battled the
| blaze for more than six hours from
! floats on the river and water towers
! adjoining the buildings. Raw rub
j ber stored in the Colonial warehouse
| fed the flames.
