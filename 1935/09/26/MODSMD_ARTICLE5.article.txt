# Wilbur, Yost, Culver Speak At Assembly
## 
### 
All 9 o'clock classes will be dis
missed today enabling students to
attend an all-University assembly to
be held in the Assembly Hall.
Scheduled to speak, welcoming
both new and old students to Stan
ford for the current year and at the
same time describing campus activi
ties and traditions, are President
Ray Lyman Wilbur, Dean Mary
Yost, and Dean George B. Culver.
The program, described as the an
nual welcoming assembly by mem
bers of the Public Exercises Com
mittee, is a Stanford tradition in it
self, having been conducted as an
opening week feature of many
school years.
