# Dramatic Manager Seeking Assistants
## 
### 
Dramatic Manager Rex Hardy is
still waiting to interview all sopho
more and junior men and women
who are interested in trying out for
the positions of assistant to him for
this year. He will be in his office in
the Union every afternoon this week
from 1 to 5 o'clock.
The salaried position of dramatic
manager will be open to either the
sophomore or junior assistant man
ager at the end of a year of valuable
office training in business methods.
