# Y.W.C.A. To Close Membership Drive
## 
### 
On Thursday, October 3, the Y.W.
C.A. will climax its membership
drive with a dinner in honor of its
new members. Wilma Baker is in
charge of the plans for the dinner,
which will be held in the Union Din
ing Hall at 6:30 o'clock. It will be
preceded by a recognition service in
the Chapel at 5:15 o'clock, to be pre
sided over by Elizabeth McClelland.
The Y.W.C.A. membership drive
opened Tuesday with a tea for the
new women. The remainder of the
time is being spent in interviewing
prospective members. Barbara
Steinbeck is in charge of the drive.
