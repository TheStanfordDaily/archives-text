# Britain, Italy Compared
## 
### 

This chart shows the land, sea and air forces at the disposal of
Italy and Great Britain, whose naval strength has been massed in the
Mediterranean Sea as a result of the Italo-Ethiopian crisis. Italy's
peacetime army of about 250,000 has been increased because of pos
sible war. Each soldier in the chart represents 500,000 men. (Asso
ciated Press Photo)
