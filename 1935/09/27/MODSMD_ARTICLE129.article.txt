# Board, Room Jobs Available for Many
## 
### 
Several room and board jobs are
available in the student employ
ment office, according to Mrs. Irene
W. Coverley, employment head.
There are also several board jobs for
students who could cook, and many
room jobs, most of which are in Palo
Alto.
Quite a number of selling positions
also open, all of which are part-time
work for commission.
