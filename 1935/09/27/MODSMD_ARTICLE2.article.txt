# Frosh Trek To Chapel Sunday
## 
### 
Matriculation services for new
Lower Division services will be
conducted in the Memorial Church
Sunday, September 29, at 11 o'clock.
Men and women sponsors v/ill also
attend the services, which are open
to other students and friends.
Addresses will be given by Dr.
Wilbur, Dean Yost, and Dean Cul
ver. The following anthems will be
sung:
Cast Thy Burden on the Lord....
Mendelssohn
Sanctus Gounod
Soloist, W. Madison Taylor
Raymond Kendall, acting Univer
sity organist, will give a recital in
the Chapel Sunday at 4 o'clock.
