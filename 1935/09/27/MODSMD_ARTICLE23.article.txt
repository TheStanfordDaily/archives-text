# Students Invited To Mining Convention
## 
### 
Students interested in Geology,
Mining, or Metallurgy are invited
to attend the 1933 regional meeting
of the American Mining and Metal
lurgical Engineers which will be
held at the Palace Hotel in San
Francisco on October 3 to 6 inclu
sive. Dean Theodore J. Hoover will
be on the reception committee, and
Professor O. C. Shepard is to be
the mining students' committee for
this meeting.
Technical papers will be presented
at meetings on Thursday and Fri
day. A field trip to the mines in the
Grass Valley district and to Yuba
Dredge No. 17 is planned for Satur
day and Sunday.
