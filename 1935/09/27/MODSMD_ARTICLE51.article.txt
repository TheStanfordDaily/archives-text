# TIRED OF MOVING
## 
### 
SEDALIA, Mo, Sept. 26 (/P). —
Perry T. Goings is weary of moving.
He filed suit today for divorce from
his wife, Alice, alleging they have
moved 65 times at her instigation
since they were married in 1924.
