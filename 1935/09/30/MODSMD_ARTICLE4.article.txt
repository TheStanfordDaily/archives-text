# Today Marks Beginning Of Daily Tryouts
## 
### 
Positions in all fields of journal
ism are open to any and all Stanford
men and women on The Stanford
Daily. Tryouts will start today at 5
o'clock in the journalism library of
the Press building, when all aspir
ants will meet with Jane Dodge,
women's editor.
This meeting will be followed by a
brief training period, which will
consist of covering assigned stories
and working in The Daily office.
At the end of two weeks tryout
ees will be selected to fill the large
number of vacancies on The Duily
caused by graduation and failures to
return to school. Opportunities for
writing features, sports, and straight
news will be open to the applicants.
Successful tryoutees will be an
nounced in The Daily on October 14.
