# 'Y' Recognition Rites To Be Held Thursday
## 
### 
As a concluding feature to the
Y.W.C.A. membership drive a recog
nition service will be held in the
Chapel at 5:15 o'clock Thursday. It
will include a candlelighting cere
mony, special choral music by Ray
mond Kindle, the new organist, and
addresses to the new members by
Elizabeth McClelland and Elizabeth
Lam.
Patricia Hamilton is in charge of
the service, Jacqueline Brown in
charge of the ushers, and Katherine
Bates will be in charge of the music.
The service will be followed by a
dinner in honor of the 40th anni
versary of the Y. Wilma Baker
heads the arrangements committee.
