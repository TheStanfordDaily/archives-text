# Fraternities Plan Strict Rush Rules
## 
### 
Rushing rules are to be very
strictly enforced this year, it was
brought out at the first meeting of
the Interfraternity Council at the
Alpha Delt house last night. "These
rules are the agreement of the fra
ternity men and it is their duty to
observe them," said Ed Littlefield,
president of the council. The Inter
fraternity Council is determined to
enforce a strict adherence to these
rules.
Purpose of the meeting was to or
ganize and make plans for the en
suing year. A special committee was
also appointed to select the winner
of the Bruce Macon Tarver schol
arship cup. The committee is headed
by Bob Mackenzie and includes
Warren Monroe and Chuck Fontius.
