# Don Blading, Poet, Here on October 7
## 
### 
Don Blading, outstanding Ha
waiian poet, will be at the Stanford
Bookstore next Monday afternoon
from 3 to 5 o'clock to autograph
I copies of his books which are illus
trated with his own pen-and-ink
sketches. He will also read selections
from his verse and recount anec
dotes of the islands.
"Memory Room" is the title of
Blading's most recent work. He is
author of the widely known "Vaga
bond's House."
