# Alumni to Hear Hoover, Wilbur
## 
### 
Highlighting the Stanford alumni
dinner to be held in Now York City
Thursday, October 10, will be the
broadcast addresses of former Pres
ident Herbert Hoover, Dr. Ray Ly
man Wilbur, and a representative of
an important educational foundation.
The theme of the dinner, which is
sponsored by Stanford Associates,
will be "The University of Tomor
row." The three principal addresses
will be broadcast over the NBC net
work from 6:30 to 7 o'clock, Pacific
time, it was announced by Harlow
Rothert, alumni secretary.
President Wilbur will leave Stan
ford Sunday night for the trip east.
In addition to"the New York meet
ing, Dr. Wilbur will speak before a
gathering of Stanford alumni in
Philadelphia on October 11. He also
plans to transact University business
in New York and Chicago on his
trip.
