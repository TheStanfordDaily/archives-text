# Roble Women Hold Activities Gathering
## 
### 
Speakers at the annual activities
dinner last night urged Roble wom
en to participate in at least one
extra-curricular activity. Leaders
in various organizations spoke, each
one pointing out the advantages
which her activity offers.
Speakers were: Bertha Calmenson,
debating; Rosemary Benet, dramat
ics; Amelia Baer, Women's Athletic
Association; Betsy McClelland,
Y.W.C.A.; Jane Dodge, publications;
Mercedes Bergman, Women's Con
ference; and Peg Schaeffer, Women's
Council.
