# Daily Aspirants To Meet in Afternoon
## 
### 
Students interested in joining the
Daily staff may still try out for it
by coming to the tryoutee meeting
scheduled for 5 o'clock this after
noon in the library of the Press
building. Tryoutecs arc expected to
work at least five hours a week in
the Daily office. If a tryoutee misses
two meetings with no adequate ex
cuse he is dropped from the list.
The names of those who are suc
cessful in securing positions on the
staff will be announced October 14.
