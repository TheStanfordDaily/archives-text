# 'Y' Groups, Soph Women To Meet
## 
### 
For the purpose of reuniting the
women of the Class of '38, the Sopho
more Club of the Y.W.C.A. will hold
its first meeting of the quarter in the
Women's Clubhouse Monday at 4
o'clock under the leadership of Jac
queline Brown.
The Racial Problems Group will
meet Monday in the Women's Club
house at 4:30 o'clock. All other in
terest groups will hold their first
meeting during the coming week.
