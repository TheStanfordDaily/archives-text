# Bawlout Proofs To Be Posted at Ad Building Today
## 
### 
Bawlout proofs will be posted
today on the bulletin board in
front of the Administration
building. Corrections can be
made at the information win
dow until 5 o'clock.

published at the first of every
quarter, containing the names,
residences, and scholastic aver
