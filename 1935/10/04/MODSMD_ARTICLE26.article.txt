# CONSUL BURNS HOME
## 
### 
LONDON. Oct. 3 (jP).—A Reuters
dispatch from Addis Ababa reported
tonight that Italian Consul Franca
at Aduwa, before leaving, burned
the consulate and that Ethiopians
believed Italy may accuse them of
lie fur a. an excuse for an invasion.
