# Obsequies Planned For Pioneer Editor
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 3 (/P).-Fu
neral services will be held at Glen
dale Saturday morning for William
E. Simpson, 76, former publisher of
the San Diego Union, who died yes
terday.
Born in Ireland, Simpson came to

California 45 years ago. After sell
ing his newspaper in San Diego, he
came to Los Angeles and entered the
real estate business.
A professor at Boston University
values control of the comic strip
more than of editorials, because
funnies are powerful moulders of
public opinion.
