# Y.W.C.A. Meeting Plans Money Drive
## 
### 
At the first Y.W.C.A. cabinet meet
ing, held Wednesday in the women's
clubrooms, plans were made for the
banquet and for a finance drive to
be started in October with a tea
given by the "Y" board, the date for
which will be announced later.
Appointments with Miss Lam, "Y"
director, are to be available for one
half hour each week. Cabinet meet
ings will be held regularly Wednes
day at 4::i0 o'clock. Members of the
cabinet for this quarter are: Betsy
McClelland, president; Adrienne
Johnston, vice-president; Esther
Pelton, secretary; and Peggy Wood
ard, treasurer.
