# Anniversary Dinner Held by 'Y' Women
## 
### 
Celebrating its 40th anniversary,
the Y.W.C.A. held a dinner at the
Union dining room last evening.
Speakers were Dr. Ellen Smith
Sadmuller, '07, San Francisco phy
sician; Mrs. John Kohler, '26, judge
of the San Francisco Juvenile Court;
Mrs. Carol Green Wilson, former
editor of the Stanford Illustrated
Review; Miss Benetta Merner, '30;
and Mrs. J. W. Mitchell, a member
of the "Y" advisory board for 35
years. Entertainment for the eve
ning was furnished by Miss Takeu
chi, who presented a group of Japa
nese songs and dances.
