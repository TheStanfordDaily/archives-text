# Playwriting Contest Opened to Students
## 
### 
All aspiring playwrights now have
a chance to try their hand in ear
nest, for the annual Masquers' play
writing contest is now open. Resi
dents of Palo Alto and the Stanford
campus are eligible to enter, with
contributions of one or three-act
plays.
Writings must be turned in to
Miss Buckingham or any member
of the dramatic society, of which
Rosemary Benet is president. The
deadline for entries has not yet been
decided upon, but the winning work
will be read on November 7.
