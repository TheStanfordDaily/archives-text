# Wilburs 'At Home' For the Transfers
## 
### 
An informal reception for new up
per division and graduate transfers
will be held tomorrow night at 8
o'clock by President and Mrs. Ray
Lyman Wilbur at their home.
Various members of the faculty,
deans, and some seniors will be
present at the reception. Lower di
vision women who did not attend
the reception last Saturday are also
invited to attend.
