# ANNOUNCE LAST TRYOUT
## 
### 
The final tryout for the Stanford
Glee Club will be held at 8 o'clock
tonight in the old Roble gymnasium,
according to Manager John F. Ellis.
Final selection of the club will be
made from this group about No
vember 1.
One of the last bits of Will Rog
ers' humor was found in his message
to youth. It read: "Don't miss the
next Mae West show."
