# Excursions Planned By Cap and Gown
## 
### 
Al a meeting yesterday, the mem
bers of Cap and Gown, women's
honorary society, decided to hold
their quarterly outing in the Santa ;
Cruz mountains on the week-end
of October 26. Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur, Mrs. Edwin Cottrell, and
other prominent campus women will
attend. Plans were also discussed
for the raising of money for the
scholarship which the society gives
every year.
Officers of Cap and Gown this
quarter are: president, Barbara
Steinbeck; vice-president, Vivian
Kay; secretary-treasurer, Dorothy
Sweet. Kay Arthur is the fourth
active member.
