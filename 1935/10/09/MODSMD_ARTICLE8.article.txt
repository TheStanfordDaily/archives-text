# Cards-Uclans Debate On Radio Saturday
## 
### 
Fraternities will be under fire
when U.C.L.A. and Stanford varsity
debaters argue over radio station
KGO Saturday afternoon from 1 A o
1:30 o'clock on the question, "Re
solved, that fraternities should be
abolished on the Stanford-U.C.L.A.
campi."
This radio debate, which will pre
cede the football game between the
two schools, will be a split-team af
fair, having one man from each
school on either side. Stanford will
be represented by Bob McKenzic
and Ed Butterworth.
