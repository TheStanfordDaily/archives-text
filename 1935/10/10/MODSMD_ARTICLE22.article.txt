# Religious Groups To Meet in Clubhouse
## 
### 
Students' conferences with relig
ious groups, deprived for years of a
permanent meeting place, may now
be held in the reading room of the
Women's Clubhouse from 3 to 5
o'clock in the afternoon, it was an
nounced last night.
Arrangements provide for general
use of the room by all the groups.
Conflict in meeting dates will be
eliminated through a clearing agent.
