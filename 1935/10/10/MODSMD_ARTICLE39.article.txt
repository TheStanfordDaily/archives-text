# Social Service Group to Meet
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Social
Service Group of the Y.W.C.A. will
be held today at 4 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Believing that the necessity for
social service arises from poor so
cial and economic conditions, the
group plans to study social service
from a non-charity angle. Practical
experience in San Francisco, San
Jose, and Palo Aito neighborhood
community houses is offered to those
who join this group.
The Freshman Discussion Group
will also meet tomorrow at 5 o'clock.
