# Club to Assist Scribes Formed
## 
### 
Meeting last night under the lead
ership of Frank J. Taylor, prominent
featui'e writer, a group of Stanford
students interested in journalism
formed the Stanford Manuscript
Club. The purpose of this organiza
tion, the first of its kind on this
campus, is to help local journalists
to market their writings.
Taylor, organizer of the club, has
recently been appointed editor of the
Stanford Illustrated Review.
In addition to his magazine duties
he is well known as a writer of non
fiction articles, his work having ap
peared in such diversified magazines
as Saturday Evening Post, Collier's,
Esquire, and Sunset. Mr. Taylor's
help will be of valuable assistance
to those interested in marketing
their work..
