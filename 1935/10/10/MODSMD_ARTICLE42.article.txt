# VERDICT STUNS WIFE
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 9 (/P).—Mrs.
Anna Hauptmann, wife of Bx-uno
Richard Hauptmann, was stunned
today when she learned that the
New Jersey Court of Errors and Ap
peals had upheld the death sentence
of her husband.
