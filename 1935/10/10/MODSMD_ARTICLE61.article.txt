# PI LAMBDA THETA MEETS
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, the women's
educational honorary society, will
hold its first meeting in the Wom
en's Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock
Thursday evening. A report on the
national convention will be made by
Stanfor delegate Dorothea Wyatt.
At 7 o'clock, directly preceding the
chapter meeting, the executive com
mittee will meet.
