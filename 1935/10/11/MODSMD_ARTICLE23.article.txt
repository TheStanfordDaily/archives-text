# Two Ethiop Youths Sail from London
## 
### 
LONDON, Oct. 10 (>P>. — Their
black eyes snapping with excite
ment, two sons of the Ethiopian
minister to London shopped for gas
masks and other military accoutre
ments today—at last having gained
their father's permission to join the
colors of their native Ethiopia.
The youths —-Joe. 23, and Ben, 22 —
sons of Minister Waronex C. Martin,
sail for Addis Ababa October 15 on
their first trip home in eleven years.
