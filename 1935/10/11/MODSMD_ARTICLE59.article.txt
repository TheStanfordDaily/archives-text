# NOBLE WARNS GREECE
## 
### 
MADRID, Oct. 10 — Prince
Eugene Lascaris, a pretender to the
Greek throne along with former
King George, declared tonight his
followers would "convert Greece
into a river of blood" unless he is
placed on the throne.
