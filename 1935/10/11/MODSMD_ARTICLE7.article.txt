# Dr. Gleason Will Preach on Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Mons. Gleason of Oak
land will preach the sermon in the
Memorial Chapel Sunday morning at
11 o'clock. Dr. Gleason is one of the
most eminent priests in the Catholic
diocese in San Francisco and pastor
of St. Francis De Sales' Church, one
of the largest in Oakland.
Raymond Kendall, acting univer
sity organist, will give a recital in
the Memorial Chapel Sunday after
noon at 4 o'clock. The program will
include "Fantasia in G Major," by
Bach; "Sketch in D Flat," by Schu
mann; and "Toccata and Fugue in D
Minor," by Bach.
