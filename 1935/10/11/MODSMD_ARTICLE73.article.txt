# Man Refuses Food But Eats Revealed
## 
### 
ST. JOSEPH, Mo., Oct. 10 (/P).—
Some concern was felt by officials
when Waldo Gore went on a hunger
strike six days ago at the county
jail. They aren't wondering about
Waldo now. Sheriff's deputies
searched his cell today. They said
: they found several loaves of bread
hidden in his bunk.
