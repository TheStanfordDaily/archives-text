# Hospital Head Will Broadcast
## Edith H. Smith Will Talk On Nursing Education 
### 
The regular Monday radio hour
over KGO at 8 o'clock this evening
will present Miss Edith H. Smith,
director of the Nursing Service in
Stanford University Hospitals, as
well as professor of nursing on the
teaching staff. She will speak on
"The University and Nursing Edu
cation."
Miss Smith is a graduate of the
Stanford School of Nursing and she
has served six years in Paris on the
staff of the League of Red Cross
Societies.
After this experience she taught
for five years at the University of
London in the Bedfore College for
Women. She also served on the staff
of the Butler Psychiatric Hospital in
Providence, Rhode Island, for the
year 1933-34.
