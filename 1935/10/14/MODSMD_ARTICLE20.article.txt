# Stanford Faculty Women Will Meet
## 
### 
The Stanford Faculty Women's
Club will hold its first meeting to
day at 3 o'clock. The members of
the club will be greeted by Mrs. Ray
Lyman Wilbur, Mrs. Percy David
son, the president of the club, and
by the members of the Board.
Following the program there will
a social hour at which time tea
will be served. This will be pre
sided over by Mrs. Branner and
Mrs. C. D. Marx.
