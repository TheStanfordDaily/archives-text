# Health Certificates Due Next Tuesday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates for all new
students are due at the Registrar's
Office on Tuesday, October 22. This
ruling also includes those having
permits to attend classes and all
other students whose certificates
have expired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
o'clock Tuesday will result in a fine
of $2. If certificates are not filed by
October 29 registration in the Uni
versity will be subject to cancella
tion by the Committee on Public
Health.
