# Freshmen Will Be Guests at Jolly-up
## 
### 
Honoring the freshman class, the
Class of '38 will hold a jolly-up in
the Women's Gymnasium Thursday
night from 7 to 8 o'clock, instead of
Wednesday night, as formerly an
nounced in The Daily.
Bill Hovey will be in charge of ar
rangements. Dulce Parker, Paul
Helms, and Audrey Brumfield are
on the dance committee.
