# Deadline Nears For Play-Writing Tilt
## 
### 
Only a few days remain in which
entries may be submitted to Miss
Buckingham for the Masquers' an
nual playwriting contest. Monday is
the last day for manuscripts to be
turned in at her office, Room 211.
As has been previously announced,
all campus and Palo Alto residents
are eligible, and any length play is
acceptable.
