# RESERVATIONS CANCELLED
## 
### 
PARIS, Oct. 14 (/P). — United
States Secretary of the Treasurer
Morgenthau disclosed today he had
cancelled reservations on an Italian
liner for his return to the United
States, in compliance with the proc
lamation of President Roosevelt.
