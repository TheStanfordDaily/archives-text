# Sophs Honor Frosh At Jolly-up Tonight
## 
### 
Sophomores and their freshman
guests will gather in the Women's
Gymnasium from 7 to 8 o'clock to
nighi to dance to the music of Fred
die Nagel's campus band.
Only lower division students will
be admitted to the dance, states Bill
Hovey, chairman of the affair. Aud
rey Brumfield, Dulce Parker, and
Paul Helms are assisting on the
dance committee.
