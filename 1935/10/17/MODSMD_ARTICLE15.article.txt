# 'Y' Groups To Meet Today at Clubhouse
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of the
Y.W.C.A. social group today at 4
o'clock and the freshman discus
sion group at 5 o'clock, both in the
Women's Clubhouse.
The social group will divide into
two sections consisting of those who
wish a training course for volunteer
community service in San Francisco
next quarter, and those who plan to
take up social work as a vocation.
Plans for the remainder of the quar
ter will be made at this meeting.
