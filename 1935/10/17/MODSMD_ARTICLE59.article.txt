One of those honorary professional societies was
discussing the election of associate members one day
this week. For the payment of the small sum of $25
gentlemen in the profession are allowed the privilege
of affiliating with highly select campus intellects. There
was a torrid discussion upon the question of whether
a pertain highly select professional man might he al
lowed to pay the small sum. Suddenly, one select
intellect looked up on the wall at the charter, and
found that the highly select professional man had only
founded the chapter.
