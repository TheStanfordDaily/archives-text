# Suggested revision for library signs: QUIET IS REQUESTED FOR THOSE WHO HAVE ALREADY RETIRED
## 
### 
The remarkable affinity of college students for do
mestic pets causes grey and wild hairs in the heads of
campus cooks, who often have to feed the organisms
and give the nitrogen cycle a spin. Baby crocodiles,
chameleons, raccoons, and love birds are favorite
presents of the home town boy to the faithful one at
college. A particular plague has settled down on the
Tri-Delt house, which shelters two dogs, three cats,
three blind mice, two turtles, and other oddities.
The shy little girl who is house manager at the
Theta house has a very tough servant problem upon
her hands. Several of her hashers are very, very large
football players, who do their hashing on the inde
pendent study plan. Not to be intimidated, the shy,
little girl conducts a kind of a hiring hall, alternately
hiring and firing the football players.
