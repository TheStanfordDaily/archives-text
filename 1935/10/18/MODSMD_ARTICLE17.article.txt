# Weaver Announces Deadline for Skits
## 
### 
The last call for Gaieties skits
was Issued yesterday by Winstead
Weaver, president of Ram's Head
Society. In order to be considered
for the show, sketches must be
turned in to any member of Ram's
Head before tonight.
Tryouts for the Gaieties chorus
start Monday afternoon at 2 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
