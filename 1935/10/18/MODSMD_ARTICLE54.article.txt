# Four Bears Growl for Bronco Meat
## 
### 

A1 Thorell, fullback; Rill Archer, halfback: Chuck Cotton, quarterback; and Floyd Blower, halfback of
the University of California team all look forward to tomorrow's game with the Santa Clara Broncos with
great anticipation. The Broncs have won the last three encounters with the Bears and these four boys mean
to do something about it. (Associated Press Photo.)
