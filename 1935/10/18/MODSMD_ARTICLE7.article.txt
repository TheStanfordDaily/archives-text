# Bridge Ready For Cables
## 
### 

Preparations for spinning the cables of the Golden Gate bridge be
tween Marin County and San Francisco occupied crews today. They
were busy erecting storm cable systems, telephone systems and various
units of the spinning equipment for the .$35,000,000 structure. The cable,
stretching from the tower in the foreground to Marin County on the
north side will be 36 M> inches in diameter. The catwalks with some of
the storm cables are shown. (Associated Press Photo.)
