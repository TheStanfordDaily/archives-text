# Espinosa Will Talk On Radio
## 
### 
The Stanford Radio Hour, heard
on station KGO at 8 o'clock this
evening, will present Professor Au
relio Macedonio Espinosa, executive
head of the department of languages
and associate editor of the Journal
of Folklore, in a talk on "The Origin
of Folk Tales."
In an advance release on his talk,
Professor Espinosa said that the ma
jority of our folk tales originated in
the Orient, and gave as an example
of the transmission of these tales the
well known tar-baby story of Joel
Chandler Harris which has been
traced back 2000 years to India.
