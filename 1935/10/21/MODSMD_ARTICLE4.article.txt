# Forester To Talk At Engineers' Meet
## 
### 
The first meeting of the student
chapter of the A.S.C.E. will be held
at the home of Professor S. B. Mor
ris, 550 Embareadero, tomorrow
evening at 7:45 o'clock. Edward I.
Kotok, head of the California divi
sion of United States Forest Experi
mental Stations, will speak on "The
Engineer and the Forester Tackle
the Water Problem."
All upper division and graduate
students majoring in civil engineer
ing are cordially invited. Refresh
ments will be served.
