# A.S.M.E. President Guest of Local Club
## 
### 
Dr. Ralph E. Flanders, president of
the American Society of Mechanical
Engineers, will be the guest of the
student chapter Thursday. He will
present a talk on "The Engineer and
Social Progress" which will be open
to all who are interested.
The A.S.M.E., having a world-wide
membership of more than 12,000 en
gineers, is the only organization rep
resenting the mechanical engineering
profession. Dr. Flanders is now on a
visit to many of the student chap
ters throughout the country.
