# Civil Engineers To Hear Forestry Talk
## 
### 
Civil engineering majors are
asked to the first meeting of the
student chapter of the A.S.C.E. to be
held this evening at 7:45 o'clock in
the home of Professor S. B. Morris,
550 Embarcadero Road. Professor
E. C. Thomas will be host in the ab
sence of Professor Morris.
Edward I. Kotok, head of the Cali
fornia Division of United States
Forest Experimental Station, will
speak.
