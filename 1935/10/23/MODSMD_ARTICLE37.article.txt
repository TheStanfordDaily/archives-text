# PRIZE CONTEST SLATED
## 
### 
Two prizes for the best memoirs
written by young naturalists have
been announced by the Boston So
ciety of Natural History. The sub
ject for the 193G competition must be
in the field of geology or mineralogy,
and for 1937 in the field of botany.
The prizes, founded by the late Dr.
William Johnson Walker, range from
$50 to $60. A memoir of unusual
merit will be awarded $100.
