# Naval Parley Plans Await Poll on Date
## 
### 
LONDON, Oct. 23 (/P). —Authori-
tative quarters said today issuance
of invitations to a meeting of world
naval powers here December 2 havo
been held up, pending an informal
polling of the powers on acceptabil
ity of that date.
