# Kaffeeklatsch Will Be Staged Monday
## 
### 
A Kaffeeklatsch will be given by
Pi Lambda Theta, women's educa
tional honor society, and Phi Delta
Kappa, men's educational honor so
ciety, on next Monday from 8 to 10
o'clock at the Women's Clubhouse.
The faculty of the School of Edu
cation and Stanford administration
officers will be honor guests.
Speakers will be Dean Grayson N.
Kefauver, Miss Louise Price, presi
dent of Pi Lambda Theta, and Wil
bur Moser, president of. Phi Delta
Kappa.
