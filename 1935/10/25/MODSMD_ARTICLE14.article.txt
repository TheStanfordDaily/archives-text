# Roble Freshman Is Given Paris Jaunt In Raffle Drawing
## 
### 
Florence Brown, Roble fresh
man, was the lucky winner of
San Francisco's Junior League
Normandie Night drawing of a
trip to Europe with all expenses
paid. The trip includes air
travel to New York, a night at
the Waldorf-Astoria before
sailing, and the voyage to
France on the new liner Nor
mandie.
A week in Paris is provided
for, plus the return trip and
$150 for additional expenses,
and to top it off, the whole plan
includes tickets and budget for
two, so that the winner may
take a friend or relative with
her.
