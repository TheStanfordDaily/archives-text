# Kendall Will Give Recital in Chapel
## 
### 
An organ recital will be given in
the Memorial Chapel this afternoon
at 4:15 o'clock by Mr. Raymond
Kendall, acting university organist.
He will play the following num
bers: Largo, J.S. Bach; "O Zion,"
Horace Alden Miller; Andante Can
tabile (opus 2), Tschaikowsky;
Gothic Suite, L. Boellman.
