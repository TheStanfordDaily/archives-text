# Groups Meet Today In Joint Assembly
## 
### 
There will be a joint meeting of
the social service, community and
professional groups of the Y.W.C.A.
in the Women's Clubhouse at 4
o'clock today, at which time Mrs.
G. B. Diller, a settlement worker of
Boston and New York, will speak on
"Group Social Work—lts Philosophy
and Methods."
