# Lost Horse Found Under Straw Pile
## 
### 
PORT HURON, Mich., Oct. 23 (IP).
—William Sherrard, Riley Center
farmer, and sheriff's officers spent
two weeks looking for the farmer's
lost horse.
Sherrard found the animal today
—buried in a strnwstack. "I found
him whon I heard him whinny," he
reported to the sheriff.
