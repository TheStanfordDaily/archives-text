# Brown's Orchestra To Play at Jolly-Up
## 
### 
Frank Brown's 12-piece orchestra
has been engaged for the jolly-up
to be given Thursday night by the
junior class, according to Esther
Pelton, chairman of the committee.
Except for the leader, this is the same
band as Eddie Stirm's, which played
for the 1935 Masque Ball.
Hallowe'en decorations will pro
vide the background for the affair,
in the Women's Gym from 7 to 8
o'clock. Besides Esther Pelton, the
committee is composed of Ruth Neu
pert, Bill Quade, and Jim Reynolds,
president of the class.
