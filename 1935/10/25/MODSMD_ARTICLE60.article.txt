# Peace Restored On Isle of St. Vincent
## 
### 
KINGSTOWN, St. Vincent,
Oct. 24 (/P). —British bluejackets
and police restored peace to rioting
Kingstown and the Isle of St. Vin
cent today.
Martial law, declared after three
Negro strikers had been killed and
eight others wounded, continued.
