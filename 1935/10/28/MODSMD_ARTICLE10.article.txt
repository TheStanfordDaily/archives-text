# S.C. Tickets Still On Sale
## 
### 
Approximately 200 more
rooters' tickets to the Stan
ford-U.S.C. football game in
Los Angeles November 9 will
go on sale at the B.A.C. from
9 to 5 o'clock today, Don Lie
bendorfer, publicity director,
announced last night.
With more than 1100 tickets
already sold, the B.A.C. has
enlarged the rooting section to
almost 1400 seats. When this
new issue of 200 tickets is gone,
the section will he completely
filled.
