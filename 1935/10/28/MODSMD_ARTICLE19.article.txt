# 'Night Club' Dance Planned by Encina
## 
### 
Encina's lobby will bo trans
formed into a night club for the
Encina Informal to be held No
vember 16 according to Dean Pie
rose, Encina Club president. There
will be tables with lamps and deco
rations to conform with this idea.
Pierose has appointed Tanner
Wilson, George Wilson, Leo Haeg
erty, Jack Tuck, and Bob Kinsley as
a committee to make arrangements.
According to present, plans, Fred
Nagel and his orchestra with Dud
ley Nix will furnish the music. Bids
will cost $2.
