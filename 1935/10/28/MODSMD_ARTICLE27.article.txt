# Professors To Talk On Forum Program
## 
### 
Opening a series of nationally
broadcast American Education For
um program, Dean Grayson N.
Kefauver and Professors Paul R.
Hanna and Harold C. Hand of the
School of Education will present
with President Frank Thomas of
Fresno State College a .symposium
on present education needs over
station KPO at 11 o'clock this morn
ing.
Later in the series Dr. C. Gilbert
Wrenn, Professors William M. Proc
tor, John C. Almack, Jesse B. Sears,
and Verna Carley will speak.
