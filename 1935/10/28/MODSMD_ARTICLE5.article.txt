# Townsend Acclaims Plan
## 
### 

Dr. Francis E. Townsend of Long Beach as he addressed the national
convention of advocates of the Townsend Old Age pension plan as the
gatherings opened in Chicago. Delegations came from all parts of the
country to attend the meeting, the purpose of which was to strengthen
the campaign for a $200-a-month payment to the elderly. (Associated
