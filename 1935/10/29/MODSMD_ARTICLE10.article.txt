# Cardinals Honor Santa Clara With Dance Saturday
## 
### 
In honor of the Santa Clara stu
dents, the Cardinals are sponsoring
an A.S.S.U. dance Saturday night in
the Women's Gym from 9 to 1
o'clock.
Freddie Nagel's orchestra will fur
nish the music and refreshments will
be served. Bids at $1 will go on sale
later in the week at the A.S.S.U.
office.
Don Clark, Cardinal member, is
in charge of the dance, and the
committee assisting him includes
Dulcc Parker, Betty Grovcr, Jack
Horton, Jack Kellogg, Jim Ludlam,
Bob McCroskey, Chet Ploeser, and
Dick Taylor.
