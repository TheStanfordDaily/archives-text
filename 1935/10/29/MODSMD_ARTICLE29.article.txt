# Few S.C. Tickets Remain at B.A.C.
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stanford-
U.S.C. game in Los Angeles on No
vember 9 will be on sale again to
day ,announced Don Liebendorfer,
director of the B.A.C. News Service,
last night. The ticket office will be
open from 9 to 5 o'clock.
About 100 tickets were sold yes
terday, bringing the size of the
rooting section up to approximately
1200. There are only 100 tickets re
maining, and when this supply is
exhausted there will probably bo
no more available, Liebendorfer
| says.
