# Dr. Martin Receives Honor from Brazil
## 
### 
Dr. Percy A. Martin, professor of
European history at Stanford, has
received the Southern Cross (Cru
ziero do Sul) in recognition of his
investigations in the field of Bra
zilian history and institutions, it
was announced yesterday.
Thi.s decoration, which is confined
entirely to non-Brazilians, was con
ferred on Dr. Martin by a decree of
President Getulio Vargas of Brazil
on September 7, the anniversary of
Brazilian independence.
Dr. Martin visited Brazil only
twice during his research studies on
that country. Most of his work was
done at Stanford in the Branner
Brazilian Library, containing one of
the finest collections of books on
South America in this country.
