# Japanese Emperor Receives Garner
## 
### 
TOKYO, Oct. 29 (/Pt.—America's
vice-president, John N. Garner,
wearing a cut-away with striped
trousers and high-laced black shoes
which showed no signs of a recent
shine, was received today by Em
peror Hirohito.
