# DEAN YOST AT HOME
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will be at home
informally to all women students
this afternoon from 4 to 6 o'clock at
her house at 532 Lasuen Street.
Marquerite Williams, Mary Aim
Wheeler, Carolyn Hibbard, and
Mary Savage will serve today.
New York University has received
more than 10.000 books during the
past few months through the activi
ties of the Society for Libraries.
