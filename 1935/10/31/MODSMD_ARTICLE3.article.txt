# Drof Claims 'Bad Blood' In Coppmen
## 
### 
Charges that there is "bad blood"
among members of the Chappie
whipperstaffer football team were
made here today by Sylvestre Misceg
Drofnats, seller of little pink pills
and coach of the big Pink Machine
of The Daily.
"Their attempt to pawn off this
member of their staff Rogo Drool
onto us," he said, "was originated
when Rogo first took a pop at Copp.
This caused bad blood among the
Chappie staff.
"But we don't care, we accept this
handicap. It will take more than
Rogo to hold the big Pink Machine
down. In fact, we're taking the Syl
vestre Drofnats ill pills, and so we
are sure they can't keep us down.
"The Daily staff is against bad
blood. We don't like it. We will
have none of it. We are so against
it that we will practically eliminate
all of it in the Chaparral staff. If
necessary, we will also eliminate the
Chaparral staff.
"For further information sec my
circular on the subject," said Drof
nats.
