# Kendall To Present Organ Recital Today
## 
### 
Raymond Kendall will present a
mid-weekly organ recital in the
Memorial Chapel this afternoon
at 4:15 o'clock.
The program will include the fol
lowing numbers: Madrigale, Simon
etti; Prize Song (from "Die Meis
tersinger')), Wagner; Andante (from
Violin Concerto), Mendelssohn;
Fugyie in G Minor, J. S. Bach; "In
dir ist Freude," J. S. Bach.
