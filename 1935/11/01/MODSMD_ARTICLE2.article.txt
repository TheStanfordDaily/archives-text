# Dr. Wilbur's Sister To Visit Y.W.C.A.
## 
### 
Mrs. Theresa Wilbur Pai.st, sister
of Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, will ad
dress the Y.W.C.A. at a tea given
in her honor on November 11. She
will discuss the national work of the
Y.W.C.A. and the relation of stu
dents to the national program. Mrs.
Pai.st has been president of the Na
tional Y.W.C.A. board since 1932.
Mrs. Paist, a Stanford alumna
of the Class of '00, arrived in Cali
fornia yesterday from Pennsylvania.
The University of California at Ber
keley and Stanford are the only
student associations which she will
