# 'Y' To Tour Chinese Cafe, Show Today
## 
### 
A Chinese theater, the famous Chi
nese telephone exchange and a Chi
nese cafe are among the places t.o bo
visited in the international acquain
tance tour of the Y.W.C.A. today.
The tour will start from the Wom
en's Clubhouse at 1 o'clock.
Miss Donaldina Cameron's Mission
home and the Chinese Y.W.C.A. will
be visited in the afternoon. Partici
pants will have the opportunity to
hear Chinese lore from the guides,
who are all Stanford students. They
include Won Loy Chan, Yung Wong,
and Edmund Jung. The tour is open
Id both men and women students.
