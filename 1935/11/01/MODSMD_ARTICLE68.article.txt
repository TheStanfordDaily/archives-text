# CHAIN GANG ATTACKED
## 
### 
ATLANTA, Oct. 31 </P>. — The
American Prison Association today
condemned the chain gang in Geor
gia and other states as "utterly in
consistent with the dictates of hu
manity'' during a spirited meeting at
which the organization defeated a
resolution asking President Roose
-1 velt to abolish the system.
