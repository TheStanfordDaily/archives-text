# Our Hasher Sez
## 
### 
There was a small fire in the Tri-Delt house at 3:30
yesterday morning. In great haste one of the women
called up the fire house and succeeded in rousing a
plenty sleepy fireman from his downy bed. The hazy
laddie had a hard time getting his information straight.
He: "Did you say 'Tri-Delt' or 'Phi Dolt??
She: "What would I be doing in the Phi Delt house
at 3:30 in the morning?
He: "Well, I d-o-n'-t k-n-o-w."
The fire was all put out about it.
We learn from our H-men that there were only
four (4) "Our Hasher Sez" skits turned in to th?
Gaieties committee, none of them authorized by Our
Hasher. You guess how funny they Were.
