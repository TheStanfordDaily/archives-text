# Ralston To Lecture On Race Problems
## 
### 
Judge Jackson H. Ralston, noted
authority on international law, will
speak on the legal aspects of racial
problems at 4:30 this afternoon in the
Y.W.C.A. clubrooms. The meeting
will be open to all those interested.
Connected with the tribunal which
formed the World Court at The
Hague, Judge Ralston is an out
standing figure in the field of inter
national relations. His lecture is
sponsored by the 'Y' Racial Prob
lems Committee.
