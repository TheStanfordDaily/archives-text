# Polo Team Invited To Pasadena Meet
## 
### 
Stanford varsity's chances for an
expense-paid trip to one of the West
Coast's largest polo meets depend
only on approval by the Faculty
Athletic Committee.
Captain Haydon Grubbs, Card polo
coach, announces that an invitation
has been given to the Indian mal
leteers to compete in Ihe Midwick
Junior Polo Tournament in Pasa
dena during the Christmas holidays.
