# Russian Institute To Meet Thursday
## 
### 
Three Stanford professors will
speak Thursday evening at the Sir
Francis Drake Hotel when the Amer
ican Russian Institute will give an
informal dinner and program dedi
cated to the furtherance of cultural
and economic relations between the
two countries.
These men are Professor Thomas
Addis of the School of Medicine,
Professor H. H. Fisher, of the De
partment of History; and Mr. Hol
land D. Roberts of the School of
Educat ion.
Tickets for the dinner, which will
start at 7 p.m., may be obtained
through Rose Isaak, executive secre
tary of the Institute, before Tuesday
noon.
