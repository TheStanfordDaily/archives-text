# 'Y' Visits Chinatown During Friday Tour
## 
### 
Chinese business houses and rec
reational centers were visited Fri
day by guests of the International
Tours Committee of the Stanford
Y.W.C.A., who toured San Fran
cisco's Chinatown.
The group inspected the only
Chinese telephone exchange in the
United States, watched a Chinese
theatrical performance, and visited
the Chinese Nationalist Daily offices
and the Tin How Temple. The tem
ple is dedicated to Buddhism, Tao
ism, and Confucianism.
Edinond Jung, a Stanford student,
was a guide. Buck Yee, special stu
dent at the University and one of
the editors of the Nationalist Daily,
explained the mechanics of publish
ing the paper, which is set by hand.
