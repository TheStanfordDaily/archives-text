# Trojan Prexy To Be at Rally
## 
### 
The principal guest at the S.C.
rally Thursday night, will be Eames
Bishop, president of the Associated
Students of Southern California.
Other speakers will include Wally
Brooke, Coach Tiny Thornhill, and
perhaps Bill Corbus, all-American
guard in 1932 and 1933.
Bishop has been invited by Brooke
to come north on Wednesday. If
Bishop cannot get away then, he
expects to fly up Thursday after
noon in time for the rally that eve
ning.
Both Brooke and Bishop will leave
for the South with the team after
the rally. Brooke will speak at the
U.S.C. rally on Friday.
