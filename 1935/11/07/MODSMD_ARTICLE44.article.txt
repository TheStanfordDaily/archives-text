# KENDALL GIVES RECITAL
## 
### 
Of special interest in the organ
recital by Mr. Raymond Kendall this
afternoon at 4:15 in the Memorial
Chapel will be Schubert's "Ave Ma
ria" and Beethoven's "Moonlight So
nata," both known and loved
throughout the world. The program
will be completed by three selections
from Cesar Franck's music for re
ligious services.
