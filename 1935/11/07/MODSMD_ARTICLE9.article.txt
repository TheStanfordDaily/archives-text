# Women's Conference Receives Delegates
## 
### 
Members of the Women's Execu
tive Committee of the University of
California will arrive on the campus
today at 5 o'clock to visit as guests
of Women's Conference. About 20
members will be present.
The two groups will hold a joint
meeting in the W'omen's Club
house, and the visitors will be en
tertained at dinner by members of
Conference. Following dinner, they
will attend the rally for the U.S.C.
game.
