# FLORIDA AWAITS STORM
## 
### 
JACKSONVILLE. Fla.. Nov. 7 (/I').
—Forewarned, ihe Florida Gulf
Coast today awaited a tropical storm
which churned across the Gulf of
Mexico after striking the southern
part of the state earlier in the week
with death-dealing fury.
