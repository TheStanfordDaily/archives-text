# Clayton Arnold Is Victim of Curiosity
## 
### 
PINE, Mo., Nov. 7 UP).—Clayton
Arnold was in the Polar Bluff jail
tonight, a victim of feminine curios
ity. The women of this small Ozarks
community had been wondering for
some time where the menfolk got
their liquor. Finally they wrote
"Mrs. F. D. Roosevelt, White House,
i Washington."
A revenue agent got Arnold. He
is awaiting 1 rial on a charge of pos
sessing untaxed liquor.
