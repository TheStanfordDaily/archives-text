# Sias To Represent Farm at Ceremony
## 
### 
In place of President Ray Lyman
Wilbur, Professor Azariah Boody
Sias, Ph.D. '26, will represent Stan
ford University at the inauguration
of Dr. Herman G. James as the
twelfth president of Ohio University
on November 15.
Approximately 500 invitations
have been extended to presidents
of colleges throughout the nation,
the constitutional officers of the
, State of Ohio, and presidents of
J learned educational societies to at
tend the elaborate program at which
Harry Woodburn Chase, chancellor
of New York University, will be the
main speaker.
