# German Folksongs Dr. Morgan's Topic
## 
### 
Dr. Bayard Quincy Morgan, pro
fessor of German at Stanford, will
give the fourth and last of his series
of German Lieder programs at Cas
tilleja Chapel Sunday evening at
7:30 o'clock. His subject will be
"German Folksongs." Dr. Morgan
will be assisted by Mrs. Elizabeth
Hammond Smith, violinist; and Dr.
Latham True, organist.
The program is open to the public.
