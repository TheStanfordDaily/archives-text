# Woman Graduates Meet at Luncheon
## 
### 
Graduate women will meet for an
informal buffet luncheon today at
12:15 o'clock on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Ann Archer is in charge of ar
rangements. Invitations have been
issued, but those who did not receive
any are also welcome, announced
Marion Wooldridge, head of the in
vitation committee.
