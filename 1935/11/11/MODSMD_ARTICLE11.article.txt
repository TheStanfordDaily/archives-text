# Chaplain to be Honored at Tea
## 
### 
Dr. David Charles Gardner, chap
lain of the University, and Mrs.
Gardner will be guests of honor at
the annual Candlelight Tea to be
held by the Union Club this Sunday.
Approximately 550 invitations
have been sent to the faculty and
friends, and the Union women are
making preparations for the affair,
which is the largest in their season.
Patricia Flavel is chairman of the
tea committee, other committees be
ing headed by Arleen Heltwood,
Doris Clayburg, Dorothy Merrill,
Virginia Slater, Enid Olivi, and Helen
Volberding.
