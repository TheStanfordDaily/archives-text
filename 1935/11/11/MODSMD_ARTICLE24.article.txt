# Research Club To Meet Wednesday
## 
### 
Papers by Dr. Helen Pryor, as
sistant medical adviser of women,
and Nathan Van Patten, director of
the Stanford Library, will be read at
a meeting of the Stanford Research
Club in the Men's Faculty Clubhouse
at 8 o'clock Wednesday evening.
Dr. Pryor will discuss "Variations
of Normal Individuals in Form and
Function" and Mr. Van Patten will
speak on "The Seventh American
Scientific Congress Recently Held in
Mexico City."
