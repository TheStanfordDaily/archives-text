# Colm Wins County Golf Championship
## 
### 
Bill Colm, a Stanford senior, won
the San Mateo County amateur golf
championship over the week-end,
defeating Bud Finger, peninsula star,
2 and 1, on the 35th hole. The match
was played on the San Mateo Mu
nicipal Golf Course.
Colm was two down earlier in the
match, but staged a comeback to win
the title anel a handsome silver cup.
