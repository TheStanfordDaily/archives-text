# Bantam Boxers To Top Garden Fights
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 10 (/P).The ban
tam weight championship tussle fea
turing Lou Salica of New York and
Sixto Escobar of Puerto Rico, at Mad
ison Square Garden Friday night,
tops this week's national boxing
program.
Salica, a member of the 1932
Olympic boxing team, and Escobar
are scheduled to go over the 15-
round route.
