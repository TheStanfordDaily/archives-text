# Olympic Club Foil Artists Take Cards
## 
### 
An admirable showing that satis
fied Coach Elwyn Bugge in every
way was the performance of the
| Stanford varsity fencers when they
\ bowed to the Olympic Club foilsmen
15 to 10 Friday nigh) in Encina Gym.
I Louis Lataillade made a splendid
showing in winning four of his five
matches; Captain Dick Ferguson
\ was next with three, Jack Lesley
won two, and John Reichmuth one.
I Leicester of the Club proved he is
I the best swordsman in this region by
I winning all five of his matches.
Rodecade was second for the club
with four. The Stanford swordsmen
will draw a bye this week.
