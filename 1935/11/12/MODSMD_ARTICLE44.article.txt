# Masquers' Tryouts To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Masquers' tryouts will be held to
day from 4 to 6 o'clock in the Little
Theater to select a cast for the read
ing which will be Monday at 8
o'clock in the Little Theater.
The Masquers will read the three
original one-act plays that won their
recent contest. Although the titles
of the winning plays have not yet
been announced, they were selected
by Dr. Margery Bailey, Professor
Elizabeth Buckingham, and the
Masquers.
