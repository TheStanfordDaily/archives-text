# Grads Plan Fete Big Game Night
## 
### 
Announcement. that Stanford
alumni will hold their sixteenth an
nual Big Game dinner dance at the
Palace Hotel in San Francisco on
Saturday, November 23, was made
yesterday by Harlow Rothert,
alumni secretary.
Rothert emphasizes the fact that
the formal affair is open to students
as well as alumni and that past
dances have been heavily attended
by students. There will be a charge
of $5.25 a plate.
Members of the Indian varsity and
the coaching staff will be guests of
honor. A number of athletic officials
are expected to attend.
