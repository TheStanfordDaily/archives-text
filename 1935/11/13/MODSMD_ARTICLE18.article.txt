# Toyon-Branner To Entertain at Dance
## 
### 
An informal dance from 9 to 12
Saturday for members of Toyon and
Branner halls was announced yes
terday by Jim Ransohoff, chairman
of the dance committee. Carmen
Dragon's 12-piece orchestra will
furnish the music. Admission is free
to those presenting membership
cards of either hall.
Also on the committee are Ned
Rosenblatt, Mark Hopkins, John
Rice, Walt White, and Bill Lowe.
