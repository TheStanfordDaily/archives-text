# P.G.&E. Head Dies From Heart Disease
## 
### 
August Frederick Hockenbeamer,
president of the Pacific Gas & Elec
tric Company, who was a consulting
professor of public utility manage
ment in the Graduate School of
Business, died Monday from heart
disease.
Mr. Hockenbeamer began his
business career as an office boy and
became president of P.G.&E. in 1927.
He was the innovator of the plan for
selling public utility securities to
companies' customers.
