# Frosh Debate Duo Meets Pacific Team
## 
### 
Meeting two freshman debaters
from the College of the Pacific, Phil
Halla and Harold Keith of the Cardi
nal frosh squad argued the negative
of the Pi Kappa Delta question.
Coached by Won Loy Chan, and
managed by Bob Hartmann, the
Cardinal yearlings put up good argu
ments against the visitors from
Stockton. Debates coming next week
for the frosh squad are with the
Girls' High School of San Fran
cisco in the City, and with St. Mary's
frosh here on the campus, both to
take place next Monday.
