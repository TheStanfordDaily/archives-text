# 27 SAVED FROM RAFTS
## 
### 
MANILA, P.1.. Nov. 13
less messages from the U.S. Destroy
• rs Bulmer and Pearry early today
announced 27 marooned passengers
nd crew members from the wrecked
British freighter Silverhazel had
been rescued from rafts in San Ber
nardino straits.
