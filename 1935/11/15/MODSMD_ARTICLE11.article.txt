# Toy on, Branner To Hold Game Jolly-up
## 
### 
Toyon and Branner will hold a
combined jolly-up and open house
immediately after the Big Came. A
12-piecc orchestra will play for
dancing in Toyon lobby and Branner
dining hall, and refreshments will be
served.
Invitation to attend arc being sent
to the women's living groups on the
campus and at California. Perry
Mocrdyke is chairman in charge of
the dance. Bids for refreshments are
now being considered.
