## 
###

l.he subject of a talk by Comptroller
Almon E. Roth before the Stanford
Mothers' Club when it meets in the
Women's Clubhouse at 2:30 o'clock
Tuesday. Also included on the pro
gram will be a .skit by residents of
Madrono.
The meeting will be preceded by
lunch at 12:30 o'clock at the Union.
Later in the afternoon tea will bo
served by members of the Pi Bela
Phi Mothers' Club, resident alum
nae, and active Pi Phi members.
