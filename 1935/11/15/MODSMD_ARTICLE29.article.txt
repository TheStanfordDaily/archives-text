# Glee Club Members Win Music Awards
## 
### 
John Ellis, tenor, and John Turner,
baritone, won music scholarships
given by an anonymous donor after
a recent singing competition. Each
winner will receive 52 free lessons
from Arturo Casiglia, an outstanding
music teacher of San Francisco.
Ellis and Turner will each take one
lesson a week for a year, starting
within a few days. The scholarships
are valued at more than $500 apiece.
Both winners are members of the
Stanford Glee Club, and were chosen
by Casiglia in preference to ap
proximately 12 other members.
