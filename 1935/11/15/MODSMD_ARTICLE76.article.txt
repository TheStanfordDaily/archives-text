# Sigma Delta Pi Will Hold Initiation Today
## 
### 
The Stanford and San Jose chap
ters of Sigma Delta Pi, national hon
orary Spanish rociety, will hold a
joint initiation today at 5 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse. Initiates of
the local chapter are Arline Glaze,
Dorothy Applen, Columbus Baldo,
Dorothy Jester, and George E. Mc-
Spadden.
The initiation will be followed by
a dinner.
