# Hurricane Mortality Larger Than War
## 
### 
JACKSONVILLE. Flu., Nov. 17
</P<.—Tropical hurricanes have taken
more lives this year "than the re
ported total of the Italo-Ethiopian
war.
An Associated Press survey
showed today that storms had
caused more than lt>oo deaths along
a far-Hung area from Honduras to
Nt wfoundland. Property damage ran
into millions.
