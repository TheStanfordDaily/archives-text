# Juvenile Judge Will Address 'Y' Group
## 
### 
Mrs. John Kohler, judge of the
Juvenile Court in San Francisco,
will be guest speaker at the Social
Service Group meeting of the Y.W.
C.A. today at 4 o'clock in the "Y"
rooms. She will speak on the gen
eral subject of juvenile delinquency
and its treatment by the court.
Mrs. Kohler will speak in the in
terest of those who expect to use
social service work as their profes
sion. Requirements of social case
work with maladjusted children will
be discussed.
The social group of the "Y," with
Marion Roller as chairman, will
meet with the special service group
today.
