# Learn These Songs
## 
### 
"Learn the Hymn and Hail Alma Mater'," say leaders of the Rally
Committee. Both these songs must be sung at the Big Game, and it is
imperative that every rooter know all the words.

THE HYMN
Where the rolling foothills rise
I Up toward mountains higher,
: Where at eve the Coast Range lies
In the sunset fire.
Flushing deep and paling,
Here we raise our voices, hailing
Thee, our Alma Mater.
Refrain
From the foothills to the bay
It shall ring,
As we sing;
It shall ring and float away.
, Hail, Stanford, Hail!
1 Hail, Stanford, Hail!

HAIL ALMA MATER
Hail! Hail! Hail! Alma Mater, Hail!
Stanford, Hail!
Thy name shall e'er prevail,
Thy sons thy glory shall acclaim,
And ever honor th/ fair name,
So, Hail! Hail! Haiil
Alma Mater, Hail!
II
Hail! Hail! Hail! Alma Mater, Hail!
Stanford, Hail!
Thy fame shall e'er prevail,
With hearts that ever true shall be.
We pledge our love anew to thee,
So. Hail! Hail! Hail!
Alma Mater, Hail!
"Learn the Hymn and Hail Alma Mater'," say leaders of the Rally
Committee. Both these songs must be sung at the Big Game, and it is
imperative that every rooter know all the words.
