# Mothers' Club To Hear Roth
## 
### 
This afternoon at 2:30 o'clock the
Stanford Mothers' Club will hear
Comptroller Almon E. Roth discuss
"The Future of Stanford" in the
Women's Clubhouse.
A group of Madrono women will
sing a popularized version of "Old
Mac Donald Had a Farm" as the en
tertainment feature and the busi
ness session will be devoted to re
ports of the recent Chrysanthemum
Tea which netted approximately
$1000.
The mothers will be greeted by
board members under the chair
manship of Mrs. George Dowrie at a
no-host luncheon at 12:30 o'clock at
the Union.
