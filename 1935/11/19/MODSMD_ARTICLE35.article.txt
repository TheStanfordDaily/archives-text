# History Club Plans Program Meeting
## 
### 
Fourteen new members of the
History Club will attend their first
program meeting of the quarter to
night at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. An informal general
discussion of the current interna
tional crisis will be opened by Pro
fessors Carl Fremont Brand, Harold
Henry Fisher, and David Harris.
The following are the new affiliated
members: Mary Elizabeth Burke,
Eunice Janet Davis, Jacqueline
Flanders, Judith Goodheart, Betty
Judell, George Harmon Knoles, J.
Alice McKnight, Alice M. Rose, Vir
ginia Louise Ross, Charles W.
Thomas, Janet E. Turner, Helen Ann
Warson, Gordon J. Wright, and Mar
jorie E. Wright.
