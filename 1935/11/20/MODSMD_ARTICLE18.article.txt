# Stanford Press Fills Red Cross Roll Call
## 
### 
Announcement was made yester
day that the Stanford Press was the
first unit on the campus, outside ihe
student body, to report completion of
its roll call in the Bed Cross. Mrs.
Gertrude Owler, who was in charge
announced the Press subscribed 100
per cent.
Mrs. William Alpha Cooper, in
charge of the campus Red Cross
drive, said last night repeated calls
for additional buttons, window flags,
and membership cards, both from
heads of organized groups and from
workers indicate this year's drive
will show better results than ever
before.
