# ENCINA ROOM DEPOSITS
## 
### 
Men wishing to reserve their
rooms in Encina Hall for the winter
quarter must make a $5 deposit at
the Dean of Men's oflice today or
tomorrow. Rooms will not be held
for those that do not make this de
posit. Those living in Sequoia and
the Old Union will pay their $5 on
Monday and Tuesday of next week.
