# Bowman Nominated As Winning Cowboy
## 
### 
SALINAS, Nov. 21 </P). — The
world's champion all-around cow
boy of 1935—Everett Bowman of
Arizona — was nominated by the
Rodeo Association of America here
today.
Bowman, who lives at Hillside,
Ariz., rolled up a grand total of
7,412Vs points riding broncos, bull
dogging steers, and whirling ropes
during the year, Fred McCarger,
secretary of the association, an
nounced.
