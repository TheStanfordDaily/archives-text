# Athletics Finish Sale Of Cramer, McNair
## 
### 
DAYTON. 0., Nov. 24 (£>). — A
whirlpool of rumors made everyone
dizzy at the minor league convention
today but it was learned from re
liable sources that:
(1) The Philadelphia Athletics had
sold Shortstop Eric McNair and Out
fielder Roger Cramer to the Boston
Red Sox and Third Baseman Pinky
Kiggins to the Now York Yankees.
(2) —Bob Quinn had agreed defi
nitely to serve as general manager
of the Boston Braves, who will be
reorganized within the next few
days on a basis that will surprise
everyone.
