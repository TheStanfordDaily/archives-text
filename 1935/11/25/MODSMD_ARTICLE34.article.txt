# Indians, Led By Nimmo, Win From Star Cal Harriers
## 
### 
Chuck Nimmo proved he hasn't
lost any distance running prowess
when he lead the Stanford varsity
harriers to a 32-23 victory over Cali
fornia in the annual 3',2-mile cross
country classic at the golf course
Friday.
Nimmo pulled down the home
stretch in 20:39.4, excellent time for
the 18-hole route, and led Lester
Voorhies of California home by 30
yards. Bob Alexander and Ed Bur
rows were third and fourth, respec
tively, about 150 yards back of the
winner.
