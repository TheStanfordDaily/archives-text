# Horsemen Click To Trim S.M.
## 
### 
Stanford's polo team clicked Fri
day afternoon to whitewash a some
what disrupted San Mateo team 12
to 1 on the highway field. Ed Mc-
Keon, Indian alternate, filled in at
back for the visitors, short one man.
Leon Derby at number one played
an outstanding game in scoring
three goals for Stanford, while Carl
Beal at the number two position was
the afternoon's high scorer with five
counters.
Pat Linfoot at three for the vis
itors couldn't seem to capitalize on
his good playing except for one
sensational goal in the last period.
"Rammy" Rasmussen at three and
Captain Bob Fullerton both played
well, scoring two points apiece.
