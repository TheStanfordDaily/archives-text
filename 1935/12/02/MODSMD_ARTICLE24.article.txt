# Former Stanford Students Married
## 
### 
Two former Stanford students
were married Saturday at a home
ceremony in San Francisco. The
bride, Mrs. John C. Cosgrove, the
former Miss Emilie Dohrman, was
graduated in '33. She was a member
of Kappa Kappa Gamma. The bride
groom, also of the class of '33, was
a member of Beta Theta Pi.
The couple, who will pass their
honeymoon on a brief motor trip,
will make their home in San Fran
cisco on their return.
