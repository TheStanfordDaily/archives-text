# Exam Schedule Begins Dec. 10
## 
### 
End-quarter examinations begin
next Tuesday for nearly four
thousand Stanford students.
Ail five-unit 8 o'clock finals will
be held from 8 to 12 o'clock Tues
day; 9 o'clocks on Wednesday, 10
o'clocks on Thursday, and 11 o'clocks
on Friday. One to three-unit courses
held on Tuesday, Thursday, and
Saturday morning will be held from
10 to 12 o'clock and those on other
days from 8 to 12 o'clock.
Five-unit 1 o'clock examinations
are scheduled for Tuesday from 2
to G o'clock and 2 o'clock on Thurs
day from 2 to 6 o'clock. Afternoon
c busses of from one to three units
which are held on Tuesday, Thurs
day, and Saturday are scheduled
fio.n 4 to 6 o'clock and all others
from 2 to 4 o'clock. Wednesday
and Friday afternoons are reserved
for special cases of conflict in ex
amination time or postponements.
