# Confabs Proposed For Industry's Ills
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Dec. 1 (/P).-A
proposal for round table discussion
by 57 groups at his conference on
industry's problems December 9 was
advanced today by George L. Berry,
industrial recovery co-ordinator.
Under the plan, representatives of
industry would be divided into 44
groups and labor into 13 for discus
sions before spokesmen are sent to a
main conference to talk over pro
posals for promoting recovery.
