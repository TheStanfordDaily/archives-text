# "BIG RED TOBOGGAN"
## 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
I am just another person who
would like to see the words to "Get
on That Bif> Red Toboggan" printed
in The Daily,
Not Even a Songster
To you and other requesters of
the Toboggan song, The Daily
complies, reprints the verses on
another page.—Ed.
