# German Group Holds Christmas Dinner
## 
### 
Celebrating Christmas, the Ger
man department will hold its an
nual holiday dinner in the main
dining room of the Union Saturday
at 6:30 o'clock. There will be en
tertainment, including songs and
carols sung by the German House
women.
Tickets, at $1 each, will be held
at the door, but all reservations and
remittances must be given to the
secretary of the German depart
ment by Wednesday. All members
of the University community and
their friends may attend.
