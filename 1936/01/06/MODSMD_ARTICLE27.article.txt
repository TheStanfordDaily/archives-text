# Men's Rush Cards On Sale Today
## 
### 
Rush cards for the next rushing
period may be obtained today after
1 o'clock at the A.S.S.U. office in the
Union, according to Johnny Edmun
son, secretary of the Interfraternity
Council.
Only dinner dates are to be avail
able this period. The last rushing
period will be held January 14, 15,
and 16, and formal pledging will
take place the 17th, followed by the
Pledge Prom in the evening. Fresh
men are requested to return rush
ing cards promptly to aid the fra
ternities in making preparations.
