# Rugby Squad Holds First Meeting Today
## 
### 
Rugby at Stanford for the cur- j
rent season gets under way this
afternoon at 4 o'clock with a squad
meeting at which all men interested
in coming out for the team are re
quested to attend.
With several of the regulars from
last year returning and a large num
ber of promising newcomers turn
ing out, enthusasm is running high
for the game in which interest has
been greatly rejuvenated by the
forming this year of a Pacific Coast
Rugby Conference comprising
teams from UCLA, Southern Cali
fornia, California, and Stanford.
