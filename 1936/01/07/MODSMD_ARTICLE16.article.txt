TUESDAY, JANUARY 7
Illustrated Lecture: 7:30 o'clock in
the Assembly Hall. President Wil
liam Pettus of (he California Col
lege in China will talk on "Chi
nese Gardens and Landscape
Paintings." The public is invited
to attend.
Debaters: There will be a meeting
of all debators, new and old, in
Room 202 at 4 o'clock.
Record Program: The first record
program of this quarter will be
presented at 4:15 o'clock in Room
84.
Quad Business StalT: Those mem
bers who are doing advertising in
Palo Alto and the Peninsula arc
requested to meet at 7:15 o'clock
in Woodpecker Lodge.
N.Y.A.: Applications must be in
before 5 o'clock tomorrow.
