# Boeing Aviation Awards Offered
## 
### 
The seventh W. E. Boeing schol
arships are being given to deserving
undergraduate college men who
have determined on aviation as a
vocation. Stanford students won
the first two scholarships.
There are several awards, the
first being valued at $5,800. It is
good for two years and is a course
enabling the student to become a
master pilot. It includes 250 hours of
flight instruction and 37(55 hours of
lecture, laboratory, and shop work.
Tho eligibility requirements
stress physical fitness and include
a written treatise on some aero
nautical subject. Students interested
in applying for the scholarships
may see Dr. Gilbert Wrenn in the
Administration Building.
