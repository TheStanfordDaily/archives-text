# Japan Faithful To Treaty, Says Treat
## 
### 
Despite American press reports,
Japan has not violated treaty pro
visions by her "occupation" of
North China, according to Dr. Pay
son J. Treat, Stanford authority on
far-eastern relations.
Dr. Treat, who is back on the
campus after a four months' tour of
the Orient, emphasized that the
number of Japanese soldiers in Chi
nese territory is strictly within lim
its determined by the Boxer Treaty,
and that they are there on the same
ba.sis as American marines.
