# Two Juniors Will Divide Essay Prize
## 
### 
Two juniors, one from Richmond,
Va., and the other from Seattle, will
divide the prize in the 1935-36 essay
contest in American Colonial His
tory established at Stanford by the
National Society of Colonial Dames
Resident in California.
Carolyn Hartwell Moore's "Wil
liamsburg: It's Building and Early
Arislocracy," and James B. Weter's
"Roger Williams: A Study in the
History of Thought," were chosen
lor first place.
