# Gallery Exhibits Pastel Paintings
## 
### 
On exhibition until January 12 in
the Stanford Art Gallery are 27
pastel paintings by Corrine Mal
vern, color illustrator of Los An
geles. The artist is noted for her
portraiture types and for strong
handling equal to oil painting tech
nique.
Miss Malvern studied with
Bridgeman, the noted anatomist
and teacher in the Art Students'
League in New York, and with other
leading eastern artists. Also on dis
play until January 26 is a group of
landscape paintings by Jane Rehn
strand. The Art Gallery is open to
the public every day between 10
and 5 o'clock.
