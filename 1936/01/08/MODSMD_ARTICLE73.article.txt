# Woman Dies After Receiving Legacy
## 
### 
AUGUSTA, Maine, Jan. 7 (/P).—
Left a trust fund of $25,000 by an
aunt in Boston, Miss Maude Rock
wood Whitman, 66, of Augusta,
lived only one day after inheriting
this large sum of money.
"Red" Grange once played eight
football games in 12 days.
