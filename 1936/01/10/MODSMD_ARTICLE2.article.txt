# Alumni Plan Farm Meeting
## 
### 
Alumni Conference Committee
members will meet next Sunday
evening to plan the first of a series
of alumni conferences which is to
be held on the Stanford campus
February 15 and 16.
Fifteen subjects are to be selected
for discussion, among which will be
problems of the Far East, taxation in
California, the European situation,
and the significance of the Constitu
tion in current national affairs.
Members of the conference com
mittee arc: Ben Duniway, chair
man, Bill Morse, Victoria Schuek,
Dorothea Wyatt, Winston Norman,
Norman Livermore, Hugh Fullerton,
Bernice Miller, Marvin Kahn, Fran
ces Terrill, and Harlow Rothert.
