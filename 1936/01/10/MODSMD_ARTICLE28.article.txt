# South Pacific May Have Airline
## 
### 
HONOLULU, Jan. 9 (/P). —Com-
mercial airlines between Honolulu
and New Zealand within a year arc
expected to follow the sailing to
night of the Coast Guard Cutter
Itasca for Pago-Pago, American
Samoa, to make preliminary sur
veys for the new route.
Aviation and weather experts
carried by the Itasca will make the
first upper air observation ever tak
en in the South Pacific Ocean.
