# Speakers for Radio Program Announced
## 
### 
Stanford Radio Hour programs
for the remainder of the month will
include a talk on "Student Life in
Germany," by Waldo H. Furgason,
on January 20; and "The Co-ordina
tion and Integration of Federal,
State and Local Tax Systems," by
Dr. James Kendall Hall, on Jan
uary 27.
Dean J. Hugh Jackson, of the
Graduate School of Business, will
discuss "Business in Retrospect and
Prospect," tonight at 8 o'clock over
station KGO. Among the questions
which he will answer are: How far
along the road to recovery did we
come in 1935? and What are the
prospects for business in the first
two quarters of 1936.
