# Stanford, Dartmouth To Meet on Gridiron
## 
### 
HANOVER, N.H.. Jan. 11 (A*).—
Stanfoid University and Dartmouth
College will meet on the football
field in 1938 and 19.19, Dartmouth
announced tonight.
The first of the two contests will
be played on the West Coast with
the return game at some not yet de
termined Eastern gridiron. Dates
I were not announced.
