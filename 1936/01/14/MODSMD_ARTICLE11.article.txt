# Graduates Offered Harvard Fellowships
## 
### 
A limited number of three-year
graduate fellowships in government
for those preparing to enter the
public service as a career are an
nounced by Harvard University.
The first year would be spent at
Harvard, the second in field work '
or a "public interneship," and the ■
third again at Harvard.
Applications must be filed before !
March 1 and addressed to 774 Wide- ,
ner Library, Harvard University, l
Cambridge, Mass.
