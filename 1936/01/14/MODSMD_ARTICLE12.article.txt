# Pickford-Fairbanks Get Final Decree
## 
### 
Mary Pickford's lawyer will cut her
last legal tie to Douglas Fairbanks
before nightfall.
Attorney Lloyd Wright said this
afternoon he will ask Superior
Judge Valentine to enter the final
divorce decree ending Hollywood's
most famous marriage.
Fairbanks, who arrived yesterday
by airplane, was reported golfing
this afternoon while the courts were
to complete he task begun a year
ago when Miss Pickford secured an
interlocutory decree.
