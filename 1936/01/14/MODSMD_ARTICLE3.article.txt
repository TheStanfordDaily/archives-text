# Masquers State Play Contest Rules
## 
### 
Masquers' contest for the best
original play of any length to be
submitted by Stanford students or
residents of Palo Alto closes in two
weeks, according to Rosemary Ben
et, president of the organization.
Tlic plays may be written about any
theme and are to be judged by
prominent members of the English
department faculty and members of
the Masquers.
The winning play will be pre-,
sented as a Masquers' reading late
in February. Manuscripts should be
left in the office of Miss Elizabeth
Buckingham.
