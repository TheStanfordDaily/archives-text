# Women To Bid Dean Yost 'Bon Voyage'
## 
### 
Honoring Dean Mary Yost, who
will leave at the end of this month
for a six month's trip to Europe, all
women students will entertain at a
tea tomorrow afternoon in the Wom
en's Clubhouse from 4 to 5 o'clock.
Members of Women's Conference
will act as hostesses during the
afternoon, and members of the
Sophomore Committee will serve.
Honey Westbrook is in charge of ar
rangements. All Stanford women
are invited.
