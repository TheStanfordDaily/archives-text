# Women Honor Dean Yost at Tea Today
## 
### 
The women students of the Uni
versity will honor Dean Mary Yost,
who leaves for Europe at the end of
this month, at a tea this afternoon
from 4 to 5 o'clock in the lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse.
Honey Westbrook is in charge of
the affair, and will be assisted by
members of Women's Conference,
who will act as hostesses, and by
the Sophomoie Committee, whose
members will serve. All Stanford
women are invited.
