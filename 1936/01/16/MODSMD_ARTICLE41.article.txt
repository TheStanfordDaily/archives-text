# Study Lists Close After Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day students
may change their study lists for this
quarter without fine. After Jan
uary 17, all program revisions must
be made by petition to the Registra
tion Committee subject to the fol
lowing fines: $2 for each course
added or dropped, or $3 where one
course is substituted for another.
Students are held responsible for
all courses and units as they appear
on their official study lists. This
applies to Physical Education as
well.
