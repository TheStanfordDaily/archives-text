# B.A.C. Announces Grid Managers
## 
### 
Junior football managers for the
1936 season were announced yester
day by the Board of Athletic Con
trol, following an announcement
Tuesday of Dan Lisberger's appoint
ment to the senior managership.
Named for junior managers
were: Willard Kovey, Sanford Low
engart, Charles Young, Howard Hol
ser, Ray Fremlin, Guy Hagerty,
Mason Funabaki, and Jerome Alex
ander.
