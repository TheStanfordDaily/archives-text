135 E. 42nd St.
New York City
Sirs:
Please! write our textbooks for us.
Please! write our lectures for us.
Please! write Our Hasher for us.
(\ou might also throw in a Bull Session or two.)
Yours at no increase in cost,
Our Hash
Seeing that yesterday was the day of absolute si
lence between Row and Roble, ihc only noteworthy
occurrence was the tremendous increase of telegraph
calls on the Roble exchange.
I see by the papers that the boxing team has re
newed practice.
I also see by the papers that fraternity houses ad
jacent to sororities have not lost their old interest in
the architecture of sorority windows.
What the sororities need now is a Rood illegal
rushing system. Do you realize, gentlemen, that the
girls don't even know who they're pledging? And
here it is two days before the gland finale!
