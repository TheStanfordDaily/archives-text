# Review Ready For Distribution Friday
## 
### 
The January edition of the Stan
ford Illustrated Review will be
ready for distribution tomorrow, ac
cording to Frank Taylor, editor of
the alumni magazine.
The featured story of the issue
will be "Stanford Federals" by Ced
ric Larson, dealing with the former
Stanford men who are now em
ployed in government service.
Also included will be a write-up
of the Engineering School entitled
"Tools of the Stanford Engineer."
