# Alpha Phi's Fall Heir To Cute Little Foundling
## 
### 
Not that it could have had any
connection with pledging—
Not that the girls are addicted to
such bad habits—
But all the same the Alpha Phi's
were startled this morning when
they opened the portals and viewed
their front lawn.
Not on the approved steps, nor in
a basket, but standing boldly on the
front lawn was a poor, lonesome,
itsy bitsy Austin bantam taxi.
