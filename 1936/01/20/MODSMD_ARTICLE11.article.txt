# German Student Life Subject Of KGO Talk Tonight
## 
### 
"There is no campus life as we
visualize it at a German university,"
according to Waldo H. Furgason, an
American German Student Ex
change Fellow last year, who will
talk on "Student Life in Germany"
tonight at 8 o'clock over station
KGO.
"Not even their sports are organ
ized as ours are," Furgason says.
"German students do everything as
individuals." The speaker will give
his opinion of German students' re
action to the Nazi regime, to world
affairs in general, and also what they
think of America and Americans.
Furgason is now a zoology graduate
here.
