# Grayson Married In Honolulu Saturday
## 
### 
Bobby Grayson and Muss Chris
tine Williams were married Satur
day in Honolulu at St. Clement's
Episcopal Church. Johnny Reisner,
one of Stanford's traveling senior
footballers was best man and Miss
Sidney Williams, sister of the bride,
was maid of honor.
More than 150 guests attended the
wedding and the reception which
followed at the Waialae Golf Club
house.
