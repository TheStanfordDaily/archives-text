# Lutz To Discuss European Crisis
## 
### 
"The European Crisis" will be the
subject of the lecture to be given by
Dr. Ralph H. Lutz, director of the
Hoover War Library, in the Assem
bly Hall tomorrow evening at 7:30
o'clock.
An authority on modern Euro
pean history, Dr. Lutz has studied
the existing militant situations at
first hand since their inception be
fore, during, and since the World
War. He returned from his last trip
abroad last autumn, and is well pre
pared to discuss Ihe present crisis.
