# Hoover Plans Rebuttal Speech
## Ex-President To Attack Recent Roosevelt Talk 
### 
Herbert Hoover will reply to
President Roosevelt's annual "State
of the Nation" ..ddress on February
12th, it was indicated yesterday by
his secretary, Paul Sexton.
Mr. Hoover's talk, to be delivered
in Portland, Ore., "will be given in
a similar tone to those that he has
given recently," according to Mr.
Sexton.
The former president returned to
his Stanford home yesterday from
Lincoln, Neb., and will work on his
speech until his departure for the
north. The address will be one of a
series he has made in recent weeks
commenting on the New Deal and
suggested changes. He was not
available for a statement last night.
