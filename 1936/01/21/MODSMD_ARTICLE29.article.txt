# Director Continues New Play Tryouts
## 
### 
Try outs for male roles in "Paths
of Glory," winter quarter play to be
presented March 6, will continue to
day. Candidates lor roles should in
terview Dr. John Uhler, director of
dramatics, in Room 203C between 9
and 12 o'clock this morning or be
tween 2 and 6 o'clock this afternoon.
Public tryouts will be held tonight
at 7:30 o'clock in the Little Theater.
A large number interviewed Dr.
Uhler yesterday, he said, but many
more strong and virile-looking men
are needed.
A. new official dictionary of the
Castilian tongue was recently com
pleted by the Spanish Academy.
