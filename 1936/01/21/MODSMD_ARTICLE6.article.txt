# Debaters Meet Pitt Team in Radio Clash
## 
### 
Broadcasting over an NBC hook
up, Stanford debaters Gib Holloway
and Jim Reynolds participated in a
non-decision debate with the Uni
versity of Pittsburgh 2500 miles
away yesterday afternoon.
Holloway and Reynolds took the
negative side of the question con
cerning Congressional veto power.
Nine minutes were allotted for the
speeches and three minutes for the
rebuttals. The debate went of!
smoothly, according to Holloway,
except that the Pitt team's rebuttal
was interrupted by the announce
ment of King George's death.
