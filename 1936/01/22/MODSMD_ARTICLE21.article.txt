# YWCA Plans Meet Sunday
## 
### 
The second student-faculty sem
inar of Y.W.C.A. this year will be
held at W. B. Allen's cabin in the
Santa Cruz mountains next Sunday,
with three Stanford professors lead
ing group discussions on the sub
ject, "Where Does the College Stu
dent Fit in the Modern World?"
Dr. Paul R. Hanna, professor of
education, Dr. Ernest R. Hilgard,
psychology professor, and Dr. E.
Van Diller of Palo Alto will speak.
Cars will leave the Women's
Clubhouse at 2 o'clock and return
at 8:30 o'clock. The seminar is open
to all students and faculty mem
bers. Since only 100 students can
be accommodated, reservations must
be made at the Y.W.C.A. office to
morrow.
