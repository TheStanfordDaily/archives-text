# Women's Choral Plans Announced
## 
### 
Winter quarter engagements
made by Women's Choral have
been announced as Stanford Moth
ers' Club on February 18, and a
Sunday afternoon program at the
Palo Alto Community Center on
March 8. KGO and KFRC radio en
gagements are also being arranged.
Tonight's regular rehearsal at
7:15 o'clock in Room 84 is the last
night at which new members may
apply.
