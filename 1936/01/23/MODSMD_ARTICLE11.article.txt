# Planes Used For Transportation By Modern Rulers
## 
### 
(By Associated Press)
King Edward VIII, who by his
flight from Sandringham to Lon
don became the first British King to
use that mode of transportation, is
only one of a number of air-minded
modern rulers.
Both Adolf Hitler and Benito
Mussolini, heads of the governments
of Germany and Italy respectively,
make extensive use of airplane.
Mussolini often pilots his own ship.
Despite the danger from enemy
ainnen, Emperor Haile Selassie of
Ethiopia also travels about his king
dom by plane.
One of the most daring of gov
ernment heads as a flier is Gen
eralissimo anci Premier Chiang Kai
