# Nine Hundred Tickets Sold For Troy Tilts
## 
### 
Nine hundred and twelve tickets
for the Indian-Trojan hoop tilts
Friday and Saturday had been sold
to students last night when the 5
o'clock deadline was reached.
B.A.C. officials said that 473 du
cats for Friday's game and 439 for
the Saturday fray were handed out
for the student admission charge of
40 cents.
This morning tickets will go on
public sale at 75 cents per copy.
None are sold to outsiders until
rooters have had their opportunity
to buy, the B.A.C. said.
