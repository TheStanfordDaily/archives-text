# Masquers To Read Winning Play
## 
### 
The play winning the quarterly
contest sponsored by Masquers,
women's honorary dramatic society,
will be read by the society some
time late in February. The deadline
for the contest is February 12, when
manuscripts must be turned in to the
office of Miss Elizabeth Buckingham
in the English Building.
Judges for the plays submitted
are Miss Buckingham and Dr.
Margery Bailey.
