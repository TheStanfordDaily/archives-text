# Group to Meet At Santa Cruz
## 
### 
Today is the last day to sign up
for the student-faculty seminar
which will be held on Sunday to
discuss the current topic of "Where
Does the College Student Fit in the
Modern World?" The group will
meet in the Allen cabin in the Santa
Cruz Mountains, with panel discus
sion lasting during the afternoon
and evening.
Dr. Paul R. Hanna, Dr. Ernest R.
Hilgard, and Dr. E. Van Diller will
lead individual groups. The three
men were popular leaders at the re
cent Asilomar conference and have
been asked to speak by request of
the students.
Cars will leave the Women's
Clubhouse at 2 o'clock and return
by 8 30. Transportation will be ar
ranged for those who have no cars.
The seminar is open to all students
and faculty members Only 100 per
sons will be able to attend. All reser
vations must be in the Y to
day.
