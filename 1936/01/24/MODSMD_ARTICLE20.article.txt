# Next Tryouts Set For 'Paths of Glory'
## 
### 
Tryoutees for "Paths of Glory"
who were present at the tryouts
Tuesday evening are requested by
Dramatics Director John U'nler to
appear again .Monday evening at 8
o'clock in the Little Theater.
No part has been definitely cast,
Dr. Uhler announces, but five or six
tryoutees for each part will be given
a week or so in which to prove
themselves before definite casting
will be decided upon.
