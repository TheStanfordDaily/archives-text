# Gridders Return Monday From Hawaii Trip
## 
### 
The last amateur football trip for
a group of Stanford gridders will
officially end some time Monday
morning when the bunch who
played two games in the Hawaiian
Islands under Pop Warner return:;
to the campus.
Those returning Monday morning
to Pier 32, San Francisco, aboard the
Mariposa, are: Bobby Grayson and
his bride, Woody Adams, Lurry
Rouble, Johnny Reisner, Noils Lar
sen, Harry Carlson, Wes Muller,
Keith Topping, Jack Holwerda, Bob
Reynolds, Ray Todd, and Waterboy
Doodle Weaver.
