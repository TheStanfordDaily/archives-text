# BENNETT IMPROVING
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 23 (/P).—Rich
ard Bennett, veteran actor who is
seriously ill in Harbor Sanatorium,
was reported to be slightly improved
today. Hospital attendants said he
had passed a "very good night."
