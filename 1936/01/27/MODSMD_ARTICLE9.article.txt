# Injun Contest Winners Can Get Wampum
## 
### 
Winners in The Daily Injun
name contest can get their Swam
pum Wampum by appearing at
the office any afternoon this week,
according to Managing Editor
Phil DufTy.
Nine Philip Morris cigarette
cartons arc in hiding from the edit
staff, and the nine consolation
prize winners arc urged to ap
pear before staff members find
them.
