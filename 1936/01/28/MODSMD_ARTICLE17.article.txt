# DRIVER ABSOLVED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Jan. 27 (/?').—A
coroner's jury today absolved Les
ter G. Humphries, Hyattsville, Md.,
truck driver, of blame in the death
of Senator Thomas D. Schall of Min
nesota, who was fatally injured
when struck by Humphries' truck
Dneomber 19.
