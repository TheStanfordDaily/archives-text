# RAILWAY INCOME UP
## 
### 
ST. PAUL, Minn., Jan. 27 (/P). —
Tho Great Northern Railway today
announced net operating income for
1935 of $23,483,854, a two-thirds in
crease over the 1934 figure of $14,-
101,650.
