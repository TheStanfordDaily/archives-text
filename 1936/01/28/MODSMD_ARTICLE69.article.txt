# Henry Cowell To Lecture Monday
## 
### 
Mr. Henry Cowell, pianist com
poser, assisted by Marie de Forest
Emery, soprano, will give a lecture
and piano recital, sponsored by the
Sunday Afternoon Concert Board of
Palo Alto Community Center, on
Monday evening, February 3, at the
Community Center.
Mr. Cowell will speak of the new
creative tendencies that he found in
his recent sojourn in New York City,
where he gave 175 lectures and con
certs during the period from Octo
ber 1 to December 18. Tickets for
Mr. Cowell's program may be ob
tained at the Community Center,
phone 22161.
