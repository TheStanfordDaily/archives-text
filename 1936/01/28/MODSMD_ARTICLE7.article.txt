# Ticket Sale For Cal Tilt Ends Today
## 
### 
When the B.A.C. doors swing
closed this afternoon at 5 o'clock,
rooters' tickets for the California-
Stanford basketball game at Ber
keley Friday will be off sale for
Stanfordites.
Bear officials have demanded
that the remainder of the Stanford
rooters' bloc! It returned by
Wednesday thus limiting the local
sale to two days.
