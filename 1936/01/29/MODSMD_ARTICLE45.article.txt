# Block Prints Shown In Art Building
## 
### 
On exhibition in Room 311 of the
Graphic Arts Building is a series of
block prints by Elizabeth Norton of j
Palo Alto. These prints, mostly of
animals, are examples of the tech
nique of block printing.
Some are in black and white but
the majority are in one, two, and
three colors. Of special interest are
"Orange Persian" and "After
Lunch." |
