# Tickets Obtainable For German Play
## 
### 
Tickets arc now on sale for the
production of "Jedermann," the
German "Everyman," which will be
produced on February 21 in the
Stanford Assembly Hall under the
direction of Dr. Kurt F. Reinhardt.
Seats are obtainable at Keeble's in
Palo Alto, or from the secretary of
the German department in Room
115. General admission tickets are
priced at 50 cents; student admission
is 25 cents.
Members of the cast now at work
include Professor B. Q. Morgan,
Professor Kurt F. Reinhardt as Jed
ermann, Jane Robinson, Betty Mc-
Glashan, Professor Hanley Sharp,
Carlton Byrne, and other members
of the department.
