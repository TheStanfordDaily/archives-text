# Dance Features Spring Rain Motif
## 
### 
With Freddie Nagel's orchestra
protected by umbrellas from cello
phane raindrops, the Union lobby
will represent a spring shower scene
for Union Club's winter formal on
Friday night. A floor show has been
planned for the supper-dance, fea
turing Yvonne Beattie, who will
sing, and Becky Lee, dancer.
Mr. and Mrs. Richard T. LaPiere,
Mr. and Mrs. Harry J. Rathbun, and
Mr. and Mrs. Stanley L. Sharp have
been chosen as patrons and patron
esses for the formal. Bids are $3,
according to Chairman Beatrice
Driver.
