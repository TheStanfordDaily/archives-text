# CAIRO STUDENTS RIOT
## 
### 
CAIRO, Jan. 29 (/P). —More than
50 students and five police were in
jured today during a short-lived riot,
at Sheiks College where police, be
labored by rocks, fired on the stu
dents.

relationships. If a means could be
devised to give these people what
they want without branding them
failures in the process, it would be
a good thing.
That a boy or girl is not a finished
product socially at this time is not
necessarily a sign of failure or in
competence, but a reflection upon
previous opportunities or unfortu
nate experiences. They deserve a
chance to develop normally now that
Ihey are more mature and recognize
the value of fuller human relation
ship.
Yours for a better school,
Impecunious Male
