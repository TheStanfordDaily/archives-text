# Former Warm Springs Patients
## 
### 

THE FOUR STANFORD men pictured regained their health at the Georgia Warm
Springs Foundation after being stricken by infantile paralysis. They are Don
Muni, upper left; Lewis Hanchett, Jr., lower left; Ralph Keating, lower center; and
Boyd White, lower right. Funds accruing from observances of President Roose
velt's birthday today will aid the institution's work. (Daily Photos)
