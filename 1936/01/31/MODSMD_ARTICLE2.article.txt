# Alumni Invited To Conference
## 8000 Asked To Attend Fourth Annual Meeting 
### 
Invitations went into the mail this
morning to 8,000 Stanford alumni in
Northern California to attend the
fourth annual alumni confex - ence to
be held on the campus February 15
and 16.
Among the topics to be discussod
at the two-day conference are:
American neutrality, socialized med
icine, social security labor relations,
and trade agreements of the Roose
velt administration.
Professor E. A. Cottrell, head of
the Department of Political Sciences
will deliver the opening address,
"Working for Governments." The
closing speech, "Exports of Natural
Resources as Influences in Foreign
Political Policy," will be given by
Dr. Alonzo Taylor, director of the
Food Research Institute.
