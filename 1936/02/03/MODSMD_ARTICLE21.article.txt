# Soph Committee Meets Tonight To Plan Carnival
## 
### 
March 7 has been set as the date
for the annual sophomore carnival,
revived after a lapse of one year by
the Class of '38, for the benefit of the
Convalescent Home Drive.
Since this year's sophomores
have never seen a carnival, it is be
ing sponsored by the Cardinals, who
have selected a committee of upper
classmen to aid the sophs. Jim Lud
lam is chairman of the advisory
group, consisting of Chick Franich.
Ruth Goodan, Phil Coghlan. and Bill
Drew.
