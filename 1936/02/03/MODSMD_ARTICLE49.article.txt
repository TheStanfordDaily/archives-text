# EX-FLYER DIES
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD. Feb. 2
tenant Morey H. Downs, pioneer
United States Navy aviator and
father of Johnny Downs, film ju
venile, died today of heart failure.
He was 59 years old.
Fourteen whales have been taken
from the stomach of a 16-foot whale
killer.
