# P.O. Quad Sale Starts Today
## 
### 
Post office Quad sales tomorrow
and Thursday will open a new "first
of the month" sales program out
lined yesterday by Circulation
Manager Bob Wright.
During the first week of each
month for the balance of the school
year members of the Quad sales
staff will cover all campus living
groups, including the Row, Wright
announced.
The installment method of paying
for the yearbook will be featured
during the campaign, $3.17 down and
the balance at the student's con
venience. Orders may be placed at
any time at the Stanford Bookstore
and the A.S.S.U. office.
