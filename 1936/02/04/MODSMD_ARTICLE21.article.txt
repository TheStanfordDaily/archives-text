# Campus Calendar
## 
### 
TUESDAY, FEBRUARY 1
Nomination Petitions: May be filed
at the Law Steps today and to
morrow.
Lecture: Professor Max Radin will
speak on "Unconstitutionality" at
7:30 o'clock in th<> Assembly Hall.
Daily Election: The polls will open
at 1 o'clock in The Daily Shack
and will close not later than 3
o'clock.
Ex Committee: Regular meeting at
7:15 o'clock in the Committee
Room.
Co-op: Meeting in the lobby of the
Old Union at 7:15 o'clock.
