# Campus Calendar
##

WEDNESDAY, FEBRUARY 5

Play Reading: "What Price Glory?"
at 8 o'clock in the Little Theater.
Admission will be 10 cents.
"Y" Dinner: For finance drive work
ers at 5:15 o'clock.
Nomination Petitions: Today from 8
to G o'clock at the Law Steps.
Daily Business Stall: Meeting at
1:15 o'clock in business office.
Any tryoutees may sign up at this
time.
Lagunita Jolly-up: 7 o'clock in the
Old Roble Gym.
Recorded Music: Program at 4:15
o'clock in Room 84.
Research Club: Meeting in the Fac
ulty Clubhouse at 8 p.m.
Program of recorded music, 4:15
o'clock, Room 84.
