# Boston U. Offers Grad Scholarships
## 
### 
Assistantships, fellowships, and
scholarships for graduate study will
be available for the academic year
1936-37 at the Boston University
Graduate School, with payment
ranging from tuition and half-room
rent to S6OO. Sixteen departments
offer assistantships, and 36 fellow
ships and scholarships are listed in
the new announcement by the
school.
Further information may be ob
tained from the dean of the grad
uate school, Boston University,
1 Boston, Massachusetts.
