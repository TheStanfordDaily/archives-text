# TOOTH BAD
## 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
Being highly displeased with a
very disagreeable toothache, I
paced the floor all evening, and when
the time came that something had
to be done, I started for the Union
to purchase the necessary drugs.
Suddenly I remembered that there
was no druggist on the campus.
Transportation at that late hour was
sparse; consequently I resumed my
trek from one end of the room to
the other.
The Union has delicious orange
juice for 15 cents a "shot" — how
about dollar aspirin tablets for a
change? After all, Paly is awfully
far away to mingle with dazzling
drugs.
Philbert
P.S. I had the tooth mended!
