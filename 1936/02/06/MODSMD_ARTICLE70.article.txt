# Relations Club Will Discuss Sanctions
## 
### 
International Relations Club will
meet thus afternoon at 4:10 o'clock in
the West Memorial Library, Secre
tary Robert Telfeyan announced
yesterday.

Boggs will lead a discussion on the
topic, "Economic Effects of Sanc
tions." All members of the club and
others interested in the subject are
asked to be present, Telfeyan said.
