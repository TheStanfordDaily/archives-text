# Netmen Trim Olympic Club by 9-1 Margin
## Cards Win All Singles As Dey Defeats Driscoll 
### 
Burying Olympic Club netmen
under an avalanche of cannonball
smashes, Sherman Lockwood's var
sity tennis team won nine of ten
matches from the Post Streeters on
the varsity courts Saturday.
The Cards won seven singles
matches without the loss of a set.
Ben Dey ,the new varsity number
one man, came through to trim the
Club champ, Larry Driscoll, in the
feature match of the afternoon.
