# 'Rough' Takes Girl to Dance On Handlebars
## 
### 
A true "rough" went the farmer
ettes one better Saturday night
| when, clad in overalls, he called for
I his Sequoia barn dance partner—on
J a bicycle.
Lacking a tandem, he pedalled to
the young lady's home in Paly on a
one-seater, perched her on the han
dle bars, and pedalled back to Se
j quoia.
Dance over, atop handle bars she
i parked again while her proud
i Lochinvar cycled her to a down
town eatery, then home again. "It
was like the old days," she said later.
