# Six Indian Tennis Stars To Meet Golden Gate Club
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity tennis team
will meet the Golden Gate Tennis
Club team on the men's courts at
1:45 o'clock Saturday afternoon.
Representing Stanford in singles
matches will be Ben Dey, Johnny
Law, Bill Seward, Bob Underwood,
Robert Pommer, and Dan Clarke.
Dey and Pommer, and Underwood
and Seward will pair off in doubles
matches.
Lloyd Budge, brother of Don
Budge, a member of the United
States Davis Cup team, plays for the
Golden Gate Club.
Frosh netmen will play Modesto
Junior College at 10 o'clock Satur
day morning on the varsity courts.
