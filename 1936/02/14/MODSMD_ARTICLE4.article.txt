# Extra Late Leave Granted To Women
## 
### 
Late leaves taken on February 22,
Washington's birthday, will not be
counted on the regular quota, de
cided Women's Council yesterday.
They must be signed out for as
usual, and the customary 2:30 limit
will be observed.

Patricia Flavel, new chairman,
also warns that cars may not be
parked in the no-parking areas even
though a licensed driver remains at
lit wheel.
