# Changes in Physics Courses Announced
## 
### 
Changes in the schedule of four
courses in physics, made primarily
for the convenience of students in
the School of Engineering, were an
nounced today by Professor D. L.
Webster, executive head of the de
partment.
Heat (Physics 33) and Heat Lab
oratory (Physics 34), formerly given
in the spring quarter, will inter
change with Electricity (Physics
35) and Electricity Laboratory
(Physics 36), formerly given in the
autumn quarter. The changes will
be effective beginning with the pres
ent spring quarter.
