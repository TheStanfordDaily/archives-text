# Sword and Sandals To Present Reading
## 
### 
Phyllis Corson and Fred Clark
will be co-starred in the leading
roles of George Kaufman's and Edna
Ferber's satire on the Barrymore
family, "The Royal Family," to be
read by Sword and Sandals in the
Little Theater on Thursday at 8:15.
Miss Corson, the J'jssica Wells of
"Dark Tower," will play Julie Cav
endish, representing Ethel Barry
more; and Fred Clerk, who had the
leading role of Damon Wells in the
same production, has the part of
Tony Cavendish, representing John
Barrymore.
