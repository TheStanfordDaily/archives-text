# Pasadena Officials To Honor Masters
## 
### 
AI Masters, graduate manager,
kff last night for the South, where
lie will be honored by Pasadena city
officials tonight as an outstanding
beneficiary to the civic welfare of
that community.
Five men are named every year
for citations of this sort in Pasa
dena. Mr. Masters' work dealt
largely with the success of the foot
ball game at the New Year's Tour
nament of Roses.
