# Scabbard, Blade Contest Closes
## 
### 
Shirley Jones, Gaieties warbler,
is the latest entry in the Scabbard
and Blade contest for campus song
stresses. All entries were closed
yesterday, and preliminary tryouts
will take place Thursday afternoon
at 4 o'clock in Woodpecker Lodge.
The winners of the preliminaries
will appear at the Lagunita Formal
Friday night, and the final winner
< is to sing at the Military Ball and
also over a national broadcast with
J Tom Coakley's orchestra.
