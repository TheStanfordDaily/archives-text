# Wisconsin Coach Joins Toledo Staff
## 
### 
TOLEDO. 0., Feb. 18 (/!').- Dr.
Clarence W. Spears, dismissed as
football coach at the University of
Wisconsin, was named athletic di
rector and head football coach at
the University of Toledo today.
The board of trustees of the uni
versity appointed him professor of
physical education at a salary of
$3,100 and football coach at a salary
of $1,000.
