# Glee Club Will Give Broadcast Sunday
## 
### 
Stanford's Glee Club will appear
as guest artists at the Palace Hotel
Dinner Concert, in Palm Court from
6:30 to 8:30 o'clock Sunday, Febru
ary 2.3, along with the Palace Hotel
Dinner Concert Ensemble.
The program will be broadcast
over a coast-wide hook-up over
NBC. Among the songs are two
written by Stanford students.
Twenty-five men from Stanford will
be in the concert
