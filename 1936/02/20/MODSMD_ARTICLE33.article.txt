# Lagunita, Frosh Give Dances
## Winter Formal, 'Encinasylum' Feature Fancy Decoration In Week-end Social Events 
### 
Lagunita Court will formally
"Usher in Spring" at its annual
winter formal Friday night, from 9
to 1 o'clock, according to Janice
Fulton, general chairman of the
dance.
Lee Hamlin's orchestra from
Piedmont will furnish the music.
The decorations and favors are to
remain a secret, but the hand
painted bids have been on sale for
a week.
Singers Will Appear
Appearing on the program will be
three competitors appearing in the
Scabbard and Blade singing con
test.
On Saturday from 9 to 12 o'clock
Encina will present its winter in
formal and transform the hall into
an "Encinasylum." The lobby and
the pool room will be decorated
with an asylum atmosphere, accord
ing to Anthony Koese, general
chairman of the affair.
Hall Men To Play
The orchestra will be composed
of hall men directed by Bud Fisher.
Patrons and patronesses for the
Encina affair are Dr. and Mrs. P. H.
Kirkpatrick, Dr. and Mrs. F. R.
Johnson, Mr. and Mrs. F. M.
Gross, and Mr. and Mrs. A. E. Fife.
Bids are on sale for $1.50 at either
Encina Hall or the A.S.S.U. offices.
