# Editor Tells Neophytes Aim Of Five W's
## 
### 
At the third tryoutee meeting
of Volume 89, candidates for Daily
editorial staff positions listened to
a talk on news-writing Thurs
day evening by Associate Editor.
John McClelland, in which he
stressed the importance of the five
journalistic W's: Who? What?
Why? Where and When?
News stories handed in at this
session will be graded and may be
called for Friday morning in the
Daily shack, according to Wom
en's Editor Dorothy Sweet, in
charge of tryoutees.
The next meeting will he Tues
day at 5 o'clock, in the Journalism
Library.
