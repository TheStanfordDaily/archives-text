# Prizes Offered For College Editorials
## 
### 
Prizes of $50 and $25 are being
offered by the Nation and the For
eign Policy Association for the best
1000-word editorials written by col
lege undergraduates on the subject
"Will Neutrality Keep Us Out of
War." Third and fourth-place prizes
of a year's subscription to the Na
tion and a year's membership in the
Foreign Policy Association are also
being given.
The editorials must reach the of
fice of the Association in New York
City not later than March 15, 1936.
They must be accompanied by a
signed statement that the editorial
is original and endorsed by some
professor.
