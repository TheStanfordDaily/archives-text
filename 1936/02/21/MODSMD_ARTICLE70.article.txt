# How I Would Have Kidnaped the Lindbergh Baby
## 
### Jeremy F. Wong 
Hashboy bum boy. Alice time talkee, singum,
makeum noise, breakum dish. Nappec kid lindbug.
Easy. Bum boy hashboy. Govmen takum money alleo
time. Nobody say nothin. Easy. I passum excise tax
on Lindbug nappee. Easy. Hashboy bum boy.

(Ed. Note: When pointed out that this would bt
unconstitutional, Jeremy F. Wong, Dean of Men
Women, Children, and Wong's Kitch, replied: "Yeh
I know, but it's fun ain't it?")
