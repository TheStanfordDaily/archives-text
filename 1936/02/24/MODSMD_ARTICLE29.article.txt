# Clipper Fights Winds To Land at Hawaii
## 
### 
The China Clipper, Manila bound,
yesterday landed at Honolulu at
2:05 p.m., Pacific time, completing
the first icg of her long-delayed
flight.
The huge flying ship, powered by
new motors, battled head winds
for the entire 2400-mile journey.
Almost 24 hours were required for
the hop. The Clipper took off from
Alameda at 2:18 p.m. Saturday.
