# Women Start Second Golf Round Today
## 
### 
Second round matches in the
women's intramural golf tournament
will open today and must be played
before March 1, according to the
women's golf manager. Matches
cannot be played on Saturday after
noons or Sunday and no green fees
will be charged.
Pairings for the second round are:
Tri Delta vs. Delta Gamma I; Kap
pa I vs. Pi Phi; Smith and Shield vs.
Lagunita; and Theta I vs. Kappa 11.
Ancient Egyptians believed that
Egypt was the center of the universe.
