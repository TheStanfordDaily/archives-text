# Travel Movies To Be Shown Tonight
## 
### 
Moving pictures of the travels of
Bayne Bcauchamp, famous for his
student tours, will be shown this
evening at the Delta Chi house at
7:30 o'clock for all those who are
interested.
Mr. Bcauchamp is planning a trip
to Alaska this summer and already
has signed up 15 Stanford students
to accompany him.
