# Hollo way Asks For Volunteers In Home Drive
## 
### 
Students interested in working
on the Convalescent Home Drive
are asked by Gib Holloway, drive
chairman, to report to him at the
A.S.S.U. offices any afternoon
from 1:30 to 2:30 o'clock.
Several of the most important j
positions — including Bean Day
chairman and treasurer —have not j
yet been filled, and he asks that
more people, especially prominent \
hall men and women, turn in their !
names.
