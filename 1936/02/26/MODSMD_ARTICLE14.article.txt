# Mrs. Hoover Speaks Before 'Y' Groups
## 
### 
Mrs. Herbert Hoover will speak to
the social service group of the Y.W.
C.A. tomorrow afternoon at 4
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
As national president of the Girl
Scouts. Mrs. Hoover has chosen for
her topic "The National Girl Scout
Organization: Its Place in Commun
ity Organization."
Following Mrs. Hoover's talk, tea
will be served by the "Y" social
group, under the charge of Frances
Bcedy.
