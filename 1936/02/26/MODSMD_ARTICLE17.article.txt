# Harmon Gerry New Purchasing Agent
## 
### 
Harmon T. Gerry, '25, former
president of the Purchasing Agents
of California, has been appointed
University purchasing agent to suc
ceed M. Urdinan, according to
Comptroller Almon E. Roth.

Gerry's appointment was con
firmed by the Board of Trustees.
According to Roth, his time will be
divided between his offices in San
Francisco at the Stanford Hospital
and his campus office in the Comp
troller's division of the Administra
tion Building.
