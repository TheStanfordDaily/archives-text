# 'S-s-s-s-s' No Longer In Rough's Vocabulary
## 
### 
Jascha Heifetz, fiddler extraordinary, who
will appear in concert here tomorrow evening,
has actually invited students to pucker up
their mouths and blow if they don't like him.
But Mr. Heifelz undoubtedly has investigated
Stanford conditions, and knows he is on the
safe side.
He probably knows, for example, that the
Paly police, gumshoeing around local cinema
houses, have broken Stanford students of
their habit of hissing at anything except bas
ketball games.
