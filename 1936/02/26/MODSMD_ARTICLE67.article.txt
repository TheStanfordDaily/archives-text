# Dowrie To Address Engineering Societies
## 
### 
Professor George W. Dowrie of the
Graduate School of Business, will
speak before a joint meeting of the
student chapters of the engineering
societies at 7:30 o'clock today in
the Women's Clubhouse.

His subject will be "Where Are
We and Where Are We Going?" All
members of the American Society
of Civil Engineers, the American
Society of Mechanical Engineers,
and the American Institute of Elec
trical Engineers arc invited.
