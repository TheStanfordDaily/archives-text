# Daily Fashion Edition Out Tomorrow
## 
### 
What to wear, and when—as
dictated by The Daily's staff of
fashion experts—will be explained
to the Farm tomorrow in the an
nual Spring Fashion Edition of
The Daily.
Every type of clothing, from
tails to garters for men and from
skirts to shorts for women, will be
described in the special edition.
The latest trends in women's wear
and correct usage of men's usual
styles are carried in the section.
New dresses and formals, mod
eled by Stanford women, arc to be
shown in the section's pictures.
Included also will be humorous
comments as well as sober reports
of the proper clothes for each oc
casion.
