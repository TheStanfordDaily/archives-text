# Reaching for Northern Hoop Title
## 
### 

THESE ARE THE FIVE "iron men" of the
University of Washington basketball team, which
is shooting for the Conference championship and
the right to enter the Olympic trials. Defeated
only twice in conference play, they are the

leaders in the northern division race. Left to
right are Chuck Wagner, forward; Bob Egge,
guard; Boc McKinstry, guard; Ed Loverich, for
ward; and Ralph Bishop, center. (A.P. Photo)
