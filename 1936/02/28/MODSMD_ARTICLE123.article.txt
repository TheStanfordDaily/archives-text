# Gabardine Trunks for Campus Bathers
## 
### 
Lagunita invites swimming parties
and Sunday picnic, and campus
bathers will display their loose fit
ting wool gabardine shorts in gay
colors with a pleated front and laced
waist.
The smartest thing for the aquatic
star is the comparatively new Scotch
plaid gaberdine trunks with short
racy lines.
