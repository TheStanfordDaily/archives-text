# Spring To Feature Copper-Toned Shoes
## 
### 
Coppery-toned shoes make their
distinguished appearance this spring.
They can be found in sandals
adorned by copper nail-heads, in the
new square-heeled and toed pumps,
and in dressier shoes as well as
street shoes.
These are particularly good with
blues, greens, and prints.
