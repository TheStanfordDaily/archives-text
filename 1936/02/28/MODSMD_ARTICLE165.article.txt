# Loungers Prefer Satin, Prints, Velvet
## 
### 
Lounging pajamas of the season
create a dramatic effect with strik
ing contrasts in color and materials.
Silvery satin in icy blue and flow
ered prints are featured in very 1
feminine fashions.
Velvet and satin combinations |
and Chinese brocades in the new
cherry shade are very much in
vogue. Hollywood stars choose Ro
man striped blouses with plain satin
trousers for their hours at home.
