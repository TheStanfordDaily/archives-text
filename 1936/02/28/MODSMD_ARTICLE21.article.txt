# Organ Recital Will Include 'Andante'
## 
### 
Raymond B. Kendall, acting Uni
versity organist, will play the "An
dante" from Mendelssohn's "Violin
Concerto" in this Sunday's after
noon organ recital in the Memorial
Chapel at 4 o'clock.
Typical of Cesar Franck's emo
tional interwoven harmonies is his
"Chorale in A Minor," also to be
played on the program.
Bach's "Adagio," from his Toc
cata and Fugue in C. Iljinsky's
"Cradle Song"; and Cesar Franck's
"Cantabile," will complete the list of
selections to be played.
