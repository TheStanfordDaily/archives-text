# Surgery Instructor To Lecture at Lane
## 
### 
"The Value and Limitation of
| Plastic Operative Procedures" is the
subject of an illustrated lecture to
be delivered by Dr. Albert D. Davis,
clinical instructor in surgery, to
night at 8 o'clock in Lane Hall,
Stanford School of Medicine.
Lane Hall is located on Webster
and Sacramento Streets in San
Francisco. Admission is free.
