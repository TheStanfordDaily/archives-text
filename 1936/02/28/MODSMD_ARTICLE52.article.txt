# Dr. Wilbur Named Educational Adviser
## 
### 
The Educational Policies Commis
sion announced yesterday at Wash
ington that Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur
has been appointed as a consultant
ex officio of that group.
The appointment of a group of
educational leaders as consultants is
important in the program of the
commission. Formed in 1935 for a
five-year term, the commission is
intended to develop long-range
planning for the improvement of
American schools.
