# 'Paths of Glory' Tickets on Sale
## Play's First Showing on Pacific Coast To Be Friday Evening; Compared with 'Yellow Jack' 
### 
Tickets for Sidney Howard's
"Paths of Glory" will go on sale for
the first time today from 10 to 2
o'clock on the Engineering Corner.
Future sales will be held at the
A.S.S.U. offices and at Keeble's in
Palo Alto. Reserved scats will be
available at $1, 75 cents, and 35
cents.
i
The play will be presented for the
first time on the Pacific Coast Fri
day night in the Assembly Hall.
Beards which have been grown by
' members of the cast during the past
! week will do much to make the play
realistic.
The play resembles "Yellow Jack,"
' seen here last spring, in its effec
tive use of lighting and in the scarc
ity of curtain drops. Only one cur
tain fall will be used, with inter
j vening changes being made entirely
I by lighting cffects.
