# Founder's Day To Be Celebrated March 9
## 
### 
Founder's Day will be celebrated
by the Stanford alumni March 9, in
Washington, D.C., at the mansion
where Senator and Mrs. Leland
Stanford lived during their Wash
ington residence.
The program will include "Tales
of the Farm" in the old days as told
by former Senator Charles B. Hen
derson of Nevada and Carl A. Loef
fler, secretary to the minority,
United States Senate.
