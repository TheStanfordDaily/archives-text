# SWIMMERS TO ELECT HEAD
## 
### 
Freshmen swimmers meet at 4:30
o'clock this afternoon at the Encina
pools to elect their captain for the
193G season. Coach Thornton re
quests that all men be out promptly.
In a recent experiment, 28 Vassar
College students gained weight and
maintained good health at a cost of
43 cents per day for food.
