# Ticket Sales For U.S.C. Contest Break Hoop Record
## 
### 
More students will attend the
Stanford-U.S.C. game Saturday
night than have attended any bas
ketball contest in the history of
Stanford, for by 10:30 yesterday
morning the last of 18112 tickets,
all that could be placcd on sale
by the 8.A.C., had been sold.
The remaining 1079 places in the
Pavilion will be occupied by
Southern California rooters, teams,
coachos, band members, wearers
of the Block 'S', newspaper men,
police, and holders of complimen
tary tickets.
