# Y.W.C.A. Sponsors Spring Reg Dance
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. is sponsoring the
.spring quarter registration dance, to
be given on Monday, March 30, from
9 to 12 o'clock in the Women's Gym.
Jackie Brown is in charge of ar
rangements for the dance.
Committee heads are: publicity,
Jerry Reed; tickets, Florence
Brown; arrangements, Maxine
Moore; patrons and personnel, Fran
cesca Young. Ernie Heckscher's
orchestra will supply the music for
the dance, which will be $1 per
couple. Charles Breier will be the
featured vocalist.
