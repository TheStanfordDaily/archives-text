# Professor Charges Mass Cheating In Examination at U.C.
## 
### 
Four hundred and fifty U.C. test
papers were held up today when
Economics Professor M. M. Wright
charged mass cheating on his mid
term examinations.
The professor stated that as many
as five or six papers were handed in
containing the same answers, indi
cating the students had copied and
exchanged the answers.
The honor system was severely
jolted by the professor's charge and
a student inquiry begun. Professor
Wright, asked that all the cheaters
report to him as a "test of honor."
