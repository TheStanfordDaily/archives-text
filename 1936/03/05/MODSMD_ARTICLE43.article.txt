# Frosh In Dire Need of More Men for Track
## 
### 
One of the scantiest turnouts in
history has frosh track mentor
Bill Ellington doing some fancy
worrying these balmy afternoons.
Weaknesses in the 880. mile,
two- mile, pole vault, ami high
jump present problems difficult
to solve. Several ineligibilities
have not aided the yearling cause.
Consequently a call is sounded
| for all Encinamcn who have the

slightest suspicion that they can
do something in track to put their
wares on display. Even "raw
material'' is welcome in the present
crisis.
