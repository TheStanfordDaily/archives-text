# Party Will Aid Student Fund
## Women To Give Annual Bridge Tea and Fashion Show for Scholarship 
### 
For the purpose of making it pos
sible for some worthy girl to at
tend Stanford University, the an- ,
nual scholarship party of the Stan
ford women of San Francisco and
the Peninsula will be held in the!
terrace ballroom of the Fairmont
on Saturday, March 14.
Bridge will start at 2:15 o'clock
and tea will be served at 4 o'clock
when advance spring fashions will
be exhibited by Stanford women.
Each year the scholarship fund is
collected in this way and presented
to a San Francisco girl who has
graduated with honors from high
school. Miss Claire Mitchell will
represent Stanford at the affair.
