# 3373 Register To Break Old Spring Record
## 
### 
First-day registration for spring
quarter continued the year's prece
dent of shattering enrollment fig
ures with 3373 students storming
the administration building, accord
ing lo figures released by J. P.
Mitchell, registrar.
This figure compares with 3257
registrations on the first day of
spring quarter last year. Mr. Mitch
ell estimated that yesterday's fig
ures included one thousand women.
The registrar stated that late and
incomplete registrations will add
several hundred students.
