# Stanford Engineers Tour Parker Dam
## 
### 
Twenty-two members of the jun
ior branch of the American Society
of Civil Engineers from Stanford
left last Tuesday for a tour of the
Parker Dam project. Seventeen
student and five professors made up
the group.
The party went first to the Colo
rado River to inspect the works of
the Parker Dam, and from there
traveled to Los Angeles, inspecting
various parts of the project on the
way.
