# Infuriated Serfs Battle Yugoslavs
## 
### 
BELGRADE, Yugoslavia. March
30 (.'!*). -Reports from Zagreb said
a huge crowd of angry Croats today
battled Yugoslav police with all
manner of weapons for several
hours.
Kioting spread to several sections
of the city and many shots were
exchanged, dispatches said. Six
persons were understood to have
been seriously injured and many
arrested.
