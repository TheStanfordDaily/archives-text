# Bradshaw To Coach Frosh Baseballers
## 
### 
As Ernie Nevets, regular frosh
baseball coach, has left for Lafa
yette College to take over his coach
duties there, Jimmy Bradshaw, pa
poose football coach, will guide the
frosh ball team for the remainder of
the season.
The freshmen will play their next
game Saturday when they meet the
Balboa high school nine. Later in
the quarter will come their big test
against the California frosh.
