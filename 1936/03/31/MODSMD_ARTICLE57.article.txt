# Bureau Describes Available Speakers
## 
### 
Speakers' Bureau Chairman
Chuck Schafer announced last night
that the bureau's first pamphlet de
scribing speeches offered will be
ready for distribution within two
weeks. He asks students who have
agreed to prepare talks to contact
him at once.
The second and last pamphlet of
the year will be issued April 24,
Schafer said. Students desiring to
join the bureau this year must ap
pear before the Review Board prior
to that date.
