# New Spring Course Announced by Craig
## 
### 
A four-unit course, "Art and
Archaeology, and Art Appreciation"
will be offered this quarter by the
School of Letters and repeated in the
summer session, Professor Hardin
Craig, chairman of the Council of
the School of Letters, announced
yesterday.
Work will include lectures, field
trips to galleries, libraries, and pri
vate collections. The class will be
limited to 29 students and those
planning to register for it. should see
Professor Craig at his office, Room
209 A, English building.
