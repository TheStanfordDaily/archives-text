# Scholarships Open To Business Students
## 
### 
Three $300 scholarships are be

School Club of San Francisco to
students planning to enter the
Harvard University Graduate School

fall of 1936. These scholarships are
open to men graduating from col
leges and universities in the bay
area this spring.
