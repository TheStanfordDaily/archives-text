# Manuscripts Deadline Scheduled for May 1
## 
### 
Manuscripts for the yearbook, in
cluding short stories, essays, poetry,
and prose will be accepted in Room
207 in the English department up
until May 1.
Prizes of $25 each are being of
fered for the best prose work and
for the best poetry. The judges for
the contest will be Max Miller,
.Judge Henry Jackson, and Derrik
Laymar.
