# Counsel Position Offered Farm Women
## 
### 
A summer camp counseling Girl
Reserves in dramatics, handicrafts,
art, and athletics is offered capable
Stanford women who apply at the
Y.W.C.A. rooms this afternoon from
1 to 4 o'clock.
Barbara Binns, '34, Santa Clara
regional director, will select coun
selors for the Girl Reserve camp in
Big Basin, Santa Cruz Mountains, to
be held July 12 to August 23. Those
interested are asked to secure an
appointment by calling the Y.W.
C;A, P.A. 4301.
