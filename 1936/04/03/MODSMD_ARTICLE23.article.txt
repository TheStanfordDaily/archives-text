# Palm Sunday To Be Observed in Chapel
## 
### 
Palm Sunday will he observed at
Stanford with a service in the Me
morial Church at 11 o'clock noxl
Sunday. The Reverend U. Charles
Gardner, chaplain of the University,
will deliver the sermon.
The Choir will sing musical selec
tions. including "Alleluia," "Behold
the Lamb of God," and "Surely He
Hath Borne Our Griefs" from "The
Messiah." Warren D. Allen, Univer
sity organist, will present an organ
recital at four o'clock Sunday after
noon.
