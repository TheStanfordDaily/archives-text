# Music Class Closed; Choral Work Given
## 
### 
Because the class is overcrowded,
Music 91—Music in History, is closed
to all other students, Warren D. Al
len announced yesterday.

A new course, Music 13 —Dramatic
Choral Music, is offered members of
Chorales and Choir for 1 unit of
credit. The object is to prepare the
students for the presentation of Han
del's "Messiah" on June 7.
