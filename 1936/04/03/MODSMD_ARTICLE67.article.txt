# Youth Case Heard By Senate Committee
## 
### 
Seventy-five young people from
student and youth organizations all
over the country plead their case for
the American Youth Act to mem
bers of the Senate Committee on
Education and Labor last week.
Youth problem testifiers described
the needs of unemployed and out
of-school young people between 16
and 25 with statistics and human in
terest stories.
The Youth Act, sponsored by the
American Youth Congress, calls for
job and education provisions for the
eight million young people now un
employed outside of school. More
than two and a half million young
people were represented by those
who spoke.
