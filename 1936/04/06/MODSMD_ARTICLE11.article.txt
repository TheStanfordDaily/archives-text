# PINK SNOW FALLS
## 
### 
ALTAMONT (Kan.), April 5 (/P).
—"Pink snow" came to Altamont
this winter. Some school children,
expecting a strawberry flavor, found
out the tint came from red soil
whipped into the snow storm by a
midwinter blizzard.
