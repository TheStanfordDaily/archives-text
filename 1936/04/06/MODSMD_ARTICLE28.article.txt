# MISCONDUCT STOPS WEDDING
## 
### 
PAPEETE, Tahiti, April 5 (/P).—
Word has been received from Rara
tonga that the marriage of the royal
blooded granddaughter of Norman
Brander and the son of the Rara
tongan king has been cancelled be
cause of "grave misconduct" by the
prospective bridegroom.
