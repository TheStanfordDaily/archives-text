# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
Quad: Studio will be open on Wed
nesday from 1 to 5 o'clock to give
out pictures ordered, and receive
proofs.
Quad Staff: Members may work
from 9:15 to 12 o'clock in the
Quad office.
Women's Conference: Will meet in
the Women's Clubhouse at 1
o'clock tomorrow.
Symphony Orchestra: The first
meeting of the quarter will be
held tonight in the band house at
7:30. All old members, and any
new or prospective members are
urged to be on hand with their
instruments. Mr. Julius Haug is
directing.
