# Wilbur Goes East To Attend Meetings
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur
left Saturday evening for Boston,
where Dr. Wilbur will attend several
medical meetings, including the
convention of the Association of
Pathologists and Bacteriologists.
From Boston they will go to New
York City, where Dr. Wilbur will
meet with the Rockefeller Founda
tion and with the General Educa
tion Board, of which he is a mem
ber. He will also preside over the
meetings of the American Social Hy
giene Association. Dr. and Mrs. Wil
bur will return to the campus in
time for University Day ceremonies,
April 25.
