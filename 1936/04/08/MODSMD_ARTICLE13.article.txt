# Farm Offers Garden Course Next Term
## 
### 
Practical Gardening, a one-unit
course, will be taught at Stanford
during the summer quarter, it was
announced yesterday. The purpose
of the course is to give students
first-land information concerning
the treatment of soil and the grow
ing of potted plants.
The class will be under the direc
tion of Miss Elsa Uppman, a gradu
ate of the California School of. Gar
dening.
