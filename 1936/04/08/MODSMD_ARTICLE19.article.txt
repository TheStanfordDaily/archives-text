# Dr. Webster Named To Education Group
## 
### 
Professor David Locke Webster of
the Stanford Physics Department
has been appointed as consultant
ex-officio for the Educational Poli
cies Commission, it was announced
today.
The commission was formed as a
co-operative effort to develop long
time planning for American educa
tion. Professor Webster will assist
the Commission by expressing opin
ions on issues submitted and re
porting the conclusions of important
committees.
