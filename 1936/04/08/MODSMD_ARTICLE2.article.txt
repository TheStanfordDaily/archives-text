# 'Sings' Planned
## 
### 
At the next Interfraternity Coun
cil meeting there will be a more
thoroughgoing investigation into the
possibilities of conducting interfra
ternity "sings" at Stanford. Hoover
is expecting a special report con
taining details of the workings of
such "sings" elsewhere, and he be
lieves that a similar program can
be conducted here.
Many eastern schools hold such
interfraternity singing contests and
derive a great deal of fellowship and
pleasure from therm. Many believe
that once such a program was start
ed here, it would lead more than
anything else to a better, more
amicable interfraternity relation
ship.
