# Off-Campus Drive Begins Wednesday
## 
### 
Ofl-campus canvassing for con
tributions to the Stanford Conval
escent Home Drive will start Wed
nesday, April 15, it was announced
last night by Art Lundin, who, with
Sylvia Case, is co-chairman of the
committee.
Approximately 40 members will
compose the committee, which will
appeal to Palo Alto citizens and off
campus students for contributions to
the fund. Students interested in
joining the committee are asked to
see either of the co-chairmen, or
to hand their names in at the A. S.
S. U. office.
