# Tryouts for Musical Start Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tryouts for the spring musical,
"Three Sheets to the Wind," will be
held tomorrow afternoon, beginning
at 2 o'clock, in the committee room
of the Old Union.
Candidates for all the parts, which
are open to both men and women,
will be given by individual confer
ence. Singers and actors both are
requested to try out. The confer
ence will be led by Frank O'Neill.
