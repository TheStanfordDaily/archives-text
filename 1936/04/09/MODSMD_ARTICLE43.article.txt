# Ex-President Hoover Returns from East
## 
### 
Former Fresidcnt Hoover returned
to his Palo Alto home yesterday
from a trip throughout the East. He
was accompanied by his secretary,
Paul Sexson.
Last Saturday night in his speech
at Fort Wayne, Ind., he further as
sailed the New Deal, and declared
that the issue of American liberty
"transcends all others."

S. J. Toney, Welling, Okla., farm
er, has carved a persimmon fiddle
for which he has been offered $50.
