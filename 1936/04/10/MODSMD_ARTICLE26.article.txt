# Lagunita Fans Restricted By Usual Rules
## 
### 
The following regulations for those
desiring to use the lake for swim
ming or canoeing have been released
by Bill Woodward, lake manager.
1. Hours of the boathouse are from
10 a.m. to 10 p.m.
2. Every canoe must be equipped
with a life preserver.
3. Canoes must not be tipped.
4. In case of accident, persons in
volved must stay with the canoe
until a life guard reaches the scene.
Use life preservers in emergencies.
| .5. Persons who turn canoes over
must report to the boathouse imme
diately and leave the lake for the
remainder of the day.
|6. Swimmers are requested not to
swim beyond the first float.
