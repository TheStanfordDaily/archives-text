# Dean Yost Writes From Balearic Isles
## 
### 
Dt'an Mary Yost, now on a six
months' trip abroad, writes Mrs.
Leona Diekema, directress of Roble
Hall, from Palma de Mallorca ir the
Balearic Islands.
Miss Yost has moved from the
capital to a little fishing village, 40
miles away, called Puerto Pollensa,
and will leave soon to join Dr. and
Mrs. Robert E. Swain, who are also
traveling in Europe, on April 17.
