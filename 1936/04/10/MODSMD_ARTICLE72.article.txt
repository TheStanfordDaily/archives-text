# BUSINESS BETTER
## 
### 
The Bird's on the wing.
Carloadings are up.
Business is better.
The people are poorer.
An increased turn-over in canoes is reported
011 Lagunita.
Current excitement at the Delta Delta Delta house
concerns a certain vigorous Sophomore woman who
seems to have fickle ideas. She signed out for an
overnight, rumor has it. The slip is alleged to have
read:
Destination: unknown.
Host: none.
Date return: unknown.
This caused quite a quiver among the women, and
they immediately suspected that she had fickle ideas
about getting married with a certain suspected Paly
gent. Indeed, there is rumor that they got as far as
Salinas, and then turned back. But all evidencfc in
dicates that she was safely tucked away in the Tri-
Delt menage that night. This just goes to show that if
women aren't going to make up their minds to pursue
us and tame us for harness, that we'll have to make
up our minds ourselves.
