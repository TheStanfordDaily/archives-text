# Athletes Robbed Of Coin, Valuables During Track Meet
## 
### 
Returning to Encina gym after
competing for Stanford in Saturday
afternoon track meets, three ath
letes found themselves robbed of
their wallets, containing cash total
ing around $25 and valuable mem
bership cards.
Those victimized were: Warren
Dixon, varsity miler; Cy Oberg, var
sity sprinter; and Bill Hawkins,
frosh hurdler.
Some of the money was scholar
ship funds of men working their
way through school.
The thief gained access to the ath
letes' lockers by ferreting out keys,
hidden in shoes or crevices around
the gym.
