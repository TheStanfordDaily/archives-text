# 'Man-Hunt' Staged For Roble Jolly-up
## 
### 
"Calling all cutters-in!" is the cry
for Roble's final jolly-up of the year,
to be held in the Women's Gym
from 7 to 8 o'clock, Thursday.
Jean Reynolds. Anne Cornwell,
and Mary Jane Hill, who are in
charge of plans for the affair, say
that everything possible will be done
to get every man on the campus out
for the last freshman women's jolly-
