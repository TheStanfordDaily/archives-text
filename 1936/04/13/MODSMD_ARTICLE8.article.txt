# Pennsylvania Flood Stops Theater Work
## 
### 
Flood conditions in Pennsylvania
have been delaying construction of
Stanford's $500,000 theater.
Comptroller Almon E. Roth, in an
exclusive statement last night, ex
plained that the Pittsburgh mill of
one of the three companies under
contract to provide steel for the
theater construction has been un
der several feet of water since the
flood developed two weeks ago. The
steel will arrive at the Farm in the
first week of May, Roth reported,
i and construction will be resumed
immediately.
