# 'Y' To Give Mexican Fete Wednesday
## 
### 
The patio of Lagunita Court will
bo used for campus entertainment
for the first time at 3:30 o'clock
Wednesday, when a colorful pro
gram of Mexican folk songs and
dances is presented by the Lindeman
sisters of San Jose, sponsored hy the
advisory board of the Y.W.C.A.
Following the program, tea will
he served to the audience. Tickets
for the Mexican program will be
sold this week at the A.S.S.U. office
and at the door of Lagunita before
the performance for 75 cents.
