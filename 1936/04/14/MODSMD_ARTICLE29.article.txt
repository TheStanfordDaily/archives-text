# Peace Committee Holds Panel Monday
## 
### 
Stanford Peace Committee will
take part in the panel discussion on
"What We Are Doing for Peace"
which is to be held in Union Hall at
8 o'olock next Monday night, April
20.
Other groups taking part are the
Women's Christian Temperance
Union, the Socialist local, the Negro
Forum, the Round Table Study
Group, and the American League
against War and Fascism. The
public is invited to attend.
