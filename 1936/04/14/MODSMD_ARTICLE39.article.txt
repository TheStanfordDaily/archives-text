# Current Events Club Plans Initial Meeting
## 
### 
First meeting of the newly organ
ized Current Events Club will be
Wednesday afternoon at 4:30 o'clock
in the Y.W.C.A. room of the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Chilton R. Bush,
head of the journalism department,
will be the guest speaker and will
talk on "Why Keep Up with Cur
rent Events?"
The club will meet once a week
to discuss current topics. Plans have
been made to follow the presidential
campaign, discuss the Townsend
Plan, and to trace the significance of
the recent Naval Treaty. Pauline
Stevens is the student chairman and
in charge of the meetings.
