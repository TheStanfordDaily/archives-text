# Review Board Plans Speakers' Meetings
## 
### 
Meetings of the Speakers' Review
Board will be held today at 1 o'clock,
Wednesday at 2 o'clock, and Friday
at 2 o'clock in the Ex Committee
room of the A S.S.U. office, accord
ing to Chuck Schafer. All approved
speakers are asked to attend.
Any students interested in speak
ing are asked to communicate with
Schafer at 137 Toyon Hall.
