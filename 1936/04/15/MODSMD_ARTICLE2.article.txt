# Dinner Dance Given For Scholarship Fund
## 
### 
To increase the funds available
for student scholarships, the Stan
ford Commission for Student Aid
and the Stanford Women's Club of
San Francisco are sponsoring a
benefit dinner-dance at the Fair
mont Hotel Friday night.

Leo Carillo, stage and cinema star,
will appear as master of ceremonies
to auction off some original draw
ings of well known commercial art
ists and cartoonists of the Pacific
Coast, the proceeds of which will
go to the Student Aid Commission.
