# Yearling Debaters To Meet Cal Here
## 
### 
Tonight at 8 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse Fro.sh Debaters
Charles Corker and Tim Hays will
uphold the honor of Stanford '39
when they meet Cal in the second of
two engagements with Cal's first
year men. Corker and Hays will
argue the affirmative of the ques
tion, "Should Roosevelt Be Re
elected?"

Volcanic dust hung in the air for
four years after the eruption of Kra
kotoa, between Java and Sumatra.
More than a cubic mile of dust was
hurled into the air.
