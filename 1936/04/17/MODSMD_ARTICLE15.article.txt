# Toyon, Branner Hall Members Give Dance Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Toyon and Branner Club mem
bers will hold their first spring dance
of the quarter tomorrow night from
9 to 12 o'clock in the Toyon lobby.
Stewart Mauss and his orchestra
will furnish the music.
Patrons for the dance will include:
Mr. and Mrs. N. Bell, Mr. and Mrs.
W. COverly, Mr. and Mrs. E. C.dc
Silva, and Mr. and Mrs. B. R. Pey
ton.
Only members will be admitted
upon presentation of their club
membership cards.
