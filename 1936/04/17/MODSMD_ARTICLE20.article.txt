# NYA Work Period Ends Sunday at 5
## 
### 
Since the government payroll for
NYA work ends Sunday, under
graduate workers must finish their
30 hours this week-end. Those who
will not be able to complete their
month's work by 5 o'clock Sunday
afternoon must report to Mrs. Cov
erley sometime today.
There are 80 NYA workers who
have not yet made their reports.
Their names are posted in front of
the Administration Building today.
All undergraduates who have done
NYA work since March 21 must
turn in their time sheets at the
accounting office before 5 o'clock
Monday afternoon.
