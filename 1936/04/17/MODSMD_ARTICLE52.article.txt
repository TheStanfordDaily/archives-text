# President's Toss Opens Baseball Season
## 
### 

PRESIDENT ROOSEVELT, according to accepted custom, was on hand to open the major league
baseball season for 1936 by throwing the first ha 11, when the Washington Senators met the New
York Yankees. Left to right: Elliot Roosevelt, Mrs. James Roosevelt, the President, Manager Joe
McCarthy of New York, and Manager Bucky Harris of Washington. (A.P. Photo)
