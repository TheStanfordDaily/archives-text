# Bunn Plans Movies To Help Hoopsters
## 
### 
With a far-seeing eye to the fu
ture, Basketball Coach John Bunn
plans to make moving pictures of
the varsity men to help his team in
the finer points as well as preserving
the present style and condition of
his men in pointing out year-to
year improvement of his young var
sity.
Bunn only awaits final B.A.C. per
mission for appropriations to install
the flicker-taking operations in the
Eneina Pavilion. Meanwhile he goes
ahead with his "individual work"
spring practice workouts.
