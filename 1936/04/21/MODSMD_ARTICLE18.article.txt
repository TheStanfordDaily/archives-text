# Symphony Tickets Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Tickets go on sale today for the
last two concerts of the San Fran
cisco Symphony Orchestra, sched
uled for Friday, April 24. and Satur
day, ApiMl 25. They may be ob
i tained at the student rate of 25 cents
in the Music Department, Room 84.
Students or faculty who have or
dered tickets for the Philadelphia
Symphony Orchestra under Stokow
sky for May 1. 2, or 3 may secure
them today or tomorrow from 10 to
11 o'clock in the music room.
