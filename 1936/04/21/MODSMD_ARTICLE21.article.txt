# Theater Dedication Scheduled for June
## 
### 
Dedication ceremonies for Stan
ford's new $500,000 theater will be
scheduled for a date between June
1 and the end of the quarter, Comp
troller Almon E. Roth announced
last night.
A student committee, headed by
Wally Brooke, will arrange plans for
the event. Construction steel for the
theater, which was delayed in ship
ment by a flooded mill in Pennsyl
vania, will arrive in approximately
two weeks, Roth reported.
