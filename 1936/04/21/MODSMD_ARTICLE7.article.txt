# Students Invited To New York Conclave
## 
### 
"Forces Behind Present World
Unrest" will be the subject of an
International Student Institute to be
held in New York this summer.
The conference is being sponsored
by International House, New York,
and the National Student Federa
tion. It will be held from June 14 to
25. Foreign and American students
are invited to apply for admission,
which will be limited to 100 students,
lo Margaret R. Taylor. International
House, New York, who is chairman
of the Institute.
