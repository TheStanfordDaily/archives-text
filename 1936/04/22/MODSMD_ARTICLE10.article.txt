# Paly Players Will Give 'Craig's Wife'
## 
### 
The Pulitzer Prize play, "Craig's
Wife," will be presented by the Palo
Alto Community Players tomorrow,
Friday, and Saturday nights, with
Rosemary Hay in the title role and
Ralph Emerson Welles directing.
Mr. Craig will be played by Wil
liam B. Owens. The supporting cast
includes Clara Baldwin, Zita Narra
more, Geraldine Spinel li, Mabel
Clark, Leland Poole, Bill Youngs,
and Harry Edwards. Tickets for any
of the three performances may be
obtained at the Community Center.
