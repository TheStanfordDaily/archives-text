# English Club Shows Amateur Movies
## 
### 
Three short moving pictures, all
amateur productions and one of
them made at Stanford, will be pre
sented by the English Club at 8
o'clock tomorrow night in the Little
Theater.
The pictures were condensed from
Edgar Allen Poe's "The Fall of the
House of Usher" and "The Oval
Portrait, and Robert Louis Steven
son's "Markhcim."
"Markheim" was produced here
with A. Grove Day of the engineer
ing department playing the lead,
while "The Oval Portrait" was shot
on the Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer lot by
the photographic department of
U.S.C. Tickets will be 25 cents, pro
ceeds going to publish "The Year
book of Stanford Writing."
