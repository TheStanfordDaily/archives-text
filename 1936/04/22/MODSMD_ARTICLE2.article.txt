# War Library Shows Peace Pamphlets
## 
### 
In conjunction with observance
of Peace Day, the Hoover War
Library yesterday put on exhibit
publications by various peace or
ganizations throughout the world.
The display will continue a week.
Included arc publications of the
League of Nations Union of Great
Britain, the National Council for
Prevention of War, an American
organization and League publica
tions from Geneva. Four posters,
having as their theme the removal
of profit from war, complete the
exhibit.
