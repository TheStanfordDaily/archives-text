# Dr. J. Elmore Dies; Ill 3 Days
## Professor Took Degrees, Taught at Stanford 
### 
Jefferson Elmore, 73, professor
emeritus of classical literature, an
other of those who helped build
Stanford, died Tuesday evening after
a sudden three-day illness.
Professor Elmore received both
his A.B. and A.M. degrees at Stan
ford in 1895 and his Ph.D. degree in
1901 after studying abroad at the
universities of Berlin and Bonn. He
served at Stanford for more than 40
years.
Funeral services will be held
Friday afternoon at 2 o'clock in the
Stanford Memorial Chapel. Dr.
D. Charles Gardner will officiate.
Cremation will follow at CyjJrcss
Lawn.
Out of respect for Professor El
more, the regular meeting of the
University Philological Society will
be held Thursday, April 30, at 4:15
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse
rather than April 23.
