Senior Social Committee A: Senior
Prom and Jolly-up committee will
meet tonight in the A.S.S.U. com
mittee rooms at 7 o'clock.
Choir: Rehearsal for the "Messiah"
at 8:10 in the music room. Regu
lar choir practice at 7:15 tonight.
Knglish Club: Three moving pic
lures, "The Fall of the House of
Usher," and "The Oval Portrait,"
by Edgar Allen Poe, and "Mark
heim," by Robert Louis Steven
son, will be presented in the Little
Theater at 8 o'clock tonight. Ad
mission will be 25 cents.
Memorial Chapel: Organ recital by
Warren D. Allen at 4:15.
Stanford Dames: Bridge group will
meet today at 2 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
