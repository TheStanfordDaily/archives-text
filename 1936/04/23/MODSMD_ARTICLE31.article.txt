# $908.65 Was Too Much for Them
## 
### 




THESE FOUR CANDIDATES for queen over the Masque Ball
put up a tremendous fight only to be outvoted by Dulce "Painless"'
Parker, who amassed $908.65 for the Stanford Convalescent Home.
The runners-up were Bette "Easy" Going (upper left) with $667.79,
Ilarle "Shirley Temple" Garth (upper right) with $622.07, Corinne
"Texas" Moller (lower left) with $486,159, and Joan "Clothes Horse"
Brandel with $406.53.
