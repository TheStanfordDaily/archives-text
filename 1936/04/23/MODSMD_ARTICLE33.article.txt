# Organ Recital Today Features Fantasia
## 
### 
An organ recital will be given by
Warren D. Allen this afternoon at
4:15 o'clock in the Memorial Chapel.
The program will consist of "Fan
tasia on 'Ad nos salutarem undam'"
by Liszt, "Evening Song" and
"Sketch in D Flat" by Schumann,
Brahms' Chorale-Prelude, "A Rose
Bursts into Bloom," and Franck's
| "Piece heroique."
