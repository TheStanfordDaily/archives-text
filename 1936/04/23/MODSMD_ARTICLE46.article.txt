# Poloists To Meet S.C. Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford poloists will have their
last chance to revenge last vaca
tion's 6-5 defeat by the University
of Southern California when they
meet the Trojans at 3 o'clock tomor
row afternoon.
Leon Derby, recent transfer from
Menlo Jaysee and prodigious goal
scorer, will play No. 1 position. In
the No. 2 saddle will be Jim Fuller
ton. At No. 3 will be Neil Basmus
sen, a hard rider and valuable man
on the varsity four. Bob Fullerton,
who is an expert horseman and out
standing for his long shots, will play
in the back position.
