# DUST TROUBLES BUDGE
## 
### 
WHITE SULPHUR, W.Va., April
21 (/P). —Dust got in the eye of Don
Budge, redhaired Oakland star,
Tuesday, causing him considerable
trouble in winning his way into the
quarter finals of the sixteenth an
nual Mason and Dixon tennis tour
nament.
