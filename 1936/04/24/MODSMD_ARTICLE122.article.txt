# Indians Boast 23 Big Meet Wins, Cal 17
## 1935 Big Meet Furnished California's First Victory in Twelve Years 
### 
Big Meet history to date shows
that Stanford trackmen in the past
42 years have overshadowed the
underwear athletes from Cal, taking
23 classics to the Bears' 17. Two
were tied.
Until last year, when Brutus
Hamilton's men emerged victorious,
Dink Templcton's team had won 11
straight victories.
