# Students To Attend Harvard Celebration
## 
### 
Two Stanford undergraduates
have been included in the invitation
to attend the Harvard Tercentenary
Celebration to be held in Cambridge
September IG, 17, and 18. Suggestion
has been made by Dr. Ray Lyman
Wilbur that two students who will
be in the East at that time be Stan
ford's official representatives.
A.S.S.U. President Wally Brooke
asks that all students who will be
in the East at that time and who are
interested in attending the celebra
tion come to his office and discuss |
the matter with him.
