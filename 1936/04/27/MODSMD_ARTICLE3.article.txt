# Eccentric Kills Dentist
## 
### 



TAKING A SNAPSHOT cost Dr. Jasper Gattuccio (upper right),
31 -yeer-old San Jose dentist and amateur photographer, his
life when he stopped his ear near San Jose to take a picture of
Peter Voiss (upper left), aged eccentric, and his picturesque two
burro cart. When lie started up again, Voiss drew a shotgun and
fatally wounded Dr. Gattuccio with a charge fired through the rear
window of his automobile. (A.P. Photo)
