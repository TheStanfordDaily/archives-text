# Sacramento Editor Dies of Pneumonia
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, April 27 (JP). —
Charles K. McClatchy, editor and
owner of the McClatchy newspapers,
died here this afternoon. He was 77
years old. Pneumonia was the im
mediate cause of death.
McClatchy was born November 1, (
1858, in Sacramento and was edu- i
cated in Sacramento schools and j
Santa Clara College.
