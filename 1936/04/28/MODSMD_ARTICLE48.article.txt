# Women's Choral On Stanford Hour
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Choral
Group, under the direction of Ray
mond Kendall, sang on the Stanford
University Radio program last night
which was released over the NBC
blue network from station KGO.
Included on the program were:
"Creation Hymn" by Rachmaninoff,
Respighi's "Mysts," "Snow Legend"
by Joseph W. Clokey and a special
arrangement of the "Stanford Loy
alty Hymn" by Mr. Kendall. After
the program the group gathered for
supper at a San Francisco restau
rant.
