# Opposition to Sales Tax Voiced by P.T.A.
## 
### 
Opposition to the repeal of the
sales tax in California was the stand
taken by the executives of the Cali
fornia Congress of Parents and
Teachers, holding a board meeting
here today in preparation for the
opening of the annual convention at
San Jose tomorrow.

Action to the same effect will be
asked to go on record for the con
vention.
