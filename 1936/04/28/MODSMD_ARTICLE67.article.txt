# Catholics Sponsor Essay Contest
## 
### 
To foster interest in Catholic his
torical research, the American Cath
olic Historical Society of Philadel
phia is sponsoring an essay contest.
The subject may be "any fact, phase,
or personage of Catholic history in
the Americas."
The contest closes October 1.
Headquarters of the society are at
715 Spruce Street, Philadelphia.
