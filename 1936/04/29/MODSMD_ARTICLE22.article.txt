# Farm Woman Wins National Story Contest
## 
### 
By placing first in i national short
story contest, Ernestine Magagna,
registered from Rock Springs, Wy
oming and living in Lagunita Court
won $100 and an offer to write a
novel for the newly organized Story
Magazine Press working in con
junction with Harper Brothers.
Every college in the United States
was permitted to contribute two
stories to the contest sponsored by
Witt-Burnett Story Magazine.
Miss Magagna's story and one by
Grant Seibert were sent in by the
English Club. Miss Magagna's will
be published.
