# Jury Hears 'Relief Racket' Witnesses
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 28 </JY
A federal grand jury investigation
of an alleged "relief racket" in San
Mateo County occupied most of the
day here, with two witnesses being
heard.
A. W. Rogers, Menlo Park car
penter, and a Department of Jus
tice agent were questioned on events
during the days of the Civil Works
Administration.
