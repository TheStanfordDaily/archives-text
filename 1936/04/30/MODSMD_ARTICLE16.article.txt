# Programs Out Today For Lagunita Dance
## 
### 
Programs designed by Gilman
Gist are out today for the Lagunita
Court semi-formal to be held at the
dormitory tomorrow night from 9 to
1 o'clock.
The theme for the dance will be
Country Club, and supper will be
served outside under the umbrellas
according to Meredith McCubbin,
general chairman of the affair.
