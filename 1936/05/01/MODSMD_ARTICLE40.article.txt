# Foreign Travel Cards Offered Students
## 
### 
Students who intend to travel
abroad are offered International Stu
dent Cards, which entitle the student
to a visa, railroad, hotel, museum,
entertainment and other fee reduc
tions up to 40 per cent. It also acts
as a passport among foreign stu
dents.
A Handbook of Student Travel is
also offered. This contains informa
tion for the benefit of new student
travelers. They sell for $1 each and
may be obtained at the A.S.S.U.
office from Wally Brooke, or at the
main office at N.S.F.A. Travel Bu
reau, 8 W. 40th Street, New York
City.
Crown Princess Maria Jose of
Italy is a qualified nurse in the
Italian Army in Africa.
