# Hamilton Chosen To Lead Y.WC..A.
## 
### 
Nominating committee of the Y.
W.C.A. have chosen Pat Hamilton
as presidential candidate in elec
tion for "Y" officers to be held in all
the women's living groups Tuesday.
Poy Woodard will run for national
representative, while Jackie Blown
and Kay Bain will compete for the
post of vice-president. Maxine
Moore and Aline Goldstein will run
for secretary, and Marjorie Boobar
and Didi Vibert for treasurer.
Further nominations can be made
through petitions signed by 15 "Y"
members. These must be given to
the nominating committee.
