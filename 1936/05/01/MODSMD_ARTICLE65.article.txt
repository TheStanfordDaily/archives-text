# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
Last of the 500: Meeting at 10 o'clock
Saturday. Members must bring 25
cents for lunch which will be
served.
Orchesis: Tryouts will take place in
the dance studio of the Women's
Gym on Tuesday afternoon. Carol
Rundal or Mrs. Radir must be seen.
Graduates: Tuesday is the last day
to file applications for graduation.
San Quentin prison has a debating
team that often beats college teams. 1
