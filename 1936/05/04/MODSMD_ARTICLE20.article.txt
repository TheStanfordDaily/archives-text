# Hall Caucus To Pick Junior Nominees
## 
### 
Delegates selected by sophomores
from campus halls, eating clubs, or
downtown will meet in caucus Wed
nesday at 7:30 o'clock in Lagunita
Court to nominate candidates for
Junior Class .president, secretary
treasurer, and junior woman on Ex
Committee.
Delegates will either be appointed
by presidents of the organizations,
or elected by the sophomores them
selves. Other sophomores are in
vited to attend the caucus.
