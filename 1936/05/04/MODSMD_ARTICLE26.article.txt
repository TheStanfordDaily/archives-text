# Nagel To Play For Union Club Informal
## 
### 
With Helen Oilman in charge,
Union Club's spring informal will
be hold next Saturday evening in
the Women's Clubhouse. Palm
Beach motif is to be featured, with
Freddie Nagel's orchestra playing.
Entertainment and refreshments will
bo furnished.
The committee in charge includes,
besides Helen Gilman, chairman,
Beatrice Springer, tickets; Allene
Clark, decorations; Erma Carter, re
freshments; Helen Volberding and
Dorothy Wendes, programs;, Joseph
ine Pino, patrons and patronesses;
Janet Dole, music; and Jane Stein
loin, publicity. It will bo an open
dance, and bids can be obtained
from Beatrice Springer.
