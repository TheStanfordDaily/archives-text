# Coed Curfew Halts Tenor; Feared Gong
## 
### 
CHAPEL HILL, N.C.. May 3 (/!»).—
James Melton, tenor of opera and
screen, began the opening number
of a local concert. A bell bonged,
sounding "taps" for University of
North Carolina coeds.
Melton ceased singing momen
tarily. "I thought I had been given
the amateur gong," he said.
