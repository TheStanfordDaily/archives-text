# Van Gogh Paintings Is Lecture Topic
## 
### 
Mr. Thomas Carr Howe, Jr., as
sistant director of the California
Palace of Art, will speak on Vincent
Van Gogh and his paintings in the
Assembly Hall tomorrow night at
7:30 o'clock.
An exhibition of the work of Van
Gogh is now on display at the Palace
of the Legion of Honor in San Fran
cisco. The exhibit is estimated at
five million dollars, and is guarded
by special police detail.

Sign at library: "Only low talk
permitted here."
