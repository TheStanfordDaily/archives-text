# Councils To Discuss Code Panel Results
## 
### 
Men's and Women's Councils will
meet in joint session at 2 o'clock
this afternoon in the Ex Committee
rooms to consider the results of last
Thursday's student-faculty panel on
the Honor Code,
Main topic for discussion, accord
ing to Pat Flavel, chairman of the
women's group, will be the problem
of educating new students to Stan
ford's Code principles and "educa
tional system."
