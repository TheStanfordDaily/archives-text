# W.A.A. Field Day, Dinner Set for June
## 
### 
The annual W.A.A. banquet is
scheduled for June 2 at (5 o'clock in
the patio of the Women's Gym. Tick
ets will be 25 cents.
Preceding the banquet, the W.A.A.
will hold its annual field day from
1 to 5 o'clock. Members will partici
pate in baseball, swimming, arch
ery, golf, riding, and tennis.
