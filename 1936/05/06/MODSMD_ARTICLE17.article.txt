# Hoovers Vote Among First In Primaries
## 
### 
Former President and Mrs.
Herbert Clark Hoover were among
the first to cast their ballots in
the state presidential primary
election yesterday at Stanford pre
cinct No. 1, the Women's Club
house.
Greetings were exchanged with
election officials, all of whom
were faculty friends of the Hoo
vers. On the precinct board were
Dr. O. L. Elliot, emeritus regis
trar; Mrs. E. K. Strong, Mrs. C. G.
Vernier, and Professors A. B.
Clark and H. W. Stuart.
