# Independents, Art Class Meet in Dorm
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will turn into a
"class-room" when Professor Buck
and his independent study class
meet in the court this afternoon.
Hortense Lowe is in charge of ar
rangements.
Saturday afternoon, Dr. Craig will
hold his contemporary art class at
Lagunita. Studying the garden and
house buildings, they will inspect
the court under the supervision of a
visiting instructor.
