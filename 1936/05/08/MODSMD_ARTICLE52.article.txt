# Unemployed? Many Jobs For Summer
## 
### 
Who said unemployment?
Mrs. Coverley has jobs of all
kinds available for students right
now for summer months.
A place is open for a girl cash
ier Sundays from 12 to 7:30;
wages—dinners during the week.
Also for a girl who can speak
French fluently for a summer re
sort job.
An experienced service station
attendant is needed, and there is a
job for a bookkeeper who has had
experience and can work two to
three hours five nights a week.
An athletically inclined man is
needed for the summer, but he
must be able to teach algebra and
Latin. A job is ready for one who
can teach riding and swimming to
children and is interested in sum
muring on a dude ranch.
