# Dr. Gardner To Give Mothers' Day Talk
## 
### 
The Reverend D. Charles Gardner
will deliver "A Mothers' Day Ad
dress" in the Memorial Church
Sunday at 11 o'clock.
The musical portion of the services
will include "Ave Maria" by Bach-
Gounod, "Ave Verum" by Mozart,
and "Almighty and Everlasting God"
by Gibbons.
