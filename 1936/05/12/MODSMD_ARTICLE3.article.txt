# Stark Calls For Cardinal Candidates
## 
### 
Students interested in member
ship in Cardinals or the Rally
Committee are asked by Card
inal Chairman Dick Stark to come
to the A.S.S.U. offices from 1 to 3
o'clock today or tomorrow for in
terviews.
Stark. Phil Coghlan, chairman
of the Rally Committee, and Dotty
Sweet, chairman of the women's
committee, will be there to talk
with applicants. These are in no
way tryouts, said Coghlan last
night. They are simply interviews
to find out the capabilities and
experience of the students.
Applicants are asked to come
prepared to list offices held both
in college and their high school.
The appointments will be an
nounced Thursday morning.
