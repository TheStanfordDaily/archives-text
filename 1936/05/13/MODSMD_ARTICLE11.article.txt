# HOOVER IN NEW YORK
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 12 (/P). — 1
Former President Herbert Hoover
arrived here today from Chicago.
He came to New York to attend the j
monthly directors' meeting of the
New York Life Insurance Company.
| Tomorrow he will go to Philadel
phia, where he is to speak Thursday.
