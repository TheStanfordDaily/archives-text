# Music Records Will Be Played Today
## 
### 
Beethoven, Brahms, and Verdi
will be featured in the recording
program today at 4:15 o'clock in the
music room. "The Cariolanus Over
ture" will be played by the British
Broadcasting Company's symphony
orchestra under the direction of
Adrian Boult.
Elisabeth Schumann will sing four
Brahms songs: "Lullaby." "Verge
bliehes Staendehen." "The Night
ingale." and "The Huntsman," ac
companied by George Reeves. The
score will include Enrico Caruso.
Rosa Ponselle, Kethberg, Giannini,
and Martinelli.
