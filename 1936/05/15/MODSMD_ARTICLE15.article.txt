# Search for Escaped Convicts Continues
## 
### 
McALESTER, Okla., May 14 (/!»).—
Search for eight fleeing Oklahoma
! convicts, who held two prison guards
and a 20-year-old farmer as hos
tages, spread today toward the Red
; River on the south and the Kiamichi
Mountains on the East.
Of 24 prisoners who rioted at the
state penitentiary yesterday, only
the eight were at large—the ruthless
band which shot and killed C. D.
; Powell, prison brickyard foreman,
1 and tossed his body into a street.
