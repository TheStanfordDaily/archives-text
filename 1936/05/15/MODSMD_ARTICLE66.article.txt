# LOSES TIP, WINS SUIT
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 14 (/Pi.—For
the loss of a finger tip by a dog's
bite, Marion Martin was awarded
$375 damages. She said the injury
ruiner her screen career, which con
sisted of posing her hands for "close
ups.'*
