# Varsity Soccer Team Defeats Alumni 3-2
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity soccer players
handed the alumfti a 3-2 setback in
a match here Friday. Today the jun
iors and sophomores will* clash to
determine the intramural soccer
champion.
Carl Schaup, Chuck Pierce, and
John Curran compose a soph trio
which may give the junior a con
siderable share of stiff opposition.
Play begins at 4:15 o'clock.
