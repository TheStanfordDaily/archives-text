# Seniors To Hold Last Jolly-up Tomorrow; Nagel Will Play
## 
### 
The senior class will hold its last
jolly-up tomorrow night from 7 to
8 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Freddie Nagel's orchestra will play.
The committee in charge, which
also will make arrangements for the
Senior Prom, is headed by Will
Hinkley, and includes Jean Albert -
son, lone Collins, Dick Dawson, Bill
Drew, Virginia Johnson, Bud Les
ser, Art Lundin, Jim McCormick,
and Bob Reynolds. All members of
the Class ol '36 are invited to at
tend the jolly-up.
