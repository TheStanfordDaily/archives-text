# Tryouts Begin For Masquers' Reading
## 
### 
Masquers, women's dramatic or
ganization, will produce as a read
ing Kathleen Norris' "Victoria" on
Wednesday evening, May 27. at 8
o'clock. Tryouts, for which everyone
is eligible, will held held in the Lit
tle Theater tomorrow afternoon
from 4 to 6 o'clock. Betty Watkins
will direct the reading.
"Victoria" is of special interest at.
this time because of the production
"Victoria Regina" in New York, in
which Helen Hayes is starring. The
story opens with the youth of the
royal ruler, and various short scenes
depicting important situations in her
life arc included in Kathleen Norris'
work.
