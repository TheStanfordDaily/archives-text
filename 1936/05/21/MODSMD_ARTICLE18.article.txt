# Marx To Give Peace Speech to Baptists
## 
### 
Professor Guido Marx will speak
at the First Baptist Church Sunday
evening at 7:45 o'clock on "The
Conscientious Objector in Time of
War."
The talk is being given under the
auspices of the Roger Williams
Club. Professor Marx was one of
the speakers at the Peace Banquet
on the recent Peace Day.
