# Y.W.C.A. Deadline Extended To Noon
## 
### 
Deadline for reservations for the
Y.W.C.A. Industrial Acquaintance
tour on Friday has been extended
until noon today, according to Kay
Fitzpatrick, chairman of the tour.
Leaving at 1 o'clock, the group
will visit several San Francisco
factories, a restaurant which caters
to waterfront workers, the Com
munist bookstore, and a Labor
Council meeting.
