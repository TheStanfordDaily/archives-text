# Bradshaw Signs On As Fresno Coach
## 
### 
Jimmy Bradshaxv, for the last
eight years frosh football and bas
ketball coach at Stanford, has
signed his contract as head foot
hall coach at Fresno State Col
lege, it was revealed last night.
Appointment of a replacement
to Bradshaw's local positions
should be decided within a week
or so. Dr. Thomas Storey and
Tiny Thornhill are in charge of
what even change will be made.
