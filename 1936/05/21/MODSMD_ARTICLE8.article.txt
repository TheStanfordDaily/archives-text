# ADDITIONAL PROGRAMS MAY BE ORDERED
## 
### 
Arrangements have been made by
the senior committee for a small
number of additional programs and
announcements which may be or
dered at the A.S.S.U. office while
they last, Roy Anderson, committee
chairman, announced last night.
Seniors are requested to put their
orders in as soon as possible.
Announcements which have al
ready been ordered will be avail
able in a few days. Orders for per
sonal cards to go with the an
nouncements are also being taken
at the A.S.S.U. office, according to
Anderson, and can be filled in a
short time.
