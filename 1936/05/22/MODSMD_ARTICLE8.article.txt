# Decathlon Features Variety of Events
## 
### 
Manager Dee Myers announces
that the annual sports Decathlon is
scheduled for Tuesday, Wednesday,
and Thursday of next week. Three
events are on tap for each day, with
an odd one being sandwiched in.
The following events arc on the
decathlon program: 50-yard sprint,
50-yard swim sprint, handball
serve, soccer kick for accuracy, foot
ball punt for distance, grid sled
push, basketball free throw, stand
ing broad jump, fence climb, and
baseball throw for distance.
