# Most Popular Senior Women To Be Chosen
## 
### 
Nominations for the "most popu
lar and outstanding girl in the
Class of '26" may be made today
and tomorrow at the A.S.S.U. office.
Nominations may be made by any
member of ihe senior class, and
need only bear the names of 10 other
seniors to make them valid. Only
senior women are eligible for the
honor.
No entrance fee or charge of any
kind is made for either nominations
or for the voting, which will take
place at the end of the week. The
winner of the contest will be kept
secret until June 12, when it will be
revealed at the Senior Ball, and a
large silver loving cup will go to
the victor.
