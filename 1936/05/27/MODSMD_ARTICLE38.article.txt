# Y.W.C.A. To Install Officers at Banquet
## 
### 
Y.W.C.A. will hold its installa
tion of new officers 'at 5:15 o'clock
Thursday evening in the Memorial
Chapel. The ceremony will be fol
lowed by a dinner at 6 o'clock in the
"Y" clubhouse.
Tickets for the dinner are 40 cents
and are being sold by members of
the Y.W.C.A. cabinet, and repre
sentatives in the women's living
groups. Marjorie Boobar is in charge
of the sale.
