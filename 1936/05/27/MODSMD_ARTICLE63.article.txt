# OWENS ENTERS MEET
## 
### 
MILWAUKEE, May 25 (/P).—Mar
quette University announced Mon
day receipt of the entry of Jesse
Owens, Negro sprint star, for the
eleventh annual central intercolle
giate track and field championships
to be held at Marquette stadium
June 5.
