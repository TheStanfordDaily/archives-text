# Y.W.C.A. Installs New Cabinet Today
## 
### 
New Y.W.C.A. cabinet members
will officially take over their offices
today at 5:15 o'clock at an installa
tion ceremony in the Memorial
Chapel. A litany in which the whole
group will join, speeches by the new
and outgoing president, and songs
by the Women's Choral Club will
feature the ceremony.
Immediately following installation,
a banquet will be held in the Y.W.
C.A. rooms in the Women's Club
house, at which old and new cabinet
members, the advisory board, and
other members of the "Y" will be
present. Tickets for the banquet are
40 cents and may be purchased in
any women's living group.
