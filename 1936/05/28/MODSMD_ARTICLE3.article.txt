# Pi Lambda Theta Initiates 12 Tonight
## 
### 
Initiation of 12 members into Pi
Lambda Theta, national honorary
education society, will take place at
7:30 o'clock tonight in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Initiates include Carolyn Beck
ham, Frances Burks, Benita Calmen
son, Ada Lecke, Margaret Lazza
rone, Tamaki Nao, Catherine Peck,
Gretchen Reinemund, Margaret
Tavener, Frances Terrell, Virginia
Swanson, and Barbara Jane Young.
