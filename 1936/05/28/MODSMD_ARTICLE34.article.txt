# Off-campus Women Elect New Officers
## 
### 
At the last meeting of the Kla
hoya Club, an organization of off
campus women, the following offi
cers were elected. President,
Nancy Carmean; vice-president,
Melba Toombs; secretary-treasurer,
Gene Glasmann.
Sylvia Case, the retiring presi
dent, will be sponsor of the organ
ization next year. The club plans to
reorganize, and as its first activity
next fall will hold a tea for the
freshman women living off-campus.
