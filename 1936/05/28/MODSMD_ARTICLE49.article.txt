# Masquers To Read 'Northanger Abbey'
## 
### 
For the last Stanford Radio Hour
of the quarter, the Masquers will
give a reading of Jane Austen's
"Northanger Abbey." These pro
grams have been a weekly presenta
tion over KGO at 8 o'clock on Tues
days.
The cast for next Tuesday's broad
cast will include: Virginia Swaf
ford, Kay Comrie, Henrietta Jones,
and Dick Dawson. Assistant Pro
fessor Margqpy Bailey is supervis
ing the reading. Rosemary Benet will
read the stage directions. The script
adaptations were made by Betty
Watkins.
