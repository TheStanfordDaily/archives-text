# RUSHTON BACK
## 
### 
A1 Rushton and his orchestra—
former favoritt* of campus dancers
as the Royal Cardinals—are back
from a year's engagement in Hono
lulu, and preparing to go on a four
months' musical tour of the country.
The San Francisco board of edu
cation requires parents to pay for
damage dune to school buildings by
pupils.
