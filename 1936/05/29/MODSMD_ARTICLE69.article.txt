# FISH EAT LOCUSTS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 28 (/P). —
The Bureau of Fisheries bobbed up
today with a bripht idea for utiliz
ing the 17-year locusts now pester
ing in many parts of the East. They
are to be fed to fish.
