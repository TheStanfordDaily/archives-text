# MAJ. MILLER'S FATHER DIES
## 
### 
Juhn Franklin Miller, 73, former
U.S. Representative from Seattle and
father of Major Stewart Miller, on
duty with the R.O.T.C. here, died at
his home in Seattle yesterday.
Although Alabama farmers in
1935 matched their 1934 production
of wheat with 60,000 bushels, the
estimated value of the crop declined
' from $71,000 to $64,000.
