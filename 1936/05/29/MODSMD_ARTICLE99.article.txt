# Picnic Next Thursday Opens Senior Week
## 
### 
As the first get-together of the
Senior Week, seniors will go to
Searsville for a picnic on Thurs
day, June 11, Phil Coghlan, chair
man has announced.
Entertainment, games, athletic
and swimming events will be pro
vided. Seniors arc asked to meet
at the Union at 10 o'clock.
