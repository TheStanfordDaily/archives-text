# Union Dining Room, Cellar To Be Open
## 
### 
The Union dining room and the Cel
lar will be open summer quarter for
the convenience of students and visi
tors.
The Union dining room will be open
weekdays between 12 and 1 p.m. and
6 and 7 p.m., and Sundays from 12:15
to 1:45 p.m. and 6 to 7 p.m. Luncheons
range in price from 45 cents up and
dinners from 50 cents up. Meal tickets
arc available for use in the dining room
at a saving of 10 per cent.
The Cellar offers a fountain and cof
fee shop service, as well as candy, to
bacco, and household remedies. It will
be open during the following hours:
Monday to Thursday inclusive, 7:30
a.m. to 10:30 p.m.; Friday, Saturday,
and Sunday evenings it will be closed
at 8 o'clock.
Special parties and menus may be
arranged through the Union office by
phoning Palo Alto 3136. Miss Joyce
Porter will be in charge of dining fa
cilities during the first part of the
summer, as well as Miss Christine
Ricker.
