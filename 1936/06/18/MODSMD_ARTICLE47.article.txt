# SUMMER CHURCH SERVICES
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood will begin
his work as University Chaplain at
Memorial Church on Sunday, June 28.
Commander W. W. Edel, district chap
lain, Twelfth Naval Reserve, San Fran
cisco, will conduct services on the
first Sunday of summer quarter,
June 21.
Organ recitals at Memorial Church
will be a feature of summer quarter.
