# 700 Register Summer Quarter
## Last Summer's Enrollment Exceeded by 40 Students 
### 
Registration this morning stood at
700, the figure reached when the Reg
istrar's office closed at 5 o'clock yes
terday afternoon. This figure exceeds
by 40 the total registration at this
same time last year.

Of the total so far, there were 261
women and 439 men.

First-day registration figures to
taled 592, an increase of 31 over the
561 of the first day of registration for
summer quarter of last year. Total
registration for the quarter last year
was 719. This year's total registration
is expected to exceed that amount by a
substantial amount.
