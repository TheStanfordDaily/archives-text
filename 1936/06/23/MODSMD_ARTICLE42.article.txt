# Faculty Women Get Aquatic Instruction
## 
### 
A swimming class for faculty women
will be given at 9 o'clock on Mondays,
Wednesdays, and Fridays, according
to an announcement made by the
women's physical education depart
ment.
The women's pool is to be open at
5 p.m. every week day and at 2 p.m.
on Wednesdays and Fridays. On Sun
days the pool will be open to men
and women from 2:30 to 4:30 o'clock.
