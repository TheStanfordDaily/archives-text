# Part-time Jobs Are Available
## 
### 
Over 50 part - time positions are
available for Stanford summer quar
ter students, according to Mrs. Irene
Coverley, appointment secretary.

The positions offer a variety of em
ployment, including hotel clerk, cafe
teria cashier, service counter, gardener,
waiter, bus boy, chauffeur, and care of
children.
Compensation ranges from room rent
to board and room with additional pay,
all depending upon the nature of the
work and the time required. Students
seeking part-time employment should
register with Mrs. Coverley.
Several full-time resort positions are
available to students with experience
in soda fountain and dining room
work.
