# English Music To Be Sung By Madrigal
## 
### 
Early English music by the San
Francisco State College Madrigal Sing
ers under ihe direction of Miss Eileen
McCall will feature the program to be
given Tuesday evening at 8 o'clock in
the Assembly Hall.
Miss McCall is an authority on an
cient music and musical instruments.
Besides her work in organizing the
madrigal singers, she has imported a
number of old instruments, such as
the lute, clavichord, and recorder, and
organized groups to play them.
The madrigal singers have performed
over both the major national networks
and at many colleges and universities
on the Coast. Songs both gay and sad,
religious and secular, romantic and hu
morous, comprise their program.
