# Calling All Men!
## 
### 
To the Stanford Rough on leap
year:
Notice is hereby given that we,
the Women of Union Dorm, be
ing of sane mind, and granting
you a chance to find out for
yourselves, invite all men students
to dance at a Jolly Up at the
Women's Clubhouse on Monday,
June 29, between the hours of
seven and eight o'clock.
