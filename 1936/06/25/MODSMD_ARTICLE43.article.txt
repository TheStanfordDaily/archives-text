# School of Education To Be Entertained
## 
### 
Faculty and students of the School
of Education with their wives and
husbands are invited to an informal
party at the home of Dean and Mrs.
Kefauver, 457 Kingsley, Palo Alto, to
morrow evening at 8 o'clock. The ac
tivities of the evening will include
dancing, outdoor and indoor games.
Cotton and tobacco are the leading
British imports from the United States.
