# Noted Scholar Will Give Courses in '37
## 
### 
Dr. Ferdinand Baldensperger, schol
ar of international reputation and au
thor of numerous outstanding books
and articles has been appointed acting
professor of Romanic Languages at
Stanford for the summer quarter of
1937.
Dr. Baldensperger is professor of
Comparative Literature at Harvard,
and formerly held that position at the
Sorbonne, Paris. He taught at Stanford
during the summer of 1928.
