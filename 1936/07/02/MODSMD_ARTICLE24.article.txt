# Bob Gros and Evelyn Weyher To Marry
## 
### 
St. Mark's Episcopal Cathedral, Salt
Lake City, will be the scene of the
marriage of Evelyn Weyher and Rob
ert Richard Gros on July 25. The
bride-to-be graduated from Rowland
Hall and came to Stanford last year
as a junior. She is a member of Delta
Gamma.

Mr. Gros received his degree from
Stanford in 1935. While in the Uni
versity his career was varied. As de
bate manager Gros engineered nation
wide and Canadian tours as well as
national broadcasts. He worked on the
Quad, was a member of Delta Sigma
Rho, Los Arcos, and Phi Beta Kappa.
