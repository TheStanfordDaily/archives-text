# PI LAMBDA THETA
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, national women's
sorority, were "at home" to all women
interested in education on June 25
from 3 to 6 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. Summer activities were
discussed.
At a business meeting held recently
Virginia Lee Block was elected presi
dent; Elizabeth Jones, vice-president;
and Carol Jane Thornton, secretary.
