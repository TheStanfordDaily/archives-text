# Symphony Orchestra Of S.F. To Play July 19
## 
### 
The Woodland Theater will be the
scene, July 19, of the opening of the
Philharmonic concerts given by the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra.
The initial event will introduce Er
nest Ansermet as conductor. He has
directed grand opera, ballet perform
ances, and symphonic programs.
Assisting him will be Naoum Blin
der, concert master, and Lajos Fen
ster.
