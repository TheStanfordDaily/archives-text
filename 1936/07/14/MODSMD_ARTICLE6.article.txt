# Lundberg Will Speak Thursday
## 
### 
Dr. George Lundberg, of Benning
ton College, will give his criticisms,
comments, and opinions on public edu
cation in an address at Thursday's Ed
ucation luncheon. Dr. Lundberg is
acting professor of sociology on the
summer Stanford faculty.
Music will be provided for the en
tertainment of the luncheon guests by
Donald Roderick, who plays the gui
tar, and Lovell Langstroth, playing the
accordion.
Tickets are available at the Edu
cation office for the luncheon, which
takes place at the Union, starting at
12:10.
