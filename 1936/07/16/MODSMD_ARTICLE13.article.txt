# 'Kangaroo Land' Is To Be Lecture Topic
## 
### 
"Kangaroo Land" is the subject
chosen by Helena Whitney Cass for
her talk Friday afternoon, July 17, at
:):30 o'clock in the Little Theater of
the California Palace of the Legion of
Honor, which is situated in Lincoln
Park, San Francisco.
There will be no admission charge
for this special illustrated lecture,
which is being sponsored by the Consul
General of Great Britain.
