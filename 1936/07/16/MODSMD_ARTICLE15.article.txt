# Dr. Lundberg To Talk On Public Schools
## 
### 
The public schools of today will be
discussed and criticized by Dr. George
Lundberg of Bennington College at the
education luncheon today in the al
cove of the Union Dining Room. Dr.
Lundberg is acting professor of so
ciology on the summer Stanford fac
ulty.
According to Carl Manner, who is in
charge of entertainment, musical num
bers will be offered by Donald Rode
rick, who plays the guitar, and Lovell
Langstroth, playing the accordion.
Tickets are available at the Educa
tion Office for the luncheon, which
takes place at 12:10 o'clock.
