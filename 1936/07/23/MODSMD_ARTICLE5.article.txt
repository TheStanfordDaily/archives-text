# Dr. Temperley Is Luncheon Speaker
## 
### 
Members of the School of Education
will be addressed by Dr. Harold W. V.
Temperley today at their regular
Thursday luncheon in the alcove of
the Union Dining Hall.
Myrra Mosher, 11-year-old violinist,
will play several selections. Miss
Mosher is from San Jose and has won
the state violin contest for five con
secutive years.
