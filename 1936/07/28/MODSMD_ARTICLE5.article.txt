# VESPERS' HELD SUNDAY
## 
### 
Vesper services were held in the Me
morial Church Sunday as the second
of a series covering music and litera
ture of the eighteenth, nineteenth, and
twentieth centuries.
The twentieth century will be the
theme of the services next Sunday
when Warren D. Allen, organist, and
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, chaplain, will
officiate.
