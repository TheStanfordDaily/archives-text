# Garabrant To Be Organist Thursday
## 
### 
Mr. Maurice Garabrant, a visiting
organist, will play at the organ recital
next Thursday at 4:15 o'clock in the
Memorial Church. Mr. Garabrant is
the regular organist at the Cathedral
of the Incarnation, Garden City, Long
Island.
During the past week, he has been
attending various classes on the cam
pus. His comments about Stanford and
California are very enthusiastic.
