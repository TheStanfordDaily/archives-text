# Max Steineke Is Home from Arabia
## 
### 
Max Steineke, a graduate from
Stanford in 1921, arrived at his home
in Los Altos last Saturday after hav
ing been in Arabia for two years.
He traveled by airplane from that
country to London, where he took the
liner Bremen for New York, and at
that point he again boarded an air
ship for ihe remainder of his journey
to San Francisco. Mr. Steineke will be
at home for a several months' vaca
tion.
