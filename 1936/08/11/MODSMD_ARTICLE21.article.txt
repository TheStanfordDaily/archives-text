# Recital Takes Place Of Vesper Services
## 
### 
An organ recital given by Warren D.
Allen, University organist, took the
place of the usual Sunday evening ves
per service Sunday in the Memorial
Church.
The program was as follows:
f Sonata No. 1 in D, Opus 42..Guilmant
j "Traumerei" Richard Strauss
Andante Cantabile from Quartet,
Opus 11 Tschaikowsky
Cantilena Carl McKinley
"Lied des Chrysanthemes" and "Va
riations de Concert". .Joseph Bonnet
