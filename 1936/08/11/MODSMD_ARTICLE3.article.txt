# Egypt Will Be Discussed at Hansen Lecture
## 
### 
"Egypt 111 Caesar's Day" will be Dr.
Hazel D. Hansen's subject when she
presents the final Tudor lecture to
morrow evening at 8 o'clock in the
Little Theater. Dr. Hansen, who is
associate professor of classics, will il
lustrate her talk with slides.
Tomorrow night's lecture will com
plete the series of Tudor lectures which
have been given this summer to pro
vide a background for the production
of Shakespeare's "Antony and Cleo
patra" and Shaw's "Caesar and Cleo
patra."
