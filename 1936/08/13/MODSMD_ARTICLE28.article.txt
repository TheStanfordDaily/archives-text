# CLASSROOM ROMANCE
## 
### 
Miss Mabel Helen Overton became
the bride of a Stanford classmate,
Ralph Evans Cotter, Jr., Saturday at
high noon. The ceremony took place
at the Piedmont home of her parents,
Major and Mrs. Winfield Scott Over
ton.
Mr. and Mrs. Cotter will live in
Oakland on their return from a wed
ding trip. The bride is a member of
Delta Gamma sorority and her hus
band of Alpha Tau Omega fraternity.
Both received their degrees from Stan
ford in 1935.
A survey taken by the University of
Chicago shows that there are 100 fe
males to every 98 males in urban cen
ters, and 108 males to every 100 fe
males in rural sections.
