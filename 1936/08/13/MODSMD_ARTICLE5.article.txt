# ENGAGEMENTS ANNOUNCED
## 
### 
The engagement of Charles Hood,
Stanford class of '35, to Miss Mary
Frances Pickel was announced today.
No date has been set for the wedding.
Miss Pickel, a graduate of the Sacred
Heart Academy in St. Louis, is at pres
ent visiting her sister in San Francisco.
Mr. Hood is a member of Alpha Sig
ma Phi and belongs to Hammer and
Coffin. He is from Elko, Nevada.
