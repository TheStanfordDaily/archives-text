# KELLY-GIBBS NUPTIALS
## 
### 
Friends here are learning with inter
est that Miss Esther Myriek Kelley,
Stanford '33, is to become the bride of
Allison J. Gibbs, young Manila at
torney, in November.
Miss Kelley, a daughter of Col. and
Mrs. R. H. Kelley of Fort William Mc-
Kinley, P.1., belongs to Delta Gamma
sorority.
