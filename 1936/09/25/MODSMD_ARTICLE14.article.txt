# Durand Chosen Power Head
## 
### 
Dr. William F. Durand, professor
emeritus of mechanical engineering,
has been elected president of the
Third World Power Conference,
which convened recently in Wash
ington, D.C. He succeeds Dr. Julius
Dorpmueller of Germany, head of
the Reich's railroads, to the posi
tion heading this organization of
3,000 scientists and industrialists
from 52 nations.
Dr. Durand is editor-in-chief of
an outstanding work on aerody
namic theory, the last volume of
which is now being published in
Germany. He is chairman of the
Special Airship Committee appoint
ed by the Secretary of the Navy to
study construction of lighter-than
air craft.
