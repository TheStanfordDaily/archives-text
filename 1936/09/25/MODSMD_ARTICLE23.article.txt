# CHAPPIE NEEDS HELP
## 
### 
Freshmen and sophomores may
try out for positions on the Chap
arral business stall sometime dur
ing the first week of school, says
Rae Simonson, manager. Workers
on Chappie receive 10 per cent cut
on ads they sell, and have a good
chance to work up to a salaried
position.
