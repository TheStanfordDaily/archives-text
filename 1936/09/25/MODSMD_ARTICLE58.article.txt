# More Parking Room Provided on Serra
## 
### 
Additional parking fox- cars of
students bound for classes in the
English building will be provided by
construction work done on Serra
street this summer.
The portion of Serra that ran in
front of the New Theater was wid
ened out to provide diagonal park
ing, and a sidewalk is being put in
on the theater side. Comptroller
Roth expects the work to be done
in time for Registration Day.
