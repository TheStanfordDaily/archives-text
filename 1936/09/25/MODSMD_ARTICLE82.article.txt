# Six Stanford Men Aid Holding of Olympics
## 
### 
Stanford's official contingent to
the Eleventh Olympiad in Berlin
numbered A1 Masters, member of
the American Olympics Commit
tee; "Slinger" Dunn, second place
winner in the discus; Harry Wolter,
coach of the baseball team; Fred
Heringer and Gordy Mallatratt,
baseball players, and John Lyman,
attache for the American team at
the Olympic village.
