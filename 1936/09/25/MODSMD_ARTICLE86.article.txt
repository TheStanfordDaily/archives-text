# Priddle Appointed Boxing Instructor
## 
### 
John Priddle of Syracuse, here
doing graduate work in physical
education, was announced as boxing
instructor by Director of Minor
Sports Harry Maloney to succeed
Myron Schall.
Schall is on a leave of absence at
the present time, and his return to
active duty at Stanford is still un
certain.
