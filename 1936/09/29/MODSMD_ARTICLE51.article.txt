# EXPLOSION KILLS TWO
## 
### 
FLINT, MICH., Sept. 2,S (/p). At
least two persons were killed and
ten injured so seriously they were
j taken to hospitals by an explosion
today which demolished the General
Tire Company's service station in
downtown Flint.
