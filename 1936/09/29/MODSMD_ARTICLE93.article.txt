# Game Pictures To Be Screened
## 
### 
Motion pictures of Stanford's
defeat at the hands of Santa
Clara will he screened in the
Little Theater at 8 o'clock to
night as a preliminary to the
Reg Dance, slated for 9 o'clock.
Filming of Saturday's game
will be the lirst in the fall series
of football pictures to be shown
on the campus. Present plans
call for screening every Stan
ford game after the actual grid
play.
