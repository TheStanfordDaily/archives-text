# Clark Granted Keeble Award
## Left End Wins Pen For Outstanding Playing 
### 
Jack Clark, rugged left end, was
named winner of the Keeble foun
tain pen award for his play as the
outstanding Stanford man in the
Santa Clara game Saturday.
This new outstanding player
award, announced yesterday, con
sists of a Lifetime pen, with the
player's name engraved thereon, and
will be given each week to the out
standing Stanford team member.
Decision each week will be made
by a poll of sports writers, the group
to be selected from those covering
the games by Don Liebendorfer,
B.A.C. publicity monger. One man is
eligible any number of weeks.
Clark, go down to Keeble's and get
your pen.
