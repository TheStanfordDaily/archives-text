# FEW TICKETS SOLD
## 
### 
Only 1020 rooters' tickets for
the U.S.C. game had been sold
at 4 o'clock yesterday, accord
ing to Don Liebendorfer, pub
licity director for the Board of
Athletic Control. Although
plenty of tickets remain for
sale today, positively none will
be sold after 5 o'clock this af
ternoon.
