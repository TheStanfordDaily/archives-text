# Stadium Jobs Open For Police, Ushers
## 
### 
Jobs are still open for students
who desire work at the stadium on
game days, according to Jake Irwin,
manager of operations at the B.A.C.
Positions open include policing, ush
ering, ticket - selling, and others.
Those interested must sign up at
the B.A.C. this week. Card notices
will later be sent out to those ac
cepted. Those who signed up be
fore registration must also sign up
again, according to Irwin, as their
previous applications are no longer
usable.
