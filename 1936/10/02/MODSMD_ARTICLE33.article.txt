# Dramatic Council Convenes Today
## 
### 
Dramatic Council will hold its first
meeting of the quarter this afternoon
at 4 o'clock in the committee room
of the Old Union.
Business under discussion will in
clude the setting of dates for the
year's productions, selection of the
first play, and the prices for season
tickets. Electrician and head usher
will also be chosen at this time.
