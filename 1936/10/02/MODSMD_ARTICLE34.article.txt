# ENTERTAIN AT TEA
## 
### 
Marking the group's first social
meeting of the fall quarter, Dean
and Mrs. Grayson N. Kefauver will
fete School of Education students
and faculty at a tea to be given from
3 to 5 p.m. Sunday, October 4, at
their home, 457 Kingsley Avenue,
Palo Alto.

Daily staff tryouts begin Monday;
report to the Press building, Jour
nalism library, at 5 o'clock.
