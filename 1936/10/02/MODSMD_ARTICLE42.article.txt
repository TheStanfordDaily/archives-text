# Three Roles In 'Front Page' Already Filled
## 
### 
Of the five wnmen and 17 men
required for the Sword and Sandais
reading of "The Front Page," to be
presented in the Little Theater
Thursday night, three parts will be
filled by Phil Brown, Jim Corner,
and Winstead Weaver.
According to Director Fred Clark,
many people have appeared for the
tryouts, and the remaining members
of the cast will be announced today.
Clark will have Press Ellington as
his associate director.
The reading will be presented in
costume, breaking away from the

traditional formal attire of those tak
ing part in the reading.
