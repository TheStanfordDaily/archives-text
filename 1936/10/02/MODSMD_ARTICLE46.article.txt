# Masquers' Annual Play Contest Opens
## 
### 
Masquers' annual original play
contest was announced yesterday by
Henrietta Jones, publicity chair
man. The contest is open to Stan
ford students and residents of Paly.
The plays may be of any length and
on any subject. They will be judged
by members of the faculty and Mas
quers and the winning play will be
read in the Little Theater.
New officers of Masquers, recently
clected, are Kay Arthur, president;
Kay Campbell, secretary-treasurer;
and Henrietta Jones, publicity chair
man.
