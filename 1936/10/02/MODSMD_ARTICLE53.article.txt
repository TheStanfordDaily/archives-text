# Judge Sentences 'Buddy' To Prison
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 1 (A I ).—ln a
dramatic scene in Superior Court
today, Judge Frank Smith sentenced
Lester H. Brockaway, with whom he
shouldered arms in the same com
pany during the World War, to serve
from 1 to 14 years in San Quentin
prison.
