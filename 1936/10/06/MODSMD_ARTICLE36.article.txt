# Claude Petty Joins Fresno State Stall
## 
### 
Claude Petty, graduate of Stanford
University, has joined the Fresno
State College faculty to replace
Paul V. Sheehan as instructor in I
journalism and commerce.
Petty, having received both the
Bachelor of Arts and Master of Arts
degrees at Stanford University, has
also served as assistant copy editor
of the San Francisco Chronicle and
later taught journalism courses at
University High School in Berkeley.
