# GRID ENTRIES CLOSE
## 
### 
Sign-ups for touch football will
close Wednesday afternoon, an
nounces Phil Klauber, manager.
Nearly 40 teams are on the rosters
already, easily enough for a season
of league play. Play is scheduled to
start next Monday afternoon, with
four games on most afternoons.
Referees for the season have been
selected Jim Mullen, Woody Adams,
and John Partridge.
