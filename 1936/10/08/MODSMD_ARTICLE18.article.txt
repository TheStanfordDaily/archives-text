# Try outs Hold Third Training Session Today
## 
### 
Third session of Daily tryoutee
training period will be held today at
5 o'clock, in Room 8 of the Press
Building. Subject will be news writ
ing and galley proof reading.
Mx*. Berton J. Ballard, instructor in
Journalism, will address the group
on essentials of a news story. Write
ups of the assembly, to be held this
morning at 11 o'clock, will be turned
in by each tryoutee as a sample of
his news writing ability. They will
later be graded and returned to the
group.
