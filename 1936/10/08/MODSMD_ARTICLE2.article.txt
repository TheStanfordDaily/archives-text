# Events Class Will Enroll Today
## 
### 
Farmsters still looking for another
unit to round out this quarter's
schedules will fill Room 214 at 1:15
o'clock this afternoon to enroll in
the social science department's new
one-unit plus-minus course in "Cur
rent Events."
Philip W. Buck, assistant professor
of political science, will open the
weekly meetings with his talk today
on the "Strategy of the Current
Presidential Campaign."
All students may enter the class by
signing blue cards at the meeting,
signing a list at the Registrar's of
fice. "C" credit depends upon regu
lar attendance and one short term
paper.
