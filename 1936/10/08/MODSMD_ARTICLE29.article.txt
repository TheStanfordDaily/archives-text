# B.A.C. Meets To Discuss Finances
## 
### 
First meeting of the B.A.C. board
this year is scheduled for tonight in
the B.A.C. rooms. Routine matters
will be discussed by the members,
with appropriations, budgets, and
other financial matters commanding
main attention.
Student members on the board are
.lim Reynolds. Frank Alustiza, and
Woody Adams, the latter selected
at the A.S.S.U. election last spring
