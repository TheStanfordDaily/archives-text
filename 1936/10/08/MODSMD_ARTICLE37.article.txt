# Tuesday Is Final Day To Change Study Cards
## 
### 
Tuesday, October 13, is the last
day students may change this quar
ter's study lists without fine and all
revised study-list cards should be
filed at the information window at
the Registrar's office by 5 o'clock on
that day.
After October 13 all program revi
sions must be made by petition to the
Registration Committee, subject, to
the following fines: $2 for each
cOui'ae added or dropped, or $3 where
one course was substituted for an
other.
Students are held responsible for
courses and units as they appear on
their official study lists filed at the
time of registration, and no credit is
given for courses which do not ap
pear thereon. This applies to phys
ical education as well as academic
subjects.
