# L.A. Racer Escapes Injury on Track
## 
### 
| WESTBURY, N.Y., Oct. 7 (£>).—
Rex Mays, young Los Angeles driver
narrowly escaped serious injury to
day when the Miller special he was
driving over the intertwining Roose
velt raceway crashed into the con
crete retaining wall at the east end
of the main stretch and was wrecked.
He came down the straightaway at
120 miles an hour and lost control
rounding the second turn. With Ihe
car out of the October 12 race, Mays
will drive relief for Babe Stapp.
The entire personnel of a Los An
geles campus orchestra were made to
; appear in court recently for posting
i bills freely about the campus. Irate
1 home owners called it a nuisance.
