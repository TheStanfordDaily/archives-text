# International Club To Meet Today
## 
### 
The World Affairs Group will hold
its first fall meeting tomorrow eve
ning at the home of Mrs. David
Starr Jordan, 330 Serra Road, at
7:45 o'clock. Professor Carl Lucas
Alsberg has been announced as the
speaker of the evening to comment
on the Pacific Relations Institute
held in Yosemite this summer.
Round table discussion following
the lecture will be led by Dr. Jerome
B. Thomas, chairman of the group.
Meeting the first Thursday of each
month, membership on each evening
is limited to fifty persons. Reserva
tions may be secured through the
secretary Mrs. Elliot Diller by call
ing 29581.
