# Band To Present Game Broadcast
## 
### 
Inaugurating a new feature for
this season's football battles, the
Stanford Band, in conjunction with
the rooting section, will present a
series of five-minute broadcasts to
be played between halves of each
home grid game.
The concert, to be preceded by a
talk which will describe the Band's
uniforms and give the names of the
directors and drum major, will take
place exactly five minutes after the
gun sounds for the end of the first
half.
This Saturday's program follows;
"Come Join the Band"—Band and
rooters.
"Spirit of the Nation"—Band.
"Cardinal Is Waving" — Band and
rooters.
"Sons of the Siacford Red"—Band
and rooters.
