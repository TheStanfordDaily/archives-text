# LOYALISTS CAPTURED
## 
### 
GIBRALTAR. Oct. 8 (U.R).—Four
armed rebel trawlers today cap
tured a govei'ninent trawler which
periodically for two weeks had
shelled I.a Linca. near this British
colony. The loyalist crew of 211 sur
rendered and were ordered shot in
Algeciray.. rebel seaport, tonight.
