# AMATEURS BOXERS TIE
##

NEW YORK. Oct. 7 <>P>. — The
United States and Europe were all
square with two victories each at
the end of the first half of the two
day international amateur boxing
tournament in the Ntw York Hippo
drome Wednesday night.
