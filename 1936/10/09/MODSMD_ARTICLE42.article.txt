# 'Cellar' Boasts New Enlarged Counter
## 
### 
Customers at the Cellar cigarette
counter yesterday were surprised to
see the new architectural change.
Six feet of plate glass were added to
the counter, which is now "L"-
shaped.
A new and fuller magazine section
has been installed behind the coun
ter. Also women's cosmetics have
been added to the Cellar stock.
