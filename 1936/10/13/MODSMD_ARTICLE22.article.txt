# Rushing Cards On Sale Today In the Union
## Rushing To Begin Friday; Freshmen May Accept Only 2 Dates at House 
### 
Cards for the first rushing period
will continue on sale today in the
A.S.S.U. office, according to Bob
Hoover, president of the Interfrater
nity Council. Twenty-seven hundred
cards are available and each house
may purchase as many as it wishes.
Rushing will begin Friday at 11:30
o'clock and will continue until mid
night, three dates being available—
11:30 a.m. to 2 p.m.; 2 to 5 p.m.; and
6 p.m. to midnight. On Saturday
only two dates are possible from
11:30 a.m. to 2 p.m. and 2 to 6 p.m.
A freshman may accept only two
dates with any one house during any
week-end period.
The week-end of the U.S.C. game
rushing will end at 8 p.m. Friday and
no rushing will take place on Satur
day.
