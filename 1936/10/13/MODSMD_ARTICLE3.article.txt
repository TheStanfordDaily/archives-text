# Engineers To Hold Social Meeting
## 
### 
A social meeting of the student
chapter of American Society of Civil
Engineers will be held tomorrow

of Professor Eugene V. Ward, 627
Salvatierra. Short talks will be giv

professors on engineering aspects of

Special invitation is extended to
civil engineering students not as yet
members of the chapter. Attendance

membership.
