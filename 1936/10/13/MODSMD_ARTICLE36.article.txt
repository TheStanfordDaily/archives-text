# Savelle To Discuss Spanish Rebellion
## 
### 
Professor Maxwell H. Savelle will
talk on "The Spanish Rebellion from
the Frontier" at 730 o'clock this eve
ning in the Assembly Hall. This lec
ture is presented by the Committee
on Public Exercises as one of the
regular Tuesday series.
Professor Savelle, who spent some
lime in Spain this summer, will give
a general historical background,
showing how the conflict first began.
He will then tell how it progressed
from a struggle of Democracy against
Privilege to one of Democracy ver
sus Fascism.
