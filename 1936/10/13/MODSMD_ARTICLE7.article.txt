# Applications Due For Fellowships
## 
### 
Applications must be made by De
cember 15 for graduate and research
fellowships to be granted by the
American Association of University
Women, it was announced today.
The fellowships are awarded in
general to women who have com
pleted two years of residence work
for the Ph.D. degree or who have al
ready received the degree. The fel
lowships are open to American
women for study in the United States
or abroad, while one is open to a
Latin American woman for study in
the United States.
Information may be obtained by
writing the secretary, Committee on
Fellowship Awards, American Asso
ciation of University Women, 1634
I Street, N.W., Washington, D.C.
