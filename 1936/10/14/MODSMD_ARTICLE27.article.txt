# Two Days Left To Buy Big Game Tickets
## 
### 
Only two days remain for rooters
to secure their tickets for the Cal-
Stanford Big Game, November 21, as
the second day of sales ended yester
day afternoon. Price of the tic kets is
$1.65, while four additional reserved
seat tickets may be obtained for the
regular price of $4.40 each.
According to word received from
the B.A.C. offices, 1283 tickets had
been sold at 5 o'clock last night, with
a much larger sale expected in the
next two days.
The booth will be open from 9
o'clock until 5 o'clock up to Thurs
day afternoon, when sale of rooters'
tickets will definitely be discon
tinued.
