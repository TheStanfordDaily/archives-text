# Socialists Schedule Rally for Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford supporters of the cam
paign for Norman Thomas and
George W. Nelson, Socialist national
candidates in 1936, will hold an or
ganizing rally tonight at 7:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse. Bill
Millis will speak on "Youth Faces
the Elections" at the meeting, which
has been called by the Stanford
Committee for Thomas and Nelson.
Membership in the committee is
open to all students and faculty at
Stanford interested in backing the
Socialist presidential campaign. The
local group is affiliated with the na
tionwide Youth Committee for
Thomas and Nelson, headed by Al
bert W. Hamilton, of Chicago.
