# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
Education Get-Together: Phi Delia
Kappa and Phi Lambda Theta,
honorary education fraternities,
are sponsoring a get-together for
all education students, to be held
in the Women's Clubhouse at 8
o'clock Thursday evening.
Cardinal M-ecting: Cardinals will
meet tonight at 7:15 o'clock in the
A.S.S.U. offices to discuss rings and
and committee reports.
History C'luh Meets: The History
Club will meet tomorrow in the
Women's Club-house at 1:30
o'clock. Professor Max Savelle will
discuss the Spanish Revolution.
Recordings: Students are invited to
attend a program of recorded mu
sic this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock
in Room 84.

With an anticipated increase in
enrollment over last year, housing
facilities for both men and women
are being taxed to the limit.
