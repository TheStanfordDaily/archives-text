# Toyon, Brainier Informal Friday
## 
### 
Toyon - Branner's joint informal
this Friday night will offer unusual
intermission entertainment in the
form of an accordion-guitar duet,
which will play in the lobby.
Patrons and patronesses for the
evening will be: Mr. and Mrs. Ernest
Giesecke, Mr. and Mrs. Stanley L.
Sharp, and Mr. and Mrs. Ordille C.
Harrold.
