# Theta Sigma Phis Reveal Fall Plans
## 
### 
Members of Theta Sigma Phi,
women's national journalism frater
nity, planned a program of activities
for autumn quarter, at their first
meeting Tuesday night at the Delta
Gamma house.
Monthly parties, meetings at which
men and women prominent in jour
nalism will speak, and tentative joint
meetings with Sigma Delta Chi,
men's national journalism fraternity,
are among the events listed for the
quarter. Annalee Whitmore, presi
dent of the organization, was in
charge of the meeting.
The society will hold a dinner in
San Francisco Sunday night, Octo
ber 18, to discuss initiation of new
members. Following the dinner, the
group will attend the current pro
duction of George White's Scandals.
