# All Stanford Men Are Invited To Roble Jolly-up
## 
### 
Roble women will entertain the
entire male student body at a jolly
up scheduled for tonight from 7 to
8 in the Women's Gym.
Those present will dance to the
music of George Haller's eleven
piece, all campus orchestra, which
features as vocalist Yvonne Beatty.
Webb Jones as master of ceremonies
will keep things going.
