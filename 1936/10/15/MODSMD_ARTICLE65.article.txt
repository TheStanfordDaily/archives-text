# BALL CLUB IN COURT
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Oct. 14 (/P»—A Superior
Court fight for control of the Seattle
Baseball Club loomed today after
George F. Vanderveer, a major
stockholder, brought legal action
against President William H. Klep
per. Vanderveer sought to obtain
stock which he said was assigned to
him by W. Vern Klepper, son of
the club president.
