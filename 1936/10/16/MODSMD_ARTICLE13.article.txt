# PEACE MEET REFUSED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 14 (/P). —
John L. Lewis, leader of the rebel
unions, today refused to consider
any peace meeting with the Ameri
can Federation of Labor until the
attitude of the federation on pro
posals to revoke its suspension of the
10 Committee for Industrial Organi
zation unions is clarified.
