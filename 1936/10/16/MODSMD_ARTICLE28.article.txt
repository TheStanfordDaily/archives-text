# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
RALLY: Interviews for the Frosh
Tryoutee Committee will be held this
afternoon from 2:30 to 4:30 in the
A.S.S.U. office in the Old Union.
NYA: Mrs. Coverley wishes all
undergraduates on NY A to come to
her office and inform her of the
number of hours they can work this
month before October 24 at 5 o'clock.
HALL DANCE: Toyon and Bran
ner hold their first joint informal of
the year tonight in the Toyon lobby.
Charlie Horton's band will play.
FOOTBALL: Frosh footballers
play the Greys this afternoon, as the
first-year men try out a series of
new plays which they liope will
bring them a victory over the
U.C.L.A. frosh next week.
FENCING: The fencing varsity
opens its season tonight at 8 o'clock
against the Funke's Academy team
in Encina Gym.
RUSHING: For men starts today,
as 800 new men trod the paths lead
ing to membership in the Row.
