# Sailing Club Opens Activities Tonight
## 
### 
To open fall activities of the Stan
ford Sailing Club, which was organ
ized last spring, Farm skippers will
meet tonight in Room 200C!, at 7:30
o'clock.
Plans are under way for some of
the members to serve as crew for
prominent San Francisco yachtsmen
some time this season. At tonight's
meeting, an election of officers will
be held. All those interested in
yachting are invited to attend.
