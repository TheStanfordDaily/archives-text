# Democrats To Hold Jolly-up Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford Young Democrats will
caper free of chargc to the music of
Hal Barnes' orchestra from 7 to 8
Wednesday evening, but Republic
ans and "those who have so far de
clined to join" the group must pay
10 cents.
The jolly-up, to be held in old
Roble gym, is backed by the 42 mem
bers of the newly organized club
and the 25 cents of each. The men's
and women's Glee Clubs and a "cer
tain campus funny man" are being
contracted to furnish entertainment.
