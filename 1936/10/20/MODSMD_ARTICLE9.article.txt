# SHIP FIRE KILLS FOUR
## 
### 
PALERMO, Sicily, Oct. 19 (UP).—
Four Italian members of the crcw of
the trans-Atlantic liner Vulcania
were killed during a fire which
broke out in third-class cabin quar
ters today.
