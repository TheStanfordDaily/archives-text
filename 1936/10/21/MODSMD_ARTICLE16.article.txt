# Theta Sigma Phi To Fete Initiates
## 
### 
Invitations to join Theta Siama
Phi, women's national journalism
fraternity, have been sent to campus
women prominent in journalism.
The fraternity will Rive a dinner in
San Francisco tomorrow night at
which initiation plans will be dis
cussed. After the dinner, the group
will attend "George White's Sean
dais." The list ot pledges will be
announced later
