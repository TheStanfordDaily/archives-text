# Distance Runners Plan Jaysee Meet
## 
### 
Although hard hit by the loss of
Ed Burrows and Warren Dixon, the
Cardinal cross-country squad is
training ardently for their meet with
San Mateo J.C. here November 6,
and will offer Bob Alexander as their
chief threat. About 10 men will be
entered in the three-mile run.
The date for the Cal meet at Ber
keley is set for a week or two before
the Big Game.
