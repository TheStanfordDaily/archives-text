# Colors To Feature Fall Handkerchiefs
## 
### 
Lovely linen handkerchiefs to
tuck in suit pockets and to go places
this fall are highly colorful. The most
promising of the new shades are
brown, navy, wine, luggage tan,
chaudron and royal. Three and four
color prints in such combinations as
green, yellow, and royal . . . gray,
black, and orange . . , and effective
combinations ot wine and purple
tones are favored for sportswear.
Large size solid color and printed
'kerchiefs serve a double purpose,
Besides being decidedly smart as
handkerchiefs, they may be worn
neckerchief fashion atop sweaters.
Latest thing for your room: Elec
tric clock, radio with illuminating
unit, and writing set all in one.
