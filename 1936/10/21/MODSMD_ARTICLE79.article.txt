# Fur Capes Return To Top Fall Frocks
## 
### 
Smart women stand up and give
three rousing good cheers for the re
vival of fur capes! Their return en
gagement is scheduled for a long run,
because, without a doubt, they are
the ideal wraps to top the smart Fall
frocks. The new capes are all han
dled so cleverly and cut so well, that
they do worlds of good in effecting
a smart and decidedly slenderizing
appearance.
Shoulder length capes are the
thing for wool or silk frocks for day
time wear .... and they can even
be worn over Fall suits. The waist
length cape is very strongly favored,
too, because, aside from being ex
tremely smart with frocks, it can
serve double duty and be worn over
untrimmed coats.

Liberty print scarves worn with
plain sweaters are a new and pop
ular Quad fashion.
