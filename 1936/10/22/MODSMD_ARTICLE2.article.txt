# Philologists Hold Meeting Today
## 
### 
This afternoon at 4:15 in the
Women's Clubhouse, the University
Philological Society will hold its
regular meeting.
Professor Alfred Coester will
speak on "Influences of the Lyric
Drama of Metastasio on the Spanish
Romantic Movement," and Dr. El
mer Bagby Atwood will discuss
"Some Medieval Versions of the
Story of Paris." The program will be
followed by election of officers.
