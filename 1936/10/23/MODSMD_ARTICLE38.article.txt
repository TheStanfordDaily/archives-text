# Allen Plays Early Romantic Melodies
## 
### 
Paralleling the work being done in
Music 92, selections from early Ro
mantic period composers were play
ed by Warren D. Allen, chapel or
ganist, in his Thursday afternoon
recital. Elliot Van N. Diller spoke
on nature in religion in 1800.
The program, an anticipation of
next Sunday's vesper music, consist
ed of the Largo and Presto from
Karl Bach's Symphony in D, a Mar
tini gavotte, Stimits, Andante in B
Flat, Mozart's Fantasia in F, the in
troduction from the "Creation" by
Haydn and Beethoven's Praise of
God in Nature.
