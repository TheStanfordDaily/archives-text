# Victorious Fencers Face Y.M.C.A.
## 
### 
After winning their first match of
the season against Funke's Academy,
Stanford varsity fencers will cross
foils with a San Francisco Y.M.C.A.
team Friday at 8 p.m. in Encina
gym.
University of California foilmen
will invade Stanford Friday, October
30, completing Stanford's listed
schedule.
Women fencers have asked for an
exhibition match against the men,
with a tentative date being set for
next Thursday. Further details are
to be arranged this week-end.
Swans have been known to break
a man's leg by the blow of their
wings.
