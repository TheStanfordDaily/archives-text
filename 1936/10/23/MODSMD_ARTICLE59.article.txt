# Farm Golfers Lead In San Jose Open
## 
### 
With Card golfers in the thick of
the battle, match play in the San
Jose City Open Championship will
begin this Saturday and Sunday, and
continue every week-end until the
champion is determined.
Led by Don Edwards, law grad,
Farm golfers shot their way into
prominent places on the list of quali
fiers in the tourney, which opened
last week.
Trailing closely behind Edwards,
who scored a brilliant 72, were
George Cannon with 73; Cal Man
ning, 74; Bill Oneal and John Crable,
75; Brown Cannon, 7G; and Jack
Wallace, 77.
Besides championship and medal
ist honors, a trophy will be presented
to the winning four-man team.
