# Toyon, Branner To Hold Dance Friday
## 
### 
Toyon-Branner's annual Informal
will be held in the Toyon lobby Fri
day evening from 9 to 1 o'clock.
Bids go on sale for $1.65 today in the
A.S.S.U. office and in the halls. Ad
mission is limited to 150 couples.
The dance committee, headed by
Bill Minx, has engaged Stuart Maus
and his 10-piece orchestra to play.
Decorations will be in Hallowe'en
keeping.
