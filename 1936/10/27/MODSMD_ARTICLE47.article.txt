# CRUICKSHANK WINNER
## 
### 
BUENO AIRES, Oct. . r > (yp).—John
Cruickshank, Scotch born profes
sional, won the Argentine open golf
championship over his "home"
course, the Olivos Country Club,
Sunday. Cruickshank compiled a
72-hole total of 290 to beat Tony Mn
nero and Johnny Revolta.

A commission of five regulates
hunting in Alaska in order to pre
serve the territory's big game.
