# Stewart Gives Talk On Regional Plans
## 
### 
Mr. James Stewart, president of
the British Library Association,
spoke extemporaneously on regional
planning in Great Britain at a meet
ing of the Bay District Library As
sociation held Friday in San Fran
cisco.
Miss Jeannette M. Hitchcock, Stan
ford librarian and president of the
organization, introduced Mr. Stewart
as well as Mr. William Carlson, for
mer president of Vanderbilt Univer
sity, who spoke on regional co-oper
ation, the principal topic of the
session. Miss Hitchcock announces
that the principal convention of the
Bay District Library Association will
take place at Stanford in the spring.
