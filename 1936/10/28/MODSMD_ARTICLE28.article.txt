# Alumni Sponsor Big Game Dance
## 
### 
Only 200 reservations remain for
the alumni - sponsored Big Game
Dinner Dance to be held at the Pal
ace Hotel, according to Joe Burris.
All proceeds from the affair will he
turned over by the alumni to the
athletic scholarship fund.
Four orchestras will be playing
and a bid to the Stanford party will
admit the couple to dance to any of
them. The Palace features Bernie
Cummins, and Gordie Hecht will
play in the Stanford Room. Bids for
the Dinner Dance will be $5.25 a
plate. They can be obtained from
Harlow Rothert at the Alumni Office
or from Burris.
