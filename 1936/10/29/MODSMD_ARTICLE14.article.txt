# Allen Cabin Chosen For Club Retreat
## 
### 
The Roger Williams Club will hold
its annual retreat at the W. B. Allen
cabin in the Santa Cruz mountains
Saturday, October 31, according to
Walter Jameson, president. Dr.
Trueblood will lead the evening dis
cussion on the subject, "Two Out
standing Problems on the Stanford
Campus."
Anyone desiring transportation is
asked to call 8559. The group will
leave at 1:30 p.m. from the Union.
Wild ostriches can run 50 to 60
miles an hour for short distances.
