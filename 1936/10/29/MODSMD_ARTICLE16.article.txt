# Cap, Gown Meets To Lay Tea Plans
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, women's honor so
ciety, will meet at the home of Mrs.
Dorothy Ormsby today for tea. With
the alumnae they will discuss the
forthcoming tea for new women to
be held at the home of Mrs. Ray
Lyman Wilbur.
Heading the discussion will be the
president of the society, Phyllis
Boothe, and assisting her will be
vice-president Betty Glover and
secretary Adrienne Johnston.
Cap and Gown annually chooses
eight outstanding women from the
senior class and the same number
from the junior class. Possibilities
are judged on scholarship and ac
tivities.
Prominent alumnae belonging to
the society are Mrs. Herbert Hoo
ver. Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur, Mrs.
Payson J. Treat. Mrs. Thomas A.
Storey, Mrs. Carol Green Wilson,
and Mrs. Dorothy Ormsby.
