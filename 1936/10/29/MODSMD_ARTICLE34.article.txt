# WRENCHES FOR BAIL
## 
### 
AMSTERDAM, N.Y. (U.R).—When
Walter Morse was fined $2 on a
reckless driving charge he offered
five monkey wrenches in lieu of bail.
The wrenches were accepted.

WASHINGTON, Oct. 28 (U.R).—The
Social Security Board announced
tonight that it is asking the Depart
ment of Justice to investigate
"fraudulent" use of its name on
posters in at least six states.
