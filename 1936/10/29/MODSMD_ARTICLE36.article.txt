# TRYOUTEES ARE SCARCE
## 
### 
With only a few aspirants appear
ing, tryouts were held yesterday
afternoon in Encina Gym for assist
ant yell leaders. Webb Jones, yell
king, was very much pleased with
the spirit of those who did turn out,
but is hopeful that many more will
try on succeeding Tuesdays and
Thursdays. Time for the next try
out is scheduled for 2:15 o'clock next
Thursday afternoon.
The state of North Carolina oper
ates an automobile license tag plant
in the state prison.
