# Stanford Fencers Plan U.C. Meet
## 
### 
Coach Elwyn Bugge's varsity fen
cers will meet a University of Cali
fornia team Friday at 8 p.m. in En
cina gym in what is expected to be
one of the best matches of this
season.
Jerry Bowen and Edwin Woods
will head Stanford's team with
Dwight Strong taking third place.
Herbert Woolley won fourth place
yesterday in the trials, with the fifth
match to be contested today.
Stanford's varsity fencers have
held to a steady win so far this year,
defeating Funke's Academy of San
Francisco 9-G, and the San Francisco
Y.M.C.A. team 16-9.
