# ROTH VISITS L.A.
## 
### 
Comptroller Almon E. Roth left
last night on the Sunset for a week's
stay in Los Angeles, during which he
will address alumni meetings, handle
some endowment matters, and at
tend the U.C.L.A. game.

Anger and fear have a relation to
the differences in the color of the
eyes and hair, and in the shape of
the heads of different persons, ac
cording to a University of California
scientist.
