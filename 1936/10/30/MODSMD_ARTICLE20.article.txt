# Britain Suppresses Anti-semitism
## 
### 
LONDON, Oct. 29 (UP). The
British government will undertake
to suppress anti-semitism in this
country, it w;is announced today
when the House of Commons con
vened.
Geoffrey Lloyd, undersecretary of
the home office, told the House that
police would take every step within
their power to halt the "mischievous
activities" of Fascists in London's
East End, who have not relaxed their
efforts to stimulate anti-Jewish feel
ing.
