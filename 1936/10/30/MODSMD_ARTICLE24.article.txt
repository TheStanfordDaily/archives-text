# Sophs Stage Novel Dance Thursday
## 
### 
A novel rally dance for the Wash
ington game sponsored by the sopho
more class will be held Thursday
evening from 7 to 8 o'clock, in
women's gym.
The committee in charge of the
rally are Harry Horton, chairman,
Martha Jane Knecht, Faye Dooling,
John Playter, and Tony Keese.
