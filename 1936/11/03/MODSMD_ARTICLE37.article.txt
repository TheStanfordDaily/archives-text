# Med Exams Set For Novice Boxers
## 
### 
All men signed up for the novice
boxing tournament whose names
appear on the medical exam list
posted in the boxing room must have
their physical exam today at 4:30 at
the Men's Health Service.
Entrance lists will remain posted
until Friday, November 6, and all
who are interested should see Harry
Maloney, John Priddle, or sign in
their weight class. Elimination bouts
start on November 10 at 4 in the
boxing room; 35 entries have signed
to date.
