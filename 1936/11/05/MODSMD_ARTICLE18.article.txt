# A.S.U. Holds Peace Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Max Savelle of the history depart
ment and H. D. Roberts of the edu
cation department will be featured
speakers at the student-faculty
peace symposium to be held in the
lounge room of the Women's Club
house at 7:30 tonight. The sympos
ium is sponsored by the Stanford
chapter of the American Student
Union.
Several student speakers, includ
ing Jackie Brown and Don Strauss,
are also scheduled. Discussion will
center around the danger of war and
the strength of the peace movement
in the various European countries.
