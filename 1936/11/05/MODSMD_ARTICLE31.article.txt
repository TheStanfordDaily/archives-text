Street today talked down inflation
and simultaneously played the stock
market on an inflation basis by sell
ing utilities, neglecting rails and bid
ding frantically for metal shapes,
motors and merchandising issues.
Lucas County,, Ohio, boasts of the
largest coal shipping port in the
