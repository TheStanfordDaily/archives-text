# "CHIC" SALE ILL
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 4 (U.R).—
Charles Partlow (Chic) Sale, actor
and author, famed for portrayals of
rural life, lay under an oxygen tent
today critically ill of lobar pneu
monia.
