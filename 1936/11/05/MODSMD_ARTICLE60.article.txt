# Allen To Repeat Vesper Program
## 
### 
A program of organ music, in
cluding the famous "Litany for All
Souls' Day" by Franz Schubert will
be played this afternoon, 4:15 o'clock,
by Warren D. Allen in the Memorial
Church.
This is a repeat program of the or
gan music in last Sunday's Vesper
Service. The complete group of
numbers to be played are: Larghetto
in F Sharp minor, S. S. Wesley;
Litany for All Souls' Day, Franz
Schubert; Fantasia and Fugue in G
minor, J. S. Bach; Sonata, No. G, in
D minor, Fcliz Mendelssohn- Bar
tholdy.
