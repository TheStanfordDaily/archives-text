# Union Serves Oaks Tea, Russian Style
## 
### 
Tea. in the traditional Russian
style, poured from a samovar, was
served yesterday'afternoon to guests
from the Oaks by Union Hall women.
This was the third in a series of
teas given by the Union for some
campus group. Barbara Chandler
wns in charge of arrangements
which carried out novel features of
the Russian tea service.
