# TRIBE WAGES WAR
## 
### 
MONROVIA, Liberia, Nov. 5 (UP).
—Twelve thousand warriors of the
Kru tribe, living in the bush on roots,
mpnkeys, and snakes, swore today to
carry on their four-year light
against the Liberian army.
