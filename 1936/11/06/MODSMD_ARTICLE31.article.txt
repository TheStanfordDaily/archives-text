# Indians To Vie In Country Run
## 
### 
First start of the season for Dink
Templeton's cross country runners
will be a dual affair with Tex Byrd's
San Mateo J.C. team over the two
and one-half mile route at the Stan
fold golf course this afternoon at 4
o'clock.
The Stanford squad, led by Alex
ander, McCurdy, Mahurin, Dern,
Desenberg, and McCall, is warned
by Manager Pirdy to appear at the
start at 3:45 o'clock. The race will
finish at the clubhouse.

Three of the greatest rivers in the
world have their sources on a plain
in Tibet at an altitude of 15,000 feet.
