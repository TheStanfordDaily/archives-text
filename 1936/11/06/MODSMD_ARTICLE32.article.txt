# Water Polo Frosh Drown Menlo 8-2
## 
### 
Coach Jack Murrietta's powerful
frosh water polo squad ran rough
shod over the Menlo Junior College
septet in Encina pool yestei'day
afternoon, to chalk up an 8-2 victory.
Tonight the Indian water babes
travel to San Fi'ancisco for a tussle
with the Dolphin Club varsity. The
frosh whipped the Dolphins here
several weeks ago and will be fa
vored to topple the Clubmen again.
