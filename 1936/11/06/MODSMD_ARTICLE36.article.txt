# GRIMES TO DODGERS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 5 (UP).—Bur
leigh (Stubble-Beard) Grimes, the
New Haven, Mo., pig farmer and last
of the major league spitballers, to
day was named manager of the
Brooklyn Dodgers, the 13th in a line
extending back to 1890.

CHICAGO. Nov. 5 (UP).—Branch
Rickey and the St. Louis Cardinals
weren't kidding when they said
Dizzy Dean will be sold. He'll be
peddled down the river to the Cin
cinnati Reds for players and cash.
The deal will be one of the big
baseball stories of the winter.
