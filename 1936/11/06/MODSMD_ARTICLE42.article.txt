# Mural Handball, Golf Under Way
## 
### 
Intramural handball this year will
be conducted by Myron Sprague, as
usual, Phil Klauber, intramural
; manager, announced yesterday.
Twenty-eight teams have signed for
i the bracket form play, and Coach
Sprague will have pairings posted
early next week.
Sign-ups for intramural golf have
i been posted on the board in Encina
| Gym, and will be up but a few days
j longer. Two men form each team,
and the foursome of any intramural
match will play their rounti without
payment of green fees.

Northeastern Arizona has a natur
al bridge formed by a petrified tree.
