# Nitery Tonight
## 
### 
No cutting in, some cutting up. After to
night's performance of "Roadside" ihe Nitery
blooming ceres (pun) starts its second run of
the season. Nobody thought it was possible
for anyone but the hashers to have fun in the
Cellar, but last week-end proved that it was.
With a larger group of students on campus
tonight, the campus Nitery should be even
more of a success. Which suggests that all the
dancing should not have to be limited to one
room, but if it gets too crowded there should
be dancing on all levels of the Cellar and
A.S.S.U. lobby, tile or no tile.
Something with which to round out an eve
ning started at a campus dramatic production,
something which may lead on to a bigger and
niftier night spot, the Nitery is something worth
looking into.
