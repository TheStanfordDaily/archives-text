# Stanford Employe Killed in Accident
## 
### 
Gottleit Raschi of 818 Emerson
Street, a janitor at the University,
24 years of age and a resident of Mil
waukee, Wis., was killed yesterday
when he lost control of his motor
cycle and overturned on Palm Drive.
Pat Green was the only witness to
the accident. Three other Stanford
students, Bert Norman, Theodore
Schmidt, and Gardner Stout took the
injured man to the hospital where
he died.
