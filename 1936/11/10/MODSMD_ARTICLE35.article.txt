# Daily Announces Staff Promotions
## 
### 
Editorial Board of the Stanford
Daily announced a series of pro
motions which went into effect No
vember first.
New head copy editors are Nancy
Carmean, Corena Crase, Hunter
Hendee, Jim Hurst, and Lloyd Lap
ham. Replacing Hendee and Hurst
as Associated Press editors are R. B.
Jones and Walt Peterson. Herbert
Alward, Gertrude Bauer, Richard
Dudman, Gene Gear, Janet Gould,
Bob Houston, Larry Livingston, Sa
rah Sergis, and Rita Szekeres have
been appointed copy readers. Bob
O'Day and Catherine Johnson have
been added to the Daily staff as re
porters.
