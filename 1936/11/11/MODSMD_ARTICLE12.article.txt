# YWCA Will Hold Armistice Day Tea
## 
### 
Reinterpretation of Armistice Day
in terms of peace among nations is
the theme of the Y.W.C.A. Interna
tional Tea, which will be given today
from 4 to 5 in the basement of the
Women's Clubhouse. Invitations to
the tea have been issued to all for
eign students on the campus, the
"Y" advisory board, and all members
of the "Y" and their friends.
Colorful Armistice Day decora
tions will bo in the tea room. A pro
gram of Mexican, German, and
French folk songs has been arranged
Mr. Sturgis Riddle from Berkele.v
and Alice Clark, Stanford student
will speak briefly on peace and it?
relation to modern life.
