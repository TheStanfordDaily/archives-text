# Many Jobs Open To Farm Students
## 
### 
Positions open at the Student Em
ployment Office, according to Mrs.
Irene Coverley, offer a number of
excellent opportunities to students
who want to be entirely or partially
self-supporting.
Jobs waiting to be filled are: ex
perienced French tutor, Latin tutor,
draftsman for landscape architecture,
secretary to take dictation from an
author, salesmen and women for a
clever Christmas novelty.
Substitute hashers to work
through Thanksgiving vacation, two
hashers to work at five lunches a
week with an opportunity for full
time work later, three men to work
for room, graduate women to work
for room and board during the win
ter and spring quarters.
