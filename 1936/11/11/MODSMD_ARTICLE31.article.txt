# Stanford Chosen University Leader
## 
### 
Stanford University was elected
president of the Association of
American Universities at its thirty
eighth annual conference held No
vember 4-6 at the University of
Texas, Dallas, Professor Ralph H.
Lutz said today following his return
from Dallas as the representative of
Stanford.
Professor Lutz was a member of
the Committee on Classification of
Colleges. The Association of Ameri
can Universities is an organization of
the 32 universities in this country
and Canada which maintain the
leading graduate schools.
