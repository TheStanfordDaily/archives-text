# Mothers' Club To Honor Newcomers
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club will
honor new members tomorrow with
a luncheon to be held at 1 o'clock in
the Live Oak Manor.
Mrs. George L. : Harrington is
chairman of the luncheon committee
and will be assisted by Mr. Curtis
Brent. Mrs. George W. Dowrie and
Mrs. John W. Shenk will sponsor the
affair. A meeting of the entire club
will be held next Tuesday, according
to Mrs. Louis Love, publicity chair
man.
