# FARM LOOKS ON....
## 
### 
Peace on the San Francisco water
front loomed nearer to actuality to
day as Assistant Secretary of Labor
E. F. McGrady pushed forward plans
for further negotiations. Certain
points of controversy were agreed
upon, with only a few barriers re
maining to final peace.
Loyalist forces in Madrid went
through a zero-hour spasm yester
day, and pushed back the rebels from
certain sections of the city. How
ever, at the same time the Fascists
scored a coup in a bombing raid, set
ting afire 40 blocks in the heaviest
air assault yet experienced by the
government army.
