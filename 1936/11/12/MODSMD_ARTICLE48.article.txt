# Y.W.C.A. Begins Holiday Drive
## 
### 
Campus collection of money in the
annual Thanksgiving Drive is start
ing this week under the direction of
the Social Service Group of the
Y.W.C.A. The purpose of the drive
is to collect money for Thanksgiving
food baskets for needy people in
Palo Alto.
This year the drive will emphasize
the dual nature of social work, which
includes both temporary relief and
personality readjustment. Baskets
will be filled with food on November
24 from 3:30 to 5:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse. Head of the drive is Jean
Blackman, who is being assisted by
Gertrude Bauer and Bethany Todd.
