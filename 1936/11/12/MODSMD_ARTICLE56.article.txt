# Handball Matches Start Friday
## 
### 
First round matches in the 1936
intramural handball competition
must be played by Friday, according
to Coach Myron Sprague. The draw
has been posted on the Encina Gym
bulletin board; all of the upper teams
must make arrangements for the
match, under penalty of forfeit.
Intramural golf has been placed in
the hands of Varsity Coach Twiggs.
Pairings will be posted in the club
house at the golf course by next
week, play to begin before the end
of the week.
