# CHIMES STOLEN
## 
### 
SPRINGFIELD, Mo., Nov. 11 (U.R).
—The South Street Christian Church
learned today with considerable dis
may that the $200 power chimes
which it installed with ceremonies
had been stolen from the Methodist
Episcopal Church at Joplin, Mo., and
actually were worth $700.
