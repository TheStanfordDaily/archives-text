# Farm Flying Club To Hold Contest
## 
### 
A spot landing and bomb dropping
contest will be held at 4:00 Sunday
afternoon at the Palo Alto Airport
by members of the Stanford Flying
Club.
In a bomb dropping contest, sacks
of flour arc dropped from a certain
height at a 25-foot, circle marked
on the ground. Spot-landing is at
tempting to land on a spot marked in
white on the ground when the motor
is killed at a certain altitude.
