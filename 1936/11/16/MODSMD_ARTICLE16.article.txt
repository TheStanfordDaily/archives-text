# CARDS WIN IN POLO
## 
### 
Stanford's polo team defeated the
Presidio of Monterey 5 to 2 last Fri
day afternoon, in a fast hard game.
The Cardinal scoring was divided
between Rammy Rasmussen and Jim
Fullerton with two goals each, and
Bar Fullerton with one goal. Dick
Collins chalked one up for the Pre
sidio and Captain Ridgf sank a 40-
yard free shot.
