# ICKES BACKS PWA
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 16 (U.R).—
Public Works Administrator Harold
L. Ickes today mustered all his in
fluence with President Roosevelt to
make PWA a permanent Federal
agency, stressing s'um clearance and
low rent housing construction.
