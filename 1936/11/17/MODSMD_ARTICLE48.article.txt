# Smooth Place
## 
### 
Those who went to the Golf Clubhouse Night
ery thought it was a plenty smooth place. But
its success cannot be assumed from that, be
cause we don't know how many students didn't
go because it was too far away.
If the Golf Clubhouse were down on the
banks of Lagunita all problems would be solved,
but, unfortunately, the millennium is not here.
The best bet, at present, seems to be with the
Old Union social lounge scheme, with the Golf
Clubhouse as a stand-by plant.
