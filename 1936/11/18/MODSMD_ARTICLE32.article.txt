# Thoits Move Into Temporary Store
## 
### 
While plans are being formulated
for the reconstruction of their store
which was destroyed by fire, E. C.
Thoits have taken possession of the
old Frazier men's store building at
University Avenue and High Street.
A shipment of new stock has ar
rived, and in about a week it is ex
pected that a complete new stock
of merchandise will be on hand.
Meanwhile the wreckage crew was
busy clearing away the old debris
from the fire-wrecked building and
a considerable amount of goods is
being salvaged.
