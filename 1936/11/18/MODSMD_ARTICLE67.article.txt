# SOCIAL WORKER TALKS
## 
### 
Mrs. J. P. Baumberger, prominent
San Francisco social worker, will
speak on "Social Service Agencies in
San Francisco" this afternoon from 5
to G o'clock in the Women's Club
house. The social service group of
the Y.W.C.A. is serving tea from 4:30
to 5 o'clock for those who wish to
meet and talk to Mrs. Baumberger
personally.
