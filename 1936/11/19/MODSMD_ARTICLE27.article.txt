# German Students Hold 'Singstunde'
## 
### 
A German "Sing Stunde" was held
yesterday afternoon at the Haw
thorne Cottage. German students,
accompanied by Professor Morgan,
of the German Department, sang
well known songs of the German
people'.
The Hawthorne Cottage is also
known as the German House, for its
residents are either students major
ing in German or interested in that
language.

Dr. Aaron Bakst, Columbia mathe
matician, every week receives two or
three "solutions" of mathematical
problems that are unsolvable.
