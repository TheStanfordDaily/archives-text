# McClell and Back From Convention
## 
### 
Returning yesterday from the
twenty-first national convention of
Sigma Delta Chi, professional jour
nalistic fraternity, John McClelland,
delegate from the Fann, brought
word that the Stanford chapter was
recognized by the national organiza
tion as having one of the most active
programs of the entire fraternity.
In a report delivered at the con
vention held in Dallas, Texas, Mc-
Clelland outlined a forum research
project being sponsored by the Stan
ford chapter in which prominent
newspapermen of the state will at
tempt to find answers to the ques
tions involved in newspaper person
nel problems.
