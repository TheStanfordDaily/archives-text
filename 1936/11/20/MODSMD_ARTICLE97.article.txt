# Thomson Leads Pros in Tourney
## 
### 
PINEHURST, N.C., Nov. 19*—Jim
my Thomson, the siege gun from
Delaware, licked par by one stroke?
on the incoming nine to lead Henry
Picard of Hershey, Pa., by 3 up at
the finish of the first half of their
third-round 36-hole match in the
19th annual P.G.A. golf champion
ship today.
Harold (Jug) McSpaden of Win
chester. Mass.. conqueror of Defend
ing Champion Johnny Revolta yes
terday, was 4 up over Leo Walper,
Bethesday, Md.
