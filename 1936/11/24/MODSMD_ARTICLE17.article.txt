# Week-end Burglar Nets Books, Clothes
## 
### 
Lawbooks, $200 worth of clothing,
and a gas circulating heater were
stolen Saturday night from Eunice
Engelke's cottage at fi4B Alvarado
Row, according to campus cop Gordy
Davis. Entrance was forced through
a window some time after 10:00 p.m.
This was the only theft during the
week-end, in spite of the empty Big
Game campus.
