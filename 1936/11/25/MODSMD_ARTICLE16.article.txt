# TRAIN CRASH KILLS SIX
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Nov. 24 (U.R). Six per
sons were reported killed and 50 in
jured tonight when a speeding Chi
cago, North Shore and Milwaukee
express train crashed into the rear
end of a north-bound elevated train
on Chicago North Side.
