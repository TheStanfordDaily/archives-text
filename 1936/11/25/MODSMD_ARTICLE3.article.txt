# POLICE SEIZE JEWELS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Nov. 24 (UP).—Cus
toms officials tonight announced that
they had seized diamonds valued at
from $100,000 to $150,000 on the
French Liner Normandie and arrest
ed two men and a woman believed to
be members of a great international
smuggling ring.
