# Encina Dance to Be 'A Night in Hades'
## 
### 
Tickets go on sale today for "A
Night in Hades," Encina Club's first
informal dance, which will be held in
Encina lobby December 5. Tickets
may be purchased at A.S.S.U. offices.
George Haller's orchestra will play
for the visitors of the nether regions
on that evening.
