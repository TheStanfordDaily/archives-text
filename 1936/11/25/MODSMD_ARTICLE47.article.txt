# TRUEBLOOD TO SPEAK
##

Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, University
chaplain, will give the Thanksgiving
address in the Civic Auditorium in
Oakland at 10 o'clock tomorrow
morning. This service is an annual
affair, and is sponsored by all denom
inations, both Catholic and Protest
ant.

ord for catching the largest black sea

i pounder, in 1916.
