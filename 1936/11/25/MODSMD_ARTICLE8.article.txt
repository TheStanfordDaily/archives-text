# Van Patten Names Holiday Libe Hours
## 
### 
This afternoon the library will lock
up at (5 o'clock and remain closed all
day Thursday in observance of
Thanksgiving, Director Nathan van
Patten announces.
On Friday it will be open from
8:30 o'clock in the morning to 4:30
in the afternoon. Saturday it will be
open from 8:30 until noon. It will
be closed all day Sunday.
