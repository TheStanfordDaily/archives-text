Women's Room Deposits: Must be

ing on Thursday or Friday. Office

applications to be accepted.
Political Science: Students wishing
to register for directed reading
and independent study during
winter quarter must sign up in
room 24 before December 9.
Last Daily: Thursday's issue of The
Stanford Daily will be the last for
the quarter. All notices for the rest
of the quarter must be handed in
not later than Wednesday after
noon.
