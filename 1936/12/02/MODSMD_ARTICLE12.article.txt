# Winter Reg Dance Set for January 4
## 
### 
Winter quarter registration dance
will be given Monday, January 4,
sponsored by Theta Sigma Phi, wom
en's honorary journalism society.
Dancing will take place in the
Women's Gym from 9 to 12 o'clock,
to a prominent campus orchestra.
Several Gaieties stars will furnish
entertainment.
A special lighting effect arranged
by Bert Goodman will be featured
for decoration. Punch will be
served.
Tickets will be on sale registration
day at the Administration Building
for $1, and may also be purchased at
the door.
