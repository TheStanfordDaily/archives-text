# Davidson To Speak At Education Club
## 
### 
Discussion of progressive educa
tion from all angles will feature Ihe
next meeting of the Education Club,
Monday night, December 7, in the
Women's Clubhouse at 7.33, accord
ing to Chris Stevenson, president.
The discussion of progressive edu
cation will be led by Dr. Percy E.
Davidson of the School of Education.
Other participants will be prominent
educators from Stanford and from
Peninsula school systems. Following
the contributions by the speakers,
open discussion by all attending will
be welcomed. A short business
meeting will follow the discussion.
At the close of the evening, refresh
ments will be served.
