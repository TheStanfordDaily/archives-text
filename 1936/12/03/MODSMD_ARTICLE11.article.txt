# Shack Quiet As Daily Completes Quarter's Work
## 
### 
Daily scribes desert typewriters
and turn to books today as quiet
reigns in the Shack until next year.
After putting the last issue of 193(5
to bed, the staff celebrated the event
with ice cream and suckers.
For the next two weeks students
will have to stir up their own infor
mation on Farm happenings since
even editors must study sometime.
The Stanford Daily will not be pub
lished until January 4, 1937,
