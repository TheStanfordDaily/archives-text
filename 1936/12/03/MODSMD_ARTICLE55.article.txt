# Best Original Poem To Win $40 Prize
## 
### 
Competitors for the Ina Coolbrith
Memorial Poetry Prize must turn in
their manuscripts to the secretary of
the Stanford English Department be
fore January 15. The contest, estab
lished by friends of the late poet lau
reate of California, offers, a prize of
$4O annually for the best original,
unpublished poem on any subject.

Rules of the contest are as follows:
All manuscripts must be typewritten,
accompanied by an identification
blank obtainable in Room 207A; they
can be of any length; the prize may
be withheld if no candidate merits
the award.
