# Organizer of A.S.U. Will Speak Here
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the Amer
ican Student Union will hold its last
meeting of the quarter at 7:30 p.m.,
Tuesday, December 8, in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Mr. Edwin Alexander, national or
ganizer of the A.S.U., will address
the group, explaining the plans and
significance of the national conven
tion, which is to be held in Chicago
Christmas vacation.
The convention will be attended by
delegates from' all chapters in the
United States. The Stanford chapter
will send one delegate, who will be
elected during the meeting.
