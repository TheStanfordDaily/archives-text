# Wilbur To Hold At Home Thursday
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur
will hold their regular at home next
Thursday evening, to which they are
inviting all friends among the faculty
and student body. New students are
particularly encouraged to attend.
This reception is held quarterly,
though it differs slightly in the au
tumn when there are separate recep
tions for the faculty and the new
students. The President and Mrs.
Wilbur will receive their guests in
formally between the hours of 8 and
10 in the evening.
