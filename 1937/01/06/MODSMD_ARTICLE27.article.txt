# Youngest Congressman
## 
### 

YOUNGEST REPRESENTATIVE in Congwss when it convened
yesterday was Lyle 11. Boren of Oklahoma, who will he 28 years old on
May 11. A Democrat, he will not be lonely for his kind. The House
will open with 3115 Democrats, 88 Republicans, and 12 of other political
creeds.
