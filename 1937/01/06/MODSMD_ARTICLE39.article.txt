# MANCHUKUAN CLASH
## 
### 
TOKIO, Jan. 5 (U.R).—A new clash
on the Manchukuo-Russia frontier
between Japanese - Manchukuan
troops and unknown Russians was
reported today by the official Domei
Agency in a dispatch from Harbin.
Florida's slot machines license col
lections totaled $453,669 through Oc
tober.
