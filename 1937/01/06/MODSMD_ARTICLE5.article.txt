# Former Farm Grid Star Succumbs
## 
### 
William Wesley Burnett, a Stan
ford grid star of the class of 1900,
died yesterday at Belmont. Burnett
operated an auto camp at Five
Points.
The former football player is sur
vived by his widow, Viola Carrie
Carmen Piercy Burnett, who was in
the class of 1905 on the Farm, and
his son, William Wesley Burnett, Jr.
