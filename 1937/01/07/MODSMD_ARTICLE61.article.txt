# 'Y' Sponsors Tour Of Orphan Asylum
## 
### 
A tour through Homewood Ter
race, orphan asylum in San Fran
cisco, will be conducted by the social
service group of the Y.W.C.A. tomor
row, starting at 1 o'clock from the
Women's Clubhouse.
During the tour, a speaker will ex
plain to the group the various phases
of child welfare work. The trip,
which is open to anyone interested,
will be over between 5 and 6 o'clock.
Reservations for transportation may
be made by calling Betty Landis at
Lagunita or the Y.W.C.A. rooms.
