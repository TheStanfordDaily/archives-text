What's all the keen excitement about
January 9th?
the King
is coming to the Fairmont ... Henry King and his haunt
ing, dance-provoking music . . . mellow, merry melody as
only a King can play it! . . . frivolity, fun and frolic — the
very "Tops" in gay good times on the top of San Francisco
where there's room to park your car! . . . mark your calendar
for January 9th — join in the royal welcome next Saturday
night at seven . . . better phone Joe Houser, catering man
ager, now to make sure of getting a table and being among
those present ... at the Fairmont!
F.vory evening (except Monday) from seven 'till one.
Dinner from 51.50 or a la carte. Couvcrt (after nine
o'clock) 75c daily SI.OO Saturday. Just ask for
Mr. Houser when calling DOuglas 8800 for reserva
tions or special party arrangements.
