# Stanfordite Reigns As Carnival Queen
## 
### 
Gretchen Reinemund, senior from
El Paso, was selected the most beau
tiful girl in the southwestern United
States, and reigned as queen over the
Southwestern Sun Carnival at El
Paso, Texas, on New Year's Day.
Coming to Stanford as a junior
transfer, she has acted as sponsor at
the Oaks, and at the Union. Spanish
is her major.
