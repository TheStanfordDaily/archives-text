# Former Farm Co-ed Injured in Wreck
## 
### 
Virginia Blair, 1936 Stanford
graduate and a member of Alpha
Omicron Pi, was critically injured in
an automobile accident near Welles
ley, Mass., last Friday night, accord
ing to an Associated Press report.
Miss Blair was driving alone when
she wrecked her car against the
abutment of an underpass. She was
taken unconscious to the Newton
Hospital in Welleslcy. She is the
daughter of Fred W. Blair of Colo
rado Springs, Colorado, and since her
graduation has been living in Boston.
