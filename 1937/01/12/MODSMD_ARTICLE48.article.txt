# Rifle, Pistol Club Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Rifle and
Pistol Club will meet tomorrow night
at 7:30 o'clock in the lobby of the
Administration Building, where
plans for the quarter will be dis
cussed and matches considered.
All students interested in joining
the club are invited to attend. Rifle
and pistol teams will be formed, and
there is a good chance for new mem
bers to place, especially in the rifle
team, announces Harry Ogilvie,
president.
