# New Enrollment Shows 135 Gain Over Last Year
## 
### 
An increase in registration of
135 over a comparable date of
last year was indicated in a
statement issued yesterday by
the Registrar's Office.
To date there are' 2792 men,
1127 women registered in the
University. This time last year
there were 2GSI men, 1103
women.
The percentage of men to
women has remained constant,
with 71 per cent men, 29 per
cent women both years.
