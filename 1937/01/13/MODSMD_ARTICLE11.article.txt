# How Men Can't Take It—Women Have Less Flu
## 
### 
Nation-wide flu epidemics seem
to have met with surprising resist
ance from Stanford's sororities, re
ports from the hospital and women's
rest home show. But one Row
woman is under the weather enough
to be in bed.
With rushing activities in full
swing, the women seem to feel it
necessary to keep fit, as each house
needs all the members it can muster
for the ordeal. Sort of a practical
demonstration of mind over matter.
Either the fraternities don't need
the brothers so badly—though they
probably keep one heap big athlete
on show—or men just naturally have
weaker constitutions, for the list of
men in the hospitals is several
inches long.
