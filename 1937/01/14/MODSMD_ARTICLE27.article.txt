# English Club Plans $50 Cash Contest
## 
### 
Twenty-five doll or* and a chance
to see one's efforts in print are the
incentives to would-be Lewises and
O'Neills in the annual Yeai'book of
Stanford Writing contest sponsored
by the English Club.
Two prizes of $25 each will be of
fered for the best piece of poetry
and the best piece of prose submit
ted in the contest, which is held
each year to select a representative
cross-section of creative writing in
the Universtiy. Manuscripts may
be left with the secretary of the Eng
lish Department, or given to Grant
Leibert.
