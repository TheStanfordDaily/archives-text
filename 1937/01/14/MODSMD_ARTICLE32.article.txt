# ORGAN RECITAL TODAY
## 
### 
Raymond Kendall, assistant organ
ist, will give the first weekly organ
recital in the Chapel at 4:15 o'clock
today. He will play "Fantasie and
Fugue in G Minor," by Bach; "Pas
torale," by Cesar Franck; and a series
of "Eight Little Organ Pieces," by
S. Karg Elert.
The Grand Coulee dam across the
Columbia river will cost $63,000,000.
