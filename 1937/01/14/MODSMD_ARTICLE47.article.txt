# Minnesota Youths Seed Timber Area
## 
### 
DULUTH, Minn. (UP).—The an
cient Romans beat their swords into
plowshares, but the CCC youths of
the Superior National Forest simply
carried their forest, fighting hoes and
spades into planting areas, and are
restoring timber recently destroyed
by wide-sweeing blazes.
Million of seedlings are being
planted in areas formerly burned or
logged.
