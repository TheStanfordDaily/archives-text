# HEAR WAGNER
## 
### 
Selections from Wagner were
played at yesterday's weekly record
ed music program in Room 84 at
4:15 o'clock. Numbers heard were
excerpts from the "Valkyrie," and a
new recording of "Tristan and
Isolde," both played by Leopold
Stokowski his Philadelphia
Symphony Orchestra.
