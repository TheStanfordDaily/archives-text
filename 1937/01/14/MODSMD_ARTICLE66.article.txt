# Sword, Sandals Set Tryouts for Play
## 
### 
Sword and Sandals, men's hon
orary dramatic society, will hold try
outs at 3 o'clock this afternoon in
Room 90 for its reading of Maurice
Maeterlinck's poetic play, "Pelleas
and Melisande."
Six men and five women will re
ceive roles in the presentation, which
will be given Wednesday, January
20, according to Director Brodbury
Ellis.
