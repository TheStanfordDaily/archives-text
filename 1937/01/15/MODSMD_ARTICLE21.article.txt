# Quad Staff Starts Assembling Annual
## 
### 
The Quad staff started yesterday
in its work of assembling the year
book. The senior section of 40 pages,
each page having 18 pictures, was the
first part of the work. The first threo
days of next week will be used for
photographing the pledge class of all
fraternities.
Appointments may be made at the
Quad Office, Palo Alto 3308. Wom
en's debating individual pictures may
be taken Monday or Tuesday. A
fencing group picture is scheduled
for early in the week.
