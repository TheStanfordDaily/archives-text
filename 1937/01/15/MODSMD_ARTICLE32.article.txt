# Kendall Gives First Recital of Quarter
## 
### 
Raymond Kendall gave the organ
recital yesterday in the Chapel at
4:15 o'clock. Selections played were
"Fantasie and Fugue in G Minor"
by Bach; "Pastorale" by Cesar
Franck; and a series of "Eight Lit
tle Organ Pieces" by S. Karg Elert.
This was the first Thursday org;in
recital for winter quarter, and will be
continued weekly.
