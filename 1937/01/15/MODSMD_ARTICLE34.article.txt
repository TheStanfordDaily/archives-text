# Cornell Professor To Speak Tuesday
## 
### 
Dr. James G. Needham, emeritus
profesor of entomology and limnol
ogy at Cornell University, will talk
on "The Common Ground of Science
and Religion"' Tuesday evening at
7:30 o'clock in the Little Theater.
Dr. Needham, a graduate of Knox
University, is lecturing at Stanford
in the Biology Department. During
the years of 1927 and 1928, he was a
visiting lecturer at the Universities of
China Foundation.
