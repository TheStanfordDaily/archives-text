# Indians to Compete In Fencing Meet
## 
### 
Indian fencers will raise their foils
in San Francisco January 29 for the
open foils tournament of the Bay Di
vision of Amateur Fencers of Amer
ica. The matches are to be individ
ual, and Stanford participants will
not compete as a team.
Louis Lataillade won the tourna
ment last year, but he is no longer in
school. His shoes will be filled this
year by Jerry Bowen, Pacific Coast
Junior Intercollegiate champ in open
foils competition. A1 Snyder, who
was runner-up in last year's open
foils championship, will also compete.
Ardwolf, or earth-wolf, is a South
American carnivore.
