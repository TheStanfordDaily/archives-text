# Holly Arthur Weds Charles Wetmore
## 
### 
Holly Arthur and Charles Wet
more, both of the Class of '30, were
married yesterday afternoon at 5:00
o'clock in the Stanford Memorial
Chapel. Miss Louise Arthur was her
sistei s only attendant. A reception
followed at the Allied Arts Guild in
Menlo Park.
Mrs. Wetmore was president of Pi
Beta Phi last year, and Mr. Wetmore
was president of Phi Delta Theta and
senior football manager. He is now
working in San Francisco.
