# Cornell Professor To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. James G. Needham, professor
of entomology and limnology at Cor
nell University, will speak tonight
in the Little Theater at 7:30 on "The
Common Grorynd of Science and
Religion." "the subject will be
treated from a developmental angle
and will show how science and re
ligion began together in an effort to
explain the universe.
Dr. Needham is lecturing at Stan
ford in the Biology Department dur
ing winter quarter. He has published
numerous books on insects and
higher animal life. During 1927, 1928,
and 1930, he was a visiting profes
sor under the China Foundation of
the universities of China. He was
awarded the King Gold Medal for
"best work on the fauna in China."
