# Mothers to Hear Education Talks
## 
### 
The School of Education will be
featured in the program to be given
by members of the Stanford Mothers'
Club at 2 o'clock this afternoon in
the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Grayson Kefauver, dean of the
school, and Dr. Verna Carley, direc
tor of teacher training, will speak.
Education was chosen as the topic of
the day, according to Mrs. Lewis
Love, publicity chairman of the
Mothers' Club, because construction
of the new Cubberley Building is
scheduled to begin this month.
