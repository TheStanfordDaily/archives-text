# Quad to Take Last Photos Tomorrow
## 
### 
Today and tomrrow are the only
days that men pledges may have
their pictures taken according to
Ruth Barnes, editor of the Quad.
Women debaters may also have
their photographs taken today. The
office will be open from 9:30 to 12
in the morning and from 1 till 5:30
in the afternoon. Anyone who
wishes to make an appointment
should call the office at P.A. 3308.
A group picture of the fencing
squad will be taken this afternoon at
4:30.
