### 
Of about 1000 rooters' tickets
available for the California
basketball game Friday night

day, it was reported by News
Director Don Liebendorfer
yesterday.
Also available today, for the
last time along with the Cal
game tickets, are the free du
cats to the St. Mary's game in

yesterday. The California

this morning, and will close at
5:00. There will be no further
sale of rooters' tickets after
today, so all those wishing to
see the game are urged to get
