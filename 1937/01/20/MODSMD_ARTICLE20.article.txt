# Personnel Manager To Address Women
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Voca
tional Guidance Committee, Miss
Dale, personnel manager of the P.G.
& E., will speak informally on the
problems of securing and holding a
job tonight at 7 o'clock in the Casa
Eucalypto at Lagunita Court.
Miss Dale, who began her carecr
in stenographic work, will answer
any questions that ambitious Stan
ford women may wish to ask.
