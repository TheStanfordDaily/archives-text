# KISS EARS, NOT LIPS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 19 (U.R).—
Dr. J. C. Geiger, San Francisco's
alert health director, suggested to
night men take advantage of the
fact women perfume the lobes of
their ears. "Why not kiss them there
instead of on the lips?" Geiger sug
gested as a means of preventing
spread of influenza.
