# Chapin Describes European Journey
## 
### 
Visa troubles in Central America,
sailing the. Atlantic with Marlene
Dietrich's chauffeur, visiting Rhodes
Scholars, and meeting Scot individ
ualists passed in humorous sequence
last night in Professor Leland T.
Chapin's reminiscences of his tour
through Central America, France,
England, and studies at the Univer
sity of Edinburgh, Scotland, at the
banquet in the Union given in
honor of his return.
Seventy-five Stanford students,
faculty members, and ex-Indian de
baters attended the banquet. Jim
Hill, student debate manager for last
quarter, presided at the dinner.
