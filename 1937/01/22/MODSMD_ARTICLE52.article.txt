# Betty Davis Elected To W.A.A. Board
## 
### 
Betty Davis was elected sopho
more representative ot the Women's
Atldetic Association at a meeting of
the W.A.A. board yesterday after
noon in the Women's Gym. She is
filling the position formerly held by
Joan King, who did not return for
winter quarter.
Further business taken up by the
board at yesterday's meeting was the
consideration of a new system of
W.A.A. awards, which will give
credit for participation in Intramural
spox-ts as well as for play on W.A.A.
first and second teams.
