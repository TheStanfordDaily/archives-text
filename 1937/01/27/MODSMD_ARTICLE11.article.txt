# ROTC Invites Campus to Ball
## Dance Programs To Have Metal Cover, U.S. Seal 
### 
Students not connected with the
R. O. T. C. or Scabbard and Blade
are especially invited to the Military
Ball Friday night, according to Dick
Macgowan, chairman of the affair.
The dance will be held in the Wom
en's Dance Studio opposite the Wom
en's Gym.
Fifty bids were sold to students
during the first two days of sale, and
many more are available. They can
be bought for three dollars at the
A.S.S.U. office.
Programs for the dance will have
metal covers with crossed sabres on
the front. The United States seal
will be embossed on the inside.
Those who have bids are asked to
call for their programs at the A.S.
S.U. office on Thursday or Friday.
A mounted section of a field ar
tillery battery will draw a gun and
caisson along the Quad and up the
Row Thursday noon to announce the
coming dance.
