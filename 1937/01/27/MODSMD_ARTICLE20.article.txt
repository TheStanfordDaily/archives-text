# CROP LOSS 50 MILLIONS
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Pan. 25 (UP). -
Black .smudge clouds tonight began
rolling off the blackened orchards of
Southern California's $100.000.000
citrus crop as freezing temperatures
lifted, leaving destruction of more
than half the value of the crop in
their wake. Temperature in several
ureas hit all time lows.
