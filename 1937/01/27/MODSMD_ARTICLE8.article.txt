# English Club Prize Offered for Story
## 
### 
If there is a "Gone With the Wind"
lying around in a drawer somewhere
on the campus, the English Club
wants it, offers to publish it, and will
reward its author with a prize of $25,
provided the judges decide it's good
enough.
Similarly, budding Edgar Arling
ton Robinsons and Edna St. Vincent
Millays will have a chance to com
pete for the $25 poetry prize. The
winning selections and others repre
sentative of Stanford writing, will be
published in spring quarter in the
Yearbook of Stanford Writing.
