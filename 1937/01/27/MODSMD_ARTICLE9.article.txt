# Haller to Play For Jolly-up Tonight
## 
### 
An All-University jolly-up, fea
turing the music of George Haller's
orchestra with Yvonne Beattie as
vocalist, will be held in the Women's
Gym tonight from 7 to 8 o'clock.
The dance, which is open to every
one on campus, will follow Roble's
first exchange dinner. Half of the
frosh girls will go to the Beta, Phi
Delt, Phi Psi, and Sigma Chi houses,
while the other half will entertain
the Row gentlemen at Roble.
