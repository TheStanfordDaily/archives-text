# Senior Class Meeting Set For Tonight
## 
### 
Seniors who did not attend the
preliminary meetings held last week
are asked to attend a general class
meeting tonight at 7:15 o'clock in
Room 214, Mort D'Evelyn, '37 presi
dent, announced yesterday.
The brief meeting, which will
probably be the only all-class gath
ering of the quarter, will be largely
concerned with the presentation of
plans of the Endowment and Alumni
Fund Committee, headed by Betty
Grover and Don Moulton.
Discussion of the fund drives and
of substitutes for the senior prom and
picnic will conclude the meeting.
