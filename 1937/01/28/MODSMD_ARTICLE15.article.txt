# Army to Stage Parade for Ball
## Section of 75's to Roll Down Row at Noon 
### 
Cannons will roll down the Row
this noon when a section of R.O.T.C.
75 Battery parades to advertise the
Mliitary Ball, to be given by Scab
bard and Blade tomorrow night from
9 to 1 o'clock in the Women's Dance
Studio.
Gordy Heche and his 12-piece or
chestra will provide the music for the
dance and four entertainers will be
featured. The Grand March, which
starts the ball, is to be entered by all
dancers present, not merely by offi
cers, according to George Lindoff.
Bids at $3 each are on sale from
9 to 5 o'clock at the A.S.S.U. office.
Those who have tickets are requested
to get their dance programs at the
office.
