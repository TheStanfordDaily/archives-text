# Fitzpatrick to Play At Paly High Dance
## 
### 
Stanford students have been wel
comed to attend an afternoon dance
Friday at the Palo Alto High School
with Eddie Fitzpatrick's complete or
chestra from the St. Francis Hotel
providing the music.
Dancing will be from 2:30 to 4:30,
with the young maestro's featured
vocalists, Kay Griffith, Rex Baker,
Bob Saunders and Bill Wallace, do
ing the vocal chores. Price of ad
mission will be 35 cents a person.
