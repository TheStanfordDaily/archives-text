# Philology Society To Hear Speakers
## 
### 
Dr. John T. Reid and Mr. James V.
Cunningham will be guest speakers
at the next meeting of the Uni
versity Philological Society tomor
row at 4:15 o'clock. "Spain as Seen
by Frank, Dos Passos, and Heming
way," is Dr. Keid's subject, and
"The Poetic Method of Propertius" is
Cunningham's.
The meeting will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse. Members who
have not paid their dues are urged to
do so at this time.
