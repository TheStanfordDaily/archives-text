# NAVY HAWAII-BOUND
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, Jan. 27 (U.R). -
Twelve giant Navy air boats will take
off from San Diego harbor for Ha
waii at 7 a.m. tomorrow, weather
permitting, Rear Admiral Ernest J.
King, commander of the aircraft base
force, announced today.
Drapes sewed by men students are
being used to decorate their rooms in
the Columbia University dormitories.
