# Lagunita Invites Fatuity to Supper
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will play hostess to
approximately 20 professors and
their wives this Sunday night at sup
per. Guests suggested by individ
uals were invited by the dormitory
as a whole.
Faculty members who have ac
cepted include: Mr. and Mrs. L. E.
Bassett, Mr. T. H. Boggs, Mr. and
Mrs. E. A. Cottrell, Mr. and Mrs.
P. E. Davidson, Mr. and Mrs. C.
Eagleton, Mr. and Mrs. E. D. Fagan,
Mr. and Mrs. R. S. Irvine, Mrs. M. M.
James, Mr. and Mrs. E. Jones, Mr.
and Mrs. P. A. Leighton.
Mr. and Mrs. D. M. Mendelowitz,
M.r and Mrs. H. D. Meritt, Miss E. R.
Mirrielees, Mr. J. A. Sellards, Dr.
and Mrs. E. C. Sewell, Mr. and Mrs.
C. P. Stone, Mr. and Mrs. P. J. Treat,
and Dean Mary Yost.
