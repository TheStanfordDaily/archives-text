# BARBER FORGETS THANKS
## 
### 
VATICAN CITY. Jai>. 31 (UP).—
Pope Pius XI discovered tonight that
onp of his oldest friends, a talkative
barber from Perugia, has disappeared
without even a word of thanks for a
$15000 gift.
