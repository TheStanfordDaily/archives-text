# Perry Downs Vines In Tennis Series
## 
### 
PALM BEACH, Fla, Feb. 2 (U.R).—
Fred Perry defeated Ellsworth Vines
in the 14th match of their profes
sional tennis series before a society
gallery of 1200 here yesterday. The
score was 2-G, 6-3, 9-7.

The Briton's victory cut Vines'
leadership in the series to 8-6.
