# Alps Guides May Carry Radio Sets
## 
### 
VIENNA (UP). — SOS calls to
summon rescuing parties to the spot
of a mountain catastrophe soon will
be in use throughout the Austrian
Alps.

If present plans materialize, like j
ships on the sea, all mountain huts, I
and possibly also the guides on dif
ficult climbs, will be equipped with J
short wave sending and receiving I
sets.
