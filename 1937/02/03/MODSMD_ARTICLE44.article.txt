# PROBE BOXER'S DEATH
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Feb. 2 (U.R).—A for
mal investigation into the death of
Tony Marino, bantamweight boxer,
will bit conducted today by District
Attorney Charles P. Sullivan of
Queens. Marino died yesterday of
cerebral concussion after losing a
bout Saturday night to Indian Quin
tana of Panama.
