# BEARCATS DOWN IDAHO
## 
### 
SALEM, Ore., Feb. 2 (U.R).—Col
lege of Idaho basketball quintet was
snowed under, 34-11, by Willamette's
Bearcats last night. The visitors
chalked up only five field goals, four
of them in the first half.
