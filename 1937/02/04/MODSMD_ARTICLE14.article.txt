# Saving a City
## 
### 

THE MISSISSIPPI, flowing backward as the Ohio flood poured
into it, battered the levees about Cairo, 111., while workers battled
desperately to save the city. Meanwhile, dreaded sandboils appeared
within the city, water gushing from them under the river's weight.
