# BUDGE WINS AT MIAMI
## 
### 
MIAMI BEACH, Fla., Feb. 4 (UP).
—Donald Budge, America's No. 1 net
star, yesterday flashed his finest form
this season to down Walter Senior,
a fellow Californian, and move into
the semi-finals of the second annual
Surf Club tennis tournament. The
score was 8-6, 6-4.
