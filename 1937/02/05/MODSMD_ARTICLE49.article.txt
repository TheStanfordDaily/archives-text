# Palms to Decorate "Shadows Dance'
## 
### 
Palms, casting their shadows by
means of indirect lighting, and silver
and black programs will carry out
the decorative scheme of the "Dance
in the Shadows" given by Union
Club tonight in Union Lobby from
9:30 to 1:30.

Irvin Jorgensen or "Our Hasher"
fame will be master of ceremonies
for the floor show. Other entertain
ment features will include imperso
nations by Jane Clary, a shadow
waltz by Barbara Curtis, and an ex
hibition ballroom dance by Mary
Elizabeth Crichton and Paul Mann.
