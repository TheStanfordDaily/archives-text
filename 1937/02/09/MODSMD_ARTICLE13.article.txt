# Influenza Sends 17 to Hospital
## 
### 
Seventeen cases of influenza on
the campus, five new and twelve old,
were reported yesterday by Dr.
Thomas Storey, men's health direc
tor, who declared that only three of
the cases last week were gastro
intestinal flu.
Women's Health Service reported
that almost thirty cases had been
reported but none were serious.
At present, according to Dr. Sto
rey, there are five old and two new
cases at the Student Guild Hospital,
six old and two new cases at the
Men's Rest Home, and one old and
one new case at the Palo Alto Hos
pital.
