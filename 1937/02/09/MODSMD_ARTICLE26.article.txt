# German Scholar Lectures Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Friedrich Schoenemann, direc
tor of the Amerika-Abteilung of the
University of Berlin, will speak on
"American Literature in Germany"
in the Little Theater at 7:30 o'clock
tonight. He will be introduced by
Professor Ralph H .Lutz, dean of
graduate study here.
Dr. Schoenemann took his doctor
ate at the University of Berlin and
has taught at the University of Mun*
ster and Berlin, specializing in North
American literature. . He has iust
completed a term of teaching at the
University of Nebraska.
Dr. Schoenemann has translated a
number of American books and has
contributed to many periodicals.
