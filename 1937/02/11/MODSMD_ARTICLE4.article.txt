# Allen To Resume Weekly Recitals
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, organist and as
sociate professor of music and educa
tion, has returned from a six weeks'
trip in the East and will resume his
organ recitals this nfternoon at 4:15
o'clock in the Chapel.
Mr. Allen made his first stop at
Chicago, late in December, for the
joint convention of the Music Teach
ers National Association and the
American Musicological Society. At
this meeting, he was made a member
of the Executive Committee of the
M.T.N.A.
