# The 						Stanford Daily
## 
### 
Owned and published daily except Saturday and Sunday by the
Associated Students of
STANFORD UNIVERSITY
Entered as second-class matter at the Post Oifice in Palo Alto,
Caliiornia, under the act of March 3, 1897.
SUBSCRIPTION RATE
S4 per year, 51.50 per quarter
(Faculty members, $2.25 and 75c)
