r r , HIRD DAY of the Hashers' Sit Down and Shut Up
Strike at the Chi O house dawned, as efforts at
conciliation and reconciliation failed yesterday. The
position of the Chi O hierarchy seemed immeasurably
strengthened as reports drifted in that two of the
hashers were seen sneaking out into the back alleys
on dark nights and talking to the girls out of the
side of their mouths.
