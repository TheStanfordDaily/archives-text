# Toyon, Branner Set 'Formal' Date
## 
### 
Toyon-Branner winter formal has
been set for Friday, February 26, in
the Toyon lobby, according to Com
mittee Chairman Stanley Christen
sen. Ernie Heckscher will provide
the music.
Bids will be on sale in the halls
today and tomorrow at $3, and will
be on general sale in the A.S.S.U.
office beginning Wednesday. Com
bining for the first time the Toyon
and Branner winter dances, the
dance committee will work with the
theme, "Casa Manana."
