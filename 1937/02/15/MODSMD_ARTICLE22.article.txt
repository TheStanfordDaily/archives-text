# Errors Total 75 In Daily Ad Contest
## 
###

Contest is still in progress. Up to
date, 75 errors have been purposely
made in advertisements for sleuths
to detect.

any one of 17 prizes, ranging from
cash to clocks, stockings to a perma

February 26, and all entries must be
made by March 1.
