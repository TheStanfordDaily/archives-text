# Men's Tuition Note Applications Due
## 
### 
Men students desiring to take out
or renew tuition notes for spring
quarter must fill out application
blanks this week, Miss Vivienne
Church, of the Dean of Men's office,
announced today.
Application forms must be turned
in by Friday, February 19, she said.
Hours of the Dean of Men's office
are 9 to 12 and 1 to 5 o'clock daily.
