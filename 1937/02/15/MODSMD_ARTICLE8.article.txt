# CAL TICKETS ON SALE
## 
### 
Tickets for the California-
Stanford basketball game will
go on sale Monday and Tues
day from 8:30 to 5 o'clock at the
Board of Athletic Control ticket
office. They will cost 40 cents
with presentation of student
body cards. The game is to be
played at the Berkeley Pavilion
on Friday night, February 20.
At the same time, tickets for
the Santa Clara game to be
played here Saturday night,
February 21, may be obtained
by presentation of student body
cards alone.
