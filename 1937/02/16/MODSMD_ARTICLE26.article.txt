# COURT ADJOURNS FOR TWO WEEKS
## 
### 
WASHNIGTON, Feb. 15 (UP). —
The Supreme Court recessed for two
weeks today with indications that
March 1, its next meeting day,
might bring some of the year's most
important decisions — the Wagner
Labor Relations Act; re-hearing of
the New York unemployment insur
ance case, and the Washington State
minimum wage law.
