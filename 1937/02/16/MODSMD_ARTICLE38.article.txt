# Pastor To Address Lunch Club Today
## 
### 
"Two Heretics: Tom Paine and Jo
seph Priestly" will be the topic for
a talk by Dr. Frederick Griffen at
this week's meeting of the Stanford
Luncheon Club, in the Women's
Clubhouse at noon tomorrow.
Dr. Griffen, who is pastor of the
First Unitarian Church of Philadel
phia and a member of the Harvard
School Board of Preachers, is regard
ed as one of the leading Unitarians in
this country. He is giving a series of
sermons in the Memorial Chapel at
the present time.
