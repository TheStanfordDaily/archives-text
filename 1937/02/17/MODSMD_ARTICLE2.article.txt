# Spanish Youths Speak Tomorrow
## 
### 
The four of the Spanish
Youth delegation now touring the
United States will speak twice at
Stanford tomorrow.
At noon they will speak at a
luncheon in the Union, and at 2:15
at a meeting of the American Stu
dent Union in the ballroom of the
Women's Clubhouse. A few tickets
for the luncheon, costing 50 cents,
are still available at the A.S.S.U.
office.
The delegation, sent to this country
by the United Youth Front in Spain,
which is a part of the popular front
government of Valencia, includes a
Catholic editor, a Red Cross nurse,
leaders of the Republican and So
cialist youth organizations, and the
head of a war orphanage.
