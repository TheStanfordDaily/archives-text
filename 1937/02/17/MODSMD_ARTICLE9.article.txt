# DOLE TO PLAY LEAD
## 
### 
Janet Dole, prominent member of
Masquers, will play the lead in the
comedy, "And So To Bed," which
will be presented this week-end by
the Palo Alto Community Players at
the Civic Theater. Miss Dole will
appear as Mrs. Samuel Pepys. James
Clancy, another Stanford student,
will play King Charles II in the Palo
Alto production.
