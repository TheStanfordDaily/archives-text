# Unitarian Minister To Preach Sunday
## 
### 
Preacher for Sunday's service in
the Memorial Church at 11 o'clock
will be Dr. Frederick Griffin, lead
ing Unitarian clergyman from Phila
delphia.
Dr. Griffin also gave the sermon
last Sunday, and completing his ap
pearances here, he will give a talk at
the 4 o'clock Sunday vesper service.
This will be a special community
service commemorating Brotherhood
Day, at which representatives of the
Jewish and tho Roman Catholic
faiths will give addresses.
