# Glee Club To Sing Over Radio Hour
## 
### 
Music by the Stanford Women's
Glee Club, directed by Raymond
Kendall, instructor in music, will be
heard on the Radio Hour over KGO
next Monday evening at 8 o'clock.
The program will include the
"Stanford Loyalty Hymn" which
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, composed and which has been
arranged by Mr. Kendall. "Also on
the program is a poem by Katherine
Adams, "Children of the Moon,"
with music composed by Elinor War
ren of Los Angeles.
