# FARMS ARE VALUABLE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 18 (UP).
—The San Francisco Chamber of
Commerce, whose principal function
is to advertise and promote the met
ropolitan features of the Golden Gate
city, admitted today it had checked
and rechecked its figures four times
before announcing that there were
248 farms within the city limits and
they had a total value of $5,861,700.
