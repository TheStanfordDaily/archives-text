# APPLICANTS CONFER
## 
### 
Applicants for Stanford Medical
School who intend to enter next fall
will have the opportunity to confer
with Dr. Carleton Mathewson, mem
ber of the Medical Admission Com
mittee, in the registrar's office next
Wednesday, Feb. 24, from 10 a.m. to
5 p.m. and Saturday, February 27,
from 9 a.m. to 12 p.m.

Wally Jensen, Encina frosh, reads
the Daily under the shower every
morning.
