# Chapin To Talk On Scotch University
## 
### 
Dr. Leland T. Chopin, assistant
professor of English, will speak on
"The University of Edinburgh and
the Scottish Tradition," in the Little
Theater at 7:30 o'clock Tuesday
night. His subject covers the devel
opment of the university from a
small town college in 1538 to its pres
ent world prominence.
Dr. Chapin returned this winter
from the University of Edinburgh,
where he continued his studies in
debate and oratory. While in Scot
land he made a tour of the country,
observing custom and tradition at
first hand.
