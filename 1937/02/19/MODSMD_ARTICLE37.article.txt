# RABBITS ON STRIKE
## 
### 
FRESNO, Feb. 17 (UP).—A sit
down strike, and not by choice, was
in progress on the flat lands south of
here tonight. The recent flood that
swirled over the area left several
small islands only a few feet in cir
cumference.
