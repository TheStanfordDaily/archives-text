# FORBIDS DEMONSTRATION
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 18 (UP).
—Lee J. Holman's Longshore "un
ion" has been forbidden to hold any
more impromptu parades or demon
strations, Chief of Police William
Quinn said today.
