# Hulme Will Speak To Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Professor Edward Maslin Hulme,
of the Stanford history department,
will speak on "The History of Stan
ford That Cannot Be Written" at the
meeting of the Stanford Luncheon
Club Wednesday noon in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, chaplain of
the Memorial Church and club ad
viser, says that Hulme "will tell of
the personal and humorous incidents
he has seen in his years at Stanford."
