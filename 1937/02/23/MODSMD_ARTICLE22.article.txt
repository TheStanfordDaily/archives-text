# Thief Loots $250 From Encina Gym
## 
### 
Bobby Grayson's All - American
watch was stolen Friday from the
coaches' room in the cast wing of
Encina Gym. in an estimated $250
haul. Jim Lawson's $75 watch and
$75 in cash were also taken.
The theft ocurred between 3 and 6
o'clock in the afternoon, according
to Deputy Sheriff Fred Free, who is
making investigations. The coaches
were out on the baseball field when
someone entered the room and broke
open the lockers. Some investigation
has already been made by campus
officers, but no clues have so far
been unearthed.
