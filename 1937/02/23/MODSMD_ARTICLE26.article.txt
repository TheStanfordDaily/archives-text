# Lawyer To Speak On Youth Drive
## 
### 
' The Needs of Youth for Jobs and
Security" will be discussed by Mr.
Jean Grattan, San Jose lawyer, at a
campaign meeting for the California
Youth Act in the auditorium of the
Palo Alto High School at 8 o'clock
Wednesday, February 24.
Aline Goldstein and Leonard Pock
man, Stanford students, will also
speak at the meeting, which has been
called by campus and Palo Alto or
ganizations supporting the California
Youth Act.
