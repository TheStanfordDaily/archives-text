# Farm Ace Reaches College Golf Finals
## 
### 
Jack Wallace, Stanford golf ace,
reached the finals of the Pacific In
tercollegiate golf championship, this
week-end. but was defeated 5 and 3
by Roger Kelly of Loyola University.
Wallace won the medal round the
day before with a 70, beating out
Kelly by one stroke.
Wallace won the semi-final match
from Al Hyman, another Stanford
player, on the twentieth hole
