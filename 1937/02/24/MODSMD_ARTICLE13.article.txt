# Chinese Scholar Applications Due
## 
### 
Applications for scholarships to
Ling Nan University, at Canton,
China, must be in the hands of Miss
Byler, of the scholarship committee,
by February 28.
The awards, made to properly
qualified men and women, cover tui
tion and room for one year at this
Chinese university, whose credits are
acknowledged at Stanford. Twelve
Stanford students have been granted
the award in the past few years; this
number exceeds that of any other
American university.
