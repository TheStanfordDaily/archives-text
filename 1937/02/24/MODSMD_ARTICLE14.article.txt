# 600 Ducats Left For Troy Tilts
## 
### 
Approximately 300 rooters' tickets
go on sale this morning for each of
the Stanford - Southern California
basketball games in the Pavilion this
week-end, after a heavy first-day
sale yesterday.
There will be no tickets sold after
the 600 are gone, rooters' or general
admission. Five hundred general
admission seats put on sale yester
day were all purchased within an
hour and a half of the opening of
sales. At 8:00 both Friday and Sat
urday nights there will be 250 stand
ing room tickets sold.
All rooters' tickets cost 40 cents,
and it has been expressly stipulated
by the B.A.C. that they are NOT
TRANSFERABLE.
