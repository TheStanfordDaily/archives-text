# BIG LEAGUER LOSES
## 
### 
SARASOTA, Fla., Feb. 23 (U.R).—
Don B. Newburn of Sarasota, .seven
times city golf champion, yesterday
regained his title as he defeated Paul
Warier, National League batting
champ, in the finals of the city
tournament, 4 and 3.
