# STAN-O-FLASHES
## 
### Harrison Standley 

To Mexico has come cactus-puss Trotsky.
His face is bewhiskered and bosky.*
His plans irrational
For a Fifth International
Like Popocatepetl soon may blow-offsky.
" See dictionary.
