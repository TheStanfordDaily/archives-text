# Lagunita To Hold All-Campus Dance
## 
### 
In place of Cardinals' cancelled
post-game dance will be Lagunita
Court's dance from 9:30 to 12 o'clock
Saturday night. An all-campus in
formal affair, bids are priced at 50
cents and are obtainable from any
Lagunita resident.
George Haller's orchestra will play
in the lobby. Stanford and U.S.C.
coaches and their wives, and mem
bers of both teams will be guests of
honor. Penny Lathrop is in charge
of the dance arrangements.
