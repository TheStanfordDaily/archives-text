# Poetry Contest Now Opening
## 
### 
Opening of the Clarence Urmy
Poetry Contest for 1937, to award a
prize of $40, is announced by the
English Department, where entries
must be left on or before May 3.
The contest is open to both gradu
ates and undergraduates regularly
enrolled in the University. Not more
than three poems may be handed in
by one writer. Manuscripts should
be submitted in triplicate, typewrit
ten if possible, and should not bear
the author's name, but should be ac
companied by a sealed envelope
bearing the title of the poem and
containing the name of the author.
Further information may be ob
tained from the department secre
tary in room 207 A.
