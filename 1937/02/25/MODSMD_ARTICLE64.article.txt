# Hinkle, Nicol Talk At Meeting Today
## 
### 
Two faculty members will speak
at today's meeting of the University
Philological Society, which will be
held at 4:15 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Dr. Theodore Nicol, of the Ro
manic Languages department, will
speak on "Censorship in the French
Historical Comedy," and George
Hinkle, instructor in English, will
discuss "The Power of the Eye in
Chapman's Poetry."

One student out of every 10 at the
University of Washington is on NYA.
