# Farm Flyers May Join National Club
## 
### 
Stanford Flying Club members
have been invited to join Alpha Eta
Rho, national flying fraternity.
Whether they will accept will be de
cided within the next two weeks.
This week-end the Flying Club is
planning a mass flight to Santa
Cruz. Approximately 15 ships will
be flown by members of the club and
will compete in a handicap race.
