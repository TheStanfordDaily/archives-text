# Luggage Is Light In Color, Weight
## 
### 
You may travel by automobile,
airplane, trailer, or train this spring
or summer vacation. But your lug
gage must be light—in color and in
weight.
Bright tweed designs on trunks,
suitcases, or bags will brighten any
one's trip this season. Tan with red
dish browns or gray with red and
black trimmings are the predominant
combinations. Even small bags arc
considerably lighter so that women
can travel with greater ease and
comfort.
For the air-minded women, a spe
cial*"aviation kit," for carrying cos
metics of all sorts, has been de
signed. It is the answer to women
who are packing with an eye to
poundage. Incidentally, the case is
just as smart and practical if you
don't intend to fly. It makes a fine
lightweight overnight bag. So with
the light luggage made this year,
anyone can have fun packing.
