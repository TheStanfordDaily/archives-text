# GAELS DOWN LOYOLA
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 25 (U.R). —
Si. Mary's Gaels went galloping on
the basketball floor of Pan Pacific
Auditorium last night where they
defeated Loyola University, 36-32,
in it non-conference game.
