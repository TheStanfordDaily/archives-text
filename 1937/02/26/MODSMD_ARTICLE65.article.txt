###

ing patent leather or soft draped
suede will carry more complete
make-up equipments this season
than ever before. In the form of
miniature grips or side-bottom vani
ties, which may be stood on dress
ing table or counter, bags will be
roomy and well fitted wilh beauty
essentials.

sity for traveling, will be made in
envelope styles or complicated
draped bags in which numerous
compartments and pockets are clev
erly concealed. Smart reptile skins,
heavy calf skins, and practical pig
skin are the appropriate materials
for these.
