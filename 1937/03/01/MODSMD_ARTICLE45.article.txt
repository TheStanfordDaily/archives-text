# ROSEMONT WINS
## 
### 
SANTA ANITA, Feb. 27 (UP). —
Rosemont won the third running of
the world's richest race here Satur
day afternoon in a nose finish over
Seabiscuit that had the record crowd
of 50,000 fans delirious with excite
ment.
