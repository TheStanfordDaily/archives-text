# Scandinavian Tour Applications Due
## 
### 
Two student travel fellowships of
$300 each will be awarded this
spring to undergraduate or graduate
students by the American-Scandi
navian Foundation and the Bureau
of University Travel at Newton,
Massachusetts.
The grants apply to membership
in a Scandinavian Travel Seminar to
be conducted this summer under the
leadership of Nils G. Sahlin of Yale
University faculty. Application
forms should be submitted to the
Bureau by April 1.
