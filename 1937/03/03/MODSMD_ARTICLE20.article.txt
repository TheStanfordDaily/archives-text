# Hall Men Must Make Deposits
## 
### 
Men's room reservations for
Sequoia, Old Union, and Encina
will fall due (he latter part of
this week and the first three
days of next week.
The men who arc now living
in Sequoia and the Old Union
who wish to reserve their rooms
for spring quarter should call
at the Dean of Men's office this
Thursday or Friday, March 4
or 5. They should also pay their
deposits at that time.
Encina men who wish to re
serve their rooms for next
quarter should call at the Dean
of Men's office Monday, Tues
day, or Wednesday, March 8,
9, or 10. They should also pay
deposits on those days.
