# NORMA TAUBELE WINS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, March 2 (U.R).—
Norma Taubele of New York, top
seeded entrant, moved into the third
round of the women's national in
door tennis championships today
with a 6-2, 6-3 victory over Olga
Kallos of New York.
