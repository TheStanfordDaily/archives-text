# Cathcart To Speak On Court Reform
## 
### 
Professor Arthur M. Cathcart, of
the Stanford Law School, will speak
in a symposium on "The President
vs. the Supreme Court" at a meet
ing of the Commonwealth Club in
San Francisco today. Cathcart will
speak against the President's plan;
while Henry H. McPike, U.S. district
attorney, will uphold Roosevelt's
proposals for court reform.
Dr. Grayson Kefauver, of the
School of Education, will lead a dis
cussion on "What the Schools May
Do for Our Children" at a luncheon
or the education section of the Com
monwealth Club Wednesday noon.
