# Slogan To Win Masque Ball Bid
## 
### 
A free Masque Ball bid is
offered as the prize in a con
test for the slogan for the 1937
Convalescent Home Drive.
Deadline is noon on Friday,
April 2.
Judges for the contest will be
members of the general drive
committee.
The last call has been issued
for members of tho many Con
valescent Home Drive commit
tees. Students who have not yet
signified their desire to work
have been asked to meet Jeeb
Halaby in the A.S.S.U. Office
this afternoon, between 2 and 3
o'clock.
