# Weer To Discuss Christian Science
## 
### 
"Christian Science: a Practical
Daily Religion," will be the subject
of a lecture to be given by John
Henry Weer, C.S., tonight at 7:30
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Weer is a member of the Board of
Lectureship of The First Church of
Christ, Scientist, in Boston, Massa
chusetts. He was formerly field rep
resentative of the Christian Science
Board of Directors. He will be in
troduced by Bob Hall, member of
the Christian Science Organization
at Stanford University, under the
auspices of which the lecture will be
presented.
