# CIRCLE "S" ELECTS
## 
### 
Circle S Society, honorary minor
sports society, last nighl announced
the election of the following new
members: Louis Folbe, Jim Fuller
ton, Cam Hall, Dick Hanna, John
Lamb, Alec McKenzie, Jack Wallace,
and Bill Wilbur.

The average yearly per capita
consumption of coffee is 12 pounds.

Doc Luckett or Joe Vigna; right
wing, Jay Holdridge or Norman
Hager; left wing, Harry Goff or Jim
Grubbs; fullback, Bob Mondavi; re
serve forward, Charles Drace.
