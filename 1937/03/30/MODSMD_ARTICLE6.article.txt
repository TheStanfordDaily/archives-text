# Library Displays Housing Exhibit
## 
### 
On display until the end of April
in the University Library is a series
of exhibits entitled "Development of
Housing Improvement in Europe and
the United States."
All of the materials are from the
"Ray Lyman Wilbur Collection on
Social Problems" in the Hoover War
Library. Dr. Wilbur was president
of Better Homes in America from
1929 to 1935 and also chairman of the
President's Conference on Home
Building and Ownership, held in
1931.
The wide range of this conference
is demonstrated in exhibits illus
trative of slum clearance, decentral
ization of industry, farm and village
housing, home financing, home
making and furnishing, negro hous
ing, and taxation.
