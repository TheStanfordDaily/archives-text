# Mothers Announce Spring Fete Plans
## 
### 
Stanford Mothers' Club will hold
its annual Spring Fete on May 18
this year. The Ogden Mills estate
in Millbrae will be the scene of the
lestival. Mrs. H. C. Moreno, who is
the benefit chairman, is in charge of
plans.
Other details of the Spring Fete
will be decided at the next meeting
of the Stanford Mothers' Club, which
is scheduled to be held on April 1(5.
