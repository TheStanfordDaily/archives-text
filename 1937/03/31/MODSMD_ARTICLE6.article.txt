# Weigle Discusses Early Journalism
## 
### 
"Newspaper publishing in Cali
fornia has made great progress since
the first paper appeared in San Fran
cisco just 90 years ago," said Clifford
F. Weigle, assistant professor of
journalism, yesterday in commenting
on his forthcoming talk over the
Stanford University Hour.
The discussion of the difficulties
encountered by the early newspapers
in California will be given by Mr.
Weigle next Monday, April 5, over
KGO at 8:30 p.m. His topic is "Cali
fornia's Pioneer Newspapers."
