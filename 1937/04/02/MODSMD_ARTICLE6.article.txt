# Trublood To Give Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood will preach
the Sunday sermon in the Memorial
Church at 11 o'clock. His topic for
his first service of the quarter will
be "Relationship Eetwcen Poetry
and Religion."
The chaplain will return tomor
ro wfrom a trip to Oregon, where he
delivered speeches at Oregon State,
Willamette University, and the Uni
versity of Oregon.
The regular 9:30 o'clock class will
be conducted as usual this Sunday,
and all students are invited.
In place of the usual vesper serv
ices, there will be an organ recital
by Warren D. Allen at 4 o'clock
Sunday in the Memorial Church.
