# 'Land of Navajo' Lecture Subject
## 
### 
"Land of the Navajo" will be the
topic of an illustrated lecture by
E. P. Hunt, associate professor of
physical education, in the Assembly
Hall, tomorrow evening at 7:30
o'clock.
Mr. Hunt will illustrate the talk
with colored movies which he took
on the Navajo reservation. The pic
tures, taken during the last four
years, represent 3500 miles of travel,
and show the life of the Indians and
the country in which they live.
Mr. Hunt will discuss the customs
and everyday life of the Navajo.s,
and will touch on their tribal cere
monies. He will also show pictures
taken during a 200-mile trip down
the San Juan and Colorado rivers
in an open boat.
