# Faculty Clubs Plan Party Friday Night
## 
### 
A joint formal party will be given
by the Men's and Women's Faculty
Clubs at eight o'clock Friday night
in the Women's Clubhouse. Program
for the party includes skits, cards, ;
games, and dancing.
Preceding the party, a no-host
dinner will be held in the Union at
6:30 o'clock. Reservations for tickets
for the dinner, priced at 85 cents,
may be made through Mrs. Glazer ,
at the Stanford Union, telephone
313(5, until five o'clock Wednesday.
