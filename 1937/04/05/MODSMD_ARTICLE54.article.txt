# DEATH ENDS QUARREL
## 
### 
CAMPO, Colo., April 4 (U.R). —
Charles Smith and Virgil Fuller en
gaged in a friendly quarrel here to
day as to which would pay their
dinner check. Fuller threw Smith
head first to the concrete floor of the
cafe. Smith died from skull fracture.
