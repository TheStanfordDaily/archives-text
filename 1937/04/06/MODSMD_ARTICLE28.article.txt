# FULLENWIDER MARRIES
## 
### 
Off on a short honeymoon today
were Frank Fullenwider, law school
graduate, and his bride, the former
Helen Margaret Angel. Fullenwider,
long time member of Ex Committee,
was prominent in student activities,
and was instrumental in drawing up
the present A.S.S.U. Constitution.
The young couple will live at Col
lege Terrace upon their return.
