# U.S. Coast Guard Prep School Burns
## 
### 
NEW LONDON, Conn., April 5
(UP). —Fire today destroyed the Ad
miral Billard Academy, prepara
tory school for U.S. Coast Guard
and Naval Academies.
Damage was estimated at more
than $50,000. Thirty-three cadets in
the three-story building escaped
without injury.

WASHINGTON, April 5 (U.R).—
The Senate today passed the Guf
fey Bituminous Coal Control Bill,
setting up a code of fair practices
for the soft coal industry, after de
feating the controversial Byrnes
amendment which was intended to
condemn sit-down strikes.
