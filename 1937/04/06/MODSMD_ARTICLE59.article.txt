# Tickets for Tibbett Concert Available
## 
### 
Tickets for the Lawrence Tibbett
concert to be given Friday evening,
April 16, are on sale at the Concert
Series office in the Old Union,
Keeble's in Palo Alto, and Sherman
Clay and Company in San Jose.
Price of admission will be $2.50,
$2, and $1.50. Mr. Tibbett's concert
is the last of the Concert Series pro
grams for the 1936-37 season.
