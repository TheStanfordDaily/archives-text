# EARTHQUAKE IN MEXICO
## 
### 
CHILPANCINCO, Guerrero, Mex
ico, April 6 (UP). —A sharp earth
quake jarred this city at 2:20 p.m.
yesterday, cracking the walls in
some buildings and frightening the
populace.
