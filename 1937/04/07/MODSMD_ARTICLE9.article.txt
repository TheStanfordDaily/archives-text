# Prom Bid Sale Hits New High
## 
### 
Approximately half the
available tickets to the Senior
Prom Friday night had been
sold when the A.S.S.U. office
closed up shop yesterday, ac
cording to official reports.
A new record high in Stan
ford ticket sales was reached
Monday when 150 Prom bids
were handed over the counter
at $2.50 per copy. Tickets
will be sold at the Pavilion
door Friday night only if
there is a supply remaining
from advance sales.
The Senior Prom this year
is informal, and open to all
students.
