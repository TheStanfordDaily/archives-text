# ARMSTRONG WINS BOUT
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 7 (UP). —
Henry Armstrong, 139, Los Angeles
negro, today hung up the prize ring
scalp of Pete Degrasse, 139 Va, Mo
hawk Indian, but it took him 10
rounds of a savage bout at the
Olympic Auditorium last night to
get it.
