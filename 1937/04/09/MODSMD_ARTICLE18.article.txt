# French Movie Hit Will Be Presented
## 
### 
"Le Bonheur," a recent success
on the French screen, will be pre
sented by Pi Delta Phi Wednesday
night, April 14. at 8 o'clock in the
Assembly Hall. The picture stars
Charles Boyer, who had the male
lead in "Garden of Allah," and
Gaby Norlay, popular Parisian act
ress.
The performance is part of a
program to finance a new library
for the French house, previously
Manzanita, on Lasuen. There are
at present 14 girls in the house, of
which Miss Lucy Lataillade is di
rector.
