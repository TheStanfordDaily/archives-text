# Speakers' Bureau To Meet Tuesday
## 
### 
Speakers' Bureau will hold a re
view board Tuesday, April 13, at 1
o'clock in Room 2(53, Engineering
Corner, Interested students should
see Chuck Schafer, manager, Mon
day in Room 14, Old Union, between
1 and 3 o'clock.

sent its sixth speaker to conduct an
open forum sponsored by the
Board of Education in San Fran
cisco. Benjamin Bock conducted
his third meeting on the topic,
"What's Happening in Europe To
day." Hal Fulkerson and Marco
Thorne have also conducted for
ums.
