# TEXTILE CONFERENCE
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 8 (U.R)

President Roosevelt tonight assured
200 delegates to the world textile
conference that in seeking a uni
versal 40-hour work week they are
proceeding in the direction of better
social conditions.
Rutgers University has a campus
run by students enrolled in the
money and banking courses which
lends money to students at low rates
of interest.
