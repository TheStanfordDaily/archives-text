# Mann Will Speak At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Devoting most of his talk to "Col
leges and University Set-ups of
Australia," C. A. Mann will speak
to the Luncheon Club this noon.
Mr. Mann will draw on his observa
tions of the United States for com
parison wtih his native country,
Australia, for his lecture.
He is a graduate student at Stan
ford, has spent some time studying
conditions in the Fiji Islands as well
as studying educational set-up in
Australia.
The Luncheon Club will meet as
usual in the lounge room of the
Women's Clubhouse at 12 o'clock.
All students are cordially invited;
admission will be 25 cents.
