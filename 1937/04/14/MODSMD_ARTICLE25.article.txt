# Seniors Requested To Order Programs
## 
### 
Upperclassmen expecting to grad
uate in June are urged to place their
orders for commencement programs
and announcements as soon as pos
sible, Bob Lake, program committee
co-chairman, declared last night.
The announcements are engraved
and formal, and the programs are
bound in white leather, with em
bossed lettering and the Stanford
crest on the cover. Costs of the ma
terials are 10 cents and $1, respec
tively. Orders may be placed at the
A.S.S.U. office.
