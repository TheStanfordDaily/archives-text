# MARCHBANK HANDICAP
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 13 (UP).
—Four days away, the $10,000 add
ed Marchbank Handicap today com
mands the center of attention at
Tanforan. The closing mile and one
eighth feature of the 25-day spring
meeting is already the subject of
heated discussion by turfmen and
the arguments won't end until the
winner steps into the charmed circle
late Saturday afternoon.
