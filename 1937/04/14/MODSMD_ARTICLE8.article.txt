# Speakers Bureau Approves 2 Talks
## 
### 
Speakers' Bureau, in starting its
new policy of approving its speakers
in spring quarter in preparation of
the following years, approved two
speakers yesterday.
Howard Hays, talking on "Fur,"
and Konrod Krous Ralph, speaking
on "Making Earthquakes Useful,"
were approved by the Speakers' Bu
reau.
If enough speakers are ready with
speeches or are preparing speeches,
the bureau will hold another Re
view Board next week. Those who
are intending to do so should see
Chuck Schafer, bureau manager, in
his office in Room 14, Old Union.
