# Christian Scientists Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Christian Science Organization
will hold its regular testimonial
meeting at 7:30 o'clock tonight on
the third floor of the Women's Club
house.
There will be a special meeting
for members afterwards. Coming
elections and the new reading room,
situated in Room 15 of the Old Un
ion, will be discussed.
