# CANINE POSTMAN
## 
### 
PROVINCETOWN, Mass., April 14
(U.R).—Bonnie, a docker spaniel, is
Louis Law's personal "letter carrier."
Bonnie carries mail from the post
office to Law's office several blocks
away.
