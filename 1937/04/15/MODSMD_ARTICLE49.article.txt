# Tennis Club Opens Women's Matches
## 
### 
Entries for the annual Stanford
women's tennis club will compete
with players from the University of
California and from Mills in the
annual spring Triangular Tennis
Day this Saturday, April 17, on the
Cal courts.
Players from Stanford will be
Betty Pearce, Patsy Kennedy,
Shu'y Huang, Jane Le Cutler, Kay
Moran, Anne Ellenwood, Barbro
Biorkman, Sally Taber, Betty Little.
Newly elected members of the ten
nis club Barbara Murphy, Jane
Eaton, and Anne Guarnette will
also compete.
