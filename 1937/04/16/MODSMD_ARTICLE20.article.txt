# Chimney Sparks Cause House Fire
## 
### 
Chimney sparks were named as
the cause of a small blaze which
partially burned the roof of the
home of Professor William Manning,
H49 Alvarado. early yesterday af
ternoon.
Estimated damages were about
$200 to $300, according to Chief Wil
liam Clemo, of the Palo Alto Fire
Department, who was in charge.
Most of the damage, he said, was
done by the water.
