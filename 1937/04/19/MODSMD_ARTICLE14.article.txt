# Y.W.C.A. Speaker To Talk on China
## 
### 
Miss Maude Russell will speak at
4 o'clock today in the Y.W.C.A. room
of the Women's Clubhouse. "Stu
dent Movement in China" will be
her subject.
Miss Russell has been a Y.W.C.A.
secretary in Shanghai, having lived
in China for nine years. She is quite
experienced in Chinese politics and
thoughts. At present she is visiting
many West Coast colleges. This
meeting is open to the public.
