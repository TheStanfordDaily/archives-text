# Bible Plays, Talk To Be Given Today
## 
### 
Beniamino Bufano, famous sculp
tor, will lecture on the "Influence of
the Bible on the Arts" at 4:15 this
afternoon in the Ail Gallery. This
is the third in a series of lectures
being given under the direction of
Professor Schapiro in connection
with the Bible exhibit in the Art
Gallery.
At 8 o'clock thus evening two plays
dealing with Biblical characters,
"Lazarus Laughs" and "Mrs. Noah
Gives the Sign," will be presented
under the sponsorship of Professor
Schapiro.
