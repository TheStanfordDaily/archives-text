# Explorer To Talk Here on Arabia
## 
### 
"Skyscrapers of the Desert" will
be the topic on which Hans Helfritz,
noted explorer, will speak in the
Little Theater at 7:30 tomorrow
night.
The German author and traveler
will describe the ancestors of our
modern buildings which he found in
the heart of the Arabian desert dur
ing his extensive travels. Movies
showing the ancient ruins of the
Babylon civilization are to be used
to illustrate the lecture.
