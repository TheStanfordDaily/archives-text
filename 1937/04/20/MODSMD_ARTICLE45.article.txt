# Card Golfers Enter Final Match Play
## 
### 
Northern California amateur golf
championships will be continued
next Saturday when 36 holes for
the quarter and semi-final rounds
are to be played. Two Card dele
gates, freshman Jack Showalter and
Bob Breyer, remain in the running

rounds.
Saturday Showalter will meet Stu

titlist, while Breyer will play Don
