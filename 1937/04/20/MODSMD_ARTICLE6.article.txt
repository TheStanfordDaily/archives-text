# Dr. Pryor Suffers From Appendicitis
## 
### 
Stricken with a sudden appendi
citis attack, Dr. Helen B. Pryor,
medical adviser of women, was has
tily removed to Palo Alto Hospital
yesterday morning for observation
and a possible operation, according
to her physician, Dr. Charles C.
Sachs.

comfortably, and will undergo X
