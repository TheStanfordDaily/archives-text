# Workers' Meeting Set for Sunday
## 
### 
As a precede to the annual West
ern Workers' Summer School, a
Workers' Education Conference will
be held here Sunday from 2 to 5:30
in the Women's Clubhouse.
The main purpose of the confer
ence, which will be attended by in
dustrial workers from San Fran
cisco and San Jose as well as by
Stanford students and faculty, is to
examine the role of labor in pres
ent-day politics. Reservations for
the Sunday conference should be
made at the Y.W.C.A. rooms, phone
4301, and applications for a scholar
ship for the school this summer
should be made at the same, place
before April 27.
