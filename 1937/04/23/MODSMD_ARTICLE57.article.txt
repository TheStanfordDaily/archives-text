# WYOMING COACH SIGNS
## 
### 
LARAMIE, Wyo., April 22 (UP).
Willard A. (Dutch) Witte signed a
one-year contract today to continue
his activities as football and basket
ball coach and director of athletics
at the University of Wyoming. Sal
ary figures were not revealed.
