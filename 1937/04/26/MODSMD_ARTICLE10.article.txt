# Stanford Review Issued Tomorrow
## 
### 
Leading article in the new edition
of the Illustrated Review, which
goes on sale tomorrow, is entitled
"Memorial Hall First Nights" and
has been written by Hugh Baker.
Feature of the review is the pic
tures of the Stanford - Washington
State basketball game, which have
never been shown here, which ac
company the basketball resume for
the '37 season.
The cover has been done by Tro
Harper and consists of moonlit water
scenes. Also there will be a group
of memorial pictures, and articles on
canning, and a story illustrated by
Howard Brody, Chonicle artist.
