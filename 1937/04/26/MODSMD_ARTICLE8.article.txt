# Applications Open For Marine Camp
## 
### 
Major J. B. Wilson, U.S.M.C., will
visit the campus this afternoon to in
terview applicants for the platoon
leaders class, in the office of the
Professor of Military Science, Room
538, at 1:30 o'clock.
The purpose of the class is to train
college students for commissions in
the Marine Corps Reserve. Train
ing consists of two annual six
weeks periods.
