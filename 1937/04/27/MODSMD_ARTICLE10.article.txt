# Funeral Rites Held For James Weter
## 
### 
Funeral services for James Weter,
A.K.L. history major, killed in an
automobile accident on Pacheco
Pass highway near Los Banos, April
19, were held in Seattle Saturday.
Four other A.K.L.'s: Allen Drury,
Nicolas Broughton, Joseph Ma
loney, and Wallace Jensen, were se
riously injured in the accident when
their car, driven by Weter, went out
of control on a curve and over
turned several times. They are now
in the Merced Mercy Hospital, with
their conditions reported as still
critical.
