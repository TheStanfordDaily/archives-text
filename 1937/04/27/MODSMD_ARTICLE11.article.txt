# Debaters To Start Problem Tourney
## 
### 
Stanford debaters will hold a
problem-solving debate tournament
of their own tomorrow at 4 o'clock
in Room 202, English Coiner.
Those taking debate for credit are
required to attend, any other inter
ested students are invited. Some of
the more experienced debaters will
judge the meet. There will be two
debaters per team, with the first two
analyzing, the second two evaluat
ing, and the rebuttalists summar
izing the arguments in each contest.
