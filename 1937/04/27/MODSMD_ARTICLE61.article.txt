# Sammy Klopstock Injured in Wreck
## 
### 
Sammy Klopstoek, captain of the
1935 Stanford track team, suffered
a broken left arm and internal in
juries last week-end, when he lost
control of the car he was driving
and plunged over an embankment
into a ditch on the Green grade
highway, near San Rafael.
Klopstoek was taken to Ross Gen
eral Hospital after passing motorists
removed him from the wreckage.
