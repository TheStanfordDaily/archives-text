# Rear Admiral Hits Coast Air Defense
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. April 2G <U.R».—
Rear Admiral Arthur B. Cuok, chief
of the Navy Bureau of Aeronautics,
today told the House Naval Affairs
Committee that it probably would
be impossible to stop an air attack
on the Pacific Coast in event of war.
He indorsed a Senate bill per
mitting the joint development of a
naval air base in the San Francisco
area from a site to be ceded to the
Government by Alameda, and Ben
ton Field, transferred to the Navy by
the Army, which adjoins it.
