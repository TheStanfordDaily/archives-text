# Debate Tourney Will Start Today
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity debaters will
pair for a debate tournament among
themselves at 4 o'clock today in
Room 202, English Corner.
The tournament will use the new
problem-solving style started by the
University of Washington. The more
experienced forensic men will judge
the contest. If there is enough in
terest, a consolation bracket will
also be run. Those taking debate for
credit are required to attend, with
those interested invited to partici
pate.
In place of the regular debate
style, the first men on the two-man
teams will analyze the question,
the latter two will evaluate and of
fer solution, with the rebuttalists
summarizing the material presented.
