# Council Discusses Code Instruction
## 
### 
At yesterday's Men's Council
meeting, plans were discussed for
giving the freshmen a better under
standing of the Honor Code. Within
two weeks each of this year's fresh
men will have been notified of time
and place to meet with a member of
Men's Council for discussion. The
groups will contain about 20 frosh.
Largest fine of the afternoon was
$5 with $5 suspended for speeding
at 50 miles an hour in front of the
B.A.C.
