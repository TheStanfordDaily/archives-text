# Coed Pistol Team To Be Organized
## 
### 
A pistol team lor women is now
being organized in the Stanford Rifle
and Pistol Club, under the direc
tion of Rita Szekeres, captain.
Members of the women's rifle team
meet at the range every Monday at
8:30 and Thursday at 7:150 o'clock to
practice for coming postal matches
with teams of Eastern colleges.
Women interested in shooting are
invited to attend.
