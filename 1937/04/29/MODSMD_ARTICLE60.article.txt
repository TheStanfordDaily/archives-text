# Sane Democracy To Be Panel Topic
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, honorary edu
cational society, will present an
open panel discussion on the ques
tion, "How can we safeguard dem
ocracy in light of changing economic
conditions?" in the Women's Club
house tonight at 7:30 o'clock.
The speakers for the evening will
be Dr. Harold Hand and Dr. W. V.
Kaulfers, both of the education de
partment, Miss Alice Bodle, Eliza
beth Lam, and student members of
the society. Miss Louise Price will
be chairman. Meeting will be open
and guests are invited.
