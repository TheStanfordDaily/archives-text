# Convalescent Home Movie To Play Today
##

Ernie Nevers and Keith Topping, !
two of Stanford's most famous grid
men. are extras in "Robin Hood of
El Dorado," which will be shown at
the Varsity Theater at 3 o'clock this
afternoon, the proceeds to go to the
Convalescent Home Drive.
Nevers was Pop Warner's "great
est fullback." He was chosen all
time all-American. Topping, one
of the Vowing Sophs, was half of the |
famous Moscrip - Topping combina- j
tion.
With a Mickey Mouse cartoon I
rounding out the program, admis- |
sion will be 30 and 40 cents. Tick- |
ets are on sale at the A.S.S.U. office.
