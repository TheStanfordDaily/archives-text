# S.F. Barn Dance Will Benefit Home
## 
### 
Sponsored by the San Francisco
Junior Auxiliary of the Stanford
Convalescent Home, the annual Barn
Dance will be held this coming Sat
urday, May 1. The place, Palace
Hotel in the city;" music by Paul
Pendarvis.
As the affair is to be a barn
dance, farmers' clothes will be the
dominating costumes. Bids are $2
per person.
