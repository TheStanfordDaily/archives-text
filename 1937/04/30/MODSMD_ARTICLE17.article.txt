# Y.W.C.A. To Hold Open House Today
## 
### 
Stanford's Y.W.C.A. is holding an
open house for all Stanford students
from 1 to 5:30 this afternoon at the
Pcace Exhibit in the Fraser Build
ing. 520 Emerson Street, Palo Alio.
Tea will be served at 3 o'clock.
The Peace Exhibit is being spon
sored by the local branch of the
Women's International League for
Peace and Freedom, headed by Mrs.
Frank J. Frost. Anti-war literature
from more than 35 national peace
organizations is on display at the
exhibit, which is open from 9 to 5
daily, ending tomorrow.
