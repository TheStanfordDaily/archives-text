# Prominent Editor To Speak Monday
## 
### 
Associate Editor of the Readers Di
gest, Barclay Acheson will speak
at a luncheon sponsored by Sigma
Delta Chi and Theta Sigma Phi,
men's and women's national profes
sional journalism fraternities, to be
held in the Union Alcove Monday at
12:15 o'clock.
In addition to having been closely
associated with the Readers Digest
since its first appearance 15 years
ago, Mr. Acheson is widely known
for his work in Near East Relief
operations.

There have been nine Episcopalian
presidents of the United States.
