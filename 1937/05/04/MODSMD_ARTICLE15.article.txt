# FRENCH READINGS
## 
### 
Andre Ferrier, noted for his work
with the French theater in San Fran
cisco, will lead a French "Theatre
et Poesic" program here tomorrow
at 4:15, under the auspices of the
Romanic Languages department.
Mr. Ferrier will include recitations
of old and modem French poetry on
the program. The affair is open to
the public.
