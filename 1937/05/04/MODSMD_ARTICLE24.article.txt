# Patrons Selected For Junior Prom
## 
### 
Five facility couples were an
nounced yesterday as patrons and
patronesses for the Junior Prom
May 14 by Wayne Richardson,
prom chairman.
Professor and Mrs. Elmer D.
Fagan, Colonel and Mrs. Bernard R.
Peyton, Instructor and Mrs. Robert
P. Terrill, Mr. and Mrs. Hawkins,
and Professor Theodore Boggs have
accepted the committee's invita
tions. Bids, priced at $3.50, are on
sale at the A.S.S.U. office.
The Prom will feature the piano
novelties and orchestra of Vincent
Lopez, currently the maestro of
New York's Waldorf-Astoria Hotel
and of Grace Moore's national radio
program. He will bring his band here
a week from Friday for its first
Northern California appearance.
