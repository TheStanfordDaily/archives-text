# Student-Professor Evening Is Planned
## 
### 
Third in a series of "Sunday
Nights with the Profs" will be held
next Sunday evening at the home of
Professor and Mrs. David Harris,
705 Alvarado Street, from 7:30 to
9 o'clock.
Three students may still sign up
at the A.S.S.U. office to attend. El
len Davis is in charge of the affair,
which is sponsored by the Student-
Faculty Relations committee to pro
mote associations between students
and professors.
