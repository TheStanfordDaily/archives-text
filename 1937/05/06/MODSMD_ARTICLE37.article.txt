# CITIZEN SHOWS COPS
## 
### 
EXCELSIOR SPRINGS, Mo., May
5 (U.R).—Marion Ballew waited until
several policemen had emptied their
guns in a futile attempt to stop a
fleeing negro burglar. Then he shied
a rock at the fugitive. The rock hit
the negro squarely on the head,
kayoing him.
