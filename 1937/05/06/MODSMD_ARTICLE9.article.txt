# Editor of Women's Press Will Speak
## 
### 
Rhoda E. McCulloch, editor of the
Women's Press, will speak on the
international aspects of the Y.W.C.A.
program at a luncheon next Monday
at the Palo Alto Community Center.
Sponsored by the Y.W.C.A. advisory
board, the luncheon will be 75 cents.
Reservations may be made at the
Stanford "Y" rooms, phone 4301.
Miss McCulloch, who has been
with the National Board of the Y. W.
C. A. since 1913, recently returned
from the Orient, whore she attended
a conference of world "Y" leaders
from 50 countries. She is associated
with a number of organizations in
terested in international affairs and
world peace.
