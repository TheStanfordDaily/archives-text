# RUMOR OF BIG IMPORT DANCE ALARMS "500"
## 
### 
Jan. 23, 1918
Surreptitious murmors about the
campus have it that members of the
"500" are becoming perturbed over
the possibilities of a certain social
event which rumor reports is to come
off in the near future. The function j
is to be nothing less than an "Import
Dance," whose avowed purpose is to
equalize campus conditions, and ;
which is reported to have the backing
of numerous prominent campus
characters of the male sex.
Hie idea is to stage a dance at
which the ladies attending are en
tirely from off the campus.
