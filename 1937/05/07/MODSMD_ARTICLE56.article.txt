# HOOVER ELECTED
## 
### 
April 24, 1894.
Herbert C. Hoover was elected
student body treasurer for next year.
His home is in Salem, Ore. He en
tered the University with the Class
of 95, and is biking geology as a
major. He is present treasurer of
his class.
