# SAFECRACKER NEEDED!
## 
### 
BEND, Ore. (UP). —An emergency
call for safecrackers was necessary
here when two new safes were in
stalled in .the offices of the county
clerk and county treasurer. While
the officials were away for the
week-end, the safes were installed.
Safe company workmen forgot to
leave the combination.
