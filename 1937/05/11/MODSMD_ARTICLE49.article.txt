# Rifle Club Meets To Elect Officers
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Rifle and
Pistol Club, both men's and women's
teams, will meet at the rifle range to
morrow night at 8:!50 o'clock to elect
new officers.
The Pistol Team shot against the
Universty of Arizona last Wednes
day in a postal match. Results of
the opponents' scores have not yet
been received.
