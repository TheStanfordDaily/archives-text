# DEMOCRATIC TRIPLETS
## 
### 
LONG VIEW. May 11 (UP).—The
triplet boys born Saturday to Mrs.
Lilie Mae Jones, a negro, today were
named Franklin, Delano and Roose
velt.

MOSCOW. Ida., May 11 (U.R).—
The University of Idaho track team,
led by Coach Mike Ryan, was en
route to California today to compete
in the West Coast Relay events at
Fresno. The Idaho team was the first
northern college to enter the meet.
