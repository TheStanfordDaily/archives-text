# War Admiral to Try For Double Record
## 
### 
BALTIMORE. Md„ May 11 (U.R).
—War Admiral today led the advance
brigade to Pimlico race track where,
on Saturday, the son of Man o' War
will try to become the sixth horse
to win both the Kentucky Derby and
the Preakness.
War Admiral will be an odds-on
favorite. Should he win, he will
move up into the circle of SlOO.OOO
winners. The Man o' War colt earned
$52,575 Saturday, bringing his earn
ings to $75,800.
Eighty-four per cent of the hog
slaughter in Denmark is in co-op
erative packing plants.
