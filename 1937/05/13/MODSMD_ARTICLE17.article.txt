# Speakers' Bureau To Hold Tryouts
## 
### 
Speakers' Bureau will hold its last
Review Board of the year next Tues
day at 1 o'clock in Room 268, Engi
neering Corner. Applicants have
been asked to see Chuck Schafer
immediately to arrange a time.
"It is highly important to join the
bureau this year," Schafer declared,
"as the list which will be sent to or
ganizations this fall will contain
only names of those people who have
joined this quarter or previously."
Students desiring to talk for the
bureau, although they have no
speech definitely written, can ob
tain aid from Schafer in his office,
Room 14, Old Union, from 1 to 11
o'clock in the afternoon.
