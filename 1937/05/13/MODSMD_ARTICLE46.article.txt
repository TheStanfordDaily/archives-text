# Organist Will Give Program in Chapel
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give a concert in the
chapel at 4:15 o'clock today. "Pomp
and Circumstance," a military march
by Edward Elgar, will open the pro
gram.
Other selections will be "Lento"
from "London Symphony" (Vaughn-
Williams), "Vive le Roi" (Raison),
"Air and Minuet" from "Water Mu
sic" (Handel), and "A Prayer for
Peace" (Held).
Another concert will be given
Sunday by Raymond Kendall, as
sistant organist, with the Tamalpais
Union High School string quartet,
directed by Raymond Pittenger.
