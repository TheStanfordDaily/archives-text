# Nippon Leaders Tour Campus
## Delegates of Economics Mission To Visit U.S. 
### 
Stanford University played host
to the Japanese Economic Mission,
a group of Nippon business leaders
who are .starting a tour of the United
States, yesterday afternoon.
Mr. Ira S. Lillick, member of the
Board of Trustees, and Mrs. Lillick
entertained the party at tea in the
Stanford Union, after the visitors
had been conducted around the cam
pus.
President Ray Lyman Wilbur was
on the San Francisco reception com
mittee for the visitors along, with
Robert Gordon Sproul, president of
Ihe University of California; Leland
W. Cutler, president of the Board of
Trustees; and Chester H. Rowell,
editor of the San Francisco Chron
icle.
Purpose of the Mission is to cement
business relationships between the
United States and Japan. The dele
gation will continue from San Fran
cisco to Los Angeles, then on to
Chicago and New York via New
Orleans. The delegates will contact
business and financial leaders in view
of improving Nippon-American re
lations.
