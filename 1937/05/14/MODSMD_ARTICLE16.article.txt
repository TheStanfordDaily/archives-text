# Tamalpais Quartet Plays Here Sunday
## 
### 
The Tamalpais Union High School
string quartet will play at the Sun
day afternoon concert at 4 o'clock in
the Memorial Church. Dr. True
blood will preach the regular Sun
day sermon at 11 o'clock.
The quartet under the direction of
Raymond Piltenger, will be accom
panied by Raymond Kendall, Uni
versity organist. Kendall will also
play selections from Bach, Mozart,
Tschaikowsky, and Cesar Franck.
