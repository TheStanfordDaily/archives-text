# Villanova Gridders Will Meet Loyola
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 13 (UP). —
Loyola University of Los Angeles
today announced Villanova Univer
sity of Philadelphia had been signed
to a one-game football contract for
this fall.
The Villanova team will meet Loy
ola here on November 28 in Gilmore
Stadium, Graduate Manager Luddy
Bremner announced. Villanova is
coached by "Clipper" Smith, former
Santa Clara mentor, while Loyola is
coached by Tom Lieb. Both are
former Notre Dame players.
