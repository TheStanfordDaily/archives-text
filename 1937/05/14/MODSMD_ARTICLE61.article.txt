# Diz Will Conduct Baseball School
## 
### 
%
PHILADELPHIA, May 13 (U.R).—
' Jimmy Wilson, manager of the
Philadelphia Phillies, and Dizzy
Dean, St. Louis Cardinal pitcher,
have signed a contract to conduct a
baseball school next year, it was
1 announced today.
