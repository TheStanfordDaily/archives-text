# Two Injured In Weekend Auto Wrecks
## 
### 
Phil Zonne, Deke sophomore and
basketball star, sustained minor in
juries early yesterday morning in an
automobile accident on the Bay
shore highway near San Francisco.
He was taken to the Palo Alto
Hospital, where it was reported that
his condition is not serious. He is
suffering from head and knee inju
ries in addition to being shaken up
badly.
Ben Day, Delt junior, missed a
turn on the Portola road a quarter
mile west of the campus at 12:30
yesterday morning and smashed his
car, escaping without serious in
jury, according to reports from the
state highway patrol. He and a
companion suffered from cuts and
bruises, and the car was reported to
be a complete wreck.
