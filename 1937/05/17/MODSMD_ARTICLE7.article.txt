# Japanese Netmen Down Farm Team
## 
### 
After outclassing their Japanese
opponents in the first two sets of
the Pacific Coast men's tournament
at Santa Barbara yesterday, Stan
ford's Bob Underwood and Bob
Pommer were defeated in three
successive sets, United Press re
ported.
The victory gave the Nippon Davis
Cup team, Capt. Jiro Yamagishi
and Sumatero Nakemo, their first
victory in their long American tour.
The score: 5-7, 2-0, 0-4, 11-9, 6-3
for the Japanese.
