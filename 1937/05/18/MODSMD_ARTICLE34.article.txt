# MONK' PLAYS SOFTBALL
## 
### 
"Monk" Moscrip, member of the
"Vowing Sophs" football team, has
turned his athletic ability to soft
ball. He made his debut Wednesday
night at Redwood City on the Mont
gomery Ward team. Though he failed
to garner a hit, "Monk" made four
putouts and three assists without an
error.
