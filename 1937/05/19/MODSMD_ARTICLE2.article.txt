# Glee Club To Install Five New Officers
## 
### 
New officers for the Stanford Glee
Club will be installed at the glee
club picnic on May 28. Men singers
and the Women's Glee Club will pic
nic together at Scarsville Lake.
New officers are: Charles Runner,
president; Hugh Cramer, vice-presi
dent; George Tyler, manager; Oliver
Cope, secretary; Scott Rice, librarian.
Carl Reiscnroth will continue his
services as accompanist.
