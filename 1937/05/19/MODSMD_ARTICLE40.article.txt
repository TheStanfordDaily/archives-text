# Grad Team Takes Women's Ball Title
## 
### 
The Killers became women's in
tramural volleyball champions yes
terday after a close 15-13, 16-14 vic
tory over the Alpha Phis on the
Women's Gym courts. Aileen Nor
ton, captain of the winning team,
which is composed of graduate wom
en and gym instructors, will receive
the W.A.A. volleyball plaque on
Field Day, June 2.
Starring for the Killers in yester
day's game were Shu-y Huang and
Aileen Norton, while Jean Welsh and
Ila Lee Ainsworth were outstanding
players for the Alpha Phis.
