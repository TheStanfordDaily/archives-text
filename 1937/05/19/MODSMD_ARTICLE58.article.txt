# Fairclough Gives Advice on Travel
## 
### 
Advising students that the best
way to prepare for a trip to Italy
this summer is to study the history
of the country, Dr. Henry R. Fair
clough. professor emeritus of class
ical literature, last night gave the
third lecture in a series being given
for the benefit of students planning
to travel in Europe this summer.
Next travel talk will be given to
morrow night at 7:30 in the Little
Theater by Professor Philip W.
Buck, political science department,
titled "American Discovery of
England."
"Helpful for traveling in Italy,"
Dr. Fairclough said, "is a moderate
command of Italian, even the
amount which can be learned aboard
ship on the way over."
