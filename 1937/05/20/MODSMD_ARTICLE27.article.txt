# ORGAN RECITAL TODAY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present a recital today at
4:15 in the Memorial Church. The
recital will include: "First Organ
Sonata," by Felix Mendelssohn-Bar
tholdy; "Largo" from the '"Cello So
nata," by Frederic Chopin; "Alle
gro Cantabile," from the Fifth Organ
Symphony by C. M. Widor; "A Rose
Bursts into Bloom," a chorale pre
lude by Johannes Brahms; and "Cho
rale in A Minor," by Cesar Franck.
