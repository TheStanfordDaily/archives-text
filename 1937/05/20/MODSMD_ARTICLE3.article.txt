# English Clubs Sells Yearbook Today
## 
### 
Subscriptions to the English Club
yearbook will be on sale for $1 this
morning at the post office from 8 to
3, according to Margaret Williams,
president of the club.
The book is the annual publication
of the English Club and is composed
entirely of contributions from Stan
ford authors. It will be ready for
distribution about May 27.
