# Lange, Trueblood Will Speak Sunday
## 
### 
Continuing the policy of having
members of the faculty read from
the scriptures at the Sunday serv
ices, Gordon C. Lange, director of
dramatics, will read Sunday at 11
o'clock in the Memorial Church.
Dr. Trueblod will preach the ser
mon, and Raymond Kendall will
have charge of the choir. Among
those professors who have read this
quarter are: Emeritus Professor A. T.
Murray, Professor L. E. Bassett,
Emeritus Professor H. R. Fair
clough, Professor Hardin Craig, and
Professor E. E. Robinson. Raymond
Kendall will present an organ re
cital at 4 o'clock.
