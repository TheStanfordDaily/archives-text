# Sailing Club Elects Lippman President
## 
### 
Frank Lippman was elected presi
dent of the Stanford Sailing Club,
Phil Ward was chosen vice-presi
dent, and Bob Coffin, secretary
treasurer at a meeting Wednesday
evening.
On June 18 and 19, Stanford will
enter a team in intercollegiate com
petition at San Pedro when the
annual Pacific Coast Intercollegiate
Regatta will be held in six-meter
yachts.
