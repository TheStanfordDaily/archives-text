# Has To See for Himself
## 
### 

EVERY TIME Phil Levy makes a discus throw he takes time out
to run down the hold to see how far he threw it, not content with
what the judges tell him. Stanford varsity man last year. Levy will
he throwing for the Olympic Club in the P.A. meet here tomorrow.
