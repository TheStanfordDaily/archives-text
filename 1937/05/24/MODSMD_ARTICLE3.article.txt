# Songs from 'Down Hatch' Sell on Quad
## 
### 
Remaining copies of the hit songs
from "Down the Hatch!" printed in
booklet form, complete with words
and music, will be sold on Quad to
day and tomorrow from 10 to 2
o'clock for 25 cents.
Among the songs published arc:
"I've Got the Devil to Thank for
You," "Nice Night for It Isn't It?"
"Your Eyes Have It," "Love in a
Mist." 'I Gotta Take Steps," and
"Down the Hatch!"
