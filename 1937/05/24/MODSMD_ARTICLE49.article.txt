# Explorer To Give Illustrated Talk
## 
### 
Captain George S. Clements, Arctic
explorer, will give a travel lecture
on the northern regions and a mo
tion picture titled, "On Top of the
World" tomorrow night at 7:30
o'clock in the Little Theater.
The picture, filled with scenes of
adventure in the Arctic, covers
12,000 miles. It is the picture rec
ord of a journey beginning in Se
attle and ending in Siberia and the
Seal Islands. It shows the life of the
Eskimos in Alaska, picturesque
scenes there, and a few scenes tak
en within 300 miles of the North
Pole.
Wednesday evening at 7:30 in the
Little Theater, the lecture series of
tours in Europe will be continued
with a travel talk on Germany.
