# Dance Drama Set For Tomorrow
## 
### 
Orchesis, women's honorary danc
ing society, will present its annual
dance drama tomorrow night at 8
o'clock in the dance studio of the
Women's Gym.
No special theme has been select
ed for this year's production but a
demonstration of principles of tech
nique and composition will be fea
tured.
Creation of contemporary dance
will be explained by Mrs. Ruth
Radir, dancing instructor.
Music for two of the numbers has
been arranged by Jean Hiler. For
the specialty dance "On the Cam
pus,'' Ralph Keating's composition,
"Redskin," will be used.
