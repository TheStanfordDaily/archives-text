# 'HATCH' SONGS ON SALE
## 
### 
Six of the hit songs from "Down
flic Hatch" have been printed and
will be on sale today from 10 to 2
o'clock on the Quad. Included in the
sheet are "Nice Night for It, Isn't It?",
"Down the Hatch," "I've Got the
Devil to Thank for You," "I Gotta
Take Steps," "Your Eyes Have It,"
and "You Conquered My Heart."
