# SWIM RECORDS SET
## 
### 
LOD», May 24 (UP). — Dorothy
Sundby of San Francisco broke her
own record and set a new Pacific-
Coast A.A.U. 100-yard free-style
swimming record of 1 minute, 2.2
seconds in a meet at the Lodi swim
ming pool yesterday.
