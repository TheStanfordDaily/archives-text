# Funeral Kites Held For Farm Grad
## 
### 
Funeral services were held yes
terday afternoon at the New Post
Chapel of the Presidio, San Fran
cisco, for Second Lieutenant Doug
las W. Smith, who died at March
Field Friday from blood poisoning.
Lieutenant Smith was graduated
from Stanford in 1933, and was a
member of El Cuadro. He was mar
ried in the Memorial Church this
spring to Rosemary Clarke, '33, and
a member of Chi Omega.
