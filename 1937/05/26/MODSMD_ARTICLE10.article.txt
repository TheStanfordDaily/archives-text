# Wilbur To Present Circle S's Tonight
## 
### 
Minor sports awards for the cur
rent year will be presented tonight
at a Circle S banquet to be given at
the Deke house at 6:15 o'clock.
The awards will be given to the
outstanding person in each minor
sport during the last year by Presi
dent Ray Lyman Wilbur. At the
same time the Tunney awards will
be presented to the outstanding box
er in each weight division.
