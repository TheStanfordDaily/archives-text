# Helen Jacobs Wins In French Match
## 
### 
PARIS, May 25 (U.R).—Helen Ja
cobs of Berkeley, Calif., advanced
to the third round of the French
hard courts tennis championships
yesterday, on a 6-1, 3-6, 6-1 victory
over Simone Barbier of France.
Charles Harris of West Palm Beach
: was eliminated in a third-round
match of the men's division by Yvon
1 Petra of France. The score was 7-5,
| 8-6, 3-6, 6-2.
