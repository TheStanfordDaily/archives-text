# Rockefeller To Be Buried Tomorrow
## 
### 
TARRYTOWN, N.Y., May 25 (UP)
—The body of John D. Rockefeller
comes home today to the hills above
Sleepy Hollow where members of
his family waited to receive it in
the great hall of his 5000-acre es
tate.
Funeral services as simple as his
life during his last few years will
be at I) a.m. (C.S.T.) tomorrow for
the man who earned $1,000,000,000
and gave away $750,000,000.
