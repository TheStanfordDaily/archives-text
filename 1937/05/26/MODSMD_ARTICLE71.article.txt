# Spanish Play Set For Friday Night
## 
### 
Two-act Spanish play, "Asi Se Es
cribe la Historia," will be presented
at 8:30 Friday night in the Little
Theater. Admission will be 10 cents;
the action of the play is such that
both Spanish - speaking and non-
Spanish-speaking students will en
joy it.
The principal players, the majority
of whom have lived in South Amer
ica, are: Jack Holterman, Berna
dine McKay, Barbara Harmon, He
lene Levin, Ben Maxson, William
Moellering, and Edwin Woods.
"Asi Se Escribe la Historia" came
from pens of the Quintero Brothers,
modern Spanish playwrights, and is
directed by Professor Reid of the
Spanish Department.
