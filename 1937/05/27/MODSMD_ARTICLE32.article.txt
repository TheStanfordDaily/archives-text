# Three Research Awards Granted
## 
### 
Three Stanford men were award
ed fellowships by the Social Science
Research Council. The amounts
range from $1000 to $2500 for ad
vanced research.
Dwight L. Palmer, Ph.D., will
study British research methods for
analyzing workers' demands in the
furthering of economic control and
security. His work will take him to
England. Edward Stone Shaw, as
sistant professor of economics at
Stanford, will study governmental
intervention in investment process
in England and Switzerland.
The third recipient of a grant-in
aid is John Thomas Dunlop, eco
nomics, who will study wage rigidi
ties in England and the United
States.
