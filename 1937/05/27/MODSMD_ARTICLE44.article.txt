# ANGELS OUTSLUG SOLONS
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 26 (U.R).—
Sacramento's Solons, second place
club in the Pacific Coast League pen
nant chase, dropped a slugging match
to Los Angeles, 0-6, here last night.
Two large clusters of runs in the
fifth and the sixth innings—four in
each—won for the Angels.
