# Texas, S.C. Settle Feud over Subsidy
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 26 (UP*).—
An athletic feud between Universi
ties of Texas and Southern Califor
nia sputtered out today when four
Texas freshmen denied reports that
U.S.C. offered $100 a month for a
good athlete to come West.
The dispute involved charges
printed by Joe Beldon, sports edi
tor of the Texas campus newspaper.
