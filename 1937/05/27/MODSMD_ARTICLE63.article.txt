# Death from Spinal Meningitis Probed
## 
### 
REDWOOD CITY, May 26 (U.R).—
Nine high school students here were
under observation by San Mateo
County health officials today fol
lowing the death of Donald R. Sneed
from spinal meningitis.
The school swimming tank was
ordered disinfected and authorities
took other precautions against spread
of the disease.
