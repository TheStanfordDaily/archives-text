# Tennis
##

Sigma Nu won the all-University
Intramural tennis championship yes
terday by defeating Chi Psi in the
I final round. Bob Helmholz of Chi
j Psi defeated Bob Harper 6-2, 6-3;
: Ed Proctor of Sigma Nu defeated
j Howard Clark 6-3, 4-6, 6-4; and
Proctor and Harper defeated Clark
and Helmholz 6-4, 6-1 in the doubles
match which decided the title.
