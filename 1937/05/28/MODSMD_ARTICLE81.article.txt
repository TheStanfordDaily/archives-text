# Seniors' Tickets Available June 4
## 
### 
Tickets for Baccalaureate and
Commencement will bo ready for
distribution on Friday, June 4. At
that time seniors may procure their
allotted two for Baccalaureate which
will be held in the Chapel and three
for Commencement in the Memorial
Amphitheater.
The amphitheater will be divided
into reserved and unreserved sec
tions and tickets will be necessary
for admission to both sections. Sen
iors may apply for additional tick
ets in the unreserved section on
June 4. They will be distributed on
Monday, June 7.
In cases of necessity, seniors may
obtain extra reserved seats for rela
tives and friends at the time of ap
plication for unreserved scats.
