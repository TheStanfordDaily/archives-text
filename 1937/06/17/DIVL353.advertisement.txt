Thursday, June 17

Hoikl : England's First Teacher of the Mathematical
Sciences."

"Vou Can't Take It with You," by Mrs. Guy Stevens Earring
ton. Little Theatre in Memorial Hall.
Wednesday, June 23
7 :30 p.m. Early Motion Pictures. Series on 'The Rise pf the Ameri
can Motion Picture." Sponsored by the Education Club. Little
Theatre (Room 100). Tickets, $1.00 for any three of five
programs.
4:15 p.m. Program of Recorded Music. Choir Room, Memorial
Church. The public is invited.
COMING EVENTS
Thursday, June 24
