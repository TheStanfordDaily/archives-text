# B.C. Economist Will Teach Here
## 
### 
Professor Henry Forbes Angus,
executive head of the department of
economics, political science, sociol
ogy, and commerce at the University
of British Columbia, will be among
the many visiting professors at Stan
ford during the summer quarter.
Professor Angus, as acting profes
sor of economics and political sci
ence, will offer a course in "Eco
nomic and Political Problems of the
Pacific Area" in the Department of
Economics, and "The British Em
pire" in the Department of Political
Science.
Professor Angus has the Bache
lor's degree from McGill University
and two degrees from Oxford Uni
versity. He has been at the Univer
sity of British Columbia since 1919.
In addition to the course to be
given by Professor Angus, the De
partment of Economics will offer this
summer "Elementary Economics,"
"Business Cycles," "Labor and Gov
ernment," "Contemporary Economic
Problems," and "History of Eco
nomic Thought."
The Department of Political Sci
ence will give "Government in Its
Relation to Business," "Political
Parties," "The American Constitu
tion," and "Public Management."
