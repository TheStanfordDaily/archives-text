# Former President Returns to Campus
## 
### 
Herbert C. Hoover, former Presi
dent of the United States, returned
to his campus home last night after
a short trip to the East.
Accompanied by his son and
daughter-in-law, Mr. and Mrs. Al
len Hoover, the ex-President left
Reno early yesterday morning by
automobile after staying in that city
overnight.
Vinnie Ream, young Washington
sculptor, was the first woman to re
ceive a federal art commission. She
executed the statue of Lincoln in
the rotunda of the Capitol under
this commission.
