# Noted Professors To Lecture at Cal
## 
### 
A series of summer session lec
tures by eminent authorities on the
Far East was announced recently by
International House at the Univer
sity of California.
Lecturers will include Arthur W.
Hummell, Congressional Library,
Washington, D.C.; Ferdinand D.
Lessing, University of California; D.
Willard Lyon, Society for Oriental
Studies, Claremont; Henry P. Eames,
Scripps College, Jaroslav Prusek,
Oriental Institute, Prague; Langdon
Warner, Harvard University; Galen
Fisher, Institute of Pacific Relations;
and Shao Chang Lee, University of
Hawaii.
