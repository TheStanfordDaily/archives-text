# 'Hot Spot' Opens Informally Tonight
## 
### 
In an unofficial opening of the
Nitery for summer quarter an infor
mal jolly-up and get-acquainted
party is being held at 7 o'clock to
night.
The party will end officially at 8
o'clock, but the Nitery remains open
until 10, and all students wishing to
stay may do so. The Nitery, opened
last quarter as a result of a Daily
campaign to get some place on the
campus where students could gather
to dance, relax, or play bridge, is
located in the old Union building.
