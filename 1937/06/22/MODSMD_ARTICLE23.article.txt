# Music Recordings Begin Tomorrow
## 
### 
Opportunities for hearing record
ings of musical masterpieces this
summer are to be offered weekly
under the auspices of the Music Di
vision. The first concert will be
given tomorrow afternoon at 4:15
in the choir room of the Memorial
Church.
To be featured this week are
Brahms' Third Symphony in F ma
jor, a woodwind Prelude and Fugue
by Pierne, Mozart's Overture to The
Marriage of Figaro, a piano duet of
Borodin's Dances, and Paul White
man's medley of song hits of the
past decade.
