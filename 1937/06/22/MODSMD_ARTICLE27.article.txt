# Angus To Speak On Changes in Pacific
## 
### 
H. F. Angus, acting professor of
economics and political science, sum
mer quarter, will speak on "Peace
ful Change in the Pacific" Thursday
at 8 p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
The well-known authority, head
of the Department of Economics and
Political Science at the University
of British Columbia, in Vancouver,
8.C., since 1930, will be introduced
by Professor Edwin Angell Cottrell
of the Stanford political science divi
sion.
