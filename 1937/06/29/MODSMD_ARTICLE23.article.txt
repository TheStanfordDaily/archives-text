# Luisetti Takes Job At Catalina Camp
## 
### 
Hank Luisetti, Stanford's all-
American basketball center, left
Saturday for Catalina Island, where
he will be a director at the Catalina
Island boys' camp.
At Catalina, Luisetti will be work
ing for Bill Hunter, director of ath
letics at U.S.C. and Sam Barry, the
Trojan basketball and baseball coach,
who are operating the camp.
