# Cellar, Union Announce Hours
## 
### 
The Union dining room is open
from 12 to 1 p.m. and 6 to 7 p.m. on
week days, and from 12:15 to 1:45
p.m. and 6 to 7 p.m. on Sundays.
Lunches are 50 cents and up; din
ners 55 cents and up. Meal tickets
allowing a 10 per cent saving are on
sale. Miss Christine Ricker and
Mrs. Helen Miller are in charge of
the dining facilities. Anyone wish
ing to arrange for special parties
and menus may do so by phoning
Palo Alto 3136.
From Monday to Thursday inclu
sive the Union Cellar is open from
7:30 to 10:30 p.m. On Friday, Satur
day, and Sunday evenings it closes
at 8 o'clock.
