# P.E. Department To Give New Class
## 
### 
Mrs. Ruth Radir, former member
of the women's physical education
department, announces a course in
Elizabethan Dancing, in conjunction
with the Summer Dramatic Festival.
Glasses in this course, which will be
given for regular academic credit,
will be held in the women's old gym
at 4 o'clock on Mondays and
Thursdays.
The course will include .both
cotirt and folk dancing and will be
open to both men and women. All
those interested in signing up are
asked to attend the first meeting of
the class next Monday.
