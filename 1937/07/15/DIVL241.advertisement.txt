Thursday, July 15
4:15 p.m. Organ Recital, by Mr. Warren D. Allen, University Or
ganist.
8:00 p.m. Lecture on "Americans at Play," by Dr. Jesse Frederick

8 :00 p.m. Dramatic Readings of farces by Lope de Rueda, Hans Sachs,

8:00 p.m. Program of Court Dances of the Sixteenth Century.

mer Drama Festival. Women's Clubhouse. The public is
invited.
