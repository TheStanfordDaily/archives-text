# Pool Will Be Open To Faculty Wives
## 
### 
According to Miss Marie Manchee,
women's swimming instructor, the
Women's Gymnasium pool will be
open Sunday afternoons to faculty
members, their wives and children
from 2:30 to 4:30. Children may
swim only if accompanied by their
parents. There will be no fee
charged for the use of the pool at
these hours. Instruction is offered to
wives of the faculty members at 10
o'clock Mondays, Wednesdays and
Fridays for a fee of $6.50, and to
children of the faculty on the same
days at 11 o'clock for $5.
