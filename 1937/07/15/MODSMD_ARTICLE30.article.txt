# Mrs. Abbie Hurd Dies in Berkeley
## 
### 
Mrs. Abbie Hurd, for many years
manager of Madrono Hall, passed
away Saturday at the home of her
daughter and son-in-law, Mr. and
Mrs. Herbert Knollin, 2640 Durant
Avenue, Berkeley, after an illness
of several months.
Mrs. Hurd moved to Berkeley
and opened a boarding house for
men students near the University of
California campus when Stanford
took over Madrono Hall as a uni
versity operated dormitory.
