# Campus To Dance In Nitery Tonight
## 
### 
The regular Tuesday night Jolly
up, the fifth in the weekly series of
informal dances will be held at 7
o'clock tonight in the Nitery.
Started under the sponsorship of
the Union, the weekly affair has
proved to be one of the most popu
lar social activities of the summer
quarter and is open to all Univer
sity students.
