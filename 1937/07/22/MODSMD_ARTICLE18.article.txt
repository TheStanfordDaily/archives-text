# Pianist to Survey Elizabethan Music
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Tilly, international
ly known concert pianist, will con
duct a brief survey of the early
keyboard music of the Elizabethan
period at 10:30 a.m. Monday at the
home of Mr. and Mrs. Frank J.
Frost of Palo Alto.
This lecture is the first of a series
of informal recitals combined with
musical discussion.
