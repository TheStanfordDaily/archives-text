# Allen Announces Recital Programs
## 
### 
Featuring selections by Schumann,
Lizst, and Bach, Warren D. Allen,
University organist, will present an
organ recital in the Memorial
Church today at 4:15 o'clock.
Sunday's concert will be offered
by Assistant Organist Raymond
Kendall at 7:30 o'clock. "Sonata in
C Minor," by eighteenth century
composer, Francesco Durnate, will
be the first number on the program,
to be followed by "Cantabile" by
Cesar Franck, "Shining Shore" and
"Fugue in C Minor" by Edward S.
Barnes, and "Sortie in F" by Alex
andre Guilmant.
