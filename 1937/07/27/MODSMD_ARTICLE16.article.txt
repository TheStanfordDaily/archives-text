# Museum Curator To Lecture Here
## 
### 
"Elizabeth Craftsmanship" will
be the subject of a lecture by Dr.
Grace McCann Morley, curator of
the San Francisco Museum of Art,
tomorrow afternoon at 4:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
Brought here by Stanford pro
fessors D. M. Mendelowitz and E. M.
Farmer, Dr. Morley will be this
week's Tudor lecturer. She came to
San Francisco from the East, bring
ing with her many innovations and
modern exhibits for the San Fran
cisco Museum.

Of last year's Notre Dame fresh
man football team 110 members were
captains of their high school teams.
