# Little Sister Visits
## 
### 
Shirley Temple, America's pre
miere box office attraction, may
spend a few hours on the Stanford
campus Saturday, according to her
brother, Jack Temple, student here,
before she boards the S. S. Malolo
for Honolulu. Shirley has just fin
ished production on "Wee Willie
Winkie," adapted from the Rudyard
Kipling story of British India.
Jack, a Delta Chi, has been active
in dramatics here both as director
and actor. After graduation at the
end of this quarter he plans to pur
sue a career in the motion picture
industry. He has previously worked
as an assistant director on the Twen
tieth-Century lot.
