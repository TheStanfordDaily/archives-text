# Stanfordites Wed At Ross Church
## 
### 
Joining the list of Stanford sum
mer brides, Miss Annice Belden
was married yesterday to Aylett
Cotton, also of Stanford, at St. John's
Episcopal Church in Ross.
Miss Belden, daughter of Mr. and
Mi's. Charles Belden of Pitchfork,
Wyoming, is a member of Kappa
Kappa Gamma, and received an A.B.
degree in the School of Social Sci
ences this June.
Aylett Cotton is the son of Judge
Aylett Cotton of San Mateo and a
graduate student in law.
