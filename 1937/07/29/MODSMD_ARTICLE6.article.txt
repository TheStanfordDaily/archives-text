# Organist To Offer Franck Program
## 
### 
Drawing from the music of Cesar
Franck, who lived during the years
1822-90, Warren D. Allen, univer
isty organist, will give an organ re
cital in the Memorial Church this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock.
The selections which he will ren
der are: Prelude; Fugue and Varia
tion; Pastorale in E; Cantabile in B;
Grand Piece Symphonique: An
dante serioso, Allegro non troppo,
Andante, Allegretto, Adagio, and
Finale.
