# Stanfordian Weds Sacramento Girl
## 
### 
Dr. Wayne Pollock, former Stan
ford man, was married yesterday af
ternoon at 5 o'clock to Miss Geor
gia Zdkrsky. D. Elton Trueblood
offiicated in the chapel.
The bride is the daughter of Mr.
and Mrs. Frank Zdkrsky of Sacra
mento. The groom, also of Sacra
mento, received his B.S. degree at
Stanford in 1924, and his M.D. from
the Stanford School of Medicine in
1928.
