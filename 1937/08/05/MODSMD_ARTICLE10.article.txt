# Cottrell To Speak On Stanford Hour
## 
### 
"Redirecting General Education in
High School and College" will be
discussed by Dr. Donald Perry Cot
trell, acting professor from Colum
bia University, during the Stanford
Radio Hour on KGO next Monday
night at 8:30.
"The needs of youth, especially
those arising during the period of
approximately ages 16 to 20, are at
present moving high schools and
colleges to extensive reorganization
of their programs of general educa
tion," Dr. Cottrell said yesterday in
discussing his speech.
