Ryan announced a "Friday the Thir
teenth Lucky Dance" open to the
campus free of charge to be held
this Friday at Toyon Hall. Nick
Morresey's St. Francis Yacht Club
Orchestra will play between the
hours of 9 and 1 o'clock.
