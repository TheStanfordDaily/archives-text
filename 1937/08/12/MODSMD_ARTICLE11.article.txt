# Thornhill Returns With Fish, Stories
## 
### 
Football Coach Tiny Thornhill
returned to the Farm yesterday after
a six weeks tour through northern
California, Oregon, and Washing
ton.
Tiny's return marks the begin
ning of another series of stories
concerning his luck as a fisherman.
Aside from the fact that he caught
several good sized fish, Tiny reports
that he had a very enjoyable trip
and vacation.
