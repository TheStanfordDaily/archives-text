# CAMPUS DANCE PLANNED
## 
### 
Due to the success of last week's
informal dance, attended by more
than 75 couples, the summer resi
dents of the Delta Chi house plan a
similar affair before finals, accord
ing to Social Chairman David S.
Brown. Entertainment will again be
provided and free punch will be
handed out to the guests who will
dance to the strains of victrola and
radio music.
