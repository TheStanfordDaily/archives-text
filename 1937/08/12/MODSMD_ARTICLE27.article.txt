# LECTURE SERIES CLOSES
## 
### 
The last of the series of Tudor
lectures being given in connection
with "Twelfth Night" will be next
Wednesday, August 16, at 8 p.m.
James Sandoe will talk on "Master
Viola," describing how Shakespeare
popularized the theme of attractive
shipwrecked ladies wearing trousers
and made it a favorite subject with
writers ever since.
