# Kefauver Leaving For North Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. Grayson N. Kefauver, dean of
the School of Education, will leave
Sunday evening for Seattle for a
consultation with the superintend
ent of schools concerning the Stan
ford Language Arts Investigation.
Tuesday evening Dr. Kefauver
will leave for Chicago, where he will
act as chairman of the committee
which is meeting to prepare the 1938
Yearbook on Guidance in Educa
tional Institutions. Dr. Kefauver
plans to return August 30.
