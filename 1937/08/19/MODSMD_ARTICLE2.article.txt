# Campus Figures Plan Vacations
## 
### 
With the approach of the month
vacation between quarters, many
prominent campus figures are plan
ning activities to keep them occu
pied until Registration Day.
Wayne Richardson, soccer and
rugby star, is returning to his home
in Molokai, T.H. George Campbell,
Japan Conference chairman, is
scheduled to speak before young
people's groups in several Washing
ton high schools and colleges on
"The More Abundant Life." P. F.
Harrington is planning to devote
the month to research.
