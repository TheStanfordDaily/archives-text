# Speed Regulation Changed on Farm
## 
### 
Important changes in campus
speed limits, according to Officer
Gordy Davis, will be a new limit of
25 miles per hour on Palm Drive and
on Lasuen street between the Quad
and Palo Alto, a reduction from the
former 30-mile limit.
On the streets adjoining the Quad,
men's and women's halls, and on the
Row, the maximum will be 20 mile.;.
According to Davis, new speed limit
signs are being erected on all cam
pus streets.
Diagonal parking will be the rule
along Serra street in front of Memo
rial Hall, instead of parallel as was
the custom last year.
