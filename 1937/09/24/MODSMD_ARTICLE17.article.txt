# Band Will Sponsor Registration Dance
## 
### 
Stanford's social life for the yrat
officially begins Tuesday, Septenibci
28, in the women's gym with the an
nual fall quarter registration dance
commonly known to old-timers a.s
the "reg dance."
The dance will be sponsored by tho
Stanford Band to raise scholar hip
funds for some member of the band
