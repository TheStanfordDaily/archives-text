# Harvard President Visitor at Stanford
## 
### 
James B. Conant, president of
Harvard University, was a visitor
on the Stanford campus for one day
during the summer quarter. Presi
dent Conant was entertained at
luncheon by President Wilbur at the
Wilbur home. After luncheon he
visited the various departments of
the University.
President Conant wus also the
guest of honor at a buffet supper
sponsored by the Stanford Faculty
