# Transfer Initiation Program Planned
## 
### 
Not only freshmen but junior
transfers will be initiated to the
Farm this fall. Under the direction
of Leonore Albertson, junior orien
tation chairman, plans are being
made to ease the task of getting
acquainted for transfer students.
On Thursday, September 30, from
7 to 8 o'clock, there will be a jolly
up for transfers only in the women's
gym. On Sunday, October 3, both
men and women will be invited to
parties to be held at faculty homes.
