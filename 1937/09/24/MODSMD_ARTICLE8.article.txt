# Daily Opens Door In Tryout Period
## 
### 
Freshmen and transfers will have
a chance to become campus news
paper men and women when The
Daily starts its tryouts Tuesday, Oc
tober 5, at 5 o'clock in room 8 of
the Press Building.
Tryoutees will be put through a
two- or three-week training period
during which they will be instructed
in writing news stories and head
lines, copyreading, proofreading, and
elements of news coverage. At the
end of the training period the most
capable tryoutees will be admitted
to The Daily staff as reporters
