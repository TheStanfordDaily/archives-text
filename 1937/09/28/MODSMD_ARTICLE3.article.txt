# MECHANICS TO STRIKE
## 
### 
SAN RAFAEL, Sept. 27 (U.R).—
Marin county automobile mechanics
today threatened to strike tomorrow
unless their demands for higher
wages and a regulated work week
were met by garage owners. Ap
proximately 450 men are involved.
They arc asking $1.00 and $1.12
hourly and a 40-hour week.
