# Rally Committee Meets Thursday
## 
### 
The Rally Committee's first meet
ing of the year will be held Thurs
day evening at 7:15 o'clock in the
Nitery, according to Isabel Gold
thwaite, chairman.
New pompoms, without which no
Stanford woman may sit in a root
ing section, have been ordered and
will go on sale at the beginning of
next week. Plans for the Cardinals
will be discussed at their first meet
ing tomorrow night.
