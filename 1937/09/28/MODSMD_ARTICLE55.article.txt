# Officers Named For Mothers' Club
## 
### 
Mrs. Almon E. Roth will continue
as director of the Stanford Mothers'
Club's student welfare work, it was
announced last week at a meeting of
the executive board and committee
chairmen at the home of Mrs. Dun
can McLeod. Mrs. Roth will be as
sisted by Mrs. Calvin P. Stone.
Appointed chairman of a com
mittee to welcome mothers of new
students was Mrs. J. W. Wear, who
is herself a newcomer to the com
munity.
Mrs. Fred A. Wickett, leaving soon
for New York, resigned as historian
to be replaced by Mrs. Harry Beal
Torrey.
First meeting during fall quarter
will be held October 19.
