# PACIFIC PREFERRED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Sept. 27 (UP).
—Americans would rather live on
the Pacific Coast than in any other
area of continental United States, a
nation-wide survey conducted by
Fortune Magazine revealed yester
day.
