# Distance Runners To Begin Training
## 
### 
Cross country trackmen begin
training for their autumn grind Oc
tober 15, Coach Dink Templeton
announced yesterday.
This year's team will be built
around Ed Burrows, Carl Mahurin,
Rusty March, Pete Ganahl, Jack
Beman and John Emmanuel. Temple
ton will welcome all men interested
in the sport to practice next week.
The schedule not yet complete will
be climaxed by the California meet
next November.
