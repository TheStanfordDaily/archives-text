sophomores to sign up as sophomore
tennis managers, Ed Barnett, senior
manager, announced yesterday.

sion requirement and offers the
men accepted the future opportunity
of getting a Block "S". All inter
ested should see Ed Barnett at the
gym tennis office at once.
