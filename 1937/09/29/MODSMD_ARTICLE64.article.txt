# Mural Sign-ups Will Start Today
## 
### 
Sign-ups for intramural touch
football, volley ball, and horseshoes
will begin today at the bulletin
board in Encina Gym. Volley ball is
seeing its first year under any official
competitive program.
Touch football will have last year's
champions, Phi Delta Theta, and
runners-up Sigma Nu attempting to
keep their hold on top honors. The
horseshoe tournament competition
will also be .keen with Delta Chi,
last year's champions, and Breakers,
runners-up, entered.
Organizations and independent
groups are urged to appoint their ;
managers immediately and have
them check over the new rule
changes adopted since last spring.
