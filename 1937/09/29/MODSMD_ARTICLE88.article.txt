# FRESNO STUDENTS STRIKE
## 
### 
FRESNO, Sept. 28 (U.R).—Publi
cation of the Fresno State College
newspaper was suspended today by
a strike of 27 student reporters and
editors. They protested that be
cause of their linotyper's alleged in
efficiency they got home too late to
study.
