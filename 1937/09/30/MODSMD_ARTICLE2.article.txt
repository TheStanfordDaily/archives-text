# Junior Transfers To Have Jolly-up
## 
### 
Junior transfers will be guests at
a jolly-up to be held in the Wom
en's Gym tonight from 7 to 8 o'clock.
All men-students are invited.
Music for the dance will be fur
nished by A1 Siebring's orchestra.
Lenore Albertson is in charge.
