# REDBIRDS DEFEAT BEARS
## 
### 
NEWARK, N.J., Sept. 29 (U.R).—
The Columbus Redbirds rallied in
the seventh inning today to beat the
Newark Bears, 5 to 4, in the opening
game of the Little World Series in
volving winners of American Asso
ciation and International League
pennant races.
