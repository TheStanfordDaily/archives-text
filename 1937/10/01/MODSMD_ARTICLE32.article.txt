# On and On, Through the Night
## 
### 

PAST MIDNIGHT AND FAR INTO THE MORNING marched the units of the gigantic American Le
gion parade in New York, while searchlights played on the former doughboys. For 18 hours the parade
moved onward, up famed Fifth Avenue, in what probably was the biggest parade spectacle in the nation's
history. Here is Fort Dearborn, Michigan, .Post No. 364, swing into line as dusk falls.
