# Glee Club To Open Operetta Practice
## 
### 
Glee Club members will gather for
their first rehearsal this afternoon Ht
4 o'clock in the Old Ruble Gym. The
meeting will consist of planning and
rehearsing for the production "Trial
by Jury," a Gilbert and Sullivan
vehicle which will be presented by
the music and drama departments
next month for the first-night pro
gram in Memorial Hall.
All former Glee Club members are
urged to be present this afternoon.
