# Developing a 'Block' to Polio
## 
### 

EXPERIMENTS by Professor E. W. Schultz, above, and Professor L. P. Gebhardl of the University
bacteriology department, have developed a "chemical block" to infantile paralysis. Professor Schultz is con
ducting the experiment on one of over 240 monkeys used. Zinc sulphate, it was found, is the best substance.
