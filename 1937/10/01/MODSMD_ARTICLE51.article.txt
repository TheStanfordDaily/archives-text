# BRUIN STARS RECOVER
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Sept. 30 (UP).—
U.C.L.A. Bruins were cheercd today
by return to practice of Tackle Lar
ry Murdock and Fullback Billy Bob
Williams, both injurfcd in early sea
son practice. The Bruins, who drew
a bye this Saturday, concentrated
on deefnse for Stanford plays in
preparation for the game at Palo
Alto October 9.
