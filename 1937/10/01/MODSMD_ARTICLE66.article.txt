# Frosh Food Under New Management
## 
### 
Food for frosh at Roble and Encina
will be dispensed under new man
agement this fall, according to an
announcement of Miss Christine
Ricker, Director of Dining Halls
and Stanford Union.
In charge of Encina Commons now
is Mrs. Dorothy Bell, a graduate of
lowa State College. Mrs. Bell has
been in charge of the Lazarus De
partment Store lunch room in Co
lumbus, Ohio. Miss Helen Hayden
will preside over the dining room at
Roble. She is a graduate of Oregon
State, where she has recently been
doing research, and comes from
New York City and Colorado.
