# Cares Dropped at Fireside
## 
### 

REVELING IN temporary respite from cares of state, President
Roosevelt is shown in an intimate family scene at the home of his
son-in-law and daughter, Mr. and Mrs. John Boettiger, in Seattle.
Seated, left to right: Mrs. James Roosevelt, President Roosevelt, his
granddaughter, Eleanor; Mrs. Roosevelt and Mrs. Boettiger. Standing
are Boettiger, left, and James Roosevelt and in the foreground is
Curtis, Roosevelt's grandson, and the Boettiger's Irish setter.
