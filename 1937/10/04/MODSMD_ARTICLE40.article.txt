# Soph Netmen Default Const Tennis Match
## 
### 
Two members of last year's once
beaten Stanford frosh tennis team,
Myron Folsom and Lloyd Gates,
gained the semi-finals of the Pacific
Coast junior championships at Ber
keley Tennis Club only to be elim
inated on default when they failed to
appear for a scheduled match Sat
urday.
Dave Brock, another ex-frosh,
captured three matches in the men's
singles before he was eliminated by
Charles Hare, British Davis Cup
star, 6-4, 6-3.
In reaching the next to last round,
Folsom and Gates came through two
early round matches without much
difficulty and then succeeded in up
setting the fourth seeded team of
Gibson and Porterfield in the quar
ter-finals, 6-2, 6-4.
Both Folsom and Gates fared less
well in the junior singles. Gates
was defeated by Bob Pelletrau, 6-4,
6-2, in the first round, while Folsom
took a 6-1, 6-2 decision from Bob
Livingston and then went down be
fore Ronald Edwards, 7-5, 6-2.
Brock swept through his first and
second round matches and then de
feated Colonel Blake, 6-3, 6-2. Fol
som paired with Brock in the men's
double event and bowed to Joe Hunt
and Bob Riggs, former national jun
ior champions.
