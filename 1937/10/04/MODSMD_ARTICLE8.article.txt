# Pompoms Go On Sale; Needed For Rooting Section
## 
### 
Rally Committee's pompom sale
will bp held this morning from 10 to
12 and 1 to 2 in front of the Post
Office and in Roble Hall. The sale
will last until Wednesday.
Women rooters must have pom
poms before they can be admitted to
the rooting section at Saturday's
U.C.L.A. game. Don Dewar, Rally
Committee chairman, describes this
year's pompoms as "bigger and bet
ter than ever."
Price of the pompoms is 25 cents.
Isabelle Goldthwaite, women's chair
man of the Rally Committee, is in
charge of the sale.
