# BARBER AMNESIA VICTIM
## 
### 
LONG BEACH, Oct. 4 (U.R).—Gus
Minor, Council BlufTs barber, went
out last Tuesday to pay his tele
phone bill. He recovered from an
attack of amnesia in a local hotel
with 12 cents in his pockets.
