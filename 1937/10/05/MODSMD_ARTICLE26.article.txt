# Quad Sets Second Tryout Meeting
## 
### 
Quad business staff will hold its
second tryoutee meeting at 7 o'clock
tonight in the Quad office. Those
who attended last week's tryoutee
meeting need not be present tonight.
Office hours for those who already
have signed up are posted on the
bulletin board in the Quad office.
There are still a few vacancies on
both the editorial and business
staffs. Editorial tryoutees may have
interviews with George McKenna,
editor of the annual, every after
noon from 1 to 4 o'clock in his of
fice in the Quad building.
