# DRESS DESIGNER DIES
## 
### 
ENGLEWOOD, N.J., Oct. 4 (U.R).—
Mme. Bertha Gounod, once a dress
designer for Their Majesties Queen
Mary of England and Queen Marie
of Rumania, died in poverty last
night. The 77-year-old dressmaker,
for two years employed in a WPA
sewing project, died of heart •dis
ease.
