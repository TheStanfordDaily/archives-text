# Motor Code Warns Student Autoists
## 
### 
Stanford students under 21 years
of age driving wtih licenses issued
by other states than California had
better obtain California licenses im
mediately or be liable to prosecu
tion under state vehicle laws.
California motor vehicle codes
allow a non-resident person over
16 but under 21 to drive in this
state for 10 days after entry without
securing a new license. Those who
have over-run this allowance should
apply at the Registration Bureau-on
Ramona Street in Palo Alto for
California licenses.
