# Hulme, Manwaring To Talk for Club
## 
### 
Speakers at the Faculty Research
Club meeting in the Faculty Club
house at 8 tonight will be Professor
E. M. Hulme and Professor W. H.
Manwaring. Hulme, emeritus pro
fessor of history, will have as his
subject, "Bernardino Ochino, a
Franciscan Heretic of the 16th Cen
tury."
Manwaring, emeritus professor of
bacteriology, will speak on "Organi
cist Theory of the Social Order in
the Light of Recent Bological Re
search."
