# Kirkman To Open Biology Seminars
## 
### 
Hadley Kirkman, instructor in
anatomy, will open the autumn se
ries of this afternoon's Biology Sem
inars today, with an address on
"The Present Status of the Golgi
Apparatus." The seminar will meet
in Room 433, Jordan Hall, at 4
o'clock.
Graduate students will serve tea,
and at 4:20 o'clock the lecture will
be delivered in Room 430. Faculty
and students of the School of Bio
logical Sciences are invited to attend.
