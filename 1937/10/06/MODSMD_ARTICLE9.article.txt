# Wesley Foundation To Hold Reception
## 
### 
The Wesley Foundation will hold
its annual reception Friday evening,
October 8, from 8 to 11 o'clock in
the reception room at the Palo Alto
Community Center, 1305 Middlefield
Road.
Thus Christian Fellowship organi
zation invites all persons of college
age residing in Stanford, Palo Alto,
or vicinity to attend this informal
"get acquainted" party.
