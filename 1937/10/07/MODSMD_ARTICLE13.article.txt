# Study Changes Due October 12
## 
### 
Change of study cards must
be filed in the Registrar's Of
fice by 5 o'clock, October 12.
Lower Division cards may be
obtained from Miss Gibson in
the Lower Division office. Up
per Division and Graduate
cards are obtainable at the in
formation window at the Reg
istrar's office.
All cards must have the sig
natures of instructors whose
courses are being dropped or
taken up and the approval of
the major department or Low
er Division adviser.
