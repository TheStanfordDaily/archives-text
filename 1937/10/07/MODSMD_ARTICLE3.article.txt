# Liberals Re-elected In Ontario Voting
## 
### 
TORONTO, Ont., Oct. 6 (UP).—
Re-election of Prime Minister
Mitchell Hepburn's Liberal govern
ment of Ontario was conceded by
the Conservative party shortly after
8:30 tonight.
Early returns showed Liberals had
won 20 scats and were leading in
30 constituencies.
