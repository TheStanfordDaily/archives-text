# Organist Will Play Selections Today
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give an organ recital at
4:15 o'clock this afternoon in the
Chapel.
Selections to be played include:
Introduction to "Faust," Gounod;
Choral from "Die Meistersinger,"
and Prelude from "Lohengrin,"
Wagner; "Jewels of the Madonna,"
Wolf-Ferrari; and Triumphal
March from "Aida," Verdi.
