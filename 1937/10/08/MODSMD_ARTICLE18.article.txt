# Gym Classes Open To Faculty Wives
## 
### 
Two courses are being offered to
Stanford faculty wives and Palo Alto
townswomen this quarter at the
women's gymnasium.
Mrs. Sylvia Bites Cain will instruct
a faculty golf class in two sections,
Monday, Wednesday at 10, and
Monday, Wednesday at 11. The sec
ond class in gymnasium work meets
Monday, Wednesday and Friday at
10 under Mrs. Maud Lombard Knapp
and Miss Marie Manchee. Some
swimming may be included in this
class. A nominal fee will be charged
for these courses.
Noon-hour swimming for women
employees of the University started
last Tuesday. A fee of $3.50 is
J charged for this privilege.
