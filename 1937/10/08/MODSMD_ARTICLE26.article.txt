# Match Plans Begun By Farm Gun Club
## 
### 
The Stanford Rifle and Pistol
Club met Wednesday night for a
business meeting in the lobby of the
Administration Building, followed
by shooting practice in the Rifle
Range.
Plans were begun for intercol
legiate match shooting, for which
rifle and pistol teams will be formed.
Regular meetings for practice will
be held every Wednesday night at
7:30 o'clock in the Range.
Officers of the club for this year
are Rita Szekeres, president and
secretary-treasurer; Cameron Hall,
captain of pistol team; and Bill
MaeComber, captain of rifle team.
