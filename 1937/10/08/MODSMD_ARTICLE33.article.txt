# WILL AVOID GERMANY
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Oct. 7 (UP). —
Paramount studio, informed of a
Nazi blast against Marlene Dietrich
for seeking American citizenship,
said today Miss Dietrich plans to
pass up Germany in her current
European tour.
