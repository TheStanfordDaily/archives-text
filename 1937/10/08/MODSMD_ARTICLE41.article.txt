# Mussolini's Son To Visit Capitol
## 
### 
NEWARK, N.J., Oct. 7 (UP).—Vit
torio Mussolini, son of Premier Be
nito Mussolini of Italy, arrived here
by airplane from the West Coast at
1:02 p.m. (E.S.T.) today.
He was escorted to a waiting auto
mobile by a group -of policemen
after telling reporters he would go
to New York for "two or three
hours" and then go to Washington.
