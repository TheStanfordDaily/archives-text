# Transfers Gather At Wilbur Home
## 
### 
Transfer and graduate students
were tendered a reception by Dr.
and Mrs. Wilbur Saturday evening.
Members of the faculty and student
body government assisted in ac
quainting the new students with each
other.
The guests ranged from junior
transfers to a college professor from
the University of Utah, who will re
verse his usual academic position
here, as a graduate student in the
Department of Education.
Dean of Women Mary Yost and
Dean of Men George B. Culver rep
resented the faculty, and Daily Edi
tor Gordy Frost and A.S.S.U. Vice-
President Barbara Murphy were
members of student officialdom
present.
