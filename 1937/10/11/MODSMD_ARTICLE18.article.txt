# Dewar Announces Rally Appointees
## 
### 
Don Dewar, Rally Committee
chairman, yesterday announced four
new appointments to the group.
Nelson Kogler was promoted from
second to first division to replace
Brian Harvey, and Didi Vibert was
appointed junior woman in place of
Connie Barnett. Neither Miss Bar
nett nor Harvey returned to school
this fall. Joy Grubb is new sopho
more woman and Lloyd Wilson is
sophomore man.
