# HERE'S HOW IT'S DONE
##

RUSK. Tex., Oct. 10 (UP).—Bill
McCluney, football coach at Rusk
High School, took a place in the
scrimmage line to show his boys
how to play. A lackler hit him and
broke his shoulder.
