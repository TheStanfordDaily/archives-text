# Freshmen Invited To Toyon Dance
## 
### 
Plans are well under way for the
Toyon-Branner joint dance to be
held this Friday, October 15, in the
lobby of Toyon Hall. The committee
consists of Chairman Bob Clerk,
Warren Buell, John Esterline, Her
shey Julien, Dave Swanberg, Frank
Van Acker, Harry Horton. and Mel
Jacoby.
All members of the freshman
class are invited to the dance. It
will furnish them with an oppor
tunity "to become acquainted with
the Halls," according to Clerk. The
selection of an orchestra has not yet
been made..
Pennsylvania permits citizens to
manufacture 200 gallons of wine in
their homes annually.
