# New Drama Club To Give Reading
## 
### 
Stanford's new student dramatic
society will present its first offering,
a reading of Maxwell Andersons
"Both Your Houses," Pulitzer prize
play, on Thursday evening, October
21, in the Little Theater.
Tryouts for parts are open to the
entire campus, and will be held from
2 to 4 o'clock this afternoon in the
Little Theater of Memorial Hall, ac
cording to Bud Cady, director of the
production and president of the so
ciety. Thirteen male and three fe
male parts are open.
''Both Your Houses" is a biting
indictment of graft and corruption
in Washington politics. Its author,
Maxwell Anderson, is a former Stan
ford man.
