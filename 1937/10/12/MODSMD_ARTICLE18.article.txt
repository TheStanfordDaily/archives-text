# Baritone To Give Program Tonight
## 
### 
With the presentation of John
Teel, baritone, tonight, the Commit
tee on Public Exercises will strike
the keynote of this year's series of
varied and interesting Tuesday eve
ning programs.
Unlike past seasons, this year's
Tuesday Series will have musical
programs as well as lectures. To
night's recital at 7:30 in Memorial
Hall includes sixteen songs in four
groups.
Schubert, Strauss, and Handel are
some of the popular composers
whose works will be included. Listed
among one of the groups to be sung
arc folk songs from different coun
tries.
Teel will be accompanied by Glad
ys Steele.
