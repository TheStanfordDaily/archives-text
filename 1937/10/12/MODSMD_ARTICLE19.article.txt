# SKI FILM TO BE SHOWN
## 
### 
A short film illustrating the activi
ties of a skiing school in Switzerland
will be shown tonight in the Quad
Little Theater at 7:30 o'clock. Spon
sored by the Stanford Ski Club, the
film graphically shows the correct
skiing form as taught to students
in the dry ski class. Admission is
free, and all interested are invited,
particularly those who are enrolled
in the course.
