# Klahoya To Honor Y.W. President
## 
### 
Jackie Brown, president of the
Stanford Y.W.C.A., will be guest to
day at the second regular meeting of
the Klahoya Club, off-campus wom
en's organization. Members will as
semble in the Women's Clubhouse
at noon.
Those who have not yet joined, but
who would like to get acquainted
with the organization are invited to
attend the luncheon. Dorothy Brown,
president of the club, announced that
plans are being discussed which
might make it possible for off-cam
pus women to eat together on other
week-days, as well as on Tuesdays.
