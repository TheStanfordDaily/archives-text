# Bulletin Board

DEBATE MEETINGS: At 2 and 4
o'clock in Room 202, English Build
ing. Debaters may attend either
section, prepared to talk on
"Preservation of Democratic
Principles."

at Deke house at noon.

of the Gaieties, will be in the
Dramatic Manager's office from 4
to 6 o'clock today to interview
prospective orchestra leaders and
those who have written songs or
lyrics for the Gaieties.

7:30 in the dance studio of the
Women's Gym.
KLAHOYA CLUB: Meets today at
noon in the Women's Clubhouse.
STUDY LISTS: All changes must
be made today.
