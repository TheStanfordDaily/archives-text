# Hawthorne House To Hold Singstude
## 
### 
Following its usual custom, the
German House will again hold sing
stundes this year. Tomorrow after
noon from 4:30 to 5:30, the first
group will meet to partake in re
freshments and sing German folk
songs according to the old custom.
All girls living in Hawthorne House
and their guests will participate.
Dr. Morgan, head of the German
department, will preside. The meet
ings will take place every other
week starting tomorrow.
