# Chaney Will Speak At Biology Seminar
## 
### 
R. W. Chaney, professor of paleon
tology at the University of Califor
nia, will lecture at 4:20 o'clock this
afternoon at the meeting of the
Biology Seminar. His address will
be on "Plant Distributing in Geologic
Time" and will be delivered in Room
430 of Jordan Hall.
Tea will be served by graduate
students in the department in Room
433.
