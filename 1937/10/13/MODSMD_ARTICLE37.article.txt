# Frosh Discussion Group Held Today
## 
### 
First meeting of the Freshman
Discussion Group, under the uus
pices of the Y.W.C.A., will be held
today at 4:30 o'clock in the Y.W.C.A.
rooms of the Women's Clubhouse.
The value and place of activities in
college life, and ways of integrating
them with other phases of college
experience will be discussed.
Beth Allen, assisted by Jean
Dodge, is head of the Freshman
Discussion Group. This year the
group plans to discuss the principal
problems which the average girl
faces during her college career.
