# Toyon Will Stage Dance for Frosh
## 
### 
All freshmen are welcomed to the
lobby of Toyon Hall this Friday
evening, October 15, where a free
dance will be held from 9 until 12.
The dance is being sponsored by
Toyon and Branner Halls, with the
express purpose of getting all
freshmen acquainted with the hall.
A1 Sebring and his orchestra, who
played many times on campus last
season, will provide the music, and
Gerry Reed will sing.
The patrons and patronesses listed
by Bob Clerk, chairman, include Mr.
find Mrs. Stanley Sharp, Mr. and
Mrs. N. E. Bradburg, and Mr. and
Mrs. J. C. t McCloskey.
