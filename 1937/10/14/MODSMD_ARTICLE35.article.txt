# 'Y' Holds Dinner In Union Tonight
## 
### 
Mrs. Rathbun, Y.W.C.A. secretary,
will speak at their buffet supper to
night on "What the Y.W.C.A. Offers
the Stanford Girl." Jackie Brown
will introduce the speaker and the
members of the cabinet. Audis Davis,
featured soloist, will lead the guests
in songs.
Committees are headed by Jean
Bolton, publicity; Olive Freeman,
decoration; Mary Kay Horton, tick
ets; Sally Davis, invitations; and
Audis Davis, entertainment. Elva
Ekwurzel is chairman in charge of
general arrangements.
