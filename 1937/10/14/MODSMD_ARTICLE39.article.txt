# Maxwell To Speak At ASME Meeting
## 
### 
Stanford Branch, American So
ciety of Mechanical Engineers, will
hold its opening meeting of the
quarter tomorrow afternoon at 4
o'clock, when Maxwell C. Maxwell,
official of Yale & Towne Co., will
give his famous demonstration talk,
on "Loxology."
The speaker, who has been sent
here by the parent society, and will
lecture in room 283, has been well
received at all his stops, and his
unique demonstration of the history,
functions, and failures of all types of
locks has drawn much favorable
comment.. All interested are in-'
vited.
