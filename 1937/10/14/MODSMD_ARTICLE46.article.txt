# Tunney, Frye Made Circle 'S' Members
## 
### 
Gene Tunney, former heavy
weight boxing champion of the
world, and Clayton Frye, former
national A.A.U. middleweight cham
pion and present boxing coach at
Stanford, have been named hon
orary members of the Circle "S," it
was announced yesterday by Harry
Maloney, director of minor athletics
here on the Farm.
Mr. Tunney has notified officials of
the Circle "S" that he expects to
be present at the annual meeting of
the club when the medals which he
donates yearly will be awarded.
