# Badminton Group Chooses Officers
## 
### 
Officers for the coming year were
elected by the Badminton Club at
its weekly meeting Tuesday night.
David Strauss was elected president.
Betty Muddox will serve a second
term as vice-president. Other offi
cers will be Duncan Robertson,
secretary-treasure*-, and Eric Frei
tag, tournament manager.
Another meeting will be held to
night at 7:30 o'clock in the Women's
Gym.
