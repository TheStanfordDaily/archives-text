# Eating Clubs Plan Smoker for Frosh
## 
### 
The seven eating clubs of Stanford
will have a supper and smoker for
the freshman class next Wednesday,
October 20.
Invitations are being distributed
today to all frosh by members of
Los Arcos, Breakers, El Capitan, El
Campo, El Cuadro, El Tigre, and El
Toro. The suppers will be held in
the clubs, then the freshmen will be
escorted by the club members to
Toyon Hall, where prominent coach
es and athletes will give short talks.
The suppers and smoker are being
held to give the freshman class an
opportunity to become acquainted
wtih life in the halls. Several rooms
in Toyon will be open for any fresh
men who wish to visit them.
