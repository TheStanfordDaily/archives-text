# Singstunde Today At German House
## 
### 
Entertainment in the form of a
Singstunde will be conducted this
afternoon at 4 o'clock in the German
House, 514 Alvarado Street, under
the direction of Professor B. Q.
Morgan.
The affair will be strictly informal,
and everyone who is interested in
German language and customs is
cordially invited by Head Resident
Frances Kanzler to attend the func
tion. German songs will be sung
from books.
The German house is a small resi
dence which at present is housing 14
women. Its purpose is to foster an
understanding of German through
the actual experience of living in a
German atmosphere and by speak
ing the language informally.
