# World Foundation Will Hear Wilbur
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur,
American member of the Advisory
Council of the World Foundation
will address a meeting of the tSan
ford group of the foundation Thurs
day evening at 7:30 o'clock at
the Faculty Clubhouse, Professor
Ralph H. Lutz, chairman, announced
yesterday.
Other speakers will be Professor
Graham Stuart of the Political Sci
ence Department and Professor
Percy Martin of the history depart
ment.
Dr. Wilbur will talk on "The World
Outlook"; Professor Stuart on "The
International Postal Union and other
Nonpolitical Agencies"; and Profes
sor Martin's subject will be "Pan-
Americanism and Our Interamerican
Relations."
