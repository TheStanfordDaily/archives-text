# A.S.M.E. To Hear Talk on 'Loxology"
## 
### 
Stanford student branch, the
American Society of Mechanical
Engineers, will hold its first meet
ing of the year this afternoon when
Maxwell C. Maxwell, official from
Yale & Towne Co., lockmakers, will
address the group.
Maxwell, who will speak at 4:00
o'clock in Room 283, brings with
him demonstration equipment that
takes an hour to erect. His talk is
entitled "Loxology," and covers the
history, construction, and failures of
all types of locks. All interested are
invitees; the meeting will be in
charge of Student Chairman Dave
Hooker and Faculty Adviser A1
Gullikson.
