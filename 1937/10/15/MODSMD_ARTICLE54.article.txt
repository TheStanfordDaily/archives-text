# Bawlouts Will Go On Sale Tomorrow
## 
### 
Autumn quarter Bawlout, official
directory of Stanford students and
irculty, will go on sale tomorrow at
the Comptroller's office.
The Bawlout contains names, ad
dresses, and telephone numbers of
both faculty members and students,
as well as grade averages of the
latter.
