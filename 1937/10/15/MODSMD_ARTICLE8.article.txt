# Roble To Meet Row At Courtesy Teas
## 
### 
Row women will have their first
formal contact with Roble frosh when
they entertain them at courtesy teas
October 22, 23, and 24, according to
Barbara Paine, Panhellenic presi
dent.
A second series of teas given by
the houses individually will consti
tute the next rushing period. These
will be held December 3, 4, and 5.
In the meantime, informal contact
between Row women and frosh will
continue as in the past.
