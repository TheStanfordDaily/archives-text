ALTOONA, Pa., Oct. 7 (UP). —
Several weeks ago, John Pryor suf
fered a broken leg. This week Pryor
exchanged his crutches for a cane.
Yesterday he ventured into the busi
ness district and fell. He's in the hos
pital again with a broken leg.
