# Wilbur Speaks As J.C. Heads Meet
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur addressed
the Northern California Junior Col
lege Association yesterday at its an
nual luncheon, held at International
House in Berkeley.
Speaking on the subject "What
Students Most Need Is Advice," Dr.
Wilbur said, "Sixty per cent of the
university students of today don't
know what they want." Dr. Harry
Tyler of Sacramento was elected
president of the association by 300
delegates from 17 Northern Califor
nia junior colleges.
