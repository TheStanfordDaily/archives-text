# Postoffice Boxes Still Available
## 
### 
A few postoffice boxes are still
available at the Stanford branch for
this quarter, according to an an
nouncement issued yesterday by
postal officials. Boxholders and
others receiving their mail by home
or general delivery are asked to
notify their correspondents of their
correct address.
Following a policy inaugurated
this quarter, the lobby of the post
office will be kept open until mid
night.
