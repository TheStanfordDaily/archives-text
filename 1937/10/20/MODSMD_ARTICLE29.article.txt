# Thornton Gets Giant Trackman For Water Polo
## 
### 
In his quest to find someone
to replace Bob Haslacher, all-
Coast goal guard of last year's
squad, Coach Nort Thornton
yesterday enlisted 5 ft. 8 in.
Tom Montgomery, Stanford
shot-putter, as a possible goalie
on the 1937 water polo team.
Montgomery, who turned in an
excellent first - day perform
remarked that "It's easy when
you can stand on the bottom."
Dick Hoover, hard - fighting
first-string" guard, was injured
below the left eye yesterday
with a seven-stitch wound, and
may be unable to play against
University of Southern Cali
fornia Friday.
