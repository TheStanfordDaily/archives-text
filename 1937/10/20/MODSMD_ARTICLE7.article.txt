# Cabinet Makes Convention Plans
## 
### 
Y.W.C.A. Cabinet met yesterday
afternoon to discuss plans for the
coming Northern California "Y"
convention to be held at Stanford
Saturday, October 30. Colleges to
be represented at the convention are
San Jose State, Sacramento Junior
College, and College of the Pacific
at Stockton.
Joyce Dunkerly, a former Stanford
student, now executive secretary of
the Y.W.C.A. at College of the Pa
cific, will be one of the speakers at
the session.
