# U.C. Physiologist To Lecture Here
## 
### 
H. S. Reed, professor of plant
physiology at the University of Cali
fornia, will be guest speaker at the
meeting of Biology Seminar at 4:20
o'clock this afternoon. He will speak
on the "Cardinal Aspects of Growth"
in Room 430 of Jordan Hall.
At 4 o'clock tea will be served by
graduate students in Room 433.
Visitors are welcome to attend the
lecture, as well as the rest of the
series, one of which is given every
Wednesday afternoon.
