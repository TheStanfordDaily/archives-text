# Past Daily Editor Gets Appointment
## 
### 
Templeton Peck, city editor of the
Los Angeles Daily News, has been
appointed lecturer in journalism for
the current academic year.
Mr. Peck was graduated from
Stanford in 1930, where during the
years 1928-29 he was editor of The
Daily. He has also been connected
with the San Francisco News and
the San Diego Sun.
