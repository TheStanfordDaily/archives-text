# Failure To Fight Loses Thil Crown
## 
### 
PARIS, Oct. 20 (UP).- The In
ternational Boxing Union , today
withdrew its recognition of Marcel
Thil as European middleweight
champion. The bald Frenchman re
cently was defeated by a technical
knockout in a New York bout with
Fred Apostoli of San Francisco.
Thil had been given until today
to defend his European title against
Cuban Kid Tunero but instead he
went to the United States, where he
lost his world title claim.
