# Row To See Roble During Rush Teas
## 
### 
Panhellenic Teas for all frosh
women will be held in the Women's
Clubhouse tomorrow, Saturday and
Sunday from 3 to 5 o'clock. This is
the first series at which Row women
and frosh will have an opportunity
to become acquainted.
The guest list will be alphabet
ically divided into three groups: A-G
tomorrow, H-P on Saturday, and
R-Z on Sunday. Quad clothes will
be worn at the first two teas, and
informal silk or wool dresses Sun
day. Freshmen will be identified
by yellow name tags, while Row
women will wear white name tags.
