# ORGAN RECITAL TODAY
## 
### 
This afternoon's organ recital by
Warren D. Allen will be given in
the chapel at 4:15 o'clock. His se
lections include Solem Prelude,
"Gloria Domini"; Ronde francaise
(in the Aeolian Mode); Largo from
the 'Cello Sonata; "Hark, a Voice
Saith, 'All are Mortal'"; and Passa
caglia and Thema Fugatum.
