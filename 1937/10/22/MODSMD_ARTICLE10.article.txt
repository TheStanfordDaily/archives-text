# Roble Club Formal Set for Tonight
## 
### 
The Roble Club's autumn formal
will be held tonight in the dining
rooms at Roble Hall, with dancing
to the music of Ernie Heckscher and
his orchestra from 9 to 1 o'clock,
with a floor show during the inter
mission.
The patrons and patronesses are:
Miss Ricker, Miss Doyle, Miss Price,
Mr. Fast, Professor Barclay, Pro
fessor and Mrs. JohnSon, Professor
and Mrs. Anderson, Professor and
Mrs. Rathbun, Mrs. Diekema, Miss
Hayden, MisS Christie, Mr. Boggs,
and Mr. Sellards.
