# Skit Deadline Set Today for Gaieties
## 
### 
All skits for this year's Big Game
Gaieties must be turned in at the
Dramatic Manager's office in the
Old Union by 5 o'clock today, ac
cording to Director Hal Kahn.
Tryouts for actors for the produc
tion will be held from 4 to 6 o'clock
next Monday and Tuesday in the
Women's Clubhouse. Clay McDan
iel, dramatic manager, stated that
more specialty numbers, comedy
acts, and impersonations are needed
for the Gaieties.
Anyone who has had experience
playing a Hammond organ is urged
to try out for the annual football
fun-fest. Those whose songs were
not chosen are requested to call for
them at the dramatic manager's
office.
