# Gaieties Players To Try Out Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for actors for this year's
Big Game Gaieties, to be presented'
November 19. will be held from 4 to
6 o'clock today in Women's Club
house, according to Director Hal
Kahn.
The song, "Fallen," by Onyet
Watson and Press Ellington, has
been selected for use in the show
in addition to those announced last
week. Anyone who submitted a
tune that was not selected is re
quested to call for it at the dramatic
manager's office in the Old Union.
Gaieties tickets will go on sale the
week of the show; no advance reser
vations will be accepted, according
to Dramatic Manager Clay McDan
iel.
