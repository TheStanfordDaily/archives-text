# FRENCH BOAT SUNK BY PRIVATE PLANE
## 
### 
PERPIGNAN, Franco - Spanish
Frontier, Oct. 24 (UP). —The French
carog boat Oued Mellah was bombed
and sunk by a "pirate" airplane late
today 50 miles east of the Spanish
Balearic Islands, according to re
ports reaching here.
