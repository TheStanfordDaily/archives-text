# Trojan Threat Fails To Appear
## Cougars Down Bruins By 3-point Margin 
### 
This highly ballyhooed threat of
Troy to upset the Golden 800-erke
ley Bears just sorta went foo. U.S.C.
and 10,000 Southern California grid
maniacs expected plenty from How
ard Jones and his boys, but got a
mere six points, which may be
enough to win some ball games, but
not one against Cal. Final score was
20-6.
Once again it has been proved that
Bill Spaulding needs 10 more foot
ball players. He has Kenny Wash
ington, but he wasn't enough to give
life to a weak Bruin, with the result
that Washington State's Cougars
have a 3-0 win in their trophy case.
Oregon State put a severe crimp in
Oregon's homecoming celebration
with its 14-0 last-period win. Out
classing t Oregon all the way, they
scored both their touchdowns in the
last six minutes of the ball game.
