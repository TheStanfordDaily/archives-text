# Panhell Entertains Freshmen at Tea
## 
### 
Panhellenic Council entertained
all frosh women at a series of teas
Friday, Saturday, and yesterday
from 3 to 5 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Barbara Paine, chairman of the
council, headed the receiving line,
which was composed of six differ
ent members each day. Janet
Brownell was head of the receiving
committee, with Cornelia Tracy in
charge of serving.
Yellow name tags were worn by
the frosh and white tags by Row
women to facilitate them in becom
ing acquainted. The Row will next
entertain the frosh at house teas to
be given December 3, 4, and 5.
