# Cardinals To Name New Senior Woman
## 
### 
To fill a vacancy on Cardinals left
by the resignation of Barbara Mur
phy, A.S.S.U. vice-president, a sen
ior woman will be appointed tomor
row night at a meeting of the group.
New appointments which were
made to Rally Committee last week
to fill vacant posts included Nelson
Kogler, Phi Delt, junior man on the
committee; Didi Vibert, Dee Gee,
junior woman; Joy Grubbs, Lagu
nita, sophomore woman; Lloyd Wil
son, Theta Delt, sophomore man.
Plans for a Big Game dance and
jolly-up will be discussed tomorrow
night.
