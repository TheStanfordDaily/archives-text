# Beadle Will Speak At Biology Lecture
## 
### 
George W. Beadle, professor of
biology, will speak at Biology Sem
inar this afternoon in Room 430 of
Jordan Hall. His address will be on
the "Inheritance and development of
eye colors in Drosophila and other
insects."
Tea will be served by graduate
students in the department preced
ing the lecture, to be presented at
4:20 o'clock. All those interested
are invited to attend.
