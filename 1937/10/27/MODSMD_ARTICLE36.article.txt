# BERKELEYITES INJURED
## 
### 
ANAHEIM, Oct. 26 (UP).—Mrs.
Julia Heber, of Berkeley, was in
jured seriously and her daughter
in-law, Mrs. Clifford Heber, of Los
Angeles, was lacerated badly about
the head today when the automobile
the Los Angeles woman was driving
collided with one driven by Paul
Dutton, of Orange.
