# Klahoya To Start Activity Campaign
## 
### 
Dorothy Brown, president of the
Klahoya Luncheon Club, took the
first steps in Women's Conference
campaign to increase the number of
off-campus women active in extra
curricular activities.
An abbreviated edition of the Ac
tivities Bulletin, to be issued by
Women's Conference fall quarter,
was distributed. An effort will be
made to give members an oppor
tunity to participate in fields that
interest them.
