# NEWSPRINT PRICE UP
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 26 (UP).
—The Crown Zcllerbach Corpora
tion, largest United States west coast
newsprint producer, today set a new
contract price of $30 a ton for the
first six months of 1938, an increase
of $7.50 per ton over prevailing
prices.
