# Allen Organ Recital To Be Given Today
## 
### 
Today at 4:15 o'clock in the Me
morial Church, Warren D. Allen,
University organist, will present
the third organ recital of the season.
His program includes: Three
Pieces, by J. P. Rameau, Dance of
the Blessed Spirits, by C. W. Gluck,
Autumn Song and Vitrail, by Alex
Gretchaninoff, and Toccata, "Thou
Art the Rock," by Henry Mulet.
