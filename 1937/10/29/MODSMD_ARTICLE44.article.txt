# Orchesis Will Hold Dance Symposium
## 
### 
Orchesis Society representatives
from eight colleges in the Bay re
gion met at Mills College on Octo
ber 23 to plan their fifth annual
dance symposium. It will probably
be held at Mills on November 14.
This year about 200 girls will take
part in the final presentation. Fol
lowing are the colleges to be repre
sented: Stanford, Mills, California,
Nevada, San Jose State, San Fran
cisco State, College of the Pacific,
and Fresno State.
