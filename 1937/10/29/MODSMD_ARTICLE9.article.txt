# Art Gallery Shows Bay Water Colors
## 
### 
Stanford Art Gallery is now ex
hibiting a group of water colors by
the Thirteen Water Colorists, a Bay
Region group of artists. An entire
wall of paintings with Coit Tower as
its subject is one of the high points
of the exhibit.
The members of the Thirteen
Water Colorists are Lonnie Bee,
W .R. Cameron, Paul T. Carey, Wil
iard R. Cox, James H. Forman,
Haines Hall, Nat Levy, Orsin Linn,
Philip Little, Maurice Logan, Al
ton C. Painter, Paul A. Schmitt, and
Francis Todhunter.
