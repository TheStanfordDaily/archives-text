# Encina Club Plans New Government
## 
### 
Walt Stoessel, president of Encina
Club, has inaugurated a new form
of government. He has formed a
representative council composed of
four men from each floor. This
council will decide all questions
concerning the club, and will also
compose a committee for dances.
President Stoessel announced yes
terday that the first dance of the
club will be held December 3. Other
than the fact that it will be informal,
no definite plans have been made.
Next meeting of the council will
take place some time this week,
when plans will be completed.
Stoessel is going to attempt to hold
a dance each quarter this year. In
addition to the dances, there will be
several smokers, featuring athletic
coaches and other px-ominent uni
versity men.
