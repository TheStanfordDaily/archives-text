# First Quad Proofs Ready at A.S.S.U.
## 
### 
Quad proofs for the first group of
individual pictures taken this year
will be available today in Room 15
of the A.S.S.U. offices, George Mc-
Kenna, editor of the annual, an
nounced yesterday.
The new office, introduced this
year as an added student conveni
ence, will be open from 9 to 5 o'clock
every day except Saturday and
Sunday. Students may call for their
proofs there instead of at the Quad
office.
