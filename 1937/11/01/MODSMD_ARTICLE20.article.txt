# Junior Orchesis Holds First Meet
## 
### 
First meeting of the Junior Or
cliesis Society was held last Thurs
day. Twelve girls attended, includ
ing representatives from all classes,
graduate students, and one faculty
member. The time was spent on
problems in elevation leading to
leaping technique.
Junior Orchesis is open to anyone
interested in dancing and may be
joined at anytime. No prerequisites
or tryouts are necessary.
