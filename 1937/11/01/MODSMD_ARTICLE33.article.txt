# Spanish Aid Units To Give War Film
## 
### 
Sponsored jointly by Irvin Jor
gensen's Student Committee for
Spanish Medical Aid, and by the
Palo Alto committee for the same
purpose, "The Heart of Spain" will
be shown tomorrow night in the
Palo Alto Community Center.
Admission price will be 25 cents,
with proceeds to go to the support
of a locally financed medical unit
stationed in Loyalist territory in
Spain. The picture will be shown at
7 o'clock, according to present plans.
"One thousand feet of pictorial dy
namite" is the subtitle of the picture,
which portrays conditions in war
stricken areas of Spain.
