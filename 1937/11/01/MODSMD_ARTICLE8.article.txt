# Jittery Actor Spends Night Pasting Paper
## 
### 
"Trial by Jury" meant a lot to
one member of the cast.
A "spectator" in the production,
he scurried avidly around before
curtain time Saturday night and got
all the other cast members to auto
graph his program.
Apparently nervous during the
play, the tyro began to squirm. A
sweaty hand, a clenched fist in his
pocket. Before he knew it, the pro
gram was in sweaty, mangled
shreds.
Reports were that the thespian
spent most of the night (after the
cast party) patching together the 60
autographs so he could paste them
into his scrapbook.
