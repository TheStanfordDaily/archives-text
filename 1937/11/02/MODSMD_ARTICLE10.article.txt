# Excom Will Hear Libe Poll Report
## 
### 
The Libe Poll will be Ex Com
mittee's first subject for discussion
at tonight's meeting. A report on the
situation, the vote, and its results
will be made.
Chaparral's plans for a photo con
test and display in the trophy case
in the Union Cellar will be brought
before the committee for discussion
and okay.
With the advent of Sunday movies
on the campus, certain rules must
be changed, since there has always
been a restriction on Sunday eve
ning activities on the Farm.
Other items for consideration will
be the confirmation of appointments
by the committee, and an S.I.P.R.
grant for money.
