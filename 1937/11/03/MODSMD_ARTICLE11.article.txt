# IT'S NOT FUNNY
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Nov. 2 (U.R). —
Groucho and Chico Marx, who per
spired profusely in a cold courtroom
until they learned they weren't go
ing to jail, said today they will fight
to the U.S. Supreme Court if neces
sary against a conviction of crimi
nally pirating a radio script. They
were fined the maximum of $1000
each.
