# New Farm Loans Raise Corn Price
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Nov. 2 (UP). -
Federal corn loans at 50 cents a
bushel will become effective within
a few days, Agriculture Department
officials revealed today after Presi
dent Roosevelt directed the Recon
struction Finance Corporation to
provide $85,000,000 to finance them.
The announcement was expected
to peg the price of corn at around
50 cents a bushel. December corn
recently sold at around 38 to 40
cents.
