# Pinza Will Appear At First Concert
## 
### 
Ezio Pinza, famous Italian basso,
will open the 1937-38 conceit series
season in the Memorial Hall No
vember 10.
Pinza is now appearing in the fall
opera season in San Francisco and
is considered one of the greatest
bassos of the day.
Tickets may be obtained at the
concert series office in the Old Un
ion. Prices are $2.50, $2, and $1.50
for single concert seats. Season
tickets are still available for gen
eral admission at $6-50, $5.50, and
$4.50. Student season tickets are
$5, $4, and $3.
