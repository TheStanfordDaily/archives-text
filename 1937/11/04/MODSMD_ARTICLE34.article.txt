# British Recognize Wally as Duchess
## 
### 
PARIS, Nov. 3 (UP).- The Duch
ess of Wiixlsor will receive official
British recognition of her status Fri
day for the first time since her mar
riage on June 3, when she and the
Duke are luncheon guests at the
British Embassy prior to their de
parture for the United States.
The luncheon invitation was ex
tended by Ambassador Sir Erie
Phipps, who obtained permission
from the British foreign office in
London and who will greet Edward's
Baltimore - born bride as "your
grace."
