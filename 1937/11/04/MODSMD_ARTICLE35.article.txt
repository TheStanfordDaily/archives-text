# Organist Will Give Fourth Program
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the fourth organ
recital of the fall season today at
4:15 o'clock in the Memorial Church.
His program consists of the Credo:
We All Believe in One God Creator,
by J. S. Bach; Soutenir, by E. H.
mare; Sarabanda, by Corelli; The
Adobe Mission . and Mirage from
"Scenes from a Mexican Desert," by
H. C. Nearing; and Lamento and
Toccato, by A. Berie.
