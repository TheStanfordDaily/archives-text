# Concert Tickets Continue on Sale
## 
### 
Contrary to announcement in The
Daily yesterday, tickets for the Con
cert Series program on Wednesday,
November 10, are still available in
the Conceit Series office in the Old
Union.
To avoid confusion the night of the
concert, tickets should be obtained
as soon as possible. A.S.S.U. cards
and receipts must be presented.
First of the four concerts sched
uled for the season will be Ezio
Pinza, noted basso of the Metropoli
tan Opera Company. Pinza will be
the first Concert Series artist to
appear in the Memorial Hall.
Concert-goers who arrive late will
not be admitted until after the first
group of numbers.
