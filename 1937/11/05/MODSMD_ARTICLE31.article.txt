# QUICK LEARNER
## 
### 
ROCHESTER, N.Y., Nov. 4 (U.R).—
Fred Coomber, 26, of Fairport,
thought he couldn't swim a stroke
until his car plunged into the Erie
Barge Canal near here. He uncon
sciously swam 25 feet to safety.
"I haven't any idea how I man
aged to get ashore," he said.
