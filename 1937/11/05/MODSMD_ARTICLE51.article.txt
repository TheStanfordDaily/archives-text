# Professor Kreps At Home' Sunday
## 
### 
Professor Theodore J. Kreps of
the Graduate School of Business will
be at home to students Sunday night
at 8:30, at his home, 2335 Waverlcy
Street. He will discuss his trip to
Geneva last summer.
The reception is sponsored by the
Roger Williams Club, and the Inter
national Club has been asked to
participate. Evelyn Jameson and
Winninette Arnold are in charge of
arrangements.
