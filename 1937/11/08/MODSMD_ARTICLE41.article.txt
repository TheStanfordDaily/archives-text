# PACKERS LOSE
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Nov. 7 (U.R). — The
world professional champion Green
Bay Packers today whipped the
league-leading Chicago Bears, 24 to
14, before a record crowd of 44,977
at Wrigley Field. It was the Bears'
first defeat of the season but they
still maintained their lead in the
Western Division.
