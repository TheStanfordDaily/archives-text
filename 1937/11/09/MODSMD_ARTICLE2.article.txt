# Editors Consider Research Project
## 
### 
Seventeen Pacific Coast newspa
per editors and college presidents,
under the direction of Professor
Rex F. Harlow of the Stanford
School of Education, met here yes
terday to discuss plans for a re
search project to determine the best
methods of presenting news of high
er education in daily newspapers.
The project, which is financed by
a $7,500 grant from Foster and
Kleiser of San Francisco, will in

newspapers with circulation of 10,000
or more, and the presidents of all
colleges of more than 500 enrollment
