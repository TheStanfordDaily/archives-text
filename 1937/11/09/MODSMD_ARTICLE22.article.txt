# Bailey Willis Set To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Bailey Willis, professor emer
itus of geology, will discuss his last
year's trip to the Orient in Memorial
Hall at 7:30 o'clock for the Tuesday
Evening Series.
Dr. Willis characterized the coun
tries which he will discuss: "Java is
a terrestrial paradise, Bali is Para
dise itself. India is Hell; the Philip
pines are a purgatory for transients;
Japan is the abode of fairies and
demons."
From September, 1936, to last
month of this year, Dr. and Mrs.
Willis traveled in Java, Bali, India,
the Philippines, and Japan. Part of
the time Dr. Willis spent in active
field work as a geologist for the Bu
reau of Science of the Philippines.
