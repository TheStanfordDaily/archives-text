# Syllabus Fees Due Today Before 5:00
## 
### 
I Five o'clock this afternoon is the
deadline for payment of syllabus
fees. The bills are to be paid at
the cashier's office in the Adminis
tration Building by cash or check.
If mailed, the envelope must have a
November 9 postmark.
All tardy payments will be fined
$2; if the syllabus fee is less than
that amount, a sum corresponding
to the fee. If fees are not paid by
| November 16, registration is subject
