# Women Will Enter Hockey Play Day
## 
### 
Women's hockey teams from Stan
ford will compete in an interschool
play day at the University of Cali
fornia on November 13. Stanford,
California, and Mills College will be
represented with four teams from
feach school. Ratings will be scored
on an interclass basis.
Those taking part in the play day
will leave the campus on Saturday
morning in buses provided by the
W.A.A.
