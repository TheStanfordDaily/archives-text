# Committee Plans Armistice Service
## 
### 
A short Remembrance Service will
be held from 10:45 to 11 o'clock No
vember 11 in Memorial Church, the
Committee on Public Exercises an
nounced yesterday. Ten o'clock
classes will be dismissed at 10:40.
A wreath will be placed by Tom
Killefer and Barbara Murphy, A. S.
S. U. president and vice-president,
and the choir will sing. A brief pe
riod of silence will be observed at
11 o'clock, the hour of the Armis
tice signing in 1918.
