# Roble Club Hears Dean Yost Speak
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost spoke to the
freshmen women last night at Roble
Club's weekly meeting on the de
velopment. and workings of student
government. She showed how the
control merged from class organi
zations to living groups, and also
pointed out the fact that women first
advocated student government at
Stanford.
Miss Yost advised the girls as to
what qualities they should take into
consideration when voting in today's
election for freshman class officers.
