# Conference Grants Gaieties 12:30's
## 
### 
Automatic 12:30's for Big Game
Gaieties night were granted to all
campus living groups yesterday by
Women's Conference.
This means that all women's living
groups will be open until that hour,
and it will not be necessary to sign
out unless a later leave is taken.
Women's Conference recommends
chaperonage be arranged for all
groups going to Del Monte Big
Game Night.
