# Red Cross Drive Starts Tomorrow
## 
### 
The 1937 Red Cross drive will
start at Stanford tomorrow, and
continue for one week, Chairman
Jan North said today. She asks co
operation with her and the commit
tee of Roy Thompson, Beth Allen,
and Hap Dey.
One-half of the funds collected go
to the national division of the Red
Cross, and one-half to the local
chapter.
