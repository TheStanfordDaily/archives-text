# Farm Sailing Club To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
President Frank Lipman has set
the first meeting of the Stanford
Sailing Club for 7 o'clock tonight in
Room 200 F. All members ai*e asked
to be present and anyone interested
in sailing is invited.
John Bowman, who last spring
represented Stanford in the Pacific
Coast Intercollegiate Regatta, will
give an account of the series and
the plans for this year's activity will
be made. President Lippman plans
for a full schedule and all those who
hope to take part must be present
at this first meeting.
