# Armistice Day Service To Be Held in Chapel
## 
### 
Ten o'clock classes will be dis
missed at 10:40 o'clock tomorrow to
enable students and faculty mem
bers to attend a Remembrance
Service in commemoration of Ar
mistice Day.
The service, sponsored by the
Committee on Public Exercises,
will be held from 10:45 to 11 o'clock
in Memorial Church. The signing
of the Armistice in 1918 will be ob
served with a brief period of silence
at 11 o'clock.
A.S.S.U. President Tom Killefer
and Vice-President Barbara Mur
phy will dedicate a wreath. The
choir will present a musical pro
gram.
