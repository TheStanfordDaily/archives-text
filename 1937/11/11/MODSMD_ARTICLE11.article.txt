# R.O.T.C. Battery, Band Will Parade
## 
### 
The Stanford band and a battery
of the Stanford R.O.T.C. will inarch
in the Palo Alto Armistice Day pa
rade at 9:45 o'clock today.
Colonel Peyton, professor of mili
tary science and tactics, is to be the
grand marshal of the parade. Stu
dents who are in the band and
those in the R.O.T.C. who are to
take part in the parade will be ex
cused from their 9 and 10 o'clock
classes.
