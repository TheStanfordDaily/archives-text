# Group To Tune In 'Brave New Worlds'
## 
### 
A meeting of the Spanish-Ameri
can discussion group will be held
Monday, November, 15, at 7:30
o'clock in the auditorium of the
Women's Clubhouse for the purpose
of listening to the broadcast, "Brave
New Worlds," dealing with Latin
America.
At 8 o'clock Dr. Alfred Coester of
the Romanic Languages Department
will give a talk on Mayan civiliza
tion which will be illustrated with
slides.
