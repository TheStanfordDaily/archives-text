# Officers Elected By Education Club
## 
### 
New officers of Ihe Education
Club were elected last night at a
party in the lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse by members of Phi
Delta Kappa and Pi Lambda Theta,
men's and women's professional edu
cation societies.
The new officers are Clarence An
gel, president; Leland Cook, first
vice-president; Tully Warren, sec
ond vice-president; and Eva Rade
macker, secretary -treasurer.
The party was planned by Elea
nore Eddy and refreshments of cof
fee, ice cream and cake were served.
Dean Kefauver gave a short talk to
the gathering.
