# GOLD SENT TO ENGLAND
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 10 (U.R).—
The Treasury announced today that
a shipment of $5,000,000 in gold to
England will leave New York on the

liner Aquitania tomorrow. This is
the second major shipment within a
week. A shipment of $10,250,000 is
moving to France on the liner Nor
mandie today.
