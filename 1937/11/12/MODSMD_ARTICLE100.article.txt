# KLAHOYA TO SEE GAME
## 
### 
Members of the Klahoya Luncheon
Club have completed plans for group
attendance at the W.S.C. game.
Those who wish to go are asked to
meet at 1:30 in the Women's Club
house, and to wear rooter's outfit.

Dance music rates as "good" mu
sic if it serves the purpose for which
it was written, Warren D. Allen,
University organist, will contend in
a broadcast over KGO Monday
night, at 9 o'clock.
