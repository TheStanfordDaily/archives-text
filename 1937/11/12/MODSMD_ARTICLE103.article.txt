# Organist Will Give Recital on Sunday
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give a concert next Sun
day at 4 o'clock in the Memorial
Church.
He will repeat the program pre
sented yesterday: Fantasia in D mi
nor, by J. M. Beobide; Recit de tierce
en taille, by Nicolas de Grigny; Sea
Surge, by Julian Nesbitt; Fantasia
on the Choral, Paternoster, by Her
man Grabnor; Rich and Poor, by
Moussorgsky; and Prayer for Peace,
by Paul Held.
