# FIRST NEWSMAN KILLED
## 
### 
SHANGHAI, Nov. 11 (U.R).—Pem
broke Stephens, correspondent for
the London Daily Telegraph, was
killed today. He was the first for
eign newspaperman to die in the
Far Eastern war.
