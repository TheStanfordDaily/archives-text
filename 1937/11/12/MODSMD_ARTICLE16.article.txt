# OIL CO. DENIES CHARGES
## 
### 
FRESNO, Nov. 11 (U.R).—Standard
Oil Company of California attorneys
today denied "trespassing" charges
made by the Government in its 22-
million-dollar suit to recover title to
oil lands in Elk Hills.
