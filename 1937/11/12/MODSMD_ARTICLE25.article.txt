# Big Game Jolly-up To Feature Griff
## 
### 
With Griff Williams set to play
for the Big Game jolly-up next Fri
day afternoon, plans are under way
for the traditional dance, to be held
in the Encina Pavilion.
The joily-up will last from 4 to 5
o'clock. Dave Ryus, in charge of
plans. announced the following
committee heads: patrons and pa
tronesses, Kitty Lawrence; decora
tion, Jack Laney, assistant, Walt
Meyer; and publicity, Jim Stone.
