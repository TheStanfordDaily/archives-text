# GOODING, MONTANA 6-6
## 
### 
GOODING, Ida., Nov. 11 (UP). -
Gooding College and Montana Mines
fought to a 6-6 tie here today in a
football game featured by the
Mines' long passes and two 35-yard
runs by Prince of Gooding, who
scored in first three minutes of play.
