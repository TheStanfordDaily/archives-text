# Biology Professor To Act on Board
## 
### 
Dr. Wiilis H. Rich, professor of
biology at Stanford, will spend part
of his sabbatical leave of absence,
which commences at the end of the
winter quarter, in the service of the
Oregon State Fish Commission.
His work will include the investi
gation of the salmon situation at the
Bonneville dam and the compilation
of data on various sea fish, to be used
in the regulation of the industry.
Dr. Rich, a member of the Stanford
faculty since 1930, was formerly head
of the research division of the Uni
ted States Bureau of Fisheries.
