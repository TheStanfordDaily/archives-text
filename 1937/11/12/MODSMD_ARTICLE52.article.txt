# Edison Memorial Beacon
## 
### 

THIS GIANT electric bulb, 14 feet high, has been completed at a
glass works in Corning, N.Y., as a land beacon on the $100,000 Edison
Memorial Tower at Menlo Park, N.J. More than 6000 pounds of
amber-tinted, heat-resisting glass were fitted over a steel skeleton
in two-inch diamond patterns. The combined hull) and skeleton
weighs six tons.
