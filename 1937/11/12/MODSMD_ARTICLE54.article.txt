# Guerard Will Talk At Club Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. Albert Guerard, for 19 years
professor of general literature at
Stanford, will lecture today at the
luncheon meeting of the Common
wealth Club of California. He will
speak on "France and the 'Little
World War'."
Dr. Guerard, noted news com
mentator of today, was bom in
Paris and studied at Sorbonne. Dur
ing the World War he was in the
intelligence and liaison service in
the United States Army. A bril
liant writer and historian of France,
Dr. Guerard was the Club gold
medal winner of 1936 for his work,
"Literature and Society."

There are now six "husband and
wife" teams teaching at the Cleve
land College of Western Reserve
University.
