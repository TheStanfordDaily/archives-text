# Wisconsin Honors Farm Instructor
## 
### 
Board of Regents of the University
of Wisconsin announced yesterday
that Virgil Anderson, Stanford in
structor of public speaking since
1919, has been awarded the Ph.D.
degree.
This award is Dr. Anderson's
third, already having the A.B. de
gree from Willamette University and
an A.M. from Stanford. He was one
of 350 students who received their
degrees, having completed require
ments during this summer's session
at Wisconsin.
