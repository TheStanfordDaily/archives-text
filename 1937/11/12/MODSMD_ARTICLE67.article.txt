# Second Row Theft Of Year Revealed
## 
### 
Second fraternity house theft of
the year occurred last Wednesday
when- campus police were notified
of the disappearance of a sum of $8
from the rooms of Ray Fremlin and
Dale Reimer in the Delta Chi house.
According to Officer Gordy Davis
no clues have been found as yet
which might lead to the arrest of a
suspect.
