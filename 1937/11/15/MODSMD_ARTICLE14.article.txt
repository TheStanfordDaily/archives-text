# Y.W.C.A. To Hold Vacation Meeting
## 
### 
Representatives from more than
40 western colleges are expected to
attend the Y.W.C.A. conference at
Asilomar from December 2G to Jan
uary 2. Many students who will be
unable to go home for Christmas are
planning to be present.
John Thompson, Westminster
Foundation director and member of
the faculty of Oklahoma University,
is to be platform speaker. Others
who will contribute are Kirby
Page, well known author, D. Elton
Trueblood, University chaplain,
George Coo, professor emeritus of
religion at. Columbia University, and
Dewey Anderson, Stanford voca
tional expert. All those interested
may sign up now at the Y.W.C.A.
rooms.
