# Post-Game Dance Planned by Toyon
## 
### 
Toyon Club will hold an after-
Big Game jolly-up and open house
this Saturday afternoon, from 4:30
to 6 o'clock in the hall's main lobby.
Owen Clarke's 11-piece orchestra
will play, featuring songs and sing
ers from the Big Game Gaieties. Re
freshments will be served during
the jolly-up.
All California and Stanford stu
dents are invited to attend the open
house, which will begin immediately
after the end of the game. During
open hours, residents may have
women guests in their rooms.
Henry Hughes heads the commit
tee in charge of the affair. He will
be assisted by Phil Olsen, Walter
Hartzell, and Jack Brewster.
