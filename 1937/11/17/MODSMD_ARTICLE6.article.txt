# Toyon To Sponsor Open House, Dance
## 
### 
Toyon After-the-Big-Game Jolly-
Up and Open House will be held
this Saturday from 4:30 to 6 o'clock.
All California and Stanford students
have been invited to attend.
The jolly-up will feature Owen
Clarke's orchestra and Gaieties
songs and stars. Jack Baker, noted
campus whistler, who has performed
with Paul Pendarvis, Phil Harris,
and Carol Lofner, will whistle sev
eral numbers with Clarke's band.*
