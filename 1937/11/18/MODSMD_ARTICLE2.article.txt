# Moot Court Tries New Case Tonight
## 
### 
Law students will swing into the
fourth of their moot court trials
this afternoon at 3:15 o'clock in
Room 12 of the Law Building.
Opposing forces in tonight's case
will be Mary Flor and Sid de GofT
versus Pete Peck and Bob Grantier.
Professor Arthur Cathcart will act
as judge.
Tonight's case is that of Herman
Hotsteak v. the Cafe Workers' Un
ion No. 21 and Foo Ong U, man
ager of the Bubbling-Over Coffee
Company.
Next trial will be Monday night,
November 22.
