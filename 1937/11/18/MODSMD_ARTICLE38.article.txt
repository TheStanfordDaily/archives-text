# TOLL CUT PROPOSED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Nov. 17 (UP).
—A. R. O'Brien, Ukiah, a director
of the Golden Gate Bridge and
Highway District, today proposed to
the directorate that bridge tolls be
cut to 30 cents one way, 50 cents
round trip, to meet cut rate automo
| bile ferry competition.
