# Trueblood To Lead Service in Chapel
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trucblood, Univer
sity chaplain, will preach the ser
mon for services in Memorial Chapel
on Sunday at 11 o'clock.
Organ prelude to the services will
he "To God Alone on High Be
Praise," by Bach, while the choir
will sing as the introit, "May Our
Mouths Br Filled with Thy Praise,"
by Rachmaninoff, and ris the an
them, ' Rejoice in the Lord Alway,"
by Purcell. Subject of the sermon
has not been announced.
