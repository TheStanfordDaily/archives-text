# SENATE BEGINS HEARINGS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 18 (UP).—
Senator Joseph C. O'Mahoney, D.,
today announced a judiciary sub
committee will begin hearings De
cember 15 on a Senate resolution
memorializing the Governor of Cali
fornia to grant a full pardon to Tom
Mooney.
