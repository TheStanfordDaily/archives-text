# O.S.C.'s Gray Fit For Cougar Battle
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Ore., Nov. 18 (U.R).—
The famed passing arm of Joe Gray
had received a heavy workout today
as the Oregon State Beavers con
tinued preparations for their home
coming clash here Saturday against
the Washington State College eleven.
