# TWO MISSIONS SAFE
## 
### 
BOSTON, Nov. 21 (UP).—Ameri
can missionaries in Taiku and Fen
chow, China, were unharmed and
mission property was undamaged,
according to latest reports to the
American Board of Foreign Mis
sions.
