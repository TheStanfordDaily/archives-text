# G.M. PLANS TO REOPEN
## 
### 
PONTIAC, Mich., Nov. 22 (U.R).—
Plans to start the wheels turning
again in General Motors plants,
closed to 14,721 employees here for
six days because of a sit-down strike
in the Fisher Body Division, were
rushed tonight by officials ot the
automobile corporation.
