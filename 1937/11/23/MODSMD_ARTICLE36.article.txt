# Doctor Lectures Today on Viruses
## 
### 
Dr. W. J. Elford of the National
Institute for Medical Research,
Hampstead, London, will lecture at
4:15 o'clock today in Room 20 of the
Chemistry Building. Dr. Elford will
talk on "Present Knowledge of the
Physical Properties of Viruses."
The lecture is being presented un
der the auspices of the Stanford
Chapter of the Society of Sigma Xi
and by the Chemistry and Biology
Seminars. The public is invited.
