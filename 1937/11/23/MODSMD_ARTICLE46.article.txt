# Roble, Kappa Play Basketball Today
## 
### 
June Murphy's Roble team will
play the Kappas this afternoon in
the semi-finals of the women's in
tramural basketball tournament.
Yesterday, Chi O's beat the Pi
Phi's 8-7, bringing them in line to
meet the winner of today's Roble-
Kappa game in the finals to be held
after Thanksgiving.
