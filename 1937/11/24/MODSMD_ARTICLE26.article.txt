# G.E. MAN TO SPEAK
## 
### 
M. M. Boring, of the General
Electric Company, Schenectady, will
visit the Stanford campus next
Tuesday and Wednesday to outline
for local engineering students the
training course which his company
makes available to college men each
year. Private interviews with Mr.
Boring may be arranged with Miss
Tracksell in the University Employ
ment Office.
