Don't Write! Don't Telegraph!
Send the Daily! Better than a letter,

formative than either —that's the "Daily,"
and that's what the folks back home will
say about it.

ford. They're interested! And the "Daily"

complete picture. Only the "Daily" can
give it to them. Subscribe now! $3.25 for
the rest of the year, and the bill can go
home, too.
