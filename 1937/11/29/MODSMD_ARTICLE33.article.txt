# Suit Won Against Palo Alto Hospital
## 
### 
John T. Simpkin, San Mateo Coun
ty deputy sheriff, was awarded $30,-
000 damages against the City of
Palo Alto and Stanford University
jointly in the superior court at San
Jose last week for a fall which he
suffered fi"om a window of the Palo
Alto Hospital.
Simpkin had asked for $100,000 in
his damage suit. After deliberating
five hours, the jury decided upon the
$30,00 award. Two physicians, who
had been named as defendants along
with the city and the University,
were excluded from liability by the
jury.
