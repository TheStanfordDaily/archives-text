# CYCLIST SEEKS MOTHER
## 
### 
CINCINNATI, Nov. 28 (UP).—
Louis Sparks, 32, today completed a
2500-mile bicycle trip from a Nevada
ranch to Cincinnati, seeking his
mother, Mrs. Bestina Sparks, from
whom he was separated when a
child.
