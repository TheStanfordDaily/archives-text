# Prince Injured in Automobile Crash
## 
### 
AMSTERDAM. Nov. 29 (UP). -
Prince Consort Bernhard Zu Lippe-
Biester-Feld, husband of Crown
Princess Juliana, was injured in an

the brain and through his condition
was not considered critical, physi

Police assured Princess Juliana, who
is expecting a baby in January, and
his mother-in-law, Queen Wilhel
