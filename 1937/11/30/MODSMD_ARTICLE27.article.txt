# Gold from Alaska Doubles Price Paid
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 29 (UP).—
Annual gold shipments from Alaska
are more than double the price paid
for the territory by the United States
70 years ago, Alaskan Governor John
W. Troy revealed today.
Troy said that gold shipments for
the fiscal year ending June 30 totaled
$15,877,705. During the administra
tion of President Andrew Johnson,
shortly after the Civil War, the Uni
ted States paid Russia $7,200,000 for
title to the land.
