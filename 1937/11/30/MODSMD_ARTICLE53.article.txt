# LASH TAKES TITLE
## 
### 
NEWARK, N.J., Nov. 29 (UP).—
Donald Ray Lash, corn - thatched
Hoosier from Auburn, Ind., extended
his rule over the nation's cross
country runners today with his 4th
straight victory in the annual cham
pionship event. He is the first run
ner in 40 years to accomplish the
feat.
