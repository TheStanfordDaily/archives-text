# OLYMPIC DIVER ILL
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 29 (UP).—
A mysterious ailment left the supple
legs of Georgia Coleman, former
Olympic diver, partially paralyzed
today. Attending physicains stated
that the malady was not spinal men
ingitis, as was earlier feared. They
were unable to diagnose the ail
ment.
