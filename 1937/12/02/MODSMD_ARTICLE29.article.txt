# Funeral Set For Stanford Alumnus
## 
### 
Funeral services for Walter Rod
gers, Stanford alumnus and a prac
ticing attorney in Palo Alio for the
last 35 years, will be held today at
2 o'clock in Stanford Memorial
Church with interment at Cypress
Lawn.
Mr. Rodgers was affiliated with
Sigma Nu fraternity and Phi Delta
Phi, legal fraternity.
