# Deposits Due For Mens Hall Rooms
## 
### 
Reservations for rooms in Sequoia
and Union living quarters for the
winter term must be made either
today or tomorrow by the payment
of a $5 deposit fee at the office of
the Dean of Men. Failure to pay
this fee will amount to forfeiture
lof present rooms for the coming
quarter for all men now residing in
these living groups.
Similar deposits must be made
for room reservations in Toyon and
Branner next Monday or Tuesday.
According to the Dean of Men's of
fice, no rooms will be held without
payment of this fee.
