# Med School Tests Given Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Medical Aptitude Test spon
sored by the Association of Ameri
can Medical Colleges will be given
next Friday at 2 o'clock in Room
214. A fee of $1 will be collected
at this time.
This test is required for admission
to the Stanford Medical School and
for most other medical schools.
Since this is the only time .it will be
&iven, it is necessary that all appli
cants for autumn, 1938, take it at
this time.
