# Allen Plays Today In Organ Concert
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present his weekly or
gan recital today at 4:15 o'clock. The
program will include the following
selections:
"Prelude for Advent," on "Veni
Emmanuel," Arthur Egerton; "O
Thou That Tellest Good Tidings to !
Zion" (Messiah), Handel; "Noel of i
the Bressan Waits," Darcieux; "No
els sur les Flutes," Daquin; "Vision,"
Rheinberger; "Marche religieuse,"!
on a theme of Handel, Guilmant. j
The same recital will be given on ;
Sunday at 4 o'clock.
