# Traffic Justifies Decrease of Toll
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Dec. 2 (UP).—
A flat 50-cent toll for cars crossing
the Carquinez Bridge would give a
"reasonable return" to the American
Toll Bridge Company, owner of the
span, transportation experts testified
at a hearing before the California
Railroad Commission, which re
sumed today.
