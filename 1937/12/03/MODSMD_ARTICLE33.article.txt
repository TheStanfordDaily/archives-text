# Roble To Sponsor Christmas Dinner
## 
### 
Christmas spirit will shine forth
from Roble Sunday night, December
12, when the annual Christmas din
ner is held. The lobby will be dec
orated with two large trees, a fire
will be blazing in the hearth, and
women, will come down to dinner
carrying lighted candles.
Miss Yost will attend the dinner,
of which Caroline Hibbard is chair
man. After dinner everyone will
join in singing Christmas carols, led
by a smaller group of singers.
