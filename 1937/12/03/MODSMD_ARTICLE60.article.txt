# 'Y' Candle Service Planned for Xmas
## 
### 
A Christmas Candlelight Service
will be conducted by the Y.W.C.A.
in the Chapel on Sunday, Dec. 12, at
5 o'clock/
The program, which is entirely
planned by the leaders of the "Y,"
will consist of carols and a talk by
a speaker unnamed as yet. On the
program! committee are Jackie
Brown, Mary Morris Boggs, and
Mary Beaton. Those in charge of
arrangements are Olive Freeman,
Mary K. Horton. and Jean Bolton.
The service will not last over 40
minutes so that students will have
sufficient time to go to their living
group parties that night. Non-mem
bers are cordially invited to attend.
