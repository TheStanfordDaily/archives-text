# Chi O Hoopsters Win Mural Final
## 
### 
In the finals of women's intra
mural basketball, the Chi O's de
feated the Kappas 17 to 12, to close
the season undefeated.
During winter quarter, there will
be intramural competition for women
in badminton and pingpong. In the
badminton contests women's doubles
and singles and mixed doubles will
be played.
Any women interested in turning
out for intramural should get in
touch with Didi Vibert.
