# MEXICO SUFFERS QUAKES
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Jan. 3 (UP).—
Mexico has suffered more than 200
earth tremor in 10 days, including
a severe one yesterday, Dr. Fran
cisco Patino Ordaz, director of the
National Seismological Service, said
today.
