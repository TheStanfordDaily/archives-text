# Ichihashi Views Japan Tonight
## Professor To Interpret Oriental Difficulties 
### 
Professor Yamato Ichihashi of the
history department will open the
Tuesday evening series with a talk
on the "Observation of Japan in
Time of War," tonight at 7:30 o'clock
in Memorial Hall.
In view of prevailing interest in
the Far East, Professor Ichihashi
will describe the present attitude of
the Japanese people as he observed
it during a recent trip to the Orient,
and will attempt to interpret the
situation in the light of its historical
significance.
Spending most of his time in Tok
yo, the Stanford authority engaged
in research from August to Decem
ber in ancient civilization and col
lected a valuable folio of photo
graphs of art objects which will be
on view in the Green Room after the
lecture.
