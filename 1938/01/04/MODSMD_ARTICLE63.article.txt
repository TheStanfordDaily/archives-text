# Intramural Hoop Lists Open Today
## 
### 
Entries for intramural basketball
and handball will open this after
noon on the Encina Gym bulletin
board, Phil Klauber, manager, an
nounced yesterday. Competition in
these sports will begin next week,
while track and field, the remaining
winter quarter event, will be held
next month.
Basketball teams are warned of
the rule which permits players to
participate with one team only in
each sport, no matter how many
squads are entered by the organiza
tions. Encina teams must comprise
players living in the same floor and
wing, and will not be allowed to
compete without adhering to this
rule.
