# POLITICAL ARMS FOUND IN PARIS
## 
### 

PARIS POLICE continue to discover caches of arms they assert were assembled by Les Cagoulards
(The Hooded Men) with intent to institute a political rebellion. Above is one of the most recent and larg
est of the caches. Stores of cartridges bore the manufactory mark of a Toledo. Spain, munitions factory.
The men at the left arc Paris detectives.
