# World Affairs Club To Hear Guerard
## 
### 
"Franco-American Relations" will
be the subject of a talk by Albert
Guerard, professor of general liter
ature at Stanford, at a meeting of
the World Affairs Group to be held
tomorrow evening at the home of
Mrs. David Starr Jordan, 330 Serra
Road, at 7:45 o'clock.
The talk will be followed by an
informal discussion. All interested
students may make reservations
with Mrs. E. P. Lesley, P.A. 21528.
