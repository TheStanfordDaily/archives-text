# Heberer To Review Play For Dames
## 
### 
H. M. Heberer will review Max
well Anderson's prize-winning play,
"High Tor," which ran in New York
last year, at a meeting of the Stan
ford Dames in the Women's Club
house today at 3 o'clock. Mr. Heb
erer directed "Trial by Jury," which
was given in the fall, and is direct
ing "Governor's Wife," the next pro
duction to be given in the Little
Theater.
The wives of all Stanford students
are eligible for membership in the
Dames and are urged to attend.
Children will be taken care of at
the clubhouse with a charge of 10
cents for each child.
