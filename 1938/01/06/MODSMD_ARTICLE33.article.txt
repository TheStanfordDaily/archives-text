# AIR MAIL MILEAGE UPPED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 5 (UP). —
The Senate today adopted a House
bill which would increase the route
mileage of the airmail system from
32,000 to 35.000 miles. The measure
now goes to the White House.
