# Mulloy Nets Win In Miami Match
## 
### 
CORAL GABLES, Fla., Jan. 5
(UP). —Gardner Mulloy, captain of
the Univeristy of Miami tennis
team, entered the quarter - final
round of the Miami Biltmore cham
pionships today by defeating Bernie
Frank of New York. Frank, yester
day's surprise winner over Wilmer
Hines of Hollywood, lost to Mulloy
in straight sets, 6-1, 6-4.
Diek McKee of Miami scored a
second-round victory over Leonard
Clark of New York, 6-3, 6-4. The
victory gave him the right to play
Bobby Riggs of Los Angeles, Amer
ica's second-ranking netman and
top-seeded in the tournament. Riggs
is expected to meet Defending
Champion Bryan "Bitsy" Grant in
the finals.
