# 'Cosi Fan Tutte' Next on Series
## 
### 
Opera comes to Stanford next
Tuesday night in Memorial Hall
when the Salzburg Opera Guild of
Austria will present Mozart's "Cosi
Fan Tutte" as the second of the
A.S.S.U. sponsored concert series.
The Guild is presenting its orig
inal cast and repertoire used dur
ing the festival season in Salzburg
and on its coast-to-coast tour which
began in New York in the early
fall.
Tickets are on sale at the Stan
ford Concert Series Office in the Old
Union as well as at Keeble's in
Palo Alto for $2.50, $2, and $1.50.
Student season tickets are still on
sale for the original prices. All seats
for this concert! are subject to sell
out.
