# Club Will Show Movie on Sunday
## 
### 
Holding its first meeting of the
winter quarter, the Roger Williams
Club will meet Sunday night at 8:30
o'clock in the Theta Xi house.
Feature of the program will be a
provue of a movie in color taken by
Walter Jameson while he was tour
ing Europe. Friends of club mem
bers are invited to attend.
