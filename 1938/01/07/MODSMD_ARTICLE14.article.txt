# 'I'D RATHER SEE THAN BE ONE'
## 
### 

"BECOMING UNSTEREOTYPED by doing other work," has set VV'olo, former caricaturist for the Sail
Francisco Chronicle, at the task of providing murals in the Convalescent Home dining room. At top is a
completed panel, described simply as "Carnal Contentment" With a "dressed for dinner" Mammy Sea
Lion, lower right, prepared to serve, Wolo demonstrates on her hungry progeny how he applies paints with
his fingertips.
