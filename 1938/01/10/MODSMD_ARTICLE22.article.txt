# Howes To Speak Oil Life of Gibbs
## 
### 
The Chemistry Department will
hold its weekly seminar today in
Room 20 of the Chemistry Build
ing, at 4:10 o'clock. Dr. L. I. Hewes,
of the U.S. Bureau of Public Roads,
San Francisco, will speak on "Per
sonal Reminiscences of J. Willard
Gibbs."
Dr. P. Van Rysselberghe will also
talk, his subject being "Comments
on the Recently Published 'Com
mentary on the Scientific Writings
of J. Willard Gibbs'."
