Flax from which linen is made will
grow better in Texas than any
where else in the nation, according
to R. H. Stanzel, chief of the Texas
agricultural experiment station here.
In a report on flax culture, Stanzel
advised farmers of the Texas coastal
region to try the new crop, pointing
out that 20,000.000 bushels of flax
are imported annually.
