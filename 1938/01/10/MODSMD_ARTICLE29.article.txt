# CRASH KILLS ONE
## 
### 
LOS GATOS, Jan. 9 (UP).—Mar
garet Wesley, 20. of Oakland, .was
killed, and Jernell Vither, 22, Oak
land, critically injured today when
their automobile plunged into a
canyon four miles from here.
