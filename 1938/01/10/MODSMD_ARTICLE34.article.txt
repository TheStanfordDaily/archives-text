# Intramural Parade....
## 
### 
Intramural handball matches will
start this afternoon, with upper
teams making arrangements for each
pairing. Thirty-two teams signed the
entry list and the bracket drawings
will be posted today.
A new record for any intramural
sport of 64 teams entries was set at
closing time Friday. These have
been arranged into 13 leagues ac
cording to the intramural manager,
and play will begin at 7 o'clock,
Wednesday night. Six games are
played per night throughout winter
quarter.
