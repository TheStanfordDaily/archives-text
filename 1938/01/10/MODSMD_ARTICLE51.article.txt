# Gym Glass Offered To Faculty Women
## 
### 
The gymnasium class for faculty
and townswomen will be offered
again during winter quarter, Miss
Catherine A. Worthinham, directou
of the course, announced yesterday.
Consisting of rhythm and gym
nastics, the class will meet at 10
a.m. on Mondays, Wednesdays, and
Fridays. For further information on
the Stanford Women's Gymnasium,
telephone P.A.-.9411, Local 358.
