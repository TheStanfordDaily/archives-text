# Wilbur Declines Installation Bid
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur an
nounced yesterday that he will be
unable to accept a recently received
invitation to attend the formal in
auguration of Dr. Oliver C. Car
michael as chancellor of Vanderbilt
University, which will take place in
Nashville, Tenn., on February 5.

president said that he is endeavoring
to secure a delegate to represent
Stanford at the ceremony.
