# Klahoya Club Will Meet Noon Today
## 
### 
Klahoya Club will hold its first
meeting for winter quarter at noon
today in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dorothy Brown, president of the
off-campus group, announced that
although there will be no speaker
today, Dean Mary Yost, Jean
Hemphill. Wixmie Jean Smith, and
Dolly Hyatt will be guests later on
in the quarter. New students are
invited to attend and become ac
quainted with the club, which of
fers wider social contacts to those
not living in halls or on the Row.
