# Comedian's Orphan Son
## 
### 

WHETHER HE'S yawning or crying is a question, but he is John
Jacoi) Hcaly, son of the film comedian who died suddenly of heart
trouble shortly after the baby was born in a Culver City hospital.
Mrs. Betty Hickman Hcaly, widow of the comedian, shown with her
baby above, said she had no other plans than rearing her son.
