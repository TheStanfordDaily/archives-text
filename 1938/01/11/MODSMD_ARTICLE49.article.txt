# Brand Speaks On Farm Radio Hour
## 
### 
Dr. Carl F. Brand, associate pro
fessor of history at Stanford, traced
the political career of the late Ram
sey Mac Donald in a talk over KGO
last night.
Calling him the "Scotsman who
more than any other of his nation
imprinted his personality on the
history of the twentieth century,"
Dr. Brand reviewed Mac Donald's
long career in the limelight begin
ning with the statesman's early La
bor leadership and concluding with
his final Conservative affiliations.
