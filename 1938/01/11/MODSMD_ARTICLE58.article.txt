# AUTO CRASHES KILL 13
## 
### 
INDIANAPOLIS, Ind., Jan. 10
(UP). —Twelve persons, including a
baby born after the mother's death,
were killed in automobile accidents
in central Indiana yesterday. Ten
persons were injured.
