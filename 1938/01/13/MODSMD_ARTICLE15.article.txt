# Growers Protest Increased Hates
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Jan. 12 (UP).—
The California Fruit Growers' Ex
change, one of the nation's most
powerful agricultural groups, today
strongly opposed the applications of
the Railroad Association of Atnrel
ca for a general 15 per cent freight
rate increase.
