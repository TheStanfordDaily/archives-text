# Officers Elected By Stanford A.S.U.
## 
### 
Elections for officers and mem
bers of the Executive Committee of
the Stanford chapter of the Ameri
can Student Union were held
Thursday, January 13, at the first
meeting of the organization.
Those elected were: Mark Hop
kins, president; Estelle Seid, secre
tary; Dan McComish, treasurer.
Other members of the committee
are: Otis Brubaker, Beth Allen,
Jean Bird, Betty Watkins, and Bob
Elkins. The next open meeting will
be held Wednesday at 4 o'clock in
the Women's Clubhouse.
