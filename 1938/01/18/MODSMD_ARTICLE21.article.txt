# Orchesis To Hold Tryouts Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts and casting for the spring
demonstration will take place at the
regular meeting of Orchesis Society
this evening, according to Elise Co
hen, president. All members are
urged to be present, and those not
attending the meeting in the Dance
Studio of the Women's Gym
nasium at 7:15 o'clock will be ex
cluded from participation.
Tryouts for women to join Or
chesis will be held on Feb. 1. Re
quirements include at least one
quarter's work in dance or rhythm,
certain technical proficiency, and
attendance at a dance class or Jun
ior Orchesis. Tryoutees will see
Margaret Jewell at the Women's
Gymnasium for further information.
