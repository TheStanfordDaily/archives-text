KANSAS CITY, Mo., Jan. 17
(UP). —Officials of the United Auto
mobile Workers Union, which Is
striking against the Ford assembly
plant said today that an attack on
their organizer, Carl Stevens, 27,
was "the climax to a series of un
speakable outrages." Stevens suf
fered a fractured skull late Satur
day night when four men accosted
him on a highway near the plant.

KILGOKE, Tex., Jan. 17 (UP).—A
blazing oil well in the heart of this
booming east Texas city threatened
several large buildings tonight as
firemen from five cities fought to
bring it under control. The fires al
ready had caused $130,000 damage.
