# A.S.S.U. Election Set February 8
## 
### 
Student body elections Feb. 8 will
fill 11 vacancies in student govern
ment positions. Men's Council
members, John Dern, Stanford
Zimet, and Edmund Barnett will
be replaced by Junior men with the
required 120 units.
Women's Council posts, now held
by Jean Hemphill, Barbara Alex
ander, and Betty Hellyer will be
filled by Junior women holding 90
units.
Also elected at this time will be
a graduate student to take the place
of James McCormick, a junior man
for Jack Calderwood's post and a
sophomore woman for that of Jean
Reynolds. Either Bill Todd or Lo
max Turner will be selected for
next year's yell leader.
