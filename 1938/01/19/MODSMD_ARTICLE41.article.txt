# Erb Will Address Oregon Students
## 
### 
Donald M. Erb, Stanford professor
of economics chosen last month for
the presidency of the University of
Oregon, left Palo Alto last night to
make his first appearance before
the Oregon student body.
Dr. Erb will speak informally to
the Eugene students at an assembly
tomorrow morning. Friday eve
ning, he will speak to Oregon edi
tors and publishers attending the
Oregon press conference at Eugene.
Dr. Erb expects to return to Stan
ford Sunday. He will take up his
official duties as president of Oregon
in March.
