# McCarthy signs
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 18 (UP). —
Johnny McCarthy, New York Giants'
youthful first baseman, had signed
his 1938 contract today. In 1937, his
first full year with the Giants, Mc-
Carthy appeared in 114 games and
batted .278.
