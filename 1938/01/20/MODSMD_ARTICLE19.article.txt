# Kendall To Present 3rd Organ Recital
## 
### 
The third organ recital of the win
ter quarter will be presented this
afternoon and Sunday at 4:15 o'clock
in the Memorial Church by Ray
mond Kendall, Assistant University
Organist.
Following is the program to be
given: Prelude and Fugue in C
Major, J. S. Bach; Air, G. Tartini;
Sketch in D Flat, Robert Schumann;
.Chorale-Prelude on "My Inmost
Hearth Doth Yearn," Johannes
Brahms; Piece Heroique, Cesar
Franck.
