# Ross Body Aim Of G-Man Search
## 
### 
ST. PAUL, Minn., Jan. 19 (UP).
—G-men who left their St. Paul
headquarters equipped with heavy
mackinaws and wading boots, failed
to return after more than eight hours
tonight and it was believed they may
have gone to the spot where Peter
Anders said he buried Charles S.
Ross, his wealthy kidnap victim.
Tlie agents left St. Paul at 8 a.m.
in two caravans, one of which was
believed to carry J. Edgar Hoover,
chief of the Federal Bureau of In
vestigation. Three of the cars were
seen to head southeast.
