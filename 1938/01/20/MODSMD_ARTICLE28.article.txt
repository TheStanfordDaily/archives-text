# Fascinated
## 
### 

FLING KWOK KEUNG, or
phaned American youth who was
adopted by a childless Chinese
merchant and reared in the Chi
nese village of Nam Hoi, arrives
in Seattle on his first trip to his
native land. Now 19 and unable
to speak English, he is fascinated
by American automobiles, cameras
and other things which he never
saw before.
