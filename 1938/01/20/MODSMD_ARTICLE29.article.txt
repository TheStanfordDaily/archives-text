# BAWL-OUTS ON SALE
## 
### 
The winter quarter bawl-outs will
go on sale sometime today in the
cashier's office, upstairs in the ad
ministration. Although the definite
time that the bawl-outs will arrive
is not known, they will be there by
the afternoon. The usual price of
15 cents and a penny tax will be
charged for the directories.
