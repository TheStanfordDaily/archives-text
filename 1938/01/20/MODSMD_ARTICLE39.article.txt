# Drillon High Man In Hockey Scoring
## 
### 
MONTREAL, Jan. 19 (UP).
don Drillon of the Toronto Maple
Leafs increased his lead in the Na
tional Hockey League scoring race
last week, statistics showed today.
With 14 goals and 18 assists for a
total of 32 scoring points Drillon
now leads his teammate, Sylvanus
Apps, and Cecil Dillon of the New
York Rangers, by six points. Dillon
is setting the pace in the American
division, five points ahead of Marty
Barry of Detroit.
