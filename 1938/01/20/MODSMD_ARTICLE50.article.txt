# MacPhail Named Dodger Executive
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 19 (UP).—The
beginning of a new deal for the
Brooklyn Dodgers was visioned to
day with the announcement of the
appointment of Larry MacPhail as
executive vice-president with com
plete authority to run the business
affairs of the club.
For the first time in nearly 13
years all the factions who control
the Dodgers have united to invest in
one man—MacPhail—complete pow
er to direct the destinies of the hap
less Brooklyn club for a long term.
