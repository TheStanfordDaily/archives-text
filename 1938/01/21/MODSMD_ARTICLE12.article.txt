# Logger Admits Kidnaping
## 
### 

PETER ANDERS, former Wash
ington and Oregon lumber camp
worker, is being held in Chi
cago for further questioning in his
reported confession of the kidnap
ing and slaying of Charles S. Ross,
Illinois manufacturer. Anders
was arrested at Santa Anita Race
Track while betting some of the
550.000 Ross ransom money on the
ponies. He also is being questioned
as a suspect in the Mattson kidnap
case in Tacoma, Wash.
