# Roble Club Plans To Give Formal
## 
### 
Plans are now being made for
Roble Club's Winter Formal, which
will be given on Feb. 12. It will be
in the form of a supper dance and
will be held in the hall's two dining
rooms.. The orchestra has not yet
been selected.
The residents of Roble will enter
tain the wives of the faculty at a
tea to be held next Wednesday.
