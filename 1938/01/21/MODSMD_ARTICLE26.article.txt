# Last Ticket Order Today For Ballet
## 
###

Ballet Rus.se to be presented in San
Francisco will be sent in today. Only
tickets paid for by 3 o'clock will be
ordered. Due to a heavy sale, orders
cannot be held for payment at the
box office.
Tickets ordered prior to January
14 have been received, and can be
obtained from Margaret Jewell at
the Women's Gymnasium.
