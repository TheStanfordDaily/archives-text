# Injured Salesman Commits Suicide
## 
### 
TRUCKEE, Cal., Jan. 20 (UP).
Badly injured in an automobile ac
cident. and apparently despairing of
obtaining aid, Clifford B. Eames, 45,
well-known Reno salesman for a
California packing firm, today com
mitted suicide in his car 200 yards
from the main highway near the
Nevada-California boundary, high
way patrol officers reported.
Authorities said Eames was found
in his wrecked car, doors of which
were locked, with a hole through
his temple where he apparently shot
himself with a .22 caliber automatic
pistol.
