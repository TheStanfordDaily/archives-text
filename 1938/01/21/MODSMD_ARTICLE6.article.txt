# Union Club Formal Set For February
## 
### 
Union Club's Winter Formal is
being planned for Saturday night,
Feb. 12, at 7 o'clock. It will be a
dinner dance with music by Hal
Barnes' orchestra. The theme is a
World Premiere.
The committee is headed by Ber
nadine McKay and includes: Ellen
Mary Mossman, patrons; Margaret
Greenwalt, programs; Katherine
Cady, entertainment; Helen Sheafe,
orchestra; Barbara Gilbert, dinner;
Barbara Cluff, publicity Kit Cart
wright, decorations; Pat Mixon,
sales.
