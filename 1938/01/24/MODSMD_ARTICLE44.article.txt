# Farm Trackmen Make All-America
## 
### 
Ray Mallot and Jack Weiershauser
of last year's Stanford track team
were named on the 1937 All-Ameri
can track team. Weiershauser, who
made a new United States record in
the 200 meter dash around one
curve was chosen in that eVent,
while Malott was chosen for the
400 meter run.
Neither Malott nor Weiershauser
was named on the All-College team
in their events, but Pete Zagar made
the Collegiate team in his event, the
discus. Lorin Benke of Washington
State College was chosen over Ma
lott in the 440 yard dash, and Ben
Johnson over Weiershauser in the
220.
