# Dance Programs Feature Ski Theme
## 
### 
Programs for Lagunita Court's
winter quarter formal Saturday
night will be in keeping with the
snow sports theme of the dance, ac
cording to Annabelle Clarke, who
is in charge of them.
The silver figure of a skier will
swoop down the front of the pro
grams against a shiny black back
ground, and mystifying ski terms
will be used inside. All bids have
been sold for the dance which will
be held from 8:30 to 12 o'clock, with
Carvel Craig's orchestra playing.
