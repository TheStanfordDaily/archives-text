# China Vice-Consul To Speak To A.S.U.
## 
### 
Patrick Sun, Chinese vice-consul
in San Francisco, will speak on
China's Fight for Life at the second
open meeting of the American Stu
dent Union on Thursday, Jan. 27.
Mr. Sun, who is 30 years old, re
ceived his education at the Tsing
Hua University in China and Ihen
was awarded his master's degree at
Stanford. He received his M.A. in
Economics and was here during the
years 1934 and 1935.
The meeting will be held at 7:30
o'clock in the basement of the Wo
men's Clubhouse and is open to all
students and faculty.
