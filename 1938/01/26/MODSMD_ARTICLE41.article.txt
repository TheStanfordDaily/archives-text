# Stein Wins Second In Diving Recount
## 
### 
A recount of points yesterday in
the varsity frosh diving competition
held last Saturday revealed that
Don Stein, burly frosh spring-board
artist, had beaten Herb Wooley of
the varsity by eight points in their
battle for second place honors.
Wooley had previously been an
nounced as having defeated Stein.
Roy Winkleman of the varsity easily
retained first place.
