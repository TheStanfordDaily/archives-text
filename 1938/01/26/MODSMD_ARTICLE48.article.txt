# 'Governor's Wife' Run Ends Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford's production of "The
Governor's Wife" ends its record
six-night run in the Little Theater
at 8 o'clock tonight. The local pre
sentation was the American pre
miere of the Spanish comedy by
Jacinto Benevente.
A large cast of Farm favorites is
featured in the play; the stars of the
production are Dan Holtom who
portrays the Governor, and Geral
dine Schreiber who plays the Gov
ernor's wife. Tickets for the final
performance are on sale in Room
202, Memorial Hall, today and out
side of the Little Theater tonight
at 40 cents. H. Miles Heberer is
directing the play, which was se
lected by Gordon C. Lange last
quarter.
