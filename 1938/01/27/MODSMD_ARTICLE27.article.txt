# "Bavarian" Dance Planned by Clubs
## 
### 
"Let's go Bavarian!"
With root beer, pretzels, and pop
corn furnishing the theme for the
first annual Inter-club Dance, eat
ing club members and their -guests
will dance to A1 Sebring's orchestra
tomorrow night in Toyon Lobby
from 9 to 1 o'clock.
Bids, at $1.50, will be on sale at
the door for general sale. The com
mittee, chairmanned by Harry Hor
ton, consists of Al Horn, Dick
Clarke, Norrie Everett, Chick Gads
by, and Herb Levy.
