# Paly Players Elect Stevens To Board
## 
### 
Dr. Thomas Wood Stevens, acting
professor of dramatics, was chosen
Tuesday to serve as an executive
board member-at-large for the Palo
Alto Community Players for the
next three months.
Although he may be absent for
part of the time, Dr. Stevens ex
pressed his willingness to serve in
that capacity, and will begin imme
diately.
