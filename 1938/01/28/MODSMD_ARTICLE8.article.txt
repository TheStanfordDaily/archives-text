# Dunne Movie Comes Here
## 
### 
"Theodora Goes Wild," Columbia
hit starring Irene Dunne and Melvin
Douglas, will be shown Sunday night
in the Memorial Theater.
The movie starts at 7:30 and tick
ets, to be purchased at. the door,
are 15 cents. There will also be a
colored cartoon, and a short golf
feature.
Showings of "The Freshman,"
"Covered Wagon," and "Love Pa
rade." formerly announced on the
schedule, have been canceled, due
to failure to obtain the films.
The movie committee, under the
chairmanship of Bill Hovey, Chi Psi,
announces that the future policy will
be to show second or third run per
formances of outstanding hits.
