# Ross To Address 'Fireside Forum'
## 
### 
Toyon Club will hold its second
"fireside forum" tomorrow evening
in Ihe lobby from 9 until 10:30
o'clock. Professor Robert T. Ross of
the psychology department will lead
the discussion, which will center on
"Interests and Attitudes."
This forum is the second of a series
which was begun last week with a
discussion led by Professor Rathbun
of the Law Department. All Toyon
Hall members who are interested
should sign up on the bulletin board.
