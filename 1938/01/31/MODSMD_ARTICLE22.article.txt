# Spanish Society Initiates Seven
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Pi, Spanish honor so
ciety, initiated seven new members
last Thursday at the Woman's Club
House. Following the ceremony, the
society celebrated with a banquet at
Bertrand's.
Those who were made members of
the club are Dorothy Adams, Ro
vena Conn, Ramon Espinosa, Mi
guel Prado, Mary Sladen, Humbert
Smith. Frnncisca Warnecke.
