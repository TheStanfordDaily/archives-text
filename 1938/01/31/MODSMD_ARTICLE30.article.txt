# Six To Be Chosen In Yell Tryouts
## 
### 
Rally Committee members and
yell leader tryoutees will meet in
the Pavilion this afternoon at 1
o'clock for the purpose of selecting
six candidates for the positions of
assistant yell leaders.
The six candidates who are chosen
from among the tryoutees will be
presented to the student body at all
home basketball games until Feb.
8, when two of them will be elected
as assistant yell leaders.
