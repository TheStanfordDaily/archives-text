# Jai-Alai Comes To The U.S.
## 
### 

PRACTICING FOB exhibition matches in Miami, Fla.. are two aces
in the fast and action-packed game of jai-alai (pronounced hi-li),
Carlos, left, and Adolfo. They discuss the merits of the hall, or pelota.
Strapped to their right wrists are the wicker cesCas, with which they
hurl or catch the ball in play against a wall.
