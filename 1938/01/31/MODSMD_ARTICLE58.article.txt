# Rev. Eliot Speaks At Vesper Service
## 
### 
Rev. Frederick M. Eliot spoke at
a vesper service in Memorial Chapel
yesterday afternoon. The invocation
was given by Rev. Herbert T. Pater
man.
The combined men's and women's
glee clubs sang the chorale, "Lo,
How a Rose 'Ere Blooming," Prai
torius, and with the University Choir
sang the Russian anthem, "Hospodi
Pomilui," by Lvovsky.
