# Ski Theme Dance Given By Lagunita
## 
### 
Skis, ice-skates and posters from
winter resorts proclaimed the win
ter sports theme of the Lagunita
Formal held at the Court Saturday
night. Approximately 200 couples
attended the dance.
Music was provided by Carvel
Craig and his orchestra. Supper was
served in the various casa social
rooms at midnight.
