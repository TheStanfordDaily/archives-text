# Farm Will Send Delegates To Meet
## 
### 
Stanford will send three delegates
to the Western Women's Intercol
legiate Conference at Pullman,
Wash., April 13, 14, and 15, accord
ing to Cynthia Coonley, president of
Women's Conference.
It was decided in Conference
meeting to send one senior and two
juniors to the meeting, delegates to
be chosen next week from a list sub
mitted by members of Conference.
Plans are also being considered
for the entertainment of student
leaders from Cal and Mills. The
women will probably be invited for
spring quarter and a program will
be planned to acquaint them with
Stanford.
