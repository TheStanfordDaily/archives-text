# Script, Songs Sought For Show
## 
### 
Early announcements for the
Spring Show already are being made
by Dramatic Manager Clay McDan
iel, who asks that all interested in
the show get in touch with him soon
at the dramatic office. The story
for the Spring Show has not yet
been selected and Stanford play
wrights will have until March 1 to
get their scripts ready. McDaniel
asks that all scripts be submitted
completely, and not in synopsis form.
Songwriters also are urged to get
in touch with him in order to re
ceive instructions as to the type of
songs needed. Casting will take
place near the end of Winter
Quarter.
