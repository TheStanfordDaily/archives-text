# VINES TAKES PERRY
## 
### 
SAN JOSE. Cal., Feb. 1 (UP). —
Ellsworth Vines, of Pasadena, de
! feated Fred Perry, of Great Britain,
6-3, 6-4 in a professional tennis
match before 2000 people here last
I night. It was the sixth victory for
Vines in the 11 matches the pair
have played this year. They meet
again tonight in Stockton.
