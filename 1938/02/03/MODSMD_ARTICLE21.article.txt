# Women Take Part In Gym Tourneys
## 
### 
Two women's intramural sports'
tournaments, badminton and ping
pong, are now in progress at the
Women's Gymnasium. Drawings
were held last week and play begun
immediately. Second round matches
are scheduled for completion by
Feb. 11.
Both singles and doubles are be
ing played in badminton with wins
in (wo out of three games necessary.
Those participating should watch
the charts in the gymnasium and
get in touch with their opponents
concerning matches to be played.
Marie Manchee, women's physical
education instructor, is in charge of
the tournaments.
