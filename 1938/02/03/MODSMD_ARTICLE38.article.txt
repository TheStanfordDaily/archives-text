# Barnes Will Play At Union Dance
## 
### 
Hal Barnes and his orchestra will
play for the Union Club dinner
dance to be held Saturday night in
the Union. Dinner will be at 7
o clock and dancing will continue
until midnight.
Professor and Mrs. H. J. Rathbun
and Mr. and Mrs. A. K. Beggs will
act as sponsors, according to Jay
rhorp and Bernie McKay, co-chair
men of the affair.
The dance committee consists of
Kit Cartwright, Helen Sheafe, Kath
erine Cady, Barbara Gilbert, Ellen
Mary Mossman, Pat Mixson, and
Barbara duff.
