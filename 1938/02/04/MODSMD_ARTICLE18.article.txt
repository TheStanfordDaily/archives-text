# Toyon Lobby Floor Sanded For Formal
## 
### 
The Toyon Formal, being held on
February 18, will have the newest
dance floor on the campus, accord
ing to Ward Keasbey, chairman. The
Toyon lobby floor is being sanded,
varnished, and waxed in preparation
for the dance.
Plans are under way for the
formal, with selection of orchestra
under consideration. The committee,
headed by Keasbey, consists of Her
shey Julien, Jim Rarnum, Henry
Hughes, John Esterilne, Oliver York,
Jack Ellis, and Mike Gudman.
