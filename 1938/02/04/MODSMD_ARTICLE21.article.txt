# Negro Contralto To Sing In Series
## 
### 
Marion Anderson, talented Negro
contralto, will appear in concert
Thursday, Feb. 24. in Memorial Hall,
according to Virginia Smith, concert
series manager.
Miss Anderson will be the third
artist to appear on the 1937-38 A.S.
S.U. sponsored concert series pro
gram. She is especially noted for
interpretations of songs other than
Negro spirituals.
Tickets may be obtained at the
concert series office in' the Old Un
ion for $2.50. A special section over
the orchestra pit and standing room
will sell for $2.
