# TRUEBLOOD TO PREACH
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, University
chaplain, will deliver the sermon at
the Memorial Church Sunday morn
ing at 11 o'clock. Music by the
choir and Acting University Organ
ist Raymond Kendall will include
Bach's "My God, I Give Myself to
Thee," and Gounod's "My Babylon's
Wave."
