# Netmen To Enter Indoor Tourney
## 
### 
Several Card tennis players, in
cluding Captain Bob Underwood,
are expected to go into action to
morrow in first-round matches in
the annual Northern California In
door Tournament to be played on
the courts in the Palace of Fine
Arts Building in San Francisco.
Underwood teamed with Bob
Pommer last year to win the men's
doubles. The pair will defend their
title this year. Other members of
the varsity squad who have sent in
their entries are Lloyd Gates, Sher
wood Armstrong, Jim Seaver, Dave
Brock, and Myron Folsom.
Bob Low. Ken Roberts, Neil Bal
lard, Jim Lundy, and Sid Terry of
the frosh squad have also entered.
