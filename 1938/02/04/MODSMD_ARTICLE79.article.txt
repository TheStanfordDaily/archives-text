# Organist To Give Recital Sunday
## 
### 
Raymond Kendall, assistant uni
versity organist, will repeat last
Wednesday's recital Sunday after
noon at 4 o'clock in the Memorial
Chapel.
Mr. Kendall's program will include
the Adagio and finale (from Con
certo in B Flat for Organ and Or
chestra) by G. F. Handel, Cantablc
by Cesar Franck, Three Sketches
by E. S. Barnes. He will conclude
the recital with Tocata and Fugue
in D Minor by J. S. Bach.
