# SUN EGRET FAVORED
## 
### 
ARCADIA. Feb. 6 (UP). — Sun
Egret, an obscure colt picked up at, a
claiming price lest than six months
ago, moved to the' lop of the list to
night as horsemen revamped the
positions of candidates for Santa
Anita's $50,000 derby.
