# Kendall Presents Recital At Church
## 
### 
Raymond Kendall, in his organ
recital yesterday afternoon in the
Memorial Church, presented to a
widely assorted audience of both on
and off campus people a variety of
organ music.
Included on Mr. Kendall's pro
gram was the Adagio and Finale
(from Concerto in B Flat for Organ
and Orchestra) by G. F. Handel,
Cantahile by Cesar Franck, Three
Sketches by E. S. Barnes. The re
cital concluded with Toccata and
Fugue in D Minor by J. S. Bach. A
new program will be presented by
Mr. Kendall Thursday and Sunday.
