# Card Fencers Bow To Olympic Club
## 
### 
Stanford fencers lost to the Olym
pic Club in the finals of the bay re
gion contest of the Amateur Fenc
ing League of America Friday night
in San Francisco. Edwin Woods,
Herbert Woolley, and John De Bon
edetti, Stanford's entrants in the
meet, defeated the Italian Club by
a score of 5 to 1, a strong Cal team,
five to one, and lost to the Olympic,
five to two.
Elwyn Bugge, Stanford fencing
coach, was pleased with his team's
showing.
