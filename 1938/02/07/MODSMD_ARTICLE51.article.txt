# Hallowed by Tradition
## 
### 
Carrying on a tradition hallowed by the
ages, The Daily staff will meet in the Shack

today at 1:15. Featured speakers will be the
editor, managing editor. Morale and meringue
will be served. Edit board meets at 1 o'clock
to start the brawl rolling.
