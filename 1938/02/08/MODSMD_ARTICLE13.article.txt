# TO ELECT OFFICERS
## 
### 
Election of Klahoya Club offi
cers will take place at noon today
in the Women's Clubhouse. Offices
to be filled for the coming year in
clude those of president, vice-presi
dent, and secretary-treasurer. In
addition, sports, social, and publicity
chairmen will be appointed. All off
campus women are urged to vote
following the weekly luncheon.
