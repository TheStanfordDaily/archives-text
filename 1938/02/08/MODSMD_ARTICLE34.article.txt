# NEW AMBASSADOR STOPS OFF AT HARVARD
## 
### 

PREPARING TO leave for his
new post as Ambassador to Great
Britain, Jo; cph P. Kennedy, cen
ter, took time off to visit his two
sons at Harvard University. At
left is Joseph Jr., Harvard senior,
and John F., sophomore, at right.
The boys are two of the nine
Kennedy children. Mr. Kennedy
succeeds the late Robert W. Bing
ham at the Court of St. James.
