# N.Y.A. Office Calls For Release Cards
## 
### 
The Student Employment Office
urges students who have not signed
NYA release cards due January 23,
24, and 25 to do so immediately.
Tutoring positions are available
for students qualified to teach phys
ics or ancient and American history.
There also is employment available
for one man and one woman to do
clerical work in a Palo Alto drug
store, and an experienced part-time
bookkeeper.
