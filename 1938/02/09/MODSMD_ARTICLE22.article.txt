# Editor Sets Today As Proof Deadline
## 
### 
Unless Quad proofs are called for
today, they will be arbitrarily se
lected by the Quad editors, George
McKenna announced.
Proofs are in Room 15 of the A.S,
S.U. office, which will be open from
9 to 5 o'clock. Publication schedules
make it imperative that the proofs
be selected at once. McKenna added.
