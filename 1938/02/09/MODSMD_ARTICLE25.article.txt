# Tryoutee Period Draws To Close
## 
### 
Tryoutees will meet for the next
to the last time this afternoon in
Room 8 of the Press Building.
Practice in proof and copy reading
will be continued, and Editor Lloyd
Lapham will discuss paper policy.
Tryoutees are expected to work at
The Daily Shack at least four hours
before the end of the period and put
in two evenings on the United Press
desk writing heads.
The last meeting will be held to
morrow afternoon at 5 o'clock in the
Press Building, and appointments
will be released Friday morning in
The Daily.
