# PRIZE OFFERED FOR PLAY
## 
### 
Prizes of $200, $100 and $50 are
being offered by the Religious
Drama Council in New York City
for the best one-act play on the
subject of Peace which would be
suitable for production in churches.
Playing time should not exceed one
hour. Plays must be submitted to
the Religious Drama Council, 71
[ West 23rd Street, New York City,
' by July 1, 1938.
