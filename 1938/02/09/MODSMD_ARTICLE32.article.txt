# Dr. Harlow Leaves For Press Survey
## 
### 
Dr. Rex F. Harlow, assistant pro
fessor of education, left yesterday
for New York and 13 eastern cities
to carry on an investigation of re
lations between colleges and uni
versities and the press by interview
ing editors, publishers, and report
ers. Dr. Harlow himself is a news
paperman and publisher of 20 years'
experience.
Purpose of the investigation is to
determine "the relationships which
should exist between the news
papers, the schools, and the public."
Forty newspapers and 2B colleges
and universities on the Pacific Coast
have co-operated in the fact-survey.
