# Skiers Offered 50 Yosemite Bookings
## 
### 
Fifty reservations at Yosemite for
this week-end are offered Ski Club
members at reduced rates, according
to announcements made by Bill
Janss, club vice president.
Since over 100 skiers visited A.S.
S.U. offices last week-end to take
advantage of the cut in prices, Janss
urged all those interested to reg
ister before tomorrow noon.
