# Mayor Hague Wins Out Again
## 
### 

NEW JERSEY legislators went to Jersey City to examine the poll
books of Hudson County, stronghold of Mayor Frank Hague, "not the
first of the dictators, but the last of the bosses." Upon their arrival
Patrolman Andrew Bookstaver. left, and Lieutenant Albert Antonacci,
right, declared they had orders to protect the vault containing the
documents. Despite the protest of the legislators, the vault seal re
mained unbroken.
