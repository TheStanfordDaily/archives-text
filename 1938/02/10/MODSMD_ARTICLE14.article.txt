# Ushers To Tryout For 'Blue Jeans'
## 
### 
All those interested in ushering for
the production "Blue Jeans" are to
apply between 4 and 6 o'clock today
at the dramatics office in the A.S.
S.U., according to Clay McDaniel,
dramatics manager.
Those with previous experience
are especially requested to apply for
the position of head usher which is
a salaried job, McDaniel added.
