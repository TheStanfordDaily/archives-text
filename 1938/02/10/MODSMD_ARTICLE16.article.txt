# Students Speak To Walrus Club
## 
### 
Four students spoke on their ex
periences at the Western Summer
School for Workers at the meeting
of the Walrus Club in the Women's
Clubhouse last night.
Didi Vibert, Eleanor Eddy, Betty
Watkins, and Bob Elkus gave short
speeches about the advantages and
reasons for this month-long sum
mer school at Berkeley. They ex
plained how 60 industrial laborers,
sent on scholarships given by labor
groups and universities, and a small
er number of college students attend
this school every year. Winninette
Arnold, president of the club, led a
round table discussion following
the speeches.
