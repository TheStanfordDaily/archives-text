# Andriano To Talk At Dessert Party
## 
### 
First of the series of Pi Lambda
Theta dessert parties will be held
tonight at 7:30 in the Lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Edna Andriano, from Los Altos,
will be the speaker and will discuss
her recent experiences in Italy as a
teacher and with Madame Montes
sori.
Pi Lambda Theta is the women's
national education society and is
sponsoring the series to give all
women in the education department
a chance to meet one another so
cially.
