# WOMEN IN SEMI-FINALS
## 
### 
PALM BEACH. Fla., Feb. 9 (UP).
—Favorites led by Marion Miley of
Kentucky entered the semi-finals of
the Palm Beach Women's Golf
Tournament today. Miss Miley won
I up from Dorothy Traung of San
Francisco.
