# Inter-Club Council Selects Officers
## 
### 
Inter-club council last night chose
its officers for the second half of
the year. For president, A1 Horn,
(El Capitan) was picked. Dick
Clarke (El Campo) was selected as
first vice president, Harvey Roths
child (Toyon Independent) second
vice president, and Frank Van Acker
(El Toro) secretary.
The outgoing officers of the coun
cil, who have served since the end
of last Spring Quarter, are: Presi
dent, Harry Cragin (El Campo);
second vice president, Harry Hor
ton (El Toro); and secretary, A1
Horn.
