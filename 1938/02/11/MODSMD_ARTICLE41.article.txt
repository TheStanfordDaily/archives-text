# SICK MAN WINS LOTTERY
## 
### 
VANCOUVER, B. C., Feb. 10
(UP). —An Australian lottery today
bestowed a fortune of $100,500 on an
unemployed Vancouver man who is
seriously ill, cable reports said. Lot
tery representatives refused to di
vulge the man's name for fear of
prosecution for selling tickets.
