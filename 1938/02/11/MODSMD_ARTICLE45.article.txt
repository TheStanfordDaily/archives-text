# THE 						STANFORD DAILY Sports
## WALT PETERSON, Sports Editor BASEBALL MOGULS MEET AT BANQUET 
### 

A GROUP OF the big bosses of baseball meet at sports writers' dinner in Philadelphia. Left to right:
Managers Joe McCarthy, New York Yankees; Joe Cronin, Boston Red Sox; Bucky Harris, Washington
Senators; Jimmy Dykes, Chicago White Sox, and Clark Giffith, owner of the Washington Senators. More
than 1000 notables were present at the banquet.
