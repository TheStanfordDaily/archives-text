# Lagunita Will Hold Election Today
## 
### 
Lagunita elections, to choose a
new president, first and second vice
presidents, secretary, and Chairman
of the Committee of Seven, will be
held today with voting before and
after lunch and dinner.
Running for president are Bar
bara Funderburgh, Lois Persons,
Marge Segerstrom, Roberta Tuthill,
and Charlou Watkins. For the of
fice of secretary, for which only
sophomores are eligible, Jean Haber,
Ruth Harris, Nancy Hurst, Barbara
Reamer, Barbara Templeton, and
Virginia Valentine have been nomi
nated.
