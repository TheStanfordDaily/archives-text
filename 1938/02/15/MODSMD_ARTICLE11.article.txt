# Fellowships Given By Government
## 
### 
Three research fellowships of $600
are offered by the' Bureau of Mines
of the United States Department of
the Interior, in cooperation with the
University of Maryland, in the field
of engineering and applied science,
according to a report from the Reg
istrar's office.
One fellowship will be assigned
foi the research project in the Metal
lurgical Division of the Bureau of
Mines, and a second will be assigned
to the Nonmetals Division. A third
is applicable to the Mining Division.
Fellows enter upon their duties
July 1 and continue for 12 months,
including one month for vacation.
Applications must be received be
fore April 1. Preference will be
given to men who have already had
one year of graduate work and have
hiid experience in research work.
