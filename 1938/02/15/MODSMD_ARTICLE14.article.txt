# Ticket Sale On For Style Show
## 
### 
Tickets for the scholarship spring
fashion show, under the joint spon
sorship of Women's Conference and
the Chaparral, will be sold this
week in the various women's living
groups by women who are to model
in the shjw. scheduled for March 1.
The sale will end the Friday before
the show.
Freddie Nagel's orchestra has been
engaged to play for the style pre
sentation, and leading San Francisco
and Palo Alto department stores
will show their latest fashions for
spring, including a full wedding
party outfit. Models were chosen
from a large group of Stanford
women by Miss Patricia Morgan,
head of the Morgan School for
Models in San Francisco.
Proceeds from the ticket sale are
to go towards a women's scholar
ship.
