# Library Exhibits Sandburg's Works
## 
### 
The Felton Memorial Library is
showing a collection of Carl Sand
burg's works because of the inter
est aroused by Mr. Sandburg's lec
ture Thursday evening.
There are two first editions in the
collection, "Chicago Poems" and
"The People, Yes." The latter vol
ume has a personal dedication to
Stanford University by the author.
In addition, there is a bibliography
of Mr. Sandburg's work and several
articles about him.
