# Peace Group Meet Scheduled Today
## 
### 
Members of the A.S.S.U. Peace
Day Committee and advisory dele
gates from Stanford living groups
will meet at 4 o'clock today in the
conference room on the second floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Final discussion of speakers for
the Peace Day assembly on April 22
will be held this.afternoon, Dan Mc-
Comish, temporary chairman of the
committee, announced. Today's
meeting will be the last until next
quarter, he said.
