# Conference Elects Delegates Today
## 
### 
Final voting will be held today on
J the nominees for delegates to the
Western Women's Conference to be
held at Pullman, Wash., April 13,
14, and 15.
Of the list submitted one senior
and two juniors will be sent to the
convention by Women's Conference.
Nominees are: Seniors, Cynthia
Coonley, Barbara Murphy, and Jane
Tanner; juniors, Maxine Dailey,
Jean Reynolds, Anabel Simpson,
Betty Davis, and Carolyn Hibbard.
