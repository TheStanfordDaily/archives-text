# Cardinals To Plan For Drive Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford's Cardinals will meet to
night at 7:15 o'clock to discuss Con
valescent Home Drive appointments
and what to do with 300 Stevens
College women when they get here.
The meeting will be held in Room 13
of the Old Union.
The drive, to be held this spring
under the direction of Cardinals,
gathers funds to be given to the Con
valescent Home. Stevens College
women will be entertained while
visiting on the campus.
