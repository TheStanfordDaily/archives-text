# PACE WANTS YACK
## 
### 
TORONTO, Out., Feb. 15 (UP).—
Georgia Pace, Cleveland Negro, to
day demanded a match with the
Canadian bantamweight champion,
Norman "Baby" Yack, after his one
sided 10-rcund win over Katzumi
Morioka, Vancouver, Canadian-born
Japanese, here last night.
