# Women Musicians To Take Field Trip
## 
### 
Members of the percussion class,
women's music group, will go to San
Francisco for a short field trip on
Saturday. Included in the trip will
be a tour of the shops in Chinatown
where percussion and wind instru
ments may be purchased.
The group will also attend the
concert of Uday Shan Kar, Hindu
dancer, and his troupe at the Opera
House. After the concert, they will
be admitted backst;ige to meet the
artist and see his fine collection of
percussion instruments.
