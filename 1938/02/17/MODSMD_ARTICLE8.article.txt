# Kendall To Offer Recital Today
## 
### 
Raymond Kendall, assistant Uni
versity organist, will present his
weekly organ recital in the Memo
rial Chapel at 4:15 o'clock. The pro
gram will include: "Diferencias
sobre el Canto del Caballero," by
Anto?ico de Cabezon; "Prelude and
Fugue in E Minor," by J. S. Bach;
"Jagged Peaks in the Moonlight"
(from "Three Mountain Sketches"),
by Joseph W. Clokey.
Adagio (Symphony VI), by
Charley M. Widor; and Chorale in
A Miiu>r by Cesar Franck. The
same program will be repeated on
Sunday, February 20. at I o'clock.
