# Roble, Encina Plan Exchange Dinner
## 
### 
Walter Stoessel, president, of En
cina Club, announced yesterday that
(arrangements were being made to
hold an exchange dinner with
Roble Wednesday, March 2. Added
activities will be a joHy-up next
Friday after the U.C.L.A. basketball
game and possibly a smoker in En
cina March 2.
Commenting on other freshman
activities, Stoessel intimated that the
frosh class' annual formal dance this
year would be bigger than ever.

plans to hold the dance jointly and
