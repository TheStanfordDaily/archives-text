# Fencers To Enter S. F. Tournament
## 
### 
Stanford fencers enter their sec
ond match of the season tonight at
8 o'clock in the San Francisco
Y.M.C.A. when they enter into in
dividual competition in the Junior
Foils tournament.
Card foilsmen entered are Merton
Davies, Herbert Woolley, Edwin
Woods, and Dick Meyerhoff.
