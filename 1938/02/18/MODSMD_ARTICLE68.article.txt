# CAMPUS OPINION
## A 						DOG'S TALE 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
i am very beautiful and i am so
sorry i am not liked, yesterday i
read in campus opinion about a dog
named buster, he i.s a vei y bad dog
and very ugly, one day he jumped
on me and tore my beautiful fur
coat, the story about, him men
tioned my kind master torn fleming.
it said he wrote the article about me
which made editor of the chappie
very angry because he wrote the
article, furthermore it is only na
tural that my picture be big because
1 am so beautiful, sometimes 1 bite
AKL's, but then dog bait shouldn't
walk by in front of my house, i
hope nobody e'se will annoy me be
cause i am so tired and i want to go
to sleep in the sun and also what
difference does it make?—siggic.
