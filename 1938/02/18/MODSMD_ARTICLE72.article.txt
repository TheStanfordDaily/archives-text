# LOUIS TO DEFEND TITLE
##

NEW YORK. Feb. 17 (UP).—The
winner of the Jimmy Adamick-Harry
Thomas fight here tomorrow night
will get a shot at Joe Louis' world
heavyweight title in April, Pro
moter Mike Jacobs promised today.
