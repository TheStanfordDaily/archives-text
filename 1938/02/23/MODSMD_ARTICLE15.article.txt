# Hays, Cuddy Win In Frosh Debate
## 
### 
Dan Hays and David Cuddy tri
umphed over Frank Mallory and
Richard Myeroff last Monday in
the second debate of the freshman
intrasquad tournament. The win
ning team upheld the affirmative of
the question, "Resolved, that the
National Labor Relations Board
should be empowered to enforce
arbitration of all labor disputes."
The tournament, which is being
conducted in round-robin style, will
last for several and winners
will represent Stanford freshmen in
the Pacific Coast Freshman and
Junior College Debate Tournament
to be held at Stockton in April.
