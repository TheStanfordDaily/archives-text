# Pershing's Health Reported Failing
## 
### 
j TUCSON. Ariz., Feb. 22 (UP). -
I Gen. John J. Pershing was in "very
serious" condition tonight, follow
ing a relapse of a rheumatic ailment,
his physician, Dr. Roland Davison,
reported.
Dr. Davison said a "complication,"
the nature of which he did not dis
close, had caused the relapse late
last night. He denied reports the
general was suffering a heart ailment
instead of rheumatism.
