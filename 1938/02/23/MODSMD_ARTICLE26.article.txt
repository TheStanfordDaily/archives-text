# Three New Men Added To Faculty
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur an
nounced yesterday the appointment
of Dr. B. Paul Davies, Herbert
Goldhamer, and Harry N. M. Win
ton to the faculty.
Dr. Davies, formerly connected
with the Students' Health Service of
the University of Minnesota, was
appointed instructor and examining
physician in the School of Hygiene
arid Physical Education, to take the
place of Dr. Harold Cramer, who has
resigned.
Mr. Goldhamer, of the University
of Chicago, was appointed instructor
in sociology for the next academic
year, Mr. Winton was appointed in
structor in history for the spring
quarter.
