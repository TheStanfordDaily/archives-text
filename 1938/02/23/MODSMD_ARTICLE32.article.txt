# PARIS EXPRESS CRASHES
## 
### 
PARIS, Feb. 22 (UP).—A Paris-
Berlin express plane crashed on an
estate outside Paris today, killing
: its three occupants. The pilot, fly
ing blind through a fog over the
Oise Valley, was seeking Le Bourgct
Airdrome.
