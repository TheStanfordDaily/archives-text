# Farm Boxers Set For Cal Matches
## 
### 
Though receiving the short end of
! a close 4-3 score from the fists of
the Uclan boxers last week, Coach
Clayton Frye's boys returned to the
Farm practically unscathed and
started intensive training for the
Cal bout tomorrow night at Cal.
A final bout with Cal at Stanford
is scheduled for a week from to
morrow and will finish the 1938
varsity boxing season, as far as in
tercollegiate matches are concerned.
