# FROSH DEBATE U.S.F.
## 
### 
Bob Newell and Milton Marks Jr.,
representing the Stanford freshman
debate squad, met Jack Keegan
and Bill O'Brien, freshmen from
the University of San Francisco,
Monday night in the basement of
the Women's Clubhouse in a non
decision contest. Marks and Newell
upheld the affirmative of the ques
tion, "Resolved, that the National
Ijabor Relations Board should be
empowered to enforce arbitration of
all labor disputes."
