Alpha Tau Omega 53, El Cuadro 12.

* * *
With all 13 league winners
crowned, first play-off games for
the 1938 all-University basketball
championship get under way tonight
in Encina Gym and the Pavilion.
Phi Delta Phi defeated nEcina
Bull Pen, 25-20, to win the pennant
in League 8 last night, and will face
Delta Chi tonight. Zeta Psi II nosed
out El Toro I, 27-24, and entered the
play-off bracket representing Loop
6. The Zetes tangle with Phi Psi
this evening. Another gonfalon win
ner was crowned last night when
A.T.O. slaughtered El Cuadro by a
53-12 count to win the championship
in League 11.
Other winners are: Loop 1, Delta
Upsilon; 2, Psi Psi; 3, Beta I; 4,
Kappa Sig I: 5, Breakers I; 7, S.A.E.;
9, Sigma Chi; 10, Sequoia I; 12, Delta
Chi; 13, Fiji.
