# Patty Berg Top Favorite In Golf
## 
### 
ORMOND BEACH. Fla„ Feb. 23
(UP). —Patty Berg of Minneapolis,
top favorite by virtue of her victory
in the medal round, tees off today in
pursuit of her fourth Florida winter
title in the women's South Atlantic
golf championships.
Pwtty, who has won three Florida
tournaments this year, scored a 74,
three under women's par, in yester
day's qualifying.
