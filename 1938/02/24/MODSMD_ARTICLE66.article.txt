# Axe Society Names Calderwood Head
## 
### 
Jack Calderwood, El Toro, last
night was elected president of Axe
Society, succeeding Bud Milligan,
for the remainder of the school year.
Chosen vice-president was Curt
Eaton, Kappa Sigma, to succeed
Andy Sojkovich, El Toro. Stojkovieh
was named new secretary-treasurer,
replacing Bill Balkan, Chi Psi. The
society met at Delta Tau Delta.
