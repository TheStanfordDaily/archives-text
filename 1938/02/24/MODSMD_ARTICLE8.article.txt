# Glee Clubs Plan Joint Programs
## 
### 
Stanford's Men's and Women's
Glee Clubs will combine for two
programs to be given on March 7
and 9, according to an announce
ment made yesterday by Oliver
Cope, manager of the men's group.
First combination engagement
will be on the Stanford Radio Hour
over KGO and the coast Blue net
work of the National Broadcasting
Company on Monday, March 7, at
8 o'clock. Both groups will sing in
dividual numbers as well as dual
ones.
The groups are planning a joint
banquet to be held in San Fran
cisco before the broadcast, Cope
said. They will combine again on
March 9 to take part in the Found
ers' Day program in the Memorial
Church.
