# Flowers Will Be Used for Contrast
## 
### 
As the first spring buds appear,
blossom forth with flowers for
trimming, and see if you don't feel
like a new person. No longer will
you have that tired feeling about
clothes like you had with dark,
heavy winter clothes.
Tie roses on a riband around your
wrist for evening, pin pink flowers
on the lapel of your dark suit, to
brighten it, add a cluster of (low
ers to your hat. Flowers offer a
beautilu 1 contrast to the many col
ors being shown this year.
