# Workers Needed For Home Drive
## 
### 
All students interested in working
on Convalescent Drive committees
are asked by Bud Milligan, general
chairman of the drive, to sign up in
his office, Room 13 in the Old Union,
between 1 and 3 o'clock today.
From 150 to 200 persons are need
ed to help in the annual campus
project to raise funds for the sup
port of the Convalescent Home.
Students can apply to help any
of several activities. There will be
committees for the off-campus drive,
for Labor Day, Bean Day and the
Masque Ball. A women's committee
will be in charge of concesisons,
freshman co-eds may apply to sell
tags on Tag Day.
