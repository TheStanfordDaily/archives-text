# Foreign Students To Discuss Peace
## 
### 
Time for the foreign students'
peace discussion at Lagunita next
Sunday has been shifted to 3 o'clock
according to the announcement of
the International Committee yester
day.
Following the round-table dis
cussion, which will be lead by Dr.
Emily Hickman of Rutgers, tea will
be served at 4 o'clock. All foreign
students are invited and are asked
to call Helen Gilman at Lagunita if
they plan to come.
Mosses were the first plants to
grow successfully on land.
