# Spring Socks Ignore Quad Tradition
## 
### 
Now that spring is here, bobby
socks will be worn on the Quad
in spite of tradition.
To match or contrast with the new
spring colors in dresses and suits
bobby socks are coming out in white,
natural, mauve, navy blue, cherry,
light blue, dusty pink, turquoise,
Burgundy, chocolate brown, and
dark green.
The latest socks are made of An
gora mesh, and are made for warm
weather. The tops of the socks had
a band of elastic to give the ankle
a trim and fitted look.
