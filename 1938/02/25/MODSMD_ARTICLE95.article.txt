# HAIR ORNAMENTS
## 
### 
Spring hair ornaments consist
mainly of curls rolled upward wilh
a sort of wave-about-to-brea-look;
clips and flowers have became
rather passe. If you have a knack
you can twist scarfs and bandanas
turban-fashion to go with youi
spring suit. A new Babushka or sort
of bonnet is vying for collegiate
popularity with the peasant-tied
bandana.
