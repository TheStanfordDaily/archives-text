# Shubert Offered On Program Today
## 
### 
Selections to be played today,
during the Monday record hour at
4:15 o'clock, are Schubert's C-Major
Symphony, Number 7, and Brahms'
First Symphony, according to Ray
mond Kendall, instructor in music.
These weekly record hours are
open to anyone, but it is only on the
first Monday of each month that new
records are reviewed. The other
three are spent in studying selec
tions related to the course on The
Modern Symphony Orchestra, which
sponsors these hours.
