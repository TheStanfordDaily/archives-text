# 'Vikings' Cast Rehearses Today
## 
### 
With casting completed for Ibsen's
"Vikings," Stanford's Division of
Speech and Drama rehearses this
week for production March 4 and 5
in Memorial Hall, Director Thomas
Wood Stevens announced yesterday.
Frances Farmer plays opposite
Gary Simpson as Hiordis, the fiery
heroine of Ibsen's Norse saga. Deal
ing with the wars, loves, and hates
of legendary heroes, the play is from
the earlier works of the Norwegian
playwright.
"The Vikings" is magnificent the
ater." Director Stevens said, "be
cause its theme is so elemental and
strongly set forth."
Seats may be reserved at $1, 75
cents, and 50 cents in Room 202,
Memorial Hall.
