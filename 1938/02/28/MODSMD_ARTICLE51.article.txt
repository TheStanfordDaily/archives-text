# AMBERS TO BOX IN L. A.
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 27 (UP).—
Lou Ambers, world lightweight box
ing champion, has been signed for
two overweight matches against un
named opponents here, Suey Welch,
boxing supervisor of Olympic Audi
torium. said tonight. The bouts will
be held in April and May.
