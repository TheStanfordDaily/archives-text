# New York Plays To Be Criticized
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, women's honorary
organization, will present Beatrice
Edmonds, reviewer of the current
Broadway stage and dramatic reader,
in the Memorial Hall on April 6,
according to plans announced last
night by Jean Hemphill, president.
Miss Edmonds, a Stanford grad
uate, will discuss the plays which
are being presented in New York
this winter, giving especial atten
tion to the one which she considers
most outstanding.
Tickets may be obtained from any
member of Cap and Gown. Prices
are 50 cents for students with stu
dent body cards, and $1 for the gen
eral public.
