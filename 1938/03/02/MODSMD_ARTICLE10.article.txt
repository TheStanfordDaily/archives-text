# Trueblood To Talk At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
"The Spiritual Program of a Uni
versity" will be the subject of an
address by Dr. D. Elton Trueblood,
University chaplain, at the regular
Luncheon Club meeting at noon to
day.
Dr. Trueblood will first present
his ideas on the ideal spiritual pro
gram of any university and conclude
with an analysis of Stanford's pres
ent program. This address and his
talk before the Walrus Club tonight
will be the chaplain's last two pub
lic appearances before he leaves for
the East. The meeting will begin at
noon in the Women's Clubhouse.
