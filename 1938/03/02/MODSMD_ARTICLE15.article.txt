# Director Needed For Spring Show
## 
### 
Position as musical director for the
Spring Show to be given by Stagers,
May 20-21, is now open, according
to Clay McDaniei, dramatic mana
ger.
Only the applications of those who
have had previous experience will
be considered. Duties of the musi
cal director include rehearsing the
orchestra and conducting it for the
two performances.
All songwriters are also urged by
McDaniei to get in touch with the
dramatics office in order to be re
ferred to those persons working on
scripts. The scripts for the, Spring
Show must be submitted in dupli
cate by the end of the quarter.
