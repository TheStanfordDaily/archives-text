# Employment Head Will Speak Today
## 
### 
Mr. Maurice McCaffrey will speak
today at 3:15 o'clock in Room 228
on the subject, '"An Interstates
Benefit Plan." Mr. McCaffrey is
rules and regulations chairman of
the California Department of Em
ployment.
His plan permits unemployment
insurance benefits of one state to be
paid to those who have earned it in
another state. He has worked out
a system based on his plan which is
now in operation in California.
Oregon, and Washington, and which
will soon be in effect throughout
the country. The public is invited to
attend Mr. McCaffrey's discusrien.
