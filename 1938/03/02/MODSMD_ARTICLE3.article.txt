# Rogers To Speak To Faculty Club
## 
### 
"A Petrographic Study of a Vol
canic Dome" will be the subject of
a talk by Dr. Austin F. Rogers, pro
fessor of minerology, at the meeting
of the Faculty Research Club, to
night at 8 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Dr. Rogers will base his talk on
rock sample of obsidian taken from
the Salton Sea in the vicinity of Im
perial Valley. He will also discuss
the volcanic formation of Mullett
Island in Salton Sea and the car
bon dioxide well that is being used
for the manufacture of dry ice.
On the same program Elmer D.
Fagan, professor of Economics, will
discuss "The Proposed California
Tax Revision to Eliminate the Sales
Tax."
