# Education Group Will Hold Party
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, women's na
tional honorary education society
will hold the last of its dessert par
ties tonight at 7:30 in the lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse, according
to Pat Hamilton, president.
Any senior or graduate woman
who did not receive an invitation or
who received one to another, but
was unable to attend, is welcome.
