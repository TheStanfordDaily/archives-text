# Labor Conference To Be Held Here
## 
### 
A Labor Conference will be held
Sunday afternoon at 2:30 o'clock in
he Women's Clubhouse under the
sponsorship of the four delegates
who attended the Western Work
ers' School last year.
The general topic of discussion
will be "What Really Happens in the
Labor Movement" with Dr. George
Hedley, professor at the University
of California and director of the
Western Workers' School, as chair
man.
Other guest speakers and their
topics will be Jenny Maytas, "In
vestigation"; Arthur Rosenbaum and
Professor Kidner, "Compulsory and
Voluntary Arbitration"; and Mar
garet Werth, "Hotel Arbitration."
