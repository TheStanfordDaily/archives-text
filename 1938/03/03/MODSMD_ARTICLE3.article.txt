# Goldblatt To Talk To A.S.U. Tonight
## 
### 
Northern California director of the
CIO, Louis Goldblatt, will talk to the
American Student Union tonight on
"Bargaining Rights for Longshore
men."
Mr. Goldblatt is a graduate of the
University of California and has
been connected with Harry Bridges
in the Warehousemen's and Team
sters' Unions.
He has been an organizer for the
I.L.W.U. and was speaker at the
Armistice Day celebration in Berke
ley last fall.
The meeting will be held at 7:30
o'clock in the Y.W.C.A. rooms in
the Women's Clubhouse.
