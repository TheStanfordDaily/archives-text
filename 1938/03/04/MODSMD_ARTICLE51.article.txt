# Richards Elected Orchesis Leader
## 
### 
Helen Richards was elected presi
dent of Orchesis, campus dance hon
orary organization, at their last
meeting, succeeding Elise Cahen.
Members of the group will assist
at a High School Dance' Symposium
to be held at Sequoia Union High
School on March 5. Over 250 girls
will take part, and Betty Waterman,
graduate dance major, will lead
half of them in a master lesson.
Margaret Jewell, dance instructor
and adviser of Orchesis, will lead
discussion on "High School Dance"
at the luncheon meeting. After
luncheon a demonstration of dance
composition will be presented by
Stanford and San Jose groups.
