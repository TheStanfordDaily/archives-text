# Deposit Deadline Set For Next Week
## 
### 
All Toyon and Branner residents
must make room reservations and
pay room deposits Monday and
Tuesday of next week, according to
an announcement from the Dean
of Men's office.
Old Union and Sequoia men must
pay room deposits and make reser
vations at the Dean of Men's office
Thursday and Friday of next week.
Only men living in the halls now
may make reservations at this time.
