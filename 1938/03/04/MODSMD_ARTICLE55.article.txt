# Deputies Warned Against Strike
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, March 2 (UP).
Sheriff Don Cox warned his stall of
unionized deputies today that they
will be subject to immediate dis
missal if they participate in a strike.
The sheriff's warning followed dis
closure that all but seven or eight
of his staff of 28 men had joined
the American Federation of State,
County and Municipal Employees,
an American Federation of Labor
Union.
