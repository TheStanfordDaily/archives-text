# SPAIN OUTLAWS DIVORCE
## 
### 
BURGOS, March 3 (UP).—A de
cree suspending all divorce pro
ceedings in Nationalist Spain was
approved today by the cabinet as the
first step to abolish divorce which
was legalized by the Madrid regime.

SAN FRANCISCO, March 3 (UP).
—The Advisory Pardon Board to
day took under consideration the
application of Coke and John Brite,
Yreka triple-slayers, for commuta
tion of their death sentence to life
imprisonment. A spokesman said
the members "pearly reached a de
cision on the Brite application," but
decline to reveal whether it was
favorable or otherwise.
