# Gallery Exhibits Desert Paintings
## 
### 
The Stanford Art Gallery is now
exhibiting the paintings of Alson
Clark, a prominent Southern Cali
fornia artist, who is noted for his
desert and mountain scenes.
His paintings have won medals
and prizes in the St. Louis and San
Francisco expositions and from the
Los Angeles Museum of Art, and
took the Huntington prize in 1925.
Clark is a member of the Allied
Arts of America and the Chicago
Society of Artists, as well as several
other art organizations.
