# Rider Places In N.C.A.A. Meet
## 
### 
For the second consecutive year
Dave Rider, captain of the Stanford
swimming team, placed second to
John Macionis of Yale in the 1500-
meter championship of the N.C.A.A.
last week.
Rider also won his heat in the 440
but failed to do better than sixth in
the finals. His time of 20:38 was con
siderably slower than his runner-up
mark of last year.
