# Leland Stanford Honored in East
## 
### 
The memory of Leland Stanford
will be commemorated in the Hall
of Fame in Schenectady, New York,
according to William A. Wick, presi
dent of the Schenectady public li
brary. A tablet, surmounted by a
photograph or portrait, will be placed
on the wall of a reading room in the
library for each man who has
brought fame to Schenectady.
Leland Stanford was born in Al
bany County and resided in Sche
nectady when a boy with his par
ents at Locust Grove, now the In
gersoll Memorial Home for Aged
Men.
