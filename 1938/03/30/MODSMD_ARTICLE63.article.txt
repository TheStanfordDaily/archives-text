# Deadline Set For Spring Show Skits
## 
### 
Today is the last chance for any
promising playwrights to exercise
their talents on spring show skils.
All skits must be turned in by 7
o'clock tonight, according to an an
nouncement yesterday.
Those writing must appear at the
Drama Council meeting to be held at
seven o'clock this evening in the
Dramatic office of the old Union
building.
