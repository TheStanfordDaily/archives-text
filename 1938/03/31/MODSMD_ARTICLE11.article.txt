# Officers Elected By Women Riders
## 
### 
The Woman's Riding Club elected
its new officers at a meeting in the
Nitery Tuesday night.
Evelyn Peyton was unanimously
elected president to replace Martha
Hall. Jerry Clift was elected secre
tary to replace Evelyn Peyton.
Nancy Milhauser was elected Jane
Hamersly's successor for business
manager. The new officers will lead
the club in planning many rides,
picnics, and moonlight barbecues.
A vote of thanks was given to the
outgoing president, Martha Hall, for
her fine work in making many girls
riding conscious. Pins in the form
of horseshoes have been ordered for
each member of the club.
