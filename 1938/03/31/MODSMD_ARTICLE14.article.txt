# Theta Sigs Initiate Ten Journalists
## 
### 
Initiated into Theta Sigma Phi,
women's professional journalism
fraternity, last night were Lynne
Ashton, Kay Cady, Betty Davis,
Gene Dulin, Eunice Emry, Jeanette
Hill, Kay Johnson, Enid Kuchel,
Beryl Randall, and Geraldine Reed.
The initiation was conducted by
Zan Hammond, president of the or
ganization, with the assistance of
other active members and Alumna
Adviser Mrs. Carol Greene Wilson.
Activities discussed by the group
following the formal initiation in
cluded plans for a garden tea to be
held later in the quarter, and visits
to San Francsico newspaper offices.
