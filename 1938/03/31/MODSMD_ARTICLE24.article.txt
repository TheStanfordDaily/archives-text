# Script Deadline Set for Tomorrow
## 
### 
Deadline for the prospective
Spring Show script will be at 5
o'clock tomorrow afternoon. Clay
McDnniels, dramatic manager, an
nounced yesterday.
Authors of the script, together
with the dramatic council, will meet
at 10 o'clock Monday morning in
McDaniels' office. Old Union, to se
lect the script which will be used.
