# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
THE EDUCATION CLUB will hold
its first hike of spring quarter next
Sunday at 2 o'clock starting from
the Engineering Corner. Bring
either a car or 10 cents for trans
portation.
MASQUE BALL COMMITTEE will
meet tonight at 7 o'clock in the
Nitery.
PHI BETA KAPPA meets today at
1 o'clock in Union Court for Quad
pictures.
RESERVATIONS for the invitational
ski trip at Yosemite this week-end
maj be made at the A.S.S.U. office
now.
PEACE DEBATE AND FORUM
will be held tonight at 7:30 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
