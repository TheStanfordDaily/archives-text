# Frosh Net Team Whips Paly High
## 
### 
Stanford's undefeated frosh net
team chalked up another decisive
win yesterday when it trounced a
team from Palo Alto High by a 7-2
count. Bob Low of the Indians top
ped Andy Roberts, 6-4, 6-3, in the
1 feature singles assignment.
The summaries:
Singles: Low (S) d. Roberts 6-4,
! 6-3; Owen (S) d. Lamb 6-4, 6-4;
Ballard (S) d. Olsen 6-2, 7-5; Rob
erts (S) d. Chappie 6-1, 6-3; Pat
terson (PH) d. Stahl 0-6, 6-3, 6-1;
Terry (S) d. Neel 6-2, 7-5.
Doubles: Low-Owen (S) d. Olsen-
Roberls 8-6, 6-3; Patterson-Chap
pie (PH) d. Terry-Ballard 2-6, 6-4,
6-4; Cook-Cannon (S) d. Neel-
Neilson 6-3, 6-0.
Denmark requires that every able
bodied man have military training.
