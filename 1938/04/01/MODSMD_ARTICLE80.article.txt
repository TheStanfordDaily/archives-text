# Women Debate Peace Problem
## 
### 
Norma Dales and Gene Glasmann
opened the Spring Quarter forensic
activities last night when they de
bated Nona Fumerton and Patricia
Hagman, a touring team from the
University of Washington.
In co-operation with the Stan
ford Peace Committee, the four
women participated in "problem
solving" symposium debate on
"How Can the United States Avoid
War?" Miss Dales and Miss Glas
mann took the case for isolation and
the evaluation speech, respectively.
Ed Kerr, debate manager, led an
audience forum on the subject fol
lowing the symposium. Mrs. Hill,
women's debate coach, accompanied
the Washington women.
