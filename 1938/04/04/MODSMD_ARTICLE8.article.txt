# Prize Offered For Best Peace Essay
## 
### 
Winner of the Stanford Dames
student essay contest on "What the
Student Can Do To Further Peace"
will receive a cash award of $10, it
was disclosed by members of the
committee several days ago.
Regulations for the competition,
which is being sponsored in con
junction with the current Peace Day
drive, specify that no paper ex
ceeding 1000 words will be accepted.
The contest will be judged by a
committee from the Stanford Dames.
