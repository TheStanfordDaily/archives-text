# Vocational Group Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
Headed by Dean Mary Yost, the
first vocational guidance meeting of
the spring quarter will meet to
morrow night at 7 o'clock in Casa
del Eucalypto of Lagunita Court to
discuss the profession of dean of
women.
Taking part in the discussion will
be Rosamunde Clarke, Stanford
graduate, who is now a student
counselor and head of one of the
living groups at Scripps.
Chairman of the Vocational Guid
ance Committee is Georgia Petti
bone. Other members include
Patricia McMurtry, Anne Steward,
Elizabeth Verheyen, Margaret Loud,
Lee Lewis, Marjorie Segerstrom,
Sally Harwood, and Janet Mclntyre.
