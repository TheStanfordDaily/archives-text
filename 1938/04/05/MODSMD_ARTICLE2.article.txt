# Sequoia's Dummy Hits Farm Vision
## 
### 
Many embryo Farm politicos
suffered a serious relapse yester
day when they passed Engineer
ing Corner, there to see a husky
effigy swinging from a lamp-post
in a lively manner.
Edging up to the scarecrow,
fearing the moment when they
would see their names plastered in
public view by their opponents,
even before nomination time, the
Farm Farleys soon recovered equi
librium upon discovering that the
figure was merely a novel adver
tisement for Sequoia's Barn Dance.
A straw hat topped a tomato
juiced fare and the latest llolly
v.ood scarf. Completing the out
fit were white kid gloves, stylish
sport coat, flannel slacks, and
House of David shoes.
