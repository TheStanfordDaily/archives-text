# Mitchell To Attend Registrars' Meet
## 
### 
Dr. J. Pierce Mitchell left the
campus yesterday to attend meet
ings of the American Association of
College Registrars in New Orleans,
scheduled for April 19 through April
21, according to his secretary, Mrs.
Elizabeth Richardson.
The organization is a national
"talk-shop" for registrar-represen
tatives from American colleges. Dr.
Mitchell will attend to some inci
dental business in New York first.
He will return to Stanford about
April 25.
