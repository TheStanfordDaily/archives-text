# BUDGE GOES SOUTH
## 
### 
OAKLAND, April 5 (UP).—Be
fore he left for Chicago on a business
trip yesterday, Donald J. Budge, j
Davis Cup tennis star, announced
that he intended to move from here j
to Los Angeles because "the climate]
here is too moist and I get the 1
sniffles."
