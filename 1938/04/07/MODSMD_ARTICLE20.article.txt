# Tryouts for Play To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for Paul Green's play,
"Johnny Johnson," to be presented
by the Speech and Drama depart
ment in the Memorial Theater on
May 6 and 7, are to be held in the
Green Room of the Memorial Hall
from 2 to 6 o'clock this afternoon.
The play, which was considered for
the Pulitzer Prize last year, is be
ing presented solely for the purpose
of pleasing the students, according
to Gordon Lange.
Twenty-five male roles are still
open to tryoutees. Insane asylum in
mates, which provide very interest
ing characters, offer the most parts
to be filled. Anyone interested may
try out. regardless of previous ex
perience.
