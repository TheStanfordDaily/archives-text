# Son of Alumnus To Star in Films
## 
### 
The 11-year-old son of a Stan
ford graduate wili proceed to earn
a half-million dollars acting for the
movies under a seven-year option
contract signed Tuesday with Para
mount Studios in Hollywood.
He is Billy Cook, son of Mr. and
Mrs. Lawrence Cook, Menlo Park
residents. Mr. Cook graduated
from Stanford in 1920.
The youngster, who received his
dramatic training in the Palo Alto
Children's Theater, will he starred
in Hollywood's first all-color air
drama, "Men With Wings." He at
tended Las Lomitas School until six
months ago when, on the advice of
friends, Mrs. Cook took him to Hol
lywood in an endeavor to secure
screen and radio work.
