# Alabama Tornado Kills Eleven People
## 
### 
GORDO, Ala., April 7 (UP). —
Eleven persons were reported killed
late today when a tornado struck
this section near the Alabama-
Mississippi lnie.
It was also reported that in
Aliceville, 20 miles to the south,
two white women and six negroes
were killed and at least 10 injured.

projects in Ireland are demanding
lower rents.
