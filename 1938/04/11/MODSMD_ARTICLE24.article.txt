# Seniors Approve Printing Contract
## 
### 
An advertising campaign to let
the world know they will soon be
graduated was approved last week
end' by the seniors' "promotion"
committee when it let the programs
and-announcements contract to L. G.
Balfour & Co., a Massachusetts
printing firm.
Ten announcements, tastefully en
graved to appeal to old friends of
the family, and one program, bound
in white age-proof leather, will cost
seniors $2, Isabel Goldthwaite and
A1 Oddy, committee chairmen, de
clared.
Sale will begin Thursday, when
samples will be displayed at the
A.S.S.U. office. Advance reserva
tion is necessary, the co-chairmen
said.
