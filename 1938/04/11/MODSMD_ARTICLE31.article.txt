# HALL COMES NEXT
## 
### 
The articles presented here
are concerned almost exclu
sively with Row politics. Re
ports of Row machinations are
published before those of Hall,
Because more complete infor
mation is available on them and
because this year they were
more dramatic.
Later articles will present a
summary of political activities
in the Halls.
