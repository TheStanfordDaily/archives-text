# Come One, Come All
## 
### 
Now that spring has arrived, the lark is safely
launched on the wing, and the snail is comfort
ably settle on the thorn, it is time for the Daily
staff to gather for its weekly house- and air
warming. Party begins at the Shack at 1:15
o'clock. Edit board meets at 1 o'clock to plan
the entertainment.
