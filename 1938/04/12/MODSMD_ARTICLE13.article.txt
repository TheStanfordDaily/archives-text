# Debate Meetings To Be Postponed
## 
### 
Debate meetings at 2 and 4 o'clock
today will be postponed until the
University of Washington debate
this Thursday at 4 o'clock in Room
202, it was announced yesterday.
Bob Curtiss and Armen Hampar,
who are now in Reno attending the
Pacific Forensic League tournament
at the University of Nevada, will
represent Stanford.
