# Quads Go on Sale Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
Quads will be on sale 111 front of
the Post Office today and tomorrow
from 8 to 12 o'clock and from 1 to
1:45 o'clock, according to an an
nouncement by Quentin Birchard,
circulation manager.
Minthorne Tompkins, sales man
agr, reports sales thus far this year
arc much greater than last year at
this time. The 1937 Quad was a
sell-out, he says, and many people
were unable to get theirs. Students
are advised to order their 1938
Quads in the near future as a sell
out is anticipated before long.
