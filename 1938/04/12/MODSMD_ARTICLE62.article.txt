# 'Summer Music' School' Planned
## 
### 
A "Summer School of Music,' -
whose visiting faculty will include
members of the Roth Quartet and
several important critics and teach
ers of music, was announced yester
day for the summer quarter by War
ren D. Allen, chairman of the Divi
sion of Music.
The Roth Quartet, sponsored by
Mrs. Elizabeth Sprague Coolidge
and other members of the "Friends
of Music" group on the peninsula,
will give concerts and also instruct
a limited number of students in a
class on chamber music.
In addition to chamber music,
there will be classes on American
music, music education, music as a
dramatic art, music history, and mu- j
