# ‘Y’ Makes Plans For Installation
## 
### 
Plans for the coming installation
of new "Y" officers, to be held next
Tuesday. April 19, were the subject
of discussion at a meeting of the
"Y" cabinet yesterday.
The installation, to be attended by
cabinet members, members of the
executive committee of the "Y," and
Palo Alto Board members, will take
place in the chapel of the Memorial
Church. Following the installation a
dinner will be held in the Women's
Clubhouse.
The committee for the installation
is as follows: Mary Kay Horton,
Jackie Brown, Gerry Clift, and Mrs.
Amelia Rathbun.
Angela Gates' personnel group will
meet today at 4 o'clock. Dean Mary
Yost will be the guest speaker.
