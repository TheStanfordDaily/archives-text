# Budge, Von Cramm Film To Be Shown
## 
### 
Pictures of the finals of the na
tional singles matches of last year,
starring Don Budge and Baron Gott
fried von Cramm, will be shown to
day at 4:15 o'clock in Room 100,
Coach Lockwood announced last
night.
Sections of the film showing the
most brilliant plays and the best
form will be run both in slow mo
tion and in regular motion. Anyone
interested in tennis is welcome, and
there will be no charge for admis
sion.
