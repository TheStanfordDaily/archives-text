# Frosh Track Meet To Be Held Today
## 
### 
The frosh track meet with Sacred |
| Heart High School of San Francisco I
| was postponed until this afternoon ,
iat 4 o'clock at Angcll Field. Sacred
Heart s times have not been very
good this season with about. 4:58 in
the mile and 2:12 in the half-mile.
Their shot-putter is good for about
47 feet.
On Saturday, the frosh will meet
J Modestl) Jaysee, who are reported to
have a good squad, having beaten
; San Mateo already this season. As
the frosh also have beaten them, the
meet promises to be a good one.
