# Dames To Meet With Miss Doyle
## 
### 
. The ' Women and the Home Arts" |
section of the Stanford Dames will
meet tonight at the home of Mrs. H.
M. Heberer, 390 Mariposa. Miss An
astasia Doyle, director of women's
residences, will lead the discussion.

All wives of Stanford students are
invited to come to the meeting, as
well as members of the section.
Fast trains link Poking and Tokyo
n GO hours' traveling time.
