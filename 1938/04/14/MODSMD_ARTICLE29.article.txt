# Cahn Seeks Music For 'Is Europe?'
## 
### 
J. P. Cahn, director of the spring
show, "Is Europe?" announced yes
terday that he is still looking for
musical numbers for the show.
Two numbers are needed. One
should be in the nature of an alma
mater song, Cahn said. The other
should be similar to the Halleluia
from "Hit the Deck." All those wish
ing further mformatiorf concerning
the songs should talk to Director
Cahn at the dramatic office.
