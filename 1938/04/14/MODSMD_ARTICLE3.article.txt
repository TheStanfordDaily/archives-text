# Papal Invitation Given to Hitler
## 
### 
BERLIN, April 13 (UP). — The
Pope today invited Fuehrer Adolf
Hitler to call on him when he visits
Rome during May, it was under
stood.
Msgr. Cesare Orsenigo, papal en
voy to Berlin, went to the Foreign
Office and conferred with Foreign
Minister Joachim von Ribbentrop
and it was understood he delivered
the papal invitation.
