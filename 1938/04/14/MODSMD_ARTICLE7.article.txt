# Masque Ball Band Choice Is Tonight
## 
### 
Final choice of an orchestra for
tlie Masque Ball, May 6, will be made
tonight at a committee meeting to
be-held in the Nitery at 7:30 o'colck.
Reports on decorations and enter
tainment will be heard, discussed,
and major details of the dance, the
theme of which is "Kid Party," will
be completed.
