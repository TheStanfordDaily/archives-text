# Sellards To Leave Stanford Position
## 
### 
Dr. John A. Sellards, associate
professor of Romanic languages, will
assume a full professorship next year
at the University of Washington and
the following year will become chair
man of the Department of Romanic
Languages at that university, it was
announced yesterday.
Besides being an associate pro
fessor at Stanford, Dr. Sellards is
acting director of his department for
summer quarter, as well as a mem
ber of the Committee on Public Ex
ercises.
