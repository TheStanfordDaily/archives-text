# Robleites To Have Easter-egg Hunt
## 
### 
Freshman women are going to for
sake their traditional sophistication
come Easter and go in for kid stunts.
Roble is having an Easter egg hunt
on the front lawn, and the girl who
finds the most eggs will get a prize.
The event will follow an annual
Easter breakfast, which will take
place at 8:45 o'clock. Anne Killefer
heads the committee in charge of the
breakfast.
