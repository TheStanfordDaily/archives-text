# Chi Omega Plans Easier Egg Hunt
## 
### 
Children at the Convalescent
Home will hunt Chi Omega Easter
eggs Sunday morning and get chicks,
ducks, and rabbits, symbolic of the
day.
The Easter egg hunt is an annual
activity of the sorority to which they
invite faculty guests. This year the
committee chairman is Helen Wads
worth. Other members include
Frances Fox, Elizabeth McCallum,
Helengrace Sawyer, and Renee
Holder).
