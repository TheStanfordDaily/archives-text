# Parley Marks End Of Circus Strike
## 
### 
NEW YORK, April 14 (UP).—The
first circus strike ended today.
Roustabouts, grooms and hawkers
of Ringling Brothers, Barnum and
Bailey, returned to work with a new
wage agreement obtained at 2:30 in
the morning after a nine-hour con
ference.
