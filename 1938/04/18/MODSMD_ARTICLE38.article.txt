# Bears Swamp Card Net Team
## Pasadena J.C. Beats Frosh; U.C. Wins 9-2 
### 
California's well balanced tennis
| team swept through Stanford by a
1 9-2 score on the Berkeley courts
| Saturday afternoon in the first of
1 the two annual engagements this
;season.
Without the services of Captain
| Bob Underwood the Indians were
j weakened considerably and Cal was
! able to take five of the six singles
1 matches.
Harman had no trouble with
! Stanford's Braly in the lead-off bat
tle, but Brock and Gates forced
their respective Cal number two
and three men to close extra sets be
fore succumbing.
Seaver, in the number four berth,
captured Stanford's singles win by
downing Hoogs in a marathon, 3-6,
8-6, 11-9.
On the bandhouse courts the
morning of the Cal match the pre
viously unbeaterf Card frosh fell
before Pasadena J. C. by a 4-3
count. The visitors took three of the
singles assignments and Freeman
and Wade cinched the match by
beating Low and Owens in the
number one doubles encounter at
7-5 in the deciding set.
