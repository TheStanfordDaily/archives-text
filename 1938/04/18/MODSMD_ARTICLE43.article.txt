# Indian Polo Club To Meet Arizona
## 
### 
Indian poloists take to their horses
this afternoon at 3 o'clock on the
highway field when they meet the
University of Arizona in the first of
a two-game series to be played here.
Polo is a major sport at the south
ern school and the Wildcats enter
the meet as favorites, having defeat
ed the Cards in two previous
matches with them.
