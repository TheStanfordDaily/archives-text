# POET'S WIFE DIES
## 
### 
PORT RICHMOND, N.Y., April 17
(UP). —Mrs. Anna C. Markham, wife
of Edwin Markham, poet who wrote
"The Man with the Hoe," died today
at her home after a long illness.
