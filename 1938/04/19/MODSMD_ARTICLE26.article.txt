# Lagunita informal Slated for May 13
## 
### 
"May Frolic" will be the theme
of Lagunita Court's spring informal
to be given Friday, May 13. The
following committee members have
been chosen by Mary Hayes, chair
man:
Tommy Thompson, programs;
Barbara Bine, patrons; Dorothy
Gray, invitations; Mollie West, re
freshments; Pat Flannigan, arrange
ments; Jean Haber, reception; Ra
mona Dunshee, publicity; and Ruth
Harris, tickets.
