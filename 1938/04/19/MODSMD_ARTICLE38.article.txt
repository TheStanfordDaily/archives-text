# BIKE RACES OPEN
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH, April 18 (UP). —
Eleven two-man teams opened the
sixth six-day bicycle race of 2500
miles on the wooden oval at Du
quesne Garden last night.

CHICAGO, April 18 (UP).—Base
ball Commissioner K. M. Landis an
nounced the New York Giants and
the Cleveland Indians had been
fined $200 for disputes in Texas dur
ing training season.
