# Senior Program Samples on Show
## 
### 
Orders for senior programs and
announcements will be taken in
front of the Post Office today at
noon. Samples will be available for
examination.
One program and 10 announce
ments will be $2, and one program
and 20 announcements will be S3.
Cash payment is necessary at the
time the order is placed. Deadline
for ordering has been set at May 6.
Programs will be approximately
30 pages, and will contain pictures
of campus scenes, the entire senior
week program, senior committees,
and lists of graduating seniors and
advanced degree holders. An
nouncements will be engraved.
Orders can also be placed at the
A.S.S.U. office.
