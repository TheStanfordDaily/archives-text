# Christian Science Lecture Scheduled
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Murmey Glenn,
C.S.B. of Boston, will speak at a
Christian Science lecture to be held
tomorrow evening at 7:30 o'clock.
Miss Glenn is a member of the
board lectureship of the mother
church.
The Christian Science organiza
tion at Stanford is responsible for
the presentation of the lecture
which will be free. All members of
the faculty and all students are in
vited to attend.
