# Peace Luncheon Ticket Sale Ends
## 
### 
Tickets for the lunchoen and
"international festival" to be held in
the Union Court Stanford Peace
Day, April 27, will go on sale at the
post office for the last time today.
At a meeting of the Peace Day
committee yesterday it was decided
to cut down the number of round
tables from 15 to approximately
nine. The decision was made after
committee members had discussed
the matter with several of the Stan
ford men who have agred to lead
round tables.
A complete list of the round table
discussion leaders and their topics
will be issued within a few days,
according to Jackie Brown, commit
tee member handling the groups.
