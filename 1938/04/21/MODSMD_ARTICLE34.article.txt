# Frosh To Debate Cal Team Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford freshman debating
team will debate with the Cal frosh
team tonight at 7:30 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. Hugh O'Don
nell, Robert Newell, and Robert
Beckham, representing Stanford,
will take the affirmative side of
the question, resolved: that the C.
I. O. is detrimental to labor.
This afternoon at 4 o'clock the
other half of the frosh debate squad
will take the negative side of this
question. This debate will take
place at Cal. Frosh debaters taking
the negative side of the question
at Cal are Sherman Melinkoff, Ned
Pugh, and Sid Terry.
