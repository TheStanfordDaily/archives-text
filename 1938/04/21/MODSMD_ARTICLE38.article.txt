# GUERARD TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Dr. Albert Guerard, Professor of
General Literature, will be the guest
speaker at the next meeting of the
Married Men's Club to be held this
Friday afternoon at 4:15 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse. He will
speak on the subject "In Defense of
Dictatorships." The Club meets each
Friday afternoon at the same time.
The human heart commonly is
spoken of as being on the left side
of the body, but it really is situated
on both sides.
