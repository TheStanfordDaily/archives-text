# Court To Insure Child Stars' Pay
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, April 21 (UP).—
A court move to safeguard the earn
ings of young screen players was
taken today as a direct consequence
of the plight of Jackie Coogan, who
is battling to retain from his mother
a share of the vast fortune he earned
as a child star.
