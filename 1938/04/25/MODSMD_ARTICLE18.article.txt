# Bradbury to Speak On Stanford Hour
## 
### 
Dr. Norris E. Bradbury, associate
professor of physics at Stanford,
will be the speaker on the Stanford
University Radio Hour over KGO
tonight at 9:15 o'clock. The sub
ject of his talk is to be "Exploring
the Upper Atmosphere."
The advancement of aviation in
its conquest of the stratosphere and
of radio communication both de
pend upon the recent discoveries of
American physicists concerning the
properties of the upper atmosphere.
Dr. Bradbury will summarize some
recent findings in properties of the
stratosphere and the ionosphere.
