# Cal Takes Cards In 6-3 Tennis Tilt
## 
### 
At full strength for the first time
in nearly a month, Stanford's tennis
team still wasn't good enough to
turn in a conference win last Satur
day on the new courts, when the
Bears came out on top 6-3 to sink
the Indians into the cellar in the
final tabulations for the season.
Summaries:
Singles: Harman (C) d. Braly 6-3,
8-6; Imhoff (C) d. Brock 4-6, 6-2,
6-3; Underwood (S) d. Peacock 6-4,
6-4; Goldman (C) d. Gates 4-6, 6-1,
6-3; Seaver (S) d. Neel 6-4, 6-2;
Morrison (C) d. Folsom 6-4, 6-4.
Doubles: Harman-Imhoff (C) d.
Brock- Underwood 6-2, 6-4; Hoogs-
Peacock (C) d. Seaver-Folsom 4-6.
6-3, 6-1; Braly-Stoefen (S) d. Hyde-
Gilmore 6-4, 6-4.
