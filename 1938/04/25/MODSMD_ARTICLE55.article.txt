# SHOOTING VICTIM DIES
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, April 24 (UP).
Frank Espnoisa, 23, of Yolo, shot
last Friday by an unidentified ranch
worker, died in the county hospital
today. The shooting followed a quar
rel on the Sacramento river bridge
here. Espinosa's assailant escaped.
Good magicians earn from $300
per week up and spend about half
a year performing before audiences.
The other hall of the year usually is
devoted to the developing of new
skills and devices.
