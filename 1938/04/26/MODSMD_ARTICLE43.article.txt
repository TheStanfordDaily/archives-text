# Frosh Netmen Trip Rams
## 
### 
Stanford's powerful yearling net
aggregation finished its season with
a 5-4 win over the previously un
beaten San Francisco J. C. team yes
terday on the varsity courts.
Captain Neil Ballard and Bob Low
turned in singles victories for the
frosh and remained undefeated for
the season. Low had plenty of
trouble getting by Harold Wagner
4-6, 6-2, 6-3, while Ballard went
through in straight sets.
Coach Sherm Lockwood announc
ed that Low and Stan Owen would
represent Stanford in the freshmen
and junior college tournament at
Ojai beginning Thursday. Six mem
bers of the varsity will also make
the trip.
Summaries:
Singles: Low (S) d. Wagner 4-6,

6-2, 6-3; Owen (S) d. Lazard 5-7, 6-2,
8-6; Ballard (S) d. Kun 6-2, 6-3;
Ennes (SF) d. Roberts 4-6, 10-8. 6-!J;
Lundy (S) d. Altfield 6-2, 3-6, 9-7;
Worthington (S) d. Berna 7-5, 6-3.
Doubles: Wagner and Lazare (SF)
d. Low and Owens 6-3, 6-3; Ballard
and Roberts (S) d. Enner and Alt
field 7-5, 3-6, 6-3; Berna and Kun
(S) d. Lundy and Stahl 8-6, 4-6. 6-4.
