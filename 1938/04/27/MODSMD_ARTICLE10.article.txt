# Guerard To Speak At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
"Peace and Internationalism" is
the subject which Dr. Albert Guer
ard, professor of literature, will dis
cuss before the regular Luncheon
Club meeting at noon today in the
Woman's Clubhouse.
Dr. Guerard is internationally
famous as a critic and political com
mentary. He has had numerous
books published on subjects ranging
from literary criticism to interna
tional languages and problems, his
latest released last year is titled
"Art for Art's Sake." He is a na
turalized citizen, having been born
in France.
