# Monterey Works Shown At Gallery
## 
### 
A group of paintings and etchings
by Tulita Westfall, Monterey artist,
is now on exhibition at the Stanford
Art Gallery where it will remain
for the rest of April.
The pictures are done for the most
part in a decorative style, emphasiz
ing color and line. Also on display
are several pencil sketches, originals
of Mrs. Westfall's larger works
which are done in oil.
The artist studied at the San
Francisco School of Fine Arts and
under Pedro Lemos, director of the
Stanford Art Gallery.
