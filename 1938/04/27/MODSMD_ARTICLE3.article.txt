# Professors Take Summer Positions
## 
### 
Paul R. Farnsworth, associate pro
fessor of psychology, and Dr. Bay
ard O. Morgan, professor of German,
have accepted appointments to the
faculty of the University of Wis
consin for summer quarter, begin
ning June 27.
Richard T. LaPiere, assistant pro
fessor of economics, has received an
appointment at Northwestern Uni
versity for one academic year.
The appointment, of Dr. Kurt F.
Reinhardt, associate professor of
German, to the faculty of the Uni
versity of Southern California for
the first period of the summer ses
sion has been announced by the
southern school. Dr. Reinhardt will
teach German and drama courses.
