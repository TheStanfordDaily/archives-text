# Students, Profs To Meet In Nitery
## 
### 
A regular informal gathering of
students and faculty members will
be held this afternoon in the Nitery
from 4 o'clock on. After a lapse of a
few weeks, the series of informal
meetings is being renewed. They
will be held every Wednesday after
noon at the same time, this quarter.
All students and members of the
faculty are cordially invited to at
tend the gathering. Cider will be
served, as usual.
