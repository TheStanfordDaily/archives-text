Foreign Service Careers
Turner's Diplomatic
School

preparation for the examinations
for the Foreign Service to be held
on September 12, 13, and Mth,
1938.. Registration strictly limited
to graduates of recognized colleges
and universities.

Colonel Campbell Turner
1774 Massachusetts Avenue, N.W.
Washington, D.C.
