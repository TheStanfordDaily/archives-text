STANFORD DAMES BRIDGE
CLUB will meet today at 2:30
o'clock at the home of Mrs. R.
Harlow, 419 Lincoln, P. A.
WOMEN'S SWIMMING POOL will
be opened every Sunday, 2:30-
4:30 for registered men and women
students.
CHILD STUDY GROUP will meet
tonight at 7:30 o'clock at the home
of Mrs. Carl S. Brand, 433 Foot
hill Drive, P. A.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANIZA
TION meets tonight at 7:30 o'clock
on top floor of the Women's Club- 1
house.
UNIVERSITY PHILOLOGICAL SO
CIETY meets today at 4:15 o'clock
in the lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse. Professor Bayard Q.
Morgan and Miss Dorothy Epplcn
will speak.
