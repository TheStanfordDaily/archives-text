# Atherton Followed By L. A. Police
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 27 (UP)
Edwin N. Atherton, former G-man
who conducted the San Francisco
police graft investigation of 1936-37,
said today he had been "shadowed"
by Los Angeles police officers when
he was in Southern California dur
ing investigation of the Harry Ray
mond bombing case.
They were not only following me
around down there," Atherton said.
But they stood in the halls outside
my office, I am satisfied they tap
ped my telephone."
