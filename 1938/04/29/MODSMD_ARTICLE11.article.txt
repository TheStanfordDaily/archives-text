# Water Carnival Plans Completed
## 
### 
Plans for ihe Junior Water Car
nival on May 6 have been complet
ed, it was announced last night by
Dave Ryus, junior class president.
Edwina Ellis, Kappa, is in charge
of the floats, and Chappie Stearns
and Barbara Day are co-chairmen
of the price committee. Walt Wick
ett will be master of ceremonies and
call the events.
Two silver cups, wearing apparel,
cover charges in the San Francisco
hotels, merchandise orders, theater
passes, and silk stockings are among
the prizes that will be given to the
contest winners.
