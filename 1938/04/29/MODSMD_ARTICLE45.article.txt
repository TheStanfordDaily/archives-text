# Winkelmann Bide For Diving Crown
## 
### 
Roy Winkelmann, two-time Coast
diving champion, will enter the
men's junior national champion
ships tonight in the Fairmont
Plunge.
He is the only Stanford entrant
on a program which includes the
senior women's AA.U. springboard
championships and a special 500-
yard free style for senior men. Since
winning the conference title last
Saturday, Winkelmann has been re
ceiving tutelage from both Ernie
BrancLsten, Olympic Games coach,
and Pete Desjardines, Olympic
champ in 1928.
