# Officers Elected By Law School
## 
### 
After a heated political campaign,
a coalition of Phi Delta Phi and in
dependents captured Law School of
fices last night, electing their slate
of Al Burford and Robert Blewett
for president and vice-president, res
pectively.
Other officers elected were Marian
Leachman, secretary; Leroy Wright,
treasurer; Claude Owens and Jack
Stevenson, membors-at-large.
