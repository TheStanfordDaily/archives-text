# Naturalist To Talk On Dinosaur Hunt
## 
### 
Arthur Sterry Coggeshall, director
of the Santa Barbara Museum of
Natural History, speaks tomorrow
night on "Exploring for Dinosaurs"
at 7:30 o'clock in Memorial Hall on
the Tuesday Evening Series.
The lecture will be illustrated by
motion pictures of the government
work in the graveyard of the Juras
sic Monsters in Utah.
In addition, Dr. Coggeshall will
show the historic film "Lost World,"
which will turn time back to bring
"living" dinosaurs to the screen. The
public is invited.
