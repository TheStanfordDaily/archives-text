# Wilbur Will Lead Forum Tomorrow
## 
###

the second of this quarter's series of
"fireside forums" tomorrow evening
from 9 to 10:30 o'clock in the lobby
of Toyon Hall.
The topic picked by Dr. Wilbur
for his talk and the discussion which
will follow it is "The Future of
Stanford University."
