# STANFORD POET COMPETES
## 
### 
Kaki Page, Stanford student, was
an entrant in the California student
poetry contest sponsored by the
Intercollegiate Fellowship of Crea
tive Art, it was announced today in
the publication of "First the Blade"
by the English Club of Mills Col
lege. Her two poems were "Inter
lude in Grand Central" and "Antici
pation."
