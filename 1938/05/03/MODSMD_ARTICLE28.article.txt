# A.S.S.U. Limits Dividex Entrants
## 
### 
The team that will manage next
year's Dividex, loose-leaf notebook
index which is annually issued by
A.S.S.U., was yet unchosen last night
after Publications Council yesterday
selected three teams of finalists,
eliminated five other teams.
The three that are still under con
sideration include Lloyd Wilson and
Lyle Cook; Ruth Bloch and Jean
Buchard; Ken Cuthbertson and
Lewis Love.
