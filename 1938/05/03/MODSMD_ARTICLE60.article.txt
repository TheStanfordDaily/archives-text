# Hosteller To Speak At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Warren Blodgelt, who is actively
engaged in the Youth's Hostel Move
ment, will speak tomorrow at noon
before the Luncheon Club in the al
cove of the Women's Clubhouse.
Mr. Blodgett, who is one of the
leading directors of the movement,
will speak on "The American
Youth's Hostel Movement." The
speech will be one of a series of
talks sponsored by the Luncheon
Club.
