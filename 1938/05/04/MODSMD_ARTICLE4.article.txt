# Expert To Speak On Youth Hostels
## 
### 
"The American Youth Hostel
Movement" has been selected as the
topic of an informal discussion be
fore the Luncheon Club at noon to
day, which will be led by Wan'en
Blodgett, local enthusiast of the
hostels.
Mr. Blodgett has been interested
in youth hostels since the beginning
of the movement. He recently re
turned from an inspection tour of
them in New England, the region
where the movement was begun in
America and where it is still strong
est.
The meeting will begin at 12
o'clock in the alcove of the Women's
Clubhouse and is open to all inter
ested students. Student Chairmen
of the meeting are Wilma Baker and
Vaughn Raymond.
