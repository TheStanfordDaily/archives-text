# Durand To Visit Dam Project
## 
### 
Dr. William F. Durand, emeritus
professor of mechanical engineering,
left yesterday on a two-week in
spection tour of government proj
ects throughout the Southwest as a
member of the consulting board of
the United States Bureau of Recla
mation.
Dr. Durand's trip will take him to
Phoenix, Yuma, Redding, and Sac
ramento before he returns to Stan
ford on May 15 to deliver a talk be
fore the Alumni Conference to be
held here on that date.
Purpose of the trip is to inspect
dams and other construction work
recently undertaken by the govern
ment and to report findings of the
board of consultants to the Bureau
of Reclamation.
