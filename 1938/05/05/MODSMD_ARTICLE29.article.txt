# Former Professor Dies in Palo Alto
## 
### 
Dr. Tuley Francis Huntington,
author and former professor of Eng
lish at Stanford, died Tuesday eve
ning at his home at 51G Kingsley
Avenue after a brief illness. Fun
eral services will be held this after
noon at 2 o'clock at the Roller and
Hapgood chapel.
Dr. Huntington's name appeared
in the 1937 edition of Who's Who.
His works include English text
books, and several novels.
