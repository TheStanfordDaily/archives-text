# MEXICAN WORKERS STRIKE
## 
### 
JUAREZ, Mexico, May 5 (UP)-
The conciliation board of the Mexi
can Workers Union, Camera Sindi
cal Obrera, will send representatives
lo the Bab Cora ranch of William
Randolph Hearst to determine the
status of a strike of employes there,
Miguel Lopez Valencia, secretary
general of the union, said today.
