# SUNDAY CHURCH SERVICE
## 
### 
"A Forgotten View of Religion"
will be discussed by Dr. Alexander
C. Purdy, Acting University Chap
lain, in his sermon at the regular
morning service on Sunday in the
Memorial Church. The University
Choir, under the direction of War
ren D. Allen, University Organist,
will sing the anthem: "Grieve not
the Holy Spirit." by Noble. The
soprano solo will be sung by Audis
Davis. At 4 o'clock in the afternoon
Warren D. Allen will present an or
gan recital.
The aggregate value of all major
crops in California during 1937 was
$481,219,000, or greater than any crop
valuation since 1929.
