# Senior Programs Continue on Sale
## 
### 
Although deadline for purchasing
senior programs and announce
ments was last Friday, orders will
be taken at the A.S.S.U. office for
several more weeks accoi'ding to
Allen Oddie, committee co-chair
man.
Seven thousand announcements at
10 cents each and 550 programs at
$1 each already have been sold.
This fulfills the contract with the
printers but arrangements have
been made for several hundred
more of each.
According to Oddie, graduates of
business and law schools are eligible
to purchase programs and announce
ments.
