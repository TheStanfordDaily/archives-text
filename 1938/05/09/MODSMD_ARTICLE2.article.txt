# Accident Injures Luisetti Slightly
## 
### 
Hank Luisetti, star basketball
player, was injured slightly early
Saturday morning when an auto
mobile in which he was riding
turned over on Foothill Road just
past the golf course foot-bridge.
The accident happened shortly
after 2 o'clock. The machine skid
ded in loose gravel, it was re
ported by Campus Officer Gordy
Davis.
Luisetti sustained a sprained
thumb and a skinned knee, Davis
said. He was released from the
Palo Alto Hospital after exam
ination.
