# KOVACS WINS NET TITLE
## 
### 
BERKELEY, May 8 (UP). —Frank
Kovucs, 18-year-old Oakland player
who is considered future Davis Cup
material, and Mrs. Bonnie Miller
Blank of Los Angeles won the major
titles in the California State Tennis
Tournament today at the Berkeley
Tennis Club here.
