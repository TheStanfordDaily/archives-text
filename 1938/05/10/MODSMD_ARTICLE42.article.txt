# SALESMAN WINS GOLF TITLE
## 
### 
SANTA CRUZ. Cal., May 4 (UP)
Mat Palacio, young San Francisco
cigar salesman, held the Northern
California Amateur golfing title to
day after defeating Ernest Pieper,
San Jose, in the final round at Pasa
tiempo Country Club. Palacio won
2 and 1.
