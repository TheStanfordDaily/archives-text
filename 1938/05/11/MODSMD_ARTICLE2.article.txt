# Chem Fraternities To Hold Smoker
## 
### 
Alpha Chi Sigma, professional
chemistry fraternity, and Phi Lamb
da Upsilon, honorary chemistry
fraternity, will jointly sponsor a
smoker to be held at 7:30 o'clock
tonight in the Women's Clubhouse.
In charge of the affair are Dave
Swanberg, John Savage, Cy Kins
man, and Harry Minor.
