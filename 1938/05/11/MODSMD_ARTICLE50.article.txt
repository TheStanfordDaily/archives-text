SIGN-IJPS for the All-University
Tennis Tournament, to be be
gun May 17, should be made on
the bulletin board of the Enema
gym. Entries close May 16; seed
ing and drawing will be settled
before the tourney on the follow
ing day. Anyone registered in the
University may enter.
SECOND INSTALLMENT of wom
en's Room and Board bills are
payable today in the Ad Building
from 8 o'clock until 5.
ENCINA SPRING FORMAL bids go
on sale at the A.S.S.U. office today.
The dance is to be held May 13.

CLUB: Meeting tonight at 7:30
o'clock on the range.
"IS EUROPE" tickets go on sale

tomorrow at the A.S.S.U. office.

and dance, May 13, in the base
ment of the Women's Clubhouse,

to stay for refreshments are re
quested to make reservations by
phoning Mrs. Tudor, P.A. 23338 or
Mrs. Stanhope, P.A. 21145.

keys must sign the list in the A. S.
