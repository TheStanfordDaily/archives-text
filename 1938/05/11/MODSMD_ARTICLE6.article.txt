# Pi Lambda Theta To Hold Initiation
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, national wom
en's educational honorary society f
will hold its annual initiation ban
quet in the Union Alcove tomorrow
evening at 6 o'clock, according to
Pat Hamilton, president. Dinner
will be preceded by the formal initi
ation of new members in the Wom
en's Clubhouse at 5:15 o'clock
Dr. Marion Brown, dean of girls
at University High School in Oak
land, will be the speaker of the eve
ning, and music will be supplied by
the Sequoia Union High School
quintet. Margaret Barry is in
charge of arrangements.
