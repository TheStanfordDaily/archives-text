# Quads Go on Sale At English Corner
## 
### 
Quads will be on sale today on the
English Corner from 9 to 12 o'clock.
Sales to date indicate that the Quad
will be a sell-out within a short
time, so students are urged to place
their orders immediately.
A limited number of books is be
ing printed and only a few books
are left, according to Quentin Bir
chard, circulation manager.
