# Business School To Hear Magnate
## 
### 
Guest of honor and chief speaker
at the twelfth annual banquet of the
Graduate Business School to be held
tomorrow afternoon at 6:15 o'clock
in the Women's Gym will be Arthur
H. Young, former vice-president of
the U. S. Steel corporation.
Speaking also will be alumni rep
resentatives and. members of the
first and second business schools.
Professor E. W. Ilall as vocalist will
give several short musical selections,
accompanied by Miss Elizabeth
Moser on the violin.
Approximately 250 students, alum
ni. faculty members, and guests are
anticipated at the banquet.
