# Heart Attack Kills Former Farm Man
## 
### 
Jay E. Lawshe, graduate of Stan
ford in 1896, died Wednesday in
Portland, Oregon, from a coronary
occlusion. The heart attack came
while he was at his desk in the Port
land office of (he New York Life
Insurance Company, with whom he
had been since 1909.
Mr. Lawshe attended the Colum
bia Law School, where he received
his LL.B. degree in 1899.
