# Press Announces Two New Books
## 
### 
Two new books will be published
this week-end by the Stanford Uni
versity Press, according to an an
nouncement by Stanley M. Croon
quist, sales manager.
The first book, "Alaska Wild
I" lowers, by Ada White Sharpies,
was written to serve as a liana book
and guide in identifying wild flow
ers for travelers in Alaska. "Letters
From the Sandwich Islands," by
Mark Twain and edited by George
Ezra Dane, is the first trade edition
of the original 22 letters of travel,
description, anecdote, and human
interest, written for the Sacramento
Union describing his trip to the Ha
waiian Islands.
