CHRISTIAN SCIENCE
ORGANIZATION
Thursday Evenings, 7:30-8:150 p.m.:
Women's Clubhouse, top floor.

ducted testimonial meetings. Main
tenance of reading room (Old Union,
13) where C.S. literature may be
read or borrowed. Open to students
and faculty every week day, 9-12
o'clock noon. President, Patricia
Johnson.
ROGER WILLIAMS CLUB
Sunday evenings, 7:15-10 o'clock;
Baptist Church, Hamilton and Bry
ant Streets.
Activity Summary: Student con
ducted services, and guest speakers
at the church followed by fireside
fellowship at living groups and fac
ulty homes. This Sunday's speaker
at the church, Dr. Jesse Wil.son of
Los Angeles, well-known author and
missionary. He will speak on
"Choosing a Vocation." Home cjf Dr.
Helen B. Pryor, 659 Middletield
Road, Palo Alto.
Professors Gordon Lange and
Miles Heberer of the Division of
Speech and Drama will talk and lead
a discussion on drama at Stanford.
