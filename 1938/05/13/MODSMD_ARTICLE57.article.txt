# 'Man Mountain' Offered Contract
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, May 12 (UP). -
Man Mountain Dean, the behemoth
wrestler who is seeking a political
career as a candidate for the Georgia
Legislature, today was offered a
movie contract.
David Loew, independent produc
er, announced he has offered Dean a
role in Joe E. Brown's latest picture,
"The Gladiator," which went into
production yesterday. He would be
a wrestler whom the comedian meets
in a championship encounter.
