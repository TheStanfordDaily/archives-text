# Stanfordite Heads Columbia Group
## 
### 
Arthur A. Lundin, a graduate of
Stanford, who is now a first-year
student at Columbia University
School of Law. New York, was re
cently elected president of the Out
of-State Organization for the coming
year. The group includes students
from 32 states who are enrolled in
the law school.
Lundin is also a member of the
Murray Moot Court, and recently
represented that court in the first
year trials of moot court competi
tion. While at Stanford Lundin was
a varsity debater and a member of
Pi Sigma Alpha, honorary political
science fraternity.
