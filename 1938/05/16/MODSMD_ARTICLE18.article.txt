# Famous Dentist Dies at S. F.
## 
### 
Dr. Arthur R. McDowell, 48. dean
of the College of Physicians and
Surgeons, and prominent nalionally
in the field of dental education, died
early yesterday in Stanford Hospi
tal.
Death was due to high blood pies
sure for which Dr. McDowell had
been treated for more than a year.
He was past president of the Am
erican Association of Dental Schools.
Funeral services will be held to
morrow morning at 10 o'clock at
the chapel of Halsted & Co., 1123
Sutter street, followed by interment
in Woodlawn Cemetery.
