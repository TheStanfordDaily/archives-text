# All-Campus Tennis Entries Due Today
## 
### 
Entries' for the all-University ten
nis tournament can be made any
time up to 4 o'clock today, according
to Eddie Leßaker, varsity tennis
manager. First ranking group will
be seeded by Coach Sherm Lock
wood, while remaining entries will
be drawn, Leßaker added. Matches
will begin tomorrow.
