# Rally Interviews Scheduled Today
## 
### 
Conducted by Isabel Goldthwaite,
Don Dewar, and Mark Hopkins, in
terviews for applicants for next
year's Rally Committee will be held
today, tomorrow, and Wednesday
from 1 to 3 o'clock in Room 13, Old
Union.
Students who are now juniors are
eligible for men's and women's
chairmanships; sophomores, for
Group I, and freshmen, Group 11.
Final selection of new Rally Com
mittee members and the two chair
men will be made at Cardinals meet
ing Wednesday. Other business will
be discussion of the current Axe

reports on the Convalescent Home
