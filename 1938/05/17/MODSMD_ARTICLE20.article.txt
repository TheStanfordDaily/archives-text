# Hulme, Buck Set For O.S.C. Term
## 
### 
Edward M. Hulme, professor
emeritus of medieval history, and
Philip W. Buck, assistant professor
of political science will be in Port
land, Oregon, from June 20 to July
29. As visiting professors, they will
teach there at the Portland Oregon
State summer session, according to
Alfred Powers, dean.
Professor Hulme will give courses
on Europe and the Renaissance.
"European Dictators," an upper di
vision course given by Dr. Buck,
will deal with contemporary figures.
Dr. Buck will also teach classes in
American government.
Professor Hulme has taught in
Oregon before, having spent (he
summer of 1925 on the University of
Oregon campus at Eugene.
