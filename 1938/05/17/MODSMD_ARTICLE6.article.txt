# Church Club Plans Evolution Forum
## 
### 
Jean Ingalls, president of the New
man Club, announced that its quar
terly meeting will be held Wednes
day evening. May 18. at 7:30 o'clock
in the conference room of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
The topic for discussion will be "Is
Evolution Still a Theory?" After the
discussion officers for the coming
year will be chosen.
