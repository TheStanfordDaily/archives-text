# Hot Club Discusses Swing Musicians
## 
### 
First meeting of the Stanford Hot
Club took place last night at 7 o'clock
in the Nitery. Later the meeting
adjourned to the home of Tom Sie
bert where various musical styles
were discussed and played.
Highlight of the meeting was
several records of the old Chicago
Rhythm Kings. These records, now
unattainable, contain exampfes of
the early works of several of the |
present name swing musicians in- |
eluding Gene Krupa, Bud Freeman,
and Muggsy Spanier.
