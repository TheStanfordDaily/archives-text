# Landscape Painter Exhibits in Gallery
## 
### 
An exhibition of 25 paintings by
Orrin A. White is now on display
in one of the show rooms of the
Stanford Art Gallery.
Mr. White is a Southern Califor
nia artist with his studios in Pasa
dena and has recently spent some
time sketching in Old Mexico. The
pictures are all done in oil with land
scapes and street scenes predomi
nating. The exhibition will remain
hanging in the Gallery for the en
tire month of June.
