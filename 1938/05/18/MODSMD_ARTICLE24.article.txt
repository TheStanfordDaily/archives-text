# Dean Yost To Talk To 'Y' Cabinet
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will speak at a
Y.W.C.A. Cabinet meeting to be
held today at 4 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Following Miss
Yost's talk, the group heads and
sub-chairmen will present full re
ports of their activity plans for fall
quarter.
Immediately after the meeting a
dinner will be held at the home of
Mrs. Harry J. Rathbun, executive
secretary. Group heads include:
Wilma Baker, personal philosophy;
Gerry Clift, activities; and ueno
public affairs.
