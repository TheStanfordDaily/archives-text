# Bristol To Teach Journalism Course
## 
### 
Appointment of Horace Bristol,
Stanford alumnus and Pacific Coast
photographer for Life and Timo
magazines, as a lecturer in journal
ism for (he autumn quarter to give
a course in Pictorial Journalism,
was announced yesterday by Dr.
Chilton R. Bush, executive head of
the Division of Journalism.
The course will be limited to .15
students and will meet on Thurs
days from 1 to 5 o'clock.
