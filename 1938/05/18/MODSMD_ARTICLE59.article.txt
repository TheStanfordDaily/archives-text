# MUST HAVE CHILDREN
## 
### 
ROME, May 17 (UP) —Bachelors
and widowers without children to
day were prohibited by government
decree from holding municipal and
provincial positions such as mayor
and counsellor. Bachelors and wid
owers holding such posts will be re
quired to resign.
