# Mexican Socialist Funeral Planned
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, May 17 (UP) —
Students planned a ceremonial fun
eral today for Momero Gonzalez, 18,
; member of the Socialist youth or
ganization killed in a riot yesterday.
Gonzalez died of wounds last
night. He and other Socialist stu
dents took possession of the pre
paratory school and the law school
yesterday in a clash with other stu
j dents. Police ousted the Socialist
students.
