# NINE COLTS NAMED
## 
### 
TANFORAN TRACK, San Bruno,
May 19 (UP). —Nine ranking colts
and fillies were named today for the
$5000 added Tanforan three-year
old championship to be run Satur
day at a mile and one-sixteenth.

Reptiles ruled the earth for about
5,000,000 years.
