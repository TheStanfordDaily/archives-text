# Union To Honor Bassett at Tea
## 
### 
The Union Club will honor Lee
Emerson Bassett, retiring professor,
at its annual garden tea to be held
this Sunday in the Union court from
4 to 5:30 o'clock.
Over 500 guests have been in
vited, and music will be supplied by
the Hawaiian band which played for
the Strawberry Brunch. In the re
ceiving line will be Professor and
Mrs. Bassett, Miss Louise Price, Un
ion director, and Virginia Cromwell,
Union Club president.
Elizabeth Meech is in charge of
the affair. Assisting her arc Mary
Beaton, Dale Case, Mary Ellen
Weer, and Ann Hughes.
