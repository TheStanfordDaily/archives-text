# Tickets Limited For Toyon Dance
## 
### 
A very few of the limited num
ber of tickets now on sale for the
annual Toyon semi-formal dance
are still available, according to Vin
cent Lemmon. chairman.
Bids to the dance, to be held Fri
day night in Toyon lobby from 9 to
1 o'clock, are $1.50. and may be se
cured at the A.S.S.U. office or from
committee members Jim Barnum,
Dan Cameron, Bill Rosenfeld, Bob
Curtis, and Mike Gudman.
Music will be provided by Noel
Thomas' 11-piece orchestra. Pa
trons and patronesses for the dance
are Mr. and Mrs. Raymond Kendall.
Mr. and Mrs. Max Savelle, and Mr.
and Mrs. P. J. Treat.
