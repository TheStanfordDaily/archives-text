# KOVACS WINS DOUBLES
## 
### 
SAN RAFAEL, Cal., May 23 (UP)
Frank Kovacs, rising young Oakland
tennis player, added another title to
his collection today after he paired
with Norman Brooks to win the
doubles championship in the Marin
County tournament on the Mill Val
ley courts. Kovacs and Brooks de
feated Ben Dey and Bob Pommer,
6-3, 6-8, 6-4 in the final match.
