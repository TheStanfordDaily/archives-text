# 24 HITS EQUAL 20 RUNS
## 
### 
WENATCHEE. Wash., May 24.—
The Wenatchee Chiefs went on a
hitting spree last night and collected
24 hits in smothering the Spokane
HaWks, 20-4, in a Western Inter
national League baseball game.

MILAN, Italy, May 24.—Italy took
a 2-1 lead over Poland in second
round Davis Cup competition when
its doubles team won.
