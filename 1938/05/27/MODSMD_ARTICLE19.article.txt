# Quads Ready At Woodpecker Lodge
## 
### 
Quads will be distributed from
1:15 to 5 o'clock this afternoon and
during the early part of next week,
it was announced at the Quad office
yesterday.
Early orders for copies with naipes
are ready through "W." and there
are also several hundred general
copies.
