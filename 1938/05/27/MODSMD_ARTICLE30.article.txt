# Summer Courses In Math Listed
## 
### 
Professor H. F. Minssen, vice
president of San Jose State College,
will conduct a course called "A
Popular Survey of Mathematics"
during the coming summer quar
ter. This course is intended for
students who have a non-technical
interest in mathematics, and will
have no prerequisites.
The Department of mathematics
will also offer a course in statistics
intended primarily for students in
biology and economics. Dr. O. G.
Harrold will teach this course.
