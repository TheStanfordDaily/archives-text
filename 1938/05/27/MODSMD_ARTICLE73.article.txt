# Choirs To Offer Liturgical Music
## 
### 
A festival of liturgical music will
be presented in Memorial Church,
Sunday evening, June 5, and Tues
day evening, June 7, by the com
bined University choirs, The Morris
Club, and A Cappella Choirs from
San Jose and Palo Alto high
schools. The program starts at 8
o'clock, and is open to the pdblie.
Widor's "Mass for Two Choirs"
will be sung, accompanied by strings
and brass, and the Brass Choir from
San Jose State College will play.
Their offering of Baroque music is
intended to conform with the mosaic
art of the chapel. They will also
support the final number, Widor's
"Organ Toccata in F."
