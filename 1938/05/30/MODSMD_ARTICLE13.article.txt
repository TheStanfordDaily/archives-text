# Bob Townsend Dies Of Crash Injuries
## 
### 
Bob Townsend, president of the
Stanford Flying Club, died in Palo
Alto hospital shortly before mid
night last night of injuries received
in an automobile accident Saturday
morning.
His automobile overturned on
Middlefield Road east of Palo Alto.
He was reported Saturday to have
suffered a fractured jaw and shoul
der injuries.
