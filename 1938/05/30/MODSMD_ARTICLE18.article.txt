# Hall Deposits For Frosh Due Today
## 
### 
All freshmen whose names are
high enough on the Toyon and
Branner waiting lists to be accom
modated, must pay $5 deposits at
the Dean of Men's office today, to
morrow, or Wednesday.
Men wishing to live in Toyon Hall
during the summer quarter may
make reservations with Miss Vivian
Church in the Dean of Men's office
after Wednesday.
