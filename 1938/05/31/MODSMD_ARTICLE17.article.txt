# POETICAL AWARDS
## 
### 
The Clarence Urmy poetry con
test with an award of $40 was won
by Ann Stanford, senior class poet,
with her poem "On the Sea," the
English department announced yes
terday. The poems, "Mary's Room,"
written by Joy Grubbs, and "Pray
er," by Elva Ekwurtzel, were award
ed honorable mention.
