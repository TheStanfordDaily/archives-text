# Seniors' Grades To Be Ready June 17
## 
### 
Graduating seniors and candidates
for advanced degrees this quarter
may call for their grades at the
Registrar's office at 10:30 o'clock on
Friday morning, June 17, after a
meeting of the Academic Council, ac
cording to an announcement yester
day.
All other students will receive
their grades by leaving a stamped,
self-addressed envelope. Envelopes
can be purchased at the Registrar's
office for five cents each. They are
sold for the benefit of the Stanford
Convalescent Home for children.
Grades will be mailed out about
June 22.
LOS ANGELES, May 30 (UP). —
Sacramento's Western Pacific soccer
football team defeated the Los An
geles Magyars, 2-1, and entered the
finals of the California champion
ship.
