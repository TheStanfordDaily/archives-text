# Orchesis To Meet With S.J. State
## 
### 
The Orthosis groups of Stanford
and San Jose State will meet on the
San Jose campus tomorrow evening.
This is the second joint dance meet
ing to be held by the societies. The
first was held last quarter on the
Stanford campus.
1 ryouts for Orchesis, which is the
campus honorary dance society, will
be held in the dance studio of the
Women's Gym today at 5 o'clock.
