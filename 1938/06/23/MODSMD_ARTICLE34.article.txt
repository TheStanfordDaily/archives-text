# Monteith To Talk On American Alps
## 
### 
First program in the Tuesday Eve
ning Series for the summer quarter
will be an illustrated lecture on
"Alps in America" by John Claire
Monteith Tuesday, June 28, at 8
o'clock in the auditorium, Memorial
Hall.
Illustrating in color his story of
the Sierra Nevadas and the Rockies,
Monteith takes the audience on a
tour of the glaciers and mountain
trails in both summer and winter
seasons. Mr. Monteith will be re
membered for his illustrated lecture
given last summer when he showed
his colored slides of "Sun and Si
lence in Death Valley."
