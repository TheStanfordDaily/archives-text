# Thursday Recital Replaced by Talk
## 
### 
Alfred V. Frankenstein, music
editor of the San Francisco Chronicle
and member of the Stanford music
faculty, will lecture on "The Cham
ber Music of Bach," at 4:15 o'clock
on Thursday in Room 84. The talk
will take the place of the usual organ
recital by Warren D. Allen, Univer
sity organist.
Frankenstein's lecture, the first of
a series which will parallel the con
certs of the Roth Quartet, will re
place the regular Thursday after
noon organ recital. The rest of the
lectures of the series will be heard
Mondays at 4:15 o'clock in the same
room.
