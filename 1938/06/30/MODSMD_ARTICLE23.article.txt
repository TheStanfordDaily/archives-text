# Trueblood Returns To Chapel Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, Chaplain
! of the University, will conduct divine
worship and deliver the sermon at
11 o'clock next Sunday morning in
the Memorial Church.
The music for the service will in
clude: organ prelude by Cabezon;
anthem—Jesu dulcis memori by Vit
toria; and choral response—Ave Ma
ria by Adrian Willaert. Warren D.
Allen, University organist, will give
an organ recital at 7:30 o'clock Sun
day evening.
