# Education Group To Hear Kefauver
## 
### 
More than 150 season tickets for
the series of Education Club lunch
eons which opens today have already
been sold. Some places are still
available and a few tickets will be
sold at the door, according to Lois
Baugher, secretary-treasurer of the
club.
"Problems of the Graduate Stu
dent" will be discussed by Dean
Grayson N. Kefauver this noon in
the main dining room of the Stan
ford Union. Miss Marion Flagg of
the music department will lead com
munity singing.
"The luncheons will be from
12 to 1 o'clock and will be over in
time for 1 o'clock classes," says
Miss Baugher.
