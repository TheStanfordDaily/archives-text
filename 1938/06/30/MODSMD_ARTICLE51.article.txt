## 
###

follow the informal reception to be
held Friday, July 1, at 8 p.m. by
Dean and Mrs. Grayson N. Kefauver
at their home, 457 Kingsley.

and their wives and husbands are in
vited to attend. Those planning to
attend are asked to leave their names
in the Education office.
