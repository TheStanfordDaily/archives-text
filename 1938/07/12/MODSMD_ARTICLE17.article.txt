# AUTOGRAPHS GIVEN
## 
### 
Lewis Mumford, lecturer and so
ciologist, autographed a number of
copies of his "The Culture of Cities"
at the Bookstore last week when he
was here for the conference. His
lecture last Saturday afternoon was
based on some of the material in his
book, which deals with the future of
the modern urban community.
Lancaster was the capital of
Pennsylvania from 1799 to 1812.
