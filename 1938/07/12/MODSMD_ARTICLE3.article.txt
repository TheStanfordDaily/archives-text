# Quartet To Play Bach Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Roth Quartet will play
Bach's "Art of Fugue," Tan
man's "Triptych," and Brahms'
"Quartet in C Minor" at their
concert in Memorial Hall to
morrow at 4:15 o'clock.
Enrolled students and faculty
may call for their free tickets
at Room 173, Administration.
Others interested may purchase
tickets at the vestry of the Me
morial Church.
