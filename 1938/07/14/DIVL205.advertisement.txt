Thursday, July 14
4:30 p.m. Lecture. "La Femme en France et en Bclgique dans le passe

8:00 p.m. Dramatic Reading. The Octoroon, by Dion Boucicault.

11:00 a.m. Church Service. Preacher: Edgar E. Robinson, Margaret
Byrne Professor of American History. Subject: "The Spirit
ual Basis of the American Dream."
5:00 p.m. Vesper Service. Laurence Frost Amphitheater.
Monday, July 18
8:00 p.m. Roth Quartet. Assisted by Carleton Sprague Smith, flutist;
Margaret Christy and Norma Letroadec, 'cellists. Second con

mer Quarter Office, Room 173, Administration Building.
Single admissions, $1.25, may be obtained at the Vestry, Me
morial Church, at Keeble's, Palo Alto, and at the door.
9:15 p.m. Stanford University Hour (KGO). Speaker: Virgil A.
Anderson, Assistant Professor of Speech and Drama. Subject:
"The Problem of Stuttering."
Tuesday, July 19
8:00 p.m. Tuesday Evening Series. Lecture, "Five Years of Hitler,"
James Kerr Pollock, Acting Professor of Political Science.
Auditorium, Memorial Hall. The public is invited.
Wednesday, July 20
4:15 p.m. Roth Quartet. Fourth concert in Series B. Presented under
