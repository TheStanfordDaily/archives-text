# Educators Invited To Kaffee Klatsch
## 
### 
All women in education have been
invited to a Kaffee Klatsch, spon
sored by Pi Lambda Theta on Fri
day evening from 10 to 11 o'clock in
the lounge of the Women's Club
house. Miss Ruth Henderson will
talk about the Virginia program.
Barbara Bruch, new social chair
man, is in charge of the arrange
ments. Other summer officers in
clude Patricia Hamilton, president;
Marian Cavassa, vice-president;
Ruth Alexander, secretary; Eliza
beth Gurinsky, keeper of records;
Margaret Brennan, program chair
man; and Lelia Taggart, member
ship chairman.
