# Belgian Lecturer Will Speak Today
## 
### 
"La Femme en France et en Bel
gique dans le passe et le present,"
the second in the series of open
lectures by Mme. Susanne Charlier-
Tassier, will be given this af
ternoon at 4:30 o'clock in the French
House, 549 Lasuen.
The subject will include the prog
ress of the French woman from her
legal and political inferiority during
the "Ancien Regime," through the
Napoleonic era, and up to her rapid
transformation since the World War.
