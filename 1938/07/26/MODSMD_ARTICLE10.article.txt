# Stagers Read Barry
## 
### 
"The Animal Kingdom," by
Phillip Barry, will be read by the
Stagers, student honorary dra
matic society, Friday night, in the
Little Theater.
The cast will include Catherine
Elwood, former Stanford star who
has lately been occupied with
radio work; Mack Gordon, Stanford
song-writer; Paul Crary, past

president of Stagers; and Dorothy
Le-Baker, a newcomer from Pasa
dena. Others in the cast are Jack
Gillis and Janet Dole, stars of
"Camille in Roaring Camp" and a
future performance, "Gringo's
Gamble"; Dorothy Tully, Joe
Whitaker, George Wilson, and Mary
Leles.
Admission will be 15 cents.
