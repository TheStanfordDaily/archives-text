# Maxwell Anderson
## 
### 
"Poetic Drama When Maxwell
Anderson Was Born" is the title
of the lecture to be given by Dr.
Henry David Gray in the Lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse at 8
o'clock Thursday evening.
Dr. Gray, who directed Maxwell
Anderson's studies during the
years the playwright spent here as
a graduate student, is known to
many Stanford students as an in
terpreter of modern drama in Eu
rope and America.
The lecture is the fourth prograr:
in the Dramatists' Series developed
in support of the Maxwell Ander
son Verse Drama Contest.
