# PERSONS
## Jackson Chosen 
### 
Dean J. Hugh Jackson of Stan
ford was elected Tuesday to take
the place of Dr. Charles D. Marx,
who recently resigned, on the di
rectorate of the Palo Alto Mutual
Building and Loan Association.
Directors include Professor D. B.
Clark, Birge M. Clark, T. C.
Christy, E. C. Thoits, Professor
Theodore J. Hoover, Lorena Cos
tello, Dean J. Hugh Jackson, How
ard P. Stevens, and S. M. Vander
voort.
