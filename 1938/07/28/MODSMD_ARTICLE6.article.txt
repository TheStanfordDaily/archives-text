# French Lecture
## 
### 
"Deux grandes ports: Bruges et
Anvers," will be the subject of
the lecture by Madame Suzanne
Charlier-Tassier this afternoon at
4:30 o'clock at the French House,
549 Lasuen.
Bruges and Antwerp were in the
14th and 18th centuries the great
centers of European trade and
commerce. Madame Charlier-Tas
sier will discuss the economic, in
tellectual, and artistic activity of
these two great ports during their
periods of grandeur and will ex
plain the causes for their deca
dence. Students and members of
the faculty are invited to attend.
