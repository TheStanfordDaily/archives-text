# Iowa Coach Leads Grid Inventors
## 
### 
Irl Tubbs, coach of the University
of lowa football team which meets
U.C.L.A. in the Los Angeles Coli
seum on Friday night, Sept. 23, is
the leading inventor of gridiron
equipment.
He holds patents on the valve
ball, now in general use throughout
the country; elastic inserts in foot
ball pants; special cleats and a seam
less ball just placed on the market.
