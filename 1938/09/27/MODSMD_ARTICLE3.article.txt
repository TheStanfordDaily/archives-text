# Santa Clara Game Tickets on Sale
## 
### 
Grid tickets to the first Stan
ford game Saturday, October 1,
with Santa Clara here arc now
on sale at the Board of Athletic
Control office. Prices are 51.50
for the reserved seats and $1.10
for the end sections.
Students will be admitted
upon presentation of student
body cards and the same will
apply to the Washington State
and Oregon battles which fol
low the Santa Clara affair here
on successive Saturdays.
