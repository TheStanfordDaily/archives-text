# 'Y' To Entertain Entering Women
## 
### 
Stanford's Y.W.C.A, will welcome
all new women with a tea today
from 3 to 5 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Freshman and transfer women
will have an opportunity to become
acquainted with the "Y" here in a
series of personal interviews with
members of the Stanford group.
These will be held from September
27 to October 12.
On Wednesday, October 12, will
be the recognition service in the
Chapel, followed by the traditional
membership dinner in the Union,
for all new and old members.
