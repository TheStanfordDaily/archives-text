# Special Reading Course Offered
## 
### 
To help students improve their
skill in silent reading and methods
of study, a new course, Psychology
R, wiil be offered this quarter. The
class will be held in Room 122 in
the Graduate School of Business.
Section I will meet on Mondays
and Wednesdays from 7:30 to 9:30
p.m. Section II will meet on Tues
days and Thursdays at the same
hours throughout the quarter. First
class meetings will be on Monday,
October 3, and Tuesday, October 4.
