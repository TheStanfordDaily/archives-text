# Transfer Jolly-up To Be Tomorrow
## 
### 
Ozzie Secrest and his orchestra
will play for the junior transfer
jolly-up to be held in the Women's
Gym from 7 to 8 o'clock tomorrow
evening. The dance is for junior
transfer women and all campus
men.
The orchestra is the only member
of the Associated College Orchestras
from Coast to Coast in this territory
and was picked to represent Stan
ford, San Mateo, and San Jose State
territory.
