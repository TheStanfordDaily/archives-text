# Dress Suit Found Near Science Lab
## 
### 
Farm physicists have always con
sidered Ryan Lab a place to find
important scientific data—but some
one found a full dress suit out near
there yesterday. With two vests, one
black, one white.
Someone can find it again if he
goes to the lost and found.
