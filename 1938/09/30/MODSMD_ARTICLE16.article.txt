# Axe Society Heads To Be Elected
## 
### 
To fill the top Raps left by the
failure of Wayland Leonard, presi
dent of Axe Society, and Don Towne,
vice-president, to return to school,
initial meeting of the honorary
group will he held Tuesday night,
10 o'clock, in the Beta house, to
elect new officers.
Acting president in charge of the
meeting will be Crowell Phillips,
who urges all members to be pres
ent at that time.
