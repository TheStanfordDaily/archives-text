# Klahoyas Will Go To Game Together
## 
### 
Klahoya Club members and all
new off-campus women are urged
to attend the Santa Clara football
game as a group, in accordance with
the club plan to /nake it easier for
new women to become acquainted
with the campus. Members will
meet in the Women's Clubhouse to
morrow afternoon at 1 o'clock, and
are reminded to bring registration
cards with them.
New Lower Division students
may meet in the Women's Club
house at 7:30 o'clock tomorrow
night and go to President Wilbur's
reception together.
