# King, Trask Win Hawaiian Primary
## 
### 
HONOLULU, T.H., Oct. 2 (UP).
Samuel Wilder King, territorial
delegate representing the Hawaiian
Islands in Congress, was renominat
ed by his party, the Republican, in
yesterday's primary, late returns in
dicated tonight.
David Trask was named by the
Democrats to oppose King. William
Crozier, an independent, apparently
failed to obtain the 20 per cent vote
necessary for a place on the ballot.
