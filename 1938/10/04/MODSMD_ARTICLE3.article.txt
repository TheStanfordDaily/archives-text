# Rooters' Tickets On Sale at B.A.C.
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the
Southern California and Cali
fornia games are on sale today
and tomorrow only, in the
B.A.C. building. The windows
will remain open from 8 to 5
o'clock each day.
For the Southern California
game, students may purchase
one rooters' ticket at $1.10 and
six getieral admisison tickets at
$1.10 or $2.75. For the Cali
fornia game purchase is limited
to four general admissions at
$4.40 and onr rooters' ticket at
$1.65. No more than two A. S.
S. U. cards may be used by
each student.
