# CANINE CUSTODY SPLIT
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 3 (UP).—
Mildred and William Hurd wanted a
divorce but couldn't agree what was
to be done about their dog. The
court settled the issue today by
awarding the animal to Mrs. Hurd
on week-days and to Hurd on Sun
day, when he will be permitted to
take it for a walk.
