# Political Union To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
First meeting of the year for the
Political Union will be held tonight
at 7 o'clock in the English Corner
rooms. The three party leaders will
be on the English Corner to direct
their followers to the rooms in
which their caucuses will be held.
All members of the Political Un
ion have been asked to attend the
meeting whether they have been
notified or not, as much important
business will be discussed. The first
question of the year will be an
nounced at the meeting, according to
Vincent Lemmon, president, and
party officers will be elected.
"An ambitious program has been
planned for this year," says Lem
mon, who has been working with
Party Leaders Howard Hayes, con
servative; Carl Dtiscnroth, liberal;
and Bob Wood, labor.
