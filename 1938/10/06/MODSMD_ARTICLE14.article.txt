# Theta Sigs Hold Initial Meeting
## 
### 
First regular meeting of Theta
Sigma Phi, women's honorary jour
nalism society, for the quarter was
held yesterday afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Report by Treasurer Eunice Em
cry showed a net intake of $548 on
Registration Dance, which the so
ciety sponsored. A committee for
drawing up a new chapter constitu
tion was appointed by President
Phyllis White.
Jean Halliburton, secretary, gave
a report on the society's national
convention, held in Los Angeles dur
ing the summer.
