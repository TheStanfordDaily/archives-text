# WHY STANFORD?
## 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
Gosh, editor, what are you trying
to do, blacken the fair name of Stan

ford? If you are so naive as to think
that Stanford people are the only
minors who drink, anyone can tell
you that liquor is served to minors
everywhere. Stanford Ls no excep
tion, and you certainly have no busi
ness to get us into trouble.
I congratulate you for your strong
crusading spirit. But please, can't
you turn it into safer channels? You
have started a big forest fire, and
you are going to have a tough time
to control it.
A. B. S.
