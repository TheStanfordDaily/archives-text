# Discussion Group To Meet Sunday
## 
### 
Beginning next Sunday, October
9, a discussion class open to all stu
dents without registration will be
held each Sunday morning from
9:30 to 10:30 in the vestry of the
Memorial Church. The class will be
devoted to a consideration of con
temporary human problems.
The first subject to be considered
will be "Freedom in the Modern
World." The discussion groups will
be conducted by Dr. D. Elton True
blood and a group of helpers, in
cluding Miss Caroline Brown,
formerly of Bryn Mawr and Rad
cliffe.
