# Symphonic Suite Featured in Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, presented the weekly organ
recital yesterday afternoon in the
Memorial Church. The programs are
held every Sunday afternoon and
are open to students and visitors.
Among his selections were "Piece
Heroique" by Cesar Franck; "Recit
de Tierce en Taille" by Grigny;
"Prelude in D major" by Bach;
and "Tabor" and "Blanik" from the
Symphonic Suite, "My Country" by
Smetana.
