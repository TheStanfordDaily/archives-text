# Schedule Change Deadline Named
## 
### 
Tuesday, October 11, at 5 o'clock
is the last day students may change
their study lists for this quarter
without fine. After October 11, all
program revisions must be made by
petition to the Registration Commit
tee subject to the following fines:
$2 for each course added or dropped
or $3 where one course is substi
tuted for another.
All program changes made since
registration should be filed at the
Registrar's Office on the regular re
vised study-list cards. Students are
held responsible for courses and
units as they appear on their official
study lists filed at the time of reg
istration, and no credit is given for
courses which do not appear thereon.
This applies to Physical Education
as well as academic subjects.
