# U. S. SAILOR SUICIDES
## 
### 
HONOLULU, T.H., Oct. 9 (UP).—
The body of Frederick George Stan
ton, 19, a seaman second class at
tached to (he U.S.S. Beaver, was
found hanging over the side of the
vessel today in what was regarded
«is a suicide. There were no notes
to explain the action, and a naval
board of investigation began an in
quiry to determine the motive.
