# Victoria Tug Frees Sand-hound Vessel
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Oct. 9 (UP). - The
Harbor Radio reported tonight the
S. S. North Coast of the North
land Transportation Company was
en route back to Seattle after being
pulled free from sand near Cape
Mudhe, off Vancouver Island at the
southern entrance to Discovery
Passage.
The North Coast, carrying 3fi pas
sengers and a cargo of freight, went
aground yesterday. The tug Salvage
King was dispatched to her aid from
Victoria, 8.C., and refloated the
vessel early this morning. Harbor
Radio reports said. She was due at
'Seattle tomorrow morning.
