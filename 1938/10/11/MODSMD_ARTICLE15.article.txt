# Students to Discuss Japan
## 
### 

FIVE STANFORDITES who visited Japan this summer while attend
ing the American-Japan Conference will tell of their experiences on
the Tuesday Evening Lecture Series tonight. From left to right, they
are: Perry Chisum, Buddy Iwata, Mary Morris Boggs, who will dis
cuss "Japanese Students," Jack Laney and Philip Woodyatt, who
will give details of the confcrence.
