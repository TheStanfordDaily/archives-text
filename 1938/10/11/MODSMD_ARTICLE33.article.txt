MEETING OF STANFORD DAMES
SEWING GROUP at home of Mrs.
John Dodds, 450 El Escarpado,
Tuesday, 2:30 o'clock.
DAILY EDITORIAL STAFF TRY
OUTEE MEETING at 4 o'clock in
Room 8 of the Press Building.
STANDING COMMITTEE OF THE
POLITICAL UNION will meet in
the Political Science Building at
4:30 this afternoon.
MEETING OF THE RALLY COM
MITTEE in the Women's Club
house at 7:15.

of skit readers will be held in
Memorial Hall from 2 to 5 o'clock.

A.S.U. will meet in Women's
Clubhouse at 3 o'clock.
YOUNG DEMOCRATS WILL MEET
TONIGHT at the Women's Club
house at 7:15 o'clock.
AXE SOCIETY MEETS TONIGHT
in the Alpha Delta Phi house at
10 o'clock to elect officers and
senior member.
ORCHESIS MEETING TONIGHT
at 7:30 in the Women's Gymna
sium.
INTERNATIONAL COMMITTEE
MEETS at 7:15 in the Nitery.
