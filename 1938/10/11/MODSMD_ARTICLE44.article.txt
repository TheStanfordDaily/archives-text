# Frosh Tennis Star Holds Junior Title
## 
### 
Larry Dee, ace Stanford freshman
tennis player, retained his Junior
Doubles title of the Pacific Coast on
Sunday afternoon with Chris Lar
sen of Marin Junior College as his
partner. They defeated Harold
Wagner and Howard Morehouse in
five gruelling sets played at Berke
ley Tennis Club.
Dee also reached the finals of the
junior singles by defeating Jack
Curley of Sacramento in the quar
ter-finals 4-6, 6-1, 6-2 and Frank
Mehner, intermountain champion
from Salt Lake City, 6-1, 6-3. Dee
meets Harold Wagner for the title
on Tuesday.
