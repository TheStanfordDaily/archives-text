# Rally Heads Call Final Tryouts
## 
### 
Rally Committee tfyouts will be
held for the last time today from 4
to 6 o'clock in Room 13 of the
Union. All students trying out for
positions have been asked to turn in
their card stunt suggestions today
also.
At the regular rally committee
meeting last night, plans were dis
cussed for the U.S.C. game rally and
pajamarino, which is tentatively
scheduled for Thursday, October 20.
New card stunts, some of which have
been suggested by tryoutces for
Rally Committee, were also dis
cussed for use at the S.C. game.
Both men and women will be re
quired to wear white shirts to the
games the rest of the year if they
want to sit in the rooters' sections.
Men must also wear their rooters'
caps, and pompons will be necessary
for all women. Didi Vibert, wom
en's chairman, has announced that
pompons will be sold at the A.S.S.U.
office for the last time this week —
tomorrow and Friday from 1 to 3
o'clock in the afternoon and Satur
day morning from 9 to 12 o'clock.
