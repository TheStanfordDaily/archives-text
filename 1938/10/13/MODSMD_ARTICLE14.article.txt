# Council Will Hear Traffic Violations
## 
### 
Women's Council will hold its
regular meeting today at 4 o'clock in
the Ex Committee office in the Old
Union, according to Chairman
Gretchen Ahlswede.
Traffic cases will be heard at 4
o'clock with late leave violations
scheduled to be heard before and
after the traffic cases. Office hours
today will be from 3:30 until 4
o'clock.
