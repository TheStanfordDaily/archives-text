# Stagers to Hold Tryouts Today
## 
### 
Stagers will hold tryouts today
and tomorrow from 4 to G o'clock
in the Little Theater for reading of
"Tower of Nesle" by Dumas, to be
presented toward the end of next
week, according to Gordy Williams.
There arc parts available for wom
en. One for a beautiful, vicious
queen, another for her attendant,
and the third for someone who can
read stage directions in such a way
as to get. the atmosphere over to the
audience.
There arc 10 male parts open, of
fering a good chance for men with
110 experience to take a fling at
campus dramatics. Two of the male
parts are romantic leads. Participa
tion in the reading will not inter
fere with the Gaieties.
