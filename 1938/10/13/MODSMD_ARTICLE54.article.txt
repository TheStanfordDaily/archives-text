# HOPES FOR PEACE
## 
### 
BARCELONA, Oct. 12 (UP).—
Withdrawal of all foreign troops
from Spanish soil would enable
Spain to .settle it.s civil war. Foreign
Minister Julio Alvarez del Vayo said
today in a Columbus Day speech to
the Spanish - American diplomatic
corps.
Prize money to be awarded ex
hibitors in the livestock pavilion at
Treasure Island totals $300,000.
