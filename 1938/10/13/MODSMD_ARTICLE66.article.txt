# Cardinals Bedimed
## 
### 
CARDINALS will give Stanford a dance after
the Oregon game, the S.C. game, the
Dartmouth game if the campus wants it. Stan
ford spirit will also be properly whipped up
for the Big Game.
But it took Ex Committee's poke Tuesday
night to get the Oregon dance, and Cardinals
is mad. Which is tough, because maybe none
of the dances will be given for one big, happy
family.
If they aren't, then Ex Committee can give
Cardinals another poke, and the latter can dis
cuss the situation with a lot more talk. What
Stanford hopes will happen, however, is to keep
the dance program intact.
With Cardinals off the dime and Ex Com
satisfied.—H. L. S.
