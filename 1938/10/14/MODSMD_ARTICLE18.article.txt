# Chaplain to Speak At Stanford Club
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood will be
the speaker at the Stanford Club
this Sunday evening, following sup
per, which will be served in the Par
ish House of All Saints Church,
Hamilton Ave., at 6 o'clock.
Forty attended last Sunday eve
ning, and participated in the dis
cussion following Commander Bry
ant's talk.
President Dave Nicodemus states
that he hopes that many more will
make the club their Sunday evening
home.
