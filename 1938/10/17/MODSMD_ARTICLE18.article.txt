# Staff Appoints New Copyreaders
## 
### 
Twelve outstanding tryoutees were
promoted to the position of copy
reader on the editorial staff of The
Daily, at the end of the tryoutee
period last week.
Of those promoted, seven were
junior transfers who had already
had experience on metropolitan or
college papers, four were freshmen,
and one was a senior. Only one
woman, Felker Morris, was ad
vanced.
Others receiving the appointment
were Bud Ames, Charles Bull, Jack
Burtt, Don Gomez, Frank Guthrie,
LouLs Lengfeld, Jim Loudon, Charles
Mitchell, Bill Richey, Vic Rowland,
and Herman Silverman.
