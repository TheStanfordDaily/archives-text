# Wilbur Receives Forum Invitation
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur has
been invited to attend the 1938 For
um on Current Problems, to be held
under auspices of the New York
Herald-Tribune October 25 and 26
at the Waldorf-Astoria in New York
City and October 27 at the New York
World's Fair, it was announced yes
terday.
It was also suggested by the com
mittees in charge that two students
or alumni be selected to attend the
conference, but no action concerning
this has been taken as yet.
A group of more than 50 promi
nent speakers will address sessions
of the conference, including Presi
dent Franklin D. Roosevelt and
Mrs. Roosevelt, Dr. Robert M.
Hutchins, president of the Univer
sity of Chicago, William Gxeen,
A. F. of L. president, John L. Lewis,
chairman of the C.1.0., and others.
