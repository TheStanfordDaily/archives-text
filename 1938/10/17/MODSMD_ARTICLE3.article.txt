# Youth Escapes From Bad Crash
## 
### 
Skidding a distance of 250 feet in
an overturned car, Roy Oscarson,
17, of Palo Alto, escaped without
injury in a collision between the
automobile he was driving and one
driven by Mrs. Alice C. Allen, su
perintendent of the Men's Rest Home,
last Saturday night about 6 o'clock.
The accident occurred at Embar
cadero Road and El Camino Real
just after dark. The two cars
crashed together at the center of the
intersection, sending Oscarson's out
of control. Neither driver was
blamed for the accident.
The car which Oscarson was driv
ing is owned by Harry Millet, Alta
Mesa.
