# 'Mayor' New Title Of Glenda Ferrel
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Oct. 16 (UP). —
Glenda Farrell, blonde film actress
now vacationing in New York, will
return to find herself owner of a
new title—Mayor Glenda Farrell of
North Hollywood.
The actress won the office in a
special write-in election with 2,550
votes. She was her own runner-up
—1680 ballots being cast for Torchy
Blane, the character Miss Farrell
portrays on the screen. Bing Cros
by was third with 902 votes and
Lewis Stone fourth with 738. All
are residents of North Hollywood.
