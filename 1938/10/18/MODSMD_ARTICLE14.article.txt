# Encina Refunds Given Out Today
## 
### 
Payment will be made today of
refunds due to students boarding at
Encina Hall during 1937-38. All men
entitled to this return must appear
in person at the Encina office be
tween 1 and 5:30 o'clock this after
noon.
A rebate of $15 will be given to
those students who paid the $85 rate
for the three quarters. On the $70
meal books, a refund of $12.30 will
be made. Receipts for the payments
will not be necessary.
Members of El Capitan, Los Arcos,
El Tigre, and El Cuadro will be giv
en their refunds through their club
managers.
