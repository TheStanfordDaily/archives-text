# Tuesday Evening Talk Cancelled
## 
### 
The third program in the
Tuesday Evening Series, sched
uled for tonight in Memorial
Hall auditorium, has been can
celled, according to word re
ceived late yesterday from the
Committee on Public Exercises.
The sudden illness of Miss
Margaret Harrison, well-known
author, traveler and foreign
correspondent, who was to
speak, caused the cancellation
of the film.
Miss Harrison, co-director
and producer of the motion pic
ture, "Grass." which was to
have been presented as the
feature of the program, will
probably speak at a program of
the Series later in the year, it
was announced by the com
mittee.
