# Accessories Prove Golfers' Delight
## 
### 
one fashion-wise designer from
one of the better fashion magazines
has perfected the perfect golf ac
cessories.
Jersey visors of all colors are
worn to protect skin, hair, and eyes.
Pigskin gloves with string backs
make the game cool and blisterless
for the feminine golfer. The final
and crowning touch to the designing
of these accessories was the creation
of an oblong leather bag for tees,
lipstick, compact, etc., which hangs
from the shoulder of the golfer by
a long leather strap.
