# Picketing Measure Will Be Discussed
## 
### 
The anti-picketing initiative will
be discussed at tonight's meeting of
the Stanford and Palo Alto Young
Democrats Club, at 8 p.m. in the
Community Center.

torney. will speak for the proposi
tion, while C. E. Todd will advocate

Roberts of Stanford will interpret
the proposition in its relation to
California. Anyone interested in
joining the Stanford Club is asked
to see R. F. Peckham at Palo Alto
8480.
