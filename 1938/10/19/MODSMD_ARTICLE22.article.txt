# Roble Jolly-up Tonight in Gym
## 
### 
All campus men are invited to the
jolly-up to be held in the Women's
Gym by Roble women tonight from
seven to eight o'clock.
Lee Smith's orchestra will play
while campus men dance Avith the
crop of '42. Barbara Gilbert is in
charge of the jolly-up and will be
helped by a group of freshman host
esses.
Serving as patronesses will be Mrs.
Leona Diekema, Miss Frances Tor
rel, and Miss Louise Powley.
