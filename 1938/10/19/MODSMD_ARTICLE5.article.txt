# Cal Man to Talk At A.S.U. Meet
## 
### 
Stanford's chapter of the American
Student Union will hold its first
meeting of the year in the Women's
Clubhouse Thursday evening at 7:30
o'clock, it was announced yesterday
by President Gene Glasmann.
Bernic Firestone, president of the
large University of California chap
ter, will be guest speaker. Plans for
the year will also be discussed,
Glasmann said.
At the conclusion of the meeting,
there will be dancing and refresh
ments in the Nitery.
