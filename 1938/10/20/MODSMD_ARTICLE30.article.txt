# Council to Plan U.C.L.A. Leaves
## 
### 
Women's Council will hold its
weekly meeting today at 4 o'clock
in the Ex Committee office of the
Old Union. Traffic cases will be
heard at 4 o'clock, with late leave
violations following.
Special late leave regulations for
the U.C.L.A. game week-end will be
discussed at the meeting. One spe
cial two-thirty for Sunday, October
30, has already been granted.
