# SUNDAY ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present an organ recital
in the chapel Sunday afternoon at
4 o'clock. The program will be us
follows: Toccata and Fugue in D
minor, Bach; Minuet from "Bere
nice," Handel; Fugue in C minor,
Buxtehude; Largo from the Cello
Concerto, Chopin; Fantasia in D flat,
St. Saens. The same program will
be repeated next Thursday after
noon.
