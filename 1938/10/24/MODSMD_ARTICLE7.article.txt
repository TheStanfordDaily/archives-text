# 'Y' Transfers To Hold Tea
## 
### 
The "Y" transfer group will hold
its second meeting this afternoon at
4 o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse
to discuss activities plans for the
year.
The group's purpose is to promote
friendship and better orientation
among the transfers. All those trans
fers who were not able to attend
last week's tea arc invited to come
this afternoon and net acquainted
with the group.
