# Stanfordites Face Liquor Charge
## 
### 
Charging violation of the state al
coholic beverage control act, war
rants for the arrest of three Stan
ford students were issued yesterday
in Mountain View justice court.
Bail was fixed at $20 cash and $40
bond by Justice of the Peace J. M.
Atkinson after Richard Burke and
E. J. Miller, State Board of Equali
zation agents, had sworn to the com
plaints.
A fine of $25 was paid yesterday
in Judge Atkinson's court by Bonnie
Collier, waitress, after she had
pleaded guilty of selling liquor to
the students, all minors, at the Rain
bow Inn.
