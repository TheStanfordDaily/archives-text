# Speeding, Parking Result in Arrest
## 
### 
Speeding on Palm Drive and park
ing on the pathway in front of the
Union Residence caused most of the
traffic arrests that were tried by the
Men's Council in its regular meeting
yesterday.
The three new Council members
officially took their seats, and further
plans were made for the scheduled
junior transfer program. A1 Horn,
chairman of the Council, was chosen
as Stanford men's representative on
the U.C.L.A. trip.
