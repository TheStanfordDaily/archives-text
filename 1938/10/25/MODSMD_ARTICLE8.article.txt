# Part-time Jobs Open to Students
## 
### 
The Stanford employment sex*vice
yesterday announced that there ai'e
several positions, both regular and
part-time, which are open to stu
dents who wish jobs.
There are several hashing jobs
open to men in-return for board.
A cashier's job is open to one girl
wishing to obtain board. For room
and board there are several posi
tions open to both men and women,
the work for the most part con
cerned with household dishwashing
and setting up. A single night's
work will be open next Wednesday
evening to two men who will work
for their dinner and a little cash.
A wearing apparel shop is offer
ing positions for two girl represen
tatives, one on the Row and one in
Lagunita. A married couple may
work in return for their room, which
is in the form of a rent-free cot
tage.
The last open job is for a young
woman to watch two small children
regularly from 2 to G o'clock every
Thursday.
