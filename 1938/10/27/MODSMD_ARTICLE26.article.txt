# FARM RATS IN PALY
## 
### 
While rats from Stanford play an
important part in "They Live Again,"
an M.G.M. Pete Smith oddity play
ing at the Stanford Theater Thurs
day, Friday and Saturday of this
week. The rats were obtained by
Ryland Madison, formerly of Stan
ford, and trained by Dr. Calvin
Stone, professor of psychology. They
arc used in a vivisection sequence.
