# YANKS ANNOUNCE TRADE
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 26 (UP).—The
New York Yankees today announced
a four-player deal with the St. Louis
Browns which sent Catcher Joe
Grenn and Outfielder Myril Hoag to
St. Louis in exchange for Pitcher
Oral Holdebrand and Outfielder Col
onel (Buster) Mills.
LOS ANGELES. Oct. 26 (UP).—
Jimmy Garrison of Kansas City won
a hair-line decision from George
Crouch of Los Angeles last night
in the 10-round main event of the
Olympic Auditorium boxing pro
gram.
