# Graduates Plan Hallowe'en Dance
## 
### 
Graduate women will entertain
the graduate men at a Hallowe'en
dance Saturday evening from 8:30
to 12 o'clock at the Nitery. All
graduate men are invited to come.
Enid Olivi is the general chairman,
and the sponsors will include Dr.
Kay Rogers, Professor and Mrs. H. J.
Rathbun and Professor L. T. Chapin.
