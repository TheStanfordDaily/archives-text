# Council Adopts New Rush Rules
## 
### 
Two new rushing rules were de
cided upon at the Interl'raternity
Council meeting: (1) Members of
fraternities may play golf with
rushees; and (2) members of fra
ternities may take rushees to foot
ball games although it may not be on
a rushing week-end.
Cards for the next rushing period,
November 4 and 5, went on sale at
the A.S.S.U. office yesterday.
Bud McDuffie announced that
rushing carried on this week-end in
Los Angeles will be illegal.
