# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
FROSII TENNIS TEAM meets to
day at 4:15 o'clock on the varsity
courts for Quad picture.
ORGAN RECITAI, in Chapel on
Sunday at 4 o'clock and Wednes
day at 4:15 o'clock.
GAIETIES CAST MEMBERS may
obtain tickets at the drama man
ager's office in the Old Union from
1 to 5 today.
"ALL'S WELL THAT ENDS WELL"
opens tonight in the Little Theater
at 8 o'clock.
FENCERS' CARNIVAL will be held
at the San Francisco Commercial
Club at 8 o'clock tonight.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S DANCE to
be held in the Nitery tomorrow
night from 8:30 to 12 o'clock. All
graduate men are invited.
