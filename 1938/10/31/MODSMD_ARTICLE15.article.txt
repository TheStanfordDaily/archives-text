# Lagunita Will Hold Jolly-up Wednesday
## 
### 
Lagunita Hall will hold a jolly-up
Wednesday evening from 7 to 8
o'clock in the Women's Gym, it was
announced yesterday. All campus
men are invited to attend.
Lcs Smith and his orchestra are
providing the music. The patrons
and patronesses will be Professor
and Mrs. William Irvin, Professor
and Mrs. Charles N. Reynolds, and
Miss Elizabeth B. Crandall. Irene
Woolach is the chairman of the com
mittee in charge of arrangements.
