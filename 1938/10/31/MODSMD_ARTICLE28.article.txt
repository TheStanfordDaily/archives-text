# Lindy at German Congress
## 
### 

COLONEL CHARLES A. LINDBERGH, rTglit, America's famed avia
tor, converses with Head Staff Engineer Wendland at the evening
reception of the Lilienthal Society's Congress in Berlin. The Colonel
recently was presented the service cross of the Order of the German
Eagle, with star, second highest German decoration, by Chancellor
Hitler, After inspecting Nazi airplane factories, Lindbergh plans to
visit Italy.
