# Union Entertains Faculty at Dinner
## 
### 
Several members of the faculty
were entertained at a dinner given
: n their honor by Union Club last
night. The committee in charge of
the affair was headed by Margaret
Greenwell.
The program, with Aline Kennar
mistress of ceremonies, included
the following skits: Life at the
French House, presented by Man
zanita; Blind Date, presented by
Mariposa; Burlesque of a House
Meeting, presented by Madrono; Re
action of Six Girls to a Buzzer, by
Union House; and a Professor Quiz
program which included faculty
members,' presented by Hawthorne
Cottage.

around the earth, travels a distance

miles.
