# Scout Leader Will Talk to 'Y'
## Practical Work Plan Will Be Discussed 
### 
"Opportunities for Girl Scout
Work in Palo Alto" will be the topic
of a talk to be given by Miss Nancy
Garratt, head of the Palo Alto Girl
Scouts, at the initial meeting of the
"Y" Social Service Group this after
noon at 4:30 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
According to Barbara Reamer,
chairman, the nucleus group will
make plans for practical work with
the Girl Scouts and at the Palo Alto
Children's Museum.
The Drama Group of the "Y," un
der the leadership of Janice Him
melwright, will also hold its organ
ization meeting today at 4 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
Plans for Armistice Day will be
discussed at the initial meeting of
the Peace Group of the "Y," which
will convene this afternoon at 4:45
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Winninette Arnold will be chairman.
