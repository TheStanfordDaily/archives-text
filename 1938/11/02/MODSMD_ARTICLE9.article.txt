# 'MEN FROM MARS' TERRORIZE U.S.
## 
### 

ARMS UPRAISED, Orson Welles, radio actor, directs the "War of the Worlds"' broadcast that caused hys
teria among radio listeners throughout the nation and caused evacuations from metropolitan areas. Welles
described a fictitious visitation of strange men from Mars. Thousands of persons in every part of the country
actually believed that the eastern part of the United States had been invaded and panic was widespread.
The Federal Communications Commission has started an investigation of the program. (Acme Telephoto)
