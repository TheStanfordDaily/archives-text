# 'Y' Frosh Group Will Meet Today
## 
### 
All freshmen "Y" members are
invited to attend a tea to be held
this afternoon, as part of the pro
gram of the Freshman Discussion
Group, at 4:30 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Organization of the group will
take place at the tea, according to
Mary Hayes, chairman. The purpose
of the Freshman Discussion Group is
to continue the orientation program
for freshman women with discus
sions on the problems concerning
studies, dates, clothes, sororities,
personal philosophy and campus
activities.
