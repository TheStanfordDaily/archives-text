# Seabiscuit Settles Supremacy
## The 						Stanford Daily Sports JOHN SHALLENBERGER, Sports Editor 
### 

RACE-TRACK ARGUMENT of 1938—Which is the host horse?—was
settled finally at Pinilico Track when Charles S. Howard's Seabiscuit
heat the famous War Admiral by three lengths in their special match

race. Ilcre is the finish of the mile and three-sixteenths race before
45,000 fans. Jockey George Wool! is on the winning horse from
California. (Acme Telephoto)
