# Ballet To Be Given For First Concert
## 
### 
Tomorrow night, November 5,
the All-American Ballet Caravan
gives its program in the Civic Audi
torium of San Jose as the opening
event of the San Jose Concert Se
ries. The presentation will consist
of '"Aid and Variation," in classic
style, "Filling Station" with music
by Virgil Thomson, and "Billy the
Kid" with music by Aaron Copland.
Students can buy tickets for the
Ballet Caravan in the A.S.S.U. office
at reduced rates.
