# 'Y' To Entertain Women Transfers
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. transfer group will
' entertain all women transfers at a
i Mexican tea Tuesday afternoon from |
! 4:15 to 5:30 o'clock.
To carry out the Mexican theme,'
| Jane Karpe will play her accordian
throughout the tea and Mrs. H. J. |
Rathbun, former "Y" secretary, will
sing Mexican songs in typical cos
tume. Ann McGuigan, Alda Leh
man, and Marian Guyselman are in
charge of the affair.
