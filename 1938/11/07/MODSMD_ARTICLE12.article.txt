# Anderson Election Rally Due Tonight
## 
### 
Arthur M. Hyde, former secretary
of agriculture and former governor
of Missouri, will speak for John Z.
Anderson, candidate for Congress, at
a political rally to be staged this
evening at the San Jose Civic Audi
torium.
An old-fashioned torchlight pa
rade, typical of political campaigns
in the '70s and '80s, and including a
delegation from Stanford, will he a
feature of the rally. Students taking
part in the parade will gather at Ihe
Fiji house at 6:50 o'clock this eve
ning.
Heading the Young Republican
group on the campus are Hal Wil
liams, Dan Feeley, and Walt
Wickett.
