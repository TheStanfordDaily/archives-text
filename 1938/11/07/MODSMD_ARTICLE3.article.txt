# Gershwin Program To Be Presented
## 
### 
The first record program of the
year will be presented this after
noon at 4:15 o'clock in the Little
Theater of the Memorial Auditor
ium. The entire hour will be devoted
to the works of George Gershwin.
A Memorial Album has just been
issued as a tribute to the late
American musician by his friend.
Nathaniel Shilkret. Under the di
rection of Shilkret a vocal and or
chestral ensemble, together with
Jane Froman, Sonny Schuyler, and
Felix Knight., soloists, have recorded
a varied program of hits from
' Swanec to "Porgy and Bess."
