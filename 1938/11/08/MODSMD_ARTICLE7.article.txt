# CINEMA IN TELEVISION
##

HOLLYWOOD, Nov. 6 (UP).—
Paramount Pictures entered the
field of television tonight with an
announcement it had taken over an
established television laboratory and
was prepared to market popular
priced receiving sets within the next
few days.
