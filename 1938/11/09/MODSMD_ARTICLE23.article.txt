# Dr. Eliot to Speak At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Luncheon Club speaker today will
be Dr. Samuel Eliot of the Arling
ton Street Church of Boston, who
will talk on "Problems of Interna
tional Conflict Today."
Dr. Eliot is the son of the former
president of Harvard. As a young
man he helped survey the Inner
Quad at Stanford in 188 G. He came
to San Francisco for the World's Al
liance of International Friendship
which met on November 6 and 7,
and last Sunday gave a sermon at
the Memorial Chapel.
