# Big Game Dance Set for Palace
## 
### 
Only official place for celebrating
Stanfordites in San Francisco on Big
Game night will be the Palace Ho
tel's Gold Room, according to Rob
ert "Bones" Hamilton, alumni sec
retary.
Coaching staff, players, the Glee
Club, visiting and local alumni, and
several hundred Stanford students
will gather in the Gold Room 011
November 19.
Cost of dinner and cover charge
for the evening has been set at $5.50
a person. Bids may be reserved in
the A.S.S.U. office, old Union, and
the alumni office in the Adminis
tration Building.
