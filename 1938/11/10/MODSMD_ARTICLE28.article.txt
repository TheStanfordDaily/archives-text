# Bulletin Board
##

MEN AND WOMEN WHO WISH to
take out or renew tuition notes for
the winter quarter should call at
the office of the Dean of Men or
the Dean of Women from Monday
to Friday, Nov. 14-18.
UNION TREASURE HUNT for
freshman women this afternoon !
from 4 to 6 o'clock.
GAIETIES REHEARSAL: 1-2 o'-
clock, Zooks; 2-3, Date Machine;
3-4. Stanford in the Rain; 4-5. ;
Tune In on Love; 5-6:30, Marco
Thome; 5:30-6, Field and Stream:
7-10. Glee Club.
BRIDGE GROUP of the Stanford;
Dames will meet this afternoon
at 2:30 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.
A.S.U. EXECUTIVE and Co-opera
tive Committees will meet at 5
o'clock this afternoon in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
JUNIOR ORCHESIS will meet at 5
o'clock today in the Women's Gym.
MEAL IICKET SALE for Tri-
Sports Day will end tomorrow.
Tickets can be purchased at all
living groups and at the Women's
Gym.
ROBLE BONFIRE HOSTESSES will
meet today with Food Committee
Chairman Mary Kay Horton in the
Rohle Clubroom at 7 o'clock.
LIVING GROI P CHAIRMEN for
Big Game Parade floats will meet
tomorrow afternoon at 2:30 o'clock
at the Women's Clubhouse.
RALLY COMMITTEE MEMBERS
and tryoutees will work on card
stunts today and tomorrow from
1 to 5 o'clock in Room 13.
