# G.E. Corporation Gives Study Prize
## 
### 
Fellowships for research work in
physics, electricity of physical chem
istry are available for senior students
or graduates who wish to study in
American or European institutions
next year, according to an an
nouncement received here today
from the Charles A. Coffin Founda
tion.
The scholarships, established by
the General Electric Corporation,
carry a minimum allowance of $500
each. Applications must be filed
with the Scholarship Committee,
Charles A. Coffin Foundation, Sche
nectady, New York, by January 15.
