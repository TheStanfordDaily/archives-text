# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
TUITION NOTES for winter quarter
must be taken out or renewed by t
those so desiring at the office of i
the Dean of Men or the Dean of
Women from Monday to Friday,
Nov. 1 to 18.
BLOCK "S" SOCIETY will meet to
night at 8 o'clock in the 8.A.C.,
according to Bob Mathews, who is
organizing the group.
MEN'S AND WOMEN'S GLEE
CLUBS will meet tonight at 8
o'clock in Room 84 for a rehearsal
of the Mothers' Club program.
APPLICANTS FOR USHERS' PO- j
SITIONS at the Gaieties should
appear at the Dramatic Office be- j
tween 1 and 5 o'clock.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE will meet
today at 1 o'clock in the Women's j
Clubhouse.
THE STANFORD BAND will hold
its regular Monday rehearsal in
the band house at 4:15 o'clock. All
members are expected to be pres
ent.
QUAD PROOFS available in Room
14 of the Old Union.
