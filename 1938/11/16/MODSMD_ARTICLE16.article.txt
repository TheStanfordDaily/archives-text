# Women Granted Big Game Leaves
## 
### 
Special 3:30 o'clock late leaves
will be granted to Stanford women
on Big Game Night, Gretchen Ahi
swede, Women's Council chairman,
announced yesterday.
The special leaves will count on
the regular quota of two-thirties.
Regulations for Thanksgiving va
cation were also revealed by
Gretchen yesterday. Wednesday,
Thursday, Friday, and Saturday
night will automatically be granted
as non-quota leaves. Plans for spe
cial Sunday late leaves will appear
in tomorrow's Daily.
