# U.S. Fetes Cuban Dictator
## 
### 

COL FULGENCIO BATISTA, Cuban dictator, left, is shown with Gen.
Malin Craig, chief of staff of the U. S. Army, as their official car left
the Union station in Washington. Batista's visit was the occasion of
the appearance of most of Washington's medals, uniforms, and rili
hers. During his stay, the Cuban dictator was widely feted.
