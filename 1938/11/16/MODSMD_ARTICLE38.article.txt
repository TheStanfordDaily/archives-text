# WOMEN HONOR STANFORD'S DEAD
## 
### 

IN STANFORD'S ARMISTICE DAY observance, the 77 students and alumni who gave their lives in the
World War were honored. Mrs. Gertrude W. Powell, past president of the Frank J. Gard unit of the "Flight
ind Forty" at Glendora, Calif., places a wreath before the tablet in the Memorial Hall foyer. With her arc
other representatives of this honor society of the American Legion auxiliary. Jack Nissen, member of the
R.O.T.C. guard of honor, stands at attention by the American flag. (Photo by Dudman)
