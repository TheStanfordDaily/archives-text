# City Boasts Only Lady Police Chief
## 
### 
POPLAR BLUFF, Mo., (UP). —
Poplar Bluff is believed to be the
only city in the United States with
a population of 10,000 or more with
a woman chief of police.
Mrs. Josephine Davis, widow of
the late Chief of Police Robert J.
Davis, was appointed by Mayor Ira
Bradley to fill the unexpired term
of her husband.
