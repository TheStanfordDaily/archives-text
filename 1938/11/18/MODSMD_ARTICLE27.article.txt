# Special Train Scheduled For Rooters
## 
### 
A special rooters' train will leave
the Paly station tomorrow morning
at 10:15 o'clock to take all Farm
rooters without cars to Cal, accord
ing to Southern Pacific agents.
The, train will arrive at the Oak
land pier at about 11:30 o'clock,
where special red cars will leave at
intervals for the Berkeley campus.
The train returning back to Stan
ford will leave the Oakland pier sta
tion at about 6 o'clock tomorrow
night, arriving here at. about 7:30
o'clock.
Tickets, which are $1 for the
round trip, are good on any South
ern Pacific trains tomorrow.
