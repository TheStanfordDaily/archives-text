# Economist to Talk At Lucheon Club
## 
### 
As usual, the Luncheon Club will
hold its regular meeting next Tues
day at noon in the Nitery. Accord
ing to information made public yes
terday by Dr. Trueblood, Mr. L. L.
Lowrin, noted economist, will be
the honored guest speaker, but as
yet the subject of discussion has
not been decided.
Mr. Lowrin is at present connect
ed with the Brookling Institute of
New York, and has come to the
United States from the international
oHice in Geneva, Switzerland.
During his present few days' visit
in the vicinity of Palo Alto, he will
also be the guest speaker at the
Commonwealth Club in San Fran
cisco.
