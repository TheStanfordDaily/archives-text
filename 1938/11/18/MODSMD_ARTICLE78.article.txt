# OREGON IN FAIR
## 
### 
SALEM, Ore., Nov. 15 (UP).—The
State Emergency Board today ap
propriated $15,000 for an agricultur
al exhibit at the Golden Gate Inter
national Exposition next year, but
delayed another $15,000 because no
money could be appropriated past
the biennium ending December 31.
