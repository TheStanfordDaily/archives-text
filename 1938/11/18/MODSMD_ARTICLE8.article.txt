# Mexicans Protest U.S. Treatment
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Nov. 17 (UP). —
Spokesmen for a group of Mexicans
repatriated from the United States
protested today that Mexican labor
| organizations were too busy attack
ing Nazi persecutions to help "our
compatriots who are unfortunate
enough to live in the United States
and suffer humiliations and vexa-
I (ions similar to the Jews in Ger
many."
"American Jews treat Mexicans
worse than anybody else, especially
in Texas, Arizona, and California,"
the spokesman said.
