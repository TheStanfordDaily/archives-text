# 'Y' TEA TODAY
## 
### 
Miss Marian Reith, Asilomar
regional secretary of the Y.W.C.A.,
will be honored by the cabinet, of
the "Y" at a tea to be given this
afternoon from 4 to 5 o'clock in the
"Y" rooms. All members are in
vited by Virginia Sterry and Eve
lyn Chapman, who are co-chairmen
of the affair.
