# 400 KILLED
## 
### 
PERPIGNAN, French-Spanish
Frontier, Nov. 20 (UP). —Red Cross
workers arriving at the frontier to
night reported 400 persons had been
killed or injured in a series of tre
mendous explosions and fires that
destroyed big loyalist munitions fac
tories at Barcelona.
