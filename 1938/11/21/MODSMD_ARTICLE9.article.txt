# This Is Home to Them Now
## 
### 

AMONG 136 JEWISH FAMILIES ousted from Sudetenland by Nazis
and refused admittance by Czechoslovakia are this mother and daugh
ter, who huddle outside their only home, a rude tarpaulin set up as a
tent. Refugees, deprived of everything, await a decision regarding
their future. Charity services arc their only source of aid.
