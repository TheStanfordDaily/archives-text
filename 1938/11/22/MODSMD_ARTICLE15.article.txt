# Ex-President
## 
### 

PACIFIC COAST ASSOCIATION
of Collegiate Registrars, who held
their 13th annual convention at
the Hollywood Knickerbocker
Hotel in Hollywood, Nov. 13, 14,
15, chose San Francisco as their
convention city for next year and
elected J. A. Anderson of Pasa
dena Junior College as their next
president. Dr. J. Pierce Mitchell,
Stanford registrar, is past presi
dent of the American Association
of Collegiate Registrars. (Photo
courtesy El wood J. Robinson
Agency.)
