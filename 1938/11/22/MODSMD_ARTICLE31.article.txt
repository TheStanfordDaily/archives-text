# ANGLO-AMERICAN TRADE PACT SIGNED
## 
### 

PRESIDENT ROOSEVELT and representatives of the British Empire meet in the East Room of the White House in Washington, D.C., to sign
the Anglo-Amertcan trade treaty, linking the United Kingdom, Canada, and the United States in trade agreements. Left to right, seated: Ar
nold Overton of the British Board of Trade; Sir Ronald Lindsay, British Ambassador, who signed for the United Kingdom, northern Ireland.
Newfoundland and the British Colonies; President Roosevelt; Prime Minister W. L. Mackenzie King of Canada; Secretary of State Cordell Hull
Standing: Assistant Secretary of State Francis Sayre; Canadian Minister Sir Herbert Mailer; Dr. O. E. Skciton, Canadian Undersecretary of
State; and C. Barnes. (Acme Telephoto)
