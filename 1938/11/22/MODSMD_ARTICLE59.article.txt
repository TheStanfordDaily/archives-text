# STARS ADOPTION UPHELD
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Nov. 21 (UP).—
The district court of appeals late
today upheld the adoption of Fred
die Bartholomew, young screen
star, by his aunt and legal guard
ian, Miss Myllicent Bartholomew.
