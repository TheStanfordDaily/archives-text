# TREATY REVISION
## 
### 
HAVANA, Cuba, Nov. 26 (UP).—
Negotiations for the revision of the
Cuban-American reciprocity treaty
may be opened "within a few days,"
Colonel Fulgoncio Batista, Army
chief of staff, said last night on his
return from a visit to the United
States.
