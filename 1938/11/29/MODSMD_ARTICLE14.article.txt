# International Club Will Hold Party
## 
### 
The International Committee will
hold its annual Christmas party at 8
o'clock next Sunday evening, De
cember 4, at the home of Dr. and
Mrs. Payson J. Treat of the history
department.
Chairman for the party is Helen
Oilman, co-chairman of the Inter
national Committee. Beth Yager has
charge of refresliments, and Shirley
Austerland will head the entertain
ment and decorations committee for
the event.
