# Amateur Cooks Dish It Up
## 
### 

TWENTY-SEVEN business and professional men in New York fussed
over a half acre of took stoves in Ihe Hotel Governor Clinton during
an amateur cooks' contest to prove "man's superiority in the culinary
arts." At left is Andre Baruch, radio commentator, who won first
prize with his lobster dish. At right is Charles Small, finance writer.
