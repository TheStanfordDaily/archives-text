# 'CONJURER' HANGED
## 
### 
HUAUTLA, Mexico, Nov. 28 (UP).
! —Led by village officials, residents
of the town of Pachuca yesterday
hanged Juan Salvador, suspected of
' witchcraft and later burned his body.
