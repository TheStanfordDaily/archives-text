# Catholics to Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
The Newman Club will hold its
second meeting of the quarter this
evening at 7:30 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. The topic to be dis
cussed will be the central act of
Catholic worship, the Mass. Father
Munier, chaplain, will head the dis
cussion.
December 11 will be the date for
the club breakfast at Wilson's. Mr.
Frank GrifTin will show his personal
technicolor pictures of the Holy Land
and with a description of that coun
try following the Communion at the
8:15 o'clock mass and breakfast.
