# Sororities to Give Courtesy Teas
## 
### 
Freshman and transfer women will
receive invitations to attend cour
tesy teas to be given by the nine
sororities on campus this Friday,
Saturday, and Sunday, according to
Jean Welch, president of Panhel
lenic.
Teas will last from 3 to 5:30
o'clock, with Quad clothes to be worn
Friday and Saturday, and dressy
silks on Sunday, with hats if desired.
Sponsors will furnish any further de
sired information.
