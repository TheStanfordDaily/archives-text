# Dr. Morgan Plans Visit to Germany
## 
### 
Dr. Bayard Quincy Morgan, pro
fessor of German, will leave on the
12th of this month for Germany, and
at Dresden will join Mrs. Morgan
and his daughter, who have been in
Dresden since last July and during
the war scare of last September.
He expects to be in Dresden for
Christmas and will visit various Ger
man universities and cities, includ
ing Berlin and Vienna. He will con
fer with the foreign office in Berlin
in regard to exchange professor be
tween Germany and America, and
will deliver some illustrated lectures
on California at Berlin, Dresden,
Vienna, and Munich, also in London
and Bristol. He expects to make a
trip to Denmark and other Scan
dinavian countries and will return
for the spring quarter.
