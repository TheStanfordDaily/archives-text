# BENNETT TO SPEAK FRIDAY
## 
### 
Merrill K. Bennett, economist in
the Food Research Institute, leaves
tomorrow for The Dalles, Ore., where
he will speak Friday before the
eleventh annual Western Wheat
League on "World Wheat Position
and Prospects." Other speakers will
include Governor-elect Charles A.
Sprague and Senator-elect Rufus C.
Holman and Harry Brown, assistant
secretary of agriculture in Wash
ington.
