# Orchesis Tryouts Set for January
## 
### 
Tryouts for Orchesis, Stanford
women's dance group, will take
place early in January, Miss Marga
ret Jewell, Orchesis adviser, an
nounced yesterday.
To be eligible for tryouts, women
students must have completed at
least one quarter of modern dance
and have had experience in Junior
Orchesis. Tryouts include practical
techniques, some immediate tech
nical problems, and simple compo
sitions.
Students who wish to try out
should give their names to Miss Jew
ell and secure more detailed infor
mation about the requirements.
