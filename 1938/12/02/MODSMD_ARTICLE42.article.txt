# Dates Fixed For Jamboree, Prom
## 
### 
Tentative dates for the Junior
Prom and the California-Stanford
Jamboree were decided upon last
night at the first meeting of Junior
Executive Committee. The dates
must be approved by Ex Committee
before they will be definite.
The largest social event of winter
quarter, the Junior Prom, will be
held on February 17. It is to be a
formal dance, and will feature some
big-name band, to be decided later.
