# ROOSEVELT VIEWS AIR FORCES
## 
### 

PRESIDENT ROOSEVILT, Navy Secretary Swanson, and other officials saw practically every type of air
plane owned by the government when the army and navy lined up the machines at Boiling Field and the
adjoining naval field at Anacostia, Washington, D.C. Arrow indicates the President in the shadow of one
of the army's giant (lying fortresses. Army's new bomber, still in the experimental stage, excited the
President's interest.
