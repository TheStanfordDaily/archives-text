# Daily Suspends For Holidays
## 
### 
Suspension of publication
after today's issue until winter
quarter was announced yester
day by Jim Stone, Daily edi
tor.
"In the face of Christmas
shopping, finals, and the A. S.
S. U. constitution, The Daily
feels that today's paper should
be the last, until January 4." he
said.
At the same time as Stone's
pronouncement, Harry Press, ex
officio head of the traditional
Daily Poker Trust, announced
that first meeting will be held
thus afternoon at 2 o'clock, will
meet subsequently through
pre-finals and finals weeks, ev
ery afternoon.
Some few others of the staff
said they were going to study,
a quick cross-section survey
showed.
