# Coach to Address Stanford Women
## 
### 
Everett Dean, basketball
coach, will address Stanford
women who have signed up
for the new Spectator Sports
course tonight at 7 o'clock in
the Basketball Pavilion. The
scheduled hour has been
changed so that those attend
ing will not be forced to miss
the Iturbi concert.
Dean will explain the rules,
techniques, and fundamentals
of basketball as the first guest
instructor in the course plan
ned to enable Stanford women
to enjoy better the games they
see through a greater knowl
edge of the sport.
Skiing will be discussed at
the second meeting of the class,
to be held at the scheduled
time, 7:15 to 9 o'clock next
Wednesday night, in the- Edu
cation Auditorium.
