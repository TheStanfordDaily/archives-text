# Montana to Exhibit 'Ranch" at Fair
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 3 (UP).
A miniature Montana ranch, occupy
ing 2,000 square feet of exhibit space,
will be "built on Treasure Island
as Montana's exhibit in the 19.')!)
Golden Gate International Exposi
tion, it was announced.
The "ranch and "ranch house"
will house a diorama depicting the
history of Montana, and will be
placed on the northeast corner of
the exposition's Hall of States.
