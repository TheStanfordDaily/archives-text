# Phi Delts to Show Football Movies
## 
### 
Motion pictures of the recent
Stanford-Dartmouth football game
will bo shown at 8 o'clock tonight at
the Phi Delt house, according to an
announcement from the Board of
Athletic Control.
They will include the whole game
in play-by-play sequence. All in
terested students, with the excep
tion of freshmen subject to rushing
restrictions, will be welcome. Other
films will be on hand for those in
terested in rehashing other games of
the 1938 season.
