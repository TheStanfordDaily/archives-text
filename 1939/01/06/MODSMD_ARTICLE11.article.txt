# Stanford Students Attend 'Y' Meets
## 
### 
Twenty-seven Stanford students
and advisers attended the annual
Y.W.C.A. and Y.M.C.A. conference
at Asilomar, which was held from
December 26 to January 2. This
year the conference was on the re
lationship between religion and con
temporary problems.
Miss Kay Rogers, secretary of the
Y, led the campus delegation, which
included 17 men and 10 women.
The group represented law, political
science, business, English, German,
medicine, and engineering. One of
the highlights of the conference was
a round-table discussion of econom
ic problems, which was led by Pro
fessor Theodore Kreps of the Stan
ford Department of Economics.
