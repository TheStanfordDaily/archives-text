# SIERRA SNOWS HEAVY
## 
### 
RENO. Nov., Jan. 5 (UP).—Tire
chains were compulsory for auto
mobiles using the Reno-Sacramento
highway over Donner summit today
as the heaviest snowfall this season
blanketed the mountains with 21
inches of snow.
