# TRIGAMIST JAILED

cause he had "always been crazy,"
Charles Mills, 44. confessed trigam
ist, was in the county jail here today
awaiting arraignment in superior
court on the charge of having ac
cumulated three wives within 18
months without benefit of divorce.
