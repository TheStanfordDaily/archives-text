# Orchesis Tryouts Set for January 19
## 
### 
Tryouts for Orchesis, campus
dance group, will be held Thursday
afternoon, January 19, in the Dance
Studio, All women students who
have completed one quarter of
Modern Dance are eligible to try
out, though it is advisable to have
had some experience in Junior Or
chesis.
Candidates will be required to
present a short original composition
in addition to their skill tests. All
women wishing to become members
of Orchesis should give their names
and secure more detailed informa
tion about requirements from Miss
Margaret Jewell, Orchesis adviser.
