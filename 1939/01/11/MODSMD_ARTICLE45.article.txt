# Ski Club to Show U.C.L.A. Movie
## 
### 
Ski Club will sponsor the showing
of movies sent to them by the
U.C.L.A. ski club at the meeting
of the Spectator Sports course to be
given at 7:15 o'clock in the Educa
tion building auditorium.
The original plans to have Dr.
Frank J. Howard, vice-president of
the Auburn Ski Club, to lecture and
show his movies have been changed
to a later date.
The U.C.L.A. movie shows skiing
; I Mount Baldy in Southern Cali
fornia, and is patterned after the
movie, "Ski Chase." The program
will be free to ail members of the
men's and women's ski classes, and
members of the ski club. All other
interested students are urged to at
tend, and there will be an admis
sion fee of 15 cents for them.
