# Ailing Mother May See Sun
## 
### 

EDWARD, DUKE OF WINDSOR, will in all probability fly to see his
mother. Queen Mother Mary of England, who is ill with heart trouble.
He will fly to Sandringham on January 13, it is reported. It is also
said that his American-born Duchess is angry at being left in
France. Picture shows the Duke then King, saluting his mother, in
1936. George is at the right.
