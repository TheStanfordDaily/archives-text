# Engineers to Hold Annual Banquet
## 
### 
George W. Malone, general man
ager of the Industrial West, has re
cently been chosen to give the ad
dress at the second annual banquet
of the Stanford engineers which will
be held next Wednesday evening in
the Stanford Union.
Under the sponsorship of Tau
Beta Pi, national engineering soci
ety, the first banquet was given last
year in order to have undergrad
uate engineers become acquainted
with the possibilities in their par
ticular fields. Its success made pos
sible next week's affair.
All members of the engineering
faculty, and undergraduate engi
neers are invited to attend the ban
quet. and tickets are available in
the A.S.S.U. office for 75 cents.
