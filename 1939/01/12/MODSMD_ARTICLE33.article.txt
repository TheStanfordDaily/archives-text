# ALL-STARS PLEASE STAFF
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Jan. 11 (UP).—
| Coaches Elmer (Gus) Henderson and
Ray Flaharty said today they were
satisfied with the progress made by
their all-star eleven which will meet
; the world professional football
champion New York Giants in a
! "pro bowl" game here Sunday.
