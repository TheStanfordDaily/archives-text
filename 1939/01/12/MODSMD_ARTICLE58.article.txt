# 'POISED,' OR POISON?
## 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
Can the author of the article
"Shall Sororities Stay?" possibly
have seen a Roble pledge class and
still be so unbelievably rash as to
label them "poised and mature"?
A good veneer job will cover a
multitude of sins!
The Upcouth and
obviously immature
