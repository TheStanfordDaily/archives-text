# Derailed Freight Slows S.P. Traffic
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 15 (UP).
—Traffic on Southern Pacific rail
road's Sierra Nevada route between
California and the East, was delayed
more than seven hours today by a
freight train derailment in Blue
Canyon, east of Sacramento.
One car of a freight left, the rails
and pulled several others willi il
about 1 a.m.
The wreck occurred in front of
the Southern Pacific's "Challenger"
passenger train westbound from
Reno. The Challenger was held up
seven hours, reaching San Francisco
at 2 p.m.
