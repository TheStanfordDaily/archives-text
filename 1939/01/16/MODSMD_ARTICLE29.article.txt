# Dr. Allen to Form String Orchestra
## 
### 
To recruit members for a string
orchestra at Stanford, which will
hold concerts in the latter part of
spring quarter, Dr. Warren D. Allen
has issued the call for all students,
alumni, faculty, and other Stanford
men and women to apply for mem
bership.
Those intexested will see Miss
Frankie Miles, secretary to the Di
vision of Music, in the Vestry, Me
morial Church, stating their prefer
ences for rehearsal hours.
