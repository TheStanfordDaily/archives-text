# Boston Bees Will Sign New Players
## 
### 
BOSTON, Jan. 15 (UP). — The
Boston Bees will mail contracts to
morrow for the .services of their
players for the '39 season.
They have already signed con
tracts with Pitcher Danny Mac-
Fayden and Outfielder A1 Simmons,
obtained from the Washington
Senators. President Bob Quinn ex
pects no difficulty with the rest of
the squad.

country.
