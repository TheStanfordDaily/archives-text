NEW X-RAY MACHINE. designed to operate at 1,000.000-volts, yet
small enough for installation in existing hospital buildings, is being
assembled, as above at the General Electric Company's plant at Sche
nectady, N.Y. L. E. Dempster, left, and W. F. Westerdorp are the
designers. With a heavy lead shield, it weighs 4000 pounds.
