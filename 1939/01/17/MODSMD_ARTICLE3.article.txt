# Spectator Group To Study Dancing
## 
### 
Choosing as her topic, "Does and
Don't of Social Dancing," Miss Eve
lyn Carey of Claremont Junior High
School in Oakland will appear on
tomorrow night's program for the
Spectator Course.
Several couples Will demonstrate
the various points which arc con
sidered in good form on the ball
room floor. Miss Carey is known as

has offered special summer courses

The Spectator Course, which
meets Wednesdays in the auditorium
of the Education Building, is open
to those not. registered in the course
