# CANADIANS TIE AMERKS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 15 (UP).—The
New York Americans Sunday night
lost ground in their drive toward
second place in the National Hockey
League when they were held to a
1-1 tie by the Montreal Canadians,
occupants of the cellar.
