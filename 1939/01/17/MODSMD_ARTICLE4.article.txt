# Encina Pow-wow Greets New Men
## 
### 
Welcoming the 30 new freshmen
who entered winter quarter, a pow
wow was held last, night at. 10 o'clock
in the main lobby of Encina.
Student body leaders, hall spon
sors and representatives, and Dean
John Bunn were introduced to the
new students.
John Dern, head Encina sponsor,
opened the meeting with a welcom
ing address. Student Body President
11. B. Lee spoke on campus activities,
and A1 Horn led an Honor Code dis
cussion. Dean Bunn also welcomed
the students.
