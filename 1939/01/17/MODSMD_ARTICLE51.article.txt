# WOMEN'S INTRAMURAL
## 
### 
All women who wish to play in
the Women's Intramural ping-pong
singles and badminton singles and
doubles must register today before
5 o'clock, it was announced. The
play-offs will start Thursday.

As far as is known, the first post
office for use by the general public
was established in 1516 between
Vienna and Berlin.
