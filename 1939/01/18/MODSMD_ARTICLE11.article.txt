# Class Will See Dance Program
## 
### 
| Miss Evelyn Carey of Claremont
Junior High School, with several
couples to illustrate, will present, a
program entitled "Do's and Don't of
Social Dancing" for the spectator
sports class tomorrow evening.
Miss Carey will bring out various
points which are considered good
dancing through demonstration by
the couples. An authority in ball
room dancing, Miss Carey has
taught special dancing courses at
the University of California.
The program, which is open to
j those who are not registered in the
j course for an admission fee of 15
j cents, will start at 7:30 o'clock in
; the Education auditorium.
