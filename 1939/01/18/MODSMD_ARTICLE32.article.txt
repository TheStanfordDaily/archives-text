# German Movie Set for Sunday
## 
### 
Second of this quarter's Sunday
Night Movies, "Fanny Elssler," will
be presented on January 22 at 7:15
o'clock in Memorial Hall. Admis
sion will be 20 cents.
"Fanny Elssler," produced by the
German film studio, Ufa, stars the
famed actress-dancer Lillian Harvey
in the title role. The story is based
on the true life of Fanny Elssler, a
favorite Austrian ballet dancer of
the early 19th century.
The German dialogue is augment
ed by English sub-titles. Also ap
pearing on the same bill will be a
short German documentary film.
