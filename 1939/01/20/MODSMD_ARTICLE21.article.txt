# Wilburs Depart On Oregon Tour
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur left last night on a trip to
Portland and Klamath Falls, Ore.
Dr. Wilbur will address the
Klamath Falls high school alumni
on Friday, will meet with the l'ac
ulty of Reed College at a Saturday
luncheon, and on Saturday evening
will address the Portland Junior
Chamber of Commerce on the sub
ject, "Grandfather vs. Grandson in
America Today."
