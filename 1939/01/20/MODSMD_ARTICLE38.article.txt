# Budge Takes Vines To Clinch Lead
## 
### 
LOUISVILLE, Ky., Jan. 19 (UP), j
—Donald Budge took a three-match !
lead over his barnstorming opponent, j
Ellsworth Vines, outpointing him in '
two sets out of three last night.
Budge displayed excellent control!
as he won the first set, 6-3, then;
dropped the second set, 3-6. He :
rallied in the third set, winning 6-4,1
to clinch the match.
