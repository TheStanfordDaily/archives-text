# CAMPUS OPINION
## CHESS CLUB URGED 
### 
The Editor, Sir:
We too have wondered why there
is no chess club at Stanford. The
mental gymnastics afforded by this
pastime might help diminish Roble
hysteria.
Please let us know if such an or
ganization is formed. We would en
joy playing around with our kings,
too.
Chessfully yours,
Frosh enthusiasts
