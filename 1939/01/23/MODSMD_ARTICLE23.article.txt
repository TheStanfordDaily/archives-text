# Passes Fly High In Fire House Football Classic
## 
### 
Not content with playing football
with just one girl, and then making
her housemother as they did a few
weeks ago, yesterday the guys in
the firehouse had a full half dozen
gals over from the Union for a
gentlemanly game of football.
Aside from the fact that one of
the women surprised everyone in
cluding herself by repeatedly
throwing 40-yard passes and act
ing generally like a football play
er, the game was reported to be
very entertaining.
So far the firehouse has not an
nounced the pledging of any new
house mothers.
