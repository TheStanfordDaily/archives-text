# Chappie to Treat 'Greek Philosophy'
## 
### 
"Needless to say," remarked Old
Boy Art Levinson, in the birth
pangs of mental effort, "the January
issue of Chappie will be the best
and funniest in history."
Featuring the fraternities and
sororities, the issue, entitled "Greek
Philosophy," will go on sale Wed
nesday.
The cover is by Doris Tucker,
marking the second time in 40 years
of Chappie history that the cover
has been done by a woman, accord
ing to Levison.
Stories and poems by Marco
Thome, Dave LefT, Sherman Mel
linkoff, Bert Rosenfeld, Willard
Stevens, Levinson, and Margaret
Micdell arc included in the issue.
