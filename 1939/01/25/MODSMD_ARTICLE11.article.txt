# Pre-Med Society To Hear Wilbur
## 
### 
The first official meeting of the
Stanford Pre-med Society will be
held this evening at 7:30 o'clock in
the downstairs lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
After a short business meeting Dr.
Wilbur, honorary sponsor of the so
ciety, will speak. All members and
other premcds who are interested in
the future of the. society are par
ticularly requested to be present, as
many important matters must be
decided at tihs meeting, according
to Sam Woolington, president.
The following faculty members
will be there: Dr. G. Ferris, Mr. H.
Bradbury, Dr. V. C. Twjtty, Dr.
W. 11. Johnson, Dr. E. W. Schultz,
Mr. J. Heath, and Dr. Saier. Re
freshments will be served.
