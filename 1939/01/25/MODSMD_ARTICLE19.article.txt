# VAUGHN AHEAD
## 
### 
PALO ALTO, Jan. 24 (UP). —
Ralph Vaughn, Southern California
cage star who set a new Pacific
Coast conference scoring record
against U.C.L.A. last week when he
tallied 36 points, was far out in
front in the Southern division scor
ing race today. In three games
Vaughn has averaged better than 21
points per contest.
