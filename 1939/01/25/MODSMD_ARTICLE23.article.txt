# CAVALIER SURVIVORS GO ASHORE
## 
### 

SAFE ON LANI) AT LAST after the horror of spending nearly 10 hours adrift on the Atlantic when the
British Imperial Airways' plane. Cavalier, crashed on a flight from Bermuda to New York, first survivors
of the crash step down a gang plank to safety after arriving in New York aboard the Esso Baytown. In the
