# LOYOLA FOLDS
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Jan. 24 (UP).—
Graduate Manager Luddy Breiner
announced today that Loyola Uni
versity's basketball team has been
disbanded for the season because of
lack of funds, failure to obtain a
suitable home court and student
apathy.
