## U. OF N. ACE DOWN-HILL RACER 
### JOHN SHALLENBERGER, Sports Editor 
JOHN SHALLENBERGER, Sports Editor


JACK STARRETT. ace down-hill racer for the University of Nevada ski team, prepares for the Inter
collegiate Ski Competitions to be hold in conjunction with the U. of N. winter carnival to he staged in
Reno January 27. 28. 28. Starrett's sister is carnival director and former women's ski champion of Cali
fornia.
