# Livingston Named Peace Chairman
## 
### 
I Accepting the resignation of Chair
man John Hewitt and electing Bob
Livingston as successor, the Peace
Council met in the A.S.S.U. council
room yesterday afternoon to outline
plans for winter quarter anti-war
activity.
The naming of Livingston to the
leadership of the Council came on
the nomination of the outgoing
chairman. A member of Interna
tional Committee, member of last
year's Peace Council, and prominent
in campus peace work, Livingston
I steps into the position with wide
knowledge of anti-war campaigns.
