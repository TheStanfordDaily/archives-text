# Harvard Offers New Scholarships
## 
### 
Harvard College is offering 21 Na
tional Scholarships this fall for stu
dents entering the freshman class
from 17 of the Middle. South, and
Far Western states. The scholar
ships are awarded on the basis of
school records, scholarship examina
tions and character references.
The amount will be adjusted in
dividually according to the finan
cial status of the family, from $100
to $1000. Applications must be filed
by March 15 at Harvard, and after
February 1 blanks may be secured
from the Scholarship Committee of
the San Francisco Harvard Club.
