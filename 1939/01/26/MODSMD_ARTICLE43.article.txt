# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
WOMEN'S GLEE CLUB meets today
at 12:45 o'clock in Memorial Am
phitheater for Quad picture.
VARSITY BASKETBALL SQUAD
meets today at 4:00 o'clock in the
Pavilion for Quad pictures.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANIZA
TION holds its regular weekly tes
timonial meeting on the top floor
of the Women's Clubhouse tonight
at 7:30 o'clock.
"Y" FIRESIDE MUSICAL GROUP
meets this afternoon at 4:30 o'clock.
DEBATE SQUAD will hold a gen
eral meeting today at 4 o'clock in
Room 200. All members please
attend.
CHARTER MEETING OF THE
CHESS CLUB tonight at 7:15 o'-
clock in the Nitery. All those in
terested in chess are invited to
attend.
DEUTSCHE SINGSTUNDE will be
held today, 4:30 o'clock, at the
German House, 514 Alvarado Road.
TRYOUTS FOR THE RAMSHEAD
ONE-ACT PLAY'S will be held at
7:30 o'clock tonight in room 125 of
the Memorial Hall. Scripts will be
returned in the office of Chester
W. Barker, secretary to the Divi
sion of Speech and Drama, for the
rest of this week.
