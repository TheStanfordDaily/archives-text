# ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the weekly or
gan recital in the Chapel at 4:15
o'clock today. Following is the pro
gram: Selections from the "Water
Music," G. F. Handel (Allegro Vi
vace—Air—Hornpipe) (Minuet—Al
legretto gincoso—Allegro maestoso);
Dance of the Blessed Spirits in the
Elysian Fields (from "La Vita
Nuova"), Intermezzo (from "The
Jewels of the Madonna"), Wolf-
Ferrari; Meditation at St. Clotildc,
Philip James; Andante and Finale,
Cesar Franck (from the Grande
Piece Symphonique).
