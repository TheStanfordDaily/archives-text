# OLSON WILL OPEN FAIR
##

Governor Olson's health will permit
him to appear personally, on Treas
ure Island Feb. 18 and open the
Golden Gate International Exposi
tion, the Governor's physician, Dr.
F. W. Scatena, revealed today.
