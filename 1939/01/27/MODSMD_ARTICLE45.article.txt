# Oliver to Deliver Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
"Pronounce or Perish" will be the
subject of the sermon to be given
by the Reverend Thomas E. Oliver
in the Memorial Chapel Sunday
morning. The music will include
"Deck Thyself, My Soul, with Glad
ness" by Bach, Venite, exutemus
Domino, by Allen, and "Lord
Christ, Thou Art the Prince of
Peace" by Bach.
A program by the University
Choir under the direction of War
ren D. Allen will be presented at 4
o'clock on Sunday afternoon.

Hooliganism is a term derived from
the Hooley gang in London's east end
around 1900, and implies ruffianism
and rowdyism.
