# Oliver to Deliver Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
The Reverend Thomas E. Oliver,
acting chaplain of the University,
will speak on the subject, "Life's
Three Demands," for the regular 11
o'clock Sunday morning Chapel
service.
Music will include Piece Ileroique
by Cesar Franck, Air in D by Bach,
Adagio from the Grand Symphonic
Piece by Franck, Venite Exultemus
Domino by Allen, and "From Al]
That Dwell below the Skies," by
Shumway.
There will be no organ recital
Sunday afternoon so that it will not
conflict wilh the sessions of the
Alumni Conference.
