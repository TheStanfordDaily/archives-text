# Autos Hunt Rabbits
## 
### 
SYRACUSE, Kas. (UP).—Riding
to the hounds is a favorite sport in
Western Kansas, but as often as not
the hunters make jackrabbit chases
in automobile's speeding across the
prairie, instead of on horseback.
