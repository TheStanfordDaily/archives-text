# DURAND, ROTH CLOSE CONFERENCE
## 
### 

DR. WILLIAM F. DURAND, professor emeritus of mechanical engineering, shown at left with Dr. Ilay
Lyman Wilbur, toastmastcr, closed the 1939 annual Alumni Conference at a banquet Sunday night with an
appraisal of world development in military aviation. Keynote of his speech was that the United States
should increase its air force by 3,000 military and 2,OtH) naval airplanes. At right is Almon E. Roth revealing
confidential information regarding personal experiences as head of the San Francisco Employers Associa
