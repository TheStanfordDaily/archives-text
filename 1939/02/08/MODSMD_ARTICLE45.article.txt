# SEALS SIGN GROSSMAN
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 7 (UP).
—Everett Grossman, right-handed
pitcher of the St. Paul, Minn., base
ball club, will report this spring
for a trial with the San Francisco
Club, the Seals' management an
nounced today. He won 11 and lost
13 games last year with the Wilkes-
Barre and Arriston baseball clubs.
The San Francisco club has an op
tion to purchase Grossman if he
makes good.
