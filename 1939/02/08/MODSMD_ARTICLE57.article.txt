MENS GLEE CLUB MEMBERS:

and evening rehearsals will not
be held. Double octet will rehearse
at 7:30 o'clock in preparation for
making rccodrs at 1:15 o'clock
Thursday.
STANFORD RIFLE AND PISTOL
CLUB: Rifle matches to be fired
tonight at meeting, 7:30 o'clock in
the range.
THETA SIGMA I*lll meeting down
stairs in the Women's Clubhouse at
5 o'clock this afternoon.
LUNCHEON CLUB meets nt noon
today in the Nitery.
I'RE-MED SOCIETY: Dr. Joseph
Catten, San Francisco psychiatrist,
will speak tonight at the Women's
Clubhouse. Meeting will begin at
7:30 o'clock.
EUTHENICS GROUP OF STAN
FORD DAMES will meet tonight
at 7:30 o'clock in Room 122 of
the Graduate School of Business.
BRIDGE GROUP OF STANFORD
DAMES will meet tomorrow at
2:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.
MEMBERS OF EN ON A CLUB will
meet today at 12:45 o'clock in front
of Encina for Quad pictures.

women's dormitory room and
board bills is due and payable at
the Ad Building today from 8 till
12 o'clock and from 1 to 5 o'clock.
