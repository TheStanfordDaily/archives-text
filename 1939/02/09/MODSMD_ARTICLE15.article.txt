# jobs Announced By Student Office
## 
### 
Notices of open positions yesterday
were released by Mrs. Irene Cover
ley, head of the Student Employment
Bureau.
A room and board job is open to a
senior or graduate man student. In
addition, there is a similar offer of
room and board awaiting any grad
uate woman with transportation who
wishes work assisting with house
work.
In the board vacancies, there arc
two openings for regular men hash
ers, in return for meals. The final
position calls for a man to work
regularly from 12 to .1 o'clock for
his noon meals.
