# Organist to Give Recital Today
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the regular
organ recital today at 4:15 o'clock
in the Memorial Chapel.
The program will include the
following: Hallelujah Chorus from
"The Mount of Olives"—Beethoven;
Adagio from the Sonata pathetique
—Beethoven; Romanza in A flat —
Mozart; Symphony V for organ —
C. M. Widor (Allegro cantabile.
Adagio, Toccata). The same pro
gram will be repeated Sunday after
noon.
