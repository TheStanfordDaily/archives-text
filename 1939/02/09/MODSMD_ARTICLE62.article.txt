# Five Hunters Held In Illegal Shooting
## 
### 
YAKIMA, Wash., Feb. 8 (UP).—
Five men, including Hunky Shaw,
former well-known Pacific Coast
baseball player, were arraigned be
fore Federal Commissioner Thomas
Granger on charges of violating the
Federal game laws by shooting
ducks over baited ground.
Four of the men also were charged
with shooting before legal hours:
Ray R. Randall, W. S. Doyle, Dr.
L. L. Lugar, and H. W. Mull.
