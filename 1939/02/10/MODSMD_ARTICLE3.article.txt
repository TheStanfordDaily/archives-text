# Six-ft. Snowman Cools Roble
## 
### 
Winter comes to the Farm!
A couplc of days ago a fra
ternity, conveniently situated
in the center of things, brought
back a trailer-load of snow,
nice cold snow. Entering into
the spirit of things, they set
to work bombarding passers
by, with startlingly accurate
results.
Not to be outdone, one of
the other row groups sent their
sophomore class out into the
hills. The ultimate result was
a 6-foot cylindrical snow man,
charmingly suave, on Roblt's
