# SLAYER FOUND GUILTY
## 
### 
SAN BERNARDINO, Feb. 9 (UP).
—A jury of 11 men and one woman
today found Jack Kermott, 28, cousin
of Novelist Sinclair Lewi.s, guilty of
manslaughter for the slaying last
Nov. 30 of his brother, James Ker
mott, 30.
