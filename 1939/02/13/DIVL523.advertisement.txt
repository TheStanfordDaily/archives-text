When a young man or woman aspires to tlie
Medical profession, it is necessary that a solid
groundwork of education he laid, on which to
build scientific knowledge. Alter high school, two
to four years of college preparation is required for
entrance to Medical Schools. Entrance require
ments are becoming more rigid each year.
During these pre-medical school years, the embry
onic doctor majors in studies bearing directly on
his medical training, such as Latin, chemistry,
biology, physiology and physics.
