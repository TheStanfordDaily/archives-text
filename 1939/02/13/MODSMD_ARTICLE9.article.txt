# British Film Set For Next Sunday
## 
### 
Next Sunday night's movie, "To
the Victor," will bring to Memorial
Hall the simple story of a man and
his dog. Based on the famed Brit
ish novel. "Bob, Son of Battle," the
English-made picture was filmed in
the Scottish highlands.
Leading character Adam McAdam
is a particularly shaggy old Scotch
man who has a champion shepherd
dog. Black Wool, and a beautiful
daughter.
Plot complications arise when the
new young man in the neighbor
hood, who owns a dog which threat
ens to rival Black Wool in the an
nual shepherding contest, falls in
love with McAdam's daughter.
