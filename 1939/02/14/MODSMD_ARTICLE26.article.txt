# PRISON Grant Sought
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 13 (UP) —
The Justice Department, it was
learned today, plans to ask Con
gress for approximately $200,000 to
strengthen Alcatraz Prison.

A fee of $9 is required for every
citizen's passport issued.
