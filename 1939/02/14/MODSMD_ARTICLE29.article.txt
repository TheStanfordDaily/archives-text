# Farm Foilsman Cops S.F. Honors
## 
### 
Stanford foilsman Herb Woolley
came through with flying colors in
the Men's Junior Foils Champion
ships held in San Francisco last
week-end. He tied for first place in
the preliminaries but dropped to sec
ond place in the play-offs.
Over 25 men were entered in the
matches. They represented fencing
clubs and teams from all of the East
Bay.
