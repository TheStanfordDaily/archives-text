# Site of Olympic Games Ski Tryouts
## 
### 

IT'S A LONG DISTANCE to the bottom of this ski run on Mt. Ilood,
near Portland, Ore., where contestants for national championships and
Olympic tryouts in downhill and slalom racing will race on April 1
and 2. The line traces the "Turtleneck Run," one of the longest and
steepest in the country.
