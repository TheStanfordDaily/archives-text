# Bryant to Speak At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Commander Stewart Bryant, well
known speaker on campus, will be
the guest at tomorrow's meeting of
the Luncheon Club, according to
Evelyn Jameson, program chair
man. He has spoken to many church
groups and other organizations in
the Bay Region recently and is
greatly interested in student affairs.
Commander Bryant's topic will be
"Does America's Stake in the Far
East justify Armed Conflict?"
Luncheon will be served for 25 cents.
