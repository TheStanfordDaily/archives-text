# FUEL TANKS CAUSE FINE
## 
### 
NASHUA. N.H. (UP).—An attempt
to be thrifty cost Norman C. Trum
polt, a truck driver, $10. He was
fined that sum in district court for
operating a truck "equipped with two
fuel tanks other than standard
equipment."
