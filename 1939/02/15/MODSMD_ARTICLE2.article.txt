# Roble Pig Tails Astound Encina
## 
### 
Ilair rihlions ami pigtails be
came the center of attraction
yesterday as Ruble's yearly re
hellion, (bis time aimed against
their unsuspecting brethren at
Encina, blossomed forth in full
splendor.
It apparently all came about
because the women were irked
at the current fad for "luitch"
haircuts now rampant. As
tounded frosh men stood with
jaws agape as familiar face after
familiar face passed by, pigtails
flying.
At last reports. F.ncina was
planning sweet revenge. Dans
last night ran the gamut from
side-burns and beards to short
pants and garters.
