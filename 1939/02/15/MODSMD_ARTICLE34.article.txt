# FRANKIAN NAMEO COACH
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 14 (UP).—
Ike Frankian, a former St. Mary's
College All-American end who has
been playing football for 21 years,
today was named head coach of the
independent Los Angeles Bulldog
professional team, succeeding Elmer
(Gus) Henderson.

WASHINGTON, Feb. 14 (UP).—
The United States Navy began its
annual maneuvers yesterday with
the greatest peace-time concentra
tion of fighting strength in the Carib
bean Sea.
