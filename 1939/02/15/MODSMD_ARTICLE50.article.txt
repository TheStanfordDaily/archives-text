# Men Wear Ski Clothes to Class
## 
### 
Men have been wearing such
comfortable clothes skiing that they
couldn't take them off when they
came down from the mountains. As
a result they are wearing some of
their ski clothes to class. The trail
cloth jackets proved so successful
in shedding water and wind that the
men wear them all the time and

j the idea was borrowed by women.
All sorts of woolen mufflers, ban
| danas, and ties are being worn to
: keep out the drafts. Warm under
j wear in the form of jockey shorts
I made with long legs have proved
j popular with the men. T-shirts are
| good to wear under the trail cloth
! jackets.
