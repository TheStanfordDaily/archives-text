# GATE SPAN IN RED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 15 (UP).
—The Golden Gate Bridge lost $82,-
084 for the six months ended Dec. 31,
IS3B, in operating expenses, William
Hadler, chairman of the finance com
mittee, told the bridge directors to
day. He said loss for the fiscal year
would total $150,000.
