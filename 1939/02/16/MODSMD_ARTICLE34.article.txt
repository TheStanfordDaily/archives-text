# Frosh Swim Stars To Enter Meet
## 
### 
Three members of the sensational.
Card frosh swimming team will en
ter, unattached, the Pacific Associa
tion Indoor Campionships to be held
at the Hayward Plunge in Hayward,
California, tonight at 8 o'clock.
Those entering will be Paul Her
ron, whose performances so far this
season have been almost phenome
nal; Scott Dudley, ace frosh breast
stroker; and Gardiner Miller, who
has been a prominent sprinter. All
three will be up against tough cham
pionship competition from former
college stars.
