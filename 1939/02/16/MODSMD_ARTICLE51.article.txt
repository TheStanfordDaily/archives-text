# WINGS CLIPPED
## 
### 
BOSTON, Mass., Feb. 15 (UP).—
A pair of third-period goals by
Bobby Bauer gave the first-place
Boston Bruins a 2-1 victory over the
Detroit Red Wings in a National
Hockey League game before 13,000 in
the Boston Garden last night.
