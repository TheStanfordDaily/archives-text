# JEWS BOMBED
## 
### 
JERUSALEM, Feb. 15 (UP).—Nine
Jews were killed today when a
bomb exploded under a Jewish bus
at Nessziona, Jewish colony near
Tel-Aviv.

taken to pipe smoking during their
examination week.
