# Battery 'A' Wins R.O.T.C. Pennant
## 
### 
The R.O.T.C. Efficiency Pennant
was awarded yesterday to "A" Bat
tery of the Stanford R.O.T.C. unit.
This award is made every month to
the battery showing the best record
for neatness and general appearance
at drill.
First year basic students of all
batteries have now completed their
gunner's examinations with the ex
ception of "C" Battery, which will
finish its examination this week. 1
