# Schoop Tickets Available at Rates
## 
### 
Student rates of $1 for orchestra
seats are available for Trudi Sehoop
concerts in San Francisco if ordered
through Margaret Jewell at the
Women's Gym. The comic ballet is
to be at the Curran Theater from
February 20 to 26. with matinees on
the 25th and 26th.
Orders for tickets must be made
before 1 o'clock on Friday for Feb
ruary 20 to 23, and those for Feb
ruary 24 to 26 must be ordered be
fore 3 o'clock on the 21st.
