# 'Diz' Honored for Mound Courage
## 
### 

PROUDLY DISPLAYING a plaque which terms him the "most
courageous athlete of 1938." is Jerome (Dizzy) Dean, above, ace
pitcher for the Chicago Cubs. Awarded to him for his outstanding
pitching against the Pittsburgh Pirates, the plaque was presented to
the Hurler by the Philadelphia Sports Writers Association at their
annual diiuicr recently.
