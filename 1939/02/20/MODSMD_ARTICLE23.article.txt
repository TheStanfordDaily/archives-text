# Peyton Appointed Attache to Berlin
## 
### 
Colonel Bernard R. Peyton, head
of the Stanford Reserve Officers
Training Corps unit, has been made
military attache to the U. S. embas
sy in Berlin.
This will be Colonel Peyton's sec
ond assignment in Germany, as he
was attached to the Army of Occu
pation in Germany following the
World War. The transfer becomes
effective in June.
The head of the R.O.T.C. unit has
been on the Farm since 1935, when
he replaced Brigadier General Don
ald Cubbison.
