# THOUSANDS STRICKEN BY FLOODS
## 
### 

FED BY MOUNTAIN streams, the flood-burdened Ohio River spreads over lowland areas from Cincinnati
toward the Mississippi, bringing the menace of influenza and pneumonia to more than 250,000 persons. A
family is shown above leaving its home on the outskirts of Cincinnati as the Ohio creeps steadily higher.
The lied Cross estimates that 10.000 families in five states have been made homeless.
