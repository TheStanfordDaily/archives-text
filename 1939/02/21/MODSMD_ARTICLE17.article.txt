# Gorin to Appear On Concert Series
## 
### 
Igor Gorin will appear on the
Stanford Concert Series March 8 in
stead of March 1, as the concert, was
originally scheduled on season tick
ets. The young baritone has be
come popular and famous .since his
American concert debut only last
year in the Hollywood Bowl; and al
though he prefers the concert stage,
sings over the radio and in the
movies.
Born in the Ukraine, the singer
received his musical education in
Austria and scored his first operatic
successes in Czechoslovakia, but he
feels that America presents the best
opportunity today for serious music
and musicians.
