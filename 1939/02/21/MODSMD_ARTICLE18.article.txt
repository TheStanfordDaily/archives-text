# Toyon, Branner Hold Dance Friday
## 
### 
Toyon and Branner men will be
hosts at an informal dance to be
held this Friday evening from 9 to 1
o'clock in the Toyon lobby, it was
announced yesterday.
Lee Scarlett's orchestra will play
for the dance, which is free for resi
dents of the two halls. Admission
for all others is 75 cents a couple.
