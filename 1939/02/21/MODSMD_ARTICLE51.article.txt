# Norris to Speak At Peace Confab
## 
### 
Kathleen Norris, novelist, will
speak in the evening session of the
Palo Alto Peace Conference to be
held tomorrow afternoon and eve
ning at the Palo Alto Community
Center. Y.W.C.A., A.S.U., and
young people's groups from Palo
Alto churches will be represented by
delegations from Stanford.
Oliver Haskell, president of the
China Aid Council for the United
States, will open the conference at 2
o'clock with an address, after which
he will lead a round table discus- >
sion group.
Topics for discussion are: "Far
East Situation," "Germany's Rela
tions with America," "Good Neigh- :
bor Policy," "American Neutrality," j
and "Armaments." Following din
ner, the conference will draw up
resolutions on the conclusions of the
meeting which will be sent to mem
bers of Congress and others.
