# Pre-med Students Will Hear Dr. Lee
## 
### 
Dr. Russell Lee, of Palo Alto, will
i .speak on "How to Make a Fortune
in Medicine" at the regular meeting
of the Pre-Medical Society in the
downstairs lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse tonight at 7:30 o'clock.
According to Sam Woolington,
president of the society, arrange
ments have been made with Dr. Leo
! Stanley, head physician at San
Quentin, for trips to the prison.
Definite plans for these trips will be
made tonight.
