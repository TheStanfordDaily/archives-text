# LONDON GETS NEW BOMB SHELTERS
## 
### 

TAKING NO CHANCES, the common people of Europe hope that war won't come this spring, as pre
dicted by many, but are preparing for it nevertheless. Above is shown one of the newest type steel-lined
bomb shelters in London, built for use as a first-aid and dressing station underground in ease of air raids.
Shelters are now available to private individuals or communities, as part of the defense plans established
by the government.
