# Theta Sig to Hear Margaret Craven
## 
### 
Margaret Craven, well known
short story writer, will speak in
formally to members of Theta Sigma
Phi, honorary and professional fra
ternity for women in journalism, at
7:15 o'clock tonight in Casa Adelfa
at Lagunita Court. Despite her
youth, Miss Craven is ranked among
the most popular authors in Amer
ica.
This is the first of a series of in
formal talks by women in journal
ism which will be sponsored by
Thcta Sigma Phi. All those inter
ested are invited to attend tonight's
discussion.
