# HOLLYWOOD EARTHQUAKE
## 
### 
_ LOS ANGELES. Feb. 23 (UP).—A
50-mile area, including Hollywood,
was shaken by an earthquake today.
The tremors were felt in Hollywood,
Lancaster, Bakersfield and the San
Fernando Valley.
