# Varsity Cindermen Win Four Events
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity trackmen cap
tured four firsts, a second, and two
third places in last Saturday's in
door cinder meet held on Treasure
Island.
Sid Hayes and Dick Simpson took
one two in the GO-yard high hurdles
in that order; Russ Wulff captured
the high jump after a prolonged bat
tle; Bill Wohle outshone the field in
the shot with a toss of 50 feet 5
inches; and Ernie Clark took a two
yard decision over Cal's Widel in
the 600-yard run.
Tom Montgomery got a third in
the shot, and the Indian relay team
took third in its event to round out
the Cardinal points for the evening.
