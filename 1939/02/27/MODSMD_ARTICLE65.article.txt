# TRAIN CRUSHES MAN
## 
### 
TACOMA, Wash., Feb. 2G (UP).—
Donald F. Parks, 20, Port Huron,
Mich., was killed early today when
he fell under a freight train while
attempting to board it while it was
switching.
During 1037, the Dominion of
Canada produced 32 per cent more
pig iron than during the preceding
year.
