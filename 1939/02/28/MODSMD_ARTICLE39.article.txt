# Badminton Expert To Talk Tomorrow
## 
### 
Badminton will be the subject of
the Spectator Sports Class when it
meets tomorrow at 7:15 o'clock in
the Education Building Auditorium.
Leslie Nichols, prominent Badmin
ton expert, will speak.
Mr. Nichols is president of the
California Badminton Association, a
member of the board of directors of
the American Badminton Association,
and was the initial organizer of the
Palo Alto Badminton Club. Movies
of experts will be shown and ex
plained.
