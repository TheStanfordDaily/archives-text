# 'Y' GROUP TO ENTERTAIN
## 
### 
Members of the Y.W.C.A Fire
side Musicale group will meet this
afternoon at 4:30 o'clock in the
rooms of the Women's Clubhouse.
According to announcement made
yesterday by Annemarie Steinbiss,
committee chairman, everyone can
bring his own favorite records. Re
freshments will be served.
