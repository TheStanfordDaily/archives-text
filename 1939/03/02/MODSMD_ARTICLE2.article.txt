# Daily Tryoutees To Hear Sheldon
## 
### 
Harry Sheldon, editor of The
Daily, will speak to the Daily try
outees who will meet today for the
last time at 4:30 o'clock this after
noon in Room 8 of the Journalism
Department.
Those who have not attended any
meetings, or who have not. com
pleted the regular assignments and
taken the exams, c;in still try out.
These people should see Jean Halli
burton in the Daily Shack tomorrow
from 1 to 2 o'clock.
