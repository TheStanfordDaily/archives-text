# Stanford Dames To Hear Terman
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold a party
tonight at 7:30 o'clock at the home
of Mrs. Grayson Kefauver, 672
Mirada. A talk by Dr. Lewis M.
Terman will be the main event of
the evening, followed by refresh
ments
Dr. Terman will speak on his
book, "Psychological Factors in
Marital Happiness," which the eu-
thenics group has been studying.
Since the group discussed several
sections of the book at the last
meeting, Dr. Terman's talk will hold
special interest.
