# INDIA RIOT KILLS FOUR
## 
### 
RANGOON, Burma, March 1 (UP).
—Four persons were killed and more
than 50 wounded in Hindu-Moslem
rioting. Police opened fire on the
rioters. All Indian-owned business
premises were closed.
