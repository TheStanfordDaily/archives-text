# Sportsmen Wear Contrasting Colors
## 
### 
For sports wear, the courageous
man, according to confidential re
ports, is blossoming oul in a num
ber of hues. One correspondent
described a male beachcomber
wearing a rococo outfit of powder
blue slacks and canary yellow
shirt.
For golf a moccasin shoe with
white top and smoked calf lower is
being worn generally. One piece of
leather goes around the foot as in a
real moccasin and makes a very
comfortable shoe.
