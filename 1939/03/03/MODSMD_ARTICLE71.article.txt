# Skirt Hangers Save On Cleaners Bills
## 
### 
Skirt hangers (the men call (heni
pants hangers) may be a nuisance,
but they cause many a deep-dyed
villainous wrinkle to disappear and
are an important saver on cleaning
and pressing bills. Lining the floor
of the closet with paper makes it
easier to sweep and actually keeps
some of the dust out.
It really doesn't take much time,
and a happy environment for your
clothes does wonders for their psy
chological reaction to you!
