# Easterners Wear New Cord Uniform
## 
### 
The uniform of the well-dressed
Harvardian, according to special re
ports from the East, this year has
a coat of corduroy with leather but
tons, side vents and slanting pock
ets.
With this jacket, flannel slacks
(they call therti bags) are worn, and
reversed calf shoes are high in pop
ularity with this combination.
Princetonians are wearing a new
version of the Tyrolean hat which
comes to a sharper point at the
crown and has a larger cord.
