# Farm Grad Wins Liege Fellowships
## 
### 
Word has been received here that
Richard J. Winzler, a graduate of
Stanford, Class of 1936, has been
awarded a Commission for Relief
in Belgium Fellowship which will
enable him to study general phys
iology at the University of Liege.
The fellowship carries a stipend and
traveling allowances of about $2000
for the academic year.
Winzler, who received his Ph.D.
degree here last year, is now a
Sterling Research Fellow in Phys
iology at the Yule University School
of Medicine.
