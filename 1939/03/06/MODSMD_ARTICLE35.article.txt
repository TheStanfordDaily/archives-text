# POLICE RETURN PIGEON
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, March 5 (UP).—
A pigeon named "Peaches" came
home—in a police car. Peaches flut
tered to the around on a down town
street corner just as a radio car
drew up The officers retrieved
her, found the name of her owner
under a wing.
