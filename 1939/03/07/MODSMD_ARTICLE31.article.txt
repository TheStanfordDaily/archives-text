# NEGRO IN L.A. MURDER
## 
### 
SEATTLE, March G (UP). —Seattle
police today searched for a negro,
self-styled as ihe "Black Magnet,"
for questioning in connection with
the death of Anya Sosoyeva, Los An
geles dancer, who was killed on the
darkened Los Angeles City College
campus the night of Feb. 24.
