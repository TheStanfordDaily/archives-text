# Gonzaga U. Signs New Grid Coach
## 
### 
SPOKANE, Wash., March 6 (UP).
John (Puggy) Hunton, Gonzaga
high schol football coach for 10
years, was named varsity coach to
day succeeding Mike Pecarovich,
new Loyola University coach at Los
Angeles.
At the same time the Rev. Leo J.
Robinson, S. J., president of Gon
zaga University, appointed Claude
McGrath as director of athletics. Mc-
Grath had been basketball and
frosh football coach at Gonzaga for
several years.
Puggy Hunton, former Gonzaga
grid star, had a remarkable record
as high school football coach. His
teams were said to have a monopoly
on the city championships.
