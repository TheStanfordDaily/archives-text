# Stojkovich Elected Axe Society Head
## 
### 
Andy Stojkovich was elected
president of Axe Society at its
luncheon meeting at the Zete house
yesterday. At the same time Gene
Coldiron was unanimously voted
vice-president.
Stojkovich, who will occupy the
oflice for the remainder of the aca
demic year, succeeds Chuck Prince,
and Coldiron replaces Larry Living
ston. Bob Richardson will retain his
post as secretary of the society. Pre
liminary plans for the annual Stu
dent-Faculty Field Day, which will
be held on Thursday, April 13, were
discussed at the meeting.
