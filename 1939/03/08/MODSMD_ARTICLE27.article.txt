# Rifle Team to Hold Postal Matches
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity rifle team will
shoot intercollegiate postal matches
this week against Boston University,
Carnegie Tech, Georgia Tech, Uni
versities of Florida, Pennsylvania,
Maine, and Kansas. Scores may be
fired tonight at the regular 7:30
o'clock meeting or Friday night.
Members of the Women's Team
are to practice as much as pos
sible to be prepared for future
postal and shoulder-to-shoulder
matches.
