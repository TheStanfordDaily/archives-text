# Luncheon Group To Hear Hopkins
## 
### 
William Stephen Hopkins, assist
ant professor of economics, will be
the speaker at today's meeting of
the Luncheon Club, according to
Evelyn Jameson, program chair
man.
Mr. Hopkins will speak upon "Ag
ricultural Labor Problems." He is
well known in this field from his
statements on the migratory worker
problems. Luncheon Club will meet
as usual at noon in the Nitery, and
luncheon will be served for 25
cents.
