# Interfraternity Confab in S.F.
## 
### 
Guest speaker at the luncheon of
the regional committee of the Na
tional Interfratemity Conference, to
be held at the Fly Trap restaurant
in San Francisco Friday, will be
Charles E. Thomas, assistant, general
secretary of Sigma Nu fraternity.
College fraternity men in the bay
region or any visitors in this area
were today urged to attend by W. E.
Gearhart, secretary of the commit
tee. Thomas has visited practically
every college and university in the
country and has also made studies
of European educational methods.
