# Chuck Smith Wins Encina Presidency
## 
### 
To fill the post left vacant by
Gordy McDonald, Chuck Smith was
elected president of Encina last
night by a preferential vote of 38-
21. Other candidates in the order of
votes received were Frank Boutin,
Ollie Hamlin, Irv Hellman, und Pete
Kingsbury.
The election was held in conjunc
tion with a smoker at which Coach
Everett Dean spoke to the frosh
about midwest basketball and the
coming U.S.C.-Cal play-ofT.
