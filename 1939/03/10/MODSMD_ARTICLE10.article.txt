# Secrest to Play At Frosh Frolic
## 
### 
With the signing of Ozzie Secrest's
band, plans for the Frosh Frolic
with Cal this Saturday are nearly
completed. The day's program in
cludes water sports in the afternoon,
a barbecue, and a three-hour dance
at Encina.
Tickets for the barbecue and dance
will be handed out at Roble and
Encina today.
