# Roble Plans Dance For County Fair
## 
### 
Roble's contribution to the County
Fair Wednesday afternoon will be a
dance in Ihe Nitery from 2 to 5
o'clock. For 5 cents a dance, stu
dents are invited to come and dance
to the latest recordings.
Pat Adams, chairman of the af
fair. urges men to bring their dates.
For lho.se who do not, however,
there will be Roble hostesses to act
as dance partners.
