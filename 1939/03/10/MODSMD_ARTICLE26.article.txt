# LARKIN MEETS BEAUHULD
## 
### 
NEW YORK. April 9 (UP). -
Lightweights feature the current na
tional boxing program this week.
At Newark tomorrow Tippy Larkin
of Garfield, N.J., and Billy Beauhuld,
from Jersey City, clash in a 10-
round return bout. Larkin won the
first via the kayo route and may get
a crack at Henry Armstrong if he
repeats.
