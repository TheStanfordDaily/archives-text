# Junior Prom Bids Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Bids for the Junior Prom, Friday,
April 22, will go on sale today at the
A.S.S.U. office. At the same time
junior class representatives will sell
tickets in all men's living groups,
according to Barney Ferguson,
chairman in charge of bids.
Price for the Prom is $3.50. Co
chairmen Bob Richardson and Jane
LeCutler are arranging special en
tertainment for the affair, which will
feature George Olsen and his or
chestra.
Present plans call for formal dress,
but President Ken Cuthbertson has
called a meeting of the Junior Class
executive council tomorrow eve
ning at 7:15 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse to consider changing it
to semi-formal. Representatives of
40 houses, clubs, and halls will vote
to determine whether campus de
mand warrants the change.
