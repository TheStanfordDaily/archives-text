# Time Change Noted In Symphony Class
## 
### 
Music 106, The Modern Symphony
Orchestra, due to an oversight, was
listed in the Time Schedule under
Art and Archeology as meeting at
9 o'clock Tuesdays and Thursdays.
It is correctly listed under Music,
however, and will be given at 11
o'clock Tuesdays and Thursdays and
at 7:30 o'clock Wednesday evenings
in the auditorium of the Education
Building.
