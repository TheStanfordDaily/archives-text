# Steinbeck Club Will Visit Camps
## 
### 
Mary Beaton was elected presi
dent of the Steinbeck Club at the
end of winter quarter to succeed
Otis Brubaker, retiring president.
At the same meeting plans were
made for a trip early Ibis quarter
to one of the Federal migratory
labor camps in the state. This will
be the first of a series of such trips,
it was decided.
Forums and panel discussions of
various aspej's of the migratory
labor problem also are on the club's
program for the quarter. Officers
hope to persuade John Steinbeck
to address a Stanford audience.
