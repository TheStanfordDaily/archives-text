# Chandler to Talk To Med Society
## 
### 
At the first spring quarter meet
ing of the Stanford Pre-Medical So
ciety, to be held tonight in the down
stairs lounge of the Woman's Club
house at 7:30 o'clock, Dr. Loren
Roscoe Chandler, dean of the Stan
ford School of Medicine, will speak
on "Medical Education in the United
States."
Dr. Chandler has also consented
to present his views on the future
of the thousands of pre-medical
students in the United States who
are rejected from medical schools
each year, according to President
Sam Woolington.
A short business meeting will
proceed Dr. Chandler's talk, and an
election will be held to select offi
cers for the coming quarter.
