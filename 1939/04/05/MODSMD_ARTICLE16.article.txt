# Morgan to Lead First Singestunde
## 
### 
Dr. Bayard Q. Morgan, recently
returned from a trip in Germany,
will conduct this quarter's first Ger
man singestunde tomorrow from
4:30 to 5:30 o'clock. The singestunde
will be held in the German house
across from the Education Building.
Refreshments will be served.
Professor K. F. Reinhardt of the
Department of Germanic Languages
requests that all students interested
in taking part in the production of
two German one-act plays register
in German Cl6l. This class in Ger
man dramatics meets tomorrow at
4:15 o'clock in Room 119, German
department.
