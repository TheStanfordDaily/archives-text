# Flying Club Wins Loening Trophy
## 
### 
The Grover Loening Trophy,
awarded annually to the outstand
ing college flying group in the coun
try, has been awarded to the Stan
ford Flying Club for the second
lime, according to Associated Press
reports.
The trophy winner is determined
each year on the basis of flying
achievement during the year and
points gained in intercollegiate com
petition. The trophy, won last year
by the Stanford flyers, was dis
played last spring in the Union tro
phy case, Dick Williams, president
of the club, reported.
