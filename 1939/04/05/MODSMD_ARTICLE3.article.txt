# Weatherbe Sets Canoeing Hours
## 
### 
The hours for canoeing and
swimming on Lake Lagunita
this quarter are from 10 to 12
every morning, from 1 to 6 in
the afternoons, and from 7 to
10 at night, according to Hal
Weatherbe, head life guard.
This schedule applies every
day of the week, except that as
yet no rule has been made con
cerning Saturday night. This
Saturday night, the lake will
be closed to canoers.
