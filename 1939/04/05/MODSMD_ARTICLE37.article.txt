# RIOTING IN MEXICO
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, April 4 (UP). —
Anti-Nationalist rioting broke out
tonight in front of the Casino Es
panol, chief Spanish club in Mexico,
whose windows were shattered by a
volley of stones and groups of
workers who drove to the building
in taxicabs.
