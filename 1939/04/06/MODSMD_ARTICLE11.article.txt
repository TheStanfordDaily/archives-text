# Spring Hall Dance Set for April 14
## 
### 
"Spring" will be the theme of the
Toyon-Branncr open dance to be
held Friday, April 14, from 9 to 1
o'clock in Toyon according to Dick
Lemmon, chairman of the dance
committee. Frank Wells and his
twelve-piece orchestra and girl vo
calist will provide the music for the
occasion.
Bids for the dance will sell for
$1.50, and the dress will be semi
formal. Patrons for the evening will
be Dr. and Mrs. Harry J. Rathbun
and Dr. and Mrs. Harold M. Bacon.
Members of the dance committee are
Jack Wallace, Karl Thursby, Bill
Walker, Carl Fisher, Lee Solomon,
and Dan Cameron.
