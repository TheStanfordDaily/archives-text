# Drama Tryouts Set for April 10
## 
### 
Open tryouts for two productions,
soon to be produced by the Division
of Spet*h and Drama, were an
nounced yesterday.
First of these is the Restoration
comedy, "Shoemaker's Holiday,"
which will be presented April 27, 20
in the Memorial Hall. The tryouts
will be held Monday, April 10, in
the ittle Theater from 2 to 5
o'clock. Parts for four women and
sixteen men will be cast.
Second production will be Hans
Toller's "No More Peace," which
will be presented May 11, 12, 13.
Tryout date will be announced later.
