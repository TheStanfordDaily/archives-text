# RAMSHEAD TRYOUTS
## 
### 
Tryouts for the reading of the
"Royal Family of Broadway," writ
ten by George Kaufman and Edna
P'erber, will be held again this after
noon at 1 o'clock in the conference
room above the Memorial Theater.
These tryouts arc open to all stu
dents and those already having
points in Ramshead arc particularly
urged to come.
