# MURDERER TO HANG
## 
### 
ALTURAS, April 6 (UP). —Supe-
rior Judge Ben V. Curler today set
Friday, June 30. for the hanging of
Harry French, member of a Modoc
County publishing family, convicted
of the shotgun "newspaper murder"
of Claude McCracken, a rival pub
lisher.
