# Glee Club Starts Concert Practice
## 
### 
A heavy rehearsal schedule, ac
cording to Herbert Wooley, director
of the Men's Glee Club, is in order
for that organization in preparation
for their annual spring concert to be
held April 22.
No definite schedule of rehearsal
has been set but rehearsal dates will
be made to suit the activities of the
members. Wooloy also announced
that there are places for men sing
ers in the glee club. No schedule of
glee club events has been prepared
but many activities are in the em
bryo stages.
