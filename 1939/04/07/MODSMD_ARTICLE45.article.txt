# CARD POLOISTS TO MEET BRITISH
## 
### 

HORSEMEN OF THE CRACK BRITISH squad from the Ilurlingham Club battle the Farm's polo team to
morrow afternoon. Above is shown a bit of action in a recent game between a California team and the
English outfit. Robert Skene, right, slated to captain the British squad in tomorrow's match, battles for
the ball with Erie Pedley of California, as Hesketh Hughes, another Britisher, tomes upon the left.
