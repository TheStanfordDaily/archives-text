# INVENTOR PROTESTS APPOINTMENT
## 
### 

\FTER YEARS OF FIGHTING Mayor Frank Hague of Jersey City. N.J., Samuel AV. Rushinore, inventor,
inds appointment of Hague's son to state's Court of Appeals too much. In protest, he's cutting down the
1200 trees on his Plain field estate, intends to raze mansion and quit New Jersey forever. Inset shows "For
sale" sign and placard reading "Because Hague Is the Law."
