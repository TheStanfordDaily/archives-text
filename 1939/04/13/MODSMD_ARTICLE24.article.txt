# COUNTY FAIR 'SUCCESS'
## 
### 
Bob Moyle's auctioneering call,
"It's for Charity," was answered
yesterday to the tune of $61.64 as
an auction of about 50 articles con
cluded the Women's Conference
County Fair. Annabelle Clark, gen
eral chairman of the County Fair,
last night pronounced it a success,
although she did not yet have figures
on the income of booths, fashion
show, or general admission.
