# Bureau Plans Heavy Schedule
## Manager Anticipates Busy Spring Season 
### 
With typical spring .quarter en
thusiasm, Bob Wood, new manager
of the Stanford Speakers Bureau, is
looking forward to one of the most
prosperous periods in the history of
the organization.
With many engagements already
scheduled for this quarter, Wood
intends to continue the plan of
personal contact with different
clubs and organizations to obtain
speaking opportunities.
The Hull reciprocal trades acts
were debated recently by Wood and
Phil Evans at the joint meeting of
the San Jose Kiwanis Club and the
Visiting Farmers.
Pat Layton has been appointed
the new junior manager to assist
Wood, and Lois Whitmore will be
an assistant in the office.
