# Chaplain Flays Smug Attitude
## 
### 
"The struggle to attain a goal is,
in itself, of more value to the indi
vidual than the actual attainment of
that goal." stated Rev. Thomas E.
Oliver, in his sermon yesterday.
Basing his address on a passage
from the Book of Hebrews, the act
ing chaplain of the University de
nounced the attitude of the average
person in Ihe modern world is that
"nothing can be done about the evils
that exist at present."
