# Card Rifle Squad Outshoots U.S.F.
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity rifle team
won by a score of 1699 to 1608 in
Friday night's shoulder to shoulder
match with the University of San
Francisco, held in the Stanford
Range. The five high men on the
Card team were Bill Giessert, Bud
Cameron, Scott Seitz, Joe Thomp
son, and Ralph Thompson.
The spring beach party at Pesca
dero will be held Sunday, April 23.
All members planning to attend
must sign up on the range front
door any time before Wednesday
night.
