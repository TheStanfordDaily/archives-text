# Arizona Poloists Down Cards, 11-4
## 
### 
Driving through five goals in the
first chukker, the University of Ari
zona polo squad defeated the Cardi
nal horsemen 11 to 4 last Friday
afternoon. Stanford won the first
game of the series 7-6 earlier in
the week.
Arizona's Wildcats never relin
quished the lead of the slow match
marred by 11 fouls. Steve Downey
made three of Stanford's four goals.
Mosse was high man for Arizona
with four.
The Farm team leaves for Los An
geles Thursday to meet U.S.C. in a
two-game series.
