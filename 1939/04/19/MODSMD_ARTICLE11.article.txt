# Glee Clubs Return For Home Concert
## 
### 
The Men's and Women's Glee
Clubs will return to the Farm on
Saturday night for their AnrTual
Home Concert. Under the direction
of George Knoles, history instructor
and club leader, the combined chor
uses have just completed a series of
radio broadcasts and guest concerts
at other universities.
Robert Ross will contribute to
the staging of the concert with color
backgrounds for each number.
The program will begin at 8:15
o'clock in the Memorial Hall. Tick
ets may be obtained at the A.S.S.U.
office in the Old Union and at Kee
ble's in Palo Alto. Student tickets
are 40 cents and general admission
is 50 cents.
