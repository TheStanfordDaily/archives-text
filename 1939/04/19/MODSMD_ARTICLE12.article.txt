# BIG MEET TICKETS
## 
### 
Rooter's tickets for the Big Meet
with California will be on sale at
the B.A.C. today and tomorrow.
The price for students is 50 cents;
for general admission, $1.10. Also
on sale on these same days are 300
10-cent tickets for the lacrosse
match between a Canadian team and
a San Francisco pick-up outfit.
