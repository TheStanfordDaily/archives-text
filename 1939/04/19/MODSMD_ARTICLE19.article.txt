# Luncheon Club To See Movies
## 
### 
Forty minutes of movies of a
school fit Yen Ching in Peking,
China, will be shown by Dr. Helen
Pryor, women's medical adviser, at
the regular meeting of the Luncheon
Club at noon today.
The pictures have to do with the
founding of a Christian college in
North China, and were taken with
in the last two or three years. The
school is built on the site of the
old summer palace of the dowager
empress.
Luncheon Club v/ill meet in the
Nitcry with lunchcon being served
to the public for 23 cent*.
