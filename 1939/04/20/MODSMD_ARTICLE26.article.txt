# Allen to Present Peace Day Recital
## 
### 
In keeping with the Peace Day ac
tivities, Warren D. Allen will pre
sent an organ recital today devoted
to peace day music. The program
will be held in the Memorial
Church at 4:15 o'clock.
The program will consist of Gloria
Domini by T. T. Noble, Funeral
March by Beethoven, Chorale in E
Major by Franck, Prelude in D Flat
by Chopin, and a Prayer for Peace
by Paul Held.
