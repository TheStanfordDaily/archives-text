# Dr. Pryor Shows Chinese Film
## 
### 
Dr. Helen Pryor, women's medical
adviser, was yesterday's speaker at
Luncheon Club showing movies of a
Christian College in North China.
Sccnes of particular interest
showed campus life at Yenching
University of Peiping. This is the
only college that has been able to
carry on in territory occupied by the
Japanese.
The film, which was taken within
the last two or three years, was
shown by Dr. Pryor through the
auspices of the Organization of Chi
nese Christian Colleges.
