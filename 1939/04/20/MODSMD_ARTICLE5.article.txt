# Parts Still Open In New Production
## 
### 
Finals tryouts for "No More
Peace," by Hans Toller, to be pre
sented May 11, 12, and 13, will be
held tomorrow in the Little Theater
from 2 to 5 o'clock.
No parts have been cast as yet,
and men are especially requested to
turn out as there are about 15 male
characters in the play, according to
YValdemar Johans<;n, director.
