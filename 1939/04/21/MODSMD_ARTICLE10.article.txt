# Dave Leff Wins Peace Essay Prize
## 
### 
For his essay on "War at Stan
ford," Dave LefT yesterday was
awarded a $5 merchandise order at
the Bookstore as first prize in Peace
Council's second annual essay con
test.
Judged by Dr. Hardin Craig, head
of the School of Letters, Dr. Philip
Buck, professor of political science,
and Harry Sheldon. Daily editor, the
contest was managed by Kathleen
Norris and Dwight Hutchinson,
members of Peace Council.
