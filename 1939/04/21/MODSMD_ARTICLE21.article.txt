# Labor Board Asks Court Enforcement
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 20 (UP).
—The National Labor Relations
Board today asked the Ninth Cir
cuit Court of Appeal here to order
the Sunshine Mining Company of
Wallace, Idaho, to comply with the
NLRB order issued June 20, 1038.
The Labor Board had ordered the
company to recognize the Interna
tional Union of Mine, Mill and
Smelter Workers of the Congress of
Industrial Organizations; withdraw
recognition of the Big Creek Indus
trial Union, an independent; rein
state workers discharged after a
1937 strike give the discharged
workers back pay; and enter into
collective bargaining with the C.1.0.
affiliate.
