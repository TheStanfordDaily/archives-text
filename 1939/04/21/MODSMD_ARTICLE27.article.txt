# Angry Fan Stabs 'Man Mountain'
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 20 (UP).-
A fan enraged by his rough tactics
jabbed a knife into Man Mountain
Dean, bearded wrestler, last night at
Olympic Auditorium as be fought
his way to his dressing room.
The knife cut a three-inch gash
in Dean's right arm, and sent him
roaring after his assailant through
the angry crowd blocking the aisle.
Six men finally stopped Dean.
