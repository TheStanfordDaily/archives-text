# Hurst to Compete For Golf Crown
## 
### 
Nancy Hurst, Lagunita junior,
moves into the finals today in the
California State Women's Golf
Tournament, after defeating Mrs.
Lamborn in her semifinals match
yesterday, 3 and 2.
Miss Hurst will meet defending
women's champion Mrs. Lafir on the
Lakeside Country Club course in
San Francisco.
