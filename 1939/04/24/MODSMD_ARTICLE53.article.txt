# TAKE A CHANCE
## 
### 
At a dance at Cal last week-end, everyone was
given a number as he went in and told that later in
the evening there would be a drawing and the man
holding the winning number would get a prize and
his girl would be dubbed sweetheart of the dance.
It was a nice party. Everyone got tight like owls.
Particularly one guy.
When the winning number was announced, he
fished in his pocket and pulled out a stub. That it was
his coat check arfd not the card for the drawing that
he had pulled out made no difference to him. It had
the winning number on it, and that was enough.
Coat check in hand, he wobbled up to the band
platform. He showed them the number.
"You my good fellow, are the winner," they told
him. They gave him the prize and called his girl
sweetheart.
He was very happy.
tOnly he says he had a boll of a time getting his
coat with the ticket from the drawing.
