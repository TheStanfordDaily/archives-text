# FARM HOLDS ANNUAL PEACE DAY
## 
### 

OSWALD GARRISON VILLARD, famed liberal editor, was the principal speaker last Thursday when
Stanford observed Peace Day with an all-day program, including a University assembly, a lunchcon in
the Union courtyard ,and round-table discussions in the afternoon. Mr. Villard is shown in top photo
graph talking with Bob Livingston, chairman of the Peace Council. Lower left, Dr. Harold.C. Brown leads
one of the discussion groups; lower right, Mr. Villard is shown speaking at the assembly. (Photos by
Dudman)
