# Mississippi Film Will Be Shown
## 
### 
The importance of the Mississippi
River to the United States will be
explained in a motion picture, "The
River," tomorrow night at 8 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
The film, a documentary presenta
tion of the geography and life of the
river, will be presented by the Stein
beck Committee.
Milen Dempster, manager of the
Wast ley Federal migratory labor
camp, will speak on the organization
of Federal labor camps prior to pre
sentation of the picture. Mr. Demp
ster has worked at Federal camps
for four years, and is at present co
operating with the Steinbeck Com
mittee in suggesting camps which
arc of interest to visitors.
