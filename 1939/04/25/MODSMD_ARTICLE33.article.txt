# S.E.C. NOMINATION
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 24 (UP).—
Leon Henderson, economist long as
sociated with the New Deal, was
nominated today by President Roo
sevelt to be a member of the Se
curities and Exchange Commission
for the remainder of the term expir
ing June 5, 1939.
A.S.S.U.'s constitution first ap
peared in 1894.
