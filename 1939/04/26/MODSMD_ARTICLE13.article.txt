# Senior Program Sale Ends Friday
## 
###

Senior program and anounce
mcnt sale set for this Friday, it,
was announced yesterday that
all orders should he placed at
Ihe A.S.S.U. office during the
next three days.
Latest, tabulations show that
to date orders have been placed
for 191 programs and 2723 an
nouncements.
The white leather programs,
priced at $1, contain the names
of candidates for all degrees,
six photographs of the Quad, an
anouncement, a list, of com
mittees and a calendar for
Senior Week.
