# Delay Granted In Desertion Trial
## 
### 
PORTLAND, Ore., April 25 (UP).
— Circuit Judge James Stapleton to
day granted a defense request for a
three-day postponement of pleading
in the cases of three San Francisco
army deserters and their girl com
panions, accused in three indict
ments of assault and armed robbery.
Judge Stapleton set Friday as the
day for entry of pleas. Each of the
six defendants—Donald Bailoy, 27,
Edward Klaus, 21, and Vern Jensen,
24, and Jewell Smith, Edna Mail
spin and Cleone Alyea, all 17—face
triple charges of assault with intent,
to rob, assault with intent, to rob
while armed, and armed assault and
robbery.
