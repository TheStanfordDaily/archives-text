# Farm Glee Club Broadcast To Mark Radio Hour Shift
## 
### 
A musical program by the combined Stanford University Men's and
Women's Glee Clubs will be presented over station KGO at 7:15 o'clock
Wednesday evening. May as the University's weekly series inaugurates
a new schedule.
Thu glee clubs will be directed in their radio appearance by Dr. ,

George H. Knoles. <
Talks in the fields of education,
German life, medioine, and litera
ture, as well as a musical program
by the men's and women's glee
clubs, will comprise the University
radio program for May, it was an
nounced today.
Beginning May 3, when the glee
clubs are scheduled to present a 15-
minute program, the university se
ries will be heard from 7:15 to 7:30
o'clock on Wednesday Evenings, in
stead of Mondays as in the past.
The May program follows:
May 3—Stanford University Men's
and Women's Glee Clubs, directed
by Dr. George H. Knoles.
May 10—Dr. John Paul Leonard,
associate professor of education,
"Can We Measure (he Results of the
Progressive School?"
May 17 — Dr. Bayard Q. Morgan,
professor of German, "An Inside
Glimpse of Germany."
May 24—Dr. Dwight L. Wilbur,
associate clinical professor of medi
cine, "Vitamins."
May 31—Dr. George F. Sensa
baugh, assistant professor of Eng
lish, "Literature and Life."
