# CUB PITCHER OPTIONED
## 
### 
Chicago. April 26 (UP).—Pitch
er Ed Carnett, left-hander obtained
from Los Angeles, was optioned to
Milwaukee of the American Asso
ciation today by the Chicago Cubs,
He was ordered to report to the
Brewers at once.
