# Frosh Help Wanted
## 
### 
FOR the rest of the frosh who, like ourselves,
haven't experienced the traditional Stanford
spring quarter Convalescent Home Drive, we 1
think it appropriate that this column attempt to j
put over the idea that this is really one drive
that is not a bore. For every cent that is put
toward the Home we get real pleasure and en- 1
tertainment. It is hoped by the drive commit- i
tees that in 1939 the frosh will give their whole- j
hearted support to this worthy cause.
