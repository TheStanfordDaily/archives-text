# Card Glee Clubs To Sing on Radio
## 
### 
Stanford's Radio Hour will go on
a new Wednesday evening schedule
next, week when Dr. George H.
Knoles directs the Stanford men's
and women's glee clubs in a pro
gram over station KGO at 7:15
o'clock. Heretofore the Radio Hour
has been presented on Mondays.
During May, talks in the fields of
education, German life, medicine
and literature wil bo presented on
the University program. Dr. John B.
Leonard, Dr. Bayard Quincy Mor
gan, Dr. Dwight L. Wilbur, and Dr.
George F. Sensabaugh have been
scheduled to speak.
