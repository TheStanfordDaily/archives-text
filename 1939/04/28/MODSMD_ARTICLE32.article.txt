# Chapin to Speak At Palace Hotel
## 
### 
Dr. Leland T. Chapin will be the
speaker at the regular Common
wealth Club luncheon today at 12:4.0
o'clock at the Palace Hotel.
Dr. Chapin, who taught for three
years at Lingnan University in
China, will talk on "International
Propaganda." His speech will be
broadcast over station KGO.
During his travels in Europe, Dr.
Chapin studied propaganda exten
sively. While in China he inter
viewed Chiang Kai-shek and also
Borodin, the Russian propagandist.
