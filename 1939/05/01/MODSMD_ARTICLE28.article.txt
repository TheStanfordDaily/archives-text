# S.F. to Receive New Phone Books
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 30 (UP).
—The latest and largest San Fran
cisco telephone directory will be
delivered tomorrow morning by the
Pacific Telephone and Telegraph
Company. The delivery will end
Thursday.
The new green-covered book con
tains 15G.274 alphabetical listings of
San Francisco telephone subscrib
ers.
