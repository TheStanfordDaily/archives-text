# TENNIS STAR DIES
## 
### 
LONDON. April 30 (UP).—Charles
Dixon, (56, a member of the British
Davis Cup team that played against
the United States in 1909, died today
at his home here. Dixon was rated
as one of the greatest tennis stars
in British history.
