# West Coast Oil Groups Under Fire
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 1 (UP). -
Asst. Atty. Gen. Thurman Arnold
said today evidence obtained by De
partment of Justice agents would be
placed before a Grand Jury soon for
a decision whether to prosecute
major West Coast oil companies on
anti-trust charges.
The case will go before a Southern
California Grand Jury.
