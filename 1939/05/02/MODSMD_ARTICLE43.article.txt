# Derby Nominees Turn in Fast Time
## 
### 
LOUISVILLE, Ky„ May 1 (UP).
—Porters Mite and Challedon, lat
est Kentucky Derby nominees to
check in at Churchill Downs, turned
in impressive trials yesterday.
Running five-eighths of a mile on
a straightaway the 'Mite turned the
distance in :59 3-5, while Challedon,
running around turns, worked the
same distance in 1:00 fiat.
