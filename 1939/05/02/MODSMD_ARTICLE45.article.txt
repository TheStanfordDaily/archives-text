# Badminton Finals Set for Friday
## 
### 
The women's badminton tourna
ment reached the quarter finals this
week, with four couples still remain
ing in the doubles competition and
two singles matcher yet to be played.
All matches—quarter-finals, semi
finals, and finals — must be finished
by Friday, according to Connie
Crommelin, in charge of the tour
nament. The gym is available for
badminton matches any day after 5
o'clock and will be available on
Wednesday after 3 o'clock.
