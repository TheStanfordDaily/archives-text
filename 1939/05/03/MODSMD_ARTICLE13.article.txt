# Track Tickets On Sale Today
## 
### 
Tickets will go on sale at the
B.A.C. this morning at 8:30
o'clock for the Stanford-U.S.C.
track meet to be held in the
Stadium Saturday.
Ticket sale will continue from
8:30 to 5 o'clock today through
Friday. Rooters tickets are 40
cents; general admission $1.10.
Student body cards must be
presented, and only two tick
ets will be given per person.
