# MOTHERS PICKET DUMP
## 
### 
BREMERTON, Wash., May 2
(UP). —Twelve irate mothers who
picketed a garbage dump with the
aid of three card tables today re
tired in defeat. They protested
transfer of the* dump to a site in
West Bremerton, only two blocks
from the Navy Yard city school.
