# OIL SCANDAL FIGURE WEDS
## 
### 
PARIS, May 2 (UP). — Henry
Blaekmer, oil man who has been
living in France since the Teapot
Dome scandal, announced through
his lawyer today that he had been
married on Feb. 1 to Eide Norena, an
opera singer.
