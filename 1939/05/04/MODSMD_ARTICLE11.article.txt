# WEIDMAN DANCE CLASS
## 
### 
Charles Weidman, concert dancer,
will conduct lessons in modern
dance tonight at 7:30 o'clock and
tomorrow afternoon at 4:30 o'clock
in the Women's gymnasium. Mr.
Weidman's class will work in com
position, on a two-part contrapuntal
rhythmic theme. Arrangements
should be made to participate in
these lessons through Margo Crain
at Casa Ventura or the women's
gym.
