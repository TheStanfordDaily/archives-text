# Barrymore Sues Elaine for Divorce
## 
### 
NEW YORK. May 3 (UP). — The
time fol* chivalry iri closing out his
marriages is past, John Barrymore
said tonight through counsel in ex
plaining cryptically his filing of a
separation suit today against Elaine
Barrie, his fourth wife.
In a joint action naming Miss
Barrie, her mother and a man de
scribed as a "confidential adviser,"
the 57-year-old stage and screen |
actor of profile renown, accused |
them of conspiring to deprive him ]
of his property. He asked an ac
counting of $300,000 in joint funds.
