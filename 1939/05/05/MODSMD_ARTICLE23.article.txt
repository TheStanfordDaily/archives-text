# Hoop Mentors To Meet Here
## 
### 
Basketball coaches of the four
Southern Division college teams
will meet at Stanford May at 10
o'clock to decide upon dates and of
ficials for the 1940 hoop season.
The meeting, which will be at
tended by Nib.s Price, California, Ev
erett Dean, Stanford, Sam Barry,
U.S.C., and Caddy Works, U.C.L.A.,
will also decide upon the expediency
of appointing a commissioner of of
ficials.
