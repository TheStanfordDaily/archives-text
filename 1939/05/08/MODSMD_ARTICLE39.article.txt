# Van Horn, Kramer Enter Net Finals
## 
### 
SANTA BARBARA. May 6 (UP).
—Welby Van Horn and Jack Kramer
battled to the finals of the Coast
men's doubles championships today
defeating Bob Peacock and Doug
Imhoff of California, 6-0, 6-3, 6-1.
Tomorrow they face Gene Mako
and Jack Tidball, who boat Bob Un
derwood and Larry Dee, 6-1, 6-0,
6-4. Van Horn and Kramer put out
Gerald Stratford and Gene Smith in
a quarter final this morning, 6-2,
6-1, 6-3.
