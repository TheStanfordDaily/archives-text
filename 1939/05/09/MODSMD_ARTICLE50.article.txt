# Lightly . . . And Politely
## PROFESSORS AND IDIOTS 
### DOUG JAQUES 
The psychology professor was explaining how it
was with idiots. "Now you can get an idiot, to draw a
horizontal line,'' he said, and drew a horizontal line
on the blackboard. 'And you can get them to draw a
vertical line." And he drew a vertical line.
"But you can't get them to draw a cross,'' he said,
taking a pass at. drawing a cross on the blackboard—
and missing it altogether.
