# S.P.U. to Debate Relief Problem
## 
### 
The Stanford Political Union will
debate the topic, "Resolved: that a
two-year residence test, be required
of all persons applying for govern
ment relief in California," at its
second meeting of the quarter to
morrow night in Room 320, Geology
Corner.
The standing committee has plan
ned three meetings before the end
of the quarter, to be held every
other week.
No caucuses have been arranged
to map out party slates for tomor
row night's session, but leaders have
indicated that debate strategy will
be determined at 7 o'clock, half an
hour before the meeting is called to
order.
