# Lunch Club Guest To Discuss Co-op
## 
### 
One of the organizers of the Palo
Alto Co-operative, Mrs. Harry Wil
liams. will be the speaker at today's
meeting of the Luncheon Club which
will be held at noon in the Nitery.
Last week Dr. Murray Luck spoke
to the club on consumers' co-opcra
tives in general, and this week Mrs.
Williams and Mrs. Mildred Cowger,
who is a member of the local group,
will explain the organization of the
Palo Alto Co-operative store and
gas station and the vocational op
portunities therein.
