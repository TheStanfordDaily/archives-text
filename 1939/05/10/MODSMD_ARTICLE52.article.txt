# Japanese Ballet To Appear in S.F.
## 
### 
Completing an extensive tour of
California cities, the famous Takara
zuka Ballet from Tokyo will return
to the Bay area for a farewell per
formance in the War Memorial
Opera House Sunday night.
The Japanese ballet was brought
to the United States by the president
of the Takarazuka Theatrical Chain,
which owns and operates 500 the
aters throughout Japan.
