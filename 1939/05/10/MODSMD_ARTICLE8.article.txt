# Orchesis to Give Santa Cruz Recital
## 
### 
Orchesis, honorary dance society,
will go to Santa Cruz tomorrow to'
present a program for the Santa
Cruz Women's Club. The informal
program will combine the presenta
tion of dance numbers by the group
with a lecture and discussion by
Miss Margaret Jewell, Orchesis ad
viser.
Many of the numbers used for this
concert, including "Your Presence Is
Requested," "Design for One,"
"Wrath," and "Together We Go,"
will be presented here in the Me
morial Theater at the Orchesis Re
cital on May 31.
