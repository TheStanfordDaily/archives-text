# Foreign Students Plan Festivities
## 
### 
The International Committee in
their meeting last night completed
plans for a party for all foreign
students to be held this Sunday.
Festivities are slated to begin, ac
cording to President Jack Grover, at
2:30 with a swimming party in the
women's pool. From there, students
will move to the home of Mr. and
Mrs. L. H. Price for a barbecue sup
per. Foreign students without trans
portation will be provided with a
"lift" by reporting to the Nitery at
5 o'clock Sunday evening.
