# Steinbeckers Plan Trip Sunday
## 
### 
Members of the Steinbeck Com
mittee are planning a visit Sunday
to the migrant labor camp near
Tulare. Cars leave the Union Col
lar corner at 10 o'clock and will re
turn by-G o'clock the same night.
Students wishing to make the
trip can register with David Max
well, P.A. 3121, or Carl Reynolds.
