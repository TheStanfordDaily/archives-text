# Junior Class Plans Picnic at Beach
## 
### 
Second annual picnic of the junior
class will be held on Saturday, May
20, at San Gregorio Beach. Grayline
busses will call for members of the
Class of '40 at the Library parking
lot at 1:30 o'clock and take them to
the beach for an afternoon of swim
ming and organized sports and a
barbecue dinner.
Following a practice successfully
initiated last year, no private cars
will be allowed at the picnic.
