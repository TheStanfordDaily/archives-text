# Contest Offers Trip to Japan
## 
### 
Between now and May 20, Stan
ford students have a chance to win
a free trip to Japan, according to a
bulletin released by the Japan As
sociation of the San Francisco and
New York fairs.
Five trips will be offered to the
persons sending 1300-word essays
answering five questions concerning
Japan. Further information can be
secured by writing the Japan Asso
ciation in San Francisco.
