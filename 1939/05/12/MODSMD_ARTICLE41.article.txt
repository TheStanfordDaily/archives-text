# Union, Lagunita Exchange Diners
## 
### 
Under the chairmanship of Bar
bara Curtis, Union Unit held a
senior exchange dinner with the
seniors of Lagunita Court Tuesday
night. The Union and Lagunita
seniors dined at Union residence
while juniors and sophomores of
Union were dinner guests of Lagu
nita.
Following the dinner, dessert and
coffee were served in the Women's
Clubhouse, at which time a puppet
show was presented by June Blodg
ett and Muriel Pollia of Union. End
ing the evening's entertainment,
lone Collins of the Roble-Union-
Lagunita Alumnae Association
spoke.

Georgia Tech defeated Cumber
land in football 222 to 3 in 1916.
