# JUDGES MAY GET RAISE
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, May 11 (UP).—
The Assembly judiciary committee
reported favorably yesterday a bill
by Assemblyman Jacob Leonard
calling for increases in the salaries
of Santa Cruz County superior
judges from $6500 to $7500 a year.
