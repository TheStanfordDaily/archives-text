# Joe Werner Named Head Sponsor
## 
### 
Joe Wciner and Andy Stojko
vich were elected head sponsor
and secretary of Encina for next
year at a meeting of sponsors held
last night.
Wciner succeeds John Dern. and
Slojkovich follows Pete Zagar
into office.
