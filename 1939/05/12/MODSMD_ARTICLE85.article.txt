# Lagunita to Hold Mothers' Day Tea
## 
### 
A Mother's Day tea in honor of
the mothers of Lagunita women will
be given Sunday afternoon from
3:30 to 5 o'clock in the Court with
the officers of the Stanford Mothers'
Club as guests.
General chairman Ls May Lazarus.
Her assistants are Mary June Mont
gomery, hostesses; Marie Friedman,
invitations; Julia Johnson, servers;
Sue Hickman, decorations; Barbara
Bine, refreshments; May Hayes,
flowers; and Jean Sturtevant, clean
up.
