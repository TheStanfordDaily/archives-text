# Yearbook Contest Closes Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day to hand
in manuscripts to the English
Club for their annual Yearbook
contest.
It's $25 for the best story and
another $25 for the best verse
picked for the book, according
to English Club.
Manuscripts may be any
length up to 10 pages. English
Club executive board will
meet Tuesday afternoon to
choose stories and poems to fill
40 pages in the book, and the
prize money will be awarded to
the best selectionsuin the com
pleted book.
