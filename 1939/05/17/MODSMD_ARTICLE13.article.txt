# Toyon to Feature Welles at Dance
## 
### 
A Toyon d;incc will be held Satur
day, May 20, from 9 to 12 o'clock
featuring the music of Frank Welles,
whose orchestra played at the last
Toyon affair.
The informal dance will be free to
;ilI Toyon residents and 50 cents
per couple to others. Patrons and
patronesses will be Mr. and Mrs.
Roy Jastram, Mr. and Mrs. Gaukl
W. Lynn, and Mr. and Mrs. George
H. Knoles.
