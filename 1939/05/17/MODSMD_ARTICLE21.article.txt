# LIFE SAVING COURSE
## 
### 
Swimming Coach Nort Thornton
last night made known his inten
tion of conducting a class in senior
life saving for all men students in
terested in passing the life-saving
tests. According to Thornton, all in
terested men should make arrange
ments with him before next Fri
day's class, which begins at 3:20
o'clock.
