# Stagg Celebrates 'Golden Jubilee'
## 
### 

"FOOTBALL'S GRAND OLD MAN," Amos Alonzo Stagg, was hon
ored by friends at a dinner in San Francisco recently on the occasion
of his entering his 50th year as a coach. Another famed veteran coach,
Glenn S. "Pop" Warner, center, congratulates Stagg while Mrs. Stagg
looks on.
