# 'Ice Follies' Star Gains Recognition
## 
### 
The producers of "Ice Follies"
felt that they had signed the great
est living skater when they were
joined by Roy Shipstad three years
ago.
After capturing the title of
American profesisonal champion, he
is nationally recognized throughout
North America as the world's
greatest artist. He has coached
skating clubs in Minneapolis, St.
Paul, and Baltimore.
Studies made in New York state
show that on the average a man
marrying at 40 will take a bride
aged 32.
