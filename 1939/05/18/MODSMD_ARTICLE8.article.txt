# Naval Reserve To Show Films
## 
### 
A group of travelogue pictures
will be shown at the Naval Reserve
quarters in the old Roble Gym to
morrow evening. Along with trips
to various parts of the world, and
an excursion through a Navy train
ing school will be shown the fic
tional experiences of a Jack Tar.
The possibility of students utiliz
ing their summer vacations in trav
eling with the United States Fleet
in the Naval Reserve is the reason
for the showing of these authentic
pictures of the Navy at home and
abroad. Physical fitness is the only
restriction of this organization.
