# EMBEZZLER CAUGHT
## 
### 
SAN BEFJNARDINO, May 18 (UP)
—Wayne Morris Taber, .'!0, sought for
nearly two years on a charge of em
bezzling $3000 from a Minnesota
bank, was arrested here today by
Federal agents.
