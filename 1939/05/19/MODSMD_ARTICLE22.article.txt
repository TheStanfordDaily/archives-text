# Chappie to Elect Officers Today
## 
### 
Election of officers of Chaparral
and Hammer and Coffin Society will
be held at a meeting of the Stan
ford chapter of Hammer and Coffin
at 3 o'clock this afternoon at El Tigre
Club.
Offices to be filled include editor,
business manager, circulation man
ager, and women's manager of
Chaparral, and secretary-treasurer
of Hammer and Coffin.
The new editor of Chapamd will
also become president of the local
chapter of Hammer and Coffin. The
new business manager will be vice
president.
