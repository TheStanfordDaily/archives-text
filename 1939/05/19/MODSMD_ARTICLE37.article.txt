# NUDISTS TO CONVENE
## 
### 
SANTA CRUZ, May 18 (UP).—
Suntan Retreat, a nudist camp in the
mountains 16 miles from here, today
announced it expected 500 delegates
from 11 Western states to attend
the international nudist conference
August 12-14.
