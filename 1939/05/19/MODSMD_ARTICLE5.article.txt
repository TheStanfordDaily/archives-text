# Religious Groups To Picnic Sunday
## 
### 
The Wesley Foundation and the
Roger Williams Club will hold a
joint picnic and worship service at
Duveneck's Hidden Villa near Los
Altos next Sunday evening. They
will leave here at 4 o'clock from
the Women's Clubhouse.
After the picnic supper, Margaret
Tuttlc will be in charge of an
"Around the Fire" worship service.
Group singing will close the meet
ing at 8:30 o'clock.
