# Chamber Music To Illustrate Talk
## 
### 
Mills College Chamber Music
group, under the direction of Walter
Herbert, will illustrate the lecture
to be given by Alfred V. Franken
stein as part of the course in Song
Literature.
The group will perform a song
cycle by Paul Hindemilh written
for voice, string quartet, flute and
piano. Several groups of songs by
Schoenberg will also be included in
the program.
Members of Friends of Music
will be admitted on their member
ship cards. Single admission tick
ets at $1 for the public and 50 cents
for students will be sold at the
door.
