# Theta Awarded Fashion Fellowship
## 
### 
The Tobe-Coburti School for
Fashion Careers has notified Harriet
Witbeck, Kappa Alpha Theta, a sen
ior, that she is the recipient of a
fellowship at the school for the year
of 1939-40. The award was made on
the basis of Miss Witbeck's work in
response to a series of fashion ques
tions, and a fashion research project
on which she has been working.
The fellowship, valued at $700, in
cludes fashion merchandising, ad
vertising, styling, display and re
porting, with trips to factories and
showrooms and two periods of actual
department store selling.
