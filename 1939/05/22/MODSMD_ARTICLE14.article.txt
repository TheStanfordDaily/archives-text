# Orchesis Program To Be May 31
## 
### 
When Orchesis, honorary dance
society, presents its program on May
31, it will be assisted for the first
time by members of the modern
dance classes. The intermediate
classes will feature a satire on
"Field Day."
Participants in the satire are
Segrid Beceche, Jessie Calderwood,
Genevieve Dexter, Alene Kenner,
Jean Hiler, Jeanne Levy, Betty Men
derson, Molly Moscr, Elizabeth
Raffin, Marilie Rice, Yvonne Sey
bold, Annemarie Steinbiss, Virginia
Sterry, Mary Watterick, and Marilyn
Whedon.
