# Jim Farley Visits S.F. Exposition
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. May 20 (UP).
Postmaster General James A. Far
ley today visited the Golden Gate
International Exposition as Treasure
Island declared Post Office Day.
A military salute of 19 guns greet
ed Farley as he drove down the
ramp from the San Francisco-Oak
land Bay Bridge to the island. Mili
tary honors were tendered him in
the Court of Nations.
