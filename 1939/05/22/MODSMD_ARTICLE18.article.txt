# Faculty, Students Attend Barbecue
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur were guests at a barbecue
luncheon yesterday afternoon at the
La Honda cabin of Sam Mac Do
nald, director of University garden
ers. Fifty members of the faculty
and administration were present.
Sons and daughters of former
Stanford students also attended the
affair.
