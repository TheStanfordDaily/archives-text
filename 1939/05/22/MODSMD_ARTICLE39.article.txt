# 'Yankee Clipper' Lands at Lisbon
## 
### 
LISBON. Portugal, May 21 (UP).â€”
The 82.500-pound flying boat Yan
kee Clipper of Pan-American Air
ways landed on the Tejo River here
at 8:48 p.m. today (3:48 E.D.T.) after
a 26-hour, 40-minute flight from
Port Washington, L.1., with 3,000
pounds of mail.
The flying boat took off from Port
Washington, N.Y., near New York
City, at 1:08 p.m. yesterday, landed
at Horta, Azores, at 2:29 a.m. E.D.T.
today, and left for Lisbon at 8:48 a.m.
