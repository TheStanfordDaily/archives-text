# Boggs Team Fakes Badminton Title
## 
### 
The Women's Intramural Badmin
ton Tournament turned out to be a
family affair last Friday when Mu
riel and Mary Morris Boggs (Alpha
Phi) took a 15-0, 15-3 win over
Elizabeth Gerrard and Louise Hager
(Gamma Phi) in the doubles finals.
Muriel Boggs already holds the
singles title, which she won two
weeks ago trouncing Ruth Appleby
(Chi O) in the finals.
