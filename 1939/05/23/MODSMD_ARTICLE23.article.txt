# 'No More Peace' Author Kills Self
## 
### 
Ernst Toller, whose "No More
Peace" was produced recently by the
Division of Speech and Drama here,
committed suicide in New York
yesterday, according to United
Press.
The revolutionary German play
wright had been forced to leave his
native country by the Nazis, who
objected to his writings. He ended
his troubles yesterday by hanging
himself in his suite at the Mayflower
Hotel, police reported.
