# Cubberley Portrait Given to Farm
## 
### 

STUDENTS. FACULTY, and friends of Stanford presented this por
trait of Helen van Uxem Cubberley, wife of the dean emeritus of
the School of Education, to the University last Thursday evening in
commemoration of her years of service to Stanford. Painted by S.
Seymour Thomas, it will hang in the library of the new Education
Building, which she and Dr. Cubberley built.
