# Engineers to Honor Hoover at Fair
## 
### 
Herbert Hoover, '95, will be guest
of honor at Treasure Island July 13
when engineers from throughout the
country will celebrate Engineers'
Day.
Mr. Hoover will speak on the con
tributions that engineering has made
to human welfare. In convention at
San Francisco at the time will be
the American Society of Mechanical
Engineers, the Institute of Radio En
gineers, and the American Institute
of Electrical Engineers.
