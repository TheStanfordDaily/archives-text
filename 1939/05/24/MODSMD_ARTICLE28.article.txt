# NATIONS VIE FOR AIR SUPREMACY
## 
### 

THESE TWO PLANES FIGURE prominently in the peaceful competition between France and the United
States for air supremacy of the world. The huge French flying boat, Lieutenant de Vaisseau Paris, is shown
at top as it rested on the waters of Port Washington. N.Y., following a leisurely flight across the Atlantic.
To the right are crew members Marc Carion and Cap tain Henri Guillaumet. The second giant Boeing
"stratoliner" to be completed at Seattle is shown at bottom. It sister ship crashed recently with the loss of
ten lives.
