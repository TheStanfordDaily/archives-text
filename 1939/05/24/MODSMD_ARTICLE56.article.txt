# Speakers Bureau To Hold Tryouts
## 
### 
Last call for undergraduates wish
ing to try out for the Stanford
Speakers Bureau has been issued by
Bob Wood, manager. Wood has ask
ed all candidates to report either to
the bureau office in the Old Union
or to D. Paul McKelvey, faculty ad
viser, in the English Corner today
from 3 to 5 or by appointment. Bu
reau office hours are every day
from 1 to 2.
The board of review, which
chooses all speakers for the bureau,
will meet Friday afternoon at 1
o'clock to hear 12 applicants.
Wood emphasized the necessity
for candidates to make applications
as soon as possible so the new
speakers will be listed in the roster
to be sent out to peninsula clubs.
