# Kefauver Elected N.S.S.E. Director
## 
### 
Grayson N. Kefauver, dean of the
Stanford School of Education, ha.s
been elected to the board of directors
of the National Society for the Study
of Education and to the vice-presi
dency of the National Progressive
Education Association.
He will continue as secretary
treasurer of the National Association
of Colleges and Departments of
Education, an organization of the
deans and heads of departments and
schools of education in the universi
ties of the country.
