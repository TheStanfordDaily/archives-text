# Allen Announces Organ Program
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist., will present his weekly or
gan recital in the Stanford Memo
rial Church today at 4:15 o'clock.
The program will include "Toccata
in D minor (Dorian Mode)" and
"Sarabande in D major" by J. S.
Bach; "Shining Shore" by E. S.
Barnes; "Allegro vivace from the
First Symphony" by Louis Viernc;
"Toccata in F" by C. M. Widor.
