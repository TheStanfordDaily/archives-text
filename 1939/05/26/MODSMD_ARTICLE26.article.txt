# Fire Crew Plans Demonstration
## 
### 
The entire procedure of fire
fighting from the first alai'm until
the last victim has been carried
down the ladder will be demon
strated by the Stanford fire depart
ment next Monday.
In conjunction with the demon
stration, the Firehouse will be
open for inspection. All equipment
will be displayed and explained.
The exhibition will feature ladder
drills by several teams, jumping
from the tower into hand nets, and
a comic act has been tentatively
scheduled. Chief R. E. Dugan is in
charge of the demonstration.
A breakfast will be held Sunday
morning in the Union in honor of
the graduating members of the fire
crew: Edward S. Beyer, Frank
Thomas Collins, and Louis J. Nis
sen, Jr.
