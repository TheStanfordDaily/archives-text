# Dr. Luck to Speak To Church Group
## 
### 
Dr. J. Murray Luck will speak
to the Wesley Foundation at 7
o'clock Sunday evening in the
Methodist Church.
Results of the election of officers
for next year are: Mary Elizabeth
Smith, president; John Merrill, vice
president; Dorothea Bernsdorf, sec
retary; and Bill Brenner, treasurer.
The Rev. Don Baldwin is the stu
dent minister and adviser to the
Wesley group.
