# Chapter Restored By Eagle Scouts
## 
### 
Twenty Eagle Scouts last night
made plans for reinstating the Zeta
chapter of the National Service
fraternity, Alpha Phi Omega. The
officers elected were President Bob
McNeil; Secretary Bill Milton;
Treasurer Wilson Cannon; Corre
sponding Secretary Douglas Burke;
Chairman of the Constitutional Com
mittee Bob Efteland, and Chairman
of the Membei'ship Committee Dave
Jackman.
The group has planned a break
fast for Sunday, June 4, at the Com
munity Center at 8 o'clock, to which
all Eagle Scouts are invited. The
next meeting will be held next
Wednesday at 7:30 o'clock in the
Nitery.
