# Spanish Students To Give One-acts
## 
### 
Two one-act plays in Spanish will
be presented by the Department of
Romanic Languages Thursday, June
1, at 8 o'clock in the Little Theater.
Admission will be 25 cents.
"La Reja" (The Window Grill),
by Joaquin and Serafin Alvarez
Quintero, is a comedy of errors built
around the Spanish tradition which
allows a girl to talk to her sweet
heart only through the grill of her
window. This first play is to be
directed by Ralph Michels.
The second play, "Rosina Es Fra
gil" (Rosina Is Delicate), by Grego
rio Martinez Sierra, will be directed
by John Turner Reid.
