# Lightly . . . And Politely
## OR ANYTHING HAWAIIAN 
### DOUG JAQUES 
One of our smart Farm men-about-town who gets
around and knows what to say and do and all the
answers went to the St. Francis to' dance the other
night.
In true man-about-town fashion he went up to
Harry Owens and asked, "Do you have, by any chance,
an arrangement of "Sweet Leilani?"
