# Chappie Tryoutees To Attend Meeting
## 
### 
All those interested in doing art
01* editorial work for next year's
Chaparral are urged by Editor-elect
Marco Thorne to attend a meeting
tomorrow afternoon at 4 o'clock in
the University Press Building.
"There will be a large staff next
year," said Thorne, "and we are go
ing to use more student-written ma
terial, reflecting Farm humor. Al
most unlimited opportunities will be
offered in the field of creative writ
ing."
