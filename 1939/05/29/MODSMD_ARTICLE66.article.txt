# Farmers to Hear Stanford Experts
## 
### 
Merrill K. Bennett and Kail
Brandt, economists of the Food Re
search Institute, will speak at the
annual meeting of the Western Farm
Economics Association in Berkeley,
June 15 and 16.
Dr. Bennett will speak on "Change
Necessary in Our Economy to Pro
mote the General Welfare" and Dr.
Brandt will speak on "Long Time
Shifts in Human and 'Physical Re
sources."
