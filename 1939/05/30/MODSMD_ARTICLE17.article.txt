# Four Shakespeare Plays to Be Read
## 
### 
Readings of four of Shakespeare's
classic plays will be presented by
Professor Elizabeth Lee Bucking
ham's class in Vocal Interpretation of
Shakespeare. The readings will be
|ivcn next week in the Little The
ater.
The tentative schedule for the
presentations is as follows: "The
Taming of the Shrew," Monday,
June 5, at 7:30 o'clock, directed by
Jean Munro; "As You Like It,"
Tuesday, June G, at 2 o'clock, di
rected by Margaret Paulson; "King
Lear," Wednesday, June 7, at 7:30
o'clock, directed by Eloi.se Lambert;
and "Macbeth," Thursday, June 8,
at 7:30 o'clock, directed by Sylvia
Onesti.
All readings will be given in the
Little Theater under the sponsor
ship of the Division of Speech and
Drama. No admission will be
charged.
