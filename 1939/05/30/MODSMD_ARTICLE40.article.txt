# Westminster Club Will Meet Sunday
## 
### 
In place of the regular evening
meeting, Westminster Club will meet
Sunday afternoon at the Presbyte
rian Church at 4 o'clock. The meet
ing will then adjourn to the Crary
cabin for the meeting, supper, hik
ing and singing.
This group's last meeting of the
year will be at 7 o'clock Sunday,
June 11;
