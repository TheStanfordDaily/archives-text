# Army Defends Hawaii from 'Attack'
## 
### 

HONOLULU'S DEFENSES BLAZE with light as the U. S. Army
stages a spectacular demonstration to show how the island's anti-air
craft units would swing into action should a "foreign power" make
any aggressive move to occupy the area. A searchlight review at Fort
Shafter is shown at top. while, in lower picture. Oahu troops man a
partially camouflaged anti-aircraft gun.
