# TIRED ROOSEVELT
## 
### 
STOCKBRIDGE, Mass., May 29
(UP). —Mrs. Ethel du Pont Roose
velt, wife of Franklin D. Roosevelt,
Jr., was "resting" today at Dr. Aus
tin Fox Riggs' sanitarium. She was
reported "overtired."
