# A.S.U. Exchange Seeks Tryouts
## 
### 
Positions arc .still open for fresh
men and sophomores wishing to
work in Ihe A.S.U. Co-operative
Book Exchange, according to Lec
Arthur.
Students interested should con
tact Miss Arthur at the Tn-Delt
house or leave their names in the
basement of the Women's Club
house.
It was announced that the Ex
change will be open from 10 to 11
o'clock and from 2 to 4 o'clock Mon
day through Thursday. It will be
open from 10 to 11 o'clock on Friday,
and from Thursday, June 8, until
the closing date, June 15, it will
remain open from 10 to 12 o'clock
and from 1 to 4:30 o'clock except
Sunday.
