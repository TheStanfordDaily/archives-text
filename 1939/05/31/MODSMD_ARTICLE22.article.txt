# Roger Williams Club Elects Heads
## 
### 
Dr. Thomas E. Oliver attended an
installation of officers of the Roger
Williams Club held at Lagunita
Court last Saturday.
Elected president was John Moore,
while Jane Karpe was installed as
secretary. Allen Edwards will be
the next treasurer. Members in
clude Marian Smith, Margaret Tut
tle, and Muriel Carpenter.
