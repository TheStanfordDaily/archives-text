# Pre-Meds to Hear Plastic Surgeon
## 
### 
Dr. George Pierce, chief plastic
surgeon at St. Francis Hospital in
San Francisco, will address the Pre-
Med Society tonight at 7:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Pierce, who arranged the plas
tic surgery exhibit at the Exposi
tion, will discuss the methods and
principles of his work and will show
and demonstrate how plastic sur
gery is accomplished and what re
sults may be expected.
