# Three Drama Men Back for Vacation
## 
### 
Back to Palo Alto for vacation
from the theatrical world are three
former Stanford students who
gained their dramatic training here
and with the Palo Alto Community
Players. They are Fred Clark who
has been in New York with the
George Abbot production "What h
Life"; Phil Brown, who has been in
the Saroyan production "My Heart's
in the Highlands," and James Corner,
who has completed two pictures at
Hollywood, and has also played in
New York and London.
