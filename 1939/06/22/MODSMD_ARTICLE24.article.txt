# President's Son To Wed This Fall
## 
### 
Ray Lyman Wilbur, Jr., son of
Stanford's President, will be mar
ried this fall, it was announced re
cently. His engagement to Miss
Martha Bugbee, daughter of Mr.
and Mrs. Ralph Bugbee, was made
known last week.
Bridegroom-to-be is the youngest
son of President and Mrs. Wilbur.
After taking degrees from Sanford
and Columbia universities, he has
recently been connected with the
Civil Service Commission of Los An
geles.
Miss Bubgee is a graduate of
Washington University at St. Louis
and took a Master's degree at Col
umbia in 1935.
