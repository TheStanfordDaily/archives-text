# Dean Will Travel In North Europe
## 
### 
Lure of travel in Scandanavia has
attracted Stanford's dean of women.
Miss Mary Yost. She is planning to
tour Sweden and Finland by auto
mobile, taking in, enroute, the meet
ing of the International Federation
of AAUW at Stockholm. She will be
accompanied by friends from Den
ver, where she plans to stop on her
way east. She is also planning to
slop in Scotland before returning to
Stanford about September 15.
