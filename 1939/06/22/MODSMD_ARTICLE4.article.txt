# Trueblood Will Speak Sunday
## 
### 
The first Sunday of the summer
quarter will also be the occasion of
the first University address of Dr.
D. Elton Trueblood since his return
from Europe. He will speak in the
Memorial Church at the regular
service, 11 o'clock, on the subject
"The Ordeal of This Generation."
Dr. Trueblood. who is the chaplain
of the University and professor of
the philosophy of religion, has been
abroad for six months. His new
book. "The Trustworthiness of Re
ligious Experience." was published
in London last month by Allen &
Unwin.
On Friday of this week he is the
speaker at the luncheon meeting of i
the Commonwealth Club.
