# Roble Jolly-up Slated Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Come one, come all!"
A jolly-up tomorrow night from
7 to 8 o'clock in the Nitery will
open the summer quarter series of
bi-monthly jolly-ups. Wednes
day's jolly-up is being sponsored
by Roble Club and a number of
Roble hostesses will make intro
ductions.
Betty Dirks and Bill Mitchell are
co-chairmen of the dance. Ev
eryone on the campus is cordially
invited to attend.
