# 854 Register For Summer Quarter
## 
### 
Eight hundred fifty-four men
and women students registered for
the summer quarter, according to
figures released late yesterday by
the Registrar's office.
This represents a decrease of
153 students, as compared to last
year's record summer quarter reg
istration of 1006 students. Of this
quarter's total registration, 545 are
men and 309 are women.
