# Process Slow, But Stanford Grows
## 
### 
Stanford grows, if not by leaps and
bounds, at least by hops and steps.
Latest figure released from the Regis_
trar's Office reveal a total of 868
students registered, 14 more than
had registered by Monday afternoon.
Of the 868 students registered, 546
are men and 322 are women. Present
enrollment is still 154 less than last
summer quarters' record enrollment
of 1022.
