# SDX Meets Here For Convention
## 
### 
Journalism students and profes
sional newspapermen will meet to
gether on the campus this summer
for the 24th national convention of
Sigma Delta Chi, professional jour
nalistic fraternity.
Delegates will gather at the San
Francisco Press Club August 31,
hold a three-day session at Stan
ford, and conclude their conference
in Los Angeles. Chester H. Rowell,
Raymond Clapper and Leo C. Ros
ten head the list of speakers for the
five-day meeting.
Dr. Chilton R. Bush, head of the
journalism department, who is in
charge of convention arrangements,
will be assisted by a committee
of professional and undergraduate
members of Sigma Delta-Chi.
