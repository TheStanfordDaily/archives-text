# Students Attend First Convocation
## 
### 
First convocation in the history of
Stanford's summer quarters was
held Sunday in the Frost Memorial
Amphitheater. Dr. D. Elton True
blood, University Chaplain, officiat
ed at the service.
Dr. Eliot G. Mears, director of
summer quarter, greeted newcom
ers to Stanford and gave a short
talk on "The Stanford Environment."
The service closed with the singing
of the Stanford hymn.
