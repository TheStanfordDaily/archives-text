# Pool to Be Open to Farm on Sundays
## 
### 
Men and women students, faculty
members, and their families may
use the women's pool from 3 to 5
o'clock on Sunday afternoons during
the summer quarter, acording to
Marie Manchee, women's gym in

structor.
The charge will be 15 cents per
person, except for women students
registered in a physical education
class.
