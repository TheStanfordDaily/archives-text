# Bob Low Loses In Net Tourney
## 
### 
Bob Low, Stanford's top-ranking
net-man, was eliminated in the
eastern intercollegiate tennis tour
nament by Carlton Rood of North
Carolina by scores of 6-4, 11-9 at
Montclaire, New Jersey, Tuesday
afternoon. Low was seeded seventh.
Larry Dee, Stanford freshman, is
seeded number one in the frosh di
vision. Reports on the freshman
matches are not avaliable.
