# Handball Tourney To Start Soon
## 
### 
A handball tournament, open to
all registered men students, will be
held soon, according to the physical
education department.
The tourney will be for doubles
only. A trophy, donated by Art
Azevedo, will be given the winning
team. All registered students inter
ested must sign-up immediately in
the men's gymnasium with Mr.
Bugge or Mr. Frye.
A volley ball tournament is also
being planned.
