# Union Cafeteria Hours Announced
## 
### 
Guests attending the Education
Conference July 7 to 6 are invited
to the Union Cafeteria, open this
summer for the first time in five
years, according to Miss Christine
Ricker, director of dining halls.
It will be open daily except Sun
day as follows:
Breakfast 7:30-8:30
Luncheon 12:00-1:00
Dinner 5:45-6:45
The main dining room serves
luncheon from 12 to 1 and dinner
daily from 6 to 7 o'clock except
Saturday. The Cellar or coffee-shop
is open daily from 7:30 a.m. to 10:30
p.m., but closes at 8 p.m. on week
ends.
The entire Union, with its main
lobby, lounge, and court-yards, is
open to the public all summer, said
Miss Ricker.
Information relating to the Union,
including the reservation of private
dining rooms, may be had in the
main office just off the lobby, or by
phoning 3136.
