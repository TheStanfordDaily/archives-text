# Business Men Convene Here
## 
### 
More than a hundred business
men had enrolled by yesterday af
ternoon for the Stanford Business
Conference, according to figures re
leased from the office of the Gradu
ate School of Business.
Discussion and study of the more
important business problems of the
day will be the goal of the third
annual conference, to be held on the
campus July 17 to 21.
Educators and business execu
tives of the Pacific Coast and the na
tion as a whole will act as leaders
for the various sessions of the con
ference.
