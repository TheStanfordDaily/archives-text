# Chaplain to Talk On Art, Religion
## 
### 
"The Relationship of Religion to
Art and Music" will be the subject
of the sermon by Dr. D. Elton True
blood, University chaplain, Sunday
morning in Memorial Church. The
chaplain will also speak at the Sun
day vesper services at 5 p.m. The
public is invited to attend.
A Union service of the churches
of Palo Alto will be held Sunday
evening at 7:45 o'clock at the Meth
odist Church in Palo Alto, at which
Dr. Trueblood will speak on "Figs
from Thistles."
