# Quartet to Swell Summer Recitals
## 
### 
Church concerts for the last three
weeks in July will be enlarged to
concert proportions with the assist
ance of the members of the Roth
Quartet and the Stanford String
Ensemble.
The second of the series to be held
this Sunday evening in the Memorial
Church at 7:30 p.m., will feature
Feri Roth, violinist, and Janos
Scholz, 'cellist. Roth will appear as
soloist in the Handel A Major So
nata and Scholz will play selections
by Boccherini and Frescobaldi. War
ren D. Allen, University organist,
will play some organ solos. The pub
lic is invited to attend.
