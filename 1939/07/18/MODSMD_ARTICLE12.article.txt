# Pi Lambda Theta Elects Taggart
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, honorary edu
cational fraternity, held election of
officers at a meeting last Wednesday
night in the Education Building.
The following persons were
elected: Lelia Ann Taggart, presi
dent; Dorothy Brown, vice-presi
dent; C. E. Van Norman, secretary;
Rosario Dias, treasurer; Alice Dolan,
keeper of records; and Lucile Hern
don, program chairman.
