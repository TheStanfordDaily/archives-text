# Parents, Teachers To Discuss Skills
## 
### 
The second meeting of the series
for parents of children registered in
the summer classes conducted by the
Stanford School of Education is
scheduled at the Campus School
Wednesday evening, July 19, at 7:30
o'clock. The purpose of the meeting
is to discuss the "place of skills in
the modern program," and to explain
the work that is going forward in
each of the classes.
The conference will be presided
over by Dr. J. Paul eonard. The
primary teacher, Miss Ruth Angelo,
the intermediate teacher, Miss
Martha Farnum, and the core
course teacher, Miss Elizabeth Spar
hawk, will each make a presentation
after which the meeting will be
thrown open for questions from the
group. Many topics of interest to
parents have been planned.
