# Syllabus Fees Payable Today
## 
### 
Syllabus fees for summer quar
ter are due and payable today,
from 8 a.m. to 12 noon and from
1 to 5 p.m., at the accounting de
partment in the administration
building. Questions regarding
charges should be submitted to the
Registrar's Office for adjustment
before payment is made.
If payment of fees is not made
by 5 p.m. today, a check, together
with the bill showing the amount
to be paid, may be mailed, pro
vided it shows a postmark before
midnight tonight, according to the
accounting department.
After today, an additional charge
of $2 will be made for late pay
ment of fees. Registration in the
University will be subject to can
cellation if fees are not paid by
July 25.
