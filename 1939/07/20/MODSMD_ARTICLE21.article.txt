# Wilbur Returns From Vacation
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur was
back at his Ad Building desk yes
terday after several weeks spent at
his summer home in Soda Springs.
Today he will meet with the Univer
sity Board of Trustees to consider
routine business.
Stanford's president will remain
on campus for about 10 days.
Toward the end of next week he
will arrange for some simple ob
servance of ground-breaking for the
Hoover Library of War, Revolution,
and Peace.
More than 33,000,000 little salmon
and trout were planted in Canadian
waters last year.
