# Speech Professor Marries in England
## 
### 
When Dr. Leland T. Chapin re
turns to the Stanford campus next
fall to take over his position as asso
ciate professor of speech and drama,
he will bring with him his bride,
the former Miss Lillian Christian
Macpherson of Edinburgh, Scot
land.
They were married on July 14, in
Christ's Church in Mayfair, Lon
don, and will spend the next few
months in England. They plan to
return to the United States in De
cember.
