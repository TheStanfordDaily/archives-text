# Dames Hold First General Meeting
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold its first
general social meeting of the sum
mer this evening at 8 o'clock at the
home of Mrs. Paul R. Hanna, 737
Coronado Street. Music and games
are planned for everyone's enter
tainment, and all Dames are cordial
ly invited to attend.
The bridge group of the Stanford
Dames will meet this afternoon at
2:30 o'clock in the Women's Club
house.
