# Veterans Defeat Stanford Golfers
## 
### 
Three Texas veterans from Dallas
dumped Stanford's golf hopes in the
Trans-Mississippi Tourney at Colo
rado Springs Friday as Don Ken
nedy was beaten by Don Schu
macher, ex-champion, 4 and 3. Art
Doering went down before Harry
Todd, 5 and 4, and Bill O'Neal was
beaten by Ohara Watt, 2 and 1.
Before bowing out of the meet,
Doering defeated Bob Brownell of
Duke, 5 and 4; Kennedy beat Lind
of Denver, 2 and 1; and O'Neal de
feated P. M. Aitkin of Lincoln, Neb.,
2 and 1.
