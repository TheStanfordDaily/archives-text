# Roth Musicians To Play Sunday
## 
### 
Sunday evening, at 7:30 o'clock
Stanford's Roth Quartet, with War
ren D. Allen, head of the music de
partment and University organist,
will offer a concert in Memorial
Church.
The Quartet in C Major by Mozart
(sometimes known as the Dissonance
quartet because of a passage in the

first movement which was consid
ered radical in Mozart's day).
Fantasia for Oi'gan in F Minor will
feature Mr. Allen.
