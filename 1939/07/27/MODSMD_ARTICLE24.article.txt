# Kefauver to Head Radio Committee
## 
### 
Grayson N. Kefauver, dean of the
School of Education, has been ap
pointed chairman of an American
Education Forum committee of three
to organize a year's programs for
half-hour educational radio broad
casts over the National Broadcasting
Company network.
Centering around the theme of
"Democracy and Education," the
programs will be in the nature of
round-table discussions. Represen
tatives of educational, civic and so
ical organizations will take part.
Broadcasts will start the first
week in November and will be at
12 noon, Eastern Standard Time.
