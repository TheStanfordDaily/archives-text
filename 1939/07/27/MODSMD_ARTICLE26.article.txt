# Director Names Next Production
## 
### 
Thomas Wood Stevens has an
nounced that "The Two Shepherds"
by Martinez-Sierra, the Granville-
Barker translation, will be the next
production by the Division of Speech
and Drama.
It will have a week's showing,
opening the run August 14. It will
play August 14, 17, 18, and 19 in the
Little Theater. Reservations may be
obtained by phoning 22041.

On Sunday, July 30, Dr. D. Elton
Trueblood has chosen as his subject
"Overcoming of Dullness" for the
regular morning service. The last
regular meeting of vesper services
will be held at 5 o'clock Sunday. Dr.
Trueblood will be the speaker.
