# Roble Club to Hold Tea Tomorrow
## 
### 
Roble Club is entertaining all
campus women at an informal tea
Friday afternoon from 3 to 5
o'clock. Mary Kay Horton, gen
eral chairman, is urging all wom
en on the campus to attend so that
they may meet the wives and
members of the faculty who will
be pouring.
Commtitee heads assisting Mary
Kay are Virginia McCloskey, in
vitations; Mary Wagner, serving;
and Barbara Brown, hostesses.
The tea will provide an opportunity
for women who are not living in
a campus residence this summer
to become better acquainted with
the group in Roble.
