# Common Problems To Be Discussed
## 
### 
Common problems of England and
America will be discussed by Dr.
D. Elton Trueblood, University
chaplain, on the Stanford Radio
Hour over station KGO at 7:15
o'clock tomorrow evening.
Dr. Trueblood, who lived in Eng
land during the first half of this
year, has had an opportunity to ob
serve the English attitude toward
this country.
He will discuss the recent moves
for greater Anglo-American friend
ship by many sections of English
public life and the numerous mis
understandings that now exist be
tween the two countries.
