# Netmen to Enter Quarter-finals
## 
### 
The Summer Quarter intramural
tennis tournament passed into the
quarter-finals last week, with only
one second round match yet un
played.
Gruenberg gained quarter-final
standing by defeating Post 6-4, 6-0;
Collyer defeated Lee, 2-6, 12-10,
6-4; Lindenmeyer beat Jacobs, 6-2,
8-6; Haber won from Hines 8-6, 1-6,
6-3; Gorter defeated Hyams 6-3,
6-1; Wooley won over Borgfeld, de
fault, and Parker beat Piddington
6-0, 6-1. Quarter-final matches will
take place this week.
