# Mrs. Price Takes Golfing Honors
## 
### 
Eighty golfers of the Women's
Golf Association of Northern Cali
fornia competed in a handicap tour
nament and attended a luncheon in
the Stanford golf course clubhouse
yesterday in the Annual Ladies'
Open Day.
Only local player to take honors
was Mrs. W. C. Price of Palo Alto
and the Stanford club, who was the
winner of the Class C division.
Mrs. William Pabst Jr. and Mrs.
J. H. Ronaw of San Jose, and Miss
Virginia Valentine of Pasatiempo
tied for low net honors in the Class
A division with net scores of 80.
Mrs. Pabst and Mrs. Ronaw tied for
low gross with 83's, although no
prizes were awarded for these
scores.
