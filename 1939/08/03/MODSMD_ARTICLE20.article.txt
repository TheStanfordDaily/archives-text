# LOW ENTERS 2ND ROUND
## 
### 
Bob Low, No. 1 varsity tennis star,
went into the second round of com
petition at Southampton, New York,
Monday by beating William Gillespie
6-2, 9-7 in the Meadow Club Tour
ney. Low drew a bye Tuesday, and
had a day of rest from tourney
Play-

Christian Science Organization
will hold its weekly meeting tonight
in the Women's Clubhouse at 8
o'clock. All students, faculty mem
bers, and their friends are invited to
attend.
