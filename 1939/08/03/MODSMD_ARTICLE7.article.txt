# Dee Wins Twice In National Net Meet
## 
### 
Larry Dee, Stanford frosh net ace,
advanced Tuesday in both singles
and doubles divisions of the national
junior championships at Culver, In
diana.
In the third round of the singles,
Dee thoroughly trounced Dick
Wade, 6-1, 6-1. Dee teamed then
with Emery Neale to whip Bob Gray
and Jack Troup. 6-1, 6-1 in the sec
ond round of the doubles compe-
