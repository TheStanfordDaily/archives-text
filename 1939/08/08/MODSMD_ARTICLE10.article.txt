# Frosh Ace Beaten In Indiana Finals
## 
### 
Larry Dee, Stanford's hope in the
National Junior Championships last
week, was eliminated in the final
round at Culver, Indiana, Saturday
7-5, 6-4 6-2, by Ted Schroeder of
Glendale. In semi-final play the
Farm freshman, seeded third in the
tourney, trounced Ted Olewine,
ranked number one Junior net man,
6-4, 6-4.
Dee combined with Emery Neale
to reach final round doubles Friday,
but went down again Saturday be
fore Jack Kramer and Ted Olewine.
