# Desmond Accepts Position at U.C.
## 
### 
Dr. Robert W. Desmond, former
acting associate professor of journal
ism at Stanford and now professor
of journalism at the Medill School of
Journalism, Northwestern Univer
sity, will return to the Coast this
fall as acting professor and head of
the journalism department at the
University of California at Berkeley.
Dr. Desmond was a visiting faculty
member at Stanford during the
spring quarter of 1938. He has also
taught at the Universities of Michi
gan and Minnesota.
