# Mother of Coach Succumbs Here
## 
### 
Funeral services will be held this
afternoon for Mrs. Adaline G. Bugge,
mother of Elwyn Bugge, Stanford
tennis coach. Mrs. Bugge died Mon
day at their home at 850 Guinda
Street.
She was 59 years old and had been
a resident of Palo Alto for six years.
She is survived by a daughter, Mrs.
Janice Lee of Seattle, and Mr.
Bugge.
