# Stevens to Direct 'Two Shepherds'
## 
### 
G. Martinez Sierra's two-act com
edy, "The Two Shepherds," which
will be presented August 1, 17, 18
and 19 by the Stanford Division of
Speech and Drama, will be the sec
ond production in the Little Theater
this summer.
Thomas Wood Stevens, director,
released the names of the cast Fri
day. Ralph Parr will play Don An
tonio, the kindly old priest, and
Fred Giari will play Don Jose Maria,
the new priest.
Ruth Cannon is creating modern
Spanish costumes for the produc
tion, and Waldemar Johansen's set
will show a courtyard with a church
on one side of the stage and a home
on the other.
