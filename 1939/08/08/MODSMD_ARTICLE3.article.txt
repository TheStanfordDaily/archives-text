# 300 Stanfordites Attend Dance
## 
### 
One hundred fifty couples danced
to the music of Ozzie Secrest and
his 11-piece orchestra at an all
campus semi-formal dance in Toyon
Hall Friday evening.
Dozens of brightly-tinted posters,
loaned from the Hoover Library on
War, Peace, and Revolution, deco
rated the walls of the hall. The dance
celebrated ground-breaking for the
structure, to take place within a
few days.
The dance was sponsored jointly
by Toyon Club and the Associated
Students. Only other date dance of
of the summer quarter will be the
Roble semi-formal, scheduled for
Friday, August 18, in the Women's
