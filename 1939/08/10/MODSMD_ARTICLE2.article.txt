# Quartet to Make Last Appearance
## 
### 
Playing for the last time on the
campus this summer, the Roth Quar
tet will take part in the regular
services in the Memorial Church
Sunday.
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, Univer
sity chaplain, will speak. The topic
of his sermon will be "The Meaning
of a Classic."
The Quartet will play two move
ments: the Andante Con moto from
the quartet in E flat major by Mozart,
and Largo from the Quartet in D
Major, opus 76, No. 5, by Haydn.
