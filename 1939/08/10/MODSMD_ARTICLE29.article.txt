# Stanford Cadets Receive Honor
## 
### 
Louis Metzger and Louis J. Nis
sen, Jr., Stanford seniors, were
among the cadets from the bay area
selected by Major General Albert J.
Bowley, Ninth Corps Area com
mander, as outstanding student re
serve officers.
The two cadets were nominated
by the Stanford Reserve Officers
Training Corps as candidates from
the campus unit.
