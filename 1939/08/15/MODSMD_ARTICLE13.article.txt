# Roble Hall to Hold Dance on Friday
## 
### 
Robleites and their guests will
dance to the music of Norman
Carroll and his orchestra in the
patio of the Women's Gym Friday
evening from 9 to 1 o'clock. The
dance will be semi-formal, with a
summer theme.
Evelyn Jameson is the chairman
in charge of the affair. Nancy Veitch,
Ruth Knoph, Lee Jordan, and Marge
Forward are assistant - chairmen,
handling publicity, clean-up, ar
rangements, and refreshments.

Harry L. Sheldon, editor of The
Daily for Volume 95, was a visitor
on the campus last week. He plans
to vacation until the first of Septem
ber, when he will leave for Europe.
