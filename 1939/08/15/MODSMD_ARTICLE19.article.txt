# Educational Films To Be Shown Here
## 
### 
Sociologically significant moving
pictures provided by the "March of
Time" will be shown to the public
in the Education auditorium tomor
row evening at 7:30 o'clock. This
program is under the auspices of
the Evaluation Workship, according
to Einora Davies, secretary.
Pictures to be shown are "TVA,"
"The Refugee," "The Child Labor
Amendment," "Young America,"
"Uncle Sam," "The Good Neighbor,"
and "Racketeers Versus House
wives." The public is cordially in
vited to attend.
