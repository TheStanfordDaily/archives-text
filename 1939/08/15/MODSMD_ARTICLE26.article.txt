# Larry Dee Loses In Second Round
## 
### 
Larry Dee, Stanford frosh net star,
was eliminated in the second round
of the eastern grass courts cham
pionship at Rye, N.Y., Tuesday,
when he was defeated by Gilbert
Hunt, 6-0, 6-4. Dee later teamed

with Welby Van Horn only to lose
to Bickel and Shostrom in the men's
doubles, 2-6, 7-5, 7-5.
Bob Low, Cardinal varsity ace,
and Jack Kramer defaulted to Don
McNeill and Frankie Parker
