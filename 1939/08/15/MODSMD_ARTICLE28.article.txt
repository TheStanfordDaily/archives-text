# JEFFREY ADDS VICTORY
## 
### 
Clyde Jeffery added another vic
tory to his list Sunday when he de
feated Holmes of England in the 200
meters in the fast time of 21.2.
Holmes and Jeffrey had previously
run a 10.5 seconds dead heat in the
hundred meters. The touring Amer
ican team annexed 12 first places
out of 16 events.
