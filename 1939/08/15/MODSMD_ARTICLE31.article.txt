# String Orchestra Presents Concert
## 
### 
Stanford's String Orchestra, di
rected by Feri Roth, presented a
concert in the Stanford Union Court
Friday evening. Members of the
Roth Quartet participated, assisted
by students and members of the
community who have been working
with Mr. Roth in chamber music
during the summer quarter.
Chairs were placed in the Union
Court and special lighting effects

were worked out by Charles E.
Fitts, assistant technical director of
dramatics.
