# HASHERS PLAN PICNIC
## 
### 
Hashers and bus boys of Roble
Dining Hall are planning an ex
pedition to Alum Rock Park one
week from Friday evening. Twelve
hash-slingers, six tray-carriers and
their girls will dance picnic style
at this resort near San Jose.
