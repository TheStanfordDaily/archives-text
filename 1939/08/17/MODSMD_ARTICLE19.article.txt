# HOOVER GOES FISHING
## 
### 
Former President Herbert Hoover
revealed recently that he plans to
leave his Stanford University home
the latter part of August for an
angling trip in southern Montana.
Although officially this is to be a
vacation trip, Mr. Hoover has ac
cepted an invitation to make an off
the-record speech at the state Re
publican meeting to be held there
at that time.
