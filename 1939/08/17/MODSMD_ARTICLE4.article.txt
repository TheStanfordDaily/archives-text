# Robleites to Dance To Carroll Music
## 
### 
Japanese lanterns, a splashing
fountain, dancing in an open court
will feature Roble Club's summer
semi-formal tomorrow night. The
dance will be held in the patio of
the Women's Gym from 9 to 1
o'clock.
Norman Carroll and his orchestra
will furnish the music. Evelyn
Jameson, assisted by Nancy Veitch,
Marge Forward, Lee Jordan, and
Ruth Knoph, is general chairman of
the dance.
