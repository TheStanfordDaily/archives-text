# Law Grad Wins In Net Finals
## Gruenberg Defeats Tanner for Title 
### 
Summer Quarter intramural ten
nis championship was won last week
by Max Gruenberg, Law School
graduate, when he defeated Obert
Tanner 6-4, 6-2 in the net finals on
the varsity courts.
Gruenberg reached the finals by
drawing a bye in the first round,
downing Post 6-4, 6-0 in the second,
beating Collyer 6-2, 6-2 in the
quarterfinals and taking Linden
meyer in the semifinal round 5-7,
6-3, 16-14 after a grueling duel.
Tanner reached the championship
round by beating Gorter 6-0, 6-2.
Only all-school intramural event
of the summer, the tennis tourna
ment attracted 29 men for first
round competition, and although
some were forced to withdraw due
to various conditions before the end
of the toumey, interest was intense
throughout.
