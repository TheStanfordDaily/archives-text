# Summer Services To End Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, chaplain
of the University, will deliver the
sermon at the final services to be
held in Stanford's Memorial Chapel
during the Summer Quarter, Sun
day, August 27. Warren D. Allen,
campus organist, will furnish music
for the program.
Next services in the chapel will
be held on the last Sunday in Sep
tember, when Dr. Trueblood will
address new students in a pre-regis
tration sermon, part of Stanford's
program for freshmen and trans
fers.
