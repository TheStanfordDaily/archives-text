# Seattle Rainiers Cinch Pennant
## 
### 
The Seattle Rainiers cinched their
first Pacific Coast League pennant in
15 years when they walloped the
second place Los Angeles Angel.s.
It was not, however, the first
Coast Championship for Jack Leli
velt, manager of Beer Baron Emil
Sick's Rainiers. A few years ago
Lelivelt won several league titles
while managing, of all teams, the Los
Angeles Angels, now skippered by
"Truck" Hannah, catcher and coach
under Lelivelt.
