# Famous Pessimist Quits Gridiron
## 
### 
When the first kick-off opens this
fall's college grid season, Gloomy
Gil Dobie, famed for his pessimistic
attitude while coaching Boston Col
lege's eleven, will be conspicuous by
his absence. Dobie, one of the wiliest
strategists on eastern gridirons for
the last 35 years, has retired to lead
the quiet life and avoid repeated
cases of every coach's ailment, the
Saturday afternoon jitters.
