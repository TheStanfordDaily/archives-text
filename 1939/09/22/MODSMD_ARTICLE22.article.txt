# U. S. PLANES IN ARMY MANEUVERS
## 
### 

HIGH OVER the rugged Southern California countryside, this formation, led by Captain Robert F. Tate,
participated in a routine cross country training flight. Picture shows the 73rd squadron of the 17th Attack
Group of the first wing air force, based at March Field, Riverside, Calif., executing precision flying in an
of elements formation high above the clouds.
