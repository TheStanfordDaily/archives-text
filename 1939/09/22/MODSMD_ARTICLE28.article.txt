# Claire MacGregor Takes Hawaii Post
## 
### 
Miss Claire MacGregor, assistant
professor of speech and drama, sail
ed recently for Honolulu, where she
has accepted a year's exchange pro
fessorship to teach at the University
of Hawaii. In her place from the
island university is Professor George
Peazey.
In the same department, Emeritus
Professor Lee Emerson Bassett is
this year being replaced by Professor
Hubert Heffner, -formerly of North
western University.
