# Sponsors to Live In Frosh Halls
## 
### 
Sponsors appeared at Stanford in
1923 as advisors and consultants for
freshmen women, and following that
a similar system was established for
Encina.
Head sponsor in Roble this year is
Esther Berry and senior president
of the hall is Jean Bolton. They will
be assisted by 12 senior sponsors and
eight junior sponsors.
Encina's head sponsor will be Joe
Weiner who will be assisted by Andy
Stojkovich in the post of secretary
treasurer.
