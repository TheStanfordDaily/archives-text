# Morris Visits East For Physics Meet
## 
### 
Dean Samuel B. Morris, head of
the School of Engineering, attended
the triennial assembly of the Inter
national Union of Goedesy and Geo
physics at Washington, D.C. this
month.
While in the east Dr. Morris also
attended joint meetings of the Amer
ican Society of Civil Engineers with
the British and Canadian Institutes
of Engineering in New York City.
