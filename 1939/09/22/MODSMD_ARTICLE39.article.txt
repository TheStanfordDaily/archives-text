# R.O.T.C. Offers Horseback Rides
## 
### 
Incoming freshmen will be given
an opportunity during the Pre-reg
period to take advantage of the .Re
serve Officers Training Corps facili
ties at Stanford in four scheduled
horseback rides around the Farm's
many equestrian trails.
Under the supervision of Gene
Coldiron and upper class R.O.T.C.
cadet officers, a maximum of 50 men
will be accommodated for each of

the rides which will start from the
R.O.T.C. stables at 3:30 and 4:30
o'clock Saturday afternoon, Sept. 23,
at 9:30 o'clock Sunday morning, and
3 o'clock Sunday afternon, Sept. 24.
A sign-up list will be placed in
the Encina entrance hail.
