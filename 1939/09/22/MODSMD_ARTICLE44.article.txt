# Hungarian Violinist To Remain Here
## 
### 
Ferenc Molnar, Hungarian violin
ist and member of the Roth Quartet
which played here during the sum
mer, will make PajO Alto his per
manent home. He decided to remain
in California when an automobile
accident unexpectedly turned a one
day concert stop into a six-month
hospital sojourn two years ago.
His musical activities will be di
vided between Palo Alto and San
Francisco, with studios in both cities.
Here, he plans to continue his musi
cal open house, inaugurated during
the summer, and will also organize
a Stanford student quartet.
