# Beardsley Elected A.B.A. President
## 
### 
New president of the American
Bar Association is Charles Alexan
der Beardsley, lecturer in the Stan
ford School of Law for the last nine
years.
Beardsley graduated from Stan
ford with the class of 1908 with Phi
Beta Kappa and Order of the Coif
honors. At that time he entered the
firm of Fitzgerald and Abbot. He
has remained with them for 31 years.
