# Two Daily Chiefs Marry in August
## 
### 
Lloyd Lapham, '3B, former editor
of the Stanford Daily and Jean
Toffee, a Daily night editor at the
same time, were married in Sacra
mento this August.
Lapham, who was a Sigma Chi
here, is now with United Press in
Sacramento, while his bride is em
ployed in the women's department
of the Sacramento Bee.
Another Daily editor, Millard
Browne, '36, was married August 25
to Jane Sweet of San Jose. Browne
was editor his senior year and a
member of Sigma Alpha Epsilon. He
is now with the Sacramento Union.
