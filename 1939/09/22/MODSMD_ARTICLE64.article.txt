# Chaparral Editor Calls for Tryouts
## 
### 
Cartoonists, writers, photographers
and students interested in novelty
art work such as making clay, pipe
cleaner or tin figures for photogra
phy, are urged to try out for Chap
arral, monthly humor magazine, by
editor Marco Thorne.
He will keep office hours in Room
11 of the Press Building to receive
prospective freshman staff members
from 10 to 12 and 1 to 4 o'clock
every day during pre-regislration
pex'iod.
