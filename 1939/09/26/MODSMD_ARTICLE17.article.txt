# Warsaw Reports Von Fritsch Death
## 
### 
LONDON, Tucs., Sept. 26 (UP).
A radio Warsaw broadcast inter
cepted here early today said Gen
eral Von Fritsch, former command
er-in-chief of the German army, was
assassinated on the Warsaw front by
agents of the Gestapo.
"Von Fritsch did not die a .sol
dier's death on the battlefield," the
broadcast said, referring to German
claims that he died in action. "He
was cowardly assassinated by agents
of (he Gestapo." The Warsaw radio
was "jammed" immediately follow
ing the Von Fritsch announcement.
