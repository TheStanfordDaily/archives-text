# GRADER KILLS EMPLOYEE
## 
### 
LAKEPORT, Cal., Sept. 25 (UP).
—Roy Graham, 57, county road em
ployee, was killed when he stepped
into the path of a grader and was
crushed, the coroner's office an
nounced today.
