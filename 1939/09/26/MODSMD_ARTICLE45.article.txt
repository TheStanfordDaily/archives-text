# S.A.E. Sophomore Injured in Fall
## 
### 
After putting the finishing touches
of paint on the topmost S.A.E. gable
Bill Wallace, sophomore, came
charging down to the porch to dis
locate and break his ankle Sunday
afternoon.
According to Dr. Lipton of the
Palo Alto Hospital, Wallace will be
released wtihin the next few days.
However, his leg will remain in a
cast for eight weeks.
