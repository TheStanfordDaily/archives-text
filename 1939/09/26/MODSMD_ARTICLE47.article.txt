# STREET EXCHANGE LIGHT
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Sept. 25 (UP).
—Caution took possession of traders
on the San Francisco Stock Ex
change today, and volume was light
compared with last week. Prices
generally edged higher, although
several issues sustained minor losses
and gave the list an irregular ap
pearance.
