# NAVY PLANS MANEUVERS
## 
###

The Navy high command is plan
ning formidable fleet training ma
neuvers at Pearl Harbor, Hawaii,
the United States Gibraltar of the
Pacific, involving most of the war
ships now based at San Pedro and
San Diego, it was learned today.
