# GIBBONS BURIAL SET
## 
### 
STROUDSBURG, Pa., Sept. 25
(UP). —Floyd Gibbons, the war cor
respondent, author and radio com
mentator, who died yesterday of a
heart attack, will be buried at
Mount Olivet Cemetery in Wash
ington, D.C., Wednesday, it was an
nounced tonight.
