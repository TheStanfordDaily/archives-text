# POLISH PLANES LAND
## 
### 
LONDON, Tuesday, Sept. 2G (UP).
—The Daily Herald said today tliat
nine Polish fliers in three small Po
lish military planes landed last night
at an unnamed Scottish airfield after
a desperate flight from Poland.
