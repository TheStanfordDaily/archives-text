# Wilbur to Talk For Assembly
## President to Greet Student Body, Faculty 
### 
Welcoming students and faculty at
the first all-University assembly of
the year, President Ray Lyman Wil
bur will speak in Memorial Hall to
morrow morning.
Seating space for members of the
faculty has been reserved on the
stage, according to the Committee on
Public Exercises, in charge of the
assembly.
Faculty members are requested by
the committee to enter the audi
torium through the west entrance
and the Green Room northwest of
the stage.
According to a University an
nouncement, all classes have been
ordered dismissed for the occasion.
The assembly is scheduled for 10:10
o'clock.
