# AVIATRIX REBUKED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Sept. 26 (UP).—
The Civil Aeronautic? Authority to
night ordered Laura Ingalls, noted
woman flier, to show cause why her
license should not be revoked for
dropping peace pamphlets in the
vicinity of the White House.
