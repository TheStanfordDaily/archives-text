# BRUINS DRILL SECRETLY
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Sept. 26 (UP).-
Coach "Babe" Horrell, wearing a
worried frown after his first string
was toppled G-0 yesterday by a
"goof" squad, sent the U.C.L.A.
Bruins into secret scrimmage today.
The team reportedly drilled against
Texas Christian plays, and will hold
its final workout tomorrow night.
U.C.L.A. plays the Horned Frogs
Friday night in the Coliseum.
