# W.A.A. Will Give Dinner for Roble
## 
### 
At a dinner meeting set for
Thursday night at Ruble, the Stan
ford Women's Athletic Association
will outline the athletic program for
the benefit of newly-enrolled co
eds. First on the list for autumn and
winter quarters will be interclass
hockey and basketball, and also in
tramural badminton.
For the spring of 1940. archery,
tennis and swimming are on the cal
endar, culminating in the Triangular
Sports Day, in which women stu
dents from Stanford, California, and
Mills participate.,
