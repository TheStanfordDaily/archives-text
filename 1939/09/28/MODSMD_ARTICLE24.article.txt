# Tennyson Wife Cites Cruelty in Divorce
## 
### 
REDWOOD CITY, Sept. 21 (UP).
—Lionel Lord Tennyson, grandson
of the famed English poet, was sued
for divorce today by Mrs. Carroll
Eiling Donner Tennyson on grounds
ol cruelty.
