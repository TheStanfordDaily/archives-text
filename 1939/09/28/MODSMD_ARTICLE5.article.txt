# Costume Class Exhibits Sketches
## 
### 
An exhibit of costume designs is
now being presented in the foyer of
Memorial Hall. Six panels of dif
ferent. sketches by advanced students
are being shown.
The designs wdrc completed in
Mrs. Helen Green's class on costume
design, and feature costumes for
"Little Women," "St. Joan," "Tobias
and the Angel," general historical
designs, and modern adaptations
from historical dresses. Students
whose work is shown in the exhibit
include Virginia Sharpe, Susy May
Lynch, Florence Hinds, Chester
Johnson, Adcle Stimmel, Irma Lee
Johnson and Helen Richards.
