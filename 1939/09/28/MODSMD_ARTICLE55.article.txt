# War Libe Slab To Be Poured
## 
### 
Pouring of the 2,580,000-pound
floating foundation block of cement
for the Hoover Library of War,
Peace, and Revolution is expected to
begin about the middle of next week,
according to information received
yesterday from John Leavers, su
perintendent of the construction
crew.
"My construction crew, at present
numbering about 40 men, will spend
the rest of the week placing the re
inforcing steel for the 'big slab',"
Mr. Leavers said yesterday. "Next
Tuesday we will begin to pour ce
ment for the outside footings, and
from there pouring will be shifted
to the foundation block."
