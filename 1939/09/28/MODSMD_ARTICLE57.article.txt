# BANKERS ELECT KOENEKE
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Sept. 27 (UP).—The
nominating committee of the Amer
ican Bankers Association tonight
chose H. W. Koeneke, Ponca City,
Okla., for second vice-president—a
post that will advance him to the
presidency in two years.
