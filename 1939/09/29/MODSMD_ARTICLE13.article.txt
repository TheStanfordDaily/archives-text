# Dean Yost to Fell Of European Trip
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will be the first
speaker of the year at Luncheon
Club, to be held in the Nitery next
Wednesday noon.
She will relate her experiences in
Europe this summer and her voyage
home on the "Queen Mary." Dr. D.
Elton Trueblood, chaplain of the
University, will be chairman of the
meetinir.
