# Band Will March At O.S.C. Game
## 
### 
The Stanford band held a suc
cessful marching rehearsal yester
day afternoon and will appear in
full uniform at Saturday's game
with Oregon State, according to Bob
Strom, manager. Bill Okker, who
has led the Cardinal musicians as
drum major for the past twelve
years, will be on hand to lead the
band again this fall.
Several places in the band are still
open, according to Strom, and any
musicians interested are asked to
report to the band house at 1 o'clock
Saturday afternoon. If necessary,
uniforms will be issued and a re
hearsal held so that the new men
may appear with the band that day.
