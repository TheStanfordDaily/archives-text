# Brooklyn Reaches Seven-year Peak
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 1 (UP). Brook
lyn's Dodgers reached their, highest
National League position in seven
years today when they clinched third
place by winning the first game of a
scheduled double-header from the
Philadelphia Phillies, 3-2. The night
cap was rained out.
The Dodgers won the game in the
seventh, when they scored twice on
Cookie Lavagetto's triple, an error,
a walk and Jimmy Ripple's single.
Casey won his 15th victory after al
lowing only three hits.
