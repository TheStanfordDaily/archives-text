# Track Candidates To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Oliver "Tex" Byrd, newly ap
pointed track coach, announced a
meeting of all varsity and freshman
track candidates for tonight at 8
o'clock in room 41 of the B.A.C.
Policies and plans for the coming
season will be discussed.
Varsity trackmen will be exam
ined today between 4 and 5 o'clock
in the B.A.C. No equipment will
be handed out until a thorough
physical examination is completed.

Unless Japan's quotations for raw
silk go down, prices on hosiery will
be sky-high this fall. A pound of
silk will make only a dozen pairs of
silk stockings.
