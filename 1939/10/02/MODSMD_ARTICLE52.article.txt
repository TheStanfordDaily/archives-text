# Bulletin Board

TRVOLTS FOR 'THE GREAT DI
VIDE" will be held today and to
morrow from 3 to 5 o'clock in the
afternoon and 7:30 to 9 o'clock in
the evening in Room 205 of Me
morial Hall.

in the Women's Clubhouse at 1
o'clock today.

the A.S.S.U. office from 1 to 5
o'clock.

basement of the Women's Club
house tomorrow at 7:30 o'clock.

members should call the airport
today to make appointments for
their physical examinations.

the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30
o'clock Wednesday, October 4.

discussed.
DAILY STAFF will meet at 1:15
o'clock today.
