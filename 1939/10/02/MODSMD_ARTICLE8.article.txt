# Pearl Harbor to Get 'Security Patrol'
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 1 (UP).-A
"security patrol" of detachments of
the United States battle fleet will be
based at Pearl Harbor, Hawaii,
guarding the Pacific from the equa
tor to Alaska, it was learned today.
