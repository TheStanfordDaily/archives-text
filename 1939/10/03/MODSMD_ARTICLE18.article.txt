# Bridgroom Jailed Alter Ceremony
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 2 (UP).—
Harold Cooper, 21, went to jail for
his honeymoon today. Superior
Judge George Steiger, before whom
the youth was arraigned on an auto
theft charge, performed the cere
mony jn which Virginia Graviana,
17, became Cooper's bride.
