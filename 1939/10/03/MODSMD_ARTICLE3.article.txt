# Tryoutee Course Mapped for Daily
## 
### 
Tryoutees for work on the Daily
Editorial staff are requested by Edi
tor Larry Livingston to appear on
October 10. This initial meeting will
be held in room 8 of the Press Build
ing at 5 o'clock, with all freshmen,
sophomores, and junior transfers
welcome.
Livingston emphasized the fact
that no previous journalistic experi
ence is necessary, since a full course
in newswriting, copyreading and
head writing will be offered during
tryout period, which will last two
weeks.
Conducting the tryout period,
Managing Editor Arch Jenkins will
be assisted by Adolph Lurie, news
editor, and Marlyn Whedon, staff
secretary. At the conclusion of the
training session an assignment and a
test on material covered will be
given and staff appointments made.
Those who cannot attend the first
meeting are asked to see Jenkins in
the Daily Shack before October 10.
