# Rally Tryouts Continue Today
## Chairman Announces Pompon Sale Finish 
### 
Searching for new ideas and new
talent, Rally Committee Chairman
Carl Phillips requested that all
freshman interested in trying out
for Rally Com work should meet
this afternoon at 1:30 o'clock in
room 13 of the Old Union above the
A.S.S.U. office.
Tryouts for new students will be
conducted throughout the afternoon,
and any new ideas for card stunts,
yells, songs, etc., will be welcomed
by the commnittee.
At the same time Phillips an
nounced that pompons will be on
sale for the last time this afternoon
for 25 cents at the A.S.S.U. office
from 1 to 5 o'clock. Pompons are re
quired for all women rooters at the
football games.
Additional announcements of in
terest to Rally Committee membei's
disclosed that all members must ob
tain their special tickets for the Big
Game at the B.A.C. office before
tomorrow afternoon at 5 o'clock. All
committee members are requested to
attend the meeting in the basement
of the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30
o'clock this evening.
