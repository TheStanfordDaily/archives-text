# Music Authority Lectures Today
## 
### 
Dom Anselm Hughes, noted Eng
lish authority on early music, will
give a lecture on "The Origins of
Harmony in Western Europe" today
at 4:15 o'clock in the Education
Building auditorium, Mr. Hughes'
lecture will deal with the archaeol
ogy of music, birth of free composi
tion, and growth of harmony.
Today's lecture will mark Mr.
Hughes' second appearance at the
University. A previous lecture tour
brought him here in 1934. Mr.
Hughes has come from Nashdom
Abbey, Burnham, Bucks, England,
where he is Prior; he is a member
of the only Benedictine order ex
tant in England.
