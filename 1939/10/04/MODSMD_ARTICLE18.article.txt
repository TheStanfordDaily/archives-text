# Stanford Dames Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford Dames will hold their
first meeting of the year tomorrow
afternoon at 2 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Mrs. C. M. Gordon will have
charge of the program, which will
be a J fashion show by Roos Bros.
Election of officers, except the presi
dent, will be held. Mrs. R. L. Mc-
Nitt, president, has asked all wives
of students to attend.
