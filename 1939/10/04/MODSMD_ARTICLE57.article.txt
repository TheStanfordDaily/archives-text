# SALVATION HEAD MOVED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 3 (UP).—
Benjamin Orames, for seven years
commander of the Salvation Army in
the territory composed of the 11
Pacific states and the Hawaiian
Islands, has been transferred to To
ronto as territorial commander for j
Canada, it was announced today.
