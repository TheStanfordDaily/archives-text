will meet tonight at 7:45 o'clock a)
the home of Mrs. David Starr Jor
dan. 330 Serra Street. Dr. If. 11.

dress the group on "The Sovirl
Policy in the Present War and Its
Implications."

for the Soviet policy and the effect
of the policy as regards the rest of
the world in the present crisis. He
will also discuss the policy's sig
nificance concerning the Communist
and National-Socialist revolutions.
