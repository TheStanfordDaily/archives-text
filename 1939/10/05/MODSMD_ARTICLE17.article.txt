# Wilburs to Greet Transfer Students
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur will give an informal recep
tion at their home on Lagunita
Knoll tomorrow evening at 8 o'clock,
for all new Upper Division and
graduate students.
Several members of the faculty
and student leaders will be present.
This is the last of the special affairs
afforded transfer students in the gen
eral orientation program of this
quarter.
