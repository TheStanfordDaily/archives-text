# PHELAN STRESSES DEFENSE
## 
### 
SEATTLE. Oct. 4 (UP).—Despite
two successive rainy days, Coach
Jimmy Phelan continued to stress
pass defense in University of Wash
ington football practice today, warn
ing that, the U.C.L.A. Bruins would
head for Seattle with a bag of trick
plays.
