# Wilburs to Receive Transfers Tonight
## 
### 
An informal reception given by
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur at their home on Lagunita Knoll
tonight at 8 o'clock will wind up the
general orientation program for
transfer students this quarter.
The reception is being held espe
cially for new Upper Division trans
fers and graduate students. Several
members of the faculty and student,
leaders will also be present.
