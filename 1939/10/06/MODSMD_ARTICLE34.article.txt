# WESLEY FOUNDATION TO HOLD DISCUSSION
## 
### 
The weekly meeting of the Wesley
Foundation will be held at 7:30
o'clock Sunday evening at 625 Ham
ilton Avenue. Four discussion
groups will meet before the regular
meeting. These will be seminars in
religion, international problems,
personal problems in religion, and
poetry of social and religious inspi
ration.
The United States was England's
best source of supply for principal
products in 1937.
