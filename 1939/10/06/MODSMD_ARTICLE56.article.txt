DEBATE MANAGER TRYOUTS—
Interviews with tryoutees today
between 4 and 5 o'clock in the
debate office of the Old Union.
GAIETIES SONGS, SKITS due to
day from 2 to 4 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
"Y" MEMBERSHIP DRIVE workers
must turn their kits in to the "Y"
office today.
CONCERT SERIES TRYOUTEES
must turn in all ticket orders to
the Concert Series office today.
