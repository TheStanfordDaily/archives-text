# Puerto Rico Ship Reports Collision
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 5 (UP).—The
Puerto Rico Line's passenger ship
Coamo radioed Coast Guard head
quarters tonight that a collision had
occurred 17 miles off Seagirt Light,
and asked that a cutter be sent to
the scene.
