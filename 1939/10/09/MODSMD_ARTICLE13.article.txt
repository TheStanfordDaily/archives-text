# S.P.U. to Elect Leaders Tonight
## 
### 
All students interested in reor
ganizing the Stanford Political Un
ion should meet tonight at 7:30
o'clock in the basement of the
Women's Clubhouse, according to
Mary Maud Mitchell, secretary of the
organization last year and acting
chairman for this evening's meet
ing.
In announcing tonight's meeting,
Miss Mitchell stressed the fact that
the student members control the
organization and its discussions
themselves and do not rely on out
side speakers for new ideas.
Officers for the coming year will
be elected at tonight's first regular
meeting.
