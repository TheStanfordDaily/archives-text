# Glee Club Calls For Tryoutees
## 
### 
Joel Carter, Stanford Glee Club
director, announces that the tryouts
for new members of the organiza
tion will be held tonight. All stu
dents are invited to try out, but
freshmen especially are encouraged
to attend this meeting. Old members
will also meet tonight at 8:30 o'clock
in Room 84.
The year's program includes a
banquet at the Palace Hotel on Big
Game night and the club plans to
have some of its members in the
supporting cast of the Gaieties.
