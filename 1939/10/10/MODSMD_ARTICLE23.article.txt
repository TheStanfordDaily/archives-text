# UNDER NAVAL ESCORT
## 
### 

PICTURED AT ITS DOCK, before leaving on the Atlantic crossing
that has brought it into the recent headlines, the Iroquois is at pres
ent nearing American shores under the protection of U.S. destroyers.
A statement by Admiral Eric Raeder of the German fleet, warning
that this American vessel might suffer the same fate of the Athenia
was the reason for the escort.
