# HERE'S A LITTLE INSIDE STUFF
## 
### 

RETURNING TO THEIR HOME TEPEE after an unsuccessful trip into Northern wilds, the Indians are
preparing religiously to score a direct hit against an invading Bruin next Saturday. Dressing before going
out for a practice session are Swede Johansen and, sit ting from left to right, Aubrey Austin, Norm Standlee,
and Rill Braheny.
