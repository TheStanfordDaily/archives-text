# Quad Schedules Picture Dates
## 
### 
Photographers will begin taking
pictures for the 1940 Stanford Quad
tomorrow morning at the Quad of
fices, according to Newton Russell,
editor. Hours at which pictures will
be taken have been scheduled each
morning from 9:30 to 12 o'clock and
each afternoon from 1 to 5 o'clock.
Members of the Quad staff started
last night at the fraternities to sign
up members for appointments and
eventually all campus living groups
will be contacted. It is requested
that, appointments be kept exactly
as signed for.
