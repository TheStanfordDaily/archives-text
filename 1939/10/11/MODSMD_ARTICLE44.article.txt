# Varsity Gymnasts Call for New Men
## 
### 
The varsity gymnastics team has
returned this quarter with great en
thusiasm in their workouts, accord
ing to Coach Ken Edwards. Three
new men have joined the team and
there is still room lor more. The
team will work out until the Cal
match in spring quarter.
Coach Edwards also stated that all
students who are interested in
learning about gymnastics or in
working out on the equipment
should see him any afternoon in the
equipment room in the gym.
