# 'Y' Social Service Plans Announced
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. social service pro
gram for this year will include dis
cussions led by prominent social
workers from San Francisco, tours
to various social projects and actual
field work, announced Marge Tight,
chairman of the group.
The first meeting of the Social
Service Group of the "Y" will be
held this afternoon at 4:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse. All
those interested, but unable to at
tend, should call Miss Tight at Palo
Alto 8795.
Other "Y" activities for today in
clude an "interest finding" meeting
of the Religion Group, led by Kay
Rogers at 4:30 o'clock in the "Y"
rooms, and a meeting of the Poster
Committee in the "Y" rooms at 4
o'clock. All meetings are open to
women interested in the activities.
