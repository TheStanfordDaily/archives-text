# Burtt Will Speak At Daily Tryouts
## 
### 
Daily tryoutees will hear the sub
ject of headline writing discussed by
Associate Editor Jack Burtt when
they gather for the second meeting
of their instruction period at 5
o'clock today in Room 8 of the
Press Building.
Students who wish to try out for
the Daily staff but did not attend
Tuesday's meeting may sign up this
afternoon. If possible they should
see Managing Editor Arch Jenkins
in the Daily office before the meet
ing.
A card file is being used to record
the work done on the Daily by try
outees and will determine, along
with grades made on written tests,
appointments to the stafT at the end
of the instruction period.
