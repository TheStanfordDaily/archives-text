# Orchesis Tryouts To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Orchesis, honorary dance society,
will hold tryouts for new members
this afternoon at 5 o'clock in the
dance studio of the Women's Gym
nasium. Tryoutees will be tested on
various techniques and will be re
quired to present a short individual
composition.
The annual dance symposium for
the Orchesis members of the Bay
Area will be held at Stanford this
year on Oct. 28. The first part of
the day will be devoted to dance
techniques, and in the afternoon
composition will be taken up. All
activity will be led by various stu
dent representatives from each
school.
