# Military Society To Be Inspected
## 
### 
William C. Moyor, eighth corps
area inspector for Scabbard and
Blade, will be on Ihe campus Sun
day at 5 o'clock, to inspect the Stan
ford Scabbard and Blade chapter.
According to Gene Coldiron, cap
tain of the Stanford chapter, all
members must appear for inspection
at the barracks.
