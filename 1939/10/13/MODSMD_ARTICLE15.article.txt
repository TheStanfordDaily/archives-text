# Stern Laboratory Formally Opens
## 
### 
At a reception held yesterday in
San Francisco honoring Mrs. Louis
Stern, her gift to the University, the
Ruth Lucie Stern Research Labora
tory, was formally opened.
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur officiated at the reception, as
alumni and friends of the University
streamed in all afternoon to view
Ibis latest modern addition to the
Stanford School of Medicine unit.
The laboratory is to be devoted
chiefly to research work in neurol
ogy, a field in which Mrs. Stern is
greatly interested. Research in other
fields such as cancer, valley fever
and tuberculosis will also be car
ried on.
