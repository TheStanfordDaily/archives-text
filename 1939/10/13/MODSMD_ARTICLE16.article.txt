# Daily Continues Tryoutee Meets
## 
### 
Fifty-seven tryoutees attended the
Daily's second instruction meeting
of the quarter yesterday afternoon
and heard a talk on headline writ
ing by Associate Editor Jack Burtt.
Actual practice in writing heads for
United Press stories concluded the
session.
Students who have not attended
the tryoutee meetings but wish to
work for a place on the staff may
still sign up by getting in touch with
Managing Editor Arch Jenkins in
the Daily office.
