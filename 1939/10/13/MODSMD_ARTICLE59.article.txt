# L.A. Frosh Wins Miller Scholarship
## 
### 
The U.C.L.A. Division of the
Y.M.C.A. has established a schol
arship in honor of Clinton Miller,
one of the Cal students who cap
tured the famous Stanford axe. Mil
ler held the position of yell leader
back in the days of '98 when the
rivalry first began.
The first scholarship award was
given to Kevork Tashjian, a fresh
man from Los Angeles High School.
It was presented to him personally
by Miller, who is a former presi
dent of the Alumni Association and
ex-officio regent of the University.
