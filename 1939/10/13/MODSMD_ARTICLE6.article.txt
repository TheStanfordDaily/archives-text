# Pickets Withdrawn In Longshore Strike
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Oct. 12 (UP).—
Hope for final peaceful settlement of
Pacific Coast waterfront disputes
grew tonight as pickets were with
drawn from docks here and long
shoremen returned to work.
