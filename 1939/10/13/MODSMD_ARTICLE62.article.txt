# COUGAR PLAYER INJURED
## 
### 
SEATTLE. Oct. 13 (UP).- Thirty
six University of Washington foot
ball players tonight left for Pullman
where they meet the Washington
State College Club Saturday. Coach
Jimmy Phelan announced that Mau
rice Stacy, reserve fullback, suffered
a knee injury in workouts and may
not compete in the state's traditional
"Big Game."
