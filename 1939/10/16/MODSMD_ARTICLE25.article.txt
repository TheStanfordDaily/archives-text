# RUSS-TURK NEGOTIATIONS
## 
### 
MOSCOW. Oct. 15 (UP).—The re
sult of the protracted negotiations
between Russia and Turkey for a
Black Sea pact still was unknown
late tonight but the best-informed
quarters here continued to believe
that the negotiations virtually have
been completed and that signature
of the agreement may be expected
soon.
