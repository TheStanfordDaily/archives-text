# FLOGGING QUIZ TO OPEN
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 15 (UP).
—Two separate hearings will open
this week on charges that 43 San
Quentin prisoners were beaten with
straps and a garden hose in a mass
flogging that followed a hunger
strike last spring.
