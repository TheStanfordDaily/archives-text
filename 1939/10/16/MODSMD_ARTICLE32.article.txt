# Tropical Storm Heads for Bermuda
## 
### 
MIAMI, Fla., Oct. 15 (UP). — A
tropical storm in the Atlantic, with
winds possibly of hurricane inten
sity over a limited area, was located
tonight about 275 miles south-south
west of Bermuda moving in a north
easterly direction.
The federal hurricane warning
system said in an advisory issued
at 9:30 p.m. (EST) that the storm
had increased in intensity and was
attended by heavy gales and "pos
sibly a limited area of hurricane
winds"—7s miles an hour or more.
