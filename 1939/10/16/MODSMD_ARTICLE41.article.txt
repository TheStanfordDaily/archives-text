# Fair Crowds Break Record
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Oct. 15 (UP).-
The .San Francisco world's fair
started its final-two weeks' run to
day with a huge crowd that sur
passed the opening-day attendance
and caused traffic tie-ups on the
San Francisco Bay Bridge.
The first pneumatic tire for an
automobile was manufactured in
1890.
