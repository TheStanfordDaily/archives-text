# 'Y' Tea to Honor Transfer Women
## 
### 
The "Y" cabinet will meet at the
home of Barbara Templeton in Los
Altos this afternoon, leaving from
the "Y" rooms at 4:30 o'clock.
Plans for entertaining all transfer
women at a tea on Thursday after
noon were discussed at the first
meeting of the "Y" transfer group
last week. Other activities will in
clude talks by women active in cam
pus activities and vocational discus
sions, according to Alda Lehman,
chairman.
Members of the committee in
charge of the tea are Widney Wat
son, refreshments and hostesses;
Joan McKee, entertainment; and
Rose Marie Douglass, invitations.
