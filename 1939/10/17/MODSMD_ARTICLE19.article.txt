# Daily Cub Class Will Hear Editor
## 
### 
Daily tryoulces will gather for the
third time today in Room 8 of the
Press Building at 5 o'clock to hear
Editor Larry Livingston and Man
aging Editor Arch Jenkins explain
"How to write a news story" and
"How to write a sports story," re
spectively.
Trial assignments are to be turned
in typewritten to News Editor
Adolph Lurie Thursday; a general
assignment has been made cover
ing the regular Tuesday lecture,
with sportswriters covering football
practice. Substitute assignment is
another lecture during the week.
At the fourth and final meeting,
to be held Thursday, an examina
tion will be given on proofreading,
headwriting, and knowledge of
campus activities.
