# Gillies Will Speak At Geology Lunch
## 
### 
Mr. Donald Burton Gillies, presi
dent of the American Mining ami
Metallurgical Institute, will speak
tomorrow at a luncheon sponsored
by the Geological and Mining So
ciety of Stanford. The lunch is to
be held at 12:15 o'clock in the
Union.
All members of the faculty and
the students in the geology depart
ment, upper division engineers and
graduates in the mining department
are invited. The price will be 30
cents for students; half the cost of a
60-cent lunch being paid by the San
Francisco section of the A.I.M.E.
