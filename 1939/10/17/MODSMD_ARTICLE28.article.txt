# Debaters Discuss War at Cal Meet
## 
### 
"Can America Stay Out of War"
was the topic under discussion when
the collegiate Monthly Debate Con
ference met last Saturday on the
Cal campus in Eshelman Hall. Rep
resenting Stanford were Helen Sev
erance, Willard Muller, and Robert
Libott.
Formal speeches were presented
in the morning, with a general round
table discussion following luncheon,
Next conference session will be on
Nov. 18 at San Jose State. The lopic
to be discussed is "Labor and
Capital."
