# Thornhill Talks To Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Giving a chalk talk for the lay
man, Stanford football coach Tiny
Thornhill addressed Luncheon
Club yesterday and entertained his
audience with diagrams of plays,
comments on school spirit, and in
formation about the team.
The Card coach diagrammed his
famous No. '20 inside tackle play
and explained the variations of the
No. 17 standard reverse play. He
revealed the fact that among coach
es, the reverse is also known as
the "headache play."
