# 'Y' Tea to Honor Transfer Women
## 
### 
An informal tea for all transfer
women will be given in the "Y"
rooms from 4 to 5 o'clock this after
noon. Cider and doughnuts will be
handled by Barbara Taylor's com
mittee. Joan McKee will have
charge of the entertainment.
Freshman women will meet at the
same time in the Women's Club
house fpr a discussion with Dr.
Bertha Mason, health director for
San Jose State College, on the sub
ject of sex hygiene.
At 4:30 o'clock junior and senior
members of the "Y" will meet in
the "Y" rooms to discuss the ques
tion of how the "Y" can better meet
the needs of upper division students
and to plan for a junior-senior dis
cussion group.
