# Wilbur Announces Fund Scholarships
## 
### 
Fellowships averaging $1500 for a
full year's work in any one of a
wide range of fields and open to
southerners who wish to work on
some problem of the South have
been announced by the Julius Ros
enwald Fund, according to President
Ray Lyman Wilbur.
Application should be made to Dr.
George M. Reynolds, director for
fellowships, '1901 Ellis Avenue.
Chicago.
