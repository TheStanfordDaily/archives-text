# Mothers' Club To Sponsor Play
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club will
sponsor Paul Vincent Carroll's Irish
comedy, "Shadow and Substance,"
for its annual fall benefit on Nov. 9,
10, and 11, it was announced yester
day. The play is to be presented in
the Memorial Hall in co-operation
with the Division of Specch and
Drama.
During its New York run, the
drama was acclaimed by critics as
the best foreign play. Tickets for the
performances, which are to be the
only showings of the production on
the Wesl Coast, may be purchased
for $1 at the A.S.S.U. offices or at
Keeble's in Palo Alio
