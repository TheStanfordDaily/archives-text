# 'WHOOSIS' ADAPTABLE
## 
### 
' How'd you like a whoosis that
turns inside out to make a bustle, a
pocketbook sash, a hood, a cowl, a
plastron, and several different styles
of turban? It's one of those cute
things they've named "Bashlik" on
account of it's an adaptation of the
so-called head-dress of the Cau
casus.
