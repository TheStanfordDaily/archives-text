# Fads to Keep Warm Amuse Farm Co-eds
## 
### 
From women's colleges come fads
designed to keep you warm. Most
startling of these are the knee
length cotton sox, embroidered in
Tyrolean style. Probably these will
never be accepted by Stanford,
where even bobbie socks are frown
ed upon.
Another warm fad already adopted
by some Stanford women is angora
mittens.
