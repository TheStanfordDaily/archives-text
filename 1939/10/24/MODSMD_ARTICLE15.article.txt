# Leads in Carroll Drama Announced
## 
### 
Leads in the Division of Speech
and Drama's production of Paui
Vincent Carroll's "Shadow and
Substance," to be given in Memorial
Hall, will be played by Mildred
Green and Richard Gleyer, it was
announced yesterday by Everett
Glass, director.
The play will be given on Friday
and Saturday nights, Nov. 10 and 11,
and on Thursday afternoon, Nov. 9.
The matinee performance, however,
is given entirely over to the Stan
ford Mothers' Club, which is spon
soring the play.
Others in the cast include: Philip
Harris, Mary McClerkin, Frank
Burt, Hugh Keenan, Helen Ann
Young. Robert Ridgeway. and Jean
Pennington.
