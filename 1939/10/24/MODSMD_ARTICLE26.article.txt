# RAIN FLOODS HONOLULU
## 
### 
HONOLULU. T.H., Oct. 23 (UP).—
Inter-island airline schedules to Hilo
were canceled and highways were
inundated today as the heaviest rain
storm of the season deluged Hono
ilulu.
