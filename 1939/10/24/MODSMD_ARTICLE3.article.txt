# Kappas Fete Farm Foreign Students
## 
### 
Foreign students of Stanford Uni
versity were feted with a party at
the Kappa Kappa Gamma house
Sunday afternoon, hosted by the
Kappas and the A.S.S.U. Interna
tional Committee.
Guests of honor were A.S.S.U.
officers, including President Ken
Cuthbertson and Vice-president
Coline Upshaw. The afternoon was
spent in informal talks and games,
and many of the foreign students
popped corn for the first time in
their lives.
