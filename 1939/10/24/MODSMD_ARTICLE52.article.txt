# Costume Director To Speak in S.F.
## 
### 
Mrs. Helen Green,' Division of
Speech and Drama costume director,
will deliver a lecture from lantern
slides on "Ballet, Then and Now,"
at 8 o'clock Thursday evening in the
north lecture room of the San Fran
cisco Museums of Art.
Following the lecture, William
Christansen, ballet master of the San
Francisco Opera Ballet, will present
a laboratory demonstration on how
ballets are built. Mrs. Green is the
costume designer and director for
the opera ballet. There is no admis
sion charge.
