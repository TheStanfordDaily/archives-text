# Museum Displays Indian Collection
## 
### 
Valued by officials of the Stanford
Museum at $5000, a collection of 400
North American Indian baskets has
been placed on display in the build
ing.
The display, which was donated by
Mrs. Frederick Henry Colburn of
San Francisco as a memorial to her
late husband, has been formed dur
ing travels among the Indian tribes.
Included among the items arc
woven water jugs ranging from a
few inches to eight feet, bowls used
by the squaws as both hats and food
dishes, and an antique bow and ar
row set.
