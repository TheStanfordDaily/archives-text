Library. A gilt of the Wells Fargo
Bank and Union Trust Company,
the exhibit is an addition to the
material on the history of business
which is being assembled at the
Graduate School of Business, ac
cording to Edwin T. Coman, libra
rian.
The coin reproductions, which are
intended to illustrate the commer
cial, political, religious, and artistic
life of various civilizations, are as
sembled on twelve trays, with ac
companying written explanations by
