# Roble to Elect Representatives
## 
### 
Roble women today will vote for
W.A.A. and Women's Conference
representatives.
The six nominees selected by the
sponsors are: Charlotte Drea, Har
riet Spicer, and Madeline Robb, for
the Women's Conference office; and
Ruth Atkins, Dorothea Paulman, and
Joan Hill for W.A.A.
There will be four balloting pe
riods. each lasting three-quarters of
an hour, starting at 11:30, 12:45. 5:30,
and 6:45.
