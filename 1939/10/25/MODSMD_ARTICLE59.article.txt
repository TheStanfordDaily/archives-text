# VOLCANO ERUPTS
## 
### 
SEATTLE, Oct. 24 (UP).—An un
verified report from Kanatak, a small
fishing village in southwestern Alas
ka, said tonight 8,400-foot Mount
VeniuminofT on the Alaska Penin
sula was erupting for the second
time this year.
