# Bookstore Issues Ballot Applications
## 
### 
Stanford students of voting age
who wish to write to their home
towns for absentee ballots may ob
tain the necessary blanks at the
Stanford Bookstore, according to
information issued by George Mur
phy, chairman of the Palo Alto Citi
zens' Committee.
At the same time the committee
announced that any notary public in
Palo Alto will provide free notariza
tion for all campus voters who wish
to certify their residence before
sending the blanks to their various
county clerks. Any additional in
formation may be obtained from the
Palo Alto Chamber of Commerce at
PA 21955.
