# Roble Entertained By Lagunita Sophs
## 
### 
Sophomore women of Lagunita
gave n "Country-Fair" tea yesterday
afternoon for the Roble women.
Candied apples and popcorn were
served in the Lagunita patio where
the freshmen had an opportunity to
meet their next-door neighbors. The
guests were also shown through
some of the Casas.
