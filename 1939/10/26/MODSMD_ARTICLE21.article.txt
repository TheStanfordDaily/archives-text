# Stanford Students Aid Women's Club
## 
### 
Assisting the Stanford Women's
Club of San Francisco with a benefit
bridge tea to be held Saturday at
the Sarah Dix Hamlin school in the
city are seven Stanford students.
They are Roberta Howell, Barbara
Moyes, Jane Bruer, Violet Schoning,
Carolyn Smith, Mavis Moore, and
Betsy McElroy.
The tea has been arranged to raise
funds for a $300 scholarship to Ik
given annually to a freshman girl
entering Stanford from San Fran
cisco.
