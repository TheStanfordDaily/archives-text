# Badminton Starts On Men's Courts
## 
### 
Badminton nets will be put. up on
the courts in the men's gym for the
U'C of the general student body
from 1 to 2 o'clock on Mondays,
Wednesdays, and Fridays, according
to Myron Sprague, associate director
of physical education.
If sufficient students are interested
in playing badminton at these times,
the nets will be put up regularly,
added Sprague.
