# Senior Skate Bids Sell Fast
## 
### 
Hurry! Hurry! Hurry!
Step right up and get your tickets
for the senior roller skating party.
The accommodations are limited - to
200 and the tickets are going fast.
The main event is on Saturday
from 3:30 to 7:30 o'clock at. the San
Mateo Skating Rink. The class will
be taken to the rink in Greyhound
busses, which will leave the library
parking lot at. 3 o'clock.
It was emphasized by Bill Jess and
Bobbie Rankin, co-chairman of the
affair, that early busses will leave
the rink for those whose social
calendar necessitates an early re
turn.
Tickets are now on sale at the
A.S.S.U. offices and by representa
tives in the living groups for 50
cents. This fee is only half the cost,
the rest being supplied from the
senior class treasury.
Dinner will be served at the rink
by Wilsons and punch will be on
tap all afternoon.
