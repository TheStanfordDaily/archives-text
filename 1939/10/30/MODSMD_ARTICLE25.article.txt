# Stanford Choir Sings On Last Day of Fair
## 
### 
Sixty-five members of the Stanford
Choir sang at the Temple of Re
ligion and Peace at the Golden Gate
International Exposition yesterday
afternoon at 4 o'clock. The singers
were directed by Warren D. Allen,
of the music department, and were
accompanied by a double quartet
from the Stanford Band, conducted
by Charles R. Bubb, Jr.
