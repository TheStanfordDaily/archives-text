# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
INTERNATIONAL COMMITTEE
hold interviews today and tomor
row from 3:30 to 5:30 o'clock in
the Ex Committee room in the Old
Union. Freshmen, sophomores,
and juniors are urged to try out.
"Y" POSTER COMMITTEE will
meet at 4 o'clock in the "Y" rooms.
DAILY STAFF meets in the shack
at 1:15 o'clock today.
CIIA PA KR A L SALES( i IRLS meet
ing in Chaparral offices at 5 o'clock
today. Important,
WOMEN'S AUXILIARY of Hammer
and Coffin meeting today at 4
o'clock in room 8 of the Journal
ism department.
GLEE CLUB meets tonight at 8 o'-
clock in room 84.
RALLY COMMITTEE MEMBERS
who have been working on card
stunts for the U.S.C. game will
meet in the Old Union at 1:15
o'clock.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE meets to
day at 1 o'clock in Women's Club
house.
