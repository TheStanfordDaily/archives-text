# Townsend Derides Ham and Eggs Bill
## 
### 
co
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 30 (UP).—
Dr. Francis E. Townsend, founder of
the pension movement bearing his
name, said tonight he intended to
vote against the $30-Every-Thursday
plan Nov. 7 "because California's
credit will not be worth a plugged
nickel if the measure passes."
Townsend said the "true cause of
old age pensions will sufTer irrepar
able damage" and will be retarded
"at least a generation if the foolish
ham and eggs proposition wins."
