# Phillips Suffers Brain Concussion
## 
### 
Sustaining a fractured skull and
a brain concussion after a ladder
struck him Saturday afternoon at
the Beta house. Carl Phillips, men's
head of Rally Committee, is reported
as slowly improving. An operation
to relieve nerve pressure was per
formed Sunday evening at the Palo
Alto Hospital.
Although the accident occurred
Saturday afternoon, the after ef
fects were not felt until Sunday
morning at 2 o'clock, when Phillips
became unconscious and was rushed
to the hospital.
Dr. Harold Sox and Miss E. West
all, the attending physician and
nurse, are unable to foretell how
long Phillips will be kept in the
hospital.
