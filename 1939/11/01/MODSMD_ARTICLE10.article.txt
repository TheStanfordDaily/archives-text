# Frye Announces Novice Ring Bouts
## 
### 
"We're gonna have plenty of good
bouts. The show will be well worth
anyone's two-bits," enthusiastically
predicted Bill Wilber, assistant box
ing coach, yesterday, regarding the
annual novice boxing tournament
which will be held Thursday, Nov.
16.
At the same time, Head Coach
Clayton Frye announced that sign
ups are progressing satisfactorily.
"The date of the first elimination
bouts has tentatively been set for
Nov. 9, but. we may move it. up if
the size of the list makes it neces
sary."
