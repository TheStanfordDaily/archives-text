# Yost Entertains South Americans
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will act as host
ess today at a luncheon given in
honor of a group of nine Soulh
and Central American women who,
coming on a good will mission to
the United States, will be guests of
the University.
Dean Yost considers their visits of
particular interest at a time such as
this, when friendly relations with
the Latin American countries arc
of special importance.
