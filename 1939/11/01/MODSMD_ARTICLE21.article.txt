# Hulten to Speak On Stanford Hour
## 
### 
Charles M. Hulten, assistant, pro
fessor of journalism, will speak over
KGO tonight at 9:45 o'clock on the
weekly Stanford Hour. His subject
will be, ''What is a good newspa
per?"
A former newspaper man of long
experience, Professor Hulten will
give the professional point of view
on the problem, which has recently
been answered through public
opinion polls throughout the coun
try.
