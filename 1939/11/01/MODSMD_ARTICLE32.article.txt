CHICAGO, Oct. 30 (UP).—One of
the leading money winners of the
1938 professional golf season, Dick
Metz of Chicago signed a one-year
contract Monday as pro at the Sub
urban Oak Park, Ill., Country Club.
He succeeded Ren Smith.
