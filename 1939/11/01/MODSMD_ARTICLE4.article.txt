# Orchesis to Dance At Sacramento
## 
### 
Ten members of Orchesis, college
dance club, will lead a dance sym
posium of Northern California high
schools and junior colleges at Sacra
mento High School on Saturday.
Approximately two hundred and
fifty secondary school students are
expected to be present at the event
which will last from 9 till 12 o'clock
in the morning. Luncheon will be
served, following period techniques
and composition,
