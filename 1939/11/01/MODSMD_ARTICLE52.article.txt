# Circle S Society Will Meet Today
## 
### 
Members of Stanford's Circle S
Society will meet at 12:15 o'clock to
day at the Delt house to have their
Quad pictures and also to discuss
plans for the coming novice boxing
tournament, according' to President
Dick Hoover.
Circle S, composed of minor sport
letterrnen, is the sponsor of the an
nual novice fisticuffing tourney,
which will be climaxed Nov. 16 in
the Pavilion.
