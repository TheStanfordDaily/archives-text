# Dance to Be Held Here on Nov. 11
## 
### 
For those who do not attend the
U.S.C. game at Los Angeles on Sat
urday, Nov. 11, Toyon and Branner
halls are sponsoring a free dance
open to the entire campus.
Don Schary and his 10-piece Ber
keley orchestra have been engaged
to play for the affair, according to
Bob Einstein, dance chairman.
Dress will be sport and only couples
will be admitted.
