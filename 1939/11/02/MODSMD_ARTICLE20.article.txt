# Record Program To Be Given Today
## 
### 
A program of recordings will be
presented by the "Y" music group
in the Women's Clubhouse at 4:30
o'clock this afternoon. All men and
women on the campus are invited,
according to Norma McKenzie,
chairman of the group.
This afternoon's program will in
clude Tschaikowsky's Fourth Sym
phony, "Prelude to Act 111 - ' and
"Bridal Chorus" from "Lohengrin,"
"One Fine Day," from "Madame
Butterfly." and from "La Boheme,"
"Mi Chiamano Mimi, the last two
sung by Lucrezia Bori.
