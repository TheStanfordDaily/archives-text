# Roth to Discuss Labor Problems
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, president of tin*
San Francisco Employers' Associa
tion, will speak to the labor prob
lems class this morning at 10 o'clock,
in Room 50 on "Waterfront Labor
Problems."
Roth was former comptroller of
Stanford and has held the position
of president of the Waterfront Em
ployers' Association of San Fran
cisco.
Henry Schmidt, president of the
San Francisco Longshoremen's and
Warehousemen's Union, will present
labor's side of the waterfront prob
lem at a future class meeting.
Schmidt is now being questioned by
the Dies Committee, according to
Clark Kerr, assistant professor of
economics.
