# Mermen Whipped By San Jose, 5-4
## 
### 
San Jose State upset Stanford last
night, 5 to 4, in a water polo game
played at San Jose. The Indian cubs
lost to the Spartan. 11 to 4.
Captain Bob Weinbe of State, led
his varsity mates with three goals.
For Stanford, Captain Bob Plate,
Bill Bleecker, Bob Allen, and Dick
Hoover all scored once.
Players attributed the upset to the
unusual conditions of the 20-yard
pool which was shallow at one end.
