# Allen to Present Recital, 4:15 Today
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give the first of a series
of Thursday organ recitals this af
ternoon in Memorial Chapel at 4:15
o'clock.
Mr. Allen has selected for his re
cital: "Sonata No. 6," by Mendel
ssohn; "Dance of the Sylphs," by
Berlioz; two sketches by Robert
Schumann; and a fantasia, "Ad nos
Salutarem undam," by Liszt.
