# SALVEMINI TO SPEAK TO EPISCOPAL CLUB
## 
### 
Professor Gaetano Salvemini will
speak to the Stanford Episcopal Club
this Sunday night on "Contemporary
Italy." The group will meet at All
Saints' Church in the parish house
at Waverly and Hamilton at 6:00
o'clock.
Professor Salvemini, who spoke
to the Luncheon Club last Wednes
day, is on campus doing research at
the Hoover War Library. He is plan
ning a book on Italian Diplomacy
during the first World War. All
those members of the club who are
interested are urged to attend this
meeting.
