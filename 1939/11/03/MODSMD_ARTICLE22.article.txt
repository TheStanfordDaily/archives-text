# WESTMINSTER CLUB TO HOLD PARTY
## 
### 
Mary Margaret Roth will lead the
meeting of the Westminster Club
this Sunday evening at 7:15 o'clock.
It will be held as usual in the Pres
byterian Church. This regular meet
ing will be followed by a sing.
Saturday night in the parish house
of the church the club is holding a
party, to which all members are cor
dially invited.
