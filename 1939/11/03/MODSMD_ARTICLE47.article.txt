# Bulletin Board
##

QUAD will be sold at the post office
this morning from 9 until 12
o'clock.
TRYOUTEES for Gaieties Technical
Crew see Chet Johnson from 2 to
4 o'clock in main lobby of Memo
rial Theater.
KLAHOYA CLUB off-campus wom
en will hold Mothers' Tea from 4
to 5 o'clock at Lagunita Court.
EPISCOPAL CLUB will meet at All
Saints Church in parish house at
Waverly and Hamilton at 6 o'clock
Sunday night.
ROGER WILLIAMS CLUB will hold
regular service Sunday evening at
7:15 o'clock at the aßptist Church.
Fireside will be held at Women's
Clubhouse at 8:30 o'clock Sunday
night.
WESTMINSTER CLUB will hold
meeting Sunday evening at 7:15
o'clock in Presbyterian Church.
POM-PONS WILL BE SOLD in the
A.S.S.U. office from 1 to 3 o'clock
today.

be taken in the stadium at 1:15
o'clock Saturday.
