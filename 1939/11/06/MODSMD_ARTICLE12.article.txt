# Gros to Discuss Crisis at Capitol
## 
### 
"Washington in Crisis" will be the
subject of a lecture by Robert Gros,
former Stanford student manager
and political instructor, in the audi
torium of the Education Building
tonight at 8 o'clock.
Mr. Gros will relate his expe
riences while sitting in on Senate
debates and conferences which were
attended by such Washington no
tables as President Franklin Roose
velt, Secretaries Cordell Hull and
Harold L. Ickcs, and Attorney Gen
eral Frank Murphy.
The price of the lecture is 35 cents
aud is sponsored by the Young Men's
and Young Women's Christian Asso
ciations. The proceeds of the lec
ture will be used for carrying on the
activities of the Associations.
