# Varsity Netmen Nose Out Frosh
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity tennis team
barely nosed out the finest Papoose
net outfit of recent years, 4-3, in a
practice match last Friday.
Freshman Jimmy Wade, a trans
! fer from Pasadena J. C. who will be
eligible to play on the varsity spring
quartet, upset Larry Dee, national
singles championship finalist last
summer, 6-0, 6-1, in the feature
match.
Results:
Singles: Wade (F) del. Dee, 6-0,
j 6—l; Low (V) del. Neale, 6-1, 6-4;
Brock (V) def. Hutchinson, 4-6,
7-5, 7-5; Gates (V) def. Ink, 4-6,
6-0, 6-3; Lamb (F) def. Owen, 3-6,
7-5, 6-1; Kruger (F) del. Rose, 6-4,
6-4.
