# Row to Entertain Rushees Today
## 
### 
Freshmen and soph transfers will
retrace their steps to the Row today
and tomorrow for the second rush
ing period of the fall season.
Luncheon and dinner engagements
will be tilled by the rushees, a total
of four dates being the maximum
number on the schedule as released
by the Interfraternity Council.
Luncheons will be held at the re
spective houses from 11:30 to 2
o'clock, and dinner dates will be
from 6 to 8 o'clock. Rushees were
advised on rush cards not to accept
more than two periods with the
same fraternity during this rush
period.
In accordance with ruling of the
rushing regulations as formulated
by the Interfraternity Board of Con
trol, rushing oft' campus or at the
S.C. game will not be permitted this
week-end, since it is not a vacation
period listed in the University
Calendar.
