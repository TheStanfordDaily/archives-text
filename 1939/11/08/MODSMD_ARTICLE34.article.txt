# Matson Line Sails For Australia Today
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 7 (UP).—
The Matson liner Monterey, carrying
700 British subjects back to New
Zealand and Australia, will sail for
Los Angeles and Transpacific ports
at noon tomorrow, it was announced
today, following settlement of labor
disputes which delayed the ship's
departure since noon today.
