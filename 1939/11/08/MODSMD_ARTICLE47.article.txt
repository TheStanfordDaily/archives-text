# OREGON HALVES RETURN
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Ore., Nov. 7 (UP).—
The return of halfbacks Bob Deth
man and Bob Olson, injured in the
Southern California game, today
cheered Oregon State football fol
lowers as the Beavers drilled for
their week-end tussle with the Uni
versity of Oregon. Dethman suf
fered a cut cheek, and Olson a
sprained ankle.
