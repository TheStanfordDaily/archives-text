# Bulletin Board

ENCINA RALLY, 10 o'clock tonight
in lobby.
TUITION NOTE applications and
renewals may be taken out all next
week, November IIJ to 17.
PRE-MED SOCIETY will meet to
night at 7:li0 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. Following to
night Pre-Meds will meet every
week instead of every other week
as before.
ROBLE JOLLY-UP in Women's
Gym tonight, from 7 to 8 o'clock.
A.S.U. OPEN MEETING at 8 o'clock
tonight in the Women's Clubhouse.
Discussion of the German-Soviet
pact by Dr. Horowitz of San Fran
cisco Junior College and Professor
Fisher of the history department.
JUNIOR WOMEN ATTITUDE TEST
will be given for the second and
last time in Room 100 between 7
and 9:30 o'clock tonight. The test
requires only 40 minutes and all
women who took it as freshmen
in 1937 are requested to appear.

POMPONS WILL BE SOLD in the
A.S.S.U. office from 1 to 5 o'clock
today. They will be required at
the U.S.C. game Saturday.
RALLY COMMITTEE GIRLS work
ing on posters for the U.S.C. game
meet in the basement of Memorial
Hall at 1:15 o'clock today.
