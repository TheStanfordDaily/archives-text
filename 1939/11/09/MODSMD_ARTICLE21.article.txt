# Foreign Students Postpone Meeting
## 
### 
The newly formed Foreign Stu
dents' Club will not meet tonight,
the A.S.S.U. International Commit
tee decided at a hurried meeting
yesterday.
Because of the conflict with the
rally tonight, Nicholas HofT's talk on
the folklore and customs of Hun
gary will be postponed until the
meeting next Thursday night. At
that time he will also interview Jean
Webb on her travels in Hungary, the
committee announced.
