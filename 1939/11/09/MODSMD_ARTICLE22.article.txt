# Allen to Present Thursday Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give the second of a
series of Thursday recitals this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock.
Mr. Allen has selected the fol
lowing pieces for his concert: "Fugue
in G Minor," by Bach; "Andante in
B Flat." by Stamitz; "Wind in the
Pine Trees," by Joseph W. Clokey;
and the Sonata No. 1 in D minor,
by Alexander Guilmant.
