# Card Drum Major Will Miss Game
## 
### 
Drum major "Big Rill" Okker
will be among those missing at the
U.S.C. game, it was announced late
yesterday. Dr. Okker has received
an offer of partnership in a valley
town, and his services will be needed.
It is unknown at present whether
Stanford's drum major will be on
hand for the Washington State and
Big games.
