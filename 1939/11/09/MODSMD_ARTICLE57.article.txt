# COWBOY, LADY REUNITE
## 
### 
CODY, Wyo., Nov. 8 (UP).—The
cowboy and the lady were reported
patching up their divorce difficulties
tonight when friends said Anne
Gould Meador Elsbury, granddaugh
ter of the late railroad magnate, Jay
Gould, had telephoned from New
York to her cowboy husband to
"come to me at once."
