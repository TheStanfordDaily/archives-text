# Rattlesnake killer Denied Habeas Corpus
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Nov. 9 (UP).—
The state Supreme Court today de
nied a writ of habeas corpus filed
by attorneys for Robert James, con
victed under the name of Major
Raymond Lisenba of the "rattle
snake murder" of his wife, Mary, in
10.15.
