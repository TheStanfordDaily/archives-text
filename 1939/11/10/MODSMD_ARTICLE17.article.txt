# State Considers Underpass Bids
## 
### 
Lowest bid to date on the Uni
versity Avenue underpass was sub
mitted by Paul J. Tyler, Oroville
contractor. His bid of $265,960.40
was $35,000 below the estimate pre
viously made for the project.
Bidding for the job was opened
in Sacramento by officials of the
Slate Department of Public Works
Wednesday.
Eleven bidders submitted esti
mates which will be examined by
state engineers and counsel. Work
is expected to be under way by
January 1.
