# Group to Hear Local Professors
## 
### 
Dr. Bayard Quincy Morgan and
Donald A. McKenzie, of the Ger
man department, will read papers
before the Philological Association of
the Pacific Coast at its 41st annual
meeting to be held at the University
of Southern California Nov. 24 and
25.
The title of Dr. Morgan's paper
will be "A Study of Fiilke's Met
rics," and McKenzie has chosen for
his subject "A Footnote to Pros
kosch's Comparative Germanic
Grammar." Friedrich Wilhelm
Strothman. also of the German de
partment, is secretary-treasurer of
the association.
