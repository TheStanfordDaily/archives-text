# MINE CAVEIN KILLS FIVE
## 
### 
RAWLINS, Wyo., Nov. 9 (UP).—
The bodies of five workmen, trapped
by a cavein in a coal mine 29 miles
southwest of Rawlins, were recov
ered tonight by rescue workers sum
moned by the wife of one of the
victims.
