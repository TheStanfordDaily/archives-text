# $100,000 Fire Hits Pomona School
## 
### 
POMONA. Nov. 12 (UP).—Fire
swept the auditorium of the Fremont
Junior High School today. The dam
age was estimated at $100,000.
Source of the blaze was not dis
covered although firemen said they
believed it started on the stage and
smouldered all night before breaking
out.
