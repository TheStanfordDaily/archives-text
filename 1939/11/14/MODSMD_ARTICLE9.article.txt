# Plane Wreckage Found in Yukon
## 
### 
WHITEHORSE, Y.T., Nov. 13 (UP).
—A Yukon Route Airways plane,
missing since it left here Thursday
for Dawson, Y. T., tonight was be
lieved to have crashed into Lake
Leßarge north of here with three
aboard.
A searching party today found
portions of wreckage and a mail sack
floating on the surface of the lake.
