# Code Violation Decision Withheld
## 
### 
Decision in the first Honor Code
violation case of the quarter was
withheld by Men's Council at its
weekly meeting Monday, accord
ing to Bob Putnam, chairman.
Also at next week's meeting four
sophomore students will be prose
cuted for alleged throwing of eggs
and tomatoes during the Pajamarino
and rally last Thursday.
Several traffic cases were heard
by the Council with fines collected
totalling over $19. Several violators
who were not present will have to
appear next week with additional
fines being levied for not answering
the summons. Putnam requested
that all students parking around the
post office should park only in the
direction in which their cars are
headed. Tickets are being given for
non-compliance with this regulation.
