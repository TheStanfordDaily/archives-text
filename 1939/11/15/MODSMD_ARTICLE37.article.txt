# 'MAULER' ASKS $7,500
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Nov. 14 (UP).—Jack
Dempsey will fly to Manila on Dec. 3
to referee the Ceferino Garcia-Glenn
Lee fight Dec. 16, only on condition
that $7,500 is deposited to his credit
in a New York bank, it was an
nounced tonight by his representa
tive, Ned Brown.
