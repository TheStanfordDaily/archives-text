# Jess, Love to Box In Novice Finals
## 
### 
Bill Jess decisioned a determined
Bill Berreyesa last night in the men's
gym to earn the right to meet Bob
Love Thursday in the 145-pound
finals of the novice boxing tourna
ment.
Kay Kitagawa will be replaced by
Dick Lyon in the finals of the 127-
pound class. Kitagawa injured his
thumb during a workout.
