# Wilbur Announces Research Grant
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur an
nounced yesterday a grant of $19,000
from the Sperry Gyroscope Com
pany, Inc., of Brooklyn, N.Y., to
support researches by Stanford
physicists in ultra short-wave radio
problems.
The grant is a continuation of
support given for the past two years
to the development, of "klystron," an
invention of Stanford physicists
making possible the production of
ultra-short radio waves of great
power and stability.
