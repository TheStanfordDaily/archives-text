# Champion Giants Trounce Pirates
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Nov. 10 (UP).—The
New York Giants, champions of the
National Football League, fattened
up their averages today as they
coasted to a 23-7 victory over the
helpless Pittsburgh Pirates before a
crowd of 10,372 at the Polo Grounds.
