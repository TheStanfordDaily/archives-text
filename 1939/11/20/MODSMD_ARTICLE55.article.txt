# Mears to Head Affairs Parley
## 
### 
Five Stanford faculty members
will participate in the 17th annual
Institute of World Affairs meeting
which will be held Dec. 10 to 15 at
Riverside, Calif.
Professors Ralph H. Lutz, Karl
Brandt, Albert Guerard, Graham
Stuart, and Eliot G. Mears are the
Stanford representatives among the
78 educators and governmental offi
cials who will speak on various as
pects of the present international
situation.
Dr. Mears is general director of
the Institute and Dr. Stuart will
serve as general chairman of the
session on American foreign policy.
