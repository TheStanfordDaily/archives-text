# Women to Make Rifle Team Plans
## 
### 
Opportunity to form a rifle team
will be offered to Stanford women
tonight at 7:30 o'clock at. the rifle
range in the basement of the Ad
ministration Building.
The club plans to engage both
the men's and women's teams in
matches'with other schools in the
coming year, according to Jean Bry
ant, vice-president of the Rifle and
Pistol club.
