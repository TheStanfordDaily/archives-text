# Salvemini to Talk On World War I
## 
### 
"Italian Diplomacy in the World
War" will be the subject of a lec
ture by Gaetano Salvernini tomor
row afternoon, at 4:15 o'clock, in the
auditorium of the Education Build
ing, according to the Committee on
Public Exercises.
Dr. Salvernini is a member of the
history department at Harvard Uni
versity. He is on leave this quarter
and is working in the Hoover War
Library. Dr. Salvernini has held in
structing posts at several Italian
universities.
