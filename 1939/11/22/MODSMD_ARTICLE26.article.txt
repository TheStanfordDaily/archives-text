# Pre-Meds to Hear Palo Alto Doctor
## 
### 
Pre-med socicty will hear Dr.
Robert Dunn, head of the obstet
rics division of the Palo Alto
Clinic, speak tonight at 7:30 o'clock
in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Dunn, who is also head obste
trician at the Palo Alto Hospital
and instructor in Obstetrics and
Gynecology at the Stanford Medical
School, will speak on the general
field of obstetrics and will point out
possible new fields in his specialty.
The talk will be illustrated by slides
and possibly a motion picture.
