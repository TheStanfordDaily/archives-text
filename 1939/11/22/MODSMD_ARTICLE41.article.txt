# COLDS HIT TROJANS
## 
### 
TUCSON, Ariz., Nov. 21 (UP).—
An epidemic of light colds hit the
U.S.C. Trojans tonight, putting
Grenny Lansdell, Al Krueger, Bill
Fisk, Bob Hoffman and Phil Caspar
under observation.
