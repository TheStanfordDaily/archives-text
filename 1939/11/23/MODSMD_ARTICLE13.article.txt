# Roth Will Speak To Commerce Men
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, former Stanford
comptroller and now president of
the San Francisco Employers' Coun
cil. and Dr. Paul Eliel, director of
Industrial Relations in the Graduate
School of Business, will speak be
fore the Labor Relations section of
the California Chamber of Com
merce at the group's annual con
vention in San Francisco, Nov. 30
and Dec. 1.
Roth, also vice-president of the
state Chamber of Commerce, will
analyze impending legislation as it
may affect labor problems.
Dr. Eliel will lead discussion on
the effects of the war-time emer
gency on labor relations. Eliel re
cently returned from a conference
on industrial relations at Princeton
University.
