# The 						Stanford Daily SPORTS
## FRESNO STATE'S STAR BACK 
### JERRY MAYER, Sports Editor 

HOB "PLUNGING PETE" PETERSON. Fresno State fullback, is rated a leading candidate for little all-
American honors this year. The 22-year-old. 201-pound junior and his teammates put their unbeaten
record to its severest test this Saturday when they face the San Jose State Spartans, considered the best
team in the Bay Region this year.
