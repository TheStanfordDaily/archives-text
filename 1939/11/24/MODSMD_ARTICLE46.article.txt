# Card Band Plays Tonight on KGO
## 
### 
The Stanford band will be heard
over station KGO at 9:30 o'clock to
night in a program of Cardinal songs
as a part of the Alumni Association's
pre-Big Game celebration. Follow
ing the broadcast, band members
will be guests of the alumni for a
tour of the San Francisco hotels.
Between the halves tomorrow the
band promises something new and
different in football stunts, accord
ing to Manager Bob Strom. There
will also be several intricate march
ing figures executed by the band.
All members are to meet at the
band house at To'clock tonight to
take the buses for San Francisco,
according to Strom.
