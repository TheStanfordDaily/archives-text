# WHEAT MARKET FIRM
## 
### 
WINNIPEG, Man., Nov. 23 (UP).
—Featured by the scarcity of of
ferings, wheat ruled firm during
most of a drab session on the Win
nipeg Grain Exchange today.
American markets were closed.
