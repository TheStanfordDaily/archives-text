# Drama Division To Display Photos
## 
### 
The Division of Speech and Drama
will present, during this week, an
exhibition of photographs of the
dance in Memorial Hall foyer. The
photographs are the work of Ralph
Samuels, Los Angeles photographer,
and the exhibit illustrates dances
presented during the last two years
by the Dance Group at U.C.L.A.
The exhibit, has been arranged by
Ralph Schram, graduate student of
speech and drama, who has been
connected with the U.C.L.A. group,
both as dancer and assistant direc
tor for the past four years.
