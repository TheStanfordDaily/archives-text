# Tryouts to Begin For Winning Play
## 
### 
Trvouts for "The Warrior's Hus
band" will be held today .and to
morrow from 3 to 5 o'clock in the
Little Theater of Memorial Hall,
according to Hubert Heffner, head
of the Division of Speech and
Drama.
The play was the winner of the
Division of Speech and Drama poll
held to determine what play Stan
ford students want to see. Accord
ing to Heffner, there are parts
available for 20 men and women.
