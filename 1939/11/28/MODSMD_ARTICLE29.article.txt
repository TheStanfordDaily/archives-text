###

social service group.
Women are asked to buy the '
stockings from representatives in the !
living groups for 5 cents apiece, fill j
them, and return them to the "Y" j
representatives or to the "Y" rooms j
by Friday, Dec. 1, Miss Tight states. ;
The committee suggests that candy,
