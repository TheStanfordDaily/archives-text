# Excom to Discuss Farm Fund Drive
## 
### 
Undergraduates' part in the an
nual appeal for the Stanford Fund
will be discussed tonight by Ex Com
mittee at its meeting at 7:15 o'clock
in the Old Union.
Members will hear the report of a
subcommittee appointed last week to
study the matter. On the subcom
mittee are Mollie West and Barney
Ferguson.
Student support of the fund drive,
promised last week by unanimous
vote of Ex Committee, will take the
form of canvassing prospective don
ors by student representatives, ac
cording to Ken Cuthbertson, A.S.S.U.
president.
