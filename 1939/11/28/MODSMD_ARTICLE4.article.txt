# Chappie Announces Christmas Issue
## 
### 
Featuring a letter written home
by Terry Green and illustrated by
Lorraine Murray, Chaparral will
make its third appearance of the
year on Wednesday, Dec. 6.
John Lawry, regularly a cartoon
ist, will make his debut as a writer
with an article about Stanford's tall
women called Stanfordia Gigantea.
Ed Hutshing will have a story
about a modern Faust situation.
Jack Dixon has drawn the color
cover, which has a definite Christ
mas theme.
