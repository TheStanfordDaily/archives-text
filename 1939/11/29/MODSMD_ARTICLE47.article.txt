# Stanford to Hold Reg Dance Jan. 3
## 
### 
Stanford's traditional reg dance
will be held on Jan. 3, 1940, under
the auspices of the A.S.S.U. All prof
its will be turned over to the Boat
house fund.
A.S.S.U. President Ken Cuthbert
son will confer with Cardinals head,
Ken Koch today to pick a chairman j
i for the affair. 1
