# Book Exchange Announces Hours
## 
### 
Used hooks should be turned in
to the A.S.S.U. Book Exchange be
fore Christmas vaction so that their
owners may benefit by the winter
quarter book sales, according to
Russ Curtis, managing director of
the Exchange.
Students leaving their texts at
the Exchange receive a receipt and,
upon sale of the text, a check from
the A.S.S.U. on presentation of the
receipt after the sale has been
made, students may secure the re
sale price, or if they decide to use
the book again, they may present
the receipt at any time and it will
be returned.
