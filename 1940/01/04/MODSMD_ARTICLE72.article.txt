# RULE CHANGES MADE
## 
### 
PALM SPRINGS. Jan. 3 (UP).—
Suggested changes in the nation's
grid rules were discussed today as
the football rules committee of the
National Collegiate Athletic Associa
tion convened in annual session.
Six new Kansas oil pools were
discovered in the first two months
of 1939.
