# CONSUL GIVES LUNCH
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 3 (UP).—
Alfredo Polzin, Brazilian consul, to
day gave a farewell luncheon to
members of the San Francisco
Chamber of Commerce goodwill tour
which leaves here Friday for South
America.

The "first robin" of spring often
has passed the winter in the locality
where it is reported.
