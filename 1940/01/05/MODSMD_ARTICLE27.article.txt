# Poloists Get New String of Ponies
## 
### 
Aided by a now string of ponies,
ihe Cardinal polo team will try to
better their record of three defeats
earned on the Arizona trip during
the Thanksgiving vacation, on which
they dropped two games to Tucson,
5-15 and 4-11, and one tilt to the
Riviera Club, 7-8.
Major Smith's best combination
this year includes Joe Smith at
number one; Art La Prade, 2; Gale
Mendicott, 3; and Bob Strom. 4.
