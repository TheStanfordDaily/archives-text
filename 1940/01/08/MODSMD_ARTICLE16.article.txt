# BOY SHOT IN ACCIDENT
## 
### 
PORT TOWNSEND, Wash., Jan. 7
(UP). — Eleven-year-old Robert
Buyer was accidentally shot and
killed today by his brother, William,
15, while target-practicing on the
beach.
