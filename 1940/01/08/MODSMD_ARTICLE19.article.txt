# Montanan Killed As Car Crashes
## 
### 
GLASGOW, Mont., Jan. 7 (UP).
Harry B. Smith, 48, until recently
employed at Fort Peck, was killed
and two companions were injured to
day when the automobile in which
they were traveling skidded into a
guard rail.
The accident occurred on the
Wiota Hill, 22 miles east of here.
The car skidded going uphill, turned
around and crashed into the guard
rail while sliding downgrade.
