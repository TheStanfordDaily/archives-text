# U.S. Navy Submarine Arrives in Portland
## 
### 
PORTLAND, Ore., Jan. (UP).-■
The long sleek, black-hulled U. S.
Davy's submarine Swordfish glided
into berth here today, marking the
first time a submersible of her clas*
has docked in Columbia River waters
since 1925.
