# Fire Causes Loss To Stanford Home
## 
### 
The historic Stanford home at
Sacramento was razed by flames yes
terday when a fire, believed to have
started from a defective heater flue,
broke out on the fourth floor.
Although damage was estimated
at $1500, no injuries were reported
among the 26 girl residents. The
mansion now houses the Stanford
Home for Girls, operated by the Sis
ters of Social Service.
