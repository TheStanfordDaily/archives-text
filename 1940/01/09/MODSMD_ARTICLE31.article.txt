# Rain Keeps Farm Golfers Indoors
## 
### 
01' Jupiter Pluvius has a strangle
hold on activities of the great out
doors on the local sports scene these
days and. as a result, most of Stan
ford's golfers haven't been out in
over a week.
Although nothing of importance
was planned for this week and no
events have had to be cancelled, Ed
die Twiggs' charges, who usually
play just about every day, will have
lost at least ten days of valuable
practice.
When the countryside dries out,
they will all be out thpre with the
varsity looking forward to another
championship at the national inter
collegiate tourney this summer.
