# Riggs Downs Two In Florida Tourney
## 
### 
ORLANDO. Fla„ Jan. 9 (UP.) — I
World's Champion Bobby Riggs, of |
Chicago, today went into quarter- \
finals of the Florida state tennis J
tournament by defeating Robert j
Robertson, Orlando, 6-0, 6-3, and
Jack Mahoney, New York, 6-4, 9-7.
Henry Prusoff, Seattle, eliminated
C. L. Varner, Orlando, 6-2, 6-1, and
Vincent Paul, New York, 6-1, 6-3.
Elwood Cooke, Portland. Ore., beat
Tom Kelly, Portland, and Bob Davis,
Rollins College player.

rectly above the North Pole, thus
appearing to stand still as the earth
revolves on its axis.
