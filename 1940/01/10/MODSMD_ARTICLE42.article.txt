# Tim Marble Sold To Tacoma Tigers
## 
### 
TACOMA, Wash., Jan. 9 (UP).—
The Tacoma Tigers of the Western
International Baseball League today
bought Harry (Tim) Marble, inlield
er, from Hollywood of the Pacific
Coast League.
Marble is a brother of the tennis
star, Alice Marble, and played two
years ago with Bellingham of the
Western International League.
