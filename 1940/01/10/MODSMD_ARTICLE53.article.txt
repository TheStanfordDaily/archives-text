# Lecturer Speaks On Hamlet Today
## 
### 
Stories of the actors who have
played Hamlet on the American
stage and the history of the play
since its first presentation in Phila
delphia in 1759 will be told by Mr.
Samuel Pearce in his illustrated
lecture, "Hamlet in America." It
will be presented in the auditorium
of the Education Building at 4:15
o'clock this afternoon.

coming book, "Hamlet in America,"
