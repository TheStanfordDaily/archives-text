# Seamans to Train New Yell Leaders
## 
### 
Head Yell Leader Johnny Seamans
yesterday revealed that, ten sopho
more men attended the first tryout
session for assistant yell leader can
didates, held yesterday afternoon in
the Pavilion.
Those trying out included Charles
Van Deusen, Ralph Clark, Larry
Dee, Bob Reynolds, Ted Doe, Biil
Gard, Harry Mitchell, John Rosen
dale, Hank Swafford, and Clare
Peck.
Further sessions in which the try
outees will be instructed in some of
the fundamentals of leading yells
and songs will be held each Monday
and Wednesday afternoon at 2
o'clock in the Pavilion.
