ABERDEEN, Wash., Jan. 10 (UP).
—The Finnish Workers' Federation
and a CIO union committee today
demanded appointment of special
prosecutors to investigate the slay
ing of Mrs. Law, wife of an Interna
tional Woodworkers of America
officer.
