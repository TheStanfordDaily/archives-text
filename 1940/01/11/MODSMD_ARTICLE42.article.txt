# Commission Boots Baer-Galento Bout
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Jan. 10 (UP).—The
New York Boxing Commission at a
turbulent meeting today blessed
and then booted the Tony Galento-
Maxie Baer 15-round charity bout
slated for Madison Square Garden
March 4.
