# Gaels Lose Fifth Tilt As Nevada Wins 47-42
## 
### 
RENO. Nev„ Jan. 10 (UP). —The
University of Nevada basketball
team tonight defeated St. Mary's of
Moraga, Cal., 47 to 42 in the first
game of a two-game series at the
Univeristy of Nevada gymnasium.
The Gaels, on a tour, so far have
lost five straight games and have
won none. It was Nevada's first vic
tory out of three starts this season.
