# Moore to Speak On Student Meeting
## 
### 
John V. Moore, who represented
the Interfaith Council at the Toronto
Student Conference during the last
week in December, will speak on "A
Message from Toronto" at the brief
worship service at the Baptist
Church, on the corner of Bryant and
Hamilton at 7:15 o'clock. The fire
side meeting of the Roger Williams
Club will take place at the Baptist
parish house next door.
