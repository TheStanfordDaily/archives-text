# Artist to Exhibit Chinese Paintings
## 
### 
Painting in the ancient Chinese
fashion will be demonstrated by
Miss Chang Kwan-Ye during the
last two weeks of January at the Art
Gallery. Miss Chang, who is making
a good-will tour of the United
States, will be at the gallery from
4 to 5 o'clock every afternoon be
ginning next. Monday.
Now on display at the gallery is
an exhibit of water colors by Miss
Chang and the master who taught
her in China. These paintings are
mounted in the traditional manner
on scrolls which are rolled up when
not in use.
Oriental subjects are also treated
in a collection of water colors by
Ethel Rose of Pasadena. The work
of this artist is distinguished by its
unusual photographic quqality. The
group includes 25 paintings,
