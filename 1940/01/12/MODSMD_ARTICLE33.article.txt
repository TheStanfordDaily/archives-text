# Stanford-Cal Cage Tickets to Be Sold
## 
### 
Tickets for the first Stanford
| California basketball game of 1940,
scheduled for the Pavilion next Fri
day, will go on sale Monday at the
B.A.C. from 8:30 to 5 o'clock. If any
tickets are left over, they will go on
sale Tuesday.
The ducats will be priced at 40
cents, accompanied by student body
cards. Each student may present
two cards.
