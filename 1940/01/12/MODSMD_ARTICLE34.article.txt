# Broncos Name New Mead Baseball Coach
## 
### 
SANTA CLARA, Jan. 11 (UP).—
Leonard Casanova, freshman base
ball coach at University of Santa
Clara since 1936, today was named
varsity baseball coach, succeeding
Justin Fitzgerald, former major
league player who resigned this
week.

World's champion, Bobby Riggs, of
Chicago, today was pushed to three
sets before he eliminated Ed Alloo,
of Berkeley. Cal., 9-7. 7-9. G-3, in
quarter-finals of the Florida state
tennis tournament.
