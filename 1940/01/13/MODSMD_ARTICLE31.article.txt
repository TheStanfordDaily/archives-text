# Cal Ticket Sale Opens On Monday
## 
### 
Tickets for the first Stanford-
California basketball game of 1940,
to be played next Friday night at
the Pavilion, will be on sale Mon
day, from 8:30 fc>' 5 o'clock, at the
B.A.C.
The tickets are priced at 40 cents,
and each one must be accompanied
by a student body card. Each pur
chaser may present two cards.
