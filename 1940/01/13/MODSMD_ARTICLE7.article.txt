# Toyon-Braimer Bids Go On Sale
## 
### 
Toyon and Branner halls will be
joint sponsors of the first formal
dance of winter quarter on Friday
evening, January 20, in the Toyon
Lobby. Carvel Craig's orchestra
will play.
Bids, priced at $1.75, went on sale
at the A.S.S.U. office today. Powers
Cameron, Toyon president, and
Bob Kinstein, dance chairman, are
in charge of arrangements.
Craig's band has played for sev
eral previous campus dances, and is
well known in the Bay Area. He
was once a student at Stanford.
