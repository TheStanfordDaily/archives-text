# Glee Club Starts Membership Drive
## 
### 
Starting today, an invitation, open
for several weeks, is being issued by
the Glee Club to all students inter
ested in singing. Everyone on the
campus interested is asked to come
to an open meeting of the club to
night in Room 84 at 8 o'clock.
Climaxing the present member
ship drive will be a Glee Club So
cial on Jan. 29 with entertainment
and refreshments. Anyone who
wants to join the club but who can
not attend the meeting tonight is
asked to contact Mr. Joel Carter of
the music department or Tommy
Dee at the Phi Kappa Sigma house.
