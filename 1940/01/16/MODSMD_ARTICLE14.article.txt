# Program Changes Due Tomorrow
## 
### 
All changes in study lists must bo
recorded in the Administration
Building before 5 o'clock tomorrow,
according to an announcement from
the Registrar's oHice. Students will
be held responsible for courses and
units as (hey appear on their of
ficial study lists filed at the time of
registration, and no credit is given
for courses which arc not on the
study lists.
Program changes must be filled
out on regular revised study list
cards, the announcement said.
After tomorrow, all program re
visions must be made by petition to
the Registration Committee, subject
to the following fines: $2 for each
course added or dropped, or $3 where
one course is substituted for an
o her. This applies to physical edu
cation as well as academic subjects.
