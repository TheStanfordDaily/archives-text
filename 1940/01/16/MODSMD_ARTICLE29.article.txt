# Quad Photo Office Opens Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Quad Studio will be open to
photograph fraternity pledges be
ginning tomorrow, according to
Newton Russell, editor of the 1940
Quad. Hours will be from 9 until
12 o'clock in the morning and from
1 until 5 o'clock afternoons.
Letters have been sent to fra
ternities telling them when to have
their pledges report. Students who
have not yet had their pictures
taken for sororities, fraternities, or
dormitories may do so at this time.
