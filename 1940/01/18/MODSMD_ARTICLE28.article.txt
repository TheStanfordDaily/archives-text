# Baer-Galento Bout Expected for Miami
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Jan. 17 (UP).-After
;i day of great hurly-burly, it seem
ed virtually certain tonight Promoter
Mike Jacobs will stage a big fight
at Miami, this winter, probably
matching Tony Galento ;uid Mnxic
Baer.
Promoter Jacobs announced to
night. "I'll have something definite
on this bv 6 p.m. tomorrow."
