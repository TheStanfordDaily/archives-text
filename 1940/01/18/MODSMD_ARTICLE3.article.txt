# 'Y' Group to Quiz Anderson Today
## 
### 
"What Do You Believe, Profes
sor?" members of the Y.W.C.A. will
ask Dr. Frederick Anderson, pro
fessor of Romanic languages this
afternoon at the first of a series
of personal philosophy discussions
sponsored by the "Y." Everyone in
terested is invited to meet in the "Y"
rooms at 4:30 o'clock.
Faculty members who will be
"quizzed" at the later meetings in
clude Professor E. E. Robinson, of
the history department, Dr. Edith
Mirrielees, professor of English, and
Dr. Philip W. Buck, professor of
political science.
The Freshman Discussion Group
of the "Y" will hear Mrs. Helen
Green, costume director of the
Speech and Drama Division speak
on make-up. personal and theatric
al, this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in
the "Y" rooms. The Social Service
Group will also hold a short meet
ing at 5 o'clock.
