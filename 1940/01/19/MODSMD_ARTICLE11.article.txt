# Stanford Engineer Heads East Today
## 
### 
Dr. F. E. Terman, executive head
of the Department of Electrical En
gineering, left yesterday for New
York City, where he will attend the
annual meeting of the board of di
rectors of the Institute of Radio
Engineers. Dr. Terman is vice
president of the organization.
Dr. Terman will also attend the
winter convention of the American
Institute of Electrical Engineers, at
which he will lead a round table
discussion and present a paper,
written jointly with W. R. Hewlett,
research associate in the electrical
engineering department, W. Y. Pan.
engineering graduate, and Winslow
Palmer.
