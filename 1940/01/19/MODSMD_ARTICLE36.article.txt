# St. Mary's Dismisses 13 Football Players
## 
### 
MORAGA, Jan. IS (UP). Thir
teen athletes, including nine mem
bers of the If).'!!) Gael varsity foot
ball squad, have been dismissed from
St. Mary's College for failure to
keep up their scholastic grades, it
was disclosed yesterday.
A 12-vear-old boy cleared $82.58
from a half acre of peppers in
Sampson county, North Carolina.
