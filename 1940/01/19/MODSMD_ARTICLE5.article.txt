# ITALY CALLS FOR BALKAN UNITY
## 
### 

TO STRENGTHEN the Balkan states against possible attack, supposedly by Soviet Russia, Italy is seem
ingly maneuvering for Balkan unity. Evidence corroborating this speculation was the recent visit of Hun
garian Foreign Minister Csaky to Rome for official business. Pictured are, from left. Foreign Minister Ciano,
Premier Teleki and Count Csaky of Hungary, and Premier Mussolini.
