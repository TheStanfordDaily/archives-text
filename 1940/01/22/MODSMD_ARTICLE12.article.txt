# NERVE CENTER OF MOCK WAR
## 
### 

BECAUSE ADEQUATE WARNING facilities are vital in air raid
defense, an elaborate system was set up and tested as an important
part of the joint Army-Navy west coast maneuvers. In San Fran
cisco's P. G. and E. Building, the nerve center of the alarm system was
located to which hundreds of civilians flashed information as "black"
forces invaded the mainland.
