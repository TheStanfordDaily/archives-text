# Orchesis to Hold Tryouts Tonight
## 
### 
O
Tryouts for Orchesis, honorary
dance group, will be held tonight at
8:15 o'clock in the Dance Studio.
Anyone with more than one quar
ter's dance experience at Stanford
is eligible to try out, according to
Margerie Gunn, publicity chair
man.
Candidates will be judged on an
original composition which they
present, their technique in present
ing it, and their ability to learn new
techniques with facility.
