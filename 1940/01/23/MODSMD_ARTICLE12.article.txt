# Robert Harcourt, Forge Head, Dies
## 
### 
Robert Henry Harcourt, teaching
specialist in forge practice at Stan
ford and in charge of the Stanford
forge shops, died Sunday evening at
his home in Palo Alto, following a
brief illness.
Mr. Harcourt has been an instruc
tor in machine shop practice, forge
practice, and sheet metal work at
Santa Barbara State, from which he
was graduated in 1917. Earlier he
was associated with the Risdon Iron
Works, Hammond Car and Elevator
Company, National Iron Works, and
Doble Steel Company of San Fran
cisco.
