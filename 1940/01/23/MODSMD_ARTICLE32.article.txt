# COUGHLIN FOR 'FRONT'
## 
### 
DETROIT, Jan. 22 (UP). —The
Rev. Charles E. Coughlin, in a Sun
day sermon bitterly criticizing news
paper accounts of the recent arrest
of 17 men in New York on charges
of sedition, "took his stand" Sunday
with the Christian Front.
The first separate children's court
was established in Chicago in 1899.
