# ISLAND SALE REPORTED
## 
### 
VANCOUVER, 8.C., Jan. 22 (UP).
— Sale of Moresby Island in the
Gulf of Georgia by Lewis Bradbury,
Santa Monica, Cal., to Major H. E.
Head, English industrialist, was an
nounced today. The island includes
1,600 acres, of which 160 is cleared.
It is heavily stocked with game.

For the first time in Japan's lonq
legal history, women have been ad
mitted to the practice of law. Three
Orientals passed the bar examina
tion recently.
