# Elected President
## 
### 

NEAL VAN SOOY, lecturer in
journalism at Stanford and pub
lisher of the A/usa Herald, was
elected president of the California
Newspaper Publishers Associa
tion last Saturday at the 52nd an
nual convention held at Coronado.
Mr. Van Sooy, a graduate of
Stanford in 19113, served before
his election as member of the ex
ecutive committee, second vice
president. and first vice-president
of the publishers' association.
