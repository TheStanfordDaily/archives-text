AMERICA - JAPAN STUDENT
CONFERENCE tryouts continue
today in room 200E from 2 to 4
o'clock; tomorrow from 2 to 2:30
o'clock.
DEBATE SQUAD meets today at 4
o'clock in room 200F.
VOCATIONAL COMMITTEE meets
today, 5 o'clock, Nitery.
NYA WORKERS must sign release
cards January 24-27 to work the
month starting Jan. 28. Workers
unable to work full quota of hours
this month should report same to
Mrs. Coverley's office immediately.
QI'AD PIC'IURES are scheduled to
day for Phi Sigma Kappa, Delta
Upsiion, Delta Kappa Epsilon, Ro
ble Club, and Japanese Club.
ALPHA PHI OMEGA meets tonight
at 7:30 o'clock upstairs in Women's
Clubhouse.
DAILY BUSINESS STAFF meeting
for all advertising men in Business
Office at 7:30 o'clock.
ORCHESIS meets at 5 o'clock in the
dance studio.
CIRCLE "S" SOCIETY meets today
at noon at the Delt house.
INTERNATIONAL COMMITTEE
will meet at 5 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse.
ENCINA COUNCIL, consisting of
the president, manager, and the
corridor representatives will have
ist, Quad picture taken at 1:45 to
day on the Encina front steps.
BADMINTON CLUB will meet to
night in the Women's Gym from 7
to 9 o'clock.

tist, was the son of a valet of Louis
