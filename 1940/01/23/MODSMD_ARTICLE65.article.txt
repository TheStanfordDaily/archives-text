# R.O.T.C. Reviewed By Artillery Chief
## 
### 
Major General Robert M. Danford,
chief of field artillery of the United
States Army, paid an unofTicial visit
to Stanford yesterday.
He visited with President Wilbur
and inspected the military training
facilities on the campus. He was
conducted on a tour of the campus
by Major Harry B. Allen, professor
of military science and tactics, and
head of the Farm R.O.T.C. units.
