# Heffner to Speak To Philology Club
## 
### 
Hubert C. Heffner, professor of
dramatic literature, and Juan B.
Rael, instructor in Spanish, will ad
dress the University Philological
Association this afternoon at 4:15
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Professor Heffner will speak on
"William Godwin's Conception of
Man's History," while Mr. Rael will
talk on "New Mexican - Spanish
Weddings, Dances, and Other Cus
toms."
Before 1793 the proceedings of the
U. S. Senate were conducted pri
vately.
