# Harvard Alumni Give Scholarships
## 
### 
Several $1000 scholarships to the
Harvard Graduate School of Busi
ness are being offered to students of
universities in the Bay Area this
year, it was announced yesterday by
Professor B. F. Haley, head of the
Stanford economics department.
The scholarships are being offered
in addition to the two $300 stipends
offered annually by the Harvard
Business School Club of Northern
California. All Stanford students
interested should get in touch with
Professor Haley at once.
