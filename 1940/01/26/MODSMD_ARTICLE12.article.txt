# Frosh to Debate St. Mary's Today
## 
### 
The freshman debate team, coach
ed by Elmer Mortenson, will meet
the St. Mary's frosh this afternoon
at 2:30 o'clock in Room 204 of Me
morial Theater.
The subject of the debate will be
"Resolved, that the United States
should follow a policy of strict eco
nomic and military isolation toward
all nations outside the Western
Hemisphere that are engaged in arm
ed international or civil conflict."
Denny Green and Jim Frolik of
Stanford will take the affirmative,
while Jack Wecker and Edward
Gunz, representing St. Mary's, will
take the negative. The public is in
vited.
