# Farm Softballers Join Paly League
## 
### 
Andy Stojkovich announced yes
terday that Stanford will enter a
team in the Palo Alto softball
league, and that anyone interested
should see him to sign up.
All in the University are eligible
and a squad of 16 will be chosen
from those who sign up. The sign
up sheet will be placed on the intra
mural board and all who wish to
sign up should do so as soon as
possible.
