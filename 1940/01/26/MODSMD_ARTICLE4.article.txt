# Division of Drama Plans Operetta
## 
### 
"The Headless Horseman," an op
eretta based upon "A Legend of
Sleepy Hollow" by Washington Irv
ing, is tentatively scheduled to be
presented this quarter under the
direction of the Divisions of Speech
and Drama and Music.
The preparation of the operetta
forms part of the work of Music 13,
Dramatic Music, for which students
may register, although everyone who
wishes to participate just for the fun
of it is invited to do so.
Rehearsals will be held in Room
84 on the following schedule: Tues
days—women, 1 o'clock; men, 4 o'-
clock. Thursdays—women, 1 o'clock;
men, 3 o'clock.
