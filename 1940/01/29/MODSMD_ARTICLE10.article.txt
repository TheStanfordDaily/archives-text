# Hoop Ticket Sales Start Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tickets for the Stanford - Santa
Clara and Stanford-St. Mary's bas
ketball games, to be held in Kezar
Pavilion, San Francisco, on Feb. 2
and 3, will go on sale tomorrow and
Wednesday in the B.A.C. office. The
sale will start at 8:30 o'clock and
will close at 5.
Tickets will also be sold at the
same time for the Stanford-Califor
nia game to be held at Berkeley,
Feb. 9. Rooters' tickets for the San
Francisco games will be 25 cents
each, and 40 cents for the California
game.
