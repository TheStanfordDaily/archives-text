# Palace to Stage 'Stanford Night'
## 
### 
An official "Stanford Night" on
Thursday in which all Farm mem
bers are invited to participate and
the addition of an ex-Stanford Gaie
ties star to his entertainment staff
are features of Vincent Lopez's stay
at the Palace Hotel's Rose Room
Bowl from Feb. 1 to Feb. 15.
The Farm's night will be head
lined by a dancing contest in which
students will compete for a prize to
see who can dance best to Lopez'
new discovery, "Put Your Little
Foot There." The featured attrac
tion will be Paul Speegle, Stanford
'30, drama critic of the San Fran
cisco Chronicle, who will present
three original sketches every night.
