# Soph Yell Leader Tryouts Continue
## 
### 
A last call for sophomore yell
leader tryoutecs was issued yester
day by Head Yell Loader Johnny
Seamans. Rally Committee will
in the near future choose six men
from the tryoutees to run for the
two assistant posts.
Training periods under present
assistants Gordy Nicoll and Ed Cook
are being held from 2 to 3 o'clock
every Monday and Wednesday.
Only qualifications needed include
sophomore standing and a C schol
astic average. There is still room for
men who have not allended previous
training sessions.
