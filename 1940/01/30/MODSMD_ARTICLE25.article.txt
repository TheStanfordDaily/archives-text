# Orchesis Initiates Five New Members
## 
### 
Five new members of Orchesis
were initiated by secret l'ites last
night at 8:15 o'clock in the dance
.studio. The initiates will be hon
ored tonight at a dinner party given
by the club at the Corner House in
Palo Alto, after which Orchesis and
members of Stanford dancing classes
will go to San Jose to see Hanya
Holm, concert dancer, and her troupe
perform.
The new members of Orchesis are:
, Martha Shutts, La Belle Carroll,
Margaret Elliott, Mary Jean Haynes,
and Joyce Glassman.
