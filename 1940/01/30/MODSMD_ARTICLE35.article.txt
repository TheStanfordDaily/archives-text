# Cardinal Foilsmen Gain Recognition
## 
### 
Richard Meyerhoff and David
Daly of the Stanford fencers car
ried away honors in the junior foil
individuals in the Ray division meet
of the American Fencing League
last Friday in San Francisco.
Meyerhoff was runner-up in the
finals, taking second place out of 14
entries. Daly took fourth, missing
third place by only two points.
The league is composed of fenc
ers of college rating, according to
Elwyn Bugge, Stanford fencing
coach.
