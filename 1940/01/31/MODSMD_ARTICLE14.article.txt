# 'George, Margaret' Tryouts to Be Held
## 
### 
Tryouts for the comedy, "George
and Margaret" will be held Friday
in the Little Theater of Memorial
Hall from 2 to 6 o'clock, according
to F. Cowles Strickland. The play,
a sophisticated English comedy, ran
for two years in London.
