# CRASH INJURES TWO
## 
### 
VICTORIA, 8.C., Jan. 30 (UP).—
An airplane carrying four passengers
from Skagway, Alaska, to Carcross,
Y.T., crashed in landing today, in
juring two women occupants, pro
vincial police said tonight.
