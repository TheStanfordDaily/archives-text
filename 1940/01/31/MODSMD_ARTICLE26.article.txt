# SWEDISH TRADE GROUP ARRIVES
## 
### 

COMING TO AMERICA to buy raw materials, machinery, steel products, cotton, and petroleum, the Swed
ish trade delegation arrives in New York. Prince Hertil, 28-year-old son of Crown Prince Gustaf Adoll a
left, heads the group, aided by Assar Gabrieison, center, a Gothenburg industrialist, and Gunar Ilagglof
chief of the trade department of the Swedish foreign office, right.
