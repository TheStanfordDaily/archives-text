# WORKERS DISCHARGED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 30 (UP). -
Western Pipe & Steel Company, re
plying to Secretary of Interior Har
old Ickes' charges of alleged irregu
larities in installing pipes to Grand
Coulee Dam turbines, announced to
night several workmen on the job
were discharged for substituting X
ray films which recorded welding
work.
