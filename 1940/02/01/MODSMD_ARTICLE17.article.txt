of Art McCue's orchestra on Feb
ruary 9 from 9 to 1 o'clock at it?

to Mary Lou Gretzer, general chair
man. Committee heads include;

Nell Plopper, Janet Bont, Holer
Johnson, Giola Buttress, Betty Au
ger, Joan McKee, and Jean Nowell.
