# Vocom Discusses Costume Designing
## 
### 
Vocom's board of experts on fash
ion design agreed at last night's "In
formation Please" program that, the
greatest opportunity for novices lies
in the field of independent custom
design.
Guest experts included Mrs. E. A.
Adams, Mrs. Frances Leslie and Mrs.
Helen Green. Chairman of the eve
ning was Miss Anastasia Doyle, fac
ulty adviser of Vocom. Most of the
questions answered pertained to the
practical aspects of the field such as
positions available and salaries of
fered.
