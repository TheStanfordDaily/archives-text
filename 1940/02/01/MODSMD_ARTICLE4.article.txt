# International Club To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The International Students Club
will meet tonight at 7:30 o'clock at
the home of its sponsors, Mr. and
Mrs. E. J. Thomas, 365 Lincoln
Street. Cars will leave as usual from
the Nitery at 7:15 o'clock.
The following committee chair
men were announced by President
Miklos HofT at the last meeting:
membership, Emilio de Jauregui:
program, Margreta Jakobsen; and
refreshment, Eleanor Henry. Mem
bership is open to both American
and foreign students upon applica
tion to the committee, according to
the constitution adopted by the
newly-formed organization.
