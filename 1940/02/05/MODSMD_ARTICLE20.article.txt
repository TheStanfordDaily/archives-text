# MERCY FLIGHT ENDS
## 
### 
HAMILTON FIELD, Feb. 4 (UP).
—An Army airplane landed safely
here tonight after a 2.000-mile mercy
flight to Miles City, Mont., carrying
two injured CCC youths who were
immediately rushed by ambulance
to the San Francisco Presidio, where
they will be treated at Letterman
Hospital.
