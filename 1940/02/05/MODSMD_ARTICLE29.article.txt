# S.P.U. Postpones February Meeting
## 
### 
The February meeting of the Stan
ford Political Union which was
scheduled for Wednesday has been
postponed until a week from Thurs
day, Feb. 15, according to Oliver
Wyman, president of the union.
The steeling committee of the
union will meet later this week to
decide on the subject for the debate.
Party caucuses will be held early
next week.
