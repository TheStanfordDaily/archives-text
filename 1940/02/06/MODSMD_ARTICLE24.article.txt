# SHIP OFFICER WOUNDED
## 
### 
OAKLAND, Feb. 5 (UP).—Lieut.
Herman Odessey, executive officer of
the U.S. Coast Guard and Geodetic
Survey ship Pioneer, was taken to
Alameda County Hospital in critical
condition today from bullet wounds
in the head.
