# Farm Speaker To Discuss Hobby
## 
### 
Displaying his collection of more
than 500 autographs, Bill Blair, of
the Stanford Speakers' Bureau, will
speak to the Palo Alto Women's Club
today on the pursuit of his hobby.
Blair's collection includes auto
graphs of Washington, Lincoln. Dis
raeli, Mussolini, Jean Harlow, and
the Duchess of Windsor.
Other speaking engagements listed
for the week include Jack Anderson's
discussion of "Submarines" to be
given at a Naval Reserve Officers'
meeting on Yerba Buena Island to
day; and a story of a bicycle trip
through Mexico by Margreta Jakob
sen, to be presented for the Edison
High School, San Jose, tomorrow.
