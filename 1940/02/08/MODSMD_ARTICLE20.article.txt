# Farm to Observe World Prayer Day
## 
### 
Stanford students are joining
with students throughout the United
States and Canada in observing the
World Day of Prayer in Memorial
Chapel at 2:15 o'clock tomorrow.
John Moore, Inter-faith Council
vepresentative, will lead the service.
The theme will be "In confidence
and faith is our strength." Worship,
hymns, and organ music by Mr.
Raymond Kendall constitute the
service, which is uniform through
out the country.
