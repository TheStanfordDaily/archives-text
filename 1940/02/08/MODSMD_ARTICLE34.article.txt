# JAMESON IN SEMI-FINALS
## 
### 
PALM BEACH. Flu., Feb. 7 (UP).
—National Champion Betty Jame
j son, of San Antonio, Tex., marched
into the semi-finals of the Palm
Beach women's golf championship
today with a G-and-5 triumph over
1 Mrs. E. B. Jarrett, of Philadelphia.
