# COMPANY ISSUES BONDS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 7 (UP).—
The California Railroad Commission
today authorized the California-
Oregon Telephone Company to issue
$18,000 in bonds to take up existing
obligations and improve service.
