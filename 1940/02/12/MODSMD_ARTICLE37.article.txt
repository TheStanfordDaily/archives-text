# Sequoia Five Faces Delta Chi Tonight
## 
### 
Intramural basketball moves into
its fifth week with the usual six
games scheduled tonight. One of the
best games of the evening should be
the Sequoia vs. Delta Chi clash at 7
o'clock in the Encina Gym. Delta
Upsilon sends its power team after
the Business School at 9 o'clock in
the Pavilion.
TONIGHT'S GAMES
Encina Gym—7 o'clock. Sequoia
vs. Delta Chi; S o'clock, Delta
Kappa Epsilon vs. Sigma Alpha
Epsilon II; 9 o'clock. Breakers
vs. El Caudro.
Pavilion—7 o'clock, Mcnlo Giants
vs. Sigma Nu II; 8 o'clock. Phi
Gamma Delia vs. El Campo; 9
o'clock. Delta Upsilon vs. Busi
ness School.
