# TESTIFIES, THEN JAILED
## 
### 

WILLIAM DUDLEY PELLEY, left, of the fascist Silver Shirts, testi
fied to acting chairman Starnes, right, and the Dies Committee that
its investigation of un-American activities made Silver Shirts unnec
essary. David Mayne, center, is said to have written disputed letters
which accused the committee of fascist connections. Pelley was jailed
after testifying.
