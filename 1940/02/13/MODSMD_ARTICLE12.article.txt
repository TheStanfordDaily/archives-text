# ALLIES FIGHT SUB
## 
### 
LISBON, Portugal, Feb. 12 (UP).—
Two destroyers, one French and one
British, were reported today to have
battled a German submarine 10
miles off Cascaes at dawn yesterday
when the U-boat attacked the Brit
ish freighter Oregon, which the two
warships were convoying.
