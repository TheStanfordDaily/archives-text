# Brooklyn Dodgers Buy Vosmik from Red Sox
## 
### 
BELLEAIR, Fla., Feb. 12 (UP).—
Another step toward building the
Brooklyn Dodgers into a formidable
power in the National League pen
nant race was taken by President
Larry MacPhail today when he
purchased Outfielder Joe Vosmik
from the Boston, Red Sox for $25,000.
