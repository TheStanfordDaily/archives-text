# Ruggers to Meet Dons Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford ruggers meet U.S.F. to
morrow afternoon at 4 o'clock in
the stadium in the play-off of a
game that was postponed because of
rain.
Coaches Harry Maloney, Jim Wy
lie, and Willard Clausen expect this
match to be a good tune-up for the
rematch with U.S.C. next Saturday
in Los Angeles.
