# Laborite to Speak To Poli Sci Group
## 
### 
J. Vernon Burke, secretary-treas
urer of Labor's Non-Partisan League
of California, will speak before a
closed dinner meeting of Pi Sigma
Alpha, honorary political science
fraternity, Thursday.
The gathering will take place in
the Garden Room club of the Union
from G to 8 o'clock in the evening,
according to Dallas A. Tuellcr, club
president.
Mr. Vernon will speak on the sub
ject, "Should the National Labor Re
lations Act Be Amended?" He will
also review material which he pre
sented to the United States Senate
Committee. The price of the dinner
will be 65 cents.
