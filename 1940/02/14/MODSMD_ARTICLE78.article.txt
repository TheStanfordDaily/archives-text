# S.F. Club Hears Journalism Head
## 
### 
Dr. Chilton R. Bush, professor of
journalism at Stanford, was guest
speaker yesterday at a luncheon
meeting of the section on propa
ganda and censorship of the Com
monwealth Club, held in the Hotel
St. Francis of San Francisco.
His topic was "Propaganda De
fined," an explanation of when items
are propaganda and when they are
not.
