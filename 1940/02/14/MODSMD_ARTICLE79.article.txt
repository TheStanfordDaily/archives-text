# Dames Will Hear Fashion Authority
## 
### 
Mrs. George Dell, fashion com
mentator, will talk on "What to
Wear and How to Wear It" at the
general meeting of the Stanford
Dames tomorrow at 2 o'clock at the
home of Mrs. Paul Hanna, 737 Coro
nado Street.
Arrangements have been made
for the member's children to be
cared for during the meeting at the
home of Mrs. T. M. Golden, 750
Amherst Street.
