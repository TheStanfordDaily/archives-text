# F.D.R. to Appear On Oregon Ballot
## 
### 
SALEM. Ore., Feb. 15 (UP).—
President Roosevelt's name will ap
pear on the Oregon primary ballot as
a candidate for the Democratic
presidential nomination, it appeared
certain today.
Petitions to nominate Roosevelt
signed by more than 1,200 persons
were filed with the Oregon Secretary
of State. Under Oregon law a can
didate's name can be placed on the
ballot without his sanction, or even
against his wishes.
The Oregon primary — scheduled
May 17—is expected to provide the
first test between President Roose
velt and Vice-President Garner.
