# Eaton Receives Council Post
## Senior is Chairman Of Women's Group 
### 
Jane Eaton was elected chairman
of Women's Council yesterday at the
final meeting of the present council.
She has served since her election
last fall.
A member of Kappa Alpha Theta,
the new chairman succeeds Jane
Greenwood.
Next week three newly elected
members, Barbara Sweetland, Car
lotta Haines, and Nell Plopper will
take office to serve until mid-win
ter elections next year.
Outgoing members besides Miss
Greenwood include Barbara Gil
bert and Jeanne Sichel.
