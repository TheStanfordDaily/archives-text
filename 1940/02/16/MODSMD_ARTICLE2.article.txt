# Cal, S.C. Ducats Go on Sale Feb. 26
## 
### 
Student tickets for the Stanford-
U.S.C. basketball games at Stanford
on February 23 and 24 will go on
sale Monday and Tuesday at the
B.A.C. from 8:30 to 5:30. The Stan
will go on sale February 26 and 27.
Student tickets will be 40 cents,
and student body cards must be
shown. A limited number of unre
served general admission tickets will
be on sale at $1.10.
