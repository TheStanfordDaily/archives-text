# Police Chief Ousted By S.F. Commission
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 15 (UP).
—Chief William J. Quinn, who pi
loted the San Francisco police de
partment through nearly 10 years of
strikes, graft investigations and a
world's fair, was ousted today by the
San Francisco Police Commission
two months before lie was to have
become eligible for a pension.
