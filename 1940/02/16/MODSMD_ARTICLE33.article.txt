# Income Tax Collector Comes to Paly Feb. 19
## 
### 
For the convenience of those who
are required to file federal income
tax returns, a deputy collector of
internal revenue will be at the Palo
Alto post office on February 19, 20,
and 21 to assist taxpayers without
charge. Single persons with net in
comes of $1000 or over or gross in
comes of $5000 or over are required
to file returns.
