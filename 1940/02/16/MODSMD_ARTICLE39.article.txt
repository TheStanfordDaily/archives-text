# Frosh Baseballers To Meet S.F. Club
## 
### 
Commerce High of San Francisco
will be the freshman baseball team's
third opponent of the season, today.
The frosh nine will go after their
first victory at, 3:30 o'clock.
Coach Harry Shipkey said that if
the diamond had not dried by game
time, the tilt will be played at 9:15
o'clock Saturday. Phil Wolf is slated
to start, with Bill Hanlon behind
the plate. If the game is postponed,
there will be batting practice at 2:30
o'clock sharp, according to Shipkey.
