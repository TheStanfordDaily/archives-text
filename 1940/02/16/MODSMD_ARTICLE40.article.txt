# SOCIALITES TRIUMPH
## 
### 
PALM BEACH. Feb. 15 (UP).—So
cialites Grace Amory and Robert
Sweeny of Palm Beach won their
way into the finals of the Palm Beach
mixed foursome golf tournament to
day with a 1-up triumph over Marion
Miley of Lexington, Ky.. and Tommy
Goodwin of New York.
