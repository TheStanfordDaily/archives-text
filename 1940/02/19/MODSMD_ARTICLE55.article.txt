# Lightly and Politely
## HARDER BOWL BLUES 
### DOUG AQUES 
If all the Lagunita hashers were placed end to end,
they would stretch to the nearest barber shop. There
is something in the combination of hair and scissors
which they find irresistible.
Of course, the purge may have changed that. The
purge came when they trimmed the head hasher last
week.
His hair was long and blond and wavy, a subject
of popular conversation among the Lagunita women,
an object of temptation to the clipper hashers.
Human passions can be blocked only so long. The
day came when they said, "Those golden locks shall
wave no more." With scissors in one hand and the
head-hasher in the other, they went to work.
It is rumoi'ed that at the climax of this blitzkrieg,
the head-hasher yelled, "You can have your pound of
hair, but not an ounce of blood."
Then came the purge.
