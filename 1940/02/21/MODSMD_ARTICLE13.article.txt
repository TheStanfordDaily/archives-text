# U.S.C. Ducat Sale Closed to Students
## 
### 
Nearly 900 student tickets for the
Stanford-U.S.C. basketball games
Friday and Saturday nights were
sold at the B.A.C. up to 5 o'clock
yesterday afternoon.
General public tickets go on sale
today and Friday from 8:30 to 5
o'clock at the B.A.C. for $1.10. No
more student ducats are available.
