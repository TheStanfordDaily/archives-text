# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
JUNIOR COMMITTEE CHAIRMEN
and officers of the class will meet
in Memorial Court of the Inner
Quad for pictures on Monday at
12:45 o'clock.
NYA WORKERS must sign release
cards tomorrow, Monday, or Tues
day if they want to work this
coming month. Cards are in Mrs.
Coverley's office.
ROOM RENT DEPOSITS for Toyon
and Branner residents who wish
to reserve their rooms for spring
quarter are due next Monday and
Tuesday at the Dean of Men's of
fice.
TUITION NOTE APPLICATIONS
for spring quarter are due today.
Undergraduate men may file them
at the Dean of Men's office, gradu
ate men at Room 170, Ad Building,
and all women at the Dean of
Women's office.
STANFORD DAMES will meet at
the home of Mr. and Mrs. F. B.
Duzeneck at Hidden Villa in Los
Altos, 8 o'clock tomorrow, for a
party.
