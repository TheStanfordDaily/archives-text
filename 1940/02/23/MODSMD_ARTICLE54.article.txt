# Workers' Groups Protest Relief Cut
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 22 (UP).
The Workers Alliance and labor's
Non-Partisan League tonight pre
pared for an invasion of Sacramento
and a protest demonstration against
legislative relief curtailment at the
capitol tomorrow.
The demonstrators were to leave
San Francisco at 6 a.m. Leaders of
the league said Gov. Culbert L. Ol
son would be asked to address the
demonstrators and hearings will be
sought with Senate and Assembly
members.
