# HOGAN, McSPADEN TIE
## 
### 
NEW ORLEANS, Feb. 25 (UP).—
Harold (Jug) McSpaden, ol Boston,
Mass., and Ben Hogan, of White
Plains, N.Y., were tied for first place
with 214 tonight after three days of
play in the $10,000 New Orleans open
golf tournament.
