# Dawson Goes Down In Houston Tourney
## 
### 
HOUSTON, Tex., Feb. 25 (UP).—
Johnny Dawson, Holywood amateur,
found himself literally up a tree to
day as he lost the championship
round of the 2Glh Houston Country
Club invitation golf tournament to
Bobby Riegel, Houston, 7 and 5.
