# Mermen Swamp Two Opponents
## 
### 
The Stanford University varsity
swimmers turned in wins over both
the Athens Club and the Stanford
freshmen swimmers last Saturday in
the Encina Pool. The varsity rolled
up 09 points, the Athens Club 44,
and the freshmen followed with 43.
Co-Captain Roy Vitousck led the
varsity by turning in a twin-win
performance. Vitousek won the 50-
yard freestyle and the 200-yard
breaststroke in near record time.
Don Smith of the irosh also turned
in two wins by taking the 220 and
the 440-yard freestyles.
