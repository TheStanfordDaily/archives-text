# Rookie Shortstop Signed by St, Louis
## 
### 
RIVERSIDE, Feb. 26 (UP). —
Branch Rickey, St. Louis Cardinal
pilot, today said he was satisfied that
Shorstop Joe Oringo's place on the
Sacramento team would be filled
competently by Babe Plattner, of
the Decatur, 111., club in the Three-I
league.
