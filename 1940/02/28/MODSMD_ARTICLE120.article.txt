# FEMININE SHIRTMAKERS
## 
### 
Even that most tailored of tailor
eds, the shirtmaker dress, has gone
feminine this spring with such
touches as sheer white batiste or lace
outlining collars and cuffs. Some
shirtmaker frocks are even made of
sheer crepe, all-over lace, or bold
striped. organdie.
