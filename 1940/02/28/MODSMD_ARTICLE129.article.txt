# California Endorses Cotton for Formals
## 
### 
Cotton formals, which seem to go
perfectly with Ca.ifornia climate and
countryside, are especially popular
this year. These range from young
looking gingham jumper - type
dresses with sheer blouses to glam
orous, filmy dimities with skirts
measuring ten yards around the bot
tom.
Cotton lace is also in the limelight.
A dress made of white cotton lace
over a black taffeta petticoat will
rival the most glamorous confection
of chiffon or silk jersey.
