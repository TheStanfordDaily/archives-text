## 
### 
Stanford's varsity swimming team
will have its minds lull during the
next two weeks, when Coach Nort
Thornton's paddlers enter two meets
in. the role of underdogs.
Fullerton J. C. invades the Farm
Saturday afternoon and is favored

meet with the varsity and the frosh.
