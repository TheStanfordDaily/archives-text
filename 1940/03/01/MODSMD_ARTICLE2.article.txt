# Johnstone Appointed To Poli Sci Academy
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur re
cently appointed William C. John
stone, '30, dean of the junior college
of George Washington University, to
represent Stanford at the annual
meeting of the American Academy
of Political and Social Science at
Philadelphia, April 12-13.
