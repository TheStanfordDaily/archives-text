# Papoose Hurlers To Elect Captain
## 
### 
Freshmen baseball players will
elect a captain for the rest of the
season today, before their game with
San Mateo J. C. here at 3:30 o'clock.
The election will be at 2 o'clock.
Kept from practicing for seven
straight days because of rain, the
lrosh will go after their first win to
day after four losses. Right-hander
Phil Wolf will open on the mound
with "Ace" Hanlon catching.
Tomorrow at 9:30 o'clock, the
Card nine will clash with San Jose
High here in a game postponed from
last Tuesday. Next Tuesday after
noon the Papooses will tackle Marin
J. C. here.
