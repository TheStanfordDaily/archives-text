# Stanford Players Score Record Hit
## 
### 
During its five-night run on the
campus, "George and Margaret"
carried on the tradition Director
Strickland set for it. It, was an out
standing hit-
Three sellouts and reports that
about 150 persons had to be turned
away on the final nights speak for
the excellence of the Stanford Flay
ers' performance. According to
Chester Barker, secretary of the
Drama Division, between 800 and
000 persons saw it.
