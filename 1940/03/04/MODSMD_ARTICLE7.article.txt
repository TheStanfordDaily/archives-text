# One-acts to Join Old Farm Spirit
## 
### 
In order to support "Back to the
Farm" week, Ram's Head is asking
everyone to "come as you are" to
the Ram's Head prize plays to be
presented this Friday and Saturday
in the Little Theater at 8 o'clock.
"Saturday Night," by Gloria
Workman, "Meet Mr. Smith" by
Bud Cady, and "Wc Shall Not Sleep"
by Ted Hatlen will be the second
series of prize one-act plays pre
sented by Ram's Head. Tickets are
50 cents, all unreserved, but they
may be obtained early by calling
P.A. 7712.
