# FLYERS ENTER ACTIVE SEASON
## 
###

look forward to an active spring season. The members shown here are Bob Peterson, in pilot's seat; in
front of plane, left, Joe Griffin and Frank Martin, president of the club. The plane belongs to Dick Wil
