# Farm Bowl Team Beats Cal Squad
## 
### 
Led by Captain Jack Wilson, the
Stanford bowling team defeated Cal
2660 to 2.195 in the first competition
between the two schools Saturday
afternoon at Dodge's Redwood Eowl
in Redwood City.
Wilson was high series with a 567
total and Louis Tsoutsouvas was
high game with a 201 on his second
game. Dewhirst and Woolsey led
the Cal bowlers. The second meeting
of the Indians and Bears is slated for
March 9.
