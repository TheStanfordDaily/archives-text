# Casa Vice-Presidents Elected at Lagunita
## 
### 
Lagunila elections for spring quar
ter were completed last week with
the selection of vice-president for
each casa.
They are Ethelmae Davis, Adelfa;
Helen Kimball, Eucalypto; Betty
Rhoades, Granada; Jessie Calder
wood, Magnolia; Betty Tillotson,
Naranja; Denny Coblenz, Olivo; and
Ruth Eales, Ventura.
