# Nippon Lands Troops Near Macao Colony
## 
### 
HONGKONG, March 5 (UP)
Four hundred Japanese troops, land
ing from collapsible boats after four
hours of shelling by Japanese de
stroyers, went ashore at Heungchau,
near the Portuguese colony of Ma
cao, today and cut the highway be
tween Macao and the Chinese town
of Shekki.
