# Two Farm Men Win Fellowships
## 
### 
Melvin L. Renquist and William J.
Barclay, graduate students here, are
among eight college graduates in the
United States who have been granted
Charles A. Coffin Fellowships, it was
announced yesterday.
Renquist received his bachelor of
arts degree in chemistry from San
Jose State College, and is now study
ing for his doctorate. Barclay grad
uated from Oregon State and is at
present studying electrical engineer
ing. Both of them will remain here
and use the fellowship awards for
research in their respective fields.
The Charles A. Coffin Fellowships
are given annually to qualified
college graduates.
