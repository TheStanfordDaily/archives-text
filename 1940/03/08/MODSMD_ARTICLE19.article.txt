# JUNIOR PROM.
## Mr. Ellington's Group. 
### 
First Stanfordites step out.
And then the Duke steps out. For,
once spring vacation is over and the
Farms becomes populated again,
Duke Ellington and his 13-picre or
chestra will play for dances at the
Junior Prom April 19. Dress will be
semi-formal, according to Greyson
Bryan. Prom chairman. Bids, priced
at $3.50 a couple, will go on sale at
the A.S.S.U. office the first of next
quarter, he said. Scheduled for the
third Friday evening of spring quar
ter, the Prom will offer dancing from
9 to 1 o'clock.
