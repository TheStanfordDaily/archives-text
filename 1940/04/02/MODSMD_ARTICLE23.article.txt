# Junior Prom Bids Placed on Sale
## 
### 
When the first Stanfordite in yes
terday's early-morning Registration
Day crowd planked down his $3.50
for the initial bid, the class of '41
inaugurated ticket sales for the
forthcoming Junior Prom.
Pete Whittlesey and his commit
teemen established a booth outside
of the Administration Building yes
terday and the bids will continue on
sale in the A.S.S.U. office. Set for
the Pavilion on the night of April 19,
the Prom, first and major dance of
spring quarter, will present music
by Duke Ellington and his outstand
ing band. Dress will be semi-formal
and dancing will begin at. 9, last
until 1 o'clock.
