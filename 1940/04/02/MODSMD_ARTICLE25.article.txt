# Lutz Announces War Libe Gifts
## 
### 
According to Ralph H. Lutz, chair
man of (he Board of Directors of
the library, the Hoover Library on
War, Revolution, and Peace has re
ceived a number of literary gifts
during the past month.
Among the more interesting is the
gift of Howard C. Phillips, which
consists of a collection of 35 vol
umes of press clippings on the his
tory of the New Deal, and four vol
umes of press articles written by
Mark Sullivan, noted writer.
Additional gifts have also been
received from the estate of the late
Dr. Frank Angell of Stanford, from
Edward Anthony of New York, and
from the American Friends Service
Committee of Philadelphia.
