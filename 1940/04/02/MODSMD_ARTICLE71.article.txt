# Course Offered In Scout Training
## 
### 
Girl Scout Training, Education 10,
is being given this quarter on Thurs
day afternoons from 3 to 4:30 o'clock
by Miss Nancy Garratt. Arrange
ments are being made for outdoor
trips and practice work with Palo
Alto Girl Scout troops.
The course will include discus
sions on girls, their needs and ways;
program planning, group govern
ment and group administration for
girls between the ages of 7 and 17. !
One or two units credit will be
given upon arrangement.
