# Pro Grid League Completes Survey
## 
### 
■ J
Dayton, 0., April 2 (UP).—Pro
fessional football is providing the
funds for many players to complete
their education, a survey of the Na
tional Football league reveals.
Brooklyn stands at the top of the
list, with nine players. The Chicago
Bears were second, eight; New York
Giants, seven, and Pittsburgh, six.
All these men have enrolled for sec
ond semester work.
