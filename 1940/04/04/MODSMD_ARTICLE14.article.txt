# Enrollment Soars To 3995 Students
## 
### 
Registration figures released last
night show a total registration to
date of 3995 students, 2743 men and
1252 women. This is an increase of
182 over the same date last year.
Breakdown of the figures show
that 82 men and 43 women have
forfeited fines by registering late.
Registration officials estimate that a
high percentage of these are east
erners who have voluntarily done
so in order to get, longer vacations.
