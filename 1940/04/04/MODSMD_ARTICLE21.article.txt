# Women's Conclave Delegates Quartet
## 
### 
Coline Upshaw. Annabelle Clarke,
Anne Townsend, and Martha Nord
ling were named by Women's Con
ference Tuesday as delegates from
Stanford to the Western Association
of Women Students convention at
the University of Oregon, Eugene,
April 15, 16, and 17.
A regional meeting of women stu
dent leaders in Pacific colleges, the
conference meets alternate years
when there is no similar national
convention. Women's Conference
last year sent three delegates to the
national meeting at the University
of Kansas.
