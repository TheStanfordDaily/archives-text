# Trosh Tennis Men Smother Menlo 7-1
## 
### 
The Stanford freshman tennis
men turned in a 7-1 victory over the
Meido Junior College team yester
day afternoon on the varsity courts.
Tom Kruger, number one for the
Frosh, turned back Andy Roberts of
Menlo, 0-4. 8-6, in the number one
match.
