# Netmen Will Meet U.C.L.A. Varsity
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity net men meet
for the second time this season the
U.C.L.A. Bruins on the varsity courts
Saturday afternoon at 2 o'clock. In
their first meeting down south the
Card team smothered the Bruins un
der a 9-0 score.
The best match should be the
Larry Dee, Bob Stanford battle.
Stanford has a good sound tennis
game and Dee had a hard three-set
match with him in their first meet-
