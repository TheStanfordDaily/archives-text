ROOMS are now available in Toyon,
Branner, Encina, and Sequoia. Ap
plications can be made at oflice of
the Dean of Men.
BADMINTON CLUB meets tonight
at 7 o'clock in the Women's Gym.
TOYON CLUB meets tonight at 10
o'clock in Toyon lobby.
DEUTSCHE SINGSTUNDE will
meet tomorrow at -1:30 o'clock at
the German House, 514 Alvarado
Row. Visitors are welcome.
INTERNATIONAL CLUB meets to
night at 355 Lincoln Street at 7:45
o'clock. Cars leave the Nitery at
7:30. Kay Kitagwa will speak on
"Writing Chinese Characters."
FROSH MEN interested in being
football managers meet Friday at
3:45 on the practice field or next
Tuesday at 3:45 o'clock.
TRACKMEN meet at 7:30 o'clock to
morrow night in the B.A.C. with
Coach Bill Ellington.
Y.W.C.A. elections will be held to
day in all women's living groups.
All "Y" members are eligible to
vote.
