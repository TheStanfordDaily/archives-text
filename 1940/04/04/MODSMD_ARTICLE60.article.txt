# LUNG OPERATION TRIED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 3 (UP).
—Skilled surgeons, using the most
modern equipment at San Francisco
Hospital .attempted a dangerous but
necessary operation to remove a kid
ney bean from the lung of 14-
months-old Gwendolyn Brasier of
Burlingame today.
