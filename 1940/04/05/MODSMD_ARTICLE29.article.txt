# Encina to Sponsor Dance on April 12
## 
### 
In connection with an intense pub
licity campaign being carried on by
Encina Club officers, members of
Ben Parkinson's publicity commit
tee are contacting Encina residents
this week in order to raise interest
in the affair, which is opened to the
public this year.
Carvel Craig has been signed to
play for the dance which is sched
uled for Saturday night, April 1?„
The Encina lobby will be made up
as a "Marine Room," and a sur
prise theme known so far only as
"The Top of the Hall" will also be
featured.
