# Weakened Frosh Face All-Star Track Team
## 
###

track squad, riddled with injuries and
scholastic ineligibilities, faces the
cream of Peninsula cinder talent to
morrow morning at 10 o'clock at An
gell Field. The high school All-
Stars, recruited irom the P.A.L., will
furnish the Papooses with plenty of
competition.
