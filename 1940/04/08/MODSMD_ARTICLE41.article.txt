# Varsity Netmen Whip Bruins, 7-2
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity netmen de
feated U.C.L.A. last Saturday for the
second time this season on the var
sity courts. The Cards lost only one
singles and one doubles match in
taking a 7-2 victory.
Results: Dee (S) def, Stanford
(UC) 4-6, 6-2, 6-3; Wade (S) def.
Gordon (UC), 2-6, 6-4, 6-4; Low
(®) def. Beach (UC), 6-2, 6-2 ; Gates
(S) def. Prodon (UC), 6-i, 6-0;
Fox (UC) def. Brock (S), 6-4, 2-6,
6-1; and Seaver (S) def. Sugich
(UC), 6-4, 6-1. Doubles: Stanford
and Gordon (UC) def. Low and
Owen (S), 6-4, (?-0; Seaver and
Brock (S) def. Morgan and Sugich
(UC), 7-5, 6-4; Folsom and Rose
(S) def. Beach and Galloway (UC),
6-2, 2-6, 6-1.
The frosh also had a busy week
end defeating both San Mateo High
School and the Peninsula Tennis
Club.
