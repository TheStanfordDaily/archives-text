# Pomona Sponsors One-act Contest
## 
### 
Pomona College is offering prizes
in a one-act play contest sponsored
college students. Fifteen prizes are
being offered, the lirst of which is
$50.
The judges include Mary In toes,
chairman of the Pasadena Play
house playreading committee, Philip
Scheuer, drama editor of the Los
Angeles Times, and Norman Phil -
brick, drama director at Pomona.
Each original manuscript should
be sent ,with a one-dollar entrance
fee, and an entry blank giving the
author's name, college, address, title
of his play, and signature, to the edi
tors of the Criterion, Student Un
ion, Pomona College, Claremont,
California.
