# SEATTLE GIRL MISSING
## 
### 
PORT GAMBLE, Wash., April 8
(UP). —More than 100 searchers
scoured beaches and wooded areas
for Martha Hammons, a 20-year-old
dramatics student from Seattle, be
lieved lost today in the heavily tim
bered country of Northwest Wash
ington.
