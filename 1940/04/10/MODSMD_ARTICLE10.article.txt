# Orchesis to Give Program Tuesday
## 
### 
Original costumes, music, and
choreography by members of Or
chesis will highlight the group's an
nual dance concert next Tuesday
night. The program is being present
ed under the auspices of the Public
Exercises Committee as a feature of
the Tuesday Evening Series.
Miss Margaret Jewell, Orchesis
adviser, is general director. Solo
dances of their own composition will
be danced by Margo Crain and
Phoebe Gallas. The music is the
work of Miss Josephine Haskell.
Mild surrealism, dance history,
contemporary social problems, and
the Hoover War Library are the
various themes to be developed.
