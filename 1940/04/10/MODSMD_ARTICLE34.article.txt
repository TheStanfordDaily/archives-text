# BAER TO FIGHT MANN
## 
### 
NEW YORK, April 9 (UP). —
Buddy Baer of Liver more and Nath
an Mann, New Haven, Conn., have
signed for a 12-round bout on May
3 in Madison Square Garden, the
Twentieth Century Club announced
todav.
