# Lightly and Politely
## SHORT STORY 
### DOUG JAQUES 
The boy needed a new pair of shoes. He went
around to caucus.
"I can fill someone's shoes," he said.
They looked at him in surprise.
"I have small feet," he said, "but with a thick pair
of socks I can fill almost anyone's shoes."
"Do you have any other qualifications?" they said.
"No," he said.
They put him up for A.S.S.U. president.
