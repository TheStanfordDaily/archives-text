# Red Cross Offers Life Saving Drills
## 
### 
The preliminary training course
for Red Cross swimming and life
saving instructors will be given on
Saturdays from 11 to 1 o'clock dur
ing the next five weeks, according
to Terry Dearborn, Palo Alto Red
Cross Life Saving chairman.
Further details may be obtained
from Mrs. Margaret Barr at the
Women's Gymnasium, from Myron
Sprague at the Men's Gym, or
from the Palo Alto Red Cross Chap
ter, telephone, Palo Alto 22144.
