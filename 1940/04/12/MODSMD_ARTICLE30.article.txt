# Orchesis to Give Concert Tuesday
## 
### 
Their annual dance concert will be
presented by members of Orchesis
as a feature of the next Tuesday
Evening Series under the auspices
of the Public Exercises Committee.
The music, costumes, and dance
movements are the original compo
sitions of the group.
Margo Crain, dance major and
newly elected president of Orchesis,
is student chairman of the concert,
assisted by Yvonne Seybold and
Phoebe Gallas.
