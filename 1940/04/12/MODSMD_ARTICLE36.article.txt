# Card, Cal Golfers Clash Tomorrow

Dick Wright, in the hospital, Stan
lord's weakened but still powerful j
golf squad meets the California |
Bears here tomorrow morning. War
ren Berl, Sandy Tatum, Jack Sho
walter, Guy Showley, Don Ken
nedy. Sherm Selix, and Jim Haugh
will probably play for the Indians.

virtue of their overwhelming victo
ries over U.S.C. and U.C.L.A. the
last two week-ends. The freshman
team will be idle this week, with
practice rounds taking place.
