# Stanford Netters Whip S.F. State
## 
### 
Winning three doubles matches
and four singles, the Stanford var
sity netmen defeated the San Fran
cisco State College 7-2 on the local
courts. Playing number one for the
Cards was Lloyd Gates, who was
beaten 6-3, 6-2 by Harold Wagner,
ranking Northern California net star.
The frosh were also busy over the
week-end defeating the Modesto
Junior College, 6-2. Emery Neale
number one for the Frosh, played
lackadaisically and was upset by
Robbie Fisher of Modesto, 7-5, 1-6,
6-4. Harper Ink, playing the sec
ond spot for the Frosh, handed Bill
Ashton a 6-1, 6-4 trouncing.
