# Bulotti Announces Tryout Schedule
## 
### 
Tryouts for (ho Spring Show.
"I'm a Strangler Hero Myself.",
will he held today, tomorrow and
Friday from 1 to 5 o'clock in Me
morial Hall, it was announced yes
terday hy Charlie Bulotti. director.
Singing tryouts are scheduled for
today, and acting tryouts for to
morrow and Friday.
"All interested students are
urged to come over and try out."
Ilulotti said. "It's a hig cast, and
we intend to treat each Iryoutee
with the utmost courtesy and in
terest."
