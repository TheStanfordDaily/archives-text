# NEW EXPLOSIVE SPLINTERS POLE
## 
### 

THOSE NUMEROUS OVERSIZE toothpicks floating: through the air were once one whole telephone pole.
Lester P. Barlow blasted it with an eight-ounce charge of his new liquid oxygen-carbon explosive called
"Glmite." Barlow, who is trying to interest the government in bis invention, held this demonstration for
newspapermen near Baltimore, Md.
