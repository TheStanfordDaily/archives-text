# Lou Nova Will Train For Come-back Attempt
## 
###

Lou Nova, an unwilling punching
bag for Tony Galento last fall, an
nounced today he had tossed his
study of Yoga out the window and
he soon will start out. to avenge
the defeat by the barrel that walks
like a man.
