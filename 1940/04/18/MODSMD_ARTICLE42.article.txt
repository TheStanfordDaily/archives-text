# Frosh Swimmers To Meet Menlo
## 
### 
Headed by Don Smith, ex-Paly
star. Stanford's frosh swim team
meets the Menlo School and Menlo
J.C. at Encina Pools tomorrow af
ternoon.
"Doc" Colyear, backstroker, and
Smith are expected to bag plenty
of points for the Papooses. Phil Ehr
lich has a bad ankle and may not be
able to compete for the frosh. Bruce
Meyer will be in there in the breast
stroke.
Tex Ritter and Buck Hafer will
handle the springboard chores for
Coach Thornton's tankmen.
