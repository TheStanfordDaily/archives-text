# Archibald to Play For Frosh Dance
## 
### 
Morris Archibald and his "music
styled for dancing" have been
signed for the dance following the
Encina-Roble exchange dinner
scheduled for next Wednesday eve
ning, April 23, it was announced yes
terday by Encina Manager Frank
Bauman. The dance will be held in
the Encina lobby from 7 to 8 o'clock.
More women than men have
signed, according to Bauman, and all
Encinamen who wish to attend are
urged to sign up today in the din
ing halls.
