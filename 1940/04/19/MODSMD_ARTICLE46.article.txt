# THIS THIEVING WORLD
## 
### 
And now at the Phi Psi house they are having a
little domestic trouble. Some cad and thief stole all
the shoes in thr house, and locked them up in the
guest room.
That is, he stole all the shoes but one pair from
each room. Yes. he left one pair: and took the shoe
laces out and burned them.
Isn't that terribly funny?
Encina, they tell me. is rather quiet these days.
Which proves that Darwin was an ass. and things don't
work the way ho said at all.
