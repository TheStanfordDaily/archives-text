# Farm Professors Attend Convention
## 
### 
Miss Catherine A. Worthingham,
assistant professor, and Miss Luell
A. Weed, instructor, in the Stanford
Department of Hygiene and Physi
cal Education for Women will at
tend the convention of the American
Association for Health, Physical Ed
ucation and Recreation to be held in
