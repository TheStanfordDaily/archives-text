# BULL SESSION
## 
### SHERMAN MELLINKOFF 
(Sherman Mellinkoff, after a torrid and suc
cessful debating tour through most of the United
States, returns to the campus today with the en
viable reputation of not having missed a Bull
Session since he and Boh Moulton departed into
the wilderness immediately after winter quarter
finals.—The Editor.)
