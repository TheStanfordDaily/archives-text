# Appendectomy Hospitalizes Cuthbertson
## 
### 
Ken Cuthbertson, A.S.S.U. presi
dent, underwent an appendectomy
at 8:30 o'clock Monday night ;it the
Stanford Hospitalj
Dr. Blake Wilbur, son of Univer
sity President Ray Lyman Wilbur,
performed the operation and re
ported Cuthbertson is getting along
well. Cuthbertson had been under
observation all day Monday.
Miss Emily Beu, second floor
nurse, announced his condition as
"very good" and that he would be
released in seven or eight days.
Coline Upshaw, A.S.S.U. vice
president, said at 4 o'clock she had
called at the hospital and that Cuth
bortson was resting comfortably.
Because of his illness, Miss Upshaw
conducted last night's meeting of Ex
Committee and will probably con
duct next week's session.
