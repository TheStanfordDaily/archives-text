# Bacteriology Lecture Hall to Be Completed
## 
### 
The new auditorium for bacteriol
ogy lectures, now being built as an
addition to the Anatomy Building, is
expected to be completed in about
six weeks or two months, according
to Dr. E. W. Sehultz, head of the
bacteriology department. Next fall,
Dr. Schultz said the auditorium will
have to seat about 85 or 90 bacteriol
ogy and medical students.
