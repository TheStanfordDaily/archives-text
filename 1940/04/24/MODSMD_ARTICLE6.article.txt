# Vaccination Slips Fall Due Monday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at.
the Registrars office on Monday,
April 29, for all new students, in
cluding those having permit to at
tend classes, and all other students
whose certificates have expired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
o'clock Monday will result in a fine
of $2; or, if not filed by May 6 reg
istration in the University will be
subject to cancellation by the Com
mittee on Public Health, according to
the Registrar's office.
