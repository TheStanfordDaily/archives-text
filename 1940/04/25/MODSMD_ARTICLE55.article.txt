# Lightly and Politely
## TRIAL BY ERROR 
### DOUG JAQUES 
A minister from Palo Alto was visiting Lagunita
recently and wanted to use the telephone. He asked
one of the girls there where a telephone was. She
told him.
But she forgot to tell him that he couldn't use it,
that it was in illegal territory.
When the woman at the switchboard heard a male
voice asking for a telephone number, she sent out an
Amazon riot squad to get this illegal male away from
her dear girls.
