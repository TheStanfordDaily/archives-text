# BATTLESHIP WILL SAIL
##

BREMERTON, Wash., April 25
(UP).—The battleship U.S.S. New
Mexico prepared to sail tomorrow for
regular duty after completion of a
routine overhaul in the Puget Sound
Navy Yard.
