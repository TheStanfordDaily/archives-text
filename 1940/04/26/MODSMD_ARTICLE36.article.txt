# ’40 ‘Name Quad' Sales End Today
## 
### 
Today is the last time students
may buy the 1940 "Quad"' with
names on the cover. Sales will be
made in front of the post office un
til noon, and during the afternoon
at the Quad office.
Emphasis in this year's edition
will be on candid pictures. Included
in the annual will be 60 candid pages
covering all phases of Stanford life.
Six colored pages and a variety of
new ideas are also being used.
A special section will feature
Stanford professors. Another will be
dedicated to the Farm's sun bath
ers. The 1940 Quad will have a 15
per cent increase in photographs, as
compared to the 1939 edition.
