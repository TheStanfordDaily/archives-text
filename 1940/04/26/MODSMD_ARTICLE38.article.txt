# Farm Professors Will Discuss War
## 
### 
Next Wednesday ai 4:15 o'clock
the International Relations Society
will hold a meeting in the Women's
Clubhouse with Professors John W.
Masland, Philip W. Buck, and Wal
ter Thompson discussing the war and
its aspects in Scandinavia.
Professor Masland will discuss the
geographical aspects of the war;
Professor Thompson, the internal
aspects; and Professor Buck, the
international significance of the war.
