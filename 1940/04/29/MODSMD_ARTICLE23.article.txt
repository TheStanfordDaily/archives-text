# Frosh Classes Hold Barbecue
## Cal, Stanford Dance At Encina Jolly-up 
### 
An enthusiastic frosh class from
Stun lord and California thoroughly
enjoyed the first annual Frosh Frolic
held in conjunction with the Big
Meet Saturday. A water carnival,
barbecue, and dance made up the
all-day program.
After the reception committee
met the visitors at the station, the
two frosh classes attended the track
meet together, sitting in a specially
reserved section.
Following the meet the frosh pro- '
ceeded to the lake, which had been I
reserved for their exclusive use. A j
program of water sports had been |
arranged in which Jack Bleecher and |
Russ Vail were the final victors.
Records were played over a public
address system while supper was I
served. Following this, the frosh
danced in the Encina lobby from 8:30
to 12. George Morris and Bob Ar
chibald's orchestra played for the
dancers.
The St. Louis Cardinals trimmed
the lading Chicago Cubs yesterday
pounding the Windy City hurlers
for 11 hits to win 7-5.
