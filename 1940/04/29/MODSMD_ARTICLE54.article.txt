# Dr. Hand to Teach At Northwestern
## 
### 
Dr. Harold Hand, member of the
Stanford faculty, will teach in the
summer session of Northwestern
University. Students from every
state and ten foreign countries will
attend. The nation's best high school
journalists, actors, and debaters will
attend the high school institute.
Special vocational and recreational
programs will be offered. Dr. Hand
will participate in teaching guidance
and personnel courses.
