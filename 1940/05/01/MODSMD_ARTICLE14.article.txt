# Proctologist to Speak At Pre-Med Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. Dudley Smith, proctologist,
will be the guest speaker tonight at a
meeting of the Pre-Medical Society
at 7:30 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. Dr. Smith will show
movies dealing with cancer. Every
one interested is invited to attend,
according to Jeanne Bairos, secre
tary.
