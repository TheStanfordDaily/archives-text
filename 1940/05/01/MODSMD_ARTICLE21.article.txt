# Workers Unearth Human Skeletons
## 
### 
UKIAH, April 30 (UP).-Four hu
man skeletons were unearthed by
WPA workers today on a construc
tion job along the Longvale - Dos
Rios Highway, 45 miles from
Ukiah.
It was believed the skeletons
might be those of victims of the
"Battle of Bloody Run" in Califor
nia's early days, when whites
rounded up the Indians in Bloody
Run Canyon and massacred them
;ifter a series of horse thefts had
aroused the settlors.
