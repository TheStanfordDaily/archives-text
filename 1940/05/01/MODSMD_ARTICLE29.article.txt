# Farm Engineers Plan Trip North
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Student
Branch of the American Institute of
Electrical Engineers are planning a
field trip this week-end to the Mo
kelumne River development of the
Pacific Gas and Electric Company.
The northern trip will include in
spection of the newest hydro-elec
tric plant on the coast.
The party will leave Saturday
morning at 8 o'clock and will return
Sunday evening. All those interest
ed are requested to sign up on the
list posted on the bulletin board of
the Engineering Building.
Mr. E. A. Crellin of the P.G.&E.
will speak tonight at 7:30 o'clock in
Room 283 at a meeting for those
who plan to go on the trip. His sub
ject will be "The Mokelumne River
Development."
