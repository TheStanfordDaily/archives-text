# San Francisco Bank Reveals Finance Plan
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 30 (UP).
The Bank of America, fourth
largest bank in the nation, today
announced a plan to raise $30,000,-
000 in new capital in a financing
program conforming with its recent
settlement of a two-year feud with \
the office of the Comptroller of Cur
rency and Secretary of the Treasury
Henry Morgenthau, Jr.
