# Mural Paddlers Try Out Today
## 
### 
Intramural Manager Andy Stojko
vich announced yesterday that trials
for the intramural swim meet would
be held today starting at 4 o'clock in
the Encina Pool. Swimmers will
qualify by time trials and not by
places.
It. was also announced the first
game in the 1940 hardball season
would be played today, with Sigma
Nu and Theta Xi tangling at 4
o'clock on the freshman diamond.
