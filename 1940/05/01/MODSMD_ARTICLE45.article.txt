# Handball Tournament Open to All Students
## 
### 
Sign-ups are now being held for
the annual all-University handball
tournament. Anyone on the campus
is eligible to compete in this tourney
and the winner's name will be en
graved on the Jay Wiggins perpetual
trophy, which was started in 1925.
All interested are urged to sign up
on the blackboard in the gym.
In many cases, premature gray
hair is hereditary.
