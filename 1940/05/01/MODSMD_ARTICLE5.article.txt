# Dr. Hulme to Speak On Old-time Farm Life
## 
### 
"Stanford Life as It Used to Be"
will be the subject of a talk to be
given tonight by Edward Maslin
Hulme. emeritus professor of history.
Sponsored by Alpha Phi Omega, the
open meeting will start at 7:30 o'-
clock upstairs in the Women's Club
house.
