# Farm Tennis Aces Enter Tournament
## 
### 
Three members of the Stanford
varsity tennis team, Larry Dee,
Jimmy Wade, and Bob Low, left for
Los Angeles yesterday to compete in
the qualifying tournament of the
National Intercollegiates. All who
qualify will be entered in the na
tionals this summer in Philadelphia.
Ten singles players and six doubles
teams will be chosen from the
Coast.
Dee will also meet Bob Shrooder,
number two man on the Southern
California varsity, in the finals of
the collegiate division of Ojai. This
match was postponed from last
week's matches.
