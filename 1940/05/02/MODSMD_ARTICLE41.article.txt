# SHAW TO SEEK RECORD
## 
###

(UP). Predicting that a new rec
ord will be established in the 500-
mile Indianapolis Speedway motor
classic on Memorial Day and confi
dent that he will be the one who
will do it, Wilbur Shaw, two-time
winner, is already nursing his eight
cylinder Maserati back to full
strength for the event.
