# Lagunita Formal Slated for May 4
## 
### 
"Midsummer Night's Dream" will
be the theme of Lagunita Court's
annual spring formal, when Lagu
nita residents entertain their guests
Saturday night from 8:30 to 12
o'clock, May 4.
"The dancing will be in the La
gunita dining room to the music of
Carvel Craig," announced LaVonne
Frandson, chairman of the dance.
Dress will be semi-formal, formals
for girls and suits or tuxes for boys.
