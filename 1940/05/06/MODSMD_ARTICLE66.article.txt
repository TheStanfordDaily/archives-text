# Spring Sports Champions
## 
### 
Jhis section is devoted to a few of the Stanford athletes,
who have turned in the most startling performances of the
past several weeks, to those men who make the spectators
fight for seats, and who have done the most to put Stanford
on the athletic map.
Along with them are included the greatest assistant track
coach of all time, and the dressiest Stanford football coaching
staff in history. The football coaches incidentally are as yet
undefeated.
