# String Ensemble To Hold Concert
## 
### 
A concert featuring the San Fran
cisco String Quarter will bo present
ed tomorrow night at 8:30 o'clock in
the Education Auditorium under the
joint sponsorship of the Stanford
Music Department and the Friends
of Music Society.
The concert is one of a series
which has enabled Farm students to
hear performances by San Fran
cisco's string and woodwind en
sembles this spring.
The members of the quartet in
clude Ferenc Molnar, who teaches
chamber music in the University's
Division of Music, Naoum Blinder,
Eugene Heyes, and William Dehe.
