## 
### 

THE FIRST INTERCOLLEGE bridge championship played at the
Ritz-Caiicton Hotel, New York, ended with a win by Iwo RadcliiTe
women, Mary Ashley, second from left, and Muriel Goldberg. The win
ners are shown here being congratulated by the runner-up Princeton
pair, William Jennings, left, and Glen Maitland. Five eastern wom
en's colleges and seven Ivy League universities competed.
