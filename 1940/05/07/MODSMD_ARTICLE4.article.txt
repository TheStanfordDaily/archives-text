# Stanford Hostess To Speak on Radio
## 
### 
Hostess to thousands of visitors
each year. Miss Mildred Worswick
will describe the wide range of
functions which attract guests to
Stanford in a talk on "Public Events
at Stanford" to be broadcast at 9
p.m. Wednesday evening on the
Stanford University radio program
over KPO.
As secretary of the University's
Committee on Public Exercises, Miss
Worswick has the responsibility for
arranging for anything from a free
concert to the laying of a corner
stone. Her chief responsibility is
planning the Tuesday Evening Se
ries of lectures and musical pro
grams.
