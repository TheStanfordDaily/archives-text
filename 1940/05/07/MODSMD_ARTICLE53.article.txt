# Poli Sci Fraternity Elects 21 Members
## 
### 
Pi Sigma Alpha, honorary polit
ical science fraternity, elected 21
new members to its organization at
a meeting last week. The new mem
bers are: graduates, Francis J. Mul
doon, Johnson Sun Pao; seniors,
Ellen M. McCandless, Mary R. Wag
ner.
Juniors include June E. Blodgett,
Muriel Boggs, Joel L. Boyce, Paul
ine Brown, Ellen Douglas, Nancy J.
Groswold, Maurice Knox, Frank L.
Mai lory, Robert F. Peckham, Betty
J. Poole, Charles K. Pyatt, David
Salmon, Katherine E. Sanger, Dav
id B. Silberman, DeLancey Smith,
Ann A. Walker, and Robert Newell.
