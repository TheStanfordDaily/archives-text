# Students to Sign For Vocom Series
## 
### 
All men students wishing to sign
up for the Men's Vocational Com
mittee interview series should do so
on the Wednesday, Thursday, or
Friday preceding the week in which
they wish to make the trips. The in
terviews will be held every Wed
nesday and Thursday throughout
May.
The series has been arranged by
the Vocom in collaboration with the
Vocational Committee of the San
Francisco Rotary Club. Names and
businesses or profesisons interested
in should be left with Stanley L.
Sharp in the Dean of Men's office.
Under present plans groups of
three or four men will go to San
Francisco to interview business and
professional leaders.
