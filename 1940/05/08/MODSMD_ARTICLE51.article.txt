# ASSESS NAZI LOSSES
## 
### 
LONDON, May 7 (UP). —Germany
has lost 300,000 lons of merchant
shipping since April 1, probably in
cluding the giant "strength through
joy" motorship, Robert Ley. 27.288
tons, the Admiralty said today.
