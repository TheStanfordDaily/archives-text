# CANADIAN GOLFERS WIN
## 
### 
VANCOUVER, 8.C., May 8 (UP.—
Two top-ranking Canadian golfers,
Ken Black and Freddy Wood, teamed
up today to defeat Ralph Guldahl,
former U.S. Open titleholder, and
Johnny Revolta, one up in an exhi
bition best ball match.
