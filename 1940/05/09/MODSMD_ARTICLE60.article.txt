# S.F. LEADERS GO NORTH
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, May 8 (UP).—
The San Francisco Chamber of Com
merce anounced today 150 business
leaders would leave here tomorrow
on a good-will trip to Red Bluff,
Redding, and Shasta Dam for the
purpose of promoting better relations
between San Francisco and North
ern California interests.
