# Vitousek Sets New Breaststroke Mark
## 
### 
Roy Vitousek, co-captain of the
Stanford swimming team, establish
ed a new world's record for the 50-
meter breaststroke Saturday as Palo
Alto dedicated its new municipal
pool at Rinconada Park.
The Farm's big blond from Hono
lulu erased the mark of 31.6 set by
Paul Friselle of the Dragon Club in
1936. Vitousek's time was 31.4.
Swimming and diving stars of Stan
ford and San Francisco contributed
to the ceremonies with a series of
exhibitions.
The census this year was the 16th
decennial count taken in the United
States.
