# Library Receives Engravings, Books
## 
### 
Works of art ran literature a close
second among gifts to the University
for the month of April, according to
Nathan Van Patten, director of the
library.
Journals ranked first in number in
additions to the library shelves.
Gifts for the Albert M. Bender room
were numerous and included original
Hogarlh engravings, framed wood
cuts and a mahogany bookcase. Six
teen books were also added to the
Frances A. Bowman Collection.
