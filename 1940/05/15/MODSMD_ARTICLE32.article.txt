# BOTH SEEK NEW WORLD RECORDS
## 
### 

TWO OF THE STARS of "Champions of 1940," million-dollar talent
show and "Olympic Games" track meet for Scandinavian relief in Los
Angeles Coliseum Friday are Taisto Maki and Susanna Foster. IVlaki,
great Finnish runner will seek a new two-mile record, and Miss Foster
will attempt to sing "E above high C."
