# Special Men's Mass To Be Held Sunday
## 
### 
On Sunday, May 19, the second
annual Trinity Sunday Communion
and breakfast will be held for Cath
olic men of Palo Alto and Stanford.
As special function of the Newman
Club, the mass for men only will be
held at St. Thomas Aquinas Church
followed by breakfast at the Palo
Alto women's clubhouse. The
speakers will be Mr. "Marchy"
Schwartz, Professor A. J. Lynn and
Reverend Carrol, pastor.
