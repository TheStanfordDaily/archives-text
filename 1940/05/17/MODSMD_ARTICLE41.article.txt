# NAVY PLANES MANEUVER
## 
### 
HONOLULU. T.H., May 1G (UP).
—Sixty-eight planes from the air
craft carrier Saratoga made a mock
attack landing at Puunene on Maui
today in the extended maneuvers of
the U.S. Navy's battle fleet.
