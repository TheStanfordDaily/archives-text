# Harvard Appoints Stanford Graduate
## 
### 
Howard Wilson Baker, Jr., who
took his Master of Arts degree at
Stanford in 1927, has been selected
as one of five young writers to hold
the new Briggs-Copeland instruc
torships of English composition at
Harvard. The appointment becomes
effective next fall.
A graduate of Whittier, and a for
mer instructor at the University of
California, Mr. Baker has achieved
recognition as a poet, novelist, and
critic.
