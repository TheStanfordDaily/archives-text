# War Libe Given Finn Relief Data
## 
### 
Dr. Ralph H. Lutz, chairman of
the Hoover Library, announced that
a collection of materials on Finnish
and Polish relief has been given to
the library by the Finnish Relief
Fund of New York. Collection boxes
and electrical transcriptions of ap
peals were included in the exhibit.

The library received a collection
of letters written by Lieutenant
Colonel Philip Van Cise covering the
World War period, along with
books, pamphlets and military items
from Mrs. Ethel Van Cise of Den
ver, Colorado.

A supplementary collection of
posters on Finnish and Polish relief
was donated by Alfred L. Castle of
Honolulu, through Dr. Perrin C.
Galpin of New York City.
