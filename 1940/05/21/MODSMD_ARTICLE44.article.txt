# MELTON BLANKS CARDS
## 
### 
ST. LOUIS. May 20 (UP).—Lanky
Cliff Melton today hurled the New
York Giants to a shutout victory
over the St. Louis Cardinals. The
Cards garnered only six blows off
Melton while the Giants combed
Curt Davis and two relief hurlers
for nine.
