# London Cuts Off Continent Phones
## 
### 
LONDON, May 20 (UP).— Tele
phone service from London to
Switzerland, Jugoslavia, Paris, and
Rome was suspended tonight with
out explanation.
Service with Rome was suspended
at 5:20 p.m., with Paris at 5:45 p.m.,
Zurich at 2:20 p.m. and Zagreb at
.5:30 p.m. The postoflice, in charge
of communications, said there was
an "indefinite delay" on such calls.
No explanation was available, the
postoffice said.
