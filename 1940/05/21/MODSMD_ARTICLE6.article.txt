# Frosh to Debate Dating Question
## 
### 
"Resolved, that Stanford women
should pay half on dates" will be the
topic for the annual Roble-Encina
debate to be held Thursday evening
at 7:30 o'clock in the Roble lobby.
The meet is sponsored by Alpha Phi
Omega, national service fraternity.
Robleites will uphold the affirma
tive and Encinamen will argue on
the negative side. Winners will be
awarded a permanent cup. Roble was
the victor last year.
Judges for the debate will be
Dean of Men John Bunn; Miss Rosa
mond Clarke, director of Union
Unit; and Harold Maile Bacon, as
sistant professor of mathematics and
senior faculty adviser of Alpha Phi
Omega.
