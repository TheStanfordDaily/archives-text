# Brown to Speak For Lunch Club
## 
### 
Problems of poverty and relief in
the United States will concern Pro
fessor Harold Chapman Brown of the
Farm philosophy department today
when he speaks for lunchers at noon
in the Nitery.
Social aspects of the issue will be
dealt with under the heading "Un
dramatic Poverty in America." The
Stanford faculty member will reveal
the reality of the unemployment
situation.
Virgil Hinshaw will preside at
the weekly session of the Luncheon
Club. Anyone on campus is invited
to attend. Buffet luncheon is served
for 25 cents. There will only be two
more meetings this quarter, includ
ing today's.
