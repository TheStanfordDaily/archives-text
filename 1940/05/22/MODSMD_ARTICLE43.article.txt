# WPA to Sponsor Campus Exhibits
## 
### 
The Federal Works Agency-Work
Projects Administration of North
ern California is sponsoring three
exhibits on the campus this week.
The Old Roble Pool Building will
be the scene of two displays, one
of Occupational Trends Research
from 9 to 5 o'clock through Friday,
and the other books published in
America from 1637 to 1876. These
will be on exhibition from 9 to 5
o'clock today.
A historical records survey will
be displayed in the Library corri
dor from 1 to 4 o'clock today.
