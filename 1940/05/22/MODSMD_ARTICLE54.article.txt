# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
JUNIOR ORCHESIS meets at 5
o'clock in the dance studio.
STANFORD DAMES AND HUS
BANDS will hold a picnic supper
at the home of Frank Duvenck,
Los Altos, tomorrow. Cars leave
Palo Alto High School at 4:15
o'clock. Each couple should bring
own box supper.
'Y' SOCIAL SERVICE GROUP
meets at Women's Clubhouse at
3:15 o'clock, where transportation
will be provided ot Atherton to
hear Dr. Clark Kerr speak.
FOREIGN SERVICE AND TRAVEL
CLUB will not meet tonight. Next
meeting will be May 29 at 7:15
o'clock in the West Memorial Li
brary.
INTERFAITII COUNCIL OFFICERS
are asked to meet today at 1
o'clock in the Nitery.
ALL SENIOR WOMEN will meet
tomorrow night at Lagunita from
6 to 8 o'clock.
