# Madrigal Singers To Be Presented
## 
### 
The Madrigal Singers from San
Francisco State College will be pre
sented Sunday night at 8:30 o'clock
in the Education Auditorium by the
Friends of Music. This will be the
second of two chamber music con
certs given here.
The singers sing in the Elizabethan
style while sealed around a table.
