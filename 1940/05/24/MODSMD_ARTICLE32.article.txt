# Senior Dance Bids Selling at A.S.S.U.
## 
### 
c
Bids for the 1040 Senior Ball, to be
held in Toyon Hall Friday evening,
June 14, are now on sale at the
A.S.S.U. office. The bids sell for
$8.50 per couple and every hundredth
ticket is given away free.
Lud Gluskin and his 20-piece Co
lumbia Broadcasting orchestra will
play for the dance, accompanied by
Martha Tilton, popular vocalist for
merly with Benny Goodman. Danc
ing will last from 11 to 4 o'clock.
