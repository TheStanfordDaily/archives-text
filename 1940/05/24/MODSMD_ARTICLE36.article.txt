# Bob Low Elected Farm Net Captain
## 
### 
Bob Low, two-year lettermen, was
elected caplain of Stanford's 1941
tennis team at a meeting of the net
squad yesterday. He will succeed
Dave Brock.
Low will lead a contingent of four
other letter-winners into action next
year, including Larry Dee, Jimmy
Wade, Stan Owen, and Alan Rose.
Low. Dee. and Wade are scheduled
to play in the National Collegiates
this summer, all having qualified at
Los Angeles early this month.
Manasas National Battlefield Park
in Virginia is the newest link in
the chain of Civil War battlefield
parks.
