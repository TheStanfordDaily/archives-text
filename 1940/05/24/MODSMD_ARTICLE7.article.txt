# Sigma Delta Chi Initiates Eight
## 
### 
Jerry Mayer was elected president
of Stanford's chapter of Sigma Delta
Chi, national men's professional jour
nalistic society, for next year. He
will succeed Dick Dudman. Dwight
Hutchinson was chosen secretary
treasurer.
Eight new members were initiated
into the organization this week, in
cluding Bud Ames, Ed Brady,
Charles Bull, Wilson Cannon, Bob
Connell, Dan Hays, Bob Low, and
Wayne Wilson.
