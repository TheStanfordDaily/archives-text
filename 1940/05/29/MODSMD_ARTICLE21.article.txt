# Farm Phi Betes To Hear Parsons
## 
### 
Bishop Edward L. Parsons of the
Episcopal church and a national
vice-president of the American
Civil Liberties Union will speak at
an open meeting of Phi Beta Kappa
tomorrow night in Ihe Education
Auditorium at 8 o'clock.
Bishop Parsons' speech will be en
titled, "Lo, the Poor Liberal," and
will follow the annual dinner which
will be hold in the Union at 6
o'clock. Newly elected members will
be guests at the dinner. Charge for
old members and their guests will be
75 cents.
Following the open meeting a
business meeting for the initiation
of new members and election of of
ficers will be held in Room 78 of the
Education Building.
