# Stanford Scholarships Awarded to 55 Students For '40-41 Academic Year
## 29 to Receive Newell Scholarships; Complete University Honors Listed By President Ray Lyman Wilbur 
### 
President Ka.v Lyman Wilbur yesterday announced the names of 55
graduate and undergraduate students who will receive scholarships and
fellowships at Stanford University during the next four quarters.
Twenty-nine of the students will receive Henry Newell scholarships,
established by the late Henry Newell of Sal City. The Newell Fund

ii, the largest at Stanford, amounting<
to nearly $225,000, and is used chiefly
for students from Utah.
