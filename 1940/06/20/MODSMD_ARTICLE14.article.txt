# Martin First In Tuesday Series
## 'America Faces The World,' His Subject 
### 
The opening program In the Tues
day Evening Series for the summer
quarter will be a lecture on "Amer
ica Faces the World" by Dr. Charles
E. Martin, acting professor of po
litical science.
The Tuesday Evening Series,
which is presented during all four
quarters of the year, is offered to
the University community without
charge. The programs are held in
the Auditorium of Memorial Hall at
S o'clock.
Dr. Martin will give a brief re
view of contemporary American
policy as regards Europe, the Amer
icas, and the Far East.
He is head of the political science
department at the University of
Washington and specializes in the
field of international law and rela
tions.
