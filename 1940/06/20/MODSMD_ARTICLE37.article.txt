# Bring Cushions To Vesper Services
## 
### 
Beginning on Sunday, June 30.
Vesper Services will be held
throughout the summer in the
Laurence Frost Amphitheater every
Sunday at 4 p.m.
Those attending are asked to
bring blankets or cushions if they do
not wish to sit on the grass.
Special music and short talks will
be heard at these services while the
chief charm will lie in quiet group
meditation drawing upon the beauty
of the amphitheater, according to
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, University
chaplain.
