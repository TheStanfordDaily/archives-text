# Tryouts Slated For Summer Play
## 
### 
Parts for ten men and four women
in Stanford's first dramatic produc
tion of the quarter, "Mr. Congress
man," will be awarded after "try
outs" to be held in the Little The
ater this evening.
Open to all registered students as
well as interested faculty members,
the tryouts will begin at 7:30 p.m.
under the supervision of F. Cowles
Strickland, director of dramatics at
Stanford for the summer quarter.
"Mr. Congressman," a satire of
contemporary United States politics,
by Leon Pearson, will have its pre
miere production in the Memorial
Theater at Stanford. Following the
tryouts this evening, the cast will go
into immediate rehearsal.
