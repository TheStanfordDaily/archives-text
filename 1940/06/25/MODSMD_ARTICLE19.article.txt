# All May Swim At Women's Gym
## 
### 
Announcement was made yester
day that the pool at the women's
gymnasium will be open to women
students for recreational swimming
Monday through Friday, 4 to 5:30
p.m., during the eight weeks' session
of the summer quarter.
On Sundays from 3 to 5 p.m. the
pool will be open to men and women
students, their wives and husbands,
and to the men and women of the
faculty listed in the current Bawl
out.
Children of students and faculty
may swim on Sundays, 3 to 4 p.m.
provided they are accompanied by
their parents.
The fee for Sunday swimming is
15 cents except for women regis
tered in classes in the gymnasium.
Badminton and volleyball courts
will be open on Sundays from 3 to
5 o'clock.
Women of the faculty may swim
Monday through Friday at 10 a.m.
The fee is $5. Employees of the Uni
versity may swim Monday through
Friday during the noon hour for
$3.50.
