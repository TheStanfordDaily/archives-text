# Berl Stroke Behind Early Leader
## 
### 
Four of Stanford's six-man golf
team were among the early finishers
in the first round of the national
collegiate championships at Man
chester, Vt., today.
Warren Berl, runner-up last year,
was one over par with a 71, a single
stroke behind the leader, Neil
Croonquist of the University of
Minnesota.
Of the remaining members of the
team, Don Kennedy shot 76, Don
Haslitt 79, and George Bradley 80.
Scores of Sherman Selix and Hal
Stone were not included among the
early reports.
