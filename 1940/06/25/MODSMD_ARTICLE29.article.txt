# Nine Stanfordites Embark for Japan
## 
### 
Fifty-eight American college stu
dents, including nine from Stanford
University, sailed yesterday aboard
the Asama Maru to attend the Sev
enth American-Japan Student Con
ference in Japan next month.
In Tokyo the delegation will meet
with 100 Japanese students to at
tempt to work out a basis for con
tinued friendly relations between
Japan and the United States.
Edmund W. Pugh, Jr., of Stanford,

Nat Brown, Jack Calderwood, Kay
Kitagawa, Virginia McCloskey,
Hugh O'Donnell, Bill Turner, Roy
Vitousek, and Marlyn Whedon.
