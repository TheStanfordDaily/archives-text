# Dr. Koo to Preach Here Next Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. T. Z. Koo, first guest speaker
this summer in Memorial Church,
will preach Sunday morning at 11
o'clock.
Dr. Koo is a leader in the move
ment promting friendly relations
among the Pacific nations, and,
through his activities in the Insti
tute of Pacific Affairs, has been asso
ciated with Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur.
