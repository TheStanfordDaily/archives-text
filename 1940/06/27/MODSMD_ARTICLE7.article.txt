# July 5 Last Day For Study Changes
## 
### 
Friday, July 5, is the last day stu
dents may change their study lists
for this quarter without fine. After
July 5 all program revisions must be
made by petition to the Registration
Committee, subject to the following
fines: $2 for each course added or
dropped, or $3 where one course is
substituted for another.
Students are held responsible for
courses and units as they appear on
their official study lists filed at the
same time as registration, and no
credit is given for courses which do
not appear thereon. This applies to
Physical Education as well as aca
demic subjects.
