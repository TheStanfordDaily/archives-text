# Irvine to Discuss Shaw in Radio Talk
## 
### 
Ireland's razor-witted sage, George
Bernard Shaw, will be the subject of
Professor William Irvine's radio talk
on station KPO and the NBC Red
Network Wednesday night at 9:30
o'clock.
Professor Irvine, of the Stanford
English department, will appear on
the Stanford University on the Air
program, which is a regular Wed
nesday night feature on NBC. He
is the author of a recent definitive
biography of Walter Bagehot.
