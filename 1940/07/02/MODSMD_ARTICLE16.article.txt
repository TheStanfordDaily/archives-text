# Terman to Address Lunch Club Today
## 
### 
"Gifted Children" will be the sub
ject of an address by Dr. Lewis M.
Terman, of the psychology depart
ment, before a luncheon meeting of
the Education Club in the Union
dining room today.
Dr. Terman began his study of
gifted children 20 years ago, and re
cently he has been engaged in fol
lowing up the careers of the gen
iuses.
The Education Club will be under
the direction of Elden B. Bushy,
who was elected president at the
first meeting.
