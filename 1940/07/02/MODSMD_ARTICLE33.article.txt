# Farm Netmen Win; Enter Finals
## 
### 
Stanford and California carry
their rivalry far afield these days.
All the way to Haverford, Pa., in
fact, where the Cardinal doubles
tennis team of Larry Dee and J.
Wade yesterday won their way to
the intercollegiate tennis finals op
posite' the Golden Bear's entry of
Peacock and Harmon.
Stanford reached the finals by
downing George Toley and Ted
Schroeder of U.S.C., 6-3, 7-9, 6-2,
6-2, as California beat another Tro
jan pair, Bill Reedy and Kenneth
Bartlett, 6-2, 7-5, 6-3.
Don McNeill of Kenyon meets Joe
Hunt of Navy in the finals of the
singles competition.
