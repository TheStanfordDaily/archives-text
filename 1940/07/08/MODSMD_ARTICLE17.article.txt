# Stanford Host To Golfers July 13
## 
### 
Outstanding golfers of the Bay re
gion will tee off July 13 for the an
nual Stanford invitational. The big
tourney will get under way with the
qualifying round next Saturday. A
few of the early birds who want to
get used to the course will be able
to take their entry rounds on Fri
day.
The match play starts on Sunday
and the finals will come the follow
ing week-end, according to John
Kohler, the Stanford Club cham
pion, who is the general chairman in
charge of the affair .
It has not been learned yet
whether or not Warren Berl, Stan
ford ace, will be here on the Farm
to defend the title he won in 1938
and 1939.
