# Bunn Appoints 3 To Men's Council
## 
### 
Final appointments for the sum
mer quarter Men's Council have
finally been confirmed. Andy Stoj
kovich, Don Ruggles and Jim Haugh
are the three men who will mete out
justice. Meeting time for the group
will be at 2:15 o'clock every Mon
day afternoon.
Men's Council usually is the elec
tive group that hears cases on honor
code violations. In summer quarter,
however, it is the body that is se
lected by the Dean of Men and it is
in charge of social as well as judi
cial events. Haugh, head of the pre
reg next fall, was the only under
graduate selected to serve this sum
mer.
