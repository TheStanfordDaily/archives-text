# Varian to Lead Pismo Dunes Hike
## 
### 
Russell Varian, research associate
in physics, is leader of the hike to
Pismo Sand Dunes on August 31,
September 1, 2, sponsored by the

Poma Prieta Chapter of the Sierra
Club.
The club has planned various
walks throughout the local vicinity
for the months July through Octo
ber. Information regarding the trips
may be had from members of the
Local Walks Committee.
