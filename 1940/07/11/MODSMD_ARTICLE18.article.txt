# Bach Featured In Organ Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, is offering two recitals this
week. The first will be given this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in the Me
morial Church, and will be com
posed entirely of works by Johann
Sebastian Bach.
Sunday's recital will be varied,
climaxed by Lamentation in D minor
by Alexander Guilmant, to be ren
dered in memory of France.
