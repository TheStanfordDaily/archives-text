# A.S.S.U. to Hold Toyon Date Dance
## 
### 
A.S.S.U. Student Manager Rich
ard A. Stepp announced today there
will be a dance in the Toyon ball
room Friday evening, July 26. Ac
cording to Dance Chairman Warren
Cleary, the music will be furnished
by Ozzie Secrest and his orchestra.
Mr. Cleary stated that the dance
will start at 9 o'clock and will end at

1 o'clock. Starting a new trend dur
ing summer quarter, the dance will
be a date affair. The bids are now
on sale for 70 cents at the A.S.S.U.
office.
The entire student body is cordial
ly invited to attend. Those desiring
dates can contact Miss Virginia Stev
ens in Union residence.
