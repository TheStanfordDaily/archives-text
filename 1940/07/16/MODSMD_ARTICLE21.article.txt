# Kovacs Downs Dee In Semifinal Play
## 
### 
Top seeded Frank Kovacs of Oak
land justified his classification last
Saturday by Smashing through
Larry Dee, the Stanford racquet
wielder. In three blistering sets with
the count of 6-0, 6-1, 6-0, the Oak
land lad took the match in the semi
finals of the Gold Racquet tourney
at North Conway, N.H.
Stanford was defeated in the semi
final doubles when Bill Reedy and
Kenneth Bartlett of U.S.C. beat Dee
and Jim Wade 6-2, 6-2, 6-4.
