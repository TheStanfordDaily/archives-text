# Vaccination Blanks Due This Thursday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at
the Registrar's office on Thursday,
July 18, for all new students, in
cluding those having permit to at
tend classes, and all other students
whose certificates have expired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
o'clock on Thursday will result in a
fine of $2. If not filed by July 25
registration in the University will be
subject to cancellation by the Com
mittee on Public Health.
