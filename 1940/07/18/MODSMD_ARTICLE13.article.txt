# Church to Sponsor Record Program
## 
### 
A program of newly-recorded
music will be played in the Choir
Room of the Memorial Church to
morrow afternoon, at 4:15 o'clock.
The Division of Music will continue
to offer other such programs if stu
dent demand calls for them.
The novelty of the Friday program
will be a piece particularly appro
priate ofr summer, "Under the
Spreading Tree." Jaromir Wein
berger has made this jolly English
folk-tune basis for seven variations
and a corking fugue for orchestra.
