# A.S.S.U. Plans For Summer Informal
## 
### 
Preparations for the A.S.S.U.
summer informal are nearing com
pletion with the selection of Ozzie
Secrest's orchestra and the final
drafting of the committees. Heading
the dance to be given in the Toyon
lobby on July 26 is Warren Cleary.
His committee heads are Alan
Houser, vice-chairman; Jack Fit
ting, publicity; George Rockwell, re
freshments; and Zelda Jolin, clean
up. Bids are now on sale at the
A.S.S.U. office.
