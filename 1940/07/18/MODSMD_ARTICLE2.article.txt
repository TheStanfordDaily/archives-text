# Rhodes Scholar To Preach Sunday
## 
### 
Reverend Clarence A. Spaulding,
D.D., will speak on "The Dilemma of
the Good Man" Sunday morning at
11 o'clock in the Memorial Church.
Dr. Spaulding, a Rhodes Scholar and
graduate of Occidental College, has
been a frequent visitor to the Stan
ford campus, having occupied the
Memorial Church pulpit on several
occasions.
Vesper services will also be con
ducted in the Laurence Frost Am
phitheater at 4:30 o'clock in the
afternoon.
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give a recital in the Me
morial Church at 7:30 o'clock in the
evening.
