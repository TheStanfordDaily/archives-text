# No-date Dance This Friday in Toyon
## 
### 
Toyon Lobby will be the scene of
the third no-date dance of the quar
ter, to be held Friday night from 7
to 9 o'clock. The dance will be in
formal and everyone is invited.
Mr. and Mrs. Armen Alchian, Miss
Rosamond Clark, and Professor and
Mrs. Max Savelle will be chaperones.
