# County Asked To Aid Celebration
## 
### 
The Santa Clara county board of
supervisors, meeting in San Jose
yesterday, were invited to partici
pate in Stanford's 50th year celebra
tion by contributing $2,000.
This amount would be added to
the $6,000 contributed by the Palo
Alto city council. The answer to the
request is expected at the next meet
ing.
A complete working model of the
200-inch Mount Palomar telescope
(world's largest) is on display at the
Westinghouse exhibit at the Golden
Gate International Exposition.
