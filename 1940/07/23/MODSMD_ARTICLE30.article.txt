# Station Available For Farm 'Hams'
## 
### 
All amateur short-wave radio
fans on the campus are invited to
make use of the extensive transmit
ting facilities of station W6NU, now
available in the old Women's Gym
under the sponsorship of the local
Naval Communications Reserve unit.
Lieut. H. V. Wardlow, commander
of the unit, announces that three
transmitters of 300-watt, 60-watt,
and portable types, are at the dis
posal of any Stanford "hams" who
care to use them.

A class in radio transmission is
held every Thursday night in the
station,
