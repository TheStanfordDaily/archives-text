# Antheil Will Play Music of Moderns
## 
### 
Mr. George Antheil's third and
last lecture on American music is
scheduled for tomorrow aftexnoon
in the Education Auditorium at 4:15
o'clock.
In previous lectures, Mr. Antheil
discussed the American composer's
heritage from the past and some of
the work being done in the present
day. In tomorrow's lecture he will
play some of the music being done
by younger men who will be the
composers of tomorrow and will in
dicate the ways in which the Ameri
can composer is interested in build
ing up his audience.
The public is invited.
