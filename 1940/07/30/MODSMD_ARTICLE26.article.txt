# Smith Speaks On Goldoni Influence
## 
### 
Works of the creator of modern
Italian comedy, Carlo Goldoni, and
his influence on the former corrupt
form of stilted Italian comedy was
the subject of the fifth in a series
of lectures sponsored by the Drama
tists' Alliance. Stanley Astredo
Smith, professor of Romanic lan
guages, was the speaker.
The history and development of
the Italian theater was sketched
briefly by Professor Smith in addi
tion to his portrayal of the life of the
first of the modern Italian play
wrights.
