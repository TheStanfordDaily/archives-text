# Dramatists to Read Prize Verse Plays
## 
### 
The Assembly Week of programs,
lectures, presentations and exhibits
presented by Dramatists' Alliance
will open next Monday with the
formal group play reading of the
prize winning Anderson Verse
Award entry.
The play winning the award is to
be cast for reading Wednesday after
noon. Anyone interested in tryout
for parts is invited to come to Room
200 (basement of History Building)
at 4:30 o'clock Wednesday.
