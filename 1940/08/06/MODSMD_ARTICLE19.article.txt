# Public Opinion Experts Due
## 
### 
Four well known authorities on
public relations will lecture at the
Short Course on Public Relations
which begins here next Monday.
Archibald M. Crossley, who in
1936 conducted the Crossley poll on
the presidential election for Hearst
newspapers, will offer three lectures
on the subject of public opinion
measurement.
Dr. Harwood L. Childs, chairman
of the editorial board, of the Public
Opinion Quarterly, and associate
professor of politics in Princeton
University, will lecture daily on
"Public Opinion and Propaganda."
Dr. Childs is a leading authority on
the subject of public opinion.
Dr. Don D. Lescohier, professor of
economics at the University of Wis
consin, will lecture daily on "Indus
trial Relations." He has done ex
tensive work in investigating labor
conditions for the United States De
partment of Agriculture and has
served as consultant for manufactur
ing, mercantile, and other organi
zations.
