# Hit Tunes To Be Feature of Dance
## 
### 
Art McCue's popular dance or
chestra will play a collection con
sisting of the Union Women's ten
favorite song hits at the Union-
Toyon summer informal Friday eve
ning at the Toyon lobby.
Patrons and patronesses for the
evening will be Miss Rosamond
Clark, Miss Grace Glenn, Mr. and
Mrs. Waldemar Johansen, and Mr.
and Mrs. Albert J. Lynd.
