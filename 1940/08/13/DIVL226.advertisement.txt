WalsteroS'
.6 the C/
q v.
Round-the-Clock hose are enchantingly sheer
and flattering . . .* mould the natural contours
of your legs perfectly . . . add a note of beau
tiful color harmony to your whole costume.
Styled to give you a new conception of hosiery
beauty! Constructed to give you longer, more
satisfactory wear.
You may choose these hose in three different
weights for all-around wear. Morn, 4-thread;
Noon, 3-thread; and Nite, 2-thread for sheer
evening use. The name is etched in the top
for easy identification. All are made of clear,
lovely crepe silk, carefully reinforced to give
you the maximum wear you may reasonably
expect from each weight.
