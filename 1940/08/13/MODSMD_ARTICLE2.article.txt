# Trio Featured In 3rd Concert
## Beethoven's C Major Sonata to Be Played 
### 
Sylvia Lent, violinist, Gunnar Jo
hansen, pianist, and Victor Gottlieb,
violincelloist, will play a series of
Beethoven sonatas and trios tonight
at 8 o'clock in the auditorium of Me
morial Hall. This is the third in a
series of four Beethoven programs
presented by these artists at Stan
ford University.
The program is as follows:
Sonata in C Major, Opus 102, No. 1
Andante—Allegro vivace—Adagio
Allegro vivace
Mr. Gottlieb and Mr. Johansen
Trio in C Minor, Opus 1, No. 3
Allegro con brio
Andante cantabile con variazioni
Menuetto: Quasi allegro
Finale: Prestissimo
Miss Lent, Mr. Gottlieb,
and Mr. Johansen
Sonata in A Major, Opus 47
Adagio sosentuoto —Presto
Ondante con variazioni
Presto
The public is invited.
