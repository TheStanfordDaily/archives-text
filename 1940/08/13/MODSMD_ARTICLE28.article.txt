# Thursday Piano Recital Cancelled
## 
### 
The Division of Music announces
the postponement of the piano re
cital to have been given on Thurs
day afternoon by George Antheil,
composer-in-residence during the
summer quarter. Mr. Antheil will
play on August 22 a program consist
ing of outstanding works by mod
ern composers.
There will be no more Thursday
afternoon recitals in the Memorial
Church this quarter. The two final
organ recitals will be presented Sun
day evenings, August 18 and 25 at
7:30 o'clock.
