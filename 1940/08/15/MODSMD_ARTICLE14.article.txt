# W.C.T.U. Group To Meet Sunday
## 
### 
At the annual union church serv
ice conducted by the local W.C.T.U.
scheduled to be held next Sunday
evening, August 18, 7:45 o'clock at
the Presbyterian Church on Forest
Avenue and Waverley Street, Ar
thur S. Maxwell will discuss "Amer
ica's Greatest Menace."
Mr. Maxwell, who succeeded
Alonzo L. Baker as editor of the
Signs of the Times, is a world trav
eler, lecturer and author.
The meeting will be held under
the auspices of the various Protest
ant denominations in Palo Alto.
Campus visitors are cordially wel
comed.
