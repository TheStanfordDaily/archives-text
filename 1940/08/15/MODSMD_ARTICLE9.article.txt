# Organist to Play In Family Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will be assisted by the Haug
family of San Francisco in the recital
scheduled for 7:30 o'clock Sunday in
the Memorial Church.
Julius Haug, the father, is violin
ist; his son Eddie plays the trumpet,
and his daughter Julia plays the
flute. Robert Fox, graduate student,
will play the oboe.
The foursome will appear in the
Second Brandenburg Concerto, sup
ported by the string orchestra which
played last Sunday, and by Dr. Allen
at the organ. Dr. Allen will play
Bach's Passacaglia and Fugue in C
Minor.
