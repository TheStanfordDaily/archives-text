# Instructor Called To Annapolis
## 
### 
Ralph J. Michels, instructor in Ro
manic languages, will leave the
Stanford campus tomorrow for the
Annapolis Naval Academy, where he
will teach Spanish in response to an
order calling him to active duty.
Mr. Michels, a reserve officer, will
have the rank of Lieutenant Junior
grade.
He has been at Stanford since
1935.
