# HARLOW TO TALK AT U.C.L.A.
## 
### 
Two weeks instruction in public
relations problems will be offered by
Rex F. Harlow, lecturer on public
relations at Stanford on the U.C.L.A.
campus beginning August 26. The
Los Angeles course is one of four
1940 public relations courses on the
Pacific Coast, with others being held
at the University of Washington,
Seattle; Reed College, Portland; and
Stanford.
