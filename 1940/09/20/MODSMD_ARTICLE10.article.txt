# Wilburs to Meet Junior Transfers
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur will be host and hostess to
new transfer and graduate students
on Friday evening, October 4, at
8:00 o'clock. This reception will be
held at their home on Lagunita
Knoll.
Other events will include a trans
fer jolly-up on Friday, September 27,
at 7:30 o'clock in the evening. This
dance, to be held in the Women's
Gymnasium, is given for transfer
women and men and all other men
on the campus. Bob McNeil is in
charge of the affair.
