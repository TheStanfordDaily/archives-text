# Chaplain to Write Religion Textbook
## 
### 
Harper and Brothers have asked
Dr. David E. Trueblood, University
chaplain and professor of philosophy
of religion, to prepare a textbook
combining philosophy and religion.
The book will be used in introduc
tion courses of the two subjects.
Plans call for it to be off the press
and ready for classroom use a year
from this fall.
