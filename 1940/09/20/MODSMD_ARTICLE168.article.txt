Saturday, September 21
Men's residences open—Branner, Toyon and Sequoia.
9:00 a.m. Aptitude test for lower and upper division students in Me
morial Hall.

8:00 a.m. All women's residences open—Union Unit and Lagunita Court.
11:00 a.m. Regular church services in the Stanford Memorial Church.
6:15 p.m. First meal served in women's residences. Dean Yost welcomes
transfers of Lagunita including Casa Ventura.
10:30 p.m. Women's residences close in accordance with women's social
regulations.
