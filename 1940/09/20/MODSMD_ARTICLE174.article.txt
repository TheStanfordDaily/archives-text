# Time Schedules To Be I'm Put on Sale
## 
### 
In order to facilitate students in
finding their courses Fall Quarter,
Time Schedules will go on sale on
the main floor of the Administration
Building on Registration Day.
The schedules, which tell the days,
hours, and places of the various
courses, sell for ten cents. They are
now on sale in the cashier's office
on the second floor of the Admin
istration Building.
