# Card File Proves Successful in '39
## 
### 
In order to enable freshmen to
break into Farm activities the so
called "card file" was established in
spring quarter, 1938, was first used
at the beginning of the last school
year, and, due to its success will be
used again this year.
The file is placed in the A.S.S.U.
office and is handled by an activities
co-ordinator appointed by Ex Com
mittee, the student legislative body.
Potential committee heads and
workers will sign up in this file in
order to have their names always on
call. Class officers and student body
officers often refer to this file in
choosing their workers.
