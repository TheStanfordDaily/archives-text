# Summer Quarter Registration Falls
## 
### 
Total registration of 866 students
was recorded for Summer Quarter,
according to Miss Miriam Remele,
editorial secretary in the Registrar's
office. This represents a decline of
63 from Summer Quarter 1939's 929
enrollees.
Of these, 564 were men and 302
were women. Tally for the previous
summer was 584 men and 345
women.
