# At Last a Crosby Wins But It Ain't Bing. Bud
## 
### 
Bill Crosby won the 14th annual
Port Stockton singles championship
by upsetting favored Bill Canning
of the University of California 6-8,
6-1, 6-3. George Spencer and Glenn
Hippensteil also from California,
waltzed off with the doubles crown
defeating Harold Demaret and Jack
Gurley 9-7, 6-3.
