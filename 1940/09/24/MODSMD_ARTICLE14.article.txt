# Excom to Appoint Committee Heads
## 
### 
With a full schedule of activities
slated for the coming quarter, Stan
ford's Executive Committee meets
tomorrow night for its first session
of the school year. According to
Student Body President Bruce Jes
sup. the committee will make sev
eral appointments to the Student Aid
and other committees, to the Union
Board of Governors, and to the
Speakers' Bureau.
Most important position to fill will
be that of the Stanford Fund Drive
head to replace Grey Bryan, who
will not return to school this year.
The committee will start plans for
the annual Big Game dance which
was started last year.
