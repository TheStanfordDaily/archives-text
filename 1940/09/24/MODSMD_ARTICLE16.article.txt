# Student Rates Cut For 'Aida' Matinee
## 
### 
A special student matinee per
formance of "Aida" will be given
in San Francisco's War Memorial
Opera House on Friday, November
1. at 1:30 o'clock, according to Miss
F. N. Miles, secretary of the Divi
sion of Music.
The performance will star Eliza
beth Rethberg, Ezio Pinza. and
George Stinson. Tickets range from
75 cents to $2.50 in price, and seats
may be reserved in the Division of
| Music.
