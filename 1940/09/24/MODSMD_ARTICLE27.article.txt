# Wilburs to Receive Saturday Evening
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur's annual reception for new
lower division students will be hold
Saturday evening, September 28. The
reception will be informal and will
start at 8 o'clock.
A similar reception will be hold
for new upper division and graduate
students Friday evening, October 4,
also at 8 o'clock.
