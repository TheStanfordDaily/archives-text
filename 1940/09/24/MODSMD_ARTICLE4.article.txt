# Stanford to Rally Thursday Evening
## 
### 
First rally of the year will be held
in the basketball pavilion Thurs
day evening at 7:30 o'clock. The
rooting section will be an all-Uni
versity get-together to boost the
season's opener with U.S.F.
Rooters will see the first Shaugh
nessy-coached Stanford team meet
the Dons Saturday in Kezar Sta
dium. This is the first time in five
years that an Indian varsity has
met U.S.F.
The rally will be completed in
time for the transfer jolly-up which
is scheduled for 8:30 o'clock. Rally
Committee will meet tomorrow
night in the Women's Clubhouse at
7:30 o'clock.
