# WOMEN'S POOL OPEN
## 
### 
The women's gymnasium pool is
open to employees of the Univer
sity at noon hours until October 31,
according to Mrs. Fenncr of the
hygiene and physical education de
partment for women. The fee will
be $1.50. The pool is also open to
women students for recreational
swimming daily, Monday through
Friday, at 4 o'clock.
