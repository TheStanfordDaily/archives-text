# All-U Assembly Tomorrow at 10
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbuf
will address students and members
of the faculty at an all-University
Assembly on Thursday morning,
September 2fi, at 10 o'clock in the
Memorial Ilall Auditorium. This
announcement was released today
by the Committee on Public Ex
ercises.
All 10 o'clock classes will be
dismissed for this assembly. It
will begin promptly at 10:10 o'clock
and will conclude in time for 11
o'clock classes.
Faculty members will sit on the
reserved stage of the auditorium.
Admission to this reserved section
will be through the west side en
trance and the Green Room.
