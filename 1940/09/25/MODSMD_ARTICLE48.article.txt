# Cougars Base Hope On Trojan Slow Start
## 
### 
PULLMAN. Wash., Sept. 24 (UP).
—Coach Orin E. (Babe) Hollingbery
today hoped for kicking and block
ing improvement before his Wash
ington State College gridders meet
Southern Cal Saturday. Thirty-six
Cougars entrained for the South,
basing their hopes on the Trojans'
tendency to start slowly.
