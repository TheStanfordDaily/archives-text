# Organist to Give Afternoon Recital
## 
### 
This afternoon at 4:15 o'clock
Herbert. B. Nanney, Stanford assist
ant organist, will present an organ
recital in the Memorial Church. He
will present a program of idealized
dance music.
The program will include "Toc
cata and Fugue in D Minor," "My
Heart Is Filled with Longing," Bach;
"Piece Heroique," Cesar Franck;
two pieces from "Mountain Sketch
es," Joseph W. Clokey; "Allegretto,"
Haydn-Nanney; and "Toccata" from
thp "Fifth Symphony." Charles
Marie Widor.
