# MeCormick Named Majority Leader
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Sept. 25 (UP).
Rep. John W. McCormack, 48-year
old Massachusetts New Dealer and
strong third-term advocate, was
named majority leader of the House
tonight at a Democratic caucus.

McCormack replaces Rep. Sam
Rayburn of Texas, who was elevated
to the speakership after the death of
Rep. William B. Bankhead.
