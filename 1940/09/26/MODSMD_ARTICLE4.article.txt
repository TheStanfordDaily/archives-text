# Enrollment Rises To Total of 4223
## 
### 
Late registrations added 43 per
sons to the Farm's diminished en
rollment figures, bringing the total
to date to 4,223. This is a decrease of
32 compared to last year's figure of
4,255 for the first day of late regis
tration.

Of those now enrolled 2904 are
men, and 1319 women. In other
words, 31 men and 12 women regis
tered late. . Since late registration
continues until October 15 there may
be a slight increase in total figures.

While total number of students
decreased, there are 30 more women

last year, while the men are 62 less.
This leaves the ratio of men to
women 2.201.
