# Wilburs at Home To New Students
## 
### 
President and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur will be at home, informally,
to new Lower Division students,
at the President's house on Lagu
nita Knoll tomorrow at 8 o'clock.
President and Mrs. Wilbur will
be at home, informally, to new
Upper Division and new graduate
students at their home on Fri
day, Oct. 4, at S o'clock. Music
will be furnished for the new stu
dents by the Sebring brothers.
