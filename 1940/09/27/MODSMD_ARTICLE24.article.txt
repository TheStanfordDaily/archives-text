# PLAN NAVAL RESERVE CAREERS
## 
### 

TWO STANFORDITES who plan to become officers in the United
States Naval Reserve are Bruce Jessup (left), A.S.S.U. president, and
Edward "Swede" Johannessen. Card football player. Jessup spent a
month aboard a training ship this fall and plans to finish the four
month course next summer at Northwestern University. Johannessen
will leave Stanford this fall for his training.
