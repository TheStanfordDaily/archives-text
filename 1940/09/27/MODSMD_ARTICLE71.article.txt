# Rifleman, Too
## 
### 

THIS PICTURE of Wilhclm Jakob
Muhlenhroich was found by San
Francisco police in the luggage of
the confessed kidnapper of little
Marc de Tristan, Jr. It shows Muh
lenhroich in the uniform of a sol
dier in the German Army in the
last war, when he had the knuckle
of his left thumb shot off. He is a
crack shot.
