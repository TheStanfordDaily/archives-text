# Officer Missing
## 
### 

ARMY AND CIVILIAN authori
ties have been pushing a wide
spread search for this officer, LI.
Col. Layson E. Atkins of the Corps
of Engineers, who disappeared on
Sept. 17 from San Francisco's Let
terman Hospital. His last assign
ment was as District Engineer of
Seattle, where he was in charge of
river and harbor improvements in
the Northwest and Alaska.
