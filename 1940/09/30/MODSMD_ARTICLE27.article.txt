# Military Society To Open Meetings
## 
### 
The first meeting of Stanford
Scabbard and Blade, honorary mili
tary society, will be Wednesday
evening at 7:15 o'clock at R.O.T.C.
headquartex-s. All members are
urged to attend.
Plans for the coming year will be
discussed, and the unit plans to an
nounce at this meeting its intention
to offer aid to the regular R.O.T.C.
units in their work this year, as
well as to co-operate fully with the
national defense program.
The unit will again award medals
to outstanding basic students, and
will again sponsor a military ball
this year. Plans for pledging and
initiation of new members will also
be discussed.
