# B.A.C. Sells 970 Big Game Tickets
## 
### 
Ticket sales for the U.S.C. game
here Oct. 30 and the Big Game at
Berkeley will resume again today
from eight o'clock to five in the
B.A.C. Approximately 970 tickets
were sold for each game yesterday.
The sale closes tomorrow.
Each student may buy one root
er's ticket for the S.C. game at $1.10
and six additional tickets at $2.20
each. For the Cal game students
may purchase a rooter's ticket at
$1.65 and six additional ones at
$4.40 each. Student body cards are
required. l
