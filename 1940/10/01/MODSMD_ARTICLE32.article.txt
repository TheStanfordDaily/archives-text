# Unseeded Frosh Eliminates Alloo
## ADMINISTRATOR DIES 
### 
NEW LONDON, Conn., Sept. 30
(UP). —Col. Francis Clark Harring
ton, 53, Works Progress Adminis
trator, died early tonight of com
plications following an operation a
week ago at Lawrence and Memorial
Hospital.

BERKELEY, Sept. 30 (UP). —
George Spencer, an unseeded
youngster from Ontario, Cal., today
scored the first major upset in the
Pacific Coast Tennis Championships
when he eliminated fifth-seeded Ed
die Alloo of Berkeley.
Spencer, a University of Califor
nia freshman, upset Alloo in a sec
ond round match, 4-6, 6-1, 9-7.
Other favorites generally ad
vanced. Bobby Riggs of Chicago
defeated Chip Cheney, Berkeley
6-1, 6-3.
Helen Jacobs of Berkeley, favor
ite in the women's division, elimi
nated Daphne Buckell of Oakland,
