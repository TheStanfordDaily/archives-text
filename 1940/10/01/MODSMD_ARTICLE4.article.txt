# U.S.N.R. to Enroll Candidates Tonight
## 
### 
The recruiting division of the
United States Naval Reserve, headed
by Arthur C. Adams, U.S.N., will ex
amine and enroll candidates tonight
at its headquarters in the old Roble
gymnasium from 7 to 9 o'clock.
Candidates must be between the
ages of 17 and 35. Those 17 and 21
must have ihe consent of their par
ents and at least one parent must be
with the candidate when the con
sent is signed. Credit for military
