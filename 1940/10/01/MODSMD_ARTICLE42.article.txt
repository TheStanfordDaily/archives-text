# Badminton Club To Hold Meeting
## 
### 
The Badminton Club will hold its
first meeting of the year tomorrow
night, Oct. 1, from 7 to 9 o'clock in
the Women's Gymnasium.
Everyone interested in badminton
is invited to attend the meeting. In
addition to the business meeting,
members will have the opportunity
of playing at this first meeting. Dues
will be collected, so the cabinet
urges every prospective new or old
member to be prepared.
Throughout the autumn quarter
the club will meet from 7 to 9
o'clock every Tuesday and Thursday
evening.
