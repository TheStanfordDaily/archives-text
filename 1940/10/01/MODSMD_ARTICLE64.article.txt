# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
ALL "Y" MEMBERS interested in
working on the "Y" committee
for the Red Cross and the British
Relief should go to the "Y" offices
any afternoon of this week. Knit
ting and sewing of bandages' will
be the chief activities.
"Y" CABINET MEMBERS will meet
tomorrow afternoon at 4 o'clock
in the "Y" rooms.
THE KLAHOYA CLUB will hold
its first meeting in the Women's
Clubhouse at noon today
WOMEN'S TENNIS CLUB tryouts
will be held today, tomorrow, and
Thursday. Tryoutees are to meet
in Room 465 of the Women's Gym,
by appointment only.
ORCIIESIS meets tonight at 7:15 in
the Dance Studio.
ANY FRESHMEN who have not
returned their Strong Tests must
turn them in today at the A.S.S.U.
office, if they wish to have them
corrected.
FIRST REGULAR DEBATE MEET
ING will be held Friday at 4
o'clock in Room 200S in the base
ment of the English corner. All
regular and freshman debaters
will please attend.
THE BADMINTON CLUB will have
its first regular meeting of the
year tonight at 7 o'clock in the
Women's Gym. Anyone interested
in playing badminton is invited
to attend.
STANFORD RIFLE AND PISTOL
CLUB will meet both tonight and
tomorrow night at 7:30 o'clock in

the range below the Administra
tion Building.
INTERCLUB COUNCIL meets at 8
o'clock tonight at El Capitan. All
members are asked to attend.
