# Speakers' Bureau Calls for Sign-ups
## 
### 
All students interested in public
speaking arc urged to report to
George Jackson, head of the Speak
ers' Bureau, it was announced yes
terday.
The Bureau is a student organiza
tion which supplies speakers for
local school and club meetings. The
training is of special value to all
speech and drama majors. Last year
the bureau had the biggest season
since its founding, and President
Jackson is hoping for an even bet
ter turn-out this year.
