# DRAMA TICKETS SELL
## 
### 
Mr. Barker of the drama depart
ment has announced that more than
half of the season tickets available
have been already sold. The buy
ers are mostly Palo Alto people and
Mr. Barker wants to announce that
these tickets are especially for the
benefit of students, hence the re
duced price. He urges students to
take advantage of the opportunity
now. Season tickets are $2 for eight
shows, or 25 cents a show, less than
