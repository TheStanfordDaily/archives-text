HAHN TENNIS TOURNAMENT—
All those interested in this novice
tourney sign on bulletin board in
Men's Gym.
BADMINTON CLUB will meet to
night from 7 to 9 o'clock.
INTRAMURAL SIGN - UPS for
football and volleyball close to
day at 6 o'clock.
CONCERT SERIES tryout meeting
in the Concert Series office across
from Barber Shop, Old Union, to
day at 4:30.
RALLY COMMITTEE tryouts in
Room 13, Old Union, 1 to 4 today
and tomorrow.
SOCIAL SERVICE GROUP of the
Y.W.C.A. will meet at 4:15 to
morrow in the Women's Club
house.
"Y" MEMBERSHIP DRIVE WORK
ERS must turn in their kits to
morrow. All listed girls should
be contacted today.
ANY GIRL interested in knitting or
sewing bandages for the Red Cross
and British War Relief should
go to the "Y" office any afternoon,
where yarn and bandages will be
supplied.
DEBATE MEETING this afternoon
at 4 in Room 200 S. Those debat
ers and freshmen who missed the
first meeting may sign up today.
FRESHMEN AND OTHERS inter
ested in trying out for positions
on the Debate Manager's staff are
asked to get in touch with Bob
Hammett at the debate office up
stairs in the Old Union today or
tomorrow from 10 to 11.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANI
ZATION will meet tonight at
7:30 in the First Christian Church,
Palo Alto. Cars will be at Roble
and Encina at 7:15.
ELECTION BOARD meets today in
Room 13, Old Union, at 5 o'clock.
WOMEN'S CLUBHOUSE EXECU
TIVE BOARD meets at 4:30 to
day in Women's Clubhouse.
CAST AND CHORUS of "Of Thee
I Sing" will meet in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock tomor
row evening.
RALLY COMMITTEE TRYOUTS
for freshmen and all sophomores
will be from 1 to 4 o'clock today
and Friday in Room 13 of Old
Union.
POMPONS are on sale from 1 to 3
o clock today and all afternoon
Friday at the A.S.S.U. office for
25 cents.

from Barber Shop, Old Union, to
day at 4:30.
RALLY COMMITTEE tryouts in
Room 13, Old Union, 1 to 4 today
and tomorrow.
SOCIAL SERVICE GROUP of the
Y.W.C.A. will meet at 4:15 to
morrow in the Women's Club
house.
"Y" MEMBERSHIP DRIVE WORK
ERS must turn in their kits to
morrow. All listed girls should
be contacted today.
ANY GIRL interested in knitting or
sewing bandages for the Red Cross
and British War Relief should
go to the "Y" office any afternoon,
where yarn and bandages will be
supplied.
DEBATE MEETING this afternoon
at 4 in Room 200 S. Those debat
ers and freshmen who missed the
first meeting may sign up today.
FRESHMEN AND OTHERS inter
ested in trying out for positions
on the Debate Manager's staff are
asked to get in touch with Bob
Hammett at the debate office up
stairs in the Old Union today or
tomorrow from 10 to 11.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANI
ZATION will meet tonight at
7:30 in the First Christian Church,
Palo Alto. Cars will be at Roble
and Encina at 7:15.
ELECTION BOARD meets today in
Room 13, Old Union, at 5 o'clock.
WOMEN'S CLUBHOUSE EXECU
TIVE BOARD meets at 4:30 to
day in Women's Clubhouse.
CAST AND CHORUS of "Of Thee
I Sing" will meet in the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30 o'clock tomor
row evening.

for freshmen and all sophomores
will be from 1 to 4 o'clock today
and Friday in Room 13 of Old
Union.

25 cents.
