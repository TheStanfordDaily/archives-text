# France Confiscates Refugees' Goods

The Official Journal today published
ordinances confiscating the property
of 11 prominent persons who fled
France just before the armistice.
They included Genevieve Tabouis,
Andre Geraud (Pertinax), Emile
Bure and Elie Bois, journalists;
Pierre Cot, former Radical leader
and Minister of Air; Baron Maurice
de Rothschild, Leon Stern, Maurice
Stern, and David Weill, all million
aires; Edouard Jonas, an antique
dealer; Henri de Kerrilis, General
de Gaulle's Canadian representative.
