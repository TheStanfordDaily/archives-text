# Dr. Shapiro Offers Courses in Classics
## 
### 
Stanford's Classics Department
yesterday announced the addition
of Dr. Schapiro's course in Hebrew
Christian Traditions to the fall cur
riculum. One unit of credit will be
given, the class meeting every
Tuesday from 5 until 6 o'clock.
Elementary Arabic will be con
ducted by Dr. Schapiro every Tues
day from 4 until 5 o'clock. Both
courses are open to all students.
Largest library in the world is
the Library of Congress at Wash
ington, D.C. It contains 1,421,285
maps and pictures, and 5,828,126
printed books and pamphlets.
