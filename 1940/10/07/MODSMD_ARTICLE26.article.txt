# ABERDEEN MAN KILLED
## 
### 
SEATTLE. Oct. 6 (UP).—John J.
Pedersen, 50 - year - old Aberdeen
longshoreman, was killed in traffic
! today while walking near the scene
; of a similar fatality last night.
