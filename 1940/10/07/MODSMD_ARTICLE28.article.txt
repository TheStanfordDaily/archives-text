# HUNTER INJURES SELF
## 
### 
PORT TOWNSEND, Wash., Oct. 6
(UP). —Louis Hansen, 20, shot him
self through the right hand and left
arm today while leaning on his rifle
during a deer-hunting excursion.
The accident occurred on Coyle
Peninsula.
