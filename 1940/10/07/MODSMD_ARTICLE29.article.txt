# HUNTER SHOOTS SON
## 
### 
SHELTON, Wash., Oct. 6 (UP).—
A father shot his son to death today
and another hunter was fatally
wounded by a bullet which pierced
the brilliant red hat he had worn
for safety on the first day of the
deer season.
