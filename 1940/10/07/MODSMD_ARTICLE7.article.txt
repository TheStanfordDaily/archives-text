# Roosevelt Group Will Rally Today
## 
### 
Stanfordites, either student or
faculty members, interested in form
ing a club supporting President
Roosevelt for re-eleetion will meet
at 5 o'clock today in the Nitery,
according to Sylvia Berry, provi
sional head of the Roosevelt-for-
President group.
"In accordance with the wishes of
the Administration, we will prob
ably affiliate our group with the
Young Democrats, a group already
organized on campus," Miss Berry
declared yesterday.
Expressing a desire to debate with
Willkie supporters on issues per
taining to the coming presidential
election, the Roosevelt club was
organized in an effort to rally Dem
acratic support on the campus.
