# Caribbean Defense Reported Secure
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 7 (UP).—
Admiral William D. Leahy, retired
Chief of Naval Operations, now
serving as Governor of Puerto Rico,
said today the Caribbean area, in
cluding the Panama Canal, "will be
practically secure against, attack"
when current defense measures
there are completed.
Leahy, here to report to President
Roosevelt on defense and internal
conditions in the island, said essen
tial defense preparations could be
completed "within a few months."
