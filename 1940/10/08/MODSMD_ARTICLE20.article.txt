# Classics Department Offers New Course
## 
### 
The Classics Department is offer
ing this quarter a course in Hebrew
Christian Traditions given by Dr.
Shapiro. The class will meet every
Tuesday from 5 until 6 o'clock, and
one unit of credit will be given. Dr.
Shapiro will also conduct a course
in Elementary Arabic on Tuesdays
from 4 until 5 o'clock. Both these
courses are open to all students.
