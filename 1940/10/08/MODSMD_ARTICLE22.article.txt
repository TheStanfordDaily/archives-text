# NEWEST SUB FOR U.S. FLEET
## 
### 

THE UNITED STATES submarine "Tuna," newest addition to Uncle
Sam's undersea Heet, is shown as it was launched at California's Mare
Island Navy Yard. The suh. which cost six mililon dollars and weighs
1,475 tons, was sponsored by Mrs. Wilhelm Lee Freidell, wife of the
rear admiral who commands the U. S. submarine Heet.
