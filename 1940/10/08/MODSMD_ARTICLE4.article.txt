# Dinner to Close 'Y' Member Drive
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. will end its fall
membership drive with a dinner to
night. from 6 until 7:30 o'clock on the
second floor of the Women's Club
house. One hundred and thirty res
ervations have already been made at
50 cents apiece.
Widtiey Watson is in charge of
planning and organization, with
Mary Leonard taking care of the
decorations.
Among the guests of honor will
be Miss Mary Yost, dean of women;
Miss Sims, director of Roble Hall;
and the Y.W.C.A. Board of Direc
tors. New members and present of
ficials of the Y will round out the
number expected to attend.
Miss Davidson, new executive sec
retary of the organization, and
Martha Phillips, president, are ex
pected to give short talks. Other en
tertainment scheduled will include
readings and group singing .under
the direction of Joan Wilson.
