# ROG KELLY COPS TITLE
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 6 (UP). —
Roger Kelly of Lakeside Sunday won
the Southern California "champion
of champions" golf tournament
with a score of 71-74—145. The tour
ney was for champions of their home
courses.
David Starr Jordan was just 40
years of age when he was chosen to
"personally conduct" the University
as its first president.
