# Wilbur to Preside At Faculty Dinner
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
preside at a joint Stanford faculty
trustee dinner to be held in San
Francisco Oct. 17 at the Stanford
Nurses Residence, it was announced
today.
The guests of honor will be
George A. Ditz, Morgan A. Gunst,
Paul H. Davis, and Dr. Russel V.
Lee. Following the dinner, these
members of the Stanford family will
discuss the affairs and problems of
the University.

Washington Square, where Fifth
Avenue begins in New York, was
once the potters field.
