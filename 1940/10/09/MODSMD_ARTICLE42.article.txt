# WHEN OLD FRIENDS GET TOGETHER
## 
### 

AMOS ALONZO STAGG, left, who was judged "too old" to coach by the University of Chicago when they
let him go a half-decade ago, returned to the scene of many triumphs last week-end when his College of
the Pacific team played Notre Dame at South Bend. He is shown Avith Mrs. Knute Rockne (center) and
Elmer Layden, Notre Dame coach.
