# Boggs to Discuss Tariff Question
## 
### 
Professor Theodore H. Boggs will
address the Foreign Trade and
Service Club tonight at its first fall
meeting in the West Memorial Li
brary at 7:30 o'clock.
His topic will be ' The Tariff Issue
in the Present Campaign." The
meeting will be open to all students,
and the talk will be followed by a
group discussion.
