# Editor to Renew Radio Stamp Quiz
## 
### 
Stamp collectors' attention!
Sidney E. Goldie, stamp editor of
the San Francisco Examiner, an
nounces the renewal of the Stamp
Quiz program on KSFO, Sunday
from 5:15 to 5:45 o'clock.
Last year students from Cal and
Stanford competed on the program
and Mr. Goldie is interested in hav
ing Stanford continue its participa
tion. Each week first and second
prizes are given. First prize is a
1941 Scott Album; second, a 1941
Scott catalogue. Finals will be held
in May.
