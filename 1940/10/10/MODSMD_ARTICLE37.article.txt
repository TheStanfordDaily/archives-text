# BEARS TAKE COLONELS
## 
### 
NEWARK. N.J., Oct. 9 (UP).
The Newark Bears captured the
little World Series tonight by de
feating the Louisville Colonels, 6-1.
It was the third triumph in four
scries for the Bears, who finished
two and a half games behind the
pennant winning Rochester Club in
the regular International League
season and disposed of Jersey City
• "id Baltimore in the playoffs.
