# Gallery to Reopen After Alterations
## 
### 
The Thomas Welton Stanford
Gallery of Art. closed for altera
tions since summer quarter, will
reopen Oct. 15. The alterations,
which include enlargement and
renovation of two rooms and re
hanging of many pictures, was nec
essary to accommodate several gifts
recently received.
