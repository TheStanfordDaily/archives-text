# Cardinals Appoint Four Sophomores
## 
### 
Cardinals at its regular meeting
Wednesday night appointed four
sophomores, two of them transfers
new to the campus this quarter, to
fill the Group 2 vacancies existing
on Rally Committee.
Named to the one available wom
an's position was Barbara Horner
Hawthorne, who headed the fresh
man dance committee and served on
the executive committee at Welles
ley last year. John Phillips, Encina,
transferred from Fresno State where
he was a member of the rally com
mittee and several other activities.
The other two men's vacancies
were filled by Carl Livingston,
Breakers, and George Tolman, El
Toro, both active in freshman class
affairs last year.
