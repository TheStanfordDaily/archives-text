# Church Groups Plan for Sunday
## CHAPLAIN ORGANIST WILL HOLD SERVICES 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, chaplain
of the Univei'sity, will conduct di
vine worship and sermon at the
Memorial Chapel at 11 o'clock Sun
day.
A program of organ music based
on hymn tunes will be presented
Sunday afternoon at 4 o'clock in
the Chapel by Dr. Warren D. Allen,
University organist. All are invited
to attend.
