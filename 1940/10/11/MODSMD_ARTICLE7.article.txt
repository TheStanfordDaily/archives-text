# ARMY CALLS NURSES
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Oct. 10 (UP).
—The American Red Cross tonight
announced 127 nurses in the Ninth
Corps Area will be called to active
duty with the U.S. Army Nurses
Corps by Dec. 15, with another 280
to be called by next July.
