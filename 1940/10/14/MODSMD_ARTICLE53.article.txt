# Mrs. Roosevelt Visits James in Los Angeles
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 13 (UP).—
Mrs. Eleanor Roosevelt, wife of thi-
President, arrived aboard an air
liner at Burbank's Union Air Ter
minal today for a farewell vLit with
her eldest son, James, before he
reports for duty in San Diego as a
Marine captain. "I am making this
trip just to see my children," Mrs.
Roosevelt said as she declined to
discuss politics.
