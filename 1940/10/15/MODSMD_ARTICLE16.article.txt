# Cardinals to Hold S.C. Game Dance
## 
### 
In accord with numerous student
requests, Cardinals yesterday an
nounced plans for an all-University
dance to be given October 26, in the
Women's Gym from 9 to 12 o'clock
after the S.C.-Stanford game.
According to Graham Barbey,
dance chairman, the dance will be
informal, the bids going at $1 per
couple. These bids will go on sale
next Monday in the A.S.S.U. office.
Charlie Travis and his orchestra, a
10-man and a girl aggregation, will
provide the music.
"I hope everybody can make this
dance," Barbey said. '"Cardinals is
giving this S.C. dance in response to
student demands. The bigger the
crowd the better."
