# Historical Essay Contest Planned
## 
### 
Max Savclle, associate professor of
history and head of the Western Civ
department, announced yesterday
that the essay contest usually spon
sored by the Colonial Dames Society
will again be held this year. The
contest, which closes on March 15,
is open to all Stanford students and
victory carries with it a $100 cash
prize.
"While we do not wish to limit the
scope of the student in choosing a
topic for the essay, it is our desire
to pay particular attention to papers
dealing with the formation of what
we call "the American ideal," said
Mr. Savelle.
Generally, the essay should be on
the colonial period of American his
tory, according to Mr, Savelle.
