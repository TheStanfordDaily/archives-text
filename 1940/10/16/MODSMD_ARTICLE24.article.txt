# Saturday Deadline Set for NYA Cards
## 
### 
All October NYA time cards are
due at the Accounting Office in the
Ad Building by 10 o'clock Saturday
morning. Students who do not want
readjustments made on NYA as
signments may call for new ones
Saturday or Monday. Those desiring
changes are asked to come to the
Student Employment Office Tues
day, October 22.
