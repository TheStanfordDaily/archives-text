service fraternity, holds open
meeting at 7:30 o'clock in the
Nitery.

Glassman will speak on "Migra
tory Problems."

at 4 o'clock in Room 84 today.

ternoon at 5 o'clock at the dance

tend.

tonight at 7:15 o'clock at the
