# Chappie Presents New Issue Today
## 
### 
Members of the Chappie editorial
staff were busy yesterday after
noon, frantically preparing a "spe
cial gift number" to be ready to go
on sale today. This "gift" consists
of a strip of film from a real
movie, especially procured to com
plete Stanford's own edition of
"College Movie Mirror."
The extra-large, 36-page edition
will go on sale this morning at, 8
o'clock at the Post Office, the En
gineering Corner, and the English
Corner.
