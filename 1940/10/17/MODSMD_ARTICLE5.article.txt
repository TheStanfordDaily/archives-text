# Rooters Need Tickets For U.C.L.A. Game
## 
### 
All students who arc attending
the U.C.L.A. game in Los Angeles
Nov. 2 must secure rooters tickets
at the B.A.C. today or tomorrow
from 8:30 to 5 o'clock. The tickets
are free, but students must have
them to get into the game.
Students may also purchase 8
additional tickets for this game at
$2.50 each. Student body cards
must be shown.
