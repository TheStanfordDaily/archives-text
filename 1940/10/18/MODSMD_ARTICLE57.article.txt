# Fencers to Clash With Italian Club
## 
### 
Stanford's fencing team goes after
its second win in three tries tonight
when it meets the Young Italian
Club of San Francisco at 7:30 at
the club's gymnasium, 678 Green
Street.

Members of the team are: Dick
Meyerhoff, Marlin Nelson, John
Boyd, Robert Helliwell, and Bob
Schultz.

Interfraternity Council announces
the pledging of James S. Hirst, '43,
R. R. Taylor, '43, James B. Kessler,
'43, Denslow Green, '43, Gordon Mag
nuson, '42, and John R. Broughton,
'42, to Alpha Tau Omega.
