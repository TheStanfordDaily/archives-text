# Hollingbery Drills Cougars on Defense
## 
### 
PULLMAN, Wash., Oct. 17 (UP).
—Coach Orin E. (Babe) Hollingbery
put his Washington State College
football club through its first scrim
mage of the week today in prepara
tion for the Stanford clash Saturday.
He continued his drive to improve
Cougar defensive tactics.
