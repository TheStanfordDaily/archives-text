# 'Y' to Entertain Transfers at Tea
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. transfer discussion
group, under the direction of Max
ine Jones, last, week made plans for
a "get-together" tea for all transfer
students.
The tea will take place next Thurs
day afternoon at 4 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse, and will be
supervised by Barbara At wood and
Hope Branum.
In order to acquaint transfer
students with the Y.W.C.A., the
campus, the "Y" cabinet, and with
each other, meetings and discussion
groups will continue to meet regu
larly during the semester.
