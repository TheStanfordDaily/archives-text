# Jones' Nebraska Team Reflects Army Spirit
## 
### 
LINCOLN. Neb. (UP). —Major
Lawrence McCeney Jones resigned
four years ago from active army
service, but the influence of his
long military career still prevails
on the gridiron.
He's Biff Jones to the football do
main, Nebraska's head coach and
athletic director, whose hard-bitten
grid teams continue to reflect the
guidance of a military man.
