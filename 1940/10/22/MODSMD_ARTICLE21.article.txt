# S.C. Game Dance Ducats Go on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets for the S.C. game dance
are now on sale in the A.S.S.U.
office. The dance, sponsored by
Cardinals in response to student de
mands, will take place in the Wom
en's Gym Saturday from 9 to 12
o'clock after the S.C.-Stanford game.

The dance will be informal, with
(he bids going at $1.00 per couple.
Charlie Travis and his orchesra, a
10-man-and-a-girl aggregation, will
provide the music.
