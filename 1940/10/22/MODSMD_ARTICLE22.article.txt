# Nazi War Reports Forbidden at Night
## 
### 
BERLIN, Oct. 21 (UP).-Gcrman
authorities today banned the send
ing of news of military nature from
Germany between the hours of 11
p.m. and 6 a.m. daily (4 p.m. to 11
p.m. E.S.T.).
The prohibition applies to Ger
man as well as rorelgn news organi
zations and to the radio. It applies to
all news of any military connection
whatever, favorable or unfavorable
Ito Germany, including reports of
j German air operations over Britain.
