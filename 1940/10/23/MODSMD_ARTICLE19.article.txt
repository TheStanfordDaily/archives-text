# Store to Sponsor Talk on Mowers
## 
### 
J. Gregory Conway, expert on
flower arrangement and symbolism,
will give a lecture and demonstra
tion of his craft today at 2:30
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
Presented under the sponsorship of
the Stanford Bookstore, the ad
dress is open to anyone who would
like to attend.
The only Occidental ever to re
ceive a diploma from Japan's Ohara
School for Moribana and Nagierc
styles in arrangement, Mr. Conway
will discuss the classic Orient. With
this he will compare the freer styles
of the West.
At present a teacher at the Uni
versity of Southern California, he
has carried on research in sym
bolism and paid particular attention
to the place of flowers in modern
architecture and decoration.
