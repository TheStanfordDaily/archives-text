# ALL PART OF GREAT LOTTERY
## 
### 

LIEUTENANT COLONEL Charles R. Morris, 65, retired, looks at the
blindfolds he applied to the eyes of President Wilson and other offi
cials who drew numbers in the draft lottery of the last war. Washing
ton officials are considering his offer to perform the same service in
the 1940 draft. Shown on the table is the ladle which stirred the cap
sules and slips which some of them contained.
