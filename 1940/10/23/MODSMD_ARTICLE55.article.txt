# Dance Tickets Sell In Student Office
## 
### 
Tickets for the S.C. game dance,
which will be held Saturday after
the S.C.-Stanford game, can be ob
tained at the price of $1 per couple
in the A.S.S.U. office.
Dancing will be from 9 to 12
o'clock and dress will be informal.
Charlie Travis . and his orchestra,
which consists of ten men and a girl
vocalist, will furnish the music.
