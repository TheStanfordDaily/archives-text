# Almack Explains Evils of Drinking
## 
### 
Dr. John C. Almack, professor of
education at Stanford University,
has written an article recently ap
pearing in the magazine "Signs of
the Times," published weekly at
Mountain View, entitled, "Should
College Students Drink?"
Dr. Almack points out in the ar
ticle that alcohol does not lead to
intellectual or artistic achievement,
training for a profession, victory on
the athletic Held, or social success
such as marriage into a good family.
The Pacific Press Publishing As
sociation headed by William L. Mil
ler. which publishes ihe magazine,
will send a circular letter to Stan
ford students urging them not to
drink.
