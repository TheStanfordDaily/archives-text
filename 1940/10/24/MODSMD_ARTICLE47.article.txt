# Younce's Absence Weakens Beavers
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Ore., Oct. 23 (UP).
Thirty-three Oregon State College
football players entrained for Los
Angeles today with Coach Lon Sti
ner worried about what injuries
will do to the right side of the Or
ange line.
Bob Saunders and Leonard
Younce, right tackle and right
guard, were out of the starting line-*
up for the game with the U.C.L.A.
Bruins Saturday.
Seventy kinds of seaweed are
eaten in Hawaii.
