# Forum to Debate F.D.R. vs. Willkie
## 
### 
Newly organized Stanford Forum
will launch its campus career with a
spirited debate on Willkie vs. Roose
velt by speakers from the Young
Republicans and Young Democrats
clubs next Wednesday, Oct. 30. The
open meeting will be held in Memo
rial Auditorium at 8 o'clock.
Four or five speakers will speak
for Willkie and the same number
for Roosevelt. Bruce Jessup, student
body president, will be guest mod
erator.
Purpose of the embryonic Forum,
which has replaced the old Stanford
Political Union, is to afford town
hall type discussions, which are
provided by no other campus or
ganization.
