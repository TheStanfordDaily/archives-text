# Organist Presents Recital Today, 4:15
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will present a program of
music in the Memorial Church this
afternoon at 4:15 o'clock. Selec
tions to be played include: "La Mar
tinella" by Heinrich Isaac,
in D Minor" by Bach, "Canon in B
Minor" by Robert Schumann, and
the "Grand piece symphonique" by
Cesar Franck.
The choir rehearsal will begin to
night at 7 o'clock in the choir room,
in order that students may attend
the rally afterward.
