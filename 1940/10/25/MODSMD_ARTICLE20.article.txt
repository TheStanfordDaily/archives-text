# Baritone to Sing At Organ Recital
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood will con
duct divine worship at 11 o'clock
Sunday morning at the Memorial
Church. Assisting Dr. Warren D.
Allen, University organist, will be
Joseph James, young negro baritone.
A program of music by Handel will
be played during the service.
At 4 o'clock Sunday afternoon
there will be an organ recital by
Dr. Allen featuring Mr. James.
James will sing: "The Worship of
God in Nature" by Beethoven, and
"Goin' Home" by Dvorak.
