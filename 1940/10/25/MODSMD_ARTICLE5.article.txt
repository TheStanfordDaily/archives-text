# Stanford Invited To Dance at Cal
## 
### 
Stanford students are invited to
attend a dance at the University of
California tomorrow night. It will
be held in the men's gymnasium on
the Cal campus at 9 o'clock.
Lionel Hampton and his orchestra
will furnish the music. Prices are 20
cents for women. .10 cents for men,
or -40 cents per couple, with a Stan
ford student body card.
