# RACING COLUMNIST DIES
## 
### 
TORONTO, Ont., Oct. 24 (UP).—
| Douglas D. R. Eppes, 63, racing col
umnist for the Toronto Globe and
i Mail, died today of a heart attack,
i Eppes served in the Boer War and
j was a major in the World War.
