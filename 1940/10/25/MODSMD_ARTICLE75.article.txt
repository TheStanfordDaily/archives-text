# TRINKETS NEED NOVELTY
## 
### 
Novelty is the keynote of Ameri
can designing, and compacts are the
most novel of all our designs. New
est. and most realistic is the palette
compact, shaped like an artist's
palette and decorated with brushes
and paint dabs. Because it is lac
quered over, it won't scratch or
scuff. Carry it to give that artistic
touch.
Paradise fox is the most recent
concoction of novelty-mad furriers.
It consists of black fox with long
white monkey fur grafted on it.
