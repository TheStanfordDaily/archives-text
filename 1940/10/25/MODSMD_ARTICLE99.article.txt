# COMPACTS DOUBLE SIZE
## 
### 
Designers' sense of proportion is
undergoing an effective change this
year. Compacts and cigarette cases
of twice the normal size are seen in
the very best purses; and the new
two-dollar lipsticks by both Lelong
and Matchiabelli are conversation
provoking as well as practical.

A sleeker version of the faithful
evening sweater has appeared this
season—a long-waisted "shirt of
mail" woven of untarnishable gilt
Lastex, with bracelet-length sleeves.
