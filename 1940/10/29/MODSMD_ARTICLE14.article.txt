# Pre-Med Society To Hear Tainter
## 
### 
Dr. Maurice Tainter, professor of
pharmacology at the Stanford
School of Medicine, will speak on
"Quackery in Medicine" at tomor
row evening's meeting of the Stan
ford Pre-Medical Society.
Ed Nielsen, president of the club,
extends an invitation to anyone who
is interested. Refreshments will be
served. The meeting will be held
in the Women's Clubhouse at 7:30.
