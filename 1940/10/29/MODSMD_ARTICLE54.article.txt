INTERNATIONAL Students Club
will meet tomorrow evening at
the home of Mrs. E. J. Thomas,
365 Lincoln Avenue, Palo Alto.
Members will meet at 7:30 o'clock
in the Nitery. Transportation will
be provided. All foreign or Amer
ican students interested are in
vited.
PANHELLENIC COUNCIL will
meet today at 4 o'clock in the
Old Union.
Y.W.C.A. CABINET meeting today
in the Y rooms of the Women's
Clubhouse at 4 o'clock.
IMPORTANT SCABBARI) AND
BLADE meeting today at 7:15
in Room 538.
QUAD PICTURES of Lagunita
Casas Naranja, Magnolia, Olivo,
and Ventura will be taken today.
FRESHMAN DEBATERS will meet
today at 4 o'clock in the cloak
room of the Memorial Theater.
SENIOR VARSITY DEBATERS
will meet today at 4 o'clock -in
Room 200 S.
PRE-MEI) CLUB meets tomorrow
evening at 7:30 in the Women's
Clubhouse. Dr. Tainter will speak
on "Quackery in Medicine."
BRIDGE SECTION of the Stanford
• Dames meets today at 2 o'clock at
home of Mrs. Graham Stuart, 548
Gerona Road.
KLAHOYA CLUB will meet for
lunch tomorrow at the Women's
Clubhouse at 12 o'clock.
STANFORD RIFLE AND PISTOL
CLUB meets tonight at 8 o'clock
in range below the Administra
tion Building.

Tuesday; Wednesday partly cloudy;

southerly wind shifting to westerly
and diminishing.
