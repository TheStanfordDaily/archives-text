# Law Association Court to Be Held
## 
### 
The Stanford Law Association will
hold a semifinal moot court session,
which will discuss the question of
the legality oi the recent destroyer
naval base deal between the United
States and Great Britain, tomorrow
evening at 8:30 o'clock in the Edu
cation Auditorium. Maurice Dowling
of the Superior Court will preside,
assisted by Mr. James Price and
Professor Robert Cathcart of Stan
ford.
