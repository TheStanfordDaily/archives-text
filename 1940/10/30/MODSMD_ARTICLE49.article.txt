# Bulletin Board

GLEE CLUB meets this afternoon
at 4 o'clock in Room 84. Dues
are payable.
STANFORD WRITERS CLUB will
hold its first fall meeting Thurs
day night at 7:30 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. Formerly
the English Club, it is open to all
students interested.
RIFLE AND PISTOL CLUB meets
in the range in the basement of
the Administration Building to
night at 7:30 o'clock.
QUAD PICTURES of Roble corri
dors Al, A 2, A3, 2 and 3 Center
will be taken today.
INTERFAITH COUNCIL will meet
in the "Y" rooms at 1 o'clock this
afternoon.
MUSICAL MEDITATION will be
held this afternoon in Memorial
Chapel from 4:15 to 4:30 o'clock.
PRE-MED CLUB meets tonight at
7:30 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse. Dr. Tainter will dis
cuss "Quacker in Medicine."
INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS
CLUB will hold a meeting today
for all students who wish to par
ticipate in the club conference.
Anyone interested is invited to
meet in the Women's Clubhouse at
4 o'clock this afternoon.

night at 7:30 o'clock in Room
200 S.

ties will be held this afternoon
from 2 to 6 o'clock in the lobby
of Memorial Hall. M
