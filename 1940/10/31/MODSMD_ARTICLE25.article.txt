# Seniors to Hold First Luncheon
## 
### 
The first Senior Luncheon will be
held today at the Women's Club
house from 12 to 1 o'clock. Tickets
are selling at 35 cents each and
there is a sales representative in
each living group.
Three committee heads have been
appointed to take care of ticket
sales. They are: Lagunita, Audrey
Steele; Union, Isabelle Gore; Row,
Tish Fleming. Head of the arrange
ment committee is Shirley Okell.
Lois Whitmore, head of the Senior
Luncheon, urged that since this is
the first affair of its kind this quar
ter, all senior women are expected
to come.
