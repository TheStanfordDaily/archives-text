# Roble Formal Bid Sale Ends Today
## 
### 
Today is the last opportunity to
purchase bids to the Roble Formal
on Nov. 9. They will be sold from
1 to 2:30 and from 4 to 6 o'clock
in the Roble lobby at $1.50 apiece.
Carvel Craig's orchestra is sched
uled to supply music for the semi
formal affair which will be held in
the Women's Gymnasium from 9 to
12 o'clock.
