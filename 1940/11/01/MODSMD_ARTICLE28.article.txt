# EARLY APOLOGIZES
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 31 (UP).—
Stephen T. Early, President Roose
velt's secretary, tonight offered
apologies to a New York City police
man who had charged he was in
jured by Early when Mr. Roose
velt's party left New York last
Monday night.
