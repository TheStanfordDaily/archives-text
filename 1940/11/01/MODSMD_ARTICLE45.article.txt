# O.S.C. HEADS SOUTH
## 
### 
CORVALLIS, Ore., Oct. 31 (UP).
—The Oregon State College football
team entrained today for California
where they meet the University of
California Bears in the Berkeley
stadium Saturday. The Orangemen
spent their last practice session here
drilling against Bear passing and
running plays.
