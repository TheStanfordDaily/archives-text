# Ski Club to Show Technicolor Movie
## 
### 
Stanford's Ski Club will present a
technicolor movie, "Ski Maniac," at
8 o'clock Thursday night in the Ed
ucation auditorium. A 25-cent ad
-1 mission will be charged for the
one-hour show.
The movies, which were taken by
Dr. Frank Howard, show scenes of
ski racing at Sun Valley, the Sugar
Bowl, and Mt. Lassen as well as
water skiing shots.
