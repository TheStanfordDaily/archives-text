# Flares to Keynote Washington Rally
## 
### 
Stanford slogans in colored
railroad dares will keynote the
special Washington game all-Uni
versity rally scheduled for Frost
Memorial Amphitheater Thursday
night at 7:15 o'clock, according to
plans announced yesterday by
Rally Committee Chairman Hill
Kimball.
The rally, first ever held in the
Amphitheater, is being arranged
by Paul Yamamoto and Bob
Snoddy. co-chairmen. They prom
ise plenty of torchlights, songs,
yells, and entertainment.
