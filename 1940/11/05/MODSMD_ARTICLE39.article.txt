# Booters Conquer Bruins; Fifth Win
## 
### 
Coming back from Los Angeles
with a win over,the U.C.L.A. Bruins,
j their fifth straight of the season, the
Stanford soccer team settled down
to a week of hard work for their
coming battle with the powerful
Spartans of San Jose State.
The squad came out of the hard
Bruin game without injury. Every
man that made the trip south got in
the game.
The Spartans are top contenders
for the conference soccer crown,
boasting of a win over the potent
University of California Bears.
