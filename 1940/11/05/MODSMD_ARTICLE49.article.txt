DELTA SIGMA RHO debate tour
ney will be held today at 4:30 in
English corner basement. Partici
pants and members are requested
to attend.
FROSII DEBATERS will meet to
day at 4:00 o'clock in the cloak
room of Memorial Theater.
SENIOR VARSITY DEBATERS will
meet today at 4 o'clock in Room
200 S.
ALL SENIORS must have their pic
tures taken before Wednesday if
they are to appear in the Quad.
FROSII can file petitions for presi
dent and secretary-treasurer of
class at Law Steps from 8 a.m.
Tuesday to 6 p.m. Wednesday.
ALL ENTRANTS in the Hahn Ten
nis Tournament should have their
third round and fourth round
matches completed by this week.
ABSENTEE VOTERS may get their
ballots notarized free of charge
in the Nitery today during voting
hours.
