# Freshmen to File Election Petitions
## 
### 
Freshmen may file petitions for
the class offices of president and
secretary-treasurer. The nomination
blanks will be available at the Law
Steps from 8 a.m. today to 6 p.m.
tomorrow.
Petitions must have 15 signatures
of those who are eligible to vote. In
order to be eligible either to vote or
for nomination, students can have
only up 1 o and including 29 units.
The election will be held a week
from tomorrow.
