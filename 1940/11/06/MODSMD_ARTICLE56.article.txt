# Nominations Open For Class of '44
## 
### 
Nominations for frosh officers
must be in by 6 o'clock this eve
ning, according to Betty Moses,
chairman of the election board. Po
sitions open are president and sec
retary-treasurer, the term to last
for the duration of the quarter.
Petitions may be obtained at the
Law Steps, must be signed by 1")
freshmen, and returned to the box
provided at the same place.
In order to either sign a petition
or run for an office the freshman
must have true freshman standing,
that is, must have a total of not
more than 29 completed units.
