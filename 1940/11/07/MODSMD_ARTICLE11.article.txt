# Symphony Opens Concert Series
## 
### 
The Musical Association of San
Francisco will again present the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra,
Pierre Monteux, conductor, in a
Friday afternoon and a Saturday
evening series of twelve programs
each.
Students wishing tickets for the
first programs on Friday afternoon,
December 6, and Saturday evening,
December 7, should make their res
ervations before Friday of this week
with Miss Frankic Miles, secretary
of the Division of Music, in the
vestry of the Memorial Church.
Season tickets for the afternoon
concerts may be purchased for $6.60
and for the Saturday evening scries
tickets are $f). Single tickets for
the Friday afternoon series may be
purchased for 55 cents and for Sat
urday evening they will be 75
cents.
