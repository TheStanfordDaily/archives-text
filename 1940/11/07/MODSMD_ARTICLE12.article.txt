# Stage Crew Head Schedules Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Gaieties stage
crew will be held tomorrow from 2
to 6 o'clock in the Memorial Hall
shop, Pete Wangenheim, crew direc
tor, announced yesterday.
He emphasized the need for those
with a talent for scenic painting,
good letterwork .and "stage art in
general." The tryouts are open to
all students.
Dance tryouts, as previously an
nounced. will be held from 2 to 4
o'clock today in the lobby of Me
morial Hall, according to Adolfo
Arias, dance director.
