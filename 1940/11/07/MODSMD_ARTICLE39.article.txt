# Card, Husky Grads Plan Supper Dance
## 
### 
Peace will reign between the
Washington and Stanford alumni
Friday evening as they combine to
put on a supper dance at the Sir
Francis Drake Hotel to celebrate the
big grid battle which is to come off
the next day.
Coaches Jimmy Phelan aVid Clark
Shaughnessy and the graduate
managers of both teams will speak,
with Hal Burdick, of the radio pro
gram "Night Editor," acting as mas
ter of ceremonies.
' The celebration starts at 6 o'clock
and will last indefinitely. The
price is only $1.25, and Stanford
students are cordially invited.
