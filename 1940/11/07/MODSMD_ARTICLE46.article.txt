# BRUINS DETERMINED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Nov. 6 (UP)—De
termined to break a streak of bad
luck that has cost them six straight
games, the U.C.L.A. Bruins tonight
entrained for Eugene, where they
will meet the University of Oregon
Webfoots.
Forty yearlings are still stabled
at the Saratoga Race Track, which
has been boarded up against on
coming snows.
