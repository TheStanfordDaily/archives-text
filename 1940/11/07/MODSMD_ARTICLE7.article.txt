# Files Interviews To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Card-file interviews of inde
pendent, non-organization and ofT
campus students will be held in
the Nitery today and tomorrow
from 1 to 2 o'clock, according to
Jim Mack, men's card file chair
man.
This procedure will enable all
men who arc not living in halls
or fraternities to become eligible
for campus activities which will
stem from the card file, Mack
added. Meanwhile, registration
cards are pouring in from all
campus living groups, he said.
Actual filing process has begun
and the completed job will be
presented in working order to Ex
Committee next Tuesday, he
stated.
