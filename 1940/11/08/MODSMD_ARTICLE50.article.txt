# Yankees Dominate Latin Net Matches
## 
### 
BUENOS AIRES, Nov. 7 (UP .—
American players continued to
dominate play in the Argentine ten
nis championships today.
Frank Guernsey of New Jersey
defeated Spaniard Jaime Durall Pu
jol, 5-7, 6-0. 6-3, 6-0 and later
learned with Don McNeill of Okla
homa City to whip an Argentine
duo. Alberto Zappa and Roberto
Cluttenbuck, 6-2, 6-0, 9-7.
