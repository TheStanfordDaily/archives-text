rUITION NOTE APPLICATIONS
and renewals for all students due
next week.
LAST ACTIVITY CARDS inter
views will be held today from 1 to
2 o'clock in the Nitery.
STANFORD FACULTY WOMEN'S
CLUB will meet Monday at 3
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
The all-Beethoven program is in
charge of Mr. Ferenc Molnar.
STAGE CREW TRYOUTS for the
Gaieties will be held from 2 to 6
o'clock in the Memorial Hall work
shop.
THE MEDICAL APTITUDE TEST
will be given today at 2 o'clock in
Room 214 for the only time this
year. All students who intend
to apply to any medical school by
Fall, 1941, must take it today, un
less they've already taken it.
TRYOUTS FOR "ST. JOAN," by
G. B. Shaw, Monday, Tuesday at 8
o'clock.
