# Hoover Addresses Students, Faculty
## 
### 
The Hon. Herbert Hoover, Stan
ford '95 and only living ex-President
of the United States, addressed the
student body and faculty in Memo
rial Auditorium Friday morning in
an off-the-record speech.
Mr. Hoover, Stanford's second old
est trustee, indicated through Presi
dent Ray Lyman Wilbur, who intro
duced him, that he might continue
to speak to the student body from
time to time.
Mr. Hoover, who has been a
trustee 28 years, spoke before the
students and faculty last spring for
the first time in several years.
