# Asilomar to Hold Co-ed Conference
## 
### 
Applications are being received
for scholarships to the coeducational
conference to be held during the
Christmas holidays at Asilomar.
Students from colleges and univer
sities in California, Arizona, New
Mexico, and Hawaii will attend.
Campus leaders who are inter
ested in the work of this conference
include Dr. Elton Trueblood, Mrs.
Harry Rathbun, John Moore, chair
man of Interfaith Council, Mrs.
Edith OfTenheiser, Miss Patricia Da
vidson, Mr. Donald Baldwin, Mr.
James Davidson. Miss Frances Dek
ker is general chairman of the Stan
ford delegation.
