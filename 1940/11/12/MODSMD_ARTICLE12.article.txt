# Mayer Goes East For SDX Conclave
## 
### 
Delegates to represent the Stan
ford chapter of Sigma Delta Chi at
the annual national convention to
be held Thursday through Sunday,
Jerry Mayer, president of the local
chapter of the national professional
journalism society, left this morning
for Des Moines, la.
The last Sigma Delta Chi national
convention was held on the Coast,
with Stanford acting as the host.
