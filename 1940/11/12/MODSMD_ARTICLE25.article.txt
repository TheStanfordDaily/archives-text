# Tuition Notes Due For Next Quarter
## 
### 
Tuition notes for winter quarter
must be renewed by both men and
women students by this Friday.
Those wishing to take out notes
must also apply at this time.
Women should apply in the Dean
of Women's office. Undergraduate
men should call at the Dean of
Men's office between the hours of
1:30 and 5 - o'clock, and graduate
men should see Mr. Jacobsen in
Room 170 of the Administration
Building at the same hours.
