# Minister to Speak To Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Reverend Augustine Jones, Stan
ford graduate and minister of the
Congregational Church at Palo Alto,
will speak at the Luncheon Club to
morrow noon in the Nitery on the
subject, "South of Hong Kong."
The speech, which is based on his
travels through the Far East, deals
especially with the relationship of
Japan to Singapore and the Dutch
East Indies. Luncheon Club, which
is open to all students, meets every
Wednesday noon.
