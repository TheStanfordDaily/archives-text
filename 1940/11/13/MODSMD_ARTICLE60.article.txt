# Bookstore Names Officers for Year
## 
### 
The stockholders of the Stanford
Bookstore held their annual meet
ing last night. The new officers for
the board of directors were elected.
They are the following members of
the faculty:
Bernard F. Haley, president;
John W. Dodds, vice-president;
Harry J. Rathbun, secretary. Other
members of the board are David E.
Faville, who is the retiring presi
dent, and J. Hugh Jackson.
