# HAWAIIAN DRAFT
## 
### 
HONOLULU, T.H., Nov. 12 (UP).
—Hawaii's selective service lottery
was under way today, with terri
torial employees drawing a total of
6,500 draft number capsules from a
revolving wire container to deter
mine the order in which the islands'
registered men will be called for
military training.
