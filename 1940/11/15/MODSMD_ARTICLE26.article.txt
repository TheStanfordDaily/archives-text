# 'Y' to Recognize Members Sunday
## 
### 
New members of the Y.W.C.A.
will be given official recognition this
Sunday at an induction ceremony
which will immediately follow the
11 o'clock service in Memorial
Church.
Dr. Trueblood will conduct the
regular service with Martha Phil
lips, "Y" secretary, assisting, and
the induction itself will be handled
by Miss Mary Yost, Dr. Trueblood.
and the Y.W.C.A. cabinet.
Among the guests who will join
the "Y" members in the section re
served for them in <the chapel will
be Dr. and Mrs. Ray Lyman Wil
bur, Coach and Mrs. Clark
Shaughnessy, Mrs. Marion Reith,
national secretary of the "Y," and
Mrs. Clare Harris, head of the San
Jose State College Y.W.C.A.
Members of Interfaith Council
and the Advisory Board will also
be represented, as well as many
Palo Alto friends of the "Y."
It is alleged that when the Duke
of Windsor was asked what he
thought of civilization, he answered,
"It's a good idea; someone ought to
start it."
