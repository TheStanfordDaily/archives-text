# Low Named Head Of Service Group
## 
### 
Kirby Low was elected president
of Alpha Phi Omega, national serv
ice fraternity, and Bob Browder
vice-president Wednesday night.
Other officers chosen were Allen
Nadeau, secretary; Steve Kline,
treasurer; Edgar Freeman, historian;
and Rene Gouldner, sergeant-at
arms. It was decided that no meet
ing would be held Thanksgiving
week, but that the following meet
ing, Nov. 27, would be open.
Bud Boeringer, All-American cen
ter at Notre Dame 14 years ago, keeps
in as good condition as any of the
University of Detroit linemen he
coaches.
