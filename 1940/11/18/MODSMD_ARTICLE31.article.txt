# WOOL CONVENTION ENDS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 17 (UP).
—The California Wool Growers As
sociation closed its 80th annual con
vention today after delegates heard
Eugene Ackerman of New York, an
authority on wool, warn against the
growing competition from "syn
thetic fibers."

NEW YORK, Nov. 17 (UP).—The
' New York Giants today scored a 7-3
victory over the favored Green Bay
i Packers in a National League foot
j ball game before 28,262 fans in the
i Polo Grounds.
