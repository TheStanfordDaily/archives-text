# ALL-AMERICAN FULLBACK?
## 
### 

STANFORD FANS hope to put the Indians' Norm Standlee in the
tailback spot of this year's All-American football team, but the Chief
has plenty of competition from other parts of the country. The Smith's
contribution to the ranks of 1940 football greats is '"Jarrin' Jawn"
Kimbrough (above), pride of Texas A. & M.
