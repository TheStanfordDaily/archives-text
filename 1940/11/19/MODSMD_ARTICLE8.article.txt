# Union to Serve Dinner on Holiday
## 
### 
Students staying on the Farm
over the Thanksgiving vacation will
not have to miss the traditional din
ner, according to Miss Christine
Ricker, director of dining halls.
A full course Thanksgiving dinner
will be served Thursday from 12:15
to 2 o'clock at. the Union dining
room. It will then be closed until
Sunday, when it will be open as
usual. The Cellar will operate on
its regular schedule with possible
earlier evening closings.
All the residents of Union, Roblc
and Lagunita halls will have Thanks
giving dinner together. It will be
held at 1 o'clock in the Lagunita
dining room.
