# Stanford Man Joins Library of Congress
## 
### 
Dr. Luther H. Evans, who took
his Ph.D. in the Department of Po
litical Science at Stanford in 1927
and was an instructor in citizenship
here from 1924 to 1927, has been
made chief assistant librarian of the
Library of Congress. Dr. Evans was
formerly national director of the
Historical Records Survey, which
has compiled reports of the existing
governmental records in all of the
states, counties, and cities in the
United States.
