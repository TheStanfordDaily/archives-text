# STRIKE POSTPONED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 24 (UP).—
Officials of the American Federation
oi Labor Carpenters' Union today
announced a five-day postponement
of a scheduled strike at 10 Los An
geles harbor shipyards engaged in
production of $40,000,000 worth of
small ships for the Navy.
