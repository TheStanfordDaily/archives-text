# Vocom Announces Results of Tests
## 
### 
Men students who took the Strong
Vocational Interest Test under the
special offer of the Men's Vocational
Committee two weeks ago should
make their appointments for inter
views immediately at the Dean of
Men's office, Vocom Chairman Frank
Mallory announced yesterday.
If any of the students who took
the test forgot to turn it in at the
prescribed time, he should get in
touch with Assistant Dean of Men
Stanley L. Sharp and arrange to
have the test corrected at the regu
lar rate.
