# MURRAY ELECTED C.I.O. HEAD
## 
### 

JOHN L. LEWIS, founder of and for five years president of the Com
mittee for Industrial Organization, congratulates Philip Murray, who
was elected to succeed him as CIO head last week. Lewis, who re
signed the presidency to keep an election pledge, retained the presi
dency of the United Mine Workers' union, a powerful CIO ailiilate
and the largest union in the country.
