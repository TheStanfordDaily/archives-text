# Pre-reg Required For Home Course
## 
### 
Those who expect to enroll for
the course, "Personal Problems in
Modern Society," to be given by
Professor J. V. Berreman winter
quarter, are asked to pre-register in
the office of the School of Social Sci
ences, Room 24, before the Christ
mas holidays.
The course deals principally with
such personal problems as occupa
tional adjustment, mate selections,
marital adjustment, and the role of
the individual in family and com
munity life. It will consist of a se
ries of lectures on these subjects by
especially qualified persons both on
the faculty and outside.
The course carries five units of
University credit and will meet, at
11 o'clock daily. It is open to all
senior men and women and to jun
iors under special circumstances who
have received the consent of the
instructor.
