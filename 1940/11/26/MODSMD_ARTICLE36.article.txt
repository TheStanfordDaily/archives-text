# Blackjack Holovak Leads Eagle Win
## 
### 
Opposition to Boston College ac
cepting a major bowl bid was shat
tered Saturday when Chick
O'Rourke and "Blackjack" Holovak
led the Eagles to a 33-7 win over
Auburn, one of the South's better
teams.
Despite the absence of their star
end, Gene Goodreault, who missed
his first game in three years, the
Eagle line tore through the Poly
forwards with the big center Glad
chuck leading the way.
