# International Club To Meet at Dinner
## 
### 
A special Spanish dinner will be
the feature of this week's meeting of
the International Students Club to
morrow evening, President Dick
Hornidge has announced. The din
ner will be held at the home of
Mrs. E. J. Thomas, 365 Lincoln Ave
nue, Palo Alto.
All members of the club who will
attend the dinner are asked to notify
either Hornidge, Shirley Wilson, or
Janet Snelling. As usual, members
will meet at the Nitery beforehand
and transporting to the Thomas
home will be provided.
LAREDO, Tex., Nov. 25 (UP). —
Vice-President-elect Henry A. Wal
lace. for a time feared stranded by
south Texas floods, arrived in La
redo at 6 p.m. en route to Mexico
City.
