# Chinese Graduate Founds Newspaper
## 
### 
Charles Leong, Stanford graduate
in journalism, has started publica
tion of the first Chinese newspaper
in the United States which is printed
entirely in English.
It is a weekly publication called
the California Chinese Press, and
will cariy the news services of both
the United Press and NEA syndi
cate. Long, who was editor of the
San Jose State College Daily and the
first Chinese on the Pacific Coast to
edit a college daily paper, is co
editor and publisher of the paper.
