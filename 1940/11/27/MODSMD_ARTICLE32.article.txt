# NAVY WANTS FAIR SITE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 26 (UP).
—The U.S. Navy informed the City
of San Francisco late today it wants
\ to acquire all of Treasure Island in
San Francisco Bay. site of the 1939-
40 Golden Gate International Ex
i position, for "naval purposes."
