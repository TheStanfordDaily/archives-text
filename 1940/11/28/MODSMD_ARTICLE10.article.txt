# Positions Offered On 'Mademoiselle'
## 
### 
Positions are open for a freshman
and a sophomore woman from Stan
ford on Ihe Mademoiselle College
Board, which puts out the college
number of this magazine in August
every year. Representatives send in
periodic reports of college fads and
fashions, the best receiving special
recognition.
Anyone who wishes to apply
should write to Mademoiselle, 1 East
57th Street, New York City. Fur
ther information may be obtained
from Harva Sprager, senior repre
sentative, or Claire Burtchaell, jun
ior representative.
