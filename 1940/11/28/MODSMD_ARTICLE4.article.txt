# Allen to Give Organ Recital Tomorrow
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present organ recitals in
Memorial Church today at 4:15
o'clock and Sunday at 4 o'clock.
The; program will include "Fan
tasia Cormatica," by Jan Sweelinck,
"Grand Fantasia," by Lemmens,
"Variations de Concert," by Bonnet,
"Fantaisic, op. 101," by St. Sacns,
and "Toccata in F Major," by C. M.
Widor.
