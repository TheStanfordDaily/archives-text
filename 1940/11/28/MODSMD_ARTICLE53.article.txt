# Jacobsen to Talk In S.F. Broadcast
## 
### 
"Faults of the Earth" will be the
subject on which Professor Lydik S.
Jacobsen of Stanford's Department
of Mechanical Engineering, will
speak in a broadcast from San Fran
cisco, Dec. 6. He will tell of earth
quake research, how scientists pre
dict earthquake cycles, the meaning
of faults, and finally what Stanford
research is doing in building earth
quake-proof buildings.
On Nov. 29 Dr. F. W. Went and
Dr. James Bonner of the California
Institute of Technology will discuss
the discovery, development, and
present-day use of Vitamin B. This
broadcast will be sponsored by the
Department of Plant Nutrition.
