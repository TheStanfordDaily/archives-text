# Y.W.C.A. Frosh Elect New Chairman Today
## 
### 
The freshman discussion group of
the Y.W.C.A. will meet today for the
last time this quarter, according to
Jeanne Chambers, chairman. At this
meeting a new presiding officer will
be elected from among the present
25 members. From now on, the
group will be independent of super
vision. Tea will be served at today's
meeting, to be held in the "Y" rooms
at 4:30 o'clock.
