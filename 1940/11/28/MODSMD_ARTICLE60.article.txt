# SHIP TIE-UP PERSISTS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 27 (UP). I
—Efforts to settle the eight-weeks I
tie-up of 47 coastwise steam schoon
ers were delayed today as neither
the Sailors Union of the Pacific nor
the Shipowners Association at
tempted to resume negotiations.
