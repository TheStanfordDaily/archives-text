# Reich Freighters To Leave Mexico
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Nov. 28 (UP).—
Telephone advices from Tampico
said tonight that the German freight
ers Idarwald and Rhein were pre
paring for another chish to sea
shortly.
The crews were aboard the ships
and the first maneuvers to leave had
been made.
