# REV ELSON TO CONDUCT
## 
### 
The Rev. Edward L. R. Elson, D.D..
Minister of the First Presbyterian
Church of La Jolla, California, will
deliver the sermon on Sunday in
Memorial Church at 11 o'clock.
Music will be provided by the
University choir, and an organ re
cital will be given at 4 o'clock by
Warren D. Allen, University Or
ganist.
