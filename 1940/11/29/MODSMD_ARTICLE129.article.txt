# Farm Debaters To Appear on Air
## 
### 
Stanford will clash with a Santa
Clara debate team Dec. 7 over radio
station KSFO at 10:30 in the morn
ing. Subject of the debate is "Re
solved, That colleges should place
greater stress upon the humanities."
Stanford will take the negative
on the question. The debate is based
upon an open forum idea, with
speakers from both sides, and a
discussion following.
This is one of a series of programs
of the radio debate league which
includes most colleges from the Bay
region.
