# Alpha Delta Sig Initiates Three
## 
### 
Three new members of Alpha
Delta Sigma, national honorary pro
fessional men's advertising society,
were initiated into the Stanford
chapter Thursday evening.
The initiates were Dan Lewis, Bill
Lane, and Jack Fitting. Entertain
ment for the program was provided
by some alumni members of the
society now with Lord and Thomas
advertising firm, who put on an
amusing advertising skit. Howard
Wiloughby, president of the San
Francisco Alpha Delta Sigma alum
ni club, spoke.
Officers of the society, recently
elected, arc: president, Warren
Mitchell; vice-president, Chuck
Phelps; and secretary, Jim Mc-
Clatchy.
