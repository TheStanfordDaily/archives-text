# New York Alums To Hear Big Game
## 
### 
New York alumni of Stanford and
California will gather to listen to
the Big Game tomorrow and cele
brate afterward at the Columbia
University Club in New York City.
A buffet supper will be served
after the game with college music
and songs in order throughout the
evening, according to an announce
ment received here. John Really,
Stanford '29, and Fred Anderson,
California '28, are in charge of the
affair.
