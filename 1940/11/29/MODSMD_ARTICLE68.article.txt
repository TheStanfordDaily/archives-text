# Shaughnessy Is 'Coach of Year'
## 
### 
Jimmy Phelan, coach of Washing
ton's Huskies, yesterday doffed his
hat in praise of Stanford's 1340 grid
outfit and its coach, Clark Shaugh
nessy.
"No coaching job in the country
this season compares with what
Shaughnessy has done at Stanford,"
Phelan said.
