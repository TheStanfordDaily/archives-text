# 'Bartered Bride' Tryouts to Be Held
## 
### 
Final preliminary tryouts for "The
Bartered Bride" will be held to
morrow evening at 7:30 o'clock in
the choir room. All those interested
on campus are urged to attend.
The Smetana folk-opera will be
presented at the end of winter quar
ter and all casting for parts will be
made from those trying out at this
time.
