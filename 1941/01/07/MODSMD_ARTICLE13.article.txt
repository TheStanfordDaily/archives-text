# Quad Staff to Sign For Working Hours
## 
### 
Sign-ups for hours on the editorial
staff of the Quad will be held today
and tomorrow between 1 and 4
o'clock. Staff members will sign for
new working hours at those times.
Any other students who have not
been on the staff in the past are
invited to come and try out for the
Quad staff. Many positions will be
open to freshmen and sophomores at
the end of the school year.
