# FIRST CARD SCORE IN BOWL
## 
### 

FIRST SCORE for Stanford in the Indians' great 21-13 win over Nebraska in the Rose Bowl New Year's
Day was made by halfback Hugh Gallarneau, shown scooting across the Cornhusker goal Vine late in the
first quarter. Number 33 is Banducci of Stanford, and number 27 is ''Butch" Luther of the Huskers.
