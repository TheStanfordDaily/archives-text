# British May Make Liners Warships
## 
### 
HONOLULU, Jan. 7 (UP).- The
Honolulu Star Bulletin said today it
had information from British sources
that the liners Awatea and Aorangi
will be withdrawn from Australian-
Canadian service and placed in mili
tary service.
The Star Bulletin said the Awa
tea was being converted into an aux
iliary armed cruiser in New Zealand
and the Aorangi will make one more
trip in passenger service and then
be converted into a hospital ship.
