# Choir to Hold First Meeting of Quarter
## 
### 
The University Choir will hold its
first meeting of the quarter tomor
row evening at 7:15 o'clock in the
choir room of the Memorial Church.
Mr. Herbert Nanney, acting organist
and director for the quarter, plans
to enlarge the choir, and invites all
members and others interested to
attend.

Mare Island Navy Yard now em
ploys more than 14,000 men.
