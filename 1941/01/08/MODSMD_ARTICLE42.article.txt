# The 						Stanford Daily SPORTS
## JERRY BRUCKER, Sports Editor BEST JOCKEYS OF THE YEAR 
### 

WINNER AND RUNNER-UP in the 1!J40 contest for the title of the
nation's outstanding jockey are Earl Dew, rigid, and Lee Taylor,
respectively. Taylor, injured in a fall, was forced out of the race on
the final day. Dew went on to win with 287 winners for the year,
defeating Taylor by one mount.
