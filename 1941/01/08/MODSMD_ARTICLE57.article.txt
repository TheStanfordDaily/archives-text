# Citrine Tells of U.S. Aid to Great Britain
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 7 (UP).—
Sir Walter Citrine, British labor
leader, said today that American
shipments of rifles after the Allied
defeat in France enabled Britain to
begin rearming again "from the
ground up."
"That is something England will
never forget," said Sir Walter.
ST. PETERSBURG, Fla., Jan. 7
(UP). —Samuel D. Hughey, 21, was
killed today when his parachute
failed to open as he jumped from a
small experimental plane which
caught fire over the municipal air
port.
