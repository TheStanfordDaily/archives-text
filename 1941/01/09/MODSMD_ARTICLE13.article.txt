# CRASH KILLS MAN
## 
### 
THE DALLES, Ore., Jan. 8 (UP).
—F. C. Mclllroy of Dee, Ore., was
killed today when his car crashed
into a Consolidated freight truck on
the icy Columbia River Highway
near here.
