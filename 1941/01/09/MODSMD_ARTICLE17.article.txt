# Assistant Organist To Give Recitals
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney, assistant or
ganist of the Memorial Church, will
present organ recitals in the chapel
today at 4:15 o'clock and Sunday
at 4 o'clock.
His first program will include
Bach's "Toccata, Adagio, and Fugue
in C Major," Godard's "Adagio Pa
thetique," Hadyn-Nanney's "Alle
gretto," and Boellmann's "Toccata
from Suite Gothique." Sunday's
recital will include Widor's "Allegro
Vivace," Reger's "Benedictus," Rach
maninoff's "Prelude in C Sharp
Minor," Bach-Grace's "Jesu, Joy of
Man's Desiring," and Lemmen's
"Fanfare."
