# Roble Speedster To Write Essay
## 
### 
All because of an aching
tooth, Patricia Marquand, Roble
woman from Pachuca, Mex.,
was sentenced to write an es
say on the dangers of speed
ing.
According to the story she
told Judge Herbert Kaufman
of San Francisco yesterday, she
was driving to a dentist when
she was clocked by an officer
driving 45 miles an hour in a
25-mile zone on the Bayshore
Highway. The judge gave her
30 days to hand in her essay.
