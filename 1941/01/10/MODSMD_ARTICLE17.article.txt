# Handbook Editor Applications Due
## 
### 
Applications for the editorship of
The Freshman Handbook, 1941-42
edition, must be in the hands of
Bruce Jessup, student body presi
dent, next Monday, according to an
nouncement made yesterday.
The job of writing and revising
the 160-page booklet which will be
mailed to all entering freshmen next
year is open to any man in the
Class of '42. It pays a salary of $50,
according to student body regula
tions. The current "Frosh Bible'
was co-edited by Wilson Cannon
and Dwight Hutchinson.
