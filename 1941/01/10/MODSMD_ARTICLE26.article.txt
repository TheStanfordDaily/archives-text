# Organist Offers Sunday Concerts
## 
### 
Organ recitals are to be given in
the Memorial Church every Thurs
day at 4:15 o'clock and every Sun
day at 4 during winter quarter.
Yesterday's concert by Herbert B.
Nanney, assistant University organ
ist, included: "Toccata, Adagio, and
Fugue in C Major" by J. S. Bach,
"Adagio Pathetique" by Godard,
"Allegrette" by Haydn-Nanney, and
"Tocacta" from Suite Gothique by
Boellman. The next concert is sched
uled for Sunday, Jan. 12, at 4.
