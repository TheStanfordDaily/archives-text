# Winter Intramural Signup Ends Today
## 
### 
Intramural* Manager Bert Pike
announces that this will be the last
day for teams to sign for winter
quarter basketball and handball
tournaments. Sign-up sheets are
posted in the gymnasium.
Handball matches will be posted
on a bracket on the gymnasium
bulletin board, while basketball
schedules will be printed in The
Daily.
