# Jones to Deliver Lecture Series
## 
### 
Delivered this year in conjunction
with the Stanford Conference on
Spiritual Leadership, the West Me
morial Lectures will be given next
week by Rufus M. Jones, emeritus
professor of philosophy at Haver
ford College.
The lectures are scheduled for
7:30 o'clock on Tuesday, Wednes
day, and Thursday evenings, Jan. 21,
22, and 23. Dr. Jones, who will
speak in Memorial Hall, has chosen
the following subjects: "The
Uniqueness of Spirit in Man,"
"Goods That Are Good without
Qualification," and "Intimations of
an Over-World."
