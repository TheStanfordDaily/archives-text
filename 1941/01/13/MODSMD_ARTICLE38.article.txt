# BETTINA AIMS AT TITLE
## 
### 
CLEVELAND. Jan. 12 (UP).—
Melio Bettina, Beacon, N.Y., belter,
attempts to regain tomorrow night
the light heavyweight title — that
part of it recognized by the National
Boxing Association—when he meets
Anton Christoforidis, Greek boxer
who punches as handsomely as he
looks.

Portugal has been an independent
tate since the twelfth century.
