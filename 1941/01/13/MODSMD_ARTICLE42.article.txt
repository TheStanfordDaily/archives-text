# Kovacs Wallops Alloo In Dixie Tennis Final
## 
### 
TAMPA, Fla, Jan. 12 (UP).—
Frank Kovacs of Oakland, Calif., to
day won the men's singles champion
ship of the 17th annual Dixie Ten
nis Tournament by beating in
straight sets Eddie Alloo, Berkeley,
Calif., who had blazed to the finals
by upsetting the defending cham
pion and the nation's No. 2 ranking
player. Kovacs won, 6-0, 6-3, 6-2.
