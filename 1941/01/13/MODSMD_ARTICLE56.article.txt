# Opera Chorus Tryouts To Be Held Tomorrow
## 
###

"The Bartered Bride" to be given in
March will be held tomorrow after
noon from 4 to 5:30 o'clock in the
Little Theater of the Memorial
Theater. Both men and women will
take part in the chorus.
