# Trojan Cage Tickets To Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Rooters tickets for the Stanford-
Southern California basketball
games in the Stanford Pavilion
January 17 and 18 will be on sale
at the B.A.C. oflire today and to
morrow. from 8:30 to 5 o'clock.
The price will be 40 cents. Gen
eral admission tickets, which will
be offered only if rooters do not
buy all the seats, will go on sale
after the rooters' sale at 75 cents.
