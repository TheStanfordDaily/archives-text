# Music Division Offers Schubert
## 
### 
The Division of Music and the
Friends of Music at Stanford will
present a concert of the music of
Franz Schubert and Robert Schu
mann, the songs will be interpreted
by Nicholas Goldschmidt, Czecho
slovakian singer and pianist.
On the Schubert portion of the re
cital, Mr. Goldschmidt will accom
pany himself and sing in German;
and in the Dichterliebe cycle of
Schumann he will be accompanied
by Dr. Herbert Jan Popper of the
Division of Music. He will use the
English translations by Matullah,
The concert is sponsored for the
benefit of all Stanford students.
