# 'Joan' Ducats Selling At Memorial Hall
## 
### 
"St. Joan," George Bernard Shaw's
modern play centering around the
famed medieval heroine, will be pre
sented at Stanford Jan. 30 and 31
and Feb. 1, Thursday, Friday and
Saturday. Tickets are now on sale.
! They may be obtained at Mr. Bar
ker's office. Room 202 in Memorial
J Hall. The price is 50 cents.
