# DUKE'S YACHT IN STORM
## 
### 
NASSAU, Bahamas, Jan. 14 (UP).
—Radio advices received here to
night said that a yacht carrying the
Duke of Windsor on a survey
through the Bahamas is unable to
land at Abaco Island because of
rough seas which have wrecked two
boats in Nassau Harbor, killing
three persons.
