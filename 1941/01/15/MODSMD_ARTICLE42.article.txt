# Union to Arrange Candlelight Tea
## 
### 
Union Club will honor Mrs. Helen
Miller, director of the Union dining
hall; Miss Patricia Davidson, new
executive secretary of the Y.W.C.A.,
and Dr. and Mrs. O. N. Anderson at
its annual Candlelight Tea on Jan. 26.
Dr. Anderson is general director of
the School of Hygiene and Physical
Education.
The tea is given by the seven
houses which make up Union Unit.
General chairman in charge of ar
rangements is Emily Johnson, Mari
posa, who will be assisted by mem
bers of the various houses. Guests
will be received in the main lobby
of Union and tea will be served from
4 to 5:30 o'clock.
