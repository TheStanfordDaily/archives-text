# Y.W.C.A. to Open Community Talks
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. is offering a new
series of presentations to women
students with the subject, "What
to Do after College in Your Com
munity" as its theme. Today's pro
gram, featuring Mrs. Hazel Robert
son, director of the Palo Alto Little
Theater as speaker, will open the
series, to be given every Thursday
from now on at 4:30 o'clock.
The "Y" plans to discuss the var
ious activities which are open for
volunteer service in large and small
cities, in rural communities, and in
towns. Today's meeting will be held
at 4:30 o'clock in the "Y" rooms.
