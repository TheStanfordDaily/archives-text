# DOERR RECUPERATING
## 
### 
EUGENE, Ore., Jan. 15 (UP).—
Bobby Doerr, second baseman of the
Boston Red Sox of the American
League, was recovering in a Eugene
hospital today after undergoing an
emergency operation for appendi
citis.
