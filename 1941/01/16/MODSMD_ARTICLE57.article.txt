# Tatum Appointed To Head Prom
## 
### 
Frank "Sandy" Tatum will be
chairman of the junior prom which
will take place some time next May.
He was selected last night at a
meeting of the Junior Class council
in the Nitery.
Tatum was selected from a group
of six students by the council,
which consisted of Peggy Perring,
Mary Menninger, .George Jackson,
and Frank Tatum.

A seven-acre site at Coloma in
El Dorado County, where gold was
discovered in California, will be
held for state park purposes by the
State Park Commission.
