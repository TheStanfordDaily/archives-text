# 'Daily' Tryoutees Hear Hutchinson
## 
### 
Seventeen prospective members of
the Daily staff, attending a tryoutee
meeting yesterday afternoon, heard
Editor Dwight Hutchinson explain
the work involved in putting out a
campus daily five days a week, and
the work required from tryoutees as
a basis for appointments to the edi
torial staff.
Neophytes will begin work imme
diately on the copy and United
Press desks of The Daily, where
they will do proof and copy read
ing, and- head writing.
The meeting was conducted by
Women's Editor Mary Etta Farrell,
who has announced that anyone in
terested in trying out for The Daily
who was unable to attend yester
day's meeting may contact her for
the duration of the week in order
to try out.
