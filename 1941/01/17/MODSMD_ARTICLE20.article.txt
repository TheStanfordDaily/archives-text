# Tryouts Continue For Yell Leaders
## 
### 
There is still time for sophomore
men to try out for yell leader posi
tions, according to Gordy Nicoll, re
tiring head cheer leader.
Tryoutees will meet again today
for training at 2 o'clock in the Pa
vilion, where they will be coached
by Joe Messina and Charley Van
Deusen, assistants.
While time still remains for try
outees to turn out, Rally Commit
tee will choose the new men some
time in the near future, and try
outees will strut their stuff at com
ing basketball games.
