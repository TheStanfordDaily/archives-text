# PRISON SUICIDE
## 
### 
PORTLAND. Ore., Jan. 16 (UP).—
Harold James Richmond, 24, an ex
convict from Folsom prison in Cali
fornia. shot and killed himself here
today after critically wounding Pa
trolman George L. Burch when the
officer attempted to question him.
