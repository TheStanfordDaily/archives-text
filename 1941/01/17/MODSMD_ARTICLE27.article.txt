# Dr. Jones to Give Sermon on Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. Rufus Jones will deliver the
sermon Sunday at 11 a.m. in Memo
rial Church. Music will be provided
by the University Choir. Herbert B.
Nanney, assistant organist, will give
a recital at 4 p.m.

MARCH FIELD, Jan. 16 (UP).—
Twenty-seven army "flying fort
resses" were en route from March
Field to Washington, D.C., tonight
to participate in an aerial demon
stration during President Roosevelt's
inauguration Jan. 20.
