# Palo Alto Theater To Open 'Circle'
## 
### 
Palo Alto Community Theater
will operi its January production,
W. Somerset Maugham's "The
Circle," Thursday night. The play,
directed by Ralph Emerson Welles
and starring Henry Cheeseman and
Suzanne Andersen, will run for
three nights.
"The Circle" is a typical English
drawing-room comedy, satirizing
the manners and morals of British
society. Reservations for all three
performances are being taken now
and may be made by communicating
with the Community Theater in
Palo Alto.
