# 'Y' Group to Hold Music Hour Today
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. offers its weekly
music appreciation hour this after
noon at 4:30 o'clock in the "Y
rooms. Everyone is invited to come,
listen to the music, and have tea.
The program will include Brahms'
First Symphony, the Overture to
Act I of 'Lohengrin by Wagner,
"Claire de .Lune" and "Afternoon
of a Faun" by Debussy, Strauss'
"Emperor Waltz," and the first move
ment of Schubert's "Unfinished Sym
phony."
