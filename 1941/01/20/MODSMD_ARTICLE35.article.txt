# STOCK MACHINES BREAK
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 19 (UP).—An
indicated breakdown in the normal
functioning of the Stock Exchange
machinery featured developments in
the nation's markets last week.

ATHENS, Jan. 19 (UP). —Greek
soldiers fighting in rain and snow
have captured a strongly fortified
region on the central Albanian front
defended by one of Italy's crack
regiments, "The Wolves of Tuscany."
a government spokesman reported
tonight.
