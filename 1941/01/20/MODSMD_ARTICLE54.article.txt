# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
ALL FKOSH BASEBALL men re
port to the Gym Monday today at
3 o'clock.
GLEE CLUB will rehearse for "Bar
tered Bride" at 7:15 tonight in the
Little Theater. Those who want to
attend the concert can leave from
the theater.
"Y" MUSIC GROUP will meet to
morrow at 4:30 o'clock in the "Y"
room of the Women's Clubhouse.
The program will feature light
popular classics. Tea will be
served at 5:30 o'clock.
ACTIVITY FILES committee will
meet in the Nitery this afternoon
at 4 o'clock.
PANHELLENIC COUNCIL will
meet today at 5 o'clock in Old J
Union.
MUSIC GROUP OF STANFORD
DAMES will meet tomorrow at
2:30 o'clock at the home of Mrs.
D. R. MacQuivey, 1101 Emerson
Street.
FOSTER COMMITTEE of the "Y"
will meet today at 4:15 o'clock in
the "Y" rooms.
QUAD PICTURES will be taken to
day of Alpha Delta Phi, Alpha
Kappa Lambda, Alpha Sigma Phi,
Alpha Tau Omega, and Beta Theta
Pi members.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE will meet
today at 1 o'clock in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
