# Writers' Group To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Writers' Club, for
merly the English Club, will meet
this evening at 7:30 o'clock in the
basement of the Women's Club
house. Plans for the publication of
The Yearbook of Stanford Writing
and for a campus-wide writing con
test with prize awards will be
discussed.
The program for the evening in
cludes readings of two of Arch
Oboler's famous radio plays. Re
freshments will be served and
members of the faculty as well as
students are welcomed.
Glen Holland, and Jean Sturte
vant, editor and business manager
of the Yearbook, respectively, will
conduct the meeting, to which new
Lower Division students who are in
terested in becoming affiliated with
a writing organization are especially
invited.
