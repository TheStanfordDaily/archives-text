# NAVY TO BUY "DIXIE"
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 20 (UP).—
The Southern Pacific Company today
announced it will sell the 445-foot
cargo-passenger ship Dixie to the
U.S. Navy for use as an auxiliary
vessel.
