# University Issues Summer Bulletin
## 
### 
The Summer Quarter Bulletin,
containing full information on ad
mission requirements, fees, and
schedules for summer quarter has
just been released by the University,
and may be obtained by writing to
the Director of the Summer Quarter.
As part of the celebration of Stan
ford's First Fifty Years, the period
of June 16-20 will be devoted to an
academic celebration, featuring a
symposium on "The University and
America's Future," and the dedica
tion of the Hoover War Library.
Several unusually interesting courses
will be offered this summer quarter
by the great scholars attracted by
the anniversary commemoration.
