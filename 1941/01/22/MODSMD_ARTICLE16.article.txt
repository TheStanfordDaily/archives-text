# Pledge Pictures Due by Friday
## 
### 
Fraternity and sorority pledges
must have their pictures taken this
week or the beginning of next week
if they are to appear in the Quad.
Other organization members who
have not yet had pictures taken may
also have theirs'taken at this time.
Students wishing these pictures
taken should make appointments
immediately at the Quad office, pref
erable for the morning, when service
is more prompt.
Those already having pictures
taken must return proofs to Room 15
of the Old Union. Room 15 will be
open from 9 to 12 o'clock and from
1 to 5 o'clock today, tomorrow, and
Friday to receive Quad proofs.
