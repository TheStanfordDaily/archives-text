# SEALS SIGN GIBSON
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Jan. 22 (UP).—
"Sad Sam" Gibson, veteran right
hander (if the San Francisco Seals,
started the signature parade for the
1941 baseball season by returning his
signed contract to President Charles
Craham today.
