# Monteux Offers Harris Symphony
## 
### 
Pierre Monteux will conduct the
San Francisco Symphony in a varied
program this Friday afternoon at
2:30 o'clock and Saturday night at
8:30 o'clock in the San Francisco
opera house.
Mr. Monteux will present the first
San Francisco performance of Roy
Harris' Symphony No. 3. Also listed
for this pair of concerts are Haydn's
"La Reine" symphony with which
the program opens, Richard Strauss'
"Thus Spake Zarathustra," and
"Dance of the Seven Veils" from the
opera "Salome."
The next pair of concerts will be
given next Friday afternoon and
Saturday night, Feb. 7 and 8, when
Sergei Rachmaninoff will be the
guest soloist, making the first of his
two guest appearances with the or
chestra.
