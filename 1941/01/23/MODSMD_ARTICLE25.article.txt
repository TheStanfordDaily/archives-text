# UPHOLDS PARTY PROMISE
## 
### 
SALEM, Ore., Jan. 22 (UP).—Sen
ator Lew Wallace, D., Portland, who
in his campaign speeches promised to
provide work for the Democratic
and republican national committee
men and committeewomen, will in
-1 induce a bill to further that aim
tomorrow.

fhc largest cellar of oak wine casks
in America, with a total capacity of
