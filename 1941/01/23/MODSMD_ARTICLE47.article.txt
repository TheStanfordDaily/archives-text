# Frosh Tracksters Open 1941 Season
## 
### 
Stanford University's freshman
track team officially opened its 1941
season at an informal meeting in
the B.A.C. building. Some 26 mem
bers of a squad of 40 were present.
Frosh Mentor Clyde Jeffrey pre
sided at the meeting . Franklin
"Pitch" Johnson, varsity coach,
gave a short talk in which he com
mented that this year's freshman
squad has potentially the best mate
rial in at least five years. He also
gave his ideas upon training and
conduct.
