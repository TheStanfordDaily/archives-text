# Government Abandons Investigation of SOS
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Jan. 26 (UP).—
The Coast Guard tonight suddenly
called off its investigation of a mys
terious distress message received last
night which was interpreted as indi
cating a ship was being shelled by
a raider about 800 miles off the
Southern California coast.
