# Russian Author To Lecture Here
## 
### 
One of Russia's foremost novel
ists and playwrights, Vladimir Nabo
kov, will conduct courses in creative
composition and modern Russian
literature during the 1941 Summer
Quarter. He will lecture in the De
partment of Slavic Languages.
Under the nom de plume of "Sir
in, Nabokov has written numerous
novels and plays. Two of his out
standing books, "Laughter in the
Dark and "Luzin's Defense" have
been translated into German,
French, and Swedish.
Nabokov is a member of a promi
nent Anglophile family of Russia
and was educated at Cambridge
University. His father was a min
ister of justice to Alexander 111 and
a member of the first Russian duma.
