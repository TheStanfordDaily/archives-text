# RUTH SUFFERS RELAPSE
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 26 (UP). —Babe
Ruth, suffering from a cold for the
past week, has returned to bed fol
lowing a conference with his per
sonal physician, Dr. Robert H. Mc-
Connell, it was reported today.
