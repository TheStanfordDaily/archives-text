# Farm Debaters Plan Radio Series
## 
### 
A new series of intercollegiate ra
dio debates has been planned by
seven northern California schools.
The last two matches will be given
as forums.
In the next to last of the series
the seven schools will meet together
in an hour discussion. Three will
be chosen to meet in the final de
bate.
Those schools participating are
Stanford, the University of Califor
nia, Santa Clara, St. Mary's, the
University of San Francisco, San
Francisco State College, and San
Jose State.
