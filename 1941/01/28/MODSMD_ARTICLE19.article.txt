# 'Y' Service Group To Meet Thursday
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. Social Service
Group will meet Thursday at 4:30
o'clock in the "Y" rooms, accord
ing to Betty Augur, chairman. Mrs.
Jackson H. Ralston, former social
service worker, will be guest
speaker. She will talk on current
legislative issues, especially those
concerning relief administration in
California and housing and slum
clearance, problems which she has
studied extensively.
