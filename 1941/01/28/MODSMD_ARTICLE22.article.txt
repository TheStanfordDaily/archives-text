# Nominators Meet In Old Union, 1-5
## 
### 
Women's Nominating Committee
will conduct its second interviewing
session today between 1 and 5 o'-
clock in the Ex Committee room in
the Old Union.
Women who are interested in be
ing interviewed but who have not
received a card are urged to come
on Thursday between 1 and 4 o'-
clock. The committee will also be in
session tomorrow from 1 to 5 o'clock.
According to Marky Nordling,
chairman of the group, those who
are going to appear before Nomi
nating Committee should inform
themselves concerning the nature of
the position they are being inter
viewed on. This education can be
easily secured by obtaining a free
copy of the A.S.S.U. constitution at
the A.S.S.U. office.
