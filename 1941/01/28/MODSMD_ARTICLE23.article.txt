# AFL Men Strike On Defense Work
## 
### 
KENT. Wash., Jan. 27 (UP).-
Sixty AFT. sheet metal workers
struck at the Northwest Metal Prod
ucts Company plant today, halting
work on national defense contracts.
The plant, located a few miles
south of Seattle, had subcontracts
for heating and air-conditioning for
Fort Lewis cantonments and 11,000
balk tanks for the navy in laying
submarine nets.
