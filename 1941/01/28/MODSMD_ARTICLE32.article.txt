# Nazi Sailors Entrain For Southwest Camp
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Jan. 27 (UP).—
Thirty-five German sailors from the
scuttled liner Columbus were
brought to the mainland from Angel
Island today and transferred to
two Southern Pacific day coaches for
the trip to a "concentration" camp
near Fort Stanton, N.M., where they
will remain for the duration of the
war.
