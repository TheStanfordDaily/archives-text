# French Ships Seen At British Base
## 
### 
JERSEY CITY. N.J., Jan. 27 (UP).
—Crew members of the American
liner President Adams today reported
the French liner He de France was
at anchor at the huge British naval
base of Singapore.
It was said there were no indica
tions the liner would be seized by
the British.
