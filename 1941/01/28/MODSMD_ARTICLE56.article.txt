# Bulletin Board

ORCHESIS meets today at 7:15
o'clock for Primitive rehearsal
and at 8 o'clock for first Pioneer
and City rehearsal.
"BARTERED BRIDE" polka rehear
sal will be held today at 4:30
o'clock in the Little Theater.
CIRCLE "S" meets at noon at the
Delta Tau Delta house. Pictures
will be taken for the Quad.

ing out for the gymnastics team
are asked to report this afternoon
at 4 o'clock in the gymnastics room
of Encina gym.

have Quad picture taken today at
2 o'clock in Union Court.

12 o'clock.

"Y" rooms.
