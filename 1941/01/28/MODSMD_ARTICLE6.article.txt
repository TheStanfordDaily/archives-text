# Harrison to Speak To Faculty Group
## 
### 
George Russell Harrison, author of
the recent best-seller, "Atoms in
Action," will speak at the Faculty
Research Club oh Wednesday eve
ning, Feb. 5.
He will also deliver a series of
four lectures to Stanford chemists
at 4 o'clock on Feb. 4, 5, 6, and 7.
