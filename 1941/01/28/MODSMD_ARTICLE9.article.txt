# Soph Class Forms New Ex Committee
## 
### 
The Sophomore Class has recently
formed an Ex Committee and
President Ralph Bjorklund and Sec
retary Joan Hill have appointed the
members who will serve in this
group. The object of this committee
is to gather new ideas and to submit
advice.
This group will manage the sopho
more dance, which is scheduled to
take place in the latter part of this
quarter, and will start plans for the
Sophomore Carnival to be held
spring quarter.
The members of the Sophomore
Ex Committee are: Joan Litchfield,
Jean Montgomery, Tommy Howard,
and George Tolman.
