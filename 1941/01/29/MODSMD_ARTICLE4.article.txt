# VACCINATION SLIPS DUE
##

Registrar's office by tomorrow, Jan.
.'5O, for all new students, including
those having permits to attend
classes, and all other students whose
certificates have expired. Failure to
file the slips at that time will result
in a fine of $2; or, if not filed by
Feb. 6. registration in the Univer
sity will be subject to cancellation
by the Committee on Public Health.
