# Nanney to Give Organ Recital
## 
### 
The Organ Recital, to be presented
this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in the
Memorial Church, will feature Her
bert B. Nanney, assistant organist.
The program will include the fol
lowing selections: Mendelssohn's
"Sonata No. 2," "Reflections" by
Horace Alden Miller, "Canon in B
Minor" from the works of Robert
Schumann, and Widor's "Toccata."
The same program will be repeat
ed next Sunday afternoon at 4
o'clock.
