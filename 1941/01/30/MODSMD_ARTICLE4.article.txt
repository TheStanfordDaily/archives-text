# SOLON DROPS DEAD
## 
### 
SALEM, Ore., Jan. 29 (UP). —
Rep. O. L. Babcock, Umatilla, drop
ped dead late today of a heart at
tack in the post office here. Bab
cock was a Republican and was serv
ing his first term from the 23rd
legislative district. He was born
Oct. 7, 1874, at Wilson, Neb.
