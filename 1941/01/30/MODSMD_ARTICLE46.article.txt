# KOVACS WINS TOURNEY
## 
### 
CORAL GABLES, Fla., Jan. 29
(UP). — Frankie Kovacs, California
tennis star, won out in the opening
rounds of the tennis tournament
here. He won from Lewis Brown
stein, 6-3, 6-4, and from Tommy
Kahler, 6-2, 6-3, while keeping up
his usual tricky game.
The Yale-Harvard rowing con
tests were begun in 1852 by six-oar
crews.
