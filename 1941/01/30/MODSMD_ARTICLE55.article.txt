will hold a special meeting to
night at 7:30 o'clock in the club
room of the airport. Members are
urged to attmid.

held at 4:30 o'clock in Hawthorne
Cottage. Refreshments will be
served. All those interested are
invited.

be held from 3 to 5 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. Please be on
time.

Pete Whittlesey, president.
