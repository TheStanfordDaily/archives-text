# Vaccination Slips Must Be in Today
## 
### 
The Registrar's office announces
that today is the last day for filing
of vaccination slips for all new stu
dents, including those having per
mits to attend classes, and all other
students whose certificates have ex
pired.
A fine of $2 will be imposed on all
those who neglect to file the slips;
and, if they are not filed by Feb. 6,
registration in the University will
be subject to cancellation by the
Committee on Public Health.
