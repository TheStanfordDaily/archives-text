# Nom Committee Ends Interviews

### 
Women's Nominating Committee
will meet today from 1 to 4 o'clock
in the Ex Committee room for the
last time, to interview candidates
for the positions of sophomore wom

vacant council posts.
Anyone interested in running for
these positions and who has not as
yet been interviewed is asked to

Feb. 3, and the elections will be
on Feb. 11.
