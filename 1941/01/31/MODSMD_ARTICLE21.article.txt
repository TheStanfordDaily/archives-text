# Lagunita Formal To Be February 14
## 
### 
Hearts in waltz time and cupids
with arrows will feature the Lagu
nita Winter Formal on Feb. 14 in
honor of St. Valentine.
Beryl Robison, head for the af
fair, has oppointed the following
committee heads: Chris Moody, dec
orations; Bernice May, arrange
ments; Mae Lazarus, refreshments;
Beverly Rank, patrons; Jean Butler,
ticket sales; and Rosalie Slater, gen
eral hostess. Gene Englander's or
chestra, popular among Stanford
students, has been obtained for the
dance by Caryl Parker, vice-presi
dent of Lagunita. The dance will be
open to Lagunita girls and their
friends.
