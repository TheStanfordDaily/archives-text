# 'Coronet' Outlines Dr. Terman Tests
## 
### 
Experiments of Dr. Lewis Terman,
head of the psychology department,
are outlined in the current issue of
Coronet magazine.
The article, entitled, "When You're
Born You're Done For," concerns
child prodigies. Written by Louis L.
Pryor, it describes a group of child
geniuses and tests conducted with
them by Dr. Terman.
