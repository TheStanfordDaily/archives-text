# Series Schedules Talk on Astronomy
## 
### 
Dr. Harlan True Stetson, world
famous astronomer and author, will
discuss the sun, moon, and stars
and their effects on human behavior
for the Tuesday Series next Tues
day evening. Dr. Stetson, formerly
an assistant professor of astronomy
at Harvard University, has achieved
wide popularity because of his rare
faculty of explaining scientific phe
nomena in terms the average lay
man can understand.
Dr. Stetson, who traveled on five
solar eclipse expeditions and author
ed several popular books on astron
omy, including, "Earth, Radio and
the Stars" and "Sunspots and Their
Effects," is popularly known as the
scientist who brought astronomy
down to earth.
