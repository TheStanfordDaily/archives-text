# Westbrook Chosen Football Manager
## 
### 
Appointment of Tucker West
brook as senior football manager for
the coming year was announced
yesterday by A1 Masters. At the
same time the appointment of seven
junior managers was announced.
They are David M. Allen, Jr.,
Tom B. Carvey, William Hosken,
Clarence N. Peiss, Richard Singer,
William Thomas, Philip S. Chapman.
