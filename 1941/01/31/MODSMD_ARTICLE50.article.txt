# GODOY WANTS DELAY
## 
### 
SANTIAGO, Chile, Jan. 30 (UP).
—Chilean heavyweight Arturo Go
doy today cabled his manager A1
Weill in New York that under no
circumstances would he be able to
fight Joe Louis before June. Weill
has signed Godoy to meet Louis for
the third time on April 15 in Los
Angeles and has been trying to reach
his fighter by cable for two weeks—
since Godoy announced he would
seek a postponement.
