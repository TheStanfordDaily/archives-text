# Educator to Talk On Music Content
## 
### 
Dr. John J. Landsbury, music ed
ucator and lecturer in the Division
of Music, will discuss "The Content
of Music" Wednesday evening at 8
o'clock in the auditorium of the
Education Building.
This will be the third of a series
of lectures and concerts held during
winter quarter, and sponsored by
the Friends of Music and the Divi
sion of Music.
Dr. Landsbury will speak on the
part played by rhythm and design in
musical composition. As illustra
tion, he will play a suite and five
other numbers of his own, and
works by Bach, Chopin, and Schu
mann.
