# Stanford Graduate Called by Reserve
## 
### 
Robert S. Thomas, '31, was recent
ly called to active duty in the Naval
Reserve Engineer's Corps from his
position as an associate engineer
with the Bureau of Reclamation in
Sacramento. Roberts, a lieutenant,
was called to his reserve position
and reported for action on Jan. 20,
1941.
Following his graduation from
Stanford he was employed by the
Bureau on Boulder Dam Concrete
Investigations at Berkeley. Trans
fer to the Denver office followed in
1933, where he served until 1936,
when transfer to Sacramento con
nected him with the Central Val
ley Project.
