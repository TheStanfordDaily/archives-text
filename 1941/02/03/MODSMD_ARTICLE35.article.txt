# SALMON LEVINSON DIES
## 
### 
CHICAGO, Feb. 2 (UP).—Salmon
O. Levinson, 75, internationally
known corporation attorney and war
debts expert who is credited with
fathering the Kellogg-Briand pact
to outlaw war, died today after a
two-year illness. Death was caused
by a nasal hemorrhage.
