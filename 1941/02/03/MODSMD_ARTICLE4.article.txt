# Stanford Writers Sponsor Contest
## 
### 
The Stanford Writers Club is
sponsoring a writing contest open to
all students. The contest will be
open until Feb. 21. A prize of $10
will be awarded for the best piece
of prose, writing of any type which
is submitted, and an equal prize will
be given for the best poetic entry.
The club plans to have well
known authors as judges, and is cur
rently trying to get Somerset
Maugham and William Saroyan.
The winning pieces will be fea
tured in the Yearbook of Stanford
Writing, which will be printed in a
new and more colorful form this
year.
