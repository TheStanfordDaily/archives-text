# Swarthout to Sing In S. F. February 5
## 
### 
Gladys Swarthout, mezzo-soprano
of the Metropolitan Opera Associa
tion, screen and radio star, is the
next celebrity on the winter series
of the San Francisco Opera Associa
tion, booked to appear in a single
concert at the War Memorial Opera
House, Wednesday night, Feb. 5.
Miss Swarthout will offer works
from the classics, arias from the op
eras in which she has starred, and
songs by American composers.
