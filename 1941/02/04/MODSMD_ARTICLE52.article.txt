# Freshman 'Y' Group
## 
### 
To Hear Hair Stylist
The Freshman Discussion Group
of the Y.W.C.A. will meet today from
5 to G o'clock in the "Y" rooms. To
day's speaker will be Mr. Julio,
well-known hair stylist at Carolyn
Kelsey's in Palo Alto. Julio has just
returned from the hair style show
in Los Angeles. He will speak on
"New Hair Trends and Hair Styles."
All freshman women are invited to
attend the meeting.
The City and County of San Fran
cisco is planning a hospitality center
for enlisted men in the Army, Navy,
and Marine Corps.
