# Pianist to Appear With Symphony
## 
### 
Setting a precedent for the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra,
Sergei Rachmaninoff will be pre
sented this Friday afternoon and Sat
urday night, Feb. 7 and 8 and the
following week-end, Feb. 14 and 15,
in a double concert pair in the San
Francisco Memorial Opera House.
At both concerts the eminent
Russian will be represented both as
pianist and composer. This week the
orchestra plays his Symphony No. 2
in E Minor, while Rachmaninoff will
perform his own Concerto No. 3.
Tickets for both concerts are avail
able at the Peninsula Box Office in
Palo Alto.
