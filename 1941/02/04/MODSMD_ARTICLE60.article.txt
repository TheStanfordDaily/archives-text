# Landsbury to Give Lecture on Music
## 
### 
Dr. John J. Landsbury will give a
non-technical lecture on "The Con
tent of Music" this Wednesday eve
ning in the Education Auditorium
at 8 o'clock. This will be the third
in the series presented by the
"Friends of Stanford Music" and the
Division of Music.
Dr. Landsbury will tell the story
of a symphony, its conception and
development. He will illustrate by
playing parts of the first movement
of an original symphony. His talk
will be especially for those who en
joy classical music but know little
about it. Admission will be free for
Stanford students.
