# Symphony Offers Noted Performer
## 
### 
Sergei Rachmaninoff will be pre
sented both as an author and com
poser this Friday afternoon and Sat
urday night in the San Francisco
Memorial Opera House.
Appearing as a soloist, he will
piny his Concerto No. 1 and his
Rhapsody on a Theme of Paganini,
while the orchestra will perform his
Symphony No. 2 in E Minor, a fa
vorite with concert audiences every
where.
At the concert next week, Rach
| maninoff will he heard with the or
chestral numbers programmed as
"Egmont," Overture, Beethoven;
Symphony No. 3 in E Flat Major,
Schumann, and Magic Fire Music
; from Wagner's "Die Walkure."
