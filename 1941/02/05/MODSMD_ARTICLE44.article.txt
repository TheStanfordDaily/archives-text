## GREAT AUSTRALIAN HORSE ARRIVES 
### JERRY BRUCKER, Sports Editor 

BEAU FERE, greatest Australian race horse since Phar Lap, is welcomed as he arrives in San Francisco
after a trip from Sydney. Purchased by Movie Magnate Louis li. Mayer for a reputed $100,000, Beau Perc
