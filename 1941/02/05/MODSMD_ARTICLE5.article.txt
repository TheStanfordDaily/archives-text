# Requests for Grad Grants Due Feb. 15
## 
### 
Students wishing to be considered
for graduate scholarships for the
year 1941-42 are asked to file their
applications in the Scholarship Sec
retary's office on or before Feb. 15,
according to an announcement made
yesterday by Mrs. Olivia Byler,
scholarship secretary.
Mrs. Byler added that applicants
should ask two of their instructors
to write letters of recommendation
to the Committee on Scholarship.
