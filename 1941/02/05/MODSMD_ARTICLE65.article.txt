# German Refugee To Address Club
## 
### 
Ruth Frankel, recent refugee from
Germany, will speak on "Three
Chapters of My Life" today at
Luncheon Club at 12 o'clock in the
Nitery.
Miss Frankel will first contrast
democratic Germany with Nazi
Germany. During the course of her
speech, she will give her impres
sions of the United States.
Admission is 25 cents. Questions
will be answered after the talk.
