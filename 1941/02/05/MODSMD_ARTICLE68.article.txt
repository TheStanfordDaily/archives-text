# Fijis Still Leading Stanford Bowlers
## 
### 
Fiji bowlers continued to lead the
Stanford League yesterday when
they defeated the Hacienda A. C.
2-1. The A.T.O.'s pulled a surprise
by downing the Phi Kaps, 2-1,
Alpha Sigma Phi also defeated
Sigma Chi 2-1 and the Sigma Nus
upset the Kappa Sigs by the same
score. Bill Cooley of the Fiji team
captured both high score and se
ries with a 210 and 544.
