# BISHOP APPOINTED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Thursday, Feb. 6
(UP). —Appointment of the Right
Rev. Joseph T. McGucken, chancel
lor of the Archdiocese of Los Ange
les, as auxiliary Bishop of Los An
geles and titular Bishop of Sanavus,
was disclosed today.
