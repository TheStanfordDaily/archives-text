# Nanney to Present Recital Today, 4:15
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney, assistant or
ganist, will be soloist at the organ
recital this afternoon at 4:15 o'clock
in the Memorial Chapel.
The following selections have
been chosen for the concert: Bach's
"Fantasie and Fugue in G Minor,"
"Andante Con Moto" by Beethoven,
"Sketch in F Minor" from the works
of Robert Schumann, "Nocturne" by
Edvard Grieg, and Alfred Silver's
"Jubilate Deo."
