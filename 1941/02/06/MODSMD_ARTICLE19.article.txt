# Christian Science Lecture Tonight
## 
### 
Peter V. Ross, C.5.8., of San Fran
cisco, will lecture on "Christian Sci
ence: Its Answer to Human Prob
lems" tonight at 7:30 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse, in place of the
meeting in Palo Alto.
Mr. Ross, who is a member of the
Board of Lectureship of the Mother
Church, the First Church of Christ,
Scientist, in Boston, is presented by
the Stanford Christian Science Or
ganization. Faculty, students and
employees of the University arc
welcome.
