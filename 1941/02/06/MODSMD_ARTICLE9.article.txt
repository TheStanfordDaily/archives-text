# International Club Hears Dr. Holsti
## 
### 
Dr. Rudolph Holsti, former Finn
ish foreign minister and now acting
professor in the Stanford political
science department, was the speaker
at last night's meeting of the Inter
national Students' Club.
Dr. Holsti spoke on his experi
ences as Finnish representative in
the League of Nations during last
year's Russo-Finnish war.
