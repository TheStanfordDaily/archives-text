# WINGS TIE MONTREAL
## 
### 
MONTREAL, Feb. 6 (UP).—The
Detroit Red Wings overcame a
three-goal handicap to tie with the
Montreal Canadiens, 4-4, in an over
time National Hockey League game
tonight.

***SOUTH BEND, Ind., Feb. 6 (UP)

— Notre Dame's faculty athletic
board meets Friday to look over a
list of candidates for Elmer Layden's
vacated football job.
