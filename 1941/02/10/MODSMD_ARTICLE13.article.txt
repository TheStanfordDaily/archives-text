# Old Oregon Church Destroyed by Fire
## 
### 
VEERBOORT, Ore., Feb. 10 (UP).
•The Catholic Church of the Visi
tation here, one of the oldest in
Oregon, burned to the ground today.
The church was built in 1883 and
contained many valuable religious
articles, some of which were saved
before the building was completely
destroyed.
