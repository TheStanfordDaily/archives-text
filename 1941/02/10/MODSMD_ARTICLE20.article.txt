# 'Y' Music Group To Meet Today
## 
### 
The Music Appreciation Group of
the Y.W.C.A. will meet today at 4
o'clock in the "Y" rooms to hear
an hour of recorded music. Today's
program will include "Peter and the
Wolf," "Waliz of the Flowers," and
the ''Overture" to "Die Meister
tersinger." Everyone is invited to
listen to the music and have tea.
