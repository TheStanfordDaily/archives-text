# Golf Tournament For Women Set
## 
### 
Play in the All-University Wom
en's Golf Tournament will begin
Feb. 15 at 2 o'clock, according to
Miss Winifried Spear, co-chairman
of the affair. Entrants will play 18
holes of golf and will then be handi
capped and divided by Miss Spear
and Bud Finger. Players will be
paired off by drawing.
Prizes will be given for low gross
and low net in each of the two divi
sions. Eddie Twiggs and the Wom
en's Athletic Association have each
donated a dozen golf balls to be
used as prizes. No entrance fee will
be required. Further tournament
announcements will appear in The
Dailv soon.
