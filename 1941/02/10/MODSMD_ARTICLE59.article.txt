# FESTIVAL CROWD RIOTS
## 
### 
BAHIA BLANCA, Argentina, Feb.
9 (UP). — Twenty persons were
stabbed tonight in a wild riot at an
Italian Red Cross festival. Members
of the Italian colony and Argentine
youths fought with clubs, bottles,
and knives.

NEVADA CITY, Calif., Feb. 9
(UP). —A threatened strike of em
ployees of Nevada County gold
mines—largest producers in Califor
nia—was averted tonight when the
Mine Workers Protective League and
mine operators reached a compro
mise agreement for a new contract.
