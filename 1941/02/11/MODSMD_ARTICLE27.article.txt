# Riggs Wins Second South Florida Title
## 
### 
WEST PALM BEACH, Fla., Feb.
10 (UP).—Former National Cham
pion Bobby Riggs, of Chicago, to
day won the South Florida tennis
championship for the second time
when he defeated Jack Kramer, of
Rollins College, Fla., 6-3, 6-2, 6-3,
In doubles title matches Hal Sur
face of Kansas Cily teamed with
Elwood Cooke, Portland, Ore., and
won from Kramer and Wayne Sabin,
also fo Portland, 6-3, 5-7, 7-5, 1-6,
8-6.
