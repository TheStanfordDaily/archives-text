# Buck Shaw Refuses To Leave Santa Clara
## 
### 
SANTA CLARA, Feb. 10 (UP).—
Head Football Coach Lawrence T.
(Buck) Shaw of Santa Clara Uni
versity said today he has asked
Notre Dame to eliminate him from
consideration as a candidate for the
coaching job recently vacated by
Elmer Layden.
