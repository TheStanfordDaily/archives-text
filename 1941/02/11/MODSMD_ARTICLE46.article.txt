# Varsity Keglers Down Alum Team
## 
### 
A match team, chosen from the
Stanford Bowling League, defeated
the Stanford Alumni Sunday after
noon at the Indian Bowl. The var
sity toppled 2754 pins to the alumni's
2538 in the three-game match.
Bill Stover of the varsity led the
Card men to victory with a fine
series of 584. He also had high game
for Stanford, rolling 218. Walt
Peterson stole top honors on the
alumni squad when he blasted the
maples for a 233 game. However,
the other alumni men failed to
come through.
A return match will be played
March 9 at the Dodge Bowl in Red
wood City.
