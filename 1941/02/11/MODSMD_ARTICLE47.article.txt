# Golfers to Play In Scotch Foursomes
## 
### 
The all - University Two - Ball
Scotch Foursome will be held Thurs
day afternoon, February 13, at 2
o'clock. The tournament will be
medal play, with prizes awarded for
low gross and low net scores. All
players will be handicapped.
Boy and girl in the team having
low net and the team having low
gross scores will receive three balls.
Second prize, two balls to each per
son, will be awarded to the team
having low net score. The third,
fourth, fifth, and sixth prizes will be
one ball apiece to the members of
the team having low net scores in
that order.
