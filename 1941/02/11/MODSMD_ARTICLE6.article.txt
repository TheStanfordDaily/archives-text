# Hudson Appointed To Summer Staff
## 
### 
Dr. Hoyt H. Hudson, head of the
Department of English and professor
of rhetoric and oratory at Princeton
University, will teach courses on
English literature and Shakespeare
at Stanford University during the
1941 summer quarter.
Dr. Hudson is co-author of a
work. "Poetry of the English Ren
aissance," considered one of the best
literary anthologies of any period of
history. He also edited a recent
publication of "John Hoskin's Direc
tions for Speech and Style," an
Elizabethan work on rhetoric.
