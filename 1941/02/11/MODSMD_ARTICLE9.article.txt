# Toyon, Branner Schedule Formal
## 
### 
Branner and Toyon men and
their guests will dance at their an
nual formal Feb. 28 from 9 to 1 in
the Toyon lobby.
Music for the formal will be fur
nished by what the committee calls,
"a bay area big name band." The
dance is jointly sponsored by the
Toyon and Branner Clubs. Herman
Silverman and Jim Atkinson are
co-chairmen for the dance.
Serving on committees are Bob
Moberly, Don Towns, Ed Hutshing,
Bob O'Neill, John Lucas, Clarence
Peiss, Dick Hartman, John Mitchell,
Bill Struthers, and John Rosendale.
