# Dean to Head Vocom Panel
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will be chairman
of tonight's panel of Women's Vo
cational Committee which will dis
cuss the choice-of-major problem
for freshman and sophomore women.
The panel will consist of Dr. Ray
mond Harriman of the classic de
partment, Professor Harry Rathbun
of the law school, Professor George
Sensabaugh of the English depart
ment, Dr. Charles Shepard of hy
giene and physical education, and
Professor Charles Fairman of the po
litical science department.
The informal discussion will be
held in the Nitery at 7:10 o'clock,
and coffee and mints will be served
when the question period ends at
8:15 o'clock.
