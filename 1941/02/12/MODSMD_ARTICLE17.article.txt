# Palo Alto Stylist To Lecture Today
## 
### 
Next Tuesday, Feb. 18, Mrs.
George Dill, Palo Alto stylist and
department store buyer, will speak
in the Education Auditorium about
the foibles and furbelows of fashion.
Mrs. Dill's lecture, "From Head to
Foot" will deal with clothes, and
how to wear them.
Twenty representative Stanford
coeds have offered themselves as
guinea pigs for the lecture. Wom
en's Conference is sponsoring the
ticket sale in all women's living
groups. Shirley Okell, chairman of
the affair, yesterday appointed Por
tia Smith to head the poster com
mittee, and Gloria Hihn to be in
charge of the ticket committee.
