# Red Cross Drive Shows 50 Pet. Gain
## 
### 
This year the American Red
Cross Roll Call was the largest since
the war years of 1917-18, according
to Frederic J. Perry, Palo Alto Roll
Call chairman. The Palo Alto area
lone amassed $11,200, a 50 per cent
increase over last year.
A vote of thanks was given by
Mr. Perry to the chairmen of the
local committees, to the 250 cap
tains, and to each individual con
tributor. Five thousand one hun
dred and seventy-seven members
were enrolled.
