# DEATH INVESTIGATED
## 
### 
SA\ T FRANCISCO, Feb. 12 (UP).
—Authorities today investigated cir
cumstances surrounding the death of
Stefano Ventro, 86, who died last
month and left most of his $100,000
estate to his landlady.
