# ACTOR'S FATHER DIES
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Feb. 12 (UP). —
Thomas E. Young, 81, father of
film actor Robert Young, died yes
terday of injuries received Jan. 30
when an automobile struck the
horse he was riding.
