# One-Act Play Scripts Due Today, 4 o'clock
## 
### 
Scripts for Ram's Head's annual
One-Act Play Contest must be
placed in the Dramatic Council box
in the A.S.S.U. office by 4 o'clock
today, according to President Lome
Stanley. The three winning plays
will be presented later this quarter.
Exact times for Monday's tryouts
will be announced later.
