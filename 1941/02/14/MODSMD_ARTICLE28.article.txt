# Arctic Explorer To Speak Tuesday
## 
### 
Vilhjalmur Stefansson, America's
greatest explorer and international
authority on Arctic conditions, will
speak for the Tuesday Series next
Tuesday night at 7:30 o'clock in the
Auditorium of the Memorial Hall.
His subject will be "New Frontiers
of Peace and War." Three years ago
Stefansson visited Stanford and
spoke before a Univei-sity assembly.
