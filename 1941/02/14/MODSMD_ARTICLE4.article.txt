# 'Drive' Workers To Be Selected
## 
### 
"Committee chairmen for the
Convalescent Home drive must use
the activity files to select their
workers," said Betty Aveiy, co
chiarman. She urged that tiic com
mittee heads come in separately, se
lect the committees, and have them
approved by her.
The planned meeting of the four
class presidents, the chairmen of
Cardinals and Axe Society was
postponed yesterday when a check
of the files indicated the activity
cards had not yet been returned
from the fraternities.
