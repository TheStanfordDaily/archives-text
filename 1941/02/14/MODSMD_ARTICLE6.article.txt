# Paly Alumni Group Has First Election
## 
### 
More than 300 Stanford alumni
from the Peninsula turned out last
Tuesday night for the formal or
ganization of the Palo Alto Stanford
Club. According to Robert "Bones"
Hamilton, alumni secretary, mem
bership now totals 248.
At the organization dinner officers
for the coming year were elected.
They include Arnold Rumwell, city
attorney of Palo Alto, as president;
Russell V. Lee and Mrs. Paul Mer
ner were chosen as vice-presidents;
and "Bones" Hamilton will fill the
position as secretary.
