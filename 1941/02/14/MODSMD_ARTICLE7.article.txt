# Prominent Oil Mail To Speak Monday
## 
### 
H. H. Hall, chief engineer of the
Standard Oil Company, will speak
on "Engineering and Production,"
Monday evening in Encina lobby.
This is the third in a scries of talks
sponsored by Men's Vocational
Committee and the office of the Dean
of Men.
The last part of the program will
be devoted to questions and an
swers. Mr. Hall's talk will be fol
lowed by a speech on Feb. 24 by
E. V. Krick, vice-president and
cashier of the American Trust
Company.
