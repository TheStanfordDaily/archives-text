# Frosh Swimmers Upset S.F. State
## 
### 
Scoring an upset victory over the
highly touted San Francisco State
varsity, Stanford's frosh mermen de
cisively defeated the visitors, 43-28,
in Friday's meet at the Encina
pools.
Swimming in a drizzling rain,
Stanford won the opening event, the
300-yard relay, and was never be
hind from then on. No records were
broken but Rey Rule and Ted Mun
roe showed marvelous talent in tak
ing first place in the 50-yard free
style and 100-yard freestyle, respec
tively. John Weingarten clinched
the victory when he swam to a sec
ond place in the 440-yard freestyle
to give the Indians the three win
ning points.
