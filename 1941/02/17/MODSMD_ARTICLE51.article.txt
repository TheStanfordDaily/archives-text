# RAIN, NO GAME
## 
### 
Ol' Jupe Pluvius, very fickle in
deed, rained showers down over the
week-end, and Stanford's varsity
nine was forced to cancel its two
scheduled games for Friday and
Saturday — Keneally's Seals and
Kenny's Park, of Oakland.
