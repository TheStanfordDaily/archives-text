# International Club To Hold Meeting
## 
### 
The International Club will hold
an informal meeting on Wednesday
evening at 7:30 o'clock at the home
of Mrs. E. J. Thomas, 365 Lincoln
Avenue. The president of the club,
Karl Lowenstein, extends an invi
tation to any interested students,
foreign or American, to attend any
International Club meetings.
Recently the group heard M.
Pierre de la Nuxe, signer of the
Treaty of Versailles, and later direc
tor of the Paris office of the League
of Nations, speak on "A French
man's View of World Affairs as They
Concern Us Today."
