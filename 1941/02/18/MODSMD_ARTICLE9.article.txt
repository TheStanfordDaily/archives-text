# Commission Reviews S.F. Trade Decline
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 17 (UP).—
Cargo tonnage handled in San Fran
cisco's state-controlled harbor de
creased 40 per cent over a period of
ten years while the city-controlled
harbor of Los Angeles suffered a loss
of only 25 per cent in the same
period, a state harbor commissioner
said today.
