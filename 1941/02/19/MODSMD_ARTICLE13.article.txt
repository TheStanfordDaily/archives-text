# Selective Service Calls Fourth Draft
## 
### 
On the first day of induction for
the third draft call, the fourth draft
call was announced by Selective
Service headquarters in Sacramento.
The fourth call will extend from
March 17 to March 31.
The third draft call ends March
14, it was announced; and any per
son whose number has been called is
expected to report to the selective
service headquarters for this district.
The induction station for this area is
at the National Guard Armory in
San Francisco.
