Among the editorial advisers who
aided Dr. Edward L. Thomdike of
Columbia University in the writing
of the Thorndike-Century Senior
Dictionary, is Arthur G. Kennedy,
professor of English philology on the
Stanford faculty.
The dictionary, to be published
this month, is a companion to the
Thorndike-Century Junior Diction
ary, the first dictionary planned for
the use of grade-school children.
The Senior Dictionary has been writ
ten for students between the ages
of 12 and 20.
