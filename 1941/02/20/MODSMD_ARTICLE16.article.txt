# 'Y' Service Group Meets Today at 4
## 
### 
The Volunteer Community Serv
ice Group of the Y.W.C.A. will meet
today at 4 o'clock in the "Y" rooms.
Dean Mary Yost will be the special
guest speaker, according to Marga
ret Brigham, chairman of the
group.
Dean Yost will speak on the sub
ject, "Believe in Yourself." This
meeting is open to all campus
women, and it is an opportunity for
everyone to come and spend an in
formal hour with Miss Yost.
