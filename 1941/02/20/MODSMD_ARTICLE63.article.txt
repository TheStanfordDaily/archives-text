# Van Dalsem Picked Head of Union Club
## 
### 
Betty Lou Van Dalsem was elected
president of the Union Club on the
first ballot at the election held last
Tuesday. Other officers chosen were
Jeanne Levy, first vice-president;
Margaret Elliot, second vice-presi
dent; and Margaret Harper, chair
man of the Committee of Eight.
The outgoing officers, who will re
tire next Tuesday, include Portia
Smith, president; Mildred Bevil,
first vice-president; Betty Bradley,
second vice-president; and Jean
Williams, chairman of the Commit
tee of Eight.
