# 'Y' to Hear Talk On Student Tours
## 
### 
Under the auspices of the Stanford
chapter of the Y.W.C.A., Dr. Wil
fred M. Mitchell of Bakersfield will
speak today at the Women's Club
house at 4:30 o'clock. Dr. Mitchell
will talk on "The Experiment in In
ternational Living," an organization
which makes its headquarters in
Putney, Vermont.
The experiment sponsors group
voyages and living in other coun
tries for college students. Each
group member lives about a month
as guest of a family in the country
he visits, where there is a young
person of corresponding age and sex.
The host family is selected by a resi
dent representative interested in
promoting the ideals of the Ex
periment.
