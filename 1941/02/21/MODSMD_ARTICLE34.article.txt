# Draftees to Tell Board of Changes
## 
### 
Stanford men who are leaving
school because of graduation or for
any other reason at the end of this
quarter have been reminded by the
local Palo Alto Draft Board No. 112
that they must report to the board
any change in their condition which
might affect their classification.
It is a violation of the Selective
Service Act to fail to notify the local
board within five days after a
change in status affecting classifica
tion. Registrants are also reminded
by the board that any change in ad
dress must also be reported within
five days.
