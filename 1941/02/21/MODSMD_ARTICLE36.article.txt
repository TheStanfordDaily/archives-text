# Stanford-S.F. Teams to Debate
## 
### 
Stanford debaters Larry Werner
and Richard Rifenbark will meet a
San Francisco State team this after
noon at 3:30 o'clock on the San
Francisco campus.
The Pi Kappa Delta national de
bate question, "Resolved: That the
nations of the Western Hemisphere
should form a permanent union,"
will be the topic, according to Bob
Hammett, Stanford manager.
Stanford will take the negative
side of the question. Next Tuesday
afternoon two freshman debaters
will meet a St. Mary's frosh team.
