# Stanford Dames To Meet Monday
## 
### 
Dr. Bertha M. Terrill of the Home
Economics Department of the Uni
versity of Vermont will bo the
speaker for the Stanford Dames
meeting next Monday. Dr. Terrill
will discuss low-cost meals, co
operative housing, and weaving.
Mrs. Lowell Turrentine of 711
Salvatierra will be hostess; the
meeting time is 2:30 o'clock. Any
woman interested in attending is
asked to telephone Mrs. A. W. Bar
ter, P.A. 4648, Thursday or Friday
morning.
