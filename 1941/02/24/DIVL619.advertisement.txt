MEXICO EXPEDITION
Available to a limited number of senior or graduate men
students, interested in Spanish-American history, arche
ology, anthropology or geology. Will leave about March
1 oth, for Sonora, Mexico, under the auspices of Asocia
cion Internacional de Amigos de Mexico. Expense, about
$100 for the contemplated three weeks trip. Will col
laborate with Prof. Eduardo Villa, Chief of the Bureau
of Historical Research, State of Sonora, Mexico. Appli
cants interested in enrollment write Mexico Advisory
Bureau, Room 1002, deYoung Bldg., San Francisco, at
once, stating age, general qualifications and nature of
study pursued.
