# SAN DIEGO QUAKE
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, Feb. 2.1 (UP).—A
mild earthquake shook the San
Diego area today at, 10:36 a.m. P.S.T.,
but police reported the quake did
no damage. Seismologists said the
temblor centered northeast of here,
along the San Andreas fault.
