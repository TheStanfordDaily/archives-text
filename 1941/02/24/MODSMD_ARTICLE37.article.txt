# 'Y' Finance Drive Falls Below Goal
## 
### 
The Y.W.C.A. Finance Drive,
which lasted from February 10th to
the 14th, did not reach its goal. Al
though the results are as yet in
complete, the collections to date
have amounted to $281.29. This is
56.5 per cent of the expectcd goal,
which was $500.

Of the members, 222 supported the
drive, as well as 154 non-members.
The total of $281.29 includes both
cash and pledge contributions. Al
though this year's goal was not
reached, an amount equal to 75 per
cent of last year's receipts was col
lected in the 1941 drive, and the
complete reports have not been tab
ulated.
