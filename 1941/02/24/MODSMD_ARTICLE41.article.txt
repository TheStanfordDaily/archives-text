# Leonard Discusses Education Leaders
## 
### 
A warning that "educational lead
ership is shifting to the politician
and the layman" was made Friday
by J. Paul Leonard, associate pro
fessor of education at Stanford.
He spoke on "Implications of Re
cent Research for Curriculum Plan
ning in Civic Education" before the
general session of the Society for
Curriculum Study, now meeting in
Atlantic City, N.J.
