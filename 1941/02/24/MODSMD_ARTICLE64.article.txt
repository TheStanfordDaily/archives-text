# Frosh to Debate With St. Mary's
## 
### 
A1 Armer and Hy Herbert, frosh
debaters, will meet two St. Mary's
speakers tomorrow afternoon at 4
o'clock in Room 205 of Memorial
Hall.
Hebert and Armer will take the
affirmative of the Pi Kappa Delta
national question, "Resolved: That
the nations of the Western Hemi
sphere should form a permanent
union." Following the match the
St. Mary's team will be the dinner
guests of the frosh.
