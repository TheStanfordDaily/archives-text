# 'Y' Group to Hear Dr. Shepard Today
## 
### 
The Marriage and the Home Group
of the Y.W.C.A. will hold its last
meeting for this quarter this after
noon at 4 o'clock in the "Y" rooms.
Dr. Charles E. Shepard, director of
men's Student Health Service, will
be today's guest speaker.
Dr. Shepard will talk on the sub
ject, "Sex Relationships in Mar
riage." All women students are in
vited to attend the* meeting, accord
ing to Norma McKenzie, chairman.
The "Y" Cabinet will also meet to
day at 4 o'clock, and the by-laws
committee will meet at 3 o'clock in
the "Y" rooms.
