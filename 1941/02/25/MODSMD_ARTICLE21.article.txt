# Players to Give College Comedy
## 
### 
The tribulations of an erudite but
retiring college professor will be
graphically presented Friday and
Saturday nights in the Education
auditorium, when Ram's Head pre
sents its reading of "The Male Ani
mal."
Battling a faculty with its mind on
football receipts, the hero, Tommy
Turner, finds football interfering not
only with his academic pursuits but
also with his family life.
Tickets are priced at 25 cents and
reservations may be made by tele
phoning Bob Rich in the Dramatic
Council office. Curtain will be at
8:15 according to Ted Marcuse, Ram's
Head reading director.
