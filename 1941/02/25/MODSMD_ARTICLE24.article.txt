# Medal Collection Donated to Farm
## 
### 
A collection of medals which the
British have awarded to their heroes
of war and peace in recent decades
has been donated to the Hoover Li
brary on War, Revolution, and Peace
by William Cavalier of San Fran
cisco.
Made up of some 40 mounted med
als, the collection contains a "series
on British victories" and a group
of "national medals." Cavalier made
the gift through President Hay Ly
man Wilbur.
Additional gifts of collections of
books and photographs were made
by Professor Eliot G. Mears of Stan
ford, Mrs. Horace R. Boynton of
Santa Barbara, and W. L. Honnold
of Los Angeles.
