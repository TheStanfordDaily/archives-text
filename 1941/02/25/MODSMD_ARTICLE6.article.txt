# Dr. Jackson Off On Eastern Trip
## 
### 
Dr. J. Hugh Jackson, dean of the
Graduate School of Business, left
yesterday morning for an extended
trip on which he will be gone six
weeks.
First stop on the trip is the Uni
versity of Washington at Seattle,
where he will address the Washing
ton Graduate School of Business at
their annual dinner next Wednesday
night.
From Seattle, Dr. Jackson will tour
the East, stopping at New York,
Pittsburgh, Philadelphia, Akron, and
other large cities. While East, Dr.
Jackson will contact alumni of the
Stanford Business School in an effort
to further the Business School's re
search project on "Top Manage
ment Organization and Control."
