# Senior Women Lunch Tomorrow
## 
### 
Senior women will gather at 12:15
o'clock tomorrow in the Women's
Clubhouse for their second informal
buffet lunch of the year.
Luncheon will be served at 12:15
o'clock, and admission will be 35
cents. Ruthelen List, general chair
man of the event, urged all senior
women to attend, and announced
that lunch would be over in time for
1 o'clock classes and labs.
The tables will be decorated to
carry out the spring motif of the
affair.
