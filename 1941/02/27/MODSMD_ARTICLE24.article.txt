# Nanney to Present Organ Recital, 4:15
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney, assistant or
ganist, will present the Thursday
afternoon recital in the Chapel to
day at 4:15 o'clock.
Nanney will open with the First
Sonata in A Minor by Borowski,
followed by Bach's "Hark, A Voice
Saith 'All Are Mortal'." The pro
gram will close with "Grand Cho
rus," by Guilmant.
