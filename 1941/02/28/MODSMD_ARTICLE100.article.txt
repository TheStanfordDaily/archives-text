# Watches, Gadgets To Hang from Belts
## 
### 
Wherever you go you'll find it
handy to carry the time around
your waist. That's why we find
watches, purses, and other conve
nient little gadgets hanging from
belts this season.
For golf there are leather belts to
wear with sweaters. These have a
zipper pouch for make-up, tees, and
anything else.
