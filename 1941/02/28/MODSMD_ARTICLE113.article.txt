# Men: Let Your Flowers Speak!
## 
### 
A tip to the men on spring cor
sages: when you are sending flow
ers to a girl, don't just call a florist
and order some standard corsage;
find out what kind of a dress your
date is wearing and pick some
thing that will be especially for her
and for that dress.
Spring flowers present an oppor
tunity to send individual and lovely
corsages at a reasonable price. One
of the loveliest corsages at the re
cent Lagunita formal was a cluster
of yellow jonquils worn on a dark
blue dress. That corsage showed
thoughtfulness and the girl who
received it couldn't have been more
thrilled if it had been orchids.
If your date's dress is perfect un
adorned, send her flowers for her
hair or give her a wrist corsage.
Many florists will also arrange an
effective flat corsage to fit on an
evening bag. Give her flowers that
suggest her personality and she'll
remember them for a long time.
