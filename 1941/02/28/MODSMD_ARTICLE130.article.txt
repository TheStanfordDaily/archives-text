# PLEATED PURSES
## 
### 
To match those much-heralded
pleats coming in on every manner
of dress this spring, outfit yourself
with a capeskin purse laid in neat
pleats. This device gives the stand
by envelope a spanking new look.

For that sometimes needed varia
tion on a navy coat and dress outfit,
acquire the gay printed hat and
purse sets. Made mainly in pastels
these duos can pull the fashion frus
trated miss out of almost any di
lemma.
