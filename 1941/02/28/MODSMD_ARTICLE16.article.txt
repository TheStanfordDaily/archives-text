# Branner-Toyon Formal Tonight
## 
### 
To accommodate the 250 couples
who will attend the Branner-Toyon
Formal tonight, the Branner Dining
Hall will be available for dancing as
well as the Toyon lobby.
Music will be furnished by Eddie
Murphy rind his 11-piece orchestra
and a loud speaking system will be
connected to Branner Hall.
Jim Atkinson and Herman Silver
man are serving as co-chairmen.
Chaperones are Col. and Mrs. Harry
B. Allen and Mr. and Mrs. Gene
Coldiron.
