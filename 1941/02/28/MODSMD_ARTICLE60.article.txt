# CHINESE GAMBLER HELD
## 
### 
STOCKTON, Feb. 26 (UP). —
Louie Kee, 39, Chinese gambler,
was jailed here today to face a
charge of murdering Wong Toy, 84,
Chinese rooming house proprietor,
a year ago.
