# Forum Offers Draft Critics
## 
### 
With Dr. Wilbur as introductory
speaker, Stanford's student forum
will discuss the role of the college
man the national defense pro
gram Wednesday night at 7:30 o'-
clock in the Education auditorium.
According to Chairman Bob Peck
ham, the panel will include: Sicl
Terry, representing the engineers'
viewpoint; Betty Avery, dealing with
the women's part in the draft and
defense programs; Bob Newell,
speaking for law, social science, and
business majors; and John Moore,
president of Interfaith Council, giv
ing the position of the conscientious
objector.
The forum will try to determine
just how students can use their
training to the fullest advantage
during the national emergency.
