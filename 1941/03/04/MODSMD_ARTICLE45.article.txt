# PLAYER WRITES BOOK
## 
### 
SAN ANTONIO, Tex., March 3
(UP). —A1 Schacht, the baseball
buffoon, has written himself a book
entitled "Clowning through Base
ball."
About 1,050,000 newcomers moved
into California during 1930-40.
