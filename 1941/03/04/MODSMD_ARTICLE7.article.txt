# Mislaid Bicycles Reported by Gordy
## 
### 
Officer Gordy Davis has announced
that three bicycles have been found
and may be claimed by identifying
them at his office. The bicycles are
believed to be local ones and have
either been stolen or forgotten by
their owners.
The bicycles are all in excellent
condition, Davis said. Besides that,
they are taking up considerable
space in Davis' office, and he is
anxious to get rid of them. .
