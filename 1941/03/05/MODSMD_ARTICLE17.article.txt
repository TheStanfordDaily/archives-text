# Sunday Concert Tickets Offered
## 
### 
Tickets for the concert to be pre
sented on Founders' Day, March 9,
by the San Francisco Symphony Or
chestra in the Memorial Church may
be obtained without charge by un
dergraduate and graduate students
today from 8:30 o'clock to 5 o'clock
in the lobby of the Administration
Building upon presentation of stu
dent body cards.
The symphony concert, which will
be held at 8 o'clock Sunday eve
ning, is part of an all-day program
devoted to commemorating the
founders of the University.
A feature of the concert will be
the playing for the first time of
"Ode to Truth," written by Roy
Harris especially for Stanford and
dedicated to the fiftieth anniversary.
