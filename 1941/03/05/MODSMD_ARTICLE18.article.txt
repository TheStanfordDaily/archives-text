# Mrs. Wilbur to Hold 'At Home' Thursday
## 
### 
Mrs. Ray Lyman Wilbur will hold
her customary At Home Thursday
afternoon from 4 to 6 o'clock at
the Wilbur residence on Lagunita
Knoll. Mrs. Wilbur has long held
these monthly At Homes on the first
Thursday of each month. Faculty
and students are cordially invited
to attend.
