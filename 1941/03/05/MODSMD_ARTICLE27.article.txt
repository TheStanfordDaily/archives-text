# Wilbur to Address S.F. Club Friday
## 
### 
President. Ray Lyman Wilbur will
address the Commonwealth Club of
California on the subject, "Where
Are We?" Friday at 12:10 o'clock in
the Palace Hotel. The luncheon
meeting tickets are selling at $1.15
apiece.
The gasoline tax in California for
the month of January amounted to
$4,536,050.
