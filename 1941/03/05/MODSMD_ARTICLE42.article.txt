# JACKIE QUITS SCHOOL
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. March 5 (UP).—
Jackie Robinson, Negro star and
only athlete ever to win letters in
the four major sports at U.C.L.A., is
quitting school for the rest of the
semester.

SANTA CLARA, March 5 (UP).—
With the skies still dripping rain,
Bronto Coach Buck Shaw opened
spring football practice at Santa
Clara Monday.
