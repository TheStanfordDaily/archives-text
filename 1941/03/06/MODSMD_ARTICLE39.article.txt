# Metropolitan Golf Open Discontinued
## 
### 
NEW YORK. March 5 (UP).—The
lack of public and player support
has forced the executive committee
of the Metropolitan Golf Associa
tion to discontinue the Metropolitan
open championship, it was an
nounced today.
