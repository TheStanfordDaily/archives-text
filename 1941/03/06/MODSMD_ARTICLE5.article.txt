# President Returns After Flood Delay
## 
### 
After an unexpected sojourn in
the Southern Pacific freight yards at
Santa Barbara, President Ray Ly
man Wilbur and Head Football
Coach Clark Shaughnessy returned
to the Farm yesterday from the
South.
Several landslides and washouts
north of Santa Barbara tied up
both north- and southbound traffic
late Monday and all day Tuesday.
The Sunset Limited, which Dr. Wil
bur was on, left Los Angeles Mon
day night but did not arrive in Palo
Alto until yesterday morning.
