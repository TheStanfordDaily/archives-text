ZATON tonight at 7:30 in the
First Church of Christ, Scientist,

stop at Roble and Encina at 7:15.

at 7 o'clock tonight in the Choir
Room of the Church. All present
and past members urged to be
present for preparation of next
Sunday's Founders' Day services.
