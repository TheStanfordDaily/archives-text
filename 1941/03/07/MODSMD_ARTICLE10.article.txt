# Farm Debaters Face San Jose
## 
### 
Stanford debaters Bob McNeill
and Jack Webster will face a San
Jose State College team tomorrow
over radio station KSFO at 2:30
o'clock.
Stanford will take the negative
side of the Pi Kappa Delta national
question: "Resolved: That the na
tions of the Western Hemisphere
should form a permanent union."
A freshman team consisting of
Ted Eberle, Howard Ledeen, and
Jack Reynolds will meet Santa
Clara today at 4 o'clock in room 205
of Memorial Hall. Stanford will take
the aflirmative of the Pi Kappa
Delta question. An audience forum
will follow the match.
Varsity debaters Jim Sargent and
Collin McClintock will meet San
Francisco State this afternoon at 4
o'clock in room 200s of the English
Corner. Stanford will speak for the
affirmative of the Pi Kappa Delta
topic.
All interested persons are invited
to be present at this afternoon's
debate.
