# Golf Notables To Meet Here
## 
### 
For a morning of softball and an
afternoon of golf, women physical
education instructors from high
schools and colleges throughout
Northern California will meet to
morrow on the Stanford campus.
Experts in golf and baseball teach
ing and playing technique will illus
trate their theories. Miss Florence
Shearer of San Francisco will show
several moving pictures illustrating
theory in softball play. Following
this demonstration, two crack Sac
ramento softball < teams will stage
an exhibition contest.
In the 1:30 o'clock golf session,
Chairman Eleanore Ginno of San
Mateo Junior College will bring
such noted women golfers as Mrs.
Sylvia Potter Cain and Mrs. Fran
ces Glover to illustrate golf tech
nique.
