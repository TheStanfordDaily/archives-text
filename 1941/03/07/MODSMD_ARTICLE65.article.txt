# TROUT LEAVES S.C.
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, March 6 (UP).—
Jack Trout of Bakersfield High,
state high school 100-yard dash
champion two years ago, has with
drawn from the University of South
ern California, leaving a major gap
in Coach Dean Cromwell's team.
