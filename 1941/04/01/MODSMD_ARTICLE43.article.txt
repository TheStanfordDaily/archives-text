# Reading Course Offered Again
## 
### 
Following a pronounced rise in
the grade-point averages of its stu
dents of the last quarter, Reading
Development, Education I, will again
be offered during the Spi'ing Quar
ter although it is not listed in the
Time Schedule.
The course includes the develop
ing of skills and adjustment of com
prehension to material of college
level, recognizing central ideas and
meaning, using reference materials,
and improving rate of understand
ing.
One credit will be offered for the
course, which will be given from 1
to 2 o'clock on Wednesdays, Thurs
days, and Fridays in Room e35 of
the Education Building.
