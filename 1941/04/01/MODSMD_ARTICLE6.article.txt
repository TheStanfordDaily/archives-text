# Professor Manna To Speak on Andes
## 
### 
Following Inca trails through the
Andes will be the topic of a lecture
illustrated by lantern slides to be
given tomorrow evening in the Edu
cation Auditorium at 7:30 o'clock by
Paul R. Hanna, Stanford professor
of education.
The lecture will cover Professor
Hanna's travels through Bolivia and
the Andes, and will describe the
civilziatiorr of the typical west coast
Indian and present some of the An
dean views concerning the United
States.
