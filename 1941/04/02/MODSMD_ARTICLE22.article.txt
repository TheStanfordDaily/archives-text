# Portland Lad Missing Since Monday Noon
## 
### 
PORTLAND, April I (UP).—Fears
for the safety of 5-year-old George
Harvey, missing from his home here
since Monday noon, increased to
night as authorities said he may
have lost his life in a slough near
his home. The small boy's dog had
led searchers to the shoreline of the
slough, but workers with grappling
hooks failed to find a body.
SALEM, Ore., April 1 (UP).—Gov
ernor Charles A. Sprague today put
his signature on seven bills appro
priating operating money for regu
lar state activities.
