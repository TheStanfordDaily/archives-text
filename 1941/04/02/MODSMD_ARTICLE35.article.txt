# VITOUSEK RETURNS AFTER LANSING MEET
## 
### 
Co-captain Roy Vitousek is ex
pected to return to the campus and
practice today. Vitousek has been
swimming in the NC.A.A. meets
held at Lansing, Mich., this vacation.
Vitousek, Stanford's ace utility man,
won his heat in both the 50-yard
freestyle and the 200-yard breast
stroke. He was second in the semi
finals but failed to qualify for the
finals.
Howard Adams, P.C.T. diving
champion and Stanford merman, won
fifth in the low board and sixth in
the high board events held at
Lansing.
