# JAPANESE MINISTER VISITS BERLIN
## 
### 

JAPAN'S FOREIGN MINISTER, Yosuke Matsuoka, second from the right, with the cane, arrives in Berlin
to see about a war with the United States. Hitler would like Japan to slow United States arms aid to
Britain. High officials greeted Matsuoka. Radio picture Hashed from Berlin. Matsuoka is now in Rome.
