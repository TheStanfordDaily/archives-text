MUSIC GROUP of the Stanford
Dames will meet Wednesday at
7:30 at the home of Mrs. Everett
Breed, 717 Hamilton Street.

Stanford Dames will meet Wed
nesday at 8 o'clock at the home
of Mrs. L. T. Lydick, 539 Yale
Avenue.
GENERAL MEETING of the Stan
ford Dames will be held on Thurs
day, April 3, at 2 o'clock at the
home of Mrs. P. R. Hanna, 737
Coronado Avenue, campus. The
speaker will be Mrs. Alexander
Bell, the interior decorator.

briefly in the Quad oflice tonight
at 7:30 o'clock to sign up for the
quarter's work.

rors" will be held in the Little
Theater tonight at 7:30 o'clock. All
students are urged to come to try
out.

meet tonight in the club rooms at
the airport. Plans for the intra
club meet will be discussed. All
members are urged to attend as
well as any others interested in
aviation.

Thomas, 365 Lincoln Ave., Palo
Alto. Cars leave the Nitery at 7:30
o'clock.
