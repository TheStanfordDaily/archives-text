# Lunchers to Hear Library Historian
## 
### 
Waldo Chamberlin, the associate
research historian of the Hoover
War Library, will be the guest
speaker at the Luncheon Club's reg
ular meeting this Wednesday noon in
the Nitery.
Mr. Chamberlin will speak on
"The Meaning of Ships Seizures."
Emphasizing the legal and historical
aspects of this war-time procedure,
Mr. Chamberlin will cite examples
from World War I.
