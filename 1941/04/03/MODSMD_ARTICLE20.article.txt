# Allen to Present Spring Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the first organ
recital of the Spring Quarter tomor
row afternoon at 4:15 o'clock in Me
morial Church.
The recital will open with Passa
caglia and Thema fugatum in C
minor by Bach and will include two
choral-preludes by Bach, "Have
mercy on me, Lord," and "Rejoice
ye, Christians." It will close with
"Hosannah" by Dubois.
The Sunday afternoon recitals
will be discontinued from Sunday,
April 6, to Sunday, May 4, bccause
of the Pro Arte Quartet concerts in
Memorial Hall at 3:30 p.m.
