# Women May Apply For Summer Jobs
## 
### 
The Stanford Student Employ
ment, Service has announced that
there are summer jobs open to Stan
ford women to act as counsellors at
girls' camps. All Stanford women
interested shoidd apply at the em
ployment office.
Any Stanford man with a life
saving certificate who is interested
in a life guard job in Southern
California during the summer should
file application with Mrs. Cook of
the employment service.
