# Jackson to Speak At Business Lunch
## 
### 
Dr. J. Hugh Jackson, dean of the
Stanford Graduate School of Busi
ness, will be the principal speaker
at a general meeting of the Fed
erated Business Teachers' Associa
tion of California at a luncheon in
the Leamington Bowl Tuesday.
A noted leader in this field, Dean
Jackson will speak on "Constructive
Business Leadership" as a part of the
general theme of the conference,
which is "Business Education Looks
Ahead." Dr. Ralph Fields, acting
assistant professor of education at
Stanford, will speak at a special
section meeting April 7 on "Guid
ance and Counseling in California
Schools."
