# BROWNS BLAST BEES
## 
### 
TEXARKANA, Tex., April 3 (UP).
—A* 13-hit attack with homers by
George McQuinn and pitcher Eldon
Auker plus three-hit pitching to
day gave the St, Louis Browns a
7-5 victory over the Boston Bees.
'Bama Rowell homered to give the
Bees their first run.

SAN FRANCISCO, April U (UP).
—Representatives of the California
Metal Trades Association and the
A.F.L. Machinists Union meet to
morrow with Federal Conciliator
Andrew J. Gallagher to discuss a
revised set of demands against "up
town" machine shops, many of
whom have or expect defense con
tracts.
