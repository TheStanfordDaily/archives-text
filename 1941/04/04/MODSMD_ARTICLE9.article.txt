# Quads Will Be Sold At P.O. from 8-12
## 
### 
The 1941 edition of the Stanford
Quad will be on sale on the post
office steps this morning from 8 to
12 o'clock, according to an an
nouncement made yesterday by
Business Manager Jim McKellar.
"There are only a limited number
of Quads left, and it will be neces
sary for every student, who has not
yet purchased one to do so now, as
those left are going at a rapid rate,"
stated McKellar.
