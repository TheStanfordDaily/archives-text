# RUSS FREIGHTER RUNS AGROUND
## 
### 

FROM THE AIR, the Russian 2677-ton freighter Vatslav Vorovski is shown where it went aground on the
sands of Peacock Spit in the mouth of the Columbia River near Astoria, Ore. Members of the crew were
removed by Coast Guard vessels. The light at the upper right is from Cape Disappointment.
