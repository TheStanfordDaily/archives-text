# Police Apply For Two-way Radios
## 
### 
Application has been filed with
the Federal Communication Com
mission by the Stanford police de
partment for installation of two-way
radio transmitters in campus patrol
car, according to Gordy Davis, chief
of police.
The F.C.C. will investigate Stan
ford and if need for the two-way
transmission exists, a permit will be
issued, Davis reports.
A personnel change in the de
partment finds Peyton Walmsley
has replaced George Burns as night
patrolman. Plans are underway to
move the police office from its pres
ent location in the corporation yard.
