# One Miner Saved, One Still Trapped
## 
### 
CENTRALIA, Pa., April (» (UP).
—One of two coal minors trapped
150 feet underground in an aban
doned mine was rescued today after
being entombed for 20 hours.
The miner, Larry Burns, 43, of
Centralis, was suffering from shock
and bruises. Fear was expressed
that Joseph Loftus, 18, also of Cen
tralia, was killed in the slide.
