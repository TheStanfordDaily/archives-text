# 'Y' Will Hold Annual Picnic This Thursday
## 
### 
The annual picnic of the Y.W.C.A.
will be held this Thursday, April 10,
in Dean Mary Yost's back yard at 5
o'clock, it has been announced by
Martha Thurmond, chairman. Ev
eryone, "Y" member or not, is in
vited. The price is 35 cents, includ
ing a special box lunch.
