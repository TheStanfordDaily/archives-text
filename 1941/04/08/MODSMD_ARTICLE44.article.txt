# VAN MUNGO REINSTATED
## 
### 
MACON, April 7 (UP). — The
Brooklyn Dodgers tonight reinstated
their star pitcher, Van Lingle
Mungo, whom they had dropped last
month for breaking training rules in
a Havana training camp.
