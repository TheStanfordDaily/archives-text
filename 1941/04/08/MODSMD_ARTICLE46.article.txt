# Bob Blatt Retains State Ski Crown
## 
### 
Bobby Blatt, Stanford's sensational
freshman skier, ran away with the
slalom at the Sugar Bowl Sunday
to retain his state combined cham
pionship.
Blatt beat out Bill Robinson, Ca
nadian champion, for the title, win
ning both slalom heats in times of
50.3 and 50.2. Bill Pitcher of Stan
lord wound up in seventh place.
