# Orchesis Invites S.J. Group Here
## 
### 
Margaret Eliot, elected president
of Orchesis at the last meeting of
the group in Winter Quarter, and
the other Orchesis members will act
as hosts for the San Jose dance
group tonight at 7:15 o'clock in the
Women's Gym.
Nineteen of the San Jose group
are expected to attend the meeting.
Their adviser, Miss Marjorie Lucas,
will direct.
