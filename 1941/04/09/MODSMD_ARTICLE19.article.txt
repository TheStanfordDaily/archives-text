# Dr. Terman Returns From Radio Institute
## 
### 
Dr. F. E. Terman, of the depart
ment of electrical engineering, re
turned yesterday from a three
month stay in the East with the In
stitute of Radio Engineers. Presi
dent of the institute, Dr. Terman
spent much of his time overseeing
the formation of new chapters of
the organization. He also was in
Washington participating in na
tional defense work.
