# Bunn Named Head Of Coast Deans
## 
### 
The Western Association of Deans
and Advisers of Men, with Stan
ford's John Bunn elected to its presi
dency, met in Berkeley last Friday
and Saturday, signalizing the group's
formal organization.
Hitherto an entirely informal
group, the association, under the
leadership of Dean Bunn and the
deans of men at California and the
College of the Pacific, this year de
cided to constitute itself into a defi
nite group.
The meeting at Berkeley included
deans from all the major schools
west of the Rockies. Next year's
meeting will take place at Pomona.
Dean Vernon Bollman of Occidental
College was elected secretary of the
association.
