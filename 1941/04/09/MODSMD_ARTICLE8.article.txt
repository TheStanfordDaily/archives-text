# Wet Weather Causes Delay in Road Repairs
## 
### 
Work accomplished last week on
the repairing and widening of Lo
mita Drive and Santa Teresa Street
was small, because of the inclement
weather. The telephone pole on the
corner of Santa Teresa and the post
office triangle was moved back about
ten feet, the amount which the road
will be widened.
