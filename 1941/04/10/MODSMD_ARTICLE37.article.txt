# Paly High to Meet Frosh Splashers
## 
### 
This afternoon at 4 o'clock Stan
ford's frosh mermen meet Palo Alto
High School in the Encina Pools.
The meet is open to men only.
Acting as the nucleus of the fresh
man team will be Captain Ted Mun
roe, distance man; Rey Runle,
sprinter; Todd Gardner, breast
stroke; and Pete Lee, back stroke.
Palo Alto's greatest threat is in the
220-yard free style, when Jim Arm
strong threatens the Indians' Cap
tain Munroe.
Saturday at 2 6'clock the fresh
men will meet San Francisco J.C. in
a preliminary to the Varsity-Olym
pic Club meet.
