# The 						Stanford Daily SPORTS
## CHUCK SMITH, Sports Editor GIRLS BREAK SWIMMING RECORDS 
### 

THESE FOUR UNIVERSITY of Hawaii girls recently hung up a new intercollegiate record when they
swam a 100-yard freestyle relay race in 57.1 seconds in competition in a telegraphic meet with mainland
United States schools. From the left: Jean Kangeter. Betty Brown, Katherine Allen, and Bobbie Podmore.
