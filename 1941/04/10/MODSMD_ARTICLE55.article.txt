# Cyril Bryner to Speak At Coast Convention
## 
### 
Dr. Cyril Bryner, Stanford Slavic
languages instructor, will be a
speaker at this year's Pacific Coast
Regional Convention of the Ameri
can Catholic Philosophical Associa
tion April 18 and 19 at University of
San Francisco. He has chosen for
his subject, "The Art of Teaching:
Its Philosophical Basis."
