# The 						Stanford Daily SPORTS
## CHUCK SMITH, Sports Editor POWELL SLIDES BACK SAFELY 
### 

JAKE POWELL, former Yankee stalwart, scrambles back to first base
as Soarselle of the Seattle Rainiers attempts to tag him out in the
first game of the Pacific Coast League, which the San Francisco Seals
won. 1-0, in 10 innings. The Seals lost a double-header the next day.
