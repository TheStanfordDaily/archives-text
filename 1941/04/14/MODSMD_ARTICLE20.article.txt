# Berkeley Festival Tickets on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets for the forthcoming Ber
keley Festival, celebrating Berke
ley's 75th birthday, are on sale in
Room 202 of the Memorial Hall. All
interested should apply to C. W.
Barker, speech and drama secretary.
The festival will be held six Sun
days starting May 4 in the Greek
Theater.
The schedule of events includes
the oratorio, "Elijah," the San Fran
cisco Opera Ballet, Offenbach's "Or
pheus in the. Underworld," the San
Francisco Symphony Orchestra,
Shaw's "St. Joan," and Shakespeare's
"Twelfth Night." Tickets are 50
cents to $2.
