# Jerk's Journal
## 
### 
GAY and informal will be the scene this af
ternoon at 1:15 o'clock when a group of
the smart journalistic set on the Farm meet at
the home of Itsa Shack for organ music and the
tea-time chat of W.P.A. Cannon, D.V.S., who will
talk on "Recent Developments in Asia and
Lower Menlo."
