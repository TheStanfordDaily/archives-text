# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
SINGING TRYOUTS for the Spring
Show will be held today from 2 to
6 o'clock on the main stage of
Memorial Hall.
SPEAKING TRYOUTS for the
Spring Show will be held tonight
from 7 to 10 o'clock in the Little
Theater and tomorrow afternoon
from 1 to G o'clock in the Ram's
Head room.
COMMONWEALTH DEBATE try
outees will meet in Dr. Leland
Chapin's office this afternoon at 4
o'clock.
A.S.S.U. INTERNATIONAL COM
MITTEE meets today at 5 o'clock
in the basement of the Women's
Clubhouse. Plans for the barbe
cue will be discussed.
GLEE CLUB will meet tonight at
8 o'clock in Room 84. Nomination
and election of officers will be held.
BADMINTON CLUB will hold its
first spring quarter meeting Tues
day night in the Women's Gym at
7 o'clock.
