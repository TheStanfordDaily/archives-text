# Faculty Clubs Party Planned Friday Night
## 
### 
The annual party of the men's
and women's faculty clubs will be
held Friday night. The party will
begin with a no-host dinner at 6:15
o'clock in the Union dining room.
Following the dinner the men will
put on a play in the Women's Club
house. After the play there will be
card games and dancing. Those who
wish reservations are asked to phone
the Union immediately.
