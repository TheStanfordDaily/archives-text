# Leiser Will Speak At Encina Smoker
## 
### 
Encouraged by the success of the
first Encina smoker last quarter, Bill
Reich, committee chairman, has
completed preparations for a second
smoker for the first-year men to
morrow night at 7 o'clock.
Bill Leiser, well known sports
writer for the San Francisco Chron
icle, and Clark Shaughnessy, varsity
grid coach, will be the principal
speakers.
