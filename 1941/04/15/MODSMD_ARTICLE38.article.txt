# Fencers Touche National Crown
## 
### 
Stanford's fencing team, coached
by Elwyn Bugge. moved into the
national spotlight Saturday night af
ter competing in the annual Intercol
legiagte Fencing Tournament. The
team emerged with the intercolle
giate foil championship, and Marl in
Nelson won 12 out of 12 matches for
the intercollegiate saber champion
ship.
The meet was held at Berkeley
and was participated in by U.S.C.,
U.C.L.A., Stanford, California, and
Utah. The foil, dueling sword, and
saber were employed, and each
school fenced every other school in
each weapon.
