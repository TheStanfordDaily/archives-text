# ITALY CAPTURES SLAVS AS SERBS FALL BACK
## 
### 
ROME, Mon., April 14 (UP).-The
official Stefani news agency said to
day that Italian forces mopping up
Jugoslav nests of resistance around
Lake Ochrida had captured more
than 1000 Serbs, two of them gen
erals.
The agency reported from Buda
pest that Hungarian troops, contin
uing their drive into Jugoslavia, had
smashed the Serbs' second defense
line and occupied some points be
yond the Francip Canal between the
Danube and Tisa rivers.
