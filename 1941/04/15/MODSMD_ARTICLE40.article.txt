# SERIES, FLATBUSH TALK
##

NEW YORK. April 14 (UP). —
1 Brooklyn fans already are talking in
terms of when does the World Series
begin. High point of interest on
this club is whether young Pete
Reisner can burn up the league in
center field and whether Kirby
; Higbee from the Phillies can win 20
I games.
