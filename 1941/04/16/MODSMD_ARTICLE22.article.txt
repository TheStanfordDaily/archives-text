# Senior Fund Sends Out Call for Cash
## 
### 
Issuing a call for all off-campus
solicitors, appointed by the senior
executive committee, to meet with
them at 1:30 o'clock this afternoon
in the A.S.S.U. office, Senior Fund
Chairmen Rosamond Upson and
Dwight Hutchinson announced yes
terday that pledges and cash returns
are mounting rapidly.
Solicitors may obtain additional
cards and receipt books in the
A.S.S.U. office any afternoon this
week, it was learned.
