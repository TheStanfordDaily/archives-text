# KSFO Radio Engineer Speaks Tonight, 7:30
## 
### 
R. V. Howard, chief engineer of
radio station KSFO, will speak on
the subject, "Theoretical and Prac
tical Aspects of Wave Propagation
as Applied to Radio Broadcasting"
tonight at 7:30 o'clock at the regular
meeting of the Stanford branch of
the American Institute of Electrical
Engineers. Everyone interested in
the subject is cordially invited to
attend.
Last spring quarter 44 per cent of
the campus voted it unlikely that
the United States would become in
volved in a European War.
