# Stanford Dames Will Tour Campus
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will entertain
the University of California Dames
at their general meeting today at 2
o'clock in the Women's Clubhouse.
From the clubhouse they will go
to the Memorial Church. Following
a program of organ music there by
Dr. Warren D. Allen, they will be
taken on a tour of the campus con
ducted by Dr. D. Elton Trueblood.
