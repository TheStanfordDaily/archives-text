# Fliers Cancel Till With Cal Aviators
## 
### 
Chuck Sharp, president of the
Stanford Flying Club, announced
that the forthcoming meet with the
University of California flyers
scheduled for this coming Sunday
has been called off. This action was
necessary because of the impossi
bility of the club to obtain a waiver
on the field for that. date.
There will be a club practice meet
instead of the California meet at
the airport at 9:30 o'clock Sunday
morning. All members should be
present, and anyone interested in
aviation is invited to be present.
