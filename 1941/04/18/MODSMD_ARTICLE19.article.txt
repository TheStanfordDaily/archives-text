# Chapel Rites Held For Vanderburgh
## 
### 
Funeral services were held yes
terday afternoon in the Stanford
Chapel for Stan Vanderburgh, jun
ior, who was killed Wednesday when
his motorcycle crashed. Mr. and Mrs.
Vanderburgh drove down from
Fresno Wednesday night in order to
attend the funeral.
Attending the services were all of
Vanderburgh's fraternity brothers
from the Phi Gamma Delta house.
The junior class of that house acted
as pall-bearers.
