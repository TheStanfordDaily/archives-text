# Opening Planned At Art Gallery
## 
### 
The formal opening of the Morti
mer C. Leventritt Collection, which
has been donated to the University
on the occasion of its 50th Anniver
sary celebration, will be held on
Sunday afternoon from 4 to 7 o'clock
at the Thomas Welton Stanford Art
Gallery.
Guests of the University and
members of the faculty and their
wives have been invited to view the
collection at this time. The sched
ule of hours when the collection will
be open to the student body will be
announced later.
Both the collection of Venetian
Art and Chinese art will be on dis
play Sunday. These collections have
been assembled by Mr. Leventritt
during the last 30 years.
