# Halls Hold Dance This Afternoon
## 
### 
Sequoia, eating clubs, and unaffil
iated freshmen are invited to the
Toyon-Branner informal dance this
afternoon from 3 to 5:30 o'clock in
the Toyon lobby, according to Co
chairmen Bob O'Neill and Scotch
Strathairn.
"Bring a date and enjoy an after
noon of dancing, bridge, ping-pong,
or bull-sessioning," urges the chair
men. "We've a new radio combina
tion with over 600 of the smoothest
and swingiest records you've ever
heard," the chairman added.
This is the first of a series of af
ternoon dances to be held during
Spring Quarter. Refreshments will
be served.
