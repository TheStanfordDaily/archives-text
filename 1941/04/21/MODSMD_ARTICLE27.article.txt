# Board Announces Husbandry Jobs
## 
### 
Work for men trained in poultry
husbandry and in planning and
judging livestock shows is offered
in the most recent release of the
State Personnel Board.
Paying an entering salary of $180
per month, several jobs are open as
game bird breeders. These men
will work in the breeding of game
birds to replenish California's sup
ply of game birds. Anyone who has
completed a two-year course in ani
mal husbandry is eligible for the ex
amination May 10.
The position of supervisor of live
stock exhibits and fair premiums
pays a salary of $180 per month and
requires graduation from college
with specialization in animal hus
bandry.
