# Thuresson Elected To Roble Position
## 
### 
Pat Thuresson defeated three
candidates to win the vice-presi
dency of Roble Club at a special
election last week. Other nominees
running for the office were Nancy
Haskins, Loralee Smith, and Jean
Louise Naffziger.
She will take over the position va
cated by Janet Atkinson, who was
elevated to the presidency when
Katherine Larkin left school this
quarter because of ill health.
