# Thompson Co-op Holds Open House
## 
### 
Walter Thompson Co-operative
House held an open house of very
informal nature yesterday afternoon.
Approximately 75 guests were treat
ed to refreshments in the back yard.
Among those present were Dr. Ray
Lyman Wilbur, Dean Mary Yost,
and Mrs. Walter Thompson, widow
of the late Professor Thompson for
whom the house has been named.
The purpose of the function was
to make it possible for those inter
ested to become better acquainted
with the general set-up and to ob
tain ideas for possible donations of
furniture and other articles needed.
