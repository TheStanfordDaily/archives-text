# HITLER'S 52nd BIRTHDAY PARTY
## 
### 

POURING OVER MILITARY MAI'S and receiving felicitations of his Army, Navy, and Air chieftains,
Adolf Hitler spent his 52nd birthday somewhere on the Balkan front behind his victoriously advancing
troops. In his private car, above, he confers with Field Marshal General Wilhelm Keitel, left, and Field
Marshal General Walthcr Von Brauchitsch. center. The picture was flown from the front to Berlin, then
radioed to New York.
