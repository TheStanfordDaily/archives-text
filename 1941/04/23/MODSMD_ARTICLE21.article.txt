# Pre-Meds to Hear Talk by Dr. Brown
## 
### 
The Stanford Pre-medical Club
will hold its first meeting of the
quarter this evening at 7:30 o'clock.
Dr. Herbert Brown of Palo Alio will
give an illustrated lecture on
"Physiotherapy." Dr. Brown has
been very much interested in this
subject, and has some very excep
tional moving pictures.
The meeting will be held in the
Women's Clubhouse. Refreshments
will be served during the usual dis
cussion period. Anyone interested is
welcome.
