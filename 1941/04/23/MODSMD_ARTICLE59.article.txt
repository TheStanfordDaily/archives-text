# Christian Science Speech Scheduled
## 
### 
The Christian Science Organiza
tion at Stanford will hear a lecture
by Dr. Hendrik J. DeLange, of New
York City, tomorrow night at 7:30
o'clock in the auditorium of the
Women's Clubhouse. Dr. DeLanqe's
topic will be entitled "Christian Sci
ence: The Science of Existence."
All students, faculty members
and friends who are interested are
invited to be present. The time for
the lecture was selected so that
those who plan to work at the Stan
ford Convalescent Home for Labor
Day will be able to attend. There
will be no charge.
