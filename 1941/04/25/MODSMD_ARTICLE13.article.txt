# OUR CHAPLAIN.
## Dr. Trueblood Fills Pulpit. 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, Univers
sity chaplain, will deliver a sermon
at the Sunday mornign service deal
ing with the Difficulties of Faith,
concerning problems of students such
as the question of miracles. The
sermon will be particularly for stu
dents of the University. Service will
begin at 11 a.m. in Memorial Church.
The University Choir, directed by
Mr. Warren D. Allen, organist, will
sing. There will be no afternoon
organ recital because of the Pro
Arte Quartet concert in Memorial
Auditorium at 3:30.
