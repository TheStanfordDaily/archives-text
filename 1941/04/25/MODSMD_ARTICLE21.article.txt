# NEW MAGAZINE.
## 'Carillon,' Chosen As Title. 
### Mr. A. A. Hampson, Jr., '43 
At a select gathering summoned
by Mr. Clarence Burley, Editor-in-
Chief of Stanford's new literary
publication, the name of this new
magazine was decided upon.
iWth due and fitting ceremony the
receptacle, available for proposed
names by those who were interested
was opened.
Mft Maxwell Myers of the Class
of '44 was honored by having his
title, "Carillon," chosen.
