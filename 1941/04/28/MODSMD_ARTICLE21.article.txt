# Vocom Arranges Visits on Campus
## 
### 
In order that all men students who
wish to take advantage of the Men's
Vocational Committee's Interview
Series will have a chance to learn
about the interviews first-hand,
members of the Vocom will visit all
men's living groups today and to
morrow at lunch and dinner.
This is the last week 'in which
students may sign up for the inter
views. The last interviews will take
place Thursday and Friday, May 8
and 9. No applicants will be con
sidered who apply after noon next
Saturday.
