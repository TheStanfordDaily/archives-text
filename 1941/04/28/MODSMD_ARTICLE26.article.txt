# A.T.O. ELOPES
## 
### 
The marriage of Dick Mayo, A.T.O.
'41, to Miss Margaret. Parsons, Pi
Beta Phi at the University of Colo
rado, came as a surprise announce
ment yesterday afternoon. The
couple, secretly married at. Reno
last November, will live at College
Terrace during the remainder of the
year.
