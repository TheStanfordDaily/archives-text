# Blatt and Pitcher Win 'Flying Skis'
## 
### 
Cal swept over Stanford in almost
every sport last week-end, but ski
ers Bob Blatt and Bill Pitcher came
through at June Lake to snatch one
bit of glory away from the Bears. In
the slalom event of the Flying Skis
International Tournament Blatt cap
tured first position, followed by
Pitcher in the second spot, with
Harry Morgan of Cal taking third.

Blatt's time was 1:57.6. Pitcher
finished in 2:05.4. Berkeleyite Mor
gan's third place was taken with a
2:14.8.
