# Mermen Cop Third in P.C.C.
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity mermen scored
a third in Saturday's Pacific Coast
Conference Championships at Los
Angeles with 37 points. Roy Vitou
sek nabbed first in the 200-yard
breaststroke, and the Indians' 300-
yard medley relay squad captured a
first.
California won the meet with 55,
and U.C.L.A. took second at 40. Bill

McCampbell of the Bears was a
double winner in the 220- and 440-
yard freestyle events.
It was the first time in the his
tory of P.C.C. competition that the
Bears from Berkeley finished better
than third.
Coach Nort Thornton kept Vitou
sek out of his favorite event, the
50-yard, because he didn't want to
take any chances with his health.
