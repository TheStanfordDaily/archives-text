# Thompson Chosen For Head Sponsor
## 
### 
Ralph Thompson, El Toro, was
selected head Ent-ina sponsor for
next year at a sponsors' meeting
held at 10 o'clock last night in
the Encina library. liill Smith,
Fiji, was chosen the new secre
tary.

Thompson takes over the post
now held by Bob Dodge, who
conducted last evening's meeting.
John Alltucker is the outgoing
secretary.
