# Inter-Farm Yacht Competition Set
## 
### 
Tentative plans are being made for
the" intercollege championship yacht
race by Bob Allan, commodore of
the Intercollegiate Yacht Racing
Association. A meeting of everyone
interested in competing in the Stan
ford Championship races will be
held at the Nitery tomorrow at 7:15.
The same boats which were used
against California, 14 Olympic Mon
otypes will be used. The race is to
take place on a Friday or Saturday
in May on the same course over
which they sailed against Cal. The
date will be decided at the meeting
tomorrow.
