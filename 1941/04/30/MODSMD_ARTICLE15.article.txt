# Seniors Consider Orchestra for Ball
## 
### 
The senior class is considering
Freddy Martin, Phil Harris, and
Jack Teagarden as possible bands
for the Senior Ball, according to
Betty Moses and By Carlson, co
chairmen of the dance.
No decision will be made for
some time, they declared, and it is
possible that several other orches
tras will be contacted before any
contracts are signed, they said. Ray
Noble, reached by Ball committee
men, said he would not be on the
Coast in time to play for the dance.
Bob Beckham is band chairman,
Carlson stated.
