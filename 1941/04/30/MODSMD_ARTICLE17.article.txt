# Quads to Be Sold Today, Post Office
## 
### 
Quads will be sold today and Fri
day on the post office steps from 8
a.m. to 3 p.m., according to an an
nouncement made yesterday by
Business Manager Jim McKellar.
"All students should get their
Quad today, as they may not last
out the week," stated McKellar yes
terday. "This is positively the last
week in which students may have
their names embossed on the cover."
