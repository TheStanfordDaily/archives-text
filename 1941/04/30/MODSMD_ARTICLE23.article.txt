# GOVERNMENT ACCEPTS LINDY'S RESIGNATION
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 29 (UP).—
The War Department today accepted
the resignation of Col. Charles A.
Lindbergh from the Army Air Corps
Reserve shortly after Presidential
Secretory Stephen T. Early observed
the flier's action "leads me to won
der whether he is returning his dec
oration to Mr. Hitler."
