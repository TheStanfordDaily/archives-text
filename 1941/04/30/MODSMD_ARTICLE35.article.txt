# Biz School Downs Theta Chi Nine, 4-1
## 
### 
Led by the excellent pitching of
Chuck Thompson, Business School II
defeated Theta Chi 4-1 in the only
game played of the scheduled in
tramural contests yesterday. Harry
Booth hurled very pretty ball for
the losers also but two runs in the
third inning and another pair in the
seventh frame licked him.
In hardball the Phi Psis won from
Encina 2W on a forfeit. All other
intramural games were postponed.
Tomorrow's schedule:
Kappa Sigma vs. Chi Psi (hard
ball)
Betas vs. El Capitan
Delts vs. Encina 2E
