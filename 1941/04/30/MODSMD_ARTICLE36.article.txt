# 'Choosing Movies' Stanford Alumni
## 
### 
''Choosing the Movies" will be the
subject of today's Luncheon Club
talk by Mrs. Howard Ray, who is a
member of the Palo Alto Motion
Picture Council. Mrs. Ray helps to
select the pictures which the Palo
Alto Times recommends, and will
probably suggest criteria by which
to accept or reject motion pictures.
The club meets at noon in Nitery.
There is a charge of 25 cents. Every
one is welcome, acocrding to Dr.
D. Elton Trueblood, faculty spon
sor of the weekly meetings.
