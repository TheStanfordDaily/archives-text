# Indian Polo Four To Face Trojans
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity polo team leaves
today for a series of three games in
Los Angeles, two of them with
U.S.C. and one with the Riviera
Country Club.
The Stanford squad has improved
qrcatly in the past few weeks of
warm weather, and have hopes of
beating the teams from the South
land. Comparative scores of the
past few games show an improve
ment of approximately 100 per cent.
Men who will make the trip are
A. T. LaPrade, Captain Joe Smith,
Mike Cates, Stan Glikbarg, and sub
stitutes Bill Duiker and Ben Snure.
The team will return to the campus
Monday afternoon.
