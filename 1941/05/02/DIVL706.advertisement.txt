AT THE JUNIOR PROM
'Be Any Type You Wish!
If it's glamour, wear SUE BERRY'S clinging white and coral silk
jersey. This particular gown is strictly formal with a gathered
bodice and a wide coral band relieving the stark whiteness of the
dress. A knee-length cape of coral jersey makes a complete and
thoroughly unusual costume.
If it's femininity or charm, SUE BERRY features any number of
cool, crisp linen and cotton formals. And they are really different!
Outstanding is a colorful black linen with brilliant yellow and green
flowers on a cocoa-brown background. The halter neck and fitted
waist make it equally attractive without the matching jacket.
