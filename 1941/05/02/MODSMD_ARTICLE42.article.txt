# S.J. State Group To Repeat Play
## 
### 
Hugh Gillis and James Clancy, San
Jose State directors who are grad
uate students in the Department of
English and Drama at Stanford,
have put on a production of "Ham
let" which is drawing better than
most popular hits.
Consequently, the cast is putting
on extra performances Saturday and
Sunday evenings.
Tickets are 50 cents. To be sure of
seats patrons should call Ballard
7800 for reservations. To reach the
Little Theater at State College, drive
down Bayshore to the lighted sign,
Fourth Street; continue through San
Jose on that street past the college
quadrangle, and at the far corner
turn left. The entrance to the the
ater will be floodlighted to guide
visitors.
