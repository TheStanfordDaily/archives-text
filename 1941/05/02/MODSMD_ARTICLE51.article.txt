# RAINIERS IN 7-2 WIN
## 
### 
SEATTLE. May 1 (UP).—The Se
attle Rainiers tonight belted two
San Diego pitchers to take a 7-2
victory over the Padres. Johnson
allowed eight hits for the win, while
his teammates reached Olsen and
Humphrey for 12 bingles.
