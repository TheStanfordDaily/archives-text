# Poloists to Meet S.C., Riviera Club
## 
### 
With its chances of victory much
better than they were in the last
encounter between the two teams,
Stanford's varsity polo team left for
Los Angeles yesterday to play three
games in the Southland.
The horsemen will meet Univer
sity of Southern Cailfornia this af
ternoon and tomorrow, and will play
a match with the Riviera Countiy
Club on Sunday afternoon, return
ing to the campus early Monday.
Men who made the trip south and
who are almost all certain of see
ing action arc Captain Joe Smith,
A. T. LaPrade, Mike Gates, Stan
"Killer" Glikbarg, Bill Druker, and
Ben Snure.
