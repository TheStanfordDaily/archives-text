# Bookstore Rebate Slips Due May 15
## 
### 
Students who wish to share in the
profits of the Stanford Bookstore
must turn in their rebate slips at i
the bookstore office by May 15. All
persons leaving school before this
time may enclose their slips in en
velopes and leave them at the
bookstore, and they will share in
the rebate.
The amount of the rebate has not
as yet been determined, but it will
be announced as soon as the audi
tor's reports are complete. In order
to receive a rebate, the orange slips
which have been given with each
purchase from the bookstore must ,
be presented.
