# Pi Lambda Theta Initiates 9 Women
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, national educa
tion honorary society for women, ini
tiated nine new members Wednes
day afternoon. The new members
include: Jeanette Armentrout, Jose
phine Bartholomew, Mrs. Joel Bar
riman, Belva Budge, Ethel Mae
Davis, Betty Johnson, Eleanor Mc-
Donnel, Mary June Montgomery,
and Mrs. Nellie Starrett.

Following the initiation, a banquet
was held in the Union dining room
at which Mrs. Hawkes, formerly
Dean of Women at George Wash
ington University, spoke.
