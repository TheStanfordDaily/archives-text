# WORKERS KILLED
## 
### 
MARTINEZ, May 4 (UP).—'Three
Filipino asparagus workers, includ
ing a Stockton labor contractor,
were drowned today when their
speeding car plunged off the end of
a Clifton Court ferry into a San
Joaquin delta slough.
