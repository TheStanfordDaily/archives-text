# RIGGS DOWNS TALBERT
## 
###

(UP). —Bobby Riggs, former national
tennis champion from Chicago and j
Clinton, S.C., today downed Billy j
Talbert, of Cincinnati, 7-5, 3-6. 6-4, I
6-4 in finals of the Tennessee Valley 1
Invitation Tournament.
