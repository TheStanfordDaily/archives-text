# Applications Due Today for A.B.
## 
### 
Those seniors who do not file
applications for graduation today
will not get their A.B. degrees in
June, according to an announce
ment from the Registrar's office.
"This is absolutely the last day
that graduation blanks will he
given out," declared Registrar J. P.
Mitchell. "From now on they will
be granted only on petition. It
will be taken for granted that
those who do not file their appli
cations do not wish to graduate."
Graduation blanks may be ob
tained at the information window
in the Administration Building.
