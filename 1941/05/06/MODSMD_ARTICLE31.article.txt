# Stanford Bookstore Plans Sunday Picnic
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Co-oper
ative Book Exchange and their
guests are planning a picnic next
Sunday at Half Moon Bay. Those
planning to attend must sign up at
the Co-op Book Exchange before
Friday. The price is 35 cents. The
party will leave the Nitery at 11
a.m. on Sunday.

The Junior Prom of 1901 con
tained "twenty dances and one
'grab'."
