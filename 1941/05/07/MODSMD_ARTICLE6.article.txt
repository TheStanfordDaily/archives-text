# Luncheon Club To Hear Turner
## 
### 
Bill Turner, Pacific Coast College
representative at a recent meeting
with President Roosevelt, will speak
at the Luncheon Club today at 12
o'clock in the Nitery.
Turner's talk, "The Menace of
Lindbergh?" will be based on his
recent' observations during his trip
in the East. Luncheon Club is open
to all students. The price has been
raised from 25 to 30 cents.
