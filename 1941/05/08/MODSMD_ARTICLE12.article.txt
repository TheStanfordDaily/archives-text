# Seniors Are Urged To Order Programs
## 
### 
Senior program sales are progress
ing according to reports from the
A.S.S.U. office and Barbara Sweet
land, chairman of the program
committee. All seniors who have not
yet obtained their programs are
urged to get them as soon as pos
sible. They may see a sample at
the A.S.S.U. office.
The programs have been specially
designed in Stanford colors and
decorated with familiar scenes of the
Quad and the names of all the grad
uates, so the seniors may keep them
as souvenirs of their undergraduate
days on the Farm.
