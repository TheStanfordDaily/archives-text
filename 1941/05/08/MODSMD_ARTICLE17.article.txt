# Roble Hall Formal Set for May 23
## 
### 
Being held in Roble Hall itself for
the first time this year, the Roble
Formal is scheduled for May 23
from 9:30 to 1 o'clock. The lobbies
and dining rooms will be cleared for
dancing, for which Hal Moreno's or
chestra will provide the music.
Committee heads planning the af
fair under the direction of Helen
Schwab, general chairman, arc Hel
en Savory, orchestra; Ann Simons,
clean-up; Rosemary Cross, recep
tion; Betty Mae Hale, arrangements;
Janet Marvin, programs; Eleanor
Day, decorations; Diane Holmes,
patrons; and June Ellis, publicity
and tickets.
