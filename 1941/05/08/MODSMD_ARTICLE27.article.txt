# Evelyn Cary Wins Fashion Fellowship
## 
### 
Evelyn Cary, Ventura, '41, won
honorable mention in the annual fel
lowship awards of the Tobe-Coburn
School for Fashion Careers in New
York, according to an announce
ment received last week.
The fashion fellowships, each of
which offers a year's tuition at the
school, were chosen from senior ap
plicants from 42 different colleges
and universities. Miss Cary is one
of the ten to whom honorable men
tion was awarded.
The Tobe-Coburn trains young
women who have had two or more
years of college for executive posi
tions in the fashion field.
