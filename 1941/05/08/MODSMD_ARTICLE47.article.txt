# GRID MENTOR NAMED
## 
### 
GRANTS PASS. Ore.. May 7 (UP).
—George Erickson, former Willam
ette University athlete, was today
named head football coach of Grants
Pass High School. Erickson replaces
Loren Tuttlf, who has been called
for military service.
