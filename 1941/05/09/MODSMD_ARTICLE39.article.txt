## CHUCK SMITH, Sports Editor MICKEY DIGS OUT OF TRAP 
### 

THIS PICTURE of Mickey Rooney might remind you of a Kansas
dust storm, but it is actually only an action-shot of the movie cyclone
blasting his way out of a pesky sand trap on the Catalina Island, Cal.,
golf course. Young Rooney is rated a good natural golfer.
