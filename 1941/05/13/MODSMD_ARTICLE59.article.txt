# Free Engineering Courses Offered
## 
### 
The California Institute of Tech
nology yesterday announced four
full-time summer technical courses
to be given as part of the Engineer
ing Defense Training Program of the
United States Office of Education.
Designed to fit men for positions
in defense industry, the courses will
be given free to those who qualify
for entrance. Among the courses
offered are: Aeronautical Engineer
ing, Basic Electrical Circuits and
Machinery, Explosives, and Produc
tion Engineering.
