# BAY AREA STRIKES TIE UP SHIPS
## 
### 

THOUSANDS OF WORKERS gather outside the gates of the Bethlehem shipbuilding plant in San Fran
cisco. refusing to pass a picket line of AFL and CIO machinist strikers in a wage dispute. Strikers num
ber 1700 and work in 11 San Francisco Bay shipyards, holding an estimated 500 million dollars of contracts
for new Navy and merchant vessels and repair work is affected.
