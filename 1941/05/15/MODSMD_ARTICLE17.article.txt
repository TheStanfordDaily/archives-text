# Women's Journalism Fraternity Pledges 8
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women's hon
orary journalistic fraternity, pledged
eight new members for the coming
year at a meeting held Monday in
Naranja social room at Lagunita
Court.
New members include Martha
Deane, Lois Emery, Rosemarie Mul
lany. Claudia Nettles, Jean Now
ell, Marilyn Reichard, Virginia Si.sk,
and Harva Sprager. All women are
juniors and hold high journalism av
erages. Formal initiation will take
place Wednesday evening at the
Benjamin Franklin Hotel in San
Mateo.
