# Japan Conference Plans Completed
## 
### 
With the announcement Monday
of the seven delegates to represent
Stanford at the eighth American-
Japan Student Conference, plans
have been virtually completed.
The conference will be held on the
University of Washington campus at
Seattle, Wash., late this summer from
August 3 to 13. During the ten-day
discussion the delegates will attempt
to better understand the various
problems which beset each country.
Approximately 50 Japanese stu
dents are making the trip from
Japan while about 100 students from
American universities and colleges
will journey to Seattle for the con
vention.
