# Carillon Receives 78 Manuscripts
## 
### 
Seventy-eight manuscripts con
sisting of poems, essays, and short
stories were submitted to the new
literary magazine, Carillon, before
yesterday's deadline.
Five members of the editorial
board of the magazine will meet to
day in their new offices in Wood
pecker Lodge to choose the 12 ar
ticles which are to be published.
The cover of the magazine is now
being designed. It will come out
May 28 and will sell for 25 cents.
