# Professor Mears Returns to Farm
## 
### 
Returning from the third general
assembly of the Pan American In
stitute of Geography and History
held at Lima, Peru, Professor Eliot
G. Mears arrived at Stanford yes
terday.
Dr. Mears was an official delegate
of the United States to the confer
ence held in late March and early
April. He returned to the U. S. on
April 24, arriving at New York on
the S. S. Santa Elena.
