# Barbecue Planned By Spanish Group
## 
### 
Everyone interested in the Span
ish language or customs is invited
to attend the "asado" or steak bar
becue being held Saturday by Sigma
Delta Pi, Spanish honor society.
Reservations may be made for 75
cents at the department office, and
those wishing rides can get them on
the English Corner at 2 o'clock Sat
urday.
Being held at Homestead Picnic
Park near Mountain View, the af
fair will feature Spanish food and
games. Tortillas will be served in
addition to steak. Entertainment will
consist of "pinata," the most popular
Spanish game, and dancing.
