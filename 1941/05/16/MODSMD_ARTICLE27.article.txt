# Lagunita Court Picks Officers
## 
### 
Anne Bossinger, '42, was elected
president of Lagunita in yesterday's
elections for Lagunita Court officers.
Christine Moody, '43, was elected
first vice-president; Bery Robison,
'43, second vice-president; and
Dorothea Paulman, '43, chairman of
the Committee of Eight.
The new officers will take on their
duties next fall quarter. They will
replace the present officers, Jane
Harrington, president; Anne Bossi
ger, first vice-president; Peggy Suss
man, second vice-president; and
Winifred Eaton, chairman of the
Committee of Eight.
