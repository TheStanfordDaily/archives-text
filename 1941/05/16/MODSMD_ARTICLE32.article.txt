# Faith Groups Plan Services
## 
### 
Senior members of the Roger Wil
liams Club will conduct a student
worship service at 7:30 p.m. in the
Baptist Church in Palo Alto Sunday
evening.
Following the service a fireside
meeting will be held at 8:30 p.m. at
the Theta Xi house at which color
movies of Olympic National Park in
Washington will be shown.
Members of the Stanford Episco
pal Club will meet at 6 p.m. Sun
day in the parish house of All Saints
Episcopal Church in Palo Alto. The
Rev. Livingstone Porter, Ph.D., will
speak to the group on University
Life in Continental Europe. An eve
ning vesper service will be held at
7:30 o'clock following the meeting.
Rev. James R. Davidson, Jr., will
preach at the 11 a.m. service and
morning prayer.
