# The 						Stanford Daily SPORTS
## CHUCK SMITH, Sports Editor KISSELBURGH BECOMES CADET 
### 

JIM KISSELBURGH, last year's all-Coast fullback from Oregon State,
receives the saber of cadet captaincy of the Air Corps Training De
tachment at Ryan School of Aeronautics, San Diego. When he finishes
the course, he'll be ready for Randolph Field.
