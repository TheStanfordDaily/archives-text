# Daisy Day Wins Magazine Post
## 
### 
Miss Daisy Day has been ap
pointed to the College Board of the
August guest issue of Mademoiselle,
it was revealed Friday.

Miss Day is one of the fifteen girls
selected by Mademoiselle to serve
on the board. Her duties will be
to write articles, and to select and
model the clothes to be used in the
college issue.

"It probably won't be all that,"
Miss Day explains. "They haven't
sent me any definite information as
yet, so I don't know exactly what I
will do."
