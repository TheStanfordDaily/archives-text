# Chronicle Editor To Tell War Views
## 
### 
Drawing from observations and
experiences during his recent tour
of war-torn Europe, Paul C. Smith,
editor of the San Francisco Chron
icle, will speak on the international
situation tomorrow night in the Palo
Alto High School auditorium.
No admission will be charged for
the affair, which is to begin at 8
o'clock. The meeting is being spon
sored by no special organization, but
140 prominent citizens of the Penin
sula have been invited to sit on the
speaker's platform.
