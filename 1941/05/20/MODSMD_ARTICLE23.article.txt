# RUSTLERS AT WORK
## 
### 
REEDSPORT, Ore., May 19 (UP)
- Three more cattle disappeared at
the hands of "butcher-on-the-spot"
rustlers the past week, authorities
reported today. The Western Doug
las County Livestock Protective As
sociation announced it would post
a reward for the rustlers, who kill
and butcher their prey wherever
they find it.
