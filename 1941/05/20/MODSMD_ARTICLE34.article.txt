# Tomorrow Last Day To Cancel Courses
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day for filing
petitions to drop courses this quar
ter, according to announcement from
the Registrar's office. "The petitions
must reach the Board by Thursday
morning, as the Board meets then,"
declared Registrar J, P. Mitchell,
Petitions may be filed at the informa
tion window of the Registrar's office.
