# International Club To Hear Consul
## 
### 
Juan Jose Martinez Lacaryo, Nica
raguan consul, will speak tomorrow
night at 8 o'clock for the Interna
tional Club. Mr. Lacaryo, promi
nent in diplomatic circles, is the old
est South American consul.
He will speak informally on the
formation of a good neighbor policy
between the Americas, and the ad
visability of exchange students. All
those interested are invited to at
tend. Mr. Lacaryo will answer
questions from the floor.
The meeting will be held at the
home of Mrs. Thomas, 365 Lincoln
Avenue. Cars will leave the Nitery
at 7:30 o'coek.
