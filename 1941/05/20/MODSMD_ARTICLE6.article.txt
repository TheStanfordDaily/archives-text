on the Law Steps" has brought $2,783 in gifts and pledges from friends
and alumni to the Stanford law school this year through the Stanford

A thorough campaign for presentation of the law school's appeal has

been made by a committee of Stan-<
ford lawyers headed by Ray C.
Wakefield, '16. When in the midst of

the Federal Communications Com

taken by Frederick E. Supple, '17,
of San Francisco.

the appeal, and as a result of the
response to date — midway in the
year's activity — several new law
scholarships for next year's students
are in prospect.
