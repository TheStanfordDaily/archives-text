# Dr. Shirley Speaks On Child Guidance
## 
### 
In a lecture at the Stanford Med
ical School, Dr. Hale F. Shirley de
clared last Friday night that Amer
ican communities must provide for
the emotional, as well as the phys
ical and educational needs of chil
dren to attain a well balanced and
socially adequate society.
Dr. Shirley, head of the child
psychiatry unit at the medical
school, stated in his talk on '"Chang
ing Attitudes in Child Guidance"
that "it is generally recognized that
foundations for personality devel
opment are laid in childhood.''
