# Flying Club Elects Balhaus, Hopkins
## 
### 
In a Monday meeting of the Stan
ford Flying Club, the members that
were present elected the officers for
the next year. Bill Balhaus was
elected president, and Thayer Hop
kins was elected vice-president.
Fred Turnbull will replace Helen
Ford as secretary.
Retiring President Chuck Sharp
made an announcement pertaining
to the club's activities for the rest
of the quarter, and made a sugges
tion that the club investigate the
purchase of a used airplane.
