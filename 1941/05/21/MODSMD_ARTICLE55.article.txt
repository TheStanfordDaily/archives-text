# Bulletin Board

PRE-MED CLUB holds its last
meeting of the quarter at the
Women's Clubhouse at 7:30 to
night. Dr. Saier will speak on
"Clinical Allergies." Officers will
be elected for next year.
INTERNATIONAL CLUB meets to
night at the home of Mrs. Thomas,
365 Lincoln Avenue. The Nicaraguan
Consul, Mr. Lacayo, will speak.
Cars leave the Nitery at 7:30 o'-
clock.
FROSH - SOPII DEBATE will be
held at the regular meeting of
Luncheon Club in the Nitery at
noon today.
FOREIGN TRADE AND SERVICE
CLUB will meet tonight at 7:30 in
the West Memorial Library. All
interested are invited.
