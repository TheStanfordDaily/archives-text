# Seniors to Publish Names, Addresses
## 
### 
A small booklet containing the
names and home addresses of all
seniors graduating next month will
be published by the senior class, it
was announced yesterday by Presi
dent Art Gore.
At the same time Gore requested
any seniors who do not expect to
graduate but who desire their names
listed in the publication to notify
him immediately. The booklet will
be put on sale for members of the
senior class during the second week
in June, Gore said.
