# New Lost-Found Lists 65 Articles
## 
### 
Sixty-five articles were turned in
to Stanford's newly created lost and
found department in Room 15 of the
Old Union in the Co-op Bookstore
in the last week and two days.
Fifteen keys, ranging from lab to
Packard, and 16 books lead the list.
Here is the complete list of recent |
articles turned in awaiting claiming J
by Farm students:
Nine pens, 2 eversharp pencils, (>
handkerchiefs, scarf, pair pigskin
gloves, 2 rings, belt buckle, 2 pins,
tie clasp, compact, 3 notebooks, 7
pair glasses, and 3 purses.
Only six states give Stanford more
students than Hawaii.
