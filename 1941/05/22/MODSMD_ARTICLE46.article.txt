# BOLLES DIES
## 
### 
NEW LONDON, Conn., May 21
(UP). —Reuben C. Bolles, 78. the man
who for 42 years has held boats
straight at the starting line of Yale-
Harvard regattas, died here yester
day. He began his work in 18D8.
