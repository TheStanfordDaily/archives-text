# Chicago White Sox Win Over Phillies, 7-3
## 
### 
CHICAGO, May 21 (UP).—The
Chicago White Sox triumphed over
the Philadelphia Pnillies in a home
game here today 7-3. The Phillies
| led until the fifth inning when the
I White Sox staged a five-run rally.
