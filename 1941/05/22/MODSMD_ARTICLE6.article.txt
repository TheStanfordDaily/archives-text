# Local Union Refuses A.F.L. Membership
## 
### 
The International Typographical
Union local 521 of Palo Alto yester
day voted unanimously not to reaffil
iate with the A. F. of L. The refer
endum was part of a national poll
conducted in typigraphical unions
throughout the United States and
Canada. Clarence Sebring, an em
ployee of the Stanford University
Press, was elected to act as delegate
to a convention in Vancouver, 8.C.,
this fall.
