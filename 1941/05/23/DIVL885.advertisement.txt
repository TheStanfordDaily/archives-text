OLD GRADS . . .
DO YOU REMEMBER WHEN?
Do you remember when about one-third
of your meals consisted of a
Peninsula Creamery Milkshake 7
It was a full meal, too!
Today, as for the past 15 years, Stanford
students are making a meal of that
"Famous Milkshake."
And for the years to come we hope to
have the pleasure of serving the Stanford
students a . . .
Sandwich and a Shake
' Home of the F.imous Milkshake"
