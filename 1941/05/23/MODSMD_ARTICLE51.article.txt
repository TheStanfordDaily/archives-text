# 'Hayfever' to Be Given Again Monday Monday Night
## 
### 
For the first time in the history of
the Stanford Players, a performance
presented for a four-night run in
the Little Theater was sold out com
pletely even before the play opened.
That play is Noel Coward's "Hay
fever," the gay modern comedy
which is now playing in the Little
Theater. A spocial extra perform
ance of the play will be given at 8:15
Monday evening. The show ends
promptly at 10:10 p.m.
