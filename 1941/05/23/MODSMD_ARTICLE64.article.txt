# Leventritt Art Viewed by 2000
## 
### 
The Mortimer C. Leventritt Col
lection of Chinese and Venetian Art
which was donated by Mr. Leventritt
on the occasion of Stanford's 50th
Anniversary has been viewed by ap
proximately two thousand people,
custodians of the gallery announced.
A new doorway to the rear rooms
of the gallery has recently been con
structed. This makes the back class
rooms accessible from both sides of
the building. The 50th Anniversary
Celebration Committee will use these
rooms as offices and for press recep
tions during the celebration weeks
in June.
The Art Gallery is open daily in
cluding Sunday to the public from
10 to 5 o'clock. There is no admis
sion charge.
