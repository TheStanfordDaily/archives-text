# BIG CONVOY CROSSES
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 22 (UP). —Col-
lier's Weekly reported today that
the largest ocean convoy in history
—50 ships loaded with an estimated
$100,000,000 worth of supplies had
crossed from Canada to England
without the loss of "so much as a
bit of paint."
Only one American in eight claims
to know the words of "The Star
Spangled Banner."
