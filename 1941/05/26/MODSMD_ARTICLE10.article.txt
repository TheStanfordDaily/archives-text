# Jessup Tries Surf Rescue
## President Foiled By Carmel Undertow 
### 
A.S.S.U. President Bruce Jessup
narrowly escaped drowning at Car
mel Saturday when he attempted to
rescue another bather who had been
caught in the undertow.
Sergeant John W. Rude of Fort
Old became exhausted battling a
strong undertow and called for help.
Jessup and Marry J. Martin, 24, of
Mare Island swam out to help but,
according to a United Press report,
"found themselves also in difficulty."
Finally Police Officer Frank L.
Haw swam through the heavy surf
with a rope and rescued all three.
Although severely exhausted, Jes
sup was not seriously injured, ac
cording to reports received here
yesterday.
