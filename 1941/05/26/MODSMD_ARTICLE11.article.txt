# Legislature Works On Six Measures
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, May 25 (UP).—
Aiming for a June 6 adjournment,
the 54th California Legislature will
speed up consideration tomorrow
of a half-dozen controversial issues,
including the budget, reapportion
ment and relief.
A free conference committee —
dominated by forces hostile to Gov
ernor Culbert Olson—is expected to
begin work on the amended $512,-
733,550 budget bill, and reapportion
ment is set for a special order of
business in the Senate.
