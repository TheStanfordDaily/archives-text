# Council to Elect Next Year's Head
## 
### 
Men's Council will elect its next
year's chairman at its meeting next
Monday. Jess Hazlitt, Warren Berl,
and Stan Owen are up for the office.
The outgoing head of Men's
Council is Hugh O'Donnell and the
outgoing secretary is Jerry Mayer.
No other business was transacted at
yesterday's meeting.
