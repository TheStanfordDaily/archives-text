# Frosh Splashers. Sink Junior Squad
## 
### 
No. 7—FROSH SPLASHERS
In the first of the annual inter
class water polo games the fresh
men defeated the juniors 10-7. High
point men for the freshmen were
Grant Cullcy and Bob Hind. John
Pollock was high man for the jun
iors with two scores.
Today the sophomores meet the
seniors at 4:30 at Encina Pools. The
winner will play the frosh tomor
row.
OAKLAND. May 26 (UP).—Larry
Brazil, San Francisco professional,
Ualified for the 45th annual U.S.
Open Championships today after
two competitors with better scores
withdrew.
