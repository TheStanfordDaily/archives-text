# MEN RETURN TO JOBS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, May 2G (UP).
In ever-increasing numbers,
shipbuilding workers returned to
their jobs at strike-bound San
Francisco Bay area shipyards today
in response to a plea from high AFL
officials to defy AFL-CIO machin
ists' picket lines.

The Class of 2401 will Ix 3 the last
class to have a flagstone put in the
walk in front of the Chapel under
the present arrangement.
