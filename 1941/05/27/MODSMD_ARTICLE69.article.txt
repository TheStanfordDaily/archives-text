# Mariposa Elects Spalding President
## 
### 
Deborah Spalding was elected
yesterday to the presidency of
Mariposa. She will replace Audrey
Krause. Phyllis Marchant, retiring
treasurer, was chosen secretary to
replace Betty Lee Louhnow, and
Margaret Sharrah was elected treas
urer.
Union residence will hold elec
tions today, and the other women's
living groups next Monday.
