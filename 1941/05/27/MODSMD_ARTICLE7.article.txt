# Farmer Stanfordites Married in Las Vegas
## 
### 
Will Rogers, Jr., and Collier Con
nell, both former Stanford students,
left Beverley Hills for Las Vegas
yesterday after telling friends they
would be married. Miss Connell is
the niece of Dr. Karl Connell, New
York surgeon. Rogers, son of the
late humorist, is publisher of the
Beverley Hills citizen.
