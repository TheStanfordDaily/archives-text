# Institute to Hear Harlow and Fagan
## 
### 
Professors Rex F. Harlow and El
mer Fagan will be speakers at the
fourth annual institute of govern
ment at Sacramento Junior College,
June 13 and 14, sponsored by the
Sacramento chapter of the Califor
nia State Employees Association.
Professor Harlow, executive di
rector of the American Council of
Public Relations and professor of
public relations, will speak at a
luncheon Saturday at which Gov.
Culbert Olson will be guest of
honor. At one of the section meet
ings Professor Fagan will present a
paper on "How to Finance the Na
tional Defense Program."
