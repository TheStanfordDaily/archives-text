# HOOD AVENGED AS BRITISH SINK BISMARCK
## 
### 

HERE'S THE 42,100-TON 11.M.5. HOOD, largest ware-raft in the world, whose sinking several days ago by the German ship Bismarck was
avenged when planes from the Ark Royal and a torpedo discharged from the British boat sent the Bismarck to Davy Jones locker.
