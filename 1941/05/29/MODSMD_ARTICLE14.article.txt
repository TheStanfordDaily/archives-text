# BLACKLIST SHIP SAILS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, May 28 (UP).
—The Nissin Maru, a former Japa
nese whaling vessel, sails tomorrow
from Port Costa with a cargo of oil
for Japan although it recently was
blacklisted by the British on suspi
cion of refueling Nazi raiders in the
Pacific. The 16,764-ton ship is one
of the largest oil carriers in the Pa
cific.
