# Senior Booklet Names Due This Afternoon
## 
### 
Deadline for entry of names in the
senior class address booklet is 3
p.m. today, according to Senior
Class President Art Gore.
Names and addresses have been
taken from graduation application
cards in the Registrar's office, Gore
said, and any changes or additions
should bo made immediately at the
A.S.S.U. office.
