# Jean Nowell Wins Theta Sig Award
## 
### 
Jean Nowell, writer of History,
1941, and junior in the Division of
Journalism, was awarded the Theta
Sigma Phi scholarship given an
nually by the San Francisco alum
nae chapter of the women's hon
orary journalism sorority.
The award of $25 was established
! this year for a woman who has dis
tinguished herself in any form of
campus journalism. It will be given
in alternate years to women students
at Stanford and the University of
California.
Miss Nowell was review editor
and head copy editor on The Stan
ford Daily before becoming writer
of History.
