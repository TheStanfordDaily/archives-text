# Professors Will Serve in Defense
## 
### 
Two members of the mechanical
engineering faculty will leave Stan
ford at the end of Spring Quarter
for industries faced with a shortage
of trained men.
Professor U. A. Patchett will spend
his next year's sabbatical leave at
the experimental laboratories of the
Chrysler Corporation which is en
gaged in defense contracts. Dr. A. L.
London will spend the summer in
the laboratories of the Pratt and
Whitney airplane motor company
engaging in transfer heat research.
