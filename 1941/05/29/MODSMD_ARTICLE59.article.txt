# Alumnae Secure Scholarship Fund
## 
### 
Proceeds from a scholarship
bridge luncheon given by the Stan
ford Women's Club will provide a
year on the Farm for some San
Francisco girl. The luncheon was
held last Tuesday at the Woodside
estate of Mrs. John L. Daehl.
Miss Lucile Otto is chairman of
the scholarship committee. Among
those having table reservations were
Mesdames Herbert C. Hoover, Ray
Lyman Wilbur, Frank. H. Walker,
Almon E. Roth, John T. Nourse,
M. C, Sloss, Joseph D. Grant, and
Dean Mary Yost.
