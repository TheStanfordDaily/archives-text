# EX-EDITOR ENGAGED
## 
### 
James Stone, editor of The Daily
in the fall of 1938, and Margaret
Hale, Stanford graduate, announced
their engagement last Sunday.
Stone, now studying for his doctor's
degree at Yale, won his Phi Beta
Kappa key while at Stanford.

The United States exported to
Britain for the first year of World
War II $779,784,000 worth of goods.
