# Daily To Continue Through Summer
## 
### 
Appearing twice a week in four
tabloid size pages, The Stanford
Daily will continue publishing dur
ing the summer session. Mr. George
R. Rinehart, acting assistant profes
sor of journalism, will supervise the
work.
Students registered in journalism
courses for the summer quarter will
compose the staff of the paper, which
will be a class project. It has been
tentatively planned to distribute the
first issue on June 23, Registration
Day, to familiarize summer students
with the campus. Publication will
cease late in August.
