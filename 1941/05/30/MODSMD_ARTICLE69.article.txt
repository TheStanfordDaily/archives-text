# Ski Club Chooses Smith New Head
## 
### 
Walter Smith, Alpha Delt junior,
was elected president of the Ski
Club at the last meeting of that
group yesterday. Smith succeeds
Dave Nottage.
Other officers elected yesterday
are Virginia Dean, women's vice
president; Dave Doud, men's vice
president; Amy Greenwell, secre
tary; Bobby Blatt, treasurer.
