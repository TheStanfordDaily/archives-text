# Wilburs at Home; Mears Receive Students
## 
###

bur will be at home to faculty
and summer quarter students in
the President's House on Lagu
nita Knoll at 8 o'clock this
evening.

Mears have invited students to
meet the faculty in the women's
gymnasium Saturday, from 4 to
