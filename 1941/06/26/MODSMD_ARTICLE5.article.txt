# Cal Student Wins ASCAP Fellowship
## 
### 
Dr. Hubert Heffner, head of the
Department of Speech and Drama,
announced recently the results of
the competition for the second
ASCAP fellowship competition for
composers and authors of college
musical plays. Melville Harold
Swanton of San Mateo, who attends
the University of California, won the
$720 Fellowship fund.
