# Dodds Discusses Basis of Tragedy
## 
### 
Discussing the philosophy of
tragedy and stressing a basic defi
nition of the term, Dr. John Dodds
of Stanford's English department
spoke last night on the first of a
seven-week lecture series spon
sored by the Dramatists' Alliance.
Dr. Dodds' subject was "The En
joyment of Tragedy."
Tragedy being the general sub
ject of the summer series spon
sored by the Dramatists' Alliance,
the various forms of this dramatic
type will be discussed by the resi
dent and visiting professors of the
School of Letters. The lectures will
be given every Monday in the lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse at 8
p.m.
