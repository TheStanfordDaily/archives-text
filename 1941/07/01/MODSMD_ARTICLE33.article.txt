# Social Education Workshop Opens
## 
### 
The Social Education Workshop
opened for its third summer yes
terday in the Education Building of
Stanford University. Here, regional
experimenters in education check
and estimate their year's practical
work.
The workshop is an integral part
of a five-year education project
conceived, planned and directed by
Professors Grayson N. Kefauver
and I. James Quillen of Stanford.
Outstanding teachers, principals, and
school supervisors throughout the*
western states have been selected to
experiment in their local schools
with new education methods dur
ing the academic year. Each summer
they meet at Stanford University to
appraise their year's work.
