# Wilbur Family to Hold Reunion on July 4
## 
### 
The summer home of the Ray Ly
man Wilburs, the Cedars, near Soda
Springs, will be the scene of a fam
ily reunion over the week-end of the
Fourth. Dr. and Mrs. Ray Lyman
Wilbur, Mrs. Blake Wilbur with her
two sons and two daughters, Dr. and
Mrs. Leonard Ely, the Wilburs' son
in-law and daughter, with their two
sons and two daughters, are already
there. Dr. Blake Wilbur will join
the group later in the week.
