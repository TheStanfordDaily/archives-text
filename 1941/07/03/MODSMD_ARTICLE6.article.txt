# Research Bequest Left to Stanford
## 
### 
A bequest of $50,000 has been left
to Stanford for the creation of the
Louis S. Haas fund for research in
child psychology, it was revealed
yesterday when the will of Mr. Haas,
San Francisco stock broker and
philanthropist who died Friday, was
filed for probate.
The grant consists of 170 shares of
Wells Fargo Bank and Union Trust
Company stock. Beatrice Lantz, who
received her M.A. in education from
Stanford in 1922, will receive the in
come from this stock to continue her
work in child psychology.
