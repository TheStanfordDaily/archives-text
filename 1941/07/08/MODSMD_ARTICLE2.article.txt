# Pro Arte Quartet To Play Brahms
## 
### 
The Pro Arte Quartet of the Uni
versity of Wisconsin will present the
fourth in a series of eight concerts
of the chamber music of Johannes
Brahms Thursday, July 10, at 8 p.m.
in the Memorial Hall. The quartet is
featuring Gunar Johansen, piano,
Ferenc Molnar, viola, and William
Dehe, violoncello. Admission is free
and the public is invited.
The program for the evening will
be as follows: Quintet in F Major
for two violins, two violas, and vio
lincello; Quartet in A Major for
violin, viola, violoncello, and piano;
Sextet in G Major for two violins,
two violas, and two violoncellos.
