# Schroeder Bows In Semi-finals
## 
### 
Ted Schroeder, Stanford senior
and national doubles champion, lost
his semi-final match of the Eastern
Clay Court Tennis Championships
to Gene Mako, former world's No. 1
doubles player, 6-1, 3-6, 6-2, 3-6,
6-3 at New York Sunday.
However, Schroeder teamed with
Jack Kramer to go into the doubles
final, running through Frank Ko
vacs and Jim Crosby, 6-2, 6-4, 8-6.
They met Frank Parker and Charles
Mattman in the finals.
