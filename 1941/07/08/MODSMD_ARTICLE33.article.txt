# Stanford Dames To Hold Meetings
## 
### 
Sewing group of the Stanford
Dames will meet at the home of Mrs.
Rex Harlow, Guinda Street, today at
2 p.m. All wives of men attending
Stanford summer session are invited.
On the evening of July 9 an in
formal get-together will be held at
the home of Mrs. John C. Almack,
Alvarado Row, for the women who
are unable to attend the afternoon
meetings. Those planning to attend
are asked to let Mrs. Almack know
ahead of time. The bridge group
will hold their meet at 2 p.m. Thurs
day, at the Women's Clubhouse.
