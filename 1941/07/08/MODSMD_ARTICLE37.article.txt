# Dr. Harsh Speaks At Women's Club
## 
### 
Comparing Greek tragedy and
modern drama, Dr. Philip Harsh,
associate professor of classics, spoke
before the Dramatists' Alliance in
the Women's Clubhouse last night.
The next speaker will be James
Vincent Cunningham, instructor in
English during the regular academic
year, who will talk on the basis of
the moral struggle in Shakespeare's
tragedies next Monday evening.
