# Net Men Advance In Oregon Tourney
## 
### 
Emery Neale and Jack Gurley, No.
1 and No. 5 men on Stanford's var
sity tennis team, advanced to the
third round of the Oregon State
Tennis Championships at Portland
yesterday without any difficulty.
Gurley has been seeded third in
the tournament which has seen Nick
Carter, first seeded man, put out al
ready. Neale, the home town boy,
did not enter until late and there
fore was not seeded, although he is
favored to win the title.
