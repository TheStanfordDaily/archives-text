# Students Earn School Expenses
## 
### 
Students in United States colleges,
universities, and professional schools
help meet their expenses through
scholarships, loans, or wages for part
time work totaling more than $50,-
000,000 a year, according to "Finan
cial Aids for College Students," a
bulletin just published by the U.S.
Office of Education.
The report is made up from fig
ures received by the National Youth
Administration from colleges and
universities co-operating in its stu
dent aid program and shows how
American institutions help worthy
students gain a college education. It
includes 1,387 institutions of higher
education listed in the Educational
Directory of the U.S. Office of Edu
cation.
