# Track Stars Tour West Indian Islands
## 
### 
Several American track stars are
at present touring the West Indies
competing in exhibition meets with
the local prides. The trip is under

the direction of Dan Ferris, secre
tary of the A.A.U.
Although no Stanford men are on
the team, most of the members
competed in the N.C.A.A. meet held
here last month. The tour serves as
a preview for the Pan American

Games which will be held next
summer.
