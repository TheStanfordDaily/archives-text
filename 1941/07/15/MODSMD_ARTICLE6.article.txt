# Dr. Prada to Talk On 'American Way'
## 
### 
"The American Way as Seen by a
Latin American" will be the sub
ject of a lecture by Carlos Garcia-
Prada, acting professor of Romanic
languages, Wednesday afternoon,
July 16, at 4:15 in the lower lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Prada will stress the social
and political aspects of North and
South America, and the points of
view which the Latin Americans
have taken in the present crisis. He
received his M.A. at the University
of Michigan in 1924 and his Ph.D.
at the University of Colombia in
1929. In 1935-36 he acted as direc
tor of universities and institutes of
higher learning in Bogota, Colombia.
