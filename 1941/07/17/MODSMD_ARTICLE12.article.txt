# Dames Plan Visit To Stanford Museum
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames have been
invited to go through the Stanford
Museum next Thursday. The group
will meet at the museum promptly
at 2 o'clock. The event has been
arranged through the courtesy of
Dr. Wilbur, and is to be the first of
the conducted tour series of the
summer. There will be no bridge
group on this day. Friday the
bridge group will meet at the Wom
en's Clubhouse at 2 o'clock.
