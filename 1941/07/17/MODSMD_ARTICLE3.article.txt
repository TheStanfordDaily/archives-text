# Rabbi to Deliver Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
Rabbi Edgar F. Magnin, distin
guished radio speaker, will deliver
Sunday services in Memorial Church
at 11 o'clock July 20. The subject
on which he will talk is "The De
velopment of Moral Resources in
National Crisis."
This is to be the last event of the
Education Conference and the first
of a series of Sunday morning vis
iting speakers. Rabbi Magnin is
from the Wilshire Boulevard Tem
ple, Los Angeles. His daughter, Mae
Magnin, was a freshman here last
year.
