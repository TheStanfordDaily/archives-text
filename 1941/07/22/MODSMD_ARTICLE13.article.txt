# GIBSON-RANZONI
## 
### 
Shirley Gibson, University of
Oregon, Kappa Kappa Gamma, and
Lieut. Robert A. Ranzoni, associate
professor of military science and
tactics on campus, were married in
Reno July 12 and will live in Palo
Alto. Lieut. Ranzoni was president
of Kappa Alpha during his senior
year at Stanford.
