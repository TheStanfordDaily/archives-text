# Pre-Meds Eligible For Army Reserve
## 
### 
Stanford medical students are
eligible for commissions in the med
ical corps administrative reserve if
they have completed more than two
years of study, Brigadier-General
J. O. Donovan announced yesterday.
Those who sign up will probably
be permitted to finish med school
and interneship unless there is an
emergency in which case they will
become second lieutenants in the
administrative department of the
medical corps. Internes, upon com
pletion of their interneship, would be
ordered to active duty as first lieu
tenants.
Brig. Gen. Donovan is state selec
tive service director.
