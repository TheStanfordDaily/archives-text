# Union Formal To Be August 8
## 
### 
The date of the Union's summer
dance has been set for August 8,
Katie Blackmore, chairman, an
nounced yesterday. It will be formal
and held in the Union patio, which
is to be lighted by Japanese lan
terns for the occasion.
"It's going to be the big affair of
the season," said Katie. "The or
chestra has not as yet been selected.
If you've any suggestions, see
Janette Grant, who's in charge of
that."
