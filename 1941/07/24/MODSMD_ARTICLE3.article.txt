# White to Address Wednesday Series
## 
### 
Leonard D. White, acting profes
sor of political science, will speak
next Wednesday afternoon on
"Washington and the States: Domi
nance or Co-operation," at 4:15 in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Dr. White has come to Stanford
from the University of Chicago,
where he is professor of public ad
ministration. He received his B.S.
degree from Dartmouth in 1914 and
his M.A. in 1915, and his Ph.D. from
Chicago in 1921.
