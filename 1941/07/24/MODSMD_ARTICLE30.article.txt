# Schroeder Wins Seabright Match
## 
### 
Ted Schroeder, who promises to
be Stanford's greatest netman since
Keith Gledhill, entered the third
round of the Sea Bright (New Jer
sey) Tournament along with 15
other of the country's top tennis
players Monday by defeating Bill
Vogt of Philadelphia, 6-2, 8-6.
Combining with Jack Kramer,
with whom he holds the National
Doubles title, Schroeder also made
his way up into the third-round of
the doubles competition in which
they are seeded first. The Card is
No. 8 seeded in the singles.
