# Navy Aviators Offer Interview
## 
### 
With the present rapid increase in
American air power the armed serv
ices are looking for men who are
interested in flying and the training
that the services are prepared to
give.
Today and tomorrow Lieutenant
George E. W. Davis, Dr. Harry D.
Williams and Chief Pharmacist Mate
Peterson representing the 12th Naval
District will be at the Board of
Athletic Control to interview men
students who are interested in be
coming naval aviation cadets. Phys
ical examinations also will be given.
Among the requirements are that
you must have two years of college
and be between 20 and 27 years of
age.
