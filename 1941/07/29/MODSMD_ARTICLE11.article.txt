# Theater Costumes, Designs on Display
## 
### 
An exhibition of costume plates
and costumes of the Peninsula and
Hillbarn Theaters is now on display
on the bulletin board in the left
corridor of the Education Building.
The costumes were designed by
Sam Rolph, executed by Joy Mc-
Farland and photographed by Aneta
Fowler. The display consists of color
plates of the costumes and photo
graphs of them as they appeared on
the stage. Three of the actual cos
tumes are on exhibit.
