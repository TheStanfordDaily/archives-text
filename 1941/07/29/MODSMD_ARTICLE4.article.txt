leased yesterday by the Palo Alto
Selective Service Board for the
356 21-year-olds who registered
July L
The numbers run from 1403 past
4500, and since the last Palo Alto
man inducted had ordered number
1896, registrants with numbers be
tween 1403 and 1896 will probably
receive questionnaires and medical
examinations shortly, local draft
officials said.
The first of the 21-year-olds will
probably be inducted in September,
officials said, although the local
board has received no official in
structions regarding examination or
calling of the recent registrants.
