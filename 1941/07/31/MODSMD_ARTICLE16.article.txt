# Slavic Professor To Talk on Fiction
## 
### 
Vladimir Nabokov, lecturer in
Slavic languages, will deliver the
next address in Wednesday Series
on "Life and Fiction" at 4:15 in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Mr. Nabokov, who won recogni
tion in Europe for his writings and
is known in the United States for
his book, "Laughter in the Dark,"
attended school in St. Petersburg
and at Cambridge University. In
1922, at Stanford, he received his
degree in foreign languages and
went to Berlin to begin his literary
career. Mr. Nabokov left Germany
in 1937 and went to France; three
years later he came to this country.
