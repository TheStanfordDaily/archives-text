# Keller to Speak For Educators
## 
### 
Franklin J. Keller, principal
of the Metropolitan Vocational High
School of New York City, will ad
dress the Thursday luncheon meet
ing of the Education Club in the
Union Dining Hall today on "Can
There Be International Understand
ing in Vocational Education?"
Convening at 12:15, the meeting
will be another in the regular Edu
cation Club luncheon meetings.
Reservations for the Education Club
meetings are regularly on sale in
the lobby of the Education Building
on the Tuesday and Wednesday
preceding the day of the luncheon.
