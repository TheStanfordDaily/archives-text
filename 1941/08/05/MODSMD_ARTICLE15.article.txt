# Chamberlain Made Fellow in History
## 
### 
Waldo Chamberlain, research as
sistant in the Hoover Library on
War, Revolution, and Peace, and in
the industrial relations division of
the Graduate School of Business,
was named a fellow of the Library
of Congress in naval history.
Dr. Chamberlain will leave about
August 20 for Washington, D.C.,
where he will make a survey of
materials on naval history in the
Congressional Library.
He has been at work here during
the past year on a study of indus
trial relations in Germany from
1914 to 1940, which will be published
next autumn.
