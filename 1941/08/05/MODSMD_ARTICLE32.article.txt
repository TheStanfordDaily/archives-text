# 'Cootie' Thompson Sent to Vancouver
## 
### 
Quentin "Cootie" Thompson, main
stay of the pitching staff of the In
dian nine for the last three years,
has been sent by the Hollywood
baseball club of the Pacific Coast
League to Vancouver in the Pacific
Northwestern circuit where he will
get more seasoning.
During his junior year on the
Farm, Thompson was offered $10,000
by a major league team but he pre
ferred to stay in school and receive
his degree.
