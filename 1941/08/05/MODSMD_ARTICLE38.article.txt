# Stanford Dames To Meet Today
## 
### 
The Sewing Group of the Stanford
Dames will meet at 2 p.m. today
at the home of Mrs. John C. Al
mack, 683 Alvarado Row.
Mrs. Norman Fenton will review
a book for the evening Dames to
morrow at 8 p.m. at the home of Mrs.
J. Paul Leonard, 551 Foothill Road.
On Thursday the Dames will be
conducted on a tour of the Hoover
Library on War, Revolution, and
Peace at 2 p.m. The bridge group
meets Friday at the Union at 2 p.m.
