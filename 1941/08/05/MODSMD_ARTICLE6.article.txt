# Navy Cadet Board To Present Movie
## 
### 
The Naval Aviation Cadet Selec
tion Board will show the Warner
Brothers production, "Eyes of the
Navy," Wednesday at 7:30 p.m. in
the Education Building Auditorium.
The movie will last for about half
an hour, after which Lieutenant
E. W. Davis of the Naval Aviation
Selection Board will answer ques
tions.
The program is under the spon
sorship of the Civilian Pilot's Train
ing Authority.
