# Gaieties Director Plans for Review
## 
### 
Ideas, scripts, and songs for the
1941 Big Game Gaieties have begun
to pile in on Asher Wilson, director
of next fall's big review with pro
duction time still three months dis
tant.
Wilson, who is attending summer
quarter, assures a show equal to the
successes of the past few years with
some of the old talent held over and
new student offerings added.
Already a general theme for the
show has been formulated with skits
beginning to be woven into it. Wil
son has several song writers work
ing that promise to provide an un
usually good crop of hits for the
show.
