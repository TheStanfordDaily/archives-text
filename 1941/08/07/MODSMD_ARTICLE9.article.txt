# Union Residence To Hold Formal
## 
### 
The summer Union Formal will
be held Friday night in the patio
of the Union court.
Colored and indirect lighting will
be used and guests will dance to the
music of Clyde Appleby and his 13-
piece orchestra from 9 until 1. Re
freshments will be served.
Miss Sims, Dr. and Mrs. Scott, and
Dr. and Mrs. Snyder will be the
patrons. The bids are still on sale,
and can be obtained from Dot
Carew, Dot Culver, Jane Lamport,
or Pat George.
Graduate women are invited.
