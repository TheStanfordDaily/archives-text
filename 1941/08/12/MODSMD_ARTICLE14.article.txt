# William Briggs To Be Honored
## 
### 
A memorial to the late Professor
William Dinsmore Briggs, head of
the Department of English, in the
form of a reading and social room
in the School of Letters Building on
the inner Quad is planned by the
English Review Club.
President Ray Lyman Wilbur has
approved plans for the memorial and
has designated a room which is to
be partitioned in half and furnished
with easy chairs, divans, lamps,
reading tables and a carpet.
