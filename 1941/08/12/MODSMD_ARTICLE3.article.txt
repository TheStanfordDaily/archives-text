# Library's Book Club Rents Best Sellers
## 
### 
A rental library, known as the
Stanford Book Club, is housed at
the loan desk of the University Li
brary. The club is open to residents
in the Stanford community, although
it is patronized chiefly by students
and faculty. Membership in the
club costs $5 a year. Books also may
be borrowed at a rate of 5 cents a
day or 20 cents a week.
