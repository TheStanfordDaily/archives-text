# Lamoure to Enter Fresno in September
## 
###

on the frosh football team of last
year and weightman on the track
squad, has enrolled at Fresno State
for the coming year due to his
banishment from further intercol
legiate athletics at Stanford by Ed
win Atherton, P.C.C. athletic com
missioner in the purge of last spring.
