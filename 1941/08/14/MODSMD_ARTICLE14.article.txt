# Trueblood to Talk On 'Responsibility'
## 
### 
Services will be held Sunday in
the Memorial Church at 11 o'clock.
Dr. Trueblood, who has been absent
from the campus for the past four
weeks, will give the sermon. His
topic of discussion is to be "The
Meaning of Responsibility."
Music will be by soloist Carol
Woodhams of Redwood City.
