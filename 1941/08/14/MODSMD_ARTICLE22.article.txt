# Replogle Defeated In Semi-final Play
## 
### 
After taking four successive
matches Dee Replogle, Stanford golf
ace from Oklahoma City, fell before
John Kraft, eventual champion, 1-
up, in the semi-final match of the
Broadmoor Invitational last week.
Kraft, a Denver school teacher,
had previously defeated Replogle
earlier in the summer in the quar
terfinals of the Western Amateur on
the same course.
