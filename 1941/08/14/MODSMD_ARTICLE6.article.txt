# Jackson Chosen Banquet Speaker
## 
### 
Dean J. Hugh Jackson of the
Stanford Graduate School of Busi
ness will deliver the commencement
address for the San Francisco chap
ter of the American Institute of
Banking tomorrow night at a ban
quet and meeting to be held at the
Palace Hotel, San Francisco. Dean
Jackson will speak on "The Price
of Progress."
Recently Dean Jackson delivered
the closing banquet address at the
week's meetings of the California
Building and Loan League at Po
mona College in Claremont.
