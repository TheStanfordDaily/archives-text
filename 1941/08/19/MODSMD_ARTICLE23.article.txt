# H. B. Lee Takes Utah State Post
## 
### 
H. B. "Beeb" Lee, former Stan
ford student body president and
member of the championship 1935-
38 basketball teams and more re
cently frosh cage coach, has been
appointed head basketball mentor
at Utah State College at Logan.
Chosen over 35 other candidates
on the recommendation of Everett
Dean, Stanford coach, Lee will take
the place of Dick Domney, director
of athletics as well as basketball
coach for the Aggies for over a
quarter of a century. Romney re
tired from the post in order to de
vote more time to his other activities.
