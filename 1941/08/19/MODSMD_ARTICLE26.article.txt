# Brownell Leads Tourney Qualifiers
## 
### 
Bud Brownell, Stanford's soon-to
be-drafted golf ace, shot a 36-hole
total of 147 at the San Francisco C.C.
last Thursday to lead all northern
California qualifiers for the National
Amateur at Omaha, starting Aug
ust 25.
The would-be junior at Stanford
next year and Pacific Coast Confer
ence champion will leave shortly
for the East with Jimmy French.
