# Mrs. Johnston Dies At Campus Home
## 
### 
Mrs. Florence B. Johnston, wife
of Dr. Oliver M. Johnston, emeritus
member of the Stanford faculty and
former head of the Romanic lan
guages department, died early yes
terday morning at the Palo Alto
Hospital after a short illness.
Professor and Mrs. Johnston came
to Stanford in 1897, where she took
an active part in the work of the
First Baptist Church of Palo Alto.
She belonged to the Stanford Fac
ulty Women's Club and the Fort
nightly Music Club.
