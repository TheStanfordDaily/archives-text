HURRY HURRY HURRY
MEN of '45
to the
INDIAN SPORT SHOP
in
ENCINA GYMNASIUM
Don't wait until the last minute to get your lockers, towels, and gym equipment.
This shop is maintained in Encina Gym for your convenience. We carry everything
you need for every sport or gym activity in quality brands at prices you can afford
to pay. Don't buy your gym equipment until you have found out the official require
ments for your class. Most of your equipment we furnish without cost to you.
While here you will want to look over our stock of Stanford T-shirts, slack sox,
and general sportswear. We think we have the most complete stock of sweaters
for Stanford students to be found in California.
Come in early and see a truly Stanford store, run by Stanford Men, for the ex
clusive use of Stanford students and faculty.
