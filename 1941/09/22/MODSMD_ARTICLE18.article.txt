# Vacancies Exist For 500 Riveters
## 
### 
According to an announcement by
the State Department of Employ
ment, openings now exist for 500
trainees as riveters. An opportunity
is thus given to youths without me
chanical experience to train for em
ployment in aircraft plants.
The period of training will last 15
days or less, and the trainees will re
ceive $2 per day during that period.
Applicants must be at least 18, in
good physical condition, with good
eyesight and hearing, be able to
purchase from $8 to $10 worth of
tools, and provide their own trans
portation to the plant.
Those interested in taking the ex
amination required for entrance into
the training course should contact
the nearest office of the Department
of Employment.
