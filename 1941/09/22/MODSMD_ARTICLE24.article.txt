# Ex-A.S.S.U. Heads Wed in Pasadena
## 
### 
The marriage of Coline Upshaw,
'40, to Ken Cuthbertson, '40, took
place on Sept. 13 at the Presbyterian
Church in Pasadena.
Miss Upshaw and Cuthbertson
were vice-president and president,
respectively, of the A.S.S.U. during
the 1939-40 academic year. Cuth
bertson, last year, attended the Har
vard graduate school of business. He
will finish his business course at
Stanford.
