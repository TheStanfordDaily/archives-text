# STORE SALES HIGEHER
## 
### 
Department store sales for the
four weeks ending August 16 were
about 29 per cent higher than for
the corresponding four weeks of
last year, whereas for the first 33
weeks of this year sales show a gain
of only 15 per cent, indicating a
substantial rise during recent
months.

Nearly one-third of last year's
graduating class of St. Mary's Col
lege has applied for, or is taking part
in some form of military activity, ac
cording to the office of the recorder.
Leading the list are the members of
die Army Air Corps followed by the
Navy Air Corps, the Naval Reserve,
the Marine Reserve, and the Army
Reserve.
