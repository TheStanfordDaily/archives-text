# Summer Classes Hit All-Time High
## 
### 
Total registration for summer ses
sion reached an all-time high for the
last four years with an enrollment
of 1,031 students, according to Miss
Miriam Remele, editorial secretary
of the Registrar's office.
There were 165 more students at
tending summer quarter than there
were in 1940; GOB men and 423 women
maintained the usual ratio.
