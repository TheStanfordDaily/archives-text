# MEN WHO POWER BIG RED MACHINE
## 
###

performance. From September 27 when they meet the dark horse University of Oregon griddcrs they will have tough going, since all P.C.C.
teams have been practicing with the idea of dumping the Indians. They are in the line: Fred Meyer, Bruno Banducci, Jack Francis, Vic Lind
skog, Chuck Taylor, Ed Stamm and Arnie Meiners. In the backfield is All-American Frank Albert, Eric Armstrong, Milt Vucinich, and Peto
