# Wolter to Attend Baseball Congress
## 
### 
Harry Wolter, Stanford's varsity
baseball coach, leaves Sept. 16 for
the East to attend the American
Baseball Congress at Battle Creek,
Mich. Wolter will be the only base-
Vial 1 coach from the Coast at the
Congress.
Thirty-six states will be repre
sented at the meeting, which will
last from Sept. 19 to 28. Wolter will
return to the Farm Oct. 4 or 5.
