# VERDIECK MENLO COACH
## 
### 
Jim Verdieck, former Stanford
center, is now assisting Walt Heincke
as football coach at Menlo J.C. Ver
dieck is also serving as head tennis
coach at Menlo. He is also working
at Stanford for his master's degree in
physical education.

The carillon in the Hoover Library
cost $100,000 and was cast in Belgium
in 1938.
