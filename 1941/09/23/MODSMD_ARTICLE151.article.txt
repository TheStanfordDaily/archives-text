# MINOR LEAGUE STARS
## 
### 
Outfield stars of the International
League and American Association
are being called to service in the
majors at the tail end of this season.
Stan Musial, up from Rochester, now
sparks the Cards; Babe Barna and
Lou NovikofF are back again, Lukon
now cavorts for Cincy, and Cleve
land has a new man in Edwards.
