# McBain Injured In Auto Accident
## 
### 
Recovering at his campus home
from injuries received in a recent
automobile accident is James W.
Mcßain, professor of chemistry. His
injuries include severe bruises and
a basal fracture of the skull.
The accident occurred September
4 in Nebraska. A defect in the car's
brakes caused it to overturn three
times.
Professor Mcßain is expected to
return to his classes in four or five
weeks.
