# Choir Rehearses Thursday Night
## 
### 
The Stanford University Choir will
hold its first rehearsal Thursday eve
ning in Memorial Church at 7:15
p.m., Dr. Warren D. Allen, Choir
Director and organist, announced
today. The Choir will prepare music
for the Matriculation service Sun
day when President Ray Lyman Wil
bur, and Deans Mary Yost and John
Bunn will be present. Dr. D. Elton
Trueblood, University Chaplain, will
preach.
Former members and tryoutees for
membership are urged to attend.
The Freshman Choir of 40 singers
met for the first time last Sunday.
They will meet again Wednesday at
7:15 p.m. in the Choir room when
several will be selected for the Uni
versity Choir.
