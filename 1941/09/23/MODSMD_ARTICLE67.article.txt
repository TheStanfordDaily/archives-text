# S.F. Theaters Offer Varied Entertainment
## 
### 
Entertainment in San Francisco
which will be of interest to students
during September includes Walt
Disney's "Fantasia," enjoying a re
turn run at the Geary Theater; Otto
Kruger in James Thurber's "The
Male Animal," at the Alcazar; and
the perennial "Tobacco Road," star
ring John Barton at the Curran.
