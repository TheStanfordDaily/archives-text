# P.O. Announces Mail Schedules
## 
### 
Any person planning to rent a
post-office box, and who has not
yet done so, can find his or her mail
in General Delivery, the Stanford
University Post Office announced
today. General Delivery, Parcel
Post, Stump, and other windows are
open during the week from 8:30 a.m.
to 6:00 p.m., but closes at 12 noon
Saturdays and all day Sunday.

Mail arrives ten times daily (ex
cept Sundays and holidays) from
4:30 a.m. to 7:05 p.m. and is usually
sorted and ready for distribution
about an hour after arrival. Mail
leaves eight times a day, final clos
ing being at 7:10 p.m.

Air mail leaves the campus for the
airport at 7:10 and 9:45 a.m., at 3:20
and 7:10 p.m. to all points, and at
5:40 p.m. for east and south.
