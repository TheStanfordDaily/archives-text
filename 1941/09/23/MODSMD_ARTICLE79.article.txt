# Farm Scientists To Aid Institute
## 
### 
The recently organized Ecuadorian
Institute of Natural Sciences in
Quito, Ecuador, has invited mem
bers of the Stanford faculty to col
laborate with the Institute.
The invitation, which was ad
dressed to Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur,
requests all naturalists of the west
ern hemisphere to exchange mate
rials and publications with the In
stitute. Purpose of the Institute is
study and investigation of natural
resources with special emphasis on
geography, botany and forestry.
Ecuador's leading scientists are
lending their services to the organi
zation.
