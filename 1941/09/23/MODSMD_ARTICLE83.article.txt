# Popper Will Give Course On Opera
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, head of the
music department, has announced
that Dr. Herbert J. Popper's Opera
Workshop, 170, will be given this
quarter. The course was omitted
from the Time Schedule.
The History of the Sonata, course
by Fejrenc Molnar, has no pre
requisites. Three Wednesday eve
ning programs illustrating the course
will be given.
