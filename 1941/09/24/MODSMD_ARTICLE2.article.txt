# Physics Head Names Instructors
## 
### 
James F. McGee, of the Univer
sity of Philadelphia, Dr. H. Morton
Hammermesh, of New York City
College, and Dr. Seville Chapman,
of the University of California have
been appointed as instructors in
Stanford's Department of Physics,
the department announced yester
day.
They will (ill vacancies resulting
from the absence of Prof. N. E. Brad
bury, on duty with the Navy, and
Prof. William W. Hansen, working
at M.I.T. and at the Sperry Gyro
scope Co. on national defense mate
rial; and from the promotion of Dr.
Hans Staub to assistant professor.
