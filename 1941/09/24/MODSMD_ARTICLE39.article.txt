# Fall Track Begins This Afternoon
## 
### 
Pitch Johnson, head track and
field coach, has issued a call for all
varsity and frosh track men to meet
this afternoon at Angell Field.
The meeting will begin fall track
activities, and all trackmen are ur
gently requested to be present. Fall
track is not only preparation for
spring track but offers competition
also.
