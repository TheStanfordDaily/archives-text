# Wilbur Addresses Farm Tomorrow
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur will
address students and members of
the faculty at an all-University As
sembly tomorrow morning at 10 a.m.
in the auditorium of the Memorial
Hall, it was announced by the Com
mittee on Public Exercises. All 10
o'clock classes will be dismissed for
the assembly.
Seats for members of the faculty
will be reserved on the stage of the
auditorium. Admission to the re
served section will be through the
west side entrance and the Green
Room.
The assembly will begin promptly
at 10:10 and will conclude in time
for 11 o'clock classes.
