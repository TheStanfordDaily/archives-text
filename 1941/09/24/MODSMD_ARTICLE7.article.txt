# Hurricane Inundates Matagorda, Texas
## 
### 
HOUSTON. Tex., Sept. 23 (UP).—
Refugees streaming into Bay City to
night said that Matagorda, Tex.,
coastal town of 1,000 population, was
completely under water as a tropical
hurricane lashed the Texas coast
with 100-mile-an-hour winds.
