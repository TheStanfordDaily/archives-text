# Tryouts for Daily Will Start Oct. 1
## 
### 
Members of the Daily Editorial
Board will address tryoutees during
tryout sessions which are scheduled
to begin on Wednesday, Oct. 1, at
4:45 o'clock in Room 8 of the Press
Building, according to Rosemarie
Mullany, women's editor.
Tryouts will extend over a two
week period at the end of which
appointments to the staff will be an
nounced. Previous experience will
not be a determining factor in the
appointments, according to Editor
Charles Bull.
