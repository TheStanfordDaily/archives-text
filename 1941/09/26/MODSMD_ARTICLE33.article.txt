# Wilburs to Receive Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Ail new lower division men and
women are invited to attend the an
nual reception by Dr. and Mrs. Ray
Lyman Wilbur at the President's
mansion atop Lagunita Knoll to
morrow night.
The reception will begin at 8
o'clock, and semi-formal dress will
be worn. A similar reception for
new upper division and graduate
students was held last night.
The Stanford stadium is the larg
est college stadium in the nation.
