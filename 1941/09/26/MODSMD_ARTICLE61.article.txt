# CAMPUS OPINION
## THANKS TO NORM 
### 
TPHE EDITOR, SIR:
During the past few days, we've been in the
process of becoming Stanford men and women. We'd
like to give ourselves all the credit for that. Actually,
if we're to be honest about it, we'll have to pass the
honors on to somebody else.
We can't name everybody who's given us a hand.
As a matter of fact, we don't even know them all.
There has. however, been one outstanding figure who
seemed to hover over it all. We know him and his
name is Norm Ross.
People arc usually rather slow in expressing their
thanks for tasks like the one Norm had. Sometimes
they don't even get. around to it at all. We didn't want
that to happen. So we've grabbed the first opportunity
to thank Norm publicly for the yeoman's service he's
done. We hope, Mr. Editor, that you'll publish this
letter because we all mean it from top to bottom.
Yours sincerely,
Earl Rex Dick McAfee
Don Davis Norm King
Bob Cardinal Rene Pa 7.
Preston Burchard Kenny Young
Yoshimoro Shituy
and the rest of the guys from Encinu
