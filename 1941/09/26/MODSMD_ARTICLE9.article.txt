# C.P.T. Course Has Room for 10 More
## 
### 
There are still ten vacancies in
Stanford's Civilian Pilot Training
Course enrollment quota. The quota
was dropped from 50 last year to 30
this year. Twenty students are now
enrolled in the course.
There is also room for six alter
nates who will replace students who
leave during the first portion of the
course. Classes meet five times a
week at 8 o'clock and three evenings
a week. Information as to enroll
ment requirements will be found in
Tuesday's Daily.
