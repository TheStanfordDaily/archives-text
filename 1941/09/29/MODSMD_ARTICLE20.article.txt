# Frosh Rally Com Tryouts Continue
## 
### 
Freshman tryouts for Rally Com
mittee will continue today from 1 to
5 o'clock in Room 13 of the Old Un
ion, according to Pi Thornton, co
chairman of Rally Committee.
Names of the ten men and ten
women chosen at the interviews will
appear in tomorrow's Daily, and
they will be asked to attend tomor
row night's meeting of Rally Com
mittee.
