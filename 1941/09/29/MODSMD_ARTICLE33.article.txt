# Key West Strikers Vote to Resume Work
## 
### 
KEY WEST. Fla., Sept. 28 (UP).—
Striking carpenters and laborers at
the $2,000,000 navy expansion project
here tonight voted to resume work
tomorrow, although the government
had not granted their demands for
a wage increase of 12'/2 cents an
hour.
