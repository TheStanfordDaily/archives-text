# CHAMP, CHALLENGER SPEND QUIET DAY
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Sept. 28 (UP).—
Champion and challenger—Joe Louis
at Greenwood Lake, N.Y., and Lou
Nova at his Pompton Lakes camp—
contented themselves today with
limbering exercises at their camps.
They will motor in to New York
tomorrow in time for the noon
weigh-in at Madison Square Gar
den.
