# ROTC Program Begins Monday
## 
### 
This afternoon at 1 o'clock the
first R.O.T.C. drill will be held.
Drill will begin at 1 and last until
4 o'clock. This year there will be
drill Monday through Friday after
noons; one afternoon a week more
than last year.
Promotion in R.O.T.C. is to be
based on a competitive merit basis
and selection of candidates to take
the advanced courses will be based
on the .student's record of achieve
ment in his first two years of train
ing.
Organization of the Stanford post
will be on a regimental basis this
year instead of last year's system of
using the battalion as the largest or
ganization.
