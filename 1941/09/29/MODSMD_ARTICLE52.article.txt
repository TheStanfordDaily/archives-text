# Riggs Wins Pacific Coast Tennis Title
## 
### 
BERKELEY, Sept. 28 (UP).—Bob
by Riggs, National Singles cham
pion, easily defeated Tate Coult
hard, University of California play
er, 6-2, 6-4, today in the first round
of men's singles at the 52nd annual
Pacific Coast Tennis Tournament.
Mixed doubles play started today
with National Doubles Champion
Ted Schroeder of Los Angeles and
Mrs. Patsy McCoy Brown of San
Francisco winning first and second
round matches. They defeated Joel
and Kay Brecheen, Berkeley, 6-0,
6-0; and Stuart Brown and Bar-
Lara Miller, Stockton, 6-1, 6-2, to
advance to the third round.
Third round winners were Robin
Hippenseil, University of Califor
nia. who defeated Carl Holmes,
Berkeley, 6-3, 6-2; and Julius Held
man, Stanford, who defeated Rob
ert Tout, Berkeley, 6-1, 4-6, 6-1.
