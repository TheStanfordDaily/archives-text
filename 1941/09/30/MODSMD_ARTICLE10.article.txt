# El Camino Quartet To Play Tuesday
## 
### 
The El Camino String Quartet
will present a concert to open the
current 1941-42 Tuesday Evening
Series October 7 at 7:30 p.m. in the
auditorium of Memorial Theater.
Sponsored throughout the year by
the Committee of Public Exercises,
Tuesday Series offers varied pro
grams of lectures, motion pictures,
concerts, readings, and ballets.
