# Hansen Elected To Gaieties Post
## 
### 
Art Hansen was elected technical
director of the Big Game Gaieties
of 1941 yesterday at Ham's Head's
first meeting of the quarter. Lois
Feldhym, originally slated to fill
the position, was forced to resign
because of academic conflicts.
At the meeting President Ben
Miller announced that the annual
Ram's Head One-Act Program will
be presented on Jan. 16-17 this year,
a date which will necessitate scripts
being turned in before the end of
fall quarter.
Others elected to positions in the
student dramatic organization were:
reading director, Frank Burt; pub
licity director, Jean Nowell; and so
cial chairman, Eva Schwartz.
