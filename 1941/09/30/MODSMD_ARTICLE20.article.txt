# Alumni to Sing At Lunch Club
## 
### 
The Wednesday Luncheon Club
wiil hold its first session of the
quarter tomorrow at 12 noon in the
Nitery. Shirley Baker and Charles
K. Field ("Cheerio" of radio fame),
members of the class of '95, will
entertain the meeting with songs
and poems of Stanford's youth.
The Luncheon Club is an infor
mal organization meeting weekly to
eat and hear speakers on topics of
current interest. Anyone is welcome,
and the price of lunch is only 25
cents.
