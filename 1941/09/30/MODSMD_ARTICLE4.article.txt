# REDS CLAIM 263 GERMAN PLANES
## 
### 
MOSCOW, Tues., Sept. 30 (UP).—
The Red air fleet claimed today that
263 Nazi planes have been destroyed
in two days of aerial onslaught
which softened up the whole Ger
man line.
