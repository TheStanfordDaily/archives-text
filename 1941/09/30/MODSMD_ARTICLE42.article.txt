# Police Seek Two In Miley Murder
## 
### 
LEXINGTON, Ky., Sept. 29 (UP).
—Police tonight broadcast the de
scription of two men— one described
as liking "golf and dancing" —who
were sought in connection with the
investigation of the slaying of Mar
ion Miley, nationally popular wom
an golfer, and the critical wounding
of her mother, Mrs. Fred Miley, 50,
in her Lexington country club
apartment Sunday.
The men were identified as For
est Turner, 24, and Sim Scarbor
ough, 41, and they were said to have
left a trail of crime from Tampa,
to Lexington, where they were
reported heading to visit a cousin
of Turner's.
