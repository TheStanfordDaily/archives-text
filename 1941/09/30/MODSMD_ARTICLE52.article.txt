# The 						Little Green Shack
## 
### 
IN a little green shack on that nice wide, new
Lomita Drive there, will be a momentous
gathering of the clan today at 2 o'clock. At this
time, in addition to various and sundry com
ments to be made by ye editor, there will be an
official unveiling of the fifteen shining new Un
derwoods by the Women's Editor. Afternoon
Club on the editor. Presence of all is vital.
