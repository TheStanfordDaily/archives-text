# CONNALLY ASKS NEUTRALITY CHANGE
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Sept. 29 (UP).—
Chairman Tom Connally of the
Senate Foreign Relations Commit
tee tonight called for Neutrality Law
changes that would allow American
merchant ships to be armed and en
ter combat zones or "ports of nations
at war."
