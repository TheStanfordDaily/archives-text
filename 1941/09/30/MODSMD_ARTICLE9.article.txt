# Debaters to Meet This Afternoon
## 
### 
First meeting of the year for the
Stanford debaters will take place at
4 p.m. this afternoon in Room 200S,
downstairs in the English Corner.
The primary purpose of the gather
ing is to elect a representative from
the debate team to the University
debate council.
Dr. A. N. Beck, of the University
of Hawaii, coach of the debate team
for this year, and an exchange pro
fessor with Professor Leland T.
Chapin, who is teaching in the
Islands, will be introduced by Bob
Hammet, team manager. This is the
first chance the debaters have had
to meet Dr. Beck.
