# Co-op Book Store Elects New Staff
## 
### 
Now officers of the Co-op Book
Exchange, elected at the organiza
tion's first meeting, Monday night,
were Frances Brown, manager, suc
ceeding Don Spiekard; and Jim
O'Donnell, president of the board.
Four new students, appointed to the
Co-op board of nine members, were
Don Spiekard, Lucille Bates, Tom
Trent and Paul Mussen.
The meeting was heid at 10 p.m.
in Room 15 of the Old Union. A
regular schedule of meetings has
not been decided upon but an
nouncement of the next gathering
of Co-op members will be made in
The Daily.
