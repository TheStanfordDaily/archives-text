# Pool Schedule Set For Women's Gym
## 
### 
Mrs. Marie Fenner yesterday an
nounced swimming arrangements
for the Women's Gymnasium pool.
For the first half of the autumn
quarter, wives of students and fac
ulty may swim every day at noon
for 15 cents. At the same time, Uni
versity employes will have the use
of the pool at $1.75 for the period.
The pool is open without charge to
women students for recreational
swimming on Mondays at 3, Tues
days and Fridays at 2. and Wednes
days and Thursdays at 4.
