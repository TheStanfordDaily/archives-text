# Daily Editor New Publications Head
## 
### 
Meeting yesterday in extraordi
nary session, Publications Council
elected Charles Bull, Daily editor,
chairman; and Barney McClure,
Chaparral editor, secretary for the
year.
Other members include Dr. C. R.
Bush, head of the Division of Jour
nalism; Dan Lewis, Daily business
manager; Bill Lane, Chaparral bus
iness manager; Bill Rowland, Quad
editor; Ralph Clark, Quad business
manager; and Jim McKellar, student
manager.
