# International Club Holds Open Meeting
## 
### 
Outlining the year's program and
getting acquainted will be projects
of tonight's meeting of the Inter
national Club to be held at 8 o'clock
at the home of Mrs. E. Thomas, 365
Lincoln Avenue. All students are
invited to attend, and transportation
will be furnished to those meeting
at the Nitery at 7:30.
