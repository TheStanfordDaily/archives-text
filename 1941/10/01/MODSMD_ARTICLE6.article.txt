# Choir Rehearses For Final Service
## 
### 
The first rehearsal for the- com
memoration service which will mark
the conclusion of Stanford's Fiftieth
Anniversary observances was held
Monday night in the organ gallery of
Memorial Church.
Voices of the Stanford Choir and
those of interested vocalists in the
community were blended in the Hal
lelujah Chorus from Handel's "Mes
siah" and "How Lovely Is Thy
Dwelling-place" by Brahms, the
two selections chosen for next Sun
day's program.
The rehearsal, which was under
the direction of the University or
ganist, Warren D. Allen, was at
tended by students, faculty mem
bers, and members of the com
munity.
