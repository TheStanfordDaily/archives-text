# Daily to Hold Last 'Open House' Toddy
## 
### 
Today is the second and last day
of "open house" at the Daily offices.
From 3 to 5 p.m. and from 7 to 9
p.m. today the business and editorial
offices of The Daily will be open to
visitors, who will be able to watch
tomorrow's six-page paper in pro
duction.
