# Seed Becomes Track Coach at San Rafael
## 
### 
Aubrey Seed, track star at Stan
ford University during the past two
years, has recently assumed duties on
the faculty at San Rafael Military
Academy, in San Rafael. Seed will
serve in the capacity of head track
coach and assistant at basketball and
minor sports. In addition to his ath
letic assignments, he will teach in the
high school academic depaximent in
the social science fields.
