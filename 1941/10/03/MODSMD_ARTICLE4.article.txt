# Block 'S' Awards Granted to Four
## 
### 
Four members of the varsity golf
team were granted their Block "S"
awards at a meeting of the Execu
tive Committee Tuesday evening.
Warren Berl, Raymond Brownell,
Charles Finger, and Dee Replogle
will receive the awards. All four
were on the team which won a na
tional intercollegiate championship.
James Lincoln will receive his
award in varsity track for winning
a place in the N.C.A.A. champion
ships.
