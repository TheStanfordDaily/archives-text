# Last Choir Tryout Scheduled for 8:15
## 
### 
Tonight will be the last opportu
nity for singers to try out for the
Thanksgiving services and commem
oration program that is to be held
Sunday morning at 11 o'clock in the
Memorial Church. Final rehearsal
will be tonight at 8:15 in the organ
gallery of the church.
Giving the program a traditional
background, the augmented choir
will present one of the anthems sung
50 years ago at the opening of Stan
ford, '"How Lovely Are Thy Mes
sengers," by Mendelssohn. The
other number to be heard on the
program will be the "Hallelujah"
chorus from Handel's "The Mes
siah."
