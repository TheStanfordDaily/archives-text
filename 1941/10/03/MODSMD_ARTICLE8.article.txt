# INDIES BOTHER JAPAN
## 
### 
TOKYO, Friday, Oct. 3 (UP).—
Japan is greatly concerned over in
formation that the Netherlands East
Indies is supplying large quantities
of potential war materials to Russia
while at the same time refusing
these materials to this country, it
w.is said in official circles today.
