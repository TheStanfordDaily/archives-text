# MEXICO OFFICIAL SHOT
## 
### 
NOGALES, Mex., Oct. 5 (UP).—
Frascuelo Valasco, former state rep
resentative to the last session of the
Mexican legislature, was shot to
death last night at Hermosillo by
Socorro de Solorzano, wife of a
Mexican union official, according to
information telephoned here tonight.
