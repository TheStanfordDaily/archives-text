# Farm Man Loses $135 In Branner Robbery
## 
### 
Two new suits, a new jacket, a
portable typewriter, and a wallet
were missing from Room 103 in
Branner Hall last Thursday evening
when Stafford Hughes, junior trans
fer, returned to his room to dress for
dinner. The missing property, val
ued at $135, was immediately re
ported to the police.
