# Allen Gives Recital Of '91 Organ Music
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, yesterday offered a special
recital at 4 p.m. in Memorial Church
which featured organ music that was
new in 1891. Before the recital he
gave a brief sketch of the history of
the organ.
The program included: Sonata, No.
1, Op. 42, in D minor by Alex Guil
mant; Andante cantabile, Fourth
Symphony by C. M. Widor; At Eve
ning, and Choral-March by Dudley
Buck; and Andante and Finale,
"Grand Piece Symphonique" by
Cesar Franck.
