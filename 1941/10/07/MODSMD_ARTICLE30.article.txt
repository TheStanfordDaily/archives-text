# Y.W.C.A. Holds Fall Tea Today
## 
### 
The Y.W.CA. today will hold its
annual reception for freshman and
transfer women. All new women are
invited to attend the tea which will
be held in the Women's Clubhouse
from 3 until 5 o'clock this afternoon.
Guests of honor will include Dean
Mary Yost, Pat Adams, A.S.S.U.
vice-president; Irma Hanibal; Bar
bara Rapp, Pan-Hellenic president;
Frances Langfeld, head sponsor at
Roble; Jeane Chambers, Rosemarie
Mullany, and Sylvia Berry.
Receiving the guests will be B. J.
Binney, 'Y' president; Miss Pat Dav
idson, Mrs. Grayson N. Kefauver,
and Lorraine Rude, 'Y' membership
chairman.
Members of the Y.W.CA. cabinet
will serve as hostesses at the tea,
which will serve as a springboard to
the 'Y' membership drive scheduled
to start this Wednesday.
