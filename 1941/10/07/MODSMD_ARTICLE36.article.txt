# Farm Officials Head Committee
## 
### 
President Ray Lyman Wilbur and
Dean J. Hugh Jackson of the Grad
uate School of Business, were
named vice-chairmen of the North
ern California Committee on Fair
Play for Citizens and Aliens of
Japanese Ancestry in a bulletin re
leased yesterday.
Five Stanford faculty members
were named as General Committee
members. They are Reginald Bell,
Joseph S. Davis, Albert W. Herre,
Grayson N. Kefauver, and Eliot G.
Mears.
The purpose of the committee is to
present any discrimination or dis
trust of Japanese residents through
out northern California as a result
of the strained diplomatic relations
existing between Japan and the
United States.
