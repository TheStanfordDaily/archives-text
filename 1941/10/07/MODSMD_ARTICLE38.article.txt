# Singstunde Moved To Morgan Home
## 
### 
Site of the bi-monthly Singstunde,
sponsored by the German depart
ment, has been moved from Haw
thorne Cottage to the campus home
of Professor B. Q. Morgan, according
to an announcement from the depart
ment. The first meeting of the year
is scheduled for next Thursday af
ternoon, and details of the time and
transportation will be announced in
The Daily before that time.

The Quartermaster Corps of the
Army has adopted a new gelatin
coating for meats, which both holds
the flavor and preserves food values.
