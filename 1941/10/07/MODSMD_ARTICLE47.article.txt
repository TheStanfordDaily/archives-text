# Cal's Hopes Fade; Jurkovich Through
## 
### 
BERKELEY. Oct. 6 (UP).—Jim
Jurkovich, speedy University of
California left half, around whom
Coach Stub Allison had built this
year's new Bear attack, today was
through with college football for
ever—by order of Dr. J. T. Harkness,
team physician.
Dr. Harkness ordered the perma
nent retirement of Jurkovich follow
ing an examination of the California
star after an injury sustained in the
Washington State game last Sat
urday.
