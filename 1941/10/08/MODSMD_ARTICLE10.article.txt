# Stanford Dames Meet tomorrow
## 
### 
Holding their first general meeting
of the quarter, members of the Stan
ford Dames will gather tomorrow at
2 p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
All wives of Stanford students are
heartily invited to attend by Mrs.
R. W. Skow, acting president.
The program of activities for the
year has been announced by the or
ganization. Groups will meet for
sewing, reading, afternoon and eve
ning bridge, cooking, music, and rec
creation. In collaboration with the
Faculty Women's Club, a group of
members will study child psy
chology.
