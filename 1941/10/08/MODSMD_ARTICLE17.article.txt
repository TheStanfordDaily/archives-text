# Memorial Service Set for Sunday
## 
### 
Memorial services for John Press
ley Phillips, '43, killed in a fall from
the Bel a house balcony Wednesday
night, will be held in Memorial
Church following the regular serv
ice Sunday at 11 a.m. They will be
conducted by Dr. D. Elton True
blood.
Members of the Beta Theta Pi
house attended the funeral services
held Saturday in Fresno, home of
the student. Among the pall bearers
were Jess Hazlett, Gene Kern, and
Jim McClatchy, fraternity brothers
of Phillips. Floral pieces were re
ceived from most of the campus
fraternities and sororities, as well as
from the residents of Encina Hall.
