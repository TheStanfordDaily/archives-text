# Train Rally Starts Tomorrow at 2:15
## 
### 
All students owning cars, or with
friends owning cars, will queue up
in front of the Fiji house tomorrow
afternoon at 2:15 for the Oregon
State train rally.
At the Palo Alto station, Joe Mes
sina and his two cohorts, the band,
and the team will appear, according
to Co-chairmen Pat Palmer and Jim
Cahill. The train leaves at 3 p.m.
