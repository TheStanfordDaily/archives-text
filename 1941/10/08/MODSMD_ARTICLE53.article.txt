# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
PI SIGMA ALPHA, honorary politi
cal science fraternity, will hold a
business meeting Thursday after
noon at 4 in the West library.
THE NATURAL HISTORY CLUB
will meet on Thursday, Oct. 9, at
7:45 in the basement of the Wom
en's Clubhouse to hear Professor
Ferris lecture on "Collecting Ex
periences in Mexico."
STANFORD'S FLYING CLUB will
meet tonight at 7:30 at the Palo
Alto Airport.
THE INTERNATIONAL CLUB will
be shown moving pictures of a
trip through Spain by Dr. Max
Savelle at the meeting at Mrs.
Thomas' home, 365 Lincoln Ave
nue, at 8 o'clock tonight.
THE CO-OP BOOK EXCHANGE
BOARD will meet at 8 tonight in
Room 15 of the Old Union.
DEBATE DINNER at 6:10 in the
Union.
THE A.I.E.E. will meet at 7:30 to
night at the Ryan Laboratory. All
interested engineers are invited
to see the high voltage demon
stration. Refreshments will be
served.
