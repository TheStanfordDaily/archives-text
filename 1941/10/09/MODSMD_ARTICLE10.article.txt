# Rifle Club Meets For Initial Shoot
## 
### 
Seventeen members signed up for
a competitive tournament at the first
meeting of the Stanford Rifle Club
last night in the basement of the Ad
Building. All individual scores will
be filed, and the top seven shooters
will make up the club's rifle team,
announced Carlos Asensio-Wunder
lich, club president. Sue Stewart,
secretary of the club, will be team
manager.
A medal will be awarded to the
member who has the highest total
score at the end of the quarter.
