# Journal Publishes Campus Discovery
## 
### 
Three research scientists in the
Stanford University Medical School
published yesterday, in the Cancer
Research Journal, a discovery that
they have made which may lead to
startling progress in the fight against
cancer. The three men are Dr.
Floyd DeEds and Dr. Robert H. Wil
son. research assistants, and Dr. Al
vin J. Cox, associate professor of
pathology.
The discovery was an accidental
one, made While the researchers
were experimenting with a new in
sect spray. They found that the in
secticide, when given in small doses
to rats, produced tumors and other
cancerous growths.
