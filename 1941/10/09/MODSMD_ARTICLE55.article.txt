# Cast of Comedy Announced Today
## 
### 
The cast of Knickerbocker Holi
day, two-act musical comedy, will
be announced this afternoon, follow
ing tryouts held last night in the
Little Theater, it was announced
last night by F. Cowles Strickland,
director. ,
Tryouts were held Monday and
Tuesday nights also. Strickland will
choose a cast of 16 and a chorus of
20 women and 20 men.
The play was written by Maxwell
Anderson and Kurt Weill and will
be produced in a few weeks. Re
hearsals will begin as soon as the
cast has been chosen.
