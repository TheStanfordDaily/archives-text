# Carillon Changes Deadline to Oct. 15
## 
### 
The Carillon, Stanford campus
magazine, has announced that its
deadline has been moved from today
to Oct. 15. The Carillon is designed
to give students a chance to have
work of general interest published.
Writers are urged to bring their j
stories, poems, and articles to the j
Carillon office in the art studio I
building between 1:30 and 5 p.m. '
They must be 2000 to 3500 words*
long, typewritten, and double
spaced.
