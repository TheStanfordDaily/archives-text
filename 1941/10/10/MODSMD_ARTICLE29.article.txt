# Y.W.C.A. Seeks New Members
## 
### 
Y.W.C.A. representatives are plan
ning to contact women students in
living groups this week-end for the
current membership drive. Under
the direction of Lorraine Rude,
chairman, 41 girls will continue the
drive until October 13.
Dues, which are $1, will entitle
campus women to a year's member
ship. This is a change from the for
mer $1.50 four-year membership.
Non-members are not prohibited
from taking part in the activities
planned by the organization for the
1941-42 year. Special projects are
being scheduled, including British
war relief work, U.S.O. aid, fresh
man and transfer discussion groups,
and an international table for for
eign students.
