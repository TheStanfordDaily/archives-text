# Organist Presents Recital in Chapel
## 
### 
Joseph M. Running, assistant
University organist, presented an
organ recital yesterday at 4:15 in
Memorial Church. The program in
cluded Fanfare by Lemmens; Ari
oso, Bach; Tocatta per I'Elevasions,
Frescobaldi; Scherzino. Rogers; and
Sonata No. il in C Minor by Men
delssohn.
He will also present a recital Sun
day at 4 p.m. in the church.
