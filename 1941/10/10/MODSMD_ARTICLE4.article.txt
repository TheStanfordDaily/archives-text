# Foreign News Ace To Be in San Jose
## 
### 
Jay Allen, veteran foreign corre
spondent who spent four months in
Nazi military camps, will tell of his
prison experiences in a talk at the
Roosevelt Junior High School in
San Jose Oct. 13 at 8 p.m. The pro
gram is sponsored by the First Con
gregational Church.
German police held Mr. Allen on
charges of spying for either Church
ill or Stalin after they had nabbed
him for illegally entering "Free"
France. After authorities had de
cided that he was not a spy, he was
sentenced to serve four months for
illegal entrance. He served his term
at Chalon and Dijon, and returned
to the United States after being re
leased.
