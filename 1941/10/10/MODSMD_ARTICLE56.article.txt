# PURSUIT SHIP CRASHES
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH. Oct. 9 (UP). —An
army pursuit ship crashed into the
Monongahela River at Courtney, 20
miles above Pittsburgh, today, just
missing a river towboat and killing
the pilot, Lieut. J. P. Lyons, of Pat
terson Field, Dayton, O.
