# Professor Praises Tact of Joe Louis
## 
### 
According to Professor Elmer E.
Nyberg, there is no one to compare
with Joe Louis in regard to diplo
macy in public statements.
Professor Nyberg, who teaches in
the School of Commerce of New
York University, pointed out that
Louis had always been modest in
all his declarations, and that even
under the hardest conditions he had
never failed to praise his opponent.
