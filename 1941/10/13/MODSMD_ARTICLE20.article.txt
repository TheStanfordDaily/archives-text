# Medieval Theme To Highlight Ball
## 
### 
"A Medieval Castle" will be the
theme of Lagunita's anual Court
Ball on October 25. For that eve
ning, the court will be transformed
into a thirteenth century castle. Er
nie Heckscher's orchestra will fur
nish the music.
Winifred Eaton is general chair
man in charge of preparations, and
committee chairmen under her are:
Allyn Scarborough and Barbara
Jones, decorations; Jean Campbell,
refreshments; Roma Christman, hos
tessing; Mary Frances Difenbaugh,
invitations; and Carol Jones, bids.
