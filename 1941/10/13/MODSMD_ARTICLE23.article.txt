# Libbott Speaks On Radio Forum
## 
### 
Stanford's first representatives in
the Intercollegiate Forum over sta
tion KFRC spoke yesterday from
11:15 to 11:30 a.m. Dr. N. B. Beck
of the speech and drama department
acted as modex-ator for the program.
Participating in the discussion was
Robert Libbott.
Topic of yesterday's forum was
"Should the federal government
regulate labor unions by law?"
Other colleges represented were
Mills, Santa Clara, and St. Mary's.
Weekly programs on the Intercol
legiate Forum are scheduled until
November 30. Eleven colleges in
cluding Stanford are presenting the
broadcasts.
