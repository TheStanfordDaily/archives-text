# Farm Swordgirls Whip Two Teams
## 
### 
Stanford's women's fencing *team
won two and lost one in the first
round of Harrow Trophy competi
tion Saturday night. The Farm
women defeated Oakland Fencers'
Club, 5-4, Funke 11, 5-4, and lost
to San Francisco Fencers' Club, 2-7.
The team, composed of Ruth At
kins, Harva Sprager, Jean Perham,
and Margaret Elliott, has shown a
marked improvement over last
year's fencers, who were eliminated
in the first round of the same con
test last October.
