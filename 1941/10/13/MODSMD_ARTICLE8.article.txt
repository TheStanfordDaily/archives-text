# Music Editor Talks On Opera Tuesday
## 
### 
Tuesday at 5 p.m. in the choir
room of Memorial Church Carlton
Smith, music editor of Esquire, will
speak to students on the "Barber of
Seville," which will be Riven by the
San Francisco Opera Guild on Fri
day, Oct. 24, at 1:45 p.m. and on
Friday, Oct, 31, at the same time.
The subscription list for tickets is
still open and tickets may be re
served with Miss Frankie Miles,
secretary of the Division of Music.
