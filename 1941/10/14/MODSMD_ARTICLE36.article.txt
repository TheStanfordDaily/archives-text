# Graduate Begins Training Course
## 
### 
Ruius E. Cook. Stanford '41, be
gan a nine months' training course
at Hamilton Field yesterday. At the
same time four former University
of California students were sworn in,
Hamilton Field officials announced.
Cook, whose parents are Mr. and
Mrs. R. O. Cook of Hartsook Inn,
Humboldt County, attended Stan
ford until his appointment. He is
the nephew of Lieutenant Com
mander L. L. Stanley of the U. S.
Navy.
