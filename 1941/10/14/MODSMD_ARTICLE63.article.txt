# Cowden Calls Senior Meeting
## Committee to Plan Activities of Class 
### 
President Bill Cowden of the Sen- |
ior Class sent out letters to the
presidents of all campus living
groups yesterday in order to gather
a representative executive commit
tee to create and carry out plans for
all senior activities during the year.
A senior representative from every
group has been asked to come to a
meeting at 8:15 tomorrow evening
in the Women's Clubhouse. From
this large committee of over fifty
a council of six will be picked to
handle class activity appointments
and to work with the class officers.
At this meeting Robert A. "Bones"
Hamilton, alumni secretary, will
make a short speech.
