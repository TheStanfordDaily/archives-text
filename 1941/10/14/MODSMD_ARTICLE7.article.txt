###

will be the subject of a talk given
tomorrow in the Education Building
at. 4:15 p.m., by Arundell Esdaile,
president of the London Library
Association and former secretary of
the British Museum, according to
Dr. Nathan van Patten, University
librarian.
Mr. Esdaile's lecture, sponsored
by the Stanford Library Association,
\Vill explain how Dr. Johnson kept
his "friendship in repair" by con
stant association with younger
people.
