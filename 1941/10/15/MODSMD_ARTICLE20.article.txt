# Journalism Group Chooses Officers
## 
### 
At a meeting last week, Sigma
Delta Chi, journalism fraternity,
elected Harold Shafer vice-presidenl,
Vic Minahan secretary, and Hemi
Silverman treasurer for the forth
coming year. Bud Ames was elected
president last spring.
The fraternity, in this, its first
meeting of the year, also discussed
its participation in the high school
press convention which is to be held
here Oct. 25.
