# A 						Cappella Tryout To Continue Today
## 
### 
V
Additional tryouts for the new a
cappella choir will be held today
between 1 and 4 p.m. or by special
arrangement with Miss Frankie
Miles or Mr. Joseph M. Running at
Memorial Church.
The choir will work on secular
music as well as classical and church
music. Some of the modern Russian
music has been selected for its par
ticular adaptation to a cappella
singing.
