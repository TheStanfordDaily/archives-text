# First Aid Group Holds First Class
## 
### 
The first class meeting of the
Stanford first-aid course, sponsored
by the American Red Cross, was
held yesterday at 4 in the Women's
Gym.
This course, according to the
health department, is open to all
women students who would like to
do something practical during the
present national crisis. At the end
of eight weeks of intensive training
in emergency care, the student will
receive a Red Cross certificate for
first aid work. Classes are to be
held on Wednesday and Friday
afternoons at 4, and a minimum of
three hours a week must be spent
in attending lectures and doing prac
tical work.
Dr. Todd of the School of Health is
in charge of the classes and has in
vited any Stanford Dame interested
to enroll in the course.
