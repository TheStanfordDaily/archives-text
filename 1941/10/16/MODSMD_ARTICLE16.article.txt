# Vaccination Slips Due October 21
## 
### 
Tuesday, Oct. 21, is the deadline
for all new students to turn in vac
cination certificates at the Registrar's
Office. This includes those having
permission to attend classes, and all
other students whose certificates
have expired. Certificates expire
after three years.
There will be a $2 fine for failure
to file certificates by 5 p.m. Tuesday,
and if the certificates are not filed by
October 28, University registration
will be subject to cancellation by the
Committee on Public Health.
