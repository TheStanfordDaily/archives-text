# Allen to Present Weekly Recital
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will present the weekly
organ recital this afternoon at 4:15
in the church. The program will
open with Toccata and Fugue in D
minor, and "Hark, a Voice Saith,
All Are Mortal' by Bach, followed
by KyHe Eleison, from "Cathedral
Windows'" by Karg-Elert, Evening
Song by Schumann and Symphonic
in G minor, op. 18, by Barnes.
