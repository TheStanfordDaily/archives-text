# BULL SESSLON
## 
### CHLSLIE SAROYAN 
Henry David Thoreau Ifl has been relieved of
his task of covering varsity football for The Daily
this week. lie has been standing around sadly, his
fingers twitching for the feel of the typewriter
keys. Not wishing to see a good man like Thoreau
go to waste, and spotting a way to fill up one day's
Bull Session, I herewith turn over this morning's
column to 11. David the younger. Take it, Davey
hoy.
