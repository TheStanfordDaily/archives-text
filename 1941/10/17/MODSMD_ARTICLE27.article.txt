# Good Will Group Convenes Tuesday
## 
### 
Choosing of delegates to the Car
negie Peace Foundation's regional
meeting in San Francisco October
24 and 25 will be the order of busi
ness for the International Pielations
Club meeting Tuesday.

science building. The club's first

Monday. Professor P. W. Buck met
the members and outlined the func
tions of the club. He stressed the
informal, round table discussion of
international affairs at fortnightly
meetings.
