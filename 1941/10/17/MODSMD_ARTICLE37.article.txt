# Debaters Discuss Plans for Quarter
## 
### 
Plans for the current quarter were
discussed at the second meeting of
the Debate Club, held last Tuesday.
Stanford's new debate coach, Dr.
Beck, is here from Hawaii on a one
year exchange professorship, while
the former debate coach, Dr. Lcland
Chapin, has gone to Hawaii.
Included in the announcements
was the news of a round-table dis
cussion to be held at 2:30 this Sat
urday afternoon at the University of
California.
