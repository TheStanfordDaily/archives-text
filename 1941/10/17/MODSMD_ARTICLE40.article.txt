# Freshman Choir Sings Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Truebiood, University
chaplain, will preach at the 11 a.m.
Sunday service in Memorial Church.
The freshmen choir, directed by
Warren D. Allen, organist, will sing,
Dr. Allen will present an organ
recital Sunday at 4 p.m. in the
church.
The Lutheran Students Associa
tion will meet at 6 p.m. Sunday, at
the First Lutheran Church, Homer
and Webster. The discussion topic
will be the history of Lutheranism.
The meeting is open to all Lutheran
students, who are also invited to at
tend the choir concert at 7:45.
The Roger Williams Bible Class
will meet at 10 a.m. Sunday at the
Baptist Church, at which the group
will study the teachings of Jesus.
The Fireside meeting will be held
at 7:30 at the home of Dr. Oliver N.
Johnston, professor emeritus, at 528
Alvarado Row. Rabbi Emil Bern
hard Cohn, noted Hebrew scholar
and formerly of Berlin, will speak.
Members of the Westminster Club
will hold vesper service Sunday at
7 p.m. in the Presbyterian Church.
Following the service Rev. Everett
Clay Thomson will lead a panel
discussion.
The Stanford Episcopal Club will
have supper Sunday at 6 p.m. in the
Parish House of the All Saints Epis
copal Church, which will be fol
lowed by a panel discussion. Eve
ning service will be held at 7:45.
The Wesley Foundation will meet
Sunday for vespers at 7:30 p.m. in
the Methodist Church.
