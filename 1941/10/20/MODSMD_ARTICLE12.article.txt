# University Organist Presented Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, presented an organ recital
yesterday afternoon in Memorial
Church. The program included Toc
cata and Fugue in D minor, and
"Hark, a Voice Saith. 'AH Are Mor
tal"' by Bach; Kyrie eleison, from
"Cathedral Windows" by Karg
jElert; Evening Song by Schumann,
and Symphonic in G minor, op. 18
! by Barnes.
