# Women's Golf Opens Saturday
## 
### 
An. All-University Women's Golf
Tournament will open Saturday, Oc
tober 25, under the sponsorship of
W.A.A.
Only entrance requirement is a
qualification round score card which
shows that the competitor has played
18 holes of golf . These cards must
be filled out by Oct. 25 when they
can be turned in to Mrs. Florence
Smith, Mary Menninger, or Carol
Rhodes at the women's gym. After
contestants have been accepted for
the tournament, they must play off
one competitive round each week.
