# DESTROYER REPORTS 11 MISSING, 10 HURT
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 19 (UP).—
The Navy Department tonight an
nounced that the destroyer Kearny,
which was torpedoed Friday by a
submarine, reported that 11 mem
bers of its crew are missing and 10
others are injured, one critically.
The department then listed the
missing—a majority of them firemen
and water tenders, indicating that
the torpedo which damaged the fast
destroyer must have struck it in or
near one of its two engine rooms.
The announcement did • not en
large on the "missing" angle. This
might, mean that all of the men were
blown into the sea or that some or
all were lying dead behind airtight
doors of a flooded bulkhead.
