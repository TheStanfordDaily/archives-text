# F.R. ORDERS 'HIGH GEAR' RUSS AID
## 
### 
HYDE PARK, N.Y., Oct. 20 (UP).
—President Roosevelt ordered relief
to Russia stepped into high gear to
night after hi.s special lend-lease ex
editor, W. Averell Harriman, re
ported that he has "great confi
dence" in the Soviet army's intention
and ability to maintain resistance
against Nazi Germany's blows.
Mr. Roosevelt sent Harriman back
to Washington post haste, and the
President will follow him back to
Washington by special train tonight.
