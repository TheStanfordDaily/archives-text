# Axe Society Plans Revival Meeting
## 
### 
Members of Axe Society will meet
this afternoon at 5 o'clock in the
Phi Delt house to discuss revival of
the group. The matter was brought
up by the action of Ex Committee's
defunct organizations committee
which recommended cancellation of
their charter.
