# ORGAN RECITAL TODAY
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will present an organ re
cital this afternoon in Memorial
Church at 4:15 p.m. The program
will open with the Overture to the
"Occasional Oratorio" by G. F. Han
del, and will include Large from
the 'Cello Sonata by Chopin, Scher

Builmant, and will conclude with
Allegro Cantabilc and Toccata from
