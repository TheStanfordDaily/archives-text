# Home Decoration 'Y' Group to Meet
## 
### 
The Home Decoration group of
the Y.W.C.A. will meet today in the
"Y" rooms of the Women's Club
house at 4 o'clock. All interested
students, both men and women, are
invited to attend.
Topic of discussion at today's
meeting will be flower arranging.
Mrs. Harry C. Rathbun will speak
on "How to Do a Lot with Nothing,"
and will illustrate her topic by help
ing students to make attractive floral
arrangements with weeds, leaves,
and other outcasts of the garden.
Martha Thurmond, chairman of
the group, has requested that all
guests bring their favorite bowl,
frog, and any such weeds and leaves
as they can.
