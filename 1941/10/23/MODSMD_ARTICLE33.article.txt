# Lew Dorsey Made Band Club Head
## 
### 
Lew Dorsey was elected president
of the Band Club at a Band meeting
Tuesday in the Men's Faculty Club
house. Ed Carpentar was chosen
vice-president, and Rene Zentner
was elected secretary-treasurer, and
publicity director.
It was also announced that Head
Drum Major Dick Hartman will be
in charge of stunts at the football
games. The group plans to meet
twice a month.
