# VANDALS LIGHTHEARTED
## 
### 
MOSCOW, Ida., Oct. 22 (UP).—
Worried over the Vandals' light
hearted attitude toward Willamette,
their next opponent, Coach Francis
Schmidt today sent his University
of Idaho gridders through a defen
sive scrimmage against Bearcat for
mations.
