BERKELEY. Oct. 21 (UP).—Uni
versity of California authorities to
day threatened to bar the use of the
Greek theater for student football
rallies unless the boys stop throw
ing squishy fruits and vegetables.
Tomatoes, etc., stain the granite, the
university said.
