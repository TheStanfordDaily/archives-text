# Branner to Play Beta Theta Pis
## 
### 
Beta Theta Pi will meet Branner
tonight in intramural volleyball at 7
o'clock in the Encina Gym, and El
Tigre will take on El Cuadro in the
only two games of the evening.
The two intramural volleyball
courts will be available for prac
tice from 8 to 10 o'clock after to
night's games.
League standings in volleyball will
be anounced tomorrow by Bob Bur
nett, intramural manager, who also
announced that horseshoe and golf
sign-ups are still open. Play will
begin for horseshoes and golf about
Tuesday of next week.
