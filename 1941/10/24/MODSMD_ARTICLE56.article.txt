# TIGERS LOSE 18-14
## 
### 
COLUMBIA, S.C., Oct. 23 (UP).—
The erratic Clemson Tigers fell from
the ranks of the unbeaten today,
losing to South Carolina 18 to 14 in
their annual grudge game before a
capacity crowd ot 24,000.
