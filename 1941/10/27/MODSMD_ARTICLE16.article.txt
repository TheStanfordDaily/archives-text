# Dr. Allen Presents Afternoon Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, presented the Sunday after
noon recital at 4 p.m. in Memorial
Church opening with Piece heroique
by Cesar Franck. The program in
cluded Prelude in E major from "The
Well - Tempered Clavichord" by
Bach; Recit de tierce en Taille by
de Grigny; Song of the Chrysanthe
mum and Romance sans paroles by
Bonnet; and concluded Pilgrim's
Chorus, from "Tannhauser" by
Richard Wagner.
