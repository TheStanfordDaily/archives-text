# Gold Ingot Stolen En Route to U.S.
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Oct. 27 (UP).
—Lost: one gold brick.
Seventeen ingots arrived at the
U.S. Mint from the Philippines.
They were all gold except one —a
phony lead brick, carefully gilded.
The missing gold ingot was worth
$13,000.
Manila police are working on the
case.
