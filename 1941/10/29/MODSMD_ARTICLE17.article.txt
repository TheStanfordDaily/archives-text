# 60,000 Expected At Bronc Game
## 
### 
AI Masters, director of athletics,
slated that judging from present
ticket returns attendance records for
the Santa Clara-Stanford football
games should be broken this Satur
day.

Returns to date indicate a crowd
of 50,000 to 60,000 will be present at
the contest. The record for the 27-
year-old series was set by last, year's
game crowd of 38,000. At present all
seals between the goal lines have
been sold, and only about three more
sections of the reserved $1.50 seats
are available. Unreserved tickets
go on sale Saturday.

A report from the Santa Clara Ob
servatory indicates that there will be
clear weather Saturday.
