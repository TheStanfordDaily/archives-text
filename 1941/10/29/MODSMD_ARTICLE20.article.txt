# Meyer to Speak For Pre-Med Club
## 
### 
Stanford pre-medical students
will meet tonight at 7:15 in the
Women's Clubhouse. Professor
Emeritus of Anatomy, Arthur W.
Meyer, will present a talk on
"Modern Medicine." This is the first
of pre-med club meetings to feature
a lecture on medicine. All students
interested are invited to attend.
During the year, pre-med club
will have noted doctors and profes
sors speak on ordinary surgery,
plastic surgery, and other timely
topics. Plans for fall quarter have
been drawn up which include tours
of hospitals, one of which will be a
tour of the Los Angeles County Hos
pital for all pre-med students who
go south for the U.S.C.-Stanford
