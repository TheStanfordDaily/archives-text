# ARMY PLANE CRASHES
## 
### 
FRESNO, Oct. 29 (UP). —An army
Pl-17 training biplane which was
being used in the search for three
missing army fighters crashed near
Balch Camp, fiO miles east, of Fresno,
today, but its occupants were re
ported uninjured.
