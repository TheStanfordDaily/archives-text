EUGENE, Ore., Oct. 29 (UP).—
Chuck Elliott and Cliff Gif Fin today
were lacked in a battle for left 1
tackle position on the University of
Oregon football team to replace Ed
I Moshofsky, injured in the U.C.L.A.
I game.
Coach Tex Oliver said Moshofsky
would be on the bench for the Wash
ington State College-Oregon en- [
counter Saturday. lie planned to
use either Elliott or Gif Tin with the
nod going to the man who looks bet
! ter in practice.
