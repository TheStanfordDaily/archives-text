# Del Lyman Goes To Cleveland Rams
## 
### 
A former U.C.L.A. tackle, Del
Lyman, was purchased today by the
Cleveland Rams, National League
professional football club. At the
same time the club revealed from
its Cleveland office that it is nego
tiating for the purchase of Detroit
blocking back Fred Venzo.
