# Bids Go on Sale For Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
With Edward Kennedy (Duke) El
lington's name safely on the dotted
line, tickets for the Sophomore Co
tillion, November 14. will go on sale
tomorrow morning at 8 o'clock in the
A.S.S.U. office.
"Bids are priced at $3.30 and will
be limited to 400." states Hy Hebert,
ticket chairman.
