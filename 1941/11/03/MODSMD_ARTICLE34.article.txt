# Gaels Score 26-0 Win Over Gonzaga
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Nov. 2 (UP).—
Held scoreless in a first-half kick
ing duel, St. Mary's of Moraga today
ripped off four touchdowns in the
last two periods to beat Gonzaga,
26-0, before a crowd of less than
1,000 in rain-swept Kezar Stadium.

PORTLAND, Ore., Nov. 2 (UP).—
A strong Fort Old. Calif., football
team fought off a final period rally
today to defeat the University of
Portland 28-25 before 4000 fans.
