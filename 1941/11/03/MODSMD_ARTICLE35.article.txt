# Kappa Sig, Delt Play-off Today
## 
### 
Kappa Sigma and Delta Tau Delta,
ignoring the fact that they have al
ready played a full game trying to
decide the winner of intramural grid
League 4, will tangle this afternoon
at 3 to play off their 0 to 0 deadlock
of last Wednesday.
Manager Bob Burnett urges teams
to play off their remaining games on
schedule, Wednesday being the last
day for preliminary play.
Tonight the leading frosh teams,
2E and 4W, meet their fellow En
cinamen from Bullpen and Base
ment to finish their schedules
Breakers and Alpha Delt also meet
their final hurdles before league
honors.
