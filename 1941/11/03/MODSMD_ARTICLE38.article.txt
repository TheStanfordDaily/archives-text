# ST. LOUIS BEATS LOYOLA
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Nov. 2 (UP).—
The St. LouLs University Billikcns,
led by fullback Dick Pfuhl, today
effectively bottled up the Loyola
Lions of Los Angeles to score a 21
to 13 victory at Gilmore Stadium.
