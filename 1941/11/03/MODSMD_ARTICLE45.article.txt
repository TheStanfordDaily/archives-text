# WINS MEXICAN GOLF
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Nov. 2 (UP).—
Wilfred Wehrle of Racine, Wis., won
the annual Mexican Amateur Golf
Championship today by beating
Verne Stewart of Roswell, N.M., 2
and 1, in the 86-hole final.
