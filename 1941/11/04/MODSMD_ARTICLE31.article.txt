# Farm Trio to Play Mozart Selections
## 
### 
Three compositions by Mozart
will be played by the Stanford Trio
on the history of the sonata program
tomorrow night in the Education
Auditorium at 8 p.m.

Carl Fuerstner will play the Piano
Sonata in A Minor. Betty Barbour,
'cellist, and Ferenc Molnar, violin
ist, the other members of the Trio
will join in the Violin Sonata No. 15
in B Flat Major and the Trio in E
Major.
