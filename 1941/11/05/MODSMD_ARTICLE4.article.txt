# Drafted 'Mother' Bid Tearful Adieu
## 
### 
The Alpha Dclt "house mother"
is drafted. This morning Joe Wei
ner, long-suffering house mother
for the Alpha Delia I'lii fraternity,
awakened to reveille and hustled
off to his good-bye party.
To the music of a few house
musicians and a great many house
noise-makers lie was taken on a
final review of the campus in a
parade made up of all the old
cars which could he mustered.
Under the sign "Good-bye Ua
suen; llello France" and other ap
prnprate sentiments for one who is
being "selectively serviced," he
was put on the K:5O train.
