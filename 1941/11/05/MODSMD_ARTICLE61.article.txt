# ALABAMA STRIKE
## 
### 
BIRMINGHAM, Ala., Nov. 4 (UP).
—Charles Price, president of the
local Brotherhood of Welders, Burn
ers and Helpers of America, an in
dependent union, said approximately
1,000 welders in Alabama had gone
on strike tonight at various indus
trial plants.

HYDE PARK, N.Y., Nov. 4 (UP).
— President Roosevelt warned
America tonight that it must face
longer working hours and harsher
times if it is to protect its way of
life against the "pagan slavery pro
posed by the would-be dictators of
the world."
