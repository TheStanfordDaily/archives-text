# Alumni Will Hold U.S.C. Game Rally
## 
### 
A Stanford rally, complete with
orchestra and spirit, will be given
for all Stanford students and alumni
Friday night from 5:30 to 8:30 at
the University Club in Los Angeles.
Students are especially invited to
the get-together, which features not
only dancing but also refreshments.
The Los Angeles Men's and Wom
en's Clubs are sponsoring the annual
rally.
"We want all Stanford men and
women to regard the club as their
headquarters," said Robert (Bones)
Hamilton.
