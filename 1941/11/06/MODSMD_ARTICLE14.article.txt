# POLICE CHIEF TRIED
## 
### 
WOODLAND, Nov. 5 (UP).—The
trial of Police Chief Clifford W.
Garrison of Woodland, charged with
taking bribes from gamblers, was
thrown into confusion late today
when the first witness was arrested
as he walked from the courtroom.
