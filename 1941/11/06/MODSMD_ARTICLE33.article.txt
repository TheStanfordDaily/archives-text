# Frosh Debators To Go to Utah
## 
### 
The freshman debate squad will
definitely send a two-man team to
compete in the Western Intercolle
giate Debate Tournament at Weber
College, Ogden, Utah, on Novem
ber 17, 18, and 19, Ed Conn, fresh
man debate manager, announced
yesterday.

The two debaters will be selected
with the next few days. Both men
will be entered in all debates, ex
temporaneous, impromptu and ora
torical speaking contests. The de
bate subject will be: "Resolved, that
the federal government should regu
late by law all labor unions in the
United States."
Two freshman teams debated this
subject last night in a practice de
bate in the Memorial Auditorium.
