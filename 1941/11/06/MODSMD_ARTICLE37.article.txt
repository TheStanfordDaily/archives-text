# Religious Group Opens Library
## 
### 
Christian Science Organization of
Stanford University announces the
opening of its new Organization
Room, located in room 3 of the Old
Union. The room is open Monday
through Friday from 8:30 in the
morning until noon for quiet reading
of the Christian Science textbook
and authorized periodicals. There
are marked books for the study of
the weekly lesson-sermon.

A lending library is maintained
where all of Mrs. Eddy's works are
available as well as the Christian
Science Monitor, ,Journal, Sentinel
and French Herald.

The room is open to students,
graduates, and faculty. The Organ
ization extends an invitation for all
interested to make use of its facili
ties.
