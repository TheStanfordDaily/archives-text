# Theta Sigma Phi To Hold Initiation
## 
### 
Three women were pledged to
Theta Sigma Phi, national honorary
fraternity for women in journalism,
at a service held last night in La
gunita Court. The new pledges are
Gail Mclnerney, Marilyn Scott, and
Dorothy Gile.
According to the rules of the or
ganization, the pledges will be ini
tiated sometime next month.
