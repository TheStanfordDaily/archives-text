# A.F.L. PICKETS NAVY
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, Nov. 6 (UP). Two
government projects, a naval train
ing station and a Marine camp, to
night were picketed by men from
six A.F.L. unions who halted work
on projects costing $5,500,000 be
cause of a wage dispute.

The Los Angeles Memorial Coli
seum seats 105,000 people.
