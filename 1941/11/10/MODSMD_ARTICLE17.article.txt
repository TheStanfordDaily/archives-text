# Paly Holds Polish Hospital Festival
## 
### 
Featuring a cavalry drill by the
Franciscan Lancers at El Camino
baseball diamond and a bazaar to
be held at the Palo Alto Community
Center, the Polish Market Day Fes
tival for the benefit of the Pader
ewski Hospital in Edinburgh, Scot
land, was held Saturday.
The celebration was given under
the auspices of the local committee
for the Paderewski Testimonial
Fund. The hospital which the fund
supports is to serve Polish civil
ians and military refugees. After
the war the fund will be used to
help rehabilitate Poland.
Following the bazaar the Jordan
Junior High School band presented
a program of orchestral music. Hal
Moreno's orchestra played for
dancing from i) until 12.
