# Stanford Alumnus Given Commission
## 
### 
John R. Casey, Stanford '40, has
been commissioned a second lieu
tenant in the Air Corps Reserve and
ordered to active duty for one year
at Hamilton Field. Lieutenant
Casey's period of active service may
be renewed during the present na
tional emergency. At the end of
that time, he may be relieved and
accept private employment with a
civilian airline while retaining his
reserve rank as lieutenant.
