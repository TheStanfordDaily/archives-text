# STRIKERS RELEASED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 9 (UP).—
Forty-four A.F.L. pickets arrested
by a police riot squad during a tu
multuous demonstration at the Em
pire Hotel were released on bail
today.

Drivers in motorcades must allow
sufficient distance between cars to
permit other motorists to overtake
and pass in safety.
