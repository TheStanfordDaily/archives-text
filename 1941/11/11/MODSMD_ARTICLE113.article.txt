# Jewelry Hearts, Petal Clips, Musts
## 
### 
Diamond Lil heart pins, faked in
rhinestones, are taking their place
right at the top of the latest musts
in milady's jewelry box. Very racy
and ravishing, they can make an old
dress new, and inspire a reputation
for swank dressing.
If your pastel wools or your for
the-City black dresses need a little
perking up, try a flower pin of pearls
for a gala bloom. Mounted on a
gold-plated base, graceful twisted
petals with a wind-blown look to
match clever little clustered earrings
picking up the center shade of the
flower.
