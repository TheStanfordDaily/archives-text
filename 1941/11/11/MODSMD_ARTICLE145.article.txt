# American Painting Backs New Cards
## 
### 
Distinguished offerings in the
playing card assortment are the
American Artists cards, with backs
that are reproductions of paintings
by John Steuart Curry, Thomas Ben
ton, and Dale Nichols.
The perennial washable Kern cards
feature a back design centered by
a clipper ship. Abercrombie and
Fitch will continue to make play
ing cards on order backed with a
photograph of a dog, a country
house, and other subjects. A mini
mum order consists of 24 packs at
$24.
