# Yarn Dogs, Horses Decorate Rooms
## 
### 
Yarn toys are novelties that you
will want for your own room as
well as for gifts this year.
Knitted horses are designed with
wide backs and arched necks just
like the circus types. Pekinese dogs
come with long, floppy, white yarn
ears and black button eyes, barely
visible through the knitted hair.
