# Librarian to Talk To Reading Group
## 
### 
Members of the reading group of
the Stanford Dames will hold their
first program meeting of the year
this afternoon at 2 p.m. at the home
of Mrs. J. B. Hurlbut in Mayfield.
Marjorie Frink of the Palo Alto Li
brary will be the speaker.
Tomorrow evening at 8 p.m. the
bridge group will be entertained by
Mrs. H. A. Domonoske, 676 Mayfield.
The music section will meet Thurs
day afternoon at 2 p.m. at the home
of W. T. Mallison.
A general meeting is scheduled
for Thursday evening at 7:30 p.m. in
the Women's Clubhouse. A talk by
Dr. Helen Pryor will feature the
session.
