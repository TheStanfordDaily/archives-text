# Labor Panel Set For Lunch Club
## 
### 
Featuring a faculty-student panel
discussion on the Pacific Labor
School and its activities, the Wed
nesday Luncheon Club will meet in
the Nitery today at 12:15.
Professors Phillip W. Buck and
William S. Hopkins, members of the
Stanford faculty and the faculty of
the school, and Kenneth Smith, Su
san Atkinson, and Mrs. Ellen Smith,
former students at the Labor School,
now at Stanford, will participate in
the discussion.
Lunch Club meets every Wednes
day during the school year. Meet
ings are informal, and all are wel
come. Lunch is 30 cents.
