# Physicist to Talk For Journal Club
## 
### 
Charles F. Otis, teaching assist
ant in physics, will address the
Journal Club of the Department of
Physics tomorrow at 4 p.m. in Room
372. His subject will be "Measur

Voltages."

and is studying here for his Ph.D.
