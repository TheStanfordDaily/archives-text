# Nominations End For Frosh Today
## 
### 
Nominations of candidates for the
two freshman class offices, president
(a man) and secretary-treasurer (a
woman), will be carried on today
until 5 p.m., when all blanks must
be turned in at the English Corner,
according to Jim White, chairman
of the Board of Elections.
Monday night the candidates will
appear at general meetings at both
Roble and Encina.
Fifteen signatures are needed to
place a name on the ballot. Elections
will be held Tuesday, Nov. 18.
