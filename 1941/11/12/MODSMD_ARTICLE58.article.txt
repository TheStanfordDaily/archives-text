TIIE STANFORD DAMES' evening
bridge group will meet tonight at
8 o'clock at the home of Mrs. H. A.
Domonoske, G76 May field.

will not hold a meeting tonight due
to conflict with the Senior Sing.

9:1)0 to 12, Sunday. Contact the
Airport for reservations.

Gaieties are to report to the Ram's

day has been canceled.
