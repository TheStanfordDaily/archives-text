# Special Daily Issue Previews Fashions
## 
### 
Inside today's Daily is a 10-page
fashion edition. Edited by Kose
maric Mullanv, Women's Editor of
The Daily, this special edition pre
views winter fashions for the cam
pus, the city, and off - campus
week-ends.
Fashion photos were taken in
Palo Alto and San Francisco shops
by John Stoncr, head photogra
pher of The Daily. Stoner was as
sisted by Lou Einstein and Hale
Field. Quad photographers.
