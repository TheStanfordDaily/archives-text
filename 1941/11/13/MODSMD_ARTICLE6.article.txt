# Organist to Play At Recital Today
## 
### 
Joseph M. Running, assistant or
ganist, will play at the weekly after
noon organ recital today at 4:15
p.m. in Memorial Church.
He will open with Nun Danket by
Karg-Elert. The rest of the program
will include Vision by Rheinberger,
Eist das Heil uns kommen her by
Bach, Suite Gothique by Boellmann
which includes Introduction-choral,
Menuet gothique, Priere a Notre
Dame, and Toccata.
