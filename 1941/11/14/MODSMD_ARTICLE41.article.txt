# Harriers to Meet Ucla on Saturday
## 
### 
With the last of the four-mile
practice runs behind it, and only
the shorter distances of a mile or so
remaining for this week, the varsity
cross-country team is rapidly reach
ing top form for its meet with
U.C.L.A. to be held tomorrow.
The Indianmen will be led by

Francis Olsen, Russ Collier and Wy
land, who will be seeking to notch
their first victory of the year over a
team which defeated them by one
point last year.
Ucla's six-man team will be led by
Capt. Leon Miller, who has turned
in the time of 25:37 over a distance
of four miles and a quarter.
