# Shriner Meeting Hears Dr. Wilbur
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur, president
of Stanford University, spoke today
at a luncheon given in honor of the
Golden Anniversary celebration by
the Islam Temple Shriners in the
Palm Court of the Palace Hotel.
Dr. Wilbur gave a brief history of
the school and pointed out "what a

different California we would have
today if it had not been for Senator
Stanford and Mrs. Stanford." He
also told of his trip to Atlanta and
of the general reaction the people
have toward higher education
through the country.
Dr. Robert E. Swain, general

chairman of the Fiftieth Anniver
sary celebration, and Frank F.
Walker, financial vice-president of
the University, also gave talks on
the school and its history.
PALM SPRINGS, Nov. 13 (UP). -
Lieutenant E. G. Gross, 25, Army Air
Corps pilot, was killed today when
the P-38D interceptor plane he was
flying crashed three miles north
east of here.
