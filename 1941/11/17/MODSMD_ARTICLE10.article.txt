# Stanford Women Hold Sports Day
## 
### 
U.C., Mills and Stanford women
met Saturday morning from 9 o'clock
until noon for their Fall Tri-Sports
Day. Hockey teams from each class
played their class team from each
of the other two schools in the meet,
honors being about equally divided
among the three.
Mills, Cal and Stanford each en
tered four singles players and four
doubles teams for the badminton
tournament which was played in the
Gym. The six-man golf teams met
on the golf course, where they had
lunch together alterwai'd. Buffet
lunch was served in the patio of the
Women's Gym for the other con
testants, where opponents got better
acquainted.
Winners of these meets are not
announced, and no formal record is
made of the outcome of events.
