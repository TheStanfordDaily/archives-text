# Pilots Sight Army Bomber Wreckage
## 
### 
BANGOR, Me., Nov. 16 (UP). -
Her crew of four apparently dead,
the chaired wreckage of a huge
twin-motored U.S. Army bombing
plane was sighted today in the wil
derness 10 miles south of Springfield.
Two pilots from Bangor Air Base
circled the region. There was no in
dication the crew had escaped, they
said.
