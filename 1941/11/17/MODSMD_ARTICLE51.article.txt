# NAVY BASE BEGUN
## 
### 
MORRO BAY, Nov. 1(5 (UP). -
Construction started this week-end
on the Morro Bay section base, a
$500,000 project which will furnish a
base for the U.S. Navy patrol force
operating from San Francisco Bay.

Time cards for NYA work are due
by 5 o'clock Monday in the cashier's
ollice of the Administration Build
ing, according to Miss Palmer. Hours
of work done Monday night may be
turned in before 10 Tuesday /horn
ing.
