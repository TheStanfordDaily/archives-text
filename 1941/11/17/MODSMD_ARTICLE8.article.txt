# Frosh Pictorial On Sale Today
## 
### 
"Know Your Freshmen," the
Freshman Pictorial Handbook, goes
on sale today at the A.S.S.U. office
for 75 cents, according to Norm
Ross, editor.
The handbook has a red and
white paper cover, and is 9 by 12
inches in size; it contains 26 pages
of pictures of the entire Class of
1945. Besides the pictures of the
freshmen, names and home towns
are also listed.
This is the first year that such a
handbook has been printed. The
project was authorized last year by l
Excom, and Ross was appointed edi
tor at that time.
Because copies were distributed to
all freshman men and women yes
terday, only a limited number are
on sale at the A.S.S.U. office.
