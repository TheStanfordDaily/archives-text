# Sailing Club Meets Tonight in Nitery
## 
### 
Stanford's Sailing Club, looking
forward to its coming meet with
U.S.C., Uela, and Cal Tech during
the Newport Harbor Yacht Club
Christmas racing week, welcomes all
old members and prospective new
ones tonight in the Nitery at 7:30.
Bob Allen, president, expects good
representation, and is especially
anxious to sec a large number of
frosh.
