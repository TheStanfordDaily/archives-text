# CIVIL SERVICE EXAMS
## 
### 
A Civil Service examination for
the position of state toll bridge col
lector will be given Dec. 6 in San
Francisco and Sacramento. The col
lecting jobs pay a starting salary of
one hundred sixty dollars a month.
Information and applications for the
examination may be obtained by
writing the State Personnel Board
in Sacramento. Applications must
be filed by midnight of November 22.
