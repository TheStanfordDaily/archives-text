# Spanish Movies At Mountain View
## 
### 
The Spanish honor fraternity of
Sigma Delta Pi has a new form of
visual education which it has sug
gested to the Spanish department.
Every week some 90 members of the
various classes in Spanish attend the
Latin American motion pictures
which are being billed in lights at
Mountain View Theater houses.
