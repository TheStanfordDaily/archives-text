# NYA Committee Passes Resolution
## 
### 
Ihe State Advisory Committee of
the NYA unanimously endorsed a
resolution to President Roosevelt
and Security Administrator Paul V.
McNutt at. a dinner meeting last
week-end. This resolution urged
continuation of the National Youth
Administration defense training and
student work program.
Recognizing the need for economy,
the consolidation of NYA and CCC
to form one youth organization un
der direction of NYA officials was
advised. 1 The resolution points out
that the NYA has the necessary ad
ministrative machinery to take over
and operate the CCC camps when
army officials are withdrawn for
more important army duties.
