# Kefauver Invites Teachers to Meet
## 
### 
"First-year teachers" who have
gone out from Stanford are invited
to meet the morning of Nov. 29 to
discuss education problems with
members of the education faculty.
Dr. Grayson N. Kefauver an
nounced the following program:
From 9 to 9:30 open house will be
held in the lobby of the Education
Building. From 9:30 to 10 a Gen
eral Session will be conducted in the
Social Hall (e7B). At 10 and contin
uing until 12 conferences will be
held in the faculty offices on special
teaching problems and interests.
