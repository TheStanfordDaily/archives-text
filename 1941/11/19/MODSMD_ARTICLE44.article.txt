# Pan-American Club To Meet Saturday
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Pan-
American Club will be held Satur
day at the home of Mrs. Maria Hogg
at 7:30 p.m. Students are to meet
first in front of the Hoover Library,
under whose auspices the club is be
ing formed.
All Latin-American students are
invited to Thanksgiving dinner by
Mrs. Hogg, who is the librarian in
charge of the Pan-American office
of the Hoover Library.
