# BRUINS POLISH OFFENSE
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 18 (UP).—
Coach Babe Horrell concentrated
on polishing up the offensive power
of the U.C.L.A. Bruins today as the
varsity squads went through a long
dummy practice. For the first time
in several weeks, the entire Bruin
squad was in uniform and in top
physical condition.
