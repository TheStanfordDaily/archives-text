# Math Club to Hold Initial Meeting
## 
### 
"The Pythagorean Theorem" will
be the topic of a speech by Paul C.
Rosenbloom at the first meeting of
the undergraduate Mathematics
Club. The meeting which is to be
held Wednesday at 4:20 p.m. in Room
73 is open to all students interested
in mathematics. Refreshments will
be served before the meeting.
