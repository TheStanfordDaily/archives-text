# PERCIVAL WREN DIES
## 
### 
AMBERLEY, Eng., Nov. 23 (UP).
! —Percival Christopher Wren, noted
British author, died Saturday after
a long illness, it was revealed to
day. Wren's most famous book was
Beau Geste, written in 1924.
