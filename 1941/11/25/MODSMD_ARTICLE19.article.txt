# Farm Jolly-up Set for Friday
## 
### 
The Big Game Jolly-up will be
held Friday in ihe Women's Gym
from 4:30 io 6:00 p.m. Gary Evers'
Orchestra will furnish the music for
the dance, according to Jack Dana,
Jolly-up chairman. Members of the
committee in charge of preparations
are Charlotte Drea and Sheldon
Parker.
The dance is open to everyone and
the committee urges that everyone
turn out to make it a really big
success.
