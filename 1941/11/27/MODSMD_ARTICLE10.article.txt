# Co-op to Elect New Manager Monday
## 
### 
The Co-op book exchange will
hold a quarterly membership meet
ing Monday afternoon at 4 in the
Nitery to elect a manager to take
office immediately. Two nominees,
Philip O'Donnell and Jim Anderson,
were chosen at a Board of Directors
meeting Tuesday from students, who
attended a tryout period for that
position last week.
At this meeting, the manager and
accountant will give their quarterly
reports. As there will be three va
cancies on the Board of Directors,
members to fill them will also be
elected.
