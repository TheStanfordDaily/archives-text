# Grade Envelopes Go on Sale Dec. 1
## 
### 
Students who wish to receive their
grades for this quarter during
Christmas vacation should leave
stamped, self-addressed envelopes at
the Registrar's Office before the end
of the quarter.
Grades will be mailed out about
December 19. For convenience of
the students, envelopes will be sold
at the Registrar's Office for five cents
each. The money received for these
envelopes is turned over to the
Stanford Convalescent Home for
children. Envelopes will go on sale
about December 1.
