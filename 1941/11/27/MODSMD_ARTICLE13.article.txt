# Big Game Jolly-Up To Be Staged Friday
## 
### 
The Big Game Jolly-up, only
all-campus jolly-up for the entire
quarter, will be held tomorrow af
ternoon from 4:30 to 6 in the Wom
en's Gym, announced Shelly
Parker, dance chairman yester
day. Gary Evers and his orches
tra will furnish music for the
dancers.
Everyone is invited to attend the
jolly-up, the only free entertain
ment of the Big Game week-end,
according to Parker, who has been
assisted in planning the dance by
Jack Dana and Charlotte Drea.
