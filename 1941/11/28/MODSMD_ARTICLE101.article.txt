# Big Nines, Draftees May Clash in Spring
## 
### 
Of interest to every big-league
baseball fan is the unverified report
that negotiations are underway to
arrange Army games with top-flight
ball clubs.
The idea was brought to life by
the fact that a large per cent of the
high-salaried players of last season
are now selectees in the United
States armed forces, and with the
lengthening of spring practice, the
chances for the deal going through
are said to be favorable.
