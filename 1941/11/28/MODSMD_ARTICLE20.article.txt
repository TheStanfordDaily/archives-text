# Poet to Broadcast To Latin America
## 
### 
Dr. Carera Andrade, modernist
Ecuadorian poet, will discuss "The
New Poetry of Ecuador" in the regu
lar shortwave Pan American broad
cast from the Hoover War library
today.
The program which began early
this fall has as its purpose the fos
tering of friendly Pan American
relationships, and is broadcast by
short wave through station KGEI
and picked up by the powerful Gua
temalan government station and re
broadcast throughout Central and
South America.
