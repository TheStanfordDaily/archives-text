# Libe Bells to Ring In Special Concert
## 
### 
James R. Lawson, carillonneur,
will present the following program
next Sunday, Nov. 30, at 1:30 p.m.
from the Hoover War Library: "The
Harmonious Blacksmith," Aria con
variazioni, from "Fifth Suite," G. F.
Handel; "The Lost Chord," Sir Ar
thur Sullivan; "Laudes Domini," Jo
seph Barnaby; "Dennis," J. G. Na
geli; "Bently," John Hullah; and
"Ave Maria," Franz Schubert.
