# Addresses Asked By Draft Board
## 
### 
All students graduating or leaving
Stanford at the end of this quarter
have been requested by the local
draft board to report to the board
their future location and occupation,
according to an announcement from
Dr. Karl M. Cowdery, assistant reg
istrar.
It was also urged that students
leaving the campus for the Christ
mas holidays report their mailing
address.
Dr. Cowdery also reminded all
students not in deferred classifica
tions whose numbers will probably
come up during the winter quarter
to check with the board before reg
istering on January T.
