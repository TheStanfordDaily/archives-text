# ARMY OPENS AIR SCHOOLS
## 
### 
MCFFETT FIELD, Jan. 5 (UP).—
The West Coast Air Corps training
confer today announced two new
schools were established this week
to train army pilots and crews in
operation of medium and heavy
bombers.
