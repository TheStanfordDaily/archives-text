# Navy Air School Trains 21 Alumni
## 
### 
More than 20 per cent of the 2200
flight students now in training at the
Navy's gigantic new air station at
Corpus Christi, Texas, are alumni
of California colleges and univer
sities.
The University of California leads
with 66 alumni represented. Stan
ford, with 21 attending, is tied with
San Jose State, U.C.L.A., and U.S.C.
for second honors. Six hundred sixtj
alumni represent the 551 schools of
the West Coast.
The Corpus Christi Air Station
trains flyers for the Navy and Ma
rine Corps air forces. The seven
months' course includes basic train
ing and advanced work with dive
bombers, patrol boats, scout sea
planes, and fighters.
