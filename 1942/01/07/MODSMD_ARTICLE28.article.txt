# Allen Will Play At Weekly Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the weekly or
gan recital in the Memorial Church
tomorrow afternoon from 4:15 to
4:45 p.m. The public is invited.
The program will be as follows:
March from "Thesee," Lully; "The
Old Year Now Hath Passed Away,"
Bach; Gigue in G, Arne; Minuet in
G, Purcell, arranged for organ by
Williams; Basso staccato (alia mar
cia), Waters; and Piece heroique, !
Franck.
