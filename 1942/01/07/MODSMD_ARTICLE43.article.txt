# Tryouts to Open For New Musical
## 
### 
Tryouts for "The Beggars' Op
era," a presentation of the music
and drama departments, will take
place next Friday and Monday eve
nings in the Little Theater from
7:30 to 10:30. Tryouts for "Marco
Millions" will continue tonight.
"The Beggars' Opera" will be
staged Feb. 5, 6, and 7 in Memorial
Auditorium. Dr. Herbert Jan Pop
uer will supervise the music part
of the program, and Dr. F. Cowles
Strickland will handle the acting.
There are numerous parts open in
both sections.
