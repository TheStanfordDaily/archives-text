# HORROR IN THE ATLANTIC
## 
### 

ACCORDING TO BRITISH reports, this is a war-time horror story that comes from the mid-Atlantic. A
British rescue ship, her sick hays jammed with injured survivors of sunken merchant vessels, was bombed
by a German plane, set afire, and lost. Here are scenes from the eventual rescue. Upper left: the group gets
to a raft as the lifeboat goes under. Left center: two rafts cf survivors escape from the blazing ship. Lower
left: a group on a raft waits to be saved. Lower right: men on raft about to be picked up. Upper right:
survivors helped aboard warship.
