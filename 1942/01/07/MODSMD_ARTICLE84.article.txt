# Navy Issues Call For Wirelessmen
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 6 (UP). The
Navy last night sent out call for 5,000
young men experienced in amateur
or commercial radio fields or with
training in radio service work. Rear
Admiral Randall Jacoobs, chief of the
Bureau of Navigation, said in a radio
(NBC) appeal "the Navy needs them
now!"
