# Dean Bunn Speaks At Toyon Smoker
## 
### 
Dean John Bunn spoke to the
Toyon Club about the effects of the
war emergency on Stanford last
night at an informal hall smoker
held at 10 p.m. in the Toyon lobby.
The program was arranged by Toyon
President Bob Hinze to start off win
ter club activities which will include
the annual Toyon Winter Formal.
Cigars and cigarettes were given
out to those present and following
the talk ice cream was served. Re
freshments were handled by Don
Craig.
