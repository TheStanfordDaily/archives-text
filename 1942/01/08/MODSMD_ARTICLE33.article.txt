# Women's First Aid Course Reopened
## 
### 
Due to popular demand another
course in First Aid is being offered
by the Women's Physical Educa
tion Department. The class will give
three units of credit and will be
held second period Monday and
Wednesday and for two hours on
Friday.
Also an evening course in Homo
Nursing will be given if enough in
terest is shown. All women inter
ested should apply at the Y.W.C.A.
i office.
