# Row Rulers Meet Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
In its first meeting of the quarter,
the Stanford Interfraternity Coun
cil will meet tomorrow night at 6:15
at the Delta Upsilon house, accord
ing to Jim Haugh, Interfraternity
president.
On tap for discussion is the or
ganization of the fraternities for air
raid and blackout warnings as well
as progress in rushing this year.
"So far, the new winter quarter
rushing system seems to be agree
able to both the freshmen and the
boys in the houses," added Haugh.
