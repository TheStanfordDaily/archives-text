# New War Course Opens in Hygiene
## 
### 
Hygiene 152, "Individual Hygiene
in Wartime," is still open to regis
tration. The course will include in
struction in the prevention of com
municable diseases, in nutrition, in
mental and occupational hygiene,
and in other problems concerned
with individual health and fitness
under war conditions.
Hygiene 152 is held in Room 42 of
the B.A.C. Monday, Wednesday, and
Friday at 9, and is open to all men
and women students.
