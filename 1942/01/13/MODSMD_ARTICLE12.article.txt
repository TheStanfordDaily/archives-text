# Frosh Debaters To Discuss Plans
## 
### 
Freshman debaters will meet with
Coach George J. Peavey at 4:15 this
afternoon in Room 102 Memorial
Hall to discuss plans for the winter
quarter.

Ed Conn, frosh debate manager,
will announce a tentative debate
schedule and will arrange for teams
to meet those opponents already
scheduled. One of the main activi
ties of the quarter will be a debate
between representatives of Roble
and Encina.
