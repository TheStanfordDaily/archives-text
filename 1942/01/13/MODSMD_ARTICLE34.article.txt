# Commission Delays On Shift Petition
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 12 (UP).
—The State Industrial Accident
Commission today postponed action
on a defense industry petition for
permission to employ women on
night shifts at straight time pay.
Present laws require that women
be paid time and a half wages for
work between 11 p.m. and 6 a.m.
Southern California airplane com
panies, which plan to employ women
in increasing numbers, were prin
cipal sponsors of the petition.
