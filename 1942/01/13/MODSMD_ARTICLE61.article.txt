# Encinamen Elect Officers Today
## 
### 
Bent on electing two of six boys
to the posts of Encina president and
Hall manager, two of the most im
portant elective posts open to fresh
men, the 450 members of Encina go
to the polls today.
Rueben Mettler, Bob Barnes, and
A1 Shansky are the candidates for
president of Encina; Ed Conn, Norm
King, and John Twitchell vie for
the managership which carries with
it the administrative duties in the
hall social club and recreational
program.
The polls will be open from 8
o'clock in the morning until 3 o'clock
in the afternoon.
