# FATHERS DEFERRED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 12 (UP).—
Married men with one or more chil
dren probably will not be called up
for military service until an army of
7,000,000 to 8,000,000 men has been
created, Selective Service Director
Brigadier General Lewis B. Hershey
said tonight.
