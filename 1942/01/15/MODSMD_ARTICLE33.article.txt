# New Draft Sign-up Set for Feb. 14-16
## 
### 
The three-day registration period
in California under the Selective
Training and Service Act will be
held Saturday, Sunday, and Mon
day, February 14-16 inclusive, with
registration places open between the
hours of 7 a.m. and 9 p.m.
An estimated 600,000 men in the
20-44 year age group will be obliged
to sign up in the nation's third reg
istration, it was announced yester
day by Brig. Gen. Joseph O. Dono
van, state director of Selective
Service.
