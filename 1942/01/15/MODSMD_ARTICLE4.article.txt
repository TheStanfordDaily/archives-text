# Cal Announces Poetry Contest
## 
### 
Announcement of the Ina Coolbrith
Memorial Poetry Contest has been
received by the English Department
from the University of California.
The prize was established by friends
of the late Ina Coolbrith, former
California poet laureate.
A prize of $35 will be awarded for
the best original, unpublished poem
on any subject, composed by an un
dergraduate. Students from Stan
ford, Mills College. St. Mary's, Santa
Clara, College of the Pacific and the
University of San Francisco are eli
gible to participate.
Stanford students wishing to enter
poems in the competition may leave
their manuscripts with the secre
tary of the Stanford English Depart
ment in Room 45 not later than Feb
ruary 27, 1942.
