# Riggs Beats Budge In Pro Tennis Tilt
## 
### 
CHIAGO, Jan. 14 (UP). —Bobby
1 Riggs increased his lead in "pay-
I off" standings of the Alexis Thomp
son professional tennis tour tonight
with a 6-3, 6-3 victory over Don
Budge.
In the other match before a crowd
of 5,000 in Chicago Stadium, Fred
Perry downed Frank Kovacs, 6-8,
; 6-3, 6-4.
