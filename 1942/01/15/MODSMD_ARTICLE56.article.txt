# Turnout for Cal League Increases
## 
### 
Four clubs are entered in the Cal
ifornia Baseball League for the 1942
season, showing a better turnout than
last year. President William Schroe
der said that prospects are much
brighter despite the war.
Schroeder also stated that twi
light games will be played if night
contests are ruled out.

Frank Albert was the only man
on the 1940 team to play an entire
game without substitution. He
played for 60 minutes during the
Big Game.
