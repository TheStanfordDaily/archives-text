# Summer Forestry Positions Available
## 
### 
Summer jobs with the State For
estry Department can be had this
year by qualified students, Miss
Palmer of the student employment
agency said. The department is in
need of fire fighters, truck drivers,
Pre look-outs, and fire-fighting fore-
Men.
Students considering such positions
during this coming summer should
report to the student employment
agency immediately, for the appli
cations must be sent soon to the State
Personnel Department.
