# Women's Nom Com Members Elected
## 
### 
Five of the eight members of
Women's Nominating Committee
were elected at yesterday's meet
ing of Women's Conference. Those
named were Mary Bills. Ann Bos
singer, Margaret Elliot, Alastair
Mat Donald, and Barbara Rapp.
The other three women on the
committee whose membership is
stipulated in the Constitution are
Pat Adams, vice-president of the
student body, Rosemarie Mullany,
chairman of Women's Council, and
Irma Jean Hannibal, president of
Women's Conference and chairman
of Nominating Committee.
