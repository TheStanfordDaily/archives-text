# Farm Man Taken By Japs on Wake
## 
### 
Ensign George H. Henshaw,
U.S.N., Stanford '40, w;is among the
men captured by the Japanese on
Wake Island, according to his
mother, Mrs. Marshall Henshaw, who
is living in Palo Alto.
It had been rumored that Ensign
Henshaw, known here as "Bucky,"
was killed in action during the Pearl
Harbor incident.
Though Ensign Henshaw was
really attached to the naval forces
at Honolulu, he had volunteered for
special communications duty at
Wake.
