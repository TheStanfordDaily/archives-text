# Thomas to Sing Tonight in S.F.
## 
### 
"I Hear America Sinking," an in
terpretation of America in song with
John Charles Thomas as soloist, will
be presented by the Oratorio Society
of San Francisco at the War Memo
rial Opera House tonight at 8:30.
The program contains several
works of modern American compos
ers while "I Hear America Singing"
is based on poems by Walt Whitman.
Mr. Thomas is aided by a chorus in
singing the American story which
has been woven into music.
The Oratorio Society, directed by
Waldemar Jacobsen, will also pre
sent a group of songs apart from the
cantata.
