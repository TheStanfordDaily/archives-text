# RUBBER SOURCES IN U.S.
## 
### 
BERKELEY. Jan. 19 (UP).—Dr.
T. Harper Goodspeed, professor of
botany at University of California,
said tonight 500.000.000 pounds of
usable rubber could be obtained by
extraction from plants growing wild
in alkali lands of six western states
— California, Nevada, Colorado,
Utah. New Mexico, and Idaho.
