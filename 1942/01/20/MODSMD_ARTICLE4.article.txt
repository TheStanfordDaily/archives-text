# M.A. Applications Due by February 2
## 
### 
Graduate students planning to
complete requirements for the Mas
ter of Arts or Engineer's degree in
June should have their applications
for admission to candidacy filed in
the Registrar's Office by February 2,
it was announced yesterday.
Applications for the degree to be
conferred in April should be filed as
soon as possible. Blanks may be ac
quired at the Registrar's Office.
