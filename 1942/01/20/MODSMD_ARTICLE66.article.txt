# Prospective Yell Leaders to Meet
## 
### 
Any sophomores interested in be
coming assistant yell leaders next
year are asked to meet with Joe
Messina, head yell leader, this af
ternoon at 4 o'clock in front of the
Pavilion.
"No experience is necessary,"
states Messina in issuing a call for
tryoutees. Messina, with his assist
ants, Bud Clary and Doc Colyear,
will coach prospective yell kings for
several weeks before giving them
practical experience at basketball
games.
In February the head yell leader
will be named for next year with
two of the sophomores chosen as
assistants.
