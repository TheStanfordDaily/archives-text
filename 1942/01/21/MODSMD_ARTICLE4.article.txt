# Row to Launch New Rush Period
##

After a week and a half without
formal rushing activity, Encinamen
again visit Stanford fraternities Fri
day and Saturday afternoon and
night in the fourth regular rushing
period.
Rushces will receivc cards today
and tomorrow for five engagements
this week-end. Two Friday periods
11:30 a.m. to 1 p.m. and 6 to 8 p.m.
and three Saturday periods 9 to 11
a.m., 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m., 6 to
8 p.m. The breakfast period on Sat
urday appears for the first time this
rush period.

before formal pledging. Here is the
remaining schedule:
