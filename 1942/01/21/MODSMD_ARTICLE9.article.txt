# Pre-Meds to Hear Defense Program
## 
### 
"National Defense and Medicine"
will be discussed in all of its phases
by Dr. Russell Lee at the meeting of
the Pre-med Club tonight. The
meeting will be in Women's Club
house at 7:30.
Plans for the quarter include tours
of hospitals in San Francisco, mo
tion pictures of surgical operations
with specialists in that particular
line lecturing on the operation, and
student participation in discussion on
current medical questions such as
plague and epidemics during war.
This last topic will be the subject of
next week's meeting.
