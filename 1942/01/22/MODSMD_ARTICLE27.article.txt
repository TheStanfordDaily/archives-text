# JAPS FLOP AGAIN IN BATAAN TRY
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 21 (UP). -
The fourth Japanese knockout at
tempt against Bataan Peninsula de
fenders in the Philippines has failed
utterly, with "very heavy" losses to
the enemy, the Army said today.
