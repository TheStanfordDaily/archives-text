# Cal Game Tickets Go on Public Sale
## 
### 
Five hundred eighty-one student
rooter tickets for tomorrow night's
Cal game have been sold, and the
remainder has been placed on pub
lic sale for 75 cents. Miss DuPrau of
the B.A.C. stated last night.
Unused tickets may be returned
to the B.A.C. until 5 o'clock tomor
row evening. Students whose names
are on the waiting list will receive
their tickets as the unused ones
come in. Only general admission
tickets will be on sale at the gate
tomorrow night.
