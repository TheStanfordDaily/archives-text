# Organist Will Give Afternoon Recital
## 
### 
Joseph M. Running, assistant or
ganist, will present an organ recital
today at 4:15 p.m. in the Memorial
Church.
The program will open with
"Credo" by Bach, and will include
"Come, Sweet Death" by Bach,
"Hymn" by Richard Goldman, "Clair
de Lune" by Kark-Elert, and
"Chorale in A Minor" by Cesar
Franck. A recital will also be given
Sunday at 4 p.m. in the church.
