# Toyon Completes Plans for Dance
## 
### 
Toyon Dance Chairman Bob Walt
yesterday announced that plans have
been completed for the informal
dance to be given Friday night.
While the dance is scheduled from 10
to 1, it will start right after the
Cal-Stanford basketball game in the
Pavilion.
The dance is open to the entire
campus and members of Bowles
Hall from the Berkeley campus.
Music will be provided through the
medium of records. In response to a
query Wait said, "Yes, it's free and
will be informal, very informal.
There will be some form of punch
for refreshment."
Patrons for the affair are Mr. and
Mrs. Charles W. Thomas, Dr. Rix
ford K. Snyder, and Dr. Nelson
Smith.
