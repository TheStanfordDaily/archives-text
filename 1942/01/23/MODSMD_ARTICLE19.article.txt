# Toyon Schedules After-Game Hop
## 
### 
Immediately following the Cal-
Stanford basketball game in the
blacked-out Pavilion Friday night,
the Toyon Club will give a dance.
The affair is scheduled to run from
10 to 1 and is open to everybody.
Refreshments will be served and
music will be provided through the
medium of records. Members of
j Bowles Hall on the Berkeley campus
have been invited to attend.
Bob Walt, Toyon Club dance
chairman, assures everybody that
the dance is "very, very informal
and, yes, it's free."

Mrs. Charles W. Thomas, Dr. Nel
