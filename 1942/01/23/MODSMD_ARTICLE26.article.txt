# Hometown Honors Marine Air Hero
## 
### 
COLFAX, Wash., Jan. 22 (UP).—
Lieut. John F. Kinney, marine avia
tor cited for heroism in the defense
of Wake Island and now believed a
prisoner of war in Japan, was
chosen today by the Junior Chamber
of Commerce as Colfax's outstand
ing yoUng man of 1941.
His parents, Mr. and Mrs. E. A.
Kinney, will be presented with a
gold key, emblematic of the award,
at a Founders' Day banquet Satur
day night.
