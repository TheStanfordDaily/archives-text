# Hogan Shoots 65 In S.F. Golf Meet
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Jan. 22 (UP).—
Ben Hogan of Hershey, Pa., the
money - winningest golfer in the
business, knocked over the Califor
nia Golf Club course in 65 today—
seven under par—to take a snug lead
in the first round of the $5,000 San
Francisco Open.
Lighthorse Harry Cooper of Min
neapolis, another pro veteran, clip
ped four strokes off par for his 68
to stay within reach of Hogan. Hor
