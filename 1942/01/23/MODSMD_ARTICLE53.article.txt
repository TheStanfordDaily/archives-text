# APOSTOLI COMEBACK
## 
### 
NORFOLK. Va.. Jan. 22 (UP).—
First class boatswain's mate Fred
Apostoli, the San Francisco bell'-
hop who fought his way up to the
world middleweight championship
and then slid back down just as
fast, tossed a warning at the boxing
world today—"Don't count me out
yet, boys, I'm coming back."
