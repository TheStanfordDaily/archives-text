# NEW TYPE RUBBER FOR U.S.?
## 
### 

OVER 500 MILLION POUNDS of rubber is available in native chry
santhemum plants growing in large quantities in California, Nevada,
Colorado, Utah, New Mexico, and Idaho, according to Dr. T. Good
speed, professor of botany at the University of California, who has
sent a report of his findings to government authorities in Washing
ton. We may soon be getting tires from that plant.
