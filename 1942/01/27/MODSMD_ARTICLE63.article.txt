# Interclub Council To Hold Smoker
## 
### 
All freshman men and new stu
dents are invited to the Interclub
Council open house and smoker to
be held in the Toyon lobby, at 7:15
Thursday night, Jan. 29.
Several speakers will give talks to
the assembled group. After the
smoker, all eating clubs will hold
open house for visitors, and Toyon
rooms will be open for inspection.
