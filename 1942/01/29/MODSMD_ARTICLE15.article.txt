# Vaccination Slips Are Due Monday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at
the Registrar's Office Monday, Feb.
2, from all new students, including
those having permits to attend class
es, and from all other students whose
certificates have expired. Failure to
file certificates by 5 p.m. Monday
will result in a fine of $2; if certi
cates are not filed led by Feb. 9, reg
istration in the University is subject
to cancellation.
