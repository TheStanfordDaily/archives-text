# Open House
## 
### 
NEWEST addition to the list of organizations
to give on-campus entertainment is Lagu
nita,

The women in Lagunita are inviting everyone
on the campus to drop in for their open house
Saturday night. This type of affair has also been
sponsored by several other living groups, in
cluding sorority open houses.
We make mention of their generosity in aid
ing the stay-on-campus, spend-less-money plan,
and are proud to list them as patriotic women.—
Harold Shafer
