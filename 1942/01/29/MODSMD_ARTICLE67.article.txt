RAM'S HEAD meeting today at 5
o'clock in the Ram's Head room,
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANIZA
TION will meet tonight at 7:30 in
the church at Forest and Bryant in
Palo Alto. Cars will be at Roble
and Encina at 7:15.
RUSH CARDS for Monday and
Tuesday and for preference night
are on sale at the A.S.S.U. office.

TOKYO, Jan. 28 (Radiocast re
corded by United Press, New York).
—Large Japanese formations downed
an enemy plane and set fire to bar
racks of the Rangoon airdrome in
a raid Tuesday night, the Domei
news agency reported today from a
Japanese base in Malaya.
