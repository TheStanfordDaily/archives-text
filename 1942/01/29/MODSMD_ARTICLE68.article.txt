# Interclub Smoker To Hear Wilbur
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur
will speak tonight at the Interclub
Council open house and smoker to
be held in the Toyon lobby at 7:lo,
Warren Berl, president of Interclub
Council, announced last night. Ev
erett Dean, varsity basketball coach,
is also scheduled to speak.
Freshman men and new students
are invited to the affair. Chairman
A1 Haas has arranged entertainment
numbers by Peggy Ingham and
Marcia Shaughnessy, two stars from
the 1941 Gaieties.
Free cigarettes will be given out
to visitors. After the smoker, Toyon
Hall and all eating clubs will be
open to inspection.
