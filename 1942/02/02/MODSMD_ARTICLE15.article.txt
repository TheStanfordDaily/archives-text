# Nominations Open For Toyon Club
## 
### 
Toyon Club will elect new officers
Thursday night, Feb. 12, from 9 to
11 p.m. in the Toyon lobby. The
cabinet confirmed Bill Beckman as
election board chairman, and acted
as a nominating body in accordance
with the constitution.
Nominated for president were
John Parker, Capitan, and Elmer
Brinckerhoff, Toro. For vice-presi
dent: Wilbur Chandler, Jim Bent
ley, Robert Walt, and Burt Berry.
For secretary: Carl Livingston and
Edmund Dews.
Nominations can be made by pe
tition up to Monday, Feb. 9, and pro
visions will also be made for write
in candidates on the ballots.
