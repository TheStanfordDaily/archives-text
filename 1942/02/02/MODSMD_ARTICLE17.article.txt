# JAP ALIENS BOOKED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 1 (UP).—
Four Japanese aliens were arrested
here today by agents of the Federal
Bureau of Investigation and booked
at the city prison, "en route to im
migration authorities."
