# Civil Service Gives Law Exam Feb. 21
## 
### 
Civil Service law examinations
for the classes of Junior Counsel and
Assistant Counsel have been sched
uled for February 21, the State Per
sonnel Board has announced. Sole
requirement for the Junior Counsel
position, which pays $l7O a month, is
admission to the state bar within
the last ten years. Two years of
full-time law practice are required
for the Assistant Counsel post.
