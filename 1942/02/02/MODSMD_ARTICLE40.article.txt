# NEW LOUIS BOUT
## 
### 
RUMSON, N.J., Feb, 1 (UP).—
Promoter Mike Jacobs will begin
negotiations tomorrow to obtain a
suitable opponent for a title match
with Champion Joe Louis at Madi
son Square Garden, March 27. Pro*-
ceeds of the bout will go to army
relief organizations.
