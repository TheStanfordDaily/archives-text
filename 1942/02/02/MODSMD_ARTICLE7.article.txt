# Vaccination Slips Due Today in Ad Building
## 
### 
Today is the deadline for all
vaccination certificates for all new
students, including those having
permits to attend classes and all
other students whose certificates
have expired, announced the Com
mittee on Public Health. Failure
to file certificates by five p.m. will
result in a fine of $2, or cancel
lation of registration in the Uni
versity if they are not filed by
Feb. 9.
