# DODGERS MANAGER
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Feb 2 (UP).—Larry
MacPhail, manager of the Brooklyn
Dodgers, today signed Leo Durocher
as playing manager of the Dodgers
for 1942 at an undisclosed salary.
