# Frosh Swimmers To Meet S.F.J.C.
## 
### 
Still undefeated, Stanford's power
ful freshman swimming team faces
the San Francisco Junior College
Saturday afternoon at 2:30 p.m. in
the Encina pools.
Boasting of three standout swim
mers in Jim Gamble. Alan Weeden,
and Hank Saltonstall, the freshmen
have a strong, well-rounded outfit.
