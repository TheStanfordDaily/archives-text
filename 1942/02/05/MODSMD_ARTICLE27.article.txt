# Youngster Missing Near Farm Campus
## 
### 
A part of the Stanford campus to
day was the scene of a county-wide
search for two-and-a-half-year-old
Patricia Connoley, who has been
missing from her home in Los 'Fran
cos Woods since Thursday.
San Mateo County Sheriff Mc-

Grath said today that the searchers
had traced the young girl's footsteps
to the banks of San Francisquito
Creek, which skirts the Stanford
University campus. He added, how
ever, that dragging of the stream
had failed to locate her body.
