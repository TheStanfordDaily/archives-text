# FIRE RAGES IN PHILLY
## 
### 
PHILADELPHIA, Feb. 8 (UP).—
A six-alarm fire, whipped by a 30-
mile-an-hour wind, destroyed a
score of buildings and damaged 50
others in north Philadelphia tonight.
