# Tokyo Broadcasts Prisoner Messages
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 8 (UP).-
Radio Tokyo today broadcast mes
sages from two lieutenants in the
U.S. Navy, now war prisoners in
Japan.
The messages were from Lieut.
(J. G.) Jack William Schwartz,
U.S.N.R., 409 Cloverdale Ave., Los
Angeles, and Lieut. (J.G.) Richard
B. Williams Jr., U.S.N.R., 665 Easy
St., Saltonville, Va.
