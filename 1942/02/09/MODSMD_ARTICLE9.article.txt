# Robber Enters Phi Kap House
## 
### 
Approximately $26 in cash was
stolen from the Phi Kap house
early Sunday morning in the third
robbery this week.
Chief of Police Gordon Davis re
ported that the thief has appeared
on two previous occasions in the past
week. Early Friday morning, a man
entered the Chi Omega sorority
house, taking $50 and a number of
cigarettes.
Ralph Thompson, head sponsor at
Encina, reported that $500 worth of
goods were stolen from various
rooms during the week of January 28
to February 3. Typewriters, over
coats, shoes, and cash were among
the missing articles.
