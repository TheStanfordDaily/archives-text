# Phelan Fellowship Deadline Nears
## 
### 
Only a few days remain in which
student writers may apply for the
$1000 James D. Phelan Award in
Literature and Art. Entry is open to
men and women between the ages of
20 and 30 who are residents of the
state.
The award is made annually and
it is necessary that the work sub
historical or modern.
