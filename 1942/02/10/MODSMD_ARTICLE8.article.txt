# Excom to Pick Pre-Reg Heads
## 
### 
A call for all men and women
students interested in helping with
transfer or freshman orientation has
been issued by George Jackson,
A.S.S.U. president, and Pat Adams,
vice-president.
"Anyone interested in any part of
the pre-reg activities 01* transfer
work should contact George or me
sometime today as appointments
will be made at tonight's Excom
meeting," Miss Adams said.
Besides the pre-reg appointments
Excom will pass its official approval
on the election results. There will
also be a final report on the Charity
Ball by Jerry Stoner.
Tonight's meeting will be short,
according to Miss Adams, and will
begin at 5:30 p.m. in the Excom
room in the Union.
