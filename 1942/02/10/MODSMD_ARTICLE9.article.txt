# Operetta Opens At Curran, Feb. 15
## 
### 
"Blossom Time" will open at the
Curran for two weeks beginning
Sunday night, Feb. 15, with mati
nees on Wednesday and Saturday.
The operetta includes Schubert's
"Serenade," "The Song of Love,"
"Lonely Heart," and other celebrated
pieces.
The cast is headed by Everett
Marshall, baritone, who is starred as
Schubert. Other members of the cast
include Marthe Errolle, contralto,
who has the role of Bellabrunn;
Frank Hornaday, tenor, Baron
Schober; Patricia Wing, Mitzi; Tru
man Gaige. Driskill Wolfe, and Betti
Davis.
