STARTING AS A PRANK of the Spokane Athletic Round-Table, the idea of sending almost any old thing
to senators and representatives drew supporters throughout the nation. Spokane gagsters called it oIT,
but not before truckloads of articles had been collected to be sent to Washington. Above, truck picks
up "presents" in a Spokane suburb.
