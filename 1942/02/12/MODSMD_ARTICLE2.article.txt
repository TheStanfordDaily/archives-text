# Irma Cunha Wins Roble Presidency
## 
### 
Irma Cunha, freshman from Hono
lulu, was elected president of Roble
Hall yesterday in the annual elec
tion. Miss Cunha replaces Sylvia
Berry who, serving as senior spon
sor, was also the head of Roble for
the past months.
Nancy Neave from Chincinnati,
Ohio, was elected to the post of vice
presidcnt, while Marcia Russell takes
over the duties of secretary-treas
urer of the hall.
The newly elected officers will re
sume their duties sometime begin
ning next quarter when they re
place the outgoing officers of the hall
who have served since the opening
of fall quarter.
