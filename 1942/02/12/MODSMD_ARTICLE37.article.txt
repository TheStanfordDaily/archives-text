# Toyon will Select Heads Tonight
## 
### 
Toyon will elect its second-term
officers tonight from 9 to 11 o'clock
in the lobby. The ballot will in
clude the following:
For president, Elmer BrinckerhofT
and John Parker; for vice-president,
Jim Bentley, Burton Berry, Wilbur
Chandler, and Bob Walt; for secre
tary, Edmund Dews and Carl Liv
ingston. Provision will also be made
for write-in candidates.
