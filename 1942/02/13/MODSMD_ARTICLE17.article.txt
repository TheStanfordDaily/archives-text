# Cal Invites Farm To Game Dance
## 
### 
The Student Engineers Council of
the University of California will hold
a sport dance immediately after the
Cal-Stanford basketball game to
night. The dance will be given at
the Hearst Gymnasium.
All Stanford students attending the
basketball game arc cordially in
vited to attend. The admission price
will be 80 cents per couple, and the
music will be furnished by Walter
Carl's orchestra.
