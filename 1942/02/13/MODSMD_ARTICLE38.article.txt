# Belts Triumph In Mural Hoop
## 
### 
The Dolts defeated the Legal
Eagles in an overtime game for the
leadership in League IV of the mural
hoop competition. The score was 31
to 29, with Kennedy starring for the
Delts and Thompson as the Lawyers'
flash.
Encina 2E won easily over 4E, 18
to 13, with Bob Rosecrance taking
high-point honors with eight buck
ets. AI Harris was also hot for 2E.
Encina Bullpen dropped a close
decision to 3W, 28 to 25. according
to unofficial reports from the gym.
The games in the Pavilion were
cancelled because of preparations for
the Charity Ball tonight.
