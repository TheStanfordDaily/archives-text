# Helen Kimball Elected Women's Council Head
## 
### 
Helen Kimball, senior and Roble
sponsor, was elected chairman of
Women's Council at yesterday's
meeting. Chairman Kimball suc
ceeds Rosemarie Mullany and as
sumes the office immediately.
Alastair Mac-Donald, senior Theta
and also a Roble sponsor, was
elected vice-chairman.
The newly elected members of
Council, Ruth Atkins, Jean Mont
gomery, and Margaret Terry, will
take over the positions of scholar
ship chairman, secretary, and head
council representative.
