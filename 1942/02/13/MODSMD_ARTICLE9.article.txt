# One-Act Plays Open Tonight
## 
### 
Three one-act plays will be pre
sented by Ram's Head tonight and
Saturday in the Little Theater.
Tickets will be sold at the door as
there will be no reserved seats. The
curtain will go up at 8 p.m. for the
convenience of those playgoers who
may wish to attend the Charity Ball
at 10.
The plays to be presented are
Adolfo Arias' drama of Nazi Ger
many, "And Then the Dusk"; "Har
vest Time," a comedy of manners by
Charles Trieschmann; and Ted Mar
cuse's "Great Grandfather Marquis."
Hal Leavitt, technical director, is
in charge of production.
