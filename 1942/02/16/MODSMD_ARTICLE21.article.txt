# Co-op to Hold Hunt, Dance on Friday
## 
### 
The co-operative groups on cam
pus will hold a scavenger hunt and
dance next Friday evening. The
group will meet at the Walter
Thompson Co-operative House at 7
p.m. and after the hunt will gather
at the house for refreshments, games,
and dancing.
Members of the Co-op House and
the Co-op Book Exchange and their
friends are invited to attend. By
special permission, the dance will
continue until 1 o'clock.
