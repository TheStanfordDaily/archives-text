# Handball Sign-ups Begin This Week
## 
### 
Red Holman, intramural manager,
has announced sign-ups for handball
will take place this week in the En
cina Gym. Competition will begin
next week or after mural hoop finals
have begun.
Finals for horseshoes are to be
held this week. Team managers are
urged to schedule games with their
listed opponent during the first of
the week.
