# Clinchy to Speak At Luncheon Club
## 
### 
Dr. Everett R. Clinchy, president
of the National Conference of Chris
tians and Jews, will speak on the
work being done by this organiza
tion at the meeting of the Luncheon
Club this Wednesday noon.
Programs for the meetings are ar
ranged by Dr. D. Elton Trueblood,
and the serving is done by the Stan
ford Mothers. The lunch costs 30
cents, and the public is invited to all
meetings. The club is now in its
fifth year of wceklv meetings.
