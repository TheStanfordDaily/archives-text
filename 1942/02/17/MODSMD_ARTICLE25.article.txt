# Course in Social Work Announced
## 
### 
Social Work and Social Pathology,
a course for those interested in the
field of social work, will be Riven
during spring quarter, according to
an announcement made yesterday by
the .sociology department.
The course, given for three units,
includes two hours a week of class
work, plus field trips to social agen
cies in the Bay area. Those inter
ested arc asked to sign up in the of
fice of the Dean of Women this week.
