# SINGAPORE GARRISON MAY ALL BE LOST
## 
### 
LONDON, Feb. 16 (UP). -Military
sources said today that the entire
Singapore garrison of about 60,000
Empire troops might have fallen
into Japanese hands, raising the pos
sibility that Britain had suffered her
biggest single loss in manpower of
the whole war.
The loss of British materiel and
equipment was admitted to have
been "very heavy" when Lieut. Gen,
A. E. Percival gave up the hopeless
and uneven fight Sunday.
