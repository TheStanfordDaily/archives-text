# Mumford to Speak Here on March 3
## 
### 
Lewis Mumford, noted author and
lecturer from New York, will visit
Stanford for the week of March 2
to 5. As a member of the National
Commission on Teacher Education,
Mr. Mumford will serve as a consult
ant to ihe Stanford committee on
general education for prospective
teachers.
During the week that he is at
Stanford, Mr. Mumford will lecture
at the regular Tuesday Evening
Series. He has chosen the topic,
"America and World Unity." and
will speak in Memorial Hall on
Tuesday, March 3.
