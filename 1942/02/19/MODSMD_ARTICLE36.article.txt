# Engineering Class Begins on Friday
## 
### 
A free course, beginning Friday
i evening, Feb. 20. in the Cubberley
| Education Building, in industrial
| safety engineering is now open for
| enrollment, according to Professor
j Eugene L. Grant.
The class will be taught by W. E.
Lovejoy, assistant to the chief safety
engineer of Standard Oil Company
of California. Professor Grant points
out that in order to enroll in the
course, students must intend to go
into defense employment or into the
armed forces at the conclusion of the
course, or shortly thereafter, and not
plan to enroll in University work
for the next academic year.
