# Organist to Play At Recital Today
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will give a recital this after
noon at 4 o'clock. The music will be
of George Washington's time. Fan
tasia in F, and Minuet from the
"Jupiter" Symphony by Mozart;
Serenade in C major by Haydn;
Adagio from the Sonata Pathetique,
op. 13, and "Hallelujah" Chorus
from "The Mount of Olives" by
Beethoven.
