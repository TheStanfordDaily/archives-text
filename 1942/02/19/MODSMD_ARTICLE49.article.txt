# MENLO LOSES TO MARIN
## 
### 
KENTFIELD, Feb. 18 (UP).—Ma
rin Junior College's basketball team
beat Menlo Junior College here last
4G-30. Bob Broderick, Menlo's
leading scorer, had a cold night again
and scored but one goal for his
team.
