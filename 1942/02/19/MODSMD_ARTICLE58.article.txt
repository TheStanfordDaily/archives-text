# FARM WON LAST IN '34
## 
### 
The last time Stanford won the
national collegiate track meet was
in 1934, with a team of seven men
who aggregated 67 points. University
of Southern California has won the
meet ever since. In this 1934 as
semblage, however, the Trojans
could come no better than third be
hind Stanford and Louisiana State.
