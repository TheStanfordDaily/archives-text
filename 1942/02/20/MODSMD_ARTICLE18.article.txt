# Pi Sigma Alphas To Elect Heads
## 
### 
Pi Sigma Alpha, honorary political
science fraternity, will hold a busi
ness meeting at 5:30 p.m. Wednesday
in the Women's Clubhouse. Follow
ing the meeting, at which officers
will be elected, there will be an in
itiation banquet in the garden wing
of the Union.
Several faculty members of the
Political Science Department will
hold a panel discussion on the rela
tion of their respective fields to the
present emergency.
Members are invited to bring
guests to the banquet. Cost of the
dinner will be 77 cents. Those at
tending should notify Mrs. Fay
Jones in the political science office
by Tuesday noon.
