# Extra Late Leave Granted to Women
## 
### 
A non-quota 2:30 late leave will
be granted all women students for
Sunday night, Feb. 21, Helen Kim
ball, chairman of Women's Council,
announced. This is only for the one
night of the week-end of Washing
ton's birthday, and will not be
counted in the total number of late
leaves for the quarter, she added,
