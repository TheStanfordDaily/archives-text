# Barry to Continue As U.S.C. Coach
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 19 (UP).—
Justin M. (Sam) Barry will continue
as head football coach of the Uni
versity of Southern California for
the 1542 season, President RuJ'us B.
von Kleinsmid announced tonight.
President von Kleinsmid said he
and the athletic board of the uni
versity felt that Barry had done a
highly satisfactory job of coaching
under the circumstances and felt he
should continue the work.
