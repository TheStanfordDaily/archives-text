# Allen to Present Thursday Recital
## 
### 
Dr.* Warren D. Allen will present
the regular Thursday afternoon or
gan recital this afternoon at 4:15 in
(he- Memorial Church.
The program will consist of the
"Chorale in E" by Kirnberger, the
"Canon in D Major" by Schumann,
the "Pastorale" by Franck, "Confi
dence" by Mendelssohn, and the
"Toccata and Fugue in D Minor" by
Bach.
