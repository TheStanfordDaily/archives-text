# PARASKIERS DO DOUBLE DUTY
## 
### 

PRETTY STRENUOUS FUN is the life of Uncle Sam's paraskicrs. Two
of the boys who'll do double duty as parachutists and ski troopers
level their sights on an objective during training "somewhere in the
Utah mountains." The soldiers take their ski training from a staff of
ten noted instructors, headed by Dick Durrance, former Olympic star.
