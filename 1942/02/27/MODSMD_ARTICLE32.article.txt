# Olson Requests Continual Alert
## 
### 
As a result of the recent subma
rine attack on the coastal area of
Santa Barbara, a warning to all
residents, especially those living
along the coastline, to be continually
on the alert, has been released by
Governor Culbert L. Olson.
Anyone sighting a plane, subma
rine, warship, or other hostile craft
lias been urgently requested by the
governor to immediately notify the
nearest police or sheriff's office.
In order that the Fourth Intercep
tor Command and other arms of the
federal government may make ap
propriate defense precautions and
dispatch armed units to the scene,
promptness in reporting to the proper
authorities is requested.
