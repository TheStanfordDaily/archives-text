# INDIANS SIGN HEVING
## 
### 
CLEVELAND, March 1 (UP).—
Pitcher Joe Heving and the Cleve
land Indians came to terms today,
and the moundsnian will report Fri
day to the Indian training camp at
Clearwater, Fla.
HAVANA, March 1 (UP).—A sin
gle by rookie first baseman Les
Burge scored the winning run today
as the Brooklyn Dodgers defeated
the New York Giants for the second
successive time, 3-2 in 12 innings.
