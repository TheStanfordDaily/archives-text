# Gas Consumption Rises in January
## 
### 
Despite war restrictions on auto
mobiles, gasoline consumption dur
ing January showed an increase of
over 10 per cent above last year's
total, according to a report issued by
the State Board of Equalization.
Gasoline distributed during the
month amounted to 166,443,483 gal
lons, an increase of 10.08 per cent.
January's total was substantially
under the record sales made during
December, however, the board re
ported.
