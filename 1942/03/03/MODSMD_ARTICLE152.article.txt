# Pale Sox Threaten Yankee Supremacy
## 
### 
The Chicago White Sox. with a
veteran pitching staff returning for
the coming season, should prove to
be an outstanding tin-eat to the
American League supremacy of the
New York Yankees this year.
John Dungan Rigney, Ted Lyons,
and particularly Lefty Thornton
Lee, sparked the Pale Hose to a first
division berth last year.
