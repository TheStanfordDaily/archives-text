# NEW BELT WITH POUCH
## 
### 
For the women at work designers
have fashioned a pouch with a flap
which can be easily fastened over
the belt; but its most xemarkable
feature is that it contains a loose
powder compact, rouge, lipstick, and
comb besides ample room for ciga
rettes, mad-money, and other essen
tials.
