# Knitted Swim Suits Are Beach Novelty
## 
### 
Form-fitting and smart are the
novelty hand-knitted bathing suits
made in clever designs. Newest
shade for the brief garments is black,
Useful in the midday sun of the
bathing resorts is a Chinese coolie
hat.
If you don't feel like swimming
there is the new kerchief dress made
of bandanas, complete with narrow
skirt, kimono sleeve, and kept to
gether by only two buttons. Bundles
adapted from the southern negro
mammy makes the beach bag carry
all.
