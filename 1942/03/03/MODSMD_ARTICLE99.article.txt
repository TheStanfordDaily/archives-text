# Notes on the Cover
##

are Jano Ellen Marks, in a spring
green wool suit with patch pockets
and a hip length jacket, and Clair
Peck in a gabardine suit, Hi-Lo shirt
with California collar, butter 'n
cream tie and Nettleton brogues. All
from Roos Bros.
