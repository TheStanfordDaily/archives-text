# Roble Will Dance At 'Crystal Ball'
## 
### 
Barbara Hill, chairman for the
Roble winter Quarter Formal, an
nounced today that the general
theme for the dance this Friday
evening will be "Crystal Ball." Sally
Breuner and Marion Wcller, co
chairmen of the decorations commit
tee, added that the Women's Gym
will be decorated for the affair with
a revolving crystal ball upon which
will play colored lights, and that
stars and planets will form a ceil
ing.
Another attraction will be a for
tune teller who will tell dancers'
fortunes by way of a crystal ball.
