# Farm K. A. Has Novel Published
## 
### 
Another Stanford writer who has
added his name to the roll of promi
nent literary men who have received
their training on the Farm is James
Gunn, Kappa Alpha from San Fran
cisco, who will have his latest book,
"Deadlier Than the Male," published
in April by Duell, Sloan and Pearce
of New York. It will be on sale at
the Stanford Bookstore early spring
quarter.
An English major, Gunn has no
plans for the future, and hopes to be
able to continue his writing, de
spite the fact that he may go into
the army.
His story is a melodrama with the
setting in present-day San Fran
cisco.
