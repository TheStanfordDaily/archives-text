# FIGHTER BREAKS ARM
## 
### 
NEW YORK, March 3 (UP).—
Jackie Wilson, Pittsburgh negro
who reigns as NBA featherweight
champion, stepped out of the tight
picture for at least three months
today with a broken arm suffered
in a bout with Terry Young.
