# GOLF MEET MOVED BACK
## 
### 
CHICAGO, March 3 (UP).—Dates
for the $15,000 Tam O'Shanter open
and the Ail-American amateur golf
tournaments to be played at the;
Tam O'Shanter Country Club have
been moved back one week, it was
announced today. The amateur will
be played July 20-25 and the open
July 23-26 under the new plan.
