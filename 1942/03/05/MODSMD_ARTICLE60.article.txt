# Herb Casey Wins Branner President
## 
### 
In the mid-year elections held
Tuesday night, Herb Casey, El Tigre,
was elected president of Branner
Club. Steve Kline was unopposed
for the manager for the school year
1942-43.
Others elected were: Jim Powell,
vice-president; Dudley Boyce, sec
retary; Jess Shenson, senior repre
sentative; Dan Eymann, junior rep
resentative, and Dick Keusinck,
sophomore representative. Accord
ing to Ted Norton. Branner election
chairman, who released the results,
the balloting was light.
