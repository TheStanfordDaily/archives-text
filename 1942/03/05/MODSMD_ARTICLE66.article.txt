# Poli Sci Society Elects President
## 
### 
Miss Barbara Smith was elected
president of Pi Sigma Alpha, hon
orary political science fraternity, last
week at their initiation banquet and
business meeting. She replaces Har
old Enarson, who did not return to
school this quarter. Miss Peggy
Sussman replaces Mrs. Fay H. Jones
as secretary of the organization.
Nine new members were elected to
the fraternity, but as yet have not
been notified of their membership.
