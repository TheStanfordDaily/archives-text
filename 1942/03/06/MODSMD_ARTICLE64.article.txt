# TO HEAR GEOLOGIST
## 
### 
At the regular Monday afternoon
meeting of the Journal Club, ad
vanced geology students will hear a
talk by Professor W. C. Krumbein,
who will speak on the origin of
sedimentary rocks. Dr. Krumbein
is from the University of Chicago
and will be studying in California
for the next three or four months on
a Guggenheim fellowship.
