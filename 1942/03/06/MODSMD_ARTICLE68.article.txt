# SCOTCH PLAY PLANNED
## 
### 
"The Wind and the Rain," a play
by Mcrton Hodge, will be given in
the Little Theater in Memorial Hall,
April 29 and 30 and May 1, 2, and 4.
The play is set in a Scotch medical
school. It is being directed by Nor
man Philbrick.
