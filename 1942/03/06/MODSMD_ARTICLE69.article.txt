# Defense Leaders To Attend Meet
## 
### 
Leading student defense officials
will attend the regional conference
of the National Student Federation
to be held today, Saturday and
Sunday at Mills College. Purpose
of the meeting is to discuss the part
to be played by college students in
the war effort.
Delegates from Stanford include
Ann Bossinger, Virginia Dean, Har
ry Morgan, Stan Owen, George
Jackson, and Ralph ShafTarzick.
Stanford is sponsoring the conven
tion together with California and
Mills.
Among the round-tables scheduled
arc: "Civilian Defense Courses in
Colleges," "Safety Precautions on
the Campus," "Athletics and Recrea
tion in Wartime," "Consumer Buy
ing by College Students," and "The
Place of Activities and Social Func
tions During the Emergency."
