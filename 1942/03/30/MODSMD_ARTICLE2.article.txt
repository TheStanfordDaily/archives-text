# Industrial Meet Discusses Labor
## 
### 
War production labor was the
topic of discussions at Stanford's
fifth annual industrial relations con
ference held here last week.
Ralph W. Smith, employment
manager of the Vega Airplane Com
pany, Gerald Tuttlc, industrial rela
tions manager of Vultec Aircraft,
Inc., Douglas Brown, director of the
industrial relations section at
Princeton, who also served on the
labor division of the War Production
Board, were among the prominent
speakers at the conference.
Paul Eliel, head of the industrial
relations division of the Graduate
School of Business, and Paul E.
Holden, professor of industrial man
agement, represented Stanford at
the meetings.
