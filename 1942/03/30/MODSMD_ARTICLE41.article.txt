# Carillon to Sell Subscriptions
## 
### 
Carillon subscriptions for the two
issues of Spring Quarter will be on
sale all day today in front of the
Ad Building. Price for the two
issue subscription will be 40 cents.
The next issue will be on sale on
the Quad in about three weeks.
Editor Charles Bull announced
that all manuscripts for the April
issue, to qualify for the Writers'
Club contest, must be submitted in
final form by Thursday, April 2.
The Writer's Club will award $5 for
the best short story and best poem to
be published in The Carillon for the
entire year, October through April.
Winners will be anounced in the
May issue.
