# BYRD WINS OPEN
## 
### 
GREENSBORO. N.C., March 29 1
(UP). —Sammy Byrd, of Ardmore, j
Pa., baseball outfielder turned pro
golfer, won the Greater Greensboro
open tournament today with a five
under-par total of 279 for his four
rounds. He shot 69-67-75-68.

AGUA CALIENTE, Mex., March
29 (UP). — Reginald Sinclair's Old
Dom today easily won the Napper
Tandy purse, a prelude to next Sim
day's $10,000 Gran National steeple
chase race.
