# Speakers to Give War Talk Series
## 
### 
Organized under the aegis of Miss
Claire Loftus, head of the Stanford
Speakers, a series of on-campus
talks on "Stanford in the War" will
be put into effect this quarter.
The talks, scheduled to be like
those of the "four-minute men" ol
World War I, will be given at house
meetings, dinners, and various group
gatherings on the campus.
Interested students arc asked t<
register with Miss Loftus in Memo
rial Hall today. She will assign top
ics and explain the idea of the talks
further at the registration.
