# Rules Governing Meetings Issued
## 
### 
All meetings held at the Univer
sity which are open to the public
or publicized as University meetings
must be approved in advance by the
Committee on Public Exercises, ac
cording to a statement issued by Dr.
Ray Lyman Wilbur, chancellor.
Invitations for meetings of visiting
conferences, associations, and all
other groups to be held at the Uni
versity are to be approved by the
President's Office prior to the issu
ance of the invitation.
After approval has been secured,
arrangements for rental charge for
facilities and reservations for the
use of rooms and buildings must be
made at the office of the Committee
on Public Exercises.
