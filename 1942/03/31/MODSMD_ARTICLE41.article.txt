# Spring Boathouse Hours Announced
## 
### 
The Stanford Boathouse will be
open every clay during Spring Quar
ter from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m., the staff
announced yesterday. Canoes will be
available and swimming will be per
mitted during all the hours that the
Boathouse is open.
There will be a full moon soon.
