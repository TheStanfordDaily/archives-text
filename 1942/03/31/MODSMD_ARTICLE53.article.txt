# '45 Harriers Face Sacramento J.C.
## 
### 
Joe Batiste, great hurdler, high
jumper, and sprinter, will lead his
Sacramento J.C. national junior col
lege champions against the Stanford
freshman next Saturday at Angell
Field starting at 2 p.m.
Batiste, who has appeared recently
in the East against Ed Dugger and
Fred Wolcott, the country's best,
has done 14.1 in the 120 highs and
has hit 23.2 in the low sticks. He
runs the century in 9.8, and has
high-jumped 6.5.
