# S.F. Lodges Aliens In Old SRA Camps
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISO, March 31 (UP).
—The single men's SRA relief camp
at Sharp Park has been converted
into a temporary detention camp for
arrested enemy aliens to relieve
overcrowding at the Silver Avenue
Immigration Station in San Fran
cisco, it was disclosed today.
