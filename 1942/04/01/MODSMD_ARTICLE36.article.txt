# Davis Speaks On War Prisoners Aid
## 
### 
"The Barbed Wire Legion" will be
the topic discussed by Dr. Darius A.
Davis, execuitve secretary of the
War Prisoners' Aid Service of the
Y.M.CA., at an open meeting Friday
evening at 8, in the Women's Club
house.
Dr. Davis represents one of three
organizations in the world today
which may carry on work in pris
oners' aid in the belligerent areas.
The other two are the Red Cross and
the American Society of Friends
Service Organization.
