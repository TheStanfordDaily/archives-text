# NAVAL SHIP LAUNCHED
## 
### 
ALAMEDA, March 31 (UP).—The
U.S.S. Pawnee, a Navy auxiliary
vessel, was launched today at an
Alameda shipyard. The ship was
christened by Mrs. Oscar Stiegler,
wife of a naval officer.
