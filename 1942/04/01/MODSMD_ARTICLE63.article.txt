# LILI WINS DIVORCE
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, March 31 (UP).-
The frequently expressed desire of
Errol Flynn to "be free" was granted
him today when his wife, pretty Lili
Damita, won a divorce from the
Irish-born star.
