# Hardin Craig Joins Carolina Faculty
## 
### 
Dr. Hardin Craig, emeritus pro
fessor of English at Stanford, will
teach at the University of North
Carolina during the Spring Quarter
and at the University of lowa during
the summer. In order to continue
his writing and research near a
large library lie may settle in Pasa
dena near the Huntington Library.
Although he is selling his home on
the Stanford campus to Professor
and Mrs. Charles Barker, he consid
ers himself "still a member of the
Stanford family."
