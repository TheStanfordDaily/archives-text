# NEW HEADS OF U.S. ARMED FORCES
## 
### 

SIMPLIFICATION OF U.S. ARMY high command, ordered by President Roosevelt as Commander-in-Chief
of U.S. Forces, puts these men in important posts. From left: Lieutenant General Lesley J. McNair, to com
mand all ground forces; Lieutenant General Henry 11. Arnold, to comamnd air forces; and Major General
Brehon B. Somervell, to head new consolidated services of supply. Infantry, cavalry, field artillery, and
coast artillery will also be commanded by General McNair.
