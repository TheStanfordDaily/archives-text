# New Play Offers Parts for Men
## 
### 
Tryouts for the last play to be
presented this season will be held
Monday night, April 6, at 7:30 o'clock
in the main auditorium of Memorial
Hall, according to Director F. Cowles
Strickland.
In the play, "He Who Gets Slap
ped" by Andreyev, there are parts
open for 12 men. It was announced
that any men are welcome to come
out of it. Women's parts arc being
cast from the acting class.
The play will be given by the
Stanford Players May 28, 29, 30, in
Memorial Hall.
