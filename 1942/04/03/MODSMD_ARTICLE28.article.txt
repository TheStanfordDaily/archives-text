# JAP PROBLEM DISCUSSED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 2 (UP).
—Problems involved in the resettle
ment of more than 100,000 Japanese
to be evacuated from Pacific Coast
military areas will be discussed at a
meeting ol state and fedeial officials
from 10 Western states in Salt Lake
City next Tuesday, it was announced
today by the War Relocation Au
thority.
