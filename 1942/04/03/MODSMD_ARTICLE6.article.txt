# Japanese Class Will Be Offered
## 
### 
A beginning course known as Ele
mentary Japanese will be offered
this quarter, Professor Raymond D.
Harriman, chairman of the School of
Letters, announced yesterday.
According to Professor Harriman,
classes will probably meet on Mon
day, Tuesday, and Thursday at 7:30
p.m. Students will enroll with Pro
fessor Harriman in his office on the*
quadrangle, 255.
Douglas Atcheson, Stanford grad
uate, and later a teacher of English
in Japanese schools, will conduct
the course. Mr. Atcheson taught in
Tung Wen College, in Shanghai, and
later in Nagasaki. He also taught in
the high school at Yamaguchi.
