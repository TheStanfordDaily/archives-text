# Edgar Robinson Receives Degree
## 
### 
Edgar E. Robinson, Stanford pro
fessor of American history, will re
ceive an honorary doctor of laws
degree from the University of Wis
consin at the University's 89th an
nual commencement exercises on
June 1.
Professor Robinson will be one of
three persons who will receive hon
orary degrees at the commencement
exercises. Dr. Hu Shih, Chinese am
bassador to the United States and
Miss Georgia O'Kecfe, New York
artist and teacher of art, will also
receive the degree.
Dr. Robinson is a native of Wis
consin and studied at the University
of Wisconsin. He has been teaching at
Stanford since 1911.
