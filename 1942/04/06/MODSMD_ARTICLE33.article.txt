# Thousands Parade On Market Street
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 5 (UP).
—Thousands of soldiers, sailors, and
war workers paraded down Market
Street today in a huge Army Day-
Easter patriotic demonstration.
The parade extended from the
Embarcadero to the Civic Center,
where the reviewing stand was lo
cated. Major General R. C. Rich
ardson ranked the procession. Mar
ket. Sireet — the "Avenue of the
Allies"—was bedecked with flags.
