# Northern Shier Nips Bobby Blatt in Slalom
## 
### 
Favored Bobby Blatt. Farm soph
skier, was nosed out for first place
in the slalom competition of the
annual Far West Kandahar ski
tournament Saturday on Mount
I loud. Don Amick of the Wash
ington Ski Club was the winner.
Blatt had the fastest single run
of the day on the half-mile course,
1:11.8, but his combined time on
both runs was 2:37, which was not
good enough to beat Amick's
2:31.8.
