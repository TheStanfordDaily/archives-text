# Artillery Team Enters Parade
## 
### 
An enlisted dctachment from Stan
ford's R.O.T.C. Field Artillery unit
will enter today's Army Day parade
in Palo Alto. The dctachment will
be a complete section, including
drivers, cannoneers, a caisson, a
75-mm. gun, and six-horse teams.
According to Colonel Harry B.
Allen, head of Stanford's R.O.T.C.
unit, the entry will amount to one
fifth of a firing battery, showing a
cross-section of a firing unit, com
pletely equipped.
Stanford students who are taking
R.O.T.C. training will not partici
pate in the parade, since their drill
for this quarter docs not begin until
this afternoon.
