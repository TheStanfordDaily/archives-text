# U.S. Subs Sink Three Jan Ships
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. April (5 (UP).—
American submarines hacking away
at lengthening Japanese supply lines
have sunk three more enemy ships
on Japan's threshold, the Navy De
partment disclosed tonight. One of
the submersibles sent a freighter to
the bottom in Japanese home waters.
