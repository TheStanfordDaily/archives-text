# Nomcom Meets Tomorrow at 1
## 
### 
Nominating Committee will meet
on Wednesday, Thursday, and Fri
day of this week in the Excom room,
according to Irma Jean Hannibal,
chairman. The committee will meet
on Wednesday and Thursday from
1 to 4 and on Friday from 1 to 6.
All women whose last names begin
with letters A through G will come
before the committee on Wednesday,
those whose names begin with H
through M on Thursday, and N
through Z on Friday.
The following offices will be open
for women, for which Nom Com will
interview any girls interested:
A.S.S.U. Vice-President (who must
be a junior at present); President
and Vice-President of Women's Con
ference (juniors) and Secretary of
Women's Conference (freshman);
Senior Woman on Excom (junior);
Junior Woman on Excom (Sopho
more); two members of Women's
Council (juniors); Graduate Mem
ber on Excom (senior or graduate);
and Member of Student Body at
Large (any class).
Excom has recommended that all
student body officers take office at
the beginning of summer quarter,
and in case they will be unable to
do so, an alternate will be appointed
until fall quarter.
Girls who are not nominated by
Nom Com may run for any office.
Nominating Committee only guar
antees that well qualified persons
appear on the ballot, according to
Miss Hannibal.
