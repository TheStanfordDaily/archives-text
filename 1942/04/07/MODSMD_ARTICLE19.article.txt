# Stanford Hospital Opens Food Clinic
## 
### 
Stanford University Hospital in
San Francisco has opened a food
clinic in its out-patient department
to supply a growing need for nutri
tional advice as part of medical con
sultation.
The food clinic has been set up to
teach normal nutrition to patients,
how to plan and buy foods necessary
to good health, and yet remain with
in a limited budget, and to instruct
patients in special diets.
