# Bids Opening For Dam Construction

### 
DENVER, April (5 (UP).—Harry A.
Bashore, assistant commissioner of
the U.S. Bureau of Reclamation, ar
rived here today to issue formal call
for bids for construction of the larg
est earth-fill dam in the world—the
$41,000.0(H) Davis Dam in the lower
Colorado River Valley area.
Work on the dam, which will re
quire three and a half years to
complete, will he started this sum
mer under an A 2 priority rating to
speed up availability of materials
