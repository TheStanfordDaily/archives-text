# Museum Displays Shell Collection
## 
### 
A new collection of land shells
from all over the world has been
completed and placed on exhibit in
the Conchological Museum in the
Geology Building.
The exhibit was arranged by Dr.
Angeline Keen, curator of paleon
tology, who is in charge of the mu
seum owned by the University and
directed by the geology department.
The shell collection is arranged ac
cording to the areas from which they
come, Paleartic, Ethiopian, Oriental,
Australasian, Nearctic, and Neo
tropical.
