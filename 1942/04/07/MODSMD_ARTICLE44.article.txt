# Novelist to Speak To Writers' Club
## 
### 
Mrs. Ivor Winters (Janet Lewis),
author of the recently published
book, "The Wife of Martin Guerre,"
will speak at the meeting of the
Stanford Writers' Club Wednesday
night at 7:30 in the Nitery. All
those interested are invited to come.
Today is the deadline for material
to be included in the Writers' Club
contest. All those wishing to enter
short stories or poems in the con
test should turn them in at the Car
illon office or to any member of the
Carillon staff.
