# Women Sponsor Applications Due
## 
### 
Irma Jean Hannibal, president of
Women's Conference, announced
: yesterday that all women interested
in becoming sponsors should give
their names to their living group
presidents.
The presidents will then give the
names to the student committee
which selects the women sponsors.
This committee chooses sponsors
upon the approval of the Dean of
Women's office. Sponsors will be
chosen not later than the first week
in May.
