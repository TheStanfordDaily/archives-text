# Girl Scout Jobs Open to Seniors
## 
### 
All senior women who will grad
uate in June and are interested in
Girl Scout work as a profession
should consult Miss Anastasia Doyle
either tomorrow or Wednesday.
In the Dean of Women's office this
Thursday and Friday the regional
director of the National Board of
Girl Scouts, Miss Vaal Stark, will
interview senior women and accept
applications for the summer Girl
Scout training program.
On completion of this training
program, candidates will be eligible
for professional Girl Scout work in
various communities upon the rec
ommendation of the board.
