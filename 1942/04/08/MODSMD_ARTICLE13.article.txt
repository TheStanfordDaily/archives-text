# DRIVE BENEFITS WILL GO TO THEM
## 
### 

THESE ARE KINDERGARTEN kids at the Convalescent Home who are recovered enough to be out of
their beds part of the time. Bed-ridden boys of the same age across the hall arc given individual in
struction at their beds. Regular classrooms arc situated in other units for "runabouts," and the children
receive full school credit for their period of confinement.
