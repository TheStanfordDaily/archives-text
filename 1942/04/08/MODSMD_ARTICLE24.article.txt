# STOCKS RELEASE SEVEN
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. April 7 (UP). —
William Schrocdcr, president of the
California Baseball League, tonight
announced the sale or release of
seven players formerly belonging to
Stockton who became the property
of the league when Stockton gave
up its franchise.
