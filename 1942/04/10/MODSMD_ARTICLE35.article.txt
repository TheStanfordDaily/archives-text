# Institute Blanks To Go in Today
## 
### 
Latin-American students at Stan
ford are asked to take the question
naires they received from the Insti
tute of International Relations to
the office of Mrs. Maria Hoge today.
The questionnaires issued by the
Institute are to ascertain who and
how many Latin-American students
are in United States universities, and
how long they plan to stay here, ac
cording to Chancellor Ray Lyman
Wilbur.
