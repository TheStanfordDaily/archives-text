# ELECT NEW AIR HEAD
## 
### 
NEW YORK, April 9 (UP).—A. N.
Kemp, of Los Angeles, was elected
president of American Airlines, Inc.,
today, succeeding C. R. Smith, who
will devote his services to the Army
air force at the request of Lieutenant
General H. H. Arnold.
