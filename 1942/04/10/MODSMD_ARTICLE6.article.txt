# Hoover Back At Stanford
## 
### 
Ex-President Herbert Hoover re
turned yesterday to his campus resi
dence after an extended stay in New
York.
He will be on campus "for some
days" in order to attend the Board
of Trustees meeting on April 23, his
secretary reported. Mr. Hoover, in
accordance with his long-standing
practice, would grant no interviews.
While in New York he maintained
a suite of rooms in the Waldorf-
Astoria, where he spent much of his
time in writing.
Due to recent developments in
the war, Mr. Hoover has done noth
ing further in his plan to send food
to the occupied countries of Eu
rope, it was reported. The main
problem now is supplying the Allied
countries.
