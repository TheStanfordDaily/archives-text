# SOPHOMORE CARNIVAL TONIGHT
## 
### 

THESE SCENES from last year's Soph Carnival will be repeated with
alterations tonight. Above is a shooting gallery, which will be replaced
tonight with the Fiji "Human Shooting Gallery"; and below Ls one of
