# Mothers to Give Garden Program
## 
### 
"And Mother Marches, Too!" is
the title of the program to be pre
sented by the Stanford Mothers'
Club at its annual spring event in
Mrs. Charles Lathrop's garden at
Alta Vista on April .10 at 2 p.m.
Including in the price of the ticket
is the program, a tour of the ex
tensive gardens, and refreshments.
Tickets may be obtained from Mrs.
Donald Smith and her committee in
Palo Alto or from representatives in
San Jose, San Francisco, and the
East Bay. They are also on sale at
Keeble's in Palo Alto.
