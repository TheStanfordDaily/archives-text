# Hospitality Center Needs Volunteers
## 
### 
The new service men's hospitality
center at the home of Mrs. Walter
Rodgers, 579 University Avenue,
will open to Palo Altans on May 3
only, and on Monday, May 4, with
formal ceremonies, it will be opened
for the men.
Volunteers for work at the center
arc asked to register by writing the
Service Men's Recreation Commit
tee, in care of the Palo Alto Cham
ber of Commerce. Immediate work
will include furniture upholstering,
painting floors, and making curtains
and pillow covers. Later, musicians
and hostesses will be needed. In
the application for work, the type
of work the person is willing to do
should be listed.
