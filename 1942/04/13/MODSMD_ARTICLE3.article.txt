# Branner Men Plan Beachcomber Ball
## 
### 
Herb Casey, president of Branner
Hall, announced that the annual
Branner Spring Dance will be held
May 15. This year's dance will have
as a theme a Beachcombers' Ball,
announced Casey. The dance will
be held in the Branner dining room
in a South Sea island setting.
Casey announced plans for the
dance at the installation of the new
ofTiccrs at the first smoker of the
quarter held last Wednesday night.
Coach Jini Lawson was guest speak
er of the evening.
