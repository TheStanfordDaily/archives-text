# Dean Praises Subs For Hoop Efforts
## 
### 
Everett Dean, Coach of the na
tional championship Cardinal bas
ketball team, yesterday praised the
efforts of his two subs, Fred Linari
and Jack Dana, who replaced Jim
Pollard and Don Burness in the
final game against Dartmouth.
The ball handling of 5-foot-8 Li
nari and his excellent floor work
were very instrumental in defeating

according to Dean.
