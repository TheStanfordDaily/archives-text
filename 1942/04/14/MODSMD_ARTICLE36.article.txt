# EDITORS AID CENSORS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Alril 13 (UP).—
The Office of Censorship announced
tonight it is inaugurating a program
under which newspaper editors
would serve as unofficial observers
in their home states to encourage
compliance with the censorship code.
