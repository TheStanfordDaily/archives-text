# Nancy Gammon Given Quad Post
## 
### 
Nancy Gammon was made junior
editor of the Quad for next winter
and spring quarters, bv action of
Publications Council yesterday, re
ceiving the pay for work done in
that capacity. Miss Gammon replaces
Elena Thorn;is.
Bill Lane, Chaparral business
manager, was appointed to obtain
speakers for a meeting of Quad,
Chappie and Daily business staffs,
at which time new advertising and
production problems will be dis
cussed.
It was further decided that Dan
Lewis, Daily business manager, will
give special training to men on his
staff who are in line for the new
business manager post, before the
new manager is chosen.
