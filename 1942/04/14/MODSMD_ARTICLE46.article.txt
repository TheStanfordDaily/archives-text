# TUNNEY AWARD WINNERS FOR 1942
## 
### 

MEMBERS OF STANFORD'S 1942 boxing team who were selected for the annual Tunney awards pose
with their coaches. Back row, left to right: Pete Gadd, Coach Harry Maloney, Coach Clayton Frye, Boh
McNeil, Al Smith, and Ira Fislunan. Front row: Hank Claflin, Ily Ilehert. F. Q. Hewitt, and Auhrey Fairfax.
