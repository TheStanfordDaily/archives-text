# Holman Announces Murals Postponed
## 
### 
Ked Holman, intramural man
ager. stated last night that finals
in the mural track meet will lie
held sometime next week and
that softball, baseball, and tennis
will also be postponed until later.
Finals in the track meet were
originally scheduled for yester
day afternoon, but now will not
be held until a more convenient
time.
