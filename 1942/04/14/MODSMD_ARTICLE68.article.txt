# Chemists Sought By Civil Service
## 
### 
United States Civil Service Com
mission is now seeking junior chem
ists to perform research, investiga
tive, or other work in some branch
of chemistry. The positions pay
$2,000 a year; no written test is re
qiured, and applicants' qualifica
tions will be judged from their ex
perience, education, and training.
Women especially are urged to ap
ply. Applications must be filed with
the Civil Service Commission, Wash
ington, D.C., and will be accepted
until the needs of the service have
been met. Information may be ob
tained from first and second class
post offices.
