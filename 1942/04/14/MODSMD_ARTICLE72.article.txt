# ALIEN BANS MAY RELAX
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 13 (UP).
—Enemy aliens who have immediate
relatives in the U.S. armed forces
may be exempted soon from con
trols now imposed upon them. The
Justice Department in Washington
is studying the problem and it was
understood officials were unanimous
in favoring proposed action.
