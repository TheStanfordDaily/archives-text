# Band to Present Military Concert
## 
### 
To build up spirit, especially dur
the war time, the Stanford Band
will present a patriotic concert to
morrow afternoon at 4:30 in the
Union court, according to Frederick
Preston Search, conductor of the
band.
"I wish it weer possible to have a
concert once every week," Mr.
Search continued, "but since il is
not there will be one every other
week."
The band will play a program of
marches, waltzes and light concert
band music. Mr. Arthur Winn of
the San Francisco International Art
Foundation will be the announcer.
