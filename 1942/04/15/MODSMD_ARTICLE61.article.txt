# Navy Headguarters Moved to 703 Market
## 
###

needs of the Navy, the San Fran
cisco and Twelfth Naval District
headquarters of the director of
Naval Officer Procurement have
been moved from the Federal
Building to 703 Market Street in
San Francisco.
This change in address will af
fect all Stanford men enlisted in
the Naval Reserve's officer train
