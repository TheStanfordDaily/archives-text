# 'Home' to Get Show Profits
## 
### 
All proceeds from this year's
Spring Show, "A Sale of Two
Cities," will be contributed to the
Convalescent Home Fund.
This plan, suggested by Bill
Moran, chairman of Dramatic Coun
cil, was adopted by Excom on Tues
day.
Final acting tryouts for "A Sale of
Two Cities" will be held this after
noon from 1 to 5 in the lobby of
Memorial Hall, and the complete
cast will be announced in Friday's
Daily, according to John Lawry,
director.
Those who did not try out yes
terday arc urged to come to today's
tryouts, Lawry said. Singing try
outs, both group and solo, were held
Tuesday afternoon, under Musical
Director Marion Scott.
