# Women's Archery Range Open to All
## 
### 
The women's archery range, lo
cated next to the women's pool, will
be open to all students on week days
from 4 to 6 p.m. and from 10 to 12
a.m. on Saturdays, according to
Jeanette Light, archery manager.
Men are cordially invited to use the
W.A.A. and physical education de
partment tackle or private bows less
than 32 pounds.
The archery manager will be pres
ent during all open hours, but she
requests that women bringing men
guests new to archery instruct them.
