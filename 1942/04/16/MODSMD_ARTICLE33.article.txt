# Student Rate Set For Social Meet
## 
### 
There will be a special rate for
Stanford, California, and San Jose
students attending the annual meet
ing of the California Conference of
Social Work in San Francisco, April
19 to 23. Among the speakers is
Chancellor Wilbur, who will speak
on "'Medical Service in Wartime,"
and Bay Area Red Cross officials.

College students may simply pre
sent their student body cards when
registering at the civic auditorium.
