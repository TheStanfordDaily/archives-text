# Radio Man Faces Espionage Charge
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 15 (UP).—
William Arthur Schuler, 23, com
mercial radio operator, late today
was booked by federal agents and
held in lieu of $20,000 bail on a
federal charge of attempted espio
nage.

He was taken into custody by
FBI men after he allegedly ap
proached a person posing as an en
emy agent and offered to transmit
messages outside the country.
