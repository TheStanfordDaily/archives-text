WASHINGTON, April 15 (UP).—
President Roosevelt's anti-inflation
program is not expected to ask new
levies beyond the $7,600,000,000
sought in the pending war revenue
bill, it was said tonight, and, as a
consequence, work on a concrete
form of the new tax measure will be
started next week.
