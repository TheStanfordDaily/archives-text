# JAP PHOTO OF ATTACK ON HAWAII
## 
### 

RADIOED FROM MOSCOW to New York, this photo is described as a Jap airview of their December 7 at
tack on Pearl Harbor. Just beyond Ford Island, center, is seen a white column of water rising from bomb
falling near the line of U.S. warships. At extreme left, oil near side of island, four ships are described as,
left to right, two cruisers, one smoking; the I'.S.S. Utah on her side; and a "special aircraft carrier" smok
ing after a hit.
