# MAIL FRAUD CHARGE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 10 (UP).
—William A. Atchison, Oakland,
waived a hearing today and con
sented to his removal to Reno, Nev.,
on an indictment charging him and
Edgar G. Hamilton with conspiracy
to use the mails to defraud.

—Dr. Monroe E. Deutsch, vice-pres
ident and provost of the University
of California, said in a radio speech
supporting the war bond drive to
night that America is fighting for
freedom of the world against the
ivrces of slavery.
