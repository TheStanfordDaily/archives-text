# Summer Students To Get Aid Funds
## 
### 
Applications for assistance from
Student Aid funds for use during
the summer quarter may be obtained
at the Registrar's office. They must
be filed before May 1. The commis
sion will announce the results about
the middle of May.
July 31 is the deadline for filing
applications for assistance from
funds to be used next year. Stu
dents should not file these applica
tions until after the close of spring
quarter.
