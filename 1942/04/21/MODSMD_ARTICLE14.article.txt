# Classes Dismissed 11:35 Today To Meet A.S.S.U. Candidates
## 
### 
All candidates for tomorrow's election will be introduced to the stu
dent body today at J 1:155 a.m. in Memorial Hall by A.S.S.U. President
George Jackson.
Candidates for A.S.S.U. president and vice-president will make short
speeches outlining their election platforms and promises.

All classes will be dismissed today
at 11:35 a.m. so that students may
attend the election assembly.
All candidates must appear at Me
morial Hall by 11:15 a.m. today or
their nominations will be consid
ered declined. Those candidates
who wish to decline nominations
must file declinations at the Law
Steps between 8:30 a.m. and 2 p.m.
today.
The election, which will fill 27 stu
dent body offices, will be held from
8:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. tomorrow. Four
polling places will be open —at the
English Corner, Engineering Corner,
Main Library, and the Biology Cor
ner.
Ex Committee will meet tomor
row night to approve the results of
the elections, as tabulated by the
Election Board.
