# Vaccination Slips Due Monday at 5
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates arc due at
the Registrar's Office on Monday for
all new students, including those
having permits to attend classes, and
all other students whose certificates
have expired, it was announced to
day. '
Failure to file certificates by 5
o'clock on Monday will result in a
fine of $2, or, if the certificates are
not filed by May 4, registration in
the University will be subject to
cancellation by the Committee on
Public Health.
