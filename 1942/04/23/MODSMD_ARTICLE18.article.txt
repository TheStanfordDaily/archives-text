# Missionary to Talk On China at War
## 
### 
Mr. David H. Adeney, graduate of
Cambridge University, will speak to
night at 7:30 in the lower lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse. His sub
ject will be "The Christian Church
Advances in War-torn China."
Mr. Adeney has lately returned
from China where he was engaged
in missionary work. He is now mis
sionary secretary for the British
Inter-Varsity Christian Fellowship.
