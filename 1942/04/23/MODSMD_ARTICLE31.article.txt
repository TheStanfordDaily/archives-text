# AS ZETES WON MURAL TRACK MEET
## 
### 

SCENES FROM TUESDAY'S annual intramural track meet, won by the Zetes with a total score of 37,
show (top photo) Jim Verdieck, Kappa Sig, winning the low hurdles, with Zete John Eikclman, Alpha Delt
Pete Gadd and Chi Psi Joe MacHarg close behind; while in the high hurdles (bottom) Eikehnan is way out
ahead with brother Zetes Jack Dana and Bill Symes fighting it for second place with Verdieck and Phi Psi
Ed DeMoss.
