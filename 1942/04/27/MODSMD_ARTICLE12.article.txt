# Victory Caravan Leaves Hollywood
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD. April" 26 (UP). -
The Hollywood Victory Caravan,
made up of 22 screen stars, tonight
left for Washington, the first of 12
cities in which the stars will appear
for the benefit of the Army Emer
gency Reilef and the Navy Relief
Society.
In addition to the name person
alities, the caravan included numer
ous technicians and executive per
sonnel, the entire party totaling
about 90 persons.
