# U.S.O. DANCE KILLING
## 
### 
SPRINGFIELD, Mo., April 26
(UP). —Two persons were killed and
a third was critically wounded early
today in an unexplained shooting
that followed a United Service Or
ganizations dance for soldiers from
nearby camps.
