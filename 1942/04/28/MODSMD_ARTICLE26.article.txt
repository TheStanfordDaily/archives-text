# LYNN CHANGES NAME
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, April 27 (UP).—
Movie Actor Jeffrey Lynn, whose
legal name formerly was Ragnar
Godfrey Lynd. tonight fded a re
quest with Secretary of State Paul
Peck that he be known henceforth
by his professional name.
