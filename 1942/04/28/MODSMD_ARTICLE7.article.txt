# C.A.P. Will Meet Tonight in Nitery
## 
### 
All members of the Civil Ait-
Patrol will meet in the Nitery to
morrow night at 7:30 to hear an ad
dress by Mr, Bowman, wing com
mander of the Palo Alto unit, who
will give the new specifications on
the CAP. which have just come
from Washington, D C., including the
schedule of an 80-hour course.
Any students who are not already
connected with the C.A.P., but who
are interested in first aid, radio
work, or aviation, are also urged to
attend.
