# SUGAR RATIONS
## 
### 
Managers of all campus fraterni
ties, sororities, and eating clubs
must apply for their certificates to
purchase sugar by Wednesday night
at 8. Applications must be made to
the sugar rationing board at Palo
Alto High School. Each house and
club must get its own certificate,
the Stanford Commissary cannot
get them, E. H. Ayres, commissary
manager, announced yesterday.
