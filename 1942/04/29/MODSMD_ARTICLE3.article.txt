# SOLDIER GETS MEDAL
## 
### 
HONOLULU, April 28 (UP).—The
Soldier's Medal has been awarded
In Harold A. Mcßride, private first
class in the U.S. Army, for heroism
in saving the life of a comrade, it
was announced tonight.
