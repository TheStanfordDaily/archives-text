# GOLF COURSE CLOSES
## 
### 
ST. PETERSBURG. Fla., April 29
(UP). —Dixie Hollins, owner of the
famous Pasadena golf course in St.
Petersburg, announced that the 18-
hole layout would be closed until
after the war, and turned into a
cow pasture. He offered the course
to the city, hut was refused.

Mosquitoes arc associated with
nalaria.
