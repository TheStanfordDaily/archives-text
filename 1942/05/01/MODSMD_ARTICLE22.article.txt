# Toyon Club Holds Formal on Friday
## 
### 
With spring flowers banking the
lobby, and doors opening onto the
lighted garden, Toyon Club will hold
its spring formal next Friday eve
ning from 9 to 1.
Committee heads, announced by
Jim Atkinson, general chairman, are
patrons, Don Craig; refreshments,
Harry Pangbum; lighting, Rene
Zentner; and decorations, Bob Walt.
Eddie Murphy's orchestra will
play. The garden between Toyon
, and Branner, where refreshments
I will be served, will be lighted.
