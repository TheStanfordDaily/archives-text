# Yale Nurses' Class Open for Summer
## 
### 
Because of the great need for
nurses during the present emergen
cy, the Yale University School of
Nursing is instituting a special sum
mer session of the school beginning
June 23.
Only women with A.B. degrees
will be accepted. Those interested
are asked to write The Dean,
School of Nursing of Yale Univer
sity, New Haven, Conn.
