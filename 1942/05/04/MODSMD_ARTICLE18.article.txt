# 'Wind and Rain' Ends Run Tonight
## 
### 
The final performance of "Wind
and Rain," a Merton Hodge com
edy on medical school life, will be
presented tonight in the Little The
ater.

Dwight Ewing, Marcia Shaugh
nessy, and Marcie Baum, share top
honors in the play. They are sup
ported by Helen Livingston, John
High, Adolpho Arias, Gordon Gibb,
and John Lawry.

The play, one of a series to give
experience to student directors, is
directed by Norman Philbrick.
