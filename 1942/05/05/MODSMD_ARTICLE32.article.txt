# Dodger Manager, Players Pay Fines
## 
### 
» »
NEW YORK, May 4 (UP).—Presi
dent Ford Flick of the National
League today fined Manager Leo
Durochcr of the Brooklyn Dodgers
$50 and three of his players $25 each
for yesterday's altercations during
the doubleheader with the Cardinals
at St. Louis.
The players fined were Field Cap
tain Dolph Camilli and Pitchers
Freddie Fitzsimmons and Chester
Kehn.
No action was taken against Man
ager Billy Southworth of the Cards
or Pitcher Whit Wyatt of the
Dodgers, who also were ejected from
the game. Apparently they took their
expulsion without too much in
vective.
