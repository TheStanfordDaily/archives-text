# F.D.R WINS VICTORY
## 
### 
PIERRE. S.D., May 5 (UP).
President Roosevelt's war program
tonight appeared to have won a ma
jor victory as former Gov. Tom
Berry took a commanding lead over
Sen. William J. Bulow in the race
for the Democratic nomination for
Senator from South Dakota. Re
turns from 79 precincts gave Berry,
an outspoken supporter of the Presi
dent, 2,095 votes to 970 for Senator
Bulow.
