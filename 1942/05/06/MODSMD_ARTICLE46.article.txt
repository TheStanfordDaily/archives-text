# Intramural Ball To Start Today
## 
### 
With only four teams entered in
the intramural baseball competition,
the squads will play a double round
robin to decide the title, according to
Mural Manager Red Holman.
The Phi Psi's will meet El Cuadro
on the frosh diamond at 4 this af
ternoon and the Kappa Sigs battle
Toyon tomorrow afternoon in the
first round.
The schedule for the remaining
rounds is posted on the bulletin
board in the Men's Gym.
In 13 games the New York Yankees
used 14 pinch-hitters, six of them
for Ed Levy, first baseman, and only
four came through.
