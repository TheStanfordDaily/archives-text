# UNITED NATIONS TAKE MADAGASCAR
## 
### 

(Acme Telephoto)
GUARDING VITAL SEA LANES in the Middle East, India, and China, and threatened by invasion by the
Japanese, the strategically important island of Madagascar has been taken under the protection of the
United Nations and invaded by picked British commandos and parachute troops. The main expeditionary
force is battling into Diego Suarez, key naval base.
