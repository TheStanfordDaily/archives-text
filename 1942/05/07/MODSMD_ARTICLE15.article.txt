# ALUMINUM INCREASE
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 6 (UP).—
The National Aircraft Standards
Committee at a meeting here today
agreed upon methods of standardiza
tion which they claimed would in
crease production of aluminum tub
ing in military aircraft building by
as much as 20 per cent.
