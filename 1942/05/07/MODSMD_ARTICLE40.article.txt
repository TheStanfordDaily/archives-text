# WHIRLAWAY WINS AGAIN
## 
### 
BALTIMORE, Md„ May 6 (UP).—
Calumet Farm's Whirlaway, 1941
horse of the year and third richest
money - winning Thoroughbred in
history, climbed another notch closer
to Seabiscuit's all-time money mark
today when he came from far behind
in the stretch to score a slim but
decisive triumph in the 38th run
ning of the $20,000 Dixie Handicap.
