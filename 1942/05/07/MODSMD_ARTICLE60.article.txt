# MINESWEEPER LAUNCHED
## 
### 
ALAMEDA, May 6 (UP). —The
minesweeper U.S.S. Swallow was
launched today at the yards of the
General Engineering and Drydock
Company. Mrs. Nellie E. Burnett,
wife of a foreman pipefitter at the
ward, acted as sponsor for the vessel.
