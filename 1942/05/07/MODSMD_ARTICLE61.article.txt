# ALLEN TO PLAY
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the regular
Thursday afternoon organ recital at
4:15 today in the Memorial Church.
The program will include "The
Chorale in A Minor" by Cesar
Franck, "Largo from the Concerto
in D Minor" by Vivaldi, "Gigue in
G Minor" by Jean-Baptiste Lully,
"FugTae in E Flat" by J. S. Bach, and
two Welsh airs, "All Thro' the
Night" and "March of the Men of
Harlech."
