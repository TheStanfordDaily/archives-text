# Hikers Will Trek 16 Miles Sunday
## 
### 
Hikers taking part in the local
physical fitness program, under the
leadership of Lieut. Comm. Stewart
Bryant, will leave from Frenchman's
Bridge on the campus at J) on Sun
day morning. The trip this week
will be 16 miles.
The group will follow a route over
the Los Altos foothills to Hidden
Villa, Frank Duveneck's ranch on
Moody Road, where members may
swim before eating lunch and start
ing for home. This trip marks the
midway point in a program which
will culminate in a 24-mile hike
on the final Sunday.
