# Toyon to Dance At Spring Formal
## 
### 
Toyon Club members and their
guests will dance to the music of
Eddie Murphy and his orchestra to
night between 9 and 1 in the Toyon
lobby at the first Toyon spring
formal.
Murphy's orchestra has played at
Toyon winter formals for the past
three years, according to Jim Atkin
son, general dance chairman. Assist
ing Atkinson and Hall President
John Parker with arrangements have
been Don Craig, patrons; Harry
Pangburn, refreshments; Rene Zerit
nor, lighting; and Bob Walt, deco
rations.
