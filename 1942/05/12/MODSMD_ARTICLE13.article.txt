# Increase in State Guard Advocated
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, May 11 (UP).-
An advisory Slate Guard committee
today recommended to Governor
Culbert L. Olson that he increase
the existing State Guard of 7,000
members to a unit of 15,000, includ
ing a mobile force capable of prompt
emergency use anywhere in the
state.
President Ray Lyman Wilbur of
Stanford University and head of the
committee said the recommendation
was formed after hearing public and
private agencies list their State
Guard needs.
