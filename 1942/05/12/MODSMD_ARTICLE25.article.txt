# YUNNAN ARMY PUSHES JAPS OUT OF CHINA
## 
### 
CHUNGKING, May 11 (UP).—
China's Yunnan province army, sup
ported by American fliers, has sent
the battered remnant of a Japanese
invasion column reeling 24 miles
back to the Burma border, where
heavy fighting is raging with Japa
nese reinforcements pushed up from
Lashio, it was announced tonight.
