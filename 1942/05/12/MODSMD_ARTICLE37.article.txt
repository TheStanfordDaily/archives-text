# Life Saving Class To Open for Men
## 
### 
Myron Sprague, Stanford swim
ming instructor, announced yester
day that any men wishing a Senior
Life Saving Certificate may report
to the pool for life saving training.
The special life saving class will
meet in conjunction with Mr.
Sprague's regular swimming classes
at 10 and 2 o'clock Monday, Wed
nesday, and Friday. Mr. Sprague
pointed out that this training, and
the certificate, are necessary for men
desiring life guard jobs for the sum
mer.
