## BROOKLYN DODGERS BUY ROWE 
### 

"SCHOOLBOY" HOWE, Detroit Tigers' pitcher, has been sold outright
to the Brooklyn Dodgers for. an unannounced sum, reported to be
from $25,000 to $45,000. Sale of the hig right-hander was announced
by Manager Jack Zeller. in Detroit.
