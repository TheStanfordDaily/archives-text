# U.S. IS STILL HOLDING MIDWAY
## 
### 

WAKE, GUAM, MANILA have all been lost to the Japanese, but one of America's outposts in the Pacific—
Midway Island—is still holding. On a recent visit there. Admiral Chester W. Nimitz, second from left,
shakes hands with Captain James L. Neefus, leader of a Marine fighter unit which shot down a Japanese
plane in a recent encounter.
