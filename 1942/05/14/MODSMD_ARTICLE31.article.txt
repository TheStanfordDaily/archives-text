# FOUR WARSHIPS IN AN HOUR
## 
### 

FOUR DESTROYERS go down the ways at the Federal shipyards,
Kearny, N.J., in 55 minutes. They were built and launched 90 per cent
complete in 7-9 months. Upper left, the Fletcher; right, Radford. Lower
right, the Quick; left, Mervine. Governor Edison called it a "naval
victory."
