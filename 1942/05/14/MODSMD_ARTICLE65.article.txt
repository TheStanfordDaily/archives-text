# Allen to Present Afternoon Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the regular
Thursday afternoon organ recital
today at 4:15 p.m. in the Memorial
Church. The program will consist
of two numbers by Bach, "Prelude in
D major" and "Jesu, Joy of Man's
Desiring"; Chopin's "Prelude in C
Minor"; Dvorak's "Largo" from the
"New World Symphony"; and Du
bois' "Toccata in G Major."
