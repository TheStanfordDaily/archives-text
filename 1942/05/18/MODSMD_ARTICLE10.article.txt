# Merrill, Fisher, Lutz to Speak
## 
### 
Professors Harold H. Fisher,
Ralph H. Lutz, and Merrill T.
Spalding will present a symposium
on "Russia and Germany" tomorrow
at 7:30 p.m. in the Memorial Hall
Auditorium.
Dr. Fisher and Dr. Lutz are pro
fessors of history and Dr. Spalding
is an assistant professor of history.
Professor Fisher will discuss the
basic international conflict. The
German drive and objectives will be
discussed by Professor Lutz, and
Professor Spalding will talk on Rus
sia's defenses and resources.
The audience will have the oppor
tunity to ask questions of the speak
ers after their speeches.
