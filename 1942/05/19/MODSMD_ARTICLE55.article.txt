# Discussion Held On Price Ceiling
## 
### 
W. A. Wallis, professor of eco
nomics, conducted a discussion group
on the price ceiling bill yesterday
afternoon under the sponsorship of
the consumer committee.
Held to answer any student ques
tions on the freezing of prices just
put into effect, the meeting cov
ered the nature of the price control
legislation, its purpose and its ef
fect on Stanford. Dodie Crile and
George Hellyer, co-chairmen of the
committee, had charge of the meet
ing.
Other discussion groups on price
control are being planned for later
in the quarter. These will deal with
the details and problems of enforce
ment.
