# Union Sets Date For Fiesta Dance
## 
### 
Final preparations are being made

Patio of the Women's Clubhouse
next Friday night.

with flowers, stalls of flowers, Mexi
can punch, and cookies will give

i dance, said.

Mary Tomlinson and Emlen Wash
burn, decorations; Sally Burke,
clean-up; Catharine Connaghan, re
freshments; Dorothy Stanley, or
