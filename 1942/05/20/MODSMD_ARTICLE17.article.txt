# Debate Council Reelects Frolik
## 
### 
Jim Frolik was re-elected debate
manager for the coming year, and
Bonner Richardson was elected as
sistant manager by Debate Council
yesterday.
Frolik is this year's winner of the
Medaille Joffre and the all-Univer
sity tournament. Richardson has
participated in debates and confer
ences this year.
At Debate Councils meeting, the
annual debate banquet was sched
uled for June 2 in the Union dining
hall. The two top debate awards,
the Ronald Kay Memorial Debate
Trophy and the Ray Lyman Wilbur
Award, will be presented to this
year's champion debaters at the I
banquet.
