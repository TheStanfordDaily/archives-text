# JAP EVACUATION
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, May 19 (UP).
—New evacuation orders issued by
the Army today will complete the
evacuation of Japanese from River
side County and begin transfer of
persons in Kern and San Bernardino
Counties toward the interior away
from restricted zones.
