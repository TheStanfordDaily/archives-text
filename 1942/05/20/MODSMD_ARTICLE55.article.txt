# Campus Continues U.S.O. Activities
## 
### 
Farm U.S.O. activity will continue j
tomorrow and Friday nights when
Casa Magnolia entertains 25 Page
Mill men during the evening and the
Delt house has eight to dinner.
Other campus living groups en
tertained over 125 soldiers last Sun
day. The Gamma Phi house had 40
men for buffet supper, while the
A. O. Pis were hostesses to 25. At
the same time the Alpha Phi house
had 30 soldiers from Tanforan for
buffet supper. Madrono also had
