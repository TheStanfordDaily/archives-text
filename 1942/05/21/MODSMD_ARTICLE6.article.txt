## 
###

history ;md dean ui graduate study
at Stanford, will discuss the subject
"War Aims" tonight at the com
mencement of the College of Phy
sicians and Surgeons of San Fran
cisco.

by Dr. Henry Clay Veatch, Jr.,
president of the College, to 33 sen
iors. Christopher Bentley will give
the valediction.
