# Ensign to Speak On Navy Program
## 
### 
Ensign S. L. Wyatt of the United
States Navy will speak to freshmen
and sophomore men interested in
the Navy's new V-l enlistment pro
gram on May 28 in Encina lobby,
Farm recruiting officials announced
last night.
The following day, May 29, a party
of naval officials from San Francisco
will give physical examinations to
applicants in Room 50 of the B.A.C.
At the same time, enlistment papers
may be obtained
