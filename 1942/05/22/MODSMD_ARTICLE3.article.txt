# Stanford Husband In Raid on Tokio
## 
### 
Among the American airmen who
bombed Tokyo with Brig. Gen.
James Doolittle was Lieut. Everett
Holstrum. husband of the former
Harriet Fisher, Gamma Phi Beta
'42.
Miss Fisher was a night editor on
The Daily last year and was to have
been a sponsor at Roble this year.
She and Lieut. Holstrum, a gradu
ate of Oregon, were married this
summer. She is now living in Ta
coma, Wash.
