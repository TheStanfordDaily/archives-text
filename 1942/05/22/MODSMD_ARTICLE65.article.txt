# SHIPBUILDING RECORD
## 
### 
PORTLAND, Ore., May 21 (UP).—
Two national shipbuilding records
will be set on Maritime Day to
morrow by the Oregon Shipbuild
ing Corporation when three com
pleted ships will be delivered, three
vessels will be launched and keels
for three others will be laid.
