# STOCK WEEK HOPEFUL
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 24 (UP).—
Stocks, bonds and commodities'
traced a "hope pattern" last week
when financial quarters were swept
by growing belief that the war may
end much sooner than had been ex
pected, although there was little in
the news to justify .such an opinion.
