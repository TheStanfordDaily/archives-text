# Phi Betes to Hold Dinner Tomorrow
## 
### 
Phi Beta Kappa, national honor
ary scholastic society, will hold its
annual banquet for 64 newly elected
members at the Stanford chapter to
morrow at 6 p.m.
After the banquet, the chapter will
hold its open meeting in the audi
torium of the Education Building.
Louis 13. Wright of the Huntington
Library will deliver an address on
"Humanistic Education and the
Democratic State." The public is in
vited to this meeting which opens at
8 p.m.
The new members will be ini
tiated after the meeting. Guests of
honor at the banquet will also be
those who were elected to Phi Bete
last autumn.
