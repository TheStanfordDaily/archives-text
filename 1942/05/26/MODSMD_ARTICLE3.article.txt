# Farm Alumnus Taking Chem Warfare Course
## 
### 
EDGEWOOD ARSENAL. Md„
May 19.—Lieutenant Joel Orchard
Henry, Stanford alumnus, is taking
an advanced course in the Chemical
Warfare School here. In civilian life
Lieutenant Henry was a junior ex
ecutive with the Hawaiian Pine
apple Company, Ltd.. of San Fran
cisco. He is a reserve officer now
on active duty with the Chemical
Warfare Service.
