# Armstrong Fight Set for San Jose
## 
### 
SAN JOSE, May 26 (UP).—Henry
Armstrong will meet Cecilio Lozado,
Mexican lightweight, here in June,
it was announced today by Promoter
Babe Griffin.
Armstrong has been out of action
since last year, except for army
camp appearances. He is attempting
to make a comeback.
The colored boxer fought under
the banner of Eddie Mead, veteran
manager who died yesterday in New
York.
