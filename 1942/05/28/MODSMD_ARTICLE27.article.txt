# NAZI DRIVES TOWARD THE CAUCASUS
## 
### 

THE NAZIS HOPE TO CROSS Kerch Strait (they've already taken the town of Kerch) and join another
drive from IJarvenkova-Izyum sector to push past Rostov and into oil-rich Caucasus, according to the plan
