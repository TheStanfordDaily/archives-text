# Quentin Inmates Plan to Buy Bonds
## 
### 
SAN QUENTIN PRISON, May 2(>
(UP). —The inmates of the world's
largest prison want to establish a
check-off—for systematic purchase
of war bonds and stamps —Warden
Clinton Duffy said tonight.
The 3500 inmates have already
purchased $18,000 worth of bonds
and stamps from their meager earn
ings.
