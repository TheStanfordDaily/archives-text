# Hanna Will Speak To Education Meet
## 
### 
Paul R. Hanna of Stanford will be
one of the principal speakers at the
80th annual convention and Repre
sentative Assembly of the National
Education Association, to be held in
Denver from June 27 to July 2.
Dr. Hanna will serve as chairman
of one of the three national seminars
in the convention. His group will
discuss post-war problems. Dr.
Hanna, chairman of the Commission
on Education and Resources, also
will make a report of the commis
sion's work to the Representative
Assembly.
