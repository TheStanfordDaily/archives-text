# Summer Workers Needed in South
## 
### 
There is a great need for volun
teer workers this summer in Los
Angeles County, to which both men
and women can contribute. The
fields open are drama, recreation,
sports, hospital and clinic aids, all
of which will provide valuable ex
perience as well as a chance to offer
services. There are also many good
openings as mountain camp coun
selors. according to Mrs. Hilary
Aicher, head of the Volunteer Place
ment Bureau in Pasadena.
The places to apply in Pasadena
120 North Oakland, and in Los
Angeles at the Central Volunteer
Bureau in the Chamber of Com
merce Building.
