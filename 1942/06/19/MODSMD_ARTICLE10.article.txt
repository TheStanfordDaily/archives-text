# 3 Dorms Open To Men This Quarter
## 
### 
Three of the University dormi
tories will be open to men students
this summer, according to the office
of the Dean of Men. The halls are
Encina, Toyon, and Sequoia. Se
quoia will be kept as a graduate
men's residence, however.
In addition to the halls there are
a number of fraternity houses oper
ating to accommodate their mem
bers. All of the Row residences ex
cept the Deke, D.U., Kappa Sigma,
Phi Delt, Sigma Nu, Theta Delt, and
Theta Xi houses are planning to re
main open during the summer. Each
fraternity chose whether or not to
remain open, according to the num
ber of members returning.
