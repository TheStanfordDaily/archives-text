# HEADS UP. LOU!
## 
### 
CHICAGO, June 18.—Lou Novi
koff denies he is a guess hitter. The
Cub outfielder, who played with Los
Angeles of the Pacific Coast League,
says the only time he guessed, the
pitch almost tore his head off.
