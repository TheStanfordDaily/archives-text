# Stanfordite Made Red Cross Official
## 
### 
Hammel D. McKay, Jr., Stanford
'31, began duty this week as an as
sistant field director of the American
Red Cross, stationed at Camp Rob
erts.
McKay, while at Stanford, was a
member of the Quad and Daily edi
torial staffs. His job with the mili
tary will include soldier's welfare
problems, furlough requests, recrea
tional equipment, and making loans
for emergency furloughs.
