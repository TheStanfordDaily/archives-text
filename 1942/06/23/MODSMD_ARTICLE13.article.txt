# Women's Pool Opens Swim Classes to Children Friday
## 
### 
The pool at the Women's Gym will
be open to women students on Mon
days, Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and
Thursdays from 4:30 to 5:30, and
from 2:30 to 4:30 each Sunday after
noon.
Faculty women and University, em
ployees may swim during noon
hours for the first eight weeks of
Summer Quarter. The fee for fac

ulty women will be $5 and for
women employees $3.50.
Classes in swimming and rhythm
will be offered for children for six
weeks beginning June 29.
Boys and girls 5 and 6 years of
age may swim on Tuesdays and
Thursdays from 10:50 to 11:20 and
dance on Mondays, Wednesdays, and
Fridays at the same time. Boys and
girls 6 and 7 years of age will swim
and dance on the same days from
11:30 to 12.
Girls 8 to 12 will swim on Mon
days, Wednesdays, and Fridays from
10:50 to 11:20 and dance on Tues
days and Thursdays at the same
time. Girls 12 to 1G will swim and
dance on the same days from 11:20
to 12.
The fee is $5 and registration will
be held at the Women's Gym this
Friday from 10 to 12 noon. Reserva
tions may be made now but will not
be held after Saturday unless paid
for.
