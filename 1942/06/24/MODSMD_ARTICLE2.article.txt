# DAILY MEN MEET LAW TURNER
## 
### 

FORMER EDITORS of The Stanford Daily, Charles Bull, '42 (left) and
Hal Shafer, '42 (right) point to a recruiting poster for the Navy Air
Corps with Cinemactress LANA TURNER in San Francisco, Miss
Turner received honorary "Wings of Gold" from the Navy for her
work in recruiting and selling war bonds. Shafer and Bull are repre
sentatives of Stanford's "Flying Indian" squadron.
