# Wartime Council Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
The first meeting of the new Con
servation Council, which is now do
ing the work formerly done by the
Consumer and Salvage committees,
will be held at 5 o'clock tomorrow
afternoon.
Actually, three committees arc
combined to form the Council.
Combined will be the Salvation
Committee, headed by Bill Hutchin
son, the Consumer Education Com
mittee, under Beverley Duncan, and
a Conservation Committee, headed
by Pete Paget.
The Council's work will be to
convert student life from a peace
time to wartime economy, and to
show how waste material can and
must be eliminated.
