# Dean Yost to Address New Women at 'Y' Tea
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will speak to all
new women students this afternoon
at 4 in Lagunita's Olivo courtyard,
when the Y.W.C.A. will give an in
formal "get-acquainted" tea for the
new girls.
Quad clothes will be worn, accord
ing to Betsy Wilson, "Y" chairman,
and the affair will last from 3:30 to
4:30.
He who takes care of his car these
days also takes care of his country.
