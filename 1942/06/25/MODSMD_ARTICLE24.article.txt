# Running to Present Organ Recital, 4:30
## 
### 
Joseph M. Running, assistant or
ganist, will present an organ re
cital in the Memorial Church today
at 4:15 p.m. The program will be
given again Sunday evening, June
28, at 7:30.
The recital will open with three
Bach, compositions, including the
well known "Prelude in E Minor."'
Two compositions by Karg-Elert and
a selection of Boellmann's will com
plete the program.
