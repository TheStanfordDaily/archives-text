# Gym Requirement Must Be Fulfilled
## 
### 
According to the School of Health
the reports indicate that not all un
dergraduate students have enrolled
in physical education classes in ac
cordance with the new University
regulation. All undergraduate men
and women students, except last
quarter seniors and Upper Division
students over 25 years of age, are re
quired to take physical education.
Men students who have not filled
out enrollment cards for physical
education should report immediately
to the office of the Men's Physical
Education Division; women students
should report to the Women's Gym
nasium.
