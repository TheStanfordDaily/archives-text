# American Dances Presented Tonight
## 
### 
Characteristic dances of the south
ern Americas occupy the stage of
the Little Theater tonight at 8. Maria
Soledad Rael of Stanford, Carlos As
ensio of Guatemala, and Danile Luk
sic of Chile, all students of the Uni
versity, will share the program with
Annabell Hall of Madera, who has
studied the dances of Mexico for
several years.
