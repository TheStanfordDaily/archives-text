# Lagunita Summer Formal Date Set
## 
### 
July 25 was set as the date for
Lagunita's Summer Formal in the
Court's Ex Committee meeting last
night. According to Lucille Terrell,
president, plans for the dance, which
will be held in the Women's Gym,
are as yet indefinite, but the com
mittee hopes to have dancing out
doors in the patio as well as inside.
Lagunita and the W.A.A. will
sponsor intramural volleyball games,
beginning Thursday, July 2. Each
casa will have a team, as will the
women's P.E. faculty.
A Sunday tea for parents and
friends of Lagunita girls will be held
sometime later in the quarter.
