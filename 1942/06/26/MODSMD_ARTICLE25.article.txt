# Net Tournament To Be Held July 6
## 
### 
Stanford's summer tennis tourna
ment will be held July 6. The tour
nament, which is an annual affair,
will be open to all men, graduates
and undergraduates, who have not
won a varsity letter or who did not
win their freshman numerals last
spring.
The list is posted now, and sign
ups close July 3. According to El
wyn Bugge, who is in charge of the
tournament, this summer's sign-up
should break all records.
The oldest American Greek letter
fraternity, Phi Beta Kappa, was or
ganized in 1775.
