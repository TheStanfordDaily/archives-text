# String Quartet To Open Series
## 
### 
Next week's Tuesday Series pro
gram will feature The London
String Quartet in the first of a se
ries of five concerts planned for
them for the summer.
The quartet also plans concerts
for Thursday evenings, July 2, 9, 16,
and Tuesday evening, July 21. The
concerts are being sponsored by the
Elizabeth Sprague Coolidge Founda
tion in the Library of Congress,
Washington, D.C., and by the Friends
of Music at Stanford.
Warwick Evans, violoncello, who
has appeared in concerts at Stanford
for the last two summers, is an orig
inal member of the quartet, which
played its first concert in London in
1908. Thomas Petre, second violin,
was also a member of the original
quartet.
First violinist John Pennington
and William Primrose, viola, both
studied music in London and joined
the group about 1928.
All concerts will be held in Memo
rial Hall, and will begin at 8 p.m.
No tickets will be necessary for any
of the performances.
