# Stanford Dames Plan Election of Officers
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will meeS
today at 2:30 p.m. to discuss plans
for the summer and to elect officers.
A short informal program will be
presented by Mrs. Lawrence G.
Thomas following the elections. Any
woman whose husband is taking
work at the University is eligible to
become a member and is cordially
invited to attend Tuesday's meeting.
