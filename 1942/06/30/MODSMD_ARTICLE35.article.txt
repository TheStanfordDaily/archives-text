# Library Makes Study Changes
## 
### 
In response to increased student
demand for adequate study facilities,
William Owens, chief of the circu
lation division of the library, today
announced the following tentative
changes in the library schedule:
1. Reserved Book Room will be
open Monday through Thursday
from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m., Friday from
8 a.m. to 6 p.m., Saturday from 8
a.m. to 5 p.m., and closed all day
Sunday.
2. Second Floor Reading Room
will be open, as usual, from 8 a.m. to
10 p.m. Monday through Friday,
Saturday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., and
closed all day Sunday.
This revised schedule went into
effect yesterday. If there is any
evidence at all of student re
sponse to the new hours, the library
will remain open, Mr. Owens said.
