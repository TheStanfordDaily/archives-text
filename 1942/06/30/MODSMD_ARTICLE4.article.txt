# BOMBERS OF TOKYO HONORED
## 
### 

(Acme Teleplioto)
BRIG. GEN. JIMMY DOOLITTLE. left, was awarded the Distinguished
Flying Cross, and 22 other members of the U.S. Army expedition
which bombed Tokyo were honored in ceremonies at Boiling Field,
Washington, D.C. Others in the picture, from left, are Col. Arthur I.
Funis; Gen. Henry 11. Arnold, commander of the Army Air Forces,
pinning a medal on Lt. Col. John A. Ililger: and Maj. Charles U.
Greening, pilot, of Tacoma, Wash.
