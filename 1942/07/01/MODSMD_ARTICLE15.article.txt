# Program Changes Due on Thursday
## 
### 
The last chance for students to
change their programs without fine
is tomorrow, Thursday, June 2. The
deadline is 5 p.m. according to Miss
Cook of the Administration Build
ing Information Bureau.

After this date changes can only
be secured through means of a pe
tition. There will also be a fine of
$2 for every course dropped and an
additional charge of $2 for every
new subject taken up.
