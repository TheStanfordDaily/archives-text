# Tennis Tourney Plans Announced
## 
### 
Sign-up sheets have been posted
in the Encina Gym for the annual
Stanford summer tennis tournament
with all non-lettermen or numeral
men eligible to compete.
Coach Elwyn Bugge. in charge of
the matches, announced that the
men's tennis courts are available for
general use only when there are no
classes. Classes are now scheduled
on all courts Monday Wednesday
and Friday from 4 to G. and on the
varsity courts only on Monday,
Wednesday and Friday from 11 to
12. Badminton courts are open in
the gym Tuesday and Thursday from
2 to 4 p.m.
