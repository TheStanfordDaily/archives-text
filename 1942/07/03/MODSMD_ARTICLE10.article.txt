# Vespers on Sunday Will Feature Trio
## 
### 
An open air evening vesper serv
ice will be held from 7:15 to 7:45
Sunday night in the Frost Memorial
Amphitheater, Charlotte Johnson,
summer chairman of Interfaith
Council, has announced. The Stan
ford Roger Williams Club is in
charge of arrangements.
The services, to which everyone is
invited, will be held under the oak
tree at the rim of the amphitheater,
according to Miss Johnson. The
Roger Williams Club Girls Trio wilt
lead in singing.
