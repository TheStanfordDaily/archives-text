# Stanford Dames Elects Officers
## 
### 
At the Tuesday meeting of the
Stanford Dames Club at the Wom
en's Clubhouse, Summer Quarter of
ficers for the organization were
elected.
Mrs. Robert Warren will be presi
dent; Mrs. Ned B. Johns, vice-presi
dent; Mis. Alfred Louchard, record
ing secretary; Mrs. Robert Glik
barg, corresponding secretary; Mrs.
Adrian Srb, treasurer; and Mrs. H.
Parley Kilburn will serve as histor
ian.
Musical entertainment for the
meeting was provided by original
recordings presented by Mrs. Lau
rence G. Thomas assisted by Miss
Joan Roarke at the piano.
