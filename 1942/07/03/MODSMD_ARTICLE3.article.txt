# CARRIER 'WASP' TURNS UP AT MALTA
## 
### 

THE PRESENCE OF TIIE "WASP," one of the Navy's newest aircraft carriers, at Malta has been officially
disclosed by the Navy. The big ship had recently been making ferrying trips to the British base on the island
with airplane reinforcements. No damage was suffered on any of the trips, according to the official an
nouncement. The Navy also named several other American vessels as being in the Mediterranean.
