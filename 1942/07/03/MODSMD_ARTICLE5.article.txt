# Latin Songs to Be Theme of Concert
## 
### 
A musical tour of the Americas
will be offered tonight at 8, in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house. Sponsored by the Drama
tists' Alliance and Sigma Delta Pi,
Spanish honor society, the concert
will open with a series of recordings
made in New Mexico by Dr. Juan
Rael. of the Spanish department, in
the summer of 1940.
Also included in the program will
be love songs from Puerto Pico,
Tangos from Argentina, and Sambas,
the new dance fad. from Brazil.
