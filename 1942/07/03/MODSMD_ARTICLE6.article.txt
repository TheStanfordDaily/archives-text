# Steiner to Speak On Japs' Traits
## 
### 
Dr. Jesse F. Steiner, acting pro
fessor of economics (sociology) will
speak on '"Basic Traits of Japanese
Character" Wednesday afternoon,
July 8. at 4:15 in the Lower Lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Steiner, executive head of the
Department of Sociology at the
University of Washington, taught at
North Japan College from 1905 to
1912. He was director of the Educa
tional Service of the American Red
Cross from 1918 to 1921, and was a
member of the research staff of the
President's Research Committee on
Social Trends.
