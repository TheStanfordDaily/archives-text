# Tartuffe Tryouts To Be Held Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for Tartuffe, French com
edy, will be held today from 3 to 5
p.m. in the Little Theater in Memo
rial Hall. Tartuffe, written by the

famous French playwright, Moliere.
will be presented August 12, 13, and
14 under the direction of Charley
Vance.
