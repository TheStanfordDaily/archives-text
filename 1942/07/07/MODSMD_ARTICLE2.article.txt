# Cocking to Speak At Education Club
## 
###

Security Agency, will speak on ''Pro
gram Planning" at the second lunch
eon meeting of the Education Club,
Thursday, at 12:15 in the garden
wing of the Union.

the summer series sponsored by the
Education Club. Tickets will be
available today only in the Education
Building from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.
