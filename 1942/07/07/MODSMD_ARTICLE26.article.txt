# Softballers Start League Play Today
## 
### 
Practice games completed, Stan
ford softballers start league play to
day with ten contests scheduled by
Director Harry Shipkey.
After the regular season is over,
winner and consolation flights will
be posted, and champions of each
will have their names inscribed on
the trophy given by the School of
Education and the School of Health.
Following are today's games,
named in order of diamond to be
played on, starting with No. 1 dia
mond at the top in each league.
At lower left-hand corner of this
page is a copy of the regulations
and rules of the Summer League.
