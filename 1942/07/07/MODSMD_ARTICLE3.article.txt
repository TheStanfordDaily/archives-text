# PEARL HARBOR VICTIM RESURRECTED
## 
### 

TIIE "SHAW," whose how was blown off during the Jap attack on Pearl Harbor, is again afloat and ready
for action. She was patched up with a temporary wooden fore section with which she limped from Hawaii
to a west coast port to undergo extensive overhauling. She is shown here going through her trial runs he
fore returning to active service.
