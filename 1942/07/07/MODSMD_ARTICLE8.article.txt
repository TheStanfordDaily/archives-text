# First Aid Classes Get Under Way
## 
### 
Men's and women's Red Cross
courses will begin this Friday when
the Senior Life Saving class holds its
first meeting at 4 in the women's
pool. This course is given once a
week from 4 to 6 p.m.
On Monday students enrolled in
the course in Motor Mechanics will
begin learning how to make auto
mobile repairs. The course is given
on Monday and on Wednesday from
7:30 to 9:30 p.m. at the Cardinal
Garage in Palo Alto.
The Standard First Aid course,
given Tuesdays and Thursday from
7:30 to 9:30 upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse, begins next Tuesday and
will be taught by Miss Ruth Young.
Enrollment in any of these courses
is still possible any day between 2
and 4 p.m. in Room 13 of the Old
Union.
