# Tuition Notes Must Be Filed July 20-25
## 
### 
It will be necessary for those de
siring tuition notes for the Autumn
Quarter to file them during the week
of July 20-25, inclusive, the dean of
men's office announced yesterday.
Men should file applications with
Dean John Bunn, the women with
Dean Mary Yost.
There are students from 34 states
registered at Stanford this summer,
according to the Bawlout.
