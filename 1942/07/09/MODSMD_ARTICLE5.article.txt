# String Quartet To Perform at 8
## 
### 
The London String Quartet will
present the third in the series of five
concerts tonight at 8 in the Memorial
Auditorium. The presentation will
be open to the public.
Led by William Primrose, consid
ered one of the foremost violists in
the country, the quartet includes
two violins, a viola, and a 'cello.
The program, topped by the famous
Quatuor. Opus 10 by Debussy, will
consist of three compositions. The
other works to be given are Beet
hoven's Quartet in C Major and the
Quartet in G by Haydn.
