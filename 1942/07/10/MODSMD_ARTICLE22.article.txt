# Bicycle Ad Receives 34 Calls in One Day
## 
### 
Bicycles are in great demand on
the campus, according to Hal Free
mon. who ought to know.
Tuesday, Freemon offered his
bike for sale in a Daily ad. Soon
after the paper came out Freemon
had 17 responses within 20 minutes.
At the end of the Hay, the number
of answers to the ad totalled 34.
