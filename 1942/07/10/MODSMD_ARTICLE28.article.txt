# TARZAN WHITE INDUCTED
## 
### 
ATLANTA, July 9.—Arthur "Tar
zan" White, member of the Ala
bama- football team which beat
Stanford in (he Rose Bowl in 1935,
was inducted into the Army today
at Fort McPherson.

conserve rubber.
