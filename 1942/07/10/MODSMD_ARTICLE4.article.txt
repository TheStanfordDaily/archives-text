# ARMY ESCORTS SABOTEURS TO TRIAL
## 
### 

(Acme Telephoto)
GUARDED BY SOLDIERS and led by an armored car, these closed auto vans containing; eight alleged
Nazi saboteurs are headed for the Justice Building in Washington, D.C., where the men went on trial
before a military court. The men, accused of having been landed from an enemy submarine with intent to
sabotage, face the death sentence if found guilty.
