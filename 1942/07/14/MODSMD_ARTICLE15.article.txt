# Sharp on Vacation
## 
### 
Stanley Sharp, assistant clean of
men and director of men's resi
dences, left yesterday for a three
week vacation in and around Yel
lowstone National Park. Routine
work will be carried on by Mr.
Sharp's office force, both at Encina
and the Administration Building.
"I'm not going to hunt, and I'm
not going to fish. I'm just going to
relax," commented Mr. Sharp. In
past summers he has lead bicycle
tours of European countries.
