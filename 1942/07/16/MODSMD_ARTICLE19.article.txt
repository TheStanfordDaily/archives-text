# In the Service
## 
### 
From Army public relations offices
comes news of the promotion of two
Stanford men. and of the commis
sioning of another.
Bain Alexander. '39, who enlisted
in the army as a private April 21,
1942, has been promoted to the rank
of sergeant. He is now stationed at
the Santa Ana Air Base in the finan
cial detachment.
Lorrill A. Palm, '33. an army trans

port pilot, has been promoted to the
rank of Captain, and is now stationed
at the Wellston Air Depot, Wellston,
Georgia.
Lt. Charles M. Parker. '38. re
ceived his navigator's wings last
week at Kelly Field, Texas, and has
been assigned to active duty with the
United State Army Air Force.
