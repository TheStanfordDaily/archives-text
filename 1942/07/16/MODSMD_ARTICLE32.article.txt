# Stanford Damps Plan Bridge, Swim Friday
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames have two at
tractions for Friday, July 17, ac
cording to Mrs. David Gafs, chair
man of the bridge group, who has
announced that all who are inter
ested are invited to the meeting at
the Women's Faculty Club at 7:30
p.m.

All the Dames and wives of grad
uate students are invited to a swim
ming party at the Women's Gym
from 1 to 3 p.m., she said. There
will be an admission fee of 10 cents.
Suits and towels will be provided.
