# Dramatics Alliance Will Present Chile Speaker
## 
### 
Miss Barbara Irgens of the Univer
sity of California, now in government
service, will discuss the '"Representa
tive Dramatists of Chile" Monday
evening at 8 o'clock in the lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse as the fifth
of the summer series of lectures on
the literature of the Southern Amer
icas presented under the auspices of
Dramatists' Alliance. The public is
invited to attend.
