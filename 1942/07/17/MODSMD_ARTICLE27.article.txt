# Lagunita to Give Sunday Services
## 
### 
Sunday has been designated as La
gunita Day, as the Memorial Church
will present its second in a series of
student sponsored services, during
the Summer Quarter.
Christine Moody will represent
Lagunita by giving a short talk, to be
followed by Dr. D. Elton Trueblood,
University chaplain, who will de
liver a brief sermon. Irma Cunha
and Geraldine Hughes will read the
scripture texts.
Contralto Jean Seward, of the
University Choir, will sing "The Lord
Is My Shepherd" in a solo.
The chapel cabinet has instigated
the series of Sunday services spon
sored by students in an attempt to
increase youth's interest in the
chapel.
