# Lisle Selby Stars In Pasadena Play
## 
### 
Lisle Selby, who is most mem
orable to Stanford playgoers for his
lead in Marco Polo last season,
opened in a new play last night at
the Pasadena Playhouse.
Selby graduated from the Univer
sity of California, but studied at
Stanford as a graduate student. While
on the Farm he appeared in "The
Far Off Hills" and ''Harvest Time."
He has been working at the Play
house since spring.
