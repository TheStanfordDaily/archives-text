# Bugge, U.S.F. Star, Joins Coast Guard
## 
### 
Jens Bugge, assistant frosh grid
coach at Stanford in 1940, enlisted
in the Coast Guard recently.
Bugge was a star end on the Uni
versity of San Francisco football
team. His father was once command
ant of West Point and served on
General Pershing's staff during the
World War. Bugge is a product of
the Russ Letlow-Al Braga era at
U.S.F.
