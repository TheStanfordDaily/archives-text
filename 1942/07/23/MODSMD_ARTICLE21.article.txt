# Running Will Present Organ Recital Today
## 
### 
Joseph M. Running, assistant Uni
versity organist, will present the
regular Thursday afternoon organ
recital in the Memorial Church to
day at 4:15.
The program will include the
Sonata No. 2, in C minor, by Men
delssohn, "Thou Art So Like a
Flower" by Schumann. Scherzo by
James Rogers, Chorale in E major by
Cesar Frank, and March from "Die
Meistersinger" by Richard Wagner.
