# CASAS TAKE CHARGE
## 
### 
Casa presidents at Lagunita Hall
announced last night plans for in
dividual casas to be in charge of the
Y for two-day periods. Under the
new program the girls in the various
casas will sign up to work at the Y
on their casa's day. This step has
been taken to speed up war work on
campus.
