# Schroeder, Wood Sweep Invitational
## 
### 
Stanford's Ted Schroeder and
partner Sidney B. Wood swept to
victory in the Seabright Invita
tional round-robin tourney Sun
day with an unblemished record of
six victories against formidable
competition.
Schroeder and Wood climaxed
their perfect record by whipping
E. Victor Seixas and Billy Talbert,
6-3. 6-4. 6-3. They had al
ready virtually clinched the title
by their earlier matches. Seixas
and Talbert finished second with
four victories and two defeats.
