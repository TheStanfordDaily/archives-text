# Folk Dance Cash Voted by Excom
## 
### 
At a practically memberless meet
ing Excom finally met last night and
conducted the following business:
Rally Committee was voted $25 for
its folk dance Aug. 7, and a motion
to engage a towel attendant for the
women's swimming pool on Satur
day afternoons was carried.
The following dates were granted:
Casa Adelfa, USO party Aug. 14
(change from the 7th), Rally Com
mittee folk dance Aug. 7 instead of
Aug. 8; Casa Ventura, USO party
Aug. 7; Toyon, Aug. 15; and Cardi
nals' play day and dance Aug. 15.
