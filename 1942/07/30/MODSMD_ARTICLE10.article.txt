# LAGUNITA ENTERTAINS
## 
### 
Lagunita is sponsoring a tea for
off-campus women this afternoon
from 3 to 5 in Lagunita Court. The
purpose of the tea is to help off-cam
pus women become better acquainted
with students living in Lagunita.
General chairman for the affair is
Miss Sue Stevens. Members of La
gunita Ex Committee are acting as
hostesses. All women students and
faculty members are invited.
