# Running Presents Afternoon Recital
## 
### 
Joseph M. Running, assistant uni
versity organist, will present the
regular Thursday afternoon organ
recital at 4:15 today in the Memo
rial Church. The program will in
clude Allegro Vivace, from the
"Water Music" and Organ Concerto
in F Major by G. F. Handel, Chorale.
"O Sacred Head Now Wounded" by
J. S. Bach, Socur Monique by F.
Couper. Shining Shore by E. S.
Barnes, and Kyrie Elison, from
'Cathedral Windows" by Karg-
Elert.
