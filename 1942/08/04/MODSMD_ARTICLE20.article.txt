# Macfarlane to Speak On Research Trends
## 
### 
"Research Trends in the Field of
Personality" will be the subject of a
lecture to be given by Mrs. Jean W.
Macfarlane, Wednesday afternoon,
August 5, at 5:45 in the lower lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. Macfarlane is now acting pro
fessor of psychology at Stanford. She
received her A.B. and Ph.D. degrees
at the University of California.
