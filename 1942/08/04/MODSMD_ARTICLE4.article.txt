# Music Continues Through August 9
## 
### 
The Nineteenth Annual Festival of
the International Society for Con
temporary Music, which began last
Saturday, will continue through
August 9 on the University of Cali
fornia campus.
Presented under the auspices of
the Committee on Music and Drama,
the festival committee includes Al
fred Frankenstein, lecturer on music
at Stanford and columnist for the
San Francisco Chronicle.
The Janssen Symphony Orchestra
of Los Angeles. Werner Janssen,
conductor, will appear in two mati
nee concerts Saturday and Sunday
at 3:30.
