# 'WAVE' DIRECTOR SWORN INTO NAVY
## 
### 

(Acme Tele photo)
AFTER THIS SWEARING-IN ceremony, Dr. Mildred H. McAfee, right, president of Wellesley College, was
in the Navy as a lieutenant-commander in the new Women's Naval Reserve Corps. Secretary of the Navy
Frank Knox, center, is shown here administering the oath while Admiral Ernest J. King looks on.
