# Funeral Services Held for Hedberg
## 
### 
Funeral services were held yester
day afternoon for John Hedberg, 35,
associate professor in the civil en
gineering department, who died at
his home Monday evening. The
service, arranged by the Tinney Fu
neral Home, was conducted by VVil
lard B. Thorp, former pastor of the
Congregational Church.
Dr. Hedberg was appointed an in
structor at Stanford in 1930. For the
past year he has had the position of
associate professor and head of the
Stanford hydraulic laboratory. He
received his Ph.D. degree at Stanford
in 1934. Before corning to Stanford
he taught at Cornell and Purdue.
