# GO ON HUNGER STRIKES
## 
### 


PROTESTING GOVERNMENT interference with their freedom, these
two imprisoned men have gone on hunger strikes. Herbert F. Oleson.
left, a conscientious objector, refuses to eat in the San Francisco County
Jail, while publisher C. Leon De Aryan, right, candidate for Congress,
weighs himself at the San Diego County jail where he's facing
charges of sedition. De Aryan refused to eat after he failed to obtain
release on his own recognizance.
