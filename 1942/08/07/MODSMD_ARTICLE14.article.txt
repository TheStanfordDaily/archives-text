# Interfaith Supper To Be Held Sunday
## 
### 
The Interfaith Counsel will hold its
summer meeting on Sunday in the
garden of Mr. and Mrs. Charles
Moser, 441 Foothill Road. The eve
ning will begin at 6 with a picnic
supper furnished by the different
groups. Miss Pat Davidson of the
Y.W.C.A. will speak at. -7.
All members of Interfaith Counsel
and any others interested are invited.
