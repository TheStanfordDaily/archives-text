# Deadline on WAVE Applications Near
## 
### 
With the deadline set for August
20, Captain C. L. Arnold, director of
Naval Officer Procurement, urged
that all requests for preliminary ap
plication forms for the Women's
Reserve be mailed immediately. The
forms must be filled out and in the
hands of the Office of Naval Officer
Procurement, 73 Market Street, San
Francisco, by August 20.
In the letter requesting the appli
cation should be: Name in full; ad
dress: age; marital status; ages of
children, if any: educational back
ground; business and professional
experience. Applicants will not be
interviewed at the office until they
are formally notified to report for
interview.
