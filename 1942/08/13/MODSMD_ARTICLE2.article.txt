# Toyon Dance Open To Student Body
## 
### 
Maestro Bill Arsenio will give the
downbeat for the Toyon sports dance
t 9 tomorrow evening. According to
Rene Zentner, dance chairman, the
dance is open to the entire student
body. It was originally a closed af
fair for Toyon residents. The dance
will continue until 1 a.m. in the
