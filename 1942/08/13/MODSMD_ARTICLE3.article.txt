# Regulations for Hall Men for Fall Issued
## 
### 
Application regulations for hall
men's living quarters for next fall
quarter were announced yester
day by the office of the assistant
dean of men.
All Lower Division men now liv
ing in Encina Hall must make a
$10 deposit to reserve a room for
next quarter. This deposit will be
payable August 20 in the office of
the director of men's residence.
The east wing of Encina's fifth
floor will he set aside for grad
uates. Reservations for these rooms
can he made at any time between
now and the end of the quarter.
There are still rooms left in
Toyon and Branner Halls for those
upper division men who have not
as yet made reservations.
