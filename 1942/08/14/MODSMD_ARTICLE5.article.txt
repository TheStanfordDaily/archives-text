# Hoover Libe Tours To Be Held Friday
## 
### 
In co-operation with a request
from A.S.S.U. President Ed Stamm,
Dr. Ralph Lutz, director of the Hoo
ver War Library, announced yester
day that student tours to the Tower
will be conducted next Friday after
noon from 1-5.
Open only to students registered
here this quarter, tickets are neces
sary for the tour, although free, and
will be available at a booth in the
Cellar next Thursday from 1-4, and
on Friday morning at the English
Corner, from 8-12.
