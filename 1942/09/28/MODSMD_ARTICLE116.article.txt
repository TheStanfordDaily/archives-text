# Eastman Wins Badminton Title
## 
### 
By defeating Frank Gibson 15-5,
15-9, Clint Eastman annexed the
campus badminton title this Sum
mer Quarter in the men's singles di
vision.
Eastman advanced to the final
round after easily defeating John
Waterhouse 15-2, 15-2. However,
the most exciting match of the tour
ney was the semi-final tussle be
tween Gibson and Bud O'Neil which
Gibson finally won 15-12, 16-19,
15-12.
Bob Vanina and Dick Pefiey won
the double final from Clint Eastman
and Rex Rathbun.
