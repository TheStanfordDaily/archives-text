# Shepherd Called To Active Duly
## 
### 
Charles E. Shepherd, director of
the Men's Health Service and pro
fessor of hygiene at Stanford, has
received a commission as major in
the United States Health Service.
He has been called into active
duty and is assigned to the Ninth
Corps area office in San Francisco.
Dr. Shepherd has been an exam
ining physician for the selective
service and has been medical di
rector of the emergency medical
service for the Palo Alto Emergency
Defense Council.
