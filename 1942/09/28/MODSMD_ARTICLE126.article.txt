# FIRST ALUMNUS
## 
### 
Newell Jefferson Cravath became
the first Trojan alumnus to hold the
post of head football coach at the
University of Southern California
when he took over the reins of the
War Horse dropped by Justin M.
(Sam) Berry when he joined the
U.S. Naval Reserves as Lt. Comdr. in
charge of the Athletic program at
St. Mary's Pre-Flight Training Base.
