# Exchange Fellows Offered to Grads
## 
### 
Stanford's Office of Pan American
Relations announces that applica
tions are now being received for ex
change fellowships with Latin Amer
ican countries for 1943.
Administered by the Department
of State, the one-year fellowships
are available to graduate students
or teachers in most fields of study.
Two students are chosen to go to
cach of the following republics:
Brazil, Chile, Mexico, Nicaragua,
Panama, Paraguay, Peru, Honduras,
and Columbia.
In the case of exchange fellowships,
the American government pays the
traveling expenses and the receiving
government in Latin America pro
vides tuition fees and living costs.
