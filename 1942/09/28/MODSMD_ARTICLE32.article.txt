# Guthrie to Head '42 Alumni Review
## 
### 
Frank Guthrie, class of '40, will
edit the Stanford Alumni Review
for the coming 1942 year. Guthrie
replaces Pete Allen, last year's edi
tor, who has taken a permanent job
as editor of the Permanente News.
While at Stanford Mr. Guthrie was
a member of the Stanford Daily edi
torial staff and sports editor for a
quarter. He was also a member of
the 1940 track team.
According to Guthrie, the Review
will continue the editorial policy,
started by Ken Smith, of depart
mentalizing the news.
