# Campus to Dance On Tuesday Night
## 
### 
Carl Livingston, chairman of Car
dinals, announces that this year's
Reg Dance will be held Tuesday
evening, Sept. 29, from 9 to 12 in
the women's gym.
All students are invited to attend
the dance, which is a regular fea
ture of the registration day.
The latest in recordings will be
presented and the price of admission
is 55 cents. Tickets may be bought
at the door.
Cardinals will offer prizes for the
winners in rhumba, fox trot and
waltz contests.
