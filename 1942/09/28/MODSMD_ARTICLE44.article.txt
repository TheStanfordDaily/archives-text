# SMITH NOMINATED
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur of
Stanford University has nominated
Walter A. Smith of Utica, N.Y., as
the official representative of Stanford
at the inauguration of Everett Need
ham Case as president of Colgate
University on Sept. 24.

Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur of
Stanford University has nominated
Dr. William F. Snow, '5)8, of New
York City, as official representative
of Stanford at the inauguration of
Dr. Harry Noble Wright as president
of City College on Sept. 30.
