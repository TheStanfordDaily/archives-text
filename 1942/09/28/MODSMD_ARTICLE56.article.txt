# Marston Assumes Fire Chief Duties
## 
### 
John M. Marston, Belmont fire
chief for 16 years, will be chief of
the Stanford fire department when
he assumes duties Oct. 1. He will be
in charge of a force with four paid
firemen and a number of student
volunteers and student employees.
Marston will replace Arden L.
Hatley, who assumed duties during
the Summer Quarter.
