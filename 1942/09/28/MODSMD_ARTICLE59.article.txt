# Free Industrial Training Offered
## 
### 
In an effort to meet the increasing
demands of industry for trained
workers, Stanford has announced a
fall schedule of free war production
training courses, to be given both in
San Francisco and at Stanford.
Classes scheduled for both Stan
ford and San Francisco include cost
accounting, industrial management,
personnel management, and precision
gauging and inspection. Advanced
industrial safety engineering will be
offered only at Stanford.
No tuition is charged for any of
these courses, according to Prof.
Eugene L. Grant, director of the pro
gram, and further information may
be obtained from the War Training
Office at Stanford.
