# Cady to Announce For Station KYA
## 
### 
Frank "Bud" Cady, director and
writer of Stanford's "Wealth of the
West" radio series, last week took
an announcing position with Station
KYA.
Earlier in the summer Cady gave
up the drama fellowship which was
awarded him by the Rockefeller
Foundation to take a post as pro
gram director of Station KGDM in
Stockton.
