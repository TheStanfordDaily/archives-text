# Stanford Flyer Killed in Action
## 
### 
Notice was received recently of
the death of Ensign Louis Love, '39,
killed in action in the Aleutians.
Before his death he had been cited
for heroism in action.
Love received his Naval Air Corps
commission last spring at Corpus
Christi, Texas, where he stood num
ber one in a class of 2500 cadets.
He worked his way through Stan
ford, where he was a member of the
Delta Tau Delta fraternity. Love
is the brother of Bob Love, '40, Delta
Tau Delta, and Betsy Love, '45,
Delta Gamma.
