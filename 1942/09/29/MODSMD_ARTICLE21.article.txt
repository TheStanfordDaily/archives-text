# Excom Will Meet Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
The main discussion of Excom
members tomorrow night will center
on the planned convention of college
war boards on the Stanford campus
sometime in November, ASSU vice
president Leslie Langnecker an
nounced yesterday.
Other topics to come before the
board will include Sunday Movies
and their budget, the Coop Book
plan, the Spring Show financial re
port of Pre-registration Chairman
Al Haas, and a general summary of
the summer quarter ASSU program.
As in the past, Excom meetings
are open to any interested Stanford
student in the Excom room of the
Student Union.
