# Mary Judson Gets WAAC Promotion
## 
### 
Mary Isabel Judson. of Palo Alto,
a Stanford graduate, was one of 117
women who were graduated to the
rank of third officer yesterday at the
third commencement ceremony of
the Women's Army Auxiliary Corps
at Ft. Des Moines, la. Yesterday's
graduating class increases the num
ber of officer graduates to 79 since the
training school started July 2.
