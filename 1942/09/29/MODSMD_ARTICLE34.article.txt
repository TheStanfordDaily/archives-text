# Journalists May Win Jacoby Award
## 
### 
Establishment of the Melville Ja
coby Fellowship, an award to be
made annually to a Stanford grad
uate in journalism who will special
ize in problems of the Orient, was
last week announced by University
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur.
The fellowship in memory of Ja
coby, Stanford '36. who was head of
the Time-Life bureau in the Far
East and was killed in an airplane
accident in Australia last April, is
being supported by funds given by
relatives and friends of Jacoby.
Jacoby's widow, the former Anna
lee Whit more, graduated from Stan
ford in 1937.
