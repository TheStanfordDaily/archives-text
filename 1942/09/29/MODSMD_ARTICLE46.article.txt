# Lt. William Gunther Dies in Plane Crash
## 
### 
Lieut. William R. Gunther, Jr., son
of Mr. and Mrs. W. R. Gunther of
Los Altos, was killed September 19
in the crash of an Army bomber in
San Francisco bay. The seven other
crew members were rescued. Lieut
enant Gunther, former Menlo Junior
College student only a few weeks
previously had received his com
mission.
