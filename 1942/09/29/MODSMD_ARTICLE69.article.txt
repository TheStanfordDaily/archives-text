# Grid Manager Positions Open
## 
### 
Positions are open for freshman
and sophomore managers for the
football squad, Tom Carvey, senior
manager, announced yesterday.
As inducement Carvey said, "Any
freshman who is interested in free
trips and free admissions to Bay
Area games plus gym credit and
manager's sweater is invited to see
me at football practice or at Room
119, Toyon."
With wartime conditions speeding
the turnover of students) frosh man
agers have a greater chance for rapid
rise, Carvey stated.
