# Stuart Takes Post On Federal Hoard
## 
### 
Dr. Graham Stuart, professor of
political science, left the campus last
week to take a position with the
Board of Economic Warfare in Wash
ington, D.C.
Dr. Stuart will hold the position
during Fall Quarter, but is expected
to return to his teaching duties here
Winter Quarter, although nothing is
definite, according to the Political
Science office.
