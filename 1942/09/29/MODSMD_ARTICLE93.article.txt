# Frosh Pick Tomato Crop For Army
## 
### 
While returning students register
today, a group of freshmen and new
lower division students and 10 Roble
sponsors will pick tomatoes as a
part of the "Victory Food Campaign"
to harvest crops for the fighting
forces.
The tomatoes, grown in Mountain
View, are to be canned and all will
be sent to the army.
Nine other Stanford students,
hired through Miss Helen Vincent
of the United States Employment
Service, worked from 8 to 5:30 help
ing to harvest the tomato crop yes
terday.
The students were Lucy Terrell,
Janet McClanahan, Peggy Shaw,
Jane Donald, Miriam King, Mary
Ann Goldman, Caroline Tyson, and
Barbara Sadler.
