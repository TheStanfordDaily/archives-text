# Stanford to Give Remain for Sale
## 
### 
Red Cross courses in first aid, sen
ior lifesaving, motor mechanics, nu
trition, and stall assistance will be
offered this quarter here in 20-hour
sessions.
In the motor mechanics course, a
prerequisite for the Red Cross motor
corps, both men and women will be
taught how to diagnose engine
trouble. War board work room vol
unteers will make stretcher pads,
roll bandages, and knit.
