# Stanford Alumni Choose New Head
## 
### 
Newly-elccted president of the
Stanford Alumni Association for
1942-43 is Floyd McElroy of San
Francisco, replacing Reginald E.
Caughey of Los Angeles.
Mr. McElroy, vice-president of the
Looniis, Sayles and Co., investment
counsels in San Francisco, graduated
from Stanford in 1921 and was pres
ident of the student body during his
senior year. Other officers chosen
were Everett R. Smith of Los An
geles, vice-president; and Mrs. Wil
liam E. Rideout of Piedmont, sec
ond vice-president.
