# Tryouts Planned For First Play
## 
### 
Tryouts for parts in "The Eve of
r.t. Mark," by Maxwell Anderson,
to lie first drama department produc
tion of the year, will be conducted
Thursday and Friday at 7:30 p.m.,
iu the Little Theater.
Parts for five women and ten men
are written into the play, which will
open November 4, according to F. C.
Strickland, director of dramatics.
Tryouts are open to all University
students except freshmen women in
their first quarter of residence. No
preparation is necessary, and no
copies of the play are available.
A drafted soldier who serves on
Bataan is the subject of the play,
Anderson's latest, which will be
premiered in New York at about the
time of its opening here.
