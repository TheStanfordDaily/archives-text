# More Enlistments Wanted by WAVES
## 
### 
The United States Navy called yes
terday for more women over 20 to
serve in the enlisted ranks of the
WAVES, women's naval reserve,
with salaries in ratings now open
ranging from $160.50 to $220.00
monthly plus subsistence.
After special training at the Uni
versity of Indiana, the University of
Wisconsin, or Oklahoma A. and M.,
WAVES will be assigned to active
duty as storekeepers, radio operators,
and in secretarial and clerical work.
