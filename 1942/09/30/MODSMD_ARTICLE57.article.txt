# BULL SESSION
## TO ALL NEW GRADUATE STUDENTS AND PROFESSORS: 
### H. D. THOREAU 
WANT you to feel at home on our Farm. Don't
feel badly because we didn't meet you at the train,
and bring you out to the campus in a convertible
with three blonds and six tires. Don't fret because
A1 Haas didn't have time to scrape up a little jolly-up
for you. Don't cry because you haven't received in
structions on how to tie your shoes and introduce your
wife to a friend.
It isn't because we aren't glad to have you, that
these little niceties haven't been performed. It's just
because the "incoming lower division students" are
real young and real dumb.
