# Boy Scouts Honor Mrs. Louis Stern
## 
### 
Tribute to Mrs. Louis Stern, god
mother to the Boy Scouts of the
Stanford Area Council, was paid by
George Harding, president of the
Council, Saturday night when she
and Oscar Alverson, deputy regional
director, were guests of honor at a
supper party at Scout headquarters
in the Community Center.
Motion pictures of activity at the
camp were shown to leaders and
workers at Camp 01-Ja-To. Mrs.
Stern and her daughter, Ruth Lucy,
made the camp possible.
