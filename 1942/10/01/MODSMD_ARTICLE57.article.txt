DAILY STAFF of Volume 102 will
hold its first meeting next Sunday
at 11 o'clock. All members of
the editorial staff must be present.
ALL WOMEN STUDENTS who
worked during this past summer
at any kind of physical labor in
defense plants, shipyards, or on
farms are asked to attend a brief
meeting tonight in the lobby of
the Women's Gym. Mrs. Margaret
Barr, assistant professor of physi
cal education for women, is mak
ing this request in view of the
current program for reorganizing
physical education in secondary
schools to meet the demands of
the war effort.
