WE'VE OHMen
— and we invite you to celebrate
the opening of our new, larger Encina Shop
the old hole in the wall the Encina Shop in Encina Hall — has grown bigger
and better this quarter . . . gas and tires have grown worse and worse! So why not
take advantage of this handy shop right on the campus that has all the fixin's of our
University Avenue store. If you're in need of a clean shirt or a new pair of slacks,
just drop around. Every day is open house, but we're holding a special powow to
show you the works (Free Smokes). How's about joining us?
