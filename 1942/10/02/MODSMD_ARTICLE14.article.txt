# Roos Bros. to Open New Encina Store
## 
### 
Today in Room 114 a new Encina
Roos Bros, shop opens for business.
The campus agent is Chuck Taylor,
and he announces a new line of
sportswear, slacks, shirts and other
haberdashery.
There will be an open house this
afternoon at which free cigarettes
will be given away and a jacket will
be given to the holder of the lucky
number. The drawing will be held
in the afternoon.
