WASHINGTON, Oct. 1 (UP).
The Office of Price Administration
tonight formally took the initial step
toward rationing meat by ordering
an overall cut of approximately 20
per cent in the beef, pork, mutton
and lamb which may be distributed
to civilians during the coming three
months.
