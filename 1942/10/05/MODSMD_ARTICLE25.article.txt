# Peru Not Invading Ecuador—Report
## 
### 
QUITO, Ecuador, Oct. 4 (UP). —
The Foreign Office today officially
denied press reports that Peruvian
troops have made incursions at
Hualtaco, Ecuadorean river port.
The newspaper El Comercio pub
lished a report from a correspondent
in Loja saying that Peruvian troops
had occupied the Ecuadorean border
village of Milagros and have con
centrated troops at La Tina. Peru
vian troops are digging trenches a
short distance from the Macaru
River, the report said.
