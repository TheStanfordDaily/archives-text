# EASTERN AIR CRASH
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 4 (UP).—Head
quarters of the Eastern Defense
Command announced this afternoon
that an airplane crashed yesterday
between 5 and 6 p.m. at Botwood,
Newfoundland, killing 10 persons.
The announcement said that there
were 37 persons aboard the plane.
