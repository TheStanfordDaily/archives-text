# Bulletin Board
##

ECONOMICS MAJORS: The quali
fying examination in economics
for transfer students will be held
Wednesday, Oct. 7, at 2 p.m. in
Room 231.
RALLY COMMITTEE TRYOUTS
will be held from 1 to 4:30 p.m.
in the Nitery, Wednesday, Thurs
day, and Friday. Positions are
available for 4 junior men, one
sophomore woman, 12 freshman
men, and 8 frosh girls.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE will meet
today at 3 p.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse.
PAN-HELL will meet today at 5
p.m.
WORKERS FOR THE Y MEMBER
SHIP DRIVE are to meet today
at 5 p.m. in the Y room. Every
body is either to attend the meet
< ing, or if not, to contact Betty
May Hale at 8630.
STUDENTS INTERESTED in sign
ing up for positions on the Stan
ford War Board will be inter
viewed from 1 to 4 p.m. Tuesday
in the Nitery.
LOS ANGELES. Oct. 4 (UP).—
One man was dead, four were in
hospitals and 24 youths were in jail
today following an outbreak of gang
fighting over the week-end.
