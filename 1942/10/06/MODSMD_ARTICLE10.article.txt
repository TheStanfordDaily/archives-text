# Frosh Women Must Have Pictures Taken
## 
###

Division women must have their
pictures taken not later than to
morrow for the freshman picture
book, Persis Johnson, Quad edi
tor, announced last night.

tryoutccs for the Quad editorial
stalT tomorrow evening at 7:15 at
the Quad office.
