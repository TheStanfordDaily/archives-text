# SALVAGE PLANS MAPPED
## 
### 
PORTLAND, Ore., Oct. 5 (UP).—
A. H. Richards, industrial salvage
head for six Western states, mapped
plans tonight for the scrap cam
paign in Oregon which will see each
of the state's 2300 industrial con
cerns canvassed at least three times
in the next 90 days.
