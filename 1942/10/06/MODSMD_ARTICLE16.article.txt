# Physical Fitness Tests End Today
## 
### 
Stanford's wartime physical fitness
testing program ends today, Dr. O.
N. Anderson, director of the School
of Health, slated yesterday.
"A time will he scheduled later in
the week for make-up tests for those
who have been excused for various
reasons," Anderson stated. Those
without an excuse from the School
of Health must complete the tests
today, except for those exempted
from the new physical training pro
gram.
Seniors who have been excused
from the program are urged to take
the tests voluntarily to compare their
fitness with the other students,
sponsors stated.
