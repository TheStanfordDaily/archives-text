# SUICIDE INVESTIGATED
## 
### 
LAS VEGAS, Nev., Oct. 5 (UP).
—The death of C. J. Simonton, dep
uty internal revenue collector, who
apparently committed suicide after
going three days without food or
water while stranded on the desert,
was being investigated tonight by
federal authorities.
