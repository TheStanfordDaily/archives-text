# Crop Crew Organizes To Aid in Harvest
## 
### 
In a race against time, the
Stanford Crow Crew will meet
this afternoon to organize Farm
volunteers for tomato and prune
picking this weekend, as the crops
reach the end of their harvest
period.
According to Janet McClana
han, Co-ordinator of Women's
Crop Crew, captains of all living
groups will convene at 4 p.m. in
the Women's Clubhouse to allevi
ate the urgent demand for aid in
local farm harvest. Captains of
all living groups will handle Sign
ups for Saturday's and Sunday's
trips.
