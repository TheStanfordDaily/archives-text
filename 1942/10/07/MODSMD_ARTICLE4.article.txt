# BRAZIL SIGNS TREATY
## 
### 
RIO DE JANEIRO, Oct. 6 (U.R)—
Trade agreements were announced
today for the sale to the United
States of more than $127,000,000
worth of Brazilian coffee, cocoa and
Brazil nuts and a quantity of manu
factured rubber goods. The agree
ments were signed by U.S. Ambassa
dor Jefferson Caffery and Brazilian
Foreign Minister Oswaldo Oranha.
