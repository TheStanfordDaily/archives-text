# Engineers Needed In Alaska, Canada
## 
### 
Engineers, particularly civil en
gineers, who can stand the rigorous
outdoor climate of Alaska and Can
ada are needed by the U.S. En
gineers on vital construction work.
The amount of experience to'a great
extent determines the yearly pay
rate. Subsistence and quarters are
furnished if the engineers reside at
a construction camp.
Applicants who are not now em
ployed in war industries or not in
immediate line for draft should call
or see Mr. Everett Van Every, Re
cruiting Specialist, at the 12th U.S.
Civil Service Regional Office. Fed
eral Office Building in San Fran
cisco. Immediate appointments will
be offered to qualified men.
