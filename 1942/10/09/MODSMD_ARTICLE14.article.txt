# BONDS STILL TAX-FREE
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 8 (UP).—
The Treasury lost another round of
its fight against so-called special
tax privileges today when the Sen
ate tentatively agreed to continue
exempting from federal taxes the
income from both outstanding and
future issues of state and local
bonds.
