# Cardinals Outline Year's Activities
## 
### 
Cardinals met yesterday afternoon
in the student body offices, and, un
der the direction of Chairman Shel
ly Parker, outlined their activities
for the coming year.
Chairmen and dates for Cardinal
sponsored functions will be an
nounced later when more definite
plans have been made.
The committee is working out a
more definite schedule of duties for
which they are responsible, and will
submit them to Excom, of which they
are a sub-committee, next week.
