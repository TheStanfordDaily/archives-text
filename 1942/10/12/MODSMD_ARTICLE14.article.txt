# Stanford Dames Elect President
## 
### 
At the first meeting of the fall
season the Stanford University
Dames elected Mrs. William Mitch
ell president for the coming term.
Her staff will consist of Mrs. Archi
bald Mcliarg, vice-president; Mrs.
William Girvan, recording secretary;
Mrs. Wilbur Dutton, corresponding
secretary, and Mrs. Emmett Dur
rani, treasurer.
Mrs. E. L. Grant and Mrs. C. F.
Brand, who were added this year to
the list of the Dames sponsors, were
the guests of honor at the meeting.
Mrs. Robert Glikbarg controlled
the meeting as chairman until the
new president was elected, and Mrs.
Harry Tennyson directed the serving
of refreshments.
