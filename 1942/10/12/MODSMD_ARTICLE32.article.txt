# Graduates Needed For Civil Service
## 
### 
All university graduates under 55
years of age, who have completed 15
units of upper division or graduate
work in the social sciences, are need
ed for the position of Personnel Aid
by the Los Angeles County Civil
Service Commission. Additional
courses in the following subjects are
desirable: Tests and measurements;
statistics; personnel management,
public administration.
Persons answering these require
ments, and students who will grad
uate before March 1, 1943, should in
quire at Room 102, Hall of Records,
Los Angeles. Applications should be
filed on or before Oct. 19, 1942,
