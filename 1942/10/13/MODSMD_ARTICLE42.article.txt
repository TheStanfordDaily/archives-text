# WAAC Lieutenant To Explain Duties
## 
### 
Stanford women will have an op
portunity to hear about the Wom
en's Army Auxiliary Corps from
one of its officers when Lieut. Mary
E. McGlinn speaks today at 4:15 p.m.
in the auditorium of the Women's
Clubhouse.
In her talk, she will explain the
procedure to follow in applying for
the WAACs and the duties and re
sponsibilities of those who do be
come members of the service. Lieut.
McGlinn is the San Francisco rep
resentative of the corps.
Although she will do no recruit
ing on the campus, she will inter
view applicants at the Community
Center in Palo Alto today between
12:30 and 2:30 p.m., and 7 to 9 p.m.
There is a region in Patagonia,
South America, that is inhabited by
a race of Dinuberians, who average
10 feet in height and are covered by
red hair.
