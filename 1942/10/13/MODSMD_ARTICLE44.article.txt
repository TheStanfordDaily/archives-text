# Former Student Dies In Airplane Accident
## 
### 
A former Stanford student, James
Brennan, 23, a member of Breakers
Club, was killed Saturday in an
airplane crash at the Bishop airport.
Gaining 200 feet altitude after the
take-off from the field, Brennan's
plane nosed down in a spin. He was
an aviation instructor at Manzanar
airport.
