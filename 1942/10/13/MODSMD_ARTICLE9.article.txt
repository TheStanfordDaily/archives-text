# Engineers Hear Willis Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford's chapter of the Ameri
can Society of Engineers will hold
their initial meeting of 1942 this
coming Wednesday at 7:30 p.m., at
the home of Professor "Pop" Rey
nolds, 853 Middlefield Road, Palo
Alto.
All interested engineers are in
vited to hear Professor Emeritus
Bailey Willis deliver a talk on his
recent experiences in the Orient.
Professor Willis has made several
trips to China and the Philippines
during the past months in order to
study some of the engineering ex
amples found there. The purpose
of his speech will be to demonstrate
how the geologist is able to assist
the engineer.
Members and guests wishing
transportation to and from the meet
ing are requested to meet at the
engineering corner at 7:15 p.m.
