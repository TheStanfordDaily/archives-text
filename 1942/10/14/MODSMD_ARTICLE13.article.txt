# German Bodies Buried Unclaimed

WASHINGTON, Oct. 13 (UP).â€”
The six German spies executed here
Aug. 8 for their part in the abortive
mission to sabotage the American
war effort are buried in the potter's
field at Blue Plains, it was disclosed
today.
Unclaimed by friends or relatives,
the six bodies were buried about a
week after the execution in graves
marked by wooden slabs, inscribed
not by their names but by numbers
ranging from 276 to 281 inclusive.
