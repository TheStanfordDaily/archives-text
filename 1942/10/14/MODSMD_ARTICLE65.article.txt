# Munitions Strikers Vote to Return
## 
### 
EAST ALTON, 111., Oct. 13 (U.R)—
Striking AFL workers at the
sprawling Western Cartridge Co.
munitions works here voted over
whelmingly tonight to return to
work on the basis of War Labor
Board directive promising to place
a mediator in the plant to handle
all labor disputes.
AFL headquarters said 95 per
cent of the workers voted to end the
walkout at a mass meeting held at
an East Alton playground. The vote
ended a strike that had completely
halted production of vital war mate
rials.
