# Freshman Women To Hold Election
## 
### 
Elections for the Freshmen repre
sentative on Women's Conference
will be held in Roble today. The list,
announced during house meeting
Monday, consists of five girls selected
by the sponsors.
The candidates are: Betty Jean
Rucker, Spokane; Joan McDonough,
Beverly Hills; Romona Reid, Santa
Rosa; Jane Sontheimer, San Jose;
Charmain Kolar, Portland. The win
ner of the election will represent the
Lower Division women in the dis
cussions concerning all the living
groups on campus.
