# Honorary Sorority Chooses Officers
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, National Wom
en's Honorary Sorority, met yester
day to elect new officers for the
coming year, Pauline Deul was
elected president; Nyrel James,
vice-president; Margaret Kurtv,
secretary; Marjorie Hastings, treas
urer; and Luella Smith, keeper of
the records.
After the election, plans for the
rest of the quarter were discussed.
