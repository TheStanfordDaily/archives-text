# Farm Mothers To Hear Wilbur
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Mothers'
Club will hear three talks when
they meet Tuesday at 2 p.m. in the
Stanford Women's Clubhouse.
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur
will speak on "Stanford in the War
Effort." James Kessler will speak
on "Functions of Stanford's Wat-
Board," and Patricia Davison, head
of the Y.W.C.A., will discuss "Stan
ford Co-eds' Aid in the Present
Agricultural Emergency." Mrs. Sam
uel B. Morris will preside.
