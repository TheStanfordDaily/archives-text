# Stanford Mermen Battle St. Mary's
## 
### 
Stanford water polo fans will get
their first look at the highly touted
Farm mermen Saturday morning
when (he Indians battle the United
States Navy Pre-Flight School
swimmers from St. Mary's.
The preliminary between the two
reserve teams will begin at 10 a.m.
followed by the varsity contest at
10:50 a.m.

field appeared set today as the Web
foot squad drilled on pass defense
primarily, but Coach John Warren
continued his search for a suitable
replacement for the injured Roy
j Dyer at right halfback.
