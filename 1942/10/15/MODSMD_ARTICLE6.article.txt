# Tickell To Attend L.A. Convention
## 
### 
Professor Frederick G. Tickell.
head of the department of Mining
Engineering, will attend the Petrol
eum Section Convention of the
American Institute of Mining and
Metalurgical Engineers, in Los An
geles October 15 and 16.
Two former students of Professor
Tickell, Charles A. Arismendi (En
gineer "42) of the Standard Oil Co.
and Preston P. Nibley (Engineer
'42) of the Standard Oil Co., will
present at this convention the re
sults of a research carried on at
Stanford last year. The title of their
paper is, "Some Effects of Inter
facial Forces on Oil Flow in Water-
Wet Sand."
