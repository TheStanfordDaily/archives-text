# STOCKS TAKE DECLINE
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 15 (U.R)—The
stock market suffered its worst set
back in almost four months today on
selling largely ascribed to technical
factors and to disclosure that the
Japs were landing heavy reinforce
ments on Guadalcanal despite strong
American opposition.
