# Guard Finds Fire Bomb in Factory
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Oct. 18 (UP).-
A crude incendiary bomb today was
discovered in a Irailer factory en
gaged in war work but the bomb
was removed by a night watchmani
before a candle wick had burned
down far enough to ignite the oil
with which the container was filled,
police reported.
The apparent attempt at arson
was discovered by Archie McPhee
ters, a gas company guard, while
| making his rounds early today,
j McPheeters said he saw a man flee
ing from the vicinity of the Airfloat
I coach manufacturing plant, which
j adjoins the gas company's property
' in the central manufacturing dis
trict.
