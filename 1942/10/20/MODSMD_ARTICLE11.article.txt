# ATLANTIC SINKING
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 19 (UP).—
The Navy today announced the
sinking of a medium-sized United
States merchant vessel in the North
Atlantic in early July. The ship
was torpedoed and shelled by an
enemy submarine. Survivors have
been landed at an East Coast port.
