# Stanford Mothers To Hear Wilbur
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur
discussing "Stanford in the War Ef
fort" will be a guest speaker at the
first fall meeting of the Stanford
Mothers' Club to be held this after
noon at 2 o'clock in the Women's
Clubhouse.

James Kessler of Palo Alto will
explain the functions and activities
of the Stanford War Board and Miss
Patricia Davidson, Stanford "Y"
head, will tell how Stanford Co-eds
are helping in the present agricul
tural emergency.
