# Certificates Due For Vaccinations
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at
the Registrar's Office on Tuesday,
October 27, for all new students, in
cluding those having permits to at
tend classes, and all other students
whose certificates have expired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
o clock on Tuesday will result in a
fine of $2; or, if not filed by Nov. 3,
registration in the University will
be subject to cancellation by the
Committee on Public Health.
