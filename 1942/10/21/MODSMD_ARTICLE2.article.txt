# Tea Today Fetes Woman Trustee
## 
### 
Honoring the first woman on the
Board of Trustees since Mrs. Leland
Stanford, a tea will be given today
for Mrs. Roger Goodan in the Wom
en's Clubhouse from 4 to 6 p.m.
Some 80 campus women have been
invited to meet the new trustee.
Mrs. Goodan has come to North
ern California from her home in Los
Angeles to attend a meeting of the
University trustees. Her appoint
ment to the board was announced
this summer.
Quad clothes are in order for the
informal affair, according to Virginia
Stimson, who is in charge. Assist
ing her with arrangements are Mir
Brasher, Fran Bennett, and Helen
Knight.
