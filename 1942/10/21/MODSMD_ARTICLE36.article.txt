# Dean Morris Will Attend Conference
## 
### 
Dean Samuel B. Morris of Stan
ford's School of Engineering left for
Chicago yesterday to attend a meet
ing of the regional advisors of the
Engineering, Science, and Manage
ment War Training program of the
U.S. Office of Education. Dean Mor
ris will act as the regional advisor
for Northern California, Nevada,
and Utah on Oct. 25 and 26.
Stanford has trained 3500 persons
under this program of tuition-free
war courses. Dean Morris will also
stop in St. Louis for a conference of
the American Water Works Assn.,
of which he is vice president.
