### 
The freshman bowling team got
off to a flying start yesterday when
Rog Helstrom, George Beckwith,
Dean Funk, Alan Baldwin, and Dave
Bienam tried out for position on the
team at the Indian Bowl.
According to Manager "Late" Wil
liams, the boys averaged well over
160. He also announces that try
outs will be held today and tomor
row between' 4 and 5 p.m. and that
members of the team will be an
