# MATURE ENTERS GUARD
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 20 (UP).—
Victor Mature, filmdom's beautiful
hunk of man. today was disenrolled
from the Temporary Coast Guard
Reserve and enrolled into the Coast
Guard, settling his military status
for the duration of the war.
