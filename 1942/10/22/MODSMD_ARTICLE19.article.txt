# Sanitary Engineer Examination Open
## 
### 
Those interested in taking an ex
amination for a position as Assistant
Sanitary Engineer should apply to
the State Personnel Board in San
Francisco or Sacramento, before
Nov. 25. The beginning salary is
$230 a month.
The qualifications for applicants
include graduation from college with
major work in sanitary engineering
and at least two years' experience in
sanitary engineering.
