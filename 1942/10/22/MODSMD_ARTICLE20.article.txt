# Professor To Give Talk on Japanese
## 
### 
Dr. Paul Farnsworth, acting head
of the psychology department, will
give a talk on "The Psychology of
the Japanese" at the meeting of the
International Club to be held this
Sunday, Oct. 25, at the home of Mr.
and Mrs. E. J. Thomas, 365 Lincoln,
Palo Alto.
Transportation will be provided in
the form of cars which will leave the
Nitery at 3:15 Sunday afternoon.
Dancing will follow Dr. Farnsworth's
talk. Foreign and American students
are urged to come.
