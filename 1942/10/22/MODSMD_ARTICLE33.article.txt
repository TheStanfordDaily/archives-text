# Students Given Last Chance for Physical
## 
### 
All men students who have not
taken the physical fitness test will
be given their last chance to do so
without penalty tomorrow from 2
to 5:30 p.m. at Angell Field.
Those required to take the test
are all undergraduate men excep
ing seniors, men over 25, and
(hose who have been excused by
the Men's Health Service.
