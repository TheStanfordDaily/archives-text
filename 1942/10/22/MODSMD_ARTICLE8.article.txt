# Pompoms Required Of Women Hooters
## 
### 
Pompoms will he required
equipment for women rooters at
the Southern California game this
Saturday, according to Jim Mc-
Brier. Rally Committee chairman.
Since stunts are being planned, no
one will be admitted without a
pompom.
Priced at 25 cents, they will be
sold today and tomorrow from I
to 5 p.m., and Saturday from 9 to
12 a.m. in the A.S.S.U. office.
