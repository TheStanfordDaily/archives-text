# Museum Presents Talks on Theater
## 
### 
Two special lectures on the theater
and dance exhibition are offered at
the DeYoung Museum in San Fran
cisco this weekend.
Dr. George Altman will lead a dis
cussion on setting for the theater,
illustrating types of sets used in
contemporary as well as historical
productions, on Saturday at 3 p.m.
Edward Counter will speak on "The
Mystery of the Mask." with authen
tic examples of Oriental and In
dian masks, Sunday at 3 p.m. Both
meetings are open to the public
without charge.
