# Cards Face Funke Fencers Tonight
## 
### 
Hunting their second victory of
the 1042 season, Stanford's fencers
will battle the Funke Fencing Acad
emy of San Francisco at 8:30 tonight
in Encina Gym.
Although not sure of the number
of men the opposition will be able
to use tonight, Coach Elwyn Bugge
announced the following five In
dians ready to go: Harvard Lomax,
John Carr, Bill Perkins, Jack Rees
and Alan Hedden.
Admission is free.
