# Parents Honored At Encina Sunday
## 
### 
Parents of the new men students
were honored at a reception in the
newly re-rdecorated Club Room at
Encina Hall yesterday evening.
Refreshments were served to the
guests, and they were shown
through the Hall by the students.
The remodelled roms on the upper
floors were of particular interest to
the parents.
The guests, who came from many
of the western states, were intro
duced to Chancellor and Mrs. Ray
Lyman Wilbur, Dean and Mrs. John
W. Bunn, Mr. S. L. Sharp, director
of Encina, and Ed Stamm, president
of the student body.
