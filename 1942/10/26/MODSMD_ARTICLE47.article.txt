# APPLE PICKING RECORD
## 
### 
HOOD RIVER. Ore.. Oct. 25 (U.R)
-Seven hundred tired Portland
apple pickers were on their way
home from Hood River orchards to
night after helping harvest more
apples than had even bcv*i picked in
one day in the history of the valley.
Tuition paid by students pays ap
proximately half the cost of the
services rendered by the University.
