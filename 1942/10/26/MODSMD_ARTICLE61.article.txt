# Gifts to Stanford Reach New High
## 
### 
At a Board of Trustees meeting
Saturday, Chancellor Ray Lyman
Wilbur announced that war-time
gilts to Stanford University during
the past year reached an all-time
high of $1,644,874. This includes the
Timothy Hopkins trust fund which
has just been fully recorded.
Chancellor Wilbur reported that
more than 3000 donors contributed
to the record total. The previous
high record of gifts was made dur
ing the 50th anniversary year of the
University in 1940-41 when a total
of $1,572,000 was received.
Of particular note this past year
was the large number of memorial
contributions.
