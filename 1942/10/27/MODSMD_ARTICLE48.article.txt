# West Point Cadet To Be Appointed
## 
### 
Congressman John Z. Anderson,
representative of the Eighth Con
gressional District, announced today
that he will make an appointment
for the year 1943 to the United
States Military Academy at West
Point.
Applications from young men in
his district must be filed with him
before Nov. 10. Selection of the ap
pointee is made by means of a civil
service elimination contest.
