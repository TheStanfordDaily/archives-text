# Card Kickers Meet Menlo J.C. Today
## 
### 
The Stanford Varsity Soccer
team will meet the Menlo J.C. team
in a regular conference game on
the home field at 4 p.m. today.
Coach Harry Maloney announced
yesterday that George Koffer and
Russ Bryan will act as co-captains,
and that men who did not play last
week will start this game. Maloney
wants as many freshmen as possible
to attend the game.
