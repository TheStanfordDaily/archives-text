# Toyon Club Plans Dance, Carnival
## 
### 
President Bill Level's of Toyon
Club announced plans last night
following a Toyon Cabinet meeting
for a combined dance and carnival
to be held in the Toyon Lobby Nov.
G, Friday night, from 8 to 11:30 p.m.
Levers will be assisted with ar
rangements for the evening by Rene
Zentner, hall dance chairman. Plans
include dancing, cards, game tables,
and refreshments. Other plans to
have feature singers and perform
ers will be revealed later. The affair
is open to Toyon Club members and
their guests.
