# Y.W.C.A. To Give Juke-Box Jolly-Up
## 
### 
"Walk your girl to the Y.W.C.A.
juke-box jolly-up!"
Beginning early to prepare for the
no car, no gas situation ahead, the
Y is sponsoring an informal dance
from 8:30 to 11:30 Friday night in
the Nitery.
The dance will also carry out the
Y transfer orientation program, and
all transfer students will be guests
of honor. New transfer women may
obtain blind dates by contacting
committee members.
Sally Burke is in charge of the
affair, and assisting her are: Neva
Haight., Barbara Gordon, Florence
Rollins, Don Wilson. Men sponsors
and representatives from the fra
ternities plan to attend the dance.
