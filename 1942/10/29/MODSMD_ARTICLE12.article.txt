# WESTERNERS TO LEAVE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 28 (UP).
—Four more Westerners have been
ordered to leave the coastal areas
of the United States as "dangerous
or potentially dangerous to mili
tary safety." the Wartime Civil Con
trol Administration announced to
night.
