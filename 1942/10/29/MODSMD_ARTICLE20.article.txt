# Tickets On Sale For 'Spook Prom'
## 
### 
There are still 58 days till Christ
mas, but there are only three days
left to buy tickets for the big all
campus "Spook Prom" on Saturday
night. Tickets for the dance are
75 cents a couple, and they may be
purchased at the A.S.S.U. office or
at the door.

The Cardinals are sponsoring the
dance to provide entertainment for
the students who do not go down
for the U.C.L.A. game. "If the In
dians are as good this Saturday as
they were last week, we should
really have something to celebrate,"
said Tom Ohliger, dance chairman.

The dance will be held in the
Women's Gym. The doors open at
9 and the dance will last till 1. It
will be an informal affair, and jeans,
barn dance costume ,or sports at
tire will be in order.
