# Vallejo Man Buys Big Oakland Hotel
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 28 (UP).—
Harry Handlery, Vallejo hotel man,
revealed today that he had pur
chased the Lake Merritt Hotel, Oak
land, for $200,000 and will take pos
session Nov. 1.
Handlery, attending a hotel con
vention here, also owns the Ca.sa
de Vallejo and Georgian Hotels,
Vallejo, and the Carquinez Hotel,
Richmond. He said he planned
"many improvements" for the Lake
Merritt Hotel.
