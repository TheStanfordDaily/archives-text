# Organist Will Play Classic Favorites
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present the regular
Thursday afternoon organ recital
today at 4:15 in the Memorial
Church.
The program will include Prelude
in B minor by J. S. Bach, Chorale in
E major by Cesar Franck, The Lon
donderry Air, an old Irish song.
Fisherman's Song, and Pantomime
from "L'Amour Brujo" by Manuel
de Falla, and Berceuse and Finale,
from "The Fire-Bird"' by Igor Stra
vinsky.
