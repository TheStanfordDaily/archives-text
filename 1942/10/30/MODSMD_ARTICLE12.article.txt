# El Tigre Arranges Halloween Dance
## 
### 
El Tigre Club members and their
friends will dance to the music of
records tomorrow night at the Club's
Halloween Costume and Masquerade
from 9 to 1 in the Toyon Lobby.
Arrangements for the dance have
been handled by Bob Vickery and
Rene Zentner, and include complete
decoration of the lobby in the Hal
loween theme.
