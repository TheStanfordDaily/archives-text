# Personnel Board Announces Exam
## 
### 
The State Personnel Board today
announced an examination for Food
Administrator of Institutions, en
trance salary $245 a month. The
position will be with the State De
partment of Institutions and will
include planning and developing the
food program for the various state
institutions.
The entrance requirements are
graduation from college with major
work in nutrition and dietetics and
wo years' experience with an in
stitution regularly serving 1500 meals
a day. Applications must be filed by
Nov. 15, and they may be obtained
from the State Personnel Board Of
fices in Sacramento, Los Angeles, or
Sun Francisco.
