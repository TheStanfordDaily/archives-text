# Car Registration Slip Problem Is Solved
## 
### 
Motor vehicle owners unable to
produce their registration cards will
not be denied gas coupon books
provided they can give a valid reason
for not possessing their cards, the
Department of Motor Vehicles an
nounced today.
According to Director James M.
Carter, an arrangement has been
worked out with the Office of Price
Administration whereby applicants,
in lieu of presenting their registra
tion cards, will be permitted to sign
certificates of non-possession, which
will be available at the schools when
applications for coupon books are
taken on November 9th.
