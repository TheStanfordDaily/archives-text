# CAR SALES BANNED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Nov. 1 (UP).
—The Office of Price Administration
tonight banned sales of 1942 model
Chevrolets, Fords and Plymouth
four-door hard-top automobiles to
civilians until Dec. 31. to permit the
Army and Navy to fill their re
quirements.

LOS ANGELES. Nov. 1 (UP).—
Painting of 8000 city street light
globes to comply with dimout regu
lations will be completed by the end
of the week, W. E. Lauer, street
lighting engineer, reported today.
