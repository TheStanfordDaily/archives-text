# Toyon Club Plans Dance, Carnival
## 
### 
Toyon Club will present a carnival
and dance this Friday evening in the
hall lobby from 8 p.m. to 11:30 p.m.
for club members and their guests.
Plans for the evening are being
handled by Bill Levers, hall presi
dent, and Rene Zentner, dance
chairman, and will include feature
singers and acts.
The hall will be decorated in car
nival fashion. In addition to square
dances and schottisches which will
be presented, card tables and game
tables will be open during the eve
ning.
