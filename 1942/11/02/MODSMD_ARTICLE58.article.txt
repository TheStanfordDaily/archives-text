# QUAKES SHAKE NATAL
## 
### 
DURBAN, South Africa, Nov. 1
(UP). —Two earthquakes, one last
ing for 20 seconds, shook the coastal
area of Natal as far as 100 miles
inland today. There was no dam
age reported. The first tremor was
at 7 a.m. and the second 54 minutes
later.
