# Pickers To Receive Pay Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomato and walnut pickers, who
worked Oct. IX, 24, 2K, may pick
up their pay tomorrow from 1
to 5 in Room 14 of Old Union. The
girls can collect their salary in |
either cash or defense stamps. If
some of the girls don't want to
collect their wages, they are asked
to tell the SWB what they want
done with it.
