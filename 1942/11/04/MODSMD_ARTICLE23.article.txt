# NOT A MAN LOST IN SINKING
## 
### 

NOT A MAN was lost when the United States destroyer O'Brien sank
recently after battling units of the Jap navy. This was the report
brought back to San Francisco by members of the crew, including
from left to right, Lieut. Comdr. Thomas Burrowes, commanding
officer; Chief YVatertender Albert Bobowiecz, and Coxwain Daniel
Burke.
