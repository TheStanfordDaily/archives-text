# Vocom To Hear Of Summer Jobs
## 
### 
Women's Vocom Summer Jobs
meeting will be held tonight from
7:15 to 8:30 upstairs in the Women's
Clubhouse.
The following girls will give five
minute talks on their summer jobs:
Jeanette Grant, Betty Mae Hale,
Virginia Kirkland, Pat Charlton,
Ann Wendling, Carol Whitmore,
Pauline Holcomb, and Isabel Faw
cett. Miss Pat Davison will act as
chairman.
Vocom urges all Stanford women
to come, especially those who held
summer jobs. A general discussion
will follow the eight speeches.
This meeting is the first of a series
to be presented by Women's Voca
tional Committee.
