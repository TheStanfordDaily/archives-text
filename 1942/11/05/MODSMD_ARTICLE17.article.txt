# Junior Executive Positions Filled
## 
### 
Helen Campbell, Betty Blystone,
Bob McClean, and Johnny Twitchell
were elected to fill the remaining
positions on the Junior Executive
Board at a class council meeting
yesterday afternoon, according to
Ferris Boothe, class president.
The election was a run-off to fill
the positions deadlocked in last
week's election. The Executive
Board will consist of Helen Sa
vory. Ham Howard, Campbell, and
Blystone, women's representatives;
Bruce Wylie. Lucian Rice, McClean.
and Twitchell, men's representa
tives: Janet McClanahan, class sec
retary-treasurer; and Boothe.
