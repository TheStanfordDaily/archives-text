# MAGNESIUM ORE FOUND
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 4 (UP).—
The Bureau of Mines today de
scribed several extensive magne
sium-bearing deposits in the Boul
der Dam area of Nevada and Cali
fornia as possible new sources of
raw material for the new magne
sium plant near Boulder Dam, Nev.
