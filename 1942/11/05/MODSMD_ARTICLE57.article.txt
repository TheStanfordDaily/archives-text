# NAVY PILOTS KILLED
## 
### 
CORPUS CHRISTI, Tex., Nov. 4
(UP). —The Navy announced to
night that the crash of a training
plane last night took the lives of
Lieut, (j.g.) Thomas Wesley Jen
kins, instructor, and Aviation Ca
det William J. McJunkin, a 1942
Notre Dame graduate.

One-half of the flour and feed
mills in Canada are located in the
province of Ontario.
