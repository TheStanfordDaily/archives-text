# Gaieties Director Names Chairman
## 
### 
Heads of the various technical
crews for the Gaieties have been an
nounced by Hall Levitt, technical
director for the show, as he stated
that there are still opportunities
available to work behind the scenes.
In charge of costume design is
Phyllis Hammond, while Paula Kan
ner has been chosen to head the
costume construction committee.
Mary Lou Linsley will take charge
of make-up, Joyce Henderson will
handle the lighting, and Skip Athey
will head the sound crew.
