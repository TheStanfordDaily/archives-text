# New Farm Reservists Must Report Today
## 
### 
All new men students, fresh
men and transfers, who enlisted
in any reserve program of the
Army, Navy, or Marines before
coming to the Stanford campus
this fall are requested to report
that fact before 4 p.m. today at the
office of the armed services repre
sentative, l)r. Karl Cowdrey, in
the Registrar's Office.
Details needed include name,
branch of service, serial number
and classification, place of enlist
ment and date of enlistment. The
University must have this infor
mation by tonight.
