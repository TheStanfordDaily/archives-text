# JAPS DON'T BELIEVE IT
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 8 (U.R)—
Tokyo radio, as heard here by United
Press, still doesn't believe it. A Jap
anese broadcast is steadfastly claim
ing:
"Attacking American forces have
been repulsed in North Africa.
"Americans have made no land
ings."
It ain't no joke—there ain't no
coke.
