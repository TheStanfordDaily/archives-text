and Friday afternoons at the Old
Union from 1 to 4:30.

mural foot'oail games.

Ski" film is being shown at 4:15
p.m. today in the Women's Gym.
Anyone interested may attend.

today at 5 p.m. in the Journalism
library. All members must at
tend.
