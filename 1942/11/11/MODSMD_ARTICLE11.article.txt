# Dorm Sale Continues In Branner Today
## 
### 
The sale of articles which have
accumulated in men's dorms prior
to two years ago will continue to
day from 12:30 to (i p.m. in the
Branner dining hall, according to
Dr. Stanley Sharp, director of
men's dormitories, who is han
dling the sale.
Over half of the 400 articles were
sold yesterday afternoon when the
sale continued for the second day.
A great many hed springs and mat
tresses in good condition are still
on hand, as are many rugs, lamps,
chairs, bookcases, trunks, suitcases,
books, and old radios.
Proceeds from the sale will go to
the student aid fund. The sale
will close today.
