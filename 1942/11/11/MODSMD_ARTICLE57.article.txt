# Rushing Bids Are Ready Today
## 
### 
All freshman women must pick
up their rushing tea invitations in
the Pan Hellenic office in the Wom
en's Clubhouse between 1 and 5 this
afternoon. They should be returned
to the Pan Hellenic office from 1 to
5 Thursday.
Junior transfers, whether regis
tered or not. have invitations to the
rushing teas and should pick them
up at the same time as the freshmen.
