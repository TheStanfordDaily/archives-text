# KAISER PLANS TO SET NEW MARK
## 
### 

THE ROBERT E. PEARY. 10,000-ton Liberty ship built by new pre-fabrication methods at the Henry J.
Kaiser shipyards at Richmond, is expected to be launched in less than a week, according to company offi
cials. This photo shows the vessel after less than two days' work had been done on it. This new mark
would hotter the record of 10 days set by Kaiser's Portland yards.
