# Religious Group To Hear Rathbun
## 
### 
Professor Harry Rathbun of the j
School of Law will be the speaker at
the Quarterly Conference of Inter
faith Council Nov. 15 at 8 p.m. at
the home of Professor J. H. Jackson,
445 El Escarpado.
Professor Rathbun will talk on the
subject "Whether Religion Has Any
thing Significant to Offer in These
Times."' Alter the talk. Professor
Rathbun will lead a discussion of
this topic.
The meeting, sponsored by Inter
faith Council, is open to all students
on campus. The members of the j
clubs which compose the council will ,
attend this meeting instead of their j
regular groups.
