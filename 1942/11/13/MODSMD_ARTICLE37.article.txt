meet Sunday at 10 a.m. in front
of the Union to go to Palo Alto
Yacht Harbor for sailing practice.

entering in the Big Game Float
Parade will meet at 5 o'clock this
afternoon in the Nitery.

Quad today at 12:50 p.m. in front
of the Education Building.
