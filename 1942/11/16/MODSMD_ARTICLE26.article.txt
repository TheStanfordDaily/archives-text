# Dodds Will Speak At Mothers' Club
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club will
hold its November meeting Tues
day, Nov. 17. at 2 o'clock in the
Women's Clubhouse. The program
will consist of a chalk talk on Moth
ers' Club finances by Mrs. C .G.
Vernier, and a talk on "The New
School of Humanities" by its dean,
John W. Dodds. Tea will be served
by the Mothers' Club of Kappa
Kappa Gamma.
