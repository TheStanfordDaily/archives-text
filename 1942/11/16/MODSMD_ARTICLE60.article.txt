# Motorists Apply For Gas Nov. 18
## 
###

books will take place in the Palo
Alto public schools, Nov. 18. To ob
tain books a motorist must bring his
application form completely and ac
curately filled arid white registra
tion slip for his car to any Palo Alto
school on Nov. 18. 19, 20.
Applications for gas rationing
books can be obtained at a number
of service stations in Palo Alto and
the two Shell stations on the campus.
