# Student Engineers To Be Interviewed
## 
### 
Mechanical, electrical, and chem
ical engineers interested in working
for the Westinghouse Electric and
Manufacturing Co. will be inter
viewed by representatives of the
company visiting the campus today
and tomorrow.

the 18th. Students may arrange in
terviews with Mr. A. W. Copley and
Mrs. S. H. Harrison, the representa
tives, through the Appointment
Service in the Administration Build
