# Two Good Men
## 
### 
THE Daily is holding its first Fall Quarter
election this afternoon from 1 to 3. Two
men left the Shack yesterday evening for din
ner to discuss the past, present, and future of
The Daily. One of them will return today to
become editor of Volume 103.
From the retiring editor to two men who have
worked hard and faithfully for The Daily, re
member that no matter who wins, you will have
the satisfaction of knowing that your, work has
been excellent, your attitude superior. Upon
the shoulders of one of you will lest the glory,
worry, and hardships of running a five-day-a
week paper.
Good luck!
