# Drivers to Apply For Gas Rationing
## 
### 
Today is the first day for car
owners to register for gas rationing.
Registration will be held from 9 a.m.
to 7 p.m. at the campus elementary
school.
They should bring forms, avail
able at any service station, already
filled out and also their white regis
tration slips. If the applicant does
not own the car, a signed statement
by the registered owner will be nec
essary.
