# L.A. County Home Seeks Supervisor
## 
### 
The Los Angeles County Civil
Service Commission has issued a
call for a home director for the El
Retiro School for girls.
Women between 25 and 55 years of
age with a full high school educa
tion and experience as a home di
rector, house mother, supervisor, or
counselor are eligible for the posi
tion. Applications must be filed with
the office of the commission, Room
102, Hall of Recoixls, Los Angeles,
before Nov. 27.
