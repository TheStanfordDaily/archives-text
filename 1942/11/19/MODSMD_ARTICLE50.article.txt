# SOLDIER WINS AWARD
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 18 (U.R)—
Corp. Marion Colbridge, 24, Logan,
Utah, was awarded the Soldier's
Medal today for rescuing 10-year
old Lawrence Fava from drowning
at the San Francisco Yacht Harbor
last August.
