# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
ALL THOSE who wanted to buy a
Chaparral this morning stick their
quarters back in their pockets,
'cause it won't be out until to
morrow.
NO QUAD PICTURES will be
taken today or tomorrow.
POMPOMS will be on sale Thurs
day and Friday afternoon at the
A.S.S.U. office from 1 to 5 p.m.
Feathers also for five cents. There
will be no Saturday sale of Pom
poms which are required.
