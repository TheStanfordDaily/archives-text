# HOOT'S FINALE
## 
### 
In the coining Stanford game with
i the St. Mary's Naval Pre-Flight,
Hoot Armstrong will have his last
chance at Stanford T doings. He has
been ineligible all year. Also it may
be the last meeting of Frankie Al
j bel t with the T he made famous.
The annual truck capacity of the
Oakland Bay Bridge is 6,000,000.
