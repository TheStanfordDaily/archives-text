# Record Expected In 1942 Football
## 
### 
New records set by this year's
football teams threaten to smash
previous records now on the books
of the American football statistical
buheau.
Four of the nation's teams are
now piling up better than 400 yards
per game which will beat the all
time record of 375.4 yards per game
held by Colorado when "Whizzer"
White was carrying the mail.
The four teams rolling up the
yardage are Georgia with 444.4 yards
per contest, Ohio State with 420.3,
Tulsa with 411.9, and Boston College
with 404.fi.
Georgia has collected 3,990 yards
in nine games, gaining 2,305 by
rushing and 1.694 by passing.
