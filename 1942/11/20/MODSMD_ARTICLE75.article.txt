# Pompoms Required For Women Rooters
## 
### 
Each woman in the rooting sec
tion tomorrow must have a pom
pom, according to a Rally Com
mittee announcement. Pom-poms
and feathers will be on sale to
day from 1 to 5 p.m. at the A.S.-
S.U. office. White shirts are re
quired for all men and women
rooters. Men must have rooters'
caps.
