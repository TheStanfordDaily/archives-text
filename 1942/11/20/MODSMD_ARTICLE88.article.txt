# Alumni to Hear Game Broadcast
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Club of
New York, New Jersey, and Con
necticut will meet again this year
in New York for the annual Big
Game broadcast. The New York
Club received Chancellor Wilbur
Nov. 2 when he was in New York and
plans were made to hear the broad
cast.
