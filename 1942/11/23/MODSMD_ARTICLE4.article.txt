# Bush Announces Essay Contest
## 
### 
"A Southwest Pacific Charter"
was announced yesterday as the
1942-43 subject for the Melville Ja
coby Essay Prize of $50 by Profes
sor Chilton R. Bush, of the Division
of Journalism.
All Stanford students are eligible
to enter the competition. Entries
must be submitted by Mar. 1. Sug
gested plan for the essay is a state

expounded in a setting of social and
political values, and political and

Time correspondent who lost his life
while covering the war in Australia
last March.
