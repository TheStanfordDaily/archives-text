# SUSPECT OUT ON BAIL
## 
### 
SALEM, Ore., Nov. 23 (U.R)—A. B.
McKillop, assistant cook at the state
hospital here who was charged with
manslaughter in the fatal poisoning
of 47 mentally deranged patients,
was released from jail under $10,000
bail late today.
