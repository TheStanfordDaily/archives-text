# BUDGET BUREAU BILL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 23 (UP).—
The Senate today passed a bill to
make the Budget Bureau a clear
ing house for all government re
ports and questionnaires—a move
which Sen. Arthur H. Vandenberg,
R., Mich., said is aimed at prevent
ing "organized regimentation."
