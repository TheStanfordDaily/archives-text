# ALPHA DELTS WIN
## 
### 
Volleyball playoffs netted two
out of three games last night for
the Alpha Delts. After losing their
first game with the Phi Kaps by a
score of 15-13, the Alpha Delts took
two in a row 15-11 and 15-13. The
outstanding player in the whole
program was generally agreed to be
Jack Flammer of the Phi Kaps.
