# HUSKY DRILL DELAYED
## 
### 
SEATTLE. Nov. 23 (UP).—Late
train schedules today prevented the
Washington Huskies from begin
ning workouts for the coming
Washington State tilt but Coach
Ralph Welch promised overtime
practice sessions to make up for
time lost in returning from the
UCLA game.
KETCHIKAN. Alaska, Nov. 23
(UP). — The body of Charles F.
Swanson, cook aboard the missing
tugboat Edward Schenk, was found
today on the beach five miles north
of Three Tree Point.
