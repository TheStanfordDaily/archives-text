# Hammett Chosen For Player Award
## 
### 
Quarterback Ray Hammett won
by a whisker from Chuck Taylor
yesterday as the final ballot tally
gave Hammett the David Keeble
award for being Stanford's most val
uable player of the season.
Hammett wins a $15 pen and pen
cil set.
Buck Fawcett ran third and Hank
Norberg fourth.
