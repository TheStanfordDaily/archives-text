# Farm Broadcasts To South American
## 
### 
Participating in the celebration of
the 100-year's anniversary of the
University of Chile, Stanford gave a
special short-wave broadcast to
South America last night.
The broadcast featured an address
by Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur,
translated into Spanish by Mrs. Ma
ria rfbge, director of the Stanford
office of Inter-American Relations.
The Stanford chorus, directed by Dr.
Warren D. Allen, sang.
