# Waacs Build New Reserve Group
## 
### 
Women between the ages of 21
and 44 who want to enlist in the
Waacs and still complete their cur
rent college or business school course
or current school year may do so
provided that this takes not more
than one calendar year, according
to an announcement made by the
War Department.
Those desiring to enroll in the new
reserve group must report at 444
Market street, Sim Francisco, for a
mental alertness test, physical ex
amination and interview with a
board of officers. Transportation will
be furnished.
In addition to the present train
ing cented in Des Moines, a new
Wane training school will soon be
established at Daytona Beach.
