# GAS VIOLATION SCORED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 4 (U.R).—
Price Administrator Leon Henderson
tonight sternly denounced Gov.
Sam H. Jones of Louisiana for en
couraging violation of gasoline regu
lations because they were "impos
sible to administer" and designed by
"Eastern interests."
