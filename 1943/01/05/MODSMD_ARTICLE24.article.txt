# CORPORATIONS FINED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 4 (U.R).—
Eight nationally known fruit and
vegetable packing corporations and
individuals were fined $19,000 today
by Federal Judge Martin I. Welsh
on charges of violating federal anti
trust laws and price-fixing.
