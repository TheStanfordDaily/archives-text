# Hanna Appointed Services Director
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur has
announced the appointment of Dr.
Paul R. Hanna, professor of educa
tion. as University Services Director,
with offices both on the campus and
in Washington, D.C.
Dr. Hanna will assist the Chancel
lor in keeping Stanford man-power
and facilities most effectively em
ployed during the war period and in
coordinating Stanford's war and
post-war projects.
