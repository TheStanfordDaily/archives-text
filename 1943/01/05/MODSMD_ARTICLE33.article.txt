A new Hawaiian section of the
Hoover Library on War, Revolution,
and Peace has been established under
the chairmanship ol' Chancellor Ray
Lyman Wilbur.
Dr. Wilbur has long been active in
the Institute of Pacific Relations and
is keenly interested in Pacific Af
fairs. Within a week after the attack
on Pearl Harbor, Dr. Wilbur and Dr.
Ralph H. Lutz, chairman of the Li
-1 brary directors, made arrangements
by trans-Pacific telephone for the
j collection of materials in Hawaii
! concerning the outbreak of World
; War 11.
