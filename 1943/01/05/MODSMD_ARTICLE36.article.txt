# Bob Koch Enrolls Again at Oregon
## 
### 
Bob Koch, Athertonized after
playing freshman football here, is
back at classes again at the Univer
sity of Oregon.
Koch performed at fullback for
Oregon one year, then enlisted in
the Navy and passed his pre-flight
training while playing football for
the St. Mary's Pre-Flighters. He was
give nan honorable discharge when
he developed chronic air sickness,
however.
He is registered at Oregon for the
Winter Quarter and is expected to
greatly bolster the Webfoot squad
lor the next season.
