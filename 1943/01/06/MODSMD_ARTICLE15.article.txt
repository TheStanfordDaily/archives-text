# MEXICAN FARM LABOR
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY. Jan. 5 (UP).—
Another contingent of 1,114 Mexican
farm workers will be sent to the
vegetable fields of California's Im
perial Valley next week, W. E. Lapp,
U.S. Farm Security Administration
representative, said tonight.
