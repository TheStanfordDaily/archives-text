# WAR RISK RATE FALLS
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Jan. 7 (U.R)—Marine
underwriters tonight announced
25 per cent reduction in war risk
insurance rates on cargoes moving
between Hawaii and the Western
Coast of the United States and
Canada.

aircraft shells.
