# Morris Will Visit Aberdeen Arsenal
## 
### 
The War Department has invited
Samuel B. Morris, dean of Stan
ford's School of Engineering, to
make a three-day visit to the Ord
nance installations at Aberdeen
Proving Ground in Maryland.
The tour will be made with a se
lected group of administrative offi
cials of leading engineering schools
and colleges in the country, accord
ing to Brigadier General J. S. Hatch
er, chief of Military Training, Office
of the Chief of Ordnance, who sent
the invitation to Dean Morris.
The engineering professor will
leave Palo Alto today and will visit
the Aberdeen Proving Ground in
Maryland from January 12 to 14.
