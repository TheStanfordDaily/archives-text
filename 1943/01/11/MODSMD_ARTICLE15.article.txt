# Journalism Groups To Meet Thursday
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi, men's national
professional journalism fraternity,
and Theta Sigma Pi, women's jour
nalism fraternity, will hold a joint
business meeting Thursday evening
at 7:30 in the Lagunita lobby. All
members are urged to be present to
discuss plans for the Knickerbocker
lecture.
