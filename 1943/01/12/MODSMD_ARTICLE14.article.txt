# Excom Approves Awards for Track
## 
### 
Circle S awards for cross country
were approved at last Wednesday's.
Excom meeting for Gordon Gray,
Bruce Meyer and Lloyd Cuff. An
additional football award was made
to Tony Medved, who has earned a
Block S.
Among the dates granted were
those for Sunday movies'on Jan. 24,
31; Feb. 7, 14. 28. and March 7. The
date of the "Y" Birthday Dinner was
changed from Feb. 18 to Feb. 25.
