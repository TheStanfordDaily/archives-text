# Singers Needed For 'Gondoliers'
## 
### 
A call for more students, not nec
essarily trained singers, to try out
for parts and the chorus of "The
Gondoliers," Gilbert and Sullivan
operetta to be given March 4, 5, and
6 was issued yesterday by C. W.
Barker, secretary of the Speech and
Drama Department.
Tryouts will be held tomorrow
night at 7:30 in ihe Little Theater.
All are eligible. Cast members and
a chorus of 50 will be selected by
Dr. Herbert Jan Popper.
Those who are chosen may enroll
in Opera Workshop for two units of
credit. No work is required other
than singing in "The Gondoliers."

Woodrow Wilson was once a his
tory professor at Princeton.
