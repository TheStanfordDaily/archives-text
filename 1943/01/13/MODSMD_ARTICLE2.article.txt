# Plans Made for Vocom Meetings
## 
### 
The five Wednesday night Wom
en's Vocational Committee meetings
will be given on nursery school
teachers, social workers, Latin
America, government service, and
personnel, according to Virginia
Stoddard. Vocom chairman.
The date ot the first meeting is
Jan. 27 from 7,30 to 8:45 in the
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
The five subjects for the Vocom
meetings were the first five choices
of a basis of reports on question
naires from the Dean of Women's
Office.
