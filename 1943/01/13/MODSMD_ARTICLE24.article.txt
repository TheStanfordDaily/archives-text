# Firehouse Boys Save Stranded Mack Cat
## 
### 
Yesterday a very black cat, de
j ciding that he wanted the broader
j view inherent in a college education,
climbed a tree at Kingscote Gardens
|to look out over Stanford-land. He
couldn't get down.
Came two small girls at his tearful
plea, but they couldn't climb up.
i Came several residents with no bet
| ter luck.
Came Fire Chief Marston in the
little red automobile with the ration
board tires, but he couldn't get up.
Came the hook and ladder wagon
with two of the Firehouse heroes in
slanting halos and clean, white,
T-shirts.
Came down the cat swiftly to a
saucer of milk. The firemen went
home with the good feeling that
I doesn't come from dinner.
