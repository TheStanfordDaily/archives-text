# Former Professor In Naval Reserve
## 
### 
Harold F. Lynn, '24, and former
professor of field geology and topo
graphic surveying in the School of
Mines, is now a lieutenant com
mander in the Civil Engineer Corps
of the U.S. Naval Reserve serving
with the "Seabees."' Lt. Comdr. Lynn
will direct overseas battalions that
are "building a bridge of Pearl
Harbors to Japan.''
Navy commissions are being of
fered to construction engineers and
superintendents, ages 19-50, to do
this same type of work. The Office
of Naval Officer Procurement, 703
Market street, is handling applica
tions.
