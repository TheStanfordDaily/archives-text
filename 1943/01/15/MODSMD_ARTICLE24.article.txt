# Allen to Act As Judge in Contest
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, Professor of
Music and Education, will be one of
the judges of the final contest to
launch some young San Francisco
musician in a new career in music.
The Young Symphony Artist Con
test is being conducted by station.
KGO in San Francisco in cooperation
with the San Francisco Musical As
sociation.
Approximately 50 young North
ern California instrumentalists will
be heard in weekly broadcasts over
KGO to determine the outstanding
young musicians of the Bay Area.
