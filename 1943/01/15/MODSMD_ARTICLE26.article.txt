# Professor Molnar Earns Citizenship
## 
### 
Mr. Ferenc Molnar, professor of
music and engineering at Stanford,
became a United States citizen Mon
day when he and his wife, Maria
Molnar, former newspaper column
ist, took the oath of allegiance in
Judge William F. James' superior
court in San Jose. Mr. Molnar, who
lives at 1052 Bryant Street, is a dis
tinguished Hungarian violinist.
