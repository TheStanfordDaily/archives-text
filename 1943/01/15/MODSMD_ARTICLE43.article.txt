# Trueblood to Give Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, chaplain
of the University Church, will give
the address at Sunday morning
services in Memorial Church at 11.
Dr. Trueblood will speak on "An
Intimation of Life's Meaning," the
same subject of which he spoke at
Dartmouth University and. Rollins
College in Florida on his recent tour
of the East.
This will be Dr. Trueblood's last
sermon for January. Visiting speak
ers will occupy the. pulpit for the re
maining Sundays.
Scientific research in Britain has
given the Allies many powerful
weapons of war. British scientists
invented and perfected "radio-loca
tion," a revolutionary discovery in
the detection of approaching air
craft. \
