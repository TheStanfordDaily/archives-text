# CANADA STEEL STRIKE
## 
### 
OTTAWA, Ont., Jan. 14 (UP).—
Two-thirds of Canada's steel pro
duction was halted today when 5,000
workers at the Algoma Steel Corp.,
Sault Ste. Marie, and 3,000 employ
| ees of the Dominion Steel and Coal
Co., subsidiaries at Trenton, N.S.,
went on strike, demanding wage in
creases.
