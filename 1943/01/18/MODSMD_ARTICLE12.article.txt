# WOMEN'S PLEDGING
## Sororities Pin 126 Freshmen After Condensed Rush Week 
### 
The largest number of neophytes
since 1934, 126, are now wearing the
pledge pins of nine Stanford sorori
ties following formal pledging Satur
day night. Delta Gamma took honors
for the largest class with 17 pledges.
Preceding pledging was an inten
sive week of rushing dates which
included only one formal dinner. On
Slaurday night, campus males camt,
saw, and were conquered by the
nuggets at the traditional open
houses.
The complete pledge list follows:
