# New Farm Frosh Receive Welcome
## 
### 
Meeting in the Nitery Sunday
afternoon, new freshmen heard
Student Body President Ed Stamm,
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur, and
prominent alumnus Paul Edwards,
editor of the San Francisco News,
speak on various phases of Stanford
life.
Stamm, in welcoming the new
students, stated that he believed
Stanford now has more spirit than
ever before, mainly because of the
increase of on-campus activities.
Dr. Wilbur warned the new frosh
that vacations are a thing of the past,
and that students must get as much
as possible out of the University be
fore being called into service.
