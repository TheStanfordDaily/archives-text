# U.S. SUBS SINK FIVE MORE SHIPS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Jan. 13 (U.R) -
American submarines have sunk five

more Japanese ships and damaged
two others in the ceaseless war of
attrition which is slowly sapping the
enemy's shipping and manpower
throughout the Pacific.
With these new losses the Jap
anese have now lost 290 ships in the
Pacific, not including sinkings by
General Douglas Mac Arthur's Amer
ican-Australian forces, an unofficial
compilation disclosed.
