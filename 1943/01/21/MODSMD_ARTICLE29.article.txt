the names of the patrons for their j
Saturday night open house to the

noon.

will hold its regular weekly meet
ing at 7:30 p.m. in the Y.W.C.A.
room, on the third of the Women's j
Clubhouse. All those interested
are invited to attend.

from 9 to 12 and 1 to 5.
