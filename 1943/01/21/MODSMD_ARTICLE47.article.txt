# Ski Membership Cards Go on Sale
## 
### 
Membership cards for the Stan
ford Ski Club may be purchased in
the A.S.S.U. office for $1.50. These
cards entitle members to the facili
ties of the University of California
Ski Lodge near Norden.
Rates at the Lodge are 50 cents a
night. Inexpensive meals are served
at the Sierra Club.
Reservations for the Cal Ski Lodge
may be made through Jane Donald,
Gamma Phi, Bob Blatt, Alpha Delt,
or Ted Holcomb, Encina. A deposit
is required.
