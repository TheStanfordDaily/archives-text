# Boxing Tourney Opens Thursday
## 
### 
Friday is the last day to enter
either the All-University or Novice
Boxing Championships, Bill Weeks,
assistant coach announced.
Thursday, FeV 4, has been sched
uled for the tournament, with run
oil matches to be held Tuesday,
Wednesday, and Friday.
The coaches have emphasized the
fact that it is not necessary to be a
proficient boxer, but that the stress
is placed mainly in physical fitness,
which is attained by rigorous, con
scientious training for a boxing
event.
Contestants may sign up for the
tournament in the Boxing Gallery.
