# FIRM WORKS IN FIFTH WAR
## 
### 
James Irving & Sons, Inc., manu
facturers of woolen garb for sol
diers in the Mexican War, Civil War,
Spanish-American War, and World
War I, are supplying wool products
for the nation's armed forces in
World War 11. Founded in 1842, the
concern now is headed by S. Lloyd .
Irving, representing the third gen
eration of the Irving family.
