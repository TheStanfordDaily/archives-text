# Men's Rushing Nears Finale
## 
### 
Men's rushing reaches its last
regular rush period today when
frosh go up the Row for both lunch
and dinner meals. Preference din
ners will be held tomorrow night,
followed by formal pledging on Sat
urday.
Lunch today will be from 11:30
to 1 p.m. and dinner from 6 to 8 p.m.
Preference dinners tomorrow will
be held from 6 to 9 p.m. Formal
pledging Saturday will be from 10
to 2 p.m.
Contact between fraternity men
and freshmen is not permitted in the
period between preference dinners
tomorrow night and formal pledging
Saturday, according to Interfra
ternity rules.
Most houses are planning dances
for their pledges Saturday night to
replace the Pledge Prom of past
years.
