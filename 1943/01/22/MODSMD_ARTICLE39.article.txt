# Thirty-Five Perish In Transport Crash
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Jan. 21 (UP).—
Thirty-five Americans, including
Eric Knight, the author, and two
State Department officials, were
killed Jan. 15 when a big transport
plane en route from the United
States to North Africa and the Mid
dle East crashed in a remote area
of New Guinea, it was disclosed to
day.
Everyone aboard—civilians and
military personnel—perished.
The Army Air Transport com
mand said that public announcement
of the disaster had been delayed
because of inachcessibility.
