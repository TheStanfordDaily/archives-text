gram in years was closed Saturday
with the addition of 286 freshmen and
transfers to the Farm's 24 fraterni
ties.

headed the pledging list. Chi Psi was
second with 18, and the Betas,
S.A.E.'s, and K.A.'s tied for third
with 16 pledged.
