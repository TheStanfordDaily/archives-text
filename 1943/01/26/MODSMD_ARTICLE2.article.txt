# Board Auxiliary Names Executive
## 
### 
Marion Doherty was named sec
retary of the War Board Women's
Auxiliary at a meeting of that or
ganization in the Women's Club
house yesterday at 3 p.m. Organizer
and present' chairman of the group
is Pat Thuresson.
Auxiliary members, representing
all the women's living groups, were
given copies of a pamphlet explain
ing the organization and activities of
the War Board. Further explana
tion was made by Coordinator Rose
mary Cross.
As chairman of the war work
room, Peg McClean announced that
the making of afghans for Mare
Island Navy Hospital was the new
project.
