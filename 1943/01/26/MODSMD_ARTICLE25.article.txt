# Hoos, Ex-Varsity Player, in Navy
## 
### 
Ensign Earl Hoos, '37, former
captain of the Stanford football
team, and a member of Tiny
Thornhill's Rose Bowl teams, has
been assigned to the military de
partment of the U.S. Navy Pre-
Flight School, St. Mary's College.
While at Stanford, Hoos, a mem
ber of El Toro, played quarter
back, and attended Business
School, and School of Education.
