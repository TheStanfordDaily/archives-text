# Rally Com to Give Dime Ball
## 
### 
This Saturday night Rally Com
mittee is sponsoring a Half and Half
Dance which will be held in the
Women's Gym from 9:30 p.m. until
1 a.m. This will give students plenty
of time to attend the basketball
game.
The theme of the dance will be
carried out in the decorations, as
well, it is hoped, as in the costumes
of those attending. One Rally Com
mittee member said, "The idea is to
dress half as if you were going to
one kind of a dance and half as if
to another—like a half barn dance
costume and a half south sea island
one."
