# Vocom to Present Nursery Program
## 
### 
Nursery School Program will be
given by Women's Vocational Com
mittee tonight at 7 p.m. in the
Women's Clubhouse Lounge. Kath
leen Guthrie, Vdcom member, will
act as student chairman.
Dr. Helen Pryor, Medical Advisor
of Women, will serve as faculty
chairman of the discussion. Miss
Dorothy Tilden, School of Health,
will speak on The Nursery School
Child; Mr. Roger Barker, Depart

of Psychology and the Nursery
School; Mr. Lucien Kinney, School
