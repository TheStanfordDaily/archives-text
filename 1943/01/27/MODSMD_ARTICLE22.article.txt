# MUSIC GROUP TO MEET
## 
### 
The music group of the Y.W.C.A.,
under the chairmanship of Virginia
Clagett, will meet today at 4 p.m. in
the "Y" roms. All girls are invited
to the hour of records. Tea will be
served. The Y.W.C.A. cabinet will
meet today at 5 p.m. in the "Y"
rooms.

Manufacturers are not to put 100
per cent wool in any garments. In
blending fabrics the least amount of
wool permitted is 20 per cent and
the maximum is 65 per cent.

Dolls are the oldest toys known.
They have been used in all lands
from the earliest times. One found
in an Egyptian tomb is estimated to
be at least 4,000 years old.
