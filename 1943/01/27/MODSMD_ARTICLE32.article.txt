# Dramatist Alliance Offers Four Awards,
## 
### 
The Dramatists' Alliance of Stan
ford University has offered four
awards in the field of drama in its
eighth annual competition. The
Stevens award will be for serious
drama, the Alden Award for short
plays, the Etherege Award for full
length comedy, and the Gray Award
for good dramatic criticism.
All contributions should be sent
to Dramatists' Alliance, Office 200Z,
at Stanford, before March 15, 1943.
