# Cardinals Schedule Sadie Hawkins Day
## 
### 
Sadie Hawkins Day has been
scheduled by Cardinals for Feb. 13,
and campus Daisy Maes are advised
to capture their men quick before
the days of Sadie Hawkins are ra
tioned along with everything else.
Maybe the Sadie Hawkins days
will continue but it's pretty certain
that there will be a lack of Lil* Ab
ners. Get your dates, plan your
costumes, save the $1 admission fee.
and join the Dogpatch dancers from
9 to 1 in the Women's Gym, Feb. 13.
