# Beta Mural Team Beats Alpha Sigs
## 
### 
In the intramurals last night the
Beta quintet beat the Alpha Sigs,
28-13. It was a one-man riot with
Phil Shean, another Stanfordite soon
going into the Army, scoring 20
points.
Zete II chalked up another win by
beating the Breakers, 43-14. High
scorer was John Gertridge with six
baskets.
In a very close game up to the last
quarter, Theta Delt I beat Theta Chi
26-16. Varsity football player Ross
Dana took scoring honors with 12
markers.
Alpha Delts beat. Kappa Sigma
23-20. After this game the Sigma
Chi five rolled over Phi Psi 22-7.
