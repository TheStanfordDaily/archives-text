# JAPS BEATEN BACK
## 
### 
CHUNGKING, Jan. 28 (U.R) — All
three Japanese-led Thai (Siamese)
columns that penetrated southern
Yunan Province from Burma have
been beaten back with losses, and
the territory is now entirely clear
of the enemy, a Chinese military
snokesman said today.
