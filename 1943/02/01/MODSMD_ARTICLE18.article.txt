# Berlin Plays Last Game for Frosh
## 
### 
Playing his last game for the Stan
ford frosh, Dick Berlin scored 12
points to lead his squad to a smash
ing 52-26 victory over San Mateo
J.C. Saturday night. Playing every
man suiting up for the game, Coach
Woody Weir used three complete
teams.
Berlin, who has received his or- 1
ders from the Navy, leaves for Spo- !
kane, Wash., today to await his call |
to active duty.
