# Physical Fitness
## 
### 
ALTHOUGH some 20 per cent of the Stanford
males are of the commando quality and
some others are not quite so virile, the emphasis
now seems to fall on the Stanford women as to
their physical fitness.
Tonight at 8 p.m. all the women are asked
to come and hear how they can lend their ef
forts toward the war and better prepare them
selves.
