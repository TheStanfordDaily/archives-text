# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
STANFORD RIFLE TEAM meets at
El Toro at 7:30 tonight.
ALL QUAD PROOFS must be
picked up and returned by Thurs
day at the office upstairs in the
A.S.S.U. Proofs may be obtained
every day between 1 and 5 p.m.
WOMEN'S WAR BOARD Auxiliary
meets today at 1 in the Women's
Clubhouse. Each women's living
group should be represented.
