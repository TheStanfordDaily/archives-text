# Buck to Address Y.W.C.A. Today
## 
### 
Dr. Philip W. Buck of the political
science department will speak on
"Covering the War Fronts" today at
4:30 on the third floor of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Tlie meeting is sponsored by the
International Relations Club and
given under the auspices of the
Y.W.C.A. word affairs committee.
The discussion is open to all inter
ested in the latest news about the
theaters of war, according to Dodie
Crile, chairman of the "Y" group.
Besides the informal discussion
there will be opportunity for ques
tions. The purpose of this meeting,
the first in a series of four, is to
project student thinking into world
planning, according to Miss Crile.
