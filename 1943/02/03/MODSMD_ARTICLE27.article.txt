# Encina to Hold Informal Dance
## 
### 
An informal dance, open to the j
University, will be given Friday
night by Encina in the new club
room. The dance committee is being
chairmanned by Stan Cumberpatch.
Encina's first social function of the
quarter will last from 8:00 to 11:30.
Music for the dance will be provided
by records, and both refreshments
and admittance will be free.

In one 9x12 wool rug is enough
jute to make 32 sand bags for use in
trench warfare.
