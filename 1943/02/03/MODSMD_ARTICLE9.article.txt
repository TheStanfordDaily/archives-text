# Emerson Chosen New Debate Coach
## 
### 
Professor James Emerson, of the
English department, will take over
for the remainder of the year the
position in the debate department
vacated by Dr. Leland T. Chapin,
former coach.
Mr. Jim Kessler, freshman debate
coach, will aid Professor Emerson in
his work with the varsity squad, and
together they will direct the activi
ties of the debate department. Mr.
Kessler will also take over two of
Dr. Chapin's classes in Speech 15.
Dr. Chapin left Saturday to enroll
in the Navy's school for colonial
government at Dartmouth. He will
receive a year's instruction at Co
lumbia University before being sent
to his post somewhere in the South
Seas.
