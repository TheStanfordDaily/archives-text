# Press to Publish Monograph Series
## 
### 
The Stanford University Press has
been appointed publisher for a new
series of monographs sponsored by
the American Association for Ap
plied Psychology under the general
editorship of Dr. Herbert S. Conrad,
Institute of Child Welfare, Univer
sity of California. Associate editors
of the series are Robert G. Bern
renter. E. A. Doll, Arthur W. Korn
hauser, Harriet E. O'Shea, C. R.
Rogers. F. L. Ruch, P. C. Symonds,
and Lewis M. Terman, emeritus
professor of psychology and educa
tion at Stanford University.
