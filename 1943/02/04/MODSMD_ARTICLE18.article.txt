# BACH RECITAL
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist will present an all-Bach
organ recital today at 4:15. The pro
gram will include. Prelude in C
minor, Sinfonia, "I Stand Before the
Gate of Heaven," Chorale-Prelude,
"Rejoice Ye, Christians," Air from
the Suite in D, and Little Fugue in
G minor, all by Bach.
