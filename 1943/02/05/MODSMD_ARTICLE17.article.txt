# Nottingham to Play For Senior Donee
## 
### 
Stanford seniors will gather in
the Women's Gym the night of Feb.
27 to dance for four hours to the
music of Gary Nottingham at the
annual Senior Prom.
Tickets will be on sale next
Wednesday in the A.S.S.U. office for
seniors at $3 a bid. Seniors will be
given preference for several days;
bids will then be open to the entire
campus.
