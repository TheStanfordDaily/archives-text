# Organist to Play At Vesper Service
## 
### 
A Vesper Service will be held
Sunday afternoon at 4 in Memorial
Chapel. Readings will be given by
David Colburn and several other
students; and Joseph M. Running,
assistant University organist, will
present a brief organ recital.
The music will consist of Prelude
and Fugue in E minor by J. S. Bach,
Sonata No. 11 in C minor by Men
delssohn, Pastorale by Cesar Franck
and Kyrie Eleison, from "Cathedral
Windows," by Karg-Elert. Students
and faculty are invited to attend.
