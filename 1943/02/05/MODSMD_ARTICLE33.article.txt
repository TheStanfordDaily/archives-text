# Draft Decimates Frosh Hoop Team
## 
### 
Stanford's frosh basketball squad
lost its second first stringer in a
week when it was announced that
guard Bill Christiansen would be
drafted in the near future and there
fore is leaving school.
Woody Weir, frosh cage mentor,
lost guard Dick Berlin to the Navy
a few days ago, and must now build
up a new starting combine around
forwards Hugh West and Babe Hig
gins, and center Dave Davidson.
