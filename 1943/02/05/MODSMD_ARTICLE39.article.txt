# Coke Time
## 
### 
MEMBERS of the Daily editorial staff will
trudge their way to the shack promptly at
noon Sunday for a short jam session around the
coke machine; and, also, to receive bouquets
and brickbats from the Edit Board.
Night editors are due at 11:15 a.m., and
H.C.E.'s at 11:30 a.m. Almighty Edit Board will
convene at 11:45 a.m.
