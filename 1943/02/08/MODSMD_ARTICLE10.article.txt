# Shoe Rationing Begins Tomorrow
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Feb. 7 (UP).—
Immediate nationwide rationing of
shoes was ordered today by the War
Production Board.
Beginning Tuesday morning, ev
ery man, woman, and child will be
entitled to purchase one pair of
shoes between then and June 15
upon presentation of stamp No. 17
from ration book No. I—the one
used for rationing of sugar and cof
fee.
