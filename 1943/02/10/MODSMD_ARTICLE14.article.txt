# GUADALCANAL BATTLE ENDS IN U.S. VICTORY
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, D C., Feb. 9 (U.R)
—The six-month battle of Guadal- :
canal has ended in a smashing U.S.
victory, it was announced today,
and American sea, air, and ground
forces are now in position to direct
even more powerful blows at other
enemy bases in the South Pacific.
Secretary of Navy Frank Knox
confirmed a radio broadcast from
Tokyo that the Japanese had given
up the fight for Guadalcanal and had
evacuated what remained of their
starving iloops.

tables and given the Japanese what
the gallant band of American-Fili
pino troops suffered on Bataan in
