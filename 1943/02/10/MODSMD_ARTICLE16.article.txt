# NAVY BOMBER CRASHES IN BAY, KILLS FIVE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Feb. 9 (UP).—
A twin-motored Navy bomber
crashed into Sim Francisco Bay and
exploded wc today, the Twelfth
Naval District reported. All live
members of the crew, as yet un
idenlificd, apparently were killed.
