# Roble Will Entertain Sophomores Friday
## 
### 
Roble en masse will entertain
sophomore girls at a Valentine tea
to be held tomorrow. After they
have had tea, the sophomores are
vited to tour the corridors and
the rooms they lived in when they"
were freshmen.
The guests of honor are: Miss
Anastasia Doyle, Miss Pat Davidson,
Miss Christine Ricker, Miss Alice
Westbrook, Mrs. Mosher, Miss Maud
James, Miss Jane Bradley, Mrs. Niel
son, Miss Grace Glenn, Miss Rosa
mund Clarke, and Miss Stephens.
