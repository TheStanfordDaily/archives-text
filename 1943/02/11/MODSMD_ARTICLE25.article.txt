# BILLIONS OF DOUGHNUTS
## 
### 
NEW YORK—The Doughnut In
stitute calculates that Americans
ate 560 million dozen, that is 6,720,-
000,000 doughnuts last year, includ
ing some 10 million dozen donated
by the industry to servicemen. The
automatic doughnut machines went
along with the American forces oc
cupying North Africa.
