# Frosh Rally Com To Give Jolly-Up
## 
### 
The Frosh Rally Com will give a
Jolly Up Dance on Feb. 19 from 8:30
to 11:30 at Encina. The dance will
carry out Stanford Jolly Up tradi
tions of no date and no charge.
Bob Knox, chairman of Freshman
Rally Com, says, "We are giving a
Jolly Up again this quarter so that
the new freshmen can get a,chance
to meet more of their class, and so
that the old freshmen can have a
good get-together."
