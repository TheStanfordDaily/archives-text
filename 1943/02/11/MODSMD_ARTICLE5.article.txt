# Flying Fortresses Hit Near Kairouan
## 
### 
ALLIED HEADQUARTERS IN
NORTH AFRICA, Feb. 10 (U.R)
American Flying Fortresses, the Big
Berthas of the ceaseless air war
against the Axis in Eastern Tunisia,
have rained high fragmentation
bombs on 20 to 25 planes parked on
enemy airfields near the Moslem
shrine of Kairouan, it was announced
tonight.
* * * *
WITH THE U.S. FLEET IN THE
SOUTH PACIFIC, Feb. 10 (U.R)
United States planes have sunk at
least two Japanese destroyers and
possibly five more in landing the first
blows of a sparring match which may
foreshadow one of the biggest air
sea battles yet fought in this area.
