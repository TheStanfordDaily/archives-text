# Council Withholds Student Degrees
## 
### 
According to the Academic Coun
cil students who leave for the armed
services the end of this quarter, and
who have another quarter ahead of
them before receiving their degree,
will not receive their degree when
they leave.

If a student goes to officer's school
he may get up to 12 units of credit
applied to his scholastic record. If
these .additional units are sufficient
1o complete graduation requirements
then the student will receive his de
gree after the war, but not before.
