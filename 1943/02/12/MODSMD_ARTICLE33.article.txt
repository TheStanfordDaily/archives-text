# Daily Sports
## 
### 

Second Lieut. Harold B. Costello,
'41, former Stanford baseball star,
has completed his Reserve Officers'
Class training with the Marine
Corps at Quautieo, Va. While an
officer candidate, he qualified as a
Marksman with the pistol and
Sharpshooter with the ride. At
Stanford, he played in the out
field for Ilarry Wolter's baseball
teams, won four letters.
