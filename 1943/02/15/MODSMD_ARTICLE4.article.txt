# White to Speak To 'Y' Tomorrow
## 
### 
Dr. Lynn T. White, professor of
history, will speak tomorrow at 4
p.m. in the 'Y' rooms on the top floor
of the Women's Clubhouse. Dr.
White will discuss reconstruction and
the student's part in planning for
peace with special emphasis on the
regional studies being given under
the department of humanities.
This discussion, a part of the series
being given on problems of post-war
organization, is presented under the
auspices of the World Affairs group
of the Y.W.C.A.
