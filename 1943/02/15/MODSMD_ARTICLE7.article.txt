# Senior Plaque Hit By Brass Shortage
## 
### 
Shortage of metal will force
seniors to substitute cement for
brass as the material of the plaque
the graduating class annually pre
sents to the University to place in
the Quad, Bob Crary, senior presi
dent announces.
Kitss Bryan, committee chair
man. said be hoped the class would
be able to secure enough lead for
the hex underneath the plate
which holds class mementos.
Plans are to leave funds to replace
the cement with a brass plaque
after the war.
