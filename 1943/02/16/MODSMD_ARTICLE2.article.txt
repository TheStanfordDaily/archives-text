# RITTER ELECTED NEW CHAIRMAN OF COUNCIL
## 
### 
Darrell L. Ritter was elected new
chairman of Men's Council last Fri
day to succeed George Tolman
whose term ended at that time.

Ritter took over his new duties
yesterday afternoon together with
the four new members of the coun
cil. The new council consists of Jim
Kessler, Jack Monteverde, Henry
Segerstrom and Vic Ferrari.
