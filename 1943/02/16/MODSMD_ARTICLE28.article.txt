# Dr. White to Speak About Peace Plans
## 
### 
Dr. Lynn T. White, professor of
history, will speak today on recon
struction and the student's part in
planning for peace. The talk is
scheduled at 4 p.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse.
This discussion is one of a series
being given on problems of post
war organization, and is presented
under the auspices of the World
Affairs group of the Y.W.C.A.
