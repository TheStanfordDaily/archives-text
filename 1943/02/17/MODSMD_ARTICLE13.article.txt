# Ex-Stanford Woman Joins Marine Reserve
## 
### 
Miss Jean Ames, Stanford grad
uate, became one of the first Cali
fornia women to enlist in the
Marine Corps Women's Reserve of
World War 11, when she was
sworn in Monday in San Francisco.
Miss Ames now lives in San
Francisco. Her father is Capt. E.
R. Ames, stationed at the Camp
Elliott Marine training center near
San Diego, Calif.
Miss Ames chose to become a
plain enlistee despite the fact that
she is eligible for officer candidacy
saying, "I just want to be an en
listee, and mostly a Marine."
