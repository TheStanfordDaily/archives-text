# FULL SCHEDULE
## 
### 
The Metropolitan Collegiate Base
ball Conference, composed of Ford
ham, New York University, Man
hattan, St. John's, Brooklyn, Hof
stra and Columbia, has drawn up a
full schedule for the 1943 season.
The campaign will begin April 3.

Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will give his mid-week or
gan recital this afternoon at 4:15 in
Memorial Chapel. The program will
consist of Toccata in D minor (Do
rian); Chorale, "Jesu, Joy of Man's
Desiring," by C. M. Widor; "Clair de
Lune" by Sigfrid Karg-Elert; Cho
rale in E major, and Chorale in A
minor by Cesar Franck. Everyone
is invited to attend.
