# Chairman Explains Late Leave Rules
## 
### 
Late leave rules for women this
weekend and Washington's Birth
day are as follows, according to
Beth Babcock. chairman of Wom
en's Council:
Everyone is entitled to the usual
Saturday night 1:30 or overnight,
and the same applies to Sunday,
when late leaves or overnights will
be non-quota.
Late leaves and overnights may
be taken on Friday and Monday
nights, but permission must be
granted for both these nights by
Miss Babcock. She may be con
tacted today from 1 to 2 in her
oilice and from 4 to (! at the Chi
Omega house, P.A. Si) 15.
