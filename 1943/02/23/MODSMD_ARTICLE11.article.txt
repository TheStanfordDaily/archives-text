# U.S. Clipper Explodes at Lisbon Base
## Yankee Clipper Sinks In Tagus River; Five Persons Die in Blast 
### 
LISBON, Feb. 22 (U.R)-A Pan-
American Airways plane, understood
to be the Yankee Clipper, carrying
40 persons from the United States,
exploded and sank as it attempted
to alight at the airline base on the
Tagus River here tonight. Early re
ports said five persons were killed.
