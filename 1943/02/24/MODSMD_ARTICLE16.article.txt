# TWO AXIS SUBMARINES SUNK IN NIGHT DUELS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 23 (UP).—
The Navy revealed today that two
Axis submarines were sunk in night
duels with American gun crews, but
Secretary Frank Knox tempered the
news with a warning that Germany
is building U-boats "much faster
than we are sinking them."
