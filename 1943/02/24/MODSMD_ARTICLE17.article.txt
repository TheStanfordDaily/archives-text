# CONSIDERED JUDGMENTS
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH, Feb. 23 (UP).—
The 10,000,000 wanted by the Army
and Navy by the end of 1943 is not
a figure "pulled out of the hat," but
represents the considered judgments
of both command staffs, Undersec
retary of War Robert P. Patterson
asserted tonight.
