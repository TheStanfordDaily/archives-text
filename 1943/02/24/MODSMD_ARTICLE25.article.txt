# Stanford to Offer Accounting Course
## 
### 
To provide war plants with trained
women accountants to take the place
of drafted men, Stanford will offer
in San Francisco a full-time course
in industrial accounting for women.
Opening March 1 at the Cogswell
Polytechnical College the free course
will continue for 14* weeks through
June 4. Classes are to meet six hours
a day five days a week. Prerequi
sites for enrollment are high school
graduation and a course in elemen
tary bookkeeping or similar business
experience.
