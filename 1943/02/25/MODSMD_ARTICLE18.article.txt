# Toyon to Choose Officers Tonight
## 
### 
Toyon elections will be held this
evening from 7 to 11 in the lobby,
Theo Linhart, election chairman,
declared.
Petitions for candidates must be
turned in by tonight.
Outgoing officers are: president,
Newton Wise; vice-president, Rene
Zentner; secretary, John Youle.
Don Craig, manager, will remain in
office until June.
