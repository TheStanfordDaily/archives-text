# Hostesses To Hear Psychology Professor
## 
### 
Dr. Calvin P. Stone, professor of
psychology, and Dr. John W. Wells
from the Veterans Administration in
Palo Alto will speak at the fourth
meeting of junior hostesses for the
TJSO tomorrow at 1 p.m. in the "Y"
rooms, top floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
This series, a war board activity
given under Ihe auspices of the USO
group of the Y.W.C.A., June Ellis,
chairman, is given this quarter at
Stanford as a part of the national
training program.
