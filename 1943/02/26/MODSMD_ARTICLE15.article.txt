# Organist to Speak in Student Forum
## 
### 
Dr. Warren Allen, University or
ganist, will speak to the Student-
Faculty Forum on "Music in War
time" Sunday afternoon at 4:15 in
the Magnolia Social Room in Lag
unita Court.
Dr. Allen will demonstrate various
types of war music. He suggests
that music may be a hindrance to
morale in wartime as well as an aid,
and will give reasons for this view
point. Dr. Allen is a past president
of the American Association of Mu
sic Teachers and is the author of a
book on the philosophy of music.
Refreshments will be served before
the meeting. Students and faculty
members are invited.
