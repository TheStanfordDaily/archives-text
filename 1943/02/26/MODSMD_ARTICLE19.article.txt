# 'Song of Freedom' To Open March 2
## 
### 
Paul Robeson in "Song of Free
dom" will play at the Varsity The
ater in Palo Alto, beginning Tues
day, March 2.
Robeson sings excerpts from "Em
peror Jones" and six songs written
especially for him by Eric Ansell,
including "Lonely Road," "Song of
Freedom," "Sleepy River," and
"Stepping Stones."
Produced in England, the picture
is directed by J. Elder Wills.
The virus causing typhoid fever is
carried by mice and woodchucks as
well as by rats, according to new
medical discoveries.
