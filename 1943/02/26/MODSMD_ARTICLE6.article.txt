# Board to Study Sorority Plan
## 
### 
Stanford's Board of Trustees, after
consideration of the sorority prob
lem at a meeting, in San Francisco
yesterday afternoon, made the fol
lowing statement.
"The Board of Trustees considered
the question of sororities at Stan
ford and referred the subject to the
Committee on Academic Affairs for
further study. The chairmen of the
Financial Committee and of the
Building and Grounds Committee
were added to the committee for this
study. Dr. Tiesidder. president
elect, was asked to participate in the
activities of this committee. Full
opportunity to present facts will be
given to those interested and a
searching analysis of the whole
question made."
