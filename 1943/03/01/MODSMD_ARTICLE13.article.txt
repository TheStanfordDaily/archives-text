# Red Cross Workshop Wakes 654 Bandages
## 
### 
Women in the Red Cross Work
shop prepared (554 surgical dressings
last Friday. Twenty-seven of the
35 workers were students.
On Saturday morning, five work
ers rolled 215 bandages.
The Workshop can use new and
untrained workers all the time, and
urges campus women to come in
when they have a free hour.
