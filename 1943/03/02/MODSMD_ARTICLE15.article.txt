# 710 BANDAGES ROLLED
## 
### 
Bandages rolled yesterday by 30
wol kers at the Red Cross work room
in the Women's Clubhouse totalled
710. A quota of 1.000 per day has

been set to help meet the Army's
! request for 180 million bandages by
i March 15 from the Pacific Coast
area. Women are urged to work any
day from 10 to 12 a.m. and 1 to 5
p.m.
