# Inter-American College To Open
## 
### 
The Pan American Union ap
proved early this month plans to
set up an Inter-American University
in Panama.
The University, which will open
about June of this year, will be in
Panama City and will be open to
students from all of the 21 American
Republics of North and South Amer
ica. The present National University
of Panama will serve as the nucleus
for the new institution, and special
ized scientific institutes will be
added.
