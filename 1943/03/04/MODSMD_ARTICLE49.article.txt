# Women Debaters Hold Discussion
## 
### 
A Stanford women's debate team
composed of Fredericka Bancroft,
Barbara Brooks and Virginia
Schwartz, assistant debate manager,
met yesterday afternoon and eve
ning for a round-table discussion
with three women debaters from the
University of Washington.
The subject of the discussion was
"the civilian in the war effort," and
the six women brought up various
phases of the place of the men and
women who, though not actually on
the war fronts, are nevertheless es
sential parts of the war effort.
The round-table discussion, open
to the public, was held in Room 125
of Memorial Hall.
