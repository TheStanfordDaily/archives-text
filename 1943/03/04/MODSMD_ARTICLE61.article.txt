who worked last quarter are
asked to visit the War Board of
fices between 2 and 4 p.m. either
today or tomorrow. All others
who are interested should come
up.

meets at 7:30 p.m. on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse
today. Everyone is invited.
QUAD PROOFS may be picked up
and returned in Room 15 in the
Old Union. Thursday from 9 to 5.
MATRICULATION TEST to be
held this evening at 7:30 in Room
•7, Education Building.
