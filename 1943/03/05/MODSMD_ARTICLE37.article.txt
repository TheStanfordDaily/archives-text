# Rockwell, Bellamy Co-Star Sunday
## 
### 
Bob Rockwell, star of stage, screen,
and radio, and member of Excom,
appears with Ralph Bellamy in ''Wild
Brian Kent" on the Ham Howard
Hits Sunday in Memorial Hall, 7:15
p.m.
Rockwell, a child actor at the film
ing of this western epic, has since
risen to greater fame as "Uncle Bob
by" on the Sunday funnies hour, and
featured performer in the Big Game
Gaieties.
