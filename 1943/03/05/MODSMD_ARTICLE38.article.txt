# Traffic Violators To Go Before Council
## 
### 
All students with traffic violation
tickets must appear before either
Men's Council at 5 p.m. Monday or
Women's Council at 4 p.m. Wednes
day, according to Beth Babcock and
Tex Ritter, council chairmen.
Those who do not appear will have
fines assessed against them which
will be recorded with the Cashier's
Office for collection.
