# Cards To Fence Bears Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Stanford fencing team will
meet the University of California
fencers tomorrow at 1 p.m. in the
Bear gym in the yearly intercolle
giate meet.
The Cards have faced the Bear
fencers three times before, winning
two and dropping one match.
Tomorrow a five-man Stanford
team will be chosen from the first
and second varsity with a sixth man
chosen as alternate.
