# Former Athlete Wins Commission
## 
### 
Kenneth M. Taix, former Farm
track star and one of the first
Stanford students to enlist in the
U.S. Marines following the dec
laration of war, has earned his
second lieutenant's commission in
the famed fighting organization.
Lieut. Taix, 20, social science
majcr, served 13 months as ser
geant in the Marines' Public Re
lations Section at San Francisco
before being called to Quantico,
Va., to work for his commission
with the Candidates' Class.
This phase of the rigorous train
ing completed, he is now enrolled
in Reserve Officers' Class for ad
vanced schooling.
