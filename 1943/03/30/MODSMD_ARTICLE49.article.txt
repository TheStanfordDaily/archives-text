# Races Postponed At Bay Meadows
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, March 29 (U.R)-
Charity races scheduled for Bay
Meadows April 2, 3. and 5, will be
postponed and the immediate future
of the track's racing season will be
debated at an opening meeting in
San Francisco within a few days, it
was announced today.
Rubber director Wililam M. Jef
fers conferred with Jerry Geisler,
chairman of the California racing
board, and while the government
official offered no encouragement for
the proposed 20-day extension of the
season, he left the final decision up
to the Bav Meadows.
