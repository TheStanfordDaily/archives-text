# First Organ Recital To Be Given Today
## 
### 
The first organ recital of the month
will be presented at 4:15 this after
noon in Memorial Church. These re
citals have been a regular feature of
the University calendar.
Joseph M. Running. Assistant Or
ganist, has announced the following
program: "Prelude and Fugue,"
Buxtehude; "Ceres. Queen of the
Night," Rowland Leach; Chorale,
"Sleepers, Wake," Bach; "Chorale,"
Richard Goldman; "Menuet," Cou
perin; "Karelian Dance," Palmgren;
"Fantasie," Frescobaldi; "Fantasie,"
Hindemith.
