# Farm Dames To Hold Meeting on Thursday
## 
### 
i The Stanford Dames will hold their
| first meeting of the Spring Quarter
Thursday, April 1, at 8 at the home
of Mrs. Hubert Heffner, 1550 Waver
ly, Palo Alto. Professor Hoyt H.
Hudson of the English Department
will give a talk on Carl Sandburg
and Edwin Arlington Robinson, both
Pulitzer Prize winners, and will il
lustrate his speech with selections of
their poetry. All wives of graduate
students are urged to come.
