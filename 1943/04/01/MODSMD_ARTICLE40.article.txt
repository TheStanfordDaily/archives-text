# 56 More Students Enroll; Men Ahead

### 
Fifty-six students registered in the
University yesterday bring the total
enrollment for Spring Quarter so
far to 2,737, a decrease of 817 stu
dents from Spring Quarter 1942.

women, since of the 5G new regis
trants, 42 were men and 34 were
women.
A year ago, 2,296 women regis

this quarter. This is a decrease of
816 male students in one year,
whereas there is just one less woman
