# New Library Hours Told by Van Patten
## 
### 
The Library now opens at 9
a.m. daily instead of 8 a.m., an
nounces Dr. Nathan Van Patten,
director of the University Libra
ries.
Library hours now are 9 a.m. to
10 p.m. Monday through Friday,
9 a.m. to 4 p.m. on Saturday, and 2
p.m. to 6 p.m. on Sunday. A study
made last quarter indicated that
the change, necessitated by lack
of personnel, would affect few stu
dents, Dr. Van Patten said.
