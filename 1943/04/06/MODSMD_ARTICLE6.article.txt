# Excom To Discuss Special Elections
## 
### 
Reorganization of the student
body and special elections will
again occupy most of Excommit
tee's time when that group meets
tonight at 7:15 in the executive
committee room.
A date for a special election will
probably be set at tonight's meet
ing, and the amendment to be
voted upon will be discussed fur
ther, according to Janet McClana
han. acting president. She urges
anyone with opinions on that sub
ject to attend the meeting.
Other business on the agenda
concerns appointments for the
War Board, Student Aid Commit
tee. Union Board of Governors
and Board of Athletic Control, and
more discussion on the Sunday
Movie Series.
