# Bowling Singles Set for April 17
## 
### 
The All-University Singles Cham
pionships in Bowling will be held
Saturday night, April 17, at 8 o'clock
at the Indian Bowl in Palo Alto.
All aspirants should get in touch
with either Late Williams or Bill
Allen at the Daily office immediately
as entries close next Tuesday. A
medal will be presented to the win
ner.
The tournament will be a handi
cap affair, with a suitable handicap
for those who have no established
average.
Entry fee will be payable at the
time a contestant signs up and will
be $1.15 per man. This fee will
cover the price of bowling, paying a
foul man, and the purchase of the
medal.
