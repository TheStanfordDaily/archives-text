# Gone to War
## 
### 
ANOTHER Stanford professor has gone to
war. Dr. Jozo Tomasevich, acting assistant
economist at the Food Research Institute, has
been called to Washington by the Board of
Economic Warfare to act as expert consultant
in the field of economic reconstruction of south
eastern Europe.
He has worked with the Food Research In
stitute since 1940 and has been studying inter
national commodity agreements under a $40,000
Rockefeller Foundation grant.
Dr. Tomasevich has written a new book which
the University Press has just published en
titled "International Agreements on Conserva
tion of Marine Resources." He was an econo
mist in the Bank of Yugoslavia before he came
to Stanford.
