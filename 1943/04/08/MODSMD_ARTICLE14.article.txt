# Interfaith Elects Two New Officials
## 
### 
At a meeting held by Interfaith
Council Tuesday afternoon in the
Women's Clubhouse, Charlotte Til
ton was elected vice-president and
Alice Tuttle was elected secretary
treasurer.
The meeting was held under the
direction of Sally Burke, president.
Plans were made for the Easter Sun
rise Service which will be held
Easter Sunday at 7:30 in Frost
Amphitheater. Plans will be com
pleted next week.
