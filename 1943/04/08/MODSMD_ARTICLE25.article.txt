# University Willed $3,000 by Professor
## 
### 
A bequest of $3,000 was left Stan
ford University in the will of Dr.
Lillien Martin, author and former
Stanford psychology professor, who
died at the age of 92 on March 26 in
San Francisco.
The fund was left to the Univer
sity for the establishment of a loan
fund for deserving students, prefer
ably women.
Gifts totaling $48,500 were left to
friends and to Mrs. Clara deGruchy,
a former colleague, to be kept in
trust for use "in mental rehabilita
tion of the old"; according to prin
ciples laid down in two books col
aborated on by Dr. Martin and Mrs.
v.eGruchy.
