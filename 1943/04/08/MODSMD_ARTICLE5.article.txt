# Jean Montgomery Sets Office Hours
## 
### 
President of Women's Council,
Jean Montgomery, has announced
that her office hours for this quar
ter are from 2 to 3 on Monday and
from 5 to (J on Thursday. Her tele
phone number is emergency
number is 9522, hut this number
should he used only in cases of
extreme emergency.
The Council wishes to emphasize
that no girl having a Saturday class
may ask for an overnight leave on
Friday except in case of an emer
gency.
