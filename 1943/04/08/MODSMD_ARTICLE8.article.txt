# Lagunita-Union Dance Date Set
## 
### 
April 24 was set as the date for the
Lagunita-Union spring formal at a
meeting of representatives from the
Lagunita casas and Union units held
recently in the Unioit Bamboo Room.
It will be in the Women's Gym
from 9 until 1 o'clock. Paul Law's
10-piece orchestra and a vocalist
will furnish music.
Bids, which may be purchased by
any Stanford woman, will be $1.75,
