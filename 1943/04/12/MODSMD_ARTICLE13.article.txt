# Rolled Bandages Exceed Quotas
## 
### 
Exceeding its- weekly quota for
the first time, the Red Cross work
room in the Women's Clubhouse last
week turned out 5.518 surgical dress
ings, 18 over their goal. Both 2x2's
and 4x4's were rolled.
An increasing number of students
rolled bandages this week, according
to Mrs. Richard LaPiere of the work
room. On Saturday morning, 26 stu
dents appeared for work.
Starting this week, the workroom
will be open from 4 to 5 p.m. on
Monday, Wednesday, and Friday
