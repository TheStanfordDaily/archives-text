# DAILY SPORTS
## Trackmen Back To Daily Work 
### 
Stanford trackmen went back to
their daily workout sked yesterday,
as San Francisco sportswriters con
tinued to congratulate Pitch John
son on the success of his spur-of
the-moment invitational meet.
Sprinters and high jumpers
worked especially hard attempting
to correct faults which showed up in
Saturday's competition. Johnson in
dicated the squad may hold an in
formal handicap meet this coming
weekend against the high schools in
the vicinity.
The Cards' first dual meet of the
season, scheduled for April 24 against
Fresno State on Angell Field, is not
yet definite, according to Johnson.
May 1 the Indians are supposed to
travel to San Jose for a meet with
the Spartans.
