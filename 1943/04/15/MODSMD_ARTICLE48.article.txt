# Stanford Fencers Compete Friday
## 
### 
Stanford's top fencing trio, Bob
Helliwell, Harvard Lomax, and Car
roll Bradbury, will compete in the
open foils competition to be held at
the Olympic Club Friday night. The
event is part of the American Fenc
ing League championships.
First, second, and third-place win
ners will enter the Pacific Coast in
dividual matches to be held shortly.
Helliwell, Lomax, and Bradbury
were the winners of a recent elimi
nation tourney held among the mem
bers of the fencing team.
