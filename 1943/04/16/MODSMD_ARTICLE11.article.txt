# Frankel To Teach Classics at Cal
## 
### 
Dr. Herman Frankel, of the Clas
sics Department, has been granted a
leave of absence this quarter to fill
the position of Sather Professor of
Classical literature at the University
of California.
Each year the Sather Professor is
chosen from among the foremost
authorities on the classics from uni
versities all over the world. In past
years, the position has been filled by
scholars from several European
countries, Sweden and Denmark, as
well as from leading universities in
the United States.
The courses are taught in Greek
and Latin alternately, and this year
Dr. Herman is using Ovid in his lec
tures on Latin literature.
