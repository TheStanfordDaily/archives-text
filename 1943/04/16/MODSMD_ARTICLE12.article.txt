# Encina Club Gives Dance Tomorrow
## 
### 
Encina will hold its first activity
of the Spring Quarter tomorrow
night with a formal given by the
Encina Club. The dance will cost
all Encinamen 10 cents but will be
open to the rest of the student body
for SI.IO.
Brad Hall and his orchestra will
provide music for the dancers in the
Encina clubroom from 9 till 1.
Helping Chairman Don Bates on
the dance committee are Don Sweet,
publicity; Rodney Morse, decora
tions; Jim Livingston, refreshments;
Claire Benchley, patrons; Martin
Andersen, orchestra; and Don Bates,
clean-up.
Free refreshments will be served.
