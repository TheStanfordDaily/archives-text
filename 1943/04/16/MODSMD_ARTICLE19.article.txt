# Gallery Features Coast Painters
## 
### 
The Stanford Art Gallery is now
featuring a collection of oil paint
ings by prominent artists from
Southern California.
Included are paintings by such
artists as Orrin White, Ruth Lind
say, Frederick Zimmerman, G.
Thompson Pritchard, and a number
of others.
Next month the Stanford galleries
will .show a similar group of pic
tures, now being collected by Pedro
de Lemos from studios in Monterey
and Carmel.
