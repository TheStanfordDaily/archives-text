# 'Dames' To Hear Reading of Play
## 
### 
Members of the reading group of
the Stanford Dames will hear Mrs.
J. B. Wells read a modern play,
"Spring Again," when they meet to
night at 8 o'clock at the home of Mrs.
Charles Coleman, 565 Bowdoin. Mrs.
Carl Lindquist is the new chairman
of the group.
On Friday noons and afternoons,
Dames have been meeting in the
Red Cross workroom on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse for
lunch and a session of rolling band
ages.
