# USO Regulations To Be Given Today
## 
### 
Miss Patricia Davidson, leader of
the Stanford P.W.C.A., and Miss June
Ellis, chairman of the USO group of
the "Y," will lead the discussion at
the first meeting of the course to
train junior hostesses today at 1

p.m. in the "Y" rooms, third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse.
The discussion will deal with
dances and home hospitality and will
present the rules and regulations for
junior hostesses.
