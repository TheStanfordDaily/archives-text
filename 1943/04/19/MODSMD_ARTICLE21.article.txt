# Cal Tennis Squad Smashes Indians
## 
### 
A hard-hitting, controlled Cal ten
| nis team smashed the Stanford In
dians to- the ground Saturday after
noon on the men's courts by taking
every match in two sets except one.
; The score was 8-1.
The single victorious man for the
Stanford team was Ted Myers who,
instead of putting on a show, played
the best tennis of the day by whip
ping Harry Buttimer off the court,
6-4, 6-3.
