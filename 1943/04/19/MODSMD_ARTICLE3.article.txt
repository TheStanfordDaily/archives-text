# ALLIES SHOOT DOWN 30 NAZIS
## 
### 
ALLIED HEADQUART ERS,
NORTH AFRICA—AIIied airfleets
shot down 30 more enemy planes
yesterday and blasted steadily at
Sicilian channel ports defying the
cornered Axis Tunisian armies,
while Allied land forces prepared
for their big push with patrol jabs
all along the 120-mile African front.
The Berlin radio said the fighting
in North Africa "is now in the phase
of preparation preceding the decis
ion for the bridgehead."
