# April 26 Deadline For Certificates
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at
the Registrar's Office Monday, April
26, for all new students, including
those having permit to attend classes,
and all other students whose certifi
cates have expired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
p.m. Monday, will mean a fine of $2:
or, if not filed by May 3, registration
in the University may be cancelled
bv the Committee on Public Health.
