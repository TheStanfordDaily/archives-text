# MILLS ART EXHIBITION
## 
### 
The works of the Mills College art
faculty are now on exhibit in the
Mills College Art Gallery, and Stan
ford students are invited to attend.
The gallery is open on Wednesday,
Friday, and Sunday from 2 to 5 p.m.
until May 28. A special reception
has been set for Sunday, May 2,
from 3 to 5 p.m.
