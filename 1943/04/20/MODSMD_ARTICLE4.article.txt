# Reservist Test Scheduled Today
## 
### 
Navy and Marine Corps reservists
will report to Memorial Hall at 9
this morning to take an all-day quali
fying examination to determine
whether they are qualified to bene
fit by further college instruction.
AH V-l men in their sixth quarter
or above and all Marine reservists in
their sixth quarter or below will be
required to take the test, which lasts
until 11 in the morning, and from 2
to 4:15 in the afternoon.
Reservists are advised by Dr. Karl
M. Cowdery, armed services repre
sentative, to bring pencils in pref
erence to pens and to get a good
night's sleep the night before.
