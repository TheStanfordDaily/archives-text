# Groups Urged to Sign For May Spring Sing
## 
### 
Campus singing groups are
urged hy Cardinals to sign up for
the Spring Sing to be held in the
Greek amphitheater Wednesday.
May 5, at 7:15. Singers may sign
at any time at the A.S.S.I', office.
"We would like to have singing
groups learn the choral arrange
ments of the Star Spangled Banner
and the school hymn," said Chair
man Vic Ferrari, "The Cardinals
are planning to open and close the
all-campus sing with the above
songs.
