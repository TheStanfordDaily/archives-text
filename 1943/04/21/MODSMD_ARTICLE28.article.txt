# Stanford Dames Elects Officers
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames, an organiza
tion for the wives of Stanford stu
dents, recently held an election of
new officers. The new officers are
Mrs. Clifford E. Robinson, president;
Mrs. Charles Coleman, vice-presi
dent; Mrs. Harold Rose, recording
secretary; Mrs. Wilbur Dutton, cor
responding secretary; Mrs. Adrian
Serb, treasurer; and Mrs. Maxwell
Doty, tea chairman.
