# Junior Prom Bids On Sale at A.S.S.U.
## 
### 
Bids for the May Day Prom,
planned by the Junior Class for
May 1 in the Women's Gym, are
selling in the A.S.S.U. office. Yes
terday's sales indicate a campus am
bition to attend one more Junior
Prom, according to Ted Eberle,
Prom chairman.
Ticket sales this week are open
only to Class of '44 members but
will be open to the entire campus
next Wednesday. An over-all limit
of 350 couples has been set.
Gary Nottingham and his 12-
piece band have been signed for the
event. Mona Low will be featured
as the vocalist.
