# Senior Life Saving Course Offered
## 
### 
A concentrated course in Senior
Life Saving will be given within
the next two weeks if there is a
sign-up of at least 12 by this after
noon.
This opportunity is being given for
anyone who wishes to take the Pre
liminary Training Course for Water
Safety Instructors to be given the
four Saturday mornings in May. This
will be immediately followed by the
Instructors' course from May 24 to
28.
Call the War Board Office to sign
up today if you are interested.
