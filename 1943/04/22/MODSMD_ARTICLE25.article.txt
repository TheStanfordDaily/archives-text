# Government Needs Farm Specialists
## 
### 
Agricultural marketing specialists
in many commodities are being
sought for civilian war service, the
Civil Service Commission an
nounced. Appointments will be made
in foreign countries, as well as
throughout the United States, and
in Washington, D.C.
Persons are needed to work with
marketing problems as affected by
Lend-Lease, food distribution, and
other war activities. Applicants must
have had at least three years of ex
perience, college training, or a com
bination of the two, in marketing
agricultural products.
Applications will be accepted at
the Civil Service Commission, Wash
ington, D.C. Further information
may be obtained at first and second
class post offices.

A $25 War Bond can be bought
for $18.75.
