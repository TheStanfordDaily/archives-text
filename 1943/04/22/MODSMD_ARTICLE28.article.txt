# Reed Discusses Tropical Disease
## 
### 
A thorough understanding of trop
ical diseases by American doctors
and the public is basically important
in winning the war, Dr. Alfred C.
Reed, associate clinical professor,
told an audience at the Stanford
School of Medicine Friday night.
"Tropical medicine comes to town
in this war with the return of mili
tary personnel carrying infection."
After the war it will still menace
through military personnel, travel
ers, and enormously increased air
transportation, he declared.
Dr. Reed advocated a four-point
program to combat spread of the
disease. It featured instruction of
medical students and the public in
the value of vaccination, and in pub
lic alertness to the importance of the
disease.
