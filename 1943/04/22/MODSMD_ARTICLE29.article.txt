# "DOGS" GET SERVICE
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO (U.R)—For sol
diers who have trouble with their
feet after long marches, the USO
club here has opened a "dog trouble
room" where chiropdists volunteer
their services and give treatment to
service men twice each week.
