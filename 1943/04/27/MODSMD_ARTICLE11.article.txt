# El Tigre Makes Plans for Dance
## 
### 
"Spring Party" is to be the theme
of El Tigre Club's annual spring
dance which will be held Friday
from 8:30 to 11:30 p.m. in the El
Tigre clubroom on Encina Commons.
Entertainment will consist of danc
ing from records, card playing, and
other games. Refreshments will top
off the evening. "This dance will be
as good, if not better, than all the
Tigre dances of the past," promises
Joe McCullough, dance chairman. It
will be an informal affaU*.
