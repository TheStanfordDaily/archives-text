# Major League Ball Games Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Major League baseball clubs'
schedule tomorrow is:
American League:
New York at Boston: Philadelphia
at Washington; St. Louis at Detroit;
and Cleveland at Chicago.
National League:
Chicago at St. Louis; Boston at
New York: Brooklyn at Philadelphia;
and Cincinnati at Pittsburgh.
Pacific Coast League:
San Francisco at Oakland; Seattle
at Sacramento; Los Angeles at Hol
lywood; Portland at San Diego.

Of the 600.000 troops moved by
railroads during the seven weeks
following Pearl Harbor, 75 per cent
