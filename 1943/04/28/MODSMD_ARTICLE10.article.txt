# TOYON PLANS FORMAL
## 
### 
Toyon's annual spring formal will
take place in Toyon Lobby on May
S from 9 to 1. Only Toyon residents
and their guests are invited. Bids
may be obtained from hall officers.
Chairman of the dance is Renez Ent
ner who is being assisted by Don
Craig, Bill Giles, and Tom Carvey.
