# McClanahan Cues To Conference
## 
### 
Entraining for Reno this afternoon
for a convention of student body
officers are Janet McClanahan, pres
ent A.S.S.U. president, and Leslie
Langnecker, former vice president.
The University of Nevada is host
for the convention, which will be
attended by student officers from
Pacific Coast colleges. Stanford's
representatives will return to the
campus Saturday morning.
Vice President Peggy Boothe will
preside over tonight's meeting of
Excom in place of Miss McClanahan.
The meeting will start at 7:15 p.m.
On the agenda are discussions of
the social policy for the Nitery, the
labor problem in the Cellar, and the
Dramatic Council minutes.
