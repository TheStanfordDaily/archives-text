# Chandler To Talk In San Francisco
## 
### 
Dr. L. R. Chandler, dean of the
Stanford School of Medicine, will
present the third of the Popular
Medical Lectures tonight at 8 o'clock
in Lane Hall, San Francisco, on the
subject of how to make more effi
cient use of the doctor's time, and
home care for illness when a doctor
is not immediately available.
Dr. Chandler will discuss the
medical manpower problem consid
ering both medical demands of the
aimed forces and civilian needs.
Lane Hall is on Sacramento Street
near Webster, at the Stanford Medi
cal School.
