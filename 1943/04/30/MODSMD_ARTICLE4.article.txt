# Board and Room Fees Must Be Paid Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day to pay the
various charges involved in regis
tration, such as board and room,
rentals, laboratory fees, and regis
tration: if these charges are not
paid, registration will be cancelled,
according to Frank F. Walker,
financial vice-president.
A 10-day period is allowed after
billing date for students to arrange
for a credit agreement with the
Dean of Men or Dean of Women,
if that is necessary. All statements
must be paid 10 days after they
have been billed. Today is the
deadline. Any students with un
paid bills must go to the account
ing office and see about them if
they wish to remain registered.
