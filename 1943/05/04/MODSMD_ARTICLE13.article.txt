# Naval Reservists Hold Big Meeting
## 
### 
A very important meeting of the
R.O.T.C. Naval Reservists will be
held Thursday night in Room 2 of
the Education Building at 7:30. Lt.
Comdr. McKena, personnel officer at
Moffat Field, will speak on a subject
which is of the utmost interest to
every Navy man. no matter what
branch of the service he is in. The
title of Lt. Comdr. McKena's speech
is "Rocks and Shoals," which has to
do with the regulations for the entire
Navy.
Every member of the Naval Re
serve is urged to attend by Comdr.
Hallan who is in charge of the cam
pus group.
