# SURGICAL DRESSINGS
## 
### 
There were 1,220 surgical dress
ings made yesterday in the Surgical
Dressing room, according to Mrs.
Richard T. LaPierre.
There were 35 students working
yesterday.

The pool in the women's gym will
be open from 3 to 5 on Sundays. It
is open to both men and women but
the men must be guests, that is, no
men can come alone.
