# Marines To Hear Major Ingram
## 
### 
Major William Ingram, U.S.M.C.,
will speak to the Stanford Marine
reservists May 12, in Room 2 of the
Education building at 7:30 p.m.
In an informal discussion concern
ing the college they will attend, the
courses they will take, and when
they will receive their orders, Major
Ingram will attempt to answer ques
tions of each of the 104 Marine re
servists at Stanford concerning their
future after this quarter.
Major Ingram will also talk about
the officers training school at Quan
tico, Va., when they may attend, and
the courses offered.
It has been announced that there
will be no P.E. 152R R.O.T.C. Ma
rine Program this quarter.
