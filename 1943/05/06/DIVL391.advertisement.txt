MOTHER'S DAY MEANS
SO MUCH THIS YEAR!
Next to their sons who are in the thick of it, the
mothers who see them off to war are doing the big
gest job of all. Not only in their great personal sacri
fice, but in the way they have pitched ihto work on
the home front ... in the home, in volunteer jobs and
in defense plants! Millions of sons are too busy, too
far away, to send gifts this Mother's Day ... so if you
know a soldier's or a sailor's mother . . . take this
occasion to send her a remembrance in his place.
A little thing, perhaps . . . but how much it will mean!
