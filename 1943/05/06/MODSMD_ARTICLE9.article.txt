# Dean Yost To Talk To U.S.O. Course
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will speak to the
members of the second USO course
being given this quarter today at 5
p.m. in the "Y" rooms, top floor of
the Women's Clubhouse. Dean
Yosh will discuss "Conversation
Openers" and the philosophy behind
being a junior hostess.
This second course is being given
for those girls who are unable to at
tend regular Friday meetings of the
first group.
