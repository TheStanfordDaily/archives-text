# Navigation Course Scheduled in Paly
## 
### 
A course to provide training in
, methods of air navigation will begin
at Palo Alto High School, Room 141,
j tonight at 7:15 p.m. and will con
tinue on Tuesdays and Thursdays
for seven weeks.
Included in the course is training
in pilotage, dead reckoning, and
radio navigation. Mr. Howard Arm-
I strong, llight leader of the Civil Air
Patrol and instructor in the Univer
sity of California Extension Divi
sion, will conduct the air navigation
course, sponsored by the Santa Clara
County Flight of the Civil Air Pa
trol. Further information can be ob
tained from Mr. Ray Ruppel, evening
: school principal.
