# STANFORD DAMES
## 
### 
The Cooking group of the Stanford
Dames will meet tonight at 7:45 at
the home of Mrs. Eugene L. Grant.
2045 Tasso street, Palo Alto. The
subject of the meeting will be can
ning, and all members are urged to
bring their favorite recipes.
Mrs. Marvin Bidstrup is chairman
of the affair.
