# Soldiers Invited To Square Dance
## 
### 
Servicemen on the campus are
particularly invited to the square
dance session tonight at 7:30 in the
Women's Gym, according to Mrs.
Miriam Lidster, instructor for the
group, which meets regularly from
7:30 to 9 p.m.
A special invitation is also ex
tended to campus women, for there
is a shortage of partners. In addi
tion to square dances, the group does
polkas, schottisches and old-fash
ioned waltzes.
