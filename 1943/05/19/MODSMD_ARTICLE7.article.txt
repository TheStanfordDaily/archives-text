# Excom To Discuss Summer Concerts
## 
### 
Discussion of a summer concert
series heads the agenda for Excom
meeting tonight at 7:15 in the Excom
room. Students and servicemen are
invited to participate in the session.
Betty May Hale will give a report
on the War Board's activities this
quarter. Appointments for the stu
dent aid committee and the Sunday
movie series will be made at the
meeting.
To be presented for Excom's ap
proval is the plan for a Committee of
Nine to handle social regulation
cases for all the sororities which was
approved by Women's Conference
Monday afternoon.
