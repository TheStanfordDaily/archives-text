recently released an article on the
ingenuity and enterprise displayed
by citizens of the Allied Nations, and j
according to this release, the Allied
peoples believe in the old saying
that "When there's a will, there's j
a way."
The fact that space and materials j
are almost always greatly reduced
in comparison to what they used to
be, some Americans have developed
a new type of "streamlined" furni- j
ture. The arms of a chair are short
ened, the seat is raised, and it be- i
comes a dining, desk, or lounge
chair.
And every time a fresh supply of |
nail polish for girls in the service j
comes into a certain British canteen, !
there is a tremendous rush for it— |
by the men. It seems that one ap
plication of colorless fingernail var
nish will keep a brilliant sheen on
buttons and even toecaps.
