# Stanford Dames Group Will Meet
## 
### 
The bridge group of the Stanford
Dames is meeting at the home of
Mrs. Isaac J. Quillen, 745 French
man's Road on the campus, on Mon
day, May 24. at 8 p.m.
The reading group met yesterday
at the home of Mrs. Carl Lindquist,
9 Perry Lane, Menlo Park. Mrs.
Charles L. Colman reviewed "See
Here, Private Hargrove!" by Marian
Hargrove.
