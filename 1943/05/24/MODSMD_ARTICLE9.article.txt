# Roy Petersen Hurt In Auto Accident
## 
### 
J. Roy Petersen, Stanford Pub
licity director, was seriously injured
Saturday afternoon when he was
knocked down by an automobile in
downtown Palo Alto.
Petersen, who was dragged 25 feet
by the automobile, suffered fractured
ribs and numerous bruises. He was
taken to the Palo Alto Hospital,
where his condition was reported to
be as good as could be expected.
