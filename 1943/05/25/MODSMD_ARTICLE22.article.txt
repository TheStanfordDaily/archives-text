# State Board Seeks Laboratory Aides
## 
### 
Applications for civil service lab
oratory helpers and assistants must
be Hied by June 1 at the office of
the State Personnel Board in Los
Angeles, San Francisco, or Sacra
mento, according to an announce
ment received here. Examinations
will be given June 19.
High school graduates who have
had at least one laboratory course,
college students with six semester
hours in laboratory courses, and
others who have had laboratory ex
perience are eligible.
The pay for laboratory assistant is
$125 a month and for laboratory
helpers, $105. Employment is prin
cipally on the campus of the Uni
versity of California in Berkeley in
the bacteriological laboratory of the
State Department of Public Health.
