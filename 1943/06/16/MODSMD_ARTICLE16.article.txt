# Cross Country
## 
### 
Stanford's cross country team,
paced by Larry Gray, Bruce Meyer,
and Rob Raitt, closed their season
by taking a close third in the Coast
championships held at Spokane.
Gray led the Indian runners to the
tape, placing fourth in the field.
The Big Meet title went to Cali
fornia, 18-39. Gray, Meyer, and
Floyd Cuff.
