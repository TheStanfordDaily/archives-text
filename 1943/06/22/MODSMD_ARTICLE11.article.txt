# Marine Station Gets New Head
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur of
Stanford University announced today
the appointment of Dr. Lawrence R.
Blinks, professor of biology, as di
rector of the Hopkins Marine Station.
Professor Blinks will succeed Dr.
Walter K. Fisher, who becomes pro
fessor of biology emeritus at the end
of the current academic year, August
31.
Chancellor Wilbur also announced
the appointment of Professor Edwin
A. Cottrell as dean of the School of
Social Sciences. Professor Cottrell,
head of the department of political
science, has been chairman of the
executive committee of the School
of Social Sciences.
