# Senior Class Fund Receives Donation
## 
### 
Following a precedent established
by the Stanford senior class of 1940,
the class of 1943 has contributed the
remainder of its class fund to the
Senior Class Scholarship Fund,
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur an
nounced today.
The exact amount of donation
will not be known until the end of
the month, University officials said.
Previous classes have contributed
over $1,000 each to the fund, which
now totals $5,195.
The income from the fund, now
about $100 a year, maintains scholar
ships for superior undergraduate
students. Elsbeth McCollum of Palo
Alto is the recipient for the coming
year.
