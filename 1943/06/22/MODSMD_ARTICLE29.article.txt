# Tryouts Continue Tonight for Play
## 
### 
Further tryouts for "Squaring the
Circle," one of the Stanford Players'
two Summer Quarter productions,
will be held tonight at 7:30 in the
Little Theater of the Memorial Hall.
"Squaring the Circle'' will be pre
sented July 14 to 17. The play is a
Soviet farce comedy about two young
married couples living in one room
in a tenement building and the com
plications involved.
Tryouts are open to any student.
The choice for the second produc
tion will be announced later and will
be given August 11 to 14 in the Little
Theater.
