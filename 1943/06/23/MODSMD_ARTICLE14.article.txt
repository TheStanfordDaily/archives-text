# Open Hours Set At Women's Pool
## 
### 
The pool at the Women's Gymnas
ium will be open to members of the
faculty and their families and to
men and women students on Sun
days from 3 to 5 p.m. A fee of 15
cents will be charged everyone ex
cept registered women students.
Open hours for recreational swim
ming during the week for women
students are Monday, Tuesday, and'
Thursday at 5 p.m.; Wednesday at
4 p.m.; and Friday at 2 p.m. There
is no fee for these hours.
The pool will be open to women
employees of the University and
wives of men students at noon Mon
day through Friday. The charge is
$3.50 for the quarter or 15 cents a
swim.
