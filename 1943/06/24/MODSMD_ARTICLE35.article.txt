# WOMEN'S TENNIS TOURNEY
## 
### 
Women may sign up for the all-
University women's tennis tourna
ment today, tomorrow, and Monday.
Signup sheets are posted in Roble
and Lagunita, and in the Gym. Any
one who plays tennis is eligible and
is urged to enter. Virginia Hobbs is
in charge of the signups.

One $18.75 War Bond will supply
a Navy flier with a fur-lined flying
jacket.
