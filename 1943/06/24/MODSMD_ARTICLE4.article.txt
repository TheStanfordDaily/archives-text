# ASTP Soldiers To Be Promoted
## 
### 
When they move up from the basic
phase to the advanced phase of the
training program, privates stationed
with the Army Specialized Training
units on campus will be promoted to
private first class, the War Depart
ment announced today.
Men who are assigned directly to
the advanced phase without basic
instruction also will be promoted to
private first class, Army officials
said.
Contracts to be negotiated with
five more colleges for participation
in the specialized training program
will bring to 150 the number in the
program by the end of July, it was
announced.
