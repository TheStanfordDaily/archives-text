# Violinist To Assist Allen in Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen. University or
ganist. assisted by Elizabeth Peirce
Kincade, violinist, will present a
recital at 7:30 p.m. Sunday in Me
morial Church.
Music played will be by Johann
Sebastian Bach.
Included on the program will be:
Toccata and Fugue in D minor;
Sonata for Violin in A major—an
dante, presto; Trio-Sonata for Or
gan, No. 3, in D minor—allegro,
adagio e dolce, allegro; Concerto for
Violin, in E major—adagio, allegro
assai; Siciliano in G minor: Toccata
in F major.
