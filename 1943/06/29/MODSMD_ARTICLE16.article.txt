# Columbia Gives Stanford $5,000
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur
j announced today that the Columbia
I Foundation had contributed $5,000
to Stanford University to be used by
j Dean Grayson N. Kefauver of the
I School of Education in creating a
' permanent postwar organization for
| international education.
Dean Kefauver is chairman of a
| liaison committee for international
I education. The committee, which he
organized, is composed of repre
sentatives from leading American
organizations of educators inter
i ested in international education.
