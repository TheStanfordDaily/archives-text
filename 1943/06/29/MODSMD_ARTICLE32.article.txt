# Stanford Woman Gets Commission
## 
### 
Marion V. Littlefield. former Stan
ford student, received a third ofii
cer's commission recently in the
WAAC and is now the head of the
photography laboratory at March
Field, Southern California air base.
Previous to joining the WAACs
last October, Lieutenant Littlefield
was doing graduate work at Stan
ford, where she had received both

her B.A. and M.A. degrees. When
on campus, she was a member of the
Alpha Omicron Pi sorority.
She also taught school and served
as dean of girls at Mountain View
High School, and before the war she
conducted a tour through Europe.
Besides her ability in photography
and personnel work, she is of special
value to the WAACs because of her
knowledge of several European
languages.
