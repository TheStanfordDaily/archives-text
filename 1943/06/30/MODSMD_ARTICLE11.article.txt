# Tax Stamps Must Be On Autos by Thursday
## 
### 
Federal tax stamps for automo
biles are on sale at the Postoffiee
for So. These stamps must be
placed on all cars tomorrow as the
old stumps expire tonight. New
tax stamps do not expire until
June 30. 1944.
