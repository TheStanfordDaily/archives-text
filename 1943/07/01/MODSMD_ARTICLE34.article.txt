# Larson, Straub Named to Excom
## 
### 
Jean Larson and Charlie Straub
were named to the summer execu
tive committee to fill the vacant
junior woman and senior man posi
tions at last night's meeting of Ex
com.
Reporting on soldier representa
tion on Excom, Janet McClanahan
said that the plan had the approval
of the commanding officers. Cadet
captains of the permanent ASTU
companies will attend the meeting
next week.
Dates granted included: July 3.
Klahoya Nitery Dance: July 9,
W.A.A. Co-Recreational Night; July
17, Buchanan Hall Dance; July 24,
Tyler Hall Dance, Fillmore Hall
Dance: July 31, Monroe Hall Dance.
