# Molnar To Form SummerSymphony
## 
### 
A symphony orchestra will be or
ganized at Stanford this quarter
composed of soldiers, regular stu
dents, and some musicians from Palo
Alto, according to Ferenc Molnar,
conductor of the Stanford Orchestra.
All students or soldiers interested
are requested to meet at the music
room behind the Art Gallery, Satur
day at 3:30 p.m. Instruments will be
loaned to students not having any.
