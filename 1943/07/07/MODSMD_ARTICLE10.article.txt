# 3,030 Stanfordites Serve Uncle Sam
## 
### 
According to the June issue of the
Stanford Alumni Review, there are
now 3.030 Stanford alumni and fac
ulty members in the armed forces
of the United States.
Of these Stanford service men,
1.756 are in the Army. 1.159 in the
Navy. 90 in the Marines, and 25 in
the Coast Guard. Ot the combined
total, 2,188 are commissioned officers,
while the other 842 (including many
reservists) are enlisted men.
