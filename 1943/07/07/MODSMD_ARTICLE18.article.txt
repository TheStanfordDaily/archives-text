# Drama Tryouts To Start Tonight
## 
### 
Final tryouts for the second speech
and drama summer show will be
held tonight at 7:30 p.m. in Room
202, Memorial Hall.
The production will be three of
Noel Coward's "Tonight at 8:30" one
acts—"Ways and Means," "Hands
Across the Seas," and "Family Al
bum." The plays will be given in
August and will be directed by John
Sollers.
"While there are. 27 parts to be
cast in all," Mr. Sollers said, "we
can get along with five men and four
women by using the same people in
each play. However, we hope to be
able to use as many actors as pos
sible."
First tryouts for the production
were held last night. Anyone inter
ested in trying out but who can't
come to the tryouts tonight should
get in touch with Mr. Sollers as re
hearsals will not start until after the
final performance of "Squaring the
Circle."
