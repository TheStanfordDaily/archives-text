# Students Invited To Friday Frolic
## 
### 
In keeping with Stanford's on
campus entertainment drive. W.A.A.
and Cardinals will sponsor a co
recreational evening Friday consist
ing of dancing and games, so that
students will have some place to go
on campus.
There will be folk and square
dancing until 9 o'clock when a shift
to ballroom dancing will be made.
For those who are so interested the
volleyball courts outside will be
open.
The dance is free and open to all
Stanford students, with or without
dates.
