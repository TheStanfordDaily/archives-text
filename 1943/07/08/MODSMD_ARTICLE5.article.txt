# Tillson Talkies Play Sunday
## 
### 
''Knight Without Armor" will be
the feature of Tillson TaLkies this
Sunday evening at the Memorial
Auditorium. Starring Marlene Diet
rich and Robert Donat, the picture is
an adaptation of the novel written
by Eric Knight, with a background
of the Russian revolutionary situa
tion around 1917.
Donat plays the role of a peasant
who rises to power and later be
comes the benefactor of a Russian
woman, Marlene Dietrich, who has
escaped from the revolution.
Two selected short subjects will
be shown along with the movie, and
as usual, the admission price is 20
cents. Opening time has been
changed from 7:15 to 7:30.
Due to shipping trouble in the
East. Nan Tillson has been having
trouble obtaining the last two in
stallments of "The Clutching Hand,"
die current serial.
