# Physical Society Meets Saturday
## 
### 
The 256 th meeting of the American
Physical Society will be held at
Stanford in Room 372 of the Physics
Department on Saturday, July 10,
at 10 a.m.
Following the reading of four con
tributed papers, two invited papers
are scheduled to be presented by L.
Martin and Hardin Jones.
After luncheon, during which sev
eral short speeches will be made,
the session will continue with a paper
read by K. K. Darrow.
At the close of the meeting, tea
will be served by the Physics De
partment.
