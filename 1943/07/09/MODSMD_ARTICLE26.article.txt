# Contempt Is a Weapon
## 
### 
(Editor's Note: The Office of War Information has collected a number
of anecdotes which the people of occupied Europe are telling to ridicule
their masters. The following is an example).
An old Dutch woman was arrested for listening to 8.8.C.
broadcasts from London and hailed before a Nazi court.
"Why did you do this?" asked the judge.
"Oh, but your Honor," she replied, "Hitler told us he would
be in London in October, 1940. I have been listening every day
since then. I would not want to miss der Fuehrer."
