# Vaccination Slips Due on July 15
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due
at the Registrar's Office on Thurs
day, July 15, for all new students,
including those having permits to
attend classes, and all other stu
dents whose certificates have ex
pired.
Failure to file certificates by 5
o'clock on Thursday will result in
a fine of 82; or. if not filed by July
22, registration in the University
will be subject to cancellation by
the Committee on Public Health-
