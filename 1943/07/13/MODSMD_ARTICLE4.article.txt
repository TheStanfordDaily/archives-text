# Art Exhibit To Be Held in Main Libe
## 
### 
In commemoration of Bastile Day,
a special exhibit of the art and archi
tecture of pre-war France has been
arranged in the main corridor of the
Stanford University Library for
Wednesday.
Among the exhibits are 11 volumes
by Houvet on the Cathedral of
Chartres,
Other exhibits are 11 portfolios on
"Masterpieces of French Art," the
gift of Dr. Clelia D. Mosher; "A His
tory of French Painting" by C. H.
Stranahan, inscribed to Senator and
Mrs. Stanford by the author; and
"Modern French Masters" by John
C. Van Dyke, the gift of Professor
E. M. Hulme.
