# Campus Soldiers To Receive Dailies
## 
### 
After several weeks of plan
ning, a scheme has been devised
to provide a copy of The Daily
each morning for every civilian and
soldier student on the campus.
Beginning today. Dailies will be
distributed in boxes at the biology,
history, and engineering corners,
at at the Library, Education Bldg.,
Cellar and Post Office. Civilian
students, STAKs, and ASTU's may
obtain them at these places.
Distribution will be at approxi
mately 9 a.m.. and the circulation
of The Daily will be doubled.
No more deliveries will be made
at the women's living groups.
