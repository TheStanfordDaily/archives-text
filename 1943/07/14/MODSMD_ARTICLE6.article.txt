# Soldiers To Attend Lagunita Informal
## 
### 
Next Sunday afternoon from 3 to
5 p.m. Lagunita will hold an in
formal dance in the social rooms of
each casa. Games, cards, and ping
pong will also be offered as enter
tainment. About 240 guests are ex
pected including Lagunita girls.
So that this number will not be
exceeded, soldiers are being asked
to sign up to go to the dance. The
social chairmen from each casa will
act as hostesses at the dance. The
affair is no-date.
