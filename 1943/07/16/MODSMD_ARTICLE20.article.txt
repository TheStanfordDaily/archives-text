# Hoppenot Restores Republic in Antilles
## 
### 
PORT DE FRANCE, Martinique—
Henri-Enetienne Hoppenot, as his
! first act of authority in the French
Antilles, has restored the republic
government and abrogated the laws
of the administration of Admiral
Georges Robert, the former pro-
Vichy commandant.
Hoppenot, director of civil services
of the French military mission in
Washington who was named by the
French committee of national libera

tion in Algiers to assume authority
from Robert, arrived in Martinique
with his pawty yesterday—Bastille
Day.
