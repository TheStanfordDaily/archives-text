# BOND SALES TOTAL $637
## 
### 
War Bond and Stamp sales in the
Quad yesterday amounted to $637,
according to the 5 o'clock total. One
1 hundred and twelve dollars worth of
i Stamps were sold, along with a $500
j Bond and a $25 Bond. Every Thurs
day has been designated as Stamp
I Day by the Stanford War Board.
