# Deadline Announced For Handbook Editors
## 
### 
Student Body President Janet
McClanahan announced that to
day is the final deadline for appli
cations for editor of the Stanford
Handbook, 1944-45 edition. The
book, which will be issued next
spring for incoming freshmen, may
be edited by one or two students
with the total salary of $50.
Miss McClanahan emphasized
that applications should be made
promptly at her office in order that
they might be approved at to
night's meeting of Excommittee.
