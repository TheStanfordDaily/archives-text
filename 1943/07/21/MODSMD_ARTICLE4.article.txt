# ASTP Men Take Language Studies
## 
### 
A group of 107 men in the East
Asia and European Language and
Area schools has been assigned to a
classification of 9 L.
Under the ASTP these men who
speak the language fluently and who
know the characteristics of the area
will undertake graduate and inde
pendent study. They will spend no
more than 12 weeks taking special
ized training, and will be transferred
as soon as they are prepared.
Each soldier will be given work
in his own area with lectui'es, semi
nars, conferences, and intensive read
ing.
Included in this group are natives
of Germany, Czechoslovakia, France.
Hungary, Austria, Belgium. Holland,
Greece, and Poland.
