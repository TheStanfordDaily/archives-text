# Tyler To Stage Unique Dance
## 
### 
Tyler Hall will stage the first cos
tume party of the quarter next Sat
urday night. It is to be a "half and
half" dance; that is, costumes will be
both formal and informal. The com
bination promises to be very enter
taining, and, according to Dick Hyde,
social chairman, there will be plenty
of surprises.
The dance, which will last from 9
to 1, is a date affair. A few soldiers
with dates are being invited, but it
will be primarily a freshman group.
Dancing will be to the music of RCA,
and refreshments will be served.
'"It will be a great dance." the Ty
ler Hall publicity committee has an
nounced. With such whole-hearted
enthusiasm behind it the "half and
half" dance should be a unique
event.
