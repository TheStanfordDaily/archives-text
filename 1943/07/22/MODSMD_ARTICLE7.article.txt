# Organist Schedules French Program
## 
### 
Modern French music will con
stitute the program to be given by
Mr. Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist. at his usual recital this after
noon at 4:15 in the Memorial Chapel.
Compositions to be played include
Claude Debussy's "Cortege, from the
Petite Suite." Louis Vierne's "Clair
de lune" and "Carillon," and Arthur
Honegger's "Chorale." Mr. Allen
will also play "Ariel" and "Romance
sans paroles" by Joseph Bonnet and
"Toccata in B minor" by A. Barie.
Mr. Allen's program for his Sunday
recital will be announced in tomor
row's Daily.
