# Blood Bank Requests Donors for August 5
## 
### 
All persons desiring; to donate a
pint of their blood to the Red Cross
Blood Bank are requested by the
Palo Alto branch of the Red Cross
to sign up before Aug. 5. The Palo
Alto branch is now taking applica
tions for the next blood donation
day, Aug. 5.
All students under 21 must have
their parents' consent before sign
ing up. They must also weigh at
least 110 pounds. Transportation
for all students meeting the re
quirements will be furnished by
the Red Cross.
A special car will take all donors
from the campus to Palo Alto, and
back.
