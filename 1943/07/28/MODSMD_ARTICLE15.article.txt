# Formal To Feature Weidner's Band
## 
### 
Art Weidner and hi.s 10-piece or
chestra have been engaged to play
for the Roble-Lagunita Formal to be
held Aug. 7, according to Peggy
Shaw, co-chairman from Lagunita.
Frances Stong, Lagunita ticket
chairman, announced that tickets for
the dance will go on sale either to
night or tomorrow morning for $1.75.
Names of the ticket committee for
both Lagunita and Roble will be
published in the Daily tomorrow.
The affair will be held from 9 lo 1
in the Women's Gym.
