# FITZSIMMONS GOES TO PHILS
## 
### 
CHICAGO—The Brooklyn Dodgers
today announced the unconditional
release of Fred Fitzsimmons, so the
veteran pitcher can take the post of
manager of the Philadelphia Phillies.
He will take charge of the Phillies
tomorrow, replacing Bucky Harris,

The nine counties, comprising the
San Francisco Bay Region, cover
nearly 7.000 square miles, an area
larger than Rhode Island and Con- ;
necticut combined.
