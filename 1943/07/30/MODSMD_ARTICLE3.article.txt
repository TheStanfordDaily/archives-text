# Lagunita Invites Campus to Dance
## 
### 
Stanford men and women are in
vited to dance to jukebox music to
night at the Nitery from 8:30 to 11:30
at a dance sponsored by Lagunita
Court according to Daisy Day, chair
man of the affair. Besides dancing,
there will be ping-pong tables set up
in the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse and also bridge games
going on. Cokes and cookies will be
served as refreshments.
'"Everyone is invited to attend this
dance," said Miss Day."and it isn't
necessary to have a date. Dates and
dateless are very welcome."
