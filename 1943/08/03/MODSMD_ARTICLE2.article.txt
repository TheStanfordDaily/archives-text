# Music Division Plans Lecture
## 
### 
Alfred Frankenstein, music editor
of the San Francisco Chronicle, will
speak Wednesday night in the Edu
cation Building auditorium on "The
Anti-Romantic Tendency in Modern
Music." The lecture, open to the
public, will begin at 7:30.
According to Dr. Warren D. Allen,
head of the Division of Music, Mr.
Frankenstein will discuss the great
changes in style that composers made
during and after the last war. Those
composers include Stravinsky. Hon
neger, and Schonberg.
