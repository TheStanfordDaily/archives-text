# Final Staff Meeting
## 
### 
THE Daily Shack will be the scene of the final meeting of all
members of edit staff for Summer Quarter, 1943, today at
2:15. Old business and new business regards the Friday picnic
and the Victory Carnival booth. Afternoon Club will be supplied
by Bill Struthers and Adolph (Barney) Gugel. Edit Board will
meet at 1:30.
