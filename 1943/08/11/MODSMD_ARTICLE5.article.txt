# Stanford Excom To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Whether or not the Concert Series
will be continued this year is to be
the chief topic of discussion at the
meeting of the Executive Committee
tonight at 7:15 in the Excom Room.
The first concert, held last Thursday,
was on an experimental basis.
Also on the agenda is the consider
ation of Associated Students' budget
for the next year and of the proposal
that student body cards entitling
holders to all student privileges be
sold to the soldiers next quarter. Any
student or soldier is welcome to at
tend the meeting and express opin
ions, according to Janet McClanahan,
ASSU president.
