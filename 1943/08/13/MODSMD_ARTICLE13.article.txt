# ASTU To Receive Credits for Work
## 
### 
All soldiers now in the ASTU,
upon completion of thfeir course of
training at Stanford, will receive
official transcripts of credit which
will be accepted at any university,
according to Dr. J. P. Mitchell, reg
istrar of the University.
The number of units each soldier
receives varies wilh his particular
course of study, but after he re
ceives his grades he will get full
college credit for the work done.
As all soldiers in the ASTU are
Stanford students, they may return
after the war and finish the required
number of units and receive degrees
from the University, Dr. Mitchell
said.
