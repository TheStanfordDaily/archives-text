# French Editions Given to Library
## 
### 
Three eighteenth century French
first editions of books by French au
thors have been given to the Stan
ford University Library by Sylvan
Bier of Carmel, Director Nathan Van
Patten has announced.
The books are "De L'Esprit des
Loix," in two volumes, by Montes
quieu, French philosopher and jurist,
published in Geneva in 1748, and"De
L'Esprit," one voulme, by Claude
Adrien Helvetius, published in Paris
is 1758. A penciled notation in the
latter book says that most of the
edition was burnt as heretical.
