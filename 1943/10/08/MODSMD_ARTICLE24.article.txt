# Med School Has New Professor
## 
### 
Dr. Oscar Ivanissevich, director of
the Institute of Clinical Surgery of
the University of Buenos Aires,
joined the faculty of the Stanford
University School of Medicine as an
exchange professor for the months of
October and November.
Dr. Leo Eloesser, of the Stanford
faculty, is now occupying Dr. Ivanis
sevich's chair at the University of
Buenos Aires.
Dean L. R. Chandler, of the School
of Medicine, announced today that
Dr. Ivanissevich will give the Hertz
stein lectures November 15-19 on the
subject of "Echinoccsis."
