# Stanford Receives One Million Dollars
## 
### 
Chancellor Emeritus Ray Lyman
Wilbur announced that Stanford
University had received one million
dollars in gifts during the fiscal year
ending August 31, 1943.
More than 2,500 persons from 45
states, 3 territories, and 4 foreign
countries contributed to this year's
total, which brought Stanford's gifts
for the last three years to $4,000,000.
