# Tresidder To Speak On Convocation Day
## 
### 
New students will be honored at
a convocation service on Sunday,
October 17, at 11 o'clock. Dr. Tresid
der will be the speaker at the cere
mony. The processions will be led
by Mary Anne Walker and Dick Wil
bur. Both old and new students on
campus are invited to the service.
