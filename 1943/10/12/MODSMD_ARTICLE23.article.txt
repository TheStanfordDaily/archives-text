# Heffner To Lead Public Exercises
## 
### 
Appointment of Capt. Hubert C.
Heffner, professor of dramatics and
head of the Department of Speech
and Drama, as chairman of the Stan
ford Committee on Public Exercises,
has been announced by President
Donald B. Tresidder.
Captain Heffner succeeds Professor
Edgar E. Robinson, chairman of the
Department of History. The former
had been in training at a military
government school at Charlottesville,
Va.
Mrs. Yvonne Harley Dane will act
as secretary to the committee in
place of Miss Mildred Worswick, who
has joined the WAVEs with a com
mission of lieutenant (j.g.).
