# Weigle Appointed Publicity Director
## 
### 
Clifford F. Weigle has been ap
pointed director of publicity, re
placing Roy Petersen, who was in
jured in an automobile accident last
May.
Mr. Weigle graduated from Stan
ford with the class of '29. He was
assistant city editor of the San Fran
cisco News before becoming assistant
professor of journalism. He has taken
leave from the Journalism Depart
ment for the year '43-'44.
