# Trade Ship Teia Brings Mydans
## 
### 
Mrs. Everett W. Smith of Stanford!
received good news yesterday in the
form of a statement from the State
Department that her son-in-law and
daughter, Carl and Shelley Mydans;
were among 1,236 Americans re
leased from the Japanese in a pris
oner of war trade.
Mr. and Mrs. Mydans are members;
of the staff of Life Magazine. They
were captured in Manila but later
taken to Hongkong where they were,
held until their release to the Teia.
Mrs. Mydans is the former Shelley
Smith, daughter of the late head of
the Stanford Division of Journalism.
