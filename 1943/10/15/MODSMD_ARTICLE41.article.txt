# Ex-Daily Editor Home on Furlough
## 
### 
First Lieut. Charles Bull, formex'
Daily editor and now in the Marine
Corps, has returned to his home in
San Mateo for a short furlough after
serving as an instructor in Corpus
Christi, Texas. Bull succeeded Wil
son Cannon as editor and put out
Volume 100 of the Daily in 1942.
Lieutenant Bull was among the
fh'st of Stanford men to join tip in
the armed forces, being a member of
the well-known Flying Indians out
fit from Stanford.
