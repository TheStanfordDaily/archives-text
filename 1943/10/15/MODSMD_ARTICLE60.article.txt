# Sunday Meeting
## 
### 
FIRST Daily staff meeting of the quarter will
be held Sunday at 12 noon in the Shack.
Edit board hopes that all Spring and Summer
Quarter staff members will gather round at
this time when a regular meeting time will
be set. Lists of promotions and staff changes
will be announced, and beats will be given out.
