# Tresidder Speaks
## 
### 
The annual Convocation Service
was presented yesterday morning in
Memorial Church, with President
Donald B. Tresidder delivering the
address. According to D. Elton True
blood, chaplain, 2.000 people were
present at the service.
The service was led by Janet Mc-
Clanahan, president of the associated
students; Dean John Bunn and Dean
Mary Yost, as well as Tresidder and
Trueblood.
In the choral music, Mrs. Warren
D. Allen sang the solo part in the
first anthem, and Pvt. Leslie Whittor,
in the second.
