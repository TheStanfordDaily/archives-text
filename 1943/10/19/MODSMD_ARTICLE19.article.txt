# Farm War Board Gets Blue Ribbon
## 
### 
The Stanford War Board was
awarded a blue ribbon for their ex
hibit Sunday at the Community Fair
in Palo Alto of vegetables grown at
the Convalescent Home this summer.
The fair was held at the Community

Center, and the War Board's exhibit
was part of the vegetable section.
Sam McDonald, caretaker at the
Con Home, is planning to give the
blue ribbon to the Con Home chil
dren.
