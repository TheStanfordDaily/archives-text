# Servicemen's Children Voted Increased Aid
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 28. — The
House voted 389 to 0 today to in
crease allowances for children of
servicemen to $30 a month for the
first child and $20 a month for each
additional child—the same scale ap
proved by the Senate last week.
Neither chamber altered the pres
ent $50 monthly allowance for a
serviceman's wife.
The present scale on allotments
for children is $12 for the first child
and $10 for each additional.
The house bill was sent to the Sen
ate which approved an identical
scale in an amendment to the Bailey -
Clark draft deferment bill now be
ing rewritten by House Military Af
fairs Sub-commi-ttee.
