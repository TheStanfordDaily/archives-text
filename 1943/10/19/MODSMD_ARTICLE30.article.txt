# Positions Filled by Daily Edit Board
## 
### 
The Daily Editorial Board met
Sunday to make special appoint
ments to the staff of Volume 104 to
take effect immediately and continue
through this quarter. Pat Clary was
named associate editor to serve with
Irma Cunha and Esther Belcw. Since
Miss Clary will graduate at the end
of this quarter she will not run in the
elections for the editor of Volume
105.
Don Langendorf was appointed
sports editor; Cyclone Covey will
serve as assistant review editor;
Dave Davis, feature editor; Berna
dette Giannini, art editor: Pat Wright,
files editor; Dave Davis, alternate
night editor; Jane Sudekum, alter
nate night editor; Ed Luce, head
copy editor.
