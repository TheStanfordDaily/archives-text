# Service in Red Cross Described
## 
### 
Miss Luoll Weed, speaking yester
day at the women's gym, discussed
the opportunities for college women
in the Red Cross service, both hos
pital and recreation work, here and
overseas. Miss Weed, who was for
mer physical education instructor at
Staniord, is now field representative
for the American Red Cross.
Although there is no immediate
place for girls of college age, they
can prepare for this work by taking
advantage of all group activities here
at Stanford.
Women over 23 who are interested
can write their application to the
office of the Pacific Area of the Red
Cross in the civic auditorium in San
Francisco. The minimum salary is
$150.
