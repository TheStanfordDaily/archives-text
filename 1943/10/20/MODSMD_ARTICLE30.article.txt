# Stanford Dames To Meet Thursday
## 
### 
A meeting of the Stanford Dames
will be held at 7 p.m. in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse,
Thursday, Oct. 21. All wives of sol
dier and civilian students are urged
to attend and help to plan the pro
gram for the year.
Sponsors are Mrs. Norman Fenton,
Mrs. F. C. Hanker, Mrs. T. J. Kreps,
Mrs. C. F. Brand, Mrs. J. L. Mothers
head, Mrs. J. B. Wells, and Mrs. J. B.
Baumberger. Honorary sponsors are
Mrs. W. Proctor and Mrs. R. T. Wil
bur.
