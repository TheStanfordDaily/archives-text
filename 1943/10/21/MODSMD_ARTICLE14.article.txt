# Women's Pool Now Open for Swimming
## 
### 
The women's pool will be open for
recreational swimming for students
on Mondays at 11 a.m., Tuesdays and
Fridays at 2 p.m., and Wednesdays
and Thursdays at 4 p.m.
University employees, Stanford
Dames and faculty wives may use
the pool at noon hours for a nominal
fee.
