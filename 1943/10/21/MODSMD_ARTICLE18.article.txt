# War Board Starts Knitting Committee
## 
### 
A knitting committee has been or
ganized by the War Board, according
to Sally Van Dyke, chairman of the
committee. At the first meeting of
the group on Tuesday, at least 40
girls turned out to knit knee bands,
beanies, and gunners' rifle mittens.
Yarn was issued by the Red Cross.
Meetings will be held on Tuesdays
and Thursdays from 3:45 to 5:45
weekly at Miss Yost's home. Anyone
interested is urged to attend on one
or both days. Records and refresh
ments will be an added feature.
