# Folk Dancing Group Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Tonight everyone on campus in
terested in folk dancing is invited to
a meeting which is being held in the
lounge of the Women's Gym at 7:15
p.m. Mrs. Miriam Lidster, who di
rected the summer folk dances on
Wednesday nights last quarter, will
lead the discussion concerning plans
for the continuance of the Wednes
day night folk dances which were so
popular with both soldiers and ci
vilians.
