# State Policemen's Wives See Campus
## 
### 
Forty women, wives of State Po
licemen, attending a convention in
San Jose, were taken on a tour of
the campus Tuesday by Captain Gor
don Davis of the Stanford Police
Department.
The group was taken to the top
of the Hoover War Library and also
attended an organ recital by D. Stir
ling Wheelwright, in the Memorial
Church.
