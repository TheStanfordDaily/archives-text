# Service Men Asked To Church Service
## 
### 
All service men and students of
Stanford University are cordially in
vited to attend a special service
7:30 Sunday evening, Oct. 31, at the
First Presbyterian Church in Palo
Alto.
The required military authority
has been obtained to issue passes to
all Army men who wish to attend the
church service. Servicemen must see
their company commander before
Saturday evening to arrange for
Sunday night passes.
