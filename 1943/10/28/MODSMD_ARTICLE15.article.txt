# HEARING REPORTER JOBS
## 
### 
Hearing reporters were offered
state jobs when the California State
Personnel Board issued an appeal to
qualified reporters to fill state civil
service positions in Sacramento, Los
Angeles, and San Francisco. The
positions pay a beginning salary of
$215 a month. The application and
information concerning the examina
tion may be obtained by writing to
or calling at the State Personnel
Board's offices in Sacramento, Los
Angeles, and San Francisco. Oct. 29
is the final date to file applications.
