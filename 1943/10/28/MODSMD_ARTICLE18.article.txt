# Sports The Stanford Daily
## SIDELINE LANTS... 
### Don Langendorf 
Yesterday we took what is known
as a physical fitness test which found
quite a few people physically unfit.
Many new features have been added
while others have been dropped. The
outstanding new feature, a killer
diller if we ever saw one, is running
a hundred yards with a person about
the same weight as you on your back.
Oh brother! Many of us are going to
be stiff for days. Some day when you
haven't anything better to do, go out
to the field and try the test. You
sucker.
After much work on the part of
mural director Harry Wolter, the
mural program is scheduled to start.
It is a shame that there isn't a man
ager appointed to help Harry. Last
year there was an intramural man
ager. Sports at Stanford are about
dead. The only thing remaining is
intramurals. Wolter is stuck with
the complete job of getting schedules
arranged and seeing that the teams
play.
