# Cashier Jobs Open To Farm Students
## 
### 
Stanford men and women who
have had experience in handling
cash and making change will soon be
able to apply for employment as as
sistant cashier-clerks. In order to
get the positions, a state civil service
examination for it will be given on
Nov. 27 by the State Personnel
Board. Entrance salary will be $135
a month; residence in the state of
California is not required.
Assistant cashier-clerks will be
employed throughout the state in the
various offices of the Department of
Motor Vehicles during the coming
license renewal period. The final
date for filing applications at the
State Personnel Board, 108 State
Building, San Francisco, is Nov. 10.
