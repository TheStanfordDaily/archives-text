# McClanahan Reveals Engagement to Cadet
## 
### 
Janet McClanahan, A.S.S.U.
president, last night announced
her engagement to Walter Morris,
who is now stationed at Yale and
will receive his commission as a
Naval Air Corps engineer in Feb
ruary. Miss McClanahan and
Morris met when they were both
students at Burlingame High
School where Miss McClanahan
succeeded Morris to the presi
dency of the student body.
No definite wedding plans have
been made by the couple.
