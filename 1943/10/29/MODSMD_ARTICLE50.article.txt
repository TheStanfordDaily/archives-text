# In The SERVICE
## 
### 
A former Stanford student, Ma
rine Corporal Jeanne M. Ames, is
attending officers' candidate school
at Camp LeJeune, New River, North
Carolina. Following Marine Corps
policy, she has been chosen from
the enlisted ranks for officer's train
ing.
Corporal Ames, the first woman
Marine from the Bay area, received
her basic training and indoctrination
at Hunter College in New York
City and then was stationed in the
personnel department of Marine
Corps headquarters, Washington,
D.C. She graduated from Stanford
in June 1942.
The Marine recruiting office in the
Bay area is at 703 Market Street,
San Francisco, n the Central Tower
Building.
