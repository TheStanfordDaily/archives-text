# Ex-Dartmouth Men Plan Get-Together
## 
### 
A Wha-Hoo-Wha for all former
Hanover Indians now at Stanford has
been issued by Pfc. Bert W. Anger,
Dartmouth '42.
Anyone interested should con
tact Pfc. Anger at Co. I, Pierce Hall,
for details of this and further get
togethers.
