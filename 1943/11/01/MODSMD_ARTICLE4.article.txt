# Symphony Tickets On Sale for $9.50
## 
### 
A reversal of decision has made
it possible for Stanford students to
purchase the regular $17 season
tickets to the San Francisco Sym
phony concerts for $9.50.
A block of seats has been set
aside directly behind the orchestra.
Orders are now being taken at the
ASSU office in the Union.
The concerts, under the direction
of Pierre Monteux, begin Nov. 20.
The Stanford tickets will be for the
Saturday evening concerts.
