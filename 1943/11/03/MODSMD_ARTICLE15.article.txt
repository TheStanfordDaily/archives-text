# Tryoutees Meet Last Time Today
## 
### 
The final meeting of the try
outees will be held at 4 p.m. today,
following the 2 o'clock edit board
meeting, according to Barbara Ste
vens, staff editor.
Permanent appointments of some
of the tryoutees to the staff will be
made upon the recommendations
submitted by the staff editor in
charge. Instructions on permanent
positions will be given.
Review stories will be due at this
time, and beat assignments will be
handed out.
