# Doctor To Speak To Y Rate Group
## 
### 
Dr. F. W. Weymouth, professor of
physiology, will address the first
meeting of the Y Race Relations
committee tomorrow at 4:30 in the Y
clubrooms.
Dr. Weymouth will tell of the
Inter-racial Committee which has
been formed in Palo Alto and which
is now working on concrete prob
lems.
There will be a Y cabinet meeting
today at 5 p.m. with President Mary-
Beth Smith presiding.
When the Y membership drive was
held, the four most popular com
mittees indicated by members were
Race Relations, World Affairs, War
Marriages, and Leadership Training.
