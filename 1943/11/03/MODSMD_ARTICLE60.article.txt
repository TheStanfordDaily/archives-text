# Menuhin Begins Concert Series
## 
### 
December 20 Stanford students
vvll be treated to a return engage
ment by Yehudi Menuhin, who will
begin the A.S.S.U. Concert Series
this year. It has been five years
since the celebrated concert violin
ist has given a performance at Stan
ford.
Marian Anderson, the noted Negro
artist, will sing Feb. 23. followed by
Bidu Sayao, the Brazilian soprano,
who will close the series on April 13.
Season tickets are now available
at the Concert Series office in the
Old Union building. Prices are:
$3.30. $4.40, and Sti.tiO. Office hours
are from 1 to 5 o'clock. Monday
through Friday, and the phone num
ber is P.A. 3870. Mail orders should
be addressed to Box 1151.
