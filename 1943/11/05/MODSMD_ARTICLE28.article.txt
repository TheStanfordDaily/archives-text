# ASTP Glee Club To Be Organized
## 
### 
Pfc. H. P. Rodrian of Co. I is or
ganizing an ASTP glee club. All
those who enjoy singing and are in
terested in the formation of a glee
club are asked to report to the side
entrance of the Art Gallery today at
1800.
Rehearsals will be held every Mon
day and Friday from 1800 to 1915.
Sheet music will be provided. All
those who have had a background in
music and those who were members
of church choirs or symphony or
chestras are especially asked to at
tend.
