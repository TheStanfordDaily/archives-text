# Bach, Franch Featured In Recital Yesterday
## 
### 
Bach's "Come Sweet Death" and
Franck's Chorale in A minor were
featured by D. Sterling Wheelwright,
acting University organist, in his or
gan recital yesterday in the Memo
rial Church.
Other selections on the program
included "A Peasant Longs for
Home" (Ole Bull), Bouree and Mu
sette (Chenoweth), and "A Mighty
Fortress Is Our God" (Faulkes).
