# Salvage Committee To Collect Greases
## 
### 
Waste fats will be collected to
morrow morning from 9 a.m. to 12
noon in front of the post office and
at 610 Mayfield Road. Campus
housewives are asked by the War
Board Salvage Committee to turn
in their creases and fats for the
war effort. Money collected from
the sale of these fats will be given
to Dimes for Diplomas.
