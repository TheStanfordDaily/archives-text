# PANHELLENIC
## Sororities Plan Open Houses 
### 
Informality has been stressed by
Dodo Suppiger, Panhellenic presi
dent, for the non-invitational open
houses to be held by the sororities
during the weekend. The new women
are requested to wear Quad clothes
and name tags during the "get ac
quainted" period.
Beginning Friday night, dessert
will be served from 7 to 9 p.m., while
Saturday sororities will serve tea
from 3 to 5 p.m. and dessert from 7
to 9 p.m. Tea will be served Sunday
from 3 to 5 p.m.
The new women have been classi
fied alphabetically, and women
should go to the sororities on the
days designated for their alphabetical
group.
The schedule is as follows:
Friday, Nov. 5, from 7 to 9 p.m.:
A through D will go to Alpha Omi
cron Pi and Alpha Phi; E through K
will go to Chi Omega and Delta Delta
Delta: L through R will go to Delta
Gamma and Kappa Alpha Theta; S
through Z will go to Gamma Phi
Beta, Kappa Kappa Gamma, and Pi
Beta Phi.
Saturday, Nov. 6, from 3 to 5 p.m.:
A through D will go to Kappa
Kappa Gamma and Pi Beta Phi; E
through K will go to Gamma Phi and
Alpha Phi; L through R will go to
Alpha Omicron Pi, Chi Omega and
Delta Delta Delta; S through Z will
go to Kappa Alpha Theta and Delta
Gamma.
Saturday, Nov. 6, from 7 to 9 p.m.:
A through D will go to Delta Gam
ma and Kappa Alpha Theta; E
through K will go to Alpha Omicron
Pi, Kappa Kappa Gamma, and Pi
Beta Phi; L through R will go to
Gamma Phi and Alpha Phi; S
through Z will go to Chi Omega and
Delta Delta Delta.
Sunday, Nov. 7, from 3 to 5 p.m.:
A through D will go to Gamma Phi.
Chi Omega, and Delta Delta Delta; E
through K will go to Delta Gamma
and Kappa Alpha Theta; L through
R will go to Kappa Kappa Gamma
and Pi Beta Phi; S through Z will go
to Alpha Omicron Pi and Alpha Phi.
