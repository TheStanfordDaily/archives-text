# ASTP's Organize Student Glee Club
## 
### 
All Stanford students interested in
singing in a glee club are invited to
siivj; in the ASTP Glee Club, which
is being organized by Pfc. H. P.
Rodrian of Co. I.
"Although the results of the first
meeting were good, more talent is
needed," said Private Rodrian.
Rehearsals are to be held every
Monday and Friday from 1800 to
1915 in the music room of the Art
Gallery.
Students who have sung in church
choirs or have had a musical back
ground are especially invited.
