# Mural Volleyball Begins Today
## 
### 
Women's intramural volleyball will
begin today with the greatest turnout
in several years, Barbara White,
chairman, has announced. Games will
be played Monday through Thurs
day, at 5 o'clock in the Women's
Gymnasium.
Enthusiasm is running high among
the women's living groups, said Miss
White, as a record total of 30 teams
signed up to compete for the winner's
plaque, won last year by Phi Beta
Phi. A mystery group, team "X," will
vie with the coed teams. The sched
ule for today is:
Oaks vs. 1A Aces
Roble 3B vs. Lagunita
Et Cetera vs. AAA
