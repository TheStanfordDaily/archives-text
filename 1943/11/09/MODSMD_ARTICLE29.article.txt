# Chapel Sponsors Prayer Meetings
## 
### 
The prayer meetings hold each day
at 12:45 p.m. in the organ loft of the
Chapel are open to all students, ac
cording to Harriette Sutherland, an
active member.
These meetings are one of the
functions of Intervarsity Christian
Fellowship, which, begun as a stu
dent group, was incorporated into the
chapel activities by Dr. Elton True
blood, chaplain.
Students take turns in presiding
for periods of a week and reading
selections from the Bible selected at
their own discretion. Each day is
assigned to prayer for a particular
mission. The meetings are over in
time for 1 o'clock classes.
Extending their activities, this
group meets every Wednesday eve
ning from 7:30 to 8:30 in the vestry.
