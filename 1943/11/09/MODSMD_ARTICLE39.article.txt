# Mees To Lecture On Star Pictures
## 
### 
"Recent Advances in Astronomical
Photography" will be the subject of
a lecture given by Dr. C. E. K. Mees,
director of research and development
of the Eastman Kodak Company, to
morrow afternoon at 4:15 in the Cub
berley Auditorium, Education Build
ing. It will be illustrated by lantern
slides.
Dr. Mees is a professor at the Uni
versity of California at Berkeley. His
lecture will be under the joint aus
pices of the Committee on Public
Exercises and the Stanford Chapter
of Sigma Xi, honorary scientific so
ciety.
Dr. James W. Mcßain will intro
duce Dr. Mees, who will be guest of
honor at a dinner given by Sigma Xi
on the evening of the lecture. The
public is cordially invited to attend.
