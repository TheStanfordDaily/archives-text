# Students Get Ration Books for Holidays
## 
### 
Students who are going home
during the Thanksgiving holidays
may get their war ration books
from Mrs. H. V. Anderson if they
call at Room 13 in the old Union
the Wednesday before vacation
between 1:30 and 5 o'clock.
Mrs. Anderson's regular hours
to give out books to students who
are going home over weekends are
Thursdays and Fridays from 1:30
to 5.
