# Red Cross Nears Quota for Week
## 
### 
The number of surgical dressings
made in the Red Cross room this
week is an improvement over last
week, but Monday's total was under
the quota of 1,000 dressings. Girls
are reminded again to work each
week during the hours for which
they are signed up.
Monday 823 2x2 and 75 4x4 dress
ings were made, Tuesday 980 2x2
and 141 4x4 were folded, and
Wednesday the quota of 1,000 dress
ings was reached.
