# Pi Lambda Theta Elects Officers
## 
### 
Election of new officers was the
main business on hand at the recent
meeting of the Upsilon Chapter of
Pi Lambda Theta, women's educa
tional honor fraternity.
The following officers were elected:
President, Ruth A. Talboy; vice
president, Miriam Philbrick; secre
tary-treasurer, Mabel Farley. Fac
ulty advisor chosen for this year was
Dr. John Almac, professor of edu
cation.
The next meeting of the fraternity
will be held Nov. 19.
