# Lincoln To Hold Saturday Dance
## 
### 
The week-end social schedule is
lopped with the "Indian Hop" put
on by the members of Lincoln Hall.
"For a wonderful evening of dancing
to the best music, refreshments, and
college atmosphere, you must at
tend our dance," said Verne Pur
cell, president of the house.
The theme is based around the
Bruin-Bear game and features the
present football stars from Stan
ford and also stars of years gone by.
To accompany the football theme,
members of the athletic department
are to be on hand as sponsoi's.
Lincoln Hall is located on Santa
Ynez across from the Gamma Phi
Beta sorority.
