# Traveler To Show Adventure Films
## 
### 
The first woman to cross the Sahara Desert alone, Mrs. Ava Hamilton,
will show a documentary film, "The Dawn of Victory," in the Memorial
Auditorium.
The lecture, which begins at 7:30 p.m., is open to the public and there
is no admission charge.
As she shows her colored motion pictures, Mrs. Hamilton will discuss
her adventures while traveling the length and breadth oi' Africa and filming
its wildest parts. The French government awarded Mrs. Hamilton a medal
for her feat in traversing the Sahara.
The fifth in the quarter's series, Mrs. Hamilton's appearance was ar
ranged by the Committee of Public Exercises. Dr. Hubert Heffner is
chairman.
