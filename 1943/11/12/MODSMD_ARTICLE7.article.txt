# Dr. Wilbur Speaks In Radio Series
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur. Chancellor
Emeritus of Stanford University, will
speak on "Better Health Care" in a
nationwide broadcast on Saturday,
Nov. 13. The program is part of the
NBC series, "For This We Fight."
originating from Washington and
heard locally over KPO from 4 to
4:30 p.m.
