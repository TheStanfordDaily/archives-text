# Jacoby Contest Subject Chosen
## 
### 
The subject of the Melville Jacoby
Essay Prize for 1943-44 is "After
complete victory over Japan, should
the United States retain the Mikado
or dethrone him and/or his apparent
successors?", Professor Chilton R.
Bush, of the Division of Journalism,
announced yesterday.
This prize of $50 is awarded an
nually for the best essay by a Stan
ford student relating to a political,
social, or economic subject on the
Orient. The award is in memory of
the late Melville Jacoby, '39, who was
in charge of Time's Far Eastern bu
reau until his death in Australia in
1942.
Questions relating to the contest
will be answered by Professor Bush.
AST students are eligible for the
award this year.
