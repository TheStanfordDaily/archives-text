# Uruguayan Officers Visit Farm Campus
## 
### 
Three high-ranking army officials
from Uruguay visited the Stanford
campus Monday morning, accom
panied by Major Miller Holland, con
sulting professor of journalism, now

Institute; and Major Saul R. Graceras
of the Uruguayan Artillery.
