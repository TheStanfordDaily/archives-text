# Tresidders To Sponsor 'Indian Summer' Dance
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Donald Tresidder
have been selected to head the
list of sponsors for the "Indian
Summer" dance to be presented at
9 p.m. Saturday at the Pavilion by
E Company, ASTU 3905. Other
sponsors will be Mai. and Mrs.
Edward C. Andersen, Dr. and Mrs.
Hugh 11. Skilling, and Dr. and Mrs.
Victor S. Carson.
