# Pocket Books, Money Given to Red Cross
## 
### 
Seventy-two pocket-edition novels
and $15.50 were contributed to the
Red Cross last week, announced
Twink Davies, chairman. Mr. and
Mrs. T. H. Boggs, Chi Omega, and
Union were the largest contributors.
These 25-cent mystery stories and
novels will be placed by the Red
Cross in overseas Christmas kits for
servicemen. Miss Davies thanks ev
eryone for their cooperation in vol
untarily giving books, as this was not
a drive.
Anyone wishing to donate more
pocket editions may turn them in to
the War Board office, Room 14 of the
Old Union.
