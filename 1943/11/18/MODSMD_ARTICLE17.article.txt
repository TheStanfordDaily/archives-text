# Con Home Hardeners Needed for Saturday
## 
### 
The Con Home needs workers this
Saturday morning to help Sam 'Mc-
Donald, the caretaker, in the victory
garden. This is the last weekend
girls will be asked to work at the
Con Home, announced Barbara Pike,
crop crew chairman.
Transportation will be provided
from Ruble at 9:15 a.m. Volunteers
may sign up with their crop crew
chairmen in each living group.
