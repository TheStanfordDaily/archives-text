# Mystery Picture Scheduled Sunday
## 
### 
Continuing this Sunday with a
weekly rather than bi-monthly ap
pearance. the Tillson Talkies will
feature the murder mystery, "Ellery
Queen's Penthouse Mystery."
The film, which was made about a
year and a half ago, stars Ralph
Bellamy and Margaret Lindsay in
the leading roles. The story, which
is one of a series of Ellery Queen
mysteries, focuses this time on Man
hattan.
The talkies, previously scheduled
at 7:15, will be shown from now on
at 7:30. In addition to the main fea
ture, there will be a selected short
film. Admission price is 20 cents.
