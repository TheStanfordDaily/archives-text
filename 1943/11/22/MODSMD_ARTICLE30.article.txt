# Debate Squad Host To San Jose Team
## 
### 
A round-table discussion on ' Post-
War Education" was held last Thurs
day night in the Seminar room of
Memorial Hall when the Stanford
Debate Squad was host to four
speakers from San Jose.
The San Jose speakers, Phyllis
Wakefield, Anne Buxton, Carmen
dale Fernandes, and Betty Jane Tol
land, were welcomed by the Stanford
introductory speaker, Carol Newton,
and the discussion continued with
the participation of the other Stan
ford speakers, Bob Wilson. Frank
Creeds, and Mabel Adkison.
Professor Harlan Adams, debate
cpach who worked with the speakers
in preparation for the discussion,
acted as moderator.
