# Red Cross Offers Office Positions
## 
### 
The Palo Alto chapter of the Red
Cross wants girls, preferably upper
classmen, to type and do office work
for the Home Service department
every afternoon, Monday through
Friday.
This work will be of special in
terest to all girls interested in Social
Service, because they will have an
opportunity to type up cases. Anyone
wanting to apply or obtain informa
tion should see Twink Davies or
leave a note for her in the War Board
office.
