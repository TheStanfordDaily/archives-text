# Mrs. Tresidder At Home Today
## 
### 
Mrs. Donald B. Tresidder will be
at home today at her residence, 623
Mirada street, from 4 to 5:30 p.m.
Receiving at the door will also be
Mrs. John R. Eden, wife of Colonel
Eden, the Army commandant at
Stanford.
Pouring will he Mrs. Marion Kirk
wood and Mrs. Francis Murray. Mrs.
Hubert HefTner, wife of the head of
the Department of Drama, and Mrs.
Davis Jacobsen have been invited to
assist.
Faculty, officers' wives, alumnae,
students, and their friends are all
cordially invited to attend the tea.
