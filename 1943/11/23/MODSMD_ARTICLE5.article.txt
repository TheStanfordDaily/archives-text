# Ration Tickets Given Today for Vacation
## 
### 
All students who eat in Uni
versity dining halls may take home
10 meat points and 5 canned goods
points if they call for them in the
lobby of the Ad Building today
from 1:30 to 5 and tomorrow from
8:30 to 12, according to Mrs. Shir
ley Anderson.
The booth has been moved from
the Old Union to the Ad Building
for the convenience of students.
