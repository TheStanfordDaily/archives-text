# Mustering Out Pay Asked by Roosevelt
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 23. —Presi-
dent Roosevelt today called on Con
gress to enact legislation providing
mustering out pay for the armed
forces, uniform unemployment com
pensation for those unable to find
immediate post-war work, and full
credit for service personnel—includ
ing the merchant marine—under the
social security law covering their
service period. Mr. Roosevelt has
also asked for a $1,000,000,000 pro
gram for education of returning
service personnel. He is likewise
giving thought to industrial recon
version and stop-gap public works
during the switch wrom war to
peace.
The President said quick action
on demobilization plans is vital to
assure the armed services the people
at home "do not propose to let them
down."
