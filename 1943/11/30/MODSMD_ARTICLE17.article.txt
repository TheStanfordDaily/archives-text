# PRE-INSTITUTE MEET
## 
### 
Before the institute, old and new
members of Excorn are to meet in
the Excom room for a short session,
according to President Janet Mc-
Clanahan. This will replace the reg
ular Wednesday night meeting.
At the first meeting of the institute
on Nov. 18 at Miss Pat Davidson's
home, some 60 students heard talks
by Janet McClanahan, Catherine
Connaghan, Norm Schieber, TSetty
May Hale, Marybeth Smith, Kathie
Guthrie, and Jim Reynolds on the
student activities which they head.
