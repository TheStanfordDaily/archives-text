# Army Rehearsal Group Organized
## 
### 
A. theater rehearsal group for men
in uniform has been formed under
the supervision of F. Cowles Strick
land of the Speech and Drama de
partment.
The object of the groups is to pro
vide recreational activity for those
men in the services who would like
to participate in performances of
plays.
All meetings of the group are
scheduled at such times as it is pos
sible for the soldiers to attend on
their own free time. The group plans
to prepare reading rehearsals of pop
ular plays for their own entertain
ment.
Mr. Strickland will be in his office,
Room 202. between 12 and 1 p.m. on
Wednesday and Friday to meet any
people interested in joining the re
hearsal group.
