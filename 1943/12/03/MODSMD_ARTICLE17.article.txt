# Meeting Planned By Church Group
## 
### 
The Westminster Group of the
First Presbyterian Church of Palo
Alto cordially invites all Stanford
students and service men to attend
a special meeting Sunday evening
at 7:30, December 5. Madame Ling
Fu Yang, who is well known
throughout this vicinity, will pre
side.
Madame Ling is a personal friend
of Madame Chiang Kai-shek, and
has traveled extensively -in Europe
and Asia. At the meeting she will
show some of her reproductions of
famous paintings which she drew
by memory from the originals.
