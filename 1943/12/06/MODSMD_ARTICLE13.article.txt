# Series To Offer 'The Informer'
## 
### 
Famous as an Academy Award
motion picture, "The Informer" will
be shown in Memorial Hall Audi

there is no admission charge.
Victor McLaglen plays the lead in
the picture and was highly com
mended for his work. The film has
been acclaimed as an outstanding
example of what motion pictures, as
art, can be.
