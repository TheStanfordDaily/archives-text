# Stoates Top Tyler In Mural Tennis
## 
### 
Shutting out a highly regarded
Tyler Hall tennis team, The Stoates
has advanced to the second round of
play in the intramural tennis tourna
ment. Emery Neal, former Stanford
varsity man beat Scott Chaffee, of
Tyler, 6-1, 6-3, in the first singles.
The Stoates first doubles combine,
composed of Neal and Ralph Ashton,
had little trouble in downing Bill
Bowes and Fulton Camp, 6-1, 6-0. As
The Stoates had already cinched the
match, the second singles contest was
not played.
