# Jews Hold Friday Service in Chapel
## 
### 
Services for Jewish men and
women at Stanford University were
held last Friday evening, with a large
attendance in the vestry of Memorial
Church.
Cpl. Nathan Metz conducted the
services with Cpl. Saul Taxon deliv
ering a short talk on the Book of
Genesis. Refreshments were served
by the Woman's Auxiliary of the
Menlo Park Temple.
Similar services will be held every
Friday evening at 6:30 p.m. to which
all Stanford students and soldiers are
invited.
