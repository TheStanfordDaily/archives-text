# Union Sophomores To Give Frosh Tea
## 
### 
The Union Club sophomores are
giving their "six-ring circus" tea
for the freshman women today. The
tea will be held in the lobby of
Union, and refreshments will be
served from 3 to 5 p.m. The sopho
mores are anxious to acquaint the
freshmen with the living quarters in
Union, and girls will be on hand at
all times to conduct tours of the
smaller houses attached to Union as
well as tours of Union proper.
The sophomores in charge of the*
tea are Betty-Lou Horton and Mary
Jane Lloyd, co-chairmen; assisted by
Ruth Alltucker. refreshments; Pat
Shephard. invitations; and Barbara
Lamb, decorations.
