# Stanford A.S.C.E. Meets Wednesday
## 
### 
The Stanford Student Chapter of
the American Society of Civil Engi
neers will hold its third meeting of
the quarter Wednesday at 7:15 p.m.
at the home of Samuel Brooks Mor
ris, dean of the School of Engineer
ing.
Featured on the program will be
an illustrated talk on the "Colorado
River Aqueduct" by Professor
Franklin Thomas, vice-chairman of
the Board of Directors of the South
ern California Metropolitan Water
District. There will also be a talk on
the "Alaska Road" given by George
Mors. All members, new and old,
and alumni are invited to attend.
