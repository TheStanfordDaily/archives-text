# 'Moor Born' Opens For Three-Night Run
## 
### 
The Stanford Players will pre
sent "Moor Born" tonight at 8:15
in the Memorial Auditorium in the
first of a three-night run. F
Cowles Strickland will direct the
play which is centered on the lives
of the famous Bronte sisters.
Tickets for 85 cents may be ob
tained at the drama department or
at the ticket office tonight.
