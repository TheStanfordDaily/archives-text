# Ram's Head Picks Fifteen Members
## 
### 
Elected to membership in Ram's
Head, honorary dramatic society, at
a meeting last Wednesday, were
eleven new and four honorary mem
bers.
The new members are Conine
Wallman, Dick Driscoll, Harrison
Elliott, Doug Hastings, Mickey
Garms, Tom Leo, Don Landale, Bill
Hyer, Janice Meiners, Jan Whitmore,
and Blossom Parke.
Honorary members are Chuck
Reeder, Cal Lobe, Fred Hassold, and
Claude Wilson.
