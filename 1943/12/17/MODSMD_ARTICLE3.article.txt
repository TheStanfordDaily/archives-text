# Students Get Ration Books for Holidays
## 
###

books at the end of Fall Quarter by
railing at the booth downstairs in
the Administration Building. Hours
are Wednesday, Dec. lilt, from 1:30
to 5 p.m., and Thursday and Fri
day, Dec. 30 and 31, from 9 to 12
a.m., and 1:30 to 5 p.m.
