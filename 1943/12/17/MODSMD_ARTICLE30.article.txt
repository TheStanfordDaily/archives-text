# Hayworth Stars In Last Talkie
## 
### 
"Angels Over Broadway," last of
the Tillson Talkies to be presented
this quarter, will be shown Sunday
at 7:30 in the Memorial Theater.
Taking the leading roles in the pic
ture are Rita Hayworth and Douglas
Fairbanks, Jr. A selected short sub
ject will also be shown. Admission
is 20 cents.
