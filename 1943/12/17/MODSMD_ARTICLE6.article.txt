# Christmas Calendar
## 
### 
WEDNESDAY. DEC. 22
7:3o—Orchesis presents Christmas program in Memorial Auditorium.
FRIDAY, DEC. 24
7:3o—Open houses start at Hayes Hall, Lagunita. Union, and Delta
Gamma.
8:00—Open house starts at Pi Phi.
9:3o—Stanford band collects groups from open houses as it starts up
the Row.
10:00—Doughnuts and cider served in Union patio.
10:30—Caroling in the Inner Quad around the lighted Christmas tree.
11:00—Midnight services in Memorial Church.
SATURDAY. DEC. 25
3:30 to 5:30—Dr. and Mrs. Tresidder will be at home to students.
