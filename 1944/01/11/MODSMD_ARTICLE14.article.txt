# Cardinals To Give Post-Reg Dance
## 
### 
The quarterly registration dance
will be given Saturday night from 9
to 12 at the Women's Gym. It is be
ing sponsored by Cardinals, and Nor
man Schieber has been appointed
chairman, according to Barbara
White, secretary of Cardinals.
Music will probably consist of rec
ords, and everyone on campus is in
vited to attend. The soldiers are par
ticularly asked to come because of
the low ratio of men to women.
