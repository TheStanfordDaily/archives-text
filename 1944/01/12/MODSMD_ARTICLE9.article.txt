# Tryouts for Play To Continue Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for the first Stanford
Players' production of the year,
"Young Man of Tomorrow," will
continue tonight at 7:30 in the
Little Theater of Memorial Hall,
announced F. Cowles Strickland,
director of dramatics. There are
17 parts in the play equally di
vided between men and women, as
are the six leading roles.
The play which was written by '
An tan ia Rouverol will have its I
first presentation before an audi
ence on Feb. 9, 10, 11, 12. and 14.
Pictures of the cast and the cast ;
listing will be printed with the
published version of the play
which will Ik* released by the Sam
uel French Co. next month.
