# Cardinals Sponsors Reg Dance in Gym
## 
### 
Dr. Harold Bacon, Mr. and Mrs.
R. E. Luckey, and Mr. and Mrs. D.
Sterling Wheelwright have accepted
invitations to be patrons and patron
esses at the post-reg dance Saturday
night in the Women's Gym.
The dance is being sponsored by
Cardinals and is a date affair. Tick
ets for the dance will go on sale at
the door for 55 cents a couple. Cardi
nals are sponsoring the dance and
members of the organization will sell
cokes for a nickel apiece during the
dance.
