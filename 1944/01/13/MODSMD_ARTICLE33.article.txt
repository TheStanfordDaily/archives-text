# Professor's Book Receives Honors
## 
### 
Professor Harold W. Bradley's
book. The American Frontier in
Hawaii, has received honors from two
different sources at the same time.
The Beveridge Prize for the best
book in the history of the Western
Hemisphere, was given by the Amer
ican Historical Association commit
tee to Professor Bradley for his book.
The Pacific Coast Branch of the
American Historical Association gave
the book an award for being the best
work in the field of Pacific area his
tory.
The Beveridge Prize was won the
time before by another Stanford
History professor. Charless A. Bark
er for his book. The Background of
the Revolution in Maryland.
