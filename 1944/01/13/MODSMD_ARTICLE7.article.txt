# Students Invited To Organ Recital
## 
### 
The winter series of organ recitaLs
at Memorial Church will commence
this afternoon at 5, with a 30-minute
program featuring two memorial
pieces.
Guilmant's "Lamentation" and
Bach's "Come Peaceful Rest" will be
heard both today and on Sunday's
program at the same hour.
Friends as well as students of the
University are invited to enjoy this
twilight hour of organ music played
by the University organist each
Thursday and Sunday at 5 p.m.
