# Frosh To Attend Jolly-up Tonight
## 
### 
"We urge all old freshmen to at
tend the jolly-up in order to have a
good time and to meet the incoming
students," declares Johnny Fulton,
chairman of the affair, which is to
take place tonight at 7:30 in the
Nitery.
Dr. Tresidder will lead off the pro
gram with a welcoming speech to the
new freshmen. Claude Wilson and
Charles Reeder, Gaieties stars, will
"give out" with a piano duet. Jim
Lund, Dick Driscoll, and Luana
Forkner, student body officers, are
scheduled to give short speeches.
