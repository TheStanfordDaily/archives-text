# Five Professors Attend Meeting
## 
### 
Five Stanford professors attended
a meeting of the Pacific Coast branch
of the American Historical Associa
tion held at Cal Tech Jan. 8, 1944.
Those who attended from Stanford
were: Edgar Robinson, George
Knowles, Harold Bradley, Dayton
Phillips, and Edward White. Pro
fessor George Knoles spoke on
"Baedker's America"and Dayton
Phillips spoke on the "Medieval
Roots of the Reformation."
At the meeting Max Savelle was

elected to the Board of Editors of the
Pacific History Keview, the quarterly
organ of the Pacific Coast Branch or
the American Historical Association
