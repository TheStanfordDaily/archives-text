# Mrs. Tresidder To Be At Home on Tuesday
## 
### 
Mrs. Donald B. Tresidder will be
at home to students and faculty of
Stanford on Tuesday, January 18,
from 4 to 5:30 p.m. The affair is the
first entertainment she will give
during the Winter Quarter. It will
be held at the Tresidder home, 623
Mirada Avenue.
