# First Tillson Talky To Star Bergman
## 
### 
Beginning the program for Winter
Quarter, the Tillson Talkies will open
with its first movie of 1944, starting
Sunday, Jan. 30.
Featured on the bill will be
"Adam Had Four Sons," starring
Ingrid Bergman and Warner Baxter,
with Susan Hayward in a minor role.
The film, which was produced
about three years ago, is placed in
England and involves the lives of
five men. Also on the program will
be a new serial entitled "Darkest
Africa," which will be continued as
an added attraction with each movie.
