# TUFTS ELECTED
## 
### 
DAVIS, Jan. 18 (UP)—Dr. Warren
P. Tufts, professor of pomology and
head of the division of pomology on
the Davis campus of the University
of California, has been elected presi
dent of the American Association for
Science.
