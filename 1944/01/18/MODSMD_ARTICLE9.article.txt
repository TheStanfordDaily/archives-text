# Students Offered Drafting Course
## 
### 
For those interested but not quali
fied for engineering, there is offered
an industrial drafting course in prep
aration for war-production plants.
The class meets Monday through
Thursday from 6 to 10 p.m. For fur
ther information, get in touch with
the Peninsula Defense Training Cen
ter in Palo Alto, or call P.A. 8285.
