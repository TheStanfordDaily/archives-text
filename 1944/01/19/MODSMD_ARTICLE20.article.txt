# Pants Rowland New PCL Prexy
## 
### 
Clarence (Pants) Rowland is the
new president of the Pacific Coast
Baseball League. Rowland, a former
member of the Chicago Cubs system,
will hold office for 10 years at a sal
ary of $12,500 a year.
The final decision was a result of
more than four hours of balloting by
league officials.
Rowland stated that he wanted
every club "to get a square deal"
and that if any of them got shaky,
he'd try to help them.
The league directors also okayed
a schedule two weeks longer than
last season, starting April 9 and end
ng Sept. 17.
