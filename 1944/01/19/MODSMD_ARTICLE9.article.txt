# Monday, Jan. 25, Is Last Day Students May Change Study Lists Without Fine
## 
### 
Monday, Jan. 24. is the last day
that students may change their study
lists for this quarter without fine.
After Monday, all revisions must be
made by petition to the Registration
Committee, subject to the following
lines: $2 for each course added or
dropped or $3 where one course is
substituted for another.
All program changes made since
registration should be filed at the
