# Phi Bete Women Choose Marriage
## 
### 
What becomes of college girls
elected to the national scholastic
honorary society of Phi Beta Kappa?
Considering the graduates of Mills
College as typical of national trend,
it may be answered that 60 per cent
honorary society of Phi Beta Kappa
distinction are now married. For
these were the figures gathered by
the Mills College Office of Record
and Placement concerning the grad
uates of that college.
Since 1929, when the Zeta Chapter
of Phi Beta Kappa was established at
Mills, 109 undergraduates have been
named, with the year 1942 showing
the largest number, 10.
