# A.S.S.U. President Calls Meeting Today
## 
### 
The chairman of Cardinals, the
War Board chairman, the vice
president of the A.S.S.U., and the
chairman of the War Bonds and
Stamp Sales committee are to meet
in the A.S.S.U. president's office
from 4 to 5 p.m. today, according to
Dick Driscoll, president.
