# Contest Director Asks for Scripts
## 
### 
"Army students are also eligible
to participate in Ram's Head one
act playwriting contest," announced
Catherine Thomas, dramatic mana
ger. "Both they and civilian students
should turn in their scripts by Jan.
2S to me or to Moofie Faris, president
of Ram's Head," Miss Thomas added.
Winners of the annual contest for
amateur playwrights will automati
cally be awarded membership in
Ram's Head. They will also have the
honor of seeing their work produced
on the stage.
The three winning plays will be
presented on Feb. 25 and 26 in the
Little Theater of Memorial Hall.
