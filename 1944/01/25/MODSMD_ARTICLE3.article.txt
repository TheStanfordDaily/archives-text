# Missionary To Talk On Life in Orient
## 
### VICKY SELLENS 
Irene Webster Smith, eminent
American missionary who returned
to this country shortly before the
war after 25 years in Japan, will
speak on her life in the Orient
Khursday night at 7:30 in the lower
A)unge of the Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Smith's work in the mis
sionary field was devoted mostly to
the foundation of homes for Japa
nese children, who would otherwise

have been sold as Geisha girls, and
to the bringing up of these children
as Christians.
Her foundling home in Japan was
visited by the Duke of Windsor as
a part of his tour of the Orient when
he was Prince of Wales.
A graduate of Dublin University,
Miss Smith has been making a lec
ture tour of the West Coast univer
sities and will come to Stanford di
rectly from the University of Cali
fornia, where she has been speaking
before Inter-Varsity Christian Fel
lowship groups.
Miss Smith's talk will be under the
auspices of the Stanford Inter-
Varsity Christian Fellowship Society.
