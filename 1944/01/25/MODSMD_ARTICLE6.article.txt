# Big Crowd Attends First Folk Dance
## 
### 
A crowd, estimated at about 50
people, attended the first meeting of
the Stanford Folk Dance Group last
Thursday night, according to Miss
Jean Bellinger, director of the group.
There is plenty of room for more
people, she said, and anyone inter
ested is invited to come to the sec
ond meeting Thursday night at 8 in
the Women's Gym.
The advanced group of dancers
will meet from 9 to 10. Miss Bellin
ger asks that anyone in the recrea
tional group who has any particular
dances he would like to learn contact
lier and she will try to arrange it.
