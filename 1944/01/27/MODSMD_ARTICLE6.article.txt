# Movie This Sunday To Star Bergman
## 
### 
"Adam Had Four Sons." starring
Ingrid Bergman and Warner Baxter,
is the main feature in the program
to be given in the Memorial Audi
torium Sunday evening at 7:15.
The picture, made a few years ago.
was filmed in England. And Miss
Bergman, who will be remembered
for her performances in "Casa
blanca" and "For Whom the Bell
Tolls," made her initial success in
this movie.
On the same program is the first
episode of the serial. "Darkest
Africa." Miss Tillson also announced
that films will be shown every other
Sunday evening throughout this
quarter. Admission price to these
movies is 20 cents.
