# P.O. Doors To Remain Open Evenings Until 8
## 
### 
The lobby of the Stanford Post
Office will hereafter remain open
until 8 o'clock every evening, in
cluding Sunday. Under the for
mer schedule, adopted during the
Pacific Coast's war emergency, the
lobby closed at 7 on weekday sand
Saturday, and at 2 p.m. on Sunday.
The new schedule has been in
stalled in order to give greater
convenience to those who have
boxes.
