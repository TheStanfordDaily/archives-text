# Deadline Postponed For One-Act Scripts
## 
### 
''Although we would appreciate
all scripts for the Ram's Head one
act playwriting contest turned in
by this afternoon, late scripts will
be accepted if they are brought to
me at the Tri-Delt house by noon
tomorrow," announced Moofie
Faris, president of Ram's Head.
Automatic membership in the
honorary dramatic society will be
awarded to the authors of the ,
winning plays which will be pre
sented in the Little Theater of
Memorial Hall on Feb. 25 and 26.
The contest is open to both army
and civilian students.
The three plays to be produced j
will be chosen over the week-end |
and the results will be announced
in The Daily at the beginning of
next week.
