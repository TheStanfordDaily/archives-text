# Vocom To Present Tips to job-Seekers
## 
### 
'"Old Grads—New Jobs," will be
the topic of discussion at the first
Vocom meeting, Wednesday, Feb. 2,
at 7:30 in the Women's Clubhouse.
Recent women graduates from Stan
ford will speak on the ways that they
have found best to get jobs and keep
them.
