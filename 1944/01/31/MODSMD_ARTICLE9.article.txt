# Ticket Sale Announced For Season's First Play
## 
### 
Tickets for '"Young Man of To
day," the Stanford Players' first
production of the year, will go on
sale today at the box office of the
Memorial Theater for 85 cents. The
play, which was written by Au
rania Rouverol, creator of Andy
Hardy, will have its premiere at
Stanford on Feb. 9, 10, 11, 12, and
14.
Tickets may lie obtained at the
box office which is on the second
floor of Memorial Hall or by call
ing P.A. 22041.
