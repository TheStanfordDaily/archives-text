# Choir Has Election For Winter Quarter
## 
### 
Clarence Thurber, president of the
Stanford -Choir, announces its or
ganization for Winter Quarter and
the appointment of Marianne Lion as
secretary in charge of personnel.
Sixteen sopranos have elected as
their part leader Margery Johnson;
12 altos, Katherine Reuter; 9 tenors,
Cyclone Covey; 14 basses, Len Tem
ple. Randall Rice will continue as
librarian of the choir, which is di
rected by D. Sterling Wheelwright,
acting organist of the University.
