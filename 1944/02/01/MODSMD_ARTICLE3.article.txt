# REDS NEAR ESTONIA
## 
### 
LONDON (UP)—The Red Army
today swept to within less than five
miles of the Estonian border and
fought its way into the streets of
Kingisepp, last German bastion on

the main road to that Baltic state.
Advancing more than 12 miles in
24 hours the Russians captured more
than 60 towns in their sweep west
ward. Two other units of Gen. Leo
nid A. Govorov's Leningrad Army
advanced down the Leningrad-Luga-
Pskov railroad—last main German
escape line from the northern sector
—and reached to within 27 miles of
Luga by capturing the rail station of
Divenskaya.
