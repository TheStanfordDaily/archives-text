# Musicians Needed For Cardinal Band
## 
### 
Although 35 men turned out for
the band rehearsal last Saturday
afternoon, more are still needed, es
pecially trumpet, trombone, and per
cussion players, announced Jack
Pickering. If enough girls turn out,
they, too, may be allowed to play.
All men and women students who
play band instruments are urged to
come out to the Bandhouse Thursday
at 5 p.m. and Saturday at 1 p.m. In
struments will be furnished to those
people who do not have them.
