# Salvage Committee Plans Drive Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford's Salvage Committee
will meet tonight at 7:30 in the
Women s Clubhouse to plan the
coming salvage drive, according to
Carol Ilurwitz. War Board chair
man.
All salvage representatives of
both men's and women's living
groups are urged to attend the
meeting which, according to Miss
Ilurwitz. is the most important of
the quarter.
