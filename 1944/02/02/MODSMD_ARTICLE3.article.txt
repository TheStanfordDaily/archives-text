# Chemists Hold Smoker at 7:30
## 
### 
The annual joint smoker of Phi
Lambda Upsilon and Alpha Chi Sig
ma, honorary chemical fraternities,
will be held tonight at 7:30 in the
Faculty Club, according to Gerould
H. Smith, graduate student who will
act as master of ceremonies. This
will be the only joint activity of the
two clubs this year, and all members
of the faculty, graduate students, and
majors in the department of chem
istry are invited.
Both of the national- fraternities
compose their membership of men
who plan to make some branch of
chemistry their life work, and are
dedicated to high scholarship and
original investigation in every
branch of the science.
