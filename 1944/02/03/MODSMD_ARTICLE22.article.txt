# Dancing Lessons on Thursdays for Soldiers
## 
### 
Soldiers who wish to learn dancing
may receive instruction at the Wom
en's Clubhouse from 9:45 to 10:15 on
Thursday nights. There will be a
series of three lessons starting with a
group of 20.
Girls will assist as individual part
ners with Julius Buckler as teacher
and Peggy Derring as pianist. Cathy
Hoover will be in charge.
Any army student desiring lessons
should see his social chairman. Girls
wishing to help should contact Cathy
Hoover at Lagunita. Miss Hoover
asked that army students wear civil
ian shoes if possible.
