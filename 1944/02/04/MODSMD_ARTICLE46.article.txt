# University's New Music Room Well Received
## 
### 
The opening of the University
music listening room Wednesday was
a huge success, according to Marie
Louise Schwabacher. chairman of
the music committee. The collection
for records, scores and music books
was greatly increased, thanks to the
generous response to the request of
the committee.
The music listening room, located
in Room 15 upstairs in the Old
Union, will be open every weekday
