# International Club Seeks Nation-Wide Affiliation
## 
### 
The Stanford International Club,
holding its first meeting Wednesday
night at the home of Mrs. Thomas,
resolved to apply for a charter to
make the club a branch of the na
tion-wide International Club.
The purpose of the club is to
foster better relations between the
United States and other countries
and to make foreign students feel
at home.
The program for the evening con
sisted of a description of Mexico by
Victoria Sellens, and Mexican and
Chinese songs. The next meeting
will be held a week from Sunday
at which time new officers will be
elected.
