# THE
						AXE AGREEMENT OF 1933
## 
### 
We hereby agree that the Stanford Axe shall be awarded as
a trophy to the winner of the Big Game starting with the season
of 1933. In event of a tie game the Axe shall remain in possession
of the previous Big Game winner.
The officers of each student body shall be responsible for the
conduct of its members in observing the above agreement.
Wakefield Taylor, President
Associated Students, University of California
Gerald H. Trautman, President
Associated Students, Stanford University
September 26, 1933
