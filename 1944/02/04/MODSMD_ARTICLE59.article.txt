# WAR BOARD
## AUXILIARY MEETS

meet Monday at 5 p.m. in the
Women's Clubhouse, according to
Carol Hurwitz. Every women's liv
ing group must have a representa
tive in attendance, and girls must
turn in war work hour lists and
bandage rolling quota lists at this
time.
