# Public Welcome To Music Room
## 
### 
Stanford's first music listening
room begins its second week of oper
ation today. The room will be open
from 1 to 8 p.m. on weekdays, and
from 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. on Saturdays
and Sundays. During these hours,
Stanford students will have an op
portunity to listen to records in the
room's collection, or to good music
on radio broadcasts.
"All Stanford students, soldiers,
and civilians are welcome to make
use of the room's facilities," accord
ing to Marie Louise Schwabacher,
chairman of the music committee.
The music listening room is Room 15
in the Old Union Building.
