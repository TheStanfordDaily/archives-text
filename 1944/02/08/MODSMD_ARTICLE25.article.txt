# Carmel Paintings To Be At Stanford Art Gallery
## 
### 
A collection of paintings by M.
DeNeale Morgan depicting scenes
from Carmel-by-the-Sea will be on
exhibit in the Stanford Art Gallery
from Feb. 8 until the end of the
month. The group of watercolors
showing rural life in Mexico which
has been exhibited in the gallery the
past few weeks will be held over un
til the new collection arrives.
