# Wallace Says F.D.R. Will Run Fourth Time
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO (UP)—Vice-
President Henry A. Wallace told
newsmen today that he believed
President Roosevelt would run for a
fourth term as he completed his
schedule of public appearances here
and prepared to leave tonight for
Seattle.
"As for me—l am in the lap of the
Gods," the Vice-President said when
asked whether he might be the Pres
ident's running mate.
