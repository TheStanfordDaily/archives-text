# Gripsholm Leaves for American War Prisoners
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 15 (UP) —
The State Department revealed last
night that the Swedish liner Grips
holm left today for Lisbon for the
first direct war-time exchange of
American and German prisoners of
war.
