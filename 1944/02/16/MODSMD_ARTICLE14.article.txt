# Guerard To Talk Next Tuesday
## 
### 
Albert Guerard, professor of gen
eral literature at Stanford Univer
sity, will be the speaker on the Tues
day Evening Series program to be
held on Washington's birthday, Feb.
22, at 7:30 p.m., in Memorial Hall,
Stanford.
For the past 15 months Mr. Gue
rard has been with Elmer Davis in
New York City, broadcasting short
wave programs to France. Mr. Gue
rard is an advocate of a "United Eu
rope" and has set forth his views in
articles which have appealed in The
New Republic, Nation, Common
Sense, Free World, The Yale Review,
Antioch, and American Scholar.
The lecture on Feb. 22 is free to the
public.
