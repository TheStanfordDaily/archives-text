# Folk Dance Group Chooses Officer
## 
### 
The advanced group of the Stan
ford Folk Dancers elected Ellen
Davis its representative to the Cali
fornia Federation of Folk Dancers.
It will be her duty to attend the fed
eration meetings throughout the
state and to report back to the group.
The recreational group will begin
promptly at 8 p.m. Thursday and the
advanced group will meet at 9 for
an hour. Members of the advanced
group attended a folk dance festival
held in San Francisco last week, and
the group will learn some of the
dances demonstrated there.
