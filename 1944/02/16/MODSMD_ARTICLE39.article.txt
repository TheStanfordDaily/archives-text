# Officers of Newman Club Chosen in Last Meeting
## 
### 
Officers for the Newman Club, an
organization of Catholic students on
campus, were elected at the last
; meeting, according to June Bouquet,
new secretary.
The new president is Don Wil
liams; treasurer, Sally Faye; secre
tary, Miss Bouquet; vice-president
for army students, Bill Carter; viee
; president of the civilian students,
Avey Quinn. Outgoing president is
John Meixner.
