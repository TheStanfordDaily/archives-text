# Group To Discuss World Changes Thursday Night
## 
### 
"The Effects of This Changing
World on Family Life" will be dis
cussed in the "Y" rooms by the
"Women in the World" group to
morrow evening at 7:15, according to
Rosemary Cross, chairman.
Caroline Kuhns and Muriel Tart
are in charge of this discussion which
will be followed by similar discus
sions every week until March 9, said
Miss Cross.
