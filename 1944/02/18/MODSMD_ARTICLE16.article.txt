# Georges Lemaitre Becomes Citizen
## 
### 
Professor and Mrs. Georges Le
maitre exchange their French citi
zenship for American at naturaliza
tion ceremonies in Santa Rosa this
week.
Mr. Lemaitre who is professor of
Romanic languages at the University,
left to present a lecture on French
literature before a club audience in
Los Angeles, after receiving his cit
izenship.
Professor Lemaitre has just com
pleted a book on Victor Hugo and is
the author of "Four French Novel
ists," a biography of Andre Maurois,
and a volume on surrealism. He was
a member of the McGill University
faculty before coming to Stanford.
