# 'Verdi' Film Set For Italian Class
## 
### 
"Giuseppe Verdi," an Italian film,
will be shown members of the
Army's Italian classes here next
Thursday at 7 p.m. in the Educa
tion Auditorium. Arrangements for
the movie were completed by Pro
fessor Luigi Sandri, head of the Ital
ian department at Stanford. Army
students in other classes, as well as
the public, are invited.
