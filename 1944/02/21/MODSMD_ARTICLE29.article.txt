# DUNHAM DANCERS COMING
## 
### 
Miss Katherine Dunham's "Tropi
cal Revue" has had its appearance in
San Francisco postponed from Feb.
27 until March 28, 29, 30, and April
2. Tickets now held for February 27
will not be honored at the War Me
morial Opera House; they must be
exchanged -at the Sherman Clay box
office for one of the above dates.
