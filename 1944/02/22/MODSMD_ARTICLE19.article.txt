# Army-Navy Civilian Jest Set March 15
## 
### 
The qualifying test for civilians
who wish to apply for the Army or
for the Navy college training pro
grams will be given on the Stanford
campus at 8:45 on the morning of
Wednesday, March 15, and will be
held in Room 214 of the History
building, according to Karl M. Cow
dery, associate registrar.
In anticipation of this test, Mr.
Cowdery said, those who who are in
terested must obtain the formal ad
mission-application certificate, fill it
out and have it properly counter
signed to present at the door of the
test. These blanks and descriptive
material may be obtained from the
office of the Dean of Men or the of
fice of the Associate Registrar.
