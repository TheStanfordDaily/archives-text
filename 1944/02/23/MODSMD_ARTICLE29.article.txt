# 'Y' World Group To Hear Dr. Boggs
## 
### 
Dr. Theodoi-e H. Boggs, acting head
of the economics department, will
speak to the World Affairs group of
the "Y" this afternoon at 4:30 in the
"Y" rooms, according to Virginia
Hobbs, chairman. His topic will be
"World Trade After the War."
Dr. Boggs will attempt to clarify
capital improvements in the future
and to discuss the effects of the war
on pre-war trade institutions, said
Miss Hobbs. Tea will be served.
