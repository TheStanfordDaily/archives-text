# TILLSON TALKIES SUNDAY
## 
### 
' Angels Over Broadway,"' starring
Rita Hayworth and Doug Fairbanks
Jr., is the feature for the Tillson
Talkies Sunday evening at 7:30 p.m.,
according to Nan Tillson, manager.
Mickey Mouse will also be there in
cartoon form and the third install
ment of "Darkest Africa" will be
shown. The third feature will be a
short on the American Red Cross to
initiate their drive next week.
