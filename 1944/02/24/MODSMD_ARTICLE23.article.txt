# Wheelwright Fells Of Today's Recital
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wheelwright, acting
organist, has announced the program
for the organ recital this Thursday,
Feb. 24, at 5 p.m.
He will play "Song Without
Words" by Bonnett; "Four Concerto
Movements" by Handel; Russell's
"Song of the Basket-Weaver"; and
"An Old Melody" (arranged).
Held weekly in the Memorial
Chapel, the recitals are open to the
public.
