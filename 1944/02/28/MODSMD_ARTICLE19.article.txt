# Hoyt, Inter-Hall Prexy, Appointed to Excom
## 
### 
Don Hoyt. president of Men's
Inter-Ilall Council, was appointed
I an ex-olTicio member of Executive
| Committee at its meeting held last
Thursday.
Excont approved the following
dates for social events: March
Gamma I'lii Open House and Ma
drona Fireside; March 10. Casa
J Ventura house dance; March 11,
Co. Hand Klahoya informal dancc
and Alpha Omicron Pi forimJ
house dance.
