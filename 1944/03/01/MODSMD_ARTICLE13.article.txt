# "Y" ENTERTAINS FRIDAY
## 
### 
Everyone is invited to come to the
Nitery this Friday night for an eve
ning of dancing, games, and fun. The
freshman members of the "Y" will
act as hostesses. There will be en
tertainment during the evening, ana
cokes will be sold. As usual, this will
be a date or non-date affair. All
soldiers and civilians are invited.
