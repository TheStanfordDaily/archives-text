# Wilbur To Speak Sunday at Chapel
## 
### 
Founder's Day will be observed by
the University with special exer
cises in Memorial Chapel the morn
ing of March 9 from 10 to 11.
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur will
speak on "Stanford Traditions," the
only address of the prograrit.
The University Choir, clothed in
the new cardinal-colored robes, will
provide the music for the occasion,
and at the conclusion of the exer
cises the carillon in the tower of the
Hoover Library will be played for
10 minutes.
President Dick Driscoll and Vice-
President Luana Forkner of the
A.S.S.U. will lay a memorial wreath
on the altar.
