# Wheelwright Will Play Tschaikowsky
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wreelwright, acting
University organist, will hold the
weekly organ recital this Thursday,
March 2 at 5 p.m. in the Memorial
Chapel.
Featuring an all - Tschaikowskv
program, Mr. Wheelwright will play
"Lyric Theme," from Symphony Pa
thetique; "Andante Cantabile," from
Symphony No. 5; and the well known
"Humoresque."
The organ recitals are open to the
public, and offer pleasant relaxation
to the campus and community.
