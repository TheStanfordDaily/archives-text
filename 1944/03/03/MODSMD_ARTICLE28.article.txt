# 'Sacraments' Is Topic of Series
## 
### 
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, Chaplain
of Stanford University, will give a
series of sermons during March on
the "Sacraments of Common Life."
The first of the series, next Sun
day, will be on "Marriage." The
second and third of the series are
entitled "Birth" and "Death" re
spectively. "I do not believe enough
attention is being given to these
sacraments," Dr. Trueblood said.
In his sermon Sunday, Dr. True
blood will consider divorce, and he
will touch on war marriages.
The last service of the month, and
the quarter, on March 26, will be de
livered by James C. Baker, Bishop of
the Methodist Church. Pacific Area.
