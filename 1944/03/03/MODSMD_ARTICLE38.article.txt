# ARMY STUDENT COUNCIL
## 
### 
The office of the Army Student
Council is now open daily, Monday
through Friday from 1700 to 1900 for
all ASTP men who wish to confer
with their ASC representatives. The
office is located near Toyon Hall in
the room which formerly housed the
army textbook office. Soldier-stu
dents are urged to present any prob
lems that may be confronting them,
individually or collectively.
