# Tryouts for Tennis Clubs To Be Held
## 
### 
Tryouts for those wishing to be
come members of the Tennis Club
Spring Quarter will be held Wednes
day from 3 to 5 and Thursday from
1 to 3, according to Tennis Club
President Janet Busse.
A Junior Tennis Club has been
newly inaugurated for those girls
who are not ready for Tennis Club
but who arc potential Tennis Club
members. Members for both of these
clubs will be chosen from the try
outees who come on Wednesday or
Thursday.
Miss Busse urges everyone inter
ested to come try out and a special
invitation is extended to those girls
who tried out for the Tennis Club
last Fall Quarter.
