# 'Papa Is All' Tickets Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Tickets for the second Stanford
Players production of Winter
Quarter, Patterson Greene's "Papa
Is All." will go on sale today for
85 cents at the box office in the
back of Memorial Hall.
The play, which is a comedy
about the tyrannical father of a
Pennsylvania Dutch Mennonite
family, will be presented in the
Little Theater of Memorial Ilall at
8:15 on the evenings of March 15.
115, 17. 18. and 20.
Tickets may be reserved by call
ing Chester Barker, secretary of
the speech and drama department,
at P.A. 22041.
