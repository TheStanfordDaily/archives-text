# Special Organ Recital Scheduled
## 
### 
The Founders' Day organ recital
will be presented this afternoon,
March 9. at 5 p.m. in the Memorial
Church. D. Sterling Wheelwright,
acting organist, has prepared a spe
cial program for the occasion.
He will play "Largo," from the
New World Symphony by Dvorak;
"The Heavens Are Telling," by
Haydn; "Benedictus," by Reger;
"Moment Musical"; Widor's "Toc
cata." from the Fifth Organ Sym
phony; and a familiar hymn, "Lead
Kindly Light," by Newman.
