# Orchesis To Hold Tryouts Friday
## 
### 
For Stanford women who are in
terested in becoming members of
Orchesis, tryouts will be held this
Friday afternoon at 4 o'clock in the
jDance Studio, according to Mrs.
Miriam Lidster, director of the group.
"The women will be tested only on
dance techniques which they have
learned in class," Mrs. Lidster said.
There will be no requirement for an
original dance composition; at least
one quarter of modern dance at
Stanford and an interest in dancing
are the only requirements. Orchesis
meets regularly one night a week
during the quarter.
