# Eucalypto Spread.
## 
### 
Casa Eucalypto is having a
spread tonight honoring the whole
of "A" wing of Roble. It is to
begin sharp at nine o'clock in the
Eucalypto social room.
The food is to be served in a
progressive style in that it will be
in the different double rooms of the
corridors. In this way the fresh
men will be able to see more of the
girls' rooms and get better ac
quainted.
The evening is to be concluded
with entertainment in the social
room. Betty Kenny is general
chairman.
