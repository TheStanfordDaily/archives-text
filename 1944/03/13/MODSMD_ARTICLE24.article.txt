# Dr. Hecht Will Talk On Energy, Vision
## 
### 
A lecture on "Energy and Vision"
will be given by Dr. Selig Hecht, out
standing authority on functioning of
the human eye. in the Cubberley
Auditorium, Education building, at
Stanford on Monday evening, March
13, at 7:30.
Dr. Hecht, who is professor of bio
physics at Columbia University, has
made fundamental contributions to
our knowledge of the keenness of
vision of the human eye, and also to
the mechanism by which the eye
distinguishes colors.
The lecture is sponsored by Sigma
Xi, national science honor society.
The public is invited.
