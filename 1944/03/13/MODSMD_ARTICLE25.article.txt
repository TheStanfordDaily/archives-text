# TUES. SERIES
## Society Plans Musical Hour 
### 
A program of chamber music by
the Music Lovers Society of San
Francisco will be represented as the
Tuesday Evening Series program in
the auditorium of Memorial Hall at
Stanford University tomorrow at 7:30
p.m.
The program is sponsored by the
Public Exercises Committee of the
University and the Friends of Music.
It will be open to the public without
admission charge.
The Music Lovers Society was
founded in 1933 by Margaret Tilly,
English concert pianist, for the pur
pose of presenting unfamiliar and
seldom heard works. Tomorrow's
program includes trios by Ilaydn and
Jean Francaix, and a quartet by
Brahms.
