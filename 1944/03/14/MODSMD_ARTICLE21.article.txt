# China Art Exhibit
## 
### 
Chang Shu-Chi. professor of fine
arts in the National Central Univer
sity, will give a demonstration of his
painting techniques from 2 to 4
o'clock tomorrow in the Thomas
Welton Stanford Art Gallery. The
Chinese artist's work will remain on
exhibition until Sunday, March 19.
Professor Chang uses a special
technique with two-color brushes,
with which he achieves delicate
shading. The demonstration tomor
row will be the last of a series of
three that the artist has conducted.
The collection of Professor Chang's
paintings, which are on continuous
display, consists of 54 of his best
works.
According to Dr. Lin Yutang, "It
is a delight to watch Professor Chang
at work. *
