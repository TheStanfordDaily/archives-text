# Rael Talks Tonight To Newman Club
## 
### 
Dr. Juan B. Rael will speak to the
Stanford Newman Club at 9 tonight
in the vestry of the Memorial Chapel.
His lecture will be an illustrated
talk on Mexico. The material is
centered around colored pictures and
material that he gathered during a
summer trip taken through Mexico
three years ago.
