# Li'l Indian Cartoons On Sale Tomorrow
## 
### 
Don't forget the sale of Li'l In
dian Cartoon Books tomorrow, is
! the warning issued to the campus
by Kay Hammond, business man
ager of the Daily. The books will
be sold at the Cellar, the Post
Office, and the History corner as
long as the supply lasts.
The price ol these books, which
are filled with cartoons of Stan
ford life, is 15 cents, and an addi
tional 5 cents will be charged for
an envelope suitable for mailing
overseas. The cartoons are those
which have been appearing in the
Daily for the past few weeks, and
have been drawn by Bob Breer, a
