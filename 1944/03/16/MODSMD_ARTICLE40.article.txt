# Farm Yell Leader, '43, Dies at Notre Dame
## 
### 
Kenneth Anderson, class of 1944,
died March 6 at Notre Dame from
complications following scarlet fever.
He was attending naval midship
man's school. Interment was in For
est Lawn Cemetery on March 11.
The former Stanford yell leader
was from Beverly Hills and was a
member of Alpha Delta Phi. Ander
son left campus in June of 1943 for
naval training at Cal Tech. His bro
ther, Sherman Anderson, also at
tended Stanford.
