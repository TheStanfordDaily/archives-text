# GRADE INFORMATION
## 
### 
Students who wish to receive their
grades for this quarter during vaca
tion should leave a stamped, self
addressed envelope at the Regis
trar's Office during the week of
March 27. Grades can be obtained
only by mail until Registration Day,
April 10. They will be mailed out
about April 5. For convenience of
the students envelopes will be sold
at the Registrar's Office for 5 cents
each. The money received for these
envelopes is turned over to the
Stanford Convalescent Home for
children.
