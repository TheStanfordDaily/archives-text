# Carillon Concert Set for Sunday
## 
### 
A carillon concert will be pre
sented from the tower of the Hoover
Library at 1:30 Sunday, according
to James R. Lawson, carillonneur.
A composition by Martin Klein,
Stanford graduate student, will be
featured. A group of students from
Madison Hall published the number
recently.
The program will include the fol
lowing modern compositions: "Suite
for Carillon" by Samuel Barber, se
lections from George Gershwin's
"Rhapsody in Blue," and "Dedica
tion," a Russian folk tune arranged
by Dmitry Krassovsky of the Hoo
ver Library staff.
