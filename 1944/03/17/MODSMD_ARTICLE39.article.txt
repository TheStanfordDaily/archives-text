# 850 Farm Women Do War Work
## 
### 
Eight hundred and fifty of the
1,050 living on campus turned in
pledge cards this quarter pledging to
do eight hours of war work a month,
announced Alice Tuttle, chairman of
the eight-hour pledge system.

Seven campus living groups went
100 per cent in turning in pledge
cards. Ihese were Casa Granada,
Casa Olivo, Elm, Madrono, Delta
Gamma, Gamma Phi Beta, and Kap
pa.
