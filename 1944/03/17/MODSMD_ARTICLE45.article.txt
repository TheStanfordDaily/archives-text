# HILTON RETURNS
## 
### 
Ronald Hilton, associate professor
of Romanic languages, is returning
Spring Quarter after a two-quarter
leave of absence, to teach elementary
Portuguese, Spanish World, and
Spanish grammar and reading
courses. Mr. Hilton has been tour
ing South America collecting mate
rial for the third edition of "Who's
Who in Latin America," as director
of its publication.
