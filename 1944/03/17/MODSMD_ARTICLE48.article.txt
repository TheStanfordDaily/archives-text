# ART DISPLAY TO OPEN
## 
### 
A display of Mexican and South
American art will open Sunday af
ternoon in the foyer of the Memo
rial Auditorium, and will remain
open to the public until Tuesday.
The collection will include water
colors, oils, inks, and charcoal draw
ings by Diego Rivera, Jose Cle
mente Orozco, and Pablo O'Higgins.
