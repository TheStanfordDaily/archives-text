# MOTHER'S CLUB MEETS
## 
### 
Genest deL'Arbre, will tell the
Mother's Club of her "Escape From
Belgium," at their meeting at 2 p.m.,
March 21, in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Edgar Robinson, professor of
American history, will speak to them
on "The Influence of the Changes in
Education." Tea will be served by
the Alpha Phi Mothers.
