# It's Far From Dull Says 'Mrs. Flicky'
## 
### 
"There is never a dull moment in
the life of a housemother," according
to Mrs. Flickinger, who keeps the
Alpha Omicron Pi house running
smoothly.
Mrs. Flickinger, better known as
Mrs. Flicky, attended the University
of lowa, where she was a member of
Delta Gamma sorority. For the past
five years she has been a house
mother at Stanford. During this time
the A.O.Pi house has become noted
for its hospitality, brownies, and de
licious lemon chiffon pie.
To Mrs. Flickinger, the job of
managing a house for 30 girls is ac
companied by all the pleasures of
caring for a large family.
