# Everyone Invited To Sophomore Hike
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Donald B. Tresidder
will lead the sophomore class, and
all other students who would like
to go, on a hike through the Stan
lord hills this Saturday afternoon.
All hikers should meet the Tres
idders in front of the Alpha Belt
house at 1 o'clock. They will drive
out into the country before be
ginning the hike.
