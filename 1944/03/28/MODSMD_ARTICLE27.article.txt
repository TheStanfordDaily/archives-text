# Pvt. James F. Keller Wins Jacoby Contest
## 
### 
Pvt. James F. Keller, Co. A, of
Chicago, was awarded the 1944
Melville J. Jacoby Essay Prize of
$50, Professor Chilton R. Bush, ex
ecutive head of the Division of
Journalism, announced yesterday.
The subject was "After complete
victory over Japan, should the
United States retain the Mikado or
dethrone him and/or his succes
sors?"
