# Hoover Library Tours Conducted This Week
## 
### 
Army students who wish to take
a guided tour through the Hoover
War Library may do so today and
tomorrow between 11 and 2 o'clock,
and 4 and 5 o'clock, Mrs. G. Inez
Richardson, Research Associate of
the Library, announced yesterday.
