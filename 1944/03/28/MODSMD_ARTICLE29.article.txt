# Stanford G.I.'s Rate In Physical Fitness
## 
### 
Stanford AST trainees, alter
working on various exercises that
comprise the Physical Fitness
Tests, have placed themselves in
the upper 10 per cent of all AST
trainees in various colleges
throughout the country who have
taken these tests. To Coach Deon
and his associates an "orchid"
for their efforts in keeping us fit.
