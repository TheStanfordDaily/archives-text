# Vow Boy Topping Fights in Pacific
## 
### 
Keith Topping, Ail-American end
at Stanford in the days of the "Vow
Boye," is now with the New Zealand-
American forces occupying Green
Island, in the New Island Group in
the Pacific. Pfc. Topping took part
in the Bougainville invasion as a
member of a machine gun unit.
Topping played end on three con
secutive Indian Rose Bowl elevens
(1934, '35, and '36) and also was a
member of the boxing, basketball,
and debate teams.
