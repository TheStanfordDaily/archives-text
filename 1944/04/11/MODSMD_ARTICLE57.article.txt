# Tryouts for Play To Begin Tonight
## 
### 
Try outs for the first Stanford
Players production of spring quar
ter, "'The Truth About Blaydes," will
be held tonight and tomorrow night
at 7:30 in the Little Theater of Me
morial Hall.
"The Truth About Blaydes" was
written by the famous playwright A.
A. Milne who has such other hits to
his credit as "Mr. Pirn Passes By."
"Perfect Alibi," and "Michael and
Mary."
There are four men's parts and
four women's parts open in the play
and all Stanford students and faculty
are eligible with the exception of
first-quarter freshman women.
The play will be presented on May
10, 11, 12, 13, and 15 in the Little
Theater. Tickets will go on sale later
this month for 90 cents.
