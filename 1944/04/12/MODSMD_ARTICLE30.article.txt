# Bunn Will Attend Eastern Meeting
## 
### 
Dean of Men John Bunn has left
Stanford for Chicago, where he will
speak at a meeting of the "National
Association of Deans and Advisers of
Men." The meeting will last four
days, beginning April 12.
Subject of Dean Bunn's speech is,
"'Academic, Religious, and Social Ad
justments of the Postwar Period."
Stanley Sharp, assistant dean of
men, is acting dean in Mr. Bunn's ab
sence.
