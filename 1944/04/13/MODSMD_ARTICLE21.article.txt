# Program Announced For Today's Recital
## 
### 
The regular organ recital given
each Thursday afternoon at 5 p.m.
by Dr. D. Sterling Wheelwright will
include today "Across the Heather"
by Goss and "First Movement, Sym
phony I" by Guilmant.

Also on the program are Sibelius'
"Romance" and three Modern Minia
tures for Organ by George Frederick
McKay, which consist of "A Child's
Prayer," "Morning Song," and "Dox
ology."
