# Hanna Appointed to Education Committee
## 
### 
Paul R. Hanna, professor of educa
tion at Stanford University, has been
appointed a member of the Advisory
Committee on Education of the W.
K. Kellogg Foundation, Battle Creek,
Mich.
The committee consists of four
members, "each of whom has made
outstanding contributions in this
field," who will advise with respect
to the present and future education
programs of the foundation, accord
ing to Emory W. Morris, president of
the foundation.
