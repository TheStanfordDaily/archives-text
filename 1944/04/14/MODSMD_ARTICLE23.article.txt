# klahoya Sets First Meeting
## 
### 
The first of Klahoya's weekly
Tuesday meetings will be held April
18 in the Women's Clubhouse from
12 to 1. The special purpose of the
meeting will be to welcome all wom
en students interested in joining Kla
hoya.
Any woman student living off
campus is eligible to become a mem
ber and is cordially invited to attend
the first meeting. Dessert will be
served and the old members will
answer any questions about Klahoya.
The club was first founded under
the Y.W.C.A. with the purpose of
giving girls living off campus an op
portunity to keep in touch with on
campus life. It is now under the
A.S.S.U. and operating as a regular
campus organization of the A.S.S.U.
Klahoya sends representatives to
Women's Council, War Board,
Y.W.C.A., Social Chairmen and
Chapel Board meetings, and other
student organizations.
