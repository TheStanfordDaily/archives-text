# Women To Play in Softball Murals
## 
### 
Women s intramural team entries
for Softball this quarter must be
turned in to the Women's Gym
nasium, or to Miss Pat Cashell or
Barbara Burroughs by Tuesday,
April 18. Games will begin as soon
as entries and tournament arrange
ments have been completed, an
nounced Miss Cashel.
Teams may have a maximum of 10
players, and living groups are urged
to combine their numbers if they do
not have enough players to organize
a team alone. .
Games will be played four times a
week in a round-robin tournament,
and are scheduled for 3 o'clock on
the outdoor fields.
