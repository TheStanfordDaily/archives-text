# Knitting Group Plans for Term
## 
### 
The Knitting Committee of the
War Board held its first meeting of
the quarter yesterday at Miss Yost's
home on Lasuen. Plans for the
spring were made and finished arti
cles were turned in.
There will be two meetings a week,
according to Sallie Van Dyke, chair
man of the committee, on Tuesdays
and Thursdays from one to lour at
Miss Yost's home. Members of the
committee must attend at least one
meeting a week, and do some outside
work if possible.
