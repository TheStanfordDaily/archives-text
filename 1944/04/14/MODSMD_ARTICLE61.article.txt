# USO Hostess Course Offered
## 
### 
Today is the last day for girls in
terested in the USO hostess course to
sign up for it in the War Board office,
according to the USO chairmen,
Dickie Shainwald and Nancy Ban
ning.
This course consists of four lec
tures instructing girls as to meeting
soldiers, sailors, and marines, dress,
and general information. At the com
pletion of the course those who have
attended all four lectures will be
given certificates.
The time and place of the lectures
