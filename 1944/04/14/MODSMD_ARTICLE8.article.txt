# International Club To Meet Sunday
## 
### 
In keeping with the Pan-American
fiesta this weekend the International
Club will feature a program of music
of South America at its meeting at
7:30 p.m. Sunday at the home of Mrs.
Edwin J. Thomas, 465 Lincoln, Palo
Alto.
The records will be played by En
rique Sidwell. Stanford debater, who
will also give a short talk on the
countries in which the music orig
inated. Other countries to be fea
tured are Japan, China, and Hawaii.
The club is open to all students
interested and those taking foreign
languages are especially invited. All
old members of the club should be
present if possible as the election of
new officers and discussion of the
constitution and bv-laws is planned.
