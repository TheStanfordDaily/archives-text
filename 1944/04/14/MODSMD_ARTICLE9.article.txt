# Willbur Will Speak On NBC Program
## 
### 
Chancellor Ray Lyman Wilbur will
deliver from New York City the
commentary for the NBC program
"Here's to Youth" which will be
broadcast nationally Saturday, April
15. The program will be heard over
KPO from 10 to 10:30.
The first part of the program will
originate in Palo Alto as a drama
tization of Palo Alto's recreational
activities for children.
