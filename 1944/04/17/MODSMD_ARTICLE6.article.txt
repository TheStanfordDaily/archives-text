# Honor Code Talk Tonight at 7:15
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will speak and
answer questions on the Stanford
Honor Code at a meeting at 7:15 to
night in the Women's Clubhouse for
all new women and others interested.
Also on the program are Women's
Council members Helen Jones, who
will speak on the history of the code;
Helen Campbell, who will discuss the
code in relation to academics; Ann
Wendling, social aspects of the code;
and Dixie Good, functions of the
council and its coordination with
campus activities. Following the
talks, the meeting will be open for
questions and discussion.
At 8:30 lockout chairmen and
heads of lockout committees in Uni
versity residences will meet in "the
lower lounge for a discussion of plans
and policies for the spring quarter.
