# Workers Needed In Faculty Homes
## 
### 
Students, both men and women,
are needed to help work at faculty
and private homes on campus, ac
cording to Dede Bergerot of the War
Board. This work will include gar
dening, lawn mowing, housework,
serving table for parties, taking care j
of children, and washing windows.
This will be paid work and will '
count as war work. All students !
wishing to apply may sign up at the i
War Board office any afternoon from 1
1 to 5. Those who have already <
signed up for this work at the em- '
ployment service must sign up again, j
"The need for these workers is I
urgent," said Miss Bergerot, "and
anyone interested should sign up at
once."
