# Nurses Will Tour Stanford Friday
## 
### 
A Nurses' Tea will be given by
Women's Conference for some of the
nurses in the Stanford School of
Nursing on Friday, April 21 at 3:30
in the Lower Lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
About 14 nurses, some of them
former Stanford students, and two
advisors, have been invited to the
tea, according to Peggy Allan who is
in charge of the arrangements. They
will be conducted on a tour of the
campus first, stopping at the Con
Home, the Lake, and the Row.
Nurse's aides and those girls who
are planning to be nurses are espe
cially urged to come to the tea; how
ever. everyone on campus is wel
come. Quad clothes may be worn.
