# Folk Dance Group To Hold Meeting
## 
### 
The advanced section of the Stan
ford Dance Group will hold a short
business meeting at 8:30 to be fol
lowed by a regular dance session, ac
cording to Miss Ellen Davis, presi
dent of the group. The advanced
group is a member of the California
Federation of Folk Dancers.
Those who have already learned
the basic steps in folk dancing may
join the advanced group. After in
struction in South American dances,
the advanced group will leadn Pas
d'Espan, a Russian dance with Span
ish flavor. The group will then re
view some of the dances learned last
quarter.
