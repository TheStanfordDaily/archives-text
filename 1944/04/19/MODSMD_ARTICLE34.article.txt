# Klahoya Club Officers Elected for This Spring
## 
### 
At the first meeting of Klahoya
Club for spring quarter, Mary Phil
lips was chosen vice-president;
Glory Richardson will continue in
her capacity as club president.
Miss Richardson invites all off
eampus women, whether they are
graduate or regular division students.
The dues, which are $1.25 per quar
ter, cover the cost of desserts served
at each Tuesday noon meeting in the
Women's Clubhouse and they help
to pay for a page in the Quad.
