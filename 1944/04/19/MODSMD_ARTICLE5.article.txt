# Men To Discuss Honor Code. 7:30
## 
### 
All new men students, both fresh
men and otherwise, will meet for a
discussion of the Honor Code this
evening at 7:30. The meeting will be
held at Buchanan Hall, 650 Mayfield,
according to Stanley L. Sharp, acting
Dean of Men.
Tom Martzloff, Honor Code chair
man, and Jim Reynolds, head of
Men's Council, will explain the ap
plications of the code. Mr. Sharp will
speak about the problems of new
students at Stanford. Refreshments
will be served after the discussion.
Dr. Tresidder has been invited to
dinner at Buchanan, and students
arriving promptly at 7:30 will have
the opportunity of meeting him be
fore he leaves.
