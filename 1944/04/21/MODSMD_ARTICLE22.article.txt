# Carillonneur To Present Recital of Spirituals
## 
### 
A program of American Negro
spirituals will be played by the Stan
ford carillon in the tower of the
Hoover Library at the Saturday
noon-hour, James R. Lawson, caril
lonneur, announced. No carillon re
cital will be given Sunday.
Saturday's recital will include
"Singin' Wid a Sword in Ma Han',"
"Poor Wayfaring Stranger," "It's Me,
0 Lord," "Deep River,'' "Sometimes
1 Feel Like a Motherless Child,"
"Steal Away to Jesus," and "Were
You There When They Crucified My
Lord."
