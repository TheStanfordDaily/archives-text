# Russell, Douglas Star in Talkies
## 
### 
Rosalind Russell and Melvyn
Douglas star in the comedy-romance,
"This Thing Called Love," scheduled
for The Tillson Talkies to be shown
in Memorial Hall at 7:30 Sunday eve
ning, according to Nan Tillson, man
ager.
The fifth and sixth installments of
"Darkest Airica," the serial which
ran through winter quarter, will also
be presented. Two chapters of
"Darkest Africa" will be shown at
the every-other-week Sunday mov
ies until the end of spring quarter in
order that the serial can be com
pleted before graduation week.
The Talkies are open to the public
for an admission charge of 20 cents.
