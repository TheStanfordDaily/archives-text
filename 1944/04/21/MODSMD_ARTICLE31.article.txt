# Women Golfers Enter Tourney
## 
### 
All women golfers who plan on
entering the women's golf tourna
ment should give their qualifying
scores for nine holes to EL J. Rucker,
chairman of the tournament, before
5 on Sunday afternoon.
Scores may be handed in to Miss
Rucker at the Kappa house or tele
phoned in to her at P.A. 9079.
Opportunities for a prize in the
tournament are open for beginning
golfers up to top-flighters, as partici
pants will be divided into several
flights according to their qualifying
scores and handicaps, Miss Rucker
announced.
The tournament has been sched
uled for late in the month of April
