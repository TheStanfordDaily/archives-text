# Radio Institute Offered Again
## 
### 
Stanford, Radio Station KPO, and
the National Broadcasting Company
will combine for the second time this
summer to offer the Radio Institute
from July 10 to Sept. 2.
The course, according to an ad
vance bulletin, is designed for those
who have already had some experi
ence in professional radio and wish
to gain further knowledge in the
field and to increase their skills; for
those who have found that a knowl
edge of radio would be of aid to them
in the position they now hold; and
for those who have completed their
education and chosen radio as the
field they wish to enter.
Classes will meet three days a
week in the San Francisco studios of
KPO and three days a week at Stan
'ord. They may be taken for Uni
versity credit if desired.
