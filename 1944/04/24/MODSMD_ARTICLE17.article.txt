# Blood Donors Sign For Thursday, May 4
## 
### 
The second Blood Hank day of
spring quarter will be Thursday,
May 4. Signups may be made all
this week in the War Board office.
Faculty members and students—
civilian and military—may donate.
Donors should weigh lift pounds,
have waited eight weeks since their
last donation, and have a release
slip if under 21. Release slips are
available in the War Board ollice;
they must be signed by both par
ents and a witness, and turned in
when the student donates.
Appointments may be made at
11:20, 11:40, 1:20, and 1:40. Blood
is donated at the Community Cen
ter in Palo Alto. Transportation,
if needed, will be provided by the
Red Cross Motor Corps
