# Spring Show Tryouts Tonite
## 
### 
Tryouts for the '44 Spring Show
will be held tonight and Wednesday
night from 7:30 to 10 in the Me
morial Auditorium.
Tonight tryouts will be made in
the specialty acts for dancing, acting,
and chorus work. Wednesday there
will be tryouts in the above things
plus singing tryoutees.
Anyone who has original music or
script for the blackface scene should
bring it the above nights also.
They would also like anyone who
can play an instrument which can
be worked into the specialty acts to
come in these tryoutee nights.
The cast announcements will be
made Friday.
