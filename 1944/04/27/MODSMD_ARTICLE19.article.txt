# Soccer Players To Sign With Coach Maloney
## 
### 
Students, graduates, and under
graduates interested in soccer may
register for this sport between now
and Monday in Coach Harry Ma
loney's office between 10 and 12 a.m.
and 2 to 5 p.m.
It is understood, however, that
graduate students may register with
out the usual unit of credit. Under
graduates may register under sim
ilar conditions and would not be
under any obligation to turn out reg
ularly three times a week for prac
tice or games, but at least one or
more times when convenient.
Students who so register will be
furnished with all equipment. This
equipment will be issued next Mon
day at 3:30 p.m. in the store room,
Men's Gymnasium.
