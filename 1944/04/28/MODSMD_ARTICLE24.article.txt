# Carillon Will Play Foster Melodies
## 
### 
Weekend music from the Hoover
War Library planned by James R.
Lawson, carillonneur. will be as fol
lows:
Saturday, April 29 at 12 noon, |
Stephen Foster melodies, consisting |
of "Ring, Ring the Banjo," "Gentle
Annie." "Old Black Joe," ''Oh Sus
ana," "Jeanie with the Light Brown
Hair," and "Old Folks at Home."
Sunday, April 30 at 1:30, works by
George Frederick Handel, consisting
of "The Harmonious Blacksmith,"
"Largo" (from Xerxes), "Sonata for
a Musical Clock," "Air" (from Ri
naldo), and "Water Music Suite."
