# Army Address Changes

###

week. Soldier-students of this
command are now classified as a
service command unit. The only
outward change is the substitution
of SOU 3905 for ASTU 3905 in the
mail address.
Word has been received that
members of similar units in other
commands have replaced the lamp
and sword with service command
patches; however, according to
latest information available, train
ees here will ocntinue to wear the
present insignia.
