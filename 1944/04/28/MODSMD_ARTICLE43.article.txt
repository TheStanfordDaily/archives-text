# Union Excom To Invite Hall Leaders for Tea
## 
### 
The Executive Committee of Union
Unit is entertaining the Roble and
Ventura sponsors and senators at a
tea at Union Residence Tuesday aft
ernoon, according to Betty Lou Hor
ton. chairman.
There will be two tours of the unit
in the atternoon, that the women may
become better acquainted with the
Union living groups.
