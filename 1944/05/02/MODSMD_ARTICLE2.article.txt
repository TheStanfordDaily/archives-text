# Casas To Present Singing Preview
## 
### 
The casas at Lagunita Court will
present a preview of their Spring
Sing selections on Thursday, May 4.
after dinner.
The preview will be held in the
patio where after-dinner coffee will
be served immediately preceding the
singing.
Casa Adelfa will sing an original
composition; Eucalypto. "I Love
You"; Granada, "Smoke Gets in Your
Eyes"; Naranja, "The Song' Is You";
Magnolia and Olivo combined. "Be
gin the Beguine."
