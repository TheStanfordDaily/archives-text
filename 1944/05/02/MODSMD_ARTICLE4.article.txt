# Bradley, Knoles At Annual Meet
## 
### 
Dr. Harold W. Bradley and Dr.
George H. Knoles of the history de
partment recently attended the an
nual meeting of the Mississippi Val
ley Historical Association. The meet
ing was held in St. Louis. Mo. on
April 20. 21, and 22.
Dr. Bradley was chairman of the
committee which arranged the meet
ing, Dr. Knoles was one of four
speakers who took part in a formal
discussion of the report of the Com
mittee on American History in
Schools and Colleges.
The Mississippi Valley Historical
Association is a national association
of people interested in American his
tory. Their annual meeting is held
each spring .
