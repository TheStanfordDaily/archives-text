# Sign-ups Begin For Carnival
## 
### 
The sign-up sheets for the events
in the Junior Water Carnival May 13
have been posted in every living
group. If you want to enter you
should sign up as soon as possible
before the events are filled, according
to Ann Wendling, chairman.
Many of the events will require
no skill and will be a lot of fun: The
novelty races and canoe-filling races
are examples.
The women and men army stu
dents are especially invited to the
carnival to participate in the events.
The faculty is also urged to par
ticipate so that the whole campus
will be represented.
The Water Carnival will feature
besides events a beauty contest in
which all the women's living groups
will compete. Refreshments will be
served during the program.
