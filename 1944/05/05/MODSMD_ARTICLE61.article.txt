# Choir To Wear New Cardinal Gowns Sunday
## 
### 
The new cardinal gowns of the
Stanford University Choir will be
used for the first time in a regular
service on Sunday, May 7.
The choir will begin the service
with a processional.
Dr. D. Elton Trueblood, University
chaplain, will give the sermon, and
Leonard Temple will be the student
reader.
