# New Draft Schedule Proposed for 26—38
## 
### 
WASHINGTON (UP) — A new
draft schedule for all men 26 and
older is expected to be drawn up
tomorrow at the annual meeting be
tween National Selective Service Di
rector Louis B. Hershey and draft
heads of the 48 states. •
It was believed the draft officials
may order a ban on induction of
men 2G to 38 until a specified date,
possibly July 1 and that men 30 to 38
may be deferred en masse until about
Dec. 1.
