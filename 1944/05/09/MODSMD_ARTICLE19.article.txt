# Poets To Compete For Prize of $40
## 
### 
Campus poets are urged to sub
mit as many poems and sonnets as
they wish for the Irene Hardy Poetry
Prize of $40, announced by the* Eng
lish department.
The contest is open to all under
graduate students at Stanford to en
courage the writing of original
poetry. Poems may be of any length.
Students interested may obtain
rules for submitting their entries at
the office of the secretary of the
English department, Rom 43. The
contest closes May 25.
The prize was established in 1925
by friends of Irene Hardy, former in
structor in English at Stanford, in
her memory as a biannual prize of
$40.
