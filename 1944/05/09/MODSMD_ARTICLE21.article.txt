# Pi Lambda Theta Plans Tea May 20
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, the Women's
Honorary Education FrateriTity, is
planning a tea on Saturday, May 20,
at 3 p.m. in the social room of the
Education building.
As guests they have invited the
Omega chapter of the fraternity from
the University of California and the
San Francisco alumni.
Besides tea in the social room, the
group will visit the rarely opened
Hoover War Library and plan to go
up to the top of the tower.
