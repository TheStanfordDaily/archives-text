# Salvage Collection Finishes Monday

### 
The second salvage collection of
the quarter will be held next Mon
day, May 15, announced Barbara.
Pike .salvage chairman. Salvage rep
resentatives in living groups should

The articles which will be collected

trical appliances, furnishings, bric
a-brac, paper, magazines, news
