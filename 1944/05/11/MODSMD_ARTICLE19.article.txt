# Casas Announce Volleyball Results
## 
### 
Results of the Inter-Casa Volley
bull Tournament sponsored by La
gunita and played on Tuesday are as
follows: Ventura 2 defeated Olivo
15-8 and 15-4; winning Granada 1
had scores of 15-7 and 15-2 against
Naranja; and Adelfa had the narrow
er lead of 3-15, 17-15, and 15-11 over
Euealypto.
The Inter-Casa Tournament is a

I new feature at Lagunita which is de
signed to bring the various casas
more closely together. It is a Round
Robin Tournament in which each
team will play every other team
Nor are the games confined to La
gunita alone. Ventura is a regular
member contributing two teams, and
| various casas are planning to chal
lenge other living groups.
