# SIR FREDERICK VISITS
## 
### 
On Friday, May 12, Sir Frederick
Puckle, recently appointed Adviser
on Indian Affairs to the British Em
bassy in Washington, D.C., will be
the guest of Stanford University
when he will visit here with Mr. and
Mrs. Godfrey Fisher, British Consul-
General and his wife, from San Fran
cisco. They will visit the Hoover
Tower, Memorial Church, and other
points of interest on the campus.
