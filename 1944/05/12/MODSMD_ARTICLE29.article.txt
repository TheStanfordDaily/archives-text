# SWB Will Collect Salvage Monday
## 
### 
Clothes, rags, electrical appliances,
furnishings, bric-a-brac, paper, mag
azines, newspapers, rubber, metals
of all kinds (especially tin cans), and
Victory Books are needed for the war
effort.
All these articles will be collected
in the second salvage drive of the
quarter which will be held next
Monday morning at 8, announced
Barbara Pike, salvage chairman.
Salvage representatives in all
campus living groups will collect
scrap over the weekend or will place
a box in a convenient place for do
nations. Everyone is urged to co
operate in this drive and save any of
the articles listed above.
