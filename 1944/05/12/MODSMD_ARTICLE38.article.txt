# Chapel Topic To Give Answers of Jesus
## 
### 
At the regular University service
of worship on Sunday morning. May
14, at 11 o'clock, Dr. D. Elton True
blood, chaplain, will give the sermon,
the subject of which will be "The
Questions Christ Can Answer."
The faculty reader of the Scripture
lesson will be Professor C. V. Tay
lor, head of the School of Biological
Sciences.
Mr. Richard Hagopian, tenor, from
Menlo Junior College, will be the
soloist; and the University Choir will
sing the anthem.
