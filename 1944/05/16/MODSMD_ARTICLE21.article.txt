# GOLF ENTRIES DUE
## 
### 
A golf tournament, the first of this
quarter, will be one of the features
of the Con Home Drive Thursday.
The tourney will be a mixed two
ball loursorrie. It will be played on
the Stanford links, and the entry fee
is 25 cents. The couples are to be
divided into three flights according
to ability, assuring an equal chance
for all participants.
