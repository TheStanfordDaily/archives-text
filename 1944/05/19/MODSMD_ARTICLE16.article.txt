# Heron To Speak On Postwar Jobs
## 
### 
Alexander R. Heron, director of
reconstruction and re-employment
for the State of California, will dis
cuss the question, "Who Will Fur
nish Postwar Jobs?" in a lecture to
be given in the auditorium of Me
morial Hall at Stanford University
on Tuesday, May 23, at 7:30 p.m.
The public is invited free of charge
to attend the lecture, which will be
one of the regular Tuesday Evening
Series sponsored by the Committee
on Public Exercises.
