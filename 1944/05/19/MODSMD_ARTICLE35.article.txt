# Carillonneur Will Play Five Bach Selections
## 
### 
A carillon recital will be presented
from the tower of the Hoover War
Library Sunday, May 21, at 1:30, ac
cording to Carillonncur James R.
Lawson.
The program will include five Bach
selections, "Prelude and Fugue in C
major," "Sarabande" (from II English
Suite), "Prelude No. 1" (from Well
Tempered Clavichord), a chorale,
"Komm, Susser Tod" ("Come, Sweet
Death"), and "Toccata in D Minor."
