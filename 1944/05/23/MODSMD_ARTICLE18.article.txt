# 50 Blood Donors Needed for June 1
## 
### 
Fifty students and faculty members
can be accommodated at the next
Stanford Blood Bank Day. which will
be Thursday. June 1. Appointments
to donate may be made at 11, 11:20,
11:40. 1, 1:20, and 1:40.
All signups for June 1 must be
made by Saturday of this week. The
sign-up sheet is in the War Board
office.
All students are eligible to donate;
if under 21. however, they must ob
tain a release slip from the War
Board and have it signed by both
parents and a witness. Donors must
weigh 110 pounds and have waited
eight weeks since their last donation.
Blood is donated at the Community
Center in Palo Alto. Transportation,
if needed, will be furnished by the
Red Cross Motor Corps.
