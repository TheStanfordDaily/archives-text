# SPONSOR INTERVIEWS
## 
### 
All Upper Division women inter
ested in being sponsors for summer
and fall, or fall and winter quarters
should remember to appear before
the Sponsor-Choosing Committee on
Thursday 9 to 12 a.m. or Friday, 1
to 4 p.m. in the Excom room. Posi
tions for sponsors will be open in
Pohle for summer and fall and fall
and winter; in Lagunita, Ventura,
and Union they will be for fall and
winter only.
