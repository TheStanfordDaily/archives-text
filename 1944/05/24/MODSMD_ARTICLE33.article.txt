# Postponed Games Playoff Tonight
## 
### 
Due to the Con Home Labor Day
barbecue yesterday night, all sched
uled games in the women's intra
mural volleyball league were post
poned until today.
They will be played off at 7 and
7:30 tonight on the women's courts.
At 7 Olivo meets Magnolia 1; Gra
nada 1, Eucalypto 2; Ventura 1, Na
ranja 1: and Eucalypto 1, Naranja 2.
At 7:30 the games are Olivo vs.
Eucalypto; Granada 1, Magnolia 1,
Ventura 1. Naranja 2; and Euca
lypto 2. Naranja 1.

One-half the flour and feed mills
in Canada are located in the prov
ince of Ontario.
