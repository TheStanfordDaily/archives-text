# Dr. Tanner To Speak At Forum Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Obert C. Tanner will open the
first meeting of the University
Christian Forum at 7:15 p.m. in the
vestry of the Memorial Church to
night. Dr. Tanner has been travel
ing in the East, and he will speak of
the present religious situation in the
light of that trip.
This group will meet regularly on
Thursday evenings every week, and
all members of the faculty and stu
dent body are invited to attend.
The purpose of the meetings is to
give people an opportunity to dis
cuss some of the practical applica
tions of Christianity as well as to
discuss philosophical and ethical
problems of Christianity.
