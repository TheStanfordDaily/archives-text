# All-Campus Hike To Be Saturday
## 
### 
Dr. Donald B. Tresidder will lead
an all-campus hike sponsored by La
gunita this Saturday at 1 p.m.. ac
cording to Mary Lou McClure,
chairman of all Tresidder hikes.
The group will leave Lagunita in
cars. They will drive approximately
four miles to the starting point of
the hike.
. Miss McClure requests all persons
who can possibly use their cars on
Saturday to call her today or to
morrow at Roble, 5171.
Box lunches will be provided for
all Lagunita girls, according to
Louise Kaiser, chairman of the hike.
Students from the other living groups
are requested to bring their own
lunches.
