# University Organist Lists Recital Program
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wheewlright, acting
University organist, has announced
the following program for the reg
ular 5 p.m. organ recital Sunday:
"Evening Rest," by Flor Peelers;
"Allegro, Symphony I," by Maquaire;
"Nocturne," by Ferrata; "A Lively
Dance." by Chenoweth; "Anno Dom
ini 1865," by Alan Floyd: and "Al
legro con fuoco, Sonata in A minor,"
by Borowski.
