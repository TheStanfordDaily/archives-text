# Daily Axe Out For Chappie
## 
### AGATHA NUSSBAUM 
Alter 24 hours of fasting and
prayer Chappie members announced
this morning that they were ready

for the execution
this afternoon.
Daily axmen,
preparing for the
ministra
tions, spent the
night in revelry
and hilarious con-j
templation of the'
grisly humor to be
involved when the
powerful, exalted
Daily team conde
scends to trounce
the weak,
insignificant Chap-s
pie team. *
As a matter of
fact, the Daily is
going to nail up

the Chappie in its own coffin and
throw away the hammer, trusting
that such an ignominious, complete
defeat will forever silence the puerile
droolings of Chappies. As to the Old
Boy—no mention will be made, as
the Daily does not believe in attack
ing personalities.
So that everyone can witness the
execrable Chappy team in action,
and get an eyeful of real baseball as
played by the Daily, the game (which
is merely a formality as far as the
outcome is concerned) is open to the
public and everyone is invited to the
sunken diamond.
The end (for the Chappie) wHI be
gin at 4 p in.
