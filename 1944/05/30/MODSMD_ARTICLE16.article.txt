# Librarian Feted At Farewell Party
## 
### 
Mrs. Florence Woods, librarian of
the Graduate School of Business,
leaves today for Portland after
spending two quarters at Stanford.
The business school held a going
away party for Mrs. Woods yesterday
afternoon at 3 at which she was pre
sented with a compact bearing the
Stanford seal as a gift from the stu
dents of the school.
The party was attended by 43 stu
dents. faculty, and office staff mem
bers of the business school.
