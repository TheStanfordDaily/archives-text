# Hall Nominations Set for Today
## 
### 
Lagunita nominations for presi
dent, first vice-president, second
vice-president, and chairman of
Committee of Seven will be today,
according to Muriel Tarr, chairman
of elections.
Declinations will take place to
morrow; speeches and introduction
of candidates, June 5; elections and
quiz show, June G.
Casa presidents and Committee of
Seven representatives will be elected
June 9.
The committee on elections work
ing with Miss Tarr are Maddy Brill,
publicity, Louise Phillips, nomina
tions; Doreen Bailey, quiz show;
Eleanor Marks, Barbara Beardslee,
voting; leadership meeting, Virginia
Hobbs and Lorraine Harband.
