# Moot Court Trial Holds Semi-Finals
## 
### 
Second semi-finals trial of the 1944
Stanford Law School Moot Court
competition will be held in the Edu
cation building tonight at 7:30.
The problem in both the semi
final and final contests concerns
"Miss Endo Harikiri," who, according
to the problem, is an American citi
zen of Japanese ancestry 22 years of
age. She is, at the time the problem
is presented, confined in a War Re
location Camp, though she is loyal to
the United States.
Tonight Miss Margaret Morton and
Miss Elizabeth Murray will argue
against Mr. Leonard Low. Finals will
be Monday. June 5.
