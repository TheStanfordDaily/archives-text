# Farm Fencers Invited To Open House June 11
## 
### 
Students interested in fencing are
invited to participate in the Fencing
Open House, Sunday, June 11, from
1 to 5 in the Women's Gym.
Following the fencing, participants
will relax in the pool and will be
served refreshments.
Men interested should contact Mr.
Bugge at the Men's Gym and women,
Dorro Converse at Lagunita.
