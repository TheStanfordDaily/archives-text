# Lagunita To Hold Dr. I.Q. Quiz Show
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will present a "Dr.
1.Q." quiz program tomorrow night
in the Eucalypio Social Room at 8,
according to Marianne Lion, in
charge of arrangements. Sarah Wills
has been selected as Dr. I.Q. and will
preside over the entire show.
A question board has been ap
pointed, and questions are being ob
lained by the combined efforts of all
court women. The contestants will
volunteer from the audience, accord
ing to Miss Lion. Prizes will be given
for correct answers.
A 25-cent admission price will be
charged; proceeds from the affair
will be turned over to the junior
scholarship fund. Refreshments will
be served lo those present.
