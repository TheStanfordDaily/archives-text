# Union Unit To Hold Elections Today
## 
### 
Elections to choose new officers for
the Union Unit Executive Committee
will be held today at Union Resi
dence. Voting will be done after the
lunch and dinner hours in the lobby,
according to Lorraine Streblow,
president of Union Unit.
Candidates have been selected by
the nominating committee of the
present executive committee. They
are, for president. Betty Lou Horton
aid Philis Ludlam; vice-president,
Mary Ellen Booth, Pat Shephard,
Marianne Shipley; Chairman of the
Committee of Seven. Jacqueline Jar
dine and Jucy Vance.
