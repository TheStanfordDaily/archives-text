# Sixth Takes Mokmer
## 
### 
ALLIED HQ., Southwest Pacific, June 8 (UP)
American troops of the Sixth Army overcame bitte*.
enemy resistance in a two-day drive and captured
Mokmer on Biak Island off the Dutch New Guinea
coast, Allied Headquarters announced today.
Capture of the airdrome, most important of the three
airstrips on the island, gives allied four-engined bomb
ers a base less than 9,000 miles from the southernmost
of the Philippines.
