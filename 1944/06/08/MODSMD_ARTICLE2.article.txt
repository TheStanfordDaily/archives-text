# Farm Dancers To Play Host At Folk Festival
## 
### 
Sunday afternoon, June 11, in the
Frost Amphitheater on the Stanford
campus, the Stanford Folk Dancers
will play hosts to the California
Dance Federation for their monthly
festival.
Dancing in which everyone may
join will continue throughout the
afternoon, the program being made
up of dances of many different coun
tries, including Swedish, Danish,
Russian, Estonian, English, and
American Cowboy dances. There
will also be several exhibition
dances.
The dancing, done on the green to
recorded folk musie, starts at 1
o'clock and continues until (5 o'clock.
The public is invited to attend and to
participate in the folk dancing.
