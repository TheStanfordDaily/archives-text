# Lagunita To Hold Senior Breakfast
## 
### 
Lagunita seniors will be honored at
the Annual Senior Breakfast on
Sunday, June 18, according to Louise
Kaiser, chairman. Students gradu
ating the end of spring and summer
quarters are invited.
The girls, who have been chosen to
act as junior waitresses, are Sue
Chute, Pat Charleton, Joyce Costello,
Lorain Harband, Jan Jacques, Louise
Kaiser, Betty Kenny, Winifred
Lamm, Joey Nell Ryan, Tommy
Thomas, Norma Trevorrow, Alice
Tuttle, Zeryne Wood.
