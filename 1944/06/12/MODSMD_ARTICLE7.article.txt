# Nimitz' Carrier Planes Blast Guam, Marianas
## 
### 
PEARL HARBOR (UP)-The U.S.
Pacific Fleets in a powerful strike
Saturday unleashed its carrier-based
aircraft along a 150-mile stretch of
the Mariana Islands at the southern
flank of Japan hitting Saipan, Tinian,
and Guam, Adm. Chester W. Nimitz
announced today.
This major action followed nu
merous recent air forays against Jap
anese held Pacific strong points.
