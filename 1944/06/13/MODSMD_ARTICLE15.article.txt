# Frosh Y Holds Fashion Show
## 
### 
Last function of the quarter for the
freshman Y.W.C.A. will be held
Thursday at 2:30 in the auditorium
on the main floor of the Women's
Clubhouse. A fashion show, display
ing clothes from the Mary Roy dress
shop, will be niven with freshman
women as models, according to Vir
ginia Walker, secretary of the frosh
"Y."
Models for the show will be Nancy
Traverso. Joan Hall, Louise Smith,
Virginia Walker. Elka Robbins, Jody
Lemay, Bobby Morse, Carol Stearns,
and Beth Johnson. Jane Frazier,
Roble songwriter, will play the piano
for the show.
