# Tour Conducted For Polish Consul
## 
### 
Visit inn the campus yesterday
were Professor W. Lednicke and Dr.
Wladyslaw Sokolowski, Consul Gen
eral of Poland. Professor Lednicke
spoke on "Cultural Russian-Polish
Relations" at a discussion presided
over by H. H. Fisher, chairman. Mr.
Fisher also conducted Professor
Lednicke and Dr. Sokolowski on a
tour of the campus which included a
visit to the top of the Hoover War
Library.
