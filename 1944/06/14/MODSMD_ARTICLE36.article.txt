# Newman Club Has Panel Discussion
## 
### 
The Stanford Newman Club will
discuss papal peace proposals at their
meeting Wednesday evening in the
vestry of Memorial Church at 8:30.
A panel discussion led by Sue Rior
dan and Pfc. Bob Hughes will start
the evening meeting. Sue and Bob
have been given recent papal papers
to read, and after their reports, the
audience will be invited to ask ques
tions and to add further to the in
formation.
As this will be one of the last
meetings fo the club for this quarter,
all Catholic students on campus are
invited to attend. President Pfc. Hal
Niekamp will present a review of the
club's activities during this quarter
and will tell of the tentative summer
quarter plans. The treasurer will
give a financial report including Fri
day's hay ride.
