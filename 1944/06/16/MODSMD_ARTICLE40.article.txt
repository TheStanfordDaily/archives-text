# Union Breakfast To Feature Skit
## 
### 
The women of Union Unit will
entertain their graduating seniors at
a breakfast in the Union main dining
room at 10 a.m. Sunday, according to
the chairman, Annette Gattuccio.
To carry out the theme, "Stanford
Days," Miss Gattuccio has written a
skit featuring Mrs. Orthenius Befud
dled, Jo Davis, and a commentator,
Ruth Lewis. Mary Ellen Booth and
Betti Fraser are in charge of decora
tions.
Guests at the senior breakfast will
be Dean Mary Yost, Miss Rosamond
Clark, Miss Ruth Williams, Mrs.
Margaret Hamlin, Mrs. Dorothy Bell,
Miss Christine Ricker, and Miss Jane
Bradley, director of the unit.
