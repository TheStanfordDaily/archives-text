# Carillon Recital Salutes Students
## 
### 
A thirty minute carillon recital will
be given by Jim Lawson, carillon
neur, at the Hoover Tower on Sun
day afternoon at 2:30.
The carillons were presented to
the Hoover Tower by the Belgian
Government in recognition of the re
lief work done by former President
Herbert C. Hoover in World War I.
This recital is presented in honor
of the students who are entering
Stanford for the first time.
