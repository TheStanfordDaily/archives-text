# Old 'Rough' Ends Course in Gas War
## 
### 
Second Lt. John A. Galen, who was
a student at Stanford University be
fore entering the Army, recently fin
ished a four weeks training program
with the 15th Unit Gas Officers,
Chemical Warfare School at Edge
wood Arsenal, Maryland.
Some of the subjects he studied
were: protective clothing, decontami
nation, treatment of gas casualties,
and chemical warfare materiel and
tactics.
