# 'Yanks in Orient' Resumed by KGO
## 
### 
"Yanks in the Orient," a special
series of 26 transcribed broadcasts
dealing with action in the China-
Burma-India threater of war, will
he resumed over KGO and the Blue
Network, on Tuesdays at 4:30 p.m.,
PWT.
Included in the programs will be
interviews with prisoners of war,
broadcasts from behind the Japanese
lines, and. a recording of the State
Department propaganda loud speaker
directed against the enemy 50 yards
in front of their advance line.
The public service series will be
handled by Lt. Col. Paul L. Jones;
Lt. Bert Parks, announcer; and Lt.
Finis Farr, continuity man and for
mer radio script writer.
