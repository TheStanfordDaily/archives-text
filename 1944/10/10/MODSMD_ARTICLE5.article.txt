# Men's Rent Due Today Instead of Thursday
## 
### 
A headline in yesterday's Daily
erroneously announced that Men's
Hoard and Koom were to he paid
on Thursday, instead of Tuesday
or today.
In other words. Men's Room
Kent, Men's Hoard, are due today,
and a fine of Si per day will he as
sessed for delinquents.
