# Daily to Welcome Staff Candidates.
## 
### 
All new students interested in
working on the editorial side of the
Stanford Daily will meet today in
the Shack at 3:00 o'clock to sign up
for active duty.
Ink-stained old timers are wel
come, and appointments to the staff
will be made on the basis of inter
est, experience, and ability. Edito
rial writers are needed, as are fea
ture writers, reporters, and writers
specializing in news of interest to
women.
Those students who cannot come
to the Daily at 3:00 should drop in
after three, but definitely today.
