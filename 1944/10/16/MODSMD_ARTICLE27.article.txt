# Union Unit Elects Officers for Fall
## 
### 
Marjorie Ambler was elected treas
urer of the Union Unit and Helen
Peterson was elected council repre
sentative at elections held last week.
* Carol Torresen was previously
elected Secretary for the Sopho
mores.
The new officers will act for the
girls of Union Residence and the
houses of Madrono, Manzanita, Mari
posa, Oaks, Elm, and Hawthorne.
This year two additional houses have
been added to the Union Unit group
also. They are Juniper Lodge with
Virginia Hobbs as president, and the
Redwoods with Elizabeth Bernhard
as president.
