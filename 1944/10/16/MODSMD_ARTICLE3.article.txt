# New Daily Tryoutees Will Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
Those trying out to become
regular members of the Stanford
Daily editorial staff will meet
Tuesday afternoon at 5:00 in Room
8 of the Journalism Building.
The try-out stories about Dr.
Tresidder's speech will be handed
in and a proof reading test will be
given. Dave Davis, editor of the
Daily, will conduct the meeting.
