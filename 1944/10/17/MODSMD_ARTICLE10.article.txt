# Strickland Names Dover Road Cast
## 
### 
Cast for A. A. Milne's "The Dover
Road," opening at the Little Theater
Wednesday, Nov. 8, was announced
yesterday by Professor F. C. Strick
land, director of the Stanford Play
ers. Tryouts for this first play of the
season have been taking place during
the past week.
Mr. Latimer—John High; Dominic
—Joaquin Aguirre; Leonard—New
ton Arnold; Anne—Hazel Spore;
Nicholas—Harvey Small; Eustasia —
Sally Stocker.
