# Rabbitt Elected New Conference Treasurer
## 
### 
Women's Conference's first meet
ing was held yesterday in the
Room of the Women's Clubhouse.
Betsy Rabbitt was unanimously
elected Treasurer for the coming
year, and it was decided to have the
Conference meetings every Monday
at 3:15 in the Chintz Room.
Sally Breuner, President, particu
larly asked girls to bring suggestions
that might benefit the new freshmen
on campus to meetings, and to bring
up problems that any girl or living
group might have.
