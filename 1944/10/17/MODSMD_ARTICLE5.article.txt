# First War Board Conference Today
## 
### 
First meeting of all Stanford
War Board auxiliary representatives
elected in women's living groups
last night will be held this after
noon in the basement of the Women's
Club House at 5:00 o'clock, accord
ing to Mary Lou Patton. SWB aux
iliary chairman.
All representatives unable to at
tend should phone Miss Patton at
8982 after 6:00 p.m. before this
Thursday.
One of the most functional and
indispensable parts of the SWB, the
auxiliary representatives meet with
the auxiliary head and then convey
War Board news to their living
groups.
