# Fitts To Hear Original Songs
## 
### 
Tryouts for those who have origi
nal songs which might be suitable
for this fall's Gaieties, the annual
variety show, will take place this
Thursday and Friday at 7:30 to 10
p.m. in Memorial Auditorium.
There will be a piano available,
and the tryouts will take place under
the supervision of Peggy Fitts, di
rector of the show.
At the same time, those interested
in working with the technical crew
may report to Corky Wollman. This
includes props, lighting, night crew,
set designing and scenery, etc.
Acting tryouts will not take place
till the following week at the same
time.
