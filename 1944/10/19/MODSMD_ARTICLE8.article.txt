# British Second Army Takes Dutch Junction
## 
### 
ALLIED SUPREME PIDQ, Thurs.,
Oct. 19 —(U.R)—The British Second
Army captured the Dutch junction,
Venray, Wednesday and was .re
ported by Berlin to have plunged
toward the West Wall on a thirty
mile front with one hundred and
fifty thousand men, while American
Forces to the south wheeled into
Bonhomme Pass which leads through
the Vosges into German Bedam.
At Aachen, the U.S. First Army
pocketed down the last haggard
remnants of the German garrison in
old Roman tunnels under the city
and opened a booming barrage with
the "Long Tom'' artillery against the
German emergency Rhine defenses
in the east.
