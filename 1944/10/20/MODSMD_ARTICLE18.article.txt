# David Hunt Wins Scholarship From American Legion
## 
### 
David A. Hunt, Stanford student,
received one of ten national com
mander's scholarships at the 26th an
nual convention of the American
Legion.
He received the scholarship upon
the recommendation of the national
commander's scholarship committee,
headed by General P. C. Harris, who
also is chairman of the Legion's com
mittee for education of orphans of
war veterans. The scholarships are
worth $400 in cash and have been
awarded annually since 1841 to sons
of deceased war veterans.
David is the son of Dr. and Mrs.
Fred W. Hunt of Tulare, California. '
