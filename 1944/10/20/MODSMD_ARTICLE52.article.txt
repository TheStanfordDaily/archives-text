# Salvage Collection To Be November 4
## 
### 
The first collection or papers, mag
azines, rubber, and metal scraps for
the Stanford salvage drive will be
made November 4, Chairmen Breen
Ratterman and Quincy Brown an
nounced yesterday.
The second collection is slated for
December 2, but the drive will be
continual and students should fill
their living groups salvage boxes
throughout the quarter, Miss Ratter
man said.
