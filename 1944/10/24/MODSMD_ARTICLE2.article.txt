# Palo Falls to Yanks; Troops Head Inland to Seize Highway
## Dowrie Will Speak at 7:30 
### 
"Uncle Shylock, Uncle Sap, or
What?" is the subject on which Dr.
George YV. Dowrie will lecture to
night at 7:30 in the auditorium of
Memorial Hall.
America's role in financing world
reconstruction in the post-war period
will be discussed in tonight's lecture,
one of the regular Tuesday Evening
Series sponsored by the University
Committee on Public Exercises. The
lecture is open to the public without
admission charge.
Dr. Dowrie, professor of finance in
Stanford's Graduate School of Busi
ness, is well-known as an economist
and the author of several books.
Among the books written by Dr.
Dowrie are "American Monetary and
Banking Policy," "Money and Bank
ing," and "Investments." Dr. Dowrie
was dean of the Business School at
the University of Minnesota for
seven years. He came to Stanford
in 192G.
