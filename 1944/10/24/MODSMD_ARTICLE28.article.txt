# Golf Club Members To Play Gardeners
## 
### 
Members of the Golf Club are
urged to appear Thursday afternoon
at 2 p.m. on the practice green near
the hockey field where they will re
ceive golf credit for weeding at least
an hour, Margaret Meadowcroft,
president of the club announced to
day.
Girls are reminded to bring a
weeder or jackknife as the W.A.A.
supply is limited. Thursday's weed
ing session will be the second of this
quarter.
