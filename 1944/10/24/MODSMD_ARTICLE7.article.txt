# Red Spearheads Reach 31 Miles Inside Reich
## 
### 
LONDON. Tues., Oct. 24—(U.R)—
Marshal Josef Stalin announced last
night that Red Army troops, invad
ing Germany on an 87 mile front, had
captured more than 400 towns in
East Prussia, and Berlin said that
Russian spearheads had reached 31
miles inside German territory.
