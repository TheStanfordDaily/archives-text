# Newman Library Open To Students
## 
### 
Newman Library, sponsored by
the Newman Club of Stanford, is
housed in the Reserve Book Room of
the Main Libe. The shelves are
straight ahead and a little to the
right as you walk in the libe.
The libe contains books of inter
est to all on campus, but especially
to members of the Newman Club,
who will enjoy such books as: "The
Confessions of St. Augustine," "The
Reed of God," by Caryll House
lander, "St. Thomas Aquinas, His
Life and Work," and countless other
volumes which make interesting
reading.
All Catholic students on campus
are invited to become acquainted
with the Newman Library.
