# Magnolia, Enema Exchange Dinner
## 
### 
Casa Magnolia and the Pre-med
army students at Encina held an ex
change dinner last night. Twelve
pre-meds went to dinner at Lagu
nita and twelve girls went to Encina
Afterwards the groups played
bridge and danced in the Magnolia
Social Room and at Encina.
