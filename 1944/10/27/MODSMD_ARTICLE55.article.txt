# Women For Motor Corps Now
## 
### 
Stanford women who wish to con
tribute their chauffering skills to the
Red Cross Motor Corps may sign the
list at the War Board office, and
training and driving schedules may
be arranged, Jeanette Grimsley, War
Board Coordinator, announced yes
terday.
Women contributing to the Pale
Alto Corps would be driving for
Dibble Hospital, Miss Grimsley
stated. Applicants must be 18 years
of age and pass Red Cross courses
in basic and advanced first aid, and
motor mechanics. Girls may work
while training. A Red Cross repreu
sentative will speak sometime
week to the women who sign up.
