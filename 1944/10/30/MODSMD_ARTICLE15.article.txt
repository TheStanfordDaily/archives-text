# Thursday Blood Donors Should Sing Up Today
## 
### 
All students planning to donate
blood this Thursday, Nov. 2, must
make their appointments today at
the War Board office, according to
Coot Coffin, chairman of Blood Bank.
Students under twenty-one must
have release slips signed by both
parents before appointments can be
made. Station wagons, provided by.
the Red Cross, will leave the Engi
neering Corner twenty minutes be
fore each appointment.
