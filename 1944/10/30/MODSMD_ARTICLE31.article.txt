# Education Department Plans Halloween Party
## 
### 
The Education Department's Hal
loween party is going to be held at
the barbecue pit on Tuesday evening
at 5:30 p.m. All of those who wish
to go over in a group are invited to
meet at the Education Building
promptly at 5:30.
George Murphy, chairman of the
Social Committee, reminds everyone
planning to come that the cost of the
picnic and the entertainment that
goes with it will be 30 cents.
