There is food that's good and plenty
At the Union's cellar store
And once you've seen and eaten
What they have, you'll ask for more.
It's a place to make appointment
For a shake, or coke, or snack
And once you've seen and eaten
You'll be sure to hurry back.
The next time you are hungry
Or have nothing else to do
Stop in and see the cellar.
You'll fi nd what I've said is true.
