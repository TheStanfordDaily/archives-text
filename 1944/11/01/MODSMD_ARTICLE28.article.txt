# Cider And Bridge At Juniper Lodge
## 
### 
A roaring fire, flowing cider, and
bridge games will highlight the in
formal house warming at Juniper
Lodge tomorrow evening, according
to Marie Wagner, party chairman.
Juniper girls will act as hostesses
at the house warming where new
junior transfers will become ac
quainted with the faculty and stu
dent guests of the girls.
Chairmen of the committees: host
ess, Jean Gait and Marilyn Clow;
preparations, Helen Van Keppel,
Mary Thompson, and Norma Tre
varrow; refreshments, Wanda Koz
lowski. Barbara Ward, and Beryl
Warrick; and requisitions, Betty Pez
zoni.
