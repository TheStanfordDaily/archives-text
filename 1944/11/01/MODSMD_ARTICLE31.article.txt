# Geology, Mining Group Meets Tonight at 7:30
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Geological
and Mining Society this quarter will
be held this evening at 7:30 in the
Nitery.
Mr. George L. Harrington will
speak on "Experiences of an Oil
Geologist in Northern Argentina."
A short business meeting will be
held, and refreshments will be
served.
