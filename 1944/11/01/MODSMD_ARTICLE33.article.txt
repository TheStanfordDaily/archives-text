# Red Cross Trains Five Stanfordites
## 
### 
Palo Alto Red Cross is offering a
training-ground to five Stanford stu
dents through its Home Service De
partment. The volunteers are doing
practice case-work in Economics
given to upper division students by
Mrs. L. H. Buehler.
Students are sent to various relief
and service agencies in the neigh
borhood to be trained in actual work
ing practices.
The young Home Service workers,
who help the professional and volun
teer Red Cross women in every facet
of aid to servicemen and their fami
lies are Olga Wier, Jean Trowbridge,
Eloise Durkheimer, Jean Wallen, and
Patricia Meyer.
