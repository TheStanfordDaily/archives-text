# Vaccination Slips Must Be In Monday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due at
the Information Window, Registrar's
Office, on Monday, November 6, for
all new students, including those
having permits to attend classes, and
all other students whose certificates
have expix-ed.
Failure to "file certificates by 5 p.m.
on Monday will result in a fine of $2;
or, if not filed by November 13, reg
istration in the University will be
subject to cancellation by the Com
mittee on Public Health.
