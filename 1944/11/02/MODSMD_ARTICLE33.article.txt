# Mexican Medicine Is Series Subject
## 
### 
Nathan van Patten, director of the
University Library, will speak on
"Early History of Medicine in Mex
ico," at the third meeting of the His
panic World series to be held tonight
at 7:30 in Room 242 (Romanic Lan
guages).
The speaker will bring a selection
of books on the history of medicine
selected from the Lane Library in
San Francisco to illustrate his talk,
according to Ronald Hilton, Associate
Professor of Romanic Languages.
