# Golf Club To Hold Playoff Tourney
## 
### 
If the weather permits, members
of the Stanford Golf Club will play
in a "Hidden Hole" tourney today
with the playoffs from 1 to 2:30.
Pre-war balls will be awarded the
girls who score the lowest on two
holes whose identity will be con
cealed from all but the tournament
manager, Vee Hickman.
About 30 girls are expected to play
in the tourney, according to Mar
garet Meadowcroft, president of the
club, which now numbers sixty girls.
Refreshments will be served to
the golfers after they finish playing
nine holes.
