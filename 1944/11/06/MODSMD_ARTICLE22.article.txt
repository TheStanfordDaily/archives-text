# 'Pom-Pom' Tickets On Sale This Week
## 
### 
Tickets for the "Pom-Pom Prom,"
the Union-Lagunita formal will go
on sale this week, announced Carol
Kellogg, publicity chairman, yester
day.The Prom will be presented on
Big Game Weekend, Nov. 18, and
will feature collegiate banners, soru's,
and spirit.
Bids will be $2 a couple.
