## 
### 
Bids for the Branner-Roble-Ven
tura formal, "La Victoire Parisienne,"
to be presented this Saturday in the
Women's Gym, are now on sale in
the various halls, Pat Charlton, Gen
eral Chairman, announced yesterday.
All girls who would like to pur
chase tickets should contact their
hall chairmen as soon as possible.
Girls are permitted to buy bids tol

include dancing to the music of
Howard Frederic and his Band of
Gold, guaranteed Parisienne decora
tions, refreshments, and even enter
tainment furnished by the cast of
"Gaieties."
