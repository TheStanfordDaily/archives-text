# Adelpha Quartet Entertains Storey
## 
### 
Casa Adelfa entertained Storey
House at after-dinner coffee last
night. The newly organized Adelfa
Quartet made its initial performance
at the affair. The singers are: Gene
Moldenhauer, Ellie Brill Win Lamm,
and Erlda Leaf.
Mildred Edwards was chairman
of arrangements for the evening's
party.
