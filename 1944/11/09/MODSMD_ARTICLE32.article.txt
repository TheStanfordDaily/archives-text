# Internationalists To Give Informal'
## 
### 
An International Club dance will
be given tomorrow night at the Nit
ery from 8 to 11 p.m., chairman
Claire Tolnay announced today. All
on campus are invited to come, with
or without dates.
The dance will be informal and
foreign records as well as smooth fox
trots will be the order of the evening.
The foreign records will be mostly
tangos and rhumbas. Bob Myhre
will be in charge of all the music.
Miss Pat Davidson and Dr. and
Mrs. Max Savclle will be the dance's
patrons and patronesses, and Esther
Belew will welcome them.
Refreshment chairman Sue Web
ster announced that cider and dough
nuts will be served.
