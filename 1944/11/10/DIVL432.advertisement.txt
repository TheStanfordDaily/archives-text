\
Rome — Naples — Salerno
Vatican City — Vatican State
;
Come to us at our Overseas De
partment, Normandy Lane. We
will help you select your package
and ship it for you! Come early!
We also can take care of
civilian parcel mailing to
Hawaii, England, Scotland.
North Ireland, Irish Free
State, Canada, Australia.
New Zealand, Algeria,
French Morocco and Tu
nisia. Remember, of course,
we ship to the U.S. Armed
Forces and to British Forces
in all theaters of war.
Overseas Department, Normandy Lane
