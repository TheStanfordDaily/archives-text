# Formal Pom-Pom Prom
## 
### 
Bids for the Lagunita-Union all-campus POM-POM PROM will go on
sale today from 1 to 3 at the ASSU oflice, Carol Kellogg, publicity chair
man, announced today.
Everyone on campus, including all girls and fellows who do not live in
Lagunita and Union, are urged to buy their tickets to the PROM. Formals,
dark suits, or tux will be in order, and will be donned by the Indians as a
proper climax to the weekend which includes rally, football game and
formal. *
