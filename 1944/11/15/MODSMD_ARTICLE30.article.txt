# Sunday Open Houses
## Redwoods To Have First Open House 
### 
Redwoods girls have extended in
vitations to President and Mrs. Don
ald B. Tresidder; Dean Mary Yost;
Miss Anastasia Doyle, assistant Dean
of Women; Miss Mary Jane Donald
son. director of Union Unit; and Miss
Betty Lou Norton, president of
Union unit, for their open house
Sunday from 3 to 5 p.m.
In addition to these guests, each
girl has invited two of her friends
to the affair. Since this is the first
time girls have lived in Redwoods,
which was formerly the Sigma Chi
house, they are anxious to show it
off to their friends.
Marie Jo McGervey is in charge
of the open house. Assisting her will
be Elsie Evans in charge of hostesses;
Hilda Chapman, clean-up; Pat Loly,
refreshments, and Jean Wallenberg,
entertainment.
