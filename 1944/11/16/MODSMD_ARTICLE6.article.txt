# FAENZA THREATENED
## 
### 
ROME, Nov. 15—British troops of
the Eighth Army hammered their
way to within five and one-half miles
of Faenza today against stiffening
German opposition, while Polish
units of the Fifth Army to the west
captured the village of San Antonio
after fierce street fighting.
