# Chairmen Appointed to ASC War Loan Drive
## 
### 
Chairmen in charge of the Sixth
War Loan Drive were appointed by
the Army Student Council at a re
cent meeting, according to Thomas
Fletcher, president. Charles Allyn
has been placed in charge of Co. A's
effort, while Herb Tearce is chair
man of Co. C.
Other business taken up at the
ASC included more plans for the
"Snowball," and a report on several
recent exchange dinners by co-Social
Chairman Ted Farrell.
