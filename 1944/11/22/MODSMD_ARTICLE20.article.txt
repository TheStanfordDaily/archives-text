# Music Listening Rooms Open During Vacation
## 
### 
The Music Listenirig Rooms 14 and
J5 in the Old Union will be open
between the following hours during
the Thanksgiving vacation:
Thursday—4:3o p.m. to 6 p.m.
Friday—3 p.m. to G p.m.
Saturday—l p.m. to 3 p.m.
Sunday—l p.m. to 3 p.m.
The rooms are now open regularly
from 3 p.m. to 6 p.m. each weekday
cxcept Wednesday, for which the
hours are from 3 p.m. to 5 p.m., and
Saturday and Sunday when it is open
1 p.m. to 3 p.m.
