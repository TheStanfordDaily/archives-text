# Daily Edit Staff Schedules Banquet
## 
### 
Daily Editorial Board, at a staff
meeting Sunday appointed Marion
Morrow as chairman of the Daily
banquet to be held Friday, December
8. Joey-Nell Ryan was named chair
man of transportation, Jim Weinberg
chairman of finances and Joe Sher
chairman of entertainment.
Staff members were reminded that
they must put their requests for pro
motion, including position desired,
number of quarters on the Daily,
present position, and quarters before
graduation, in the Daily promotion
box some time during the next week.
There will be no staff meeting this
Sunday but all staff members are
reminded that double points are
awarded for desk work.
