# Folk Dancers Prepare Tonight for Bond Rally
## 
### 
The Stanford Folk Dancers will
meet today from 7 to 9 p.m. in the
Women's Gym. Dorothy Frost, presi
dent of the club, announced today
that the group will practice the
dances that will be exhibited at the
Sixth War Loan rally in Oakland,
December 2.
The Stanford dancers are also
planning to attend the next festival
of the Folk Dance Federation of
California to be held December 10
in Berkeley.
All students are invited to attend
the meetings of the group every
Wednesday evening. No experience
in folk or square dancing is required.
