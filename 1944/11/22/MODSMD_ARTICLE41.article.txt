# Sixth Loan Drive Begins on Monday
## 
### 
Machinery of the War Stamp and
Bond Committee for the Sixth War
Loan Drive will go into action when
Stanford students return from
Thanksgiving vacation, according to
Mildred Edwards, committee chair
man.
Synchronized with the nation-wide
war loan drive, the Stanford com
mittee will begin sales November 27
and continue through December G. A
goal of $27,500 has been set, which
will be reached if at least half of the
students buy a $25 war bond, Miss
Edwards continued.
Freshmen women will sell bonds
from booths on campus and a ther
mometer indicator near the Cellar
will record progress of the bond
sales.
