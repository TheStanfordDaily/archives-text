# Freshmen Entertained At 'Y' Tea Yesterday
## 
### 
Freshmen women were guests of
the YVVCA yesterday afternoon at a
tea in the Women's Clubhouse. Miss
Patricia Davidson, YWCA secretary,
received the women who were later
given an opportunity to meet the
speakers, Miss Rosamond Clarke and
Mrs. Payson Treat.
Miss Clarke, director of Roble, told
the girls of good places to eat and
interesting sites to visit in San Fran
cisco. Mrs. Treat, wife of the profes
sor of history, spoke on some of
Stanford's traditions.
