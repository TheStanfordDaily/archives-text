# Union Excom Meets Today
## 
### 
Members of Union Unit Excom will
meet in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse at 4:45 p.m. to
day, according to Betty Lou Horton,
president of the Unit.
At this meeting, plans for the Ma
drono open house to be held the
afternoon of December 3, and the
Union Christmas party December 17.
Decisions made in today's Excom
session will be passed on to the girls
in the Unit during house meetings
this evening, over which the officers
who were present will preside.
