# Farm Text Used By Oregon Schools
## 
### 
"Looking at Life Through Ameri
can Literature," by Nellie Mae Lom
bard, published this fall by the Stan
ford University Press, has just been
adopted by the state of Oregon as a
required text-book for all high
schools except those in Portland, ac
cording to word received by Stanley
M. Croonquist, sales manager of the
Press.
The 100-page volume incorporates
Miss Lombard's original plan for
teaching American literature and
contains the titles of 1,800 poems, es
says, short stories, plays and books
in annotated reading lists adapted to
high school students' abilities and
interests.
