# STETTINIUS TO SUCCEED HULL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 27 (U.R)—President Roosevelt today accepted with
deep regret the resignation of ailing Secretary of State Cordll Hull, and
four hours later nominated Hull's understudy—44-year-old Under-secre
tary Edward R. Stettinius, Jr.—to be his successor in the Number One
J cabinet post.
