# Calendar Sales Start This Week
## 
### 
Cap and Gown calendars for the
year 1945 featuring photographic
scenes of Stanford life, will go on
sale this week, according to Cally
Curtis, Cap and Gown member in
charge of calendar sales.
Calendars this year will have in
fra-red photographs printed in dua
tone. They will cost $1 each, or if
board and envelope for mailing are
included, $1.15. Buyers may pay $1.25
and Cap and Gown will mail the
calendar.
Proceeds from the sale of these cal
endars will go to a Scholarship Fund,
from which one scholarship is given
every quarter, including summer
quarter, to a Stanford woman.
