# Internationalists Cancel Barbecue
## 
### 
International Club has cancelled
Sunday barbecue because most of the
members are planning to attend the
San Jose concert of Casadesus, Ser
vet Duran announced today.
There will be a regular meeting
Sunday, December 7. at Mrs. Thomas
home on Lincoln Avenue.
This meeting will be for the new
members to become better acquaint
ed with the older members, and plans
for a barbecue to open Winter Quar
ter will also be discussed. Refresh
ments will be served.
