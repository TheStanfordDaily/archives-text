# 'Y' Group To Hear Adjustment Talk
## 
### 
Mrs. Rudy Kalenborn will speak
to the YWCA Home and Marriage
group today at 4 p.m. in the "Y"
rooms, on "Adjustments in Marri
age." Introducing her will be Mrs.
Harry Rathbun, adviser and co
ordinator of the group's discussions.
Mrs. Kalenborn, a resident of Palo
Alto and graduate of San Jose State,
will speak of the adjustments which
she and her wide variety of acquain
tances have found to be necessary
during the initial years of marriage.
