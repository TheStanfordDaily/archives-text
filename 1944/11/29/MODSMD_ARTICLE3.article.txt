# MED. SCHOOL LECTURE
## 
### 
Brig. John Rawlings Rees, M.D.,
F.R.C.P., consulting psychiatrist of
the British Army, will lecture on
'The Shaping of Psychiatry by War"
at Lane Hall. Stanford Medical
School, San Francisco, next Friday
at 8 p.m. The lecture will be open to
members of the medical and nursing
professions.
