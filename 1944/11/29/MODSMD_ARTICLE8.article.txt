# Stanford Debaters To Attend Tourney With Southern Cal
## 
### 
Stanford debaters in the Varsity
Debating Tournament at Pasadena
College will leave in time for the
first rounds beginning at 8:30 a.m.
Friday, ending 4 p.m. Saturday.
Topic of the debate is "Compulsory
Arbitration of Labor Disputes."
Those taking part include Carol
Newton, debate manager; Mary
Frances Westcott, James Weinberg
and Marshall Small. There will be
five rounds before the two final elim
ination rounds.
Those entering the auditory con
test are Miss Westcott, speaking on
"France"; Mr. Small, extemporane
ous problems: Miss Westcott, sight
reading, and Miss Newton, im
promptu.
