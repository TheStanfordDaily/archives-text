# Stanford Hospital Nurses' Tea Given
## 
### 
All girls interested in nursing or
becoming nurses are invited to at
tend a tea for 29 nurses of Ihe Stan
ford Lane Hospital in San Francisco
Saturday from 3 to 5 p.m., in the
Eucalypto Social Room.
Bernadine Barber is in charge of
the affair. Girls will be able to talk
to the nurses and learn about the
main advantages and disadvantages
of the nursing profession.
