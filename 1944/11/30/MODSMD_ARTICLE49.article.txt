# Sunday Movies Feature "Lincoln"
## 
### 
Shaughnessy Shows goes cultural
this weekend with its presentation
of "Abe Lincoln in Illinois," starring
Raymond Massey, this Sunday eve
ning at 7:30. The movie was adapted
from the Pulitzer Prize play of the
same name by Robert Sherwood.
Notwithstanding, Janice Shaugh
nessy, chairman of the shows, be
lieves that variety is the spice of life,
and doubling with "Lincoln" will be
another great character of American
history. Kit Carson, hero of Chapter
II of that thrilling, chilling story of
the wild west.
Don't miss this opportunity to
learn a little American Civ at the
Memorial Auditorium this Sunday
evening for only 20 cents.
