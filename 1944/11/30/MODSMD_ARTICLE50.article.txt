# Dancers To Join Oakland Festival
## 
### 
Stanford Folk Dancers, under the
direction of Clarice Dechent, will be
among the 400 gay-costumed dancers
who will participate in the Sixth
War Loan Rally in the Oakland Civic
Auditorium on Saturday from 8 p.m.
to 10 p.m.
All campus folk dancers must be
in costume or in service uniform,
Dorothy Frost, president of the
group, announced yester day. All
members of the Stanford club who
have not made arrangements for
transportation to Oakland should call
Miss Frost, 22151.
