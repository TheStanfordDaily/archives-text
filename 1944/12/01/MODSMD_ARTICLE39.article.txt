# ASCE Holds Field Trip Tomorrow
## 
### 
Opening a full schedule of events
for the coming year the Stanford
chapter of the ASCE is holding its
first field trip tomorrow morning
from 8:30 a.m. to 4 p.m., according
to Wayne Sturgis, president of the
local chapter.
The trip will originate at the En
gineering Corner and members will
bring their 1 inches. Old clothes are
recommended.
The society is visiting the Penin
sula water supply for the city of San
Francisco and will be led by Mr.
George Davis, superintendent of
Peninsula properties. The trip is
being made by special permission
since the projects to be reviewed
are not open to the public.
