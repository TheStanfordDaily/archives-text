# Teapot Dome Secretary Dies In E1 Paso, Texas
## 
### 
EL PASO. Texas, Nov. 30 (U.R)-
Albert Hall, secretary of the interior
in the cabinet of President Warren
G. Harding, who insisted that history
would clear his name of the Teapot
Dame charges which clouded his
political career, died tonight in an
El Paso hospital.
