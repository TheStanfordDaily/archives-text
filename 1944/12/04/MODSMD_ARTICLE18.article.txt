# Six Roble, Branner Sponsors Chosen
## 
### 
The Sponsor Choosing Committee
has announced the selection of six
new sponsors for Winter and Spring
quarters at Roble and Branner, ac
cording to Sally Breuner, chairman.
New Roble sponsors will be Mar
jorie Ambler, Margaret Miller, Jean
Champion and Jackie Jardin. Wini
fred Lamb and Cathie Houser will be
sponsors at Branner.
