# Jazz Society To Meet at 8
## 
### 
The Stanford Hot Jazz Society will
hold its second meeting of the quar
ter tonight in the Music Listening
Room at 8 p.m.
Marvin Bolei of San Jose will be
the guest speaker. His program for
the evening will feature records that
he played last Saturday over the
KSFO Saturday Swing and Hot Ses
sion. All of the records played will
be in the New Orleans style and
many of them are very rare.
