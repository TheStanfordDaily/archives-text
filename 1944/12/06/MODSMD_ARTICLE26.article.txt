# Cap and Gown's Calendars on Sale
## 
### 
All copies of Cup and Gown Cal
endars in the first shipment were sold
before noon yesterday, announced
Cally Curtis, sales chairman. An
other shipment is expected to come
this afternoon.
Calendars will be sold at the Cel
lar, in the ASSU office and in all
women's living groups by Cap and
Gown members. They cost $1 each;
51.15 if envelope and board for mail
ing are included, or $1.25 if Cap and
Gown mails them.
Calendars this year feature photo
graphs of Stanford taken in infra
red and printed in duo-tone.
