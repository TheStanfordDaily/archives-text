# War Bond Sales Hit $19,275 Mark
## 
### 
War bond .sales yesterday totaled

ford Sixth War Loan drive to $19,-
275.
Hasher auctions boosted sales, with
Lagunitu bringing in a total of $8,000
and Roble holding a lead with a total
of $10,000. Competition remains keen
between the different hasher teams.
Girls selling bonds in the booth
at the Cellar corner are doing a rush
business. Bonds pledged at the auc
tions may be purchased at the
booths, but buyers should state
which hasher team they are support
ing.
Pledges must be fulfilled by Thurs
day, Dec. 7., the day the drive ends.
