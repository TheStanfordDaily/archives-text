# Speakers' Bureau Helps War Board
## 
### 
Acting for War Board in its effort
to increase student participation in
war activities, J. D. Williams and
Donna Pickus of the Stanford Speak
ers' Bureau spoke this week at The
Chalet, Cubberley House, Parnie
Storey House, and Madrono.
Williams, head of the Speakers'
Bureau and Miss Pickus outlined
Red Cross services, the functions of
War Board on campus, and the im
portance of the current Sixth War
Loan drive in this week's talks, the
first of a series to be given by the
Speakers' Bureau to halls having a
low percentage of war work hours.
