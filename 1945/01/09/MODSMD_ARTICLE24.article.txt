# Folk Dance Group Plans Open House
## 
### 
Stanford's folk dancers will hold
an open house tomorrow night from
7:30 to 10 p.m. in the Women's Gym,
Club President Dorothy Frost has
announced. The dancers will demon
strate advanced dances, as well as
teach old favorites to newcomers.
Members of the Hoe Downers, a
folk dance club of Palo Alto, will be
on hand to demonstrate their famous
square-dancing technique.
All old members of the Stanford
Club are asked to wear their gayest
costumes to the open house, accord
ing to Miss Frost. Transfers, fresh
men and members of International
Club are especially invited to come
to the affair.
