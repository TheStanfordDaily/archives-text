# Bunn Calls 4F Meeting
## 
### 
As a result of recent manpower
legislation concerning 4F's, Dean of
Men. John Bunn has caller 1 a special
meeting of Stanford men in the lim
ited draft classifications for tomorrow
night at 7:30 p.m. in the Toyon
lounge.
Stanford 4F's will be able to secure
full particulars as released by the
Regional Coordinator of Selective
Service, Lieutenant Commander F.
Edward Walker. Men in doubt as to
whether or not they should continue
their college programs are advised
to attend.
