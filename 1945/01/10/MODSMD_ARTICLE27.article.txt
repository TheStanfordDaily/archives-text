# Redwoods to Entertain Manzanita, the Oaks
## 
### 
Redwoods will hold its first party
of the quarter Thursday at 7:30 p.m.
for Manzanita and the Oaks . It will
be an inter-house spread, featuring
singing, games, and crazy prizes.
Roberta Gianella is in charge of the
affair. The committee chairmen are
Hilda Chapman, refreshments; Diana
Meek, arrangements; Ann Zukerman
and Pauline Robinson; entertainment
and Edith Von Bibra, decorations.
