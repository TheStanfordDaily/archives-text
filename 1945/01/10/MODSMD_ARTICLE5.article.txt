# BLOOD BANK SIGN-UP
## 
### 
Thursday, Jan. 18. will be the first
Blood Bank day of the quarter, and
a sign-up sheet will be on the Wat-
Board door in the old Union Build
ing, Coot Coffin, Blood Bank chair
man, said yesterday. Appointments
may be made at 11:20, 11:40, 1:20
and 1:40. The Palo Alto Red Cross
will furnish transportation between
the campus and the receiving center.
