# U.S. Sixth Plunges 10 Miles Into Luzon
## 
### 
ALLIED HDQ., LEYTE, Philippine
islands, Thursday, Jan. 11 (U.R)—U. S.
Sixth Army troops drove ten miles
into Luzon yesterday, siezing four
towns and an airfield while war
planes knocked out three railways
and highway bridges in the path of
Jap reinforcements rushing to the
Lingayen Gulf front from the Manila
area.
