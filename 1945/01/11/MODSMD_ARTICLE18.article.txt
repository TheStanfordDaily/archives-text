# First Biology Talk Tomorrow at 4:15
## 
### 
A special seminar, sponsored by
the School of Biological Sciences,
will present Dr. Philip Levine, at
4:15 p.m. tomorrow in Room 430.
He will speak on "Genetic and
Constitutional Causes of Fetal and
Neonatal Morbidity." Dr. Levine is
; the director of the biological division
; of the Ortho Research Foundation of
Linden, N. J., and has recently been
lecturing in San Francisco.
Main field of his research has been
| related to blood grouping tests in
cases of disputed paternity. He has
written laws for Wisconsin and New
