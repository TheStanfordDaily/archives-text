# Enrollment Totals 2,524
## 
### 
Enrollment for winter quarter at
Stanford yesterday jumped to 261
more civilian students registered
than at the same time last year, with
the third day of registration's total
set at 2,524 civilians.
870 men and 1,054 women have en
rolled to date.
Of Stanford students registered for
medical school in San Francisco, 115
men and six girls are studying in the
city, while first-year students are in
cluded in the campus total. 158
nurses are also in training at the
school, officially lisled as Stanford
students.
