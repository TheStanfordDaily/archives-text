# Symphony Opens Season Tomorrow
## 
### 
The San Francisco Symphony Or
jhestra will hold its first concert of
1945 tomorrow afternoon at the War
Memorial Opera House in San Fran
cisco. The program will be repeated
Saturday night.
Efrein Kurtz, leader of the Kansas
City Symphony Orchestra, will be
guest conductor. Jan Smeterlin, Pol
ish pianist, will be soloist.
The program consists of "Suite for
Strings," by Corelli"; Metamorpho
sis on a Theme by Carl Maria von
Weber," by Paul Hindemith; "Con
certo Number 2," by Serge Rach
maninoff, and "Symphony Number 6,
the Pathetique." by Tschaikovsky.
