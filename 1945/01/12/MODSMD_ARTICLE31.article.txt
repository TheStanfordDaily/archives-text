# Hot Jazz Society To Meet Monday
## 
### 
Stanford's Hot Jazz Society will
hold its first meeting of the quarter
next Monday night at 8 p.m. in the
Music Listening Room, according to
President Joann Shipp.

How to listen to jazz will be the
purpose of this first meeting, and
both students and members of the
faculty are cordially invited to at
tend.
