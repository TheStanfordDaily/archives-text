# Gym Will Be Available Far Team Practices
## 
### 
Miss Dorothy Mac Donald, chair
man of the Women's Intramural
Basketball Tournament, announced
today that the Women's Gym will be
open this Saturday from 8 to 12 and
Sunday afternoon from 2 to 4 for
all teams wishing to practice for the
tournament, which starts next week.
She added that the teams must sign
on the blackboard in the main lobby
of the gym in order to reserve a
court. Two teams can sign for the
same hour.
