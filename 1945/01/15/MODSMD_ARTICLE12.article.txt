# Last Day to Change Studies Is Jan. 22
## 
### 
Last day students may change their
study lists for this quarter without
fine will be Monday, January 22.
After that date all program re
visions must be made by petition to
the Registration Committee, subject
to the following fine: two dollars
for each course added or dropped, or
$3 when one course is substituted
for another.
All program changes made since
registration should be filed at the
Registrar's Office on the regular re
vised study list cards.
Students are held responsible for
courses and units as they appear on
their official study lists, and no credit
is given for courses which do not
appear on these lists. This applies
to physical education as well as to
academic subjects.
