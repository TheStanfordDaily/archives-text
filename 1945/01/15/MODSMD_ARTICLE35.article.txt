# Violinist To Play At Jan. 22 Concert
## 
### 
Zino Francescatti, noted French
violinist, will open the 1945 concert
events of the San Francisco Opera
Association with a program in the
War Memorial Opera House next
Monday evening, January 22.
Tickets for this concert are now
on sale at the Opera box office, Nor
mandy Lane, City of Paris, EXbrook
8585.
Mr. Francescatti made his first
public appearance at the age of 5.
and at 10 he gave the Beethoven
Concerto- It was in the fall of 1939
that he came to the United States
and at that time established himself
with his New York debut.
