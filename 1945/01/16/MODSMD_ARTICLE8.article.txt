# Election Board Reviews Rules
## 
### 
Members of the newly appointed
election board will meet at 7:130 this
evening in the Nitery to review the
election rules before they are pre
sented to Excom on Wednesday
night, according to Barbara Stofer,
chairman of the board.
Offices to be filled by the all-cam
pus election to be held on Jan. 24
are those of: president and secretary
of the Freshman Class, two positions
on Men's Council, two positions on
Women's Council, and president of
Women's Conference.
Members of the Board serving with
Miss Stofer are Mary Ann Duemling,
Joyce Verrill, Virginia Butler, Dick
Silberman, Don Tanner, and Bob
Howell.
