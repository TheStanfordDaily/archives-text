# 15 Bandage Rollers Needed Per Hour
## 
### 
Fifteen bandage rollers an hour
are needed in the Red Cross Sur
gical Dressings room to meet the
Stanford quota, according to Marie
Wagner, committee chairman.
Tlic room, on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse, is open
Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thurs
day at 10, 11, 1, 2. and Women
are asked to sign up at the begin
ning of the hour.
