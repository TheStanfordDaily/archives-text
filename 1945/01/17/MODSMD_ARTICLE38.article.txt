# Yost Talks To Roble On War Scholarship
## 
### 
At the Roble House meeting Mon
day evening, Dean Mary Yost spoke
on scholarship and the wartime re
sponsibilities of Stanford women.
New Roble sponsors were also intro
duced. They are Patty Patten, Mar
garet Miller, Jacqueline Jardine,
Marjorie Ambler, and Jeanne Cham
pion.
At the corridor meetings which
followed the house meeting, it was
announced that the Roble birthday
dinner will be held January 31. Mar
gery Swett has been appointed gen
eral chairman for the affair. Plans
were also discussed for the formation
of a Roble glee club, and the forth
coming ping pong tournament was
explained.
