# University Organist To Give Organ Recital
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wheelwright, assistant
University Organist, will present an
organ recital tomorrow at 4:15 p.m.
Selections to he presented are: Prel
ude and Fugue in E Minor (The
Cathedral) by Bach; Dreams by
Guilmant; Sonata in E Minor by
Rogers, Minuet by Boccherini; Rev
erie-Improvisation on an Old French
Church Tune by Bedell; and Finale I
Organ Symphony by Maquaire.
