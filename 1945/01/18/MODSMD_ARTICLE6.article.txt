# Senior Committee To Hear Election Interviews, Friday
## 
### 
Senior Nominating Committee will
meet in the Excom Room Friday
from 1 to 3 p.m. to hear interviews
for the coming election to be held
January 24.
Offices to be filled at the election
are: President of Women's Confer
ence, two positions on Women's
Council, two positions on Men's
Council, and president and secretary
of the Freshman Class.
A person may be nominated
through an interview with the com
mittee or by circulating his own
petition.
The committee consists of Helen
Jones. Virginia Wardlaw, B. J. Cal
fee, Marian Johnson. Luana Forkner,
Sally Breuner, and Mary Lou Mc-
Clure.
