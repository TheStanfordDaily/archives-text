# Turn In Study Lists Monday
## 
### 
Students must submit all changes
in study lists for this quarter by
Monday, January 22, or be fined.
After that date program revisions
must be made by petition to the
Registration Committee, subject to
the following fines:
Two dollars for each course added
or dropped; three dollars when one
course is substituted for another.
All program changes made since
registration should be filed at the
Registrar's Office on the regular re
vised study list cards.
Students are held responsible for
courses and units as they appear on
their official study lists, and no credit
is given for courses which do not
appear on these lists. This applies
to physical education as well as to
academic subjects.
