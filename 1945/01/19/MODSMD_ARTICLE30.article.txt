# Tanner Chooses Democracy Topic
## 
### 
"Taproots of Democracy" will be
the topic for Sunday's sermon in the
Stanford Memorial Chapel by Act
ing Chaplain Obert C. Tanner.
Services begin at 11 a.m. with a
prelude, "Agnus Dei" by Bizet. Play
ing the prelude will be Elizabeth
Peirce Kincade, violinist; Charles
Frisbie, 'cellist; and D. Sterling
Wheelwright, organist. A solo,
Franck's "Panis Angelicus," will be
given by Robert Gettys, tenor, ac
companied by the trio. The Univer
sity Choir.will sing Tkach's anthem,
"To Thee We Sing."
