# Students To Debate Peacetime Draft
## 
### 
Six speakers will debate the pros
and cons of peacetime conscription
tonight at the meeting of Debating
Society at 7:45 in Room 200 T.
Those upholding the affirmative
are Marilyn Dey. Edmund Maher,
and Donna Pickus, while Richard
Jennings, Gordon Levy, and' Mar
shall Small will oppose peacetime
conscription.
The question, "Resolved, that a
system of compulsory military train
ing of one year should be instituted
as a permanent peacetime policy for
men between the ages of 17 and 21."
will he the subject of a debate with
Cal on February 14 and will also be
debated before the San Leandro
town council some time in March.
