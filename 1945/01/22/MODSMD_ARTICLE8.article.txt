# Wells To Lecture On Battlefronts
## 
### 
Carveth Wells, who has traveled
extensively in Africa and Asia, will
speak on "East and West of Suez" at
the Tuesday Series tomorrow eve
ning at 7:UO in Memorial Audito
rium.
Mr. Wells' background includes
surveying in the Malayan jungles,
leading African safaris, railroad
building in Canada, and heading ex
peditions to the Caucasus mountains,
Lapland, and Japan. In 1942 he
served as an orientation lecturer for
Army men preparing for the African
invasion.
People and customs of the coun
tries with which he is familiar and
which are now serving as battle
fronts will be discussed mainly by
Mr. Wells.
