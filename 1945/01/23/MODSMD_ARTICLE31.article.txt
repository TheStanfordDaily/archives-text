# WEEKEND DANCES
## Toy on Settles For Glassman 
### BOB PECK 
With Excom turning" thumbs down
on Busse, Toyon has done the next
best thing for their all-campus for
mal February 3 and signed an or
chestra just one syllable away from
Benny Goodman—Benny Glassman.
Glassman's popular Peninsula or
chestra is not new to the Farm,
Benny having played several en
gagements here before. His style?
Something smooth and something
hftt.
Tables Turned
Freshman women, famed for ask
ing men to their traditional tfoble
formal, will have the tables turned
this time and they will have to wait
for the telephone to ring.
Tickets to the all-campus dance
will be placed on sale soon, and sales
are limited to ensure the dance floor
from becoming too crowded.
Oops!
Sweetheart decorations by Jack
Gilliland and his committee will pro
vide backdrops to Goodman's (Oops!
Glassman's) orchestra.
Other features of the dance will be
an as yet undisclosed Sweetheart
ring, intermission entertainment,
free refreshments, and a good or
chestra. But. yeah, what a diff'rence
a syllable makes!
