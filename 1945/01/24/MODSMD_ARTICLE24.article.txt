# Dancers Practice For Ranch Rumpus
## 
### 
The Stanford Folk Dancers will
meet in the Women's Gym from 7:30
to 10 tonight, according to Dorothy
Frost, president of the club. The
group will practice the Swedish ox
dance as they plan to exhibit this
dance and the Swedish weaving
dances at the coming Ranch Rum
pus.
A meeting of the committee plan
ning the Rumpus will meet after the
regular dancing session. All persons
interested in folk and square danc
ing are invited to attend the weekly
sessions of the group.
