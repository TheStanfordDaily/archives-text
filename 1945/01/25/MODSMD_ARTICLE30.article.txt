# Try outs for Play Continue Tonight

### 
Last tryouts for "The Little Foxes"
by Lillian Hellman, a drama to be
presented by the Stanford Players
during the first week in Mareh. will
be held at 7:30 p.m. today in the i
Little Theater.
Tryouts opened last night. There ;
are parts for four women and six :
men. They may be filled by either
students or faculty. Rehearsals will
begin immediately.
Tallulah Bankhead starred in the
play in San Francisco, and Bette
Davis played the lead in the movie.
