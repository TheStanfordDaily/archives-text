# Tyler Hall To Hold Fireside Friday
## 
### 
Tyler Hall is sponsoring a closed
fireside this Friday night, announced
fireside chairman Jack Siemer. Good
music is promised by Dick Reinhardt
and Jim Sheehy, in charge of rec
ords. Refreshments of cider and
cookies will be served. Others on the
fireside committee making prepara
tions for the theme "Return to the
Moose" are Don Nevius and George
West fall.
Tyler firesides are tentatively
planned for every Friday night from
8:30 to 11:30, Siemer added. Also on
Tyler's social calendar is an all
campus dance scheduled for March
<3.
