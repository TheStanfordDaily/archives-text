# Wheelwright To Give Organ Recital Sunday
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wheelwright, assistant
University organist, will present an
organ recital at 4 p.m. this Sunday
in the Stanford Memorial Church.
Wheelwright will begin his re
cital with "Impromptu for Organ
II" by Coleridge-Taylor, the Eng
lish negro composer who wrote a
series of impromptus published a
generation ago. This will be fol
lowed by Cesar Franck's "Chorale
in A Minor."
The finale will be "Symphony I,"
by Vierne.
