# Archery Tourney Enrollment Open
## 
### 
Competing with university clubs
throughout the country, the Olym
pic Archery Tournament, in which
all men and women on campus may
compete, will begin the week of Feb
ruary 4, announced Betty Shipley.
Scores will be hailed in to the
Jadquarters beginning February 4.
Practice for the Stanford archers
will be in the basement of the Edu
cation Building one night a week.
Students interested should call
Miss Shipley, 8703, from 3 p.m. to 6
p.m., today or tomorrow.
