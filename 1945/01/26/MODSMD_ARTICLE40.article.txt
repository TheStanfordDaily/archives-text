# Reception at Tresidder Home Set for Sunday
## 
### 
A reception-open house for all
students enrolled since October,
1944. will be given by Dr. and Mrs.
Donald B. Tresidder at the presi
dent's home Sunday from 7:30 to
p.m.
Another reception for older stu
dents is planned for the following
Sunday at the same time.
Games and refreshments will be
offered.
