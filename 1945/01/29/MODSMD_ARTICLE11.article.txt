# Spiegelberg to Discuss Bible at Chapel Hoard
## 
### 
The Bible, its modern worth and
the value that can be placed on ti,
will be the topic of the talk Dr.
Frederic Spiegelberg, professor of
humanities, will give to Chapel
Board tomorrow night from 9 to 10
o'clock.
In addition to the board members
who are selected from each living
group on campus, any student who
is interested may attend the meet
ing. After Dr. Spiegelberg's talk he
will lead the group in a discussion.
