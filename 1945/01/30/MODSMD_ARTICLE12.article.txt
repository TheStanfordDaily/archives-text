# Britt Trio to Give Concert Friday
## 
### 
Beethoven, Brahms, and Dohn
anyi's music will be featured next
Friday when the Britt Trio, with
violin, and piano performs at
Memorial Hall as the second event
of this season's concert series.
Artists in the Trio are Horace
Britt, trained in Paris, Viola Was
terlain, a prominent woman violinist,
and Conrad Held who played for
many years in the South Mountain
Quartet sponsored by Mrs. Elizabeth
Sprague Coolidge.
Tickets for the performance will
be available at the Concert Series
office, Old Union Building every day
from 1 to 5 p. m. They are $1.20,
$1.80. 52.40, and S3.
