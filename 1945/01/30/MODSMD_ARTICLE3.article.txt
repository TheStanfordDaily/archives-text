# Koenig Will Discuss Our Scientific History
## 
### 
American scientific activity be
tween the years 1790 and 1830, chief
ly in the field of natural history, will
be discussed by Frederick O. Koenig,
professor of chemistry, at the second
meeting of the American Studies
Symposium Thursday afternoon at
4:15 in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
