# AGAINST CONSCRIPTION
## 
### 
According to Major Elliott on the Town Meeting of
the Air last week, a year's military training will bo
beneficial in case of actual war only if the training is
only a few years old.
Suppose we don't do any better about preventing
the next war than we did about this one, and thus the
next war comes in '5 years.
If Major Elliott's word "few" can mean at the most
five years, then with a million 18-year-olds per year
we will be losing (25-5) x 1.000.000 20,000.000 man
years. our best man years down the drain.
Nan ross
8 Vine Street,
Menlo Park
