# KEFAUVER TO SPEAK
## 
### 
Dr. Grayson N. Kefauver, Assist
ant Chief of the Division of Cultural
Cooperation of the State Depart
ment, and former head of the De
partment of Education at Stanford,
will speak on the subject of Inter
national Education on Wednesday,
January 31, at the Palace Hotel at
3 p. m.
A few months ago Dr. Kefauver
attended the International Education
Conference in London. He is the
outstanding authority in the field of
international education.
