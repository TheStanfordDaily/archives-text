# Birthday Fete Held at Roble
## 
### 
Roble's A lobby and dining room
will be blocked off tonight for the
birthday dinner at which 120 Roble
women who have birthdays between
August and January will be feted.
"This dinner is going to be noth
ing short of wonderful," said Marg
ery Swett, general chairman. "We're
not only going to have entertainment
throughout the meal, but Bugs
Bunny and his associates are going
to be stationed all around the din
ing room," she added.
