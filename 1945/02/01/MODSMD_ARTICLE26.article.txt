# Wheelwright Gives Recital Today
## 
### 
An organ recital will be given at
4:15 p.m. today at the Memorial
Church by D. Sterling Wheelwright,
assistant University organist.
Selections include "Night" by Jen
kins, "Toccata and Fugue in D Mi
nor" by Bach, Bonnet's "Reverie,"
"Gavotte" by Thomas, "Girl with
Flaxen Hair" by Debussy, and Sul
livan's "The Lost Chord."
The Stanford student body and
the general public are welcome.
