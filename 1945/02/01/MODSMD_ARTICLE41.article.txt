# 'Land Of Oz' Bids On Sale Next Week
## 
### 
Bids for the Roble-Branner-Ven
tura formal to be held February 17
in the Women's Gym will go on sale
for $2.00 February 8, according to
Charleen Widmer, ticket chairman.
"Bid salesmen will be appointed in
all Freshman women's dorms," said
Miss Widmer, "and tickets may be
obtained from them only."
Jan Lamb, in charge of decora
tions. has announced that the gym
will be in a "Land of Oz" setting,
with all the citizens of Oz-land on
hand.
Art Weidner and his peninsula or
chestra will play for dancing, which
will continue from 9 until 1.
