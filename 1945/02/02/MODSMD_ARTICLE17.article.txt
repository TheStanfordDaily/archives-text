# Councilman Elect
## 
### 
Herb Zimmerman, recently elected
to Men's Council, is a senior major
ing in Economics. Alter graduating
this quarter he plans to attend the
Graduate School of Business.
He was born in Tsingtao, China
and lived there for 18 years. He be
gan at Stanford in 1939, but the Navy
interrupted his college career in 1942.
A year later he was honorably dis
charged from Naval Intelligence.
Zimmerman then spent a year with
the government doing secret work
translating Japanese and Chinese
maps. He then came back to Stan
ford to ifnish his education.
Besides being a member of Men's
Council he is President of Sequoia
