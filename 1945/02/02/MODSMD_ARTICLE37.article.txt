# Gymnasium Swimming Pools Closed to Army
## 
### 
Because of the cold and flu epi
demic, Colonel Eden and the director
at Dibble have requested that swim
ming pools in Encina gym be closed
to military trainees until the sick
call decreases. Army students taking
swimming courses will be assigned to
other activities until the pools are
opened aagin. Civilian swimming
classes are not effected by this order
and will continue to meet.
