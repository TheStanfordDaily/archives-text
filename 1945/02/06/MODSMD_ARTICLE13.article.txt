# Young Democrats Meet Tonight, 7:30
## 
### 
The Stanford Young Democrats
will hold their first meeting of the
quarter this evening at 7:30 in the
Alumni room upstairs in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. All old members
should attend, as plans will be
made for the activities of the rest
of the quarter. Other interested
students are also invited to attend,
according to Lita Warner, secre
tary.
