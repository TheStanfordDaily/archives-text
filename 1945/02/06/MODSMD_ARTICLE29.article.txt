# Yds, Da Hold Boy Sez 'Hit's a Beeg Boggin'
## 
### 
Geevcs da singink, geeves da
yallink.
Geevcs da shoutink end da shrieks,
For da Hold Boy is hanouncing,
"It giffs Cheppie in two weeks."
i Find da keedies, it's a hoggin.
; Find da ladies end da gents,
| That da Cheppie's at hay new low
price
; Of fife end twenty cents.
—Grunion Scluneckenklipp
