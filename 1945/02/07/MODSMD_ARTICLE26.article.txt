# Farm Researcher Dies in Hospital
## 
### 
Lieutenant Commander Stewart F.
Bryant. USN, retired, died yesterday
in the Stanford Hospital in San
Francisco after a long period of ill
ness. Following his retirement as a
lieutenant commander in 1930 he
undertook research in political
science at Stanford and became West
Coast director of the League of Na
tions Association.
Because of his interest in world
peace, he lectured and wrote ex
tensively on international relations
and attempted to increase knowledge
and understanding of Russia.
