# Lanz Will Speak On Rusian Policy
## 
### 
Dr. Henry Lanz, professor of Slav
ic languages and philosophy, will
speak to the World Affairs Group
today in the "Y" rooms at 2 p.m.,
announced Elinor French, chairman.
Dr. Lanz will discuss "Russian Pol
icy Toward Germany."
'"With the Russians far inside the
borders of the Reich and almost at
the German capital, more than just,
military problems are going to arise
very soon. What Russian political
views will be on the treatment of
postwar Germany is of great impor
tance to all of us." said Miss French.
