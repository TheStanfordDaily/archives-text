# Palo Alto Postmaster Re-elected to Board
## 
### 
Associated Cooperatives re-elected
Ralph W. Evans, postmaster of Palo
Alto, to the board of directors and
president of the annual meeting to
be held July 14-21 at Camp Sierra,
Fresno County.
This organization is the chief sup
ply agency for 30 consumer coopera
tives in California. It also acts as
the educational arm of the coopera
tive movement in the West.
