# Wheelwright Will Give Organ Recital Today
## 
### 
Dr. D. Sterling Wheelwright, as
sistant University organist, will give
an organ recital today at 4:15 p.m.
in the Memorial Church.
He will play "Largo in E" by Han
del, "Piece Heroique" by Franck,
"Springtide" by Grieg, "Rondo Fran
caise" by Boellmann, "Clouds" by
Ceiga, "Prelude and Fugue in G
Minor" by Bach.
