# Wallace Elected Union President
## 
### 
Jeanne Wallace was elected presi
dent of Union Residence over her
opponents Marianne Shipley and
Lucy Vance in yesterday's elections,
Betty Lou Horton, president of Union
Exeom, announced last night.
At the same time, Anita Zimmer
man won over Maxine Hedges in the
contest for the vice-presidency. Patty
Robinson won the office of secretary
from Mary Ellen Cutler, Midge Hex
ter, and Helen Van Keppel.
Helen Peterson, who was running
against Lorn a Robertson, was elected
chairman of the Committee of Nine.
