# Noted Actor Will Speak
## 
### 
Mr. Cedric van Nibroc, formerly
with the Stratford-on-Avon Players
in England, will speak on the history
of the Gilbert and Sullivan operas
at. 4:15 p.m. Wednesday, February
14 in the main auditorium of Me
morial Hall.
lie will tell about the personalities
of the authors, give anecdotes of
productions, and give some criticism
of several of the most important
operas'.
His remarks will be illustrated by
three of the principal singers of the
Gilbert and Sullivan Opera Com
pany, who will be heard in solos,
duets, and trios from some of the
operas discussed.
Mr. Van Nibroc also served as
stage manager for three summers
with the D'Oyly Carte Opera Com
pany.
The program will be under the
auspices of the Department of Speech
and Drama. There will be no admis
sion charge, and the public is invited-
