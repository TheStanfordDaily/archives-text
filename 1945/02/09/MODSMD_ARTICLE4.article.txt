# Spanish Honorary Plans Nitery Sing
## 
### 
Spanish Honorary Society, Sigma
Delta Pi, will be the sponsors of the
All-Campus Spanish Sing scheduled
for Tuesday, February 13. according
to Pat Charlton, president. The sing
ing, lead by Dr. Sangiorgi, will begin
at 7:30 p.m. in the Nitery and last
until 8:30 p.m.
Everyone who enjoys singing is
urged to come. Song sheets will be
provided for those who are unfami
liar with the words. Refreshments
and perhaps a Spanish dance or two
will wind up the evening.
