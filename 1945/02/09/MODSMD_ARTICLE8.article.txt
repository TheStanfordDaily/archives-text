# Last Blood Bank Sign-Ups Monday
## 
### 
Students are reminded that Mon
day, February 12 will be the last day
to sign-up for appointments for the
quarter's third Blood Bank Day next
Tuesday, according to Coot Coffin,
in charge of this branch of War
Board activities.
Tlie sign-up sheet is posted on
the door of the War Board office
and releases, which must be signed
by parent or guardian if the donor
is under 21, can be obtained from
the office or from your living group
representative on War Board.
A Red Cross station-wagon will
provide the transportation and will
leave Engineering corner 20 minutes
befoi'e each individual appointment.
