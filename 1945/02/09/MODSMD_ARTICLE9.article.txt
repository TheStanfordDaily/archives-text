# Organ Recital Will Be Given Sunday
## 
### 
Sunday's organ recital will be giv
en by D. Sterling Wheelwright, as
sistant University organist, in the
Memorial Church at 5 p.m. It will
include Handel's "Largo in E,"
Maquaire's "Allego, Symphony I."
Other numbers on the program are
"Springtide" by Grieg, "Rondo
Francaise" by Bocllmann. "Clouds"
by Ceiga, and "Prelude and Fugue in
G Minor" by Bach.
The a'Cappella Choir of Mission
High School, San Francisco, will
assist in the program of the follow
ing Sunday.
