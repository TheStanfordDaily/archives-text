# New Ski Club Officers Named
## 
### 
Charlotte Townsend was elected
president of the Stanford Ski Club
at its meeting Thursday, February
9. Other newly-elected officers are
David Curry, vice-president; Breazy
Rosenthal, secretary: Jane McCrillis,
treasurer.
Outgoing officers are as follows:
Joanne Scowcroft, president; Paul
ine Holcomb Secor, vice-president;
Charlotte Townsend, secretary
treasurer.
Miss Rosenthal announced that
another meeting will be held soon
"to acquaint the members with one
another and with the ski club."
