# 'Oz-Land' Formal Set for Saturday
## 
### 
Plans for the Roble-Branner-Ven
tura "Land of Oz" formal scheduled
for Saturday, February 17, in the
Women's Gym, are nearing comple
lion according to Mary Lesnett, gen
eral chairman. "The decoration and
entertainment committees and even
the orchestra are working around
the Oz theme," said Miss Lesnett.
Art Weidner and his 10-piece or
chestra will play for dancing, which
will continue from 9 until 1. They
will be accompanied by a girl voca
list.
Bids are on sale for $2 at the three
Frosh women's dorms.
