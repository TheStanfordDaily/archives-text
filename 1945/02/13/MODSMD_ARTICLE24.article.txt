# 'Gilbert, Sullivan' To Be Topic for Van Nibroc Talk
## 
### 
Mr. Cedric van Nibroc will speak
on "An Hour With Gilbert and Sulli
van" tomorrow at 4:15 p.m. in the
main auditorium of Memorial Hall.
His lecture will include the history
of the Gilbert and Sullivan operas,
personalities of the authors, anec
dotes of productions, and criticism of
several of the most important operas.
Mr. Van Nibroc was formerly with
the Stratford-on-Avon Players in
England, and was stage manager of
the D'Oyly Carte Opera Company
for three years. Consequently he
received much of his information
from first-hand acquaintances.
Three of the main singers of the
Gilbert and Sullivan Opera Company
will also be at the lecture. They will
illustrate Mr. Van Nibroc's talk with
solos, duets, and trios from some of
the operas discussed.
The program will be presented by
the Speech and Drama Department.
Everyone is invited to attend," and
there will be no admission charge.
