# Faculty Will Hear Twitty, Savelle
## 
### 
Victor C. Twitty, professor of
biology, and Maxwell H. Savelle,
professor of history will be the
speakers at the Faculty Research
Club meeting at 8 this evening in
the Men's Faculty Clubhouse.
Professor Twitty will speak on
"Experiments on the Embryological
Basis of Hereditary Differences."
Professor Savelle will speak on "The
Flight from Reason in Some Recent
Historical Writings."
