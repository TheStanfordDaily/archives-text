# 'Nisei' Are Subject Of 'Y' Racial Talk
## 
### 
Speaking on "Your Altitude
Toward the Returning Nisei and
Issci," Miss Motoko Hirose, instruc
tor in the CATS program, will open
the lecture series sponsored by the
YWCA Race Relations group today
at 3 p.m.
She will discuss the problems they
face in adjustment to conditions and
public opinion on the Pacific coast,
and will suggest ways in which oth
ers can help their readjustment, and
the difficulties of housing and em
ployment.
Tea and cookies will be served at
this meeting, to be held in the "Y"
rooms of the Women's Clubhouse,
according to Co-chairmen Virginia
Butler and Claire Tolnay.
