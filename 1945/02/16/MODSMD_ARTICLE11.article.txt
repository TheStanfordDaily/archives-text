# Opera Star To Sing At Sunday Recital
## 
### 
Vivian Delia Chiesa, concert and
opera star, will appear at the San
Francisco Opera House next Sunday
afternoon in a double recital with
Andre Segovia. Miss Delia Chiesa
has starred on the "American Mel
ody Hour" and "Album of Familiar
Music" radio programs, and has had
a long operatic career.
Andre Segovia, her co-star on the
Sunday program, appears here in
the course of his first concert tour
through the United States, but he
has given guitar performances in
Europe, Asia, Africa.
