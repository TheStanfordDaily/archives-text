# Needs Decoration Help
## 
### 
All women interested in helping
with decorations for the Sadie Haw
kins' Dance, to be held March 10,
have been asked to meet at the
Nitcry at 1:30 next Wednesday,
February 22. A separate meeting for
men will be held in the Nitcry at 3
the same afternoon. According to
Anne Armstrong and Dick Bern
hardt. co-chairmen of the decoration
committee, workers are needed and
a artistic ability is not essential.
