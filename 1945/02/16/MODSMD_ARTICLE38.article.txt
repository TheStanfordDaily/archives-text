# MOTHER'S CLUB TO MEET
## 
### 
"When, What, and How do Stan
ford Students Eat" will be the topic
Miss Christine Ricker, director of
dining halls, will speak at the regular
February meeting of the Stanford
Mother's Club Tuesday, February 20
at 2 p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
A recital of Sapnish dances by
two Stanford students, Mary Cogs
well and Peggy Bradford will be
part of the program.
