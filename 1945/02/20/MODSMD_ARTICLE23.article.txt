# Quad Announces New Picture Date
## 
### 
Nancy Gilmer, managing editor of
the Quad, announced today that lite
following living groups are in line
for Quad picture*: Cubberly, Du
rand, Lathrop, Roth, Arthur, Cleve
land, Monroe, Tyler, Chinese Club,
and Washington. Pictures will also
be taken of Cap and Gown.
Only these living groups will be
given appointments, and those who
failed to get their pictures taken
when their living group was called,
will not be able to get their picture
in the Quad.
Pictures for these named halls
must be taken by Saturday, Feb. 24.
and no extensions will be granted.
Appointments will be made at the
Quad office.
