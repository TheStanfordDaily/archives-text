# Barker Will Speak At Meeting March 1
## 
### 
A symposium meeting will be held
March 1 when Charles A. Barker,
associate professor of history, will
discuss "Henry George—Democratic
Thinker of California." Following
this talk, on March 8. Edwin Cottrell,
dean of the School of Social Sciences,
and Lowell Tuprentine, professor of
law. will discuss "Administrative
Regulatory Action and Control."
