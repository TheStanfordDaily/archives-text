# Students Must Apply For Loans This Week
## 
###

students wishing to make applica
tions or renewal of their applica
tions for cash loans for spring
quarter tuition should do so some
time this week with the dean of
men or dean of women.
Applications for Student Aid
Grants for spring quarter must he
made also this week in Mr. Win
hiffler's office, room 164 of the Ad
Building.
