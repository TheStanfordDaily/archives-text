# Dr. Wheelwright To Play Sunday
## 
### 
An hour of choral and organ music
will be presented on Sunday, Febru
ary 25, at 4 p.m. at the Memorial
Church by the A Cappella Choir
of Mission High School, San Fran
cisco, and D. Sterling Wheelwright,
assistant University organist.
The choir consists of 82 voices, and
under the direction of Isabelle C.
Becker has been heard annually in
the San Jose Choir Festival each
December, and over the radio. Last
November the choir provided a
choral background for the Army-
Navy War Show in Seals' Stadium.
Soprano and tenor soloists will be
heard at the Stanford concert.
