# Basketball Players Vie in Gym Tonight
## 
### 
Four teams will clash tonight in
the girls' basketball tournament to
determine the teams who will play
on March 6 for the championship.
At 7:45 p.m. Adelfa will play Euca
lada in the Thursday league and at
8:45 Faculty will tangle with Russell
to determine the Tuesday winners.
The committee in charge of the
tourney promises that the games will

have lots of excitment in the 8-
minute quarters which will be played
tonight. Both men and women are
urged to come to the gym and cheer
for the 'teams.
