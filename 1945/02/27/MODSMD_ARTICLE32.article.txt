# Knitting Contest Begins Monday
## 
### 
Women's halls and houses will
compete in an afghan knitting con
test to start next Monday, March 5,
according to Emily Metzger, chair
man of the Knitting Committee.
The victorious living group must
knit the most afghan squares during
the week of the contest. Houses en
tering the contest are urged to reg
ister with Miss Metzger.
Once again girls arc reminded that
all sweaters taken out fall and winter
quarters must be finished and turned
in at the War Board Office on or
before Monday, March 19.
