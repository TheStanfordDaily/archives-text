# Tickets Remain For 'Little Foxes'
## 
### 
Tickets for the special matinee
performance, March 10, and the
Monday night performance, March
12, of "The Little Foxes" by Lillian
Hellman, are now on sale for 90
cents in room 202 of Memorial Hall
or at the Peninsula box office in
Palo Alto, according to Chester W.
Barker, secretary of the Speech and
Drama Department.
Stanford Players will present this
drama in the Little Theater of Me
morial Hall for five nights, begin
ning Wednesday, March 7, and end
ing Monday, March 12, plus the spe
cial matinee showing. Evening per
formances will begin at 8:15 p.m..
and the Saturday matinee at 2:15
p. m.
