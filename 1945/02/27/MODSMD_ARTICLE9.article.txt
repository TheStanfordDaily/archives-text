# MacArthur Restores Philippine Civil Rule
## 
### 
General Douglas Mac Arthur to
night restored civil rule of the
Philippines to the Commonwealth
government in a special ceremony
in the liberated capital. Reviewing
the three years of "bitterness, strug
gle and sacrifice" since he withdrew
his troops from Manila, Mac Arthur
solemnly declared that "by these
ashes" of the city "the enemy has
wantonly fixed the pattern of his
own doom."
The ceremony was broadcast to
radio networks in the United States
and Australia from the Philippines
capital as large numbers of civil and
military officials cheered and up
pauded Mac Arthur and President
Sergio Osmena.
