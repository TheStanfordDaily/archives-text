# Gordy Warns About Pedlars
## 
### 
Watch out for magazine pedlars,
warns Gordy Davis, Stanford police
department head. George Siar, alias
George Searle, an itinerant magazine
soliciter was arrested on campus yes
terday after getting $31 from gullible
Stanford students.
Siar had traveled across the
country from his home state, Mis
souri and had been selling false sub
scriptions of nationally known maga
zines, taking orders from a non
existant company.
Students are warned never to buy
anything from pedlars unless they
arc licensed by the Stanford police
department.
