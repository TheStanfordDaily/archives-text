# 'Dutch Boy' Clogs Disturb Farm Peace
## 
###

toed, wooden-soled, leather shoes,
are threatening the popularity of the
comfortable moccasins. Appearing
in a variety of colors, the determined
tapping of these thick-soled wooden
shoes can be heard for miles around.
