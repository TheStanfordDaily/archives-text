# Graduate Women Offered Awards

Accounting scholarships are being
offered to young women college
graduates by the Price Watcrhouse &
Co., Certified Public Accounting Co.
It is not necessary to have had any
undergraduate training in accounting
to be eligible for one of the 50
scholarships, and the course will
ijegin for successful applicants in
June at Northwestern University in
Evanston, 111.
Successful trainees are promised a
10b with the firm upon completion of
he course. Salary covering the
training period will be given as
.veil as nine months' employment
vith the fiim which the student

agrees to take. If mutually satis
factory, employment may continue
indefinitely.
Late in March, representatives
from the Price Warerhouse & Co.
will be in San Francisco and on
campus so that senior applicants
may arrange appointments with
them.
Senior women desiring more in- |
formation on the scholarships should I
