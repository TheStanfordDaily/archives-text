# Buck To Lecture On Peace Leagues
## 
### 
Speakng on the Dumbarton Oaks
peace conference and the coming
United Nations meeting in San Fran
cisco, Dr. Philip Buck, professor of
political science, will compare the
peace which may result from World
War II with the League of Nations
peace, announced Carol Kellogg,
chairman.
The discussion is sponsored by the
World Affairs Group of the YWCA,
now combined with the defunct In
ternational Club, and is open to
faculty and students. It will be
Thursday at 4:15 p.m., in the *'Y"
rooms of the Women's Clubhouse.
Refreshments will be served at 4
p.m.
