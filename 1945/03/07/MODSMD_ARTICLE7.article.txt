# RADCLIFFE FELLOWSHIP
## 
### 
Radcliffe College in Cambridge,
Mass. is offering three fellowships of
$500 each in a "Management Train
ing Program" beginning July 30,
1945.
Qualified women, who will grad
uate before July of this year, should
make applications promptly for the
fellowships.
Registration in the course is also
open without a fellowship. Tuition
for the course is $450.
