# Busses Run Every 20 Minutes on Week Days
## 
### 
The new bus schedule to Palo Alto
applies to all week days including
Saturday. Busses leave Stanford from
the top of the Row at 20 minutes
past, 20 minutes to, and on the hour,
every hour.
After 7:30 p.m. busses will leave
the campus on the hour and half
hour.
Busses leave the S. P. Depot in
Palo Alto at 10 past, half past, and
10 to the hour, every hour. The Sun
day schedule is the same as before:
on the half hour.
