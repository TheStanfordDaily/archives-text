# Dr. Durand Given Scientific Honors
## 
### 
Dr. Robert C. Miller, director of
the California Academy of Sciences,
has just announced that Dr. William
F. Durand, emeritus professor o:
mechanical engineering in Stanford
University, was unanimously elected
an honorary member of the Academy
at its annual meeting.
This is the highest honor that the
academy confers. Dr. Durand has
been a member of the California
Academy of Sciences since 1921, and
a Fellow sincc 1926.
