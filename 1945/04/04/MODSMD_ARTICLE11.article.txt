# Board and Room To Be Paid Today
## 
###

residences must be paid today
between the hours of 9 a.m. and
12 noon and from 1 to 5 p.m. in the
Ad Building.
The room and board total (less
the $25 room deposit paid in fall
quarter) is $160.50 Ration books
must be also returned today.
