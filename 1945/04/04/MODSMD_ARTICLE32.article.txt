# Mariner Training Starts in Palo Alto
## 
### 
A 25-hour course for the training
of Girl Scout Mariner skippers and
mates will be offered at the Girl
Scout House in Palo Alto from 10
a.m. to 3 p.m., April 9 to 13, accord
ing to Mrs. Milton Siebert, Palo Alto
Scout worker. Mrs. Albert J. Allen
of Los Angeles will teach the course.
She is also director of the Mariner
Camp at Balboa Island.
Skippers must be at least 21 years
old, and mates must be 18 or over,
said Mrs Sieber.t but no previous
Mariner experience is required.
Further information may be obtained
by calling Mrs. Spence, secretary, at
the Girl Scout House, Palo Alto 6191.
