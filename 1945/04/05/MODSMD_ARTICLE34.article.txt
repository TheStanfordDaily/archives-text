# Kiel Hit by U.S. Heavy Bombers
## 
### 
LONDON, April 4 (UP).-More
than 1000 U.S. Eighth Air Force
heavy bombers escorted by 850 fight
ers today shot up airdromes in
northwest Germany and bombed
submarine yards at Kiel and Ham
burg, loosing half of their bomb
weight on Kiel where great fires
were started in the third attack on
naval targets in five days.
RAF Lancasters in "great force"—
probably 600 or 700—and escorted by
fighters bombed the communications
city of Nordhausen for the second
straight day and the German radio
reported Allied bombers over a wide
area of western, northwestern, and
central Germany including Berlin.
