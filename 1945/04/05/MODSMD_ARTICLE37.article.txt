# LIFEGUARD NEEDED
## 
### 
Lifeguards are needed at the Wo
men's Gym Pool on Monday, Tues
day and Thursday at 2 p.m. and on
Tuesday at 4 p.m.
Girls who hold a Senior Red Cross
Life Saving Certificate are eligible
for the job and should contact Mrs.
Fenner at the Women's Gym.
The pay is 50 cents an hour.
