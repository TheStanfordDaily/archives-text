# Vinson Nomination Unanimously OK'd
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 4 (UP).—
The Senate today unanimously ap
proved the nomniation of Fred M.
Vinson to be director of war mobili
zation and reconversion and there
was immediate speculation whether
he would lift some of the home front
restrictions imposed by his prede
cessor, James F. Byrnes.
