# Dr. Allen To Give Recital in Chapel
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen will give the
organ recital this Sunday at 4:15
p.m. in the Memorial Church.
Included on his program will be
Easter Prelude, "O Filii et Filiae"
(Sons and Daughters of the King),
by Arthur Egerton; chorale-preludes
lor Easter, "Christ Lay in Bonds of
Death," "Today the Son of God Tri
umphs," and"The Blessed Christ Is
Risen Today," by J. S. Bach; "Awake
Thou Wintry Earth," by Bach-Whit
ford; "In Paradisum," "Hosannah!"
"Chorus Magnus," by T. Dubyois;
"Communion," "Kyrie Eleison," by
i Richard Purvis.
