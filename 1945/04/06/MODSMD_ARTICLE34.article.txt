# Conference Meets Monday Afternoon
## 
### 
Women's Conference will meet
Monday at 3:15 p.m. in the Chintz
room of the Women's Clubhouse,
according to Pam Strathairn, new
president.
Agenda for the meeting will be
published in Monday's Daily. "Con
ference will try to be concerned
mainly with discussion of affairs con
cerning women students and will
place less importance on announce
ments," Miss Straithairn said.
