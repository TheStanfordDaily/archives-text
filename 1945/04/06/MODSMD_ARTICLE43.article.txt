# Roble Kitchen Staff Honors Store Keeper
## 
### 
Roble kitchen staff honored Fred
King, Roble storekeeper since April
4, 1C24, at an afternoon party Wed
nesday, in honor of his twentieth
year at Roble.
Special guests were Miss Florence
Smith, assistant director of dining
halls; Miss Christine Ricker, director
of dining halls; Mrs. Erna Lehan, di
rector of Lagunita dining hall, and
Miss Clara Palmer, director of Roble
dining hail.
