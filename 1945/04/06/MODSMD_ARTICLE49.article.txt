# Women's Pool Open Saturdays
## 
### 
Plans for opening the swimming
pool at the Women's Gym were
made at the meeting of the Women's
Athletic Association at 4 p.m. yes
terday. Hours will be from 2 to 5
p.m. Saturday, according to B. J.
Rucker, president. The pool will be
limited to Stanford students, faculty,
and guests. The men's pool will be
open on Sunday.
Spring softball was discussed at
the meeting and plans were also for
mulated at the captains' meeting last
night. The tournament will start
Tuesday at 7 p.m., the days being
Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday
of each week. A full schedule will
be given in the Daily next week.
