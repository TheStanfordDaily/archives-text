# Peace Plans Topic Of 'Y' Discussion
## 
### 
Deciding to emphasize the present
peace conference in relation to the
postwar world, the World Affairs
Group of the YWCA met yesterday
| to formulate this quarter's plans,
i Most of the discussions planned will

take an overall view of future peace
decisions rather than localizing them,
said Carol Kellogg, chairman.
The meetings will be on Thurs
days, at 4:15 p.m., as usual. Refresh
ments will be served.
