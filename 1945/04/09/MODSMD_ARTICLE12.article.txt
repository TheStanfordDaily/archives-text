# Shaughnessy Wed In Chapel Service
## 
### 
Janice Shaughnessy, daughter of
ex-Stanford Football Coach Clark
Shaughnessy, was married yesterday
at 5 p.m. in the Memorial Chapel to
Lt. William Kreutzman, '44.
The bride graduated from Stanford
in June and has been an instructor
of physical education for the last two
quarters.
