# Board Will Meet In Vestry Tonight
## 
### 
Representatives to Chapel Board
from all campus living groups are
asked to attend the meeting of Chap
el Board in the vestry of the Me
morial Church tonight from 9 to 10.
At that time ushers and scripture
readers for the services in the Chapel
will be selected.
Dr. Elton Trueblood, University
chaplain, will relate to the group
some of his experiences during his
stay in the East and at the Garret
Biblical School in Evanston, Illinois.
He has been an sabattical leave from
Stanford for nine months and has
just recently returned.
