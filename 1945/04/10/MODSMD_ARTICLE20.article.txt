# Intramural Games To Be Scheduled
## 
### 
The intramural Softball program
is scheduled to get underway on
Thursday night, according to
Harry Wolter, with seven teams en
tered in the race.
The entries include Monroe, the
defending champions, Tyler, Phi
Kappa Psi, Buchanan, Toyon, USA,
and the Legal Eagles. Paced by
ace pitcher, Bob Rosburg, the Mon
roe ten has an excellent chance of
repeating last year's victory.
Games are scheduled to start at
7:15, or earlier if possible, on Tues
day and Thursday evenings. A com
plete schedule of contests should be
worked out by Coach Wolter by this
afternoon.
