# Ventura Plans Fun At Lake Saturday
## 
### 
Casa Ventura, freshmen women's
hall, is making plans for a party at
Lagunita Lake next Saturday after
noon. Boating and swimming races
are on the program, in addition to
games and refreshments. Carol Bull,
house president, promises that it will
be an afternoon full of fun for all the
girls.
On the social calendar Casa Ven
tura is scheduling a May Day garden
party for all former residents of the
house. As yet the plans for other
social events for the quarter have
not been completed by the senators
of the house.
