# Barker Will Conduct Chapel Service Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Charles Barker, professor of
history, will read one of his favorite
selections of literature at the Vesper
Service in the choir loft of the Me
morial Church tonight from 7 to
7.20. The program will be preceded
and followed by 10 minutes organ
recitals.
The service is open to everyone on
campus.
