# Medical Schedule For Men Reported
## 
### 
Dr. O. N. Anderson's office has
announced the following schedule
for the Men's Health Service and
the Army medical dispensary.
Civilian students will report for
medical attention and consultation
from 1 to 5 p.m. each day. Company
A sick calls will be at 9 a.m. every
day, and Company C at 10 a.m. every
day. Both companies will report at
10 a.m. on Sundays and holidays.
