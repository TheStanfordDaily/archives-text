# COAL DISPUTE SETTLED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. April 10 (UP).—
The soft coal operators and the
United Mine Workers reached an
agreement on a new wage-hour con
tract tonight after a five weeks' old
dispute which culminated in gov
ernment seizure of 235 strike-bound
"captive" mines in seven states.
