# Dr. Cole To Talk On Archaeology
## 
### 
"Piecent Developments in North
American Archaeology, with Special
Reference to the Mississippi Valley,"
will be the subject of a talk by Fay-
Cooper Cole, professor of anthropol
ogy, of the University of Chicago,
at the American Studies Symposium
meeting Thursday afternoon, 4:15
p.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
Dr. Cole has been chairman of the
Department of Anthropology at the
University of Chicago since 1929. He
is author of several books and an
authority on Philippine history and
ethnology, race and race problems.
He has participated in archaeologi
cal expeditions in the Southwest,
has conducted expeditions among
pagan tribes of the Philippines. He
was the leader of the Field Museum
Expedition to the Malay Peninsula,
Sumatra, Java, and Borneo. He is
a Fellow of the American Associa
tion for the Advancement of Science.
