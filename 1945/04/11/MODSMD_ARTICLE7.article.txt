# W.A.A. Announces Swimming Hours
## 
### 
Mary Stafford and Joan Fullin
wider are the new freshman and
junior representatives on the board
of the Women's Athletic Association.
The board met at 5 p.m. yesterday in
the Women's Gym.
Free hours for swimming in the
women's pool were definitely decided
at the meeting. The pool will be
open Mondays and Thursdays from
3 to 6 p.m.; Tuesdays and Wednes
days from 5 to 6 p.m.; and Saturdays
from 2 to 5 p.m. Swimmers may
wear their own suits, and arc re
quired to wear caps.
Also discussed were plans for a
field day in the near future.
