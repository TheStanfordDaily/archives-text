# Chinese Education 'I' Meeting Topic
## 
### 
Intcrnational Club meets for the
first time this quarter, Sunday. April
15, at 7:30 p.m. The meeting will be
held at the home of Mr. and Mrs.
E. J. Thomas, 365 Lincoln. Palo Alto.
The guest speaker will be Dr. A.
Pen-Tung Sah, president of the Uni
versity of Amoy, China, and a 1924
Stanford graduate. He will discuss
"China and Its Education."
The meeting is open to the public
and guests as well as members are
invited. A special invitation has been
extended to the members of the Chi
nese Club, according to Mob Myhre,
. president of the I Club.
Refreshments will be served.
