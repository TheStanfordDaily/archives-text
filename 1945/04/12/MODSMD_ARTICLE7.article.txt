# 1945 Civilian Student Total Higher Than 1944
## 
### 
Latest, registration figures for the
spring quarter as released by the
Registrar's office yesterday are as
follows: civilian men, 88(5; women,
1(534. This is a total increase of 335
over last year's civilian registration
figures; this year's total Is 2520.
Army students attending Stanford
number 207, with the Civil Affairs
Training School adding 190, to bring
the military total to 397, and the
over-all registration total to 2917.
