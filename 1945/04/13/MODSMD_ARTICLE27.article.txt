# Medical Students Take Test Today
## 
### 
All students who expect to apply |
for admission to medical school in
1945 or in the spring of 1946 should

afternoon. The test is given by the

Colleges and is a normal require
ment for admission to medical
schools.
The test will be given in room 214
at 2:00 this afternoon, and will not j
be given again this year. There is a !
charge of $1.50 made, payable at the j
time of taking the test.
