# Chinese Talk To Be Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. A. Pen-Tung Sah, president of
the National University of Amoy,
China, will be the guest speaker at
the International Club meeting this
Sunday, April 15, at 7:30 p.m. The
club meets at the home of Mr. and
Mrs. E. J. Thomas, 365 Lincoln, Palo
Alio.
Dr. Sah, who graduated from
Stanford in 1924 and is now serving
as a visiting professor of electrical
engineering, will speak on "China
and Its Education."
Everyone is welcome, according to
Bob Myhre, president, whether he
is a member of the club or just a
guest. A special invitation is extend
ed to the members of the Chinese
Club.
Refreshments wll be served.
