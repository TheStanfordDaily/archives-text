# Spring Show Tryouts For Olio Acts Monday
## 
### 
Tryouts for the olio acts of the
Spring Show will Ik* held Mon
day night between 7:.'H) and 10 at
the Little Theater. This is a chance
to submit ideas for songs, dances,
; or specialty acts, according to Co
rinne Wollman, director.
Anyone interested in workng on
; stage, make-up, props, lighting, or
costume crews should sign up then,
said Miss Wollman.
The cast for the melodrama,
"From Itags to Hiches," scheduled
to headline the show, will he an
nounced in Monday's Daily.
