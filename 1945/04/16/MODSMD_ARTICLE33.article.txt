# ROOSEVELT SERVICES
## 
### 
Special church services for Frank
lin D. Roosevelt were conducted
yesterday morning at Memorial
Chapel. Dr. D. Elton Trueblood.
University chaplain, delivered the
sermon, "Mr. Roosevelt's Unfinished
Work," which he concluded with
the reading of the prayer with which
the late President led the nation on
D-Day.
The President's favorite hymn
was sung by the University Choir,
and "Oh. Captain, My Captain," was
read in memory of him. Service*
were concluded with the placing of
a floral wreath on the altar and play
ing of taps.
