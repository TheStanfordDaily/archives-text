# '45 Spring Show Cast Announced
## 
### 
The cast list for the Spring Show
has been announced by Corky Woll
man, president of Ram's Head and
director of the show.
They are: Stewart Harrison as
Prince Charlie, Newton Arnold as
Ned Nimble, Johnny High as Albert
Cooper, Bob Blum as Brown, Hal
Campbell as old Montgomery, Rene
Zentner as Mike, Willie Davis as
Japanese Joe, Jane Saulsbery as
Flossie, Mary Buchner as Flora
Bradley, Murray Stirton as Gertrude
Clark, and Janice Swan as Mother
Murphy.
Dates of rehearsals will be an
nounced later in the Daily.
