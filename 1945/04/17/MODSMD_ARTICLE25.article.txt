# Hoover To Speak On War Conference
## 
### 
Herbert C. Hoover, former Pres
ident of the United States, and
Stanford alumnus, will be heard
over KPO from 5:30 to 6 today,
speaking from Philadelphia, Penn.,
on "The San Trancisco Conference
and the Peace."
Mr. Hoover's speech will be pre
sented before the Philadelphia
Foreign Policy Association and
broadcast by the National Broad
casting Company.
