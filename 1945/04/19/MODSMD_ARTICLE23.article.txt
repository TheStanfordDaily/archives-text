# WATER CARNIVAL
## 
### 
Excom last night approved the fol
lowing appointments for the junior
water carnival:
Barbara Beardslee, general chair
man; Joan Fullinwider, prizes; Mar
ion Bush, publicity; K. J. Supple and
Ruth Maxwell, floats; Merlon All
bright., printing; Pat Dana, Marilyn
Weiss, and Rolf Westley, arrange
ments; Tom Ashley, refreshments.
