# Student Will Play Carillon on Sunday
## 
### 
James Lawson, carillonneur, will
be assisted Sunday in the carillon
recital from the Hoover Library by
Raymond Sanders, student carillon
neur.
The program will be: "Awake
Thou Wintry Earth," from the can
tata "Praised Be the Lord," by J. S.
Bach; "Deep River"; excerpts from
"A Day in Venice," by Ethelbert
Nevin; "Waltz in A," by Brahms; and
"Carillon Dance," by Wilmot Pratt.
This concluding number will be
played by Sanders.
The recital will close with the
hymn "Bless Thou the Gifts Our
Hands Have Brought," by Robert
Schumann.
