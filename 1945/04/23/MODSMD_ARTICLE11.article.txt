# Tickets on Sale For Comic Opera
## 
### 
Tickets for the spring opera. 'The
Merry Wives of Windsor" by Otto
Nicolai. are now on sale in Room 202
of Memorial Hall. The opera will be
given May 24, 25, and 26 at 8:15 p.m.
in Memorial Auditorium.
It is a comic optra which is based
on the Shakespeare comedy, and is
being offered by the Stanford Play
ers in co-operation with the Division
of Music.
Herbert Jan Popper of the Music
Department will direct an orchestra
! of about 50 players and a chorus of
40 together with 12 soloists. The
whole production will be under the
! supervision of F. Cowles Strickland
lof the Department of Speech and
i Drama.
Wendell Cole is providing the set
i lings. The opera is in three acts with J
! nine scenes
