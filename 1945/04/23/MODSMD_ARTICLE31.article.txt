# Vaccination Record Due Next Monday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due
at the Registrar's office on Mon
day, April 30, for all new students,
including those having permit to
attend classes, and all others whose
certificates have expired. Failure
to file certificates by 5 p.m. on
Monday afternoon will result in a
fine of S2; or. if not filed by May 7,
registration in the University will
be subject to cancellation by the
Committee on Public Health.
