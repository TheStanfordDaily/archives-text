# Folk Dance Group To Learn Huaino
## 
### 
Stanford Folk Dancers will meet
tonight from 8 to 10 p.m. in the Wo
men's gym. Elementary dances will
be taught during the first half of
the evening, and Miss Clarice
Dechent will teach the Bolivian Hua
ino at 9 p.m.
Dottie Frost, president of the folk
dancers, has reminded all Huaino
dancers to bring large handkerchiefs,
which are used in several steps of
the dance. Miss Frost pointed out
that the Huaino will be presented for
the first time in California at the
Folk Festival to be held at Stanford
on May 27.
Club meetings are open to anyone
interested in folk dancing and folk
arts.
