# Music Today First In Allied Series
## 
### 
The first, of several Thursday or
gan recitals devoted to the music
of the United Nations will be played
by Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, this afternoon at 4:15 in
the Memorial Church.
Included in the program will be
"The Netherlands Song of Thanks
giving," a traditional folk song; "Re
cit de Tierce en Taillc" by Nicolas
dc Grigny; "Land of Hope and
Glory" by Sir Edward Elgar; "Shin
ing Shore" by Edward Shippen
Barnes; "Puritan Procession" from
the organ suite, "Pioneer America"
by Scth Bingham;; "Meadowland"
the Red Army song; and "Joshua"
by Modeste Moussorgsky.
