# MUSSOLINI SEIZED
## 
###

Mussolini, dictator who led Italy into
war and brought her disaster, has
fallen into the hands of Italian pa
triots and attempted to flee after Al
lied armies and anti-Fascists caused
a rising in Italy, according to Swiss
reports tonight.
