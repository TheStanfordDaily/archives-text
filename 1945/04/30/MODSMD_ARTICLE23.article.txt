# ALLIES CAPTURE MILAN
## 
### 
ROME, April 29 (UP). —Allied
roops today captured Milan, the
cradle city of Fascism, outflanked
Venice and broke through the Swiss
border in a lightning thrust that split
disorganized Nazi remnants in north
ern Italy.
