# Final Sponsor Tryouts Today
## 
### 
Sponsor Choosing Committee, un
der the chairmanship of Lorrain
Streblow, will meet today from 2 to
5 and 7 to 8 p.m. in the Excom room
to interview girls interested in be
coming sponsors of freshman women.
Girls trying out must be of at least
first-quarter junior standing and
have a C average, preferably better.
Those chosen will be sponsors either
summer and fall quarters or fall and
winter quarters.
Girls interested in running in the
all-campus election, May 16, as well
as trying out for sponsor, are urged
to try out before the committee to
day, as their status as a.sponsor will
not be affected by participating in
the election. Appointments will not
be announced until after the elec
tion.
Members of the committee are:
Yvonne Guilbert, Winifred Lamm,
Jo Davis, Lorraine Harband, B. J.
Miller, Patty Patten, Marylou Mc-
Clure, and Pam Strathairn.
