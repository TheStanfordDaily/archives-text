# Seen from an Ivory Tower
## 
### 
"Oh dear!" cried Alice, "I'm getting awfully tired of
swimming around and the water's cold and my teeth
are beginning to chatter."
She started swimming towards the shore again, but it
seemed to remain as far away as ever, no matter how
hard she struggled to reach it. And she got tireder and
tireder and irritabler and irritabler.
"Oh, damn!" she exclaimed at last in a distraught
voice, brushing the wet hair out of her eyes for what
seemed to be the hundredth time. "I don't believe
that I shall ever get anywhere, especially with all
this silly hair getting in my way and my feet all
tangled up in my frock!"
