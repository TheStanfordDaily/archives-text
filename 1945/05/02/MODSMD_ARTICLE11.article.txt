# Carnival Is Feature Of Con Home Week
## 
### 
Tyler's rat race, Buchanan's
Varga girls. Ruble's taxi-dancers,
an<l Union's telegraph operators
will jam the Pavilion Friday night
when the Soph Carnival is opened
to the whole campus.
For a dime, script tickets may
he purchased enabling carnival
goers to buy ice cream, candied
apples, root beer, or cokes; to
shoot water pistols at candles,
throw hoops over Oaks' limbs, or
dance to the music of the Army
hand. All proceeds from the Car
nival will go to the Con Home, ac
cording to chairman Johnny Jen
kins.
