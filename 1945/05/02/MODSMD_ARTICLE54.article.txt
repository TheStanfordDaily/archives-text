# Excom Tonight; Agenda Listed
## 
### 
Executive Committee will meet
at 7:15 o'clock tonight in the Excom
Room of the Old Union, according
to Marylou McClure, A.S.S.U. vice
president.
Agenda fur the meeting is as fol
lows:
Minutes

Appointment
Rules
Proposed constitution
V-E Day program
Report of Honor Code Standing
Committee
Dimes for Diplomas
All students arc invited to attend
and take part in the discussion.
