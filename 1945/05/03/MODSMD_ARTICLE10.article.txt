# Truman Confirms Death of Fuehrer
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 2 (UP).—
President Truman revealed today
that it had been officially confirmed
on the best authority now available
1 Hat Adolf Hitler is dead. He said
he did not know how the Fuehrer
died, nor would he divulge the
source of this information.
