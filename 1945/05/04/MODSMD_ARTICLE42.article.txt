# Frosh Edition Staff Meets Tomorrow at 1
## 
### 
Staff members of the Daily Frosh
Edition which will come out May
14, will meet in the Daily Shack
at I p.m. Saturday to plan the is
sue, according to Nancy Bannick,
editor.
Freshmen who cannot come at
this time should notify Miss Ban
nick by noon today.
