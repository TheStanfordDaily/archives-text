DELEGATES, press and radio representatives and a portion of the
public swarmed to the steps of San Francisco's fabulous Opera House as
the United Nations gathered to form a peace that should not and will not
be destroyed again by the aggressive acts of any power-loving country.
In the minds of the people who have come to San Francisco to enforce
that peace, as in the mind of a waiting world, is one thought, "We must
not fail." '
