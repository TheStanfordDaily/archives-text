# Room Deposits In Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
Undergraduate women now living
in University residences or the Co
operatives must pay room deposits
for autumn quarter today and to
morrow in the Administration build
ing from 8:30 to 12:00 a. m. and from
12:30 to 4:30 p.m.
Undergraduate women now living
off-campus who wish to reserve
rooms in a residence for fall quar
ter must pay their deposits on Fri
day, May 11, in Miss Doyle's office,
room 160, Ad building. Room de
posits for summer quarter should be
paid on May 28.
