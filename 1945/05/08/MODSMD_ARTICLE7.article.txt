# Y.W.C.A. Group to Hear Trueblood Thursday
## 
### 
Dr. D. Ellon Trueblood, University
chaplain, will discuss the "Fair Em
ployment Practices Commission" for
the Y.W.C.A. Race Relations Group
Thursday at 4 p.m. in the "Y" rooms
of the Women's Clubhouse, an
nounced Ellic Brill, co-chairman.
The commission is a federal agen
cy created to consider the welfare
and interests of minority racial
groups. Problems such as housing
and employment are stressed.
