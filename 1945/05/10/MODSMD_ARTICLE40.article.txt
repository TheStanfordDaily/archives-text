# Scholarship Won By Local Girl
## 
### 
Doris Todd, daughter of Dr. Lois
Todd, Women's Medical Adviser,
has been awarded a national schol
arship to Smith College by the
Seven College Conference, accord
ing to information received Monday
from the Conference. Miss Todd, a
Senior at the Palo Alto High School,
received one of seven awards given
to students on the West Coast. She
plans to enter medicine after her
studies at Smith.
The award has a maximum value
of $1100, and may be renewed by
the individual colleges.
Smith College, a women's college
in Northampton, Massachusetts, is
one of seven women's colleges par
ticipating in the awards made by
the Seven College Conference.
