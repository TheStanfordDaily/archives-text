# Lagunita Hears Professors Talk
## 
### 
Plans for postwar organization
were discussed last night in the Eu
calypto social room by Professors
Theodore Boggs, Thomas Bailey, and
Edwin A. Cottrell, as women of La
gunita listened and questioned the
speakers.
Dr. Boggs reviewed the economic
implications of Bretton Woods. Dean
Cottrell discussed the San Fran
cisco Conference, while Professor
Bailey pointed out the mistakes
made in the peace conference fol
lowing the first World War.
Doris Butler was in charge of the
arrangements for the discussion pe
riod. The professors were guests at
dinner before speaking.
