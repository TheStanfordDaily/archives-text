# Army on Okinawa Progresses Slowly
## 
### 
GUAM, May 10 (UP).-U.S. Tenth
Army troops on southern Okinawa
pushed slowly forward Wednesday
with the support of a thundering
naval and air bombardment, it was
announced today.
American casualties amounted to
16,435 killed, wounded, and missing
at the end of Monday's fighting.
