# Bach to Be Heard In Organ Recital
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will give an organ recital
this Sunday at 4 p.m. in the Memo
rial Church. Included on the pro
gram will be Chorale Improvisation
on "A Mighty Fortress Is Our God";
"Hallelujah Chorus" (Mount of Ol
ives) by Beethoven; "All Through
the Night"; "March of the Men of
Harlech"; "Sieiliano in G minor" and
"Fugue in E fiat" (St. Ann) by J. S.
Bach.
