# Colman Will Star In Sunday Movie
## 
### 
Wardlaw Winners presents "The
Prisoner of Zenda" Sunday in the
Memorial Auditorium. The movie is
an old-fashioned mixture of ro
mance and adventure with Ronald
Colman, Madeleine Carroll, and
Douglas Fairbanks, Jr., playing the
leading roles. "Phantom Empire,"
Gene Autry serial, will also be
shown. Time is 7:15 p.m.; price, 20
cents.
