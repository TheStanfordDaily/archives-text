# Women's Conference To Settle Closing Hours
## 
### 
The final report on Saturday night
closing hours for all women's halls
will be given at today's meeting of
Women's Conference at 3 p.m. in the
Chintz room of the Women's Club
house, according to Eleanor Marks,
vice-chairman.
A committee was appointed last
week to work out the problem by
devising a system whereby individ
ual houses could phone in each Sat
urday if a majority of students in
the house Wrint the house to stay
open until 1:30.
An Election Board member will
speak to Conference members on
the preferential voting system and
the all-campus election which will
be held Wednesday.
Any students interested are invit
ed to attend.
