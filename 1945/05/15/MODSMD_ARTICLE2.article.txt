# Recent Books Will Be Read
## 
### 
Condensations of two popular
novels will be read at the spring
quarter Reading Hour tomorrow at
4:15 p.m. in the Little Theater of
Memorial Hall.
Elaine Maltzman will read "A
Tree Grows in Brooklyn." by Betty
Smith, a book which still continues
to sell as one of the most popular
books of the past ten years.
Sue Sheldon will read Margaret
Lee Runbeck's "Our Miss Boo," a
collection of short sketches concern
ing the daily activities of a 4-year
old girl.
The Reading Hour is under the di
rection of Dr. Paul McKelvey of the
Department of Speech and Drama.
This is the first time it has been
given in the afternoon. No admis
sion will be charged and the pub
lic is invited.
