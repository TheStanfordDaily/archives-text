# Nominations End; Voting Tomorrow
## Candidates To Appear At Assembly 
### 
Candidates for the 26 offices open
in the ASSU election will be pre
sented to the student body at the
annual all-campus election assembly
tomorrow at 10 a.m in the Memorial
Auditorium. All 10 o'clock classes
will be dismissed in order that every
student may attend the assembly.
The assembly, held before each
annual election in order that the
candidates may be introduced to
the student body, will open with
songs and cheers led by Ric Supple.
Barbara White, present ASSU presi
dent, will then present the nominees
running for each of the offices. Only
candidates for president and vice
president of the ASSU are to make
brief addresses.
