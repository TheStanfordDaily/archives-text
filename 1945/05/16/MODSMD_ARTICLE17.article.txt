# ASC Plans Dance To Honor Grads
## 
### 
In honor of the graduating class,
Mechanical Engineers, Term 6, of
the AST, an ASC-sponsored Army
Dance will be held Friday evening,
May 18, at 8:30 p.m. in the Encina
pavilion.
On hand to supply rippling and
syncopatin' rhythm will be the Ar
my and Student Dance Band under
the leadership of our Lou Baldacci.
This dance will be the last of the
ASC-sponsored dances for the pres
ent quarter. Refreshments will be
served through the auspices of the
American Red Cross. Admission is
free, and all Servicemen and Stan
ford students, who will be asked to
present their student body cards, are
invited.
