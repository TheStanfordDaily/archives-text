# Series Presents Pinza Tomorrow
## 
### 
Ezio Pinza will appear at the Me
morial Auditorium tomorrow eve
ning at 8:15, under the sponsorship
of the Concert Series. Pinza's famed
basso has been heard at the Metro
politan since 1926. and he is well
known for his roles in ' Aida" and
| "Faust."
' His program will be as follows:
I
Aria: Casa Sposo, from Rinaldo—
Handel.
Aria: Si Tra I Ceppi. from Berenice
—Handel.
Ari: Dormi. Amori. from La Flora—
Da Gafliano.
II Maritimo and Novara la Bella,
Piemontese folk songs arranged by
Leone Sinigaglia.
II
Trois jours cle vendage—Hahn.
L'Heureux vagabond—Bruneau.
Plaisir d'amour—Martini.
Au Pays—Holmes.
111
The Lament of lan the Proud —,
Charfes Griffes.
Winter—Edward Harris.
To One Unknown—John Alden Car
oenter.
"Do You Remember?"—Mischa Le
vitki.
Cato's Advice (eighteenth century
drinking song)—Bruno Huhn.
IV
I Due Tarli—Riccardo Zandonai.
Fiocca la Neve—Pierto Cimara.
