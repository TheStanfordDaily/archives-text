# Lagunita Hasher Team To Hold Bond Auction
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will be the scene
of a bond auction immediately after
dinner tonight, when the Court
hasher team begins its fight for the
title of campus champ. All women
are advised to bring their wallets
and check books.
Entertainment by Hasher Frank
Hoffman and a quartet of men hash
ers will serve as background to the
race for sales. In addition to the
competition among hashers, easas
within Lagunita will vie for the
carton of cigarets offered as prize
for highest purchases.
