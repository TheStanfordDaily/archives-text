# MEARS' MONOGRAPH
## 
### 
All delegates and advisers at the
United Nations Conference have re
ceived copies of a monograph by
Dr. Eliot G. Mears, professor of
geography and international trade
at Stanford. In the monograph Dr.
Mears urges the setting up of a
world trade agency as it is provided
for in the Dumbarton Oaks agree
ment.
