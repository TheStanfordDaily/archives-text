# Dean to Address Women Monday
## 
### 
Dean Mary Yost will speak at
Monday's meeting of Women's Con
ference which will be held at 3 p.m.
in the Chintz Room of the Wom
en's Clubhouse, according to Elea
nor Marks, vice-chairman.
Miss Yost will give a report on a
meeting she will attend in San
Francisco with University women,
some students, and some of the
women delegates to the San Fran
cisco Conference, on "Problems
Women Will Have to Face Tomor
row."
Newly elected women's officers
are urged to come to Conference,
Miss Marks said, and also any other
students that are interested are in
vited to come.
