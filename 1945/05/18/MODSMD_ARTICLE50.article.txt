# FINAL UNCIO FORUM
## 
### 
In the final UNCIO forum at the
Palo Alto High School, Monday,
four experts from Stanford and Palo
Alto will serve as panel members.
The discussion will be mainly on
the achievements of the United Na
tions Conference. The members
from Stanford will be Dr. Shau
Wing Chan, Dr. Rudolf Holsti, and
Dr. Philip Buck; and the member
from Palo Alto will be Dr. Harry
B. Reynolds. The meeting will start
at 7:30 Monday evening.
