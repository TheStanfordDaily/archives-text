# Rabbis Will Lead Chapel Services
## 
### 
Two Jewish leaders, Rabbi Irving
F. Reichert and Rabbi Reuben R.
Rinder, will be the guests of Stan
ford University at the Memorial
Church on Sunday morning at the
11 a.m. service.
Rabbi Reichert is chief rabbi of
Temple Emanu-El in San Francisco,
and Rabbi Rinder is cantor.
At the service the former will
preach the sermon, while the latter
will sing the ancient Hebrew re
sponses of the Temple. Cantor Rin
der will sing from the chancel and
will be answered by the Stanford
Choir, also singing in Hebrew.
This is the only visit of these men
to Stanford for the current academic
year and everyone is invited.
