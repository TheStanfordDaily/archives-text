# Belgian Sweaters Due Next Monday
## 
### 
Girls are reminded that next
Monday, May 28. is the last day on
which sweaters for Belgian War Re
lief may be turned in at the Wax-
Board office.
They must be completed and de
posited. together with a slip bearing
the name and living group of the girl
who knitted the article, in the box
placed in the office for that purpose,
according to Pam Meyer, chairman.
