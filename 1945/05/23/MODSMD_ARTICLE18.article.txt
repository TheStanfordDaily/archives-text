# Keesing to Speak On 'Dependencies'
## 
### 
"Future of Postwar Dependencies"
will be the topic of Dr. Felix Kee
sing's speech for the Y.W.C.A. World
Affairs Group this Thursday at 4:30
p.m. in the "Y" rooms of the Wom
en's Clubhouse, announced Carol
Kellogg, chairman.
Dr. Keesing, professor of anthro
pology, will discuss the wishes of
the smaller nations in Europe for
self-determination, and will attempt

Refreshments will be served at
4:15.
