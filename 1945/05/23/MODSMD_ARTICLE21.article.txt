# Juniors Plan City Skyline for Prom
## 
### 
Promising a panorama of San
Francisco at night with a skyline
outlined by the lights of the city,
the Junior Prom committee chair
men are busy planning decorations
for Saturday, June 2. Theme of the
dance is "Harbor Lights."
Anne Robinson is general chair
man of the junior dance. Heading
the decorations committee is Shir
ley Pettit. K. J. Supple is in charge
of tickets; Jean Richards, in charge
of arrangements; Lois Williams,
publicity; and Barbara Beardslee,
patrons.
