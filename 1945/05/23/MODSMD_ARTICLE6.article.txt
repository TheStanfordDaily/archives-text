# Phi Beta Kappa To Hear Dr. Hicks
## 
### 
The anual open meeting of the
Stanford chapter of Phi Beta Kappa
will be held Thursday, May 24, in
the Women's Clubhouse at 8 p.m.
The Phi Beta Kappa address will be
delivered by Dr. John D. Hicks,
Morrison Professor of American
History and Dean of the Graduate
Division at the University of Cali
fornia.
The subject of Dr. Hicks' address
will be "The American Diplomatic
Revolution." Profcsso rHicks is a
distinguished historian who has held
academic and administrative posi
tions at several leading American
universities. He is the author of five
well-known works and of numerous
articles in the field of American
history.
Following the address, which is
open to the public, an informal re
ception honoring Professor Hicks
and the newly elected members of
Phi Beta Kappa will be given by the
officers and members of the Stan
ford chapter, according to an an
nouncement made by Professor
Thomas S. Barclay, secretary of the
chapter.
