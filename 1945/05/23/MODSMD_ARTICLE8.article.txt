# Canoe Removal Time Extended to Thursday
## 
### 
The time for removal of canoes
from the Boathouse has been ex
tended until Thursday. Boats may
be removed any time between 2
and 3 p.m., provided all accounts
have been settled at the A.S.S.U.
Boats may be stored at the
Boathouse for one dollar per
month during the closed season,
according to Jim Emerson, stu
dent manager.
