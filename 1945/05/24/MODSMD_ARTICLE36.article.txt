# Ravens at Tyler 'Fall' Saturday
## 
### 
Weird lighting, black ravens, and
other features of Edgar Allen Poe
tales will decorate Tyler Hall Sat
urday from 9 to 1 during its open
to-the-campus dance.
Dancers will wear sport clothes
and music will be played from rec
ords. If the weather is good, stu
dents can dance outside as well as
inside, according to dance and so
cial chairman Bob Blum.
Theme of the dance will be "The
Fall of the House of Tyler." Cob
webs will be all over the rooms and
fluorescent lighting and paint will
also be features. Hot cider will be
served.
John Bell and Klaas 80l are in
charge of lighting.
