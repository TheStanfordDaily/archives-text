# All Belgian Sweaters Must Be In May 28
## 
### 
Farm women must turn in all
sweaters being knitted for Belgian
War Relief at the War Board Office
on or before Monday, May 28, ac
cording to Pain Meyer, chairman
of knitting committee.
To each sweater must be firmly
attached a slip bearing the name of
the girl who completed the article
and the name of her living group,
said Miss Meyer.
