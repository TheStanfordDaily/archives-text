# Rogers, Astaire Star In 'Top Hat' Sunday
## 
### 
Ginger Rogers and Fred Astaire
will be shown in their old dancing
partnership Sunday when Wardlaw
Winners presents "Top Hat" in the
Memorial Auditorium at 7:30 p.m.
Other actors in the film are Edward
Everett Horton and Helen Brod
crick. Also to be shown is the last
serial of "The Phantom Empire,"
starring Gene Autry.
