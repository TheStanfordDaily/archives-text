# Evans To Discuss History, Religion
## 
### 
Dr. Matthew Evans, instructor on
Western Civ, will lead a discussion
on "Christianity and the Study of
History" at an open meeting of the
Wesley Club Sunday evening. The
meeting will be held at 7:30 p.m. in
the Faculty Men's Clubhouse.
All former or present students of
Dr. Evans and any other interested
people are invited to attend this
meeting. A lively discussion period
is expected, according to A 1 Strain,
secretary of the organiaztion. Re
freshments will be served.
