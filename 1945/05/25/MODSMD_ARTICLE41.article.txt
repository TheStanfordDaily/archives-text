# 'Recovery of America' To Be Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
"The Recovery of America" will
be Dr. D. Elton Trueblood's subject
Sunday at the Memorial Church
service at 11 a.m. The University
Choir will provide the music.
Dr. Warren D. Allen, University
organist, will play an organ recital
at 4 on Sunday in the Memorial
Church to which all are invited.
