# Grade Envelopes Go On Sale June 4
## 
### 
All students may receive their
grades by mail this summer, if they
leave a self-addressed, stamped en
velope at the information window
of the Registrar's office. These en
velopes may be obtained any time
after June 4 for five cents. Proceeds
will go to the Stanford Convalescent
Home.
Those students who have com
pleted the work for their Bachelor's
degree may call for their grades on
Friday, June 15, at 1:15 p.m.
