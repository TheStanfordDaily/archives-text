# Dr. Allen to Give Memorial Recital Tomorrow at 4:15
## 
### 
In honor of the war dead, Dr.
Warren D. Allen will present the
last organ recital of the quarter in
Memorial Day services planned for
this afternoon at 4:15 in the Memo
rial Church. Dr. D. Elton True
blood will give the invocation and
benediction .
The organ recital will consist of
the following pieces: "Piecc Hero
iquc," by Cesar Franck; chorale
prelude, "We Cry to Thee," by
Bach; "Lamentation in D minor," by
Alex Guilmant; chorale - prelude,
"Hark, A Voice Saith 'All Are Mor
tal'," by Bach; "The Mariners'
Hymn," by J. B. Dykes; and
"Marche Heroique," by St.-Saens.
All students and faculty are in
vited to this program commemorat
ing Memorial Day.
The first recital for the summer
quarter will be on Sunday, June 24,
at 7:30 p.m. Thursday recitals will
be resumed on June 28 at 4:15 p.m.
The Sunday recitals for July and
August will all take place at 7:30
