# Twenty 1945 Quads Remain to Be Sold
## 
###

of the 200 additional copies or
dered earlier this quarter.
Those wishing to order one may
do so at the Quad olfict. There
will he no more sales at the post
oflice, according to I'at Ackerson,
circulation manager.
