# TOVARICH TO BE GIVEN
## 
###

Jose State College is presenting De
val's famous "Tovarich" on June 1,
at 8:30 p.m., in the Montgomery
Theater of the Civic Auditorium.
The tickets will be 65 cents for stu
dents and $1 for adults, including
tax.
