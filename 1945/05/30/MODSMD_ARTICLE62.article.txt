# Australians Visit Campus Institute
## 
### 
The Food Research Institute at
Stanford had two visitors from the
United Nations Conference this
week. On Sunday afternoon Eu
gene Staley, a member of the Inter
national Secretariat of UNCIO and
an executive of the Foreign Service
Educational Foundation, met with
senior staff members of the Food
Research Institute.
J. B. Brigden, financial counselor
to the Australian legation in Wash
ington, D.C., and Australian dele
gate to the Confernece, visited the
Institute on Monday.
