# Jones to Speak At Los Angeles
## 
### 
Professor Eliot Jones left yester
day for Los Angeles to participate
in a five-day session of the Insti
tute on Community Development,
conducted by the University of Cal
ifornia at Los Angeles.
At the Thursday session, which
will be devoted to transportation,
Dr. Jones will give an address on
"Transportation and Community
Development" in the evening, and
in the afternoon will conduct a pan
el on the transportation situation of
the Los Angeles metropolitan area.
