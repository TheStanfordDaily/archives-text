# Lagunita Leaders Hold Discussion
## 
### 
At Lagunita the post - election
leadership meeting of all new and
old Court and casa officers was held
last night under the chairmanship
of Barbara Seaver.
Speakers were Pam Strathairn,
outgoing chairman of Women's Con
ference, who explained the relation
ship of the Court to the campus;
Joey Nell Ryan, outgoing president
of Lagunita, who discussed activities
within the Court; and Alice Webb,
outgoing president of Casa Euca
lypto, who discussed casa activities.
