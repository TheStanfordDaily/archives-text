# June 7 to Be Last Day to Give Blood
## 
### 
Thursday, June 7, will be the last
Blood Bank day of spring quarter,
according to Chairman Coot Coffin.
The sign-up sheet for appointments
will be posted on the door of the
War Board office until noon of next
Monday.
The Red Cross station wagon will
leave engineering corner 20 minutes
before each scheduled appointment
in order to provide transportation
for the individual donors.
