# Apply for Ration Books June 11-13
## 
### 
Students may obtain their number
four ration books for vise after the
end of spring quarter by applying
in person at the booth in the lobby
of the Ad Building Moandy, June
11. from 1 to 5; Tuesday, June 12,
from 9 to 5; and Wednesday, June
13, from 9 to 5.
Students who are returning for
summer quarter and who eat or
hash in a University dining hall must
file ration books on the day that
room and board payment is due.
