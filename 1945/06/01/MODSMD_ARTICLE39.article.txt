# Last Blood Bank Day Next Thursday
## 
### 
Stanford's last Blood Bank day of
spring quarter will be next Thurs
day, June 7. Studunts may make ap
pointments until noon on Monday
at which time the sign-up sheet will
be taken down from the door of the
War Board office, according to
Chairman Coot Coffin.
Transportation for each donor will
be provided by the Red Cross station
wagon which is to leave Engineering
corner 20 minutes before each
scheduled appointment.
