# 'Y' MEETING THURSDAY
## 
### 
At a joint meeting of the Y.W.C.A.
Advisory Committee and Cabinet
next Thursday the yearly Y.W.C.A.
report will be read and discussed,
announced Miss Pat Davidson, "Y"
secretary.
The meeting will be at 10 a.m. in
the "Y" rooms of the Women's
Blubhouse, and coffee will be
served.
