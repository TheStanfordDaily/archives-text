# Berg Named Chief Of Business Staff
## 
### 
Paul Berg has been appointed
business manager of the Daily for
the summer quarter. He has been
working on the Daily since he came
here last October from his home in
Rockford, 111.
He was editor of the annual in
high school and worked for three
years on the school paper.
Berg has been a house officer in
Buchanan and is now serving as
sponsor in Toyon.
