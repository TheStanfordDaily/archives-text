# Greninger Given G E. Appointment
## 
### 
Dr. A. B. Greninger, Stanford '28,
has been appointed metallurgist of
General Electric's chemical depart
ment.
Dr. Greninger received his grade
and high school education in Chile
where his father has been manager
of a copper company. For two years
after graduating from Stanford with
two degrees in engineering he
worked with his father in Chile, re
turning to the United States in 1933
to take up advanced study in metal
lurgy at Harvard, thereby earning
his Doctor of Science degree.
Until early 1941 he was assistant
professor of metallurgy at Har
vard. and then joined General Elec
tric to do metallurgical work in the
company's lamp department at
Cleveland.
