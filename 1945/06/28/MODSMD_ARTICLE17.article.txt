# Ex-Quad Editor Becomes Author
## 
### 
Earl J. Hadley, Stanford '08. who
edited both the Chaparral and the
Quad, has written "The Magic Pow
der," a history of the cement indus
try and the Universal Atlas Cement
Company.
Mr. Hadley's book is designed to
illustrate the range of contacts of the
modern architect and engineer with
the whole field of business. It refers
particularly to the Stanford estate
and to the University Museum in
the Earthquake.
