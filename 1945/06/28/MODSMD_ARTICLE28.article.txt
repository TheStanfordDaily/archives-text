# New Farm Gifts Total $3,200,000
## 
### 
Stanford has received 2800 gifts
in the nine past months, represent
ing a value of 53.200.000. This is a
greater total of gifts than in any
previous year since the University
was founded. Among them are a
number of memorials, many of them
established in honor of Stanford
alumni who have lost their lives in
the war.
The nine-month total includes
$1,761,000 for general endowment;
$199,000 for the School of Medicine;
$192,000 for scholarships; $122,000 for
the Hoover Library; and $100,000 for
the School of Humanities.
