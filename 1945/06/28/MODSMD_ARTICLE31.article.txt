# TYLER FIRESIDE
## 
### 
Tyler, high in the hills and fa
mous for firesides, plans its first
closed fireside of the quarter to
morrow night at 8:30 with genial
new Tylermen pouring the cider
and spinning the records.
For the benefit of new students,
firesides is farm language for in
formal parties — both open and
closed—held almost weekly at vari
ous men's halls on the campus.
Until election of Tyler officers
next week. Resident Assistant Sid
ney Ross is making arrangements
for Friday's fireside.
