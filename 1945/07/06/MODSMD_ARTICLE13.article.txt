# Pool in Gym Open Sundays From 3-5
## 
### 
The pool in the women's gym will
be open to women students, the
faculty and their families, and men
students who are guests of women
students or faculty, on Sundays from
3 to 5.
Faculty children will be allowed
in the pool only from 3 to 4.
An admission price of 15 cents
will be charged all those except
women r.tudents.
