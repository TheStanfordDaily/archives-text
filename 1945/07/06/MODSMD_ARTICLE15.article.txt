# Donat, Landi Star In-Sunday Movie
## 
### 
Robert Donat and Elissa Landi are
the co-stars in "The Count of Monte
Cristo," first summer offering of the
regular Sunday Series of movies
given in Memorial Auditorium on.
alternate Sundays at 7:30 p. m.
Betty Ann Porter, in charge of
Porter's Pics, has arranged for two
episodes of the new serial, '"King of
the Wild" to be shown with the
full-length feature.
Boris Karloff will be seen as a
half man-half ape character in the
jungle setting of "King of the Wild."
Admission is 20 cents.
