# New Officers Preside At 'Dames' Meeting
## 
### 
New officers of the Stanford
Dames Society presided at the sec
ond meeting of the quarter held yes
terday in the Women's Clubhouse.
The new officers, selected at the
June meeting are Mrs. Everett
Stone, president; Mrs. George Moore,
vice president; Mrs. Antone Romney,
secretary, and Mrs. R. A. Bolomey,
treasurer.
