# Former Varsity Player Commissioned Ensign
## 
### 
Robert J. Cardinal, who during his
three years at Stanford was a mem
ber of the varsity golf, basketball
and baseball teams, was recently
commissioned an Ensign in the
Naval Reserve and designated a
Naval Aviator at the Training Bases,
Pensacola, Fla.
Ensign Cardinal is the son of Mr.
and Mrs. Emile Jean Cardinal of
San Francisco. Pie will be ordered
to duty either at an instructor's
school for further training or at an
operational base.
