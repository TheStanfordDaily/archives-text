# 'Military Nerves': Topic for Series
## 
### 
"Nerves and the Military," will
be the topic discussed by Joseph
Catton, M. P., at the Tuesday Even
ing Series, at 8 p. m., in the audi
torium of Memorial Hall.
Dr. Catton, a graduate of the Uni
versity of California Medical School,
has been a member of the Stanford
Medical School faculty since 1919.
As a major in the first World War
and as a former head of the Psy
chiatric Induction Center, No. 1, in
San Francisco, Dr. Catton has been
in constant touch with the veterans
of World War I and World War 11.
