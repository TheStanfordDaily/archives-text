# Soloist Featured In Sunday Recital
## 
### 
Joseph Tissier, tenor soloist, will
assist Dr. Warren D. Allen in Sun
day's organ recital at 7:30 p. m. in
Memorial Church.
The program will include the
recitative and aria from Haydn's
''Creation," an aria from Handel's
"Judas Maccabaeus," music based
on plainsong melodies and British-
American tunes, and chorale-pre
ludes on Lutheran melodies.
