# Bach to be Played In Sunday Recital
## 
### 
Music of Johann Sebastian Bach
is to be featured at the organ recital
given by Warren D. Allen. Uni
versity organist, Sunday at 7:30 p.m.
at the Memorial Church.
The program includes: a chorale
prelude. 'I Believe in One God,"
Siciiano in G Minor, "Come Sweet
Death," Toccata in F major. Prelude
in B flat minor, and Passacaglia and
Thema fugatum in C minor.
