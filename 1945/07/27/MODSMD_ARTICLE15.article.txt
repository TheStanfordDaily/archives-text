# GLOBES AT BOOKSTORE
## 
### 
A number of new Rand McNally,
hand made, 12 inch globes have been
received by the book store. In three
styles, the table type globes come in
blue and antique bronze and range
in price from $10.50 to $18.25. Each
globe is supplied with a corrective
service for a revision after final
boundary settlements have been
made.
