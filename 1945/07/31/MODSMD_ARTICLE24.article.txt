# Engineering Society Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
The students chapter of the Amer
ican Society of Civil Engineers will
hold a social meeting in the Faculty
Clubhouse tonight at 7:30.
Lieut. Com. H. N. Wallin, guest
speaker for the evening, will show
a sound-motion picture entitled "The
Harbor Moves to France."
All members of the Society, as
well as the CATS, are urged to
attend.
