# Spanish Films Will Show on Thursday
## 
### 
Four short Spanish American mo
tion pictures will be shown Thursday
at 8:00 p. m. in Cubberley Audi
torium.
The movie, part of which was
taken by Disney during his trip in
Latin America, was shown here last
summer in conjunction with the
Latin American Workshop and
proved so popular that it is being a
repeat performance this year, ac
cording to Grace Knopp, publicity
chairman of the Workshop.
