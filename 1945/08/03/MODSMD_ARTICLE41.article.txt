# GI Dance Tonight
## 
### 
Walt Fleher and his "Dance With
out a Care" GI swing band return
to the pavilion tonight to furnish
music for this week's regular Friday
night dance.
Bert Feinberg and Bill Helfand,
managers of the band, report: "Walt
and his boys have been working hard
and are ready to bring you that
same smooth playing they've fur
nished in the past."
-The dance, admission free and
open to the whole campus, will be
both date and stag. It will be held
from 8:30 p.m. to 11:30 p.m.
Refreshments will be furnished by
the Palo \lto Red Cross.
