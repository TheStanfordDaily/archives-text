# Recital Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen will give an
organ recital Sunday at 7:30 p.m.
in Memorial Church. The program
will include Handel's "Thanks Be
to Thee," the Kyrie Eleison by Karg-
Elert, selections from the scores of
Wagner, the Fantasia on "The
Garden Hymn" by Shepherd, and
Bach's Fantasia and Fugue in C
minor.
