# Albeneri Trio Gives Concert Tonight
## 
### 
The Albeneri Trio will give its
second in a series of four concerts
in Memorial Hall at 8 p.m. today
with a program including Mozart's
Trio in E Major, K. 542; Fame's
Trio in D Minor, Op. 120; and Trio
in B Flat Major, Op. 99 by Schubert.
Alexander Schneider, violinist;
Benar Heifetz, cellist; and Erich
Kahn, pianist are trio members.
