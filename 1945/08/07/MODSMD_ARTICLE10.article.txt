# Bids Go on Sale For Toyon Formal
## 
### 
"Anchors Aweigh" will be the
theme of the Toyon Formal, to be
held August 18 in the Toyon lobby.
Tickets, which went on sale yes
terday to Toyon men, will be avail
able to the general public for $2.00
at the A.S.S.U. office on Thursday.
Noel Thomas and his 10 piece
band will play for the dance, which
will be semi-formal.
