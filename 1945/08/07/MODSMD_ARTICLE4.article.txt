By United Press
A truckload of California fruits and vegetables, picked in the "nation's
salad bowl" is loaded at Salinas, Calif., on this American Airlines,
Consolidated Vultee Model 39, cargo plane, to be carried overnight to
markets east of the Mississippi. -Payload of the plane is 18,500
pounds—more than 9 tons—permitting the largest tonnage of fresh
fruits and vegetables ever flown by a commercial air cargo plane.
