# IN THE SERVICE
## 
### 
Donald E. Wood has completed
his work at the Platoon Commanders
school. Quantico, Virginia, and has
been commissioned a second lieu
tenant in the Marine Corps.
The new officer attended Stanford
University and the University of
California.
Lt. Wood will now be assigned to
an infantry unit for additional train
ing or will be sent to a specialist

school. He enlisted in the Marines
in November, 1942.
Edgar "It" Taylor recently was
commissioned a second lieutenant in
the United States Marines.
Lieutenant Taylor attended Stan
ford and the University of Wash
ington. He played football two years ;
and is a member of Phi Kappa Psi |
fraternity.
