# Bach on Program Of Organ Recital
## 
### 
Cesar Franck's Chorale in A minor
will start Dr. Warren D. Allen's
organ recital Sunday at 7:30 p.m.
in Memorial Church.
Dr. Allen will be assisted by Mrs.
Marjorie Benson Dickinson in "My
Heart Ever Faithful" by Bach and
O'Hara's "One World." The pro
gram aslo includes the D Minor
sonata of Guilmant and the sin
fonia "I Stand Before the Gate of
Heaven" by Bach.
