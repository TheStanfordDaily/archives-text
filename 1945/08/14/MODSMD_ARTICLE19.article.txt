# Hoover to Accept Set of Records
## 
### 
Former President Herbert Hoover
will accept a set of recordings of the
plenary sessions at the San Fran
cisco Conference in the Little Theatre
Thursday at 10 a. m.
The presentation will be made by
John W. Elwood, manager of Sta
tion KPO after his lecture to stu
dents attending the Radio Institute.
Mr. Elwood and Mrs. Inez G. Rich
ardson, co-director of the Stanford-
KPO Institute, are lecturers in the
weekly course on Broadcasting in
the United States, a survey course
in broadcasting history.
The recordings were made of the
public sessions by KPO engineers
They include speeches in English
and some translations into French
and Russian,
