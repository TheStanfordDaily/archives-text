# 'Mayerling' Picked For Sunday Show
## 
### 
"Mayerling," the French film with
Charles Boyer and Danielle Dar
rieux, is Porter's Pix selection for
Sunday. It will be shown at 7:30
p. m. in Memorial Auditorium, to
gether with the next two chapters
of "King of the Wild," the jungle
serial with half-ape, half-man
Karloff.
Made in Boyer's pre-Hollywood
days and one of the best known
French productions, "Mayerling"
will carry English subtitles.
