# Honorary Society Initiates Beadle
## 
### 
Dr. George W. Beadle, of the
biology department, has been elected
to membership in the American
Philosophical Society, the oldest
learned society in the country.
Dr. Beadle, who was honored for
distinguished research in the field of
genet ics, was one of four Americans
chosen from the field of the geo
logical and biological sciences.
Founded by Benjamin Franklin in
1743, the Society has a resident and
foreign membership limited to 560
"scholars of distinction."
