# Allen Gives Organ Program at 4:15
## 
### 
Dr. Warren D. Allen, of the music
department, will give an organ re
cital today at 4:15 p. m. in Memorial
Church.
Compositions included in the pro
gram are Bach's "Prelude in C
Minor," the "Berceuse Heroique" by
Debussy, Lemare's "Victory March,"
and three compositions by Franck.
The organ recital will be repeated
Sunday at 7 p. m.
