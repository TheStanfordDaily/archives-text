# Kennedy Will Give Service
## 
### 
Dr. Gerald Kennedy, one of the
younger leaders of Christian forces
in America, will give the last sermon
of the quarter on Sunday in Me
morial Church.
Dr. Kennedy is from Lincoln, Ne
braska, and will speak in keeping
with the tradition of having the
last service of the quarter conducted
by a visiting minister.
Holy Communion will be cele
brated after the regular service. This
ceremony is held in the Chapel at
stated intervals throughout the year
in response to a request made in the
will of Mrs. Stanford.
