# Eucalypto Wins Volleyball Match
## 
### 
Eucalypto volleyball team won the
Roble-Lagunita League champion
ship yesterday in a two to one game
victory over the P.T.'s.
The first game was won by the
P.T. .3 with a score of 15 to 4, while
the Eucalypto team won the remain
ing two games with scores of 15 to
10 and 15 to 8. according to score
keeper Virginia Law.
A box of candy was awarded to
the winning team.
Willie Harmon acted as captain of
the P.T. team, and Dickie Shainwald,
Lagunita league representative,
captained the Eucalypto team.
