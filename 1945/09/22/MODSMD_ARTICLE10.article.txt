# Farm Handbook Available To All
## 
### 
For the first time in many years
the Stanford Handbook, known as
the "Frosh Bible," will be available
to old students as well as incoming
freshmen.
The 1945-46 edition of the Hand
book, outlining Farm history, cus
toms, and traditions, has been com
pletely re-written by Nada Skid
mote, editor, and Beth Mac Vicar,
associate editor.
Announcement of distribution to
old students will be made later.
