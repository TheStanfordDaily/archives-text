# Farm Draws Diverse Group
## 
### 
Stanford's registration Monday will
claim seven women from diverse
parts of the world including four
recently liberated from the Santo
Tomas prison camp in Manila.
Planning to further their educa
tion at the Farm are Elizabeth and
Shirley Silen, Jacquelyn Flannery,
and Evelyn Wythe who were among
those liberated by United States
troops from Santo Tomas last spring.
Entering from India is Ruth Suth
erland and from another part of the
British Empire two Canadian sisters,
Barbara and Miriam Weston, who
experienced the Blitz while work
ing in wartime London, arc plan
ning to register.
