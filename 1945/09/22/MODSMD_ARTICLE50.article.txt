# Nine Appointed To Sports Board
## 
### 
The Board of Athletic Control,
Stanford's sports governors, has an
nounced nine appointments for the
coming school year.
Student members of the B.A.C.
are Bruce Jessup, Harry Mason, and
Bob Kennedy, A.S.S.U. president.
Alumni members are D. W. Burbank,
San Francisco attorney, Lewis Vin
centi, former basketball star and now
a Pasadena attorney, and Dr. Lloyd
Reynolds, a San Francisco surgeon.
The three faculty members of the
board are Professor Lawrence
Thomas, Dr. Owen Anderson, and
Dean John Stalnaker.
