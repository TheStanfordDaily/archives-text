# Groups Have New Directors
## 
### 
Two of Stanford's larger women's
residences have new directors and
three of the smaller houses have new
housemothers this quarter, accord
ing to the Dean of Women's office.
Mrs. Lucie Lataillade, director of
Casa Ventura two years ago and at
Pomona College last year, is the new
director of Lagunita Court.
Branner's new director is Mrs.
Elizabeth Hartshorn.
Mrs. Kathleen Spriggs, formerly
at the University of Washington, is
housemother at Durand.
Mrs. Winifred Legg, former direc
tor of Maxinkuckee vacation club in
Culver, Indiana, is housemother at
Russell.
Mrs. Walter J. East comes from
DePauw University in Indiana to
act as housemother for Hurlburt
House.
Directors and housemothers re
taining their positions are:
Miss Rosamond Clarke at Roble;
Miss Mary Jane Donaldson at Un
ion residence; and Mrs. Ida Huener
yager at Casa Ventura.
Mrs. F. A. Maycumber at Cubber
ley; Mrs. C. P. Morgan at Roth;
Mrs. C. F. Osborn at Jordan; Mrs.
Robert Reynolds at Storey; Mrs.
Harry T. Upshaw at Lathrop; Mrs.
Theress Wenzlick at the Chalet.
