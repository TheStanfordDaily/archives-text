# Pacific Tennis Gets First Upset
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Sept. 24.—(UP)
—Lt. Fred Thompson, St. Louis, Mo.,
engineered the first major upset of
the 56th Annual Pacific Coast tennis
championships here today when he
ousted Hank Pfister, San Francisco,
1944 Pacific Coast service champion,
4-6, 6-2, 6-3, from the men's singles
division.

Lome Main, 15-year-old school
boy from Vancouver, B. C., featured
the day's opening matches with his
easy 6-0, 6-0 win in the boys' singles
division. Using a two-handed style
for all shots, including backhand,
Main was favored to win the boys'
title despite the fact he is un
heeded.
