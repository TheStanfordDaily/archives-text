# Sunday Deadline Set For Concert Tickets
## 
### 
All renewals for season tickets
to this season's Concert Series
should be in the mail by Sunday,
according to Jo Glasson, Concert
Manager. New reservations should
be made early, Miss Glasson said,
to assure a good selection of seats
and the advantage of season tickets
: - ates.
There will be four concerts this
year featuring well-known artists,
most of whom have never appeared
at Stanford before.
The series will open November 1
with the appearance of Joseph
Szigeti, violinist.
Pierre Monteux will conduct the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra
at the next program, February 6.
Luboshutz and Nemenoff, duo
pianists, will appear March 11; and
Rise Stevens, Metropolitan Opera
Company soprano will sing May 20.
The San Francisco Symphony
played for the 1939-40 series. In this
first appearance since the war, any
suggestions for a program will be
welcomed at the Concert Series
office.
Requests for tickets may be mailed
to the Concert Series, Box 1151,

Stanford. Single tickets will be
available October 7.
Annette Kirkman and LaVerne
O'Neil are assistant concert man
agers.
Season tickets are from $5 to $7.20,
depending upon the seats.


PIERRE MONTEUX
