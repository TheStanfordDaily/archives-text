# Injured Student Starts Recovery
## 
### 
Charles Clifford, first-year Stan
ford med student who yesterday was
reported suffering from a brain con
cussion. is now "resting well" and
in "excellent condition," according
to Palo Alto Hospital authorities.
Clifford was taken to the hospital
Monday evening, following an auto
accident on Foothill Road.
Since it was feared that the con
cussion might result in a lapse of
memory, Clifford was calling for
friends to visit him at the Palo Alto
Hospital in hopes that they could
help him recall details about him
self.
