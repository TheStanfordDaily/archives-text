# FLEET WILL BE IN
## 
### 
PEARL HARBOR, Sept. 26.—(UP)
—Adm. William F. Halsey's U.S.
3rd Fleet will arrive at West Coast
ports from Washington to southern
California for Navy Day celebrations
on October 27, the Navy announced
today.
The fleet will comprise 54 ships
led by Plalsey himself, with his flag
aboard the battleship South Dakota.
