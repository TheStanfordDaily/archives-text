# C.O.P. Injury Riddled
## 
### 
STOCKTON, Calif., Sept. 26.—
(UP).—Only 23 members today re
mained in playing condition on
Coach Amos Alonzo Stagg's College
of Pacific football squad as the Tig
ers prepared to meet St. Mary's
Pre-Flight in the first game of the
season Friday night.
The COP club has played four
games, lost three and tied one so
far this season. It will be the first
tilt of the year for the Airdevils.
