# Lossie Bear Captain
## 
### 
BERKELEY, Calif., Sept. 26.—
(UP). —Coach Buck Shaw today an
nounced Bob Lossie, University of
California center, who played 56
minutes last Saturday in the St.
Mary's game, will captain the
Bears when they tangle with U.S.C.
here Saturday.
All injured players, except Webb
Jessup, end, will be back in the
line-up. The first and second teams
scrimmaged today, stressing passing
defense and offensive blocking. Keith
Jackson, second string quarterback,
may start with the varsity in the
U.S.C. game, Shaw said.
The Bear coach, taking a pessi
mistic attitude, added: "The odds
are a long ways from even."
