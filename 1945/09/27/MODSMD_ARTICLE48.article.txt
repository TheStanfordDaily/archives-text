# Wilson Leads Oregon
## 
### 
EUGENE. Ore., Sept. 26.—(UP).
—Elliott Wilson, two-year letter
man and a veteran of Marine cam
paigns in the South Pacific, was se
lected today to captain the Uni
versity of Oregon against Washing
ton in Seattle Saturday.
Jake Leicht, newly-arrived half
back, failed to arrive in time for
scrimmage as expected.
