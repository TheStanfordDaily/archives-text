# WARNING TO SPAIN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Sept.. 26.—(UP).
—The Spanish people were warned
today that they eannot expect any
help from the United States until
they rid themselves ol' Generalissimo
Francisco Franco and his Fascist
regime.
The State Department also re
vealed that the policy of black ball
ing the Franco government from
the family of nations was set forth
by the late President Roosevelt last
March. He pledged then also that
"our victory over Germany will car
ry with it the extermination of
Nazi and similar ideologies."


Generalissimo
Francisco Franco
