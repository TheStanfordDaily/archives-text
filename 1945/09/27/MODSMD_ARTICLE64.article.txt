# Six ERC's Move To Minnesota U.
## 
### 
Trading Stanford's balmy climate
for a Minnesota winter, six ERC's
will leave Sunday, transferring to
the ASTU at the University of Min
nesota for Japanese Area and Lan
guage study.
In order to qualify for Japanese
Area and Language these men had
to have a minimum AGCT score of
130 and at least two more terms in
the ASTRP before reporting for
active duty.
Men being transferi'ed are: Pvts.
Norman D. Bradshaw, Morris S.
Bureloff, Thomas R. Couzens, Rob
ert D. Davies, Saul Halbstein, and
John T. Kane.
