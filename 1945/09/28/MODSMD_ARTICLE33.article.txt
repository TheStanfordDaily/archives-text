# Harpist To Play At Chapel Sunday
## 
### 
The string section of the Univers
ity Orchestra, including the two
harpists, Miss Joanne Johnson and
Miss Verda Mae Griesinger. will
participate in Sunday morning serv
ices. They will accompany the
Choir on Handel's anthem "Thanks
Be to Thee" and assist on the hymns.
The harps, however, will remain
silent when the orchestra plays the
processional March "Cornelius" by
Mendelssohn.
