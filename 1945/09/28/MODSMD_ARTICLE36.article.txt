# Poetry Anthology Asks Manuscripts
## 
### 
Announcing that November 6 is
the last date for submitting manu
scripts for the Annual Anthology of
College Poetry, the National Poetry
Associations has invited Stanford
students to send in entries.
Each work must be written on one
side of a single sheet and must bear
the author's name, home address,
and college. Manuscripts should be
sent to the National Poetry Asso
ciation, 3210 Selby Avenue, Los
Angeles 34, Calif.
