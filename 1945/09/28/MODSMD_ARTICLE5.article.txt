# Clinic To Treat Speech Defects
## 
### 
The Speech Clinic, sponsored by
the Speech and Drama Department
and under the direction of Professor
Virgil Anderson, is beginning its
14th year of free service for stu
dents with all types of speech de
fects.
Work to correct defects is done
individually without credit and on
an independent basis. In the past
the clinic has helped men lose their
juvenile voices and has corrected
severe cases of stuttering.
Those interested should call Pro
fessor Anderson on Extension 588
or 590. or see him in room 204 A in
the back of the Memorial Audito
rium.
