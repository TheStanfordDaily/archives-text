# BAVARIAN TROUBLE
## 
### 
MUNICH, Sept. 30 (UP).—Wil
helm Hoegner, newly appointed
minister-president of Bavaria, as
serted today that bands of armed
marauders, many of them Germans
from other areas of the former
Reich, have been carrying off newly
harvested crops.
