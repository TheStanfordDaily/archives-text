# Union Elects Margy Power Unit Secretary
## 
### 
Margy Power was elected secre
tary of Union Unit at the election
last week. According to house
rules, the secretary must be a
sophomore and so the election was
delayed until the new sophomores
had moved into the Unit.
Tonight, Union Residence will
hold another election for secretary
of the Residence. As soon as the
new secretary is in office, plans will
begin for Union's social events of
fall quarter.
The other officers of the Unit are:
President Anita Zimmerman, Vicc-
Presiclent Annette Gattuccio, Treas
urer Betty Reiss, Head Sponsor
Jacqueline Jardine, and Chairman
of Council Representatives Joan
LcMay.
