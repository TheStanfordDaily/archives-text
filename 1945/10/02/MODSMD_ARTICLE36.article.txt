# ASTU Resumes Friday Dances
## 
### 
The Army will play host to the
Navy and civilians Friday night at
the Pavilion when the first Army
sponsored dance of the quarter will
be held.
Sandy Wilbourne, chairman for the
dance, says, "We want to see a lot
of sport jackets and Navy blues
there Friday night, but what we
really want to see is a lot of
skirts.'*
This dance. like all Army
dances, is admission free and both
date and stag.
Time for the dance is from 8:30
to 11:30; music will be furnished
by Walt Fleher's "Dance Without
a Care" collection of records.
Refreshments will be served by
the Rtd Cross.
