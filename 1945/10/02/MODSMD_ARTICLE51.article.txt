# YWCA Drive Starts Today
## 
### 
Representatives from all women's
living groups for the Y.W.C.A. mem
bership drive are to meet today at
1 p.m. at the Y.W.C.A. clubroom.
Those who are unable to attend
should send a substitute.
The membership drive begins to
day in all women's living groups
and will continue until Friday. The
membership fee is $1 and the drive
will be run on a voluntary basis.
One girl in each living group will
be in charge.
Farm women have supported the
Y.W.C.A. for the past 53 years.
