# 'Bringing Up Baby' To Show Sunday
## 
### 
Patty Whittemore, head of the
Sunday Movie Series this fall, has
announced that "Bringing Up
Baby," starring Cary Grant and
Katherine Hepburn, will be the
feature this Sunday.
The sixth and seventh chapters
of the summer serial, "King of the
Wild" with Boris Karlolf, will also
be shown. The movie will be held
in the Memorial Auditorium, 7:30
p.m. Admission is 20 cents.
