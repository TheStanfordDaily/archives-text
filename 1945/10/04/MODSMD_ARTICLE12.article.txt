# Memorial Services For Driscoll Today
## 
### 
Memorial services for former
ASSU president Dick Driscoll,
who died Tuesday afternoon in
the Palo Alto hospital, will be
held at 4 p. m. this afternoon in
the Memorial Chapel. Dr. D.
Elton Trucblood will conduct the
services.
Driscoll died as the result of
an auto accident which occurred
early Saturday morning.
Excom is sending, in the name
of the Associated Students, a
spray of flowers to Driscoll's fu
neral which will be held Saturday
in Los Angeles.
