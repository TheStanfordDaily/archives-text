# Russia Requests Classics, Science
## 
### 
To help in the nation-wide
"Books for Russia" campaign, Stan
ford students and faculty members
have been asked to do their share
in helping to restock the plundered
libraries of the Soviet Union, the
campaign publicity office in Palo
Alto announced recently.
The Stanford Bookstore has been
established as an on-campus depot
to collect book donations, and a box
has been placed at the end of the
typewriter department to hold the
books, the Bookstore said.
President Donald B. Tresidder has
been named chairman of the North
ern California area during the fall
and winter drive. Books mainly de
sired are the "classics," and tech
nical and scientific works published
since 1926.
