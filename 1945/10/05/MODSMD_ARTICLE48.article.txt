# WAA Field Day To Be Next Week
## 
### 
The WAA Field Day will be held
next Friday and not today as prev
iously announced in the Daily. The
program for the affair will include
swimming, volleyball, perhaps base
ball, and movies of Stanford.
The Field Day is to welcome all
new women students, freshmen and
transfers, and will be held in the
Women's Gymnasium. More infor
mation concerning the activities
will be disclosed next week.
