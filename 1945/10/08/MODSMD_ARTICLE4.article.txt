# Collector Thorpe Guest of Hot Jazz
## 
### 
Willie Thorpe, one of the Bay
Area's top collectors of jazz records,
will be guest collector at a meeting
of the Hot Jazz Society tonight at
8 in the 'Y' rooms of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Mr. Thorpe is a quitarist and has
played in Louis Armstrong's and
Jimmy Dorsey's orchestras.
Juanita Smith, president of the
Hot Jazz Society last year, will pre
sent the guest. All those interested
are invited to attend.
