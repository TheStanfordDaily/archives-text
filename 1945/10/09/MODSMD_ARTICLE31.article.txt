# Jardine Announces Music Room Hours
## 
### 
The Music Listening Room op
ened yesterday and will be open
from 1 to 6 p.m. Monday through
Friday; 1:30 to 3:30 p.m. Sunday;
and from 7 to 9 Wednesday and
Thursday evenings; according to
Jackie Jardine. chairman.
Everyone is invited to come to
the Music Listening Room, relax,
smoke cigarettes, and listen to
good music. Miss Jardine said.
