# Intra-murals Begin Tonight
## 
### 
WAA volleyball intra-murals will
begin tonight at 7 p.m. in the gym
nasium. Phyllis Leveen, in charge
of the arrangements, said that thirty
eight teams —that is a team from
almost every group on campus—
will participate.
The schedule is arranged as fol
lows:
Branner vs. Union at 7 p.m.
Aclelfa vs. 300 Club at 7 p.m.
Juniper vs. Eucalypto at,7 p.m.
PT's vs. Roble Rowdies at 8 p.m.
Ventura vs. Granada at 8 p.m.
Sim vs Chalet at 8 p.m.
Joy Girls vs. Madrona at 8:30 p.m.
