# Industry Absorbs Population Jump
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 9.—(UP).—
New industries will absorb south
ern California population growth,
Wholesale Grocer Nathan Cum
mings, of Chicago, predicted today.
The president of Consolidated
Grocers Corp., the nation's largest
wholesale grocery syndicate, said
the shutdown of war plants would
not kill industrial expansion.
Cummings, one-time Baltimore
shoe clerk who rose to head a 20
million dollar wholesale grocery
empire, leaves this week to inspect
Pacific coast subsidiaries of the or
ganization.
