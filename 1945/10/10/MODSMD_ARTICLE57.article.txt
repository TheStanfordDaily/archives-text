# HERO AWARDED MEDAL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 9.—(UP).—
Pfc. Clarence B. Craft, of Santa
Ana, Calif., who wiped out 30 Jap
anese in a spectacular one-man at
tack that succeeded where at bat
talion had failed, will be awarded
the Medal of Honor at the White
House Friday, the War Department
announced tonight.
