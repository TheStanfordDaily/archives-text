# Supporters May Accompany Peron
## 
### 
BUENOS AIRES, Oct. 10.—(UP).
—Six nationally prominent support
ers of Col. Juan D. Peron may join
him in retirement, it was reported
today as the cabinet met to con
sider the internal political situa
tion caused by the resignation of
Argentina's "strong man" as vice
president, war minister, and secre
tary of labor.
The cabinet met today to consider
the political situation and adjourned
without issuing a statement.
Political quarters reported that
Gen. Diego Mason, a leader in the
1943 revolt as the result of which
Peron and his fellow army men
attained power, was the leading
candidate for war minister.
