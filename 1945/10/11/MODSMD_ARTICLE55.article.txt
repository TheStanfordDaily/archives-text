# Stenn Impressive
## 
### 
ONTARIO, Cal., Oct. 10—.(UP).—
The Fourth Air Force Fliers spent
this morning trying to break up St.
Mary'k. Preflight pass plays, and
Coach Lt. Johnny Baker said he was
impressed with the defensive play
of End Nick SusoefT and Tackle
Paul Stenn.
The Fliers will work out here to
morrow morning, and go to San
Francisco by plane in the afternoon.
They play St. Mary's Preflight Sun
day at Kezar stadium.
