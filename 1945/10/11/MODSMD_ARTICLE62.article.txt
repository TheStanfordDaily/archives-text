# AST Echoes

### STAN DON 
As the smoke and haze clear
away, and the captain's bed is taken
off the fire escape, once again En
cinasylum rings with the happy
laughter of furlough-fatigued ERC
voices. After a week's rest for us
the hundred-odd best reasons for
a tougher basic training will grab
their slide rules and books and
throw themselves into their studies
and all the extra-curricular activi
ties, mopping, sweeping, etc.
Indeed, the ERC's were con
spicuous by their absence, espe
cially at the Friday night Army
Dance. In fact, men in general
were scarce. Roughs, are yon
afraid of too many co-eds wanting
to become Stanford women?
Caught after Ihe dance in an ex
hausted state while sipping milk
in the back room at Rosotti's,
Joey C. Heidelberg coyly quipped,
"Even I had my hands full!''
