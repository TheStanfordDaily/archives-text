# Okinawa Battered By 135-Mile Gale
## 
### 
MANILA, Friday, Oct. 12.—(UP).
—A death dealing typhoon, termed
the fworst in 20 years, battered
Okinawa Wednesday, leveling U.S.
army and naval installations and
leaving a toll of casualties that may
run into hundreds, it was reported
today.
Scores of sailors' bodies were re
ported washing up on the beaches
after furious 135-mile an hour
winds ripped small vessels from
their moorings and battered larger
craft.
Several correspondents and army
officers who reached Manila on the
first available transportation said
land installations, such as hospitals
and army camps, were levelled.
Seveial small navy craft arc missing,
they said.
