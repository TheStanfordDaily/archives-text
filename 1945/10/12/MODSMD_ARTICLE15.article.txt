# Klahoya Social, Sing 3:30 Today
## 
### 
Members of Klahoya will meet
today in the Women's Clubhouse
from 3:30 to 4:30 for a social. Sing
ing ol Stanford songs and getting
acquainted with one another will be
the chief activity.
Carol Boettcher will lead the sing
ing and has asked all members of
the off-campus women's organization
to bring their Frosh Bibles.
