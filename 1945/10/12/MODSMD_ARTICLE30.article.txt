# Bishop Speaks Here Sunday
## 
### 
Stanford's first professor of phi
losophy, Bishop Edward L. Parsons,
will be guest speaker at the Me
morial Church at eleven o'clock
Sunday morning.
Bishop Parsons is Bishop of Cali
fornia and was the chief force be
hind the building of Grace Cathed
ral on Nob Hill.
Mary Richards will read the Old
Testament Lesson and Evan William
will give the New Testament scrip
tures. Again there will be two
choirs, each of forty voices. The
chancel choir will be robed in the
new cardinal gowns.
