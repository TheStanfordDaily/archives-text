# Increase of Traffic Brings Raise in Fines
## 
### 
Fines for violation of campus
speed limits have been raised by
Chief of Police Gordon Davis in
agreement with members of Men's
Council. The increased number
of cars on campus has necessitated
this change, according to Coun
cil Secretary Tom Hood.
Fifty cents for every mile per
hour in excess of the speed limit
will be charged for the first of
fense, 75 cents for the second of
fense, and one dollar for the third.
