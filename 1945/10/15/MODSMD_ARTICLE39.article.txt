# Activities of Year Planned by WAA
## 
### 
Stanford will have a sports day
with San Jose and possibly San Ma
teo at the women's gym November
3, W.A.A. announced last night.
Sports for the day will be hockey,
swimming, archery, tennis, and
badminton.
All sports representatives and
representatives on W.A.A. will meet
at the Women's Clubhouse Wednes
day at 7 p.m. to discuss plans for the
year. All representatives are urged
to bring suggestions and ideas to the
meeting.
