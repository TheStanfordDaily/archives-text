# Steaks, Roast Beef On Buchanan Menus
## 
### 
Eight more places are opened
for diners at newly-formed Bu
chanan dining hall. Johnny Erlich,
table manager, announced last
night.
The dining hall, which opened
Monday noon apparently has lived
up to its aim of "good food or
bust!" Steaks greeted the men
Monday night and roast beef
Tuesday night.
Also places arc opened for diners
at Monroe hall which also opened
Monday noon under independent
management.
