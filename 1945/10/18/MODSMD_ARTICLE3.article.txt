# Farm Invited To Visit Roth
## 
### 
Roth House will welcome all Farm
students with dancing, bridge, and
refreshments at their open house this
Friday, according to Jo Glasson,
head of the arrangements commit
tee.
The doors will open to visitors
at 8:30 and the affair will last until
midnight. Thirty navy men have
been extended invitations also, al
though the entire campus is in
vited.
Assisting Jo with arrangements for
entertainment and refreshments,
which they promise to be quite dif
ferent, are Joanne Stepnek, Kathy
Moore, and Shirley Calder.
