# Late Leave Rules To Be Discussed
## 
### 
Recommendations for the much
abused 2 a.m. late leaves will be
drawn up at a meeting of the new
Standing Committee on Social Reg
ulations today at 1 p.m., according
to Beatty O'Hair, chairman.
The committee was appointed at
Women's Conference Monday. Mem
bers are Miss O'Hair, Eleanor Marks
representing Women's Council; and
Ruth Lcvison, Midge Hexter, Mar
garet Kehoe, and Sally Woodard
from Women's Conference.
