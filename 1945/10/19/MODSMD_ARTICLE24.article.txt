# Dean Announces Fees for Guests
## 
### 
Dr. Stanley L. Sharp, Dean of
Men's Residences, announced today
that, the regulation requiring pay
ment of $1 per night at ex-fraternity
houses by guests was still in effect.
"We have a moral and legal re
sponsibility to do this," Mr. Sharp
emphasized.
Men wishing to stay at Encina
should make arrangements not later
than 11 a. m. Charge at Encina is
75 cents to cover laundry fees.
