# AST Echoes
## 
### STAN DON 
It looks as if Lagunita's rep for
beautiful women attracted all the
Roughs Friday last. After a delight
ful evening spent with these gor
geous coeds, Joey C. Heidelberg re
marked, "Even the punch was
good!" The boys at Encina are still
talking over the swell time Lag
showed us. Highlight of the party
was the dancing in the patio with
all the ingredients for a moonlight
cocktail. This was the AST's' last
chance to wolf—in suntans, for as
you may have seen, the Army is
now in its itches.
Wo have received sundry an
swers to last week's list of pertinent
questions. As to how many curves
back from Rosotti's—Gertie queried,
"Oh, there are some?" But Port
land Pooh, still in a puzzle over the
number of counter seats in the Cel
lar, sent us her answer. "Well, as
far as I can see, counting by ones
there are roughly 35 or 40."
All of Eneinasylum is talking
about October 12 at 1700. As im
probable as it may sound, someone
was in front of Heidelberg in the
chow line. Don't ask us who it was,
but the boy deserves a brand new
E.B. medal.
Remember, girls, only 45 more
shopping days till V-N Day.
