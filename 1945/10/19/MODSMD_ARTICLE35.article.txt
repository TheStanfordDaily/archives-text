# Officers Elected By Chinese Club
## 
### 
Officers were elected and plans for
a picnic and dance were discussed
at a recent meeting of the Chinese
Club.
Presiding over the elections was
Duron Lee, summer quarter presi
dent of the club. The members voted
Gordon Chun to fill the presidency
this quarter while the offices of vice
president and secretary were filled
by Woon Chan and Lee Ling.
Plans for a closed picnic-dance to
be held tomorrow were discussed.
Also discussed was an open dance
that will be given November 10 at
the Women's Clubhouse. Admission
to this dance shall be free. The
social committee directed by Woon
Chan is in charge of the decorations,
refreshments and music.
