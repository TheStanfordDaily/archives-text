# Canterbury Club To Meet Sunday
## 
### 
The Stanford Canterbury Club of
Episcopal students will hold its Sun
day evening meeting at the parish
hall of All Saints' Episcopal Church,
Hamilton Avenue at Waverly Street,
in Palo Alto, at 6 o'clock.
Evan Williams will lead a discus
sion on church unity, and the Rev.
Vesper O. Ward, canon of Grace Ca
thedral, San Francisco, will visit the
group.
