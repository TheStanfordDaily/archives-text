# Truman Will Avoid Conscription Topic
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 21 (UP).—
Congressional sources said tonight
that they expect President Truman
to sidestep the explosive question of
compulsory military training in his
address to a joint session of Con
gress Tuesday.
