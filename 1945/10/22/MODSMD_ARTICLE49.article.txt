# Golf Tourney Sign-up List Closes Today
## 
### 
Sign-ups for the mixed two-ball
foursome end tonight, according to
Eleanor Brotman, president of the
Golf Club. The tournament will be
held Wednesday from 1 to 3 p.m. at
the golf course.
Students may sign up either with
or without a partner. Those who
sign up without, will be matched.
When a student signs up, he should
give his score on nine holes, Miss
Brotman said.
Both boys and girls may sign up
with either Miss Brotman or Mary
A 1 Smith at Lagunita, 22151; Jan
Tarbele, Roble, 5171; Mary Elizabeth
Hall. Ventura, 8731; Margaret Kehoe,
Russoll, 9070; and Beatty O'Hair,
Lathrop, 3703.
Navy boys who are playing in
the tournament should sign up with
their physical education instructors.
After the play-ofTs, prizes of golf
balls will be given out, and refresh
ments will be served in the club
house.
