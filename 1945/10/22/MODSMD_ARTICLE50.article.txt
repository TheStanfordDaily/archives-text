# Army Honors Cal As 925 Graduate
## 
### 
BERKELEY, Oct. 21 (UP).
While one of their professors and
the university received awards for
assisting in development of the
atomic bomb, 925 University of Cal
ifornia seniors participated in grad
uation exercises today.
After being handed diplomas by
President Robert G. Sproul, gradu
ates watched high-ranking Army
officers present a scroll honoring the
university and a pin commemorat
ing the work of Prof. Ernest O.
Lawrence, atomicreseacher.
