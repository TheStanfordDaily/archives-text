# BUS STRIKE SETTLED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 22 (UP).
—The Pacific Greyhound bus strike
which has hampered travel in seven
Western states has been settled,
Federal Conciliator Omar Hoskins
announced tonight, and company of
ficials said the drivers would resume
operations at once.
