# Embargo Proposed For Houston by I.C.C.
## 
### 
HOUSTON, Tex., Oct. 22 (UP)
An embargo against the strike
bound port of Houston was recom
mended today by C. B. Cox, service
agent for the Interstate Commerce
Commission.
An I.C.C. check showed more than
I 800 rail cars, some containing per
ishables, tied up in the area. An
embargo would halt shipping to the
docksides.
