# Roble Picture Deadline Nov. 7
## 
### 
Deadline for individual pictures
for Roble residents is November 7,
instead of Nov. 27 as reported prev
iously. Appointments may be made
any afternoon at the Quad office from
1 to 5.
Senior pictures deadline has been
extended till Friday, the staff has
announced. All pictures must be
taken by then to appear in the
special senior section.
The 1945 Quads should be out
within a week, Business Manager
Ann Lilienthal has announced. Dis
tribution will be made at the Quad
office. Workers are still needed to
give out the Quads.
