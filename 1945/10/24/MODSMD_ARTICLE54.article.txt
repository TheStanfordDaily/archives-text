# STEEL DEMANDS REJECTED
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH, Oct. 23 (UP).—
U.S. Steel Corporation rejected the
CIO United Steelworkers' demand
for a $2-a-day overall pay boost to
day and declared it could not afford
to increase wa,*es in any amount
under the government's existing
wage-price policy.
