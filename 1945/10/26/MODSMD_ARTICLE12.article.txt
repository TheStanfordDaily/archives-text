# Alumnus Earns Colonel Rank
## 
### 
Abe McllinkofF, Stanford gradu
ate and former editor of the Daily,
has been promoted to lieutenant
colonel, according to word received
here. He is now serving as intelli
gence officer with the 312 th Fighter
Wing in China, where he was sent
last year.
Colonel Mellinkoff was ordered
into active duty in 1941 as a lieu
tenant and was sent to Hamilton
Field in the public relations depart
ment of the Air Forces. From Ham
ilton Field he was sent to the West
ern Sea Frontier as Fourth Air
Force liaison officer and promoted
to the rank of major.
From there he was sent to the
China-Burma-India theater and has
been there since.
