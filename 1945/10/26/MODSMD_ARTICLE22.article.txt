# CELLAR OPEN NIGHTS
## 
### 
The Cellar will be open from 9
to 10:o0 Monday through Thurs
day evenings, starting next Monday
night, announced Fred Bremen
kamp yesterday. Bremenkamp was
recently appointed by Excom to re
cruit enough hashers to keep the
Cellar open evenings.
