# Southern Branch Mothers To Meet
## 
### 
Mrs. George Harrington, president
of the Mothers' Club of Stanford,
and Mr. H. Donald Winbigler, reg
istrar and Associate Professor of
Speech and Drama and Education at
Stanford University, will go south
to speak before the Mothers' Club,
Southern Branch.
The meeting will be held in Los
Angeles at 2 p.m. Tuesday, October
30, in the Colonial Room of the Am
bassador Hotel.
