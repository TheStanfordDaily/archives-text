# Social Com Set Today
## 
### 
The new Social Committee for the
housing program will meet today at
2 p.m. in the Excom Room, accord
ing to Joan Bailey, chairman.
The committee will begin work on
a plan for entertaining the fresh
men women in the various houses
and casas during winter and spring
quarter. It will also plan a pro
gram for educating the freshmen
on the different types of living
groups.
Committee members are Miss
Bailey, Betsy Brown and Thco Mc-
Guire, freshman representatives;
Mary Elizabeth Hall, Ventura; Gloria
Cassidy and Babs Wilbur, Lagunita;
Jackie Boucher, Union Unit; Bev
Leggett, the Row; and Shirley Marx,
Branner.
