Connie Wheeler and Tom Scofield
pulled the big upset of the all
campus mixed doubles tennis tour
nament Friday when they beat first
seeded Gene Corman and Barbara
Brooke, 6-4, 6-4.
Corman and Brooke were over
whelming favorites to win this
match and go victorious through the
final round but Wheeler and Sco
field had different ideas.
Will Enter Scmi-Finals
The steady playing Wheeler-Sco
field combination will go into the
semi-finals against Barbara Beards
lee and Grover Miller who upset
third seeded Mary Ed Davis and
Scv Chapman, 6-4, 6-3, in their third
round match.
Miller and Beardslcc, unseeded in
the tournament, beat the Davis-
Chapman combination to knock out
the last of the seeded players.
Seeded Players Out
First-seeded Corman and Brooke
lost their match; second seeded
Gregg Coward and Bill Dabney
were forced to default in the sec
ond round; third seeded Davis and
Chapman lost their third round
match; and fourth seeded Virginia
Hobbs and J. D. Williams lost their
third round match to George Lcong
and Tappy Kimball, 6-0, 5-7. 10-8.
Also entering the semi-final round
are Marianne Supple and Joe Owen
by virtue of their victory over Dick
ie Shainwald and Larry Poteat, 1-6,
6-4, 6-3.
