# Series Topic Guild Music
## 
### 
A concert of 16th and 17th cen
tury chamber music will be the
program for the Tuesday Evening
Scries in Memorial Hall tomorrow
night 7:30, presented by the
Friends of Music.
The Madrigal Guild, composed of
Bay Area artists, will present Eng
lish, Dutch and Italian madrigals,
a German motet, an aria from
Monteverdi's opera, "Orfeo," lute
ayres, folk, court and troubadour
songs, and a Lully suite for flute and
harpsichord.
