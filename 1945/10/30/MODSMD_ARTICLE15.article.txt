# Dr. Fisher Was On Nazi Blacklist
## 
### 
Dr. Harold H. Fisher, director of
the Hoover Library, was blacklisted
by the Nazis and subject to im
mediate arrest and execution upon
the German conquest of the United
States.
Besides being a professor of history
and director of the Hoover Library,
Dr. Fisher is a noted authority on
Russian history and author of "The
Bolshevik Revolution, 1917-1918."
Approximately 100 American edu
cators, writers, diplomats, and
scientists who helped the Poles were
blacklisted by the Nazis shortly aft
er the Allied invasion of Europe.
