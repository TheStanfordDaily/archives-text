# Religions' Review For Chapel Board
## 
### 
A discussion of the differences
between religious denominations
will follow the regular business of
the Chapel Board at the meeting
tonight, Leland Morrison, president,
has announced.
The discussion will be led by Dr.
D. Elton Trueblood, chaplain of
Stanford University and supervisor
of the organization.
"The Chapel Board is composed
of students who are interested in
religious questions. The only limi
tation on membership is that the
students are willing to support the
organization,'' Morrison said.
The Chapel Board meets every
Tuesday evening from 9 to 10 in
the room located at the rear of Me
morial Chapel. All students are in
vited io attend.
