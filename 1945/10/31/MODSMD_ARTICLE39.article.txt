# Punting Ace Back In Nevada Lineup
## 
### 
RENO', Nev., Oct. CO (UP).—Lloyd
(the Horse) Rude will carry the
punting burden for the Nevada
Wolfpack Saturday when they clash
with Fresno State, Coach Jim Aiken
announced ionight.
Rude has been the team's top
kicker but was forced to relinquish
his role for nearly a month because
of an injury.
