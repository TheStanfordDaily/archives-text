# U.C.L.A. Has Chalk Talk
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Oct. 30 (UP).—
Continued rain prevented heavy
drills today at the University of
California at Los Angeles, and Coach
Bert Labrucherie held a light signal
workout before taking his football
squad inside for a chalk talk.
Bob Sholaas, 190-pound right end
who attended high school at Ben
son Polytechnic, Portland, Ore..

joined the squad. He was transfer
red by the Navy from Redlands,
Cal.
