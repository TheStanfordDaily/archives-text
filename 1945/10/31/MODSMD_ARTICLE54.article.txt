# Cougars Ready
## 
### 
PULLMAN, Wash., Oct. 30 (UP).
—Washington State College Cougars,
in a slaming scrimmage session in
the college fieldhouse, today com
pleted their last full scale workout
before Saturday's tilt with Cali
fornia at Berkeley.
Coach Phil Sarboe lost Edin Stod
dard, veteran quarterback from
Coeur D'Alene, Ida., who dropped
from school this semester because
of the serious illness of his daugh
ter.
