# 'Favorite Wife' To Show Sunday
## 
### 
"My Favorite Wife," starring Cary
Grant and Irene Dunne, with Gail
Patrick, will be the feature of the
Farm's Films Sunday night, ac
cording to Patty Whittemore, in
charge of the Sunday series.
The comedy features the eternal
triangle, when after being missing
seven years Miss Dunne turns up
on the day her husband, Cary Grant,
marries again, said Miss Whitte
more.
The tenth and eleventh chapters
of the current serial "King of the
Wild," starring Boris Karloff, will
