BURNED ALIVE—U.S. soldiers uncover bones of more than
100 Americans captured at Bataan and Corregidor and
burned alive by Japs at prisoner-of-war camp. Medical corps
men hope to identify some of the remains through study of
teeth. This camp was at Puerto Princesa, Palawan, Philip-
Pine Islands. u. S Army Signal Corps Photo
