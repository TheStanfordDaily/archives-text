# Remaining Song Books Go on Sale this Week
## 
### 
The remaining 200 Stanford
song books will be on sale sonic
time this week, it was decided
yesterlay at the meeting of the
Music Council.
Al the first meeting of the coun
cil under its new chairman,
Frances Goodrich, plans were also
discussed for a revision of the
group's charter and additional
work on the music talent list.
This list is a compilation of the
names of all students on campus
interested in doing entertainment
for activities on the Farm.
