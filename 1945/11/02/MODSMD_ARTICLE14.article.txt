meet tonight from 7 to 10 p.m. in
the Memorial Auditorium, an
nounced Mary Lou Patton, director.

one in the Cuban scene, particular
ly the dancers, will rehearse, and
at 10 a.m. everyone in the Stan
ford scene will meet. Those wanted

bury, Rena Jean Black, Jcnie

the French, Sac, Revelation, Cuban,
and Stanford sccnes will rehearse
at 7:30 p.m., and the Closer will re
heai'se from 9 to 10 p.m. In the
Closer, all choruses are wanted, plus
the Souvenir Boy, Babbie Rogers,
Pat Mooney, and Genie Mitchell.

all of Act II will rehearse, and on
Wednesday the schedule will be: 7-8,
Opener; 8-8:30, Alaska; 8:30-9:30,
Russia; 9:30-10, Venus.
Everyone must be there on Wed
nesday, emphasized Miss Patton,
since the orchestra will play.

Saturday.
