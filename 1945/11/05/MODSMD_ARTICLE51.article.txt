# International Club To Hold first Meeting
## 
### 
The International Club will hold
its lirst on-campus meeting of
this quarter Wednesday at 7:30,
when it meets in the Lower
Lounge of the Women's Club
house, President Rohie Robinson
announced. Meetings of the or
ganization were formerly held on
Sunday evenings in a private resi
dence.
C. Langdon White, professor of
geography and speaker of the
evening, will talk on "Geopolitics."
Miss Robinson urges all who are
interested to come to the meet
ing.
