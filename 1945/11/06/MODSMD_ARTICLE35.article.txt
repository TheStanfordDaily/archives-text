# 'Play Ball' At Juniper
## 
### 
Football will be the theme of the
decorations for a closed dance which
will be held at Juniper Lodge Sat
urday, Nov. 10, from 0 until 1.
Decorations will be in red and white
in keeping with the theme and the
Stanford color, according to Wanda
Kozlowski, general chairman of the
event.
Dancing, entertainment, and food
will be offered to all girls living in
the house and their guests.
Those in charge of the affair are
Wanda Kozlowski, general chair
man; Nancy Lipsett, food; Shirley
Smith, entertainment; Byrl Warrick,
df-orations; and Phyllis Leveen,
cleanup.
