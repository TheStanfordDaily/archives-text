# STEAMSHIP SERVICE RESUMED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Nov. 6 (UP).
—The U.S. Maritime Commission
announced today the resumption
Wednesday of intercoastal steam
ship service between the Atlantic
and Pacific coasts.
