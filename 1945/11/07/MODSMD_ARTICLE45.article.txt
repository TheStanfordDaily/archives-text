# Wet Field for Bruins
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Nov. 5 (UP) —
I The University of California at Los
j Angeles practice field was so wet
today that Coach Bert Laßrucherie
let his squad off with no practice—
just a few sprints to limber them
up and keep them warm.
! Bocause their next game is still
I a week and a half away, he skipped
even a chalk talk.
