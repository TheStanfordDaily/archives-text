# Klahoya Host To Navy Men
## 
### 
Eighty Navy men will be guests
at a dance given by Klahoya this
Friday, Nov. 9, in the Women's Club
house.
Audrey Jacobs, president of the
organization for off-campus women,
stated that the affair will be informal
with entertainment, mixers, and re
freshments.
The darce will feature a football
theme, with a painting of Lil In
jun highlighting the decorations.
Patronesses arc to be Mrs. Phillip
O'Connell, Mrs. Edith Duncan, and
Mrs. Dorothy Tibby.
