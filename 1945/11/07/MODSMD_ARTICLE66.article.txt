# Future of France Told by Guerard
## 
### 
Albert L. Guerard, professor of
general literature, addressed the
Beverly Hills Forum in Los Angeles
last night on "The Future of
France."
A noted authority on France and
Frcnch history, Mr. Guerard has
written "The France of Tomorrow,"
"French Civilization," and"The
Napoleonic Legend."
During the war he worked for the
Office of War Information, broad
casting by short - wave to Nazi
ruled France from New York and
Washington on behalf of the United
States government.
