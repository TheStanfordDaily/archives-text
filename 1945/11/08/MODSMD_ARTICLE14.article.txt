# Rally Com Visits Lagunita Monday
## 
### 
"Come Join the Band" says Rally
Com to Lagunita and Ventura resi
dents. The committee is planning
another informal rally, in Lagunita
Court next Monday night at 6:45.
The rally will emphasize the learn
ing of Stanford songs and yells in
preparation for next Thursday night's
afTair. Rally Com member Ted Hol
comb is in charge of the Lagunita-
Ventura event.
Cheer Leaders Newt Arnold. Bill
Lippincott and Charles Jackman
have been directing this week's in
formal rallies, and they will be
present at Lagunita Monday night.
