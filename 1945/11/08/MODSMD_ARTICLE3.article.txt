# Knoll Women Plan fireside
## 
### 
Indian cherubs will float about in
a Stanford Heaven which the grad
uate women of Lagunita Knoll are
creating for their fireside Friday eve
ning. Men from Tamarack, McKin
ley, JefTerson, and Fillmore Halls
have been invited to the affair which
will last from 8:30 to 11:30, accord
ing to President Barbara Gordon.
Refreshments, games and dancing
will be on the entertainment agenda.
Some off-campus women have been
invited.
The Knoll, temporary home of 20
Stanford graduate women, is the
former home of President Wilbur.
