# AST's To Sponsor Fireside Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford's AST's will sponsor an
other fireside in Encina lounge to
morrow night from 8:30 to 11:30.
The fireside, a closed affair, will
feature dancing, a roaring fire,
plenty of dark corners, and punch
and sheet cake for refreshments.
Bland Simmons, president of the
Army Student Council, is general
chairman for the affair; assisting
him will be Stan Manus and Sandy
Wilbourne. Erv Witkin and Bob
Davis will provide entertainment
and John Mieskalski will be in
charge of MP's.
