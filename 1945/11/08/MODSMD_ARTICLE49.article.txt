# RAF FLYER BREAKS WORLD SPEED RECORD
## 
### 
HERNE BAY, England, Nov. 7
(UP). —RAF Group Captain Hugh
Wilson shattered the official world
speed record today by piloting his
jet - propelled Gloster "Britannia"
plane four times over a measured
course at an average of 606 miles
per hour—better than a mile each
six seconds.
