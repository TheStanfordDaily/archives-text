# JUNIPER DANCE
## 
### 
Juniper Lodge will hold a football
dance, with "Play Ball" as the theme,
tomorrow night from 9 to 1. The
closed affair will feature good music,
a roaring fire, and good food, accord
ing to the committee. Decorations
and entertainment will be in the
spirit of the football theme. The
dance will be informal.
