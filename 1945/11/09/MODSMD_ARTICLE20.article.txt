# Choir To Gather At Yacht Harbor
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Univer
sity Choir are traveling to the Sea
Scout base at the Palo Alto Yacht
Harbor tonight at 8 p.m. for a so
cial gathering.
The 85 singers will elect a new
president to replace Frank Allen,
retiring head. Officers who will re
tain their positions are Bonnie Mae
Hanson, secretary, and John Phillip
Ellis, librarian.
Games and dancing will constitute
the entertainment and refreshments
will be served.
