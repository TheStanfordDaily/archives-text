# Carillonneur To Play Victory Tunes Sunday
## 
### 
Armistice Day program will be
featured by James R. Lawson in
his carillon recital from the Hoo
ver Library, Sunday afternoon at
2 p.m.
The selections included will be
"Victory Rhapsody for Carillon,"
by Percival Price, carillonneur of
the University of Michigan; Han
del's "Largo," "None But the Lone
ly Heart," by Tchaikovsky; Albert
Hay Malotte's setting of "The
Lord's Prayer," and "Onward
Christian Soldiers," by Sir Arthur
Sullivan, arranged by Anton Brees,
carillonneur of the Florida Sing
ing Tower.
