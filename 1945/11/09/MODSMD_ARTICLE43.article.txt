# Farm Engineers To Meet Monday
## 
### 
Student chapter of the American
Society of Civil Engineers will hold
a business meeting in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse
Monday, Nov. 12 at 7:15 p.m.
Business will include approval of
applications of new members, dis
cussion of annual initiation dinner,
and a decision on support of the new
Wing Scholarship.
Officers for the group are Chan
ning Cathcart, president; Larry
Ames, vice-president; John Olson,
recording secretary; Wesley Risling,
corresponding secretary, and Jim
Wilcox, treasurer.
