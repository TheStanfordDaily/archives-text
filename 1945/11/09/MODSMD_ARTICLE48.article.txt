# Army Sets Second Fireside Tonight
## 
### 
A fireside that will feature real
fire is one of the aspects of the
social event that the Army Student
Council is planning for members of
the AST unit. The time and place of
the big blaze is tonight from 8:30
to 11:30 in Encina Lounge.

Besides an active fireplace, there
will be dancing and refreshments.
Extra entertainment will be provided
by Irv "Platinum Voice" and Bob
"Golden Layrnx" Davis. Added note
—Major Eric Duckstead will also be
present.—Kane, Welter.
