# Quad Staff Plans Banquet Tonight
## 
### 
Staff members and tryoutees of the
Quad will meet at 6:15 tonight at the
Quad offices to attend the postwar
Quad banquet, Editor Millie Edwards
has announced.

gether" of all Quad workers, will be
at L'Omelette's and the charge will
be $1.85, according to Managing Ed
itor Virginia Walker. "There will
be enough cars for everyone," she
said, "and some cars will be return

those who cannot remain."
Quad staff members are urged to
