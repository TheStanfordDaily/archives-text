# English Review Club Meets Wednesday
## 
### 
The English Review Club will
meet for the second time this
Wednesday at 8 p.m. at the home
of Dr. Francis R. Johnson, 2225
Webster St., Palo Alto.
An original story by a Stanford
student will be read and discussed,
according to John E. Cobey, club
secretary. There will also be a
review of Somerset Maugham's
play, "Sheppey."
Graduate students, members of
the faculty, and seniors in the
English Department arc invited
to attend the meeting.
