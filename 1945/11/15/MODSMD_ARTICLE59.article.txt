# Sale of 46 Quads Continues Today
## 
### 
Post office sales of the 194G Quad
will be from 8 to 12 today, accord
ing to Caryl Finn, Quad circulation
manager. Students are urged to Ret
their Quads early this year, as last
year they were sold out before the
first of the year.
Sales will continue at the same
times at the post office until the
supply runs out. The price is $5.00
plus 25 cents for name engraving.
The yearbook will definitely be
issued near the end of spring quar
ter.
