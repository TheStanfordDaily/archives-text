# Bush Tells Topic For Essay Prize
## 
### 
"The Future of Philippine-U.S.A.
Relations" was announced yesterday
as the 1945-46 topic for the Melville
Jacoby Essay Prize by Professor
Chilton R. Bush, of the Division of
Journalism,
A prize of $50 is awarded annually
for the best qssay by a Stanford stu
dent on a specific topic that relates
to the Orient. The closing date for
acceptance of essays is March 15.
The award was established three
years ago in memory of the late Mel
ville Jacoby (A.B. '.'3B, A.M. '39), Far
Eastern correspondent for "Time."
