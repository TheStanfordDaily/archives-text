###

of California students who attempt

early Thursday morning was dis
missed following their arraignment
Friday afternoon before Justice of
the Peace Edward E. Hardy in Palo
Alto, Dean of Students John M.
Stalnaker revealed last night.
The students, Hugh Daniel Wal
lace, 20, and Edwin Hinchman Bur

released Friday to return to their
campus.

administrative or student discipli
