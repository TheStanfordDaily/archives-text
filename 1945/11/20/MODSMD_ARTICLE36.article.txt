# Chiang Soldiers Enter Manchuria
## 
### 
CHUNGKING, Nov. 19.—'UP—Chi
nese Nationalist troops have driven
40 miles into Manchuria through a
breach in the Great Wall and have
captured the rail center of Suichung
on the road of Mukden, government
sources said today.
The two-army Nationalist drive
under Lt. Gen. Tu Li-ming was said
to have swept through Suichung, 40
miles northeast of captured Shan
haikwan, and to be rolling on along
the coastal railroad and highway
toward the Port of Hulutao, 43 miles
north of Suichung.
