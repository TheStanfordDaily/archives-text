# Dance Bids on Sale At 9 Today
## Red and White Ball To Have Farm Theme, Skinnay Ennis' Band 
### 
Bids for the Red and White Ball
go on sale today at the A.S.S.U. of
fice from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. The tick
ets are selling for $5.
Skinnay Ennis' orchestra will be
the big name band playing for the
Cardina 1 -sponsored dance, which
will be held Saturday from 9 p.m.
to 1 a.m. in the Pavilion.
According to Marylou McClure,
the Pavlion will be transformed into
a miniature Inner Quad. The
"Farm" theme will be further car
ried out in the use of the red and
white colors.
This being Stanford's first real
post-war social event, the bids are
expected to go quickly and it is ad
vised to get them as soon as pos
sible.
Headed by Miss McClure, other
members of the committee are Jean
Crowell, decorations; Willie Davies,
publicity; Ken Dole, bids; Lee
Smith, arrangements; Patty Robin
son, refreshments; Joan Fullinwider,
patrons; and Julie Crommelin, or
chestra.
