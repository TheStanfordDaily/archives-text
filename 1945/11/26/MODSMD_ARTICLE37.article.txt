# Holy Cross Gets Orange Bowl Bid
## 
### 
MIAMI, Fla.. Nov. 25 (UP).—Holy,
Cross College of Worcester, Mass.,
has officially received and accepted
an invitation to play in the Orange
Bowl football game here on New
Year's Day, it was announced today
by George E. Whitten, president of
the Orange Bowl Committee.
Orange Bowl committeemen made
no statement regarding an opponent
for the Eastern team. In recent days,
however, local sports writers have
been beating the drums for a home
town team—the University of Mi
ami.
