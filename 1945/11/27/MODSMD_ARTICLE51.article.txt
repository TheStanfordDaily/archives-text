# Atomic Bomb Forum to Consider Question of International Control
## 
### 
A forum on the question of the
atomic bomb, sponsored by Delta
Sigma Rho, debate society, will be
held Wednesday, Dec. 28, at 7:30, in
the Cubberley Auditorium.
Presided over by Thomas A. Bai
ley, the forum will open with three
short speeches from Paul Kirkpat
rick, acting dean of the School of
Physical Sciences; Seville Chapman,
assistant professor of physics, and
Philip Buck, professor of political
science.
Dr. Kirkpatrick will begin by
giving, as far as secrecy allows,
a scientific approach to the bomb
proper. Dr. Chapman will talk
on the urgency of having an in
ternational control of the bomb.
Dr. Buck will conclude the talks
by speaking on the difficulties that
would be incurred in setting up an
international control.
Following the three speeches,
there will be an allotted time of
about fifteen to twenty minutes in

which questions may be asked of the
professors by the audience.
The rest of the time will he open
for discussion, in which any per
son present may freely express his
own opinion on the subject of the
bomb, as well as challenge or ask
further questions of any persons.
Everyone on campus or off-cam
pus is urged to attend.
