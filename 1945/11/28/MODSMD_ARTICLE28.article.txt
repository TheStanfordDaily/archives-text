# Poly Sci Society Adds 14 Members
## 
### 
Pi Sigma Alpha, the national po
litical science honorary society, will
hold a meeting tomorrow afternoon
at 4 o'clock in the West Library.
After having been inactive dur
ing the war, Pi Sigma Alpha is re
suming its campus activities.
The following eligible seniors and
graduates were elected to member
ship in the society at a meeting held
last week: Gloria Anderson, Carol
Atkinson. Marjorie Benedict, Eliza
beth Case, Mary Davies, Adorea
Goodman, Frances Jennings, Bar
bara Lamb, Louise Phillips, Polly
Ross, Pat Ann May, Lowell Noonan,
and Marion Both.
