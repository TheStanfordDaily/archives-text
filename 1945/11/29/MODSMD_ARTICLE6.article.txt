# Violinist Plays Today in Recital
## 
### 
Audrey Avis Assen, violinist, will
play at the recital today at 4:15 p.m.
in the Stanford Memorial Church.
Miss Assen will play Mozart's
Concerto No. 5 in A Major for Vio
lin and Orchestra. She is a graduate
of the University of Oregon and was
for a time assistant to Professor Un
derwood in that institution. She has
been heard on NBC and played in
the symphony orchestra last summer
under Sir Thomas Beecham.
