# REVISE POTSDAM—U.S.
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Nov. 29 (UP).—
President Truman revealed today
that the United States is seeking to
break the four-power deadlock over
control of Germany through revision
of the Potsdam declaration.
He declined-to give specific infor
mation on the grounds that a de
tailed discussion might prejudice
negotiations.
