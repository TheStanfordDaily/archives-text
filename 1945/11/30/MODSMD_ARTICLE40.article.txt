# Cap & Gown Tea Today
## 
### 
Cap and Gown, senior women's
honor society, will hold its final tea
for the quarter this afternoon at 4
o'clock at the home of Mrs. Dare
Stark McMullin, 500 Melville Ave
nue, Palo Alto.
Old and new members and alum
nae will attend the social function.
All on-campus members of Mortar
Board, national honorary society, are
also invited.
Entertainment will be provided by
the new members, who will present
an original song which they have
prepared for the occasion.
All Cap and Gown members who
have not made appointments for
their Quad pictures must do so to
day in the Quad office, announced
Barbara Lamb, president of Cap and
Gown.
