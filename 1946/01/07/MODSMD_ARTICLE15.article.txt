# Adelfa Casa Plans Active Schedule
## 
### 
Adelfa Casa in Lagunita Court has
started an active social season this
quarter with an Open House yester
day from o to 5 p.m. and plans a
dancing party next Saturday at
Yacht Harbor.
The Open House was arranged by
Barbara Seaver, president; and
Anne Shimmel, vice - president.
Veterans from Encina, Hayes, and
Taylor Halls were invited. Dancing
and card games furnished enter
tainment for the guests. Refresh
ments were served.
