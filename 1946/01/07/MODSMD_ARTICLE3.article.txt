# Red Cross Needs 12 Nurses' Aides
## 
### 
The Red Cross is making an ap
peal for 12 Stanford Women to sign
up for the Nurses Aid course. Three
units of University credit will be
given for the course and grades will
be on the plus-minus basis.
Women interested are asked to be
at the Women's Gymnasium this
evening from 7 to 10 p.m. for the
first class. Mrs. Margaret Riasctti
will speak. It is not necessary to
have signed up previously.
