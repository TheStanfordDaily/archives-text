# Professor Beadle To Leave Faculty
## 
### 
Dr. George Weils Beadle, member
of the Stanford University biology
faculty since 1937, has resigned to
accept a professorship in biology at
California Institute of Technology.
He will remain at Stanford, how
ever, until July, 1946.
Dr. Beadle received his Ph.D. at
Cornell University in 1930. He was
a Fellow at California Institute of
Technology in 1930-35. and instruc
tor in 1935-36. In 1936-37 he was
assistant professor of genetics at
Harvard University.
