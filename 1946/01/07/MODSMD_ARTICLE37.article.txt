# PLANE CRASHES
## 
### 
BIRMINGHAM, Jan. 6 (UP).—
Fifteen passengers and a crew of
four miraculously escaped death to
day when a Pennsylvania Central
Airlines plane overshot the runway
at Birmingham airport and plunged
into a 20-foot ditch.
