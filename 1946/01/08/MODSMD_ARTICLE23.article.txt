# Vocom to Honor Soph Members
## 
### 
Members of Soph Vocom will be
honored at a party given by regular
Vocom Thursday at 4:15 p.m. in the
Lower Lounge of the Women's Club
house, Chairman Mary Ed Davis
has announced.
Mary Jane Bull is in charge of
the refreshments. Soph Vocom
members are invited to the regular
meeting which will follow the re
freshments. Future meetings for
women will be planned.
