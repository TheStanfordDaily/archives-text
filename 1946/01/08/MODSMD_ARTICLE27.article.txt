# Excom to Meet Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Excom will hold its first, meeting
of the quarter tomorrow night at.
in the Excom room of the old
Union, A.S.S.U. President Boh Ken
nedy announced last night.
Main topic for discussion, Ken
nedy said, would be the possibility
of raising salaries of A.S.S.U. Con
cert Series managers. Excom meet
ings arc open to all students wishing
to attend.
