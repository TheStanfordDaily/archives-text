WITH the return of a peacetime schedule for the
University, THE STANFORD DAILY must en
large it staffs. The business staff of the paper
has positions open in all aspects of the business manage
ment of the DAILY. Men students, long missing from
the staff's roster, are especially urged to take advantage
of this opportunity to join the business staff. The DAILY
is a large business with a budget well over $20,000 for
the school year and requires capable management. The
benefits of such work include valuable experience and
contacts, advancement to salaried positions, and upper
division credit. No experience is necessary. For further
information sec the business manager in the Shack from
2 to 5.
