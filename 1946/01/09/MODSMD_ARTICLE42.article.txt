# Choir Will Meet Tomorrow at 7
## 
### 
The Stanford University Choir
will hold its first meeting of the
quarter tomorrow at 7 p.m. in the
choir room of Memorial Church. At
tendance is urged for all a cappella
chancel choir members as vacancies
will be filled from members of the
gallery choir and new applicants.
Members and former member of
the choir who have had six units of
Music 1 may register for Religious
Music, Music 112, with or without
lectures on Tuesdays and Thursdays i
at 1 p.m.
