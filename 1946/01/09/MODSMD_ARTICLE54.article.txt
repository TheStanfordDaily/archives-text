# YWCA Will Plan Winter Activities
## 
### 
Y.W.C.A. affairs for this week will
include mainly committee meetings
in preparation for activities which
will begin next week, according to
Miss Pat Davidson, director.
The World Affairs group will
make plans for the winter quarter
including discussion of next Thurs
day's meeting at a luncheon today
in Lagunita at 12. Ginny Butler is
chairman of the World Affairs com
mittee.
Y.W.C.A. cabinet will meet this
Sunday to outline activities for this
quarter at a tea at Miss Pat's house,
541 Salvatierra, at 3:30 p.m.
