# Organist Will Present Recital Tomorrow, 4:15
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present an organ recital
tomorrow at 4:15 p.m. in Memorial
Church.
The program will consist of Ro
manze in A fiat, by W. A. Mozart;
Sketch in D fiat major and Romanze
and Scherzo, Symphony IV, by Rob
ert Schumann; On the Steppes of
Central Asia, by Borodin, and two
chorale-preludes for the new year,
by J. S. Bach.
Recitals are given on the first
Sunday in the month at 7:30 p.m.
and every Thursday at 4:15 p.m.
