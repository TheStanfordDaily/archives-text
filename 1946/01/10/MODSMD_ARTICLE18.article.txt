# Allen to Feature Mozart, Borodin
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will play assorted pieces in
his organ recital to be given today
at 4:15 in Memorial Church.
Included in the program are Ro
manze in A flat, by W. A. Mozart;
Sketch in D flat major and Romanzc
find Scherzo, Symphony IV. by Rob
ert Schumann; On the Steppes of
Central Asia, by Borodin, and two
chorale-preludes for the new year,
by J. S. Bach.
Recitals are given on the first
Sunday in the month at 7:150 p.m.
and every Thursday at 4:15 p.m.
