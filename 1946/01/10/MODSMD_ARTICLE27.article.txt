# Choir Will Meet Tonight in Chapel
## 
### 
The Stanford University Choir
will hold its first meeting of the
quarter tonight at 7 o'clock in the
choir room of Memorial Church.
All a cappella chancel choir mem
bers are especially urged to attend
as vacancies will be filled from
members of the gallery choir and
new applicants.
Members and former members of
the choir who have had six units
of Music I may register for Reli
gious Music, Music 112, with or
without lectures on Tuesdays and
Thursdays at 1 p.m.
