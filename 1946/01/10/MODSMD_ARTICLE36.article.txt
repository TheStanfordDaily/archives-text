# Intramurals Will Begin Next Week
## 
### 
Women's intramural basketball
teams have been organized to be-
leagues on Monday, Tuesday,
and Wednesday nights next week.
At least ten girls should be on
each team representing a living
group or any other group. Shirley
Sneath, basketball manager, will
handle the games.
According to Mrs. Fenner, there
were about thirty teams in the in
tramural basketball leagues last
year, and she hoped for a large
turn-out this season.
Each quarter intramural sports
are organized by the Women's Ath
letic Association.
