# Fear of Diphtheria Epidemic Passes
## 
### 
The danger of a diphtheria epi
demic has passed, reports Dr. Lois
I'. Todd, medical adviser for
women.
The disease was introduced to
the campus by a girl Who had a
sore throat on registration day.
The sore throat developed into
diphtheria. The girl was sent to
the rest home and her immediate
contacts were tested. Two of the
girls were positive and have been
isolated. However, anyone having
a sore throat is requested to re
port to the health service as a
necessary precaution.
