# PRISONERS RECAPTURED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Jan. 13 (UP).
—Three escaped Italian prisoners of
war were captured today on a ranch
four miles south of Half Moon Bay
where they had been working under
assumed names, Clinfton W. Stein,
special agent in charge of the San
Francisco FBI office, reported today.
