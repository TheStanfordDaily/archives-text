# Education School To Show Movie
## 
### 
The School of Education will
show the National Education Asso
ciation film "Assignment: Tomor
row" this afternoon at 4:30 in the
auditorium of the School of Educa
tion Building.
"Assignment: Tomorrow" is the
story of the more than 800.000
teachers in this country. It is the
first documentary film that has been
made dealing with the American
teacher. It presents the significance
of the work of teachers as individ
uals and in organized groups.
