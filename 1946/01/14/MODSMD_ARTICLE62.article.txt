# UNRRA Plans Clothing Drive
## 
### 
One garment per student is Stan
ford's quota in the National Cloth
ing Drive sponsored by the UNR
RA. The drive, which will take
place on Jan. 22 and 23, has a na
tional quota of 100,000,000 garments
plus additional bedding and shoes.
Phyllis Sutherland has been ap
pointed the Farm's chairman of the
drive. The only requisite for clothes
submitted is that they must be clean
and sanitary. The garments will be
sent to the peoples of Europe, the
Philippines, and the Far East.
