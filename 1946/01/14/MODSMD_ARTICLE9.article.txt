# Fraternity Council Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
A short meeting of the Intcrfra
ternity Council will be held to
morrow night in the Excom room
at 8 o'clock. The meeting, which
was changed from 7:30 to 8 be
cause of the Bob Hope show, is
scheduled to discuss the adoption
of rushing rules for the next three
quarters and to elect two new
members to the Intcrfratcrnity
Board. All fraternity presidents
are urged to attend.
