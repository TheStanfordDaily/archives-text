# Cleveland Plans Friday Fireside
## 
### 
Preparations for the Cleveland
Hall fireside this Friday night were
in high gear today, as the occupants
of the hall worked on dance plans.
The whole campus is invited to drop
in during the evening to dance, guz
zle cider, munch cookies, or just
sit.
Cleveland social chairman Jim
Chcehy will be in command in the
phonograph department. He states
that the best dance music on campus
will be heard at Cleveland, featur
ing selections by Stan Kenton,
Woody Herman, Benny Goodman,
Ha! Mclntyre, and other top disc
artists.
