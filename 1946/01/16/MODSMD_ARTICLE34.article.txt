# Greene to Be On Symposium
## 
### 
Theodore M. Greene, visiting pro
lessor of humanities from Princeton,
will open the winter quarter series
of symposiums on American studies
tomorrow afternoon when he speaks
on "Some Academic Illusions."
The series, which is sponsored by
the Humanities Department, will be
held throughout the quarter in the
Lower Lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
The talk tomorrow will begin at
4:15 p.m.
