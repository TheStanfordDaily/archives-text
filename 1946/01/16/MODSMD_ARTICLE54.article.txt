# Stanford Dames Meet Thursday
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the na
tional organization of University
Dames is welcoming both old and
new members at the general meet
ing tomorrow at 8 p.m. in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Morton J. Renshaw will be the
speaker, and he will show colored
slides of Alaska and Stanford. There
will also be special music.
All married women who are reg
istered in the University and wives
of all students are invited to attend.
