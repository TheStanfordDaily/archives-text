# Colored Slides Tonight At Dames' Meeting
## 
### 
A general meeting welcoming old
and new members to the Stanford
chapter of the national organization
of the University Dames will be
held tonight at 8 in the Women's
Clubhouse.
Morton J. Renshaw will show col
ored slides of Alaska and Stanford.
There will also be special music. All
married women who are registered
in the University and wives of stu
dents are invited to attend.
