# Historians Meet Here Saturday
## 
### 
First of two one-day sessions of
the Pacific Coast Branch of the
American Historical Association
will be held here tomorrow when
members from the San Francisco
Bay Area gather at 9 a.m. in the
Education Building.
Franklin C. Palm, of the Univer
sity of California, will be chairman
of tomorrow morning's general ses
sion. "Historians and the War" will
be the theme of the afternoon ses
sion, presided over by Edward M.
Hulme of Stanford.
Edgar E. Robinson of Stanford
will preside over the dinner meet
ing, with a talk by Raymond J.
Sontag of California scheduled.
