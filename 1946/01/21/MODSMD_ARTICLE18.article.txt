# IMPORTANT NOTICE TO VETS
## 
### 
All textbooks and non-expend
able items purchased for veterans
in training by the United States
Veterans Administration are the
property of the government while
the veterans are in training and are
not to be disposed of during that
time, according to Robert S. Raven,
U.S. Veterans Co-ordinator.
These items may be called for at
any time by the Veterans Adminis
tration. Upon completion of train
ing it is understood that such items
will be given to the veteran at that
time, said Raven.
