# Married Couples To Eat at Encina
## 
### 
Arrangements for married stu
dents and their wives to eat in En
cina Commons under the same con
ditions as single students can now
be made, announces John M. Stal
nakor, dean of students.
Adjustment for the number of
meals thus far lost will be made
from the total cost of $110 a quar
ter. The plan will be on an experi
mental basis the remainder of the
quarter, announced Stanley L.
Sharp, director of men's residences.
Meals arc served three times a
day at the Commons, from Sunday
through Friday, with no meals on
Saturday. No refund can be made
on the meals not eaten there.
