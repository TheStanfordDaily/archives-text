# Women's Conference Plans Jolly-Up Today
## 
### 
Women's Conference will meet
today at 3 p.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse to discuss plans for the
all-campus jolly-up which it will
sponsor Friday night in the Wom
en's Clubhouse, according to Patty
Patten, chairman.
The manner in which the winner
of the scholarship cup was deter
mined will be explained.
Conference members will also
make general plans for the all
campus carnival it will give March
1. The carnival will bo a closed date
and money will be used to renew
the Conference scholarship.
There will be a report from Hous
ing Committee; Carol Newton will
give a report on the budget; and
there will be a Vocom report.
