# PERSIAN PREMIER RESIGNS
## 
### 
TEHRAN, Iran, Jan. 21 (UP).—
Premier Ebrahim Hakimi resigned
after a three months' term of office
which saw the important northern
province of Azerbaijan break away
from the Central Government and
thrust Iran into a dispute with the
Soviet Union, her powerful neigh
bor to the north.
