# Eye Doctors Shown New Techniques
## 
### 
Dr. Hans Barkan, Dr. Dohrman
Pischel, and Dr. Avery Hicks of
Stanford were among the forty-five
prominent military and civilian eye
surgeons and scientists who attend
ed the meeting of Peninsula and
Bay Area ophthalmologists at Dib
ble General Hospital recently.
Demonstrations of the more inter
esting medical and surgical eye
cases at Dibble were presented with
emphasis on techniques and proce
dures used. An overall picture of
Dibble's blind rehabilitation pro
gram was given and papers on vari
ous phases of eye surgery at the
hospital were presented.
