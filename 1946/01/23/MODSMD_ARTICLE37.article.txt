# Fliers Will Plan Trip at Meeting
## 
### 
Definite plans for a cross-country
trip in the near future will be dis
cussed at the Stanford Flying Club's
meeting tomorrow night at 7:30 in
the Lower Lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse, according to President
Bob Jones.
Jones has obtained five planes and
the group will discuss and decide on
a suitable landing field on the pe
ninsula for their destination.
"Rates with local airports will be
discussed at this meeting also.'' said
Jones.
All students who have never
flown before but are interested in
I aviation will be cordially welcomed
I at the meeting, along with veterans,
added Jones.
