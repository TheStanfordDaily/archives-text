# Hot Jazz Group Elects Anderson President
## 
### 
Oscar Anderson was elected pres
ident of the Stanford Hot Jazz So
ciety at its meeting Monday night.
Nancy Wright wa,s chosen vice
president while Helen Russell will
be the new secretary-treasurer.
A program of records presented
by Anderson was the entertainment
of the evening. Frank Goulette is
planning the program for next
week's meeting, which will be held
in the rooms of the Women's Club
house at 8 p.m. Monday.
