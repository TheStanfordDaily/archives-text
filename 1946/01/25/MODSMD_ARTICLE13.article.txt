# Astrology Theme For Roble Dinner
## 
### 
Signs of the zodiac and heavenly
bodies will decorate Roble's dining
room for the annual Birthday Din
ner on Jan. 29 at 6 p.m.
The dinner is a tradition at Roble,
and is given twice a year for all
those whose birthdays fall in that
half of the year. Girls having birth
days in fall or winter quarter have
been sent invitations including their
horoscope and sign of the zodiac.
