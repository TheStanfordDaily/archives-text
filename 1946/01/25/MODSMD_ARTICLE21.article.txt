# Thursday Deadline Set For Vaccination Blanks
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates must be
turned in to the Registrar's Office
by Thursday for all new students,
including those having permits to
attend classes, and all others
whose certificates have expired.
A $2 fine will be made against
those who fail to file their certifi
cates by 5 p.m. on Thursday. Reg
istration in the University will be
subject to cancellation by the
Committee on Public Health if
they are not led by February 7.
