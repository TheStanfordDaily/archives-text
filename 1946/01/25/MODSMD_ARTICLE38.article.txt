# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
GEOLOGY JOURNAL CLUB will
meet Monday at 4:10 in room 334
of the Geology Building to hear a
review of the "Geology and Oil
Possibilities of Iran" by L. Clause
Roark.
STANFORD FOLK DANCERS will
meet tonight from 8 to 10 in the
Nitery to reorganize the group.
Both new and old members are
welcome to attend.
STANFORD'S SYMPHONY FORUM
will meet today at 4 p.m. in the
Excom room of the Old Union,
Chairman Betty Leuschel an
nounced yesterday. All living
group representatives are asked to
come or send a substitute, Miss
Leuschel said.
STANFORD HOT JAZZ SOCIETY
is planning a program of live jazz
for its meeting Monday at 8 p.m.
in the "Y" room of the Women's
Clubhouse. Frank Golett of San
Jose State has arranged for the
jam session.
DELTA TAU DELTA members will
meet Monday night at the home
of H. J. Jepson, Oak and San Be
nito avenues in Atherton.
