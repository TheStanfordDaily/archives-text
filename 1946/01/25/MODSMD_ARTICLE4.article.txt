# Bunn Resigns Teaching Post
## 
### 
The resignation of John William
Bunn as professor of hygiene and
physical education was announced
today by Dr. Donald B. Trosidder,
president of Stanford University.
Now on a year's sabbatical leave,
a part of which he spent in Europe
in connection with the army's ath
letic program there. Mr. Bunn is
now making a general survey of
physical education and athletic pro
grams in colleges and universities
through the country.
At Stanford since 19150, Bunn was
formerly head basketball coach and
dean of men.
