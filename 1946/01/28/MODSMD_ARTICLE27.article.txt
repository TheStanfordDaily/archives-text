# Roble to Hear Greene Talk Informally Tonight
## 
### 
Theodore M. Greene, visiting pro
fessor of humanities, will speak to
Koble women tonight in the main
lobby,
Dr. Greene's topic will be "Why
Did You Come to College?" After
the informal talk, there will be a
discussion period in which the girls
will have a chance to ask questions.
