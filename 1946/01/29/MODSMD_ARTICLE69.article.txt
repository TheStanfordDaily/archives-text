# Row Excom Discusses Spring Quarter Dance
## 
### 
Row Excom yesterday discussed
the all-campus dance the Row
plans to give the second week of
spring quarter. The theme of the
dance and where it will be held
will be decided next week.
Plans for improving freshman
and transfer entertainment and
for encouraging more informal
entertainment were discussed.
Row houses decided to have
their vice - presidents represent
them at Row Excom, rather than
the house presidents as was sug
gested.
