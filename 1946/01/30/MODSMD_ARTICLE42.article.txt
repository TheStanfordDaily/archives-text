# Cal Chemist To Lecture
## 
### 
Paul L. Kirk, professor of bio
chemistry at the University of Cali
fornia, will speak on "Some New
Microchemical Developments of In
terest to Biologists" at the Biological
Science Department's weekly sem
inar tomorrow afternoon at 4:15 in
room 430 of Jordan Hall.
Dr. Kirk is interested in protein
and amino acid chemistry and has
developed and perfected a number
of microchemical methods. He has
also recently announced the success
of the new technique and "Laughlen
antigen'' in 150 syphilis blood sam
ples. No false diagnoses were made
in the tests. The test, invented in
1935 by Canada's Dr. George F.
Laughlen, was not made practical
for general use for four years.
Dr. Kirk was visiting professor at
the Hopkins Marine Station in the
summer of 1935. He was also a
member of the staff of the metal
lurgical laboratory of ihe University
of Chicago which worked on the
atomic bomb.
Faculty, students, ar 1 visitors are
invited to attend the 112 nninar which
will be preceded by a tea at 4 p.m.
