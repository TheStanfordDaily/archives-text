# Badminton Club To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Badminton Club
will hold its first meeting tonight at
7:30 in the Women's Clubhouse, an
nounced badminton manager Elsie
Wallin. Men as well as women are
invited to the initial meeting, as
well as to participate in the election
of officers to be held next Wednes
day.
