# Vaccination Papers Are Due Tomorrow
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates must be
turned into the Registrar's Office
by tomorrow by all new students
including those who have permits
to attend classes and all others
whose certificates have expired.
Those students who have failed
to turn in the certificates by 5 p.m.
Thursday will be fined 82. Regis
tration in the University will be
subject to cancellation by the
Committee on Public Health if
they are not in bv Feb. 7.
