# Naranja Asks Halls to Dance
## 
### 
Casa Naranja of Lagunita Court
will give a dance right after the
basketball game Friday night with
the College of the Pacific. Invita
tions are being sent to Washington,
Fillmore, McKinley and Buchanan.
General chairman of the dance is
Roberta Mace. Tish Lee is in charge
of invitations and Beth Rubin is in
charge of the refreshments. Ruth
Levison is president of the casa.
The theme is the opening of Monte
Carlo. A French atmosphere will
invade Naranja for the evening,
