# Faculty to Revive Old Farm Tradition
## 
### 
Mr. and Mrs. Harry Williams will
entertain twenty Branner women
this evening at after dinner coffee,
reviving one form of the Farm tradi
tion of student-faculty social rela
tions.
The after dinner coffee is the first
of several planned by the student
affairs committee of the Y.W.C.A.
under the chairmanship of Jean
Strong. Members of the committee
will assist Mr. and Mrs. Williams.
Facility guests at the after dinner
affair will be Dr. and Mrs. John L.
Mothershead, Dr. and Mrs. Paul R.
Farnsworth and interim chaplin Dr.
and Mrs. Jesse Lyons.
