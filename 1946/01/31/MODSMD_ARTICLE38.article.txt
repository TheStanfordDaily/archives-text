# Hawaiian Club to Hear Music, Arrange Dance
## 
### 
Stanford's Hawaiian Club will hold
a meeting and musical session to
nights at 7 p.m. in the Lower Lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse, accord
ing to President Don Helbush.
Main purpose of the meeting will
be to formulate plans for the forth
coming dance and serenade. There
will be a songfest featuring Ha
waiian music after the regular meet
ing.
