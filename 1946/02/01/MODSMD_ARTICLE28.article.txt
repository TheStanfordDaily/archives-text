# Protein Research Financed for Farm
## 
### 
A grant of $2,000 from Swift and
Company of Chicago for research
work in proteins has been received
by Stanford University, it was an
nounced today by Donald B. Tresid
der, president of the University.
The money will be used in studies
on the "Separation and Character
ization of the Proteins of Certain
Organs and Tissues," being con
ducted at Stanford by Dr. J. Mur
ray Luck, professor of biochemistry.
