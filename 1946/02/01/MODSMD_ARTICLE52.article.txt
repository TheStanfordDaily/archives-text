# Casino Is Theme Of Naranja Dance
## 
### 
Men from Washington, Encina,
McKinley, and Buchanan Halls will
be entertained by Casa Naranja at
Lagunita at a "De Monte Carlo"
dance tonight following the basket
ball game.
Following the Monte Carlo theme,
a sophisticated French decor, spe
cially costumed "French" hostesses,
and a suave casino atmosphere will
be featured at the dance.
Roberta Mace is general chairman
of Ihe affair. Tish Lee has charge of
invitations; Beth Rubin is chairman
of the refreshments committee. Pres
ident of Casa Naranja is Ruth Levin
son.
