# Philippine Bank Note Sent by WAC
## 
### 
A Philippine 20 - peso note has
been received by Stanford Univer
sity to be used as "pin money" by
a GI veteran of the New Guinea
campaign, it was announced today
by President Donald B. Tresidder.
The note, valued at $9.60, was the
gift of Ist Lieut. Jean P. Smith,
WAC, who received her Master of
Arts degree in English at Stanford
in 1919.
The money was sent to the Stan
ford Fund by Lieutenant Smith "for
the New Guinea campaign in which
I also served from June 1944 to July
1945."
