# 'Russia, Asia' Next at Series
## 
### 
"Russia and Asia" will be discussed
by Dr. Stefan Osusky, former
Czechoslovak ambassador to France,
at th" Tuesday Evening Series next
week.
"What of Russian policies, of Asia's
future?" will be among the ques
tions the speaker will discuss in his
lecture beginning at 7:30 p.m. in the
auditorium of Memorial Hall.
Dr. Osusky, who holds degrees in
philosophy, psychology, and law,
served as chairman of the Super
visory Committee of the League of
Nations. He was minister to the
Court of St. James in 1918 and sec

retary general of the Czechoslovak
delegation to the Peace Conference
in 1919.
In addition, he was delegate to
various international conferences
and to the Reparations Commission
and the League of Nations Assem
bly. The diplomat is author of sev
eral books, including the recently
published "Essential Russia."
