# Husbands Guests Of Dames Tonight
## 
### 
A "fun night" is planned for the
general meeting of the Stanford
Dames club tonight at 7:45 at the
Women's Clubhouse. Husbands will
be special guests for the evening.
The bridge section of the club, of
which Mrs. Fred Tinkham is chair
man, is in charge of the meeting.
All types of games such as Chinese
checkers, bridge, and other table
games will be played. All married
women who are attending Stanford
and wives of students now regis
tered are invited to attend.
