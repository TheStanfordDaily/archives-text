# Scientists to Hear Tresidder Speak
## 
### 
Dr. Donald B. Tresidder will
speak on "Some Aspects of the
Problem of Federal Aid to Scientific
Research" at the joint seminar of
the Schools of Biological and Physi
cal Sciences tomorrow afternoon at
4:15 in room 430 of Jordan Hall.
Both Senators Kilgore and Mag
nuson have drawn up bills which
deal with the subject of federal aid
to scientific research.
The seminar, which will be pre
ceded by a tea at 4 p.m., is open to
faculty, students, and visitors.
