# BREMERHAVEN AREA SET
## 
### 
FRANKFURT. Feb. 4 (UP).—The
Bremerhaven staging area, where
the families of occupation troops
coming over here to live will be
processed, is expected to be ready
around April 1, Army Headquarters
announced today.
