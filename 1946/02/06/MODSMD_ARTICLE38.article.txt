# Navy Unit to Give Military Formal
## 
### 
A formal Military Ball is on the
schedule for Stanford's N.R.O.T.C.
unit for the night of February 21,
according to John Aldon, general
chairman of the dance.
The ball, which will be given in
Toyon from 9 to 11, will be a closed
affair for members of the N.R.O.T.C.
unit and their guests.
Sweet music, soft lighting, har
monious decorations, and "smooth"
refreshments are promised for the
event. Intermission time will fea
ture Don Toussaint of Gaieties fame
at the keyboard, a harmony trio,
and a popular ballad soloist. Music
will bo supplied by a band.
