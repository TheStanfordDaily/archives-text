# Dr. Meinesz to Talk At Geology Club Today
## 
### 
Dr. Veiling Meinesz, well
known geophysicist, will lecture
at the meeting of the Geology
Journal Club this afternoon at 4
in room 334 of the Geology Build

seminar this evening at 8 in the
same room. The subject of this
lecture will he "Gravity Anoma

Earth's History and Related Sub
jects."
Everyone is invited to attend.
