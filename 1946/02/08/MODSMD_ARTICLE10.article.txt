# Jazz Group To Perform
## 
### 
Frank Goulette and his Firewater
Five will be presented by the Stan
ford Hot Jazz Society this Monday
at 8 p.m. in the Nitcry in a program
of Dixieland jazz.
The lineup of the band will be as
follows: Oscar Anderson, Stanford,
on trumpet; Wright Cowger. Stan
ford, on the trombone; Frank Gou
lette of San Jose State on sax; Ted
Hoffman, Stanford, on piano; Carl
Data, Stanford, playing bass; and
Dick Frost of San Mateo J.C. on
drums.
Students and faculty are invited
to attend this first live jazz program
presented by the Jazz Socicty this
year.
