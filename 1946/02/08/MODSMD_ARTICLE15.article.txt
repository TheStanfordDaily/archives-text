# Winter Hits Redwoods
## 
### 
"Let it snow, let it snow, let it
snow" while the Redwoods holds its
ski lodge party tonight from 8 to
.11:30.
From the Redwoods windows the
campus will bo viewed through
snow, and throughout the hall the
winter sports theme will be carried
out. According to decorations chair
man Jane Smith, three fireplaces
will be ablaze.
Besides dancing, there will be a
weiner roast on the outside patio.
Ski clothes and other such casual
apparel will be in style.
Johnson Hall and other guests of
the girls living in the Redwoods
have been invited to attend the
closed party.
Ruth Cook and Sally Fair are
planning the refreshments, and
Bobby Pcttingill will handle the
arrangements.
