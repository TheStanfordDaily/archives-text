# CHANGE OF HEART
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 7 (UP).—
Capt. Alwin D. Kramer, USN, toid
the Pearl Harbor inquiry today that
the Japanese "winds" message he
once believed foretold a diplomatic
break, he now regards as one of
many "false alarms."
