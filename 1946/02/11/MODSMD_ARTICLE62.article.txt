# Youth Meet At Concord
## 
### 
Student leaders and young vet
erans representing colleges and high
schools throughout the country will
meet at Concord, Massachusetts, to
day, for the last meeting of a four
day conference on world govern
ment.
The conference was called by Stu
dent Federalists, an organization
founded in Scarsdale, N. Y. high
school in 1942 to campaign for a
democratic world federation. Joining
in sponsoring the Concord meeting
are independent groups of world
government at Yale, Harvard, Wel
lesley, Columbia, and other colleges,
leges.
Meetings were limited to delegates,
but there will be an open session
for the press and citizens of Concord
this afternoon.
