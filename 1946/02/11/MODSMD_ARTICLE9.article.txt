# Russian Music Series Theme
## 
### 
"The Deeply 'Human' in Russia's
Music and Letters" will be the topic
of the Tuesday Evening Series to
morrow night in the auditorium of
Memorial Hall.
Alfred Mirovitch, pianist - com
poser and educator, will speak of
the works of Dostoievskyj, Tchai
kowsky, Chekhov, and Moussorgsky.
A half-hour of piano selections will
follow the lecture.
Mr. Mirovitch, a native of Russia,
was graduated from the Conserva
tory of St. Petersburg, Russia, in
1909. He has since toured the world
nine times, playing in recitals and
with symphony orchestras in Eu
rope, Asia, Africa, America, and
the Antipodes.
