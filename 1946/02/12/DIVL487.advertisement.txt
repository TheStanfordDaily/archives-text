i
man's heart is through his stomach
Whoever whipped up that old wheeze about catching
your man by means of a big dinner was strictly oft the
ball. Any modern gal knows that the following is the
truth, the whole truth, and nothing else, s'help us: The
best way to let your big handsome male know you
really care, is to send him a gift from Roos Bros. Yes,
whether it's for Valentine's or Christmas, St. Patrick's
Day or Fourth of July, Roos Bros has the gift for him.
