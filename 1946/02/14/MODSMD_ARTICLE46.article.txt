# Russell Dance Has Ski Theme
## 
### 
Invitations to the Russell House
formal dance, which will be held
Saturday, February 16, have been
sent to all Row houses, according
to Barbara King, dance chairman.
Skiing will be the theme of the
dance, which will be held from 9
until 1, and decorations will feature
crossed skis over the fireplace, and
murals depicting snow and ski
scenes on the walls. A ski tow will
be rigged in front of the house.
Refreshments of cake and punch
will be served, and Art Weidner
and his orchestra will play.
