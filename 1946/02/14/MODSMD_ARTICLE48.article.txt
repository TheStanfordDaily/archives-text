# PROFITS FOR ASSU
## 
### 
Forty per cent of winter quarter
Dividex profits will go to the ASSU,
Excom ruled last night. The re
maining 60 per cent will be divided
between Barney McClure and Don
Allan, publishers of the Dixidex.
There had been some question as to
whether campus publications would
receive any share of the profits. Di
videx publication is handled under
a different contract each year.
