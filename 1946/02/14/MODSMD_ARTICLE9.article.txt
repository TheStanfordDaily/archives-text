# Open House On Sunday
## 
### 
Final open house for freshman
and transfer women will be hold
Sunday from 2:30 to 5 p.m., accord
ing to Social Committee.
Group A, Madrono, Elm, Haw
thorne, Lathrop, Storey, Mariposa,
and Manzanita, will entertain Group
3, Casa Ventura, Casita, all now
off-campus women, and new resi
dents of Branner.
Quad Clothes Worn
Group B, Russell, Roth, Hurlburt,
Jordan, Cubberley, Chalet, and Du
rand, will entertain Group 1, Roble
A and B wings; and Group C, Union
Residence, the Oaks, Granada, Oli
vo, Adelfa, Magnolia, Eucalypto,
and 'Naranja, will entertain Group
2, Roble C and center wings.
New students at Redwoods,
which is being shrunk at the end
of the quarter, are also invited to
attend any of the open houses.
Quad clothes will be worn and
refreshments will be served only
during the first hour.
Sign in Corridor
Freshmen and transfers should
sign up with their corridor repre
sentative on Social Committee for
the houses they plan to attend,
Joan Bailey, chairman, said. But
they should be sure to go to all the
groups they sign up for, she atlded.
Lagunita will be considered as
one unit for the open houses, but
in room drawing each casa will be
considered separately.
It is suggested that some groups
of women start at the top of the
Row and work down, so that the
upper Row houses will not be con
gested in the late afternoon.
