# A.S.C.E. Members To Meet Monday
## 
### 
All members of the Stanford
Chapter of the American Society of
Civil Engineers are asked to attend
a business meeting to be held in the
small corner room of the Women's
Clubhouse Monday at 7 p.m.
Members will consider contribu
tion to the Wing Scholarship, the
passing of applications of new mem
bers. and a field trip and social meet
ing to be held this quarter.
Members of the chapter will leave
at 4:30 p.m. Tuesday, Feb. 1!) from
the Engineering Corner to attend a
dinner meeting of the San Francisco
section of the A.S.C.E. George S.
Vincent, highway bridge engineer
of the Public Road Administration,
will speak on "Vibration of Suspen
sion Bridges." A sign-up sheet is
posted in the engineering building
for all those who wish to attend .
