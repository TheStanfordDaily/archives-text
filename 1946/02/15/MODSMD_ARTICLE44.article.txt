# SMITH TO REPLACE HARRIMAN
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Feb. 14 (UP).—
President Truman tonight accepted
the resignation of W. Averell Har
riman as ambassador to Soviet Rus
sia.
He will appoint as his successor
Lt. Gen. Walter Beddell Smith,
wartime chief of staff to Gen.
Dwight D. Eisenhower.
