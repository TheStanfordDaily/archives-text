# Economist Will Speak At Symposium Series
## 
###

tor in economics, will speak on
"The Tendency Toward Intensive
Agriculture, as Illustrated by De
velopments in the Salinas Valley"
Thursday at 1:15 p.m. in the Low
er Lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Mr. Jones's talk will be the
third of the winter quarter series
of the Symposium on American
Studies, sponsored bv the De
partment of Humanities. The
public is cordially invited.
