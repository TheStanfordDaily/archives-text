# Book on Guernsey Given to Library
## 
### 
The Hoover Library has received
"Press-Diary of Island Life During
German Occupation 1940-1945" from
Major Robert B. Hitchman.
The booklet, published on the is
land of Guernsey, is according to
Major Hitchman "the best report
of the German occupation of the
Channel Islands that I have found."
