# ATOM BOMB LEAKS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 20 (UP).-
The Senate Atomic Energy Commit
tee today turned to reported "leaks'''
of atom bomb secrets and summoned"
FBI Director J. Edgar Hoover and
Maj. Gen. Leslie R. Groves, chief of
the Army's bomb project, to testify
at closed sessions tomorrow.
James Mclnerney, head of the Jus
tice Department's espionage division,
also will testify.
