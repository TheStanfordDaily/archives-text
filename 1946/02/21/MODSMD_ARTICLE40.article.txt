# WAA Heads Plan Farm 'Olympics'
## 
### 
Plans for Olympic Day, which
will be held on April 13, were for
mulated at yesterday's meeting of
the Central Committee, announced
Alice Lovett, W.A.A. president anil
general chairman for the even.
All classes will participate in the
competition, which will include
tugs -of - war, pie - eating contests,
push ball games, tandem-bike races,
and various contests.
The freshman class will be in
charge of events and props; the
sophomores, entries and publicity;
the juniors, arrangements and priz
es; and seniors, concessions.
