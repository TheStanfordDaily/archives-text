# LAST UNION GET-TOGETHER
## 
### 
Union Unit will hold its last get
together of the quarter Monday at
a Come-As-You-Are-Party honor
ing Redwoods, which is leaving the
Unit this quarter, General Chairman
Moyna Taggart announces.
Two skits, songs by six members
of the Unit and* food will form the
evening's entertainment, which will
be held in the main lobby of Union
from ft to 10 p.m.
