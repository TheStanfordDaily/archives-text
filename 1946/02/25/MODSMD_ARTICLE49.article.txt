# Mrs. Tresidder to Be 'At Home' Tomorrow
## 
### 
Mrs. Donald B. Tresidder will be
at home to students and faculty to
morrow afternoon from 4 to 5:30 at
623 Mirada Avenue.
Members of the "Stanford fam
ily" are cordially invited to attend
the open house at the President's
home. The newest of Stanford's
traditions occurs the last Tuesday
of each month.
