# Roble Sets Date For Southern Ball
## 
### 
Springtime in the South will be
the theme of the Roble Formal, the
"Magnolia Blossom Ball," to be held
March 9 in the Women's Gym from
9 to 1.
Music will be provided by Art
Weidener's orchestra, and one of
the highlights of the evening will be
the special refreshments to be
served, announced Ellen Magnin,
general chairman for the dance.
The theme of life on the levee will
be carried out by unusual decora
tions designed by Joan Trautman,
decoration commitatee chairman.
Bids will go on sale this week to
women living in Roble. and may be
open for the whole campus next
week. Price of bids is $2.50.
