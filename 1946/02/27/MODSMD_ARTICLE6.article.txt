# Bob Coate Elected Encina President
## 
### 
Bob Coate, returned veteran, was
elected president of Encina in elec
tions held all day yesterday in the
Encina Library.
Others elected include Ted Lee,
secretary, also a veteran; and Glenn
Fish back, treasurer.
Nominees were chosen by a com
mittee consisting of Coate, Frank
McCaslin, Philip Ellis, and Slosson
Jong.
Two hundred two votes out of
a possible 500 were recorded.
