# MacFarland Gets Science Award
## 
### 
Dr. F. M. MacFarland, professor
of histology, emeritus, of Stanford
University received the highest
award given by the California
Academy of Sciences at their an
nual meeting February 20. 194G,
when they made him honorary
member. The award was made
when he retired after 12 years from
the presidency of the Academy.
Prior to his term as president.
Dr. MacFarland was corresponding
secretary in 1919, and was vicc
president in 1932. He also served
as acting director of the Museum
and Steinhart Aquarium.
