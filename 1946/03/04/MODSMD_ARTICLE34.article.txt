# Row Excom to Meet, Elect Officers Today
## 
### 
How Excom will meet today at
5 p.in-, according to Connie Wheel
er, president.
New vice-presidents of the Row
houses who will heroine members
of Row Kxcom in spring quarter
will visit today's meeting. They
will elect a president and secre
tary-treasurer to take ofTice spring
quarter.
Plans for the all-campus Row
dance to be given next quarter will
he discussed.
