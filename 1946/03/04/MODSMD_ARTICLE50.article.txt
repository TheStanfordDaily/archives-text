# LOOKIT, NO EARS!
## 
### 

SCIENTISTS ARE SPLITTING HAKES about earless Harvey (left), San Francisco's contribution to a
puzzling world situation. Says Harvey, striking a mournful pose, "I dread being mistaken for a guinea
pig." His happier brother, Milton (right)—why Milton?—is less self-conscious of his physical appear
ance: he was born with half-ears.
