# 'Protein Fibers' Topic Of Seminar Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Some Aspects of the Dynamics
of Protein Fibers" will be the topic
of Harold Lundgren, chemist of the
Protein Division, U.S. Department
of Agriculture, Western Regional
Laboratory, Albany, Calif., at the
Biological Science Department's
weekly seminar tomorrow after
noon at 4:15 in room 430 of Jordan
Hall.
The seminar, which is open to
faculty, students, and visitors, will
be preceded by a tea at 4 p.m.
