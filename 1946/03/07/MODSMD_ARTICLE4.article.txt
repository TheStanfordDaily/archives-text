# Veterans Should Turn In Document Of Terminal Leaves at Ad Building
## 
### 
The l)can of Students' ofTice announces that veterans who entered
Stanford while on terminal leave and are here under I'uhlic Law 34(>
("G.I. Bill") should bring to the Stanford University Veterans' Records
Office in room 1(M, Administration Building, one copy of an official docu
ment showing two items: the day on which their terminal leave began
and the day on which it expired.
If this paper is presented to a University Veterans' Records inter
viewer. a proper certificate of attendance can he made to the Veterans'
Administration, requesting that both tuition and books for the period of
terminal leave be included in the charges to he paid by the government.
This new procedure is authorized as one of the amendments to the
original Servicemen's Readjustment Act of 1911. The new provision is
retroactive to cover any period of instruction here while on terminal
leave since June 22, 1944.
