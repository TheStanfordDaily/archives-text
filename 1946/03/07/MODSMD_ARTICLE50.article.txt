# Allen to Present Organ Music Today
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will present an organ recital
today at 4:15 p.m. in Memorial
Church.
Included in the program will be
"Toccata on a Gregorian Theme"
by Edward S. Barnes; "Minuet from
'Berenice' " by G. F. Handel; "Pre
lude in C Minor" by J. S. Bach;
"Gavotte in F" by Padre Martini;
"Evening Song and Finale, Sym
phonic Etudes," by Robert Schu
mann.
