# Juniper Will Hold Open House Friday
## 
### 
Juniper Lodge opens its house by
special invitation to men from Roo
sevelt, Tyler, Jefferson, Sigma Nu,
and Delta Upsilon this Friday eve
ning. The affair will be an infor
mal dance and the"It Might as Well
Be Spring" theme which will be
carried out by garlands of colored
flowers promises to give "spring
fever" to all who attend.
Dancing will be to "off the rec
ord" music. Cards will be provided
for those who wish to play bridge
anct refreshments will be served.
Grace Ablow, social chairman for
Juniper, is in charge of the affair.
