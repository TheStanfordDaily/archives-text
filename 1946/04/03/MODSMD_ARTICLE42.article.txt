# Publishers Open Writers' Contest
## 
### 
The Atlantic Monthly Press in as
sociation with Little, Brown &
Company is conducting the first At
lantic Monthly Novel Contest to
make manuscripts written in foreign
languages as well as in English eligi
ble without restrictions as to au
thor or subject.
The winner of the contest which
will close Jan. 15, 1947 will be paid
$10,000 on the date of the announce
ment of the prize. Previous winners
have included "Jalna" by Mazo de
la Roche; "Peking Picnic" by Ann
Bridge, and"The Family" by Nina
Fedorova.
For a circular giving complete de
tails, address Atlantic Novel Contest
for 1947. 8 Arlington street, Boston
16, Mass.
