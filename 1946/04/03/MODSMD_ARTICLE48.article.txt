# Farm Alumna Serves Red Cross in Africa
## 
### 
Serving with the American Red
Cross in the Africa-Middle East
Command is Barbara Mordecai, a
Stanford graduate of the class of
'4:1. Prior to leaving for an over
seas .assignment, she worked at
the Pacific area of the Red Cross.
She has been stationed at Casa
blanca. French Morocco, and now
assists in the operation of a Red
Cross Officers' Club in that city.
Miss Mordecai's home is in Ma
dera. where she entered the Red
Cross in 1944.
