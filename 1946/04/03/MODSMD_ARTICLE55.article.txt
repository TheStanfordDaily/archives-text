# Stylist to Address Dames on Clothes
## 
### 
The Stanford Dames will hold
a meeting tomorrow at S p.m. in
the Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Dorothy Shaltner, stylist
at O'Connor - Moffet's in San
Francisco, will talk on the sub
ject of 'Clothes for 'You'." Wives
of students and married women
students are cordially invited to
attend.
