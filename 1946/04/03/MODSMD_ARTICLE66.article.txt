# 'I' Club Meet Next Sunday
## 
### 
Stanford's International Club will
hold its first meeting of spring quar
ter next Sunday at 7:30 p.m. in the
home of Mr. and Mrs. Edwin J.
Thomas in Palo Alto, according to
John Menzel, president of "I" Club.
A special invitation is extended to
incoming foreign students, veterans,
and returning Stanford students who
are interested in meeting students
from other countries informally and
in discussing international questions.
International Club's tentative pro
gram of activities for spring quarter
includes a dance, a campus meeting
at Lagunita, a dinner prepared and
served by the students from India,
a Turkish barbeque, informal discus
sions, a meeting at the Tresidder
home, and a picnic in the Santa Cruz
Mountains.
