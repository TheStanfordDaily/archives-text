# SPRING SHOW TRYOUTS BEGIN
## 
### 
The Spring Show will be given
this year on May 17 and 18. All
student - written musical comedy
scripts must he turned in to the
Drama Council odice in the Student
Union before April 10. Unfinished
scripts will be accepted. Tryouts
for the Spring Show will be on
Monday, April 8, and Wednesday.
April 10, at 7:30 p.m. in Memorial
Auditorium. These are not the final
tryouts. Singers must bring their
own music. An accompanist will
be provided.
