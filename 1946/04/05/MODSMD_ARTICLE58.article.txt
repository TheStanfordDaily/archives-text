# ANTI-FASCISTS CHARGED
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 4 (UP).—
The House Un-American Activities
Committee voted tonight to press
contempt charges against 16 exec
utive board members and the ex
ecutive secretary of the Joint Anti-
Fascist Refugee Committee, a New
York City organization.
