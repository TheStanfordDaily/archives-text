# Blind Vet Gives Up Seeing-Eye Dog
## 
### 
SAN DIEGO, April 7 (UP).-Ma
rino veteran William Snyder, 20,
blinded at Peleliu beachhead, to
night refused to accept the gift of
a Socing-Eye dog because others
"need it. more than I do."
Snyder told the Ladies of the
Grand Army of the Republic that
ho had been waiting for a dog only
three months and "some follows
have waited for years. They are
the ones who should be helped."
"The government takes care of its
veterans," Snyder said. "I'm getting
a guide-dog through the Veterans
Administration. Those ladies should
Rive that dog to someone who can't
afford to buy one. I can wait."
