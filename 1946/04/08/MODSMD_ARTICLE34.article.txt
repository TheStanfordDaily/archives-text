# Rail Mediation Board Ends Hearing Today
## 
### 
CHICAGO, April 7 (UP).—A fed
eral arbitration board neared com
pletion of a hearing on rail wages
and work rules today amid carrier
demands for increased rates and a
threat by two unions of a nation
wide rail strike over the rules issue.
