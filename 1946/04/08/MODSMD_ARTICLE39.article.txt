# John Jenks Aids Navy Tennis Win
## 
### 
John H. Jenks, former Stanford
student now an ensign in the Navy,
was one of a team of four Army and
Navy tennis players which de
feated the Philippine Davis Cup
team in a match held recently.
Jenks, whose family lives in Palo
Alto, had one quarter to finish be
fore graduation when he entered
the Navy. An engineering student,
he was president of the Stanford
chapter of the American Society of
Civil Engineers. He plans to re
turn to Stanford after his discharge.
