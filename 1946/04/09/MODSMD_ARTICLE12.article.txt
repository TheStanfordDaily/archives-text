# Women Grads to Meet In Clubhouse Thursday
## 
### 
Graduate women will meet in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse Thursday noon for the
first of their regular bi-weekly
luncheon meetings for this quar
ter. Luncheon will be 40 cents.
Specific plans will he discussed
for the April 27 informal dance;
and it is hoped that a large crowd
will attend, according to Jean
Levorscn. president of the grad
uate women's organization.
