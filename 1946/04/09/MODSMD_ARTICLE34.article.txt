# The 						Oaks Wins Union Plaque
## 
### 
The Oaks, with a grade - point
average of 2.74, had the highest
scholarship standing of any house
in Union Unit for winter quarter, it
was learned Sunday night when the
Scholarship Plaque was presented
to them. The announcement was
made by Dr. 11. Donald Winbigler,
Registrar, who made the presenta
tion at an informal dessert gather
ing following dinner Sunday eve
ning.
Ice cream was served in Union
Court prior to the presentation and
members of Union Unit provided
music during the program. Chris
Beck, president of Union, intro
duced Dr Winbigler. Carmen Fit
qua was in chargo of arranging the
program.
