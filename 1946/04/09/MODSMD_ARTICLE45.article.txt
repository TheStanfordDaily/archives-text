# Cubberley Invites Men to Informal
## 
### 
Cubberley House will entertain
Stanford veterans Friday, April 12
from 8 to 11:30. Invitations to the
open house have been sent to Har
rison, McKinley, Roosevelt, Van
Buren, and Garfield halls, but Ger
tie Keyston, social chairman, an
nounces that all veterans are wel
come. Cubberley is located at 569
LaSuen.
