# COOPER HACK
## 
### 
EVANSVILLE, Ind., April 8 (UP).
—Pitcher Morton Cooper rejoined
the Boston Braves today after an ab
sence of nine days for a business trip
to Kansas City, Mo., and said he was
in shape to take the mound at any
time. Cooper said he pitched 27 in
nings while he was away.
