STANFORD JAZZ SOCIETY will
meet for the first time this quar
ter tonight at 8 in the "Y" rooms
of the Women's Clubhouse. The
program of records will be pre
sented by Oscar Anderson and
Nancy Wright, and some impor
tant business will be discussed.
All old members and those inter
ested in joining the organization
are urged to attend.
PI LAMBDA THETA, women's
honorary educational fraternity,
will hold its second business
meeting of the quarter at 7:30 to
night in the social room of the
Education Building. At this meet
ing the committees appointed at
the April 4 meeting will report,
election of members for the April
26 initiation will be held, final
plans for the 20th anniversary
banquet on April 26 will be made,
and refreshments will be served
after the meeting.
