# Hot Session Due For Jazz Addicts
## 
### 
Jazz in the true New Orleans
tradition will be played by the
City Squires at the third hot
session of the Stanford Hot Jazz
Society Monday night at 8 in the
Nitery.
The program will be the first
public appearance of the group in
this area. Three of the musicians
are members of the Los Angeles
Jazz Society and are veterans of a
series ol jazz sessions in Holly\yood.
They have recorded six sides on
the Southland Jazz label under the
name of the Jewel City Stompers.
