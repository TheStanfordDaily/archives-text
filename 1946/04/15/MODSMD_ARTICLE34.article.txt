# MIDWEST MEAT SHORTAGE
## 
### 
CHICAGO, April 14 (UP).—The
meat shortage has become critical
in some Midwest and Eastern areas,
a survey showed tonight, as gov
ernment agencies moved to get
more meat and other foods back
onto the family dinner table.
