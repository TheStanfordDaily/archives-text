# FRAME HOUSE STOLEN
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 14 (UP).—
Dean Daly, manager of Metropoli
tan Airport, complained today to
police that a 15-foot by 20-foot par
titioned frame house located on the
airport grounds was stolen. He had
it for sale at $1)00.
