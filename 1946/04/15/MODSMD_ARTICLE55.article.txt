# Students to Make Tour of Mexico
## 
### 
An educational tour of Mexico
will highlight Stanford's summer
quarter program of South American
studies.
Dr. and Mrs. Juan B. Rael and
Dr. and Mrs. C. Langdon White will
be sponsors of a 40-student, 35-day
tour tentatively scheduled to leave
Palo Alto August 17, returning Sep
tember 21.
Students will visit Guadalajara,
Mexico City, Vera Cruz, Cordoba,
Oaxaca, Puebla, Taxco, and Cuer
navaca. The grand finale will be
the celebration of Mexican Inde
pendence Day in Mexico City on
September .16.
Interested students should apply
for still-open spots on the tour ros
ter to either Dr. White or Dr. Rael.
