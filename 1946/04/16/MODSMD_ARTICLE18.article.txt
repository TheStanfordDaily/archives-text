# Von kleinsmid, USC Head, Retires
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, April 15 (UP).—
Dr. Rufus B. Von Kleinsmid, retired
today after 25 years as president of
the University of Southern Califor
nia and was named to life tenure in
the new post of chancellor of the
university.
The board of trustees named him
acting president until a successor
can be chosen.
