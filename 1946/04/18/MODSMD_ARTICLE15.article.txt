# Vocom to Give Frosh Program
## 
### 
In a series of talks designed for
Roble women, Voconi will present,
the first of three programs Monday
evening when four women from the
Humanities Department will tell
about their majors and their work
connected with them.
Voconi has written to heads of
various departments asking for
recommendations and these four
women have been invited on the
basis of these recommendations,
Chairman Mary Ed Davis an
nounces.
Speakers invited are Marie Mc-
Gervey, drama; Betty Stellar, Pa
cific-Asiatic Relations, of the Hu
manities Department; Marjoric
Johnson, Romanic languages; and
Eunice Erb, English.
Miss Davis will be chairman of
the program, which will deal with
the speakers' choice of majors, their
work in other departments, and
main requirements in an attempt to
help Roble women choose their
majors.
Similar programs will be present
ed May 6 on science and May 20 on
social sciences.
