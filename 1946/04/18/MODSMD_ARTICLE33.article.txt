# Flying Club Holds Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford Flying Club members
will hold their first meeting of the
quarter tonight at 7:30 in the Nit -
ery, announced President Bob
Tones.
Representatives of a group wish
ing to establish a flying field for the
use of the club will be present to
outline their plan. An offer of spe
cial rates from a San Jose field will
also be discussed.
Arrangements will be made for
the showing of several reels of film
taken by club members while in
the service.
Any student interested in flying
who has not previously attended
club meetings will be welcomed to
night, according to Jones. Election
of officers will be held.
