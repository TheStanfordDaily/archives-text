# Pi Sigma Alpha Elects Neophytes
## 
### 
Pi Sigma Alpha elected ten new
members Wednesday afternoon and
appointed a permanent vocational
committee to assist seniors in po
litical science in obtaining contacts
for positions and other postgraduate
activities.
Elected members are Robert Bra
den, Helen English, Norman Fen
ton, Esdras Hartley, Ruth Levison,
George Kahin, Jerry Magner, Wil
lard P. Norberg, Tully Warren, and
Lawrence Weiler.
A dinner meeting May 8 at Long
barn will be held by Pi Sigma Al
pha.
