# Women to Name Fashion Models
## 
### 
House presidents are asked to
bring the names of the women from
their living groups who will model
in the Women's Conference Fash
ion Show, when Conference meets
at 3 p.m. today in the Women's
Clubhouse, Chairman Patty Pat
ten has announced.
The fashion show will be held at
3 p.m. Thursday, May 0, in Union
Court, and one woman from each
living group will model. Proceeds
from the show will go for the Con
ference scholarship fund. Palo Alto
stores will provide the clothes.
Housing Committee minutes will
be read and a discussion of the
Committee's two plans for a ratio
system will follow.
