# Red Cross Seeks Collectors' Items
## 
### 
Road maps and foreign stamps
have been requested by the Palo
Alto chapter of the American Red
Cross for use in entertaining the
servicemen in the hospitals.
Road maps, still war scarcities,
are desired by servicemen for plan
ning trips either at home or for
their first vacation as civilians. The
I stamps, both foreign and rare
| American issues, are wanted by the
servicemen for their collections.
According to the Red Cross, stamp
collecting and stamp-swapping with
other collectors are one of the "big
games" of convalescents.
A box has been placed in the
manager's office of the Daily Shack
where donations may be placed at
any time during the day. Maps and
stamps may also be mailed to or
, left with the local Red Cross ehap
j ter, 25 University Avenue.
