# Hawaiian Students Plan Closed Luau
## 
### 
Stanford's Hawaiian Club played
host to twenty members of its San
Jose counterpart last Thursday eve
ning. Starting out with a short bus
iness meeting, members of the Hui
Kupa Aina voted through plans for
a closed Luau to be given the latter
part of May.
Electric guitars and ukueleles fur
nished music for the joint meeting
in the Lower Lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
All Stanford students interested
in the islands are urged to attend
tonight's meeting at 7:30 in the
Clubhouse, when plans for a lei day
celebration will be discussed in a
short meeting.
