# Organist to Give Historical Recital
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist. will present the first in a
series of historical recitals today at
4:15 p.m. in Memorial Church.
Included in the program are Fan
tasy on the song "On frcudt Ver
ier," by Paulus Hofhaiiner; "Pleni
Sunt," by Konrad Brumann; Can
zone." by Andrea Gabrieli; "Ricer
care," by G. Piorluigi da Palestrina;
"Prelude and Fugue in G Minor"
and "Toccata for the Elevation." by
Girolamo Frcseobaldi; and "Eve
ning Hymn and Trumpet Tune and
Air." by Henry Purcell.
