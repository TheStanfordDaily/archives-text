# Court Capers All-Camp us
## 
### 
Everyone who likes to dance, play
ping pong, volleyball, or bridge is
invited to Union Residence's Court
Capers, Friday night from 8:30 to
11:30, according to the chairman of
the event, Loraine Pielhop. No ad
mission will be charged.
Dates Not Needed
The Capers will be an informal,
all-campus open house. Dancing
will be held under the arcades of
the Union Court. Colored lanterns
and spots will light the Court and
the volleyball games.
Students may come with or with
out dates, announced the committee
planning the affair. Hot dogs, ice
cream cones, and cokes will be sold
in booths in the court.
All Living Groups Invited
Every living group on campus has
received an invitation to the Capers,
but all off - campus students are
urged to attend the event, declared
Miss Pielhop. Union Residence has
tried to present varied entertain
ment to suit everyone on campus,
she added.
