# GARFIELD TO BE GUESTS AT HAWTHORNE PARTY
## 
### 
Hawthorne House has invited the
men from Garfield Hall to an in
formal party tonight from 8:30 to
12, announced Myrna Strock, chair
man of the affair. It is to be a closed
dance, and during the evening there
will be dancing, games .and re
freshments, said Miss Strock.
