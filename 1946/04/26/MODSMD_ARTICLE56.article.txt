# GRAD WOMEN WILL GIVE GARDEN PARTY AT KNOLL
## 
### 
Graduate women will hold a no
date dance called the "Grad Garden
Party" at The Knoll Saturday night
from 9 to 1. All graduate men, vet
erans, and several men's halls have
been invited, according to Martha
Stropes, chairman.
Refreshments will be served and
the terrace and garden will be open
to the guests. Card tables will be
set up for those who do not wish
to dance.
