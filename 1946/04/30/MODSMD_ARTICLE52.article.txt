# Signups Open For Intramural Tennis
## 
### 
Sign-ups for the men's intramural
tennis tournament have been ex
tended until Friday afternoon, with
the initial matches re-scheduled for
the following Monday.
Competition will be conducted
under a tournament elimination
system, while drawings will deter
mine the seeding. Two singles sets
and one doubles set will constitute a
match.
Thus far only eight organizations
are entered in the racquet battle,
but indications are for a much
larger turnout. Practice sessions
usually find men from Jefferson
Hall, Chinese Club, Encina Hash
ers, Fillmore Hall, Legal Eagles I
and 11. Graduate School of Business
Club, and Jackson Hall sharpening
their form for next week's opening
matches.
