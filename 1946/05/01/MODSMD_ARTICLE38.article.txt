# Second, Third Tennis Rounds Must Be Played
## 
### 
All mixed doubles teams in the
all-campus tennis tournament are
advised to play their second and
third round matches immediately,
Connie Wheeler announced.
Scores for the doubles tourna
ment must be posted in the men's
and women's gyms. First seeded in
the tournament are last year's win
ners, Grover Miller and Barbara
Beardslee.
