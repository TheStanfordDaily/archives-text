# Roy Bursch Heads Student Teachers
## 
### 
Roy Bursch has been elected
president of the Stanford chapter
of the California Student Teachers'
Association, recently formed on the
jampus. Henry Isaksen is vice
president and David Atkinson sec
retary-treasurer.
The first regular meeting of the
organization will be held Monday at
1 p.m. in room 7 of the Education
Building.
