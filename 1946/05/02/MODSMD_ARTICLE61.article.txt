# Law Opens Dining Hall for Russ Ball
## 
### 
For the first time since 1942, La-
gunita's dining hall will be the
scene of a Court dance. The "Bala
laika Ball" tomorrow night from 9
to 1 will be limited to Lagunita and
Ventura women and hashers.
Stan Robinson's orchestra will
play for the formal affair. White
candles and spring flowers will dec
orate the hall, while lively Russian
motifs will decorate the social
rooms and lobby of Lagunita, ac
cording to Bodo Reed, decorations
chairman.
Bids, selling for $3, can now be
purchased by special guests of La-
gunita.
