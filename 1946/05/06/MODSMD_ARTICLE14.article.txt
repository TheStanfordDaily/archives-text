# Navy to Recall V-E Wednesday
## 
### 
Headlines a year ago told of Ber
lin's fall, the German collapse in
Italy, and finally on May 8 the un
conditional surrender of Germany
to the United Nations.
In celebration of that memorable
day the Stanford N.R.O.T.C. unit is
planning a full day's program on
Wednesday. The naval armory in
the basement of the Education
Building will be open for inspec
tion by students interested in vari
ous types of Navy gear.
Movies will be shown in Cubbcr
ley Auditorium during the after
noon and at 4 p.m. the day's ac
tivities will be climaxed by a re
view on Harry Maloney Field.
Capt. Charles E. Crombe, Jr.,
head of the Navy training schools
at Stanford, has arranged the first
V-E Day anniversary celebration
for all Stanford students and fac
ulty members.
