# Conference to Appoint Nominating Committee
## 
### 
Women's Conference will choose
five senior women to serve on
Nominating Committee when it
meots today at 3 p.m. in the Wom
en's Clubhouse, according to Patty
Patten, chairman.
Nominating Committee will meet
May 14 and 15 to interview women
interested in running for women's
offices in the A.S.S.U. elections.
