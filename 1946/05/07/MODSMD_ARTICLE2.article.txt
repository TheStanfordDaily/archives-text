# Missionary Secretary Chapel Board Speaker
## 
### 
Miss Phyllis Rae Aden, supporter
of the missionary movement, will
speak to Chapel Board tonight at
its meeting at 9 in the vestry of
the Memorial Church. Miss Aden is
traveling secretary for the Student
Volunteer Movement.
Miss Aden was born in Buenos
Aires of missionary parents who
served as educational leaders in the
city. Her 18 years' experience in
Argentina gave her an apprecia
tion and understanding of Latin
American people.
