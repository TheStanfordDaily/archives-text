# Jackson Hall to Give Barbecue Saturday
## 
###

the house is returned to Chi Psi
fraternity, Jackson Hall will hold a
closed barbecue Saturday night at
6:30 for Jackson men and their ,
dates.

will be an all-campus dance to be
gin after the final curtain has been
rung down on "East Lynne."
