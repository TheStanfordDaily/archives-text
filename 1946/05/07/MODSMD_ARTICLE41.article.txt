# NROTC Rifle Squad Defeats New Mexico
## 
### 
The Stanford N.R.O.T.C. rifle
team defeated the University of
New Mexico squad in a three-posi
tion postal match, 1.254 to 1,202. Po
sitions used were prone, kneeling,
and off-hand.
Competing for Stanford were
Varner, Baier, Cook, Hardin, White,
Alton, Sharp, Roland, Boles, and
Petersen.
Harry Varner is captain of the
team, and the coach is Chief Gun
ner's Mate Soukey.
