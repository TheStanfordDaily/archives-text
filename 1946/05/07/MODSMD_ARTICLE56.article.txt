RALLY COMMITTEE'S MEETING
will be held tonight at 7:30 at
Russell House instead of tomor
row.
STANFORD BADMINTON CLUB
will meet tonight at 7 in the
Women's Gym.
ALL COMMITTEE CHAIRMEN of
the Women's Conference fashion
show will meet today at 5 p.m.
in the Women's Clubhouse.
MEN'S GLEE CLUB will meet to
night at 7 in the choir room of
the church. New members are
invited.
ALL MEMBERS of Kappa Alpha
fraternity are asked to attend the
meeting at the house at 7 p.m.
Thursday.

MEN will meet in front of the

make a tour of the Con Home.

off-campus women, will hold a
luncheon meeting today at noon.

hold a chapter meeting in Fill
more Hall Thursday evening at
7:30. All actives and pledges must
attend, under penalty of fine for
absence.
