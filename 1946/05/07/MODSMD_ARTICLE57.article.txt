# Frosh Bible Handout By Rally Com Still On
## 
### 
Frosh Bibles will be distributed
this morning from 10 to 12 in the
Cellar and on Engineering corner of
Quad. All students new to Stanford
this quarter and those returning
students who have not received
copies of the Bible may pick them
up today.
The handbook, which is being
distributed by Rally Com, is free. It
contains an outline of campus ac
tivities and organizations and many
Farm songs and yells.
