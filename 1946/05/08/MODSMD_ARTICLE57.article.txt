# Recital Set For 8 Tonight
## 
### 
Three Stanford students will pre
sent a recital under the auspices of
the Student Concert Association, a
division of Music Council, tonight
at 8 in the Women's Clubhouse.
Marianne Lion, a senior business
major of Cubberley House, will
sing the aria "One Fine Day"from
Madame Butterfly, and several Ne
gro spirituals. Miss Lion, a mezzo
soprano, will be accompanied by
John White, veteran and newcomer
to Stanford.
Pianist Ellen Larson, guest artist,
will play selections from Chopin,
McDowell, and Liszt. Miss Larson,
Cubberley House, is a sophomore
majoring in economics.
