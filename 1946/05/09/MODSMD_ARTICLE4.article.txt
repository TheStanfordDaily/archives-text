# NURSES' TEA FRIDAY
## 
###

class of nurses will be entertained
at a tea Friday in the Women's
Clubhouse at 3:45 p.m.
All women interested in the nurs
ing profession are invited to come
and meet the visiting women. Quad
clothes will be worn.
