# THURSDAY ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Warren D. Allen, University or
ganist, will feature music by J. S.
Bach in his weekly recital this af
ternoon at 4:15 in Memorial Church.
The Bach music he will play will be
"Prelude in B Minor"; "Prelude in
E Major"; "Chorale - Prelude. O
Guiltless Lamb of God"; "A Sav
ing Health to Us Is Brought";
"Come, Redeemer of Our Race";
and "Toccata in D Minor."
