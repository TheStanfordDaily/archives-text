# Chaplain to Give Mother's Day Talk
## 
### 
Merrimon Cuninggim, visiting
chaplain, will present a special
Mother's Day sermon at the 11
o'clock service Sunday in the Me
morial Church. The topic of the
sermon will be "Mother's Day Is
Done."
The program will include:
Organ prelude: "Air in D" by
Bach.
Introit: "What Wondrous Love Is
This, O My Soul?" early American
folk-hymn.
Anthem: "Grant Us Thy Peace"
by Gounod.
Anthem: "What Wondrous Love"
by A. Buchanan.
Sermon: Chaplain Cuninggim.
Readers: Mary Ellen Ayres and
Newton Arnold.
