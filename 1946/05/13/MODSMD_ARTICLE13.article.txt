# Candidates Interviewed
## 
### 
Nominating Committee will meet
in the Excom room tomorrow from
1 to 3 p.m. and Wednesday from 1
to 2 p.m. to interview nomination
candidates for offices limited to
women.
These offices arc the A.S.S.U.
vice-president, positions on Wom
en's Council, president, vice-presi
dent, and secretary of Women's
Conference, and Excom representa
tives of women's districts.
Students who arc nominated by
the committee do not need to take
out a petition, and those whom the
committee does not nominate are
urged to take out a petition for their
nomination. No distinction will be
made between those nominated by
petition and those by the committee.
Committee members arc Char
mian Kolar, A.S.S.U. vice-presi
dent; Patty Patten, chairman of
Women's Conference; Barbara
Bcardslcc, chairman of Women's
Council; Peg Miller, senior presi
dent of Roble; and five other sen
ior women, Beth Mac Vicar, Chris
Beck, Anita Zimmerman, Mary
Martin, and Nancy Banning.
