# Student Recital Features Pianist
## 
### 
The Stanford Music Council will
present a student recital featuring
pianist Newton Munro Merrick of
Encina Wednesday night at 8 in the
auditorium of the Women's Club
house.
When living in New York, Merrick
studied with Stewart Robb, fellow
of the Royal College of Music, Lon
don, and Frank LaForge, interna
tionally famous composer, pianist,
and coach.
