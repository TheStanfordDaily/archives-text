# OPA LIFTS PRICE CONTROL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. May 12 (UP), -
The OPA tonight lifted price con
trols on a variety of goods and serv
ices. including custom fabricating of
certain types of whisky barrels,
crinoline cheese cloths, and some
photography services.
