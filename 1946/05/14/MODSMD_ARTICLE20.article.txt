# GRADUATE WOMEN
## 
### 
Graduate women will give a
bridge party at the Knoll Friday at
8 p.m. The bridge is open to the
campus. Prizes will be given. First
prize is two dinners at L'Omelette,
and there will be a booby prize as
well. Punch will be served during
the evening.
Reservations can be made with
Martha St ropes, 8875, or with Mad
eline Brill 2-1108. Price is 25 cents.
