# Hiking Club Explores Butano Forest Sunday
## 
### 
Hiking Club will sponsor a hike
on Sunday, May 19, in Butano
Forest near Pescadcro, according
to President Ed Omit/. The group
will leave the Education Build
ing promptly at 8 a.m.
Anyone wishing to go should
phone Frances Knausc at Bran
ner by Wednesday evening so
that lunches and transportation
may be arranged. As cars are
greatly needed, those who can
provide one should notify Frances
Knausc. All hikers arc asked to
