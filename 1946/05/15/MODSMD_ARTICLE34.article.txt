# Con Home Golfers To Clash Tomorrow
## 
### 
The nine-hole mixed two-ball
foursome for the Con Home Drive
will be played tomorrow after
noon at the Stafford golf course,
according to Dickie Shainwald,
tournaments manager.
Following the play-offs, prizes
donated by Palo Alto stores and
the Cellar will be awarded.
Students who have not turned
in their entry fee of 50 cents
which covers the green fee. should
do so in the A.S.S.U. office today
from 1 to 2 p.m., Miss Shainwald
said.
