# Navy Baseball Nears Finals
## 
### 
As the finals of the navy intra
mural league draw near, the Halsey
unit established themselves as
prime threats to walk oft with the
championship with unexpected ease.
Teeing off against the NAS unde
feated nine, the Halsey baseballers
coasted into a 9-G victory. Scoring
7 runs in the first canto and with
Will Walker spacing 4 hits the Hal
sey boys stepped into the finals of
the Softball round.
Elsewhere on the sporting scene,
Halsey again was involved this time
reaching the finals of the volleyball
contest by downing the Staff six 2-0
in hard-fought games. This victory
added with the softball win gives
Halsey three places in the finals.
Only the Amphibs and NAS team
seems to have a chance to grab the
crown.
On the slate for this weekend is a
baseball tiff pitting the strong Am
phibs against the once vanquished
NAS combo. Amphibs, a hard-hit
ting outfit, will meet a strong field
ing and good pitching nine in the
NAS.
