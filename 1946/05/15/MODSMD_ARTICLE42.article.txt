# Hackett Plays Saturday At Gay Nineties Dance
## 
###

Gay Nineties dance to he given
Saturday from !> to 1. Ray Hack
ett will supply the music. Deco
rations will carry out the Nine
ties theme with can-can dancers
and tandem bicycles. Tickets arc
$2 and will be sold at the A.S.S.U.
office.
