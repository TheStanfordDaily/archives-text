Day .Chairman Patty Robinson is pinning on Boh Kennedy, A.S.S.U.
president. The tags are white with red printing and are on sale today
for 10 cents, all money going to (he Con Home Drive.
Salesmen will be stationed at History and Engineering corners, the
Cellar, Post OfTicc, and in front of both Lihes.
