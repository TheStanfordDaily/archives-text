# HEAD STUDENT POLICEMAN
## 
### 
A head student policeman is
needed immediately, according to
B. J. Calfee, student manager, who
will take applications in the A.S.S.U.
office. The "head cop'' receives a
salary of 90 cents an hour and is
in charge of organizing policemen
for student functions where they
are on call.
Regular student police are also
wanted. They receive 75 cents an
hour.
