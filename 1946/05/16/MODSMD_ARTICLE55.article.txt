# BULLETIN BOARD
## 
### 
AETNA LIFE INSURANCE will
have a representative on campus
Thursday, May 16. Anyone in
terested in speaking with him
should contact Mrs. Marx of the
Appointment Service, phone 9411,
local 288.
DIKING CLUB will hike to Butano
Forest this Sunday. The group
will leave from the Education

Building promptly at 8 a.m. Hik
ers are asked to bring $1 to cover
expenses.
STANFORD FLYING CLUB will
hold a meeeting tonight at 7:30 in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse. Movies showing air
planes in action will be shown.
BETA CHI'S will meet at Cleveland
at 7:30 tonight.
HAWAIIAN CLUB Luau tickets
have been reserved and must be
picked up from Ellie French at
Elm Cottage before Monday.
MEN STUDENTS are needed for
student policing at 75 cents an
hour. Any man interested should
call Jim Shechy at 24511 or leave
his name at the A.S.S.U. office.
DEBATE SOCIETY members will
meet at 4 p.m. today.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE Advis
ory Board will not meet today.
SONS AND DAUGHTERS of Stan
ford alumni who are planning on
attending the traditional barbecue
Sunday should call Betty Wel
lington at 9043 or Max King and
Ken Dole at 8837.
