# CARFARES GOING UP
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. May 10 (UP).
—Street car and bus fares in San
Francisco will be increased from
seven cents to 10 cents or three
for-a-quartcr tokens effective to
morrow at 5 a.m.—for a few days,
at least.
Legality of the increased fares
will be tested Tuesday in Superior
Court here.
