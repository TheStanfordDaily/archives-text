# HOWARD SIGNED AS COACH
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, May 19 (UP).
—Bill Howard, well-known grid
coach and former USC quarterback
under Howard Jones, today was
signed as head coach of the San
Francisco Clippers of the Pacific
Coast Professional Football League.
