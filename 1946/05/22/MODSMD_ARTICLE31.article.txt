# Conservation Letters To Be Given at Rally
## 
### 
Stanford students will receive
food conservation letters at to
day's assembly to send home to
their parents for support in the
drive to save food for Europe's
and Asia's starving peoples, ac
cording to Chris Beck, letter
author.
Students are asked to urge their
parents to answer to the A.S.S.IJ.
in support of Stanford's "small
limited part" in the nationwide
campaign.
