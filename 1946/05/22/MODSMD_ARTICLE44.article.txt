# Berg Demonstrates Golf Swings Today
## 
### 
Patty Berg, champion woman
golfer, will be on campus this af
ternoon from 1:15 to 3 to demon
strate shots and swings on the
hockey field beside the Women's
gym-
While she was a lieutenant in
the Marine Corps, Miss Berg was
in an automobile accident that
threatened to leave her physically
handicapped, but she was deter
mined to recover. After months
of slow practice she was ready to
play championship golf again.
Miss Berg, twice winner of the
National Women's Championship
Tournament, is sponsored by the
W.A.A. and the physical educa
tion instructors for women. All
students interested are invited to
see Miss Berg's exhibition today.
