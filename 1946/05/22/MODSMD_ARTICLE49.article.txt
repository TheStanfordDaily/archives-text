# Cal Phi Psi's Deny Hatchet Participation
## 
### 
The Stanford Axe is still in the
hands of a group of University of
California students today as the
police investigation of the theft of
the famed trophy went ahead.
The Daily Californian reported
that a group of Stanford students
attempted to negotiate for the re
turn of the Axe with a California
fraternity on Friday. May 10, and
then again on Tuesday, May 14.
The fraternity involved, the Phi
Psi's. denied that they were par
ticipants in the theft, the Daily
Californian reported.
