# Telephone Scout At Stanford Today
## 
### 
Stanford women interested in
working for the telephone company
will be interviewed by Mrs. Martha
Heise, representative of the San
Francisco employment office of the
Pacific Telephone and Telegraph
Company, today.
Mrs. Heise, the former Martha
Phillips, class of '41, will be in
room 120 of the Graduate School of
Business at 3:30 p.m.
