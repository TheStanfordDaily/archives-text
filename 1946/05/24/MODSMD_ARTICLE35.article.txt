# Imported Island Dishes At Hawaiian Club Luau
## 
### 
Stanford's Hawaiian Club went
ahead with their plans today for an
authentic Island Luau feast tomor
row afternoon, from 2 to 7.
The luau will be held at the boat
house and will feature Hawaiian
food, guitar playing, and Island at
mosphere. The food for the occa
sion has been imnorted and will
feature, among other things, several
poi dishes.
