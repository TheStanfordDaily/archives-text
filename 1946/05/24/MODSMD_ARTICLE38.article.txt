# HARMON SHOOTS 67
## 
### 
SPOKANE. Wash., May 23 (UP).
—Sid Harmon kept the town of
Walla Walla, Wash., on top of the
$1,500 P.G.A. Northwest Open Golf
Championship today by shooting a
five-under-par 67 for a 134 total at
the halfway mark in the tourna
ment.
