# Conference Meet Honors Dean Yost
## 
### 
Miss Mary Yost, dean of women,
will be guest of honor at the last
meeting of Women's Conference to
day at 3 p.m. in the Women's Club
house.

Mrs. Donald B. Tresiddcr will
honor Miss Yost "at home" to
morrow from -1 to 5:510 p.m. at (12.'!
Minula Street.

and will present the changes for
Conference approval.
