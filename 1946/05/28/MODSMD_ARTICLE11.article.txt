# CAP AND GOWN ELECTIONS
## 
### 
Marie Wagner was elected presi
dent of Cap and Gown Honor So
ciety at its Friday night meeting.
Beth Mac Vicar is new vice-presi
dent, Nada Skidmore, secretary, and
Alice Lovett, treasurer.
A Cap and Gown banquet was
held at l'Omelette Sunday night for
actives and alumnae of the organ
ization, the first since before the
war.
