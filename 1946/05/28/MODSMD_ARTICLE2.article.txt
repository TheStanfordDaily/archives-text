professor of geography in Stanford's
School of Humanities, will relate !
some first-hand stories of pioneer- j
ing in Siberia, gathered from five !
trips across the wasteland, at the j
Tuesday Series tonight at 7:30 in j

color slides by Dr. Cresscy, who j
was wartime consultant to the State
Department, the Board of Economic I
Warfare, and the U.S. military in- j
telligence and is ?n authority on

his many years of Asiatic travel and

today; "Asia's Lands and Peoples,"
and "China's Geographic Founda
tion."
