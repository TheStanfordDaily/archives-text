# Rally Com Dance Set for Saturday
## 
### 
All Farm students are invited to
Rally Corn's free sport dance, Sat
urday night from 9 to 1, at the Knoll,
Chancellor Wilbur's former home.
In keeping with a "Carrousel"
theme, merry-go-round horses and
a carnival atmosphere will highlight
the decorations. Dancing will be
held in the ballroom of the mansion
and in the patio behind the home.
Stan Robinson and his 11-piece
band will play for the dance in their
second appearance at a Farm event.
They were first heard at the last
Lagunita formal.
