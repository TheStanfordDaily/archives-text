# Educators to Meet At Farm in July
## 
### 
Featuring educational leaders of
national reputation, a three-day
conference on "Leadership in Ad
ministration" will be held at the
Stanford University School of Edu
cation July 18, 19, 20, it has been
announced by Acting Dean Lucien
B. Kinney.
Keynote speakers at the summer
conference will be Dr. Harold Ben
jamin, director of the international
educational relation division, U. S.
Office of Education; Dr. Kenneth W.
MeFarland, superintendent of
Schools, Topeka, Kan.; Dr. Edwin
A. Lee, dean of the University of
California at Los Angeles School
Education; and Prof. William H.
Cowley of the Stanford School of
education.
Subjects to be discussed in gen
eral meetings will be the leadership
role of administration, personnel
responsibilities of administrators,
international responsibilities for ed
ucation, and the roles of vocational
and general education. Section
meetings will be held on other lead
ership problems.
A related feature event this sum
mer will be the Seminar in Educa
tional Leadership and Administra
tion, which will be offered in two
four-week parts during the eight
week Stanford School of Education
summer session, June 20 to August
16.
