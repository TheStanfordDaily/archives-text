# MAN GUMS TOES
## 
### 
MOULTRIE, Ga., May 29 (UP).—
All morning it seemed to Henry Foot
that his feet felt funny.
Removing his shoes for.the sec
ond time, he found his toes sticking
together. Instead of foot powder,
he had used adhesive dental pow
der, for holding false teeth.
