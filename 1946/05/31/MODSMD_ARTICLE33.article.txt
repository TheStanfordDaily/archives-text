# 27 Awarded Circle Block

### 
Twenty-seven men were awarded
Circle Block "S" awards for partici
pation in intercollegiate athletic
competition at Excom Wednesday
night, following recommendation by
the men's instructors.

wins the majority of his bouts
against California, or who wins the
majority of his bouts in Foil, Epee,
or Saber in the Pacific Coast Inter
collegiate Fencing Meet," were given
to James H. Crooker, Wade H.
Hover, David C. White, and Fred
