# Union Breakfast To Honor Seniors
## 
### 
Union Unit will hold its annual
Senior Breakfast Sunday when
women of the Unit will gather in
the main Union dining room at 10
a.m. Seniors will be honored with
a program and gifts, announces
Jackie Boucher, general chairman.
Miss Pat Davidson and Mrs. Har
old M. Bacon are to be the guest
speakers, speaking to the seniors
and the engaged girls respectively.
Included on the program will be a
skit written and presented by the
junior waitresses and a song writ
ten by Jane Frasier and Jody Le
May for the engaged girls.
