# Dr. Sharp Will Start Year's Leave in Fall
## 
### 
Dr. Stanley L. Sharp, director of
men's residences, has been granted
a year's leave of absence, starting
next September 1. Dr. Sharp has
been connected with the University

for the past 17 years. He held the
position of men's residence director
during the last four years.
He plans to leave on an extended
vacation at the conclusion of sum
mer quarter.
