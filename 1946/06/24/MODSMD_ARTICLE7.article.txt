# Three Grads Win Journalism Award
## 
### 
Three Stanford women were
among the winners of the Sigma
Delta Chi annual Scholarship
Award, according to an announce
ment by the journalism fraternity's
vice-president in charge of under
graduate affairs, Professor Frank
Thayer.
Farm graduates receiving the
certificate were: Clara Forzano. Pa
tricia Patten and Joan Hodgdon.
One hundred and three men and
women journalism students through
out the nation at schools where
Sigma Delta Chi chapters are locat
ed qualified for the distinction which
takes into consideration all four
years' work.
