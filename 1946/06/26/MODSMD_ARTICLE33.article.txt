# The 						Ordeal Is Over But Dad's Unknown
## 
### 
Truca Greencross. cat. gave
birth to two kittens, one gray and
white and one with a touch of
brown, yesterday afternoon at 2.
Campus followers of Mrs. Green
cross' pregnancy, one of the long
est in the memory of local experts
in such matters, will be glad to
know that mother and offspring
are doing well. The father is still
unknown.
