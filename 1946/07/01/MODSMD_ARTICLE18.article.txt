# Physics Department Gets Science Library
## 
### 
The library of the Stanford Chap
ter of the Northern California Asso
ciation of Scientists is now located
in the Physics Department library
room. Art Berrnan, secretary of the
chapter, announced yesterday.
The NCAS. affiliated with the
Federation of American Scientists,
was formed to unite all scientists in
the area to make the public aware
of the consequences of poor na
tional and international policy in
vital matters such as atomic control.
Berrnan said.
Literature on file in the new li
brary includes the text of the Ache
son report, copies of the official pub
lication of the atomic scientists and
NCAS literature, he said.
