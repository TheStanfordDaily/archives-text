# EVELYN SCHILLING ELECTED
## 
### 
Evelyn Schilling was elected
president of the Upsilon chapter of
Pi Lambda Theta. honorary associ
ation for women, at a meeting held
Thursday night in the Education
Building. Lois H. Flint, retiring
president, presided over the meet
ing. during which plans for the com
ing year Were discussed and new
officers elected. The next meeting
will be held on Wednesday.
