# 'Scandal' Tryouts Scheduled Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for "School for Scandal,''
Richard Sheridan's comedy of 18th
century England, will be held to
night at 7:30 in the main audito
rium. Norman Philbrick. director,
announced today.
The cast calls for 13 male and 7
female characters. All those trying
out should read the play first if pos
sible.
"School for Scandal" will be the
second production of the Stanford
Players' summer season. It will be
presented in the main auditorium
of Memorial Hall on August 15. 16,
and 17.
