# Students Wanted For Football Jobs
## 
### 
Interesting and remunerative jobs
are available to students for football
games in Stanford Stadium this fall,
according to an announcement from
the Athletic Department.
Applications are desired at once in
the Athletic Department Building,
room 26, Monday through Friday,
from 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m., and Satur
day from 8:30 a.m. to 12 noon.
Many types of positions arc to be
filled in the following departments:
ticket selling, ticket taking, traffic
supervision, policing, ushering, pro
gram sales, and general clerking.
