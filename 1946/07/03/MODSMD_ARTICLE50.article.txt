# Cafeteria Opens Summer Schedule
## 
### 
To alleviate the noon rush at the
Cellar, a cafeteria is being operated
during summer quarter. Located in
the Union Residence dining hall to
the right of the main lobby, the
cafeteria is open to the public, and
serves salads, drinks, and hot dishes.
Other eating facilities at the Union
are the dining room, to the left of
the main lobby, open for both the
noon and evening meals; and the
Cellar, which is operated almost
continuously except for brief clean
up periods "in the morning and af
ternoon. Sandwiches and milk are
available at the Cigar Store at noon.
