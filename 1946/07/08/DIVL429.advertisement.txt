AIRLINE OPENINGS
FOR
COLLEGE MEN AND WOMEN
Air transportation is expanding rapidly today and offers col lege men
.'iiid women the opportunity to start an interesting career with great pos
sibilities for advancement.
I nitcd Air Lines is especially interested in obtaining young men and
women with college background to work in its passenger and traffic dc
partnicnt. In its downtown San Francisco and Oakland offices openings
exist for counter sales agents and reservations agents. At the San Fran
cisco Airport, United needs .young men as passenger agents. For these
positions the airline needs intelligent, courteous, and diplomatic young
people, age 20 to 30.
The positions offer advancement to senior passenger agents, counter
sales agents and reservations agents, and other supervisory positions.
1 nitcd s employees receive such benefits as half fare air travel, vaca
tion with pay, periodic wage adjustment, and an insurance plan.
Apply in person. Monday through Friday at -121 Powell St., or S. F.
Airport.
