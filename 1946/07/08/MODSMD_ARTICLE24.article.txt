# Carlson Honored With AMA Medal
## 
### 
Dr. A. J. Carlson, Stanford
trained physiologist, was awarded
the American Medical Association's
Distinguished Service Medal for
1946 at the opening session of the
association's house of delegates.

The award recognized his emi
nence as an authority on the physi
ology of the heart and circulation.
Dr. Carlson was graduated from
Stanford in ITO3.
