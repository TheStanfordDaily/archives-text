# House of 7 Gables' On Tuesday Series
## 
### 
The motion picture, "The House
of Seven Gables," will be shown
tomorrow night at 8 in Memorial
Hall auditorium as the regular
Tuesday Evening Scries program.
This screen version of Nathaniel
Hawthorne's classic shows only
slight changes from the original
story. George Sanders and Marga
ret Lindsay star in portraying this
story of a curse that persisted
through the ages and a love that
could not be defeated by misfor
tune and disaster.
Tuesday Evening Series programs
are open to the public and there is
no charge for admission.
