# First Meeting Planned For International Club
## 
### 
Mr. Kurban, member of the work
ing committee of the All - India
Trade Union Congress and vice
president of the Indian Railway
men's Federation, will talk on "In
dia aud Its Labor Movement" to
night at 8:30 at the Women's Club
house.
At 7:30, prior to Mr. Kurban's
talk, the International Club will
hold its first meeting of the quarter
in the alumni room. Activities for
the remainder of the quarter will
be planned and new officers will be
elected.
Old members and all persons in
terested in international problems
are invited to attend both the club
meeting and the address. American
as well as foreign students are wel
come in International Club, accord
ing to Ganti Subrahmanyam. chair
man pro tem.
