# Second-best Rag Blurbs for Aid
## 
### 
Do you want to make oodles? Do
you want money to count when
you haven't anything else to do?
Are the moths in ydur wallet com
plaining about the current food
shortage?
Chaparral's Old Boy Frank Q.
Hewitt says, "Copious quantities of
monetary reimbursement are avail
able." In order to gain these highly
desirable financial ends one must
only write, draw, or think up a
humorous thing. For example, any
thing.
Mail your material (cartoons, car
toon ideas, stories, poems or your
little sister), carry it, or let it walk
itself to the Chaparral office.
