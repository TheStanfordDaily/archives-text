# Vaccination Forms Are Due Thursday
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due
at the Registrar's office tomorrow
for all new students, including those
having permits to attend classes, and
all others whose certificates have
expired.
Failure to file certificates by to
morrow will result in a fine of $2
or. if not filed by Thursday. July 23,
registration may be canceled by the
Committee on Public Health.
