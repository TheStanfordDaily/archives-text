# Photo Contest Won By 'Bookstore' Study
## 
### 
Bradley Breyman won the pho
tography contest sponsored by the
Cap and Gown Calendar with his
camera study of the Stanford Book
store. First prize was $20. Second
prize went to Martin Hoist, and

Philip Welch won third prize of $10.
Clarence Olso received honorable
mention.
The contest was judged by James
McClure and was conducted to gel
material for the fall Cap and Gowr,
Calendar.
