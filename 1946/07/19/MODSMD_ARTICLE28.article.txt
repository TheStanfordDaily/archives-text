# One-Acts to Try 'Center Staging'
## 
### 
Rehearsals for the three experi
mental one-act plays which will be
presented July 31 and August 1 in
the Nitery got under way in Me
morial Hall yesterday.
The three plays, "Winter's End,"
"The Scheming Lieutenant" and
"Fumed Oak,l will be experiments
in center staging. They will be per
formed in the middle of the Nitery
with the audience seated on all four
sides of the actors.
