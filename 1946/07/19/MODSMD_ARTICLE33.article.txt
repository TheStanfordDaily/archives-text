# Former Instructor New Chicago Prof
## 
### 
James V. Cunningham, former in
structor in English literature at
Stanford and at present an assistant
professor at the University of Ha
waii, has accepted a position as as
sistant professor of English at the
University of Chicago, it was
learned here this. week.
Cunningham is the author of "The
Helmsman." a book of poems. He
received his doctorate and master's
degree at Stanford. He assisted
James Gunn in writing "Deadlier
than the Male" several years ago.
Mrs. Cunningham, the former Do
lores Galliger, also from Stanford,
will accompany Professor Cunning
ham east.
