# 'Christian Community' Is Sunday's Subject
## 
### 
Sunday morning, July 21. at 11, in
the Memorial Church, Elbert C.
Cole, the visiting chaplain, will
preach a sermon entitled "The
Christian Community."
The anthem, "Let Hearts Awak
en," will be played, and Marilyn
Cleek will be the soloist for the
hymn, "The Silent Sea."
