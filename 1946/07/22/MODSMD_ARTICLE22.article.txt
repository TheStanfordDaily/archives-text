# Fund Established As Fitch Memorial
## 
### 
A fund in memory of Mrs. Wil
liam Fitch, who died July 3 at the
Palo Alto Hospital, is being estab
lished at Stanford. It will be used
either for a scholarship or loan fund
or to provide records for the music
listening room and will be known
as the Mary Ann Erwin Fitch Fund.
Gifts toward the fund are being
received from friends of Mr. and
Mrs. Fitch and the Erwin family by
Miss Jean Stuart, 450 Santa Rita
Avenue, Palo Alto.
Mrs. Fitch had spent her life on
the Stanford campus. The daughter
of Mr. and Mrs. Ernest Erwin of
Stanford, she attended Castilleja
School and was graduated from
Stanford.
