# Second Lag Whirl Has 'Farm' Theme
## 
### 
'"The Farm" will be the theme of
the second Lagunita open house to
night from 7:30 to 11:30 p.m. Cider,
milk and doughnuts will be served
and unless it rains there will be
dancing in the court.
Dancing in the Magnolia and Eu
calypto social rooms will include a
mixer and other novelty dances.
Entertainment will consist of ping
pong and bridge games.
