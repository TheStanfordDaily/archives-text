# Rubin to Discuss China Experiences
## 
### 
Jerome Rubin will discuss his ex
periences in Chungking and Shang
hai during 1945 at the meeting of
the Chinese - American Friendship
Association Monday at 4:15 p.m. in
the Women's Cubhouse, according
to Margaret Caiter, president of the
association.
The informal meeting will also
feature some short readings of Chi
nese poetry by Mrs. Lungshin Wu,
instructor in Chinese at Stanford.
The program has been arranged
by Fred Vernon, vice-president, and
is open to any student or professor
who is interested in furthering Chi
nese-American friendship and un
derstanding.
Refreshments will be served.
