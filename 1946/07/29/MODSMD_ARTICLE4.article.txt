Maurice, San Francisco apartment

$66,517 estate to the Stanford Uni

research, it was learned yesterday
when the instrument was filed for
probate.
The will established the fund in
memory of Mrs. Maurice who died

According to Dr. Loren R. Chan

immediate plans have been formu
lated for use of the bequest. Dr.
Ludwig Einge, clinical professor of
obstetrics and gynecology, has been

number of years.
