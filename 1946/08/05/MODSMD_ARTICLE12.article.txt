# Bach, Weinberger On Organ Program
## 
### 
The weekly organ recital of D.
Sterling Wheelwright, assistant or
ganist, Thursday afternoon at 4:15
in Memorial Church will include the
"St. Anne" Fugue of Bach and the
"Bible Poems" of Weinberger.
The programs are open to the
public without charge and are pre
ceded at 4 by a short talk on the
works to be played by Dr. Wheel
wright.
Heard as guest organist for the
monthly musicale at Memorial
Church last night was Myron J.
Roberts. He was assisted by so
prano Marjorie Benson Dickenson
and the University Summer Choir
under the direction of Mr. Wheel
wright.
