# Encina Men Plan Adobe Creek Party
## 
### 
Encina men will offer a complete
afternoon and evening of swimming,
tennis, badminton, and dancing next
Friday from 3 to 1 at their Adobe
Creek Lodge party. All Encina men
are reminded that they must sign
up for the party in Dr. Sharp's of
fice before tonight if they want to
attend.
The Encina Club dance, for En
cina men and their dates only, will
be a trial-run for future off-campus
affairs.
Refreshments will be served in
the form of hot dogs, potato salad,
ice cream, and Coca-Cola.
