# Petition Passes For 'Peace Day'
## 
### 
An annual international holiday
to express the will of the people of
the United Nations for peace is now
being circulated by members of
Excom.
The petition is addressed to James
F. Byrnes and came from the Com
mittee for Peace Day in the United
Nations in Boston, Mass.
It states in part that an "annual
international holiday will strength
en and make effective the determi
nation of the people of the United
Nations to practice tolerance, to live
together in peace and to unite their
strength to maintain international
peace and security."
It further urges representatives of
the United Nations present for adop
tion at the next session a proposal
for the establishment of such an
annual international holiday.
