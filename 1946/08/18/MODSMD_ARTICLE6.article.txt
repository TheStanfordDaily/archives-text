# Dr. Paul Hanna to Plan Children's Publication
## 
### 
Dr. Paul R. Hanna, professor of
education, has been chosen to plan
the first draft of a publication by
the Educational Policies Commis
sion, entitled "'Education for All
American Children."
Dr. Hanna will leave for Wash
ington, D.C., Sept. 1 to spend three
weeks with the permanent staff of
the commission.
