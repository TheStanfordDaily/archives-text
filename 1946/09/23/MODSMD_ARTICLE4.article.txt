# Tickets for Big Game, U.S.C. Sell This Week
## 
### 
Student body football tickets for
the Southern California and Cali
fornia tjames go on sale at the Bas
ketball Pavilion Thursday and Fri
day of this week and Monday and
Tuesday of next week, from 8:30
a.m. to 5 p.m. This is the only time
tickets to these games will be avail
able; no student tickets will be sold
at the stadiums.
With the exception of the above
mentioned games, all students will
j be admitted to the remaining games
| upon presentation of their student
body cards.
A charge of $1.50 will be made
for the Oct. 26 game with S.C. here,
and $1.80 for the "Big Game" at
Berkeley Nov. 23. There will be no
charge for the U.C.L.A. game at Los
Angeles Oet. 12. but a special ticket
must be secured in advance. Season
tickets for persons not members of
s the student body may be obtained
I at the S.A.C. office for $10.80.
