# Grid Manager Offers Rewards for Helpers
## 
### 
Murray Prusmack, the head
football manager, is looking for
men to work as assistants. The
jobs satisfy the lower division
physical education requirement,
and there is a chance some will
make trips with the team and
eat at the training table. All
will get complementary tickets
to the gsmes. Prusmack will see
all applicants at 4 p.m. today in
the football dressing room of
Encina Gym.
