# Dance Band Needs Vocalist Players
## 
### 
Rehearsals for a new all-campus
dance band will be held next Wed
nesday night at 7:15 at The Knoll
and every Wednesday night there
after, according to Howard Mai'tin,
who will manage the band assisted
by Jack Little and Jim Sheehy.
All former members of the Toyon
band as well as all other student in
strumentalists are invited to appear
at the first rehearsal to audition for
membership.
Auditions for vocalists, other
than those especially scheduled
with Jack Little at 42 Encina, will
be held from 8:30 to 10 Saturday
morning at The Knoll.
