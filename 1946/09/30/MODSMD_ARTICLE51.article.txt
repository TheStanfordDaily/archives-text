WOMEN'S TENNIS CLUB tryouts
will be held today from 1 to 3 p.m.
and Wednesday from 4 to 6 p.m.
at the women's tennis courts.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE members
will meet at 3 p.m. today in the
Women's Clubhouse, announced
Betty Davis, president. Miss Elva
Brown, new Chief Counselor, will
speak.
PHI KAPPA PSI will meet tonight
at 8 at the house. All members
are requested to attend.
PHI PHI. fraternity men's honorary
fraternity, will meet today at 1
p.m. at the D.U. house.
EL TOKO members will hold a
meeting tonight at 7 in the north
eastern social room of Toyon.
ALL SIGMA NU'S, including grad
uates and transfers, are urged to
attend an important meeting at
the Sigma Nu house tonight at
7:30 according to Ed Wiiford.

mulate plans for the tea to wel
come all new Stanford women,
announces Alice Hanssen. All
members are asked to attend.

4:30 Tuesday afternoon in the
journalism library. All members
arc asked to attend this important
meeting.
