# Dr. Tresidder Plans Concert
## 
### 
A weekly record concert for all
members of the "Stanford Family"
will be inaugurated tomorrow eve
ning at 7:30 in the living room of
the home of Dr. and Mrs. Tresidder.
The program, entitled "Music at
Home" will be under the direction
of James Schwabaeher, assistant in
the Department of Music and a
founder of the San Francisco Sym
phony Forum. The first program
will feature Arturo Toscanini con
ducting the NBC orchestra in the
brilliant "Italian in Algiers" and
Serge Koussevitzky conducting the
Boston Symphony in selections from
Moussorgsky's "Pictures at an Exhi
bition" in the original transcription
for orchestra by Ravel.
Marian Anderson, American con
tralto, will -sing two arias from the
Messiah as well as the song which
she hs made her own, Schubert's
'Ave Maria."
The program will begin promptly
at 7:30 and will last one hour. All
Stanford students, faculty members
and villagers are cordially invited to
attend.
