# Managers, Cooks Meet Thursday
## 
### 
House managers and cooks of
the fraternities and housemothers
and cooks of the women's houses
are requested to meet Thursday at
2 p.m. in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse to discuss new
commissary procedures, Interfrater
nity Council has announced.
The problems of purchasing sup
plies, obtaining delivery, and other
questions regarding the use of the
new Stanford Village commissary
will be considered. Mr. Carl D.
Rutledge will speak.
It was announced that Interfra
ternity Council has purchased a
truck for the use of all houses in
delivering supplies.
