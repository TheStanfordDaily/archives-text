# HULL'S CONDITION CRITICAL
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 3 (UP).—
Cordell Hull suffered a second re
lapse in Bethesda Naval Hospital
and his doctors said today that the
75-year-old former Secretary of
State, who suffered a stroke Mon
day night, was in a "most critical"
condition.
