# Wimbledon Champ Downed by Cochell
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 3 (UP),
j —Earl Cochell, unseeded Los An
geles net star, turned in the major
I upset of the Pacific Coast Tennis
Championships here today when he
defeated Yvon Petra, the French
star and Wimbledon champion, 6-4,
6-2. Cochell was in front all the
| way in both sets and the tiring
j Frenchman failed to fight back.

National champion Jack Kramer
meanwhile advanced to the quarter
finals with an easy 6-4, 6-2 victory
I over Hugh Stewart, Los Angeles,
and will meet Lennart Bergelin,
Swedish star tomorrow. Bergelin
moved ahead with a 0-6, 7-5. 6-3
victory over Sam Match, Los An
geles.
