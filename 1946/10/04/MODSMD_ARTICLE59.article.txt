# Pi Lambda Theta Plans First Meeting of Year
## 
### 
Pi Lambda Theta, women's hono
rary education society, will hold its
first meeting Thursday of next week
at 7:30 p.m. in the social room of
tho Education Building.
The meeting will include election
of officers and plans for a program
for the year. In addition, the new
president, Mrs. Helen Hawkes, who
has recently returned from the na
tional biennial council, will report
on the action of the council.
