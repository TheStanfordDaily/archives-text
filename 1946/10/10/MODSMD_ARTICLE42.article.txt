# New Law School Dean Introduced
## 
### 
Dean Carl Spaeth, new head of
the School of Law, was formally
presented to the members of the
school at the quarter's first meeting
of the Stanford Law Association,
held yesterday afternoon in Memo
rial Auditorium.
The Association's business meet
ing heard reports on its moot court
and legal aid programs. Sandy Ta
tum and Mort Silvers were nomi
nated lor the Executive Board, and
a motion for increased dues was
placed on the ballot for the coming
Association elections. President Bob
Steel presided.
