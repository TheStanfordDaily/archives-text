# Giants Seek Prospects
## 
### 
SAN" FRANCISCO. Oct. 10 (UP).
—A tryout camp for prospective
professional baseball players will be
staged in Seals Stadium Oct. 15-19
under auspices of the San Francisco
Seals, New York Giants and Salt
Lake City Bees, it was announced
today.
