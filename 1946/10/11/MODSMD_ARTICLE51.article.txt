# King's Medal Given Professor Terman
## 
### 
Professor Frederick E. Terman,
dean of the School of Engineering,
has been awarded the King of Eng
land's special medal for service in
the cause of freedom, according to
a letter received Wednesday by
Mrs. Terman from the British Em
bassy in Washington.
During the war, Dr. Terman
worked at the Radio Research Lab
oratory at Harvard, where counter
measures to combat enemy radio
and radar were developed.
