# Graduate Women Give Luncheon on Thursday
## 
### 
All graduate women are invited
to a luncheon Thursday at 11:50 in
the Lower Lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse. Those planning to at
tend are asked to write a card to
Hilltop House by Monday so that
arrangements may be made. Lunch
will cost 40 cents.
Social program will be discussed
and officers will be elected.
