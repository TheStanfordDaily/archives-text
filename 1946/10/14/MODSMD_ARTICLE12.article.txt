# Students Get Priority On Season Ticket Sale
## 
### 
Subscriptions to the Stanford
Players' '4ti-'l7 season may be ob
tained by students until 5 p.m.
today, after which sale will be
opened to I'alo Alto people.
Stressing that he wished Stan
ford students !o have first chance
at the limited number of seats, F.
Cowles Strickland announced that
half of the 2.0(10 seats reserved for
students had already been sold.
Tickets may be bought from 10
to 12 a.m. and from 2 to 5 p.m.
today from the box office in front
of Memorial Hall. After today,
however, students must compete
with Palo Alto purchasers for sub
scription preference.
