# CALIFORNIANS IN NET WIN
## 
### 
MEXICO CITY, Oct. 13 (UP).—
Margaret Osborne and Tom Brown
of California, top-seeded mixed
doubles team, defeated Esther Re
yes and Francisco Guerrero of Mex
ico in a second-round match in the
Pan - American tennis tournament
today, 6-3, 6-2, to advance to the
quarter finals.
