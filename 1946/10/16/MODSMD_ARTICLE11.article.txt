# Library Director Attends Meeting
## 
### 
Edwin T. Coman, Jr., director of
the Stanford Gradaute School of
Business Library, will represent
Stanford at the California Library
Association at Coronado, California,
Oct. 16 to 19.
Mr. Coman will participate in the
panel discussion on "Library Serv
ice to Business," as well as serving
on the committee for regional co
operation. In view of his position
on the library standards committee,
Mr. Coman will take part in prepa
rations of the library job analysis
and classification demonstration
which is to be presented at a gen
eral assembly of the association.
