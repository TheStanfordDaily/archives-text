# Tickets Now Available For Branner Formal
## 
### 
Branner Hall will hold the first
formal dance of the season on cam
pus Saturday night. Oct. 19, when
the girls will present their "Autumn
Nocturne" theme.
The bids for the dance, which sell
for $2 a couple, are available to
Branner girls only until today. The
tickets can now be purchased by
the entire campus from Mary Allen
at Branner, at 3121.
Dancing will last from 9 to 1 to
the music of Jack Little's and How
ard Martin's "Melody Masters" in
the lobby and patio of Branner.
