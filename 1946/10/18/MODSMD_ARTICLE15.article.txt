# Village Holds Hop After Rally Tonight
## 
### 
The first Village social event for
all Stanford students lakes place
tonight when AVC sponsors a
date, no-datc dance after the
rally in the Village Central Gym.
A special bus service will take
students to and from the dance.
Busses will leave from the Pa
vilion after the rally, and several
will run hack and forth from the
Village during the evening.
Pat Clarke, acting chairman of
the AVC, Stanford chapter, has
stated that "the dance tonight will
render a definite community serv
ice, both in filling a social need
for village residents and in help
ing to co-ordinate campus and
Village activities."
Admission to the dance is free.
