# ADMIRAL WAESCHE DIES
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Oct. 17 (UP).—
Adm. Russell R. Wacsche, retired
wartime commandant of the Coast
Guard, died at the Bethesda, Md.,
Naval Medical Center today after
an illness of nearly 10 months. He
was (>O.
