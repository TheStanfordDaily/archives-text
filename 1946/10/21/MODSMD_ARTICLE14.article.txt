# Jordan Art Show Opens
## 
### 
Aii exhibit of 50 prints from the
collections of David Starr Jordan
and Mrs. Michael will be
shown in the Stanford Art Gallery
starting tomorrow and continuing
until Nov. 10.
The Art Gallery was reorganized
and reopened one year ago under
the direction of George Harris. Sine
then it has stressed contemporary
art in most of its displays.
A series of water colors and ink
drawings by combat artists show
ing the scenes of destruction in all
theaters of the war was the first ex
hibit of the school year. The dis
play, entitled "Devastation," belongs
to the War Department.
Future exhibits this year will in
clude work in all fields of art, in
cluding etchings, lithography, and
photography.
