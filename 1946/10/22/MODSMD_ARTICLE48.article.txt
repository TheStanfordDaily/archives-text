# Farm Wins Award For Army Training
## 
### 
A certificate of distinction has
been awarded Stanford by the War
Department for training soldiers in
the Army Specialized Training Pro
gram during World War 11, it was
announced yesterday.
In an accompanying letter, Lt.
Gen. LeR. Lutes, Headquarters,
Army Service Forces, said, "The
War Department wishes to extend
. . . the thanks of a grateful gov
ernment" for the University's co
operation in the program.
The Stanford ASTP unit trained
11,928 men between 1943 and 1945.
Dr. Raymond D. Harriman, profes
sor of classics, served as co-ordina
ior.
