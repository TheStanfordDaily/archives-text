# Saturday Dances To Be Informal
## 
### 
Providing social events for this
week-end, the U.S.C.-Stanford twin
dances will take place Saturday
night, from 9 to 1 in the Pavilion and
Women's Gym. The dances will be
informal.
Bids have been sold out for the
Pavilion dance featuring the band
of Charlie Barnet; tickets at $2.40
are selling rapidly for the Women's
Gym dance with entertainment by
Dick Foy's orchestra.
Ruth Levison is committee chair
man of gym decorations which will
carry out the pom-pom theme. Bar
bara Allen has charge of Pavilion
decorations.
Patrons and patronesses of the in
formal dances will be announced
soon.
