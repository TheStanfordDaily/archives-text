# Stanford Dames To Hold Meetings
## 
### 
Two "special interest" meetings of
the Stanford Dames are to be held
Thursday evening at 7:45 p.m.
The first, a meeting of the group's
Reading Section, will be held on
campus in the Women's Clubhouse.
The program will feature Mrs. Vir
gil Anderson's reading of the play
"Dream Girl."
The Child Study Section will
gather at the home of Mrs. John
Stalnaker, 111 Martin Street, Palo
Alto. The speaker on this program
is to be Mrs. Robert Franck.
Wives of all Stanford students
are cordially invited to attend these
meetings.
