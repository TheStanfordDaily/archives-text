# 'Y' Tea to Honor Women Sponsors
## 
### 
Sponsors from Lagunita, Roble,
and Branner will be honored at a
tea given by the Y.W.C.A. from 3:30
to 5 p.m. today in the Women's Club
house. All directors and house
mothers of women's living groups
will be guests at the affair.
Jean Hanssen is in charge of the
informal gathering. Assisting her
are vice-chairman Barbara Aitken
and hostesses Audrey Hamilton,
Joyce Pabst, Mary Wilbur, and
Eleanor Hamilton. The clean -up
committee will be headed by Wanda
Wilbur and Kiane Brown.
