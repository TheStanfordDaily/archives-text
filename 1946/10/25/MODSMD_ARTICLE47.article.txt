# Hoedowners Plan Meeting Monday
## 
### 
The Hoedovvners, Stanford folk
and square dance group, will meet
Monday night at 7:30 in the Wom
en's Gym. All men and women stu
dents, wives of veterans, and fac
ulty are invited to attend.
The meeting will be under the
direction of Ed Carpenter, a square
dancer and caller from Colorado
and a member of the original square
dance group before the war.
Besides learning many new square
dances, the Italian quadrille, the
hampo, and the hopak will be the
advanced dances of the evening.
The third meeting of the group
will be Friday, Nov. 1, in the Wom
en's Gym at 8 p.m.
