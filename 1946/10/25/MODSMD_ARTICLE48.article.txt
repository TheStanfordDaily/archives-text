# Botanical Material Presented to Farm
## 
### 
What is said to be the third larg
est botanical collection in the world
has been bequeathed to Stanford by
the will of the late Dr. Edward
Louis Herman Knoche, San Jose
botanist.
The collection includes a botani
cal library of 5,000 volumes valued
at $12,500; 25,000 pamphlets worth
$8,750, and another $20,000 worth of
unmounted sheets of dried plants
and herbs.
