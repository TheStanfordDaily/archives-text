# Union, Village 310 Exchange Dinners
## 
### 
Union Residence and Village
House 310 held an exchange dinner
Wednesday night, with 24 of the men
dining at the girls' residence and
24 of the girls eating dinner at the
Village dining room.
'"This is the second exchange date
for the 'Club 310'," announced Holly
Hartley, house president. "We have
several other exchange dinners
scheduled for the near future, which
I think should continue our lead
over other houses in the Village,"
he added.
