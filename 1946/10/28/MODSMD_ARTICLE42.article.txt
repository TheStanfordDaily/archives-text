# Latin Jobs Speech Scheduled by Club
## 
### 
Betsy Savage will discuss job op
portunities in Central and South
America at the regular meeting of
Sigma Delta Pi, honorary Spanish
fraternity, this Friday afternoon in
the Lower Lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.

Miss Savage spent several months
in Mexico last summer. The meet
ing will begin at 4:15. The public
in general and Spanish Regional
majors in particular are invited to
attend.
