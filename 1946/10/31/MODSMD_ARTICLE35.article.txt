# Graduate Dance To Be Tomorrow
## 
### 
All graduate men and veterans
are invited as guests of honor at
an informal, no-date dance to be
given in the Women's Clubhouse to
morrow night from 9 to 1. The
theme of the dance is "autumn," and
the perennial Halloween refresh
ments, cider and doughnuts, will be
served.
