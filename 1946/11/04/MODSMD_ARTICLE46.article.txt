# Vacation Jobs Open To Farm Students
## 
### 
Christmas jobs will be listed
with the Student Employment Of
fice shortly. Students who desire
to work during Christinas vaca
tion should see Miss Adelaide
Palmer of the employment office
before Thanksgiving weekend.
Jobs will be listed from the im
mediate vicinity and the Penin
sula and from other towns as far
south as Los Angeles. Most of the
jobs will start around Dec. 14.
after finals, and will last through
Christmas.
Employment for boys will he
mostly with the post office, rail
way express, and sales work,
(iirls will find many sales and
clerical positions.
Post office jobs are usually as
signed in order of priority of ap
plication: students planning to do
that work are advised to register
early with Miss Palmer.
