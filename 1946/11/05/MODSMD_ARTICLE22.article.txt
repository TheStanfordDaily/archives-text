# 'Y' Holds Tea Today For Graduate Women
## 
### 
Stanford's graduate women will
be guests at a tea today at 4 p.m. in
the third floor "Y" rooms of the Wo
men's Clubhouse. The Y.W.C.A. so
cial committee is sponsoring the af
fair.
Barbara Aitken is in charge of
the tea. Cake and cider will be
served at the affair. All graduate
women arc urged to attend the tea.
