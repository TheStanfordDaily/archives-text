# Union Goes Fishy For 'Codfish Ball'
## 
### 
The Lower Lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse will be made to
look like San Francisco's Fisher
man's Wharf Saturday night when
Union Residence presents the "Cod
fish Ball," announced June Sword
and Cathy Morris, co-chairmen for
the affair. The dance will be open
to the whole campus, and bids are
on sale this week in the A.S.S.U.
office. The bids sell at $2.40 per '
couple for the dance, which will
last from 9 to 1.
