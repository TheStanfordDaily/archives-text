# One-Act Play Tryouts Scheduled for Tonight
## 
### 
All Stanford students who will be
here next term are eligible for parts
in the two long one-act plays to
be given Jan. 15-18 by the Stan
ford Players. Tryouts are being
held tonight at 7:30 in room 205,
Memorial Auditorium, and tomor
row and Friday afternoons at 3 in
the main theater on the "Macbeth"
set.
The plays are "My Heart's in the
Highlands," by William Saroyan,
and "John Doe." by Bernard Dryer.
Rehearsals will begin Nov. 11.
