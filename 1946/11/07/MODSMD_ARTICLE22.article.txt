# Men's Council
## 
### 
Stan Shpetner and Dick Baltic
polled 358 and 357 votes respec
tively to become members of
Men's Council. A total of 1,299
Stanford men voted in this elec
tion. Ted Norton with 313 votes
and Bob Cardinal with 288 votes
were the third and fourth choices.
This was the only election on the
ballot that was not voted prefer
entially.
