# AN ALL-CAMPUS DANCE
## 
### 
Tickets for the University Chorus
square dance at the Knoll tomor
row at 8 p.m. will be on sale at the
A.S.S.U. office today for the whole
student body for 75 cents per per
son. The dance features Ed Carpen
ter as dance caller, Doug DuLac in
a magic show, and refreshments for
all.
