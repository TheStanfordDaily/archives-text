# Los Altos Bids Sell To Business Dance
## 
### 
The Graduate School of Business
will hold its annual informal dance
this Saturday night at the Los Altos
Country Club.
Kenny Taix's orchestra will play
from 9 p.m. until 1 a.m., and the
bids are selling at the Business
School library for two dollars.
