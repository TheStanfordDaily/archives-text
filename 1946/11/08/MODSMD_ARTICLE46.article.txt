# Power Failure Blacks Farm for 30 Minutes
## 
### 
Power failure blacked-out resi
dences and business establishments
from southern San Carlos to Palo
Alio and Stanford for approximately
30 minutes last night.
Mr. George Prine, superintendent
of the Redwood City substation
which supplies the power for this
district, stated, "The trouble orig
inated here. We believe it was
caused by faulty relays. Emergency
repairs were made pending actual
localization of the trouble."
