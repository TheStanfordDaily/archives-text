# Libe to Be Open During Vacation
## 
### 
Library hours during the Thanks
giving vacation have been an
nounced. The University library will
be open from 8 a.m. until 5 p.m.
Wednesday, Nov. 27; from 9 a.m.
until 5 p.m. Friday, Nov. 29; and
from 9 a.m. until noon Saturday,
Nov. 30.
The library will be closed all day
Thanksgiving, Thursday, Nov. 28,
and the following Sunday, Dec. 1.
