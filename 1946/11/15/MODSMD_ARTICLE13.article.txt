# Even Dogs Feel The Meat Shortage
## 
### 
George York of the Fijis is
paring the floor tonight.
lie had paused for a brew
along the Bayshore Highway
when a large, shaggy dog was
run down. Dashing onto the
street, he carried the pooch out
of the way of other cars only to
he rewarded by the dog sinking
its teeth into his hand.
York was treated at the Palo
Aito Clinic and the dog was sent
to the Palo Alto Animal Shelter
where it was destroyed. At present
York is awaiting reports from a
test in Berkeley to determine
whether the bowser had rabies.
In the meantime, he has been in
structed not to bite anybody.
