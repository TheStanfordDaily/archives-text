# American Blood Sold On China Black Mart
## 
### 
SHANGHAI, Nov. 14 (UP).-The
American Red Cross demanded to
day that the Foreign Liquidation
Commission take an estimated 290
measurement tons of American
blood plasma out of the Shanghai
black market, which has been sell
ing it to Chinese as a "quick re
storative for depleted systems,"
These tons of plasma are worth
510.000.000 at current black market
prices. The commission sold it t to
Shanghai merchants as "surplus" at
about 50 cents a pint.
