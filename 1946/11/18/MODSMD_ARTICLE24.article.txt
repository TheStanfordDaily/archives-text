# Former Faculty Man Dies Suddenly
## 
### 
Dr. Laurence D. Bailiff, 50, asso
ciate professor of Spanish at U.C.-
L.A , died suddenly at his Westwood
home last Tuesday evening.
The body was discovered Wed
nesday morning by his mother, Mrs.
Mary F. Bailiff, when she entered
his apartment to prepare his break
fast. He had taught classes on the
day of his death, and was appar
ently in good health.
Dr. Bailiff was born in Lancaster,
Kansas. He took a Ph.D. at Stan
ford in 1923. In the course of his
teaching career, he was on the fac
ulty at Stanford, University of Cali
fornia, and University of Wyoming.
