# Stanford Man Cited for Bombing Raids
## 
### 
Edwin M. Wilson, Stanford Uni
versity student and former lieuten
ant commander in the Naval Air
Corps, was recently awarded the Air
Medal and gold stars in lieu of the
second, third, and fourth Air Medal
by Captain James E. Baker, com
manding officer of Naval Air Sta
tion, Oakland.

The presentation was based on
meritorious flights while a member
of Bombing Squadron 11 based
aboard the aircraft carrier USS
Hornet. Wilson's battle engage
ments include New Georgia, Bou
ganville, Solomons, Okinawa, For
mosa, Philippines, French Indo-Chi
na, and Hong Kong.
