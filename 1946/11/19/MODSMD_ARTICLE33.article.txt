MEN LIVING in Stanford Village
houses 306-316, 108, and 110, who
wish to make room reservations
in University residences for win
ter quarter, should file deposits
and room application cards today
at the Village near the post of
fice, in Civic Center.
RED AND WHITE POM-POMS,
made by Union' Residence girls,
will be sold at the Union Resi
dence desk from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
through Friday for 25 cents.
KLAIIOYA will hold its weekly
luncheon meeting in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse
today at 12.
VILLAGE CHESS CLUB meets
Wednesday night at 7 in the Vil
lage Counselor's office.
VILLAGE COUNCIL meets tonight
at 6:30 in the Village Counselor's
office.
SOPII EXCOM will meet tonight at
7:30 in a room off the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
HAWAIIAN CLUB will meet to
night at 7:30 on. the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse. Buddy
Scott will preside. Thursday
night's meeting has been canceled.
All Kamaainas should be present
to discuss plans.
COMMITTEE HEADS for the jun
ior class dance and members of
Junior Excom will meet tonight
at 8:45 downstairs in the Wom
"en's Clubhouse.
HOME ARTS GROUP of the Stan
ford Dames Club will meet at the
Women's Clubhouse at 7:45 Wed
nesday evening. A speaker will
talk on decoration of tables for
Thanksgiving and Christmas.
READING GROUPS of the Stan
ford Dames Club will meet at 7:45
at the Women's Clubhouse Thurs
day evening. Miss Blackner will
read Christmas carols.
S.A.E. PLEDGES will meet at 7:45
p.m. at the S.A.E. house.
JUNIOR CLASS EXCOM will meet
tonight at 8:45 in the Women's
Clubhouse. All committee heads
for the junior dance are asked to
attend.
ALPHA DELTA SIGMA will meet
at 4 p.m. in the Journalism li
brary. Market survey wil be giv
en out. At 7:30 members will
meet again at Journalism library
to hear a talk by Bob Knollin
from San Francisco.
EL TORO CLUB will meet tonight
at 7 in the northeast social room
of Toyon.
STANFORD STUDENT FEDERAL
ISTS will hold an open discussion
on the control of atomic energy
at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday in room
35 of the Education Building. All
those interested are invited to at
tend.
STANFORD BRIDGE CLUB will
meet at the Women's Clubhouse
at 7:15 tonight, announces How
ard Shomate, president.
CARDINALS will meet tonight at
7:30 in the Excom room.
