# Attendants Needed For Cattle Boats
## 
### 
Livestock attendants for UNRRA
cattle-boats going to Europe are
still needed, it was disclosed re
cently by Ihe Brethren Service
Committee.
Shipments are being made to
Greece, Yugoslavia, Poland, Bre
men, and Italy from various At
lantic ports. The trips usually last
from five to seven weeks with three
to five days' shore leave being
granted in Europe.
Further information on the trips
can be obtained from the A.S.S.U.
office.
