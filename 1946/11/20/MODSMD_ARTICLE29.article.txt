# Production of Crystals Will Be Explained
## 
### 
War-developed techniques for the
mass production of quartz crystals
for radio will be disclosed by Ber
tram Ryland tonight at 7:30 before
a meeting of the Stanford Radio
Club in room 283. He will describe
how the new methods overcame the
former need of employing skilled
craftsmen to produce the delicate
crystals.
Club secretary Lester Roberts
said the meeting would be open to
all interested persons.
