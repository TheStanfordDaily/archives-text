# Men Rooters Attention—
## 
### 
Men rooters attention!
Thiity card stunts are going to be
given in a 20-minutc half this Sat
urday. In order for the stunts to
be effective the following instruc
tions must be observed:
1. Please co-operate with Rally
Com in the seating arrangement.
2. Cards will be larked to the
scats with instruction cards. Sit
directly over the cards. Don't
touch the cards until the half.
3. Stunts will begin immediately
after the second quarter.
4. Line up quickly with the bot
tom row before the half begins.
5. Remember your row and seat
number for the animated stunts
which will be found on the top of
your instruction cards.
G. Immediately notify Rally Com
members of vacant seats.
7. Many of the stunts wi»l be
flip stunts; therefore hold the
card that you are using for the
present stunt in one hand. Place
the card for the next stunt on the
hack of this card so that you will
he ready to flip into the next
stunt. Speed is a necessity he
cause of the limited time of stunts
and for the continuity of the sto
ries. Repeat this for all stunts
whether or not they are flips, for
you will then have the card ready
for the next stunt even if it is an
up-down one.
8. There must be a white back
ground in the rooting section if the
stunts are to go over successfully.
