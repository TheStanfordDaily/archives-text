# Frosh Men Campaign Against Early Dates
## 
### JACK OGLESBY 
Following the suggestiuns of an
editorial in the Dailj in regard to
eager heaver date is, the class of
'*>U is putting on a campaign in
Encina to do away with the evils
of early dating.
Notices have been posted on
doors throughout the hall con
demning the practice and calling
for a strong stand against it hy
the freshmen.
A black list has also been posted
in the lobby with the names of
students who jump the gun.
