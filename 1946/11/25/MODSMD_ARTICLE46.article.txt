# WEDNESDAY 1:30'S FOR ALL
## 
### 
Women's Council is granting non
quota 1:30 late leaves to all women
for Wednesday. University regula
tions provide for automatic 1:30
leaves on Thursday, Friday, and
Saturday nights during the vacation
period. Any 2 o'clock leaves taken
will be counted on the quota. The
regular sign-out procedure must be
observed during the holidays.
