# Square Dance Classes Open to Men in Winter
## 
### 
Square dance classes will be open
to men again winter quarter, Mrs.
Licister, dance instructor, has an
nounced. The class will be offered
Tuesday and Thursday at 5 p.m.,
with 1 hour dhr.
Mrs. Lidster has also asked that
any men interested in a modern
dunce class see her on Reg day. The
hours have not yet been arranged,
but there is a possibility that the
class will be offered.
