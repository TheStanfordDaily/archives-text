# Vet Story Writers Offered $500 Prize
## 
### 
Five hundred dollars first prize
is offered by the "New Republic
Magazine" for the best short story
written by a veteran now at
tending college under the G.I. Bill
of Rights.
Second prize of $250 and five
additional prizes of $50 each will
also be awarded.
Manuscripts must be typed on
one side of the paper and can
not exceed 1,800 words in length.
They should be mailed to Short
Story Contest. 1(1 E. l!)th St.. New
