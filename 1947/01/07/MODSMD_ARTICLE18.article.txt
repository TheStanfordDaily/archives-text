# Stanford Student Drowning Victim
## 
### 
Allen Richard Arnold, 21-year
old graduate student, was drowned
Nov. 28 when he was struck by a
large wave while swimming near
Carmel.
Arnold, a veteran who was living
at the Village, was picnicking with
some other Stanford students about
12 miles south of Carmel. Elizabeth
Peabody was also knocked down by
the wave, but clung to a rock until
she was rescued by Edward Bab
bott and Virginia Vernon.
Arnold's body was not recovered.
