# ORGAN RECITAL TODAY
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wheelwright, reopen
ing his weekly organ recitals, will
present his first musical program
this afternoon at 4:15 in Memorial
Church. He will play "Cathedral
Prelude and Fugue" by Bach;
"Dreams" by Guilmant; "Chorale in
A Minor" by Franck; "From the
Land of the Sky-Blue Water" by i
Cadman; "A Primitive Organ" by
Yon; "Reverie on a French Folk
song" by Bedell; and "Finale, First
Organ Symphony" by Maquaire.
