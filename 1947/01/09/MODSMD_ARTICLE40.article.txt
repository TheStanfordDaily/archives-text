# CREWMEN MEET
## 
### 
The largest crew turnout in Stan
ford's history will begin intensive
winter training Thursday.
All crewmen, except returning
lettermen, will meet with Captain
Stan Cumberpatch at 4:10 p.m. in
front of the S.A.C. This first work
out will consist of conditioning ex- j
ercises and roadwork. Men wish
ing to try out who have not signed
up should attend this meeting.
