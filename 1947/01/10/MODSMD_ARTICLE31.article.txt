# Head Chef of Cellar Dies of Pneumonia
## 
### 
Martin Prezevich, for six years
head clief at (he Cellar, died of
pneumonia suddenly last week at
the age of 55.
He was horn in Ka/ujn. Yugo
slavia, on July 4. 1891, and came
to this country when he was quite
young. For 15 years he was a
member of the State Guard.
Surviving him is his wife, Pearl
Prezevieh.
