# Jayvee Cagers Drop Thriller to Long Beach
## 
### 
Stanford's Jayvee quintet dropped
a 4S-47 thriller to a fast Long Beach
City College five Friday night at the
pavilion.
In the final minute of play, with
the score tied at 47-47, Tom Amberry
made a free toss to give the south
erners the winning point. Amberry
also led the scoring with a total of
24 points for the evening.
