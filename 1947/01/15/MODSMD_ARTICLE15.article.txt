# Late Leave Petitions Due Before Friday
## 
### 
All girls who wish special late
leaves for Daily or Cellar work
should file a petition at the Wom
en's Council office, room 15 of the
Student Union, before Friday,
stating the number of late leaves
desired per week and the days
on which they will he used.
Petitions must also be tiled be
fore Friday for such work as
baby-sitting. If the petitions for
baby-sitting are granted, a mem
ber of Women's Council must be
notified before or immediately
after each leave is used.
