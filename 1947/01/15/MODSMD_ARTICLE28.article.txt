# Farm Psych Prof Appointed Editor
## 
### 
Calvin P. Stone, professor of psy
chology, has been elected editor of
the Journal of Comparative and
Physiological Psychology, which is
owned and published by the Amer
ican Psychological Association.
His editorial duties began Jan. 1,
1947. He was elected for a term of
six years. The Journal is published
bi-monthly.
The Journal covers literature in
the field of animal psychology and
such aspects of physiological psy
chology as bear principally upon
the more general topic of compara
tive psychology.
