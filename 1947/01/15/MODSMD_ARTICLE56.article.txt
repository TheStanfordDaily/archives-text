# Eating Clubs Make Plans for Rushing
## 
### 
Interclub Council met last night
in Toyon Hall to discuss club rush
ing and eating arrangements. Phil
Chandler, El Capitan, secretary of
the Council, presided.
Dick Huhn, Breakers, and Bill
MacPherson, El Campo, were named
a committee on rushing and directed
to contact Encina and Village officers
for arrangements.
