# SC ATHLETE NAMED
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Jan. 15 (UP).—
Pollard who ran up 156 points—tops
for the nation—as a halfback for
Loyola High School, today was nam
ed December Southern California
athlete for the month.
Pollard averaged nine yards per
carry last season. His point total
was the largest in southern Califor
nia since Glenn Davis, who was to
become an Army star, tallied 236
points for Bonita High School in
1942.
