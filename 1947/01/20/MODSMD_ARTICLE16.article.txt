# Bailey Willis Speaks Today to Geoolgy Club
## 
### 
Bailey Willis, professor emeri
tus of geology, will tell the story
of two pioneer geologists of the
West to the Geology Journal Club
today at 4 p.m. in Room 320 of the
Geology Building.
The subjects of his speech are
G. K. Powell and J. W. Gilbert,
who explored and studied the pla
teau region of the Colorado River
and the Great Basin in Utah, Ari
zona, Nevada, and California.
Dean A. I. Levorsen of the
School of Mineral Sciences will
act as chairman of the meeting.
The public is invited.
