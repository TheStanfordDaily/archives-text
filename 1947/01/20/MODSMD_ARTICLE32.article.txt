# Sports Staff Needs Help
## 
### 
All men interested in
working on the sports
staff are requested to
meet tonite at 8:30 in the
Daily Shack. No experi
ence is needed. No col
umnists need apply.
Those not able to at
tend the meeting can
contact Don Davies at
the Alpha Delta Phi
House, or Or Apperson
at Village 308.
Tea and strumpets
will not be served.

AMPHIBIOUS is the word for Boh An
derson. a Farm hoy who is equally at home on
a football turf or in a pool of water. Last
fall he played a lot of left halfback for Mar
chie Schwartz; now Coaches Tom Havnie ami
Ernie Brandsten (shown kibitzing, above) are
busily grooming hj, n for some more national
championships in the aqueous sport. (Photo
Courtesy San Francisco Chronicle)
