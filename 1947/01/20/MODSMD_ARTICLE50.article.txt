# French Conversational Club Meets Tomorrow
## 
### 
The second meeting of the newly
formed French Club will take place
tomorrow afternoon at 4:15 in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house. Organized for those inter
ested in improving or learning
spoken French, the conversational
club will be conducted entirely in
French and will meet every Tues
day afternoon.
Guest speakers, French record
programs and community sings are
to be included in future meetings.
