# AFL WALKOUT PLANNED
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 19 (Ul>).
—More than 500 AFL workers in
five Bay Area tanneries will walk
out tomorrow morning in support of
i demands for a 17 1-2 cent cost of
living wage increase, uniun official
reported today.
