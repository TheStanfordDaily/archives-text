# French Club Plans Correspondence
## 
### 
The second meeting of the newly
organized French Club held yes
terday afternoon emphasized the
furtherance of understanding and
co-operation between France and
the United States which coincides
with the I.S.C. program now active
on campus.
The group plans to send a month
ly package of food and one of cloth
ing to either a French child or a
student, with whom a correspond
ence will be conducted.
Yesterday's meeting was well at
tended and entirely conducted in
French. Meetings will be held ev
ery Tuesday afternoon at 4:15 in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
