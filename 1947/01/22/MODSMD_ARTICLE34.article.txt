# College Pay Too Low Young Quits Illinois
## 
### 
CHICAdO, Jan. 21 (UP).—Buddy
Young, Illinois star halfback, an
nounced today he was quitting
school, but he denied any immedi
ate plans to join a professional foot
ball club.
"I have not been approached by
any professional club, either in the
All-Artierica Conference or the Na
tional League," he said.
"I don't know whether I'm going
to play professional ball, but I have
to quit school. It's a financial thing.
"All I'm interested in is getting
regular financial support for my
family—and I can't do it on $90 a
month."
