# YWCA Gives Tea For New Women
## 
### 
New women students may meet
Mrs. Donald B. Tresidder, Miss Elva
Fay Brown, Dean of Women, and
Mrs. Fran Nielson, member of the
Y.W.C.A. advisory board, at a tea
this afternoon at 3:30 in the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
The Y.W.C.A. cabinet will also be
guests at the tea sponsored by the
"Y" Social Activities Committee
headed by Jean Hanssen. All new
women students are invited to the
meeting in the "Y" rooms on the
top floor of the clubhouse.
The "Y" Political Effectiveness
Committee is completing plans for
bringing Stanford students together
to meet their delegates to the re
cent Chicago conference on National
Student Organization.
All interested students are invited
to meet tomorrow at 7:30 on the
second floor of Women's Clubhouse
when the Chicago delegates will be
present.
