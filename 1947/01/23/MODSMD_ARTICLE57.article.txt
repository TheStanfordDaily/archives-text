# Encina Officers Hold First Meet
## 
### 
Encina Excom, composed of newly
elected hall officers and wing pres
idents. held its first meeting of the
quarter Tuesday night.
Tentative dates for Encina's pro
posed social schedule were dis
cussed. Also considered were the
following lucrative topics: (1) the
possibility of two bottles of milk
with each meal, (2) the possibility
of having a late breakfast, (3) im
provement of the game room.
New wing presidents, elected
Monday night, are Sam Brenner,
Stuart Richardson, Bob Gibson,
Dick Kelton, Bob Jacobson, Cal
Newman, Forden Athearn, Roy
Moe, Bob Tuthill, Bill Leigh, Don
Stamm, and John Rysdorp.
