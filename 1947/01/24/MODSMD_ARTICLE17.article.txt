# DRY YEAR
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, Jan. 23.—(UP)
The Sacramento Valley may face
one of the driest years on record as
a result of rainfall so far 50 per cent
below normal and a light snowpack
in the mountains, the U.S. Bureau of
Reclamation said today.
