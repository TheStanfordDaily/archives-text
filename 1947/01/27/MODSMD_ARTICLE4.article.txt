# Glee Club Presents Recital Wednesday
## 
### 
Men's Glee Club, directed by
Joel Carter, will present a recital
this Wednesday at 8 p.m. in the
Women's Clubhouse.
In addition to numbers by the
general choir, several soloists
from the group will sing.
The volunteer organization is
under the Music Council and is
made up of men students who
meet each week.
