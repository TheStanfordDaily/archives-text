# PASSENGER TRAIN COLLIDES
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH, Jan. 26 (UP).—
The Pennsylvania Railroad's crack
passenger train, the Pennsylvanian,
collided with three derailed freight
cars at nearby Pitcairn early today
while en route from Chicago to
New York.
