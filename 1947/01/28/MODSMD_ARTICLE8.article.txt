# Navy to Interview Men Tomorrow
## 
### 
A Navy recruiting team will be at
the Palo Alto post office tomorrow
to interview prospective volunteers
for the Naval Reserve program.
The Navy will accept all men who
have had previous naval service,
and those without previous service
between the ages of 17 and 18 1 *>,
and between 30 and 39.
No call will be made upon vol
unteers except in the case of a na
tional emergency. Attendance at
weekly meetings or participation in
annual cruises is not required, but
those who do attend will be paid
accordingly.
