# Mangini's Picked For Senior Picnic
## 
### 
A senior class picnic was planned
by the class excom last night. Date
of the affair will be Sunday, Feb. 16,
President H. D. Thoreau announced.
Co-chairmen will b" B. J. Elliott
and Bob Ayres, who tentatively
plan volleyball and baseball games
at the Mangini picnic grounds.
Further plans will be announced
next week when committee chair
men will be announced.
