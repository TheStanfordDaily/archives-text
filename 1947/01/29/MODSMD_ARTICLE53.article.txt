# Tresidder Concert To Feature Pianist
## 
### 
Miss Margaret Ann Stiver, young
California pianist, will make her
debut tonight at 7:30 o'clock at the
home of Dr. Donald B. Tresidder as
a feature of "Music at Home." Miss
Stiver, pupil of Elise Belenky, will
play selections by Scarlatti, Cho
pin, and Debussy.
The records played will include :
works by Handel, Smetana, Wag- j
ner, and Roy Harris. All students ]
and faculty are invited to attend.
