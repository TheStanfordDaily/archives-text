# Navy Offers Posts In Reserve Plan
## 
### 
A Navy recruiting team will be on
the second floor of the SA.C. to
morrow from 9 to 5 to interview
prospective volunteers for the Naval
Reserve program.
The Navy will accept all men who
have had previous naval service,
and those without previous service
between the ages of 17 and 18 1 and
between 30 and 39.
No call will be made for volun
teers except in the case of a na
tional emergency. Attendance at
weekly meetings or participation in
annual cruises is not required, but
those who do attend will be paid
accordingly.
