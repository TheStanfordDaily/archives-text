# Chinese Play To Be Given
## 
### 
Of special interest to all Pacific-
Asiatic majors will be "An Evening
in Cathay," to be presented by a
Chinese Cultural Theater Group in
the San Francisco Marine Memorial
Theater Feb. 4, 5, G, according to Dr.
Shan Wing Chan.
"An Evening in Cathay," on its
second international tour, will pre
sent Chinese artists from Shanghai
giving songs, dances and scenes from
Chinese classical plays.
Tickets for the play, produced by
Averil Tom, may be secured at the
City of Paris Box Office or at the
Marine Memorial Auditorium at
$3.00, $2.40, $1.80 and $1.20.
