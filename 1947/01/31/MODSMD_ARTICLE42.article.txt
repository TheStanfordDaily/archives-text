# Fulton Injured On AAU Tour
## 
### 
Johnny Fulton, former Stanford
student and track star, pulled a
muscle in a quarter-mile race Jan.
29 at Hamilton. New Zealand. Ful
ton, on an exhibition tour under the
auspices of the A.A.U., fell heavily
after running only 50 yards of the
race. The pulled muscle caused
him to double up with pain as he
fell. It will not be known for several
days whether Fulton will be able to
compete again before the end of the
tour.
