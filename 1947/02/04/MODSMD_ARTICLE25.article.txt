# Truman Names Alum Federal Board Member
## 
### 
President Truman today nominat
ed Lawrence Clayton of Massachu
setts, a Stanford graduate, class of
1914, for membership on the board
of governors of the Federal Reserve
System. His term will expire Feb
ruary 1, 1952.
Clayton, a native of Salt Lake
City, received the law degree at
Harvard in 1917. He was assistant
to Board Chairman Mariner S.
Eccles from December 1934 until he
resigned in January 1945.
