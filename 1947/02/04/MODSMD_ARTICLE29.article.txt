# BLANDY NEW CHIEF
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Feb. 3 (UP).—
Vice Adm. William H. P. Blandy.
56-year-old naval ordnance expert,
was named commander-in-chief of
the U.S. Atlantic Fleet today to
succeed Adm. Marc A. Mitscher
who died at Norfolk, Va., today.
