# Scholarships Announced
## 
### 
Zonta International, service club
for executive and professional wom
en, announces the $500 Amelia Ear
hart Scholarship for 1847, for grad
uate study for women in the field
of aeronautics.
The scholarship, in memory of
Amelia Earhart, a former member
of Zonta International, may be used
at Stanford where graduate level
aeronautical work is offered. Any
woman in aeronautics may apply.
Applications must be filed before
March 1. 1947, with Miss J. Winifred
Hughes, chairman of the committee,
Syracuse University, Syracuse, N.Y.
