# Baker to Address YWCA Committee
## 
### 
John Earl Baker, speaker at the
Tuesday Evening Series, will talk
before the Political Effectiveness
Committee of the Y.W.C.A. this af
ternoon.
The committee has planned a "get
acquainted tea" to precede the talk
at 3:45 in the "Y" room. Mr, Baker
will speak at 4:45 p.m.
All men and women students are
invited to attend.
