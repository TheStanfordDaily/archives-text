# Relief Fund Asked
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. Feb. S.—(UP) —
Secretary of State George C.
Marshall has notified congress that
appropriations for continuing relief
programs abroad after UNRRA ex
pires should have top priority over
all foreign policy legislation, it was
disclosed today.
In a letter to Senate President
Arthur H. Vandenberg, R., Mich.,
Marshall listed that as number one
on a schedule of "urgent items in
order of priority" which the State
Department will submit to congress.
