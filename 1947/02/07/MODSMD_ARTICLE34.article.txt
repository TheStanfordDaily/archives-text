# Dr. Hanna to Aid German Training
## 
### 
Dr. Paul R. Hanna, professor of
education at Stanford, will take part
in the re-education of the German
people after his present assignment
in the Panama Canal Zone is com
pleted.
Dr. Hanna at present is a consult
ant for the education program of
the Central American republic. He
plans to fly to Washington, D.C., on
Feb. 15 for a brief conference with
officials there before taking off for
Berlin.
As a United States military con
sultant, the Stanford educator will
spend six weeks in Germany in
specting' their teacher training pro
gram. He will also assist in setting
up a general curriculum pattern for
practice teaching and in surveying
social science courses to check on
their complete de-nazification.
