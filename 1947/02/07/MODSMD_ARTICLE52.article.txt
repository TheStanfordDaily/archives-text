# GAMBLING CHECK URGED
## 
### 
• REDWOOD CITY, Feb. 6 (UP).—
The San Mateo County Grand Jury
today sought state funds for an
investigation of "unchecked gam
bling" in the county which they
charged was being ignored by coun
ty authorities.
Jury spokesmen said they would
appeal to the State Legislature for
funds for a clean-up drive.
