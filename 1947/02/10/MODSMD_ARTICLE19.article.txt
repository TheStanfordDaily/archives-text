# Rogers to Speak Before Geologists
## 
### 
"The Contributions of Harvard
and Yale to Mineralogy" will be the
topic of an address by Dr. Austin
Flint Rogers, professor of mineral
ogy emeritus, before the Geology
Journal Club this afternoon at 4:15
in room 320 of the Geology Depart
ment.
Presented under the auspices of
the School of Mineral Sciences, the
lecture is open to the public.
