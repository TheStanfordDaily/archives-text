# Rushing Activities Over This Week
## 
### 
Rushing activities for 1947 will
culminate this week with rushing
meals tomorrow and Wednesday.
Rushees will be invited by rush
card notices to visit the fraternities
for both noon and evening meals.
Jell Howe, president of Interfra
ternity Council, announces that for
mal pledging will take place from
9 to 12 Saturday, Feb. 15.
Pledging ceremonies will probably
be held in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Saturday night has been set as
the night of the Formal Pledge
Prom. Howe says that each house
will be in charge of arrangements
for its own dance and there will be
no combined pledge dance.
