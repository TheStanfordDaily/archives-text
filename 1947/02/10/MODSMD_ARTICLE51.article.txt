# Coldiron Announces Mural Sked Change
## 
### 
Intramural Basketball Chief
Gene Coldiron announced today
that, because of the boxing
matches next Thursday night in
the Pavilion, all Mural games
scheduled for the Pavilion on that
date must be played at the Vil
lage Gymnasium.
All team managers should
please note the following changes
in the schedule: AKL will meet
Encina 2E in the Village Gym at
9:00 tonight.
On Wednesday night, Phi Kappa
Sigma clashes with Toyon No. 2,
and on Thursday SAE plays En
cina 3W. Both games start at 9:00
in the Village Gym.
