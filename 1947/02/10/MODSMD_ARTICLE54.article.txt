# BRUINS RESUME CREW
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Feb. 9 (UP).—
After a suspension since 1942 be
cause of the war, University of Cali
fornia at Los Angeles oarsmen take
to the water again tomorrow when
the 1947 crew signup is held.
Coach Ben Wall is, who was Bruin
mentor from 1934 through 11)42, will
be assisted by Bob Hillen, coxswain
and letterman from 1937 to 1939.
