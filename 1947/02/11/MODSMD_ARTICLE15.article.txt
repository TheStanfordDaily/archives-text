# 15-Unit Russian Course Offered
## 
### 
The Department of Asiatic and
Slavic Studies plans to offer an in
tensified course in Russian language
this summer quarter, which will
cover a full year's work (15 units)
by meeting three hours a day, five
days a week.
Students interested in this course
should register as soon as possible
at the Asiatic and Slavic Depart
ment office, room 50C. The class
will be limited to 12 students.
After completion of the summer
course, an adaptation of a similar
program given for the Army during
the war, students may continue in
the regular class in Intermediate
Russian offered during the next aca
demic year.
