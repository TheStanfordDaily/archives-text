# Carillonneur to Play For Lincoln's Birthday
## 
### 
Lincoln's birthday will he com
memorated by a special recital of
the .'l5-bell carillon in the Hoo
ver War Library tomorrow noon.
James R. Lawson, carillonneur,
will play three pieces, "The Battle
Hymn of the Republic," "The
Stars and Stripes Forever" by
John Philip Sousa, and"Amer
ica."
