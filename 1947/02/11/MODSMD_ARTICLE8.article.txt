# McPherson New Toyon President
## 
### 
Residents of Toyon Ilall last night
elected William MePhcrson as pres
ident of Toyon Club for the rest of
the academic year. Robert Brown
was chosen vice-president and Rich
ard Burke secretary.
Outgoing president Frank Creede
opened the meeting. Before elect
ing officers, the hallmen voted to
hold an open-house dance, with
band, on February 28.
