# Men's Faculty Meets Tonight
## 
### 
Two Farm faculty members,
Wayne S. Vucinich, history instruc
tor, and Professor Graham P. Du-
Shane, biology, will speak before
the Men's Faculty Club tonight at
K at the Faculty Clubhouse.
Mr. Vucinich's discussion on
"Russian Liberation of Bulgaria
in IK7X and 1011: An Historical
Parallel?" will illustrate imperial
Russia's and the Soviet Union's
policies regarding Bulgaria. He
served on the Allied Control Com
mission in Bulgaria during the
last war.
Professor DuShane's subject of
"Development of Color in Verte
brate Animals" deals with his ex
periments in the transplantation of
certain embryo cells of one color to
another embryo to form different
color combinations in animal parts.
