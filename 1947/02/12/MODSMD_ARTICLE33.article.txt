# Russian Offered Summer Quarter
## 
### 
Department of Asiatic and Slavic
i Studies will offer an intensified
i course in Russian this summer
quarter.
The course will cover a full year's
work (15 units) by meeting three
hours a day, five days a week. Com
pletion of the course will enable the
student to continue in the regular
Intermediate Russian course offered
in the next academic year.
Interested students should regis
ter as soon a.s possible at the de
; partment office, room 50C. The class
: will be limited to 12 students.
