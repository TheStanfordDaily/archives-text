# Hilgard Talks On Teachers
## 
### 
Dr. Ernest R. Hilgard, professor
of psychology and education, will
speak on "The Division of Labor
Between the Teacher and the Psy
chologist" tonight at 8 in Cubberley
Auditorium.
He will discuss the psychologist
as a pure scientist, the educator as
an applied scientist, and the teacher
as a practitioner of the applied sci
ence of learning, showing that the
three are mutually beneficial.
Dr. Hilgard came to Stanford in
15)33 from Yale University, where be
received the Ph.D. degree in 1930.
He has been executive head of the
Department of Psychology since
1942. He was also a member of the
summer faculty at Mills College,
the University of Oregon, and U.S.C
In 1940 he received the Warren
medal in experimental psychology.
He was collaborator for the division
of child development and teacher
personnel of the American Council
of Education from 1940 to 1941.
The talk is the fifth in the School
of Education's Faculty Lecture Se
ries. It is open to the public with
out charge.
