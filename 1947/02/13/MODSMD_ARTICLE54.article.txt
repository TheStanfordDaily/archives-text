# Engineers Advised On Future Jobs
## 
### 
"Unlimited opportunities are open
to all young engineers," asserted W.
H. Hale, chief engineer of Richmond
Oil Refinery, in his talk last night to
the men in Village House 117, on
the problems to be faced by men
entering the engineering field from
college.
The speech, the first of a series
of vocational talks by professional
engineers sponsored by and for the
engineering students living in House
117, was arranged through Dr. Rob
ert N. Bush of the Stanford Ap
pointment Service.
