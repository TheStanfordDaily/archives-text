# Union Dance Bids On Sale in Houses
## 
### 
Tickets for the dinner dance,
"Chateau des Arbres," to be given
by Union Unit Saturday. Feb. 22.
are on sale in individual houses.
The dinner dance will start at G p.m.
with hors d'oeuvres served in the
houses, and will end at 1 a.m.
Dinner will be served in the main
dining room promptly at 8. Dancing
will follow in the main lobby to the
music of Vincent Raff and his or
chestra.
Reservations for dinner tables
may be made at the time of pur
chasing the tickets.
