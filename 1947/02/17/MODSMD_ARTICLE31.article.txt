# History Contest To Remain Open
## 
### 
Undergraduate students may still
turn in essays for the Colonial
Dames contest, according to Dr.
Max Savelle, history professor. The
deadline is March 1.
The essays may be on any topic
concerning early American history
prior to 173. Manuscripts should be
no more than 10,000 words, 5,000
words being adequate. They should
be typed, double-space, and in du
plicate form.
Those interested may contact
Professors Savelle, Bailey, Bestor,
Knoles, or White. History teachers
will aid students in finding topics
and material.
At the end of winter quarter, the
$100 award will be announced.
