# ROBLE'S "SHALIMAR" FRIDAY IN WOMEN'S GYM
## 
### 
Tickets are on sale at the A.S.S.U.
Office for 1>2.50 per couple to "Shali
mar," Roble's Winter Formal which
will be held Friday night from 9
to 1 in the Women's Gym.
Stan Robinson and his band will
play for the dance. Purple, silver,
and black decorations will carry out
the Persian theme, according to
Hetty Fannin, chairman.
A limited number of passes will
be distributed at the dance so that
couples may spend part of the time
at the Lagunita Formal, which is
to be held the same night in the La
gunita dining room.
