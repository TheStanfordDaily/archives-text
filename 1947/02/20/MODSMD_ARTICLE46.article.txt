# Badminton Club Invites Players
## 
### 
Stanford's Badminton Club in
vites students and faculty members
to play tonight at 7:30 in the Wom
en's Gym.
Last year the club, discontinued
during the war, was reorganized. In
May, the National Badminton As
sociation sponsored an Oakland
open city tournament. Four Stan
ford players entered, and one
brought back a trophy.
This year the club meets every
Thursday night at 7:30 in the Wom
en's Gym. The highlight of the sea
son will be a men's singles and
mixed doubles tournament for Stan
lord students only.
