# Decision Planned On War Memorial
## 
### 
The War Memorial Committee
will meet Saturday. March 1. at 10
a.m. in General Secretary Thomas
P. Carpenter's office in an attempt
to reach a final decision concerning
Stanford's war memorial.
Information about war memorials
on other campuses will be presented
at this time. This material was ob
tained by a survey to give the com
mittee suggestions for fitting war
memorials.
The committee will also discuss
the information turned in by Patty
Whittemore, chairman of the Stu
dent Union Committee.
