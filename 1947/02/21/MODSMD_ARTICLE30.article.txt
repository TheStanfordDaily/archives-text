# Wesleyan Official To Talk at Farm
## 
### 
The Wesley Foundation of the
Methodist Church will have Jim
Chubb, National Methodist Youth
Secretary, as speaker for a week.
Chubb will speak Sunday at the
11 a.m. Methodist Church service,
at the 6:45 p.m. Youth Chapel in
the church, and at 7:30 at the Wes
ley Foundation at G25 Hamilton.
During the week he will speak
Tuesday at 8 p.m. at an informal
"chat and chew" at GG6 Channing
Avenue; Thursday at 8 p.m. in the
"Y" room of the Women's Club
house; and Friday at 7:30 p.m. at
the Stanford Village Clubhouse.
All students and their guests are
welcome at the meetings.
