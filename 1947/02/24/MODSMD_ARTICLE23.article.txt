# Carillon Recital Begins A.R.C. Drive Today
## 
### 
James R. Lawson, earillonncur,
will present a special Red Cross
recital on the 35-bell carillon of
the Hoover Library at noon today
to Inaugurate the annual student
fund drive.
His program will consist of the
Welsh folk song "March of the
Men of Harlech," and the folk
songs "Waltzing Matilda" from
Australia, "Santa Lucia" from It
aly, "Die Lorelei" from Germany,
and '"Au Clair de la Lunc" front
France.
