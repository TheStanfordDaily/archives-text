# ‘No Theme’ Is Theme Of Alpha Sig Dance
## 
###

on music and refreshments at their
formal dance Saturday from 9 to
p.m. The Melody Masters will
play.

dance. Tom Self, social chairman,
said. "We are tired of racking our
brains for a new theme."
