# Anthology Prints Winters' Fiction
## 
### 
Professor Yvor Winters of Un-
English Department has contributed
a short story, "The Brink of Dark
ness," to "Anchor in the Sea," an
anthology of psychological fiction,
which makes its appearance today.
His wife, Janet Lewis Winters,
has a short historical novel, "The
Wife of Martin Guerre," published
in the same volume.
Another local writer, Jessamyn
West, author of "The Friendly Per
suasion,"' is represented by
short story, "Hie Child's Day."
