# Tresidder Concert To Feature Tenor
## 
### 
Tagliavini, Italian tenor, will be
featured in the quarter's final Music
at Home recorded concert at Dr.
Tresidder's home tonight from 7:30
to 8:30, according to James Schwa
bacher, program director.
The "Gaites Parisiennes" ballet
music, Mozart's "Horn Concerto,"
the fourth movement from Mahler's
"Fourth Symphony," and the Pre
lude to "Carmen" will also be heard.
Students and faculty are cordially
invited to attend.
