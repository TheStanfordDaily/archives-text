# Student Quartet To Play Classics
## 
### 
The Stanford String Quartet will
play chamber music tonight at H in
the Women's Clubhouse, according
to Miss Audrey Aasen. director.
The program, the last in the series
of student recitals sponsored by
Music Council, will include music
by Mozart, Beethoven, Franck, and
Debussy.
All students and faculty are in
vited. Admission is free.
