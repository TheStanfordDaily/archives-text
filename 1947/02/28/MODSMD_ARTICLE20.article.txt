# SMYTH TO TALK
## 
### 
Dr. Henry D. Smyth, author of
the government's report on atom
bomb projects, will speak on the
history of nuclear physics in the
Cubberley Auditorium at 4:15 p.m.
today. His lecture is sponsored by
Sigma Xi and will be open to the
public, according to Dr. Carl G.
Lindquist, secretary of the Stanford
chapter of Sigma Xi.
