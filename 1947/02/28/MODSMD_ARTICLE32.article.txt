# Elks' Scholarship Forms Due by Tomorrow
## 
### 
Application blanks for the Elks
National Foundation Scholarship
Contest must be filed by tomorrow.
Blanks may be obtained at the of
fice of Mrs. Olivia Byler, scholar
ship secretary, in the Administra
tion Building.
Prizes ranging from $100 to $700
will be awarded to those who win
the "Most Valuable Student
Award" in this nationwide contest.
Applicants will be judged on the
criteria of personality, citizenship,
general worthiness, leadership, and
patriotism.
