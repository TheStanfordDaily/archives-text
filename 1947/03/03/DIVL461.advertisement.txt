. WHAT IS SERVICE?
Wo Of the Crow Pharmacy feel that it is the added effort put forth in ministering to your
prescription, drug and toiletry needs. For your convenience we invite students' charge
accounts, and are more than glad to deliver your purchases. You will be struck immediately
by the atmosphere of courtesy and friendliness. Whether it's cashing a check, filling your
doctor s prescription, or selling you perfume, we want you to be—and feel—a member of
the Stanford Crow Family Just ask yourself, "Where else can an Injun lad or lassie get
SO MUCH for SO LITTLE? . . nowhere, of course! So won't you drop in and take
ndvantage of our Services?
