# 'Well Logging' Talk On for Geologists
## 
### 
Schlumberger Well Surveying
Corporation will present a lecture
on "Electrical Well Logging" Wed
nesday at 1 p.m. in Room 320, ac
cording to A. I. Levorsen, dean of
the Department of Mineral Sciences.
Motion pictures will accompany
the lecture, which will be given by
Mr. Milton E. Loy and Mr. R. D.
Ford. The lecture is open to all
students and faculty members.
The lecturers will be available for
individual questioning before the
lecture begins from 2 until 4 p.m.
