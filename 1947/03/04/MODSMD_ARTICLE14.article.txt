# Cap and Gown Members Must Pay Dues Today
## 
### 
Cap and Gown members are to
pay their dues today, according to
president Marie Wagner. Payment
can be made either to Miss Wagner
at Russell House or to Alice Lovett
at the Women's Gym. Club dues arc
$1.50, but new members are being
assessed MO cents more to cover the
extra cost of pins. Miss Wagner
said.
