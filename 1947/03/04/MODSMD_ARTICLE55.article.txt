# Students Offered Courses in Mexico
## 
### 
College students throughout the
United States have been invited to
attend the two summer centers of
Mexico and Guatemala, sponsored
by the University of Houston, Presi
dent E. E. Oberholtzer of the Uni
versity of Houston has announced.
The courses offer at low cost
standard college credit in sociol
ogy, history, or Spanish to students
of all departments and divisions,
on both undergraduate and gradu
ate levels.
These centers are recognized by
the Veterans Administration and
veterans receive their usual subsist
ence allowance. Knowledge of
Spanish is not required.
Further information may be ob
tained from Dr. Joseph S. Werlin at
the University of Houston, Houston,
Texas.
