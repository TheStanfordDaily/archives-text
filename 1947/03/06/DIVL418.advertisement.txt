%
"The ninute they met. it was friendship at first sight.

and it was the marvelous coffee served here 24 hours.
It's fresh all the time. Everybody likes it. It's fit for a
king. Come in and try it some time. And maybe you'll be
lucky enough to meet the Duke.
(Signed)
