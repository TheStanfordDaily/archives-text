###

three or four students to be sent
abroad on the A.S.S.U. - sponsored
summer survey trips will be named
the first week of next quarter. These
students will be chosen from the 10
students, selected out of the 80 ap
plicants, through interviews with
the I.S.C. selection board.

on the referendum "Do You Want
1.5.C.?" to be held early next quar
ter as announced bv Martin Ander
son, A.S.S.U. president, in yester
