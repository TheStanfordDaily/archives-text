# CORONATION BALL BIDS SELL

### 
Bids will go on sale next week for
the Chinese Club's "Coronation
Ball," a semi-formal dance in the
Colonial Poom of the St. Francis
Hotel on April 5, announced Baron
Lowe, president.
A queen will Im* elected at the
ball, which is open to the campus,
