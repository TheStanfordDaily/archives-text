# ROTH RESIGNS FROM N.F.A.S.
## 
### 
Almon E. Roth, former Stanford
comptroller, has resigned his post
as head of the National Federation
of American Shippers because of ill
health and will return to his San
Francisco law practice. Frazer A.
Bailey, president of Matson Naviga
tion Company, will succeed him.
Roth was the first president of the
N.F.A.S.
