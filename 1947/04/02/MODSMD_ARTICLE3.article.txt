# French History Will Be Taught
## 
### 
Dorothy Thompson, instructor in
history, will take over a class in
French history spring quarter.
The course will cover a general
survey of the political, economic,
and cultural development of France.
Particular emphasis will be placed
on the last four centuries of French
history and on the political and so
cial problems of the Third and
Fourth Republics.
Dr. Thompson lived in France,
where she studied at the Sorbonnc.
