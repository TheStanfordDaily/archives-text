# New Students to Have Chest X-rays Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford Health Service requests
all new students to report tomorrow
for chest X-rays. The X-ray trailer
will be stationed at the rear of the
Women's Gym. The trailer will be
open from 9 to 12 and from 1 to 4.
In addition to new students, all
old students who have not yet had
chest X-rays are also requested to
report to the trailer.
