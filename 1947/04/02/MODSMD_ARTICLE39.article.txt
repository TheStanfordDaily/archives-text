# Toyon, Eating Clubs To Present Firesides
## 
### 
Beginning this quarter, Toyon
Club and Stanford's seven eating
clubs will jointly present a fireside
in the Toyon lounge every Friday
night, according to Toyon social
chairman, Bob Brown.
These affairs will be open to all
Toyon and eating club men. Two or
three outside living -groups will also
be invited each week.
