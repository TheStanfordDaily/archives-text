# Near East Study Given in Spring
## 
### 
Dr. Wayne Vucinich of the De
partment of History will teach a
course on "History of the Near East
Since 1800," this quarter.
The course will trace the rise and
development of nationalism, liber
alism, and socialism in the Near
East. Special emphasis will be
placed on history of Balkan states
and Turkey. Pan-Slavism and Pan-
Arabism will also be included.
The course will be given at 11
o'clock on Mondays, Wednesdays,
and Fridays.
Dr. Vucinich joined the faculty in
the autumn from the State Depart
ment.
