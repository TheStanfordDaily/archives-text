# Track Signup Closes Today
## 
### 
Today is the last clay for men in
terested in entering the intramural
track meet to sign up, Gene Cold
iron, director of intramural activi
ties, announced last night.
A new signup list is being com
piled and all men who participated
in last quarter's track meet will
have to sign up again in order to
be eligible to compete.
Intramural managers can bring
the list of names, with the events
the men wish to participate in, to
Jack Weiershauser at the athletic
office.
Preliminary trials will be held on
Thursday and the finals will be held
