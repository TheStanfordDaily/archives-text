# Two Faculty Men Win Army Awards
## 
### 
Dr. Wayne S. Vucinich and Major
William R. Kugler of the Stanford
faculty will bo awarded Bronze Star
medals at a full-dress parade held
by the Stanford R.O.T.C. today at
3:45 on the soccer field.
Dr. Vucinich was cited by the
War Department for meritorious
service in co-operating with the
Army as an expert on Balkan af
fairs. A lieutenant in the Navy, he
was loaned to the Army to carry
out liaison work with the Russian
and Bulgarian forces in Bulgaria
during 1944 and 1945. A graduate
of the University of California, his
home is in Wilmington, California.
Major Kugler was cited for his
activities as 'a supply officer in Eng
land and on the continent during
the invasion of Europe in 1944. A
graduate of U.C.L.A., he resides in
Los Angeles.
