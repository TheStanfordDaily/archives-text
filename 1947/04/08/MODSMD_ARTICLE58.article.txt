# Folk Dance Festival To Be Held at Stanford
## 
### 
â–  Members of all the clubs of the
California Folk Dance Federation
have been invited to the Stanford
campus to participate in the Folk
Dance Festival to be held the after
noon and evening of May 11.
Fifteen hundred will dance in the
afternoon program, according to
members of the Hoedowners' Club.
The evening program will include
professional exhibitions and dances
for group participation.
