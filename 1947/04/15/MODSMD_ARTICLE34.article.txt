# French Club to Hear San Francisco Consul
## 
###

day at 4:15 p.m. in the lower lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse, accord
ing to Nancy Smith, leader of the
group.
A. Rousselet, French vice-consul
at San Francisco, will speak in
French on "La Situation de la
France dans le Monde."
