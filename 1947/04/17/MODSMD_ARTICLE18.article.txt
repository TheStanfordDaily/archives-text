# Mothers' Club to Give Village Books, Journals
## 
### 
Books and subscriptions to maga
zines will soon be given to the Vil
lage for their new reception room,
according to the East Bay Chapter
of the Stanford Mothers' Club.
The Town and Gown Club in
Oakland will be the setting of a
benefit matinee tea to be held May
6 at 2 p.m.
