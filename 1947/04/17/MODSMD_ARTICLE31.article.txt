# Fitzpatrick Band At Toyon Formal
## 
### 
"Only two more days are left
to buy bids for the annual Toyon
spring formal this Friday," Toyon
social chairman Bob Brown an
nounced last, night.
"We're going to have the smooth
music of Eddie Fitzpatrick and a
spring atmosphere with everything
but the swallows," said Brown.
Bids are now on sale at the A.S.-
S.U. office for $2.40.
