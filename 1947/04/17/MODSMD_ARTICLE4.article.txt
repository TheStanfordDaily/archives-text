# Organist to Play In Church Today
## 
### 
An organ recital will be given in
the Memorial Church today at 4:15
p.m., according to D. Sterling
Wheelwright, acting organist.. The
program will include: "'When Thou
Art Near," Bach; "Fantasy, A
Mighty Fortress,'' Faulkes;
"Dreams," Stoughton; "Spring
Dance." Anonymous; and "Pilgrims'
Chorus," Wagner.
