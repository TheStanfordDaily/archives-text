# Boxers: Pick Up Medals at Gym
## 
### 
Winners of last quarter's All -
University boxing matches can pick
up their medals at the Gym store,
it was announced recently by the
Athletic office.
The "following are entitled to
awards: Bobby Lobdell, 125 pounds;
George Jess, 130 pounds; Pete Wil
lett, 135 pounds; Frank Hewitt, 145
pounds; Ferris Boothe, 155 pounds;
Yank Levin, 175 pounds; and Joe
Crammer, heavyweight.
