# Eating Clubs Meet Rushees
## 
### 
The first group of prospective eat
ing club members ate with the Stan
ford eating clubs last night in the
Encina Commons. Two other large
groups have been invited for lunch
and dinner Monday.
Of the almost 200 rushees who at
tended Wednesday night's open
house in Toyon, approximately 120
signed up for a meal with one of
the clubs, Inter-Club rushing chair
man Norm Swanson estimated.
These men will all have eaten a me j l
with a club by Monday, he said.
However, sign-up lists for e
will remain posted in t..e ~..
until Saturday and at
the end of next week. Men ~i o nni 0
on these lists will dine with .he
clubs during the week.
Eating club members assisting
Swanson on the Inter-Club Coun
cil rushing committee include John
Thompson, El Toro; Dick Lavin,
Los Arcos; Phil Tuttle, El Capitan;
Evan Pugh, El Quadro; and Art
Baker, El Tigrc.
