# Folk Dance Group Plans Bay Region Festival
## 
### 
The "Hoedowners" will meet to
night at 7:30 in the Women's Gym.
All Stanford students, whether be
ginners or advanced, who are inter
ested in folk-dancing are invited to
attend. In the future the group will
meet on Friday instead of Monday
nights so that mou students may
come.
On May 12 the Hoedowners will
act as hosts to various folk-dance
groups from the Bay Region at a
folk-dance festival to be held on the
campus and in Palo Alto
