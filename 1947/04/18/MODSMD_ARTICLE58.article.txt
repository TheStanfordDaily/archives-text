# Squad Races For 'Orphans'
## 
### 
Stanford's Crew resumes action
this Sunday with an interclass race
which will feature festivities of the
opening of the Palo Alto Yacht
Club's current season. Entered in
the race will be freshman, sophomore,
and upper division shells. The one
mile contest, which will end at the
Yacht Club boat house, is scheduled
to start at 12:30.
Coach Lou Lindsey has commented
that a large number of new oars
men are developing very rapidly,
and several of them will be seen in
action Sunday for the first time. s
