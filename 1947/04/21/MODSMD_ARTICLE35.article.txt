# Debaters Win Recognition In Chicago
## 
### 
Stanford debaters Robert Bennett
and Donald Logan returned from
the National Student Congress held
in Chicago under the auspices of
Delta Sigma Rho, honorary forensic
fraternity, with the distinction of
having had their proposals for labor
legislation and federal medical as
sistance pass the national assembly,
according to Leland T. Chapin,
Stanford debate coach.
Bennett, debate manager, was
elected door leader of the Republi
can or "right of center" party at the
convention.
Logan had previously received
first-place speaking honors in a con
test for California state universities.
Several Eastern universities have
extended invitations to send a de
bate team to the East next year.
