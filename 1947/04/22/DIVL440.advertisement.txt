My Search Is Over!"
cried Diogenes, dropping his lantern
EVERY TIME DIOGENES comes into our shop and gets a look
at Art Tracy (our honest shop foreman) he drops his lantern in surprise.
(The cleanup man then wearily gets out his broom and sweeps up the glass.)
"Here," says Diogenes, as well as our other customers, "Here is an
honest mechanic!" (Imagine that!)
Stanford students hearing of the strictly fair deal here at Palo Alto
Nash Company have driven in for repairs and service. And Art Tracy and
his carefully chosen staff have pleased them mightily by skilled work and
that fair deal.
Why don't you drive in next time your car (any make) needs attention?
It will certainly be to your advantage if you do!
Count- on that.
Palo Alto Company
Dick Bullis Walter Bl.ick
High at Channing, Palo Alto Telephone 6650
