# Willis to Lecture Tonight on Latins
## 
### 
Bailey Willis, professor emeritus
of geology, will give the Hispanic
Series lecture tonight at 7:30 in the
Cubberley Auditorium, according to
Ronald Hilton of the Romanic Lan
guages Department.
Dr. Willis will speak on "Our
South American Neighbors." The
lecture will be given under the joint
auspices of the Committee on Pub
lic Exercises and the Committee on
Latin American Studies, Hilton said.
