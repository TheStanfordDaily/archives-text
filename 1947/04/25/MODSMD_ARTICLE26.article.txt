# Hindustan Group Elects Officers; Makes Plans
## 
### 
The Stanford chapter of the Hin
dustan Students' Association met at
the Village Sunday for the first time
this quarter.
Newly elected officers include Shi
dram Hegde, president; Ved Vrat,
secretary; and A. Ibrahim, treas
urer. Plans for lectures and a picnic
later in the quarter were discussed.
