# S.F. School Gives Fine Arts Course
## 
### 
The California School of Fine Arts
announces its six-week summer ses
sion beginning June 23, according
to Douglas MacAgy, school director.
Enrollments are now being taken
at the school offices, 800 Chestnut
street, San Francisco, between 2
and 5 p.m. Monday through Friday
and 6:30 to 8 p.m. Monday, Wed
nesday, and Friday. Both day and
evening courses will be given.
Classes in portraiture, landscape,
design, and sculpture will be offered.
