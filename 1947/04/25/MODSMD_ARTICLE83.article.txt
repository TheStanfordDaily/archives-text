# MEN'S UNMENTIONABLES
## 
### 
What the book totting Esqie wears
or doesn't wear under his pants and
shirt is entirely his own business.
However an informal survey by the
nation's fashion experts in locker
rooms, fraternity houses, and public
baths brought the following results:
The most worn underpinnings of
the average male today are navy
white, army khaki, and marine
green. Only the fortunates who are
able to procure them are wearing
the more popular, form-fitting "snug
gies."
