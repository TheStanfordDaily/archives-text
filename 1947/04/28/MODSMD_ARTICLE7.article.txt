## 
###

Political Science, will review "Thun
der Out of China" today for the
Chinese-American Friendship Group
at 4:15 in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse, according to
Jerry Rubin, club president.
The book is co-authored by Teddy
White find Annalee Jacoby. Miss
Jaeoby is a Stanford graduate.
