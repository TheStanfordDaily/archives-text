# Band to Perform In Bowl Concert
## 
### 
Stanford University's 80 - piece
band will present a concert Sunday
at 4 p.m. in the Frost Amphitheater,
director Julius E. Shucat announced
yesterday.
The hour-long program will be
open to the public without charge.
Highlight of the Sunday concert
will be the presentation of "Atlan
tis Suite," by Safranek, director
Shucat said. The number deals with
the mythical continent which sup
posedly left only the Azores Islands
as evidence of its existence.
