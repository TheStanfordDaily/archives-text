# Plastic Leg Snags A Jillion Coppers
## 
### 
"Pitch your pennies at the plastic
leg in front of the Cellar, and make
your contribution to the Convales
cent Home," advised Jeanne Foy
ers, chairman of the Copper Coin
Drive.
"The leg is almost filled now. but
it will still hold a few more copper
coins," she added.
The filled leg will be displayed at
the Sophomore Carnival, and people
may guess the number of coins in it
to win a prize, according to Miss
Rogers.
