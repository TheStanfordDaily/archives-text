dates for graduation in June ap
peared in Friday's and Monday's
Dailies. The list published below
contains only the names of addi
tional candidates whose applications
for the A.B. and B.S. degrees had
been received up to noon Thursday,
according to Miss Miriam F. Remele,
editorial secretary in the Registrar's
office. The complete provisional list
of 578 names is posted in front of
the Administration Building.

day for filing applications for grad
uation in June is tomorrow. Appli
cation blanks may bo obtained at
the Information desk in the Regis
