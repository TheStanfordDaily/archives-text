# This Work Deserves Praise
## 
### 
Last week's Convalescent Home Drive
broke all records set by previous Con Home
Drives. The success of this program is due
to the careful planning and efforts of the stu
dents working on the campaign.
The chairman, Bob Coate, as well as all the
committee members working with him de
serve a great deal of credit for executing their
jobs with precision and for forfeiting their
time to make the drive go over the top.
The smoothness of the campaign procedure
can be attributed in a large part to the thor
ough plans which were laid out far in advance
of the week. Profiting by the mistakes of
former Con Home Drive chairmen, Coate and
his helpers set out to top former drives.
All the events of the program were enjoyed
by the students who participated in them. A
special vote of thanks should go to the on
and off-campus solicitors who spent time and
effort in collecting contributions.
Stanford can be proud of its part in sup
porting the Con Home this year.—M.S.
