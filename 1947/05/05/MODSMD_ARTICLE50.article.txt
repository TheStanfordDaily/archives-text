# Archibald Writes Story On Shipyard Disunity
## 
### 
Mrs. Katherine Archibald, Stan
ford history instructor, published a
new book, April 26, entitled "War
time Shipyard, A Study of Social
Disunity."
Mrs. Archibald began work at
the Moore Drydock in 1042 to gather
material for the book, a comprehen
sive study of the social conflicts
and disunity in American shipyards
during the war.
