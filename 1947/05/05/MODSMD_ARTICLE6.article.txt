# Gallery to Exhibit Egyptian Textiles
## 
### 
A collection of 40 ancicnt Egyp
tian textiles—most of them taken
from tombs—will go on display to
morrow for three weeks at the
Stanford Art Gallery.
The textiles, made by the Copts
during the first to ninth centurics,
are an important addition to the
University's collection.
Other forms of Egyptian
bronzes, jewelry, and mummy
masks—will augment the exhibit.
Regular gallery hours are Tues
day through Saturday, 10 a.m. to 5
p.m., and Sunday from 1 to 5 p.m.
The exhibit will also be open after
the Tuesday Evening Series to
morrow night.
