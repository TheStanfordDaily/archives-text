# Phone Companies Reject Proposal
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, May 4 (UP).—
Striking long distance telephone
workers tonight accepted a govern
ment compromise proposal to end
their nationwide walkout with a
$5.14 wage increase, but the Amer
ican Telephone and Telegraph Com
pany rejected it.
