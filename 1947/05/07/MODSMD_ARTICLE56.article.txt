# Organist to Present Recital Today at 4 :15
## 
### 
D. Sterling Wheelwright, acting
University organist, will present an
organ recital at 4:15 p.m. today in
Memorial Church.
Dr. Wheelwright's program will
include "Prelude and Fugue in G
Minor," by Bach; "Arioso," by Bach;
the Allegro movement from "Organ
Symphony No. 1," by Maquaire;
"Reverie," by Bonnet; "Gavotte,"
by Thomas; "An Old Melody"; and
"Toccata, Symphony No. 5," by Wi
dor.
