# Vets' Center Moves To New Office Units
## 
### 
The Stanford Veterans' Center
is moving from the Ad Building
today into Building 3 of the pre
fabricated office units, according
to Veterans' Co-ordinator George
I). Barahal.
The new Center, including
Guidance, the Records Office, and
all veterans' officials, will be open
for business tomorrow.
Building 3 is the farthest unit
east of the Education Building.
The Records Office will occupy
one wing of the unit, the Guid
ance Center the other, and Mr.
Barahal's office will be in the
section joining the two wings.
