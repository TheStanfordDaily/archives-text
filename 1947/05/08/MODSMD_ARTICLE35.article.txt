# BULLETIN BOARD
## 
### 
MEN'S ROOM DEPOSITS of $10
can be paid Monday or Tuesday
at the cashiers' booths in the lobby
of the Administration Building
from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The mon
ey should be turned in together
with a room application - agree
ment card.
INTER - VARSITY CHRISTIAN
FELLOWSHIP will meet tonight
at 7 in the YWCA room of the
Women's Clubhouse.
CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANI
ZATION will meet in the Vestry
of the Memorial Church tonight
at 7.
GRAI) WOMEN will have a salad

luncheon today at noon in the
lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
INSTITUTE OF INTERNATIONAL
RELATIONS will meet today at
5 p.m. in the Excom room of the
Excom room of the Nitery. All
interested students are asked to
attend.
CHINESE CLUB will tonight
at 7:15 in the Chinese Clubhouse
to discuss the rebuilding of the
house's foundation, according to
Jack Quan, president.
STANFORD DAMES bridge section
will meet tonight at 7:45 in the
lower lounge of the Women's

Clubhouse. Wives of Stanford
students are invited.
THE NEWMAN CLUB will hold
its regular discussion meeting to
night at 7:30 in the Parish Hall.
WAA BOARD will meet at 7:30 to
night in the Women's Gym.
THE RIFLE TEAM will 'meet to
night at 7 in the Range building.
THE CHINESE CLUB will hold its
last meeting of the quarter to
night at 7:15 in the Women's
Clubhouse.
