SPRING SHOW REHEARSALS
Tonight, full cast excluding cho
rus, board of directors, arid pro
moters will meet at 7:15 in the
Ram's Head room. Tomorrow the
entire company and orchestra will
Rehearse at a.m., and Sunday
at 2 p.m. on the main stage of the
Memorial Auditorium.
MEN'S ROOM DEPOSITS of $10
can be paid Monday or Tuesday
at the cashiers' booths in the lob
by of the Administration Building
from 9 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. The
money should be turned in to
gether with a room application
agreement card.
BRANDEIS CLUB will meet this
Monday evening at 7:30 in the
lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse. Dr. Frederic Spiegel -
berg will be guest speaker.
lIOEDOWNERS will meet tonight
at 7:30 in the Women's Gym.
WESLEY FOUNDATION will meet
Sunday at 7:30 p.m. at the Meth
odist Church, 025 Hamilton Ave
nue. A panel discussion will fea
ture "Students Look at the State
of the Church."
QUADS are on sale at the Quad of
fice for $5.75.
FIRST BAPTIST CHURCH speak
ers' series will present a discus
sion of "Immortality," Sunday at
5 p.m.
SOCIAL PROGRAM evaluation
cards are due today, and may be
sent to Box 222(5. Those sent to
sponsors must be turned in to
morrow.
