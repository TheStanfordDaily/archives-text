# Catholic Students Plan Beach Party
## 
### 
All Stanford Catholics are invited
to a beach party at San Gregorio
tomorrow, according to A 1 Forbes,
president of Newman Club.
Cars will be leaving the Post Of
fice on campus and the Village gates
at 11 a.m. and 1 p.m.

Newman Club.
Those interested should call A 1

information.
