# Buck Meets Ten Rhodes Candidates
## 
### 
Dr. Philip W. Buck met with ten
prospective candidates for Rhodes
Scholarships Wednesday afternoon
in the West Memorial Library to
discuss the method of selection and
the terms of competition for the
scholarships.
The living costs, courses of study,
scholastic requirements, and de
grees granted at Oxford were also
reviewed. Dr. Buck said that it is
possible for the student to spend
more than half the year living in
Europe.
