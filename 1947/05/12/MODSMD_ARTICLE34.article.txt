# Escort Extinguishes Date's Flaming Tresses
## 
###

saved Ruth Woolf, Manzanita, from
serious injury Saturday night. As
Miss Woolf leaned over her birth
day cake to blow out the candles,
her long hair caught fire.
Her escort, John R. Walker,
quickly grabbed her hair and smoth
