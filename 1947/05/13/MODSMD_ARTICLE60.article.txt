# Ship Tickets Sell For Europe Trip
## 
### 
Round-trip steamship transporta
tion will be available to students
traveling to a summer session at
Zurich, Switzerland, at a cost of

about $328.50, according to Alfred
Munk, chairman of the Internation
al Information Institute.

The ship will sail from New Yoik
between June 21 and 25 and will
leave Le Havre, France, on or be
fore Sept. 10. Other transportation
will be provided between Le Havre
and Zurich at a cost of about $20
each way, Munk said.
