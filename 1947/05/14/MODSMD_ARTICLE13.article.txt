# Student to Attend UNESCO Meeting
## 
### 
Lura G. Camery, Pi Lambda The
ta, has registered to represent Stan
ford in the Mountain - Plains Re
gional Conference of the United Na
tions Educational, Scientific, and
Cultural Organization, accoxding to
the Social Science Foundation of
the University of Denver. The con
ference will take place tomorrow,
Friday, and Saturday.
Sponsored by the United Nations
Commission for UNESCO, the con
ference is part of that organization's
program of serving as a liaison
agency with American organizations
interested in its work.
The conference will aim to ac
quaint the delegates with UNESCO's
program and to assist them in de
veloping plans for their own com
munities and groups.
