# Grail Offers $100 Prize For Top Spanish Major
## 
### 
A $100 prize will be given an
nually to the Spanish major grad
uating in June with the best rec
ord for the last two years at Stan
ford, by Miss Mary Vertrees. Miss
Vertrees, who got her A.B. in Span
ish in 1912 and her M.A. in 1925 at
Stanford, and now teaches at the
Huntington Park High School, is
giving the annual prize in memory
of her parents.
