# Toyon Residents To Vote Monday
## 
### 
Residents of Toyon go to the polls
Monday to vote for Hall officers,
Gordon Osser announced today.
"Ballots may be cast in the Toyon
office from noon until (5 p.m.," Osser
declared.
Nominees for the office of presi
dent include: Bruce Allen, Frank
Hubbard, and Jack Young.
Vice-presidential candidates are:
Herbert Camp, Erwin Mittleman,
and Ralph Miller.
Allen Buckley, Harry Blake, and
Chuck Moore are running for Hall
secretary.
