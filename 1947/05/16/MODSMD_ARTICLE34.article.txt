# Men to Sign Up For Village Rooms
## 
### 
Village men who have made their
room deposits for fall quarter may
sign house charts Monday and
Tuesday in the Village Post Office,
Claire Cunningham, assistant Vil
lage Counselor, announced yester
day.
Assignments to buildings will be
made in accordance with the previ
ously announced breakdown, he
said.
In those buildings where there is
a mixture of .single rooms and dor
mitory space, drawings for the
rooms will be made at the time of
signing up.
Mr. Cunningham warned that
signups will have to be made before
Friday to get preference in room as
signments. After that time, it will
be on a first come, first served basis,
with present residents getting no
preference over new students. New
students will be taken in order of
application.
