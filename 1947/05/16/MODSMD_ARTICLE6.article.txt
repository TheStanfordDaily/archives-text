# Cars Collide, Farm Students Injured
## 
### 
Five Stanford students were in
jured Wednesday when cars driven
by Bill Purdy and Stanley Cottrell
collided on Kings Mountain Road.
Besides the drivers, Mary Lee
Prouty, Tom Belknap, and Valerie
La Breche were injured. Miss Prouty
suffered a broken leg. Miss Le Breche
was treated for cuts and bruises.
