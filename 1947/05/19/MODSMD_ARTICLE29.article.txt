# Toyon Hall to Elect New Officers Today
## 
### 
Residents of Toyon Hall go to the
polls today to cast their votes for
hall officers, Cordon O.'ser, election
chairman, announced today.
Nominees for the ollice of presi
dent include: Bruce Allen, Frank
Hubbard, and Jack Yuun; 1 ,.
Vice-presidential candidates are
Herbert Camp, Erwin Mitt'.eman. and
Ralph Mi lies.
Allen Buckley, Larry Blake, and
Chuck Moore are running for hall
secretary.
