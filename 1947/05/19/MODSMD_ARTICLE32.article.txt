# Guatemala Offers Summer Courses
## 
### 
"Complete information is now
available on two summer schools in
Guatemala" announced Lois David
son, chairman of the education com
mittee of the International Infor
mation Institute.
The two universities will have
sessions from the middle of July to
the middle of August. They will be
iun by American universities and
taught in English. The whole trip
will cost about $700.
Both schools plan field trips and
excursions in connection with the
classes.
Those desiring information should
call Miss Davidson at Lagunila.
