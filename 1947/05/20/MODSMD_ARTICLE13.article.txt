# Toyon Accepts University's Flag Riot Bill
## 
### 
Concurring with the recommend
ation of the Hall committee ap
pointed last week to investigate the
Jolly Roger riot, Toyon students last
night voted to accept the $423 bill
assessed by the University.
The committee reported that the
charges were not excessive. They
will be split with Encina and the
Village.
Bill McPherson, president of Toy
on, announced results of yesterday's
election. New officers are:
Frank Hubbard, president; Herb
ert Camp, vice-president; and Allen
Buckley, secretary.
Dr. Robert N. Bush, director of
the Appointment Service, gave a
talk on the Appointment Service,
credit evaluation, and job opportuni
ties and trends.
