# Housing Com Will Revamp
## 
### 
Housing Committee will select
new members and officers this week,
to be announced Thursday, accord
ing to Taffy Countryman, chair
man.
All women interested in being on
the committee next year should turn
in their names to the house presi
dents, who will in turn take the list
to area excom meetings. There a
selection will be made of double the
number of places to be filled on
Housing Committee.
Advisory Board of Women's Con
ference, composed of the officers of
Conference, and the presidents of
the area excoms will appoint the
new members and submit their
names to Women's Conference for
approval Thursday.
