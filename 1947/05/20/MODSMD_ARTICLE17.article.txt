# Series Film Shows Rise Of Movies
## 
### 
One of the March of Time movie
series, "The Movies March On," and
a motion picture of the American
Merchant Marine, "America Sails
the Seas," will be shown tonight at
7:30 on the Tuesday Evening Series.
The March of Time movie, pro
duced in 1939, shows the progress
of American films from the earliest
efforts to contemporary techniques.
Excerpts from many well - known
pictures are included as illustra
tions. The Museum of Modern Art's
work in collecting, annotating, and
distributing historic movies is also
described in the film.
"America Sails the Seas" tells
how the Merchant. Marine operates,
where it goes, what it carries, and
why it is vital to national defense
and peacetime commerce. Combat
scenes are also included.
Material from the Hopkins Trans
portation Library is on exhibit in
the main corridors of the Univer
sity Library as a companion feature
to the movies.
