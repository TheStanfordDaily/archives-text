# Theta Xi's Schedule Annual Costume Dance
## 
### 
"Come to our dance dressed like
characters from Rue Pigalle or the
Montmartre," advised Frank Shoes
ter, chairman of Theta Xi's annual
closed costume dance, scheduled for
Saturday night at the fraternity
house.
Decorations and refreshments will
be in keeping with the theme for
the dance, "La Soiree Apache."
Shoester promised.
