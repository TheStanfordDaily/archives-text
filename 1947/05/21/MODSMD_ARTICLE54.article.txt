# C. Langdon White To Teach in Lima
## 
### 
Professor C. Langdon White will
be visiting professor of geography at
Ohio State University during the
summer quarter, and will spend the
academic year 1947 - 1948 at San
Marcos University, Lima, Peru.
At San Marcos University, Pro
fessor White will collaborate with
the city of Lima in building up an
Institute of Geography and he will
serve as visiting professor of social
geography at San Marcos.
After his work at San Marcos,
Mr. White will travel in each of the
South American countries doing
field work and research in human
geography. His primary interest
will be in studying industrial po
tentialities throughout South Amer
ica.
Mr. White is also under contract
to write a geography of Latin Amer
ica.
