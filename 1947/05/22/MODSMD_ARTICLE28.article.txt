# Law Association To Hold Election
## 
### 
Run-off election for the offices of
president, vice-president, and sec
retary of the Law Association will
be today from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. in
front of the Law Library.
Morton Silvers and John Whit
ney are the candidates for the presi
dency. For th« vice-presidency three
candidates, John Hannegan, Richard
Lyon, and Bill Glenny, are still
running because of a tie for second
place. Jack Burdick and Irving
Hellman are the candidates for sec
retary.
Ward Hower was elected treas
urer and Dick Jencks executive
board member in the elections held
yesterday.
