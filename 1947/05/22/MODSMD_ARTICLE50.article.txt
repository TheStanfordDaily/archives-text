# Theta Xi Apache Dance To Bring Paris Scenes
## 
### 
Theta Xi fraternity will bring
Paris sewers and sidewalk cafes to
the campus Saturday night from 9
to 1 in their revival of a pre-war
house function, the Apache Dance,
said Frank Schuster, social chair
man.
Guests at the closed dance are re
quested to dress for the occasion, he
said. Patrons for the dance are: Mr.
and Mrs. Ward B. Kindy; Mr. and
Mrs. Juan B. Rael, and Mr. and Mrs.
Loren Slentz.
