# AVC to Sponsor Concert of Ballads
## 
### 
Earl Robinson, composer of "Bal
lad for Americans" and noted bal
lad singer, will present a concert of
favorite American songs in the Palo
Alto High School auditorium Thurs
day, it was announced Wednesday
by Mrs. Sylvia Hoyt, secretary of
Stanford AVC.
The concert will be co-sponsored
by the Palo Alto and Stanford chap
ters of AVC and money obtained
will be used to send delegates to
the National AVC convention this
June in Milwaukee. Tickets for the
performance will sell for $1 per
seat.
Tickets will be available at the
Peninsula Box Office in Palo Alto,
in front of the Cellar Friday, Mon
day, and Tuesday, or from any AVC
member.
