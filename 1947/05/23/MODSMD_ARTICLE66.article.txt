# Alumnus Named To FCC Position
## 
### 
President Truman has nominated
a graduate of Stanford for a new
seven - year term on the Federal
Communications Commission. He is
Roy C. Wakefield, 51-year-old for
mer resident of Palo Alto and
Fresno.
Wakefield will begin his new term
July 1, if his nomination is con
firmed. He was originally appointed
to the FCC by the late President
Roosevelt after having served as
president of the California Railroad
Commission. His apponitment to
the Railroad Commission by Gov.
Frank C. Merriam in 1937 followed
a career of practicing law and hold
ing numerous public offices in
Fresno.
