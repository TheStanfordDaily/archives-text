# Envelopes Required To Receive Grades
## 
### 
Students who wish to get their
grades for spring quarter during
vacation should leave a stamped,
self - addressed envelope at the
Information window in the Ad
ministration Building before the
end of this quarter.
Envelopes will he sold at the
Information window for five cents
each; all money received will he
turned over to the Con Home.
Candidates for degrees at the
end of spring quarter may call
for their grades at the Informa
tion window after 1:30 p.m., June
13.
