# Spaeth to Speak On Latin America
## 
### 
Carl B. Spaeth, dean of the School
of Law, will speak on 'Argentina
and Her Neighbors" at the final lec
ture of the quarter in the Hispanic-
Series Wednesday night at 7:30 in
the Cubberley Auditorium.
During the war Dean Spaeth
served as assistant co-ordinator of
Inter-American Affairs and for two
years was the representative in
Montevideo, Uruguay.
When appointed professor of law
and dean of the Law School last
fall, Dean Spaeth was serving in the
State Department's Office of Infor
mation and Cultural Affairs.
