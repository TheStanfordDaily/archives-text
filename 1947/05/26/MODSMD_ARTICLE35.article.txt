# KSU to Sell Records Of Spring Sing Tunes
## 
### 
KSU recorded all songs at the
Spring Sing and plans to sell rec
ords with the desired living group's
song on one side and a choice of
the two winning songs on the
other side. Art Levinson, pub
licity manager announced. Rec
ords will sell for $1.25.
Students should place their or
ders by either phoning 2-15971 or
going to the studios in the Memor
