# Riders Nosed Out In Thursday Meet
## 
### 
The Stanford riflemen, engaging
for the first time in competition
since 1943, were nosed out by a
mere nine points, 895 to 880. last
Thursday night by the San Mateo
J.C. squad.
The five-man Indian squad per
formed well over expectations as
they gave the powerful Bulldogs a
run for their money.
Larkin Jackson, Stanford, was
high man for the meet. The other
Farm men competing were Brodi
Spencer. Bill Huggins, Bob Har
per. and Bill Wake.
