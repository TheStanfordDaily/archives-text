# National Guard Desires Recruits
## 
### 
The Palo Alto National Guard
unit is still seeking recruits for its
organization, according to E. A. Mof
fat, battery commander. The unit
still has openings for men with ex
perience, particularly in the admin
istrative and executive lines.
The National Guard offices are in
room 155 at the Palo Alto High
School.
The offices are open from 7:30 to
9:30 p.m.
Members of the National Guard
will receive pay and allowances over
and above money paid out for the
GI Bill.
