# Cardinals to Plan Fall Activities
## 
### 
Cardinals will meet tonight at 7:30
in the Excom room to discuss plans
for fall quarter and to terminate
this year's business, according to
Ann Lilienthal, president.
Miss Lilienthal said that the or
ganization was planning an informal
dance to be given the first Saturday
of Fail quarter. The affair is to be
a semi-jolly-up and will feature
ping-pong, etc., as well as dancing.
The members will also discuss
tentative arrangements for the Home
coming weekend at tonight's meeting.
