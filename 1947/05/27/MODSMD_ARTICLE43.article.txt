# Sales Managers To Hold 'Clinic'
## 
### 
Sales managers from San Fran
cisco will put on a sales clinic for
the Graduate School of Business to
morrow at 6 p.m. at a dinner meet
ing in the Union.
The purpose of the meeting is to
present "Various Opportunities in
the Selling Field," and Professor
David Faville will be the chairman.
Mr. Frank Bodine, from Westing
house, will speak on sales engineer
ing; Mr. Irving Granicher of Cal
Pack will speak on food sales; and
Mr. Ray Rhodes of Paul H. Raymer
Company will discuss radio net
work brokerage.
