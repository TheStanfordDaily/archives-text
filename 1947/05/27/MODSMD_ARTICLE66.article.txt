# CIR to Nominate Officers for Fall
## 
### 
Nominating Committee for the
Conference on International Rela
tions will meet today at 2 p.m. in
the Excom room to nominate officers
for the Conference next year.
At the last meeting of the quarter
Saturday at 10 a.m. in the "Y" room
of the Women's Clubhouse, the en
tire Planning Committee will vote
on the officers selected by the Nom
inating Committee. All members
should attend so that there will be
a quorum.
