# Alpine Club Elects Officers Tonight
## 
### 
The Alpine Club will elect new
officers tonight at 7:30 in room 121
of the Graduate School of Business.
The forthcoming trip to Yosemite
will be discussed and colored slides
of previous Yosemite climbs will be
shown.
"All members arc urged to attend,"
said Larry Taylor, president of the
club. Those interested in partici
pating in the summer activities arc
also invited."
