# Roble Plans Lawn 'Luau' for June 4
## 
### 
Roble will have its farewell din
ner, or luau, June 4, at 6 p.m. on
the lawn in front of Roble, accord
ing to Pat Auman, chairman.
During dinner entertainment will
be provided by Mona Carter, fresh
man girl, who will do a hula dance,
and by the Roble hashers. After
dinner each corridor will give a skit
or a song.
Special guests for the luau will
be Miss Pat Davidson, Mi.ss Elva
Brown. Miss Anastasia Doyle, Miss
Mary Yost, Miss Christine Ricker,
and the former Roble sponsors.
