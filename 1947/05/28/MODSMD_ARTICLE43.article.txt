# Officers Elected By Brandeis Club
## 
### 
Cal McGillis was elected presi
dent of the Stanford Brandeis Club.
The meeting Monday night featured
an address given by Rabbi Gittin.
Other officers of the group are
Gloria Fishkin, secretary; Chuck
Morris, treasurer; Bonnie Munter,
cultural chairman; Arlene Gold
stone, social chairman; and Lynne
Powell and Peter Koestenbaum, ex
ecutives-at-large.
In redefining the purpose of the
club, McGillis said, "The club is to
provide an opportunity for Stan
ford students of Jewish faith to be
come acquainted with one another
and to participate in Stanford re
ligious activities."
