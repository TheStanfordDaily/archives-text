# Encina, Toyon Bikes Need Storage Tags
## 
### 
All bicycles in and about Toyon
and Encina must be tagged for
storage on or before Monday,
Chief of Police Gordon Davis said
yesterday.
Storage tags may be obtained at
the Encina Office.
All non-tagged bicycles will be
considered abandoned and will be
removed from the area for dis
posal, Davis said.
