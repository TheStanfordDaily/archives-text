# Institute Council To Elect Officers
## 
### 
The Executive Council of the In
stitute of International Relations
will hold its last, meeting of the
quarter today at 4:45 p.m. in the
Excom room. The meeting is open
to all interested persons, the Coun
cil has announced.
Election of officers to fill the posi
tions of president, vice - president,
secretary, and treasurer will he
conducted by members of Ihe Coun
cil. Interviews of the candidates for
these offices were completed yester
day afternoon.
The Council will also discuss
methods of electing international
committee heads.
