# Social Dance Class Planned for Fall
## 
### 
Social Dancing will be offered for
the first time fall quarter on Mon
day, Tuesday, and Thursday at 5
p.m., Mrs. Miriam Lidstcr, women's
physical education instructor, an
nounced. The class is open to both
men and women.
Tango, rumba, and samba, as well
as the waltz and fox trot will be
taught.
Mrs. Lidstcr also corrected the
time Dance Composition is being of
fered. The new time for the lecture
is Wednesday at 4 p.m. instead of
Wednesday at 3 p.m., and the lab
oratory period is either Friday from
1 to 3 p.m. or 3 to 5 p.m.
This class is open to both men
and women who are interested in
dance.
