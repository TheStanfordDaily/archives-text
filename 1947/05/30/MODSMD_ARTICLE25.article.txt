# CARILLON PROGRAM PLANNED
## 
### 
Stanford's Art Gallery and the
Hoover Library will be open on
June M and 15 for graduates and
their families.
In addition, the carillon will give
recitals during the senior class
pilgrimage, preceding the Bacca
laureate Service, and preceding
the Commencement exercises.
The Hoover Library will be
open to visitors on Saturday from
10 a.m. to 5 p.m., and on Sunday
from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
