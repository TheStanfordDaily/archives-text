# Lost and Found Items Held in ASSU Office
## 
### 
"The year's almost over and still
no one has claimed all these lost
articles," said Miss Imogene Aten
in the ASSU office as she held out
a visored cap full of odds and ends
that have been turned in after
dances and other campus functions.
The collection includes a pair of
plastic-rimmed glasses, a wallet, a
I ring of pearls, earrings, a pipe,
lipstick, lapel pins, a black evening
bag, and costume jewelry.
