# Phi Betes Elect Bacon President
## 
### 
Harold Bacon was elected presi
dent of the Stanford chapter of Phi
Beta Kappa Monday night, succeed
ing Charles N. Reynolds.
Other new officers include Rich
ard Foster Jones and Robert S. Tur
ner, vice - presidents; Thomas S.
Barclay, re-elected secretary; Sam
uel Thurman, Jr., treasurer; and ex
ecutive board: E. A. White, William
G. Hoover, Sylvia Barry Hoyt, and
Ferris Boothe.
