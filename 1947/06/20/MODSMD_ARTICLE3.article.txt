# Record Set For Reg Day Enrollment
## 
### 
Summer quarter registration fig
ures hit a new first-day high of
2,655 yesterday, an increase of 59
over the first figures for summer
quarter 1946, the University Infor
mation Office announced.
Registering yesterday were 2,102
men and 553 women, for a provi
sional ratio of 3.8. Figures for the
first day of summer quarter 1946
were 1,992 men and 604 women.
The number of veterans enrolled
is not yet available, the Information
Office said.
Additional enrollments today and
next week may bring the total reg
istration figures up to approximately
3,000, close to last summer's record
breaking sign-up.
