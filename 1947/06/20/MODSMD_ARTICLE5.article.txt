# Village Library Hours Announced
## 
### 
The Stanford Village library will
remain open from 6 to 10 in the
evening Monday through Friday
during the early weeks of summer
session, D. S. Bushnell, Village li
brarian, announced this week.
All those who plan lo use the
library during the summer are re
quested to leave a list of the books
they would like to have available
with Bushnell during the first two
weeks of the quarter.
The number of students who use
the Village library during the early
weeks will determine the number of
hours that it will remain open for
the rest of the quarter.
The library is located adjacent to
the Village chapel.
