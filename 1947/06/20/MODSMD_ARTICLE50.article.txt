# '49-ers Keep Albert
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. June 1!) (UP).
-Frankie Albert, star quarterback
on Coach Buck Shaw's T-formation
San Francisco Forty-niners, today
said he will return to the club for
summer practice and will be in the
backtield when the team opens All-
America Conference play.
Joe Davis, former USC star, also
signed his 1947 contract today.
