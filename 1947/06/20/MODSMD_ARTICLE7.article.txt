# Engineering School Gets Memorial Fund
## 
### 
A gift of $2600 started the Walter
Elliot Memorial Fund, the income of
which is to benefit the School of
Engineering. The fund was set up by
friends of the late Walter Elliot,
Class of 1911.
Elliot was general drilling super
intendent of Shell Oil Company at
the time of his death, February 7.
He was an employee of Shell Oil for
30 years.
