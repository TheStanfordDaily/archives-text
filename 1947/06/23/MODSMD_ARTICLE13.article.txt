# Maritime Unions in Agreement On 5 Per Cent Wage Increase
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, June 22. Two CIO maritime unions whose partici
pation in the brief nationwide shipping dispute threatened to tie up all
West Coast operations today worked under new contracts awarding their
a live per cent wage increase.
The American Communications Association and the Cooks' and Stew-

ards' Union signed contracts with
the Pacific American Shipowners'
Association which granted them
raises identical to the five per cent
boosts which ended the East Coast
phase of the dispute.

ports that there are 31,170.000 tele
phones to 140,000,000 persons in the
United States.
