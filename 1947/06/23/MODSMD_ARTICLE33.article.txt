# Chinese Theater Group to Perform
## 
### 
The Chinese Cultural Theater
Group of Shanghai will present a
program of ancient Chinese music,
dance, and drama at the Community
Theater in Palo Alto tonight.
The program, "An Evening in
Cathay," is part of the United Serv
ices to China drive and proceeds
will go to this group, accoxcling to
Dr. Blake Wilbur, local chairman of
the drive. Tickets are on sale at
the Peninsula Box Office, 338 Uni
versity Avenue.
