# Theater Group Posts Ibsen Play Auditions
## 
### 
Tryouts for Henrik Ibsen's "A
Doll's House" will be held Wednes
day and Thursday at 7:30 p.m. in
the Little Theatre, according to Mel
Ellett, graduate student in dramat
ics.
The play will be directed by El
lett, and will be presented in the
Studio Theater Aug. 13, 14, 15,
and 16.
