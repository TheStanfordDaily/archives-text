# Folk Dancing To Commence Shortly
## 
### 
Two series of folk dancing classes
for adults will begin the first week
in July at the Community Center,
1305 Middlefield Road.
Tuesday evening classes will be
for beginners; Wednesday evening
classes will be for advanced stu
dents. Polkas, schottisches, waltzes,
American square dances, and dances
from many European countries will
be taught free of charge by the Rec
reation Department. All are wel
come, with or without partners.
