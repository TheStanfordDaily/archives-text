# Annapolis to Admit Naval Reservists
## 
### 
Legislation ponding in Congress
will authorize the annual appoint
ment of 160 Naval Reserve enlisted
personnel to the Naval Academy at
Annapolis.
The reservists, to participate in
the 1948 competitive examinations,
must have enlisted in the Naval Re
serve not later than July 1, 1947.
Also, they must have attended at
least 27 drill periods between July 1,
1947, and April 12, 1948. The Secre
tary of the Navy will make the
appointments from the results of the
examinations.
