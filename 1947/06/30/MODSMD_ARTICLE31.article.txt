# Encina Open House Hailed as Success
## 
### 
"Success of Encina's summer open
house for Lagunita, last Friday, was
clue to the cooperation of the ten
man house committee," said Jerry
Lokey, dance chairman.
Members of the committee were:
Mike Halpern, coordinator; Earl
Girby, decorations; Dick Cornew,
program; John Bernard, refresh
ments; Jim Eklund, furniture; Jack

Kamena, sound system; Dick Cath
rinett, master of ceremonies; Ralph
Field, Dave Rosenthal, and Bob
Price, publicity.
