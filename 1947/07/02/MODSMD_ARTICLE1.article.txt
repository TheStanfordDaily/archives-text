# Registration For Summer Reaches 3,110
## 
### 
Three thousand, one hundred and
ten students, the lax-gest number
ever to register for summer quar
ter, had turned in registration books
by Monday, the Registrar's office
announced yesterday. This figure
does not include 23 men signed up
for the School of Naval Administra
tion.
The previous summer quarter
high was 3,084, set last year.
This year's total registration in
cludes 2,440 men and 670 women.
Two thousand, eighty-one veterans
are attending classes. Of these 1,981
are men and 100 women.
Unofficial calculations place the
ratio at 3.7.
