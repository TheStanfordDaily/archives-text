# Examinations Ready For Revenue Agents
## 
### 
Examinations are ready for the
positions of Internal Revenue Agent
and Special Agent (tax fraud) in
California. Nevada, and Arizona, the
United States Civil Service Com
mission announced yesterday.
Salaries range from $2,644 to
$4,902 a year.
Applications are available at post
offices and United States Civil Serv
ice offices, and they must be re
ceived not later than July 16. 1947.
The commission urges all qualified
persons to apply at once for the ex
amination.
