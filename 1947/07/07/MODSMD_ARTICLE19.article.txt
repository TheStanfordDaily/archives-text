# Daily Editorials
## Saucers Are Flying 
### 
If these flying saucer stories are true, we can only call to
mind the opening chapters of Wells' "War of the Worlds."
If these discs do not exist, then the American mind is on the
verge of embarking on the greatest wave of mass hysteria to
hit the country since the Gold Rush. There is one grain of com
fort, however. Not one disc has been seen on or near the Stan
ford campus. Either Stanford is to be spared from the direct
Martian assault, or the Farm's population surpasses the entire
United States in level-headedness and self-control.
