# Six Farm Tankers Rated All-America
## 
### 
Paced by Bob Anderson, Stanford
yesterday snared six positions on
the 1947 All - American swimming
team selected by swimming coaches
of America. Michigan led the selec
tions with eight spots.
Besides Anderson, team positions
went to .Alan Weeden, Fred Taoli,
Em Cashin, John Neville, and Ray
Rule.
Honorable mention was given to
Stanford's Jack Weeden and Don
Hester, 150 - yard backstroke and
200-yard breaststroke, respectively.
Yale's Eli's and the Michigan State
Spartans were given seven of the
top 19 positions.
