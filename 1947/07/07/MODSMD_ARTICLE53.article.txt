# Science Teachers In Institute Today
## 
### 
An Institute for Elementary Sci
ence Teachers, to be held by sci
ence teachers from numerous Cali
fornia schools, will be held at Stan
ford starting today under the aus
pices of the School of Education. The
institute will end Saturday.
Dr. Glenn O. Blough, United
States Office of Education science
specialist, will direct the confer
ence. He will be assisted by B.
Frank Gillette, assistant in educa
tion on the School of Education fac
ulty.
The conference program will in
clude consideration of audio-visual
aids, classroom techniques, and the
opportunity to examine and use
experimental apparatus commonly
used in science courses in elemen
tary schools.
