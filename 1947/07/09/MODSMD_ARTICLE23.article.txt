# 'I' Club Will Hear Steinmetz Speak
## 
### 
Dr. H. C. Steinmetz, head of the
psychology department at San Diego
State College, will be guest speaker
at the second meeting of the Inter
national Club Sunday at the Vil
lage Clubhouse, Jean Richmond,
group social chairman, stated yes
terday. The meeting starts at 7:30
p.m.
Dr. Steinmetz will speak on "A
Democratic Far Eastern Policy."
Author and lecturer, and friend of
the late Marine General Evan Carl
son, Dr. Steinmetz spent eight years
in the Orient.
Transportation will be furnished
by students with cars in front of the
Bookstore until 7:20 p.m., Miss
Richmond said.
