# Strickland Announces One-Act Play Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for six one-act plays,
scheduled for production in the
Little Theater, will he held today
and tomorrow from 4 to 6 p.m. in
room 205, Memorial Hall, F.
Cowles Strickland, associate pro
fessor of speech and drama, an
nounced yesterday.
Since casting will not he limited
to students of the Drama Depart
ment, anyone interested may try
out. Strickland explained.
