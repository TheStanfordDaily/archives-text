# Hall Announces Text For Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
"With what measure ye mete, it
shall be measured unto you" is
the text from which his sermon
for Sunday, July 13, will be taken,
George J. Hall, University chap
lain. announced. Church services
are held at 11 a.m. in the Memo
rial Church.
Virginia Palmer and Mark Hat
field will read selections from the
Old and New Testaments, Dr. Ilall
stated.
