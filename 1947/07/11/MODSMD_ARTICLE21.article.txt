# War Libe Shows Material on Dam
## 
### 
Documents and literary material
depicting the building of the Hoo
ver Dam are currently on display
in the Hoover War Library, Philip
T. McLean, reference librarian, dis
closed yesterday.
The exhibition, situated in the
main foyer, includes a miniature
model of the dam presented to the
library by the Department of the
Interior, and a copy of the federal
order which officially changed the
name from Boulder Dam to Hoover
Dam.
Materials, models, and photo
graphs will be on display through
out July.
