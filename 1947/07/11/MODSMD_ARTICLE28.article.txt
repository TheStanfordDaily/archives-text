# Steinmetz Speaks At Village Sunday
## 
### 
'"A Democratic Far Eastern Pol
icy" is the topic of a lecture to be
delivered by Dr. H. C. Steinmetz,
head of the psychology department
at San Diego State College, at the
second meeting of the International
Club, Jean Richmond, group social
chairman, has announced.
The meeting will be held Sunday
at 7:30 p.m. at the Stanford Village
Clubhouse.
Students with cars will meet in
front of the Bookstore until 7:20,
furnishing transportation to the Vil
lage, Miss Richmond said.
