# COLLEGE CONGRESS
## 
### 
Call for interested students to
serve on the planning committee for
the Western College Congress at
Stanford next fall was made yester
day by Lloyd Hall, co-chairman.
The first meeting of the committee
will be held tomorrow morning at
10 in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
