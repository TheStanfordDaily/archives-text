# Encina Men Plan Summer Policies
## 
### 
Encina Excom yesterday after
noon approved an amendment to the
hall constitution calling for revised
terms of office, Head R.A. Hal Pain
ter said.
Encina officers will be elected, in
augrated, and will serve for the one
quarter, Painter explained. The
president of Encina will be elected
next week.
Wing presidents on Encina Excom
elected are: Dave Rosenthal, John
Bernard, Marshall Small, Vic Lyons,
F. E. Kimball, Ralph Field, Alan
Bletz, and Willard Gilson.
