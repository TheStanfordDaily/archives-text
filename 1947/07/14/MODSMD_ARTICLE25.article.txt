# ISC Representatives Cable from England
## 
### 
Bob Hoyt, chairman of ISC, re
ported last night that according to a
cablegram received from London,
Lawrence "Shorty" Grannis and
Bill Ferrar, Stanford's representa
tives in Europe, have arrived.
Grannis said that he would be in
England till July 22, but that Ferrar
had already left for Prague. It is
hoped to have the first of the com
plete reports in the near future,
Hoyt said.
