# Pool Names New Daily Appointees
## 
### 
Sixteen appointments to the Daily
business staff were announced yes
terday by Business Manager John
Pool.
John McPherrin was promoted to
advertising manager, and Gene
Sherwood was named circulation
manager.
Acting as assistants to McPherrin
will be Coral Coss, Lorene Living
stone, Jack McCurdy, Roberta Mar
tin, Patricia O'Farrell, Lee Sneath,
Merritt Spidell, Jean Thayer, and
Jeanne Thomas.
Members of the office staff will
include Elbe Bricca, Dick Lower,
Nancy Truitt, and John Weidler.
Joan Hollister will serve as assist
ant circulation manager.
