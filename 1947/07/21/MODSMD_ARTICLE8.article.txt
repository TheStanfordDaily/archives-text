# 'Park Lights' Spot Lagunita Formal
## 
### 
The Lagunita summer formal will
be held Friday night from 9 till 1.
according to Jane Vinson, chairman.
The theme for the formal will be
"Park Lights." Bids will be on sale
in the lobby for Lagunita girls and
their guests. Music will be provided
by Carlton Aekley and his orches
tra.
Girls in charge of the different
committees are as follows:
Bids, Mary Lou Cecil; publicity,
Barbara Bronson; decorations. Pat
Loly; refreshments, Cathy Potter;
clean-up, Jeanne Delp; patrons, Di
ane Roth; and arrangements, Lor
raine Pielhop.
