# Tenor to Join Organist In Recital Tomorrow
## 
### 
W. Madison Taylor, tenor, will
assist Warren D. Allen, University
organist, in his recital in the Memo
rial Church at 4:15 tomorrow, Dr,
Allen announced yesterday. Mr.
I Taylor will sing an aria and recita
tive from "Judas Maccabaeus" by
Handel.
Selections by Dr. Allen will be:
"Fugue in E Flat (St. Ann)," Bach;
"Larghetto in A Flat," Joseph Jon
gen; "Vision," Rheinberger; and
"Toccata in G Major," Th. Dubois.
