# JACKSONS VISIT HAWAII
## 
### 
Dean and Mrs. J. Hugh Jackson
left by Clipper Saturday for Hono
lulu where Jackson will present
charters to the first three Kiwanis
chapters to be organized outside
the North American continent, the
Graduate School of Business an
nounced yesterday.
Dean Jackson is one of the inter
national officers of Kiwanis. The
Jacksons will spend 10 days in the
Islands.
