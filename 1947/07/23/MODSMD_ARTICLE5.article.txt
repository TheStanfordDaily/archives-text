# Art Gallery Exhibits Hill Century Paintings
## 
### 
Eighteen paintings from the Stan
ford collection of oils are now on
display at the Art Gallery, Mrs.
Helen Green Cross, gallery director,
announced yesterday.
Still lifes and landscapes, dating
largely from the 18th century, are
in the display.
Gallery hours are from Tuesday
through Saturday. 10 a.m. to 5 p.nl.,
and Sunday, 1 to 5 p.m.
