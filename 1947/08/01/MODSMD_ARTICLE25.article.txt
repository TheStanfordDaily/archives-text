# Ann Curtis to Swim at Menlo Saturday
## 
### 
The national championship Crystal Plunge swimming aggregation
headed by American champion Ann Curtis will participate in an aquatic
spectacle Saturday at the Menlo Circus Club. Starting time for the show,
which is for the benefit of the Woodside-Atherton Junior Auxiliary of
the Stanford Convalescent Home, is set at 3:45 p.m.
Miss Curtis will swim a scratch 200-meter freestyle race against seven
teammates, all of whom will swim in Chicago on Friday, Aug. 22.
