# Farm Holds Congress Of Western Colleges
## 
### 
Planning Committee for next au
tumn's College Congress will meet
again tomorrow morning at 10 in
the basement of the Women's Club
house, Lloyd Hall, committee co
chairman, announced yesterday.
Representatives from approxi
mately 40 of the major universities
in the Western states will comprise
the Congress when it meets here
Nov. 24, 25, and 26.
Working sessions and programs of
the Congress will deal with the
problems of international organiza
tion for world peace.
