# Medals Are Available For Ex-Navy Personnel
## 
### 
Honorably discharged Navy vet
erans may apply at any Navy re
cruiting office for their Victory and
American Defense Service medals,
according to the Twelfth Naval Dis
trict headquarters.
No medal distribution has been
announced by the Army to date.
The Victory medals are for active
duty service between Dec. 7, 1941,
and Dec. 31. 1946. American De
fense Service medals go to persons
on active duty between Sept. 8. 1939,
and Dec. 7, 1941, the Navy bulletin
announced.
