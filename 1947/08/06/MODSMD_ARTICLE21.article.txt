The regular Thursday organ re
cital will be presented by Warren D.
Allen, University organist, at 4:15
p.m. in the Memorial Church.
Dr. Allen's program will include
a Bach prelude, adaptations on
Welsh hymn - tunes, two Brahms
chorale - preludes, a Buxtehude
fugue, and a pi-elude to a Bach
chorale by Respighi.
