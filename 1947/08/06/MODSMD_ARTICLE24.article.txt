# Schroeder Advances In Net Tournament
## 
### 
Ted Schroeder, ex-Farm netster,
moved into the third round of the
Eastern grass courts championship
yesterday when he defeated Young
Leonard Steiner of Brooklyn, 6-4
and G-4. Schroeder has been seeded
number one in the tournament.
Frank X. Shields of New York
pulled the upset of the day when he
defeated Sam Match of Los Angeles,
6-3 and 6-1.
