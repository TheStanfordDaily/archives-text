# Water Safety Classes Slated by Red Cross
## 
### 
A water safety and lifesaving in
structor's course will be given soon
by the Palo Alto chapter of the
American Red Cross, according to
an announcement by Mrs. Margaret
Barr, of the Physical Education De
partment.
Mrs. Barr, who is chairman of the
Red Cross commission on water
safety for Palo Alto, says anyone 19
years old or over who has a current
senior lifesaving certificate is eli
gible for the course. Those inter
ested may call the Palo Alto chap
ter of the Red Cross at 2214. T. Hours
and days of the classes will be ar
ranged according to the convenience
of those enrolling.
