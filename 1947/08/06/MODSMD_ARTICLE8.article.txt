# Ferris Returns to Farm After Entomology Trip
## 
### 
Gordon Ferris, professor of biol
ogy and curator of entomological
collections, has returned to the
campus after a 4,500-mile tour of
the Rocky Mountains and the
Southwest. He brought new speci
mens for both the entomological and
botanical collections.
Professor Ferris, accompanied by
his wife, assistant curator of the
Dudley Herbarium, visited the Di
nosaur National Monument in Utah,
the Telluride area of Colorado, San
ta Fe, the Chaco Canyon National
Monument, and Mesa Verde Na
tional Park.
