# Grass Fire Is Quickly Put Out by Fire Dept.
## 
### 
Yesterday's fire at 5:25 p.m. was
out in a matter of minutes, an
nounced Fire Chief John Marston.
Occurring in the grass along
Foothill Road between Portola
Road and the golf course, the
blaze was quickly stopped and the
fire department was "back in
service" at 5:50 p.m., the fire chief
said.
