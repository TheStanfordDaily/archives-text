# Beisser, Knox In Net Wins
## 
### 
SPOKANE, Wash., Aug. 7 (UP).—
Defending champion Clint Knox,
Portland, Ore., and three Californians
| today won their way into semi-finals
! of the men's singles in the annual
| Inland Empire tennis tourney at
S Comstock Park.
Knox fought off Bob Lewis, Salt
Lake City, 6-4, 2-6, 6-4 to survive
quarter-final play. Jim Livingstone,
San Francisco, eliminated Les Pat
ten, Spokane, 10-8, 6-8, while Ar
nold Beisser, also of San Francisco,
defeated Joe Brasch of Spokane,
6-3, 6-4. Merwin Miller, Santa
Monica, took Clyde Knox, Portland,
6-3, 7-9, 12-10.
In men's doubles play Charles
Hickox, San Francisco, and John
Priest, Tacoma, Wash., upset Bob
and Fred Lewis, Salt Lake City, 6-3,
1-6, 6-4, to qualify for semi-final
! play.
I Bodil Borgesen. of Oslo. Norway,
turned back Lucille Winner, Coulee
Dam, Wash., 6-1, 6-3, in the women's
singles.
In mixed doubles, June Crowe,
' Santa Monica, and Livingstone de
i fcated Miss Borgensen and Brasch,
6-2, 8-6, and Arvilla McGuire,
Berkeley, Cal., and Miller took Betty
Mulloy, Spokane and Patten, 6-2, 6-0.
