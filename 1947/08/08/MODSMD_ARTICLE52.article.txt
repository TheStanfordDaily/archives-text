# McNeill Upset By Falkenburg
## 
### 
SOUTH ORANGE, N. J., Aug 7
(UP). —Playing his best tennis since
he returned from Europe a month
ago. Bob Falkenburg, the cannon !
ball kid from Beverly Hills, Cal., I
today upset fourth seeded Don Mc-
Neill of Verona. N. J., 6-3, 4-6, 6-4, j
in the 21st annual eastern grass court
championships.
The victory over the defending
champion, places Falkenburg in the
semi-final round opposite Gardnar
Mulloy of Coral Gables, Fla.
Top seeded Ted Schroeder of
Glendale, Calif., although he was
extended to the limit before jump
ing to a 12-10, 6-3, victory over Earl
Cochell of Forest Hills, L. I.
Margaret Osborne of San Fran
cisco beat Dorothy Head of Alameda, I
6-1, 6-1, and Louise Brough of Bev
erly Hills tripped Barbara Krase of
San Francisco, 6-1, 7-5.
Beverly Baker of Santa Monica
defeated Louise Snow of Bakers
lield, Cal., 6-4. 7-5, and Shirley Fry
of Akron. 0., topped Gertrude Moran
of Santa Monica. 6-4, 7-5.
