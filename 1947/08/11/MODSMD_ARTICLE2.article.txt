# Feller to Discuss UN At Thursday Meeting
## 
### 
Abraham H. Feller, general coun
sel of the United Nations, will speak
on "The United Nations Today,"
Thursday evening at 8 in Cubberley
Auditorium, according to the Com
mittee on Public Exercises.
Under the chairmanship of Dr.
Carl B. Spaeth, dean of the Stan
ford Law School and vice president
of the World Affairs Council, the
meeting will also be addressed by
Eugene Staley, Council educational
director. A question and answer
period will follow the lectures.
