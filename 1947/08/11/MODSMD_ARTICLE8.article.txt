# Architectural Courses Offered by Paly Club
## 
### 
The Architectural Club of Palo
Alto is offering a course in per
spective drawing beginning tonight
at 7 in the basement of the Palo
Alto Public Library, W. Leslie
Heald, president, announced yester
day.
The purpose of the club is to as
sist all those interested in prepar
ing for the State Board examinations
in architecture. Classes will be held
Monday and Wednesday nights from
7 to 9 throughout the remainder of
the year. Fee for this quarter is
$12.50.
