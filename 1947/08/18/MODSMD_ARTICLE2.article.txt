# Stanford Student Injured on Coast
## 
### 
John Hood, 20, Stanford student,
Alpha Sigma Phi, was carried un
conscious to the San Mateo Com
munity hospital with a fractured
skull last night after Coast Guards
men had spent an hour rescuing
him from his hazardous position on
the face of Devil's Slide, San Mateo
County oceanside cliff.
Coast Guardsmen finally were able
to haul Hood to safety by using an
emergency wire stretcher and haul
ing him up 300 feet to the top of
Devil's Slide.
