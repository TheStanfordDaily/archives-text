# SWEDISH PRINCE DIES
## 
### 
STOCKHOLM, Sweden, Aug. 17
(UP). — Prince Eugen, youngest
brother of King Gustav V and one
of Sweden's foremost painters, died
today at the age of 82.
The prince suffered a heart attack
on Aug. 2—one day after his 82nd
birthday—and was forced to return
from a seaside resort to receive
medical attention in Stockholm.
