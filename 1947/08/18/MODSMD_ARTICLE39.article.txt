# Summer Grades to Be Obtainable by Mail
## 
### 
Students who wish to receive
their grades by mail for summer
quarter should leave a stamped,
self-addressed envelope at the in
formation window in the Admin
istration Building before the end
of the quarter, the Registrar's of
fice announced yesterday.
For the convenience of the stu
dents, envelopes will he sold at the
information window for 5 cents
each, Mr t s. Maralyn A. Porter, as
sistant in the Registrar's office,
said. The money received for the
envelopes will he given to the
Stanford Convalescent Home for
Children.
