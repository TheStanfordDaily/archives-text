# Memorial Church Scene Of Bach Music Recital
## 
### 
University Organist Warren D.
Allen, assisted by Marjorie B. Dick
inson, soprano, will present a pro
gram of Bach organ music at the
final recital tomorrow afternoon at
4:15 in the Memorial Church.
Scheduled numbers include Toc
cata in C and Aria in A Minor, Air
from the Suite in D Major, Prelude
and Fugue in E Minor, Recitative
and Aria from the St. Matthew Pas
sion, and "My Heart Ever Faithful"
from the Pentecost Cantata.
Allen will resume his weekly re
citals Oct. 2 at 4:15 p.m.
