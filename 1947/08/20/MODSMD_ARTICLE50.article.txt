# Miller Leaves As Housing Director
## 
### 
Food and Housing Director Wil
liam H. Miller assumes his new po
sition as dining halls director at
the Virginia Polytechnic Institute
in Clarksburg, Virginia, today, the
Stanford Business Office announced
yesterday.
Miller completed his work here
at the Farm yesterday after being
food and housing director since
April, 1946.
The Business Office said that a
replacement for Miller had not been
announced by the University.
