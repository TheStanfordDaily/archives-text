# Dean Bartky to Speak To Annapolis Faculty
## 
### 
Dr. A. John Bartky, dean of the
School of Education, will leave
campus tomorrow for Annapolis,
Md., where he will lecture to the
Academy faculty, the Education
Department announced last night.
Dr. Bartky will lecture on meth
ods of teaching and the general
offerings of college curricula. He
will return to Stanford Sept. 15.
