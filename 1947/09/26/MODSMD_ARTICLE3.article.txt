# Du Charme to Take '48 Quad Pictures
## 
### 
The Stanford Quad has signed a
contract with the du Charmc Studio
of San Francisco, noted photogra
pher of portraits and particularly of
wedding pictures, to take the indi
vidual pictures for the 1948 Stanford
Quad. "We are very much pleased
to have such an outstanding studio
take our individual pictures," staled
Eiill McPherson, editor of the 1943
Quad, "and feel certain that this
will be one of the major actors in
making the '48 Quad one of the
best Quads ever published.*'
