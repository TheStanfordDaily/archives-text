# OPPORTUNITY KNOCKS
## 
### 
Here's a rare opportunity to learn the advertising business from the
ground floor up! There are many positions open on the Daily advertising
staff, some of them paying propositions. No experience necssary, but
willingness to learn and work, and a pleasant personality are essential.
We in ed reliable people—people we can count on to spend about two
afternoons a week at the Shack and get their copy in on time. Freshmen
are especially wanted.
