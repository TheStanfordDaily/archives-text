# New Libe Schedule Begins Tomorrow
## 
### 
Regular library Tiours will begin
tomorrow, F. J. Priddle, chief of
the library circulation department,
said yesterday. The library will be
open from 8 a.m. to 10 p.m. Mon
day through Friday. On Saturday
the library will close at 4 p.m. Sun
day hours are 2 p.m. to 6 p.m.
The library in room 220 at Stan
ford Village will be open from 4
p.m. to 10 p.m., Monday through
Friday. It will be closed Saturday,
but will be open from 2 to 6 p.m.
Sunday.
Mr. Priddle also said new lights
have been installed in the Reserve
Book Room.
