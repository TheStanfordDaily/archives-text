# Farm Vets Eligible For Financial Aid
## 
### 
Stanford veterans who need fi
nancial aid may apply for grants
from the Dimes for Diplomas fund,
Mrs. Olivia Byler, scholarship sec
retary, announced yesterday.
To be eligible for the grants, a
veteran must have registered at
Stanford originally on or after Sept.
1, 1940, Mrs. Byler said. Grants
will be awarded on the basis of fi
nancial need and satisfactory schol
arship.
The ASSU established the Serv
icemen's Memorial Scholarship from
the Dimes for Diplomas fund as a
memorial to the Stanford war dead.
Blanks may be obtained in the
Scholarship Committee Office, Ad
ministration Building, every morn
ing except Saturday.
