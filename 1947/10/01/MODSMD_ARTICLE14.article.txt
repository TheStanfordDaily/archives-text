# Film Company Seeks Entrance into Russia
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Sept 3ft (IIP). —
A Hollywood film company today
said it had cabled Joseph Stalin
for permission to send Ift players ,
and a camera crew to Russia to
make scenes for the movie "As
signment: Russia."
