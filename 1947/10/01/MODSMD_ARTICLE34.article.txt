# Men's Glee Club Issues Vocal Call
## 
### 
The first meeting of the Stanford
Glee Club will be today at 4 p.m.
in the choir room at the rear of the
Memorial Church. All voices are
needed, and tenors are in great de
mand.
According to James H. Schwaba
cher, director of the Glee Club, the
group will sing everything from
medieval drinking songs to modern
spirituals and from hillbilly corn to
Broadway hits, with "lots of close
harmony in between."
John Leicester, publicity director,
announced yesterday that the Glee
Club will meet twice a week and
will sing for rallies, parties, alumni
meetings, and other organized func
tions. Several club parties are be
ing planned.
