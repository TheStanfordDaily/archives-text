# Five Opponents On Soccer Sked
## 
### 
With the first soccer game loom
ing—a contest with San Francisco
J.C., on Oct. 18—Coach Dave Tap
pan urged all former soccer players
and all newcomers interested in the
sport to turn out immediately.
"We also need a manager," the
student coach said. The man who
fills this position will earn a circle-
S, minor sport sweater.
Five games are scheduled, with a
possibility of more being added. A
practice game with San Mateo J.C.
has been tentatively set as a pre
season contest.
