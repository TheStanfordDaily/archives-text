# CHOLERA VACCINE ARRIVES
## 
### 
OAKLAND, Oct. 1 (UP). A sec
ond shipment of cholera vaccine to
combat a bubonic plague epidemic
in Egypt was rushed from the Navy
medical supply depot here today
aboard a naval air transport bound
for Cairo.
More than 900 pounds of vaccine
was aboard the transport, which
will fly first to Brooklyn, N.Y., he
fore making the trans - Atlantic
"mercy" hop. First vaccine ship
ments arrived in Cairo aboard
NATS planes yesterday.
