# SPARS Will Hold First National Reunion
## 
### 
The first national reunion of the
SPARs, women's reserve of the U.S.
Coast Guard, will be held in Wash
ington, D.C., on Nov. 22. The SPARs
were officially established on Nov.
2.'5, 1942, and numbered approxi
mately 12,000.
The fifth anniversary reunion will
officially start with a banquet at the
Statler Hotel at 7 p.m. on the 22nd.
Any SPAR interested in attending
should write the SPAR Reunion
Committee, 900 Massachusetts Ave
nue, N.E., Washington, D.C., for de
' tails.
