# Campus Assembly Set for Tuesday
## 
### 
The first all-campus assembly of
the school year will be held Tues
day morning, the Committee on
Public Exercises announced yester
day. All 11 o'clock classes will be
dismissed for the assembly, which
will be held in the Memorial Au
ditorium.
Dr. Donald B. Tresidder will be
the principal speaker on the pro
gram and will be introduced by
I Tom MartzlofT, ASSU president.
The Stanford Band will play "Come
Join the Band" and the assembly
will close with the Stanford hymn,
"Hail, Stanford, Hail!"
