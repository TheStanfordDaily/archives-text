# Five Scholarships Offered by China
## 
### 
The Chinese government this year
is again offering five cultural schol
arships of $1500 each to be given to
qualified Stanford students, who
must have had at least one year of
Chinese, according to Dr. Anthony
E. Sokol, of the School of Humani
ties.
Recipients of these scholarships,
open to both men and women un
dergraduates, will spend a major
part of their work in Chinese stud
ies during that year.
Applications for the cultural
scholarships should be submitted to
the Asiatic and Slavic Department
in the School of Humanities at the
earliest possible time. Dr. Sokol
would like to interview interested
students prior to their application.
