# Student Ticket Sale Commences Today
## 
### 
Student tickets for the Santa
Clara, UCLA, USC, and Cal games
go on sale this morning at 8:30 at
the Basketball Pavilion. Tickets will
be sold until 5 p.m. today, and again
at the same hours on Tuesday and
Wednesday. Student body cards are
needed.
No charge is being made for the
Santa Clara games, although for
students to attend those games, they
must secure the student tickets
within the next three days, for no
student tickets will be available
later.
