# All-Campus Assembly Tomorrow
## 
### 
All 11 o'clock classes will be dis
missed tomorrow for the first all -
campus assembly of the year.
Dr. Donald B. Tresidder will ad
dress the student body from Me
morial Auditorium.
The Stanford band opens the pro
gram with Stanford songs beginning
with ''Come Join the Band." The
band is led by J. E. Shucat, who will
also lead the singing. Dr. Tresid
der's talk follows the music.
The assembly will close with the
playing and singing of "Hail, Stan
ford, Hail."
