# Five Women Finish Study in Dietetics
## 
### 
Five women, including one from
Chile, were recently graduated from
the dietetics department of Stanford
Hospital in San Francisco.
The graduates include Helen Da
vidson, Ruth Sigler, Naomi Fenske,
Helen Rosenfeld, and Audrey John
son. The five women have com
pleted a year of dietetic study fol
lowing their graduation from col
lege.
