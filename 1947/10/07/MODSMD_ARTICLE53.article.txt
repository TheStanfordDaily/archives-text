# Students Total 8,189; Ratio Now Stands 3.12
## 
### 
Final reg figures, as announced
yesterday by Registrar 11. Donald
Winbigler, have boosted Stan
ford's all-time high in enrollment
to 8,189, an increase of 973 over
last year's total.
A total of 0,201 men and 1,988
women are included in this year's
enrollment, whereas 5,202 men
and 1,954 women were enrolled at
this time last year. The ratio now
stands at 3.12.
