# Tickets Sell For Concerts
## 
### 
Students planning to attend the
Stanford Concert Series should not
delay in purchasing their tickets,
since only a limited number of
tickets remain, announced Barbara
Aitken, Series manager.
Tickets selling for $(5.80, $5.10,
and $3.50 may be purchased Mon
day through Friday, from 1 to 5
p.m., at the Concert Series office in
the Nitery.
Featured in the Series this year
will be the world-famous pianist,
Artur Rubinstein. Concert - goers
will also hear Father Flanagan's
Boys' Town Choir; Jennie Tourel,
Metropolitan mezzo-soprano; Pierre
Monteux and the San Francisco
Symphony; and Patricia Travers,
violinist.
