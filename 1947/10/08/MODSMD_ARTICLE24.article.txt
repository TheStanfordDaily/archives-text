# Freshmen to Attend Tresidder Open House
## 
### 
All freshmen are invited to attend
a reception at the home of Dr. and
Mrs. Donald B. Tresidder Sunday
from 3:30 to 5:30 p.m., according to
Guyla Runyan, student body vice
president.
Freshmen will meet the Tresid
ders; Dr. and Mrs. Lawrence Kimp
ton; Miss Elva Brown, counselor for
women; Dr. Alvin C. Eurich; and
Shelton Beatty, counselor for men.
