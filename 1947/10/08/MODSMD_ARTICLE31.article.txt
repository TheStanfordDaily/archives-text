# Bowling Leagues Planned
## 
### 
A meeting of all persons inter
ested in forming bowling leagues
will be held tonight at the Village
bowling alleys at 7:30.
R. W, Fulkins, manager of the
alleys, says that all bowlers, men
and women, experienced and inex
perienced, will be invited to par
ticipate in the leagues. The leagues
will not be restricted to Village

residents but will be open to all
students.
Bowling at the Village proved to
be a popular intramural sport last
year with several leagues in opera
tion. The alleys are on the corridor
leading south from the Village mess
hall near the first apartment build
ings.
