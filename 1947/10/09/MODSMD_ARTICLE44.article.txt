# Polo Tryouts Set Saturday
## 
### 
Stanford's Polo Association will
hold its first meeting of the fall
season Saturday morning at 9 o'clock
in the Red Barn. Besides presenting
its plans for the year, the association
will again open its rolls to new
members who are able riders, have
athletic ability, and are enthusiastic
about playing the game.
Bob Lawson, SPA prexy, will
serve as an instructor aided by Bill
Decker and Todd Helser of last
year's team. The trio will outline
training plans for the quarter.
After the meeting the Indian polo
team will hold its first scrimmage
of the season in way of demonstra
tion. The team has several matches
already carded for the quarter.
