STANFORD STUDENTS: A SMALL INVESTMENT
FOR ONE OF THE
A BEST evenings you
EVER HAD
A small investment at the Redwood Lodge will bring you one of the very
nicest evenings you ever had.
Yes, here's a pleasant place with a collegiate atmosphere that can serve
a mouth-watering meal, furnish excellent dance music and entertainment—
all for a moderate price—and no cover charge.
The place to go any night of the week except Wednesday—the Redwood
Lodge is on El Camino, south of the campus—You can't miss it! and you
really shouldn't!
REDWOOD LODGE
