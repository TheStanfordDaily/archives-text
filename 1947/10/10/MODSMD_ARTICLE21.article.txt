# IIR Planning Group To Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
An important meeting of the In
stitute of International Relations
Planning Committee will be held to
morrow at 10 a.m. in the "Y" rooms
of the Women's Clubhouse, accord
ing to acting chairman Lloyd Hall.
The committee will be divided into
small work groups at this time to
arrange for a Western College Con
gress to be held at Stanford Nov. 24,
25, and 26.
