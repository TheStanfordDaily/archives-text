# Women Swimmers To Hold Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for new members of the
Women's Swimming Club will start
today at 4 at the Women's Gym, ac
cording to Margaret Vandenburg,
WAA publicity manager. Tryouts
will also be held tomorrow morning
at 10, next Friday at 4 p.m., and
next Saturday at 10 a.m.
Girls trying out for the Swimming
Club will be judged on form and
endurance.
The club will do speed swimming,
formation swimming, and some
water polo. Inter-school meets are
planned in speed and formation
swimming.
The club will meet Monday eve
nings at 8 to swim. Meetings will
follow.
The Swimming Club is a newly
organized part of WAA.
