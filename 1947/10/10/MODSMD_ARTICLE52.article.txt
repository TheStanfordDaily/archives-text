# Folk Dance Group To Be at Pavilion
## 
### 
Lloyd Shaw and his troupe of
Cheyenne Mountain Dancers will
present their only West Coast pro
gram of round and square dances
at the pavilion Friday, Oct. 24, at
8 p.m., it was announced last night.
On Saturday, Oct. 25, and the fol
lowing Sunday and Monday, Dr.
Shaw will conduct a workshop in
the pavilion on square dancing and
American round dances.
A pre-sale for Stanford students
will be held today at the Women's
Gym for tickets to both the pro
gram and teh workshop. Program
admission price is 5)0 cents for the
general public, HO cents for students.
No seats are reserved.
The combination price for tickets
for the program and workshop will
be $5.60 for the general public and
$5.25 for students. Admission to the
workshop alone will be $5.
As the workshop will be open to
all Bay Area dancers, tickets may
be secured by sending reservations
with checks, made payable to Stan
ford University, to the Department
of Physical Education for Women.
The limit on workshop attendance
will be 250.
Workshop hours will be from 1 to
4 p.m. and 7 to 9 p.m. Saturday, 11
a.m. to 1 p.m., 2:30 to 4:30, and 7 to 9
p.m Sunday, and 7:30 to 9 pm. Mon
day, Oct. 25-27.
