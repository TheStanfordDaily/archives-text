# Senior Signups, Roble Photos Due
## 
### 
Seniors have their last chance to
day from 9 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. to make
their picture appointments at the
Quad oflice, business manager
George Berland announced yester
day. Pictures cannot be taken with
out an appointment, he added. A
charge of one dollar will be made
for each picture.
Second-floor Roble residents are
asked to report to the Quad oflice
today for their Frosh Book and
Quad photos. Corridors 2A and 2
center should come from 8 to 12
a.m., corridor 2B from 1 to 3 p.m.,
and 2C from 3 to 5 p.m.
Women should wear white
blouses.
