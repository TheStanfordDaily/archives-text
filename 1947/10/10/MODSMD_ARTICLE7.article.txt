# Farm Flyers Plan Practice Session
## 
### 
Sunday will he "P-Day" for the
Stanford Flying Club.
Practice Day will open at 11 a.m.
at Livermore Field for the flyers
when they concentrate their flying
ability in accuracy landings and
flour-sack bombing for the coming
Nov. 1 Pacific Coast Intercollegiate
Air Meet at Livermore, club presi
dent Walt Lull said at last night's
meeting in the Women's Clubhouse.
"We will practice at Livermore
from 11 a.m. till 12:30 p.m.," he said.
Planes are available for club flyers
to rent for the flight to the field.
Pilots will be charged only for flight
time in the rented planes.
Secretary Phil Riley will have
lunches at the field for the group.
Planes leave Palo Alto and Santa
Clara Valley airports at 10:30 a.m.
Flyers having questions may con
tact Lull by calling P.A. 8635.
