# KSU TRYOUTEEES TO MEET
## 
### 
KSU staff will hold a final meet
ing with tryoutees who have signed
up and any others who are inter
ested today at 5 p.m. in room 205,
Memorial Hall, announced Steve
Phillips, manager of KSU. The sta
tion is particularly interested in
commercial time salesmen, adver
tising account executives, and com
mercial writers.
Phillips pointed out that KSU is
the only commercial college radio
station in the West (40 per cent of
the time is commercial), and is thus
the only place where commercial
radio experience can be gained.
