# AVC to Present Panel Discussion
## 
### 
With the world in its most preca
rious position since 1930, Stanford
Chapter AVC will present an infor
mal panel discussion on the crux of
world affairs, the Marshall Plan,
Thursday evening at 7:30 in Cub
berley Auditorium, according to
Preston Burchard, vice-chairman.
Hinged on the question "Will the
Marshall Plan aid or hinder world
security?" two competent men, Ray
mond Lawrence of the Stanford
Journalism Department and George
Hitchcock of the California Labor
School have been secured to present
their views. Easton C. Rockwell,
vice-chairman of the Hoover Insti
tute, will moderate the discussion.
