# Classbook Photos Still Being Taken
## 
### 
Branner freshmen will have their
pictures taken for the '51 Classbook
at the Quad office today from 10
a.m. to 12 and 1 to 5 p.m., according
to Bill McPherson, Quad editor.
All off-campus freshmen and any
Branner and Roble freshmen who
have not had their pictures taken
yet should come to the Quad office
tomorrow.
All seniors who have not made
appointments for their Quad pic
tures arc urged to do so in the next
two weeks, according to George
Bergland, business manager.
Apponitments may be made from
9 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. at the Quad of
fice.
The photos will be taken between
Oct. 17 and Nov. 20 at the Quad
ofiicc, he said.
