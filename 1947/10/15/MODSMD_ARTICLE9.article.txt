# Off-Campus Frosh Quad Photos Due
## 
### 
Quad pictures of off - campus
freshmen will be taken today and
tomorrow in the Quad office from 10
a.m. until noon and from 1 to 5 p.m.,
according to Bill McPherson, edi
tor.
Roble and Branner freshmen who
have not had their pictures taken
will be taken at that time also.
Senior pictures will be taken from
Oct. 17 to Nov. 14. All seniors who
have not signed up for an appoint
ment are urged to do so, McPherson
said.
