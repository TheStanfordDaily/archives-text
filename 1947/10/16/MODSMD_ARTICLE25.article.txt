# Sigma Nu Formal
## 
### 
Under a theme of black, white,
and gold. Bruins and Indians will
join forces Saturday evening at the
annual White Rose Ball, sponsored
by Sigma Nu, from 9 to 1 in the
Basketball Pavilion.
With suits or tuxes and evening
dresses named correct attire for
men and women, respectively, ball
goers will dance to the music of Les
Brown and his orchestra.
Priced at $5, bids will be on sale
today and tomorrow at the ASSU
office from 9 a.m. until noon and
from 1 to 4 p.m. Any unsold bids
may be purchased at the Pavilion
Saturday night.
