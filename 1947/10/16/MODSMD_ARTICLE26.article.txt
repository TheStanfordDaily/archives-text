# Rhodes Applicant Interviews Set
## 
### 
Candidates for Rhodes Scholar
ships will be interviewed tomorrow
from 1:30 to 4:30 p.m. and Saturday
from 9 a.m. to 12 noon, according
to Philip Buck, chairman of the
Stanford Committee on Rhodes
Scholarships.
"No student will be eligible to be
interviewed who has not filed a
written letter of intention to com
pete for a scholarship previous to
Tuesday noon," emphasized Mr.
Buck.
Other students wishing to com
pete should contact Professors
Buck, Spaeth, Koenig, or Johnson
immediately.
