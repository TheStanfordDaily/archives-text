# Branner Chooses Lewis President
## 
### 
Mimi Lewis, Sandra Day, and
Maribeth Harelson are the new
president, first vice-president, and
second vice-president of Branner
Hall, as a result of elections held
yesterday, Doris Cooper, election
chairman, announced.
Other new officers include Amy
Morrison, secretary; Elaine Walton,
treasurer; Jean McCrea, Women's
Conference representative, and Com
mittee of Five members Marilyn
Schwartz, Charlotte Nourse, Betty
Gillespie, and Ruth Kilpatrick.
