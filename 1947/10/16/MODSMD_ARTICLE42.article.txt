# Conference Party To Honor Kimpton
## 
### 
Dean and Mrs. Lawrence A.
Kimpton, the new dean of students
and his wife, and Miss Elva Fay
Brown, chief counselor for women,
will be guests at a Women's Confer
ence tea this afternoon at 4:15 in
the lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse, according to Taffy
Countryman, Conference vice-pres
ident.
A short business meeting will
precede the tea.
