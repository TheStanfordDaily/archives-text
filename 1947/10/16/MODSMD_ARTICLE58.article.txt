# Few Symphony Tickets Remain
## 
### 
Only a few season tickets for the
3Gth season of the San Francisco
Symphony Orchestra remain on
sale, according to Biddy Boland,
chairman of the Symphony Forum
committee. These tickets will be
sold the rest of this week in the
Nitery from 2 to 4 p.m. at prices
ranging from $14.50 to $20.40.
The concerts will be presented on
17 Thursday evenings in the War
Memorial Opera House, San Fran
cisco, from Nov. 13 through March
22. Featured in the series will be
Pierre Monteux and the San Fran
cisco Symphony Orchestra and
many eminent soloists.
