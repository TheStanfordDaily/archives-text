# Ski Club Raises Membership Fee
## 
### 
Membership fee in the Stanford
Ski Club v/as raised to $2.50 at the
first meeting of the group Wednesday
night. The dollar increase will pay
for a membership in the California
Ski Association, plus a year's sub
scription to the C'SA's newspaper,
"The Skier."
Mardi Bemis was elected secretary
of the club, replacing Caroline Neil
son who resigned. Art Lubic was
appointed publicity chairman. Jim
Brehler announced that this year's
Stanford Ski Team will be a ten
man team.
Membership cards for the new
year 1947-48, will be ready by the
next meeting of the ski club in No
vember.
