# SC Train Tickets On Sale at ASSU
## 
### 
Rooters' train tickets for the USC
game are now on sale at the ASSU
office, according to ASSU president
Tom Martzloff.
Round trip coach tickets cost
$13.69; pullmans may also be pur
chased at a higher price. The root
ers' train will leave here at 7:40
p.m. Nov. 7 and will arrive in Los
i Angeles at 8 the next morning.
Rooters can leave Los Angeles at
I 2:30 Sunday morning and arrive
j here at 3:15 p.m., or may take the
1 noon Daylight which leaves Los
i Angeles at 12:15 p.m. Sunday and
J arrives here at 9:20 p.m.
The Daylight will not cost more
than the other train, but, there is
limited space, and reservations must
be made l'or it by Nov. 3, MartzlofT
said.
