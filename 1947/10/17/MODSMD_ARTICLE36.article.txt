# BRANNER PLANS FORMAL
## 
### 
Confetti Carnival is the theme of
Branner's autumn formal to be held
Oct. 25 from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. in the
Branner lobby, according to Jean
McCrea, committee publicity chair
man. Leonard Auletti and his or
chestra will play. Decorations will
consist of confetti and balloons.
