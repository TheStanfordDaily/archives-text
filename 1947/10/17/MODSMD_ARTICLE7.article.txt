# KSU's New Frequency Is 590 on Radio Dial
## 
### 
Students are reminded that the
new frequency for KSU, campus
radio station, is 590 on your dial.
The frequency was changed from
880 in order to assure a better re
ception.
The only commercial broadcast
for tonight is Gaite Parisienne at
10:30. The station will broadcast
assorted music that "students want
to hear."
