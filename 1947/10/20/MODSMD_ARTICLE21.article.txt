# Cartoonist to Appear On Farm Tomorrow
## 
### 
Justin Murray will appear in per
son tomorrow in the Bookstore for
the first sale of his new book, "Ca
ble Car Daze," from 1 to 3 p.m. He
will autograph copies, which will
sell for $3.50.
"Cable Car Daze" is a book of
cartoons and captions depicting the
problems of San Francisco's cable
cars. Although this is Justin Mur
ray's first book, some of his cartoons
have been printed in magazines.
