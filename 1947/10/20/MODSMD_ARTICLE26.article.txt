# Car Registration Will End Today
## 
### 
Today at 5 p.m. is the last day for
free registration of cars, announced
Gordon Davis, police chief. After
today a late registration fee of $1
will be charged. Students, faculty,
and staff members who have not
registered their cars must do so at
the police office in the Corporation
Yard.
Also several traffic citations arc
now delinquent, Davis said. Those
who have received parking or
speeding citations should call at the
police office within the next 48
hours to clear them. Failure to re
spond will result in an additional
fine, he said.
