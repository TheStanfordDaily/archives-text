# Board Will Hire Fashion Agents
## 
### 
Stanford is on the list of schools
from which Bates Fabric, Inc., will
choose representatives to serve this
year on their College Board, accord
ing to a recent announcement.
Any student interested in serving
on this board, which aids the com
pany in designing, coloring, styling,
and general acceptance of iheir
product, should contact Margip
Hanson at the Daily Shack between
3 and 4 any afternoon.
The representatives chosen will
be paid $150 and will receive an all
expense trip to New York, where
they will stay at the Waldorf-As
toria for a week.
Students applying should have an
interest in advertising.
