# Brannerites Plan Confetti Carnival
## 
### 
Confetti Carnival, Branner's fall
formal, will be held Saturday night
from 9 to 1 in Branner lobby with
music provided by Leonard Aulet
ti's band.
Tickets will be on sale for $2 to
Branner Hall until Tuesday night.
Beginning Wednesday, remaining
tickets will be sold in the ASSU
office, according to Jean McCrae,
ticket chairman.

Mel Lewis and Martha Redding.

Bancroft, decorations manager;
Betty Stockton, arrangements; Anne
Phleger, patrons; Jean McCrae,
publicity; Shirley Smith. blind
dates; and Anna Kutz, refresh
