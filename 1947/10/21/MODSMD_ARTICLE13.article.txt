# Spanish Talk Scheduled By Sigma Delta Pi
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Pi, Spanish honorary
society, will feature a talk in Span
ish by Aurelio M. Espinosa, ex
head of the Romanic languages de
partment, in its meeting today at
4:15 p.m. in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
This combined business and social
meeting will honor the four hun
dredth anniversary of Cervantes,
author of "Don Quixote," according
to Lawton B. Kline, president of the
society.
