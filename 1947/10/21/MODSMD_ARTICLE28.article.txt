# TROJANS ENROLL 21.945
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Oct. 20 (UP).—
University of Southern California
today reported a fall record-break
ing enrollment of 21.945 students in
cluding 17,525 men and 4,420 wom
en. Of the total, 14,399 are veterans.
