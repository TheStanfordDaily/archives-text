# Bridge Club Picks Tournament Team
## 
### 
The Stanford Bridge Club will
hold its second qualifying round to
night to pick the team to represent
the Indians in the first semi-annual
Stanford-California bridge tourna
ment to be held on the Farm Nov. 1.
Thirteen players will compete for
each school in the meet. A return
engagement will be scheduled in
Berkeley next spring, according to
Howard Shomate, club president.
New officers will be elected at to
night's meeting which will begin
promptly at 7:15 in the lower lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Membership in the club is open
to all students and dues are 25 cents
per meeting.
