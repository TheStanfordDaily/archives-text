# Clothes, Hooks, Money Sought to Aid Europe
## 
### 
In addition to collecting money
for the University of Naples during
the Four Feathers drive, SIRO is
accepting donations of clothes,
books, and money for CARE pack
ages to aid Europe, according to
Molly Shaw, co-chairman.
Donations may be brought to the
SIRO office from 4 to 6 p.m. on
Mondays and Thursdays.
SIRO also needs workers to help
collect clothes and wrap packages
for mailing to Europe.
