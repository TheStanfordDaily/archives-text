# Commission Rule Changed by Army
## 
### 
The War Department has an
nounced that its plan of competitive
tours to determine Regular Army
commissions will go into effect soon,
Colonel Basil H. Perry, professor of
military science and tactics, said
yesterday.
ROTC graduates and commis
sioned reserve officers are eligible.
Upon completion of the one-year
tour the highest sixth of the com
petitors will be given Regular Army
commissions.
Those interested in more infor
mation about the tours should con
tact Colonel Perry.
