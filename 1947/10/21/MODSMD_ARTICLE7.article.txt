# Far East History Is Theme of Meet
## 
### 
Claude A. Buss, professor of Far
Eastern history, will discuss eco
nomic and social reconstruction in
the Far East at today's meeting of
the World Affairs Council of North
ern California, 417 Market Street,
San Francisco. The informal meet
ing will start at 4 p.m.
Mrs. Frank A. Gerbode, member
of the board of trustees of the
World Affairs Council of Northern
California, joins Dr. Buss in the dis
cussion. Mrs. Gerbode is the wife
of a Stanford Medical School faculty
member.
Both Dr. Buss and Mrs. Gerbode
attended the 10th International In
stitute of Pacific Relations Confer
ence at Stratford-on-Avon, Eng
land.
