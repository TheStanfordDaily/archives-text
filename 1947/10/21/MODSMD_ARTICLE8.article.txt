# Dr. Schultz to Attend Chicago Conferences
## 
### 
Dr. Edwin W. Schultz, head of the
department of bacteriology and ex
perimental pathology, leaves tonight
by air for various scientific meetings
in Chicago.
While in Chicago, Dr. Schultz will
attend a board of trustees' meeting
of the American Board of Pathology
as well as meetings of the College of
American Pathologists and the
American Society of Clinical Pa
thologists. He will return to the
campus Oct. 30.
