ATTENTION PARENTS!
If you have a boy or girl between the ages of 2 z and 4' 2 this is your
chance to help him get a real shirt in life.
Our school is operating on a defin te training program which has been
carefully worked out after study and evaluation of existing nursery
school programs. We are fully equ.pped and specially trained to direct
your child in his creative play and social contacts.
Mr. Thompson has had special work under Dr. Blatz, eminent child
psychologist, Toronto University, Canada, arid has acquired a wide range
of experience with pre-school children at the Fn.dric Burke Nursery
School, San Francisco State College.
Visiting hours 9 to 1 1 a.m. week days.
