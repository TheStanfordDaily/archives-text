# 'MUSIC AT HOME'
## 
### 
The traditional Hallelujah Chorus
from"The Messiah" will conclude
tonight's informal "Music at Home"
concert at the Tresidder home, from
7:30 to 8:30.
Composers to be heard include
Beethoven, Ives, Mozart, Schubret,
and Shostakovich, whose "Age of
Gold" ballet music will be played.
Students and faculty are cordially
invited.
