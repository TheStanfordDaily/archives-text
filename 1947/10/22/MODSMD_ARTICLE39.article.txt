# String Ensemble Gives Friday Night Concert
## 
### 
At the opening concert of the
chamber music series, the Paganini
Quartet will play this Friday night
at 8:15 in Cubberley Hall. The
quartette is comprised of two vio
lins, a viola, and a 'cello.
Tickets for the concert, which is
under the supervision of the Stan
ford Friends of Music, will be on
sale at Memorial Hall from 10 a.m.
till noon and from 1-5 p.m. for $1.80.
Special students tickets will sell for
$1, including tax.
