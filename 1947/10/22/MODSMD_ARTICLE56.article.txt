# Confetti Carnival At Branner Saturday
## 
### 
Amid a shower oi balloons and
confetti, Branner's fall formal, Con
fetti Carnival, will be held Satur
day night from 9 to 1 in Branner
lobby with music provided by
Leonard Auletti's band.
The dance is to be semi-formal,
with suits as correct attire for men
and formals for women.
Bids at $2 will be on sale today
at the ASSU office.
