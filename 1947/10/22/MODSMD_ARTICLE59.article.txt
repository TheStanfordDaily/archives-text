# Women's Rifle Team To Be Formed Soon
## 
### 
Inauguration of a women's rifle
team is near at hand, Frances Lynn,
"rifier publicist," announced last
night.
Interested women will be taught
the nomenclature of the rifle and
later will participate in meets with
other colleges. Rifles will be fur
nished by the University, but am
munition must be purchased by the
students.
The initial round of practice may
start Wednesday at 3 p.m. at the
ROTC rifle range, Miss Lynn said.
