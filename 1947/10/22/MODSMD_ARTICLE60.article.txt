# VACCINATION SLIPS DUE
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates are due al
the Registrar's office on Tuesday for
all new students, . including those
having permits to attend classes, and
all others whose certificates have
expired. Failure to file certificates
by 4:30 p.m. on Tuesday will result
in a fine of $2; or, if not filed by
Nov. 4, registration in the Univer
sity will be subject to cancellation
by the Committee on Public Health.
