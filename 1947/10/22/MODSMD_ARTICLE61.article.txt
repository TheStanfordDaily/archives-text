CANDLELIGHT SERVICES will be
held tonight at 0 in the Memorial
Church.
STANFORD SKI CLUB excom will
meet at 12:30 p.m. today in the
Nitery.
MEMBERS OF THE STANFORD
DAMES will meet tomorrow eve
ning at 7:45 in the club room of
the Women's Clubhouse.
CAMERA CLUB will meet Thurs
day night at 7:30 in the Village
Auditorium. The meeting will
feature a slide-illustrated Kodak
lecture, "How to Make an En
largement."
RAM'S HEAD MEETING today at
5 p.m. in the Ram's Head room.
All active and inactive members
should attend.
MEN'S GLEE CLUB will meet at
4:15 p.m. today in the choir room.
INTER-CLUB COUNCIL meets to
night at 7:30 in the main lounge
at Toyon.
SIRO REPRESENTATIVES will
meet today at 4:30 p.m. in the
shack next to Encina Commons.
THE INTERNATIONAL CLUB will
hold a Spanish dinner Sunday at
7:30 p.m. at the Faculty Club
house. Reservations can be made
with Jean Richmond, club treas
urer.
THE HAWAIIAN CLUB will meet
today at 7:30 p.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse.
WOMEN INTERESTED in ushering
at "The Father" Friday and Sat
urday nights should contact Betty
Jacobs at Lagunita Court.
