# Oregonians Plan Dance After Game
## 
### 
Former Oregon State students,
both graduates and undergraduates,
are planning an informal dinner
dance reunion to follow the Stan
ford - Oregon State football game
Nov. 1.
The dinner dance will be held at
the MofTett Field Officers' Club.
Arrangements are under the direc
tion of E. L. Rada, graduate student
in economics, who may be contacted
at Mountain View 3450 for further
information and reservations.
