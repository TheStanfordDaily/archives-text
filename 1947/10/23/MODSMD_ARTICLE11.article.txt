# Farm Football Team To Get Big Send-off
## 
### 
A large send-off rally for the
Indian team is being planned by
Rally Com, under the co-chair
manship of Dana Turkington and
Dud Dc Groot, when they leave
today for Seattle to play the Uni
versity of Washington Huskies.
The rally will start at 3 p.m. at
the Men's Gym. Coach Marchie
Schwartz will give a short talk,
there will be songs and yells, and
the Stanford Band will play, said
Miss Turkington.
Everyone is urged to come help
the team load its pontoons, life
preservers, life rafts, and "spirit"
aboard the "water-bound wagon"
for the Washington game.
