# Frosh Class To Hold Rally
## 
### 
An all-freshman pep rally will be
held tomorrow night at 7 on the
steps of Encina Hall to get them
into the "spirit" for Saturday's UC
LA frosh game, announced Sanford
VVcisblatt, chairman.
In addition to songs and yells,
Chuck Taylor, coach of the frosh
team, will be introduced with the
team. Rally Com is also attempting
to get the Stanford band for the
rally.
Weisblatt urges all freshmen to
attend the game Saturday and sup
port their team. The game begins at
2:30 p.m. in the stadium. Women
are urged to bring their pom-poms
and men their rooting caps.
