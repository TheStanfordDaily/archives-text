ATTENTION PARENTS!
If you have a boy or girl between the ages of 2V2 and 4Vi this is
your chance to help him get a real start in life.
Our school is operating on a definite training program which has been
carefully worked out after study and evaluation ot existing nursery
school programs. We are fully equipped and specially trained to direct
your child in his creative play and social contacts.
Mr. Thompson has had special work under Dr. Blatz, eminent child
psychologist, Toronto University, Canada, and has acquired a wide range
of experience with pre-school children .it the Fredric Burke Nur'.i-iy
School, San Francisco State College.
Visiting hours 9 to 1 1 a.m. week days.
