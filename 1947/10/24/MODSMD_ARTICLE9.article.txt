# Stanford Plans Bar Association
## 
### 
Organizing the Stanford chapter
of the National Lawyers' Guild is
the purpose of the meeting to be
held next Friday at 8 p.m. in room
100.
The proposed constitution is to be
discussed, and Benjamin Dreyfus,
executive secretary of the San
Francisco chapter, will speak on the
structure and aims of the National
Lawyers' Guild.
The group is the only national
lawyers' bar association which ad
mits student members. The Stan
ford chapter will be open to any
member in good standing of the
Stanford Law School without re
gard to sex, race, color, or religious
or political belief or affiliation.
