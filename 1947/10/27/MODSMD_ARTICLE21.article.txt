###

week, according to Margaret Van
denburg, WAA publicity manager.

Jones, Ellen Mitchell, Jared Fitch,
Thomas, Harriet Hermann, Marjorie
Best, Kay Sanders, Lottie Martens,
Marilyn Dana, Peg Moore, Doris
Spencer. Venetia Taft, Dorothy

swim tonight at 8 at the Women's
Gym. The business meeting will be
at 9.
