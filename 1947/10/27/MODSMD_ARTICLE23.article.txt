# Mendelowitz Art To Be Displayed
## 
### 
An exhibition of landscapes in
watercolor and portraits in oil by
Daniel Mendelowitz, associate pro
fessor of art at Stanford, is now on
display at the Stanford Art Gallery,
according to Mrs. Helen Green
Cross, gallery director.
Many of the watercolors are
scenes from the lower Peninsula,
including two of the foothills near
San Jose and three of the Alviso
area. Another group includes paint
ings done last summer by Mendelo
witz of the Oregon coast, San Juan
Island, and Vancouver Island.
Also on display are etchings of
Loren Barton, which come from the
Stanford print collection.
Art Gallery hours are 10 a.m. to
5 p.m., Tuesdays through Saturdays,
and 12 to 4 p.m. Sundays. The gal
lery is closed Mondays.
