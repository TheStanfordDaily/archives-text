# Varsity Rifle Club Formed on Farm
## 
### 
Coach Bill Huggins announced
yesterday that Stanford is forming
a varsity rifle team to compete in
the Northern California Intercolle
giate Rifle Conference. An organi
zational meeting will be held at
p.m. today at the indoor rifle range
located in-back of the ROTC stables.
Huggins said Stanford's schedule
will be under way before confer
ence matches start in January. He
added that experience is "unneces
sary but desirable." Guns will be
furnished but those interested are
required to purchase their own am
munition. Minor sports letters will
be awarded.
