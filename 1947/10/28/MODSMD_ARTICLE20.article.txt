# Big Game Gaieties Solo Cast Named
## 
### 
Soloists for the Big Game Gaieties
have been named by George Eck
stein, director.
They include: Nancy Toll, Roger
Starr, Bob Gettys, Donna Richards,
Bobbie Pettingill, Peggy Cox, Mir
iam Worland, and Karl Hutchinson.
The complete chorus will rehearse
tonight at 7:30 in the Ram's Head
room, according to Don Sheffey,
musical director.
Dancers will rehearse tomorrow
night at 7:30.
