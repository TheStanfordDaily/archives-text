# GLEE CLUB AT TRESIDDERS'
## 
### 
The Stanford Men's Glee Club,
numbering 50 voices, will make its
first visit to President Tresidder's
home tomorrow evening from 7:30
to 8:30 in the weekly "Music at
Home" concert.
The Glee Club's varied program
will range from Bach's "Jesu Joy of
Man's Desiring" to newly arranged
Stanford songs. Recorded selections
to be heard include the first move
ment of Beethoven's "Eroica Sym
phony" and the "Symphonia India"
by Carlos Chavez.
All students and faculty are cor
dially invited to attend this week's
concert.
