# SF Symphony Series To Open Next Month
## 
### 
Two works of Beethoven, the
overture to "Fidelio" and his Pas
toral Symphony (No. G) will be
played by Pierre Monteux and the
San Francisco Symphony at the
opening concerts Nov. 13, 14, and 15.
William Pijper's Symphony No. 3
and the Suite from Richard Strauss's
opera "Der Rosenkavalier" will also
be included on the program.
Tickets are on sale at the Sym
phony Box Office, Sherman, Clay.
