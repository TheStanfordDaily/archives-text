# YARD MENDS ROOFS
## 
### 
As a result of the heavy rainfall
this past week, the Corporation
Yard has been forced to repair
many a leaky roof and damaged
drainpipe, Carroll Blacker, super
intendent of buildings and grounds,
reported yesterday. The rainfall for
this time of year has been excep
tionally heavy, he said. Since Sept.
1, 2.23 inches have been recorded.
