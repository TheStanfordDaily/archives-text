# Few SC Rooters' Train Tickets Left
## 
### 
Tickets for the rooters' train to
the SC game wiil be sold in the
ASSU office until Monday, after
which they may be purchased at the
Palo Alto SP ticket office, the AS
SU office said today.
There are only 25 seats left on the
noon Daylight leaving Los Angeles
Sunday, they said.
The rooters' train will leave here
at 7:40 p.m. Nov. 7 and will arrive
in Los Angeles at 8 the next morn
ing. It will have a special lounge
car.
