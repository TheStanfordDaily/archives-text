# Millbrae Players Plan Meeting Friday Night
## 
### 
Students at the Village interested
in acting and backstage work are
invited to attend the meeting of the
Millbrae Players at Stanford Village
tomorrow at 8:30 p.m. Signs will
indicate where the meeting will be
held. Ralph Schram, director of
the Millbrae Players, will show and
explain color slides of past Millbrae
productions. Students will be wel
come in costumes, jeans, or house
dresses as there will be a Hallow
een party after the program.
The group will present "First
Lady" at the Village Nov. 7. 8, 14,
and 15. Tickets cost 60 cents.
