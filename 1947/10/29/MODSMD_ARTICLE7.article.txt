# Chappie Sells On Quad Today
## 
### 
"Only four covers? Somebody
dropped a numeral," grated the Old
Boy, gripping his hammer tightly.
"Why, we have almost two dozen!"
The outside cover, he explained
patiently, is d six months' labor of
love by a poor, starving artist.
"It doesn't count," he said. "It
puts Dali's daubs to shame; but the
other twenty—wow!"
A bit more calmly, the Old Boy
explained that Chaparral will be
sold at five locations on campus:
History corner, Engineering corner,
Memorial Court, the Cellar, and the
Post Of!ice.
Sales hours will be from 8 a.m. to
noon—but you'd better come early,
he warned.
