# Beer Mugs, Calendars New Bookstore Items
## 
### 
Beer mugs and social calendars
are among new articles being sold
at the Stanford Bookstore.
The beer mugs are made in the
style of an old-fashioned stein and
have a Stanford seal in red on the
front of them. The social calendars
are composed of photographs on
three themes: California, skiing, and
lature.
