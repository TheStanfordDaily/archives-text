# Allen Continues Baroque Series
## 
### 
In the second of a series of four
programs of Baroque organ music,
Warren D. Allen, University organ
ist, will be assisted Sunday evening
by Harold C. Schmidt and Barbara
Bjornstrom, violinists, and the Stan
ford Chamber Ochestra, directed by
Leonard Ratner.
Dr. Allen will present a program
including music by Corelli, Handel,
and Bach. The program, in the Me
morial Church, will begin at 7:30.
