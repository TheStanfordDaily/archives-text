# '51 Election Rally Presents Nominees
## 
### 
Assuming its new role as "big
brother" to the freshmen, the Class
of '50 will sponsor '51's election
rally next Wednesday, sophomore
president Hank Rosin announced
today.
"At great personal expense, we
will undertake to single-handedly
carry through this vitally important
meeting in the true democratic tra
dition as initiated last year by our
selves as freshmen, and to aid the
frosh in knowing their candidates
and future leaders," rally chairman
Bob Armstrong continued.
The rally will begin at 7:30 in
the Cubberley Auditorhim and will
feature speeches by freshman pres
idential and Excom candidates,
Armstrong stated. Candidates will
be contacted by mail, he said
