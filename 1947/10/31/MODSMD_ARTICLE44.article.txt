# KSU Goes Dramatic Each Wednesday Night
## 
### 
The KSU Radio Workshop pre
sents KSU's only dramatic half
hour show each Wednesday night at
8:30. Plays for presentation are se
lected from scripts given over the
national networks and original dra
mas written by Stanford students.
Producer Charles R. Patton alter
nates between comedy, drama, and
mystery. Leading roles on this year's
earliest productions have been acted
by Ronnee Herrmann, Monte Him
melbaum, Kay Lawrence, Aljean
Levin, and Norman Wilner.
