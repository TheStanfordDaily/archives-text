# Forty-niners Win; Browns Bop Bills
## 
### 
The championship hopes of the
Buffalo Bills were severely battered
in All America Conference play to
day when the Bills were licked by
the Cleveland Browns, 28 to 7, and
the New York Yankees downed the
Baltimore Colts, 35 to 21.
The San Francisco Forty-niners
defeated the Los Angeles Dons, 26
to lfi, in the other game played.
A crowd of 43.176 setting a new
attendance record at Buffalo saw the
powerful Browns, leading the West
ern Division race, march 81 yards
to a touchdown at the opening kick
off.
