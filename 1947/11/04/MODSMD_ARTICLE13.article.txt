# University Student Dies in Crash
## 
### 
A 20-year-old Peruvian student,
Ramon Pardo, was killed yesterday
morning when the car he was driv
ing crashed into a tree one mile
south of Coyote on the Monterey
Road.

When Pardo apparently fell asleep
at the wheel his convertible coupe
left the road, police reported.
A lower division student, Pardo
resided at 846 Hillcrest Drive in
Redwood City.
