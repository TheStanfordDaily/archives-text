# Jazz Society to Hear Unreleased Recordings
## 
### 
The Stanford Jazz Society meet
ing tonight will feature Les Smith
of San Francisco with a program
from his private collection, which
contains many unreleased record
ings of Kid Ory's band.
Smith will also show the simi
larity between Lu Watters' band
and several early jazz groups. All
students are invited to attend the
meeting, which will be held in room
200-R at 8 p.m.
