# 'Y' Offers Community Service Job Information to Stanford Women
## 
### 
The time for the Marriage and the Home lecture scheduled for this
evening at 7:15 has been changed to 7:30 so that students can see the be
ginning of the Pajamarino Rally.
"Career and Marriage?" is the lecture scheduled. Mrs. Gerald Van
Bergen, director of the Palo Alto chapter of the Red Cross will speak.
She will emphasize the fact that children should be placed before
any career, but that it is possible to successfully combine the two.
Men are invited to this lecture for the first lime in the Marriage and
the Home lecture series, according to Dottie Lamar, president of the
Senior "Y."
