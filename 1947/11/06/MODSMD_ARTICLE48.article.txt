# Pool Names New Business Staff
## 
### 
Nine appointments to the busi
ness staff of The Daily were an
nounced yesterday by John T. Pool
Jr., business manager. They will
serve for the 1947-48 college year.
John McPherrin was named re
gional advertising manager and Jim
Donald will be local advertising
manager. Howard Taylor and Jerry
Franks were appointed assistant re
gional advertising managers, and
Norm Worthington was named as
sistant local advertising manager.
Charlotte Agnew will be office
and classification advertising man
ager; Lee Ziegler, promotion man
ager; Gene Sherwood, circulation
manager; and Stan Grams will be
comptroller, Pool said.
