# Prize Movie To Be Shown
## 
### 
The Academy Award winning
movie "Seeds of Destiny" will be
shown all this week in the Little
Theater and Cubberley Auditorium.
This picture is based on the state
ment by Hitler that if the Nazis
went down in defeat they would
leave behind ruin, hunger, stone
heaps, rats, epidemics, and death.
The film shows the utter destruc
tion, which Hitler promised, of
present-day Europe.
The schedule of the showings on
the hour and half-hour is as fol
lows:
Today, 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. in
the Little Theater; tomorrow, 10
a.m. to 12:30 p.m. in Cubberley Au
ditorium; Wednesday, 10 a.m. to
12:30 p.m. in the Little Theater;
Thursday, 9 a.m., 0:30 a.m., and 11
a.m. to 12:30 p.m.; Friday, 10 a.m.
to 12:30 a.m.
The movie lasts approximately 20
minutes.
