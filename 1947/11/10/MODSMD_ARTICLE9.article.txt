# Card Pilot Swims When Plane Sinks
## 
### 
OAKLAND. Nov. 9 (UP).—Lieut.
J. C. Soderstrom, 25, Stanford stu
dent and Naval Reserve pilot, went
out for a plane ride yesterday—but
returned by boat.
Soderstrom was forced to crash
land his Corsair fighter plane in the
bay when his engine failed as he
neared the Oakland airport. He
scrambled out of the downed plane
and paddled ashore in a rubber life
raft before a rescue boat could
reach the scene. He w>is thoroughly
wet, but unhurt.
