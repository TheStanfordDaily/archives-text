# TAFT CRITICISES MARSHALL
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Nov. 10 (UP).—
Sen. Robert A. Taft, in a sweeping
criticism of the Marshall Plan,
said tonight he was in favor of
"reasonable" European aid hut not
tremendous expenditures that
would lead to unbearable taxes,
inflation and restoration of price
controls.
