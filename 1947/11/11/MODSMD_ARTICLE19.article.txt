# Documentary Shows German Destruction
## 
### 
"Seeds of Destiny," the Academy
Award winning documentary film,
will be shown today from 10 a.m.
to 12:30 p.m. in Cubberlcy Audito
rium.
The film shows the utter destruc
tion of Europe today. It is based on
Hitler's statement that if the Nazis
lost the war they would leave hun
ger, ruin, rats, stone heaps, epi
demics, and death.
The movie will be shown tomor
row in the Little Theater from 10
a.m. to 12:30 p.m., and in the Vil
lage Auditorium during the pro
gram there tomorrow at 7:15 p.m.
On Thursday the movie will be
shown in the Little Theater at 9
and 9:30 a.m. and from 11 a.m. to
12:30 p.m. The schedule of the pic
ture on Friday is from 10 a.m. to
12:30 p.m. in the Little Theater.
