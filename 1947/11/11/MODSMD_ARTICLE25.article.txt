# Heckscher Plays For Encina Dance
## 
### 
Ernie Heckscher, San Francisco
band leader, will make a return
appearance at Stanford Saturday
night from 9 to 1, sponsored by the
Encina "To H With '51" dance
committee.
Heckscher, a former Stanford ito,
now swings the baton before a
nine-piece orchestra, which, accom
panied by a vocalist, will play at
the Encina dance following the
Oregon game.
Tickets are now on sale to the
whole campus, and may be pur
chased for $1.80 from Encina wing
social chairmen or at sales booths
outside the Cellar and the Admin
istration Building.
