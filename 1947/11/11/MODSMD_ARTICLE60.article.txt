# Arsonist Fires Minton Car
## 
### 
Flames, evidently started on pur
pose, caused slight damage to the
automobile of M. J. Minton, Palo
Alto, at its parking place near the
Stanford Post Office shortly before
6 p.m. Monday, according to Fire
Chief John Marston.

The prompt arrival of the Stan
ford fire department, which was no
tified by a passerby, held the dam
age to the left front tire.
Minton, an employee of the Post
Office, was on duty at the time.
