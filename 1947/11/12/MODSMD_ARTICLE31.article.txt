# KSU Selects Twin 'Queens'
## 
### 
KSU's "Queen for a Day" is—
twins! Chi Psi house will play host
to the Wadsworth twins, Carolyn
and Charlotte, today at noon with
orchids (furnished by florist John
Skrabo), songs (furnished by the
hashers' quartette), and lunch (fur
nished by chef George Altchison.
"I thought I was seeing double
when the Wadsworth twins came
over to KSU and said, 'We've been
chosen 'Queen for a Day'," Jim
Crooker, special events director, re
marked.
The twins and one of the Chi Psi's
will be interviewed over KSU to
night at 7:45.
"The Affidavit," a mystery drama,
will be presented over KSU to
night at 8:30. Starring roles will be
handled by Phil Pierce and Elea
nor Haines, while the show will be
produced by Lottie Martens and
Aljean Levin.
