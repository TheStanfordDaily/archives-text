# Fred Hannum Is New Men's Council Scribe
## 
### 
Fred Hannum, 23-year-old Army
veteran and senior at Stanford, has
succeeded Phil Chandler as secre
tary of Men's Council, according to
Jim Welch, council chairman.
Chandler's resignation, tendered
for academic reasons, was accepted
yesterday; and Hannum, also a
member of Excom, formally took
over his duties.
