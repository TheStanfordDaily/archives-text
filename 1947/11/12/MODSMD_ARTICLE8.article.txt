# 'Seeds of Destiny' to Be Shown At Little Theater and Village
## 
### 
The Academy Award winning documentary film "Seeds of Destiny"
will be shown today in the Little Theater from 10 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and
in the Village following the rally tonight at 7:15 and 8:15.
Tomorrow the film will be shown in the Little Theater at 9 and 9:30
a.m. and from 11 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. The Friday schedule is from 10 a.m. to
12:30 p.m. in the Little Theater.
The movie was put out by UNRRA to describe the conditions in
Europe and some of the work that it was doing. Now that UNRRA has
been dissolved, the World Student Service Fund is showing the movie.
The film is based on the Hitler statement that were the Nazis defeated
they would leave hunger, rats, ruin, stone heaps, epidemic, and death
behind. The movie shows this utter destruction and the condition of the
children, the future leaders of the world, says the picture.
