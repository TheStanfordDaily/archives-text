# Membership Renewals In Sigma Xi Asked
## 
### 
A call for the renewal of mem
berships in Sigma Xi, national sci
ence fraternity, was made yester
day by Dr. Seville Chapman, secre
tary-treasurer.
Reinstatement of lapsed member
ships may be made by contacting
the Stanford chapter, he said.
The objective of Sigma Xi is to
encourage original investigation in
science, pure and applied.
