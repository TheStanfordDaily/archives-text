# Students Organize 'Deutscher Verein'
## 
### 
A committee was appointed Tues
day, at a meeting of students, to
present the charter for a Stanford
German club to ASSU for approval.
The name of the club will be Deul
scher Verein.
The committee will also decide
upon future activities.
All persons interested in the club
are asked to contact Maurice For
shaw, 132 Toyon, or Henry Blauth,
German Department, in room 419
(Physics corner).
