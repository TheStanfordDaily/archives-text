# Hugh Dalton Resigns; Cripps Is Successor
## 
### 
LONDON, Nov. 13 (UP).—Chan
cellor of the Exchequer Hugh Dal
ton, a power in the Labor party for
years, resigned tonight after admit
ting in Commons that he disclosed
secrets of his budget statement yes
terday to a newspaper man.
King George at once accepted the
resignation and Economics Minister
Sir Stafford Cripps was named
Chancellor to succeed Dalton.
