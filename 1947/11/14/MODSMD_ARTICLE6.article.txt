# Troy Daily Praises Stanford Rooters
## 
### 
A front page story in Monday's
Daily Trojan commended the
"spirit" of Stanford rooters at the
game in Southern California last
Saturday. According to the Troy
daily, at the end of the first half
"silence reigned" in the SC sec
tion while the Farm rooting sec
tion "cheered its team members
loud and long."
The Daily Trojan stated that at
the end of the pre-game in the
Coliseum between the SC jay
vccs and the frosh, the SC root
ing section gave the first of its
"many weak yells."
