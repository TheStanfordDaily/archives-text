# Veteran Farm Barber Dies
## 
### 
James Penington, proprietor of
the Stanford Union barber shop
for the past 30 years, died at his
home Friday evening from natural
causes.
Funeral services will be conducted
at 2:30 this afternoon at the Tinney
Funeral Home by the Rev. O. Wes
ley Janzen of the East Palo Alto
Community Church.
Penington is survived by his wife
Bertie, who lives at 1120 College
Avenue, Palo Alto.
He was born in Sheridan, Oregon,
and has lived in Palo Alto for 33
years.
