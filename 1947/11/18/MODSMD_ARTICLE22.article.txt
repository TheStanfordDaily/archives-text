# 'I' Club Plans Dance Meet
## 
### 
A program of international dances
and songs will be presented by In
ternational Club this Sunday eve
ning at 7:30 in the Cubberley Audi
torium, announced Ara Galstaun,
chairman of the 'I' Club.
More than 15 dances from India,
Turkey, Europe, Hawaii, the U.S.A.,
and South America will be pre
sented in a two - hour program.
Dancers from Stanford and Univer
sity of California are expected to
participate.
Mrs. Lidster, teacher of modern
dance at Stanford, will introduce
the dancers with a short talk.
Students and the faculty are cor
dially invited.
