# Soph Scratch Sheet Of Activities Planned
## 
### 
The sophomore class will soon
become the proud parents of a
houneing baby scratch sheet of '50
activities, called "Soph Soap," the
Soph Excom announced yester
day.
"Soph Soap" will contain perti
nent news of '50 doings, plans for
future doings, and reports of past
events. The first copies will be
distributed to sophomore living
groups and boxes at the ASSU
office sometime before Thanks
giving vacation. They will give
the running times of all class
functions next quarter.
