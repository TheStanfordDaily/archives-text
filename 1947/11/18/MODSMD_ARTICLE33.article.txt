# Pianist Guest Star With Symphony
## 
### 
Zadel Skolovsky, Canadian-born
pianist and recipient of the coveted
Namburg award, will appear as guest
soloist with the San Francisco Sym
phony on Nov. 27, 28, and 29.
Under the direction of Pierre Mon
teux, the orchestra will play the
Tschaikowsky Opus, Symphony
Number 5 by Shostakovish, and
"Minstrel Show" by Morton Gould.
Skolovsky will perform the Tschai
kowsky selection.
Tickets are available at the Sym
phony Box Office. Sherman-Clay &
Co. in the city, and at the Peninsula
Box Office in Palo Alto.
