# BRUINS HUSTLE IN SEC RET
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. Nov. 18 (UP).—
The UCLA Bruins hustled through
another passing and dummy scrim
mage session behind barred gates to
day as coach Bert Laßrucherie em
phasized offense for the Saturday
game with the Southern California
Trojans.
Laßrucherie announced that end
Tom Fears and center Don Paul had
been elected co-captains for the
game and honorary captains for the
season at today's training table.
