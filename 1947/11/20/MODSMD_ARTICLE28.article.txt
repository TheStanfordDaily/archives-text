# Presbyterians to Fete UC, Farm Members
## 
### 
The Westminster Club of the Palo
Alto Presbyterian Church is hold
ing a joint gathering for University
of California and Stanford members
alter the Big Game Saturday in the
YWCA rooms of the Women's
Clubhouse.
General festivities, for winners
and losers, will last until six, and
will consist of songs and refresh
ments. All Presbyterian students
are cordially invited to attend.
