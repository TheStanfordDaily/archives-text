# Geologists to Hear Talk on Landslides
## 
### 
H. H. Neel, Stanford '34, will give
a lecture on control and correction
of landslides today at 4:15 p.m. in
room 320, Geology Building, Dr.
Siemon William Muller, professor
of geology, announced yesterday.
During the past four years Neel
was in charge of landslide problems
for the Tidewater Associated Oil Co.
in the Coast Ranges near Ventura,
Dr. Muller said.
Geology and engineering students
as well as others interested in the
subject are invited.
