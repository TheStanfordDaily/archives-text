# Farm Marksmen To Hold Practice
## 
### 
Stanford's rifle team will hold a
practice session today at 2:30 at the
ROTC rifle range.
Following the varsity practice, the
Stanford Rifle and Pistol Club will
hold a meeting on the rifle range,
beginning at 4:30.
All men and women intei-ested
are invited to attend the meeting.
Tentative plans for non-conference
matches will be discussed. Rifle
Club dues are $1.
Ammunition will also be ordered.
The price is $3.50 for a carton of
500 rounds. Minimum order is set
at one carton.
