# Language Professors Plan Convention Here
## 
### 
One hundred language professors
from universities and colleges
throughout the West will attend the
4Uth annual meeting of the Philo
logical Association of the Pacific
Coast in the Education Building
Friday, Dr. B. Q. Morgan, head of
the Germanic languages department
and local chairman for the conven
tion, announced yesterday.
The day's program will consist of
three separate sessions and an in
formal dinner at L'Omelette.
Eight Stanford faculty members
will be among the 34 speakers at
the all-day meeting.
