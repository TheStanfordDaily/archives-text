# Navy Jobs Open To Seniors, Grads
## 
### 
Applications by college seniors
and graduates without work expe
rience for the professional positions
of chemist, engineer, librarian,
physicist, metallurgist, mathemati
cian, and psychologist are now be
ing taken by the Navy Department
at first- and second-class post of
fices.
Applicants may specify their de
sired place of employment, the
principal research establishments
being located in California, Florida,
Maryland, Virginia, and Washing
ton, D.C. Dec. 1(5 is the final date
for submitting applications.
