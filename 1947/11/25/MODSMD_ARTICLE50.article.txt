# ANOTHER GAME TONIGHT
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity will play
its second game in as many nights
when it meets the San Francisco
Olympic Club in the Pavilion at
8:15.
Fred Linari, who played on the
alumni team last night, will play
for the Flying "O" men, as will
Ward Lame, Dick Spillane, Joe
Mirchovich, Al Dutil, and Her
man Hennessy.
