# Beetles Will Wipe Out Weed and Then Starve
## 
### 
BERKELEY, Nov. 24 (UP).—The
vanguard of a hungry horde of
000,000 bettles imported from Aus
tralia started on a giant feeding
spree today which eventually will
wipe out a poisonous California
weed—and the beetles, too. They'll
starve when the weeds are gone.
