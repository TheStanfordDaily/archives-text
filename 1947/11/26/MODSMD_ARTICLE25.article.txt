# Encina Announces Holiday Schedules
## 
### 
Encina diners will receive table
service for the first time this quar
ter when they sit clown to eat their
turkey at 2 p.m. tomorrow. Both
the east and the west lines will be
open.
All other meals tomorrow through
Sunday will be handled through the
west line only. Breakfast tomorrow
will be from 8:30 to 9 a.m., and from
8 to 8:30 a.m. Friday and Saturday.
Other meal times are as follows:
lunch, Friday and Saturday, 12 to
12:30 p.m.; dinner, Friday and Sat
urday, 6 to G:3O p.m.; brunch, Sun
day, 10 to 10:30 a.m.; and dinner,
Sunday, 5:45 to 6:35 p.m.
Because tomorrow is a holiday,
laundry exchange will take place at
the hall from 10 to 11 a.m. and from
12:30 to 2 p.m. today and Friday this
week only.
The barber shop will be open ev
ery day except tomorrow, and the
Encina Cellar will maintain usual
hours throughout the vacation.
