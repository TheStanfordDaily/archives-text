# Debate Team To Meet Cal
## 
### 
Members of the Debating Society
will meet the University of Cali
fornia in two debates this week.
Dow Carpenter and John Belice will
debate with a California team on
the question, "Resolved, that the
United States should support, the
Chinese Nationalist Government,"
today at 3 p.m. in room 35 of the
Education Building, according to
John Menzel, debate Manager.
Wednesday at 4 p.m. on the Ber
keley campus, Martin Vitousek and
Edv/ard Cramer of Stanford will
lake the negative of the same reso
lution, opposing any United States
support to the Chinese Nationalists.
Tom Brand and Townsend Brown
II will describe the difficulties in
forming a federal world government
at a meeting of the San Jose Inter
national Club today.
John Hines and Jack Marcum
will participate in a constructive
debate on socialized medicine be
fore the Debating Society Wednes
day at 7:30 p.m. in room 35 of the
Education Building, Menzel said.
