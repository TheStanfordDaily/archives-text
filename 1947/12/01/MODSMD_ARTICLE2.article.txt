# Series to End With Musical
## 
### 
Concluding the Tuesday Evening
Series for the fall quarter, the Uni
versity Orchestra and Chorus will
present a joint program in Memorial
Auditorium tomorrow evening at
7:30. The program will be the first
appearance of the year for both
groups.
Directors of the two organization?
are Professor Harold Schmidt of the
University Chorus and Dr. Leonard
Ratner of the University Orchestra.
Both men are new to Stanford this
year, Dr. Ratner coming from the
University of California and Profes-.
sor Schmidt from Western College
in Ohio.
