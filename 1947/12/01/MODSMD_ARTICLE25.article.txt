# Ram's Head Asks For One-Act Plays
## 
### 
The beginning of winter quarter
is the Headline for turning in origi
nal one-net plays to Ram's Head,
according to Elaine Maltzmann,
Ham's Head secretary.
The plays may be on any subject
as long as they are one-acts, Miss
Maltzmann said. The winners in
the annual Ram's Head competition
will be produced in February.
Plays should be submitted to ei
ther Miss Maltzmann, Bob Blum.
Ram's Head president, or Paul
Baker, dramatic manager.
