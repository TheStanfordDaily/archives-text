# Tau Beta Pi Group Initiates Members
## 
###

wore initiated into the Stanford
chapter of Tau Beta Pi, national
honorary engineering society, last
Tuesday at the Faculty Clubhouse.

Adams, Fred E. Anderson, William
P. Bartley, Preston W. Byington,

page, Edward A. Cornwall, Charles
L. Cutting, Alan R. Eagle, William

Roger E. Helstrom, Grant O. Hen
inge, Robert B. Hicks, Jacob R. Is
ken, Robert E. Johnston, William E.
Kershaw, Donald King, Solomon
Levin, Alan Logan, Dickerson Mc-
Afee, John W. McDonald, Albert J.

George J. Nothwang.

Frederick W. Terman, John N.

The final meeting of the quarter
will be held Thursday evening.
