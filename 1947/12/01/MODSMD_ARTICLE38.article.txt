# Essay Contest Open to Students
## 
### 
Stanford undergraduates who can
express their thoughls on "An
American Program for World Peace
in the Present Crisis" in 5,000 to
G.OOO words are eligible to enter
the $3,000 essay contest sponsored
by the Tamiment Social and Eco
nomic Institute.
Manuscripts, which should be
typed double space, are to be sub
mitted by April 23, 1948, to 7 East
15th Street, New York 3, N.Y.
A first prize of $1,000. with 13 ad
ditional prizes amounting to $2,000,
wil be awarded. Manuscripts should
be clipped to an identifying paper
containing the author's name, col
lege and home addresses, telephone
number, and name of college.
