# Army, Navy View Research Project
## 
### 
The Stanford Research Institute
will come under the scrutiny of 12
Army and Navy representatives
from Washington, D.C.. and Wright
Field, Ohio, Dec. 8 and 9 when it
presents its progress report on the
expansibility of the United States
aircraft industry.
Dr. William F. Talbot, director,
and Weldon B. Gibson, project di
rector, will host the visiting service
personnel at luncheon in Atherton
Dec. 8.
