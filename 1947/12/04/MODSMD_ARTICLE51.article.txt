# Club to Show Film On Rio de Janeiro
## 
### 
"Rio, Marvelous City," a color
film loaned to the Portuguese Club
through the courtesy of the Moore-
McCormick Lines, will be shown to
all interested students in Cubber
ley Auditorium tomorrow at 4:15
p.m., according to club president
Kitty Royal.
Refreshments will be served to
Portuguese Club members directly
following the movie, in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse,
Miss Royal stated.
