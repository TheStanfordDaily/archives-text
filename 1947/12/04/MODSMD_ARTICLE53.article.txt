# Seattle Mothers' Club To Give Holiday Dance
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club of
Seattle will sponsor a dance on Dec.
22 at the Olympic Hotel in Seattle
for students, alumni, and guests, it
was announced yesterday.
The dance is informal, and will
last from 0:30 until 12:30. Music
will be provided by Bob Harvey's
orchestra. Tickets at $3.75 per
couple may be obtained from John
Prince at the Sigma Chi house, PA
8650.
