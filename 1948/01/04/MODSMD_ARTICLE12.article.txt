# Folk Dance Series Begins for Adults
## 
### 
Beginners' folk dancing for adults
will start a new series tomorrow at
8:15 p.m. in the ball room of the
Palo Alto Community Center.
Polkas, schottisches, and waltzes
will be taught, with many European
and early American dances based
on these steps, and also American
square dances,
Anyone may attend, and it is not
necessary to bring a partner. A fee
of $1 for three months will be
charged.
