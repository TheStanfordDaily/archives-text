# KA's Plan Open Trademark Dance
## 
### 
The advertising department of
the Kappa Alpha Corporation an
nounces a new line called "The
Trademark Dance." This new
product will be unveiled to the
public for the first time on Satur
day from 9 to 1 at the Kappa Alpha
House, 562 Lasuen.
There will be food and refresh
ments and dancing to the orchestra
of Jack Bancheros. Bring your date
and come as your favorite ad!
