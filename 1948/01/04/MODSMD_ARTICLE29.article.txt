# German Author Will Teach Here
## 
### 
Ernst Weichert, one of present
day Germany's best known authors,
will come to the United States next
summer to teach in the German
department at Stanford University.
His appointment marks the first
attempt since the war to continue
the tradition of bringing outstand
ing Europeans into the graduate
work of the German deparment,
said Dr. Friedrich W. Strothmann,
department head.
The 60-year-old author and
scholar was interned in the ill
famed Buchenwald concentration
camp.
