# Basketball Tickets Sell at SAC Today
## 
### 
Tickets for the Stanford-UCLA
and Stanford-USC- basketball
games to be played in the Pavilion
this weekend will be available at
the SAC today. The booth will be
open from 8:30 a.m. to 12 noon and
from 1 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.
Rooters' tickets are free for the
Bruin game, 50 cents each for the
Trojan contest. Student body cards
must be presented, but no student
may present more than two cards.
