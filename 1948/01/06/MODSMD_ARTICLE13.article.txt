# Heinrich Freund Services Today
## 
### 
Funeral services will be held this
afternoon for Heinrich A. Freund,
authority on international law who
died Saturday night following a
heart attack. Freund had been car
rying on research work as a fellow
in the Slavic studies of the Hoover
Library.
Freund was born in Poland 62
years ago. During the war he es
caped from Nazi controlled terri
tory to England, and later Austra
lia, where he was attached to the
Australian Institute of International
Affairs.
He came to Stanford last October.
Freund had written several books
and was a member of the Inter
national Law Association in Lon
don and the American Sociely of
International Law in Washington,
D.C.
