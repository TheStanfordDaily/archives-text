# Folk Dance Class Open to Stanford
## 
### 
A beginning folk dancing class for
adults will start, tonight at the
Community Center in Palo Alto
from 8 to 10, it was announced yes
terday. The class will last three
months, and all Stanford students
interested in joining are invited to
attend.
There is also an intermediate class
Wednesday nights, and an advanced
group which meets Thursdays at the
Community Center.
