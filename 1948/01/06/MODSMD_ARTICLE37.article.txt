"V
This corner is at Ihe disposal ol
any Stanford Student to use as
he pleases. Send your personal
notices, want ads, poems, jokes,
or even plugs tor good ole
Longbarn to Eddie at you know
where.
Senders of any plug which we
print receive a card entitling
them to a free dinner at Long
barn.
At the football game it is a shame
Someone stands up to block your
view.
To remedy this
A game you'll never miss
If you cover their seats with glue
—E.K.
The Longbarn Space
Ees the place
Where you "sling"
For your supper.
—P.M.D.
The Bull, that is.
Dear P.M.D.—The same goes for
you. too. The sooner you send
it the soonor you get it.—Ed.
A
Longbarn
and
Dinah's Shack
