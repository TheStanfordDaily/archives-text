# Crewmen Meet Today
## 
### 
All men signed up for crew are
asked to report to the SAC build
ing at 3:30 this afternoon, accord
ing to Crew Captain J. J. Gates.
Purpose of the meeting will be to
weigh in and launch the winter
quarter program.
Gates added that anyone interest
ed in crew development would be
welcome to attend the meeting.
"We have a fine outfit now and it
looks as if we may have a success
ful spring season."
