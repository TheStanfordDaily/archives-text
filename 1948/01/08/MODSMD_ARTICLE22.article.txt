# RETRACTION
## 
### 
It was stated in Wednesday's
Daily that "all University books on
music, musical scores, and records
will be moved into the new library
on the Knoll." On the contrary,
only books and records of interest
to advanced and graduate students
will be contained in this library.
Other books and records of interest
to undergraduates will be in the
Main Library and Reserve Book
liootn.
