This corner is at the disposal of
any Stanford Student to use as
he pleases. Send your personal
notices, want ads, poems, jokes,
or even plugs for good ole
Longbarn to Eddie at you know
where.
Senders of any plug which we
print receive a card entitling them
to a free dinner at Longbarn.

I have studied my histology
'Till my eyes are dim and red;
I'd love to go out 'cheering', boys,
I just gotta get to bed.
I have studied biochcm, my dear,
'Till the letters dance and leap;
I'd love to stay and neck with you,
But I just gotta get some sleep.
I have yet to read anatomy.
Though eyes be dim and red,
But I'll still go down to LONG
BARN,
Though I never get to bed!
—F. E. Yates
The vogue is longer skirts right
now
In fashion's changing pace;
Milady wants to lift our gaze
From shapely shanks to facc.
