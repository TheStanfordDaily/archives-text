# Marksmen Get Prizes
## 
### 
First place silver medals, which
were won by the Stanford Rifle Club
when it finished on top of the Class
B competition at the recent Palo
Alto Rod and Gun Club invitational
shoot, will be presented to team
members today at 4:15 at the ROTC
Range.
Co-captain Bill Huggins finished
as high point man for the Indians
in the competition which was held
at Redwood City, and which in
volved local rifle groups.
