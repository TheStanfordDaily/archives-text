# Library to Exhibit Gutenberg's Bible
## 
### 
In co-ordination with National
Printing Week. Jan. 11 to 18, the
Library will exhibit a collection of
books varying from the first printed
volume, Gutenberg's Bible, to re
cent releases of the Stanford Press.
"The Making of Books," a motion
picture illustrating the latest print
ing methods of a large modern pub
lishing house, also will be presented
to students. The showing of the film
is scheduled for next Tuesday at 5
p.m. in the Cubberlcy Auditorium.
