# Leading Soprano To Sing on Farm
## 
### 
Jennie Tourel, referred to as "the
perfect singer," will make her Stan
ford appearance Friday, January 1(J,
in the Memorial Auditorium.
"Tickets for this performance are
now available in the Nitery," an
nounced Barbara Aitken, Concert
Series manager.
The tickets may be purchased at
prices ranging from $1.80 to $3.00.
The office hours of the Nitery are
from 1 to 5 p.m., Monday through
Friday.
Jennie Tourel, a leading mezzo
soprano of (he Metropolitan, will
include in her program Schubert's
"Ave Maria," a selection from
"Samson and Delilah" by St. Sacns,
the famous "Habanera" from Bizet's
"Carmen," plus selections by De
bussy, Grieg, and Schumann.
