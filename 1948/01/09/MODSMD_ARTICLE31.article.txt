# Cards for Film Series Available Till Monday
## 
### 
Monday is the last day students
may obtain membership cards for
the Stanford University Film Soci
ety. Cards may be obtained either
by sending a check or money order
for $1.50, along with a self - ad
dressed, stamped envelope, to the
Committee on Public Exercises,
room 161 A, Administration Build
ing, or by getting them at the com
mittee's office between 8:30 a.m. and
4:30 p.m., Marylou Bruna, secre
tary, announced.
The series, which will be present
ed on Mondaj's at 7:15 p.m., will
feature outstanding films of the
early 20th century. Many famous
stars such as Thcda Bara, Richard
Barthelmess, Lillian Gish, Charlie
Chaplin, Mary Astor, and Douglas
Fairbanks will be shown during the
quarter.
