# LAST DAY
## Mural Signup Ends at Noon 
### 
Deadline for entering teams in
this quarter's intramural sports has
been extended until 12 noon today,
according to Director Chuck Taylor.
Competition will begin Monday in
basketball, table tennis, and bowling.
The schedule of games will be pub
lished in The Daily Monday.
Bowling, this quarter, will be or
ganized as a league sport instead of
having elimination matches.
In addition to the three regular
sports, there will be a special noon
volleyball league, which will not
count in yearly standings. However,
the winner of the league will receive
a trophy.
