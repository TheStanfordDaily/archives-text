# Entries Desired In Photo Contest
## 
### 
The first all-student photography
exhibit will be presented by the
Stanford Art Museum, starting Jan.
15. The photo contest to secure 30
8 x 10 prints for the exhibit is spon
sored by the Camera Club.
Prizes totaling $10 in photo
graphic equipment are offered by
the Camera Club for the best prints
in the exhibit. The only require
ment to enter this exhibit is that the
photographic data accompany the
picture. Color prints, toned photos,
and paper negative derivations are
all acceptable if in Bxlo size. En
tries should be submitted to the
Village Counselor's office or the
ASSU office.
