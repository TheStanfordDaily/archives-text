# 'Fighting Lady' Shown Tomorrow
## 
### 
"Fighting Lady," a technicolor
film depicting 14 months in the life
of an Essex - class aircraft carrier
during World War 11, will open the
winter quarter Tuesday Evening
Series tomorrow night, in Memorial
Auditorium. The public is invited
without charge.
The picture was filmed by Navy
combat photographers aboard the
ship and is narrated by Lt. (j.g.)
Robert Taylor.
