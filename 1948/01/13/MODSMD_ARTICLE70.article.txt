# Magnolia to Hold Carnival Tonight
## 
### 
With proceeds earmarked for
CARE, Casa Magnolia will sponsor
a fund-raising Mardi Gras tonight at
9;30 in the Social Room, according to
co-chairmen Nancy Alden and Phyl
Brown.
Carnival concessions, at five cents
a chance, will include potato races,
'leg art," bingo games, fortune tell
ing, and caricatures.
Refreshments, accompanied by re
corded music, will be sold at 10:3U.
Tickets to the affair may be pur
chased at the cashier's booth in Up
stairs Magnolia.
