# Farm Flying Club To Hear Elsmore
## 
### 
Memberships to the Indian Flyers
Cooperative, Incorporated, will be
open to prospective Farm students
tonight when the club meets in the !
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house at 7:30.
Club pilots fly a 65-horsepower 1
Taylorcraft for only $2.75 per hour
as compared to commercial prices
running between $7 and $8.
Tomorrow night at 7:30 the Stan
ford Flying Club will present Kay T.
Elsmore as guest speaker. Elsmore
directed rescue operations in New
Guinea's "Shangri-la Valley" for the
surviving two soldiers and WAC of
a C-47 crash.
