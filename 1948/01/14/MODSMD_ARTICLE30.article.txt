# Special Air Rates Offered Skiers
## 
### 
Direct air flights to snow country
are now available to skiers.
The newly formed Airway Ski
Tours has regular scheduled planes
flying between Oakland and Reno.
Buses at Reno take the skiers to
nearby Mt. Rose.
The Sunday flight leaves Oakland
.'it X a.m. The return flight leaves
the Reno Airport at 8:30 that night.
The trip takes one hour and five
minutes.
A weekend special leaves Satur
day morning and returns Sunday
evening. The cost of the flights is
below regular airline rates.
The first flight of the new Airway
Ski Tours was made last Sunday.
Special guests on the first flight
were representatives from Bay Area
radio stations and newspapers, in
cluding The Stanford Daily.
