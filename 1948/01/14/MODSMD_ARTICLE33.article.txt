# business Students To Attend Clinic
## 
### 
The class in sales management of
the Graduate School of Business
will attend a one-day sales clinic
at the Merchandise Mart in San
Francisco today, David E. Faville,
professor of marketing, announced.
The clinic is being conducted by
John Licy of the Lacy Sales Insti
tute of Boston. The purpose of the
conference is to consider the new
est and most useful ideas on sales
manship techniques and training.
