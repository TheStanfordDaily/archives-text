# Kelly, Poole Win Drama Contest
## 
### 
Robert Kelly and Roy Poole are
the authors of the winning one
act plays, Allen Fletcher, general
director, announced yesterday. They
will receive memberships in Ram's
Head for their plays, "The Deceiv
er" and "Publicity." The third win
ning play will be announced tomor
row.
Cast and crew tryputs will be held
tonight from 7:30 to 10 p.m., and
tomorrow from 2:30 to 5:30 p.m. and
from 7:30 to 10 p.m. Everyone is
urged to come and try out. Parts
in the plays count toward member
ship in Ram's Head.
The plays will be given Feb. 19,
20, and 21.
