# Education Series To Start Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Education Faculty
Lecture Series opens in Cubberley
Auditorium at 7:30 tonight with a
talk by Dr. Lawrence G. Thomas,
associate professor of education, on
"The Scientific Method as a Way
of Life."
The discussion of ' Textbook Im
provement and International Un
derstanding" by Dr. I. James Quil
len, originally scheduled for Jan.
29, has been postponed until March
5, stated Kenneth Munford, chair
man of the series.
