# Prospects Sought For Merchandising
## 
### 
Men interested in post - college
jobs in the field of merchandising
will have the opportunity today and
tomorrow of meeting L. L. Robin
son, personnel manager of the Oak
land mail order division of Mont
gomery Ward & Co., who will be
conducting interviews on campus.
All prospective candidates will
meet with Robinson in room 122,
Schol of Business, at 9:30 a.m. to
day. Individual interviews will be
held this afternoon and tomorrow.
Appointments for personal inter
views with Robinson may be made
at the Appointment Service office,
room 166, Administration Building.
