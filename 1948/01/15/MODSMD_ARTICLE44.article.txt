# Hey Whatta Score!
## 
### 
KINGSTON, R. 1., Jan. 14 (UP).—
Sparked by forward Dike Hole, the
undefeated Rhode Island State
Rams swamped Brooklyn College
104 to 80 before 1.200 fans at Rod
man Hall here tonight.
Fifty-five tallies were made in
the first 10 minutes of the fast
moving game—34 for Rhode Island
arid 21 for the King's men. At the
end of the first half the Rams held
a comfortable margin of 14 points,
with a 53-39 score.
