# Yell Candidates Will Meet Today
## 
### 
A total of 20 persons have applied
for yell leader positions, according
to Jack Davies, head yell leader.
The first training meeting for
these tryoutees will be held today
in the Education Auditorium from 4
lo 5 p.m. Anyone who has not yet
applied may apply at this meeting,
Davies said.
The tryoutees will be taught
Stanford yells at this meeting; and
in the basketball game following
the next, they will begin to appear
at the games.
Selection of six candidates will be
made in about two or three weeks,
Davies said.
