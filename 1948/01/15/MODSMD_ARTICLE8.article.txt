# Houssay to Visit Stanford Saturday
## 
### 
Dr. Bernardo A. Houssay, Argen
tine physiologist who won a Nobel
prize in medicine last year, will be
on campus Saturday, announces
President Donald B. Tresidder.
Dr. Houssay received the Nobel
prize for his work on the pituitary
gland and its relation to diabetes.
He was formerly a physiologist on
the medical school faculty of the
University of Buncos Aires, but left
his post there following a disagree
ment with the Peron administra
tion.
