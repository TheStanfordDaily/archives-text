# Frosh Girl Nominated For Science Honors
## 
### 
Mary Addleman, Roble 3A, re
ceived notice Wednesday that she
has been nominated for membership
in the American Association for the
Advancement of Science. Mary, who
was a finalist in the Wcstinghouse
Talent Search last year, conducted
a series of experiments in the effects
of light on the chlorophyll of grow
ing plants.
In the course of her work she
detected an error in the Encyclo
paedia Britannica.
