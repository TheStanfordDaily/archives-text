# Villagers to Host Stanford Mothers
## 
### 
The Stanford Village Club will be
the setting for the regular monthly
meeting of the Stanford University
Mothers' Club tomorrow at 2 p.m.
Speakers of the afternoon will be
Dean Lawrence A. Kimpton and
Herbert J. Wunderlich, Village
counselor. Tea will be served by
the villagers, an I the meeting will
conclude with guided tours around
the Village conducted by Leo Souza.
