# NSA Begins New Group
## 
### 
In addition to activities referred to
it by Excom, NSA will initiate a
Forum Committee and a Student
Leadership Committee, which will
be peculiar to the campus organiza
tion.
Under the Forum Committee, per
sons from the Mills College speak
ers bureau, students with experienc
es or information of interest to the
campus public, and artists not ap
pearing in conjunction with the
Concert Series will be programmed
at Stanford.
The Student Leadership Commit
tee will gather material from other
universities who are using student
government tests concerning campus
elections. Compilation of the infor
mation into a report to Excom will
be administered by Election Board,
if the report is adopted.
