# 'Federalists' Begin Membership Drive
## 
### 
To launch their winter quarter
membership drive, Student Feder
alists will open a booth in front of
the Cellar today to make pamphlets
dealing with the Federalist move
ment available to students.
In conjunction with the drive,
Dick Kelton, attired in the garb of
an old-time politician, will discuss
the advantages of world federation
in front of Cubberley at 11:55 this
morning.
Joan Witzman, activities chair
man. has announced that the booth
will be open for at least two weeks.
