# Stanford to See Movies in French
## 
### 
Four French documenary films
will be shown at Stanford Friday
evening at 7:30 in Cubberley Au
ditorium under the auspices of the
Romanic languages department.
The films are "Le Chantier dans
les Ruines," "La Comedie avant
Moliere," '"Les Chemins de La mar
line, and "Matisse." They were
secured through the French con
sulate in San Francisco.
