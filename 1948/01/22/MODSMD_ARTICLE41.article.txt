# Emporium Will Hold Student Interviews

###

ard Carver, representatives of the
Emporium, will interview men and
women interested in the merchan
dising field next Monday and Tues
day.

a training program including basic
sales experience and short-term as

ful completion of the training pro
gram will lead to assignments in
the various operating divisions of
