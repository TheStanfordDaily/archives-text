# Phi Rho Sigma to Tour San Quentin Tomorrow
## 
### 
Phi Rho Sigma, Stanford medical
fraternity, will be the guests of
Dr. David G. Schmidt, on a field trip
of San Quentin Penitentiary Satur
day afternoon, Herman Behla, pub
licity manager, announced today.
Dr. Schmidt is an alumni member
and also head of the division of phy
chology at the prison.
The tour will follow a luncheon
to he held at 1 p.m. All members in
terested should meet at the fratern
ity house at 1 p.m.
