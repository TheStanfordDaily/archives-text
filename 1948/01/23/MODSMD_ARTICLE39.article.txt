# Math Professors to Hold Meet at UC
## 
### 
Three members of the Mathematics
department will attend the annual
meeting of the Northern California
section of the Mathematical Associa
tion of America, which will be held
at the University of California to
morrow. They are Professors Har
old Davenport, George Polya, and
Harold M. Bacon.
Dr. Davenport, who is visiting fac
ulty member from University Col
lege of London, will deliver the main
invited paper, on ' The Geometry of
Numbers."

! Dr. Polya will report on the sym
posium on ''How to Solve It," which
he directed last summer at the New
Haven meeting.
Dr. Bacon will deliver a paper on
"A Matrix Arising in Correlation
Theory."
