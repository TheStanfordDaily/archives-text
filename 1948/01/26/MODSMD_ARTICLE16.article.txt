# CRAFT WARNING
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Jan. 25 (UP).
—The Weather Bureau issued a
small craft warning at. 3 p.m. today
in San Francisco Bay for northeast
erly winds of 25 to 35 miles an hour.
The Bureau said the winds would
decrease by Monday noon.
