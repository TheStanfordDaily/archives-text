# Negro Scholar To Give Talk On Literature
## 
### 
Dr. John Lovell Jr., associate pro
fessor of English at. Howard Univer
sity in Washington, D.C., will dis
cuss "Contemporary American Lit
erature" tomorrow night on the
Tuesday Evening Series. The lec
ture, which will begin at 7:30 in
Memorial Auditorium, is open to
the public without charge.
Dr. Love!l is currently in the
West, as visiting lecturer for the
American Friends Service Commit
tee. At Howard, often referred to as
"the capstone of Negro education,"
he is coach of the debating team,
director of the Howard Players, and
sponsor of the campus literary mag
azine, in addition to his teaching
duties.
The colored educator's major
fields are Whitman, the American
drama, and the social backgrounds
of American literature.
