# Yates to Head New SIRO Village Office
## 
### 
Burt Wilner, SIRO co-chairman,
announced that the organization's
Village office in Building 155 A is
now open.
Gordon Yates, SIRO representa
tive in the Village, will be in
charge of the oflice, which will be
open from 12:45 to 1:45 p.m. Mon
day through Friday and from 9 to
11 Saturday morning.
All wrapping of packages will be
done in the Village office because
there is a limited amount of space
in the SIRO campus shack. Wilner
said. The office hours on campus
will remain 12:110 to 1:30 p.m. Mon
day through Friday and 9 to 11 Sat
urday morning.
