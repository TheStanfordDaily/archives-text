# KUHARICH FOR McKEEVER
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Jan. 25 (UP) —
Joe Kuharich, one of Notre Dame's
all-time great linesmen, today sign
ed a one-year contract as head foot
ball coach at the University of San
Francisco, replacing Ed McKeever.
