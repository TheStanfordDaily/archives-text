# Hoedowners to Teach New Square Dances
## 
### 
The Hoedowners will meet to
night at 7:30 in the Women's Gym.
Two new square dances will be
taught. All dances taught last week
and several from la.st quarter will
be reviewed.
Dances included in the review
will be the tuljak, korobushka, la
cus and tzyganotchka, waltz, contra j
dance, Circassian circle, Doria waltz,
schottische, polka, hambo, hopak,
lado blanquito, and the fundamen
tals of square dance.
