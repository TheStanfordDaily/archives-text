# Students Must File Vaccination Slips
## 
### 
All new students must present
vaccination certificates at the
Registrar's ofliee by Feb. 3.
Included are those* -students
having permits to attend classes
and all others whose certificates
have expired.
Failure to file certificates by
1:30 p.m. Feb. 3 will result in a
line of $2. If certificates are not
filed by Feb. 10, registration in
the University will be subject to
cancellation by the Committee on
Public Health.
