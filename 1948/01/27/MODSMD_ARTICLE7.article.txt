# Gallery Displays Period Art Prints
## 
### 
A collection of 16th century prints
showing the great cities of Europe
during the medieval period, with
documentation from"The Culture
of Cities," by Lewis Mumford, goes
on display today in the Stanford Art
Gallery and will be shown through
Feb. 15, it. was announced yester
day.
The prints, loaned by Mr. Moore
S. Achenbach of San Francisco, are
supplemented by plates from the
Life Magazine show, "The Medieval
World," and by contrast, with a
group of illustrations of modern city
planning.
