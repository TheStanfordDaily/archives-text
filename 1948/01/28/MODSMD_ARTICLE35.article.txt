# Cardinals to Hold Leap Year Dance
## 
### 
Cardinals at their meeting last
night planned for the traditional
Sadie Hawkins dance, to be held on
Feb. 28, the eve of "leap year day."
Evie Heitze, Cardinal in charge of
the dance, said that everyone should
wear hillbilly clothes, as much in
the style of Dogpatch as possible.
Dancing will begin in (he Village
Gym at 9 p.m. and will last until
I a.m., said Miss Reitz. Bids, which
the girls are supposed to buy, will
be under $2; the exact price will be
announced laler.
