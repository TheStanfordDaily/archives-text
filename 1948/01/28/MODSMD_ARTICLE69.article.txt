# Life Philosophy Group To Discuss Christianity
## 
### 
An informal student group, to
discuss some of the basic concepts
of the Christian philosophy, will
meet this afternoon at 4:15 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
The group is sponsored by the
Life Philosophy Commission of the
YWCA, under the chairmanship of
Shipley Newlin. Miss Newlin urges
all to come, "especially those who
enjoy a good bull session where
they can express their opinions and
discuss their favorite ideas."
