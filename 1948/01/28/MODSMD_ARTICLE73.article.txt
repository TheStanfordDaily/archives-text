# Stanford Stories To Appear Soon
## 
### 
Edited by Wallace Sterner, dean
of Stanford's Creative Writing
School, " 1T47 Stanford Short Sto
ries," will be published by the Uni
versity Press in early April.
A collection of the stories of 13
of Stanford's young writers, it gives
the reader an insight into the trend
of writing of young postwar writers.
One of the many books celebrat
ing the California Centennial, the
"Mother Lode Album" by Mrs.
Otheto Weston, will not be released
until Feb. 5. Mrs. Weston's book, a
history of the gold area of Cali
fornia, includes such monuments as
the Chinese adobe in Hornitos.
The album contains narly 200
pictures of historical interest.
