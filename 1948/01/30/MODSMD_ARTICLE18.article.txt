INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE
COMMITTEE will meet today at
4:15 p.m. in the lIP office.
WESTERN COLLEGE CONGRESS
committee meets Saturday at 10
a.m. in the Women's Clubhouse.
WESLEY FOUNDATION meets on
Sunday night at 625 Hamilton
Avenue. There will be another in
a series of discussions on interna
tional community. Lunch will be
served at 6:30 p.m.; the meeting
will start at 7:30. Everyone who
is interested is invited.
DAILY STAFF MEMBERS will
meet for the last time this vol
ume Sunday at 1 p.m. in the
shack.
CANTERBURY CLUB will meet
this Sunday evening at All Saints'
Church on the corner of Waver
ley and Hamilton, Palo Alto, at
7:45. The Pev. Arthur Casaday
will lead a discussion on "The
Value of Prayer."
CHRISTIAN STUDENTS are in
vited to join the Westminster Fel
lowship in a discussion about
"The Manifestation of God," fol
lowing youth vespers at 7:15 p.m.
For information call PA 2-4994.
