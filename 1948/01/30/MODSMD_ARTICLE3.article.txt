# Scripts Needed For Spring Show
## 
### 
Original scripts for the Spring
Show are due by March 1, an
nounces Betty Lou Berzon, drama
publicity director.
Roy Poole will direct the musical,
which will be given on May 14 and
15. A committee to select the show
has been appointed with Poole as a
member.
Synopses of plots will be accepted,
but complete scripts and music are
desired, Miss Berzon said. They
may be turned in to Paul Baker in
the Dramatic Manager's office in
the Old Union.
