# Sunday Deadline For Table Tennis
## 
### 
Living groups that have entered
teams in the intramural table tennis
tournament will have until Sunday
to finish their first-round matches,
according to Chuck Taylor, intra
mural director.
The final date for these matches
was shifted by Taylor to allow more
teams to remain in the competition.
Intramural bowling is proceeding
at a satisfactory rate, Taylor an
nounced. The teams are meeting
their schedules every week, but
Taylor urged them to keep on time.
