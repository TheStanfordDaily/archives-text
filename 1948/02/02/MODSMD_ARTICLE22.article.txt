# Sign Up for Yosemite; Ski Club to Hold Meet
## 
### 
Sign-ups for Ski Club members
wishing to go to Yosemite this week
end will begin at 9 o'clock this
morning in the ASSU office, accord
ing to Joanne Whittier, vice-presi
dent.

The club will hold its regular
meeting Wednesday evening at 7:15
in Cubberley auditorium. Movies
will be shown, and all members are
urged to attend.
