# Dancing to Be Taught At Hoedowner Meeting
## 
### 
Hoedowners will hold their regu
lar teaching session tonight at 7:30
in the women's gym. Mrs. Miriam
Lidster and the old Hoedowners
will be the instructors.
Dances which will be taught are
the Danish Schottische, Cuckoo

Waltz, Liltle Man in a Fix, Kalian
Quadrille, Road to the Isles, and
the Hopak.
Everyone interested in folk danc
ing is urged to attend. There is a
need for girls because there have
been more'boys than girls at recent
meetings.
