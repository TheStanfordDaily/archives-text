# SDA Will Hold First Meet Thursday Night
## 
### 
The first all-campus meeting of
Students for Democratic Action will
be held Thursday night at 7:30 in
the Women's Clubhouse, according
to Charles Woodard, chairman pro
tem ol the Stanford chapter.
The meeting will be devoted to
the formulation of policy, the elec
tion ol officers, and the drawing up
of by-laws. All those who are in
terested in working for an effective
national, liberal student organiza
tion are invited to attend.
