# SIRO to Arrange? Boxes for Europe
## 
### 
SIRO will hold a packaging parly
af its Village office in Building 1558
on Saturday morning from 9 to 11,
stated Burt Wilner, SIRO co-chair
man.
The new Village office will serve
as headquarters for the packaging
of food and clothing to be sent
to needy persons abroad, he said.
However, some wrapping will still
be done at the shack by Encina
Commons for those who cannot get.
to the Village. The headquarters
has been moved to the Village due
to the larger facilities available
there, he announced.
Office hours for the Vilage office
are 12:45 to 1:45 p.m., Monday
through Friday, and 9 to 11 Satur
day morning. Hours for the SIRO
shack remain 12:30 to 1:30 p.m.,
Monday through Friday, and 9 to 11
a.m. Saturday.
