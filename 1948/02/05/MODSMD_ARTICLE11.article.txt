# Casa Ventura Elects Spring Term Officers
## 
### 
New Casa Ventura officers were
announced Tuesday by outgoing
house president Ki Morris.
The new officers are Carolyn Spi
cer, house president; Joanne Har
per, vice-president; Carolyn Morri
son, secretary-treasurer; and social
chairman, Theo McGuire.
Outgoing officers include Ki Mor
ris, president; Carolyn Spicer, vice
president; Vclma Prosser, secrctary
trcasurer, and Margaret Vanden
burg, social chairman. Theo Mc-
Guire was temporary chairman be
fore elections.
