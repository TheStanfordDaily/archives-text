# BARRETT SIGNS
## 
### 
BOSTON, Feb. 4 (UP) — After
completing the purchase of a home
| in suburban Newton, Charlie (Ked)
Barrett dropped in at Braves Field
I today and signed his 1948 contract
with the Boston Braves.
Long a resident of North Holly
wood. Calif., Barrett has been with
i the Braves for the past two years.
Ilis last year's record was 11 wins, 12
| losses for an earned run average
of 3.54.
