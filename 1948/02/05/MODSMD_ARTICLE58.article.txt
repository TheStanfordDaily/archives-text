# Four Farm Journalists Join Sigma Delta Chi
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Chi, professional
journalistic fraternity, will hold an
informal party at Belt's Friday from
3 to 4:30 p.m.
At a meeting with the San Fran
cisco professional chapter at the
San Francisco Press Club the fol
lowing new members were initiated:
Lorry Lokey, Bob Bullock, and
Wells Keddie. undergraduates; and
Tom Lanphier, alumnus.
