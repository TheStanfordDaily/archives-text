# Library to Have Section on Skiing
## 
### 
A section on skiing will soon be
added to stacks in the main library,
according to Lloyd Seymore, Ski
Club president. Thirty dollars'
worth of books are now on order.
"Anybody who has any books on
1 skiing which would be beneficial to
| everyone, and which are not old or
: dated, that he would like to add to
| this collection, is urged to do so,"
stated Seymore.
Books on skiing areas, exercises,
I what to do when in trouble, and
i skiing safety will be included in
! the section. Several of the books
will be pictorial.
Up to this time five books written
in German have been the only
source of information on skiing in
I the library.
