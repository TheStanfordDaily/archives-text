# Disability Pay Bill Restriction Sought
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 8 (UP),—
The House Armed Services Com
mittee is drafting legislation to stop
Army and- Navy officers from draw
ing tax-free "disability" retirement
pay when they are still healthy
enough to hold down lucrative jobs
in private business, it was disclosed
today.
