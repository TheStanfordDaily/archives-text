# Branner Chooses Lewis President
## 
### 
Mel Lewis was elected president
of Branner Hall Thursday; Kate Pe
terson was chosen first vice-presi
dent; and Jenny Lee, second vice
president.
Pat Peterson and Sally Tymstra
were chosen secretary and treasur
er, respectively. Mary Holdeness is
the new Women's Conference rep
resentative. Those elected to the
Committee of Five are Pat Penny,
Marion Baker. Joan Harris, Connie
Fish, and Doris Cooper. Jaqui At
kin is editor of the Branner Banner.
