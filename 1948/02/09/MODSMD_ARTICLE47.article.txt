# Row Open Dances Host 200 Couples
## 
### 
A near-capacity crowd of Stan
ford couples trekked up the Row
Saturday night to attend the first
in a series of fraternity-sponsored
open dances.
From 9 to 1 some 200 Farm
couples danced and partied at Delta
Tau Delta, Sigma Nu. Theta Xi, and
Phi Gamma Delta fraternities.
The second four-hour record and
refreshment session, scheduled for
this Saturday, will be sponsored by
Alpha Tau Omega, Delta Chi, and
Theta Delta Chi.
"Plans are tremendous," com
mented Jim Welch, Interfraternity
Council president. "The fraternities
are guaranteeing a really fine time,
so get your date now."
