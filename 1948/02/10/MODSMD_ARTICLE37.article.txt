# 'Hearts in Waltz-Time' Branner Dance Theme
## 
### 
"Two Hearts in Waltz-Time" will
be the theme of the Branner winter
formal on Saturday from 9 p.m. to
1 a.m. A 1 Capelli's orchestra will
furnish music for the dance, which
i will cost $2 per couple.
! Tickets for the dance will go on sale
in the ASSU office Wednesday.
