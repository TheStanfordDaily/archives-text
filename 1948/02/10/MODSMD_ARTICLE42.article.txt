! gram, broadcasts tonight from the
I Sigma Chi house. Bob Gettys and
Roy Roberts, emcees, will interview
i Fred Boensch, Brad Lundborg,
house president, and Ben Stillwell,
; son of the late General Stillwell.
Musical entertainment will be by
! Don O'Neill, Bob Connolly, a quar
tet. and a house rendition of "The
Sweetheart of Sigma Chi." A Candy
! Quiz will close the show.
