# Circle 'S' Society Meets Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford's honorary minor sports
society, the Circle "S" Society, will
hold its first meeting tonight at 7:30
in the Nitery, alter nearly five years
of inactivity.
The society, which has been in
active since 1943, was organized by
Harry Maloney, Stanford's former
boxing coach.
Heading the activation of the
Circle "S" Society are Winston
Mumby, one of the Cards' out
standing wrestlers, and Kim Fletch
er.
