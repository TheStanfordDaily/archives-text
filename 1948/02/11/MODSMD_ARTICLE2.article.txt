# Flyers Will Meet For Quad Photo
## 
### 
Stanford Flying Club members
will meet in the third floor confer
ence room of the Women's Clubhouse
tonight at 7:30 to have Quad photos
taken and to see several Army Air
Corps movies.
Club President Walt Lull said the
club will decide on a Saturday after
noon date for a mass plane flight to
the Oakland Airport.
Pilots will be taken through the
Transocean Airlines terminal, the
airport flying schools, and one of the
large, four-motored DC-4's based at
the field.
Club meetings and memberships
are open to Farm students interest
ed in flying, Lull said.
