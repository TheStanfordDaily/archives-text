# Searsville Rises During Last Storm
## 
### 
Ernest M. Brandsten, advisory di
rector of water sports, reported yes
terday from Searsville Lake that
there was a total of 1-48 inches of
rain recorded during last week's
storm.
"The barometer is at present
dropping slowly," he said, "and if
Weather Bureau reports material
ize, we will have rain tomorrow
morning."
Brandsten mentioned that the
1.48 inches resulted in a four-inch
rise of the lake itself, and that these
four inches represent approximately
one-half million gallons of water.
