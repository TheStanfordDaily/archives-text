# Committee Appointments, Adoption Of 1948 Program Slated by SDA
## 
### 
SDA (Students for Democratic Action) will meet tomorrow night at 8 in
the lower lounge of the Women's Clubhouse to adopt a program for the
year, and to appoint planning and special committees, announced chairman
pro-tem Charles Woodard.
Committees will meet this quarter to plan a full scale SDA program for

spring quarter, member Preston
Burchard said. Committees will be:
Program and Activities, Member
ship. Public Relations, Students'
Rights, and Nominations.
