# Branner to Hold Valentine Formal
## 
### 
Branner Hall's winter formal will
feature A 1 Capelli's orchestra this
Saturday from 5) to 1. "Two Hearts
in Waltz Time" is the theme for the
event, which will be held in the
Branner lobby.
Tickets are $2 per couple and are
on sale in Branner and in the ASSU
office, Old Union.
The dance decorations, under the
direction of Marian Stolz. will be
silhouettes and valentine sayings in
blue and silver in the lobby, and in
red and white in the arcades.
