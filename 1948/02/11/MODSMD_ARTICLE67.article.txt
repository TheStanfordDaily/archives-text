# Swordsmen Meet SF State Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford swordsmen will meet the
San Francisco State fencing team in
a 25-bout match to be held at 8 p.m.
tomorrow in the Encina Gym. Five
men will be entered by each team,
and every Stanford foiler will tan

gle with each one of the visitors.
Coach Elwyn Bugge announced
that one point will be given for each
bout won. The team winning 13 out
of the possible 25 points wins the
match.
