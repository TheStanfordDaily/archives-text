# King, Queen to Feature Formal Mardi Gras Ball
## 
### 
Lagunita Court's winter formal,
the Mardi Gras Ball, will feature the
music of Lee Scott and his orchestra
who will play from 9 to 1 Friday
night.
A king and queen will be chosen
by a ticket-number drawing. There
will be further intermission enter
tainment and refreshments.
Tickets are on sale before and
after lunch and after dinner this
week in the Lagunita lobby.
