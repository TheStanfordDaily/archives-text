# YEAGER TURNS DOWN OFFER
## 
### 
BOULDER, Colo., Feb. 11 (UP) —
Jim Yeager, former head football
coach at Colorado University, today
turned down an offer to become head
football coach and director of ath
letics at Nebraska University.
