# Stanford Club Corner
## Stanford Village 
### 
Dr. Harold A. Ehrensperger, na
tional secretary of student work for
the Methodist Church, will be the
main speaker at an open forum in
the Village Auditorium Sunday at
7:45 p.m. This forum is sponsored
by the International Club, Stanford
Wesley Foundation, and Stanford
Village.
Sunday evening's talk will be on
the theme "The Student's World."
During the 1946-47 school year,
Dr. Ehrensperger lectured in India
and China and conferred with na
tional and university leaders in both
nations.
