# JACKIE SIGNS CONTRACT
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Feb. 12 (UP)—Jackie
Robinson, first negro to play major
league baseball observed the birth
day of Abraham Lincoln today by
signing a "very pleasing" 1948
Brooklyn Dodger contract.
