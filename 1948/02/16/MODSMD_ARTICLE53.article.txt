# Players to Offer Moliere Comedy
## 
### 
Tryouts for Moliere's comedy,
"The Miser," will continue this af
ternoon from 4 to 6 in the Little
Theater.
Roles will be east for eight men
and five women, and will be open
to students outside the Speech and
Drama department.
Several members of the cast will
be required to rehearse during the
latter part of winter quarter al
though the play will not begin its
Little Theater run until April 14,
according to director Hal Todd. Full
rehearsals will start spring quarter.
