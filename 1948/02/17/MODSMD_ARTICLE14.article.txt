# Friday First Day For Holiday Leaves
## 
### 
Vacation for Washington's Birth
day for girls with no Saturday
classes starts Friday after classes,
and continues until Monday at 10:30
p.m., according to Women's Council.
Those who have Saturday classes
but are excused by the instructor
may also leave Friday.
For those girls remaining in then
residences there will be non-quota
l:3o's Friday through Sunday. Girls
must sign out for non-quota late
leaves.
