# PLANES COLLIDE OVER BAY
## 
### 
HAMILTON FIELD. Feb. 18 (UP)
—An Air Force Reserve pilot was
killed today when his AT-6 crashed
in San Francisco Bay after colliding
with another training plane while
' ving in low-altitude formation.
