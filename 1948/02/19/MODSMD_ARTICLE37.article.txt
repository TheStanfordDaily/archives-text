# Cardinals Issue Call For Campus Guides
## 
### 
Cardinals yesterday issued a
call for campus tour guides. All
students interested in this work
should sign up at the ASSU office
today or tomorrow.
Bob Levison, Cardinals chair
man, explained that campus tours
are arranged with various high
schools and take place nearly ev
ery week. More than IGO high
school students toured the cam
pus last week alone, and students
to help in this work are urgently
needed.
Circle "S" Society conducts
campus tours for athletes inter
ested in Stanford.
