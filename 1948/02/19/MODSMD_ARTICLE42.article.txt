# Administration Dinner Held by Eating Clubs
## 
### 
Stanford's eating clubs held their
annual administration dinner last
night at Encina Commons.
Mr. and Mrs. Thomas Carpenter,
Dr. and Mrs. Alfred H. Grommon,
Mrs. Eliene J. Sumner. Dr. and Mrs.
Lawrence Kimpton, Mr. and Mrs. B.
Frank Gillette, Miss Catherine
Smith. Miss Elva Brown, Mr. and
Mrs. Alfred Brandin, Dr. and Mrs.
H. Donald Winbigler, and Mr. and
Mrs. Shelton Beatty were guests of
honor.
