# Jay Hughes Wins In Encina Election
## 
### 
Jay Hughes defeated two oppo
nents to become president of En
cina Hall in last night's election.
A tie in the preference system
voting for the office of vice-presi
dent will necessitate a run-off bal
loting between George Strieker and
Zeppelin Wong. The date for this
election will be set within a week,
Lawrie Gordon, election committee
chairman, stated.
According to the election commit
tee, such an occurrence in prefer
ence voting has never before oc
curred at Encina.
Approximately 62 per cent of the
residents cast ballots.
