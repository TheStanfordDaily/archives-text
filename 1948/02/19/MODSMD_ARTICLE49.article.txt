# Author Speaks Tonight On U.S. Foreign Policy
## 
### 
Dr. A. J. Muste, national secre
tary of the Fellowship of Recon
ciliation. will speak this evening in
Palo Alto on "An Approach to
American Foreign Policy."
He is to speak at the social room
of the Methodist Church. Hamilton
and Webster Street, at 8 p.m. The
public is invited.
Dr. Muste is the former principal
of the Labor College and minister of
the Labor Temple in New York. He
is the author of "Non-Violence in an
Aggressive World," and "Not by
Might."
