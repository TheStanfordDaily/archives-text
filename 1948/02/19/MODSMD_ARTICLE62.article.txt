# Card Cagers Leave Today
## 
### 
The Stanford varsity basketball
squad will board the noon Daylight
today at 12:17 heading for Los An
geles and another weekend series
with USC and UCLA.
The first game Friday night will
be played against UCLA as it was
last week. George Stanich, Bruin
center, who has been bothered by a
bad wrist, is reported to be fully
recovered and ready to give Ste
phenson a good battle at the pivot
spot.
Of the two. the USC game figures
to be the tougher assignment, and
the Cards will have to find some
way to out-rebound the Trojans be
fore they will be able to improve
upon the outcome of the last two
games.
