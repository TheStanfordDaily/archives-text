# Folk Dancers Twirl In Village Aud Tonight
## 
### 
Folk dancing classes, sponsored
by the Village Chistian Association
J and open to the campus, will be
: given again tonight from 8 to 10 at
the Village Auditorium.
Dance students will receive in
struction in the Black Hawk Waltz,
the Hambo. and the Cotton-Eye Joe,
; among others, lan Murray, publicity
director, said.
