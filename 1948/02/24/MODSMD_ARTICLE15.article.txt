# Farm Will Vote WCC Resolutions
## 
### 
Stanford students will vote to
morrow on the resolutions adopted
last fall by the Western College
Congress, said B. J. Denning, one
of the Stanford delegates to the
congress.
The resolutions were posted on
Quad bulletin boards and in living
groups.
Booths will be set up for off-cam
pus and Village students on the His
tory corner and in front of the Cel
lar. Campus living groups will vote
in their halls.
