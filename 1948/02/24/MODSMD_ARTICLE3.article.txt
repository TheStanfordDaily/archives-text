# Stanford Red Cross Donations Due Today
##

in all drive contributions to the
ASSU office today. Diana Powers,
drive chairman, announced.

of Farm donations to the Red
Cross, she said, the committee
will not be able to determine
whether to extend the drive into
a second week.
