# One Day Delay In WCC Voting
## 
### 
Voting on the Western College
Congress resoltions will be delayed
; until tomorrow, B. J. Fitger. con
gress representative on campus,
stated yesterday.
Students living on campus and in
the Village will vote in their dining
i halls at noon. Off-campus students
will vote at the History corner from
8 a.m. to 3 p.m. and at the Cellar
1 from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Students will be asked to fill out
a two-page questionnaire accepting
or rejecting the Congress resolu
tions. said Miss Denning.
A series of articles describing the
| resolutions appeared in The Daily
two weeks ago. and a set of the
resolutions adopted has been sent to
each living group, according to Miss
Dennings.
