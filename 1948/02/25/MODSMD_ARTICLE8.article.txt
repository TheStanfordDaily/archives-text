# Yell Tryoutees Vie for Spring Ballot Places
## 
### 
Six candidates for yell leader po
sitions on spring quarter ballots will
be chosen this afternoon, according
to Jack Davies, head yell leader.
All tryoutees for one of the six
positions must come to the Rally
Committee office in the Union from
3 to 5 p.m. today.
Final selection will be made by
a committee of Tom Martzloff, AS
SU president; Barbara Carter and
Dave Heyler, co-chairmen of Rally
Corn; Bob Levison, member of
Cardinals; and Davies.
