# World Government Subject of Debate
## 
### 
Three Stanford debaters, Dick
Kolton, Robert Bennett, and Ronald
Klein, will compete with students
from 15 or 20 other schools today at
Linfield College, McMinnville, Ore
gon.
Subject for the debate is federal
world government. Speakers will
not know which side they are going
to debate until just before argu
ment begins. They are prepared to
speak either for or against world
government.
Stanford's representatives left on
Tuesday for Oregon.
