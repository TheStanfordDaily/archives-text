# WILL PLAY HERE TONIGHT
## 
### 

PATRICIA TRAVERS, pretty 20-year-old concert violinist, will
make her initial Stanford appearance tonight at 8:15 in the Memorial
Auditorium. Tickets for the program will be on sale today from 1 to
5 p.m. in the Concert Series office of the Nitery and will be sold tonight
at the box office, said Barbara Aitken, Concert Series manager. Price
of the tickets ranges from $1.80 to $3.
