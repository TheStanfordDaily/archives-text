# Stanford Press Pushes California Travelogues
## 
### 
Stanford University Press has
gone in for travel folders this year.
They are now publishing 100.000
copies of "How to See and Enjoy
California," for the Greyhound Bus
Company and the American Auto
mobile Association.
They are also publishing "Califor
nia: A Literary Traveler's Guide,"
which is a pamphlet mapped out for
the interest of the "literary travel
er" in California.
