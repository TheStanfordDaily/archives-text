# Sadies Foot the Bill For Aimers Saturday
## 
### 
'"Sadies, better huriv up and
get your Abners' tickets!" is the
advice of Bob Levison. chairman
of the Sadie Hawkins Day dance
to be held in the Village Gym
from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. Saturday.
Tickets are on sale for $1.80 at
the ASSU office.
Stanfordites, turned Dogpatch
hicks for the night, will swing to
music by Bob Emerson and his
orchestra and down doughnuts
and coffee during intermission.
Prize?, will be awarded to the
most authentically dressed Dog
patch folk.
