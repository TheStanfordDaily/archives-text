# Cal Cage Tickets Will Sell Monday
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stanford-
California basketball games at Ber
keley on Friday, March 5, and at
Stanford on Saturday, March 6, will
go on sale at the athletic office on
campus Monday from 8:30 a.m. to
4:30 p.m.
Only those students who did not
get tickets for the first California
game here may purchase tickets for
the March 6 game Monday. On
Tuesday morning the sale will be
opened to all students, regardless of
whether or not they bought tickets
for the first game.
Price is 50 cents. Student body
cards must be presented, but not
more than two may be presented by
any one student.
