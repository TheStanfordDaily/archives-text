# 'Stanford Speaks' On India's Hopes
## 
### 
Three Stanford students, each a
member of a major Indian sect, will
discuss the problems of India over
San Jose Radio Station KEEN at 1
p.m. Sunday. Theirs will be the
third of the current events series
"Stanford Speaks."
Waquar Ahmed, a Moslem from
Pakistan; Vishwa Gopal Jhingram,
a Hindu from India; and Rajendra
Vikramsing, a Sikh from India, will
present their viewpoints on the
hopes and problems of India.
Dr. Frederic Spiegelberg, an in
structor in Asiatic and Slavic lan
guages at Stanford, will also par
ticipate in the discussion.
