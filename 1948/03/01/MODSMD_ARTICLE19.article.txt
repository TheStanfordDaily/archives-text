# Weeden's Photo Wins First Prize in Exhibit
## 
### 
"Upper Merced River," a photo
graph by Don Weeden. won first place
in the popularity contest in the Art
Gallery's student photography ex
hibit last week. Weeden received a
two dollar prize.
About 500 votes were cast by vis
itors to the exhibit. The winning
picture was not among the winners
picked bv the Camera Club.
Weeden's photograph pictured a
deer on the river bank. In the fore
ground were dark shadows of tan
gled underbrush.
