# SPRING SHOW SCRIPTS
## 
### 
Scripts for the Spring Show,
Ram's Head production, must be
turned in no later than noon today
in room 13 of the Old Union, an
nounced Paul Baker, dramatic man
ager.
If authors do not have scripts
completed, a scenario with repre
sentative dialogue and songs will be
accepted. The prize-winning script
and its author will be announced
spring quarter.
The Dramatists' Alliance of Stan
ford has awarded a $15 prize to
Robert Kelly, author of "The De
ceivers," one of the three one-act
plays recently presented by Ram's
Head, announced Allen Fletcher,
general director of the show.
