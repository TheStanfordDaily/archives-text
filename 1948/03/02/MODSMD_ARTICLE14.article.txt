# YWCA Will Hold Vocational Talks
## 
### 
To help Stanford women in their
vocation choice, the YWCA will
present today two women outstand
ing in the vocational guidance field.
Miss Ruth Peabody and Miss Laura
Ault.
Miss Peabody, well known for her
vocational guidance and religious
education work, will speak on ''How
to Choose a Profession" at 3:30 this
afternoon in the "Y" rooms.
Miss Ault, National YWCA Per
sonnel Secretary, will interview
students interested in professional
YWCA service in the "Y" rooms
from 11 a.m. to 4 p.m.
