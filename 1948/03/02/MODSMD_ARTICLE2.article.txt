# Seniors Promote Ice Cream Sale
## 
### 
Senior class members will hold
their annual campus-wide ice cream
sale Thursday from 3:30 to 10:30 p.
m.. said Bob Coate, class president.
Jeeps will make deliveries to the
different living groups.
There will be a meeting today
at 5:15 p.m. in the Woman's Club
house of all seniors interested in
helping with the sale. Coate urged
everyone to come so plans may be
completed.
