## 
### 
ATHENS, GREECE. Mar. 1 (UP)
-Sixty sailors have been arrested
in an effort to crush a "large-scale"
communist plot to organize mutiny
in the Greek navy, the Navy Min
istry announced today.
Four government troops were re
ported injured by rocks. The alleged
mutineers attempted to surround

open fire, the Ministry said.

story printed last week by the right
