# Folk Dancing Open To Men Students
## 
### 
Advanced folk dancing will be
open to men spring quarter. This
course is not listed under men's
physical education in the Time
Schedule.
Mrs. Miriam Lidster will teach the
course, which will include dances
which have not been previously
taught in beginning classes of Hoe
downers. Dances will include the
Amanor Waltz, Joropo, La Bamba,
Ranchera, Chiapanecas, Russian
Peasant Dance, Scotch Sword Dance,
and others.
