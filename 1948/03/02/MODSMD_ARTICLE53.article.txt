# FEHRING ARRIVES
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES. March 1 (UP).—
William (Dutch; Fehring, recently
appointed assistant football coach at
University of California at Los An
geles, arrived today from the Uni
versity of Oklahoma where he spent
the last two seasons as line coach.
Fehring is a graduate of Purdue
and spent nine years there on the
coaching staff.
