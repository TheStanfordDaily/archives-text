# Frosh Nine Wins 6 to 3
## 
### 
With pitchers Lloyd Westphal and
Denny Tuffanelli limiting Jefferson
i High of Daly City to six hits, the
Stanford Freshman baseballers post
ed their third straight victory by
winning 6 to 3 on the Frosh Diamond
yesterday afternoon.
Lefty Lloyd got off to a shaky start
in the first inning when the Jeffs
pushed across two runs on two hits
: and a pair of walks. Lloyd then set
tled down and allowed only one more
hit in his four inning stint.
A walk to Bob Ames, an error,
a wild pitch, and Larry Fink's single
tied it up in the bottom half of the
first. Hits by Tuffanelli and Sal Co
letto in the third put the Frosh in
the lead and the same two figured
in the three run game-clinching ral
ly in the fifth.
Notes—Tuffanelli's three singles
boosted his season total to eleven
for team leadership . . . second base
man Ken La Crosse's over the shoul
der catch in the second was the field
ing play of the game ... an attempt
ed squeeze play by the Jeffs was
! turned into a double play to end a
Jefferson threat . . . next game is
scheduled for Thursday with the
Frosh playing host to Lincoln High
of San Jose in the quarter's last
j game.
