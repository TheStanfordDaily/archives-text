# Lutz to Head Professors' Association
## 
### 
Dr. Ralph H. Lutz, professor of
history and dean of graduate study
at Stanford, has been elected presi
dent of the American Association of
University Professors.
The association, which has a
branch at Stanford, closed its con
vention at St. Louis Saturday.
Dr. Lutz has been a member of
the Stanford faculty since 1920, and
from 1940 to 19*14 he was director
of the Hoover Library.
Between the years 1944 and 1946
he was a member of the National
Council of the association.
Other officers elected at the con
vention include:
John A. Kinneman of Illinois
State Normal College, first vice
president. and Marie J. Weiss of
Tulane University, New Orleans,
second vice-president.


DR. RALPH 11. LUTZ
