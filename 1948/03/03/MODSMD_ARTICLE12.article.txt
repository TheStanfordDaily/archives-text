# Palo Alto Holds Inflation Forum
## 
### 
The question "How Can We Whip
Inflation?" will be answered from
Democratic and Republican view
points tonight at 7:45 in the Chil
dren's Theater of the Palo Alto
Community Center.
The forum, sponsored by the Palo
Alto Unified School District, adult
education department, will feature
George E. Outland, former U.S.
Congressman, speaking for the Dem
ocratic solution. Templeton Peck, of
the editorial staff of the San Fran
cisco Chronicle, will uphold the Re
publican viewpoint.
