# AAUP Will Honor Lutz with Dinner
## 
### 
A dinner meeting of the Stanford
chapter of the American Association
of University Professors will be held
at the Stanford Union on Thursday,
March 11, at 1:15 p.m.
The dinner is given in honor of
Dean Ralph H. Lutz. who was re
cently elected national president of
the AAUP.
Dinner reservations may be ob
tained by phoning Local 558 or Lo
cal 436 before Monday noon.
