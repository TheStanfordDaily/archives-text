# Flyers Hear Talk on 'Copters
## 
### 
Helicopter flight and construction
will be described tonight at the
quarter's last meeting of the Stan
ford Flying Club in the upper
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse
at 7:30.
A representative from the Stanley
Hiller Jr. 'copter plant in Palo Alto
will give the illustrated talk. Club
president Walt Lull said the pilots
also will discuss plans for another
cross-country flight during the first
weeks of next quarter.
Tonight's meeting is open to all
students and faculty members.
