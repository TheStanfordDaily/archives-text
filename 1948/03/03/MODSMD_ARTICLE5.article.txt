# Debaters Discuss Politics Tonight
## 
### 
Politics will be the keynote of the
Stanford Debating Society when
they meet tonight at 7:30 in the
Cubberley Auditorium, room 35.
The subject of the debate will be:
"Resolved, that this house believes
the Republican party has more to
offer the American people than the
Democratic party."
Speaking for the Republican party
will be Forden Athearn and Thomas
Brand, while Arthur Herzog and
George Velliotse will defend the
Democratic viewpoint. This is the
final debate of the quarter.
