# Stanford Ski Team In Yosemite Meet
## 
### 
This weekend finds Stanford's ski
team competing in the PCISU meet
at Badger Pass, Yosemite. Captain
Jim Brehler will field a squad in
cluding Don Williams, Raleigh An
drews, Bartow Fite, Dave Taylor,
and Bill and Tito Sigal.
The meet opens Friday with the
cross-country event, while compe
tition in jumping, slalom, and down
hill will follow on Saturday and
Sunday.
