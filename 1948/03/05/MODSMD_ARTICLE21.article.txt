# KSU STAFF PARTY
## 
### 
KSU will hold a staff party at
L'Omelette from 10 to 2 tomorrow
night. All present and past staff
members are invited.
The program will include dancing
and refreshments. The affair is ei
ther date or stag and all people
seeking rides should meet at the
station after the basketball game to
morrow evening.
KSU will remain on the air from
10 to 10:30 p.m. during Dead Week.
