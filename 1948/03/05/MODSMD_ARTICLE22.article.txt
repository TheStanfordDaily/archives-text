# Stanford Players To Go on Air
## 
### 
Members of the Stanford Players
will present a radio dramatization
of Norman Corwin's "Savage En
counter" over Radio Station KVSM
in San Mateo, Sunday at 1:30.
Stanford Players who participate
in this program will be guest artists
on the Veterans' Theater of the Air
program which is regularly sched
uled for that time.
Members of the cast are Roy Poole,
Jules Irving, John Hume, Allen
Fletcher, Harold Todd. Priscilla
Pointer, and the director, Betty Mc-
Gee.
