# San Jose Thespians Will Perform Here
## 
### 
San Jose State College will move
its entire cast of "Medea," by Eu
ripides, to the Stanford Little The
ater for three special performances
April 1, 2, and 3 at 8:15 p.m.
The San Jose production will be
directed by Dr. John Clancy, who
received the Ph.D. degree from the
Stanford speech and drama depart
ment.
Tickets may be purchased for 60
cents in room 202, Memorial Hall, or
may be reserved by phoning Palo
Alto 22041.
