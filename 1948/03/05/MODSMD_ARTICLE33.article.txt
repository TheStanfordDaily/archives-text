# Vucinich to Offer New Islam Course
## 
### 
Dr. Wayne Vucinich will offer a
new course, "The Islamic World,"
beginning next quarter, it was an
nounced yesterday by the history
department. The three-unit course,
meeting Monday, Wednesday, and
Friday, will be given at 10 a.m.
The course will be a survey of the
Islamic world since 1800, and will
cover an account of the rise of Arab
nationalism and the Arab League,
the position of the Islamic states and
communities in Africa, the Balkans,
the Near East, the Soviet Union, and
Central and Southeast Asia.
