# Committee Asks Help on Publicity
## 
### 
Students interested in working on
publicity for the foreign scholar
ships plan, in connection with the
campus-wide referendum to be held
soon, should contact the Foreign
Scholarship Committee according to
Bob Coate, committee member.
The committee will maintain a
desk in the lIR office this week.
The Daily will feature a series of
articles, pro and con, on the pro
posed plan later in the week.
Members of the committee are
Hardin Holmes, B. J. Denning, Jim
Welch, Jose de Larios, Joe Babich,
Otakar Fischer, Bob Coate, Chuck
Martin, and Dean Lawrence H.
Kimpton.
