# J.O. Perrine Will Speak On Radar
## 
### 
Dr, J. O. Perrine. assistant vice
president of the American Tele
phone and Telegraph Company, will
lecture on radar and microwaves to
night at 7:30 in Memorial Audito
rium.
He will discuss the application of
radar and microwave developments
to peacetime uses and will perform
many short-wave ''tricks." He will
use them to light a fluorescent lamp
held in mid-air with no connecting
wires, bounce them off metallic sur
faces, and "bend"' them around cor
ners.
The talk is sponsored by the joint
Stanford chapter of the Institute of
Radio Engineers and the American
Institute of Electrical Engineers.
