# Stanford Pair Reach College Tennis Finals
## 
### 
Stanford's tennis partnership of
Fred and Bob Lewis advanced to
the semifinals of doubles play in the
Northern California Intercollegiate
Tennis Tournament at the Berkeley
Tennis Club this weekend.
In singles competition, Stanford
hopes fell early in the going as the
powerful USF net team swept into
to finals. The Dons placed Ilarry
Likas and Conway Catton in yes
terday's battle for the singles crown.
TWO GO THE LIMIT
Hie Lewis brothers advanced to
the doubles finals Saturday by win
ning two grueling three-set matches
in succession. In the quarterfinals,
the Stanford pair dropped the first
set to Cal'< n and Don Lowenbein of
USF, but rallied to win the match,
4-6, 6-3. 1-4.
COP'" duo of Hank Pfister and
Don Hrmilton extended Fred and
Bob to another three-set match be
fore the Indians won. 6-4, 2-6, 6-3.
As a result, the Lewis brothers
entered the championship round
against Harry Roche and Harry Li
kas of the top-flight USF team.
LEWIS DUO BOPS CAL
Earlier in the match play, the
Lewis pair defeated California's
doubles team of Grenfell and Goss
ler by a score of 10-8, 6-2.
Stanford's singles players did not
fare as well in the rugged toulna
ment. Dick Mechem survived the
first round by tripping Roy Oakes of
Santa Clara, 6-4, 6-8, (5-3, but the
Card netter fell before Hank Pfister
of COP in the second pairing, 6-2,
6-3.
MILLER ELIMINATED
Grover Miller of the Farm team
also lost out in the second round
by dropping the decision to J.
Woolfon of San Francisco State,
6-3, 6-2.
Third round competition elimi
nated Fred Lewis from the singles
play as he lost to Kon McCarthy of
Freno, 6-3, 6-2. Fred had already
disposed of Don Lowenbein of USF,
6-4, 6-4, in a second round upset.
THIRD ROUND JINX
Bob Lewis won his first round
match from Leonard Warren of
California, 10-8, 6-0, and then added
a 6-0, 6-1 victory over A. Stewart
of Fresno. But Bob dropped out in
the third round as another USF
star, George Kraft, chalked up a
6-2, 6-4 victory.
Miller and Mechem dropped their
first round doubles match to Collins
and Arbuckle of COP. 6-4, 6-1.
