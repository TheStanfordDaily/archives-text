# Press Publishes Five Centennial Hooks
## 
### 
In order to aid tourists in their
enjoyment of the three-year Cali
fornia Centennial celebration, the
Stanford University Press is pub
lishing several new books, accord
ing to Donald P. Bean, director of
the Press.
A thumbnail sketch of the scenic
highlights of California is to be
found in James McClure's forth

coming book "California Land
marks," which will be a companion
volume to the new edition of "His
toric Spots in California."
The Press has also prepared a
"Literary Traveler's Guide," which
is a descriptive catalog of old. new,
and forthcoming books on Califor
nia and the West.
