# Concert Series Opens in Belmont
## 
### 
Notre Dame Music Association in
Belmont will present three concerts
this spring. Proceeds from the series
will go to a building fund for a new
College of Notre Dame auditorium.
Richard Dyer-Bennett, the "20th
century minstrel," will open the se
ries Friday. The second program on
April 8 will feature a joint recital
1 by Teresita and Emilio Osta, Latin
American dancer and pianist. Eula
Beal, noted young contralto, will
present the final program May 20.
Tickets for the concerts will go
on sale today at the Peninsula Box
Office in Palo Alto.
