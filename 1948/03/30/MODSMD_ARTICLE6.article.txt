# Dr. E. Shultz Returns From Eastern Meetings
## 
### 
Dr. Edwin H. Shultz. executive
head of the department of bacter
iology. has returned to campus from
a series of scientific meetings in
the East. He attended meetings of
the American Board of Pathology
and the American Association of
Pathologists and Bacteriologists in
Philadelphia, and of the Federation
of American Societies for Experi
mental Biology in Atlantic City.
