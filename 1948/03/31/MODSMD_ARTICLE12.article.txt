# Student-Faculty Art on Display
## 
### 
An annual exhibit of work done
by students and faculty of the Stan
ford art department will open to-*
day at the Art Gallery, Dr. Hay
N. Faulkner, head of the depart
ment, announced.
The show, which will continue
through April 18. contains examples
of the best work done by Stanford
students this year in oil and water
color painting, wood sculpture,
chalk drawing, wood block printing,
architecture, and house design.
A special showing of the exhibit
will be given tomorrow at 7:30 p.m.
for art majors and art faculty. Cof
fee and doughnuts will be served.
