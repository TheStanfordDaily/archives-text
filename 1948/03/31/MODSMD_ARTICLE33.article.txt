# Pianist Brereton S.F. Guest Artist
## 
### 
Robert Brereton, young American
pianist, will be guest artist with the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra
in the Opera House Thursday night,
Friday afternoon, and Saturday
night.
Brereton's number with the or
chestra will be Beethoven's Con
certo No. 3 in C Minor, Op. 37. The
world premiere of San Francisco's
Frederick Jacobi's "Symphony in
C" will also be presented along with
"Variations on a Theme" by Haydn
and "Enigma Variations"
