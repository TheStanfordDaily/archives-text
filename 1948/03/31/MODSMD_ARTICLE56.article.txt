# Whitaker Named Council Chairman
## 
### 
Dr. Douglas M. Whitaker. acting
vice-president of the University and
dean of the School of Biological Sci
ences. has been appointed chairman
of a National Research Council
committee.
The committee will award ap
proximately 100 Atomic Energy
Commission pre - doctoral fellow
ships in biological sciences to train
men for research in biology, bio
physics. and the pre-medical sci
ences.
Fellowships will be drawn from a
$250,000,000 fund established by the
U.S. Atomic Energy Commission.
