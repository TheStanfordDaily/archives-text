# 7,575 Enroll For Quarter
## 
### 
One hundred and thirty-two stu
dents registered yesterday to bring
the total enrollment in the Univer
sity to 7,575 on the third day of
spring quarter.
On the same day last year, 6,950
students were registered.
Fifty per cent of the spring en
rollment consists of veterans, ac
cording to the accounting depart
ment. Vets numbered 3,774 last
Tuesday, and several more regis
tered yesterday. Veterans enrolled
in the medical school in San Fran
cisco are not included in this figure.
Slightly more than 4,000 vets were
enrolled winter quarter.
Broken down, yesterday's figures
showed that 5,771 men and 1,864
women were attending classes.
