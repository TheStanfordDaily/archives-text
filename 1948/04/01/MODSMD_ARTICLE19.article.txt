# Spring Show Tryouts Planned for Next Week
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Spring Show will
be held Monday and Tuesday, Roy
Poole announced yesterday. Try
outees will meet in the Ram's Hear!
room from 7:30 to 10 p.m. on Mon
day and from 3 until 5 p.m. and 7:30
to 10 p.m. on Tuesday.
"We need anybody and every
body." Poole said, "and especially
actors, singers, dancers, and crew
members. No experience is neces
sary in previous Stanford shows,
and new talent is especially
sought."
