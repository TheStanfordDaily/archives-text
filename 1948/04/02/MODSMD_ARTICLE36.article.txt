# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
NEWMAN CLUB will hold a pic
nic this Sunday at Big Basin. All
Catholic students are invited. Cars
will leave St. Thomas Aquinas
Church parish hall between 10:30
and 11 a.m. Lunches and trans
portation will be furnished by the
club.
ALL KUGBY PLAYERS who want
to attend the annual banquet to
be held at Moflett Field Wednes
day night should sign up on the
bulletin board in the gym by
Monday.
SIGMA DELTA CHI will meet this
afternoon at 2 in the Journalism
library.
URANDEIS CLUB will meet Mon
day at 8 p.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse.
CHINESE CLUB will meet tonight
at 7:30 at the Chinese Clubhouse.
528 Salavtierra.
VILLAGE HOUSES are reminded
to hold elections for Village
Council representatives before
Monday night.
WESTERN COLLEGE CONFER
ENCE planning committee will
meet at 10 tomorrow morning at
the YWCA to tabulate the results
of balloting.
BEETHOVEN CHORUS will re
hearse at the Knoll today at 5
p.m., Sunday at 4 p.m., and Mon
day at 7 p.m. Tenors will meet at
3:30 p.m. Sunday.
