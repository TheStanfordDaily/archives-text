# NSA Travel Pamphlet Goes on Sale Today
## 
### 
One hundred fifty more copies
of "Study, Work, Travel Abroad
—Summer 1948." the booklet pub
lished by NSA for students who
plan to go abroad this summer,
have been received.
Since 110 of these have been
signed up for. 40 will go on pub
lic sale today from 9:30 to 12 a.m.
and from 1 to 2 p.m. in front of
the Cellar.
In case it rains, the pamphlets
will be sold in the ASSU office.
The price is 10 cents.
