# Pre-Reg Chairmen Interviews Begin
## 
### 
Selection of chairmen for the 1948
freshman pre-reg program will be
made this week. Interviews of all
candidates will be held in the AS
SU president's office from 2 to 3 p.m.
today and from 3 to 4 p.m. tomor
row.
All interested men and women
are urged to turn out. No previous
experience is required of the candi
dates, but a high degree of interest
is vital.
The pre-reg program is designed
to orient freshmen each quarter to
Stanford life. Chairmen meet the
incoming frosh several days before
the regular quarter begins.
Students unable to appear for in
terviews at today's or tomorrow's
hours may make other arrange
ments by calling the ASSU office.
