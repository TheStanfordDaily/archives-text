# Cal Pair Injured In Fall at Dance
## 
### 
Jny E. Richards, 21, University of
California student, is still in critical
condition in a Berkeley hospital af
ter he and his dancing partner,
Phoebe R. Nichols, 19, San Fran
cisco, fell 15 feet through open
French doors at a fraternity dance
Friday night.
Richards, a Kappa Sigma, suffered
a fractured skull from the fall and
Miss Richards was reported out of
danger last night with a fractured
collar bone.
The two dancers were attending
a dance at the Zeta Psi fraternity
house when they whirled through
the doors to a concrete stairway be
low.
