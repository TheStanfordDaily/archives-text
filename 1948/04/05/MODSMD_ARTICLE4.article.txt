# Board, Room Bills Due by Saturday
## 
### 
Saturday is the deadline for
payment of hoard and room hills
for all University residences, ac
cording to an announcement made
yesterday hy Controller Duncan I.
IVfcFadden.
Campus residents should pay
their hill, at the cashier's office
in the Administration Building-
Payments at the Village may he
made at the newly opened cash
ier's office in the lobby of the Vil
lage administration building. The
Village office will he open from
9:30 a.m. until noon and from 1
to 3:30 p.m. during the week. On
Saturdays it will be open from
9:30 to 11 a.m.
