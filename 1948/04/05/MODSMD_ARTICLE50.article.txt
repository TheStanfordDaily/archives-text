# Tickets Sell For Players' Spring Events
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale for the
Stanford Players' spring produc
tions. Chester W. Barker, secretary i
of the speech and drama depart- j
ment, announced last week. Tickets |
will be sold in room 202 of the Me- j
morial Auditorium.
Spring events will be Moliere's j
"The Miser" in the Little Theater on
April 15, 16, and 17; Sir James ]
Barrie's "Dear Brutus" in Memorial
Auditorium on April 29, 30, and May
1; and Benjamin Britten's modern
opera "Peter Grimes" in Memorial
Auditorium on May 27, 28, and 29.
All seats for "The Miser" cost GO
cents, for "Dear Brutus" 90 cents,
and for "Peter Grimes" $1.20.
