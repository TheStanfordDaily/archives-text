# Oarsmen Prep For Cal, UCLA
## 
### 
Stanford's crew operations were
stamped "urgent" last week as ihe
oarsmen buckled down to their final
month of pre-season workouts.
Back in (he harness after a short
between - terms layoff, the Card
crews settled down to a rigorous
training program that includes a
minimum daily eight-mile pull. In
laying out the month's plans Clark
scheduled two weeks of intensive
endurance and power runs, includ
ing four-hour Sunday stints, a week
of gradually increased speed runs,
and a final week of racing strokes
and starts.
On May 1 the varsity, jayvee. and
frosh eights will journey to UCLA
to meet the Bruins and San Diego
State in a 2,000-meter affair.
The following week the big tiff
with California will be run off on
the Oakland estuary with San Diego
again competing. Good showings in
these meets might lead to Stanford
entries in the Portland Rose Festi
val on June 10, or possibly Pough
keepsie later in June.
