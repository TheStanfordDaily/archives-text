# ASSU Officers Tell Spring Office Hours
## 
### 
Student officers will hold regular
office hours during the quarter to
discuss problems of student govern- !
ment.
ASSU President Tom Martzloff
will hold office hours in the presi- i
dent's office, Old Union, from 1 to
2 p.m. Monday through Friday.
Vice - President Guyla Runyan's
hours are Tuesday and Thursday
from 11 to 12 a.m., and Wednesday
from 1 to 2 p.m. in the same place.
Ken Croft, off - campus Excom
representative, will be available
Thursday from 10 to 11 a.m. and
Friday from 11 to 12 a.m. He may
also be contacted any time through j
the ASSU office.
