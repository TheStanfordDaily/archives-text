# Spring Show Tryouts Will Be Held Today at 3
## 
### 
Final tryouts for the annual
Spring Show will be held today
from 3 to 5 p.m. and 7:30 t6 10 p.m.
in the Ram's Head room in the rear
of Memorial Hall, said Roy Poole.
Spring Show director, yesterday.
Actors, singers, dancers, and crew
members are needed. All partici
pants will receive points toward
membership in Ram's Head. Stan
ford's student dramatic society.
