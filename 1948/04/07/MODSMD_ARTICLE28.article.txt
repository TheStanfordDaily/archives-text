# Flyers to Plan Spring Agenda
## 
### 
"Where to fly this quarter" will
ie the question for Stanford Flying
Club members tonight when they
meet at 7:30 in the Women's Club
house.
Club president Walt Lull said
spring quarter flight programs will
be drawn up on the basis of how
much money the majority of pilots
can afford to spend.
Spring quarter flying in the past
has seen the club make several trips
to Santa Cruz and Hollister.
Lull said a student will offer
memberships in a Farm glider club
to interested flyers. The glider will
be maintained on a co-operative
cost plan.
Stanford Flying Club member
ships arc open for $1 per quarter
to any student or faculty member
interested in flying.
