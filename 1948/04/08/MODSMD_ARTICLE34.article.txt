# Theta Sigma Phi Will Initiate Six
## 
### 
Initiation for six new members of
Theta Sigma Phi, national honorary
women's journalistic fraternity, will
be held this afternoon at 5 at Hurl
burt House.
Initiates include Shirley Rogers.
Lila Spitzer, Sandra Meitus, Mabel
Adkison, Agnes Bard, and Mrs.
Rosemary Cameron.
At a dinner meeting following the
initiation, members will hear talks
by Miss Elinore Cogswell, editor of
the Palo Alto Times; Mr. and Mrs.
Frank Taylor, well - known local
writers; and Mrs. Chilton R. Bush.
