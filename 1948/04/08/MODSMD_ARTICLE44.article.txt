# Roble Sells Socks For Relief Drive
## 
### 
Argyle and plain - colored socks
manufactured on the Farm by Roble
women will be sold in Roble lobby
between 1 and 5:30 p.m., said Janice
Williams, chairman of the sock sale.
There will be assorted sizes for
both men and women. They will
sell for $5 a pair. Miss Williams asks
that people come early before sizes
and styles are depleted.
The sock sale will begin the Roble
relief drive, which is under the di
rection of Ann Elliott. All proceeds
from the sale will go to the Euro
pean scholarship fund.
