# ROTC Fraternity Pledges New Men
## 
### 
Scabbard and Blade, a fraternity
organized to promote excellence in
ROTC work, initiated 11 new mem
bers yesterday as part of Stanford's
ROTC Army Day parade.
New members are:
Ted Burke, Len Hoar. Louis Law
rence. Chuck Moore, Dan Motrin,
Al Patterson. Bill Perkins, Martin
Priester, Del Russel, Orin Rush, and
Wallace Stenson.
In addition 13 veterans received
War Theater medals in recognition
of the World War II service.
