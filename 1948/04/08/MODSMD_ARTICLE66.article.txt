# Students Asked To Register Cars
## 
### 
All new students are requested to
register their automobiles immedi
ately to avoid payment of a late reg
istration fee. Chief of Police Gordon
Davis announced. Old students,
whose stickers have come off. are
requested to come in for replace
ments.
Traffic violation fines are now
payable at the police office in the
Corporation Yard, instead of at the
cashier's office in the Administra
tion Building.
