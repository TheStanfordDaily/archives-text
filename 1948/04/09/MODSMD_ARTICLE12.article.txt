# ‘Y’ Interviews Girls To Fill Summer Posts
## 
### 
The YWCA will hold interviews
this afternoon for girls interested in
holding offices in the "Y" next quar
ter. Interviews will be held on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house from 1 to 3.
Girls will be interviewed for both
elective and appointive positions,
said Betsy Wilson, chairman of the
nominating committee.
