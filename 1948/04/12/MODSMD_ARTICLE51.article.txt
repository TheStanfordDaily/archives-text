# Vern Jones Gets Wrestling Title
## 
### 
Vern Jones, veteran wrestler, and
at present the Farm wrestling coach,
became the Pacific Coast champ in
the 147-pound class in the matches
held over the weekend.
To earn his title, Jones defeated
Southworth of Oregon State and
Lauson of San Jose State, both by
decisions.
A short time ago, the Stanford
matman won a place to compete in
the National Olympic Trials in
Ames, lowa. He will represent Dis
trict 15 in the trials, which will be
held in late April.
While competing for Stanford,
Jones lost only one match all sea
son, the one against Cal in which
he was injured.
