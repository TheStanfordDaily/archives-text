# Varsity, Jayvees Win Golf Matches
## 
### 
In their first regular match of the
'48 season, the varsity golf team
downed a Stanford alumni squad,
to 10' j, Saturday on the Stan
lord course, where the Cards will
host the NCAA golf championships
in late June.
Playing for the alumni were such
well-known and capable golfers as
Warren Berl, George Traphagen,
and Bud Edwards. The team meets
San Jose Friday.
