# Farm, Cal Debate 3rd Party Issue
## 
### 
The question of desirability of a
third party in American politics will
be argued tonight at 7:30 by debaters
from Stanford and the University of
California.
George Velliotes will be the
chairman of the debate to be held
in Cubberley Auditorium in which
David Leavitt and Bob Stanley of
Stanford's debating society will
speak in favor of third parties.
At the same time the issue, '"Re
solved, that a third party is de
sirable in American politics" will
be debated at the University of
California.
Ronald Klein and Bob Bennett,
also members of Stanford's debating
society will defend the negative
viewpoint at Berkeley.
