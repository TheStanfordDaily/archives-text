# NSA Publication Copies Available
## 
### 
The National Students' Association
(NSA) has secured a limited num
ber of copies ot" its national publi
cation, "Student Leadership and
Government in Higher Education,"
for the edification of all interested
candidates participating in the com
ing campus elections.
The 23-page booklets are now on
sale at the ASSU office, Old Union,
for five cents.
Divided into sections on Student
Leadership, Student Government,
and Resources Material, the booklet
assumes eight basic principles and
describes the following general
forms of student government: com
munity government, council or or
ganizational representation, and the
student senate or legislature (the
type used at Stanford).
