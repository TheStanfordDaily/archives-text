# Primary Voters To Register Here
## 
### 
NSA will register Stanford voters
for the coming state Presidential
primaries, it was stated yesterday.
Registration will take place on
campus Tuesday and Wednesday at
the Engineering corner from 9:30
a.m. until noon, and from 1 to 4
p.m., and in Stanford Village lobby
on Tuesday and Wednesday eve
nings from 6:15 to 8:30.
To register either on campus or
in the Village, students must not
be receiving any financial support
from home, NSA stressed. Students
must also be able to meet residence
requirements.
Since Stanford is in Santa Clara
County and the Village in San Ma
foo Countv. students must register
in the county in which they live.
