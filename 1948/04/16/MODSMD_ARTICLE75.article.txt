# Riding Club Holds Tryouts Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tryouts for Kiowas. bareback rid
ing club, will be held from 10 to
12 tomorrow morning at the Red
Barn. President Bob McCann has
announced.
Prospective members must be able
to put the horse through three gaits
including walking, trotting, and
cantering bareback. They must be
able to get oft' and on bareback and
to jump the horse two feet.
Dues for the club are $5 per quar
ter. Members may rent horses for
$1.50
