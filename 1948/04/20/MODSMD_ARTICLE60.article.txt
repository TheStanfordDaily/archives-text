# Stanford Mothers To See Two Plays
## 
### 
"Questioning Fate" and "A Wed
ding Morning," two plays by
Schnitzler, will be presented at the
meeting of the Stanford Mothers'
Ciub this afternoon at 2 in the
Women's Clubhouse.
Under the direction of F. Cowles
Strickland, associate professor of
speech and drama, the plays will
feature three junior artists in resi
dance, Frances Waller, Richard
Hawkins, and Walter Witcover.
Nancy Mac Naught and Robert R.
Brown, advanced students in acting
will direct under Professor Strick
land. who will speak in the inter
mission and introduce the perform
ers.
Officers for the coming term will
be elected at the meeting, and tea
will be served.
