# Dick Kelton Wins Oratory Tournevy
## 
### 
Dick Kelton won the Stanford
division of the Hearst Newspaper
Oratory Contest in the Ben Fran
klin Oratory Tournament. John
Hines, assistant debate manager,
announced.
On April 29 at 8 p.m. he will
compete with orators from 20 Bay
Area schools at the University of
San Francisco auditorium.
The Bay Area division winner
will be awarded a $200 prize and
will compete in the state division.
The society will debate "Military
Men Should Not Hold Public Of
fice" tonight at 7:30 in room 35 of
Cubberley Hall. Ernie Schmidt and
Henry Moffatt will speak. John
Hines will be the chairman.
