# Engineers Offered Two Scholarships
## 
### 
Two scholarships in civil engi
neering are available to students
who have been regularly registered
at Stanford for a year, according to
Professor E. L. Grant, head of the
department of civil engineering.
The scholarships amount to S3OO
and $400 each, and are available to
students who will have completed
90 units of work at the end of
spring quarter, have a C average,
and intend to follow civil engineer
ing as a life work.
Application forms may be ob
tained in room 266 and should be
turned in by May 1.
