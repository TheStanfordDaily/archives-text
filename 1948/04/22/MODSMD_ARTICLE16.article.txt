# Guthrie House Invites All Campus to Formal
## 
### 
All the campus is invited to Guth
rie House tomorrow night for the
house formal. Bert Whiting will
play from 10 to 1.
While dress is formal for house
members, guests are free to dress
semi-formally. Barbara Bradley an
nounced. Decoration theme is "The
Land of Oz."
