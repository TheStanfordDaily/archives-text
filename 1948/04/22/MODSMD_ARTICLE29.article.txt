# Warren Announces Approval of Budget
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO, April 21 (UP).—
Gov. Earl Warren today announced
final approval of a $903,261,010 state
budget for the 1948-49 fiscal year—
the largest in the state's history.
Warren announced he cut $36,-
557,500 from the budget and special
appropriation bills passed by the
Legislature. He said the cuts were
made to get conformity between
state spending and expected in
come.
