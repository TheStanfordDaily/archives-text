CONVALESCENT HOME chairmen
will meet today at 5 p.m. in the
Nitery.
CALIFORNIA NEWMAN CLUB
dinner attendants should sign up
tonight at the club meeting. Cars
will leave the parish hall Sunday
at 4 p.m. for all who are going
to the dinner.
NEWMAN CLUB will meet tonight
at 7:30 in St. Thomas Aquinas
Hall. Transportation will be fur
nished.
GRADUATE WOMEN'S LUNCH
EON will present Mrs. Allen Taft
of the League of Women Voters
today in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse from 11.50
to 12:45. Admission is 50 cents.
lIR EXECUTIVE COUNCIL will
meet today at 4 p.m. The attend
ance of all committee heads is
requested.
CANTERBURY CLUB will have a
fireside in the Women's Club
house tomorrow between 8:30 and
12 p.m. All Episcopal students
and guests are invited.
DESERET CLUB will meet tonight
on the top floor of the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:30.
