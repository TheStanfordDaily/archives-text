# Physics, Engineering Groups to Hold Picnic
## 
### 
Faculty and students in the
physics and electrical engineering
departments will hold their annual
picnic at Flood Park Saturday at
1 p.;n.
A picnic dinner and refreshments
will be served to members of the
IRE and AIEE free. Fifty - cent
tickets for families and friends are
available at the physics department
office or from Les Roberts in the
electrical engineering department.
