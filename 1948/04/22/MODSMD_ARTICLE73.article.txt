# Friends of Music Feature Dexters
## 
### 
Betty Barbour Dexter, 'cellist,
and Benning Dexter, pianist, will
present a recital of sonatas Satur
day night at 8:15 in Cubberley Au
ditorium as part of the Friends of
Music series.
Beethoven. Brahms, and Debussy
will be the featured composers.
Tickets are now on sale at the
Memorial Hall box office. General
admission is $1.80; student tickets.
$1.
Mr. and Mrs. Dexter are both
members of the music department
faculty at San Jose State College.
