# Organist to Play Bach Music Today
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney will present
the weekly organ recital this after
noon in the Memorial Church at
4:15.
Mr. Nanney will begin his pro
gram with the Prelude and Fugue
in G Minor by Bach. He will also
present Bach's three chorale pre
ludes: "Blessed Jesus, We Are
Here." '"Come God. Creator, Holy
Ghost." and "Lord Jesus Christ,
Unto Us Turn." The Preludio from
Josef Rheinberger's Sonata No. 7
in F Minor, Grieg's "Nocturne." and
the "Carillon de Westminster" by
Louis Vierne complete the program.
