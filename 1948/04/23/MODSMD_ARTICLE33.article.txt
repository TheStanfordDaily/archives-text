# Orchids to the Women
## 
### 
A glance at the front page today might lead you to believe
that it is war time at Stanford and all the men away. Actually,
though, women just happened to make the news today.
And to all the queens who made the news—to Ginny Davis,
who spoke up for a more liberal social program for women; to
3. J. Denning, who will represent Stanford at the United Nations;
and to all the girls who were tapped by Cap and Gown—The
Daily says, "Atta girls!"-—T. M. S.
