# Spring Sing Signups Hit Record This Year
## 
### 
There are more Spring Sing sign
ups this year than ever before. Hap
Black, Spring Sing chairman, an
nounced yesterday. There is a large
turnout of novelty groups, he added.
Included in this category is a
group from Hilltop House, which
will sing "We're Just the Gals Who
Can't Say No"; the Sigma Nu
Quartet: a Chi Csi Quartet, which
will render "Honey" or a surprise
number: and several other male
quartets.
Late-signers Hurlburt and Jordan
will sing "It's a Grand Night for
Singing."
