# Sponsors May File Applications Today
## 
### 
Applications for Encina Hall and
Village sponsors for next year will
be taken at the ASSL' ollice today
until 5 p.m. Contrary to an earlier
story, eligible men for the positions
include any students with junior
class standing as of next autumn
quarter.
Sponsors receive free rooms,
and they have their choice of
roommates. The positions call for
men with leadership ability and
free time. Interviews will be held
later this week when all candi
dates have filed their applications.
