# U.S. Releases Nazi Saboteurs
## 
### 
WASHINGTON. April 2(5 (UP)
Two Nazi saboteurs sentenced to
long prison terms in 1042 have been
released from prison and returned to
Germany, the White House disclosed
today.
The men are living in the Ameri
can Occupation Zone as free men
but supervised by the American
military commander. They were
flown to Germany last Thursday in
an army transport.
