# Fall Sponsors Try Out at 3
## 
### 
Prospective sponsors for next full
will be interviewed today from 3 to
4 p.m. upstairs in the Women's Club
house, said June Selder, head of the
sponsor choosing committee.
Interviews will also be held
Thursday from 3 to 4 p.m. and Fri
day from 2 to 4 p.m.
Women are reminded to come on
the day they checked in their ap
plications. If they haven't filled out
an application, and still want to
apply, they should call Miss Selder
at PA 2-2151.
Summer sponsors may apply later
in the quarter, Miss Selder said.
