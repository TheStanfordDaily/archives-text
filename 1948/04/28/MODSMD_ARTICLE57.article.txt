i Sign-ups for ping-pong, golf, and I
! men's volleyball tournaments run ,
in conjunction with the Convalescent j
Home drive, should be turned in to |
; Chairman Tom Elke or to the ASSU
| office by Friday.
These tournaments will being '
' Monday and all finals will be held
next Thursday of Convalescent
Home Week. Participants will be
charged 25 cents apiece for each ;
I tournament they play. Proceeds will
! be donated to the fund for the Home
children. Prizes of money orders and
dinners contributed by Palo Alto
merchants will be offered to winners

of each tournament.
The ping-pong tournament will
begin Monday and players will be
contacted by the committee for game
schedules. Games will be played
before and after dinner Monday,
Tuesday, and Wednesday.
Teams composed of one man and
one woman may sign up for the golf
tournament to be played on Thurs
day morning.
Elke urged each men's living group
to enter a team in the volleyball
tournament which will begin at the
' end of this week. Games schedules
will be announced later.
