# Campus Dance
## 
### 
Tickets for the annual Convales
cent Home Dance will go on sale
today for $1.80 in campus booths
and in the large living groups, Dana
Turkington, chairman of the dance
said yesterday.
The dance, which will conclude the
Convalescent Home activities, will
be held Saturday night in the Wom
en's Gym from 9 to 1. Giant toy
animals will carry out the toyland
theme of the dance.
Bob Emerson's band, which also
played for the Sadie Hawkins dance,
will provide the music for the affair,
Miss Turkington added.
