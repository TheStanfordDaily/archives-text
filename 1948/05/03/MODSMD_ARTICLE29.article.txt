# Farm Receiving Electro Equipment
## 
### 
Stanford has received more than
$.10,000 worth of electrical and other
types of equipment from the Federal
Works Agency in recent months, the
University announced Tuesday.
The equipment includes receivers,
transmitters, loud speakers, weather
kites, radar gear, and various meters
and tools. It will be used in ad
vanced instruction and research in
electrical engineering, physics, and
chemistry.
