# Typewriter Sale For Vets Planned
## 
### 
The War Assets Administration
announced this week that new and
used standard office typewriters will
soon be available to veterans
through an "envelope sale."
Veterans who are in need of a
typewriter have 10 days in which to
mail a self-addressed envelope to
the War Assets Administration.
Each veteran who sends in an en
velope must print the words "type
writer sale" in the upper left corner
of his envelope.
All envelopes should be sent to the
War Assets Administration, Priority
Claimants Division, 33 Berry Street,
San Francisco.
Veterans whose names are drawn
will be notified by mail when to
appear to inspect the typewriters.
All sales are on a cash and carry
basis.
