A STANFORD ROUGH
STAN MARCUS
smokes
CHESTERFIELDS
He says:
"Cool and mild, yet a man's cig
arette; that's what I want. Ches
terfield fills the bill."
Chesterfield—year after year—first
choice of college men and women.
