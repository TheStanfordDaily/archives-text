# Army Pinmen Win Tourney
## 
### 
Last week five members of the
Stanford ROTC staff successfully in
vaded Camp Stoneman for the Sixth
Army's southern division bowling
championships. The ROTC keglers
defeated Camp Stoneman 2708 to
2658.
Captain Jacob Hutchinson was
the leading scorer from Stanford
with 611 pins. He was followed by
Master Sergeant Dwight Herrington,
548 pins; First Sergeant William
Terry, 520 pins; Technical Sergeant
Reuben Selleck; and Technical Ser
geant William Blackstock, 506 pins.
