# TRIESTE BORDER CONTROLS
## 
### 
TRIESTE. May 2 (UP).—Strin
gent border controls designed to
keep Yugoslav communists out or
Anglo - American occupied Trieste
over the May Day weekend will be
lifted at 5 a.m. tomorrow, Allied
authorities announced today.
