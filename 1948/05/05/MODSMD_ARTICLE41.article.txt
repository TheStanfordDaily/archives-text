# Registration Starts For Engineer Jobs
## 
### 
Application forms and instructions
for registration as a professional en
gineer or engineer-in-training are
now available in room 271, Dean
Frederick Terman's office.
Those applying as engineers must
have at least six years' experience in
engineering. Graduates of approved
engineering schools are credited with
four years' experience.
Engineering students who will
graduate before June 30, 1948, are
eligible to apply.
