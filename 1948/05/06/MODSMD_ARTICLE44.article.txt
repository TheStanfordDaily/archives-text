# Argentinian Tours U.S. Athletic Plants
## 
### 
Argentina's director of physical
education, Carlos Romero-Brest, will
be a visitor on the Stanford cam
pus tomorrow.
On a brief tour of the United
States to inspect physical education
facilities, he will visit both the men's
and women's athletic plants.
Grether Raul, Stanford student
from Argentina, will be interpreter
for the visiting Argentinian.
