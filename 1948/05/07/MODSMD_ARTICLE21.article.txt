# Journal Club hears Mine Expert Talk
## 
### 
Dr. Olaf P. Jenkins, chief of the
state division of mines will be the
speaker Monday at the weekly
meeting of the Stanford Journal
Club.
The state official will talk on "The
Program of Geological Survey in
California." The meeting will be
held in room 320. Geology Building,
at 4 p.m.
Dr. Jenkins is the son of the late
Oliver P. Jenkins, former profes
sor of physiology who came as a
faculty member when the university
was founded.
