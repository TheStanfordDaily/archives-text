# Pool Will Close If Rules Disobeyed
## 
### 
Women's pool will be closed for
recreational swimming if students
do not keep it clean, announced Mrs.
Maud Knapp, head of the women's
physical education department. The
pool is now open to women students
and their guests from 1 to 4:30 p.m.
on Sundays.
Mrs. Knapp has announnced that
the following rules must be ob
served by swimmers:
Everyone must take a shower be
fore entering the pool and after
lying in the grass.
No one may enter the pool with
a skin infection, a cold, a bandage,
or sun-tan lotion.
Street shoes may not be worn on
the cement walk.
