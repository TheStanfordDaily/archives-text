# Prize Art On Display
## 
### 
Robert Bigelow won the $100 first
prize in the annual Humanities
Prize Contest in Art. His winning
paintings are on display in the foyer
of the Art Gallery.
Each contestant submitted at least
three paintings. The paintings were
judged by Victor Arnautoff, assist
ant professor of graphic art; Spen
cer Macky, director of the Califor
nia School of Arts and Crafts; and
Ernest Hilgard, head of the psychol
ogy department.
All other entries should be picked
up on Monday or Tuesday.
The Gallery will be closed on
Wednesday, Thursday, and Friday
when the "Stanford Builds" exhibit
will be installed. This show will
open Saturday, said Mrs. Robert
Cross, associate director of the Gal
lery.
