## 6 TO 3 Bruins Top Net Team

Stanford's varsity tennis squad, I
serious and cautious in its 6 to 3
loss to UCLA Saturday, scared Herb
Flam and company out of their con
fidence.

a last, aggressive game in the cru
cial singles and doubles matches of
the day. He lost the singles match j
to Gene Garrett, 6-4, 6-8, 9-11, after
starting strong in the first set with
steady slams.
The second for Lewis
came in the number one doubles
match. Bob and Fred Lewis forced !

rett into 26 games in the last set, j
but lost, 6-4, 1-6, 12-14.

his 2-6, 2-6 los indicates. Mechem i
