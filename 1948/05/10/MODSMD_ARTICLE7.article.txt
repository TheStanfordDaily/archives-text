# It's Beach Time Saturday at Rio
## 
### 
The "'Road to Rio" will entice
juniors and seniors along its wind
ing way when the two classes join
in the junior - senior beach party
Saturday at Rio del Mar.
Swimming, eating, and just plain
loafing plus bonfires in the evening
Will make up the day's bill-of-fare.
Bids for the party, which will last
from 1 to 8 p.m., will be on sale at
the ASSU office for $1.80.
