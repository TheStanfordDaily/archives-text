# DUs Win Trophy For Canoe Skill
## 
### 
Last night Phil Lively and A 1
Laakso presented the Delta Upsilon
fraternity house with a trophy which
they won for defeating their frater
nity brothers from California in the
sport of canoe-tipping.
Thursday evening Lively and
Laakso were the victors in the intra
fraternity tussle, which was held at
the San Francisco Sportman's Show
before several hundred enthusiastic
spectators. In the best three out of
five contests, Laakso knocked one
of the Cal men out of his canoe
in the first round. The Stanford men
won the next two rounds by swamp
ing their opponents.
Tuesday and Wednesday evening's
preliminaries were held to determine
the top teams from each school.
