# Rio Beach Party Is Thrown Open
## 
### 
The Junior-Senior Beach Party,
"Road to Rio," to be held Saturday,
has been opened to the entire cam
pus because of demand according to
Hilde Warfield, pubilicty chair
man. Tickets will be sold tomorrow
through Friday for $1.80 in front of
the Cellar and at the ASSU office.
The party will last from 11 a.m.
to 8 p.m. Dinner will begin at 5:30,
thus giving everyone time to get
back to the campus for other affairs.
Refreshments will be served
throughout the afternoon.
