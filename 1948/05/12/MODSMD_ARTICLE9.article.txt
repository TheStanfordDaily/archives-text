# Larceny Issue Goes on Sale
## 
### 
Chaparral's "Larceny Issue" will
be on sale this morning from 7:45
until all copies are sold, according to
Steve Hvman, circulation manager.
"Larceny?" he said. "Yes; the
I editorial staff got the spring fever
something awful and clipped the
funniest stories, cartoons, poems,
and jokes from the other college
magazines in the country. It's your
big chance to see the nation's best
wits at work, all for two-bits."
The magazines will be sold on all
1 four corners of the Quad and in
front of the Post Office and Cellar.
