# Friends Have Job Openings
## 
### 
Charles Schweizo, western rep
resentative of the American Friends
Service Committee, will be in the
"Y" room today to meet anyone
interested in summer opportunities
with the Friends projects, according
to Stanford Council for UNESCO
and the Summer Projects Commit
tee of the YWCA.
Among projects are work camps,
housing projects, and rehabilitation
in American areas of social and in
dustrial conflict; institutes and
seminars for study of social, eco
nomic, and religious problems by
college and high school students;
and international service seminars
for the numerous foreign students
in American universities.
