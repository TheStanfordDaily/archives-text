# Frosh Netters Win Again
## 
### 
Maintaining their strong form
shown in the last two matches, the
freshmen tennis men defeated Stock
ton Junior College yesterday, 6 to 1.
The frosh won all five singles
matches, but split in the doubles.
It was their filth win out of seven
matches.
Dick Mechem, Vincent Schmie
den and John Huneke racked up
the best scores of the match. Me
chem defeated Hobart Miller at
number one, 6-3, 6-0. Schmieder
defeated Bill Cunningham at num
ber two, 6-0, 6-2. And Huneke de
feated Dare Campbell at number
four, 6-2, 6-1.
Addison Appleby, number three,
beat Jack Atwater, 3-6, 6-2, 6-0,
while Bob Sederholm, playing num
ber five, outlasted Phil Korbholz.
Sederholm won 6-2, 9-7.
In the doubles matches Phil Me
chem and Sederholm teamed up to
defeat Miller and Cunningham 6-2,
6-2.
