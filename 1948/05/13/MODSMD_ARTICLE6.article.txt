# Union, Lag Elect Officers
## 
### 
Union unit and Lagunita Court
election results were tabulated last
night.
Erna Radalj was chosen president
of Lagunita. The first vice-president
is Nancy Williams; Jody Johnson is
second vice-president. Margaret
Deane was elected chairman of the
Committee of Seven.
Derce Quackenbush, of Union
Residence, is the new president of
Union Unit. Nancy Keeney of Ma
drono was elected vice-president,
and Cynthia Stoltze. Manzanita. was
elected to the chairmanship of the
Committee of Eight.
