# "IRON CURTAIN"
## 
### 
NEW YORK, May 12 (UP)—Four
Russian composers today sought a
court injunction against the use of
their names or music in the motion
picture, "The Iron Curtain," whose
exhibition touched off a riot last
midnight in front of the Roxy Thea
ter in the Broadway theater district.
