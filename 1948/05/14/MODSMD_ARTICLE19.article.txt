# Roble Women Plan Sunday Barbecue
## 
### 
Barbecued steaks will highlight
the Roble picnic, which will be held
from 3 to 8 p.m. Sunday. The bar
becue will be served from 5:30 to
6:30 p.m.
Entertainment will include vol
leyball, ping pong and croquet in
front of Roble, card games and
dancing in Roble lobby, and soft
ball at the Women's Gymnasium.
Zelle Letts is general chairman.
