# Alumni to Hear Carillon Recital
## 
### 
James R. Lawson, carillonneur.
will present a special carillon recital
tomorrow at 12:30 p.m. in honor of
the annual Alumni Conference.
Included in the program are:
"Now Is the Month of Maying"
by Thomas Morley, "O Mistress
Mine" by William Bvrde, "Sumer Is
Icumen In," "Awake Thou Wintry
Earth" from Bach's cantata "Praised
Be the Lord," "One Morning in
May" by John Jacob Niles, "Come
Where My Love Lies Dreaming"
by Stephen Foster, "Summertime"
from "Porgy and Bess" by George
Gershwin.
"Shepherds' Dance" from Bee
thoven's Sixth Symphony, "Estre
llita" by Manuel Ponce, and "On
Hearing the First Cuckoo in Spring"
by Frederick Delius.
