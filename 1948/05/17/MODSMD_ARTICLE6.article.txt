# COUNCIL REVISION MEETING
## 
### 
The Women's Conference Coun
cil Area Revision Committee will
hold an open meeting from 4 to 5
p.m. today in the Klahova room of
the Women's Clubhouse.
"Anyone having views, pro or
con, is requested to attend." said
Mary Ellen Rice, who issued the
announcement.
Mary Ellen Harding is chairman
of the Revision Committee.
