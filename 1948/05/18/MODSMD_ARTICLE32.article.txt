# Amphitheater Site For Spring Sing
## 
### 
Dow Carpenter will be master of
ceremonies of the Spring Sing Fri
day evening in the Frost Amphi
theater. according to Hap Black and
Ann Fraser, co-chairmen of the
event.
Singing wil start at 7:15 p.m. Ann
Fraser promises unique lighting ef
fects for the show, but says there
will be no street dancing afterwards
as last year.
Cups and honorable mention will
be the only awards made.
