# Fellowship Hears Religious Lecture
## 
### 
Bill Dunlop. graduate of Princeton
Theological Seminary, will keynote
the Stanford Christian Fellowship's
observance of Christian Emphasis
Week on Stanford campus.
"What is Wrong With the
Church?" is the the theme of Dun
lop's talk which will be held at
7:30 this evening across from the
top-floor lounge of the Stanford
Women's Clubhouse.
President John Jenks has extended
a special invitation to all students
and faculty members to attend this
meeting.
