# Petty Theft Wave Hits Encina Gym
## 
### 
Encina Gym authorities re-issued
a warning to men to keep their
lockers locked. More than $200, and
personal belongings were taken from
lockers between May 1 and 10.
Most of the burglaries took place in
| the late afternoon. Other items taken
include clothes, wallets, and watches.
The highest personal loss was $75.
