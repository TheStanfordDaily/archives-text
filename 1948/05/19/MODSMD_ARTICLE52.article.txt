# Spring Sing Set Friday; 33 Groups Participate
## 
### 
With more than 6,000 persons expected to attend, 33 living
groups have been toning up their vocal chords for the singing
chanmpionship of Stanford University.
The eighth annual Spring Sing will be presented at 7:15 Fri
day night, rain or shine, in the Frost Amphitheater.
Cups will be given to the winners of the men's group, women's
group, and small unit, such as a 112

quartet.
Last year the Stanford Medical
School freshmen won the men's
group cup. Union won the women's
divisiop, and the Phi Kappa Psi
quartet walked off with the prize
for the best small unit.
THE GROUPS may sing either a
single song or a medley of not more
than three sings. Dow Carpenter
is master of ceremonies.
A list of living groups and their
order of singing has been announced
by Spring Sing co-chairmen Hap
Black and Ann Fraser.
The program is as follows:
UNION; Sigma Chi; Theta Delta
Chi; Lagunita Casas—Magnolia. Oli
vo, Naranja; Phi Sigma Kappa;
Branner; Tovon; Village 117; Roble;
Kappa Alpha; Lathrop; Storey; Rus
sell; Beta Theta Pi; Chi Psi; Ventura;
Theta Chi; Village 110; Cubberley;
Guthrie; Phi Kappa Sigma; Frosh
Med School; Lagunita Casas—Euca- .
lypto, Adelpha, Granada; Phi Delta
Theta; Roth; Durand; Village 310,
312, 314; Stanford Nurses; Sigma Nu;
Hurlburt; Jordan.
Novelty groups:
Theta Xi; Male Quartette; Hill
top; Sigma Nu Quartette; Barber
Shop; Four Kernels of Corn: Chi
Psi Quartette; Encina.
