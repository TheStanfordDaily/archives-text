# Quad Will Print 300 Extra Copies
## 
### 
Yesterday the 3.000 th 1948 Stan
ford Quad was sold. To meet the
increased demand for Quads, 300
additional copies will be printed.
They will be sold each weekday in
the ASSU office, at the Quad office,
and in front of the Cellar from 10
a.m. to noon, said Editor Bill Mc-
Pherson last night.
