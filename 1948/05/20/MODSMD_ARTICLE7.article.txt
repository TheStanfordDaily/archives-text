# LD Pre-Reg For Summer Begins Today
## 
### 
Summer pre-registration for low
er division students begins today.
Students needing seat cards
should secure their appointment
slips from the Lower Division office,
room 164 C, Administration Building,
today. Order of appointment has
been determined by a drawing sys
tem such as that used for fall quar
ter pre-registration.
Seat cards will be available to
morrow and Saturday morning.
Advisers must sign all study lists.
These, with seat cards, must be
turned in to the registration clerk.
Registration summer quarter will
be held June 17. Classes will begin
the next day.
