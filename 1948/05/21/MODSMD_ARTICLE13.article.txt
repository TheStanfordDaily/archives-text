# Lowell High Alumni To Hold Reunion
## 
### 
Alumni of Lowell High School.
San Francisco, will hold an infor
mal reunion dance at Marines Me
morial Club Ballroom, Sutter and
Mason Streets, Sunday from 8 p.m.
to 1 a.m.
Further information may be ob
tained from Bill Rogers, Building
110, Village; Don Magnin, Toyon; or
Stan Bernhard, 57 Ashbury Street,
San Francisco.
