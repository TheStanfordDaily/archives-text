# Conference Holds Treasurer Tryouts
## 
### 
Tryouts for the position of treas
urer of Women's Conference next
year will be held Monday from 3
to 5 p.m. on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse, Shirley Caul
der, present treasurer, said yester
day.
All junior and senior women are
eligible for the job. No previous
experience is necessary. Women
who are interested but who cannot
come Monday should contact Miss
Caulder at PA 8630.
The treasurer will be appointed
bv Advisory Board.
