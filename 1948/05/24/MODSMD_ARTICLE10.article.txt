# Roble Goes Hawaiian for Final Party
## 
### 
Hawaiian festivity will again reign
in the traditional Luau, Roble's
farewell dinner, on June 2, accord
ing to Janice Robertson, Roble vice
president.
The dinner will be held on the
pillow - covered front lawn, with
corrugated mats, palm sprays, and
fruit carrying out the theme. Leis

and occasional flowers will com
plete the setting.
After dinner entertainment from
each corridor will be presented in
the main lobby. At this time the
Roble scholarship will also be given.
Junior and senior sponsors will
return to Roble for the Luau.
