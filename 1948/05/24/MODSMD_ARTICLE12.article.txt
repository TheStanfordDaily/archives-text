# Interviews Begin For Wocom Post
## 
### 
Interviews for treasurer of Wom
en's Conference next year will be
held today from 3 10 5 p.m. in the
Klahoya room on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse.
Women applying must have jun
ior or senior standing. No experi
ence is necessary. The treasurer is
appointed by Advisory Board upon
recommendation of the interview
ing group, and serves one year.
