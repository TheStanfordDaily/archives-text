# Off Elected Quad Editor
## 
### 
Elected to the editorship of the
1949 Stanford Quad at the annual
election Friday was Ted Off. He has
appointed Genevieve McDuffee man
aging editor; Sally Foote business
manager, and Brad Breyman head
photographer. Other appointments
will be announced later.
A member of the photography and
editorial staff last year and man
aging editor of this year's Quad, Off
has been a member of the Quad staff
for three years.
The annual Quad banquet will be
held tonight at Dinah's Shack.
