# Wadsworth Wins KSU Grand Prize
## 
### 
Caroline Wadsworth was the re
cipient of KSU's weekend trip for
two at Carmel Wednesday as winner
of the grand prize on the station's
quiz program, "Balogh's Bonanza."
She and her guest will be flown
to Monterey where they will be
met by a station wagon from Rancho
Los Laureles, the lodge at which
they will stay.
Miss Wadsworth won the grand
prize after competing with 18 other
contestants.
"Balogh's Bonanza" will be con
tinued next week, at which time
regular prizes will be awarded.
