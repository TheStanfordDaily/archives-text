# Lost and Found Has 500 Items
## 
### 
The lost and found service at the
Firehouse now has more than 500
items registered, according to Fire
Chief John Marston.
"We have anything from shoes to
Western Civ textbooks,"' Chief
Marston said.
Anyone who has lost an article on
the campus should check with the
Firehouse to see if it has been
turned in. Marston said.
