CALIFORNIA RAILFANS' ASSO
CIATION' meets tonight at 7:45
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse.
CANDLELIGHT SERVICES will be
held tonight and June 2 at 9 p.m.
in the Memorial Church.
MEN'S GLEE CLUB members, past,
present, and prospective, will
meet today at 4:15 p.m. in the
choir room for pleasure singing.
Please bring music.
ORCHESIS will hold a meeting lor
members and apprentices at 5
p.m. in the Women's Gym.
STANFORD FLYING CELT. mem
bers will meet tonight at 8 in the
lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse for an election meet
ing. An Army Air Corps film.
"Target for Tonight," will be
shown.
INDIAN FLYERS' COOPERATIVE.
INC. will meet tonight at 7:15 in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse to pay flying bills and
formulate summer flying policy.
CAP AND GOWN wiil meet today
in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse at 5:15 p.m.
WOMEN'S CONFERENCE FASH
ION SHOW money and tickets
must be turned in to the ASSU
office by 1 p.m. today.
APPLICATIONS for the fall quar
ter reg dance must be turned in
to the ASSU office today.
'IS QUADS will be on sale tomor
row from 10 a.m. to noon in
front of the Cellar.
ENGINEERING DINNER will be
held at 6:30 p.m. tomorrow at
Eertrand's on Middlefield Road.
Those wishing to attend must sign
up on the bulletin board in the
Engineering Building.
ENGLISH REVIEW CLUB will give
a tea in the Women's Lounge to
day at 3:30 p.m. for Dr. Marjorie
Nicolson of Columbia University.
Faculty and graduates in the Eng
lish department are invited.
EL CUADRO will meet tonight at
6:30 in the Toyon social room.
UNIVERSITY CHORUS will meet
this afternoon at 4:30 at the Knol!.

meet at 4:15 with Dr. Popper at
the Lodge.

in Toyon.
