# Joseph Wins Jacoby Prize
## 
### 
Jim Joseph, a senior majoring in
journalism, lias been awarded the
annual Melville Jacoby Essay Prize
for 1948. it was announced yesterday
by Dr. Chilton R. Bush, head of the
Stanford Institute for Journalistic
Studies.
The Jacoby Prize is awarded an
nually for the best essay by a Stan
ford student relating to a political,
social, or economic subject on the
Orient. Topic for the contest this
year was "The Outlook for the Ear
ly Establishment of a Trusteeship
for Korea,"
The award was set up in mem
ory of Melville Jacoby, who was
killed in an airplane accident in
Australia early in 1942. At that time
he was Far Eastern correspondent
for Time and Life magazines.
