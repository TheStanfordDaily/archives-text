# Phillips to Head Alpha Delta Sigma
## 
### 
Alpha Delta Sigma, national pro
fessional advertising fraternity,
elected new offi ers for coming
school year yesterday. They were
Steve Phillips, this year's general
manager of KSU, as president of the
fraternity, and Lee Zeigler, promo
lion manager of The Daily, as sec
retary-treasurer of the fraternity.
The fraternity works each year on
advertising projects with the assist
ance of professional advertising
men. This year the project was an
advertising survey of Stanford, in
which more than 400 students were
interviewed.
