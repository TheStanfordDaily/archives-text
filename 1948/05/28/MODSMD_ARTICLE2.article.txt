# Kimpton Announces Scholarship Raise
## 
### 
"We're going to try to pull every
one through," Dean of Students
Lawrence N. Kimpton said last night
in speaking of the University's deci
sion to increase scholarships.
"In other words we don't want a
single person to drop out of Stan
lord because of the tuition raise,"
the dean added.
All scholarships wiil be increased
20 per cent up to $100. Also the
number of scholarships will be in
creased 20 per cent. Grants in aid
will also be increased substantially.
Shelton L. Beatty, chief counselor
for men, said he would like to hear
from students who will be hard hit
by the tuition raise. He wants to
know what measures the University
can take to help individual cases.
