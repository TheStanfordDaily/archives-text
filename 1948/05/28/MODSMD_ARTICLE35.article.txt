# Nuss Wins Scholarship
## 
### 
Sharon Nuss, next year's Concert
Series manager and head sponsor at
Koble next year, was awarded the
Women's Conference scholarship at
a Conference tea yesterday, Phyl
Sutherland, president, said.
The scholarship will be good for
one year. Money has also been giv
en by Conference to the Donald B.
Tresidder Memorial and the Mary
Yost Fund. Miss Sutherland said.
The new officers of Conference
for next year were inaugurated at
the tea, and new introduced.
The new officers are Gigi Marion,
president; Nancy McDermoth, vice
president; Marjorie Gavin, secre
tary; and Elaine Walton, treasurer.
