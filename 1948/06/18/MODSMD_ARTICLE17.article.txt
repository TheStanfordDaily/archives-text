# UC Prof to Give Talk Here Monday
## 
### 
George Stewart, author and pro
fessor at the University of Califor
nia, will give an informal talk
-Monday at 4:15 p.m. in the social
room of the School of Education.
Professor Stewart is author of
"Storm," "Fire," "Names on the
Land," and other books.
Stephen Spender, a writer of po
etry and prose, will speak on Tues
day. He has written "Poems of
Dedication,' "European Witness,"
and "Returning to Vienna 1947."
