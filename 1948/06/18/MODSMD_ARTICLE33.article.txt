# Farm Prof Flies to London
## 
### 
Dr. James Murray Luck, Stan
ford professor of biochemistry, left
by air yesterday for London, where
he will represent the National
Academy of Sciences at a Royal
Society conference on scientific in
formation June 20 to July 2.
Appointment of Dr. Luck as the
delegate of the National Academy
was made by its president, Dr. A.

N. Richards of the University of
Pennsylvania.
