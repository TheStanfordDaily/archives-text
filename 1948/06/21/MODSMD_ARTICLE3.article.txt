# 3,000 Enrolled; Winbigler Expects Late Registration
## 
### 
In figures released by the Regis
trar's Office late Friday, registration
for the Summer Quarter stands at
2,927 students. A breakdown of this
total reveals that 2,285 men have
registered, against f>42 women.
Registrar H. Donald Winbigler
stated that he expects heavy late
registration because some city school
systems did not close until Friday.
San Francisco and Beverly Hills
are in this category.
The enrollment for this Summer
Quarter is higher than that of the
1947 summer session. Last summer
totalled 2,655 students.
With registration expected to climb
over the 3,000 mark, a record attend
ance for Stanford's summer program
is assured.
