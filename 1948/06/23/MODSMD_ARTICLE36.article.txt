# Young GOP Meets Today
## 
### 
Stanford - Palo Alto Young Re
publicans will meet tonight to listen
to the proceedings of the 1948 Re
publican national convention by ra
dio and to hear Arthur Carmichael
talk on the role of the delegate at
the convention.
Mr. Carmichael is a member of
the Santa Clara County Republican
Central Committee and served as
campaign manager for Congress
man Jack Anderson in the recent
primary election. He also served
as a member of the California del
egation to a former Republican
convention.
The meeting will be held at 609
Kingsley Avenue at 7:30, and is
open to all Stanford students. Any
one desiring transportation is asked
to contact George Bond at the law
library.
