# Student to Join UN Conference
## 
### 
Howard Lockwood, 28-year-old
student of international relations at
Stanford, will attend an eight-week
United Nations internship program
at Lake Success beginning July 12,
it was announced yesterday by Dr.
Alvin C. Eurich, acting president
of the University.
Mr. Lockwood, who was selected
by a student - faculty committee,
will work without pay. He will
give personal and professional as
sistance to staff members of the
secretariat and will attend three
lectures and a seminar each week.
The program will be conducted
by the Bureau of Personnel of the
United Nations.
Forty positions were made avail
able for students from all member
nations. Five posts were open to
students from co 1 leges and univer
sities in the United States.
