# Pay Room, Board Bills This Week
## 
### 
Room and board for the summer
quarter is payable through June 30,
it was announced today by John I'.
Clarkin, assistant controller.

Students living in campus resi
dences may pay their bill at the
cashier's office, Room IGS, on the
second floor of the Administration
Building. The office is open from 8
to 12 a.m. and 1 to 4:30 p.m., Monday
through Friday.
Stanford Village residents may
pay room and board charges at the
cashier's office in the Village admin
istration building, which is open
from 10 to 11:30 a.m.
