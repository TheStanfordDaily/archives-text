# 'Grimes' Gets Radio Preview
## 
### 
KNBC's "Music for Your Varying
Moods" program will present a pre
view of the Stanford production of
the Benjamin Britten opera "Peter
Grimes," through recordings, Sun
day at 11 a.m.
The opera will be presented at
the Memorial Opera House in San
Farncisco Tuesday in a one-day
performance. It was first presented
here during spring quarter and won
praise from critics throughout the
area.
The KNBC program will be con
ducted by Irving Deakin, critic and
commentator and will include two
unfamiliar works by Britten's life
long teacher, the late Frank Bridge.
