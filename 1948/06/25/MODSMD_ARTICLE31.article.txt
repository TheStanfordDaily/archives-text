# PARAPLEGICS TO BENEFIT
## 
### 
Paralyzed veterans suffering from
a permanent and total disability
due to a spinal cord injury or dis
ease incurred in the service will
receive, under a law signed by the
President this week, federal grants
up to $10,000 for the purpose of
purchasing or remodeling homes
designed for wheel chair living, the
Veterans Administration has an
nounced.
