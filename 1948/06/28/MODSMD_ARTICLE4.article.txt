# KNBC Plans UN Program
## 
### 
The positive achievements of the
United Nations will be discussed
over KNBC Friday night at 7 on
a special program commemorating
the third anniversary of the sign
ing of the United Nations Charter.
The broadcast will be highlighted
by a transcribed speech by the late
Jan Mataryk of Czechoslovakia,
which he made before the UNCIO
in 1945.
Frank Clarvoe, editor of the San
Francisco News, will lead the dis
cussion on "The United Nations:
Three Years from San Francisco."
Included in the discussion group
will be:
Mrs. Christina Grant Harris,
member of the Hoover Institute at
Stanford and formerly with the
State Department; and Dr. H.
Frederick Peters, a professor and
author at Reed College in Portland.
