# Sunday's Sermon Topic Announced
## 
### 
Chaplain R. M. Minto will take
"One Purpose Under God" as the
topic for his sermon Sunday morn
ing in Memorial Church.
The service will begin at 11 a.m.
with an organ prelude by Herbert
B. Nanney, organist. The choir an
them for this Sunday will be Han
del's "Lord, to Thee," sung by the
University Choir.
