# Research Director Names New Aide
## 
### 
Dr. J. E. Hobson, director of the
Stanford Research Institute, this
week announced the appointment
of George Herbert, formerly of the
electrical engineering staff of Mich
igan State College, as his assistant.
The new assistant will serve as
Dr. Hobson's personal representa
tive and will co-ordinate technical
and administrative branches of the
institute.
