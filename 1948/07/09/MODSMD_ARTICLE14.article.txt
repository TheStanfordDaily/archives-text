# Chaplin Minto to Talk On Spiritual Power
## 
### 
Chaplain R. M. Minto will speak
about "The Reality of Spiritual
Power" at the service this Sunday
morning in the Memorial Church.
The service will begin with a pre
lude and Fugue by Brahms and the
Aria from the Tenth Concerto by
Handel which will be played by
Herbert B. Nanney, organist.
