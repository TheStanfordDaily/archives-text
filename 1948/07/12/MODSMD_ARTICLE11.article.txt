# Hilton Named To Quarterly
## 
### 
Professor Ronald Hilton, acting
executive head of the Romanic lan
guages department for the summer
quarter, has been named a contrib
uting editor of "Books Abroad." an
international literary quarterly.
The journal is devoted to com
ment on foreign books and is pub
lished by the University of Okla
homa Press. Its objective is to pro
mote international understanding
by the dissemination of literary in
formation.
Professor Hilton is director of the
Hispanic American Program and
editor of the reference series,
"Who's Who in Latin America."
Part IV of the series, dealing with
Brazil, is scheduled for fall publi
cation by the University Press.
