# Encina Is Host At Smoker Tonight
## 
### 
An all - campus smoker will be
featured in the lounge of Encina
Hall tomorrow night, Sam Bren
ner, president of Encina, announced
yesterday.
The program will feature a talk
by Cal Newman on the Honor Code
and a short discussion of Stanford's
student government by ASSU Pres
ident Don Davies and Vice-Presi
dent Marianna Olmstead.
Highlight of the evening will be
the showing of motion pictures of
the 1940 Rose Bowl game in which
the Stanford team, boasting Albert
and Standlee, beat the eleven from
Nebraska.
"We promise you a good evening
and hope as many as possible will
attend the smoker," Brenner said.
