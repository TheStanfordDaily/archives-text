# Personnel Board Seeks Applicants
## 
### 
"Structural, mechanical, and elec
trical engineers are needed for the
designing of new hospitals and insti
tutions made possible under the new
state building program," the State
Personnel Board announced.
Applications for electrical engi
neers at $415 a month will close on
July 31; those for structural engi
neers, also at $415 a month, will close
on August 28.
Also wanted are eye, ear. nose, and
throat doctor, a consultant in ele
mentary education, and a forest fire
dispatcher. All applicants for these
and other positions must be United
States citizens.
Further information concerning
these openings may be obtained at
the board's offices in Sacrameto, San
Francisco or Los Angeles.
