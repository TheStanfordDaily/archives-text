# Prof to Talk In Roundtable
## 
### 
Throe experts on Balkan affairs—
all from the staff of Stanford—will
attempt to answer the question "Is
There a Crack in the Iron Cur
tain?" over KNBC's "University of
Chicago Roundtable" Saturday at
1:30 p.m. PDST.
Harold H. Fisher, chairman of
the Hoover Institute and Library
at Stanford and student of Russian
affairs, will direct the discusion on
the significance of recent events in
Yugoslavia. Joining him will be
Wayne Vucinich, professor of Bal
kan history at Stanford, and Ana
tole Mazour, professor of Russian
history at Stanford and currently a
visiting professor at the University
of Chicago.
The program will originate in the
KNBC studios in San Francisco.
