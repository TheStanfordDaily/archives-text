# Open Hours Announced At Women's Gym Pool
## 
### 
The swimming pool in the Wom
en's Gym is open to faculty mem
bers, their wives and guests, mar
ried students, women students and
their guests, and men students with
women guests, an ASSU officer said
yesterday.
The pool is open Monday and
Wednesday from 7:15 to 8:15 p.m.,
and Friday from 3 to 5 p.m. It is
open Sunday from 2 to 5 p.m.
The only time the pool is open to
children is from 2 to 3 p.m. on
Sunday.
