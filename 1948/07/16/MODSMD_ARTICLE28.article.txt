# Dean Gives Key Talk at Dedication
## 
### 
J. Hugh Jackson, dean of the
Graduate School of Business and
international treasurer of Kiwanis,
was the main speaker last week at
the dedication of the Kiwanis Youth
Center Building in Soquel. His
subject was "Our American Heri
tage."
The $30,000 Youth Center Build
ing was sponsored and constructed
under the auspices of the Kiwanis
Club of Central Santa Cruz County.
It represents the first major accom
plishment of the club, which was
organized only three years ago.
Dean and Mrs. Jackson were
guests of honor at the dedication
ceremonies.
