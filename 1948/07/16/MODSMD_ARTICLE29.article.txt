# Economists Sought By Civil Service
## 
### 
A job opportunity for economists
who seek work in the U.S. Bureau
of Reclamation in California, Ari
zona, and Nevada has been an
nounced by the U.S. Civil Service
Commission.
The position is that of economist
in the bureau and offers a salary
ranging from $5,905 to $9,965.
Applicants will not be required
to take a written test but will be
rated on their experience.
Application forms and informa
tion may be obtained from the Di
rector, Twelfth U.S. Civil Service
Region, 129 Appraisers Building,
630 Sansome Street, San Francisco.
