# Harris in Capital As Govt. Adviser
## 
### 
Dr. David Harris, professor of
history, left Wednesday night for
Washington, D.C., on a mission
termed by the State Department as
technical. His flight to Europe is
connected with the administration
of Western Germany.
After five years, Dr. Harris re
signed from the State Department,
where he was associate chief of the
Division of Central European Af
fairs, to return to Stanford.
