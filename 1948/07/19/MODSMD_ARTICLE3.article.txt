# SAVE REBATE SLIPS
## 
### 
Students are urged not to throw
away their Bookstore rebate slips,
but to put them in the box by the
side door of the Bookstore.
All profits from such rebate dona
tions no toward Stanford's Four |
Feathers Relief Drive.
