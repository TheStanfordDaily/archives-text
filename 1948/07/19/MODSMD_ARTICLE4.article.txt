# Business Conference Opens Here Today
## 
### 
More than IJOO prominent Bay
Area businessmen will gather here
today for the Seventh Annual
Stanford Business Conference with
the theme ''Management's Stake in
Free Enterprise."
Senator Ralph E. Flanders of
Vermont will open the conference
this morning with a speech on
"The Engineering Approach to
Management."
Seymour E. Harris visiting pro
fessor from Harvard, will speak on
the European Recovery Program
tonight and Schuyler C. Wallace,
visiting professor from Columbia,
will address the conference on
"Elections and Long-Run Econom
ic Developments" tomorrow night.
Speeches on other aspects of the
business world will be given dur
ing both days. J. Hugh Jackson,
dean of the Graduate School of
Business, is chairman of the con
ference.
