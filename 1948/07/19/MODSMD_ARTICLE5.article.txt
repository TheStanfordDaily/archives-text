# Julamboree Set For Wednesday
## 
### 
The all - campus Julamboree,
originally planned for last Wednes
day, will be held in the Women's
Gym from 3 to 10 Wednesday eve
ning. Planned by ASSU for both stu
dents and faculty, it is an informal
affair.
The Palomanians, a Palo Alto folk
dance group, will entertain and the
swimming pool will be open.
Refreshments will be coffee, cakes,
cookies, and cokes.
