# Arson Suspected At Sunday Fire
## 
### 
Stanford firemen are keeping an
eye on sections of land near the
intersection of Page Mill and Aras
tradero Roads, which are still
smouldering after a large grass fire
Sunday, Fire Chief John Marston
reported yesterday.
Investigation into the possibility
that arsonists set the fire, and an
other a mile and a half farther
south on Page Mill Road, is also
continuing, according to Chief
Marston.
