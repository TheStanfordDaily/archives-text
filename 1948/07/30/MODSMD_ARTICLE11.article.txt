# Turkish Dinner Set for Sunday
## 
### 
The International Club is holding
a Turkish dinner on Sunday at the
home of Mrs. E. Thomas, 365 Lin
coln Avenue, Palo Alto.
The entire dinner is being pre
pared by the Turkish students at
tending Stanford, and all Stanford
students are invited. Tickets sell
for a dollar and may be obtained
by calling DAvenport 2-7H27.
Patlican Karniyarik Borek (Pey
nirli). and Baklava will be the main
dishes served. Entertainment and
games will follow the dinner.
