# Encina, Toyon Set Beach Party Date
## 
### 
A beach party for Encina and
Toyon is scheduled for Aug. 7, En
cina social committee announced
yesterday.
Encina Commons will be closed
for dinner that night and box
lunches will be issued to all those
holding seven-day meal tickets. In
addition, each seven - day ticket
holder may purchase one extra
lunch for a guest for 50 cents.
"All those planning to attend
must sign up with their wing social
chairmen by Monday night so that
the dining hall may be notified,''
the committee stated.
