# Beach Party Held At Rio del Mar
## 
### 
Encina Commons will be closed
tomorrow night for a joint En
cina-Toyon beach party at Rio
del Mar, the social committee an
nounced yesterday.
Box lunches will be provided
for all those holding seven-day
meal tickets and one extra guest
lunch may be purchased for 50
cents.
The party will feature sports
and swimming. Free liquid re
freshments will be served from
noon to midnight.
"Anyone who needs transpor
tation should see his wing social
chairman. It is possible that a
bus will be obtained if enough
people need rides," Encina social
chairman Dan Hendrickson said.
