# Psychology Grant Received by Farm
## 
### 
Stanford has received a grant of
$17,392 from the U.S. Public Health
Service for an extension of the
University's current training pro
gram in clinical psychology, Dr.
Ernest R. Hilgard, executive head
of the psychology department, an
nounced yesterday.
The new grant will enable the
department to continue during the
next academic year the expanded
clinical training program which it
began last fall as part of a national
mental health program.
Under terms of the grant, three
Stanford graduate students will be
designated by the USPHS for
training fellowships in clinical psy
chology.
The grant will also provide for
four faculty members in the Stan
ford psychology department, Dr.
Hilgard said.
