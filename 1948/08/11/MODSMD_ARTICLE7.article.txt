# Choir to Present Program of Bach
## 
### 
A program of organ and choral
music by Johann Bach will be
given in the Memorial Church to
morrow at 4:15. Herber B. Nan
ney as organist will be assisted by
the University Choir.
The program will include:
"Prelude and Fugue in B Minor";
"Sonata No. 1 in E Flat Major"; the
chorales ' Jesu. Priceless Treasure"
and "Jesu. Joy of Man's Desiring";
and the chorale - prelude "Come,
Saviour of the Gentiles."
