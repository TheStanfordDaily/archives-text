# Follow Your Hunch To the Melody Munch
## 
### 

The ASSU-sponsored, all-campus "Melody Munch"' will be held at
the Union tonight from 7:15 to 12. The free event is the final campus
function of the summer, and will feature entertainment, community
singing, dancing in the Court and the Women's Clubhouse, with plenty
of watermelon, coke, and cookies for all.
The Nitery will also be open for bridge players. Short skits will be
presented by Brownlee Corrin, baby-killing star of the Gaieties, KSU,
Union Unit, and many others. Afterward there will be a community
sing, with watermelon hcing served by the Melody Munch Committee.
The entire party will be broadcast over KSU.
