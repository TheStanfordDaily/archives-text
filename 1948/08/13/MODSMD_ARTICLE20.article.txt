# LAST SUNDAY MOVIE
## 
### 
The Sunday Evening Movies will
come to an end Sunday with the
showing of Laurel and Hardy in
"Flying Dueces."
Also on the bill are the last two
chapters of serial "Zorra's Black
Whip." Admission price is 20 cents.
The show starts at 7:30.
