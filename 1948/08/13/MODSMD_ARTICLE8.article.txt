# Campus Program Will Celebrate India's Freedom
## 
### 
A celebration commemorating the
first anniversary of Indian Independ
ence will take place in Cubberley
Auditorium at 4 o'clock Sunday.
The program will feature talks by
Dr. Harold Fisher of the Hoover In
stitute and Dr. Frederic Spiegel
berger, instructor in Asiatic and
Slavis studies. Vilya Dutt, a grad
uate student from India, will speak
on "There is an India" in contrast to
the recent lecture here about the
absence of any Indian nation.
Highlight of the afternoon will be
a performance of an Indian ballet
by Miss Medha Yodh, a graduate
student from India.
The celebration is open to the
public. Refreshments will be served.
