# Engineers Tour Electronic Labs
## 
### 
Twenty-five businessmen and en
gineers from the San Francisco Bay
Area inspected Stanford's electron
ics facilities last Tuesday.
The men are members of the
electronics section of the San Fran
cisco Chamber of Commerce. Their
visit to Stanford is one of a series
of tours being made to electronics
centers in the Bay Area.
The group was shown through
the electronics laboratory in the
electrical engineering department
and the microwave laboratory in
physics.
Members of the section heard
short addresses by Dr. Frederick E.
Terman, dean of the School of En
gineering; Dr. Edward L. Ginzton,
assistant professor of applied phys
ics; and Ralph A. Krause, director
of research at the Stanford Re-
search Institute.
