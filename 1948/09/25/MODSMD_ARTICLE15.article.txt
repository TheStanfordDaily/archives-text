# Frosh Get Priority In ROTC Courses
## 
### 
Priority for enrollment in the
ROTC program at Stanford will be
given to freshmen through Monday.
Colonel Basil H. Perry, professor of
military science and tactics, an
nounced yesterday.
The ROTC program this year in
cludes courses in Field Artillery,
Quartermaster Corps, and Air
Force. The Ordnance Corps course
has been discontinued.
All branches of the ROTC of
fer two years of elementary in
struction and two years of advanced
instruction. Upon completion of the
advanced course, graduates will be
eligible to receive commissions in
the reserve as second lieutenants.
