# Orchestra Opens Enrollment Today
## 
### 
Student musicians interested in
joining the Stanford University Or
chestra may see Dr. Leonard Rat
ner, director, in room 100. the
Knoll, today between 2 and 4 p.m.,
tomorrow between 1 and 3 p.m.,
and Thursday between 2 and 4
p.m.
First rehearsal of the orchestra
will be held Thursday at 7:15 p.m.
at the Knoll.
Orchestra plans for the coming
year include concerts autumn and
winter quarters, and participation
in the spring quarter opera produc
tion. Selected groups from the or
chestra will appear at various oth
er functions such as music depart
ment recitals and University thea
ter presentations.
