# 'The Westerner' Fills Screen Sunday Eve
## 
### 
"The Westerner," starring Gary
Cooper and Waller Brennan, will
open the annual Motion Picture
Series on Sunday night. Oct. 15. at
7:30 in the Memorial Auditorium.
"I Taw a Putty Tat," a short
comedy, will also be included in
the program. Admission is 20
cents.
