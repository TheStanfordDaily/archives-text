# Registration Day Ends with 7,963; Ratio Remains 3:1
## 
### 
Just 37 students short of last
year's total, regular registering
closed yesterday with 7,963 stu
dents enrolled in the University.
The standard 3: 1 ratio stood
slightly higher, with women total
ing 1,831 and men reaching 6,132.
Last fall 1,937 women and 6,063
men were registered.
Students either returning regis
tration books or registering today
will have to pay a $2 fine. Oct. 19
is the last day on which a student
may enroll for the autumn quarter.
