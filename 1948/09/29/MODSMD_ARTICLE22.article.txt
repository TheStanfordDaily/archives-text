# Palo Alio Filling Stations Of Probable Serious Shortage of Fuel
## 
### 
C
Palo Alto filling station operators are stretching their present supply
of gasoline in order to make it last through the month. Despite prom
ising reports from the production forecasts, operators in Palo Alto
find they are running short.
Some station operators in Palo Alto are rationing their gasoline to
five gallons per customer while others sell unlimited quantities until
they run out. Operators are also conserving their supply by closing
early on Sundays.
Most station operators indicated they were very low on ethyl.
