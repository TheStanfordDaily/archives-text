# Poetry Anthology Deadline Nearing
## 
### 
Nov. 5 will be the closing date
for the submission of manuscripts
for the Annual Anthology of Col
lege Poetry, it was announced yes
terday by the National Poetry As
sociation.
According to the association, each
effort must be on a separate sheet
and each must carry the statement.
"The verse entitled ' ' is my
own personal effort." It must also
. be signed, giving the name of the
college attended and the author's
home address.
Submissions may be sent to the
National Poetry Association, Dennis
Hartman, secretary, 3210 Selby Av
enue, Los Angeles 34.
