# Girl Scout Leadership Course Time Now Set
## 
### 
A one-unit course in Girl Scout
leadership will be conducted Wed
nesday evenings from 7:30 to 9 by
Mrs. Louise Woodward, former re
gional staff member for the Girl
Scouts. Class meetings will be held
in room 162b of Cubberley.
Any woman student interested in
group work is eligible for the
course. An additional two units
may be obtained by acting as leader
for a Girl Scout troop during the
course. It is being sponsored by the
Palo Alto Girl Seoul Council.
