# 'Othello' Tryouts Close Tomorrow
## 
### 
Students and faculty interested
in securing parts in "Othello" may
try out today from 3 to 5 and from
7 to 10 p.m. in Memorial Audito
rium. The last tryout period will
be tomorrow afternoon from 3 to 5.
Dr. A. N. Vardac, director of the
production, has announced that
many parts are open for both men
and women, and urges anyone in
terested in drama to "come on out."
