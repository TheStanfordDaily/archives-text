# Ram's Head Biz Manager Tryouts
## 
### 
Applicants for business manager
of Rani's Head, Stanford dramatic
organization, should go to room 13
in the Old Union today and Fri
day for interviews.
Kay Lloyd, present manager of
the organization, urges all students
interested in the production and
managerial side of the theater to
try out for the position, which pays
$100 per quarter.
Besides a business manager, nu
merous assistants will be chosen
with the prospect of becoming man
ager.
Applicants unable to come at the
appointed time should contact Kay
Lloyd to arrange for an interview
at some other time.
