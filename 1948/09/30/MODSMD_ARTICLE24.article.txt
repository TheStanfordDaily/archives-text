# Senior Engineers To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
All engineering majors who ex
pect to be looking for jobs within
the next 12 months in any of the
many engineering fields are invited
to attend a meeting tonight at 7
in the Cubberley Auditorium.
A representative from the Stan
ford Appointment Service will be
on hand to guide a discussion of
such pertinent topics as jobs, job
hunting, and salaries, and to an
swer any questions that may have
to do with these subjects.
The meeting, called at this time
in an effort to eliminate much of
the rush for job guidance next
June, is being sponsored by the
ASME (American Society of Me
chanical Engineers), the ASCE
(American Society of Civil Engi
neers), and the AIEE (American
Institute of Electrical Engineers).
