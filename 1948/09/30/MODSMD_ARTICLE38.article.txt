# Killing Classes Now Open at Red Barn
## 
### 
There are openings in several classes in English and Western riding
and polo, it was announced yesterday by Allan Ross, director of the
Stanford Riding Sbhool.
Classes are two hours each and meet twice weekly. Equitation fee is $45
per quarter. Casual riding fee is $2 per hour.
Information concerning classes may be obtained from Ross at the
Old Red Barn.
