# USF
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Sept. 29 (/P).
Quarterback Fred Klemenok gave
an impressive passing show as the
University of San Francisco Dons
worked out behind locked gates
today in preparation for Sunday's
grid clash with the Nevada Wolf
pack.
Klemenok. using some of the new
offensive plays given the Dons by
Coach Joe Kuharich for the Ne
vada tussle, completed nine of 11
passes for four touchdowns in a
scrimmage.
