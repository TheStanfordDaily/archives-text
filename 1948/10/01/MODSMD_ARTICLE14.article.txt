# Professor Will Attend Washington Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. Joseph M. Pettit, member of
the Stanford electrical engineering
department faculty, leaves Oct. 10
to attend a invitational
scientific meeting in Washington.
D.C. The Research Development
Board of the Army and Navy called
the meeting, which will be a com
munications symposium.
Before returning to the campus
Oct. 15, Dr. Pettit will go on to
New York in connection with Navy
research projects he is conducting
on vacuum tube development.
