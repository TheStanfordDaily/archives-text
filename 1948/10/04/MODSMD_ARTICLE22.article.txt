# Folk Dancing Enters Full Swing
## Hoedowners to Twirl At Meeting Tonight 
### 
"Cotton-Eyed Joe," "Road to the
Isles." polkas and other popular folk
dances will resound tonight from
the interior of the Women's Gym
when the Hoedowners hold their
first meeting from 7:30 to 10 p.m.
Under the direction of Miss Mar
jory Kinney, a member of the PE
staff, and older members of the Hoe
downers, students. faculty, and
staff will be taught the intricacies
of folk dancing.
An advanced class will also be
formed this year for those interested
in learning the more difficult steps.
Any new students interested in
joining the Hoedowners should at
tend this meeting.
