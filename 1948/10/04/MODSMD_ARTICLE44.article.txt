# Room, Board Bills Due Saturday A.M.
## 
### 
Room and board payments for
University residences are clue today
through Oct. 9. There will be a
fine if bills are paid late.
Cashiers' offices are located in the
Encina lobby and in the Village ad
ministration building. Encina office
will be open from 8:30 a.m. to noon
and from 1 to 4 p.m., Monday
through Friday, and from 8:30 to
11:30 a.m. Saturdays. Stanford Vil
lage office is open weekdays from
0:30 a.m. until noon and from 1 to
3:30 p.m. weekdays, and from 9:30
to 11 a.m. Saturdays.
