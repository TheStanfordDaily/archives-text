# Mural Contests Start Thursday
## 
### 
At a meeting of intramural man
agers Thursday night in the SAC,
Chuck Taylor, intramural director,
out'ined the mural plans for the
autumn quarter.
Taylor announced that sign-ups
for intramural play will end at 4
p.m. tomorrow and said that com
petition would begin on Thursday.
Football games are scheduled for
4:15 and 5:15 p.m. at the regular
intramural fields. Volleyball is
scheduled fo<- 4:15 and 5:15 p.m. at
the volleyball courts by Encina
Gym.
Bowling is to be held at the
Village at 7 to 8:30 and 8:30 to 10
p.m. The minor sports contests are
to be played at the convenience of
the opponents before a deadline set
by the intramural staff.
Changes from last year are that
in table tennis and handball four
men will comprise a team instead
of two as was the case previously.
Five men will make up a bowling
team.
