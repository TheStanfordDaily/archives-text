# Vets Get Checks Sooner This Year
## 
### 
Veterans, this quarter your sub- !
sistence checks will be arriving |
sooner, according to Congressman |
Jack Z. Anderson.
Congressman Anderson received
from Robert P. Shields, manager of
the Regional Office of the Veterans
Administration, a letter which
said:
"By Nov. 15, we expect to have
checks out to all veterans whose
complete records we have, and
whose enrollment papers are re
ceived in the Regional Office during
the month of September. For la
ter enrollments, which result in
our not receiving their paper un
til sometime in October, we expect
to have the checks out on or before
Dec. 5."
