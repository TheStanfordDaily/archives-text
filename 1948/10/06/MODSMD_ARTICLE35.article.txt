# Clinic Announces Speech Classes

###

Clinic announced yesterday that
the autumn class for stutterers will
meet today at 7:30 p.m. in room
PE 48.
The class, under the supervision

speech and drama, will provide
croup therapy, which includes
speaking experiences, exercises, and
psychotherapy. Additional atten
tion will be devoted to discussions
on the nature and kinds of treat
ment of stuttering. A short social
hour will conclude each meeting.
