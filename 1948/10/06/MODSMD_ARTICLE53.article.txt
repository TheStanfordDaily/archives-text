# Sign-up for Mural Leagues Extended
## 
### 
Chuck Taylor, intramural direc
tor, announced yesterday that the
time limit for sign-ups for intra
murals has been extended until
neon today.
Taylor also said that there will
be a meet in'- ot' intramural mana
gers in room 41 of the SAC Thurs
day night at 7:30 and emphasized
that all managers must attend.
There will be a meeting of all
those interested in officiating in
tramural football games in the in
tramural office in the SAC at 7:30.
