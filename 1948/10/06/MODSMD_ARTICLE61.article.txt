# Engineers Will Meet To Hear Dr. Hobson
## 
### 
Stanford Branch of the American
Institute of Electrical Engineers
and Institute of Radio Engineers
will hold its first meeting of the
year tonight at 7::50 in room 521.
Dr. J. E. Ho.bson, director of the
Stanford Research Institute, will
speak on "Industrial Research To
day." Dr. Hobson was named the
"Outstanding Young Electrical En
gineer of the United States" in
1940 by Eta Kappa Nu.
Plans for the year's activities will
be presented at the meeting, which

engineering.
