# Swimming Croup To Hold Tryouts
## 
### 
Women students who feel more
at home in water than on land are
asked to exhibit their swimming
form tomorrow morning at 9 before
the Women's Swimming Club. The
club tryoutees will be judged on
their execution of the basic strokes,
swimming form, diving, and endur
ance.
Club Sponsor Marion Ruch said
yesterday that both ballet and com
petitive swimmers were needed. "If
any girl is unable to appear Satur
day morning, she should contact
Mary Kennery, club president, at
Lagunita. or myself, at the Wom
en s Gym, Miss Ruch added. ''We
will arrange another time for her
appearance."
