# Career Choice Aid Open to Women
## 
### 
Plans for an expanded program
to aid women students in the choice
of vocations were begun yesterday
at a meeting of Women's Vocational
Committee.
This year's program will present
complete information for helping
women obtain jobs, as well as lec
tures on various careers, Diane
Porter, chairman, announced.
A series of talks entitled "The
Major Key" will be presented to
freshman women at Roble and
Branner during the latter part of
fall quarter, Miss Porter said.
