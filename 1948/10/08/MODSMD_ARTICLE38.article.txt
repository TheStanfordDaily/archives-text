# Train Tickets Sell For UCLA Game
## 
### 
Arrangements for tie'eets for the
special rooters' train bound for the
UCLA football game can be made at
the ASSU Office. The train will
leave the Southern Pacific depot at
7:40 p.m., Oct. 15, and will arrive in
Los Angeles Oct. 16 at 8 a.m.
Return trains will leave the fol
lowing Sunday from Los Angeles at
8:15 a.m., 12:15 p.m., and 7:55 p.m.
They will arrive in Palo Alto at 5:44
p.m., 9:20 p.m., and 7:45 a.m., re
spectively.
For round-trip tickets the coach
will cost $15.52, the Pullman $30.92
(lower). $29.34 (upper).
Round-trip tickets for one-way
Pullman and one-way coach will be
$25.64 (lower) and $24.90 (upper).
