# Depot, ASSU Office Take SP Reservations
## 
### 
Reservations for the rooters'
train to the UCLA game can be
made at the ASSU office as well
as at the depot. Southern Pacific
has announced.
The special train will leave the
SP depot at 7:40 p.m. Friday and
will arrive in Los Angeles Satur
day morning at 8 a.m.
Return trains will leave Sun
day from Los Angeles at 8:15
a.m., 12:15 p.m., and 7:55 p.m.
and will arrive in Palo Alto at 5:44
p.m., 9:20 p.m., and 7:45 a.m., re
spectively.
Round-trip tickets will cost
$15.52 for coach, $30.92 for Pull
man (lower), and $29.90 (upper).
