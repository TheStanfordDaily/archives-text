# Biz Libe Head Takes New Post
## 
### 
Edwin T. Coman Jr., director of
the Business School Library, was
installed Saturday in Santa Bar
bara as president of the California
Library Association. Mr. Coman
succeeds Howard M. Rowe, libra
rian of the Santa Barbara public
libraries.
Others from Stanford who will
attend the association meetings are
Raynard C. Swank, director of the
Stanford libraries; Miss Alice
Charlton, chief of catalog division;
Miss Hortense Berry, senior libra
rian; and Philip T. McLean, libra
rian and research consultant of the
Hoover Library.
