# Bulletin Board Rules Announced
## 
### 
The following rules apply to the
two open bulletin boards on Engi
neering corner and History corner
of the Quad:
1. All notices are to be put on
white cards, either 3x5 or 4x6
inches.
2. All notices are to be dated.
3. Notices are to be allowed on
the board for two weeks. If a per
son wishes to have a notice up longer,
the stated date must be erased and
a new date entered, or a new card
may be put up.
4. Nothing is to be put on the two
side borders and the top border.
Posters may be put on the bottom
border, since they are not to be put
on the bulletin board itself.
