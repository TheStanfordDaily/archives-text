# Knight Is Selected For Alumni Group
## 
### 
Lt. Governor Goodwin J. Knight
is among the 30 Stanford alumni
selected as members of the 1948-49
Stanford Alumni Association Ad
visory Council, it was announced
recently by William S. Kellogg,
president of the association.
The council includes outstanding
alumni representatives chosen both
in this country and abroad.
The duties of the council, Mr.
Ke'logg said, are to assist in main
taining and promoting the activities
of the Alumni Association.
