# Geographers Hold First Fall Meeting
## 
### 
The Phi Chapter of Gamma Theta
Upsilon, the national geographic fra
nity, will meet for the first, time this
quarter tomorow evening at 7:30 at
the home of Mr. and Mrs. Jack Tay
lor. 2066 Oakley, Menlo Park. It
will be a business meeting for old
members only, Secretary William
Wake announced.
Members are asked to meet at
the Engineering corner at 7:15 p.m.
for transportation. Plans for the
coming quarter will be discussed,
and refreshments will be served.
