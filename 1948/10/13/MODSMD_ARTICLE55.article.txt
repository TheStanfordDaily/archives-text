# Educator to Talk On Quaker Needs
## 
### 
Dr. Thomas E. Jones, noted edu
cator, relief worker, and author,
will speak on "Quaker Education and
Community Needs" Saturday night
at 7:30 at the Girl Scout House,
Community Center, Middlefield and
Melville Roads, Palo Alto.
Preceding Dr. Jones' talk, the
American Friends Service Commit
tee will hear Robert Plank, psychi
atric social worker, speak on "Im
pressions of Present-Day Vienna and
of Quaker Work There," following
which there will be a panel discus
sion on the value of summer work
projects in peace education. The
afternoon session begins at 4. The
public is invited to attend both ses
sions.
