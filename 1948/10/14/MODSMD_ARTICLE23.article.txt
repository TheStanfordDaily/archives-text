# Russian Revival Film To Be Shown Tonight
## 
### 
Another film in the Revival of
Early Films Series, "The End of St.
Petersburg," will be shown tonight
at 7:15, under the auspices of the
Committee on Public Exercises.
The movie, starring Vera Baraovi
skaya and Ivan Chuvelev, is open
to members of the Stanford Uni
versity Film Society. There are no
memberships available at the pres
ent time.
