# Quad to Finish Up Frosh Pics Today
## 
### 
Today is the deadline for all fresh
men to have their pictures taken
for the Quad and the Frosh Book.
Photos are being taken from 10 to
12 a.m. and 1 to 5 p.m. in the Quad
office.
Any Clas of '52 members living at
Encina. the Village, Roble, or Bran
ner, who missed their original days,
are urged to have their pictures
taken today.
Women are asked to wear white
blouses, and men should wear coats
and ties for their photographs. Cost
is set at $1.03 for the picture and $1
for the Frosh Book.
Senior pictures will be taken be
ginning tomorrow. Students should
make their appointments now.
