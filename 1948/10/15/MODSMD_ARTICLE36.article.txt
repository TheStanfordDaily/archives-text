# Faculty Warblers To Meet Monday
## 
### 
"The Barbershop Balladiers," fac
ulty men's singing group, will meet
Monday at 8 p.m. at the home of
Lewis A. Day, 1527 Waverley
Street, for singing and appropriate
refreshment.
Albert James, graduate of the
Wisconsin Conservatory of Music,
and M.B.A. from Stanford in 1947,
will direct the singing. All male
members of the faculty who like to
sing, or just listen, arc invited.
Future meetings are scheduled as
follows:
Nov. 1, home of C. Easton Roth
well, Interdale, Porto'.a Road; Nov.
15, home of Tom Spragens, 830 Es
condido Road, Stanford.
