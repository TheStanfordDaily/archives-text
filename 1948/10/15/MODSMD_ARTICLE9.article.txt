# UCLA Homecoming Starts This Morning
## 
### 
'The Injun Band in Circus
Land" starts off the biggest home
coming celebration in UCLA his
tory today in Los Angeles. The
gala festivities will continue
through Saturday night.
The weekend scheifule includes:
FRIDAY
10:30 a.m.—Rally and coronation
on the UCLA quad.
6:30 p.m.—Stanford Club pre-
UCLA game rally, buffet sup
per, dancing at the University
Club, 614 South Hope Street.
7:30 p.m.—Parade and fireworks
at open-air theater.
10:30 p.m. to 1 a.m.—Rally Dance
at Men's Gym.
SATURDAY
2:00 p.m.—Queen and winning
floats parade at Coliseum.
2:30 p.m.—Stanford-UCLA game.
After the game, fraternitic> and
After the game, fraternities
and sororities will hold open
houses.
One floater and one regular 1:30
leave must be taken by all wom
en wishing to spend Fridav and
Saturday .night., .of .the .UCLA
weekend at Los Angeles.
