# Future Scribes Hold Conference
## 
### 
Over 300 young journalists gath
ered here Saturday for the twenty
fifth annual convention of the Cen
tral Calfiornia Scholastic Press As
sociation. High schools as far north
as Chico and as far south as Fresno
were represented at the conference.
The delegates were welcomed in
the Education Auditorium at an
opening session which featured ad
dresses by Miss Jessie Smith and
Frederic O. Glover. Panel meet
ings followed, where students heard
various problems of newspaper and
yearbook publishing discussed by
leaders in the field of journalism.
After the annual dinner, dele
gates met in critical discussion
groups. A general meeting, campus
tours, and a jolly-up in the Nitery
completed the day's program.
