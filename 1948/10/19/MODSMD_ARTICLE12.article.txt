# Business Staff Workers Sought

### 
Two promotion staff members are
being sought by the Daily business
office to fill the new positions of
assistant promotion manager and
newsletter artist.
The positions were created re
cently when the promotion staff
was enlarged. The work includes
mailing of promotional letters to
potential advertisers, conducting
campus surveys, and meeting with
promotion staffs of other universi
ties.
Persons interested in the work
should contact Promotion Manager
Bob Woodward in the Daily bus
iess office between 1 and 2 p.m. to
day or tomorrow. Woodward said
that the position of assistant man
ager would eventually lead to a
promotion to the managership.
