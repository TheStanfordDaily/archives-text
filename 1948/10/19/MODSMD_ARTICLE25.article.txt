# Stanford Student Hurt by Golf Ball
## 
### 
A broken watch crystal and minor
cuts were the only damages inflicted
when a golf ball shattered Michael
E. Spiro's car window yesterday.
Spiro, a student living at the Stan
ford Firehouse, was driving along
Junipero Sena Boulevard near the
eighth hole of the Stanford Goll
Course when the ball went through
his window, Stanford police stated.
The sprayed glass cut the ieft side
of Spiro s face and neck. He was
treated and released by Palo Alto
Hospital.
