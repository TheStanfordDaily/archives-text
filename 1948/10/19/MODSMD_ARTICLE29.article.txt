# Faulkner Elected Secretary of PAA
## 
### 
Dr. Ray Faulkner, executive head
of Stanford's art department, was
elected secretary of the Pacific Arts
Association for 1949.
Notification was made this week
by retiring President Archie Wede
meyer, coordinator of the Art Cen
ter School, Los Angeles.
Pacific Arts Association is com
posed of members from 11 Western
States, Hawaii, and Alaska. The
purpose of the organization is to
promote arts in the American pub
lic schools. It is one of the four
major regional associations in the
United States to coordinate its pro
gram with that of the National Art
Education Association.
