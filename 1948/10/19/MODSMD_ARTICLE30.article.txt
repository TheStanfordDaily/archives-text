# Variety of Stock Sold by Bookstore
## 
### 
The shelves of Ihe Stanford
Bookstore are stocked with many
items other than books and school
supplies, according to Mrs. Helena
Bauchou, manager.
Besides the many Stanford pen
nants, stuffed animals, stickers, and
greeting cards, the Bookstore has
recently added Indian blankets,
new stationery, and fraternity pen
nants. Table size radios are also
available in limited numbers.
