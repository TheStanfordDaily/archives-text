# Folk Dance Group Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
Folk dance enthusiasts are invited
to join with the Village Folk Dance
group tonight at 7:30 in the Village
Auditorium for an evening of danc
ing.
The Circle Schottische. Cotton
eyed Joe, and Wooden Shoes will
all be featured dances, according to
Bill Barnhart, leader of the Village
will teach an Indian group dance,
and Barnhart said numerous square
dances would be held during the
evening.
