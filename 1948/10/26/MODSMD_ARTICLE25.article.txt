# Frosh Post Open On WAA Board
## 
### 
Freshman women who would like
to serve on the Women's Athletic
Association board should sign up
on the WAA bulletin board in the
Women's Gym lobby today and to
morrow.
Interviews for the position will
be held tomorrow, Thursday, and
Friday from 4 to 6 p.m.
The duties of the freshman rep
resentative on the WAA board are
to attend the board meeting every
Tuesday at 7 p.m., to be personnel
manager for those interested in
Field Day events, and to do general
board duty.
