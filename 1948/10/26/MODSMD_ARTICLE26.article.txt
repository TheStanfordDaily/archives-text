# 'Y' Service Group Will Meet Today
## 
### 
Any girl interested in community
service work is invited to the YW
CA meeting this afternoon, an
nounced Ship Newlin, chairman of
the Community Service Committee.
The meeting will be held at 4:15 in
the "Y" lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
The purpose of the meeting is to
acquaint girls with the opportuni
ties for service in Palo Alto com
munity agencies. Included among
these groups are the Red Cross and
the Girl Scouts. Girls may also
serve at the Community Center and
the Palo Alto Junior Museum.
