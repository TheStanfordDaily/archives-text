# Debate to View Politics Monday
## 
### 
The Stanford Debate Society will
sponsor a political debate Monday
at 8 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
Jack Marcum, debate captain,
and M. E. Harrison will support the
Dewey-Warren ticket, while John
Hines, debate manager, and George
Velliotes, Debate Club member,
will speak for Truman and Barkley.
The Wallace-Taylor ticket will be
upheld by Tom Lupher and Ernie
Schmidt.
Each speech will last 10 minutes,
and five minutes will be given for
rebuttal. At the conclusion of the
debate a shift of opinion ballot will
be taken and an open discussion
will be held.
Tom Brand will be chairman of
the debate. Everyone is invited.
