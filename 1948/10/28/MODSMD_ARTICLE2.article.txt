# Friends of Music Will Offer Sonata
## 
### 
The opening of the Chamber
Music Series, sponsored by the
Friends of Music at Stanford will
feature a violin and piano sonata
recital Friday night at 8:15 in Cub
berley Hall.
The guest artists are Frances
Wiener, violinist, and Lev Shorr,
pianist. Both musicians are from
San Francisco, and have chosen the
Schumann Sonata in A Minor,
Mozart Sonata in E Flat, and Stra
vinsky's "Duo Concertant," com
posed in 1932.
Tickets may be purchased at the
door the night of performance or
through Mrs. Russell V. Lee, at the
Peninsula Box Office. General ad
mission is $1.80; students' admis
sion, $1.
