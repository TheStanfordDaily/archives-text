# ICMA Scholarships Open fo Applicants
## 
### 
Interested students may obtain
application forms for International
Circulation Managers' Association
scholarships through the Institute
of Journalistic Studies. The appli
cations may also be had by writing
to the educational committee chair
man, H. Phelps Gates. Christian
Science Monitor, Boston, Mass., or
to Arthur Daniel, The Atlanta
Journal, Atlanta, Ga.
Applications will be received up
to May 10, and the awards will be
announced at the annual conven
tion of ICMA in June.
