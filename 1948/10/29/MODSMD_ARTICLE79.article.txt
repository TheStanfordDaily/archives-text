# Luisetti Visits Stanford Cagers; Exhibits Shots
## 
### 
Hank Luisetti. one of basketball's
al-time greats, was a guest of
Coach Everett Dean's Stanford bas
ketball team this week. The former
Stanford All - American suited up
and treated the cagers to an exhi
bition of his famous one-handed
push shot. He also demonstrated
free - throw shooting before the
combined varsity and frosh group
of some 75 players.
Luisetti will coach an amateur
cage squad this winter. He will
have men such as Babe Higgins,
Andy Wolfe. Jack Nichols, and
Frank Laney on the team: The or
ganization will be sponsored by a
San Francisco automobile agency.
