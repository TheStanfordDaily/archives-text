# Dance Concert At Tuesday Series
## 
### 
A concert of original dance
sketches by Mary Tiffany, gifted
young Los Angeles dancer, will be
presented tomorrow night at 7:30 in
Memorial Auditorium as part of the
Tuesday Evening Series.
Miss Tiffany studied choreogra
phy and dance technique under
Martha Graham, leading exponent
of modern dance in the United
States, and was later a member of
her troupe. In addition to solo en
gagements in New York, British
Columbia, and the West Coast, she
has appeared in several motion pic
tures and in the Broadway musical
comedy, "Stove-pipe Hat."
Formerly a pre-medical student
at Stanford, Miss Tiffany first be
came interested in modern dance
when she saw a Martha Graham
production at the University.
