# SF Stanford Alumni Clubs to Fete Kimpton
## 
### 
Dean of Students Lawrence
Kimpton will be honored at dinner
Wednesday by the Stanford Men's
Club and the Stanford Women's
Club of San Francisco.
The meeting will be held at the
Commissioned Officers' Club at
Hunter's Point at 6 p.m. Reserva
tions are $2.50. Stanford alumni
and their guests are welcome.
