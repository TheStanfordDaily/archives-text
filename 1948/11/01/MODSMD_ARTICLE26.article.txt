EL TOKO BULLS will hear a dis
cussion of the prevention and
cure of hoof and mouth disease
by A 1 Kinsey tonight at at the
usual time and place.
GAIETIES CHORUS will rehearse
tonight at 7:30 in the dance studio
of the Women's Gym.
EL CAMPO will meet tonight at
7:30 in the Toyon game room.
RALLY COMMITTEE will meet
tonight at 8 in the lower lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.

ALL FRESHMAN MEN meet at
Encina today, Wednesday, and
Friday at 2:30 p.m. and tomorrow
and Thusday at 3 p.m. to gather
wood for the Big Game bonfire.
SOLICITORS and office workers
are needed for the SIRO drive
to start next Monday. Office
hours are from 3:30 to 5:30 p.m.
daily in the lIR shack.
UNIVERSITY CHORUS, both men
and women, will meet today at 4
in Woodpecker Lodge.
