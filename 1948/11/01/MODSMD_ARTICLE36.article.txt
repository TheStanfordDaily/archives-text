# Branner Formal
## 
### 
The first dorm formal of the fall
quarter will be given this Saturday
by Branner Hall.
Branner girls and their guests
will dance from 9 to 1 o'clock to the
music ol Howard Tenedrie and his
Band ot Gold. Tenedrie has recent
ly concluded engagements at Rio
del Mar Country Club and at the
Hotel Utah in Salt Lake City.
According to Lorea Teaque, dec
oration chairman, the ballroom will
bo transformed into a Fathom Fan
tasy. Branner girls may purchase
bids for $2 from their corridor
salesmen.
