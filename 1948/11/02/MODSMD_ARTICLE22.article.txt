# Grads in English Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
The English Review Club, whose
members include 141 English grad
uate students currently at Stanford,
will meet tomorrow afternoon at
4 in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse, President Harry H.
Crosby announced.
Edward Glenn will speak on
"Ilobbes' Idea of Fancy."
Officers elected at a recent meet
ing were: Harry H. Crosby, presi
dent; William G. Stryker, vice
president; and David M. Kirk, sec
retary. Professor Francis R. John
son is faculty adviser for the club.
Each Monday, Crosby stated, the
club will hold luncheon meetings
in the Briggs room and will invite
a professor to lead the discussion.
