# Santa Clara County Charter
## 
### 
To the Editor:
We urge a "Yes" vote on the proposed Charter for Santa Clara County.
It will make possible a eo-ordinated, business-like administration of
county affairs, something not attainable under the present horse-and-buggy
era law.
J. E. BRENNER THOMAS S. BARCLAY
W. B. OWENS CHARLES FAIRMAN
GEORGE E. OSBORNE GRAHAM H. STUART
LOWELL TURRENTINE HARRY J. RATHBUN
CARL B. SPAETH HOLBROOK WORKING
JOHN B. HURLBUT JOSEPH S. DAVIS
M. R. KIRKWOOD J. HUGH JACKSON
M. K. BENNETT EDWIN T. COMAN, JR.
HERBERT E. DOUGALL
