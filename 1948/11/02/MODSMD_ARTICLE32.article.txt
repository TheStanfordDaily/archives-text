# Nitery Social Will Be Held
## 
### 
The first "Afternoon in the
Nitery," all-campus get-together
sponsored by the Social Committee,
will be held at the Union tomorrow
from 4 to 5:30 p.m.
Featured entertainers will include
the Chi Psi Quartet, Ray Pascoe
and his accordion, Lois Croonquist,
A 1 Rosenberg and Dick Moore and
their guitars, and Bob Setrakian
with some impersonations. Doug
Crawford, chairman, will act as
master of ceremonies. Special en
tertainment will be presented each
Wednesday.
The social committee, directed by
ASSU Vice-President Erline Ander
son, hopes to again make the Nitery
a regular student meeting place.
