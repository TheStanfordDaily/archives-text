# College Board Contest Open
## 
### 
Mademoiselle's Col'ege Board
contest deadline has been extended
to Friday for Stanford women, Miss
Anastasia Doyle, associate counsel
or for women, announced yester
day.
The contest winners will receive
a free trip to New York next June,
a job for a month on Mademoiselle,
and a salary and expenses for that
period.
To enter the contest, a woman
must be a registered student and
must submit a trial report on some
phase of campus activity to Made
moiselle.
Miss Doyle urged any women
who want to enter the contest to
apply at her office for inclusion on
a "recommended" list which she
will submit.
