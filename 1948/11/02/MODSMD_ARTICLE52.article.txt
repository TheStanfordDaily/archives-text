# Thursday Is Deadline For New Reg Photos
## 
### 
New students who did not have
their pictures taken at registra
tion time will not be allowed to
register winter quarter until they
have done so.
All such students must report
to the west entrance of the lower
lobby of Memorial Auditorium
from 9 a.m. to noon or from 1 to
5 p.m. no later than Thursday,
unless they have been given spe
cial appointments.
