# AWS Will Sponsor Yearly Nurses' Tea
## 
### 
AWS Annual Nurses' Tea will be
held tomorrow from 3 to 4:30 p.m.
in the Alumnae Room at the Wom
en's Clubhouse, Gigi Marion, AWS
Cabinet head, announced yester
day.
"AWS has invited the girls from
the Stanford Lane Hospital in San
Francisco to the tea.'' Miss Marion
explained.
All women considering nursing
careers or who are interested in
getting acquainted with the nurses
already in nursing school are in
vited to the tea.
