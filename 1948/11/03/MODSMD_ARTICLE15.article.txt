# Fall Officers Elected By Graduate Women
## 
### 
Louellen Chase was elected pres
ident of the Graduate Women's
Club at the group's first meeting
last week. Other officers elected
were:
Pauline Sergo, secretary: Mary
Ellen Rice, social chairman; Billie
Street, publicity chairman; and
Christina Fucile, treasurer.
Plans for the club this quarter
include a tea for all the graduate
women, and a fireside.
