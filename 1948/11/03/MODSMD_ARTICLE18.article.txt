# YWCA Music Hour Will Include Opera
## 
### 
A music listening hour sponsored
by the "Y" will be held at 4:15 this
afternoon in the "Y" rooms of the
Women's Clubhouse, according to
chairman Grace von der Mehden.
This week's program includes se
lections from light opera and Vic
tor Herbert melodies, Miss von der
Mehden said. The whole campus is
invited, and refreshments will be
served.
