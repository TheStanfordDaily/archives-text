# Nitery Social Planned For This Afternoon
## 
### 
The all campus social, "After
noon in the Nitery," will be held
at the Union from 4:30 to 5:30.
"Come and see good student en
tertainment, and meet lots of new
people in an informal atmosphere,"
urges Erline Anderson, head of
the Social Committee, which is
sponsoring the affair, which will
include dancing.
