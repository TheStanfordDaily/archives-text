# Lockwood Speech Featured at ‘I’ Club
## 
### 
International Club met Sunday
night at 7:15 in the Eucalypto social
room at Lagunita Court. Featured
at the meeting were talks by the
seven foreign scholarship students
on their initial thoughts and im
pressions upon first arriving in the
United States, and a talk by Howard
Lockwood on the UN interneeship
program.
Sushil Guha, president of the club,
announced to the attending audience
that I Club would appreciate the
offer of any of the major living
groups to hold I Club meetings in
their lounges and social rooms.
I Club is a committee of lIR with
the purpose of providing a social
meeting ground for American and
foreign students.
