# Frosh to Gather Firewood Today
## 
### 
Men of the class of '52 will meet
in front of Eneina today at 3 p.m.
to gather wood for the Big Game
Bonfire, which will be lit Nov. 18
after the Big Game Rally.
Wood gatherers will also leave
tomorrow at 2:30 p.m.
Building of the bonfire is custom
arily assigned to the freshman class.
After gathering wood, which in
cludes among other things railroad
ties, scrap lumber, and boxes, the
frosh men wi'.l build the fire all
day Nov. 18. Freshman women will
serve coffee and doughnuts to the
workers.
