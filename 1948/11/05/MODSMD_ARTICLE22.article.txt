# WAA Plans Swim With San Jose
## 
### 
The Stanford Women's Swim
ming Club will entertain the San
Jose State women's swimming team
tomorrow morning in the Women's
Gym pool. The morning's program
will include freestyle, backstroke,
breaststroke, and medley races as
well as exhibition ballet numbers
for groups of less than four and
groups of more than four.
The WAA is providing a lunch
for the eirls of both schools after
they finish swimming. San Jose
State is the first group to be en
tertained by the Stanford club this
year. Next Saturday the club will
swim with Cal and Mills as part of
the tri-school play-day to be held
at Stanford.
