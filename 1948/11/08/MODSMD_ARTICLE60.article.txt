# Fraternity Photos
## 
### 
Fraternity pictures for the 1949
Quad are being taken this week and
part of next at the Quad office, 10
a.m. to noon and 1 to 5 p.m.
Men coming for photographs are
requested to wear coats and ties;
the pictures cost $1.03 each.
While no individual appointments
are necessary, fraternity members
are asked to follow this schedule.
Today—Chi Psi, Delta Chi, Delta
Kappa F.psilon, Delta Tau Delta,
Delta Upsilon.
Tomorrow and Wednesday—Kap
pa Alpha, Kappa Sigma, Phi Delta
