# 'Y' Offers Projects For Summer Pastimes
## 
### 
Do you know what you're going
to do next summer?
Women students may hear what
opportunities in jobs and travel are
offered them at the YWCA summer
projects meeting today at 4:15 in
the "Y" lounge on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Speakers at the meeting will be
El'en Eliel, who studied for a year
in Switzerland, and Elaine Elliol
who took part in the students in in
dustry program at Hartford, Conn.
Mary Kilbourne will relate her
experiences as a recreational direc
tor in Mexico last summer, and
Elizabeth Taggart will tell of her
government studies at a Washing
ton seminar.
