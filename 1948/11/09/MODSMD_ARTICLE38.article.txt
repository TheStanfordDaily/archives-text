# Cal Professor to Speak To Christian Fellowship
## 
### 
The Stanford Christian Fellow
ship will hear Dr. Moyer, professor
and research physicist at the Uni
versity of California, speak to them
on "The Rationality of Christian
ity" tomorrow evening at 7:30 on
the top floor of the Women's Club
house.
Bob Laughlin says the Fellow
ship is interdenominational, and all
students and faculty are invited to
hear Dr. Moyer.
