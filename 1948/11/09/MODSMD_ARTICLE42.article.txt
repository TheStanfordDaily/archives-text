# Dr. Eurich Will Travel To Stephens College
## 
### 
Dr. Alvin C. Eurich, acting presi
dent, is the official delegate from
Stanford for the inauguration of Dr.
Homer Price Rainey as president of
Stephens College in Columbia, Mo.
Dr. Eurich, as a member of the
Board of Curators of Stephens Col
lege, will participate in the aca
demic procession preceding the in
auguration today.
Dr. Rainey has been president of
three universities. At the age of 31
he was the youngest college presi
dent in the United States as presi
dent of Franklin College in Indiana.
