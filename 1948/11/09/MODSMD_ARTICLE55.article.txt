# SIRO Aids UN
## 
### 
Two out of three children in con
tinental Europe are suffering from
tuberculosis.
More than 230 million children
throughout the world are receiving
less than the necessary diet lor
subsistence.
In Europe 50.000.000 children are
existing under standards far lower
than pre-war.
These reports have been turned
over to the United Nations Appeal
for Children by the Economic and
Security Council of the UN. It is
the purpose of the UN Appeal for
Children Fund to provide needy
children everywhere with food,
clothing, and medical care.
One-fourth of the Relief Fund
Drive will be allocated to the UN
Appeal for Children.
