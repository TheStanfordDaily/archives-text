# Birthday Dinner Tonight at Roble
## 
### 
Koblc Hall's traditional quarterly
birthday dinner will bo held to
night, according to Mel Lewis,
chairman of the affair. The event is
held one during each quarter to
honor all Roble women who have
birthdays during the period.
Consistent with the "football"
theme, the dining hall will be called
the "Stadium." Entertainment is
planned for the affair.
