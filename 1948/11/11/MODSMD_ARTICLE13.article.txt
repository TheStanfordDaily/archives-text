# Farm to Host Science Group
## 
### 
Stanford will be host to 30 of the
nation's outstanding scientists next
week as a feature of the annual fall
meeting of the National Academy
of Sciences.
The group will come to Stanford
Thursday, Nov. 18, following a
three-day program at the Univer
sity of California where six Stan
ford faculty members will deliver
papers.
Dr. Thomas Addis, professor
emeritus of medicine; Dr. Felix
Bloch, professor of physics; L. A.
Manning and O. G. Villard Jr., both
of the electrical engineering facul
ty; Gilbert M. Smith, professor of
botany; and D. C. Spencer, profes
sor of mathematics, will participate
in the opening activities at UC.
