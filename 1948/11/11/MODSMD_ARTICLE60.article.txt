# Nanney Presents Afternoon Recital
## 
### 
The Thursday afternoon organ
recital will be presented today at
4:15 in the Memorial Church by
Herbert B. Nanney.
Nanney will open the program
with two selections from Bach: the
Fantaisie in G Minor and Air from
the Suite in D. He will play Johann
Krebs' Trio in F Major and Men
delssohn's Third Sonata in A Major
(Con Moto Maestoso and Andante
Tranquillo). The closing selection
will be the Finale from Louis
Vierne's Tirst Symphony.
The public is invited.
