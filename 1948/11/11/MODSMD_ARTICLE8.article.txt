# Three Appointed To Med Faculty
## 
### 
Three new appointments to the
faculty of the School oi Medicine
were announced yesterday by Dean
Loren R. Chandler.
Dr. Monroe Jerome Hirsch has
been appointed acting assistant
professor of physiology. He was for
merly assistant professor of physi
ology at Ohio State University and
before that a research associate at
Staniord. Dr. Hirsch received his
A.B. degree from the University of
California and his Ph.D. in physi
ology from Stanford.
Jo Ellen Purtle, since 1944 assist
ant director of the Stanford Nurs
ing Service, is the new educational
director of the School of Nursing.
She received her A.B. from Fresno
State College.
