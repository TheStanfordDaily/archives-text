# Bulletin Board
## 
### 
MEN OF 4E F.A'CINA of last year
will hold their annual reunion
Sunday, 3 to 6 p.m., at Rsosotti's.
CHRISTIAN STUDENTS are in
vited to a discussion on "Under
standing the Old Testament," led
by Dr. G. C. Oxtoby. The discus
sion will be held after vespers at
7:15 p.m. Sunday at the Presby
terian Chuch.
ALPHA PHI OMEGA, national
service fraternity, will bold a
wiener roast tonight at 8:30 at
the barbecue pit near Lake Lagu
nita. .
WESLEY FOUNDATION will meet
Sunday at 6:30 for supper fol
lowed by a talk by a couple who
worked in London's slums this
past summer.
VILLAGE FOLK DANCE GROUP
will meet in Village Auditorium
at 7:30 tonight.
ALPINE CLUB Alpiners are re
minded of the Bergsteigers* Ball
at the William Tell Hotel this
evening, and of the climb this
Sunday of Indian Rock in Berke
ley.. Meet in front of the old Ad
Building at 9 Sunday morning.
