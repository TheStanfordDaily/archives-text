# 'Big Jim' Heads Rally Attractions
## 
### 
"Big Jim" Reynolds, well-known
Stanford alumnus, will be the fea
tured speaker at the Big Game
rally Thursday night. Lynn Cox and
Hap Black, co-chairmen, said.
The rally will start at 7:15 p.m. at
Lake Lagunita, and the bonfire
built by the Class of '52 will imme
diately follow, with students ser
pentining around the giant fire.
Shell Oil Co. is providing fire
works this year, Miss Cox said.
' This rally is primarily a 'spirit'
rally," Miss Cox added, "and will
consist of the traditional yells and
songs which always precede the Big
Game."
The Stanford Band and yell lead
