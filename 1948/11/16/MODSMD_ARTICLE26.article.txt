# Concert at Knoll By Music Classes
## 
### 
A concert of works for string
and wood instruments will be pre
sented on Wednesday at 8:30 p.m.
in room 202 of the Knoll.
The concert will be given by the
chamber music classes under the
direction of Dr. Leonard G. Ratner.
George Houle, oboe, and Caswell
Neal, horn, will be featured.
The University Singers under
the direction of Herbert B. Nanney
will offer a group of ballets and
madrigals on the same program.
Participants in the program in
clude Barbara Bjornstrom, Helen
Hallawell, Rex Underwood, Marcia
McKee, Harold Schmidt, George
Houle, Caswell Neal, and Leonard
Ratner.
