## 
###

tions on the Southern Pacific
Daylight and night trains for Nov.
24 are completely sold out, the
SP has announced.
Trains for the return trip on
Nov. 28 have also been entirely
tilled. Cancellations remain an
unknown quantity, but any that
are turned in will go to the stu
dents who happen to be there at
the time.
