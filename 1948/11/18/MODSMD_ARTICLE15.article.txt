# Big Game Dance
## 
### 
Bob Crosby and his "Club Fif
teen" Orchestra will play for the
Big Game Dance planned at Cal
Saturday night from 9 p.m. to 2 a.m.
in the Cal men's gymnasium. Tick
ets may be obtained for $3 at the
ASSU office.
Crosby has appeared regularly
on his own radio program and in
the movies. He is famous for the
jazz work of his "Bobcats."
Large murals will be used to dec
orate the Cal gym. A jazz combo
may play during intermissions.
