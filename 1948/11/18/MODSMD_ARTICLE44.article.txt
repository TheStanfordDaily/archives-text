# Chapel Organ Recital Scheduled for Today
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney will present
the regular organ recital in the
Memorial Church at 4:15 this after
noon.
The program includes Muffat's
"Toccata"; "Our Father, Who Art
in Heaven" by Dietrich Buxtehude;
"Vom Himmel Hoch" by Johann
Pachabel; the "Gavotta" from Pa
dre Martini's 12th Sonata; Karg-
Elert's "Harmonies du Soir"; and
Bach's "Toccata in D Minor."
