# ALPHA SIG HOUSE ROBBED
## 
### 
Burglars looted the Alpha Sigma
Phi fraternity house over the Big
Game weekend, President Dwight
Sale said today.
"Nearly every room was ran
sacked, but only radio-phonograph
sets were taken," Sale said. Mem
bers losing the radio-phonographs
were Karl Hutchinson, Joe McLain,
and Ed Vopat.
