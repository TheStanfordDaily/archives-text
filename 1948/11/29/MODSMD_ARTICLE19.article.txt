# Girl Scouts Begin Training Program
## 
### 
Two representatives of the Red
wood City Girl Scout district will
be on campus tomorrow to enroll
women interested in a training pro
gram for Girl Scout troop leaders.
Appointments may be made at the
Women's Counselor's office, Build
ing B, from 2 to 4 p.m.
Senior Girl Scout leaders must be
21 or over to qualify for the posi
tion. Younger women may apply
to help with intermediate Scout
groups.
