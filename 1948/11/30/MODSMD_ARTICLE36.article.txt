# White Xmas At Toyon Ball
## 
### 
"Whitje Christmas" comes to Toy
on Saturday night. The formal is
being held to celebrate Toyon's re
cently decorate'd lobby.
The dance will feature music by
Howard Fredericks' orchestra. Re
freshments will be served through
out the evening.
Bids for the formal go on sale to
Toyon men today, and to the whole
campus Thursday.
