# Professional 'Y' Workers Sought
## 
### 
Mrs. Edith Lerrigo, regional di
rector of the YWCA, will arrive at
Stanford tomorrow to interview all
women interested in professional
YWCA work. Interviews will be
held Wednesday morning from 9 to
12 and Thursday afternoon from 1
to 4 in the "Y" lounge.
Thursday morning Mrs. Lerrigo
will meet with the Advisory Board
from 11 to 12. At 12 she is invited
to a combined Advisory Board-
Cabinet luncheon in the "Y"
lounge. Mrs. Lerrigo will also sit
in on the first "Y" Council meeting
of the quarter Thursday afternoon
at 4. %
