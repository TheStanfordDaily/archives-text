# ATO WINS 14 TO 6
## 
### 
Trailing 6-0 at the half, the ATO
intramural grid machine tallied
twice to down the SAE Golds 14-6
in the University semi-finals. Clay
ton Neill's pass to Jim Trabucco,
followed by another aerial to Don
Glass for the conversion, cinched
the contest over last year's intra
mural champs.
