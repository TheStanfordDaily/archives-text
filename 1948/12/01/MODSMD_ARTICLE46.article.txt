# Hispanic Series Topic On Minerals in Brazil
## 
### 
"Brazil and Its Mineral Sciences"
will be discussed by Dr. Charles F.
Park Jr., professor of geology, at
the Hispanic Series lecture tonight
at 7:30 in Cubberley Auditorium.
Dr. Park was formerly chief of
the metalliferous deposits section of
the U.S. Geological Survey. He
joined the Stanford faculty in 1546.
The talk will be illustrated with
colored slides which Dr. Park took
on a recent tour. Professor George
Myers of the Natural History Mu
seum will be chairman.
