# Asilomar Meeting Set for Holidays
## 
### 
During the Christmas holidays
students from California, Arizona,
Nevada, and Hawaii will meet at
Asilomar on Monterey Bay. There
they will join in discussion groups
of the student-faculty YMCA-YW
CA conference.
Any students interested in at
tending the conference may get
further information in the YWCA
office on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse, stated Foofi
Wilson, Asilomar Conference Com
mittee chairman.
