# Final Tryouts Held At Little Theater
## 
### 
''Fina! tryouts for the comedy
"Builcl No Fence Around Me" will
be held today from 3 to 5 p.m. in
the Little Theater and from 7:30 to
9 p.m. in room 205.
"All parts cue excellent roles,"
said Dr. A. Nicholas Vardae, direc
tor of the show. "We want an all
campus turnout for this show."
Dr. Vardae revealed there is a
possibility of giving the show for
other audiences after the Stanford
run Feb. 2. 3. 4, and 5.
The three-act comedy is by a
Stanford playwright.
