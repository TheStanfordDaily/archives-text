# Choirs Plan Xmas Service
## 
### 
The Stanford music department
is planning an additional choral
program to highlight this year's
Christmas service which will be
presented in the Memorial Church
on Dec. 12.
Christmas carols will be per
formed by three groups: the Uni
versity Choir, directed by Harold
C. Schmidt; the University Singers,
directed by Herbert B. Nannev, or
ganist; and the University Brass
Choir, under the direction of Julius
E. Shuchat.
The program, which is to include
carols by Bach, Mendelssohn, and
Brahms, will be climaxed by join:
presentation of "The Hallelujah
Chorus" from"The Messiah."
