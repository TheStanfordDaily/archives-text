# Professor Hanna Leaves for East
## 
### 
Dr. Paul Hanna, Stanford pro
fessor of education, left last night
by air for two weeks in the East
where he will take part in several
educational conferences.
Today and Friday he will attend
a meeting of the Cleveland con
ference, which is made up of 50 se
lected educators from colleges and
universities all over the United
States. Dr. Hanna and Dr. Alvin C.
Enrich are Stanford members of
this group.
Dr. Hanna is scheduled for other
conferences next week. He will re
turn to the campus on Dec. 16.
