# Social Roundup
## Toyon Formal 
### 
The Toyon White Christmas For
ma! opens the holiday season to
morrow night in the newly redeco
rated Toyon lounge. Howard Fred
ericks' orchestra will play from 9
to 1.
Nancy Toll, the Toyon Quartet,
and others will be featured enter
tainers during the evening. Re
freshments include a Rickey's smor
gasbord open throughout the eve
ning.
Bids at 5i.25 are on sale at the
ASSU office, and may also be pur
chased at the door. Toyon and
Roble have made the agreement
that a ticket to either formal to
morrow night admits a couple to
one or both.
