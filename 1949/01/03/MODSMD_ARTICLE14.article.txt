# Anthology Prints Four Farm Poems
## 
### 
Poems by four Stanford students
have been chosen for publication in
the "Annual Anthology of College
Poetry," a compilation of the best
poetry written by college men and
women in America.
The four poems selected are "Ex
perience" by Janet Whitehead, "The
Willow Tree" by Joann Colin, "The
Moon" by Barbara Moore, and
"While We Pass" bv Frankie Paley.
