# UNESCO Talks Sponsored by IIR
## 
### 
More than 60 student leaders
from Northern California high
schools attended a regional confer
ence on UNESCO at Asilomar on
Dec. 3 and 4, under the sponsorship
of the Institute of International Re
lations.
Forty Stanford students, active
in lIR work, also participated in the
weekend conference.
Margaret Frances Bacon, Stan
ford senior, was conference chair
man.
