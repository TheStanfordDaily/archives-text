# Election Results Released by Club
## 
### 
Newman Club, the Stanford or
ganization for Catholic students,
has announced the results of their
elections last quarter.
New officers are Andy Ilanley.
president; Mary Desmond, vice
president; Nancy Foss, secretary;
Tony Malo. treasurer.
To begin this quarter's activities,
the club is planning a social eve
ning for their first meeting Thurs
day night from 7:45 to 10.
Dancing and refreshments will be
offered for all those who can dig
deep enough to pay their dues to
the treasurer at the door.
The party will be held at St.
Thomas Aquinas Ilall, Homer and
Waverley Streets, Palo Alto. Cars
will leave Robie at 7:20 p.m. Thurs
day.
