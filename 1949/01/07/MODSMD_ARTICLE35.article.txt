# Gold Bars Offered Former Officers
## 
### 
Men between the ages of 21 and
27 who have served honorably on
active duty for at least one year as
a commissioned officer in any of the
Armed Forces between Dec. 7, 1941,
and Sept. 2, 1945, or who are now
in the Army of the United States,
may apply for direct appointment
as second lieutenant, Regular Army.
Selected applicants, who must
have graduated or are to graduate
from a recognized university, will
be commissioned in the Regular
Army and will be detailed in the
arm or service for which the appli
cant is considered most qualified.
Further information can be ob
tained from the Stanford military
department.
