# Graduate Students Register for Exam
## 
### 
The Graduate Record Examina
tion. a series of general achievement
tests in broad areas of study and ad
vanced tests in special subjects, will
be given on the Stanford campus on
Feb. 7 and 8. A large number of
graduate schools in the United
States and Canada recommend or
require that the results of this ex
amination be submitted as one of
the credentials for admission. The
exam is given once each three
months on a nation-wide basis.
Students wishing" to take the tost
must register with Mrs. Marjorie
Schull, Temporary Building B, room
50, before noon, Jan. 20. No appli
cations will be accepted after that
date. A fee of $10, payable at the
beginning of the first testing session,
is required.
