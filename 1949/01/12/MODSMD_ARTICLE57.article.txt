# Farm Professors On State Board
## 
### 
Two faculty members will serve
on the State Board of Education
committee for the accreditation of
Chico State College as a teacher
training institution.
Dr. Robert N. Bush, associate
professor of education and director
of the Appointment Service, and Dr.
Alfred H. Grommon, director of ad
missions and associate professor of
education and English, will spend
next Monday and Tuesday at Chico
College as members of the accredi
tation committee.
