# Tickets for Plays Almost Sold Out
## 
### 
AM tickets for "The Hairy Ape"
are sold out. and tickets for "Build
No Fence Around Me" are going
fast, with a few tickets for Feb. 7
and 8 evening shows and Feb. 5
matinee remaining, Chester W.
Barker of the drama department
announced yesterday.
However, at the same time Bar
ker encouraged persons who would
like to see "The Hairy Ape" but
who do not have tickets to place
their names on waiting lists, as
some tickets are always returned
at the last minute. Tickets are 60
cents. The show runs Jan. 26-29.
Tickets for "Build No Fence
Around Me" are $1.20.
