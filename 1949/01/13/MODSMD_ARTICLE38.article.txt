# Tryouts for 'Relapse' To Be Held Next Week
## 
### 
Tryouts for "The Relapse," a
Restoration comedy, will be held
Monday and Wednesday from 4 to
G p.m. in the Memorial Auditorium.
Tryouts are open to the entire
campus. The 25 parts offer wide
variety in the production.
The play will be given March 3,
4, and 5. Author of the work is
John van Brugh.
