# HOUSE BUILT IN TWO DAYS
## 
### 
SANTA ROSA, Jan. 12 .—Build
ing contractor Hugh B. Codding
announced tonight the completion
at 4:30 p.m. of a five-room house
on which construction was started
yesterday morning. Mr. and Mrs.
Charles Colvin and their infant
daughter were all moved in by 5
o'clock.
