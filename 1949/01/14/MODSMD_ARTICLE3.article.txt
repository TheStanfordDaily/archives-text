# Room and Board Bills Are Due tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day to
pay room and board bills, the
Comptroller's oflice announced.
Bills not paid by noon tomorrow
will be subject to fines.
Bills may be paid in the cash
ier's oilice in the Encina lobby.
The otlice is open from 8:30 a.m.
to noon and from 1 to 4 p.m. on
weekdays, and Saturday from
8:30 to 11:30 a.m.
