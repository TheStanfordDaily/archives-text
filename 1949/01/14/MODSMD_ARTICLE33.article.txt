# Organist Will Give Recital oh Sunday
## 
### 
An interlude with the real at
mosphere of the Sabbath will be
presented by Organist Herbert B.
Nanney in a special organ recital
this Sunday afternoon at 3 in the
Memorial Church.
Among his selections. Organist
Nanney will play Sonata No. 1 in
E F!at Major by Bach; Fantaisie in
F by Mozart; two Chorale-Preludes
by Brahms; Fugue in E Flat Major
by Bach; and "Thou Art the Rock"
by Henri Mulet.
Special guests at the recital will
be the members of the San Jose
chapter of the American Guild of
Organists.
