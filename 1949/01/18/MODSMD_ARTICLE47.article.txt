# KUHARICH SIGNS NEW GRID CONTRACT AT USF
## 
### 
SAN FPANCISCO, Jan. 17 C/P).—
Football Coach Joseph Kuharich
has been signed to a new three
year contract "with a substantial
raise in salary" at the University
of San Francisco. No figures were
cited.
Kuharich concluded his first year
as head coach at USF last season.
