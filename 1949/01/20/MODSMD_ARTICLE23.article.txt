# Monday Last Day For Quad Photos
## 
### 
Next Monday will be the last day
for individual Quad pictures as
that will be the photographer's last
day at the Quad office.
These living groups are scheduled
for this final round of photographs:
Today — Village women. Village
men.
Tomorrow—Village men.
Monday—Village men and frater
nity pledges.
Hours at the Quad office are 10
a.m. to noon and 1 to 5 p.m. Pic
tures cost 51.03. Men should wear
coats and ties, and women white
blouses.
