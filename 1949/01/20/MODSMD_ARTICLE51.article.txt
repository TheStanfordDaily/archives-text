# In the count of the Beloved Oak
## 
### 
"Where did you ever find a
skirt and sweaters that match
so perfectly?" gasped my
roommate as I unwrapped my
latest purchases. I let her
wonder in wide-eyed amaze
ment while I slipped into my
new dusty pink outfit.
"The Clothes Closet, natu
rally," was my calm answer.
"They have just received
them as a part of their spring
shipment. Do you notice the
length of the skirt? These
new skirts, made of wonder
ful wool gaberdine, come in
three lengths, short, medium,
and long, so you can get just
the right fit.
"The sweater sets match so
perfectly because they are
dyed in the same lot. They're
Helen .Harper's wool .and
rabbits' hair .sweaters, and
they come in all the beautiful
Spring pastels."
"Having all three pieces
match makes you look taller
and slimmer," commented my
roommate. "I'm going to the
Clothes Closet this very af
ternoon."
The skirts are $12.95; the
short-sleeved slip-on is $4.95
and the long-sleeved cardi
gan $7.95. Why don't you go
see them too? Don't forget,
the Clothes Closet's at 520
Kamona near University.
