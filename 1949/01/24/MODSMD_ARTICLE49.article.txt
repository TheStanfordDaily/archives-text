# Today Is Deadline For Quad Pictures
## 
### 
Individual pictures for the 1949
Quad will be taken for the last time
today.
On the schedule are Village men
and fraternity pledges who did not
have their pictures taken for the
Frosh Book. They are asked to re
port to the Quad office from 10 a.m.
to noon or from 1 to 5 p.m.
Coats and ties should be worn
for the pictures, which cost $1.03.
