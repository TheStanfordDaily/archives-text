# Row Girls Set Date for Dance
## 
### 
Bob Britton and his orchestra
will play at the annual Women's
Row Dance Feb. 4 from 9 p.m. to
1 a.m. in the Colonial Room of the
St. Francis Hotel, Betty White, Row
Excom president, said yesterday.
Row girls will get their tickets
next week. Miss White said. The
affair is not formal but the women
should wear dressy dresses.
Professor emeritus and Mrs. Al
bert Guerard will act as patron and
patroness.
