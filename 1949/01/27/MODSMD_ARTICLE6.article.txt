# Farm Research Class To Survey Television
## 
### 
The future of television in the
Palo Alto area will be investigated
by survey teams of the Market Re
search class of the Graduate School
of Business, beginning today.
The survey by 32 members under
Professor C. D. Hadley will con
tinue through Feb. 4. All persons
taking part must carry cards signed
by the Palo Alto chief of police.
