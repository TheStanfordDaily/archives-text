# 'Barbary Coast" To Show Sunday
## 
### 
San Francisco in the roaring days
when men were men will flash on
the Sunday Evening Movie screen
in "Barbary Coast."
Edward G. Robinson, as a tough
crook of the Barbary Coast, stars in
the action-packed melodrama. Co- '
stars are Brian Donlevy, Joel Mc-
Crea, Walter Brennan, and Miriam
Hopkins.
San Francisco is at its roughest
as the hard - drinking, easy - loving
gangsters fight the Vigilantes who
are determined to "clean up" the !
Barbary Coast.
