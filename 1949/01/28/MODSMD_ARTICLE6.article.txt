# Whiskerinos Sign Up Today for Contest
## 
### 
Freslnnen and sophomores en
tering the Jamboree Whiskerino
contest should sign up at the Post

free shave at Austin's Burn Fri

Jamboree weekend.
Signups for other contests in
the Jamboree program will be
