# Vocom Will Discuss Journalism Wednesday
## 
### 
"Journalism and Advertising:
Facts and Fantasies" is the title <>l
the Vocom program to be held on
Wednesday at 7:15 p.m. in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse
Speakers for the program will in
clude: Miss Elinor Cogswell, editor
of the Palo Alto Times; Miss Jo
anne-O'Brien, former Daily art edi
tor, now working for Sunset Maga
zine; and Mrs. Lucrezia Kemper.
San Francisco advertising execu
tive.
Dr. Chilton R. Bush, head of the
journalism department, will act as
moderator. Sandy Meitus is chair
man ol the program.
