# THETA CHI FIRE
## 
### 
Theta Delta Cbi's kitchen stove
caught fire yesterday morning at 11
and the flames spread to the rear
wall of the room. Stanford firemen
extinguished the blaze within 30
minutes, but an estimated $50 dam
age resulted from the fire.
