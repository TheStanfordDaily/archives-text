# Demerit Plan to Go Into Effect Tomorrow
## 
### 
The demerit plan, which was vot
ed in by the women last week, will
go into effect tomorrow, AWS has
announced.
The bonus system is still in effect.
Under the new late leave regula
tions. freshmen and sophomores
may acquire five demerits in the
rest of their Stanford career; jun
iors, four; and seniors, three, with
out penalty.
