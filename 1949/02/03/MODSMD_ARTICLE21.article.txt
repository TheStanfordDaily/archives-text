# 'Y' Hour Features Gershwin's Music
## 
### 
All Gershwin tunes will be played
at the Junior YWCA Listening Hour
today from 4:15 to 5:30 p.m. in the
'"Y" lounge.
Corky Meserve, in charge of the
program this week, said that some
of the most popular of the records
are "Porgy and Bess." "American
in Paris," "Cuban Overture," and
'Second Rhapsody."
