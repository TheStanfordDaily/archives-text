# Mangrum Hot!
## 
### 
TUCSON. Ariz., Feb. 3 (/P). —
Shooting one of his best rounds of
1949, Lloyd Mangrum, Chicago, 111.,
today took the first-round lead in
the $10,000 Tucson Open Golf Tour
nament with a six-under-par G6.
One stroke behind were the long
hitting Chick Harbert, Detroit; Eric
Monti, Santa Monica, Calif.; and
Johnny Bulla, Pittsburgh, Pa.
Within easy striking distance were
11 contenders with cards of 66.
Jimmy Demaret, Ojai, Calif.,
twice Tucson Open champion, was
two strokes off the pace with 69.
