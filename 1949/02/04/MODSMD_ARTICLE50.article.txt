# 'I' Club Will Give Norwegian Party
## 
### 
Norwegian entertainment and food
is offered everyone at the I Club
party at 7 p.m. Sunday evening at
the home 61' Mis. Edwin Thomas,
365 Lincoln Ave., Palo Alto.
Krum kaga, the Norwegian ver
sion of an ice cream cone, will be
served following a program planned
by Eleanor Sather. The program in
cludes singing Norwegian drinking
songs, listening to other Norwegian
songs on the llute, Norwegian poems
and a talk on Norwegian literature
and international understanding.
Hostessing for the I Club will be
two little girls dressed in the Nor
wegian national costume.
