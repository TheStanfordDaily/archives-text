Miss Darwell is the star in the
world premiere opening of Alexan
der Greendale's comedy of pioneer
Texan life, "Build No Fence Around
Me."
She won an Oscar in 1940 for her
portrayal of Ma Joad in "The
Grapes of Wrath." She has had
supporting roles on the stage and
screen for 33 years.
