# Kellberg Wins Medalist Honors in Golf Tourney
## 
### 
Despite downpours of rain on
Thursday and Friday the All-Uni
versity golf tournament qualifying
round was completed last week with
Ernie Kellberg winning medalist
honors with a sparkling two-under
par 09. Lynn Miller and freshman
Dick McElyea tied for second spot
with a pair of 74's.
Coach Bud Finger announced
that first-round matches must be
played and scores turned in by this
Friday. lie also said that prizes will
be awarded to the champion, run
ner-up, and medalist.
