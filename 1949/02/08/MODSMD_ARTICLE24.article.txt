# Psych Professor's Stat Book on Sale
## 
### 
Dr. Quinn McNemar, professor of
psychology, is the author of a new
book. "Psychological Statistics,"
published by John Wiley & Sons,
New York.
The book is described by its pub
lishers as "designed to serve as a
concise introduction to statistical
methods and provide a continuous
transition to more advanced topics.''
Dr. McNemar, a member of the
Stanford faculty since 1931. is also
author of "Revision of the Stanford
Binet Scale," published in 1941, and
of numerous articles in psychologi
cal journals.
