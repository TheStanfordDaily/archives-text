# Edwards to Speak At Alumni Meeting
## 
### 
Paul C. Edwards, associate editor
of the San Francisco News and
president of the Board of Trustees
of Stanford, will be the featured
speaker at the first Alumni Con
ference. He will speak on "Stanford
Today and Tomorrow" at a banquet
Feb. 10, at the UniverstLy Club in
Sacramento.
Mr. Edwards' talk will follow an
afternoon program of talks ny six
Stanford faculty members at the
Sacramento College Auditorium.
Alumni Director Jack W. Shoup
said that anyone interested in Stan
ford is invited to the conference,
which is for residents of Northern
California outside the Bay Area.
