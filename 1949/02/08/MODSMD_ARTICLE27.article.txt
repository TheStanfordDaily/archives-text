# Ventura Will Hold Formal at Palace
## 
### 
Casa Ventura will dine and dance
Saturday to the music of Del
Courtney's orchestra at the Palace
Hotel, announced Marge Wheat,
Ventura dance chairman.
The formal dinner dance will be
held in the Garden Court starting at
8:30 p.m.
Other members of the dance
committee are Pat O'Farrell, Carol
Morrissey, and Sandy Meitus.
