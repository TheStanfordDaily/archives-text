# Opera Workshop Presents Recitals
## 
### 
Stanford's Opera Workshop will
present two public recitals of work
in-progress. Joel J. Carter, assistant
director, announced yesterday.
The first recital will be given to
night at 8:15 in Woodpecker Lodge.
Act I will feature "Don Giovanni"
by Mozart, "Aida" by Verdi, "Otel
lo" by Verdi, and "Madame Butter
fly" by Puccini.
Tomorrow night also at 8:15 in
Woodpecker Lodge, the second re
cital will present "The Barber of
Seville" by Rossini, "La Traviata"
by Verdi, and "Aida," also by Ver
di.
There is no charge.
