# Branner Election Results Revealed
## 
### 
Jean Steinmetz was elected presi
dent of Branner in yesterday's elec
tions, outgoing president Judy Min
ton announced early last night.
Other officers elected were Lor
ry Tabin, first vice-president and
social chairman; Phyllis Floyd,
second vice-president; Pat Camp
bell, treasurer; and Sue Kelly, sec
retary.
A new committee of five was also
voted in. They are Barbara Vesey-
Brown, Mary Hirch, Peg Jones,
Joyce Trattner, and Janet Broch.
