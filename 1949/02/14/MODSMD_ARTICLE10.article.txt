# SC, UCLA Tickets Sell Today at SAC
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the Stan
ford - SC and Stanford - UCLA
basket ball games, Friday and Sat
urday at the Pavilion, will go on
sale today at the SAC.
One-half of the tickets will go
on sale al 8:30 a.m., and the other
half will be released at 1 p.m.
Student body cards must be
shown, but no more than two
may be presented by one student.
