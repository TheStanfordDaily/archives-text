# Indian Debaters Defeat San Jose
## 
### 
In the manner of the Stanford
football and cage teams, the pair
representing the Stanford debate
squad defeated San Jose State in
their match Tuesday night. The
two students who appeared as guest
debaters at San Jose were Pete
Slusser and Mary Kilbourne.
Miss Kilbourne, one of the
Farm's few women debaters, made
a very good showing in this meet
ing, which dealt with the federal aid
to education problem. The Stanford
team took the negative side of the
question.
Jack Marcum and Art Herzog,
representing the debate club at the
Rocky Mountain Speech Tourna
ment. left by plane yesterday for
Denver. The competition will begin
tomorrow and will continue for three
days.
