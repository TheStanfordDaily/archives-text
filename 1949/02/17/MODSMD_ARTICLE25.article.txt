# Stanford Will Begin New NAACP Chapter
## 
### 
A Stanford chapter of the Na
tional Association for the Advance
ment of Colored People is sched
uled to be formed this afternoon at
4:15. The group is to meet in the
conference room on the third floor
of the Women's Clubhouse.
A report on discrimination in
schools will be presented to the
group. Temporary officers are to be
elected and charter membership
rolls will be opened.
