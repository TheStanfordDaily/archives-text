# 'I' Club Talk Features Big Game Hunting
## 
### 
"I" Club's meeting Sunday at 7:30
in the Nitery will feature a talk by
Vikram Singh on "Hunting Big
Game in India." Also on the eve
ning's program is the playing of
some Hindu records and interviews
at 8 by KSU.
"Everyone is really welcome to
attend the meeting and stay for the
community sinking and dancing that
will follow," said David Chan. "I"
Club president.
At noon Saturday, '"I" Club will
play host to all foreign students from
Cal, Mills College, and Marin JC,
and all Stanford students interested
in working with a proposed organ
ization of this group.
A luncheon will be held in Union
dining room and a campus tour will
follow in the afternoon.
