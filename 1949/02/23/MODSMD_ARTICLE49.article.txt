# Rain Postpones Seals' Initial Spring Practice
## 
### 
BOYES HOT SPRING. Feb. 22
(.•Pi.—Rain spoiled the start of the
San Francisco Seals' spring train
ing season for the second straight
day today.
Manager Frank J. O'Doul ordered
his men to suit up at 10:30 a.m.. but
a few minutes later he sadly can
celed the order and told them to
report back tomorrow morning.
Last year. too. the rain seriously
interfered with the Seals' training
camp. The year before that they
trained in Hawaii.
