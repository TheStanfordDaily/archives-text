# In the Court of the Beloved Oak
## 
### 
Everyone knows that '"White
Stag" means good sport
clothes, but what surprised me
is what White Stag can do in
denim! They have produced
a new kind of iridescent den
im called "gold tone," which
comes in tangerine, navy, and
faded blue. From it they have
fashioned the smartest play
clothes I have seen this year.
Included in their collection
is a piece called the train
man's jacket, guaranteed to
catch the eye. With it you
might wear matching shorts,
clam diggers, deck trau
(slacks to you), or barndanc
er's skirl, a gently gored skirt
having a high back pleat and
large patch pockets in front.
Also included in this match
ing collection ale a halter and
a box jacket.
If you are partial to an old
stand - by. you'll like White
Stag jeans. Remember that
White Stag fashions are ex
clusive with the Clothes Clos
et. 520 Ramona just ofl' Uni
versity. Come down and see
these smart casuals today.
