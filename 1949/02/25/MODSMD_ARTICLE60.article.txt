# Painter to Speak On Segregation
## 
### 
'"Segregation in Washington, D.C."
will be the topic of a short talk by
Betsy Graves Reyneau at this af
ternoon's meeting of the Stanford
chapter of the National Association
for the Advancement of Colored
People. The meeting will be Held at
4:15 in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Miss Reyneau is one of the paint
ers whose work is included in the
exhibit of top Negro artists current
ly on display at the Jordan Junior
High School. She was formerly a
member of the Fair Employment
Practices Commission in Washing
ton, D.C.
The organization's charter will be
presented to the membership for
approval. Charter members will
still be admitted.
