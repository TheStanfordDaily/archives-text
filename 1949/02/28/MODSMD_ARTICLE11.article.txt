# Women to Submit Residence Cards
## 
### 
Women who have applied for res
idence changes will make out choice
cards tomorrow tor the 40 openings
that are now available.
Cards may be filled out at Miss
Doyle's office with 10 to 15 prefer
ences. Miss Doyle and the Housing
Committee will have the girls placed
by Wednesday, and on Thursday
the lists will be published announc
ing the new residences.
Preference is given according to
class in autumn and winter draw
ings, that is, a senior may get her
first choice even though she has
drawn a higher number than a
sophomore.
