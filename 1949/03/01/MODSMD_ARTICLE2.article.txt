# Willis Bequeaths Quake-proof House
## 
### 
The earthquake-proof house that
Dr. Bailey Willis built on campus
and lived in until his his death
Feb. 19 was willed to his four chil
dren, it was disclosed yesterday
after reading of his will.
Located near the Stanford post
office, the house was built on a
foundation which would permit it
to roll and swing in a heavy earth
quake in order to protect his col
lection of fine antique china from
breakage.
