# Service Dedicated To Ash Wednesday
## 
### 
A candlelight communion service
will be held in the chapel tonight
in commemoration of Ash Wednes
day. The service will be at 9 and
is open to all, irrespective of de
nomination.
In addition to the usual Wednes
day devotional music, Dr. Johnston
will give a short talk. The service
will not exceed half an hour.
The regular Wednesday evening
candlelight services will continue
throughout Lent. A second com
munion service wil! be held the
Thursday before Easter.
