# Orchestra Tryouts
## 
### 
Orchestra tryouts for the spring
opera production, "Schwanda, the
Bagpiper," will be held at the Knoll
. at 7 p.m. next Thursday.
The modern Czech folk opera by
; Jaromir Weinberger will be pre
sented May 26 to 26 in the Memo
rial Auditorium. The orchestra will
be comprised of from 45 to 50 mem
! bets.
