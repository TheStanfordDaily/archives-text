# Libraries Set Holiday Hours
## 
### 
The main library has announced
the following hours for spring va
cation:
The Library closes at 6 p.m. on
March 18. It will be open from 8
a.m. to 12 noon on Saturday, March
19. but will be closed March 20.
From March 21 to 25 it will be open
from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.. and on March
2G from 9 a.m. to noon. It will be
closed again all day March 27 and
will be open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
on March 28. Regular hours will
resume on March 29.
All books withdrawn by students,
graduate and undergraduate, are
due March 18. To keep books out
between quarters, students must
have them renewed on or after
March 14. Books returned after
March 21 will be considered over
due and subject to fine. Students
should avoid having their names
sent lo the Registrar's Office by re
turning or renewing books before
leaving the campus.
The Western Civilization Library
will be open at its regular hours
through March 14.
On March 14 studnts may take
out any number of books they wish.
'Ihese books will be due on March
28 when the Library will re-open
after spring vacation.
