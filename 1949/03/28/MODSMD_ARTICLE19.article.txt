# Theta Xi Planning For Apache Dance
## 
### 
Paris, the people's Paris, will be
transported to the Theta Xi house
for the fraternity's annual Apache
dance, according to Dick "Frenchy"
Henderson, Theta Xi social chair
man.
The party is open to the campus,
and costumed dancers will dance to
the music of suave Frank Beazh
and his Pig Alley Playboys, who
were deported from the most color
ful part of Paris, according to Mon
sieur Henderson.
