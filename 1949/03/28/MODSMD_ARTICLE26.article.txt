# Dailey Returning
## 
### 
Bill (Bull) Dailey will be back daily this quarter in The Daily
3ull Session slot.
Academic work forced Bill to take a leave of absence last
quarter from his humor column, but he will be back this quarter
with biqqer and better lauqhs.
For you readers the column will furnish the lift needed to
round out a morning of serious classes. For us on The Daily,
Bull Session rounds out an editorial page of varied features.
Bill's first column will appear on this page tomorrow.
