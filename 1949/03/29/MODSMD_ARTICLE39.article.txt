# Actress Will Give Speech Thursday
## 
### 
Miss Frances Rowe, distinguished
British actress, will give an informal
discussion of contemporary condi
tions in the British and American
theaters in the Little Theater Thurs
day at 4:15 p.m.. according to Mr.
Chester Barker, secretary of tne
speech and drama department.
Miss Rowe is currently playing
the feminine lead in George Bern
ard Shaw's "Man and Superman,"
now at the Curran in San Francisco.
She is a member of the American
National Theater and Academy.
Students and the public are in
vited to attend the lecture, under
the auspices of the speech and
drama department, free of charge.
Following the discussion, there will
