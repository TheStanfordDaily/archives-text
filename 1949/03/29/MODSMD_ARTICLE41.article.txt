# Art Gallery Shows Koehler Collection
## 
### 
A unique collection of oils and
watercolors by Florence Koehler is
now on exhibit at the Art Gallery.
Mrs. Koehler was an American by
birth and a European by adoption.
She died in Rome in 1944.
The paintings, which were ob
tained through the Western Asso
ciation of Art Museum Directors,
belong to Mrs. Henry D. Sharpe of
Providence, R.I. The exhibit will
remain on view through April 3.
Gallery hours are from Tuesday
through Saturday, 10 a.m. to 5 p.m..
and Sunday 12 to 4 p.m. The public
is invited.
