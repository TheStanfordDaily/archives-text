# Crossed-up Rough Ignores Crossing
## 
### 
A Stanford student who tried to
drive across the railroad tracks the
hard way—where there is no cross
ing—received a jail booking and a
wrecked tire for his eflorts early
yesterday morning.
The student was charged with
drunken driving and booked in the
San Mateo County jail when police
caught him trying to get his car
across the tracks at Santa Cruz Ave.
in Menlo Park about 3:30 a.m.
