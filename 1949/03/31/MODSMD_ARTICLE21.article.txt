# Nanney Will Give Organ Program
## 
### 
A program of Bach, Beethoven,
and Brahms will he presented by
Organist Herbert B. Nanney at the
Memorial Church this afternoon at
4:15.
Nanney's recital will include the
"Concerto No. 1 in G Major" by
Bach. "Prelude and Fugue in G
Minor" by Brahms, and "Adagio
Cantabile" from "Sonata Pathe
tique" by Beethoven, plus several
other selections from these com
posers.
Anyone interested in seeing the
console of the organ is invited to
the choir loft immediately follow
ing the recital.
