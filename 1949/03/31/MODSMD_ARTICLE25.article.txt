# Folk Dancers Urge Students to Sign Up
## 
### 
All Stanford students are invited
to register for tiie adults' folk danc
ing classes at the Palo Alto Com
munity Center, 1305 Middlefield
Road, tonight from 7 to 10. The
registration lee is $1 for the three
months' series.
Beginners will meet Tuesdays
from 8:15 to 10 p.m. for instruction
in the waltz, polka, and schottische
and about 30 simple European and
American dances based on these
steps.
Intermediate instruction is given
Wednesday and advanced dancing
on Thursday from 8:15 to 10 p.m.
and may be taken concurrently. All
dances are held in the ballroom of
the Community Center and arc
sponsored by the Palo Alto Recrea
tion Department.
