# Village Presents Orientation Show
## 
### 
An orientation program for new
students at Stanford Village was
presented Sunday night at the Vil
lage Clubhouse by the Village Ac
tivities Committee.
Speakers on the program were
Sheldon Beatty, counselor for men;
Eugene Dils, resident counselor at
the Village, and Tom Armstrong,
Village Excom representative. They
spoke to acquaint the students with
the various phases of University ad
ministration and with student gov
ernment.
Piano interpretations by Roy
Roberts, dancing, and refreshments
completed the program. Women from
Union Residence, Casa Espahola,
and Building 123, Village, also at
tended the program. Chairman was
Bob Celuni.
