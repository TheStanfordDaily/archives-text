# Boathouse to Feature Hot Dogs, Soda Pop
## 
### 
A concession featuring hot
dogs, soft drinks, and possibly
milkshakes is now being installed
at the Lake Lagunita Boathouse,
according to John Pool, student
manager. The concession is spon
sored by Cardinals.
Operated by concessionaire Hal
Williams, who served Pavilion
patrons at the Benny Goodman
dance, the stand is expected to
have its grand opening within a
few days.
A portion of the proceeds will
go to the ASSU and wiil he
turned over to the Boathouse
fund. Pool said.
