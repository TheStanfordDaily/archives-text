# Ski Movies, Talk Top Friday Show
## 
### 
Excellent pictures of skiing all
over the world together with an in
teresting commentary by Lowell
Thomas Jr. characterized the ski
movie "Adventures in Skiing" put
on by the Committee on Public Ex
ercises last Friday night.
Thomas described his own expe
riences in skiing in Sun Valley,
Idaho; Alta. Utah; Mt. Washington,
Vermont; Strawberry Hill, N.Y.;
Alaska; and Tucson, Arizona.
