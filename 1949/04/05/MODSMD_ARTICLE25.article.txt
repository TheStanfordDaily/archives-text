# NSA to Be Affiliated With Education Council
## 
### 
The United States National Stu
dent Association was voted to as
sociate membership in the Ameri
can Council on Education recently.
The NSA is the only student or
ganization to hold membership in
the council and in the U.S. Com
mission for UNESCO.
The NSA also announced that its
second annual congress will be held
at the University of Illinois from
Aug. 24 to Sept. 2.
