# DODGERS DROP DALLAS
##

DALLAS, Tex., April 4 (A 3 ). —
Three-run homers by Gil Hodges
and Koy Campanella sparked the
Brooklyn Dodgers to an 8-3 vic
tory over the Dallas Eagles today
in a steady rainfall.
