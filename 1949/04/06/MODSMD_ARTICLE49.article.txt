# CIBA Statistics
## 
### GEORGE ANDERSON 
UCLA's hard-hitting Bruins
jumped off to an early lead in CIBA
play by taking double victories
from Stanford and St. Mary's on
consecutive weekends. Seven Bruin
hitters are over the .300 mark for
the first two weeks of league play.
They are led by pitcher Bobby An
drew. who had six safeties in eight
official times at bat, including two
home runs.
Stanford's portside chucker, Dave

Dahle, leads the league by virtue of
his four hits in as many times at
bat against California last Satur
day. Dick Clegg, John Claypool.
Ray Winterhalter, and Bud Klein
are other Indian regulars batting
over .300.
