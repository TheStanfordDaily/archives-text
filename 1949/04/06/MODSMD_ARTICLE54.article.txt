# Navy Documentary Films to Be Shown
## 
### 
A group of three Navy documen
tary films will be shown this eve
ning at 7:30 in Burgess Recreation
Park theater, Stanford Village.
They are: "The Fleet That Came
to Stay," depicting the Okinawa
campaign; "Fury in the Pacific," on
the Pelieu invasion; and "Silent
Service," describing American sub
marine warfare in the Pacific.
During the program, Captain
Ransom K. Davis, professor of naval
science, will give out permanent
commissions to local reserve offi
cers. Officers who are to get these
commissions are instructed to call
Dan Hollywood, yeoman first class,
at DA 2-4028.
The program is open to the pub
lic. but seating capacity is limited.
