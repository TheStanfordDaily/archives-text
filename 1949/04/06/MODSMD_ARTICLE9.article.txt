# Case Acquitted By Men's Council
## 
### 
Men's Council yesterday acquit
ted two students charged with vio
lating the Honor Code. The viola
tion was alleegd to have taken place
in a German 822 final examination
last quarter.
The case was brought before
Council by the instructor in the
course, on the basis of similarities
in the students' exam papers. Both
students appeared before Council.
Their testimony, together with the
evidence available, convinced Coun
cil that the Honor Code had not
been violated.
