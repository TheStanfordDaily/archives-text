# Room, Board Bills Due on Saturday
## 
### 
Only two and a half paying days
are left until late fines will be lev
ied on room and board bills. Sat
urday noon is the deadline.
Students living on campus may
pay their bills at the cashier's ofiiee
in the lobby of Enema, which is
open weekdays from 8:30 a.m. to 12
noon and from 1 to 4 p.m., and Sat
urdays from 8:30 to 11:30 a.m.
Students living at Stanford Vil
lage should pay at the Village eash
ier's office in the Civic Center. It
is open from 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.
and from 1:30 to 3:30 p.m. weekdays,
and from 9:30 to 11 a.m. on Satur
days.
