# Proclamation
## 
### 
KN'OW ALL DOGPATCH MEN. what ain't married, by these pres
ents and especially Li'l Aimer Yokum:
WHEREAS there be inside our town limits a passel of gals what ain't
married and craves something 1 awful to be dated, and
WHEKEAS there be in Dogpatch plenty of young men what could
date these gals but acts ornery and won't, and
WHEKEAS we deems dating joys as being the birthright of our fair
Dogpatch womanhood,
WE IIEKEBY PROCLAIMS AND DECREES, by right of the power
and majesty vested in us as Mayor of Dogpatch,
F RID AY, APRIL S
SADIE HAWKINS DAY
AUTHORITY of the law and the statute laid down by our re
vered first Mayor of Dogpatch, Ilekzebiah Hawkins, who had to make
it to get his own daughter, Sadie, off his hands, she bein' the homliest
gal in all these hills and no two ways about that, either.
GIVEN UNDER OUR HAND AND SEAL, this, the Bth day of April,
H)l!), in this town of Dogpatch.
