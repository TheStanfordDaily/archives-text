# Board and Room Due Tomorrow
## 
### 
Saturday noon is the deadline for
payment of room and board bills
before they are subject to late fine.
Campus residents pay at the cash
ier's office in the lobby of Encina.
It is open weekdays from 8:30 a.m.
to 12 noon and from 1 to 4 p.m., and
Saturdays from 8:30 to 11:30 a.m.
Stanford Village residents should
pay at the Village cashier's office in
the Civic Center, open week days
from 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and
1:30 to 3:30 p.m., and from 9:30 to
11 a.m. on Saturdays.
