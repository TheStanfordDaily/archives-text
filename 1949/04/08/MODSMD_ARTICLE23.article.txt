# Frosh End House Visits This Week
## 
### 
Next week will be the last time
for freshman girls to attend open
houses in the women's living groups,
it was announced last night at Wom
en's Housing Committee meeting.
General open houses for inspec
tion will be held from 1:30 to 5:30
on the following dates:
April 26, 27, and 28 for Row
houses; April 18 for Union and Un
ion Residences; and April 20 and 21
for Lagunita and Ventura.
