# Row Council Fills Offices
## 
### 
Peter "Walt" Varellas, Theta Chi,
was elected president of Interfra
ternity Council Wednesday after
noon at the organization's annual
election. New secretary-treasurer is
Roger Olson, Alpha Delta Phi.
Varellas replaces Ted LeVino of
Theta Delta Chi, who has been IFC
president since last spring quarter.
In addition to being head of the
Student Police, Varellas is also
president of Theta Chi.
Outgoing secretary-treasurer is
Don Davies, summer student body
president and member of Alpha
Delta Phi.
