# ASSU Office Openings Told
## 
### 
The offices open in the spring
ASSU elections were announced
yesterday by John Marin, Election
Board chairman.
The primary election will be held
May 4 and the final on May 11,
Marin said.
The following offices will be open:
Student body president, student
body vice - president, senior class
president, senior class vice - presi
dent, senior class secretary-treasur
er, junior class president, junior
class secretary-treasurer, sophomore
class president, and sophomore class
secretary-treasurer.
Pending approval of the new con
stitution, no statement can be made
concerning Excom positions or SAC,
since if the new document is passed,
considerable revision will be made
in these offices, Marin added.
