# Espanola to Hold Latin Fiesta Dance
## 
### 
Casa Espahola will celebrate the
"Sabado de Gloria" this Saturday
night with a closed carnival dance
at their house.
Sabado de Gloria is a religious
holiday celebrating the Glorious
Saturday before Easter. This fiesta,
common to Latin American coun
tries, is comparable to the Mardi
Gras celebration at the opening of
Lent.
