# Seniors to Order Cards, Programs
## 
### 
Seniors are urged to put in their
orders for graduation announce
ments, programs, and personal cards
as soon as possible.
These items will be on sale this
week and next at the ASSU office
from 9 to 12 a.m. and 1 to 5 p.m.
Announcements are 15 cents per
person; programs, $1.50; and per
sonal cards are from $1.75 to $2.50
per 100 cards depending on the size
of the engraving .
