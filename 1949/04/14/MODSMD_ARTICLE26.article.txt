# Vets to Get Notice After Processing
## 
### 
Veterans who have recently en
rolled at Stanford under the GI Bill
or Public Law 1G should not expect
to receive immediate notice of re
ceipt of their enrollment papers in
the San Francisco regional Veter
ans Administration office, Robert P.
Shields, manager, announces.
Shields asks that these veterans
not make inquiries concerning their
subsistence. As in the past, the vet
eran will be immediately informed
if any essential information is miss
ing from his enrollment papers.
Notice of award will be sent to
the veteran when his enrollment
papers have been processed.
