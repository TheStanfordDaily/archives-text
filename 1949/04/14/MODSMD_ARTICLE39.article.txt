# Palo Alto Group to Hold Easter Dawn Service
## 
### 
An Easter Sunday sunrise serv
ice will be held at 5:30 a.m. on the
hill above the intersection of Foot
hill Rd. and Frenchman's Rd. Rev.
David Jacobsoti of Palo Alto will
deliver the sunrise message in the
service sponsored by the Palo Alto
Ministerial Association.
All religious groups and any one
interested are invited to attend.
