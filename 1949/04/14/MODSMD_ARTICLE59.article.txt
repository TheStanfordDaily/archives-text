# Farm Netters Beat Broncos
## 
### 
Victory came back from the hin
terlands yesterday to smile on the
Stanford tennis team as the Indians
scalped the Santa Clara Broncos 9
to 0 on the Farm courts.
Today the Cards go after their
second win in a row when they
meet Cal Poly on the home grounds
at 2 p.m.
Santa Clara sports teams are get
ting their lumps from the Redmen
and yesterday's tennis match was a
complete rout. Stanford did not
drop a set to the Broncos, and most
of the Mission Town netmen did not
struggle much.
Bob Lewis took over for Kirke
Mechem in the first singles to down
Yamate easily, and from there on it
was one Stanford victory after an
other.
