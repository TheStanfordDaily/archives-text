# Steel Bridges Displayed In Engineering Exhibit
## 
### 
Photographs of steel bridges,
awarded prizes for combining archi
tectural beauty with practicality,
are now on display in the Engineer
ing Building.
Included in this display, which is
sponsored by the Stanford student
chapter of the American Society of
Civil Engineers and Professor Jack
R. Benjamin, is a picture of the
new steel and concrete cut-oft' near
South San Francisco.
