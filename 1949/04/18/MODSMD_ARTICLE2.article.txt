# Women's Groups Hold Open Houses For Frosh, Juniors
## 
### 
A series of general open houses
will be held by the various women's
living units beginning this week.
Freshman and junior transfer wom
en will be able to inspect the houses
they did not visit at after-dinner
coffee affairs.
Today and tomorrow, Union Resi
dence and all Union units will be
open for general inspection. Women
will be able to visit Lagunita and
Casa Ventura on Wednesday and
Thursday. The Row houses will be
open the following week on Tues
day, Wednesday, and Thursday, the
26th, 27th, and 28th of April. The
hours for the open houses will be
from 1:30 to 5:30 daily.
Room drawings will be held dur
ing the second week of May.
