# Library Will Show Photos of Quake
## 
### 
The Library is commemorating
the anniversary of the 190(5 earth
quake with an exhibit of pictures
and documents pertaining to the dis
aster.
In special extra editions the Daily
Palo Alto and the Palo Alto Trib
une gave lengthy accounts of de
struction on the Stanford campus.
A leaflet tells about a meeting to
arrange relief measures for San
Francisco sufferers who migrated
down the Peninsula to seek aid.
Also on display is a log account,
illustrated with photographs of the
town and written by a Palo Alto
resident.
