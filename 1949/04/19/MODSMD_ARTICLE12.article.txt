# Union Units Open For Final Visits
## 
### 
Freshman and junior transfer
women who did not get a chance to
visit Union Residence or the Un
ion units last quarter may do so this
afternoon. Today is the last day
that these living units will be open
for such inspection. Women inter
ested in visiting these residences
may drop in any time between 1:30
and 5:30.
Similar open houses will be held
at Lagunita and Casa Ventura to
morrow and Thursday afternoons at
the same time. The Row houses
will be open next week.
