# Women Serenade Sterling Residence
## 
### 
Nearly 90 Row women serenaded
Dr. and Mrs. Wallace Sterling at
their home last I'hursday night to
welcome him as president of the
University.
They sang various songs includ
ing "Stanford in Spring," "Sing a
Song of Colleges," "The Stanford
Hymn," and "In the Evening by
the Moonlight."
In the middle of the serenade the
singers entered the Sterling mansion
at the invitation of the president
and completed their repertoire
there.
