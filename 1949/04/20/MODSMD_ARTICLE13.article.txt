###

cording to Shelton L. Beatty, men's
counselor.

need to make a new $25 deposit.

be here summer quarter must sign
up Thursday. They will be permit

Seven-day meal tickets for En
cina Commons will be sold to both
graduate and undergraduate stu
dents. Only graduate students will
be eligible to purchase five - day
