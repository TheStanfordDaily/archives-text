# Debaters to Argue Negro Civil Rights
## 
### 
The question "Resolved, that
President Truman's Civil Rights
Program, in so far as ii refers to
the Negroes, should be adopted"
will be debated Monday at 8 p.m.
in Cubberley Auditorium by Stan
lord and the University of San
Francisco.
Defending the question for Stan
ford will be Arnold Klink and Sal
vatore Bossio. Speaking for the
negative wi'l lie Robert Cohhurst
and Geo Pie Lee of USF.
