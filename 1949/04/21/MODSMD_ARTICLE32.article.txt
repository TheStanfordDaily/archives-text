# Educators to Hold Old-Fashioned Picnic

### 
Education Council is sponsoring

Faculty, graduates, and under
graduate students of the School of
Education are invited to the pro

and boating. Those who wish to at
tend are to meet at 1:15 in front of

Admission charge is 45 cents for
