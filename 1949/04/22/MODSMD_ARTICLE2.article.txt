# Today Is Last Day to Buy 1949 Quads with Names
## 
### 
Orders for 1949 Quads with names
printed on the covers will be taken
tor the last time today.
The yearbooks will be available
in front of the Cellar from 10 a.m.
to 1 p.m. and at the Quad office
lrom 1 to 5 p.m.
Price of the volume is $7.25 with
the engraved name, $7 without.
Special features this year include
individual pictures of Encina and
Village residents and a new section
devoted to freshman sports.
