# Miss Weed to Return Monday from Boston
## 
### 
Miss Luell A. Weed, associate
professor of physical education for
women, returns Monday from Bos
ton where she represented Stanford
at the annual Founder's Day exer
cises of Boston University.
The Founder's Day program was
held in connection with the conven
tion of the American Association for
Health, Physical Education, and
Recreation. Professor Weed is pres
ident of the Western division of the
association.
