# Red Cross Issues Call For Volunteer Knitters
## 
### 
A call for knitters to help the
Palo Alto Area Red Cross chapter
fill a newly assigned quota was is
sued yesterday by Mrs. J. B. Duck
worth, knitting chairman.
Volunteers are asked to make
socks for veterans' hospitals and
children's sweaters for Navajo In
dian school children. Yarn is fur
nished by the F!ed Cross and may
be obtained from the chapter house
at 400 Mitchell Lane in Palo Alto.
