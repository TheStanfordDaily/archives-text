# 'Streets of Laredo'
## 
### 
There'll be dancing in the streets
next Friday, free dancing, when
Farm students turn out in cowhidc
and ten-gallon hats for the freshman
free all-campus dance, "Streets of
Laredo," from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m.
The shindig, a date affair, will be
held in the circle between the Hoo
ver Library and Memorial Audito
rium. Jack White, freshman class
president, said the streets will be
decorated and scattered with con
cessions.
Bud Stone and his orchestra will
be stationed on the steps of Memo
rial Auditorium.
