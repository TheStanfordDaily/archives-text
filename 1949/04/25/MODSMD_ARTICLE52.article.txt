# Seniors to Sponsor Shangri-La Picnic
## 
### 
Beer, a barbecued dinner, base
ball. volleyball, and dancing are all
included in the $1.50 admission
price to the all-campus picnic this
Sunday at Shangri-La. Sponsored
by the class of '49, the affair is date
or no-date and begins at 1:30 in the
afternoon.
The games and beer will be avail
able all afternoon, and dinner will
be served at 5:30. At 6:30 the or
chestra will start playing.
The '49ers will sell tickets all
week at the ASSU office, and Wed
nesday, Thursday, and Friday in
front of the Cellar.
The traditional senior spring
quarter ice cream sale will be all
day Wednesday. Eskimo pies and
ice cream cups will be sold on Quad
and in all living groups until the

supply runs out or until 9 p.m.
Tickets to the picnic will also be
sold in the major living groups
thoughout the week, according to
co-chairman Betty White.
