# All Tickets Sold for Play
## 
### 
Although tickets for all perform
ances of ' The Petrified Forest" are
sold, persons wishing to see the play
may be able to get returned tickets
and uncalled - for reservations by
calling at the box office just before
curtain time.
Last-minute turnbacks will go on
sale at 8 o'clock each night of the
show, Mrs. Donna Millen, Stanford
Players' secretary, announced yes
terday.
"The Petrified Forest" by Robert
Sherwood will open a consecutive
four-night run in the Little Theater
at 8:15 tomorrow night.
