# Men Will Discuss Law with Vocom
## 
### 
Men's Vocom will stage its third
program of the quarter Thursday
night in the Encina lounge on the
subject of opportunities in the field
of law.
The speakers, as announced by
Pat De Young, Vocom chairman,
will be James Quinby and John Su
tro, members of local law firms, and
Dean Carl Spaeth of the Stanford
Law School.
All interested men are welcome
to the program.
