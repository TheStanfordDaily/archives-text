# Seniors Sell Ice Cream Bars Today
## 
### 
Today the seniors will sell ice
cream on Quad and in the evening
in the living groups until 9, 'an
nounced Mary Orr and Leo Schus
ter. co-chairmen of the sale. There
will be a choice of your favorite
flavor in Dixie Cups or Eskimo Pies.
Swimming, baseball, lots of food,
iree beer, and outdoor dancing, all
for the price of $1.50, are being of
fered to the campus Sunday by the
seniors. The occasion is the picnic
at Shangri-La (next door to Adobe
Creek Lodge) from 1:30 to 11 p.m.
This will be a date or no-date affair.
A barbecued dinner and beer will
be served at 5:30 p.m. Also there
will be lots of free beer all after
noon according to Betty White, pic
nic chairman.
Dancing to an orchestra on an
outdoor dance floor will begin at
(i:30 p.m. and continue until 11 p.m.
At $1.50 all this is a bargain in
any man's language, according to
the seniors. Tickets will be sold all
day for the remainder of the week
in the ASSU office and from 10 to 3
in front of the Cellar.
