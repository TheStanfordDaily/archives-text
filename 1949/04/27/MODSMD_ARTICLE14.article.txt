# Insurance Training Program Offered
## 
### 
Men students interested in life
insurance sales work may now take
advantage of new openings offered
by the Standard Insurance Co. This
firm, well established in the North
west, is offering training for men to
handle new territories being opened
up in California.
Interviews, to be given Monday,
are to be scheduled through the
Appointment Service in Temporary
Building C. Further information
on these opportunities is available
there.
