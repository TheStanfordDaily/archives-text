# Paintings, Handicraft Show at Art Gallery
## 
### 
An exhibition of paintings and
other* work by the members of the
art faculty of San Jose State Col
lege is now on display at the Art
Gallery. The show will remain open
till May 15.
In addition to paintings in vari
ous media, the exhibit includes
.sculpture, ceramic work, jewelry,
and hand-woven textiles.
