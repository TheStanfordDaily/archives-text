# Women Will Visit Row Houses Today
## 
### 
The Women's Row houses will
hold open house again today and
tomorrow. This will give freshman
and transfer women a chance to
make final inspection of these
houses before drawing for rooms
next week.
The houses will be open from 1:30
to 5:30 each afternoon. However.
Durand wil! not be open after 4
this afternoon, and Jordan will not
be open after 4 tomorrow. Hostesses
will be on hand to guide visitors
through the houses and answer any
questions.
