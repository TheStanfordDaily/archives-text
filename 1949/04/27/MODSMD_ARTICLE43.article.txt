# Magnolia Awarded Scholarship Cup
## 
### 
Casa Magnolia was awarded the
scholarship cup at AWS Cabinet
meeting yesterday afternoon.
Magnolia's average of 3.13 is one
of the highest ever recorded. Casa
Espanola came in second with an
average of 2.94, and Casa Olivo third
with an average of 2.9.
1 ne Oaks won the scholarship cup
last quarter with an average of
2.95. This quarter nine women's
residences had averages of 2.8 or
better.

A shamachie lectured at Stanford
on March 8, 1935.
