# 'Big Swim' to Have Seashore Theme
## 
### 
Encina will hold its last dance
of the year Saturday night from
9 to 1.
As its name implies, the "Big
Swim" will have a seashore atmos
phere to be supplied by marine dee
orations and couples attired in all
modes of beach clothes.
Hal Mead and his orchestra will
supply the music, and professional
and amateur entertainers will fill in
during the intermissions. Refresh
ments will also be served.
Encina social chairmen are sell
ing bids at 51.20 a couple.
