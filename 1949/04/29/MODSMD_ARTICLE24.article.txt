# Local Educators Will Meet Here
## 
### 
More than 200 local educators will
meet here tomorrow for the spring
conference of the Bay Area section
of the California Audio-Visual Edu
cation Association.
The conference will include dem
onstrations in the classroom uses of
picture mounting and projection,
radio, lantern slides, textiles, and
pottery. The conference will being
at 9:30 a.m. with an informal social
gathering in the School of Edu
cation Building, followed by a short
business meeting.
Participating as section leaders in
the hour-long demonstrations will
be Dr. William Iverson and Dr. Fan
nie Shaftel of the Stanford School
of Education faculty.
