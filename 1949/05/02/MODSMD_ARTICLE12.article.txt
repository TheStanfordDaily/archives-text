# Theta Sigma Phi To Hold Banquet
## 
### 
Theta Sigma Phi, women's pro
fessional journalism fraternity, will
hold its annual Matrix Table dinner
tonight at 6 in the Stanford Union,
president Paula Nusser stated.
Katherine Kerry, fashion com
mentator and novelist, and Ruth
Comfort Mitchell Young and Kath
leen Norris, novelists, will be guest
speakers.
Actives, pledges, and alumni will
be present. New pledges include
Sue Tolnay, Aljean Levin, Margie
Botts, Dee Keatley, and Joan Harz
feld, Miss Nusser said.
