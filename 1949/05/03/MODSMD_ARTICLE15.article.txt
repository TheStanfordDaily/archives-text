# Union Kitchen Worker Killed in Crash Sunday
## 
### 
Robert Louis Stocker, 53, a kitch
en worker at the Stanford Union,
was killed early Sunday morning in
a two-car collision at the Bayshore
Highway and Embarcadero Road
near Palo Alto.
Two other Stanford kitchen
workers were injured in the same
wreck. George Sima suffered deep
face cuts, while Sam Nelson is in
the Santa Clara County Hospital
with a possible fractured leg.
