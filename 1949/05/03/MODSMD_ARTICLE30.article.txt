# Musical Begins Friday
## 
### 
Peninsula Volunteers Inc. will
present 'The Golddiggers of 1949."
a musical extravaganza, at 8:30 Fri
day and Saturday evenings in the
Sequoia Union High School audi
tor ium.
Several Stanford actors are in
cluded among the cast of 140.

Tickets are on sale at Joseph
Magnin's in Palo Alto, and the Bank
