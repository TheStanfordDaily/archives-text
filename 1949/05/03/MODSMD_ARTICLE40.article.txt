# Senior Cruise Tickets Sell Thursday, Friday
## 
### 
Tickets for the senior moonlight
cruise will sell at the ASSU office
Thursday and Friday at $4.50 a
couple. Seniors have first prefer
ence on the tickets, but if there is
a surplus they will go on sale to the
rest of the campus.
The annual cruise around San
Francisco Bay is scheduled for Fri
day. May 13, from 8:30 until mid
night. Dancing and refreshments
will also be on the agenda.
