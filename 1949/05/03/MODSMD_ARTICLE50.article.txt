# STILL ANOTHER BOWL
## 
### 
JACKSONVILLE. May 2 (.-P) —
A new bowl game came into the
post-season football picture tonight.
To be known as the Senior Bowl,
the game will match two squads of
college seniiors and is scheduled in
the Gator Bowl the night of Dec. 20.
