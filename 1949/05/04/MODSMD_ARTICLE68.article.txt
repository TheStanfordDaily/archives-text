# Farm Women Play In Tennis Tourney
## 
### 
Stanford was represented in the
intercollegiate women's division of
the Ojai Invitational Tennis Tourna
ment ut Ojai. April 28 to 30. by
Martha Miller and Joan Symon in
the singles, and Dorothy Deatherage
and Nancy Kully in the doubles.
Miss Miller won her first round
but lost the second to the winner of
the tournament, Jean Doyle of Ari
zona. Miss Symon won her first two
rounds but lost in the semi-finals.
The doubles team also lost in the
semi-finals.
