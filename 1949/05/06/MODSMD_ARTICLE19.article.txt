# Pick-up Jobs Available At Employment Service
## 
###

has a large assortment of pick-up
jobs available to students wishing
to eaxn a little spending money in
their spare time.

The jobs, usually comprising only
a few hours' work, include every
thing from gardening to washing
dishes.
