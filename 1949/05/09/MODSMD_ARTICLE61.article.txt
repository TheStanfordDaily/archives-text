# Lemonade Sale to Aid Union Scholarship Fund
## 
### 
Special'y brewed lemonade will
be sold at 10 cents a cup at a booth
in front of the Cellar entrance to
Union Court from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.
The thirst - quencher will be
served by women from The Oaks
and Mariposa in order to support
the Union Scholarship Fund.
Sally Sears and Jane Hammond
are in charge of the sale.
