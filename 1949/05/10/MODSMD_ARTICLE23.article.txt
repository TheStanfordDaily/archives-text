# WAA Interviews Held This Week
## 
### 
Nominations and interviews for
officers on next year's WAA board
will be held tomorrow, Thursday,
and Friday.
Nominations may be made to
morrow in the lobby of the Wom
en's Gym.
Senior board members will be at
the gym Thursday from 9 to 12 a.m.
and 1 to 5 p.m. and Friday from 9
to 12 a.m. to discuss the requisites
and possibilities of the various posi
tions with those nominated and with
anyone else who is interested in
running for the offices.
Offices open now include presi
dent. vice - president, secretary,
treasurer, corresponding secretary,
publicity manager, senior class rep
resentative. junior class representa
tive, and sophomore class represen
tative.
