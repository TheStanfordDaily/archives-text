# Bookstore Rebates Due on Saturday
## 
### 
Saturduy noon is the clewline for
turning in rebate slips, student?
were reminded by the director o:
the Bookstore, Mrs. Helena T. Bau
chou. No cash register receipts will
be accepted after that time.
Rebates will be made on or be
fore June 10. Students leaving the
University before that time may
leave a self - addressed envelope
with the Bookstore and their rebate
checks will be mailed to them.
Bookstore hours are 8 to 5 week
days, 8 to 12 Saturday.
