# Poetry Contests Open to Students
## 
### 
Two * poetry competitions for
Stanford students are currently be
ing conducted by the English de
partment.
The Irene Hardy Poetry Prize of
$100 is offered for the best poem
written by an undergraduate. Com
petition for the Clarence Urmy Po
etry Prize of $60 is open to any
student, graduate or undergraduate,
registered at the University.
Closing date for entries in both
contests is May 27. Further infor
mation may be obtained from Mrs.
Ann B. Hayes, room 45, in the Eng
lish department.
