# False Propaganda

To the Editor:
We, students from Asia, have read with considerable pain and regret.
Mr.'"Jan Van Gindertaol's "exposition" and defense of the Dutch govern
ments-colonial policy in Indonesia.
These articles are full of such deliberate distortions and maliciously
false propaganda that we feel it would be an insult to the intelligence
oi the American readers and the freedom-struggle of the Indonesian
people to give any rebuttal.
However, we wish that the self-styled "expert" on world affairs would
confine himself to fields about which he knows something.
