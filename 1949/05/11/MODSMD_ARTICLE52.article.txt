# Daily Sports
## Gordon White Out Of Football Game 
### 
Friday's intra-squad football game
took a slight jolt yesterday when
it became known that Gordy White,
veteran tackle, will not be able to
play.
White was injured two weeks ago
when he was kicked on the spine.
While the injury was not painful,
the team doctors have ruled against
White's participation for the re
mainder of the spring practices.
