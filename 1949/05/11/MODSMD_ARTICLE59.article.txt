# Moonlight Ride, Formals to Highlight Weekend
## Baby Dance 
###

the arrival of a new baby , . . the 1
Sigma Nu Baby Dance, to be held
Saturday night from 9 to 1.
All baby lovers are urged to come
to the dance dressed in appropriate j
attire. Dick Saltzman and his horn
blowers will provide the entertain
ment for the aance.
