# Lemonade to Be Sold At Union Court Today
## 
### 
Women from The Oaks and Ma
drono will sell fresh lemonade from
10 a.m. to 3 p.m. at a special booth
in front of Union Court.
The lemonade sale is part of a
three-day campaign to raise funds
for the Union Scholarship.
Sally Sears and Jane Hammond
are in charge of the sale.
