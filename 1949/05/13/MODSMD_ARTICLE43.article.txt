# Angell Field Hosts Block 'S' Barbecue
## 
### 
The Block S Buek-of-the-Month
Club barbecue will be held at Angell
Field today at 6 p.m.
Highlighting the meeting will be
the presentation of the Dave De-
Swarte-Jaek Eller memorial trophy
to the most improved sophomore
football player of last year. Don
Campbell won the award last year.
Block S men still in school are
asked to wear their sweaters as proof
of membership.
