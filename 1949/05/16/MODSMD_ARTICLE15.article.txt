# Tickets to 'Gone with the Wind' At Mem Aud Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Tickets to the MGM production
"Gone With the Wind." which will
be shown at Memorial Auditorium
Saturday night as part of the Con
Home Drive, will go on sale this
morning at 9 in the ASSU office
lor 85 cents apiece.
Proceeds from the movie will go
to the Con Home Drive. The tickets
will sell all week. Fifteen hundred
tickets are available.
Clark Gable and Vivian Leigh

star in this epic of the South during
the Civil War.
The movie is being presented in
the Con Home this year in an effort
to give students who donate to the
drive something for their money,
said Bob Leefeldt. chairman of the
drive.
