# Traffic Accidents Injure Students
## 
### 
Two students were injured in
week-end traffic accidents in this
vicinity.
John McKnight, an Encina frosh,
suffered a cut on his chin when a
car driven by Howard Halbert, a
sophomore living at 680 Lowell Ave.
in Palo Alto, struck a telephone
pole on the Alpine Road at Bishop
Lane. The accident occurred Sun
day afternoon.
John B. Nelson, a Stanford stu
dent. received minor leg injuries
Sunday night when he was struck
by a car while crossing El Camino
Real at Menlo Park.
