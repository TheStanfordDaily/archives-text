# Phi Betes Pick 6% of Seniors
## 
### 
Eighty-six Stanford students in
the Class of '49 have been elected
to membership in Phi Beta Kappa,
national honorary scholastic society.
Membership in Phi Beta Kappa,
oldest fraternity in the United
States, is one of the highest aca
emic honors which can be con
ferred upon an undergraduate.
Members are selected on the basis
112 academic excellence and repre
sent the top six per cent of their
graduating class.

Eighty-six Stanford students in
the Class of '49 have been elected
to membership in Phi Beta Kappa,
national honorary scholastic society.
Membership in Phi Beta Kappa,
oldest fraternity in the United
States, is one of the highest aca
emic honors which can be con
ferred upon an undergraduate.
Members are selected on the basis
112 academic excellence and repre
sent the top six per cent of their
graduating class.
