# Alpine Club Will Show Classic Films Tonight
## 
### 
Two classic mountaineering mov
ies, "Daredevils of the Alps." and
"Tragedy of Mount Everest," will be
presented by the Alpine Club to
night ;it 7:30 in Cubberley Audito
rium.
"Daredevils of the Alps" records

est film shows scenes from the ex
pedition which was stopped by an
avalanche only 800 feet from the
summit of the world's highest peak.
