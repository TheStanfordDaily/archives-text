# DOUBLE VICTORY
## 
### 
Both the varsity and frosh base
baliers won their contests yester
day.the varsity clubbing their way
to a 7-5 win over St. Mary's while
the frosh were taking the measure
of San Francisco City College 11
to 3.
Bob Nava hit 3 tor 4, and Charlie
Cole poled a homer to right field to
hand Jack Heinen his first league
win of the year.
