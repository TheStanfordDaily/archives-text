# MURAL ANNOUNCEMENTS
## 
### 
No one out for a major sport
this quarter may participate in
coming intramural events, mural
director Chuck Taylor announced.
Today at noon is deadline time
for signing up for the intramural
track meet, he said. Track pre
lims are scheduled for Friday,
and all competitors should report
promptly at 4.
It is absolutely necessary that
each entrant be signed up in his
correct event before track time,
Taylor said.
