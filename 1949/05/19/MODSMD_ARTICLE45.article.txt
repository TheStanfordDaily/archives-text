HAWAIIAN CLUB meets at 7:30
tonight in the Women's Club
house to show movies of Hawaii
taken by United Airlines.

afternoon at Lathrop.

attend the business meeting of
the Chinese Club from 8 to 10 to
morrow evening at the Village
Clubhouse. Al! those who wish
to go on the spring picnic May 28
must be there.

bers will hold elections for 1949-
f)0 officers tonight in the third
floor conference room of the
Women's Clubhouse.

the Lotte Lehmann concert to
night should report to the Con
cert Series office between 1 and 3
p.m. today.

meet tonight at 7:30 in the Wom
en's Clubhouse. The meeting lasts
one hour and is open to all inter
ested students.
