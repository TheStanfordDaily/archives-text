# Ruble Barbecue
## 
### 
Residents of Ruble Hall will hold a
date or no-date party on Sunday.
A steak barbecue will be served
at the barbecue pit behind the Boat
house. Entertainment in Roble, fol
lowing the barbecue, will include
dancing and cards.

General chairman of the event is
Carol Clayter. Committee chairmen
include: Outdoor entertainment,
Saralee Canfield and Janice Ide; in
door entertainment, Alice Jones:
food, Nancy White and Ann Massey;
publicity, Tommie Craig; clean-up,
Noel Shoemaker.
