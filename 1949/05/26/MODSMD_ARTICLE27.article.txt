# Nanney Presents Holy Day Program
## 
### 
A special Ascension Day program
of organ music will be presented by
University Organist Herbert B.
Nanney at the organ recital to be
held at 4:15 this afternoon in Me
morial Church.
Featured on the program will be
three compositions by Johann Se
bastian Bach. They will be: "Today
the Son of God Triumphs," "Rejoice
Now. Christian Souls," and Sinfonia
to 'I Stand Before the Gates of
Heaven."
