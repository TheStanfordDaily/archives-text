# AWS Will Model Styles for Travel
## 
### 
"Accent on Travel,'' AWS fashion
show, will be held at 3:30 p.m. to
day in front of Casa Ventura. Pro
ceeds of the show will go to the
AWS scholarship fund.
Models from each women's resi
dence will show clothes from stores
in Palo Alto. Tickets may be ob
tained from any of the models or
at the show. Admission price is 50
cents.
Nancy McDermoth will supervise
the show, and Peggy Jenks is gen
eral chairman.
Ice cream will be sold during the
show.
Tickets will be sold at the gate.
Men are also welcome.
