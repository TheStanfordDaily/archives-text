# Military Ball
## 
### 
Scabbard and Blade has invited
all members of the ROTC, NROTC,
reserve and regular units, and all
campus friends to attend the Mili
tary Ball tomorrow night at the
Devonshire Club in San Carlos.
Ross Headley and his band will
provide music for the annual dance.
Tickets may . be obtained at $2.40 a
couple from any Scabbard and Blade
member, the ASSU Office, or the
military science department in the
SAC. They will also be sold at the
door.
Formal attire is acceptable, al
though uniforms are preferable.
Girls are to wear formals.
