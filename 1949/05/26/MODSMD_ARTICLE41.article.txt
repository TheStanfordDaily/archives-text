# Biz School Picnic
## 
### 
Tickets for the annual Business
School Spring Barbecue and Dance,
being held Friday at Adobe Creek
Lodge, arc now on sale at the Busi
ness School Library for $1.50 per
couple.
The program for the day includes
swimming, volleyball, and Softball
in the afternoon, a barbecue dinner
served at 6:30. and dancing under
the stars.
