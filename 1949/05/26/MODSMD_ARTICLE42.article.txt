# Philological Association To Hold Annual Dinner
## 
### 
Albert L. Guerard, Stanford pro
fessor emeritus of general litera
ture, will speak on "Decisive Books"
at the annual banquet of the Stan
ford Philological Association tonight
in the Garden Room of the Stan
ford Union.
Dr. Hazel D. Hansen, president of
the association, will preside at the
(5 o'clock banquet at which honor
will be paid to Dr. Stanley Astredo
Smith, professor of Romanic lan
guages. who will retire in Septem
ber. Professor Smith is a former
president of the association.
