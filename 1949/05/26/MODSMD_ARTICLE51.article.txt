# Lewis Smashes Larson 9-7, 6-3
## 
### 
Bob Lewis, fast-improving Indian
netman, scored one of die biggest
upsets in local net history when he
beat USF's ace left-hander Art Lar
sen in straight sets, 9-7, 6-3. on the
Golden Gate tennis courts.
This smashing net victory by
Lewis places him high among the
contenders for the NCAA cham
pionship to be held in Texas next
month. Larsen is ranked 13th in the
nation, above last year's NCAA title
holder, Harry Likas.
However. Lewis's great individual
effort was all in vain, as the Indian
netmen were edged by a great USF
team 5 to 4". Sam Match of the
Dorus easily defeated the Cards'
Kirke Mechem 6-0, 6-1, in the first
singles match.
Other results: Singles — Katten
(USF) def. Lycette (S), 5-7, 6-3,
6-1; Lowenbein (USF) def. F. Lew
is (S). 6-2. 6-3; Barbour (S) def.
Sample (USF) 3-6, 6-2, 6-3; Green
(USB') def. Schmieder (S) 1-6, 6-3
6-0.
