# MOOT COURT BOARD
## 
### 
Dudley J. Goul has been elected
chairman of next year's Stanford
Moot Court Board, it was announced
at the board's annual election last
week. Goul will take over from re
tiring chairman Bob Simpson.
Others elected to the board in
cluded David Lenihan, Armen Ham
par, Harry O. Van Potten. Leonard
J. Lewis, Lewis Fenton, Clifford L.
Duke Jr.. Edward C. Scoyen, and
Harlan K. Veal.
