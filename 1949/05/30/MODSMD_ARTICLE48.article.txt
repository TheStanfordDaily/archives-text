# Farm Tennis Squad Bops Outclassed Bears, 7 to 2
## 
### 
The Stanford tennis team finished
the season on the right note Satur
day as the Indians walloped Cali
fornia 7 to 2 on the home courts.
Kirke Mechem bested George
Gosler in the first match 6-3, 3-6,
6-3, and from there on the Bears
did not have a chance. Gosler was
the Bears' hope for a victory, and
when the Indian ace put him down
the Cards were on their way.
Bob Lewis made it 2 to 0 for the
Indians by blasting Sockolov in two
sets and the Cards were off to the
races.
Albert slowed the Indian netters
when he stopped Errol Lycette in
two sets to give the Bears their first
victory. The Bears had to wait until
the last double for their other win.
Fred Lewis and Lucien Barbour
won their singles matches, and
Vince Schmieder sewed up the
match with a victory in the sixth
singles.
The Lewis brothers teamed up to
take the first doubles, and Mechem
and Bob Forbes won the second

doubles to put the total at 7 to 1
for the Indians. California captured
me last doubles match, but by that
time the Cards had their sweep over
the Bears for the season.
