# Haynes Foundation Awards 3 Grants
## 
### 
Three $2,500 grants will be
awarded next year by the John Ran
dolph Haynes and Dora Haynes
Foundation. The purpose of the
grants-in-aid is to assist young
scholars in the fields of government,
economics, public law, history, so
ciology, and other social disciplines
in financing leaves of absence for
special research.
For the academic year 1949-50, the
three grants will be awarded to
young faculty members of California
universities and colleges nominated
by the heads of the social science
departments in these institutions.
