# Chief of Student Police Needed for Next Year
## 
### 
Those interested in the position of
chief of student police for next year,
1949-1950, will please apply in per
son on Friday at 4:15 in the Excom
room. Interviews will be held by
John Pool and John Lilienthal.
The position carries a salary of
$30 a month and entails the super
vision of all ASSU functions, with
the aid of a staff organized by the
chief. Short reports are required on
each function. It is necessary that
the person chosen be able to or
ganize about 20 student policemen
to be on call when needed.
