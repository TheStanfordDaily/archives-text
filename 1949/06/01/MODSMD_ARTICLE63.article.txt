# Closing Organ Recital To Be Held Tomorrow
## 
### 
The final organ recital in Memo
rial Church for the spring quarter
wiil be presented by Dr. Warren D.
Allen, assisted by Doriot Anthony,
flutist, tomorrow at 4:15 p.m.
The Stanford professor of music
and education will play pieces by
Bach. Handel, and Boellman.
Miss Anthony, flutist in the Los
Anyeles Philharmonic Orchestra
and a favorite soloist and chamber
music artist in Southern California,
will join with Dr, Allen in playing
Bach's "Sonata in E Minor for Flute
and Keyboard." Miss Anthony is
currently playing in the "Shvanda"
opera orchestra.
