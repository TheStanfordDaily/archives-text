# KZSU Banquet to Be Held Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
"Operation Whing-Ding"—other
wise known as the KZSU Party—
will be held at the Mountain View
American Legion Hall tomorrow
night immediately following the
Spring Sing.
All KZSU staff members should
pay the 50-cent admission fee in
advance to Gerry Winkler, Mary
Hill, Bob Gettys, or Chuck Black.
A special KZSU broadcast, written,
produced, and acted by eminent staff
members will be the feature of the
evening. Refreshments will be
served.
