# Hefter Elected Cardinals Prexy
## 
### 
Mickey Hefter was elected pres
ident of Cardinals for next \ear at
the Cardinals banquet last night.
Paula Smith is the new secretary,
and Pete Siusser was appointed Big
Game Homecoming chairman for
next fall. Downing McKee has
charge of the football movies, which
will be shown each week.
Both retiring and new members
lof Cardinals attended the banquet.
