# ENCINA STORAGE
## 
### 
Students who own goods stored
at Encina or Toyon before Septem
ber, 1948, are requested to claim
and remove them by June 10. Mrs.
Ellene Sumner, Encina resident di
rector, said that any stored items in
this category which arc not,claimed
will be given to the Convalescent
Home rummage sale.
