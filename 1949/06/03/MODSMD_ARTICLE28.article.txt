# Signup Date Set For Graduate Tests
## 
### 
The Graduate Record Examina
tion (profile tests and advanced
tests) will be given to candidates
for graduate work on Aug. 1 and 2
at Stanford.
All students planning to take the
test must register with Mrs. Anne
D. Severin, the examiner, before
noon on July 14. She will be in
Temporary Building C from 8:30
a.m. to 4:30 p.m., Monday through
Friday, and from 8:30 a.m. to 12
noon on Saturdays.
A $10 examination fee will be
charged on the day of the test.
Registration material can be ob
tained from the examiner.
