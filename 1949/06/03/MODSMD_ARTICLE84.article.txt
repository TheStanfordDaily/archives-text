# Electronics Program Open to Undergrads
## 
### 
The United States Navy is offer
ing a summer work program to un
dergraduate students majoring in
electronics.
The students would be employed
to do research, testing, development,
and aircraft overhaul for the Naval
Air-Missile Test Center at Poinl
Mugu, Calif.
Men interested should contact the
Student Employment Office, room
10, Temporary Building C, for fur
ther details.
