# Merton of Columbia Will Not Lecture Here
## 
### 
Professor Robert K. Merton of
Columbia University will not be on
campus this summer as visiting pro
fessor of sociology as announced in
the Summer Quarter Bulletin.
His place will be taken by Pro
fessor Paul A. F. Walter Jr. (Ph.D.,
Stanford, '38), professor of sociol
ogy and chairman of the depart
ment at the University of New
Mexico, who will teach two courses:
Sociology 113, Collective Behavior,
and Sociology 150. Race and Cul
tural Relations.
