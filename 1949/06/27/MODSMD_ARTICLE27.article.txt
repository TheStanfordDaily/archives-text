# Encina Commons Meal Schedule Announced
## 
### 
Encina Commons is open this
summer to accommodate the 400-
odd residents of the hall, most of
whom will eat in the hall, and
their guests.
"No non-resident will be served
unless he is the legitimate guest
of some person who has a meal
ticket." said Jean Conner, direc
tor of the dining hall. Guests
must be arranged for in advance
at the Commons' office, the hours
of which are 8 to 11:30 a.m. and
1 to 5 p.m.
Hours for the meals, which will
be served on the east line only
are: breakfast, 7 to 7:30 (or a
snack from 8 to 8:30); lunch, 12 to
12:45; and dinner, 5:45 to 6:30.
