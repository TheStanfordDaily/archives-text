# Encina Commons Meal Schedule Announced
## 
### 
Eiuina Commons is open this
slimmer to accommodate the 400-
odd residents of the hall, most of
whom will eat in the hall, and
their guests.
"No non-resident will he served
unless he is the legitimate guest
of some person who has a meal
ticket." said Jean Conner, direc
tor of the dining hall. Guests
must he arranged for in advance
at the Commons' olticc, the hours
of which are X to 11:30 a.m. and
1 to 5 p.m.
Hours for the meals, which will
he served on the east line only
are: breakfast, 7 to 7:30 (or a
snack from S to 8:30); lunch, 12 to
12:15; and dinner, 5:45 to 0:30.
Only two meals will he served on
Sunday—a brunch from 9:45 to
10:30. and dinner at the usual
time.
