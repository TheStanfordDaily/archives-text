# Cashier Announces Tuition Deadlines
## 
### 
Fines will be imposed for pay
ment of room, board, or tuition
bills after the July 6 deadline, the
cashier's office announced yester
day.
Students on a deferred tuition
plan must make their regular pay
ments on or before the scheduled
date or similar penalties will be
laid upon the delinquent.
The cashier's office is in the lobby
of Encina Hall.
