# Moe, Kellberg Out of Ames Match
## 
### 
Stanford golfers Bud Moe and-
Ernie Kellberg were defeated in the
first round of NCAA play at Ames,
Iowa, Wednesday.
Only two Bay Area men stayed in
the national collegiate play. Both of
them are from San Jose State.
The Ames course par was broken
for the first time in years in the
qualifying round. Both Moe and
Kellberg qualified with scores sev
eral strokes above par.
Gardner Dickinson of Louisiana
State defeated Moe 2 and 1. and Bob
Olson of Michigan defeated Kell
berg 1 up.
