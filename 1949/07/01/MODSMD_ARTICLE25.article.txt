5, will feature "Latin America
and the Bold New Program." Ar
thur P. Whitaker is the speaker.
Time: 8 p.m.
STANFORD BRIDGE CLUB will
meet Tuesday evenings at 7:15
throughout summer quarter. A
duplicate bridge match will be
held efich week downstairs in the
Women's Clubhouse of the Un
ion.
DAILY BUSINESS STAFF will
meet Tuesday afternoon at 5 in
the ofTice at the Daily Shack. Old
and new members are requested
to attend.
DAILY EDIT STAFF will meet
Tuesday at 2 p.m. at the Shack.
All old and new staff members
are requested to attend.
