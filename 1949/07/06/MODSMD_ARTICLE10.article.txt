# Room, Board Bills Deadline Is Today
## 
### 
Today is the deadline for pay
ment of room and board bills for
students living in campus residences
during summer quarter, it was an
nounced by Controller Duncan I.
McFadden.
Bills will be subject to late fine
after Wednesday.
The Cashier's Office in the lobby
of Encina Hall is open from 8 a.m.
to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday.
The office is closed on Saturday.
At the same time McFadden an
nounced that during the summer
the Cashier's Office at the Stanford
Village would be. open weekdays
from 9:30 a.m. to noon for payment
of Village apartment rentals.
