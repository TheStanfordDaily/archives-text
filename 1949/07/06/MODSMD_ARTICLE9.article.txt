# Vets' Credit Expires At Bookstore Aug. 5
## 
### 
Deadline for Stanford veterans
to charge books and supplies at
the Bookstore under the GI Bill
during summer quarter is Aug. 5,
George D. Barahal, veterans' co
ordinator, announced yesterday.
No supplies may be obtained
after Che closing date. Identifica
tion cards, used by veterans un
der Public Laws 16 and 346. are
good until Aug. 5.
