# Phone Requests Hit Air on KZSU
## 
### 
An all - request record program
will be aired for the first time to
night from 7 to 11 over campus ra
dio station KZSU.
"On 'Request Time'," said Nancy
Toll, station publicity director, "we
will receive phone calls all evening
and will play records at the request
of the person who phones in."
"All you do," she continued, "is
call DA 2-3971 and we do the rest."
