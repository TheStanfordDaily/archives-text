# Col. Henry Barret To Head Air ROTC
## 
### 
Colonol Henry L. Barrett has been
appointed professor of air science
and tactics at Stanford. He will be
in charge of the training program
for Air ROTC students here.
He has been public information
officer of the First Air Force at Slo
cum Air Force Base New Rochelle,
N.Y., since March 1948, and is its
former deputy chief of staff.
