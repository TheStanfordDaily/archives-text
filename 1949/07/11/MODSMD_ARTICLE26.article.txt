# Letters to The Editor
## 
### 
To the Editor:
I am a J.C. transfer and have
"been around"' a good deal. I
worked on the "rag" at J.C. and 1
feel qualified to give you a few
"pointers" on how to make your
"sheet" a little more "snappy."'
One good "trick" I "think" would
be to have a "movie," or "cinema"'
review sometimes, maybe. Why
don't you try this out? Give it a
"whirl" and see if you get any
"results."
A J.C. TRANSFER
P.S.—I think the "Bull Session" is
"lousy."
