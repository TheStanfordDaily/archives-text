# Hilton Will Speak On Latin America
## 
### 
A first-hand report of conditions
in Latin America will be given by
Professor Ronald Hilton of Stan
ford in a Hispanic Series lecture at
8 p.m. Wednesday in Cubberley
Auditorium.
A discussion period, led by Ar
thur P. Whitaker, professor of Latin
American history at the University
of Pennsylvania, who is a visiting
member of the Stanford faculty this
summer, will follow the lecture.
Professor Hilton returned from
South America late in June. lie
visited most of the South American
countries on his trip, but he will
discuss primarily current affairs in
Brazil and Argentina, the countries
where he spent most of his time.
