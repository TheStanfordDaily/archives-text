# Grad Record Exam Signup Tomorrow
## 
### 
Candidates for graduate work
may take the Graduate Record Ex
amination (Profile Tests and Ad
vanced Tests) which will be given
at Stanford Aug. 1 and 2.
Students who plan to take the
test should register with Mrs. Anne
D. Severin before noon tomorrow.
She is in Temporary Building C
from 8 to 10 a.m. and from 3 to 4
p.m. today and between 8 and 10
tomorrow morning.
The examination fee is $10, pay
able on the day of the testing. This
fee entitles the candidate to take the
eight Profile Tests and one Ad
vanced Test. It also entitles him to
have a transcript of the test rec
ord sent to three institutions.
