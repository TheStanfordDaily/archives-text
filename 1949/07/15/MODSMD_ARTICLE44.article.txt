# Gonzalez Parries Clay Court Threat

CHICAGO, July 1-4 (UP). —De-
fending champion Richard Gonzalez
of T,os Angeles turned back the
challenge of Arthur Larson, San
Francisco, today in a national clay
courts tennis quarterfinal. 3-6, 6-4,
1-6. fi-4. 6-1.
Second-seeded Frank Parker of
Los Angeles ran over Jack Tuero
of Now Orleans, 6-1, 6-0. 6-2.
Victor Seixas of Philadelphia,
seeded third, defeated Fred Kova
leski, Detroit, 4-6. 6-2. 6-4, 6-1.
Fourth-seeded Herb Flam. Bev
erly Hills, won his quarterfinal
match with Hugh Stewart, Pasa
dena, 6-2. 7-5, 6-2.
