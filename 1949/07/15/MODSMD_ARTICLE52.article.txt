# Bacon, Moeller To Give Concert
## 
### 
Ernst Bacon, pianist - composer,
and Lillian Moeller, lyric soprano,
will appear in a concert July 19 at
Stanford as a feature of the Tues
day Evening Series.
The concert, which will begin at
8 p.m. in Memorial Hall, will be
presented by the Stanford Commit
tee on Public Exercises and is open
to the public without charge.
Mr. Bacon, resident artist and
professor of composition at the
school of music at Syracuse Univer
sity, is a visiting member of the
Stanford music department during
the summer quarter.
Mr. Bacon's last appearance at
Stanford was in January when he
appeared in a joint recital with his
wife, 'cellist Analee Camp.
