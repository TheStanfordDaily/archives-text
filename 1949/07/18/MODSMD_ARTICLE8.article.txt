# Herbert Hoover Visits The Stanford Campus
## 
### 
Former President Herbert Hoo
ver visited the Stanford campus
Thursday, consulting briefly with
Dr. Wallace Sterling, University
president; Dr. II. II. Fisher, chair
man of the Hoover Institute and
Library; and other Hoover Li
brary officials.
Mr. Hoover left after his visit
here for the Bohemian Grove to
take part in the annual encamp
ment of the Bohemian Club of
San Francisco.
