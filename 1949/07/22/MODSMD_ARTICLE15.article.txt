# Grad Writes Book On News Reporting
## 
### 
Dr. Laurence C. Campbell, a for- I
mer Stanford University student, is
co-author of "Newsmen at Work,"
which has just been published by
Houghton Mifflin Company.
The role of the reporter for the
American press and radio is exam
ined fully in the new work by Dr.
Campbell and Professor Roland E.
Wolseley.
"Newsmen at Work" is the first
basic analysis of newsgathering and
newswriting to examine at length
other news media besides the news
paper, according to a publisher's
press release. Radio, magazines,
house publications, business papers,
syndicates, and wire services are
discussed. Stressed also, says the
release, is the reporter's social role.
