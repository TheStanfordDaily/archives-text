# Letters to the Editor
## 
### 
To the Editor:
Pertaining to Miss Haekett's letter and her query regarding honesty on
the Stanford campus:
Perhaps high school students took the skirts from her automobile, but
ran they be blamed for "borrowing" my School Administration book
(Moehlinan)? My name is inscribed inside the cover so there shouldn't
be a question of ownership involved.
A STANFORD STUDENT
