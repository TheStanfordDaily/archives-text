# Johnston to Speak Sunday on 'Habits'
## 
### 
Chaplain Johnston will deliver his
sermon, "Habits of a Christian," at
the service in Memorial Church
next Sunday. The Summer Choir
under Harold C. Schmidt will sing
'Now God Be Praised from Heaven
Above" and Vulpius' "Kyrie Elei
son" and "Gloria" from"The Mass
of St. Hugh."
For the last Sunday service of
July, Dr. Johnston's topic will be
"Science and Religion."
Wednesday night the regular
candlelight service will be held.
Thursday at 4:15 p.m. Herbert B.
Nanney, University organist, will
play a program of eighteenth cen
tury organ music.
