# Vets Must Avoid Law Test Fee by July 29
## 
### 
All veterans attending Stanford
under the provisions of Public
Law l(i who arc required to take
the Law School Admission Test
on Aug. (> arc urged to see the
Veterans Administration Train
ing Officer in room 40, Temporary
Building C, prior to July 29 to
arrange for avoidance of the test
fee.
The Stanford office has been in
formed that if prior arrangements
are not made by that date the
veteran will be required to de
fray the cost of the test person
ally.
