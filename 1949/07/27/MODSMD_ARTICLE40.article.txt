# ‘I’ Club to Present Cultural Program
## 
### 
The International Club will pre
sent a cultural show Sunday night
at 7:30. It will take place in the
social room on the third floor of the
Ed Building.
Motion pictures and musical re
cordings from India, China, Turkey,
Brazil, and the Philippine Islands
will comprise the program of the
show, to which students, faculty
members, and the general public
are cordially invited.
