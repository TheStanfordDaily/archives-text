# Snead Sets Western Open Record with 268
## 
### 
ST. PAUL, Minn., July 31 (UP).
—Unrelenting Sammy Snead never
gave the others a chance today as
he shot a five-under-par 67 to win
the $15,000 Western Open Golf
Championship in a record 268
strokes.
The cool White Sulphur Springs,
W.Va., ace clipped 20 strokes off par
for his 72-hole total.
