# U.S. Civil Service Exams Announced
## 
### 
The U.S. Civil Service Commis
sion announced yesterday a libra
rian examination for positions in
various federal agencies in Wash
ington, D.C., and vicinity.
Persons appointed from this ex
amination will perform or direct the
performance of work in federal li
braries. In some instances, they
will have full administrative re
sponsibility for the functioning of a
library and for the activities of the
library staff.
No vvritten test will be given

competitors. To qualify they must
have had four years of education or
experience in library work, plus
additional professional library ex
perience.
j Further information and applica
tion forms may be obtained from
i the commission's local secretary,
Lawrence Davick, at the U.S. Post
Office Building, Waverley and Ham
ilton, Palo Alto. •
Applications must be received in
j the Commission's Washington of-
I fice not later than Aug. 23.
