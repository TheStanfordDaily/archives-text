# Music Department Will Present Piano And Chamber Music Recitals at Knoll
## 
### 
Two music programs will be pre
sented by the Stanford music de
partment in the next week at the
Knoll. -
Wednesday there will be a piano
recital by Miss Eve Crossman. Miss
Grossman is from New York and
has studied at the Juilliard School
of Music, Syracuse University, and
Stanford University. She has been
studying composition and piano
under Ernst Bacon, visiting profes
sor of music this summer.
She will play selections from
Bach, Beethoven, Mozart, and De
bussy.
The program will begin at 8 p.m.

and is open to the public.
On Aug. 16 at 8 p.m. there will
be a recital by the chamber music
class under the direction of Mr.
Bacon.
The recital will be held in the
ballroom of the Knoll, and it is open
to the public.
