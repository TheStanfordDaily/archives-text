# 'I' Club to Sponsor Picnic Saturday
## 
### 
The T" Club is sponsoring a pic
nic at Seacliff Beach Saturday from
10 a.m. to 5 p.m. Cars will leave
from the front of Hoover Library at
10.
The price is $1.50, which will buy
lunch and recreation. Those inter
ested should sign up with Willard
Waterous by Wednesday in room
822, Temporary Building B. Those
who have already signed up and
those who plan to do so should
leave notice of whether they can
provide transportation.
"Bring your ukes, guitars, ac
cordions. and other musical instru
ments along," the"I" Club re
quested.
