# LARRY FINDS HIS BIKE
## 
### 
Larry Bacon, the 11 - year - old
whose bicycle was stolen last week,
got it back yesterday.
The police found it parked in
back of the Post Office.
A thief broke through the bar
riers of the driveway at Manzanita,
where Larry lives, and stole the
bike which Larry had bought with
money he earned himself.
