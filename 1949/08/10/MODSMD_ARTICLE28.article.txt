# Frosh Plan Picnic At Adobe Creek
## 
### 
Adobe Creek will be the scene of
the All-Frosh Picnic, to be held on
the afternoon and evening of Aug.
1!). Sponsored by third- and fourth
floor Encina freshmen, the affair is
open to rill freshmen living on these
floors, either stag or with dates.
Afternoon activities will include
swimming, tennis, softball, football,
and volleyball. At 6 p.m a dinner
of wieners, potato salad, and soft
drinks will be served, and the eve
ning will be spent dancing under
the stars.
Those wishing to attend the pic
nic are reminded to sign up on the
bulletin boards in Encina. Trans
portation will be provided.
