# Klinker New President Of Education Council
## 
### 
Bill Klinker won the race for
president of the Education Council
over his rival. Frank Fiscalini, by
a count of 95-05.
Vice-president of the council will
be Lois Croonquist, who polled 105
votes to 4(5 for the next contender,
Willard Hillyar.
The other offices: secretary, Lois
Parker; treasurer, Ken Pasqualetti;
social hairman, Shirley Plaisted;
publicity chairman, Joan Maxwell.
