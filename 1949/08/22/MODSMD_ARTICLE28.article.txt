# Elephant Breaks Actress's Ribs
## 
### 
HOLLYWOOD, Aug. 21 (UP).—
A friendly pat given an elephant
resulted today in two broken ribs
for French actress Denise Dai cel.
Working in a jungle picture, Miss
Darcel gave an elephant a friendly
pat but the animal staggered and
threw the actress off balance and
onto a pile of rocks.
