# Debate Members To Discuss Plans For Tournament
## 
### 
Old members of the Debate So
ciety will hold their first meeting of
the year tomorrow evening at 7:30
in room 210 of the History Building.
Freshmen and new debators will be
notified soon regarding their partici
pation in new debate activities.
The first of the society's projects
will be the Western Speech Associ
ation tournament to be held at
Stanford November 21 through 23.
All Pacific Coast schools will be in
vited to the speech tournament.
Subject of the debate is "Re
solved, that the United States should
establish the Marshall Plan for the
Orient." In addition to the debate,
Tom Brand, debate manager, said
there will be divisions in extempo
raneous and oratorical speaking.
