# Med College Test Applications Due Soon at Princeton
## 
### 
Applications for the Medical Col
lege Admission Test must be re
ceived at the Princeton office of the
Educational Testing Scrvice on or
before Oct. 8.
Only applicants for admission to
classes beginning the study of med
icine in the fall of 1950 should take
the test which will be given on Oct.
22.
Although the test will be given
again in January, all candidates are
urged to arrange to take the test
in October, so that records will be
made available immediately to med
ical schools.
Application blanks and copies of
the Bulletin of Information may be
obtained from the Medical Admis
sions Office, Temporary Building A,
room 40, or direct from Educational
Testing Service, P.O. Box 592,
Princeton, New Jersey.
