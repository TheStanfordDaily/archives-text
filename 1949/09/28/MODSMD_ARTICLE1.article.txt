# This Morning's Headlines
## Sproul Speech 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Sept. 27 (/b-Dr.
Robert Gordon Sproul, president of
the University of California, told
new students on the UCLA campus
today:
"You are students at one of the
most academically free universities
in the country."
"The University of California was
started as a center for scientific re
search and a hotbed of material
ism," the president said, adding that
a university must be built upon a
"foundation of truth."
Provost Clarence A. Dykstra said
15,016 students are registered for
the current semester, an all - time
high at UCLA.
