# New Physics Libe Opened
## 
### 
A new Physics Library with ma
terial for graduate students in the
Physics and Electrical Engineering
Departments has taken up quarters
in Rooms 405 and 400 of the Physics
Building.
The library opened summer quar
ter. It occupies the two large rooms
formerly used for the microwave
laboratory, which has moved into
the new Microwave Building.
The rooms have been completely
redecorated, shelving has been in
stalled, and there is seating space
for about 30 students.
The library will be open Monday
through Friday from 9 to 12 a.m. 1
to 5 p.m., and 7 to 10 p.m. Satur
days it is opened from 9 to 12 in the
morning.
The library is under the new
physical sciences and engineering
division of the Stanford Library and
is under the supervision of Miss
Augusta Houseman.
