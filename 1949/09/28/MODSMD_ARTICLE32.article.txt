# Michigan Tickets Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Tickets for the Stanford-Michigan
football game here Saturday will go
on sale at the Pavilion at 8:30 a.m.
Sale will continue on Thursday and
Friday from 8:30 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Student body cards must be pre
sented at the ticket window. Price
of the tickets is $1.20.
Tickets for the UCLA game Octo
ber 8 will also be distributed at
that time. There will be no charge,
but a ticket must be obtained for
admission to the game.
Student rooters' tickets are not
transferable, and will not be sold at
the Stadium.
Sale of tickets for other games
will be held October 5, 6, and 7.
