p.m.

at 7 in the TV room in Toyon.

p.m.

at 7:15 in the lower lounge of the

needs planners and secretaries for

Quad will be taken today and

will meet today at 4:15 p.m. in
the choir room of the Memorial
Church. All who signed up, and
any others interested, are urged to
attend.

will meet at Belt's at 5 p.m. today.

climbing at Miraloma this Sun

day. Meet in front of the Quad at

nis shoes. Everyone is invited.

ushers will meet at 4:15 p.m. to

the Nitery. Anyone interested is
invited to attend.
