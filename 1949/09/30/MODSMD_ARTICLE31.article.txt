# Coeds' Volleyball Signups Due Today
## 
### 
Women's Intramural Volleyball
team signups are due at 2 p.m. today.
Games will begin Tuesday night at
7:15 in the Women's Gym, according
to Barbara Metzgej* and Margaret
Pottinger, volleyball managers.
Sign-up slips may be returned to
the gym and should be placed in
the designated box. Teams set to
play this Tuesday will be notified by
phone over the weekend.
Games are scheduled for Tuesday
and Thursday nights. Houses should
state their preference. All residences
will receive final by mail as to what
teams and what time they will be
playing.
