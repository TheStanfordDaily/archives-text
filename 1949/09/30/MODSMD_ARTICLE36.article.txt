Freshmen will hear their own
voices and those of their friends,
transcribed from last Saturday's
Frosh Jolly-up, on a special show to
be aired at 7:30 p.m. Sunday.
The broadcast will feature inter
views tape - recorded during the
dance. Entertainment includes the
Theta Chi Hawaiian Ukulele Quar
tet and the unusual piano of Bill
Bowles.
At 9:30 Claude Rosenberg's exclu
sive interview with Athletic Direc
tor Fritz Crisler of Michigan, re
corded yesterday afternoon at the
Oakland Airport, will provide a
post-game treat for sport fans.
