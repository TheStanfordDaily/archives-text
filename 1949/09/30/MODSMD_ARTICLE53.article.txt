# T.S. Barclay Visits Michigan Campus
## 
### 
Thomas S. Barclay, Stanford pro
fessor of political science, is visit
ing professor at the University of
Michigan at Ann Arbor for the first
semester of the 1949-50 year. Pro
fessor Barclay left early this month
and will return for the spring quar
ter. During February and March he
will study in Washington, D.C.
Taking Barclay's place in the Po
litical Sciense Department is Pro
fessor Hugh A. Bone from the Uni
versity of Washington at Seattle.

The versatile helicopter lias found
a new rtle, that of stocking remote
lakes with game fish.
