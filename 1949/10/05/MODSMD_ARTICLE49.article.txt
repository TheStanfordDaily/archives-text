# Vet Construction Loans Available
## 
### 
Loans for the construction of
homes may now be obtained by eli
gible California veterans under the
terms of a newly enacted progress
payment plan.
Veterans' . applications for con
struction loans will be accepted by
the State Department of Veterans'
Affairs on the basis of approved
plans and specifications. However,
since delays are unavoidable at the
start of the new program, veterans
not in a rush to build are asked to
wait a month or two before apply
ing.
Full information about the plan
may be obtained from offices of the
Department of Veterans' Affairs at
515 Van Ness Avenue, San Fran
cisco; 2129 Grove Street, Oakland;
or 852 The Alameda, San Jose.
