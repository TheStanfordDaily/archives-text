# Church Recital To Present Bach
## 
### 
University Organist Herbert B.
Nanncy will present his regular or
ean recital this afternoon from 4:15
to 4:45 in Memorial Church.
Works to be played are: Krebs'
Prelude and Fuguet in E Minor;
Bach's "Allein Gott in der Hoeh sei
Ehr'" arid Prelude and Fugue in E
Minor; Eranck's Pastorale; Haydn's
Allegretto; and Widor's Allegro Vi
vace from his Filth Symphony.
