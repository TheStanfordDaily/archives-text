# Class of '51 Plans Hard Times Dance
## 
### 
Plenty of beer, dancing, enter
tainment, and n mero'-s door prizes
will be on hand lor juniors at the
Harvest Moon Hard Times Dane
at Mangini'u next Friday.
Sponsored by the class ol '51, tin
dance will start after the rally foi
ihe Oregon State game and wil! 1. si
until kick-off time.
Junior men and women arc in
vited to come no-date, and lot' of
transportation will be provided frot
(he Pivilion to Mangini's and back
' again Inter on to meet lock-outs.
