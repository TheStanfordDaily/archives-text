# WEISSTEIN TO START
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Oct. 13 l/P)
Sophomore Julian Weisstein in a
surprise move was handed the start
ing role today as quarterback for
UCLA against Santa Clara here
Saturday.
Coach Red Sanders said it was a
reward for good work in practice
the past week and creditable show
ing previously. Regular Jim Bu
chanan will be on deck for duty
when needed.
Lone doubtful participant is tackle
Roy Janson.
