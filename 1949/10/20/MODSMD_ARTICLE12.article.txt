# 'Meet Your Team' Is Theme of OSC Rally
## 
### 
A 'Meet Your Team" rally will
ho held tomorrow night before the
Stanford-Oregon State game. The
rally will begin at 7:30 p.m. in the
Pavilion.
The purpose of the rally is to
introduce individual members to
the student bodv, as well as to
honor the team as a whole. The
team will march in one by one
through a spotlight, while Dean
Borba and his assistants lead yells
and songs.
The Stanford Hand, fresh from
its Washington trip, will be on
hand to furnish peppy music for
the rally. The song leaders will
also be present.
