# New Officers Elected To Village Council, VAC
## 
### 
New officers have been elected by
the Stanford Village Council and
the Village Activities Committee.
Chosen Council President was
Gordon Dane; vice-president, Ralph
Brogdon; treasurer, Alan Reed; and
secretary, Eleanor Sullivan.
Ralph Brogdon was elected also to
top position in the VAC in order to
facilitate co-ordination between the
two groups. Chuck Miller was un
animously named secretary - treas
urer of this committee.
