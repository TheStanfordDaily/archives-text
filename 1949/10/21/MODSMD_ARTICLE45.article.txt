# AWS to Host College Guests
## 
### 
Women student leaders in col
leges with which Stanford is sched
uled to play football games here
have been invited to spend the
weekend as guests of the Stanford
AWS, it was decided at AWS meet
ing yesterday.
The vice-president of the student
body and the president of the AWS
of the colleges whose teams will be
playing in the Stanford Stadium
will be given a bed and meals in
one of the women's living groups
the weekend she is here.
Each guest will be hostessed by
the president of the bouse or other
officers. It is hoped to further inter
college relations by this move.

meeting and it was hoped that ill 1
