# Encina Formal
## 
### 
Blue champagne will flow freely
tomorrow night from 9 to 1 in En
cina lounge when the social event
of the quarter for the class of '53
takes place. Serenading the frosh at
the Blue Champagne formal will be
Howard Fredric and his Band of
Gold.
Tickets are available from fresh
man wing social chairmen until 11
this morning, and also at the door.
Although it is a formal, any suit will
be appropriate for the men.
