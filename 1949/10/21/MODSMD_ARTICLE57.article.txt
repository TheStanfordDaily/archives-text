# Quad Deadline Today For Biz School Photos
## 
### 
Today is the last day Business
School pictures will he taken for
the 1950 Quad. The Quad office
will be open from 10 to 12 a.m. and
from 1 to 5 p.m. No pictures can
he taken after 5 o'clock.
Monday and Tuesday of next
week pictures will be taken of the
School of Education for the senior
section of the Quad.
