# Candlelit Church Service Tonight
## 
### 
The weekly Candlelight Service
will be held in Memorial Church to
night from 9 to 9:30.
The student-conducted devotional
is an informal service of prayer and
meditation with organ music and
student reading of the lessons and
prayers.
Anyone wishing to participate in
the service should contact Ron Han
del, head of the Candlelight Serv
ice Committee, at the Zeta Psi
house.
