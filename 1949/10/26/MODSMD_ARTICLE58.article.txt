# Church Group to Give Halloween Fete Friday
## 
### 
All Stanford students arc invited
to a Halloween party to be given
Friday at 8 p.m. by the Westminster
Fellowship of the First Presbyterian
Church in Palo Alto.
The party will be of the usual
Halloween type with ghosts, gob
lins, cider, and apples.
