# Church News
## Bishop Campbell To Talk Sunday 
### 
Guest preacher at All Saints'
Church, Wavcrley at Hamilton,
Sunday will be the Right Reverend
Donald B. Campbell. Bishop Camp
bell is the Bishop of the Diocese of
Los Angeles and will speak at the
11 a.m. service.
In the evening Bishop Campbell
will be a guest of the Canterbury
Club at a buffet supper. Following
the dinner, he will talk to the group
at the meeting.
All Episcopalians from Southern
California are especially urged to
attend the service and dinner in
order to welcome their bishop to
Stanford.
