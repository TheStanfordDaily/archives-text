# Frosh To Rally At Pajamarino
## 
### 
Pajama-clad freshmen will as
semble at Encina at 7 tonight for
the traditional pre-SC weekend Pa
jamarino.
The Stanford Band will accom
pany the male members of the class
of '53 from Encina to Roble where
there will be a rally in front of the
women's hall.
After this rally, the Roble women,
clad in skirts and pajama tops, will
accompany the men back to Encina
where there will be dancing until
10 p.m. Music will be recorded.
