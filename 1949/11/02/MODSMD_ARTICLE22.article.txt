# Farm Books Listed Among Year's Best
## 
### 
Two Stanford University Press
books, "This Is My America," by
Max Savelle, and "This Fortunate
Man," by Edward De Witt Taylor,
have been listed in a collection of
the 50 best Western books of the
year.
The books, selected by the Rounce
and Coffin Club of Los Angeles, are
now on exhibit in the main Library.
"Religion of No Religion," by Dr.
Frederic Spiogclberg, professor of
Asiatic and Slavic languages, is also
included as an outstanding Western
publication during the past year.
Dr. Spiegelberg's book was pub
lished by the Greenwood Press ol
San Francisco.
