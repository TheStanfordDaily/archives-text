# Library Presented Stanford Message
## 
### 
A penciled note from Governor
Leland Stanford to the police chief
of San Francisco has been added to
the Stanford Library historical col
lection.
The brief letter was written late
in October, 1877, at the height of
riotous meetings being conducted
by the incendiary labor leader, Den
nis Kearny.
The note is among historical pa
pers given to the library by Mrs.
Dorothy Lyman from the late Dr.
Lyman's collection of Californiana,
considered the largest private col
lection of its kind in the state.
