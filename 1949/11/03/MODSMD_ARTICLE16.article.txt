# Debaters Choose Brown as Captain
## 
### 
At a meeting held this week the
Stanford Debating Society elected
Townsend Brown to be this year's
debate captain and Pete Slusser to
represent the Debate Society on the
Debate Council.
Plans were made for the partici
pation of the society in the Western
Speech Asociation Tournament
which is to be held in the Stanford
campus November 21, 22, and 23.
Events in the tournament will in
clude: debate, oratory, interpretive,
and impromptu.
Tom Brand, debate manager,
urges that all students interested in
representing the school at the tourn
ament or in being judges sign up
with him at the debate manager's
office, Room 1, Stanford Union.
