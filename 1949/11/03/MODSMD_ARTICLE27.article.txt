# Quad Office Slates More Senior Photos
## 
### 
Seniors in the School of Hu
manities and Sciences whose
names begin with the letters F
through L will have their pictures
taken for the last time today.
Those whose names begin with M
through R will be taken tomor
row and Monday.
Any seniors who have missed
appointments for pictures should
coinc to the Quad office as soon
as possible.
The office is open between 10
and 12 a.m. and 1 and 5 p.m.
Charge for the pictures is $1. Men
should wear coats and ties and
women white blouses.
