# Reverend Sterling En Route Home
## 
### 
The Rev. William Sterling, father
of Dr. Wallace Sterling, newly in
augurated president of Stanford
University, is en route home today
by train to his home in Colborne,
Ontario.
Recovered from the heart attack
which he suffered several days
after the inauguration and which
forced him to rest in the hospital
for two weeks, the 76-year-old re
tired United Church of Canada min
ister left for Canada on the City of
San Francisco. He will arrive home
over the weekend.
