# Summers Abroad Planned by IIR
## 
### 
Have you always had a yen to
watch the fireworks at Versailles, or
scale the Jungfrau, or view the fab
ulous British crown jewels?
If you want to go to Europe, as a
traveler, worker, or student, the
UNESCO committee at the lIR has
information about the various op
portunities open to American college
students. Whether you want to go
first-class or on a shoestring, you
can be sure you'll find something to
suit your needs in the well-stocked
files at the lIR.
You should make arrangements
early, during fall quarter if possible,
because the devand for reservations
runs far ahead of the supply.
UNESCO can help you make ship
and plane reservations, as well as
contact the various student organi
zations which sponsor student v/ork.
Someone will be at the UNESCO
desk j't the lIR every afternoon af
ter 3 to answer your questions and
help you plan your trip abroad.
