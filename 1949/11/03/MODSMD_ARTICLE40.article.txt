# Med School Prof To Speak Tonight
## 
### 
Dr. Windsor Cutting, professor of

the Stanford Medical School, will
speak tonight to all interested pre
med students at 7:30 in the Bacteri

The meeting is being sponsored by

be made of those who are interested
