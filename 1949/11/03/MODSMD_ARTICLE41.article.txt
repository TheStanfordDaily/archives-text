# Village News
## Folk Dance Festival 
### 
An evening festival of folk and
square dancing this Friday will
mark this term's mid-point for the
Stanford Vil'age Folk Danee Group.
Gay colored clothes, full skirts, and
costumes are definitely in order for
an evening of fun and dancing.
Festivities start at 8 in the Vil
lage auditorium and will last until
11. Informal exhibitions of more
difficult dances will be given by
members of Advanced Hoedowners
who are present. Willy Grishaw and
guest caller Jack Twolmby will be
present.
All people who are interested in
square or folk dancing are invited
arid are promised a wonderful time.
A donation of 25 cents per person is
requested io cover the necessary
costs.
