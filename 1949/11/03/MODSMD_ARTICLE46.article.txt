# Ball Slated By Branner
## 
### 
"Bali H'ai Ball" is the theme cho
sen for Branner Hall's autumn
dance to be given Friday, November
11, from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. The dressy
dance will be held in the Branner
lobby.
Following the theme, the hall will
be decorated like a sophisticated
South Pacific night club.
Eddie Allie's orchestra will pro
vide the music. A floor show—con
sisting of songs, dances, and a hula
—will be presented during the in
termissions.
Refreshments will also be served.
Bids costing $2 will go on sale next
week.
Lois Driggs is general chairman
in charge of the dance. Committee
members are Ruth Anderson, deco
rations; Marilyn Fallquist, tickets;
Barbara Pearson, publicity; Mary
Ann Walters, entertainment; Bobby
Koch, arrangements; Marguerite
| Odell, clean-up; and Mary Lou Jen
kins, refreshments.
