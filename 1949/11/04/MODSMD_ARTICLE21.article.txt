# Display Shows Relief Theme
## 
### 
"They Still Need You" is the
theme of the new display in the
Stanford Bookstore window. For the
rest of this week and all of next
week the window will he taken up
with exhibits connected with the
Stanford student relief drive.
The theme is lettered in white on
the red background, and flanked by
two photographs of needy children
one of them receiving medical treat
ment.
Two globes of the world, and a
group of pamphlets, complete the
present display.
