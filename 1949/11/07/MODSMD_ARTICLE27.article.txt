# Law School to Hear Helen Gahagan Douglas
## 
### 
Phi Delta Phi will present the
second in its current lecture series
on Friday night at 7 in Memorial
Auditorium.
Representative Helen Gahagan
Douglas of Los Angeles will address
the Law School faculty and student
body and other interested persons
on California's critical water prob
lem. She will discuss in detail the
Colorado River Compact and the
Central Valley Project.
