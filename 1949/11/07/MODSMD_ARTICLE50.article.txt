# Humanities, Sciences Pictures Scheduled
## 
### 
Today is the last day that Quad
pictures will be taken of seniors
in the School of Humanities and
Sciences whose last names begin
with the letters M through 11. To
morrow and Wednesday the rest
of Humanities and Sciences will
he taken.
Bill Larmer, Quad editor, re
minds seniors that the School of
Humanities and Sciences category
includes the Schools of Social Sci
ences, Humanities, Physical Sci
ences, and Biological Sciences.
Pictures will be taken at the
Quad office from 10 to 12 a.m. and
1 to 5 p.m. The charge is $1.
