# Romanic Language Staff Offers New Winter Seminars
## 
### 
Three new courses in the general
and comparative history of Romance
literature will be offered at Stanford
winter quarter. The seminar courses
will be conducted by Professor Ron
ald Hilton of the Romanic Lan
guages Department.
The courses cover three periods:
The Middle Ages, the sixteenth
and seventeenth centuries, and the
modern period since 1700.
The courses will cover literature
of France, Italy, Spain, and Portu
gal. They are intended for students
in Romanic languages as well as for
students desiring a general knowl
edge of Romance literature. Ability
to read the texts of one country in
the original would be helpful, but
not necessary.
