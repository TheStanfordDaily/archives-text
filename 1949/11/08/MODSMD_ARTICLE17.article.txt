# Visiting Minister To Preach Sunday
## 
### 
Dr. Henry Pitney Van Dusen, one
of the nation's most distinguished
ministers, will deliver two sermons
at Memorial Church Sunday.
Dr. Van Dusen, president of the
Union Theological Seminary in New
York, will speak on "When Life Is
Most Real" at the 11 o'clock morn
ing service. His topic will be "The
Healing of the Nations" at a special
4:30 service in the afternoon.
