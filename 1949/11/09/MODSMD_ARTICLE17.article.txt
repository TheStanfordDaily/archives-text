# SEN. REED KILLED IN FALL
## 
### 
PARSONS, Kas., Nov. 8 (/P)—Sen
ator Clyde M. Reed (R., Kas.), was
killed tonight in a fall downstairs at
his home. He was 78.
The Senator, who returned to his
home from a hospital only last week
after an attack of influenza, appar
ently died instantly.
