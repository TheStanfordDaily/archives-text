# Big Game Fashions Will Be Presented
## 
### 
A fashion show displaying clothes
particularly intended for wear during
the Big Game week festivities will
be presented at the Clothes Closet in
Palo Aito tomorrow afternoon at 4
p.m.
In collaboration with the "Miss
Plumb Beautiful" contest conducted
by Revlon on this campus, the win
ning girl will appear as one of the
models.
Other models include Lynn Siel,
Joyce Oldham, Skippy brand, Jane
Sanson, Saralee Canfield, Mary Miles,
Dawn Blacker, Sally Crowl, and Ann
Alabaster.
