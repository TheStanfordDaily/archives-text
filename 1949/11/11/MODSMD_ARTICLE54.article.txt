# Barber to Air Big Game
## 
### 
The Big Game will be broadcast
throughout the nation by CBS with
Rod Barber, noted baseball and
football announcer, at the mike.
The broadcast, however, will not
be heard on the Pacific Coast, in
Hawaii, or in Montana and Nevada.
The Tidewater Associated Oil Com
pany has a contract with the PCC
tor all games involing Conference
teams.
This broadcast will mark the first
time this season that a Pacific Coast
game will be sent throughout the
nation.
