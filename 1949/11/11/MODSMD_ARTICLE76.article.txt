# Branner Rooms
## 
### 
A room judging contest will be
held Sunday in Branner at 1 p.m.
Following the judging, prizes will
be awarded to the winners. Re
freshments will also be served.
Rooms will be judged for inge
nuity, originality, and economy. The
first- and second-place winners will
be awarded bookcases, while third
place winners will be given certifi
cates. The most attractive room in
each corridor will be chosen, and
the owners will also be awarded a
certificate.
