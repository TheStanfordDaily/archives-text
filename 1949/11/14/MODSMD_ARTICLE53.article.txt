Jim Crooker will present the
voice of an anonymous Stanford
queen tonight at 9:30. The rough
who guesses her identity will be in
vited to present her with an orchid.
Next week, the woman of his choice
will become the mysterious queen.
At 10:30 another female, better
known but just as anonymous, will
try to tear the boys from their
books. She's Stanford Sadie —need
we say more?
