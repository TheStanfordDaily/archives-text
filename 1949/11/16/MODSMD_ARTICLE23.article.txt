# GAME DANCE TICKETS
## 
### 
Big Game Dance tickets will make
a "grand tour of dining halls" to
morrow, and will be sold in front of
the Cellar from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m.
Tickets will be sold at Encina
Commons during lunch hour and at
the Village dining room at dinner.
Tickets are $4.80 per couple.
