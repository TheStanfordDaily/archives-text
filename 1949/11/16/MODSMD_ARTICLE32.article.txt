# Dutch Big Game

To the Editor:
It i& a long-standing tradition at Stanford that Big
Game dales yo Dutch the girls pay their share—and
that the girls mention the fact so that the poor, im
poverished hoy will not he embarrassed financially
oi otherwise. Come on girls, shell out.
