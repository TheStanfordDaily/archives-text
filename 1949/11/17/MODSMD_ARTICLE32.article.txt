# Car Reg Stickers Must Be in Sight
## 
### 
Automobiles regularly driven on
campus must have a registration
sticker in sight at all times, Police
Chief Gordon Davis warns campus
drivers.
Registration is urged for cars
driven on campus, and not only for
those belonging to drivers living 011
campus. Persons desiring informa
tion on registration should contact
Davis in the Police Department at
the Corporation Yard.
