# Farm Relief Drive Closes at $3,900
## 
### 
Stanford's fall relief drive neared
its close yesterday with the Village,
under the chairmanship of solicitor
Paul Niewold, reporting a virtual
100 per cent of its residence con
tributing, and with the total collec
tion upped to $3,900.
Committee and area chairmen for
the drive will meet some time next
week, and will be asked to present
reports on this year's drive and rec
ommendations for next year's.
