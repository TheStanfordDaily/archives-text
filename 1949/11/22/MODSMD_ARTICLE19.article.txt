# Civil Service Sets Professional Aides Exam for Dec. 3
## 
### 
The Civil Service Commission has
announced that the Junior Profes
sional Assistants written examina
tion will be given on the Stanford
campus Saturday, December 3. The
exam will begin at 8:30 a.m. in
Rooms 268, 30, 283, and 273 in the
Engineering Building.
Students who notified the com
mission that they desired to take
the examination in San Francisco
but who now wish to take it on
campus should notify the commis
sion immediately in order that they
may be given admission cards to the
exam on campus.
