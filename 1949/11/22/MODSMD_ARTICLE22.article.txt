# Thanksgiving Leaves Taken From Quotas
## 
### 
Women's Council has announced
that all Thanksgiving vacation late
leaves will be counted off the
women student's quota.
These leaves were accounted for
when the present late leave plan
was drawn up last year, and the
plan was made liberal enough so
that no non-quota leaves are nec
essary.
