# Women Will Draw For Room Changes
## 
### 
Women who wish to change resi
dences winter quarter may fill out
residence choice cards and draw
numbers to establish their priority
Tuesday in Miss Doyle's office, tem
porary building D, room 30.
Preference will be given according
to class and number drawn, and girls
may draw as partners if they wish.
Freshmen are not eligible to draw at
this time. The Daily will print ten
tative lists of vacancies on Tuesday.
Requests for changes within houses
or unitf; are also due on Tuesday and
will be handled through the house or
unit.
