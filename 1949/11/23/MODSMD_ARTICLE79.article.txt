# Latest Alumni Review Goes on Sale at Union
## 
### 
The November issue of the Alum
ni Review, which goes on sale this
week at the Union, contains a com
plete text of Dr. Sterling's inaugural
speech, as well as a story of the in
auguration.
Also included in the Review is an
article by Dean Frederick Terman
on the School of Engineering, the
story of Big Game ticket realloca
tion, a column by Dean Kimpton on
fraternities, and an article by Daily
Sports Editor John Breier.
