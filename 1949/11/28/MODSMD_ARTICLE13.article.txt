# Farm Symphony To Give Concert
## 
### 
The Stanford Symphony Orches
tra will present a concert in Memo
rial Auditorium Sunday afternoon
at 4.
Sandor Salgo will conduct a var
ied program of Haydn, Debussy, and
Vaughan Williams. Assisting the
orchestra in the Debussy cantata
"L'Enfant Prodigue" will be Janis
Grimes, soprano; Harold C. Schmidt,
tenor; Raymond Hammett. bass; and
members of the University Chorus.
