# Quad Now Has Senior Proofs
## 
### 
Proofs are now available of all
senior Quad pictures taken through
November 2. This includes numbers
1000 through 1500, and takes in Busi
ness School, School of Education,
School of Engineering, and the first
part of the School of Humanities and
Sciences.
Proofs may be picked up at the
Quad office this week between 1 and
5 p.m. every day, and must be re
turned within three days.
The rest of the seniors' proofs will
be available later this week, at times
to be announced later.
