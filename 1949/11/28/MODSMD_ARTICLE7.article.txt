##

HO G OTA — Colombia's ruling
Conservatives named Laureano Go
mez president today in an election
which went ofT quietly despite acute
political tension.
Gomez had no opposition for the
four-year term. The Liberal party,
the main opposition to the Conser
vatives, had withdrawn its candidate
and called for a boycott of the elec
tion.
