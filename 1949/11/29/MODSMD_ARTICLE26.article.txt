# Big Game Movies Show Tonight and Thursday
## 
### 
Motion pictures of the Stanford-
California game will he shown to
night and Thursday night at 7:30
in Cubberley Auditorium.
If the crowd is not too large,
Downing McKee, chairman of the
movies, announced that attempts
will be made to show cither the
Stanford-Idaho or the Stanford-
Southern California game along
with the Big Game pictures.
