# Rules for Winter Residence Listed
## 
### 
Women not returning to a Uni
versity residence winter quarter
should return blue cards releasing
their rooms to their residence's of
fice. This includes women who are
graduating.
For men, today is the last day
to sign transfer of room deposit
cards for winter quarter room res
ervations in Encina, Toyon, and
Stern.
Men planning to stay on campus
during Christmas vacation should
sign up immediately in their hall
offices. December 19 will be the last
day to check out of Stern and Toyon
into Encina, where all Christmas
stay-overs will be housed.
University residences for both
men and women will reopen for
winter quarter on January 2.
