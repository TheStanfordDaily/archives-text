# 'Boy Meets Girl' Tryouts Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for the cast of "Boy Meets
Girl," a Hollywood farce by Bella
and Sam Spewack which will be
produced during the winter quar
ter, will be held this afternoon from
3:30 to 6 in Room 205 of the Memo
rial Auditorium.
The reading for parts will con
tinue at the same time tomorrow
afternoon in the Little Theater and
Friday on the stage of the Memo
rial Auditorium.
Professor A. Nicholas Vardac, di
rector of the play, yesterday isssued
a call for all University dramatic
talent, including freshmen. The pro
duction is given by the Stanford
Players.
