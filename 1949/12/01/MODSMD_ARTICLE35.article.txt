# Women Now Able To Play in Band
## 
### 
Women can now join the band.
Although there are to he no more
rehearsals this quarter, students
wishing to join are urged to pre-reg
for Music Gl, University Band.
Winter quarter band is entirely a
concert organization. This year the
musicians will have the use of the
new concert library which was re
cently donated by a group of San
Diego alumni.
Members are needed in all sec
tions, but especially woodwinds. In
struments will be furnished for all
those who do not have their own.
Rehearsal times are Tuesdays at
7 p.m. and Wednesdays and Thurs
days at 4 p.m. for woodwinds and
brass respectively. The course is
taken for one unit or can be audited.
