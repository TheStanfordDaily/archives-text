# Gaieties Discs Now Available at KZSU
## 
### 
Orders for Gaieties records will be
taken until Wednesday, announced
Mary Hill, KZSU station manager.
The more orders there are, the
lower the prices will be, she said.
With present sign-ups, the 12-inch
pressings will be sold at a maximum
of $2 apiece.
Each record will contain three or
four songs. People may sign up on
lists posted in the back hall of Me
morial Hall and in the KZSU office
downstairs, or liiey may call DA
2-3971 and state which songs they
want.
