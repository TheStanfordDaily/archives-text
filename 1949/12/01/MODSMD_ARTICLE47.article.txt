# Orchesis Admits Six New Members
## 
### 
The initiation of six girls in the
modern dance group Orchcsis was
held last night.
The girls have served two quar
ters as apprentices and participated
in the opera "Shvanda, the Bagpi
per." Two quarters of apprentice
ship and participation in one major
dance performance arc required for
initiation.
•Girls initiated were Dixie Black,
Marilyn Sather, Shirley Scotten,
Elizabeth Dunn, Helen Gaylord, Su
zanne Kelly,and Ranveig Drowry.
