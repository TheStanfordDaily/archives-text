# Geography Dept. Requests Snaps
## 
### 
Snapshots taken of life through
out the world, including the United
States, are requested by the Geog
raphy Department for use in class
room study.
Topics such as industry, agricul
ture, cloud formations, or landscapes
are most in demand. The photo
graphs should be preferably 2 x 2 in
size and in natural color. They will
be used only for copying and will
be returned promptly to the own
ers.
Persons having snapshots they
would be willing to lend arc asked
to contact Professor Alfred Sum
ner's office, 260 K, in the basement
of the Engineering Building any af
ternoon between 1 and 3.
