# Klahoya Chooses Seven New Officers
## 
### 
Joan Hamann was elected presi
dent of Klahoya, Stanford's off-cam
pus women, at a luncheon yesterday.
Other officers selected for the
coming academic year were: Carol
Roselyn, vice - president; Verne
Dowdell, secretary; Judy McDaniel,
treasurer; Joanna Winters, recorder;
Shirley Hadley, chairman of lunch
eons; and Theron Brown, publicity.
Outgoing officers were Lois
Croonquist, president; Joan Ham
ann, vice - president; Joanna Win
ters, secretary; and Sylvia Davidson,
treasurer.
