# Biz Formal
## 
### 
Students, faculty, and alumni of
the Biz School will be dancing in
formal attire tomorrow night at the
Peninsula Country Club from 9 to 1.
Tickets are on sale in the Biz School
Library, and by committee members
for $2.50 a couple.
Gary Evers and his orchestra will
supply the music for the affair. Tro
phies will be presented to the win
ners of the Biz School tennis tourna
ment at that time.
