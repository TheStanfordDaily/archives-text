# Fun House Dance Slated Saturday
## 
### 
Falling stairs, a maze, and a fun
house will be among the many
novelties greeting roughs and gals
attending the Phi Sig Fun House
dance Saturday night. Sponsored by
Phi Sigma Kappa fraternity, the
dance will be open to the entire
campus from 0 to 1 p.m.
The Four Tones will be on hand
to provide music for dancing and
refreshments will be served at the
Phi Sigma Kappa House. Admission
is free.
