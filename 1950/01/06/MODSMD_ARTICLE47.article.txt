# Women Sign Today For Cage Contest
## 
### 
Sign-ups for women's basketball
intramurals arc due today at 2 p.m.
in the lobby of the Women's Gym.
All houses entering teams in the
tournament should plan on at least
one practice game, announced Ruth
Ann Bortner, basketball manager.
Games will be scheduled next week
Monday through Thursday from 7
to 10 p.m.
