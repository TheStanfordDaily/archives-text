# Quad Photos Lag Today, Tomorrow
## 
### 
Today and tomorrow are the last
days the Quad will photograph La
gunita women for the living groups
section of the 1950 yearbook.
This will be the last chance for
Lagunita women who have not al
ready been photographed for the
senior section to have their pictures
included in the Quad.
The Quad office is open between
10 a.m. and 12 noon, and 1 and 5
p.m. Women should wear white
blouses for the pictures. A charge
' of $1 is made.
