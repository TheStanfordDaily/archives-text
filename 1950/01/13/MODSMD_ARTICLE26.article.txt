# Mothers to Hear Panel on Stanford
## 
### 
"Stanford, Past and Present" will
be the topic of a panel discussion by
Stanford students at the regular
meeting of the Stanford Mothers'
Club Tuesday. Dean of Students
Lawrence A. Kimpton will moder
ate.
The meeting will begin at 2 p.m.
in the Women's Clubhouse and all
mothers are invited to attend. Tea
will be served by Mrs. E. F. Til
brick, social chairman of the group.
A luncheon served in the Union
dining hall will precede the meet
ing. Tor reservations call Mrs. Ver
ner Allen at DA 2-3118.
