# 19th Annual Book Contest Opens
## 
### 
} A book contest, sponsored by the
Commonwealth Club of California,
is now open to California authors.
Entries and entry blanks for the
19th annual contest must be .sub
mitted by midnight, January 31.
Two gold medals will be awarded
for the best fiction and non-fiction
story, and three silver medals for
the next best, one for the best book
of poetry, and a third for the out
standing child's book.
Entries may be made by the au
thor or publishers, who should mail
at least two copies, together with an
entry blank, to Stuart R. Ward,
Secretary, Literature Medal Award
Jury, Commonwealth Club of Cali
fornia, Hotel St. Francis, San Fran
cisco 19.
