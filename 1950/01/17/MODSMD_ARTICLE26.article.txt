# Air Commander's Visit Postponed
## 
### 
The visit of Major General John
E. Upston to the Stanford campus
has been postponed because of the
death of General Henry A (H?p)
Arnold.
As commanding general of the
Fourth Air Force, General Upston
will accompany the body of Gen
eral Arnold to Arlington National
Cemetry.
General Upston was originally
scheduled to audit an education
seminar, inspect the Army and Air
Force ROTC units at Stanford, and
visit President Sterling today.
