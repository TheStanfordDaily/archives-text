# Quad Takes Ventura, Roble Pictures Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day that Ven
tura women will be photographed
for the living groups section of
the 1950 Quad. All those who
have not already been photo
graphed for the senior section
should come to the Quad office
today.
Roble women who did not have
their pictures taken for the frosh
book will also be taken today and
tomorrow.
The Quad office is open from 10
a.m. to 12 noon and from 1 to 5
p.m. A charge of $1 is made for
the pictures. Women should wear
white blouses.
