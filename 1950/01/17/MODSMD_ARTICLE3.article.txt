# Mine Workers Idle
## 
### 
PITTSBURGH —No contract, no
work strikes today idled (58,00 Unit
ed Mine Workers in six states and
one of the largest coal mine owners
shut off credit at company stores.
United States Steel Corporation,
through its subsidiary Union Sup
ply Co., today told the miners in ef
fect: No work —no credit.
The miners who stayed away from
the pits today did so despite John
L. Lewis' suggestion — traditionally
an order—that they dig coal.
