# Library Director To Attend Meet
## 
### 
Dr. Raynard C. Swank, director
of University libraries, will leave
Monday to attend the mid-winter
conference of the American Library
Association in Chicago.
He will be chairman of a meeting
on documentary films at the ALA
sessions, and will also attend meet
ings of the Audio-Visual Board and
of the executive board of the Divi
sion of Cataloging and Classifica
tion.
Dr. Swank will go to New York
and Denver on library business be
fore returning to the campus Feb
ruary 6.
