# Research Unit to Hear Lecture on Volcanism
## 
### 
"Volcanism in the United States
Northwest" will be the subject of a
lecture by John W. Funkhouscr
Wednesday evening at 8 before the
U. S. Naval Reserve Volunteer Re
search Unit 12-3.
Funkhouscr, a member of the unit
and a graduate student in the De
partment of Biology, will deal with
the recent crustal instability in the
northwestern states of our country.
