# 'Ways of Praying' Is YWCA Topic,
## 
### 
The YWCA will present the first
in a series of three discussions en
titled "Ways of Praying" tomorrow
from 7:15 to 8:15 p.m. in the "Y"
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse
Dr. G. Arthur Casaday of the
First Congregational Church and
Paul Watson, student leader, will
direct the discussion.
The second program, "Achieving
Personal Maturity," will be pre
sented January 26; and the last, "Is
Faith Necessary?" on February 2
All programs will be open to the
campus.
