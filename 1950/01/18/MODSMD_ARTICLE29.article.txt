# Faculty Research To Hear Speakers
## 
### 
Stanford Faculty Research Club
members will hear talks by two pro
fessors who have recently returned
from the Orient at a meeting to
night at 8 in the Faculty Clubhouse
Dr. Gordon Ferris, professor of In
ology, will speak on "Collecting
Scale Insects in China." Dr. Fred
eric Spiegelberg, assistant professor
of Asiatic and Slavic Studies, will
discuss "What Survives of Buddhism
in Tibetan, Siamese, and Ceylonese
Monasteries."
