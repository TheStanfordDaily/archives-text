# Eugene Perl Leads NAACP Chapter
## 
### 
Eugene Perl was elected president
ul the Stanford Chapter of the Na
tional Association for the Advance
ment of Colored People at a meeting
yesterday afternoon.
Plans were formulated for Na
tional Negro History Week, February
12 to 19, and a future membership
drive was discussed.
Other officers elected were Naomi
Grossberg, vice-president; Lois Dei
mei, secretary; and Joanna Winters,
membership-at-large.
