# Eleven Reporters Join Daily Staff
## 
### 
Eleven reporters were added to
the Stanford Daily staff yesterday
after a two-week tryout period. The
new reportrs are:
Ruby Bernstein, Mary Agnes
Bernt, Donn Byrne, Bob Finkel,
Sharlene Hoffman, Mark Kasanin,
Charlotte Kempfer, Diek Lentz,
Beverly Oden, Lloyd Rosenfeld,
Mary Spiegl.
