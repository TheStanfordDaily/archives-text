# Ski Club Film Tickets Sell Till Wednesday
## 
### 
Tickets are on sale at the ASSU
office for the film "Twenty - five
Years of Skiing," to be presented in
Memorial Auditorium Wednesday at
7 p.m.
Dr. Frank Howard will narrate
the two-hour technicolor movie. Dr
Howard, who is an expert skier and
photographer, has produced the film
from the best pictures that he has
taken over a 25-year period. It ig
ratfd the best ski film of 1949-1950.
The film is being sponsored by the
ASSU and all proceeds from the
program will go to aid the Stanford
