# Quad Photos Union Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day that the
Quad will take pictures of women
from Union and Union Units for the
living group sections of the yearbook
All those who have not already been
photographed by the Quad should
come to the Quad office between 10
a.mi and 12 noon or 1 and 5 p.m. to
day.
The six houses included in the
Unions Units are Manzanita, Madro
no, Mariposa. Maison Frangaise, Casa
Espanola, and Elm.
Women should wear white blouses
for the pictures. Charge for the pic
tures is $1.
