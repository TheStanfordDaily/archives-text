# Hoedowners Plan Winter Square Dancing Classes
## 
### 
Hoedowners is offering participa
tion in several new activities for ex
perienced or inexperienced folk and
square dancers.
In addition to the two festivals
sponsored each year, a group of
dancers has been invited for the sec
ond time to attend the Ski Carnival.
A band is being organized to play
for square dances. Anyone interested
in playing should contact Jack Ken
dall, president of Hoedowners, or
Mrs. Miriam Lidster, faculty director.
Beginning classes are held in the
Women's Gym Mondays at 7:30 p.m.
Advanced groups dance on Thurs
days at 7:30 in the Women's Gym.
Both beginners and advanced danc
ers have classes every Friday in the
Village Auditorium.
