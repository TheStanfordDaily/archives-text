# Work Abroad Is Vocom Topic
## 
### 
'Foreign Service: It's A Small
World," is the title of the third pro
gram in a series to he presented by
Women's Vocom The program will
be held tomorrow at 7:15 p.m. in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Dr. Graham Stuart of the Stanford
Political Science Department will
speak on jobs in the United States
Foreign Service, and will act as mod
erator.
Director of the Stanford YWCA,
Miss Grace Steinbeck, will describe
jobs in international organizations.
Carl E. McDowell, of the Stanford
School of Business, will talk on job
opportunities in the foreign trade
field.
Chairman Mary Kraus announced
that the program is open to all in
terested students.
