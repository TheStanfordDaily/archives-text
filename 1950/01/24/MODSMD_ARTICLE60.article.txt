# Skillen Elected To AWS Housing Com
## 
### 
Mary Skillen was elected chair
man of the AWS Housing Commit
tee at a meeting last week.
The committee is planning after
dinner coffees in the various wom
en's living groups to acquaint fresh
men and other new girls with the
available housing. The coffees will
be held this quarter and spring
quarter, beginning February (3, and
will be accompanied by talks in the
freshman corridors of Roble and
Branner by girls representing the
different living areas. Following
the completion of this program in
the spring, open houses will be held
by all houses.
