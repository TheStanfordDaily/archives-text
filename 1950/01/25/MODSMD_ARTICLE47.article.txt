# Grandin Earns State Position
## 
### 
Hank Grandin, skiing for the
Stanford Ski Club, placed eighth in
a field of 50 in the Southern Divi
sion "B" Championships at Mt. Wa
terman this past weekend. He has
been asked to enter the State "B"
Championships at Big Pines Febru
ary 4 and 5.
Other "B" racers represening the
Central California District may be
admitted to the championships with
the approval of the vice-president
of the Central California FWSA Dis
trict, inasmuch as there were no
Central District Eliminations.
