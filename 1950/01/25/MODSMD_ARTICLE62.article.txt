# Village Men Elect Today
## 
### 
Stanford Village residents will
hold elections today in accordance
with their new constitution, which
was approved at Monday night's
meeting of the Village Council.
Two positions are open. Wally
Burton of 117 and Stuart Dane, 210,
president under the old constitu
tion, are candidates for president of
the Village Couficil.
David Hoffman, 108, and Warren
James, 314, are candidates for presi
dent of the Village Activities Board,
the newly established group which
will replace the old VAC.
