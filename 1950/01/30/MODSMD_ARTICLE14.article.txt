# Newspaper Work Is Vocom Topic
## 
### 
Women's Vocom will present the
fourth of its winter quarter pro
grams Wednesday, February 1, in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse at 7:15 p.m.
The subject of the programs is
"Advertising and Journalism: It's
Right to Write." This topic ranked
third in the results of the polls taken
by Women's Vocom last quarter.
Refreshments will be served af tc
the program.
