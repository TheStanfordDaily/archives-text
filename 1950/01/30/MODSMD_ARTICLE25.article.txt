# Red Cross Drive Talent Show Set
## 
### 
A student-faculty talent show will
be held on campus February 17 in
connection with the Red Cross
drive. All proceeds will go toward
Stanford's share of the national Red
Cross quota for the Palo Alto area.
Susan Ray and Roy Roberts will
be co-chairmen for the program.
Also on the committee are Marge
Swingley and Janet Houck. Jo
Lyon, Stanford drive chairman, is
co-ordinating the show with (he
campus solicitation, which will be
February 13 through 17.
Tickets for the talent show will go
on sale the end of next week for $1.
Miss Lyon said a talent show is
being given again this quarter in
response to the demand created by
the show held in conjunction with
the Foreign Relief Drive last fall.
