# Bay Ship Collision
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO — The Joel
Chandler Harris, Coastwise Lines
transport, was damaged today in a
ship collision in San Francisco har
bor.
The bow of the Pacific Transport
Line's Lincficld Victory rammed
into the stern of the Harris, opening
a hole near tlic No. 5 hatch.
