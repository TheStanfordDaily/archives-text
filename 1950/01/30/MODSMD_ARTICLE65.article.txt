# Union Residences Plan Winter Dance
## 
### 
"A Night on Nob Hill" has been
selected as theme of the Union Unit
winter formal dance.
The affair will be held in the
Room of the Dons, Mark Hopkins
Hotel, on February 25 from 9 to 1.
Paul Miller and his orchestra will
furnish the music. A buffet dinner
will be provided.
Girls living in any one of the Un
ion Units may purchase bids, which
will go on sale soon, from a repre
sentative in their house.
