# Beginning Hoedowners Meet Tonight in Gym
## 
### 
The three regular Hoodowner
groups will meet this week. All
those who are interested in folk and
square dancing are invited to come.
Tonight the beginners' group will
meet at 7:30 in the Women's Gym.
Thursday the advanced group will
meet at the same time, also in the
Women's Gym. Friday will be
"come one, come all" for square
dancing at the Village at 7:30.
