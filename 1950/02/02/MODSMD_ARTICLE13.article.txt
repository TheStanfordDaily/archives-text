# Borba Issues Call For Yell Leaders
## 
### 
Tryouts for yell leaders will be
held for the last time tomorrow af
ternoon from 5 to 8 in Cubberley
Auditorium, Head Yell Leader Dean
Borba announced.
The tryouts, which are a prelimi
nary to spring quarter nominations
and elections, are open to students
interested in positions as assistant
or head yell leaders.
Experience in cheer leading is not
necessary.
Students will have an opportunity
to lead yells at basketball games
this month.
The present yell leaders will be
at the tryouts to discuss last year's
activities.
