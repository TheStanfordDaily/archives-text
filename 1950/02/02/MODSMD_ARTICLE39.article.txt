# Organist Features Handel, Arne, Bach
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney will present
the weekly organ recital this after
noon at 4:15 in Memorial Church.
Today's program will include:
"First Concerto in G Minor" by
Handel, "Fantaisie in F" by Mozart,
Arne's "Flute Solo," and Bach's
"Prelude and Fugue in B Flat Ma
jor."
