# MAJOR LEAGUER SUES
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Feb. 1 (/P) —
George Caster, 42, termer major
league pitcher, today demanded
$50,000 damages for a traffic injury
to his arm which he said had ended
his baseball career.
