# Horse Sense Talk At Lag Tonight
## 
### 
Do you want to know why a horse
can sleep standing up when a mule
can't, or any other vital facts about
horses and their care?
Dr. Bill Linfoot, polo player and
veterinarian, and Allen Ross, head
of the Riding School, will be on
hand at Lagunita tonight at 7:30 to
speak and answer questions.
This is the first of a series of horse
talks which the Riding Club will
sponsor weekly at different campus
residences. All men and women in
terested in riding are invited to at
