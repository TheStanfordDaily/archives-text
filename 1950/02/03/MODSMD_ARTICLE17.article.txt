# SIRO Will Park Boxes For Europe Tomorrow
## 
### 
A packing party, to prepare boxes
of clothes and books for Europe, will
be held tomorrow morning from 9
to 12.
SIPO asks anyone interested to
come to the SfRO office at that time.
The packages are to go to the Free
University of Berlin, the German
Red Cross in Luneburg, and indi
viduals in Germany, as well as to
people in the Netherlands and
Greece.
