# Life of George Bernard Shaw Is Subject of Tuesday Series
## 
### 
George Bernard Shaw, well
known Irish wit and playwright,
will be the subject of the Tuesday
Evening Series lecture to be given
tomorrow evening at 7:30 in Memo
rial Auditorium by Dr. William Ir
vine.
Dr. Irvine, professor of English at
Stanford, is the author of the re
cently published biography, "The
Universe of G.8.5."
In discussing the life of the un
known Irishman who rose to be
come one of the great men of litera
ture, Professor Irvine will draw
from material gathered in writing
his book. Shaw's character and at
titude toward politics and life will
bo featured in the lecture.
