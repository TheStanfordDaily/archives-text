# 'Invincible' Record
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO -Hustled along
by a strong tail wind, the Pan
American Airways Clipper "Invin
cible" flew from Honolulu to San
Francisco today in G hours and 50
minutes.
Pan American said this was a new
record for the 2,400-mile flight by
a commercial plane. The previous
record of 6 hours, 55 minutes was
set by a DC-0 in 1948.
