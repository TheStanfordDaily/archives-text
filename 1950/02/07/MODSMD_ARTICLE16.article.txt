# Tibetan Tripitaka Exhibited at Libe
## 
### 
The Tibetan Tripilaka, a volume
of a sacred canon of Buddhism, is
included in an exhibit of Tibetan
arts and crafts and contemporary
folk lore now on display in the
Stanford Library.
The exhibit has been prepared by
Dr. Frederic Spiegel berg, member
of the Asiatic and Slavic Studies
faculty, who recently visited Ti
betan lamaseries high in the Sikki
mesc Himalayas.
The exhibit is open to the public
and will be on display through Feb
ruary 28.
