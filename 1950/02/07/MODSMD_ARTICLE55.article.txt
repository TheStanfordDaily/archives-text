# Dutch Missionary Talks At Women's Clubhouse
## 
### 
Miss Corrie ten Boom, missionary
from Holland, will speak tonight at
7:MO on the main floor of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
Miss ten Boom was imprisoned for
10 months by the Germans. After
the war she returned to missionary
work in Germany.
She will appear as a guest of the
Stanford Christian Fellowship and
was brought to this country by the
Inter-Varsity Christian Fellowship.
