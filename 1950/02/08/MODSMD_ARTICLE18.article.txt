# Authority to Talk On Saddle-breds
## 
### 
The Stanford Riding Club will
present a talk on saddle-bred horses
by Mrs. Mary Price tomorrow at
7; 15 in the Roble lobby.
Mrs. Price, well-known authority
on saddle - breds, has been riding
and breaking horses for over 40
years. She has just returned from a
search for saddle - breds in Ken
tucky, Missouri, and other states,
and will describe her experiences
on this tour as well as the breaking
and training of such horses.
Hie meeting is open to all men
and women interested in riding. Re
freshments will be served.
