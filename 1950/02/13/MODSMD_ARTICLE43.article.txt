# Farm Men Enter Cage Teat
## 
### 
Stanford's unofficial entry in the
Examiner-Pacific Amateur Athletic
Association basketball tourney will
got its first test Thursday night
when 12 intramural players face the
Sunset Optimist Club of San Fran
cisco.
The Stanford boys, playing under
the sponsorship of Dinah's Shack,
will face a team that has one win
to its credit in the day-old tourna
ment. The Sunset team defeated a

group of former Stanford players
yesterday. It will be the first game
for the Dinah's squad.
