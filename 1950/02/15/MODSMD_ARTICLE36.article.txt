# On Your Dial-880
##

Jazz-boy George Bahrs will set
the Firehouse Five on the turntable
tonight at 10:30. The live will play,
among other things, "Blass Bell"
and "Everybody Knows My Baby."
Bringing jazz fans news of Billie
Holliday's appearance in San Fran
cisco, George will twirl some of her
records too.
George's "Dixieland Show," fea
turing records from his and others'
collections as well as the KZSU li
brary, is broadcast each Wednesday
at this time.
