# CON SIGNS
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO. Feb. 14 (/P> —
live San Francisco Seals announced
today they had come to terms with
star pitcher Con Dempsey.
Neither Dempsey nor Seal Vice-
President Charlie Graham, in
charge of contracts, would reveal
the terms.
