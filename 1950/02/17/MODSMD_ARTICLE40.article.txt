# L.A. Stanford Alumnae Offer Scholarships
## 
### 
A $600 scholarship for next year is
being offered by the Stanford Wom
en's Club of Los Angeles to all upper
division women from the Los An
geles area.
Applications should be in before
spring vacatiofi, in order to permit
the club to interview applicants. Pre
requisites for this scholarship are
financial need and a B average.
Undergraduates in residence who
wish to apply for scholarships for
next year are also urged to put in
their applications immediately. The
deadline for these applications is
April 15.
Data concerning restrictions for
the many other scholarships which
Stanford offers may be obtained from
the Bulletin of Information, pub
lished annually by the University.
