# Gilkey Concludes Religious Week
## 
### 
Dr. Charles W. Gilkey will close
"Religion in Life" Week at Stanford
this Sunday. His sermon for the 11
o'clock service is entitled "New
Grain Out of Old."
The University Choir will provide
, the music. Harold C. Schmidt will
direct the choir, and Herbert B.
j Nannoy will be at the organ.
At 4:30 Sunday afternoon, Dr.
Gilkey will be the speaker at the
I vesper service. His topic then will
! be "The Atmosphere We Choose."
