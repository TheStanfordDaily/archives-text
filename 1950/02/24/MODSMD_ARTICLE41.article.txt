# Orchesis Invited To Cal Symposium
## 
### 
Orchesis, the Stanford dance
group, has been invited to attend
the northern college dance sympo
sium at the University of California
Saturday, February 25. Eight col
leges in all will be represented.
In the morning a master lesson
will be given by Wclland Lathrop
and Ann Hclprin, local dancers. A
concert will be held in the after
noon. Fifteen members of Orchesis
will be present.
Mills College, California, San
Francisco State, Nevada, Fresno
State, College of the Pacific, and
Stanford are sending groups of
dancers.
