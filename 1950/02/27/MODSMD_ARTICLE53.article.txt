# Sterling Appointed to Academy Group
## 
### 
Dr. Wallace Sterling was one of the
17 members appointed Friday to the
Air Force Academy Committee of the
Bay Area Council.
William E. Waste, chairman of the
council, explained that the commit
tee is not advocating any particular
site for location of the academy, but
will insist that every site meeting re

quirements be studied. Preliminary
investigation of local sites has been
completed by the district engineer of
the army.
Other members include Dr. Robert
O. Sproul, president of the University
of California, nine chamber of com
merce presidents, and several avia
tion men.
