# Club to Sell Box Lunches for DP's
## 
### 
A box social to raise funds to help
establish a family of Displaced Per
sons in the community will be held
by the Westminster Club today at
5:30 p.m. at the First Presbyterian
Church. All students are invited.
Box lunches prepared by women
of the club will be auctioned to the
highest bidders. Proceeds will be
used to buy household items for a
family being brought to Palo Altc
from Europe by the church.
The Rev. David Jacobsen, former
associate pastor of the church, will
be the speaker.
