# Ski Club Elects Officers Tonight
## 
### 
Ski Club officers will be elected
at the club meeting at 7:30 tonight in
Cubberley Auditorium.
Peggy Vorse, Bob Greenebaum,
John Miller, and Steve Lee, members
of the nominating committee, will
suggest candidates. Nominations
from the floor will also be acceptcd.
"A Nation Skis," a movie demon
strating Swiss techniques, will be
shown
A Ski Club Excom meeting for
everyone is scheduled for 4:30 p.m.
