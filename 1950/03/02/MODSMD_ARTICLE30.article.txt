# Organist to Perform Bach Chorale Prelude
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney will give the
regular organ recital today at 4:15
p.m. in Memorial Church.
His selections will include "Fugue
in C Major" by Buxtehude; ''Trio
in F Major," Krebs; "Chorale Pre
lude on 'Forty Days and Forty
Nights'," Bach; "Sonata No. 3 for
Organ," Kindesrnith; and "Carillon
de Westminster," Vierne.
