# ILWU Signs Oath
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO The CIO In
ternational Longshoremen's and
Warehousemen's Union announced
tonight its officers, including Pres
ident Harry Bridges, have signed
non-Communist affidavits under the
Taft-Hartley Act.
The signing will permit. Bridges'
National Labor Relations Board
union to appear on the ballot in any
election which may be held as the
result of the AFL teamsters' efforts
to claim union jurisdictions now
held by the ILWU.
