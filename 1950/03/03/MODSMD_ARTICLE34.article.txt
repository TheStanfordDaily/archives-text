# Branner Formal Bids Still on Sale
## 
### 
Extra bids for the Branner formal
Saturday night are now on sale at
the ASSLJ Office for $2 each.
The dance, "Mardi Gras," will be
held in the Branner lobby from 9 to 1.
Music will be provided by Burt
Whiting and his orchestra. During
the intermission the Med School
Quartet will sing several pieces, and
Alue Niven will perform some Span
ish dances.
In keeping with lhe theme, multi
colored balloons, crepe paper, Con
fetti, and large murals of carnival
figures will decorate the walls.
