# Creative Music Contest Is Open
## 
### 
A $100 prize is being offered by
the Humanities Department in a
contest for creative work in music,
open to all registered students in the
University. The medium of the
work is not restricted; it may be
either vocal or instrumental,
Contestants should submit either
one piece in large form, such as a
sonata or string quartet, or a group
of not less than three shorter pieces
such as songs or piano selections.
Entries must be turned in to the
Music Department by May 2.
