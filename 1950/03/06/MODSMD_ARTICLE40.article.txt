# Gym Squad Loses at Cal
## 
### 
Stanford's gymnastic squad was
defeated Saturday by the Univer
sity of California 66 to 15.
The Indians were able to get only
one first place during the meet. Bob
White topped the competition on the
horizontal bar. The rest of the local
scoring was won through second
and third places.
Dick Marquardt, who was nar
rowly beaten on the rings, placed
second in the all-around competi
tion; Keith McGregor was third.
