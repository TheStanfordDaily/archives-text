# Legislators to Meet
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO — Governor War
ren today called a second special
session of the Legislature for tomor
row—just to correct an error in a
1949 bill that gave salary increases
to officers of the wrong county.
His action means there will be
three sessions rolled up into one:
the annual budget session, a spe
cial session called to act on an 18-
point program, and a second special
session to permit correction of an
error last year when the Legislature
voted Amador County officials pay
boosts intended for officials of El
Dorado County.
