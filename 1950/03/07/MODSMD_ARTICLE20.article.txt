# Lake to Stay Open Rest of Quarter
## 
### 
Lake Lagunita will remain open
foe swimming and boating during
dead week and finals week. The
regular open hours, 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Saturdays and Sundays and I to 6
p.m. weekdays, will be maintained.
Lifeguards are on duty during the
open hours. Swimming and boating
are prohibited at all other times.
The lake will also be kept open
during spring vacation for students
remaining on campus between
quarters. Tentative plans arc being
made to open the lake in the morn
ings during the vacation.
