# Coplon Trial to Jurors
## 
### 
NEW YOICK A federal court
jury deliberated tonight the guilt or
innocence of Judith Coplon and
Valentin A. Gubitchev on charges of
plotting to spy for the Russians.
Federal Judge Sylvester J. Ryan
put the fate of the ex-government,
girl and the Soviet engineer in the
hands of the six men and six women
jurors at 4:34 p.m. (EST).
The trial, attracting international
interest, marks the first time in the
cold war that a Russian citizen has
been brought into a U.S. court as
an accused spy.
