# Stanford Singers In Bach Concerts
## 
### 
The tenth concert in the Bach an
niversary series will be presented
this afternoon at 4:15 in Memorial
Church.
Herbert B. Nanney, Memorial
Church organist, assisted by Mari
lyn Hopwood, Harold C. Schmidt,
Carl Hutchinson, and the Stanford
| University Singers, will perform.
Today's program is open to the
public and will include the follow
ing works: Prelude and Fugue in A
Minor, two chorale preludes from
the "Orgelbuechlein," Cantata 135,
; Allegro from Trio Sonata No. 5 in
| C Major, three settings of the cho
rale "Jesu, Meine Freunde," and
Futfiie in F< Flat Major (St Anne'*:)
