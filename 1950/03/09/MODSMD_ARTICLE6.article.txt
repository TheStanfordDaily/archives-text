# Narcotics Controversy
## 
### 
SACICAMKNTO — Attorney Gen
eral Fred M. Howscr charged today
that federal narcotics officers arc
' trying to cover up their own blun
ders and bungling" by accusations
against the State Narcotics Bureau
in connection with the Abe Davidian
slaying.
"Narcotics enforcement," said
How: or. "is so important it demands
a legislative inquiry into the con
flict. between federal and state nar
cotics officers."
