# Sala and Cole Will Aim For NCAA Tank Record
## 
### 
Ralph Sala and Pete Cole will go
after the National College 1.500-
meter freestyle record tomorrow
morning at 11:30 at Encina pool fol
lowing a meet between the varsity
and Cal Poly.
The record is 18:51).3, set in 1934
by Jack Medica of San Jose
State, but last week in the Big Ten
meet, Jack Taylor ol Ohio State
broke the record by doing the dis
tance, just short of a mile, in
18:58.1.
