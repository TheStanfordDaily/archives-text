# 'Album' Held Over Snuday
## 
### 
The Art Gallery showing of ''The
Stanford Family Album," a display of
photographs and personal possessions
of Senator and Mi's. Leland Stanford
and their son, Leland Junior, has
been extended through this Sunday.
The display had originally been
scheduled to close Friday, but Mrs.
Helen Cross, associate director of the
Art Gallery, announced today that the
showing has been extended to give
Palo Alto area residents a chance to
view it on an additional weekend.
The exhibit has been ai ranged by
Mrs. Cross in the form of an album,
with historical objects placed in large,
gilded frames originally purchased by
Mrs Stanford for her art collection.
