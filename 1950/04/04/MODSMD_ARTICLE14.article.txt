# Services Planned For Holy Week
## 
### 
In celebration of Iloly Week, the j
University chaplains will conduct ;
services of prayer and meditation j
today and tomorrow in Memorial j
Church at 4:15.
On Thursday afternoon at 4:15,
Herbert B. Nanney will play at the
Bach Anniversary Series concert.
Thursday eveninq at 8, Chaplain
Johnston and Mr. Minto will hold
a holy communion service in the
church.
The observance of Holy Week
will continue on Friday with serv
ices in the church from noon till 3.
The chaplains, assisted by ministers
from Palo Alto, will officiate. The
Seven Words of Christ will be the
theme for this service.
Easter Sunday will be celebrated j
in Frost Amphitheater at fi a.m. I
The Church Committee will assist
the chaplains and ministers from !
the the community in the sunrise
service. Van Kussrow, tenor, will
sing a solo with Robert Keine at the
organ. At 11, Chaplain Johnston
will preach on "The Pilgrim Faith"
in Memorial Church for the Festi
val Service.
