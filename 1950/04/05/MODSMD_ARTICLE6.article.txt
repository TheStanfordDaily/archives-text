# Slot Machine Ban
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO — The California
Legislature slapped a tight ban on
slot machines today.
Effective 00 days after the session
ends, it will be against the law to
own, manufacture, store, or deal in
the gambling devices with the lure
of jackpot payoffs. It is illegal un
der present law to play slot ma
chines, but mere possession is not.
