# Four- Year Lettermen Hold Meeting Today
## 
### 
All Block "S" Society members
who have earned four blocks in
any one sport will meet today in
front of the Main Li be at 1 p.m.
to have their picture taken for the
Block "S" page of the 1950 Quad.
Men who will earn their fourth
letter in a spring sport this year
will be included in this shot.
