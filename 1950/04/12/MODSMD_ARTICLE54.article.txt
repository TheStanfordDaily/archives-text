# Yardley, Chavalas Named for Tour
## 
### 
Frank Walsh, director of basket
ball for the San Francisco Cow
Palace, announced yesterday be had
completed arrangements to take a
team of West Coast basketball play
ers on a six-week tour this summer
of eight South American countries.
Players so far selected by Walsh
are: Stan Christie, center, USC;
George Yardleytf forward, and Gus
Chavalas, guard, both of Stanford;
Joe McNamee, center, and Rene
Herrerias, guard, both of USF.
