# Epstein to Teach At Berlin School
## 
### 
Dr. Fritz T. Epstein of the Hoover
Library Staff, who was stationed in
Berlin after the war, will return to
that city to teach history at the Free
University during the spring and
summer.
Dr. Epstein will leach courses and
seminars in modern history and
serve as a specialist in the field of
historical research under a U.S.
State Department exchange pro
gram.
The Stanford professor's assign
ment is intended to strengthen ties
which were established between the
two universities.
