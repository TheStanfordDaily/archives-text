# Alpha Sigs Plan Open Dance for Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Alpha Sig house will be
transformed into a "house of the
dudes" tomorrow night from 9 to 1
when the fraternity will sponsor its
annual all - campus Dude Ranch
dance.
Heading up the list of all-Western
decorations are a '49er barroom
complete with checkered* table
cloths, oil lamps, and a wide assort
ment of empty bottles of all sizes,
shapes, and labels.
