# Fencers Place Second In Intercollegiate Meet
## 
### 
Stanford's fencing team placed
second in the Pacific Coast Inter
collegiate Championships held Sat
urday afternoon and evening. The
Indian epee team placed first in the
meet; the foils, second; and the sa
ber, second.
Richard Gross was the individual
standout for Stanford. He placed
first in both the foil and epee and
second in the saber. Gus Schless
was third in the epee.
