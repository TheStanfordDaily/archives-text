# Maragon Perjury Trial
## 
### 
WASHINGTON — The perjury
trial of John Maragon. the former
bootblack who made friends at the
White House, opens tomorrow in
federal district court.
Maragon has pleaded innocent to
charges of lying about business and
financial affairs to a Senate com
mittee that investigated operations
of five percenters — persons who
represent others in deals with the
government for a fee that usually is
five per cent.
