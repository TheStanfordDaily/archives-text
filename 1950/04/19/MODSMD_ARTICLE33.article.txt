# On Your Dial-880
## 
### 
Election results will be broadcast
on KZSU today starting at 4:30
p.m. As soon as results come in,
they will be reported by remote
control direct from the Union. Elec
tion returns and recorded music
will be presented until all results
are in.
Two programs, however, will be
heard at their regular time. "Andre
Presents," the Bill Bowles - Scotty
Doggett - Dan Evans show, will be
broadcast from 9:30 to 10. George
Bahrs' "Dixieland Show" will be
presented at 10:30.
