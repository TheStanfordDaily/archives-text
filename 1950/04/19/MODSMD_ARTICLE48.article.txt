# Unemployment Seminar Will Be Held Tomorrow
## 
### 
A seminar on unemployment will
be held tomorrow at 7:30 p.m. in the
San Jose Civic Auditorium. All
students who arc interested in jobs
in Santa Clara County are invited
to attend.
The program will be divided into
industrial, retail, and governmental
sections. Tickets arc free. They
may be obtained at the Placement
Service.
