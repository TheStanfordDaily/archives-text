# Scabbard and Blade Elects New Officers
## 
### 
Willie Kea was elected at last
week's meeting to the presidency of
Scabbard and Blade for 1950-51.
Elected with Kea as officers of
the honorary military fraternity
were Stu Whittlesey, vice - presi
dent; Conrad von Bibra, treasurer;
and John Merriam, secretary.
Bob Volk was named pledge
chairman.
