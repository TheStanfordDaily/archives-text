# Faculty Members At History Meeting
## 
### 
Two members of the Stanford
History Department faculty, Dr.
George Knoles and Dr. Edward
White, are attending meetings of
the Mississippi Valley Historical
Association this week in Oklahoma
City, Okla.
Dr. Knoles will read a paper at
tomorrow's session on "My Ameri
can Impressions," an English criti
cism of American civilization since
1919.
The Stanford history professor
spoke Tuesday at a meeting of the
Stanford Club in Denver, Colo.
Dr. Knoles will return to the
Stanford campus Monday, while Dr.
White, who is on leave this quar
ter, will go to Washington. D.C., to
undertake some historical research.
