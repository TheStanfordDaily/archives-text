# Closed Events
## 
### 
A crowded social calendar for
this weekend reflects the party
time spirit of spring quarter, with
14 closed parties scheduled.
Lagunita Court's annual spring
formal, April Arabesque, is slated
for tonight with dancing to the
music of Frank Beazley and his or
chestra. Also on the calendar for
today are Delta Tau Delta, Phi
Gamma Delta, and Beta Theta Pi
fraternity parties.
Closed formats on the calendar
for tomorrow night include the an
nual Theta Delta Chi and Durand
House combined dance and the
Roth House spring formal. Parties
are also slated by the following:
Frosh wing Toyon, Village 1508
and 312, and Delta Upsilon.
