# Dames' Bridge Group To Meet Thursday
## 
### 
All wives of Stanford students are
welcome to attend the bridge sec
tion of the Stanford Dames, accord
ing to Mrs. L. L. Baskins of Stan
ford Village and Mrs. G. R. Dunn
of Redwood City, chairmen of the
group.
The bridge group will meet on
Thursday evening in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse
and there will be tables for ad
vanced, intermediate, and beginning
players.
