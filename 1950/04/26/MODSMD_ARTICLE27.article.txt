# Minto to Speak On Roundtable
## 
### 
"What Can Unification of
Churches Do for World Peace?"
will be the topic for discussion on
the Stanford Student Roundtable
broadcast today at 3 p.m. on station
KIBE.
Speakers will be the Rev. Robeit
M. Minto, associate chaplain at
Stanford, and three students:
Tracy Strong Jr., graduate stu
dent in education; Franklin Held,
engineering student; and Thomas
Bartlett, lower division.
Henry Grandin, student in the
Graduate School of Business, will
be the moderator.
The program will be rebroadcast
Sunday at 3 p.m. on KIBE.
