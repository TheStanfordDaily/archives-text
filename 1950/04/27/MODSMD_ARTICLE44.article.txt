# Bookstore Selling Senior Programs
## 
### 
Senior programs and announce
ments are now on sale at the
Stanford Bookstore.
The programs, which cost .$1.25,
contain names of those receiving
any degree in June, list of class
officers, chairmen of committees,
Class Week activities chairmen,
and pictures of the class.
Announcements of graduation
sell for 15 cents and can he pur
chased in the stationery depart
ment of the Bookstore.
The tentative deadline on sales
is May 13.
