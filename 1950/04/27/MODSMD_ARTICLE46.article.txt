# Five Colleges Enter Riders in Horse Show
## 
### 
Riders from five Bay Area col
leges will compete in a 10-event in
tercollegiate horse show Saturday
at 1:30 p.m. on Stanford's practice
polo field near the Red Barn, just
1 off Junipero Serra Boulevard on
Los Arboles Avenue.
Trophies will be awarded for
winners of three events: advanced
, horsemanship, open jumping, and
Western trail competition. The show
will be open to the public without
i charge.
