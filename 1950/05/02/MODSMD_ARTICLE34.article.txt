# Red Cross Life Saving Review Course Offered
## 
### 
Holders of Red Cross water safety
instructor's cards and senior life
saving certificates may enroll in the
two refresher courses being offered
by the Palo Alto Area Red Cross
chapter this May. They will meet to
day, Thursday, and May 9 and 11
from 7 to 10 p.m. at the Palo Alto
High School pool.
