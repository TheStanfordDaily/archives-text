# KIBE To Air Goldet On UN
## 
### 
Dr. Antoine Goldet, United Na
tions official, will give his views of
the question "Is Russia Trying to
Undermine the UN?" on a Stanford
Student Roundtable broadcast to
day at 3 p.m. on station KIBE.
Dr. Goldet, principal director of
the UN Department of Economic
Affairs, will be the speaker in a re
corded interview conducted by
Hank Grandin, roundtable moder
ator and a student in the Graduate
School of Business.
Dr. Goldet presided at the UN
Model Assembly which met Friday
under the auspices of the Stanford
lIR.
The roundtable program will be
rebroadcast Sunday at 3 p.m. on
KIBE.
