water." That aqua ROOS BROS, suit she's wearing was made for swimming . . . it's a mixture of
quick-drying nylon and layon. The beach coat from the CLOTHES CLOSET seconds our wet mo
tion . . . it's a gay plaid Dan River cotton, wrinkle-shed and water-repellent.
