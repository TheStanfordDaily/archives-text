# Advanced Course For First Aiders
## 
### 
Stanford students who wish to
take an intensive advanced first aid
course to qualify for instructors'
training may enroll in the special
class tonight at the Palo Alto Area
Red Cross chapter house, 400 Mitch
ell Lane, Palo Alto.
The class will meet from 7 to 10
p.m. with Lt. Clarence Anderson of
Palo Alto as the volunteer instruc
tor.
