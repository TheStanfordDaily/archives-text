# Quad Staff Elects Editor Tomorrow
## 
### 
The Quad election for next year's
editor will be held tomorrow, Bill
Larmer, present editor, announced.
All members of the editorial staff
may vote at the Quad office any ;
time during the day.
Thursday night the annual ban
quet will be held at Chez Yvonne.
All members of the Quad editorial,
business, and photography staffs
are invited.
