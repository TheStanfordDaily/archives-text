# Cardinal Tryouts Close Wednesday
## 
###

try out for the 10 positions open on
Cardinals next year. Interviews will
be held today and tomorrow from
3:30 to 5 p.m. in tbe Excom room.
There are six positions for seniors
and four for juniors to be filled.
Past experience in student activ
ities, interest in and knowledge of
Cardinals activities, and the presen
tation of original ideas for Cardi
nals are the bases for the selection
of members.
