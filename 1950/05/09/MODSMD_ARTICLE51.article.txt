# Men's Glee Offers '50 'Sing-Capades'
## 
### 
"The Sing-Capades of 1950" will
be presented Friday evening in
Cubberley Auditorium by the Stan
ford Men's Glee Club.
Under the direction of Norman R.
Cory, the club will offer a wide se
lection of numbers ranging from
"The Battle Hymn of the Republic"
and "Were You There?" to Cole
Porter's "Night and Day," accord
ing to Buz Howe, president.
