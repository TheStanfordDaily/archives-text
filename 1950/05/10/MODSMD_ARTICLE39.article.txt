# Houses Plan For Foreign Students
## 
### 
Representatives of living groups
that wish to house next year's for
eign scholarship students will meet
at the lIR shack May 16 at 4:30 p.m.
The purpose of the first meeting
will be to sign up for next year's
foreign students. Living groups that
participate in the foreign scholar
ship program pledge themselves to
provide expenses and board and
room for the students.
Living groups should be prepared
to tell if they want a foreign schol
arship student for more than one
quarter.
A second meeting will be held on
May 24, when living-group repre
sentatives will choose the five for
eign scholarship students that they
want to house.
