# Stanfordites Visit Spanish Consulate
## 
### 
Five members of the Foreign Ca
reer Association visited the Span
ish consulate in San Francisco last
Friday.
Alberto Herce, acting consul, gave
a brief resume of the consulate's
commercial and cultural relations
with the United States, and com
mented on Spain's government and
people.
The Foreign Career Association is
made up of a group of students in
terested in private and government
caerers. Periodically they take field
trips to consulates, import - export
firms, banking houses, etc., to learn
about the opportunities available in
foreign work, and make contacts for
future employment.
