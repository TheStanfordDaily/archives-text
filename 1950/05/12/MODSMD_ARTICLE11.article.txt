# Howser to Speak, Visit Cellar Today
## 
### 
Attorney-General Fred N. Howsor
will meet informally with a group of
interested students and faculty about
4:15 this afternoon in the Cellar.
In connection with his compaign
for renomination at the primaries this
June, Howser is speaking at the Ma
sonic Temple in Palo Alto at 3 this
afternoon. Included in his audience
will be various local Republican
groups.
Howser's Palo Aito speech will deal
with current California problems, in
cluding the present crime situation.
In addition to this, he will refute
current accusation leveled at his de
partment of the present administra
tion.
