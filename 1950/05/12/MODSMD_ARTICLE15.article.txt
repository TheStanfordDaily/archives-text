# Chappie Comics To Crash Campus This Wednesday
## 
### 
The Chappie will come out next
Wednesday.
This issue features the annual
Crash comics, including comic strips,
phony ads, and a four-page parody
on The Daily.
Among the comic strips will be the
Prone Ranger, Little Asinine, Little
Ebineizer, Steve Crevice, Capt. Mar
bles, and Petrol Gulch.
There will also be a story entitled
"Wounded Eagle," or, "Who Put the
Bird in Mrs. Murphy's Aviary?"
Crash Comics will cost 30 cents.
