# Tickets for Soph Cotillion on Sale
## 
### 
Tickets for the Sophomore Cotil
lion, which will be held tomorrow
night from 9 to 1 in the Gold Room
of the Fairmont Hotel, v/ill be on sale
today for the last time, announced
Wayne Alley, Cotillion chairman.
They may be bought from living
group representatives and at the
ASSU office for $2.80.
Music for the formal affair will be
provided by Howard Frederic and
his orchestra.
The committee in charge of the Co
tillion is Sally Casella, Lorie Tabin,
and Tom Griffiths.
