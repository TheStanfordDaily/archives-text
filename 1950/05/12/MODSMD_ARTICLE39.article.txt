# Roble Spring Ball Has Chinese Motif
## 
### 
Chinese lanterns and fortune cakes
will complete the picture of "Spring
in a Chinese Garden" at the Roble
formal tonight in the Women's Gym
from 9 to 1.
Tickets are on sale for $2.40 from
Roble corridor representatives and
from Carole Quiat at Branner.
Freshmen from Roble, Branner, Ven
tura, and off-campus are invited.
Music for the affair will be by
Harry Diner's orchestra. Refresh
ments will be served.
General chairman for the dance is
Rachel Oatman.
