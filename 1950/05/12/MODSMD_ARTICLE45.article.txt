# Natators Meet Cal For Second Time
## 
### 
The varsity and frosh swimming
teams meet Cal tomorrow in a
double dual meet at Berkeley. It
will be the second meeting for the
two schools; the Indians won two
last month.
Leading the varsity will be co
captains Jack Weeden and Ralph
Sala, Pete Cole, Bob Wycoff, Bob
Frojen, Ernie Polte, and Bruce Put
nam, while Mel Smith, Bert Holi
day, George Mowat, AI Marsh,
Dayr Peterman, and Don Hanson
will spark the Papooses.
