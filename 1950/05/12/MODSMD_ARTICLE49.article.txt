# Frosh Nine Faces Bears
## 
### 
In what will be the rubber game
of a three-game series, Stanford's
frosh baseballers will lock horns
with Cal's freshman team at Berke
ley tomorrow afternoon.
Stanford, besides gunning for a
Cal win, will also be after their
seventh straight triumph. Not since
the first Cal game have the Pa
pooses tasted defeat.
Probable starters include: Bob
Murphy, pitcher; Jack Shepard,
catcher; Jim Agar, first base; Frank
Yapp, second; Jim Harding, third;
Dick Cole, shortstop; Gene Shirley,
left field; A 1 Stellar, center field;
and George Zaninovich, right.
