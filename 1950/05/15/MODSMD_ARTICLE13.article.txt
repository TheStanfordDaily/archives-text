# Students To Quiz Profs
## 
### 
Are students smarter than pro
fessors?
Five students will challenge five
faculty members in a quiz battle
Wednesday at 7:30 in Cubberley
Auditorium. Proceeds of the 25-
cent admission charge will go to the
Con Home. Tickets will be sold at
the door.
Members of the faculty team in
clude Dr. Virgil Whitaker, Rev.
Robert Minto, Dr. Arnaud Leavelle,
Dr. Paul Baran, and Dr. Thomas
Bailey. The student team is com
posed of Diane Porter, Sandra Day,
Pitch Johnson. Art Herzog, and Hap
Black. Quizmaster for the evening
will be Dow Carpenter.
Joyce Oldham is in charge of the
show, which is sponsored by Car
dinals.
