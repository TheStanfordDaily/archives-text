# Sigma Chi's Pick 1950 Sweetheart
## 
### 
Joyce Engblom of Roble was pre
sented as the 1950 Sweetheart of
Sigma Chi at the fraternity's annual
Sweetheart Ball Saturday night.
Miss Engblom was chosen from 15
nominees representing nearly all
the women's residences.
Making up the Sweetheart's court
were Tina Clapp of Lagunita, Sue
Harr of Madrono, Peggy Pooley of
Roble, and Mary White of Roble.
About 250 persons watched while
House President Don Wells in
troduced the chapter's new Sweet
heart. '
