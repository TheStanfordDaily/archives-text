# State, Local Job News
## Job Openings Sought in East 
### 
In an effort to increase potential
job opportunities for Stanford grad
uates, the Placement Service is cur
rently mailing leaflets to West Coast
and Eastern employers describing
the variety of positions which Uni
versity graduates can fill.
The leaflet, "Building Your Busi
ness for the Future," is particularly
intended for small firms. It points
out that such small firms may use
graduates as possible future execu
tives and lists the types of jobs for
which Stanford students are espe
cially well qualified.
