# 'Battle of Brains' Matches Students Against Faculty
## 
### 
The student-faculty quiz show,
matching a team of five professors
against five students, will be held
tomorrow night at 7:30 in Cubberley
Auditorium.
Tickets for the "battle of brains"
will be sold at the door for 25 cents.
Proceeds will go to Con Home.
A student team composed of Pitch
Johnson, Diane Porter, Art. Herzog,
Sandra Day, and Hap Black will
challenge faculty members Dr.
Thomas Bailey, Dr. Arnaud Lea
vellc, Dr. Virgil Whitaker, Dr. Cyril
C. Means, and Rev. Robert Minto.
Dow Carpenter will be quizmaster.
