# Tickets Sell To Senior Ball
## 
### 
Tickets to the Senior Ball, featur
ing the music of Freddy Martin and
his orchestra, will go on sale today
in the ASSU Office.
The Ball, which will be held June
15 at the Diablo Country Club, is
open to the entire campus. How
ever, only seniors may buy tickets
during the first two weeks of ticket
sales. Alter May 29 tickets will be
available to all students.
The tickets are $8 per couple and
include the price of breakfast and
use of the pool at the club.
Two orchestras will provide con
tinuous music throughout the eve
ning. Martin's orchestra, featuring
vocalist Merve Griffin and the Mar
tin Men, will play for the main por
tion of the dance, while Alex Duehin
and his orchestra will furnish music
for dancing while breakfast is being
served from 2 to 4 a.m.
Free bottles of champagne will be
raffled off during the evening, and
free souvenir photos will be available
to all couples who want them.
The dance will begin at 10 p.m.
Persons wishing to spend the night
may make reservations by writing
directly to Diablo Country Club,
Diablo. Calif.
