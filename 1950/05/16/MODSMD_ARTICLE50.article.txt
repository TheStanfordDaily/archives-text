# Off-Campus Room Signups Scheduled
## 
### 
Village and off-campus men who
wish to live at Toyon or Stern
Halls should sign up tomorrow and
Thursday, Ted Roelfsema of the
Men's Housing Committee said yes
terday.
Signup cards will be available at
the ASSU office and at the Village
Student Personnel Office.
Village and off-campus men are
to be assigned to 70 per cent of the
total vacancies in Toyon and Stern
Halls.
