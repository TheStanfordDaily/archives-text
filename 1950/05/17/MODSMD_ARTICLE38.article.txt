# Players Slate Tryouts For June Comedy
## 
### 
Tryouts will be held Monday and
Tuesday for "Accent on Youth," a
modern comedy by Samuel Ra
phaelson, which opens June 28 as
the first production of the Stanford
Players' summer season.
Director Norman D. Philbrick,
assistant professor of Speech and
drama, urges students or faculty
members who will be here summer
quarter to try out. Parts lor three
women and six men are available.
Monday's tryouts will be held
from 4:15 to 6 p.m. in the Little
Theater. On Tuesday the tryouts
will be held from 8:30 to 10 p.m. in
Room 205 next to the KZSU studio
upstairs in Memorial Hall.
Rehearsals will begin May 25, but
no scheduled rehearsals will be held
during dead week or finals.
Those interested in trying out
may obtain copies of the play in
Room 102 in Memorial Hall.
