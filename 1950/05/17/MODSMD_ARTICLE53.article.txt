# 'Mitch' Given Gimbel Prize
## 
### 
Emery Mitchell, Stanford's pow
erhouse athlete from Eureka, was
awarded the J. Gimbel Attitude
Prize yesterday.
This prize is annually awarded
the Stanford senior who has shown
the best mental attitude toward
athletics during his competitive ca
reer.
"Mitch" was a star fullback on the
Indian football teams of '48 and '49
and is undefeated as a heavyweight
boxer during his career here on the
Farm.
