# Employment Com Interviews Set
## 
### 
Interviews for the student-faculty
employment committee for next
year will he held tomorrow from 3
to \ p.m. and Monday from 2 to 3
p.m. in the ASSU president's office.
The committee decides who can
sell goods or advertise on campus.
Next year if will also take care of
hashing complaints. The student
half of the committee is composed
of an Excom member and three
other students. Two of these must
have worked for the University
Ted Buttner, next year's ASSU
president, will select the student
members. Anyone who wishes to
apply but who can't be interviewed
at the times announced should leave
a note for Buttner in the ASSU
office.
