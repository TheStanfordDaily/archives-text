# Chorale Preludes Head Keine Recital
## 
### 
Robert Keine, assistant organist,
will play today at the regular Thurs
day afternoon organ recitals in the
Memorial Church. The recital starts
at 4:15 p.m.
Mr. Keine will play three chorale
preludes and Fuge in G Major by
J. S. Bach; the Mass for the use of
Parishes by Couperin; Diferencias on
"El Canto del Caballero" by Cabezon;
"Chorale" Opus 57, No. 4, by Joseph
Jongen; and a chorale prelude by
J. P. Kellner.
