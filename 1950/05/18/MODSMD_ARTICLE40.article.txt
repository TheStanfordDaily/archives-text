# Mural Track Meet Trials Tomorrow
## 
### 
Entrants in the intramural
track meet are reminded that the
trials will start promptly at 4 p.m.
tomorrow.
Final events will he held Mon
day afternoon at 4.

400 contestants. Director Chuck

office up to this afternoon.

tie between Delta Chi and the
Dekes.
