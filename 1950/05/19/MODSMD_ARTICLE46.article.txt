# International Group to Meet
## 
### 
Monday night in the Women's
Clubhouse, the second meeting of a
new campus group organized to
study the problems of a new world
government will feature talks con
cerning future activities.
Different ideas for future proj
ects will be presented by Bob Inglis
and Fritjof Thygeson. Other sug
gestions and discussion will come
from the group.
The new organization, as yet un
named, is still forming on campus.
Its stated purpose is studying "the
problems involved in setting up a
world organization for assuring
peace by enforceable international
law."
Membership is open to all Stan
ford students and faculty. At pres
ent, the group includes individuals
from lIR, Student Federalists, At
lantic Union, and NAACP.
