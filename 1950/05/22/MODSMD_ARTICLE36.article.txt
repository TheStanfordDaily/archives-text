# Senior Ice Cream Sale To Be Held Wednesday
## 
### 
The senior class will hold their
last ice cream sale this Wednesday.
Trucks will lie stationed at the
History and Engineering corners i
during the afternoon. At night
members of the class will sell ice i
cream at living groups on campus
and at the Village.
