# Commies in Cambodia
## 
### 
PNOMPENH, Indo-China Official
French sources reported today that
Communist troops of Ho Ghi-Minh
had infiltrated the southern Indo-
China state of Cambodia and
opened a corridor to Thailand to ob
tain smuggled arms.
French military quarters estimate
that about 3,000 of Ho's Communists
have trickled over the north border
of Cambodia and occupied a broad
zone. They are said to have opened
overland routes to Thailand through
the coastal region fronting on Ihe
Gulf of Siam.
