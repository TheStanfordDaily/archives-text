# Farm Orators Enter Contest Final Rounds
## 
### 
Alan Enthoven and Joe Farman,
Stanford orators at the San Fran
cisco World Trade Fair last week,
were successful in the preliminary
rounds of oratory.
They will speak in the final round
of the contest in the Scottish Rite
Auditorium at Sutter and Van Ness
in San Francisco tomorrow at a
banquet beginning at 6:30 p.m.
Guest of honor will be Mayor Elmer
Robinson.
