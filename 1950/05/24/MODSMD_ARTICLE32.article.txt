# Recording Revives 1950 Spring Show
## 
### 
A recording tape of the entire
Spring Show will be played at 5 this
afternoon in the Little Theater. Or
ders for records will then be taken.
Anyone interested in hearing the
performance may attend.
Music folios containing six songs
from the show are now on sale at
the Bookstore for $1. The songs are
"Mind of My Own," "Away from
You," "Hullaballoo," "After the
Morning After," "Someday," and
"Human Nature."
