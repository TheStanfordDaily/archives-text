# Eating Clubs Name Kelson New Prexy
## 
### 
Leland Kelson, 23, of Los Arcos,
was recently elected inter - club
president for the 1950-51 academic
year.
Kelson, who hails from Seattle, is
a member of the Stanford Graduate
School of Business. He succeeds
Sarge Littlehale of El Toro, who
will graduate from Business School
in "June.
Kelson has served this year as
club representative on the Eating
Club Building Committee, which is
working on final plans for the clubs'
proposed $135,000 building.
