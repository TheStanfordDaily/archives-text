# Three Men Are Chosen For Fraternity Board
## 
### 
Three undergraduate fraternity
men were recently chosen to serve
one-year terms on the Interfrater
nity Board of Control, Dave Lari
mer, Interfraternity Council presi
dent, announced last week.
The three are Walt Peter, Alpha
Kappa Lambda; James Wood, Zeta
Psi, and Art Brown, Theta Chi.
They were elected to the Board by
members of Interfraternity Council
