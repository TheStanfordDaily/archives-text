# Knight Plans Visit Here Wednesday
## 
### 
The Young Republicans of Stan
ford have formed plans for Lt. Gov.
Goodwin Knight's visit to campus
next, Wednesday. Lt. Gov. Knight
is a Stanford Law School alumnus
and was at one time one of the
Farm's top debaters.
At. their meeting last Tuesday, the
Young Republicans read and ac
cepted a constitution for the forma
tion of a new club. At the meeting,
the officers elected were president,
Mike Gugliemetti; vice - presidents,
Merrill Mcnsor and Howard Brooks;
and secretary - treasurer, Ralph
Brogdon.
The members of the three com
mittees formed are Political Actions,
Dick Draklich; Political Studies.
Howard Brooks; and Publicity,
Doug White.
