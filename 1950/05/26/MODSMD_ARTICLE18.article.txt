# Time Schedules Ready Tomorrow
## 
### 
Autumn quarter preliminary
time schedules will come out to
morrow. Students will be able to
get them at the office of the Reg
istrar in Temporary Building A.
Pre-registration for the autumn
quarter starts Tuesday for upper
division and graduate students.
They will complete pre-register
ing Wednesday and lower division
students will begin Thursday.
Lower division pre - registration
for this summer will be completed
tomorrow. Students in doubt as
to procedure may refer to the
summer quarter time schedule or
to yesterday's Daily.
