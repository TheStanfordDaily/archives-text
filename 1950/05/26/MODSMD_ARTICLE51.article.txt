# Farm Debaters Place in Contest
## 
### 
Stanford debaters Joe Farinan
and Al Enthoven placed second and
third respectively in an oratorical
contest Wednesday sponsored by
the Junior Chamber of Commerce
in San Francisco as part of its
World Trade Fair Week.
Subject for oratory was Ameri
can enterprise in world commerce.
There were 15 entrants represent
ing universities and colleges in the
Bay Area. The winner was Ishab
Zhair, Indonesian student at the
University of California.
The contest was held a I the Scot
tish Rite Auditorium in San Fran
cisco. Guest of honor was Mayor
Elmer Robinson.
