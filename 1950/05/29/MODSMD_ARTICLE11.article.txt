# Graduation Honors Listed
## 68 to Receive Degrees 'With Great Distinction' 
### 
As a recognition of high scholastic attainment, Stanford awards
the Bachelor's Degree with "great distinction" to approximately
five per cent and with "distinction" to approximately ten per cent
of the graduating class each year. The awards are based primarily
on the work of the last two years.
Students whose scholastic standing is high and whose names do

not appear on the list are urged to
call at the Scholarship Committee
Office, Temporary Building A, to
inquire about eligibility require
ments for graduation honors.
