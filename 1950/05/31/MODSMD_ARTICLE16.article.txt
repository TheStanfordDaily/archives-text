# UD Pre-Reg For Autumn Ends Today
## 
### 
Today is the last day of upper di
vision and graduate pre-registration
for autumn quarter. Lower division
pre-registration will begin tomorrow.
Those students in the upper di
vision who have not yet done so
should complete pre-registration
procedure and turn in their official
study cards before 4:UO this after
noon. These cards must be given In
a registration clerk at the Nitery or
turned in at room GO of temporary
building A before the deadline.
Lower division pre-registration
starts tomorrow when students will
consult with their advisors and get
their signatures on official study
lists. This may also be done Friday.
The Nitery will be open Friday,
Saturday, and Monday for signing
class lists and for getting study cards
officially stamped.
