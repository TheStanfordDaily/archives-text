# George Houle
## 
### 
George Houle, graduate student
and assistant director of the Stan
ford Symphony Orchestra, won the
1950 Humanities prize in musical
composition of $100 with his "Sona
tina for Oboe and Harpsichord" and
a group of songs.
The prize is awarded annually by
the School of Humanities and ad
ministered by the Music Depart
ment. Other entrants in the contest
may pick up their manuscripts at
the Music Department office.
