# OFFICIAL NOTICES

### 
CIVIL SERVICE
The U.S. Civil Service Commis
sion has announced an examination
for making probational appoint
ments to the position of Chemist,
GS-9 to GS-12, in the various fed
eral agencies in the states of Ari
zona, California, and Nevada. Sal
aries for these positions range from
$4,000 to $(>,400 a year.
No written test is required. Com
petitors will be rated on the basis
of their professional experience and
graduate study in chemistry.
Further information and applica
tion forms may be obtained from
the Regional Director, Twelfth U.S.
Civil Service Region, Room 128,
Appraisers Building, 630 Sansome
Street, San Francisco 11, California,
or from the Secretary, Board of U.S.
Civil Service Examiners, at any
first- or second - class post office
where this announcement is posted.
Applications must be filed with
the Director, Twelfth U.S. Civil
Service Region, at the above ad
dress, and will be accepted until
further notice.
