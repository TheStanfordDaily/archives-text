# Official
## ROOM, BOARD BILLS 
### 
Wednesday, July 5, is the deadline
for payment of University room and
board fees for the summer quarter.
Fees may be paid at the cashier's
window in the lobby of Encina Hall
from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. today, Mon
day, and Wednesday. Bills will be
subject to fines after Wednesday.
