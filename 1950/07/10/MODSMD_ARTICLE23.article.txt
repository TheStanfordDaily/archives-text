# Student Activities YOUNG DEMOCRATS
## 
### 
Five members of the Palo Alto-
Stanford Young Democrats will
travel to Los Angeles on July 21 to
attend the state convention. Repre
senting the local group will be Carl
Stover, president; Bob Bennett,
vice-president; Hobert Burns, Dick
Kelton, and Joan Witzman.
The convention, to be held at the
Alexandria Hotel, will frame the
junior party's platform and will
elect state officers for the coming
two - year term. Striving for in
creased Democratic registration and
for the election of Democratic can
didates in November, the delegates
will be addressed by Helen Gaha
gan Douglas, James Roosevelt, and
Pat Brown, California's Democratic
candidates.
