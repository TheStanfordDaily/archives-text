Old Union will be open from 8 a.m.
(o 10:15 p.m. for all students, Ex
com anniounced at its recent meet
ing.
If the room is loeked, the key may
be picked up at the cigar store in
the Union Cellar. The student tak
ing the key will be held responsible
to see that the key is returned be
fore 10:15 at night.
