# Informal at Encina Will Be Friday Night
## 
### 
The Encina Summer Informal
will be held this Friday rather
than Saturday, as previously an
nounced, in Encina lounge. All
students, with or without dates,
are invited, and grad students
are urged to bring their wives.
Bert Whiting's orchestra will
play for dancing from i) to 1. Ad
mission is free for the sport-attire
event, and refreshments will be
served throughout the evening.
