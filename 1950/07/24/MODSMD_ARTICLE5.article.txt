# Tuchow Gets Job With Warner Bros.
## 
### 
Seymour Tuchow. recently ap
pearing with the Stanford Players
in '"A Storm, A Tiger," has been
signed by Warner Brothers studio
to a long-term contract.
This contract arose directly with
his performance in Greendale's
play. Tuchow came to Stanford as
a Junior Artist-in-Residence, hav
ing won this award in a national
competition during the sprign of the
present year.
A graduate of Wayne University,
Tuchow was active in community
theater, summer theater, and pro
fessional radio work in the East be
fore reeciving the Junior Artist-in-
Residence fellowship at Stanford
for the summer of 1950.
