# FOREIGN STUDENTS
## 
### 
Ninety-eight students from
abroad who are attending Stanford
during the summer quarter will be
guests of the Palo Alto Rotary Club
at a picnic Sunday afternoon at the
Portola ranch of Dr. Russel V. Lee.
Eighty-five men and 13 women
foreign students from 31 countries
have been invited to attend the af
fair, designed to welcome them into
to community and introduce them
to an American-style picnic.
