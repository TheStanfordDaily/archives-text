# STANFORD DAMES
## 
### 
The Stanford Drama Department
will present a program of two one
act plays at the summer general
meeting of the Stanford Dames to
be held Thursday in the upper
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
The meeting time is 7:45 p.m.
Wives of all students new to Stan
ford as well as present members of
the Dames are cordially invited to
attend. Business will be held to a
minimum, and refreshments will be
served following the program.
Mrs. Richard G. Henninger is in
charge of arrangements for the
meeting. Assisting her as refresh
ment chairman will be Mrs. R. H.
Braun.
