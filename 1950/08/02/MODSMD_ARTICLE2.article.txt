# Watermelon Feed Planned
## 
### 
ASSU will sponsor an all-cam
pus Watermelon Feed this Friday
in Union Court from 3 to 5. The
party is the last of the summer
quarter and it is open to every
Stanford student.
Bill Ottey, chairman of the event,
has contracted for 50 watermelons
to feed all comers. There should be
enough servings for about 400 peo
ple. The party is open to all with
out charge.
Plans for entertainment include
record music, and there will also be
community singing. Everyone is in
vited to come and finish summer
quarter on the Farm in real country
style.
