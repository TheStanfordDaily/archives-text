# SOMETHING'S ROTTEN...
## 
### 
The Santa Clara County grand
jury announced yesterday that an
incomplete investigation showed
the county jail is $3,200 short in its
accounts due to "irregularities."
Jury Foreman J. Bradley Clayton
said no indictments were returned
as yet. but the investigation will
continued.
The shortages in jail accounts
occurred over the past 18 months
