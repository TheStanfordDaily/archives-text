# Memorial Service For Dr. Price Sunday
## 
### 
A memorial service for Dr.
George Clinton Price, emeritus
professor of zoology at Stanford,
will be held Sunday at 5 p.m. in
the Memorial Church.
The service will be conducted
by the Rev. li. Marvin Stuart,
pastor of the First Methodist
Church of Palo Alto. He will be
assisted by Prof. Lee Emerson
Bassett, faculty colleague of Dr.
Price.
The Stanford professor is sur
vived by his widow. Edith Bayse
Price, and a son. Dr. John B.
Price of Stanford.
