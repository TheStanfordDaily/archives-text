# Ed Grad to Study New School Movies
## 
### 
A Stanford graduate student in
education will spend next year
studying ways of improving sound
motion pictures for classroom use.
He is Richard S. Hirsch, 22GA
Roosevelt Way, San Francisco, and
he is the winner of a fellowship in
the instructional film research pro
gram at Pennsylvania State Col
lege.
He will conduct research in fields
of psychology and education to im
prove sound motion pictures for
school use.
Hirsch will begin his work at the
Pennsylvania school as soon as he
finishes a military training school
study for the United States Office of
Education.
He is a graduate of the University
of Minnesota and received a Master
of Arts degree at Stanford last year.
He will return here to complete
work for his Doctor of Philosophy
degree after finishing his research
at Pennsylvania State.
