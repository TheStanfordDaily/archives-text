# Schwartz Heads Large Grid Staff
## 
### 
Coach Marchie Schwratz is start
ing his sixth year as head coach
here at Stanford. Other members
of the Stanford coaching staff this
year include Phil Bengtson, head
line coach; Mai Elward, end coach
and chief scout; Dutch Fehring, as
sistant coach; Pete Kmetovic and
Jim Cox, assistant coaches.
New head freshman coach this
season is Bob Ghilotti. Assisting
Ghilotti are former Stanford play
ers Ken Rose, Jim Castagnoli, A 1
Rau, and Don Campbell.
Mack Kimberly of Montana State
will be head coach of the Stanford
JV's this season. He will be as
sisted by Emery Mitchell.
