# 'What Price Glory' Tryouts Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Players'
production of "What Price Glory?"
scheduled for presentation October
25-28, will be hold tonight at 7:30
in the Studio Theater, Memorial
Auditorium.
Fencers, versatile dramatic ac

urged to try out for parts in Shake
speare's "Julius Caesar," the new
Stanford Players winter opener.
Tryouts will he held today and
tomorrow from 4 to 6 and 7:30 to 10
