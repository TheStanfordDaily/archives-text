# Lag to Host Farm At Fair Saturday
## 
### 
Lagunita Court will be trans
formed into an old-fashioned fair
ground to entertain all Farm stu
dents, now and old, at the "Farm
Fair" Saturday night.
The affair will be open to all
Farm roughs and gals, with or
without, dates. Festivities will start
at t) p.m. and last until 1 a.m.
