# Fifty Teachers Needed In Japanese Colleges
## 
### 
The Stanford Appointment Serv
ice announces that 50 American
professors are needed to teach in
universities in Japan for the next
two years. The subjects for teach
ing will be English language, liter
ature, social sciences, natural sci
ences, physical sciences, education,
music, and library science.
Applicants must be college grad
uates, preferably with a Ph.D. de
gree, and teaching experience in an
American college or university.
Further information and applica
tion forms can be obtained from the
Institute of International Educa
tion, 2 West 45th Street, New York
19. by October 15.
