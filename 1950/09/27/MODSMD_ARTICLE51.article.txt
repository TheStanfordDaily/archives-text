# School Offices Closed Saturdays
## 
### 
It won't be business as usual
| come Saturday morning at Stan
ford — nor any other Saturday
morning, for that matter.
For an indefinite period, all Uni
versity administration offices will
be closed Saturdays. Only the Reg
istrar's information office will be
open on that day—and it will be
from 8 a.m. to 12 noon.
Last year it was a different story.
Administration offices were open a
half - day on Saturdays, and stu
dents could save their University
business until the end of the week.
But even the student cashier's of
fice in Encina will be closed Satur
days under the new order. By way
of compensation, University officials
have announced that office hours
during the working week will be
increased. Instead of opening at
8:30 a.m. and closing at 4:30 p.m.,
office staffs will open their doors at
8 a.m. and quit at 5 p.m. Monday
through Friday.
