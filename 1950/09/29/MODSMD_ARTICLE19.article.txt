# Roble Deadline Today For Frosh Pictorial
## 
### 
Roble freshman women will be
photographed for the last time to
day at the Quad office. These pic
tures will be used in the Class of
'54 Pictorial, which will be pub
lished early in November, and in
the yearbook. Ail Robh freshmen
who have not already been photo
graphed should come to the Quad
office today between 10 a.m. and
noon or 1 and 5 p.m.
White blouses should be worn for
the photographs, and $1 will be
charged.
