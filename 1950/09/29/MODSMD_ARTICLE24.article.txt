# Village Wives Aid Community Chest
## 
### 
Among solicitors helping out the
Community Chest drive in Menlo
Park will be 38 young wives and
mothers from Stanford Village.
They are preparing to collect
contributions from the Village's 400
families of married students under
the leadership of Mrs. D. B. Adams,
district chairman.
Mrs. Pat Kerr, her chief assistant,
helped organize canvassers and list
families in each court and buildinu.
