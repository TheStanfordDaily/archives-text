# Mural Managers Hold First Meet Tomorrow
## 
### 
It is urgent that all living groups
and organizations, desiring to enter
the fall intramural schedule, elect a
leader to be present at the first
managers' meeting to be held to
morrow at 7:15 p.m. in Room 47,
Athletic Office Building. Even
though teams are not yet organized,
intention of participating in the
mural games must be signified so
that space may be reserved.
