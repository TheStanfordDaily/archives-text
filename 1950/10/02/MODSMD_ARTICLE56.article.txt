# Graduate Students Win Scholarships
## 
### 
The Western AEC Fellowship
Board at the University of Cali
fornia has awarded two Stan
ford graduate students government
scholarships for atomic research.
The Stanford students to receive
the "pre-doctoral fellowships" are
Jack E. Coppage, 515 O'Kecfe
Street, Palo Alto, mechanical engi
neering; and Daniel R. Shawc,
Gardnerville, Nev., geology.
The winners will study in fields
closely enough related to atomic
energy to fit them for employment
in the atomic program.
The awards provide $1,600 a year
for study in the physical sciences
and $1,500 in the biological sci
ences, with extra sums for depend
ents.
