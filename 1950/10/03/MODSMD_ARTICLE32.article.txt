# Istanbul Publisher To Meet Turk Students
## 
### 
Ahmet Emin Yalman, Turkish
publisher, will visit Palo Alto to
day. He will meet Turkish students
at the home cf Dr. Eleanor Bisbee,
220 Kipling Avenue.
Yalman is editor and publisher of
the Istanbul Vatam. He was edu
cated in this country. His hostess
was formerly a professor at Robert
College in Istanbul.
Turkish students are invited to
meet Yalman after 5 p.m.
