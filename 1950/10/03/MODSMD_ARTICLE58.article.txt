# PALO ALTO BADMINTON CLUB
## 
### 
The Palo Alto Badminton Club
invites old members as well as
newcomers to come to Jordan High
School, Middlefield Road, Palo Alto,
every Monday night at 7:30 and
share in the tournaments, matches,
and numerous parties scheduled by
the club. Membership is $2 per
quarter and 50 cents an evening for
the "birds." For further informa
tion contact Brenda Owens at DA
3-9411.
