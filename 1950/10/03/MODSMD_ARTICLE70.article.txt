# Tickets Now Sell For Revival Films
## 
###

memberships will not warrant a
Thursday afternoon showing, the
Committee on Public Exercises
announced yesterday. Member
ships to the evening showings are
still on sale in Room 55 of Tem
porary Building B.
The first movie of the series,
''The Birth of a Nation," will be
shown Thursday at 7:30 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium.
