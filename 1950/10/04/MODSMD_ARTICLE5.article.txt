# Loyalty Bill Signed
## 
### 
SACRAMENTO —A bill to re
quire more than 300,000 public em
ployees and a possible million civil
defense workers to take loyalty
oaths was signed today by Gov.
Earl Warren.
And then he "happily" became
the first of them to swear that he
is not a member of any subversive
organization. His oath—taken ps a
"civil defense worker" was admin
istered by Secretary of State Frank
M. Jordan.
Public employees have a 30-day
period starting today in which to
sign the oath or be dropped from
governmental payrolls.
