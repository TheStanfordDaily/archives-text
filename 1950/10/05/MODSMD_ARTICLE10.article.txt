# Nanney Will Present Bach Program Today
## 
### 
An all-Bach program of organ
music will be featured at the regu
lar Thursday afternoon organ reci
tal in the Memorial Church today
at 4:15 p.m.
Herbert B. Nanney, University
organist, will play two chorale pre
ludes—"ln Thee Is Joy," "Come,
Savior of the Gentiles"; the Trio
Sonata No. 1 in E flat major (Al
legro moderato, Adagio, Allegro);
the Adagio from Toccata, Adagio,
and the Fugue in C Major; and
Prelude and Fugue in G Major.
The recital is open to the public.
