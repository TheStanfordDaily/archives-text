# Rally Com Names Frosh Members
## 
### 
Twenty new members of Rally
Com—all of them freshmen—were
approved by Excom Wednesday
night. They are:
Women — Joan Andreas, Judy
Cleaver, Sandra Collins, Babs
Froude, Betty Herr, Marion
Hughes, Joyce Hupp. Lyn Kucken
berg, Sa'ly Porter, Suzie Fitger, and
Pat Sumner.
Men — Pete Cosovich, Austin
Dowling, Jinn Harris, Ted Hughes,
Fred Kunzel. Eill Northway, Dick
i Pauley, Oliver Thornton, find Carl
Walston.
