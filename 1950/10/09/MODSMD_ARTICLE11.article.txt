# Students Warned To Move Autos
## 
### 
Students with cars parked on
Galvez or Escondido streets in the
Stern - Crothers - Encina area- have
until 8 this morning to move them.
The final surfacing on the streets
will be completed today, culminat
ing the two-month repaving proj
ect. The streets will be reopened
tomorrow.
Police Chief Gordy Davis warned
that cars remaining in the area are
liable to be splattered with tar and
may be towed away at the own
ers' expense. Safe parking areas
arc on the Toyon side of Stern,
Crothers, or Encina, and by the
Pavilion.
