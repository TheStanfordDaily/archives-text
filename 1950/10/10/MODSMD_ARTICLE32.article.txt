# Library Curator Has Book Printed
## 
### 
The Hoover Library's curator of
the Japanese collection, Nobutaka
Ike, is the author of a recently pub
lished book on the Japanese demo
cratic movement of 1875-1890.
The title of the book, printed by
the Johns Hopkins Press and is
sued under the auspices of the In
ternational Secretariat, the Insti
tute of Pacific Relations, and the
Hoover Institute, is "The Begin
nings of Political Democracy in Ja
pan."
Ike is a graduate of the Univer
sity of Hashington, received the
Ph.D. from Johns Hopkins, and is
also a Research Associate of the
Hoover Institute and Library.
