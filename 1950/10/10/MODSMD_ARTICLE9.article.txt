# Group to Discuss Local Problems
## 
### 
Committees representing Stan
ford and the City of Palo Alto will
meet Friday afternoon to discuss
problems of mutual interest to the
city and the University.
On the agenda for the meeting
is a proposal to establish a water
treatment plant on Stanford land to
serve both areas. The operation
and possible expansion of the Palo
Alto Hospital and the development
of University lands contiguous to
Palo Alto will also be discussed.
Representing Stanford will be
Dean Clarence H. Faust, Alf Bran
din, and George F. Morell. Presi
dent Wallace Sterling is an ex-offi
cio member of the committee.
