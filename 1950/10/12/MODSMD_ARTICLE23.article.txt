# NANNEY ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Herbert B, Nanney will present
the second organ recital at 4:15 p.m.
today in the Memorial Church.
Compositions of Italian composers,
including Gabrieli, Frescobaldi,
Pasquini, Martini, Bnssi, d'AntalfTy,
and Cavazzoni will be played. The
