# TO URUGUAY
## Brandt Leaves On UN Mission 
### 
Dr. Karl Brandt, economist and
professor of agricultural economics
in the Stanford Food Research In
stitute, leaves tonight for a two
month stay in Uruguay as a mem
ber of a joint mission studying agri
cultural problems in the South
American country.
Dr. Brandt will serve as a special
ist in agricultural marketing and
prices on the mission sponsored* by
the International Bank for Recon
struction and Development and the
Food and Agriculture Organization
of the United Nations.
Head of the mission is Sir Mau
rice Hutton, who was chairman of
the British Food Mission to the
United States during the latter part
of World War 11.
Dr. Brandt will return to Stan
ford in mid-Deccmbcr.
