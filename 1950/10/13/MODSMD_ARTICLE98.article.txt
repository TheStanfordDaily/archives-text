# CAR RADIO STOLEN
## 
### 
The theft of a car radio was re
ported yesterday to police by David
H. Allen, Jr., 123 Toyon Hall.
The right wing window of his 1937
DeSoto sedan was forced open after
8 p.m. Monday and the radio was
stolen, the student said.
Allen's car was parked in the
Toyon parking area opposite Encina
Hall.
