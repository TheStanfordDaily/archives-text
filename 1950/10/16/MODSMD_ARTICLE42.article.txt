# Wright to Speak On Oriental Views
## 
### 
Dr. Arthur F. Wright of the Stan
ford History Department will speak
tonight on the attitude of the Orient
toward world government. The talk,
scheduled for 8:30 p.m. in the Wom
en's Clubhouse, is being sponsored
by the Stanford Student Federal
ists, who invite the public to attend.
Dr. Wright will discuss the diffi
culties of relations among the na
tions of the Orient and between the
West and the Orient, and possible
solutions to these problems through
world government.
Dr. Wright spent considerable
time in the Orient in pre-war years,
including stays at Peking and Kyo
to. At present, he is editor-elect of
the Eastern Quarterly.
