# Gallery Shows Egyptian Art
## 
### 
An exhibit of Egyptian art ob- j
iects, some of them purchased by
Mrs. Leland Stanford during a trip
to the Middle East in 1898, is now
on display in the Stanford Art Gal
lery.
The display has been prepared for
study by students in Western
Civilization classes. The exhibit is I
the first in a series planned to cover
periods of history studies in this i
course.
Background for the exhibition ;
arc 30 large photographs of ancient
Egypt.
The display will remain in the i
gallery through Oct. 22. Also on
exhibit are surrealistic paintings 1
and lithographs. The gallery is"
open to the public without charge
Tuesday through Saturday from 10
a.m. to 5 p.m. and on Sunday from
12 noon until 4 p.m.
