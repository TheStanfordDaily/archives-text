# Red Cross Presents Class for New Parents
## 
### 
A class designed to prepare new
j and prospective parents for the
I baby's arrival and care, given by
the Palo Alto Red Cross, starts to
night.
The class will meet Mondays and
Thursdays from 7:30 to 9:30 p.m. for
three weeks. Interested parents
should register by calling the Red
Cross, DA 2-2143, or by coming to
the first meeting of the class at the
chapter house, 400 Mitchell Lane
(across from the SP depot).
