# Offices Will Close Saturday Mornings
## 
### 
Stanford's administration offices
are no longer open Saturday morn
ings.
At present the offices are open for
business from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Mon
day through Friday. However, the
information office in the Registrar's
Office will remain open Saturday
from 8:30 a.m. to 12 noon.
Under the new change in hours,
the offices are open one hour longer
per week than they were last year.
