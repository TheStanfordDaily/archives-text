# Costa Rica Fellowship Awarded Stanfordite
## 
### 
Thomas L. Karnes of Oak Park,
111., Stanford graduate student in
history, has been awarded a Costa
Rican fellowship and a State De
partment grant for a nine-month
study in Costa Rica.
The State Department travel
grant, plus funds provided by the
Costa Rican government under an
exchange student agreement, will
finance his research into the role
of Costa Rica in attempts to form a
confederation of Central American
countries.
At Stanford, Karnes has been
studying for the Ph.D. under Pro
fessor John J. Johnson of the His
tory Department.
