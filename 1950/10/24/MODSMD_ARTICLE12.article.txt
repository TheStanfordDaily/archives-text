# '52-'54 Jamboree To Be Held Friday
## 
### 
Members of the classes of '52 and
'54 will have a chance to get ac
quainted at the Frosh-Junior Jam
boree on Friday at the Women's
Gym.
George Bingham's 15 - piece or
chestra will furnish music for the
date or no-date affair from 8:30
to 12.
Old-time movies will be shown
during the evening. George Denny,
who is in charge of entertainment,
will put on variety-show type acts.
Refreshments will be served.
