# TWA Lowers Fare On Flights Abroad
## 
### 
Trans World Airlines will re
duce trans - Atlantic fares 50 per
cent for students traveling abroad
beginning Nov. 1. The rate will ap
ply to travel between school and
home of students between the ages
of 12 and 22 years enrolled in full
time academic courses.
Students from any nation are eli
gible, but they must produce cer
tificates from educational authori
ties attesting to age, course of
study, and reason for travel. This
student fare applies to east-bound
flights between Nov. 1 and March
31; and to west-bound trans-Atlan
tic travel between Dec. 1 and June
