# Last Art Lecture To Be Given Today
## 
### 
Jacqueline Onslow - Ford, writer
and wife of artist Gordon Onslow-
Ford, will tell "Why Painting Has
Become Subjective" in an illus
trated lecture today at 4:15 p.m. in
Stanford's Cubberley Auditorium.
The lecture will be the fourth and
last in a series given in conjunction
with a Stanford Art Gallery ex
hibit, "From Surrealism to Meta
plastics." The talk will be open to
the public without charge.
