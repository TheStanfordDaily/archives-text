# Revival Series Shows War Film
## 
### 
Tonight, Revival Scries members
will have a chance to see on film
the current presentation of the
Stanford Players, "What Price
Glory?"
Victor McLaglen, Edmund Lowe,
and Dolores Del Rio share top hon
ors in the movie to be shown in
Cubberley Auditorium at 7:30 p.m.
Admission is by membership in
the Stanford Film Society only. No
further tickets are available for
this quarter.
