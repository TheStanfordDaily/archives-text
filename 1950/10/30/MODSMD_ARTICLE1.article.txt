## Yank Drive Nears Big Power Plant 
### 
TOKYO, Monday, Oct. 30 (UP)—
An American spearhead pushed to
within 34 miles of the Orient's big
gest power plant on the Manchurian
border today, but on their right,
10,000 savagely attacking Commu
nists had wiped out one-third of
the South Korean Sixth Division.
The South Korean Sixth Division
had lost three towns to the suici
dally attacking Reds—Onjong, some
28 miles northeast of the American
spearhead, and Kwangsong, 12 miles
southeast of Onjong, and Tacpyong,
12 miles cast.
