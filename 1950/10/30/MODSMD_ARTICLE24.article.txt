# High School Day Chairman Needed
## 
### 
Interviews will be held today and 1
tomorrow from 3:30 to 5 p.m. for
students interested in trying out for
the chairmanship of the proposed
High School Day committee.
Excom and Cardinals, sponsors of
the idea, will conduct the inter
views in the Cardinals office, up
stairs in the Old Union.
