# Nearby Party Spots Filed in Revised List
## 
### 
Joan Anthony and Bud Nutting
are co - chairmen of a committee
which is revising the files listing
nearby restaurants and places
where parties may be held. The
files will list facilities and prices
of spots in San Francisco and the
Bay Area.
When the files are completed,
they will be placed in the ASSU
office and mimeographed copies will
be sent to each social chairman.
