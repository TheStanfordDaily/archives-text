# Sokol Speaks To the 'I' Club
## 
### 
An analysis of ways and means of
making the stay of foreign students
more pleasant at Stanford was spir
ited into a two-hour discussion by
Dr. Anthony Sokol, a professor of
Asiatic and Slavic studies, when he
met with the International Club
Friday.
Dr. Sokol led the club in a search
for more satisfactory ways to make
Stanfordites from foreign countries
more at home in the social life
of the campus.
