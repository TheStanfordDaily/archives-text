# Shaw Reported Worse
## 
### 
AYOT ST. LAWRENCE, England
- Ailing playwright George Ber
nard Shaw, reported "very weak"
alter taking a sudden turn for the
worse, told friends early today, "I
am tired. I want to sleep."
Lady Astor, life-long friend of
the 94-year-old Irish author, re
ported his critical condition. After
a visit yesterday she said, "I am
afraid he hasn't long to live. We
expect him to go at any moment."
