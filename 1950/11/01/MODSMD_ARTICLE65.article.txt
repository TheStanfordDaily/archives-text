# Bay Architecture Is Lecture Topic
## 
### 
"Is There a Bay Area Style of
Architecture?" — controversial topic
among home builders all over the
United States—will be answered by
Albert Henry Hill in a talk today
at 4:15 p.m. in the Stanford Art
Gallery.
Hill, who is a lecturer in' archi
tecture at Stanford this fall, will
discuss the current gallery exhibit.
"Domestic Art of the Day Region,"
a display of Bay Area architecture.
The talk will be open to the public
without charge.
