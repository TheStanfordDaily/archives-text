# Loyalty Oath Open Meeting Is Monday
## 
### 
A tentative meeting for an open
discussion concerning the Loyalty
Oath has been set for Monday at
8 p.m. in Cubbcrley Auditorium,
Ted But I nor, ASSU president, an
nounced yesterday.
The meeting will be arranged by
the new committee appointed by
Excom to take charge of discussions
of the Loyalty Oath question. Har
vey Wagner, ASSU debate mana
ger; Carl Stover, ASSU Forum
| Committee chairman; and Alberta
i Engvall have been named as the
I committee members, Buttner said.
