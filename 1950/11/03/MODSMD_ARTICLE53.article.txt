# Sanders Reports UCLA In Year's Worst Shape
## 
### 
Ninety-degree temperature heated
a light scrimmage yestorday for the
UCLA Bruins, who Coach Henry
(Red) Sanders says are in their
worst physical shape of the year.
He named fullback Dave Wil
liams definitely out for the Oregon
State game Saturday, tailback Bob
Moore available only for punting,
and stellar linemen Breck Stro
schein and Bruce MacLachlan as
nursing injuries that may handicap
their offensive abilities.
