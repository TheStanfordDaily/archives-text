# VILLAGE MOVIES
## 
### 
"Stanley and Livingston," the
story of Stanley's search through
the African jungles for Dr. Living
ston, will be shown at the Village
theater tomorrow and Sunday.
Spencer Tracy and Walter Brennan
are co-starred.
There will be two showings each
day, one at 6:45 and one at 8:45.
Season passes are no longer neces
sary for admission . The price has
been reduced to 30 cents per person.
