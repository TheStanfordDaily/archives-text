# Eleven-Year-Old Dies After Game
## 
### 
Eleven-year-old Robert Holt of
Fresno was taken fatally ill in th-
Stanford Stadium at the end of the
first quarter of the Stanford-USC
football game Saturday.
Holt, was taken to a first aid sta
tion, where he had convulsions, and
was immediately removed to the
Palo Alto Hospital by ambulance.
He died at p.m. without regain
ing consciousness. Doctors believe
he died of_ a brain hemorrhage.
Young Holt, son of Mr. and Mrs.
John E. Holt, of Fresno, was attend
ing the game with his brother, Wil
liam, and a group of Fresno people.
