# All This for Little Pig
## 
### 

THE FIRST TROY GAME—Centuries of combat ensued (according to the legend) following the
first battle between the Indians of the North and the Men of Troy. The Indians, right, prepare
to battle for their beloved Little Pig, Indian maiden, kidnaped by the Trojans. According to the
myth, portrayed at Friday night's rally, Little Pig was recovered and lived happily ever after
even up to the year 1950. —Daily photo by Tom Peterson
