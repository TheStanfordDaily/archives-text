# Bruin Center Wins Award
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Nov. 6 (UP) —
Hard-hitting Donn Moomaw, bril
liant 18-year-old UCLA center and
linebacker, was named Southern
California's athlete of the month
for October today by the Helms
Hall Board.
The board cited the Santa Ana
sophomore's inspired play as instru
mental in the Bruins' surprising
grid record this year.
