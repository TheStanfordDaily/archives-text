# Grad Appointed To Peru Embassy
## 
### 
Stanford graduate Willard F.
Barber has been appointed as a
Foreign Service reserve officer of
class one and assigned as Counselor
of Embassy and Deputy Chief of
Mission at Lima, Peru, the State
Department has announced.
Barber, who received his A.B. and
M.S. degrees here, has been with
the State Department 12 years. Af
ter leaving Stanford, he did further
graduate work in diplomacy and
international affairs at Columbia
University. He was awarded the
Einstein Prize for Excellence in
American Diplomacy by Columbia
in 1933.
