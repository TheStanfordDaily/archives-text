# Engineer Speaks On Jets Tonight
## 
### 
John M. Dodds, General Electric
Company scientist, will speak to
night on "Jet Propulsion" before
the Stanford student branch of the
American Society of Mechanical
Engineers.
The lecture, which will be open
to the public, will be given at 7:30
in Room 283 on the second floor of
the Engineering Building.
The speaker will discuss some of
the engineering problems in the de
velopment of gas turbines and jet
propulsion equipment.
Mr. Dodds graduated in electrical
engineering from the University of
Washington and has been with
General Electric since 1915. He is a
member of the firm's apparatus de
partment staff in San Francisco.
