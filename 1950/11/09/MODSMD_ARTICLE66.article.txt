# Fresno Assembly Features Faville
## 
### 
Dr. David E. Faville, professor of
marketing in the Stanford Gradu
ate School of Business, will be
speaker of the day at the general
assembly luncheon of the Pacific
Coast Electrical Association at
Fresno today.
Widely known for his knowledge
in the field of marketing, Dr. Fa
ville was the first president of the
Northern California chapter of the
American Marketing Association
and has served on the board of
directors of the national organiza
tion of that association.
