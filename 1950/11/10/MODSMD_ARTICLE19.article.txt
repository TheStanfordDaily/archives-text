# P.O. Windows, Banks Close Armistice Day
## 
### 
The post oflice windows and the
Palo Alto banks will be closed to
morrow, Armistice Day. The lob
by of the post office will be open
as usual, however, and mail will
be distributed in the boxes.
The reserve book room of the
main library will be open from S
a.m. until 10 p.m.
