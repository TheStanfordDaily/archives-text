# 'Crystal Ball' Is Sunday Night Film
## 
### 
The Sunday Night Movies this
week will present "Crystal Ball" with
Ray Milland and Paulette Goddard
sharing top billing. The show starts
at 7:30 and admission is the usual
20 cents.
For both Saturday and Sunday
nights the Village Movies has sched
uled "Guadalcanal Diary," starring
Preston Foster and Lloyd Nolan.
Admission is open to everyone at
6:45 p.m. and 8:45 p.m. for 80 cents
plus tax.
