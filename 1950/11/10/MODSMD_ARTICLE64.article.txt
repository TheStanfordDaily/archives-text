# Branner Entertains Roble, Stern Guests
## 
### 
Branner was host for dinner
Wednesday to Miss Gladys Keene,
Roble director; Miss Patricia Da
vidson, Stern director; Jane Ala
baster, Roble president; and Nancy
Brown, head sponsor at Roble.
After dinner, coffee was served
in the lobby. Girls talked person
ally with the guests and had a
chance to receive information about
Stern Hall and Roble.
