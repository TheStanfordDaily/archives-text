# Once Around the Quad
## Official Notices QUAD PHOTOGRAPHS 
### 
Today is the last day that the
Quad has scheduled for photo
graphing Humanities and Sciences
seniors. Those whose names begin
with V through Z should come in
today. Tomorrow and Wednesday,
seniors in the Law School and the
School of Mineral Sciences will
have pictures taken. Any member
of the class of '51 who has missed
an appointment may come in any
time this week.
