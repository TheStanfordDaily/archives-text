# G.E., Link-Belt Need Engineers
## 
### 
General Electric Co. representa
tives will hold a general meeting in
room 268, Engineering Building, to
morrow at 3:15 p.m. All students in
terested in the company are invited
to attend.
The G.E. Co. will also interview
electrical, mechanical, chemical, ami
industrial engineers with B.S. or M.S.
or M.B.A. degrees Thursday and Fri
day. Men are needed for their test
program and to work at their Han
ford, Washington, plant.
G.E. will also interview physicists
soon who have a B.S. and M.S.
The Link-Belt Co. wi'l interview
design engineers Thursday at 3:30
p.m. at the Placement Service.
The company is interested in jun
iors and senior design engineers,
especially those who will receive
their B.S. degrees in December.
