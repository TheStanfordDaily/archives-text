# Canterbury Club To Hear Dr. Fitch
## 
### 
Dr. Robert E. Fitch, theologian,
professor, and author, will speak on
"The Significant Christian Person"
at 8 p.m. tomorrow in the Parish
House of All Saints' Episcopal
Church, Hamilton at Waverley,
Palo Alto.
All Stanford students are in
vited. A discussion period will fol
low Dr. Fitch's talk. Refreshments
will be served by the Canterbury
Club, the student group which is
sponsoring the speech. Dr. Fitch
spoke at the Memorial Church this
fall on Matriculation Sunday.
