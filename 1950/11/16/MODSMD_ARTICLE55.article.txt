# Mendelowitz Exhibits Work
## 
### 
Twenty watercolors painted last
year in France by Stanford Art
Professor Daniel IVJ. Mendelowitz
will be on exhibit today through
Dec. 2 in the Kenneth Slaughter
Galleries, 173 Maiden Lane, San
Francisco.
Professor Mendelowitz spent last
fall and winter painting scenes such
us the Seine, Notre Dame Cathedral,
and the Latin Quarter in Paris and
views of Antilles on the French Ri
viera.
He has been a member of the Art
Department faculty since 1934.
He spent last year's sabbatical
leave teaching at Columbia Univer
sity, painting in France, and tour
ing art museums in Italy, Holland,
Belgium, Switzerland, and England.
