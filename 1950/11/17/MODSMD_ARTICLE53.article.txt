# Weekend Social Events
## 
### 
Open houses will be given this
weekend by Union, Ventura. Phi
Sigma Kappa, Cubberley, Russell,
Lathrop, Storey, and Delta Chi.
Starting off the weekend social
events, the Delta Chi's will hold
their open house after the rally to
night until 12:30. Dancing to rec
ords, and refreshments of sand
wiches, coffee, cider, and cake will
be featured. Ping pong and bridge
games will also be available for the
guests.
Immediately after the game to
morrow, Cubberley, Russell, La
throp, Storey, Phi Sigma Kappa,
and Ventura will hold open houses.
The Phi Sigs will serve smorgas

bord and punch to the guests and
dancing to records, and movies will
be featured.
At the Ventura open house, which
will last until 7:30, cider and
doughnuts will be served. Re
freshments will also be served at
the Women's Row get-togethers.
Concluding the weekend events,
t|ie Union open house will be held
tomorrow night in the main Union
lobby beginning at 7:30.
Decorations will follow the theme
of the party, "Army Sees Rod."
Dancing to records has been plan
ned, and cake and coffee are on
the menu. Union women will act as
hostesses.
