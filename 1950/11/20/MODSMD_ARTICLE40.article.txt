# Frosh Coed Wins $300 Elks Contest
## 
### 
Priscilla Gurr, Stanford freshman,
added another $300 check to a
growing total awarded her by* the
Mountain View Elks Lodge.
The latest check, which followed
another of $300 and one of $150, was
a prize in a national contest, spon
sored by the Elks, for "the most
outstanding student."
All three awards were won while
Miss Gurr was a senior at Mountain
View High School.
