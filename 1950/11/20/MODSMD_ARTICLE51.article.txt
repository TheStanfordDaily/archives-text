# Indian Soccer Tilt With SFSC Put Off
## 
### 
S lan 112 o v (1' s scheduled soccer
game with Sail Francisco State
College was postponed from last
Saturday at 10 a.m. until Wed
nesday afternoon, the week fol
lowing the I'ig Game.
The game date was changed
because two minor lakes were
discovered, one in front of each
goal, just befroe the contest. Al
though the players were game,
tin- hall just won't roll in lakes,
so the whole affair was called off
temporarily.
