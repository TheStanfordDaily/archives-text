We would be the last people in the world to deny the space and money
saving advantages of the Long-Playing record, and its superior con
venience where continuous musical works of some length are in
volved. As for works which are comfortably contained on one or two
single 78 rpm discs, however, one is often forced on LP to obtain a
miscellaneous hodgepodge in order to acquire one short work that one
wants. With this consideration in mind we present a carefully se
lected list of items comprising one and two 78 rpm discs, from our
recent shipments of choice HMV and Columbia recoids from Europe.
We would like particularly to call your attention to the records of the
fine young English pianist, Denis Matthews, whose interpretations of
18th century piano music are above reproach; also particularly note
worthy are the performances of Europe's two leading oboists, Leon
Coossens of England, and Waldemar Wolsing of Denmark.
