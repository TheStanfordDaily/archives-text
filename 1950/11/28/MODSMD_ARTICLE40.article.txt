# SOCIAL CHAIRMEN
## 
### 
Social chairmen of living groups
and organizations will meet tonight
at 7:15 in the Nitery. They will dis
cuss next quarter's social calendar.
Speeches will be given by Miss
Elva Brown, counselor for women;
Richard Balch, counselor for men,
and A 1 McCombs, managing editor
of The Daily. It is important that
all social chairmen attend or send
another representative, said Jo
Lyon, ASSU vice-president.
