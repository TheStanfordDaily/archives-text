# Collier's Picks Bill and Russ
## 
### 
End Bill McColl and tackle Russ
Pomeroy were named to the Col
lier's All - Star Far West Team.
Gary Kerkorian and Harry Huga
sian received honorable mention. As
far as the other positions go, it was
at end, Wilkinson; at tackle, Peters;
guards, Richter and MacLachlan;
center, Moser; quarterback, Don
Heinrich; halfbacks, Jim Monachino
and Ollie Matson; fullback, Hugji
McElhenny.
The players were selected follow
ing weekly reports from more than
250 coaches. These were then con
solidated and the selections made.
