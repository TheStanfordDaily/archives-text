# Banyan Reading Heads Program
## 
### 
The Speech and Drama Depart
ment will present an interpretative
reading of a modern version of a
Paul Bunyan story by Mrs. Court
ney Perren Brooks at 4:15 in the
Little Theater today.
Mrs. Brooks is an instructor in
speech at San Jose State College.
Selections from the works of Dor
othy Parker, Frank Stanton, and
James Thurber will also be includ
ed in this final reading hour of the
quarter.
The program, which was origi
nally scheduled for Thursday, is
open to everyone.
