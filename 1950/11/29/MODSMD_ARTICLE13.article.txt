# FINAL DAY FOR SENIOR PICTURES
## 
###

Stanford Quad will photograph
seniors for the senior section of
the 1051 yearbook.
All students receiving decrees
this year who wish their pictures
to appear in the Quad should
come to the yearbook office today
between 10 a.m. and noon or I
and 5 p.m.
