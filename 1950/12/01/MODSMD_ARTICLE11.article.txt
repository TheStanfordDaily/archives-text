# '52 Get-Together At Rossot's Today
## 
###

activities for the quarter with a
! reunion at Possotti's this afternoon
' at 3.

| week Ret-together will be "all the
liquid refreshments you can drink
, for 50 cents."
A car pool, organized by Stan
Dawson, will stop in front of wom
en's living groups to pick up coeds
without rides at 2:45 this afternoon.
