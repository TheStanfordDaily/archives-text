# Green to Talk Here Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Canon Bryan Green of
Birmingham, England, will be the
guest minister at the 11 a.m. serv
ice in Memorial Church Sunday.
He will speak on "Finding a Real
Faith."
Sunday afternoon at 3:30, Green
will be in Encina lounge for an in
formal question and answer period.
All students are welcome.
At 9 a.m. Sunday, there will be a
celebration of Holy Communion in
Memorial Church.
Two special services will be given
in Memorial Church at 11 a.m. and
4 p.m. Sunday, Dec. 10.
