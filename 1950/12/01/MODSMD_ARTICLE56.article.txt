##

were selected this week to serve as
freshman members of Ruble's Com
mittee ol Five. This is the
tee on social regulations at Roble
which takes care of all the social
records of Roble girls.
These two freshmen will take the
place of the two senior sponsor
members on the committee at the
beginning of winter quarter, and
the remaining three members will
be selected for spring quarter.
