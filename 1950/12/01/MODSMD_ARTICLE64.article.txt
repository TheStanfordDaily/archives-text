# Frosh Make First Cage Bid Today
## 
### 
The curtain rings up on the frosh
basketball season at 3 p.m. today
when Coach Bob Burnett's hoop
sters take on Antioch High School.
They meet Piedmont tomorrow.
Burnett will field a starting line
up of Harold Ellis and Don De-
Long at forwards, Dave Epperson
at center, and Oleg Suzdaleff and
Don Carlson at guards.
