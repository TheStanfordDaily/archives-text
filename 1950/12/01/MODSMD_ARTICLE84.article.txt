###

eral chairman of the Stanford Stu
dent Federalists. He and the fol
lowing officers were elected Mon

Dick O'Connell, vice-chairman in
charge of educational activities;

charge of program and public re
lations; Lee Langan, secretary
treasurer; Alice Endcrton, member
ship secretary.
