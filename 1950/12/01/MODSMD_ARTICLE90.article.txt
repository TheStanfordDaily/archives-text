# Foreign Students May Stay at Tahoe
## 
### 
Stanford's foreign students can
spend a three-day vacation at Lake
Tahoe during the Christmas holi
days as part of a tour sponsored by
the International House of the Uni
versity of California. Traveling and
room expenses are included in a
reduced rate of $10.75.
The group will be housed in cab
ins at Tahoe Vista. Reservations and
a $2 deposit must be made with
Willard Watcrous, foreign students'
adviser, by Monday morning.
