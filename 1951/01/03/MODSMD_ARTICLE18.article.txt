# Two Women Added To Honorary List
## 
### 
Names of two students, Pat Penny
and Nancy "Chips" Ward, were
omitted from the list of 13 new Cap
and Gown members published last
quarter in The Daily.
Miss Penny was a Branner sponsor
and is chairman of Women's Coun
cil. Miss Ward has been on Rally
Com, Housing Com, and was vice
president of Lagunita.
