# Editorial Positions Open on Daily Staff
## 
### 
New students and old students
who plan to enter activities now
that they have seen the results of
their first quarter at Stanford are
invited to serve on The Daily.
Those interested in positions on
The Stanford Daily should leave
their names with Margie McKeon,
staff editor, this week. The Daily
plans to train new cub reporters
for Winter Quarter during the
next few weeks.
