officials today what's being planned j
in civilian defense in California,
from bandage supplies to bacterio
logical warfare defense.

defense office went into a blow by j
blow description of civilian planning
against attack after Gov. Earl War
ren told the group that California
faces the greatest crisis in its history.
The city officials were gathered
for an emergency meeting called by
the League of California Cities.
