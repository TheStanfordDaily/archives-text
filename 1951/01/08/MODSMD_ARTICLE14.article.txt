# Cal Game Tickets Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Rooters' tickets for the California-
Stanford basketball games at Berke
ley Jan. 12 and at Stanford Jan. 13
will be on sale today at the ticket
booth in the BAC Building from 8:30
a.m. to >1:30 p.m.
Tickets are 50 cents and will be
distributed to students with student
hotly cards as long as the supply
lasts.
Rooters' tickets for the Santa
Clara and USF games to be played
it the Cow Palace Jan. 19 and 20
will be sold in the BAC ticket booth
Irom 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Jan. lfi.
Price to students with student body
cards will be 50 cents.
