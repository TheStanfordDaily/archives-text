# Sterling to Give Lecture in L.A.
## 
### 
Stanford University President
Wallace Sterling will deliver the
1951 William Henry Snyder Lecture
tomorrow at 2 p.m. at Los Angeles
City College.
Dr. Sterling's subject will be "Ed
ucating for Leadership."
The annual lectures were estab
lished in 1935 in honor of the first
director of Los Angeles City Col
lege.
