# Debate Tryouts Set for Monday
## 
### 
Tryouts for the annual debate be
tween Stanford and California will
he held at 'Monday nieht in Cub
berley Auditorium instead of in the
Women's Clubhouse.
The topic of the debate is: "Re
solved, that the libeling of religious
and racial groups shall be made a
civil and criminal offense." Stanford
is to debate the affirmative, but those
trying out may present either side.
Tryout speeches should consist of
a five - minute constructive argu
ment and a three-minute rebuttal.
The debate is scheduled for Mon
day, Feb. 19, in Cubberley Audito
rium.
