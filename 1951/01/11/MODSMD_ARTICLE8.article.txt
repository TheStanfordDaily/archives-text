# 'Ike' in Europe
## 
### 
THE HAGUE, The Netherlands —
Gen. Dwight D. Eisenhower arrived
here late today on the third leg of
a tour which so far has uncovered no
new fighting men for his new Euro
pean army — but plenty of requests
for more American aid.
