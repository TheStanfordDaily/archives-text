# Nanney Gives Quarter's First Organ Recital
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney will play at the
I first organ recital of the quarter to
day at 4:15 p.m. in Memorial Church.
His program includes: Toccata,
Adagio, and Fugue in C Major by
j Bach; Fantasie in F by Mozart;
Schmucke dich, o liebe Seele by
| Brahms; and Fifth Concerto for Or
-1 gan in F Major by Handel.
