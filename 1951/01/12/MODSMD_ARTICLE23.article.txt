# Engineering Exam Set for March 3
## 
### 
An examination for Junior Me
chanical Engineer with a salary
range of $281 to $341 per month has
been tentatively scheduled for
March 3.
Recent graduates and senior stu
dents are eligible to take the exam,
which is an opportunity for employ
ment with the State of California.
Seniors must produce evidence of
graduation or its equivalent before
they can be considered eligible for
appointment.
Official applications can be made
by form No. 678, which may be ob
tained through the Placement Serv
ice, Inner Quad.
