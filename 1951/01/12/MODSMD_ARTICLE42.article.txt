# Eastern Company Needs Engineers
## 
### 
Manufacturers of refrigerators
and air-conditioning equipment, lo
cated at York, Pennsylvania, are in
terested in employing mechanical
engineers, production control men,
industrial engineers, sales engineers,
and accountants, the Placement
Service announced.
The mechanical engineers are
needed for development, design, re
search, and application engineering.
Operations and planning in the
manufacturing division are included
in the production control positions.
The industrial engineering positions
are in supervision and control.
The sales engineers and account
ants must have majors in their re
spective fields.
Interested students may contact
the Placement Service. Inner Quad,
for additional information.
