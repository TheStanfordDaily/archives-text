# JAZZ SOCIETY
## 
### 
The Stanford Hot Jazz Society
will meet tonight at 8 in the Nitery
for a quarterly business meeting.
There will be election of officers and
signups for dance tickets. Final
plans for the club party will be dis
cussed. Program for the evening will
be New Orleans style clarinet. Please
bring any unusual records you may
have of this type.
