# Climber of Andes Has Story in Post
## 
### 
The experiences of David Harrah.
Stanford senior, and his companions,
who scaled Yerupaja in the Andes,
arc described in an article in this
week's Saturday Evening Post.
Harrah was one of the two mem
bers of the expedition who reached
the top of the peak. Although he
lost several of his toes from frost
bite, Harrah feels that his achieve
ment was worth it.
During Christmas vacation Har
rah described his experiences to the
American Alpine Club in New York
City. He also spoke to two groups
in his home town of Seattle.
Harrah, a philosophy major, has
returned to Stanford to complete his
senior year.
