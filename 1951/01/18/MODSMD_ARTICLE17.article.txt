# Mazzoni Gets Daily Biz Job
## 
### 
Excom last night approved the
minutes of last Friday's Publica
tions Board meeting which included
the appointment of Jim Mazzoni as
Daily business manager. Mazzoni
replaces Bill Chuchill, who was
forced to resign at the beginning of
this quarter because of poor health.
Mazzoni, a 19-year-old sophomore
majoring in political science, has
been local advertising manager on
the Daily business staff this year af
ter serving as assistant local ad man
ager during his freshman year. The
new business manager's home is
Geyserville.
Replacing Mazzoni as local ad
manager will be Mary E. May, also
a sophomore.
