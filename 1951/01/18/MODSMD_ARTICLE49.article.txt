# Friends Weekend Applicants Due
## 
### 
Students interested in attending
the American Friends Weekend In
ternational Service Seminar should
apply to W. M. Waterous, counselor
for foreign students, by tomorrow at
the latest.
The seminar will be held near
Santa Cruz Jan. 26-28. There is no
fee for attendance, and transporta
tion is provided. The topic is "Ethi
cal and Religious Motivation in So
cial Change."
Guest discussion leaders will be
Mrs. Mary Wright, curator of the
Chinese collection, Hoover Library,
and Dr. Robert E. Fitch, professor of
Christian ethics, Pacific School of
Religion.
