# Our Respects Paid...
## 
### 

A TRIBUTE Representatives of the Associated Students present a bouquet of flowers to Mrs.
David Stari Jordan, widow of the University's first president, at her home here Friday. The oc
casion was the 100 th birthday anniversary of Dr. Jordan. Pictured with Mrs. Jordan are (left) Jo
Lyon, ASSU vice president; Ted Buttner, ASSU president; and Miss Jane Hussey, vice-president
of Jordan House, women's residence named for the great educator.
