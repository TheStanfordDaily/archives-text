# Union Group Hold Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford Chapter of Atlantic Un
ion will meet tonight at 8 to discuss
"A Practical Solution for the World
Crisis." The meeting is being held
jointly with the Palo Alto chapter in
the Episcopal House, 555 Waverley.
Guest speaker will be Mrs. H. M.
Pavlo of New York, a member of the
National Committee for Atlantic Un
ion. who is making a number of ad
dresses on the West Coast.
