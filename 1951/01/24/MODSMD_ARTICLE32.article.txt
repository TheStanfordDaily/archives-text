# Study Abroad Eased for '51
## 
### 
The Institute of University Stud
ies Abroad announced today that ar
rangements have been completed
with five European universities to
ease summer school registration for
American students.
The Institute of University Studies
Abroad will facilitate advance regis
tration and guarantee that much
past confusion can now be avoided.
Every effort is being made to see that
Americans will be able to get to the
courses and that they will not be
stranded for lack of proper trans
portation.
In 1951 there are to be summer
schools at the Sorbonne. Madrid.
Fribourg, Oxford, and at the Uni
versity College, Dublin. More com
plete information is available at the
Stanford 11H office.
