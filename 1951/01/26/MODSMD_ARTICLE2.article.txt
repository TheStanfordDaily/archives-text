# India's Relief Tabled
## 
### 
WASHINGTON—The Senate For
eign Relations Committee registered
its dislike for India's attitude toward
the Korean war today by sidetrack
ing legislation to send 2.000,000 tons
of American grain to the famine
threatened country.
