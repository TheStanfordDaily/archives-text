# Foreign Students On Radio Sunday
## 
### 
Two foreign scholarship students
at Stanford, one from Germany and
the other from Austria, will discuss
Russian propaganda on the program
"The Big Lie" Sunday at 10:30 a.m.
on station KEEN.
Klaus Vollmer, psychology stu
dent from Berlin, and Karl Kisler,
political science student from Vien
na, will tell of examples of Soviet
propaganda they have found in their
home countries. Both are spending
a year at Stanford on a student body
scholarship.
They will be interviewed by Dor
othy Manes and Tom Bartlett, mem
bers of the Stanford lIR, sponsor of
the program.
