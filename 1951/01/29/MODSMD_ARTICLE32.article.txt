# Indian Giver
## 
### 

FULL BLOODED—Mrs. Dora Astarola, a full-blooded Owyhee
Indian, has her blood pressure taken prior to donating a pint of
blood on the Military Procurement Car turned over to the Red
Cross by the Western Pacific Railroad for use in obtaining much
needed blood in the isolated regions of Nevada and Utah.
