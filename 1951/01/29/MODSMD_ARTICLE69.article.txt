# Reactor Training Offered Graduates
## 
### 
Applications are now available at
the Placement Service for the Oak
Ridge School of Reactor Technology,
which offers graduate training in
the field of reactor development.
Outstanding students with under
graduate or graduate degrees in
chemistry, engineering, metallurgy,
or physics may submit applications
by March 1 for the school year 1951-
52.
Applicants must be citizens of the
United States. Pay is $285 per month
for the 12-month training period.
Further information may be ob
tained at the Placement Service,
Building 1, Inner Quad.
