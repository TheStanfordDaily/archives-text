# Bids Remain For All-Stars
## 
### 
The long lines present at last
week's ticket sales for the Louis
Armstrong dance have now disap
peared, and bids can be bought
without any wait. The tickets, which
sell for $2.40 a couple, will go on
sale at 9 a.m. today at the Engineer
ing Corner and at 2 p.m. in Union
Court.
They will also be on sale at the
ASSU office from fl to 5. About 180
bids will be available today, as there
were 40 tickets left from yesterday.
Tomorrow bids will go on sale at
10 a.m. at the Engineering corner,
and at 3 p.m. in Union Court. They
will be on sale from 9 to 5 in the
ASSU office and will also be sold
Thursday if there arc any left.
