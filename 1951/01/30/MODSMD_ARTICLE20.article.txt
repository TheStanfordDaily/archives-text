# Levin, Rebele Vie for Editor
## 
### 
Aljean Levin and Rowland
Rebele will compete for the ed
itorship of Volume 119 of The
Stanford Daily when the Daily
staff goes to the polls between 1
and 4 p.m. today. Both candi
dates are associate editors on
Volume 118 of The Daily.
Miss Levin, a journalism major
from Beverly Hills, hasworked on
The Daily for 11 quarters. She has
been feature editor and art editor of
The Daily, and is a past vice-presi
dent of Theta Sigma Phi, women's
professional journalism fraternity.
A member of Cap and Gown, Miss
Levin is a senior living at Lagunita.
Rebele, a journalism major from
San Francisco, has also worked on
The Daily for 11 quarters. lie is a
member of Sigma Delta Chi, profes
sional journalism fraternity, and has
worked on the Hanford Sentinel in
Hanford. The 20-year-old senior is
a member of Alpha Sigma Phi fra
ternity.
Both candidates have worked up
through the ranks from reporter.
The results of the election, along
with staff promotions for the new
volume, will be announced to the
staff between 4:30 and 5 this after
noon.
Members of the outgoing and in
coming editorial hoards will hold a
joint banquet tonight at Ramor
Oaks.
The entire Daily staff will gather
at Dinah's Saturday night for the
semi-annual banquet honoring the
new editor.
