# Union to Hold Formal in S.F.
## 
### 
The annual Union Residence and
Units winter formal will be held Sat
urday night in the St. Francis Yacht
Club in San Francisco from U to 1.
"Starlight Harbor" is the theme of
the dance.
Music will be provided by Larry
Cannon and his orchestra. During
the evening refreshments will be
served.
Co - chairmen of the tormal are
Jackie Harris and Evelyn Wads
worth.
Professors Claude A. Buss and
John Chester Miller of the History
Department were guests at Unioh
last Wednesday for dinner and af
ter-dinner coffee.
