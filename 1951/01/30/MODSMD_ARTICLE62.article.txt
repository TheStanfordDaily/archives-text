# Press Issues Hoover Book
## 
### 
Herbert Hoover's counsel to the
nation during the past two critical
years is available today in book form
with the publication of his "Ad
dresses Upon the American Road,
1948-1950."
The single volume, issued by the
Stanford University Press, contains
all of Mr. Hoover's speeches, official
letters, press statements, and pub
lished articles since the autumn of
1948.
Sections of the book are devoted
to the former President's opinions
on foreign policy, the reorganization
of the federal government, world
peace, rearmament, and disarma
ment.
