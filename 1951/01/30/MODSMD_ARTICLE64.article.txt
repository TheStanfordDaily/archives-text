# Engineers to See Film About Jobs
## 
### 
A sound color film which de
scribes the activities and operations
of Procter and Gamble Co. will be
shown Thursday at 3:30 p.m. at Cub
berley Auditorium by a representa
tive of the company.
Mr. D. G. Hunt of Procter and
Gamble will be on hand to discuss
with students interested in working
for the company opportunities ex
isting with the firm.
All students in electrical, mechan
ical, industrial, chemical, and civil
engineering as well as metallurgy,
physics, and chemistry are urged to
attend the meeting.
