Ambitions
RETAILING OFFERS YOU A
CAREER WITH A FUTURE
Interesting, responsible positions in merchandising, advertising, fash
ion, management, personnel, or teaching await men and women gradu
ates of the foremost School of Retailing. A one-year graduate program
leading to a Master's degree combines practical instruction, market
contacts, and supervised work experience—with pay—in leading New
York stores. Special programs for Bachelor's degree candidates and tor
non-degree students.
Request Bulletin C-70
NEW YORK UNIVERSITY
SCHOOL OF RETAILING
