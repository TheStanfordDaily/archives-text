# JV Cagers Host Navy Air Station
## 
### 
The Farm's Brave basketballers
meet Alameda Naval Air Station to
day at 3 p.m. in the Pavilion.
Alameda will bring into the Pa
vilion two of the leading players in
the service loop. They are center
Bill Manning and forward Bob Clay
ton, both high scorers and excellent
floor men.
The Braves will open with Dan
Evans and Paul Johnson at forwards,
Bill Stevenson at center, and the
guard spots a toss -up between
George Zaninovich, Tom DuFour,
and Fritz Fenster.
