# Faculty to Attend Drama Meeting
## 
### 
Five members of the Speech and
Drama Department will take part in
the annual Northwest Regional Con
ference of the American Educational
Theater Association this weekend at
the University of Oregon.
Dr. Norman Philbrick, acting head
of the department and executive
secretary of AETA, will represent
the national organization at the con
ference at Eugene.
Dr. Robert Dicrlam, acting profes
sor of speech and drama, will deliver
papers before two section meetings.
Chester Barker, executive secre
tary of the Speech and Drama De
partment, will be chairman of the
conference section on business and
promotion aspects of the theater.
