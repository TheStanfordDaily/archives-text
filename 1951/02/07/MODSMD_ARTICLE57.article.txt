# Farm Debaters to Host Forensic League Tourney
## 
### 
The ASSU Debate Society will
play host to ten Bay Area schools in
the fifth renewal of the annual Bay
Area Debate League tournament
Friday night. The meeting will be
held at 7:30 in Room 6 of the Educa
tion Building.
The general topic "The Role of the
Federal Government in Labor-Man
agement Relations" will be discussed
in each of six panels.
A meeting of Debate Society
members who are working in this
and the Cal Tech tourney will be
held tomorrow night at 7:30 on the
third floor of the Women's Club
house.
Debate Manager Harvey Wagner
requests that the six panel discus
sion leaders for Friday's meet come
tomorrow so that preparations may
be completed.
Debate squad members who are

to speak in the Cal Tech contest will
present a practice exhibition debate.
Joel Leetham and Bruce Bailey will
speak for the affirmative and Leroy
Sloan and Harvey Wagner will talk
for the negative.
