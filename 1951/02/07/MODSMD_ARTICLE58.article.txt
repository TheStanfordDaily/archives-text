# Russian Language Course Offered
## 
### 
Plans have been completed to of
fer an intensive 15 - hour weekly
course in the Russian language dur
ing the spring quarter at Stanford,
it was announced yesterday by Dr.
Anthony E. Sokol, head of the De
partment of Asiatic and Slavic Stud
ies.
The class, which will be taught by
Professor Jack A. Posin, will be lim
ited to between 12 and 15 students
who are graduates or near enough
to graduation so that their require
ments have been met.
