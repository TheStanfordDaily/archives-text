# Nanney Will Present Lenten Music Program
## 
### 
This afternoon at 4:15 in Memorial
Church, Organist Herbert B. Nan
ney will present a program of Len
ten music. The program will include:
"Forty Days and Forty Nights," by
Bach; "O Man, Bemoan Thy Griev
ous Sin," by Bach; Fugue on the
"Kyrie," by Couperin; "Bencdictus,"
by Reger; "Agnus Dei," by Bing
ham; and "Chorale in E Major," by
' Franck.
