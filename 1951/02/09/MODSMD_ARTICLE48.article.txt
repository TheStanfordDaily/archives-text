# Salgo Directs Last Concert
## 
### 
The fifth Chamber Music Scries
concert, and the last this quarter,
will be held Sunday at 4 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium. It will be
open to the public without charge.
The program will include Brahms'
horn trio, Opus No. 40 in E flat, and
will feature Caswell Neal, horn;
William Jenkinson, violin; and Nor
ma Reddert, piano.
The concerts are prepared under
the direction of Sandor Salgo, as
sistant professor of music.
