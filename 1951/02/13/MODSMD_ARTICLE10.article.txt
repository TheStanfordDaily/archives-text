# Meet on Yell Leading Scheduled at Cubberley
## 
###

everyone interested in yell leading
today in Cubberley Auditorium at
4 p.m. The yell leaders will be
present to explain what require
ments are necessary for the position
and will also give a briefing on
Stanford yells. These meetings will
continue every day this week at
the same time and place.
