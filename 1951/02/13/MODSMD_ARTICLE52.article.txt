# Con Home Boys Join Scout Troop
## 
### 
Under the direction and sponsor
ship of Alpha Phi Omega, a Boy
Scout troop has recently been,estab
lished in the Convalescent Home. In
charge of the troop is John Planting,
alumni secretary of the national
service fraternity.
Members of the fraternity visit the
Con Home Tuesdays and Thursdays
to help boys of scout age pass their
Boy Scout tests.
