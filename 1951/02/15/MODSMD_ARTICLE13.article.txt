# DR. STERLING WILL GO EAST
## 
### 
President Wallace Sterling of
Stanford University will leave by
train for New York Saturday to
attend a meeting of the Commis
sion on Financing Higher Educa
tion Feb. 26-27.
During his trip he will address
Stanford alumni groups in Reno
Sunday, Salt Lake City Monday,
and Denver March 4.
He will spend a week in New
York and is expected back on the
campus March 6.
