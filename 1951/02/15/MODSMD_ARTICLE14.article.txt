# '52 Excom to Air Plans for Prom
## 
### 
An open meeting of class of '52 ex
com will be held Monday night in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse. All juniors arc invited
to attend.
"This meeting is very important
because its purpose is to initiate
plans for the Junior Weekend in
April," said Brad Blaine, class presi
dent. "Special attention will be giv
en to the Water Carnival and prom,"
he added. Plans for a roller skat
ing party and class reunion this
quarter will also be discussed.
The meeting will be followed by
entertainment, which is being plan
ned by George Denny. Refreshments
will be served.
