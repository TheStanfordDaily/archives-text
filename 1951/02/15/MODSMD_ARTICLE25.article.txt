# Six Roughs Disciplined
## 
### 
Six Stanford men students ap
peared before Palo Alto Police Judge
Richard Doyle yesterday on a charge
made by Palo Alto taxicab drivers,
of squirting water at cabs and their
drivers.
The six were apprehended by
Stanford police last week.
At the request of Richard Balch,
chief counselor for men, the students
were turned over to his office for dis
ciplinary action with the agreement
that the office assume responsibility
for the men's future behavior.
All six were sentenced to 15 hours
at the Con Home by Balch, with the
stipulation that the work must be
completed by March 23. Otherwise
their registration for spring quartet
will not be allowed.
