# Nanney to Present U.S. Music Recital
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney, organist, will
play a program of organ music by
American composers at the regular
Thursday organ recital in Memorial
Church this afternoon at 4:15.
He will play "Fanfare," by Virgil
Thompson; "Prelude in B Flat," by
Nanney; "Dirge" (Passacaglia for
Organ), by Douglas Moore; "Caril
lon," by Lro Sowerby; "Chromatic
Study on the Name of Bach,"' by
Walter Piston; "Prelude on Jam Rc
cedit Igneus," by Brucc Simonds;
and "Vexilla Regis," by Richard
Purvis.
