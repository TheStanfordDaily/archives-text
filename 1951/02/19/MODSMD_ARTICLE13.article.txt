# All of '52 Asked To Class Meeting
## 
### 
All juniors are invited to attend
an open meeting of the class of '52
tonight at 7:.'srt in the lower lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.
"The purpose of the meeting is
to initiate plans for the Junior
Weekend in April," said Brad
Hlaine, class president.
Plans for a roller-skating party
on March 4 and a class reunion at
L'Ominic's on March 10 will also
lie discussed.
The meeting will be followed by
entertainment planned by George
Denny and refreshments.
