# Toyon to Present All-Campus Dance
## 
### 
An all - campus dance celebrating
George Washington's birthday will
he presented by Toyon Hall Wed
nesday night from 0 to 1. Bids for
the dressy sport dance are selling for
$1.20 a couple at the ASSU office
and at the Toyon Hall office.
Jimmy Blass, who recently fin
ished an engagement at the St. Fran
cis. will provide music for the event.
Entertainment will be supplied by
Bill Wraith. George Geering, and
Raj' Williams. Refreshments will be
served.
