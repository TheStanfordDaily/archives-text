# Marines to Train Women Officers
## 
### 
The Marine Corps has announced
that the Women's Officer Training
Class is open for women between the
ages of 19 and 25.
General requirements are as fol
lows: the applicant must be single, a
citizen of the United States, in good
physical condition and of good moral
character. The applicant must also
be a student or a graduate of a na
tional or regional accredited college
or university. Former women Ma
rines who will not be 27 by July 1
will also be accepted.
Application should be made at the
Federal Office Building, Fulton and
Market, San Francisco.
