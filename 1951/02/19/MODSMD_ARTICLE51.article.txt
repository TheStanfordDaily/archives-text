# Golf Match Play Gets Under Way
## 
### 
With qualifying rounds already
out of tho way, the 32 entrants in the
r.nnual All-University Golf Tourna
ment will begin the first round of
match play today.
In the qualifying round, medal
honors went to Keith Beekman with
a torrid one-under-par 70. Next in

Jack Knosher, Grant Spaeth, and
