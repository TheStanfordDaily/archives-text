# Orchesis to Join Mills Symposium
## 
### 
Twenty members of Orchesis at
tended the annual modern dance
symposium held Saturday at Mills
College. Besides Stanford and Mills,
the University of California, San
Jose State, San Francisco State,
and Chico State also attended the
symposium.
Stanford presented "Force, Coun
ter-Force," one of the dances they
did here for their Tuesday show. The
dance choreography was done by
Suzanne Kelly.
