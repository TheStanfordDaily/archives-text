# Folk Operas Will Be Given
## 
### 
Two classic American folk operas
will be presented March 8, 9, and 10
in the Memorial Auditorium by the
Stanford Players and the Depart
ment of Music.
Tickets are now on sale at the Me
morial Hall box oflice at $1 20, Mon
day through Friday, 9 to 12 and 1
to 5. For reservations, phone DA
3-0665.
The Players and the 'Music De
partment are presenting the West
Coast premiere of "The Jumping
Frog of Calaveras County," adapted
into a folk opera by Lukas F'oss from
the tall tale by Mark Twain; and
"The Devil and Daniel Webster" by
Douglas Moore, based on the story
by Stephen Vincent Benet.
