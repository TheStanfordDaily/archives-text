# Bernstein to Give Concert in S.F.
## 
### 
The Israel Philharmonic Orchestra
will present concerts tomorrow and
Wednesday in the San Francisco
Opera House.
The orchestra, comprised of musi
cians from 20 different countries, is
making a tour of 40 United States
cities. It is presented by the Ameri
can Fund for Israel Institutions.
Serge Koussevitsky will conduct
the first San Francisco symphony,
and Leonard Bernstein the second.
Tickets are available at the Opera-
Symphony Box Office, Sherman Clay.
