# Roble to Present Spring Informal
## 
### 
Roble will hold a closed informal
dance Friday night from 9 to 1. The
dance, called "Beaux and Belles," has
a spring theme and the attire for
women is cotton dresses. Admission
is $1.
There will be dancing in A dining
room and A lobby. Refreshments
will be served in B dining room. The
music will come from records.
Committee chairmen for the affair
are: Ann Hyne, decorations; Beth
Sieroty, arrangements; Claire Fos
ter, refreshments; Anne Eliot, pub
licity; Bambi Witherell, cleanup;
Judy Schaeffer, bids; Sally Lanser,
entertainment; Jane Shortt, tickets.
