# Esquire Motif To Hit Stern
## 
### 
Stern Hall's free all - campus
"Good Times" Esquire dance will
feature the music of Bob Emerson
and his orchestra tomorrow evening
from 9 to 1. Emerson has recently
completed a six weeks' engagement
in San Francisco.
Refreshments will be provided and
there Will be fires and easy chairs in
Stern's four lounges. Dick Konig,
over-all chairman for the dance, re
ports that appropriate decorations
have been received through the
courtesy of Esquire magazine, and
will be used to carry out the theme
of the fiance.
