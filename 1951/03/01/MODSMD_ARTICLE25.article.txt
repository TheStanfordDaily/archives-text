# Once Around the Quad
## Student Activities

EUROPEAN TRAVEL prospects
will be outlined by Jim Brown,
campus representative of Ameri
can Express, tonight at 7:30 in
Room 124.
JUNIOR EXCOM will meet tonight
at 7:30 in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
AWS will hold a board meeting to
day at 4:15 p.m. in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
FOLKDANCERS meet tonight at
7:30 in the dance studio. Elemen

to 8, and square dance from 8 to
8:30.
BADMINTON CLUB will meet to
night at 8 in the Women's Gym.
VARSITY RIFLE TEAM will have a
shoulder-to-shoulder match with
Cal today. Members will leave the
range at 12:45.
