# Sadie Hawkins Dance Will Be Held at Austin's Red Barn
## 
### 
Although it's not Leap Year, girls
will ask fellows on Friday, March 9.
At this time the Sadie Hawkins
dance will be held at Austin's Red
Barn.
Howard Frederick's band will
play. Entertainment will also include
the Soph Quartet featuring Bob
James, Gayle Plummer, Jim Hughes,
and Bill Winden.
From Monday through Friday,
tickets will be sold at the ASSU of
fice and in all women's dorms Mon
day night. Besides the 25-cent tick
et, admission will cost the girl a
penny for every pound she weighs.
