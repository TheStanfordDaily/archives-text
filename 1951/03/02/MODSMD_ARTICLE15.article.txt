# '52ers Don Skates For Party Sunday
## 
### 
Members of the class of '52 and
their guests are invited to the junior
cxcom - sponsored roller skating
party Sunday night at B:.'io in Skate
Haven, 3175 Middlefield Road.
Chairman of the event is Elise
McKown. A junior cxcom party will
precede the roller-skating affair.
Final plans for a class reunion at
L'Ommie's on March 10 were dis
cussed at the '52 cxcom meeting last
night.
