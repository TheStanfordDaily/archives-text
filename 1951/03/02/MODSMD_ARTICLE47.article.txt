# Once Around the Quad
## Student Activities 
### 
ALPINE CLUB will have its Berg
steigers' Ball tonight at 8:30 at
L'Omelette. Those going on the
overnight practice climb at Napa
are to meet at History corner at
3 p.m. Saturday. See bulletin
board for what to bring.
FOLKDANCERS meet tonight at
7:30 in the Village Auditorium.

Both beginners and advanced are
invited.
A SQUARE DANCE will be held at
Rossotti's at 8:30 p.m. Saturday.
All students are invited.
EXPERIMENT IN INTERNATION
AL LIVING will hold a meeting
in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse Monday at 7:30
p.m. Slides will be shown of
American students living with
French, Swedish, and Mexican
families in those countries.
ORESTEIA tryouts will be held
next week for three plays of Aes
chylus to be presented during
spring quarter. The tryouts will
be in Room 129, Memorial Hall,
on Monday at 7:30 p.m.. Tuesday
3-5:30 p.m., Wednesday 7:30 p.m.,
and Thursday 3-5:30 p.m.
