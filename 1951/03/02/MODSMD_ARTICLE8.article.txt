# Stern Hall Brings Esquire to Campus
## 
### 
Esquire comes to the campus to
night with Eskie and his pin-ups at
Stern Hall's free all-campus "Good
limes" Esquire Dance from 9 to 1
at Stern Hall.
Bob Emerson and his orchestra
will provide the music for dancing
in Stern's "Esquire Ballroom" and
four modern lounges.
Stern's own Esquire Quartet, Phil
Hudncr, Jim Schwarzman, Ap Eyre,
and Dick Rhodes, will handle inter
mission entertainment.
Dress for the affair is informal.
Decorations will feature blow-ups
of recent Esquire covers and en
largements of some of the more fa
mous Esquire lovelies.
