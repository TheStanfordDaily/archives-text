# House Drawings Near for Coeds
## 
### 
Women students not returning to
a University residence spring quar
ter must fill out yellow cards, avail
able at their residence, between
today and Wednesday and return
them to the resident director, house
mother, or R.A. not later than noon
Wednesday. No refund request will
be granted alter March 15.
Drawing for change of residence
for spring quarter will take place
Wednesday in Building 100 for
sponsors. Off-campus girls of up
per division standing who desire to
live on campus can draw Thursday
along with sophomores and upper
division students now living on
campus.
