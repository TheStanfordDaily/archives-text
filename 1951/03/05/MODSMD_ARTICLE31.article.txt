# Firms Offer Jobs
## CO-PILOTS NEEDED 
### 
Co-pilots are wanted by the Pan-
American Airways.
Qualifications for the job are 750
hours solo or command experience,
a current commercial license, and
current instrument rating. In addi
tion, the applicant must be a U.S.
citizen between the ages of 21 and
28, and have had two years of col
lege.
The pilots will be assigned to the
Atlantic Division, the East Coast, or
Europe.
Base pay is $300 plus allowances.
Interested men should phone Mr.
Okerlund, Diamond 4-1723, Ext. 231,
for interview appointments at Mills
Field.
