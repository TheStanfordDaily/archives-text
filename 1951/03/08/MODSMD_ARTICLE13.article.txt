# Founders' Day To Halt Classes
## 
### 
Classes will be suspended tomor
row at 10 a.m. for ceremonies in
Memorial Church, celebrating the
66th year of Stanford University.
Dr. Sterling will make an address
entitled "The Adventure of the
Diver."
Stanford was founded March 9,
1885, and was formally opened Oct.
1, 1891.
Founders' Day ceremonies will
also include an anthem and motet
by the University Choir.
