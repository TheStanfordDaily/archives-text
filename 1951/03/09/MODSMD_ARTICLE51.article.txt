# Geary Takes First
## 
### 
Sharon Geary, Stanford's best
woman swimmer since Brenda
Helser, has received three first
place medals in the Pan American
Games being held this week in
Buenos Aires.
Miss Geary won the 100-meter
freestyle Sunday, beating the
American champion, Jackie La
vine, in 1:08.3. She teamed up
with Miss Lavine, Betty Mullen,
and Maureen O'Brien to win the
women's 400-meter freestyle re
lay Tuesday, and Thursday she
anchored the winning medley re
lay team.
