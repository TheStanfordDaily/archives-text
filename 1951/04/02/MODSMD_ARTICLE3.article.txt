# Atlantic Pact
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April I—The ad
ministration revealed today—on the
eve of the Senate's historic vote on
sending U.S. troops to Europe—that
Atlantic Pact Allies have doubled
their arms production since 1949
and will double it again this year.

The figures, contained in Defense
Mobilizer Charles E. Wilson's quar
terly report to President Truman,
were promptly hailed by adminis
tration supporters as the "complete
answer" to charges that Western
European Pact members are drag
ging their feet on the mutual de
fense program.
