# Farm's Conklin In Golf Finals
## 
### 
Stanford's sophomore sensation,
Roland Conlflin, will meet Ken
Venturi of San Jose State in the
finals of the Northern California In
tercollegiate Golf Championship at
Pasatiempo next Saturday.
Conklin, who is currently third
man on the Stanford golf team,
reached the finals by defeating
Grant Spaeth, also of Stanford,
one up.
Venturi, last year's San Francisco
City champion, won over Stanford's
John Elliott. He hit four birdies
and an eagle on the front nine, shot
even par on the back nine for a
five-under, 67.
