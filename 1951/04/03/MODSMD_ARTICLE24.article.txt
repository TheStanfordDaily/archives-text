# '51 Will Hold Get-Together
## 
### 
The senior class will hold its last
reunion of the year in L'Ommie's
bark room tomorrow night. The get
together, for seniors only, will begin
at 8:30 and last until L'Ommie's
closes.
Entertainment during the eve
ning will be provided by the Phi
Delt-DU band. The reunion is a no
date affair.
"This reunion will be the last
| chance for ail seniors to get together
and see old friends and talk over
old times," said Jan Edwards, chair
man of the program.
