# Organ Class Given At Palo Alto High
## 
### 
Palo Alto High's Evening School
is offering beginning and interme
diate courses in organ.
Classes meet once a week. The
beginners' class (requiring one
year's previous piano experience)
meets on Monday nights. The inter
mediate course (requiring two years
of piano) meets on Wednesday
nights.
Hours are 7:30 to 9:30. The classes
meet at 721 Emerson Street, Palo
Alto.
Registration fee is $1.50.
