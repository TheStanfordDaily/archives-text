# Frosh Plan Beach Bust
## 
### 
A caravan of frosh cars will head
out to San Gregorio beach Saturday
for a Class of '54 beach party, to be
held between 12:30 and 6 that af
ternoon.
The party will feature contests,
athletic and otherwise, including
girls' athletic events, a Hawaiian
shirt contest, and a voicc-and-uku
lele competition.
Frosh President Larry Keating,
announcing the party, added that
there would be "free cats" for the
'54ers.
