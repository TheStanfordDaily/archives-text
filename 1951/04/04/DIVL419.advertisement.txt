Dear Stanford,
Longbarn is starting a new quarter with a brand
new feature designed especially for you..,we're
calling it "Stanford Nite."
It means that every Friday night Longbarn wil 1.
have on hand:
(1) Dick Roberts and his fine coinbo to play
your kind of music.
(2) A student talent show made up of talent from
campus and the surrounding area.
(3) An informal atmosphere created by the "quad
clothes" dress and the usual Longbarn
friendliness.
We cordially invite you all to attend our "Stan
ford Nite" opening this Friday night, and hope you
will enjoy our new idea in restaurant entertain
ment#
Sincerely yours,
Willie and Eddie
P.S.: If you wish to join in the show, contact
Dick Roberts, DA 3-8635... first prise is two free
dinners and all runners-up get free juice.
