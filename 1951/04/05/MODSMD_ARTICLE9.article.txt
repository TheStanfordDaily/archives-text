# Paying of Bills Begins Today
## 
### 
Board and room rent bills for the
spring quarter become payable to
day at. the Cashier's office in Inner
Quad. The office in Building 1 is
open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday
through Friday. Deadline for pay
ment is April 15.
Village students may pay at the
Village Cashier's office from 9:30
a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and 1:30 to 3:30
p.m. Monday through Friday.
Payments made after April 15 arc
subject to fine.
