# KEEN to Feature Athenian Scholar
## 
### 
Fotini Kaplanidou, '49-'5O foreign
scholarship student from Athens,
Greece, will be interviewed by
Stanford student Fred Forsman on
KEEN's "Big Lie" program Sun
day morning at 10:30.
Miss Kaplanidou, who has recent
ly left for her home in Greece, will
discuss Russian propaganda. The
interview has been tape-recorded.
Next week Herman Ermolaev, a
former foreign scholarship student
from Rostov, will discuss Russian
collective farming.
