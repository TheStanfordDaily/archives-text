# Forensic Tryouts To Begin Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Pacific Forensic
League Tournament, to be held ut
Stanford during the week of April
24, will be given today at 4:15 p.m.
in Room 201 of the History Build
ing.
The tryouts will consist of a five
minute speech and a three-minute
speech on both sides of the ques
tion, "The Non-Communist Nations
Should Form a New Internatonal
Organization." Four students will
he chosen to take part in debate
and discussion.
