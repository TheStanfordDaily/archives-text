# Chorus to Sing In S.F. Concert
## 
### 
The Stanford University Chorus
of 200 members, under the direction
of Harold C. Schmidt, will be the
assisting artists in the San Fran
cisco Symphony's nineteenth series
of concerts Thursday, Friday, and
Saturday.
The performances will be held
at the San Francisco Opera House at
8:30 p.m. Thursday, 2:15 p.m. Fri
day, and 8:30 p.m. Saturday.
The chorus, in its first appearance
with the Symphony this year, will
be under the direction of Bruno
Walter, who is guest conductor for
two weeks in the absence of Pierre
Monteux.
Walter will conduct the Second
Symphony by Mahler and Haydn's
Symphony No. 88 in G Major.
