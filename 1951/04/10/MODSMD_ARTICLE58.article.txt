# Bookstore Sells Senior Programs
## 
### 
Senior class week programs and
1051 graduation announcements may
be ordered now, and until May 12,
at the Stanford Bookstore.
The programs are made up like
small memory books, with white
leather covers. They include pic
tures of the campus, lists of class
officers, and committee chairmen,
and the names of all candidates for
degrees.
Programs cost $1.35 and an
nouncements are 15 cents apiece.
All profits from their sale will no
into the '5T treasury and will be
used, for the Senior Ball and Class
Day activities.
