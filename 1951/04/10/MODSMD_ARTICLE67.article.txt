# 'Choephoroe' Chorus Members Announced
## 
### 
Omitted from the cast of the
"Orcsteia" which was published in
Thursday's Daily were members of
the "Chocphoroc" chorus. The
"Choephoroe" is the second play in
the Greek trilogy to be presented at
Stanford May 31, June 1 and 2.
In the chorus are Sue Alter, Pat
Beverly, Pat Currier, Joy Dirstine,
Milan Draehman, Priscilla Dunn,
Helen Gaylord, Sara Halhert, Lil
lian Kreling, Jean Leonard, Fran
ces Lusk, Joyce Schocning, Phyllis
Smith.
