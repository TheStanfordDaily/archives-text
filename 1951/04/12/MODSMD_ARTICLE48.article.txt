# Seniors May Order '51 Announcements
## 
### 
The Stanford Bookstore is taking
orders now and until May 12 for
Senior Class "Week programs and
1951 graduation announcements.
The programs include pictures of
the campus, lists of class officers,
committee chairmen, and the names
of all candidates for degrees. They
are made up like small memory
books, with white leather covers.
Programs cost $1.35 arid' an
nouncements are 15 cerits apiece.
All profits from their Sale will go
into the '51 treasury arid will be
used for the Senior Ball And Class
Day activities.
