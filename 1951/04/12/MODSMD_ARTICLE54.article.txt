##

Mayer has announced that fresh
man tryouts for sophomore foot
hall managers will he held during
spring practice.
Mayer points out that soph man
agers receive PE credits, award
sweaters, training table privileges,
and the opportunity to travel with
the team. Interested freshmen
should contact Mayer on the foot
ball practice field between 3:30
and 5:30 p.m.
