# Humanities Major Added to Courses
## 
### 
A major in humanities v/as added
to the Stanford curriculum last
night.
The announcement was made by
Professor John W. Dodds, chairman
of the Honors Program in Humani
ties, at a special meeting of the
members fo the program.
The humanities major will call for
completion of the honors program
and 30 units of upper division work
(approved by the Honors Commit
tee) in the departments of Art and
Architecture, Asiatic and Slavic
Studies, Classics, English, Germanic
Languages, History, Music, Philos
ophy, Romanic Languages, and
Speech and Drama.
The student majoring in humani
ties will graduate with a Bachelor
of Arts in Humanities.
