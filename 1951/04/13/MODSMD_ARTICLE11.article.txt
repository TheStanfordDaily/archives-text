# Model UN Progresses
## 
### 
The 15-man Soviet delegation will
hold its initial caucus here tomorrow
to prepare for the three-day session
of the Model United Nations, con
vening at Stanford April 26.
Russian psychology, the Soviet po
litical outlook, and Soviet tactics will
be studied by the delegates when
they meet al 10 tomorrow morning
in the 11R. office, delegation head
George Velliotes.
A second delegation, representing
Cuba, will attend the conference
from Stanford. Student World Fed
eralists will compose this group.
According to the latest tally, 29
schools have confirmed that from five
to nine students will represent them
at the Model UN. Delegates, each
standing for a member nation, from
colleges and universities of the 11
western states will be sent to fill the
60-nation quota.
