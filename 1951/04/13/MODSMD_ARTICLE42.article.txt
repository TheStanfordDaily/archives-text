# Army, Air ROTC To Hold Inspection
## 
### 
The annual formal inspection of
Stanford's Army and Air Force
ROTC units will be held next Mon
day and Tuesday, it was announced
yesterday by Colonel James V. Col
lier, PMS&T, and Colonel Henry L.
Barrett, PAS&T.
Inspection teams from both the
Fourth Air Force and the Sixth
Army will be here. Heading the
Army inspection team is Colonel
Lyle J. Fitzpatrick. Heading the Air
Force team from Hamilton Field is
Major General William A. Hall,
commanding general of the Fourth
Air Force.
The inspections will include visits
to classrooms to study teaching
methods, an inspection of adminis
trative procedures, and conferences
with University and ROTC officials
to determine ways of improving
present methods, if necessary.
