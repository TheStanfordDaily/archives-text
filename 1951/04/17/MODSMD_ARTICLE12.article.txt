# BLANKS ARRIVE FOR DRAFT TEST
## 
### 
San Jose's draft board has re
ceived a large number of application
blanks for the Selective Service
qualification test. Chief Counselor
for Men Richard L. Balch said yes
terday.
Students must apply in person and
show student body card, draft card,
and evidence of age. Board No. 62
in San Jose is located at 317 Bank
of America Building.
Balch also advised students to tell
their home draft boards that they
were taking the test.
Complete details for filling out the
blanks, and a list of the California
testing centers, were listed in yes
terday's Daily. The test will be
given three times, May 26, June 16,
and June 30.
