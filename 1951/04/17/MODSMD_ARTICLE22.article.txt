# Bishop Will Speak In Religion Class
## 
### 
Rt. Rev. Robert Cocil Mortimer,
Lord Bishop of Exeter, England,
will address Dr. Alexander Miller's
comparative religion class at 11 a.m.
today in Room 54.
The bishop is Regius Professor of
Moral and Pastoral Theology at Ox
ford University.
He will make a tour of the Uni
versity and Hoover Library in the
afternoon. The bishop is on the West
Coast to deliver a series of lectures
on canon law at the University of
California.
