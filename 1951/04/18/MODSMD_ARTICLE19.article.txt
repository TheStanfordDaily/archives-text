# Contest in Music Composition Open
## 
### 
All Stanford students are eligible
lor a $100 prize being offered by the
Humanities Department for a musi
cal composition. Rules that have
been announced are:
1. Entries should be submitted to
the music office at the Knoll no
later than May 1. They may be
picked up at the same place after
the award has been made.
2. Compositions submitted must
be in ink and must be legible. No
penciled manuscripts will be con
sidered.
o. Entrants may submit either a
piece in major form, or a group of
at least three shorter pieces.
4. There are no restrictions as to
medium.
