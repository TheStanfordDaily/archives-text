# Sign-ups Begin For Blood Donors
## 
### 
Stanford students may make an
appointment tomorrow to give
blood for United States soldiers in
Korea, at a desk set up in front of
the Cellar from 8 to 5 p.m.
The bloodmobile from San Jose
will set up its equipment next Wed
nesday in the Women's Clubhouse
from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. At that time
it will collect blood from those who
sign up tomorrow.
