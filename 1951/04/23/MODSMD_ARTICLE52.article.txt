# Roble Formal Bids Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Bids for the Koble spring formal
May 4 will go on sale today at $2 per
couple.
"Spring Fever" is the theme of
the dance, which will be held in the
Women's Gym. Jimmy Blass and
his orchestra will furnish the music.
Paula Hynes is general chairman
of the dance. Committee members
include: Lucie Wells, invitations;
Marty Lewis, tickets; Buzzy Dama
rosh, decorations; Jeanie King and
Janeene Knipe, arrangements; Di
anne Gorman, clean-up; Ruth Nus
baum, refreshmets; Lois Fellows
and Helen Ann Emrich, publicity.
