# Fencers To Open Season Thursday
## 
### 
Coach Elwyn Bugge's fencers will
engage in their first match of the
year when they meet San Francisco
State Thursday night in the Encina
Gym. Both the varsity and JV's will
compete.
The Indians are led by Rich
Gross, who tied for first in the
recent Northern California Open
Championships, and Ralph Amado.
who tied for third in the same
event.
The Gators also boast a powerful
aggregation sparked by Jerry Biag
ginni, California collegiate champ.
