# Lawyers Enter Area Moot Finals
## 
### 
The Stanford Law School will
vie with Santa Clara in the finals of
the Bay Area Intercollegiate Moot
Court competition Friday afternoon
in San Francisco.
Representing Stanford in the con
test, sponsored by the Barristers'
Club of San Francisco, are Mis.
Marilyn Green and Robert L. Ben
nett. Clayton Janssen and Jack
Halpin are acting as counsel on the
brief.
The Stanford team won its way
into the finals by beating USF in
the preliminary round held April
13, while, on the same date, Santa
Clara defeated California.
