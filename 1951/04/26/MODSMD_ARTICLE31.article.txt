# Boys' Camp

### 
Representatives from the Coffee
Creek Ranch, a private boys' camp
near Trinity Center, California, will
be on campus tomorrow to inter
view men interested in working at
the camp this summer.
The camp, which is open from
June 21 to Aug. 5, is especially in
terested in men over 21. Further
details may be secured at the Stu
dent Employment Office, Building 1
of the Inner Quad.
Appointments to see the repre
sentatives may be made at the Stu

be here ut 10 a.m.
