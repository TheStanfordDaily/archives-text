# WAA Seeks Nominations
## 
### 
The nominating committee of WAA
seeks nominations of WAA officers.
Suggestions may be left in the nomi
nation box in the lobby of the Wom
en's Gym until May 4,
The committee will interview pros
pective candidates on May 3, 4, and
7 and choose those eligible to run
for office.
Officers to be elected on May 14 for
the 1951-52 Board are president, vice
president, corresponding secretary,
recording secretary, treasurer, and
publicity chairman. Senior, junior,
and sophomore representatives will
also be elected.
All registered women students
with a C average are eligible.
