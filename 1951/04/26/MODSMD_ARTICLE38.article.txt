# Six Editors Chosen For Law Review
## 
### 
Six Stanford law students have
been selected to head the editorial
staff of the quarterly Stanford Law
Review during the 1951-52 school
year, it was announced yesterday
by Carl B. Spaeth, dean of the Law
School.
The new officers were selected on
the basis of outstanding legal and
scholastic ability from more than a
score of candidates.
Heading the Law Review next year
will be John F. Wells, president and
editor-in-chief; David E. Salisbury,
managing editor; Don E. Glass, case
editor; George K. Doty and John 11.
Halpin. Jr., note editors; and Donald
B. Jarvis, article and book revit-w
editor.
