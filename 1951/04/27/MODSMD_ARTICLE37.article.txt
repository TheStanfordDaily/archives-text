# Cards Golf Champs
## 
### 
Stanford is the only Pacific Coast
Conference school whose golf team
has ever won the national crown.
The Cards did it five times: in 11)38,
1939, 15)41, 1942. and 1946.
From the PCC, the only golfers
who have ever placed in the NCAA
are Warren Berl, in 1939; Bud
Brownell, in 1941; and Sandy Ta
tum, in 1942. All are from Stanford,
Tatum being the only national
champion.
