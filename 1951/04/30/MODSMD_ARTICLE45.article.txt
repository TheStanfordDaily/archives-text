# YWCA to Hold Farm Work Camp
## 
### 
Stanford's YWCA Committee on
Effective Citizenship Program is
sponsoring a weekend work camp
from May 11 to 13 at the Thornton
Farm Labor Camp. The purpose of
the weekend is to acquaint students
with the living and working condi
tions of migrant workers.
Featured at the weekend will be
a square dance and recreational
program on Saturday night. May 12,
with both students and the migrant
laborers participating.
Anyone interested in the weekend
may obtain more information at the
offices of the Stanford YWCA.
