# Tickets on Sale For '52 Reunion
## 
### 
Tickets for 50 cents a person to
the junior reunion Saturday night
will go on sale today at the Cellar
from 10 a.m. to 12 noon and will
continue to be sold at those hours
during the week.
The class of '52 get-together, to
be held at Shangri-La, will start at
9 p.m. and will feature a variety
show at 10:30. Recorded music will
be provided for dancing.
