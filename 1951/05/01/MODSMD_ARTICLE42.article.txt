# Frosh to Hold Party At Adobe Creek in May
## 
### 
Adobe Creek will be the scene for j
a freshman party near the end of j
May. announces Frosh Excom.
Freshmen will take over the place
for badminton and swimming in the
afternoon and dinner and dancing
in the evening. The affair will be
dutch at $2.50 per person.
