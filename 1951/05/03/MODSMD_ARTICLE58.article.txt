# Movie Featuring Opera Stars To Be Shown to Film Society
## 
### 
"Opera in Film" and "Images Me
dievales" will be shown at 4:15 p.m.

Enrico Caruso, Nino Martini, Glad

made up of excerpts from movies
made between 1915 and 1938.
"Images Medievales" is a new
French color film which gives a pic
ture of life in the Middle Ages,
from amorous activities in the court
