# Roble And Toyon To Present Spring Dance This Weekend
## 
### 
Roblc will present its formal
"Spring Fever" tonight from 9 to 1
in the Women's Gym. All freshman
women are invited.
Music will be played by Jimmy
Llass and his orchestra. The spring
motif is going to be carried out in
the decorations and the bids.
An Apache dance and French
songs will highlight the entertain
ment at Toyon's all-campus formal,
"Rue des Fleurs," tomorrow night
in the Toyon lounge.
A Frenchman, who speaks very

little English, will act as door man.
All girls are to receive carnations.
Bids are still on sale at Toyon and
the ASSU office for $3.
