OFF-CAMPUS CON HOME DRIVE
SOLICITORS will meet promptly
at 7 tonight in the Cardinals of
fice, upstairs in the Old Union.
SPRING SHOW CHORUS will re
hearse tonight at 7:30 in the
Kam's Head room.
RALLY COM will meet tonight at
7:30 in the lower lounge of the !

Women's Clubhouse. Elections for
next year's officers will be held,
and plans for this month's Spring
Sing will be discussed.
FOLK DANCE REHEARSALS will
be held tonight. Squares at 7:15,
English at 8, Danish-German at
!). Slavic at 10.
WOMEN'S TENNIS CLUB will hold

their last tryout today at I p.m. on
the women's courts.
SWIM CLUB will meet tonight at
the women's pool at 7:30.
STANFORD DANCE CLUB meets
tonight at 7:30 in the auditorium
of the Women's Clubhouse. The
j lindy - jitterbug will be taught.
Advanced group meets at 9. Ev
erybody's welcome. Free refresh
ments.
UNIVERSITY CHORUS will not
meet this afternoon. The next
meeting will be Wednesday night.

at 7 in the TV room at Toyon.
