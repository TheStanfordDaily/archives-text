# Rebate Deadline Set for May 15
## 
### 
Rebate slips may be turned in
through May 15, the Stanford Book
store has announced.
Students should pick up envelopes
at the Bookstore and list their re
ceipts up to, but not including, May 1.
Cash dividends will be paid on or
before June 10, and checks will be
mailed after Sept. 1.
Students leaving the University
may deposit their receipts at the
Bookstore with a self-addrcsscrl en
velope, and their dividends will be
mailed to them.
