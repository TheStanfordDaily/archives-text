# Excom Acts With PT&T
## 
### 
Excom followed through on the
Pacific Telephone and Telegraph
Company's letter of last month
threatening removal of all pay
phones on campus if phone tam
pering continued.
Excom, in conjunction with the
company, drafted a sign which
will be placed in all pay phones
in the near future. The sign reads
as follows:
"Any misuse of these telephone
facilities is a violation of the Fun
damental Standard and will make
these booths subject to immedi
ate removal by the telephone
company.
"(Signed) Exec. Com., ASSU"
