# Series Members To See 'Shadows'
## 
### 
Revival Series members will see
"Street of Shadows" and "Shadows
and Light Reflection" today at 4:15
and 7:30 p.m. in Cubberley Audito
rium.
Director G. W. Pabst and com
poser Arthur Honegger are teamed
with French theater and cinema
stars — Louis Jouvet, Viviane Ro
mance, and Pierre Fresnay —in
"Street of Shadows." The melo
drama is a story of First World War
espionage and counter-espionage in
the Balkans.
