# Literature In Asia Is Series Topic
## 
### 
Wallace Stegner, professor of
English and director of the Stan
ford Creative Writing Center,
will deliver a lecture entitled
"Literary Lessons out of Asia"
tomorrow night at 7:30 in Memo
rial Hall auditorium.
Thp address is a feature of the
regular Tuesday Evening Series,
sponsored by the Committee on
Public Exercises, and is open to the
public without charge.
Stegner has just returned from a
two - quarter trip to the Orient,
where he investigated contemporary
literary achievement-).
Hi;; recent books include a vol
ume of short stories, "Women on
the Wall." and a fictional biography
of .Toe Hill, noted labor organizer,
"The Preacher and the Slave."
