# Circus Time to Hit California Campus
## 
### 
Circus week will hit the Uni
versity of California campus Fri
day and Saturday when for the fust
time since 1940 the students will
stage the California Circus.
Ihc event, one of the highlights
of prewar student life, has been re
juvenated in all its past glory with
concessions, sideshows, an aqua
cade, an official queen, dances, and
general circus hullabaloo rounding
out a fun-packed weekend.
