# Union to Present Flapper Dance
## 
### 
Union Unit will present a '"Flap
per Twenties" party Saturday night
in Union Court, from 8:30 to 12:30.
A Charleston contest, fashion show,
old-time movies, dancing, and a
gambling casino will be featured.
The fashion show will be held in
the center of the Court from 9:30
to 10. "Any man or woman who has
a flapper costume or anything from
a raccoon coat to a bathing suit
should wear it," said Diane Gold
stone. chairman. A prize will be
given to the person wearing the best
costume.
The Phi Dclt-DU band will play
for a Charleston contest at 11. Dur
ing the evening Charleston lessons
will be given by Mariposa women.
