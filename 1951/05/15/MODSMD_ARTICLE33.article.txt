# 'Games' Subject Of TV Program
## 
### 
"Let's Explore the Theory of
Games" will be the title of Stan
ford's next television show at 1:30
p.m. today over Station KRON-TV
in San Francisco, one of the week
ly "Operation Education" presenta
tions in which Stanford, Cal, and
San Francisco and Alameda Coun
ty schools are co-operating.
The program will develop the
idea that business, economics, and
war are "games" in the eyes of
mathematicians and statisticians.
