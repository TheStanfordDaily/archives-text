# Engineering Aids
## 
### 
Civil Service examinations will
be given for physical science and
engineering aid positions in Wash
ington, D.C., and vicinity, at sala
ries ranging from $2,G50 to $3,825 a
year.
Qualifications for the positions
are experience, appropriate educa
tion, or both.
Further information and applica
tion forms may be secured at most
first- and second-class post offices
or from the U.S. Civil Service Com
mission, Washington 25, D.C.
