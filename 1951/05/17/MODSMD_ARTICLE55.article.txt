## 
###

second annual steak barbecue Sun
j day from 4:30 to 11 p.m. at 1247
I Cowper Street.

j dancing. Tickets are on sale in the
j living groups. They will also be
sold outside the Cellar today and
I tomorrow from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m.
