# National Officer To Speak Tuesday
## 
### 
Mrs. Barlett Heard, vice - presi
dent of the YWCA of America, will
speak to the Stanford YWCA
luncheon meeting next Tuesday
from 12 to 1 in the lower lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse. She will
discuss the national and interna
tional YWCA programs and tell
how they are training "Y" women
toward better citizenship.
