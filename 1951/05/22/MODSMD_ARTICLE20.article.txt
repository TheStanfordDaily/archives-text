# Symphony Will Perform Sunday
## 
### 
The Stanford Symphony Orchestra,
under the direction of Sandor Salgo,
will present a concert Sunday eve
ning in Memorial Auditorium at
8:15. The concert, which is open to
the public, will take the place of the
Sunday Evening Movies.
On the program will be Symphony
No. 1 in C Minor by Brahms, Over
ture to "Benvenuto Cellini" by Ber
lioz, Mother Goose Suite by Ravel,
and two arias from "Don Giovanni"
by Mozart. Two soloists ffom San
Francisco — Nancy Kronburg, so
prano, and Lawrence Mason, tenor—
will sing the two Mozart arias.
MiSs Kronburg, one of the finalists
in the Atwater Kent competition, will
sing "Non mi dir," and Mason, who
played the Devil in "The Devil and
Daniel Webster" and Smiley, the
owner, in the "Jumping Frog of Cala
veras County," will perform "II mio
tesoro."
