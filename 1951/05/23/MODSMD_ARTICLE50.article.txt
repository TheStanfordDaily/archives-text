# Polomen Beat Judson 7 to 1
## 
### 
The Stanford polo team tallied
seven goals to one by the Judson
School in a game played yesterday
at the Stanford polo field.
The Card team, consisting of
Duog Allen at No. 1, Alex Echa
varria at No. 2, Allan Scherer at
No. 3, and Karl Walter at No. 4,
rolled up six of the goals in the
final chukker. Judson scored its
sole goal in the first period.
Stanford will play Judson in a 1
return game tomorrow at 3 p.m.
