# Senior Directories On Sale at ASSU
## 
### 
Directories for the Class of 1951
are on sale at the ASSU oflice this
week for 50 cents.
The Senior Directory will contain
the names, home addresses, and de
grees of all members of the Class of
1951. The illustrated booklet will
also contain the Class Will, Poem,
Oration, and History.
