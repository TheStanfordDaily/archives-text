# Tomorrow's Gypsies
## 
### 

A FULL HOUSE Modeling hearts, clubs, and earrings as an
advertisement for the Thcta Chi Gypsy Dance Saturday night
arc (1 to r) Joyce Baltantine, Midge Price, Nancy Ashby, Joan
McLaughlin, and Jill Nisbet. A moonlight hayride will highlight
the dance which is open to the campus tomorrow night from
9 to 1. Festivities will include a gypsy fortune teller, free re
freshments, starlight dancing, and a cabaret. Gypsy costumes
will be the style for the date affair, and the recently remodeled
house will be decorated in appropriate Gypsy fashion.
