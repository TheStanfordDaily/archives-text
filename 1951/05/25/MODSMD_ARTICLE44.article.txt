# Meet Postponed
## 
### 
The Pacific Coast Conference
track meet, originally scheduled
for tomorrow in the Coliseum,
has been postponed until Tuesday
because of the draft test which is
being given tomorrow morning.
Stanford's 18 - man team will
participate In the trials Monday
and the finals Tuesday. Perennial
champion USC is favored again to
capture the meet, while Northern
Division champion Washington
State, Cal, and UCLA pose as
Stanford's chief rivals for second
place.
