# Graduation Usher Sign-ups Are Open
## 
### 
Moil and women students who
wish to usher at Raccalaureate and
Commencement June 17 may sign
up at the Public Exercises office.
Room ID, Building 1. until June 5.
Ushers will work from 9:30 a.m.
to noon for Baccalaureate and from
3:30 to 7 p.m. for Commencement.
Pay is $1 an hour.
Students who want tickets to
Commencement and Baccalaureate
mailed to them must turn in their
participation ticket order cards by
Friday. Those planning to pick them
up at the Public Exercises office
must do so by June 4.
