# GRASS FIRE PUT OUT
## 
### 

GRASS FIRE —The Fire Department, under Chief John Mar
ston's direction, put out a grass fire beneath the Knoll at 12:32
p.m. yesterday. The half-acre blaze was finally quenched with
300 gallons of water.
