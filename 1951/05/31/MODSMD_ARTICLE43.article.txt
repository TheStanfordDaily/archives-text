# Bridge Games May Be Held in Summer
## 
### 
The Stanford Duplicates, Farm
bridge club, will hold weekly bridge
games during summer quarter if
there is enough student interest.
Merrill Ely, spring quarter direc
tor of the club, is leaving Stanford.
Ely was one of the two-man Stan
ford team which played in the In
tercollegiate Bridge Tournament in
Chicago. He war the only freshman
to take part in the tournament.
