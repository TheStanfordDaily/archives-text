# PHI KAPS WIN VOLLEYBALL
## 
### 
Phi Kappa Sigma won the
University intra mural volleyball
championship last night on the
Union Court when they downed
the Phi Delts in two out of three
games. The whining scores for
the Phi Kaps were 15-fi, 12-15.
