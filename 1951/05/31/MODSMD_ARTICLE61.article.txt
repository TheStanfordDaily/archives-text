# Music Prizes Are Awarded
## 
### 
Cynthia Troxell and Wayne Na
deau have been awarded the School
of Humanities Prize in Music Com
position; they will divide the $100
prize.
Miss Troxell, an undergraduate,
composed a trio for oboe, clarinet,
and viola and Nadcau, a graduate
student, wrote two movements for
string quartet.
These works will be presented
Thursday, June 7, at 12:15 at the
Knoll at a meeting of the Stanford
Composers' Workshop.
