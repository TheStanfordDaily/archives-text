# ASK BOARDS ABOUT TESTS
## 
### 
Students desiring results of the
May 26 Selective Service College
Qualification Test must write
their local boards, Richard L.
Balch, Chief Counselor for Men,
said yesterday.
The Educational Testing Serv
ice, Princeton, N.J., will score the
tests and forward results to local
boards. Grading will be com
pleted about June 20, ETS esti
mates.

details.)
