# Final '51 Reunion Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
A farewell Senior Class Reunion
will be held in L'Ommie's back room
tomorrow night. The affair will get
under way at 8 p.m. and will con
tinue until closing time. Gene Allen,
chairman of the Event, urges all sen
iors, along with their friends and
members of the faculty to attend this
final get together before graduation.
