# Social Committee Has Summer Jobs
## 
### 
Interviews for positions on the
ASSU summer social committee
will be held today from 3 to 5 p.m.
in the Union office of Carol Pen
ny, vice-president. Any student is
eligible to try out.
The committee will plan an all-
University social program and put
on the events. The first event is the
Lag open house mixer Saturday.
