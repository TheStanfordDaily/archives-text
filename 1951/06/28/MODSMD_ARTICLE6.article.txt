# ASSU Finance Rules Stated
## 
### 
John Motheral, student manager,
has issued information on handling
funds through the ASSU.
Most organizations on campus
bank with the Students Organiza
tions Fund. Those who do must ob
serve the following rules:
1. A purchase order must be ob
tained at the ASSU office before
every purchase is made.
2. Payment and credit orders are
available at the ASSU office and
must be used for all payments and
credit establishments.
Motheral urges all group treas
urers and others who handle group
finances to familiarize themselves
with the rules immediately. Proce
dures for obtaining orders and oth
er information are available at the
ASSU office.
Motheral will be available to an
swer questions in the ASSU office
or his office behind that room on
MTTh 3:30-5:30 p.m., W 1-5:30 p.m.,
and F 1-3 p.m.
