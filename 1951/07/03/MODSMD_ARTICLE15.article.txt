# ORGAN RECITAL
## 
### 
Thursday at 4:15 p.m. Miss Lena
Mae Ashpaugh will be the organist
at the weekly organ recital in the
Stanford Memorial Church.
The Rev. R. M. Minto requests
that former members of the Church
Committee and those interested in
reading the Scriptures or in usher
ing at the Sunday church service
meet in the Vestry tonight at 7.
