# Stanford Bridge Club Invites New Members
## 
### 
Now students interested in play
ing bridge are encouraged to join
the Stanford Bridge Club, which
will meet tonight at 7:30 in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Students need not be experts to
join the club, a working knowledge
and interest in the game being suf
ficient, according to club member
George Flewelling.
