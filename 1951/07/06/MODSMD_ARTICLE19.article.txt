# Art Gallery Shows Palo Alto Display
## 
### 
Current exhibits at the Stanford
Art Gallery include work by the
Palo Alto Art Club. Members whose
paintings are being shown range
from 10-year-olds in the club's art
classs, through doctors and lawyers
who paint as a hobby, up to na
tionally known portrait artists.
The Camera Club and the book
binding and flower arrangement
groups are also exhibiting in the
show, which will continue at the
Gallery through July 15.
The Art Club, now in its 30th
year, has about 100 adult members.
Mrs. Herbert Hoover was its origi
nal president.
