# GIFTS HONOR MILLER
## 
### 
Six gifts honoring the memory of
Fabian Miller, former city engineer
of Palo Alto, were recently made to
Stanford. The gifts will be used to
purchase library books on civil Engi
neering. Miller passed away in Seat
tle May 2 of this year.
