# ASSU to Sponsor Sports Day at Pool
## 
### 
The ASSU is sponsoring an all
campus box lunch and sports day
Saturday at the Women's Pool. The
pool will be open to all students
and faculty from 2 to 6 p.m.
There will be competition in
swimming, softball, and volleyball,
beginning at 2.
Encina and Lagunita dining halls
will be closed for the evening meal
Saturday, and those eating there
will be furnished a box lunch at the
picnic. Dinner will begin at 5.
Students who do not eat at En
cina or Lagunita must bring their
own lunches. The ASSU will sup
ply Cokes.
Co-chairmen for the event are
Jackie Miller and Bob Sampson.
