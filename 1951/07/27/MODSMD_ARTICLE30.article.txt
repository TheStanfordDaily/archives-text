# Dr. Fisher On Town Meeting
## 
### 
Dr. Harold H. Fisher, professor of
history and chairman of the Hoover
Institute and Library, wil lact as one
of George Denny's assistant modera
tors in America's Town Meeting of
the Air Monday, July 30, at 8 p.m. in
the San Francisco Civic Auditorium.
A Korean, an Indian, and an
American will discuss "Can Asia Be
Saved from Communist Imperial
ism?" Speakers will include Dr.
George Paik, Minister of Education,
Republic of Korea; Rammanohar Lo
hia, peasant leader and foreign policy
chairman of the Socialist party of
India; and Harlan Cleveland, deputy
assistant for programs, ECA.
Free tickets to the broadcast will
be available for Stanford students
and staff at the reception office of the
Hoover Library Saturday morning
from 9 to 12 and Monday from [) a.m.
to 5 p.m. Foreign students have been
invited especially to attend the Town
Meeting program.
