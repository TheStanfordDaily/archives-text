# EX-INDIANS
## Bryan and Held Win in England 
### 
Ex-Indian trackmen Gay Bryan
and Bud Held led an American
track and field team to victory over
a British all-star squad Wednesday
night at Liverpool, England. Bryan,
former sprint and basketball star at
Stanford, won the broad jump with
a leap of 23 ft. 11 n > in.
Held, holder of several javelin
throwing records, tossed the spear
191 It. 11 in. to take first in that
event. He also took third place in
the pole vault when he cleared the
bar at 12 ft. 6 in.
The meet was scored on the basis
of events won, with the Americans
coming out on top, eight to six.
