# Draft of 300,000 Expected in Year
## 
### 
" WASHINGTON, Aug. 1G (UP) —
The government expects to draft
300.000 men during the present fis
cal year —an average of 25.000 a
month—and may call low-ranking
college students in mid-term if they
have been deferred once, it was dis
closed today.
Some 550,000 men were called up
by their local boards between last
September and June 30, the end of
the last firscal year. The Defense
Department already has asked Se
lective Service for 35,000 men in
August, 34,000 in September, and
41,000 in October.
