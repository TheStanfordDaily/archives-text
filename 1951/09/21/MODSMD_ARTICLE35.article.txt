# UCLA
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Sept. 20 (UP)-
The UCLA Bruins went through 15
minutes of signal drills today and
then departed for a hotel to remain
in seclusion until tomorrow night's
game here against Texas A. and M.
The Aggies arrived early in the

afternoon from College Station,
Texas, and worked out during the
afternoon in Memorial Coliseum be
fore going to a Pasadena hotel where
they will remain until gametime.
