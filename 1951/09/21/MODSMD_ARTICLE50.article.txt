# Reg Dance Is Slated Tuesday
## 
### 
The annual, but newly styled,
tail reg dance will be presented
next Tuesday evening in the Wom
en's Gym.
Swimming will be featured from
7 to 9:30 at the "Co-Rec KickofT."
Dancing will be to the music of
Jimmy Blass's orchestra in the dec
orated main gym. There will be
square dancing in the dance studio
for beginners and professionals
alike.
Besides dancing, there will be op
portunity for card games, ping pong,
and numerous groups of tables with
chairs for those who wish to sit and
enjoy the refreshments.
A limited number of tickets will
be sold Monday and Tuesday in
front of the Pavilion at $2 per
couple.
