# Players Announce Four Productions
## 
### 
The Stanford Players will pre
sent four productions for the year
1951-52. They will be "The Mer
chant of Venice," directed by Dr. A.
Nicholas Vardac, on Nov. 8, 9, 10;
Eugene O'Neill's "Desire Under the
Elms," also directed by Dr. Vardac,
on March 6, 7, 8; a new script di
rected by F. Cowlcs Strickland, on
May 1, 2, 3; and an opera, "The
Love for Three Oranges," staged by
F. Cowles Strickland and directed
by Sandor Salgo, to close the sea
son on May 29, .10, 31.
Anyone interested in obtaining a
season ticket for tiie four plays for
$3 should communicate with the
Stanford Players, Stanford Univer
sity.
