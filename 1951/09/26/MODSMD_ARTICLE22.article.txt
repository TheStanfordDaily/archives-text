# Fall Art Season Opens at Gallery
## 
### 
An exhibition of oils and tempera
paintings by Stanford Professor Vic
tor Arnautoff will open the fall season
of art shows this week at the Stanford
Art Gallery.
One of the best known artists of the
Bay Region, Professor ArnautofT first
attracted popular notice with his
murals in San Francisco's Coit Tower
on Telegraph Hill and George Wash
ington High School.
His murals arc also to be seen in
the South San Francisco Post Office
and the post offices in Richmond and
Pacific Grove. He is a former student
of Diego Rivera and attended the
California School of P'ine Arts .
