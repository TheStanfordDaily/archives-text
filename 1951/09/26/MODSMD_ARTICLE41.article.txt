# AWS Hosts House Leaders
## 
### 
The second annual AWS Leader
ship Conference was held on cam
pus last Friday and Saturday to ac
quaint officers of women's resi
dences with their jobs and to give
them a chance to discuss common
problems.
A banquet at Union dining hall
Friday night opened the conference.
Speakers were Margaret Gairdner,
AWS president, who spoke on
"Aims of AWS"; Richard L. Balch,
chief counselor for men, "Looking
at the Women of Stanford Through
Men's Eyes"; and H. Donald Win
bigler, dean of students, "The
Woman and Her Government."
Miss Elva F. Brown, chief coun
selor for women, spoke to the morn
ing session on "Your Unrealized Op
portunities as a Student Leader."
