# Girls' Gym Pool Opens Tomorrow
## 
###

ming pool will open tomorrow.
Hours tor women students only will
be Mondays, 3-6; Tuesdays, 5-6;
Wednesdays. 4-6; Thursdays, 5-6;
und Fridays, 4-5.
Sundays the poo] will be open
for women students, faculty and
their guests. Men students may
swim only as guests of women stu
dents.
Noon swimming for University
employees will begin on Monday,
Oct. 1.
