# Students Swamp Co-Rec Kickoff
## 
### 
Approximately 100 couples were
turned away from the Co-Rec Kick
off, which drew over 1,000 persons
to the Women's Gym last night.
Co-Rec made $550 profit from the
affair, which cost them $550 to pre
sent. Tickets sold totaled 546.
Though swimmers reported the
pool was a little cold, some 40 per
sons took part in the aquatic part
of the Kickoff.
