TRACK CANDIDATES for fresh
man and varsity squads will meet
in Room 41 of the BAC tomorrow
at 4:15 p.m. Freshmen and soph
omores are also needed for man
agers.
PHI LAMBDA UPSILON. national
honorary chemistry fraternity, is
sponsoring a first - aid seminar
this afternoon at 4:15 in Room 20
of the Chemistry Building. Dr.
Clark of the Stanford Student
Health Service will speak.
ALPHA CHI SIGMA will hold a
meeting today in Room 3 of the
Chemistry Building at 3:45 p.m.
Promptness is requested and
members not present will be sub
ject to penalties.
NEWMAN CLUB will hold an open
house today at 8 p.m. at 1247
Cowper. Cars will leave Branner,
Roble, Lagunita, Union, and
Hurlburt at 7:45. Mass will be at
8:30 and 11 a.m. on Sunday.

CHRISTIAN SCIENCE ORGANI
ZATION will meet today at 7:15
p.m. in the Faculty Men's Club-
House. Be at the Post Office at
7 for rides. There will be a busi
ness meeting for all members fol
lowing the regular meeting.
VWCA CABINET interviews will
be held this afternoon from 3:30
to 5:30 in the Women's Clubhouse.
All women are eligible.
DAILY needs students to work on
the business side. See Connie
Libbey at the shack this after
noon.
KZSU announces tryouts will be
held in Room 204 of the Memorial
Auditorium today from 2 to 5
p.nj. end tomorrow from 3 to 6
p.m.
STANFORD SADIE tryouts will be
held in Room 204 of the Memorial
Auditorium at 5 p.m. today and
tomorrow.
