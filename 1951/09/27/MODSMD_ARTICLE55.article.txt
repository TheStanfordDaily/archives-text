# L.A. TIMES GOES TO 10c
## 
### 
LOS ANGELES, Sept. 26 (UP) —
The Los Angeles Times announced
tonight in editions dated Thursday
that single copies of the weekday
Times will cost 10 cents per copy,
effective Oct. 1.
