# Radio Engineers Honor Farm Scientists
## 
### 
Two Stanford scientists have been
honored by election as Fellows of
the Institute of Radio Engineers.
Dr. Lester M. Field of Palo Alto,
professor of electrical engineering,
and Russell H. Varian of Menlo
Park, research associate at the Uni
versity's Microwave Laboratory,
are the men honored. They were
among 45 leading radio engineers
and scientists voted recently to the

highest membership grade of the in
stitute as recognition of outstand
ing contributions to radio engineer
ing or allied fields.
Presentation of the Fellow
awards with citations will be made
by the president of the institute at
its annual banquet next March 5 at
the Waldorf-Astoria Hotel in New
York. This will be a featui-e of the
IRE national convention.
