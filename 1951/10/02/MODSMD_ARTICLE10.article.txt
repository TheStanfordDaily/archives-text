# Rally Com Will Select Frosh
## 
### 
Today and tomorrow are the last
days for freshmen to be interviewed
for the Freshman Committee on
Rally Com. The interviews are be
in:* held in the ASSU president's
office in the Student Union.
Twenty-four freshmen, both boys
and girls, will be chosen on the
basis of originality, dependability,
ideas, and willingness to work. The
winners will be announced in The
Daily on Thursday.
The Freshman Committee will be
in charge of the Frosh Pajamarino
and also will work hand in hand
with Rally Com in planning and
running rallies and card stunts.
