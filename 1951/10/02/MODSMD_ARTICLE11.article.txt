# Friday Last Day For Board Bills
## 
### 
Payment of room and board bills
for autumn quarter is due this Fri
day, Oct. 5.
Bills not paid before the deadline
will be subject to a late-payment
fee.
The cashier's office in Building 1, j
Inner Quad, will be open every
day through Friday from 8 a.m. to
5 p.m. Students living at Stanford
Village may pay their room and
board bills at the Village cashier's
office from 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m.
and from 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
