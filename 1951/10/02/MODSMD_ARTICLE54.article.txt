# Distaff Club Will Open Fall Program
## 
### 
The Stanford Distaff Club will
open its fall program with an Au
tumn Roundup at 8 tomorrow eve
ning in the Women's Clubhouse.
Program numbers will include an
authentic square dance by the Stan
ford Hoedowncrs and a group of
Western songs to be sung by Lois
and Betty Bean of the University
Press staff. Mistress of ceremonies
will be Willa Woodard of the Cor
poration Yard.
The Distaff Club, organized last
spring, is open to all women office
workers, research assistants, and li
brarians.
