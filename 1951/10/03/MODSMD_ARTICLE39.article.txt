# Concert Tickets Still Sell at Nitery
## 
### 
Season tickets for the Concert
Series are still on sale in the Con
ceit Series office in the Nitery from
1 to 5 p.m., and single tickets for
the Oct. 17 concert featuring Sal
vatore Baccaloni, bass, will go on
sale Friday.
The other concerts will feature
Elena Nikolaidi, contralto, Jan. 21;
Rudolf Firkusny, pianist, Feb. 25;
the San Francisco Ballet Company,
April 20; and Joseph Szigeti, vio
linist, May 19.
Student prices are $8.50, $7.10,
$5.70, and $4.30.
