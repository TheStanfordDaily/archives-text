# Tryouts for Play Today at Mem Aud
## 
### 
Tryouts for "The Merch-nt of
Venice" will be held today on the
main stage in Memorial Hall from
3 to 5 p.m. and from 7 to 9:30 p.m.
All Stanford students arc invited to
read in the tryouts.
"The Merchant of Venice" will be
presented to the public on Nov. 8,
9. and 10 by the Stanford Players
as their first major production of
the 1951-52 season.
Dr. A. Nicholas Vardac of the
Department of Speech and Drama
will direct. Mr, Clarence Dcrwcnt,
president of Actors' Equity, has
been engaged to play the role of
Shylock in the production.
