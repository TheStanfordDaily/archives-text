# Room and Board Bills Due Friday
## 
### 
Friday is the last, day to pay
room and board bills.
Bills not paid by the deadline will
be subject to a late-payment fee,
University Controller Duncan Mc-
Fadden announced.
Payment on campus is made at
the cashier's office on Inner Quad.
Students living in Stanford Village
may pay their room and board bills
at the Village cashier's office.
