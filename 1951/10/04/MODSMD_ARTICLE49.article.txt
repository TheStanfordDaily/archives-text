# Nanney Gives Bach Recital in Chapel
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney will play an
organ recital of J. S. Bach today at
4:15 p.m. in Memorial Church. The
program includes:
Prelude and Fugue in F Minor
Three Chorale Preludes
Fugue in G Major
Adagio from Sonata No. 1, in E
Flat Major
Sinfonia to "We Thank Thee,
God," arranged by Grace
