# Time Limit Set For Paying Fines
## 
### 
Fines for traffic citations must be
paid within three days, warned
Gordon Davis, campus police chief,
yesterday as he stated that a num
ber of students have failed to pay
their fines.
A late - payment fee is charged
against those who fail to appear
within the three-day limit, Davis
said.
Davis reported that a number of
autos have been tagged by police
since the opening of the quarter for
parking in restricted areas.
