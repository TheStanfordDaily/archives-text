# Women to Try Out For Crew Helpers
## 
### 
!
Try outs for women interested in
working on the Crew Auxiliary will
be held next week.
The auxiliary acts as a secretarial
staff for the crew. Their work in
cludes keeping the crew scrap-book,
helping repair crew equipment,
ushering and selling food at the
crew meets, and publicizing crew
activities.
Time and place of the try out
will be announced soon in The
Daily.
