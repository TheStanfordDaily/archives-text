# Frosh Play Day Slated Tomorrow
## 
### 
Freshman women will gather to
morrow from 1:15 to 3 p.m. for the
annual Frosh Play Day which will
bo given in the women's gymna
sium by the Women's Athletic As
sociation.
Demonstrations of swimming,
tennis, and modern dance will be
followed by participation of the
freshman women in swimming, ten
nis, and volleyball. Refreshments
will be served after the games.
Sue Ranley, chairman, empha
sizes that the purpose of the Play
Day is to "acquaint frosh women
with WAA and the various sports
clubs."
