# Placement Office Offers Positions
## 
### 
Openings for men experienced in
radio and TV repairing, drafting,
and odd jobs such as carpentry and
plumbing have been announced by
the Student Employment Office.
For this weekend there are gar
dening jobs open.
Veterans who live in the Oak
land area may apply for jobs in the
Oakland post office over the Christ
mas holidays. The forms for this
job may be obtained at the Student
Employment Office. Christmas jobs
in other post offices will not be
available for two or three weeks.
