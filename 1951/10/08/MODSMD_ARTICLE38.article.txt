# Mural Play Opens Fire
## 
### 
Intramural competition opens this
afternoon with a full schedule of 14
football games on tap. Tilts will be
played at 4 and 5 each afternoon.
The defending champion Firehouse
five plus two will roll into action
against the best the Alpha Sigs
have to offer.
Each day's scheduled games will
appear on the sports page. Compe
tition will begin in all fall quarter
sports — volleyball, table tennis,
horseshoes, handball, and bowling^—
very shortly, announced Mural Di
rector Elwyn Bugge.
