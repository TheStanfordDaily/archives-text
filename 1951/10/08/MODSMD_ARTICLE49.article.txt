# Crew Auxiliary To Hold Tryouts
## 
### 
The Stanford Crew Auxiliary will
soon hold tryouts for girls inter
ested in doing secretarial and other
volunteer work for the crew.
Keeping the crew scrapbook,
sending newsletters, passing out
programs at races, and publicizing
events are a few of the many jobs
which the Auxiliary undertakes.
Meetings are held every Saturday
from 0:'$0 to 11:30 a.m. The Daily
will announce time and place of
tryouts.
