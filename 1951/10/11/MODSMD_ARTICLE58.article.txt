# TROOPSHIP FROM KOREA
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, Oct. 10 (UP)
—A troopship with 1,334 Army ro
tation and reserve personnel from
Korea aboard is due to arrive here
Friday, it was announced today.
Under the provisions of recently
enacted security regulations the
names of all military transport ves
sels in movement arc withheld until
the ships arrive in port.
