# VACCINATION
##

the Registrar's office Oct. 23 for all
new students. Failure to file cer
tificates by 5 p.m. on that day will
result in a fine of $2: or if not filed
by Oct. 30. registration in the Uni
versity will be subject to cancella
tion by the Committee on Public
Health.
