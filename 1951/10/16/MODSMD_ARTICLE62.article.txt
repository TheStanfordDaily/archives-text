# Crew Auxiliary Tryouts Slated This Afternoon
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Women's Crew
Auxiliary will be held today from
4 to 5:30 p.m. in the lower lounge of
the Women's Clubhouse.
The Auxiliary helps out the crew,
which is unsupported by the Uni
versity. They act as a secretarial
stafT, send out newsletters, publicize
crew activities and help repair crew
equipment. This year they are plan
ning to set up a new bookkeeping
system, and a file for honorary mem
bers. Regular meetings are held
Saturdays from 9:30 to 11:30 at the
French House.
