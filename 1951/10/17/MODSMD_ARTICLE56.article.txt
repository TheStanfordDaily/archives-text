# On the Korean Front
## 
### 

ALLIES SWEEP AHEAD—Five tank-led United Nations divi
sions swept ahead on the east-central front, with Kumsong, the
main Communist supply depot, as the objective. Gains of up
to eight miles have been reported in the three day offensive.
On the western front (1), northwest of Yonchon, Allied troops
beat back two furious Red counter-attacks. South Korean troops
opened an offensive on the east coast (2) with Kosong, 100 miles
north of the 38th parallel, as the objective. —ACME Telephoto
