# Row Becomes Midway
## 
### 

ANCHORS AWAY—Row cuties Nancy Howe, Wendy Fish, and
Carolyn Hornkold, top to bottom, are getting into the nautical
mood for the Women's Row "Sea-Going Carnival." The all
campus affair, sponsored by the nine women's living groups
on the Row, will start tomorrow night after the Santa Clara
Rally. —Daily Photo by Dick Schwarz
