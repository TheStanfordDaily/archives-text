# Nanney Will Play In Church Recital
## 
### 
An organ recital by Herbert B.
Nanney will be given at 4:15 this
afternoon in Memorial Church. The
program will be made up of 17th
and early 18th-century music.
Today's program will include
"Toccata" by George Muffat, "Ca
priccio" by Jan Sweelinck, "Toc
cata" and "Pastorale" by Johann
Pachrlbel, variations on "Fortuna
My Foe' by Samuel Scheidt, "We
Pray Now to the Holy Spirit" by
Dietrich Buxtehude, and "Con
certo No. 4, in C Major" by J. S.
Bach.
