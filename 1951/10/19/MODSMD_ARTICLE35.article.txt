# Stanford Series Begins on KIBE
## 
### 
Radio station KIBE in Palo Alto
will begin a series of special Stan
ford radio programs tomorrow
morning at 11:45. Curt Hungerford,
Farm speech and drama major, will
be the emcee of the show, which
will run 15 minutes.
The program will consist of news,
announcements of coming events,
musical entertainment, and pos
sibly interviews. The show is de
signed to make Peninsula residents
more aware of Stanford.
