# Institute Works On Algae Growth
## 
### 
The Carnegie Institute of Wash
ington has announced that it is con
tinuing a project at Cambridge,
Mass., for the development of a
commercial process for the culture
of rapidly growing algae, for which
the Department of Plant Biology at
Stanford has been carrying on re
search.
Tiie process may eventually be
come a useful food source.
Headed by Dr. Charles France,
the department at Stanford expects
to continue studying the environ
mental factors that affect the growth
rate of the algae.
