# *Y' Will Honor New Director at Tea
## 
### 
The YWCA Advisory Board will
give a dressy tea in honor of Bjorg
Hanson, new "Y" director, this af
ternoon from 3:30 to 5:30 in the
Women's Clubhouse. Invitations
have been issued to all women's
residence presidents, members of
AWS Cabinet, and members of the
YW Cabinet, but the affair is open
to all YW members who are inter
ested.
Because of the tea, the weekly
coffee hour will not be held this
week but will be resumed next
Tuesday.
