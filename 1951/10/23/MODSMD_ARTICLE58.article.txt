# Campus Businesses Must Have Permit
## 
### 
A representative of a company or
a private person who wishes to con
duct business on campus is required
to petition the Student - Faculty
Committee for a business permit.
Petitions may be obtained from
the Student Employment Office and
should be turned in hv Mondav in
order to be considered at the meet
ing on Tuesday, Oct. 30.
The committee is composed of
four students and four faculty
members and its purpose is to reg
ulate all commercial activities on
campus.
