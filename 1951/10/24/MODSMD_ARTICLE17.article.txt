# Western Books Now on Display
## 
### 
The main library is now display
ing the tenth exhibition of Western
Books in the upstairs lobby.
Thsi display features the best ex
amples of binding, type, and pub
lishing produced during 1950 by
printers from Seattle to San Diego.
This year's exhibition is larger
than last year's, containing 37
books. Two of them, "Our Desert
Neighbors," by Edmund C. Jaeger,
and "Your Rugged Constitution," by
Bruce and Esther Findlay, are from
the Stanford University Press.
