# Vets' Credit Cards Good Till Saturday
## 
### 
Saturday is the last day for use
of autumn quarter veterans' credit
cards for the purchase of books and
supplies at the Stanford Bookstore,
Walter J. Findeisert of the Stanford
Veterans' Records and Guidance
Center announced yesterday.
