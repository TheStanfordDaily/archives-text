# Organ Music to Be Presented Today
## 
### 
A program of Renaissance and
modern music will be presented in
Memorial Church today at 4:15 p.m.
by Herbert B. Nanney, organist.
Selections played will include
Ricercare on the First Tone by Ca
vazzoni; "Die Nobis, Maria" by
Cabezon; Fantasia on the Sixth
lone by Gabrieli; "Veni Creator"
by Titelouze; "Majesty of Christ
Praying That His Father Should
Glorify Him," from "L'Ascension"
by Messiaen; Pastorale by Milhaud;
and Fanfare by Thomson.
