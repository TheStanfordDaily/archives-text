# Three Farm Coeds Will Show Horses
## 
### 
Stanford will be represented by
three coeds at the Grand National
Horse Show which starts Friday at
the Cow Palace.
Vivian Mann has entered her
black quarter horse mare in the
lightweight stock horse competition
and in Western equitation classes.
Rae Dean Spaulding will be driv
ing in several fine harness classes
with her six-year-old bay mare.
Anne Giannini will be riding her
sister's saddlebred and her own
five - gaited in the saddlebred
classes.
