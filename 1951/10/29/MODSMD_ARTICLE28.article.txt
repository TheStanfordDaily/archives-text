# Once Around the Quad
## 
### 
LAGUNITA Quad picture proofs
must be returned today to the
photographer in the Lagunita
lobby, between the hours of 5:30
and 7:30 p.m.
GAIETIES construction crew will
work every afternoon this week,
and every night except Friday.
Party Wednesday night.
JOURNAL CLUB will meet this
evening at 7:30 in Room 320,
Geology Building. Dr. Orlo E.
Childs of the Phillips Petroleum
Company will speak on "Geology
of the Uinta Basin."
SWIM CLUB meeting will be held
touight at 7:30 in the lounge of
the Women';* Gym. Swimming
after a short business meeting.
ELECTRONICS GRADUATES with
Bachelor of Science degrees from
the December. March, and June
classes will be interviewed Friday
by L. A. Beer of International
Business Machines for positions
in the East. For a group meeting
of tryoutees at 3:30 p.m. Thurs
day, sign up at the Placement
Service, second floor, Building 1.
OFF-CAMPUS WOMEN'S EXCOM
will meet today at 3:15 p.m. in
the Women's Clubhouse.
OFF-CAMPUS FRESIIMAN WOM
EN will meet today at noon in
the Women's Clubhouse.
WOMEN'S ROW EXCOM will meet
today at 4:55 p.m. at Hurlburt.
DANCE CLUB will teach the jit
terbug at the Women's Clubhouse
tonight at 7:30. Everyone is in
vited.
OFF-CAMPUS FROSII will meet
tonight in the Nitery at 7:30.
STL DENTS INTERESTED in form
ing a group concerned with the
problems of peace will meet this
evening at 7:45 in Room 53 of
Cubberley.
I CLUB Planning Committee will
meet today at 3:15 p.m. in the
11R shack.
NAACP will meet at 4:15 p.m. today
in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse. All interested
people are urged to attend.
