# Library Displays Book Examples
## 
### 
Thirty-seven examples of books
published by Western presses, as
selected by the Bounce and Coffin
Club, arc on display in the main li
brary until Friday.
Among the hooks selected for this
year's display are two published by
the Stanford University Press,
'Your Rugged Constitution" by
Bruce and Esther Findlay, and "Our
Desert Neighbors" by Edmund C.
Jaeger.
This is the largest group of books
that has been displayed in the club's
10 years of selection. The books
present some excellent examples of
this year's outstanding printing and
quality of .subject matter.
