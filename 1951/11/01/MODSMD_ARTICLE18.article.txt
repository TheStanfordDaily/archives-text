# 'Broken Blossoms' Is Revival Flick
## 
### 
"Broken Blossoms," a 1918 tear
jerker starring Donald Crisp, Lil
lian Gish, and Richard Barthelmess,
will be tonight's revival movie. This
was one of the earliest full-length
pictures and the first, to experiment
in half - tone photography. Only
season-ticket holders will be admit
ted. The show starts in Cubberley
Auditorium at 7:30.
