# Topic Announced For Essay Prize
## 
### 
"Whose Side Is India On?" will
be the subject for this year's Mel
ville Jacoby Essay Prize. Fifty dol
lars will be awarded to the Stan
ford student who writes the best
essay on this topic.
Closing date for the contest is
March 7, 1952. Essays must be 5,000
to 10,000 words, typewritten, one
copy. Footnotes are not necessary,
but a complete list of references
should be appended. Quotations
should be used sparingly.
Essays may be submitted to Clif
ford F. Weigle, Institute for Jour
nalistic Studies. Judges will be se
lected after March 9, and the award
will be announced at or before the
annual commencement convoca
tion.
