# Military Scientist To Attend ROTC Artillery School
## 
### 
Col. J. V. Collier, professor of mil
itary science and tactics at Stan
ford, left Wednesday for the artil
lery school at Fort Sill, Oklahoma,
where he will meet with officers
from ROTC artillery units through
out the country.
During a five - day conference,
which is the first of its type to be
held, the representatives will discuss
recent progress and accomplish
ments and will exchange ideas for
improvement of the college ROTC
artillery program.
After the discussions close. Col.
Collier will take a two-week leave
to visit his new granddaughter, born
recently at Fort Smith. Arkansas,
and will return to Stanford Nov. 21.
