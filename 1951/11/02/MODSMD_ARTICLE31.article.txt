# MURAL ROUNDUP
## ATO and SAE Play Tie Game 
### 
ATO I and SAE I buttled to a
scoreless tie yesterday, although
both teams had numerous scoring
opportunities.
The game put the accent on the
airways, as passers Bob Sanguinetti
and Gay Bryan of SAE matched
their arms against that of ATO's
Clayton Neal. Bryan also played
the receiver for some of Sanguinet
t is passes.
Bryan and Ray Schwartz of ATO
also fought a two-man battle for
the punting honors.
