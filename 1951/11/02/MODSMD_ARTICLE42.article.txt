# Sunday Movies Feature New Hit
## 
### 
"The Second Woman," a psycho
logical thriller starring Betsy
Drake, will be featured at this
week's Sunday Night Movie. "The
Second Woman" toured the local
cinema houses just last month and is
the first of a series of new movies
scheduled for the remaining Sun
day nights in the quarter.
In addition, the next installment
of the serial, "The James Brothers
of Missouri," and a Charley Dog
cartoon wil be shown. The show
will start at 7:30.
