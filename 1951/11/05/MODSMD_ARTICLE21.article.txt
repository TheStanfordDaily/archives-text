# PEO Sisterhood Slates Fair Friday
## 
### 
The JPr chapters of the PEG Sister
hood will hold their annual Chapter
House Fair Friday afternoon at
Women's Clubhouse.
The fair will feature the sale of
handicrafts of all kinds, including
jewelry, Christmas decorations, and
baked goods.
PEO's and non-members alike are
invited to attend the affair and may
make tea reservations with Leslie
Robinson, DA 2-G278.
