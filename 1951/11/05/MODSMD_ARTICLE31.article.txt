GAIETIES CONSTRUCTION CREW
will work Monday through Fri
day from 2 to 5:30 p.m. and next
Sunday from 1 to 5. There will
be no work at night this week.
GAIETIES MAKEUP CREW aspi
rants will meet Tuesday at 5 in
the Ram's Head room, or contact
Jean Leonard at Union before the
meeting.
HIGH SCHOOL DAY GUIDE IN
TERVIEWS will be today from
3:30 to 5 p.m. in the Cardinals
office and from 1 to 5 p.m. in the
Excom office tomorrow for inter
ested ' students.

I CLUB PLANNING COMMITTEE
will meet at 3:15 p.m. today at
the IIR shack.
IIR EX BOARD will meet today at
4:15 p.m. in the ASSU Excom
room.
INTERFAITH COUNCIL will meet
at 7:15 in the vestry of Memorial
Church. Religious group presi
dents are asked to be present at
this first meeting.
PEACE PROBLEMS IORUM will
meet at 7:45 tonight in Room 61
of Cubberley.
, ROW EXCOM will meet today at
4:55 p.m. at Stillman.

dan, Guthrie, and Roth women

row at Guthrie. Women are to
sign up on the appointment sheets
in each house and report to Gu
thrie for the picture. Wear a
white blouse and bring $1.25.
SWIM CLUB meeting tonight at
7:30 at the women's pool.
JOURNAL CLUB will have a tea
at 3:45 p.m. and a program to
day at 4. Bob Weimar, graduate
tudent, will lecture on "Geology
of the Navajo Country, Utah and
Arizona," in Room 320 of the
Geology Building.
