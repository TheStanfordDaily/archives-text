# REBATES NOW AT BOOKSTORE
## 
### 
Come and pet it!
Additional seven and a half per
cent rebate payments will be
made by the Stanford Bookstore
beginning this morning at 9.
The business office in the rear
of the store will be open from 9
to 11 in the morning and 2 to 4
in the afternoon to disburse the
surplus dividend payments.
The rebates, bringing the total
for last year's purchases to 22 Vs
per cent, will be paid by the busi
ness office until all of the money
has been claimed.
