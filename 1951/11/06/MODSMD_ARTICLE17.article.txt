# Highway Project To Cost 5 Million
## 
### 
A five and a half million dollar
face liftins is scheduled for Bay
shore Highway during the coming
year, it was announced by state
highway authorities last week.
The multimillion-dollar improve
ment is slated to center along the
road stretch from San Mateo to San
Francisco.
Among the projects planned is
the extension of the freeway to Six
teenth Avenue in San Mateo as well
as "grading, paving, and building
structures" the length of the road
way from San Francisco to Santa
Clara Countv.
