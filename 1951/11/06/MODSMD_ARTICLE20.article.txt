# Bridge Club Announces Last Week's Winners
## 
###

Guthrie were north and south win
ners in the 12-table Stanford Bridge
Club competition last Tuesday.
Jerry Smith and G. Towgaf took top
honors east to west.

day at 7:30 p.m. in the main lounge
of the Women's Clubhouse.
