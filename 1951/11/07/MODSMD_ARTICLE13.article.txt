# JAN HICKS

Through close association with
members of Women's Council, I
have come to realize the scope and
importance of their work and would
like to enter actively into it. It is
of great importance to you to choose
wisely a girl who will represent you
in the work of interpreting the
Honor Code and Fundamental
Standard so that there will be fewer
misunderstandings and violations of
them.
