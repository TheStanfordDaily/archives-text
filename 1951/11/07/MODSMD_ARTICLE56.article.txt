# Miss Elva Brown To Speak in L.A.
## 
### 
Miss Elva Fay Brown, Chief
Counselor for Women at Stanford,
will be the speaker at a meeting of
the Stanford Mothers' Club of
Southern California Nov. 19.
The meeting and tea, scheduled
for 2 p.m., will be held in the audi
torium of the Southern California
Gas Company, 810 South Flower
Street. Los Angeles.
