# Farm Bridge Club Reports Winners
## 
### 
Winners at the last Tuesday night
meeting of the Stanford Bridge
Club were announced yesterday as
16 card enthusiasts were cited for
their play.
North and south winners were:
first place — Ivy Oegscher, Dean
Cook; second placo—Jane Blanton,
Knthy Thorpe; third place—Francis
Bishop, Director John White.
East and west winners were: first
placo—G. Crowley, J. Stein; tied for
second—H. Jones, H. Gates, and A.
Fenerin, A. Glanville; tied for third
—Tom Warren. C. T. Chen, and
May Huggins, Jerry Harrison.
