# New Additions To Be Dedicated
## 
### 

SATURDAY DEDICATION SITE is the completed Crothers Hall, home of grad students of law.
Ceremonies to mark the hall's two new wings will be held Saturday morning with Judge George
Crothers, '95. the hall's donor, as special guest of honor. An open house for the entire campus
to inspect the new guarters will follow the Oregon State game. A combo will provide music for
dancing and refreshments will be served.
