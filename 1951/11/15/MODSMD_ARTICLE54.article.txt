# Cooking Talk
## 
### 
"All This and Turkey, Too" will
bo the title of Mrs. Mildred Cow
ger's talk to the Homo Arts section
of Stanford Dames tonight.
Mrs. Cowger's talk will feature
some interesting discussion on the
Adele Davis book, "Lot's Cook It
Right."
The meeting will begin at 8 p.m. |
in the lower lounge of the Wom
en's Clubhouse on the campus. I

Wives of Stanford students and
married women registered as stu
dent > are invited to attend.
