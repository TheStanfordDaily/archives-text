# Stewart to Talk On Mexican Art
## 
### 
Virginia Stewart, author of the new
book "45 Contemporary Mcxican
Artists," will appear on the next His
panic - American Series program in
Cubbcrley at 7:30 tonight.
Miss Stewart will discuss Mcxican
art and illustrate her lecture with
selected color slides. The program
will be open to the public without
charge.
Her recent book, published this fall
by the University Press, presents
Mexico's art renaissance through the
words and works of the artists.
