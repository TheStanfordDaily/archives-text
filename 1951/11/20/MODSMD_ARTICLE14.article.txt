# Holiday Library Hours Announced
## 
### 
Open hours for the Main Library
and the Western Civilization Li
brary have been announced for the
Thanksgiving recess.
Both libraries will close at 6 p.m.
tomorrow and will not be open
at all Thanksgiving Day. Friday
they will be open from 8 a.m. to
5 p.m.
The Main Library will be open
from 8 a.m. to 12 Saturday, and it
will observe its regular hours from
1 to 5 p.m. Sunday. The Reserve
Book Room will remain open until
10 p.m. Sunday night.
The Western Civ Library will be
open from 9 a.m. to 12 Saturday,
and Sunday it will be open from 1
to 10 p.m.
