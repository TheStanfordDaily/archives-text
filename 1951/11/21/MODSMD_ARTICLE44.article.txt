# Cal Raid Halted By Frosh Guards
## 
### 
A pre-dawn raid on the Stanford
campus by two carloads of Cal stu
dents was cut short yesterday when
frosh guards halted the group at a
Palm Drive road block.
Though no violence ensued, the
Cal students were ordered back to
their campus by police who were
called to the scene. Police Chief
Gordon Dnvis said the UC students
actcd politely and that no action
would be taken by Stanford au
