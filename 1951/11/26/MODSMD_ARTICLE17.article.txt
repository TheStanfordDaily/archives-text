# Dinner-Dance Ends I Club's Quarter
## 
### 
The International Club will hold
a dinner dance at the Pink Horse
Ranch as its last social event of the
quarter Sunday at 7 p.m.
Club members and those inter
ested in attending are asked to sign
the list posted on the bulletin board
in Mrs. Vogel's office in the Dean
of Students' office. The price will
be posted, and transportation will
be arranged .
