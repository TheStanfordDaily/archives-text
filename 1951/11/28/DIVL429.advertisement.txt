Fourth Annu.il HOWARD S TOUR
to the
UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII SUMMER
SESSION
—For College Cirls Only—
OFFICIAL STATUS:
1. Escorted by mainland university housemothers.
2. Members to attend University of Hawaii summer session.
3. Tour recommended at University residence halls.
4. University of Hawaii jurisdiction over all members.
TRAVEL:
1. Roundtrip via Pan American Strata Cruiser from West Coast to Hawaii.
2. At members special choice, return on S.S. i'Lurline."
SOCIAL ACTIVITIES:
1. A gala Welcome Party, a major civic event.
2. A Hostess Party, for formal introduction to local friends.
3. An evening dance.
4. A formal dancc.
5. An Aloha Luau, old time Hawaiian feast.
SICHTSEEINC:
1. An all day excursion around the Island of Oahu.
2. A half day Waimanalo-Koko Head Drive.
3. A special half day excursion on outskirts of Honolulu.
BEACH ACTIVITIES:
1. Bcach cottage for dressing rooms at Waikiki.
2. Outrigger canoe rides.
3. Constant Bcach Boy attendant on bcach.
4. Bcach club membership.
5. Special program on surf and bcach sports.
COST:
COMPLETE PRICE IS $549.00 — which includes all of the aforemen
tioned benefits and services, as well as the transfers, tips, and federal
and territorial taxes pertaining to the tour.
Tour Organized and Managed by:
Mr. J. D. Howard, Hawaii's Travel Adviser and originator of houscmothcr-
I cscorted University of Hawaii tours.
MEMBERSHIP IS LIMITED AND CONTROLLED. WITH ACCEPTANCE
MADE EXCLUSIVELY THROUCH MAINLAND HOUSEMOTHERS WHO
WILL BE THE INDIVIDUAL S COUNSELOR IN HAWAII. CIRLS ON
THIS CAMPUS APPLY THROUCH:
Mrs. V. E. BRAMMINC
Housemother, Delta Delta Delta
University of California
Berkeley, California
