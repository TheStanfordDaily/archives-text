# 'Players' Tryouts Start Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tryouts for parts in the first
Stanford Players production in win
ter quarter will be held tomorrow,
from 3 to 5 and 7:30 to 10 p.m., and
Friday, from 7:30 to 10 p.m.
The play, "The Goad Woman of
Setzuan" by Bertolt Brecht, will be
presented Jan. 30 through Feb. 2.
It attempts to subject modern so
ciety to a scientific examination, and
in the process exposes and satirizes
many character types in their greed,
hypocrisy, and dishonesty.
The cast v/ill include nine women
and 15 men. Tryouts are open to
the entire campus.
