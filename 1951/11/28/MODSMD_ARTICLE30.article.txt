# Young Democrats Hold First Meeting Tonight
## 
### 
The Palo Alto - Stanford Young
Democrats will hold the first meet
ing of the school year tonight at
7:30 in Longbarn's back room.
Besides the election of officers,
there will be consideration of the
desirability of becoming an on-cam
pus organization, and the new Po
litical Union plan for the campus
will be presented.
