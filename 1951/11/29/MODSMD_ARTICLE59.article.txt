# 'Crystal Nocturne' Is Lag Ball Theme
## 
### 
"Crystal Nocturne" is the theme
of Lagunita's winter formal to be
this Saturday niuht from 'J
to 1. Supplying the music for the
affair will be the Lamplighters'
Ban-). Lag women may still pur
chase bids from their casa repre
sentatives at $. r J apiece
