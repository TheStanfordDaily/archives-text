# Dames to Hear Bassett Read 'Christmas Carol'
## 
### 
Lee Emorson Bassett, founder of
Stanford's Department of Speech
and Drama, will read Charles Dick
ens' "A Christmas Carol" at next
Thursday's meeting of the Stanford
Dames.
The meeting will be held in the
upper lounge of the Women's Club
house at 8 p.m.
