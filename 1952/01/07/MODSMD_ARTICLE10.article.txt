# Cal Gives TV Psych Course
## 
### 
Cal will inaugurate its TV exten
sion course plan Jan. 15 with the
first of a series on child psychology.
The course, first to be given on tele
vision for credit on the West Coast,
will be on the air Tuesdays and
Thursdays from 10:45 to II a.m. for
13 weeks. It will be taught by Dr.
Mary Cover Jones, lecturer in psy
chology at Berkeley.
Registration blanks may be ob
tained from the Department of Cor
respondence Instruction, University
of California Extension, Berkeley 4.
The course may he taken for either
one or three units of credit.
