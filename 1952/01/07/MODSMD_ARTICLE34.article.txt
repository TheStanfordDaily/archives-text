# Intensive Russian Will Be Offered
## 
### 
An intensive course in elementary
Russian will be given this summer
by the Asiatic and Slavic Depart
ment. It will cover, in a ten-week
period, the work done in the three
quarters of the course given during
the regular academic year and will
meet three hours a day, five days a
week.
Registration is limited to an en
rollment of 12. Students who are
interested in taking the course may
see J. A. Posin, Temporary Build
ing C, Room 24, any day this week
at 10 a.m.
