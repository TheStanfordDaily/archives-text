# Bailey Will Give Ballad Program
## 
### 
The first Reading Horn of the
winter quarter, scheduled for to
morrow evening at in the
Little Theater, will present Dr.
Margery Bailey in a program of
ballads.
The program is being offered un
der the auspices of the Speech and
Drama Department and is open to
the public.
Miss Bailey has been on the Stan
ford faculty for 30 years, dividing
her teaching between the 18th cen
tury and classics in drama. She
has published works in the two
fields and has appeared in several
productions of plays from these pe
riods. Her last appearance was as
Ilecuba in "The Trojan Women" at
the Uillbarn Theater in San Mateo.
