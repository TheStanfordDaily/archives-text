# Nanney to Present Organ Selections
## 
### 
Organist Herbert B. Nanney will
play selections by Bach, Haydn,
Franck, and Purcell in a recital to
day at 4:15 p.m. in Memorial
Church.
The program: "Trumpet Tunc and
An," Henry Purcell; "Chorale Pre
lodes for fhr New Year," J. S.
Bach; "Cantahile," Cesar Franck;
"The Musical Clocks," Franz Josef
Haydn; and "Toccata and Fugue in
D Minor," J. S. Bach.
