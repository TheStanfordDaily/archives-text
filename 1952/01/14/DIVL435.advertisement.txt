UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII SUMMER SESSION
—For College Cirls Only—
OFFICIAL STATUS:
1. Escorted by mainland university housemothers.
2. Members to attend University of Hawaii summer session.
3. Tour accommodated at University of Hawaii residence hall.
4. University of Hawaii jurisdiction over all members,
I TRAVEL:
1. Roundtrip via Pan American Strato Cruiser from West Coast to Hawaii. I
2. At members special choice, return on S S. "Lurline."
SOCIAL ACTIVITIES:
1. A gala Welcome Party, a major civic event.
2. A Hostess Party, for formal introduction tu local friends.
3. An evening dance.
4. A formal dance.
5. An Aloha Luau, old time Ifawaiian feast.
SICHTSEEINC:
1. An all day excursion around the Island of Oahu.
2. A half day Waimanalo-Koko Head Drive.
3. A special halt day excursion on outskirts of Honolulu.
BEACH ACTIVITIES:
1. Beach cottage for dressing rooms at Waikiki.
2. Outrigger canoe rides.
3. Constant Beach Boy attendant on beach.
4. Beach club membership.
J». Special program on surf and beach sport*.
COMPLETE PRICE IS $549.00 — which includes all of the aforemen
tioned benefits and services, as well as the transfers, tips, and federal
and territorial taxes pertaining to the tour.
Tour Organized and Managed by:
Mr. J. D. Howard, Hawaii's Travel Adviser and originator of housemother
escorted University of Hawaii tours.
