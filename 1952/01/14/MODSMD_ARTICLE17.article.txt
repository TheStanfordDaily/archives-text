# Beckett Will Head '53 Prom Planning
## 
### 
Garner Beckett was named chair
man of the Junior Prom at a class of
'53 Excom meeting Wednesday after
noon. The prom, the major class
function of the quarter which is open
to the entire campus, is scheduled for
March 8.
Plans arc being formed to obtain a
big name band for the formal affair.
Committees will be chosen soon, and
Beckett requests juniors who are in
terested in working on the dance to
contact him.
A class reunion is scheduled for
Feb. 21, with Stan Blumcnthal as
chairman.
