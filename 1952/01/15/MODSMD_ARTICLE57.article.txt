# Group Plans Trip To Spanish Movie
## 
### 
Sigma Delta Pi members are
sponsoring a trip to San Jose to the
movies for those interested in Span
ish. "Fuenteovejuna," a drama by
Lope de Vega, will be presented at
8 p.m. tomorrow.
Cost will be 30 cents per person.
Anyone who needs a ride or who
can furnish one is asked to contact
Miss Grace Knopp in the evenings
at DA 2-7636 or at Stanford Exten
sion 566 from 11 to 12 noon and
from 1:30 to 3:30 p.m. Pick-up point
will be the post office.
