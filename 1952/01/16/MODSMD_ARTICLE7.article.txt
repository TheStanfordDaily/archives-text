# Nixon on McGrath
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Jan. 15 (UP) —
President Truman's appointment
of Attorney General J. Howard
McGrath to investigate corruption
in government is a "bald-faced
declaration" that there will he no
clean-up, Sen. Richard Nixon (R.,
Calif.) charged tonight.
