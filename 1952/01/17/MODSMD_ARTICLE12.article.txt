# Campus Tire Thief Active
## 
### 
A thief with a liking for tires from
late model Oldsmobiles is busy on
campus, police reported yesterday.
Two late model Oldsmobiles were
stripped of their right front tires and
wheels yesterday morning while
their owners attended class. Joel
Franklin of Menlo Park said that his
car was parked on Lomita Drive
near the Chemistry building when
the thief struck.
The second Olds owner, David Ep
person, of Alpha Delta Phi, said that
his car was parked behind Memorial
Auditorium.
Police Chief Gordon Davis urged
Stanfordites to be on the alert for
people lurking near aulos.
