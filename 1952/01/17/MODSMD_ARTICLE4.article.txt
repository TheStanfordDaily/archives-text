# Farouk Gets Heir
## 
### 
CAIRO, Jan. Hi—A son was born
to King Farouk and his 18-year-old
commoner wife, Queen Narriman—
an event hailed by cheering crowds
as a good omen in Egypt's struggle
against Great Britain.
The child, born early this morn
ing, was named Ahmed Fund, after
his paternal grandfather, and King
Farouk issued a royal command
uiving his first-born son the title
"Prince of Said"—prince of Lower
Fgypt, and heir to his crown.
