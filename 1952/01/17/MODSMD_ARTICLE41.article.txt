# Revivals Feature 2 British Movies
## 
### 
Tonight's Revival Series films will
be "Song of Ceylon" and "Night
Mail," two famous British docu
mentaries of the mid-'thirties.
"Song of Ceylon" deals with the
lives of the Singhalese and the ef
fect of the Western world on them
"Night Mail" shows the hard work
and human meaning behind the
nightly journey of the London to
Glasgow mail.
Only season ticket holders will be
admitted. The show starts at 7:30
in Cubberley Auditorium.
