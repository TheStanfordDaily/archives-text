# Greek Contralto To Perform Here
## 
### 
In the second concert. of this
year's series, Greek contralto Elena
Nikolaidi will be featured Monday
evening at 8:115 in Memorial Audi
torium.
Miss Nikolaidi, who sang with the
San Francisco Opera in 1050 and is
singing this year at the Met, has
made tours throughout Europe and
America.
Tickets for the performance may
be obtained at the ASSU Concert
Series office in the Nitery, which
will be open tomorrow and Friday
from 1 to ») p.m. and Saturday from
8 to 12 a.m. Students may also get
the tickets, which range in cost
from $1 to $2.50, by calling DA
3-3870 for reservations.
