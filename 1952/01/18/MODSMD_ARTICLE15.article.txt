# Visiting Bishop To Preach Sunday
## 
### 
The Rt. Rev. William F. Lewis,
Episcopal Bishop of Nevada, will be
the guest preacher' at the 11 a.m.
service of worship in the Stanford
Memorial Church Sunday. Bishop
Lewis' sermon topic will be "The
God Who Speaks."
Bishop Lewis was educated at St.
Luke's School, Wayne, Pennsylva
nia; at Harvard University, where
he was graduated in 1923; and at
the General Theological Seminary,
New York.
