# Bob White Elected Frosh Crew Coach
## 
### 
Bob White former California cox
swain was elected to the post of
crew coach by Stanford oarsmen,
Wednesday. White, now attending
graduate school, replaces Jim Beggs.
White was last year's frosh coach
and his crew, after losing an early
season race, went undefeated, includ
ing a win against the Cal Frosh.
Crew workouts will get underway
this coming Monday.
