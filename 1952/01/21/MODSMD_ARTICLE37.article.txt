# Countess Tolstoy To Talk on Father
## 
### 
Countess Alexandra Tolstoy,
daughter of Count Leo Tolstoy, the
Russian novelist who wrote "War
and Peace," "Anna Karenina," and
other works, will lecture at the Palo
Alto Women's Club Wednesday at
8 p.m. "My Father, Leo Tolstoy"
will be her topic.
Wednesday's lecture will lie the
only opportunity for the general
public to hear Countess Tolstoy
speak here.
Tickets may he obtained at the
lIR Shack between 3 and 5 p.m. to
day, tomorrow, and Wednesday.
Admission is Si, tax included. Tick
ets will also be available at the
door on the evening of the lecture.
