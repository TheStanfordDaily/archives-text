# McColl Receives Breitbard Award
## 
### 
Bill McColl will receive the Breit
bard Athletic Foundation Star of
the Month award for December, it
was announced last night by Man
aging Director Bill Whitney.
The award will mark the third
time the foundation has honored
McColl. He received Star of the
Month awards in February and No
vember, 1947, when he was an All-
Southern California basketball and
football player for Herbert Hoover
High School of San Diego.
McColl is expecterl in San Diego
for presentation of the Star of the
Month award at a San Diego Ki
wanis luncheon Jan. 29.
