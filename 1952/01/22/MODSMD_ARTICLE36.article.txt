# PLEDGE BREAKING
## 
### 
If any man now breaks a frater
nity pledge, he cannot pledge an
other house for a period of two full
quarters in residence, including
summer quarter, following the one
in which the pledge breaking oc
curs. If a fraternity breaks a man's
pledge, he is free to pledge another
fraternity at any time. In either
case, the action must be taken be
fore the IFC president.
