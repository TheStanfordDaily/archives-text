# Distaff to Hear 'Lovely Miracle'
## 
### 
Philip Johnson's play "The Lovely
Miracle" will lie read by a group of
Stanford Distaff Club members be
fore the club's Book Section tonight
at 8 at the campus home of Profes
sor and Mrs. Konrad B. Krauskopf
at 450 El Escarpado.
Parts in the play will be read by
Mrs. Jeanne Smith, Miss Elizabeth
Retan, Cecil Walker, and Miss Kath
leen Duffy. Mis. Isabel Marx will
take the part of the narrator.
All members of the Distaff Club
have been invited to attend the play
reading.
