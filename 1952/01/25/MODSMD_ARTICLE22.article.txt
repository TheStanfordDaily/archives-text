# Excom Publicizes Scholar Program
## 
### 
Excom and the Foreign Scholar
ship Committee yesterday mailed a
letter recommending adoption of
Stanford's Foreign Scholar program
to 186 universities and colleges
throughout the nation. A pamphlet
explaining the program was en
closed with each letter.
The letter, written by ASSU
President Wayne Alley and the
Scholarship Committee, citsd the
popularity of the program both here
and in Europe, and offered aid to
any school wishing to inaugurate a
similar project.
