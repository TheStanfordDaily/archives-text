# Cartel Set By 'I' Club
## 
### 
International Club is sponsoring
a panel discussion Sunday at 4 p.m.
in the conference room of the Hoo
ver Library on "Our Silent Youth."
The discussion will be recorded for
a later radio program.
Stanford students Daniel Char
bonnier from France; Eva Mendel,
Colombia; Gabriel Morell, Italy;
Shareef Al Mujahid, Pakistan; and
Clotean Helm, United States, will
take part in the program. Joan
Cauldron will moderate.
An informal discussion will be
held before the recording. Anyone
interested may attend.
