# Mrs. Knapp Made Full Professor
## 
### 
Mrs. Maude L. Knapp, head of the
Department of Physical Education
for Women at Stanford, has been
promoted to a full professor.
Mrs. Knapp is a graduate of the
University of Oregon and received
her Master's degree from Wiscon
sin in 1924. She has held high state,
Western, and national offices in her
field.
She was on the executive board of
the National Association of Direc
tors of Physical Education in 1928-
39 and served two terms as presi
dent of the Western Society of
Physical Education for Women in

Earlier she was secretary-treas
urer and vice-president of the Cali
