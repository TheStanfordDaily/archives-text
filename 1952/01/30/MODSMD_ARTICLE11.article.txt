# Ad Men Will Hear Model Campaign
## 
### 
An advertising campaign for
Beach Boy Frozen Pineapple Juice
a hypothetical product created by
the Junior Ad Club of San Fran
cisco —is being presented to the
members of Alpha Delta Sigma ad
vertising fraternity at Dinah's to
night at 8.
This campaign, prepared by
young advertising people of the San
Francisco area, won first place at
the national convention of the Ad
vertising Association of the West in
Denver last year.
