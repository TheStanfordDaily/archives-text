STANFORD GROTTO of the Na
tional Speleological Society will
meet tonight at 7:30 in Room 334.

illustrated, and plans made for a
visit to Silver Cave.

noon at 5.

be taken this afternoon at 4:30.

night at 7:30 in Holy Trinity
Chapel, Menlo Park. There will
be no Civ discussion section to
day.

partment, before noon tomorrow.
