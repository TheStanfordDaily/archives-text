# Faulkner's Works Featured Today
## 
### 
The second Reading Hour of the
winter quarter series will Lie pre
sented this afternoon at 4:15 in the
Little Theater.
Included in the program will be
two short stories written by Somer
set Maugham, the distinguished
English writer, and by William
Faulkner, an American writer who
has recently been the recipient of
widespread acclaim by some of our
most respected critics.
The Reading Hour is being pre
sented under the auspices of the
Department of Speech and Drama.
The public is invited to attend.
