# Delegates Sought For Model UN
## 
### 
Interviews for students wishing to
try out, for a position on the Stanford
delegation to the Model United Na
tions will take place in the lIR shack
this afternoon from 4 to 5.
Ted Collier and George Vclliotes
are co-chairmen of the delegation,
which will represent the United
States. The Model UN will be held
in April at the University of South
ern California.
Anyone not able to be interviewed
this afternoon may call DA 2-9159
in the evening or leave a note for
Ted Collier at the lIR shack.
