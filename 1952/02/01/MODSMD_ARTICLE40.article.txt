# TENNIS
## Cards Battle Palo Alto Club 
### 
Stanford's tennis team, with high
hopes for an outstanding season,
take on the Palo Alto Tennis Club
Saturday at 1 p.m. on the local
courts.
Bob Renker, Card tennis coach,
will use Tom Lewyn, George Gen
try, Charlie Hickox, Bob Sibert, Jim
Cornish, Art Fish, George Stewart,
and Dean Brinkman to battle the
Paly aggregation headed by Ellis
Slack, one of the top doubles play
ers in the country.
