Fourth Annual HOWARD S TOUR
to the
UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII SUMMER SESSION
—For College Cirls Only—
OFFICIAL STATUS: B
1. Escorted by mainland university housemothers.
2. Members to attend University of Hawaii summer session.
3. Tour accommodated at University of Hawaii residence hall.
4. University of Hawaii jurisdiction over all members.
TRAVEL:
1. Roundtrip via Pan American Strato Cruiser from West Coast fo Hawaii. |
2. At members special choice, return on S.S. "Lurline."
SOCIAL ACTIVITIES:
1. A gala Welcome Party, a major civic event.
2. A Hostess Party, for formal introduction to local friends,
3. An evening dance.
4. A formal dance.
5. An Aloha Luau, old time Hawaiian feast.
SICHTSEEINC:
1. An all day excursion around the Island of Oahu.
2. A half day Waimanalo-Koko Head Drive.
3. A special half day excursion on outskirts of Honolulu.
BEACH ACTIVITIES:
11. Beach cottage for dressing rooms at Waikiki. H
2. Outrigger canoe rides.
3. Constant Beach Boy attendant on beach.
4. Beach club membership.
5. Special program on surf and beach sports.
COMPLETE PRICE IS $549.00 — which includes all of the aforemen- I
tioned benefits and services, as well as the transfers, tips, and federal I
and territorial taxes pertaining to the tour.
Tour Organized and Managed by:
Mr. J. D. Howard, Hawaii's Travel Adviser and originator of housemother- g
escorted University of Hawaii tours.
MEMBERSHIP IS LIMITED AND CONTROLLED. WITH ACCEPTANCE 1
MADE EXCLUSIVELY THROUCH MAINLAND HOUSEMOTHERS WHO I
WILL BE THE INDIVIDUAL'S COUNSELOR IN HAWAII. CIRLS ON 1
THIS CAMPUS APPLY THROUCH:
Mrs. V. E. BRAMMINC
Housemother, Delta Delta Delta
2300 Waring Street.
University of California
Berkeley, California
