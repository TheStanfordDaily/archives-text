# Fourteen Indians Prepare Debates
## 
### 
After weeks of preparation, in
cluding a mock tournament, 14 de
baters are ready to represent Stan
ford at Cal Tech, where they will
compete against teams from 30 col
leges and universities. The subject
is "Should the federal government
adopt a permanent program of wage
and price controls?"
Entering the Upper Men's Divi
sion are the following teams: Ar
nold Gold and Harvey Wagner;
Bruce Bailey and LaForest Phillips.
In the Lower Men's Division are:
Dave Jacobson and Chandler Myers;
Brad. Jeffries and Arnold Mendell;
Pat Parsons and Fred Whitaker;
Buzz Gregorio and Jack Moran
Elizabeth Gonser and Virginia Pat
terson will represent Stanford in
the Women's Division.
