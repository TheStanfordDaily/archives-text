# Netters Win First Match
## 
### 
Stanford's varsity tennis team
opened its season Saturday with a
7 match to 2 win over the Palo Alto
Tennis Club.
In the feature match, Stanford's
Tom Lewyn lost a close battle to
Ellis Slack, one of the Bay Area's
best doubles players, 1-G, 6-2, 5-7.
George Gentry staved off two
match points to come from behind
and beat Bill Holmes, 4-G, G-4, 8-G
in the second singles match.
In the third spot, Charlie Hickox
disposed of Jim Frolick 6-4, 6-4. At
fourth singles, Bob Sibert lost to
Israeli champ Ray Gornitsky, 0-G,
7-5, G-4.
Dean Brickman defeated Leon
McKee, 7-5, 6-2, and George Stew
art defeated Roger Schuyler, 6-1,
G-4, to give Stanford two more wins.
Stanford swept the doubles as
Lewyn and Gentry defeated Slack
and Sandy McCay, 4-6, 6-3, 6-4;
Hickox and Sibert beat Frolick and
Gornitsky, 6-2, 6-1; and Brinkman
and Stewart beat McKee and
Schuyler, 6-4, 6-4.
Card Coach Bob Renker said the
team looked good for a first match,
and should improve within the next
few weeks.
