# Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
The Sophomore Cotillion will
wind up the Frosh-Soph Jamboree
Saturday night from 9 to 1 at the
j Devonshire Club in San Carlos.
Tickets for the traditional hatch
- et-burying dance will be on sale for
I $2.75 in front of the Cellar today,
| tomorrow, and Wednesday from 9
j to 12 and from 1 to 4. and in Encina
| lunch and dinner lines.
The dance will be dressy and will
I feature Merle Howard's orchestra.
[ Co-chairmen are Diane Rexroth,
] lan Campbell, and Norm Morgan.
