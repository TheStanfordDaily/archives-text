# AWS Primaries Set for Tomorrow
## 
### 
Thirteen girls have been nomi
nated for AWS top women's offices.
Primary elections will be held
tomorrow with the final balloting
scheduled for Thursday.
Running for president are Sally
St. John and Ann Miller. Four girls
are on the vice-presidential ballot:
Jenny Johnson, Jean Bobis, Carolyn
Thomson, and Joan Bramson. The
candidates for secretary are Dianne
Goldman, Nancy Sterret, Amber
Spinning, Marge Ross, Ann Franks,
Klancy Clark, and Susan Stark.
