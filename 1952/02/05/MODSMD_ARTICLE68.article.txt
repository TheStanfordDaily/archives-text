# MEXICO TOUR
## 
### 
An educational tour of Mcxico will
be conductor! June 26-July 23 by
Prof, and Mrs. Juan B. Rael of
Stanford. Cost from L A.: $493.
Tour includes Guadalajara, Urua
pan, Patzcuaro, Morelia, L.in Jose
de Purua, Mexico City, Forfin,
Puebla Cucrnavaca, Tarco, Aca
pulro, and other interesting places.
Consult Prof. Rael in Room 246,
Romantic Languages, MWh 9-10
and MIWThF 2-3. Res. Phone
DA 3-4081.
