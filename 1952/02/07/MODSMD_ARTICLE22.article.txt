# Sophs(?) Raid Roble; Bible Suites Spared
## 
### 
Sophomores are suspectcd of en
tering Foble during the semiannual
Birthday Dinnei last night. Girls
returning to their coriidors imme
diately following the dinner dis
covered "To Hell With '55" in hp
tick on the full-length mirrors in
each coj ridor.
The efficient saboteurs also drip
ped glue on the floor of the johns
and put molasses on the toilet seats.
Toilet paper was strewn around too.
Emergency measures were taken
immediately to alleviate the situa
tion. Roblean ; were relieved to find
the Bible Suite johns hadn't been
molested.
