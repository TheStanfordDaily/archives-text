# Debater Will Vie In KCBS Contest
## 
### 
While a 14-man squad is vying
for honors at Col Tech this week
end, the Stanford Debate Society
will also take part in a radio de
bate on KCBS.
Don Gillin, a history major, will
represent Stanford on the radio de
bate which will be broadast at
10:30 a.m. Sunday on the San Fran
cisco station.
Gillin will debate against repre
sentatives from San Jose State, St.
Mary's, and USF on the subject,
"Should George McLean's old-age
security plan be adopted?"
