# Toy on Will Hold Valentine Dance
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale for the
Toyon "Sweethearts on Parade"
dance Saturday. They sell for $1.20
a couple and are available at the
ASSU office or from Toyon wing
representatives.
Toyon Social Chairman George
Geering promises a good time will
be had by all at the Valentine's af
fair, which will get under way at 9
and continue through 1. Dance
music will be intermixed with en
tertainment,.
