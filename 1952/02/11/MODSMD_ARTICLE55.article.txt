# Special King Sale Termed Success
## 
### 
"The special sale of '52 class rings
last Thursday was sucessful as in
past years,' said Mrs. Helena Bou
chou, manager of the Stanford
Bookstore.
People who ordered their rings at
the sale will be assured of getting
them around the first of June.
Senior rings are still obtainable
at the Bookstore at the original
price, with rebates. If they are or
dered within two weeks, students
can expect delivery on these also,
in June.
