# University Singers To Give Program
## 
### 
The University Singers will pre
sent. a program before the Music
Section of Hie Stanford Distaff Club
tomorrow at 8 p.m. at the campus
home of Professor and Mrs. Charles
Moser, 441 Gerona Road.
The program, under the direction
of Herbert B. Nanney, will include
English madrigals, chansons, and an
old French song, "The Battle of
Marignan."
All members of the Distaff Club
are invited to attend the program.
