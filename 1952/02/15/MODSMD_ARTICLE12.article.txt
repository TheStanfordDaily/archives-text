# BEATING
## Arrest Hinted In Hayes Case 
### 
Police Chief Gordon Davis re
ported yesterday that he had a
"good idea" who the three boys are
who severely kicked and slugged
Stanfordite Charles Hayes in front
of Toyon Hall about midnight Tues
day.
Stating that Hayes was able to
give additional information yester
day, Davis said lie hoped to "hook"
the culprits soon.
Hayes was released from Palo
Alto Hospital yesterday after be
ing treated for a broken nose and
facial lacerations. He still claims
the attack was unprovoked.
