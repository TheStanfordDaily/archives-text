# Orchesis to Dance At Sequoia High
## 
### 
Seven Orchesis members from
Stanford will give a program Satur
day at the high school symposium
on modern dance to be held in Red
wood City's Sequoia Ifigh School.
Approximately ten high schools in
the Bay Area will be represented,
and 150 students are expected.
Joan Hunt, Barbara Brooke, Gar
ry Kindy, Sue Clum, Helen Gay
lord, Sally Halbert, and Joan Mou
ritsen are the Orchesis representa
tives who will demonstrate how
dance technique develops from the
simple to the complex.
