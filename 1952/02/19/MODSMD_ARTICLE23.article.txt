# Library Continues Surplus Book Sale
## 
### 
Students who enjoy browsing the
shelves of old book stores and who
are interested book collectors are
encouraged to visit the surplus book
room in the Main Library. The room,
open all day, is located on the mam
floor in the receiving room.
The books range from the complete
works of Thackeray and the Memoirs
of Napoleon to problems in economics
and insurance.
All the titles are on sale at nominal
prices. They are books for which de
mand has passed, books that are out
of date and in some cases duplicates.
