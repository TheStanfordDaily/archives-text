# San Mateo Group Will Hear Hilgard
## 
### 
"Is the Old-Fashioned Family
Gone Forever" will be Dean Erne A
R. Hilgard's topic when he speaks at
the second annual dinner of the San
Mateo County Family Service
Agency tonight.
The agency, a member of the San
Mateo County Community Chest,
offers professional casework service
to persons needing help with family
problems.
