# Queen to Decorate
## 
### 
LONDON Queen Elizabeth II
will hold her fir..l investiture next
week to dub new knights of her
realm and award decorations to
British war heroes, Buckingham
Palace announced today.
The colorful ceremony, first semi

will take place in the grand ball
room of Buckingham Palace, where
the young Queen will stand on a
dais and tap tlie new knights on the
shoulder with a sword as they
