# '52 Wrestling Squad
## 
### 

LOADED FOR BEAR—Stanford's 1952 wrestling squad. Left to
right (front row) Bob Neighbor, Oliie Lyons, George Russell, and
Bob lessen. In the rear are Bruce Bailey, Ken James, Pat Madden,
Phil Wilson, and Rod Garner. The wrestlers face Cal tonight in
their last match of the season. Cal was the Pacific Coast co
champ last year with Washington State and is again gunning for
that honor. —Daily photo by Dick Schwarz
