# Spring Show Contest Opens
## 
### 
Ram's Head is offering a $200
prize for the best script, words and
music, submitted in its annual
Spring Show competition. Deadline
for entries is March 3.
Anyone may submit a script, but
only registered Stanford students
are eligible for the prize. If two
students colaborate in v liting the
words and music, the prize will be
split.
Scripts may be left in the Ram's
Head office in the Union or may be
given to Robin Stevens, director.
The show, a musical, is presented
every spring quarter by Ram's
Head.
For further information, anyone
interested may call Stevens at UL
mar 1-1491 or leave a message for
him on the Ram's Head bulletin
board.
