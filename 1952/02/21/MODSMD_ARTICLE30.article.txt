# Juniors to Hold Reunion Tonight
## 
### 
Members of tlie cla'-s of '53 will
meet tonight in (he back room of
L'Ommie's for a junior reunion. The
get-together is slated for 8:30.
Tlie Alpha Delt Dixieland combo,
directed by Dick Shooshan, will
provide music, and refreshments
will be available.
Stan Blumenthal is chairman of
the affair.
