# Korea War Vet Joins Farm ROTC
## 
### 
Major Henry B. Glaister, a recent
arrival from Korea, reported to the
Stanford ROTC last week to take
over the instructorship vacated by
Captain Lloyd Goeppert, who was
recently transferred.
Major Glaister spent the entire
year of 1951 with the First Corps
Artillery in Korea. He has been in
the Army since 1.941. A graduate of
the University of California, he
played four years of college base
ball and later turned professional,
spending three years in the Pacific
Coat League with the Oakland
club.
