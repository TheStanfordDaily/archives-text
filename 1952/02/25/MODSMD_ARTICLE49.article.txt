# Farm Professor Verses 'Parzifal'
## 
### 
Dr. Bayard Q. Morgan, professor
of German emeritus, collaborated
with E. H. Zeydel of the University
of Cincinnati in a book published
recently by the University of Worth
Carolina Press.
The publication is a verse trans
lation of the medieval epic, "Parzi
fal," by Wolfram von Eschenbach.
