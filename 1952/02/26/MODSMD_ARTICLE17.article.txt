# Western Radio, TV Conference Slated
## 
### 
The Western Radio and Televi
sion Conference on "the greater use
of radio and television in the public
interest" will be held at Stanford
this weekend.
Judge Justin Miller, chairman of
the National Association of Tele
vision Broadcasters, will speak Fri
day night on the need for support
of tha TV industry's new code,
which will go into effect the next
day.
Mrs. Inez Richardson, executive
assistant at the Hoover Institute
and Library, will be chairman of
the conference, which will include
representatives from commercial
and educational radio and televi
sion stations, schools and colleges,
and civic groups.
