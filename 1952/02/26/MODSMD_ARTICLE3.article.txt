# Rosenbergs to Die
## 
### 
NEW YORK. Feb. 25—The death
sentences of Julius and Ethel Ro
senberg were upheld today by the
U.S. Circuit Court of Appeals,
which agreed with a federal judge's
decision that the man and wife
should become the first American
civilians ever to be executed for
spying on their country.
The Rosenbergs were convicted
of giving America's atomic secrets
to Russia.
In a three-to-two decision, the
Appellate Court also upheld the
conviction of Morton Sobell, 34-
year - old electronics expert and
childhood friend of Rosenberg. So
bell was tried with the couple and
was sentenced to 30 years in prison.
