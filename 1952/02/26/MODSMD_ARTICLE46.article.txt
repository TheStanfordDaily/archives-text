# Campus 'Y' Elects Officers for '52
## 
### 
New officers were elected by the
Stanford YMCA at their last meet
ing. The officers, which will be in
office for the remainder of the 1952
academic year, are: Merle Asper,
president; Pete Barney, vice-presi
dent; Dan Heil, recording secretary;
Vic Fennell, corresponding secre
tary; and Don Ginter, treasurer.
The group will honor Bruce Ma
guire, traveling representative for
the regional YMC'A, at a dinner on
Thursday night.
