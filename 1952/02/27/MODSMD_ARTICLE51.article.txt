# 'A Bit of Heaven' Tickets Sell Now
## 
### 
Tickets for the Branner dance, "A
Little Bit of Heaven," go on sale
today in the ASSU office for $2.40
each. The dance will be held Satur
day night from 9 to 1 in the Bran
ner lobby and will be dressy.
Music will be provided by Jimmy
Blass and his orchestra, and enter
tainment by the Branner "Live
Five." Pictures of the dance will be
taken by photographer Gary Hol
lander.
