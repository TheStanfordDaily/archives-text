# 'The Well' Planned For Sunday Film
## 
### 
"The Well," a highly-acclaimed
drama of prejudice and fear in a
typical American town, will be this
week's Sunday Movie.
Hie movie is vaguely based on the
tragic Kathy Fiscus story, .except
that here it is a Negro girl who falls
into a well and a white construction
engineer, portrayed by Henry Mor
gan, is suspected of kidnapping her.
The show will start at 7:30 in the
Memorial Auditorium. Admission
will be the perennial twenty cents.
