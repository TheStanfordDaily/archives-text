# Village Is Scene Of Dames Social
## 
### 
Tonight at the Stanford Village
auditorium the Stanford Dames will
hold their annual bridge social at
7:45.
Reservations may be made by call
ing DA 5-2888. Tallies will be sold
for 35 cents each. Mrs. James Whip
ple, chairman, announced that singles
as well as tables of four are welcome.
Refreshments will be served.
