# Queen Expecting?
## 
### 
LONDON British newspapers
began predicting today that Queen
Elizabeth II postponed her corona
tion until next year because she is
going to have another baby.
The mass circulation Sunday Pic
torial said "rumors of a sentimental
nature" developed after Elizabeth's
gynecologist, Sir William Gilliatt,
visited Clarence House the day af
ter she flew home from Africa to
be proclaimed Queen.
