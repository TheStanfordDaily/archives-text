# Pre-Reg Com Tryouts Set
## 
### 
Tryouts for pre-registration com
mittee will be held tomorrow from
2 to 4 p.m. in the Excoin room, and
Wednesday from 3 to 5 p.m. in the
ASSU president's office.
The committee consists of tbout
40 members and plans to carry out
the orientation program for all new
students entering Stanford in the
fall. Students with ideas for im
proving the existing program as
well as those who will be at Stan
ford summer quarter are especially
needed.
Some of the pre-reg committee's
jobs are planning the welcome as
sembly, frosh picnic, campus tours,
academic talks, transfer dinner and
dance, men's smoker, women's cof
fee hour, and freshman Sunday at
Memorial Church.
