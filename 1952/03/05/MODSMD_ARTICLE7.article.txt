# Wages and Prices Will Be Debated
## 
### 
Dave Jacohsen and Chandler
Myers will take the affirmative
against two representatives from
San Francisco State when they de
flate the permanent wage and price
controls issue tonight. Open to the
public, the debate will be held in
Room 231, History Corner, at 7:30.
Jack McDonald, Daily editor, will
represent Stanford on the KCBS
program, "University Platform," to
be broadcast at 2 p.m. on Saturday.
Representatives from UC, Stanford,
San Jose State, and San Francisco
State will present their views on
the subject, "Who Should Control
Student Publications?"
