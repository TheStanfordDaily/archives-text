# Tennis Jayvees Top City College
## 
### 
The Stanford jayvee tennis team
won its opening match of the season
against San Francisco City College,
8 to 1. The match was held at the
Golden Gate Park courts.
In the feature match, Gary Gray
outserved Don Mays to win 6-4,
6-2. At second singles, George
Stewart downed Robert Mays, 6-4,
6-3. In the third spot, Bob Barton
beat Bob Elies in amarathon, 6-3,
4-6, 6-4.
Bob Bedford dropped Stanford's
only match to Sandy Berman, 6-4,
6-3. Dick McKenzie and Tom Terry
beat their opponents, John Lew and
Joe Bradkin, respctively, by the
identical scores of 6-1, 6-0.
Coach Bob Renker's team swept
the doubles as Gray and Stewart
downed the May brothers, 6-0, 6-1.
Red ford and Terry won over Elies
and Crncich, 6-0. 6-2; and Barton
and McKenzie beat Berman and
Lew, 6-4, 6-3.
