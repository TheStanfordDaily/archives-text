THE WINNER —Pete McCay, Stanford broad jumper displays
perfect form as he broad jumps 23 feet 2 inches. This jump won
the event for McCay, as the Indians triumphed in a triangular
meet Saturday. —Daily photo by Ed Forner


UP AND OVER—Bob Mathias, left, and Bill Storum, second from right, compete in the high hur
dles event Saturday. Mathias won the event, while Storum placed third behind Bob Legler
of Fresno, far right. Other runner in the picture is Bill Gillespie, San lose State hurdler, who
placed fourth. -—Daily photo by Herb Smith
