# Village Opens To Non-Vets
## 
### 
The Federal Housing Authority
has granted special permission by
which apartments in Stanford Vil
lage will be made available to non
veterans.
Non-veterans may sign up for va
cancies on a first-come first-served
basis starting at 8:.'50 this morning
at the Housing Office in Building
100 on Inner Quad.
Veterans, however, will still be
given priority on vacancies. Mar
ried couples with children will have
preference on the larger apart
ments.
