# Farm Law Grad Wins Navy Gross
## 
### 
Former law student Paul M. Mc-
Closky, now a lieutenant in the
Navy, was awarded the Navy Cross
for "extraordinary heroism ... in
action against enemy aggressor
forces in Korea on 29 May. 1951."
The Navy's second-highest, decora
tion was presented during cere
monies at Camp Pendleton this
week. He has also won the Silver
Star.
McClosky completed two years in
the Stanford Law School before
joining the Navy, and will resume
his studies on the Farm in June.
