# Spiegelberg Book To Be Exhibited
## 
### 
A book written bv Stanford Pro
lessor Frederic Spiegelberg and
published by the Greenwood Press
of San Francisco has been selected
by the American Institute of
Graphic Arts for its annual "Fifty
Books of the Year" exhibition.
"Spiritual Practices of India," by
Spiegelberg, was selected as a fine
example of American bookmaking.
The exhibition will open today at
the New York Public Library.
