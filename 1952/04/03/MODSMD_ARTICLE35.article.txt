# Summer Openings
## 
### 
Representatives from the North
rop Aircraft, Company and South
ern Pacific will be here tomorrow to
interview engineering students in
terested in summer and full-time
employment.
Northrop Aircraft is interested in
aeronautical, mechanical, electrical,
and civil engineers at all degiee
level ;. They would al:o like to see
any men who will be seniors next
year and want summer employ
ment.
Southern Pacific needs civil and
mechanical engineers for positions
such as rodmtn, instrumentmen,
junior engineers, inspectors, drafts
men, estimators, cost analysts, and
statisticians. Summer work is also
available.
