# Krupotich Is Elected Remele Hall President
## 
### 
Officers elected for spring quarter
for Remele Hall, the Biz School
dorm, are as follows: Ed Krupotich,
president; John Elliott, vice-presi
dent; Jerry Bogdan, financial vice
president and comptroller; Chuck
Clemons and John Bransten, social
chairmen; and Dean Daily, athletic
chairman.
Co-chairmen for the annual
Brookdale Weekend are Sandy
Perry and Nate Alexander.
