# Petitions Available For Credit Evaluation
## 
### 
Transcripts of work completed
at other institutions are being
evaluated in the Registrar's Office
for those who turn in petitions.
Forms are available at the infor
mation desk of the Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A.
New students entering this
quarter with advanced standing
need not petition. Military serv
ice and training is also evaluated
for those who file petitions.
