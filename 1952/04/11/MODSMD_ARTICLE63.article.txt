# Poetry
## 
### 
Dylan Thomas, famous English
poet, is programmed to give a read
ing Wednesday at the San Fran
cisco Museum of Art.
Thomas, now on an extended
tour in the United States, will read
selections from his own work and
dramatic sequences from the works
of other poets.
The performance is at 8:30. Tick
ets may be obtained at the Museum
for $2.
