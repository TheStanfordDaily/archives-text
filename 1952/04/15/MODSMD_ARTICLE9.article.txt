# Public Relations Com To Provide Posters
## 
### 
Organizations needing posters but
lacking the personnel to make them
will be able to hire artists through
Public Relations Committee.
Persons interested in painting
posters, by hand or silk screen, arc
asked to see Roberta Parker, Art
Director, in the Public Relations
Office, top floor of the Union, today
or tomorrow from 10:00 to 12:00.
Artists will be paid for their work.
