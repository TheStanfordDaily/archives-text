# FACULTY PARTY
## 
### 
A joint party sponsored by the
Men's and Women's Faculty Club
will be held this Friday at the Wo
men's Clubhouse. Professor Virgil
Anderson, Speech and Drama De
partment, is the Program Chairman.
The Men's Club will present a
show directed by Prof. Lee Emer
son Bassett. Following the program,
there will be bridge, canasta, and
dancing to a six-piece combo.
