# Reed President To Receive Medal
## 
### 
For the second time a man close
ly affiliated with Stanford Univer
sity is to receive the coveted Wil
liam Freeman Snow medal for dis
tinguished service to humanity.
Ernest Boyd MacNaughton, presi
dent of Reed College, consulting
professor of banking in the Stanford
Graduate School of Business, board
chairman of the First National
Bank of Portland, Oregon, and
president of the Oregonian Publish
ing Company, will receive the
award in Los Angles April 23.
Previous Snow Medalists include
the late Dr. Ray Lyman Wilbur.
