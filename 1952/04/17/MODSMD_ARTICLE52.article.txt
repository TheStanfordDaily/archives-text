# The 						Woman's Role Slated for 'Y' Topic
## 
### 
The YWCA Marriage and the
Family Committee will meet tomor
row at 4:15 on the third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse.
The topic of the meeting is "The
Woman's Responsibility to the
Community." Mrs. W. D. Pabst will
speak and will answer such ques
tions as, "What can the husband and
wife do together?" "How can the
wife help her husband through
community work?" and others. All
students are welcome.
