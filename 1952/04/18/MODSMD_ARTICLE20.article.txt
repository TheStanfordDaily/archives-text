# Over 300 Pledge To Donate Blood
## 
### 
Students pledged to donate blood
in the coming Red Cross drive this
Tuesday and Wednesday now total
over 300, drive chairman Pete Halle
reported last night.
The quota set for Stanford is 700
pints.
Appointment booths, manned by
A Phi O fraternity members, will
remain open today and Monday
from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.
