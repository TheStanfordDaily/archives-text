# More Parking Area Provided
## 
### 
The reserved parking area in the
triangular lot across from Stern
Hall has been reduced by two
thirds, Police Chief Gordon Davis
reported yesterday, thus increasing
room lor general student parking.
He said that the restricted area,
which once affected 30 autos, will
now affect only 12.
The chief also reported that
parking facilities are being in
creased around campus with the
street being widened in front of
Encina and the Toyon lot being
enlarged.
