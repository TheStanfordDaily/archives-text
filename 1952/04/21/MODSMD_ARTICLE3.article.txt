# PT&T Compromise
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO—CIO Commu
nications Workers today accepted a
compromise 15 - cent hourly wage
boost for plant employees to end
the two-weeks-old strike against
the Pacific Telephone and Tele
graph Company in Northern Cali
fornia and Nevada.
A union spokesman said picket
lines would be immediately dis
solved and workers would return to
their regular shifts tomorrow.
