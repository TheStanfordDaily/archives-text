# Con Home Game Signups Are Due
## 
### 
Signups for the Con Home tourna
ment are due tomorrow night on
the lists that have been posted in all
living groups.
Five sports arc in the tournament
which will raise money for the an
nual Con Home Drive May 3-10.
The sports are two-man volleyball,
six-man volleyball, girls' volleyball,
mixed volleyball, bridge, and ping
pong. Only one volleyball team may
enter from each small house, and
the games must be played by May 10.
Each entrant must pay a fee of
j 25 cents. The games will be played
wherever the two contending teams
decide. Pairings will be announced
in the Daily and will also be posted
in the Men's and Women's gyms.
Prizes will be awarded to the win
! nets of the tournament. They will
be announced at the Con Home
dance May 10.
Chairman of the tournament is
! Bob Medearis.
