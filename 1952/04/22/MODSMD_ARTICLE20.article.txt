# Men May Apply Now to Sponsor
## 
### 
Men who wish to sponsor in a
frosh dorm either during the sum
mer or next fall quarter may make
application now.
Application blanks are available
both at the ASSU office and at the
Encina office, but should be turned
in to Encina.
Any man who will have junior
standing by the time he begins
sponsoring is eligible.
The deadline for application is
May 1. Final seelction will be made
by interviews for which appoint
ments may be made when the ap
plication is filed.
