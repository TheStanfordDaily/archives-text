# CAMPUS DANCES
## Delta Chi 'Ball' 
### 
Gangsters, thugs, and other such
rogues are wanted at the Delta Chi
all-campus "Rogues' Ball" this Sat
urday night from 9 to 1 at the Delta
Chi house. The costume for the
dance is roguish attire. The Saints
will play.
