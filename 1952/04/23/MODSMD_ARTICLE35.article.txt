# Two Stanfordites To Address Rally
## 
### 
Stanfordites Don Starr and Tim
Sharpe, members of the local
Young Republican Club, will speak
at a giant Young Republican rally
in San Luis Obispo Monday night.
The two will help organize Young
Republican groups in that area.
Starr is treasurer of the local group
and Sharpe is chairman of the Cali
fornia College Service Committee.
