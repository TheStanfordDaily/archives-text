# Old Boy Sells Issue Today
## 
### 
The Old Boy will be on Quad this
morning, peddling his Annual Ex
change Issue of the Chappie.
The best of the rest of the college
humor mags will be in this issue.
Loads of jokes, cartoons, and arti
cles guaranteed to brighten up a
nine o'clock.
This treasury of college humor
will be "sold" for only thirty (30)
cents at the four corners of Quad,
the Cellar, and the Post Office.
