# British Scholar Will Talk Today On Shakespeare
## 
### 
Sir John Tresidder Sheppard,
provost of King's College, Cam
bridge, will lecture today at 4:15 in
Ihe lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse. His topic will be
"Shakespeare's Schooldays."
Sir John is a British classical
scholar who is at present giving a
series of lectures at (lie Universliy
of California. He has published
many worki on classical books .such
as the Iliad and such authors as
Sopho les and Aeschylus. The lec
ture is open to the public.
