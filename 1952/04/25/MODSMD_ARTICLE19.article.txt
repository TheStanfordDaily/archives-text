# Senior Programs Go on Sale Monday
## 
### 
Senior graduation announcements
! and programs will go on sale Mon
day in the stationery department of
the Bookstore. Deadline for order
ing is May 10. They will be dis
tributed June 1 only to those who
have ordered in advance.
Announcements are 15 cents each,
and programs $1.35.
