# Student-Faculty Com Has P.O. Box
## 
### 
New plans are now under way
for the Student - Faculty Educa
tion Commission. The commission
has rented a post oflice box and
will answer all mail concerning
educational problems.
All mail should he addressed to
Post Oflice Hox No. 2035, Stan
ford. Letters should contain ideas
for improvement of existing prob
lems and criticism of the present
situation.
Lucile ileuhen end Skip Irving
are the two new members of the
commission.
