# Daily Will Publish List Of Spring Graduates
## 
### 
Beginning tomorrow, The Daily
will publish a provisional list of
candidates for A.B. and B.S. de
grees at the end of spring quarter.
This list will include, however,
only applications which were re
ceived by 5 p.m. Tuesday. The
deadline for applications for grad
uation this quarter is next Tues
day. Blanks may he obtained at
the information desk, Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A.
