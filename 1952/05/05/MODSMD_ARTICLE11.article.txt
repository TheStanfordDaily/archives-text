# CON HOME EVENTS ACTIVITIES
## 
### 
Today—Tag Day.
Tomorrow — Strawberry Brunch
in Union Court.
Wednesday—Sam McDonald La
bor Day at the Con Home and bar
becue dinner.
Thursday — Co-ed Carwash on
lower row and Toy Dinner in
women's living groups.
Friday — Soph Carnival in Pa
vilion.
Saturday—Con Home Dance at
Devonshire Club with crowning
of king and queen and awarding
of other prizes.
All Week—King and Queen bal
loting, Copper Coin Leg, On and
OfT campus soliciting.
