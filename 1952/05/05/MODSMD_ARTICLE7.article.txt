# Golf Tourney

Con Home's Golf Tournament will
be played tomorrow instead of Wed
nesday as announced previously. The
tournament will be a two-ball four
some for women students with male
partners.
All starters will contribute 50<? to
the Con Home Drive in place of a
green fee. Prizes will be awarded the
winning team. Foursomes will meet
at the clubhouse at 1:15 p.m. and
tee oil between 1:30 and 2:30 p.m.
