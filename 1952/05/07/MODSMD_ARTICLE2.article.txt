# Taft Sweeps Ohio
## 
### 
COLUMBUS, O.—Sen. Robert A.
Taft and Sen. Estes Kefauvcr rolled
up big leads in the Presidential
delegate-at-large races tonight as
returns mounted in Ohio's primary
election.
Taft was leading Harold E. Stas
sen for all 10 delegate at large seats
at the Republican national conven
tion in Chicago this July.
Sen. Estes Kefauver, the Tennes
see crime hunter, was leading with
all eight of his delegates at large in
the Democratic column.
Willi 547 of 10,160 precincts re
porting, Taft has 186,295 to 51,487
for Stasscn.
