# JV's Try To Stretch Win Streak
## 
### 
Winners of nine in a row and
with only three more yames re
maining on their schedule, the
Brave.j play two foes this weekend.
Today at 3:15, on their own dia
mond, the jnyvees tangle with the
College of Marin. Saturday they
journey lo Berkeley for the rubber
match with the Cal Cinnamon
Bears.
Coach Dick Clegg will probably
use today's encounter Lo brush up
his boys for Cal. Pitchers he will
most likely use are Dave Freeman,
Stan Fetterman, and lion Hoffman.
Saturday, in an attempt to make
it two in a row over the Cal jayvees
and win the season's series:, he will
come buck with Kay Ilighsmith and
Gil Duncan, who checked the Cin
namons with but four hits in last
week's 8 to 4 win.
The Braves play their season's
closer a week from Saturday, with
a tilt against Vallejo JC.
