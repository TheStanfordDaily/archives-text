# 'Try and Get Me' Is Sunday Movie
## 
### 
"Try and Get Me," a story of
murder and mob violence, is this
week's Sunday Night Movie. The
film tells of the lynching of an in
nocent man. The movie was banned
in San Jose. Its story is said to be
based on a similar lynching that
occurred there in the mid-thirties.
Cathleen Ryan, Lloyd Bridges,
and Frank Lovcjoy are starred.
The show will start at 7:30 in Me
morial Auditorium.
