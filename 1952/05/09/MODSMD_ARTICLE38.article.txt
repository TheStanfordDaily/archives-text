# YWCA Elections Set for Monday
## 
### 
Candidates for the elected offices
of the YWCA were announced at a
membership luncheon yesterday.
Running for president are Jan
Thomason, Ellie Myers; vice presi
dent, Alice Kwong; secretary, Irene
Matthews, Virginia Andreas; treas
urer, Mary Wicks, Joanne Presley.
Voting will take place during
house meetings Monday. Appointed
officers will be announced next
week.
