# Farm to Compete In Joffre Debate
## 
### 
Bruce Bailey, Hal Rogers, and
Alan Enthoven will compete with
three California speakers during
the Joffre Debate, t.o be held Tues
day in Wheeler Auditorium, Berke
ley.
Speakers will debate a specific
topic under the general subject
"Radicalism in French Politics."
The winner will be presented with
the Joffre Medal by the French
consulate in San Francisco. The
debate is in honor of Joffre, a hero
in French history.
