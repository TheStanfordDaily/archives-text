# BRANNER DANCE
## 
### 
Branner will present its spring
dance, "The Cotton Ball," Friday
night from 9 to 1. The spring motif
will be carried out in the decora
tions and in the dressy cottons worn
by the girls, and music will be by
the Lamplighters.
General chairman is Pat Carroll.
Bids are $2 per couple.
