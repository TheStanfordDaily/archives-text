# Engineer Students Win Essay Honors
## 
### 
Lawson C. Stafford and John L.
Robb, juniors, took first and second
prizes in the 1952 Mary A. Earl Mc-
Kenney essay contest for engineer
ing majors, George F. Seivabaugh,
acting head of the English Depart
ment, announced yesterday.
The subject of the essays for the
1952 contest was "Science and the
Literature of Imaginary Voyages."
Stafford, a mechanical engineering
major, won $100 and Robb, an in
dustrial engineering student, won
$25.
Both students will receive suit
able trophies in addition to the cash
prizes.
