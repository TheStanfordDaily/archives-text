# Toyon and Stern Sign-ups Planned
## 
### 
Village and oft-campus men in
terested in living in Stern or Toyon
for fall quarter are asked to
fill out sign-Tip cards which will be
available in the office of the Housing
Supervisor, room 101-A. Inner Quad,
between 8 a.m. and 5 p.rn. tomorrow.
Students should state their prefei -
ence on the cards and hand them in
before «) p.m. tomorrow.
Eating Club members should net
sign, as arrangements will be made
for them through their respective
clubs,
Men who have not completed 45
units of work and who have not been
in attendance spring quarter will not
be eligible for the sign-up.
