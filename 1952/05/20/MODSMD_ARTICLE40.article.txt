# Stanford Alumnus Gets Music Post
## 
### 
Grosvenor William Cooper, a Phi
Beta Kappa from Stanford, has been
appointed chairman of the music
department at the University of
Chicago. He will take his new
post ori July 1, besides retaining his
present status as an assistant pro
fessor of humanities.
Cooper graduated from Stanford
