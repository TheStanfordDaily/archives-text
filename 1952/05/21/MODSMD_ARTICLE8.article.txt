# Hint-Filled Travel Book Really at IIR
## 
### 
Going abroad this summer? If so,
the lIR travel booklet, "Introduc
tion to Europe and Latin America"
might help you.
The booklet is available free of
charge at the lIR Shack. It con
tains hints on tipping, passports,
vaccination, exchange money, and
transportation costs. It also lists or
ganizations and individuals who are
familiar with Stanford students and
are willing to help them if any
problems arise.
