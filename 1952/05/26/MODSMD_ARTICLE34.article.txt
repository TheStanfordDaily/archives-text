# Ted Collier Wins New Stuart Award
## 
### 
Ted Collier was presented the
Graham H. Stuart Award at a Pi
Sigma Alpha, honorary political
science fraternity, banquet held in
honor of Dr. Stuart Saturday niyht
at Pasture's.
The award, an eight-volume set
of the Encyclopedia of Social Sci
ences, was newly instituted this
year and was presented to Collier
by Dr. Stuart himself. It is intended
for the outstanding senior gradu
ating in political science.
Runners-up for the award were
Paul Dnvies Jr., Ray Ilaile, Jackie
Harris, and Marvin Milton. They
each received a year's subscription
to the American Political Science
Review.
