# Kerkorian Signs With Steelers
## 
### 
Gary Kerkorian, quarterback on
this year's Card Rose Bowl eleven,
signed a contract Saturday to play
with the Pittsburgh Steelers of the
National Football League. Gary is
the second member of last year's
team to sign a pro contract, Bill
McColl having earlier signed with
the Chicago Bears.
Other Cards in pro ball now in
clude: Frankie Albert, Norm Stand
lee, Bruno Bandueci, and Vie Lind
skog.
