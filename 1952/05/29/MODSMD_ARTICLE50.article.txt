# Shrinkage Lists Open on June 2
## 
### 
Women may apply for shrinkage
on June 2 in Room 101-A, Inner
Quad, if they were not placed in
one of their first three choices in
room drawing and did not cancel
their number.
Women should submit three resi
dence and one area choice to Housing
Com, and when a vacancy arises it
may be accepted or rejected. In the
meantime women will live in the
residences they were placed in dur
ing regular drawing.
Shrinkage continues through the
summer and the first two weeks of
fall quarter. After this time women
are removed from shrinkage lists
until they draw again.
