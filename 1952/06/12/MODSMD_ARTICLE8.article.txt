DIRECTORY
The senior class directory is on sale
at the ASSU office from 8:30 a.m. to
5:00 p.m. It includes the names and
home addresses of all members of the
class of '52.
The book also contains the class his
tory, poem, oration, and will as well
as a page on which seniors may write
in changes of friends' addresses.
The price is 52 cents.
