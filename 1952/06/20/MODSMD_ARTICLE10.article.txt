# Revival Tickets Sell at Mem Aud
## 
### 
The 1952 Summer Revival Film
Series is presenting a group of
"comedy" movies each Thursday
evening. Season tickets for the
series, to which Stanford students
and faculty have priority, go on sale
today in the Memorial Hall box office
for $1.80.
Eight showings will be presented.
No single admissions will be sold.
The schedule includes:
"The Ghost Goes West." "It Hap
pened One Night," "Mr. Smith Goes
to Washington,""The Baker's Wife,"
"Volpone," "My Little Chicadee,"
"Passport to Pimlico," and "Duck
Soup" and"The Barber Shop" (the
last two on a single bill).
