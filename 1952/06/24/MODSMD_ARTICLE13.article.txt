# Lake Lag Is Closed; Sunbaths Allowed
## 
### 
Lake Lagunita is closed for swim
ming and boating for the summer.
The lake is fast running dry, and
the only permissible activity is sun
bathing on the boathouse float.
Those who swim in the lake from
now on are violating a University
regulation and will be subject to
penalties imposed by Men's and
Women's Councils.
