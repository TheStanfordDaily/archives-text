# UP ROUNDUP
## Malik Insists Chinese Reds, No. Koreans Hear UN Debate 
### 
UNITED NATIONS. N.Y.. June
23 (UP) — Soviet United Nations
Delegate Jacob A. Malik today be
gan what looked like a repeat per
formance of the maneuvers he used
to paralyze the Security Council
shortly after the Korean war erupt
ed.
Malik — again wielding power as
Council president for June—insist
ed that Chinese and North Korean
Reds be invited to debate an Amer
ican proposal for a Red Cross in
quiry into Communist germ war
charges.
He warned that, otherwise, he
could not agree to debate of the
Amerian proposal.
