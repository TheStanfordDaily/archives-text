# Winters Receives Literary Award
## 
### 
\
In recognition of his work in po
etry and criticism, Yvor Winters,
Stanford professor of English, has
received a $1,000 award from the
National Institute of Arts and Let
ters.
The award, one of seven in the
field of literature, was presented to
Dr. Winters recently at the Annual
Ceremonial in New York of the
American Academy of Arts and
Letters and the National Institute
of Arts and Letters.
Professor Winters Was cited for
"his work in both poetry and criti
cism, as a moralist, a logician and a
seeker of perfection in literary
form."
