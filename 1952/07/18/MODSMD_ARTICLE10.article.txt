# Crosby Will Star In Sunday Movies
## 
### 
"If I Had My Way," starring Bing
Crosby and Gloria Jean, will be the
movie this Sunday. In the show are
a great many outstanding songs, in
cluding "Meet the Sun Halfway,"
"April Played the Fiddle," and the
title song, "If I Had My Way." Also
on the bill is a cartoon and "So You
Want to Be a Cowboy," a Joe Mc-
Doakas short.
An all-campus coffee hour will
be held at Stern Hall immediately
after the movies. Richie Swartz,
Stern Hall social chairman, is in
charge of the function.
