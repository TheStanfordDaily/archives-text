# ANSU Schedules Farm Sports Day
## 
### 
ASSU will sponsor an all-campus
Sports Day at the women's pool a
week from tomorrow. There will be
swimming and volleyball to music
starting at 2, and dancing until 11
p.m.
Box suppers will be served to
those living in campus dormitories.
Off-campus students are invited to
bring picnic suppers. Free cokes
will be served.
The pool will be open until 9 p.m.
