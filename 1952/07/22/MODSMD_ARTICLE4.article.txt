# Sterling and Family Vacationing in East
## 
### 
Dr. and Mrs. Wallace Sterling
left Saturday for a month's vaca
tion in the East. Dr. Sterling will
take part in the National Commis
sion for the Financing of Higher
Education this week in Cotuit. Mass.
From Cape Cod the Sterlings will
drive to Hamilton. Ontario, for a
ten-day stay at Rosewood Farm,
Mrs. Sterling's father's home. On
the way back to Stanford, they will
spend some time in Colorado.
The Sterlings' three children.
Bill, 13, Susan, 10, and Judy, 7, are
traveling with them.
