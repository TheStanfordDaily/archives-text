# Rev. Conn to Give Sermon Sunday
## 
### 
The Rev. Howard J. Conn of the
Plymouth Congregational Church,
Minneapolis, Minnesota, will be the
preacher at the Memorial Church
'service at 11 a.m. Sunday. Mr.
Conn's sermon topic is "Living Up
to Our Possibilities."
The Rev. Howard Conn is a grad
uate of Stanford University (1932).
and graduated from the Yale Divin
ity School in 1936. He has been rec
tor of the Plymouth Congregational
Church for the past eight years.
James Erickson will assist in the
service, and the anthem will be sung
by the choir under the direction of
Herbert B. Nanney.
