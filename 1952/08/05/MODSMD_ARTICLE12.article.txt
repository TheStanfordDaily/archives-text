# MEETING ON DROUGHT
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Aug. 4 (UP) —
The government today summoned
Southern farm leaders to a meeting
in .Atlanta Wednesday to speed
emergency loans to drought-strick
en farmers and prepared to add
North Carolina to the growing list
of disaster states.
