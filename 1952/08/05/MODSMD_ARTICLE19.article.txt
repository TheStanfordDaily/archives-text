organist, wil play the following pro
gram of music by Johann Sebas
tian Bach at 4:15 p.m. Thursday.
Fantasia on St. Theodulph; two
chorale preludes, "Come, Saviour
of the Gentiles," "Rejoice Now,
Christian Souls"; Prelude and
Fugue in F Minor; "When Thou Art
Near," arranged by Schreiner; Con
certo No. 1, in C Major.
