# Dames Will Hold Last Bridge Meet
## 
### 
The Bridge Section of Stanford
Dames will hold its last meeting for
the summer next Thursday at 8 p.m.
in the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse.
Mrs. Jack Shannahan, chairman
of the section, has announced there
will be tables for beginning, inter
mediate, and advanced players.
There will be canasta for those who
wish it.
Mrs. Folmer Christensen will be
in charge of the refreshments.
All Stanford Dames are invited to
attend.
