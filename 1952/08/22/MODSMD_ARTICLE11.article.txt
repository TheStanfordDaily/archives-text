# Adlai on Ike
## 
### 
MINOCQUA. Wis., Aug. 21 CUP)
—Gov. Adlai E. Stevenson said to
day that Dwight D. Eisenhower is a
"me-too" candidate "in a sense"' be
cause the Republican nominee give
general approval to the Democratic
record of the past 20 years.
The Democratic Presidential can
didate made his "me-too" charge
against Eisenhower in a news con
ference held outdoors at the north
ern .Wisconsin summer home where
he is taking a three-day holiday.
