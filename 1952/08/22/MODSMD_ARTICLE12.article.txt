# Ike on Korea
## 
### 
KANSAS CITY. Kan.—Dwight D.
Eisenhower said today that meeting
Communist aggression in Korea
with force had kept us out of "a
much greater and more serious"
war, but he did not subscribe to air
attacks on Chinu.
