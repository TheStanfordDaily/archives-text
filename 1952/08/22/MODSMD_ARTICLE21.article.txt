# Students Can Enter Poetry Contest
## 
### 
College students are invited to
submit original verse in the Tenth
Annual College Competition for
possible publication in the Annual
Anthology of College Poetry.
Rules are as follows: Manuscripts
must be typed or written in ink on
one side of a sheet. Student's name,
home address, name of college, and

: college address must appear on each
' manuscript.
Students may submit as many
! manuscripts as they desire.
Manuscripts should be sent to the
National Poetry Association, 3210
Selby Avenue. Los Angeles 34. Cali
fornia.
