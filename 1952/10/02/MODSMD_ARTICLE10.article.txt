# Today Is Last Day To Buy, Sell Books At A Phi O Project
## 
### 
With more than 100 books still on
the shelves, the A Phi O Book Ex
change opens for the last time today
to sell books from 1 to 4 p.m. in the
southwest corner of Temporary
Building A, in back of the Civ Libe.
Dud Herschbach, chairman of the
project, said that books will not
be received, but only sold this af
ternoon. The Exchange will reopen
Oct. 22 through 25 to return money
and books to students who left their
texts with the service fraternity.
