# Concert Tickets Go on Sale Today
## 
### 
Season tickets for the annual
Concert Series will be on sale in
front of the Cellar this morning be
tween 10 and 12.
The sale in front of the Cellar will
be held today only, but tickets can
also be purchased any day between
1 and 5 p.m. in the Nitery, above
the Cellar.
Anyone interested in ushering for
the series is invited to visit the
Nitery between 1 and 5 p.m. for
further information.
