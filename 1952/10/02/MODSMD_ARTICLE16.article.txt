# Room and Board Bills Deadlined
## 
### 
The deadline for payment of Uni
versity board and room bills is Fri
day, Oct. 10, the controller's office
has announced. Payment is to be
made at the cashier's office, Build
ing 1, fnner Quad. The office is
open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday
through Friday.
Students living at Stanford Vil
lage may make payment at the Vil
lage cashier's office, 0:30 a.m.-.12:30
p.m. and 1:30-3:30 p.m., Monday
through Friday.
Checks with the statement en
closed may be mailed lo the cash
ier's office but must be received by
Oct. 10.
