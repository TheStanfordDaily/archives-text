# French Group Visits Farm
## 
### 
Fifteen French government offi
cials visited Stanford Research In
stitute Sept. 25 as part of a six
weeks' study of U.S. industrial, ag
ricultural, and governmental proce
dures.
The goal of the group was to
obtain a first-hand picture of the
American free enterprise system
and the benefits of increased pro
ductivity shared equitably be
tween consumers, workers, and
management.
The visit was arranged by the
Mutual Security Agency under its
productivity and technical assist
ance program to assist the French
officials in giving the most effective
government support to the French
productivity drive.
'Ihe French governmental group
included 11 deputies of the French
National Assembly and four other
high government officials, and rep
resented all non-Communist parties
in France.
