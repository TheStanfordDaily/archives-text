# Nanney Schedules Bach Organ Recital
## 
### 
Music by Joliann Sebastian Each
will be featured in an organ recital
today by Herbert B. Nanney, Uni
versity organist, at 4:15 p.m. in the
Memorial Church.
The selections will include "Fan
tacie in G Minor," "Three Choral
Preludes," ' Prelude and Fugue in B
Flat Major," "Largo" from "Sonata
No. 5 in C Major," and "Prelude and
Fugue in G Major."
Recitals are planned for every
Thursday at 4:15 p.m. in the church
and will cover the works of histori
cal organ masters such as Han
del, Mozart, Brahms, Mendelssohn,
Franck, end several modern organ
composers.
