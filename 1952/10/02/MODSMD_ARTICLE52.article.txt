# Dr. Gabor Begins Lecture Series
## 
### 
Dr. D. Gabor, Mullard Reader in
Electronics at the Imperial College
of Science and Technology, London,
will begin a series of four lectures
this morning at 11 in Room 12G ERL.
The title of the first lecture will
be "Dynamics of Electron Streams."
Two additional talks on the same
subject will be offered this after
noon at 3 and tomorrow morning at
9. A fourth lecture, titled "Commu
nication Theory," will be given at 3
tomorrow afternoon.
