# Brush Fire Rages
## 
### 
SAN JOSL Am 8,000-acre brush
fire 30 miles southeast of here near
Mt. Hamilton continued out of con
trol today as forestry officials
searched for a "horseback arson
ist."
The blaze started Wednesday six
miles north of Mt. Hamilton in the
San Antonio Valley and worked its
way southwest when, according to'
fire fighters, an arsonist repeatedly
set new blazes behind them as they
were getting the central fire under
control.
