# Farm Room Bills Headlined Friday
## 
### 
Friday is the deadline for pay
ment of University room and board
bills.
Payments may be made at the
cashier's office, Building 1, Inner
Quad. The office is open from 8
a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Fri
day.
Students living at Stanford Vil
lage may pay at the Village cashier's
office, 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. and
1:30 to 3:30 p.m., Monday through
Friday.
Checks with the statement en
closed may be mailed to the cash
ier's office but must be received by
Friday.
