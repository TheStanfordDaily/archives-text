# Health Insurance Applications Due For Fall Quarter
## 
### 
Autumn quarter applications of
Stanford's faculty members and
non - academic employees for the
University's voluntary health insur
ance plan will be accepted up to
Nov. 1, to be effective Dec. 1.
The joint plan offers medical and
surgical coverage by the California
Physicians Service and hospitaliza
tion coverage by the Blue Cross.
The program, which is reopened
for membership quarterly, offers
protection for hospitalization, sur
gery, and doctor care for all depend
ent family members of those en
rolled.
The California Physicians' Serv
ice will accept applications and
cover pre-existing conditions if ap
plication is made within 60 days
from the date of employment.
Information on tbe above pro
gram is available through the Stan
ford payroll office.
