# Fall Hours Given For Women's Pool
## 
### 
Women students will be permitted
to use the women's swimming pool
at the following hours this quarter,
according to Maude Knapp, Direc
tor of Physical Education for Wom
en: Tuesday and Thursday, 2 to 3
p.m.; Wednesday and Friday, 3 to 5
p.m.; and Monday through Thurs
day, 5 to 5:30 p.m.
The pool will also be open at noon
for women staff and faculty mem
bers. A fee of 15 cents per swim
will be charged.
