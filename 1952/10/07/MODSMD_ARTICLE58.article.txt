# Absentee Voting Information Given
## 
### 
Students wishing to learn about
absentee voting procedure in any
of the 48 states may contact Mrs.
Katherine Millar of the Palo Alto
League of Women Voters, DA
2-7392, any day after 3 p.m.
Voters are reminded that they
can request the services of the
League Speakers' Bureau, whose
members will, without charge, give
non - partisan information on the
California ballot measures to any
group of 10 or more. To reserve a
speaker, call Mrs. Nathan Finch, DA
2-2716.
