# Dr. Freudenberg To Give Seminars
## 
### 
Dr. Karl Freudenberg, director of
the Chemical Institute and professor
at the University of Heidelberg,
Germany, will give two special
seminars at the Stanford Chemistry
Department today and tomorrow.
They will be held at 4:15 p.m. in
Room 20 of the main Chemistry
Building. "Natural Tannins" will be
his topic today.
