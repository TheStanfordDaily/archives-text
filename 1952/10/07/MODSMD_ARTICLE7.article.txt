# Funeral Is Today For Stanford Grad
## 
### 
Funeral services will be held this
afternoon for Jan Edwards Falk, '51,
at Gray's, Divisadero at Post in San
Francisco.
Miss Falk, the daughter of Mrs.
Otto H. Falk, Jr., was known on
campus as Jan Edwards before she
was officially adopted by Mr. Falk
following her graduation.
She was president of Union Resi
dence, a member of Cap and Gown,
and a candidate for ASSU vice
president.
Eight weeks ago, while living in
an apartment in San Francisco, Miss
Falk was stricken with acute leuke
mia. She died Sunday in a San
Francisco hospital.
