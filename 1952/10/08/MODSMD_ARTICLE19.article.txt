# Truman Hits San Francisco
## 
### 


TRUMAN IN SAN FRANCISCO —President Truman is greeted
with an assortment of Eisenhower placards and shouts of "We
Like Ike" as he arrives at the Palace Hotel in San Francisco last
Saturday. Truman later left the hotel to deliver a. major speech
in the Oakland Auditorium Saturday night.
—Daily photo by Ed Forner
