# 1952 Reg Statistics Reveal Big Decrease
## 
### 
Reg figures are still below the
corresponding totals of last year,
according to the Registrar's Of
fice.
The latest totals released yes
terday show 5,291 men and 1,935
women registered as contrasted
with 5,489 men and 2,042 women
at this time last year.
