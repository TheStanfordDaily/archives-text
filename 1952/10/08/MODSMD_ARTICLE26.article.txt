# Publicity Group Adds Members
## 
### 
The Public Relations Committee
announced yesterday the appoint
ments to the committee of Richard
Miller, Meralee Kahn. Barry Galvin,
Nancy Coe, Marjorie C. Kelsey, and
Ken Schecter.
The committee, the official public
relations agent for the ASSU, acts
as a center of information about
publicity media and as an educative
body in public relations, administers
ASSU publicity regulations, and
aids student, organizations in plan
ning and co-ordinating publicity
campaigns.
The committee's current project
is the Activities Booklet, a short
description of all campus organiza
tions, which is slated for publica
tion next week.
