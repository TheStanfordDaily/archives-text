# Navy Outlines Therapy Plan
## 
### 
The Navy has announced a medi
cal training program in the field of
physical therapy for senior women
students.
Women accepted will be appointed
as ensigns in the Navy Reserve and
will receive full pay while in their
last year at Stanford.
Lieut. Commander Linnea Alenius
will be at the Physical Therapy De
partment in Building 101, Stanford
Village at 11 a.m. tomorrow to dis
cuss the progx'am with all who are
interested.
