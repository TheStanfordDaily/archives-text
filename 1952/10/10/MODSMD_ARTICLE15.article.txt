# Hyslop to Preach Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
The Rev. Ralph Hyslop of the Pa
cific School of Religion will be the
visiting preacher at Stanford Me
morial Church this Sunday at 11 a.m.
His topic will be "Our Wisdom and
God's Will."
Dr. Hyslop is Associate Professor
of Historical Theology and the His
tory of Christianity at the Pacific
School of Religion.
Music for the service will be sung
by the Memorial Church Choir under
the direction of Harold C. Schmidt.
Herbert B. Nanncy will be at the
organ.
Dr. Hyslop will also speak at the
Sunday Afternoon Club.
