# Interviews Planned for Prospective AWS VP Job
## 
### 
Jeanie Bobis, new president of
AWS, has announced that, inter
views for the position of executive
vice-president of the campus wo
men's organization will be held today
from 2 p.m. to 4 p.m., and on Tues

day from 1 p.m. to 3 p.m. The lower
elubroom which is located in the
basement of the Women's Clubhouse
will serve as the site for the per
sonal conferences.
i Since the office of vice-president

was left vacant when Miss Bobis left
it to assume the duties of president
(when Sally St. John resigned from
that position) it must be filled by
selecting the most capable girl as de
termined by an AWS committee.
