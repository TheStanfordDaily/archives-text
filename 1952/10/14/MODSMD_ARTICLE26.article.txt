# VILLAGE ELECTIONS
## 
### 
Village 212 and 214 announced
results of their elections yesterday.
Jim Walters is president of 212.
Other officers are Tim Moore, social
chairman; Hayward Robertson, bus
iness manager; and Mike Wogan,
athletic director.
The president of 214 is Davis
Spencer. Dereck von Schausten is
vice-president, Elliot Serafin is sec
retary-treasurer, Roy Raguse is ath
letic chairman, and Fred Nord is the
social chairman.
