# Counseling Center Promotes Two
## 
### 
Two promotions at the Counseling
and Testing Center were announced
this week by President Wallace
Sterling.
Mrs. Barbara Cannell was named
assistant director. She will act as
chief psychologist and be directly in
charge of the graduate training cen
ter. Dr. Henry A. Bamman has been
appointed senior counselor in
charge of remedial reading and
study skills.
In his new job, Dr. Bamman will
continue direction of the reading
work of the center, conduct research
into techniques of remedial educa
tion, and supervise the teaching of
graduate students in remedial read
ing.
