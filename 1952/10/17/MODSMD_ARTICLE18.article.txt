# ASSU Leadership Training Approved
## 
### 
Excom approved an ASSU lead
ership orientation conference at its
Wednesday night meeting.
After tentatively scheduling the
conference for Friday afternoon.
Oct. 31, Excom appointed Charlotte
Trego to work with Roy Alexander,
Cardinals president, in organizing it.
Oscar Weiskopf, ASSU foreign
scholar from Germany, gave a short
talk of appreciation and presented
Dick Weismann, representing the
ASSU, with a book as a token of
friendship from his parent school,
Munich University.
