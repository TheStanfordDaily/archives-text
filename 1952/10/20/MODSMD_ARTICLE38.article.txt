# MURAL BOWLING BEGINS
## 
### 
Intramural bowling starts to
morrow night on the Village al
leys, according to Elwyn F. Bugge,
mural director, 'limes for matches
are 7 aiui 8:30 p.m.
The alleys will only he open
for mural plav this quarter. Lath
team is requested to furnish an
extra man for pin-setting duty.
Regular wages will be paid for
this service.
