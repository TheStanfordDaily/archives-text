# Swim Club Sets Saturday Tryouts
## 
### 
Swim Club has announced that
tryouts will be held Saturday morn
ing from 9 to 11 in the Women's
Gym.
A list of necessary skills and re
quirements has been posted in the
gym, and any girl interested is in
vited to attend a meeting at 7:30 to
night in the gym.
The organization meets every
Monday night during fall and spring
quarters and every other week dur
ing winter quarter. In the fall, com
petition in water form and speed
comprises the meetings and is or
ganized by various members in the
club. No swimming goes on winter
quarter, but plans are made lor the
spring water show.
