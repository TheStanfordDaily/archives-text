# Special Program For Stutterers Offered by Clinic
## 
### 
Meetings begin tonight in a special
program of speech therapy for adult
stutterers which is being offered this
quarter by the Stanford Speech and
Hearing Clinic.
The training will consist of one
group meeting each week, supple
mented by individual therapy for
persons needing or requesting it.
The meetings will be held Tuesdays
at 7:30 p.m. in the Speech and Hear
ing Clinic on the third floor of the
Knoll, room 300.
Adults from this vicinity, as well
as Stanford students, may enroll by
attending a class meeting.
