# Crothers to Host Farm Open House
## 
### 
Crothers Hall will hold an open
house Saturday from 4:30 to 6:30
p.m. after the Stanford-Washington
game.
Judge George E. Crothers, San
Francisco, patron of the hall, will be
here for the event.
A three-piece combo will provide
music for dancing, and refreshments
will be served.
Tours will be conducted through
the new wing, dedicated last year,
as well as through the rest of the
building.
