# Class of '55 Will Meet at Mangini's
## 
### 
Sophomores will congregate at
Mangini's Bain tomorrow afternoon
for an all-Class of '55 reunion. The
Portola Road spot, just beyond
Rossotti's, will be the scene of the
renewal of friendships from 2:30
until 6 p.m.
Marco Vitulli, class president, an
nounced that the party will be a
chance to meet new members of '55.
Refreshments will be provided,
and Pope Smith and his Royal
Archbishops of Jazz will add music
at no extra cost. A group of Beta
sophs will undertake entertainment
chores.
