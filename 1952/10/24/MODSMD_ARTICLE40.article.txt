# Frosh Book Sale Begins on Monday
## 
### 
Subscriptions for the Frosh Book
will be sold for $1 Monday and
Tuesday from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. at
Engineering corner and at the Post
Office.
The books will arrive in about
two weeks. No surplus will be or
dered.
