# McFadden Authors Business Article

controller, is author of an article on
accounting in the October issue of
College and University Business.

can serve, not only as a system of
records, but also as an aid in man
agement decisions. The author dis
cusses applications of this principle,
now widely recognized in commer
cial accounting, to educational ac
