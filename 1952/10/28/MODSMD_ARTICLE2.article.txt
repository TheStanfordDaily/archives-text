# Photos Scheduled For Row Women
## 
### 
All residents on Women's Row
will have their pictures taken be
ginning today for tlie 1953 Quad.
Photos will be taken at the Quad
office through Friday from 9 to 12
a.m. and 1 to 4 p.m.
Stan-up sheets have been placed
in each house and should be stancd
promptly. Cost of photo is $1.25.
Lagunita and Branncr sopho
mores, juniors, and seniors, mean
while. are reminded today is the last
day to have their pictures taken for
the Quad from 11 to 12 a.m. and 1
to 4:15 p.m. in the Quad office.
Women arc advised, in both cases,
to wear white blouses.
