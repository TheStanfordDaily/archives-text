# Women's Club to Hear Speech on Germany
## 
### 
Milton Mayer, recently returned
from Germany, will .speak at 3 p.m.
at the Palo Alto Women's Club,
Sunday, on "Germany and the Pros
pects for Peace."
Before going to Germany last year
Mayer was an assistant to Robert A.
Hutchins at the University of Chi
cago. He has written articles for
such national magazines as Harper's,
Life, and Commonwealth.
