# Culture Calendar
## 
### 
ART: The San Francisco Museum of Art is intro
ducing a new form of art exhibition with its "Art
in Cinema" series. Films representing both classic
and contemporary achievements in the art of the
film will be shown each Friday this month at 8 p.m.
Also on display at the Museum are exhibitions on:
"Watercolors from Permanent and Loan Collections";
"Thirty U.S. Contemporaries"; "Paintings for Japan";
and ''Recent Works by Bay Region Artists."
MUSIC: This week's San Francisco Museum of
Art Concert will be given Wednesday evening at 8.
Alice Ehlers, harpsichordist, and Alex Murray, vio
linist, will present two sonatas by Bach and one by
Mozart for the final concert in the program.
