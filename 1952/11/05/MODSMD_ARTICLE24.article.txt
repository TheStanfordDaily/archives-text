# Mathias to Sign Biography Copies
## 
### 
Bob Mathias will be on hand at
the Stanford Bookstore Thursday
from 2 to 3 p.m. to autograph copies
of his biography "Bob Mathias,
Champion of Champions."
Jim Scott, author of the recent
book, is a sportswriter on the Berk
eley Daily Gazette and intimate
friend of the Mathias family. In his
book he tells the story of how the
boy from Tulare, Calif., became the
"greatest athlete of all time."
