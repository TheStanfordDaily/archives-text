# 'Happy Life' Topic Of Star's Talk to Dames
## 
### 
Mrs. Lois Moran Young, former
star of Broadway and movies, will
speak to the November meeting of
the Stanford Dames tonight at 8 in
the Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. Young will talk on "Recipe
for a Happy Life." She is now ac
tive in dance therapy for the Palo
Alto Veterans' Hospital and the
Convalescent Home. No reservations
are required, and refreshments will
he served.
