# 'Sexual Deviations' Is Lecture Subject Today
## 
### 
"The Nature and Cause of Sexual
Deviations" will be the subject of a
lecture by Dr. Frank A. Beach this
afternoon at 4:15 in Cubberley Au
ditorium. The lecture will be the
loui th in the 1952 Gimbel
Lectures on Sex Psychology.
The Gimbel Lectures, sponsored
jointly by Stanford and the Univer
sity of California, began Monday
evening. Dr. Beach lectured at Lane
Hall of the Stanford University
School of Medicine in San Fran
cisco. He lectured at Berkeley on
Tuesday evening, and at Lane Hall
again last night. The series ends to
morrow evening with a third lec
turc at Lane Hall.
Dr. Beach is a psychobiologist and
professor of psychology at Yale Uni
versity. Last year's Gimbet Lecturer
was Dr. Alfred Kinsey.
