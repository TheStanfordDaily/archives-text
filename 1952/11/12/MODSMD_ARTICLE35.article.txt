# Farm Students In S.F. Play
## 
### 
Several students and alumni of
the Stanford Speech and Drama De
partment will participate in the pro
duction of "Twentieth Century," to
be staged by the San Francisco Ac
tors' Company.
The play will open tonight at 8:30
in the Theater at the Beach, San
Francisco.
Nancy Langston, whose produc
tion of "Good Woman of Setzuan"
was staged last year in the Studio
Theater, is serving as assistant di
rector of the Actors' Company and
is playing the part of Dr. Johnson in
"Twentieth Century."
Hugo Melehione, a member of
Stanford's drama faculty, has done
the set designs, and lighting is by
Joel Rubin, at present a graduate
i student in speech and drama at
Stanford.
