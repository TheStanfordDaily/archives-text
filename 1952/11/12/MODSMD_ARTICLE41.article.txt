# Sixteen Cards Have Been "All-America"
## 
### 
Stanford has had sixteen all-
Americans. The first ones were Jim
Lawson and Ernie Nevers from the
middle 'twenties powerhouses. The
most recent ones were Bill McColl
and Gary Kerkorian from last years
Rose Bowl crew. Two-time Card ail-
Americans include Nevers, McColl,
Frankie Albert, Bob Grayson, Horse
Reynolds, Bill Corbus, and Monk
Moscrip. The rest of the list in
cludes Ted Shipkey, Seraphim Post,
Don Robesky, Walt Heinecke, Phil
Moffatt, Bones Hamilton, and last,
but certainly not least, Chuck Tay
lor.
