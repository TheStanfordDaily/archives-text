# Answer to Mrs. McMullin
## 
### 
To the Editor:
Since the person using the signature DARE STARK
McMULLIN states candidly her belief that only the
President should speak for Stanford, may we hope
that (now she has made the nation safe) she will
cefise to infringe on his privileges?
MARGERY BAILEY
Associate Professor of English
(Department owned by Mrs McMullin)
