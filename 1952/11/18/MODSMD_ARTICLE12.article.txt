# Frosh Girls Feed. Bonfire Builders
## 
### 
The freshman woiften are doing
their part toward building the Big
Game bonfire by serving refresh
ments at late hours to those who are
providing the brawn.
Girls from Roble, Branner. and
off campus are serving coffee and
doughnuts. Under the organization
procedure set up by co-chairmen
Pat Sumner at Roble and Sally
Hammcrieh at Branner, five girls
are serving at .1 p.m., 8 p.m., and I
a.m. until Thursday when the bon
fire is completed.
