# Tea Will Honor English Faculty
## 
### 
A major achievement by Stanf
ford's Department of English—the
publication of five books by faculty
members during a single academic
quarter—will be celebrated at a tea
this afternoon.
The event, to which the faculty
and students of the department
have been invited, will be held in
the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse on the campus from to
5:30 p.m.
The five authors who will be hon
ored are Robert W. Ackerman, John
W. Dodds. Richard Foster Jones,
George F. Sensabaugh, and Arthur
Yvor Winters.
