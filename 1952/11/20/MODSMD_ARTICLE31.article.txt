# Gun Club Opens Its Turkey Shoot
## 
### 
Stanford Gun Club's annuel tur
key shoot opens today and will con
tinue through Tuesday.
The range will be open from 9
a.m. to 4:30 p.m. every day, plus
Friday and Sunday nights from 7:15
to ( J:3O. A tin key will be given
away for 9 very 10 persons shooting.
The gobblers weigh 12 pounds.
The Guri Club will provide luck
targets for novices and skill tar
gets for old hands.
The price iu four shots for $1. The
high scorer in each group of 10 will
receive a turkey.
The shooting range is located
across from the men's gym in HOTC
park.
