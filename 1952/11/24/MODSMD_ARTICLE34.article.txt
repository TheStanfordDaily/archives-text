# POLO ASSOCIATION
## 
### 
New officers of the Stanford Polo
Association are: president, Ralph
Perlberger; treasurer, Hal Hender
son; social chairman, Margot Miller;
equipment manager, Harry Bettis.
Plans were also made for entertain
ing the Colorado polo team over
Thanksgiving when they will be on
campus to play a two-game series
against the Indians.
