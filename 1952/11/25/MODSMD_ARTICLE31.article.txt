# Girard to Discuss Artist and Society
## 
### 
Andre Girard, famed French
painter, will discuss at the Inter
mezzo series two of his teachers,
Pierre Bonnard and Georges Roualt,
at 4 p.m. tomorrow in the Bender
Room of the Main Library.
Girard, the outstanding student of
Roualt and Bonnard, will also dis
cuss an artist's relation to society.
