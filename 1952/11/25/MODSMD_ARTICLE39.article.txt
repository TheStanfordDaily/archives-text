# SENIORS TO BE FETED
## 
### 
Seniors on this years football
team will be feted to a steak dinner
by the PALO Sports Club tonight at
Rickey's Studio Club. Originally
bear meat was slated as the main
course, but it was changed after the
Big Game result. Gordy Soltau, star
end of the San Francisco 49ers, will
appear on the program.
