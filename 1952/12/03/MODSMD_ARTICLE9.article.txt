# Farm Band Wins Combo Contest
## 
### 
The Rhythm Vendors, a campus
orchestra, won a combo contest at
last, night's annual dinner for the
members of the Sun Jose local of
the American Federation of Musi
cians.
The combo is well known on the
Stanford campus, having played for
various campus functions and in
local night-spots for the past year
and a half.
Members of the four-man combo
are Frank Hohnan, trumpet; Boh
Feebler, piano; Jim Brcnnan, drums;
and John Fitch, saxophone and
clarinet.
