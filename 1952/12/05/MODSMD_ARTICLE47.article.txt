# El Tigre Will Host West Shrine Team
## 
### 
El Tigre eating club will host the
West team of the annual Shriners'
East-West football game during
their training Dec. 21 to 27.
Under the auspices of the Inter
club Council, the 25 team and five
staff members will eat three meals
a day at the club with a big turkey
and-ham dinner being served' on
Christmas Day.
Chuck Taylor, assistant coach for
the West team, will also be at the
training table with the team.
