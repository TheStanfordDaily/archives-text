# Riding School Plans Horse Show on Sunday
## 
### 
Stanford horse - fanciers will see
an abundance of riding and hoof
talent in action when the Stanford
Riding School presents its quarterly
horse show Sunday at 1 p.m. at tin*
Red Barn.
The planning committee has plan
ned a series of events consisting of
jumping, pairs, and horsemanship.
Trophies and ribbons will be
awarded m all classes, advanced
and beginning, to competitors who
were developed from the riding
classes.
Mrs. VV. CI. Barrett, noted San
Mateo horse breeder, will judge the
proceedings.
