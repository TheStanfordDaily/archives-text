# Cal Tickets Sell
## 
### 
The BAC has announced that
tickets for two basketball games
with Cal will be on sale today and
tomorrow in the Pavilion from 9
to 4.
The tickets, for the Friday game
at Berkeley and the Saturday
game at Stanford, sell for 50 cents.
Students must also obtain tick
ets for the Jan. 12 and 13 games
with UCLA, for which there will
be no charge.
