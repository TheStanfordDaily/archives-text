# Blood Drive Needs Helpers
## 
### 
Planners are now being sought for
the spring quarter Blood Drive, in
tended to be the biggest in Stan
ford's history.
Anyone interested in working on
the planning committee for the
drive is asked to meet Wednesday
at 5 p.m. in the Excom room.
The week-long drive will include
University faculty and staff for the
first time in Blood Drive history.
An attempt will also made to fill a
larger than ever student quota.
Chairman for the drive will be
Ken Schechter, former Navy flyer
now studying at Stanford. The
story of a brush with death Schech
ter had in Korea appeared in the
Saturday Evening Post last fall.
