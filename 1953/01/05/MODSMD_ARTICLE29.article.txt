# Chile Fire Uncovers Red Propaganda Cache
## 
### 

BEFORE HOLOCAUST —Firemen fight fire at Valparaiso, Chile, shortly before the explosion of
tons of dynamite in a powder magazine. Huge bales of Communist propaganda have been
found in the smoking ruins of building where at least 51 persons were killed and 350 injured.
Rescue workers digging for additional bodies found nearly two tons of Red propaganda mate
rial, printed in Russia and Mexico. . —NEA Telefoto
