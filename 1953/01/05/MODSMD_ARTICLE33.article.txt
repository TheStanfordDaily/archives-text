# Stanford Obtains Strauss Original
## 
### 
An original Johann Strauss, 11,
manuscript, bearing the "Waltz
King's" autograph, has been ac
quired! by the Memorial Library of
Music at Stanford.
It is the manuscript of "A Night
in Venice," written about 1883, and
comprising some 418 leaves.
The precious score was purchased
by Dr. Nathan van Patten, profes
sor emeritus of bibliography at
Stanford.
