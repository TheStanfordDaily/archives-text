# Indian Sailors Win Newport Championship
## 
### 
Stanford's sailing team, which has
been runner-up for two consecutive
years, cracked through to win the
eighth annual Pacific Coast Inter
collegiate Championship over the
holidays.
Charles Merrill and Kim Munhol
land made up the Card Class A
team, Clark King and Gwen George
were in the Class B competition, and
Jane Brabyn was in Class C.
