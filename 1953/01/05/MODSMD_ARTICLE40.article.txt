# Library Election
## 
### 
Newly elected members of the
Stanford University Library Staff
Association are Ivan Johnson, vice
president and president - elect for
1954, and Mrs. Anne Scheidecker,
secretary-treasurer.
The Library Executive Committee
will consist of these new officers:
Mrs. Donna Matthews, 1953 presi
dent, and Miss Irene Barquist,
member at large for 1953.
