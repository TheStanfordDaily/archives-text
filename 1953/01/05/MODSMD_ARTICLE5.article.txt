# Benefits Due To GI Alumni
## 
### 
Stanford University recently in
stituted two benefits in recognition
of military service by former stu
dents.
Maturity dates have been extend
ed and interest waived on notes for
tuition and student, loans due from
those who have been called into
service since June 27, 1950. This
benefit will apply throughout the
individual's period of service. The
same procedure was followed dur
ing World War 11.
The University has also extended
its Gold Star Scholarship program
to graduate students. The scholar
ships, which are open to children
of Stanford alumni who died in
World War 11, have heretofore been
available only to undergraduates.
