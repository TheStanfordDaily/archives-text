# Kreps Will Speak At Dames' Party
## 
### 
The annual party for husbands of
members of Stanford Dames will be
held at 8 p.m. Thursday in the up
per lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Guest speaker at the party will be
Dr. Theodore Kreps, professor of
business economics.
Reservations for the party may
be made by contacting Mrs. Jerry
Weide, DA 5-3561.
