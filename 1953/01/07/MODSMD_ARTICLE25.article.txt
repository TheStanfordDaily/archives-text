# Music Professor To Give Recital
## 
### 
Ozan Marsh, head of the piano de
partment at Jordan College of Music,
Butler University, will give a piano
recital at Stanford Friday at 830
p.m. in the Little Theatre, Memorial
Hall.
The recital is the first in a pro
posed series of exchange concerts
or lectures with other institutions
planned by the Stanford Music
Department.
Tickets are $1, on sale at the Music
Department and the office of the
Committee on Public Exercises.
