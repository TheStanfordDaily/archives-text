# Distaffers Set First '53 Meeting
## 
### 
Stanford Distaff Club will hold
its first winter quarter monthly
luncheon meeting tomorrow.
Those who wish to furnish their
own lunches will meet in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
Coffee will be served by faculty"
women sponsors of the club.
Other members will meet in the
Garden Room of the Stanford Un
ion.
