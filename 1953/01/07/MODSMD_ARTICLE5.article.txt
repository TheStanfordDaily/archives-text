# Their Parents to Die?
## 
### 

POSTPONEMENT—ChiIdren of convicted A-bomb spies Julius
and Ethel Rosenberg are escorted from Sing Sing Prison in
Ossining, New York, by Attorney Emmanuel Bloch after a visit
to their parents. The convicted couple's date of execution has
been postponed from the original January 14 date by Judge
Irving Kaufman so that they may appeal for clemency to the
President. —NEA Wirephoto
