# BOOKSTORE HOURS
## 
### 
Today is the last day that the
Stanford Bookstore will be open
from 8 a.m. until 8 p.m. in order to
accommodate the winter quarter
book rush. These hours are for the
convenience of students unable to
purchase books at regular hours.
This system, inaugurated for the
first time last quarter, has great|y
improved the rush problem in the
Bookstore, said the Bookstore man
ager.
