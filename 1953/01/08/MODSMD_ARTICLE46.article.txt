# DOCTORAL CLUB OFFICERS
## 
### 
The Doctoral Club, a social organ
ization for graduate students in the
School of Education, held its quar
terly election of officers last Tues
day.
Officers for winter quarter are:
Arden Ruddell, president; Robert
Russell, program chairman; Velma
Watson, secretary-treasurer; Edith
Merritt, social chairman; and Jack
Murphy, Education Council repre
sentative.
Dr. I. J. Quillen is faculty adviser
to the group.
