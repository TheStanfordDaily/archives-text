# Tryouts Today For One-Acts
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Ram's Head Win
ter Quarter One-Acts will continue
today from 3 to 5 and 7:30 to 10 p.m.
Sn the Ram's Head room.
There are parts for seven women
and nine men in the three one-act
plays to be presented in the Little
Theater on Feb. 19, 20, and 21. Call
backs will be tomorrow.
People are also needed to work
on construction, painting, costume,
lights, sound, and makeup crews.
Interested students may come to
tryouts or ygn up by contacting the
Ram's Head office in the Union this
week.
