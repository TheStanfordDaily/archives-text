# PUBLIC AFFAIRS
## Discussion Group To Meet Monday 
### 
The Monday noon public affairs
discussion group will meet Monday
in the Women's Clubhouse to evalu
ate the past program and to consid
er a possible change in procedure.
"All students who attended last
quarter's discussions are especially
urged to come," said Betty Ann
Cain, chairman of the YWCA public
affairs ' committee, which sponsors
the group.
This will be the first meeting of
the quarter for the coeducational
group open to all students inter
ested in discussing and learning
about public affairs. A hot luncheon
is served for 25 cents.
