# Play Tryouts Set Tonight
## 
### 
Tryouts for the Stanford Players'
production of "The Knight of the
Burning Pestle" will be held tpnight
at 7:30 in Room 205, Memorial Hall.
There are speaking parts for 14
men and four women. Additional
men and women are needed to play
members of the Elizabethan theater
audience. Tryouts are open to all
students. Previous acting experience
is not necessary.
"The Knight of the Burning Pes
tle," which was first produced at
Stanford fifty years ago,' will be
presented as part of the Stanford
Players' subscription series in Me
morial Auditorium on March 5, 6,
and 7. It will be directed by Dr.
Wendell Cole of the Speech and
Drama Department.
The play was written by Beau
mont and Fletcher.
