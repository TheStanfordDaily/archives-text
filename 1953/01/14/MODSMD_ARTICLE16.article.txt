# Education Group Tryouts Slated
## 
### 
Is education your prime interest
at Stanford?
The Student - Faculty Education
Commission, the student - faculty
liaison now working to improve
Stanford education, will hold try
outs for new members today and to
morrow from 3 to 5 in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
The commission, composed of five
students and five faculty members,
studies lower division advising, in
dependent study, language require
ments, departmental appraisal by
seniors, freshman orientation, and
other scholastic problems.
