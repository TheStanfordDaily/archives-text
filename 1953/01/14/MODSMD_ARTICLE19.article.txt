# Minto Plans Anniversary
## 
### 
Preparations are being completed
by Chaplain Robert M. Minto for a
special program on Sunday, Jan. 25,
to commemorate the fiftieth anni
versary of the dedication of Stan
ford Memorial Church.
The program will include the reg
ular morning service, a choral con
cert, and an open house.
The Rt. Rev. Stephen F. Bayne,
Bishop of Olympia, Washington, will
be the guest at the 11 a.m. service
on the anniversary day.
Memorial Church was dedicated
at its first service on Jan. 25, 1903.
