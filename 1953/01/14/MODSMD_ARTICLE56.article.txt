# Marriage Talk Is Tomorrow
## 
### 
A series of informal lectures open
to all men and women students on
marriage and the home will start
tomorrow at 7 on the third floor of
the Women's Clubhouse.
Mrs. William K. Mac Curdy, a
marriage and family expert, will
open the series with a discussion of
"The Purpose of the Engagement
Period."
The other lectures in this quar
ter's series will be "Physical As
pects of Marriage" by Dr. Robert
Dunn of the Palo Alto Clinic; "Psy
chological and Spiritual Adjust
ments" by Dr. Bertha Mason of
San Jose; and a panel of married
students discussing problems young
couples face today.
