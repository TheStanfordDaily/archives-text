# KZSU Plans Two News Services
## 
### 
KZSU, Stanford's radio station,
880 on the dial, is offering two new
campus news services this quarter.
The first is a service similar to
the Daily's "Once Around the
Quad." Announcements of meet
ings may be called in to the station
at DA 2-3971 to be included on
Lucky Strike's "Worldwide Date
lines" at 7:45 and on "News Round
up" at 1L p.m.
The second service is that of
broadcasting ski reports Thursday's
and Fridays t oi> Lucky Strike's
"Let's Look at Sports" at 10:45 p.m.
