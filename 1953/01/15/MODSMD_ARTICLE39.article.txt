# Gallery Exhibit
## 
### 
"Portraits" of the interiors of
homes owned by some of'the most
famous personalities in the United
States are now on display at the
Stanford Art Gallery.
The 25 gouache paintings by Da
vid Payne depict today's tastes and
manner of living. Among the rooms
shown are the living room in the
modern guest house of Mrs. John D.
Rockefeller, 111, and the dining
rooms of Gloria Swanson and Mrs.
Irving Berlin.
