# NO SILK HAT FOR IKE
## 
### 
NEW YORK — President - elect
Dwight D. Eisenhower will set a
precedent by wearing a black Hom
burg instead of a high silk hat at his
inauguration on Tuesday.
Eisenhower's headquarters also
announced that Eisenhower woidd
forego the usual wing collar and
wear a straight collar with his dark
club coat and striped trousers when
he takes the oath as the 34th Presi
dent of the United States.
