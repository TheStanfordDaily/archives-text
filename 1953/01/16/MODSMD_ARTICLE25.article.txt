# February Date Set For Row Formal
## 
### 
Date of the Row dance has been
set for Friday, Feb. 13, 9 p.m. to 1
a.m. It will be held in the Rose
Room of the Palace Hotel in San
Francisco.
Ray Hackett's orchestra will pro
vide the music.
Co-chairmen for the event are
Anne Giannini and Carol Holgerson.
