# Spiegelberg to Address Westminster Fellowship
## 
###

ant professor of Asiatic and Slavic
Studies at Stanford, will speak on
"The Purpose of Man in World
Thought" at 8 p.m. Sunday, at a
Westminster Fellowship meeting at
the First Presbyterian Church, 701
Wavcrley, at Forest.
The Fellowship is under the di
rectorship of the Rev. Richard
Gatchcl.
The meeting will start with ves
pers at 7:15 p.m.
