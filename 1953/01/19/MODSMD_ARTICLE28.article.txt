# Home Nursing Class Will Open Tomorrow
## 
### 
Home Nursing I, designed to
teach the layman simplicity and
safety in home care of the sick,
will begin tomorrow at the Palo
Alto Red Cross Chanter House.
The class is limited to twelve,
and will be given for three weeks
from 2:00 to 4:00 p.m. on Tuesdays
and Fridays.
Students may register by tele
phoning liAvenport 2-214.'{ or by
calling in person at the chapter
house.
