# Sumner Named DPE Sponsor
## 
### 
Delta Phi Epsilon, the national so
cial and professional fraternity for
men planning to enter the foreign
service or foreign trade, has selected
Professor Alfred Rockwell Sumner of
the geography department as sponsor
of the Stanford chapter for next year.
Sumner, an expert on geopolitics,
will discuss the United States posi
tion in world trade with members of
the fraternity on Feb. 3. An exten
sive rushing program will begin on
that date.
