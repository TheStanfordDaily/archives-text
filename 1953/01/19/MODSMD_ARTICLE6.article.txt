# Frosh Talent Show Tryouts Scheduled
## 
### 
Tryouts for the frosh talent
show will be hold in the Wom
en's Clubhouse Thursday at 4:150
and Friday at 4. The show will
be presented the evening of Feb.
11, preceding the frosh stag dance,
and will be held in either Cub
berle.v or Mem Aud.
Applications for tryouts may be
secured from class Excom repre
sentatives, and are to be handed
back no later than Wednesday at
10:30 p.m.
