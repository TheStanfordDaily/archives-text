# Polo Association To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Stanford Polo Association
will hold its first meeting of the
quarter tonight at 7:15 p.m. in the
Nitery. The polo team's plans for
the quarter will be discussed.
The team is contemplating trips to
Santa Barbara during the last
weekend of January and the first
three of February to compete in a
six-goal tourney.
Stanford has received an invita
tion from the National Indoor Polo
Association to compete in the Na
tional Intercollegiate Tournament.
The event will be held in New York
in March.
President Ralph Perlberger said
the group would discuss the invita
tion tonight and invited anyone in
terested in polo to attend the meet
ing.
