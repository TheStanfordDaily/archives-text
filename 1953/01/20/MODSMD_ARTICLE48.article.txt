# Debaters Speak In San Francisco
## 
### 
Four Stanford debaters are
scheduled 1o speak in San Francisco
today on the merits of a Fair Em
ployment Practices Commission.
The Union Square Optimist Club
will play host to Paul Mast, Clint
Wooster, Terry Arnold, and Al
Brouse. Wooster will be the mod
erator.
This is the first of many talks
given by members of the Debate
Society to various local organiza
tions. Today's affair is at the Sir
Francis Drake Hotel. Dr. Hillbrun
ner of the Speech and Drama De
partment is the sponsor.
