# Novelist to Open Intermezzo' Talks
## 
### 
Novelist Jessamyn West, speak
ing on "The Story," will open the
winter quarter Intermezzo series
tomorrow afternoon at 4:15 in the
Bender Room of the Main Library.
It will be her only public appear
ance during her week at the Crea
tive Writing Center here.
Miss West is the author of
"Friendly Persuasion" and"The
Witch Diggers."
