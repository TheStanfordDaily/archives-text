# String Quartet Plays Tonight
## 
### 
The Juilliard String Quartet will
give a concert that will include
compositions by Bartok, Schubert,
and Beethoven tonight at 8:1 fi in
Cubberley Auditorium. Tickets,
which are unreserved and cost $1,
may be obtained at the Memorial
Hail ticket office.
The quartet is composed of Rob
ert Mann and Robert KofT. violins;
Raphael Hillyer, viola; and Arthur
Winograd, 'cello. All four are in
residence at the Juilliard School of
Music in New York, where they
are members of the school's en
semble faculty.
The quartet will play Bartok's
Fifth Quartet.
