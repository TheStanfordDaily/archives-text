# Novelist Will Speak In Intermezzo Series
## 
### 
Jessamyn West will speak on
"The Story" at an informal gather
ing in the Bender Room of the
Stanford Library today at 4 p.m.
Her talk is being presented as
part of the Intermezzo series.
Miss West's novels include
"Friendly Persuasion" and"The
Witch Diggers."
