# TV Series To Feature Rev. Minto
## 
### 
The Rev. Robert M. Minto, chap
lain of Stanford Memorial Church,
will appear on the Stanford tele
vision series tomorrow at 7 p.m.,
channel five.
"Malaya: Tin. Rubber, and Rod
Guerillas" is the subject of this
week's program of the 111-week
scries. Chaplain Minto served in
the British Royal Navy during
World War II as a "colonial chap
lain" in Malaya, Thailand, and
Sumatra.
With him on the program will be
Dr. Claude A. Buss, professor of
history, and Dr. Alfred R. Sumner
of the Geography Department.
