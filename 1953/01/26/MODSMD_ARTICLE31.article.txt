# Speech Clinic Has Class for Stutterers
## 
### 
Bothered with the problem of
stuttering? The Stanford Speech
and Drama Department has organ
ized an adult stuttering clinic
which meets on Tuesday evenings
at the Knoll from 7:30 to 9.
Students may enroll by attending
any of the meetings. A social and
refreshment period follows each
meeting, and individual help is
available in addition.
