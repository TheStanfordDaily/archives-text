# Frosh Talent Show Cast Picked; Script Ready
## 
### 
All tryouts for the frosh talent
show have been held, and the cast
will be announced in Thursday's
Daily. The theme has been chosen,
and the script written.
The talent show will be held on
Feb. 14, before the frosh dance.
Frosh only will he admitted on
show of class cards.
