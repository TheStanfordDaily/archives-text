# VACCINATION CERTIFICATES
## 
### 
Vaccination certificates for all new
students are due at the Registrar's
Office by Monday, Feb. 2, at 4:30 p.m.
Failure to file certificates by this
date will result in a fine of $2.00.
Students failing to file by Monday,
Feb. 9, will be subject to suspension
from the University by the Commit
tee on Public Health.
