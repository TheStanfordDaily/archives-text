# QUAD PHOTOS

taken today, tomorrow, and Thurs
day in the Quad office, from 9 to 12
a.m. and 1 to 4:45 p.m. Thursday will
be the last day.
Students who have not yet been
photographed are urged to come in
early and avoid the last minute rush.
