# Chemist delivers Convention Talk
## 
### 
Dr. Raymond H. Ewell, manager
of the Chemical Economics Service
of Stanford Research Institute, was
featured speaker at the twenty
sixth annual convention of the As
sociation of American Soap and
Glycerine Producers, being held
this week at the Waldorf-Astoria
Hotel, New York City.
Dr. Ewell discussed "The Outlook
for Fatty Acid Raw Materials" on
the opening day's program yester
day. He shared the luncheon speak
ing honors with Lowell Thomas,
noted news commentator.
