# Cards Trade Foes In Weekend Games
## 
### 
The annual independents vs. PCC
round robin gets under way this
weekend as St. Mary's and Santa
Clara tee off against Stanford and
California on respective nights in
the Cow Palace.
The Cards face Santa Clara in the
opener at 8 Friday and the Bears
take on St. Mary's at 9:30. Satur
day night the Cards will switch
teams and times, taking on St.
Mary's in the 9:30 tilt.
