# POLLARD ALL-PRO
## 
###

ace now performing with the Min
neapolis Lakers in the National
Basketball Association, was recent
ly selected on the NBA players' al 1 —
pro basketball squad. Pollard, who
also played in the recent NBA East-
West All-Star game, received eight
points. Only basketball greats who
topped him were Bob Cousy and
George Mikan.
