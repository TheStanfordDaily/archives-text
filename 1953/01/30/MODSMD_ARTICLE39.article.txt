# Britisher to Speak In Sunday Service
## 
### 
Muriel Lester, of London, will
speak on "The Meaning of Prayer"
Sunday in Memorial Church at the
11 a.m. service.
Miss Lester, who is making a
coast-to-coast tour of the United
States, will be speaking in Memo
rial Church for the fourth time.
Miss Lester went to India in 1926
where she stayed for weeks with
Mahatma Gandhi. She has made
several visits to Japan and China in
the course of her six round-the
world tours.
