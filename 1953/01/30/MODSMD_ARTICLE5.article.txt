# Stewart, 'Harvey' To Romp in Flicks
## 
### 
James Stewart and an invisible
rabbit arc the stars of "Harvey,"
the Sunday flick which will be
shown in Mem Aud at 7:30 this
weekend.
Josephine Hull has a supporting
role in the comedy. Short features
will also be shown. Twenty cents
is the admission price.
The two full - length pictures
which were shown last weekend
played to a full house and were so
successfully received that a double
bill will be featured again Feb. 8.
