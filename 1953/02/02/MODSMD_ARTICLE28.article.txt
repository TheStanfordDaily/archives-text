# Cal Is Debating Costs of Union
## 
### 
University of California officials
are now trying to decide how much
they should try to raise for their
proposed student union.
Student groups at Cal have asked
for a building that would cost about
$9 Ynillion. The center would in
clude a large auditorium as well as
recreational and office facilities.
The $9 million figure represents a
jump of $3 million over the $6 mil
lion suggested two years ago by an
ASUC survey committee.
