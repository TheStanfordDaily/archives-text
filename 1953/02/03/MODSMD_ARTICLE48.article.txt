# Grads Plan Coffee Hour
## 
### 
The Graduate Students' Associa
tion will hold its second coffee hour
of the quarter from 3:30 to 5 this
afternoon in the lounge of the Busi
ness School.
All graduate students and faculty
members are welcome to meet each
other informally and discuss plans
for the group's activity.
The Grad Association's schedule
for the quarter includes: (1) coffee
hour, Feb. 25 in the lounge of
Crothers Hall, sponsored by the
Law Association; (2) open house,
March 1 at 7:30 in the Village Au
ditorium, featuring dancing, re
freshments, and games.
The council of the association
will hold its first in a series of
weekly meetings on Monday at 4
in the Women's Clubhouse, to carry
out the business and planning of
the group.
