# MG OWNERS
## 
### 
An MG Club for owners of the
English sport cars and interested
students is being started by Ed
Hagen, Stanford student. Hagen
said the main reason for MG en
thusiasts banding together would be
to promote community trips.
Hagen, campus representative for
British Cars of Burlingame, may be
contacted at DA 3-8760 from 12 to 1
p.m. and 6 to 7 p.m.
