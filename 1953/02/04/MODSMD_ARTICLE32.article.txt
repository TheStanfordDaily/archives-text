# Injured Grad Student Resting Comfortably
## 
### 
George Fowles, geophysics grad
student who was injured in a traffic
accident in Alameda County this
weekend, was "zesting comfortably"
in the Palo Alto Hospital yesterday.
Fowles. who lives in Ogden, Utah,
suffered a fractured collar bone, a
leg injury, and cuts and bruises in
the accident.
