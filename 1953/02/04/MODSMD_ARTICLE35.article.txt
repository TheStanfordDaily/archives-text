# Skiing Week Plan Reservations Open
## 
### 
Reservations for 25 of the 52 Sun
Valley Learn-to-Ski Weeks annu
ally reserved by the Stanford Ski
Club for spring vacation remain on
sale to club members at the ASSU
office.
The widely popular Learn-to-Ski
22-28. includes for $92 a week's pass
on all lifts, six days of lessons by
the nation's most famous ski school,
comfortable accommodations in the
Skiers' chalets, and the renowned
cuisine of the Continental.
Reservations, requiring a $20 de
posit and Ski Club membership
cards, will remain on sale until Feb.
1(5. Further information may be
obtained by calling club president
Jim Hughes at DA 5-2GD4.
