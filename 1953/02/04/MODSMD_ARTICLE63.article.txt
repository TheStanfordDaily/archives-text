# Biologist Honored On 84th Birthday
## 
### 
Congratulatory letters from 70 of
the world's most eminent biologists,
including Julian Huxley of Lon
don's University College, were pre
sented this week to Dr. Charles
Manning Child, Stanford lecturer
and professor emeritus of the Uni
versity of Chicago.
The occasion was his 84th birth
day, which he observed quietly at
home on Feb. 2.
Dr. Child has been at Stanford
since 1939, lecturing and pursuing
research. An internationally re
nowned biologist, he is a specialist
in embryonic development and re
generation. He originated the the
ory of physiological gradients, one
of the most widely accepted ex
planations of the causes of embry
onic development.
