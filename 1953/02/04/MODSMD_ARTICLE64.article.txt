# 'Y' to Feature Marriage Lecture
## 
### 
The "Marriage and Home" series
of the YWCA will feature Dr. Ber
tha Mason who will lecture on "The
Psychological and Spiritual Adjust
ments of Marriage" tomorrow at 7
p.m. on the third floor of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
All students interested are invit
ed to attend. Refreshments will be
served.
Next Wednesday the series will
present Dr. Robert D. Dunn from
the Palo Alto Clinic. He will speak
on "The Physical Aspects of Mar
riage."
