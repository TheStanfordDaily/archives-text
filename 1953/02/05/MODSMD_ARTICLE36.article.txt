# Living Group Elections
## Kappa Sigma 
### 
New officers of Kappa Sigma
were elected last Monday. They are
Marvin Tennefoss, president; Gor
don Galloway, vice-president; Skip
Curl, social chairman; Doug Perry
and Leo Schwaiger, rushing chair
men; Art Shroeder, grand master
of ceremonies; and Ed Mackey,
secretary.
