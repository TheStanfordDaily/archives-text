# Nanney Will Give All-Bach Recital
## 
### 
Herbert B. Nanney, University
organist, will play a program of
Bach music in his weekly organ
recital this afternoon in the Me
morial Church at 4:15.
On the program will be:
Concerto No. 4 in C Major; Par
tite on "Christ, Thou Who Art
the Light of Day"; Chorale Pre
ludes, "Hark, a voice saith. All
are mortal," "Lord Christ, Reveal
Thy Holy Face"; Prelude and
Fugue in A Minor.
