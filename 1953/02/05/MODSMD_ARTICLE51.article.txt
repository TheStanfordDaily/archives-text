# Film Series Sets Comedy Tonight
## 
### 
"A Letter to Three Wives," an
adult comedy, will be shown on the
Film Series tonight at 7:30 in Cub
berley Auditorium. Jeanne Crain,
Linda Darnell, Ann Sothern, and
Paul Douglas star in the movie.
A Film Series membership card,
which will give admittance to the
remaining four films in the series,
may be purchased at the door to
night for $1.80, tax included.
