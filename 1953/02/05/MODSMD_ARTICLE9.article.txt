# Races Planned For Weekend Ski Carnival
## 
### 
Skiers and snow enthusiasts plan
to leave today and tomorrow for Yo
semite Valley where the annual
Stanford Ski Carnival will be held
during the weekend.
Saturday morning skiers will con
vene at Badger Pass for a day of
skiing and other snow sports. Three
big races, the Giant Slalom, Bunny
Racc, and the Obstacle Race are
scheduled as the main events of the
day.
Saturday evening a banquet will
be held at the Ahwahnee Hotel.
Those attending will be entertained
by a group of Stanfordites, and
awards will be presented to winners
of the three races.
Free ski lessons will be given Sun
day for those interested either in im
proving their skill or in learning
how to ski.
