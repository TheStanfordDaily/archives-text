# A 						Phi O to Hold Open Meet Sunday
## 
### 
Alpha Phi Omega fraternity will
hold an open meeting Sunday at
2:30 p.m. at the Bowman Alumi
House. Students interested in join
ing the service fraternity are wel
come to attend.
Captain Curtis Kurtz, A Phi O
adviser, will explain the functions
of the fraternity at Stanford.
According to John Gessford, vice
president in charge of pledges, the
organization is open to all men who
have participated in the Boy Scout
ing movement.
