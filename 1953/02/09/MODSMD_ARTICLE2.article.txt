# Engraved Quad Deadline Nears
## 
### 
Wednesday will be the last day
to purchase Quads with names
engraved on them. They will be
sold from 8 to 4 at Engineering
corner and the Post Office.
The final day for Quad pictures
is Thursday. Anyone who hasn't
had his picture taken yet may do
so any day until then from 9 to
5 at the Quad office. Price is
$1.25.
All salesmen who have Quad
subscription books should turn
them in tomorrow between 12:30
and 4.
