# DEBATE
## War Is Over; Fight Goes On 
### 
The Stanford Debate Society will
delve back into the heated days pre
ceding the American Revolution and
discuss "Should the American Rev
olution Be Suppressed?" in a Debate
Society open house Wednesday
night in Room e7B, Cubberley.
Two Englsh students, Ron Hall
and Derek Howard, currently study
ing under the ASSU schoalrship
plan, will contend that the rebellion
should be squashed. Terry Arnold
and Fred Whitaker will present the
American point of view.
To be conducted in a humorous
and entertaining vein, the debate
will start at 7:30. Afterward, re
freshments will be served.
