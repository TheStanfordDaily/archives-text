# Car Sideswiped In Bayshore Accident
## 
### 
A car driven by Ron Allen, Stan
ford junior and promotion manager
of the Stanford Daily, was damaged
when it was sideswiped on Bayshore
highway north of South San Fran
cisco Thursday night.
According to California Highway
Patrol reports, Allen was driving his
car south when a second car swept
across the middle lane of the high
way, causing the accident.
Allen set damage to his car at $350.
