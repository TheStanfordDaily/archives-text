# Dulles Gives Ike Report on Europe
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 9 (UP) —
Secretary of State John Foster Dul
les returned from Europe today and
promptly gave President Eisenhow
er a "mildly optimistic" report on
prospects for the proposed West Eu
ropean army.
Dulles and Foreign Aid Chief
Harold E. Stassen arrived at 9:30
a.m. (EST), aboard the President's
private plane. They went quickly
to the Whitfc House where they
spent an hour giving Mr. Eisen
hower the "high points" of their 10-
day tour of seven European coun
tries.
"I am encouraged by the situation
in Europe, but see difficulties
ahead," Dulles told reporters.
