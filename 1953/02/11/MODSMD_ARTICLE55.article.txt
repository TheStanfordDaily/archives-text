# Skiers Prep For Opener
## 
### 
Stanford's ski team will compete
in its first meet of the season this
weekend. The four - way meet
which is being sponsored by Mo
desto Junior College will be held at
Dodge Ridge Friday and Saturday.
College of Pacific and UCLA will
also be competing in the meet.
The Stanford team for this
weekend's competition includes
the following: Rufus Morgan, Boh
Armstrong, Mickey Wirth, Dave
Clark, Irving Thaiberg, and Doug
Collins.
Captain Doug Collins announced
that extensive tryouts would also
be held this weekend for team
berths since the present team is not
necessarily permanent.
Among top contenders for team
berths Collins named Jim Howell,
Mike Adams. Paul Stewart, Bob
Volk, Chick Ehrhorn.
