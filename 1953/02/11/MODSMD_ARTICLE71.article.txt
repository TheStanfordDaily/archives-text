# Bill Needham Named Cellar Banner Manager
## 
### 
Nancy Clark, chairman of Public
Relations announced Friday that Bill
Needham had been appointed Ban
ner Manager. Needham will be in
charge of the large display banner
in front of the Cellar.
Student organizations desiring to
use the banner space for advertising
may contact Needham on Tuesdays
10-11 and Wednesdays 1-2 at the
ASSU office.
