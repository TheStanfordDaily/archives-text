# Interfaith Council Sets Sunday Panel
## 
### 
Interfaith Council will sponsor a
panel discussion and question ses
sion on "Interfaith Co-operation" at
this Sunday's meeting of the Sun
day Afternoon Club at 4 in Bowman
Alumni House. The meeting is open
to anyone interested.
The moderator of the panel will
be John H. Trueman, instructor in
history. The panel members will
represent the Protestant, Catholic,
Jewish, and Baha'i faiths.
