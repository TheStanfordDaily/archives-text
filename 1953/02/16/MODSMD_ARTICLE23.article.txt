# Who Is Rosen?
## 
### 
To the Editor:
Critic Ron Rosen seems blissfully unhampered by
any knowledge of music in his Friday review of the
De S;ibata concert. It is too bad the "cheering" audi
ence did not appreciate the full horror of the con
ductor's "superficiality."
We all know De Sabata . . . who is Rosen?
KAY ALLEN REVA FELDMAN
JUDY PUGMIRE ANN PARSONS
VIRGINIA BELL VIRGINIA FENTON
ANNE GIANNINI PAT SILLIMAN
JUANA RATHBUN VIRGINIA SCHURZ
