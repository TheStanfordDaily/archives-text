# Engineer Award Won by De Nevers
## 
### 
Noel Dc Nevers has been named
the year's Outstanding Junior in
Chemical Engineering by the Stan
ford Chapter of the American In
stitute of Chemical Engineers.
De Nevers, a resident of Stern,
received the award from Jerry Mil
ler, president of the student chap
ter of the society. The award con
sists of an engraved certificate, a
membership in the AICE, and a pin.
