# Mothers' Club to Meet Tomorrow at Lagunita
## 
### 
The Stanford Mothers' Club will
meet tomorrow at 2 at Lagunita
Hall to hear a panel discussion of
various phases of interest to the un
dergraduate student on the Stan
lord campus. The meeting will be
under the direction of Miss Elva
Brown, Chief Counselor of Women.
Jeanne Bobis, former AWS presi
dent, wll conduct the discussion, and
panel members will include Micki
Beller, Dawn Backus, Lola Sullivan,
Doris Feigenbaum, Nancy Howe,
Connie Feeley, and Jan Hanson.
Reservations may be secured by
contacting Mrs. Hex Harlow, DA
3-7384.
