# Debaters Will Go To Cal Tech Meet
## 
### 
Stanford debaters travel to Cal
Tech this week to vie with speakers
from all Western colleges for the
regional championship. The winners
of the contest will go to the national
debate championships to be held at
West Point.
The team will leave for Pasadena
Thursday, the debates taking place
Thursday evening and all day Fri
day and Saturday.
Entered in the upper division
men's debate from Stanford are
Terry Arnold and Fred Whitaker,
Dave Jacobson and Chandler My
ers, and Bill Hosier and Clint Woos
ter. In the upper division women's
are Elizabeth Gonser and Jinx Pat
terson, and in the lower division
men's are Carl Weidman and A 1
