# Prom Ticket Sale To Begin in Today
## 
### 
Class of '54 excom members will
sell tickets for the Junior Prom in
living groups today. Price is $3.75.
The all-campus dance will be held
Friday. March (i, at the Bay Meadows
Club House from 9 to 1. Dress will
be formal.
Ralph Flanagan and his orchestra,
featuring the Young Sisters and the
Singing Winds, will make his first
appearance on the West Coast for
the event.
