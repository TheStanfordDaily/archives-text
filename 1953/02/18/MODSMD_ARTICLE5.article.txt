# Gallery Shows American Art
## 
### 
The American Section of the In
ternational Fine Arts Exhibition re
cently circulated in Japan is now
on display at the Stanford Art Gal
lery.
The exhibition was sponsored by
the Mainichi Newspapers of Japan
to encourage friendship and cultu
ral understanding between the East
and West.
The paintings were selected by
the American Federation of Arts as
examples of the wide diversity of
approach and the variety of techni
cal expression current in the United
States. Thirty-one contemporary
American artists are represented.
