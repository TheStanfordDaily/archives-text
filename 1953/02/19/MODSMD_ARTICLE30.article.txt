# Frosh Coed Named Phi Sig's Queen
## 
### 
Jo Kegley, Roble freshman, was
named the 1953 Phi Sigma Kappa
Moonlight Girl at a formal dance
held in her honor at Raneho Rafael
last Saturday night.
Selected as her attendants were:
Jenny Johnson, Lagunita; Ann
Hoag, Branner; Helga Biermer, La
gunita; and Mimi Walker, Lagunita.
