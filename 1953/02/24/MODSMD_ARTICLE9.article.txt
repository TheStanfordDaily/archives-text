# Miller? DeMille? Mills? Dutch Have Your Tour
## 
### 
The Netherlands National Tourist
OfTice is organizing a unique wind
mill tour. Only per ons whose
names indicate that their forefa
thers' professions had some con
nection with mills may participate.
Those with names like Miller. Mills,
Millis, DcMille, Du Moulins, Muhl
er. etc, will be qualified to take
these tours.
