COME TO THE PROM—These five fetching females are all set to attend the junior Prom which
will be held March 6 at the Bay Meadows Clubhouse. Holding the signs are Mardi Ruch and
Ann Poulson; in the front row are Ruth Nusbaum, Judy Thomas, and Chris Johnson. Tickets
for the Prom will go on sale at the Cellar today from 10 to 2. Price is $3.75 per couple.
—Daily photo by Don Hoffman
