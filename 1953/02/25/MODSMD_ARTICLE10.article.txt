# Famous Farm Botanist Dies
## 
### 
Dr. Douglas H. Campbell, 93, one
of the world's most distinguished
authorities on botany and a mem
ber of Stanford's first staff of in
structors, died Monday night at his
home on campus. He was professor
emeritus of botany here.
Dr. Campbell came to Stanford
from the University of Indiana with
Stanford's first president, Dr. David
Starr Jordan, for the opening of the
University in 1891.
Dr. Campbell, long recognized as
a world authority on the structure
and reproduction of mosses and
ferns, wrote many volumes on bot
any. The last, published in 1940, was
"Evolution of the Land Plants."
