# Indian Consul To Talk Here
## 
### 
M. Azim Husain, consul general
of India in San Francisco, will speak
at Stanford tomorrow at 2:15 in
Room fi of the Education Building.
"The Place of Philosophy in the
Indian Educational System and Its
Future" will be his topic. The talk
will be given at a meeting of Pro
fessor Frederic Spiegelberg's class
in Indian philosophy. The public is
invited.
Husain assumed the duties of
consul general in July of last year,
after spending three months in the
United States studying the adminis
tration of American foreign policy.
