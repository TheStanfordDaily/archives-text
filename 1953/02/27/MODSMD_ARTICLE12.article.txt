# Debaters Head for Forensic Tourney
## 
### 

TRAVELING DEBATERS—Here is the debate team traveling to USF tomorrow to participate
in the Forensic Association Debate Tournament. They are (left to right): Glen West, Chandler
Myers, Bill Hosier, Pete West, Clint Wooster, Don Webster, and Mr. Hillbruner, adviser to
the group. The debates will be conducted Lincoln-Douglas style. Also attending the debate,
but not pictured, are Dave Jacobson, Marilyn Myers, Jinx Patterson, and Elizabeth Gonser.
Representatives from ten Bay Area schools will attend the tournament.
—Daily photo by Don Hoffman
