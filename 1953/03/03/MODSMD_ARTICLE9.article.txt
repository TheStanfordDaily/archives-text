# Tribute Services For Dr. Campbell Will Be Tomorrow
## 
### 
Stanford will pay its final tribute
to Dr. Douglas H. Campbell, a
member of the University's pioneer
faculty, at services in Memorial
Church on the campus at 4 p.m. to
morrow.
President Wallace Sterling will
deliver a memorial address. Chap
lain Robert M. Minto will conduct
the service, and Herbert B. Nanney
will be the organist.
Dr. Campbell, emeritus professor
of botany, died last week. He was
93 and the last survivor of the 15
professors on the faculty when
Stanford opened in 1891.
