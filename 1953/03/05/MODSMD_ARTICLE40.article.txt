# Lake Lag Rules Announced
## 
### 
1. No one except Stanford students shall be allowed on the boat
house or in Lake Lagunita.
2. No swimming or canoeing when life guards arc not on duty.
3. There shall be no swimming beyond the rafts at any time.
4. Canoes and boats should not be overloaded.
5. No non-swimmers shall be allowed in the canoes.
6. No one shall change places in a canoe or boat off shore.
7. No running on the boathouse deck.
8. All living groups are warned to lock all canoes to racks, and keep
their paddles in lockers provided.
9. Name of owner MUST be painted on canoes.
10. Do not leave valuables in the dressing rooms or in the boathouse.
11. Power boats of any description are forbidden on Lake Lagunita.
