# A 						Phi O Book Sale Will Open Today
## 
### 
I J
Alpha Phi Omega's Book Exchange
will be open to receive and sell
books today, tomorrow and Wednes
day from 9 a.m. to noon and from 1
to 4 p.m. in Room 10f», main floor
rear of the Main Library.
The Exchange accepts students'
books and places them on sale
during these three days. Later in
the quarter, the exchange will re
open to return either money or
books to the students who put them
up for sale. Prices are set on the
basis of the book's condition.
