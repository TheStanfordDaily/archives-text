# Casting Begins For 'Frisco '49' Today
## 
### 
Tryouts for the 1953 Spring Show,
"Frisco '49," will be held tonight,
tomorrow night, and Thursday
night from 7 to 10 in the Little
Theater. Those students who tried
out last quarter for singing parts
are asked to attend the tryouts and
bring their own music.
Parts are open for singers, danc
ers, straight actors, and crew mem
bers.
