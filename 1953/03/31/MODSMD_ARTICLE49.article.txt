# Charity Nets $3743 From Farm Drive
## 
### 
A total of $3,743 was collected this
year in one of Stanford's most suc
cessful annual winter charity drives.
More than 400 solicitors were en
rolled from Stanford service groups
to contact students in their resi
dences.
Donations are being divided be
tween the Red Cross and the World
University Service. The World Uni
versity Service, until this year known
as the World Student Service Fund,
channels aid from American univer
sities and colleges to those in 32
countries.
