# Expert Will Talk On Flu Epidemics
## 
### 
Bay Area residents who suffered
through the recent "flu" epidemics
will have an opportunity to hear an
authority discuss the disease tomor
row afternoon at 1:30 at the Stan
ford School of Medicine.
Dr. Ernest Jawetz, associate pro
fessor of microbiology at the Uni
versity of California Medical
School and an expert on respira
tory diseases, will lecture in Lane
Hall, Sacramento Street near Web
ster, in San Francisco. It will be
open to the public without charge.
His lecture is titled "Influenza:
Past and Present." He will trace
development of medical knowledge
about the disease, including some of
its more puzzling aspects.
