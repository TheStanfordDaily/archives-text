# Air Force ROTC To Be Reviewed
## 
### 
A group of officers from the Air
Force ROTC Headquarters in
Montgomery, Ala., will arrive at
Stanford Thursday to conduct a
two-day federal inspection of the
Farm Air Force ROTC unit.
The inspection will include a pa
rade and review by the entire Air
Force cadct corps of 700 students.
The public is invited to attend the
parade, which will be held on the
soccer field starting at 3:15 p.m.
