# CAGE STAR VOSS DIES
## 
### 
Ed Voss, star center on the 1942
Stanford NCAA basketball cham
pions, died in Oakland of polio last
weekend. Voss starred for the
Cards from 1941 to 1943, and also
played for the Oakland Bittners and
the Ouklafid Blue and Gold AAU
teams. He also appeared in the
Stanford alumni game Feb. 3.
