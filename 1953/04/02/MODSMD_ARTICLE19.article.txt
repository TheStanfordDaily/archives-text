# Con Home Brunch Chairman Sought
## 
### 
Freshman women interested in
being general chairman of the an
nual Strawberry Brunch should
come to the "Y" office on the third
floor of the Women's Clubhouse to
morrow between 3:15 and 4:30 and
Monday between 3 and 4.
The Strawberry Brunch, part of
Con Home Week in May, is an an
nual project of the freshman wom
en. On this occasion freshman
women serve strawberry shortcake
and coffee in Union Court. The
proceeds go to the Con Home.
