# Air ROTC Parade To Be Held Today
## 
### 
A group of officers from the Air
Force ROTC Headquarters, Mont
gomery, Alabama, headed by Col.
Axel E. Altberg, will be at Stanford
today to conduct a two-day federal
inspection of the Air Force ROTC
unit here.
At 3:15 today, the entire Air
Force cadet corps of 700 students
will stage a parade and review for
the visiting officials. The parade
will be held on the soccer field and
the public is invited to attend.
