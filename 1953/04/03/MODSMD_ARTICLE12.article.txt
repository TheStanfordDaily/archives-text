# Professors Attend Health Convention
## 
### 
Mrs. Maud Knapp, professor of
physical education and director of
the Women's Physical Education
program, and Professor John Nixon
of the Education Department at
tendee] the annual convention of the
California Association for Health,
Physical Education and Recreation
which ended its four-day session at
Fresno State College yesterday.
Mrs. Knapp is president-elect of
the CAHPER for the 1953-54 year,
while Nixon was chairman of the
section on Responsibilities of Lead
ers.
