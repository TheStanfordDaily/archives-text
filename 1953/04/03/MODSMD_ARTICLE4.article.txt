# Alpine Club Plans Practice Climb
## 
### 
The Alpine Club will hold a prac
tice climb Sunday at Miraloma
Park. Meeting time and place arc
10:30 a.m. at History corner. Be
ginners are welcome.
To qualify for the two spring
quarter Yosemite trips, members
must attend three practice climbs
and demonstrate, at the end of that
time, an ability to climb and handle
ropes, according to Rowland Tabor,
president.
