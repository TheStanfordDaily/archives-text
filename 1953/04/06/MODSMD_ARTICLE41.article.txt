# CHARLIE HICKOX WINS
## 
### 
Charlie Hickox, number one man
on the Stanford Tennis team, scored
one of the top upsets in the first
round of play in the 56th annual
Bay Counties Invitational Tennis
Tournament at the Olympic Club
Saturday.
Hickox downed Joe Wolfson of
Hayward, who was seventh seeded,
8-6, 4-6, 6-1.
Another Farm netter, Ait Fish,
bowed to second-seeded Harry Li
kas of San Francisco, 6-2, 6-1.
The tournament will conclude
next Sunday.
