# 'POLOISTS LOSE
## 
### 
A Stanford team lost to Central
Valley, 10 to 4, Sunday in the open
ing game of the San Francisco Polo
League at Golden Gate.
The Indian team, given a* two
goal handicap, held their foes even
throughout the first half, which
ended 4-4 all. Central Valley, how
ever, poured through two goals in
each of the last three chukkers to
clinch the game.
Bob Smith and Palmer Jackson
were the only members of the Stan
ford team to score, while also riding
were Alan Ross and Carl Walters.
Jack Conant led the winners with
three goals.
