# TAFT ON TIDELANDS
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, April 7 (UP) —
Senate Republican Leader Robert
A. Taft served notice today that he
will keep the Senate hard at work
until it votes on the "tidelands" bill
to establish state ownership of sub
merged coastal lands.
