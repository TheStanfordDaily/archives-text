# Talent Show Set For Tomorrow
## 
### 
Twelve acts will be presented in
the Frosh Talent Show tomorrow
night at 7:30 in Memorial Audi
torium.
The 40-minute production has a
cast of 50 and consists of music,
dancing, and comedy acts. The show
is open to the public free of charge.
Background music will be pro
vided by Spike Lynch's band.
Among the acts featured will be
the "Freshman Girls' Trio," "Poor
Little We," "Stanford's Johnny Ray,"
dance numbers, and an elaborate
grand finale.
Liz McCollister is chairman of the
show.
