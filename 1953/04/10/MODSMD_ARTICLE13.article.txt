# Sunday Night Flick To Offer O. Henry
## 
### 
O. Henry's "Full House" is the
Sunday night movie this week.
Starting time is 7:30 p.m. Sunday.
Five of the short stories of Wil
liam Sidney Porter are presented:
"The Cop and the Anthem," "The
Gift of the Magi," "The Last Leaf,"
"Carillon Call," and "The Hansom
of Red Chief."
