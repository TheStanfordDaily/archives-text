# Junior Class Cops Crew Race
## 
### 
The junior class shell, stroked by
Jerry Thursby, won the annual in
terclass crew race at the Palo Alto
Yacht Harbor Wednesday. The class
of '54 finished the course one length
ahead of a freshman shell.
One and a half lengths behind the
frosh were the seniors, while an
other length back was the sopho
more shell.
Thursby held a pace of 30 strokes
per minute most of the way.
