# Club to Meet At New Time Sunday
## 
### 
Sunday Afternoon Club will hold
its first meeting this quarter Sun
day at a new time, 12:30 p.m., in
Bowman Alumni House. The Rev.
Alexander Miller, lecturer in reli
gion, will speak on "Is There a Real
Basis for Faith?" as part of the
club's exploration of faith this
quarter. Refreshments will be
served, and anyone interested is
welcome.
