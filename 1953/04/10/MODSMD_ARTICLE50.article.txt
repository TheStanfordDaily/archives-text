# GSA Open House Sunday at Village
## 
### 
The Graduate Students' Associa
tion will hold an open house and
dance in the Village Clubhouse
Sunday from 8 to 11 p.m. Cards
for membership in the Association
are now on sale for 25 cents at the
GSA booth in the Pavilion.
Future activities planned by the
Association include May Day Play
Day at Mangini's on May 1, and a
coffee hour on May 21.
