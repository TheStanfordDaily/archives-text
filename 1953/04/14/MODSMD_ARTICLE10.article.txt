# Stillman Show Set For Tomorrow
## 
### 
Still man House will present a
show, "Old Faces of '53," tomorrow
night at 7:30 featuring a melodrama,
a variety act, and a can-can line,
"The Gaslight Girls." Refreshments
will be served.
Admission to the show is 25 cents.
Proceeds will be contributed to the
Women's Row Scholarship Fund.
