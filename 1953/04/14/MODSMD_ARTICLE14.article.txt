# Stern's Formal Set for Saturday
## 
### 
Mobiles, modern art, and Bobby
Anderson's band will be featured in
Stern Hall's all-campus formal Sat
urday night, "Mobilaya."
Two hundred bids, to be ex
changed for miniature mobiles, are
being sold for $2 each in the ASSU
and Stern offices. The dance will be
held from 9 to 1 in the hall and the
adjacent patio. There will be re
freshments and entertainment at.
intermission. Ed Cook is chairman
of the dance.
