# Con Home Games Signups Will End
## 
### 
Signups for the Con Home tour -
naments end at dinnertime tonight.
Individuals may sign up in their
living groups today for competition,
in horseshoes, bridge, men's volley
ball (two- and sixman), women's
volleyball (six-man), and ping pong
(singles and doubles).
Fees for each event will be 50
cents per person, and must be paid
to living group athletic managers by
tonight. Individuals may enter
more than one event, and living
groups may enter more than one
team in each event.
