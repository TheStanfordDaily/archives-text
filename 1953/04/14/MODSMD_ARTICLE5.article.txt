# POW Convoy Rolls
## 
### 
TOKYO, Tuesday, April 11 The
Communists announced today at
Panmunjom that two more convoys
totaling 45 vehicles will leave prison
camps along the Yalu River Wed
nesday morning bringing Allied
.sick and wounded prisoners to free
dom.
All air attacks along the "road to
freedom" weer halted by the United
Nations today as 20 Red - flagged
ambulances and trucks began mov
ing down through North Korea with
the first Allied captives released
from Red prison camps.
