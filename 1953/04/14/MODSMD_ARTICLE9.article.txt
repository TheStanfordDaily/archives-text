# School to Be Held For Yell Leaders
## 
### 
All men interested in running for
head yell leader or trying out for
assistant yell leader must attend a
school to be given by Zeke Wallace
tomorrow night at 7 at the Sigma
Nu house.
Recommendations necessary to
become a candidate will be given
out at the tryouts.
Wallace emphasized that men try
ing for assistant yell leader jobs, as
well as those planning to run for
head yell leader, must attend.
