# Summer Work Proposed by 'Y'
## 
### 
There will be a meeting of all
students interested in "Y" summer
projects at 4:30 tomorrow in the
YWCA office, third floor of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Programs offered for the tummer
include study in universities
abroad, work camps and commu
nity service abroad and in the
United States, intei-nships in indus
try and government, work in insti •
tutional service units, international
student seminars, and leadership
training schools.
These projects are sponsored by
the National Student Council of the
YWCA and YMCA, the American
Friends Service Committee, and the
Institute of International Educa
tion. In some of the projects then
is an opportunity to earn money,
while in others there is a nominal
cost for which scholarships arc
available.
