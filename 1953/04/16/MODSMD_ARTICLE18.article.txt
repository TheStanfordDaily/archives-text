# Stern Formal Sat.
## 
### 
The modernistic architecture of
Stern Hall will be combined with
modernistic decorations Saturday
night to set the scene for Stern's
spring all-campus formal, "Mobi
laya."
Matt Kahn is directing the ad
vanced art classes in the construc
tion of the decorations, which will
include eight large mobiles to be
suspended from the ceilings. Bob
by Anderson's band will provide
music for dancing both inside the
hall and in the open patios.
Bids arc on sale at the ASSU and
Stern offices for $2. Dinner jackets
or tuxes arc appropriate for the
dance.
