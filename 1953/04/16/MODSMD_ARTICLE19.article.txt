# Alpha Sig Dudes
## 
### 
The annual Alpha Sig Dude
Ranch Dance will be presented this
Saturday from 0 to 1 at 534 Salvn
ticrra. The dance is open to the en
tire campus and no admission fee
will be charged.
A Western band will supply mu
sic. and roulette and chuck-a-luck
will also be offered to the attending
couples. •
