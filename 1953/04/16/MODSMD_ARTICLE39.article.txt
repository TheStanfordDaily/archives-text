###

Moon" are on the bill for tonight's
Thursday Revival Series, starting at
7:30 in Cubberley.
"Intolerance," made in 1916, is
one of the masterpieces of D. W.
Griffith. "A Trip to the Moon" was
filmed in 1902 and is one of the
earliest commercial pictures ever
produced.
