# Your Blood Turned Into Plasma
## 
### 

ANOTHER STEP — Under the watchful eyes of a laboratory
worker, blood plasma is fractionated in a 250-gallon centrifuge.
Mixed with alcohol, plasma is spun at high speed until liquids
are removed. The room is kept at 35 degrees during fraction
ating operation.


BEING READIED FOR KOREA—Before the dried plasma is her- j
metically sealed in a metal container, the instruction label and
tape sling are attached to the bottle. Companion bottle of sterile
water and intravenous injection set complete the dried plasma
"kit."
