# Frosh Entertain Santa Clara Nine
## 
### 
• ' I
The Santa Clara frosh baseballers
provide the opposition this after
noon for the Stanford frosh at 3:15
on the Stanford diamond.
Coach Dick Clegg's freshmen en
tertain Burlingame High School to
morrow afternoon at 2:30 on the
Sunken Diamond.
Coach Dick Clegg's Stanford
freshman baseballers entertain Bur
lingame High School tomorrow af
ternoon at 2:30 on the frosh dia
mond.
Clegg will go with his usual start
ing lineup with his starting
moundsman yet to be announced.
