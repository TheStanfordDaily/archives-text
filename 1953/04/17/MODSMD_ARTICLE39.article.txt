# Students Attend Union Conference
## 
###

Committee will meet at the Uni
versity of California today for the
30th annual conference of the As
sociation of College Unions.
ASSU President Dick Weismann,
Union Planning Committee Chair
man Jack Fraser, Marty Trieb, and
Pete Bing will attend a workshop
on the building of new college un
ions.
