# Rules Announced For Women's Pool
## 
### 
Regulations governing the use
of the women's swimming pool
were announced yesterday by the
Women's Athletic Department.
They are:
1. Persons who may use the
pool on weekends arc: Stanford
men and women, their wives or
husbands or male guest; a Stan
ford woman and her mother and
her sister, 18 years old or older,
or a college student.
2. A Stanford woman may have
two guests on weekends.
The right to refuse admittance
to the pool is reserved.
