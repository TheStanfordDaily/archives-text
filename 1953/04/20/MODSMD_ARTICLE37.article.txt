# 1953 Sponsoring Applications Due
## 
### 
Junior and senior women who
want to sponsor next year must pick
up applications today or tomorrow
at the ASSU office. They must be
filled out and returned by tomor
row. Appointments for interviews
may be made when they are re
turned.
Applications for men sponsors are
also available at the ASSU office.
They should be returned to the En
cina office before Friday.
