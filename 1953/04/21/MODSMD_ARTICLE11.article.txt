# Hospital Reports O'Hara Improving
## 
### 
The condition of Ron O'Hara,
Stanford freshman who was seri
ously injured in a head-on collision
on Alpine Road Sunday, was listed
as "improving" by Palo Alto Hospi
tal last night.
O'Hara will remain in the hospital
for several days. He was operated
on Sunday night for compound
fractures of the arms and legs and
head injuries.
A San Jose woman was killed and
five other persons were injured in
the accident. Police reports said the
accident occurred when the car
driven by the San Jose woman was
attempting to pass another car and
struck O'Hara's Studebaker.
