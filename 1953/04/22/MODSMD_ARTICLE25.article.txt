# Biology Major Given Science Fellowship
## 
### 
Richard Eppley, Washington State
College senior in botany, has been
awarded a $1,400 National Science
Foundation fellowship which will
enable him to enroll for graduate
work in botany.
At Stanford, Eppley is to work
under the supervision of Dr. C. S.
French, director of the Division of
Plant Biology of the Carnegie In
stitution of Washington, located on
the Stanford campus.
