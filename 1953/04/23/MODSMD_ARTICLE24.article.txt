# Blanks Available For ASSU Post
## 
### 
Students interested in applying
for the position of ASSU Director of
Public Relations for 1953-54 are
invited to submit applications, in
writing, on or before May 1, 1953.
Application blanks are available
in the ASSU and Pub Rel offices.
The position is open to students
who will have junior, senior, or
graduate standing during the aca
demic year 1953-54.
To gain information regarding the
responsibilities and duties of the
position, a conference with the
present director is suggested.
