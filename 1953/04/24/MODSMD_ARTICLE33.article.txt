# LAG. VENTURA COFFEE HOURS
## 
### 
Lagunita and Ventura will hold
a coffee hour after the Sunday
night movies this weekend. The
open houses, for Lag and Ventura
girls and their dates only, will last
from 10 to 11:30. p.m. Coffee and
doughnuts will be served, and there
will be records for dancing.
