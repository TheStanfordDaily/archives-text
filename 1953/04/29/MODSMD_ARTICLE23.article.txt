# German Club Plans House Possibility
## 
### 
A reactivated German Club will
meet tonight at 7:30 in Bowman
Alumni House. The group will dis
cuss possibilities of obtaining a Ger
man House or International House
as a residence for German language
majors.
Carole Chasin is chairman of the
meeting.
Interested students are invited to
attend.
