# GUADALAJARA
## Summer School 
### 
An accredited bilingual summer
school sponsored by University
of Guadalajara in co-operation
with Stanford University faculty
members will be held in Guada
lajara, Mexico, june 28-Aug. 8,
1953. Offerings include art,
folklore, histcjry, language, and
literature courses. $225 covers
six-week tuition, board and
room. The Stanford professors
participating will be Aurelio M.
Espinosa, Sr., Daniel Mendelo
witz, and juan B. Rael. Consult
Prof. Rael, Room 400, Physics
Bldg., daily at 9 and at 2.
