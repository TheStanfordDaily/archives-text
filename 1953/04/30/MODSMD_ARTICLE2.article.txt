# Stanford Lawyers To Meet Hastings
## 
### 
Stanford law students, victorious
in their first - round Inter - Law
School Moot Court battle, are pre
paring to take on a Hastings Law
School Monday.
The trial will be the first to be
held in the moot court room of the
new Hastings building in San Fran
cisco.
The eventual winner of the
Northern California division, which
also includes Santa Clara and the
University of San Francisco, will
meet the top Southern California
team next October.
Forrest N. Barr and John J. Hop
kins, third-year Stanford law stu
dents, out-argued University of Cal
ifornia representatives Wednesday
evening at Stanford.
