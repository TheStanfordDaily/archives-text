# Hawaii Greets
## 
### 
HONOLULU Another "Freedom
Airlift" plane landed here today
with 41 Americans released from
| Communist prison camps.
Dusky hula girls swaying to the
music of ukuleles and guitars pro
vided the traditional Hawaiian
greeting for the second plane bring
ing disabled ex-prisoners back to
their homes or U.S. hospitals.
A brilliant Hawaiian sunset cast
its rays on Hickain Field as the big
double-deck C-97 transport landed
at 5:03 p.m. (8:03 p.m. PDT).
