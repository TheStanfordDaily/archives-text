# Tenor to Appear In Concert Series

### 
William Olvis, tenor, will present
a concert in Memorial Auditorium
at 8:15 next Monday evening, un

cert Series. This will be the last
concert presented by the Series this
quarter.
Olvis was awarded the Atwater-
Kent Prize in 1949 when he was 29,
and then made his dobut as the star
of a special operatic scries under
the supervisioif of Dr. Carl Ebert.
Olvis's wife, Norma, is his accom
panist.
Tickets for the Olvis concert arc
available at the Concert Scries of
fice in the Nitery, which will be
open today from 1 to 5 p.m., 9 to 12
a.m. tomorrow, and 1 to 5 p.m.
Monday afternoon. Tickets will also
be sold at the door on the night of

and $2.50.
