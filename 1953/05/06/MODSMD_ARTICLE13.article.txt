# House Drawing
## 
### 
House drawing will be held
downstairs in the Women's Club
house tomorrow from 8 a.m. to 5
p.m., and third-quarter freshmen
and any women wishing to change
residences may draw.
Cards for listing housing prefer
ences will be given out. at the
drawing. Students may draw
alone, in pairs, or in threes.
Placements will be made accord
ing to class and number. The
women's housing ratio reserves
one-half of each house for sen
iors, one-third for juniors, and
one-sixth for sophomores.
