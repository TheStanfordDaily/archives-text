# Baird Is New Health Officer
## 
### 
Dr. Glenn H. Baird is the new
Stanford University health officer,
it was announced yesterday by
President Wallace Sterling.
He succeeds Dr. George S. Luck
ett, emeritus professor of hygiene,
and will serve, in a non-faculty ca
pacity, as director of the University
Public Health Service. Professor
Rodney R. Beard of the Stanford
Medical School has been acting
health officer since Dr. Luckett's re
tirement last Sept. 1.
