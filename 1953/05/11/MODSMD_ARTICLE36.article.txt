# Swimmers Top Bruins
## 
### 
Shuddering against a chilling
wind, Stanford's varsity swimming
team won easily over UCLA, (53 to
23, Friday.
Outstanding Tor Stanford in the
dual competition were Dan Osborne,
winner in the 220- and 440-yard
freestyle races; Larry Heim, who
beat Jim Gaughran by two feet in
the 100 freestyle in 52.8; and Lee
Gunnerson, who turned in a best
mark of 24.5 to win the 50 free
style.
The Indians finish up their season
with a dual meet with California for
both the varsity and the freshmen
at Berkeley this week, and the Pa
cific Coast Conference, Southern
Division, Championships at USC on
May 21, 22. and 23.
