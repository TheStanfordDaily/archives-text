# Class of '53 Plans High Jinks
## 
### 

STEADY NOW—Four members of the class of '53 pound in some "Burma-Shave" signs by
way of publicity for the annual senior class reunion, The '53 Roundup, to be held this Friday
at Pink Horse Ranch all afternoon and evening. Baseball, volleyball, and swimming are on
the schedule for the afternoon. In the evening there will be dancing.
—Daily photo by Verne Dunn
