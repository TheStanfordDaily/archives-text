# Men May Apply For Stern, Toyon
## 
### 
Village and off-campus men in
terested in living in Stern or Toyon
Hall for autumn quarter should
sign up Friday between 8 and 5.
Cards will be available in the Hous
ing office, Room 101-A, Inner Quad.
Men may sign up for both Stern
and Toyon, but must indicate their
first choice. Club members and men
who have not completed 45 units of
work or who have not been in at
tendance spring quarter arc not eli
gible for the sign-up.
The sign-up for transfer of room
deposits from spring to autumn
quarter will be held in all campus
residences on Thursday. All cards
must be signed by that date. Men
failing to fill out transfer of room
deposit cards will lose their room
assignment for autumn quarter.
