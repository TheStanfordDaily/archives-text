# Cotton to Address Young Republicans
## 
### 
The Young Republican Club will
meet tonight at 7:30 at L'Ommie's.
Speaker will be Aylett Cotton,
member of the San Mateo County
Central Committee. Cotton was the
first member of the Young Republi
can organization in the county, and
was campaign manager for Vice-
President Nixon in the last elec
tion.
His subject will be "Republican
Politics in the Coming Year."
