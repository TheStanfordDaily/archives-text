# San Jose to Stage 'Grass Harp' Here
## 
### 
The San Jose State speech and
drama faculty will stage a reading
of Truman Capote's "The Grass
Harp" Friday and Saturday nights
at 8:15 in the Little Theater. The
reading will employ music and a
narrator.
Tickets for the program are 50
cents for students arjd 75 cents for
adults.
