# Newman Club Will Hold Steak Bar-B-Q
## 
### 
Newman Club, at 1247 Cowper in
Palo Alto, will hold its annual
spring steak barbecue this Sunday
from 4:30 to 7 p.m.
Games, volleyball, races, and en
tertainment are planned for the
event, which will be held on the
back lawn of the clubhouse.
Tickets may be bought at the AS
SU office or from living group rep
resentatives for $1.50.
