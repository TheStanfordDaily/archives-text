# Guest to Perform In Organ Program
## 
### 
Valerian E. Fox, oruan instructor
at the University of Portland, and
music director at St. Thomas More
Church there, is to play a varied
program of organ music in the Stan
ford Memorial Church next Monday
at 8:30 p.m.
His program will include works
by Purcell, Bach, N. E. Fox, Alain,
Widor, Dallier, Torres, and Rhein
berger.
