# Social Co-ordinator
##

cial co-ordinator will be held to- |
morrow and Wednesday from 2:15 I
to 4 in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse. A sign-up sheet
for time of interview will be posted
in the ASSU office.

is to mediate between the women
students and the* ASSU Social Co
ordinating Board. Junior women
with a C average are eligible for I
this position.
