# Bid Sales to Open For Senior Prom
## 
### 
Bids for the Senior Prom on June
11 will go on sale next week. Price
will be $10 per couple and will in
clude the dance bid, breakfast, and
use of the facilities of the Mt. Diablo
Country Club.
"Music in the Morgan Manner"
will be played for the seniors by
Russ Morgan and his band.
The dance starts at 10 p.m. and
ends at 4 a.m. but breakfast, swim
ming and other activities will con
tinue through the afternoon.
