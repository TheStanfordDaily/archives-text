# Thurman to Speak At JC Graduation
## 
### 
Professor Samuel 1). Thurman,
Jr., acting dean of the Stanford
School of Law, was named as speak
er at the 34th annual Menlo Com
mencement on June 5, by Dr. Wil
liam Kratt, president of Menlo
School and College.
Louis B. Lundborg, vice - presi
dent of the Bank of America and a
Stanford graduate, will also speak
at the graduation.
Thurman will address the school
division at 10:30 and Lundborg will
speak at the college commencement
at 2 o'clock. The school and college
will confer 13G certificates, diplo
mas, and degrees upon its graduates
at the outdoor ceremonies.
