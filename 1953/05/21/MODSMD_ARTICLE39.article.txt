# Practicing for Concert
## 
### 

Members of the Stanford concert band are practicing for their an
nual band concert, to be presented a week from Sunday in Memorial
Auditorium. Starting time is scheduled for 8:15 p.m.
Under the direction of Director Julius Shuchat, the band will pre
sent a varied program of concert selections and marches.
Music to be presented will include "The Universal Judgment," and
"Royce Hall Suite."
As in previous years, there will be no admission charge to the
concert.
