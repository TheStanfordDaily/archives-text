# Law Students Gain Moot Court Finals
## 
### 
Four second - year law students
have reached the finals of the Moot
Court competition.- John Sutter,
Dick Graham, Lou Sackin, and Dick
Konig will compete for the $100 first
prize in autumn quarter.
Alternates selected were Morris
Ormseth and Ross Wilkey. Judges
for the contest will be California
judges yet to be named.
