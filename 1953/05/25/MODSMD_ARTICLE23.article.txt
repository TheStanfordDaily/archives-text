# Paly Senior Wins Farm Math Award
## 
### 
Peter Stauffer, Palo Alto High
School senior, has recently been
awarded a $660 scholarship to Stan
ford as first prize in the Stanford
University Competitive Examina
tion in Mathematics, which was held
April 11.
Stauffer won from a field of grad
uating high school students from
California. Oregon, Washington, and
Arizona. This was the first year the
examination was given outside of
California. Twelve honorable men
tions were also awarded.
