# ACADEMIC FREEDOM
## 
### 
Discussion on the problems of aca
demic freedom will be conducted to
day by the ASSU Academic Freedom
Committee, at 3:15 p.m., on the third
door of the Women's Clubhouse.
The various aspects of the problem
in relation to the rights and re
sponsibilities of students and faculty,
and the possible effect of Congres
sional inquiry on the intellectual in
tegrity in universities will be ex
plored.
