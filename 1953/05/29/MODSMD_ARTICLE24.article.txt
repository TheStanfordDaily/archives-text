# SENIOR DIRECTORIES
## 
### 
Senior directories, containing ad
-1 dresses, class oration, history, will,
i and poem, will be sold for 75 cents
on Tuesday in all upper class living
residences.
They will also be sold outside the
Education Building Tuesday and on
' Business School corner Wednesday.
