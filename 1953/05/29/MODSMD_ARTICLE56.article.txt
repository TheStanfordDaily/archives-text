# AWS Conference Scheduled in Fall
## 
### 
The AWS Leadership Conference
will be held on the Stanford campus
Sept. 24. The initial conference will
be from 7 to 9 p.m. in the Bowman
Alumni House, at which Dean VVin
bigler will give a talk on leadership,
and Jeunie King will outline AWS
activities.
On 25, the conference will
meet ut 8:30 a.m. in the Women's
Clubhouse and will break down into
discussion groups. There will be a
banquet at 11:30 in Union at which
President Sterling will be the guest
speaker.
