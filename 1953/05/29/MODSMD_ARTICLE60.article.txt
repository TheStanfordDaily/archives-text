# Seniors Wend Way to Last Blast
## 
### 

SENIOR BALL PLANNERS—Enjoying the activities that will be
featured at the Mt. Diablo Country Club before the Senior Ball
on June 11 are Mcl Butler, Rosemary Woods, and Phyllis Smith.
Bids, at $10 a couple, will be on sale at the ASSU office until
the day of the Ball and will also be sold at the door. The Ball

is open to all classes and is the one occasion when corsages are
in order. Russ Morgan will play from 10 to 2 with Howard
Frederics taking over until 4. Breakfast, at no extra charge, will
then be served. Above map shows the route to the club.
