# Three Food Spots Open on Campus
## 
### 
The Cellar, the Union Cafeteria,
and Encina Commons will be open
this summer to serve students. The
Cellar will maintain visual hours
and the cafeteria will be open Mon
day through Friday and Sunday
evening through the dinner hour.
Tickets for Encina Commons may
be purchased at either the Toyon
or Crothers office.
