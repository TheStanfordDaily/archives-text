# Ford Foundation Gives Farm $49,500
## 
### 
The Ford Foundation has made
grants totaling $249,500 to five lead
ing American universities to enable
them to undertake self-surveys of
their training and research pro
grains for the scientific study of
human behavior, it was announced
Sunday by Bernard Berelson, direc
tor of the Behavioral Sciences Divi
sion of the Foundation.
A. grant of $49,500 was made to
Stanford University and $50,000
each to the University of Chicago,
Harvard University, the University

of Michigan, and the University of
North Carolina. The grants are for
the academic year 1953-54.
