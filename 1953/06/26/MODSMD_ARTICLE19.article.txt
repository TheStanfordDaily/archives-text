# Budapest String Quartet to Play
## 
### 
The Budapest String Quartet will
give a concert of chamber music
at 8:15 p.m. Wednesday, July 1, in
Cubberley Auditorium of the Edu
cation Building.
The world-famous quartet is com
posed of Joseph Roisman and Jac
Gorodetzky, violins, Boris Kroyt,
viola, and Mischa Schneider, violon
cello.
The program for their Stanford
recital will include selections by
Haydn, Bartok, and Ravel.
Tickets are on sale at the Memo
rial Hall box office from 9 a.m. to
noon, and 1 to 5 p.m. (DA 3-0665)
and at the Peninsula Box Office in
Palo Alto (DA 2-2122). Prices are
$1.50 for general admission and $1
for students.
The concert is sponsored by the
Stanford Committee on Public Ex
ercises.
