# ‘Good Earth’ Is Film Series Show
## 
### 
Another famous movie of social
import. "The Good Earth," starring
Paul Muni and Luise Rainer, will
be shown this Thursday evening at
the Revival Film Series in Cubber
ley Auditorium. Show time is 7:30.
Admission is by membership in
the Film Society. Membership tick
ets may be purchased for $IXO at
the Memorial Hall box office and at
the door.
