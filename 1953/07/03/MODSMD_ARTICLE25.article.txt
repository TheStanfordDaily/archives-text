# Planetarium Trip
## 
### 
Sign-ups at the ASSU office for
the San Francisco Planetarium trip
Thursday night, July 9, close Tues
day at 5. Studnts, staff, faculties,
and their families are eligible for
the sign-up. The $1.85 fee, payable
at time of sign-up, includes round
trip transportation and admission to
the Planetarium.
