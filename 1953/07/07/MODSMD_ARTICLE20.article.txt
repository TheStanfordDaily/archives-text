# Farm Counselor Revises Book
## 
### 
Dr. Henry A. Berman of the Stan
ford University Counseling and
Testing Center is the author of a
revised editon of "How to Improve
Your Study Habits," published by
Pacific Books of Palo Alto.
Originally written by Dr. Samuel
N. LeCount of the College of Marin,
the manual is designed primarily to
help college students improve their
techniques of reading and study.
Dr. Berman has added two new sec
tions on how to improve spelling
ability and how to build a better vo
cabulary.
