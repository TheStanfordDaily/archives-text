# Slides to Review Stanford History
## 
### 
Stanford University's history will
be reviewed by slides tomorrow at
5 p.m. in the vestry of Memorial
Church by the Memorial Church
Committee.
It will be the second time that the
valuable slides will have been
shown on campus. They depict the
growth of Stanford and include
many scenes taken before the 190G
earthquake when many campus
building's were destroyed.
Following the showing of the
slides, the committee will hold a
short Bible study session and then
idjourn at 9 p.m. in time for the
Candlelight Service in the church.
