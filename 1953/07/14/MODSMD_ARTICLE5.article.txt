# Haskey to Talk On Education
## 
### 
Dean Laurence D. Haskey. visiting
professor of education, will discuss
"Moral and Spiritual Values in
American Education" tomorrow at
A p.m. at an all-School of Education
assembly in Cubberley Auditorium.
All education classes will be dis
missed during the lecture.
Dr. Haskey has been Dean of the
College of Education, University of
Texas, since 1947. In 1940 he served
as technical consultant on the Pres
ident's Commission of Higher Edu
cation.
Currently he is chairman of the
Southwestern Co - operative Pro
gram in Educational Administration.
