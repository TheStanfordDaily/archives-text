# Biz Conference Opens Monday
## 
### 
A new record attendance of over
450 is expected at the twelfth an
nual Stanford Business Conference
which opens Monday, according to
Dean J. Hugh Jackson of Stanford's
Graduate School of Business.
Built on the theme "New Dimen
sions in Business Management," the
program of talks by leading busi
ness executives and educators from
the Pacific Coast and the nation is
drawing registrants from all parts
of the state.
Erwin D. Canham, editor of the
Christian Science Monitor, is the
keynote speaker. He will address
the opening session of the confer
ence each day, Monday through
Friday.
