# Thieves Pilfer NROTC Rifles
## 
### 
Two rifles were stolen from the
Navy ROTC armory in the base
ment of the Education Building
sometime Wednesday night, police
reported yesterday.
The rifles were securely locked in
a special cabinet along with six oth
er guns, Police Chief Gordon Davis
said.
He said that the thief, or thieves,
had forced three padlocks before
getting to the guns.
