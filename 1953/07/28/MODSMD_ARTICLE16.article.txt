# KZSU Begins Two Week Schedule
## 
### 
KZSU, Stanford's radio station,
hit the airwaves for the summer
yesterday, and will continue broad
casting for two weeks, promising a
variety of programs including Dix
ieland and drama.
Manned by students of the 11th
Summer Radio-Television Institute,
the station will be on the air from
7 to 11 p.m., Monday through Fri
day. The station can be heard in
j living quarters around the campus.
