##

Robert Lop e r of Stanford's
Speech and Drama faculty will read
;i one-act play by George Bernard
Shaw, "The Admirable Bashville,"
;it 4 p.m. Thursday in the Little
Theater. The reading hour is open
to all without charge.
Loper came to Stanford recently
from the Shakespeare Institute at
Stratford-on-Avon, England, where
lie had carried on graduate study
for the past two years. He holds
A.B. and A.M. degrees from the
University of Colorado.
The play, a humorous account of
the career of an early-dav prize
fighter, was written in blank verse
by Shaw and parodies the plot of
one of his youthful novels
