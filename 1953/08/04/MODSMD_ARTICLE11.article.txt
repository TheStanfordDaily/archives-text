# Frolik-Bristol Win Doubles Tourney
## 
### 
Jim Frolik and Roberta Bristol
swept the finals of the mixed doub
les tennis tournament when they
defeated seeded duo Ken Burton
and Virginia Grose in three sets
Sunday, 7-5, 4-U. 7-5.
Match play was close and it was
a service break in the last set that
won for Frolik and Bristol.
In the final set. Frolik and Bristol
won the first four games and then
lost the next five games to trail 4-5.
It was then that they were able to
break through Burton's service and
win the next three games and the
match.
The finals climaxed a month's
competition in match play.
