# Tulsa Play Director Will Lecture Here
## 
### 
Theodore Viehman. director of
the Tulsa Community Theater of
Tulsa, Oklahoma, will deliver a lec
ture on "Opportunities in the Amer
ican Community Theater" at 4:15
p.m. today in the Little Theater.
The lecture, under the auspices of
the Stanford Department of Speech
and Drama and the Stanford Play
ers, will be open to the public with
out charge.
