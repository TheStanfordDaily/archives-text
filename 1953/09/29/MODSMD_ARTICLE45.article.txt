# University Singers To Hold Rehearsal
## 
### 
The first rehearsal of the Univer
sity singers will be held at the choir
room of Memorial Church at noon
Thursday.
Anyone interested in joining this
| small choral group, specializing in
madrigals, chansons, and carols,
! should contact Herbert B. Nanny,
the director, at his office. Room 1325,
The Knoll, during registration. Pre
vious choral experience is desirable.
The group meets twice a week for
rehearsal and one unit of credit may
be earned for participation.
