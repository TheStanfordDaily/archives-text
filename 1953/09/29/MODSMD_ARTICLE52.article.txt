# Betas Fined $1,000 For Booze Party
## 
### 
The Beta Theta Pi fraternity was
found guilty of violating the campus
liquor prohibition at a party held
at the house during graduation
week in June and was fined $1,000
by the intcrfraternity Board, meet
ing July 20.
Five hundred dollars of the fine
was suspended on good behavior.
The action brings to three the
number of fraternities cited by the
IFB for breaking the drinking ban.
The other two were Delta Kappa
Epsilon, which was suspended for
five quarters, and Sigma Nu, sus
pended for three quarters.
