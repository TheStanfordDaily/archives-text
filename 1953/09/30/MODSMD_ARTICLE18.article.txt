# Stanfordite Buys Oregon Bulletin
## 
### 
Robert W. Chandler, '53, yesterday
announced that he had purchased
the Bend, Oregon, Bulletin and will
take over as editor and publisher
tomorrow. Purchase price was not
announced.
Chandler, who was a member of
the Class of '40, returned to Stan
ford last spring to receive his A.B.
in Journalism. He was also editor
of the Stanford Review last year.
A veteran of World War 11, Chan
dler has also been Northwest busi
ness representative for United Press,
worked on the business staff of the
Denver Post, and done public rela
tions work in Denver. More re
cently he was on the public relations
staff of the Richfield Oil Company.
