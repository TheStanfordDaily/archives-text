# Band
## 
### 
Positions are open for all instru
ments in the Stanford Band, man
ager Jim Killen anounccd yesterday.
The band, one of the three top
marching bands on the Pacific Coast,
is especially in need of trumpet and
cornet players.
Band is listed as Music 161 and
is given for one unit credit. Those
interested in joining are requested
to bring a change-of-program card
to the first rehearsal, tomorrow night
at 7 p.m.
The bandhoase will be open from
10 to 12 a.m. and from 3 to 4 p.m. to
day for uniform issue.
