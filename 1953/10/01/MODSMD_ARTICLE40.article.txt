# Freeway Addition Will Open Today
## 
### 
Ceremonies sponsored by the San
Francisco Chamber of Commerce at
11 a.m. today will mark the opening
to traffic of approximately two more
miles of the Bayshore freeway be
tween Army and Bryant Streets.
J. W. Willard, 111, Chamber presi
dent, will preside at the brief cere
monies at which Mayor Elmer E.
and Frank B. Durkee,
State Director of Public Works and
chairman of the California High
way Commission, will officially open
the new freeway by severing a chain
with a cutting torch.
