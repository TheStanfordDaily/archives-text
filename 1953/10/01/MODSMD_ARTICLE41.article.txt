# Indian Aviators To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Indian Flyers, Stanford's un
official flying club, will hold their
first meeting of the quarter tonight
at 7:30 in Room 276, upstairs in
Engineering Corner.
Students interested in joining the
club are asked to attend the meet
ing. Membership is open to all
ages, classes, and sexes.
This quarter the Flyers plan to
initiate ground school instruction
which can be carried through to
the commercial pilot level.
Club officers a/e: Jack Knoblock,
president; Bill Black, vice-presi
dent; Bill Albee, treasurer; and Pat
Gillies, secretary.
