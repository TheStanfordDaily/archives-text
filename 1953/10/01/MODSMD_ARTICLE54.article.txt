# Lag Coeds Prepare for Farm Fair
## 
### 

FARM FAIR IN THE MAKING—Six of the hardworking Lagunita residents currently preparing
for Saturday's Farm Fair are shown with a collection of fittings for the Fair's many booths. From
the left, the coeds are Sue Hirst, Melba Mamiya, M. K. Getzoff, lo Ginevra, Gail Erickson, and
Arleen McDougall. The Farm Fair will be held from 8 to 12 p.m. Saturday night in the Lagunita

court, and will feature Dean Winbigler as Fair auctioneer.
—Daily photo by Don Cheu
