# Tryouts Set For Campus Activities
## AWS COMMITTEE 
### 
AWS committee interviews begin
this Monday from 2 to 3:15 on the
second floor of the Women's Club
house, and continue Tuesday from 4
to 5:30 and Wednesday from 2 to
3:15.
Applicants will be interviewed for
the election (seniors only), housing,
| social regulations, and scholarship
| committees.
These committees will be made
up of members from seven areas on
campus — Lagunita, Roble, Union,
Women's Row. Ventura, Branner,
and Off-Campus.
