# S.F. Symphony Ticket Sale Is Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tickets for the 42nd season of the
San Francisco Symphony Orchestra
will go 011 sale tomorrow noon at
the Nitery.
The Stanford Symphony Forum
has announced they will be sold on
a strictly first - come, first - served
basis. Six tickets will be the maxi
mum lor one person, except for
blocks in large women's residences.
Tickets for individual perform
ances may be bought and sold dur
ing the year through the ASSU
Concert Scries Board.
Stanford students will be offered
over 300 tickets at half the normal
price. Rates for the 18 Thursday
evening concerts are as follows:
Grand Tier, Dress Circle, and Or
chestra—s22.so.
Balcony and Balcony Circle—sl7.
A few seats in the front rows of
the orchestra will be on sale for $9.
Leopold Stokowski will conduct
the first concert on Nov. 12 in the
San Francisco Opera House.
