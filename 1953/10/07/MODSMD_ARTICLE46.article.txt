# Folkdancers Club To Meet Tonight
## 
### 
i.
The Stanford Folkdancers will
hold their first meeting tonight
from 8 to 10 p.m. in the Women's
Gym. It is open to ail who are in
terested.
Folkdancers meet every Wednes
day night at the Gym and every
Friday from 8 to 11 p.m. in the
Village Auditorium. Expericneed
dancers are invited to join the ex
hibition group which meets Wed
nesdays at 7:30 p.m.
