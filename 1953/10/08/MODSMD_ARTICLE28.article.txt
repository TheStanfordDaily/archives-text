# FINE TIME HAD BY ALL
## 
### 
NEW YORK, Oct. 7 (UP)—Base
ball Commissioner Ford Frick re
vealed today that he had fined
Brooklyn manager Chuck Dressen
and Yankee outfielder Irv Noren
$100 each for separate outbursts
during the World Series.
Dressen drew his fine for arguing
with plate umpire Art Gore on Phil
Rizzuto's foul tip in the fourth
game of the Series at Ebbets Field.
