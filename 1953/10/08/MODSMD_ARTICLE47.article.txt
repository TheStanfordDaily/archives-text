# PASSENGER BUS BURIED
## 
### 
OSLO, Norway, Oct. 7 (UP) —A
mile-wide avalanche of earth and
rock crumbled of! a cliffside above
Oslo fjord today, burying a passen
ger bus on a highway and destroy
ing a railroad bed just ahead of a
train. Nine persons were hurt.
