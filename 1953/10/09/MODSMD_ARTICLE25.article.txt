# Church Plans
## Miller to Preach 
### 
The Rev. Alexander Miller, lec
turer in religion at Stanford, will
preach the sermon on "Allergy and
Atrophy" at the 11 a.m. Memorial
Church service on Sunday.
Professor Harold C. Schmidt will
direct the Memorial Church Choir
in anthems for the service, and
Professor Herbert B. Nanney will
be at the organ.
Following the service a coffee
hour, sponsored by the YWCA, will
be held at Bowman Alumni House.
