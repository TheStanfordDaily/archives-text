# Hospital Reports Hanson Improved
## 
### 
Don Hanson, Stanford sophomore
who was critically injured in an au
tomobile wreck on Alpine Road Oct.
1, was said to be "improving" by
authorities at Palo Alto Hospital
yesterday.
They pronounced his condition as
"favorable" and said he is slowly
regaining consciousness.
The accident, in which Hanson
was injured, claimed the life of Alec
Gerbodc, a member of Alpha Sigma
Phi fraternity.
Two other persons were injured in
the crash.
