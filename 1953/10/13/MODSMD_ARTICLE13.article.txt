# Quad Continues Freshman Photos
## 
### 
Quad photographers will continue
taking pictures of freshman men
through the i-est of this week, Quad
Editor Ralph Buchwalter announced
yesterday.
Buchwalter has emphasized that
freshman men must appear at En
cina at the time for which they have
signed, as the photographer will re
main in Encina only long enough I
for men who have made appoint
ments. Broken appointments, he
said, will delay the publication of I
the Froshbook. scheduled to appear |
before Big Game.
