# Edwards Named Trustee Emeritus
## 
### 
Paul C. Edwards, associate editor
of the San Francisco News, yester
day completed a 10-year term on
the Stanford Board of Trustees and
was elected a trustee emeritus by the j
board.
Mr. Edwards, a resident of Los j
Altos, served as president of the
board for five years ending last May.
He is also a past president of the
Stanford Associates and of the Com
monwealth Club of California.
M. C. Sloss of San Francisco is
the only other trustee emeritus of
the board. He served from 1920 until
his retirement in 1951.
