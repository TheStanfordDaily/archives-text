# Folkdancers Plan To Dance Saturday
## 
### 
A square dance with live music
will be held tomorrow at 8 p.m. at
Lee's Ranch, sponsored by the Stan
ford Folkdanccrs.
Everyone is invited to join the
old-fashioned square dance. Willy
(Hogjowls) Grishaw will call, to the
music of his band. Admission is 25
cents a couple; the same price stag.
Lee's Ranch is in Los Trancos
Woods, one-fourth mile past Ros
sotti's. Maps are available on Quad
in the pockets of posters publiciz
ing the dance.
