# Ex-Stanfordite Killed in Crash
## 
### 
Roy "Bud" Moe, former outstand
ing golfer at Stanford, was killed
Saturday night when his auto rolled
over on Sandhill Road.
Moe, who last played on the Card
team in 1950 and represented Stan
ford in three national champion
ships, came from an outstanding
golfing family.
Married to Janet Hall, he played
second man lor three seasons. In
his final year two fine performances
on a wet course helped Stanford to
a PCC championship.
No further information was avail
able on the accident, which occurred
at 10:50 p.m.
