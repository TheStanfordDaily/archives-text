# Mrs. Sterling To Host Faculty Club
## 
### 
Mrs. J. E. Wallace Sterling, wife
of the University president, will be
hostess at the opening meeting of
the Newcomers' Section of the Stan
ford Faculty Women's Club this af
ternoon at 2.
Mrs. Sterling will also give some
impressions of her European tour
this summer. Harold C. Schmidt,
associate professor of music, will
lead some music students in choral
readings. Honored guest will be
Mrs. F. M. McFarland, who founded
the Newcomers' Section in 1930.
