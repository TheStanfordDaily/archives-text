# Education Group To Hold Interviews
## 
### 
Interviews for the Student-Fac
ulty Education Commission will be
held tonight at 7:30 in the courtroom
of the Women's Clubhouse.
Purpose of the commission is to
bring about closer understanding
between students, faculty, and ad
ministration on the problem of at
taining a Stanford education. It
deals with such subjects as lower
division advising and requirements,
student - faculty relations, and the
Honor Code.
Juniors are especially urged to
try out.
