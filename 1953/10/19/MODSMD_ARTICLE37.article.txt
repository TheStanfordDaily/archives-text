# Interviews Slated For IIR Conference
## 
### 
Interviews for students who want
to be on the Stanford delegation to
a conference on "Plans for Perma
nent World Peace" will take place
today, tomorrow, and Wednesday
from 4 to 5 p.m. at the lIR Shack.
Fifteen delegates will be chosen
for this conference, which will be
held by the University of California
on Nov. 5-7 at the Berkeley campus.
