# Frosh Book Pictures Scheduled by Quad
## 
### 
Frosh Book photographs will be
taken today, tomorrow, and Wed
nesday for Rohlc Hall residents
and ofT-campus freshman women.
Photographs of Branner women
will be taken on Thursday.
Quad Editor Ralph Buckwalter
promises that the Frosh Book will
be out by Thanksgiving, if no
unforeseen obstacles arise.
Because of rising printing and
operating costs, this year's Frosh
Book will cost $1.25 rather than
$1.03 as in the past.
