# IOWA STATE RIOT
## 
### 
AMES, la., Oct. 19 (UP)—lowa
State college students rioted for the
second night Monday when again
denied a "Skip Day" to celebrate
the Cyclones' 13-6 upset gridiron
victory over Missouri Saturday.
About 4,000 shouting students
gathered on the campus.
