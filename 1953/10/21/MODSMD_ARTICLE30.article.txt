# Slide Rule Course To Start Tonight
## 
### 
The first of three lectures on the
use of the slide rule will be given
tonight at 7:30 p.m. in Room 208,
Engineering Building.
The lecture will cover multiplica
tion, division, and solving of propor
tions by means of the slide rule.
Subsequent lectures will deal
with problems of trigonometric
scales, square and cueb roots, and
logarithmic scales.
The course, presented yearly by
Tau Beta Pi, national engineering
honor society, will be administered
by George Bahrs, Bob Levin, and
Neal McKenzie, members of the
Stanford chapter of the society.
They will give lectures and assist
students in working sample prob
lems.
