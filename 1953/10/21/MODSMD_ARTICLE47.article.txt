# Fulbright Award Deadline Is Oct. 31
## 
### 
Students planning to seek 1954
Ful bright scholarship awards are
urged by Associate Registrar Elmer
C. Wagner to start working on their
applications immediately.
The applications are due Oct. 31,
Wagner stated, and he pointed out
that some time is required to as
semble the recommendations, tran
script, health record, and other re
quired data.
Graduate students or those who
will receive the A.B. next June are
eligible. Additional information and
application forms may be secured at
the associate registrar's office.
