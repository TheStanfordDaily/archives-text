# Workman at Lab Suffers Hip Injury
## 
### 
Ben Belk, an employee at the W.
W. Hansen Microwave Lab. suffered
a broken right hip yesterday when
he was hit by a traveling overhead
crane.
The accident occurred about 1
p.m. Belk, who works as the sig
nal man for the cement-filling crane,
hunched down as it passed over
him, but he misjudged the clearance.
