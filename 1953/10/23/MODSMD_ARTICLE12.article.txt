# United Nations Week Celebrated
## 
### 
United Nations Week celebration
on campus reached its climax last
night in an assembly in Cubberley.
Today information posters about
the United Nations organization will
still be displayed in the Main Li
brary and around Quad.
Some 30 foreign students ate at
various women's residences last
night to put the celebration of the
U.N. birthday at a more personal
level.
At last night's meeting Bob von
Pagenhardt talked about the United
Nations, Jackie Whiteman regarding
her stay in Norway with the "Ex
periment in International Living,"
and Don Lozabnick of his meetings
in Istanbul and Berlin.
