# African Educator To Visit Stanford
## 
### 
T. B. Davie, principal and vice
chancellor of the University of Cape
Town, South Africa, will be a visitor
on the Stanford campus tomorrow.
Dr. and Mrs. Davie, escorted by
President Wallace Sterling, will in
spect the campus in the morning
and then be honored guests at a
luncheon at the Sterling residence.
The University of Cape Town
came into existence in 1918. The
usual language of instruction is
English, but some first-year courses
in science arc duplicated in Afri
kaans.
