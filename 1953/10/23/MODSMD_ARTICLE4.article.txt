# ATO Hog Wallow Set for Tomorrow
## 
### 
ATO's annual Hog Wallow, all
campus dance, will be held tomor
row night from 8 to 12 in Rudy's
Alpine Inn barn.
Main features of the dance will
be the music of Bobby Anderson's
Novelty Band and the presence of
Peaches VII. the current ATO mas
cot. Admission to the dance is free.
