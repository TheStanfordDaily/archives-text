# GAIETIES BLOCK TICKETS
## 
### 
Block seat tickets for the annual
Ram's Head Gaieties go on sale to
day. .Sales will end Nov. 3. The
show is scheduled to run Tuesday
through Friday, Nov. 17 to 20. The
number of block tickets for the last
two performances is limited.
Prices are 75 cents, $1, and $1.25.
Tickets may be obtained in Ram's
Head office, DA 2-0579.
