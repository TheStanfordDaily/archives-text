# Block Seats on Sale For 1953 Gaieties
## 
### 
Sales are continuing today for
block seats to the Ram's Head Gai
eties. Sales will end Nov. 3. The
production is slated to run Tuesday
through Friday, Nov. 17 to 20. The
number of block seats to be sold for
the last two performances is limited
to insure good seats for single ticket
holders. Block seats are not limited
to Stanford groups.
Prices are 75 cents, $1, and $1.25.
Tickets may be obtained in Ram's
Head office, daily from 3 to 4, DA
2-0579.
