# UCLA Schedules Two Operas Here
## 
### 
Two one-act comic operas will lie
given here Nov. 14 by the UCLA
Opera Workshop tour, it was re
cently announced by Professor Hu
bert Crouse Heffner of the Speech
and Drama Department.
The operas are Gluck's "The Un
formed Drunkard" and "Trouble m
Tahiti," a modern satiric work by
Leonard Bernstein.
Tickets for the operas, sponsored
here by the Stanford Players, am on
sale now at the Memorial Audito
rium box office for $1.
