# '57 Open House
## 
### 
LOOKING AHEAD —Five Roble coeds set the theme for Sun
day night's Roble Open House, " '57 Futurism," with an invi
tational poster for all freshmen. The freshman dorm will be
open from 9 to 12 for dancing, card games, and refreshments
and will be decorated in an abstract, futuristic style. Members
of the above welcoming committee are Sandra Sue Carlson,
Lynne Grubb, Les Heche, Bev Brown, and Marcia Jewel.
— Daily photo by Don Cheu
