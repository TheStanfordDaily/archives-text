# Dames Will Hold Card Party Tonight
## 
### 
\
The Stanford University Dames
have planned an evening of bridge
in the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse tonight at 8. All stu
dents' wives are cordially invited to
attend. Mrs. Charles Gowens and
Mrs. Robert Lueders, co-chairmen
of the Dames bridge committee, will
serve refreshments.
Anyone needing transportation
may call Mrs. Ned Bowler at DA
3-3941, if living in Stanford Village,
or Mrs. Wilbur Ebersold, DA 5-5805,
if living outside of the Village.
