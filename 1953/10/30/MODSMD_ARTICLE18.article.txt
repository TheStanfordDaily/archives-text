# New IIR Officers Chosen This Week
## 
### 
Thornton Garrett was elected
vice-president, and Bill Bosworth,
executive secretary of the Institute
of International Relations, this week.
Both Garrett and Bosworth are
senior political science majors and
members of Los Arcos eating club.
Garrett has worked with the lIR
since his sophomore year. He head
ed the ASSU Election Board last
year. His new duties include rep
resenting the lIR on ASSU Execu
tive Committee and Social Co-ordi
nating Board, as well as assisting
lIR president Norm Sweet, who was
elected last spring.
Bosworth joined the lIR in his
freshman year.
