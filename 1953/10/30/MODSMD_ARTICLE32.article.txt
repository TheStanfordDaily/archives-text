# Grad Students Will Hold Party Tonight
## 
### 
The Graduate Student Associa
tion will hold its first dance of the
year, a Halloween party, at Pink
Horse Ranch from 9 to 1 tonight.
Dress for the affair is levis and
"loud" shirts for the men and cot
ton for the girls.
There will be a 25-cent charge for
members and a 50-cent charge for
non-members. The dance is open
only to grad students and their
5 guests.
