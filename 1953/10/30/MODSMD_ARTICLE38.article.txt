# Sunday Service Theme To Be All Saints' Day
## 
### 
The Rev. R. M. Minto, chaplain of
the University, will deliver the ser
mon for All Saints' Day at the 11
a.m. service this Sunday at Memo
rial Church. His topic will be "Our
Heritage of Heroes."
Chaplain Minto will conduct a
communion service in Memorial
Church at 9 a.m. Sunday, and the
YWCA will sponsor a coffee hour at
Bowman Alumni House immediate
ly after the 11 a.m. service.
