# 'Mlle' Reporter To Visit Stanford
## 
### 
Miss Rachel Mellinger, campus
reporter for Mademoiselle magazine,
will visit here Wednesday and
Thursday to tell women undergrad
uates about Mademoiselle's 1953-54
national College Board.
A student must be a member of
the College Board to compete for a
guest editorship. Each June 20 guest
editors work in New York to pre
pare the August College Issue.
Miss Mellinger will also tell Stan
ford English professors about Made
moiselle's Annual College Fiction
Contest, open to undergraduate
women.
