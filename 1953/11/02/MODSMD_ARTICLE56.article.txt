# Grant Given For African Research
## 
### 
Three members of the Stanford
Food Research Institute will study
economic development of Africa
under a $150,000 Carnegie Corpora
tion grant, it was announced yester
day.
Merrill K. Bennett, director of the
institute, W. O. Jones, and S. D.
Neumark will make the study. They
have all done previous research on
Africa.
The project will center on the un
derdeveloped region of Africa south
of the Sahara Desert. The research
ers will conduct comparative stud
ies on trade, transportation, and re
sources in that area.
The Food Research Institute was
established at Stanford in 1021 with
the help of a $704,000 grant from the
Carnegie Corporation.
