# Calendar Sale Set By Cap and Gown
## 
### 
The 1954 Cap and Gown calendars
will be on sale on Quad today from
10 a.m. until 3 p.m.
The calendars, which come in wall
and desk sizes, cost $1.03. They con
tain photographs of the Stanford
campus and space for writing daily
memoranda.
Tables will be set up during the
day at the Library, the Post Office,
the Cellar, and Engineering Corner.
Cap and Gown, the senior women's
honorary society, sells calendars to
raise funds for two scholarships, one
for an American student and one
for a foreign student.
