# Foreign Mail Rates Climb
## 
### 
/ New foreign mail increases went
into effect Nov. 1, according to
Stanford Post Oflice officials.
The increases affect mail to all
countries except Canada and Mex
ico, which remain the same at three
cents per ounce.
First-class mail to all other coun
tries is now eight cents for the first
ounce and four cents for each addi
tional ounce.
Post card rates arc four cents for
singles and eight cents for doubles.
Printed matter may be sent for
two cents for the first two ounces
and Vfz cents for each additional
two ounces.
Additional information on other
classes of mail will be found at any
of the windows at the Post Office.
