# British Attache To Speak at Stanford
## 
### 
Archibald Campbell, colonial at
tache of the British embassy in
Washington, will speak informally
in the lower lounge of the Women's
Clubhouse at 2:15 p.m. today.
Students from Britain, the Com
monwealth, and the British depend
encies and those interested in the
current problems of these areas, arc
invited to attend.
The attache, who is in the Bay
Area to attend a confcrence at Asi
lomar, will be introduced by Carl
F. Brand, professor of history.
