# Hawaiian Movies To Be Presented
## 
### 
Hawaiian Club will show two
travelogue movies tomorrow at 7:30
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium. Ev
eryone on campus is invited. Ad
mission is free.
The movies, "Treasure Island"
and "Highways to Hawaii," arc be
ing shown to present factual infor
mation on Hawaii to interested peo
ple, says Dave Eldredge, president.
During intermission club mem
bers will serve free pineapple juice.
They will entertain with Hawaiian
songs and hula dances.
