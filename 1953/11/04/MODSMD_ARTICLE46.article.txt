# Textile Show Opens at Art Gallery
## 
### 
A textiles show, "Cloth of Gold to
Printed Cottons," will open at the
Stanford Art Gallery tomorrow.
The display of fabrics from the
permanent collection of New York's
Metropolitan Museum of Art will
trace the development of textile de
sign from the fifteenth to the twen
tieth century.
Old fabrics shown will include
Gothic velvets, seventeenth century

taffetas and satins.
Modern textiles range from linens
printed in old Italian designs to ab
stract silks and cottons.
Also showing at the campus gal
lery will be the second exhibit of the
Western Civilization Series, photo
graphs and casts of Hellenistic Greek
and Roman art and architecture.
Both exhibitions will remain until
Nov. 22.
