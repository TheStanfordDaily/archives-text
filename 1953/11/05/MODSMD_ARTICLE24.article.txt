# ASSU to Provide Code Violation Box
## 
### 
The Council for Student Control
has placed a box in the ASSU office
where students may report viola
tions of the Honor Code or the Fun
damental Standard.
Anyone seeing such a violation
who cannot contact the members of
the Council is asked to place his
name and address in the box, and
Council members will contact him.
The Council for Student Control,
members of Men's and Women's
Councils, are responsible for inves
tigating all such violations.
