# Frosh Chairmen Chosen For Big Game Previews
## 
### 
Chairmen have been chosen for
the committees planning the Big
Game Previews, a freshman fashion
show sponsored by the Stanford
YWCA, to be held Nov. 13. They
are as follows:
Edith Strauss, general chairman;
Carolyn Leys, entertainment; San
dra Opsahl, publicity; Lillian Bud
worth, decorations and costumes;
Ann Sussex, arrangements; Belinda
Ricklefs, refreshments.
