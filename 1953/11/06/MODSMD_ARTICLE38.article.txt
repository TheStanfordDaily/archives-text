# Bonfire Wood Collection Set
## 
### 
The class of 1957 will start actual
wood collection for its Big "Game
Bonfire Monday afternoon, collec
tion chairman Mickey Townsend
has announced. Wood donations will
be collected in trucks manned by
freshman crews from Nov. 9 to
Nov. 17 inclusive.
The bonfire is scheduled to be
built in one "Big Push" this year,
starting on Nov. 18 and extending
through the night to Nov. 19. The
Big Game Rally and burning of the
bonfire will take place that night.
Wood donations are still sought
by the bonfire committee; they may
be phoned in to DA 3-8201 or DA
3-9411, Extension 298.
