# German Club Plans Films for Meeting
## 
### 
The German Club will hold a
meeting tonight at 7:30 in Bowman
Alumni House.
Two films dealing with German
art will be shown. The first is "Road
to Sculpture," which gives an in
sight into the methods of teaching
at the Berlin Academy of Sculpture.
The second is "Ernest Barlach," the

story of the life and works of Bar
lach, one of Germany's greatest
writers, dramatists, and sculptors.
Refreshments will be served at
the conclusion of the meeting.
