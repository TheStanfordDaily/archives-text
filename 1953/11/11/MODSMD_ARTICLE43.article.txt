# Roff Will Be Speaker At Off-Campus Lunch
## 
### 
The Off-Campus Men's Organiza
tion will sponsor its first luncheon
of the year this Thursday, accord
ing to Jim Flanagan, Men's Club
president.
The Off-Campus Women are to
be guests at the luncheon, which
will be held at noon in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
Hadley Roff, editor of The Daily,
will be the featured .speaker. He
will discuss the editorial policies of
the paper and its operation.
All off-campus men arc invited
to attend the luncheon.
