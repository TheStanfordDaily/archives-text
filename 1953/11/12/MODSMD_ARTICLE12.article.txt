# Potential Saboteurs From Cal Thwarted
## 
### 
Seven Cal students planning to
set fire to the Big Game bonfire
were stopped by police in Palo Alto
at 4 a.m. yesterday morning.
Police spotted them "loitering" in
town and called Stanford Chief of
Police Gordon Davis to question
them. After questioning them, Davis
sent them back to Berkeley with a
warning to "stay ofT the Stanford
campus # and keep out of trouble."
