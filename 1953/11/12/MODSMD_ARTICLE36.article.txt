# Nanney Will Present Baroque Organ Recital
## 
### 
Professor Herbert B. Nanney,
University organist, will play a pro
gram of Baroque organ music at
the organ recital in Memorial
Church at 4:15 this afternoon. The
program will include ."A Mighty
Fortress Is Our God" by Walther,
Fifth Concerto for Organ in F Ma
jor by Handel, Capriccio by Swee
linck, "Com Himmel Hoch" by Pa
chelbel, and"The Primrose" by
Peerson.
