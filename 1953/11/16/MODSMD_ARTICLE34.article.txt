# CARDS TIE L. A. RAMS
## 
### 
CHICAGO. Nov. 15 (UP) — The
Chicago Cardinals, a 21-point under
dog, scored with 58 seconds left to
day to tie the Los Angeles Rams, 24
to 24, and knock them out of a tie
for first place in the National Foot
ball League's western division.
