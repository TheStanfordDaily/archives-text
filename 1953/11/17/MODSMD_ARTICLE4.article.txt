# Farm Alumni Plan Reunions
## 
### 
Stanford alumni invading the Bay
Area for the Big Game have a busy
week before them.
In addition to the Big Game Gai
eties and the Big Game Rally and
Bonfire, there will be a number of
class reunions held in San Fran
cisco on Friday, Nov. 20.
The class reunions, in chronologi
cal order, arc:
1913 —Fortieth reunion, Friday, at
University Club, San Francisco.
Cocktails, 5:30; dinner, 7 p.m.
1918 — Thirty-fifth reunion, Fri
day, at the Fairmont Hotel, San
Francisco, beginning at 5:30 p.m.
Cocktail party.
923 — Thirtieth reunion, Thurs
day, at Rickey's Studio Inn, Palo
Alto. Cocktails, 7 p.m.; dinner, 8
p.m.
1928—Twenty-fifth reunion, Fri
day, at Hotel Mark Hopkins, San
Francisco, beginning at 6:30 p.m.
Banquet.
1933—Twentieth reunion, Friday,
Hotel Mark Hopkins, San Francisco.
Cocktails, 6:30 p.m.; dinner, 8:30
p.m.
1938 — Fifteenth reunion, Friday,
Hotel St. Francis, San Francisco,
beginning at 5:30 p.m.
1943 — Tenth reunion, Friday,
Fairmont Hotel, San Francisco, be
ginning at 7 p.m.
1948—Fifth reunion, Friday, Fair
mont Hotel, San Francisco, from 6
to 9 p.m.
All alumni are invited to the fol
lowing two parties: Stanford Men's
Club of San Francisco cocktail
party at Fairmont Hotel, Friday, 6
p.m., and Northern California Jun
iors party at Sir Francis Drake Ho
tel, Friday, 9 p.m.
The day of the game there will
be a box lunch at the Bowman
Alumni House from 11:30 a.m. to 1.
And last, but not least, the Big
Game.
