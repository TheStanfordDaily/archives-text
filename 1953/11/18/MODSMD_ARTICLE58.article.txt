# Registrars Elect Hall '53-54 Prexy
## 
### 
Harvey Hall ,Registrar, was elect
ed president of the Pacific Coast
Association of Collegiate Registrars
and Admissions Officers at their
annual meeting in Vancouver, Brit
ish Columbia, Nov. 9, 10, and 11.
Hall will hold the office of presi
dent for the coming year, and will
preside at next year's meeting in
Phoenix, Arizona. One hundred
and seventy colleges and junior col
leges are represented in the organi
zation, which includes institutions
from California, Arizona, Montana,
Idaho, Washington, Oregon, Nevada,
Alaska, the Hawaiian Islands, and
British Columbia.
E. C. Wagner, Associate Regis
trar, accompanied Hall in represent,
ing Stanford.
