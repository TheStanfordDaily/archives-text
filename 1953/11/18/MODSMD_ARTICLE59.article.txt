# Research Club To Hear Speakers
## 
### 
The Research Club of Stanford
University will have two speakers
at its meeting for faculty and staff
at 8 p.m. today at the Men's Fac
ulty Club.
Professor Richard A. Ogg Jr. of
the Department of Chemistry will
speak on "Chemical Applications of
Nuclear Magnetic Resonance."
Professor of Classics Lionel I. C.
Pearson will speak on "The Lost
Histories of Alexander the Great."
