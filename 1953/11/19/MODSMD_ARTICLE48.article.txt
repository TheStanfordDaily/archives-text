# Grads to Hold Coffee Hour, Elections
## 
### 
The Graduate Student Association
will sponsor a pre-Big Game coffee
hour tomorrow afternoon from 3:30
to 5:30 in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
Election of officers will be held at
the coffee hour. Those running arc

Glen Smith, president; Carolyn Me
sick, secretary; Olga Sanders and
Hall Kuttner, treasurer; and George
Scithers, organizational chairman.
There are no candidates for social
and publicity chairmen.
