# Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
The Rev. D. Clifford Crummey,
minister at the Grace Methodist
Church, Stockton, will preach the
sermon Sunday in Memorial Church
at the 11 a.m. service of worship.
His topic will be "Giving Thanks for
Things That Endure."
The Rev. R. M. Minto, chaplain
of the University, will conduct the
service. Anthems will be sung by
the Memorial Church Choir, direct
ed by Professor Harold C. Schmidt.
Professor Herbert B. Nanney will
be at the organ. A coffee hour,
sponsored by the YWCAi will be
,held at Bowman Alumni House im
mediately following the service.
