# Psychological Warfare Tried Against Cal
## 
### 
A monoplane flew over the Uni
versity of California campus Fri
day night making four passes and
bombarding the campus and Greek
Theater with leaflets.
The leaflets, drawn into the theater
by the intense heat of the UC bon
fire, read as follows:
OPPRESSED STUDENTS OF UC
Rise up against your oppressors!
Throw off your shackles of filth and
hunger!
Why be mere cogs in a mere cold,
impersonal machine?
Save yourselves from further em
barrassment and inevitable defeat,
Realize your impotence. Your at
tempts to burn the bonfire were
to no avail.
SURRENDER NOW!
Department of Psychological
Warfare
Stanford University
The plane was not identified, and
officials believed no investigation
would be made.
