# Freshman Excom Applications Due
## 
### 
All freshmen who intend to apply
for membership on the Freshman
Executive Committee .must have
their applications filed at the ASSU
office today.
The purpose of the Frosh Excom
is to plan the different activities for
the year, such as the frosh-soph
jamboree, talent show, and dance.
All those who are not appointed
members of the Excom will be
chosen in the future to serve on
special committees for thp various
activities.
