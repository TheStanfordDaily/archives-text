# Student Leaders To Confer at Mills
## 
### 
The Student World Affairs Coun
cil of Northern California will hold
its annual leadership conference at
Mills College on Saturday.
Students from more than 20 col
leges will attend the conference,
which will feature discussion
groups, workshops, and panels. Bob
Jessen of Stanford will conduct one
of the discussion groups.
Dr. C. Oliver from the University
of California's Boalt Hall will ad
dress the conference.
