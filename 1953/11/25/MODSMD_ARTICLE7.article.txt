# New 'Hazards Com' Seeks Suggestions
## 
### 
Stanford's newest committee, the
Committee for Investigation and
Prevention of Campus Hazards
(Hazards Com) is beginning a pro
gram which committee members
hope will help lower the accident
rate here on campus.
The committee would appreciate
suggestions as to how to eliminate
hazardous conditions which exist,
especially traffic and parking haz
ards. Suggestions may be submitted
to Bill Jones. Village 312, phone DA
3-0451, or to Anne Evans, telephone
DA 3-8975, Guthrie House.
