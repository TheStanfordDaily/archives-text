# Upper Division, Graduate Pre-Reg to Begin Monday
## 
### 
Pre-registration of upper division
and graduate students for winter
quarter will begin next Monday and
will last through Tuesday. The
Registrar's Office lias announced the
following procedure for these stu
dents:
Obtain an official study list card
from your major school or depart
ment and fill out your program in
ink with the approval and signature
of your major professor.
If your study list includes any
courses marked in the winter
quarter time schedule, sign the ap
propriate class lists at the informa
tion desk, Registrar's Office, Room
111-A, between 8 a.m. and 12 noon,
or between 1 and 5 p.m., on Mon
day or Tuesday. Your name on the
class list assures you of admission
to the class, provided you are pres
ent at the first class meeting.
Turn in your official study list
card in person to the check - out
clerk at the information desk, Reg
istrar's Office, Room 111-A.

Time schedules will be available
at the information desk Friday.
Pre-rcgistration for lower divi
sion students will begin next Wed-

nesday and continue until Tuesday,
Dec. 8, at 12 noon. A more detailed
announcement regarding lower di
vision pre-registration will be re
leased at a later date.
