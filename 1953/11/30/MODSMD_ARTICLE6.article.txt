# English A Final Moved To Avoid Civ Conflict
## THIS MORNING'S HEADLINES 
### 
The Committee on Schedule
and Examinations has changed
the time of the English A final
examination from Tuesday, Dec.
15, at 4 p.m. to Monday, Dec. 14,
at 2 p.m.
The change was made, accord
ing to the committee, to avoid
having the English exam imme
diately following the Western
Civilization final.
