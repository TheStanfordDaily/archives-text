# Winter Pre-Reg to Begin Today for Grads and UD
## 
### 
Upper division and graduate stu
dents will begin pre - registration
for winter quarter today and to
morrow.
Students may obtain an official
study list card from their major
school or department. The program
should be filled out in ink and
signed by the major professor.
If the study list includes any
courses marked "§" in the winter
quarter time ♦schedule, the student
must sign the appropriate class lists
at the information desk, Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A, between 8 a.m.
and 12 noon or between 1 and 5 p.m.
today or tomorrow.
The official study list card must
he turned in in person to the
check-out clerk at the informa
tion desk, Registrar's Office, be
fore 5 p.m. tomorrow.
Pre-registration for lower divi
sion students will begin Wednesday.

A more detailed announcement re
garding lower division pre-registra
tion will be released tomorrow.
