# Concert Tickets To Sell Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tickets for the remaining three
concerts in this year's ASSU Con
cert Series will go on sale tomorrow
in the Concert Series office in the
Nitery, according to Concert Man
ager Nell McDill.
Office hours are 1 to 5 p.m. Mon
day through Friday and 9 to 12
Saturday morning.
The concerts will be given by Jo
seph Szigeti, violinist, on Jan. 23;
Grant Johannesen, pianist, on Feb.
8; and the San Francisco Ballet
Company on April 10. Prices for
tickets to each of the concerts are
$2 .50, $2, $1 50. and $1.
