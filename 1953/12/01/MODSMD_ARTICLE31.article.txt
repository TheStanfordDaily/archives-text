# Business Club Plans Discussion
## 
### 
A discussion on "Small Business"
will be presented by the Business
School Club tomorrow night at 7:30
in the Biz Club lounge in the base
ment of the Business School.
Speakers for the evening will be
Robert Kerner, owner and manager
of Standard 5-, 10-, and 25-eent
stores; Emmet J. Ca.shin Jr., a real
tor with Fox and Carskadon; and

Norman Brown of the W. J. Nickel -
son Co.
They will discuss advantages and
success in small business, and col
lege courses which are most useful
in preparation for small business.
Students of the Business School
are urged to attend, and interested
undergraduates, faculty members,
and guests are also welcome.
