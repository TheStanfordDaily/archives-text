# Excom to Name NSA Co-ordinator
## 
### 
ASSU President Don Lozabnick
said last night that he would an
nounce the appointment of a NSA
co-ordinator at tonight's meeting of
the Executive Committee.
The co-ordinator, whose appoint
ment is subject to approval by Ex
com, will be responsible for organ
izing a Stanford NSA Committee
and administering any NSA pro
gram the Associated Students un
dertakes.
Lozabnick also said that Excom
would discuss the possibility of the
ASSU joining with IIR to sponsor a
"Stanford Tour" of Europe next
summer similar to the one held last
year.
Excom might also consider the
revised constitution of the Public
Relations Committee, Lozabnick
said.
