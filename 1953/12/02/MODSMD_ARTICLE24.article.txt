# Frosh-Soph Coed Exchange Tonight
## 
### 
Sophomore and freshman women
will meet tonight in an exchange at
the Women's Clubhouse from 8:30
to 9:30.
The purpose of the exchange is
to help the coeds from the two
classes to become better acquainted.
Sophomore Lynn Lercara is in
charge of planning the affair. Work
ing as a committee under her are
Patty Halperin, Renee Reed, Jackie
Rouse, Jean Scholl, Debbie Swan,
and Mary Anna Watanabe.
