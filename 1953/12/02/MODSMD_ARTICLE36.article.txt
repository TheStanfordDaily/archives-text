# TANNER ON SHRINE TEAM
## 
### 
Stanford center Ted Tanner,
along with eight other football
seniors, was chosen to the West
squad of the Shrine East - West
game yesterday by Chuck Taylor,
head coach of the West team.
The other eight seniors are
Carlton Massey, All - American
end from Texas; Cotton Davidson,
slick quarterback from Baylor;
guards Milt Bohart of Washing
ton and Phil Branch of Texas;
tackle Dean Chambers of Wash
ington: center Stan Sheriff of Cal
Poly's undefeated eleven; and
halfbacks Jerry Coody of Baylor
and Veryl Switzer of Kansas
State.
