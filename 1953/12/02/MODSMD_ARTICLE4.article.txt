# LD Pre-Reg To Continue
## 
### 
Winter quarter pre - registration
for lower division students begins
today and continues through next
Tuesday.
Students may confer with their
advisers today, tomorrow, and Fri
day before sinning class study lists.
The appointment time on the pre
registration permits which have
been mailed to all lower division
students is for signing class lists,
not for seeing advisers.
Courses marked with a "§" in the
winter quarter time schedule re
quire the student to sign a class list
at the information desk, Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A, between 8 a.m.
and noon or 1 to 5 p.m. tomorrow,
Friday, or Monday, or between 8
a.m. and noon on Saturday or Tues
day.
Appointments for signing class
lists begin Thursday at 8 a.m. and
continue through Tuesday noon.
Students may sign class lists at the
time indicated on the permit or any
time thereafter. A student's name
on a class list assures him of ad
mission to the class provided he is
present at the first class meeting.
Students who did not receive a
permit should go to the Lower Di
vision Officc, Room 111-D. Official
study list cards must be turned in,
in person, to the check-out clerk at
the information desk, Registrar's
Office, Room 111-A, before noon on
Tuesday, Dec. 8.
