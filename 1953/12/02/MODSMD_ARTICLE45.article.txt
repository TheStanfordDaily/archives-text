# Distaff Club Bridge Section Meets Tonight
## 
### 
The Bridge Section of the Stan
ford Distaff Club will meet tonight
at 7:15 at the home of Mrs. Isabel
Marx, 1491 Hamilton Avenue.
Other meetings planned for the
club include a luncheon tomorrow
in the Garden Wing of the Stanford
Union and a Christmas party at
the home of President and Mrs.
Wallace Sterling on Dec. 18.
