# Shoup Former Excom Member
## 
### 
Guy Shoup, 19-year-old junior,
was appointed National Students
Association co-ordinator last night
by the Executive Committee.
Shoup is the outgoing Excom rep
resentative of the present junior
class. He is one of three Excom
members who voted against NSA
when Excom approved membership
for Stanford on Oct. 21.
A history and pre - law major,
Shoup is a junior sponsor at the
Village and a member of Phi Delta
Theta.
In his freshman year Shoup was
active on the freshman class excom
and worked on the frosh paper. His
father, Jack Shoup, is the director
of the Stanford Alumni Association.
