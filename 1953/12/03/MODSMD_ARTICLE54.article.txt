# Corsage Sales Aid Yost Fund
## 
### 
The recent Big Game chrysanthe
mum corsage sale, sponsored by the
Associated Women Students, earned
$330.50 for the Dean Mary Yost
scholarship.
AWS Scholarship Committee
chairman Barbara Wheat reported
that this sale met with such success
that AWS has decided to make it an
annual project.
The corsages of white chrysan
themums with the traditional red
block "S" were made and sold by
150 Stanford women.
The flowers were sold at alumni
parties at the Mark Hopkins and
the Fairmont the night before the
game and on campus at open houses
and luncheons Big Game morning.
