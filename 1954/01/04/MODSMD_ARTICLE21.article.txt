# Stanford Gallery Shows Finnish Art Display
## 
### 
An exhibit of Finnish arts and
crafts will be on display at the
Stanford Art Gallery through Jan.
10.
The show is a sampling of con
temporary Finnish work, including
a collection of lithographs, color
woodcuts, and serigraphs. Ceramic
and glass art and traditional "ryijy"
rugs are also on exhibit.
Objects on display have been se
lected from the Smithsonian Insti
tution in Washington, D.C., and are
being circulated by the American
Federation of Arts.
