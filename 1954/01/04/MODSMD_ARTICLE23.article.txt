# Concert Band Offers Credits to Applicants
## 
### 
Candidates for Stanford's concert
band may receive one unit credit by
registering for Music 161, band
manager James Killen announced
yesterday. The concert band will
be open to both men and women.
Rehearsals will be held Tuesday
nights at 7:15 for the full band and
Thursday nights at 7:15 for the bas
ketball band; lirst meeting will be
tomorrow night. Julius Shuchat
will conduct the concert band,
which gives an annual concert late
in spring quarter.
