TO FIGHT CANCER—Professor Edward L. Ginzton, director of the microwave laboratory, ac
cepted a grant for $24,915 on Dec. 18 to help finance the final work on an electron accelerator for
treatment of cancer. The grant, awarded to the University by the American Cancer Society, was
presented to Professor Ginzton by Mrs. James Graham Sharp, president of the Palo Alto unit of
the society. When completed, the accelerator will be mounted on the apparatus in the back-!
ground and installed in the University's School of Medicine in San Francisco.
