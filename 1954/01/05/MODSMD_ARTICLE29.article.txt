# Blood Drive Staff Seeks Student Help
## 
### 
Students interested in helping
with the winter quarter blood drive
have been asked to meet at 7:15 to
night in the Executive Committee
room of the ASSU office by drive
chairman Ken Schechter.
Assisting Schechter with the drive
is Fred Stern, representing the jun
ior class.
The drive is being sponsored by
Cardinals and the junior class, but
all students may work on the drive.
