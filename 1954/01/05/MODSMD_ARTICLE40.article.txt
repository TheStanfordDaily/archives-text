# Stanford Sailors Third at Newport
## 
### 
Hampered by tough luck in most
of the events, a Stanford sailing
team placed third in the Newport
Intercollegiate Sailing Regatta Dec.
19 and 20.
The Indian team, composed of
Mike Jager, Charles Merrill, and
Kim Munholland, scored 149'/2
points to finish third behind UCLA
and Washington.
Stanford was leading the first day
of the races after Jager had won
two straight events, but in the third
race he hit a marker and withdrew
costing the Cards 12 points.
The second day, Stanford suffered
two disqualifications.
Jager scored 56% points during
the regatta, Merrill .'57, and Munhol
land 59 3 ,4.
