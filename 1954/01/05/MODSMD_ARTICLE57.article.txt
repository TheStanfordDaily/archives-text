# Study in Munich
## 
### 
Students may now apply to
spend the junior year in Munich,
under an intercollegiate foreign
study program furnished by the
Liberal Arts College of Wayne
University.
Students wishing to apply for
this program must have at least
two years of college German and
the approval of the college which
they are now attending.
All students wishing to apply
for admission may obtain applica
tions from "Junior Year in Mu
nich," Wayne University, Detroit,
Mich. All application forms must
he submitted before April 15.
Scholarships are available and
applications for financial assist
ance must be submitted along
with the general application.
