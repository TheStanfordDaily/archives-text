# A 						Phi O Hits Peak in Sales

### 
Alpha Phi Omega's book exchange
experienced "best day we've ever
had" yesterday, according to project
chairman Pete Baumgartner. The
service fraternity handled 176 books
and received $455.33 during the day.

today from 9 a.m. to'noon and from
1 to 4 p.m., and tomorrow from 9

10(j to help students sell their old

exchange so far this quarter: An

a total of $692.18 received to be re
turned to students when the ex
change reopens Jan. 28, 29, and 30.
Unsold books will also be returned
at this time.
A servicc charge of five cents is
