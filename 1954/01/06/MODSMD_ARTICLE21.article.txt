IT WON'T BE LONG NOW—Workmen at Groton, Conn., put
the finishing touches on the USS Nautilus, the world's first
atomic-powered submarine. Under construction for more than
a year, the sub will be christened by Mrs. Eisenhower Jan.2l.
It will be able to travel thousands of miles under water at a
speed better than 20 knots. —NEA Telephoto
