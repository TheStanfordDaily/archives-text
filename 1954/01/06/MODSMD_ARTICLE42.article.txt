# WOOD RANKED TENTH
## 
### 
Four present and former Stan
ford trackmen were mentioned in
the 1953 Track and Field News'
world ranking last month.
Jerry Wood, who was rated tenth
in the high hurdles, is the only
Indian thinclad still in school.
Other former Cards ranked were
Bud Held, first in the javelin; Bob
Mathias, eighth in the high hurdles;
and Howard Hertz, eighth in the
shot put.
