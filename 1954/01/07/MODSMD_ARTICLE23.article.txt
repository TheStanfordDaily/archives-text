# Film Society Will Present David Harum'
## 
### 
"David Harum," starring the late
Will Rogers, will be shown tonight
at 7:30 p.m. in Cubberley Auditor
ium to open the Stanford Film So
ciety's winter quarter series.
Admission is by membership in
the society only, and no single tick
ets will be sold. Admision cards en
title the holder to see all of the nine
films which will be shown every
Thursday this quarter.
Priced at $1.80, the cards avail
able to both students and the gen
eral public. They will be sold this
week from 12:30 to 4 p.m. at the
I Memorial Hall box office. They will
also be sold at the door tonight.
Mail orders may be sent to the box
office. A stamped, self-addressed
envelope should be included and
checks made payable to Stanford
| University.
Representative films made by dis
| tinguished American and European
directors from 1929 to 1949 will com
pose the series.
