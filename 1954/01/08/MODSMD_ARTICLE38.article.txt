# S.F. Symphony Exchange Open
## 
### 
The ASSU Concert Series will
begin a student ticket exchange for
next Thursday's San Francisco
Symphony Seritfs today. Anyone
who wishes to buy or sell tickets
to the Symphonies may do so at
the Concert Series office in the Ni
tery from 1 to 5 p.m. on Mondays
through Fridays.
The Concert Series is also selling
tickets for the concerts to be held
in Memorial Auditorium during the
remainder of the year: Joseph Szi
geti, violinist, Jan. 23; Grant Johan
nesen, pianist, Feb. 8; San Francisco
Ballet Company, April 10.
Anyone interested in ushering for
the Szigeti concert is asked to check
in the office in the Nitery.
