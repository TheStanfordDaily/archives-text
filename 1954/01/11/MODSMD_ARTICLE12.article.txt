# AIR CRASH
## 
### 
SHHEVEPORT, La., Jan. 10 (UP)
—An amphibious plane crashed in
snow and sleet near Shrevcport to
night, killing 12 persons including
Tom Braniff, president of an airline
which has flown almost three bil
lion passenger miles without a sin
gle fatality.
