# Volleyball Team Places in Tourney
## 
### 
Six Stanford men brought laurels
home Saturday when they came in
fourth in a Northern California
A.A.U. sponsored volleyball tourna
ment held at San Francisco State.
The six man team had to play 20
games and 15 different teams to win
one of the top spots in the tourna
ment. The meet lasted from 9 a.m.
until 6:30 p.m.
The team members were Paul
Norgaard. Russ Stolfi, Gene Dils,
John Lacata, Peanuts Miller, and Pat
Dougherty.
