# Board and Room Bills Payable Now
## 
### 
Board and room bills are now
payable at the cashier's office,
Building 1, Inner Quad, according
to the controller's office.
The office is open from 8 a.m. to
5 p.m. Monday through Friday.
Students who live at Stanford
Village may make payment at the
Village cashier's office, 9:30 a.m. to
12:30 p.m. and 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.,
Monday through Friday.
Students who cannot pay this
week should arrange for a defer
ment with the Counselor for Men or
Women.
