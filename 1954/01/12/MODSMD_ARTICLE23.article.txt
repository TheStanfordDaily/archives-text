# Smith Collection To Be Exhibited
## 
### 
A representative group of con
temporary paintings from the art
collections of Smith College will be
exhibited at the Art Gallery today
through .Jan. 31.
Called "Smith College Collects,"
the show contains 27 examples of
American, Italian, and English oils,
watercolors, and drawings.
The exhibit was selected from
Smith College Museum's permanent
collection, which has been assem
bled over a period of many years.
In the last lew years the pace of
acquisition of contemporary paint
ings has increased. The show at
Stanford is composed primarily of
pictures produced since World War
11.
Gallery hours are 10 a.m. to 5
p.m. Tuesdays through Saturdays,
and noon to 4 p.m. Sundays. The
Gallery is close*l Mondays.
