# IIR Plans Luncheon To Honor Mrs. Lord
## 
### 
Mrs. Oswald B. Lord, U.S. repre
sentative to the U.N. Commission
on Human Rights, will speak at a
luncheon to be given in her honor
Tuesday, Jan. 19, by the Institute ot
International Relations.
The lunchcon will be at Ramor
Oaks from 12:30 to 2:30. The pri':e
is $1.50.
Sixty places will be available to
students. Barbara Klass will take
reservations today and tomorrow
between 3 and 4 p.m. at the lIR
Shack from anyone interested.
