# Young Democrats Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Young Democrats of the
Stanford Area will hold their first
meeting of winter quarter tomor
row night at 8 in the Little Theater
of Palo Rlto High School. Rides will
leave the Post Office at 7:30.
Guest speaker will be Fran Win H.
Williams, secretary-counsel in the
West Coast region of the National
Asssociation for the Advancement
of Colored People. Williams, who
lives in Palo Alto, is a San Fran
cisco lawyer and has been with
NAACP since 1945.
