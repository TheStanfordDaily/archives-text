# Room and Board Hills Are Due Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last day for
paying University board and room
hills without fine, the controller's
office has announced.
Payment should he made at the
cashier's office, Building 1, Inner
Quad. The office opens at S a.m.
and closes at 5 p.m.
Students living at Stanford Vil
lage should make payment at the
Village cashier's office, 9:30 a.m.
to 12:.'»0 p.m. and l:.'! 0 to 3:30 p.m.
Checks accompanied by the
statement may he mailed to the
cashier's office hut must he post
marked no later than Jan. 15.
A student who cannot pay now
should try to arrange for a defer
ment with the Counselor for Men
or Women.
