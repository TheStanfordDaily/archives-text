Associated Women Students and
Women's Athletic Association of
fices must be returned to the ASSU
office by 5 p.m. tomorrow.
Petitions may be picked up at the
ASSU office from 8 a.m. to noon
and 1 to 5 p.m.

president, vice-president, secretary,
and social chairman.

more. junior, and senior classes and
president, vice-president, and sec
retary are the WAA positions to be
filled.
