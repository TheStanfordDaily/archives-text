# Petitions Now Due For AWS Posts
## 
### 
Candidates for Associated Wom
en Students and Women's Athletic
Association officers should turn in
their signed nomination petitions to
the ASSU office by 5 p.m. today.
Declinations arc due on Monday
by 5 p.m. at the ASSU office.
The AWS offices to bo filled are
president, vice-president, secretary,
and social chairman.
The positions to be filled for WAA
are president, vice-president, secre
tary, and representatives from the
sophomore, junior, and senior
classes.
Nominations received yesterday
include Amber Spinning, for presi
dent; Marilyn Milcr, vice - presi
dent; Mary Post, secretary; and
Phyllis Stewart, social chairman.
