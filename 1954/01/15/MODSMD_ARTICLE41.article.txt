# EWBANK NAMED COLT COACH
## 
### 
BALTIMORE, Jan. 14 (UP)—Wil
bur (Weeb) Ewbank, who helped
make the Cleveland Browns' line
one of the strongest in the business,
was named head coach of the Balti
more Colts today with instructions
to "bring this town a championship."
