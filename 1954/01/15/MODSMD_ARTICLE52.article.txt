# Bay Area Novelist Is Guest Speaker
## 
### 
Carol Green Wilson, Bay Area
novelist and former editor of the
Stanford Review, will be guest
speaker at a professional meeting
of Theta Sigma Phi tonight at 6:30
at Ramor Oaks.
Theta Sigma Phi is an honorary
professional fraternity for women
engaged in journalism. Besides the
regular members of the group, six
other women students engaged in
journalism will be guests at the din
ner.
