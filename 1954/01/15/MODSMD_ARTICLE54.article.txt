# Contests Open To Students
## Biographies 
### 
Three prizes in the field of biog
raphy are available to native-born
Californiaas under the James D.
Phelan Awards.
Applicants for the awards must
be between the ages of 20 and 40.
The awards offered are $500, first
prize; $400, second prize; and $300,
third prize.
Applicants must submit a book or
chapters from a book, and must
apply on special forms which may
be obtained from the office of the
James D. Phelan Awards in Litera
ture and Art, 629 Phelan Building,
San Francisco 2.
The competition closes March 15.
