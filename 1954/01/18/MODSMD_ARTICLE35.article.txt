# Geology Grad Hurt As Cliff Crumbles
## 
### 
Claude Minard Jr., Stanford
graduate student in geology, was
injured Friday afternoon while do
ing geology field work at Alum
Rock Park, five miles west of San
Jose.
He was resting in Palo Alto Hos
pital yesterday; his condition was
described as "satisfactory."
Minard, who lives with his par
ents at 2380 Waverley, Palo Alto,
was climbing a cliff in search of
rock formations when the cliff gave
way. Minard was knocked uncon
scious by the drop. He was observed
by passing hikers, who called on
ambulance immediately.
Minard sustained no fractures in
the fall. His only injuries are cuts
and bruises on his face and body.
