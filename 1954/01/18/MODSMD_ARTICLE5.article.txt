# Quad to Complete Photos This Week
## 
### 
The Quad will take pictures, this
week only, of all sophomores, jun
iors, and seniors who have not been
photographed or who wish to have
their pictures retaken, Co - editor
Ralph Buckwalter announced yes
terday.
No freshmen will be photographed
except those who have joined a fra
ternity or eating club and who want
their pictures to appear with these
organizations. Freshmen who have
already had their pictures taken
may request to have their pictures
included with their fraternity or
eating club.
Buckwalter said that Quads arc
on sale now at the yearbook office
and that a limited supply of Frosh
Books are also on sale.
