# Museum Official To Give Series Talk
## 
### 
F. C. Francis, keeper of printed
books in the British Museum, will
give the Tuesday Evening Series
lecture tomorrow night at 7:30 in
Memorial Auditorium.
The talk will be titled "The Brit
ish Museum in Peace and War."
The British librarian oversees the
largest collection of books in the
English language. He will discuss
the growth of the Museum, its col
lections, and some of its wartime
experiences.
Francis will be introduced by
Francis R. Johnson, Stanford pro
fessor of English.
