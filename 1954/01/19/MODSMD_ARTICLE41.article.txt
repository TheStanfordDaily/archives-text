# Off-Campus Men To Elect Officers
## 
### 
The off-campus men will hold a
special meeting tomorrow night at
7:30 p.m. in the lower lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse to elect officers
for the year.
Nominations will be made for the
offices of president, vice-president,
secretary, and treasurer. New nom
inees will compete against those
who have already been nominated
during the past week .
Non-members will also be eligible
to vote.
After elections, Lawson Warbur
ton will show colored slides taken
on his tour of Europe last summer.
