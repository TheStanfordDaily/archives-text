# German Club Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The German Club will hold its
first meeting of the quarter tonight
at 7:30 in the main lounge of the
Women's Clubhouse.
A one - half - hour tape-recorded
greeting made by University of Ber

lin students who attended Stanford
last year will be played at the
meeting.
• A tourist film on Hamburg will be
shown. Refreshments will be served
at the end ol the meeting.
