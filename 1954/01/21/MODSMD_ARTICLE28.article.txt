# Grad Scholarship Blanks Available
## 
### 
Students who wish to apply for
graduate scholarships and fellow
ships J'or the academic year 1954-55
may secure application blanks from
the Graduate Awards Secretary,
Mrs. Walker, in the office of the
Dean of the Graduate Division,
Room 1-H on the Inner Quad.
Applications must be submitted
on or before Feb. 15 in order to be
considered.
Mrs. Walker has a variety of
books available in her office on
financial help for students, as well
as scholarship and fellowship offers
from universities throughout the
United States and abroad.
