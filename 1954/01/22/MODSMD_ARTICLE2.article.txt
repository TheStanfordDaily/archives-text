# Nautilus Launched
## 
### 
GROTON, Conn., Jan. 2l (UP) —
The U.S.S. Nautilus, the world's
first atomic-driven submarine, was
christened today by Mrs. Dwight D.
Eisenhower, opening a new era of
nuclear power for peace as well as
war.
The $55,000,000 craft slipped down
the building ways into the Thames
River as Mrs. Eisenhower smashed
a bottle of American champagne
across its broad bow and said, "I
christen thee U.S.N.S. Nautilus."
Fifteen thousand witnessed the
launching.
Atomic power is capable of taking
the Nautilus around the world in
29 days without surfacing.
