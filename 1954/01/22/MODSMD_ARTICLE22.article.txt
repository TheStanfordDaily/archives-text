# Fire Horn to Sound Air Raid Signal Monday
## 
### 
The Stanford campus will par
ticipate in a state-wide air raid
drill at noon Monday, Fire Chief
John Marston announced yesterday.
The drill will consist of a three
minute period of short % blasts on the
fire horn, followed by three long
all-clear signals. The purpose of
the drill is both to test the equip
ment and familiarize students with
the signal. Marston said.
No participation is necessary by
students.
