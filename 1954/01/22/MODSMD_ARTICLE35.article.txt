# Co-Rec to Sponsor Prom Next Friday
## 
### 
"Winter Wunderbar" will be the
theme of the formal prom sched
uled for Jan. 29, from 9 p.m. to 1
a.m., in the Women's Gym. The Co-
Rec Association will sponsor the
dance.
Jack Fisher's nine-piece band will
be featured. Bids will cost $3 and
will be sold on Quad next week and
at the door.
