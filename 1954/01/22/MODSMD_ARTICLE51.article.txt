# Chaplain of Yale To Give Sermon
## 
### 
The Rev. Sidney Lovett, chaplain
and Master of Pierson College at
Yale University, will deliver the
sermon at the 11 a.m. service of
worship at Memorial Church this
Sunday, in commemoration of the
fifty-first anniversary of the dedi
cation of the church. His topic will
be "On Doing the Right Thing in
the Right Way."
Immediately following the serv
ice, Dr. Lovett will meet students
and friends at the YWCA-spon
sosred coffee hour at Bowman
Alumni House.
The Memorial Church Choir, un
der the direction of Professor Har
old C. Schmidt, will sing anthems
for the service, and Professor Her
bert B. Nanney will be at the organ.
