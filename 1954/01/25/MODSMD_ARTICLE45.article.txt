# Poet to Give Intermezzo Series Talk
## 
### 
Edward Weissmiller, a "Yale
Younger Poet" and professor of po
etry at Pomona College, will give a
free public lecture in the Bender
Room of the Stanford Main Library
at 4:15 p.m. tomorrow.
"The Author Surprised" is the

title of his talk, which is one of the
Intermezzo program series.
Wallace Stegner, professor of
English and director of the Stanford
Creative Writing Center, will intro
duce the speaker.
