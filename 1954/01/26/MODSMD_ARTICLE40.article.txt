# Hamburgers to Aid Polio Dimes Drive
## 
### 
Three thousand hamburgers will
be given away tomorrow at Kirk's
Drive-In aft a March of Dimes bene
fit clay.
Co-sponsoring the event are the
Palo Alto Junior Chamber of Com
merce and Kirks. Under the plan,
those who receive the free hamburg
ers will be asked to contribute to
the March of Dimes in containers at
the Drive-In.
The hamburgers will be served
by members of the San Francisco
49er football team who are donating
a day's work to the March of Dimes.
