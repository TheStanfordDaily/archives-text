# Economic Report To Be Debated
## 
### 
The President's Economic Report
and proposed economic legislation
will be up for debate Monday night
when Dr. Theodore Kreps, profes
sor in Stanford's Graduate School
of Business will address the Politi
cal Union's first meeting of winter
quarter.
The noted economist will open the
meeting with a non-partisan analysis
of the Report. Debate from the floor
will follow.
The meeting will begin at 8 p.m. in
Cubberley Auditorium, and will br
open to all interested students and
faculty members.
The Union's publicity committee
will meet Saturday morning at 10
o'clock in the Nitery.
