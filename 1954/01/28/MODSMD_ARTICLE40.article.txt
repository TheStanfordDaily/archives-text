# Palace Hotel to Be Military Ball Site
## 
### 
Stanford's third annual Military
Ball will be held at the Palace Hotel
in San Francisco on Friday, Feb. 5,
from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. Music will
be furnished by Ray Hacketl and his
orchestra.
The ball is sponsored by Stan
ford's three ROTC honor societies,
Arnold Air Society, Scabbard and
Blade, and Quarterdeck. Each so
ciety will crown a queen at the
dance.
Tickets can be secured now by
any Stanford ROTC cadet for $2.50.
Doug Rogers, the Arnold Air repre
sentative for the ball, is .selling bids
from 2 to 4 p.m. Mondays through
Fridays in the BAC Building.
