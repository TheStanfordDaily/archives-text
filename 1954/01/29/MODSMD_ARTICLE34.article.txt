# Winter Carnival Reservations Open
## 
### 
Reservations for the Yosemite
Valley Winter Carnival to be held
over the weekend of Feb. 5, 6, and 7
will be available at the ASSU office
until Monday afternoon.
The Winter Carnival is an annual
activity sponsored by the Stanford
Ski Club. Races open to all classes
of Stanford skiers will be held Feb.
5 at 11 a.m. There will be a bunny
race with an easy course for all be
ginners, the giant slalom for inter
mediates, and an obstacle race for
all classes.
Stanford Ski Club members must,
have their club cards to make reser
vations.
