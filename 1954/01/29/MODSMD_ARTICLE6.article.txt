# Workers Sought For Jamboree
## 
### 
Freshmen interested in working
on one of five committees for the
freshman-sophomore jamboree are
asked to sign up in the ASSU office
today or tomorrow.
Freshmen and sophomores plan
ning to compete in the beard-grow
ing contest for the Jamboree are
asked to sign up in the Encina Bar
ber Shop by Tuesday.
