# MOOT COURT
## 63 Students Vie Tonight 
### 
Sixty-three members of the first
year law class will take part in the
Hilmer Oehlmann First-Year Moot
Court Competition tonight.
The trials will be held in Rooms
161-R, 161-B, and in the Moot Court
room in the law building. Starting
time is 7 p.m.
Five winners, selected by 15 sec
ond- and third-year students acting
as judges, will be named. Prizes
for the competitioon are made
available from a fund contributed
by friends of Hilmer Oehlmann,
student in the law school class of
1953.
