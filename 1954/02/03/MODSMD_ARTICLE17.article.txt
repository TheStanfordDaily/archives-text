# Tickets on Sale For Johannesen Recital
## 
### 
Tickets are on sale today through
Friday from 1 to 5 p.m. and Satur
day morning from to 12 at the
ASSU Concert Series office in the
Nitery for the piano recital by
Grant Johannesen in Memorial Au
ditorium next Monday. The tickets
cost $1, $1.50, $2, and $2.50.
Johannesen writes music for the
piano, for voice, for instrumental
combinations, and for presentation
in the theater. Piano Playhouse
featured some of his lighter compo
sitions on a recent broadcast-
He has mastered five languages,
and is interested in the theater, both
American and foreign, as well as
music.
