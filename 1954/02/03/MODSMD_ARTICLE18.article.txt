# Ex-Farm Botanist W.F. Wight Dies
## 
### 
William Franklin Wight, lecturer
and botanist at Stanford from 1926
to 1941, died yesterday at his ranch
In Southern California.
Wight earned his Master's degree
at Stanford in 1900 after graduating
from Michigan State College. He
worked for the U.S. Department of
Agriculture for a number of years
before joining the Stanford faculty.
He was promoted to botanist by
the government In 1928, and lie con
ducted a number of experiments
while teaching here. In the later
years, he was in charge of an ex
perimental peach orchard on -cam
pus.
