# SOPH CARDS
##

The week-long Sophomore Class
Card Drive concludes today.
Sophomore Activities Board
members will canvass the living
groups they represent, giving soph
omores a final chance to purchase
their class cards.
The cards are on sale for $3, and
are good for the next three years.
The cards will definitely be used for
coming events such as next week
end's Jamboree. I
