# One-Acts Tickets Sell Today
## 
### 
Tickets for Ram's Head Winter
One-Acts go on sale today from
12:30 to 4:30 p.m. at the Memorial
Hall box office. All tickets are $1.
The One-Acts will be presented
Feb. 18, 19, and 20 in the Little
Theater of Memorial Hall from 7 to
10 p.m.; they will consist of three
plays.
"The Intrusion" is a serious com

edy, centering around the situation
crsated when a naive secretary
comes into explosive contact with
modern super-efficiency in an office.
"Molly and Me" is a farce depict
ing what a Miss America contest
can do to married life.
"The Sheriff" is a serious work
dealing with the problem of justice
versus right in a frontier town of
1881.
