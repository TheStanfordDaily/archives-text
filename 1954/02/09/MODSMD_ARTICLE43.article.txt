# Council Acquits 2 Men Students
## 
### 
Two men students were acquitted
of alleged Honor Code violations
early last week by the Council lor
Student Control.
The Council, composed of the
members of Men's and Women's
Councils, acquitted the two men
after two separate trials involving
alleged violations in the same mid
term examination last- quarter. Both
eases were brought to the attention
of the Council by a third student
who took the same examination -
