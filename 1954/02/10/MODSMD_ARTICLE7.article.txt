# Sterling to Report On European Trip
## 
### 
Dr. Wallace Sterling, president of
Stanford University, will be guest
speaker at the weekly Open House
of the World Affairs Council of
Northern California tomorrow at
5:15 p.m. in the Nob Hill Theater of
the Fairmont Hotel.
Dr. Sterling will report on his
two-month tour of Britain and Eu
rope during which he visited many
of the principal universities of the
world. The talk, entitled "Busman's
Holiday," is open to the public
without charge
