# Beaux Arts Ball Bids Will Continue on Sale
## 
### 
Ticket sales for the Beaux Arts
Ball scheduled for Saturday night
will continue today and tomorrow
at the Cellar. If any tickets are
left Saturday night they will be
sold at the door. The cost is $2.
The dance, to be held at the
Sunny View Club in Mountain
View, will be modeled after the
artists' ball in the movie "An
American in Paris" and will have
a black - and - white costume
theme.
