# Radio Show Is Set By Air RO Cadets
## 
### 
Five Stanford Air RO cadets will
present a weekly radio show "The
Atomic Age," describing the devel
opment of airpower in the atomic
age, commencing tomorrow. The
program will be broadcast every
Saturday from 5:30 to 5:45 p.m. over
station KEEN.
Program material has been ob
tained from flights around the na
tion, on-the-spot recordings, and
interviews with prominent airmen.
The program is written and nar
rated by Richard Pauley.
Other cadets taking part are John
Donovan, Terry Bateman, Gerry
Fortis, and Don Lawrence.
