# Skiers Finish Fifth
## 
### 
Stanford skiers captured fifth
place Saturday at the annual
Northern California Intercollegiate
ski championships held at Sugar
Bowl. Team members Ketil Borde
placed fourth in the cross-country
and Macky Wirth seventh in the
giant slalom.
Top honors at the meet were ta
ken by the University of Nevada,
which led in the giant slalom and
cross-country events. Due to foul
weather conditions, the jumping
event was canceled.
