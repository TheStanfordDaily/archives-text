# Western Civ Panel Tryouts to Be Held
## 
### 
Tryouts will be held tomorrow
at 4:15 p.m. in Room 210 for the
ASSU Speech Association Western
Civilization discussion panel.
The topic of the panel discussion
to be held on Feb. 24 in the Wom
en's Clubhouse will be "Was ,17th
and 18th Century Mercantilism an
Economic or Political Institution?"
Any student of Western Civ is
eligible to try out for the panel. All
candidates will be asked to present
a three- to five-minute oral thesis
before participating in a one-half
hour discussion with the judges.
