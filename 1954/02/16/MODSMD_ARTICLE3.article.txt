# Speaker Will View Foreign Service
## 
### 
Joel Carrington Hudson, retired
foreign service officer, will give his
views of "The Foreign Service To
day" in an open discussion meeting
tomorrow night at 8 in Bowman
Alumni House.
Hudson has been with the Foreign
Service for 31 years. Before he re
tired he was consul general in Mi
lan, and once was the commercial
attache in Bucharest, Rumania. He
has served the United States For
eign Service in the Near and Far
East, South America, and Europe.
Tomorrow night's discussion group
is being sponsored by Delta Phi Ep
silon, foreign trade-foreign service
fraternity, in an effort to interest
prospective members, especially
freshmen and sophomores, accord
ing to President Barry Galvin.
Galvin stressed, however, that this
informal discussion group is open to
aiyone interested in the foreign
service.
