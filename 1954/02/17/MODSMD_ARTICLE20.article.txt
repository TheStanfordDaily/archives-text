# Women to Draw For New Housing
## 
### 
Spring quarter residence drawing
for women will be held tomorrow
from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
Girls may draw singly or with one
or two other girls.
Off - campus women other than
freshmen may draw tomorrow, pro
vided they have paid their $25 room
deposit. Cancellation and shrinkage
information may be found in hous
ing notices in the residences.
Sponsor residence drawing is to
day from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. in the
Housing Office, Room 101-A.
Vacancies, totaling 35, in campus
residences are as follows: Lagunita,
7; Ventura, 2; Union, 1; Madrono, 1;
Durand, 3; Guthrie, 2; Jordan, 5;
Lathrop, 3; Roth, 3; Russell, 2; Still
man, 3; and Storey, 3.
