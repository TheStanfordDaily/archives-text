# YWCA CANDIDATES
## 
### 
Any YWCA member interested in
running for office in the coming
elections may come to the "Y" office
on the third floor of the Women's
Clubhouse from 2 to 4 p.m. today,
1 to 5 p.m tomorrow, or 1 to 3 p.m.
Thursday.
Officers to be elected are: presi
dent, vice-president, secretary, and
treasurer. Chairmen will be ap
pointed for the eight "Y" program
areas: Public Affairs, Drives, Mar
riage and the Home, Freshman Pro
gram, Publicity, Community Serv
ice, Coffee Hour, and Christian Her
itage.
