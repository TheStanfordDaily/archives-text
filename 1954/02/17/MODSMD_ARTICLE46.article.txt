# Geological Society Talks Begin Today
## 
### 
Dr. M. King Hubbert, a repre
sentative of the Shell Oil Company,
will speak today to the Geological
Society at 4 p.m. in Room 320, Geol
ogy Corner.
The lecture is the first of a series
of three lectures on "The Geologi
cal Consequences of the Rise of the
Human Species." Dr. Hubbart will
deliver the remaining two talks to
morrow and Friday afternoon in
the same room at the same time.
