# Crothers Drubs Kappa Sig, 42-22
## 
### 
Crothers I easily defeated Kappa
Si« II by a score of 42-22 in mural
basketball last night in the Encina
Gym.
It was the fourth straight victory
for Crothers.
With the score 6-5 in favor of
Crothers, Red Gresham dropped in
two quick field goals and Crothers
was never in danger after that.
Gresham wound up with high point
honors. He had 13 points. Bill
Barry also hit eight for the winners.
The Kappa Sigs had trouble on
rebounds, where Crothers had a de
cisive advantage.—B.J.
