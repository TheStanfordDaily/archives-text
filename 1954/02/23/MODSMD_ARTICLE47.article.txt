# 'Wild One' May Be Too Wild for P.A.
## 
### 
The Palo Alto Board on Com
mercial Amusements has called for
a review on "The Wild One," star
ring Marlon Brando, before permit
ting it to be shown in Palo Alto.
The film is based on the famed
Hollister incident of 1947, when a
motorcycle convention nearly ripped
the community apart.
Mrs. Lloyd G. Hornby, the chair
man of the board, said "very de
batable" reviews of the picture
have been received. Although no
Palo Alto theater has requested to
show the film, any theater which
does is requested to give the board
a review first.
