# RAM'S HEAD
## Spring Show Tryouts Open 
### 
Tryouts for the Ram's Head
Spring Show will be held tomorrow
night in the Little Theater from 7
to 10 p.m. and will be continued
Friday night and through Wednes
day of next week.
This year 160 persons are needed
to work as singerS, dancers, actors,
extras, and production crew mem
bers.
No work will be done on the show
this quarter. The tryouts are being
held early in order to provide for an
extra week of rehearsals next quar
ter and to facilitate the making of
costumes.
