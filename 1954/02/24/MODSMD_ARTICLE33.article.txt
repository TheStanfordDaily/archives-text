# Players Seek Cast For Molnar Play
## 
### 
Tryouts for "The Plays the Thing"
by Ferenc Molnar will be held today
from 4 to 6 p.m. and tomorrow from
7 to 10 p.m. in Room 205 of IVfemo
rial Auditorium. The tryouts are
open to anyone in the University.
"The Play's the Thing,'' which is
the Stanford Players' first play of
the spring quarter, will be produced
in the Little Theater for eight per
formances, April 14-17 and 21-24.
