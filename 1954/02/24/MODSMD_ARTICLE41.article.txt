# SENATOR ACCUSES BUSCH
## 
### 
WASHINGTON, Feb. 23 (UP) —
Sen. Edwin C. Johnson (D., Colo.)
accused brewer August A. Busch
today of using the St. Louis Cardi
nals to promote a beer "monopoly"
at the expense of minor league
baseball.
Johnson is president of the West
ern Baseball League.
