# '56 Activities Com Will Meet Tonight
## 
### 
The Sophomore Activities Com
mittee will meet fit 5:15 p.m. today
in the Women's Clubhouse.
The 50 members of the committee
represent all sophomore living
groups, and off-campus members of
the class. Organization of ticket
sales for the Sophomore Cotillion
will be discussed. Activities Board
members will sell tickets in the liv
ing groups they represent.
Starting tomorrow, tickets will be
sold at the Cellar and Engineering
Corner for $2.75. The Cotillion will
be held March (5 at Barnum's Ocean
Room in San Francisco from 9 p.m.
to 1 a.m., and will feature the
American Broadcasting Television
Orchestra.
