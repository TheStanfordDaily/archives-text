# Ticket Sale Begins For Soph Cotillion
## 
### 
Tickets for the Sophomore Cotil
lion will sell today for $2.75 at the
Cellar and Engineering Corner. The
dance will be held March 6 from 9
to I in Barnum's Ocean Room, San
Francisco.
Sophomore Activities Board
members will also sell tickets in
their living groups, and tickets will
be sold tonight in the Encina dinner
lines.
The American Broadcasting Tele
vision Orchestra will play for the
semi-formal dance.
