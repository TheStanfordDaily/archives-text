# Librarian to Talk On Thomas' Poetry
## 
### 
William B. Ready, chief acquisi
tions librarian for the Stanford Uni
versity Libraries, will speak to the
Thursday Lunch Club today on
"Dylan Thomas, the Man and the
Poet." He will supplement the talk
with recordings of Thomas reading
his own work.
The meeting will be on the upper
lloor of the Women's Clubhouse at
12 noon. Lunch will be served for
25 cents: all are invited to attend.
