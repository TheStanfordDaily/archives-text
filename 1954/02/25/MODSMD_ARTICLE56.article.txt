# Drama Students To Act in P.A. Play
## 
### 
J
Three students in the Speech and
Drama Department will be featured
in the Palo Alto Community Thea
ter's production of Maxwell Ander
son's "The Masque of Kings."
Celeste Ashley will appear as the
Empress Elizabeth, Gretchen Grills
will be seen as Baroness Mary Vet
sera, and Joel Rogosin will play the
role of Count Hoyos.
"The Masque of Kings" will play
seven performances in the Civic
Theater: today, tomorrow, and Sat
urday, and March 3, 4, 5, and 6.
