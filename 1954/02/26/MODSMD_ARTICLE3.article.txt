# Grad and Upper Division Pre-Reg Begins Monday
## 
### 
Spring quarter pre-registration
for upper division and graduate
students will begin Monday and
end at 5 p.m. Tuesday.
The Registrar's Office has an
nounced that students should
obtain an official study list card
from their major department and
fill out their program in ink.
The card must be appioved by a
faculty adviser.
To enroll in courses marked §
in the spring quarter time sched

ule, students may sign class lists
at the information desk in the
Registrar's Office from 8 a.m. to
12 noon or from 1 to 5 p.m. on
Monday or Tuesday.
Study list cards should be
turned in, in person, to the
check-out clerk at the informa
tion desk before 5 p.m. Tuesday.
Lower division pre-reg will
begin Thursday and continue
through Tuesday noon, March 9.
