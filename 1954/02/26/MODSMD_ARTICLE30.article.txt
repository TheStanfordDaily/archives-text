# Skiers to Compete In Tresidder Meet
## 
### 
The Stanford ski team will com
pete this weekend for the Far West
ern Intercollegiate Ski Champion
ship at the Tresidder Memorial Cup
Meet to be held at Yosemite Valley.
The six competitors for the title will
be Stanford, California, UCLA,
COP, Modesto Junior College, and
last year's winner, the University
of Nevada.
The meet, held in honor of former
Stanford President Dr. Donald B.
Tresidder, is sponsored jointly by
Stanford and the Yosemite Winter
Club.
Competing for Stanford will be
team captain Doug Collins, Macky
Wirth. Bob Burton, Bob Smith,
Fritz Hoedemaker, Ketil Borde, and
Per Ditlev-Simonsen.
The jumping event will begin to
day at 11 a.m. on the 30-meter hill
•at Badger Pass, followed at 2:30 p.m.
by the four-mile cross-country race
starting at the top of the lift and
ending at the Badger ski house.
The downhill race will take place
tomorrow at 2 p.m. on the Rail
Creek slope and the slalom on Sun
day at 10:30 a.m. Awards will be
given at 2:30 p.m. on Sunday at
Badger.
