# Library to Give Recital Of Welsh Music, Poetry
## 
### 
A recital of recorded Welsh mu
sic and poetry will be presented
from 12:30 to 1:15 p.m. today in the
Bender Room of the Main Library
by William Ready, chief acquisi
tions librarian, to mark the Welsh
Feast of Dewi Sant, St. David's
Day.
Poems of Dylan Thomas, read by
the author, will include "A Win
ter's Tale," "There Was a Savior,"
"Fern Hill," and "Do Not Go Gen
tle."
