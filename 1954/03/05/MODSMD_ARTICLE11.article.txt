# Baroque Concert Scheduled Sunday
## 
### 
A program of Baroque music will
be presented Sunday afternoon at
4 in Cubbcrley Auditorium as the
fourth concert in the Music Depart
ment Chamber Music Series.
A chamber orchestra, conducted
by Sandor Salgo, will present the
Bach Concerto in F minor for harp
sichord, with Putnam Aldrkh as
soloist, and the Vivaldi Concerto
Grosso in D minor, with Raymond
Vaught, Barbara Seagrave, and
Kathryn Vaught as soloists.
The Stanford Chorale, conducted
by Harold Schmidt, will perform
three madrigals by Monteverdi.
