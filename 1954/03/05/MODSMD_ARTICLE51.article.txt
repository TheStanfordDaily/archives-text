# TRAVEL FILM
## 
### 
"Kayaks Down the Nile," a film
lecture travelogue, will be narrated
by explorer John Goddard on March
12 at 8 p.m. in Roosevelt Junior
High School, San Jose.
Tickets may be obtained by phon
ing CYpress 3-3469 or at the door
the evening of the performance.
Prices are $1.50 and $1.20, with half
price for students.
