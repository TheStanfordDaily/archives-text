# TERMAN SPEAKS AT BERKELEY
## 
### 
"The Discovery and Encourage
ment of Exceptional Talent" was
the subject chosen by Dr. Lewis
M. Terman, emeritus professor of
psychology at Stanford, for his
Bingham Lecture at the Univer
sity of California last week.
Dr. Terman is the first recipient
of the "Bingham Lecture" award.
It was established to honor per
sons whose work contributes to
ward "the discovery of talent."
Dr. Terman is known throughout
the world for research on intelli
gence and achievement measure
ment.
