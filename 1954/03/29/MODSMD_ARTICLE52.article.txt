# CHEMICAL ENGINEERING
## 
### 
Chemical Engineering has been
added to the name of the De
partment of Chemistry, Professor
George" H. Parks, head of the de
partment, has announced.
Approximately 40 per cent of the
undergraduate and 10 per cent of
the graduate students in the depart
ment are in chemical engineering.
Courses in chemical engineering
train students in large-scale oper
ations of the chemical industry and
in unit operations, instrumentation,
distillation, and other technical sub
jects.
The department will now be
known as the Department of Chem
istry and Chemical Engineering.
