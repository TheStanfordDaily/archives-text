# Play Tryouts Begin Today

### 
Open tryouts for the Stanford
Players' production of Euripides'
"Iphigenia in Tauris" will be held
today and tomorrow from 7:30 to 10
p.m. in Room 205, Memorial Audi
torium.
Robert Loper of the Speech and
Drama Department is to direct the
Greek tragedy, which will have a
cast of nine women and seven men
in speaking roles.
Loper suggested that students in
terested in trying out should read
the Vellacott translation before try
ing out. Copies of this translation

of the Stanford Library.
The play will be presented from
