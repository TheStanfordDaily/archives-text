# Ballet to Give Special Recital
## 
### 
A special matinee performance by
the San Francisco Ballet will be
given on Sunday at 3 p.m. in Me
morial Auditorium, Concert Series
manager Nell McDill has announced.
The scheduled performance will still
be given at 8:15 p.m. Saturday in
Memorial Auditorium.
Tickets for the Saturday perform
ance are sold out, but tickets lor
the Sunday matinee are available
for $2.50, $2, $1.50, and $1. They
will b f sold at the Concert Series
office in the Nitery from 1 to 5 p.m.
today through Friday, at noon in
front of the Cellar, and at the Pe
ninsula Box Office in Palo Alto.
