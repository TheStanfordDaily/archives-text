# Symphony Forum Will Hear Jorda
## 
### 
San Francisco Symphony conduc
tor Enrique Jorda will speak to
members of the Stanford Symphony
Forum (students holding symphony
tickets) tonight at 8 in the lower
lounge of the Women's Clubhouse.
Jorda recently was appointed to
replace Pierre Monteux as conduc
tor of the symphony. His visit, to
Stanford is being sponsored by the
Stanford Symphony Foium.
