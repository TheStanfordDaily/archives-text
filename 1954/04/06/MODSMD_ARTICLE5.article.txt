# Free Immunization To Begin Today
## 
### 
The Health Service will begin
its campus - wide immunization
program today. This program is
open to all registered Stanford
students free of charge. All who
are interested in the plan may go
to the Service any Tuesday or
Thursday during the remainder
of the quarter. Hours are from 1
to 5 p.m.
