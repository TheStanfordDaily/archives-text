# IIR Will Present 'Experiment Way'
## 
### 
"The Experiment Way," a film de
scribing student life and travel
abroad with groups from the Ex
periment in International Living,
will be shown tomorrow at the For
eign Language Film Series.
The film will follow a German
feature, "The Film Without a
Name," which will be shown at 3:15
p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium.
For those wishing additional in
formation about the Experiment,
lIR will hold an informal discussion
Friday at 1:45 p.m. downstairs in
the Women's Clubhouse.
