# Timoshenko Book Published, on Sale
## 
### 
The "Collected Papers" in French,
German, and English of Professor
emeritus Stephen P. Timoshenko,
one of the foremost international
authorities on theoretical and ap
plied engineering mechanics, has
been published by McGraw-Hill
and is now on sale.
The volume includes his scientific
writings in the three languages as
they were originally published, plus
many early papers first issued in his
native Russian and later translated.
A second new book, released al
most simultaneously by the same
publisher, is his "History of Strength
of Materials"
