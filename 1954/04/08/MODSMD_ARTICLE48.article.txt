# Indian Poet, Teacher to Lecture
## 
### 
Buddhadeva Bose, noted Indian
poet and teacher, will give a lecture
on ' Modern Bengali Literature" at
2:15 p.m. today in the Bender Room
of the Stanford Library.
The Bengali writer is spending the

year as a member of the faculty of
Pennsylvania State College. He is
currently visiting West Coast uni
versities on a lecture tour.
Ihe talk is open to the public
without charge.
