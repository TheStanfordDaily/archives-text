# LUNCHEON CLUB
## 
### 
Reinhold Nicbuhr of the Union
Theological Seminary in New York
will speak in a recorded talk before
the Thursday Lunch Club today at.
noon on the subject "The Provi
dence of God." The meeting will be
held on the upper floor of the Wom
en's Clubhouse.
The Rev. Alexander Miller, Stan
ford lecturer in religion, will lead a
discussion following the recording.
Lunch will be served for 30 cents.
The public is invited.
