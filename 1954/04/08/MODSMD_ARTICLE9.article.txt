# Room, Board Bills Due Tomorrow
## 
### 
Tomorrow is the last, day for (lay
ing University board and room bills
without fine, the controller's office
has announced.
Payment should be made at the
cashier's office, Building 1, Inner
Quad. The office opens at 8 a.m. and
closes at 5 p.m.
Students living at Stanford Vil
lage should make payment at the
Village cashier's office, 9:30 a.m.
to 12:30 p.m. and 1:30 to 3:30 p.m.
Checks accompanied by the state
ment may be mailed to the cashier's
office but must be postmarked no
later than tomorrow.
A student, who cannot pay now
should try to arrange for a defer
ment with the Counselor for Men or
Women.
