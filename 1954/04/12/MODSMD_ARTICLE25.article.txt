# Wallet Raider Gets $100 from Phi Kaps
## 
### 
A thief entered the Phi Kappa
Sigma house early Thursday morn
ing and stole about $100 from the
wallets of sleeping students, accord
ing to Stanford Police Chief Gordon
Davis.
Davis said there is no evidence to
indicate whether or not the thief is
the same man who has recently
stolen wallets from several profes
