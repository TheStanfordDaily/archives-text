# French Author Here Wednesday
## 
### 
Rom Landau, author and authori
ty on French North Africa, will
speak' Wednesday at 4:15 p.m. in the
lower lounge of the Women's Club
house.
Landau will discuss recent events
in Morocco, Tunisia, and Algeria and
their relation to the international
scene. He was observer at the
United Nations Moroccan debates
last fall.
The author of about 30 books,
Landau has specialized in philosoph
ical subjects. His book, "God Is My
Adventure," is an international best
seller. His newest volume is "Among
the Americans," which contains
chapters on the West, including the
Bay Area.
