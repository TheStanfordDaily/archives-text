# Quad Editor to Be Elected Thursday
## 
### 
Editor of the 1955 Quad will be
elected Thursday afternoon by pres
ent staff members. Those eligible
for the post are members of this
year's Edit Board: Dave Davies,
Chuck Falkner, Wick Musson,
Lucymae Rice, and Jack Wennberg.
A list of staff members eligible to
vote is posted in the Quad office.
Polls will be open from 3 to 5:30
p.m. Results will be announced at 6
p.m. or as soon as the ballots have
been tallied.
