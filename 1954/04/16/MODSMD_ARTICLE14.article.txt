# Bids Now on Sale For Stern Formal
## 
### 
Tickets are now on sale at the
ASSU office, in front of the Cellar,
and at the Stem Hall office for the
Stern Formal, to be held April 24.
Price of bids is $2.
The San Jose Country Club, near
Alum Rock Park east, of San Jose,
will be the scene of the dance. The
"Springtime" theme will be carried
out. in floral decorations. Clyde Ap
pleby's orchestra will play for danc
ing from 9 p.m. to 1 a.m. Dance
chairman is Bob McCue.
