# Indian Consul Will Speak Tomorrow
## 
### 
Azim Husain, consul general of
India, San Francisco, will speak to
morrow afternoon at Bowman
Alumni House. His talk, which is
sponsored by the lIR, will be held
after a short reception at 4.
Husain, who has held the San
Francisco position since 1952, has
been active in Indian politics since
1930.
