# Candidate Given Con Home Labor
## 
### 
Ron Wagner, one of the candidates
for junior class president, has been
sentenced to 12 hours of hard labor
at the Convalescent Home for ex
ceeding his campaign allowance.
Wagner loft, his jalopy, covered
with posters and slogans, parked for
three days in a 45 minute zone. The
parking tickets he accumulated to
taled more than the $10 allowance.
