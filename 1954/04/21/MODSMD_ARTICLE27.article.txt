# COOKIE SALE
## 
### 
Cap and Gown will hold the last
of three cookie sales on Quad to
day to raise money for their For
eign Scholarship Fund. Cookies will
be sold from 10 a.m. until 3 p.m. at
the Library, Post Office, and Cellar.
Price is 10 cents per bag.
