# Seals Shift To Daytime Games
## 
### 
SAN FRANCISCO, April 20 (UP)
—Day baseball returned here today
as the San Francisco Seals of the
Pacific Coast League voted to play
night baseball at home on Tuesdays
and Fridays only.
The change goes into effect next
week when the Seals return to the
local stadium for a series against the
Hollywood Stars. Day games, now
slated for Wednesday, Thursday,
Saturday, and Sunday, will start at
1:30 p.m.
