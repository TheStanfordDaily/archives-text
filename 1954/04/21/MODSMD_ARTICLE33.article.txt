# Frosh Mine Hosts St. Ignatius Today
## 
### 
The Stanford frosh nine meets St.
Ignatius HS on the frosh diamond at
3:15 p.m. today. Coach Dick Clegg
will start Dick Jones or Ray Looney
for the Papooses.
The Wildcats will be up against
such hitters as outfielder Bob Gur
gen, who connected with a double to
beat the Cal frosh; second baseman
Bob Fletcher, who hit a triple in the
inning to beat Capuchino;
shortstop Rudy Figueroa, who has
been swatting extra-base hits; and
third baseman Howard Messner,
who also has been doing well with
the bat.
