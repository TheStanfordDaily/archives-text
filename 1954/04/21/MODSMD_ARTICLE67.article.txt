# News in Posies
## 
### 
"The flowers lliat bloom in the
spring, tra la" could very well be the
theme sour for spring '54 fashions.
Posies decorate cufflinks, hats,
blouses, dresses, bathing Miils, bags,
shoes, and petticoats.
A final touch—rubber flowers on a
batbing cap!
