# THURSDAY LUNCH CLUB
## 
### 
The Thursday Lunch Club will
meet at noon today to hear a talk
by John Driggs on the fundamental
ideas and ideals of the Church of
Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.
The meeting will be on the upper
floor of the Women's Clubhouse.
Alexander Miller, lecturer in re
ligion, will lead a discussion follow
ing the talk.
