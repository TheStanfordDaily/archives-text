# Folkdancers Hold Party Tonight
## 
### 
Stanford Folkdancers will hold
their annual party tonight in the
Village Auditorium from 8 to 11.
Three square callers, Bill Callan
der, Ken Mallory, and Alan-Wil
munder, will call the American
squares. There will be no instruc
tion.
The party, including refreshments,
is open free of charge to anyone
who wishes to come, stag or in
couples.
The annual party is presented by
the Board of the Stanford Folk
dancers. Committe chairmen are Liz
Littler, program; Margaret Brown,
decorations; Ann Knoles, publicity.
