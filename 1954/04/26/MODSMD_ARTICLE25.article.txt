missing from the Reference Room.
They are: Britannica Book of the
Year, 1953; Collier's Encyclopedia,
Volume G; Chambers' Encyclopedia,
Volume 14; James Tobin, "18th Cen
tury Literature"; Encyclopedia
Americana, Volume 7; and "Hawai
ian Statehood, History."
Officials stated, "It is difficult and
expensive to replace single volumes
of sets, and sometimes nearly im
possible. We urge tha. students who
