# Army RO to End Annual Inspection
## 
### 
Stanford's Army ROTC will finish
up its annual federal inspection to
day. Three officers from the Army
Reserve Adviser Group, visiting
campus to check on general prog
ress of the Army program here dur
ing the past year, will inspect cadets
of the unit at a formal parade this
afternoon.
The three officers, Colonel V. R.
Smith, Major D. N. Nethery, and
Major C. M. Kingstedt, have visited
classes, examined Army property
assigned here, and conferred with
University officials during their
visit.
