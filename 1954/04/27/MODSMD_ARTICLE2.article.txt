# Alpha Sigs to Hold 'Commie' Dance
## 
### 
The Communist May Day will be
the theme of the Alpha Sigma Phi
costume dance to be held Saturday
from 0 p.m. to 1 a.m. at the Alpha
Sig house, 534 Salvatierra Street.
Admission is free, and refreshments
will be served.
The Commie May Day Dance re
places the annual all-campus Dude
Ranch Dance that the Alpha Sigs
usually sponsor. Music will be by
Spike Lynch and a four-piece com
bo, Additional entertainment will
also be ode red.
According to Bob Cathcart, social
chairman, the funnier the costumes,
the better. A few suggestions arc
commissar, peasant, hero med
al, or red herring.
