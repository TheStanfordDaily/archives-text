# Drives Com Sets Interviews Today
## 
### 
The Drives Committee will inter
view prospective members from 4
to 5:30 p.m. today in the Women's
Clubhouse. Freshmen, sophomores,
and juniors arc eligible to try out
for next year's committee..
Three members —an on - campus
men's representative, an on-campus
women's representative, and a rep
resentative, from the Village—will
be selected and will serve on the
committee for a year.
Drives Com is responsible for
choosing the organizations which
will receive the funds collected dur
ing the fall and winter quarter
drives, and also choosing the chair
men of these drives.
Interviews for selecting next
year's chairmen will be held some
time next week.
