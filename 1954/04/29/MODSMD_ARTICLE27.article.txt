# Herberg Will Give Sunday Sermon
## 
### 
Will Herberg, New York philoso
pher and theologian, will deliver a
sermon Sunday at 11 a.m. in Memo
rial Church on "The God of the
Bible and the Gods of Modern
Man."
During a two-week stay on cam
pus, Herberg will deliver another
sermon, "Faith and Responsibility

Herberg was once associated with
the Young Communist League and
with Communist publications, but
broke with the party in 1929 and is
now described as a "virulent anti-
Communist." i
