# U. of Berlin Offers Grant
## 
### 
The Berlin Affiliations Committee
of the Institute of International Re
lations has announced that the Free
University of Berlin is offering a
scholarship for any graduate student
or graduating senior.
The scholarship includes living
expenses and tuition. There is no
allowance for transportation to Ber
lin. However, lIR officials stated
that there is the possibility of a
Fulbright, travel grant to cover trav
eling costs.
The Affiliations Committee has
stressed that fluency in German is
not an immediate requirement.
There are no restrictions as to field
of study.
Applications may be made to the
Berlin Affiliations Committee at the
lIR Shack any afternoon from 4
to 6.
