# Austrian Culture Displayed in Libe
## 
### 
Austrian culture is the subject of
a display in the lobby of thr Main
Library, which will be exhibited
through May 15.
The lIR - sponsored display has
been organized by Erich Hochleit
ner, who is from Austria. It includes
maps of Austria, graphs, photo
graphs, and documents, which at
tempt to show a cross-section of
Austrian culture.
