# SQUARE DANCE
## 
### 
Willy "Hogjowls" Grishaw will
call squares and his Hayseed Six
will furnish the music at a square
dance tomorrow night.
The dance will be Irom 9 to 12 at
Mangini's Barn, located behind
Rudy's Alpine Inn.
Admission charge is 50 cents per
couple or stag.
Stanford Folkdancers will spon
sor this square dance.
