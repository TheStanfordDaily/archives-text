# Herberg to Start Talk Series Today
## 
### 
Will Herberg, Jewish philosopher
theologian visiting Stanford for two
weeks, will deliver the first of a se
ries of talks today at 4:15 p.m. in
the Women's Clubhouse.
His talk, a faculty-studcnt semi
nar, will deal with "Approaches to
Religion—the Philosophical-Ration
al Approach."
Herberg will continue the series
on Wednesday and Friday, at which
times ho will discuss "Approaches to
Religion — the Mystical Approach"
and "Approaches to Religion — the
Biblical- Existenial Approach."
Both metings are open to all stu
dents.
