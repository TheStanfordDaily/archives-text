# Lindeen Elected GSA President
## 
### 
Gordon Lindeen, Jr., was elected
president of the Graduate Students'
Association in a special runoff elec
tion held Friday.
In the regular election the pre
vious week, Brice Wightman, an
electrical engineering major, was
named vice-president.
In discussing his program, Lin
deen, a law student, expressed the
desire to extend the scope of the
GSA to enable it. to meet the needs
of all graduate students. He em
phasized "graduate control of grad
uate affairs," with an expanded so
cial and membership program.
Wightman has said he will devote
himself to the expansion of GSA
membership, and to the support of
a continued active social calendar.
