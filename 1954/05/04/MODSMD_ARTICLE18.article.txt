# IIR Travel Booklet Sale Begins Today
## 
### 
The lIR will sponsor a sale of
travel booklets today at Engineer
ing Corner. The booklets, which
cost 25 cents, include contacts of
Stanford students living abroad as
well as information on visas, hotels,
and currency.
After today's sale, booklets may
be purchased at the lIR Shack on
Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday
from 4 until 5 p.m.
