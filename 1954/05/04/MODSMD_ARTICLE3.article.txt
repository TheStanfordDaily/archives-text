# Graves Will Speak Tomorrow Night
## 
### 
Richard Graves, a candidate for
the gubernatorial nomination on the
Democratic ticket, will discuss the
aspects of freedom of education
within the state before the Political
Union on Wednesday night.
Graves' address, which will begin
at 8 p.m. in Cubberley Auditorium,
is titled "A Challenge to Free Edu
cation in California."
The 47-year-old California poli
tician has worked extensively since
1930 in the fields of public adminis
tration, crime prevention, prison re
form, civil defense, and post-war
reconstruction.
