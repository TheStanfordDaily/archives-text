# Cadet Selectors To Visit Campus
## 
### 
The Aviation Cadet Selection
Team of both the Navy and Marine
Corps will visit Stanford today, to
morrow, and Thursday. Purpose of
the visit is to furnish information
concerning both regular and reserve
air ofTicci" selection programs.
The team will hold interviews at
the office of Dean of Students H.
Donald Winbigler and in the NROTC
Armory, located in the basement of
C übber ley.
Qualifying examinations will be
given at the Armory on each of the
three days between 10 a.m. and 10
p.m. Appointments for evening ex
aminations should be obtained from
any of the team members before 3
p.m. on the day of the test.
