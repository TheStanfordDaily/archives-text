# Sales to Continue For Spring Show
## 
### 
Ticket sales continue today for the
Ram's Head Spring Show, "Any
thing Goes." They may be obtained
at Engineering Corner from 8 a.m.
to 3 p.m., at Memorial Hall box of
fice from 12:30 to 4:30 p.m., and at
the Peninsula Box Ofiicc in Palo
Alto. Prices are 75 cents, $1, and
$1.25.
The show opens this Thursday in
Memorial Auditorium at 8:15 p.m.
It will run through Saturday.

rations for the Spring Show, see
page 4.)
