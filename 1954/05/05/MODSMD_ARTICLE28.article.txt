# Law Wives Receive Honorary Degrees
## 
### 
Twenty-three wives of graduating
law students received honorary de
grees of P.H.T. (Putting Hubby
Through) at a dinner ceremony Fri
day night at the Old Plantation.
Dean and Mrs. Carl Spaeth of the
Law School were honored guests.
Professor John Hurlbut delivered
the commencement address and the
degrees were presented by Mrs.
Lowell Turrentine, honorary presi
dent of the Law Wives' Association.
