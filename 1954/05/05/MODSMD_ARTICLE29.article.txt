# Frosh Netters Foil SJS, 6-3
## 
### 
In their final match of the season,
the frosh netmen downed the San
Jose State yearlings, G-3, on the home
courts yesterday.
In the featured duel of the day,
Stanford's Denny Wetterholm put up
strong fight but Spartan Roger
Smith's pin-point shots were too
much for him. Wetterholm ended up
on the short end of a 3-6, 6-3, 7-5
score.
Other results: Jackson (S) def.
Smallshire (SJ), 6-3, 8-6. Hogue (S)
def. Kirk (SJ), 6-1, 6-2. Warriner
J) def. M. Ely (S), 3-6, 6-3, 6-3.
C. Ely (S) def. Murray (SJ), 6-3,
5-7, 9-7. Symon (S) def. Engel (SJ),
6-0, 6-3.
Jackson and Wetterholm (S) def.
Smith and Murray (SJ), 6-2, 6-3.
Crosby and Hogue (S) del'. Warriner
and Smallshire (SJ>, 6-1, 6-1. Engel
nd Kirk (SJ) def. Symon and M.
Ely (S), 6-3, 4-6, 6-1.
