# Clubs Elect Armbrust Prexy
## 
### 
Martin Armbrust, junior, Los Ar
cos, was elected president of Inter
club Council and Dick Anderson,
sophomore, was elected secretary
treasurer in the elections held Mon
day.
Eighty-two per cent of the mem
bers of the seven eating clubs voted.
The voting was by a preferential bal
lot.
Martin Armbrust won the race for
president on a platform that proposed

a new method of freshman rushing.
He also proposed a program of ath
letic and social functions to increase
unity between the seven clubs, and
promised to strive for a closer rela
tionship between the Interclub
Council and the University.


DICK ANDERSON
—Secretary-treasurer

—President
