# Frosh Fined $150 For Drunk Driving
## 
### 
A Stanford freshman was fined
$150 when he pleaded guilty to a
drunk driving. charge in Palo Alto
Municipal Court Tuesday.
The student was arrested at 1:20
a.m. on Middlefield Road by Palo
Alto police. He will be allowed to
pay the fine in three monthly in
stallments of $50.
